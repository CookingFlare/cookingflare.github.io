How to Make Homemade S'mores Bars


S’mores are the quintessential campfire treat that brings back childhood memories of warm summer nights. The combination of graham crackers, chocolate, and gooey marshmallows is a simple pleasure that never gets old. While making s’mores on a campfire is always a fun and delicious activity, you can recreate that same satisfying flavor in the comfort of your own kitchen with these homemade s’mores bars. These easy-to-make bars will satisfy your s’mores craving without the need for a campfire!

In this blog post, we will guide you through the steps to create the perfect homemade s’mores bars. These bars are an excellent addition to any party, family gathering, or just as a fun weekend baking project. By following the simple instructions below, you can create an irresistible treat that will have everyone coming back for seconds.

Ingredients for Homemade S’mores Bars

To make these homemade s’mores bars, you’ll need a few simple ingredients. Thankfully, none of them are difficult to find, and you likely have many of them already in your kitchen.

For the Crust:

  • 1 ½ cups graham cracker crumbs
  • ¼ cup sugar
  • ½ cup unsalted butter (melted)

For the Chocolate Layer:

  • 1 ½ cups milk chocolate chips (or semi-sweet chocolate chips for a richer flavor)
  • ¼ cup heavy cream

For the Marshmallow Layer:

  • 2 cups mini marshmallows (or larger marshmallows cut into smaller pieces)
  • 1 tbsp unsalted butter

Optional Garnish:

  • Additional graham cracker crumbs for sprinkling
  • Extra chocolate chips for decoration

These ingredients are a blend of simple pantry staples that come together to form the layers that give s’mores bars their signature flavor. From the buttery graham cracker crust to the melting marshmallows and rich chocolate, this combination is a sure winner.

Step-by-Step Guide to Making S’mores Bars

Step 1: Preheat the Oven

Start by preheating your oven to 350°F (175°C). You’ll want to make sure it’s fully heated before you begin baking.

Step 2: Prepare the Crust

The crust is the base of your s’mores bars and gives them a nice, crunchy texture that balances out the softness of the marshmallows and chocolate.

  • In a medium-sized bowl, combine the graham cracker crumbs and sugar.
  • Add the melted butter and mix everything together until the crumbs are evenly coated with butter. The mixture should hold together when pressed, but not be too wet.

Once the crust mixture is ready, line an 8x8-inch (or similar size) baking dish with parchment paper or lightly grease it with butter or cooking spray. Press the graham cracker mixture evenly into the bottom of the prepared baking dish. Use the back of a spoon or your fingers to press it down firmly, ensuring it’s compacted.

Step 3: Bake the Crust

Place the baking dish in the preheated oven and bake the graham cracker crust for about 10-12 minutes, or until it’s lightly golden brown. You don’t need to overbake the crust, as it will continue to set while cooling.

Once done, remove the dish from the oven and allow the crust to cool for a few minutes while you prepare the chocolate layer.

Step 4: Make the Chocolate Layer

While the crust cools, you can begin to prepare the chocolate layer. This step is all about melting the chocolate and creating a smooth, gooey filling that will become a delicious layer on top of the graham cracker base.

  • In a microwave-safe bowl, combine the milk chocolate chips and heavy cream.
  • Microwave the mixture in 20-30 second intervals, stirring well after each interval, until the chocolate is fully melted and smooth. Be careful not to burn the chocolate—if you prefer, you can melt it over a double boiler on the stovetop instead.

Once the chocolate is melted and creamy, pour it over the graham cracker crust. Use a spatula to spread it out evenly, covering the entire surface.

Step 5: Add the Marshmallow Layer

Now comes the best part—the marshmallows! These bars wouldn’t be complete without a gooey, sweet marshmallow layer.

  • Scatter the mini marshmallows over the chocolate layer. You want to cover the surface with a generous layer of marshmallows. If you’re using larger marshmallows, be sure to cut them into smaller pieces so they distribute evenly.
  • Dot a little butter over the marshmallows, as this will help them melt more smoothly and create that golden-brown color when they bake.

Step 6: Bake the Bars

Now that you’ve assembled all the layers, it’s time to bake everything together.

  • Return the baking dish to the oven and bake the bars for about 10-12 minutes, or until the marshmallows are golden brown and slightly toasted. Keep an eye on them towards the end of baking to make sure the marshmallows don’t burn.

Once baked, remove the dish from the oven and let it cool completely at room temperature. If you’re in a rush, you can place it in the refrigerator to speed up the cooling process.

Step 7: Cut into Bars and Serve

Once the s’mores bars have cooled and set, it’s time to cut them into squares. Use a sharp knife to slice through the marshmallows, chocolate, and graham cracker crust. You’ll want to use a sawing motion to prevent the marshmallows from pulling away from the chocolate.

For the perfect serving size, cut the bars into squares or rectangles, depending on your preference. Serve them as-is, or sprinkle with extra graham cracker crumbs or chocolate chips for a little extra flair.

Step 8: Store Leftovers (if there are any!)

These homemade s’mores bars are sure to disappear quickly, but if you happen to have leftovers, they can be stored for later. Keep the bars in an airtight container at room temperature for up to 3 days. Alternatively, you can refrigerate them to extend their freshness for up to a week.

If you want to freeze them for a longer shelf life, wrap individual bars in plastic wrap and store them in a freezer-safe bag for up to 2 months. Simply thaw them at room temperature before serving.

Tips and Tricks for Perfect S’mores Bars

To make your homemade s’mores bars even better, here are a few additional tips and tricks:

  1. Use High-Quality Chocolate: While milk chocolate chips work well, you can also experiment with dark chocolate or semi-sweet chocolate for a more decadent flavor. The quality of the chocolate really impacts the final taste.

  2. Toast the Marshmallows: If you love that charred marshmallow flavor, you can toast the marshmallows under a broiler for a few seconds at the end of baking. Keep an eye on them to avoid burning, but this method gives the bars a nice, smoky flavor.

  3. Add a Twist with Other Ingredients: Feel free to get creative! You can add crushed nuts, like pecans or almonds, to the graham cracker crust or sprinkle a pinch of sea salt on top of the marshmallows for a sweet and salty balance.

  4. Make It Gluten-Free: For a gluten-free version, simply use gluten-free graham crackers or substitute with other gluten-free crackers or cookies.

  5. Microwave for Quick Melting: If you don’t want to bother with melting chocolate in a double boiler, a microwave works perfectly fine for melting chocolate chips and heavy cream. Just make sure to stir the chocolate every 20 seconds to avoid burning.

Why You Should Try These S’mores Bars

These homemade s’mores bars are not just an easy way to enjoy the classic s’mores flavor, but they also come with the benefit of being a no-fuss treat that anyone can make, whether you’re an experienced baker or just starting in the kitchen. The recipe is versatile, customizable, and guaranteed to please kids and adults alike.

Plus, these bars are a great dessert for almost any occasion—whether it’s a summer party, a casual family dinner, or even a quick weeknight treat. With minimal effort, you can create something that tastes just like the outdoors but without the mess or hassle of a campfire.

Conclusion

In summary, these homemade s’mores bars are the perfect dessert for anyone who loves the flavor combination of graham crackers, chocolate, and marshmallows. With just a few simple ingredients and a few easy steps, you can enjoy all the deliciousness of s’mores without needing to build a fire. Whether you’re making them for a special occasion or simply to satisfy a craving, these s’mores bars will always be a crowd-pleaser.

Now that you know how to make them, don’t hesitate to try out this recipe for yourself! Get ready to indulge in these gooey, chocolatey treats that everyone will love. Happy baking!
