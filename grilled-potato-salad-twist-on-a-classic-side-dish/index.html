Grilled Potato Salad: Twist on a Classic Side Dish


Potato salad is a beloved classic, often found at picnics, potlucks, and backyard barbecues. Its creamy and tangy flavors make it the perfect accompaniment to grilled meats and other summer dishes. While the traditional version is undeniably delicious, why not give it a twist by adding some smoky goodness? Enter the Grilled Potato Salad, a unique take on the classic side dish that will elevate your summer gatherings to a whole new level.

Ingredients

  • 2 pounds of baby potatoes, halved
  • 2 tablespoons of olive oil
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • 4 slices of bacon, cooked and crumbled
  • 1/2 cup of mayonnaise
  • 2 tablespoons of Dijon mustard
  • 2 tablespoons of apple cider vinegar
  • 1/4 cup of finely chopped red onion
  • 2 tablespoons of finely chopped fresh dill
  • 2 tablespoons of finely chopped fresh chives

Instructions

  1. Preheat your grill to medium-high heat.

  2. In a large bowl, toss the halved baby potatoes with olive oil, salt, and black pepper until well coated.

  3. Place the potatoes on the grill, cut side down, and cook for about 10-12 minutes, or until they are tender and have grill marks. Flip them over halfway through for even cooking.

  4. Remove the potatoes from the grill and let them cool slightly. Transfer them to a cutting board and cut them into quarters.

  5. In a separate bowl, combine the mayonnaise, Dijon mustard, apple cider vinegar, red onion, dill, and chives. Mix well until all the ingredients are thoroughly incorporated.

  6. Add the grilled potatoes and crumbled bacon to the bowl with the dressing. Gently toss until the potatoes are evenly coated.

  7. Season with additional salt and black pepper to taste.

  8. Cover the bowl with plastic wrap and refrigerate for at least 1 hour to allow the flavors to meld together.

  9. Serve chilled and enjoy!

Why Grilled Potato Salad?

Grilling the potatoes adds a smoky and charred flavor that takes this classic dish to a whole new level. It brings out the natural sweetness of the potatoes and adds a delightful crunch to every bite. The combination of the creamy dressing, tangy Dijon mustard, and fresh herbs creates a flavor explosion that will have your taste buds dancing with joy.

Not only does grilling the potatoes enhance the taste, but it also adds a visually appealing element to the dish. The grill marks on the potatoes give them a rustic and inviting appearance, making this salad a showstopper on any table.

Serving Suggestions

Grilled Potato Salad pairs well with a variety of dishes. It makes a great side dish for grilled steaks, burgers, or chicken. You can also serve it alongside grilled vegetables or as part of a summer buffet. The possibilities are endless!

Make it Your Own

Feel free to customize this Grilled Potato Salad recipe to suit your preferences. You can add other grilled vegetables like bell peppers or zucchini for an extra burst of flavor and texture. If you’re a fan of spice, sprinkle some cayenne pepper or paprika on top for a kick. Experiment with different herbs and spices to create your own signature twist on this classic side dish.

In Conclusion

Grilled Potato Salad is a delicious and unique twist on the classic side dish. With its smoky flavors, creamy dressing, and fresh herbs, it’s sure to become a favorite at your summer gatherings. So fire up the grill, gather your friends and family, and indulge in this delectable summer delight. Happy grilling!
