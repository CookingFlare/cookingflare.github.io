Harvest Quinoa Salad: Nutritious and Delicious Fall Recipe


Fall is the perfect season to indulge in hearty and wholesome meals that celebrate the bountiful harvest. And what better way to embrace the flavors of autumn than with a delicious and nutritious Harvest Quinoa Salad? Packed with seasonal ingredients, this recipe is a delightful combination of colors, textures, and flavors that will leave you feeling satisfied and nourished.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup quinoa, rinsed
  • 2 cups vegetable broth
  • 1 cup diced butternut squash
  • 1 cup chopped kale
  • 1/2 cup dried cranberries
  • 1/2 cup toasted pecans
  • 1/4 cup crumbled feta cheese
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons balsamic vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon maple syrup
  • 1 teaspoon Dijon mustard
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. In a medium-sized saucepan, bring the vegetable broth to a boil. Add the rinsed quinoa and reduce the heat to low. Cover and let it simmer for about 15-20 minutes, or until the quinoa is fluffy and the liquid is absorbed.

  2. While the quinoa is cooking, preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C). Place the diced butternut squash on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper. Drizzle with olive oil, sprinkle with salt and pepper, and toss to coat. Roast in the oven for about 20-25 minutes, or until the squash is tender and lightly browned.

  3. In a small bowl, whisk together the olive oil, balsamic vinegar, maple syrup, Dijon mustard, salt, and pepper. Set aside.

  4. Once the quinoa and butternut squash are ready, transfer them to a large mixing bowl. Add the chopped kale, dried cranberries, toasted pecans, and crumbled feta cheese. Drizzle the dressing over the salad and toss gently to combine.

  5. Serve the Harvest Quinoa Salad immediately as a main dish or as a side to your favorite fall-inspired meal. Enjoy the vibrant colors and the delightful medley of flavors that will tantalize your taste buds.

Why Quinoa?

Quinoa is a versatile and nutritious grain that offers a wide range of health benefits. It is gluten-free, high in protein, and packed with essential vitamins and minerals. Quinoa is also a good source of dietary fiber, which aids digestion and helps you feel fuller for longer. In this Harvest Quinoa Salad, the grain serves as a nutritious base, providing a satisfying and wholesome element to the dish.

The Autumnal Twist

What truly sets this Harvest Quinoa Salad apart is the addition of seasonal ingredients that embody the essence of fall. The sweet and earthy butternut squash adds a comforting element, while the vibrant kale adds a touch of freshness and a burst of color. The dried cranberries lend a subtle sweetness, and the toasted pecans provide a delightful crunch. Combined with the tangy feta cheese and the flavorful dressing, this salad is a true celebration of autumn’s harvest.

Conclusion

Embrace the flavors of fall with this Nutritious and Delicious Harvest Quinoa Salad. It’s a simple yet satisfying dish that highlights the beauty and abundance of the season. Whether you enjoy it as a main course or as a side dish, this salad is sure to become a fall favorite in your kitchen. So gather the ingredients, let the aromas of autumn fill your home, and indulge in this wholesome and flavorful recipe. Happy cooking!
