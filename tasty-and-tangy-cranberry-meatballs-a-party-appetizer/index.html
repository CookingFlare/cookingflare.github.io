Tasty and Tangy Cranberry Meatballs: A Party Appetizer


When it comes to hosting a party or get-together, having the perfect appetizer can set the tone for the entire event. That’s where these tasty and tangy cranberry meatballs come in. They are a crowd-pleaser and will leave your guests craving for more. The blend of savory meatballs and sweet-tart cranberry sauce creates a flavor explosion that will have everyone reaching for seconds. So, without further ado, let’s dive into the recipe and discover how to make these delectable party appetizers.

Ingredients

For the Meatballs:

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1/2 cup bread crumbs
  • 1/4 cup milk
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper

For the Cranberry Sauce:

  • 1 cup cranberry sauce
  • 1/2 cup ketchup
  • 2 tablespoons brown sugar
  • 2 tablespoons apple cider vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 1 teaspoon Worcestershire sauce
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground ginger
  • 1/4 teaspoon red pepper flakes

For Garnish:

  • Fresh parsley, chopped (optional)

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C) and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.

  2. In a large bowl, combine all the meatball ingredients. Using your hands, mix until everything is well combined.

  3. Shape the meat mixture into 1-inch meatballs and place them on the prepared baking sheet.

  4. Bake the meatballs in the preheated oven for 15-20 minutes, or until they are cooked through and browned on the outside.

  5. While the meatballs are baking, prepare the cranberry sauce. In a saucepan, combine the cranberry sauce, ketchup, brown sugar, apple cider vinegar, soy sauce, Worcestershire sauce, ground ginger, and red pepper flakes. Stir to combine.

  6. Place the saucepan over medium heat and bring the cranberry sauce mixture to a simmer. Let it cook for 5-7 minutes, stirring occasionally, until the sauce thickens slightly.

  7. Once the meatballs are done, transfer them to a large bowl and pour the cranberry sauce over them. Gently toss the meatballs until they are evenly coated with the sauce.

  8. If desired, sprinkle the meatballs with chopped fresh parsley for a pop of color and freshness.

  9. Serve the cranberry meatballs on a platter with toothpicks for easy grabbing, and watch them disappear in no time!

Tips and Variations

  • If you prefer a stronger tangy flavor, add more apple cider vinegar or a squeeze of fresh lemon juice to the cranberry sauce.
  • For a bit of heat, sprinkle some additional red pepper flakes over the meatballs before serving.
  • These meatballs can also be served over cooked rice or as a filling for sliders for a fun twist.

Conclusion

These tasty and tangy cranberry meatballs are a surefire way to elevate any party or gathering. Their irresistible sweet and savory flavor profile will have your guests singing your praises. So, the next time you’re planning a get-together, consider making these meatballs for a memorable appetizer that will leave everyone wanting the recipe. Enjoy!
