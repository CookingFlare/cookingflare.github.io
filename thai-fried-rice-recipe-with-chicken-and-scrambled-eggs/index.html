Thai Fried Rice Recipe with Chicken and Scrambled Eggs


Are you craving a delicious and flavorful Thai dish that you can whip up in no time? Look no further than this mouthwatering Thai Fried Rice recipe with chicken and scrambled eggs. Packed with bold flavors and aromatic spices, this dish will transport your taste buds straight to the streets of Thailand. Plus, it’s incredibly easy to make, making it the perfect option for a quick and satisfying meal. So, let’s get cooking!

Ingredients

To create this delectable Thai Fried Rice, you’ll need the following ingredients:

  • 2 cups of cooked jasmine rice (preferably chilled)
  • 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts, thinly sliced
  • 3 large eggs, lightly beaten
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 red bell pepper, diced
  • 1 carrot, diced
  • 1 cup of frozen peas
  • 2 tablespoons of vegetable oil
  • 2 tablespoons of soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon of oyster sauce
  • 1 tablespoon of fish sauce
  • 1 teaspoon of sugar
  • Fresh cilantro, for garnish
  • Lime wedges, for serving

Instructions

  1. Heat one tablespoon of vegetable oil in a large wok or skillet over medium-high heat. Add the chicken and stir-fry until cooked through and lightly browned. Remove the chicken from the wok and set aside.

  2. In the same wok, add another tablespoon of vegetable oil and swirl it around to coat the surface. Add the minced garlic and chopped onion, and sauté until fragrant and golden brown.

  3. Push the garlic and onion to one side of the wok, creating a space in the center. Pour the beaten eggs into the center of the wok and scramble them until they are just cooked. Mix the scrambled eggs with the garlic and onion.

  4. Add the diced red bell pepper, carrot, and frozen peas to the wok. Stir-fry the vegetables for a few minutes until they are tender-crisp.

  5. Return the cooked chicken to the wok and mix it with the vegetables.

  6. Add the chilled cooked rice to the wok, breaking up any clumps with a spatula. Stir-fry the rice with the other ingredients, ensuring that everything is well combined.

  7. In a small bowl, whisk together the soy sauce, oyster sauce, fish sauce, and sugar. Pour this sauce over the rice mixture in the wok and stir-fry for another minute or two, until the rice is evenly coated and heated through.

  8. Remove the wok from the heat and garnish the Thai Fried Rice with fresh cilantro. Serve the dish hot with lime wedges on the side for an extra burst of tangy flavor.

Tips and Variations

  • For an extra kick of heat, add a sliced red chili pepper to the wok when stir-frying the vegetables.
  • Feel free to customize this recipe by adding other vegetables of your choice, such as broccoli, mushrooms, or baby corn.
  • If you prefer a vegetarian version, simply omit the chicken and add more vegetables or tofu.
  • To make this dish even more authentic, consider using Thai jasmine rice, which has a fragrant aroma and a slightly sticky texture.

Enjoy the Authentic Flavors of Thailand

This Thai Fried Rice recipe with chicken and scrambled eggs is a fantastic way to bring the vibrant flavors of Thailand into your own kitchen. With its aromatic spices, tender chicken, and fluffy scrambled eggs, this dish is sure to satisfy your cravings for a delicious and satisfying meal. So, gather your ingredients, fire up your wok, and get ready to embark on a culinary journey to the streets of Thailand. Enjoy!
