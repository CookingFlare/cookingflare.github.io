Italian-Style Breakfast Strata Recipe with Sausage and Eggs


  1. In a large skillet over medium heat, cook the Italian sausage until browned and cooked through. Remove the sausage from the skillet and set aside.

  2. In the same skillet, add the onion, garlic, red bell pepper, and mushrooms. Sauté until the vegetables are tender, about 5 minutes.

  3. Add the cherry tomatoes and baby spinach to the skillet. Cook for an additional 2 minutes, until the spinach wilts slightly. Remove the skillet from heat and set aside.

  4. In a large mixing bowl, whisk together the eggs, milk, Parmesan cheese, dried oregano, salt, and pepper.

  5. Place half of the cubed Italian bread in the prepared baking dish. Spread the cooked sausage over the bread, followed by the sautéed vegetables. Top with the remaining bread cubes.

  6. Pour the egg mixture evenly over the bread and sausage mixture. Press down slightly to ensure all the bread is soaked.

  7. Sprinkle the shredded mozzarella cheese over the top.

  8. Cover the baking dish with aluminum foil and let it sit for 15 minutes to allow the bread to absorb the liquid.

  9. Bake the strata in the preheated oven for 45 minutes. Then, remove the foil and bake for an additional 10-15 minutes until the top is golden brown and the strata is set.

  10. Remove from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes before serving.

Serving Suggestions

This Italian-Style Breakfast Strata is a hearty and flavorful dish that’s perfect for a weekend brunch or a special occasion breakfast. The combination of Italian sausage, eggs, and vegetables creates a deliciously satisfying meal that will leave everyone asking for seconds.

To serve, cut the strata into squares and plate them. You can enjoy it as is or pair it with a side salad or some fresh fruit for a complete meal. Serve it with a cup of coffee or a glass of freshly squeezed orange juice to start your day off right.

Final Thoughts

The Italian-Style Breakfast Strata recipe is a versatile dish that allows you to customize it to your liking. You can switch out the Italian sausage for bacon or ham, and add different vegetables or cheeses to suit your taste. It’s a great way to use up leftover bread and incorporate a variety of flavors into one delicious breakfast casserole.

Make this Italian-inspired dish for your next brunch gathering or surprise your family with a special breakfast treat. The combination of flavors and textures in this strata will surely impress your guests and leave them wanting more. Enjoy!
