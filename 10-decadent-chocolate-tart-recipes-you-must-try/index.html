10 Decadent Chocolate Tart Recipes You Must Try


Chocolate tarts are the epitome of indulgence, combining rich flavors with a delicate, buttery crust. Whether you’re celebrating a special occasion or simply treating yourself, these 10 decadent chocolate tart recipes will satisfy your sweet tooth and impress your guests. Let’s dive into these delightful creations that are sure to elevate your dessert repertoire!

1. Classic Dark Chocolate Tart

Ingredients:

  • For the crust:

    • 1 ½ cups all-purpose flour
    • ½ cup unsweetened cocoa powder
    • ½ cup powdered sugar
    • ½ cup unsalted butter, chilled and cubed
    • 1 large egg yolk
    • 2-3 tablespoons ice water

  • For the filling:

    • 1 cup heavy cream
    • 12 ounces dark chocolate (70% cocoa), chopped
    • 3 large eggs
    • ½ cup granulated sugar
    • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
    • A pinch of salt

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the crust: In a large bowl, combine the flour, cocoa powder, and powdered sugar. Add the chilled butter and mix until crumbly. Stir in the egg yolk and add ice water, one tablespoon at a time, until the dough forms. Chill for 30 minutes.
  2. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C). Roll out the dough and press it into a tart pan. Bake for 15 minutes. Let it cool.
  3. Make the filling: In a saucepan, heat the cream until just boiling. Remove from heat and add the chopped chocolate. Stir until smooth. In a bowl, whisk together the eggs, sugar, vanilla, and salt. Gradually add the chocolate mixture to the egg mixture, whisking constantly.
  4. Pour the filling into the cooled crust and bake for another 25 minutes. Let it cool before serving.

2. Salted Caramel Chocolate Tart

Ingredients:

  • For the crust:

    • 1 ½ cups crushed graham crackers
    • ½ cup unsalted butter, melted
    • ¼ cup granulated sugar

  • For the filling:

    • 1 cup heavy cream
    • 8 ounces semi-sweet chocolate, chopped
    • ½ cup homemade or store-bought salted caramel sauce
    • Sea salt for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the crust: Mix the crushed graham crackers, melted butter, and sugar until combined. Press the mixture into a tart pan and bake at 350°F (175°C) for 10 minutes. Let cool.
  2. Make the filling: Heat the cream in a saucepan until it simmers. Remove from heat and add the chopped chocolate, stirring until melted and smooth.
  3. Pour the chocolate filling into the cooled crust. Drizzle the salted caramel sauce over the top and swirl gently with a knife. Refrigerate until set. Before serving, sprinkle with sea salt.

3. Chocolate Ganache Tart with Raspberries

Ingredients:

  • For the crust:

    • 1 ½ cups chocolate cookie crumbs
    • ½ cup unsalted butter, melted

  • For the filling:

    • 1 ½ cups heavy cream
    • 12 ounces dark chocolate, chopped
    • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
    • Fresh raspberries for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the crust: Combine chocolate cookie crumbs and melted butter. Press into a tart pan and bake at 350°F (175°C) for 8 minutes. Cool completely.
  2. Make the filling: Heat the heavy cream until it simmers. Remove from heat and add the chocolate and vanilla, stirring until smooth.
  3. Pour the ganache into the cooled crust and let it set in the fridge for at least two hours. Top with fresh raspberries before serving.

4. Espresso Chocolate Tart

Ingredients:

  • For the crust:

    • 1 ½ cups all-purpose flour
    • ½ cup cocoa powder
    • ½ cup powdered sugar
    • ½ cup unsalted butter, chilled and cubed
    • 1 large egg yolk
    • 2-3 tablespoons ice water

  • For the filling:

    • 1 cup heavy cream
    • 8 ounces dark chocolate, chopped
    • 2 tablespoons espresso powder
    • 2 large eggs
    • ½ cup granulated sugar
    • Pinch of salt

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the crust: Follow the same steps as the Classic Dark Chocolate Tart.
  2. Make the filling: Heat cream until boiling, then add chocolate and espresso powder, stirring until smooth. In another bowl, whisk eggs and sugar, then combine with the chocolate mixture.
  3. Pour into the tart shell and bake at 350°F (175°C) for 20 minutes. Cool before slicing.

5. Nutella Chocolate Tart

Ingredients:

  • For the crust:

    • 1 ½ cups almond flour
    • ½ cup cocoa powder
    • ½ cup coconut oil, melted
    • ¼ cup maple syrup

  • For the filling:

    • 1 ½ cups Nutella
    • 1 cup heavy cream
    • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
    • Chopped hazelnuts for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the crust: Mix almond flour, cocoa powder, melted coconut oil, and maple syrup until combined. Press into a tart pan and chill for 30 minutes.
  2. Make the filling: Beat Nutella, cream, and vanilla until smooth. Pour into the crust and chill until set. Garnish with chopped hazelnuts before serving.

6. Chocolate Orange Tart

Ingredients:

  • For the crust:

    • 1 ½ cups all-purpose flour
    • ½ cup unsweetened cocoa powder
    • ½ cup powdered sugar
    • ½ cup unsalted butter, chilled and cubed
    • 1 large egg yolk
    • 2-3 tablespoons ice water

  • For the filling:

    • 1 cup heavy cream
    • 8 ounces dark chocolate, chopped
    • Zest of 1 orange
    • ¼ cup fresh orange juice
    • 2 large eggs
    • ¼ cup granulated sugar

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the crust: Follow the same procedure as the Classic Dark Chocolate Tart.
  2. Make the filling: Heat the cream, then add the chocolate, orange zest, and juice, stirring until smooth. Whisk in the eggs and sugar, then pour into the tart shell.
  3. Bake at 350°F (175°C) for 25 minutes. Allow to cool before serving.

7. Mint Chocolate Tart

Ingredients:

  • For the crust:

    • 1 ½ cups chocolate cookie crumbs
    • ½ cup unsalted butter, melted

  • For the filling:

    • 1 cup heavy cream
    • 8 ounces dark chocolate, chopped
    • ½ teaspoon peppermint extract
    • 2 large eggs
    • ¼ cup granulated sugar
    • Fresh mint leaves for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the crust: Combine cookie crumbs and butter, press into a tart pan, and bake at 350°F (175°C) for 8 minutes. Cool.
  2. Make the filling: Heat cream until simmering. Remove from heat, add chocolate and peppermint extract, stirring until smooth. Whisk in eggs and sugar.
  3. Pour into the crust and bake for 20 minutes. Cool and garnish with fresh mint before serving.
