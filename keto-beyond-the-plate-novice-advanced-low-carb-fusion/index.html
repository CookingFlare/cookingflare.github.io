Sizzling Salads: Bold & Nutritious

Kohlrabi & Pickled Rhubarb Slaw

Ingredients:

  • 1 kohlrabi, shaved into thin strips
  • 1/4 cup thinly sliced pickled rhubarb
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • Juice of 1 small lemon
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley, chopped

Instructions:

  1. Toss kohlrabi and pickled rhubarb in a bowl.
  2. Whisk together olive oil, lemon juice, salt, and pepper in a separate small bowl.
  3. Pour dressing over vegetables and mix well.
  4. Sprinkle with chopped parsley before serving.
  5. Revel in the unexpected, tangy burst of flavor.

Dazzling Delicacies: Sophisticated Small Bites

Tiny portions, massive impact - discover the allure of these sophisticated small bites that encapsulate the essence of delightful low-carb fusion.

Smoky Prosciutto Cups with Manchego Crust

Ingredients:

  • 12 cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 12 slices prosciutto
  • 1/2 cup grated Manchego cheese
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • Olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C).
  2. Slit each tomato horizontally, then place them on a lined baking sheet, cut side up. Drizzle with olive oil and season with salt and pepper.
  3. Wrap each tomato with a prosciutto slice and sprinkle with Manchego. Bake for 20-25 minutes, until golden.
  4. Indulge in the harmonious marriage of smoky and cheesy goodness.

Captivating Creations: Moreish Main Courses

Break free from the shackles of traditional keto and step into the world of modern gourmet - these main courses are the epitome of innovative low-carb excitement.

Lemon & Thyme Grilled Scallops with Cauliflower Purée

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb large scallops
  • Juice and zest of 1 lemon
  • 2 sprigs fresh thyme
  • Salt and pepper
  • 1 head of cauliflower
  • 4 tbsp unsalted butter
  • 2 tbsp heavy cream

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your grill to high.
  2. In a bowl, mix lemon juice, zest, thyme, salt, and pepper. Add scallops and coat thoroughly. Set aside to marinate for 15 minutes.
  3. For the cauliflower purée, chop cauliflower into florets and steam until tender. Puree with butter, cream, salt, and pepper in a blender or food processor, until smooth.
  4. Remove scallops from marinade and grill for 2-3 minutes per side, until nicely charred.
  5. Serve scallops atop a generous dollop of cauliflower purée and savor the aromatic blend of flavors.

Tantalizing Treats: Sweet Tooth Satisfaction

Save room for dessert! Gorgeously crafted, these sweet delights prove that a life embracing Keto Beyond the Plate is a life entirely ripe with opportunity and scrumptious surprises.

Chocolate Avocado Mousse with Nutty Swirl

Ingredients:

  • 2 ripe avocados
  • 1/2 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1/2 cup powdered erythritol
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • 1/2 cup unsalted toasted nuts, finely ground

Instructions:

  1. Scoop avocado flesh into a food processor.
  2. Add cocoa powder, powdered erythritol, and vanilla extract. Process until combined.
  3. With the processor running, slowly add the heavy cream until the mixture is smooth.
  4. Transfer half the mousse to another bowl, fold in the ground nuts, and set aside.
  5. Layer the nutty mousse with plain mousse in glasses or small serving dishes. Chill for an hour before serving.
  6. Indulge in every lusciously rich spoonful as you massage your satisfied palate.
