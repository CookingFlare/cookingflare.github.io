Bountiful Broccoli: 7 Green Infused Broccoli Juice & Smoothie Recipes


Broccoli, the superhero of greens, has once again swooped in to save the day with its unmatched nutritional value. Rich in fiber, vitamin C, potassium, and antioxidants, broccoli is a powerhouse of health benefits. A versatile ingredient, broccoli can be found starring in both juices and smoothies, offering a delightful way to sneak in those extra nutrients. In this post, we’ll share 7 refreshing broccoli-infused recipes to elevate your breakfast or snack game.

1. Green Superhero Smoothie

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup fresh broccoli florets
  • 1 ripe banana
  • 1 cup spinach
  • 1 cup unsweetened almond milk
  • 1 tsp honey

Instructions:

  1. Place the broccoli florets and banana into a blender and blend until smooth.
  2. Add spinach, almond milk, and honey into the blender with the puréed broccoli-banana mixture.
  3. Blend again until smooth and creamy.
  4. Pour your Green Superhero Smoothie into a glass and enjoy a nutritious breakfast.

2. Broccoli Avocado Powerhouse Juice

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup fresh broccoli florets
  • 1 cup spinach
  • 1 cup chopped cucumber
  • 1 small avocado
  • 1/2 lemon, juiced

Instructions:

  1. Rinse the broccoli florets, cucumber, spinach, and avocado.
  2. Feed the ingredients through a juicer, one by one.
  3. Pour your freshly squeezed Broccoli Avocado Powerhouse Juice into a glass and enjoy its green goodness!

3. Broccoli Blueberry Boost Smoothie Bowl

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup fresh broccoli florets
  • 1 cup unsweetened almond milk
  • 1/2 cup blueberries
  • 1/2 banana
  • 1 tsp chia seeds

Instructions:

  1. Add the broccoli florets, almond milk, and frozen blueberries into a blender and blend until smooth.
  2. Transfer the smoothie to a bowl and freeze for 15 minutes.
  3. While waiting, chop the remaining half banana and set aside.
  4. Remove the smoothie bowl from the freezer, top it with the sliced banana, and sprinkle with chia seeds.
  5. Enjoy your Broccoli Blueberry Boost Smoothie Bowl!

4. Green Macha Elixir

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup fresh broccoli florets
  • 1 tsp matcha green tea powder
  • 1/2 cup almond milk
  • 1/2 cup cold water

Instructions:

  1. Blanch the broccoli florets in boiling water for a few seconds, then rinse with cold water to maintain their color.
  2. In a blender, combine the blanched broccoli, matcha green tea powder, almond milk, and cold water.
  3. Blend until smooth, and pour your Green Macha Elixir into a glass to enjoy a wake-me-up breakfast.

5. Broccoli Ginger Immunity Cooler

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup fresh broccoli florets
  • 1-inch piece of fresh ginger
  • 1/2 lemon, juiced
  • 1 1/2 cups water

Instructions:

  1. Rinse the broccoli florets and ginger.
  2. Feed the ginger and broccoli through a juicer, and combine with water in a pitcher.
  3. Stir well, and add lemon juice.
  4. Refrigerate for 30 minutes to let the flavors blend.
  5. Pour your rejuvenating Broccoli Ginger Immunity Cooler into a glass.

6. Broccoli Pineapple Power Punch

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup fresh broccoli florets
  • 1 cup chopped pineapple
  • 1 cucumber
  • 1 cup water
  • 1/2 lime, juiced

Instructions:

  1. Rinse the broccoli florets and cucumber.
  2. Feed the cucumber, pineapple, and broccoli through a juicer in that order.
  3. Combine the juice with water and lime juice in a pitcher.
  4. Stir well, and let the Broccoli Pineapple Power Punch chill in the refrigerator.
  5. Once chilled, pour and enjoy!

7. Broccoli Berry Bananza

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup fresh broccoli florets
  • 1 cup mixed berries (strawberries, blueberries, raspberries)
  • 1 banana
  • 1 cup unsweetened almond milk
  • 1/2 cup spinach

Instructions:

  1. Toss the frozen mixed berries, banana, broccoli florets, and spinach into a blender.
  2. Pour in the unsweetened almond milk and blend until smooth.
  3. Pour your Broccoli Berry Bananza smoothie into a glass and enjoy a refreshing and nutritious meal.

Welcome broccoli to your menu this season and embrace its bountiful health benefits! From smoothies to juices, these recipes are not only incredibly delicious but also prove that incorporating more greens into your diet is easier and more enjoyable than you might have thought. So raise a glass, sip, and toast to a healthier you – one Broccoli Smoothie at a time. Happy sipping, and let us know which one becomes your favorite!
