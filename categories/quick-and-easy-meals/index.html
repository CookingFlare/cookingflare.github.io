10-Minute Mediterranean Couscous Salad Recipe

If you’re looking for a quick and delicious salad that captures the flavors of the Mediterranean, then this 10-Minute Mediterranean Couscous Salad is the perfect recipe for you. Packed with fresh ingredients and bursting with vibrant flavors, this salad is not only easy to make but also incredibly satisfying. Ingredients 1 cup couscous 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved 1 cucumber, diced 1 red bell pepper, diced 1/2 red onion, finely chopped 1/2 cup Kalamata olives, pitted and sliced 1/2 cup feta cheese, crumbled 1/4 cup fresh parsley, chopped 2 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil 2 tablespoons lemon juice Salt and pepper to taste Instructions Cook the couscous according to the package instructions. Fluff it with a fork and let it cool for a few minutes. In a large bowl, combine the cooked couscous, cherry tomatoes, cucumber, red bell pepper, red onion, Kalamata olives, feta cheese, and fresh parsley. In a small bowl, whisk together the extra virgin olive oil and lemon juice. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Pour the dressing over the couscous salad and toss gently to combine, ensuring all the ingredients are well coated. Serve immediately or refrigerate for a few hours to allow the flavors to meld together. Tips and Variations For an extra burst of flavor, you can add some crumbled feta cheese or sliced grilled chicken to the salad. If you prefer a different type of grain, you can easily substitute the couscous with quinoa or bulgur. Feel free to customize the salad to your taste preferences by adding or omitting any ingredients you like. Some popular additions include diced avocado, roasted red peppers, or chopped fresh mint. This salad makes a perfect side dish for grilled meats or a light lunch on its own. It’s also great for meal prep, as it can be made ahead of time and stored in the refrigerator for a few days. Simply combine 1 (14.5 oz) can of artichoke hearts, 1 package of frozen spinach, 1 cup of cream cheese, 1/2 cup of mayonnaise, 1/2 cup of shredded cheddar cheese, and 1/4 cup of chopped fresh parsley in a bowl. Simply thread cherry tomatoes, mozzarella balls, and fresh basil leaves onto toothpicks. Drizzle with olive oil and balsamic glaze, and serve immediately. Recipe 3: Bacon-Wrapped Dates Sweet and savory, these bite-sized treats are sure to impress. Simply wrap dates with bacon slices and bake in the oven at 400°F (200°C) for 10-12 minutes, or until the bacon is crispy. Recipe 4: Mini Quiches These individual quiches are perfect for a crowd and can be made in just 20 minutes. Simply mix 1 cup of shredded cheddar cheese, 1/2 cup of diced ham, and 1/2 cup of diced bell peppers in a bowl. Pour into mini muffin tin cups and bake at 375°F (190°C) for 12-15 minutes, or until the edges are golden brown.
