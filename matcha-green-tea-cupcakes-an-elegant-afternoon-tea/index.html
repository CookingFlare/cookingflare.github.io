Matcha Green Tea Cupcakes: An Elegant Afternoon Tea


Ah, the afternoon tea – a delightful pause between lunch and dinner, an exquisite time to indulge in elegant delights and engage in sophisticated chit-chat. And what better way to elevate this beloved ritual than by serving Matcha Green Tea Cupcakes. These delectable treats are a delightful fusion of tradition and innovation, drawing inspiration from the venerable custom of tea ceremonies in Japanese culture.

The botanical beauties of Matcha Green Tea offer a fantastic way to invigorate both taste buds and senses, with an uplifting, yet calming finish. As you savor every layer of this soft, moist, and delicate cupcake, you will experience a symphony of flavors that transcends the ordinary. So let us embark on a journey together, exploring the secrets behind crafting these exquisite Matcha Green Tea Cupcakes and learn how to make them a centerpiece of your elegant afternoon tea gathering.

Ingredients:

For the Matcha Green Tea Cupcakes:

  • 1 3/4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 1/2 cups granulated sugar
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 teaspoons matcha green tea powder (or to taste)
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/3 cup milk
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

For the Matcha Green Tea Buttercream Frosting:

  • 1 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 4 cups powdered sugar
  • 1/4 cup matcha green tea powder
  • 1/4 cup heavy cream
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C) and line a cupcake tin with 12 liners. This will make it easier to remove the cupcakes once they’re done baking.

For the Cupcakes:

  1. In a large bowl, cream together the softened butter and granulated sugar until light and fluffy. This step helps in creating a moist and tender cupcake.

  2. Add the eggs one at a time, whisking well after each addition to fully incorporate. Ensure each egg is well combined before moving on to the next.

  3. In a separate bowl, sift together the flour, matcha green tea powder, baking powder, baking soda, and salt to eliminate any lumps and ensure an even distribution of the dry ingredients.

  4. Gradually pour the dry ingredients into the wet mixture, alternating with the milk and vanilla extract. Mix until just combined, being careful not to overmix, which could result in a tough cake texture.

  5. Fill each cupcake liner with batter, using a 1/4 cup measure for even distribution. Then, place the pan in the oven and bake for 18-20 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted in the center comes out clean.

  6. Once done, remove the cupcakes from the oven and let them cool in the pan for 5 minutes. Then transfer them to a wire rack to cool completely before frosting.

For the Matcha Green Tea Buttercream Frosting:

  1. In a large bowl, cream together the softened butter and powdered sugar until you achieve a smooth and fluffy consistency.

  2. Add the matcha green tea powder, heavy cream, vanilla extract, and salt. Mix well to ensure even distribution of the matcha powder. Adjust the powdered sugar and cream to reach your desired consistency.

  3. Once the cupcakes have cooled, frost them generously with the Matcha Green Tea Buttercream Frosting. Generously garnish with matcha dusting on top or around the edges of the cupcake for an enhanced green tea flavor experience.

  4. Serve your exquisite Matcha Green Tea Cupcakes on a charming afternoon tea spread, accompanied by delicate sandwiches, freshly baked scones, and a floral tea of your choice. Enjoy the intriguing aroma and delightful taste of these elegant treats with cherished friends and family.

Your Matcha Green Tea Cupcakes are now ready to be savored, uniting the elegance of afternoon tea with the vibrant spirit of Japan. Indulge in these stunning confections and transform your typical afternoon tea experience into an enchanting ritual you will never forget. So, gather your ingredients, and embark on this scrumptious culinary adventure today!
