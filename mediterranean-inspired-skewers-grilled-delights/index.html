Mediterranean-Inspired Skewers: Grilled Delights


When it comes to summer grilling, Mediterranean-inspired skewers are a perfect choice for a flavorful and healthy meal. The combination of fresh vegetables, tender meats, and aromatic herbs creates a delightful culinary experience that will transport your taste buds to the shores of the Mediterranean. In this blog post, we will explore some mouthwatering recipes for Mediterranean-inspired skewers that are sure to impress your family and friends at your next barbecue.

Classic Chicken Souvlaki Skewers

Ingredients:

  • 1.5 pounds of boneless, skinless chicken breasts, cut into 1-inch cubes
  • 1/4 cup extra virgin olive oil
  • 3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons of fresh lemon juice
  • 1 tablespoon of red wine vinegar
  • 1 teaspoon of dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 red onion, cut into chunks
  • 1 red bell pepper, cut into chunks
  • 1 yellow bell pepper, cut into chunks
  • Wooden skewers, soaked in water for 30 minutes

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, whisk together the olive oil, garlic, lemon juice, red wine vinegar, oregano, salt, and pepper. Add the chicken cubes to the marinade and toss to coat. Cover the bowl and refrigerate for at least 1 hour.
  2. Preheat the grill to medium-high heat.
  3. Thread the marinated chicken, red onion, and bell peppers onto the soaked wooden skewers, alternating the ingredients.
  4. Grill the skewers for 10-12 minutes, turning occasionally, until the chicken is cooked through and the vegetables are charred and tender.
  5. Serve the chicken souvlaki skewers with a side of tzatziki sauce and warm pita bread.

Grilled Halloumi and Vegetable Skewers

Ingredients:

  • 8 ounces of halloumi cheese, cut into 1-inch cubes
  • 1 zucchini, sliced into rounds
  • 1 yellow squash, sliced into rounds
  • 1 pint of cherry tomatoes
  • 1/4 cup of extra virgin olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons of fresh lemon juice
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon of dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Wooden skewers, soaked in water for 30 minutes

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, whisk together the olive oil, lemon juice, garlic, oregano, salt, and pepper.
  2. Thread the halloumi cheese, zucchini, yellow squash, and cherry tomatoes onto the soaked wooden skewers, alternating the ingredients.
  3. Brush the skewers with the olive oil mixture.
  4. Preheat the grill to medium heat.
  5. Grill the skewers for 5-7 minutes, turning once, until the halloumi is golden and the vegetables are tender.
  6. Serve the grilled halloumi and vegetable skewers with a drizzle of balsamic glaze and a sprinkle of fresh basil.

Mediterranean Shrimp Skewers

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound of large shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 1/4 cup of extra virgin olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons of fresh lemon juice
  • 3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon of dried oregano
  • 1/2 teaspoon of smoked paprika
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 lemon, sliced
  • Wooden skewers, soaked in water for 30 minutes

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, whisk together the olive oil, lemon juice, garlic, oregano, smoked paprika, salt, and pepper.
  2. Add the shrimp to the marinade and toss to coat. Cover the bowl and refrigerate for 30 minutes.
  3. Preheat the grill to medium-high heat.
  4. Thread the marinated shrimp and lemon slices onto the soaked wooden skewers.
  5. Grill the skewers for 2-3 minutes per side, until the shrimp are pink and opaque.
  6. Serve the Mediterranean shrimp skewers with a squeeze of fresh lemon juice and a sprinkle of chopped parsley.

Conclusion

Mediterranean-inspired skewers are a simple yet impressive addition to any summer gathering. With their vibrant colors, bold flavors, and easy preparation, these grilled delights are sure to become a staple on your outdoor menu. Whether you choose classic chicken souvlaki, flavorful halloumi and vegetable skewers, or succulent Mediterranean shrimp, these recipes are guaranteed to elevate your grilling game and satisfy your cravings for Mediterranean cuisine. So fire up the grill, gather your favorite ingredients, and savor the taste of the Mediterranean with these delectable skewers. Cheers to good food, good company, and the joy of cooking outdoors!
