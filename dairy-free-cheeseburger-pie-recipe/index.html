Dairy-Free Cheeseburger Pie Recipe


If you’re craving a comforting, hearty meal that’s both flavorful and satisfying, this Dairy-Free Cheeseburger Pie is the perfect solution. This delicious dish brings together all the classic flavors of a cheeseburger, minus the dairy. It’s an ideal option for those who are lactose intolerant, vegan, or simply looking to try something new.

In this recipe, you’ll find a savory filling made with seasoned ground beef, sautéed onions, and a rich, dairy-free cheese substitute that mimics the creaminess and flavor of traditional cheeseburger toppings. The crust is buttery, flaky, and perfectly complements the savory filling, while a few extra ingredients like mustard, ketchup, and pickles help capture that authentic cheeseburger taste.

Whether you’re making it for a family dinner, a meal prep week, or serving it at a gathering, this Dairy-Free Cheeseburger Pie will surely become a new favorite in your recipe collection. Let’s dive into the step-by-step process and tips for making this easy yet mouthwatering dish.

Ingredients

Before we begin, let’s gather the ingredients. This recipe is easy to prepare with ingredients that are commonly found in most kitchens or specialty stores that carry dairy-free products.

For the crust:

  • 1 ½ cups of all-purpose flour
  • ½ teaspoon of salt
  • ¼ cup of dairy-free butter (or vegan butter)
  • ¼ cup of water (cold)
  • 1 tablespoon of olive oil (optional, for added crispiness)

For the filling:

  • 1 lb ground beef (you can substitute with ground turkey, chicken, or a plant-based meat alternative)
  • 1 medium onion, diced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons tomato paste
  • 1 tablespoon Dijon mustard
  • 2 tablespoons ketchup
  • ½ cup dairy-free cheese (shredded, such as cashew cheese or almond-based cheese)
  • ¼ cup pickles, chopped (optional but adds a great flavor)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • ½ teaspoon smoked paprika (optional for a smoky flavor)
  • ½ teaspoon onion powder
  • ½ teaspoon garlic powder

For the topping:

  • 2 tablespoons dairy-free cheese, shredded (optional)
  • Fresh parsley for garnish (optional)

Now that we have our ingredients ready, let’s get started on preparing this mouthwatering Dairy-Free Cheeseburger Pie!

How to Make Dairy-Free Cheeseburger Pie

Step 1: Prepare the Pie Crust

The first step in making a delicious Dairy-Free Cheeseburger Pie is preparing the crust. To do this, start by combining the flour and salt in a large mixing bowl. Cut in the dairy-free butter using a pastry cutter or your fingers until the mixture resembles coarse crumbs. The butter should be cold to ensure the crust stays flaky.

Next, add in the cold water, one tablespoon at a time, and mix it into the dough until it just comes together. If the dough feels too dry, you can add a little more water, but be cautious not to make it too wet. Once the dough forms into a ball, cover it with plastic wrap and refrigerate it for about 30 minutes. This will make it easier to roll out and prevent it from shrinking while baking.

Step 2: Cook the Beef and Onions

While the dough is chilling, it’s time to prepare the filling. In a large skillet, heat a bit of oil over medium heat and add the diced onions. Sauté the onions until they become soft and translucent, about 5-7 minutes. Add the minced garlic and cook for an additional minute until fragrant.

Add the ground beef (or plant-based alternative) to the skillet and cook it until browned, breaking it up with a spoon as it cooks. Once the meat is fully cooked, drain any excess fat, if needed, and add the tomato paste, Dijon mustard, and ketchup. Stir everything together until well combined.

Season the mixture with salt, pepper, smoked paprika, onion powder, and garlic powder. Adjust the seasoning to your taste, and let it simmer for 5-10 minutes to allow the flavors to meld. Once done, remove the pan from heat and stir in the dairy-free cheese. The cheese will melt into the mixture, creating a creamy and cheesy filling.

For an added touch of authenticity, you can mix in some chopped pickles into the filling, which will give your cheeseburger pie that iconic tangy flavor you find in a regular cheeseburger.

Step 3: Roll Out the Pie Dough

While the filling is cooling, roll out the chilled pie dough on a lightly floured surface. You’ll want to roll it out to fit your pie dish or baking pan. Gently transfer the dough to the pie dish, ensuring it covers the bottom and sides completely. Trim any excess dough around the edges, and use a fork to lightly prick the bottom of the dough. This will help prevent the crust from puffing up as it bakes.

Step 4: Assemble the Cheeseburger Pie

Now that your crust and filling are ready, it’s time to assemble the pie. Spoon the cheeseburger filling into the prepared pie crust, spreading it out evenly. If you’d like a cheesier topping, sprinkle a little more dairy-free cheese on top of the filling before baking.

Step 5: Bake the Pie

Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C). Place the pie in the oven and bake for 30-35 minutes or until the crust is golden brown and the filling is bubbling. If you find that the crust is browning too quickly, you can cover the edges with aluminum foil to prevent burning.

Once the pie is done baking, remove it from the oven and let it cool for about 5-10 minutes before serving. This allows the filling to set slightly and makes it easier to slice.

Step 6: Serve and Enjoy

Once the pie has cooled, slice it into wedges and serve! You can garnish with fresh parsley or add a few extra pickle slices on top for that authentic cheeseburger feel. For a complete meal, serve it with a side salad, crispy fries, or steamed veggies.

Tips for a Perfect Dairy-Free Cheeseburger Pie

  1. Customize Your Filling: Feel free to get creative with the filling! You can add vegetables like bell peppers, mushrooms, or spinach for extra flavor and nutrients. If you prefer a spicier kick, add a bit of hot sauce or chili flakes to the filling.

  2. Use a Pre-Made Dairy-Free Pie Crust: If you’re short on time or don’t want to make your own crust, you can easily find pre-made dairy-free pie crusts at most grocery stores. Just be sure to check the ingredients to ensure it meets your dietary preferences.

  3. Make it Vegetarian: If you want to make this recipe vegetarian or vegan, simply swap out the ground beef with plant-based ground meat alternatives such as lentils, tempeh, or a veggie burger mix.

  4. Use a Vegan Cheese: There are many excellent dairy-free cheeses available in stores. Cashew cheese, almond-based cheese, or soy-based cheese can all work well in this recipe. Make sure to pick a variety that melts well to achieve that gooey, cheesy texture.

  5. Make it Ahead of Time: You can easily prepare this pie ahead of time! Assemble the pie, cover it with plastic wrap, and refrigerate it for up to 24 hours before baking. Alternatively, you can bake it and store it in the fridge for a quick meal throughout the week.

Why You’ll Love This Dairy-Free Cheeseburger Pie

1. Comforting and Hearty

This Dairy-Free Cheeseburger Pie is a hearty, satisfying meal that hits all the right notes. The savory filling, cheesy goodness, and crispy crust make it an ideal comfort food dish. It’s a great way to enjoy the flavors of a cheeseburger without the dairy!

2. Great for Meal Prep

This dish is perfect for meal prep. The leftovers taste just as delicious, and it can easily be reheated for a quick lunch or dinner. You can also freeze the pie for up to three months, making it a great option for busy weeks.

3. Perfect for Special Diets

Whether you’re avoiding dairy for health reasons, following a vegan diet, or dealing with lactose intolerance, this Dairy-Free Cheeseburger Pie is a perfect alternative that everyone can enjoy. It’s a great way to make everyone at the table feel included, regardless of dietary preferences.

4. Endless Variations

The best part about this recipe is that it’s highly customizable. You can swap out ingredients and adjust the seasonings to suit your taste. Whether you like it spicy, cheesy, or loaded with veggies, this pie can be adapted to fit your personal preferences.

Conclusion

It’s easy to prepare, customizable, and packed with all the delicious flavors of a traditional cheeseburger, without the dairy. Perfect for family dinners, meal prepping, or even a casual gathering, it’s sure to be a hit.

Give it a try, and you may just find yourself making it over and over again for its unbeatable taste and versatility. The warm, savory filling, flaky crust, and creamy dairy-free cheese come together to create a dish that everyone can enjoy.
