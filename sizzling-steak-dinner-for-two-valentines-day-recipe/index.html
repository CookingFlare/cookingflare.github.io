Sizzling Steak Dinner for Two: Valentine's Day Recipe


Valentine’s Day is just around the corner, and what better way to celebrate than by cooking up a sizzling steak dinner for your loved one? This recipe is perfect for a romantic evening at home, and it’s sure to impress your special someone. So, let’s get started!

Ingredients

For the steak:

  • 2 ribeye steaks (approximately 1 inch thick)
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • 2 tablespoons of olive oil

For the garlic butter:

  • 4 tablespoons of unsalted butter, softened
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon of fresh parsley, chopped

For the roasted potatoes:

  • 4 medium-sized potatoes, washed and cut into wedges
  • 2 tablespoons of olive oil
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • 1 teaspoon of dried rosemary

For the sautéed asparagus:

  • 1 bunch of asparagus, trimmed
  • 2 tablespoons of olive oil
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • Juice of half a lemon

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C). Place the potato wedges on a baking sheet and drizzle with olive oil. Sprinkle with salt, pepper, and dried rosemary. Toss until the potatoes are evenly coated. Roast in the oven for 30-35 minutes, or until golden brown and crispy.

  2. While the potatoes are roasting, prepare the garlic butter. In a small bowl, combine the softened butter, minced garlic, and chopped parsley. Mix well until all the ingredients are evenly incorporated. Set aside.

  3. Heat a cast-iron skillet over high heat. Season the ribeye steaks generously with salt and pepper on both sides. Drizzle olive oil over the steaks and rub it in to coat them evenly. Place the steaks in the hot skillet and sear for 3-4 minutes on each side, or until a golden crust forms. Reduce the heat to medium and continue cooking for an additional 4-6 minutes for medium-rare, or until cooked to your desired doneness. Transfer the steaks to a cutting board and let them rest for a few minutes.

  4. While the steaks are resting, prepare the sautéed asparagus. Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Add the asparagus spears and season with salt and pepper. Sauté for 4-5 minutes, or until the asparagus is tender-crisp. Squeeze fresh lemon juice over the asparagus and toss to coat.

  5. Slice the steaks against the grain into thick slices. Serve the sizzling steak slices on a warm plate, topped with a generous dollop of garlic butter. Arrange the roasted potatoes and sautéed asparagus alongside the steak.

  6. Pour a glass of your favorite red wine, dim the lights, and enjoy this delicious and romantic sizzling steak dinner with your loved one.

Conclusion

There you have it – a mouthwatering sizzling steak dinner for two, perfect for a romantic Valentine’s Day celebration. The tender ribeye steaks, paired with the flavorful garlic butter, roasted potatoes, and sautéed asparagus, will create a truly memorable dining experience. So, put on your chef’s hat, and show your special someone just how much you care with this indulgent and delicious meal. Happy Valentine’s Day!
