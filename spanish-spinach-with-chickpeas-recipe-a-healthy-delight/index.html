Spanish Spinach with Chickpeas Recipe: A Healthy Delight


If you’re looking for a delicious and nutritious dish that’s bursting with flavor, then look no further than Spanish Spinach with Chickpeas. This traditional Spanish recipe is not only easy to make but also packed with healthy ingredients that will leave you feeling satisfied and nourished. Whether you’re a fan of Spanish cuisine or simply looking to add more plant-based meals to your diet, this recipe is sure to become a staple in your kitchen.

Ingredients

To start off with this flavorful dish, you’ll need the following ingredients:

  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon smoked paprika
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • 1/2 teaspoon red pepper flakes
  • 1 can (15 oz) chickpeas, drained and rinsed
  • 1 can (14 oz) diced tomatoes
  • 1/2 cup vegetable broth
  • 1 lb fresh spinach, washed and trimmed
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Lemon wedges for serving

Instructions

  1. Begin by heating the olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Add the chopped onion and sauté until it becomes soft and translucent.

  2. Once the onion is cooked, add the minced garlic, smoked paprika, cumin, and red pepper flakes to the skillet. Stir the ingredients together and allow them to cook for a minute or two until the spices become fragrant.

  3. Next, add the drained and rinsed chickpeas to the skillet, stirring them to coat in the aromatic mixture.

  4. After the chickpeas have been coated with the spice mixture, add the diced tomatoes and vegetable broth to the skillet. Allow the mixture to come to a gentle simmer.

  5. Once the mixture is simmering, add the fresh spinach to the skillet in batches, allowing each batch to wilt before adding more. Stir the spinach into the mixture until it’s completely wilted and combined with the other ingredients.

  6. Season the Spanish Spinach with Chickpeas with salt and pepper to taste. Stir the dish well to ensure the flavors are evenly distributed.

  7. Serve the Spanish Spinach with Chickpeas hot, accompanied by lemon wedges for a fresh burst of citrus flavor.

Health Benefits

Not only is this Spanish Spinach with Chickpeas recipe a delight for the taste buds, but it’s also incredibly healthy. Spinach is a nutrient powerhouse, packed with vitamins A, C, and K, as well as iron, magnesium, and antioxidants. Chickpeas, on the other hand, are a great source of plant-based protein and fiber, making this dish a satisfying option for vegetarians and vegans alike. The combination of these two superfoods in this recipe makes for a meal that’s not only delicious but also nourishing.

Conclusion

In conclusion, Spanish Spinach with Chickpeas is a dish that perfectly encapsulates the vibrant flavors of Spanish cuisine while providing a healthy and fulfilling meal option. Whether you’re preparing it for a family dinner or a solo weeknight meal, this recipe is versatile, easy to make, and sure to leave you feeling satisfied. So, next time you’re in the mood for a healthy and flavorful dish, give this Spanish Spinach with Chickpeas recipe a try, and savor every delightful bite.
