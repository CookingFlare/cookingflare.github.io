Craving Pizza? Try This Low-Carb Keto Pizza Casserole!


Pizza is undeniably one of the most popular comfort foods around the world. However, if you’re following a low-carb or keto lifestyle, indulging in a classic pizza may not align with your dietary goals. But fear not! We have the perfect solution for your pizza cravings - a delicious and satisfying Low-Carb Keto Pizza Casserole. This recipe combines all the flavors you love about pizza without the guilt. So, let’s dive right in!

Ingredients

  • 1 medium-sized cauliflower head, grated
  • 1 pound ground beef or turkey
  • 1 cup sugar-free pizza sauce
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/4 cup sliced black olives
  • 1/4 cup sliced pepperoni
  • 1 tablespoon dried oregano
  • 1 tablespoon dried basil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C). Grease a baking dish with cooking spray or a little olive oil to prevent sticking.

  2. In a large skillet, heat some olive oil over medium heat. Add the ground beef or turkey and cook until browned. Season with salt, pepper, dried oregano, and dried basil. Stir well to combine the flavors.

  3. Once the meat is cooked, remove it from the heat and set it aside.

  4. In a separate large bowl, combine the grated cauliflower and pizza sauce. Mix well until the cauliflower is evenly coated with the sauce.

  5. Transfer half of the cauliflower mixture to the greased baking dish. Spread it out evenly to form the base layer.

  6. Next, add a layer of the cooked ground beef or turkey on top of the cauliflower mixture. Spread it out evenly to cover the entire base layer.

  7. Sprinkle half of the shredded mozzarella cheese over the meat layer. This will help bind the casserole together as it melts.

  8. Now, add the remaining cauliflower mixture on top of the cheese layer. Spread it out evenly to form the second layer.

  9. Finally, sprinkle the remaining shredded mozzarella cheese over the cauliflower layer. Top it off with the sliced black olives and pepperoni.

  10. Place the casserole in the preheated oven and bake for 25-30 minutes or until the cheese is melted and bubbly.

  11. Once cooked, remove the casserole from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes before serving.

  12. Serve your Low-Carb Keto Pizza Casserole hot and enjoy the flavors of pizza without the guilt!

Tips and Variations

  • Feel free to add your favorite pizza toppings to the casserole, such as bell peppers, onions, mushrooms, or even some jalapeños for an extra kick.

  • If you prefer a vegetarian option, you can omit the ground beef or turkey and replace it with your choice of plant-based protein or extra veggies.

  • To make the casserole more crispy, broil it for a couple of minutes at the end of the cooking time. Keep a close eye on it to prevent burning.

  • Leftovers can be stored in an airtight container in the refrigerator for up to 3 days. Simply reheat in the oven or microwave when ready to enjoy.

Final Thoughts

Craving pizza but don’t want to derail your low-carb or keto lifestyle? This Low-Carb Keto Pizza Casserole is the answer to your prayers. Packed with all the delicious flavors of pizza, this recipe is a satisfying and guilt-free alternative. So, give it a try and indulge in a slice of heaven without compromising your dietary goals. Enjoy!
