Delicious Blueberry Crumble Bars: Sweet and Tart


If you’re a fan of the delightful combination of sweet and tart flavors, then you’re in for a treat with these delicious blueberry crumble bars. Bursting with juicy blueberries and topped with a buttery crumble, these bars are the perfect balance of fruity sweetness and crispy texture. Whether you’re hosting a summer brunch or simply craving a delightful dessert, these blueberry crumble bars are sure to impress your taste buds and leave you wanting more.

Ingredients

For the Crust and Crumble:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • Zest of 1 lemon
  • 1 cup unsalted butter, cold and cubed
  • 1 egg
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract

For the Blueberry Filling:

  • 4 cups fresh blueberries
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/4 cup cornstarch
  • Juice of 1 lemon

Instructions

Making the Crust and Crumble:

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C) and line a 9x13-inch baking pan with parchment paper, leaving an overhang on the sides for easy removal.
  2. In a large mixing bowl, combine the flour, sugar, baking powder, salt, and lemon zest.
  3. Add the cold, cubed butter to the dry ingredients and use a pastry cutter or your fingers to work the butter into the mixture until it resembles coarse crumbs.
  4. In a small bowl, whisk together the egg and vanilla extract. Pour the egg mixture into the flour and butter mixture, and stir until a dough forms.
  5. Set aside 1 1/2 cups of the dough for the crumble topping, and press the remaining dough into the bottom of the prepared baking pan, creating an even layer.

Making the Blueberry Filling:

  1. In a separate bowl, gently toss the blueberries with sugar, cornstarch, and lemon juice until the blueberries are evenly coated.
  2. Spread the blueberry filling over the crust in the baking pan.
  3. Crumble the reserved dough over the blueberry filling, creating an even layer of crumble topping.

Baking and Serving:

  1. Bake the blueberry crumble bars in the preheated oven for 45-50 minutes, or until the top is golden brown and the blueberry filling is bubbling.
  2. Allow the bars to cool completely in the pan before using the parchment paper overhang to lift them out onto a cutting board.
  3. Cut into squares or bars of your desired size and serve.

Enjoying the Blueberry Crumble Bars

These delightful blueberry crumble bars are best enjoyed with a dollop of whipped cream or a scoop of vanilla ice cream. The sweet and tart flavors of the blueberry filling, combined with the buttery crumble, create a mouthwatering experience that is perfect for any occasion. Whether you’re indulging in a leisurely afternoon treat or impressing guests at a gathering, these blueberry crumble bars are a delightful addition to your baking repertoire.

So, the next time you find yourself with an abundance of fresh blueberries, give this recipe a try and savor the delightful combination of sweet and tart flavors in every bite. You won’t be disappointed!
