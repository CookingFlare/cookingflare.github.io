BBQ Pulled Pork: Slow-Cooked Recipes for Tender Goodness


Welcome to our ultimate guide to BBQ pulled pork! In this post, we will share with you some mouthwatering slow-cooked recipes that will leave you craving for more. Get ready to experience the tender goodness of BBQ pulled pork, as we take you through a culinary journey like no other.

Introduction

BBQ pulled pork is a classic dish that never fails to impress. The slow-cooking process results in tender, juicy meat that practically melts in your mouth. Whether you’re hosting a backyard BBQ party or simply craving a hearty meal, these recipes are sure to satisfy your taste buds.

Recipe 1: Classic BBQ Pulled Pork

Ingredients:

  • 4 pounds pork shoulder
  • 2 tablespoons brown sugar
  • 2 tablespoons paprika
  • 1 tablespoon salt
  • 1 tablespoon black pepper
  • 1 tablespoon garlic powder
  • 1 tablespoon onion powder
  • 1 teaspoon cayenne pepper
  • 1 cup BBQ sauce

Instructions:

  1. In a small bowl, combine the brown sugar, paprika, salt, black pepper, garlic powder, onion powder, and cayenne pepper to make a dry rub.
  2. Rub the dry rub all over the pork shoulder, ensuring it is evenly coated.
  3. Place the pork shoulder in a slow cooker and cook on low heat for 8-10 hours, or until the meat is tender and easily shreds with a fork.
  4. Once the pork is cooked, remove it from the slow cooker and shred it using two forks.
  5. Return the shredded pork to the slow cooker and mix in the BBQ sauce.
  6. Cook for an additional 30 minutes to allow the flavors to meld together.
  7. Serve the BBQ pulled pork on buns or alongside your favorite sides.

Recipe 2: Sweet and Tangy BBQ Pulled Pork

Ingredients:

  • 4 pounds pork shoulder
  • 1 cup ketchup
  • 1/2 cup brown sugar
  • 1/4 cup apple cider vinegar
  • 2 tablespoons Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 tablespoon Dijon mustard
  • 1 tablespoon smoked paprika
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon black pepper

Instructions:

  1. In a medium bowl, whisk together the ketchup, brown sugar, apple cider vinegar, Worcestershire sauce, Dijon mustard, smoked paprika, salt, and black pepper.
  2. Place the pork shoulder in a slow cooker and pour the sauce over it, ensuring the meat is well-coated.
  3. Cook on low heat for 8-10 hours, or until the pork is tender and easily shreds with a fork.
  4. Once cooked, remove the pork from the slow cooker and shred it using two forks.
  5. Return the shredded pork to the slow cooker and mix it with the remaining sauce.
  6. Cook for an additional 30 minutes to allow the flavors to meld together.
  7. Serve the sweet and tangy BBQ pulled pork on buns or as a delicious topping for loaded fries.

Recipe 3: Spicy Chipotle BBQ Pulled Pork

Ingredients:

  • 4 pounds pork shoulder
  • 2 chipotle peppers in adobo sauce, minced
  • 1 cup BBQ sauce
  • 1/4 cup apple cider vinegar
  • 2 tablespoons brown sugar
  • 1 tablespoon smoked paprika
  • 1 tablespoon cumin
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon black pepper

Instructions:

  1. In a small bowl, mix together the minced chipotle peppers, BBQ sauce, apple cider vinegar, brown sugar, smoked paprika, cumin, salt, and black pepper.
  2. Place the pork shoulder in a slow cooker and pour the chipotle BBQ sauce over it, ensuring the meat is coated evenly.
  3. Cook on low heat for 8-10 hours, or until the pork is tender and easily shreds with a fork.
  4. Once cooked, remove the pork from the slow cooker and shred it using two forks.
  5. Return the shredded pork to the slow cooker and mix it with the remaining chipotle BBQ sauce.
  6. Cook for an additional 30 minutes to allow the flavors to meld together.
  7. Serve the spicy chipotle BBQ pulled pork on buns, tacos, or as a filling for quesadillas.

Conclusion

There you have it - three delicious BBQ pulled pork recipes that are guaranteed to satisfy your cravings. Whether you prefer a classic flavor, a sweet and tangy twist, or a spicy kick, these slow-cooked recipes will leave you wanting more. Fire up your slow cooker, gather your ingredients, and get ready to indulge in the tender goodness of BBQ pulled pork. Happy cooking!

Note: Remember to adjust the cooking time and temperature according to your slow cooker’s instructions and the size of your pork shoulder. Enjoy responsibly and share the joy of these mouthwatering recipes with your friends and family!
