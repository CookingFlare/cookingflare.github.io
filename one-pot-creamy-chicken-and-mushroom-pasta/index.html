One Pot Creamy Chicken and Mushroom Pasta


If you’re looking for a delicious and easy one-pot meal, look no further than this creamy chicken and mushroom pasta recipe. This dish is a perfect combination of tender chicken, flavorful mushrooms, and creamy pasta, all cooked together in one pot for easy preparation and minimal cleanup. Whether you’re cooking for your family or hosting a dinner party, this recipe is sure to impress.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound boneless, skinless chicken breasts, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 8 ounces mushrooms, sliced
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • 3 cups chicken broth
  • 2 cups heavy cream
  • 12 ounces fettuccine or any pasta of your choice
  • Fresh parsley, chopped (for garnish)

Instructions

  1. Heat the olive oil in a large pot or Dutch oven over medium-high heat. Add the chicken pieces and cook until browned on all sides. Remove the chicken from the pot and set aside.

  2. In the same pot, add the chopped onion and sauté until softened. Add the minced garlic and cook for another minute. Then, add the sliced mushrooms and cook until they are golden brown and have released their juices.

  3. Sprinkle the thyme, oregano, salt, and black pepper over the mushroom mixture. Stir well to combine the flavors.

  4. Pour in the chicken broth and heavy cream, and bring the mixture to a simmer. Add the pasta to the pot, making sure it is submerged in the liquid as much as possible. Cook, stirring occasionally, until the pasta is al dente and the sauce has thickened, about 15-20 minutes.

  5. Return the browned chicken to the pot and stir it into the creamy pasta mixture. Cook for an additional 5-10 minutes, or until the chicken is fully cooked through.

  6. Once the chicken is cooked and the pasta is tender, remove the pot from the heat. Let the pasta rest for a few minutes to allow the sauce to thicken further.

  7. Serve the creamy chicken and mushroom pasta in bowls, garnished with freshly chopped parsley.

Tips for Success

  • Use heavy cream for the creamiest sauce, but you can also substitute with half-and-half for a lighter version.
  • If you prefer a thicker sauce, you can add grated Parmesan cheese at the end and stir until melted.
  • Feel free to customize the recipe by adding spinach, sun-dried tomatoes, or other vegetables of your choice.

Final Thoughts

This one-pot creamy chicken and mushroom pasta is a complete meal in one dish, making it perfect for busy weeknights or lazy weekends. The combination of tender chicken, earthy mushrooms, and rich, creamy sauce is sure to satisfy even the pickiest of eaters. Plus, with minimal cleanup required, you’ll have more time to enjoy your delicious creation and less time spent washing dishes. So, the next time you’re craving comfort food with a touch of elegance, give this recipe a try and let its simplicity and flavor speak for themselves. Enjoy!
