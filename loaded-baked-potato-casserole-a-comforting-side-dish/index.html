Loaded Baked Potato Casserole: A Comforting Side Dish


If you’re a fan of comforting, hearty side dishes, then you’re in for a treat with this loaded baked potato casserole recipe. This dish takes all the delicious flavors of a classic loaded baked potato and transforms them into a crowd-pleasing casserole that’s perfect for family dinners, potlucks, and gatherings with friends. With layers of creamy mashed potatoes, crispy bacon, gooey cheese, and zesty green onions, this casserole is sure to become a new favorite in your recipe collection.

Ingredients

Before we dive into the cooking process, let’s gather the essential ingredients for this delectable dish:

  • 6 large russet potatoes, peeled and cubed
  • 1/2 cup sour cream
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1/2 cup chopped green onions
  • 1/2 cup crispy bacon, crumbled
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

Step 1: Preparing the Potatoes

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C) and grease a 9x13 inch baking dish.
  2. Place the peeled and cubed potatoes in a large pot of salted water and bring to a boil. Cook for about 15 minutes, or until the potatoes are fork-tender.
  3. Once the potatoes are cooked, drain them and return them to the pot.

Step 2: Mashing the Potatoes

  1. Add the softened butter, sour cream, garlic powder, salt, and pepper to the pot with the cooked potatoes.
  2. Mash the potatoes and mix until smooth and creamy. You can use a potato masher or a hand mixer for this step.

Step 3: Assembling the Casserole

  1. Spread half of the mashed potatoes into the prepared baking dish, creating an even layer.
  2. Sprinkle half of the shredded cheddar cheese, chopped green onions, and crispy bacon over the layer of mashed potatoes.
  3. Repeat with the remaining mashed potatoes and top with the remaining cheese, green onions, and bacon.

Step 4: Baking the Casserole

  1. Place the assembled casserole in the preheated oven and bake for 25-30 minutes, or until the cheese is melted and bubbly, and the edges are golden brown.
  2. Once cooked, remove the casserole from the oven and let it rest for a few minutes before serving.

Serving Suggestions

This loaded baked potato casserole pairs perfectly with a variety of main dishes. Whether you’re serving it alongside grilled chicken, steak, or as part of a holiday feast, its rich and savory flavors are sure to complement your meal. You can also customize the toppings to suit your preferences - try adding sliced jalapeños for a spicy kick or diced tomatoes for a refreshing twist.

Conclusion

With its creamy, cheesy, and indulgent layers, this loaded baked potato casserole is a guaranteed crowd-pleaser. Whether you’re looking for a comforting side dish to accompany your favorite entrees or a dish to bring to a potluck, this recipe is a winner. It’s easy to prepare, versatile, and absolutely delicious. Give it a try and watch it become a beloved addition to your culinary repertoire!
