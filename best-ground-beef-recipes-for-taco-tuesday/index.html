Best Ground Beef Recipes for Taco Tuesday


Taco Tuesday is one of the most anticipated meals of the week, and it’s easy to see why. The vibrant flavors, the customizable toppings, and the comforting combination of textures make tacos a family favorite. Among the many ways to prepare tacos, ground beef is a classic choice that never disappoints. Whether you’re looking for a quick and easy dinner or a recipe that will impress your guests, ground beef tacos can be versatile and full of flavor.

In this post, we’re diving into the best ground beef recipes for Taco Tuesday. From traditional taco fillings to creative twists, there’s something for everyone. Let’s explore how you can take your Taco Tuesday game to the next level with these mouthwatering recipes.

Classic Ground Beef Tacos

When it comes to tacos, sometimes you just can’t beat the classics. Ground beef, seasoned perfectly, wrapped in a crispy or soft taco shell, and topped with your favorite ingredients—it’s a meal that satisfies every time.

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 packet taco seasoning (or homemade seasoning)
  • 1/2 cup water
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Taco shells (soft or hard)
  • Shredded lettuce
  • Chopped tomatoes
  • Shredded cheese
  • Sour cream
  • Salsa

Instructions:

  1. In a large skillet, heat a bit of oil over medium heat and add the ground beef. Cook, breaking it apart with a spatula, until browned.
  2. Add the chopped onion and garlic. Cook for another 2-3 minutes until the onion is soft and translucent.
  3. Stir in the taco seasoning and water, and simmer for about 5 minutes until the beef is fully coated in the seasoning and the sauce has thickened.
  4. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  5. Heat your taco shells in the oven according to package instructions or lightly toast them in a dry skillet.
  6. To assemble, spoon the seasoned ground beef into each shell and top with your favorite toppings such as lettuce, tomatoes, cheese, sour cream, and salsa.
  7. Serve and enjoy!

Ground Beef and Sweet Potato Tacos

Looking for a healthy twist on Taco Tuesday? Ground beef and sweet potatoes make a fantastic pairing, creating a dish that is both hearty and nutritious. The slight sweetness of the potatoes complements the savory flavor of the beef, while the texture adds a nice contrast.

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 2 medium sweet potatoes, peeled and diced
  • 1 small onion, diced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tsp chili powder
  • 1/2 tsp cumin
  • 1/4 tsp paprika
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Taco shells (soft or hard)
  • Avocado slices
  • Fresh cilantro
  • Lime wedges

Instructions:

  1. Start by cooking the sweet potatoes. Heat a drizzle of olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Add the diced sweet potatoes and cook, stirring occasionally, for 10-12 minutes or until they are tender and slightly crispy on the outside. Remove them from the skillet and set aside.
  2. In the same skillet, add the ground beef and cook until browned. Drain any excess fat.
  3. Add the onion and garlic, cooking for an additional 3-4 minutes until softened.
  4. Sprinkle in the chili powder, cumin, paprika, salt, and pepper. Stir to combine, and add the cooked sweet potatoes back into the skillet.
  5. Stir everything together and cook for another 2-3 minutes to ensure the flavors meld.
  6. Warm your taco shells and then fill them with the ground beef and sweet potato mixture.
  7. Top with fresh avocado slices, cilantro, and a squeeze of lime juice.
  8. Serve and savor!

Ground Beef and Black Bean Tacos

If you love adding extra texture and protein to your tacos, then ground beef and black beans are a match made in taco heaven. This recipe is hearty, satisfying, and packs a nutritional punch.

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1 can (15 oz) black beans, drained and rinsed
  • 1 small onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tsp ground cumin
  • 1 tsp chili powder
  • 1/2 tsp smoked paprika
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Taco shells (soft or hard)
  • Shredded lettuce
  • Diced tomatoes
  • Shredded cheddar cheese
  • Sliced jalapeños (optional)
  • Sour cream

Instructions:

  1. Brown the ground beef in a skillet over medium heat, breaking it up as it cooks. Once browned, drain any excess fat.
  2. Add the chopped onion and minced garlic to the skillet and cook for about 3 minutes until the onion softens.
  3. Sprinkle in the cumin, chili powder, smoked paprika, salt, and pepper. Stir to coat the beef and onion mixture in the spices.
  4. Add the black beans to the skillet and cook for another 3-4 minutes until heated through.
  5. Warm the taco shells in the oven or on a skillet, and then fill each shell with the ground beef and black bean mixture.
  6. Top with shredded lettuce, diced tomatoes, cheese, jalapeños, and sour cream.
  7. Serve with a side of salsa and enjoy!

Ground Beef and Avocado Tacos with Cilantro Lime Slaw

For a refreshing and vibrant take on Taco Tuesday, this recipe adds the creamy richness of avocado and the crunch of a tangy cilantro lime slaw. The combination of the creamy, zesty slaw with the savory beef filling is perfect for those looking for a burst of fresh flavors.

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1 small onion, diced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 packet taco seasoning
  • 1/2 cup water
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Taco shells (soft or hard)
  • 2 ripe avocados, sliced
  • 1 cup shredded cabbage
  • 1/4 cup fresh cilantro, chopped
  • 2 tbsp mayonnaise
  • 1 tbsp lime juice
  • 1/2 tsp chili powder (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Brown the ground beef in a skillet over medium heat. Add the diced onion and minced garlic, cooking for 3-4 minutes until the onion softens.
  2. Stir in the taco seasoning and water. Let it simmer for 5 minutes, then season with salt and pepper to taste.
  3. In a separate bowl, combine the shredded cabbage, cilantro, mayonnaise, lime juice, and chili powder. Toss to coat the cabbage mixture evenly.
  4. Warm your taco shells and fill them with the seasoned ground beef.
  5. Top with sliced avocado and a generous portion of the cilantro lime slaw.
  6. Serve and enjoy the perfect balance of flavors and textures.

Ground Beef Taco Salad

For a lighter, yet equally satisfying, Taco Tuesday option, why not skip the shells and turn your tacos into a salad? This Ground Beef Taco Salad is a fresh and filling dish with all the flavors you love about tacos—without the carbs.

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1 packet taco seasoning
  • 1/2 cup water
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 4 cups mixed greens (romaine, spinach, or iceberg)
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1/2 cup shredded cheese
  • 1/4 cup sliced black olives
  • 1/4 cup red onion, thinly sliced
  • 1/4 cup sour cream
  • 1/4 cup salsa
  • Tortilla chips (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Brown the ground beef in a skillet over medium heat, breaking it apart as it cooks. Add the taco seasoning and water, stirring to combine. Let it simmer for 5-7 minutes until the beef is fully coated and the sauce thickens.
  2. In a large bowl, toss the mixed greens, cherry tomatoes, cheese, black olives, and red onion.
  3. Top the salad with the seasoned ground beef mixture.
  4. Drizzle with sour cream and salsa.
  5. Optionally, garnish with crushed tortilla chips for an extra crunch.
  6. Serve and enjoy a low-carb twist on Taco Tuesday!

Ground Beef Tacos with Chipotle Sauce

For taco lovers who enjoy a smoky, spicy kick, chipotle sauce is the secret ingredient you need to elevate your taco night. This recipe combines smoky ground beef with a zesty chipotle sauce that packs a punch.

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tsp ground cumin
  • 1 tsp smoked paprika
  • 1/2 tsp ground chipotle pepper (or more, depending on spice preference)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Taco shells (soft or hard)
  • Fresh cilantro for garnish
  • Lime wedges
  • Chipotle Sauce:
    • 1/2 cup sour cream
    • 1 tbsp chipotle in adobo sauce (finely chopped)
    • 1 tbsp lime juice
    • Salt to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a skillet, brown the ground beef over medium heat. Add the chopped onion and garlic, cooking until soft.
  2. Stir in the cumin, smoked paprika, chipotle pepper, salt, and pepper. Cook for an additional 2-3 minutes to let the spices bloom.
  3. For the chipotle sauce, mix the sour cream, chipotle in adobo sauce, lime juice, and salt in a bowl.
  4. Warm the taco shells and fill them with the seasoned ground beef.
  5. Drizzle with the chipotle sauce and garnish with fresh cilantro.
  6. Serve with lime wedges on the side for an extra zing.

Final Thoughts

Ground beef tacos are a versatile and beloved option for Taco Tuesday. From classic flavors to more creative twists, there are countless ways to prepare ground beef tacos that will delight your taste buds. Whether you enjoy adding sweet potatoes, black beans, or a zesty chipotle sauce, these recipes are sure to inspire your next Taco Tuesday celebration. Don’t forget to experiment with different toppings, shells, and sauces to create a meal that’s uniquely yours.
