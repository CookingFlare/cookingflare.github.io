Instant Pot Pizza: A Fun and Fast Family Dinner


Who doesn’t love pizza? It’s the ultimate comfort food, and it’s perfect for feeding the family, whether you’re having a casual weeknight meal or entertaining guests. Traditional pizza recipes often require hours of preparation, but what if we told you that you could make a pizza in your Instant Pot in less time, and with minimal effort? That’s right! Instant Pot pizza is a game-changer that will elevate your dinner routine and give you the flexibility to create different flavors that everyone in your family will love.

In this blog post, we will guide you through the process of making a delicious, homemade pizza using your Instant Pot. You’ll be amazed by how easy it is, and the results will leave your taste buds craving more. Whether you’re a pizza-making pro or a beginner in the kitchen, this recipe is approachable, customizable, and full of flavor.

Why Use an Instant Pot for Pizza?

You might be wondering: why should you make pizza in an Instant Pot instead of using a conventional oven or a pizza stone? The truth is, the Instant Pot’s ability to generate steam and maintain consistent heat makes it an excellent choice for baking pizza. It creates the perfect environment for cooking a pizza with a crispy crust and gooey melted cheese. Plus, it’s faster and more energy-efficient compared to using an oven, especially when you’re looking to whip up dinner quickly.

Another bonus? The Instant Pot is perfect for families on the go. If you have a busy household, the Instant Pot simplifies the cooking process by cutting down on prep time and offering a no-fuss method for creating a delicious pizza.

What You’ll Need

Before we dive into the step-by-step process, let’s go over the ingredients and equipment you’ll need for making your Instant Pot pizza.

Ingredients:

  • Pizza Dough: You can use store-bought pizza dough, or if you’re feeling adventurous, you can make your own. Homemade dough takes just a few minutes to prepare and can be used for various recipes beyond pizza.
  • Pizza Sauce: You can opt for store-bought pizza sauce, or if you prefer, make your own using crushed tomatoes, garlic, olive oil, and a mix of herbs like oregano, basil, and thyme.
  • Shredded Cheese: Mozzarella is the most common choice for pizza, but feel free to get creative with your cheese selection. Cheddar, parmesan, or a blend of different cheeses all work well.
  • Toppings: This is where you can get really creative! You can add a variety of toppings, such as pepperoni, sausage, mushrooms, bell peppers, onions, olives, or even pineapple (if you’re into it!).
  • Olive Oil: You’ll use this to grease your Instant Pot and prevent the dough from sticking.
  • Seasonings: Don’t forget to season the crust and the sauce. A sprinkle of Italian seasoning or garlic powder can take your pizza to the next level.

Equipment:

  • Instant Pot: This is the star of the show! The Instant Pot will be used to cook your pizza. Be sure to have the trivet or a steaming rack that comes with your Instant Pot to keep the pizza elevated while cooking.
  • Parchment Paper: You’ll need parchment paper to line your pizza for easy removal once it’s cooked.
  • Rolling Pin: To roll out the dough into a perfect circle to fit inside your Instant Pot.

Now that you have all the ingredients and tools, let’s move on to the fun part — making the pizza!

Step-by-Step Guide to Making Instant Pot Pizza

Step 1: Prepare the Dough

If you’re using store-bought pizza dough, take it out of the package and let it come to room temperature for easier handling. If you’re making your dough from scratch, combine your flour, water, yeast, salt, and a bit of olive oil in a mixing bowl. Knead the dough until it’s smooth and elastic, then let it rest for about 1 hour in a warm spot so it can rise. Once your dough has risen, punch it down and prepare it for rolling.

On a lightly floured surface, use a rolling pin to roll out the dough into a circle. You want it to be slightly smaller than the diameter of your Instant Pot’s inner pot so that it fits comfortably inside. The dough should be about 1/4-inch thick.

Step 2: Prep the Instant Pot

Before you start assembling the pizza, prepare your Instant Pot. Lightly grease the bottom of the inner pot with olive oil, which will help prevent the dough from sticking. Place the trivet inside the pot, which will act as a support for the pizza. The trivet helps the pizza cook evenly and prevents it from becoming soggy. If you don’t have a trivet, you can use a steam rack or a heatproof dish that fits inside your Instant Pot.

Step 3: Assemble the Pizza

Now, it’s time to get creative with your toppings! Place a piece of parchment paper on top of the trivet, and carefully transfer the rolled-out dough onto the paper. Be gentle to avoid tearing the dough. Once the dough is in place, spread a layer of pizza sauce evenly over the surface, leaving a small border around the edges for the crust.

Sprinkle a generous amount of shredded mozzarella cheese over the sauce. Then, add your favorite toppings. Whether it’s classic pepperoni, mushrooms, bell peppers, or sausage, load up the pizza with all the ingredients you love. Just make sure not to overstuff it, as too many toppings can make it difficult to cook evenly.

Step 4: Cook the Pizza

Now comes the moment you’ve been waiting for: cooking your pizza! Close the Instant Pot lid and set the valve to the “sealing” position. Select the “Pressure Cook” or “Manual” setting on your Instant Pot and cook on high pressure for 10 minutes.

While it may seem like a short cooking time, the Instant Pot will create a steamy environment that helps the dough cook through while maintaining a nice, crispy crust. The pressure cooking method ensures that the cheese melts perfectly and the toppings cook to perfection.

Step 5: Quick Release

Once the cooking time is up, perform a quick release by turning the valve to “venting” to release any remaining pressure. This step is crucial to avoid overcooking your pizza.

Step 6: Remove and Serve

Carefully remove the pizza from the Instant Pot using the trivet handles. Let it cool for a couple of minutes before slicing. Use a pizza cutter or a sharp knife to slice it into portions. Serve with your favorite dipping sauce, a side salad, or some garlic bread for a well-rounded meal.

Tips for the Perfect Instant Pot Pizza

  • Use Parchment Paper: It’s essential to line the pizza with parchment paper to ensure easy removal from the Instant Pot. This also prevents the pizza from sticking to the pot.
  • Customize Your Pizza: The beauty of making pizza in the Instant Pot is that you can customize it however you like. Experiment with different sauces (like white sauce or pesto), cheeses, and toppings.
  • Get Creative with Crusts: While classic pizza dough is always a winner, you can also try using gluten-free pizza dough, cauliflower crust, or even flatbreads if you want to switch things up.
  • Keep the Toppings Balanced: When adding toppings, be mindful not to overload the pizza. A thin layer of toppings will cook more evenly than a heavily loaded pizza.
  • Add Fresh Herbs: For an extra burst of flavor, top your pizza with fresh basil or oregano after it’s done cooking.

Variations of Instant Pot Pizza

One of the best things about making pizza in the Instant Pot is that you can experiment with different types of pizzas. Here are some fun variations to try:

1. Margherita Pizza:

For a simple yet delicious pizza, spread pizza sauce on the dough and top with fresh mozzarella, basil, and a drizzle of olive oil.

2. Vegetarian Pizza:

If you’re a fan of veggies, pile your pizza high with mushrooms, bell peppers, onions, spinach, and olives. Add a sprinkle of feta cheese for a tangy twist.

3. BBQ Chicken Pizza:

For a unique flavor, swap the pizza sauce with BBQ sauce and top with cooked chicken, red onions, and cilantro.

4. Meat Lovers Pizza:

Go all out with a combination of pepperoni, sausage, bacon, and ham for a hearty meat lover’s pizza.

5. Breakfast Pizza:

Try something new by using scrambled eggs, bacon, and cheese for a fun and savory breakfast pizza.

Final Thoughts

Instant Pot pizza is the perfect solution for a busy family looking for a fun, fast, and delicious dinner. Not only is it easy to make, but it’s also a great way to get the family involved in the kitchen. Everyone can pick their favorite toppings, and you can create a personalized pizza in no time. Plus, it’s a great way to enjoy a homemade meal without spending hours in the kitchen.

So, the next time you’re craving pizza but don’t have the time to wait for the dough to rise and the oven to heat up, reach for your Instant Pot. In just a few simple steps, you’ll have a pizza that’s sure to impress!
