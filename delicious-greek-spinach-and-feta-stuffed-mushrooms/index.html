Delicious Greek Spinach and Feta Stuffed Mushrooms


If you’re a fan of Greek flavors and looking for a delicious appetizer or side dish, these Greek Spinach and Feta Stuffed Mushrooms are a must-try. They are bursting with savory flavors and are a perfect addition to any gathering or family meal. The combination of earthy mushrooms, vibrant spinach, and rich feta cheese creates a mouthwatering dish that will leave everyone craving for more.

Ingredients:

  • 20 large cremini or button mushrooms, stems removed
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 3 garlic cloves, minced
  • 2 cups fresh spinach, chopped
  • 1/2 cup crumbled feta cheese
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/4 cup breadcrumbs
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the Mushrooms: Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C). Clean the mushrooms and remove the stems, setting them aside for later use. Place the mushroom caps on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper.

  2. Make the Filling: In a large skillet, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the chopped onion and sauté until softened, about 3-4 minutes. Add the minced garlic and cook for another minute. Then, add the chopped spinach and cook until wilted. Remove the mixture from the heat and let it cool for a few minutes.

  3. Prepare the Stuffing: In a bowl, combine the feta cheese, Parmesan cheese, breadcrumbs, dried oregano, and the cooked spinach mixture. Mix well until all the ingredients are evenly combined. Season the mixture with salt and pepper to taste.

  4. Stuff the Mushrooms: Using a spoon, generously fill each mushroom cap with the spinach and feta mixture, pressing it down gently to ensure the caps are fully filled.

  5. Bake the Stuffed Mushrooms: Place the stuffed mushrooms in the preheated oven and bake for 20-25 minutes, or until the mushrooms are tender and the filling is golden brown.

  6. Garnish and Serve: Once out of the oven, garnish the stuffed mushrooms with freshly chopped parsley for a pop of color and added freshness. Serve the mushrooms warm and enjoy the delightful flavors.

These Greek Spinach and Feta Stuffed Mushrooms are a fantastic appetizer for parties or a delightful side dish for a family dinner. The combination of the earthy mushrooms, savory spinach, and tangy feta cheese creates a harmonious blend of flavors that will impress your guests and leave them asking for the recipe. This dish is not only delicious but also packed with nutrients, making it a healthy option for everyone to enjoy.

So, next time you’re in the mood for a flavorful and satisfying appetizer, give these Greek Spinach and Feta Stuffed Mushrooms a try. They are sure to become a favorite in your recipe collection and will leave a lasting impression on anyone who tries them. Enjoy!
