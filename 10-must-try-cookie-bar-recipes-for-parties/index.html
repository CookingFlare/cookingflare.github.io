10 Must-Try Cookie Bar Recipes for Parties


Cookie bars are the ultimate party treat. They’re easy to make, incredibly versatile, and loved by everyone—no matter their age. Whether you’re throwing a small gathering or attending a potluck, cookie bars are guaranteed to impress. Ready to elevate your dessert game? Here are 10 must-try cookie bar recipes that will leave your guests coming back for seconds (and thirds!).

1. Classic Chocolate Chip Cookie Bars

You can never go wrong with a classic. These chocolate chip cookie bars are everything you love about traditional chocolate chip cookies—soft, gooey, and brimming with chocolate chips—but with the added convenience of baking them in a pan.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup unsalted butter, melted
  • 1 cup brown sugar
  • ½ cup granulated sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 2½ cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • ½ tsp salt
  • 2 cups semi-sweet chocolate chips

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C) and line a 9x13-inch baking pan with parchment paper.
  2. Mix melted butter with brown sugar and granulated sugar until smooth. Add eggs and vanilla extract, and whisk until combined.
  3. Gradually fold in the flour, baking powder, and salt until you have a thick dough. Stir in chocolate chips.
  4. Spread the dough evenly in the prepared pan and bake for 25–30 minutes. Let it cool completely before cutting into squares.

2. Fudgy Brownie Cookie Bars

Imagine the rich decadence of a fudge brownie combined with the chewy goodness of a cookie—they merge beautifully in these irresistible bars.

Ingredients:

  • ½ cup unsalted butter, melted
  • ¾ cup cocoa powder
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • ½ cup all-purpose flour
  • ¼ tsp salt
  • ½ cup mini chocolate chips

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 325°F (165°C) and grease an 8x8-inch baking pan.
  2. Whisk melted butter and cocoa powder together until smooth. Stir in sugar, then add eggs and vanilla.
  3. Fold in flour and salt until fully combined but avoid overmixing. Sprinkle mini chocolate chips into the batter.
  4. Spread evenly in the pan and bake for 30–35 minutes. Allow to cool completely before cutting.

3. Peanut Butter Cup Cookie Bars

Peanut butter lovers, this one’s for you! These cookie bars are loaded with the creamy goodness of peanut butter and chunks of peanut butter cups.

Ingredients:

  • ½ cup unsalted butter, softened
  • ¾ cup peanut butter
  • ¾ cup brown sugar
  • ¼ cup granulated sugar
  • 1 large egg
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1½ cups all-purpose flour
  • ½ tsp baking powder
  • ½ tsp salt
  • 1 cup chopped peanut butter cups

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C) and line a 9x9-inch pan with parchment paper.
  2. Cream butter, peanut butter, brown sugar, and granulated sugar together. Add egg and vanilla and mix.
  3. Incorporate flour, baking powder, and salt into the mix, then fold in peanut butter cups.
  4. Spread the dough evenly in the pan and bake for 20–25 minutes. Cool completely before serving.

4. Salted Caramel Cookie Bars

Sweet and salty come together in perfect harmony with these salted caramel cookie bars. They’re ideal for sophisticated parties or indulgent late-night snacking.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1 cup brown sugar
  • ½ cup granulated sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 2½ cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • ½ tsp salt
  • ¾ cup caramel bits
  • 1 tsp flaky sea salt

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease a 9x13-inch pan or line it with parchment paper.
  2. Beat butter, brown sugar, and granulated sugar together until creamy. Add eggs and vanilla extract.
  3. Gradually mix in flour, baking soda, and salt until well combined. Fold in caramel bits.
  4. Spread the dough into the baking pan and sprinkle flaky sea salt over the top. Bake for 25–30 minutes.

5. Lemon Burst Cookie Bars

Add a burst of tangy freshness to your dessert table with these citrus-infused cookie bars. Perfect for Spring and Summer parties!

Ingredients:

For the Cookie Base:

  • 1 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • ¾ cup granulated sugar
  • 1 large egg
  • 2½ cups all-purpose flour

For the Lemon Topping:

  • ¾ cup granulated sugar
  • 3 large eggs
  • ¾ cup freshly squeezed lemon juice
  • ¼ cup all-purpose flour

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C) and grease a 9x13-inch pan.
  2. Make the cookie base by creaming butter and sugar, then adding the egg and flour. Press into the pan evenly and bake for 15 minutes.
  3. Whisk all topping ingredients until smooth. Pour over the par-baked cookie base and bake for an additional 20–25 minutes.

6. Oatmeal Raisin Cookie Bars

A wholesome twist on cookie bars, these oatmeal raisin bites offer chewy, hearty goodness that’s great for any crowd.

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups rolled oats
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp cinnamon
  • ½ tsp baking soda
  • ½ cup unsalted butter, softened
  • ¾ cup brown sugar
  • ¼ cup granulated sugar
  • 1 large egg
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 cup raisins

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C) and line an 8x8-inch pan with parchment paper.
  2. Combine oats, flour, cinnamon, and baking soda. In another bowl, cream butter and sugars, then add egg and vanilla.
  3. Mix wet and dry ingredients until fully incorporated, then stir in raisins.
  4. Spread evenly in the baking pan and bake for 20–25 minutes.

7. Funfetti Cookie Bars

Brighten up your party with these colorful, sprinkle-filled cookie bars! They’re ideal for kids’ parties or festive holiday gatherings.

Ingredients:

  • ½ cup unsalted butter, softened
  • ¾ cup granulated sugar
  • 1 large egg
  • 1½ tsp vanilla extract
  • 1½ cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • ½ tsp salt
  • ½ cup rainbow sprinkles

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C) and line a 9x9-inch pan with parchment paper.
  2. Cream butter and sugar, add the egg and vanilla, then incorporate flour, baking powder, and salt.
  3. Fold in sprinkles and spread the dough into your baking pan. Bake for 20–25 minutes.

8. Cookies ’n Cream Oreo Bars

Get ready to wow your party guests with cookies ’n cream goodness packed into every bite. The crushed Oreos take these cookie bars to the next level.

Ingredients:

  • ½ cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1½ tsp vanilla extract
  • 1½ cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1½ cups crushed Oreos

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease an 8x8-inch pan.
  2. Beat butter and sugar together, add eggs and vanilla, then fold in flour and baking powder until combined.
  3. Stir in crushed Oreos and spread the dough evenly into your pan. Bake for 25–30 minutes.

9. Pumpkin Spice Cookie Bars

Perfect for a fall-themed gathering, these pumpkin spice cookie bars are warm, spiced, and utterly delicious.

Ingredients:

  • ½ cup unsalted butter, melted
  • ½ cup pumpkin puree
  • ¾ cup brown sugar
  • 1 large egg
  • 1½ tsp cinnamon
  • ½ tsp nutmeg
  • 1½ cups all-purpose flour
  • ½ tsp baking powder

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C) and grease a 9x9-inch pan.
  2. Combine melted butter, pumpkin puree, and brown sugar, then add the egg and spices.
  3. Slowly mix in flour and baking powder until the dough comes together.
  4. Spread into the pan and bake for 20–25 minutes.

10. Raspberry Jam Crumble Bars

For a fruity touch, these raspberry jam crumble bars are a crowd-pleaser that pairs well with coffee or tea.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 large egg yolk
  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup raspberry jam
  • ½ cup rolled oats

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C) and line a 9x13-inch pan with parchment paper.
  2. Cream butter and sugar, then mix in the egg yolk and flour until crumbly. Press ¾ of the dough into the pan.
  3. Spread raspberry jam evenly on top, then sprinkle with oats and remaining dough.
  4. Bake for 30–35 minutes until golden brown.

These cookie bar recipes are perfect for any occasion! Whip one up for your next party, and watch your guests’ faces light up with joy.
