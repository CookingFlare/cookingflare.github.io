Wholesome Instant Pot Lentil Curry Recipes for Plant-Based Meals


Lentils are a powerhouse of nutrition, rich in protein, fiber, and essential vitamins and minerals. When paired with an array of spices and vegetables, they create a delightful symphony of flavors that can elevate any meal. The Instant Pot makes cooking lentil curry not only fast but also incredibly convenient. In this blog post, we will explore several mouthwatering lentil curry recipes that are not only plant-based but also packed with wholesome ingredients that nourish the body and satisfy the palate.

Why Choose Lentils for Your Plant-Based Meals?

Lentils are an excellent choice for anyone looking to incorporate more plant-based meals into their diet. They are:

  1. Nutrient-Dense: Lentils are high in protein, making them a fantastic meat alternative. They also contain iron, folate, and magnesium, contributing to overall health.

  2. Versatile: Lentils can be easily combined with various spices, vegetables, and grains, allowing for endless variations in meals.

  3. Quick Cooking: The Instant Pot significantly reduces the cooking time for lentils and curries, making it easy to whip up a delicious meal even on busy days.

  4. Sustainable: As a plant-based protein source, lentils have a lower environmental impact compared to animal-based proteins.

  5. Budget-Friendly: Lentils are affordable and can be purchased in bulk, making them an economical choice for nutritious meals.

Essential Ingredients for Lentil Curry

Before diving into the recipes, let’s cover some essential ingredients that will enhance your lentil curry experience:

  • Lentils: Brown and green lentils hold their shape well, while red lentils cook down and create a creamy texture.
  • Vegetables: Common vegetables used in lentil curry include onions, garlic, ginger, carrots, tomatoes, and spinach.
  • Spices: A blend of spices such as cumin, coriander, turmeric, garam masala, and chili powder adds depth and flavor.
  • Coconut Milk: For a creamy texture and rich flavor, coconut milk is a fantastic addition to many lentil curry recipes.
  • Broth or Water: Use vegetable broth or water as the cooking liquid to infuse flavor into the lentils.

Recipe 1: Classic Instant Pot Lentil Curry

Ingredients

  • 1 cup brown or green lentils, rinsed
  • 1 medium onion, diced
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1-inch piece ginger, grated
  • 1 can (14 oz) diced tomatoes
  • 1 cup vegetable broth
  • 1 can (14 oz) coconut milk
  • 1 carrot, diced
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • 1 teaspoon coriander
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric
  • 1 teaspoon garam masala
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh cilantro for garnish

Instructions

  1. Sauté Aromatics: Set your Instant Pot to the sauté mode. Add a splash of water or vegetable broth, then sauté the onions for about 5 minutes until translucent. Add garlic and ginger, and sauté for an additional 1-2 minutes until fragrant.

  2. Add Spices: Stir in cumin, coriander, turmeric, and garam masala. Cook for another minute to toast the spices.

  3. Combine Ingredients: Add the rinsed lentils, diced tomatoes, vegetable broth, coconut milk, and diced carrots. Stir well to combine.

  4. Pressure Cook: Close the lid and set the Instant Pot to manual high pressure for 15 minutes. Once the cooking time is complete, allow for a natural release for 10 minutes, then quick release any remaining pressure.

  5. Finish and Serve: Open the lid and stir the curry. Adjust seasoning with salt and pepper as needed. Serve hot, garnished with fresh cilantro.

Recipe 2: Spicy Instant Pot Lentil Curry with Spinach

Ingredients

  • 1 cup red lentils, rinsed
  • 1 medium onion, diced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1-inch piece ginger, grated
  • 1 can (14 oz) diced tomatoes
  • 4 cups vegetable broth
  • 2 cups fresh spinach
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric
  • 1 teaspoon chili powder (adjust to taste)
  • Salt to taste
  • Juice of 1 lemon

Instructions

  1. Sauté the Base: In the Instant Pot, set to sauté mode, add a splash of water and sauté the onions until they soften. Add in the garlic and ginger, cooking for an additional minute.

  2. Spice it Up: Stir in cumin, turmeric, and chili powder, allowing the spices to bloom for 1 minute.

  3. Add the Lentils: Add the rinsed red lentils, diced tomatoes, and vegetable broth. Stir well to combine.

  4. Pressure Cook: Close the lid and set to manual high pressure for 10 minutes. Perform a quick release once the timer goes off.

  5. Incorporate Spinach: Open the lid and stir in the fresh spinach until wilted. Add salt and lemon juice to taste. Serve with rice or naan.

Recipe 3: Creamy Instant Pot Coconut Lentil Curry

Ingredients

  • 1 cup green lentils, rinsed
  • 1 medium onion, diced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1-inch piece ginger, grated
  • 1 can (14 oz) coconut milk
  • 2 cups vegetable broth
  • 1 tablespoon curry powder
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce or tamari
  • 1 cup mixed vegetables (e.g., peas, bell peppers)
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Sauté the Onions: In your Instant Pot, set to sauté mode, add a bit of water and sauté the onion until translucent. Add garlic and ginger, cooking for another minute.

  2. Add Coconut Milk and Spices: Stir in the curry powder and soy sauce, followed by the coconut milk, vegetable broth, and rinsed lentils. Mix well.

  3. Pressure Cook: Close the lid and set to manual high pressure for 12 minutes. Allow for a natural release.

  4. Add Vegetables: Once the pressure is released, stir in mixed vegetables. Adjust seasoning with salt and pepper to taste. Serve hot.

Tips for Perfect Lentil Curry

  • Pre-soaking Lentils: While lentils do not require soaking like beans, soaking them for a few hours can reduce cooking time and improve digestibility.
  • Flavor Enhancements: Experiment with different spices like fenugreek, mustard seeds, or curry leaves to customize your curry.
  • Vegetable Variations: Feel free to add any seasonal vegetables you have on hand, such as sweet potatoes, zucchini, or cauliflower.
  • Serving Suggestions: Lentil curry pairs beautifully with brown rice, quinoa, or even cauliflower rice for a low-carb option.

Conclusion

Wholesome Instant Pot lentil curry recipes are not only easy to prepare but also bursting with flavor and nutrients. These plant-based meals are perfect for anyone looking to boost their diet with healthy ingredients. Whether you opt for a classic recipe or try a spicy twist, you’ll find that lentil curry can be a delightful addition to your weekly meal plan. With the Instant Pot’s convenience, you can enjoy a delicious and nourishing meal in no time. So, gather your ingredients and start cooking—your taste buds and body will thank you!
