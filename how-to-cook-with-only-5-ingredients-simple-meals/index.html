How to Cook with Only 5 Ingredients: Simple Meals


Cooking doesn’t have to be a daunting task. In fact, some of the most delicious meals can be created using just a handful of ingredients. Whether you’re a busy professional, a college student on a budget, or simply someone who appreciates simplicity, mastering the art of cooking with only 5 ingredients can open up a world of culinary possibilities. In this blog post, we’ll explore some simple yet delectable recipes that require just 5 ingredients, proving that less can indeed be more in the kitchen.

1. Garlic Parmesan Roasted Potatoes

Ingredients:

  • 4 large potatoes, washed and cubed
  • 3 tablespoons olive oil
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 400°F (200°C).
  2. In a large bowl, toss the cubed potatoes with olive oil, minced garlic, Parmesan cheese, salt, and pepper until the potatoes are evenly coated.
  3. Spread the potatoes in a single layer on a baking sheet and roast for 30-35 minutes or until golden brown and crispy, flipping once halfway through.

2. Caprese Stuffed Avocado

Ingredients:

  • 2 ripe avocados, halved and pitted
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1/2 cup fresh mozzarella, diced
  • Fresh basil leaves, chopped
  • Balsamic glaze for drizzling

Instructions:

  1. Scoop out a little bit of flesh from each avocado half to create a larger hollow.
  2. In a bowl, combine the cherry tomatoes, mozzarella, and basil. Season with salt and pepper.
  3. Spoon the tomato-mozzarella mixture into the avocado halves.
  4. Drizzle with balsamic glaze before serving.

3. Lemon Garlic Butter Shrimp

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound large shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 4 tablespoons unsalted butter
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • Zest of 1 lemon
  • Fresh parsley, chopped
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a large skillet, melt the butter over medium heat. Add the minced garlic and cook until fragrant.
  2. Add the shrimp to the skillet and cook for 2-3 minutes per side or until pink and opaque.
  3. Stir in the lemon zest and chopped parsley. Season with salt and pepper.

4. Creamy Pesto Pasta

Ingredients:

  • 8 oz fettuccine pasta
  • 1/2 cup store-bought pesto
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • Fresh basil for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Cook the fettuccine pasta according to the package instructions. Drain and return to the pot.
  2. Stir in the pesto, heavy cream, and grated Parmesan cheese until the pasta is evenly coated.
  3. Garnish with fresh basil before serving.

5. Honey Mustard Glazed Salmon

Ingredients:

  • 4 salmon fillets
  • 1/4 cup Dijon mustard
  • 1/4 cup honey
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 teaspoon minced garlic

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. In a small bowl, whisk together the Dijon mustard, honey, soy sauce, and minced garlic.
  3. Place the salmon fillets on a lined baking sheet and brush the honey mustard glaze over each fillet.
  4. Bake for 12-15 minutes or until the salmon is cooked through.

Conclusion

Cooking with only 5 ingredients can be a liberating experience, allowing you to focus on the flavors and simplicity of each dish. By utilizing a few quality ingredients, you can create meals that are not only delicious but also quick and easy to prepare. Whether you’re a novice cook or a seasoned chef, these recipes are sure to inspire your culinary creativity. So, embrace the magic of simplicity and embark on a flavorful journey with these delightful 5-ingredient dishes.
