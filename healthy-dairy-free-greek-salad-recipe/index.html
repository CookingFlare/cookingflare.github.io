Healthy Dairy-Free Greek Salad Recipe


Are you looking for a refreshing and healthy dish to enjoy on a warm summer day? Look no further than this delicious and nutritious Dairy-Free Greek Salad recipe! Packed with fresh vegetables, flavorful herbs, and a zesty dressing, this salad is a perfect addition to any meal or can stand alone as a satisfying light lunch. Plus, it’s dairy-free, making it suitable for those with lactose intolerance or anyone looking to incorporate more plant-based options into their diet.

Ingredients

  • 3 large tomatoes, diced
  • 1 cucumber, diced
  • 1 red onion, thinly sliced
  • 1 green bell pepper, chopped
  • 1/2 cup Kalamata olives, pitted and halved
  • 1/2 cup extra-virgin olive oil
  • 1/4 cup red wine vinegar
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 cup dairy-free feta cheese, crumbled (optional)
  • Fresh parsley for garnish

Instructions

  1. In a large mixing bowl, combine the diced tomatoes, cucumber, red onion, green bell pepper, and Kalamata olives. Toss gently to mix the ingredients evenly.

  2. In a small bowl, whisk together the extra-virgin olive oil, red wine vinegar, dried oregano, salt, and pepper to create the dressing.

  3. Pour the dressing over the vegetable mixture and toss until everything is well coated.

  4. If desired, sprinkle the dairy-free feta cheese over the salad and gently toss to incorporate.

  5. Let the salad marinate in the refrigerator for at least 30 minutes to allow the flavors to meld together.

  6. Once ready to serve, garnish the salad with fresh parsley for a pop of color and added freshness.

Serving Suggestions

This Dairy-Free Greek Salad can be enjoyed on its own as a light and satisfying meal, or it can be served as a side dish alongside grilled chicken, fish, or your favorite plant-based protein. For a complete Mediterranean-inspired meal, pair this salad with warm pita bread and a side of hummus.

Benefits of Dairy-Free Greek Salad

Not only is this salad bursting with flavor, but it also offers a range of health benefits. The combination of fresh vegetables provides a generous dose of vitamins, minerals, and dietary fiber. The olive oil in the dressing is rich in heart-healthy monounsaturated fats, while the Kalamata olives add a delightful briny flavor and a dose of healthy fats. Additionally, by using dairy-free feta cheese, this recipe offers a cruelty-free and lactose-free alternative to traditional Greek salad, making it inclusive for individuals with dietary restrictions.

Conclusion

In conclusion, this Healthy Dairy-Free Greek Salad is a delightful blend of vibrant colors, bold flavors, and wholesome ingredients. Whether you’re hosting a summer gathering, packing a picnic, or simply craving a nutritious meal, this salad is sure to impress. Embrace the flavors of the Mediterranean with this dairy-free twist on a classic dish, and savor the health benefits it brings to the table. Enjoy!
