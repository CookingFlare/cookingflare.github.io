Rich and Creamy Instant Pot Cheesecake Recipe for Dessert Lovers


Are you a dessert enthusiast looking for a show-stopping treat to impress your family and friends? Look no further than this indulgent Instant Pot cheesecake recipe. With its rich and creamy texture, coupled with the convenience of using an Instant Pot, this dessert is a game-changer for any cheesecake lover. Whether you’re a seasoned baker or a novice in the kitchen, this recipe is sure to elevate your dessert game to a whole new level.

Ingredients

For the Crust:

  • 1 ½ cups graham cracker crumbs
  • ¼ cup granulated sugar
  • ½ cup unsalted butter, melted

For the Filling:

  • 32 oz cream cheese, softened
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 4 large eggs
  • ½ cup sour cream
  • ½ cup heavy cream
  • 2 tbsp all-purpose flour

For the Topping (Optional):

  • Fresh berries or fruit compote
  • Whipped cream
  • Chocolate shavings

Instructions

Step 1: Prepare the Crust

  1. In a medium bowl, combine the graham cracker crumbs, sugar, and melted butter. Mix until the crumbs are evenly coated with the butter.

  2. Press the crumb mixture into the bottom of a 7-inch springform pan, ensuring an even layer along the bottom and slightly up the sides. Set the pan aside.

Step 2: Prepare the Filling

  1. In a large mixing bowl, beat the cream cheese until smooth and creamy.

  2. Add the granulated sugar and vanilla extract to the cream cheese, and beat until well combined.

  3. Add the eggs one at a time, mixing well after each addition.

  4. Mix in the sour cream, heavy cream, and flour until the filling is smooth and velvety.

Step 3: Assemble and Cook

  1. Pour the filling over the prepared crust in the springform pan.

  2. Cover the springform pan with aluminum foil, ensuring it’s secure but not too tight.

  3. Add 1 ½ cups of water to the Instant Pot and place the trivet in the pot. Carefully lower the springform pan onto the trivet.

  4. Close the Instant Pot lid, set the valve to the sealing position, and cook on high pressure for 35 minutes. Once done, allow for a natural pressure release for 10 minutes before releasing any remaining pressure.

  5. Carefully remove the springform pan from the Instant Pot and let the cheesecake cool at room temperature for an hour. Transfer it to the refrigerator and chill for at least 4 hours or overnight.

Step 4: Add Toppings and Serve

  1. Once the cheesecake is thoroughly chilled, carefully remove it from the springform pan.

  2. If desired, add a generous dollop of whipped cream and a handful of fresh berries or a drizzle of fruit compote on top. For a decadent touch, sprinkle some chocolate shavings over the cheesecake.

  3. Slice the cheesecake into portions and serve to your eager guests.

Conclusion

This rich and creamy Instant Pot cheesecake is a delightful way to indulge in a classic dessert with a modern twist. Its luscious texture and delectable flavor will leave your taste buds singing with joy. Whether it’s for a special occasion or a casual gathering, this cheesecake is sure to become a favorite among your loved ones. So, fire up your Instant Pot and treat yourself to a slice of bliss with this irresistible dessert masterpiece.
