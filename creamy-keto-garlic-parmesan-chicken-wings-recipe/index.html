Creamy Keto Garlic Parmesan Chicken Wings Recipe


If you’re on the lookout for a delicious yet low-carb appetizer or main dish, these creamy keto garlic parmesan chicken wings are bound to be a hit. Packed with flavor and easy to prepare, this recipe is a fantastic addition to your keto-friendly repertoire. Whether you’re hosting a party, looking for a savory snack, or simply craving a comforting meal, these chicken wings are sure to satisfy.

Ingredients

  • 2 lbs chicken wings
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1/2 cup grated parmesan cheese
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley for garnish

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Chicken Wings: Begin by preheating your oven to 400°F (200°C). Line a baking sheet with parchment paper and place the chicken wings on it. Season the wings with salt and pepper, ensuring they are evenly coated. Bake the wings for 40-45 minutes or until they are golden and crispy.

  2. Make the Creamy Garlic Parmesan Sauce: In a saucepan over medium heat, melt the butter. Add the minced garlic and sauté for 1-2 minutes until fragrant. Pour in the heavy cream, then add the grated parmesan cheese, oregano, and thyme. Stir the mixture frequently until the cheese has fully melted and the sauce has thickened slightly. Season with salt and pepper to taste.

  3. Combine the Wings and Sauce: Once the chicken wings are cooked, transfer them to a large bowl. Pour the creamy garlic parmesan sauce over the wings and toss gently until they are evenly coated.

  4. Serve and Enjoy: Arrange the wings on a serving platter, garnish with fresh parsley, and serve immediately. These wings are best enjoyed hot and fresh out of the oven.

These creamy keto garlic parmesan chicken wings are a delightful combination of tender chicken and a rich, flavorful sauce. The creaminess of the parmesan sauce complements the crispy texture of the wings, creating a truly satisfying dish. In addition to being keto-friendly, this recipe is also gluten-free, making it a versatile option for those with dietary restrictions.

Whether you’re following a ketogenic diet or simply looking for a delicious twist on classic chicken wings, this recipe is a must-try. The creamy garlic parmesan sauce adds a luxurious touch to the dish, elevating it to a whole new level of indulgence. Serve these wings at your next gathering, and watch as they disappear within moments, leaving your guests raving about the irresistible flavor.

Final Thoughts

Creamy keto garlic parmesan chicken wings are a testament to the fact that low-carb dishes can be both nutritious and incredibly tasty. With minimal effort and a handful of ingredients, you can whip up a batch of these wings that will impress even the most discerning palates. Whether you’re a fan of creamy sauces or simply enjoy a good plate of chicken wings, this recipe is sure to become a favorite in your culinary arsenal.

So, fire up your oven, gather your ingredients, and treat yourself to a batch of these mouthwatering chicken wings. Your taste buds will thank you, and you’ll have a go-to keto-friendly recipe that never fails to delight.
