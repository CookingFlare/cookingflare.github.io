Sandwiches and Wraps That Are Perfect for Meal Prepping


Meal prepping can be a game-changer for anyone looking to maintain a healthy diet, save time, and reduce stress throughout the week. Whether you’re packing lunches for work, school, or simply need quick meals ready to go, sandwiches and wraps are fantastic options for meal prepping. They are easy to prepare, portable, and highly customizable, making them perfect for a wide variety of tastes and dietary preferences.

In this post, we’ll explore some sandwich and wrap ideas that will not only save you time but also keep your meals fresh and flavorful throughout the week. Whether you’re a meat lover, vegetarian, or prefer something more on the healthy side, there’s something for everyone.

Why Sandwiches and Wraps Are Ideal for Meal Prepping

1. Portability

One of the biggest advantages of sandwiches and wraps is their portability. They are compact, easy to pack, and perfect for taking on-the-go. Whether you’re heading to the office, a picnic, or running errands, these meals travel well without the need for extensive refrigeration or special containers.

2. Variety

There’s a wide range of fillings, spreads, and vegetables you can use to create the perfect sandwich or wrap. The variety is endless, so you can switch up your meals every week and never get bored.

3. Customization

Sandwiches and wraps are incredibly customizable. You can easily adjust the ingredients to fit specific dietary needs, whether you’re following a gluten-free, low-carb, vegan, or paleo diet. The flexibility makes them suitable for almost anyone.

4. Ease of Preparation

Unlike more complex meals, sandwiches and wraps can be assembled in a matter of minutes. With just a few ingredients and the right tools, you can whip up a week’s worth of meals quickly.

5. Long Shelf Life

With the right ingredients and proper wrapping, sandwiches and wraps can stay fresh in the fridge for up to five days. The key is to layer ingredients in a way that prevents sogginess, such as using lettuce or parchment paper to create barriers between the bread and moist ingredients.

Best Sandwiches and Wraps for Meal Prepping

Now that we’ve covered why sandwiches and wraps are great for meal prepping, let’s dive into some delicious and easy-to-make ideas.

1. Chicken Caesar Wraps

This classic wrap is a twist on the beloved Caesar salad, combining grilled chicken, romaine lettuce, parmesan cheese, and Caesar dressing. It’s simple to make, delicious, and can be stored easily for the week.

Ingredients:

  • 2 grilled chicken breasts (sliced)
  • 4 large flour tortillas
  • 2 cups romaine lettuce (shredded)
  • ½ cup grated parmesan cheese
  • ¼ cup Caesar dressing (store-bought or homemade)
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a large bowl, toss the shredded lettuce with Caesar dressing until well-coated.
  2. Place the tortillas on a flat surface and layer with grilled chicken slices, the dressed lettuce, and parmesan cheese.
  3. Roll up the tortillas tightly, folding in the sides as you go.
  4. Wrap each sandwich tightly in parchment paper or plastic wrap and store in the fridge for up to 4-5 days.

2. Veggie Hummus Wrap

For those who prefer a plant-based meal, the veggie hummus wrap is a perfect choice. It’s packed with nutritious vegetables, healthy fats, and protein-rich hummus for a filling and satisfying meal.

Ingredients:

  • 4 large whole wheat tortillas
  • ½ cup hummus (store-bought or homemade)
  • 1 avocado (sliced)
  • 1 cucumber (sliced)
  • 1 bell pepper (sliced)
  • 2 cups mixed greens
  • 2 tablespoons crumbled feta cheese (optional)
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Lay the whole wheat tortillas on a flat surface.
  2. Spread a generous amount of hummus over each tortilla.
  3. Layer with sliced avocado, cucumber, bell pepper, and mixed greens.
  4. Sprinkle with crumbled feta cheese, and season with salt and pepper.
  5. Roll up the tortillas, folding in the sides to keep the filling secure.
  6. Wrap each in parchment paper or plastic wrap and refrigerate.

3. Turkey and Avocado Club Sandwich

A hearty and satisfying sandwich, the turkey and avocado club combines lean turkey breast, crispy bacon, fresh avocado, and a tangy mayo-mustard dressing for a delicious combination of flavors.

Ingredients:

  • 2 slices whole grain bread (or gluten-free bread)
  • 4 oz lean turkey breast
  • 2 slices crispy bacon
  • ½ avocado (sliced)
  • 1 tablespoon mayonnaise
  • 1 teaspoon Dijon mustard
  • Lettuce and tomato slices
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a small bowl, mix the mayonnaise and Dijon mustard together to make the sauce.
  2. Toast the bread slices lightly and spread the sauce on both slices.
  3. Layer the turkey, crispy bacon, avocado, lettuce, and tomato on one slice of bread.
  4. Top with the second slice of bread and press down gently.
  5. Slice the sandwich in half and wrap tightly in parchment paper or plastic wrap.

4. Tuna Salad Wrap

The tuna salad wrap is a classic lunch choice that can be made in minutes. With its creamy tuna filling and crunchy vegetables, it’s a meal that satisfies both hunger and taste.

Ingredients:

  • 1 can of tuna (drained)
  • 2 tablespoons mayonnaise
  • 1 tablespoon Dijon mustard
  • 1 celery stalk (chopped)
  • ½ red onion (chopped)
  • 1 tablespoon relish or capers (optional)
  • 4 large flour tortillas
  • Lettuce or spinach leaves

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, combine the drained tuna, mayonnaise, mustard, chopped celery, onion, and relish or capers (if using).
  2. Stir until well-combined and season with salt and pepper.
  3. Lay the tortillas flat and place a few leaves of lettuce or spinach on each.
  4. Spoon the tuna salad onto the center of each tortilla.
  5. Roll up the tortillas tightly, folding in the sides.
  6. Wrap each in parchment paper or plastic wrap and store in the fridge.

5. Buffalo Chicken Wrap

For those who enjoy a bit of spice, the buffalo chicken wrap is a fantastic option. The combination of spicy buffalo sauce and creamy ranch dressing makes this wrap a bold and flavorful meal.

Ingredients:

  • 2 grilled chicken breasts (shredded)
  • 4 large tortillas
  • ¼ cup buffalo sauce
  • ¼ cup ranch dressing
  • 1 cup shredded lettuce
  • 1 small carrot (shredded)
  • 1 celery stalk (chopped)
  • 1 tablespoon blue cheese crumbles (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Toss the shredded chicken with buffalo sauce in a bowl.
  2. Lay the tortillas flat and spread a little ranch dressing on each.
  3. Add the buffalo chicken, shredded lettuce, shredded carrot, and chopped celery.
  4. Sprinkle with blue cheese crumbles if desired.
  5. Roll the tortillas tightly and wrap in parchment paper or plastic wrap.

6. Caprese Sandwich

A light and refreshing sandwich, the Caprese is a classic Italian combination of mozzarella, tomatoes, fresh basil, and a drizzle of balsamic glaze. This is an ideal meal prep option for those who love fresh, bright flavors.

Ingredients:

  • 2 slices whole wheat or sourdough bread
  • 4 oz fresh mozzarella cheese
  • 1 large tomato (sliced)
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • 1 tablespoon balsamic glaze
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Lay the slices of bread flat and layer the mozzarella cheese, tomato slices, and fresh basil.
  2. Drizzle with balsamic glaze and season with salt and pepper.
  3. Top with the second slice of bread and press gently.
  4. Wrap tightly in parchment paper or plastic wrap.

7. Grilled Veggie and Pesto Wrap

For a vegetarian and flavorful meal, the grilled veggie and pesto wrap combines charred vegetables with a rich pesto sauce for a Mediterranean-inspired meal that’s perfect for meal prepping.

Ingredients:

  • 4 large tortillas
  • 1 zucchini (sliced)
  • 1 red bell pepper (sliced)
  • 1 yellow bell pepper (sliced)
  • 1 red onion (sliced)
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • ¼ cup pesto sauce (store-bought or homemade)
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat a grill or grill pan over medium heat.
  2. Toss the sliced vegetables with olive oil, salt, and pepper.
  3. Grill the vegetables until charred and tender, about 5-7 minutes.
  4. Lay the tortillas flat and spread pesto sauce evenly on each.
  5. Layer the grilled vegetables on top of the pesto sauce.
  6. Roll up the tortillas tightly and wrap in parchment paper or plastic wrap.

Tips for Meal Prepping Sandwiches and Wraps

  • Avoid Sogginess: To prevent your sandwiches and wraps from becoming soggy, consider adding moisture-heavy ingredients like tomatoes or cucumbers closer to when you plan to eat them. You can also use a barrier like lettuce to protect the bread from moisture.

  • Use Quality Bread: Choose a hearty bread or wrap that can hold up well to the fillings and doesn’t become too mushy over time. Whole grain or sourdough breads are excellent options.

  • Prep in Bulk: If you’re prepping for the week, it’s a good idea to prepare the ingredients in bulk (grilled chicken, sliced vegetables, etc.) and assemble the sandwiches or wraps each day. This ensures maximum freshness.

  • Wrap Properly: To maintain the freshness of your wraps and sandwiches, wrap them tightly in parchment paper, foil, or plastic wrap. This helps to keep the fillings intact and prevents air from getting in.

  • Keep Dressings on the Side: If you’re worried about the dressing making your wraps soggy, you can always pack the dressing separately and add it just before eating.

Conclusion

Sandwiches and wraps are versatile, delicious, and easy to prepare, making them perfect for meal prepping. Whether you prefer a classic turkey club, a fresh veggie wrap, or something with a bit of heat like a buffalo chicken wrap, there are endless options to satisfy your taste buds. With the right ingredients and a little bit of planning, you can create a week’s worth of meals that are both convenient and full of flavor.

Start prepping today, and enjoy tasty, homemade sandwiches and wraps throughout the week!
