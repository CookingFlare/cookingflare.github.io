Bite-Sized Meatball Sliders for a Hearty Appetizer


If you’re looking for a delicious and satisfying appetizer that will please even the most discerning palates, look no further than these bite-sized meatball sliders. Perfectly seasoned meatballs nestled between soft slider buns, topped with melted cheese and a tangy sauce - they are a crowd-pleaser that will leave your guests wanting more. Whether you’re hosting a party, a game night, or simply craving a hearty snack, these sliders are sure to hit the spot.

Ingredients

For the meatballs:

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • ½ cup breadcrumbs
  • 1 egg
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons parsley, finely chopped
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • ½ teaspoon black pepper

For the sauce:

  • 1 cup ketchup
  • 2 tablespoons brown sugar
  • 1 tablespoon Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 teaspoon mustard
  • ½ teaspoon garlic powder
  • ½ teaspoon onion powder

For the sliders:

  • 12 slider buns
  • 12 slices of cheese (cheddar or mozzarella work well)

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).

  2. In a large bowl, combine the ground beef, breadcrumbs, egg, minced garlic, chopped parsley, salt, and black pepper. Mix well until all the ingredients are evenly incorporated.

  3. Shape the meat mixture into small meatballs, about 1 inch in diameter. Place them on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper.

  4. Bake the meatballs in the preheated oven for 15-20 minutes, or until cooked through and lightly browned.

  5. While the meatballs are baking, prepare the sauce. In a small saucepan, combine the ketchup, brown sugar, Worcestershire sauce, mustard, garlic powder, and onion powder. Cook over medium heat, stirring occasionally, until the sauce thickens slightly, about 5 minutes.

  6. Once the meatballs are cooked, remove them from the oven and toss them in the sauce until evenly coated.

  7. To assemble the sliders, slice the slider buns in half horizontally. Place a meatball on the bottom half of each bun and top with a slice of cheese. Place the top half of the bun on top.

  8. Arrange the sliders on a baking sheet and return them to the oven for 3-5 minutes, or until the cheese has melted and the buns are slightly toasted.

  9. Remove the sliders from the oven and let them cool slightly before serving. Serve them warm and enjoy!

Tips and Variations

  • Feel free to customize these sliders by adding your favorite toppings. Try sliced pickles, caramelized onions, or a dollop of mayonnaise for an extra flavor boost.
  • If you prefer a spicier kick, add some hot sauce or red pepper flakes to the sauce mixture.
  • These sliders can be made ahead of time and reheated in the oven just before serving. They also freeze well, so you can make a big batch and have them on hand for impromptu gatherings or quick snacks.

So there you have it - a recipe for bite-sized meatball sliders that will impress your guests and satisfy your cravings. The combination of juicy meatballs, gooey cheese, and tangy sauce, all sandwiched between soft slider buns, is simply irresistible. Prepare to be the star of your next gathering with these hearty appetizers that are sure to please everyone. Enjoy!
