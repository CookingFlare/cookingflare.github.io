Paleo Turkey Meatball Subs Recipes: 5 Delicious and Grain-Free Options


Looking for a delicious and healthy alternative to traditional meatball subs? Look no further! In this post, we’ll be sharing five mouthwatering Paleo turkey meatball subs recipes that are not only grain-free but also incredibly flavorful. Get ready to satisfy your cravings with these nutritious and easy-to-make options!

1. Classic Italian Turkey Meatball Sub

Prep Time: 15 minutes
Cook Time: 25 minutes
Total Time: 40 minutes
Servings: 4

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound ground turkey
  • 1/4 cup almond flour
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh basil
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1/2 teaspoon sea salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1 cup marinara sauce
  • 4 large lettuce leaves
  • 4 grain-free sub rolls

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C) and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
  2. In a large bowl, combine the ground turkey, almond flour, basil, parsley, garlic, oregano, salt, and black pepper. Mix well until all the ingredients are evenly incorporated.
  3. Shape the turkey mixture into meatballs, approximately 1 inch in diameter, and place them on the prepared baking sheet.
  4. Bake the meatballs for 20-25 minutes, or until cooked through and golden brown.
  5. While the meatballs are baking, heat the marinara sauce in a saucepan over medium heat until warmed through.
  6. To assemble the subs, place a lettuce leaf on each sub roll, followed by a generous amount of meatballs and marinara sauce.
  7. Serve hot and enjoy!

2. Spicy Mexican Turkey Meatball Sub

Prep Time: 20 minutes
Cook Time: 30 minutes
Total Time: 50 minutes
Servings: 4

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound ground turkey
  • 1/4 cup almond flour
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh cilantro
  • 1/4 cup diced red onion
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1/2 teaspoon chili powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon sea salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon cayenne pepper (optional)
  • 1 cup salsa
  • 4 large lettuce leaves
  • 4 grain-free sub rolls

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C) and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
  2. In a large bowl, combine the ground turkey, almond flour, cilantro, red onion, garlic, cumin, chili powder, paprika, sea salt, and cayenne pepper. Mix well until all the ingredients are evenly incorporated.
  3. Shape the turkey mixture into meatballs, approximately 1 inch in diameter, and place them on the prepared baking sheet.
  4. Bake the meatballs for 25-30 minutes, or until cooked through and slightly crispy on the outside.
  5. While the meatballs are baking, heat the salsa in a saucepan over medium heat until warmed through.
  6. To assemble the subs, place a lettuce leaf on each sub roll, followed by a generous amount of meatballs and salsa.
  7. Serve hot and enjoy!

4. Asian-Inspired Turkey Meatball Sub

Prep Time: 20 minutes
Cook Time: 30 minutes
Total Time: 50 minutes
Servings: 4

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound ground turkey
  • 1/4 cup almond flour
  • 1/4 cup chopped green onions
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon grated fresh ginger
  • 1 tablespoon coconut aminos
  • 1 tablespoon sesame oil
  • 1/2 teaspoon sea salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1/2 cup almond butter
  • 4 large lettuce leaves
  • 4 grain-free sub rolls

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C) and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
  2. In a large bowl, combine the ground turkey, almond flour, green onions, garlic, ginger, coconut aminos, sesame oil, salt, and black pepper. Mix well until all the ingredients are evenly incorporated.
  3. Shape the turkey mixture into meatballs, approximately 1 inch in diameter, and place them on the prepared baking sheet.
  4. Bake the meatballs for 25-30 minutes, or until cooked through and slightly crispy on the outside.
  5. While the meatballs are baking, heat the almond butter in a saucepan over low heat until melted and smooth.
  6. To assemble the subs, place a lettuce leaf on each sub roll, followed by a generous amount of meatballs and almond butter.
  7. Serve hot and enjoy!

5. Mediterranean Turkey Meatball Sub

Prep Time: 20 minutes
Cook Time: 30 minutes
Total Time: 50 minutes
Servings: 4

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound ground turkey
  • 1/4 cup almond flour
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley
  • 1/4 cup chopped sun-dried tomatoes
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1/2 teaspoon sea salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1/2 cup hummus
  • 4 large lettuce leaves
  • 4 grain-free sub rolls

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C) and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
  2. In a large bowl, combine the ground turkey, almond flour, parsley, sun-dried tomatoes, garlic, oregano, salt, and black pepper. Mix well until all the ingredients are evenly incorporated.
  3. Shape the turkey mixture into meatballs, approximately 1 inch in diameter, and place them on the prepared baking sheet.
  4. Bake the meatballs for 25-30 minutes, or until cooked through and slightly browned.
  5. While the meatballs are baking, heat the hummus in a saucepan over low heat until warmed through.
  6. To assemble the subs, place a lettuce leaf on each sub roll, followed by a generous amount of meatballs and hummus.
  7. Serve hot and enjoy!

These Paleo turkey meatball subs recipes are perfect for those following a grain-free or Paleo lifestyle, or anyone looking to enjoy a healthier version of a classic sandwich. Experiment with different flavors and toppings to make them your own. These delicious and nutritious subs are sure to become a family favorite!

Remember to always use high-quality ingredients and adjust the seasoning according to your taste preferences. Enjoy the flavors of these Paleo turkey meatball subs guilt-free, knowing that you’re nourishing your body with wholesome and delicious ingredients.
