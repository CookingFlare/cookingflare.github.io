One Pot Ratatouille Pasta


If you’re looking for a delicious and wholesome meal that’s easy to make and requires minimal cleanup, then this One Pot Ratatouille Pasta recipe is perfect for you. This dish combines the vibrant flavors of traditional ratatouille with the comfort of pasta, all cooked in one pot for added convenience. Whether you’re a busy parent, a student, or simply someone who loves a hearty and flavorful meal, this recipe is sure to become a favorite in your kitchen.

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 eggplant, diced
  • 1 zucchini, diced
  • 1 red bell pepper, diced
  • 1 yellow bell pepper, diced
  • 1 can (14 oz) diced tomatoes
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 8 oz penne pasta
  • 3 cups vegetable broth
  • Fresh basil, for garnish
  • Grated Parmesan cheese, for serving (optional)

Instructions

  1. Heat the olive oil in a large pot over medium heat. Add the diced onion and minced garlic, and sauté until the onion is soft and translucent.

  2. Add the diced eggplant, zucchini, and bell peppers to the pot. Cook, stirring occasionally, until the vegetables are slightly softened, about 5 minutes.

  3. Pour in the can of diced tomatoes, dried oregano, dried basil, salt, and pepper. Stir to combine all the ingredients.

  4. Add the penne pasta to the pot, followed by the vegetable broth. Stir gently to ensure the pasta is submerged in the liquid.

  5. Bring the mixture to a boil, then reduce the heat to a simmer. Cover the pot and let the pasta cook for about 15-20 minutes, or until the pasta is al dente and most of the liquid has been absorbed.

  6. Once the pasta is cooked, remove the pot from the heat. Taste and adjust the seasoning if needed.

  7. Serve the One Pot Ratatouille Pasta in bowls, garnished with fresh basil and a sprinkle of grated Parmesan cheese if desired.

Flavorful and Convenient

This One Pot Ratatouille Pasta is a celebration of fresh and vibrant ingredients, all cooked together to create a satisfying and flavorful dish. The combination of eggplant, zucchini, and bell peppers provides a hearty and nutritious base, while the herbs and tomatoes infuse the pasta with a rich and aromatic taste.

The best part of this recipe is the convenience of cooking everything in one pot. Not only does this save time on cleanup, but it also allows the flavors of the vegetables and pasta to meld together beautifully, creating a cohesive and delicious meal.

Versatile and Customizable

One of the great things about this recipe is its versatility. You can easily modify the ingredients to suit your preferences or make use of whatever you have on hand. For example, you could add mushrooms, spinach, or even chickpeas for added protein. Additionally, if you prefer a different type of pasta, feel free to swap the penne for your favorite shape.

Conclusion

This One Pot Ratatouille Pasta is a fantastic addition to your recipe repertoire, offering a satisfying and flavorful meal that’s both convenient and versatile. Whether you’re cooking for a family dinner or meal prepping for the week, this recipe is sure to impress with its vibrant colors and enticing aromas. Give it a try and enjoy the delightful flavors of this one pot wonder!
