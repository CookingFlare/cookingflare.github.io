Rich and Gooey S'mores Brownies: A Campfire-Inspired Dessert


There’s something truly magical about the combination of chocolate, marshmallows, and graham crackers. The classic campfire treat of s’mores has been enjoyed for generations, and today, we’re taking that beloved flavor combination and turning it into a decadent dessert: Rich and Gooey S’mores Brownies.

Ingredients

For the Brownie Base

  • 1 cup unsalted butter
  • 2 cups granulated sugar
  • 4 large eggs
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 3/4 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt

For the S’mores Topping

  • 1 1/2 cups semi-sweet chocolate chips
  • 1 1/2 cups mini marshmallows
  • 1 cup crushed graham crackers
  • 1/2 cup milk chocolate, melted (for drizzling)

Instructions

  1. Preheat and Prepare: Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C) and line a 9x13 inch baking pan with parchment paper, leaving an overhang on the sides to easily lift the brownies out later.

  2. Make the Brownie Batter: In a microwave-safe bowl, melt the butter in 30-second intervals until fully melted. Stir in the granulated sugar, then add the eggs and vanilla extract, mixing well. In a separate bowl, whisk together the flour, cocoa powder, and salt. Gradually add the dry ingredients to the wet ingredients, mixing until just combined. Be careful not to overmix.

  3. Bake the Brownies: Pour the brownie batter into the prepared pan and smooth the top with a spatula. Bake for 25-30 minutes, or until the edges are set and the center is just barely jiggly.

  4. Add the S’mores Topping: Remove the brownies from the oven and immediately sprinkle the semi-sweet chocolate chips over the top. Return the pan to the oven for 2-3 minutes to allow the chocolate chips to melt. Once melted, remove the brownies from the oven and sprinkle the mini marshmallows and crushed graham crackers over the top.

  5. Finish with a Drizzle: Drizzle the melted milk chocolate over the marshmallows and graham crackers, creating a beautiful swirl pattern.

  6. Chill and Serve: Allow the brownies to cool in the pan for at least 30 minutes, then transfer the whole pan to the refrigerator to chill for an additional 1-2 hours. Once fully chilled, use the parchment paper overhang to lift the brownies out of the pan and onto a cutting board. Cut into squares and serve.

Tips for Success

  • Don’t Overbake: It’s better to slightly underbake the brownies to achieve that rich and gooey texture. The residual heat will continue to cook the brownies as they cool.

  • Use Quality Chocolate: For the best flavor, use high-quality chocolate in both the brownie batter and the s’mores topping.

  • Variations: Get creative with your s’mores brownies by adding chopped nuts, a sprinkle of sea salt, or a swirl of peanut butter before baking.

The Perfect Union of Flavors

These Rich and Gooey S’mores Brownies are a delightful fusion of the beloved campfire treat and a classic dessert. The fudgy brownie base provides a rich foundation for the melty chocolate, pillowy marshmallows, and crunchy graham crackers. Whether you’re enjoying these brownies at a summer barbecue, a cozy winter gathering, or simply as a sweet treat for yourself, they are sure to bring joy with every bite.

So, gather your ingredients, preheat your oven, and get ready to savor the irresistible flavors of s’mores in a whole new way with these indulgent brownies.
