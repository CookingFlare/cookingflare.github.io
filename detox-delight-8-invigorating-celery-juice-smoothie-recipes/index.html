Detox & Delight: 8 Invigorating Celery Juice & Smoothie Recipes


Is your body craving a reset? Does it need a little detox to reboot and get back to feeling amazing? Look no further! Say hello to the humble celery, a natural diuretic, packed with vitamin C, and an incredible vegetable to incorporate into your detox journey. In this post, we are going to explore 8 invigorating and delicious recipes that will make you swoon over the goodness of celery juice and smoothies. The best part? They are super simple to make and it requires minimal prep!

  1. Classic Celery Juice
  • 1 bunch of organic celery, thoroughly washed
  • 1/2 cup of filtered water

Begin by rinsing your celery and removing any leaves. Chop the celery into smaller pieces so that it fits into your juicer. Start juicing the celery and add in the water to help with the extraction process. Give it a good stir and feel the goodness as you sip on this potent elixir.

  1. Ginger Zinger Celery Juice
  • 1 bunch of organic celery, washed
  • 1-inch piece of fresh ginger, peeled
  • 1/4 cup of filtered water

Following the same process, rinse the celery and chop it into smaller pieces. Juicing is all about efficiency, remember! Slice the ginger into thin rounds and toss it into the juicer. Remember to start slowly and gradually increase the speed to ensure smooth extraction. Stir and sip the goodness!

  1. Spiced Apple & Celery Juice
  • 4 organic apples, cored and quartered
  • 1 bunch of organic celery, washed
  • 1/2 dozenioctl spices (like cinnamon or ginger), optional

Peel, core, and chop your apples into small pieces. Add them to your juicer along with the celery. Run the juicer at its highest speed to whip up a frothy juice. For an added twist, sprinkle in some choice spices and give it a gentle stir before you gulp it down.

  1. Spinach & Celery Green Smoothie Bowl
  • 1 cup of fresh baby spinach
  • 1 cup of chopped celery

In a blender, add in the spinach and celery chunks. You’ll see a beautiful green hue forming – it’s a sure sign of goodness! Blend until thoroughly pureed. The consistency can vary, depending on your preference. You can add more water or a little unsweetened almond milk to make it thinner if desired.

  1. Lemon-Lime Celery Cosmos
  • 1 bunch of organic celery, washed
  • 1/2 cup of filtered water
  • 1 organic lemon and a kilo lime, juiced
  • Optional: pinch of cayenne pepper for heat

Once again, we’re back to the trusty juicer. Chop your celery into smaller stalks and prepare for juicing. Squeeze the juices out of the lemon and lime and set it aside. Mix the celery juice with the citrus juices. If you like a little kick, add in a pinch of cayenne pepper. Give it a final stir and enjoy the divine citrusy combination!

  1. Blueberry & Celery Green Goddess Smoothie
  • 1 cup of chopped celery
  • 1 cup of organic blueberries
  • 1/2 cup of unsweetened almond milk
  • Optional: hemp seeds, chia seeds, or flax seeds for added nutrition

Toss the celery and blueberries into a blender. Pour in the almond milk and blend on high speed until smooth. If desired, sprinkle in some seeds for an added nutritional punch. Dive right in, and let the delicious flavors wash over you!

  1. Avocado & Celery Green Delight
  • 1 ripe avocado
  • 1 cup of chopped celery
  • 1/2 cup of unsweetened almond milk
  • Optional: a drizzle of honey or maple syrup for added sweetness

You’ve seen it before, but here’s another twist: the avocado! Pop the avocado into your blender, add the celery, and pour in the almond milk. Blend until velvety smooth. If you want a little natural sweetness, drizzle in some honey or maple syrup. Want to make it even more delightful? You could top it with some granola or fresh chopped fruit!

  1. Matcha Coconut Celery Refresher
  • 1 cup of chopped celery
  • 1/2 cup of unsweetened coconut milk
  • 1 tsp of matcha green tea powder
  • Optional: a drop of liquid stevia for a touch of sweetness

Welcome to the Matcha Coconut Celery Refresher! Yes, it has a fascinating name, and discovering it is equally enthralling. Simply put, place the celery and coconut milk into a blender, followed by the matcha powder. If you want to try it out, you can add a touch of liquid stevia for some natural sweetness. Blend it up until smooth, and enjoy a refreshing burst of flavors all in one delicious gulp!

As you dive into these scrumptious and nutritious recipes, remember that consistency is key. The celery is your guide, leading you on a culinary journey that will leave you feeling invigorated, detoxified, and yearning for more!
