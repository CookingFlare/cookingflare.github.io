Buffalo Chicken Wrap: Spicy and Tangy


If you’re a fan of bold flavors and crave something satisfyingly spicy, look no further than the Buffalo Chicken Wrap. This delicious handheld delight combines tender chicken, zesty buffalo sauce, crisp vegetables, and creamy dressing all wrapped up in a soft tortilla. It’s perfect for lunch, dinner, or even a hearty snack on the go.

Ingredients

Here’s what you’ll need to whip up this mouthwatering Buffalo Chicken Wrap:

  • 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1/2 cup buffalo sauce (homemade or store-bought)
  • 4 large flour tortillas
  • 1 cup shredded lettuce
  • 1 cup diced tomatoes
  • 1/2 cup diced red onion
  • 1/2 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1/4 cup ranch or blue cheese dressing
  • Optional: sliced jalapeños for an extra kick

Instructions

Step 1: Cook the Chicken

  1. Prepare the Chicken: Season the chicken breasts with salt and pepper on both sides.

  2. Grill or Pan-Sear: Heat a grill pan or skillet over medium-high heat and cook the chicken breasts for about 6-7 minutes per side, or until fully cooked through and no longer pink in the center.

  3. Shred the Chicken: Once cooked, let the chicken rest for a few minutes, then shred it using two forks or chop it into bite-sized pieces.

Step 2: Toss with Buffalo Sauce

  1. Coat with Buffalo Sauce: In a mixing bowl, toss the shredded chicken with the buffalo sauce until evenly coated. Adjust the amount of sauce according to your desired level of spiciness.

Step 3: Assemble the Wraps

  1. Warm the Tortillas: Microwave the flour tortillas for about 10-15 seconds each to make them pliable.

  2. Layer the Ingredients: Lay out the tortillas on a clean surface. Divide the shredded lettuce, diced tomatoes, red onion, and shredded cheese evenly among the tortillas.

  3. Add Buffalo Chicken: Spoon the buffalo chicken mixture onto each tortilla, spreading it out evenly over the vegetables.

  4. Drizzle with Dressing: Drizzle ranch or blue cheese dressing over the chicken mixture. Add sliced jalapeños if desired for an extra spicy kick.

  5. Wrap it Up: Fold in the sides of each tortilla, then roll tightly from the bottom up, securing the filling inside.

Step 4: Serve and Enjoy

  1. Slice and Serve: Use a sharp knife to slice each wrap in half diagonally. Serve immediately while still warm and enjoy the delicious blend of flavors and textures.

Tips for Perfect Wraps

  • Tortilla Choice: Opt for large, soft flour tortillas for easier wrapping and a better texture.

  • Customize to Taste: Adjust the amount of buffalo sauce and dressing according to your preference for spiciness and creaminess.

  • Make Ahead: Prepare the buffalo chicken mixture ahead of time and assemble the wraps just before serving to keep the tortillas fresh and avoid sogginess.

  • Side Suggestions: Serve with a side of crispy french fries, coleslaw, or a fresh green salad to complement the flavors.

Final Thoughts

The Buffalo Chicken Wrap is a versatile and flavorful dish that never fails to satisfy cravings for something spicy and tangy. Whether you’re preparing it for a quick lunch or a casual dinner, its simple yet robust ingredients make it a favorite among spicy food enthusiasts. Experiment with additional toppings like avocado slices or cilantro for added freshness and complexity. With its combination of tender chicken, fiery buffalo sauce, and cool, creamy dressing, this wrap is sure to become a staple in your meal rotation. Enjoy the deliciousness and share the joy of homemade Buffalo Chicken Wraps with family and friends!
