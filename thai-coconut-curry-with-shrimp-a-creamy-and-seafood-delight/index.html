Thai Coconut Curry with Shrimp: A Creamy and Seafood Delight


If you’re a fan of Thai cuisine and enjoy the delicate flavors of seafood, then this Thai Coconut Curry with Shrimp recipe is a must-try. Bursting with creamy coconut milk, aromatic herbs, and succulent shrimp, this dish is a true delight for your taste buds. From the moment you take the first bite, you’ll be transported to the vibrant streets of Thailand.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound of fresh shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 2 tablespoons of vegetable oil
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 red bell pepper, thinly sliced
  • 1 tablespoon of red curry paste
  • 1 can (14 ounces) of coconut milk
  • 1 tablespoon of fish sauce
  • 1 tablespoon of brown sugar
  • 1 cup of chicken or vegetable broth
  • 1 cup of fresh basil leaves, torn
  • 1 lime, juiced
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Cooked jasmine rice, for serving

Instructions

  1. In a large skillet or wok, heat the vegetable oil over medium heat. Add the chopped onion and minced garlic, and sauté until fragrant and translucent.

  2. Add the sliced red bell pepper to the skillet and cook for a few minutes until slightly softened.

  3. Push the vegetables to one side of the skillet and add the red curry paste to the cleared area. Stir-fry the paste for about 1 minute to release its flavors.

  4. Pour in the coconut milk, fish sauce, and brown sugar. Stir well to combine all the ingredients and bring the mixture to a gentle simmer.

  5. Add the chicken or vegetable broth to the skillet and stir to incorporate. Allow the curry sauce to simmer for about 5 minutes, allowing the flavors to meld together.

  6. Gently place the shrimp into the skillet, making sure they are submerged in the curry sauce. Cook for approximately 3-4 minutes, or until the shrimp turn pink and opaque.

  7. Stir in the torn basil leaves and squeeze the lime juice over the curry. Season with salt and pepper to taste.

  8. Serve the Thai Coconut Curry with Shrimp over a bed of cooked jasmine rice. The fragrant rice will soak up the rich and creamy sauce, enhancing the overall experience.

Tips and Variations

  • Feel free to adjust the spiciness of the curry by adding more or less red curry paste according to your preference.
  • If you’re not a fan of shrimp, you can substitute it with other seafood such as scallops or fish fillets.
  • For a vegetarian option, omit the shrimp and replace it with your favorite vegetables like broccoli, carrots, or snap peas.
  • Garnish the dish with a sprinkle of fresh cilantro or sliced red chili for an extra burst of color and flavor.

Conclusion

This Thai Coconut Curry with Shrimp is a perfect harmony of creamy coconut milk, aromatic spices, and succulent seafood. The richness of the curry sauce, combined with the freshness of the shrimp, creates a dish that is both comforting and exotic. Whether you’re a seasoned Thai food lover or trying it for the first time, this recipe is sure to impress. So gather your ingredients, put on some relaxing Thai music, and let the flavors of Thailand transport you to a culinary paradise.
