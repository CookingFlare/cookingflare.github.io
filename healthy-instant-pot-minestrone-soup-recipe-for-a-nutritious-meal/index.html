Healthy Instant Pot Minestrone Soup Recipe for a Nutritious Meal


Are you looking for a delicious and nutritious meal that can be prepared in no time? Look no further! This Healthy Instant Pot Minestrone Soup recipe is packed with flavor, loaded with vegetables, and will leave you feeling satisfied and nourished. With the help of your trusty Instant Pot, you can have a hearty bowl of soup on the table in just a matter of minutes. So, let’s dive right in!

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 carrots, diced
  • 2 celery stalks, diced
  • 1 zucchini, diced
  • 1 cup green beans, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 1 can diced tomatoes
  • 4 cups vegetable broth
  • 1 can kidney beans, drained and rinsed
  • 1 can cannellini beans, drained and rinsed
  • 1 cup small pasta (e.g., macaroni or shells)
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley, chopped (for garnish)

Instructions

  1. Set your Instant Pot to the sauté function and heat the olive oil. Add the diced onion and minced garlic, and sauté until they become fragrant and translucent.

  2. Next, add the diced carrots, celery, zucchini, and green beans to the pot. Sauté for a few minutes until the vegetables start to soften.

  3. Add the diced tomatoes, vegetable broth, kidney beans, cannellini beans, dried basil, dried oregano, salt, and pepper to the pot. Give everything a good stir to combine.

  4. Close the lid of your Instant Pot and set the pressure release valve to the sealing position. Cook on high pressure for 5 minutes.

  5. Once the cooking time is up, do a quick release of the pressure by carefully turning the pressure release valve to the venting position. Be cautious of the hot steam.

  6. Open the lid of your Instant Pot and add the small pasta. Give it a stir to ensure the pasta is submerged in the liquid.

  7. Close the lid again and set the pressure release valve to the sealing position. Cook on high pressure for 4 minutes.

  8. When the cooking time is complete, allow for a natural pressure release for 5 minutes before carefully turning the pressure release valve to the venting position to release any remaining pressure.

  9. Open the lid and give the soup a good stir. Taste and adjust the seasoning if needed.

  10. Ladle the Healthy Instant Pot Minestrone Soup into bowls and garnish with fresh parsley.

Tips and Variations

  • Feel free to customize this Healthy Instant Pot Minestrone Soup recipe to your liking. You can add other vegetables like bell peppers, spinach, or kale to boost the nutritional value even further.
  • If you prefer a thicker soup, you can mash some of the beans with a fork before adding the pasta.
  • This recipe can easily be made vegan by using vegetable broth and omitting any cheese garnish.

Now, sit back, relax, and enjoy a bowl of this Healthy Instant Pot Minestrone Soup. It’s not only good for you but also incredibly delicious. Perfect for a quick weeknight meal or for meal prepping ahead of time. Give it a try and let us know how you like it!
