Soup-er Easy: Quick and Delicious Tomato Soup for Beginners


Are you ready to whip up a delicious and comforting bowl of tomato soup in no time? Look no further! This quick and easy tomato soup recipe is perfect for beginners and seasoned cooks alike. Whether you’re craving a warm bowl of soup on a chilly day or need a simple yet satisfying meal, this recipe has got you covered. Let’s dive into the simple steps to create a delightful tomato soup that will impress your taste buds and leave you wanting more.

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 medium onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can (28 ounces) whole peeled tomatoes
  • 1 cup vegetable broth
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1/4 cup heavy cream (optional)
  • Fresh basil leaves for garnish

Instructions

  1. Sauté the Aromatics: In a large pot, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the chopped onion and sauté until it becomes translucent, about 5 minutes. Then, add the minced garlic and continue to cook for another minute, stirring frequently.

  2. Simmer the Tomatoes: Pour the entire can of whole peeled tomatoes, including the juice, into the pot. Break up the tomatoes with a wooden spoon to release their juices. Allow the mixture to simmer for about 10-15 minutes, stirring occasionally.

  3. Blend the Mixture: Once the tomatoes have softened, carefully blend the mixture using an immersion blender until smooth. If you don’t have an immersion blender, you can transfer the mixture to a regular blender in batches and blend until smooth.

  4. Add the Broth and Seasoning: Pour in the vegetable broth and stir to combine. Season the soup with sugar, salt, and pepper to taste. Allow the soup to simmer for an additional 10 minutes to let the flavors meld together.

  5. Finish with Cream (Optional): If you prefer a creamier tomato soup, stir in the heavy cream until fully incorporated. This step is optional but adds a luxurious richness to the soup.

  6. Serve and Garnish: Ladle the tomato soup into bowls and garnish with fresh basil leaves for a pop of color and flavor. Pair it with your favorite crusty bread or a grilled cheese sandwich for a comforting meal.

Tips for Success

  • If you prefer a smoother consistency, you can strain the soup through a fine-mesh sieve after blending to remove any remaining tomato seeds or skin.
  • For an extra kick of flavor, consider adding a pinch of dried basil, oregano, or red pepper flakes to the soup while it simmers.

Why Tomato Soup?

Tomato soup is a classic comfort food that never goes out of style. It’s versatile, satisfying, and incredibly easy to make, making it a staple in many households. Whether you’re cooking for yourself, your family, or guests, a steaming bowl of tomato soup is sure to warm the soul and please the palate.

In Conclusion

With just a handful of simple ingredients and minimal effort, you can create a bowl of homemade tomato soup that rivals any gourmet version. This recipe is perfect for beginners looking to expand their culinary repertoire and for anyone seeking a quick, flavorful meal. So, the next time you’re in the mood for something comforting and delicious, give this easy tomato soup recipe a try. Your taste buds will thank you!
