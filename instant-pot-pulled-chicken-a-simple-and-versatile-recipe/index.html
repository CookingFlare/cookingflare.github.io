Instant Pot Pulled Chicken: A Simple and Versatile Recipe


When it comes to creating quick and delicious meals, the Instant Pot is a game-changer. One of the most popular dishes you can prepare with this handy kitchen appliance is pulled chicken. Whether you’re planning a weeknight dinner, meal prepping for the week, or hosting a gathering, Instant Pot pulled chicken is the answer. Tender, juicy, and bursting with flavor, this recipe is a must-have in every cook’s repertoire.

In this post, we’ll explore everything you need to know about making Instant Pot pulled chicken. From step-by-step instructions to tips for customization, you’ll have all the tools you need to make this dish a regular in your kitchen.

Why Use an Instant Pot for Pulled Chicken?

The Instant Pot has revolutionized the way we cook, offering a faster and more convenient method for preparing dishes that usually take longer. Pulled chicken, typically cooked low and slow, is perfect for the Instant Pot because it can go from frozen to fork-tender in a fraction of the time.

Here are some reasons why making pulled chicken in the Instant Pot is a fantastic idea:

  1. Speed: Traditional pulled chicken recipes can take several hours in a slow cooker or oven. The Instant Pot drastically reduces cooking time while still achieving that melt-in-your-mouth texture.

  2. Juiciness: The sealed cooking environment of the Instant Pot locks in moisture, ensuring the chicken stays juicy and flavorful.

  3. Hands-off cooking: Once you’ve added the ingredients and sealed the lid, the Instant Pot does the rest, allowing you to focus on other tasks.

  4. Versatility: Pulled chicken can be used in a variety of dishes, making it a versatile option for breakfast, lunch, or dinner.

Whether you’re making sandwiches, tacos, wraps, or a simple chicken salad, this Instant Pot pulled chicken recipe will be the foundation of countless meal options.

Ingredients for Instant Pot Pulled Chicken

Before we dive into the cooking process, let’s go over the ingredients you’ll need. The beauty of this recipe is that it uses simple pantry staples, and you can easily adjust the flavors based on your preferences.

  • Chicken breasts or thighs: Both cuts work well, but chicken thighs tend to be juicier and more forgiving. If you’re using frozen chicken, no worries—the Instant Pot can handle that too.

  • Chicken broth or water: Liquid is essential for the Instant Pot to come to pressure. Broth adds a rich, savory flavor to the chicken.

  • Spices and seasonings: For a basic pulled chicken, you’ll need garlic powder, onion powder, paprika, salt, and pepper. You can add more flavor by using chili powder, cumin, or even a dash of cayenne for heat.

  • Barbecue sauce (optional): If you’re making pulled chicken sandwiches or tacos, barbecue sauce is a must-have. You can use store-bought or homemade.

How to Make Instant Pot Pulled Chicken

Now that we’ve covered the ingredients, it’s time to get cooking. Follow these simple steps to make your own Instant Pot pulled chicken.

Step 1: Prepare the Chicken

Start by placing the chicken breasts or thighs in the bottom of the Instant Pot. If you’re using frozen chicken, don’t worry about thawing it first—the Instant Pot will cook it perfectly.

Step 2: Add Liquid and Seasonings

Pour in the chicken broth or water. This is crucial because the Instant Pot requires liquid to build pressure. Then, sprinkle your chosen spices over the chicken. You can go with a simple combination of salt, pepper, garlic powder, and onion powder, or add more complex seasonings like smoked paprika, cumin, or chili powder for added depth.

If you want to make the pulled chicken even more flavorful, consider adding a splash of apple cider vinegar or a tablespoon of honey to balance out the savory and sweet notes.

Step 3: Seal the Instant Pot and Set Cooking Time

Close the Instant Pot lid, ensuring the valve is set to “sealing.” Set the Instant Pot to Manual or Pressure Cook on high pressure for 10-12 minutes if using chicken breasts, or 12-15 minutes for chicken thighs. If your chicken is frozen, increase the cooking time by 2-3 minutes.

Step 4: Natural Release

Once the cooking time is up, allow the Instant Pot to naturally release pressure for 5-10 minutes. This step helps keep the chicken moist and tender. Afterward, you can carefully switch the valve to “venting” to release any remaining pressure.

Step 5: Shred the Chicken

Once the pressure has fully released, remove the chicken from the pot. It should be tender enough to shred with just a fork. If you’re having trouble, use two forks to pull apart the chicken into juicy, tender strands.

If you’d like, you can return the shredded chicken to the pot with the remaining cooking liquid for extra flavor. You can also stir in some barbecue sauce at this point if you’re planning to use the chicken for sandwiches or tacos.

Tips for Perfect Pulled Chicken

  • Use bone-in chicken for extra flavor: If you want even more depth of flavor, consider using bone-in chicken thighs or breasts. The bones add richness to the broth and result in even juicier chicken.

  • Make it spicy: If you like heat, add a pinch of cayenne pepper or a few dashes of hot sauce to the cooking liquid. You can also stir in some chopped jalapeños or chipotle peppers for a smoky kick.

  • Custom sauces: While barbecue sauce is a classic pairing for pulled chicken, feel free to experiment with other sauces. A buffalo sauce, honey mustard, or teriyaki sauce can create unique variations that are equally delicious.

  • Shredding tools: If you have a hand mixer or stand mixer, you can use it to shred the chicken quickly. Just place the cooked chicken in a large bowl and mix on low speed for a few seconds. It’s a fast and easy way to get perfectly shredded chicken.

  • Freezer-friendly: Pulled chicken is an excellent dish for meal prepping. Once the chicken is shredded, you can portion it out and freeze it for later use. Simply store the chicken in an airtight container or freezer bag, and it will keep for up to 3 months. When ready to use, defrost and reheat on the stove or in the microwave.

Creative Ways to Use Pulled Chicken

Now that you’ve got your perfectly shredded pulled chicken, let’s talk about all the ways you can enjoy it. This versatile dish can be used in so many different meals, making it perfect for meal prep or a quick dinner. Here are some creative ideas for using your Instant Pot pulled chicken:

1. Pulled Chicken Sandwiches

One of the most popular ways to enjoy pulled chicken is in a sandwich. Simply pile the chicken on a soft bun and drizzle with barbecue sauce. Add some coleslaw for a tangy crunch, or pickles for a burst of acidity.

2. Pulled Chicken Tacos

Fill soft corn or flour tortillas with shredded pulled chicken, and top with your favorite taco fixings. Sautéed peppers, fresh cilantro, lime wedges, and a drizzle of sour cream make for a perfect taco combination.

3. Pulled Chicken Salads

For a lighter option, use the pulled chicken as a topping for salads. Combine it with greens, avocado, cherry tomatoes, and a tangy vinaigrette dressing. You can even add some cheese or crispy tortilla strips for extra texture.

4. Pulled Chicken Wraps

Wrap the pulled chicken in a whole wheat or spinach tortilla for a delicious, portable lunch or dinner. Add vegetables, cheese, and a creamy dressing or salsa for added flavor.

5. Pulled Chicken Pizza

Top your pizza dough with pulled chicken, barbecue sauce, red onions, and cheese for a fun twist on the classic. You can even add cilantro after baking for a fresh touch.

6. Pulled Chicken Nachos

Spread tortilla chips on a baking sheet, and top with pulled chicken, shredded cheese, and jalapeños. Bake until the cheese melts, then drizzle with sour cream and guacamole for a game-day favorite.

7. Pulled Chicken Bowls

For a hearty, satisfying meal, serve your pulled chicken over a base of rice, quinoa, or cauliflower rice. Top with roasted vegetables, avocado, and a drizzle of your favorite sauce for a nutritious bowl.

Conclusion

Instant Pot pulled chicken is one of those recipes that will quickly become a go-to in your kitchen. Its tender texture, versatility, and ease of preparation make it the perfect meal for any occasion. Whether you’re making sandwiches, tacos, or wraps, this pulled chicken is sure to impress.

Don’t forget that this recipe is highly customizable—feel free to adjust the seasonings, add extra spices, or top with your favorite sauces to make it uniquely yours. With the Instant Pot at your side, you’ll have a delicious meal on the table in no time.

Give this Instant Pot pulled chicken recipe a try and watch as it becomes a family favorite that you can enjoy in countless ways!
