Roasted Garlic and Herb Mashed Potatoes


Who doesn’t love a comforting bowl of creamy mashed potatoes? This classic side dish is a staple at family gatherings, holiday feasts, and weeknight dinners. Elevate your mashed potatoes game with this mouthwatering recipe for Roasted Garlic and Herb Mashed Potatoes. The combination of roasted garlic and fragrant herbs takes this dish to a whole new level of flavor. Let’s dive into the recipe and create a dish that will have everyone coming back for seconds!

Ingredients:

  • 2 pounds of Yukon Gold potatoes, peeled and cut into chunks
  • 1 head of garlic
  • 1/4 cup of unsalted butter
  • 1/2 cup of milk
  • 1/4 cup of sour cream
  • 1 teaspoon of fresh thyme, chopped
  • 1 teaspoon of fresh rosemary, chopped
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Roast the Garlic:

    • Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C).
    • Cut the top off the head of garlic to expose the cloves.
    • Drizzle the garlic with olive oil, wrap it in foil, and roast for about 30-40 minutes until the cloves are soft and caramelized. Allow it to cool.

  2. Cook the Potatoes:

    • In a large pot, cover the potato chunks with cold water and add a generous pinch of salt.
    • Bring the water to a boil, then reduce the heat and simmer for 15-20 minutes until the potatoes are fork-tender. Drain well.

  3. Prepare the Mixture:

    • In a small saucepan, melt the butter over low heat.
    • Squeeze the roasted garlic cloves into the melted butter and mash them into a paste.
    • Stir in the chopped thyme and rosemary, then remove from heat.

  4. Mash the Potatoes:

    • Using a potato masher or a fork, mash the cooked potatoes in a large bowl until smooth.
    • Pour in the butter-garlic-herb mixture, milk, and sour cream.
    • Continue mashing and stirring until everything is well combined and creamy.

  5. Season and Serve:

    • Season the mashed potatoes with salt and pepper to taste.
    • Transfer to a serving bowl and garnish with additional herbs if desired.

Tips for Success:

  • For extra creamy mashed potatoes, use a potato ricer or a food mill to mash the cooked potatoes.
  • Adjust the amount of milk and sour cream based on your desired consistency.
  • Don’t skip roasting the garlic; it adds a rich, sweet flavor to the dish.

Final Thoughts:

Roasted Garlic and Herb Mashed Potatoes are a crowd-pleaser that pairs perfectly with roasted meats, grilled vegetables, or simply enjoyed on their own. The aromatic blend of garlic and herbs will fill your kitchen with an irresistible aroma, making it hard to resist a taste before serving. Whether you’re hosting a dinner party or craving a cozy night in, this recipe is sure to become a favorite in your culinary repertoire. Treat your taste buds to a bowl of these creamy, flavorful mashed potatoes – you won’t be disappointed!

Enjoy creating and savoring this delightful dish with your loved ones. Happy cooking!
