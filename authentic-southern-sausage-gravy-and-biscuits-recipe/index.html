Authentic Southern Sausage Gravy and Biscuits Recipe


If you’re looking for a hearty and delicious breakfast or brunch option, look no further than this Authentic Southern Sausage Gravy and Biscuits recipe. This classic comfort food dish is a staple in the Southern United States, known for its rich flavors and satisfying taste. With a creamy gravy made from flavorful sausage and served over flaky homemade biscuits, this dish will surely become a favorite in your household.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground pork sausage
  • 1/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • 3 cups whole milk
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • 8 homemade biscuits (recipe below)

Instructions

  1. In a large skillet, cook the ground pork sausage over medium heat until browned and cooked through. Use a wooden spoon to break up the sausage into small crumbles.

  2. Once the sausage is cooked, sprinkle the flour evenly over the sausage in the skillet. Stir well to coat the sausage in the flour, allowing it to cook for about a minute.

  3. Slowly pour in the milk while stirring continuously. This will help prevent lumps from forming in the gravy. Continue stirring until the mixture comes to a simmer.

  4. Reduce the heat to low and let the gravy simmer for about 5-10 minutes, or until it thickens to your desired consistency. Stir occasionally to prevent sticking.

  5. Season the gravy with salt and pepper to taste. Be sure to taste and adjust the seasoning as needed, as the sausage may already be seasoned.

  6. While the gravy simmers, prepare the homemade biscuits.

Homemade Biscuits

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tablespoon baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, cold and cubed
  • 3/4 cup whole milk

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 450°F (230°C).

  2. In a large bowl, whisk together the flour, baking powder, and salt.

  3. Add the cold, cubed butter to the flour mixture. Using a pastry cutter or your fingertips, cut the butter into the flour until it resembles coarse crumbs.

  4. Pour in the milk and stir until the dough comes together.

  5. Turn the dough out onto a floured surface and gently knead it a few times to bring it together.

  6. Roll out the dough to a thickness of about 1/2 inch. Use a biscuit cutter to cut out biscuits and place them on a baking sheet.

  7. Bake the biscuits for 10-12 minutes, or until they are golden brown.

Serving

To serve, split the homemade biscuits in half and place them on a plate. Ladle a generous amount of the sausage gravy over the biscuits, allowing it to soak into the flaky layers. Serve immediately and enjoy!

Conclusion

This Authentic Southern Sausage Gravy and Biscuits recipe is a true taste of the South, bringing together the comforting flavors of sausage gravy and homemade biscuits. Whether you’re enjoying it for breakfast or brunch, this dish is sure to satisfy your cravings and leave you feeling warm and satisfied. Give it a try and experience the deliciousness of Southern comfort food in your own kitchen.
