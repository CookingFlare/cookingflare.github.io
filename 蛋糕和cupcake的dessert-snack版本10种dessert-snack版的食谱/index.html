蛋糕和cupcake的dessert snack版本：10种dessert snack版的食谱


If you’re a dessert lover like me, you’re always on the lookout for new and exciting ways to enjoy your favorite treats. That’s why I’ve put together a collection of 10 delightful dessert snack versions of egg cakes and cupcakes that are sure to satisfy your sweet tooth and leave you craving for more.

1. Mini Lemon Meringue Egg Cake Bites

These delightful little treats are bursting with zesty lemon flavor. The fluffy egg cake base is topped with a dollop of sweet and tangy lemon curd and a cloud of lightly toasted meringue. They are the perfect bite-sized indulgence for any occasion.

2. Chocolate Hazelnut Cupcake Truffles

Indulge in the rich and decadent flavors of chocolate and hazelnut with these delightful cupcake truffles. Crumbled chocolate cupcakes are mixed with creamy hazelnut spread, rolled into bite-sized balls, and coated in a layer of smooth dark chocolate. These truffles are an irresistible treat for any chocolate lover.

3. Raspberry Cheesecake Egg Cake Bars

Combine the creamy richness of cheesecake with the light and airy texture of egg cakes in these delightful bars. A buttery egg cake base is topped with a layer of creamy raspberry cheesecake filling and finished with a swirl of tart raspberry coulis. These bars are a perfect balance of sweet and tangy flavors.

4. Salted Caramel Cupcake Pops

Take your cupcake game to the next level with these adorable and delicious cupcake pops. Moist vanilla cupcakes are crumbled and mixed with silky salted caramel sauce, then formed into bite-sized pops, dipped in creamy white chocolate, and finished with a sprinkle of sea salt. These pops are a delightful combination of sweet and salty flavors.

5. Matcha Green Tea Egg Cake Sandwiches

For a unique twist on the classic egg cake, try these matcha green tea egg cake sandwiches. Soft and fluffy matcha-flavored egg cakes are sandwiched together with a layer of sweet red bean paste, creating a delightful fusion of flavors and textures. These sandwiches are a perfect choice for tea time or a light afternoon snack.

6. Red Velvet Cupcake Whoopie Pies

Experience the allure of red velvet cupcakes in a fun and portable whoopie pie form. Soft and velvety red velvet cake rounds are sandwiched together with a luscious cream cheese filling, creating a handheld treat that’s perfect for sharing or enjoying on the go.

7. Blueberry Lemon Egg Cake Parfaits

Layered desserts are always a hit, and these blueberry lemon egg cake parfaits are no exception. Tender egg cake cubes are layered with creamy lemon custard, fresh blueberries, and a sprinkle of crunchy granola, creating a delightful parfait that’s as beautiful as it is delicious.

8. Peanut Butter Cup Cupcake Brownie Bites

If you’re a fan of the classic combination of chocolate and peanut butter, these brownie bites are a must-try. Rich and fudgy brownie bites are topped with a dollop of creamy peanut butter frosting and a mini peanut butter cup, creating a bite-sized indulgence that’s perfect for any chocolate and peanut butter lover.

9. Mango Coconut Egg Cake Tarts

Transport your taste buds to the tropics with these delightful mango coconut egg cake tarts. Buttery egg cake tarts are filled with a luscious mango coconut custard, topped with a dollop of whipped cream, and finished with a sprinkle of toasted coconut. These tarts are a refreshing and tropical treat that’s perfect for warm weather.

10. Cookies and Cream Cupcake Ice Cream Sandwiches

Combine the best of both worlds with these indulgent ice cream sandwiches. Chocolate cupcakes are transformed into soft and chewy cookies, which are then sandwiched together with a generous scoop of cookies and cream ice cream. These sandwiches are the perfect way to enjoy the classic combination of cupcakes and ice cream in a fun and portable format.

I hope you enjoy trying out these delightful dessert snack versions of egg cakes and cupcakes. Whether you’re hosting a party, looking for a sweet treat to enjoy with your afternoon tea, or simply craving something indulgent, these recipes are sure to delight your taste buds and satisfy your cravings. Happy baking and snacking!
