Easy Thanksgiving Desserts for a Sweet Ending


Thanksgiving is all about giving thanks, spending time with loved ones, and enjoying delicious food. And what better way to end your Thanksgiving feast than with a mouthwatering dessert that will leave your guests craving for more? If you’re looking for easy-to-make yet impressive desserts to sweeten the end of your Thanksgiving celebration, look no further! We’ve curated a list of delightful and simple dessert recipes that will surely be the highlight of your holiday spread.

Pumpkin Pie Bites

Ingredients:

  • 1 can of pumpkin puree
  • 1/2 cup of sugar
  • 1 teaspoon of cinnamon
  • 1/2 teaspoon of nutmeg
  • 1/4 teaspoon of cloves
  • 1/4 teaspoon of ginger
  • 1/2 teaspoon of salt
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 can of refrigerated crescent dough
  • Whipped cream (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C) and grease a mini muffin tin.
  2. In a bowl, mix together the pumpkin puree, sugar, spices, salt, and eggs until well combined.
  3. Roll out the crescent dough and cut it into small squares. Press each square into a muffin cup.
  4. Fill each dough cup with the pumpkin mixture.
  5. Bake for 12-15 minutes or until the dough is golden brown.
  6. Let the pumpkin pie bites cool before serving. Top with whipped cream if desired.

Apple Crisp

Ingredients:

  • 4-5 medium apples, peeled and sliced
  • 1/2 cup of flour
  • 1/2 cup of oats
  • 1/2 cup of brown sugar
  • 1/4 cup of white sugar
  • 1 teaspoon of cinnamon
  • 1/4 teaspoon of salt
  • 1/2 cup of butter, softened

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C) and grease a baking dish.
  2. Place the sliced apples in the baking dish.
  3. In a separate bowl, mix the flour, oats, sugars, cinnamon, and salt.
  4. Add the softened butter to the dry ingredients and mix until crumbly.
  5. Sprinkle the crumb mixture over the apples.
  6. Bake for 30-35 minutes or until the topping is golden brown and the apples are tender.
  7. Serve warm with a scoop of vanilla ice cream.

Pecan Pie Bars

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup of flour
  • 1/2 cup of powdered sugar
  • 1/2 cup of butter, softened
  • 1 cup of brown sugar
  • 1/2 cup of corn syrup
  • 1/4 cup of butter, melted
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 teaspoon of vanilla extract
  • 1 1/2 cups of chopped pecans

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C) and grease a baking dish.
  2. In a bowl, mix the flour, powdered sugar, and softened butter.
  3. Press the mixture into the bottom of the baking dish to form the crust.
  4. Bake the crust for 15-20 minutes until lightly golden.
  5. In another bowl, combine the brown sugar, corn syrup, melted butter, eggs, and vanilla extract.
  6. Stir in the chopped pecans.
  7. Pour the pecan mixture over the baked crust.
  8. Bake for an additional 25-30 minutes or until set.
  9. Let it cool before cutting into bars.

These easy Thanksgiving desserts are sure to impress your guests and leave them with a sweet ending to the holiday meal. Whether you prefer the classic flavors of pumpkin and apple or the nutty goodness of pecans, these recipes are perfect for celebrating gratitude and togetherness. Try them out this Thanksgiving and watch as your loved ones indulge in these delectable treats. Happy baking!

Remember, the joy of Thanksgiving lies not only in the food but also in the memories created and shared around the table. So, savor every moment and every bite of these delightful desserts. Wishing you a warm and wonderful Thanksgiving filled with love, laughter, and lots of sweetness!
