Allergy-Friendly Rice Krispie Treats: A Fun and Delicious Snack


If you have food allergies or sensitivities, it can sometimes be challenging to find tasty treats that you can enjoy without worry. That’s why I’m excited to share this recipe for Allergy-Friendly Rice Krispie Treats with you today! These treats are not only delicious, but they are also free from common allergens like dairy, gluten, and nuts. Whether you have allergies or not, these treats are sure to be a hit with everyone!

Ingredients

  • 4 cups of allergy-friendly crispy rice cereal
  • 1/4 cup of allergy-friendly margarine
  • 1 package (10 ounces) of allergy-friendly marshmallows
  • 1 teaspoon of vanilla extract
  • A pinch of salt

Instructions

  1. In a large saucepan, melt the margarine over medium heat. Make sure to use an allergy-friendly margarine that suits your dietary needs.

  2. Once the margarine is melted, add the marshmallows to the saucepan. Stir constantly until the marshmallows are completely melted and well combined with the margarine. This should take about 5 minutes.

  3. Remove the saucepan from heat and stir in the vanilla extract and salt. Mix well to ensure the flavors are evenly distributed.

  4. Add the crispy rice cereal to the saucepan and gently fold it into the marshmallow mixture until all the cereal is coated.

  5. Transfer the mixture into a greased 9x13-inch baking dish and press it down firmly using a greased spatula or your hands. Make sure to spread it evenly across the dish.

  6. Allow the treats to cool and set for at least 1 hour. This will ensure that they hold their shape when cut into squares.

  7. Once cooled, cut the treats into squares of your desired size. You can make them smaller for bite-sized treats or larger for a more substantial snack.

Tip: To prevent the treats from sticking to the knife while cutting, you can grease the knife with a little margarine or cooking spray.

  1. Serve and enjoy! These Allergy-Friendly Rice Krispie Treats are perfect for parties, lunchboxes, or any time you’re craving a sweet and chewy snack.

Variations

Feel free to get creative with your Allergy-Friendly Rice Krispie Treats! Here are a few ideas to inspire you:

  • Add dairy-free chocolate chips, dried fruit, or allergy-friendly sprinkles to the mixture before pressing it into the baking dish for an extra burst of flavor and fun.

  • Drizzle melted dairy-free chocolate or allergy-friendly caramel sauce over the cooled treats for an indulgent twist.

  • Swap out the crispy rice cereal for a gluten-free alternative like puffed quinoa or gluten-free oats for a different texture.

  • Experiment with different flavors of allergy-friendly marshmallows, such as strawberry or vanilla, to add a unique twist to your treats.

No matter how you choose to customize your Allergy-Friendly Rice Krispie Treats, they are sure to be a crowd-pleaser. These fun and delicious snacks are not only allergy-friendly but also easy to make and enjoy. So go ahead, indulge in a treat that’s safe and satisfying for everyone!
