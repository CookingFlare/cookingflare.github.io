Indulgent S'mores Brownies: A Campfire Classic Reinvented


When you think of summer nights, there’s a good chance that the aroma of campfire smoke and the sweet scent of melting chocolate wafting through the air come to mind. S’mores, the quintessential campfire treat made of graham crackers, chocolate, and gooey marshmallows, have been a beloved classic for generations. But what if we told you that you could take this nostalgic favorite and elevate it into a decadent dessert? Welcome to the world of indulgent s’mores brownies – a delightful fusion that combines the rich, fudgy goodness of brownies with the iconic flavors of s’mores.

The Perfect Blend of Flavors and Textures

Imagine sinking your teeth into a warm, chocolatey brownie, only to be greeted by the melty marshmallow and rich chocolate layers reminiscent of your favorite campfire treat. The contrast between the dense, fudgy brownie base and the light, airy marshmallow creates a delightful texture that will leave you craving more. Each bite is a celebration of flavors that transport you back to those starry nights spent around the campfire, sharing stories and laughter with friends and family.

Ingredients You’ll Need

Creating these indulgent s’mores brownies is simpler than you might think! Here are the ingredients you’ll need:

For the Brownies:

  • 1 cup (2 sticks) unsalted butter – The foundation of any great brownie is rich, creamy butter.
  • 2 cups granulated sugar – Sweetness is key for a perfect brownie texture.
  • 4 large eggs – Eggs provide structure and moisture.
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract – A subtle hint of vanilla enhances the chocolate flavor.
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour – The base of the brownie that binds everything together.
  • 1 cup unsweetened cocoa powder – For that deep chocolate flavor.
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder – To give the brownies a light lift.
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt – Balances the sweetness.

For the S’mores Topping:

  • 1 cup mini marshmallows – These will melt beautifully over the brownies.
  • 1 cup chocolate chips – Choose semi-sweet or milk chocolate for a creamy finish.
  • 1 cup crushed graham crackers – Adds that unmistakable s’mores crunch.
  • 1/4 cup melted butter – Helps bind the graham cracker crust.

Kitchen Tools You’ll Need

Before you start mixing your ingredients, gather the following kitchen tools to make your baking experience smooth:

  • Mixing bowls
  • Whisk or electric mixer
  • Spatula
  • 9x13-inch baking pan
  • Parchment paper (optional, for easy removal of brownies)
  • Oven mitts

Step-by-Step Instructions

Now that you have all your ingredients and tools ready, let’s dive into the preparation of these indulgent s’mores brownies.

Step 1: Preheat Your Oven

Begin by preheating your oven to 350°F (175°C). This ensures that your brownies bake evenly and come out perfectly fudgy.

Step 2: Prepare Your Baking Pan

Line a 9x13-inch baking pan with parchment paper, leaving some overhang on the sides. This will make it easy to lift the brownies out once they are baked. If you don’t have parchment paper, simply grease the pan with butter or cooking spray.

Step 3: Make the Brownie Batter

  1. In a large mixing bowl, melt the butter in the microwave or on the stovetop until fully liquefied. Allow it to cool slightly.

  2. Add the granulated sugar to the melted butter and whisk until combined. The mixture should be smooth and glossy.

  3. Next, add the eggs one at a time, mixing well after each addition. Stir in the vanilla extract.

  4. In a separate bowl, sift together the flour, cocoa powder, baking powder, and salt. Gradually add the dry ingredients to the wet mixture, stirring until just combined. Be careful not to overmix, as this could lead to tough brownies.

Step 4: Bake the Brownies

Pour the brownie batter into the prepared baking pan and spread it evenly using a spatula. Bake in the preheated oven for 25 to 30 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out with a few moist crumbs. Keep in mind that overbaking will lead to dry brownies, so check them a couple of minutes early.

Step 5: Prepare the S’mores Topping

While the brownies are baking, you can prepare the s’mores topping. In a medium bowl, combine the crushed graham crackers and melted butter, mixing until the crumbs are well coated.

Step 6: Add the Toppings

Once the brownies are done baking, remove them from the oven and sprinkle chocolate chips evenly over the top. Follow with a generous layer of mini marshmallows, and finally, add the graham cracker mixture on top.

Step 7: Broil for That Perfect Finish

Place the brownies under the broiler for about 1-2 minutes, watching closely to ensure the marshmallows toast and become golden brown. This step adds that signature campfire flavor, mimicking the experience of roasting marshmallows over an open flame.

Step 8: Let It Cool

Remove the brownies from the oven and let them cool in the pan for about 10-15 minutes. Once cooled, use the parchment paper to lift them out of the pan and onto a cutting board. Allow them to cool completely before slicing into squares.

Serving Suggestions

These indulgent s’mores brownies can be served in various ways to enhance your dessert experience:

  • Warm and Gooey: Serve them warm right out of the oven with a scoop of vanilla ice cream on top. The heat from the brownies will melt the ice cream, creating a deliciously gooey dessert.

  • Drizzled with Chocolate Sauce: For an extra layer of indulgence, drizzle some chocolate sauce over the top before serving. You can even use caramel sauce for a unique twist!

  • Add Fresh Berries: Fresh strawberries or raspberries add a refreshing contrast to the rich brownies. Simply scatter them on top or serve alongside for a pop of color and flavor.

Storing Your Brownies

If you have any leftovers (which is a big “if” with these delicious treats!), store them in an airtight container at room temperature for up to three days. For longer storage, place them in the refrigerator for up to a week. You can also freeze the brownies for up to three months. Just be sure to wrap them tightly in plastic wrap and then place them in a freezer-safe bag.

Conclusion

Indulgent s’mores brownies are a delightful way to reinvent a classic campfire treat. With their rich chocolate flavor, gooey marshmallow topping, and crunchy graham cracker crust, they are perfect for summer gatherings, cozy family nights, or simply satisfying that sweet tooth. So, gather your loved ones, whip up a batch, and let the nostalgia of summer campfires come flooding back with every delicious bite.

Whether you’re an experienced baker or a novice in the kitchen, these brownies are sure to impress and bring smiles to those you share them with. So go ahead, indulge in this delightful dessert, and make your next gathering a little sweeter with these irresistible s’mores brownies!
