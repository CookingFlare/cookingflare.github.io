Instant Pot Lentil Soup Recipe: A Nutritious and Filling Option

Are you looking for a delicious and healthy meal that can be prepared in no time? Look no further than this Instant Pot Lentil Soup recipe. Packed with nutrients and bursting with flavor, this soup is the perfect option for a quick and nutritious meal. Ingredients 1 cup dried lentils 1 onion, diced 2 carrots, chopped 2 celery stalks, diced 3 cloves of garlic, minced 1 teaspoon cumin 1 teaspoon paprika 1 bay leaf 4 cups vegetable broth Salt and pepper to taste Instructions Rinse the lentils under cold water and set them aside. Turn on the Instant Pot and select the sauté function. Add a drizzle of olive oil and sauté the onion, carrots, celery, and garlic until they become tender and fragrant. Add the cumin, paprika, and bay leaf to the Instant Pot and stir well to combine the flavors. Pour in the vegetable broth and add the rinsed lentils. Give it a good stir and season with salt and pepper to taste. Secure the lid of the Instant Pot and set the pressure release valve to the sealing position. Select the manual or pressure cook function and set the timer for 15 minutes. Once the cooking time is complete, allow the pressure to release naturally for about 10 minutes. Then, carefully turn the pressure release valve to the venting position to release any remaining pressure. Open the Instant Pot lid and give the soup a stir. The lentils should be tender and the flavors well combined. Serve the soup hot and garnish with fresh herbs, such as parsley or cilantro, if desired. Why Choose Instant Pot Lentil Soup? Not only is this Instant Pot Lentil Soup recipe incredibly easy to make, but it also offers a host of health benefits.
