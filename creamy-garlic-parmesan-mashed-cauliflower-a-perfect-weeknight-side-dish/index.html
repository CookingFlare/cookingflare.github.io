Creamy Garlic Parmesan Mashed Cauliflower: A Perfect Weeknight Side Dish


Are you looking for a delicious and healthy alternative to mashed potatoes? Look no further than this creamy garlic parmesan mashed cauliflower recipe. It’s a perfect weeknight side dish that is not only easy to make but also incredibly flavorful. Whether you’re trying to cut back on carbs or simply looking to add more veggies to your diet, this dish is sure to become a new favorite in your meal rotation.

Ingredients

  • 1 head of cauliflower, cut into florets
  • 4 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1/4 cup of grated parmesan cheese
  • 2 tablespoons of butter
  • 1/4 cup of heavy cream
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh chives for garnish (optional)

Instructions

  1. Start by steaming the cauliflower florets until they are tender. This should take about 10-15 minutes.

  2. While the cauliflower is steaming, melt the butter in a small saucepan over medium heat. Add the minced garlic and sauté for 1-2 minutes until fragrant.

  3. Once the cauliflower is tender, transfer it to a food processor. Add the sautéed garlic, parmesan cheese, and heavy cream.

  4. Blend the mixture until smooth and creamy. If you prefer a chunkier texture, you can use a potato masher instead of a food processor.

  5. Season the mashed cauliflower with salt and pepper to taste. Give it a good stir to ensure the seasonings are well incorporated.

  6. Transfer the mashed cauliflower to a serving dish and garnish with fresh chives if desired.

Why Mashed Cauliflower?

Mashed cauliflower is a fantastic alternative to traditional mashed potatoes for several reasons. Cauliflower is low in carbs and calories, making it an excellent choice for anyone following a low-carb or keto diet. Additionally, cauliflower is rich in vitamins, minerals, and antioxidants, making it a nutritious addition to your meal.

Flavor Variations

One of the great things about this recipe is that it’s incredibly versatile. You can easily customize the flavors to suit your preferences. Here are a few variations to consider:

  • Herb Infused: Add a sprinkle of fresh or dried herbs such as thyme, rosemary, or parsley for a fragrant and herbaceous twist.

  • Spicy Kick: For those who enjoy a bit of heat, add a pinch of red pepper flakes or a dash of hot sauce to the mashed cauliflower.

  • Cheesy Bacon: Amp up the savory goodness by mixing in crispy bacon bits and extra shredded cheese.

Serving Suggestions

Creamy garlic parmesan mashed cauliflower pairs beautifully with a wide range of main dishes. Whether you’re serving it alongside a juicy steak, roasted chicken, or grilled fish, it’s sure to be a hit. You can also use it as a base for a variety of toppings such as sautéed mushrooms, caramelized onions, or a drizzle of balsamic glaze.

Final Thoughts

Creamy garlic parmesan mashed cauliflower is not only a delicious and satisfying side dish, but it’s also a great way to incorporate more vegetables into your diet. With its creamy texture and rich flavor, it’s hard to believe that it’s a low-carb and healthier alternative to mashed potatoes. Give this recipe a try, and you might just find yourself reaching for mashed cauliflower more often than you’d expect. Enjoy!
