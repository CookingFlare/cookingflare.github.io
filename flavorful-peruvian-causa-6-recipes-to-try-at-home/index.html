Flavorful Peruvian Causa: 6 Recipes to Try at Home


If you’re a fan of bold and vibrant flavors, then Peruvian cuisine is sure to tickle your taste buds. One dish that perfectly embodies the essence of Peruvian flavors is Causa. This traditional Peruvian dish combines layers of mashed potatoes, zesty fillings, and a touch of spice, resulting in a truly satisfying culinary experience. In this blog post, we bring you not just one, but six delicious Causa recipes that you can easily recreate in the comfort of your own kitchen. So, let’s dive right in!

Classic Chicken Causa

Ingredients:

  • 4 large yellow potatoes, boiled and mashed
  • 2 tablespoons of lime juice
  • 1 tablespoon of ají amarillo paste (Peruvian yellow chili paste)
  • 2 cups of cooked chicken breast, shredded
  • 1/2 cup of mayonnaise
  • 1 avocado, sliced
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, combine the mashed potatoes, lime juice, ají amarillo paste, salt, and pepper. Mix well until the ingredients are fully incorporated.
  2. Take a round mold or a small bowl and layer the mashed potatoes at the bottom, creating a base for your causa.
  3. Spread a layer of shredded chicken on top of the mashed potatoes.
  4. Drizzle a layer of mayonnaise over the chicken.
  5. Repeat the layers until you reach the top of the mold.
  6. Garnish with avocado slices and a sprinkle of ají amarillo paste.
  7. Carefully remove the mold and serve chilled. Enjoy!

Spicy Tuna Causa

Ingredients:

  • 4 large yellow potatoes, boiled and mashed
  • 2 tablespoons of lime juice
  • 1 tablespoon of ají amarillo paste
  • 2 cans of tuna in oil, drained
  • 1/4 cup of red onion, finely chopped
  • 1/4 cup of cilantro, finely chopped
  • 1 tablespoon of mayonnaise
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Follow the same steps as the Classic Chicken Causa to prepare the mashed potatoes.
  2. In a separate bowl, combine the tuna, red onion, cilantro, mayonnaise, salt, and pepper. Mix well.
  3. Layer the mashed potatoes, tuna mixture, and ají amarillo paste in the mold.
  4. Repeat the layers until you reach the top.
  5. Garnish with a sprinkle of ají amarillo paste and some chopped cilantro.
  6. Chill in the refrigerator before serving. Enjoy the spicy kick!

Vegetarian Causa

Ingredients:

  • 4 large yellow potatoes, boiled and mashed
  • 2 tablespoons of lime juice
  • 1 tablespoon of ají amarillo paste
  • 1 cup of cooked quinoa
  • 1/2 cup of black olives, sliced
  • 1/4 cup of red onion, finely chopped
  • 1/4 cup of feta cheese, crumbled
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the mashed potatoes as mentioned earlier.
  2. In a bowl, combine the cooked quinoa, black olives, red onion, feta cheese, salt, and pepper.
  3. Layer the mashed potatoes, quinoa mixture, and ají amarillo paste in the mold.
  4. Repeat the layers until you reach the top.
  5. Sprinkle some feta cheese on the final layer.
  6. Chill and serve. Enjoy this flavorful vegetarian delight!

Seafood Causa

Ingredients:

  • 4 large yellow potatoes, boiled and mashed
  • 2 tablespoons of lime juice
  • 1 tablespoon of ají amarillo paste
  • 1 cup of mixed seafood (shrimp, squid, and fish), cooked and chopped
  • 1/4 cup of red onion, finely chopped
  • 1/4 cup of celery, finely chopped
  • 1 tablespoon of mayonnaise
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the mashed potatoes as mentioned earlier.
  2. In a bowl, combine the mixed seafood, red onion, celery, mayonnaise, salt, and pepper.
  3. Layer the mashed potatoes, seafood mixture, and ají amarillo paste in the mold.
  4. Repeat the layers until you reach the top.
  5. Garnish with some chopped celery and a drizzle of ají amarillo paste.
  6. Chill and serve. Dive into the savory flavors of the sea!

Beef Causa

Ingredients:

  • 4 large yellow potatoes, boiled and mashed
  • 2 tablespoons of lime juice
  • 1 tablespoon of ají amarillo paste
  • 1 cup of cooked beef, shredded
  • 1/4 cup of red onion, finely chopped
  • 1/4 cup of tomatoes, diced
  • 1 tablespoon of mayonnaise
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the mashed potatoes as mentioned earlier.
  2. In a bowl, combine the cooked beef, red onion, tomatoes, mayonnaise, salt, and pepper.
  3. Layer the mashed potatoes, beef mixture, and ají amarillo paste in the mold.
  4. Repeat the layers until you reach the top.
  5. Garnish with a sprinkle of ají amarillo paste and some diced tomatoes.
  6. Chill and serve. Get ready for a hearty and satisfying meal!

Shrimp and Avocado Causa

Ingredients:

  • 4 large yellow potatoes, boiled and mashed
  • 2 tablespoons of lime juice
  • 1 tablespoon of ají amarillo paste
  • 1 cup of cooked shrimp, chopped
  • 1 avocado, diced
  • 1/4 cup of red onion, finely chopped
  • 1/4 cup of cilantro, finely chopped
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the mashed potatoes as mentioned earlier.
  2. In a bowl, combine the cooked shrimp, avocado, red onion, cilantro, salt, and pepper.
  3. Layer the mashed potatoes, shrimp mixture, and ají amarillo paste in the mold.
  4. Repeat the layers until you reach the top.
  5. Garnish with a sprinkle of ají amarillo paste and some chopped cilantro.
  6. Chill and serve. Indulge in the creamy goodness of shrimp and avocado!

Conclusion

Peruvian Causa is a versatile and flavorful dish that can be enjoyed in various forms. Whether you prefer the classic chicken filling or want to explore vegetarian or seafood options, these recipes offer something for everyone. The layers of mashed potatoes, zesty fillings, and a hint of spice come together to create a culinary masterpiece that is sure to impress your taste buds. So, what are you waiting for? Gather your ingredients and embark on a Peruvian culinary adventure from the comfort of your own kitchen. Enjoy the mouthwatering flavors of Peruvian Causa!
