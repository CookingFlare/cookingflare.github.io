Thai Pineapple Fried Rice with Chicken and Shrimp: A Sweet and Savory Combo


If you’re craving a burst of flavors in your next meal, look no further than Thai Pineapple Fried Rice with Chicken and Shrimp. This delightful dish brings together the perfect balance of sweet and savory, making it a favorite among Thai cuisine enthusiasts. In this recipe, we’ll guide you through the steps to create this mouthwatering dish that will leave you wanting more.

Ingredients

To make Thai Pineapple Fried Rice with Chicken and Shrimp, here’s what you’ll need:

  • 2 cups of cooked jasmine rice (preferably chilled)
  • 1 cup of diced chicken breast
  • 1 cup of peeled and deveined shrimp
  • 1 cup of fresh pineapple chunks
  • 1/2 cup of diced red bell pepper
  • 1/2 cup of diced green bell pepper
  • 1/2 cup of diced onion
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons of vegetable oil
  • 2 tablespoons of soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon of fish sauce
  • 1 tablespoon of oyster sauce
  • 1 tablespoon of curry powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon of sugar
  • Fresh cilantro, for garnish

Instructions

  1. Heat the vegetable oil in a large pan or wok over medium heat. Add the minced garlic and sauté until fragrant.

  2. Add the diced chicken breast to the pan and cook until it’s no longer pink. Then, add the shrimp and cook until they turn pink and are cooked through. Remove the chicken and shrimp from the pan and set them aside.

  3. In the same pan, add the diced onion, red bell pepper, and green bell pepper. Sauté until they become tender.

  4. Push the vegetables to one side of the pan and add the chilled jasmine rice to the other side. Break up any clumps with a spatula and cook until the rice is heated through.

  5. In a small bowl, mix together the soy sauce, fish sauce, oyster sauce, curry powder, and sugar. Pour the sauce over the rice and vegetables, and mix well to combine.

  6. Add the cooked chicken, shrimp, and pineapple chunks to the pan. Stir everything together until all the ingredients are well coated with the sauce.

  7. Continue cooking for another 2-3 minutes, allowing the flavors to meld together and the pineapple to become slightly caramelized.

  8. Remove from heat and garnish with fresh cilantro.

Serving Suggestions

Thai Pineapple Fried Rice with Chicken and Shrimp is a complete meal in itself, but you can also serve it with some additional sides to enhance the experience. Here are a few suggestions:

  • Serve with a side of Thai cucumber salad for a refreshing contrast to the savory flavors of the fried rice.
  • Top with chopped roasted cashews for an added crunch and nutty flavor.
  • Serve with a wedge of lime on the side for an extra zing of tanginess.

Conclusion

Thai Pineapple Fried Rice with Chicken and Shrimp is a delightful dish that brings together the sweetness of pineapple with the savory flavors of chicken and shrimp. The combination of ingredients creates a harmony of flavors that will leave you wanting more. This recipe is perfect for those who enjoy exploring the diverse world of Thai cuisine and want to try something new and exciting. So, why not give it a try and experience the magic of Thai flavors in your own kitchen? Enjoy!
