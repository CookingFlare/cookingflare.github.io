Savor the Spice with Dairy-Free Curry Chicken Skewers

3 min · 517 words

Craving a burst of flavor that will transport your taste buds to far-off lands? Look no further than these delectable dairy-free curry chicken skewers. Packed with aromatic spices and succulent chicken, this recipe is a surefire way to elevate your next meal. Whether you’re hosting a summer barbecue or simply want to add a touch of exotic flair to your dinner table, these skewers are guaranteed to impress.

Ingredients:

  • 1.5 lbs of boneless, skinless chicken thighs, cut into chunks
  • 1 cup of coconut milk
  • 2 tablespoons of curry powder
  • 1 teaspoon of ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon of paprika
  • 1 teaspoon of turmeric
  • 3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon of fresh ginger, grated
  • 1 tablespoon of lime juice
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Wooden skewers, soaked in water for 30 minutes

Instructions:

1. Marinate the Chicken

In a large bowl, combine the coconut milk, curry powder, cumin, paprika, turmeric, minced garlic, grated ginger, lime juice, salt, and pepper. Mix well until the marinade is smooth and well-combined. Add the chicken chunks to the bowl and toss to coat evenly. Cover the bowl with plastic wrap and refrigerate for at least 2 hours, allowing the flavors to meld and infuse the chicken with the rich, aromatic spices.

2. Prepare the Skewers

Preheat your grill to medium-high heat. While the grill is heating up, thread the marinated chicken chunks onto the soaked wooden skewers, ensuring an even distribution of meat on each skewer. This step can be a fun activity for the whole family, as everyone can get involved in assembling their own skewers according to their preferences.

3. Grill to Perfection

Once the grill is hot and ready, place the skewers on the grates, allowing them to cook for 5-7 minutes on each side, or until the chicken is fully cooked through and has developed a delicious charred exterior. Be sure to rotate the skewers occasionally to ensure even cooking and to prevent any hot spots on the grill from scorching the meat.

4. Serve and Enjoy

Once the chicken skewers are done, transfer them to a serving platter and garnish with fresh cilantro or a squeeze of lime juice for an extra burst of freshness. These dairy-free curry chicken skewers pair beautifully with a side of fluffy basmati rice, a crisp salad, or even a warm, pillowy naan bread to soak up the flavorful juices.

Conclusion

With its vibrant blend of spices and tender, juicy chicken, these dairy-free curry chicken skewers are a feast for the senses. Whether you’re a seasoned curry aficionado or a newcomer to the world of exotic flavors, this recipe is a fantastic introduction to the tantalizing world of Indian cuisine. So, fire up the grill, gather your loved ones, and savor the spice with these irresistible skewers that are sure to become a staple in your culinary repertoire. Happy grilling!
