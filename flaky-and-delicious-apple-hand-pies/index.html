Flaky and Delicious Apple Hand Pies


If you’re a fan of apple desserts, then you’re in for a treat with these flaky and delicious apple hand pies. These handheld delights are perfect for any occasion, whether it’s a cozy family gathering or a picnic in the park. The combination of buttery pastry and sweet, cinnamon-spiced apple filling is simply irresistible. So, roll up your sleeves and let’s get baking!

Ingredients

For the pastry:

  • 2 ½ cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup unsalted butter, cold and cubed
  • 6-8 tablespoons ice water

For the apple filling:

  • 4-5 medium-sized apples, peeled, cored, and diced
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground nutmeg
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice

For the egg wash:

  • 1 egg, beaten
  • 1 tablespoon milk

Instructions

  1. Start by making the pastry. In a large bowl, combine the flour and salt. Add the cold cubed butter and use a pastry cutter or your fingertips to cut the butter into the flour until the mixture resembles coarse crumbs.

  2. Gradually add the ice water, one tablespoon at a time, until the dough comes together. Divide the dough into two equal parts, shape them into discs, and wrap them in plastic wrap. Refrigerate for at least 30 minutes.

  3. While the dough is chilling, prepare the apple filling. In a mixing bowl, combine the diced apples, granulated sugar, flour, cinnamon, nutmeg, and lemon juice. Toss everything together until the apples are evenly coated.

  4. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C). Line a baking sheet with parchment paper.

  5. Take one disc of chilled dough and roll it out on a lightly floured surface until it’s about 1/8 inch thick. Use a round cookie cutter or a glass to cut out circles of dough, approximately 4-5 inches in diameter.

  6. Place a spoonful of the apple filling onto the center of each dough circle. Be careful not to overfill, as it may cause the pies to burst while baking.

  7. Fold the dough over the filling, creating a half-moon shape. Press the edges together firmly to seal the pies. You can use a fork to crimp the edges for a decorative touch.

  8. Transfer the hand pies onto the prepared baking sheet. Repeat the process with the remaining dough and apple filling.

  9. In a small bowl, whisk together the beaten egg and milk to create an egg wash. Brush the tops of the hand pies with the egg wash, which will give them a beautiful golden color when baked.

  10. Bake the hand pies for about 20-25 minutes, or until they are golden brown and the filling is bubbly. Allow them to cool on a wire rack for a few minutes before serving.

These flaky and delicious apple hand pies are best enjoyed warm, straight from the oven. The buttery pastry melts in your mouth, while the sweet and spiced apple filling bursts with flavor. Serve them with a scoop of vanilla ice cream or a dollop of whipped cream for an extra indulgence.

Whether you’re a seasoned baker or just starting out, these apple hand pies are a must-try. They’re easy to make, portable, and guaranteed to impress everyone who takes a bite. So, grab your rolling pin and create some unforgettable memories with these delectable treats. Enjoy!
