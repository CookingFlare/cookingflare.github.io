The Ultimate Classic Margherita Pizza Recipe


There’s no denying that pizza is a universally loved dish. With its crispy crust, gooey cheese, and tantalizing toppings, pizza has become a staple in households around the world. And when it comes to classic pizzas, the Margherita pizza reigns supreme. With its simple yet flavorful combination of fresh ingredients, this pizza is a true crowd-pleaser. So, without further ado, let’s dive into the recipe for the ultimate classic Margherita pizza.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound pizza dough
  • 1 cup tomato sauce
  • 8 ounces fresh mozzarella cheese, sliced
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • Extra virgin olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 475°F (245°C) and place a pizza stone or baking sheet on the middle rack to heat up. This will ensure a crispy crust.

  2. On a lightly floured surface, roll out the pizza dough to your desired thickness. Remember, a thinner crust will result in a crisper pizza, while a thicker crust will be softer.

  3. Transfer the rolled-out dough onto a piece of parchment paper. This will make it easier to transfer the pizza onto the hot baking surface later.

  4. Spread the tomato sauce evenly over the surface of the dough, leaving a small border around the edges for the crust.

  5. Arrange the slices of fresh mozzarella cheese on top of the sauce, ensuring even coverage.

  6. Tear the fresh basil leaves into smaller pieces and scatter them over the cheese.

  7. Drizzle a generous amount of extra virgin olive oil over the pizza. This will add a rich flavor and help the toppings meld together.

  8. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Remember, the cheese and tomato sauce may already have some saltiness, so adjust accordingly.

  9. Carefully transfer the pizza, along with the parchment paper, onto the preheated baking surface in the oven.

  10. Bake for about 10-15 minutes or until the crust is golden brown and the cheese is bubbling and slightly browned.

  11. Once baked, remove the pizza from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes before slicing. This will allow the flavors to develop and prevent any burning accidents.

  12. Serve your freshly baked Margherita pizza hot and enjoy!

Tips and Variations

  • For added flavor, you can sprinkle some freshly grated Parmesan cheese over the pizza before baking.
  • If you prefer a bit of heat, add some thinly sliced chili peppers or a sprinkle of red pepper flakes to your Margherita pizza.
  • Experiment with different types of tomatoes for the sauce, such as San Marzano or cherry tomatoes, to enhance the taste.
  • If you’re feeling adventurous, try adding some thinly sliced prosciutto or olives to the pizza for a twist on the classic Margherita.

So there you have it, the ultimate classic Margherita pizza recipe. With its simple yet delicious combination of fresh ingredients, this pizza is sure to become a favorite in your household. Whether you’re hosting a pizza night or simply craving a homemade slice, give this recipe a try and savor the flavors of this timeless dish. Buon appetito!
