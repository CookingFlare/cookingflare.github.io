Irresistible Chocolate Peanut Butter Cupcakes


If you’re a fan of the classic combination of chocolate and peanut butter, then you’re in for a treat with this recipe for irresistible chocolate peanut butter cupcakes. These decadent treats are perfect for any occasion, whether it’s a birthday celebration, a potluck with friends, or just a cozy night in. The rich, moist chocolate cake combined with the creamy, nutty peanut butter frosting creates a flavor explosion that will leave you and your guests craving for more.

Ingredients

For the Chocolate Cupcakes:

  • 1 3/4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 cups granulated sugar
  • 3/4 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons baking soda
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 cup whole milk
  • 1/2 cup vegetable oil
  • 2 teaspoons pure vanilla extract
  • 1 cup boiling water

For the Peanut Butter Frosting:

  • 1 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1 cup creamy peanut butter
  • 3 cups confectioners’ sugar
  • 1 teaspoon pure vanilla extract
  • 1/4 cup heavy cream

Optional Garnish:

  • Mini chocolate peanut butter cups
  • Chopped peanuts

Instructions

For the Chocolate Cupcakes:

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C) and line a muffin tin with cupcake liners.
  2. In a large bowl, combine the flour, sugar, cocoa powder, baking powder, baking soda, and salt.
  3. Add the eggs, milk, oil, and vanilla extract to the dry ingredients and mix until well combined.
  4. Gradually stir in the boiling water, mixing until the batter is smooth.
  5. Fill each cupcake liner about 2/3 full with the batter.
  6. Bake for 20-22 minutes or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.
  7. Allow the cupcakes to cool in the pan for 5 minutes, then transfer them to a wire rack to cool completely.

For the Peanut Butter Frosting:

  1. In a large bowl, beat the butter and peanut butter together until smooth and creamy.
  2. Gradually add the confectioners’ sugar, mixing well after each addition.
  3. Stir in the vanilla extract.
  4. Add the heavy cream, one tablespoon at a time, until the frosting reaches your desired consistency.

Assembly:

  1. Once the cupcakes are completely cooled, use a piping bag or offset spatula to frost each cupcake with the peanut butter frosting.
  2. If desired, top each cupcake with a mini chocolate peanut butter cup or a sprinkle of chopped peanuts for extra flair.

Serving and Storage

These irresistible chocolate peanut butter cupcakes are best enjoyed fresh, but any leftovers can be stored in an airtight container at room temperature for up to 3 days. Be sure to store them in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight to maintain their freshness.

Whether you’re a seasoned baker or just starting out, these chocolate peanut butter cupcakes are a must-try. With their perfect balance of rich chocolate and creamy peanut butter, they are sure to become a new favorite in your dessert repertoire. So, gather your ingredients and get ready to indulge in the delightful combination of flavors that will leave everyone craving for more!
