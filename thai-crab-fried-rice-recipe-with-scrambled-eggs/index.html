Thai Crab Fried Rice Recipe with Scrambled Eggs


If you’re craving the exotic flavors of Thai cuisine, look no further than this delightful Thai Crab Fried Rice with Scrambled Eggs recipe. This dish combines the briny sweetness of crab meat with the aromatic spices and fluffy texture of scrambled eggs, all enveloped in fragrant jasmine rice. It’s a symphony of flavors and textures that will transport your taste buds to the bustling streets of Bangkok.

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups of jasmine rice, cooked and cooled
  • 200g crab meat, cooked and shredded
  • 3 eggs, beaten
  • 1 cup mixed vegetables (such as carrots, peas, and bell peppers)
  • 3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 2 shallots, finely chopped
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon fish sauce
  • 1 tablespoon oyster sauce
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon white pepper
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • Fresh cilantro for garnish
  • Lime wedges for serving

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the Rice:

    • Begin by cooking the jasmine rice according to the package instructions. Once cooked, spread the rice out on a baking sheet to cool completely. This will help prevent the rice from becoming too sticky when fried.

  2. Scramble the Eggs:

    • Heat a tablespoon of oil in a large wok or skillet over medium heat. Pour in the beaten eggs and gently scramble them until they are just set. Remove the scrambled eggs from the wok and set aside.

  3. Fry the Aromatics:

    • In the same wok, heat the remaining oil over medium-high heat. Add the minced garlic and chopped shallots, and stir-fry until fragrant and golden brown.

  4. Stir-Fry the Crab Meat and Vegetables:

    • Toss in the shredded crab meat and mixed vegetables, and stir-fry for a few minutes until the vegetables are tender-crisp and the crab meat is heated through.

  5. Add the Rice:

    • Increase the heat to high and add the cooled jasmine rice to the wok. Use a spatula to break up any clumps and evenly distribute the rice with the other ingredients.

  6. Season the Fried Rice:

    • Drizzle the soy sauce, fish sauce, and oyster sauce over the rice. Sprinkle in the sugar and white pepper. Toss everything together until the rice is well-coated with the sauces and the ingredients are evenly distributed.

  7. Incorporate the Scrambled Eggs:

    • Gently fold the scrambled eggs back into the fried rice, taking care not to break up the eggs too much. Continue to stir-fry for another minute to ensure that the eggs are evenly distributed throughout the rice.

  8. Serve and Garnish:

    • Transfer the fragrant Thai Crab Fried Rice with Scrambled Eggs to a serving platter or individual plates. Garnish with fresh cilantro and serve with lime wedges on the side for an extra burst of citrus flavor.

Tips and Tricks:

  • For an extra touch of indulgence, consider using jumbo lump crab meat for a richer flavor and larger, more succulent pieces in your fried rice.
  • If you prefer a spicier kick, add a teaspoon of Sriracha or a sprinkle of crushed red pepper flakes to the rice when seasoning.

In Conclusion:

This Thai Crab Fried Rice with Scrambled Eggs recipe is a celebration of Thai flavors and a wonderful way to bring the essence of Thailand into your own kitchen. With the delicate sweetness of crab, the comforting fluffiness of scrambled eggs, and the umami-rich fragrance of jasmine rice, this dish is a true delight for the senses. Whether enjoyed as a main course or as a side dish to complement a wider Thai feast, this fried rice is sure to become a favorite in your culinary repertoire. So, gather your ingredients, fire up your wok, and embark on a culinary journey to the heart of Thailand with this exquisite recipe!
