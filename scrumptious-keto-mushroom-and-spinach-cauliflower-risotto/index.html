Scrumptious Keto Mushroom and Spinach Cauliflower Risotto


As the weather starts to cool down, our cravings for warm and comforting dishes begin to rise. One of the most popular and beloved dishes during the fall and winter seasons is risotto. However, traditional risotto is often high in carbohydrates, making it difficult to fit into a ketogenic diet. Fear not, dear keto enthusiasts! Today, we will be sharing a scrumptious keto mushroom and spinach cauliflower risotto recipe that is not only delicious but also low in carbs.

Ingredients

  • 1 head of cauliflower
  • 2 tablespoons of butter
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cups of mixed mushrooms (such as cremini, shiitake, and oyster), sliced
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 cup of fresh spinach leaves
  • 1 cup of grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/2 cup of heavy cream
  • 1/2 cup of chicken broth
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley, chopped (optional)

Instructions

Step 1: Prepare the Cauliflower

Preheat the oven to 400°F (200°C). Rinse the cauliflower and remove the leaves and stem. Cut it into florets and pulse in a food processor until it resembles rice. Transfer the cauliflower “rice” to a baking sheet and drizzle with 1 tablespoon of butter. Toss to coat and roast in the oven for 20-25 minutes, or until tender and lightly browned. Let it cool slightly.

Step 2: Sauté the Onion and Mushrooms

In a large skillet, melt the remaining 1 tablespoon of butter over medium heat. Add the chopped onion and sauté until translucent, about 3-4 minutes. Add the sliced mushrooms and cook until they release their moisture and start to brown, about 5-7 minutes. Add the minced garlic and cook for an additional minute.

Step 3: Combine the Cauliflower and Mushroom Mixtures

Add the roasted cauliflower “rice” to the skillet with the mushroom mixture. Stir to combine and cook for 1-2 minutes, allowing the cauliflower to absorb the flavors.

Step 4: Add the Spinach and Cheese

Add the fresh spinach leaves to the skillet and cook until wilted, about 1-2 minutes. Stir in the grated Parmesan cheese until melted and well combined.

Step 5: Add the Cream and Broth

Pour in the heavy cream and chicken broth, stirring constantly to prevent the mixture from sticking to the bottom of the skillet. Bring the mixture to a simmer and cook for 2-3 minutes, or until the liquid has been absorbed and the risotto has a creamy consistency. Season with salt and pepper to taste.

Step 6: Serve and Enjoy

Transfer the keto mushroom and spinach cauliflower risotto to a serving dish and garnish with chopped fresh parsley, if desired. Serve immediately and enjoy the creamy, comforting goodness!

Conclusion

This keto mushroom and spinach cauliflower risotto recipe is a game-changer for those who crave a warm and comforting dish without the high carb content. By using cauliflower “rice” and reducing the amount of cream and cheese, we have created a dish that is not only delicious but also keto-friendly. So go ahead, give it a try, and indulge in the creamy, savory goodness without any guilt!
