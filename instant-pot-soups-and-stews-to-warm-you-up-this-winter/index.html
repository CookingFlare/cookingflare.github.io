Instant Pot Soups and Stews to Warm You Up This Winter


When the cold winter winds start whispering through your windows, there’s nothing more comforting than wrapping your hands around a bowl of warm, hearty goodness. Soups and stews take center stage as some of the most satisfying, soul-soothing meals during chilly months. And when you pair those cozy creations with the efficiency and ease of an Instant Pot, you’ve got a winning combination. These warming recipes are not only delicious but also perfect for busy days when you want a nourishing meal without spending hours in the kitchen.

In this blog post, we’ll walk through five irresistible Instant Pot soup and stew recipes that bring together wholesome ingredients, seasonal flavors, and unmatched convenience. Whether you need a protein-packed stew, a creamy soup, or a light broth-based option, there’s something here to suit every craving. So grab that Instant Pot off the shelf, and let’s get cooking!

1. Creamy Instant Pot Butternut Squash Soup

This silky smooth butternut squash soup is delicately spiced and oh-so-comforting. Perfectly balanced with its sweet and savory flavors, it’s a quintessential winter dish. Even better, this recipe is naturally vegetarian, and it can be made vegan with minor adjustments.

Ingredients

  • 1 medium butternut squash, peeled and cubed
  • 1 medium onion, chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 4 cups vegetable broth
  • 1 cup coconut milk (or heavy cream for non-vegan option)
  • 1 tsp ground ginger
  • 1 tsp ground cinnamon
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Set the Instant Pot to sauté mode and add a drizzle of olive oil. Cook the onion and garlic until fragrant.
  2. Add cubed butternut squash, vegetable broth, ground ginger, cinnamon, salt, and pepper. Stir everything together.
  3. Seal the Instant Pot and set it to high pressure for 12 minutes. Once done, allow a natural release for 5 minutes, then release remaining pressure manually.
  4. Use an immersion blender to puree the soup until creamy and smooth. Stir in coconut milk or heavy cream and adjust seasoning as needed.
  5. Serve warm and garnish with a sprinkle of cinnamon or chopped parsley for added flavor.

2. Instant Pot Beef Stew Loaded with Vegetables

Beef stew is a timeless winter favorite, brimming with tender chunks of beef, hearty vegetables, and rich broth. Making it in the Instant Pot means you can achieve that slow-cooked flavor in less than half the time.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb beef stew meat, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 4 cups beef broth
  • 3 medium carrots, sliced into chunks
  • 2 medium potatoes, peeled and diced
  • 1 celery stalk, chopped
  • 2 tbsp tomato paste
  • 1 cup frozen peas
  • 2 tsp Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 tsp dried thyme
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Turn the Instant Pot to sauté mode and brown the beef on all sides. Add a bit of oil to prevent sticking. Once browned, remove the beef and set aside.
  2. Deglaze the pot with a splash of beef broth, scraping up any browned bits.
  3. Add the browned beef back to the pot along with the carrots, potatoes, celery, tomato paste, Worcestershire sauce, dried thyme, and the remaining beef broth. Season with salt and pepper.
  4. Seal the lid and cook on high pressure for 35 minutes. Once done, allow the pressure to release naturally.
  5. Stir in the frozen peas and let them warm through using the sauté mode. Serve hot with crusty bread for an ideal winter meal.

3. Instant Pot Chicken Noodle Soup

When you’re feeling under the weather or just need an old-fashioned hug for your soul, nothing beats a bowl of chicken noodle soup. Ready in no time thanks to the Instant Pot, this recipe is comfort food at its absolute best.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb chicken breast or thighs
  • 6 cups chicken broth
  • 2 medium carrots, diced
  • 2 celery stalks, chopped
  • 1 medium onion, diced
  • 2 cups egg noodles
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 tsp dried parsley
  • 1 tsp dried thyme
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Add chicken, chopped vegetables (carrots, celery, onion), garlic, chicken broth, parsley, thyme, salt, and pepper to the Instant Pot. Stir gently to combine.
  2. Seal the lid and cook on high pressure for 10 minutes. Once done, release the pressure manually.
  3. Remove the chicken and shred it using two forks. Return the shredded chicken to the pot.
  4. Add egg noodles and set the Instant Pot to sauté mode. Cook until the noodles are tender, about 5-7 minutes.
  5. Ladle into bowls and enjoy this warm, wholesome soup with a sprinkle of fresh parsley if desired.

4. Hearty Lentil Stew with Vegetables

Packed with plant-based protein, fiber, and vibrant vegetables, this lentil stew is a nutrient-rich yet hearty winter meal. Perfect for vegetarian and vegan diets, it’s as nutritious as it is satisfying.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup dried lentils, rinsed
  • 4 cups vegetable broth
  • 2 medium carrots, diced
  • 1 cup diced tomatoes (canned or fresh)
  • 2 cups chopped kale or spinach
  • 1 medium onion, chopped
  • 3 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon smoked paprika
  • 1 bay leaf
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Set the Instant Pot to sauté mode and add a drizzle of olive oil. Cook the onion and garlic until translucent.
  2. Stir in carrots, lentils, diced tomatoes, and vegetable broth. Add cumin, smoked paprika, bay leaf, salt, and pepper.
  3. Seal the lid and cook on high pressure for 15 minutes. Perform a natural pressure release for 5 minutes, then manually release the remaining pressure.
  4. Stir in the kale or spinach and let it wilt with the residual heat from the stew.
  5. Remove the bay leaf before serving. Enjoy this hearty vegan stew with a slice of warm bread or crackers.

5. Instant Pot Creamy Potato Soup

Creamy potato soup is ultimate comfort food for frosty evenings. The combination of velvety texture, rich flavors, and ease of preparation makes it a winner for your winter rotation.

Ingredients

  • 4 medium russet potatoes, peeled and diced
  • 1 medium onion, chopped
  • 3 garlic cloves, minced
  • 4 cups chicken or vegetable broth
  • 1 cup milk (or heavy cream for richness)
  • 1/2 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Optional toppings: chopped green onions, crumbled bacon, sour cream

Instructions

  1. Set the Instant Pot to sauté mode and cook the onion and garlic briefly until fragrant. Add diced potatoes and broth. Stir well.
  2. Seal the Instant Pot and set it to high pressure for 10 minutes. Quick-release the pressure once done.
  3. Use a potato masher or immersion blender to mash the soup to your desired texture. Stir in milk (or cream) and shredded cheddar cheese. Adjust seasoning with salt and pepper.
  4. Ladle into bowls and top with green onions, bacon bits, and a dollop of sour cream for a creamy, indulgent touch.

Final Thoughts

Winter may be chilly, but these Instant Pot soup and stew recipes will keep you warm, well-fed, and ready to embrace the season. They’re perfect for meal prep, easy enough for weeknight dinners, and versatile to fit your unique tastes. Plus, the Instant Pot makes cleanup a breeze, so you can spend less time washing dishes and more time enjoying every flavorful bite.

Don’t let the cold weather win—download these recipes, grab your Instant Pot, and create meals that’ll bring the warmth of the season to your table!
