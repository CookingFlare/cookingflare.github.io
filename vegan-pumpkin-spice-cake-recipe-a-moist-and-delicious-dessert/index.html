Vegan Pumpkin Spice Cake Recipe: A Moist and Delicious Dessert


If you’re a fan of pumpkin spice and looking for a delectable vegan dessert, look no further! This Vegan Pumpkin Spice Cake recipe is the perfect treat for any occasion. Not only is it incredibly moist and flavorful, but it’s also dairy-free and egg-free, making it suitable for those following a vegan lifestyle.

Ingredients

For the cake:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 1/2 cups granulated sugar
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 2 teaspoons ground cinnamon
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground nutmeg
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground ginger
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground cloves
  • 1 cup pumpkin puree
  • 1/2 cup unsweetened applesauce
  • 1/2 cup vegetable oil
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 cup almond milk (or any non-dairy milk)
  • 1 tablespoon apple cider vinegar

For the cream cheese frosting:

  • 1/2 cup vegan butter, softened
  • 8 ounces vegan cream cheese, softened
  • 4 cups powdered sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C) and grease a 9-inch round cake pan.

  2. In a large mixing bowl, whisk together the flour, sugar, baking powder, baking soda, salt, cinnamon, nutmeg, ginger, and cloves.

  3. In a separate bowl, combine the pumpkin puree, applesauce, vegetable oil, vanilla extract, almond milk, and apple cider vinegar. Mix well until all the wet ingredients are thoroughly combined.

  4. Gradually add the wet ingredients to the dry ingredients and mix until just combined. Be careful not to overmix, as this can result in a dense cake.

  5. Pour the batter into the prepared cake pan and smooth the top with a spatula.

  6. Bake for 35-40 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.

  7. Remove the cake from the oven and let it cool in the pan for 10 minutes. Then, transfer it to a wire rack to cool completely.

  8. While the cake is cooling, prepare the cream cheese frosting. In a mixing bowl, beat the softened vegan butter and cream cheese until smooth and creamy.

  9. Gradually add the powdered sugar, one cup at a time, and continue beating until the frosting is light and fluffy. Stir in the vanilla extract.

  10. Once the cake has cooled completely, spread a generous layer of cream cheese frosting over the top and sides of the cake.

  11. Serve and enjoy!

Tips and Variations

  • For an extra burst of flavor, you can add a handful of chopped walnuts or pecans to the cake batter before baking.
  • If you prefer a spicier cake, increase the amount of ground cinnamon, nutmeg, ginger, and cloves to your taste.
  • Feel free to experiment with different frostings or toppings. A drizzle of maple syrup or a sprinkle of cinnamon sugar can add a delightful touch to this already delicious cake.
  • This cake can be stored in an airtight container at room temperature for up to 3 days. It can also be refrigerated for a longer shelf life.

So there you have it, a Vegan Pumpkin Spice Cake recipe that is sure to impress everyone, whether they follow a vegan lifestyle or not. With its moist texture, warm spices, and creamy cream cheese frosting, this cake is the perfect dessert for autumn gatherings, holiday parties, or any time you’re craving a sweet treat. Give it a try and indulge in this delightful vegan dessert!
