Mango Coconut Rice Pudding: A Creamy and Tropical Dessert


If you’re looking for a dessert that’s both creamy and packed with tropical flavors, then Mango Coconut Rice Pudding is the perfect treat for you. This delightful dish combines the richness of coconut milk, the sweetness of ripe mangoes, and the comforting texture of rice to create a dessert that will transport your taste buds to a sun-soaked paradise. Whether you’re hosting a dinner party or simply craving a taste of the tropics, this recipe is sure to be a hit.

Ingredients

To make this luscious Mango Coconut Rice Pudding, you’ll need the following ingredients:

  • 1 cup of jasmine rice
  • 2 cups of coconut milk
  • 1 cup of water
  • 1/2 cup of sugar
  • 2 ripe mangoes, peeled and diced
  • 1 teaspoon of vanilla extract
  • A pinch of salt
  • Ground cinnamon for garnish
  • Toasted coconut flakes for garnish

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Rice: In a medium saucepan, combine the jasmine rice, coconut milk, water, sugar, and a pinch of salt. Bring the mixture to a gentle boil over medium heat, stirring occasionally to prevent the rice from sticking to the bottom of the pan.

  2. Simmer: Once the mixture comes to a boil, reduce the heat to low and let the rice simmer, covered, for about 20-25 minutes or until the rice is tender and has absorbed most of the liquid. Stir occasionally to ensure the rice cooks evenly and doesn’t stick to the bottom of the pan.

  3. Add the Mangoes: After the rice has cooked, gently fold in the diced mangoes and vanilla extract, allowing the residual heat to soften the mangoes slightly while infusing the pudding with their sweet flavor.

  4. Chill and Serve: Once the mangoes are incorporated, remove the rice pudding from the heat and let it cool to room temperature. Then, transfer it to the refrigerator and chill for at least 1-2 hours until it’s nicely set.

  5. Garnish and Enjoy: When you’re ready to serve, spoon the chilled Mango Coconut Rice Pudding into individual bowls and sprinkle each portion with a dusting of ground cinnamon and a generous sprinkle of toasted coconut flakes for added texture and flavor.

Tips and Variations

  • For an extra creamy texture, you can use canned coconut cream in place of coconut milk.
  • If you prefer a stronger coconut flavor, add a few drops of coconut extract to the pudding along with the vanilla.
  • To make this dessert vegan-friendly, simply swap the regular sugar for a plant-based sweetener like agave syrup or maple syrup.

Final Thoughts

Mango Coconut Rice Pudding is a dessert that captures the essence of a tropical getaway in every spoonful. The velvety smoothness of the coconut-infused rice, combined with the bursts of juicy mango, creates a dessert experience that is both indulgent and refreshing. Whether you’re serving it at a summer soirée or simply treating yourself to a taste of the exotic, this creamy delight is sure to be a crowd-pleaser. So, go ahead, whip up a batch of Mango Coconut Rice Pudding, and let its sunny flavors transport you to a blissful oasis of sweetness.
