Cozy Winter Barbecue Recipes for Indoor Grilling


Winter is here, and while the cold weather may have you thinking that barbecue season is over, it doesn’t have to be! Indoor grilling can bring the cozy warmth and delicious flavors of barbecue right into your kitchen, making it the perfect way to enjoy comforting meals during the chilly months. In this blog post, we’ll explore a variety of cozy winter barbecue recipes that are perfect for indoor grilling. From hearty mains to soul-warming sides, these recipes will bring the joy of barbecue into your home, no matter the weather outside.

Smoky Chipotle Chicken Skewers

Prep Time: 20 minutes
Cook Time: 15 minutes
Serving: 4

Ingredients:

  • 1.5 lbs boneless, skinless chicken breasts, cut into chunks
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon chipotle chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon smoked paprika
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Wooden skewers, soaked in water for 30 minutes

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, mix the olive oil, garlic, chipotle chili powder, smoked paprika, cumin, salt, and pepper.
  2. Add the chicken chunks to the bowl and toss to coat them evenly with the marinade. Allow the chicken to marinate for at least 15 minutes.
  3. Preheat your indoor grill to medium-high heat.
  4. Thread the marinated chicken chunks onto the soaked skewers.
  5. Grill the skewers for 6-7 minutes on each side, or until the chicken is fully cooked and has a delicious smoky char.

Serve these smoky chipotle chicken skewers with a side of creamy avocado dip and a fresh green salad for a satisfying winter barbecue meal.

Tangy BBQ Pulled Pork Sliders

Prep Time: 15 minutes
Cook Time: 8 hours (slow cooker)
Serving: 6

Ingredients:

  • 3 lbs pork shoulder
  • 1 cup BBQ sauce
  • 1/2 cup apple cider vinegar
  • 1/4 cup brown sugar
  • 1 tablespoon smoked paprika
  • 1 tablespoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon cayenne pepper
  • Slider buns
  • Coleslaw for topping

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, mix the BBQ sauce, apple cider vinegar, brown sugar, smoked paprika, garlic powder, and cayenne pepper.
  2. Place the pork shoulder in a slow cooker and pour the sauce mixture over it.
  3. Cook on low for 8 hours until the pork is tender and easy to shred.
  4. Once cooked, shred the pork using two forks and mix it with the cooking juices.
  5. Toast the slider buns and fill them with the tangy BBQ pulled pork. Top with coleslaw for an extra crunch.

These tangy BBQ pulled pork sliders are the epitome of comfort food and are perfect for a cozy winter gathering with friends and family.

Grilled Garlic Parmesan Corn on the Cob

Prep Time: 10 minutes
Cook Time: 15 minutes
Serving: 4

Ingredients:

  • 4 ears of corn, husked
  • 1/4 cup butter, melted
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your indoor grill to medium heat.
  2. In a small bowl, mix the melted butter and minced garlic.
  3. Brush the garlic butter mixture over the corn.
  4. Place the corn on the grill and cook for 10-15 minutes, turning occasionally, until the kernels are tender and slightly charred.
  5. Remove the corn from the grill and sprinkle with grated Parmesan cheese, salt, pepper, and fresh parsley.

This grilled garlic parmesan corn on the cob is a delightful side dish that brings the flavors of summer right to your winter barbecue table.

Conclusion

Indoor grilling during the winter season allows you to savor the smoky, charred flavors of barbecue without having to brave the cold. These cozy winter barbecue recipes are perfect for creating heartwarming meals that will keep you and your loved ones satisfied during the chilly months. So, fire up the indoor grill, gather your favorite ingredients, and get ready to enjoy the warmth and flavors of barbecue right in the comfort of your own home. Happy grilling!
