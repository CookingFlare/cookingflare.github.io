BBQ Chicken Quesadillas: Cheesy and Flavorful Recipes for Grilling


If you’re a fan of grilling and love the combination of gooey cheese, tender chicken, and zesty barbecue sauce, then BBQ chicken quesadillas are a must-try for your next cookout. These cheesy and flavorful quesadillas are a crowd-pleaser, perfect for entertaining or simply enjoying a delicious meal with your family. In this recipe, we’ll explore how to create the ultimate BBQ chicken quesadillas that will have everyone coming back for seconds.

Ingredients

  • 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1 cup of your favorite BBQ sauce
  • 1 teaspoon of smoked paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon of garlic powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 4 large flour tortillas
  • 2 cups of shredded Monterey Jack cheese
  • 1/2 cup of diced red onion
  • 1/4 cup of chopped fresh cilantro
  • Olive oil or cooking spray for grilling

Instructions

Step 1: Prepare the BBQ Chicken

  • Season the chicken breasts with smoked paprika, garlic powder, salt, and pepper.
  • Preheat your grill to medium-high heat and oil the grates to prevent sticking.
  • Grill the chicken for 6-7 minutes per side, or until fully cooked through.
  • Brush the BBQ sauce onto the chicken during the last few minutes of grilling, allowing it to caramelize and create a flavorful glaze.
  • Once cooked, remove the chicken from the grill and let it rest for a few minutes before slicing it into thin strips.

Step 2: Assemble the Quesadillas

  • Lay out two of the flour tortillas and sprinkle an even layer of shredded Monterey Jack cheese on each tortilla.
  • Divide the sliced BBQ chicken, diced red onion, and chopped cilantro equally between the two tortillas.
  • Top each with another layer of shredded cheese and place the remaining two tortillas on top to form two quesadillas.

Step 3: Grill the Quesadillas

  • Preheat a large skillet or grill pan over medium heat and lightly brush with olive oil or cooking spray.
  • Carefully transfer the assembled quesadillas to the skillet or grill pan and cook for 3-4 minutes on each side, or until the tortillas are golden brown and the cheese is melted.
  • If using a grill pan, you can also grill the quesadillas directly on the grill for added smoky flavor.

Step 4: Serve and Enjoy

  • Once the quesadillas are golden and crispy, remove them from the heat and let them rest for a minute before slicing into wedges.
  • Serve the BBQ chicken quesadillas with additional BBQ sauce, sour cream, guacamole, or salsa for dipping and enjoy the irresistible combination of flavors.

Conclusion

BBQ chicken quesadillas are a fantastic way to elevate your grilling game and impress your guests with a mouthwatering dish that brings together the best of barbecue and Mexican cuisine. Whether you’re hosting a summer barbecue, a game day gathering, or simply craving a delectable meal, these cheesy and flavorful quesadillas are sure to become a favorite in your recipe repertoire. So fire up the grill, gather your ingredients, and get ready to savor the irresistible taste of BBQ chicken quesadillas.
