Sizzling Garlic Shrimp Stir Fry Recipe


Indulge your taste buds with a delightful and easy-to-make Sizzling Garlic Shrimp Stir Fry. This dish is a perfect blend of flavors and textures that will leave you craving for more. With succulent shrimp, aromatic garlic, and a medley of colorful vegetables, this stir fry is a feast for both the eyes and the palate. Let’s dive into the recipe and unleash the sizzle in your kitchen!

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound of large shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 red bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 yellow bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 cup of sugar snap peas
  • 1 small onion, sliced
  • 2 tablespoons of soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon of oyster sauce
  • 1 teaspoon of sesame oil
  • 1/2 teaspoon of red pepper flakes (optional)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 tablespoons of vegetable oil

Instructions:

  1. Prep the Shrimp: Season the shrimp with salt and pepper. Heat 1 tablespoon of vegetable oil in a large pan over medium-high heat. Add the shrimp and cook until pink, about 2-3 minutes per side. Remove the shrimp from the pan and set aside.

  2. Sauté the Aromatics: In the same pan, heat the remaining tablespoon of vegetable oil. Add the minced garlic and sauté until fragrant, about 30 seconds. Be careful not to burn the garlic.

  3. Add Vegetables: Toss in the sliced red and yellow bell peppers, sugar snap peas, and onion. Stir-fry for 2-3 minutes until the vegetables are slightly tender but still crisp.

  4. Combine Flavors: Return the cooked shrimp to the pan. Add soy sauce, oyster sauce, sesame oil, and red pepper flakes (if using). Stir well to coat the shrimp and vegetables evenly.

  5. Finish and Serve: Cook for another 1-2 minutes until everything is heated through. Adjust seasoning with salt and pepper according to your taste. Remove from heat.

  6. Serve Hot: Transfer the sizzling garlic shrimp stir fry to a serving dish. Garnish with some chopped green onions or sesame seeds for an extra pop of flavor and visual appeal.

Tips for Success:

  • Fresh Ingredients: Use fresh shrimp and crisp vegetables for the best texture and flavor.
  • Quick Cooking: Stir fry dishes come together fast, so have all your ingredients prepped and ready to go.
  • Customize: Feel free to add your favorite vegetables or swap shrimp for chicken or tofu for a different twist.
  • Serve Creatively: This dish pairs perfectly with steamed rice or noodles. Get creative with your presentation to impress your guests.

Unleash the aroma and flavors of this sizzling garlic shrimp stir fry in your kitchen today. Whether you’re cooking for a quick weeknight dinner or entertaining guests, this recipe is sure to be a crowd-pleaser. Enjoy the sizzle and the satisfaction of creating a delicious meal that’s bursting with Asian-inspired goodness!
