Decadent Caramel Stuffed Chocolate Chip Cookies: A Sweet Surprise


There’s something truly magical about biting into a warm, freshly baked chocolate chip cookie. The soft and gooey center, combined with the crisp edges, creates a delightful texture that is hard to resist. But what if we took this classic treat to the next level of decadence? Enter the Decadent Caramel Stuffed Chocolate Chip Cookies – a sweet surprise that will have your taste buds dancing with joy.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 cup packed brown sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 2 cups chocolate chips
  • 20 caramel candies, unwrapped

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C) and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.

  2. In a large mixing bowl, cream together the softened butter, granulated sugar, and brown sugar until light and fluffy. This should take about 2-3 minutes.

  3. Add the eggs one at a time, mixing well after each addition. Stir in the vanilla extract.

  4. In a separate bowl, whisk together the all-purpose flour, baking soda, and salt. Gradually add the dry ingredients to the wet ingredients, mixing until just combined.

  5. Fold in the chocolate chips, ensuring they are evenly distributed throughout the cookie dough.

  6. Now comes the fun part – stuffing the cookies with caramel! Take a tablespoon of cookie dough and flatten it in the palm of your hand. Place a caramel candy in the center and carefully fold the dough around it, sealing it completely. Repeat this step until all the dough and caramel candies are used.

  7. Place the stuffed cookies onto the prepared baking sheet, leaving some space between each cookie to allow for spreading while baking.

  8. Bake for 12-15 minutes or until the edges are golden brown. The centers may still appear slightly undercooked, but don’t worry – they will firm up as they cool.

  9. Once out of the oven, let the cookies cool on the baking sheet for 5 minutes before transferring them to a wire rack to cool completely.

  10. Enjoy these Decadent Caramel Stuffed Chocolate Chip Cookies warm or at room temperature for the ultimate indulgence. The combination of the rich chocolate, gooey caramel, and buttery cookie dough is truly a sweet surprise that will leave you craving more.

Tips and Variations

  • For an extra flavor boost, consider adding a sprinkle of sea salt on top of each cookie just before baking. The saltiness will balance out the sweetness of the caramel and chocolate.
  • You can experiment with different types of chocolate chips, such as dark chocolate or white chocolate, to create unique flavor combinations.
  • If you prefer a softer and chewier cookie, reduce the baking time to 10-12 minutes.
  • These cookies can be stored in an airtight container at room temperature for up to 5 days. To reheat, simply warm them in the microwave for a few seconds or pop them in a preheated oven for a few minutes.

So why settle for an ordinary chocolate chip cookie when you can indulge in the extraordinary? These Decadent Caramel Stuffed Chocolate Chip Cookies are a sweet surprise that will elevate your baking game and satisfy your cravings for something truly decadent. Give them a try and prepare to be amazed by the gooey caramel center that awaits you with every bite. Enjoy!
