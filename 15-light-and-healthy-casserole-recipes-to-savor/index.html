15 Light and Healthy Casserole Recipes to Savor


Casseroles are the ultimate comfort food, combining convenience, flavor, and the ability to feed a crowd. However, many traditional casserole recipes can be heavy and loaded with calories. Fortunately, there are plenty of light and healthy alternatives that maintain the heartiness and satisfaction of a classic casserole without compromising your health goals. Here’s a collection of 15 light and healthy casserole recipes that you can savor, each packed with nutritious ingredients and bursting with flavor.

1. Quinoa and Vegetable Casserole

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup quinoa, rinsed
  • 2 cups vegetable broth
  • 1 zucchini, diced
  • 1 red bell pepper, diced
  • 1 cup spinach, chopped
  • 1/2 cup onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon Italian seasoning
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1/2 cup shredded mozzarella cheese (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. In a medium saucepan, combine quinoa and vegetable broth, bringing to a boil. Reduce heat to low, cover, and simmer for 15 minutes until liquid is absorbed.
  3. In a large skillet, sauté onion and garlic until translucent. Add zucchini, bell pepper, and spinach; cook until tender.
  4. In a large bowl, mix cooked quinoa, sautéed vegetables, Italian seasoning, salt, and pepper. Spread the mixture into a greased baking dish.
  5. If using, sprinkle mozzarella cheese on top. Bake for 20-25 minutes until heated through and cheese is bubbly.

2. Sweet Potato and Black Bean Casserole

Ingredients:

  • 2 large sweet potatoes, peeled and sliced
  • 1 can black beans, drained and rinsed
  • 1 cup corn kernels (fresh or frozen)
  • 1 red onion, diced
  • 2 teaspoons cumin
  • 1 teaspoon chili powder
  • 1 cup salsa
  • 1/2 cup Greek yogurt (for topping)

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 400°F (200°C).
  2. In a baking dish, layer half of the sweet potato slices. Top with black beans, corn, and onion. Sprinkle with cumin and chili powder.
  3. Add the remaining sweet potato slices on top and pour salsa over the entire dish.
  4. Cover with foil and bake for 30 minutes. Remove foil and bake for an additional 15 minutes until sweet potatoes are tender.
  5. Serve warm topped with a dollop of Greek yogurt.

3. Spinach and Ricotta Pasta Bake

Ingredients:

  • 8 oz whole grain pasta
  • 1 cup ricotta cheese
  • 2 cups fresh spinach
  • 1 cup marinara sauce
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. Cook pasta according to package instructions until al dente; drain.
  3. In a large bowl, combine cooked pasta, ricotta, spinach, marinara sauce, garlic powder, salt, and pepper.
  4. Transfer the mixture to a greased baking dish and sprinkle with Parmesan cheese.
  5. Bake for 25-30 minutes until bubbly and golden on top.

4. Mediterranean Chicken Casserole

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups cooked chicken, shredded
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1 cup chopped cucumbers
  • 1/2 cup Kalamata olives, pitted and sliced
  • 1/4 cup feta cheese
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons lemon juice
  • 1 teaspoon oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. In a large bowl, combine all ingredients, mixing well to combine.
  3. Transfer the mixture to a greased baking dish.
  4. Bake for 20-25 minutes until heated through and flavors meld together.

5. Cauliflower Rice Casserole

Ingredients:

  • 4 cups cauliflower rice
  • 1 cup diced carrots
  • 1 cup peas
  • 1 cup corn
  • 1/2 cup onion, chopped
  • 2 eggs, beaten
  • 1 cup shredded cheese (cheddar or mozzarella)
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. In a large bowl, mix cauliflower rice, carrots, peas, corn, onion, eggs, and half of the cheese. Season with salt and pepper.
  3. Spread the mixture into a greased baking dish and top with remaining cheese.
  4. Bake for 30-35 minutes until golden and firm.

6. Turkey and Vegetable Casserole

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb lean ground turkey
  • 1 cup chopped broccoli
  • 1 cup chopped bell pepper
  • 1 cup mushrooms, sliced
  • 1 cup low-sodium chicken broth
  • 1 teaspoon Italian seasoning
  • 1 cup brown rice, cooked

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. In a skillet, brown turkey until fully cooked. Add broccoli, bell pepper, and mushrooms; sauté until tender.
  3. In a large bowl, combine the turkey mixture, chicken broth, Italian seasoning, and cooked rice.
  4. Transfer to a baking dish and bake for 25-30 minutes until heated through.

7. Zucchini and Tomato Gratin

Ingredients:

  • 3 large zucchinis, sliced
  • 2 cups cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1/2 cup breadcrumbs (whole wheat)
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. In a large bowl, combine zucchini, tomatoes, garlic, olive oil, salt, and pepper.
  3. Spread the mixture in a greased baking dish and top with breadcrumbs and Parmesan cheese.
  4. Bake for 30-35 minutes until vegetables are tender and top is golden.

8. Eggplant Parmesan Casserole

Ingredients:

  • 2 medium eggplants, sliced
  • 2 cups marinara sauce
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/2 cup whole wheat breadcrumbs
  • 1 teaspoon Italian seasoning
  • Olive oil spray

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Lightly spray eggplant slices with olive oil and roast for 20 minutes until soft.
  3. In a baking dish, layer half of the marinara sauce, half of the eggplant, and half of the cheese. Repeat the layers and top with breadcrumbs.
  4. Bake for 30-35 minutes until cheese is bubbly and golden.

9. Lentil and Vegetable Casserole

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups cooked lentils
  • 1 cup diced carrots
  • 1 cup diced celery
  • 1 cup diced tomatoes (canned or fresh)
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon thyme
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. In a large bowl, combine all ingredients, mixing well.
  3. Transfer the mixture to a greased baking dish and cover with foil.
  4. Bake for 40-45 minutes, removing the foil during the last 10 minutes.

10. Broccoli and Cheddar Quinoa Bake

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup quinoa, rinsed
  • 2 cups broccoli florets
  • 1 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • 2 eggs, beaten
  • 1 cup low-fat milk
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. Cook quinoa according to package instructions. Steam broccoli until tender.
  3. In a large bowl, combine quinoa, broccoli, cheese, eggs, milk, salt, and pepper.
  4. Pour the mixture into a greased baking dish and bake for 30-35 minutes until set.

11. Chicken and Asparagus Casserole

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups cooked chicken, shredded
  • 1 bunch asparagus, trimmed and cut into pieces
  • 1 cup low-fat cream of mushroom soup
  • 1/2 cup Greek yogurt
  • 1/2 cup shredded cheese
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. In a large bowl, mix chicken, asparagus, soup, yogurt, cheese, salt, and pepper.
  3. Spread the mixture into a greased baking dish and bake for 25 minutes until heated through.

12. Mexican Quinoa Casserole

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup quinoa, rinsed
  • 1 can black beans, drained
  • 1 cup corn
  • 1 cup salsa
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • 1/2 cup shredded cheese

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook quinoa according to package instructions.
  3. In a large bowl, combine cooked quinoa, black beans, corn, salsa, cumin, and half the cheese.
  4. Transfer to a greased baking dish, top with remaining cheese, and bake for 20-25 minutes.
