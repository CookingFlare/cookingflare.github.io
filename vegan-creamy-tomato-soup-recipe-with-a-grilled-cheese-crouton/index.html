Vegan Creamy Tomato Soup Recipe with a Grilled Cheese Crouton


Who doesn’t love a hot bowl of tomato soup? It’s a classic comfort food that brings back memories of cozy winter evenings and childhood lunches. But what if you’re following a vegan diet? Can you still enjoy the creamy goodness of tomato soup without the dairy? Absolutely! In this recipe, we’ll show you how to make a vegan creamy tomato soup that’s so delicious, you won’t miss the dairy at all. And to take it up a notch, we’ll even add a grilled cheese crouton on top. Let’s get cooking!

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 can (28 ounces) crushed tomatoes
  • 1 can (14 ounces) coconut milk
  • 1 cup vegetable broth
  • 1 tablespoon tomato paste
  • 1 tablespoon maple syrup
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper, to taste

For the Grilled Cheese Crouton:

  • 4 slices of your favorite vegan bread
  • 4 slices of vegan cheese (such as vegan cheddar or mozzarella)
  • Vegan butter for spreading

Instructions

  1. In a large pot, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the diced onion and minced garlic, and sauté until the onion becomes translucent and fragrant.

  2. Add the crushed tomatoes, coconut milk, vegetable broth, tomato paste, maple syrup, dried basil, and dried oregano to the pot. Stir well to combine all the ingredients.

  3. Bring the mixture to a boil, then reduce the heat to low and let it simmer for about 20 minutes, allowing the flavors to meld together.

  4. After simmering, use an immersion blender or transfer the soup to a blender to puree until smooth and creamy. Be careful when blending hot liquids - make sure to vent the blender lid or use an immersion blender directly in the pot.

  5. Return the soup to the pot and season with salt and pepper to taste. If the soup seems too thick, you can add a bit more vegetable broth to thin it out.

  6. While the soup is simmering, let’s make the grilled cheese crouton. Preheat a non-stick skillet over medium heat.

  7. Butter one side of each slice of bread. Place two slices, buttered side down, in the skillet. Top each slice with a slice of vegan cheese, then place the remaining bread slices on top, buttered side up.

  8. Cook the sandwich for about 2-3 minutes on each side, until the bread is golden brown and the cheese is melted.

  9. Remove the sandwich from the skillet and let it cool for a minute. Then, cut the sandwich into small cubes to create the grilled cheese crouton.

  10. Ladle the creamy tomato soup into bowls and top each serving with a handful of grilled cheese croutons.

  11. Serve the soup hot and enjoy the creamy, tomatoey goodness with the delightful crunch of the grilled cheese crouton!

Conclusion

This vegan creamy tomato soup with a grilled cheese crouton is the perfect combination of comfort and flavor. The velvety texture of the soup, combined with the crunchy, cheesy crouton, will make every spoonful a delight. Whether you’re following a vegan diet or just looking for a healthier alternative to traditional tomato soup, this recipe is sure to satisfy your cravings. So, grab a bowl, cozy up, and indulge in this vegan twist on a classic favorite. Bon appétit!
