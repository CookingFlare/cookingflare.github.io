Delicious and Satisfying Italian Sausage and Rice Stuffed Peppers


If you’re in the mood for a hearty and flavorful dish, look no further than these delicious Italian Sausage and Rice Stuffed Peppers. This recipe combines the savory goodness of Italian sausage with the satisfying texture of rice, all packed into a vibrant bell pepper shell. It’s a dish that’s sure to impress both family and friends!

Ingredients

To make this mouthwatering dish, you’ll need the following ingredients:

  • 4 large bell peppers (any color)
  • 1 pound Italian sausage, casings removed
  • 1 cup cooked rice
  • 1 small onion, diced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1 cup marinara sauce
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C). Line a baking dish with parchment paper or lightly grease it to prevent sticking.

  2. Cut the tops off the bell peppers and remove the seeds and membranes. Rinse the peppers under cold water to remove any remaining seeds. Set aside.

  3. In a large skillet, cook the Italian sausage over medium heat until browned and cooked through. Use a wooden spoon to break up the meat into smaller pieces as it cooks.

  4. Once the sausage is cooked, add the diced onion and minced garlic to the skillet. Cook for an additional 2-3 minutes, or until the onion becomes translucent and fragrant.

  5. Next, add the cooked rice, dried oregano, dried basil, salt, and black pepper to the skillet. Stir well to combine all the ingredients and cook for another 2-3 minutes to allow the flavors to meld together.

  6. Remove the skillet from heat and add 1/2 cup of marinara sauce to the sausage and rice mixture. Stir until everything is well coated.

  7. Spoon the sausage and rice mixture into each bell pepper, filling them to the top. Place the peppers in the prepared baking dish.

  8. Pour the remaining marinara sauce over the stuffed peppers, making sure to evenly distribute it.

  9. Cover the baking dish with aluminum foil and bake in the preheated oven for 25 minutes.

  10. After 25 minutes, remove the foil and sprinkle the shredded mozzarella cheese over the tops of the peppers. Return the dish to the oven and bake for an additional 10-15 minutes, or until the cheese is melted and bubbling.

  11. Once the peppers are done baking, remove them from the oven and let them cool for a few minutes before serving.

  12. Garnish with fresh basil or parsley, if desired, and enjoy your delicious and satisfying Italian Sausage and Rice Stuffed Peppers!

Conclusion

These Italian Sausage and Rice Stuffed Peppers are a delightful combination of flavors and textures. The savory Italian sausage, tender rice, and gooey melted cheese create a dish that is both comforting and satisfying. Whether you’re serving them as a main course or as a side dish, these stuffed peppers are sure to please even the pickiest eaters. So, next time you’re in the mood for something delicious and different, give this recipe a try and savor the flavors of Italy right in your own kitchen!
