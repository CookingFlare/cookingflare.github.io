Savory and Spicy Cajun Chicken and Shrimp Pasta with Andouille Sausage


Are you a fan of bold flavors, a hint of spice, and a hearty meal that will satisfy your cravings? Look no further than this delicious Savory and Spicy Cajun Chicken and Shrimp Pasta with Andouille Sausage recipe! Packed with a medley of flavors and textures, this dish is perfect for those who love the vibrant tastes of Cajun cuisine. Let’s dive right in and get cooking!

Ingredients

  • 8 ounces of linguine pasta
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 8 ounces of chicken breast, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 8 ounces of shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 4 ounces of Andouille sausage, sliced
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 1 green bell pepper, diced
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 can (14.5 ounces) of diced tomatoes
  • 1 cup of chicken broth
  • 2 teaspoons of Cajun seasoning
  • 1/2 teaspoon of paprika
  • 1/4 teaspoon of cayenne pepper (adjust according to your spice preference)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley, chopped (for garnish)

Instructions

  1. Cook the linguine pasta according to the package instructions until al dente. Drain and set aside.

  2. In a large skillet, heat the olive oil over medium-high heat. Add the chicken breast pieces and cook until they are browned and cooked through. Remove the chicken from the skillet and set aside.

  3. In the same skillet, add the Andouille sausage slices and cook until they are browned and slightly crispy. Remove the sausage from the skillet and set aside.

  4. Add the shrimp to the skillet and cook until they turn pink and are cooked through. Remove the shrimp from the skillet and set aside.

  5. In the same skillet, add the diced onion, green bell pepper, and minced garlic. Cook until the vegetables are tender and fragrant.

  6. Add the diced tomatoes, chicken broth, Cajun seasoning, paprika, and cayenne pepper to the skillet. Stir well to combine. Season with salt and pepper to taste.

  7. Bring the sauce to a simmer and let it cook for 10 minutes, allowing the flavors to meld together.

  8. Add the cooked linguine pasta, chicken breast, Andouille sausage, and shrimp to the skillet with the sauce. Toss everything together until well coated.

  9. Cook for an additional 2-3 minutes, allowing the pasta and proteins to absorb the flavors of the sauce.

  10. Remove the skillet from the heat and garnish with freshly chopped parsley.

  11. Serve hot and enjoy the explosion of flavors in every bite!

Tips and Variations

  • To make this dish even spicier, increase the amount of cayenne pepper or add a dash of hot sauce.
  • If you prefer a milder version, reduce the amount of Cajun seasoning and omit the cayenne pepper.
  • Feel free to experiment with different types of pasta, such as penne or fettuccine, to suit your preferences.
  • You can also add other vegetables like cherry tomatoes or sliced mushrooms for added texture and flavor.
  • For a creamier version, stir in a splash of heavy cream or coconut milk at the end.

So there you have it, a mouthwatering recipe for Savory and Spicy Cajun Chicken and Shrimp Pasta with Andouille Sausage! Whether you’re cooking for a special occasion or simply craving a comforting meal, this dish is sure to impress. Gather your ingredients, unleash your inner chef, and get ready to indulge in a burst of Cajun flavors. Bon appétit!
