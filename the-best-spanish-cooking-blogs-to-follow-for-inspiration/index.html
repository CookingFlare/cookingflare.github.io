The Best Spanish Cooking Blogs to Follow for Inspiration


When it comes to exploring rich culinary traditions, few countries offer the depth and variety of Spanish cuisine. Known for its use of fresh, high-quality ingredients, bold flavors, and regional diversity, Spain’s cooking styles reflect centuries of cultural influences. From the coastal Mediterranean dishes to hearty stews from the northern regions, Spanish food has something for every palate. For those looking to deepen their understanding of Spanish cooking or simply find inspiration for their next meal, Spanish cooking blogs are a treasure trove of recipes, tips, and culinary insights.

In this post, we’ll explore some of the best Spanish cooking blogs to follow, whether you’re a beginner or an experienced chef. These blogs provide authentic recipes, step-by-step instructions, and creative takes on traditional dishes. Let’s dive into the world of Spanish cuisine and discover the top food blogs that will help elevate your culinary skills.

1. The Spanish Kitchen

Why You Should Follow It:

The Spanish Kitchen offers a fantastic array of authentic Spanish recipes that cover both traditional and modern interpretations of the country’s iconic dishes. The blog’s founder, who hails from Spain, provides a personal touch in every recipe, making you feel as though you’re cooking alongside a native chef. Whether you’re looking for tapas, seafood, or desserts, The Spanish Kitchen has something for every occasion.

What to Expect:

  • Detailed Recipes: Each recipe on The Spanish Kitchen is thoroughly explained with clear steps, tips, and serving suggestions.
  • Focus on Local Ingredients: The blog emphasizes the use of regional and seasonal ingredients, which is key to creating authentic Spanish meals.
  • Regional Dishes: The blog often explores recipes from specific regions of Spain, helping you discover lesser-known but equally delicious dishes from places like the Basque Country or Andalusia.

Popular Recipes:

  • Paella Valenciana
  • Spanish Tortilla (Spanish Omelette)
  • Gambas al Ajillo (Garlic Shrimp)

2. Spanish Sabores

Why You Should Follow It:

Spanish Sabores is run by a passionate food writer and travel enthusiast, Lauren Aloise, who is based in Spain. This blog offers a delightful combination of travel experiences and food, often linking specific regions of Spain with their corresponding culinary specialties. Lauren’s love for Spanish cuisine shines through in her detailed posts, which often include personal stories and insights.

What to Expect:

  • In-Depth Travel and Food Guides: Beyond just recipes, Spanish Sabores provides detailed guides to the culinary culture of different Spanish regions. It’s a fantastic resource if you want to explore the country’s gastronomy in a more holistic way.
  • Easy-to-Follow Recipes: Lauren’s recipes are designed for all levels of experience, with clear instructions and beautiful photography.
  • Wine Pairings: Spanish Sabores often pairs dishes with wine recommendations, as Spain is famous for its wine culture.

Popular Recipes:

  • Patatas Bravas
  • Sopa de Ajo (Garlic Soup)
  • Churros con Chocolate

3. The Spanish Chef

Why You Should Follow It:

The Spanish Chef is a must-follow for anyone serious about mastering Spanish cuisine. Whether you’re looking to recreate iconic dishes or experiment with contemporary takes, this blog covers a wide spectrum of recipes. The Spanish Chef is written by a professional chef who brings years of experience to the table, providing you with techniques and tricks that are usually only found in restaurant kitchens.

What to Expect:

  • Chef-Level Techniques: This blog goes beyond just providing recipes and delves into professional cooking techniques that will help elevate your dishes.
  • Step-by-Step Video Tutorials: The Spanish Chef often includes video tutorials alongside recipes, making it easier to follow along and learn visually.
  • Recipe Variations: If you’re interested in experimenting, the blog often provides multiple variations of classic Spanish dishes.

Popular Recipes:

  • Arroz con Pollo (Chicken and Rice)
  • Tortas de Aceite (Olive Oil Biscuits)
  • Paella de Marisco (Seafood Paella)

4. La Tienda Blog

Why You Should Follow It:

La Tienda Blog is associated with La Tienda, one of the most well-known Spanish food retailers in the United States. While La Tienda Blog’s focus is on showcasing the best Spanish products available, it also offers a plethora of traditional recipes that highlight the use of these premium ingredients. If you love using top-quality olive oil, Iberian ham, or Spanish cheeses, La Tienda Blog is an invaluable resource.

What to Expect:

  • Ingredient-Focused Recipes: Many of the recipes on this blog center around La Tienda’s products, making it an ideal resource for those who want to use authentic Spanish ingredients.
  • Educational Content: The blog offers in-depth articles about the history and importance of various Spanish foods and beverages, such as jamón ibérico and sherry.
  • Sustainable Cooking: The blog emphasizes sustainability by encouraging the use of high-quality, locally sourced ingredients.

Popular Recipes:

  • Jamón Ibérico Croquettes
  • Salmorejo (Tomato Soup)
  • Spanish Tortilla de Patatas

5. My Kitchen in Spain

Why You Should Follow It:

My Kitchen in Spain is a blog written by renowned Spanish food writer and chef, Sarah. Her approach to Spanish cooking is warm, inviting, and informative. Whether you are craving classic Spanish comfort food or are interested in learning how to prepare regional dishes, My Kitchen in Spain has a diverse range of recipes to explore.

What to Expect:

  • Seasonal Recipes: Sarah’s recipes focus on the use of seasonal produce, which helps you cook with the freshest ingredients available.
  • Authentic Spanish Dishes: The blog is known for its traditional Spanish recipes, many of which are passed down through generations.
  • Comprehensive Cooking Tips: Sarah offers cooking tips for both beginners and seasoned chefs, ensuring that every reader can improve their Spanish culinary skills.

Popular Recipes:

  • Pisto Manchego (Spanish Ratatouille)
  • Cochinillo Asado (Roast Suckling Pig)
  • Flan de Huevo (Spanish Egg Flan)

6. Spanish Food Guide

Why You Should Follow It:

Spanish Food Guide is a comprehensive online resource dedicated to Spanish gastronomy. The blog provides readers with easy-to-follow recipes along with information about the origins and cultural significance of the dishes. Spanish Food Guide stands out for its attention to both modern and traditional Spanish cuisine, making it a great place to explore the evolution of Spain’s culinary heritage.

What to Expect:

  • Detailed Regional Breakdowns: The blog offers in-depth exploration of Spain’s culinary regions, with a focus on local ingredients, dishes, and food culture.
  • Recipe Cards: Each recipe is accompanied by a printable recipe card, making it easier to cook without needing to scroll through long posts.
  • Helpful Cooking Videos: Spanish Food Guide frequently posts cooking videos that show the process of preparing classic Spanish dishes, making it easier for readers to replicate them at home.

Popular Recipes:

  • Paella Mixta (Mixed Paella)
  • Calamares a la Romana (Fried Squid Rings)
  • Tarta de Santiago (Almond Cake)

7. Tapas Revolution

Why You Should Follow It:

Tapas Revolution, created by Spanish chef Omar Allibhoy, is a fantastic blog for anyone interested in creating delicious and authentic tapas. Omar is well-known in the UK for bringing Spanish tapas to the forefront, and his blog is packed with vibrant recipes that focus on small plates perfect for sharing. If you’re looking to throw a tapas party or simply want to experiment with bite-sized dishes, Tapas Revolution is a must-visit.

What to Expect:

  • Fun and Creative Tapas: The blog showcases a variety of tapas recipes, from the traditional to more inventive takes on classic dishes.
  • Quick and Easy Recipes: Tapas Revolution is perfect for home cooks who want to create impressive meals without spending hours in the kitchen.
  • Dips and Sauces: Tapas would be nothing without delicious sauces, and Omar’s blog features several inventive options.

Popular Recipes:

  • Albondigas (Spanish Meatballs)
  • Patatas a la Pobre (Poor Man’s Potatoes)
  • Pan con Tomate (Tomato Bread)

8. Cooking with Alba

Why You Should Follow It:

Cooking with Alba is a wonderful blog that blends traditional Spanish flavors with a modern twist. Alba, the founder, is dedicated to sharing Spanish home cooking recipes with readers worldwide. Her blog is approachable, offering a range of recipes that reflect the heart and soul of Spanish family meals.

What to Expect:

  • Comforting Home Recipes: Cooking with Alba focuses on simple, comforting dishes that families in Spain cook at home.
  • Detailed Step-by-Step Instructions: Alba provides clear, easy-to-follow instructions that make even the most complicated recipes manageable.
  • Incorporating Modern Elements: While the focus is on traditional recipes, Alba often incorporates modern cooking techniques to make her dishes more accessible to contemporary kitchens.

Popular Recipes:

  • Empanadas Gallegas (Galician Empanadas)
  • Fabada Asturiana (Asturian Bean Stew)
  • Tarta de Queso (Spanish Cheesecake)

9. The Sherry Corner

Why You Should Follow It:

For those who appreciate the complexities of Spanish wines, The Sherry Corner is a blog dedicated to sherry wine and its culinary pairings. Although the focus is on sherry, The Sherry Corner also provides delicious recipes that incorporate sherry as a key ingredient. It’s a unique resource for wine lovers who want to enhance their cooking with the depth of Spanish wines.

What to Expect:

  • Sherry Pairing Ideas: Each recipe on The Sherry Corner often includes suggestions for pairing dishes with different types of sherry, from dry to sweet varieties.
  • Recipes with Sherry: The blog provides recipes that use sherry as an ingredient, showcasing its versatility in both savory and sweet dishes.
  • Wine Education: Learn more about the history and production of sherry, and why it holds such an important place in Spanish cuisine.

Popular Recipes:

  • Sherry-Infused Risotto
  • Pollo al Jerez (Chicken in Sherry Sauce)
  • Tarta de Santiago with Sherry Cream

Conclusion

Spanish cuisine is rich in flavor, history, and culture, and exploring it through these exceptional food blogs is an excellent way to dive deeper into the country’s gastronomic traditions. Whether you’re looking for traditional recipes, modern twists on old favorites, or a focus on regional specialties, these blogs offer something for every aspiring chef. By following these culinary experts, you’ll be able to bring the tastes and techniques of Spain directly into your own kitchen, making your meals both delicious and memorable.

Happy cooking!
