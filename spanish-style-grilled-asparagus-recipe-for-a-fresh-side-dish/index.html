Spanish Style Grilled Asparagus: Recipe for a Fresh Side Dish


When it comes to fresh, vibrant side dishes, Spanish-style grilled asparagus is a delightful choice that brings a burst of flavor to any meal. This simple yet elegant dish captures the essence of Mediterranean cuisine with its bold flavors and healthy appeal. Whether you’re hosting a summer barbecue or simply craving a light and flavorful accompaniment to your main course, this recipe is sure to impress your taste buds and those of your guests.

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound fresh asparagus, trimmed
  • 2 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon smoked paprika
  • 1 teaspoon sea salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper
  • 1 tablespoon fresh lemon juice
  • 1 teaspoon lemon zest

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the Asparagus: Begin by preparing the asparagus. Wash the asparagus spears thoroughly under cold water and pat them dry with a clean kitchen towel. Trim off the tough ends of the asparagus to ensure tender, flavorful results.

  2. Marinate the Asparagus: In a small bowl, combine the extra virgin olive oil, minced garlic, smoked paprika, sea salt, and black pepper. Whisk the ingredients together to create a fragrant and flavorful marinade. Place the trimmed asparagus spears in a shallow dish and pour the marinade over them, ensuring that each spear is evenly coated. Allow the asparagus to marinate for at least 15 minutes, allowing the flavors to infuse the spears.

  3. Preheat the Grill: While the asparagus is marinating, preheat your grill to medium-high heat. The grill should be clean and lightly oiled to prevent the asparagus from sticking.

  4. Grill the Asparagus: Once the grill is hot and ready, carefully place the marinated asparagus spears on the grill grates. Grill the asparagus for 3-4 minutes on each side, or until they are tender-crisp and have developed a slightly charred appearance. The cooking time may vary depending on the thickness of the asparagus spears, so keep a close eye on them to prevent overcooking.

  5. Finish with Fresh Lemon: As soon as the asparagus is done grilling, transfer the spears to a serving platter. Drizzle the grilled asparagus with fresh lemon juice and sprinkle the lemon zest over the top. The bright acidity of the lemon juice adds a refreshing zing to the smoky flavors of the grilled asparagus, creating a harmonious balance of taste.

  6. Serve and Enjoy: Your Spanish-style grilled asparagus is now ready to be served. Arrange the spears on a platter and garnish with a sprinkle of additional sea salt and black pepper, if desired. This vibrant and flavorful side dish pairs beautifully with grilled chicken, seafood, or even as part of a vegetarian spread. Enjoy the delightful combination of smoky, earthy, and citrusy flavors with each bite.

Conclusion:

Spanish-style grilled asparagus is a celebration of simplicity and bold flavors, making it a perfect addition to any culinary repertoire. This versatile side dish not only showcases the natural beauty of asparagus but also embodies the spirit of Mediterranean cuisine with its use of olive oil, garlic, and smoked paprika. Whether you’re a seasoned home cook or a novice in the kitchen, this recipe offers a delightful way to elevate your meals with minimal effort and maximum flavor. So, fire up the grill and savor the irresistible taste of Spanish-style grilled asparagus – a fresh side dish that’s sure to impress!
