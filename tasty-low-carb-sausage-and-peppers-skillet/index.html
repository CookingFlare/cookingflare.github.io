Tasty Low Carb Sausage and Peppers Skillet

4 min · 685 words

Looking for a delicious and healthy meal that’s quick and easy to prepare? Look no further than this Tasty Low Carb Sausage and Peppers Skillet recipe! Packed with flavor and low in carbs, this dish is perfect for those following a low-carb or keto lifestyle. Plus, it’s a one-pan wonder, making cleanup a breeze!

Ingredients

  • 1 pound of your favorite low-carb sausage, sliced
  • 1 red bell pepper, thinly sliced
  • 1 green bell pepper, thinly sliced
  • 1 yellow bell pepper, thinly sliced
  • 1 onion, thinly sliced
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon of dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon of dried basil
  • 1/2 teaspoon of crushed red pepper flakes (optional)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 tablespoons of olive oil

Instructions

  1. Heat the olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Add the sliced sausage and cook until browned, about 5 minutes. Remove the sausage from the skillet and set aside.

  2. In the same skillet, add the sliced bell peppers and onion. Cook until the vegetables are tender, about 8-10 minutes.

  3. Add the minced garlic, dried oregano, dried basil, crushed red pepper flakes (if using), salt, and pepper to the skillet. Stir well to combine and cook for an additional 2 minutes.

  4. Return the cooked sausage to the skillet and stir everything together. Cook for another 3-4 minutes, allowing the flavors to meld together.

  5. Remove from heat and serve hot. This dish can be enjoyed on its own or paired with cauliflower rice, zucchini noodles, or a side salad for a complete meal.

Why Choose Low Carb Sausage and Peppers Skillet?

Not only is this recipe bursting with flavor, but it’s also a great option for those looking to reduce their carb intake. By using low-carb sausage and replacing traditional pasta or rice with low-carb alternatives like cauliflower rice or zucchini noodles, you can enjoy a satisfying meal without the guilt.

Low-carb diets have been shown to have numerous health benefits, including weight loss, improved blood sugar control, and reduced risk of chronic diseases. This recipe provides a delicious way to incorporate more low-carb options into your diet.

Tips and Variations

  • Experiment with different types of low-carb sausages to find your favorite flavor combination. Whether you prefer spicy Italian sausage or a milder chicken sausage, the choice is yours!
  • Feel free to add other vegetables to the skillet, such as mushrooms or cherry tomatoes, to amp up the nutritional value and add extra color to your dish.
  • If you prefer a saucier skillet, you can stir in a can of diced tomatoes or tomato sauce before adding the sausage back to the skillet.
  • Leftovers can be stored in an airtight container in the refrigerator for up to 3 days. Simply reheat in a skillet or microwave when ready to enjoy.

Conclusion

This Tasty Low Carb Sausage and Peppers Skillet recipe is a winner in terms of taste, convenience, and health benefits. With just a few simple ingredients and minimal preparation, you can create a flavorful and satisfying meal that will keep you on track with your low-carb goals. Give it a try and see for yourself how delicious and easy low-carb cooking can be!
