Quick and Easy Instant Pot Baked Potatoes Recipe


If you’re in need of a quick and easy side dish or a simple meal, look no further than the Instant Pot. This versatile kitchen appliance can make cooking a breeze, and one of its standout features is the ability to perfectly cook potatoes in a fraction of the time it takes to bake them in the oven. In this recipe, I’ll show you how to make delicious baked potatoes in your Instant Pot, complete with fluffy insides and crispy skins. Let’s dive in!

Ingredients

  • 4 medium-sized russet potatoes
  • 1 cup of water
  • Salt, to taste
  • Toppings of your choice (butter, sour cream, chives, bacon bits, cheese, etc.)

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Potatoes: Begin by thoroughly washing the potatoes to remove any dirt. Use a fork to poke a few holes in each potato. This will allow steam to escape during the cooking process.

  2. Add Water to the Instant Pot: Pour the cup of water into the inner pot of your Instant Pot. This will create the steam necessary to cook the potatoes.

  3. Insert the Trivet: Place the trivet that came with your Instant Pot into the inner pot. This will keep the potatoes out of the water, allowing them to steam evenly.

  4. Pressure Cook the Potatoes: Carefully place the prepared potatoes on the trivet inside the Instant Pot. Close the lid and set the valve to the sealing position. Select the “Manual” or “Pressure Cook” setting and set the timer for 12 minutes on high pressure.

  5. Natural Pressure Release: Once the cooking cycle is complete, allow the pressure to release naturally for 10 minutes. After 10 minutes, carefully release any remaining pressure by turning the valve to the venting position.

  6. Check for Doneness: Using a pair of tongs, carefully remove the potatoes from the Instant Pot. To check for doneness, insert a fork into the center of each potato. If it slides in easily, the potatoes are done. If they need a bit more time, return them to the Instant Pot and cook for an additional 1-2 minutes under high pressure.

  7. Serve and Enjoy: Once the potatoes are cooked to perfection, it’s time to add your favorite toppings. Split each potato open with a knife, fluff the insides with a fork, and season with salt. Then, load them up with your desired toppings, such as butter, sour cream, chives, bacon bits, or cheese.

Why Make Instant Pot Baked Potatoes?

The Instant Pot offers a few key advantages when it comes to making baked potatoes. First, it significantly reduces the cooking time compared to traditional oven baking. This is ideal for busy weeknights when you want a hearty side dish without the long wait. Additionally, the pressure cooking method ensures that the potatoes are evenly cooked and come out with the perfect texture every time. No more worrying about unevenly cooked potatoes or tough skins.

Customizing Your Baked Potatoes

One of the best things about baked potatoes is the endless possibilities for customization. Once your potatoes are cooked, you can get creative with the toppings. Classic options like butter, sour cream, and chives are always a hit, but feel free to experiment with your favorite flavors. Try adding chili, steamed broccoli, or even pulled pork for a hearty meal in a potato.

In Conclusion

The Instant Pot is a game-changer when it comes to making quick and easy baked potatoes. With just a handful of ingredients and minimal effort, you can enjoy perfectly cooked potatoes that are ready to be loaded with your favorite toppings. Whether you’re serving them as a side dish or making a meal out of them, this Instant Pot baked potatoes recipe is sure to become a staple in your kitchen. So, fire up your Instant Pot and get ready to enjoy a satisfying and fuss-free dish that the whole family will love!

That’s it for this recipe. I hope you give it a try and enjoy the convenience and deliciousness of Instant Pot baked potatoes. Happy cooking!
