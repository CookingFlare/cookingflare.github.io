Pasta with Roasted Garlic and Spinach: A Healthy and Flavorful Option


If you’re looking for a delicious and healthy pasta dish that’s bursting with flavor, look no further than this Pasta with Roasted Garlic and Spinach recipe. It’s a perfect combination of wholesome ingredients and mouthwatering flavors that will satisfy your cravings while keeping your health in check.

Ingredients

  • 12 ounces of whole wheat pasta
  • 1 head of garlic
  • 2 tablespoons of olive oil
  • 4 cups of fresh spinach
  • 1/4 teaspoon of red pepper flakes
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Grated Parmesan cheese for garnish

Instructions

Step 1: Roast the Garlic

Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C). Cut off the top of the garlic head to expose the cloves. Place the garlic on a piece of aluminum foil, drizzle it with a tablespoon of olive oil, and wrap it tightly in the foil. Roast the garlic in the oven for 30-35 minutes, or until the cloves are soft and golden brown. Once roasted, allow the garlic to cool before squeezing the cloves out of their skins.

Step 2: Cook the Pasta

While the garlic is roasting, bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Add the whole wheat pasta and cook according to the package instructions until al dente. Drain the pasta, reserving a cup of the cooking water.

Step 3: Sauté the Spinach

In a large skillet, heat the remaining tablespoon of olive oil over medium heat. Add the red pepper flakes and sauté for a minute to infuse the oil with a hint of heat. Then, add the fresh spinach to the skillet and cook it until it wilts, stirring occasionally. This should take about 3-4 minutes.

Step 4: Combine the Ingredients

Once the spinach is wilted, add the roasted garlic cloves to the skillet. Use a fork to mash the garlic into the spinach, creating a fragrant and flavorful base for the pasta. Season the mixture with salt and pepper to taste.

Step 5: Bring It All Together

Add the cooked pasta to the skillet with the spinach and garlic. Toss everything together, adding a splash of the reserved pasta cooking water if needed to loosen the sauce and help it coat the pasta evenly.

Step 6: Serve and Enjoy

Divide the pasta with roasted garlic and spinach among serving plates or bowls. Garnish each serving with a generous sprinkle of grated Parmesan cheese for a savory finish. Serve it hot and enjoy the delightful flavors and wholesome goodness of this healthy pasta dish.

Final Thoughts

This Pasta with Roasted Garlic and Spinach recipe is a fantastic way to incorporate more greens and whole grains into your diet without sacrificing taste. The roasted garlic adds a rich, earthy flavor to the dish, while the spinach brings a vibrant pop of color and nutrients. It’s a versatile recipe that can be enjoyed as a light lunch, a satisfying dinner, or even as a side dish alongside your favorite protein.

So, the next time you’re craving a comforting pasta dish that’s both healthy and flavorful, give this recipe a try. It’s a simple yet impressive dish that will delight your taste buds and nourish your body. Enjoy the process of cooking and savoring this delightful meal, knowing that you’re treating yourself to a wholesome and delicious culinary experience.
