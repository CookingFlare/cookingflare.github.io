Fourth of July BBQ Desserts: No-Bake Recipes to Try


As the Fourth of July approaches, the excitement of summer barbecues fills the air. The sizzling sounds of the grill, the laughter of friends and family, and the vibrant colors of fireworks create a festive atmosphere. While savory dishes often take center stage at these gatherings, desserts play a crucial role in rounding out the meal. This year, why not impress your guests with some delightful no-bake desserts? They are easy to prepare, require minimal effort, and are perfect for the hot summer days. Here are some scrumptious no-bake dessert recipes that will make your Fourth of July BBQ unforgettable.

1. Red, White, and Blueberry Parfaits

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups of Greek yogurt (plain or vanilla)
  • 2 cups of mixed berries (strawberries, blueberries, raspberries)
  • 1 cup of granola
  • 2 tablespoons of honey (optional)
  • Fresh mint leaves for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the Berries: Wash the berries thoroughly. Slice the strawberries if they are large, leaving the blueberries and raspberries whole.
  2. Layer the Ingredients: In clear cups or mason jars, start by adding a layer of Greek yogurt at the bottom. Follow this with a layer of granola, then a layer of mixed berries.
  3. Repeat the Layers: Continue layering until you reach the top of the cup. Aim for at least two layers of each ingredient to create a beautiful visual effect.
  4. Drizzle with Honey: If you prefer a sweeter parfait, drizzle honey on top of the final layer of berries.
  5. Garnish and Serve: Add a fresh mint leaf on top for a pop of color. Serve immediately or refrigerate until ready to serve.

These parfaits are not only visually appealing but also packed with protein and antioxidants, making them a healthy dessert option.

2. No-Bake Strawberry Shortcake

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound of fresh strawberries, hulled and sliced
  • 1 cup of heavy whipping cream
  • 1/4 cup of powdered sugar
  • 1 teaspoon of vanilla extract
  • 1 package of store-bought shortcake biscuits or sponge cake
  • Mint leaves for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the Strawberries: In a bowl, combine the sliced strawberries with a tablespoon of powdered sugar. Let them sit for about 15 minutes to release their juices.
  2. Whip the Cream: In a separate bowl, whip the heavy cream, remaining powdered sugar, and vanilla extract until soft peaks form.
  3. Assemble the Shortcake: On a plate, place a shortcake biscuit, top it with a generous dollop of whipped cream, and add a layer of strawberries. Repeat the process to create a second layer.
  4. Garnish: Finish with a final dollop of whipped cream and a few mint leaves for decoration.
  5. Serve: Enjoy immediately or chill for a short while before serving.

This no-bake strawberry shortcake is a classic dessert that captures the essence of summer and is sure to be a hit at your BBQ.

3. Chocolate Mousse Cups

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup of heavy cream
  • 1/2 cup of semi-sweet chocolate chips
  • 2 tablespoons of sugar
  • 1 teaspoon of vanilla extract
  • Whipped cream and chocolate shavings for topping

Instructions:

  1. Melt the Chocolate: In a microwave-safe bowl, melt the chocolate chips in 30-second intervals, stirring in between until smooth. Allow it to cool slightly.
  2. Whip the Cream: In a separate bowl, whip the heavy cream, sugar, and vanilla extract until soft peaks form.
  3. Combine: Gently fold the melted chocolate into the whipped cream until fully incorporated. Be careful not to deflate the mixture.
  4. Chill: Spoon the mousse into individual cups and refrigerate for at least 1 hour to set.
  5. Serve: Top with whipped cream and chocolate shavings before serving.

These chocolate mousse cups are rich, creamy, and indulgent, making them a perfect treat for chocolate lovers.
