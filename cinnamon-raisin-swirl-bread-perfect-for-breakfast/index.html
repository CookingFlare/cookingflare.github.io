Cinnamon Raisin Swirl Bread: Perfect for Breakfast


Are you looking to elevate your breakfast game with a delightful homemade treat? Look no further than this irresistible Cinnamon Raisin Swirl Bread recipe. This soft, fragrant bread with a sweet cinnamon swirl and juicy raisins is the perfect way to start your day on a cozy and comforting note. Whether enjoyed toasted with a spread of butter or used to make scrumptious French toast, this bread is sure to become a breakfast favorite in your home.

Ingredients:

For the Bread:

  • 1 cup warm milk
  • 2 1/4 teaspoons active dry yeast
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/3 cup unsalted butter, melted
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 2 eggs
  • 4 cups all-purpose flour

For the Filling:

  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 2 tablespoons ground cinnamon
  • 1 cup raisins

Instructions:

  1. Activate the Yeast:

    • In a bowl, combine the warm milk and yeast. Let it sit for about 5 minutes until it becomes frothy.

  2. Prepare the Dough:

    • In a large mixing bowl, combine the sugar, melted butter, salt, eggs, yeast mixture, and 2 cups of flour. Mix until smooth.
    • Gradually add the remaining flour, 1/2 cup at a time, until a soft dough forms.

  3. Knead the Dough:

    • Turn the dough onto a floured surface and knead for about 6-8 minutes until it is smooth and elastic.

  4. Let it Rise:

    • Place the dough in a greased bowl, cover it with a clean towel, and let it rise in a warm place for about 1 hour or until doubled in size.

  5. Make the Filling:

    • In a small bowl, mix the sugar and cinnamon for the filling.

  6. Assemble the Bread:

    • Punch down the risen dough and roll it out into a rectangle.
    • Brush the dough with melted butter and sprinkle the cinnamon sugar mixture evenly.
    • Sprinkle the raisins over the cinnamon sugar.

  7. Roll and Shape:

    • Starting from one long end, roll the dough tightly into a log.
    • Place the rolled dough into a greased loaf pan, cover it, and let it rise for another 30-45 minutes.

  8. Bake to Perfection:

    • Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C).
    • Bake the bread for 30-35 minutes until golden brown and hollow-sounding when tapped.

  9. Enjoy the Aroma:

    • Allow the bread to cool slightly before slicing. The aroma of cinnamon and raisins will fill your kitchen, making it hard to resist a warm slice.

Serving Suggestions:

  • Serve slices of this Cinnamon Raisin Swirl Bread warm with a dollop of cream cheese or jam for a delightful breakfast.
  • Use any leftovers to make delicious French toast or bread pudding for a decadent brunch.

Conclusion:

This Cinnamon Raisin Swirl Bread is not just a breakfast staple; it’s a labor of love that fills your home with warmth and comfort. Whether you’re a seasoned baker or new to the world of bread-making, this recipe is sure to impress your taste buds and those lucky enough to share it with you. So why wait? Roll up your sleeves, preheat your oven, and get ready to savor the simple joys of homemade bread. Happy baking!
