Pineapple Fried Rice Stir Fry: A Tropical Twist on a Classic Dish


If you’re looking for a delicious and exotic dish to add to your culinary repertoire, look no further than Pineapple Fried Rice Stir Fry. This vibrant and flavorful recipe combines the sweetness of pineapple with the savory goodness of fried rice, creating a tantalizing tropical twist on a classic dish.

Ingredients

To create this mouthwatering Pineapple Fried Rice Stir Fry, you’ll need the following ingredients:

  • 3 cups cooked jasmine rice
  • 1 cup diced pineapple
  • 1 cup diced chicken or tofu for a vegetarian option
  • 1 red bell pepper, diced
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 eggs, lightly beaten
  • 1/2 cup frozen peas
  • 1/4 cup cashew nuts
  • 3 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons fish sauce
  • 1 tablespoon curry powder
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric
  • 2 green onions, chopped
  • Fresh cilantro for garnish
  • Cooking oil

Instructions

Step 1: Prepare the Ingredients

Start by cooking the jasmine rice according to the package instructions and set it aside. Then, prep all the vegetables, pineapple, and protein of your choice. Having everything ready to go will make the cooking process smooth and enjoyable.

Step 2: Sauté the Aromatics

In a large wok or skillet, heat a tablespoon of cooking oil over medium-high heat. Add the minced garlic and finely chopped onion, and sauté until fragrant. This will create a flavorful base for the stir fry.

Step 3: Add the Protein and Vegetables

Push the sautéed aromatics to the side of the wok and add the diced chicken or tofu to one side. Cook until the protein is browned and cooked through. Then, add the diced red bell pepper, frozen peas, and diced pineapple to the other side of the wok. Stir everything together and let the vegetables and protein mingle their flavors.

Step 4: Scramble the Eggs

Push the protein and vegetables to the side of the wok again, creating a space for the eggs. Pour the lightly beaten eggs into the empty space and let them cook for a minute without stirring. Then, gently scramble the eggs and mix them with the rest of the ingredients in the wok.

Step 5: Add the Rice and Seasonings

Now, add the cooked jasmine rice to the wok, breaking up any clumps as you stir it into the mixture. Drizzle the soy sauce and fish sauce over the rice, and sprinkle the curry powder and turmeric on top. Stir everything together until the rice is evenly coated with the seasonings and has a beautiful golden color.

Step 6: Finish and Garnish

Add the cashew nuts and chopped green onions to the wok, and give everything one final stir. Let the flavors meld together for a couple of minutes, then turn off the heat. Garnish the Pineapple Fried Rice Stir Fry with fresh cilantro for a pop of color and an extra burst of flavor.

Enjoy the Tropical Delight

Serve the Pineapple Fried Rice Stir Fry in bowls or on plates, and savor the delightful combination of sweet pineapple, aromatic spices, and savory goodness. This dish is perfect for a weeknight dinner with the family or a gathering with friends. It’s a versatile and satisfying meal that will transport your taste buds to a tropical paradise.

Conclusion

Pineapple Fried Rice Stir Fry is a delightful fusion of flavors that will take your taste buds on a culinary adventure. With its vibrant colors and tantalizing aromas, this dish is a feast for the senses. Whether you’re a fan of traditional fried rice or you’re looking to try something new, Pineapple Fried Rice Stir Fry is sure to become a favorite in your recipe collection. So, gather your ingredients and embark on a culinary journey to the tropics with this delectable dish.
