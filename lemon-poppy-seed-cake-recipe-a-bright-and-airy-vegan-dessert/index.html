Lemon Poppy Seed Cake Recipe: A Bright and Airy Vegan Dessert


Are you craving a delightful and refreshing dessert that will satisfy your sweet tooth without any guilt? Look no further than this Lemon Poppy Seed Cake recipe! Bursting with bright citrus flavors and a light, airy texture, this vegan dessert is sure to impress both your taste buds and your friends. Plus, it’s incredibly easy to make, requiring just a few simple ingredients. So, let’s get baking!

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 tablespoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup plant-based milk (such as almond or soy milk)
  • 1/2 cup vegetable oil
  • 1/4 cup freshly squeezed lemon juice
  • Zest of 2 lemons
  • 2 tablespoons poppy seeds

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease a 9-inch round cake pan and set it aside.

  2. In a large mixing bowl, combine the flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt. Mix well to ensure the ingredients are evenly distributed.

  3. In a separate bowl, whisk together the plant-based milk, vegetable oil, lemon juice, and lemon zest. Pour the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients and stir until just combined. Be careful not to overmix, as this can result in a dense cake.

  4. Gently fold in the poppy seeds, ensuring they’re evenly distributed throughout the batter.

  5. Pour the batter into the prepared cake pan and smooth the top with a spatula.

  6. Bake in the preheated oven for 35-40 minutes or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.

  7. Once baked, remove the cake from the oven and let it cool in the pan for 10 minutes. Then, transfer it to a wire rack to cool completely.

  8. While the cake is cooling, you can prepare a simple lemon glaze to enhance the citrus flavor. Sift 1 cup of powdered sugar into a bowl and gradually whisk in 2-3 tablespoons of freshly squeezed lemon juice until you achieve a smooth, pourable consistency. Drizzle the glaze over the cooled cake, allowing it to cascade down the sides.

  9. Once the glaze has set, slice the cake and serve. You can garnish with additional lemon zest or a sprig of fresh mint for an extra touch of elegance.

Enjoy the Zesty Delight!

This Lemon Poppy Seed Cake is the perfect dessert for any occasion, from casual gatherings to formal dinner parties. Its bright and tangy flavors are guaranteed to put a smile on your face and leave you feeling refreshed. Plus, it’s vegan-friendly, making it a fantastic option for those with dietary restrictions. So, go ahead and indulge in a slice of this airy and delicious treat. Your taste buds will thank you!

Remember, you can always experiment with variations of this recipe. For a tropical twist, try substituting the lemon juice and zest with lime or orange. You can also add a touch of vanilla extract for an extra layer of flavor. The possibilities are endless!
