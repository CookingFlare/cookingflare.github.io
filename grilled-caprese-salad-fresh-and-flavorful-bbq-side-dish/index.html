Grilled Caprese Salad: Fresh and Flavorful BBQ Side Dish


There’s something magical about the combination of ripe tomatoes, fresh mozzarella, and fragrant basil. This classic Italian trio comes together in a delightful salad known as Caprese. While the traditional Caprese salad is a refreshing dish on its own, grilling the ingredients takes it to a whole new level. The smoky charred flavor adds depth to the sweetness of the tomatoes and creaminess of the mozzarella. In this blog post, we’ll explore how to create a mouthwatering Grilled Caprese Salad that’s perfect for your next BBQ gathering.

Ingredients:

  • Ripe tomatoes
  • Fresh mozzarella cheese
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • Olive oil
  • Balsamic vinegar
  • Salt and pepper

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the Grill: Preheat your grill to medium-high heat. If you’re using a gas grill, aim for a temperature of around 375-400°F. If you’re using a charcoal grill, ensure that the coals are evenly distributed.

  2. Slice the Tomatoes and Mozzarella: Cut the tomatoes and mozzarella into ½ inch thick slices. This will allow them to hold up well on the grill without becoming too soft.

  3. Grill the Tomatoes: Lightly brush the tomato slices with olive oil and season them with a pinch of salt and pepper. Place the tomato slices directly on the grill grates and cook for about 2-3 minutes on each side, or until grill marks appear. Be gentle when flipping the tomatoes to prevent them from falling apart.

  4. Grill the Mozzarella: Brush the mozzarella slices with olive oil and place them on the grill. Grill for approximately 1-2 minutes on each side, or until they develop a slight golden crust. The goal is to soften the mozzarella slightly and infuse it with a hint of smokiness.

  5. Assemble the Salad: Once the tomatoes and mozzarella are grilled, it’s time to assemble the salad. Begin by layering the tomato slices on a serving platter, followed by the grilled mozzarella slices. Tuck fresh basil leaves between the layers for a burst of herbal aroma.

  6. Drizzle with Dressing: In a small bowl, whisk together some olive oil and balsamic vinegar. Drizzle the dressing over the assembled salad, allowing the flavors to meld together.

  7. Season and Serve: Finish the Grilled Caprese Salad with a sprinkle of salt and freshly ground black pepper. Serve it immediately to savor the warm, melty goodness of the grilled mozzarella.

Tips and Variations:

  • Add a Twist with Pesto: For a flavor boost, consider spreading a thin layer of pesto between the tomato and mozzarella slices before grilling them. The herbaceous and garlicky notes of the pesto will elevate the dish to a whole new level.

  • Explore Different Tomatoes: While traditional red tomatoes work beautifully in this recipe, don’t hesitate to experiment with heirloom varieties or even cherry tomatoes on skewers for a fun twist.

  • Opt for Fresh Herbs: If you have access to other fresh herbs such as mint or oregano, feel free to incorporate them into the salad for added complexity.

  • Serve with Crusty Bread: To make the Grilled Caprese Salad a complete meal, pair it with slices of crusty bread or a side of grilled garlic bread.

The Perfect BBQ Side Dish:

Grilled Caprese Salad is a delightful and unexpected addition to any BBQ spread. Its vibrant colors and bold flavors are sure to impress your guests, making it an ideal choice for summer gatherings. Whether you’re hosting a backyard cookout or simply craving a taste of summer, this fresh and flavorful salad is a must-try. So fire up the grill and get ready to elevate the classic Caprese to a whole new level of deliciousness!
