Slow Cooker Chicken Noodle Soup: A Winter Must-Have


Winter is here, and what better way to warm up than with a steaming bowl of Slow Cooker Chicken Noodle Soup? This classic comfort food is not only delicious but also incredibly easy to make. With just a few simple ingredients and a slow cooker, you can have a hearty and flavorful soup ready to enjoy in no time.

Ingredients

  • 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 4 carrots, peeled and sliced
  • 3 stalks of celery, sliced
  • 1 medium onion, diced
  • 4 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 8 cups of chicken broth
  • 2 cups of water
  • 2 bay leaves
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • 1 teaspoon dried rosemary
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 8 ounces of egg noodles
  • Fresh parsley, for garnish

Instructions

  1. Place the chicken breasts in the slow cooker. Add the carrots, celery, onion, and garlic.

  2. Pour in the chicken broth and water. Add the bay leaves, dried thyme, dried rosemary, salt, and pepper. Give everything a good stir to combine the flavors.

  3. Cook on low heat for 6-8 hours or on high heat for 3-4 hours, until the chicken is cooked through and tender.

  4. Once the chicken is cooked, remove it from the slow cooker and shred it using two forks. Return the shredded chicken to the slow cooker.

  5. Add the egg noodles to the slow cooker and cook on high heat for an additional 30 minutes, or until the noodles are tender.

  6. Taste the soup and adjust the seasoning if needed. Remove the bay leaves.

  7. Serve the Slow Cooker Chicken Noodle Soup hot, garnished with fresh parsley for an extra touch of flavor.

Why Slow Cooker Chicken Noodle Soup?

Slow Cooker Chicken Noodle Soup is the perfect winter must-have for several reasons. Firstly, the slow cooker does all the work for you. Simply throw in the ingredients, set it, and forget it. This makes it an ideal recipe for busy individuals or those who want to spend less time in the kitchen.

Secondly, the flavors of this soup develop beautifully as it simmers low and slow. The chicken becomes tender, and the vegetables release their natural sweetness. The aromatic herbs infuse the broth, creating a comforting and soul-warming aroma that fills your home.

Lastly, this soup is not only delicious but also incredibly nutritious. The chicken provides lean protein, while the vegetables offer an array of vitamins and minerals. The broth is soothing and hydrating, making it the perfect remedy for cold and flu season.

Tips for the Perfect Slow Cooker Chicken Noodle Soup

  • Use boneless, skinless chicken breasts for convenience, but you can also use bone-in chicken for added flavor. Just make sure to remove the bones before serving.

  • Feel free to add other vegetables to the soup, such as peas or corn, to customize it to your liking.

  • If you prefer a thicker broth, you can mix a tablespoon of cornstarch with a little water and stir it into the soup during the last 30 minutes of cooking.

  • Leftovers can be stored in the refrigerator for up to 3 days or frozen for later use. Just make sure to freeze the soup without the noodles, as they tend to become mushy when thawed.

In Conclusion

Slow Cooker Chicken Noodle Soup is the ultimate winter comfort food. It’s easy to make, packed with flavor, and guaranteed to warm you up from the inside out. So grab your slow cooker and get ready to enjoy a steaming bowl of this winter must-have. Stay cozy and satisfied all season long!
