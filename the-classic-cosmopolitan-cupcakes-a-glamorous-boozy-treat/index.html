The Classic Cosmopolitan Cupcakes: A Glamorous Boozy Treat


If you’re a fan of classic cocktails and delectable desserts, then these Cosmopolitan cupcakes are about to become your new obsession. Combining the zesty, citrusy flavors of a Cosmo cocktail with the irresistible sweetness of cupcakes, this recipe is a delightful fusion of two beloved indulgences. Whether you’re hosting a girls’ night, a bachelorette party, or simply craving a luxurious treat, these glamorous boozy cupcakes are sure to impress.

Ingredients

For the Cupcakes:

  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 1/2 tsp baking powder
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1/2 cup whole milk
  • 1/4 cup fresh lime juice
  • Zest of 1 lime
  • 2 tbsp orange liqueur
  • 2 tbsp vodka

For the Cosmopolitan Syrup:

  • 1/4 cup cranberry juice
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 2 tbsp orange liqueur
  • 2 tbsp vodka

For the Frosting:

  • 1 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 4 cups powdered sugar
  • 2 tbsp fresh lime juice
  • Zest of 1 lime
  • 2 tbsp orange liqueur
  • 2 tbsp vodka

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Cupcakes:

    • Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C) and line a cupcake tin with paper liners.
    • In a medium bowl, whisk together the flour, baking powder, and salt.
    • In a separate large bowl, cream together the butter and sugar until light and fluffy. Add the eggs, one at a time, beating well after each addition.
    • Gradually add the dry ingredients to the wet ingredients, alternating with the milk. Mix in the lime juice, lime zest, orange liqueur, and vodka until the batter is smooth and well combined.
    • Divide the batter evenly among the cupcake liners and bake for 18-20 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean. Allow the cupcakes to cool completely.

  2. Make the Cosmopolitan Syrup:

    • In a small saucepan, combine the cranberry juice and sugar over medium heat. Stir until the sugar is dissolved, then bring to a simmer for 5 minutes.
    • Remove from heat and stir in the orange liqueur and vodka. Let the syrup cool to room temperature.

  3. Prepare the Frosting:

    • In a large bowl, beat the softened butter until creamy. Gradually add the powdered sugar, mixing well after each addition.
    • Add the lime juice, lime zest, orange liqueur, and vodka, and beat until the frosting is light and fluffy.

  4. Assemble the Cupcakes:

    • Using a toothpick or skewer, poke several holes in the tops of the cooled cupcakes.
    • Brush the Cosmopolitan syrup over the cupcakes, allowing it to soak in.
    • Once the cupcakes have absorbed the syrup, pipe or spread the frosting on top, and garnish with a twist of lime zest for an extra touch of elegance.

Tips and Tricks

  • For a non-alcoholic version, simply omit the orange liqueur and vodka from both the cupcake batter and the syrup.
  • To enhance the citrus flavor, consider adding a splash of orange juice to the cupcake batter.
  • These cupcakes are best enjoyed within a day or two of baking, as the flavors are at their peak freshness.

Indulge in Elegance

The Classic Cosmopolitan Cupcakes are a celebration of sophistication and indulgence. With their vibrant citrus notes and subtle boozy kick, they embody the spirit of a timeless cocktail in a charming dessert form. Whether you’re savoring them at a chic soirée or treating yourself to a moment of luxury, these cupcakes are sure to elevate any occasion with their glamorous allure. So, gather your ingredients, embrace your inner mixologist, and let the allure of the Cosmopolitan Cupcakes whisk you away to a world of culinary refinement and decadence. Cheers to the sweet life!
