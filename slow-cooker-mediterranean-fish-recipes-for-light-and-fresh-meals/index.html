Slow Cooker Mediterranean Fish Recipes for Light and Fresh Meals


If you’re looking for a healthy and delicious way to enjoy fish, look no further than these Slow Cooker Mediterranean Fish Recipes. Packed with flavor and nutrients, these recipes are perfect for those who want to savor the taste of the Mediterranean while keeping their meals light and fresh.

Recipe 1: Slow Cooker Lemon and Herb Fish

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound white fish fillets (such as cod or tilapia)
  • 1 lemon, sliced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons fresh parsley, chopped
  • 1 tablespoon fresh dill, chopped
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Season the fish fillets with salt and pepper on both sides.
  2. Place the lemon slices on the bottom of the slow cooker.
  3. In a small bowl, mix together the minced garlic, chopped parsley, chopped dill, and olive oil.
  4. Spread the herb mixture evenly over the fish fillets.
  5. Place the fish fillets on top of the lemon slices in the slow cooker.
  6. Cover and cook on low heat for 2-3 hours or until the fish is cooked through and flakes easily with a fork.
  7. Serve the fish with the lemon slices and garnish with additional fresh herbs if desired.

Recipe 2: Slow Cooker Tomato and Olive Fish Stew

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound white fish fillets (such as halibut or snapper), cut into chunks
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can (14 ounces) diced tomatoes
  • 1/2 cup Kalamata olives, pitted and halved
  • 1/4 cup capers
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried thyme
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley, chopped for garnish

Instructions:

  1. In the slow cooker, combine the diced onion, minced garlic, diced tomatoes (with their juices), Kalamata olives, capers, dried oregano, dried thyme, salt, and pepper.
  2. Stir well to combine all the ingredients.
  3. Add the fish chunks to the slow cooker and gently mix them with the tomato and olive mixture.
  4. Cover and cook on low heat for 3-4 hours or until the fish is cooked through and the flavors have melded together.
  5. Serve the fish stew hot, garnished with fresh parsley.

Recipe 3: Slow Cooker Mediterranean Fish and Vegetable Medley

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound white fish fillets (such as haddock or sole)
  • 1 zucchini, sliced
  • 1 yellow squash, sliced
  • 1 red bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Season the fish fillets with salt, pepper, dried basil, and dried oregano on both sides.
  2. In a large bowl, toss together the sliced zucchini, sliced yellow squash, sliced red bell pepper, cherry tomatoes, minced garlic, and olive oil.
  3. Place the vegetable mixture in the bottom of the slow cooker.
  4. Lay the seasoned fish fillets on top of the vegetables.
  5. Cover and cook on low heat for 2-3 hours or until the fish is cooked through and the vegetables are tender.
  6. Serve the fish and vegetable medley hot.

These Slow Cooker Mediterranean Fish Recipes are the perfect way to enjoy a light and fresh meal with the flavors of the Mediterranean. With minimal prep and hands-off cooking, you can let your slow cooker do all the work while you savor the delicious results. Give these recipes a try and elevate your fish dishes to a whole new level of taste and convenience. Enjoy!
