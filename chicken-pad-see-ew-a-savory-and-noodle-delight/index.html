Chicken Pad See Ew: A Savory and Noodle Delight


If you’re a fan of Thai cuisine, then you’re in for a treat with this delicious recipe for Chicken Pad See Ew. Packed with savory flavors and tender noodles, this dish is sure to become a favorite in your household. Whether you’re cooking for yourself or hosting a dinner party, Chicken Pad See Ew is a versatile and satisfying option that will leave everyone craving for more.

Ingredients

  • 8 ounces wide rice noodles
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 8 ounces boneless, skinless chicken breast, thinly sliced
  • 2 large eggs, lightly beaten
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon oyster sauce
  • 1 tablespoon dark soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon fish sauce
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • 1 cup broccoli florets
  • 1 cup carrots, thinly sliced
  • 1 cup baby bok choy, halved
  • Fresh cilantro, for garnish

Instructions

  1. Begin by soaking the rice noodles in warm water for about 15 minutes, or until they become soft and pliable. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a large wok or skillet, heat the vegetable oil over medium-high heat. Add the minced garlic and cook until fragrant, about 1 minute.
  3. Add the thinly sliced chicken breast to the pan and cook until it’s no longer pink in the center. Remove the chicken from the pan and set it aside.
  4. Push the chicken to one side of the pan and add the beaten eggs to the other side. Scramble the eggs until they’re fully cooked, then mix them together with the chicken.
  5. Add the soaked rice noodles to the pan, followed by the soy sauce, oyster sauce, dark soy sauce, fish sauce, and sugar. Stir-fry everything together until the noodles are evenly coated in the sauce.
  6. Toss in the broccoli florets, sliced carrots, and halved baby bok choy. Continue to stir-fry for another 2-3 minutes, or until the vegetables are tender-crisp.
  7. Remove the pan from the heat and garnish the dish with fresh cilantro.
  8. Serve the Chicken Pad See Ew hot and enjoy!

Tips and Variations

  • For an extra kick of flavor, add a teaspoon of chili flakes or a few slices of Thai bird’s eye chili to the dish.
  • Feel free to substitute the chicken with beef, shrimp, or tofu to cater to different dietary preferences.
  • If you prefer a vegetarian version, omit the meat and add extra vegetables or tofu for protein.
  • Customize the dish by adding other vegetables such as bell peppers, snap peas, or mushrooms.
  • To make the dish spicier, you can also include a teaspoon of Sriracha or your favorite hot sauce.

Conclusion

Chicken Pad See Ew is a Thai classic that combines the rich flavors of soy sauce, oyster sauce, and fish sauce with tender noodles and succulent chicken. This savory and noodle delight provides a perfect balance of textures and tastes, making it a go-to option for any occasion. Whether you’re looking to impress guests or simply satisfy your own cravings, this recipe is a must-try. So, gather your ingredients, fire up the stove, and get ready to indulge in the deliciousness of Chicken Pad See Ew!
