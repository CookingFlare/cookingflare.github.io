Decadent Triple Chocolate Cupcakes with Ganache Filling: A Chocoholic's Dream


If you’re a self-proclaimed chocoholic, then you’ve come to the right place. These Decadent Triple Chocolate Cupcakes with Ganache Filling are a chocolate lover’s ultimate fantasy. Moist, rich, and utterly indulgent, these cupcakes are a celebration of all things chocolate. From the velvety chocolate cake to the luscious ganache filling and the creamy chocolate frosting, every bite is a symphony of chocolate flavors that will leave you craving for more.

Ingredients

For the Cupcakes:

  • 1 3/4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 3/4 cups granulated sugar
  • 3/4 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons baking soda
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup whole milk
  • 1/2 cup vegetable oil
  • 2 teaspoons pure vanilla extract
  • 1 cup boiling water

For the Ganache Filling:

  • 8 oz semi-sweet chocolate, finely chopped
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • 1 tablespoon unsalted butter, at room temperature

For the Chocolate Frosting:

  • 1 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 3 1/2 cups powdered sugar
  • 1/2 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • 1 teaspoon pure vanilla extract
  • A pinch of salt

Instructions

Cupcakes:

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C) and line a cupcake pan with paper liners.
  2. In a large bowl, whisk together the flour, sugar, cocoa powder, baking powder, baking soda, and salt.
  3. Add the eggs, milk, oil, and vanilla extract to the dry ingredients and beat on medium speed for 2 minutes.
  4. Stir in the boiling water until the batter is well combined.
  5. Fill each cupcake liner about 2/3 full with batter.
  6. Bake for 20-22 minutes or until a toothpick inserted into the center of a cupcake comes out clean.
  7. Allow the cupcakes to cool in the pan for 5 minutes before transferring them to a wire rack to cool completely.

Ganache Filling:

  1. Place the finely chopped chocolate in a heatproof bowl.
  2. In a small saucepan, heat the heavy cream until it just begins to simmer.
  3. Pour the hot cream over the chopped chocolate and let it sit for 2-3 minutes.
  4. Gently stir the mixture until the chocolate is completely melted and smooth.
  5. Add the butter and stir until it is fully incorporated.
  6. Allow the ganache to cool to room temperature.

Chocolate Frosting:

  1. In a large bowl, beat the softened butter until smooth and creamy.
  2. Gradually add the powdered sugar and cocoa powder, alternating with the heavy cream.
  3. Beat in the vanilla extract and salt until the frosting is light and fluffy.

Assembly:

  1. Using a sharp knife, core the center of each cupcake and fill it with the cooled ganache.
  2. Pipe or spread the chocolate frosting on top of each cupcake.

Tips for Success

  • Make sure to use good quality cocoa powder and chocolate for the best flavor.
  • Allow the ganache to cool sufficiently before filling the cupcakes to avoid a runny filling.
  • For a professional finish, use a piping bag to frost the cupcakes, or simply spread the frosting with a knife for a rustic look.

Indulge in Chocolate Heaven

Once you sink your teeth into these Decadent Triple Chocolate Cupcakes with Ganache Filling, you’ll experience a chocolate paradise like no other. The moist, tender cake, the luxurious ganache filling, and the creamy frosting come together to create a truly decadent treat that will satisfy even the most intense chocolate cravings. Whether you’re baking for a special occasion or simply indulging in a sweet escape, these cupcakes are sure to delight your taste buds and leave you longing for just one more bite. So, roll up your sleeves, preheat your oven, and let’s dive into the irresistible world of chocolate bliss!
