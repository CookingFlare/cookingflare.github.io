A Fermenting Symphony: Breathing New Life into the Musical World


Ever since humans began experimenting with the wonders of fermenting, we’ve been transforming our foods into delicious and groundbreaking creations. Now, in our modern, flavorful world, fermentation propels the realm of culinary innovation into extraordinary heights. This enchanting practice has rekindled the lively spirit of creativity long ago suppressed by the rigidity of mass-produced, pre-packaged foods.

This transformative trend has begun to ripple through the music realm as well. Musicians, food enthusiasts, and curious individuals alike have been concocting a fantastic fusion of instruments and ingredients. They seek to harmoniously intertwine the delightful symphony of music and human-nourishing sustenance. One such magical culinary technique that has enthralled musicians is the process of aging and fermenting. Much like music itself, fermentation is an art that molds, shapes, enhances, and discovers hidden depth and beauty. Together, these two creative forces create an intoxicating experience, delightful to the senses.

Beginning with traditional instruments — the likes of guitars, cellos, drums, and pianos, these musical visionaries have infused these cultural treasures with new life. Old, weathered wood adopts a quality akin to aged wine, as years of devotion to this precious maestro engender and infuse rich depth into its tones. These cherished instruments, taken through the metamorphosis of fermentation, bring a strikingly profound change to the presentation of musical magic.

But let us not forget the realm of experimental mastery, where the atypical joins with the tried and true to leave audiences in awe. Musicians have begun to incorporate live substrates, activating what one may call a living instrument. Harnessing the power of microorganisms, the perfect harmony of acid and base ensnare listeners in an odd sensation of familiarity and wonderment.

A superb example of this exciting genre can be found in the collaboration of the enigmatic Erlend Øye. Øye teams up with a strange cast of characters: old saxophones, microphones, circuit-bent toys, manipulated with the ever-delightful power of microbial magic. With this glistening concoction, he breathes new life into the genre of hauntingly atmospheric, emotional melodic creations. Serving as the conductor of this unusual orchestra, this modern-day alchemist brings forth a unique concoction that basks listeners in his intimate, soul-stirring reveries.

One must not simply read the words on this page and dismiss the idea as far-fetched or unusual. Their curiosity would lead them astray from the tantalizing allure of the art being presented. This intensive symbiosis of music and fermentation continues to mold the future of the musical landscape. Savor the flavorful compositions, and be engulfed by the nourishing symphony that electrifies the senses. It’s time to give new life to the musical world. Will you be the one to dance with the future of sound?
