Vegan Broccoli Cheddar Soup: Creamy Goodness Without the Cheese


There’s something deeply comforting about a warm, creamy bowl of soup, especially when it’s packed with wholesome ingredients and bursting with flavor. This vegan broccoli cheddar soup recipe is a celebration of everything that’s great about plant-based cooking – it’s hearty, satisfying, and incredibly delicious. Whether you’re a committed vegan, a curious omnivore, or someone who simply enjoys a good bowl of soup, this recipe is sure to hit the spot.

Ingredients

  • 1 head of fresh broccoli, chopped into florets
  • 1 large onion, finely chopped
  • 3 garlic cloves, minced
  • 4 cups vegetable broth
  • 1 cup raw cashews, soaked in water for at least 2 hours
  • 1 large potato, peeled and diced
  • 1 carrot, peeled and diced
  • 1/2 cup nutritional yeast
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Cashew Cream: Drain the soaked cashews and place them in a blender with 1 cup of water. Blend until smooth and creamy, then set aside.

  2. Sauté the Aromatics: In a large pot, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the chopped onion and minced garlic, and sauté until softened and fragrant.

  3. Add the Vegetables: Add the diced potato and carrot to the pot, and cook for a few minutes until they start to soften. Then, add the broccoli florets and continue to cook for another 5 minutes.

  4. Make the Soup Base: Pour in the vegetable broth and bring the mixture to a gentle boil. Reduce the heat and let the soup simmer until the vegetables are tender.

  5. Blend it Up: Using an immersion blender or a regular blender, carefully blend the soup until smooth and creamy. Return the blended soup to the pot.

  6. Add the Flavor: Stir in the cashew cream, nutritional yeast, and soy sauce. Season with salt and pepper to taste, and let the soup simmer for an additional 10 minutes to allow the flavors to meld together.

  7. Serve and Enjoy: Ladle the soup into bowls and garnish with a sprinkle of nutritional yeast or a drizzle of olive oil. Pair it with some crusty bread or a simple salad for a complete meal.

Why This Recipe Works

This vegan broccoli cheddar soup owes its rich and creamy texture to the magic of cashew cream. By blending soaked cashews with water, you create a luxurious base that mimics the richness of traditional cream without any dairy. The addition of nutritional yeast brings a cheesy, umami flavor to the soup, making it a satisfying and flavorful dish that’s perfect for any occasion.

One of the best things about this recipe is its versatility. You can customize it to suit your taste by adding extra vegetables, experimenting with different spices, or adjusting the consistency to your liking. It’s a fantastic way to sneak some extra greens into your diet and make the most of seasonal produce.

Final Thoughts

This vegan broccoli cheddar soup is a testament to the fact that plant-based cooking can be just as indulgent and satisfying as traditional recipes. It’s a celebration of fresh, vibrant flavors and the incredible potential of vegan ingredients. Whether you’re looking for a comforting weeknight dinner or a dish to impress guests at a dinner party, this soup is sure to become a firm favorite in your recipe repertoire. So, grab a spoon and savor the creamy goodness of this vegan delight!
