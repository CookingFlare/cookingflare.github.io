BBQ Pork Tacos: Sweet and Smoky Goodness


When it comes to tantalizing flavors, few dishes can compete with BBQ pork tacos. The harmony of sweet and smoky notes, combined with tender, juicy pork, makes these tacos a crowd-pleaser at any gathering. Whether you’re hosting a summer barbecue, enjoying a cozy family dinner, or simply craving something delicious, BBQ pork tacos are a perfect choice. In this blog post, we’ll explore how to create these mouthwatering tacos from scratch, the best methods for cooking the pork, and some delightful toppings to elevate your dish.

The Perfect BBQ Pork

To kick things off, let’s focus on the star of the show: the BBQ pork. The key to flavorful pork tacos lies in the marination and cooking process. Here’s a step-by-step guide to preparing the BBQ pork.

Ingredients for BBQ Pork

  • Pork Shoulder: 3-4 pounds, bone-in or boneless
  • BBQ Sauce: 1 cup, homemade or store-bought, preferably sweet and smoky
  • Dry Rub:
    • 2 tablespoons brown sugar
    • 1 tablespoon smoked paprika
    • 1 tablespoon garlic powder
    • 1 tablespoon onion powder
    • 1 tablespoon salt
    • 1 teaspoon black pepper
    • 1 teaspoon cayenne pepper (optional for heat)

Instructions

  1. Prep the Pork: Begin by trimming any excess fat from the pork shoulder. This will help prevent your tacos from becoming too greasy.

  2. Make the Dry Rub: In a bowl, combine all the dry rub ingredients. Mix well until fully incorporated.

  3. Season the Pork: Generously rub the dry mixture all over the pork shoulder, ensuring that it is evenly coated. For optimal flavor, let the pork marinate in the refrigerator for at least 2 hours, but ideally overnight.

  4. Cook the Pork: There are multiple cooking methods you can use to achieve tender, pull-apart pork. Here are three popular options:

    • Slow Cooker: Place the seasoned pork shoulder in the slow cooker and pour in half of the BBQ sauce. Cook on low for 8 hours or until the pork is tender and easily shredded with a fork. Once done, shred the meat and mix it with the remaining BBQ sauce.

    • Oven Roasting: Preheat your oven to 300°F (150°C). Place the pork in a roasting pan and cover it tightly with aluminum foil. Roast for about 4-5 hours, until the pork is tender. Shred the meat and mix with BBQ sauce before serving.

    • Grilling: For those who love that smoky flavor, grilling is a fantastic option. Preheat your grill to medium heat. Sear the pork on all sides for about 10 minutes, then move it to indirect heat. Cook for approximately 1.5 to 2 hours, basting with BBQ sauce periodically until the pork is tender. Shred and mix with more sauce before serving.

Tips for Making BBQ Pork Tacos

  • Choose the Right Cut: Pork shoulder is the best cut for this recipe due to its marbling and fat content, which ensures juicy meat.

  • Use Quality BBQ Sauce: The sauce you choose can significantly impact the flavor of your tacos. Consider making your own BBQ sauce to customize the sweetness and smokiness to your liking.

  • Rest the Meat: After cooking, let the pork rest for about 15 minutes. This allows the juices to redistribute, ensuring each bite is flavorful.

Assembling Your BBQ Pork Tacos

Now that we have our delicious BBQ pork ready, it’s time to assemble the tacos! Choosing the right tortilla and toppings will take your BBQ pork tacos to the next level.

Selecting Tortillas

  • Corn Tortillas: These are traditional and provide a slightly chewy texture that complements the BBQ pork perfectly. Heat them on a skillet for a few seconds on each side to enhance their flavor.

  • Flour Tortillas: If you prefer something softer, flour tortillas are a great option. They can hold more filling and are less prone to breaking.

Topping Options

The beauty of tacos is their versatility. Here are some topping ideas that pair wonderfully with BBQ pork:

  • Coleslaw: A crunchy, tangy coleslaw adds freshness and balances the richness of the pork. You can use a simple mix of cabbage, carrots, and a tangy vinaigrette or creamy dressing.

  • Pickled Red Onions: The acidity and crunch of pickled red onions bring a delightful contrast to the sweet and smoky pork.

  • Fresh Cilantro: Chopped cilantro adds a burst of freshness and herbal notes that brighten the dish.

  • Avocado or Guacamole: Creamy avocado slices or a dollop of guacamole provide a rich texture and flavor.

  • Sour Cream or Greek Yogurt: A drizzle of sour cream or Greek yogurt adds creaminess and balances the spices.

  • Lime Wedges: A squeeze of fresh lime juice elevates the flavors, adding brightness to each bite.

Assembling the Tacos

  1. Start with a warm tortilla as the base.
  2. Pile on the shredded BBQ pork.
  3. Add a generous spoonful of coleslaw.
  4. Top with pickled onions, cilantro, and avocado.
  5. Finish with a drizzle of sour cream and a squeeze of lime.

Serving Suggestions

BBQ pork tacos are fantastic on their own, but there are some delicious side dishes you can serve alongside them for a complete meal:

  • Mexican Street Corn (Elote): Grilled corn on the cob slathered with mayo, cheese, chili powder, and lime is a classic pairing.

  • Black Beans and Rice: A simple side of seasoned black beans and fluffy rice adds heartiness to your meal.

  • Chips and Salsa: Crunchy tortilla chips served with fresh salsa or guacamole make for a great appetizer.

Storing Leftovers

If you find yourself with leftover BBQ pork, fear not! It can be easily stored for future meals. Allow the pork to cool completely, then transfer it to an airtight container. It can be stored in the refrigerator for up to 3 days or frozen for up to 3 months. When ready to enjoy, simply reheat in the microwave or on the stovetop, adding a splash of BBQ sauce to keep it moist.

Conclusion

BBQ pork tacos are more than just a meal; they’re an experience. The combination of sweet and smoky flavors, paired with the right toppings, creates an unforgettable dish that everyone will love. Whether you’re cooking for family, friends, or just treating yourself, these tacos are sure to impress. So fire up your grill, gather your ingredients, and get ready to savor the deliciousness that is BBQ pork tacos. Happy cooking!
