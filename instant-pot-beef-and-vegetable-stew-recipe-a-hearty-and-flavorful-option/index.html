Instant Pot Beef and Vegetable Stew Recipe: A Hearty and Flavorful Option


Are you craving a warm and comforting meal that’s packed with flavor? Look no further than this Instant Pot Beef and Vegetable Stew recipe. With just a few simple ingredients and the convenience of an Instant Pot, you can have a delicious and satisfying meal on the table in no time.

Ingredients

  • 2 pounds beef stew meat, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 3 carrots, peeled and sliced
  • 3 celery stalks, sliced
  • 2 potatoes, peeled and diced
  • 1 cup frozen peas
  • 4 cups beef broth
  • 1 tablespoon tomato paste
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • 1 teaspoon dried rosemary
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Start by setting your Instant Pot to the sauté function. Add the beef stew meat and cook until browned on all sides. This will help seal in the juices and add a depth of flavor to the stew.

  2. Once the meat is browned, add the diced onion and minced garlic. Sauté for a few minutes until the onion is translucent and the garlic is fragrant.

  3. Next, add the carrots, celery, potatoes, and frozen peas to the Instant Pot. Stir everything together to combine the flavors.

  4. In a separate bowl, whisk together the beef broth, tomato paste, dried thyme, dried rosemary, salt, and pepper. Pour this mixture over the ingredients in the Instant Pot.

  5. Secure the lid on the Instant Pot and set the pressure valve to the sealing position. Cook on high pressure for 25 minutes.

  6. Once the cooking time is complete, allow the pressure to release naturally for about 10 minutes. Then, carefully quick release any remaining pressure.

  7. Give the stew a good stir and taste for seasoning. Adjust the salt and pepper if needed.

  8. Serve the Instant Pot Beef and Vegetable Stew hot, garnished with fresh parsley if desired. This hearty and flavorful stew pairs well with crusty bread or a side salad.

Why Choose an Instant Pot?

The Instant Pot has taken the cooking world by storm, and for good reason. It’s a multifunctional kitchen appliance that combines the functions of a pressure cooker, slow cooker, rice cooker, and more. This means you can prepare a wide variety of meals with ease and in a fraction of the time compared to traditional cooking methods.

With the Instant Pot, you can achieve tender and flavorful results, all while saving time and effort. The pressure cooking function allows you to cook tougher cuts of meat, like stew meat, to perfection in a short amount of time. The sealed environment also helps to lock in the flavors and nutrients of the ingredients, resulting in a more delicious and nutritious meal.

In addition to its convenience and efficiency, the Instant Pot offers a wide range of safety features, making it a reliable choice for home cooks of all skill levels. So whether you’re a busy professional, a parent on the go, or simply someone who enjoys delicious home-cooked meals, the Instant Pot is a kitchen tool that can greatly enhance your culinary experience.

Final Thoughts

This Instant Pot Beef and Vegetable Stew recipe is a go-to option when you’re craving a hearty and flavorful meal. The tender beef, combined with the assortment of vegetables and aromatic herbs, creates a delicious and satisfying stew that will warm you from the inside out.

With the help of the Instant Pot, you can enjoy the flavors of slow-cooked comfort food without spending hours in the kitchen. So why not give this recipe a try? Your taste buds will thank you, and you’ll have a hearty and flavorful option to add to your recipe repertoire. Happy cooking!
