Zesty Dairy-Free Lemon Garlic Shrimp Pasta Recipe


If you’re looking for a refreshing and zesty pasta dish that’s dairy-free and bursting with flavor, then you’re in for a treat! This Zesty Dairy-Free Lemon Garlic Shrimp Pasta is a delightful combination of succulent shrimp, tangy lemon, and aromatic garlic, all brought together with a luscious dairy-free sauce. It’s a perfect dish for a light and satisfying meal that’s sure to impress your family and friends. Let’s dive into the recipe and discover how to create this tantalizing dish in your own kitchen.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound of shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 12 ounces of your favorite pasta
  • 4 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • Zest of 2 lemons
  • Juice of 2 lemons
  • 1/2 cup of vegetable broth
  • 2 tablespoons dairy-free butter
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley for garnish

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Shrimp: Start by seasoning the shrimp with a pinch of salt and pepper. In a large skillet, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the seasoned shrimp to the skillet and cook for 2-3 minutes on each side until they turn pink and opaque. Once cooked, remove the shrimp from the skillet and set aside.

  2. Cook the Pasta: In a large pot of boiling salted water, cook the pasta according to the package instructions until al dente. Once cooked, drain the pasta and set it aside.

  3. Make the Sauce: Using the same skillet that was used to cook the shrimp, add the minced garlic and sauté for 1-2 minutes until fragrant. Be careful not to let the garlic burn. Next, add the vegetable broth, lemon zest, and lemon juice to the skillet. Allow the mixture to simmer for 5 minutes, allowing the flavors to meld together. Then, stir in the dairy-free butter until it’s fully melted and the sauce is smooth.

  4. Combine the Ingredients: Add the cooked pasta to the skillet with the lemon garlic sauce, tossing to coat the pasta evenly. Then, gently fold in the cooked shrimp, ensuring that they are well incorporated into the pasta and sauce.

  5. Serve and Garnish: Once the shrimp and pasta are well combined with the sauce, it’s time to plate up this delightful dish. Divide the pasta among serving plates, and garnish with a sprinkle of fresh parsley for a pop of color and a hint of freshness.

Tips for Success

  • To enhance the lemony flavor of the dish, you can add more lemon zest or juice according to your preference.
  • If you prefer a spicier kick, consider adding a pinch of red pepper flakes to the sauce.
  • For a gluten-free option, use your favorite gluten-free pasta in place of traditional pasta.

Enjoy the Zesty Delight!

Now that you’ve mastered this Zesty Dairy-Free Lemon Garlic Shrimp Pasta recipe, it’s time to savor the delightful flavors and share this culinary creation with your loved ones. Whether you’re hosting a dinner party or simply treating yourself to a special meal, this dish is bound to become a favorite. The combination of zesty lemon, savory garlic, and succulent shrimp will leave your taste buds dancing with delight. So, gather your ingredients, roll up your sleeves, and get ready to embark on a culinary adventure that’s sure to impress!
