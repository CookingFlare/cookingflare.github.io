One-Pot BBQ Chicken Recipes for a Taste of Summer


Summer is the perfect time to fire up the grill and enjoy delicious BBQ flavors with friends and family. If you’re looking for a hassle-free cooking experience without compromising on taste, one-pot BBQ chicken recipes are a fantastic choice. These recipes not only infuse your chicken with smoky and sweet flavors but also minimize the cleanup, allowing you to spend more time savoring the summer moments. Here are three mouthwatering one-pot BBQ chicken recipes to elevate your summer gatherings.

1. Honey BBQ Chicken Skillet

Ingredients:

  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1 cup of your favorite BBQ sauce
  • 2 tablespoons honey
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Optional: chopped green onions for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat.
  2. Season chicken breasts with salt and pepper, then place them in the skillet.
  3. Cook the chicken until browned on both sides.
  4. In a small bowl, mix the BBQ sauce and honey.
  5. Pour the sauce mixture over the chicken, ensuring it’s coated evenly.
  6. Cover the skillet and let it simmer for 15-20 minutes until the chicken is cooked through.
  7. Garnish with chopped green onions before serving.

This Honey BBQ Chicken Skillet recipe offers a perfect balance of sweetness and smokiness, making it a crowd-pleaser at any summer gathering.

2. BBQ Chicken and Potato Foil Packets

Ingredients:

  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken thighs
  • 4 cups baby potatoes, halved
  • 1 cup BBQ sauce
  • 1 red bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 red onion, sliced
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Aluminum foil

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your grill to medium heat.
  2. Season chicken thighs with salt and pepper.
  3. In a bowl, toss the halved baby potatoes with BBQ sauce.
  4. Place each chicken thigh on a piece of aluminum foil.
  5. Divide the BBQ-coated potatoes, bell pepper, and onion among the foil packets.
  6. Seal the foil packets tightly.
  7. Grill the packets for 20-25 minutes until the chicken is cooked through and the potatoes are tender.

These BBQ Chicken and Potato Foil Packets are not only delicious but also make for a visually appealing dish that’s easy to serve and enjoy outdoors.

3. One-Pot BBQ Chicken and Rice

Ingredients:

  • 4 chicken drumsticks
  • 1 cup long-grain rice
  • 1 ½ cups chicken broth
  • 1 cup BBQ sauce
  • 1 red bell pepper, diced
  • 1 yellow bell pepper, diced
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a large pot, brown the chicken drumsticks on both sides.
  2. Add the diced onions, bell peppers, and garlic, sautéing until fragrant.
  3. Stir in the rice, chicken broth, and BBQ sauce.
  4. Season with salt and pepper.
  5. Cover the pot and let it simmer for 20-25 minutes until the rice is cooked and the chicken is tender.

This One-Pot BBQ Chicken and Rice recipe is a complete meal in itself, combining protein, grains, and veggies in a flavorful dish that’s sure to be a hit at summer picnics or weeknight dinners.

These one-pot BBQ chicken recipes are designed to simplify your cooking process while delivering bold and tangy flavors that capture the essence of summer. Whether you’re hosting a backyard BBQ or looking for a quick and tasty weeknight meal, these recipes are bound to impress your guests and leave them craving more. So, fire up the grill, gather your loved ones, and savor the flavors of summer with these delicious one-pot BBQ chicken recipes!
