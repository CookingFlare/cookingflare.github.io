Spicy One Pot Cajun Pasta Recipe


If you’re a fan of bold flavors and easy cleanup, this Spicy One Pot Cajun Pasta recipe is about to become your new best friend. This dish is a harmonious blend of spicy Cajun seasoning, tender pasta, succulent shrimp, and a creamy sauce that will have your taste buds dancing with delight. The best part? It all comes together in just one pot, minimizing the mess and maximizing the flavor. Let’s dive into the details of this tantalizing recipe!

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound of shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 1 tablespoon of Cajun seasoning
  • 1 tablespoon of olive oil
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 red bell pepper, thinly sliced
  • 1 green bell pepper, thinly sliced
  • 10 oz of Andouille sausage, sliced
  • 3 cups of chicken broth
  • 1 cup of heavy cream
  • 12 oz of fettuccine pasta
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Season the Shrimp: In a bowl, toss the shrimp with Cajun seasoning until evenly coated. Set aside.

  2. Sear the Shrimp: Heat the olive oil in a large pot over medium-high heat. Add the seasoned shrimp and cook for 2-3 minutes per side until pink and slightly caramelized. Remove the shrimp from the pot and set aside.

  3. Saute the Aromatics: In the same pot, add the chopped onion and minced garlic. Saute for a few minutes until fragrant.

  4. Add the Peppers and Sausage: Stir in the sliced red and green bell peppers along with the Andouille sausage. Cook until the sausage is browned and the peppers are tender-crisp.

  5. Combine the Ingredients: Pour in the chicken broth and heavy cream, then add the fettuccine pasta. Stir well to combine all the ingredients.

  6. Simmer to Perfection: Bring the mixture to a gentle boil, then reduce the heat to a simmer. Let the pasta cook in the creamy sauce, stirring occasionally, until it’s al dente and the sauce has thickened.

  7. Add the Shrimp: Once the pasta is cooked, return the seared shrimp to the pot. Gently fold them into the pasta and sauce until heated through.

  8. Season and Serve: Season the Cajun pasta with salt and pepper to taste. Garnish with fresh parsley for a pop of color and freshness.

Tips for Success:

  • Adjust the level of spiciness by increasing or decreasing the amount of Cajun seasoning according to your preference.
  • For a seafood twist, you can swap the shrimp for crab meat or scallops.
  • To make this dish even creamier, you can sprinkle some grated Parmesan cheese on top before serving.

The Final Word

This Spicy One Pot Cajun Pasta is a feast for the senses, offering a symphony of flavors and textures in every bite. The combination of Cajun spices, succulent shrimp, and creamy sauce creates a dish that’s perfect for a cozy dinner at home or for entertaining guests. Plus, with just one pot to clean up, you can spend less time washing dishes and more time savoring the delicious results. So, roll up your sleeves, gather your ingredients, and get ready to delight your palate with this irresistible Cajun pasta creation!
