Savory Stuffed Bread Recipes for a Filling Picnic Option


As the weather warms up, there’s nothing quite like the joy of packing up a picnic basket and heading outdoors for a leisurely meal. When it comes to picnic fare, sandwiches are a classic choice, but why not take it up a notch with some delicious savory stuffed bread recipes? These delightful creations are not only easy to prepare but also incredibly satisfying. Whether you’re planning a picnic in the park or a day at the beach, these savory stuffed bread recipes are sure to be a hit with everyone.

Cheesy Spinach and Artichoke Stuffed Bread

Ingredients:

  • 1 loaf of Italian bread
  • 1 cup of chopped spinach
  • 1 cup of artichoke hearts, chopped
  • 1 cup of shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup of grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/2 cup of mayonnaise
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. Slice the Italian bread in half lengthwise and scoop out some of the bread from the inside to create a hollow space.
  3. In a mixing bowl, combine the chopped spinach, artichoke hearts, mozzarella cheese, Parmesan cheese, mayonnaise, minced garlic, salt, and pepper.
  4. Spread the mixture evenly inside the hollowed-out bread.
  5. Place the stuffed bread on a baking sheet and bake for 15-20 minutes, or until the cheese is melted and bubbly.
  6. Slice and serve warm.

Spicy Chicken and Pepper Jack Stuffed Bread

Ingredients:

  • 1 loaf of French bread
  • 2 cups of cooked and shredded chicken
  • 1 cup of diced red bell pepper
  • 1 cup of diced green bell pepper
  • 1 cup of diced onion
  • 1 cup of shredded pepper jack cheese
  • 1/2 cup of sour cream
  • 1 teaspoon of cumin
  • 1 teaspoon of chili powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Slice the French bread in half lengthwise and hollow out the center.
  3. In a bowl, mix together the shredded chicken, diced bell peppers, diced onion, pepper jack cheese, sour cream, cumin, chili powder, salt, and pepper.
  4. Stuff the mixture into the hollowed-out bread.
  5. Wrap the stuffed bread in foil and bake for 20-25 minutes, or until heated through.
  6. Unwrap the bread, slice, and serve.

Mediterranean Veggie Stuffed Pita Bread

Ingredients:

  • 4 large pita bread rounds
  • 1 cup of diced cucumber
  • 1 cup of diced tomato
  • 1/2 cup of sliced Kalamata olives
  • 1/2 cup of crumbled feta cheese
  • 1/4 cup of chopped fresh parsley
  • 1/4 cup of olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons of red wine vinegar
  • 1 teaspoon of dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a large bowl, combine the diced cucumber, diced tomato, Kalamata olives, feta cheese, chopped parsley, olive oil, red wine vinegar, dried oregano, salt, and pepper.
  2. Cut the pita bread rounds in half to create pockets.
  3. Stuff the veggie mixture into the pita pockets.
  4. Wrap the stuffed pita bread in parchment paper and refrigerate until ready to serve.

These savory stuffed bread recipes are not only perfect for picnics but also ideal for a quick and delicious meal at home. The versatility of these recipes allows for endless variations, so feel free to get creative with your favorite fillings and flavors. Whether you’re a fan of gooey melted cheese, flavorful meats, or fresh veggies, there’s a stuffed bread recipe to satisfy every craving. So, grab your picnic blanket, pack up some refreshing drinks, and indulge in these delightful and filling savory stuffed bread creations on your next outdoor adventure.
