Wholesome Nut-Free Oatmeal Breakfast Bars


Looking for a delicious and nutritious breakfast option that’s both filling and nut-free? Look no further than these Wholesome Nut-Free Oatmeal Breakfast Bars! Packed with wholesome ingredients and bursting with flavor, these bars are the perfect grab-and-go breakfast or snack option for those with nut allergies.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups old-fashioned oats
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup honey
  • 1/4 cup unsweetened applesauce
  • 1/4 cup melted coconut oil
  • 1/4 cup ground flaxseed
  • 1/4 cup chia seeds
  • 1/2 cup raisins
  • 1/2 cup dried cranberries
  • 1 teaspoon cinnamon
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C) and line a baking dish with parchment paper.
  2. In a large mixing bowl, combine the oats, flour, ground flaxseed, chia seeds, cinnamon, and salt. Mix well to combine.
  3. In a separate bowl, whisk together the honey, applesauce, and melted coconut oil until well blended.
  4. Pour the wet mixture into the dry mixture and stir until all the ingredients are well combined.
  5. Fold in the raisins and dried cranberries, ensuring they are evenly distributed throughout the batter.
  6. Transfer the batter to the prepared baking dish and press it down firmly to create an even layer.
  7. Bake in the preheated oven for 25-30 minutes, or until the edges are golden brown.
  8. Remove from the oven and let the bars cool completely in the baking dish before cutting into squares or rectangles.

Serving Suggestions

These Wholesome Nut-Free Oatmeal Breakfast Bars are incredibly versatile and can be enjoyed in various ways:

  • Enjoy them as a grab-and-go breakfast option, perfect for those busy mornings when you’re rushing out the door.
  • Pack them in your kids’ lunchboxes for a wholesome and nut-free snack.
  • Crumble them over yogurt or oatmeal for added texture and flavor.
  • Serve them alongside a cup of hot tea or coffee for a satisfying mid-morning or afternoon pick-me-up.

Nutritional Benefits

These Nut-Free Oatmeal Breakfast Bars are not only delicious but also packed with essential nutrients:

  • Oats: Rich in fiber, oats help to keep you feeling full and satisfied throughout the morning.
  • Flaxseed and Chia Seeds: These tiny seeds are packed with omega-3 fatty acids, fiber, and antioxidants.
  • Raisins and Dried Cranberries: These dried fruits add a touch of natural sweetness and are a great source of vitamins and minerals.

Make It Your Own

Feel free to customize these bars to suit your taste preferences and dietary needs. Here are a few ideas to get you started:

  • Add a handful of chopped dark chocolate or shredded coconut for an indulgent twist.
  • Swap out the raisins and dried cranberries for other dried fruits such as chopped apricots or cherries.
  • Experiment with different spices such as nutmeg or ginger to add a unique flavor profile.
  • If you prefer your bars to be sweeter, add a tablespoon or two of maple syrup or agave nectar.

Conclusion

These Wholesome Nut-Free Oatmeal Breakfast Bars are not only delicious but also a convenient and nutritious option for those with nut allergies. Packed with wholesome ingredients, these bars are sure to keep you fueled and satisfied throughout the morning. Give them a try and enjoy a tasty and nut-free breakfast on the go!

Remember to always consult with a healthcare professional if you have any specific dietary concerns or allergies. Enjoy!
