Thai Red Curry with Chicken and Vegetables: A Spicy and Wholesome Dish


If you’re a fan of bold and vibrant flavors, then Thai red curry with chicken and vegetables is a dish that should be on your must-try list. This aromatic and spicy curry is a staple in Thai cuisine, known for its rich and creamy sauce, tender chicken, and a colorful medley of vegetables. In this recipe, we’ll take you through the process of creating this delightful dish in your own kitchen, allowing you to savor the authentic taste of Thailand right at home.

Ingredients

To make this Thai red curry, you’ll need the following ingredients:

  • 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts, thinly sliced
  • 2 tablespoons of Thai red curry paste
  • 1 can (14 ounces) of coconut milk
  • 1 red bell pepper, thinly sliced
  • 1 yellow bell pepper, thinly sliced
  • 1 cup of snap peas, trimmed
  • 1 medium carrot, thinly sliced
  • 1 small onion, thinly sliced
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon of fresh ginger, grated
  • 2 tablespoons of fish sauce
  • 1 tablespoon of brown sugar
  • 1 tablespoon of vegetable oil
  • Fresh basil leaves for garnish
  • Cooked jasmine rice for serving

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Ingredients: Start by slicing the chicken breasts, bell peppers, carrot, and onion into thin, even pieces. Mince the garlic and grate the ginger, ensuring that all the ingredients are prepped and ready to go.

  2. Sear the Chicken: In a large, deep skillet, heat the vegetable oil over medium-high heat. Add the sliced chicken breasts and cook until they are lightly browned on all sides. Once cooked, transfer the chicken to a plate and set it aside.

  3. Create the Curry Base: In the same skillet, add the Thai red curry paste and sauté it for a minute to release its flavors. Pour in the coconut milk, stirring to combine with the curry paste. Allow the mixture to simmer gently, letting the flavors meld together.

  4. Add the Aromatics and Vegetables: Once the curry base has thickened slightly, add the minced garlic, grated ginger, and sliced onion. Stir well to incorporate the aromatics into the sauce. Then, add the sliced bell peppers, snap peas, and carrot, allowing them to cook until they are tender-crisp.

  5. Season and Finish: Stir in the fish sauce and brown sugar, adjusting the seasoning to taste. Return the seared chicken to the skillet, allowing it to simmer in the curry sauce until fully cooked and infused with the flavors. Once everything is cooked through, remove the skillet from the heat.

  6. Serve and Enjoy: Ladle the fragrant Thai red curry over bowls of steamed jasmine rice. Garnish with fresh basil leaves for a burst of herbal aroma and a touch of brightness. Serve the curry hot, allowing the layers of flavors to captivate your taste buds with each spoonful.

Final Thoughts

Thai red curry with chicken and vegetables is a dish that perfectly balances spice, creaminess, and the freshness of vibrant vegetables. The interplay of the aromatic red curry paste, the richness of coconut milk, and the umami notes of fish sauce creates a symphony of flavors that is both comforting and invigorating. Whether you’re a fan of Thai cuisine or simply enjoy exploring new flavors, this dish is sure to become a favorite in your culinary repertoire. So, roll up your sleeves, gather your ingredients, and embark on a flavorful journey to the heart of Thailand with this exquisite red curry recipe.
