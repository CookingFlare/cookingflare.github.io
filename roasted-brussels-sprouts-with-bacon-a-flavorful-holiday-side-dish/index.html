Roasted Brussels Sprouts with Bacon: A Flavorful Holiday Side Dish


The holiday season is a time for gathering with loved ones and savoring delicious meals together. When it comes to holiday feasting, having an array of flavorful side dishes is essential. One dish that never fails to impress is the hearty and delectable Roasted Brussels Sprouts with Bacon. This dish combines the earthy goodness of Brussels sprouts with the savory richness of bacon, resulting in a mouthwatering side that will have everyone reaching for seconds.

Ingredients

For the Roasted Brussels Sprouts:

  • 2 pounds fresh Brussels sprouts, trimmed and halved
  • 3 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder

For the Bacon:

  • 6 slices of bacon, chopped into small pieces

Instructions

  1. Preheat the Oven: Begin by preheating your oven to 400°F (200°C). This will ensure that the Brussels sprouts roast evenly and develop a beautiful caramelization.

  2. Prepare the Brussels Sprouts: In a large mixing bowl, toss the trimmed and halved Brussels sprouts with olive oil, salt, black pepper, and garlic powder. Ensure that the Brussels sprouts are evenly coated with the seasoning.

  3. Roast the Brussels Sprouts: Spread the seasoned Brussels sprouts in a single layer on a baking sheet. Place the baking sheet in the preheated oven and roast the Brussels sprouts for 25-30 minutes, or until they are tender and golden brown, stirring once halfway through the cooking time.

  4. Cook the Bacon: While the Brussels sprouts are roasting, cook the chopped bacon in a skillet over medium heat until it is crispy and evenly browned. Once the bacon is cooked, remove it from the skillet and place it on a plate lined with paper towels to drain any excess grease.

  5. Combine the Roasted Brussels Sprouts with Bacon: Once the Brussels sprouts are beautifully roasted and the bacon is crispy, combine the two in a large serving bowl. Toss gently to evenly distribute the bacon throughout the Brussels sprouts.

  6. Serve and Enjoy: Transfer the Roasted Brussels Sprouts with Bacon to a serving dish and garnish with a sprinkle of freshly cracked black pepper. This flavorful holiday side dish is ready to be enjoyed alongside your festive spread.

Tips for Perfect Roasted Brussels Sprouts with Bacon

  • Trimming the Brussels Sprouts: When preparing the Brussels sprouts, be sure to trim the stems and remove any discolored outer leaves. This will ensure that the Brussels sprouts roast evenly and develop a pleasing texture.

  • Even Bacon Distribution: To ensure that every bite is bursting with savory goodness, evenly distribute the crispy bacon throughout the roasted Brussels sprouts.

  • Customize the Seasoning: Feel free to customize the seasoning to suit your taste preferences. You can add a sprinkle of red pepper flakes for a hint of heat or a drizzle of balsamic glaze for a touch of sweetness.

  • Make-Ahead Option: You can partially prepare this dish ahead of time by roasting the Brussels sprouts and cooking the bacon separately. When ready to serve, simply reheat the Brussels sprouts in the oven and toss them with the crispy bacon.

Roasted Brussels Sprouts with Bacon is a delightful holiday side dish that brings together the robust flavors of winter produce and the indulgent appeal of bacon. Whether it’s served alongside a traditional holiday feast or as part of a cozy winter dinner, this dish is sure to be a crowd-pleaser. The next time you’re planning your holiday menu, consider adding this flavorful and comforting side to the lineup. Happy cooking and happy holidays!
