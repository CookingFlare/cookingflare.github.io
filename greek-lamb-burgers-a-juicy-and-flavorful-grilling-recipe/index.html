Greek Lamb Burgers: A Juicy and Flavorful Grilling Recipe


There’s nothing quite like sinking your teeth into a juicy, flavorful burger fresh off the grill. And when it comes to burgers, Greek Lamb Burgers are a game-changer. These succulent patties are bursting with Mediterranean flavors and are guaranteed to elevate your grilling game. So, fire up the grill and get ready to savor the taste of Greece with this mouthwatering recipe.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground lamb
  • 1/2 cup crumbled feta cheese
  • 1/4 cup chopped kalamata olives
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley
  • 1/4 cup chopped red onion
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cumin
  • Salt and pepper, to taste

Instructions

  1. In a large bowl, combine the ground lamb, crumbled feta cheese, chopped kalamata olives, chopped fresh parsley, chopped red onion, minced garlic, lemon juice, dried oregano, ground cumin, salt, and pepper. Mix well, ensuring that all the ingredients are evenly distributed throughout the meat.

  2. Once the mixture is well combined, divide it into four equal portions. Shape each portion into a patty, ensuring that the patties are evenly sized and about 1/2 inch thick. Pro tip: Wetting your hands with cold water will prevent the mixture from sticking to your hands while shaping the patties.

  3. Preheat your grill to medium-high heat. Brush the grill grates with oil to prevent the burgers from sticking.

  4. Place the lamb patties on the preheated grill and cook for about 4-5 minutes per side, or until they reach your desired level of doneness. Use a meat thermometer to ensure they reach an internal temperature of 160°F (71°C) for medium.

  5. While the burgers are grilling, you can prepare your preferred burger toppings. Consider using fresh lettuce, sliced tomatoes, thinly sliced red onions, and a creamy tzatziki sauce for an authentic Greek experience.

  6. Once the lamb burgers are cooked to perfection, remove them from the grill and let them rest for a couple of minutes. This will allow the juices to redistribute, ensuring a moist and flavorful burger.

  7. Now it’s time to assemble your Greek Lamb Burgers. Place each patty on a toasted bun and top it with your favorite toppings. Don’t forget to slather the bun with some tzatziki sauce for an extra burst of flavor.

  8. Serve the burgers immediately and enjoy the taste of Greece in every bite. These Greek Lamb Burgers are perfect for a summer barbecue, a casual dinner party, or even a weeknight family meal.

Conclusion

Greek Lamb Burgers are a fantastic alternative to traditional beef burgers. The combination of ground lamb, crumbled feta cheese, kalamata olives, and aromatic herbs and spices creates a flavor profile that is truly irresistible. Whether you’re a fan of Greek cuisine or simply looking to try something new, these juicy and flavorful burgers are sure to impress. So, gather your friends and family, fire up the grill, and indulge in the deliciousness of Greek Lamb Burgers. Opa!
