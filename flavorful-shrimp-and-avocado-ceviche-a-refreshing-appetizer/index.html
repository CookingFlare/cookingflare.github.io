Flavorful Shrimp and Avocado Ceviche: A Refreshing Appetizer


Looking for a light and refreshing appetizer that will impress your guests? Look no further than this Flavorful Shrimp and Avocado Ceviche recipe! Bursting with zesty flavors and vibrant colors, this dish is perfect for any occasion. Whether you’re hosting a summer barbecue or simply craving a healthy snack, this ceviche will surely satisfy your taste buds.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound of fresh shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 2 ripe avocados, diced
  • 1 red bell pepper, finely chopped
  • 1 small red onion, finely chopped
  • 1 jalapeno pepper, seeds removed and finely chopped
  • 1 cup of cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1/4 cup of fresh cilantro, chopped
  • Juice of 3 limes
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Start by cooking the shrimp. Bring a pot of water to a boil and add the shrimp. Cook for about 2-3 minutes, or until the shrimp turn pink and opaque. Drain and set aside to cool.

  2. Once the shrimp have cooled, chop them into bite-sized pieces and transfer them to a large mixing bowl.

  3. Add the diced avocado, red bell pepper, red onion, jalapeno pepper, cherry tomatoes, and fresh cilantro to the bowl with the shrimp.

  4. In a separate bowl, whisk together the lime juice, salt, and pepper. Pour the dressing over the shrimp and avocado mixture, gently tossing to combine.

  5. Allow the ceviche to marinate in the refrigerator for at least 30 minutes to allow the flavors to meld together.

  6. Serve the ceviche chilled in individual serving bowls or as a dip with tortilla chips.

Tips and Variations

  • For an extra kick of heat, leave the seeds in the jalapeno pepper or add a dash of hot sauce to the dressing.
  • If you prefer a milder flavor, you can omit the jalapeno pepper altogether.
  • Feel free to customize the recipe by adding other ingredients such as diced mango, cucumber, or even pineapple for a tropical twist.
  • To make this dish even more refreshing, serve it with a side of crisp lettuce leaves or on top of a bed of mixed greens.

Health Benefits

Not only is this shrimp and avocado ceviche bursting with flavor, but it’s also packed with nutritional benefits. Shrimp is a great source of lean protein and is low in calories, making it an excellent choice for those watching their waistline. Avocado is rich in heart-healthy monounsaturated fats and provides essential vitamins and minerals. Combined with the fresh vegetables and herbs, this ceviche is a nutritious and delicious option for a light and satisfying appetizer.

Conclusion

Flavorful Shrimp and Avocado Ceviche is a refreshing and vibrant appetizer that will tantalize your taste buds. With its zesty flavors and colorful ingredients, this dish is sure to impress your guests and leave them craving for more. So, why not give this recipe a try and bring a taste of the tropics to your next gathering? Enjoy the combination of succulent shrimp, creamy avocado, and tangy lime juice in every bite. It’s time to elevate your appetizer game with this delicious ceviche!
