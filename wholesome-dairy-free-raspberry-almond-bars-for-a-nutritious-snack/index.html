Wholesome Dairy-Free Raspberry Almond Bars for a Nutritious Snack


Dairy-free, gluten-free, and packed with the goodness of raspberries and almonds, these Wholesome Raspberry Almond Bars are the perfect nutritious snack to satisfy your cravings. With a burst of fruity flavor and a delightful crunch, these bars are not only delicious but also easy to make. Whether you’re looking for a quick breakfast on-the-go or a healthy afternoon pick-me-up, these bars have got you covered.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups gluten-free oats
  • 1 cup almonds, chopped
  • 1 cup dried raspberries
  • 1/2 cup almond butter
  • 1/4 cup maple syrup
  • 1/4 cup coconut oil, melted
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C). Line a baking dish with parchment paper, leaving some extra hanging over the sides for easy removal later.

  2. In a large bowl, combine the gluten-free oats, chopped almonds, and dried raspberries. Mix well to distribute the ingredients evenly.

  3. In a separate microwave-safe bowl, add the almond butter, maple syrup, melted coconut oil, vanilla extract, and salt. Microwave for 30 seconds, or until the almond butter is slightly melted.

  4. Pour the almond butter mixture over the dry ingredients in the large bowl. Using a wooden spoon or your hands, mix everything together until well combined. The mixture should be sticky.

  5. Transfer the mixture to the prepared baking dish and press it down firmly using your hands or the back of a spoon. Make sure it is evenly spread out and compacted.

  6. Bake in the preheated oven for 20-25 minutes, or until the edges turn golden brown. The bars will still be slightly soft in the center, but they will firm up as they cool.

  7. Once baked, remove the dish from the oven and let it cool completely in the pan. This will allow the bars to set properly.

  8. Once cooled, use the overhanging parchment paper to lift the bars out of the pan. Place them on a cutting board and cut into desired-sized bars or squares.

  9. Store the Wholesome Raspberry Almond Bars in an airtight container at room temperature for up to one week. Alternatively, you can refrigerate them for a firmer texture.

Nutritional Information

  • Serving Size: 1 bar
  • Calories: 220
  • Total Fat: 12g
  • Saturated Fat: 3g
  • Cholesterol: 0mg
  • Sodium: 100mg
  • Total Carbohydrate: 24g
  • Dietary Fiber: 4g
  • Sugars: 8g
  • Protein: 6g

Conclusion

These Wholesome Dairy-Free Raspberry Almond Bars are a fantastic way to enjoy a nutritious snack without compromising on taste. Packed with fiber, healthy fats, and natural sweetness from raspberries, they provide a boost of energy and leave you feeling satisfied. Plus, they are dairy-free and gluten-free, making them suitable for those with dietary restrictions or preferences.

Whether you’re preparing a quick breakfast or need a mid-day pick-me-up, these bars are a convenient and wholesome option. Make a batch in advance and have them on hand whenever hunger strikes. You can even customize the recipe by adding other dried fruits or nuts to suit your taste preferences.

Give these Wholesome Raspberry Almond Bars a try and experience the delightful combination of flavors and textures they offer. Your taste buds and body will thank you for this nutritious snack!
