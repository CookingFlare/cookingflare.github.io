Sous Vide Parmesan Roasted Potatoes: Golden and Cheesy


If you’re a potato lover, you’ve likely tried roasted potatoes in various forms. But have you ever considered using sous vide to achieve the perfect texture and flavor in your roasted potatoes? Enter Sous Vide Parmesan Roasted Potatoes – a delightful twist on the classic roasted potato dish that will elevate your culinary game to a whole new level.

Ingredients

  • 2 pounds of Yukon Gold potatoes, washed and cut into 1-inch cubes
  • 4 tablespoons of olive oil
  • 3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon of dried thyme
  • 1 teaspoon of dried rosemary
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 cup of grated Parmesan cheese
  • Fresh parsley for garnish

Instructions

Step 1: Preheat the Sous Vide Water Bath

Fill a large pot or container with water and set your sous vide machine to 194°F (90°C). This temperature will ensure that the potatoes cook evenly and become tender while retaining their natural moisture.

Step 2: Prepare the Potatoes

Place the cubed Yukon Gold potatoes in a large bowl. Drizzle the olive oil over the potatoes, then add the minced garlic, dried thyme, dried rosemary, salt, and pepper. Toss the potatoes until they are well coated with the seasoning mixture.

Step 3: Vacuum Seal the Potatoes

Transfer the seasoned potatoes to a vacuum seal bag, ensuring that they are in a single layer to allow for even cooking. Vacuum seal the bag to remove any excess air, which will help the potatoes cook evenly in the sous vide water bath.

Step 4: Sous Vide Cooking

Once the water bath has reached the set temperature, carefully lower the sealed bag of potatoes into the water. Ensure that the bag is fully submerged. Cook the potatoes in the water bath for 1 hour, allowing the sous vide method to infuse the flavors into the potatoes while maintaining their natural texture.

Step 5: Finishing in the Oven

After the hour of sous vide cooking is complete, preheat your oven to 425°F (220°C). Carefully remove the bag of potatoes from the water bath and transfer the contents to a baking sheet lined with parchment paper. Sprinkle the grated Parmesan cheese over the potatoes, ensuring an even coating.

Step 6: Roasting the Potatoes

Place the baking sheet in the preheated oven and roast the potatoes for 20-25 minutes, or until they develop a golden crust and the cheese has melted and turned slightly crispy. Keep an eye on them to ensure they don’t burn.

Step 7: Garnish and Serve

Once the potatoes are golden and crispy, remove them from the oven. Garnish with freshly chopped parsley for a pop of color and added freshness. Serve the Sous Vide Parmesan Roasted Potatoes hot as a delightful side dish or as a standalone snack.

Final Thoughts

The sous vide cooking method allows the potatoes to absorb the flavors of the seasonings and herbs while maintaining a perfect, tender texture. The finishing touch in the oven creates a golden, crispy exterior with a cheesy, savory flavor that will delight your taste buds.

Sous Vide Parmesan Roasted Potatoes are a versatile dish that pairs well with a variety of main courses, making them a perfect addition to your culinary repertoire. Whether you’re hosting a dinner party or simply craving a comforting side dish, these golden and cheesy potatoes are sure to impress.

So, the next time you’re in the mood for roasted potatoes with a gourmet twist, consider trying this sous vide recipe for a delightful culinary experience that’s bound to become a household favorite!
