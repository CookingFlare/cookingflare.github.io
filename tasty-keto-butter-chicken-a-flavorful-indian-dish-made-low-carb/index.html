Tasty Keto Butter Chicken: A Flavorful Indian Dish Made Low-Carb


If you’re a fan of Indian cuisine but are following a low-carb or keto lifestyle, you might think that indulging in the rich, creamy flavors of butter chicken is off the table. However, with a few simple ingredient swaps and a bit of creativity, you can enjoy a delicious and satisfying keto-friendly version of this beloved dish right in the comfort of your own kitchen.

Ingredients

For the Chicken Marinade:

  • 1.5 lbs (680g) boneless, skinless chicken thighs, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 1/2 cup (120g) plain Greek yogurt
  • 2 tablespoons lemon juice
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1 teaspoon garam masala
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric
  • 1 teaspoon ground ginger
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper

For the Butter Chicken Sauce:

  • 4 tablespoons (56g) unsalted butter
  • 1 large onion, finely chopped
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon grated fresh ginger
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1 teaspoon garam masala
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric
  • 1 teaspoon chili powder (adjust to taste)
  • 1 cup (240ml) tomato puree
  • 1 cup (240ml) heavy cream
  • Salt, to taste
  • Fresh cilantro, for garnish

Instructions

  1. In a bowl, combine the Greek yogurt, lemon juice, ground cumin, paprika, garam masala, turmeric, ground ginger, minced garlic, salt, and black pepper. Add the chicken pieces to the marinade, ensuring they are well coated. Cover and refrigerate for at least 1 hour, or ideally overnight.

  2. Preheat the oven to 400°F (200°C). Place the marinated chicken pieces on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper. Bake for 15-20 minutes or until the chicken is fully cooked through.

  3. In a large skillet, melt the butter over medium heat. Add the chopped onion and sauté until it becomes translucent. Add the minced garlic and grated ginger, stirring for another minute.

  4. Sprinkle the ground cumin, paprika, garam masala, turmeric, and chili powder into the skillet, stirring constantly for about 30 seconds to release the flavors.

  5. Pour in the tomato puree and bring the mixture to a gentle simmer. Allow it to cook for 5-7 minutes, stirring occasionally.

  6. Lower the heat and stir in the heavy cream, allowing the sauce to simmer gently for another 10 minutes. Season with salt to taste.

  7. Add the baked chicken pieces to the sauce, stirring to coat each piece thoroughly. Simmer for an additional 10 minutes, allowing the flavors to meld together.

  8. Once the butter chicken is ready, garnish with freshly chopped cilantro before serving.

Serving Suggestions

This keto butter chicken pairs beautifully with cauliflower rice, allowing you to soak up all the luscious sauce. Alternatively, serve it alongside a crisp, refreshing garden salad for a complete and satisfying meal.

Final Thoughts

With its creamy, aromatic sauce and tender, flavorful chicken, this keto butter chicken recipe is a testament to the fact that low-carb eating doesn’t have to mean sacrificing on taste. Whether you’re following a keto lifestyle or simply looking to explore the vibrant flavors of Indian cuisine in a healthier way, this recipe is sure to become a new favorite in your culinary repertoire. So, roll up your sleeves, gather your ingredients, and embark on a culinary journey that celebrates the fusion of delicious flavors and smart, wholesome eating. Enjoy!
