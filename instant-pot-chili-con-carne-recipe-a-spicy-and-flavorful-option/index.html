Instant Pot Chili con Carne Recipe: A Spicy and Flavorful Option


Chili con carne, a classic dish that originated in the southern United States, is a staple in many households. This hearty, spicy, and flavorful stew is made with ground beef, beans, and tomatoes, and can be cooked in a variety of ways. In this recipe, we’ll show you how to make a delicious and easy-to-make Instant Pot chili con carne that’s perfect for a cozy night in.

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1 large onion, diced
  • 3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 red bell pepper, diced
  • 2 tbsp chili powder
  • 1 tsp ground cumin
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • 1/2 tsp cayenne pepper
  • 1 can (14.5 oz) diced tomatoes
  • 1 can (15 oz) red kidney beans, drained and rinsed
  • 1 can (16 oz) pinto beans, drained and rinsed
  • 1 cup beef broth
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • Optional toppings: shredded cheese, sour cream, diced onions, and crushed tortilla chips

Instructions:

  1. Press the “Saute” button on the Instant Pot and heat the pot until it reads “HOT”. Add the ground beef and cook, breaking it up with a spoon, until it’s browned, about 5-7 minutes.
  2. Add the diced onion, minced garlic, and diced red bell pepper to the pot. Cook until the vegetables are softened, about 5 minutes.
  3. Stir in the chili powder, ground cumin, dried oregano, and cayenne pepper. Cook for 1 minute, until the spices are fragrant.
  4. Add the diced tomatoes, red kidney beans, pinto beans, and beef broth to the pot. Stir to combine.
  5. Close the lid of the Instant Pot and make sure the valve is set to “SEALING”. Press the “Manual” or “Pressure Cook” button and set the cooking time to 30 minutes at high pressure.
  6. When the cooking time is up, allow the pressure to release naturally for 10 minutes, then quick-release any remaining pressure.
  7. Open the lid and stir the chili. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  8. Serve the chili hot, topped with your choice of optional toppings.

Tips and Variations:

  • To make this recipe more substantial, serve it over cooked rice, noodles, or with some crusty bread.
  • If you prefer a thicker chili, you can simmer it on the “Saute” function for a few minutes or add a little cornstarch or flour to thicken it.
  • To add some extra heat to your chili, you can add more cayenne pepper or use hot sauce to taste.
  • You can also customize this recipe by adding your favorite toppings, such as diced jalapenos, sour cream, or shredded cheese.

Why Instant Pot?

The Instant Pot is a great way to cook chili con carne because it allows for quick and easy cooking, while also tenderizing the meat and vegetables. The pressure cooker function helps to break down the connective tissues in the meat, making it tender and flavorful. Additionally, the Instant Pot’s sauté function allows for easy browning of the ground beef and cooking of the vegetables, making this recipe a one-pot wonder.

Conclusion:

This Instant Pot chili con carne recipe is a delicious and easy-to-make option for a cozy night in. With its spicy and flavorful ingredients, it’s perfect for satisfying your cravings and warming your belly. Whether you’re a chili con carne newbie or a seasoned pro, this recipe is sure to become a staple in your household. So go ahead, give it a try, and enjoy the warm and comforting goodness of this Instant Pot chili con carne!
