Cashew Chicken Stir Fry: A Nutty and Crunchy Dish for All Ages


If you’re looking for a delicious and nutritious meal that’s quick and easy to prepare, then look no further than this Cashew Chicken Stir Fry recipe. Packed with protein, healthy fats, and a medley of colorful vegetables, this dish is sure to become a family favorite. The combination of tender chicken, crunchy cashews, and vibrant veggies creates a symphony of flavors and textures that will tantalize your taste buds.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound boneless, skinless chicken breasts, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 1/2 cup low-sodium soy sauce
  • 1/4 cup rice vinegar
  • 2 tablespoons honey
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon grated fresh ginger
  • 1 tablespoon cornstarch
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1 red bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 yellow bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 cup snap peas
  • 1 cup broccoli florets
  • 1/2 cup unsalted cashews
  • Cooked white or brown rice, for serving

Instructions

  1. In a small bowl, whisk together the soy sauce, rice vinegar, honey, garlic, ginger, and cornstarch. Set aside.

  2. In a large skillet or wok, heat the vegetable oil over medium-high heat. Add the chicken pieces and cook until browned and cooked through, about 5-7 minutes.

  3. Remove the chicken from the skillet and set aside. In the same skillet, add the bell peppers, snap peas, and broccoli. Stir-fry for 3-4 minutes, or until the vegetables are crisp-tender.

  4. Return the cooked chicken to the skillet. Pour the sauce over the chicken and vegetables, and toss to coat everything evenly. Cook for an additional 2-3 minutes, or until the sauce has thickened slightly.

  5. Stir in the cashews and cook for another minute, allowing the cashews to warm through.

  6. Serve the Cashew Chicken Stir Fry over cooked rice and garnish with sliced green onions, if desired.

Nutritional Information

This Cashew Chicken Stir Fry is not only delicious but also packed with nutritional goodness. It’s a great source of protein, fiber, vitamins, and minerals. The combination of lean chicken, colorful vegetables, and heart-healthy cashews makes it a well-rounded meal that’s perfect for all ages.

  • Calories: 320 per serving
  • Protein: 25g
  • Fat: 12g
  • Carbohydrates: 25g
  • Fiber: 4g

Tips and Variations

  • For a vegetarian twist, you can substitute tofu for the chicken and use vegetable broth instead of chicken broth.
  • Feel free to customize the vegetables based on your preferences or what’s in season. You can add carrots, snow peas, or water chestnuts for extra crunch and color.
  • If you prefer a spicier kick, add a dash of red pepper flakes or a drizzle of sriracha to the sauce.

In Conclusion

Cashew Chicken Stir Fry is a versatile and satisfying dish that’s perfect for a busy weeknight dinner or a casual weekend meal. Its vibrant colors, bold flavors, and satisfying crunch make it a hit with both kids and adults. Plus, it’s a great way to sneak in some extra veggies for picky eaters. So, give this recipe a try and watch it become a regular feature on your family’s menu. Enjoy!
