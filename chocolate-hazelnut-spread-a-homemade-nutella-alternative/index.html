Chocolate Hazelnut Spread: A Homemade Nutella Alternative


If you’re a fan of chocolate spreads, you’ve probably tasted the famous Nutella. However, have you ever considered making your own chocolate hazelnut spread at home? Not only is it a fun and rewarding culinary project, but it also allows you to customize the flavors and ingredients to your liking. In this blog post, I’ll share with you a delightful recipe for homemade chocolate hazelnut spread that’s sure to rival the store-bought version.

The Magic of Chocolate and Hazelnuts

Before we dive into the recipe, let’s take a moment to appreciate the magical combination of chocolate and hazelnuts. The rich, nutty flavor of hazelnuts complements the deep, indulgent taste of chocolate, creating a decadent spread that can be enjoyed in countless ways. Whether spread on toast, drizzled over ice cream, or used as a filling for pastries, chocolate hazelnut spread is a versatile and irresistible treat.

The Recipe: Homemade Chocolate Hazelnut Spread

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups of raw hazelnuts
  • 1 cup of powdered sugar
  • 1/2 cup of unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1 teaspoon of pure vanilla extract
  • 1/2 teaspoon of salt
  • 1/4 cup of vegetable oil

Instructions:

  1. Roast the Hazelnuts: Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C). Spread the raw hazelnuts in a single layer on a baking sheet and roast them for about 12-15 minutes, or until the skins start to blister, and the nuts become fragrant.

  2. Remove the Skins: Once the hazelnuts have cooled slightly, transfer them to a clean kitchen towel and rub them together to remove the skins. It’s okay if some skin remains.

  3. Blend the Hazelnuts: Place the roasted hazelnuts in a food processor and blend them until they form a smooth, buttery paste. This process may take a few minutes, so be patient.

  4. Add the Cocoa and Sugar: Once the hazelnuts have turned into a smooth paste, add the powdered sugar, unsweetened cocoa powder, vanilla extract, and salt to the food processor. Blend the mixture until all the ingredients are well combined.

  5. Incorporate the Oil: With the food processor running, slowly drizzle in the vegetable oil. Continue blending until the chocolate hazelnut spread reaches your desired consistency – smooth and spreadable.

  6. Taste and Adjust: Give the homemade chocolate hazelnut spread a taste and adjust the sweetness or cocoa intensity to suit your preference. You can add more sugar, cocoa, or a pinch of salt to balance the flavors.

  7. Store and Enjoy: Transfer the chocolate hazelnut spread to a clean, airtight jar and store it at room temperature. It can be refrigerated, but it may become firm, so bring it to room temperature before using.

Get Creative with Homemade Chocolate Hazelnut Spread

Now that you’ve created your own batch of chocolate hazelnut spread, the possibilities are endless. Use it as a dip for fresh fruit, spread it on warm crepes, or even incorporate it into your baking for a delightful twist on classic desserts. You can also experiment with adding other flavors such as cinnamon, espresso, or a hint of sea salt to elevate the taste even further.

Conclusion

Homemade chocolate hazelnut spread is a delicious and customizable alternative to store-bought options like Nutella. By using quality ingredients and following this simple recipe, you can enjoy the satisfaction of creating your own decadent spread from scratch. Whether you make it for yourself or share it with friends and family, this homemade chocolate hazelnut spread is sure to become a beloved staple in your kitchen. So, grab your apron and get ready to indulge in the delightful world of homemade spreads!

I hope you enjoy making and savoring this delightful chocolate hazelnut spread as much as I do. Happy spreading!
