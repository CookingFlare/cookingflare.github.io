Classic Mojito Pancakes: A Refreshing and Boozy Breakfast Treat


Who says pancakes are just for breakfast? With this unique twist on a classic recipe, you can now enjoy a refreshing and boozy breakfast treat that will kick-start your day with a burst of flavor. Introducing Classic Mojito Pancakes – a delightful combination of zesty lime, refreshing mint, and a hint of rum.

Ingredients

To make these mouthwatering pancakes, you’ll need the following ingredients:

  • 1 ½ cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons granulated sugar
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • ½ teaspoon baking soda
  • ¼ teaspoon salt
  • Zest of 1 lime
  • 2 tablespoons freshly squeezed lime juice
  • 1 tablespoon rum (optional)
  • 1 cup buttermilk
  • 1 large egg
  • 2 tablespoons melted butter
  • Fresh mint leaves, chopped (for garnish)
  • Powdered sugar (for dusting)

Instructions

  1. In a large mixing bowl, whisk together the flour, sugar, baking powder, baking soda, and salt. Add the lime zest and give it a quick mix to evenly distribute the zest throughout the dry ingredients.

  2. In a separate bowl, combine the lime juice, rum (if using), buttermilk, egg, and melted butter. Whisk until well combined.

  3. Pour the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients and gently fold them together until just combined. Be careful not to overmix; a few lumps are perfectly fine.

  4. Heat a non-stick skillet or griddle over medium heat. Once hot, lightly grease the surface with butter or cooking spray.

  5. Using a ladle or measuring cup, pour approximately ¼ cup of batter onto the skillet for each pancake. Cook until bubbles form on the surface, then flip and cook for an additional 1-2 minutes, or until golden brown.

  6. Transfer the cooked pancakes to a plate and cover with a clean kitchen towel to keep them warm while you cook the remaining batter.

  7. Serve the Classic Mojito Pancakes stacked high on a plate. Garnish with fresh mint leaves and dust with powdered sugar for an extra touch of sweetness.

Tips and Variations

  • For an extra burst of lime flavor, serve the pancakes with a lime-infused syrup made by mixing lime juice and powdered sugar. Drizzle it over the pancakes just before serving.

  • If you want to make these pancakes kid-friendly, simply omit the rum. The lime and mint flavors will still shine through, creating a delightful treat for all ages.

  • Experiment with different toppings such as fresh berries, whipped cream, or a dollop of mascarpone cheese for an indulgent twist.

  • Don’t have buttermilk on hand? No problem! You can easily make your own by adding 1 tablespoon of vinegar or lemon juice to 1 cup of milk and letting it sit for 5 minutes.

Conclusion

Classic Mojito Pancakes offer a unique and delightful twist on the traditional pancake recipe. With the tangy lime, refreshing mint, and a hint of rum, these pancakes are sure to become a favorite breakfast treat. Whether you’re enjoying them on a lazy Sunday morning or serving them to impress your brunch guests, these pancakes will bring a burst of flavor to your table. So why not give them a try and take your breakfast game to the next level? Happy stacking and flipping!
