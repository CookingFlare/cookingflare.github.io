Inside-Out S'mores Cupcakes: Cocoa Ambrosia


Ah, the humble s’more! Summertime delicacy made of toasted marshmallows nestled between graham crackers and chocolate squares- the perfect bite. But why stop at just a campsite treat when you can turn that simple s’more into an indulgent cupcake that’ll make your taste buds want to dance? Let’s dive into the delicious world of Inside-Out S’mores Cupcakes and bake up a storm in cocoa ambrosia!

To start, you’ll need the following ingredients:

  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/2 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1 1/2 tsp baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, at room temperature
  • 1/2 cup whole milk
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract

For the Fluffy Marshmallow Frosting:

  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, at room temperature
  • 3 1/2 cups powdered sugar
  • 2 1/2 tsp cornstarch
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 3/4 cup aquafaba (chickpea liquid)
  • 15 large marshmallows (to toast until golden brown)

For the Crispy Graham Cracker Crumble:

  • 1 cup graham cracker crumbs
  • 1/3 cup unsalted butter, melted
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar

Now that we have our ingredients ready, let’s break down the process:

  1. Begin by preheating your oven to 350°F (175°C), and line a muffin tin with cupcake liners. We want our cupcakes to have a cozy home!

  2. In a medium bowl, combine the flour, sugar, cocoa powder, baking powder, and salt. Whisk until well incorporated – this is your dry ingredients mixture.

  3. In another bowl, cream the butter until it’s fluffy and light yellow. Add the milk, eggs, and vanilla extract, and mix until everything’s combined. Add the dry ingredients to this wet mixture in three parts, alternating with two splashes of milk, making sure you get a smooth batter at the end.

  4. Fill each cupcake liner 2/3 of the way with the batter. These beauties don’t rise much, so don’t worry about overcrowding or overfilling.

  5. Bake for 20-25 minutes or until a toothpick inserted in the center comes out clean. Make sure not to peek or play with your oven door too early, as this is the perfect breeding ground for flat, dense cupcakes!

  6. While your cupcakes are baking, let’s move on to the Crispy Graham Cracker Crumble. In a separate bowl, combine the graham cracker crumbs with the melted butter and sugar. Mix well until it forms a crumbly texture. Set this aside for later.

  7. In the same bowl that you used for your dry ingredients, whisk together the powdered sugar, cornstarch, salt, and vanilla extract. You and this bowl go way back now, don’t you?

  8. Make your Fluffy Marshmallow Frosting effort a breeze by using a hand mixer or stand mixer to whip up the unsalted butter. Gradually add in the powdered sugar mixture, followed by the aquafaba and salt. Beat until stiff peaks form.

  9. To toast your marshmallows: Hold a marshmallow with a pair of wet fingers or toothpicks over an open flame or preheated broiler until they’re golden brown. Be prepared for some ooey-gooey meltdown!

  10. Allow your cupcakes to cool completely before diving headfirst into the frosting and toasted marshmallows. Generously dollop a healthy amount of frosting onto each cupcake, creating a fluffy swirl. Sprinkle the Crispy Graham Cracker Crumble on top, and finally, arrange the toasted marshmallows.

  11. Now, it’s time to savor this delightful creation! Inside-Out S’mores Cupcakes feature all the delicious elements of a s’more- chocolate, crisp graham crackers, and sweet marshmallows- packed into every scrumptious bite.

The cocoa ambrosia has arrived! Give these Inside-Out S’mores Cupcakes a shot, and you’ll be rewarded with warm memories in the cozy embrace of a heavenly cupcake. Don’t be afraid to experiment and add your creative flair with other ingredients and flavor combinations. After all, the kitchen is the playground for those seeking a culinary adventure!
