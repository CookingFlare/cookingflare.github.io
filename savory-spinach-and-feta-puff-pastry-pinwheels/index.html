Savory Spinach and Feta Puff Pastry Pinwheels


Are you looking for a delicious appetizer or snack that’s easy to make and sure to impress? Look no further than these flavorful Savory Spinach and Feta Puff Pastry Pinwheels! With a crispy puff pastry exterior and a savory filling of spinach and feta cheese, these pinwheels are a crowd-pleaser that will have everyone coming back for more.

Ingredients

  • 1 package (17.3 ounces) puff pastry sheets, thawed
  • 8 ounces frozen spinach, thawed and drained
  • 1 cup crumbled feta cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1/4 teaspoon red pepper flakes (optional)
  • 1 egg, beaten (for egg wash)

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C) and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.

  2. In a medium-sized bowl, mix together the thawed and drained spinach, crumbled feta cheese, grated Parmesan cheese, minced garlic, salt, black pepper, and red pepper flakes (if using). Stir until well combined.

  3. On a lightly floured surface, unfold the thawed puff pastry sheets. Roll each sheet into a rectangle measuring approximately 12x10 inches.

  4. Spread the spinach and feta mixture evenly over each puff pastry sheet, leaving a small border around the edges.

  5. Starting at one of the longer edges, tightly roll up the puff pastry sheet into a log. Repeat with the second sheet.

  6. Using a sharp knife, cut each log into 1-inch thick slices. Place the pinwheels, cut-side down, onto the prepared baking sheet.

  7. Brush the tops of the pinwheels with the beaten egg wash. This will give them a beautiful golden color.

  8. Bake in the preheated oven for 18-20 minutes, or until the pinwheels are puffed up and golden brown.

  9. Remove from the oven and let the pinwheels cool slightly before serving. They can be enjoyed warm or at room temperature.

Serving Suggestions

These Savory Spinach and Feta Puff Pastry Pinwheels are perfect for any occasion. Serve them as an appetizer at your next party or get-together, or enjoy them as a tasty snack. They also make a great addition to a brunch spread or a light lunch when paired with a fresh salad.

Tips and Variations

  • If you prefer a milder flavor, you can substitute the feta cheese with mozzarella or cheddar.
  • Add a sprinkle of dried oregano or basil to the spinach and feta mixture for an extra burst of flavor.
  • For a touch of sweetness, try adding a handful of chopped sun-dried tomatoes to the filling.
  • If you’re short on time, you can use pre-made puff pastry sheets instead of thawing and rolling out the pastry yourself.

In Conclusion

These Savory Spinach and Feta Puff Pastry Pinwheels are a delightful combination of flaky pastry and savory filling. With just a few simple ingredients and minimal prep time, you can create a delicious and impressive appetizer that will have everyone asking for the recipe. So go ahead, give these pinwheels a try, and get ready to become the star of your next gathering!
