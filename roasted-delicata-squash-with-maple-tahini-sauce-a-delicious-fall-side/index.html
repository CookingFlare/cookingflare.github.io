Roasted Delicata Squash with Maple Tahini Sauce: A Delicious Fall Side


Fall is a season that brings an abundance of flavors and colors to our tables. And one vegetable that truly shines during this time is the delicata squash. With its creamy texture and subtly sweet taste, delicata squash is the perfect ingredient for a hearty and comforting fall side dish. In this recipe, we’ll take this humble squash to a whole new level by roasting it to perfection and pairing it with a delectable maple tahini sauce. The result? A dish that will not only satisfy your taste buds but also impress your guests. Let’s get started!

Ingredients

  • 2 delicata squashes, halved lengthwise and seeds removed
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1/4 cup tahini
  • 2 tablespoons maple syrup
  • 2 tablespoons lemon juice
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/4 teaspoon cayenne pepper (optional)
  • Fresh parsley, for garnish

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C).

  2. Slice the delicata squashes into 1/2-inch thick half moons. Place them on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper.

  3. Drizzle the olive oil over the squash slices and season with salt and black pepper. Toss gently to ensure each piece is coated with the oil and seasoning.

  4. Spread the squash slices in a single layer on the baking sheet. Make sure they have enough space between them to allow for even roasting.

  5. Place the baking sheet in the preheated oven and roast for about 25-30 minutes, or until the squash is tender and lightly browned around the edges. Flip the slices halfway through the cooking time to ensure even browning.

  6. While the squash is roasting, prepare the maple tahini sauce. In a small bowl, whisk together the tahini, maple syrup, lemon juice, minced garlic, and cayenne pepper (if using). Adjust the consistency by adding water, a tablespoon at a time, until the sauce reaches your desired thickness.

  7. Once the squash is done, remove it from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes.

  8. Drizzle the maple tahini sauce over the roasted delicata squash slices. Garnish with fresh parsley for an extra pop of color and flavor.

  9. Serve the roasted delicata squash with maple tahini sauce as a side dish alongside your favorite protein or as a standalone vegetarian main.

Tips and Variations

  • For an extra touch of sweetness, sprinkle some cinnamon over the squash before roasting.
  • If you don’t have delicata squash on hand, you can easily substitute it with butternut squash or acorn squash.
  • Feel free to experiment with other sauces or dressings to complement the roasted squash. Balsamic glaze, honey mustard, or even a simple squeeze of lemon juice can all work wonders.

Final Thoughts

Roasted delicata squash with maple tahini sauce is a delightful fall side dish that celebrates the flavors of the season. The sweetness of the squash pairs beautifully with the nutty and slightly tangy notes of the maple tahini sauce. Whether you’re hosting a dinner party or simply looking for a comforting side dish, this recipe will not disappoint. So, embrace the flavors of fall and give this delicious dish a try. Your taste buds will thank you!
