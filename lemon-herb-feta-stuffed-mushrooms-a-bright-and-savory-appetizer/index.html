Lemon-Herb Feta Stuffed Mushrooms: A Bright and Savory Appetizer


When it comes to appetizers, nothing beats the delightful combination of flavors and textures found in Lemon-Herb Feta Stuffed Mushrooms. This recipe is a surefire way to impress your guests with its bright and savory taste, making it an ideal choice for any gathering or dinner party. The earthy aroma of mushrooms combined with the tangy feta cheese and the zesty lemon and herb flavors creates a symphony of tastes that will leave everyone craving for more.

Ingredients

To make these delectable Lemon-Herb Feta Stuffed Mushrooms, you will need the following ingredients:

  • 24 large white mushrooms, stems removed and reserved
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/2 cup breadcrumbs
  • 1/2 cup crumbled feta cheese
  • 2 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley
  • 1 tablespoon chopped fresh thyme
  • Zest of 1 lemon
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

Step 1: Preparing the Mushrooms

Start by preheating your oven to 375°F (190°C). Then, thoroughly clean the mushrooms and remove their stems, being careful not to break the caps. Chop the mushroom stems finely and set them aside for later use.

Step 2: Filling Preparation

In a large skillet, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the chopped onion and sauté until it becomes translucent. Next, add the minced garlic and the chopped mushroom stems, and continue cooking until the mushrooms are tender and any liquid has evaporated. Remove the skillet from the heat and allow the mixture to cool slightly.

In a mixing bowl, combine the sautéed mushroom mixture with the breadcrumbs, crumbled feta cheese, chopped parsley, chopped thyme, and lemon zest. Season the mixture with salt and pepper to taste, and mix until all the ingredients are well combined.

Step 3: Stuffing the Mushrooms

Take each mushroom cap and fill it generously with the prepared filling, mounding it slightly. Place the stuffed mushrooms on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper, and bake them in the preheated oven for about 20 minutes, or until the mushrooms are tender and the filling is golden brown.

Step 4: Serving

Once the Lemon-Herb Feta Stuffed Mushrooms are cooked to perfection, remove them from the oven and allow them to cool for a few minutes. Arrange them on a serving platter and garnish with a sprinkle of fresh parsley or a drizzle of olive oil for an extra touch of flavor.

Conclusion

These Lemon-Herb Feta Stuffed Mushrooms are a true delight for the taste buds, offering a burst of flavors in every bite. Whether you’re hosting a party or simply looking for a delicious appetizer to enjoy at home, this recipe is guaranteed to be a hit. The combination of zesty lemon, savory feta, and aromatic herbs makes these stuffed mushrooms a standout dish that will leave a lasting impression on your guests. So, don’t hesitate to try this recipe and treat your loved ones to a bright and savory appetizer that is sure to become a favorite. Enjoy!

Remember to bookmark this recipe and share it with your friends and family so they can also enjoy this delightful appetizer. Thank you for reading, and happy cooking!
