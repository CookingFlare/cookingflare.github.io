Flavorful Cranberry Walnut Bread for a Festive Christmas


The holiday season is upon us, and there’s no better way to embrace the festive spirit than by filling your home with the warm, inviting aroma of freshly baked cranberry walnut bread. This delightful treat is perfect for Christmas morning breakfast, afternoon tea, or as a thoughtful homemade gift for friends and family. In this recipe, we’ll explore how to create a flavorful cranberry walnut bread that will become a staple in your holiday baking repertoire.

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 3/4 cup orange juice
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1 large egg
  • 1 tablespoon grated orange zest
  • 1 1/2 cups fresh or frozen cranberries, coarsely chopped
  • 1/2 cup chopped walnuts

Instructions:

  1. Preheat and Prepare: Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease and flour a 9x5 inch loaf pan.

  2. Mix Dry Ingredients: In a large bowl, combine the flour, sugar, baking powder, baking soda, and salt.

  3. Combine Wet Ingredients: In a separate bowl, whisk together the orange juice, vegetable oil, egg, and orange zest.

  4. Combine Mixtures: Gradually add the wet ingredients to the dry ingredients, stirring just until moistened. Do not overmix.

  5. Fold in Cranberries and Walnuts: Gently fold in the chopped cranberries and walnuts until evenly distributed throughout the batter.

  6. Bake: Pour the batter into the prepared loaf pan and smooth the top. Bake for 55-60 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.

  7. Cool and Serve: Allow the cranberry walnut bread to cool in the pan for 10 minutes before removing it to a wire rack to cool completely. Slice and serve at room temperature.

Tips for Success:

  • Use fresh cranberries for the best flavor, but frozen cranberries can be used as well.
  • Coating the cranberries with a light dusting of flour before adding them to the batter will help prevent them from sinking to the bottom of the bread.
  • For an extra festive touch, drizzle a simple orange glaze over the cooled bread before serving.

Embracing the Festive Spirit:

The combination of tart cranberries, crunchy walnuts, and zesty orange in this bread captures the essence of the holiday season. The vibrant colors and flavors make it a perfect addition to your Christmas table, whether you’re hosting a gathering or simply enjoying a cozy day at home. The aroma of this bread baking in the oven will infuse your home with warmth and cheer, creating a truly festive ambiance.

Conclusion:

This flavorful cranberry walnut bread is a wonderful way to celebrate the holiday season with a homemade treat that’s sure to delight everyone who tastes it. Whether you’re an experienced baker or just starting out, this recipe is accessible and rewarding. So, roll up your sleeves, preheat your oven, and let the magic of Christmas come alive with every slice of this delightful bread. Happy baking, and may your holiday season be filled with joy, laughter, and delicious moments shared with loved ones.
