How to Make the Perfect Chicken Parmesan: A Step-by-Step Guide


Chicken Parmesan is a classic Italian dish that never fails to satisfy. The combination of tender chicken, rich tomato sauce, and melted cheese creates a mouthwatering experience that will leave you craving more. In this step-by-step guide, we’ll walk you through the process of making the perfect Chicken Parmesan right in your own kitchen.

Ingredients

To make Chicken Parmesan, you’ll need the following ingredients:

  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 large eggs, beaten
  • 2 cups breadcrumbs
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 cups marinara sauce
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Olive oil for frying

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C) and prepare a baking sheet by lining it with parchment paper.

  2. Start by pounding the chicken breasts to an even thickness. This will ensure that they cook evenly and result in a tender and juicy dish. Place each chicken breast between two sheets of plastic wrap and gently pound them with a meat mallet or the bottom of a heavy pan until they are about 1/2 inch (1.3 cm) thick.

  3. Season the chicken breasts with salt and pepper on both sides.

  4. Set up a breading station by placing the flour, beaten eggs, and breadcrumbs in separate shallow dishes. Add the grated Parmesan cheese to the breadcrumbs and mix well.

  5. Dip each chicken breast into the flour, making sure to coat both sides. Shake off any excess flour.

  6. Next, dip the floured chicken breast into the beaten eggs, allowing any excess egg to drip off.

  7. Finally, coat the chicken breast in the breadcrumb and Parmesan mixture, pressing gently to ensure an even coating. Repeat this process for all the chicken breasts.

  8. Heat a large skillet over medium heat and add enough olive oil to coat the bottom of the pan. Once the oil is hot, carefully place the breaded chicken breasts into the skillet. Cook for about 3-4 minutes on each side, or until the chicken is golden brown and cooked through.

  9. Transfer the cooked chicken breasts to the prepared baking sheet.

  10. Spoon a generous amount of marinara sauce over each chicken breast, covering them completely.

  11. Sprinkle the shredded mozzarella cheese evenly over the sauce.

  12. Place the baking sheet in the preheated oven and bake for about 15-20 minutes, or until the cheese is melted and bubbly.

  13. Once cooked, remove the Chicken Parmesan from the oven and let it rest for a few minutes before serving.

  14. Serve the Chicken Parmesan hot, garnished with fresh basil leaves if desired. This dish pairs perfectly with a side of pasta or a fresh salad.

Conclusion

Congratulations! You have successfully made the perfect Chicken Parmesan. The crispy breaded chicken, tangy marinara sauce, and gooey melted cheese combine to create a taste sensation that will impress your family and friends. Whether you’re cooking for a weeknight dinner or a special occasion, this classic Italian dish is sure to be a hit. So put on your apron, gather your ingredients, and get ready to enjoy a homemade Chicken Parmesan that rivals any restaurant version. Bon appétit!
