Autumn Apple Recipes: 20 Delicious and Easy Options for Fresh Apples


Autumn is the perfect time to embrace the abundance of fresh apples. With their crisp texture and sweet-tart flavor, apples are incredibly versatile and can be used in a variety of dishes. From comforting desserts to savory main courses, there’s no limit to the delicious creations you can make with apples. In this article, we’ll explore 20 mouthwatering apple recipes that are both easy to prepare and bursting with autumn flavors.

1. Apple Pie Smoothie

Start your morning off right with a refreshing and nutritious apple pie smoothie. Simply blend together a ripe apple, a frozen banana, a dash of cinnamon, a spoonful of almond butter, and a cup of almond milk. This smoothie tastes like a slice of homemade apple pie, without the guilt!

2. Caramel Apple Galette

Impress your guests with this rustic yet elegant caramel apple galette. Roll out store-bought puff pastry and arrange thinly sliced apples on top. Drizzle with caramel sauce and fold the edges of the pastry over the apples. Bake until golden brown and serve warm with a scoop of vanilla ice cream.

3. Apple Cider Pork Chops

For a savory dish that combines the flavors of apple and pork, try these apple cider pork chops. Sear bone-in pork chops in a hot skillet, then deglaze with apple cider and simmer until cooked through. The result is tender, juicy pork chops with a hint of sweetness from the apple cider.

4. Apple Walnut Salad

This refreshing apple walnut salad is a perfect side dish for any autumn meal. Toss together mixed greens, thinly sliced apples, toasted walnuts, crumbled blue cheese, and a simple vinaigrette made with apple cider vinegar, Dijon mustard, and olive oil. The combination of flavors and textures is simply irresistible.

5. Apple Cinnamon Pancakes

Indulge in a cozy weekend breakfast with these fluffy apple cinnamon pancakes. Mix grated apples and ground cinnamon into your favorite pancake batter, then cook until golden brown. Serve with a drizzle of maple syrup and a sprinkle of powdered sugar for a truly delightful treat.

6. Apple Cranberry Stuffed Pork Loin

For a show-stopping centerpiece at your next dinner party, try this apple cranberry stuffed pork loin. Butterfly a pork loin and fill it with a mixture of sautéed apples, dried cranberries, breadcrumbs, and herbs. Roll it up, tie with kitchen twine, and roast until the pork is tender and the filling is golden.

7. Apple Cheddar Grilled Cheese

Take a classic grilled cheese sandwich to the next level with the addition of sliced apples and sharp cheddar cheese. Layer the ingredients between two slices of bread and grill until the cheese is melted and the bread is crispy. The combination of sweet and tangy flavors is simply divine.

8. Apple Cinnamon French Toast Casserole

This apple cinnamon French toast casserole is the ultimate make-ahead breakfast dish. Tear day-old bread into chunks and toss with a mixture of beaten eggs, milk, sugar, cinnamon, and diced apples. Let it sit overnight in the refrigerator, then bake until golden and puffy in the morning.

9. Apple Pecan Baked Brie

Elevate your cheese board with this elegant apple pecan baked brie. Place a wheel of brie on a baking sheet and top it with thinly sliced apples, chopped pecans, and a drizzle of honey. Bake until the cheese is melted and gooey, then serve with crackers or crusty bread.

10. Apple Cinnamon Oatmeal

Warm up on a chilly autumn morning with a hearty bowl of apple cinnamon oatmeal. Cook rolled oats with diced apples, a sprinkle of cinnamon, and a splash of milk until thick and creamy. Top with a dollop of Greek yogurt and a drizzle of honey for an extra touch of sweetness.

11. Apple Butter BBQ Ribs

For a finger-licking good dinner, try these apple butter BBQ ribs. Slather baby back ribs with a mixture of apple butter, BBQ sauce, brown sugar, and spices. Slow-cook in the oven or on the grill until the meat is tender and the sauce is caramelized. Serve with a side of coleslaw for a complete meal.

12. Apple Cinnamon Bread

Fill your home with the irresistible aroma of freshly baked apple cinnamon bread. Mix grated apples, cinnamon, sugar, flour, and butter to create a moist and fragrant loaf. Slice and enjoy it warm with a pat of butter for a comforting treat any time of the day.

13. Apple Cranberry Crisp

Warm apple cranberry crisp is the quintessential fall dessert. Toss together sliced apples, dried cranberries, sugar, and a touch of lemon juice. Top with a buttery oat crumble and bake until the fruit is bubbly and the topping is golden brown. Serve with a scoop of vanilla ice cream for pure bliss.

14. Apple Sage Stuffing

Enhance your Thanksgiving feast with this delectable apple sage stuffing. Sauté diced apples, onions, and celery in butter until softened. Combine with cubed bread, fresh sage, chicken broth, and seasoning. Bake until golden and fragrant, then savor the comforting flavors of this traditional side dish.

15. Apple Cider Donuts

Nothing says autumn like the aroma of freshly fried apple cider donuts. Mix apple cider, cinnamon, nutmeg, and sugar into a simple dough, then fry until golden brown. Dust with powdered sugar or cinnamon sugar for a delightful treat that pairs perfectly with a cup of hot coffee.

16. Apple Pecan Stuffed Acorn Squash

For a vegetarian main course that celebrates the flavors of fall, try this apple pecan stuffed acorn squash. Roast halved acorn squash until tender, then stuff with a mixture of sautéed apples, pecans, quinoa, and herbs. Bake until the filling is heated through and serve as a satisfying meal.

17. Apple Cinnamon Granola

Make your own wholesome apple cinnamon granola and enjoy it as a breakfast cereal or a snack. Toss together rolled oats, chopped apples, cinnamon, honey, and a touch of oil. Bake until golden and crispy, stirring occasionally. Serve with yogurt or milk for a nutritious and delicious start to your day.

18. Apple Brie Quesadillas

Elevate the humble quesadilla with the addition of sliced apples and creamy brie cheese. Layer the ingredients between two tortillas and cook until the cheese is melted and the tortillas are golden. Cut into wedges and serve as an appetizer or a light lunch.

19. Apple Cinnamon Energy Balls

Fuel your day with these apple cinnamon energy balls. Blend together dates, dried apples, oats, almond butter, cinnamon, and a touch of honey. Roll into bite-sized balls and refrigerate until firm. These little bites are perfect for a quick snack on the go.

20. Apple Gingerbread Trifle

End your autumn feast on a high note with this indulgent apple gingerbread trifle. Layer gingerbread cake, caramelized apples, whipped cream, and a drizzle of caramel sauce in a glass trifle dish. Repeat the layers and refrigerate until the flavors meld together into a heavenly dessert.

Autumn is the perfect time to explore the wonderful world of apple recipes. With these 20 delicious and easy options, you’ll never run out of ways to enjoy fresh apples. From sweet to savory, there’s something for everyone in this collection of autumn-inspired dishes. So grab a bag of apples and get cooking!
