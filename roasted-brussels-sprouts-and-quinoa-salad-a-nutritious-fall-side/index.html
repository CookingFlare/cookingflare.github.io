Roasted Brussels Sprouts and Quinoa Salad: A Nutritious Fall Side


Fall is the perfect season to indulge in comforting and nutritious dishes that warm your soul. And what better way to celebrate the flavors of autumn than with a delicious Roasted Brussels Sprouts and Quinoa Salad? Packed with wholesome ingredients and bursting with flavors, this salad is a delightful addition to any meal. So, let’s dive right into the recipe!

Ingredients

  • 1 pound Brussels sprouts, trimmed and halved
  • 1 cup quinoa, rinsed
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon smoked paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • 1/4 cup dried cranberries
  • 1/4 cup toasted pecans, roughly chopped
  • 1/4 cup crumbled feta cheese
  • 2 tablespoons maple syrup
  • 2 tablespoons apple cider vinegar

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C).

  2. In a large bowl, toss the halved Brussels sprouts with olive oil, smoked paprika, garlic powder, salt, and pepper until well coated.

  3. Spread the Brussels sprouts evenly on a baking sheet and roast for about 20-25 minutes, or until they are golden brown and crispy. Make sure to stir them halfway through cooking to ensure even browning.

  4. While the Brussels sprouts are roasting, cook the quinoa according to the package instructions. Once cooked, fluff it with a fork and set it aside to cool slightly.

  5. In a small bowl, whisk together the maple syrup and apple cider vinegar to make the dressing.

  6. In a large serving bowl, combine the roasted Brussels sprouts, cooked quinoa, dried cranberries, toasted pecans, and crumbled feta cheese.

  7. Drizzle the dressing over the salad and gently toss everything together until well combined.

  8. Taste and adjust the seasoning with salt and pepper, if needed.

  9. Let the salad sit for about 10 minutes to allow the flavors to meld together.

  10. Serve the Roasted Brussels Sprouts and Quinoa Salad warm or at room temperature.

Enjoy the Flavors of Fall

This Roasted Brussels Sprouts and Quinoa Salad is a perfect balance of textures and flavors. The tender Brussels sprouts pair beautifully with the nutty quinoa, while the dried cranberries add a touch of sweetness. The toasted pecans bring a delightful crunch, and the tangy feta cheese adds a creamy finish. The maple syrup and apple cider vinegar dressing tie all the ingredients together, enhancing the overall taste of the salad.

Not only is this salad delicious, but it’s also a nutritional powerhouse. Brussels sprouts are rich in vitamins A, C, and K, as well as dietary fiber and antioxidants. Quinoa is a complete protein, packed with essential amino acids, and it’s also gluten-free. The dried cranberries provide a dose of antioxidants, while the toasted pecans offer heart-healthy fats. This salad is a wholesome addition to your fall menu, providing you with both taste and nourishment.

So, the next time you’re looking for a side dish that celebrates the flavors of fall, give this Roasted Brussels Sprouts and Quinoa Salad a try. It’s a nutritious and satisfying option that will leave you craving for more. Enjoy the harvest season with this delightful salad!

Note: Remember to adjust the quantities of the ingredients based on your serving size. Feel free to customize the salad by adding other ingredients like roasted butternut squash or dried cherries. Get creative and make it your own!
