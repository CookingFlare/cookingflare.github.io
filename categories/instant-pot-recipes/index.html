Instant Pot Chicken Fricassee Recipe: A French-Inspired Dish

French cuisine is renowned for its rich flavors, intricate preparations, and exquisite presentation. Today, we’re going to bring a taste of France to your table with a classic dish that’s both elegant and effortless: Instant Pot Chicken Fricassee. This heavenly recipe is a perfect blend of tender chicken, creamy sauce, and aromatic spices, all cooked to perfection in the Instant Pot. Ingredients 1 1/2 pounds boneless, skinless chicken thighs, cut into 1-inch pieces 2 tablespoons butter 1 medium onion, diced 2 cloves garlic, minced 1 cup all-purpose flour 1 cup chicken broth 1 cup heavy cream 1 teaspoon dried thyme 1/2 teaspoon paprika Salt and pepper, to taste Fresh parsley, chopped (optional) Instructions Press the “Saute” button on the Instant Pot and melt the butter. Add the diced onion and cook until it’s translucent, about 5 minutes. Add the minced garlic and cook for an additional minute, stirring constantly to prevent burning. Add the chicken pieces and cook until they’re browned on all sides, about 5-7 minutes. Remove the chicken from the pot and set it aside. Add the flour to the pot and cook for 1 minute, stirring constantly. The mixture should form a smooth paste. Gradually add the chicken broth and heavy cream, whisking continuously to prevent lumps. Bring the mixture to a simmer and cook for 2-3 minutes, or until it thickens slightly. Add the thyme, paprika, salt, and pepper to the pot. Stir to combine. Return the chicken to the pot and stir to coat with the sauce. Close the lid of the Instant Pot and set the valve to “Sealing”. Press the “Manual” or “Pressure Cook” button and set the cooking time to 10 minutes at high pressure. When the cooking time is up, allow the pressure to release naturally for 10 minutes, then quick-release any remaining pressure. Open the lid and check the chicken for tenderness. If it’s not cooked to your liking, close the lid and cook for an additional 5 minutes. Taste and adjust the seasoning as needed. Serve the Chicken Fricassee hot, garnished with chopped parsley if desired. Tips and Variations For a richer sauce, use 1 1/2 cups of heavy cream or add 1/2 cup of grated Parmesan cheese. To add some color and texture, stir in 1 cup of frozen peas during the last 2 minutes of cooking. For a spicy kick, add 1/4 teaspoon of cayenne pepper or red pepper flakes to the sauce. This recipe freezes well, so feel free to make a batch and store it in the freezer for up to 3 months. Simply thaw and reheat when needed. Conclusion Instant Pot Chicken Fricassee is a show-stopping dish that’s sure to impress your family and friends. With its tender chicken, creamy sauce, and aromatic spices, it’s a perfect representation of French cuisine. The best part? It’s incredibly easy to make and requires minimal effort, thanks to the Instant Pot. So go ahead, give this recipe a try, and experience the magic of French cooking in the comfort of your own home. Spicy Instant Pot Thai Basil Chicken Recipes

If you’re a fan of spicy and flavorful dishes, then you’re in for a treat with these Spicy Instant Pot Thai Basil Chicken recipes. Bursting with the bold flavors of Thai cuisine, these recipes are quick, easy, and perfect for busy weeknight dinners or weekend gatherings. Get ready to tantalize your taste buds with these mouthwatering creations! Recipe 1: Spicy Instant Pot Thai Basil Chicken Stir-Fry Ingredients: 1 pound boneless, skinless chicken breasts, cut into bite-sized pieces 2 tablespoons vegetable oil 3 cloves garlic, minced 1 small onion, thinly sliced 1 bell pepper, thinly sliced 1 cup fresh basil leaves 2 tablespoons soy sauce 1 tablespoon fish sauce 1 tablespoon oyster sauce 1 teaspoon sugar 1 teaspoon red pepper flakes (adjust to taste) Salt and pepper, to taste Instructions: Set your Instant Pot to sauté mode and heat the vegetable oil. Add the minced garlic and sauté for about 1 minute until fragrant. Add the chicken pieces and cook until they are browned on all sides. Stir in the onion, bell pepper, soy sauce, fish sauce, oyster sauce, sugar, and red pepper flakes. Close the lid and set the Instant Pot to manual high pressure for 5 minutes. Once the cooking time is complete, carefully release the pressure using the quick release method. Open the lid and stir in the fresh basil leaves. Let it sit for a few minutes to allow the flavors to meld together. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Serve the spicy Thai basil chicken stir-fry over steamed rice or noodles. Enjoy! Recipe 2: Spicy Instant Pot Thai Basil Chicken Curry Ingredients: 1 pound boneless, skinless chicken thighs, cut into bite-sized pieces 2 tablespoons red curry paste 1 can (14 ounces) coconut milk 1 cup chicken broth 1 bell pepper, thinly sliced 1 zucchini, sliced 1 cup bamboo shoots 1 cup fresh basil leaves 1 tablespoon fish sauce 1 tablespoon brown sugar Juice of 1 lime Salt and pepper, to taste Instructions: Set your Instant Pot to sauté mode and add the red curry paste. Cook for a minute, stirring constantly. Add the chicken pieces and cook until they are browned on all sides. Pour in the coconut milk and chicken broth. Stir well to combine. Add the bell pepper, zucchini, bamboo shoots, fish sauce, and brown sugar. Stir again. Close the lid and set the Instant Pot to manual high pressure for 8 minutes. Once the cooking time is complete, allow the pressure to release naturally for 5 minutes, then use the quick release method. Open the lid and stir in the fresh basil leaves and lime juice. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Serve the spicy Thai basil chicken curry over steamed jasmine rice. Enjoy! Recipe 3: Spicy Instant Pot Thai Basil Chicken Soup Ingredients: 1 pound boneless, skinless chicken breasts, cut into bite-sized pieces 4 cups chicken broth 1 can (14 ounces) coconut milk 1 bell pepper, thinly sliced 1 carrot, sliced 1 cup sliced mushrooms 1 tablespoon red curry paste 2 tablespoons fish sauce 1 tablespoon brown sugar Juice of 1 lime 1 cup fresh basil leaves Salt and pepper, to taste Instructions: Set your Instant Pot to sauté mode and heat some oil. Add the chicken pieces and cook until they are browned on all sides. Stir in the red curry paste and cook for another minute. Pour in the chicken broth and coconut milk. Stir well to combine. Add the bell pepper, carrot, mushrooms, fish sauce, and brown sugar. Stir again. Close the lid and set the Instant Pot to manual high pressure for 6 minutes. Once the cooking time is complete, carefully release the pressure using the quick release method. Open the lid and stir in the lime juice and fresh basil leaves. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Ladle the spicy Thai basil chicken soup into bowls and serve hot. Enjoy! These Spicy Instant Pot Thai Basil Chicken recipes are sure to become your new favorites in no time. With their bold flavors and the convenience of using an Instant Pot, they are perfect for satisfying your cravings for Thai cuisine.
