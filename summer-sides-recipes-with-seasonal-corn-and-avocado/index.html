Summer Sides: Recipes with Seasonal Corn and Avocado


Summer is the perfect time to enjoy fresh and vibrant ingredients, and two of the stars of the season are corn and avocado. These versatile ingredients can be used to create delicious and refreshing side dishes that are perfect for any summer gathering. In this blog post, we will explore three mouthwatering recipes that showcase the flavors of corn and avocado. So, let’s get started!

1. Grilled Corn and Avocado Salad

Ingredients:

  • 4 ears of corn, husked
  • 2 ripe avocados, diced
  • 1 red bell pepper, diced
  • 1/2 red onion, finely chopped
  • 1/4 cup fresh cilantro, chopped
  • Juice of 2 limes
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your grill to medium-high heat.
  2. Place the corn directly on the grill grates and cook for about 10 minutes, turning occasionally, until the corn is charred and tender.
  3. Remove the corn from the grill and let it cool slightly. Once cooled, cut the kernels off the cob and transfer them to a large bowl.
  4. Add the diced avocados, red bell pepper, red onion, and cilantro to the bowl with the corn.
  5. Squeeze the lime juice over the salad and season with salt and pepper. Toss gently to combine.
  6. Serve immediately or refrigerate until ready to serve. This salad pairs well with grilled meats or as a standalone side dish.

2. Creamy Corn and Avocado Dip

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups fresh corn kernels
  • 2 ripe avocados
  • 1/4 cup Greek yogurt
  • 1/4 cup mayonnaise
  • 1 jalapeño, seeded and minced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • Juice of 1 lime
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Tortilla chips, for serving

Instructions:

  1. In a blender or food processor, combine the corn kernels, avocados, Greek yogurt, mayonnaise, jalapeño, garlic, and lime juice. Blend until smooth and creamy.
  2. Season the dip with salt and pepper to taste. Adjust the seasonings according to your preference.
  3. Transfer the dip to a serving bowl and refrigerate for at least 30 minutes to allow the flavors to meld together.
  4. Serve the creamy corn and avocado dip chilled with tortilla chips. It’s a crowd-pleasing appetizer that will disappear in no time!

3. Grilled Avocado and Corn Salsa

Ingredients:

  • 2 ripe avocados, halved and pitted
  • 2 ears of corn, husked
  • 1/2 red onion, finely chopped
  • 1/4 cup fresh cilantro, chopped
  • Juice of 1 lime
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Tortilla chips, for serving

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your grill to medium-high heat.
  2. Place the avocado halves, cut side down, and the corn directly on the grill grates. Cook for about 5 minutes, turning the corn occasionally, until the avocados are lightly charred and the corn is tender and slightly charred.
  3. Remove the avocados and corn from the grill and let them cool slightly. Once cooled, cut the avocados into small dice and cut the kernels off the cob.
  4. In a bowl, combine the diced avocado, corn kernels, red onion, cilantro, lime juice, and olive oil. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  5. Toss gently to combine all the ingredients.
  6. Serve the grilled avocado and corn salsa with tortilla chips. It’s a refreshing and satisfying dip that will be a hit at any summer party.

These three recipes are just a starting point for incorporating seasonal corn and avocado into your summer sides repertoire. Get creative and experiment with different flavors and ingredients to make these dishes your own. Whether you’re hosting a backyard barbecue or attending a potluck, these corn and avocado recipes are sure to impress. Enjoy the flavors of summer with these delicious and easy-to-make side dishes!
