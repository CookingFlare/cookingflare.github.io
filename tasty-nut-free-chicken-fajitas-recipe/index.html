Tasty Nut-Free Chicken Fajitas Recipe


Are you looking for a delicious and nutritious meal that is nut-free? Look no further! Our Tasty Nut-Free Chicken Fajitas Recipe is sure to satisfy your cravings and impress your family and friends. Packed with flavor and easy to make, this recipe is a winner for any occasion. So, let’s dive right into it!

Ingredients

  • 1 pound boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 2 bell peppers (any color), thinly sliced
  • 1 large onion, thinly sliced
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 teaspoons chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • Juice of 1 lime
  • 8 small flour tortillas

Instructions

  1. Start by preparing your chicken. Slice the chicken breasts into thin strips, about 1/2 inch wide. This will ensure quick and even cooking.

  2. In a large skillet, heat the olive oil over medium-high heat. Add the chicken strips and cook until they are no longer pink in the center, about 5-6 minutes. Remove the chicken from the skillet and set it aside.

  3. In the same skillet, add the sliced bell peppers, onion, and minced garlic. Cook until the vegetables are tender-crisp, about 4-5 minutes. The vibrant colors and aroma will make your kitchen smell amazing!

  4. While the vegetables are cooking, prepare the seasoning for your fajitas. In a small bowl, combine the chili powder, cumin, paprika, salt, and black pepper. Mix well to ensure the spices are evenly distributed.

  5. Once the vegetables are cooked, return the chicken to the skillet. Sprinkle the seasoning mixture over the chicken and vegetables, and give everything a good stir to coat evenly. Cook for an additional 2-3 minutes to allow the flavors to meld together.

  6. Squeeze the juice of one lime over the chicken fajitas. The tangy citrus will add a burst of freshness to the dish.

  7. Warm the flour tortillas in a dry skillet or microwave according to the package instructions. This step is essential to make the tortillas soft and pliable.

  8. Serve the chicken fajitas on warm tortillas and garnish with your favorite toppings, such as salsa, sour cream, guacamole, or shredded cheese. The possibilities are endless!

  9. Enjoy your Tasty Nut-Free Chicken Fajitas! The combination of tender chicken, flavorful vegetables, and zesty lime will leave you craving for more.

Tips and Variations

  • If you prefer spicier fajitas, feel free to add some crushed red pepper flakes or a dash of hot sauce to the seasoning mixture.

  • For a gluten-free option, use corn tortillas instead of flour tortillas.

  • Customize your fajitas by adding other toppings like chopped tomatoes, cilantro, or sliced avocado.

  • If you’re short on time, you can marinate the chicken in the seasoning mixture for 30 minutes to overnight before cooking. This will enhance the flavors even more.

  • Leftover chicken fajitas make a great filling for quesadillas or a topping for salads. Get creative with your leftovers!

So there you have it - our mouthwatering Tasty Nut-Free Chicken Fajitas Recipe. With its vibrant colors, bold flavors, and easy preparation, it’s a must-try dish for any occasion. Whether you’re cooking for your family or hosting a dinner party, these fajitas are sure to impress. So grab your skillet and get ready to savor the deliciousness!
