Creamy and Savory Scalloped Corn: A Side Dish with a Twist


When it comes to side dishes, we often find ourselves gravitating towards the usual suspects—mashed potatoes, green beans, or a simple garden salad. While these classics have their place on the dinner table, why not spice things up with a side dish that’s both creamy and savory, with a delightful twist? Enter the creamy and savory scalloped corn—a dish that combines the sweetness of corn with the richness of a creamy sauce, all baked to golden perfection. This recipe is not only a crowd-pleaser but also a unique addition to any meal.

Ingredients

  • 4 cups of fresh or frozen corn kernels
  • 1 cup of heavy cream
  • 1/2 cup of milk
  • 1/4 cup of all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 cup of unsalted butter
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon of thyme
  • 1 teaspoon of paprika
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 cup of shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1/2 cup of grated Parmesan cheese
  • Fresh parsley for garnish

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C) and grease a baking dish with butter or cooking spray.

  2. In a saucepan, melt the butter over medium heat. Add the chopped onion and minced garlic, and sauté until they are soft and fragrant.

  3. Sprinkle the flour over the onion and garlic, stirring constantly to create a roux. Let it cook for a minute to get rid of the raw flour taste.

  4. Slowly pour in the heavy cream and milk, stirring continuously to avoid lumps. Allow the mixture to simmer and thicken.

  5. Once the sauce has thickened, add the thyme, paprika, salt, and pepper. Stir well to incorporate the seasonings.

  6. In a large mixing bowl, combine the corn kernels with the creamy sauce, shredded cheddar cheese, and half of the Parmesan cheese. Mix until the corn is evenly coated.

  7. Transfer the creamy corn mixture to the greased baking dish, spreading it out evenly.

  8. Sprinkle the remaining Parmesan cheese over the top of the corn mixture, adding an extra layer of richness.

  9. Cover the baking dish with aluminum foil and bake for 20 minutes. Then, remove the foil and bake for an additional 10-15 minutes, or until the top is golden and bubbling.

  10. Once out of the oven, let the scalloped corn rest for a few minutes before garnishing with fresh parsley.

Serving Suggestions

Creamy and savory scalloped corn pairs beautifully with a variety of main dishes. It makes a delightful accompaniment to roasted chicken, grilled steak, or even as part of a vegetarian spread. The creamy texture and delicate sweetness of the corn are a perfect match for heartier entrees, adding a touch of sophistication to any meal.

Final Thoughts

The creamy and savory scalloped corn is a side dish that’s sure to impress. Its rich, comforting flavors and creamy texture elevate any meal to a new level of indulgence. Whether you’re hosting a dinner party or simply looking to add a touch of elegance to a weeknight dinner, this dish is a winner. So, why not step out of the ordinary and give this delightful twist on a classic side dish a try? Your taste buds will thank you!
