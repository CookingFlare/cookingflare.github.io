Pasta with Clams: A Simple and Elegant Seafood Pasta Dish


If you’re a fan of seafood and pasta, then you’re in for a treat with this delightful recipe for Pasta with Clams. This dish is a perfect combination of simplicity and elegance, making it ideal for a cozy dinner at home or a special occasion with friends and family. With just a few high-quality ingredients, you can create a restaurant-worthy meal that will impress even the most discerning palates.

Ingredients

To make this delicious Pasta with Clams, you will need the following ingredients:

  • 1 pound of linguine or spaghetti
  • 2 pounds of fresh clams, scrubbed and cleaned
  • 4 cloves of garlic, thinly sliced
  • 1/2 teaspoon of red pepper flakes
  • 1/2 cup of dry white wine
  • 1/4 cup of extra virgin olive oil
  • 1/4 cup of fresh parsley, chopped
  • Salt and black pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Begin by cooking the pasta according to the package instructions in a large pot of salted boiling water. Cook the pasta until it is al dente, which means it should be tender yet still firm to the bite. Once the pasta is cooked, drain it and set it aside.

  2. While the pasta is cooking, prepare the clams. In a large skillet, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the sliced garlic and red pepper flakes to the skillet and sauté for about a minute, or until the garlic becomes fragrant.

  3. Add the cleaned clams to the skillet and pour in the white wine. Cover the skillet with a lid and let the clams steam for about 5-7 minutes, or until they have opened. Discard any clams that do not open.

  4. Once the clams have opened, remove the skillet from the heat. Use a slotted spoon to transfer the clams to a bowl, leaving the flavorful cooking liquid in the skillet.

  5. Return the skillet to the heat and add the cooked pasta to the skillet with the clam cooking liquid. Toss the pasta in the flavorful liquid and let it simmer for a minute or two to absorb the flavors.

  6. Add the cooked clams back to the skillet with the pasta and toss everything together gently. Season the pasta with salt and black pepper to taste.

  7. To finish, sprinkle the chopped fresh parsley over the pasta and clams, and give everything a final toss to combine.

  8. Serve the Pasta with Clams immediately, garnished with an extra drizzle of extra virgin olive oil and some additional chopped parsley.

Tips and Variations

  • For an extra burst of flavor, you can add a squeeze of fresh lemon juice to the pasta just before serving.
  • If you prefer a richer sauce, you can add a pat of butter to the skillet when tossing the pasta with the clam cooking liquid.
  • Feel free to customize this dish by adding other seafood such as shrimp or mussels for a seafood medley pasta.

Enjoy the Delights of Pasta with Clams

Pasta with Clams is a dish that celebrates the natural brininess of the sea, the freshness of the clams, and the simplicity of Italian cuisine. This recipe is a wonderful showcase of how a few quality ingredients can come together to create a memorable and satisfying meal. Whether you’re a seafood lover or simply looking to expand your culinary repertoire, Pasta with Clams is a must-try dish that will undoubtedly leave a lasting impression. So, gather your ingredients, roll up your sleeves, and embark on a culinary adventure that promises to delight your taste buds and those of your loved ones. Buon appetito!
