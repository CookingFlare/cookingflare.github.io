Greek Lemon Roasted Potatoes: The Perfect Side Dish for Any Meal


When it comes to versatile and delicious side dishes, Greek lemon roasted potatoes always steal the show. These flavorful potatoes are the epitome of Mediterranean cuisine, offering a perfect balance of zesty lemon, aromatic herbs, and crispy edges. Whether you’re hosting a dinner party or simply looking to elevate your weeknight meals, these Greek lemon roasted potatoes are sure to impress your taste buds and those of your guests.

Ingredients

  • 2 pounds of Yukon Gold potatoes, washed and cut into wedges
  • 1/3 cup of olive oil
  • 1/4 cup of freshly squeezed lemon juice
  • 4 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon of dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon of dried thyme
  • 1 teaspoon of paprika
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1/2 cup of chicken or vegetable broth
  • Fresh parsley for garnish

Instructions

  1. Preheat the Oven: Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C) and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.

  2. Prepare the Potatoes: In a large bowl, combine the potato wedges, olive oil, lemon juice, minced garlic, oregano, thyme, paprika, salt, and pepper. Toss the potatoes until they are evenly coated with the mixture.

  3. Arrange on the Baking Sheet: Place the seasoned potato wedges in a single layer on the prepared baking sheet, ensuring that they are not overcrowded. This allows the potatoes to roast evenly and develop a crispy exterior.

  4. Roast the Potatoes: Roast the potatoes in the preheated oven for 30 minutes, then remove them from the oven and carefully flip each wedge. Return the baking sheet to the oven and continue roasting for an additional 20-25 minutes or until the potatoes are golden brown and crispy.

  5. Add the Broth: Once the potatoes are roasted to perfection, remove the baking sheet from the oven and pour the chicken or vegetable broth over the potatoes. This step infuses the potatoes with even more flavor and helps to keep them moist.

  6. Garnish and Serve: Transfer the Greek lemon roasted potatoes to a serving dish, garnish with freshly chopped parsley, and serve hot.

Serving Suggestions

These Greek lemon roasted potatoes pair beautifully with a variety of main dishes. Serve them alongside grilled chicken, roasted lamb, or as part of a festive holiday spread. Their vibrant flavors and crispy texture make them a delightful addition to any meal.

Final Thoughts

Greek lemon roasted potatoes are a delightful departure from traditional side dishes, offering a burst of Mediterranean flavors with every bite. Whether you’re a fan of Greek cuisine or simply looking to add a new side dish to your repertoire, these potatoes are a must-try. Their versatility and crowd-pleasing nature make them a perfect choice for any occasion, from casual family dinners to elegant gatherings. So, next time you’re in the mood for a side dish that’s both comforting and exotic, give these Greek lemon roasted potatoes a try and transport your taste buds to the sunny shores of Greece.
