Deliciously Creamy Peanut Butter Chocolate Pie: A Match Made in Heaven

3 min · 505 words

If you’re a fan of the classic combination of peanut butter and chocolate, then this recipe is for you. Our Deliciously Creamy Peanut Butter Chocolate Pie is the perfect dessert to satisfy your sweet tooth and indulge in the rich flavors of these two beloved ingredients. With a smooth and creamy texture, this pie is a match made in heaven.

Ingredients

For the crust:

  • 1 ½ cups of crushed chocolate sandwich cookies
  • 5 tablespoons of melted butter

For the filling:

  • 1 cup of creamy peanut butter
  • 1 package (8 ounces) of cream cheese, softened
  • 1 cup of powdered sugar
  • 1 teaspoon of vanilla extract
  • 1 ½ cups of heavy cream

For the chocolate ganache:

  • 1 cup of semi-sweet chocolate chips
  • ½ cup of heavy cream

Instructions

  1. Start by making the crust. In a medium-sized bowl, combine the crushed chocolate sandwich cookies and melted butter. Mix until the crumbs are evenly coated. Press the mixture into the bottom and sides of a 9-inch pie dish. Place it in the refrigerator to set while you prepare the filling.

  2. In a large mixing bowl, beat the creamy peanut butter, softened cream cheese, powdered sugar, and vanilla extract until smooth and well combined. Set aside.

  3. In a separate mixing bowl, whip the heavy cream until stiff peaks form. Gently fold the whipped cream into the peanut butter mixture until fully incorporated.

  4. Take the pie crust out of the refrigerator and pour the peanut butter filling into it. Smooth the top with a spatula or the back of a spoon. Return the pie to the refrigerator and let it chill for at least 2 hours, or until set.

  5. While the pie is chilling, prepare the chocolate ganache. In a microwave-safe bowl, combine the semi-sweet chocolate chips and heavy cream. Heat in the microwave for 30-second intervals, stirring well after each interval, until the chocolate is melted and the mixture is smooth.

  6. Once the pie is fully set, pour the chocolate ganache over the top, spreading it evenly with a spatula. Return the pie to the refrigerator and let it chill for an additional 30 minutes, or until the ganache is set.

  7. When ready to serve, remove the pie from the refrigerator and let it sit at room temperature for a few minutes to soften slightly. Slice and enjoy!

Tips and Variations

  • For an extra indulgent touch, sprinkle some chopped peanuts or chocolate shavings on top of the pie before serving.
  • If you prefer a sweeter filling, you can increase the amount of powdered sugar to your liking.
  • Feel free to use a store-bought chocolate cookie crust if you’re short on time.
  • This pie can be stored in the refrigerator for up to 3 days. Just make sure to cover it tightly with plastic wrap or an airtight container.

Conclusion

With its irresistibly smooth and creamy peanut butter filling, paired with a decadent chocolate ganache, our Deliciously Creamy Peanut Butter Chocolate Pie is a dessert that will leave you craving for more. Whether you’re serving it to guests or indulging in a slice all by yourself, this pie is sure to satisfy your sweet tooth and bring a smile to your face. So go ahead, give this heavenly combination a try and enjoy every delicious bite!
