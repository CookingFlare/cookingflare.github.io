Instant Pot Lemon Garlic Chicken: Fresh and Zesty


If you’re looking for a quick and flavorful chicken dish, then look no further! Our Instant Pot Lemon Garlic Chicken recipe is the perfect combination of fresh and zesty flavors that will leave your taste buds craving for more. With the help of your trusty Instant Pot, you can have a delicious and succulent chicken dinner on the table in no time.

Ingredients

  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 4 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 2 lemons, juiced and zested
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 cup chicken broth

Instructions

  1. Start by seasoning your chicken breasts with salt, pepper, dried thyme, and dried oregano. Rub the seasoning into the chicken to ensure it’s evenly coated.

  2. Set your Instant Pot to the sauté function and heat the olive oil. Once the oil is hot, add the minced garlic and sauté for about a minute until fragrant.

  3. Carefully add the seasoned chicken breasts to the Instant Pot, making sure they are in a single layer. Sear each side for 2-3 minutes until golden brown. This step will help seal in the juices and add extra flavor to the chicken.

  4. Pour in the chicken broth, lemon juice, and lemon zest. Give everything a good stir to combine the flavors.

  5. Secure the lid on your Instant Pot and set the valve to the sealing position. Select the pressure cook function and set the timer for 8 minutes.

  6. Once the cooking time is up, allow the pressure to release naturally for 5 minutes, then do a quick release to release any remaining pressure.

  7. Carefully remove the chicken breasts from the Instant Pot and set them aside on a plate. Cover with foil to keep them warm.

  8. Switch the Instant Pot back to the sauté function and let the liquid simmer for a few minutes until it reduces and thickens slightly.

  9. Serve the lemon garlic sauce over the chicken breasts and garnish with fresh herbs if desired. This dish pairs perfectly with steamed vegetables or a side salad.

Tips and Variations

  • For an extra burst of flavor, add some sliced lemons and garlic cloves to the Instant Pot before cooking. This will infuse the chicken with even more citrusy goodness.
  • If you prefer a thicker sauce, you can mix a tablespoon of cornstarch with a tablespoon of water and add it to the Instant Pot after cooking. Stir well and let it simmer until the sauce thickens to your desired consistency.
  • To make this recipe even more versatile, you can shred the cooked chicken and use it in wraps, sandwiches, or salads.
  • Feel free to adjust the seasoning according to your taste preferences. You can add more or less garlic, thyme, or oregano to suit your palate.

So there you have it – a delicious and easy Instant Pot Lemon Garlic Chicken recipe that will become a family favorite in no time. The fresh and zesty flavors of lemon and garlic combine perfectly with the tender and juicy chicken. With the help of your Instant Pot, you can have a mouthwatering meal on the table in under 30 minutes. Enjoy!
