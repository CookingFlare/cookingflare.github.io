Weekend Brunch Goals: Recipes for Delicious Brunch Cocktails


Brunch is a delightful way to leisurely start your weekend, and what better way to elevate this experience than with delicious brunch cocktails? Whether you’re hosting a gathering or simply indulging in a relaxing morning at home, these cocktail recipes will add a touch of sophistication and flavor to your brunch spread. From classics with a twist to refreshing and innovative concoctions, here are some tantalizing recipes to elevate your weekend brunch goals.

Classic Mimosa

The classic mimosa is a timeless brunch cocktail that never fails to impress. Its simplicity and refreshing taste make it a perfect choice for any brunch occasion. To make this iconic drink, you’ll need chilled champagne or sparkling wine and fresh orange juice. For a twist, consider using blood orange juice for a vibrant and tangy variation.

Ingredients:

  • 1 part chilled champagne or sparkling wine
  • 1 part fresh orange juice (or blood orange juice for a twist)
  • Orange slices or zest for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Fill a champagne flute halfway with chilled champagne or sparkling wine.
  2. Slowly pour the fresh orange juice over the champagne.
  3. Gently stir to mix the flavors.
  4. Garnish with a slice of orange or a twist of orange zest.

Spicy Bloody Mary

For those who prefer a savory and spicy kick to their brunch cocktails, the Bloody Mary is a go-to choice. This bold and flavorful drink is known for its unique combination of tomato juice, vodka, and a zesty blend of spices. To elevate the traditional Bloody Mary, consider adding unique garnishes like pickled vegetables, crispy bacon, or even a mini slider for a truly indulgent brunch experience.

Ingredients:

  • 2 oz vodka
  • 4 oz tomato juice
  • 1/2 oz lemon juice
  • Dash of hot sauce
  • Dash of Worcestershire sauce
  • Pinch of salt and pepper
  • Celery salt for rimming the glass
  • Assorted garnishes (olives, pickles, celery, bacon, etc.)

Instructions:

  1. Rim a highball glass with celery salt by running a lemon wedge around the rim and dipping it into the salt.
  2. Fill the glass with ice.
  3. In a shaker, combine vodka, tomato juice, lemon juice, hot sauce, Worcestershire sauce, salt, and pepper.
  4. Shake well and strain into the prepared glass.
  5. Garnish with your choice of assorted garnishes.

Pineapple Coconut Mojito

For a tropical twist on a classic cocktail, the pineapple coconut mojito is a refreshing and vibrant option. Its combination of fresh mint, pineapple juice, coconut rum, and sparkling water creates a delightful blend of flavors that is perfect for a sunny brunch gathering.

Ingredients:

  • 6-8 fresh mint leaves
  • 1 oz fresh lime juice
  • 1 oz coconut rum
  • 2 oz pineapple juice
  • 2 oz sparkling water
  • Pineapple wedges and mint sprigs for garnish

Instructions:

  1. In a glass, muddle the fresh mint leaves with lime juice.
  2. Fill the glass with ice.
  3. Add the coconut rum and pineapple juice.
  4. Top with sparkling water and stir gently.
  5. Garnish with pineapple wedges and mint sprigs.

Lavender Lemonade Prosecco

Lavender lemonade prosecco is a delightful and elegant cocktail that brings a touch of floral and citrusy notes to your brunch table. The subtle fragrance of lavender combined with the effervescence of prosecco creates a sophisticated and refreshing drink that is sure to impress your guests.

Ingredients:

  • 1 oz lavender simple syrup
  • 1 oz fresh lemon juice
  • Chilled prosecco
  • Lavender sprigs for garnish

Instructions:

  1. In a champagne flute, combine the lavender syrup and fresh lemon juice.
  2. Top with chilled prosecco.
  3. Stir gently to mix the flavors.
  4. Garnish with a sprig of lavender.

Conclusion

Elevate your weekend brunch goals with these delightful and flavorful cocktail recipes. Whether you prefer the timeless elegance of a mimosa, the boldness of a Bloody Mary, the tropical flair of a pineapple coconut mojito, or the sophistication of a lavender lemonade prosecco, there’s a brunch cocktail for every preference. Cheers to a leisurely and indulgent brunch experience!

Enjoy these recipes responsibly and always drink responsibly. Cheers!
