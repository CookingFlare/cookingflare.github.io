Caramel Apple Upside-Down Cake: A Decadent Fall Dessert


Are you ready to indulge in a mouthwatering dessert that perfectly captures the essence of fall? Look no further than this delectable Caramel Apple Upside-Down Cake. With its rich caramel topping and tender apple slices, this cake is sure to become a favorite in your autumn baking repertoire.

Ingredients

For the caramel topping:

  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/4 cup water
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream

For the cake batter:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground nutmeg
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/2 cup milk

For the apple layer:

  • 2 large apples, peeled, cored, and thinly sliced

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C) and grease a 9-inch round cake pan.

  2. In a medium saucepan, combine the granulated sugar and water for the caramel topping. Cook over medium-high heat, stirring occasionally, until the sugar has dissolved and the mixture turns a deep amber color. This should take about 8-10 minutes.

  3. Remove the saucepan from the heat and carefully whisk in the butter until melted and fully incorporated. Slowly pour in the heavy cream while whisking constantly. Be cautious as the mixture may bubble up. Set aside to cool slightly.

  4. Pour the caramel sauce into the greased cake pan, making sure to spread it evenly across the bottom.

  5. Arrange the apple slices on top of the caramel sauce in a decorative pattern. Set aside.

  6. In a medium bowl, whisk together the flour, baking powder, salt, cinnamon, and nutmeg. Set aside.

  7. In a large bowl, cream together the softened butter and granulated sugar until light and fluffy. Add the eggs, one at a time, beating well after each addition. Stir in the vanilla extract.

  8. Gradually add the dry ingredients to the butter mixture, alternating with the milk. Begin and end with the dry ingredients, mixing until just combined after each addition. Be careful not to overmix.

  9. Carefully pour the cake batter over the apple slices in the cake pan, spreading it evenly with a spatula.

  10. Bake in the preheated oven for 40-45 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center of the cake comes out clean.

  11. Remove the cake from the oven and allow it to cool in the pan for 10 minutes. Then, place a serving plate upside down on top of the cake pan and carefully invert the cake onto the plate, allowing the caramel and apple layer to become the top of the cake.

  12. Let the cake cool completely before serving. This cake is best enjoyed slightly warm or at room temperature.

Serving Suggestions

This Caramel Apple Upside-Down Cake can be served on its own, or for an extra indulgence, top it with a scoop of vanilla ice cream or a dollop of freshly whipped cream. The warm caramel and tender apple slices pair perfectly with the moist and flavorful cake.

Conclusion

As the autumn leaves fall and the air turns crisp, treat yourself to a slice of this decadent Caramel Apple Upside-Down Cake. With its luscious caramel topping and comforting flavors, this cake is the epitome of fall desserts. Whether you’re hosting a dinner party or simply craving a sweet treat, this recipe is sure to impress. Enjoy the enchanting flavors of fall with every bite of this delightful dessert.
