Grilled Salads: Creative Ideas for BBQ Entertaining


Summer is here, and it’s the perfect time to fire up the grill and enjoy outdoor dining with family and friends. While grilling is often associated with juicy steaks, succulent burgers, and sizzling sausages, it’s time to think beyond the traditional BBQ fare and explore the world of grilled salads. Yes, you heard it right – grilled salads!

Grilled salads offer a unique twist to your BBQ spread, infusing smoky flavors into fresh and vibrant ingredients. They are not only delicious but also a great way to incorporate more vegetables into your diet. So, let’s dive into some creative ideas for grilled salads that will elevate your BBQ entertaining game.

Grilled Romaine Lettuce with Citrus Dressing

Start your grilled salad journey with a classic favorite – grilled romaine lettuce. This simple yet flavorful salad will leave your taste buds craving for more. Begin by halving a few heads of romaine lettuce lengthwise and brushing them with olive oil. Place them on a preheated grill and cook for about 2-3 minutes per side until they are slightly charred and wilted.

Once the lettuce is grilled to perfection, transfer it to a serving platter, and drizzle it with a tangy citrus dressing. To make the dressing, whisk together the juice of one orange, one lemon, and one lime, along with a tablespoon of honey, a clove of minced garlic, and a pinch of salt and pepper. Pour the dressing over the grilled romaine lettuce, and garnish with some toasted pine nuts and crumbled feta cheese. Your guests will be impressed by the smoky flavor and the burst of citrusy goodness in every bite.

Grilled Watermelon and Feta Salad

Grilled watermelon might sound unconventional, but trust me, it’s a game-changer. The heat of the grill caramelizes the watermelon, intensifying its sweetness and adding a subtle smokiness. To make this unique salad, cut your watermelon into thick slices and brush them with a little olive oil. Grill the slices for about 2 minutes per side until they have grill marks and are slightly softened.

Once the grilled watermelon is ready, cut it into bite-sized pieces and toss them with fresh mint leaves and crumbled feta cheese. Drizzle some balsamic glaze over the salad to balance the sweetness of the watermelon. The combination of juicy watermelon, creamy feta, and refreshing mint will take your taste buds on a delightful journey.

Grilled Corn and Avocado Salad

Corn on the cob is a BBQ staple, but have you ever tried grilling the corn for a salad? Grilled corn adds a smoky and slightly charred flavor to any dish, making it a fantastic addition to a summer salad. Begin by grilling the corn on a hot grill, rotating it occasionally until the kernels are tender and lightly charred. Once the corn is cool enough to handle, cut the kernels off the cob.

In a large bowl, combine the grilled corn kernels with diced avocado, cherry tomatoes, red onion, and chopped cilantro. Squeeze the juice of a lime over the salad and season with salt, pepper, and a pinch of chili powder for an extra kick. Toss everything together gently, and you have a vibrant and satisfying grilled corn and avocado salad.

Grilled Vegetable Medley Salad

If you want to showcase the beauty of grilled vegetables, a medley salad is the way to go. Gather an assortment of your favorite vegetables like zucchini, bell peppers, eggplant, and asparagus. Slice the vegetables into even-sized pieces and brush them with olive oil. Grill them until they are tender and have beautiful grill marks.

Once the vegetables are grilled to perfection, arrange them on a platter and sprinkle with crumbled goat cheese and toasted almonds. Drizzle some balsamic glaze or a simple vinaigrette over the salad for added flavor. The combination of smoky vegetables, creamy goat cheese, and crunchy almonds will make this grilled vegetable medley salad a showstopper at your BBQ gathering.

Conclusion

Grilled salads are a fantastic way to add depth and flavor to your BBQ entertaining. From grilled romaine lettuce to watermelon, corn, and a medley of vegetables, these creative ideas will elevate your summer dining experience. So, fire up that grill, experiment with different ingredients, and enjoy the smoky goodness of grilled salads. Your taste buds and your guests will thank you!
