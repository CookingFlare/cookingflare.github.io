Step-by-Step Guide to Making Korean Seafood Pancakes


If you’re a fan of Korean cuisine, you’ve probably tried or at least heard of Haemul Pajeon, which is a delightful Korean seafood pancake. This dish is a popular appetizer or snack in Korea and is enjoyed for its crispy texture and savory flavor. If you’re looking to bring the taste of Korea into your own kitchen, then follow this step-by-step guide to making Korean seafood pancakes.

Ingredients

For the Pancake Batter:

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup cold water
  • 1 egg
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon sesame oil
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt

For the Filling:

  • 1 cup assorted seafood (shrimp, squid, and/or mussels), chopped into bite-sized pieces
  • 1/2 cup scallions, chopped
  • 1/2 cup onion, thinly sliced
  • 1/2 cup carrots, julienned

Dipping Sauce:

  • 3 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon rice vinegar
  • 1 teaspoon sesame oil
  • 1 teaspoon sesame seeds
  • 1/2 teaspoon sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon red chili flakes (optional)

Instructions

Step 1: Prepare the Pancake Batter

In a mixing bowl, combine the all-purpose flour, cold water, egg, soy sauce, sesame oil, and salt. Whisk the ingredients together until you have a smooth batter. Set the batter aside while you prepare the filling.

Step 2: Prepare the Filling

In a separate bowl, mix the assorted seafood, scallions, sliced onion, and julienned carrots. Make sure the seafood and vegetables are well combined.

Step 3: Combine the Batter and Filling

Pour the prepared filling into the pancake batter. Gently fold the ingredients together until the seafood and vegetables are evenly coated with the batter.

Step 4: Cook the Pancakes

Heat a non-stick skillet over medium heat and add a generous amount of oil. Once the skillet is hot, pour a ladleful of the pancake batter into the skillet, spreading it out evenly to form a circular shape. Cook the pancake for 3-4 minutes on each side, or until it is golden brown and crispy.

Step 5: Make the Dipping Sauce

While the pancakes are cooking, prepare the dipping sauce by combining soy sauce, rice vinegar, sesame oil, sesame seeds, sugar, and red chili flakes (if using) in a small bowl. Stir the ingredients until the sugar has dissolved.

Step 6: Serve and Enjoy

Once the Korean seafood pancakes are cooked to perfection, transfer them to a serving plate. Cut the pancakes into wedges and serve them with the prepared dipping sauce. Enjoy the crispy, savory goodness of this Korean delicacy!

Tips and Variations

  • Feel free to customize the seafood filling based on your preferences. You can add more shrimp, squid, or mussels, depending on what you like.
  • If you prefer a spicier flavor, you can add chopped red or green chili peppers to the pancake batter.
  • For a vegetarian version, you can omit the seafood and add more vegetables like zucchini, bell peppers, or mushrooms.

Final Thoughts

Korean seafood pancakes are a delightful treat that brings together the flavors of the sea with the crispiness of a pancake. Whether you’re serving them as an appetizer at a dinner party or enjoying them as a snack, these savory pancakes are sure to be a hit. With this step-by-step guide, you can recreate the authentic taste of Korean cuisine in your own kitchen. So, gather your ingredients, follow the instructions, and savor the deliciousness of homemade Korean seafood pancakes!
