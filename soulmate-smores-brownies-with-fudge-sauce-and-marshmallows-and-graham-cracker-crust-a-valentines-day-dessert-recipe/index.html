Soulmate S'mores Brownies with Fudge Sauce and Marshmallows and Graham Cracker Crust: A Valentine's Day Dessert Recipe


Valentine’s Day is just around the corner, and what better way to celebrate love than with a decadent and indulgent dessert? These Soulmate S’mores Brownies with Fudge Sauce and Marshmallows and Graham Cracker Crust are the perfect treat to share with your special someone.

Ingredients

For the Graham Cracker Crust:

  • 1 1/2 cups graham cracker crumbs
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, melted

For the Brownie Batter:

  • 1 cup unsalted butter
  • 2 cups granulated sugar
  • 4 large eggs
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt

For the Fudge Sauce:

  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter
  • 1 cup semisweet chocolate chips
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt

For the Topping:

  • 2 cups mini marshmallows

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease a 9x13-inch baking dish and set aside.

  2. In a medium bowl, combine the graham cracker crumbs, sugar, and melted butter. Mix until well combined. Press the mixture into the bottom of the prepared baking dish to form an even crust.

  3. In a microwave-safe bowl, melt the butter for the brownie batter. Add the sugar and stir until well combined. Add the eggs, one at a time, mixing well after each addition. Stir in the vanilla extract.

  4. In a separate bowl, sift together the flour, cocoa powder, and salt. Gradually add the dry ingredients to the wet ingredients, stirring until just combined.

  5. Pour the brownie batter over the graham cracker crust in the baking dish, spreading it out evenly.

  6. Bake for 25-30 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out with a few moist crumbs. Remove from the oven and let cool slightly.

  7. While the brownies are cooling, prepare the fudge sauce. In a saucepan, combine the heavy cream, butter, chocolate chips, sugar, and salt. Cook over medium heat, stirring constantly, until the chocolate chips are melted and the sauce is smooth.

  8. Pour the fudge sauce over the slightly cooled brownies, spreading it out evenly. Top with mini marshmallows, gently pressing them into the fudge sauce.

  9. Return the brownies to the oven and broil on low for 2-3 minutes, or until the marshmallows are golden brown and toasted. Keep a close eye on them, as they can burn quickly.

  10. Remove from the oven and let cool completely before cutting into squares.

These Soulmate S’mores Brownies with Fudge Sauce and Marshmallows and Graham Cracker Crust are a sweet and gooey delight. The combination of the crunchy graham cracker crust, fudgy brownie, rich fudge sauce, and toasted marshmallows creates a dessert that is sure to impress your Valentine.

So, this Valentine’s Day, take the time to show your loved one just how much they mean to you with a homemade treat that is made with love. These Soulmate S’mores Brownies are the perfect way to make your Valentine’s Day extra special. Enjoy!
