15 Light Recipes for a Nutritious Summer BBQ

As the warm sun casts its golden glow and the days grow longer, summer beckons us to gather outdoors and fire up the grill. While traditional BBQ fare often leans towards heavier, calorie-laden options, this summer, why not embrace a lighter, more nutritious approach? Here are 15 light recipes that will not only tantalize your taste buds but also keep your health in check. 1. Grilled Vegetable Skewers Ingredients: Bell peppers (red, yellow, green), cut into chunks Zucchini, sliced Cherry tomatoes Red onion, cut into chunks Olive oil Salt and pepper Dried oregano Instructions: Preheat your grill to medium-high. In a bowl, toss the vegetables with olive oil, salt, pepper, and oregano. Thread the vegetables onto skewers, alternating colors for a vibrant look. Grill for 10-15 minutes, turning occasionally, until tender and slightly charred. Serve warm, drizzled with balsamic glaze if desired. 2. Citrus Herb Grilled Chicken Ingredients: 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts Juice of 2 oranges Juice of 1 lemon 2 cloves garlic, minced Fresh herbs (parsley, cilantro, or basil), chopped Olive oil Salt and pepper Instructions: In a bowl, whisk together citrus juices, garlic, herbs, olive oil, salt, and pepper. Marinate the chicken in the mixture for at least 30 minutes. Preheat the grill to medium-high and grill the chicken for about 6-7 minutes per side, or until cooked through. Let rest for a few minutes, slice, and serve with a fresh salad. 3. Quinoa Salad with Grilled Shrimp Ingredients: 1 cup quinoa, rinsed 2 cups water 1 pound shrimp, peeled and deveined Olive oil Lemon juice Garlic powder Salt and pepper Fresh parsley, chopped Instructions: Cook quinoa in boiling water according to package instructions. Let cool. Toss shrimp with olive oil, lemon juice, garlic powder, salt, and pepper. Grill shrimp on medium heat for 2-3 minutes per side, until pink and opaque. In a large bowl, combine quinoa, grilled shrimp, and parsley. Serve chilled or at room temperature. 4. Avocado and Black Bean Salad Ingredients: 2 ripe avocados, diced 1 can black beans, rinsed and drained 1 cup corn (fresh or frozen) 1 red bell pepper, diced ½ red onion, finely chopped Juice of 1 lime Fresh cilantro, chopped Salt and pepper Instructions: In a large bowl, combine avocados, black beans, corn, bell pepper, and onion. Drizzle with lime juice and season with salt and pepper. Gently toss to combine and garnish with cilantro. Serve chilled or at room temperature. 5. Grilled Pineapple with Honey and Mint Ingredients: 1 ripe pineapple, peeled and sliced into rings 2 tablespoons honey Fresh mint leaves Instructions: Preheat grill to medium heat. Brush pineapple rings with honey. Grill for about 3-4 minutes per side, until caramelized and grill marks appear. Garnish with fresh mint leaves and serve as a refreshing dessert. 6. Zucchini Noodles with Pesto Ingredients: 4 medium zucchinis, spiralized ½ cup basil pesto (store-bought or homemade) Cherry tomatoes, halved Olive oil Parmesan cheese, for serving Instructions: In a skillet, heat olive oil over medium heat and sauté zucchini noodles for 2-3 minutes until slightly tender. Remove from heat and toss with pesto and cherry tomatoes. Serve immediately, topped with grated Parmesan cheese. 7. Grilled Salmon with Dill Yogurt Sauce Ingredients: 4 salmon fillets Olive oil Salt and pepper 1 cup Greek yogurt 2 tablespoons fresh dill, chopped Juice of 1 lemon Instructions: Preheat grill to medium-high. Brush salmon with olive oil and season with salt and pepper. Grill salmon skin-side down for about 6-8 minutes per side or until cooked through. In a bowl, mix Greek yogurt, dill, and lemon juice for the sauce. Serve salmon with a dollop of dill yogurt sauce. 8. Watermelon Feta Salad Ingredients: 4 cups watermelon, cubed 1 cup feta cheese, crumbled Fresh mint leaves Balsamic vinegar Instructions: In a large bowl, combine watermelon and feta. Drizzle with balsamic vinegar and toss gently. Garnish with fresh mint leaves and serve chilled. 9. Spicy Grilled Shrimp Tacos Ingredients: 1 pound shrimp, peeled and deveined 2 tablespoons olive oil 1 teaspoon chili powder 1 teaspoon cumin Salt and pepper Corn tortillas Cabbage, shredded Avocado, sliced Lime wedges Instructions: In a bowl, combine shrimp, olive oil, chili powder, cumin, salt, and pepper. Grill shrimp for 2-3 minutes per side until cooked through. Assemble tacos by placing shrimp on corn tortillas and topping with cabbage and avocado. Serve with lime wedges. 10. Greek Yogurt Dip with Veggies Ingredients: 1 cup Greek yogurt 1 clove garlic, minced 1 tablespoon lemon juice Fresh herbs (dill, parsley, or chives), chopped Assorted fresh vegetables (carrots, cucumbers, bell peppers) for dipping Instructions: In a bowl, mix Greek yogurt, garlic, lemon juice, and herbs. Serve the dip in a bowl with an assortment of fresh vegetables for dipping. 11. Caprese Skewers Ingredients: Cherry tomatoes Fresh mozzarella balls Fresh basil leaves Balsamic reduction Salt and pepper Instructions: On small skewers, thread a cherry tomato, a basil leaf, and a mozzarella ball. Repeat until all ingredients are used. Drizzle with balsamic reduction and sprinkle with salt and pepper before serving. 12. Grilled Portobello Mushrooms Ingredients: 4 large portobello mushrooms Olive oil Balsamic vinegar Salt and pepper Garlic powder Instructions: Preheat grill to medium heat. Brush mushrooms with olive oil, balsamic vinegar, and season with salt, pepper, and garlic powder. Grill for about 5-7 minutes per side, until tender. Serve as a side dish or on a bun as a healthy burger alternative. 13. Chilled Cucumber Soup Ingredients: 4 cucumbers, peeled and chopped 1 cup Greek yogurt 1 tablespoon olive oil Juice of 1 lemon Salt and pepper Fresh dill or mint for garnish Instructions: In a blender, combine cucumbers, Greek yogurt, olive oil, lemon juice, salt, and pepper. Blend until smooth. Chill in the refrigerator for at least 30 minutes before serving. Garnish with fresh dill or mint. 14. Shrimp and Avocado Lettuce Wraps Ingredients: 1 pound shrimp, peeled and deveined Olive oil Lime juice Salt and pepper Butter lettuce leaves Avocado, diced Cilantro for garnish Instructions: In a bowl, toss shrimp with olive oil, lime juice, salt, and pepper. Grill shrimp for 2-3 minutes per side until cooked through. Serve shrimp in butter lettuce leaves topped with diced avocado and cilantro. 15. Fruit Skewers with Yogurt Dip Ingredients: Assorted fruits (strawberries, pineapple, grapes, melon) 1 cup Greek yogurt Honey (optional) Chopped nuts (optional) Instructions: Thread assorted fruits onto skewers. In a bowl, mix Greek yogurt with honey if desired. Serve fruit skewers with yogurt dip on the side, and sprinkle with chopped nuts for crunch.
