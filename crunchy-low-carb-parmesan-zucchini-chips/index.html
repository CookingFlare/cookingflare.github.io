Crunchy Low Carb Parmesan Zucchini Chips


If you’re looking for a delicious and healthy snack that’s low in carbs but high in flavor, then these crunchy parmesan zucchini chips are just what you need. Whether you’re following a low-carb diet or simply want to enjoy a guilt-free snack, these zucchini chips are the perfect solution. They are easy to make, incredibly tasty, and a great way to sneak some veggies into your diet.

Ingredients

  • 2 medium zucchinis
  • 1/2 cup grated parmesan cheese
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon onion powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon paprika
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • Cooking spray

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 425°F (220°C) and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.

  2. Wash the zucchinis and slice them into thin rounds, about 1/8 inch thick. It’s important to make the slices as uniform as possible to ensure even cooking.

  3. In a medium bowl, combine the parmesan cheese, garlic powder, onion powder, paprika, black pepper, and salt. Mix well to combine all the seasonings.

  4. Place the zucchini slices in a single layer on the prepared baking sheet. Lightly spray the slices with cooking spray to help the seasoning adhere.

  5. Sprinkle the parmesan seasoning mixture evenly over the zucchini slices, ensuring each slice is coated with the delicious mixture.

  6. Place the baking sheet in the preheated oven and bake for 15-20 minutes, or until the zucchini chips are golden and crispy. Keep an eye on them towards the end of the cooking time to prevent burning.

  7. Once the zucchini chips are done, remove them from the oven and allow them to cool for a few minutes. This will help them crisp up even more.

  8. Serve the zucchini chips as a delightful snack on their own or with your favorite dip. They’re best enjoyed fresh out of the oven, but you can store any leftovers in an airtight container for a day or two.

Health Benefits

Zucchinis are a fantastic source of vitamins and minerals, including vitamin C, vitamin A, and potassium. They are also low in calories and carbohydrates, making them an excellent choice for anyone looking to maintain a healthy diet. By baking the zucchini slices instead of frying them, you can enjoy the crispy texture without the added oil and calories.

The parmesan cheese adds a savory and salty flavor to the zucchini chips, making them an irresistible snack for any occasion. Plus, with the added seasonings, you’ll be treated to a burst of delicious flavors with every crunchy bite.

Final Thoughts

These crunchy low carb parmesan zucchini chips are a fantastic alternative to traditional potato chips or other high-carb snacks. They are easy to make, packed with flavor, and are sure to satisfy your craving for something crispy and delicious. Whether you enjoy them on their own or as a side to your favorite dish, these zucchini chips are a versatile and guilt-free treat that everyone will love.

So, next time you’re in the mood for a savory snack, give these zucchini chips a try. They’re a delightful way to enjoy the goodness of zucchinis in a whole new light. Enjoy!
