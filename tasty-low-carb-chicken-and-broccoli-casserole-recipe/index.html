Tasty Low Carb Chicken and Broccoli Casserole Recipe


If you’re looking for a delicious and healthy meal that won’t derail your low-carb diet, this Tasty Low Carb Chicken and Broccoli Casserole Recipe is a must-try. Packed with flavor and nutrients, this casserole is the perfect combination of tender chicken, crisp broccoli, and creamy cheese sauce. Plus, it’s incredibly easy to make!

Ingredients

  • 2 cups cooked chicken breast, shredded
  • 4 cups fresh broccoli florets
  • 1 cup cheddar cheese, shredded
  • 1/2 cup sour cream
  • 1/2 cup mayonnaise
  • 1/4 cup chicken broth
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • 1/2 teaspoon onion powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C). Grease a 9x9-inch baking dish and set aside.

  2. In a large bowl, combine the shredded chicken, broccoli florets, and cheddar cheese. Mix well and set aside.

  3. In a separate bowl, whisk together the sour cream, mayonnaise, chicken broth, minced garlic, dried thyme, onion powder, salt, and black pepper. Ensure all the ingredients are well combined.

  4. Pour the sour cream mixture over the chicken, broccoli, and cheese. Stir gently until everything is evenly coated.

  5. Transfer the mixture to the greased baking dish, spreading it out evenly.

  6. Bake in the preheated oven for 25-30 minutes, or until the casserole is bubbling and the cheese is golden and melted.

  7. Remove from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes before serving.

  8. Serve this delicious low carb chicken and broccoli casserole as a main dish or as a side to complement your favorite protein. It pairs well with grilled chicken, steak, or even fish.

Why This Recipe is a Winner

This Tasty Low Carb Chicken and Broccoli Casserole Recipe is a winner for several reasons. Firstly, it’s a low-carb dish, making it suitable for those following a keto or low-carbohydrate diet. The combination of chicken and broccoli provides a good source of protein and fiber, while keeping the carb count low.

Secondly, this casserole is incredibly easy to make. With just a few simple steps, you can have a satisfying and flavorful meal on the table in no time. It’s perfect for busy weeknights when you want something nutritious but don’t have much time to spare.

Lastly, the creamy cheese sauce adds a delightful richness to the dish, making it incredibly satisfying and comforting. The flavors of the chicken, broccoli, and cheese complement each other perfectly, creating a delicious harmony of flavors.
