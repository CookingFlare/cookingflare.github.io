Decadent Chocolate Peppermint Cheesecake Bars: A Holiday Indulgence


The holiday season is all about indulgence, and what better way to treat yourself and your loved ones than with a batch of decadent chocolate peppermint cheesecake bars? These delightful bars combine the rich, velvety goodness of chocolate with the refreshing kick of peppermint, all layered on top of a luscious cheesecake filling. Whether you’re hosting a festive gathering or simply craving a sweet holiday treat, these cheesecake bars are sure to impress.

Ingredients

For the Crust:

  • 1 1/2 cups chocolate cookie crumbs
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, melted

For the Cheesecake Filling:

  • 24 oz cream cheese, softened
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 3 large eggs
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1/2 cup sour cream

For the Chocolate Peppermint Layer:

  • 12 oz semisweet chocolate, chopped
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • 1 tsp peppermint extract
  • Crushed candy canes for topping

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 325°F (160°C). Line a 9x13-inch baking pan with parchment paper, leaving an overhang on the sides for easy removal later.

  2. Make the Crust: In a medium bowl, combine the chocolate cookie crumbs, sugar, and melted butter. Press the mixture firmly into the bottom of the prepared pan. Bake for 10 minutes, then set aside to cool.

  3. Prepare the Cheesecake Filling: In a large bowl, beat the cream cheese and sugar until smooth and creamy. Add the eggs one at a time, beating well after each addition. Mix in the vanilla extract and sour cream until fully combined. Pour the filling over the cooled crust.

  4. Bake the Cheesecake: Place the pan in the preheated oven and bake for 40-45 minutes, or until the edges are set but the center still jiggles slightly. Remove from the oven and let it cool completely.

  5. Make the Chocolate Peppermint Layer: In a microwave-safe bowl, heat the chopped chocolate and heavy cream in 30-second intervals, stirring until smooth. Stir in the peppermint extract. Pour the chocolate mixture over the cooled cheesecake layer and spread it evenly. Sprinkle crushed candy canes on top.

  6. Chill and Serve: Refrigerate the cheesecake bars for at least 4 hours, or preferably overnight, until set. Once chilled, use the parchment overhang to lift the bars out of the pan and onto a cutting board. Cut into squares and serve.

Tips for Success

  • Use high-quality chocolate for a rich and luxurious flavor.
  • Allow the cheesecake bars to chill thoroughly for the best texture and flavor.
  • Feel free to customize the toppings with additional crushed peppermint candies, chocolate shavings, or a drizzle of melted white chocolate.

Conclusion

These Decadent Chocolate Peppermint Cheesecake Bars are the perfect holiday indulgence, combining the classic flavors of chocolate and peppermint with the creamy goodness of cheesecake. Whether you’re celebrating with family and friends or simply treating yourself to a festive dessert, these bars are guaranteed to bring joy and delight to the holiday season. Enjoy the magic of the holidays with every blissful bite of these indulgent cheesecake bars!
