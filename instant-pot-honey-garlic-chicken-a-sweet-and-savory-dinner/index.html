Instant Pot Honey Garlic Chicken: A Sweet and Savory Dinner


Are you looking for a delicious, easy-to-make dinner that the whole family will love? Look no further than this Instant Pot Honey Garlic Chicken! Packed with sweet and savory flavors, this dish is perfect for busy nights when you want something quick but full of flavor. With the help of your Instant Pot, you can have a tender, juicy chicken dinner ready in under 30 minutes, making it the perfect meal for anyone with a hectic schedule.

Why Choose Instant Pot Honey Garlic Chicken?

The Instant Pot is a kitchen game-changer. It’s a versatile tool that can cut down cooking time significantly without compromising on flavor or texture. This Honey Garlic Chicken recipe harnesses the power of the Instant Pot to create tender, flavorful chicken in record time. The combination of honey and garlic creates a sauce that’s both sweet and savory, making it a perfect pairing with chicken. The best part? You can make it all in one pot!

This recipe is ideal for those who love a balance of flavors. The sweetness from the honey pairs wonderfully with the savory depth of the garlic, and the Instant Pot locks in all the moisture, ensuring your chicken is never dry. Whether you’re cooking for yourself or for a crowd, this dish is sure to impress.

Ingredients for Instant Pot Honey Garlic Chicken

To make this dish, you’ll need the following ingredients. The beauty of this recipe lies in its simplicity. These are pantry staples, so you probably already have most of them on hand!

  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts (or thighs, if preferred)
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/4 cup honey
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce (for a gluten-free option, use tamari)
  • 1 tablespoon apple cider vinegar or white vinegar
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme (or fresh thyme, if you prefer)
  • 1 teaspoon paprika (for a bit of smoky flavor)
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt (to taste)
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper (to taste)
  • 1/2 cup chicken broth (or water if you prefer)
  • Chopped parsley or green onions for garnish (optional)

Instructions for Making Instant Pot Honey Garlic Chicken

Making this dish is incredibly straightforward, thanks to the Instant Pot. Here’s a step-by-step guide to help you prepare this flavorful dinner:

Step 1: Sauté the Chicken

Start by setting your Instant Pot to the “Sauté” mode and allowing it to heat up. Add 2 tablespoons of olive oil to the pot. Once the oil is hot, add the chicken breasts and sear them on both sides for about 2-3 minutes per side, or until they are lightly browned. This step helps lock in the juices and creates a delicious crust on the chicken. Once seared, remove the chicken from the pot and set it aside.

Step 2: Make the Honey Garlic Sauce

In the same Instant Pot, add the minced garlic and sauté it for about 30 seconds, just until it becomes fragrant. Be careful not to burn it. Then, add the honey, soy sauce, apple cider vinegar, thyme, paprika, salt, and black pepper. Stir the mixture well to combine all the flavors.

Step 3: Pressure Cook the Chicken

Return the chicken breasts to the Instant Pot and pour the sauce over the top. Add the chicken broth (or water) to the pot as well. This will create enough liquid for the Instant Pot to come to pressure. Close the lid, set the valve to “Sealing,” and select the “Pressure Cook” or “Manual” mode. Set the timer for 8 minutes if you’re using chicken breasts. If you’re using thighs, you may want to set it for 10 minutes. Once the cooking cycle is complete, allow the Instant Pot to naturally release pressure for 5 minutes, then carefully do a quick release to release any remaining pressure.

Step 4: Serve and Garnish

Once the pressure has been released and it’s safe to open the lid, remove the chicken from the pot. If you want to thicken the sauce, set the Instant Pot back to the “Sauté” mode and let the sauce simmer for a few minutes until it reduces to your desired consistency.

To serve, drizzle the sauce over the chicken breasts and garnish with freshly chopped parsley or green onions for a burst of color and freshness.

Tips for the Best Instant Pot Honey Garlic Chicken

Here are a few tips to ensure your Instant Pot Honey Garlic Chicken turns out perfectly every time:

  • Use chicken thighs for extra juiciness: While chicken breasts are a great option, chicken thighs are slightly more forgiving and stay juicy even if slightly overcooked. If you prefer dark meat, go with thighs!
  • Adjust the sweetness and saltiness: Feel free to adjust the amount of honey and soy sauce to suit your personal taste. If you prefer a sweeter sauce, add more honey. If you like it saltier, add a little more soy sauce.
  • Thicken the sauce: If you prefer a thicker sauce, you can add a cornstarch slurry (mix 1 tablespoon of cornstarch with 1 tablespoon of water) to the pot and stir it in while the Instant Pot is on “Sauté” mode. Let it cook for 2-3 minutes to thicken up.
  • Serve with a side of rice or vegetables: This chicken is great with steamed rice, mashed potatoes, or a side of roasted vegetables. The honey garlic sauce is delicious when drizzled over your side dishes as well.
  • Leftovers: This dish makes great leftovers! The chicken stays moist and the flavors intensify the next day. Store it in an airtight container in the fridge for up to 3-4 days.

Why This Recipe Is Perfect for Weeknight Dinners

There are many reasons why this Instant Pot Honey Garlic Chicken should be a go-to recipe for weeknight dinners:

  1. Quick and Easy: With the help of your Instant Pot, this meal comes together in under 30 minutes. There’s no need to slave away in the kitchen after a long day.
  2. Minimal Prep Time: All you need to do is sear the chicken and make the sauce—no complicated steps, and it’s ready to go in a matter of minutes.
  3. One-Pot Meal: Everything is made in the Instant Pot, which means minimal cleanup. You won’t have multiple pans to wash, making it a stress-free dinner.
  4. Family-Friendly: The balance of sweet and savory flavors appeals to both adults and kids, making it a hit for the entire family. Plus, it’s easy to customize for different tastes.
  5. Versatile: You can pair this dish with various sides like rice, vegetables, or noodles, and it works equally well with chicken breasts or thighs.

The Magic of the Instant Pot

The Instant Pot isn’t just a pressure cooker—it’s a versatile kitchen appliance that can also sauté, steam, slow cook, and even make yogurt. When it comes to dishes like Honey Garlic Chicken, the Instant Pot’s ability to lock in flavors and moisture while cutting down cooking time is invaluable.

By using the “Pressure Cook” function, you can cook chicken to tender perfection in a fraction of the time it would normally take in the oven. This is especially helpful if you’re trying to get a meal on the table quickly after a busy day at work or school.

Pairing Suggestions for Honey Garlic Chicken

While this dish is delicious on its own, it’s even better when paired with the right sides. Here are some ideas:

  • Steamed Jasmine or Basmati Rice: The aromatic flavors of these rice varieties are a great complement to the sweet and savory sauce.
  • Mashed Potatoes: Creamy mashed potatoes make the perfect base for soaking up the honey garlic sauce.
  • Roasted Vegetables: Roasted carrots, broccoli, or green beans would work wonderfully with this dish. Their natural sweetness complements the honey in the sauce.
  • Crispy Brussels Sprouts: If you’re looking for a healthier option, roasted Brussels sprouts offer a slight bitterness that balances out the sweetness of the chicken.

Final Thoughts on Instant Pot Honey Garlic Chicken

This Instant Pot Honey Garlic Chicken is a perfect example of how a few simple ingredients can come together to create a dish that is both delicious and satisfying. Whether you’re a busy parent, a working professional, or someone who just loves a tasty, easy-to-make dinner, this recipe checks all the boxes. It’s quick, flavorful, and requires minimal effort. With the Instant Pot doing most of the work, you can enjoy a restaurant-quality meal in the comfort of your own home without spending hours in the kitchen.

So, the next time you’re craving something sweet, savory, and utterly delicious, try out this Instant Pot Honey Garlic Chicken. It will become a favorite in your weeknight dinner rotation, and your family will be asking for seconds!
