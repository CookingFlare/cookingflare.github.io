Angler's Table: The Ultimate Seafood Recipes Cookbook for Fishermen and Foodies


Whether you are a seasoned angler or simply an avid seafood lover, the Angler’s Table is the ultimate cookbook for those who appreciate the freshest catches of the day. In this culinary treasure, seafood recipes have been lovingly crafted by fishermen and seasoned chefs from around the world. Delight in not just the diverse flavors, but also the shared experiences of enjoying and preserving the fruits of one’s labor from the high seas.

Dive into these mouth-watering selections of fresh seafood dishes that star as the main course, or become the perfect accompaniments to your favorite meal. Explore the vast culinary landscape of seafood with simple recipes, elaborate creations, and everything in between. Immerse yourself in the world of angling and discover the perfect harmonious bond between quality fish and rustic flavors that celebrate the taste of nature.

  1. Seared Tuna with Mango Salsa

The seared tuna dish adorned with a lively burst of mango salsa is an enticing combo of fresh flavors. Seared to perfection and served with a zesty fruit salsa, it’s equally dreamy to feast on if you’re a lone angler or a group of lovable seafood fanatics.

Ingredients:

  • 4 tuna steaks, about 1 inch thick
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • ½ diced red onion
  • 1 large minced jalapeño
  • 2 teaspoons honey
  • 1 ripe mango, diced
  • ¼ cup chopped fresh cilantro
  • ½ cup halved cherry tomatoes
  • Lime wedges

Instructions:

  1. Season the tuna steaks with salt and pepper.

  2. In a large skillet, heat the olive oil over medium-high heat. Sear the tuna steaks for about 3 minutes per side, or to your desired doneness.

  3. In a bowl, mix together onion, jalapeño, honey, mango, cilantro, and cherry tomatoes to create the mango salsa. Season to taste.

  4. Serve the seared tuna alongside the fresh mango salsa. Provide lime wedges for squeezing over the tuna.

  5. Feast away on this wholesome and sumptuous dish that celebrates fresh fish with just the right heat, tang, and sweetness!

  6. Warm Crab Cakes with Avocado Remoulade

This delightful Angler’s Table recipe boasts flavorful crab cakes with a moist and tender interior that is tastefully dressed up with a tangy, delectable avocado remoulade. Crab cakes are always a hit at family feasts and gatherings, guaranteed to tantalize any seafood aficionado’s palette.

Ingredients:

  • 1 small remoulade, recipe follows
  • 4 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 cup finely crushed saltine crackers
  • 1 egg, lightly beaten
  • 1 tablespoon minced garlic
  • 1 teaspoon hot sauce (such as Frank’s RedHot)
  • 1 teaspoon Worcestershire sauce
  • Salt and freshly ground black pepper
  • 2 pounds crabmeat
  • 1 large egg white
  • 1/4 cup finely chopped red bell pepper
  • 3/4 cup finely chopped green onions
  • 1/2 cup finely chopped fresh parsley
  • Avocado slices

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, mix the crushed saltine crackers, egg, garlic, hot sauce, Worcestershire sauce, salt, and pepper. Stir in the crabmeat, egg white, red bell pepper, green onions, and parsley until just combined.

  2. Shape the mixture into 12 equal-sized crab cakes and chill for an hour.

  3. In a large cast iron skillet, heat olive oil over medium-high heat. Sear the crab cakes for 3-4 minutes per side, until crispy and golden brown.

  4. Plate the warm crab cakes with a dollop of creamy avocado remoulade and slices of avocado for a delightful textural contrast.

  5. Enjoy this pocket-sized gourmet sensation that’s perfect for a fancy casual gathering or a light seafood-themed meal shared among fishermen and food enthusiasts alike.

  6. Pan-Seared Scallops with Lemon Risotto

Angler’s Table shares their exquisite pan-seared scallops that burst with a heavenly gentle sweetness, coupled with an equally delectable creamy lemon risotto. This divine seafood and rice dish is a masterful expression of culinary expertise, showcasing finesse and indulgence.

Ingredients:

  • 1 1/2 cups Arborio rice
  • 7 cups chicken stock
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • Salt and freshly ground black pepper
  • 1/2 cup finely chopped onion
  • 2 cups white wine
  • 2 cups chopped fresh spinach
  • 2 pt cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1 1/2 lbs sea scallops
  • 1/3 cup freshly squeezed lemon juice
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh chives
  • Grated lemon zest

Instructions:

  1. For the risotto, heat the stock in a saucepan over low heat.
  2. In a separate pot, heat the butter and oil over medium heat. Add onion and cook until soft. Add the rice and stir until well coated. Pour in the wine and stir until the liquid is absorbed.
  3. Add the stock to the rice one ladleful at a time, allowing each ladleful to be absorbed before adding the next. This process should take about 20 minutes.
  4. Stir in spinach and cherry tomatoes, and continue to cook until the rice is creamy and al dente.
  5. For the scallops, pat them dry with paper towels, then salt and pepper them. In a large skillet over high heat, add olive oil and scallops and sear for 1 to 2 mins per side, until golden brown.
  6. Assemble the Pan-Seared Scallops with Lemon Risotto by layering the chef’s-choice risotto and seared scallops, finishing with a sprinkle of chives and lemon zest.
  7. Get ready to indulge in an epicurean adventure with Pan-Seared Scallops and Lemon Risotto—an opulent harmony of flavors that marries the ocean’s royalty with the warm embrace of Italy.
