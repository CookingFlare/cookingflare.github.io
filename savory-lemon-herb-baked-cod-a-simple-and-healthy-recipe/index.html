Savory Lemon Herb Baked Cod: A Simple and Healthy Recipe


If you’re looking for a delicious and nutritious meal that’s quick and easy to prepare, then look no further than this savory lemon herb baked cod recipe. Packed with flavor and loaded with health benefits, this dish is perfect for busy weeknights or lazy weekends when you want something satisfying without spending hours in the kitchen.

Ingredients

  • 4 cod fillets
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 lemon, zested and juiced
  • 1 tablespoon fresh parsley, chopped
  • 1 tablespoon fresh dill, chopped
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C). Line a baking sheet with parchment paper or aluminum foil for easy cleanup.

  2. Place the cod fillets on the prepared baking sheet and drizzle with olive oil. Make sure both sides of the fish are coated evenly.

  3. In a small bowl, combine the minced garlic, lemon zest, chopped parsley, and dill. Sprinkle this herb mixture evenly over the cod fillets, pressing it gently to adhere.

  4. Squeeze the juice from the lemon over the seasoned fillets. The lemon juice adds a tangy brightness that complements the savory herbs perfectly.

  5. Season the fish with salt and pepper to taste. Remember, a little goes a long way, so start with a light sprinkling and adjust to your preference.

  6. Place the baking sheet in the preheated oven and bake for about 15-20 minutes, or until the cod is opaque and flakes easily with a fork. Cooking times may vary depending on the thickness of the fillets, so keep an eye on them to avoid overcooking.

  7. Once cooked, remove the baking sheet from the oven and let the cod rest for a few minutes before serving. This allows the flavors to meld and the fish to become even more tender and moist.

Serving Suggestions

This savory lemon herb baked cod pairs well with a variety of side dishes. Here are a few suggestions to complete your meal:

  • Roasted Vegetables: Toss some seasonal veggies like carrots, bell peppers, and zucchini with olive oil, salt, and pepper, then roast them in the oven alongside the cod for a simple and nutritious side.

  • Quinoa Salad: Make a refreshing quinoa salad with cucumber, cherry tomatoes, feta cheese, and a lemon vinaigrette. The lightness of the salad balances the rich flavors of the cod perfectly.

  • Mashed Cauliflower: For a low-carb alternative to mashed potatoes, steam some cauliflower florets and mash them with a bit of butter, garlic, and salt. The creamy texture complements the flaky cod beautifully.

Health Benefits

Not only is this savory lemon herb baked cod delicious, but it’s also packed with health benefits. Cod is an excellent source of lean protein and is rich in omega-3 fatty acids, which are essential for heart health and brain function. The fresh herbs add a burst of antioxidants and anti-inflammatory compounds, while the lemon juice provides a dose of vitamin C and aids in digestion.

Conclusion

With its vibrant flavors and healthful properties, this savory lemon herb baked cod recipe is a winner on all fronts. Whether you’re a seafood lover or simply looking to incorporate more nutritious meals into your diet, this dish is sure to satisfy your taste buds and keep you feeling nourished. So go ahead and give it a try – your body and your taste buds will thank you!

Remember to experiment with different herbs and spices to personalize this recipe to your liking. Cooking should be a fun and creative experience, so don’t be afraid to make it your own. Enjoy!
