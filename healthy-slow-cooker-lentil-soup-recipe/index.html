Healthy Slow Cooker Lentil Soup Recipe


When it comes to comfort food, a hearty bowl of soup is hard to beat. This Healthy Slow Cooker Lentil Soup is not only delicious and satisfying, but it’s also packed with nutritious ingredients that will nourish your body and warm your soul. Whether you’re looking for an easy weeknight dinner or a meal prep option for the week ahead, this recipe is a perfect choice. Let’s dive into the details of this wholesome and flavorful soup!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup green or brown lentils, rinsed
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 carrots, diced
  • 2 celery stalks, chopped
  • 3 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon ground turmeric
  • 1/2 teaspoon smoked paprika
  • 6 cups vegetable broth
  • 1 can (14 oz) diced tomatoes
  • 2 cups chopped spinach or kale
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley for garnish

Instructions

  1. Prep the Ingredients: Start by rinsing the lentils and chopping the onion, carrots, and celery. Mince the garlic and set everything aside.

  2. Combine Ingredients in the Slow Cooker: In the slow cooker, add the lentils, chopped onion, carrots, celery, minced garlic, ground cumin, turmeric, smoked paprika, vegetable broth, and diced tomatoes. Give everything a good stir to ensure the spices are evenly distributed.

  3. Set and Forget: Cover the slow cooker and cook the soup on low for 6-8 hours or on high for 3-4 hours. The slow cooking process allows the flavors to meld together and the lentils to become tender.

  4. Add Leafy Greens: About 30 minutes before serving, stir in the chopped spinach or kale. This will add a pop of color and an extra dose of vitamins to the soup.

  5. Season and Serve: Once the lentils are tender and the greens are wilted, season the soup with salt and pepper to taste. Ladle the soup into bowls, garnish with fresh parsley, and serve hot.

Why This Recipe Works

  • Nutrient-Packed: Lentils are a great source of plant-based protein, fiber, and essential nutrients. Combined with a variety of vegetables, this soup provides a wholesome and balanced meal.

  • Convenient and Time-Saving: The beauty of using a slow cooker is that you can simply set it and forget it. This makes the recipe perfect for busy days when you want to come home to a comforting meal without the hassle of cooking.

  • Versatile and Customizable: Feel free to customize this recipe with your favorite vegetables or add a touch of heat with some red pepper flakes. You can also adjust the seasoning to suit your taste preferences.

  • Meal Prep Friendly: This soup keeps well in the refrigerator for up to 4 days, making it an excellent option for meal prepping lunches or quick dinners throughout the week.

Final Thoughts

This Healthy Slow Cooker Lentil Soup is a wholesome and satisfying dish that’s perfect for nourishing your body and warming your soul. Whether you’re new to cooking with lentils or a seasoned pro, this recipe is sure to become a favorite in your kitchen. The simplicity of the slow cooker method combined with the nourishing ingredients makes this soup a winner for busy individuals and families alike. Give it a try and savor the comforting flavors of this nutritious soup!
