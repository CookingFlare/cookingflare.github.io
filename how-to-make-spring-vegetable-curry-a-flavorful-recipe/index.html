How to Make Spring Vegetable Curry: A Flavorful Recipe


Spring is the perfect time to enjoy fresh vegetables bursting with vibrant flavors and colors. If you’re looking for a delicious and healthy way to celebrate the season, look no further than this Spring Vegetable Curry recipe. Packed with nutrient-rich vegetables and aromatic spices, this curry will tantalize your taste buds and leave you feeling satisfied.

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 tablespoon ginger, grated
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon ground coriander
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon chili powder (adjust to taste)
  • 1 cup coconut milk
  • 1 cup vegetable broth
  • 1 cup green beans, trimmed and cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 1 cup peas
  • 1 cup asparagus, cut into 1-inch pieces
  • 1 cup carrots, sliced
  • 1 cup cauliflower florets
  • Salt, to taste
  • Fresh cilantro, for garnish

Instructions

  1. Heat the vegetable oil in a large skillet or pot over medium heat. Add the chopped onion and sauté until translucent, about 5 minutes.

  2. Add the minced garlic and grated ginger to the skillet and cook for another minute, until fragrant.

  3. In a small bowl, combine the ground cumin, ground coriander, turmeric powder, and chili powder. Add this spice mixture to the skillet and cook for a minute, stirring constantly to coat the onions and aromatics.

  4. Pour in the coconut milk and vegetable broth, stirring well to combine with the spices. Bring the mixture to a simmer.

  5. Add the green beans, peas, asparagus, carrots, and cauliflower florets to the skillet. Season with salt to taste. Stir to coat the vegetables with the curry sauce.

  6. Cover the skillet and cook for about 10-15 minutes, or until the vegetables are tender but still crisp. Stir occasionally to ensure even cooking.

  7. Once the vegetables are cooked to your desired tenderness, remove the skillet from heat. Let the curry sit for a few minutes to allow the flavors to meld together.

  8. Serve the Spring Vegetable Curry hot, garnished with fresh cilantro. It pairs perfectly with steamed rice or warm naan bread.

Tips and Variations

  • Feel free to customize this recipe by adding or substituting your favorite vegetables. Broccoli, bell peppers, and zucchini are all great additions to this curry.
  • For a creamier curry, you can add a few tablespoons of plain yogurt or cream towards the end of cooking.
  • If you prefer a spicier curry, increase the amount of chili powder or add a chopped green chili pepper.
  • This curry can be made in advance and refrigerated for up to 3 days. The flavors will intensify over time, making it even more delicious.

So there you have it – a flavorful and satisfying Spring Vegetable Curry recipe that celebrates the abundance of the season. With its vibrant colors, aromatic spices, and nutrient-rich vegetables, this curry is a true delight for both the palate and the senses. Give it a try and let the fresh flavors of spring transport you to a culinary paradise!
