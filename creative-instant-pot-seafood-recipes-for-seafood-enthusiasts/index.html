Creative Instant Pot Seafood Recipes for Seafood Enthusiasts


If you’re a seafood enthusiast looking for creative and delicious recipes to try in your Instant Pot, you’ve come to the right place. The Instant Pot is a versatile and convenient kitchen appliance that can help you prepare a wide variety of seafood dishes with ease. From succulent shrimp to tender fish fillets, the Instant Pot can do it all. In this blog post, we’ll explore some creative Instant Pot seafood recipes that will tantalize your taste buds and impress your family and friends.

1. Garlic Butter Shrimp Scampi

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound of large shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 4 tablespoons of unsalted butter
  • 4 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1/2 cup of chicken broth
  • 1/4 cup of white wine
  • 1 tablespoon of freshly squeezed lemon juice
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 8 ounces of linguine pasta
  • Fresh parsley for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Turn on the sauté function of your Instant Pot and melt the butter.
  2. Add the minced garlic and sauté for a minute until fragrant.
  3. Pour in the chicken broth, white wine, and lemon juice, then season with salt and pepper.
  4. Add the shrimp to the pot and stir to coat them with the garlic butter sauce.
  5. Close the lid and set the Instant Pot to manual high pressure for 1 minute.
  6. While the shrimp is cooking, prepare the linguine pasta according to the package instructions.
  7. Once the cooking cycle is complete, perform a quick pressure release and carefully open the lid.
  8. Serve the garlic butter shrimp scampi over the cooked linguine and garnish with fresh parsley.

2. Thai Coconut Seafood Soup

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound of mixed seafood (shrimp, mussels, squid, etc.)
  • 1 can of coconut milk
  • 2 cups of seafood or chicken broth
  • 2 tablespoons of red curry paste
  • 1 tablespoon of fish sauce
  • 1 tablespoon of brown sugar
  • 1 red bell pepper, thinly sliced
  • 1 cup of sliced mushrooms
  • 1 stalk of lemongrass, bruised
  • Fresh cilantro for garnish
  • Lime wedges for serving

Instructions:

  1. Add the coconut milk, seafood or chicken broth, red curry paste, fish sauce, and brown sugar to the Instant Pot.
  2. Stir the ingredients together until well combined.
  3. Add the mixed seafood, red bell pepper, mushrooms, and bruised lemongrass to the pot.
  4. Close the lid and set the Instant Pot to manual high pressure for 2 minutes.
  5. Once the cooking cycle is complete, allow for a natural pressure release for 5 minutes, then perform a quick pressure release.
  6. Discard the lemongrass stalk and stir the soup gently.
  7. Ladle the Thai coconut seafood soup into bowls and garnish with fresh cilantro.
  8. Serve with lime wedges on the side for squeezing over the soup.

3. Lemon Garlic Butter Salmon

Ingredients:

  • 4 salmon fillets
  • 4 tablespoons of unsalted butter
  • 4 cloves of garlic, minced
  • Zest of 1 lemon
  • Juice of 1 lemon
  • 1 teaspoon of dried dill
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh dill for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Turn on the sauté function of your Instant Pot and melt the butter.
  2. Add the minced garlic and sauté for a minute until fragrant.
  3. Stir in the lemon zest, lemon juice, dried dill, salt, and pepper.
  4. Place the salmon fillets in the Instant Pot and spoon the lemon garlic butter sauce over them.
  5. Close the lid and set the Instant Pot to manual high pressure for 3 minutes.
  6. Once the cooking cycle is complete, perform a quick pressure release and carefully open the lid.
  7. Transfer the salmon fillets to a serving platter and garnish with fresh dill.

These creative Instant Pot seafood recipes are sure to elevate your home cooking game and bring the flavors of the sea to your table. Whether you’re a fan of shrimp, fish, or a medley of seafood, the Instant Pot has got you covered. So, dust off your Instant Pot and get ready to impress your taste buds with these irresistible seafood dishes. Enjoy!
