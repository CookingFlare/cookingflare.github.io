Grilled Stuffed Mushrooms: Savory BBQ Appetizers


When it comes to barbecue appetizers, Grilled Stuffed Mushrooms are a flavorful and savory option that never fails to impress. These bite-sized delights are packed with a delicious combination of earthy mushrooms, savory fillings, and a smoky grilled flavor that will leave your guests coming back for more. Whether you’re hosting a backyard cookout, a family gathering, or just craving a tasty snack, these grilled stuffed mushrooms are sure to be a hit.

Ingredients:

  • 24 large mushrooms, stems removed
  • 1/2 cup breadcrumbs
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/4 cup finely chopped onion
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley
  • 1/4 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/4 cup cooked and crumbled bacon (optional)
  • BBQ sauce for brushing

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the Grill: Preheat your grill to medium heat, around 350-400°F (175-200°C).

  2. Prepare the Mushrooms: Clean the mushrooms and remove the stems. Use a spoon to gently scrape out the gills and create a cavity for the stuffing.

  3. Prepare the Filling: In a bowl, combine the breadcrumbs, Parmesan cheese, chopped onion, minced garlic, olive oil, dried oregano, salt, black pepper, parsley, mozzarella cheese, and bacon (if using). Mix well until all the ingredients are evenly combined.

  4. Stuff the Mushrooms: Using a small spoon, generously fill each mushroom cavity with the prepared filling, pressing it down gently to ensure it’s tightly packed.

  5. Grill the Mushrooms: Place the stuffed mushrooms on the preheated grill, directly over the heat. Close the grill lid and cook for 10-12 minutes, or until the mushrooms are tender and the filling is golden and crispy.

  6. Add BBQ Flavor: In the last few minutes of grilling, brush the stuffed mushrooms with your favorite BBQ sauce, allowing it to caramelize and infuse the mushrooms with a sweet and smoky flavor.

  7. Serve and Enjoy: Remove the grilled stuffed mushrooms from the grill and arrange them on a serving platter. Garnish with fresh parsley and serve hot.

Tips and Variations:

  • Vegetarian Option: Omit the bacon and add finely chopped vegetables like bell peppers or spinach to the filling for a delicious vegetarian version.
  • Cheese Lover’s Delight: Experiment with different types of cheese such as blue cheese, cheddar, or gouda to create unique flavor profiles.
  • Spicy Kick: For those who enjoy a bit of heat, add a pinch of cayenne pepper or a few dashes of hot sauce to the filling mixture.

These Grilled Stuffed Mushrooms are a versatile appetizer that pairs perfectly with a wide range of dishes, from grilled meats to crisp salads. The combination of tender mushrooms, flavorful fillings, and a hint of smokiness from the grill creates a memorable appetizer that is sure to become a favorite at your gatherings. So fire up the grill, gather your ingredients, and treat your taste buds to these delightful savory BBQ appetizers.
