Tart and Tangy Homemade Cranberry Relish Recipe


Looking for a delicious and tangy accompaniment to elevate your holiday meals? Look no further! This homemade cranberry relish recipe is bursting with flavor and will leave your taste buds tingling with delight.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound fresh cranberries
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 orange, zest and juice
  • 1 lemon, zest and juice
  • 1/2 cup chopped walnuts
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon

Instructions

  1. Start by rinsing the cranberries thoroughly under cold water. Discard any damaged or bruised berries.

  2. In a medium-sized saucepan, combine the cranberries, sugar, orange zest, orange juice, lemon zest, and lemon juice. Stir well to ensure that the sugar is evenly distributed.

  3. Place the saucepan over medium heat and bring the mixture to a gentle boil. Reduce the heat to low and let the cranberries simmer for about 10 minutes, or until they start to burst and become soft.

  4. Remove the saucepan from the heat and let the cranberry mixture cool for a few minutes. It’s important to allow the relish to cool slightly before adding the remaining ingredients.

  5. Once the cranberry mixture has cooled, stir in the chopped walnuts and ground cinnamon. These additional ingredients will add a delightful crunch and depth of flavor to the relish.

  6. Transfer the relish to a glass jar or airtight container and refrigerate for at least 2 hours, or overnight if possible. This will allow the flavors to meld together and intensify.

  7. When you’re ready to serve, give the cranberry relish a quick stir to ensure the ingredients are evenly distributed. Spoon it into a serving bowl and garnish with a sprig of fresh mint or a sprinkle of orange zest for an extra pop of color and freshness.

Serving Suggestions

This tart and tangy homemade cranberry relish is a versatile condiment that pairs well with a variety of dishes. Here are a few serving suggestions to get you started:

  • Turkey: Serve a dollop of the cranberry relish alongside your Thanksgiving turkey for a burst of flavor that complements the savory meat perfectly.

  • Sandwiches: Spread a generous layer of the relish on your favorite turkey or chicken sandwich for a tangy twist. The sweetness of the cranberries will balance out the savory elements of the sandwich.

  • Cheese Platter: Add a spoonful of the cranberry relish to your cheese platter for a delightful contrast of flavors. It pairs particularly well with sharp cheddar or creamy brie.

  • Roasted Vegetables: Drizzle some of the relish over roasted vegetables, such as Brussels sprouts or sweet potatoes, to add a touch of sweetness and acidity to the dish.

Conclusion

There you have it – a simple and delicious homemade cranberry relish recipe that will elevate your holiday meals and leave your guests asking for more. The tartness of the cranberries, combined with the sweetness of the sugar and the zesty citrus flavors, creates a relish that is bursting with bright and tangy notes. The addition of walnuts and cinnamon adds a delightful crunch and warmth. So, why settle for store-bought cranberry sauce when you can easily whip up this homemade relish in no time? Give it a try and let your taste buds experience the magic of homemade goodness.
