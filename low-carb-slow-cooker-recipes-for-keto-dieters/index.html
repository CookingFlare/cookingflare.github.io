Low-Carb Slow Cooker Recipes for Keto Dieters


The slow cooker is a lifesaver for busy individuals who want to enjoy a healthy, home-cooked meal without too much fuss in the kitchen. For keto dieters, it's essential to have a reliable and versatile slow cooker recipe collection that caters to their specific dietary needs. In this post, we'll share an array of mouth-watering and nutritious low-carb slow cooker recipes perfect for those following a keto diet. Slow Cooker Zucchini Boats Are you tired of the same old boring slow cooker recipes? Spice up your menu with this simple yet elegant recipe. The idea of using zucchinis as boats for your favorite filling is pure genius. By using the freshest zucchinis and a low-carb tortilla wrapper as a base, you'll have a satisfying meal without sacrificing flavor. Ingredients:

4 medium zucchinis, hollowed and cut into boats 1 pound ground beef or ground turkey 1/4 cup almond flour 1 teaspoon cumin 1/2 teaspoon paprika Salt and pepper to taste 2 tablespoons olive oil 1/4 cup shredded cheddar cheese (6g net carbs)

Instructions:

Preheat the slow cooker to low heat. In a large skillet, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the ground beef or ground turkey and cook until browned. Add the almond flour, cumin, paprika, salt, and pepper to the skillet. Stir well. Stuff each zucchini boat with the beef or turkey mixture and top with shredded cheddar cheese. Transfer the stuffed zucchinis to the slow cooker and cover with a lid. Cook for 4-5 hours or until the zucchinis are tender and the cheese is melted. Serve hot, garnished with chopped fresh cilantro or parsley.

Nutritional information (approximate):

Calories: 350 Protein: 35g Carbohydrates: 10g (5g net carbs) Fat: 25g

Delightful Low-Carb Beef Stew When it's chilly outside, there's nothing like a warm, comforting bowl of beef stew to cozy up with. This recipe uses tender beef shanks and a medley of vegetables, all simmered to perfection in a flavorful broth. Plus, by avoiding potatoes and sugar-laden ingredients, we transform a classic dish into a keto-friendly masterpiece. Ingredients:

2 pounds beef shanks (6-7 inches long) 2 tablespoons olive oil 1 large onion, chopped (3g net carbs) 3 cloves garlic, minced (1g net carbs) 2 carrots, sliced (6g net carbs) 2 celery stalks, sliced (2g net carbs) 1 large bunch of fresh parsley, chopped (1g net carbs) 1 cup beef broth (1g net carbs) 1 teaspoon dried thyme 1/2 teaspoon dried oregano Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

Season the beef shanks with salt and pepper. In a large skillet, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Sear the beef shanks until browned on all sides. Add the onion, garlic, carrots, celery, and half of the parsley to the skillet. Cook until the vegetables are tender-crisp. Transfer the cooked vegetables to the slow cooker and add the beef shanks, beef broth, thyme, oregano, and remaining parsley. Cook for 10-12 hours on low heat or until the meat is tender and easily falls off the bone. Taste and adjust seasoning as needed. Serve hot, garnished with chopped fresh parsley and a side of roasted vegetables.

Nutritional information (approximate):

Calories: 500 Protein: 45g Carbohydrates: 20g (10g net carbs) Fat: 35g

Low-Carb Creamy Broccoli Soup Cold weather calls for a rich and comforting bowl of creamy soup. But what if you had to give up traditional potatoes or cornstarch thickeners? Don't worry, we've got a simple solution for you. This low-carb broccoli soup combines steamed broccoli with heavy cream and finished with a dash of nutmeg â€“ guaranteed to satisfy your cravings. Ingredients:

1 large head of broccoli, stems and florets (2g net carbs) 2 tablespoons unsalted butter 1 medium onion, chopped (3g net carbs) 3 cloves garlic, minced (1g net carbs) 1/2 cup heavy cream 1/2 cup shredded cheddar cheese (6g net carbs) 1/4 teaspoon salt 1/4 teaspoon black pepper 1/2 teaspoon ground nutmeg Fresh parsley or chives for garnish

Instructions:

In a steamer, cook the broccoli until tender. In a large skillet, melt the butter over medium heat. Add the onion and garlic, and cook until the onion is translucent. In the slow cooker, combine the cooked broccoli, onion mixture, heavy cream, cheddar cheese, salt, pepper, and nutmeg. Stir well. Cook for 30-minute to 1 hour or until heated through. Taste and adjust seasoning as needed. Serve hot, garnished with chopped fresh parsley or chives.

Nutritional information (approximate):

Calories: 400 Protein: 15g Carbohydrates: 15g (8g net carbs) Fat: 30g

Additional Tips and Considerations:

Always check the ingredient labels for added sugars, artificial flavorings, or high-carb fillers that might deviate from your keto diet. Choose grass-fed beef, pasture-raised chicken, and wild-caught fish for optimal nutrient content. To maintain healthy ketone levels, keep portion sizes moderate and avoid refined oils or added sugars. Weigh your food accurately to track your macronutrient intake, and experiment with various spices to elevate the flavor profiles.

By incorporating these mouth-watering low-carb slow cooker recipes into your weekly meal plan, you'll enjoy a boost of nutrition and flavor while maintaining a keto lifestyle. Whether you're a seasoned low-carber or just starting out, this guide provides a stress-free way to indulge in wholesome, satisfying meals that nourish both body and soul.
