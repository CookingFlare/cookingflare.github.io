Pasta with Creamy Pistachio Pesto: A Nutty and Flavorful Pasta Dish


Pasta is a beloved staple in many kitchens around the world, known for its versatility and ability to absorb flavors. One of the most delightful ways to elevate simple pasta is by adding a unique and creamy pesto sauce. In this post, we will explore a delicious recipe for Pasta with Creamy Pistachio Pesto, a dish that combines the rich nuttiness of pistachios with fresh herbs and a touch of cream. Perfect for a weeknight dinner or a special occasion, this pasta dish is sure to impress your family and friends.

The Unforgettable Flavor of Pistachio Pesto

Traditional pesto is usually made with basil, garlic, pine nuts, olive oil, and Parmesan cheese. While delicious, it’s always exciting to mix things up and try new flavor combinations. Enter pistachio pesto! This variation not only adds a unique twist to the classic recipe but also provides a delightful creaminess that pairs beautifully with pasta.

Pistachios are packed with healthy fats, protein, and antioxidants, making them a nutritious choice for our pesto. Plus, their vibrant green color adds a visually appealing touch to the dish. When blended with fresh herbs, olive oil, and cream, pistachios create a luscious sauce that clings perfectly to the pasta, resulting in a dish that is both flavorful and satisfying.

Ingredients You’ll Need

To create this creamy pistachio pesto pasta, gather the following ingredients:

For the Pistachio Pesto:

  • 1 cup roasted unsalted pistachios (shelled)
  • 2 cups fresh basil leaves, packed
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/2 cup extra virgin olive oil
  • 1/4 cup heavy cream
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • Zest of 1 lemon

For the Pasta:

  • 12 ounces of your favorite pasta (spaghetti, fettuccine, or penne work well)
  • Salt, for pasta water
  • Additional grated Parmesan cheese and crushed pistachios for garnish
  • Fresh basil leaves for garnish

Step-by-Step Instructions

1. Preparing the Pistachio Pesto

To start, we need to make the creamy pistachio pesto. Here’s how to do it:

  1. Blend the Ingredients: In a food processor, combine the roasted pistachios, fresh basil leaves, minced garlic, and grated Parmesan cheese. Pulse until the mixture is coarsely chopped.

  2. Add the Olive Oil: While the food processor is running, slowly drizzle in the extra virgin olive oil until the mixture becomes a smooth paste. You may need to scrape down the sides of the bowl to ensure everything is well combined.

  3. Add Cream and Seasoning: Add the heavy cream and lemon zest to the pesto mixture. Pulse a few more times until well blended. Taste and season with salt and pepper as needed.

  4. Set Aside: Once your pesto is ready, set it aside while you cook the pasta.

2. Cooking the Pasta

Now that we have our creamy pistachio pesto ready, it’s time to prepare the pasta.

  1. Boil Water: In a large pot, bring salted water to a boil. Use about 1 tablespoon of salt for every 4 quarts of water.

  2. Cook the Pasta: Add the pasta to the boiling water and cook according to the package instructions until al dente. Stir occasionally to prevent sticking.

  3. Reserve Pasta Water: Before draining the pasta, reserve about 1 cup of the starchy pasta water. This will be useful for adjusting the consistency of the sauce later.

  4. Drain the Pasta: Drain the pasta in a colander, and do not rinse it. The starch on the pasta helps the sauce adhere better.

3. Combining Pasta and Pesto

Now comes the exciting part—combining the pasta with the creamy pistachio pesto!

  1. Mix the Pasta and Pesto: In the same pot you used to cook the pasta, add the drained pasta back in. Pour the creamy pistachio pesto over the pasta and gently toss everything together until the pasta is well coated.

  2. Adjust the Consistency: If the sauce seems too thick, gradually add some reserved pasta water, a tablespoon at a time, until you reach your desired consistency. The starchy water helps to create a silky sauce that clings to the pasta perfectly.

  3. Taste and Adjust: Taste the pasta and adjust the seasoning with more salt, pepper, or lemon juice if desired.

4. Serving the Pasta

To serve your creamy pistachio pesto pasta:

  1. Plate It Up: Divide the pasta among serving plates or bowls.

  2. Garnish: Top each serving with additional grated Parmesan cheese, crushed pistachios, and fresh basil leaves for a pop of color and flavor.

  3. Enjoy: Serve immediately and enjoy the nutty, creamy goodness of this unique pasta dish!

Tips for Perfect Pasta with Creamy Pistachio Pesto

  • Use High-Quality Ingredients: The flavor of your pesto will depend significantly on the quality of the ingredients you use. Opt for fresh basil, high-quality olive oil, and good Parmesan cheese for the best results.

  • Experiment with Herbs: While basil is traditional, feel free to experiment with other herbs like parsley, arugula, or spinach for a different flavor profile.

  • Make It Vegan: To make this dish vegan, substitute the heavy cream with coconut cream or a plant-based cream alternative, and use nutritional yeast instead of Parmesan cheese.

  • Add Protein: For a heartier meal, consider adding grilled chicken, shrimp, or roasted vegetables to the pasta.

  • Store Leftovers: Store any leftovers in an airtight container in the refrigerator for up to 3 days. Reheat gently on the stovetop, adding a splash of water or broth to loosen the sauce.

Conclusion

Pasta with Creamy Pistachio Pesto is a delightful dish that is both comforting and gourmet. Its creamy texture and unique nutty flavor make it a fantastic option for any pasta lover looking to try something new. Whether you’re cooking for yourself or entertaining guests, this pasta dish is sure to be a hit. So gather your ingredients, follow the steps, and indulge in a bowl of this delicious creamy pistachio pesto pasta today!
