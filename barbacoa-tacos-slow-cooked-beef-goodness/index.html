Barbacoa Tacos: Slow-Cooked Beef Goodness


Taco lovers, rejoice! Today, I am going to share with you an absolutely mouthwatering recipe for Barbacoa Tacos. These tacos are filled with tender, flavorful, slow-cooked beef that will have your taste buds dancing with delight. So, roll up your sleeves, put on your apron, and let’s get cooking!

Ingredients

For the Barbacoa:

  • 2 pounds beef chuck roast
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 chipotle peppers in adobo sauce, finely chopped
  • 1 tablespoon ground cumin
  • 1 tablespoon dried oregano
  • 2 teaspoons smoked paprika
  • 1 teaspoon ground coriander
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1/4 cup apple cider vinegar
  • 1/4 cup lime juice
  • 1/4 cup beef broth

For the Tacos:

  • Soft corn tortillas
  • Fresh cilantro, chopped
  • Diced onions
  • Lime wedges
  • Salsa verde

Instructions

  1. Start by preparing the Barbacoa marinade. In a bowl, mix together the minced garlic, chopped onion, chipotle peppers, ground cumin, dried oregano, smoked paprika, ground coriander, salt, black pepper, apple cider vinegar, lime juice, and beef broth. Set aside.

  2. Trim any excess fat from the beef chuck roast and place it in a slow cooker. Pour the marinade over the beef, making sure it is evenly coated. Cover the slow cooker and let the beef marinate for at least 2 hours, or overnight for the best flavor.

  3. Once the beef has marinated, set the slow cooker to low heat and cook for 8-10 hours, or until the beef is tender and easily shreds with a fork. The slow cooking process will infuse the meat with all the delicious flavors of the marinade.

  4. Once the beef is cooked, remove it from the slow cooker and transfer it to a cutting board. Use two forks to shred the beef into bite-sized pieces. Return the shredded beef to the slow cooker and mix it with the remaining juices.

  5. Now it’s time to assemble the tacos. Warm the soft corn tortillas in a dry skillet or on a griddle until soft and pliable. Place a generous amount of the Barbacoa beef onto each tortilla.

  6. Top the Barbacoa with fresh cilantro, diced onions, and a squeeze of lime juice. For an extra kick, drizzle some salsa verde over the top.

  7. Serve the Barbacoa Tacos immediately and enjoy the explosion of flavors in each bite. These tacos are perfect for any occasion, whether it’s a casual weeknight dinner or a festive gathering with friends and family.

Conclusion

There you have it, my friends. A recipe for Barbacoa Tacos that will leave you craving for more. The slow-cooked beef, infused with aromatic spices, creates a taste sensation that is simply unbeatable. So, gather your ingredients, fire up your slow cooker, and get ready to indulge in the goodness of these mouthwatering tacos. Trust me, once you try them, you’ll be hooked!

Remember, cooking is all about experimenting and adding your own personal touch. Feel free to customize this recipe to suit your taste preferences. Whether you prefer to add some sliced avocado or a dollop of sour cream, these Barbacoa Tacos will always be a crowd-pleaser. So, go ahead and unleash your culinary creativity!

Happy cooking and enjoy your Barbacoa Tacos!
