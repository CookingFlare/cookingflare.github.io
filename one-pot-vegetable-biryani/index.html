One Pot Vegetable Biryani


If you’re looking for a flavorful, aromatic, and easy-to-make dish that’s perfect for a family dinner or a casual get-together, then One Pot Vegetable Biryani is the answer. This classic Indian dish is a medley of fragrant basmati rice, colorful vegetables, and a blend of spices that will tantalize your taste buds. The best part? It all comes together in just one pot, making clean-up a breeze. Let’s dive into the recipe and get ready to savor the delightful flavors of Vegetable Biryani.

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups basmati rice, soaked for 30 minutes and drained
  • 2 tablespoons ghee or vegetable oil
  • 1 large onion, thinly sliced
  • 2 tomatoes, finely chopped
  • 1 cup mixed vegetables (carrots, peas, beans, cauliflower)
  • 1/2 cup plain yogurt
  • 1 teaspoon ginger-garlic paste
  • Whole spices (1 bay leaf, 2-3 cloves, 1-inch cinnamon stick, 2-3 green cardamom pods)
  • 1 teaspoon red chili powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 teaspoon biryani masala
  • Salt to taste
  • 3 cups water
  • Fresh cilantro leaves for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Heat the ghee or oil in a large, heavy-bottomed pot over medium heat. Add the whole spices and sauté until they release their aroma.

  2. Add the sliced onions and sauté until they turn golden brown. This will add a rich flavor to the biryani.

  3. Stir in the ginger-garlic paste and sauté for a minute until the raw aroma disappears.

  4. Add the chopped tomatoes and cook until they turn soft and the oil starts to separate.

  5. Now, it’s time to add the mixed vegetables and sauté for a few minutes until they are slightly tender.

  6. In a small bowl, whisk the plain yogurt with red chili powder, turmeric powder, biryani masala, and salt. Add this mixture to the pot and stir well to coat the vegetables.

  7. Gently fold in the soaked and drained basmati rice, ensuring that it’s well coated with the flavorful mixture.

  8. Pour in the water and give everything a gentle stir. Bring the mixture to a boil.

  9. Once the water starts boiling, reduce the heat to low, cover the pot with a tight-fitting lid, and let the biryani simmer for about 15-20 minutes. Avoid the temptation to uncover the pot during this time to allow the rice to cook perfectly.

  10. After 15-20 minutes, turn off the heat and let the biryani rest for another 10 minutes. This step allows the flavors to meld together beautifully.

  11. Finally, gently fluff the biryani with a fork, garnish with fresh cilantro leaves, and serve hot.

Serving Suggestion:

One Pot Vegetable Biryani pairs wonderfully with a cooling cucumber raita and a side of spicy mint chutney. For a complete meal, serve it alongside papadums or crispy fried onions for added crunch.

Conclusion:

One Pot Vegetable Biryani is a wholesome and satisfying dish that brings together the goodness of vegetables and aromatic spices in one delightful package. Whether you’re a seasoned cook or just starting your culinary journey, this recipe is sure to impress with its simplicity and bold flavors. So, gather your ingredients, grab your favorite pot, and embark on a culinary adventure with this delectable Vegetable Biryani recipe. Enjoy!
