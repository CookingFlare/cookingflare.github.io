Pumpkin Spice Cupcakes with Cream Cheese Frosting: A Fall Indulgence


Fall is the perfect time to indulge in the warm and comforting flavors of pumpkin spice. And what better way to satisfy your cravings than with delicious Pumpkin Spice Cupcakes with Cream Cheese Frosting? These cupcakes are moist, flavorful, and topped with a creamy and tangy cream cheese frosting that perfectly complements the rich pumpkin spice flavor.

Ingredients

For the cupcakes:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground nutmeg
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground cloves
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground ginger
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/2 cup packed brown sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 cup pumpkin puree
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

For the cream cheese frosting:

  • 8 ounces cream cheese, softened
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 4 cups powdered sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C). Line a muffin tin with cupcake liners and set aside.

  2. In a medium bowl, whisk together the flour, baking powder, baking soda, salt, cinnamon, nutmeg, cloves, and ginger. Set aside.

  3. In a large mixing bowl, cream together the softened butter, granulated sugar, and brown sugar until light and fluffy.

  4. Add the eggs one at a time, beating well after each addition. Then, mix in the pumpkin puree, milk, and vanilla extract until well combined.

  5. Gradually add the dry ingredients to the wet ingredients, mixing until just combined. Be careful not to overmix, as this can result in dense cupcakes.

  6. Divide the batter evenly among the prepared cupcake liners, filling each about 2/3 full.

  7. Bake for 18-20 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center of a cupcake comes out clean.

  8. Remove the cupcakes from the oven and let them cool in the tin for 5 minutes, then transfer them to a wire rack to cool completely.

  9. While the cupcakes are cooling, prepare the cream cheese frosting. In a large bowl, beat the softened cream cheese and butter together until creamy and smooth.

  10. Gradually add the powdered sugar, one cup at a time, beating well after each addition. Finally, mix in the vanilla extract until well combined.

  11. Once the cupcakes have cooled, generously frost each cupcake with the cream cheese frosting. You can use a piping bag fitted with a decorative tip for a more professional look, or simply spread the frosting with a knife.

  12. Serve and enjoy! These Pumpkin Spice Cupcakes with Cream Cheese Frosting are perfect for any fall occasion, from cozy gatherings to Thanksgiving celebrations.

Conclusion

Pumpkin Spice Cupcakes with Cream Cheese Frosting are the ultimate fall indulgence. The warm and comforting flavors of pumpkin spice combined with the creamy and tangy cream cheese frosting create a dessert that is simply irresistible. Whether you’re hosting a fall-themed party or simply craving a delicious treat, these cupcakes will not disappoint. So grab your apron, preheat your oven, and get ready to indulge in the flavors of the season with these delectable Pumpkin Spice Cupcakes with Cream Cheese Frosting. Happy baking!
