Spicy Instant Pot Thai Basil Chicken Recipes

If you’re a fan of spicy and flavorful dishes, then you’re in for a treat with these Spicy Instant Pot Thai Basil Chicken recipes. Bursting with the bold flavors of Thai cuisine, these recipes are quick, easy, and perfect for busy weeknight dinners or weekend gatherings. Get ready to tantalize your taste buds with these mouthwatering creations! Recipe 1: Spicy Instant Pot Thai Basil Chicken Stir-Fry Ingredients: 1 pound boneless, skinless chicken breasts, cut into bite-sized pieces 2 tablespoons vegetable oil 3 cloves garlic, minced 1 small onion, thinly sliced 1 bell pepper, thinly sliced 1 cup fresh basil leaves 2 tablespoons soy sauce 1 tablespoon fish sauce 1 tablespoon oyster sauce 1 teaspoon sugar 1 teaspoon red pepper flakes (adjust to taste) Salt and pepper, to taste Instructions: Set your Instant Pot to sauté mode and heat the vegetable oil. Add the minced garlic and sauté for about 1 minute until fragrant. Add the chicken pieces and cook until they are browned on all sides. Stir in the onion, bell pepper, soy sauce, fish sauce, oyster sauce, sugar, and red pepper flakes. Close the lid and set the Instant Pot to manual high pressure for 5 minutes. Once the cooking time is complete, carefully release the pressure using the quick release method. Open the lid and stir in the fresh basil leaves. Let it sit for a few minutes to allow the flavors to meld together. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Serve the spicy Thai basil chicken stir-fry over steamed rice or noodles. Enjoy! Recipe 2: Spicy Instant Pot Thai Basil Chicken Curry Ingredients: 1 pound boneless, skinless chicken thighs, cut into bite-sized pieces 2 tablespoons red curry paste 1 can (14 ounces) coconut milk 1 cup chicken broth 1 bell pepper, thinly sliced 1 zucchini, sliced 1 cup bamboo shoots 1 cup fresh basil leaves 1 tablespoon fish sauce 1 tablespoon brown sugar Juice of 1 lime Salt and pepper, to taste Instructions: Set your Instant Pot to sauté mode and add the red curry paste. Cook for a minute, stirring constantly. Add the chicken pieces and cook until they are browned on all sides. Pour in the coconut milk and chicken broth. Stir well to combine. Add the bell pepper, zucchini, bamboo shoots, fish sauce, and brown sugar. Stir again. Close the lid and set the Instant Pot to manual high pressure for 8 minutes. Once the cooking time is complete, allow the pressure to release naturally for 5 minutes, then use the quick release method. Open the lid and stir in the fresh basil leaves and lime juice. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Serve the spicy Thai basil chicken curry over steamed jasmine rice. Enjoy! Recipe 3: Spicy Instant Pot Thai Basil Chicken Soup Ingredients: 1 pound boneless, skinless chicken breasts, cut into bite-sized pieces 4 cups chicken broth 1 can (14 ounces) coconut milk 1 bell pepper, thinly sliced 1 carrot, sliced 1 cup sliced mushrooms 1 tablespoon red curry paste 2 tablespoons fish sauce 1 tablespoon brown sugar Juice of 1 lime 1 cup fresh basil leaves Salt and pepper, to taste Instructions: Set your Instant Pot to sauté mode and heat some oil. Add the chicken pieces and cook until they are browned on all sides. Stir in the red curry paste and cook for another minute. Pour in the chicken broth and coconut milk. Stir well to combine. Add the bell pepper, carrot, mushrooms, fish sauce, and brown sugar. Stir again. Close the lid and set the Instant Pot to manual high pressure for 6 minutes. Once the cooking time is complete, carefully release the pressure using the quick release method. Open the lid and stir in the lime juice and fresh basil leaves. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Ladle the spicy Thai basil chicken soup into bowls and serve hot. Enjoy! These Spicy Instant Pot Thai Basil Chicken recipes are sure to become your new favorites in no time. With their bold flavors and the convenience of using an Instant Pot, they are perfect for satisfying your cravings for Thai cuisine. Mouthwatering Instant Pot Beef and Broccoli Stir-Fry Recipes

Are you craving a delicious and satisfying meal that is quick and easy to make? Look no further than these mouthwatering Instant Pot Beef and Broccoli Stir-Fry recipes. With the convenience of the Instant Pot, you can have a flavorful and nutritious meal on the table in no time. So, grab your apron and let’s get cooking! Recipe 1: Classic Beef and Broccoli Stir-Fry Ingredients: 1 pound beef sirloin, thinly sliced 2 cups broccoli florets 1/2 cup low-sodium soy sauce 1/4 cup brown sugar 2 cloves garlic, minced 1 teaspoon fresh ginger, grated 1 tablespoon cornstarch 1/4 cup water 2 tablespoons vegetable oil Salt and pepper, to taste Steamed rice, for serving Instructions: In a small bowl, whisk together the soy sauce, brown sugar, garlic, ginger, cornstarch, and water. Set aside. Season the beef slices with salt and pepper. Set your Instant Pot to the sauté function and heat the vegetable oil. Add the beef slices to the Instant Pot and cook until browned on all sides. Remove the beef and set aside. Add the broccoli florets to the Instant Pot and sauté for 2-3 minutes, until slightly tender. Return the beef to the Instant Pot and pour in the soy sauce mixture. Secure the lid on the Instant Pot and set the vent to the sealing position. Cook on high pressure for 5 minutes, then do a quick release of the pressure. Serve the beef and broccoli stir-fry over steamed rice and enjoy! Recipe 2: Spicy Sesame Beef and Broccoli Stir-Fry Ingredients: 1 pound beef flank steak, thinly sliced 2 cups broccoli florets 1/4 cup low-sodium soy sauce 2 tablespoons hoisin sauce 1 tablespoon sesame oil 1 tablespoon Sriracha sauce 2 cloves garlic, minced 1 tablespoon cornstarch 1/4 cup water 2 tablespoons vegetable oil Salt and pepper, to taste Sesame seeds, for garnish Steamed rice, for serving Instructions: In a small bowl, whisk together the soy sauce, hoisin sauce, sesame oil, Sriracha sauce, garlic, cornstarch, and water. Set aside. Season the beef slices with salt and pepper. Set your Instant Pot to the sauté function and heat the vegetable oil. Add the beef slices to the Instant Pot and cook until browned on all sides. Remove the beef and set aside. Add the broccoli florets to the Instant Pot and sauté for 2-3 minutes, until slightly tender. Return the beef to the Instant Pot and pour in the sauce mixture. Secure the lid on the Instant Pot and set the vent to the sealing position. Cook on high pressure for 5 minutes, then do a quick release of the pressure. Serve the spicy sesame beef and broccoli stir-fry over steamed rice, garnish with sesame seeds, and enjoy! Recipe 3: Teriyaki Beef and Broccoli Stir-Fry Ingredients: 1 pound beef tenderloin, thinly sliced 2 cups broccoli florets 1/2 cup teriyaki sauce 2 tablespoons honey 2 cloves garlic, minced 1 tablespoon cornstarch 1/4 cup water 2 tablespoons vegetable oil Salt and pepper, to taste Green onions, sliced, for garnish Steamed rice, for serving Instructions: In a small bowl, whisk together the teriyaki sauce, honey, garlic, cornstarch, and water. Set aside. Season the beef slices with salt and pepper. Set your Instant Pot to the sauté function and heat the vegetable oil. Secure the lid on the Instant Pot and set the vent to the sealing position. Cook on high pressure for 5 minutes, then do a quick release of the pressure. Serve the teriyaki beef and broccoli stir-fry over steamed rice, garnish with sliced green onions, and enjoy! These Instant Pot Beef and Broccoli Stir-Fry recipes are perfect for busy weeknights when you want a flavorful and nutritious meal without spending hours in the kitchen. Delicious Instant Pot Chicken Enchiladas Recipe for Southwestern Flavor

Looking for a delicious and easy dinner recipe that will transport you to the flavors of the Southwest? Look no further than this mouthwatering Instant Pot Chicken Enchiladas recipe. Packed with bold flavors and cooked to perfection in your trusty Instant Pot, these enchiladas will have your taste buds dancing with joy. So, put on your apron and let’s get cooking! Ingredients 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts 1 tablespoon olive oil 1 small onion, diced 2 cloves garlic, minced 1 teaspoon ground cumin 1 teaspoon chili powder 1/2 teaspoon dried oregano 1/2 teaspoon salt 1/4 teaspoon black pepper 1 cup chicken broth 1 can (10 ounces) red enchilada sauce 1 can (4 ounces) diced green chilies 8 flour tortillas 2 cups shredded Mexican blend cheese Optional toppings: diced tomatoes, sliced avocado, chopped cilantro, sour cream Instructions Start by setting your Instant Pot to the “Saute” function. Add the olive oil and heat it up. Add the diced onion and minced garlic to the pot and saute until the onion is translucent and fragrant. In a small bowl, combine the ground cumin, chili powder, dried oregano, salt, and black pepper. Mix well. Season the chicken breasts with half of the spice mixture, making sure to coat both sides. Add the chicken breasts to the Instant Pot and cook for 2-3 minutes on each side, or until they are browned. Pour the chicken broth, red enchilada sauce, and diced green chilies into the pot, along with the remaining spice mixture. Stir well to combine. Secure the lid on the Instant Pot and set the valve to the sealing position. Select the “Manual” or “Pressure Cook” function and set the timer for 10 minutes. Once the cooking time is complete, allow for a natural pressure release for 5 minutes, then carefully perform a quick release to release any remaining pressure. Remove the chicken breasts from the pot and shred them using two forks. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C). Take a tortilla and spoon some of the enchilada sauce mixture onto it. Add a portion of the shredded chicken and a sprinkle of shredded cheese. Roll the tortilla tightly and place it seam-side down in a baking dish. Repeat with the remaining tortillas. Pour any remaining enchilada sauce over the rolled tortillas and sprinkle with the remaining shredded cheese. Cover the baking dish with aluminum foil and bake in the preheated oven for 20 minutes. After 20 minutes, remove the foil and bake for an additional 5-10 minutes, or until the cheese is melted and bubbly. Remove the enchiladas from the oven and let them cool for a few minutes before serving. Serve the Instant Pot Chicken Enchiladas with your favorite toppings, such as diced tomatoes, sliced avocado, chopped cilantro, and a dollop of sour cream. Enjoy the Flavors of the Southwest There you have it, a delicious Instant Pot Chicken Enchiladas recipe that is sure to satisfy your cravings for Southwestern flavors. With its tender shredded chicken, flavorful sauce, and gooey melted cheese, these enchiladas are a crowd-pleaser that will have everyone coming back for seconds. So, fire up your Instant Pot and get ready to indulge in this irresistible dish. Happy cooking! ...

Instant Pot Chicken and Spinach Stuffed Peppers Recipe: A Creative and Delicious Option

As the weather starts to cool down, our taste buds crave warm and comforting dishes that can be easily prepared at home. One such recipe that fits the bill perfectly is Instant Pot Chicken and Spinach Stuffed Peppers. This creative and delicious option is a game-changer for those looking for a healthy and satisfying meal that can be prepared in no time. Ingredients 4 large bell peppers, any color 1 pound boneless, skinless chicken breast or thighs, cut into small pieces 2 cloves garlic, minced 1 cup fresh spinach leaves 1 cup cooked white rice 1 can (14.5 oz) diced tomatoes 1 teaspoon paprika 1/2 teaspoon salt 1/4 teaspoon black pepper 1/4 teaspoon cayenne pepper (optional) 2 tablespoons olive oil 1/4 cup shredded cheddar cheese (optional) Instructions Step 1: Prepare the Peppers Rinse the bell peppers and cut off the tops. Remove the seeds and membranes, and place them in the Instant Pot. Add 1 cup of water to the Instant Pot, and close the lid. Make sure the valve is set to “sealing”. Press the “manual” or “pressure cook” button and set the cooking time to 5 minutes at high pressure. Step 2: Cook the Chicken and Spinach In a large skillet, heat the olive oil over medium-high heat. Add the chicken and cook until browned, about 5-7 minutes. Add the garlic, spinach, paprika, salt, black pepper, and cayenne pepper (if using) to the skillet. Cook until the spinach is wilted. Stir in the cooked rice, diced tomatoes, and chicken broth. Bring the mixture to a simmer. Step 3: Assemble the Stuffed Peppers Once the peppers are cooked, remove them from the Instant Pot and set them aside. Add the cooked chicken and spinach mixture to the peppers, dividing it evenly among the four peppers. Top each pepper with shredded cheddar cheese (if using). Step 4: Finish Cooking the Stuffed Peppers Close the Instant Pot and set the valve to “sealing”. Press the “manual” or “pressure cook” button and set the cooking time to 5 minutes at high pressure. Let the pressure release naturally for 10 minutes, then quick-release any remaining pressure. Serve the stuffed peppers hot, garnished with chopped fresh parsley or cilantro (if desired). Tips and Variations Use any combination of bell pepper colors you like, or a mix of colors for a festive look. Substitute ground turkey or beef for the chicken if you prefer. Add some heat to the dish by using hot sauce or red pepper flakes. Serve the stuffed peppers with a side of steamed vegetables or a green salad for a well-rounded meal. Conclusion Instant Pot Chicken and Spinach Stuffed Peppers is a creative and delicious recipe that is perfect for a weeknight dinner or a special occasion.
