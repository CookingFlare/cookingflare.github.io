Delightful Keto Blueberry Muffins: A Guilt-Free Treat


If you’re on a keto diet or looking to reduce your carb intake, you might think that delicious baked goods are off the menu. However, with these delightful keto blueberry muffins, you can indulge in a guilt-free treat that’s low in carbs and high in flavor.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups almond flour
  • 1/3 cup erythritol or sweetener of your choice
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 3 large eggs
  • 1/2 cup unsweetened almond milk
  • 1/4 cup melted coconut oil
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 cup fresh blueberries

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C) and line a muffin tin with paper liners.

  2. In a large bowl, whisk together the almond flour, erythritol, baking powder, and salt.

  3. In a separate bowl, beat the eggs, then stir in the almond milk, melted coconut oil, and vanilla extract.

  4. Add the wet ingredients to the dry ingredients and stir until just combined.

  5. Gently fold in the fresh blueberries, being careful not to overmix.

  6. Divide the batter evenly among the muffin cups, filling each about 2/3 full.

  7. Bake for 25-30 minutes, or until the muffins are golden brown and a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.

  8. Allow the muffins to cool in the pan for a few minutes, then transfer to a wire rack to cool completely.

Tips for Success

  • Be sure to use fresh blueberries for the best flavor and texture.
  • If you prefer a sweeter muffin, you can increase the amount of sweetener to suit your taste.
  • These muffins can be stored in an airtight container at room temperature for up to 3 days, or in the refrigerator for up to a week.

Nutritional Information

  • Serving Size: 1 muffin
  • Calories: 180
  • Total Fat: 15g
  • Saturated Fat: 6g
  • Cholesterol: 62mg
  • Sodium: 110mg
  • Total Carbohydrates: 7g
  • Dietary Fiber: 3g
  • Sugars: 2g
  • Protein: 6g

Why Choose Keto Blueberry Muffins?

These keto blueberry muffins are not only delicious but also offer a range of health benefits. Almond flour, the primary ingredient, is a good source of protein, healthy fats, and fiber. The use of erythritol as a sweetener keeps the muffins low in carbs and suitable for a keto lifestyle. The addition of fresh blueberries provides a burst of antioxidants and natural sweetness without spiking your blood sugar levels.

By incorporating these keto blueberry muffins into your diet, you can satisfy your cravings for baked goods while staying true to your low-carb, keto goals. Whether enjoyed as a quick breakfast, a satisfying snack, or a delightful dessert, these muffins are sure to become a staple in your keto recipe collection.

Final Thoughts

Delightful keto blueberry muffins are a guilt-free treat that proves you don’t have to sacrifice flavor and enjoyment when following a keto lifestyle. With simple ingredients and easy preparation, you can whip up a batch of these muffins to enjoy throughout the week. So go ahead, indulge in a delicious and satisfying baked good that aligns perfectly with your keto journey.
