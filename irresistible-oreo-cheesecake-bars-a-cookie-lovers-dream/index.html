Irresistible Oreo Cheesecake Bars: A Cookie Lover's Dream


If you’re a fan of decadent desserts that combine the rich flavors of Oreo cookies and creamy cheesecake, then you’re in for a treat with this irresistible recipe for Oreo Cheesecake Bars. These bars are the perfect fusion of two beloved treats, creating a delightful indulgence that will satisfy even the most discerning sweet tooth. Whether you’re hosting a dinner party, celebrating a special occasion, or simply craving a delicious homemade dessert, these Oreo Cheesecake Bars are sure to impress and delight.

Ingredients

For the Oreo Crust:

  • 24 Oreo cookies
  • 1/4 cup unsalted butter, melted

For the Cheesecake Filling:

  • 16 oz cream cheese, softened
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1/2 cup sour cream
  • 12 Oreo cookies, roughly chopped

For the Topping:

  • 1 cup semi-sweet chocolate chips
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • 6 Oreo cookies, crushed

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Oreo Crust:

    • Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C). Line an 8x8-inch baking pan with parchment paper, leaving an overhang on the sides for easy removal.
    • In a food processor, pulse the Oreo cookies into fine crumbs. Alternatively, place the cookies in a resealable plastic bag and crush them with a rolling pin.
    • In a bowl, mix the Oreo crumbs with melted butter until well combined. Press the mixture into the bottom of the prepared baking pan, creating an even layer. Bake for 10 minutes, then remove from the oven and set aside.

  2. Prepare the Cheesecake Filling:

    • In a large bowl, beat the softened cream cheese and granulated sugar until smooth and creamy. Add the eggs, one at a time, mixing well after each addition. Stir in the vanilla extract and sour cream until fully incorporated. Gently fold in the chopped Oreo cookies.
    • Pour the cheesecake filling over the pre-baked Oreo crust, spreading it into an even layer.

  3. Bake and Chill:

    • Place the baking pan in the preheated oven and bake for 35-40 minutes, or until the edges are set and the center is slightly jiggly. Remove from the oven and let the cheesecake bars cool to room temperature. Then, cover the pan with plastic wrap and refrigerate for at least 4 hours, or overnight, to set.

  4. Prepare the Chocolate Topping:

    • In a heatproof bowl, combine the semi-sweet chocolate chips and heavy cream. Microwave in 30-second intervals, stirring each time, until the mixture is smooth and glossy.
    • Pour the chocolate topping over the chilled cheesecake layer, spreading it evenly. Sprinkle the crushed Oreo cookies on top of the chocolate layer.

  5. Serve and Enjoy:

    • Lift the chilled cheesecake bars out of the pan using the parchment paper overhang. Place them on a cutting board and slice into bars using a sharp knife. Serve and indulge in the irresistible combination of Oreo cookies and creamy cheesecake.

These Oreo Cheesecake Bars are a true delight for cookie lovers and dessert enthusiasts alike. The rich, chocolatey Oreo crust provides the perfect base for the luscious, creamy cheesecake filling, while the decadent chocolate topping adds an extra layer of indulgence. Whether you’re making them for a special occasion or simply to treat yourself, these bars are guaranteed to be a hit. Enjoy every delectable bite and savor the blissful marriage of Oreo cookies and cheesecake in every mouthful.
