Baker's Dozen: 13 Original Recipes to Unveil Your Love for Pizza and Flatbreads


Ah, pizza – a timeless favorite stretching across generations, bringing joy and warmth to countless households. Its humble origin might have been the streets of Naples, but pizza has come a long way since its birth. Its journey has brought us beyond the realm of what a traditional margherita or pepperoni slice could ever be. Today, we prepare to venture further down the rabbit hole, exploring new heights with flatbreads inspired by diverse flavors and cultural traditions. Drawing upon our passion, let’s bring the kitchen magic alive with 13 fresh recipes to spice up your culinary repertoire!

1. Butter Kale and Crispy Bacon Pizza

Kale obsessed? We’ve got you! Give your taste buds a dance between rich, savory bacon and luscious kale.

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb pizza dough
  • ½ cup pizza sauce
  • ½ cup shredded mozzarella
  • 4 oz crumbled goat cheese
  • 4 slices of bacon, cooked and chopped
  • 1 cup chopped kale, sautéed briefly in olive oil
  • 4 tbsp unsalted butter, melted
  • Salt and pepper, to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 475°F (245°C).
  2. Roll out pizza dough over a floured surface, let it rest.
  3. Spread pizza sauce evenly, then layer on mozzarella, goat cheese, and bacon.
  4. Layer the sautéed kale, followed by drizzling the melted butter.
  5. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  6. Bake for 12-15 minutes, or until the crust is golden and cheese is bubbling.

2. Prosciutto and Fig Flatbread

Aromatic and sweet, this flatbread combines the savory prosciutto with the deep sweetness of figs, creating a divine flavor profile.

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb pizza dough
  • 4 oz goat cheese
  • ½ cup crumbled blue cheese
  • ½ cup baby arugula
  • 8 slices of prosciutto, chopped
  • 6-8 fresh figs, sliced
  • 1 tbsp honey
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • Salt and pepper, to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 425°F (220°C).
  2. Roll out the pizza dough, transfer to baking sheet.
  3. Drizzle the olive oil and honey over the dough.
  4. Scatter goat cheese, blue cheese, prosciutto, and sliced figs over the top.
  5. Bake for 15-20 minutes, until the crust turns golden brown.
  6. Garnish with fresh arugula before serving.

3. Mediterranean White Flatbread

For healthier alternatives, experiment with Mediterranean flavors, filled with fresh vegetables, olives, and feta.

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb pizza dough
  • 1 cup mozzarella, shredded
  • ½ cup feta, crumbled
  • 1 zucchini, thinly sliced
  • 1 red bell pepper, thinly sliced
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • ½ cup kalamata olives, pitted
  • ¼ cup fresh basil, chopped
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • Salt and pepper, to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 425°F (220°C).
  2. Roll out the pizza dough.
  3. Combine mozzarella, feta, zucchini, red bell pepper, cherry tomatoes, olives, and basil.
  4. Spread the olive oil over the dough, followed by the cheese and vegetable mixture.
  5. Bake for 15-20 minutes, or until the crust is golden brown.

4. Pesto Alfredo Potato Flatbread

Transform the humble spud into a hero, livening up the humble tater for an indulgent, fusion-inspired twist.

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb pizza dough
  • ½ cup alfredo sauce
  • ½ cup pesto sauce
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella
  • 1 large russet potato, thinly sliced
  • Salt and pepper, to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 425°F (220°C).
  2. Mix alfredo sauce and pesto sauce, set aside.
  3. Roll out pizza dough, create an even layer of the pesto alfredo mix, then sprinkle the mozzarella.
  4. Scatter the potato slices, season with salt and pepper.
  5. Bake for 18-22 minutes or until crust and potatoes are golden.

5. BBQ Chicken and Pineapple Flatbread

Grilled BBQ and pineapple – this hybrid is for those with a sweet-savory tooth, perfect for a relaxed summer evening.

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb pizza dough
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella
  • ½ cup BBQ sauce
  • 1 cup cooked shredded chicken
  • 1 fresh pineapple, cut into chunks with rind removed
  • Salt and pepper, to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 425°F (220°C).
  2. Roll out pizza dough on parchment paper.
  3. Spread a thin layer of BBQ sauce on dough, followed by the shredded mozzarella.
  4. Top with BBQ chicken and pineapple chunks.
  5. Bake for 20-25 minutes, until the crust is golden and pineapple is caramelized.
