Flavorful and Healthy Roasted Brussels Sprouts Salad: A Fresh Side


When it comes to creating a delicious and nutritious side dish, roasted Brussels sprouts salad is a true winner. This flavorful and healthy salad is a delightful combination of roasted Brussels sprouts, vibrant vegetables, and a zesty dressing. Whether you’re looking for a refreshing side for a summer barbecue, a light lunch option, or a colorful addition to your dinner table, this salad has got you covered.

Ingredients

To create this delectable roasted Brussels sprouts salad, you will need the following ingredients:

  • 1 pound Brussels sprouts, trimmed and halved
  • 1 red bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 yellow bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 red onion, thinly sliced
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons balsamic vinegar
  • 1 teaspoon Dijon mustard
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1/4 cup crumbled feta cheese
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley

Instructions

Step 1: Roasting the Brussels Sprouts

Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C). Place the halved Brussels sprouts on a baking sheet and drizzle with 2 tablespoons of olive oil. Season with salt and pepper, then toss to coat evenly. Roast in the preheated oven for 25-30 minutes, or until the Brussels sprouts are tender and caramelized.

Step 2: Preparing the Dressing

In a small bowl, whisk together the remaining olive oil, balsamic vinegar, Dijon mustard, minced garlic, and a pinch of salt and pepper. This vibrant dressing will add a burst of flavor to the salad.

Step 3: Assembling the Salad

In a large mixing bowl, combine the roasted Brussels sprouts, sliced red and yellow bell peppers, red onion, and cherry tomatoes. Pour the dressing over the vegetables and gently toss to coat everything evenly.

Step 4: Adding the Final Touches

Once the salad is well-coated with the dressing, sprinkle the crumbled feta cheese and chopped fresh parsley over the top. These final touches will add a delightful pop of color and flavor to the dish.

Serving Suggestions

This roasted Brussels sprouts salad is a versatile dish that can be served in a variety of ways. It makes a fantastic side dish for grilled chicken or fish, a light and satisfying lunch on its own, or a vibrant addition to a potluck or picnic spread. The combination of savory roasted Brussels sprouts, crisp vegetables, and tangy dressing creates a symphony of flavors that will delight your taste buds.

Nutritional Benefits

Not only is this salad a treat for the taste buds, but it also offers a range of nutritional benefits. Brussels sprouts are packed with vitamins, fiber, and antioxidants, making them an excellent addition to a healthy diet. The colorful assortment of vegetables in this salad provides an array of essential nutrients, while the olive oil and feta cheese offer healthy fats and a touch of richness.

Final Thoughts

In conclusion, this roasted Brussels sprouts salad is a wonderful way to elevate your dining experience with a burst of fresh flavors and vibrant colors. Whether you’re a fan of Brussels sprouts or looking for a new way to enjoy vegetables, this salad is sure to impress. With its easy preparation and delightful combination of textures and tastes, it’s a dish that will become a staple in your recipe collection. So, gather your ingredients, fire up your oven, and treat yourself to this delightful and healthy salad – your taste buds will thank you!

Remember, when you’re in the mood for a fresh and flavorful side dish, this roasted Brussels sprouts salad is a delicious choice that’s perfect for any occasion. Enjoy!
