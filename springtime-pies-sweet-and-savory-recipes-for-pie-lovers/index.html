Springtime Pies: Sweet and Savory Recipes for Pie Lovers


Spring is the perfect season to indulge in the sweet and savory goodness of pies. From vibrant fruit-filled desserts to hearty savory creations, there’s a pie for every craving. Whether you’re hosting a springtime gathering or simply want to treat yourself, these mouthwatering pie recipes will surely satisfy your taste buds. So, let’s dive into the world of Springtime Pies!

1. Strawberry Rhubarb Pie

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups of fresh strawberries, hulled and sliced
  • 2 cups of rhubarb, trimmed and cut into 1/2-inch pieces
  • 1 cup of granulated sugar
  • 1/4 cup of all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon of lemon juice
  • 1/2 teaspoon of vanilla extract
  • 1 package of pre-made pie crusts

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. In a large mixing bowl, combine the strawberries, rhubarb, sugar, flour, lemon juice, and vanilla extract. Toss until well coated.
  3. Roll out one pie crust and place it into a 9-inch pie dish. Trim any excess dough.
  4. Pour the strawberry rhubarb filling into the pie crust.
  5. Roll out the second pie crust and cut it into strips or use a pie crust cutter to create a lattice design on top of the filling.
  6. Bake for 45-50 minutes, or until the crust is golden brown and the filling is bubbling.
  7. Allow the pie to cool before serving. Enjoy!

2. Spinach and Feta Galette

Ingredients:

  • 1 package of pre-made pie crusts
  • 4 cups of fresh spinach, washed and chopped
  • 1 cup of crumbled feta cheese
  • 1/2 cup of sliced sun-dried tomatoes
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon of olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 egg, beaten (for egg wash)

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. In a skillet, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the minced garlic and sauté for 1-2 minutes, until fragrant.
  3. Add the spinach to the skillet and cook until wilted. Season with salt and pepper.
  4. Roll out the pie crust and place it on a baking sheet.
  5. Spread the spinach evenly over the pie crust, leaving a 2-inch border around the edges.
  6. Sprinkle the feta cheese and sun-dried tomatoes over the spinach.
  7. Fold the edges of the pie crust over the filling, creating a rustic edge.
  8. Brush the edges of the crust with the beaten egg.
  9. Bake for 25-30 minutes, or until the crust is golden brown.
  10. Allow the galette to cool slightly before serving. Slice and enjoy!

3. Lemon Meringue Pie

Ingredients:

  • 1 package of pre-made pie crusts
  • 1 cup of granulated sugar
  • 1/4 cup of cornstarch
  • 1/4 teaspoon of salt
  • 1 1/2 cups of water
  • 1/2 cup of freshly squeezed lemon juice
  • 3 large egg yolks
  • 2 tablespoons of unsalted butter
  • Zest of 1 lemon
  • 4 large egg whites
  • 1/4 teaspoon of cream of tartar
  • 1/2 cup of granulated sugar

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Roll out the pie crust and place it in a 9-inch pie dish. Trim any excess dough.
  3. In a saucepan, whisk together the sugar, cornstarch, and salt. Gradually whisk in the water and lemon juice until smooth.
  4. Cook the mixture over medium heat, stirring constantly, until it thickens and comes to a boil.
  5. In a separate bowl, whisk the egg yolks. Slowly pour about 1/2 cup of the hot lemon mixture into the egg yolks, whisking constantly.
  6. Pour the egg yolk mixture back into the saucepan and cook for an additional 2 minutes, stirring constantly.
  7. Remove the saucepan from the heat and stir in the butter and lemon zest until melted and combined.
  8. Pour the lemon filling into the prepared pie crust.
  9. In a clean mixing bowl, beat the egg whites and cream of tartar until soft peaks form. Gradually add the sugar, beating until stiff peaks form.
  10. Spread the meringue over the lemon filling, making sure to seal the edges.
  11. Bake for 12-15 minutes, or until the meringue is golden brown.
  12. Allow the pie to cool completely before serving. Delight in every tangy, creamy bite!

These Springtime Pies are just the beginning of your pie-filled adventures. Let your imagination run wild with different fillings, crusts, and decorative designs. Whether you prefer the sweetness of fruits or the savory goodness of vegetables and cheese, there’s a pie waiting to be baked and enjoyed. So, gather your ingredients, preheat the oven, and let the aroma of freshly baked pies fill your kitchen. Happy pie-making!
