Delicious One Pot Sausage and Peppers


When it comes to quick and flavorful meals, one pot dishes are the way to go. They’re convenient, easy to make, and require minimal cleanup. If you’re a fan of hearty, savory flavors, then this delicious one pot sausage and peppers recipe is sure to become a staple in your cooking repertoire. Whether you’re cooking for a family dinner or hosting a casual get-together, this recipe is a crowd-pleaser that’s bound to impress.

Ingredients

  • 4 Italian sausage links
  • 2 bell peppers (red and green), sliced
  • 1 large onion, sliced
  • 3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 can (14 oz) diced tomatoes
  • 1/2 cup chicken broth
  • 1 teaspoon Italian seasoning
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley for garnish

Instructions

  1. Sear the Sausage: In a large, deep skillet or pot, heat some olive oil over medium-high heat. Add the sausage links and cook them until they’re browned on all sides. This should take about 6-8 minutes. Once done, transfer the sausages to a plate and set them aside.

  2. Saute the Vegetables: In the same skillet, add the sliced onions and bell peppers. Cook them for 5-7 minutes, or until they start to soften and caramelize. Stir in the minced garlic and cook for an additional minute.

  3. Combine the Ingredients: Pour in the diced tomatoes (with their juices) and chicken broth. Add the Italian seasoning, salt, and pepper. Stir everything together, making sure to scrape up any browned bits from the bottom of the skillet.

  4. Simmer and Add the Sausage: Return the browned sausage links to the skillet, nestling them into the vegetable mixture. Bring the liquid to a simmer, then reduce the heat to medium-low. Cover the skillet and let everything simmer for 20-25 minutes, or until the sausage is fully cooked and the flavors have melded together.

  5. Serve and Enjoy: Once the sausage is cooked through, and the peppers are tender, it’s time to serve. You can slice the sausage links into smaller pieces if desired. Spoon the sausage and peppers onto a serving dish, garnish with fresh parsley, and serve it as is or with a side of crusty bread or over a bed of cooked pasta or rice.

Why You’ll Love This Recipe

This one pot sausage and peppers recipe is a flavor-packed dish that brings together the savory goodness of Italian sausage with the sweetness of bell peppers and onions. The combination of these ingredients creates a harmonious blend of flavors and textures that is simply irresistible.

Not only is this dish delicious, but it’s also incredibly versatile. You can serve it as a standalone meal, or use it as a filling for sandwiches, wraps, or even as a topping for pizza. The leftovers, if there are any, make for a fantastic next-day lunch or dinner.

Additionally, this recipe is a great way to incorporate more vegetables into your diet. Bell peppers are a rich source of vitamins and antioxidants, while the Italian sausage adds a hearty protein punch.

Final Thoughts

The beauty of one pot meals lies in their simplicity and the way they bring people together around the dinner table. This one pot sausage and peppers recipe is a prime example of how a few humble ingredients can come together to create a truly memorable meal. Whether you’re a seasoned home cook or just starting out in the kitchen, this recipe is a must-try. So, gather your ingredients, grab a skillet, and get ready to enjoy the delightful flavors of this one pot wonder!
