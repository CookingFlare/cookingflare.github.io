Delicious Vegan Tofu and Veggie Stir-Fry


If you’re looking for a flavorful and healthy vegan dish that’s quick and easy to make, then you’re in the right place. This delicious tofu and veggie stir-fry is packed with protein, nutrients, and vibrant flavors that will delight your taste buds. Whether you’re a seasoned vegan cook or just starting to explore plant-based recipes, this stir-fry is sure to become a staple in your culinary repertoire.

Ingredients

For the Stir-Fry:

  • 14 oz (400g) firm tofu, drained and cubed
  • 1 tablespoon cornstarch
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons sesame oil
  • 1 red bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 yellow bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 cup broccoli florets
  • 1 cup snap peas
  • 1 cup sliced carrots
  • 4 green onions, sliced
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon fresh ginger, grated
  • 1/4 cup vegetable broth
  • Cooked rice or noodles, for serving

For the Sauce:

  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons rice vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon maple syrup
  • 1 teaspoon cornstarch

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Tofu: Start by pressing the tofu to remove excess water. Once drained, cut the tofu into cubes and toss them in 1 tablespoon of cornstarch until coated.

  2. Make the Sauce: In a small bowl, whisk together 1/4 cup of soy sauce, 2 tablespoons of rice vinegar, 1 tablespoon of maple syrup, and 1 teaspoon of cornstarch. Set the sauce aside for later use.

  3. Cook the Tofu: Heat 1 tablespoon of sesame oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat. Add the coated tofu cubes and cook until golden brown and crispy on all sides. Once cooked, remove the tofu from the skillet and set it aside.

  4. Prepare the Veggies: In the same skillet, add another tablespoon of sesame oil. Add the sliced bell peppers, broccoli florets, snap peas, and carrots. Stir-fry the vegetables for 3-4 minutes until they are tender-crisp.

  5. Add Aromatics: Create a well in the center of the vegetables and add the minced garlic and grated ginger. Stir-fry for 1 minute until fragrant.

  6. Combine and Simmer: Return the cooked tofu to the skillet with the vegetables. Pour the prepared sauce and 1/4 cup of vegetable broth over the tofu and veggies. Stir gently to coat everything in the sauce. Allow the mixture to simmer for 2-3 minutes until the sauce thickens slightly.

  7. Serve: Once the stir-fry is ready, sprinkle sliced green onions over the top. Serve the tofu and veggie stir-fry over cooked rice or noodles.

Tips and Variations

  • For an extra kick of heat, add a pinch of red pepper flakes or a drizzle of sriracha to the stir-fry.
  • Feel free to customize the vegetables based on your preferences or what’s in season. You can add mushrooms, snow peas, or water chestnuts for added texture and flavor.
  • If you prefer a gluten-free option, use tamari instead of soy sauce and ensure all other ingredients are gluten-free.

In Conclusion

This vegan tofu and veggie stir-fry is a versatile and satisfying dish that’s suitable for any occasion. Whether you’re cooking for a quick weeknight dinner or entertaining guests, this stir-fry is a crowd-pleaser. The combination of crispy tofu, colorful veggies, and savory sauce creates a harmonious blend of flavors and textures that will leave you craving more. So, roll up your sleeves, gather your ingredients, and get ready to enjoy a delightful culinary experience with this delectable vegan stir-fry!
