Chewy and Nutty Peanut Butter Cookies: A Classic Combination


There’s something truly irresistible about the combination of chewy and nutty flavors in a classic peanut butter cookie. Whether you’re a peanut butter fanatic or just looking for a delightful treat, these cookies are sure to please your taste buds. With their soft texture and rich taste, they are a timeless favorite that never fails to bring joy.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1 cup creamy peanut butter
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 cup packed brown sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 2 and 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 and 1/2 teaspoons baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • Additional granulated sugar for rolling

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C) and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.

  2. In a large bowl, cream together the softened butter, creamy peanut butter, granulated sugar, and brown sugar until light and fluffy. This will take about 2-3 minutes using an electric mixer on medium speed.

  3. Add the eggs one at a time, beating well after each addition. Then, stir in the vanilla extract.

  4. In a separate bowl, whisk together the all-purpose flour, baking soda, and salt. Gradually add the dry ingredients to the wet ingredients, mixing until just combined.

  5. Scoop out portions of dough, about 1.5 tablespoons each. Roll them into balls between your palms, and then roll each ball in granulated sugar to coat evenly.

  6. Place the coated dough balls onto the prepared baking sheet, leaving about 2 inches of space between each cookie.

  7. Gently press down on each cookie with a fork, creating a crisscross pattern on the top. This not only adds a decorative touch but also helps the cookies bake evenly.

  8. Bake the cookies in the preheated oven for 10-12 minutes, or until they are lightly golden around the edges. Be careful not to overbake, as you want them to remain chewy.

  9. Once baked, remove the cookies from the oven and allow them to cool on the baking sheet for 5 minutes. Then, transfer them to a wire rack to cool completely.

  10. Serve these delightful peanut butter cookies with a glass of milk or your favorite hot beverage. They are perfect for any occasion, from casual gatherings to special events. Enjoy the classic combination of chewy texture and nutty flavor!

Tips and Variations

  • For an extra burst of flavor, consider adding some chopped peanuts or chocolate chips to the cookie dough before baking.
  • If you prefer a crunchier texture, bake the cookies for an additional 1-2 minutes.
  • You can also experiment with different types of nut butter, such as almond or cashew, to create unique variations of this classic recipe.
  • These cookies can be stored in an airtight container at room temperature for up to a week, but they are so delicious that they may not last that long!

Conclusion

Chewy and nutty peanut butter cookies are a timeless treat that never goes out of style. With their soft texture and rich flavor, they are the perfect combination of simplicity and indulgence. Whether you’re baking for a party, a family gathering, or just craving a sweet treat, these cookies are sure to satisfy. So, grab your apron, preheat that oven, and get ready to enjoy the classic combination of chewy and nutty peanut butter cookies!
