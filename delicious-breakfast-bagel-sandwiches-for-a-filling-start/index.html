Delicious Breakfast Bagel Sandwiches for a Filling Start


When it comes to breakfast, there’s nothing quite like a delicious and filling bagel sandwich to kickstart your day. This versatile dish allows for endless possibilities, from classic combinations to more adventurous flavors. In this recipe, we’ll explore some mouthwatering variations of breakfast bagel sandwiches that are sure to satisfy your morning cravings.

Ingredients

For the Bagels:

  • 4 plain or everything bagels
  • 4 large eggs
  • 4 slices of cheddar or pepper jack cheese
  • 4 slices of cooked bacon or turkey bacon
  • 1 ripe avocado, sliced
  • 1 tablespoon of butter
  • Salt and pepper to taste

For the Spicy Mayo (optional):

  • 1/4 cup mayonnaise
  • 1 tablespoon sriracha sauce
  • 1 teaspoon lemon juice
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
  • Salt to taste

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Bagels:

    • Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C). Cut the bagels in half and lightly toast them in the oven for about 5 minutes, until they are slightly crispy.

  2. Cook the Eggs:

    • In a skillet, melt the butter over medium heat. Crack the eggs into the skillet and cook until the whites are set but the yolks are still runny. Season with salt and pepper to taste.

  3. Assemble the Sandwiches:

    • If you’re opting for the spicy mayo, mix all the ingredients in a small bowl. Spread the spicy mayo on the bottom half of each bagel.
    • Layer the cooked egg, cheese, bacon, and sliced avocado on the bottom half of the bagel. Top with the other half of the bagel.

  4. Serve and Enjoy:

    • Your delicious breakfast bagel sandwiches are ready to be served! Pair them with a hot cup of coffee or a refreshing glass of orange juice for a delightful morning meal.

Variations

Veggie Lover’s Delight:

For a vegetarian option, skip the bacon and add sautéed spinach, sliced tomatoes, and a dollop of creamy hummus for a nutritious and flavorful breakfast bagel sandwich.

Smoked Salmon Sensation:

Replace the bacon with smoked salmon, and top it with a dollop of cream cheese and a sprinkle of capers for a sophisticated and indulgent twist on the classic bagel sandwich.

Southwest Fiesta:

Spice things up by adding some sliced jalapeños, grilled bell peppers, and a drizzle of chipotle mayo for a zesty and vibrant breakfast creation.

Mediterranean Magic:

Incorporate some Mediterranean flavors by layering the bagel with sun-dried tomatoes, feta cheese, and a handful of arugula for a refreshing and exotic breakfast experience.

Final Thoughts

Breakfast bagel sandwiches are not only a delicious way to start your day, but they also offer endless opportunities for creativity and personalization. Whether you’re craving a hearty, meaty option or a lighter, veggie-packed alternative, there’s a breakfast bagel sandwich to suit every taste. With this recipe and its versatile variations, you’ll never run out of ideas for a satisfying and flavorful morning meal. So, next time you’re in need of a filling start to your day, whip up one of these delightful breakfast bagel sandwiches and savor every delightful bite!
