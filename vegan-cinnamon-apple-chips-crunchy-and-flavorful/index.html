Vegan Cinnamon Apple Chips: Crunchy and Flavorful


Apple chips are a delicious and healthy snack that combines the natural sweetness of apples with the warm, comforting flavor of cinnamon. If you’re craving a crunchy snack that’s both satisfying and wholesome, look no further than these Vegan Cinnamon Apple Chips. This homemade treat is incredibly easy to make and perfect for those who want to indulge in a guilt-free, plant-based snack. Whether you’re looking for a quick snack between meals or something to pack in your lunchbox, these apple chips will satisfy your cravings in a healthy way.

In this post, we’ll guide you through the step-by-step process of making your own vegan cinnamon apple chips at home. These chips are not only vegan but also gluten-free and packed with nutrients, making them an ideal snack for everyone, from children to adults. Plus, they come with a delicious cinnamon kick that adds a cozy touch to the crisp apple slices.

Why Choose Vegan Cinnamon Apple Chips?

Before we dive into the recipe, let’s take a moment to appreciate why vegan cinnamon apple chips are the perfect snack option.

1. Simple Ingredients

This recipe is made with only a few basic ingredients—apples, cinnamon, and a little bit of sweetener (optional). You won’t find any preservatives, artificial flavors, or chemicals in this snack, making it a wholesome option.

2. Nutrient-Rich

Apples are rich in fiber, vitamins, and antioxidants, which help support digestion, boost immunity, and promote healthy skin. When paired with the powerful benefits of cinnamon—known for its anti-inflammatory and blood sugar-regulating properties—you have a snack that’s both delicious and nourishing.

3. Customizable Flavors

While cinnamon is the star of this recipe, you can easily tweak the flavor profile to suit your taste. You can add a pinch of nutmeg, a dash of vanilla extract, or even a drizzle of maple syrup to create a snack that’s uniquely yours.

4. Low in Calories

If you’re trying to maintain a healthy weight or looking for a low-calorie snack option, these vegan cinnamon apple chips are a great choice. They’re naturally low in fat and sugar, especially compared to store-bought chips and other processed snacks.

5. Easy to Make

One of the best things about making vegan cinnamon apple chips at home is how simple the process is. With just a few steps and a bit of patience, you can have a big batch of homemade, crunchy apple chips ready to enjoy.

Now that we’ve covered why these apple chips are such a great snack, let’s move on to the recipe itself. Here’s how you can make these irresistible cinnamon apple chips right in your kitchen.

Ingredients

For the Apple Chips:

  • 4 medium-sized apples (choose a firm variety like Fuji, Gala, or Granny Smith for best results)
  • 1 tablespoon ground cinnamon
  • 1–2 teaspoons of sweetener (optional, such as maple syrup, agave, or coconut sugar)
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice (to prevent browning)
  • A pinch of sea salt (optional, for added flavor)

Equipment Needed:

  • Sharp knife or mandoline slicer
  • Baking sheet(s)
  • Parchment paper or silicone baking mats
  • Cooling rack (optional, for extra crispness)

Instructions

Step 1: Prepare the Apples

Start by washing your apples thoroughly to remove any dirt or wax. Once they’re clean, core and slice the apples. You can do this by hand using a sharp knife or, for consistency, use a mandoline slicer to get even thin slices. The key to perfectly crisp apple chips is to slice the apples very thinly, about 1/8 inch thick. This allows the chips to dry out and crisp up evenly in the oven.

To prevent browning, immediately toss the apple slices in lemon juice. This step will help keep the apples looking fresh while they bake and also add a slight tanginess to the chips.

Step 2: Season the Apple Slices

Once the apples are sliced and coated in lemon juice, it’s time to season them. In a small bowl, mix together the ground cinnamon and sweetener of your choice. If you prefer a stronger cinnamon flavor, you can increase the amount of cinnamon to your liking. If you like a little sweetness, add in a teaspoon or two of maple syrup, agave, or coconut sugar.

Sprinkle the cinnamon mixture over the apple slices and toss them gently to ensure that all the pieces are evenly coated. Make sure to coat both sides of the slices with the seasoning for maximum flavor.

Step 3: Arrange the Apple Slices on a Baking Sheet

Line your baking sheet with parchment paper or a silicone baking mat to prevent the apple slices from sticking. Lay the apple slices out in a single layer on the baking sheet. Avoid overlapping the slices, as this will cause them to bake unevenly. If you need to use multiple baking sheets, feel free to divide the apple slices between them.

Step 4: Bake the Apple Chips

Preheat your oven to 200°F (93°C). This low temperature is essential to help dehydrate the apples slowly without burning them.

Place the baking sheet in the preheated oven and bake the apple slices for 2 to 3 hours. The exact time will depend on the thickness of your slices and your oven’s settings. After the first hour, check the apple chips every 30 minutes to ensure they are drying out evenly. Flip the apple slices halfway through the baking process to help them crisp up on both sides.

The apple chips are done when they’re golden brown, crispy, and snap easily when you bend them. If they’re still a little soft, give them some extra time in the oven. Just keep an eye on them to avoid over-baking.

Step 5: Cool and Store

Once the apple chips are finished baking, remove them from the oven and let them cool on the baking sheet for about 10 minutes. This will help them crisp up even more as they cool. If you have a cooling rack, transfer the chips to the rack for extra air circulation and crispness.

Once cooled, store the apple chips in an airtight container at room temperature. They should stay fresh for up to a week, although they’re likely to disappear much sooner!

Tips for Perfect Vegan Cinnamon Apple Chips

  1. Choose the Right Apples: The type of apple you use will impact the flavor and texture of your apple chips. Firmer varieties like Fuji, Gala, or Granny Smith apples hold up better during the baking process and result in crispier chips. Avoid soft or mealy apples, as they may become mushy while baking.

  2. Use a Mandoline for Even Slicing: If you want perfectly uniform apple slices, a mandoline slicer is your best bet. This tool ensures that all the slices are the same thickness, which leads to even baking and consistent crispiness.

  3. Experiment with Flavors: While cinnamon is the classic spice to pair with apples, don’t be afraid to get creative. Try adding a pinch of nutmeg, ginger, or cloves for a spiced apple chip variation. You can even drizzle the finished chips with a little maple syrup or sprinkle a bit of sea salt for a sweet-and-salty twist.

  4. Bake Low and Slow: The key to achieving perfectly crispy apple chips is baking them at a low temperature for a long period of time. While it may take a few hours, this slow drying process ensures that the chips get crisp without burning.

  5. Make a Big Batch: These apple chips store well, so feel free to make a large batch. They make a great snack for work, school, or a picnic, and they’re a perfect addition to your meal prep routine.

How to Enjoy Vegan Cinnamon Apple Chips

There are so many ways to enjoy your homemade vegan cinnamon apple chips. Here are a few ideas:

  • Snack on them: The most obvious way to enjoy these chips is just as a snack! Grab a handful when you’re feeling hungry for something crispy and sweet.
  • Pair with Nut Butter: For an added protein boost, try dipping your apple chips in almond butter, peanut butter, or any nut butter of your choice. The creamy texture complements the crispy chips perfectly.
  • Top your Oatmeal or Yogurt: Sprinkle some apple chips over your morning oatmeal or non-dairy yogurt for added texture and flavor.
  • Serve with Vegan Cheese: Serve the chips alongside your favorite vegan cheese for a sweet and savory snack combo.
  • Pack them for a Road Trip: These apple chips are a great snack to take on the go. They’re lightweight, portable, and won’t spoil during travel.

Conclusion

Vegan cinnamon apple chips are the perfect combination of crunch, flavor, and nutrition. They’re easy to make, customizable, and incredibly satisfying. Whether you’re looking for a healthy snack or a fun way to use up apples, this recipe is a winner. By making your own apple chips at home, you can enjoy a delicious, preservative-free snack whenever you want.

So, why not give this recipe a try? Your taste buds will thank you!
