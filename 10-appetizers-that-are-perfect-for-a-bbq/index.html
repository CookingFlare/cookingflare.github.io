10 Appetizers That Are Perfect for a BBQ


Summer is here, and with it comes the perfect excuse to fire up the grill and gather with friends and family for a backyard BBQ. But, what’s a BBQ without some delicious appetizers to get the party started? Here are 10 mouth-watering options that are sure to please even the pickiest of eaters.

1. Grilled Shrimp Skewers with Spicy Mango Sauce

These bite-sized shrimp skewers are a crowd-pleaser, and the spicy mango sauce adds a sweet and tangy twist. Simply marinate the shrimp in a mixture of olive oil, lime juice, garlic, and cumin, then grill until pink and slightly charred. Serve with a side of spicy mango sauce made by blending together diced mango, red pepper flakes, lime juice, and honey.

2. Bacon-Wrapped Dates

Who doesn’t love the combination of sweet and savory? These bacon-wrapped dates are an easy and impressive appetizer to make. Simply stuff dates with crumbled blue cheese and wrap with bacon slices. Bake in the oven until the bacon is crispy, then serve warm.

3. Grilled Peach and Prosciutto Salad

This refreshing salad is perfect for a summer BBQ. Simply grill sliced peaches until caramelized, then top with prosciutto, arugula, and a drizzle of balsamic glaze.

4. Spicy Corn Fritters

These crispy corn fritters are packed with flavor and spice. Simply mix together cornmeal, flour, eggs, and spices, then fry in hot oil until golden brown. Serve with a side of spicy aioli made by blending together mayonnaise, sriracha, and lime juice.

5. Mini Caprese Skewers

These bite-sized caprese skewers are a colorful and easy appetizer to make. Simply alternate cherry tomatoes, mozzarella cheese, and fresh basil leaves on skewers, then drizzle with olive oil and balsamic glaze.

6. Grilled Pineapple and Bacon Sliders

These sweet and savory sliders are a perfect BBQ appetizer. Simply grill pineapple slices until caramelized, then top with crispy bacon, cheese, and a bun. Serve with a side of spicy BBQ sauce.

7. Roasted Chickpeas with Lemon and Garlic

These crispy roasted chickpeas are a healthy and addictive snack. Simply rinse and dry chickpeas, then toss with olive oil, lemon juice, garlic, and spices. Roast in the oven until crispy, then season with salt and serve.

8. Grilled Asparagus with Lemon Aioli

This refreshing appetizer is perfect for a summer BBQ. Simply grill asparagus until tender, then serve with a side of lemon aioli made by blending together mayonnaise, lemon juice, and garlic.

9. Mini Quiches with Caramelized Onions and Goat Cheese

These individual quiches are a perfect appetizer for a party. Simply mix together eggs, cream, and spices, then pour into mini muffin tin. Top with caramelized onions and goat cheese, then bake until set.

10. Spicy Pigs in a Blanket

These spicy pigs in a blanket are a fun and easy appetizer to make. Simply wrap mini hot dogs in croissant dough, then bake until golden brown. Serve with a side of spicy mustard made by blending together mustard, hot sauce, and honey.

These 10 appetizers are sure to impress your guests and satisfy their cravings. Whether you’re hosting a backyard BBQ or a summer party, these recipes are perfect for any occasion. So, fire up the grill and get ready to party!
