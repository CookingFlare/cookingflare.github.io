Pumpkin Spice Pull-Apart Bread: Perfect for Fall


If there’s one thing that signals the arrival of fall, it’s the warm and comforting aroma of spices wafting through the kitchen. This Pumpkin Spice Pull-Apart Bread recipe is the perfect way to welcome the season with open arms. Imagine a soft, pillowy bread infused with the flavors of pumpkin and a blend of autumn spices, all held together in a delightful pull-apart loaf that’s as fun to make as it is to eat.

Ingredients:

  • 4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 2 1/4 teaspoons active dry yeast
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1/4 cup unsalted butter
  • 1/2 cup pumpkin puree
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

For the Spice Mixture:

  • 1/2 cup brown sugar
  • 2 teaspoons ground cinnamon
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground nutmeg
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground ginger
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground cloves

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the Dough:

    • In a large mixing bowl, combine 2 cups of flour, sugar, salt, and yeast.
    • In a saucepan, heat the milk and butter until warm (about 120-130°F).
    • Add the warm milk mixture, pumpkin puree, eggs, and vanilla extract to the dry ingredients. Mix well.
    • Gradually add the remaining flour until a soft dough forms.
    • Knead the dough on a floured surface until smooth and elastic.
    • Place the dough in a greased bowl, cover, and let it rise until doubled in size.

  2. Make the Spice Mixture:

    • In a small bowl, combine the brown sugar, cinnamon, nutmeg, ginger, and cloves. Set aside.

  3. Assemble the Bread:

    • Punch down the risen dough and divide it into small pieces.
    • Roll each piece into a ball and dip it in melted butter, then coat it in the spice mixture.
    • Place the coated balls in a greased loaf pan, stacking them as you go.
    • Cover the pan and let the dough rise again until puffy.

  4. Bake to Perfection:

    • Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C).
    • Bake the bread for 30-35 minutes, or until golden brown and cooked through.
    • Allow the bread to cool slightly before serving.

Serving Suggestions:

This Pumpkin Spice Pull-Apart Bread is best enjoyed fresh out of the oven when the warm bread pulls apart easily, revealing layers of spiced goodness. Pair it with a cup of hot coffee or cider for the ultimate fall treat. You can also drizzle a simple glaze over the bread for an extra touch of sweetness.

Embrace the Flavors of Fall:

With its irresistible aroma and cozy flavors, this Pumpkin Spice Pull-Apart Bread is a must-have for your fall baking repertoire. Whether you’re hosting a gathering or simply craving a special treat, this recipe is sure to impress. So, roll up your sleeves, gather your ingredients, and let the magic of fall baking unfold in your kitchen. Happy baking!

In conclusion, this Pumpkin Spice Pull-Apart Bread recipe is a delightful way to celebrate the flavors of fall. The combination of pumpkin and warm spices creates a comforting treat that’s perfect for chilly autumn days. So, why not indulge in a slice (or two) of this delicious bread and savor the essence of the season? Enjoy every pull-apart moment!
