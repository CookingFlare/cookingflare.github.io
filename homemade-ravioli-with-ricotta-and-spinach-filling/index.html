Homemade Ravioli with Ricotta and Spinach Filling


There’s something truly satisfying about making your own pasta from scratch. The process might seem daunting at first, but trust me, it’s worth the effort. Today, I’m going to share with you my favorite recipe for homemade ravioli with a delightful ricotta and spinach filling. This dish is perfect for a cozy dinner at home or for impressing your guests with your culinary skills. So let’s get started!

Ingredients

For the pasta dough:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 3 large eggs

For the filling:

  • 1 cup ricotta cheese
  • 1 cup cooked spinach, drained and chopped
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/4 teaspoon garlic powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste

For the sauce:

  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh basil leaves
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese

Instructions

  1. Prepare the pasta dough: In a large mixing bowl, combine the flour and eggs. Use your hands to mix until a dough forms. Knead the dough on a floured surface for about 5 minutes until it becomes smooth and elastic. Wrap the dough in plastic wrap and let it rest for 30 minutes.

  2. Make the filling: In a bowl, mix together the ricotta cheese, cooked spinach, grated Parmesan cheese, garlic powder, salt, and pepper. Stir until well combined.

  3. Roll out the dough: Divide the dough into four equal portions. Take one portion and flatten it with your hands. Using a rolling pin, roll out the dough into a thin sheet, about 1/8 inch thick. Repeat with the remaining portions of dough.

  4. Fill the ravioli: Spoon about a teaspoon of the filling onto one half of the rolled-out dough, leaving some space between each spoonful. Fold the other half of the dough over the filling. Press down around each spoonful of filling to seal the ravioli. Use a sharp knife or a ravioli cutter to cut out the individual ravioli.

  5. Cook the ravioli: Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Carefully drop the ravioli into the boiling water and cook for about 2-3 minutes, or until they float to the surface. Remove the cooked ravioli with a slotted spoon and transfer them to a plate.

  6. Make the sauce: In a small saucepan, melt the butter over medium heat. Add the chopped fresh basil leaves and cook for about 1 minute until fragrant. Remove the saucepan from heat.

  7. Serve and enjoy: Place the cooked ravioli on serving plates and drizzle the butter and basil sauce over them. Sprinkle with grated Parmesan cheese for extra flavor. Serve immediately and savor the deliciousness!

Conclusion

Homemade ravioli with ricotta and spinach filling is a labor of love that yields incredible results. The tender pasta, combined with the creamy ricotta and flavorful spinach, is a true delight for the taste buds. Don’t be intimidated by the idea of making your own pasta - with a little practice, you’ll become a pro in no time. So, roll up your sleeves, gather your ingredients, and embark on a culinary adventure that will leave everyone craving for more. Enjoy your homemade ravioli experience!
