Savory and Creamy Buffalo Chicken Bread Bowl Dip


Are you ready to take your game day snacks to the next level? Look no further than this mouthwatering Savory and Creamy Buffalo Chicken Bread Bowl Dip recipe. Packed with bold flavors and a creamy texture, this dip is sure to be a crowd-pleaser. And the best part? It’s incredibly easy to make!

Ingredients

  • 2 cups cooked chicken, shredded
  • 1 cup cream cheese, softened
  • 1/2 cup sour cream
  • 1/2 cup buffalo sauce
  • 1/2 cup ranch dressing
  • 1 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1/4 cup chopped green onions
  • 1 round bread loaf, hollowed out (sourdough or Italian bread works well)
  • Optional: additional chopped green onions for garnish

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C).

  2. In a large mixing bowl, combine the cooked chicken, cream cheese, sour cream, buffalo sauce, ranch dressing, shredded cheddar cheese, and chopped green onions. Mix well until all the ingredients are evenly incorporated.

  3. Spoon the mixture into the hollowed-out bread loaf, making sure to fill it up completely. Don’t worry if it seems like a lot of dip – the bread will soak up some of the moisture, resulting in a deliciously creamy texture.

  4. Wrap the filled bread loaf in aluminum foil, making sure to seal it tightly. Place the wrapped loaf on a baking sheet and bake in the preheated oven for about 25-30 minutes, or until the dip is heated through and the cheese is melted and bubbly.

  5. Remove the foil from the bread loaf and continue baking for an additional 5-10 minutes, or until the top is golden brown and slightly crispy.

  6. Once baked, remove the dip from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes. Garnish with additional chopped green onions, if desired.

  7. To serve, simply tear off pieces of the bread bowl and dip them into the creamy buffalo chicken mixture. Alternatively, you can also provide tortilla chips, celery sticks, or carrot sticks as dippers.

Tips and Variations

  • If you prefer a spicier dip, feel free to increase the amount of buffalo sauce or add some hot sauce to the mixture.
  • For a milder version, reduce the amount of buffalo sauce or replace it with barbecue sauce.
  • If you’re short on time, you can use rotisserie chicken instead of cooking the chicken yourself. Simply shred the chicken and proceed with the recipe as directed.
  • This dip can be prepared ahead of time and refrigerated until ready to bake. Just make sure to allow it to come to room temperature before baking.
  • Leftovers can be stored in an airtight container in the refrigerator for up to 3 days. Simply reheat in the oven or microwave before serving.

Conclusion

There you have it – a delicious and easy-to-make Savory and Creamy Buffalo Chicken Bread Bowl Dip recipe that will elevate your game day snacking experience. The combination of buffalo sauce, cream cheese, and shredded chicken creates a flavor explosion that will have your taste buds dancing. So gather your friends and family, and get ready to enjoy this irresistible dip. Trust us, it will be a touchdown at any party or gathering!
