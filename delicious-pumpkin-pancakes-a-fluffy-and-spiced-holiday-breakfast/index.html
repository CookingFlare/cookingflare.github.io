Delicious Pumpkin Pancakes: A Fluffy and Spiced Holiday Breakfast


Who doesn’t love a warm and comforting breakfast on a holiday morning? These delicious pumpkin pancakes are the perfect way to start your day with a touch of seasonal flavor. Packed with the goodness of pumpkin and a blend of warm spices, these fluffy pancakes are sure to become a family favorite. So, let’s dive into the recipe and bring the cozy aroma of pumpkin and spices to your holiday breakfast table.

Ingredients:

  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 3 tablespoons brown sugar
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground ginger
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground nutmeg
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground cloves
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1/2 cup pumpkin puree
  • 1 large egg
  • 2 tablespoons melted butter
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Instructions:

  1. In a large bowl, whisk together the flour, brown sugar, baking powder, baking soda, salt, cinnamon, ginger, nutmeg, and cloves.

  2. In another bowl, combine the milk, pumpkin puree, egg, melted butter, and vanilla extract. Whisk until smooth and well combined.

  3. Pour the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients and stir until just combined. Be careful not to overmix the batter; a few lumps are okay.

  4. Heat a lightly oiled griddle or non-stick skillet over medium heat. Pour 1/4 cup of batter for each pancake onto the griddle. Cook until bubbles form on the surface of the pancakes and the edges start to look set, then flip and cook until golden brown on the other side.

  5. Serve the pancakes warm with your favorite toppings such as maple syrup, whipped cream, or chopped nuts.

Tips for Perfect Pumpkin Pancakes:

  • For extra fluffy pancakes, let the batter rest for 5-10 minutes before cooking.
  • If you prefer a thinner batter, you can adjust the consistency by adding a little more milk.
  • To keep the pancakes warm while you cook the entire batch, place them on a baking sheet in a 200°F oven.

Why Pumpkin Pancakes?

Pumpkin isn’t just for pies; it’s a versatile ingredient that adds moisture, flavor, and nutrition to a variety of dishes. These pumpkin pancakes are not only delicious but also a great way to incorporate the benefits of pumpkin into your breakfast. Rich in fiber, vitamins, and antioxidants, pumpkin is a nutritious addition to your morning meal. Plus, the warm spices like cinnamon, ginger, and nutmeg evoke the cozy feeling of the holiday season, making these pancakes a perfect treat for special occasions.

So, this holiday season, treat yourself and your loved ones to a delightful breakfast with these fluffy and spiced pumpkin pancakes. Whether it’s Thanksgiving, Christmas, or a leisurely weekend morning, these pancakes are a wonderful way to celebrate the season and create lasting memories around the breakfast table. Enjoy every bite of these flavorful pancakes and savor the joys of the holiday season. Happy cooking!
