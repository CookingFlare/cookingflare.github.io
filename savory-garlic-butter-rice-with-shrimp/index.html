Savory Garlic Butter Rice with Shrimp


If you’re a fan of seafood and love the rich flavors of garlic and butter, then this savory garlic butter rice with shrimp recipe is a must-try for your next dinner. It’s a delightful dish that combines the succulent taste of shrimp with the aromatic essence of garlic and the creamy goodness of butter, all served over a bed of fluffy, flavorful rice. This recipe is perfect for a special dinner at home or for entertaining guests. Let’s dive into the details of how to create this mouthwatering dish.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound of large shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 2 cups of long-grain white rice
  • 4 cups of chicken or vegetable broth
  • 4 tablespoons of unsalted butter
  • 4 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon of salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon of black pepper
  • 1/4 cup of fresh parsley, chopped
  • 1 lemon, sliced for garnish

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Shrimp: In a large skillet, melt 2 tablespoons of butter over medium-high heat. Add the minced garlic and sauté for about a minute until fragrant. Add the shrimp to the skillet and cook for 2-3 minutes on each side until they turn pink and opaque. Once cooked, transfer the shrimp to a plate and set aside.

  2. Cook the Rice: In a separate large pot, melt the remaining 2 tablespoons of butter over medium heat. Add the rice and stir to coat the grains with butter. Cook the rice for 2-3 minutes, stirring occasionally, until it starts to turn slightly golden brown. This helps to enhance the nutty flavor of the rice.

  3. Add the Broth: Pour the chicken or vegetable broth into the pot with the rice. Season with salt and pepper, then bring the mixture to a boil. Once boiling, reduce the heat to low, cover the pot, and let the rice simmer for 18-20 minutes, or until all the liquid is absorbed and the rice is tender.

  4. Combine the Shrimp and Rice: Once the rice is cooked, gently fold the cooked shrimp and chopped parsley into the rice, ensuring that the shrimp is evenly distributed throughout the dish. The residual heat will warm the shrimp while infusing the flavors into the rice.

  5. Serve and Garnish: Transfer the savory garlic butter rice with shrimp to a serving platter or individual plates. Garnish with slices of fresh lemon for a burst of citrusy brightness. This will complement the richness of the dish beautifully.

Tips and Variations

  • For added depth of flavor, consider using seafood broth instead of chicken or vegetable broth.
  • Feel free to customize the recipe by adding your favorite vegetables, such as bell peppers, peas, or asparagus, during the cooking process.
  • To make this dish spicy, add a pinch of red pepper flakes or a dash of hot sauce for a kick of heat.

Conclusion

This savory garlic butter rice with shrimp recipe is a delightful combination of succulent seafood and aromatic, buttery rice. The flavors meld together beautifully to create a dish that is both comforting and sophisticated. Whether you’re cooking for a special occasion or simply craving a restaurant-quality meal at home, this recipe is sure to impress. Enjoy the delightful flavors of the sea and the richness of garlic and butter in every satisfying bite!
