Nut-Free Chocolate Chip Granola Bars: A Healthy and Portable Snack


Are you looking for a delicious and healthy snack that you can take on the go? Look no further than these nut-free chocolate chip granola bars! Packed with wholesome ingredients and bursting with chocolatey goodness, these granola bars are not only a treat for your taste buds but also a convenient snack for any time of the day. Whether you need a quick energy boost during your busy workday or a satisfying post-workout snack, these nut-free chocolate chip granola bars have got you covered.

Ingredients

Here’s what you’ll need to make these delightful granola bars:

  • 2 1/2 cups rolled oats
  • 1/2 cup honey or maple syrup
  • 1/3 cup coconut oil
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup chocolate chips
  • 1/4 cup dried cranberries or raisins
  • 1/4 cup sunflower seeds
  • 1/4 cup pumpkin seeds

Instructions

  1. Preparation: Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C) and line a baking dish with parchment paper.

  2. Mixing the Base: In a large bowl, combine the rolled oats, dried cranberries, sunflower seeds, pumpkin seeds, and chocolate chips. Set this mixture aside.

  3. Creating the Binding: In a small saucepan, heat the honey or maple syrup, coconut oil, vanilla extract, and salt over low heat. Stir the mixture until everything is well combined and the coconut oil has melted.

  4. Combining the Ingredients: Pour the liquid mixture over the dry ingredients and stir until everything is evenly coated.

  5. Pressing into the Pan: Transfer the mixture to the prepared baking dish and press it down firmly using the back of a spoon or your hands.

  6. Baking: Bake the granola bars in the preheated oven for 20-25 minutes, or until the edges are golden brown.

  7. Cooling and Cutting: Allow the granola bars to cool completely in the pan before lifting them out using the parchment paper and cutting them into bars of your desired size.

  8. Storage: Store the nut-free chocolate chip granola bars in an airtight container at room temperature for up to a week, or refrigerate them for longer shelf life.

Why Nut-Free?

Many people have nut allergies, making nut-free snacks a necessity for them. By omitting nuts from this recipe, we ensure that everyone can enjoy these delicious granola bars without any worries. The combination of seeds and dried fruit not only adds a delightful texture and flavor but also provides essential nutrients, making these bars a well-rounded snack option.

Health Benefits

These nut-free chocolate chip granola bars are not only delectable but also offer a range of health benefits. Rolled oats are a great source of fiber and can help support heart health. Additionally, the seeds in this recipe provide healthy fats, protein, and various vitamins and minerals. The inclusion of dark chocolate chips adds a dose of antioxidants and a rich, indulgent flavor. With no artificial preservatives or additives, these homemade granola bars are a wholesome alternative to store-bought snacks.

Conclusion

In conclusion, these nut-free chocolate chip granola bars are the perfect solution for anyone seeking a healthy and portable snack option. Whether you need a quick bite to keep you going through a busy day or a satisfying treat after a workout, these granola bars are a delightful choice. By making a batch of these granola bars at home, you can ensure that you always have a nutritious and delicious snack at your fingertips. Enjoy the convenience and goodness of these homemade granola bars, and say goodbye to store-bought snacks for good!

So, what are you waiting for? Gather the ingredients, follow the simple steps, and treat yourself to these irresistible nut-free chocolate chip granola bars. Your taste buds and your body will thank you!
