Light and Fresh Low Carb Greek Shrimp Salad


Are you craving a light and refreshing meal that’s bursting with Mediterranean flavors? Look no further than this delightful Low Carb Greek Shrimp Salad recipe. Packed with protein, healthy fats, and a variety of vibrant vegetables, this dish is perfect for those looking to enjoy a satisfying, low-carb meal without sacrificing flavor. Whether you’re hosting a summer gathering or simply want to treat yourself to a nutritious and delicious meal, this Greek shrimp salad is sure to impress. Let’s dive into the details of this delightful recipe!

Ingredients

For the Salad:

  • 1 pound of large shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 1 English cucumber, diced
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1/2 red onion, thinly sliced
  • 1/2 cup Kalamata olives, pitted and halved
  • 1/4 cup crumbled feta cheese
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley

For the Dressing:

  • 1/4 cup extra virgin olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons red wine vinegar
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

Step 1: Prepare the Shrimp

  1. In a large pot of boiling salted water, cook the shrimp for 2-3 minutes or until they turn pink and opaque.
  2. Drain the shrimp and rinse with cold water to stop the cooking process. Set aside.

Step 2: Make the Dressing

  1. In a small bowl, whisk together the olive oil, red wine vinegar, dried oregano, minced garlic, salt, and pepper until well combined. Set aside.

Step 3: Assemble the Salad

  1. In a large mixing bowl, combine the cooked shrimp, diced cucumber, halved cherry tomatoes, sliced red onion, Kalamata olives, and crumbled feta cheese.
  2. Drizzle the dressing over the salad and gently toss to coat all the ingredients.
  3. Sprinkle the chopped fresh parsley over the salad as a finishing touch.

Serve the Low Carb Greek Shrimp Salad immediately, and enjoy the delightful combination of flavors and textures.

Nutritional Benefits

This Low Carb Greek Shrimp Salad is not only a feast for the taste buds but also a nutritious choice for a well-balanced meal. The shrimp provides a lean source of protein, while the olive oil and feta cheese contribute heart-healthy fats. Additionally, the abundance of fresh vegetables offers essential vitamins and minerals, making this salad a wholesome and satisfying option for those following a low-carb lifestyle.

Serving Suggestions

This salad can be enjoyed on its own as a light and satisfying meal, or it can be served as a side dish alongside grilled fish or chicken. Feel free to customize the salad by adding your favorite ingredients, such as avocado, artichoke hearts, or bell peppers, to elevate the flavors even further.

Final Thoughts

With its vibrant colors, bold flavors, and nutritional benefits, this Light and Fresh Low Carb Greek Shrimp Salad is a must-try for anyone seeking a delightful and healthy meal option. Whether you’re a fan of Mediterranean cuisine or simply looking for a new recipe to add to your repertoire, this salad is sure to become a favorite. Embrace the simplicity and freshness of this dish, and savor each bite as you indulge in the goodness of this Low Carb Greek Shrimp Salad.
