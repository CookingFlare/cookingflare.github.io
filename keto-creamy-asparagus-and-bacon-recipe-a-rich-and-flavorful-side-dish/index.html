Keto Creamy Asparagus and Bacon Recipe: A Rich and Flavorful Side Dish


If you’re looking for a delicious and satisfying side dish to complement your keto meal, this creamy asparagus and bacon recipe is a must-try. Packed with flavor and nutrients, this dish is a perfect addition to your low-carb, high-fat diet. The combination of tender asparagus, crispy bacon, and creamy sauce will tantalize your taste buds and leave you wanting more. Let’s dive into the details of this mouthwatering keto-friendly recipe.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound fresh asparagus, trimmed
  • 6 slices bacon, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Asparagus: Begin by washing the asparagus spears and trimming off the tough ends. Cut the asparagus into 2-inch pieces and set aside.

  2. Cook the Bacon: In a large skillet over medium heat, cook the chopped bacon until it becomes crispy and golden brown. Use a slotted spoon to transfer the cooked bacon to a plate lined with paper towels, leaving the bacon drippings in the skillet.

  3. Sauté the Asparagus: In the same skillet with the bacon drippings, add the minced garlic and sauté for about 30 seconds until fragrant. Then, add the asparagus pieces to the skillet and cook for 5-7 minutes, or until they are tender-crisp.

  4. Prepare the Creamy Sauce: Pour the heavy cream into the skillet with the asparagus and garlic. Bring the mixture to a gentle simmer, stirring occasionally. Once the cream begins to thicken slightly, add the grated Parmesan cheese and stir until it melts and the sauce becomes creamy. Season with salt and pepper to taste.

  5. Combine the Ingredients: Return the cooked bacon to the skillet and gently toss everything together to ensure that the bacon and asparagus are evenly coated with the creamy sauce.

  6. Finish and Serve: Sprinkle the chopped fresh parsley over the creamy asparagus and bacon before serving. The vibrant green asparagus, the rich and creamy sauce, and the savory bacon combine to create a visually appealing and delicious side dish that is sure to impress.

Nutritional Information

This keto-friendly creamy asparagus and bacon dish not only tastes incredible but also offers a nutritious boost. Asparagus is a good source of fiber, folate, and vitamins A, C, and K. Paired with the protein and healthy fats from the bacon and cream, this dish provides a well-rounded balance of macronutrients to support your keto lifestyle.

  • Calories: Approximately 320 per serving
  • Total Fat: 28g
  • Saturated Fat: 14g
  • Cholesterol: 75mg
  • Sodium: 450mg
  • Total Carbohydrates: 6g
  • Dietary Fiber: 2g
  • Protein: 12g

Serving Suggestions

This creamy asparagus and bacon dish pairs beautifully with a variety of keto entrees. Consider serving it alongside grilled chicken, pan-seared salmon, or a juicy steak for a complete and satisfying meal. The creamy sauce also makes it a great accompaniment to cauliflower rice or zucchini noodles, adding even more depth and flavor to your low-carb dining experience.

Conclusion

With its luscious creamy sauce, tender-crisp asparagus, and savory bacon, this keto-friendly side dish is a true delight for the senses. Whether you’re following a ketogenic diet or simply looking for a delicious way to enjoy asparagus, this recipe is a winner. Impress your family and guests with this rich and flavorful dish that proves that keto cooking can be both indulgent and wholesome. Enjoy every mouthful of this creamy asparagus and bacon recipe, and savor the satisfaction of a well-executed keto culinary masterpiece.
