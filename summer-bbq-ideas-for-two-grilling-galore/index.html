Summer BBQ Ideas for Two: Grilling Galore


Looking for some sizzling summer BBQ ideas for you and your special someone? Look no further! We’ve got you covered with these mouthwatering recipes that are perfect for two. Fire up the grill and get ready for a grilling galore!

1. Grilled Lemon Herb Chicken Skewers

Ingredients:

  • 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1 lemon, juiced and zested
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons of fresh herbs (such as thyme, rosemary, or basil), chopped
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Skewers

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the grill to medium-high heat.
  2. Cut the chicken breasts into bite-sized pieces and thread them onto the skewers.
  3. In a small bowl, combine the lemon juice, lemon zest, minced garlic, chopped herbs, salt, and pepper.
  4. Brush the marinade onto the chicken skewers, making sure to coat them evenly.
  5. Grill the skewers for about 8-10 minutes, turning occasionally, until the chicken is cooked through and has a nice charred exterior.
  6. Serve hot and enjoy!

2. Grilled Shrimp Tacos with Mango Salsa

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 pound of large shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 1 tablespoon of olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon of chili powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon of cumin
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 4 small flour tortillas
  • 1 mango, diced
  • 1/2 red onion, diced
  • 1/4 cup of fresh cilantro, chopped
  • Juice of 1 lime

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the grill to medium heat.
  2. In a bowl, toss the shrimp with olive oil, chili powder, cumin, salt, and pepper until evenly coated.
  3. Grill the shrimp for 2-3 minutes per side, until they turn pink and are cooked through.
  4. Warm the tortillas on the grill for about 30 seconds on each side.
  5. In a separate bowl, combine the diced mango, red onion, cilantro, and lime juice to make the salsa.
  6. Assemble the tacos by placing some grilled shrimp on each tortilla and topping with the mango salsa.
  7. Serve immediately and enjoy the burst of flavors!

3. Grilled Vegetable Skewers with Balsamic Glaze

Ingredients:

  • Assorted vegetables of your choice (such as bell peppers, zucchini, mushrooms, and cherry tomatoes), cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 2 tablespoons of olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons of balsamic vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon of honey
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the grill to medium-high heat.
  2. Thread the vegetable pieces onto skewers, alternating between different vegetables.
  3. In a small bowl, whisk together the olive oil, balsamic vinegar, honey, salt, and pepper to make the glaze.
  4. Brush the glaze onto the vegetable skewers, making sure to coat them evenly.
  5. Grill the skewers for about 10-12 minutes, turning occasionally, until the vegetables are tender and slightly charred.
  6. Remove from the grill and serve as a side dish or as a vegetarian main course.

4. Grilled Peaches with Vanilla Ice Cream

Ingredients:

  • 2 ripe peaches, halved and pitted
  • 1 tablespoon of honey
  • 1/2 teaspoon of cinnamon
  • Vanilla ice cream

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the grill to medium heat.
  2. Place the peach halves cut-side down on the grill and cook for about 3-4 minutes, until they have grill marks.
  3. Flip the peaches over and drizzle the honey and sprinkle the cinnamon over the top.
  4. Grill for an additional 3-4 minutes, until the peaches are soft and caramelized.
  5. Remove from the grill and serve the grilled peaches with a scoop of vanilla ice cream on top.
  6. Indulge in this delightful summer dessert!

These summer BBQ ideas for two will surely make your outdoor dining experience unforgettable. So grab your apron, fire up the grill, and enjoy a delicious meal with your loved one. Happy grilling!
