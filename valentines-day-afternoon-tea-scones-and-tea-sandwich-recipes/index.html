Valentine's Day Afternoon Tea: Scones and Tea Sandwich Recipes


Valentine’s Day is the perfect occasion to indulge in a romantic afternoon tea with your loved one. And what better way to celebrate than by baking some delicious scones and preparing delightful tea sandwiches? In this blog post, we will share two delectable recipes that are sure to impress your special someone. So let’s get started!

Classic Scones

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 tablespoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, cold and cubed
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/2 cup dried cranberries or chocolate chips (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 425°F (220°C) and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
  2. In a large bowl, whisk together the flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt.
  3. Add the cold cubed butter to the dry ingredients. Using a pastry cutter or your fingertips, cut the butter into the flour mixture until it resembles coarse crumbs.
  4. In a separate bowl, combine the milk and vanilla extract. Gradually pour the milk mixture into the dry ingredients, stirring until just combined.
  5. If desired, fold in dried cranberries or chocolate chips for an extra touch of flavor.
  6. Turn the dough out onto a lightly floured surface and gently knead it a few times until it comes together. Be careful not to overwork the dough, as this can result in tough scones.
  7. Pat the dough into a 1-inch thick circle and cut out scones using a round biscuit cutter.
  8. Place the scones onto the prepared baking sheet and bake for 12-15 minutes, or until they are golden brown.
  9. Allow the scones to cool on a wire rack before serving. Serve with clotted cream, jam, and a piping hot cup of tea.

Cucumber and Cream Cheese Tea Sandwiches

Ingredients:

  • 8 slices of white bread
  • 1 cucumber, thinly sliced
  • 4 ounces cream cheese, softened
  • Fresh dill, chopped
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Trim the crusts from the bread slices and lay them out on a clean surface.
  2. Spread a thin layer of softened cream cheese onto each slice of bread.
  3. Arrange the thinly sliced cucumbers evenly over half of the bread slices.
  4. Sprinkle fresh dill, salt, and pepper over the cucumber slices.
  5. Place the remaining bread slices on top to create sandwiches.
  6. Using a sharp knife, cut the sandwiches into desired shapes, such as triangles or rectangles.
  7. Serve the tea sandwiches immediately or cover them with a damp paper towel and refrigerate until ready to serve.

Conclusion

Valentine’s Day Afternoon Tea is a delightful way to show your love and appreciation for your significant other. By preparing these scrumptious scones and refreshing tea sandwiches, you can create a romantic atmosphere and enjoy a memorable experience together. So grab your apron, gather the ingredients, and get ready to impress your loved one with these delicious treats. Happy Valentine’s Day!
