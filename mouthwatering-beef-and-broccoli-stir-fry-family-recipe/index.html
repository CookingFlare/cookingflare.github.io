Mouthwatering Beef and Broccoli Stir-Fry: Family Recipe


There’s something truly special about a homemade stir-fry that brings the family together around the dinner table. This mouthwatering Beef and Broccoli Stir-Fry recipe has been passed down through generations in our family, and today, I’m excited to share it with you. Packed with flavor, tender beef, and crisp broccoli, this dish is a surefire way to elevate your weeknight dinner routine. Let’s dive into the recipe and discover the secrets behind this beloved family dish.

Ingredients

For the Marinade:

  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons oyster sauce
  • 1 tablespoon brown sugar
  • 1 tablespoon rice wine or dry sherry
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon grated fresh ginger
  • 1 teaspoon cornstarch

For the Stir-Fry:

  • 1 1/2 pounds flank steak, thinly sliced against the grain
  • 3 tablespoons vegetable oil, divided
  • 1 pound broccoli, cut into florets
  • 1/2 cup beef broth
  • 2 tablespoons oyster sauce
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 1 teaspoon cornstarch
  • Cooked rice, for serving

Instructions

Step 1: Marinate the Beef

In a bowl, whisk together the soy sauce, oyster sauce, brown sugar, rice wine, garlic, ginger, and cornstarch to create the marinade. Add the sliced flank steak to the marinade, ensuring each piece is well-coated. Let the beef marinate for at least 30 minutes to allow the flavors to infuse.

Step 2: Prepare the Broccoli

While the beef is marinating, blanch the broccoli in a pot of boiling water for 2 minutes, then immediately transfer it to a bowl of ice water to stop the cooking process. Drain the broccoli and set it aside.

Step 3: Sear the Beef

In a wok or large skillet, heat 2 tablespoons of vegetable oil over high heat. Once the oil is hot, add the marinated beef in a single layer, allowing it to sear for 1-2 minutes without stirring. Flip the beef slices and sear the other side for an additional 1-2 minutes, then transfer the beef to a plate.

Step 4: Create the Sauce

In the same wok or skillet, add the remaining tablespoon of vegetable oil. Stir in the blanched broccoli and sauté for 2-3 minutes until it becomes vibrant green and slightly tender. Return the beef to the wok.

Step 5: Finish the Dish

In a small bowl, whisk together the beef broth, oyster sauce, soy sauce, and cornstarch to create the sauce. Pour the sauce over the beef and broccoli, stirring continuously until the sauce thickens and coats the ingredients evenly.

Step 6: Serve and Enjoy

Once the sauce has thickened, remove the wok from the heat. Serve the Beef and Broccoli Stir-Fry over a bed of steamed rice and enjoy the delightful mix of flavors and textures.

Conclusion

This Beef and Broccoli Stir-Fry is a testament to the joy of home-cooked meals and the traditions that bring families together. Whether it’s a busy weeknight or a leisurely weekend dinner, this recipe is a delicious way to add some excitement to your dining experience. Try it out, make it your own, and savor the delightful flavors that have been cherished in our family for years.

So, there you have it - a cherished family recipe that’s sure to become a favorite in your home too. Gather your loved ones, roll up your sleeves, and let the aromas of this Beef and Broccoli Stir-Fry fill your kitchen. Happy cooking!
