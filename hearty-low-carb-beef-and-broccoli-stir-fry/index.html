Hearty Low Carb Beef and Broccoli Stir-Fry


If you’re looking for a delicious and satisfying low carb meal, look no further than this hearty Beef and Broccoli Stir-Fry recipe. Packed with flavor and nutrients, this dish is perfect for those following a low carb diet or simply looking to incorporate more healthy options into their meals. With tender beef, crisp broccoli, and a savory sauce, this stir-fry is sure to be a crowd-pleaser.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound beef sirloin, thinly sliced
  • 2 cups broccoli florets
  • 1 red bell pepper, thinly sliced
  • 1 yellow bell pepper, thinly sliced
  • 1 small onion, thinly sliced
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon grated ginger
  • 2 tablespoons low sodium soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon oyster sauce
  • 1 tablespoon sesame oil
  • 1 teaspoon cornstarch
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil, for cooking

Instructions

  1. In a small bowl, whisk together the soy sauce, oyster sauce, sesame oil, cornstarch, and a pinch of salt and pepper. Set aside.

  2. Heat the vegetable oil in a large skillet or wok over medium-high heat. Add the beef slices and cook until browned, about 2-3 minutes per side. Remove the beef from the skillet and set aside.

  3. In the same skillet, add the minced garlic and grated ginger. Cook for about 1 minute, until fragrant.

  4. Add the sliced onions, bell peppers, and broccoli florets to the skillet. Stir-fry for about 3-4 minutes, until the vegetables are crisp-tender.

  5. Return the beef to the skillet and pour the sauce mixture over the ingredients. Stir well to coat everything evenly.

  6. Continue cooking for another 2-3 minutes, until the sauce has thickened and the beef is cooked through.

  7. Remove from heat and serve hot. You can enjoy this stir-fry on its own or serve it over cauliflower rice for a complete low carb meal.

Tips and Variations

  • For a spicier kick, add a teaspoon of red pepper flakes or a dash of Sriracha sauce to the sauce mixture.
  • Feel free to add other low carb vegetables to the stir-fry, such as mushrooms, snap peas, or zucchini.
  • If you prefer a vegetarian option, you can substitute the beef with tofu or tempeh.
  • To make this dish gluten-free, use tamari sauce instead of soy sauce and ensure that the oyster sauce is also gluten-free.

Why Choose Low Carb?

A low carb diet has gained popularity in recent years due to its potential health benefits. By reducing your intake of carbohydrates, you can stabilize blood sugar levels, promote weight loss, and improve overall health. This Beef and Broccoli Stir-Fry is a perfect example of a low carb meal that doesn’t compromise on taste or satisfaction.

Broccoli is a nutrient-dense vegetable that is low in carbs and high in fiber, vitamins, and minerals. It adds a vibrant green color and a satisfying crunch to this stir-fry. Combined with lean beef, this dish provides a good balance of protein and vegetables, making it a well-rounded meal.

In Conclusion

This Hearty Low Carb Beef and Broccoli Stir-Fry is a delicious and nutritious option for anyone looking to enjoy a flavorful meal while sticking to a low carb diet. With its tender beef, crisp vegetables, and savory sauce, it’s a dish that satisfies both your taste buds and your health goals. So why not give it a try and see how this stir-fry can become a staple in your low carb cooking repertoire? Enjoy!
