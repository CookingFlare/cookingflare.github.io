Caramel Apple Crisp: A Warm and Gooey Fall Dessert


There’s something truly magical about fall. The crisp air, the vibrant foliage, and the comforting flavors that come with the season. And what better way to embrace the autumnal spirit than with a delicious Caramel Apple Crisp? This warm and gooey dessert is the perfect treat to enjoy on a chilly evening, and it’s surprisingly easy to make. So, let’s dive right in!

Ingredients

  • 6 medium-sized apples (preferably Granny Smith)
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup rolled oats
  • 1 cup brown sugar
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground nutmeg
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, melted
  • 1 cup caramel sauce
  • Vanilla ice cream (optional, for serving)

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease a 9x9-inch baking dish and set it aside.

  2. Peel, core, and slice the apples into thin wedges. Place the apple slices in a large mixing bowl.

  3. In a separate bowl, combine the flour, rolled oats, brown sugar, cinnamon, and nutmeg. Mix well to ensure all the ingredients are evenly distributed.

  4. Pour the melted butter over the dry ingredient mixture and stir until the mixture resembles coarse crumbs.

  5. Take half of the crumb mixture and press it firmly into the bottom of the greased baking dish. This will form the base of your apple crisp.

  6. Arrange the apple slices evenly on top of the crumb base.

  7. Drizzle the caramel sauce over the apple slices, making sure to cover them completely.

  8. Sprinkle the remaining crumb mixture over the caramel-coated apples, creating a deliciously crunchy topping.

  9. Place the baking dish in the preheated oven and bake for approximately 45 minutes, or until the top is golden brown and the apples are tender.

  10. Once baked, remove the dish from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes. This will allow the caramel to thicken slightly.

  11. Serve the Caramel Apple Crisp warm, either on its own or with a scoop of vanilla ice cream for an extra indulgence. The combination of the gooey caramel, tender apples, and crispy topping is simply irresistible!

  12. Enjoy the comforting flavors of fall with every spoonful of this delightful dessert.

Tips and Variations

  • You can use any variety of apples for this recipe, but Granny Smith apples are preferred for their tartness, which balances well with the sweetness of the caramel.

  • Feel free to experiment with the spices. Add a pinch of cloves or ginger for a spicier twist, or sprinkle some chopped nuts over the topping for added crunch.

  • Don’t have caramel sauce on hand? No problem! You can easily make your own by gently heating condensed milk with sugar until it turns into a rich, caramel-colored sauce.

  • Leftovers can be stored in an airtight container in the refrigerator for up to three days. Simply reheat in the oven or microwave before serving.

Conclusion

Caramel Apple Crisp is a quintessential fall dessert that embodies the cozy and comforting flavors of the season. With its tender apples, crunchy topping, and luscious caramel sauce, it’s a treat that will warm both your heart and your taste buds. So why not embrace the spirit of fall and indulge in this delightful dessert? Gather your ingredients, follow the simple steps, and get ready to savor the deliciousness of Caramel Apple Crisp. Enjoy!
