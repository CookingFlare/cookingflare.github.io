Top 10 Easy Summer Picnic Recipes for a Perfect Day Out


In the sweltering heat of summer, there’s no better way to make the most of the sunshine than by taking a picnic to your favorite park or beach. Picture a blanket spread out under a shady tree, your feet up, and the kids playing happily nearby – all while feasting on scrumptious, homemade treats. Read on for our top 10 easy summer picnic recipes that’ll make for a delightful day out!

  1. Grilled Chicken and Vegetable Skewers Prep time: 10 minutes Cooking time: 15 minutes

Thread boneless, skinless chicken breasts, cherry tomatoes, zucchini, bell peppers, and red onion on skewers, and grill until the chicken is perfectly cooked. Serve with a zesty dipping sauce for added zest.

  1. Caprese-inspired Salad Prep time: 10 minutes

Slice fresh mozzarella and juicy tomatoes in equal portions and layer them with fresh basil leaves. Drizzle balsamic glaze on top, and you have a crowd-pleasing summer delight!

  1. Summer Quinoa Salad Prep time: 15 minutes

Quench your thirst with a chilled, flavorful quinoa salad featuring cooked quinoa, corn kernels, black beans, diced tomatoes, avocado chunks, and a dollop of lime-cumin dressing. A healthful and satisfying side dish or main meal.

  1. Mini Pesto Pizza Bites Prep time: 20 minutes

Transform refrigerator pizza dough into bite-sized pieces. Top with store-bought pesto and chopped sun-dried tomatoes. Bake until bubbly and crispy.

  1. Fruit Kabobs with Yogurt Dip Prep time: 5 minutes Cooking time: 5 minutes

Load up fruit kabobs with cubes of pineapple, watermelon, strawberries, and blueberries. Grilled briefly for an added smoky dimension. Enjoy with a cool, creamy yogurt dip.

  1. No-Bake Strawberry Cheesecake Bars Prep time: 15 minutes

For dessert, whip together a no-bake cheesecake mixture, layer it with smushed strawberries, and chill until firm. Create the perfect balance of texture and refreshing flavors.

  1. Honey-Glazed Ham Rolls Prep time: 15 minutes

Pick up a pre-cooked ham from the deli counter or prepare one at home. Slice it thinly and serve with a drizzle of honey- mustard glaze tucked in a warm roll.

  1. Pasta Salad with Herbs Prep time: 15 minutes

Combine cooked pasta, chopped veggies, and a sprinkling of herbs like basil and dill in a bowl. Drizzle olive oil and scrutinize for taste. You’ll have a light, tasty dish perfect for your picnic.

  1. Sliced Vegetable Sandwich Prep time: 5 minutes

The sandwich is a versatile creation that can be customized to your liking. Fresh bread filled with slices of cucumber, red onion, avocado, and tomato – don’t forget the vegan mayonnaise spread!

So, the next time you plan to spend some leisurely hours outdoors, equip yourself with these picnic-friendly meals. We’ve ensured that the possibilities provide varied flavor profiles that your friends and family will enjoy. From grilling to chilling, you can make any day a perfect day out. Happy picnic-ing!
