One Pot Creamy Pesto Chicken and Tortellini


If you’re looking for a delicious and easy weeknight dinner that’s packed with flavor, then you’ve come to the right place! This One Pot Creamy Pesto Chicken and Tortellini recipe is a crowd-pleaser that will have your taste buds dancing with joy. With just a few simple ingredients and minimal clean-up, this dish is perfect for busy families or anyone who wants a satisfying meal without spending hours in the kitchen.

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 pound boneless, skinless chicken breasts, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup chicken broth
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1/2 cup basil pesto
  • 1 package (20 ounces) refrigerated cheese tortellini
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Freshly grated Parmesan cheese, for garnish
  • Fresh basil leaves, for garnish

Instructions

  1. Heat the olive oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat. Add the chicken pieces and cook until browned on all sides, about 5 minutes. Remove the chicken from the skillet and set aside.

  2. In the same skillet, add the chopped onion and minced garlic. Cook until the onion is translucent and the garlic is fragrant, about 2 minutes.

  3. Pour in the chicken broth and scrape the bottom of the skillet to release any browned bits. Bring the broth to a boil and let it simmer for 5 minutes to reduce slightly.

  4. Stir in the heavy cream and basil pesto. Return the chicken to the skillet and stir to coat the pieces with the creamy pesto sauce. Season with salt and pepper to taste.

  5. Add the refrigerated cheese tortellini to the skillet, making sure they are submerged in the sauce. Cover the skillet and let the tortellini cook for about 10 minutes, or until they are tender and cooked through.

  6. Once the tortellini are cooked, give everything a gentle stir to combine the flavors. If the sauce is too thick, you can add a splash of chicken broth or cream to thin it out.

  7. Serve the One Pot Creamy Pesto Chicken and Tortellini hot, garnished with freshly grated Parmesan cheese and fresh basil leaves. Pair it with a crisp green salad or some crusty bread for a complete meal.

Tips and Variations

  • Feel free to customize this recipe to suit your taste preferences. You can add some chopped sun-dried tomatoes or roasted red peppers for an extra burst of flavor.
  • If you prefer a lighter version of this dish, you can substitute half-and-half or milk for the heavy cream. The sauce won’t be as rich, but it will still be creamy and delicious.
  • Leftovers can be stored in an airtight container in the refrigerator for up to 3 days. Simply reheat in the microwave or on the stovetop for a quick and satisfying meal.

So there you have it - a simple and mouthwatering recipe for One Pot Creamy Pesto Chicken and Tortellini. With its creamy sauce, tender chicken, and cheesy tortellini, this dish is sure to become a favorite in your household. Give it a try and let your taste buds experience pure bliss!
