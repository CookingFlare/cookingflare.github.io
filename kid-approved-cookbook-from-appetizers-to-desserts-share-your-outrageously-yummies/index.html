Kid-approved Cookbook: From Appetizers to Desserts, Share Your Outrageously Yummies


Kids can be quite picky when it comes to food, and parents often struggle to find meals that their children will not only enjoy but also find appetizing. It’s no wonder why creating a kid-approved cookbook that includes a variety of dishes - from appetizers to desserts - has become a sought-after resource for many families everywhere. In this post, we will share some of the best recipes you can find to make everyone at home happy – and we’re looking for your outrageously yummies to feature!

Appetizers

1. Mini Chicken & Waffles Bites

Want to surprise your little ones with a twist on their favorite chicken nuggets and waffles? These mini chicken & waffles bites will certainly do the trick! Kids will get to enjoy a delightful fusion of sweet and savory flavors.

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound boneless chicken breasts
  • Bisquick or homemade pancake batter
  • Cooking oil
  • Maple syrup (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Cut the chicken breasts into small, bite-sized pieces.
  2. Coat the chicken pieces with the pancake batter and place them in the frying pan with cooking oil on medium heat. Cook until both sides turn golden brown.
  3. Stack the chicken bites on top of mini waffles, drizzle with maple syrup, and enjoy!

2. Veggie Pizzettes

Creating a healthy and appealing appetizer for kids is simple with these mini pizza-style Veggie Pizzettes. They’ll devour their greens while enjoying a meal they love!

Ingredients:

  • Mini pita bread
  • Marinara sauce
  • A variety of veggies (spinach, bell pepper slices, cherry tomatoes, etc.)
  • Shredded mozzarella cheese

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F.
  2. Cut the pita bread into quarters and place them on a baking sheet.
  3. Spread a thin layer of marinara sauce, top with veggies, and sprinkle with shredded mozzarella cheese.
  4. Bake for 8-10 minutes, or until cheese is melted and bubbly. Serve and let your kids create their own mini masterpieces!

Main Dishes

3. Cheesy Taco Pasta Bake

Kids love tacos, so they will definitely be drawn to this pasta bake that brings all the deliciousness one could expect from a taco!

Ingredients:

  • 1 box pasta (elbow or penne)
  • Ground beef
  • Taco seasoning
  • Diced tomatoes
  • Shredded cheese
  • Sour cream (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Cook the pasta according to the package instructions, then drain.
  2. Cook the ground beef and season it with taco seasoning.
  3. Combine cooked pasta, ground beef with sauce, and diced tomatoes in a baking dish.
  4. Top with a generous amount of shredded cheese, and bake for 15-20 minutes, or until the cheese is bubbly and slightly golden. Garnish with sour cream, and let your kids dig in!

4. Sushi Rolls for Home Cooks

Kids love the concept of a yacht, and you can recreate the iconic rolls at home with just a few simple ingredients and some creativity.

Ingredients:

  • Sushi rice
  • Nori sheets
  • Cucumber
  • Carrots
  • Avocado
  • Cream cheese (optional, soften to room temperature before using)

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the sushi rice according to the package instructions.
  2. Lay the nori sheet on a bamboo sushi mat, spread a thin layer of rice evenly, leaving some space at the top edge of the nori sheet.
  3. Place veggies and cream cheese on the bottom half of the rice.
  4. Roll the sushi tightly, and use a damp cloth to secure the edges.
  5. Cut the sushi roll into bite-sized pieces, and let your little ones enjoy their very own sushi experience at home!

Desserts

5. Neapolitan Milkshakes

Incorporate three tasty flavors - strawberry, chocolate, and vanilla - with this delicious Neapolitan Milkshake recipe.customize the flavor combinations or incorporate other sweet treats you know your kids love!

Ingredients:

  • 2 scoops vanilla ice cream
  • 2 scoops chocolate ice cream
  • 2 scoops strawberry ice cream
  • Milk, enough to blend
  • Whipped cream (optional, for topping)
  • Maraschino cherry (optional, for topping)

Instructions:

  1. Grab a blender, add scoops of vanilla, chocolate, and strawberry ice cream before pouring your preferred amount of milk. Blend until smooth and creamy.
  2. Pour the milkshake into a tall glass, then top it off with whipped cream and a cherry if you want to make it extra special!
  3. Grab some fun straws and let your kids decide which color tastes best - there’s sure to be a winner!

6. Magical Marshmallow Pops

This dessert will leave your kids magical with its adorable appearance and sugary taste. You can choose any cookie cutters that match your kids’ favorite characters or cartoon shapes.

Ingredients:

  • Marshmallows
  • Sugar cookies or white cake mix
  • Chocolate or candy melts
  • Sprinkles and candy (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Bake the sugar cookies or prepare the white cake mix according to the package instructions and let it cool. Then, flatten the dough and use cookie cutters to shape your desired form.
  2. Insert a stick or skewer directly into the marshmallow’s center and press gently onto the cookie base.
  3. Melt the chocolate or candy melts and use a fork to drizzle the sauce over the marshmallow pops.
  4. Decorate with sprinkles and candies as desired.

Each of the recipes listed above has been tested and approved by young taste-buds, ensuring the yummyness that kids will love. Whether you’re looking for ways to incorporate more vegetables or are in search of the perfect after-dinner dessert, these dishes are sure to become family favorites in no time. Are you ready to join the kid-approved cookbook movement? Share your outrageously yummies and let’s continue to make mealtimes a happy time for all ages!
