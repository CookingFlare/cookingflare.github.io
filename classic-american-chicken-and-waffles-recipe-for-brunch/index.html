Classic American Chicken and Waffles Recipe for Brunch


Are you ready to elevate your brunch game with a classic American dish that combines the best of both savory and sweet flavors? Look no further than the iconic Chicken and Waffles! This beloved dish has been a staple of Southern cuisine for decades and has made its way onto brunch menus across the country. In this recipe, we’ll guide you through creating the perfect crispy fried chicken paired with fluffy waffles, resulting in a delightful harmony of flavors and textures that will leave your taste buds singing.

Ingredients

For the Fried Chicken:

  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1 cup buttermilk
  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon onion powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Vegetable oil for frying

For the Waffles:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tablespoon baking powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 2 tablespoons granulated sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 3/4 cups milk
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, melted
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

For the Maple Butter:

  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1/4 cup pure maple syrup
  • Pinch of salt

Instructions

For the Fried Chicken:

  1. In a large bowl, pour the buttermilk over the chicken breasts and let them soak for at least 30 minutes.
  2. In another bowl, combine the flour, paprika, garlic powder, onion powder, salt, and pepper.
  3. Heat the vegetable oil in a deep skillet or Dutch oven to 350°F (175°C).
  4. Dredge the buttermilk-soaked chicken breasts in the seasoned flour mixture, ensuring they are evenly coated.
  5. Carefully place the coated chicken breasts into the hot oil and fry for 12-15 minutes, or until golden brown and cooked through.
  6. Once done, transfer the fried chicken to a wire rack set over a baking sheet to drain any excess oil.

For the Waffles:

  1. Preheat your waffle iron according to the manufacturer’s instructions.
  2. In a large bowl, whisk together the flour, baking powder, salt, and sugar.
  3. In another bowl, whisk the eggs, milk, melted butter, and vanilla extract until well combined.
  4. Pour the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients and stir until just combined, being careful not to overmix.
  5. Pour the batter onto the preheated waffle iron and cook according to the manufacturer’s instructions, until golden and crisp.

For the Maple Butter:

  1. In a small bowl, whip together the softened butter, maple syrup, and a pinch of salt until smooth and creamy.

Assembly

Once the fried chicken and waffles are ready, it’s time to assemble this delightful dish. Place a waffle on a plate, top it with a piece of crispy fried chicken, and finish with a dollop of maple butter. Drizzle with additional maple syrup if desired, and serve with your favorite brunch accompaniments such as fresh fruit, hot sauce, or a side of coleslaw.

Conclusion

The classic American Chicken and Waffles is a brunch masterpiece that perfectly balances the crispy, savory goodness of fried chicken with the light, fluffy sweetness of waffles. Whether you’re hosting a weekend brunch with friends or simply treating yourself to a special morning meal, this recipe is sure to impress. So, roll up your sleeves, get that frying oil hot, and prepare to savor every delightful bite of this beloved Southern comfort food. Enjoy!
