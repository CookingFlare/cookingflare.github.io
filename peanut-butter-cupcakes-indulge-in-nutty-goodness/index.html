Peanut Butter Cupcakes: Indulge in Nutty Goodness


Who doesn’t love the creamy, nutty goodness of peanut butter? And when it’s combined with the fluffy sweetness of cupcakes, it’s a match made in dessert heaven. If you’re a fan of both, then you’re in for a treat with these delectable Peanut Butter Cupcakes.

Ingredients

To create these irresistible Peanut Butter Cupcakes, you’ll need the following ingredients:

  • 1 ¾ cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 ½ teaspoons baking powder
  • ½ teaspoon baking soda
  • ¼ teaspoon salt
  • ½ cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1 cup creamy peanut butter
  • 1 ¼ cups granulated sugar
  • 3 large eggs
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 cup buttermilk

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C). Line a muffin tin with cupcake liners and set it aside.

  2. In a medium-sized bowl, whisk together the all-purpose flour, baking powder, baking soda, and salt. Set the dry ingredients aside.

  3. In a separate large bowl, cream together the softened unsalted butter, creamy peanut butter, and granulated sugar until light and fluffy. This usually takes about 2-3 minutes using an electric mixer on medium speed.

  4. Add the eggs, one at a time, beating well after each addition. Make sure to scrape down the sides of the bowl to incorporate all the ingredients.

  5. Stir in the vanilla extract.

  6. Gradually add the dry ingredients to the butter mixture, alternating with the buttermilk. Begin and end with the dry ingredients, mixing until just combined.

  7. Divide the batter evenly among the cupcake liners, filling each about two-thirds full.

  8. Bake the cupcakes in the preheated oven for 18-20 minutes or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.

  9. Once baked, remove the cupcakes from the oven and let them cool in the muffin tin for about 5 minutes. Then transfer them to a wire rack to cool completely.

Frosting

Now that your Peanut Butter Cupcakes are ready, it’s time to add the finishing touch with a delicious frosting. You have a couple of options here:

Option 1: Peanut Butter Buttercream Frosting

  • 1 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1 cup creamy peanut butter
  • 4 cups powdered sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 2-3 tablespoons milk (as needed for consistency)

  1. In a large bowl, beat the softened unsalted butter and creamy peanut butter until well combined and creamy.

  2. Gradually add the powdered sugar, one cup at a time, beating well after each addition.

  3. Stir in the vanilla extract.

  4. Add milk, one tablespoon at a time, until the desired consistency is reached. The frosting should be smooth and spreadable.

  5. Once the cupcakes have cooled completely, frost them generously with the Peanut Butter Buttercream Frosting.

Option 2: Chocolate Ganache Drizzle

  • ½ cup heavy cream
  • 1 cup semi-sweet chocolate chips

  1. In a small saucepan, heat the heavy cream over medium heat until it begins to simmer. Remove from heat.

  2. Add the semi-sweet chocolate chips to the hot cream and let it sit for a minute to melt.

  3. Stir the mixture until smooth and glossy.

  4. Let the ganache cool for a few minutes, then drizzle it over the cooled cupcakes.

Serving and Storage

These Peanut Butter Cupcakes are best enjoyed fresh. Serve them at room temperature and watch as your friends and family indulge in the nutty goodness.

If you have any leftovers, store them in an airtight container at room temperature for up to 3 days. You can also freeze them for up to 2 months. Just make sure to thaw them before serving.

Conclusion

Peanut Butter Cupcakes are the perfect combination of nutty and sweet flavors. Whether you choose to top them with Peanut Butter Buttercream Frosting or a luscious Chocolate Ganache Drizzle, these cupcakes are sure to satisfy your cravings. So go ahead, indulge in the nutty goodness and treat yourself to a batch of these delightful Peanut Butter Cupcakes!
