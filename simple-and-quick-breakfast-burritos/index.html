Simple and Quick Breakfast Burritos


If you’re looking for a delicious and satisfying breakfast that you can whip up in no time, breakfast burritos are the way to go. Packed with protein, veggies, and cheese, these simple and quick breakfast burritos are the perfect way to start your day on a tasty note. Whether you’re rushing out the door or just want a hassle-free morning meal, these breakfast burritos are sure to become a staple in your breakfast routine.

Ingredients

To make these delightful breakfast burritos, you’ll need the following ingredients:

  • 6 large eggs
  • 1/2 cup diced bell peppers
  • 1/2 cup diced onions
  • 1 cup cooked and crumbled breakfast sausage
  • 1 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh cilantro
  • 6 large flour tortillas
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Filling: In a large skillet, heat 1 tablespoon of olive oil over medium heat. Add the diced bell peppers and onions and sauté until they are tender. Then, add the cooked breakfast sausage to the skillet and stir to combine. Remove the mixture from the skillet and set it aside.

  2. Scramble the Eggs: In the same skillet, heat the remaining tablespoon of olive oil over medium heat. Crack the eggs into the skillet and scramble them until they are cooked through. Season the eggs with salt and pepper to taste.

  3. Assemble the Burritos: Lay out the flour tortillas on a clean surface. Spoon the scrambled eggs onto each tortilla, followed by the sausage and veggie mixture. Sprinkle each burrito with a generous amount of shredded cheddar cheese and fresh cilantro.

  4. Roll the Burritos: To roll the burritos, fold the sides of each tortilla over the filling, then roll it up tightly from the bottom, ensuring that the filling is securely tucked inside.

  5. Cook the Burritos: In a clean skillet, heat a bit of olive oil over medium heat. Place the rolled burritos seam-side down in the skillet and cook them for a few minutes on each side until they are golden brown and the cheese is melted.

  6. Serve and Enjoy: Once the burritos are cooked to perfection, remove them from the skillet and place them on a serving platter. You can serve them whole or cut them in half for easy munching. Pair them with your favorite salsa, hot sauce, or sour cream for an extra burst of flavor.

Tips and Variations

  • Make it Spicy: If you like a bit of heat, add some chopped jalapeños or a sprinkle of cayenne pepper to the egg mixture.
  • Vegetarian Option: Skip the sausage and add in more veggies like mushrooms and spinach for a meatless alternative.
  • Freeze for Later: These breakfast burritos freeze beautifully. Once assembled, wrap them individually in foil and store them in the freezer for a quick grab-and-go breakfast option.

There you have it! With just a few simple steps, you can create these delicious and satisfying breakfast burritos that are sure to become a morning favorite. Whether you’re cooking for yourself or a hungry crowd, these burritos are a guaranteed hit. So, go ahead and give this recipe a try, and get ready to elevate your breakfast game!
