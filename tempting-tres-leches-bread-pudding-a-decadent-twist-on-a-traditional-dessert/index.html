Tempting Tres Leches Bread Pudding: A Decadent Twist on a Traditional Dessert


If you’re a dessert lover like me, you’re probably always on the lookout for new and exciting ways to indulge your sweet tooth. Well, look no further because today, I have a recipe that will take your taste buds on a journey of pure delight. Get ready to experience the heavenly flavors of Tres Leches Bread Pudding!

The Perfect Fusion of Two Classics

Tres Leches, a popular Latin American dessert, meets the classic comfort of bread pudding in this decadent twist on a traditional favorite. The result? A mouthwatering treat that will leave you craving for more.

Ingredients

To whip up this luscious dessert, you’ll need the following ingredients:

  • 6 cups of cubed French bread
  • 1 can of evaporated milk
  • 1 can of sweetened condensed milk
  • 1 cup of whole milk
  • 4 large eggs
  • 1 teaspoon of vanilla extract
  • 1/2 teaspoon of ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 teaspoon of nutmeg
  • 1/4 teaspoon of salt
  • Whipped cream (optional, for serving)
  • Fresh berries (optional, for serving)

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C) and grease a baking dish.

  2. Place the cubed French bread in the greased baking dish, ensuring an even layer.

  3. In a mixing bowl, whisk together the evaporated milk, sweetened condensed milk, whole milk, eggs, vanilla extract, ground cinnamon, nutmeg, and salt until well combined.

  4. Pour the milk mixture evenly over the bread cubes, ensuring that every piece is soaked.

  5. Gently press down on the bread cubes with a fork or spatula to ensure they absorb the liquid.

  6. Let the bread pudding sit for about 15 minutes to allow the bread to fully soak up the milk mixture.

  7. Place the baking dish in the preheated oven and bake for approximately 45-50 minutes, or until the bread is golden brown and the pudding is set.

  8. Remove the bread pudding from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes before serving.

  9. Serve the Tres Leches Bread Pudding warm, topped with a dollop of whipped cream and fresh berries for an extra touch of decadence.

Tips and Variations

  • For a twist on the classic recipe, consider adding a tablespoon of rum or a splash of your favorite liqueur to the milk mixture for an extra kick of flavor.
  • Experiment with different types of bread, such as brioche or challah, to add a unique texture and taste to your bread pudding.
  • If you prefer a denser pudding, let the bread soak in the milk mixture for a longer period of time before baking.
  • Leftovers can be stored in the refrigerator for up to three days and enjoyed cold or reheated for a delicious treat any time.

Indulge in Decadence

Tres Leches Bread Pudding is the ultimate fusion of two beloved desserts, combining the rich and moist texture of Tres Leches with the comforting warmth of bread pudding. With its velvety custard-like texture and heavenly flavors, this dessert is sure to impress your taste buds and satisfy your sweet cravings.

So why wait? Gather your ingredients, follow the simple steps, and treat yourself to a slice of pure indulgence. Your taste buds will thank you, and your friends and family will be begging for more. Enjoy!

Note: This recipe serves approximately 8-10 people. Adjust the quantities accordingly if needed.
