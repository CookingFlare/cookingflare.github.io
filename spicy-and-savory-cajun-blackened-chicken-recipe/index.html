Spicy and Savory: Cajun Blackened Chicken Recipe


If you’re a fan of bold, spicy flavors, then you’re in for a treat with this Cajun blackened chicken recipe. This dish is all about bold seasoning and intense heat, creating a flavor profile that is sure to excite your taste buds. Whether you’re hosting a summer barbecue or simply looking to spice up your weeknight dinner, this Cajun blackened chicken is a perfect choice. So, let’s dive into the details and get ready to tantalize your palate!

Ingredients:

  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 2 tablespoons paprika
  • 1 tablespoon garlic powder
  • 1 tablespoon onion powder
  • 1 tablespoon dried oregano
  • 1 tablespoon dried thyme
  • 1 teaspoon cayenne pepper
  • 1 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1 teaspoon white pepper
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, melted

Instructions:

  1. Start by preheating your cast-iron skillet over high heat. It’s essential to use a cast-iron skillet for this recipe, as it will help achieve that perfect blackened crust on the chicken.

  2. While the skillet is heating up, prepare the seasoning mix. In a small bowl, combine the paprika, garlic powder, onion powder, oregano, thyme, cayenne pepper, black pepper, white pepper, and salt. Mix everything together thoroughly to create the Cajun seasoning.

  3. Next, generously coat each chicken breast with the Cajun seasoning mix. Make sure to coat both sides of the chicken evenly, pressing the seasoning into the meat to ensure maximum flavor.

  4. Once the skillet is hot, carefully place the seasoned chicken breasts in the skillet. You’ll want to cook the chicken in batches to ensure even cooking and that beautiful blackened crust. Cook each side for about 2-3 minutes, or until the chicken is cooked through.

  5. As each batch of chicken cooks, transfer it to a serving platter and brush the top of each piece with a generous amount of melted butter. This step not only adds richness to the dish but also helps to mellow out the heat from the Cajun seasoning.

  6. Once all the chicken is cooked and generously buttered, serve it immediately. You can garnish with a sprinkle of fresh chopped parsley for a pop of color and freshness.

Serving Suggestions:

This Cajun blackened chicken is incredibly versatile and pairs well with a variety of side dishes. For a complete Cajun-inspired meal, consider serving it with classic sides like dirty rice, cornbread, or a fresh garden salad. The spicy and savory flavors of the chicken will complement the sides beautifully, creating a well-balanced and satisfying meal.

Final Thoughts:

Cajun blackened chicken is a true flavor explosion that is guaranteed to impress your family and friends. With its bold seasoning and mouthwatering aroma, this dish is perfect for those who love a bit of heat in their meals. Plus, it’s a quick and straightforward recipe that delivers maximum flavor with minimal effort. So, the next time you’re craving something spicy and satisfying, give this Cajun blackened chicken recipe a try - you won’t be disappointed!
