Tasty Vegan Pesto Pasta with Cherry Tomatoes


As the weather starts to warm up, there’s nothing quite like a light and refreshing pasta dish to satisfy our cravings. Today, we’re going to explore a recipe that’s not only delicious but also packed with nutrients - vegan pesto pasta with cherry tomatoes. This dish is perfect for a quick weeknight dinner or a special occasion. In this post, we’ll dive into the world of vegan pesto, explore the benefits of cherry tomatoes, and provide a simple recipe to make this dish at home.

The Magic of Vegan Pesto

Pesto is a classic Italian sauce made from basil, garlic, pine nuts, Parmesan cheese, and olive oil. However, traditional pesto recipes often include Parmesan cheese, which makes it non-vegan. Fear not, dear vegans! We can easily create a vegan version of pesto by substituting the cheese with nutritional yeast. Nutritional yeast has a nutty, cheesy flavor that pairs perfectly with basil and garlic.

The Power of Cherry Tomatoes

Cherry tomatoes are a staple in many kitchens, and for good reason. They’re sweet, tangy, and bursting with flavor. But did you know that cherry tomatoes are also packed with nutrients? They’re an excellent source of vitamin C, lycopene, and potassium. Lycopene is a powerful antioxidant that has been linked to several health benefits, including reducing the risk of certain cancers and heart disease.

The Recipe

Now that we’ve explored the world of vegan pesto and cherry tomatoes, it’s time to bring it all together. Here’s a simple recipe for vegan pesto pasta with cherry tomatoes:

Ingredients:

  • 8 oz. vegan pesto
  • 12 oz. pasta of your choice (we recommend linguine or fettuccine)
  • 1 pint cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh basil leaves for garnish (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Cook the pasta according to the package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a large skillet, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the garlic and cook for 1-2 minutes until fragrant.
  3. Add the cherry tomatoes to the skillet and cook for 3-4 minutes until they start to release their juices.
  4. Add the vegan pesto to the skillet and stir to combine with the cherry tomatoes and garlic. Cook for an additional 1-2 minutes until the sauce has thickened slightly.
  5. Add the cooked pasta to the skillet and toss to combine with the pesto sauce. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  6. Serve hot, garnished with fresh basil leaves if desired.

Tips and Variations

  • Use fresh, high-quality ingredients: The quality of your ingredients will directly impact the flavor and texture of your dish. Choose fresh, ripe cherry tomatoes and good-quality vegan pesto.
  • Experiment with different pasta shapes: While linguine or fettuccine work well with this recipe, feel free to experiment with different pasta shapes. Penne or bowtie pasta could add a fun twist to the dish.
  • Add some protein: If you’re looking to add some protein to your dish, consider adding some sautéed tofu, tempeh, or seitan to the skillet with the cherry tomatoes and garlic.
  • Make it a meal prep: This recipe is perfect for meal prep. Cook the pasta and pesto sauce ahead of time, then assemble the dish just before serving.

Conclusion

Vegan pesto pasta with cherry tomatoes is a dish that’s sure to become a staple in your kitchen. It’s easy to make, packed with nutrients, and bursting with flavor. Whether you’re a vegan or just looking for a healthy and delicious meal option, this recipe is sure to please. So go ahead, give it a try, and enjoy the taste of Italy with a vegan twist!
