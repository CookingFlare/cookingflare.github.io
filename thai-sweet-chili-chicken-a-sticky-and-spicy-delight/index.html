Thai Sweet Chili Chicken: A Sticky and Spicy Delight


If you’re a fan of Thai cuisine, then you’ve probably had the pleasure of tasting the mouthwatering flavors of Thai Sweet Chili Chicken. This dish is a harmonious blend of sweet, spicy, and tangy, creating a truly delightful experience for your taste buds. The sticky glaze that coats the chicken adds a wonderful depth of flavor and makes it incredibly satisfying to eat. In this blog post, I’ll be sharing with you my tried and tested recipe for Thai Sweet Chili Chicken that will surely become a favorite in your household.

Ingredients

To make this delicious Thai Sweet Chili Chicken, you will need the following ingredients:

  • 500g chicken breast, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 1/2 cup cornstarch
  • 1/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon black pepper
  • Vegetable oil, for frying
  • 1/2 cup Thai sweet chili sauce
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons rice vinegar
  • 2 tablespoons honey
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon grated ginger
  • 1/4 teaspoon red pepper flakes (optional)
  • 2 green onions, thinly sliced (for garnish)
  • Sesame seeds (for garnish)

Instructions

  1. In a shallow bowl, mix together the cornstarch, flour, salt, and black pepper. Coat the chicken pieces in the mixture, making sure they are well coated.

  2. Heat vegetable oil in a deep pan or a skillet over medium-high heat. Fry the chicken in batches until golden brown and crispy. Remove the cooked chicken from the oil and drain on a paper towel-lined plate.

  3. In a separate saucepan, combine the Thai sweet chili sauce, soy sauce, rice vinegar, honey, minced garlic, grated ginger, and red pepper flakes (if using). Stir the mixture well and bring it to a simmer over medium heat. Let it simmer for about 5 minutes until it thickens slightly.

  4. Add the fried chicken to the saucepan and toss it gently to coat each piece with the sweet chili sauce. Continue cooking for an additional 2-3 minutes, allowing the chicken to absorb the flavors.

  5. Remove the pan from the heat and transfer the Thai Sweet Chili Chicken to a serving plate. Garnish with sliced green onions and sprinkle with sesame seeds for an added touch of flavor and visual appeal.

  6. Serve the Thai Sweet Chili Chicken hot and enjoy the sticky and spicy delight that awaits you. It pairs perfectly with steamed rice and a side of stir-fried vegetables.

Tips and Variations

  • If you prefer a milder version of Thai Sweet Chili Chicken, reduce the amount of red pepper flakes or omit them altogether.
  • For an extra kick of heat, add some chopped Thai chili peppers to the sauce.
  • You can also customize this recipe by using different proteins such as shrimp or tofu instead of chicken.
  • Feel free to adjust the sweetness and tanginess of the sauce to suit your taste by adding more or less honey and rice vinegar.

Conclusion

Thai Sweet Chili Chicken is a crowd-pleasing dish that brings together the flavors of Thailand in a sticky and spicy glaze. With its perfect balance of sweetness, spiciness, and tanginess, this dish is sure to impress your family and friends. Give this recipe a try and let your taste buds take a trip to the vibrant streets of Thailand. Happy cooking!
