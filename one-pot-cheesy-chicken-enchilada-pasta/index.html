One Pot Cheesy Chicken Enchilada Pasta


If you’re a fan of Mexican flavors and comforting pasta dishes, then you’re in for a treat with this One Pot Cheesy Chicken Enchilada Pasta recipe. It’s a delightful fusion of two beloved cuisines – Italian and Mexican – coming together in one delicious and easy-to-make dish. This recipe is perfect for busy weeknights when you want a satisfying meal without spending hours in the kitchen. Plus, the one-pot aspect means less cleanup – always a win in my book!

Ingredients

Here’s what you’ll need to create this mouthwatering One Pot Cheesy Chicken Enchilada Pasta:

  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 pound boneless, skinless chicken breasts, diced
  • 1 small onion, diced
  • 1 red bell pepper, diced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can (10 oz) red enchilada sauce
  • 1 can (14.5 oz) diced tomatoes
  • 2 cups chicken broth
  • 8 oz uncooked penne pasta
  • 1 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1 cup shredded Monterey Jack cheese
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • Fresh cilantro, for garnish

Instructions

  1. Start by heating the olive oil in a large pot over medium-high heat. Add the diced chicken and cook until it’s no longer pink, making sure to stir occasionally to ensure even cooking.

  2. Once the chicken is cooked through, add the diced onion, red bell pepper, and minced garlic to the pot. Cook for a few minutes until the vegetables soften and become fragrant.

  3. Pour in the red enchilada sauce, diced tomatoes, and chicken broth, stirring everything together to combine. Bring the mixture to a gentle boil.

  4. Add the uncooked penne pasta to the pot, making sure it’s submerged in the liquid. Reduce the heat to a simmer, cover the pot, and let it cook for about 15-20 minutes, or until the pasta is tender, stirring occasionally.

  5. Once the pasta is cooked, stir in the shredded cheddar and Monterey Jack cheeses until they melt and coat the pasta with their gooey goodness. Season with salt and pepper to taste.

  6. To serve, ladle the Cheesy Chicken Enchilada Pasta into bowls and garnish with fresh cilantro for a burst of vibrant flavor and color.

Enjoy Your Culinary Creation

Now that you’ve prepared this delectable One Pot Cheesy Chicken Enchilada Pasta, it’s time to savor every cheesy, flavorful bite. This dish is a crowd-pleaser, perfect for family dinners or casual gatherings with friends. The fusion of spicy enchilada sauce, tender chicken, and cheesy goodness all mingling with al dente pasta creates a truly memorable dining experience.

So, the next time you’re craving a simple yet indulgent meal, consider giving this recipe a try. It’s a testament to the magic that happens when two beloved cuisines come together in perfect harmony. Enjoy!

Note: This recipe yields approximately 4-6 servings, depending on portion size.

In conclusion, this One Pot Cheesy Chicken Enchilada Pasta is a fantastic way to liven up your dinner routine with a fusion of Mexican and Italian flavors. It’s a convenient, one-pot wonder that brings together the best of both worlds, creating a dish that’s sure to become a household favorite. Give it a go and let your taste buds revel in the delightful medley of flavors!
