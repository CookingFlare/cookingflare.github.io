Instant Pot Pasta Recipes: Quick and Easy Dinner Ideas


Are you tired of spending hours in the kitchen preparing dinner after a long day at work? Instant Pot pasta recipes are here to save the day! With the magic of the Instant Pot, you can whip up delicious and satisfying pasta dishes in no time. From creamy mac and cheese to flavorful marinara pasta, the Instant Pot can handle it all. In this post, we’ll explore some mouthwatering Instant Pot pasta recipes that will become your go-to dinner ideas for busy weeknights.

Creamy Garlic Parmesan Pasta

Prep Time: 5 minutes Cook Time: 10 minutes Serves: 4

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound fettuccine
  • 4 cups chicken or vegetable broth
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Place the fettuccine in the Instant Pot and add the chicken or vegetable broth.
  2. Stir in the minced garlic and season with salt and pepper.
  3. Close the lid and set the Instant Pot to manual high pressure for 5 minutes.
  4. Once the cooking is complete, quick release the pressure and open the lid.
  5. Stir in the heavy cream and Parmesan cheese until the sauce is creamy and smooth.
  6. Garnish with fresh parsley and serve hot.

Spicy Tomato and Sausage Pasta

Prep Time: 10 minutes Cook Time: 15 minutes Serves: 6

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound Italian sausage, casing removed
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon red pepper flakes
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 can (28 ounces) crushed tomatoes
  • 1 pound penne pasta
  • 2 cups water
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh basil for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Set the Instant Pot to sauté mode and brown the Italian sausage, breaking it into crumbles.
  2. Add the chopped onion and garlic, and sauté until fragrant.
  3. Stir in the red pepper flakes and dried oregano.
  4. Pour in the crushed tomatoes and add the penne pasta on top.
  5. Add the water and season with salt and pepper.
  6. Close the lid and set the Instant Pot to manual high pressure for 4 minutes.
  7. Once the cooking is complete, quick release the pressure and open the lid.
  8. Garnish with fresh basil and serve hot.

Lemon Garlic Shrimp Pasta

Prep Time: 15 minutes Cook Time: 5 minutes Serves: 4

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound linguine
  • 1 pound large shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • Zest and juice of 2 lemons
  • 1 cup chicken broth
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • Fresh parsley for garnish
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Place the linguine in the Instant Pot and add the shrimp on top.
  2. Stir in the minced garlic, lemon zest, and chicken broth.
  3. Close the lid and set the Instant Pot to manual high pressure for 0 minutes (yes, zero!).
  4. Once the cooking is complete, quick release the pressure and open the lid.
  5. Stir in the heavy cream and Parmesan cheese until the sauce is creamy.
  6. Squeeze the lemon juice over the pasta and season with salt and pepper.
  7. Garnish with fresh parsley and serve hot.

Conclusion

With these quick and easy Instant Pot pasta recipes, you can enjoy a delicious and satisfying dinner without spending hours in the kitchen. The Instant Pot truly revolutionizes the way we cook, making it possible to create flavorful pasta dishes in a fraction of the time. Whether you’re craving a creamy, cheesy pasta or a zesty, spicy one, there’s an Instant Pot pasta recipe for every palate. Try out these recipes and elevate your weeknight dinner game!
