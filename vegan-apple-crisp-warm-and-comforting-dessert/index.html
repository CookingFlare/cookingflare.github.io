Vegan Apple Crisp: Warm and Comforting Dessert


There’s nothing quite like the aroma of warm apples and cinnamon wafting through your home on a crisp autumn day. And when it comes to the perfect comfort dessert, Vegan Apple Crisp takes the crown. This delightful recipe combines the natural sweetness of apples with a crunchy oat topping, creating a dessert that is not only vegan-friendly but also incredibly delicious.

Ingredients

  • 6 medium-sized apples (preferably Granny Smith or Honeycrisp)
  • 1 cup rolled oats
  • 1/2 cup almond flour
  • 1/2 cup coconut sugar
  • 1/4 cup melted coconut oil
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground nutmeg
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease a baking dish with coconut oil or use parchment paper to prevent sticking.

  2. Begin by peeling, coring, and slicing the apples into thin, even slices. Place the slices in a large mixing bowl and drizzle them with lemon juice. Toss gently to coat, ensuring the apples don’t turn brown.

  3. In a separate bowl, combine the rolled oats, almond flour, coconut sugar, melted coconut oil, cinnamon, nutmeg, and salt. Mix well until all the ingredients are evenly incorporated.

  4. Take half of the oat mixture and spread it evenly over the bottom of the greased baking dish. Layer the apple slices on top, making sure they cover the entire surface.

  5. Sprinkle the remaining oat mixture over the apples, ensuring an even distribution. Press down gently to create a cohesive topping.

  6. Bake the Vegan Apple Crisp in the preheated oven for approximately 40-45 minutes, or until the apples are tender and the topping is golden brown.

  7. Once baked, remove the dish from the oven and allow it to cool slightly before serving. This dessert is best enjoyed warm, so let it cool for about 10 minutes.

  8. Serve the Vegan Apple Crisp on its own or with a scoop of vegan vanilla ice cream for an extra indulgent treat. The combination of the sweet, tender apples and the crunchy oat topping is sure to satisfy your dessert cravings.

Tips and Variations

  • Experiment with different types of apples to find your preferred flavor and texture. Granny Smith apples provide a tartness that balances well with the sweetness of the crisp, while Honeycrisp apples offer a juicy and crisp bite.

  • Add a handful of chopped walnuts or pecans to the oat mixture for an extra crunch and nutty flavor.

  • If you prefer a sweeter dessert, increase the amount of coconut sugar in the recipe or drizzle some maple syrup over the apples before adding the oat topping.

  • For a gluten-free version of this Vegan Apple Crisp, ensure that your oats and almond flour are certified gluten-free.

  • Leftovers can be stored in an airtight container in the refrigerator for up to 3 days. Reheat in the oven or microwave before serving.

Whether you’re a vegan or simply looking for a healthier dessert option, this Vegan Apple Crisp is a must-try. Its warm and comforting flavors will make it a hit among friends and family, and you’ll love how easy it is to make. So go ahead and indulge in this delicious treat that celebrates the natural goodness of apples. Enjoy!
