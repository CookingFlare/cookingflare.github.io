Crusty Whole Grain Baguette Recipe


Are you ready to elevate your bread-baking skills with a delicious and wholesome twist? Look no further than this Crusty Whole Grain Baguette recipe! This recipe combines the classic French baguette with the goodness of whole grains, resulting in a crusty exterior and a soft, flavorful interior that will leave you craving for more.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups whole wheat flour
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 ½ cups lukewarm water
  • 2 ¼ tsp active dry yeast
  • 1 ½ tsp salt
  • 1 tbsp honey
  • Olive oil for greasing

Instructions

  1. Activate the Yeast: In a small bowl, combine the lukewarm water, yeast, and honey. Let it sit for about 5-10 minutes until it becomes frothy.

  2. Mix the Flours: In a large mixing bowl, combine the whole wheat flour, all-purpose flour, and salt. Make a well in the center and pour in the activated yeast mixture.

  3. Knead the Dough: Mix the ingredients until a dough forms. Transfer the dough to a floured surface and knead for about 10-15 minutes until it becomes smooth and elastic.

  4. First Rise: Place the dough in a lightly greased bowl, cover with a damp cloth, and let it rise in a warm place for about 1-1.5 hours or until doubled in size.

  5. Shape the Baguettes: Once the dough has risen, gently punch it down and divide it into two equal portions. Shape each portion into a baguette by rolling it out and folding the edges towards the center. Place the shaped baguettes on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper.

  6. Second Rise: Cover the shaped baguettes with a damp cloth and let them rise for another 30-45 minutes.

  7. Preheat the Oven: While the baguettes are rising, preheat your oven to 450°F (230°C) and place a baking pan with water on the bottom rack to create steam.

  8. Score the Baguettes: Using a sharp knife or blade, make 3-4 diagonal cuts on the top of each baguette.

  9. Bake: Place the baguettes in the preheated oven and bake for 20-25 minutes or until they are golden brown and sound hollow when tapped on the bottom.

  10. Cool and Enjoy: Once baked, transfer the baguettes to a wire rack to cool. Slice, serve, and enjoy these crusty whole grain baguettes with your favorite spreads or as a side to soups and salads.

There you have it – a simple yet rewarding recipe for Crusty Whole Grain Baguettes that will impress your family and friends. The combination of whole wheat flour, a touch of honey, and the magic of yeast creates an irresistible aroma and taste that will make this recipe a staple in your baking repertoire. Give it a try and savor the satisfaction of baking your own delicious bread at home!

Remember, the key to a perfect baguette lies in patience and precision. From mixing the dough to scoring the loaves, each step contributes to the final result. So, roll up your sleeves, dust off your apron, and embark on a baking adventure that will fill your kitchen with warmth and the delightful scent of freshly baked bread. Happy baking!
