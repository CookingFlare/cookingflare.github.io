Creamy Scalloped Potatoes Recipe for a Cheesy Side Dish


When it comes to comfort food, few dishes can compete with the creamy goodness of scalloped potatoes. This classic side dish is a favorite at family gatherings and holiday feasts, and for good reason. The combination of tender potatoes, rich cream, and melted cheese creates a dish that is both satisfying and indulgent.

Ingredients

  • 2 pounds Yukon gold potatoes, thinly sliced
  • 2 cups heavy cream
  • 1 cup grated Gruyere cheese
  • 1 cup grated cheddar cheese
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1/4 teaspoon nutmeg

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C). Grease a baking dish with butter or cooking spray and set aside.

  2. In a saucepan over medium heat, melt the butter. Add the minced garlic and cook until fragrant, about 1 minute. Sprinkle in the flour and whisk until smooth, creating a roux.

  3. Slowly pour in the heavy cream while whisking continuously to prevent any lumps from forming. Continue whisking until the mixture thickens, about 5 minutes. Remove from heat.

  4. Add the grated Gruyere cheese and half of the grated cheddar cheese to the cream sauce. Stir until the cheese has melted and the sauce is smooth. Season with salt, black pepper, and nutmeg.

  5. Arrange a single layer of thinly sliced potatoes in the greased baking dish. Pour about one-third of the cheese sauce over the potatoes, ensuring they are evenly coated.

  6. Repeat the process with another layer of potatoes and cheese sauce. Continue layering until all the potatoes and cheese sauce are used, finishing with a layer of cheese sauce on top.

  7. Sprinkle the remaining grated cheddar cheese over the top layer of cheese sauce. This will create a golden and bubbly crust when baked.

  8. Cover the baking dish with aluminum foil and bake for 30 minutes. Remove the foil and bake for an additional 20-25 minutes, or until the potatoes are tender and the top is golden brown.

  9. Once cooked, allow the scalloped potatoes to rest for a few minutes before serving. This will help the dish set and become even creamier.

Serving Suggestions

Creamy scalloped potatoes make a delicious side dish for a wide variety of meals. They pair perfectly with roasted meats such as beef, chicken, or pork. They can also be enjoyed alongside a simple green salad for a lighter meal option.

Tips and Variations

  • For a twist on the classic recipe, try adding thinly sliced onions or cooked bacon between the layers of potatoes.
  • Experiment with different types of cheese to create your own unique flavor combinations. Gouda, fontina, or Parmesan are all great options.
  • If you prefer a crispy top, broil the scalloped potatoes for a few minutes after baking to achieve a golden crust.

In Conclusion

Creamy scalloped potatoes are a timeless dish that never fails to impress. Whether you’re hosting a dinner party or simply craving a comforting side dish, this recipe is sure to satisfy. The combination of tender potatoes, rich cream, and melted cheese creates a taste sensation that will have everyone coming back for seconds. So go ahead, indulge in the creamy goodness of scalloped potatoes and elevate your next meal to a whole new level of deliciousness.
