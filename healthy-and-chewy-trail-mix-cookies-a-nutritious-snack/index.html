Healthy and Chewy Trail Mix Cookies: A Nutritious Snack


Imagine sinking your teeth into a deliciously chewy cookie packed with the goodness of trail mix. These Healthy and Chewy Trail Mix Cookies are not only a delightful treat but also a nutritious snack that will keep you energized throughout the day. Loaded with a variety of wholesome ingredients, these cookies are perfect for those seeking a guilt-free indulgence.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup oats
  • 1 cup whole wheat flour
  • 1/2 cup almond butter
  • 1/4 cup honey
  • 1/4 cup coconut oil
  • 1/4 cup unsweetened applesauce
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup chopped almonds
  • 1/2 cup dried cranberries
  • 1/4 cup dark chocolate chips
  • 1/4 cup shredded coconut

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C) and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
  2. In a large mixing bowl, combine the oats, whole wheat flour, baking soda, and salt. Mix well.
  3. In a separate microwave-safe bowl, combine the almond butter, honey, coconut oil, and unsweetened applesauce. Microwave on high for 30 seconds, or until the mixture is smooth and well combined.
  4. Add the vanilla extract to the wet mixture and stir well.
  5. Pour the wet mixture into the dry ingredients and mix until everything is evenly combined.
  6. Fold in the chopped almonds, dried cranberries, dark chocolate chips, and shredded coconut. The mixture should be thick and sticky.
  7. Using a cookie scoop or your hands, portion out the cookie dough onto the prepared baking sheet. Flatten each cookie slightly with the palm of your hand.
  8. Bake for 12-15 minutes, or until the edges turn golden brown.
  9. Remove from the oven and let the cookies cool on the baking sheet for 5 minutes before transferring them to a wire rack to cool completely.
  10. Store the cookies in an airtight container for up to one week.

Why Choose Trail Mix Cookies?

These Healthy and Chewy Trail Mix Cookies are not your average sweet treat. They are packed with wholesome ingredients that provide a myriad of health benefits. Let’s take a closer look:

  • Oats: Rich in fiber, oats help regulate blood sugar levels, promote heart health, and aid digestion.
  • Whole Wheat Flour: Unlike refined flour, whole wheat flour retains the bran and germ, making it a great source of fiber, vitamins, and minerals.
  • Almond Butter: Packed with healthy fats and protein, almond butter provides sustained energy and promotes satiety.
  • Honey: A natural sweetener, honey adds a touch of sweetness while also providing antioxidants and antibacterial properties.
  • Coconut Oil: This healthy fat improves cholesterol levels and boosts brain function.
  • Unsweetened Applesauce: It adds moisture to the cookies while reducing the need for excessive oil or butter.
  • Almonds: These nuts are a fantastic source of vitamin E, healthy fats, and antioxidants.
  • Dried Cranberries: Bursting with flavor, dried cranberries are high in fiber and contain disease-fighting antioxidants.
  • Dark Chocolate Chips: Rich in antioxidants, dark chocolate improves heart health and enhances mood.
  • Shredded Coconut: It adds a tropical twist to the cookies while providing fiber and healthy fats.

Conclusion

With these Healthy and Chewy Trail Mix Cookies, you can enjoy a guilt-free indulgence while nourishing your body with wholesome ingredients. Whether you’re looking for a quick snack on the go or a treat to satisfy your sweet tooth, these cookies are the perfect choice.

So, why not whip up a batch of these delightful cookies and experience the perfect combination of chewiness, sweetness, and nutrition? Enjoy the goodness of trail mix in every bite and savor a snack that fuels both your body and your taste buds.
