Savory Breakfast Stromboli Recipes to Impress Your Brunch Guests


As we all know, breakfast is the most important meal of the day, and what better way to start the day than with a delicious and savory breakfast stromboli? In this post, we will explore some mouth-watering savory breakfast stromboli recipes that are sure to impress your brunch guests.

Recipe 1: Spinach, Feta, and Sun-dried Tomato Stromboli

For this recipe, you will need:

  • 1 sheet of puff pastry, thawed
  • 1/2 cup of fresh spinach, chopped
  • 1/2 cup of crumbled feta cheese
  • 1/4 cup of sun-dried tomatoes, chopped
  • 1/4 cup of olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C). Roll out the puff pastry and spread the chopped spinach, feta cheese, and sun-dried tomatoes on one half of the pastry, leaving a 1/2 inch border around the edges. Fold the other half of the pastry over the filling and press the edges together to seal. Brush the pastry with olive oil and bake for 20-25 minutes, or until golden brown.

Recipe 2: Mushroom, Bacon, and Cheddar Stromboli

For this recipe, you will need:

  • 1 sheet of puff pastry, thawed
  • 1 cup of sliced mushrooms
  • 6 slices of cooked bacon, crumbled
  • 1/2 cup of cheddar cheese, shredded
  • 1/4 cup of chopped fresh parsley
  • 1/4 cup of olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C). Roll out the puff pastry and spread the sliced mushrooms, crumbled bacon, and shredded cheddar cheese on one half of the pastry, leaving a 1/2 inch border around the edges. Sprinkle the chopped parsley on top of the filling and fold the other half of the pastry over the filling and press the edges together to seal. Brush the pastry with olive oil and bake for 20-25 minutes, or until golden brown.

Recipe 3: Bell Pepper, Onion, and Ham Stromboli

For this recipe, you will need:

  • 1 sheet of puff pastry, thawed
  • 1 cup of sliced bell peppers
  • 1 cup of sliced onions
  • 6 slices of cooked ham, diced
  • 1/2 cup of cheddar cheese, shredded
  • 1/4 cup of chopped fresh parsley
  • 1/4 cup of olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C). Roll out the puff pastry and spread the sliced bell peppers, sliced onions, and diced ham on one half of the pastry, leaving a 1/2 inch border around the edges. Sprinkle the shredded cheddar cheese and chopped parsley on top of the filling and fold the other half of the pastry over the filling and press the edges together to seal. Brush the pastry with olive oil and bake for 20-25 minutes, or until golden brown.

Recipe 4: Asparagus, Prosciutto, and Goat Cheese Stromboli

For this recipe, you will need:

  • 1 sheet of puff pastry, thawed
  • 1 cup of fresh asparagus, trimmed
  • 6 slices of prosciutto, thinly sliced
  • 1/2 cup of crumbled goat cheese
  • 1/4 cup of chopped fresh parsley
  • 1/4 cup of olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C). Roll out the puff pastry and spread the trimmed asparagus, thinly sliced prosciutto, and crumbled goat cheese on one half of the pastry, leaving a 1/2 inch border around the edges. Sprinkle the chopped parsley on top of the filling and fold the other half of the pastry over the filling and press the edges together to seal. Brush the pastry with olive oil and bake for 20-25 minutes, or until golden brown.

Tips and Variations

  • To make the stromboli more crispy, you can brush the pastry with egg wash before baking.
  • You can also add some dried herbs, such as thyme or rosemary, to the filling for extra flavor.
  • If you want to make the stromboli more substantial, you can add some cooked sausage or chicken to the filling.
  • You can also use different types of cheese, such as mozzarella or parmesan, depending on your preference.

In conclusion, these savory breakfast stromboli recipes are sure to impress your brunch guests. With their delicious fillings and crispy pastry, they are the perfect way to start your day. So why not try making one of these recipes for your next brunch gathering? Your guests will love it!
