15-Minute Chicken Parmesan Meatballs Recipe


Are you looking for a quick, easy, and delicious meal to satisfy your family’s cravings? Look no further than these 15-minute chicken parmesan meatballs. This recipe takes the classic flavors of chicken parmesan and transforms them into perfectly portioned meatballs that are sure to be a hit at the dinner table. The best part? You can have this mouthwatering meal ready in just 15 minutes, making it perfect for busy weeknights or whenever you need a satisfying meal in a hurry.

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound ground chicken
  • 1/2 cup breadcrumbs
  • 1/4 cup grated parmesan cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon onion powder
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 cup marinara sauce
  • 1/2 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Fresh basil, for garnish

Instructions:

  1. In a large bowl, combine the ground chicken, breadcrumbs, grated parmesan cheese, egg, garlic powder, onion powder, dried oregano, salt, and black pepper. Use your hands to gently mix everything together until well combined.

  2. Once the mixture is well combined, shape it into meatballs, about 1 1/2 inches in diameter. This should yield approximately 12 meatballs.

  3. In a large skillet, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the meatballs to the skillet and cook for 8-10 minutes, turning occasionally, until they are golden brown and cooked through.

  4. Once the meatballs are cooked, pour the marinara sauce over them in the skillet. Sprinkle the shredded mozzarella cheese over the top. Cover the skillet with a lid and let the cheese melt for 2-3 minutes.

  5. Once the cheese is melted and bubbly, remove the skillet from the heat. Garnish the meatballs with fresh basil and serve immediately.

Tips and Variations:

  • For a spicy kick, add a pinch of red pepper flakes to the meatball mixture.
  • Serve the meatballs over cooked spaghetti or zucchini noodles for a complete meal.
  • Feel free to customize the seasonings to your liking, adding more herbs or spices to suit your taste preferences.

These chicken parmesan meatballs are a fantastic way to enjoy the comforting flavors of a classic chicken parmesan dish in a fraction of the time. Whether you’re cooking for a busy weeknight or hosting a casual gathering, this recipe is sure to impress. Plus, the simplicity of the dish makes it a great opportunity to get the kids involved in the kitchen.
