10 Easy Recipes for Cooking with Ground Meat


Ground meat is one of the most versatile ingredients in the kitchen. Whether you’re working with beef, pork, turkey, chicken, or lamb, ground meat can be transformed into an incredible variety of dishes that are perfect for weeknight dinners, meal prep, or even party appetizers. It cooks quickly, absorbs flavors wonderfully, and often requires minimal ingredients to produce a hearty, satisfying meal. In this post, I’m sharing 10 easy and delicious recipes that highlight the versatility of ground meat, guaranteed to become favorites in your culinary repertoire.

1. Classic Spaghetti Bolognese

A timeless favorite, spaghetti Bolognese is a simple yet hearty dish that gathers family and friends around the table. Using ground beef or a mix of beef and pork, this recipe balances rich tomato flavors with aromatic herbs for a satisfying meal.

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb ground beef or beef/pork mix
  • 1 medium onion, finely chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 can (14 oz) crushed tomatoes
  • 2 tbsp tomato paste
  • 1/2 cup red wine (optional)
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • Spaghetti pasta, cooked

Instructions:

  1. Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Add onion and garlic, sauté until softened.
  2. Add ground meat and cook until browned and no longer pink.
  3. Stir in tomato paste, crushed tomatoes, and red wine. Sprinkle oregano, salt, and pepper.
  4. Reduce heat and let simmer for 20-30 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  5. Serve over cooked spaghetti with a sprinkle of fresh parsley or Parmesan cheese.

2. Savory Ground Meat Tacos

For a quick and crowd-pleasing dinner, these savory ground meat tacos come together in under 30 minutes. Ground beef, turkey, or chicken works well. Customize with your favorite toppings and sauces.

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb ground meat (beef, turkey, or chicken)
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 small onion, diced
  • 2 tsp chili powder
  • 1 tsp cumin
  • 1/2 tsp paprika
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Small corn or flour tortillas
  • Toppings: diced tomatoes, shredded lettuce, chopped cilantro, shredded cheese, sour cream, salsa

Instructions:

  1. Heat olive oil in a skillet and sauté onions until translucent.
  2. Add ground meat and cook until fully browned.
  3. Stir in chili powder, cumin, paprika, salt, and pepper.
  4. Warm tortillas in a pan or microwave.
  5. Spoon meat mixture into tortillas and add desired toppings.

3. Stuffed Bell Peppers with Ground Meat

Bright bell peppers filled with a savory ground meat and rice mixture make for a colorful and nutritious dish that’s perfect for prep-ahead meals or dinner parties.

Ingredients:

  • 4 large bell peppers (any color)
  • 1 lb ground beef or turkey
  • 1 cup cooked rice
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 1 cup tomato sauce
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp dried basil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella or cheddar cheese

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cut tops off bell peppers and remove seeds.
  3. In a skillet, cook onion and ground meat until browned. Drain excess fat.
  4. Add cooked rice, tomato sauce, garlic powder, basil, salt, and pepper. Mix well.
  5. Stuff peppers with the meat mixture, place in baking dish.
  6. Cover with foil and bake 30 minutes.
  7. Remove foil and sprinkle with cheese, bake another 10 minutes until melted.

4. Ground Meat and Vegetable Stir Fry

A fast, nutrient-rich stir fry featuring ground meat paired with crisp vegetables, perfect over steamed rice or noodles.

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb ground pork or chicken
  • 2 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 3 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 cup broccoli florets
  • 1 red bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 carrot, julienned
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp hoisin sauce
  • 1 tsp sesame oil
  • Green onions for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Heat vegetable oil in a wok or large skillet.
  2. Add garlic and cook until fragrant, about 30 seconds.
  3. Add ground meat, cook until fully browned.
  4. Toss in broccoli, red bell pepper, and carrot. Stir fry 4-5 minutes until vegetables are tender-crisp.
  5. Stir in soy sauce, hoisin, and sesame oil. Mix well.
  6. Garnish with green onions and serve over rice or noodles.

5. Ground Meat Meatballs with Herb Yogurt Sauce

Juicy, tender meatballs made with ground beef or lamb, paired with a refreshing herb yogurt sauce. Ideal as an appetizer or part of a main meal.

Ingredients:

Meatballs:

  • 1 lb ground beef or lamb
  • 1/2 cup breadcrumbs
  • 1 egg
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 1 tsp ground cumin
  • Salt and pepper

Yogurt Sauce:

  • 1 cup Greek yogurt
  • 2 tbsp fresh mint, chopped
  • 2 tbsp fresh cilantro, chopped
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • Salt to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 400°F (200°C).
  2. Mix meatball ingredients together, form into small balls.
  3. Place on baking sheet and bake 20 minutes until cooked through.
  4. Combine all yogurt sauce ingredients.
  5. Serve meatballs hot, drizzled or dipped in herb yogurt sauce.

6. Ground Meat and Potato Casserole

A comforting dish layering seasoned ground meat, sliced potatoes, and cheese, baked to golden perfection.

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb ground beef or turkey
  • 4 large potatoes, thinly sliced
  • 1 medium onion, sliced
  • 2 cups shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1 cup beef broth
  • 1 tsp smoked paprika
  • Salt and pepper
  • 2 tbsp olive oil

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Sauté onion in olive oil until soft, add ground meat, season with salt, pepper, and paprika; cook until browned.
  3. In a greased baking dish, layer half the potatoes, top with meat mixture, then remaining potatoes.
  4. Pour beef broth over the top and cover with foil.
  5. Bake 45 minutes, remove foil, sprinkle cheese and bake uncovered another 15 minutes until golden.

7. Asian-Style Lettuce Wraps

Fresh, crunchy lettuce cups filled with seasoned ground meat and a burst of Asian-inspired flavor. Great for light lunches or appetizers.

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb ground chicken or pork
  • 1 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 1 bell pepper, finely diced
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 tbsp grated ginger
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp hoisin sauce
  • 1 tsp rice vinegar
  • 1 head butter lettuce or iceberg leaves, separated
  • Chopped peanuts and green onions for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Heat oil in a pan, fry garlic and ginger until fragrant.
  2. Add ground meat and cook until browned.
  3. Stir in diced bell pepper, soy sauce, hoisin sauce, and rice vinegar.
  4. Cook until vegetables are tender.
  5. Spoon mixture into lettuce leaves, garnish with peanuts and green onions.

8. Homemade Sloppy Joes

A nostalgic favorite, homemade sloppy joes made with ground beef or turkey are savory, saucy, and perfect sandwiched between toasted buns.

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb ground beef or turkey
  • 1 small onion, diced
  • 1/2 cup ketchup
  • 2 tbsp Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 tsp mustard
  • 1 tbsp brown sugar
  • Salt and pepper
  • Hamburger buns

Instructions:

  1. Sauté onion in a skillet until translucent.
  2. Add ground meat, cook until browned.
  3. Stir in ketchup, Worcestershire sauce, mustard, brown sugar, salt, and pepper.
  4. Simmer 10 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  5. Spoon onto buns and serve.

9. Ground Meat Shepherd’s Pie

A classic comfort food, this shepherd’s pie layers savory ground lamb or beef with vegetables and creamy mashed potatoes, baked until bubbly.

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb ground lamb or beef
  • 1 medium onion, chopped
  • 2 carrots, diced
  • 1 cup frozen peas
  • 2 cups mashed potatoes (prepared)
  • 1 tbsp tomato paste
  • 1 cup beef broth
  • Salt and pepper
  • 1 tbsp olive oil

Instructions:

  1. Brown ground meat and onions in olive oil.
  2. Stir in carrots, tomato paste, peas, and beef broth; simmer until sauce thickens, about 10 minutes.
  3. Transfer meat mixture into a baking dish.
  4. Spread mashed potatoes over the top.
  5. Bake at 400°F (200°C) for 20–25 minutes until potatoes are golden.

10. Ground Meat Chili

A warming bowl of chili made easy with ground meat, beans, spices, and tomatoes. Perfect for game day or cozy dinners.

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb ground beef or turkey
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 can (15 oz) kidney beans, drained
  • 1 can (14 oz) diced tomatoes
  • 2 tbsp chili powder
  • 1 tsp cumin
  • 1/2 tsp smoked paprika
  • Salt and pepper
  • 1 tbsp vegetable oil

Instructions:

  1. Heat oil in a large pot, sauté onions and garlic.
  2. Add ground meat, cook until browned.
  3. Stir in chili powder, cumin, paprika, salt, and pepper.
  4. Add beans, tomatoes, and a cup of water or broth.
  5. Simmer for 30 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  6. Serve with sour cream, shredded cheese, and cornbread.

With these 10 easy ground meat recipes, you have a well-rounded collection for all occasions—light weeknight meals, big family dinners, casual gatherings, and even meal prep. Ground meat’s flexibility shines whether it’s combined with pasta, vegetables, or hearty starches. Try swapping different meats or spice blends to keep things fresh and exciting in your kitchen adventure. Bon appétit!
