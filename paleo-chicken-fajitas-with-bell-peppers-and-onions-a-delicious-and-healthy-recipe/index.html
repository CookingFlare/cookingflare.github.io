Paleo Chicken Fajitas with Bell Peppers and Onions: A Delicious and Healthy Recipe


Are you looking for a quick, easy, and healthy recipe that’s perfect for a weeknight dinner or a weekend lunch? Look no further! Today, we’re going to share with you a recipe for Paleo Chicken Fajitas with Bell Peppers and Onions that’s sure to become a staple in your kitchen.

What You Need

  • 1 pound boneless, skinless chicken breasts, cut into thin strips
  • 2 large bell peppers (any color), sliced
  • 1 large onion, sliced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon smoked paprika
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • 4-6 Paleo tortillas (made from almond flour or coconut flour)
  • Optional toppings: avocado, salsa, cilantro, lime wedges

Instructions

  1. In a large skillet, heat the olive oil over medium-high heat.
  2. Add the chicken to the skillet and cook for 5-6 minutes, or until browned and cooked through. Remove the chicken from the skillet and set aside.
  3. Add the sliced bell peppers and onions to the skillet and cook for 5-6 minutes, or until they start to soften.
  4. Add the minced garlic to the skillet and cook for an additional 1-2 minutes, or until fragrant.
  5. Add the cumin and smoked paprika to the skillet and stir to combine.
  6. Add the cooked chicken back into the skillet and stir to combine with the vegetables and spices.
  7. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  8. Warm the Paleo tortillas by wrapping them in a damp paper towel and microwaving for 20-30 seconds.
  9. Assemble the fajitas by placing a portion of the chicken and vegetable mixture onto a tortilla and topping with your desired toppings.

Why This Recipe is a Game-Changer

This Paleo Chicken Fajitas with Bell Peppers and Onions recipe is a game-changer for several reasons. First, it’s incredibly easy to make and requires minimal ingredients. Second, it’s packed with protein and fiber from the chicken and vegetables. Third, it’s a great way to get your daily dose of vitamins and antioxidants from the bell peppers and onions. And finally, it’s a great way to customize your meal to your liking with a variety of toppings.

Tips and Variations

  • Use any color bell pepper you like, or a combination of colors.
  • Add some heat to your fajitas by incorporating diced jalapeños or serrano peppers.
  • Substitute the chicken with shrimp or steak for a different protein option.
  • Add some extra flavor to your fajitas with a squeeze of fresh lime juice or a sprinkle of cilantro.
  • Make this recipe in bulk and refrigerate or freeze for a quick and easy meal later in the week.

Conclusion

Paleo Chicken Fajitas with Bell Peppers and Onions is a delicious and healthy recipe that’s perfect for any occasion. With its quick cooking time and minimal ingredients, it’s a great option for a busy weeknight dinner or a weekend lunch. And with its customization options, you can make it your own and enjoy it again and again. Give it a try and let us know what you think!
