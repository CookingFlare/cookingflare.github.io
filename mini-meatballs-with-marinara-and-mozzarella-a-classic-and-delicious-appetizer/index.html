Mini Meatballs with Marinara and Mozzarella: A Classic and Delicious Appetizer


Are you looking for a crowd-pleasing appetizer that will leave your guests begging for more? Look no further than these Mini Meatballs with Marinara and Mozzarella! This classic dish is not only delicious but also easy to make. Whether you’re hosting a dinner party or just want to enjoy a tasty snack, these mini meatballs are sure to hit the spot.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1/2 cup breadcrumbs
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley
  • 1/4 cup milk
  • 1 egg
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried basil
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • 2 cups marinara sauce
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C) and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.

  2. In a large mixing bowl, combine the ground beef, breadcrumbs, Parmesan cheese, parsley, milk, egg, minced garlic, dried oregano, dried basil, salt, and pepper. Mix well until all the ingredients are fully incorporated.

  3. Roll the mixture into small, bite-sized meatballs, about 1 inch in diameter. Place them on the prepared baking sheet, making sure to leave some space between each meatball.

  4. Bake the meatballs in the preheated oven for 15-20 minutes, or until they are cooked through and golden brown on the outside.

  5. While the meatballs are baking, heat the marinara sauce in a saucepan over medium heat. Once heated, reduce the heat to low and let it simmer until the meatballs are ready.

  6. Remove the meatballs from the oven and transfer them to a serving dish. Pour the marinara sauce over the meatballs, making sure they are fully coated.

  7. Sprinkle the shredded mozzarella cheese over the top of the meatballs and sauce. Return the dish to the oven and bake for an additional 5-10 minutes, or until the cheese is melted and bubbly.

  8. Once the cheese has melted, remove the dish from the oven and let it cool slightly before serving. Garnish with fresh parsley if desired.

Serving Suggestions

These Mini Meatballs with Marinara and Mozzarella are perfect as an appetizer for any occasion. Serve them with toothpicks for easy snacking at parties, or pair them with a side of pasta for a more substantial meal. They also make a great addition to sub sandwiches or sliders, adding a burst of flavor and texture.

Tips and Variations

  • Experiment with different types of ground meat, such as turkey, chicken, or pork, to create a unique twist on this classic recipe.
  • If you prefer a spicier sauce, add a pinch of red pepper flakes or a dash of hot sauce to the marinara.
  • For a healthier alternative, you can bake the meatballs in the oven instead of frying them. Simply place them on a greased baking sheet and cook for 15-20 minutes, or until they are cooked through.
  • Feel free to customize the recipe by adding your favorite herbs and spices to the meatball mixture. Get creative and make it your own!

So, the next time you’re in need of a delicious and satisfying appetizer, give these Mini Meatballs with Marinara and Mozzarella a try. With their savory flavors and cheesy goodness, they’re sure to be a hit with everyone who tries them. Enjoy!
