BBQ Sausage and Peppers: Easy and Delicious Recipes for Summer


Summer is the perfect time to fire up the grill and enjoy some delicious barbecue. And what better way to do that than by making BBQ sausage and peppers? This classic combination of juicy sausages and sweet, tangy peppers is sure to be a hit at your next summer gathering. Whether you’re hosting a backyard barbecue or simply want to enjoy a tasty meal with your loved ones, these easy and delicious recipes will surely satisfy your taste buds.

Grilled Sausage and Peppers Skewers

Ingredients:

  • 1 pack of your favorite sausages (such as Italian, bratwurst, or chorizo)
  • 2 bell peppers (red, yellow, or green), cut into chunks
  • 1 onion, cut into chunks
  • Olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your grill to medium-high heat.
  2. Soak wooden skewers in water for about 30 minutes to prevent them from burning.
  3. Thread the sausages, bell peppers, and onions onto the skewers, alternating between them.
  4. Drizzle the skewers with olive oil and season with salt and pepper.
  5. Place the skewers on the grill and cook for about 10-12 minutes, turning occasionally, until the sausages are cooked through and the vegetables are tender and slightly charred.
  6. Remove the skewers from the grill and let them rest for a few minutes before serving.
  7. Serve the grilled sausage and pepper skewers hot with your favorite condiments, such as mustard, ketchup, or barbecue sauce.

BBQ Sausage and Peppers Foil Packets

Ingredients:

  • 4 sausages (any variety you prefer)
  • 2 bell peppers (red, yellow, or green), thinly sliced
  • 1 onion, thinly sliced
  • 4 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons of olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon of Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 tablespoon of brown sugar
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your grill to medium heat or preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C).
  2. In a bowl, whisk together the olive oil, Worcestershire sauce, brown sugar, minced garlic, salt, and pepper.
  3. Cut four large pieces of aluminum foil, enough to wrap each sausage and vegetable packet.
  4. Place one sausage in the center of each foil piece and divide the sliced bell peppers and onions equally among the packets.
  5. Drizzle the olive oil mixture over the sausages and vegetables, making sure to coat them evenly.
  6. Fold the foil over the sausages and vegetables, sealing the edges tightly to create a packet.
  7. Place the foil packets on the grill or in the oven and cook for about 20-25 minutes until the sausages are fully cooked and the vegetables are tender.
  8. Carefully open the foil packets and serve the BBQ sausage and peppers hot. You can enjoy them as is or serve them on buns for a delicious sausage and pepper sandwich.

BBQ Sausage and Peppers Pizza

Ingredients:

  • 1 pre-made pizza dough or pizza crust
  • 1/2 cup of your favorite barbecue sauce
  • 1 pack of your favorite sausages, sliced
  • 1 bell pepper (red, yellow, or green), thinly sliced
  • 1 onion, thinly sliced
  • 2 cups of shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Fresh cilantro or parsley for garnish (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to the temperature specified on the pre-made pizza dough or crust package.
  2. Roll out the pizza dough or place the pre-made crust on a baking sheet.
  3. Spread the barbecue sauce evenly over the pizza dough or crust, leaving a small border around the edges.
  4. Top the sauce with the sliced sausages, bell peppers, and onions.
  5. Sprinkle the shredded mozzarella cheese over the toppings.
  6. Bake the pizza in the preheated oven for the specified time on the pizza dough or crust package, or until the cheese is melted and bubbly, and the crust is golden brown.
  7. Remove the pizza from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes.
  8. Garnish with fresh cilantro or parsley if desired.
  9. Slice the BBQ sausage and peppers pizza into wedges and serve hot.

These BBQ sausage and peppers recipes are versatile, easy to make, and bursting with flavor. Whether you prefer skewers, foil packets, or pizza, there’s a recipe here that will satisfy your cravings. So fire up the grill, invite your friends and family over, and enjoy these delicious dishes for a perfect summer barbecue.
