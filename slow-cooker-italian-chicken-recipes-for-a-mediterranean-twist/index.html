Slow Cooker Italian Chicken Recipes for a Mediterranean Twist


Are you tired of the same old chicken recipes? Do you want to add a Mediterranean twist to your cooking? Look no further! In this blog post, we will explore some delicious slow cooker Italian chicken recipes that will transport your taste buds to the beautiful shores of the Mediterranean. These recipes are not only flavorful but also incredibly easy to make, thanks to the magic of slow cooking. So, grab your apron and let’s get cooking!

1. Lemon Herb Chicken

Ingredients:

  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1 lemon, sliced
  • 3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon dried oregano
  • 1 tablespoon dried basil
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • ½ teaspoon black pepper
  • ½ cup chicken broth

Instructions:

  1. Place the chicken breasts at the bottom of your slow cooker.
  2. Layer the lemon slices on top of the chicken.
  3. In a small bowl, mix together the minced garlic, dried oregano, dried basil, salt, and black pepper.
  4. Sprinkle the herb mixture evenly over the chicken.
  5. Pour the chicken broth into the slow cooker.
  6. Cover the slow cooker and cook on low for 6-8 hours or on high for 3-4 hours, until the chicken is cooked through and tender.
  7. Serve the lemon herb chicken with a side of roasted vegetables or a fresh green salad for a complete Mediterranean-inspired meal.

2. Tomato Basil Chicken

Ingredients:

  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken thighs
  • 1 can diced tomatoes, drained
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon dried basil
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • 1 teaspoon dried rosemary
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • ¼ teaspoon red pepper flakes (optional)
  • Fresh basil leaves, for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Place the chicken thighs in the slow cooker.
  2. In a medium bowl, mix together the diced tomatoes, diced onion, minced garlic, dried basil, dried thyme, dried rosemary, salt, and red pepper flakes (if using).
  3. Pour the tomato mixture over the chicken, making sure it covers the chicken evenly.
  4. Cover the slow cooker and cook on low for 6-8 hours or on high for 3-4 hours, until the chicken is tender and the flavors have melded together.
  5. Garnish the tomato basil chicken with fresh basil leaves before serving. Pair it with some crusty bread or pasta for a hearty Mediterranean meal.

3. Mediterranean Chicken Stew

Ingredients:

  • 4 bone-in, skin-on chicken thighs
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 can diced tomatoes
  • 1 cup chicken broth
  • ½ cup pitted Kalamata olives
  • ¼ cup sun-dried tomatoes, drained and chopped
  • 2 teaspoons dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • Salt and black pepper, to taste
  • Fresh parsley, for garnish

Instructions:

  1. In a large skillet, heat some olive oil over medium heat. Brown the chicken thighs on both sides until golden brown. Transfer the chicken thighs to the slow cooker.
  2. In the same skillet, sauté the diced onion and minced garlic until fragrant and translucent. Add the diced tomatoes, chicken broth, Kalamata olives, sun-dried tomatoes, dried oregano, dried thyme, paprika, salt, and black pepper. Stir well to combine.
  3. Pour the tomato mixture over the chicken in the slow cooker.
  4. Cover the slow cooker and cook on low for 6-8 hours or on high for 3-4 hours, until the chicken is cooked through and the flavors have melded together.
  5. Serve the Mediterranean chicken stew hot, garnished with fresh parsley. Enjoy it with some crusty bread or couscous for a wholesome and satisfying meal.

Conclusion

These slow cooker Italian chicken recipes with a Mediterranean twist are sure to impress your family and friends. With their robust flavors and tender meat, they are perfect for any occasion. Whether you’re hosting a dinner party or simply craving a comforting meal, these recipes will transport you to the sun-drenched shores of the Mediterranean. So, dust off your slow cooker and start cooking your way to culinary bliss. Buon appetito!
