Quick and Easy Instant Pot Chicken Tortilla Soup Recipes

Are you craving a delicious and comforting meal that can be prepared in a flash? Look no further than these quick and easy Instant Pot chicken tortilla soup recipes. Packed with flavor and ready to eat in no time, these recipes are perfect for busy weeknights or lazy weekends. Recipe 1: Classic Chicken Tortilla Soup Ingredients: 1 pound boneless, skinless chicken breasts 1 tablespoon olive oil 1 small onion, diced 3 cloves garlic, minced 1 can (14.5 ounces) diced tomatoes 1 can (4 ounces) diced green chilies 4 cups chicken broth 1 teaspoon cumin 1 teaspoon chili powder Salt and pepper to taste 1 cup frozen corn 1 cup black beans, drained and rinsed Juice of 1 lime Fresh cilantro, chopped, for garnish Tortilla chips, crushed, for garnish Sour cream, for garnish Instructions: Set your Instant Pot to sauté mode and heat the olive oil. Add the diced onion and minced garlic, and sauté until fragrant and translucent. Add the diced tomatoes, green chilies, chicken broth, cumin, chili powder, salt, and pepper. Stir well to combine. Place the chicken breasts on top of the mixture, ensuring they are fully submerged. Secure the lid and set the Instant Pot to manual high pressure for 10 minutes. Once the cooking time is complete, perform a quick release to release the pressure. Carefully remove the chicken breasts and shred them using two forks. Return the shredded chicken to the Instant Pot, along with the frozen corn and black beans. Stir well to combine. Set the Instant Pot to sauté mode and simmer the soup for an additional 5 minutes to heat through. Stir in the lime juice and adjust the seasonings to taste. Serve the soup hot, garnished with fresh cilantro, crushed tortilla chips, and a dollop of sour cream, if desired. Recipe 2: Creamy Chicken Tortilla Soup Ingredients: 1 pound boneless, skinless chicken thighs 1 tablespoon olive oil 1 small onion, diced 3 cloves garlic, minced 1 can (14.5 ounces) diced tomatoes with green chilies 1 can (4 ounces) diced green chilies 4 cups chicken broth 1 teaspoon cumin 1 teaspoon chili powder Salt and pepper to taste 1 cup frozen corn 1 cup black beans, drained and rinsed 1 cup heavy cream Juice of 1 lime Fresh cilantro, chopped, for garnish Tortilla chips, crushed, for garnish Sliced avocado, for garnish Instructions: Set your Instant Pot to sauté mode and heat the olive oil. Add the diced onion and minced garlic, and sauté until fragrant and translucent. Add the diced tomatoes with green chilies, green chilies, chicken broth, cumin, chili powder, salt, and pepper. Stir well to combine. Place the chicken thighs on top of the mixture, ensuring they are fully submerged. Secure the lid and set the Instant Pot to manual high pressure for 15 minutes. Once the cooking time is complete, perform a quick release to release the pressure. Carefully remove the chicken thighs and shred them using two forks. Return the shredded chicken to the Instant Pot, along with the frozen corn and black beans. Stir well to combine. Set the Instant Pot to sauté mode and simmer the soup for an additional 5 minutes to heat through. Stir in the heavy cream and lime juice. Adjust the seasonings to taste. Serve the soup hot, garnished with fresh cilantro, crushed tortilla chips, and sliced avocado. Recipe 3: Spicy Chicken Tortilla Soup Ingredients: 1 pound boneless, skinless chicken tenders 2 tablespoons olive oil 1 small onion, diced 3 cloves garlic, minced 2 jalapeños, seeded and diced 1 can (14.5 ounces) fire-roasted diced tomatoes 1 can (4 ounces) diced green chilies 4 cups chicken broth 1 teaspoon cumin 1 teaspoon chili powder Salt and pepper to taste 1 cup frozen corn 1 cup black beans, drained and rinsed Juice of 1 lime Fresh cilantro, chopped, for garnish Tortilla chips, crushed, for garnish Shredded cheese, for garnish Instructions: Set your Instant Pot to sauté mode and heat the olive oil. Add the diced onion, minced garlic, and diced jalapeños. Sauté until fragrant and translucent. Add the fire-roasted diced tomatoes, green chilies, chicken broth, cumin, chili powder, salt, and pepper. Stir well to combine. Place the chicken tenders on top of the mixture, ensuring they are fully submerged. Secure the lid and set the Instant Pot to manual high pressure for 8 minutes. Once the cooking time is complete, perform a quick release to release the pressure. Carefully remove the chicken tenders and shred them using two forks. Return the shredded chicken to the Instant Pot, along with the frozen corn and black beans. Stir well to combine. Set the Instant Pot to sauté mode and simmer the soup for an additional 5 minutes to heat through. Stir in the lime juice and adjust the seasonings to taste. Serve the soup hot, garnished with fresh cilantro, crushed tortilla chips, and shredded cheese. There you have it - three delicious and easy Instant Pot chicken tortilla soup recipes.
