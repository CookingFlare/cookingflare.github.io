10 Allergy-Friendly Recipes for a Cozy Brunch


Brunch – it’s the weekend meal that combines the best of breakfast and lunch, offering a laid-back opportunity to connect with loved ones over delicious food. However, planning a brunch can be tricky when you or your guests have food allergies or dietary restrictions. No one should have to miss out on the joy of brunch, which is why we’re bringing you ten allergy-friendly recipes to make your next gathering both inclusive and flavorful. These dishes are free from common allergens like nuts, gluten, dairy, and eggs while still delivering all the comfort, color, and taste your brunch table deserves.

1. Fluffy Gluten-Free Pancakes

Who doesn’t love a towering stack of pancakes? These gluten-free gems are made using a blend of gluten-free oats and banana, making them naturally sweet and satisfying. To make them allergy-friendly, we use a flaxseed egg instead of a chicken egg.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup gluten-free oat flour
  • 1 ripe banana
  • 1 tbsp flaxseed meal mixed with 3 tbsp water (flax egg)
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1 cup plant-based milk (like oat or coconut milk)
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • Pinch of salt

Instructions:

  1. In a small bowl, mix the flaxseed meal and water. Let sit for 5 minutes.
  2. In a blender, combine all ingredients, including the flax egg. Blend until smooth.
  3. Heat a non-stick pan over medium heat and coat lightly with oil or cooking spray.
  4. Pour small dollops of the batter and cook until bubbles form. Flip and cook the other side until golden brown.

Serve with maple syrup and fresh berries for a vibrant finishing touch.

2. Egg-Free Vegetable Frittata

Egg frittatas may seem out of the question for someone with an egg allergy, but this plant-based version made with chickpea flour is a game-changer. Packed with veggies, it brings vibrant color and hearty nutrients to your table.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup chickpea flour
  • 1 cup water
  • 1 tsp turmeric
  • 1 cup diced bell peppers, zucchini, or spinach
  • 1 garlic clove, minced
  • Salt & pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. In a bowl, mix chickpea flour, water, turmeric, and seasoning until smooth.
  3. Sauté vegetables in an oven-safe skillet until softened.
  4. Pour chickpea mixture over vegetables, smooth out the surface, and bake for 15–20 minutes.

This egg-free frittata is light, savory, and pairs well with a side salad or toast.

3. Nut-Free Granola Parfaits

Granola is a brunch essential, but many store-bought varieties are packed with nuts. This version skips the nuts and uses seeds and coconut for added crunch.

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups rolled oats (certified gluten-free if needed)
  • ¼ cup sunflower seeds
  • ¼ cup pumpkin seeds
  • ¼ cup shredded unsweetened coconut
  • 2 tbsp maple syrup
  • 1 tsp cinnamon
  • ½ cup dried cranberries or raisins

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 325°F (165°C).
  2. Mix oats, seeds, coconut, and cinnamon in a large bowl. Drizzle with maple syrup.
  3. Spread the mixture onto a baking sheet and bake for 15–20 minutes, stirring halfway through.
  4. Once cooled, mix in dried fruit.

Layer this allergy-friendly granola with dairy-free yogurt and fresh fruit for a parfait guests will rave about.

4. Dairy-Free Avocado Toast

A brunch classic gets a creamy, dairy-free twist with this avocado toast recipe. Swap butter for mashed avocado seasoned to perfection.

Ingredients:

  • 2 ripe avocados
  • Juice of half a lemon
  • Pinch of salt and pepper
  • Gluten-free bread

Instructions:

  1. Toast your chosen gluten-free bread slices.
  2. In a bowl, mash avocados with lemon juice and seasoning.
  3. Spread the mixture onto the toast and top with optional add-ons like cherry tomatoes or microgreens.

5. Sweet Potato Hash

Sweet potatoes are not only allergy-friendly—they’re also delicious, versatile, and nutrient-rich. This hash offers a savory side dish perfect for rounding out your brunch spread.

Ingredients:

  • 2 medium sweet potatoes, diced
  • 1 red bell pepper, diced
  • 1 small onion, chopped
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp smoked paprika
  • Salt and pepper

Instructions:

  1. Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat.
  2. Add sweet potatoes and cook for 5 minutes.
  3. Toss in onions, bell peppers, and seasoning. Cook another 10–15 minutes, stirring occasionally.

This vibrant hash pairs beautifully with a dollop of guacamole.

6. Gluten-Free Waffles

Upgrade your brunch with crispy waffles free from gluten and dairy, but full of flavor. They’re made with rice flour and sweetened with coconut sugar.

Ingredients:

  • 1½ cups rice flour
  • 1 tbsp flaxseed meal
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 2 tbsp coconut sugar
  • 1¼ cups oat milk
  • 1 tbsp coconut oil

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your waffle iron.
  2. In a bowl, combine all ingredients to form a smooth batter.
  3. Cook the batter in the waffle iron until golden brown.

Serve with fresh berry compote or a drizzle of honey.

7. Vegan Breakfast Sausage Patties

For anyone missing meat products, these plant-based sausage patties offer all the smoky, savory flavor without any allergens.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup cooked quinoa
  • ½ cup rolled oats (ground into flour)
  • 1 tsp smoked paprika
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tbsp tamari (gluten-free soy sauce)

Instructions:

  1. Combine all ingredients into a thick mixture.
  2. Shape into small patties and cook in a skillet until crispy on both sides.

Great as-is or stacked on a breakfast sandwich!

8. Allergen-Free Berry Smoothie Bowls

No brunch is complete without something on the lighter side. Smoothie bowls are quick, simple, and customizable.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup frozen berries
  • 1 ripe banana
  • 1 cup unsweetened oat milk
  • Toppings: shredded coconut, chia seeds, and fresh fruit

Instructions:

  1. Blend berries, banana, and oat milk until smooth.
  2. Pour into a bowl and finish with your favorite toppings.

9. Mini Gluten-Free Muffins

Muffins are brunch staples, and these gluten-free ones fit the bill perfectly. They’re moist, tender, and totally satisfying.

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups almond flour or sunflower seed flour for nut-free options
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 2 tbsp maple syrup
  • ½ cup mashed banana

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. Mix all ingredients and pour batter evenly into mini muffin tins.
  3. Bake for 15 minutes, then let cool before serving.

10. Dairy-Free Chia Pudding

Finish your brunch with a creamy, make-ahead chia pudding that caters to everyone at the table.

Ingredients:

  • 3 tbsp chia seeds
  • 1 cup coconut milk
  • 1 tbsp maple syrup or agave
  • Dash of vanilla extract

Instructions:

  1. Mix chia seeds, milk, sweetener, and vanilla in a jar or bowl.
  2. Refrigerate overnight, stirring occasionally to prevent clumps.

Serve in small cups with a topping of fresh fruit or a sprinkle of granola.

With these allergy-friendly recipes, you can create a cozy brunch spread perfect for everyone at your table. From savory to sweet, these dishes leave no guest behind when it comes to enjoying the most satisfying meal of the weekend. Whether you’re hosting a group with diverse dietary needs or cooking for yourself, these recipes strike the perfect balance between inclusivity and deliciousness. Your brunch game just leveled up!
