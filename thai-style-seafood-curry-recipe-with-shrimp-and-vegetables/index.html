Thai-Style Seafood Curry Recipe with Shrimp and Vegetables


If you’re a fan of Thai cuisine and love seafood, then you’re in for a treat! Today, we’re going to take you on a culinary journey by sharing a delicious Thai-style seafood curry recipe. This aromatic and flavorful curry is made with succulent shrimp and a medley of vibrant vegetables. Get ready to indulge in a symphony of spices and textures that will transport your taste buds straight to Thailand!

Ingredients

For this Thai-style seafood curry, you’ll need the following ingredients:

  • 1 pound of shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 1 tablespoon of vegetable oil
  • 1 onion, thinly sliced
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 red bell pepper, thinly sliced
  • 1 green bell pepper, thinly sliced
  • 1 cup of coconut milk
  • 2 tablespoons of Thai red curry paste
  • 1 tablespoon of fish sauce
  • 1 tablespoon of brown sugar
  • 1 cup of chicken or vegetable broth
  • 1 cup of snap peas
  • 1 cup of cherry tomatoes, halved
  • Fresh cilantro, for garnish
  • Steamed jasmine rice, for serving

Instructions

  1. In a large skillet or wok, heat the vegetable oil over medium heat. Add the sliced onion and minced garlic, and sauté until they become fragrant and slightly translucent.

  2. Push the onions and garlic to one side of the skillet, and add the shrimp to the other side. Cook the shrimp until they turn pink and are cooked through, which should take about 2-3 minutes per side. Once cooked, remove the shrimp from the skillet and set them aside.

  3. In the same skillet, add the sliced red and green bell peppers. Sauté them for about 2-3 minutes until they start to soften.

  4. Now it’s time to add the star of the show – the Thai red curry paste! Stir in the curry paste and cook it with the peppers for about a minute, allowing the flavors to meld together.

  5. Pour in the coconut milk, fish sauce, and brown sugar. Stir well to combine all the ingredients and let the mixture simmer for a couple of minutes.

  6. Gradually add the chicken or vegetable broth to the skillet, stirring continuously. This will help to thin out the curry while still maintaining its rich and creamy texture.

  7. Add the snap peas and cherry tomatoes to the skillet, and let them cook for an additional 2-3 minutes until they are tender-crisp.

  8. Finally, return the cooked shrimp to the skillet and gently stir everything together, making sure the shrimp is evenly coated with the flavorful curry sauce. Cook for another minute or so, just until the shrimp is heated through.

  9. Remove the skillet from the heat and garnish with fresh cilantro.

  10. Serve the Thai-style seafood curry hot with steamed jasmine rice. The fragrant rice will help to balance the flavors and absorb the delicious curry sauce.

Conclusion

Congratulations! You’ve just learned how to make a tantalizing Thai-style seafood curry with shrimp and vegetables. The combination of succulent shrimp, vibrant vegetables, and aromatic spices will transport you to the bustling streets of Thailand. This dish is perfect for impressing guests or simply treating yourself to a taste of exotic flavors. So, put on your apron, gather the ingredients, and get ready to embark on a culinary adventure that will leave you craving for more!
