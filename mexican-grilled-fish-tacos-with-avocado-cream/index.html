Mexican Grilled Fish Tacos with Avocado Cream


Are you in the mood for a flavorful, flavorful meal that combines the freshness of the sea with the richness of the countryside? Look no further than these mouth-watering Mexican grilled fish tacos, complete with a vibrant avocado cream that will transport your taste buds to the sun-kissed tables of a Mexican fiesta. This delightful seafood dish is perfect for a casual dinner party or a cozy night in, and it's remarkably easy to prepare. Ingredients

4 tilapia fillets (or your preferred white fish, weigh 6 oz each) 1/2 cup lime juice 2 tbsp olive oil 1 tsp ground cumin 1 tsp smoked paprika 1/2 tsp salt 1/4 tsp black pepper 8 corn tortillas 1 ripe avocado 1/2 cup sour cream 1/2 lime, juiced Chopped fresh cilantro, for garnish Sliced radishes, for garnish Queso fresco or crumbled feta, for garnish (optional)

Instructions Prepare the fish Preheat your grill or grill pan to medium-high heat. In a small bowl, whisk together lime juice, olive oil, cumin, smoked paprika, salt, and pepper. Place the fish fillets in a shallow dish and brush the spice mixture evenly over both sides of each fillet. Set the fish aside for 15-30 minutes to allow the marinade to penetrate the delicate flesh. Grill the fish Grill the fish for 4-6 minutes per side, or until it's cooked through and flakes easily with a fork. Transfer the cooked fish to a plate and let it rest for a few minutes before cutting it into small pieces. Make the avocado cream In a blender or food processor, combine avocado, sour cream, and lime juice. Blend until smooth and creamy, stopping to scrape down the sides as needed. Taste and adjust the seasoning with salt, pepper, or a squeeze of additional lime juice. Assemble the tacos Warm the tortillas by wrapping them in a damp paper towel and microwaving for 20-30 seconds. Fill each tortilla with a piece of grilled fish, a spoonful of avocado cream, and a sprinkle of cilantro. Serve immediately and garnish with sliced radishes and crumbled queso fresco or feta, if desired. Variations and Tips Feel free to customize this recipe to your liking by adding diced onions, diced tomatoes, or shredded lettuce to your fish tacos. For an added layer of flavor, try mixing a sprinkle of cumin or chili powder into the avocado cream. When choosing a fish, select a firm, white variety like tilapia, mahi-mahi, or halibut. For a vegetarian option, substitute the fish with roasted or sautéed plantains or sweet potatoes. Tips for Perfect Grilled Fish Tacos

To ensure the fish cooks evenly, pat it dry with a paper towel before grilling. Grill the fish at medium-high heat to achieve a crispy, caramelized exterior and a tender interior. When working with fish, it's essential to not overcrowd the grill, as this can cause it to become tough and dry. Cook the fish in batches, if necessary. To make this recipe more substantial, serve the tacos with a side of Mexican rice, beans, or grilled corn on the cob.

The Magic of Avocado Cream Avocado cream is a game-changer when it comes to enhancing the flavors of any dish, and these fish tacos are no exception. This creamy sauce provides a silky, cooling element that complements the bold flavors of the grill. When blending the avocado, be sure to stop occasionally to scrape down the sides of the blender to prevent any lumps from forming. With its creamy texture and delicate flavor, avocado cream is a versatile ingredient that can be used in numerous recipes, from guacamole to pasta sauces. Experiment with its incredible possibilities and discover how this creamy treat elevates the humble taco. Why These Tacos are a Fiesta in Your Mouth The combination of melted queso fresco or feta cheese, tart lime juice, crunchy radishes, and the thoughtfully balanced flavors of griddled fish make these tacos an irresistible culinary experience. Each bite will leave you wanting another, because with this magical fusion of flavors and textures, every table becomes a fiesta. When cooking with local, sustainable, and high-quality ingredients, every meal is an opportunity to create a shared memory with those around you. Gong with this Mexican grilled fish tacos recipe with avocado cream is sure to create a flavorful memory with your family and guests. Nutrition Information (approximate) per serving:
