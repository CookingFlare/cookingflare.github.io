Delicious and Nutritious Stir Fry Recipes with Tofu and Vegetables


Are you looking for a quick and healthy meal that is packed with flavor? Look no further than these delicious and nutritious stir fry recipes with tofu and vegetables. Stir fry is a versatile and easy cooking technique that allows you to create a variety of dishes using a wide range of ingredients. In this blog post, we will explore three mouthwatering stir fry recipes that will satisfy your taste buds and keep you feeling nourished.

1. Spicy Tofu and Broccoli Stir Fry

Ingredients:

  • 1 block of firm tofu, drained and cubed
  • 2 cups broccoli florets
  • 1 red bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 yellow bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 small onion, sliced
  • 3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon sesame oil
  • 1 tablespoon sriracha sauce (adjust according to your spice preference)
  • 1 teaspoon ginger, grated
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Cooked rice or noodles, for serving

Instructions:

  1. Heat a large skillet or wok over medium-high heat. Add sesame oil and sauté the minced garlic and grated ginger for a minute until fragrant.
  2. Add the cubed tofu and cook until lightly browned on all sides. Remove from the skillet and set aside.
  3. In the same skillet, add the sliced onions, bell peppers, and broccoli florets. Stir fry for about 3-4 minutes until the vegetables are tender-crisp.
  4. In a small bowl, whisk together soy sauce, sriracha sauce, salt, and pepper. Pour the sauce over the vegetables and stir to coat evenly.
  5. Return the tofu to the skillet and toss everything together. Cook for an additional 2-3 minutes until the tofu is heated through.
  6. Serve the spicy tofu and broccoli stir fry over cooked rice or noodles. Enjoy!

2. Teriyaki Tofu and Mushroom Stir Fry

Ingredients:

  • 1 block of firm tofu, drained and cubed
  • 2 cups sliced mushrooms (any variety you prefer)
  • 1 cup snow peas, trimmed
  • 1 carrot, julienned
  • 3 green onions, chopped
  • 3 tablespoons teriyaki sauce
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • 1 teaspoon sesame seeds (optional)
  • Cooked rice or noodles, for serving

Instructions:

  1. Heat vegetable oil in a large skillet or wok over medium-high heat. Add the cubed tofu and cook until golden brown on all sides. Remove from the skillet and set aside.
  2. In the same skillet, add the sliced mushrooms and cook until they release their moisture and start to brown.
  3. Add the snow peas and julienned carrots to the skillet. Stir fry for about 2-3 minutes until the vegetables are tender-crisp.
  4. Return the tofu to the skillet and pour teriyaki sauce over the ingredients. Stir everything together until well coated.
  5. Cook for an additional 2-3 minutes until the tofu is heated through and the flavors are well combined.
  6. Sprinkle sesame seeds over the stir fry for an extra crunch and serve over cooked rice or noodles. Enjoy!

3. Garlic Ginger Tofu and Bok Choy Stir Fry

Ingredients:

  • 1 block of firm tofu, drained and cubed
  • 4 baby bok choy, halved lengthwise
  • 1 red bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 cup snap peas
  • 3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon ginger, grated
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon rice vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon honey (or maple syrup for a vegan option)
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Cooked rice or noodles, for serving

Instructions:

  1. Heat vegetable oil in a large skillet or wok over medium-high heat. Add the cubed tofu and cook until golden brown on all sides. Remove from the skillet and set aside.
  2. In the same skillet, add the minced garlic and grated ginger. Sauté for about a minute until fragrant.
  3. Add the bok choy, sliced bell pepper, and snap peas to the skillet. Stir fry for 3-4 minutes until the vegetables are tender-crisp.
  4. In a small bowl, whisk together soy sauce, rice vinegar, honey, salt, and pepper. Pour the sauce over the vegetables and stir to coat evenly.
  5. Return the tofu to the skillet and toss everything together. Cook for an additional 2-3 minutes until the tofu is heated through.
  6. Serve the garlic ginger tofu and bok choy stir fry over cooked rice or noodles. Enjoy!

These delicious and nutritious stir fry recipes with tofu and vegetables are perfect for busy weeknights when you want to whip up a quick and satisfying meal. Feel free to customize the recipes by adding your favorite vegetables or adjusting the spice level to suit your taste. So, grab your skillet and get ready to create a flavorful stir fry that will leave you craving for more!
