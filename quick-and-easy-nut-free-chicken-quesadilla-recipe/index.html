Quick and Easy Nut-Free Chicken Quesadilla Recipe


If you’re looking for a delicious and hassle-free meal that’s perfect for busy weeknights or casual gatherings, then you’re in for a treat! This Quick and Easy Nut-Free Chicken Quesadilla recipe is a game-changer. Packed with flavor, protein, and a hint of spice, it’s sure to become a go-to favorite in your recipe collection.

Ingredients:

  • 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon chili powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon onion powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 4 large flour tortillas
  • 2 cups shredded Monterey Jack cheese
  • 1/2 cup diced tomatoes
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh cilantro
  • Sour cream, salsa, and guacamole for serving

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the Chicken: Start by preheating a skillet over medium-high heat. Season the chicken breasts with cumin, chili powder, garlic powder, onion powder, salt, and pepper. Drizzle olive oil over the seasoned chicken and rub it in. Cook the chicken in the skillet for 6-7 minutes on each side, or until fully cooked through. Once done, transfer the chicken to a cutting board and let it rest for a few minutes before cutting it into thin slices.

  2. Assemble the Quesadillas: Lay out the flour tortillas on a clean surface. Sprinkle an even layer of shredded Monterey Jack cheese over half of each tortilla. Arrange the sliced chicken, diced tomatoes, and chopped cilantro over the cheese. Fold the tortillas in half to cover the filling.

  3. Cook the Quesadillas: Wipe the skillet clean and return it to medium heat. Place a filled tortilla in the skillet and cook for 2-3 minutes on each side, or until the cheese is melted, and the tortilla is golden and crispy. Repeat the process with the remaining quesadillas.

  4. Serve and Enjoy: Once the quesadillas are cooked, transfer them to a cutting board and let them cool for a minute. Slice each quesadilla into wedges and serve with sour cream, salsa, and guacamole on the side.

This Nut-Free Chicken Quesadilla recipe is perfect for those with nut allergies or anyone looking for a simple yet satisfying meal option. The combination of tender chicken, gooey cheese, and flavorful spices makes for a winning flavor profile that’s sure to please even the pickiest of eaters. Plus, the quick and easy preparation means you can have a delicious meal on the table in no time.

Whether you’re cooking for yourself, your family, or hosting a casual get-together, these quesadillas are a versatile crowd-pleaser. You can customize them with your favorite toppings or add a side salad for a complete meal. They also make for a great lunch option when you’re in need of a tasty and filling midday meal.

So, the next time you’re craving a fuss-free, nut-free meal that doesn’t skimp on flavor, give this Quick and Easy Nut-Free Chicken Quesadilla recipe a try. It’s a fantastic addition to any cook’s repertoire and is bound to become a regular feature on your menu rotation. Enjoy!
