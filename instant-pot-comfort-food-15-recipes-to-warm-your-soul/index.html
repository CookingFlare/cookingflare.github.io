Instant Pot Comfort Food: 15 Recipes to Warm Your Soul


When the temperatures dip and the winds pick up, there’s nothing quite like a bowl of comforting food to warm your body and soul. Whether you’re winding down after a busy day, spending a cozy evening with family, or simply in need of a meal that feels like a hug, Instant Pot comfort food recipes offer the perfect solution. These easy-to-make dishes are hearty, flavorful, and ideal for those moments when you crave something satisfying.

Here, we present 15 delicious Instant Pot comfort food recipes that are sure to make you feel cozy, nourished, and content. From creamy soups and stews to rich casseroles and comforting pasta dishes, the Instant Pot is your trusty kitchen companion for whipping up soul-warming meals in no time. Let’s dive in!

1. Creamy Chicken and Rice Soup

This creamy chicken and rice soup is a family favorite, and with the Instant Pot, you can have it ready in less than an hour. Tender chicken, rice, and vegetables are cooked in a rich, velvety broth that will leave you feeling full and satisfied. The addition of heavy cream makes the soup indulgent, while fresh herbs and a squeeze of lemon provide a burst of flavor.

Ingredients:

  • 2 chicken breasts
  • 1 cup white rice
  • 4 cups chicken broth
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1 cup carrots, chopped
  • 1 cup celery, chopped
  • 1 medium onion, diced
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Add the chicken, rice, chicken broth, carrots, celery, onion, garlic, thyme, salt, and pepper to the Instant Pot.
  2. Seal the lid and cook on high pressure for 10 minutes.
  3. Once done, carefully release the pressure, remove the chicken, and shred it.
  4. Return the shredded chicken to the pot, then stir in the heavy cream.
  5. Simmer for an additional 5 minutes to allow the flavors to meld together. Serve hot!

2. Beef Stew with Potatoes and Carrots

A classic beef stew is the ultimate comfort food, and the Instant Pot makes this hearty dish faster than traditional methods. Tender beef, potatoes, carrots, and onions are cooked in a savory broth, creating a deliciously filling meal that is perfect for chilly nights.

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb beef stew meat, cut into cubes
  • 4 cups beef broth
  • 3 medium potatoes, peeled and diced
  • 2 carrots, sliced
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 3 garlic cloves, minced
  • 2 tablespoons tomato paste
  • 1 teaspoon dried rosemary
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Select the sauté function on your Instant Pot and brown the beef stew meat in batches.
  2. Once browned, add the onion and garlic, sautéing until fragrant.
  3. Stir in the tomato paste, rosemary, thyme, salt, and pepper, then add the beef broth.
  4. Add the diced potatoes and carrots to the pot.
  5. Seal the lid and cook on high pressure for 35 minutes.
  6. After the cooking time, perform a quick release and stir the stew before serving.

3. Mac and Cheese

Mac and cheese is the ultimate comfort food for many, and the Instant Pot makes it easier than ever to enjoy a creamy, cheesy version of this classic dish. The noodles cook directly in the sauce, allowing for maximum flavor in every bite.

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb elbow macaroni
  • 4 cups chicken broth
  • 2 cups sharp cheddar cheese, shredded
  • 1 cup milk
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Add the elbow macaroni and chicken broth to the Instant Pot.
  2. Seal the lid and cook on high pressure for 4 minutes.
  3. After the cooking time, perform a quick release and stir in the milk, butter, shredded cheese, salt, and pepper.
  4. Mix until the cheese has melted and the sauce is creamy. Serve immediately!

4. Chicken Alfredo Pasta

This chicken Alfredo pasta is a rich and creamy dish that’s quick to prepare thanks to the Instant Pot. The tender chicken and pasta are enveloped in a luscious Alfredo sauce that’s rich, buttery, and indulgent.

Ingredients:

  • 2 chicken breasts, diced
  • 1 lb fettuccine pasta
  • 4 cups chicken broth
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1 cup Parmesan cheese, grated
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Select the sauté function on your Instant Pot and cook the diced chicken until browned on all sides.
  2. Add the garlic and sauté for 1 minute.
  3. Pour in the chicken broth and add the fettuccine pasta, breaking it in half if necessary.
  4. Seal the lid and cook on high pressure for 4 minutes.
  5. After the cooking time, perform a quick release and stir in the heavy cream, butter, and Parmesan cheese.
  6. Season with salt and pepper, and serve hot.

5. Vegetable Soup

This vegetable soup is a healthy and comforting option that’s packed with nutrients. The Instant Pot makes it easy to prepare a batch of this hearty soup with a variety of vegetables like carrots, potatoes, and green beans, all cooked in a savory broth.

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups vegetable broth
  • 3 carrots, sliced
  • 2 potatoes, diced
  • 1 cup green beans, chopped
  • 1 cup corn kernels
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Add all the ingredients to the Instant Pot.
  2. Seal the lid and cook on high pressure for 10 minutes.
  3. After the cooking time, perform a quick release and stir the soup.
  4. Taste and adjust the seasoning if necessary, then serve.

6. Chicken Pot Pie

Chicken pot pie is a classic comfort food, and this Instant Pot version makes it quicker and easier to enjoy. The tender chicken, vegetables, and creamy sauce are topped with a flaky pie crust for the perfect bite.

Ingredients:

  • 2 chicken breasts, cooked and shredded
  • 2 cups chicken broth
  • 1 cup frozen peas and carrots
  • 1 medium onion, diced
  • 2 cups heavy cream
  • 1 teaspoon thyme
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 sheet pie crust

Instructions:

  1. Select the sauté function on your Instant Pot and cook the onion until soft.
  2. Add the chicken broth, peas, carrots, shredded chicken, thyme, salt, and pepper.
  3. Stir in the heavy cream and cook on high pressure for 5 minutes.
  4. Pour the mixture into a greased baking dish and top with a pie crust.
  5. Bake in the oven at 375°F for 20-25 minutes or until the crust is golden brown.

7. Pulled Pork

Pulled pork is a fantastic comfort food that’s perfect for feeding a crowd. With the Instant Pot, you can have tender, flavorful pulled pork in a fraction of the time it takes with traditional methods. Serve it with coleslaw or on a bun for a satisfying meal.

Ingredients:

  • 2 lbs pork shoulder
  • 1 cup apple cider vinegar
  • 1 cup chicken broth
  • 1 tablespoon smoked paprika
  • 1 tablespoon brown sugar
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Rub the pork shoulder with paprika, brown sugar, garlic powder, salt, and pepper.
  2. Add the apple cider vinegar and chicken broth to the Instant Pot.
  3. Place the pork in the Instant Pot and seal the lid.
  4. Cook on high pressure for 60 minutes, then perform a natural release.
  5. Shred the pork with two forks, then serve with your favorite sides.

8. Spaghetti Bolognese

Spaghetti Bolognese is a beloved comfort food that is made even easier with the Instant Pot. The rich, meaty sauce is perfect for tossing with al dente pasta and topped with freshly grated Parmesan.

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 can crushed tomatoes (14 oz)
  • 1 cup beef broth
  • 2 teaspoons dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1 lb spaghetti
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Select the sauté function on your Instant Pot and brown the ground beef along with the onion and garlic.
  2. Add the crushed tomatoes, beef broth, oregano, basil, salt, and pepper.
  3. Break the spaghetti in half and add it to the pot.
  4. Seal the lid and cook on high pressure for 8 minutes.
  5. After the cooking time, perform a quick release, then stir and serve with Parmesan cheese.

9. Chili

Chili is the epitome of comfort food, with its hearty beans, tender meat, and flavorful spices. The Instant Pot allows you to prepare chili quickly without compromising on flavor. Whether you like it mild or spicy, this chili recipe is customizable to your tastes.

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1 can kidney beans, drained
  • 1 can black beans, drained
  • 1 can diced tomatoes (14 oz)
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 2 tablespoons chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Select the sauté function on your Instant Pot and brown the ground beef with the onion and garlic.
  2. Add the kidney beans, black beans, diced tomatoes, chili powder, cumin, salt, and pepper.
  3. Seal the lid and cook on high pressure for 15 minutes.
  4. After the cooking time, perform a quick release and stir the chili.
  5. Serve with sour cream, shredded cheese, or tortilla chips.

10. Beef and Broccoli

Beef and broccoli is a classic takeout dish that’s easy to make at home with the Instant Pot. The beef is tender and flavorful, and the broccoli soaks up all the savory sauce. Serve it over steamed rice for a filling and comforting meal.

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb flank steak, sliced thin
  • 2 cups beef broth
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon oyster sauce
  • 1 tablespoon brown sugar
  • 2 cups broccoli florets
  • 1 tablespoon cornstarch

Instructions:

  1. Add the beef broth, soy sauce, oyster sauce, and brown sugar to the Instant Pot.
  2. Add the sliced beef and cook on high pressure for 5 minutes.
  3. After the cooking time, perform a quick release and add the broccoli florets.
  4. Mix the cornstarch with water to form a slurry and stir it into the pot to thicken the sauce.
  5. Serve over steamed rice and enjoy!

11. Sweet and Sour Chicken

Sweet and sour chicken is a favorite comfort food with its crispy chicken and tangy, sweet sauce. With the Instant Pot, you can easily make a healthier version that still has all the flavor and texture you love.

Ingredients:

  • 2 chicken breasts, cubed
  • 1 cup pineapple chunks
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 1 bell pepper, diced
  • 1/2 cup vinegar
  • 1/2 cup brown sugar
  • 1/4 cup ketchup
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce

Instructions:

  1. Add the cubed chicken, pineapple, onion, and bell pepper to the Instant Pot.
  2. In a bowl, whisk together the vinegar, brown sugar, ketchup, and soy sauce.
  3. Pour the sauce over the chicken and vegetables.
  4. Seal the lid and cook on high pressure for 10 minutes.
  5. Perform a quick release and stir the mixture before serving.

12. Sloppy Joes

Sloppy Joes are a messy, flavorful comfort food that’s easy to make with the Instant Pot. With savory ground beef, onions, and a tangy tomato sauce, this dish is perfect for serving on buns for a classic comfort meal.

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 1 bell pepper, diced
  • 1 can tomato sauce (14 oz)
  • 2 tablespoons ketchup
  • 1 tablespoon Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 tablespoon mustard
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Select the sauté function on your Instant Pot and brown the ground beef with the onion and bell pepper.
  2. Add the tomato sauce, ketchup, Worcestershire sauce, mustard, salt, and pepper.
  3. Seal the lid and cook on high pressure for 10 minutes.
  4. Perform a quick release and stir the mixture.
  5. Serve the sloppy joe filling on hamburger buns for a delicious meal.

13. Lasagna

Lasagna is a beloved comfort food, and the Instant Pot makes it possible to enjoy this dish without the lengthy bake time. Layers of noodles, meat sauce, and cheese are cooked together, resulting in a delicious, cheesy casserole.

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 2 cups marinara sauce
  • 9 lasagna noodles, broken in half
  • 2 cups ricotta cheese
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 tablespoon dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Select the sauté function on your Instant Pot and brown the ground beef with the onion.
  2. Stir in the marinara sauce, salt, and pepper, then layer the broken lasagna noodles on top.
  3. Layer the ricotta cheese, mozzarella cheese, and Parmesan cheese on top of the noodles.
  4. Seal the lid and cook on high pressure for 20 minutes.
  5. After the cooking time, perform a quick release and serve the lasagna with extra Parmesan on top.

14. Stuffed Bell Peppers

Stuffed bell peppers are a comforting and healthy meal that’s easy to make in the Instant Pot. Ground beef, rice, and seasonings are stuffed into tender bell peppers, then cooked in a savory tomato sauce.

Ingredients:

  • 4 bell peppers, tops cut off and seeds removed
  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1 cup cooked rice
  • 1 can diced tomatoes (14 oz)
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Mix the ground beef, cooked rice, diced tomatoes, basil, garlic powder, salt, and pepper together.
  2. Stuff the mixture into the hollowed-out bell peppers.
  3. Pour a little extra diced tomatoes into the Instant Pot.
  4. Place the stuffed peppers on top of the tomatoes and seal the lid.
  5. Cook on high pressure for 10 minutes, then perform a quick release and serve hot.

15. Chicken and Dumplings

Chicken and dumplings are the epitome of comfort, and the Instant Pot makes this dish quicker than traditional methods. The tender chicken, vegetables, and fluffy dumplings come together in a rich, creamy broth.

Ingredients:

  • 2 chicken breasts, cooked and shredded
  • 1 cup carrots, chopped
  • 1 cup celery, chopped
  • 1 cup peas
  • 4 cups chicken broth
  • 2 cups heavy cream
  • 1 teaspoon thyme
  • 1 cup biscuit mix
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Add the chicken, carrots, celery, peas, chicken broth, heavy cream, thyme, salt, and pepper to the Instant Pot.
  2. Mix the biscuit mix with milk to form a dough.
  3. Drop spoonfuls of the dough into the pot.
  4. Seal the lid and cook on high pressure for 10 minutes.
  5. Perform a quick release, stir gently, and serve hot!

These 15 Instant Pot comfort food recipes are the perfect way to bring warmth and satisfaction to any meal. Whether you’re cooking for your family or preparing a solo dinner, the Instant Pot makes comfort food quick, easy, and oh-so-delicious. Enjoy these recipes that will fill your home with wonderful aromas and leave everyone at the table smiling!
