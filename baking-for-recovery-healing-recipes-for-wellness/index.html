Baking for Recovery: Healing Recipes for Wellness


In times of stress, uncertainty, or when you’re on a journey towards wellness, turning to the therapeutic art of baking can be incredibly soothing and healing. Baking not only engages your senses but also allows you to create wholesome treats that can nourish both your body and soul. In this blog post, we’ll explore a selection of baking recipes tailored for recovery and well-being.

Orange & Almond Flourless Cake

Ingredients:

  • 2 large oranges
  • 6 eggs
  • 250g almond meal
  • 200g honey
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • A pinch of salt

Method:

  1. Wash the oranges, then boil them whole for 2 hours. Once cooled, blend them into a smooth puree.
  2. Preheat the oven to 180°C (350°F) and grease a cake tin.
  3. In a bowl, beat the eggs and honey until light and fluffy.
  4. Fold in the almond meal, baking powder, and a pinch of salt.
  5. Gently mix in the orange puree.
  6. Pour the batter into the cake tin and bake for 40-45 minutes.
  7. Allow the cake to cool before serving.

This flourless cake is not only gluten-free but also bursting with citrusy goodness and nutty flavors, making it a perfect treat for those looking for a healthier indulgence.

Oat & Banana Breakfast Cookies

Ingredients:

  • 2 ripe bananas, mashed
  • 1 ½ cups rolled oats
  • 1/3 cup raisins
  • 1/3 cup chopped nuts
  • 1 tsp cinnamon
  • 1 tbsp honey

Method:

  1. Preheat the oven to 180°C (350°F) and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
  2. In a bowl, combine the mashed bananas, oats, raisins, nuts, cinnamon, and honey.
  3. Mix until well combined.
  4. Scoop spoonfuls of the mixture onto the baking sheet and flatten them slightly.
  5. Bake for 15-20 minutes until golden brown.
  6. Allow the cookies to cool before enjoying them for a nutritious breakfast or snack.

These wholesome breakfast cookies are packed with fiber, potassium, and natural sweetness, making them an ideal choice for a quick energy boost during your recovery journey.

Turmeric & Ginger Spiced Bread

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups whole wheat flour
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1 ½ tsp ground turmeric
  • 1 tsp ground ginger
  • ½ tsp cinnamon
  • ½ cup honey
  • ½ cup olive oil
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup grated carrots
  • ½ cup chopped nuts

Method:

  1. Preheat the oven to 180°C (350°F) and grease a loaf pan.
  2. In a bowl, whisk together the flour, baking soda, turmeric, ginger, and cinnamon.
  3. In another bowl, combine the honey, olive oil, and eggs.
  4. Gradually add the wet ingredients to the dry ingredients and mix until just combined.
  5. Fold in the grated carrots and chopped nuts.
  6. Pour the batter into the loaf pan and bake for 45-50 minutes.
  7. Let the spiced bread cool before slicing and serving.

This aromatic and nutrient-rich spiced bread is not only delicious but also anti-inflammatory, thanks to the healing properties of turmeric and ginger.

Baking for recovery is a therapeutic and rewarding experience that allows you to nourish your body with wholesome ingredients while indulging in delicious treats. Whether you’re looking to boost your mood, support your immune system, or simply enjoy a moment of creativity, these healing recipes are sure to inspire and uplift you on your wellness journey. Happy baking!
