Irresistible Green Bean Casserole Recipe for Thanksgiving


Thanksgiving is a time for indulgence and creating memorable dishes that bring families together. One dish that always makes an appearance on the Thanksgiving table is the classic Green Bean Casserole. This irresistible recipe combines tender green beans, creamy mushroom sauce, and crispy fried onions to create a dish that is both comforting and delicious.

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 pounds fresh green beans, trimmed and cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 1 cup diced onion
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 8 ounces mushrooms, sliced
  • 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup chicken or vegetable broth
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1/4 teaspoon nutmeg
  • 1 cup crispy fried onions

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).

  2. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Add the green beans and cook for 5 minutes, or until they are crisp-tender. Drain the beans and set them aside.

  3. In a large skillet, melt the butter over medium heat. Add the diced onion and minced garlic, and cook until the onion is translucent and the garlic is fragrant.

  4. Add the sliced mushrooms to the skillet and cook until they have released their moisture and are golden brown.

  5. Sprinkle the flour over the mushroom mixture and stir to combine. Cook for 1 minute, stirring constantly.

  6. Slowly pour in the chicken or vegetable broth, stirring constantly to prevent lumps from forming. Add the heavy cream, salt, black pepper, and nutmeg, and stir until the sauce thickens.

  7. Add the cooked green beans to the skillet and toss them in the sauce until they are well coated.

  8. Transfer the green bean mixture to a greased casserole dish and spread it out evenly.

  9. Sprinkle the crispy fried onions over the top of the casserole.

  10. Bake the casserole in the preheated oven for 25-30 minutes, or until it is heated through and the onions are golden brown and crispy.

  11. Remove the casserole from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes before serving.

Serving Suggestions

The Irresistible Green Bean Casserole pairs perfectly with other Thanksgiving classics like roasted turkey, mashed potatoes, and cranberry sauce. It adds a vibrant pop of color to the table and the combination of flavors and textures will have everyone coming back for seconds.

Make It Your Own

While the classic Green Bean Casserole is always a hit, feel free to customize it to your taste. Here are a few ideas:

  • Add some cooked bacon or crispy prosciutto for a smoky flavor.
  • Mix in some shredded cheddar cheese for an extra layer of richness.
  • Sprinkle some toasted almonds or breadcrumbs on top for added crunch.
  • Experiment with different types of mushrooms, such as cremini or shiitake, for a unique twist.

Conclusion

The Irresistible Green Bean Casserole is a Thanksgiving must-have that will delight your taste buds and impress your guests. With its creamy mushroom sauce and crispy fried onions, this classic dish is sure to become a family favorite. So, gather your ingredients, preheat your oven, and get ready to create a Thanksgiving masterpiece that will leave everyone asking for seconds!
