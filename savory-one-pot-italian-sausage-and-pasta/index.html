Savory One Pot Italian Sausage and Pasta


There’s something special about a delicious one-pot meal that combines all the flavors and textures in one dish. And when it comes to Italian cuisine, nothing beats the hearty combination of sausage and pasta. This Savory One Pot Italian Sausage and Pasta recipe is a perfect example of a satisfying and comforting meal that can be prepared with minimal effort and maximum flavor. So, let’s dive right in and discover how to create this mouthwatering dish!

Ingredients

To make the Savory One Pot Italian Sausage and Pasta, you will need the following ingredients:

  • 1 pound of Italian sausage
  • 1 medium-sized onion, diced
  • 3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 bell pepper, diced
  • 1 can (14 ounces) of diced tomatoes
  • 2 cups of chicken broth
  • 2 cups of uncooked penne pasta
  • 1 teaspoon of dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon of dried basil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Grated Parmesan cheese for garnish
  • Fresh basil leaves for garnish

Instructions

Now that we have all the ingredients ready, let’s get cooking!

  1. Heat a large pot or Dutch oven over medium heat. Add the Italian sausage and cook until browned, breaking it up into crumbles with a wooden spoon. This will take about 5-7 minutes.

  2. Once the sausage is cooked, remove it from the pot and set it aside. Leave about a tablespoon of the rendered fat in the pot.

  3. In the same pot, add the diced onion, minced garlic, and diced bell pepper. Sauté until the vegetables are softened and fragrant, about 5 minutes.

  4. Add the diced tomatoes, chicken broth, uncooked penne pasta, dried oregano, and dried basil to the pot. Stir everything together until well combined.

  5. Bring the mixture to a boil, then reduce the heat to low and cover the pot. Let it simmer for about 15-20 minutes, or until the pasta is cooked al dente and most of the liquid has been absorbed. Stir occasionally to prevent sticking.

  6. Once the pasta is cooked, return the cooked Italian sausage to the pot. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Stir everything together, allowing the flavors to meld for a few minutes.

  7. Remove the pot from the heat and let it sit for a couple of minutes to cool slightly. This will allow the sauce to thicken.

  8. Serve the Savory One Pot Italian Sausage and Pasta in bowls, garnished with grated Parmesan cheese and fresh basil leaves.

Conclusion

This Savory One Pot Italian Sausage and Pasta recipe is the epitome of comfort food. With its rich flavors and simple preparation, it’s the perfect dish to satisfy your cravings on a busy weeknight or impress your guests at a dinner party. The combination of Italian sausage, pasta, and aromatic herbs creates a symphony of flavors that will transport you straight to Italy. So, grab your pot and get cooking – this recipe is sure to become a family favorite!

Remember, cooking is all about experimenting and adding your own personal touch. Feel free to customize this recipe by adding vegetables like zucchini or mushrooms, or even swapping the Italian sausage for a vegetarian alternative. The possibilities are endless, and that’s the beauty of cooking. So, go ahead and make this Savory One Pot Italian Sausage and Pasta your own culinary masterpiece!
