Korean Spicy Rice Cakes: A Fiery and Flavorful Dish


If you’re a fan of Korean cuisine, then you’ve probably heard of tteokbokki, the delicious and spicy Korean rice cakes. Tteokbokki, also known as spicy rice cakes, is a popular street food in Korea that has gained popularity worldwide for its fiery and flavorful taste. In this blog post, we’ll take a closer look at this mouthwatering dish and share a simple recipe for you to try at home.

What are Korean Spicy Rice Cakes?

Korean spicy rice cakes are made from cylindrical rice cakes called tteok, which are made from glutinous rice flour. These chewy rice cakes are cooked in a spicy sauce made from gochujang, a Korean chili paste, along with other ingredients like soy sauce, sugar, and garlic. The dish is typically garnished with green onions and sesame seeds, adding a pop of freshness and nuttiness to the overall flavor.

The Fiery and Flavorful Sauce

The key to the mouthwatering taste of Korean spicy rice cakes lies in the sauce. Gochujang, the Korean chili paste, is the star ingredient that gives the dish its fiery kick. It’s a fermented chili paste made from red chili peppers, glutinous rice, fermented soybeans, and salt. The combination of sweet, spicy, and savory flavors in the sauce creates a symphony of taste that will leave you craving for more.

Cooking Korean Spicy Rice Cakes at Home

Now that you know a bit more about Korean spicy rice cakes, let’s dive into the recipe. Here’s how you can recreate this fiery and flavorful dish in the comfort of your own kitchen:

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound of Korean rice cakes (tteok)
  • 2 cups of water
  • 2 tablespoons of gochujang (Korean chili paste)
  • 1 tablespoon of soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon of sugar
  • 3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon of vegetable oil
  • 1/2 cup of sliced green onions
  • 1 tablespoon of sesame seeds

Instructions:

  1. Soak the rice cakes in cold water for about 30 minutes to soften them.
  2. In a bowl, combine the gochujang, soy sauce, sugar, and minced garlic to make the spicy sauce.
  3. Heat the vegetable oil in a large pan over medium heat.
  4. Add the soaked rice cakes to the pan and stir-fry them for a few minutes until they become slightly soft.
  5. Pour the spicy sauce over the rice cakes and add water to the pan.
  6. Stir everything together and let it simmer for about 10-15 minutes, or until the rice cakes are fully cooked and the sauce thickens.
  7. Garnish the dish with sliced green onions and sesame seeds.
  8. Serve the Korean spicy rice cakes hot and enjoy!

A Spicy Delight for Your Taste Buds

Korean spicy rice cakes are a perfect combination of heat, flavor, and chewiness. Each bite is a burst of spicy goodness that will leave you wanting more. Whether you’re a fan of Korean cuisine or just looking to try something new, this dish is sure to impress.

So, why not take a culinary adventure and whip up a batch of Korean spicy rice cakes in your own kitchen? With this simple recipe, you can recreate the flavors of Korea and satisfy your cravings for a fiery and flavorful dish.
