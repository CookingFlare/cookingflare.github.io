Grilled Shrimp Skewers with Peanut Sauce Recipe


If you’re a fan of seafood and you love the smoky flavor of grilled dishes, then you’re in for a treat with this Grilled Shrimp Skewers with Peanut Sauce recipe. It’s a perfect blend of succulent shrimp, vibrant flavors, and a rich peanut sauce that will make your taste buds dance. This dish is not only delicious but also easy to prepare, making it an ideal choice for a quick weeknight dinner or a weekend barbecue with friends and family.

Ingredients

For the Shrimp Skewers:

  • 1 pound of large shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 2 tablespoons of olive oil
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon of paprika
  • 1 teaspoon of cumin
  • Salt and black pepper to taste

For the Peanut Sauce:

  • 1/2 cup of creamy peanut butter
  • 2 tablespoons of soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons of honey
  • 1 tablespoon of rice vinegar
  • 1 clove of garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon of grated fresh ginger
  • 1/4 cup of warm water

For Serving:

  • Fresh cilantro, chopped
  • Crushed peanuts
  • Lime wedges

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Shrimp: In a bowl, combine the olive oil, minced garlic, paprika, cumin, salt, and black pepper. Add the shrimp and toss to coat evenly. Cover the bowl and let the shrimp marinate in the refrigerator for at least 30 minutes.

  2. Preheat the Grill: While the shrimp is marinating, preheat your grill to medium-high heat.

  3. Make the Peanut Sauce: In a small bowl, whisk together the peanut butter, soy sauce, honey, rice vinegar, minced garlic, grated ginger, and warm water until smooth. Set the peanut sauce aside for later use.

  4. Skewer the Shrimp: Thread the marinated shrimp onto metal or soaked wooden skewers, leaving a little space between each shrimp to ensure even cooking.

  5. Grill the Shrimp: Place the shrimp skewers on the preheated grill and cook for 2-3 minutes per side, or until the shrimp are opaque and slightly charred. Be careful not to overcook the shrimp to maintain their tender texture.

  6. Serve and Enjoy: Once the shrimp skewers are cooked, transfer them to a serving platter. Drizzle the peanut sauce over the grilled shrimp, sprinkle with chopped cilantro and crushed peanuts, and serve with lime wedges on the side.

Tips and Variations

  • For a spicy kick, add a pinch of cayenne pepper to the shrimp marinade or the peanut sauce.
  • If you prefer a thinner consistency for the peanut sauce, simply add more warm water until you reach your desired texture.
  • These grilled shrimp skewers can be served as a main dish with a side of rice and steamed vegetables, or as a delightful appetizer for a gathering.

In Conclusion

Grilled Shrimp Skewers with Peanut Sauce is a delightful dish that brings together the succulent flavors of shrimp and the rich, nutty taste of peanut sauce. This recipe is a crowd-pleaser and is sure to become a favorite in your cooking repertoire. Whether you’re hosting a summer barbecue or simply craving a flavorful seafood dish, these grilled shrimp skewers will satisfy your cravings and leave you wanting more. So fire up the grill and treat yourself to this delectable culinary experience!
