Sous Vide Ratatouille Stuffed Peppers: A Colorful Delight

If you’re looking for a vibrant and flavorful dish that will impress your guests, look no further than these Sous Vide Ratatouille Stuffed Peppers. This recipe combines the rich flavors of ratatouille with the tender sweetness of bell peppers, resulting in a dish that is not only visually stunning but also a true delight for the taste buds. Ingredients 4 large bell peppers (assorted colors for a visually appealing presentation) 1 zucchini, diced 1 yellow squash, diced 1 eggplant, diced 2 cups cherry tomatoes, halved 1 onion, finely chopped 4 cloves of garlic, minced 2 tablespoons tomato paste 2 tablespoons olive oil 1 teaspoon dried oregano 1 teaspoon dried thyme Salt and pepper to taste Fresh basil leaves for garnish Instructions Step 1: Prepare the Sous Vide Ratatouille Preheat the water bath of your sous vide machine to 185°F (85°C). In a large bowl, combine the zucchini, yellow squash, eggplant, cherry tomatoes, onion, garlic, tomato paste, olive oil, dried oregano, dried thyme, salt, and pepper. Toss to coat the vegetables evenly with the seasonings. Divide the ratatouille mixture among several vacuum-sealable bags, ensuring that each bag contains an equal portion of the vegetables. Seal the bags using a vacuum sealer, ensuring that the vegetables are in a single layer to allow for even cooking. Once the water bath has reached the desired temperature, submerge the sealed bags of ratatouille in the water and cook for 1 hour. Step 2: Prepare the Bell Peppers While the ratatouille is cooking, prepare the bell peppers by cutting off the tops and removing the seeds and membranes from the inside. Blanch the bell peppers in boiling water for 2-3 minutes, then immediately transfer them to an ice water bath to stop the cooking process. Once the peppers have cooled, pat them dry with paper towels. Step 3: Assemble and Cook the Stuffed Peppers After the ratatouille has finished cooking, remove the bags from the water bath and carefully open them to release the aromas. Preheat the water bath of the sous vide machine to 165°F (74°C). Stuff each bell pepper with the sous vide ratatouille mixture, ensuring that each pepper is generously filled. Place the stuffed peppers in a single layer in a vacuum-sealable bag, ensuring they are not overcrowded. Seal the bag using a vacuum sealer and submerge it in the water bath. Cook the stuffed peppers for 1 hour to allow the flavors to meld and the peppers to become tender. Once cooked, carefully remove the stuffed peppers from the bag and place them on a serving platter. Step 4: Serve and Enjoy Garnish the stuffed peppers with fresh basil leaves for a pop of color and a hint of freshness. These Sous Vide Ratatouille Stuffed Peppers are best enjoyed warm, allowing the flavors to fully develop. ...
