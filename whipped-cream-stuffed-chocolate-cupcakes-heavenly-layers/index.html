Whipped Cream Stuffed Chocolate Cupcakes: Heavenly Layers


Prepare for a scrumptious treat that will have your taste buds dancing with joy! These divine whipped cream stuffed chocolate cupcakes consist of rich, moist chocolate cake interspersed with fluffy whipped cream – a delightful marriage of textures and flavors that takes chocolate cupcakes to a whole new level. Today, we’re sharing this incredible dessert recipe that’s sure to make you the talk of your next gathering

Ingredients:

For the Chocolate Cupcakes:

  • 1.5 cups all-purpose flour
  • 3/4 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1.5 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1.5 teaspoons baking soda
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup unsalted butter, at room temperature
  • 1.5 cups granulated sugar
  • 4 large eggs
  • 1.5 teaspoons pure vanilla extract
  • 1 cup buttermilk
  • 1/2 cup strong brewed coffee, cooled
  • 1/2 cup unsweetened natural cocoa powder

For the Whipped Cream Filling:

  • 1 cup cold heavy whipping cream
  • 2 tablespoons granulated sugar
  • 1 teaspoon pure vanilla extract
  • 1/2 teaspoon pure almond extract

For the Chocolate Ganache Frosting:

  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 12 ounces semisweet chocolate, chopped

Instructions:

Prepare the Chocolate Cupcakes

  1. Preheat Oven: Begin by preheating your oven to 350°F (175°C) and lining two 12-cup muffin tins with your favorite cupcake liners.

  2. Mix Dry Ingredients: In a medium bowl, sift together the flour, cocoa powder, baking powder, baking soda, and salt. Set it aside for now.

  3. C cream Butter and Sugar: With an electric mixer, cream together the butter and sugar until light and fluffy, which should take about 3 minutes on medium speed.

  4. Add Eggs: Gradually incorporate the eggs, one at a time, mixing well after each addition and scraping down the sides of the bowl as necessary.

  5. Add Flavor: Stir in the vanilla extract.

  6. Alternate Adding Ingredients: Gently mix in the flour mixture and buttermilk alternately, in three batches, beginning and ending with the flour mixture. Stir in the cooled coffee and the additional 1/2 cup cocoa, ensuring that everything is well incorporated.

  7. Bake: Using an ice cream scoop or spoon, fill each liner 2/3 full with the batter. Bake in the preheated oven for roughly 18-21 minutes or until a toothpick inserted in the center comes out clean.

  8. Cool: Allow the cupcakes to cool in the pans for a few minutes before transferring to a wire rack to cool completely.

Prepare the Whipped Cream Filling

  1. Whip Cream: In a large bowl, combine the heavy cream, sugar, vanilla extract, and almond extract. Whip the cream with an electric mixer on high speed until the mixture holds stiff peaks.

Prepare Chocolate Ganache Frosting

  1. Heat Cream: Bring the heavy cream to a simmer in a saucepan.

  2. Melt Chocolate: Place the chopped chocolate in a large heatproof bowl.

  3. Combine: Once the cream has reached a simmer, pour it over the chocolate, stirring gently with a whisk until the chocolate has melted and the ganache is smooth.

Assemble the Cupcakes

  1. Create Well: Using a cupcake corer or a sharp small knife, remove a small portion from the center of each cooled cupcake to create a well.

  2. Stuff with Whipped Cream: Fill the wells with the whipped cream.

  3. Frost: Pour the ganache into a saucepan and reheat it gently until it’s smooth and pourable. Allow it to cool for a minute or two, then pour it over the cupcakes.
