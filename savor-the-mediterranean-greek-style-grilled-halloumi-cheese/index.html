Savor the Mediterranean: Greek-style Grilled Halloumi Cheese


If you’re looking for a delicious and unique addition to your Mediterranean-inspired menu, Greek-style grilled halloumi cheese is a must-try. This traditional Cypriot cheese has a high melting point, making it perfect for grilling and frying. When prepared with Mediterranean flavors, it becomes a savory and satisfying dish that will transport your taste buds to the sun-soaked shores of Greece.

Ingredients

  • 1 block of halloumi cheese
  • 2 tablespoons of olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon of dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon of dried thyme
  • 1 teaspoon of crushed red pepper flakes
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • Zest of 1 lemon
  • Freshly ground black pepper

Instructions

  1. Begin by slicing the halloumi cheese into thick slices, about 1/2 inch in thickness. Pat the slices dry with a paper towel to remove any excess moisture.

  2. In a small bowl, combine the olive oil, dried oregano, dried thyme, crushed red pepper flakes, minced garlic, and lemon zest. Stir the mixture well to ensure the flavors are evenly distributed.

  3. Place the halloumi slices in a shallow dish and pour the marinade over the cheese, making sure each slice is coated evenly. Allow the cheese to marinate for at least 30 minutes, allowing the flavors to infuse.

  4. Preheat your grill to medium-high heat. Once the grill is hot, place the halloumi slices directly on the grates. Grill for 2-3 minutes on each side, or until grill marks form and the cheese is golden brown.

  5. As the halloumi cheese grills, sprinkle freshly ground black pepper over the slices to add an extra layer of flavor.

  6. Once the halloumi is grilled to perfection, remove it from the heat and transfer it to a serving platter. Drizzle any remaining marinade over the grilled cheese for an extra burst of flavor.

  7. Serve the Greek-style grilled halloumi cheese immediately, while it’s still warm and gooey. You can accompany it with a side of fresh Greek salad, olives, and warm pita bread for a complete Mediterranean experience.

Tips and Variations

  • For a touch of sweetness, consider adding a drizzle of honey or a balsamic glaze to the grilled halloumi before serving.
  • Experiment with different herbs and spices to customize the flavor profile of the marinade. Fresh rosemary, basil, or mint can add a refreshing twist to the dish.
  • If you don’t have access to a grill, you can also pan-fry the halloumi cheese in a lightly oiled skillet over medium-high heat until golden brown on both sides.

Embrace the Mediterranean Flavors

Greek-style grilled halloumi cheese is a versatile dish that can be served as an appetizer, a side dish, or even as the star of a vegetarian main course. Its unique texture and robust flavor make it a standout addition to any Mediterranean-inspired meal. Whether you’re hosting a summer barbecue or simply craving a taste of the Mediterranean, this recipe is sure to impress your guests and transport them to the azure waters and sun-drenched landscapes of Greece. So fire up the grill, savor the flavors, and enjoy a taste of the Mediterranean with this delightful Greek-style grilled halloumi cheese.
