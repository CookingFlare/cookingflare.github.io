Fire Up Your Grill: 5 Tangy Marinades for BBQ Chicken Thighs


Are you ready to take your BBQ game to the next level? Look no further than these mouthwatering marinades for BBQ chicken thighs that will make your taste buds dance with joy. Whether you’re hosting a backyard cookout or simply craving a delicious grilled meal, these tangy marinades will add a burst of flavor to your chicken thighs. Let’s fire up the grill and get cooking!

1. Lemon and Herb Marinade

Ingredients:

  • 1/4 cup fresh lemon juice
  • Zest of 1 lemon
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon fresh rosemary, chopped
  • 1 tablespoon fresh thyme, chopped
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, combine the lemon juice, lemon zest, minced garlic, olive oil, rosemary, thyme, salt, and pepper.
  2. Place the chicken thighs in a resealable plastic bag and pour the marinade over them.
  3. Seal the bag and refrigerate for at least 2 hours, or overnight for maximum flavor.
  4. Preheat your grill to medium-high heat and lightly oil the grates.
  5. Grill the chicken thighs for about 6-8 minutes per side, or until they reach an internal temperature of 165°F (74°C).
  6. Remove from the grill and let them rest for a few minutes before serving.

2. Sweet and Spicy Marinade

Ingredients:

  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons honey
  • 2 tablespoons ketchup
  • 1 tablespoon Sriracha sauce
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon ginger, grated
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, whisk together the soy sauce, honey, ketchup, Sriracha sauce, minced garlic, grated ginger, salt, and pepper.
  2. Place the chicken thighs in a resealable plastic bag and pour the marinade over them.
  3. Seal the bag and refrigerate for at least 1 hour, or up to 4 hours for maximum flavor.
  4. Preheat your grill to medium-high heat and lightly oil the grates.
  5. Grill the chicken thighs for about 6-8 minutes per side, or until they reach an internal temperature of 165°F (74°C).
  6. Remove from the grill and let them rest for a few minutes before serving.

3. Teriyaki Marinade

Ingredients:

  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons rice vinegar
  • 2 tablespoons brown sugar
  • 1 tablespoon sesame oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon ginger, grated

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, whisk together the soy sauce, rice vinegar, brown sugar, sesame oil, minced garlic, and grated ginger.
  2. Place the chicken thighs in a resealable plastic bag and pour the marinade over them.
  3. Seal the bag and refrigerate for at least 30 minutes, or up to 2 hours for maximum flavor.
  4. Preheat your grill to medium-high heat and lightly oil the grates.
  5. Grill the chicken thighs for about 6-8 minutes per side, or until they reach an internal temperature of 165°F (74°C).
  6. Remove from the grill and let them rest for a few minutes before serving.

4. Honey Mustard Marinade

Ingredients:

  • 1/4 cup Dijon mustard
  • 2 tablespoons honey
  • 2 tablespoons apple cider vinegar
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, whisk together the Dijon mustard, honey, apple cider vinegar, minced garlic, paprika, salt, and pepper.
  2. Place the chicken thighs in a resealable plastic bag and pour the marinade over them.
  3. Seal the bag and refrigerate for at least 1 hour, or up to 4 hours for maximum flavor.
  4. Preheat your grill to medium-high heat and lightly oil the grates.
  5. Grill the chicken thighs for about 6-8 minutes per side, or until they reach an internal temperature of 165°F (74°C).
  6. Remove from the grill and let them rest for a few minutes before serving.

5. Pineapple and Ginger Marinade

Ingredients:

  • 1/4 cup pineapple juice
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons brown sugar
  • 1 tablespoon fresh ginger, grated
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon sesame oil

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, whisk together the pineapple juice, soy sauce, brown sugar, grated ginger, minced garlic, and sesame oil.
  2. Place the chicken thighs in a resealable plastic bag and pour the marinade over them.
  3. Seal the bag and refrigerate for at least 30 minutes, or up to 2 hours for maximum flavor.
  4. Preheat your grill to medium-high heat and lightly oil the grates.
  5. Grill the chicken thighs for about 6-8 minutes per side, or until they reach an internal temperature of 165°F (74°C).
  6. Remove from the grill and let them rest for a few minutes before serving.

These tangy marinades will transform your BBQ chicken thighs into a flavor-packed delight. Whether you prefer the zesty freshness of lemon and herbs, the sweet and spicy kick of honey and Sriracha, the savory goodness of teriyaki, the classic combination of honey and mustard, or the tropical twist of pineapple and ginger, there’s a marinade to suit every palate. So fire up your grill, marinate those chicken thighs, and get ready for a BBQ feast like no other. Enjoy!
