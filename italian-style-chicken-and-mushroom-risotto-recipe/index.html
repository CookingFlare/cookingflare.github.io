Italian-Style Chicken and Mushroom Risotto Recipe


If you’re looking for a comforting and flavorful dish to elevate your dinner table, Italian-style chicken and mushroom risotto is a perfect choice. This creamy, savory dish is a labor of love, requiring patience and attention to detail, but the end result is well worth the effort. The combination of tender chicken, earthy mushrooms, and creamy Arborio rice is a culinary delight that will impress your family and friends.

Ingredients

  • 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1 cup Arborio rice
  • 4 cups chicken broth
  • 1 cup dry white wine
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 8 oz. cremini mushrooms, sliced
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley for garnish

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Chicken: Season the chicken breasts with salt and pepper. In a large skillet, heat 1 tablespoon of olive oil over medium-high heat. Add the chicken breasts and cook until golden brown and cooked through, about 6-7 minutes per side. Remove the chicken from the skillet and let it rest for a few minutes before slicing it into bite-sized pieces.

  2. Sauté the Mushrooms: In the same skillet, melt 1 tablespoon of butter over medium heat. Add the sliced mushrooms and sauté until they are golden brown and all the liquid has evaporated, about 5-7 minutes. Remove the mushrooms from the skillet and set them aside.

  3. Cook the Aromatics: In a large pot, heat the remaining tablespoon of olive oil over medium heat. Add the chopped onion and garlic, and sauté until the onion is translucent and fragrant, about 3-4 minutes.

  4. Toast the Rice: Add the Arborio rice to the pot with the aromatics and stir to coat the rice with the oil. Cook for 1-2 minutes, stirring constantly, until the edges of the rice become translucent.

  5. Add the Wine: Pour the white wine into the pot and stir until the liquid is absorbed by the rice.

  6. Simmer the Risotto: Add a ladleful of chicken broth to the pot and stir the rice frequently until the liquid is absorbed. Continue adding the broth, one ladleful at a time, stirring constantly and allowing each addition to be absorbed before adding the next. This process will take about 18-20 minutes, and the rice should be creamy but still slightly al dente.

  7. Incorporate the Chicken and Mushrooms: Once the risotto is cooked to your desired consistency, stir in the cooked chicken and mushrooms. Add the grated Parmesan cheese and the remaining tablespoon of butter, and stir until the cheese is melted and the risotto is creamy.

  8. Serve and Garnish: Divide the risotto among serving plates, garnish with fresh parsley, and serve immediately.

Tips for Success

  • Use Arborio rice for its high starch content, which is essential for achieving the creamy texture of risotto.
  • Stir the risotto frequently to release the starch from the rice, creating a velvety texture.
  • For a vegetarian version, substitute vegetable broth for the chicken broth and omit the chicken. You can also add other vegetables like peas or asparagus for a pop of color and flavor.

Final Thoughts

Italian-style chicken and mushroom risotto is a timeless classic that never fails to impress. The creamy texture, rich flavors, and comforting aroma of this dish make it a standout choice for a special dinner at home. Whether you’re a seasoned home cook or new to the world of risotto, this recipe is sure to become a favorite in your culinary repertoire. So, roll up your sleeves, pour yourself a glass of wine, and get ready to create a memorable dining experience with this delightful Italian dish. Buon appetito!
