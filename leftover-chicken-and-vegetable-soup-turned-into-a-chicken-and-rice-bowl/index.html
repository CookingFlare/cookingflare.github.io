Leftover Chicken and Vegetable Soup Turned into a Chicken and Rice Bowl


If you’re anything like me, you love a good hearty soup. There’s something so comforting about a warm bowl of chicken and vegetable soup, especially when it’s homemade. However, sometimes we find ourselves with a bit of leftover soup in the refrigerator, and while reheating it for another meal is an option, why not take it to the next level? In this recipe, we’re going to transform that leftover chicken and vegetable soup into a delicious chicken and rice bowl that’s perfect for a quick and satisfying meal.

Ingredients

  • Leftover chicken and vegetable soup
  • Cooked white or brown rice
  • Shredded cooked chicken (if not already in the soup)
  • Chopped fresh vegetables (such as bell peppers, carrots, or snow peas)
  • Soy sauce
  • Sesame oil
  • Green onions, chopped
  • Sriracha or chili garlic sauce (optional)
  • Sesame seeds for garnish

Instructions

Step 1: Prepare the Rice

If you don’t already have cooked rice on hand, start by cooking your desired amount of white or brown rice according to the package instructions. Fluffy rice makes the perfect base for our chicken and rice bowl.

Step 2: Transform the Soup

Take your leftover chicken and vegetable soup and pour it into a large pot. If the soup doesn’t already contain shredded cooked chicken, add some to the pot. Heat the soup over medium heat until it comes to a gentle simmer.

Step 3: Add Fresh Vegetables

While the soup is heating, prepare some fresh vegetables to add a pop of color and crunch to the dish. Sliced bell peppers, julienned carrots, or snow peas work well in this recipe. Add the vegetables to the simmering soup and cook for a few minutes until they are just tender-crisp.

Step 4: Season the Chicken and Rice Bowl

Once the soup and vegetables are heated through, it’s time to add flavor to our chicken and rice bowl. Drizzle in a splash of soy sauce and sesame oil, adjusting the amounts to suit your taste. These ingredients will give the dish a savory, umami-rich flavor.

Step 5: Assemble the Chicken and Rice Bowl

In serving bowls, ladle a generous portion of the transformed chicken and vegetable soup over a bed of cooked rice. The combination of the tender chicken, flavorful broth, and vibrant vegetables will create a beautiful and inviting bowl.

Step 6: Garnish and Enjoy

To finish the dish, sprinkle chopped green onions and sesame seeds over the top for a burst of freshness and nuttiness. If you like a bit of heat, add a drizzle of sriracha or chili garlic sauce for a spicy kick. Now, grab your chopsticks or a fork and dig into your homemade chicken and rice bowl.

Conclusion

Transforming leftover chicken and vegetable soup into a chicken and rice bowl is a fantastic way to breathe new life into a familiar dish. The addition of fresh vegetables, rice, and a few simple seasonings takes the soup to a whole new level, creating a comforting and satisfying meal that’s perfect for lunch or dinner. Next time you find yourself with leftover soup in the fridge, consider giving this recipe a try. It’s a delicious way to minimize food waste while enjoying a delightful and flavorful meal.
