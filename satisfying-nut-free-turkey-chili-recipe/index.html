Satisfying Nut-Free Turkey Chili Recipe


Are you looking for a comforting and hearty meal that’s perfect for a cozy night in? Look no further than this delicious nut-free turkey chili recipe! Packed with flavor, protein, and wholesome ingredients, this chili is a crowd-pleaser that will leave everyone satisfied. Whether you’re hosting a gathering or simply craving a comforting bowl of warmth, this recipe is sure to become a staple in your kitchen.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground turkey
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 red bell pepper, diced
  • 1 green bell pepper, diced
  • 1 can (15 oz) diced tomatoes
  • 1 can (15 oz) tomato sauce
  • 1 can (15 oz) black beans, drained and rinsed
  • 1 can (15 oz) kidney beans, drained and rinsed
  • 2 cups chicken broth
  • 2 tablespoons chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Optional toppings: shredded cheese, sour cream, diced avocado, chopped cilantro

Instructions

  1. In a large pot or Dutch oven, cook the ground turkey over medium heat until no longer pink. Use a wooden spoon to break it into crumbles.

  2. Add the diced onion, garlic, and bell peppers to the pot. Cook for 3-4 minutes, or until the vegetables begin to soften.

  3. Stir in the diced tomatoes, tomato sauce, black beans, kidney beans, chicken broth, chili powder, cumin, paprika, oregano, salt, and pepper.

  4. Bring the chili to a simmer, then reduce the heat to low. Let it simmer for 30-40 minutes, stirring occasionally.

  5. Taste the chili and adjust the seasoning if needed.

  6. Serve the nut-free turkey chili hot, topped with your choice of shredded cheese, sour cream, diced avocado, and chopped cilantro.

Why You’ll Love This Chili

This nut-free turkey chili is not only delicious, but it’s also a great source of lean protein and fiber. The combination of ground turkey and beans makes it a satisfying and nutritious meal. Additionally, the vibrant colors of the bell peppers add a pop of freshness to the dish, while the aromatic blend of spices creates a comforting and inviting aroma in your kitchen.

This recipe is perfect for those with nut allergies or dietary restrictions, as it excludes any nuts or nut-based ingredients. It’s a versatile dish that can easily be customized to suit your taste preferences. Whether you prefer a mild chili or enjoy a little heat, you can adjust the seasoning to your liking.

Not to mention, this chili is a fantastic make-ahead meal that tastes even better the next day, making it ideal for meal prepping or leftovers. It’s a great option for busy weeknights when you want a fuss-free dinner that doesn’t compromise on flavor.

So, if you’re in the mood for a comforting and nourishing meal that’s easy to make and full of flavor, give this nut-free turkey chili recipe a try. Your taste buds and your loved ones will thank you!

Wrapping Up

This nut-free turkey chili is a versatile and satisfying dish that’s perfect for any occasion. With its simple preparation and delightful flavors, it’s a recipe that will quickly become a favorite in your household. Whether you’re cooking for a crowd or simply treating yourself to a cozy night in, this chili is a wonderful option that’s sure to leave a lasting impression. Enjoy the warmth and comfort of a bowl of this delicious chili, and savor every spoonful of its hearty goodness.
