Grilled Halloumi: Recipes for the Perfect BBQ Cheese


When it comes to grilling, most people immediately think of steaks, burgers, and hot dogs. However, there’s a delicious and unexpected addition that can take your BBQ game to the next level: grilled halloumi. This unique cheese is the perfect addition to any grill session, offering a salty, chewy texture with a satisfyingly crispy char when grilled. In this blog post, we’ll explore the versatility of halloumi and provide you with a variety of mouthwatering recipes to elevate your BBQ experience.

What is Halloumi?

Before we dive into the recipes, let’s talk about what halloumi actually is. Halloumi is a semi-hard, unripened cheese that hails from Cyprus. It’s traditionally made from a mixture of goat’s and sheep’s milk, giving it a rich and tangy flavor. One of the most unique aspects of halloumi is its high melting point, making it the perfect cheese for grilling. When heated, halloumi develops a golden-brown crust while maintaining its firm, chewy interior, making it a delightful addition to any BBQ spread.

Grilled Halloumi Skewers

Ingredients:

  • 1 block of halloumi cheese, cut into cubes
  • Cherry tomatoes
  • Red onion, cut into chunks
  • Bell peppers, cut into chunks
  • Olive oil
  • Fresh oregano
  • Salt and pepper

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your grill to medium-high heat.
  2. Assemble the skewers by alternating the halloumi cubes, cherry tomatoes, red onion, and bell peppers.
  3. Drizzle the skewers with olive oil and season with salt, pepper, and fresh oregano.
  4. Grill the skewers for 2-3 minutes on each side, or until the halloumi develops a golden-brown crust.
  5. Serve the skewers hot off the grill and enjoy!

Grilled Halloumi Salad

Ingredients:

  • 1 block of halloumi cheese, cut into slices
  • Mixed salad greens
  • Cherry tomatoes
  • Cucumber, sliced
  • Kalamata olives
  • Red onion, thinly sliced
  • Olive oil
  • Balsamic vinegar
  • Fresh lemon juice
  • Salt and pepper

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your grill to medium heat.
  2. Brush the halloumi slices with olive oil and place them on the grill.
  3. Grill the halloumi for 2-3 minutes on each side, or until grill marks form.
  4. In a large bowl, toss the mixed salad greens, cherry tomatoes, cucumber, kalamata olives, and red onion.
  5. In a small bowl, whisk together olive oil, balsamic vinegar, and fresh lemon juice to create the dressing.
  6. Place the grilled halloumi on top of the salad, drizzle with the dressing, and season with salt and pepper.
  7. Serve the grilled halloumi salad as a refreshing and satisfying BBQ side dish.

Grilled Halloumi Burgers

Ingredients:

  • 1 block of halloumi cheese, cut into thick slices
  • Burger buns
  • Romaine lettuce
  • Sliced tomatoes
  • Red onion, thinly sliced
  • Burger sauce
  • Olive oil
  • Salt and pepper

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your grill to medium-high heat.
  2. Brush the halloumi slices with olive oil and season with salt and pepper.
  3. Grill the halloumi for 2-3 minutes on each side, or until golden and crispy.
  4. Assemble the burgers by placing the grilled halloumi on the burger buns, along with lettuce, tomatoes, red onion, and burger sauce.
  5. Serve the grilled halloumi burgers alongside your favorite BBQ sides for a delightful meatless option.

Conclusion

Grilled halloumi is a versatile and delicious addition to any BBQ menu. Whether you’re looking for a meatless option or want to surprise your guests with a unique and flavorful dish, these recipes are sure to impress. So, fire up the grill, grab some halloumi, and get ready to elevate your BBQ game with the irresistible taste of grilled halloumi. Cheers to delicious grilling!
