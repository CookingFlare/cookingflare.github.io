Creamy and Dreamy Mint Chocolate Chip Ice Cream


Who doesn’t love the refreshing taste of mint combined with the indulgence of chocolate? Mint chocolate chip ice cream is a classic treat that never fails to satisfy your sweet tooth. In this recipe, we’ll show you how to make a creamy and dreamy mint chocolate chip ice cream right in the comfort of your own home.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups heavy cream
  • 1 cup whole milk
  • 3/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon pure vanilla extract
  • 1 teaspoon peppermint extract
  • Green food coloring (optional)
  • 1 cup chocolate chips

Instructions

  1. In a medium saucepan, combine the heavy cream, whole milk, granulated sugar, and salt. Heat the mixture over medium heat, stirring occasionally, until it reaches a simmer. Make sure the sugar is fully dissolved.

  2. Remove the saucepan from the heat and stir in the vanilla extract and peppermint extract. If desired, add a few drops of green food coloring to give your ice cream that classic minty hue.

  3. Transfer the mixture to a bowl and cover it with plastic wrap, making sure the plastic wrap touches the surface of the mixture. This will prevent a skin from forming. Chill the mixture in the refrigerator for at least 4 hours, or overnight.

  4. Once the mixture is thoroughly chilled, pour it into your ice cream maker and churn according to the manufacturer’s instructions. This usually takes about 20-25 minutes, but the time may vary depending on your machine.

  5. While the ice cream is churning, chop the chocolate chips into smaller pieces. You want them to be bite-sized, but not too small.

  6. About 5 minutes before the churning process is complete, add the chocolate chips to the ice cream maker. Let it mix for the remaining time, allowing the chips to distribute evenly.

  7. Once the ice cream is done churning, transfer it to an airtight container and freeze for at least 2 hours, or until firm.

  8. When you’re ready to serve, scoop out the creamy and dreamy mint chocolate chip ice cream into bowls or cones. Enjoy it on its own or as a delightful accompaniment to your favorite desserts.

Tips and Variations

  • For an extra burst of minty freshness, you can add some finely chopped fresh mint leaves to the ice cream mixture before chilling.
  • If you prefer a smoother texture, you can strain the mixture after heating to remove any possible lumps.
  • Feel free to experiment with different types of chocolate. Dark chocolate, white chocolate, or even mint-flavored chocolate can all add a unique twist to your ice cream.
  • If you don’t have an ice cream maker, you can still make this recipe. Simply pour the chilled mixture into a shallow dish and place it in the freezer. Every 30 minutes, remove the dish from the freezer and use a fork to stir and break up any ice crystals. Repeat this process until the ice cream reaches the desired consistency.

Now that you have this delicious recipe in your arsenal, go ahead and treat yourself to a bowl of creamy and dreamy mint chocolate chip ice cream. It’s the perfect dessert for those hot summer days or any time you’re craving a cool and refreshing indulgence. Enjoy!
