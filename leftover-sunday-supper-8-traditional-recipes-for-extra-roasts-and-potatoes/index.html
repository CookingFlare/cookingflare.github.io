Leftover Sunday Supper: 8 Traditional Recipes for Extra Roasts and Potatoes


Are you tired of staring at that leftover roast and potatoes in your fridge from Sunday’s dinner? Don’t let them go to waste! With a little creativity and some simple ingredients, you can transform those leftovers into delicious meals that will have your family begging for more. In this post, we will share with you eight traditional recipes that will breathe new life into your extra roasts and potatoes. Let’s get cooking!

1. Roast Beef Hash

Take your leftover roast beef and potatoes and chop them into small, bite-sized pieces. In a skillet, heat some olive oil and sauté diced onions until translucent. Add the chopped beef and potatoes, season with salt, pepper, and your favorite herbs, and cook until everything is heated through and slightly crispy. Serve with a fried egg on top for a hearty and satisfying breakfast or brunch.

2. Shepherd’s Pie

Shepherd’s Pie is a classic comfort food dish that is perfect for using up leftover roast and potatoes. Start by sautéing onions, carrots, and peas in a skillet until they are tender. Add the chopped roast beef and potatoes, along with some beef broth and Worcestershire sauce for extra flavor. Transfer the mixture to a baking dish and top with a layer of mashed potatoes. Bake in the oven until the top is golden brown and the filling is bubbling. Enjoy the warm and comforting flavors of this traditional dish.

3. Roast Beef and Potato Soup

Soup is a great way to use up leftovers, and this Roast Beef and Potato Soup is no exception. In a large pot, sauté onions, garlic, and celery until fragrant. Add beef broth, diced tomatoes, chopped roast beef, and diced potatoes. Season with salt, pepper, and your favorite herbs. Simmer until the potatoes are tender and the flavors have melded together. Serve with a crusty bread for a satisfying and cozy meal.

4. Roast Beef and Potato Tacos

Who says leftovers can’t be fun? Turn your roast beef and potatoes into delicious tacos. Warm up some tortillas and fill them with shredded roast beef, diced potatoes, and your favorite toppings like salsa, guacamole, and cheese. Add a squeeze of lime for a burst of freshness. These tacos are perfect for a quick and easy weeknight dinner or a fun taco night with friends.

5. Roast Beef and Potato Croquettes

Transform your leftovers into crispy, golden brown croquettes. Mash the leftover potatoes and mix them with the chopped roast beef, grated cheese, and breadcrumbs. Form the mixture into small patties and coat them in more breadcrumbs. Fry them in hot oil until they are golden brown and crispy on the outside. Serve with a dipping sauce of your choice for a delicious appetizer or snack.

6. Roast Beef and Potato Salad

If you’re in the mood for something lighter, try making a roast beef and potato salad. Chop the leftover roast beef and potatoes into bite-sized pieces and toss them with mixed greens, cherry tomatoes, sliced cucumbers, and your favorite salad dressing. Add some crumbled feta or blue cheese for extra flavor. This salad is refreshing, satisfying, and perfect for a quick and healthy lunch.

7. Roast Beef and Potato Curry

Spice things up with a flavorful roast beef and potato curry. In a large pot, sauté onions, garlic, and ginger until fragrant. Add curry powder, cumin, coriander, and turmeric, and cook until the spices are toasted. Add chopped roast beef, diced potatoes, coconut milk, and vegetable broth. Simmer until the potatoes are tender and the flavors have melded together. Serve with steamed rice and naan bread for a delicious and aromatic meal.

8. Roast Beef and Potato Hand Pies

Last but not least, indulge in some savory hand pies filled with roast beef and potatoes. Roll out some store-bought puff pastry and cut it into squares. Fill each square with a spoonful of the chopped roast beef and potatoes, fold it over, and seal the edges with a fork. Brush the tops with an egg wash and bake in the oven until they are golden brown and flaky. These hand pies are perfect for a quick and portable meal or a tasty snack on the go.

Conclusion

Don’t let your leftover roast and potatoes go to waste! With these eight traditional recipes, you can turn them into delicious meals that will satisfy your cravings and impress your family and friends. From comforting soups to crispy tacos, there’s something for everyone. Get creative in the kitchen and enjoy the flavors of these hearty and flavorful dishes. Happy cooking!
