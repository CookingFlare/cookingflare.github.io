Succulent BBQ Chicken Recipes for Summer Gatherings


Summer is the perfect time to gather with friends and family and enjoy the great outdoors. And what better way to celebrate than with a delicious BBQ? If you’re looking for some succulent BBQ chicken recipes to impress your guests at your next summer gathering, you’ve come to the right place. In this blog post, we’ll be sharing three mouthwatering BBQ chicken recipes that are sure to be a hit. So grab your apron and fire up the grill, because it’s time to get cooking!

1. Honey Mustard BBQ Chicken Skewers

Ingredients:

  • 2 pounds of chicken breast, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 1/4 cup of honey
  • 1/4 cup of Dijon mustard
  • 2 tablespoons of soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon of olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon of garlic powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, whisk together the honey, Dijon mustard, soy sauce, olive oil, garlic powder, salt, and pepper until well combined.
  2. Add the chicken pieces to the bowl and toss until they are evenly coated in the marinade. Let the chicken marinate in the refrigerator for at least 30 minutes.
  3. Preheat your grill to medium-high heat.
  4. Thread the chicken pieces onto skewers, leaving a little space between each piece.
  5. Grill the skewers for about 10 minutes, turning them occasionally, until the chicken is cooked through and slightly charred.
  6. Remove the skewers from the grill and let them rest for a few minutes before serving. Enjoy!

2. Spicy BBQ Chicken Wings

Ingredients:

  • 2 pounds of chicken wings
  • 1/4 cup of ketchup
  • 2 tablespoons of hot sauce
  • 2 tablespoons of Worcestershire sauce
  • 2 tablespoons of brown sugar
  • 1 tablespoon of apple cider vinegar
  • 1 teaspoon of smoked paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon of garlic powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, whisk together the ketchup, hot sauce, Worcestershire sauce, brown sugar, apple cider vinegar, smoked paprika, garlic powder, salt, and pepper until well combined.
  2. Place the chicken wings in a large resealable plastic bag and pour the marinade over them. Seal the bag and refrigerate for at least 2 hours, or overnight for best flavor.
  3. Preheat your grill to medium heat.
  4. Remove the chicken wings from the marinade and discard the excess marinade.
  5. Grill the wings for about 15-20 minutes, turning them occasionally, until they are cooked through and crispy.
  6. Serve the spicy BBQ chicken wings with your favorite dipping sauce and enjoy!

3. Tangy BBQ Chicken Drumsticks

Ingredients:

  • 2 pounds of chicken drumsticks
  • 1/2 cup of BBQ sauce
  • 1/4 cup of orange juice
  • 2 tablespoons of brown sugar
  • 1 tablespoon of soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon of apple cider vinegar
  • 1 teaspoon of smoked paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon of garlic powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, whisk together the BBQ sauce, orange juice, brown sugar, soy sauce, apple cider vinegar, smoked paprika, garlic powder, salt, and pepper until well combined.
  2. Place the chicken drumsticks in a large resealable plastic bag and pour the marinade over them. Seal the bag and refrigerate for at least 2 hours, or overnight for best flavor.
  3. Preheat your grill to medium-high heat.
  4. Remove the chicken drumsticks from the marinade and discard the excess marinade.
  5. Grill the drumsticks for about 20-25 minutes, turning them occasionally, until they are cooked through and the skin is crispy.
  6. Serve the tangy BBQ chicken drumsticks with a side of coleslaw and enjoy!

These succulent BBQ chicken recipes are guaranteed to be a hit at your summer gatherings. Whether you prefer the sweet and tangy flavors of the honey mustard skewers, the spicy kick of the BBQ wings, or the tanginess of the drumsticks, there’s something for everyone. So fire up the grill, gather your loved ones, and enjoy a delicious summer feast. Happy grilling!
