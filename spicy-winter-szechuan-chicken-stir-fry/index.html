Spicy Winter Szechuan Chicken Stir-Fry


Winter is the perfect time to indulge in comforting, spicy dishes, and this Spicy Winter Szechuan Chicken Stir-Fry is just the ticket to warm you up on a cold evening. Packed with bold flavors and a kick of heat, this dish is a celebration of the vibrant and aromatic Szechuan cuisine. Here’s how to bring the heat to your kitchen with this flavorful recipe.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound boneless, skinless chicken breast, cut into thin strips
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon Shaoxing wine (or dry sherry)
  • 2 teaspoons cornstarch
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon ginger, minced
  • 1 tablespoon Szechuan peppercorns, crushed
  • 1-2 tablespoons Szechuan chili flakes
  • 1 red bell pepper, thinly sliced
  • 1 green bell pepper, thinly sliced
  • 1 cup snow peas
  • 4 green onions, sliced
  • Cooked white rice, for serving

Instructions

  1. In a bowl, combine the chicken strips with soy sauce, Shaoxing wine, and cornstarch. Toss to coat the chicken evenly and set aside to marinate for 15-20 minutes.

  2. Heat a wok or a large skillet over high heat. Add the vegetable oil and swirl to coat the bottom of the pan. Add the marinated chicken and stir-fry for 3-4 minutes until the chicken is golden and cooked through. Remove the chicken from the wok and set aside.

  3. In the same wok, add a bit more oil if needed and reduce the heat to medium. Add the minced garlic, ginger, crushed Szechuan peppercorns, and Szechuan chili flakes. Stir-fry for 1-2 minutes until the aromatics are fragrant and the chili flakes have infused the oil.

  4. Add the sliced bell peppers and snow peas to the wok. Stir-fry for 2-3 minutes until the vegetables are tender-crisp.

  5. Return the cooked chicken to the wok and toss to combine with the vegetables.

  6. Add the sliced green onions and give everything a final toss.

  7. Serve the Spicy Winter Szechuan Chicken Stir-Fry over steamed white rice and enjoy the fiery flavors of Szechuan cuisine.

Tips for Success

  • Adjust the amount of Szechuan chili flakes according to your heat preference. Start with a smaller amount and adjust to your taste.
  • Crush the Szechuan peppercorns just before using to maximize their flavor.
  • Ensure all the ingredients are prepped and ready to go before you start stir-frying, as the process is quick and requires constant attention.

Why We Love This Dish

The Spicy Winter Szechuan Chicken Stir-Fry is a delightful blend of heat, fragrance, and texture. The bold flavors of the Szechuan peppercorns and chili flakes infuse the dish with a distinctive and invigorating kick that is perfect for warming up during the winter months. The combination of tender chicken, crunchy vegetables, and aromatic spices makes this stir-fry a complete sensory experience.

There’s something truly satisfying about the balance of flavors in Szechuan cuisine, and this dish exemplifies that beautifully. It’s a testament to the culinary art of achieving harmony between heat, spice, and freshness. Whether you’re a fan of spicy food or looking to explore the vibrant world of Szechuan flavors, this stir-fry is sure to captivate your taste buds and leave you craving more.

So, if you’re ready to embrace the fiery allure of Szechuan cuisine this winter, give this Spicy Winter Szechuan Chicken Stir-Fry a try. It’s a flavorful journey that promises to warm you from the inside out, leaving you with a newfound appreciation for the bold and aromatic wonders of Chinese cooking.
