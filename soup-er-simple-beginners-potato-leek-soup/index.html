Soup-er Simple: Beginner's Potato Leek Soup


Welcome to our kitchen, where we’re cooking up something soul-warming and delicious - Potato Leek Soup! This timeless classic is perfect for beginner cooks and seasoned chefs alike. With just a handful of simple ingredients and easy steps, you can create a hearty, creamy soup that’s sure to become a favorite in your recipe collection.

Ingredients

  • 4 large potatoes, peeled and diced
  • 3 leeks, white and light green parts only, sliced
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 4 cups of vegetable or chicken broth
  • 1 cup of heavy cream
  • 2 tablespoons of butter
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh chives for garnish (optional)

Instructions

Step 1: Prep the Veggies

Start by preparing your vegetables. Peel and dice the potatoes, then slice the leeks, making sure to rinse them thoroughly to remove any dirt or grit. Chop the onion and mince the garlic.

Step 2: Sauté the Aromatics

In a large pot over medium heat, melt the butter. Add the chopped onion and minced garlic, and sauté until they become fragrant and translucent. This step will fill your kitchen with an irresistible aroma, setting the stage for a delicious soup.

Step 3: Cook the Potatoes and Leeks

Once the aromatics are ready, add the diced potatoes and sliced leeks to the pot. Stir everything together, allowing the vegetables to cook for a few minutes until they start to soften.

Step 4: Add the Broth

Pour in the vegetable or chicken broth, covering the vegetables completely. Bring the mixture to a gentle boil, then reduce the heat and let it simmer until the potatoes and leeks are tender. This is where the magic happens, as the flavors meld together, creating a rich and comforting base for the soup.

Step 5: Blend to Creamy Perfection

Once the vegetables are tender, it’s time to transform the mixture into a creamy soup. Using an immersion blender or transferring the mixture to a regular blender in batches, carefully blend the soup until it reaches a smooth and velvety consistency.

Step 6: Finish with Cream

Return the blended soup to the pot if necessary, then pour in the heavy cream, stirring gently to incorporate it. This step adds a luxurious creaminess to the soup, elevating it to a whole new level of comfort and flavor.

Step 7: Season and Serve

Season the soup with salt and pepper to taste, adjusting the seasoning until it’s just right for you. Ladle the soup into bowls, garnishing with fresh chives if desired, and serve it hot with your favorite bread or crackers.

Conclusion

There you have it - a simple yet sensational Potato Leek Soup that’s perfect for any occasion. Whether you’re seeking a quick weeknight meal or a comforting dish to warm up a chilly day, this recipe has got you covered. So, roll up your sleeves, gather your ingredients, and let the aroma of this Soup-er Simple creation fill your kitchen. Enjoy every spoonful of this delightful soup, and share the warmth with your loved ones.

We hope you enjoy making and savoring this recipe as much as we do. Happy cooking!
