Vegan Panna Cotta Recipe with Fresh Berries and a Coconut Cream


If you’re a fan of creamy, indulgent desserts but want to stick to a vegan diet, this Vegan Panna Cotta with Fresh Berries and Coconut Cream is the perfect treat for you. This dairy-free and gelatin-free dessert is not only delicious but also easy to make, making it an ideal option for any occasion. Let’s dive into the recipe and create a delightful vegan dessert that will impress even the non-vegans in your life.

Ingredients:

For the Panna Cotta:

  • 1 can (13.5 oz) full-fat coconut milk
  • 1 1/2 cups unsweetened almond milk
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 tablespoon agar agar powder
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

For the Berry Compote:

  • 2 cups mixed fresh berries (strawberries, blueberries, raspberries, blackberries)
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract

For the Coconut Cream:

  • 1 can (13.5 oz) full-fat coconut milk, refrigerated overnight
  • 1 tablespoon powdered sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract

Instructions:

Making the Panna Cotta:

  1. In a saucepan, combine the coconut milk, almond milk, and sugar. Sprinkle the agar agar powder over the mixture and let it sit for 5 minutes.
  2. Place the saucepan over medium heat and bring the mixture to a simmer, stirring occasionally. Simmer for 5-7 minutes until the agar agar powder has completely dissolved.
  3. Remove the saucepan from the heat and stir in the vanilla extract.
  4. Pour the mixture into serving glasses or ramekins. Let it cool to room temperature, then cover and refrigerate for at least 4 hours or until set.

Making the Berry Compote:

  1. In a saucepan, combine the mixed berries, sugar, lemon juice, and vanilla extract.
  2. Cook over medium heat, stirring occasionally, until the berries release their juices and the mixture thickens slightly, about 10-12 minutes.
  3. Remove from heat and let the compote cool to room temperature.

Making the Coconut Cream:

  1. Open the refrigerated can of coconut milk and scoop out the solid coconut cream that has risen to the top, leaving the liquid behind for another use (such as smoothies).
  2. In a mixing bowl, whip the coconut cream, powdered sugar, and vanilla extract until smooth and creamy.

Assembling the Dessert:

  1. Once the panna cotta is set, spoon the berry compote over the top of each serving.
  2. Finish by dolloping a generous amount of coconut cream on top of the berries.

Serving:

Serve the Vegan Panna Cotta with Fresh Berries and Coconut Cream chilled, garnished with a fresh berry or mint leaves for an extra touch of elegance.

Conclusion:

This Vegan Panna Cotta with Fresh Berries and Coconut Cream is a delightful dessert that showcases the beauty of plant-based ingredients. The creamy texture of the panna cotta, the burst of flavor from the berry compote, and the richness of the coconut cream come together to create a dessert that’s both visually stunning and incredibly delicious. Whether you’re hosting a dinner party or simply craving a sweet treat, this vegan-friendly dessert is sure to impress. Enjoy the delightful flavors and textures of this dairy-free indulgence!
