Healthy and Zesty Quinoa Salad with Avocado


If you’re looking for a refreshing and nutritious dish to add to your recipe collection, look no further than this Healthy and Zesty Quinoa Salad with Avocado. Packed with protein, fiber, and healthy fats, this salad is not only delicious but also incredibly satisfying. Whether you’re a dedicated health enthusiast or just someone looking to incorporate more nutritious meals into your diet, this recipe is sure to become a staple in your kitchen.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup quinoa
  • 2 cups water or vegetable broth
  • 1 ripe avocado, diced
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1/2 cup cucumber, diced
  • 1/4 cup red onion, finely chopped
  • 1/4 cup fresh cilantro, chopped
  • 1/4 cup fresh parsley, chopped

For the dressing:

  • 3 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons fresh lemon juice
  • 1 teaspoon honey or agave nectar
  • 1 teaspoon Dijon mustard
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Cook the quinoa: Rinse the quinoa under cold water and drain. In a medium saucepan, bring the water or vegetable broth to a boil. Add the quinoa, reduce the heat to low, cover, and simmer for 15-20 minutes, or until the liquid is absorbed. Remove from the heat and let it cool.

  2. Prepare the dressing: In a small bowl, whisk together the olive oil, lemon juice, honey or agave nectar, Dijon mustard, salt, and pepper until well combined.

  3. Assemble the salad: In a large mixing bowl, combine the cooked quinoa, diced avocado, cherry tomatoes, cucumber, red onion, cilantro, and parsley. Pour the dressing over the salad and toss gently to coat all the ingredients.

  4. Chill and serve: Cover the bowl with plastic wrap and refrigerate for at least 30 minutes to allow the flavors to meld together. Before serving, give the salad a gentle toss and adjust the seasoning if necessary.

Health Benefits

This Healthy and Zesty Quinoa Salad with Avocado is not only a flavor-packed dish but also a powerhouse of nutrients. Quinoa is a complete protein, containing all nine essential amino acids, making it an excellent choice for vegetarians and vegans. The addition of avocado provides healthy fats, while the fresh vegetables and herbs contribute an array of vitamins, minerals, and antioxidants. The dressing, made with olive oil and lemon juice, adds a burst of flavor and heart-healthy monounsaturated fats.

Serving Suggestions

This salad makes for a satisfying and standalone meal, perfect for a light lunch or dinner. It also pairs beautifully with grilled chicken, fish, or tofu for a more substantial entrée. Serve it alongside a bowl of soup or as a refreshing side dish at your next barbecue or potluck. The vibrant colors and zesty flavors of this salad are sure to impress your guests and leave them asking for the recipe.

Conclusion

Incorporating more nutritious and wholesome meals into your diet doesn’t have to be challenging or bland. This Healthy and Zesty Quinoa Salad with Avocado is a testament to the fact that healthy eating can be delicious, satisfying, and enjoyable. By choosing nutrient-dense ingredients and preparing them with care, you can elevate your culinary experience while nourishing your body from the inside out. So, roll up your sleeves, gather your ingredients, and get ready to savor the goodness of this vibrant and zesty quinoa salad with avocado. Your taste buds and your body will thank you!
