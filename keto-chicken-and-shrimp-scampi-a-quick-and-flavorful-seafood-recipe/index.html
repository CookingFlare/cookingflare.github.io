Keto Chicken and Shrimp Scampi: A Quick and Flavorful Seafood Recipe


If you’re following a keto diet and looking for a quick and flavorful seafood recipe, then look no further than Keto Chicken and Shrimp Scampi. This delicious dish combines tender chicken, succulent shrimp, and a rich garlic butter sauce to create a meal that’s both satisfying and low in carbs. Plus, it’s easy to make and perfect for those busy weeknights when you want something delicious without spending hours in the kitchen.

Ingredients

  • 2 chicken breasts, boneless and skinless
  • 1 pound of shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 4 tablespoons of butter
  • 4 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1/4 cup of chicken broth
  • 1/4 cup of white wine (optional)
  • 1/4 cup of fresh parsley, chopped
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Start by preparing the chicken. Season the chicken breasts with salt and pepper on both sides.
  2. In a large skillet, melt 2 tablespoons of butter over medium-high heat. Add the chicken breasts and cook for about 6-8 minutes per side, or until they are cooked through and no longer pink in the center. Remove the chicken from the skillet and set it aside.
  3. In the same skillet, melt the remaining 2 tablespoons of butter. Add the minced garlic and sauté for about 1-2 minutes, or until it becomes fragrant.
  4. Add the shrimp to the skillet and cook for about 2-3 minutes per side, or until they are pink and opaque. Be careful not to overcook the shrimp, as they can become tough and rubbery.
  5. Once the shrimp is cooked, remove it from the skillet and set it aside with the chicken.
  6. Deglaze the skillet by adding the chicken broth and white wine (if using). Scrape any browned bits from the bottom of the skillet and allow the liquid to simmer for a few minutes, until it has reduced slightly.
  7. Return the chicken and shrimp to the skillet, along with any juices that have accumulated. Stir everything together to coat the chicken and shrimp in the garlic butter sauce.
  8. Cook for an additional 2-3 minutes, or until everything is heated through.
  9. Remove the skillet from the heat and sprinkle fresh parsley over the top of the dish.
  10. Serve the Keto Chicken and Shrimp Scampi hot and enjoy!

Why Keto Chicken and Shrimp Scampi?

This recipe is not only delicious and satisfying, but it’s also perfect for those following a keto diet. The chicken and shrimp provide a good source of protein while being low in carbs. The garlic butter sauce adds rich flavor without adding unnecessary carbs or sugars. Plus, this dish comes together in just under 30 minutes, making it a great option for busy weeknights when you want a homemade meal without spending hours in the kitchen.

Conclusion

Keto Chicken and Shrimp Scampi is a quick and flavorful seafood recipe that’s perfect for those following a keto diet. With tender chicken, succulent shrimp, and a rich garlic butter sauce, this dish is sure to satisfy your taste buds without derailing your low-carb lifestyle. Give it a try and see how delicious eating keto can be!
