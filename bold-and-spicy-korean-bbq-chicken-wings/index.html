Bold and Spicy: Korean BBQ Chicken Wings


If you’re a fan of bold, spicy flavors and love digging into some finger-licking good chicken wings, then you’re in for a treat! Today, we’re going to dive into the world of Korean BBQ Chicken Wings – a dish that perfectly balances sweet, savory, and spicy flavors. These wings are a popular choice for gatherings, game nights, or simply when you’re craving a delicious and satisfying meal. So, let’s roll up our sleeves and get ready to create a tantalizing dish that will have everyone coming back for more!

Ingredients

For the Chicken Wings:

  • 2 lbs chicken wings
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon black pepper

For the Korean BBQ Sauce:

  • 1/2 cup soy sauce
  • 1/4 cup brown sugar
  • 2 tablespoons honey
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon grated fresh ginger
  • 2 tablespoons gochujang (Korean chili paste)
  • 1 tablespoon sesame oil
  • 2 tablespoons rice vinegar
  • 1 teaspoon toasted sesame seeds
  • 2 green onions, finely chopped (for garnish)

For Garnish (optional):

  • Thinly sliced green onions
  • Toasted sesame seeds

Instructions

  1. Preheat the Oven: Preheat the oven to 400°F (200°C). Line a baking sheet with parchment paper.

  2. Prepare the Chicken Wings: In a large bowl, toss the chicken wings with vegetable oil, salt, and black pepper until they are evenly coated.

  3. Bake the Wings: Arrange the chicken wings in a single layer on the prepared baking sheet. Bake in the preheated oven for 45-50 minutes, turning them over halfway through, until they are golden brown and crispy.

  4. Make the Korean BBQ Sauce: While the wings are baking, it’s time to prepare the flavorful Korean BBQ sauce. In a saucepan, combine the soy sauce, brown sugar, honey, garlic, ginger, gochujang, sesame oil, and rice vinegar. Stir the mixture over medium heat until the sugar has dissolved and the sauce has slightly thickened. Remove from heat and stir in the toasted sesame seeds.

  5. Coat the Wings: Once the wings are done baking, transfer them to a large mixing bowl. Pour the Korean BBQ sauce over the wings and toss them until they are thoroughly coated.

  6. Serve and Garnish: Arrange the saucy Korean BBQ chicken wings on a platter and sprinkle with thinly sliced green onions and toasted sesame seeds for an extra pop of flavor and color.

Tips and Tricks

  • For extra crispy wings, you can broil the wings for an additional 2-3 minutes after coating them with the Korean BBQ sauce.
  • If you prefer a milder spice level, you can reduce the amount of gochujang in the sauce, or substitute it with a milder chili paste.

Enjoy the Flavor Fiesta!

Get ready to indulge in the bold and spicy flavors of these Korean BBQ Chicken Wings. The combination of savory soy sauce, sweet brown sugar, and spicy gochujang creates a symphony of flavors that will tantalize your taste buds. Whether you’re hosting a party or simply treating yourself to a flavorful meal, these wings are sure to be a hit. So, gather your ingredients, put on your apron, and let’s cook up a storm with these irresistible Korean BBQ Chicken Wings!
