Irresistible Nutella Stuffed Pancakes: A Chocolate Lover's Dream


If you’re a chocolate lover like me, then you’re in for a treat! Today, I’m sharing with you a recipe that will satisfy all your chocolate cravings and take your pancake game to the next level. Get ready to indulge in the heavenly combination of fluffy pancakes and gooey Nutella. These Nutella Stuffed Pancakes are truly irresistible and will make your taste buds dance with joy!

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 3 1/2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 tablespoon sugar
  • 1 1/4 cups milk
  • 1 egg
  • 3 tablespoons melted butter
  • Nutella (as much as your heart desires!)

Instructions

  1. In a large bowl, whisk together the flour, baking powder, salt, and sugar. This will ensure that all the dry ingredients are well combined and evenly distributed.

  2. In a separate bowl, whisk together the milk, egg, and melted butter. Make sure to mix until all the ingredients are fully incorporated.

  3. Pour the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients and gently stir until just combined. Be careful not to overmix, as this can result in tough pancakes.

  4. Heat a non-stick skillet or griddle over medium heat. You can also use a lightly greased pan if you don’t have a non-stick surface.

  5. Spoon about 1/4 cup of pancake batter onto the skillet and spread it into a circle. Make sure to leave some space in the center for the Nutella.

  6. Take a generous spoonful of Nutella and place it in the center of the pancake batter. Use the back of the spoon to spread the Nutella slightly, but be careful not to spread it all the way to the edges.

  7. Top the Nutella with another 1/4 cup of pancake batter, covering the Nutella completely. Gently spread the batter to ensure it covers the Nutella without disturbing it too much.

  8. Cook the pancake for about 2-3 minutes on each side, or until golden brown. Flip carefully to avoid any Nutella leakage.

  9. Repeat steps 5-8 with the remaining batter and Nutella, until you have made all the pancakes.

  10. Serve the Nutella Stuffed Pancakes warm, with a drizzle of maple syrup or a sprinkle of powdered sugar. You can also add some fresh berries or whipped cream for an extra touch of decadence.

Now, sit back, relax, and enjoy every mouthful of these indulgent Nutella Stuffed Pancakes. They are perfect for a lazy weekend brunch or a special treat for someone you love. So go ahead, take a bite, and let the rich chocolatey goodness melt in your mouth. Trust me, you won’t be able to resist!

Note: This recipe makes approximately 6-8 Nutella Stuffed Pancakes, depending on the size of your pancakes. Feel free to adjust the quantities according to your preferences and appetite.

I hope you found this recipe for Nutella Stuffed Pancakes delightful and easy to follow. These pancakes are sure to become a favorite among chocolate enthusiasts. Whether you enjoy them for breakfast, brunch, or dessert, they are bound to bring a smile to your face. So why wait? Gather your ingredients, fire up the stove, and let the Nutella magic begin! Happy cooking and happy eating!
