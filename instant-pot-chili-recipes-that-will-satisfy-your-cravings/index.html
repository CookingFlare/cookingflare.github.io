Instant Pot Chili Recipes That Will Satisfy Your Cravings


Chili is one of those comforting, hearty dishes that warm you from the inside out. Whether you’re seeking something to serve at a family gathering, an easy weeknight dinner, or a meal prep option, chili is always a great choice. The beauty of chili lies in its versatility — you can add or omit ingredients based on your preferences, and it always tastes incredible. But what if you could make a delicious chili in a fraction of the time it usually takes?

Enter the Instant Pot, a game-changer for busy home cooks. This modern kitchen appliance dramatically reduces cooking time while intensifying the flavors of your dish. Gone are the days of slow simmering on the stove for hours; the Instant Pot brings you the rich, hearty goodness of chili in under an hour. Whether you’re a meat lover, vegetarian, or prefer a spice level that’s just right, there’s an Instant Pot chili recipe for you.

In this blog post, we’ll explore several Instant Pot chili recipes, each with its own twist. From classic beef chili to unique variations like turkey chili and vegetarian options, you’re sure to find a recipe that satisfies your cravings.

1. Classic Beef Instant Pot Chili

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1 medium onion, diced
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 bell pepper, diced
  • 1 can (14 oz) diced tomatoes
  • 1 can (6 oz) tomato paste
  • 1 can (15 oz) kidney beans, drained and rinsed
  • 1 can (15 oz) black beans, drained and rinsed
  • 1 can (4 oz) green chilies, diced
  • 2 cups beef broth
  • 2 tbsp chili powder
  • 1 tsp cumin
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • 1 tsp oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 tbsp olive oil

Instructions:

  1. Sauté the beef: Set your Instant Pot to the “Sauté” mode. Add olive oil and let it heat. Once hot, add the ground beef and cook until browned. Break up the beef into crumbles as it cooks.
  2. Add aromatics and spices: Once the beef is browned, add the diced onion, bell pepper, and garlic. Sauté for 2-3 minutes until the vegetables soften. Stir in chili powder, cumin, paprika, and oregano, allowing the spices to bloom and release their fragrance.
  3. Deglaze: Pour in a little bit of beef broth and scrape up any browned bits from the bottom of the Instant Pot.
  4. Add the rest of the ingredients: Add the diced tomatoes, tomato paste, kidney beans, black beans, green chilies, and the rest of the beef broth. Stir everything to combine.
  5. Cook: Secure the lid on your Instant Pot and set it to “Manual” mode for 20 minutes on high pressure. Once done, let the pressure release naturally for about 10 minutes, then perform a quick release for the remaining pressure.
  6. Serve: Give the chili a final stir, taste, and adjust seasoning if needed. Serve hot with your favorite toppings, such as shredded cheese, sour cream, and cilantro.

This classic beef chili is rich, hearty, and perfect for any occasion. The Instant Pot makes it quick and easy without sacrificing the flavor you love.

2. Spicy Turkey Chili

If you’re looking to switch things up from beef, turkey is a leaner option that still offers plenty of flavor. Spicy turkey chili is a great choice for those who like a little heat.

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb ground turkey
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 bell peppers, diced
  • 1 can (14 oz) crushed tomatoes
  • 1 can (15 oz) pinto beans, drained and rinsed
  • 1 can (15 oz) black beans, drained and rinsed
  • 1 cup chicken broth
  • 1 tbsp chili powder
  • 1 tsp ground cumin
  • 1 tsp smoked paprika
  • 1/2 tsp cayenne pepper (or more if you like it spicier)
  • 1 jalapeño pepper, finely chopped (optional)
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Brown the turkey: Turn the Instant Pot to the “Sauté” mode. Add olive oil, then the ground turkey. Cook until browned and crumbled, about 5-7 minutes. Stir occasionally to break up the meat.
  2. Sauté the vegetables: Add the diced onion, bell peppers, garlic, and optional jalapeño. Sauté for another 3-4 minutes until the vegetables are softened.
  3. Add spices and liquids: Stir in chili powder, cumin, paprika, cayenne, salt, and pepper. Let the spices cook for 1-2 minutes before adding the crushed tomatoes, pinto beans, black beans, and chicken broth.
  4. Cook: Lock the lid in place and set the Instant Pot to “Manual” mode for 15 minutes on high pressure. After the cook time is done, allow the pressure to release naturally for about 5 minutes before performing a quick release.
  5. Serve: Taste the chili and adjust the seasonings if needed. Serve hot with toppings like chopped green onions, shredded cheese, or a dollop of sour cream.

The ground turkey makes this chili lighter while still giving you that rich and spicy kick that chili lovers crave. It’s a great option for those who want a lower-fat version of chili without compromising flavor.

3. Vegetarian Instant Pot Chili

For those who prefer a plant-based diet or are simply looking for a hearty vegetarian meal, this Instant Pot vegetarian chili will hit the spot. Packed with beans, vegetables, and spices, this chili is both filling and satisfying.

Ingredients:

  • 1 onion, diced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 bell pepper, diced
  • 2 zucchini, diced
  • 1 can (14 oz) diced tomatoes
  • 1 can (15 oz) kidney beans, drained and rinsed
  • 1 can (15 oz) black beans, drained and rinsed
  • 1 cup vegetable broth
  • 1 tbsp chili powder
  • 1 tsp ground cumin
  • 1 tsp smoked paprika
  • 1 tsp oregano
  • 1/2 tsp cinnamon (optional for a warm touch)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 tbsp olive oil

Instructions:

  1. Sauté the vegetables: Set the Instant Pot to “Sauté” mode. Add olive oil, then the diced onion, garlic, bell pepper, and zucchini. Sauté for 4-5 minutes until the vegetables soften.
  2. Add spices: Stir in the chili powder, cumin, paprika, oregano, and cinnamon. Let the spices toast in the oil for 1-2 minutes to enhance their flavors.
  3. Add the rest of the ingredients: Pour in the diced tomatoes, kidney beans, black beans, and vegetable broth. Stir well to combine.
  4. Cook: Seal the Instant Pot lid and cook on “Manual” mode for 12 minutes on high pressure. Once the time is up, let the pressure release naturally for 5-10 minutes before releasing the remaining pressure.
  5. Serve: Taste the chili and adjust the seasonings as necessary. Serve with toppings like avocado slices, fresh cilantro, or a sprinkle of cheese (if not fully plant-based).

This vegetarian chili is bursting with fresh flavors and makes for a great plant-based meal that everyone can enjoy. The mix of beans and veggies provides a nice balance of textures and is packed with nutrients.

4. Instant Pot White Chicken Chili

For those who prefer a lighter, creamy chili, white chicken chili is a fantastic choice. The tender chicken, combined with white beans and a savory broth, makes this dish a comforting and satisfying meal.

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb boneless, skinless chicken breasts or thighs
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 cans (15 oz each) white beans (such as cannellini or great northern), drained and rinsed
  • 1 can (4 oz) green chilies, diced
  • 1 cup chicken broth
  • 1 tsp cumin
  • 1 tsp chili powder
  • 1/2 tsp paprika
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream (or coconut milk for dairy-free)
  • 1 tbsp olive oil

Instructions:

  1. Sauté the onion and garlic: Set the Instant Pot to “Sauté” mode. Add olive oil and let it heat up. Add the diced onion and garlic and sauté for 2-3 minutes, until the onions become translucent.
  2. Add spices: Stir in cumin, chili powder, paprika, salt, and pepper, letting the spices toast for a minute.
  3. Add the chicken and other ingredients: Place the chicken breasts or thighs into the Instant Pot, followed by the white beans, green chilies, and chicken broth. Stir to combine.
  4. Cook: Lock the lid and set the Instant Pot to “Manual” mode for 15 minutes on high pressure. When the cooking cycle is complete, perform a quick release.
  5. Shred the chicken: Remove the chicken breasts or thighs, shred them with two forks, and return the chicken to the pot.
  6. Add cream and serve: Stir in the heavy cream and taste for seasoning adjustments. Serve with toppings like shredded cheese, avocado, and fresh cilantro.

This white chicken chili is creamy, comforting, and packed with flavor. It’s a lighter take on traditional chili but still offers that rich, satisfying taste.

5. Instant Pot Chili Mac

For an even heartier twist, try making chili mac in your Instant Pot. This dish combines the rich, flavorful chili with pasta for a one-pot meal that’s sure to satisfy any appetite.

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can (15 oz) kidney beans, drained and rinsed
  • 1 can (14 oz) diced tomatoes
  • 1 tbsp chili powder
  • 1 tsp ground cumin
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • 2 cups beef broth
  • 1 1/2 cups elbow macaroni
  • 1 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Sauté the beef: Use the “Sauté” function on your Instant Pot. Brown the ground beef, breaking it up as it cooks, about 5-7 minutes.
  2. Add the vegetables and spices: Add the diced onion and garlic to the pot and sauté for an additional 2-3 minutes. Stir in the chili powder, cumin, and paprika, and cook for another minute.
  3. Add the rest of the ingredients: Add the diced tomatoes, kidney beans, beef broth, and elbow macaroni. Stir well.
  4. Cook: Seal the Instant Pot and set it to “Manual” mode for 5 minutes on high pressure. Once done, release the pressure quickly.
  5. Add cheese and serve: Stir in the shredded cheddar cheese until melted and creamy. Serve hot, topped with more cheese, sour cream, or fresh cilantro.

Chili mac is the perfect comfort food, combining the best of chili and mac and cheese in one satisfying dish.

Conclusion

Instant Pot chili recipes are a must-try for anyone who loves bold flavors and comforting meals but doesn’t have the time to spend hours in the kitchen. With these recipes, you can create everything from classic beef chili to lighter vegetarian and chicken versions, all in the blink of an eye. The Instant Pot not only saves time but also locks in rich flavors, making every bowl of chili a satisfying and delicious experience. Whether you’re cooking for a family, hosting a gathering, or just craving a warm, hearty meal, these Instant Pot chili recipes are sure to hit the spot.
