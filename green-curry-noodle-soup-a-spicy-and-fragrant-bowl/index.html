Green Curry Noodle Soup: A Spicy and Fragrant Bowl


If you’re a fan of Thai cuisine, then you’re in for a treat! Today, I’m going to share with you a delicious and aromatic recipe for Green Curry Noodle Soup. This spicy and fragrant bowl is perfect for those who crave bold flavors and a touch of heat. So, let’s get cooking!

Ingredients:

  • 2 tablespoons green curry paste
  • 1 can (14 ounces) coconut milk
  • 4 cups vegetable broth
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon brown sugar
  • 1 tablespoon lime juice
  • 2 cups mixed vegetables (such as bell peppers, mushrooms, and broccoli)
  • 8 ounces rice noodles
  • 1 cup tofu, diced
  • Fresh cilantro, for garnish
  • Lime wedges, for serving

Instructions:

  1. In a large pot, heat the green curry paste over medium heat. Stir it around for a minute or two to release its flavors.

  2. Slowly pour in the coconut milk, stirring constantly to combine it with the curry paste. Let the mixture simmer for about 5 minutes until it thickens slightly.

  3. Add the vegetable broth, soy sauce, brown sugar, and lime juice to the pot. Stir well to combine all the ingredients. Bring the soup to a boil and then reduce the heat to low.

  4. Toss in the mixed vegetables and let them cook in the simmering soup for about 5 minutes until they are tender-crisp.

  5. While the vegetables are cooking, prepare the rice noodles according to the package instructions. Drain them and set them aside.

  6. Once the vegetables are cooked, add the diced tofu to the soup and let it heat through for a few minutes.

  7. To serve, divide the cooked rice noodles among bowls and ladle the green curry soup over them. Garnish with fresh cilantro and serve with lime wedges on the side.

That’s it! Your Green Curry Noodle Soup is ready to be enjoyed. The combination of the aromatic green curry paste, creamy coconut milk, and crunchy vegetables will tantalize your taste buds. The heat from the curry paste can be adjusted to your preference, making it a versatile dish for both spice lovers and those who prefer milder flavors.

Feel free to experiment with different vegetables or proteins to personalize this dish. You can add sliced chicken, shrimp, or even tofu to make it heartier. The beauty of this recipe lies in its flexibility and adaptability.

So, the next time you’re craving a bowl of something comforting, give this Green Curry Noodle Soup a try. Its spicy and fragrant flavors will transport you to the bustling streets of Thailand, right in the comfort of your own home. Enjoy!
