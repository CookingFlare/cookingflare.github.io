BBQ Beef Brisket Sliders: Mini Sandwiches for Any Occasion


If you’re looking for a crowd-pleasing dish that’s perfect for any occasion, BBQ beef brisket sliders are a fantastic choice. These mini sandwiches are packed with flavor and can be served as an appetizer, a main course, or even as a party snack. The tender, smoky beef brisket, paired with the tangy barbecue sauce and the soft slider buns, creates a mouthwatering combination that is sure to impress your guests. Whether you’re hosting a summer barbecue, a game day party, or a casual get-together, these BBQ beef brisket sliders are a guaranteed hit.

Ingredients

For the Beef Brisket:

  • 1 4-5 pound beef brisket
  • 2 tablespoons of your favorite BBQ rub
  • 1 tablespoon of smoked paprika
  • 1 tablespoon of garlic powder
  • 1 tablespoon of onion powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste

For the BBQ Sauce:

  • 1 cup ketchup
  • 1/2 cup brown sugar
  • 1/4 cup apple cider vinegar
  • 2 tablespoons Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 tablespoon Dijon mustard
  • 1 teaspoon smoked paprika
  • Salt and pepper to taste

For the Sliders:

  • 12 slider buns
  • Sliced dill pickles (optional)
  • Coleslaw (optional)

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Beef Brisket:

    • Preheat your smoker to 225°F (107°C).
    • Trim the excess fat from the brisket, leaving a thin layer to keep the meat moist during the long cooking process.
    • In a small bowl, combine the BBQ rub, smoked paprika, garlic powder, onion powder, salt, and pepper. Rub this mixture all over the brisket, ensuring that it’s evenly coated.
    • Place the brisket in the smoker and cook for 10-12 hours, or until the internal temperature reaches 200°F (93°C). The key to tender brisket is low and slow cooking, so be patient!

  2. Make the BBQ Sauce:

    • In a saucepan, combine the ketchup, brown sugar, apple cider vinegar, Worcestershire sauce, Dijon mustard, smoked paprika, salt, and pepper. Bring the mixture to a simmer over medium heat, then reduce the heat and let it simmer for 15-20 minutes, or until it thickens slightly.

  3. Assemble the Sliders:

    • Once the brisket is done, remove it from the smoker and let it rest for 30 minutes. Then, use two forks to shred the meat.
    • Toss the shredded brisket in the BBQ sauce until it’s well coated.
    • To assemble the sliders, place a generous amount of the BBQ beef brisket on the bottom half of each slider bun. Top with sliced pickles and coleslaw if desired, then cover with the top half of the bun.

  4. Serve and Enjoy:

    • Arrange the sliders on a platter and watch them disappear as your guests dig in! These mini sandwiches are best served warm, and they pair perfectly with a side of coleslaw, potato salad, or corn on the cob.

Conclusion

BBQ beef brisket sliders are a versatile and delicious dish that’s ideal for any gathering. The rich, smoky flavor of the beef brisket, combined with the sweetness and tang of the BBQ sauce, creates a winning combination that will leave everyone coming back for more. Whether you’re hosting a casual backyard barbecue or a festive celebration, these sliders are sure to be a hit with your family and friends. So fire up the smoker, get your hands on a quality beef brisket, and treat your guests to these irresistible BBQ beef brisket sliders!
