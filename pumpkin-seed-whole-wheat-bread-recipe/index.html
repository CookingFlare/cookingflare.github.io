Pumpkin Seed Whole Wheat Bread Recipe


Are you looking to add a wholesome and delicious twist to your baking routine? Look no further than this Pumpkin Seed Whole Wheat Bread recipe! Packed with fiber and nutrients, this bread is not only a treat for your taste buds but also a nutritious addition to your daily diet. Let’s dive into the steps of creating this hearty and flavorful bread right in your kitchen.

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups whole wheat flour
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 packet active dry yeast
  • 1 1/2 cups warm water
  • 1/4 cup honey
  • 1/4 cup pumpkin seeds
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon salt

Instructions:

  1. Activate the Yeast: In a small bowl, mix the warm water, honey, and yeast. Let it sit for about 5-10 minutes until the mixture becomes frothy.

  2. Prepare the Dough: In a large mixing bowl, combine the whole wheat flour, all-purpose flour, salt, and olive oil. Slowly pour in the yeast mixture and knead the dough until it becomes smooth and elastic.

  3. First Rise: Place the dough in a greased bowl, cover it with a damp cloth, and let it rise in a warm place for about an hour or until it doubles in size.

  4. Shape the Bread: Once the dough has risen, gently punch it down and knead in the pumpkin seeds. Shape the dough into a loaf and place it in a greased loaf pan.

  5. Second Rise: Cover the loaf pan with a damp cloth and allow the dough to rise for another 30-45 minutes.

  6. Preheat and Bake: Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C). Bake the bread for 30-35 minutes or until it sounds hollow when tapped at the bottom.

  7. Cool and Enjoy: Once baked, remove the bread from the oven and let it cool on a wire rack. Slice it up, serve with your favorite spreads, and enjoy the nutty goodness of Pumpkin Seed Whole Wheat Bread!

Benefits of Pumpkin Seed Whole Wheat Bread:

  • Rich in Fiber: Whole wheat flour and pumpkin seeds provide a good amount of dietary fiber, aiding in digestion and keeping you full longer.
  • Nutrient-Rich: Pumpkin seeds are a great source of essential nutrients like magnesium, zinc, and iron, adding a nutritional boost to your bread.
  • Heart-Healthy: The combination of whole wheat and pumpkin seeds supports heart health by lowering cholesterol levels and promoting overall cardiovascular well-being.
  • Satisfying and Delicious: The nutty flavor of pumpkin seeds complements the earthy taste of whole wheat, creating a satisfying and flavorful bread that you’ll love to indulge in.

Whether you enjoy a slice for breakfast, as a snack, or alongside your favorite meals, this Pumpkin Seed Whole Wheat Bread is sure to become a staple in your baking repertoire. Experiment with different seed variations or add a touch of cinnamon for an extra twist. Happy baking and savor every bite of this wholesome creation!

Remember, homemade bread is not only a delightful treat but also a labor of love. Share the joy of baking with family and friends by serving up a batch of this Pumpkin Seed Whole Wheat Bread. Happy baking!
