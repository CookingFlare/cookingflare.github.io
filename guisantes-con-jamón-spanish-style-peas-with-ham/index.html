Guisantes con Jamón: Spanish-Style Peas with Ham


If you’re looking for a flavorful and easy-to-make dish that captures the essence of Spanish cuisine, Guisantes con Jamón is the perfect choice. This traditional Spanish-style peas with ham recipe combines the sweetness of peas with the savory richness of ham, creating a delectable dish that is sure to delight your taste buds. Whether you’re a seasoned cook or just starting your culinary journey, this recipe is a great addition to your repertoire.

Ingredients

To prepare Guisantes con Jamón, you will need the following ingredients:

  • 2 cups of frozen peas
  • 150g of Serrano ham or prosciutto, diced
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 tomato, diced
  • 1/2 teaspoon of sweet paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon of ground cumin
  • 1/2 teaspoon of salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon of black pepper
  • 1/4 cup of dry white wine
  • 2 tablespoons of olive oil

Instructions

Step 1: Sauté the Aromatics

Start by heating the olive oil in a large pan over medium heat. Add the chopped onion and minced garlic, and sauté until the onion becomes translucent and the garlic is fragrant. This will form the flavorful base of the dish.

Step 2: Add the Ham and Tomato

Once the aromatics are tender, add the diced Serrano ham (or prosciutto) to the pan. Sauté the ham until it starts to brown slightly and releases its savory aroma. Then, add the diced tomato and cook until it begins to soften and release its juices.

Step 3: Season and Simmer

Now it’s time to add the sweet paprika, ground cumin, salt, and black pepper to the pan. Stir the spices into the mixture, allowing their aromas to infuse the ingredients. Then, pour in the dry white wine, which will add a subtle acidity and depth of flavor to the dish.

Step 4: Add the Peas

Once the wine has reduced slightly, add the frozen peas to the pan. Stir everything together, ensuring that the peas are well coated with the flavorful mixture. Reduce the heat to low, cover the pan, and let the peas simmer until they are tender and infused with the delicious flavors of the ham and spices.

Step 5: Serve and Enjoy

After the peas have cooked to perfection, remove the pan from the heat. Transfer the Guisantes con Jamón to a serving dish and garnish with a sprinkle of fresh parsley for a pop of color and freshness. This dish pairs beautifully with crusty bread or a side of fluffy white rice.

A Taste of Spain

Guisantes con Jamón is a delightful representation of the vibrant and diverse culinary landscape of Spain. From the smoky notes of the Serrano ham to the earthy sweetness of the peas, each bite is a celebration of traditional Spanish flavors. Whether you’re preparing this dish for a special occasion or simply craving a comforting meal, Guisantes con Jamón is a timeless classic that is sure to become a favorite in your kitchen.

Final Thoughts

In conclusion, Guisantes con Jamón is a dish that embodies the essence of Spanish gastronomy. With its simple yet robust flavors, this recipe invites you to savor the rich tapestry of Spanish cuisine. Whether you’re exploring new culinary horizons or reminiscing about past travels, this Spanish-style peas with ham recipe promises to transport you to the sun-drenched landscapes of Spain with every delicious bite. So, roll up your sleeves, gather your ingredients, and immerse yourself in the art of creating Guisantes con Jamón – a culinary journey to the heart of Spain.
