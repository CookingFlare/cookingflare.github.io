Vegan Chocolate Peanut Butter Pie: A Heavenly Combination


Who doesn’t love the combination of chocolate and peanut butter? It’s a match made in dessert heaven! And the best part? You can enjoy this indulgent treat guilt-free with a vegan twist. This Vegan Chocolate Peanut Butter Pie is not only delicious, but it’s also cruelty-free and packed with plant-based goodness. So let’s dive right into the recipe!

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups of chocolate cookie crumbs
  • 1/4 cup of melted vegan butter
  • 1 cup of creamy peanut butter
  • 1/2 cup of maple syrup
  • 1/4 cup of coconut oil, melted
  • 1 teaspoon of vanilla extract
  • 1/2 cup of coconut milk
  • 1/4 cup of cocoa powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon of salt
  • 1/2 cup of chopped peanuts (optional, for garnish)

Instructions

  1. In a medium-sized bowl, combine the chocolate cookie crumbs and melted vegan butter. Mix well until the crumbs are evenly coated. Press the mixture into the bottom of a 9-inch pie dish, forming an even crust. Place it in the refrigerator to chill while you prepare the filling.

  2. In a separate large bowl, combine the creamy peanut butter, maple syrup, melted coconut oil, and vanilla extract. Stir until the ingredients are well combined and smooth.

  3. In a small saucepan over low heat, whisk together the coconut milk, cocoa powder, and salt until the mixture is smooth and heated through. Remove from heat and let it cool slightly.

  4. Pour the cooled cocoa mixture into the peanut butter mixture. Stir until everything is well combined and the filling is smooth and creamy.

  5. Take the pie crust out of the refrigerator and pour the peanut butter filling into it. Use a spatula to spread the filling evenly.

  6. Place the pie in the refrigerator and let it chill for at least 4 hours, or until it’s firm.

  7. Once the pie has chilled and set, remove it from the refrigerator. If desired, sprinkle the chopped peanuts on top for added crunch and decoration.

  8. Slice and serve! This Vegan Chocolate Peanut Butter Pie is perfect for any occasion. Whether you’re hosting a dinner party or simply treating yourself, this heavenly combination of chocolate and peanut butter is sure to satisfy your sweet tooth.

Tips and Variations

  • If you want to add some extra flair to your pie, you can drizzle some melted vegan chocolate on top before serving.
  • For a nut-free version, you can substitute the peanut butter with almond butter or any other nut or seed butter of your choice.
  • If you prefer a sweeter pie, you can increase the amount of maple syrup or add a sweetener of your choice to taste.
  • Feel free to experiment with different crust options. You can use a traditional pie crust or even a gluten-free crust if you have dietary restrictions.

So there you have it, a delectable Vegan Chocolate Peanut Butter Pie that’s sure to impress both vegans and non-vegans alike. Indulge in the rich and creamy combination of chocolate and peanut butter with every bite. This dessert is not only a treat for your taste buds but also a celebration of cruelty-free and plant-based eating. Enjoy!
