Spanish Inspired Cheese and Olive Tapas Recipe


Tapas are a beloved Spanish tradition that has gained popularity worldwide. These small, flavorful dishes are perfect for sharing with friends and family, whether you’re hosting a casual get-together or a more formal gathering. One classic tapas dish that never fails to impress is the Spanish inspired cheese and olive tapas. This dish combines the rich, creamy flavors of cheese with the briny goodness of olives, creating a harmonious blend that will leave your taste buds craving for more.

Ingredients

  • 250g Manchego cheese, cut into cubes
  • 200g green olives, pitted
  • 200g black olives, pitted
  • 2 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon smoked paprika
  • Freshly ground black pepper, to taste

Instructions

  1. In a large bowl, combine the green and black olives. Set aside.

  2. In a small saucepan, heat the extra virgin olive oil over medium heat. Add the minced garlic and cook until fragrant, about 1-2 minutes.

  3. Pour the garlic-infused olive oil over the olives and toss to coat them evenly.

  4. Sprinkle the dried oregano, smoked paprika, and freshly ground black pepper over the olives. Mix well to ensure the seasonings are evenly distributed.

  5. Cover the bowl with plastic wrap and refrigerate for at least 2 hours to allow the flavors to meld together.

  6. When ready to serve, remove the olives from the refrigerator and let them come to room temperature.

  7. Arrange the cheese cubes on a serving platter or individual plates.

  8. Place a toothpick in each cheese cube, then spear an olive onto the toothpick, securing it to the cheese.

  9. Repeat until all the cheese cubes are topped with olives.

  10. Serve the Spanish inspired cheese and olive tapas as an appetizer or alongside other tapas dishes for a complete Spanish dining experience.

Tips and Variations

  • You can use any type of cheese you prefer for this tapas recipe. Manchego cheese is traditional, but feel free to experiment with other varieties like goat cheese or feta.

  • For an extra kick of flavor, add a pinch of red pepper flakes to the olive mixture.

  • Serve the tapas with crusty bread or crackers to complement the cheese and olives.

  • If you prefer a milder garlic flavor, you can sauté the minced garlic in the olive oil for a shorter time.

  • This tapas recipe can easily be scaled up or down depending on the number of guests you’re serving. Adjust the quantities of cheese and olives accordingly.

  • Get creative with your presentation! You can serve the tapas on a wooden board, a slate platter, or even individual cocktail picks for a more elegant look.

Conclusion

The Spanish inspired cheese and olive tapas recipe is a delightful combination of flavors that will transport you to the vibrant streets of Spain. With its simplicity and versatility, this dish is perfect for any occasion. Whether you’re hosting a dinner party or simply looking for a quick and delicious snack, give this tapas recipe a try and let your taste buds embark on a culinary adventure. ¡Buen provecho!
