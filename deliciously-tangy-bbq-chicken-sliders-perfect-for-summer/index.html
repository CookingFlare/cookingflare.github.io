Deliciously Tangy BBQ Chicken Sliders: Perfect for Summer


Summer is finally here, and with it comes the perfect excuse to fire up the grill and indulge in some delicious and refreshing BBQ. While traditional BBQ favorites like ribs and burgers are always a hit, sometimes it’s nice to mix things up and try something new. That’s where these tangy BBQ chicken sliders come in – a flavorful and satisfying twist on the classic BBQ chicken sandwich.

The Perfect Combination of Flavors

These sliders are all about the perfect balance of sweet, tangy, and savory flavors. The tangy BBQ sauce is the star of the show, with a depth of flavor that’s both sweet and sour. But it’s not just the sauce that makes these sliders special – it’s the combination of tender and juicy chicken, crispy and crunchy coleslaw, and soft and fluffy buns.

The Recipe

To make these tangy BBQ chicken sliders, you’ll need the following ingredients:

  • 1 pound boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1/4 cup tangy BBQ sauce (see below for recipe)
  • 4 hamburger buns
  • 1 cup coleslaw (see below for recipe)
  • 2 tablespoons mayonnaise
  • 1 teaspoon Dijon mustard
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Lettuce, tomato, and pickles for garnish (optional)

Tangy BBQ Sauce Recipe

  • 1 cup ketchup
  • 1/2 cup apple cider vinegar
  • 2 tablespoons brown sugar
  • 2 tablespoons Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 teaspoon smoked paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon onion powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Combine all the ingredients in a saucepan and bring to a simmer over medium heat. Reduce the heat to low and let cook for 5-7 minutes, stirring occasionally, until the sauce has thickened slightly.

Coleslaw Recipe

  • 2 cups shredded cabbage
  • 1 cup shredded carrots
  • 2 tablespoons mayonnaise
  • 1 tablespoon apple cider vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon sugar
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Combine all the ingredients in a bowl and stir until well combined. Let sit at room temperature for at least 30 minutes to allow the flavors to meld.

Assembly and Cooking

To assemble the sliders, preheat a grill or grill pan to medium-high heat. Grill the chicken breasts for 5-7 minutes per side, or until cooked through. Meanwhile, toast the buns on the grill or in a toaster.

Once the chicken is cooked, slice it into thin strips. Spread a layer of tangy BBQ sauce on the bottom bun, followed by a few slices of chicken, a spoonful of coleslaw, and a dollop of mayonnaise. Top with the top bun and serve immediately.

Tips and Variations

  • For an extra crispy coleslaw, chill it in the refrigerator for at least an hour before serving.
  • Swap out the mayonnaise for Greek yogurt or sour cream for a lighter and tangier topping.
  • Add some diced jalapeños or hot sauce to the coleslaw for an extra kick of heat.
  • Use leftover chicken for a delicious chicken salad or wrap.

These tangy BBQ chicken sliders are the perfect addition to any summer cookout or backyard BBQ. With their sweet and tangy BBQ sauce, crunchy and refreshing coleslaw, and soft and fluffy buns, they’re sure to be a hit with both kids and adults alike. So fire up the grill, gather your friends and family, and get ready to indulge in some deliciously tangy BBQ chicken sliders this summer!
