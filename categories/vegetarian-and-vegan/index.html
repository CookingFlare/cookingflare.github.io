Ingredients 1 cup quinoa, rinsed and cooked according to package instructions 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved 1 cucumber, diced 1 red bell pepper, diced 1/2 red onion, thinly sliced 1/2 cup Kalamata olives, pitted and halved 1/4 cup fresh parsley, chopped 1/4 cup fresh mint, chopped 1/4 cup extra virgin olive oil 2 tablespoons lemon juice 2 tablespoons red wine vinegar Salt and pepper to taste Instructions Cook the quinoa according to the package instructions. Once cooked, let it cool to room temperature. In a large bowl, combine the cooked quinoa, cherry tomatoes, cucumber, red bell pepper, red onion, Kalamata olives, parsley, and mint. In a separate small bowl, whisk together the extra virgin olive oil, lemon juice, red wine vinegar, salt, and pepper. Adjust the seasoning according to your taste preferences. Pour the dressing over the quinoa and vegetable mixture. Gently toss until everything is well coated. Let the salad sit for about 10 minutes to allow the flavors to meld together. Serve the Vegan Mediterranean Quinoa Salad chilled or at room temperature. Enjoy! Health Benefits This Vegan Mediterranean Quinoa Salad is not only delicious but also incredibly nutritious.
