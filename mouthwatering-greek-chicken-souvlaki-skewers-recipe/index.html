Mouthwatering Greek Chicken Souvlaki Skewers Recipe


Greek cuisine is renowned for its bold flavors, fresh ingredients, and vibrant presentation. Among the many delectable dishes, chicken souvlaki stands out as a favorite. This dish, consisting of marinated chicken grilled on skewers, is not only delicious but also easy to prepare. In this post, we’ll delve into the art of making mouthwatering Greek chicken souvlaki skewers that will transport your taste buds straight to the sun-soaked shores of Greece.

What is Souvlaki?

Souvlaki is a traditional Greek fast food consisting of small pieces of meat and sometimes vegetables grilled on a skewer. The word “souvlaki” derives from the Greek word “souvla,” meaning “skewer.” While it can be made with various meats, chicken souvlaki is particularly popular due to its light and juicy texture, infused with Mediterranean spices.

Ingredients You Will Need

To create the perfect Greek chicken souvlaki skewers, gather the following ingredients:

For the Marinade:

  • 2 pounds of chicken breast (cut into 1-inch cubes)
  • 4 tablespoons olive oil (extra virgin for the best flavor)
  • 3 cloves garlic (minced)
  • 2 tablespoons fresh lemon juice (about one lemon)
  • 2 teaspoons dried oregano (preferably Greek oregano)
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon paprika (smoked or sweet)
  • Salt and pepper (to taste)

For the Skewers:

  • Wooden or metal skewers (if using wooden skewers, soak them in water for at least 30 minutes to prevent burning)
  • Vegetables for grilling (optional, such as bell peppers, red onion, and zucchini)

For Serving:

  • Pita bread
  • Tzatziki sauce (yogurt sauce with cucumber and garlic)
  • Fresh salad (cucumber, tomato, and red onion)
  • Lemon wedges (for garnish)

The Marinade Magic

Step 1: Prepare the Marinade

The secret to mouthwatering chicken souvlaki lies in the marinade. This step is crucial as it tenderizes the chicken and infuses it with flavor. In a large bowl, combine the olive oil, minced garlic, lemon juice, dried oregano, ground cumin, paprika, salt, and pepper. Whisk them together until well blended.

Step 2: Marinate the Chicken

Add the cubed chicken to the marinade, ensuring that each piece is well coated. Cover the bowl with plastic wrap or transfer the chicken and marinade to a resealable plastic bag. Refrigerate for at least 1 hour, but for best results, let it marinate overnight. This extended marination time allows the flavors to penetrate deeply into the chicken.

Skewering the Chicken

Step 3: Prepare the Skewers

While the chicken marinates, prepare your skewers. If you’re using wooden skewers, remember to soak them in water for about 30 minutes to prevent them from burning on the grill. If you’re using metal skewers, you can skip this step.

Step 4: Assemble the Skewers

Once the chicken has marinated, it’s time to assemble the skewers. Thread the marinated chicken cubes onto the skewers, leaving a little space between each piece to ensure even cooking. If desired, alternate the chicken with pieces of bell peppers, red onions, or zucchini for added color and flavor.

Grilling the Souvlaki

Step 5: Preheat the Grill

Preheat your grill to medium-high heat. If using a charcoal grill, ensure the coals are hot and glowing. A well-heated grill is essential for achieving that perfect char on the chicken.

Step 6: Grill the Skewers

Place the assembled skewers on the grill. Cook for about 10-15 minutes, turning occasionally to ensure even cooking. The chicken is done when it reaches an internal temperature of 165°F (75°C) and has a nice golden brown exterior. If using vegetables, grill them until they are tender and slightly charred.

Serving Suggestions

Step 7: Prepare the Plate

Once the chicken souvlaki is grilled to perfection, remove the skewers from the grill and let them rest for a few minutes. This resting period allows the juices to redistribute, ensuring moist and flavorful chicken.

Step 8: Create a Delicious Spread

Serve your chicken souvlaki with warm pita bread, tzatziki sauce, and a fresh salad. You can also drizzle a little extra olive oil and lemon juice over the salad for added flavor. Garnish with lemon wedges for a zesty touch.

Tips for the Perfect Souvlaki

  1. Quality Chicken: Choose high-quality, fresh chicken breast for the best results. Organic or free-range chicken can enhance the flavor and texture.
  2. Marination Time: Don’t rush the marination process. The longer the chicken marinates, the more flavorful it will be.
  3. Skewering Technique: Ensure the chicken pieces are of uniform size for even cooking. If using vegetables, cut them similarly to the chicken.
  4. Don’t Overcrowd the Grill: Give the skewers enough space on the grill to ensure proper heat circulation.
  5. Experiment with Flavors: Feel free to experiment with different herbs and spices in the marinade, such as thyme or coriander, to give your souvlaki a unique twist.

Conclusion

Mouthwatering Greek chicken souvlaki skewers are a delightful addition to any meal, whether it’s a casual family dinner or a festive gathering with friends. The combination of marinated chicken, fresh ingredients, and vibrant flavors makes this dish a favorite among food lovers.

Now that you have this recipe at your fingertips, it’s time to fire up the grill and impress your loved ones with your culinary skills. Enjoy the process and savor every bite of this delicious Greek classic. Happy cooking!
