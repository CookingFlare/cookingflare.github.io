Thai Pineapple Fried Rice with Chicken: A Tropical Twist


If you’re looking for a dish that’s bursting with flavor and brings a taste of the tropics to your dinner table, then Thai Pineapple Fried Rice with Chicken is the perfect choice. This vibrant and aromatic dish combines the sweetness of pineapple, the savoriness of chicken, and the fragrance of jasmine rice, all brought together with a blend of Thai spices and seasonings. It’s a delightful fusion of sweet and savory, with a hint of tanginess, making it a true crowd-pleaser.

Ingredients

To create this delectable Thai Pineapple Fried Rice with Chicken, you’ll need the following ingredients:

  • 2 cups of cooked jasmine rice, preferably chilled
  • 1 cup of diced chicken breast
  • 1 cup of fresh pineapple chunks
  • 1/2 cup of diced bell peppers (red, yellow, or green for added color)
  • 1/2 cup of diced carrots
  • 1/2 cup of green peas
  • 3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 2 eggs, lightly beaten
  • 3 tablespoons of cooking oil
  • 2 tablespoons of fish sauce
  • 2 tablespoons of soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon of curry powder
  • 1 teaspoon of sugar
  • A handful of chopped cilantro for garnish
  • Lime wedges for serving

Instructions

Step 1: Preparing the Ingredients

  1. Start by cooking the jasmine rice according to the package instructions. Once cooked, spread the rice out on a baking sheet and let it cool in the refrigerator for at least 2 hours. Chilled rice works best for this recipe as it prevents the grains from sticking together.
  2. While the rice is chilling, prepare the other ingredients. Cut the chicken breast into small, bite-sized pieces, and chop the pineapple, bell peppers, and carrots into uniform chunks. Mince the garlic and set it aside.

Step 2: Cooking the Chicken

  1. Heat 1 tablespoon of cooking oil in a large wok or skillet over medium-high heat. Add the diced chicken breast and cook until it’s no longer pink and has a golden brown color. Remove the chicken from the wok and set it aside.

Step 3: Stir-Frying the Vegetables

  1. In the same wok, heat the remaining 2 tablespoons of cooking oil. Add the minced garlic and stir-fry for about 30 seconds until fragrant.
  2. Add the diced bell peppers, carrots, and green peas to the wok, and stir-fry for 3-4 minutes until the vegetables are slightly tender but still crisp.

Step 4: Adding the Rice and Pineapple

  1. Push the stir-fried vegetables to one side of the wok, and pour the beaten eggs onto the empty side. Let the eggs cook for a few seconds, then scramble them until they’re fully cooked.
  2. Add the chilled jasmine rice to the wok, breaking up any clumps as you stir it with the vegetables and eggs.
  3. Pour the fish sauce, soy sauce, and curry powder over the rice, and stir well to ensure the seasonings are evenly distributed.
  4. Gently fold in the cooked chicken and fresh pineapple chunks, and continue to stir-fry for another 3-4 minutes until everything is heated through.

Step 5: Final Touches and Serving

  1. Sprinkle the sugar over the rice and give it a final stir to incorporate the sweetness.
  2. Turn off the heat, and taste the rice. Adjust the seasonings if needed.
  3. Transfer the Thai Pineapple Fried Rice with Chicken to a serving platter, and garnish with chopped cilantro.
  4. Serve the dish with lime wedges on the side for an extra burst of citrus flavor.

Enjoy a Taste of Thailand

With its vibrant colors and harmonious blend of flavors, Thai Pineapple Fried Rice with Chicken is a dish that brings the essence of Thai cuisine to your home. Whether you’re cooking for a special occasion or simply craving a taste of the tropics, this recipe is sure to delight your taste buds and transport you to the sunny shores of Thailand. So, gather your ingredients, fire up the stove, and embark on a culinary journey to the heart of Southeast Asia with this delightful and aromatic dish.
