Vegetable Beef Barley Soup: Hearty and Wholesome


When it comes to comfort food, few things can beat a steaming bowl of Vegetable Beef Barley Soup. This hearty and wholesome dish is a classic for a reason – it’s packed with tender beef, nutritious vegetables, and satisfying barley, all simmered together in a rich, flavorful broth. Whether you’re looking for a cozy meal on a chilly evening or a nourishing option to warm you up during the winter months, this soup is a go-to choice that never disappoints. In this blog post, we’ll explore how to make this delicious soup from scratch and share some tips for getting the most out of it.

Ingredients

To make a pot of Vegetable Beef Barley Soup, you’ll need the following ingredients:

  • 1 pound of beef stew meat, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 1 cup of pearl barley
  • 4 carrots, peeled and sliced
  • 3 stalks of celery, chopped
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 6 cups of beef broth
  • 2 cups of water
  • 1 can (14.5 ounces) of diced tomatoes
  • 2 bay leaves
  • 1 teaspoon of dried thyme
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley for garnish

Instructions

  1. Brown the Beef: In a large pot or Dutch oven, heat a tablespoon of oil over medium-high heat. Add the beef stew meat and cook until browned on all sides, about 5-6 minutes. Remove the beef from the pot and set it aside.

  2. Sauté the Vegetables: In the same pot, add the carrots, celery, onion, and garlic. Sauté the vegetables for 5-6 minutes, or until they begin to soften.

  3. Combine the Ingredients: Return the browned beef to the pot. Add the pearl barley, beef broth, water, diced tomatoes (with their juices), bay leaves, and dried thyme. Season with salt and pepper to taste.

  4. Simmer the Soup: Bring the soup to a boil, then reduce the heat to low and let it simmer, covered, for 1-1.5 hours, or until the beef is tender and the barley is cooked through.

  5. Adjust the Seasoning: Taste the soup and adjust the seasoning as needed. Remove the bay leaves and discard them.

  6. Serve and Enjoy: Ladle the Vegetable Beef Barley Soup into bowls, garnish with fresh parsley, and serve hot. Pair it with crusty bread or a side salad for a complete meal.

Tips and Variations

  • Make it in Advance: Vegetable Beef Barley Soup tastes even better the next day, so consider making a big batch and storing it in the refrigerator for a quick and satisfying meal throughout the week.

  • Add More Vegetables: Feel free to customize the soup by adding your favorite vegetables, such as peas, green beans, or spinach.

  • Use a Slow Cooker: If you prefer a hands-off approach, you can also make this soup in a slow cooker. Simply brown the beef and sauté the vegetables as instructed, then transfer everything to the slow cooker and cook on low for 6-8 hours.

  • Make it Vegetarian: For a vegetarian version, simply omit the beef and use vegetable broth instead of beef broth. You can also add extra beans or legumes for added protein and texture.

Final Thoughts

Vegetable Beef Barley Soup is a true crowd-pleaser that’s perfect for any occasion. Its rich flavors, nourishing ingredients, and comforting warmth make it a staple in many households. Whether you’re cooking for your family, meal prepping for the week, or simply craving a bowl of something soothing, this soup is sure to hit the spot. So, grab your ingredients, follow the simple steps, and treat yourself to a bowl of this hearty and wholesome soup – you won’t be disappointed!
