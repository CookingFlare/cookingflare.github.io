Homemade Chicken Parmesan: A Comforting Italian Dish


There’s something inherently comforting about Italian cuisine. The rich flavors, the hearty textures, and the aromatic herbs all come together to create a truly satisfying dining experience. One dish that embodies all these qualities is the classic Chicken Parmesan. This beloved Italian-American dish is a staple in many households, and for good reason. With its crispy breaded chicken cutlets, gooey melted cheese, and tangy marinara sauce, Chicken Parmesan has the power to elevate any meal into a special occasion. In this blog post, I’ll guide you through the process of making Homemade Chicken Parmesan from scratch, so you can enjoy this comforting dish in the comfort of your own home.

Ingredients

For the Chicken Cutlets:

  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 3 large eggs
  • 2 cups Italian seasoned breadcrumbs
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Olive oil for frying

For the Assembly:

  • 2 cups marinara sauce
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • Fresh basil leaves for garnish

Instructions

Step 1: Prepare the Chicken

  1. Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C).
  2. Place the chicken breasts between two sheets of plastic wrap and pound them to an even thickness, about 1/2 inch.
  3. Season the chicken with salt and pepper.
  4. Set up a breading station with three shallow dishes: one with flour, one with beaten eggs, and one with a mixture of breadcrumbs and grated Parmesan cheese.
  5. Dredge each chicken breast in the flour, then dip into the egg, and finally coat with the breadcrumb mixture, pressing gently to adhere.

Step 2: Fry the Chicken

  1. In a large skillet, heat about 1/4 inch of olive oil over medium-high heat.
  2. Working in batches, add the breaded chicken cutlets to the skillet and cook until golden brown and crispy, about 3-4 minutes per side.
  3. Transfer the cooked cutlets to a paper towel-lined plate to drain any excess oil.

Step 3: Assemble the Dish

  1. Spread 1 cup of marinara sauce in the bottom of a 9x13 inch baking dish.
  2. Arrange the fried chicken cutlets over the sauce.
  3. Spoon the remaining marinara sauce over the chicken.
  4. Sprinkle the shredded mozzarella and grated Parmesan cheese over the top.
  5. Bake in the preheated oven for 25-30 minutes, or until the cheese is bubbly and golden brown.

Step 4: Serve and Enjoy

  1. Let the Chicken Parmesan rest for a few minutes before serving.
  2. Garnish with fresh basil leaves, and serve with your choice of pasta or a simple green salad.
  3. Enjoy the comforting flavors of Homemade Chicken Parmesan with your loved ones.

Conclusion

Homemade Chicken Parmesan is a labor of love that yields delicious results. The crispy chicken, gooey cheese, and flavorful marinara sauce come together to create a dish that is both comforting and satisfying. Whether you’re cooking for a special occasion or simply craving a taste of Italy, Homemade Chicken Parmesan is sure to impress. So, roll up your sleeves, gather your ingredients, and embark on a culinary journey that celebrates the rich flavors of Italian cuisine. Buon appetito!
