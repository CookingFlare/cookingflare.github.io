Pasta with Creamy Lemon Basil Sauce: A Fresh and Zesty Pasta Option


When it comes to pasta, there are endless possibilities for creating delicious and satisfying meals. One of the best things about pasta dishes is their versatility and ability to be paired with a wide range of flavors. Today, we’re going to explore a recipe for Pasta with Creamy Lemon Basil Sauce, a dish that brings together the bright and zesty flavors of lemon and basil with the comforting creaminess of a pasta sauce. This recipe is perfect for those who are looking for a fresh and vibrant pasta option that’s both easy to prepare and incredibly flavorful.

Ingredients

To create this delightful Pasta with Creamy Lemon Basil Sauce, you’ll need the following ingredients:

  • 12 ounces of your favorite pasta (spaghetti, fettuccine, or penne work well)
  • 1 tablespoon of olive oil
  • 3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 cup of heavy cream
  • Zest of 1 lemon
  • Juice of 1 lemon
  • 1/2 cup of freshly grated Parmesan cheese
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1/4 cup of fresh basil, chopped
  • Additional Parmesan for serving (optional)

Instructions

  1. Cook the Pasta: Begin by cooking the pasta according to the package instructions. Remember to salt the water generously to ensure the pasta is well-seasoned. Once the pasta is al dente, drain it and set it aside.

  2. Prepare the Creamy Lemon Basil Sauce: In a large skillet, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the minced garlic and sauté for about a minute, or until fragrant. Be careful not to let the garlic brown.

  3. Add the Cream and Lemon Zest: Pour the heavy cream into the skillet with the garlic and bring it to a gentle simmer. Add the lemon zest and stir to incorporate the bright citrus flavor into the sauce.

  4. Create the Creamy Base: Once the cream has simmered for a few minutes, add the lemon juice and grated Parmesan cheese to the skillet. Stir the sauce until the cheese is melted and the sauce has thickened slightly. Season with salt and pepper to taste.

  5. Add the Basil: Remove the skillet from the heat and stir in the chopped basil. The fragrance of the fresh basil will infuse the sauce with a delightful aroma that perfectly complements the lemony notes.

  6. Combine the Pasta and Sauce: Add the cooked pasta to the skillet with the creamy lemon basil sauce. Gently toss the pasta until it is well coated with the sauce.

  7. Serve and Enjoy: Once the pasta is evenly coated with the creamy lemon basil sauce, it’s ready to be served. You can garnish each serving with an extra sprinkle of Parmesan cheese for added richness, if desired.

Conclusion

Pasta with Creamy Lemon Basil Sauce is a delightful way to elevate a classic pasta dish with the bright and zesty flavors of lemon and basil. This recipe is perfect for a quick and satisfying weeknight dinner, or for impressing guests with its vibrant and refreshing taste. The creamy sauce perfectly complements the pasta, creating a harmonious blend of comforting and invigorating flavors in every bite. Whether you’re a fan of traditional Italian cuisine or simply enjoy exploring new flavor combinations, this Pasta with Creamy Lemon Basil Sauce is sure to become a favorite in your recipe collection. So, gather your ingredients and give this recipe a try – you won’t be disappointed!
