Roasted Vegetable Soup: A Hearty and Healthy Paleo Recipe for Fall


As the autumn leaves start to fall and the crisp air sets in, our bodies crave hearty and comforting meals that warm the soul. What better way to satisfy this craving than with a rich and flavorful roasted vegetable soup that’s also Paleo-friendly? In this recipe, we’ll combine the natural sweetness of roasted vegetables with the savory goodness of aromatic spices to create a soup that’s both nourishing and delicious.

Ingredients

  • 2 medium-sized sweet potatoes, peeled and cubed
  • 1 large red onion, chopped
  • 3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 2 medium-sized carrots, peeled and chopped
  • 2 stalks of celery, chopped
  • 2 tablespoons of olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon of ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon of smoked paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon of salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon of black pepper
  • 4 cups of vegetable broth
  • 1 can of diced tomatoes (14 oz)
  • Fresh parsley, chopped (optional)

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 425°F (220°C).
  2. In a large bowl, toss together the sweet potatoes, red onion, garlic, carrots, and celery with the olive oil, cumin, smoked paprika, salt, and black pepper until they’re evenly coated.
  3. Spread the vegetable mixture out in a single layer on a large baking sheet and roast in the preheated oven for 30-40 minutes, or until the vegetables are tender and lightly caramelized.
  4. Remove the baking sheet from the oven and let it cool slightly.
  5. In a large pot, combine the roasted vegetables, vegetable broth, and diced tomatoes. Bring the mixture to a boil, then reduce the heat to low and simmer for 20-25 minutes, or until the flavors have melded together and the soup has reached your desired consistency.
  6. Use an immersion blender to puree the soup until it’s smooth, or leave it chunky if you prefer.
  7. Taste and adjust the seasoning as needed.
  8. Serve the soup hot, garnished with chopped fresh parsley if desired.

Tips and Variations

  • To add an extra layer of depth to the soup, try roasting the vegetables for an additional 10-15 minutes, or until they’re nicely browned.
  • If you prefer a thicker soup, you can puree some or all of the soup in a blender and then return it to the pot.
  • Feel free to customize the recipe to your taste by adding or substituting different spices, herbs, or vegetables.
  • Consider serving the soup with a swirl of coconut cream or a sprinkle of chopped fresh herbs for added flavor and texture.

Nutrition Information

Per serving (serves 6-8):

  • Calories: 240
  • Protein: 4g
  • Fat: 10g
  • Saturated Fat: 1.5g
  • Carbohydrates: 35g
  • Fiber: 6g
  • Sugar: 10g
  • Sodium: 400mg

This roasted vegetable soup is not only delicious, but it’s also packed with nutrients and is suitable for a Paleo diet. The sweet potatoes and carrots provide a boost of vitamin A, while the cumin and smoked paprika add a dose of antioxidants. The vegetable broth and diced tomatoes add moisture and flavor, making this soup a hearty and satisfying meal for the fall season.

So go ahead, grab a spoon, and dig in to this comforting and nourishing bowl of roasted vegetable soup. Your taste buds and body will thank you!
