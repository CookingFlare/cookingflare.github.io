Quick and Easy Wraps Perfect for Lunch


Are you tired of the same old boring lunch options? Do you find yourself reaching for the same sandwich day after day? Well, it’s time to mix things up and try something new! Quick and easy wraps are the perfect solution for a delicious and satisfying lunch that will keep you energized throughout the day. Whether you’re a busy professional or a stay-at-home parent, these wraps are a game-changer when it comes to lunchtime!

Why choose wraps?

Wraps are not only incredibly tasty, but they are also versatile and convenient. You can customize your wraps with a variety of fillings, from savory to sweet, to suit your taste preferences. Plus, they are easy to pack, making them an ideal option for on-the-go lunches. Say goodbye to soggy sandwiches and hello to a burst of flavor in every bite!

Mediterranean-inspired Veggie Wrap

Ingredients:

  • 1 large tortilla wrap
  • 2 tablespoons hummus
  • 1/4 cup sliced cucumbers
  • 1/4 cup sliced tomatoes
  • 1/4 cup sliced red onions
  • 2 tablespoons crumbled feta cheese

Instructions:

  1. Lay the tortilla wrap flat on a clean surface.
  2. Spread the hummus evenly on the wrap, leaving a small border around the edges.
  3. Layer the sliced cucumbers, tomatoes, and red onions on top of the hummus.
  4. Sprinkle the crumbled feta cheese over the vegetables.
  5. Roll the wrap tightly, tucking in the sides as you go.
  6. Slice the wrap in half and enjoy!

Asian-inspired Chicken Teriyaki Wrap

Ingredients:

  • 1 large tortilla wrap
  • 1/2 cup cooked chicken, shredded
  • 2 tablespoons teriyaki sauce
  • 1/4 cup shredded carrots
  • 1/4 cup sliced bell peppers
  • 1/4 cup sliced avocado
  • 1 tablespoon chopped cilantro

Instructions:

  1. Lay the tortilla wrap flat on a clean surface.
  2. Drizzle the teriyaki sauce evenly over the wrap.
  3. Spread the shredded chicken, shredded carrots, sliced bell peppers, avocado, and chopped cilantro on top of the sauce.
  4. Roll the wrap tightly, tucking in the sides as you go.
  5. Slice the wrap in half and dig in!

Southwestern-inspired Black Bean Wrap

Ingredients:

  • 1 large tortilla wrap
  • 1/4 cup black beans, drained and rinsed
  • 2 tablespoons salsa
  • 1/4 cup shredded lettuce
  • 1/4 cup diced tomatoes
  • 2 tablespoons shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1 tablespoon chopped fresh cilantro

Instructions:

  1. Lay the tortilla wrap flat on a clean surface.
  2. Spread the salsa evenly on the wrap.
  3. Layer the black beans, shredded lettuce, diced tomatoes, shredded cheddar cheese, and chopped fresh cilantro on top of the salsa.
  4. Roll the wrap tightly, tucking in the sides as you go.
  5. Slice the wrap in half and savor every bite!

Conclusion

There you have it, three quick and easy wraps that are perfect for lunch! Whether you’re in the mood for a Mediterranean-inspired veggie wrap, an Asian-inspired chicken teriyaki wrap, or a Southwestern-inspired black bean wrap, these recipes will surely satisfy your taste buds. So, ditch the dull sandwiches and give these wraps a try. You won’t be disappointed!
