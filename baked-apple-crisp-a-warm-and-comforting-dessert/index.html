Baked Apple Crisp: A Warm and Comforting Dessert


There’s something magical about the aroma of baked apples wafting through the house on a crisp autumn evening. The combination of tender apples, warm spices, and a crunchy oat topping makes baked apple crisp the perfect dessert for those cozy nights in. Today, I’m going to share with you my favorite recipe for this delicious and comforting treat.

Ingredients

  • 6 medium-sized apples (any variety you prefer)
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup rolled oats
  • 1 cup brown sugar
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, cold and cubed
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground nutmeg
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • Vanilla ice cream or whipped cream for serving (optional)

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C). Grease a 9x9-inch baking dish and set aside.

  2. Begin by peeling, coring, and slicing the apples. You can use a knife or an apple slicer for this step. Aim for slices that are about 1/4 inch thick. Place the apple slices evenly in the greased baking dish.

  3. In a mixing bowl, combine the flour, rolled oats, brown sugar, ground cinnamon, ground nutmeg, and salt. Mix well to ensure all the ingredients are evenly distributed.

  4. Add the cold, cubed butter to the dry mixture. Using a pastry cutter or your fingers, cut the butter into the dry ingredients until the mixture resembles coarse crumbs. This step is important as it creates that irresistible crunchy topping.

  5. Sprinkle the oat mixture evenly over the sliced apples, covering them completely. Press down gently to ensure the topping sticks together.

  6. Place the baking dish in the preheated oven and bake for 40-45 minutes, or until the apples are tender and the topping is golden brown and crisp.

  7. Once baked, remove the dish from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes before serving. This dessert is best enjoyed warm, so scoop a generous serving into bowls and top with a scoop of vanilla ice cream or a dollop of whipped cream if desired.

  8. Serve and savor the delightful combination of warm, tender apples and the satisfying crunch of the oat topping. The sweet aroma will fill your kitchen, creating a cozy and inviting atmosphere that’s perfect for sharing with loved ones.

Tips and Variations

  • Experiment with different apple varieties to find your favorite combination. Granny Smith apples add a tartness that balances well with the sweetness of the crisp, while Honeycrisp apples bring a natural sweetness to the dish.

  • For an extra burst of flavor, consider adding a handful of raisins or dried cranberries to the apple mixture before topping it with the oat mixture.

  • To make this dessert gluten-free, simply substitute the all-purpose flour with a gluten-free flour blend or almond flour.

  • If you have leftovers, store them in an airtight container in the refrigerator. Reheat in the oven or microwave before serving to bring back that freshly baked taste.

Baked apple crisp is a timeless dessert that never fails to bring comfort and joy. Whether you’re looking for a simple dessert to enjoy on a cozy night at home or want to impress your guests with a delicious homemade treat, this recipe is sure to become a favorite. So grab your apron, preheat your oven, and get ready to indulge in the warm and comforting flavors of baked apple crisp.
