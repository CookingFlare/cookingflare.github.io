Crunchy Low Carb Parmesan Crusted Chicken Tenders


If you’re on a low-carb diet or just looking for a delicious and crunchy chicken recipe, then you’re in for a treat. These Crunchy Low Carb Parmesan Crusted Chicken Tenders are not only easy to make but also incredibly flavorful and satisfying. Whether you’re cooking for yourself, your family, or a group of friends, this recipe is sure to be a hit. So, let’s dive in and discover how to make these mouthwatering chicken tenders.

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound of chicken tenders
  • 1 cup of grated parmesan cheese
  • 1/2 cup of almond flour
  • 1 teaspoon of garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon of paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon of salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon of black pepper
  • 2 eggs
  • Cooking spray

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C) and prepare a baking sheet by lining it with parchment paper and spraying it with cooking spray.

  2. In a shallow bowl, beat the eggs until well combined.

  3. In another shallow bowl, mix the almond flour, grated parmesan cheese, garlic powder, paprika, salt, and black pepper until thoroughly combined.

  4. Dip each chicken tender into the beaten eggs, allowing any excess to drip off, then coat it with the parmesan and almond flour mixture, pressing gently to adhere the coating to the chicken.

  5. Place the coated chicken tenders onto the prepared baking sheet, leaving some space between each piece.

  6. Lightly spray the top of the chicken tenders with cooking spray to help them brown and become crispy.

  7. Bake in the preheated oven for 15-20 minutes, or until the chicken is cooked through and the coating is golden and crispy.

  8. Once done, remove the chicken tenders from the oven and let them rest for a few minutes before serving.

Serving Suggestions:

These Crunchy Low Carb Parmesan Crusted Chicken Tenders are versatile and can be served in various ways. You can enjoy them as a main dish with your favorite dipping sauce, such as marinara, ranch, or honey mustard. They also make a delicious addition to salads, wraps, or sandwiches. For a complete low-carb meal, pair them with a side of steamed vegetables or a fresh green salad.

Nutritional Information:

Each serving of these chicken tenders provides a good source of protein and healthy fats, while keeping the carb count low. The parmesan and almond flour coating adds a satisfying crunch without the need for breadcrumbs or flour. This makes it an excellent choice for those following a low-carb or keto lifestyle.

Conclusion:

These Crunchy Low Carb Parmesan Crusted Chicken Tenders are a delightful twist on the classic chicken tenders, offering a low-carb, high-protein option that doesn’t compromise on flavor or texture. Whether you’re cooking for yourself or a crowd, this recipe is sure to become a favorite. So, next time you’re in the mood for something crispy and satisfying, give these chicken tenders a try and enjoy a guilt-free indulgence!
