Coconut Rum Raisin Ice Cream: A Boozy Twist


Who doesn’t love a delicious scoop of ice cream on a hot summer day? But why settle for ordinary flavors when you can indulge in something extraordinary? I present to you the Coconut Rum Raisin Ice Cream: A Boozy Twist. This delightful concoction combines the creamy goodness of coconut with the rich, sweet flavor of rum-soaked raisins. Get ready to tantalize your taste buds with this unique and indulgent treat.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups heavy cream
  • 1 cup whole milk
  • ¾ cup granulated sugar
  • 4 large egg yolks
  • 1 cup sweetened shredded coconut
  • ½ cup raisins
  • ½ cup dark rum
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Instructions

  1. In a medium saucepan, combine the heavy cream, whole milk, and granulated sugar. Heat the mixture over medium heat until it reaches a gentle simmer. Stir occasionally to dissolve the sugar completely.

  2. In a separate bowl, whisk the egg yolks until they become pale and slightly thickened. Slowly pour the hot cream mixture into the bowl while continuously whisking to temper the eggs. This step ensures that the eggs don’t scramble when added to the hot liquid.

  3. Pour the combined mixture back into the saucepan and cook over low heat, stirring constantly, until the custard thickens enough to coat the back of a spoon. Be careful not to let it boil.

  4. Remove the saucepan from the heat and stir in the sweetened shredded coconut. Let the mixture cool completely, then cover and refrigerate for at least 2 hours or overnight. This step allows the flavors to meld together and the custard to chill.

  5. Meanwhile, place the raisins in a small bowl and pour the dark rum over them. Let the raisins soak for about 1 hour, or until they are plump and have absorbed the rum.

  6. Once the custard has chilled, strain out the coconut pieces using a fine-mesh sieve or cheesecloth. Add the soaked raisins, along with any remaining rum, to the custard. Stir in the vanilla extract.

  7. Pour the mixture into an ice cream maker and churn according to the manufacturer’s instructions. This usually takes around 20-25 minutes, but make sure to follow the specific guidelines for your machine.

  8. Transfer the churned ice cream into a lidded container and freeze for at least 3 hours, or until firm. The longer it freezes, the better the flavors will meld together.

  9. Once the Coconut Rum Raisin Ice Cream is fully frozen, it’s time to serve and enjoy! Scoop it into bowls or cones, and if you’re feeling fancy, garnish with a sprinkle of toasted coconut flakes or a drizzle of caramel sauce.

  10. Indulge in the creamy, boozy goodness of this Coconut Rum Raisin Ice Cream, and let its tropical flavors transport you to a beachside paradise. It’s the perfect treat to savor on a warm summer evening or whenever you’re in need of a little indulgence.

So, what are you waiting for? Gather your ingredients, follow the simple steps, and treat yourself to a scoop (or two) of this delightful Coconut Rum Raisin Ice Cream. Your taste buds will thank you for the boozy twist and the burst of tropical flavors. Enjoy!
