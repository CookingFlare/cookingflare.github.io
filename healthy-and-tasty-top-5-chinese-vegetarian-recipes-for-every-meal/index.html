Healthy and Tasty: Top 5 Chinese Vegetarian Recipes for Every Meal


Chinese cuisine is renowned for its diverse flavors, textures, and unique ingredients that make every dish an adventure for your taste buds. Thanks to its vast variety of vegetables, beans, and grains, Chinese vegetarian cooking is not only satisfying but also incredibly healthy and delicious. In this blog post, we’ve prepared a list of the top 5 Chinese vegetarian recipes perfect for any meal of the day.

1. Spicy Mapo Tofu

Mapo tofu, a classic Sichuan dish, is typically made with silky soft tofu and ground pork, seasoned with a mouth-numbing Sichuan peppercorn sauce. Our vegetarian version retains all the flavor and texture while omitting the meat.

Ingredients:

  • 400g soft tofu, cut into cubes
  • 1 zucchini, finely chopped
  • 2 green onions, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1-inch piece ginger, minced
  • 1 tablespoon Sichuan peppercorns
  • 1 tablespoon fermented black beans, rinsed and mashed
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon rice vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon chili bean paste
  • 1 teaspoon cornstarch mixed with 2 tablespoons water

Instructions:

  1. Heat a large wok or frying pan over medium-high heat and sauté the zucchini until slightly softened. Set aside.
  2. In the same pan, add a splash of oil and sauté garlic, ginger, Sichuan peppercorns, and black beans until fragrant.
  3. Add the tofu, zucchini, and soy sauce. Cook for 2 minutes, then stir in the cornstarch mixture, and bring to a simmer.
  4. Finally, mix in the rice vinegar, chili bean paste, and green onions. Cook for another minute and serve immediately.

2. Mung Bean Noodles in Hot Soy Sauce

This refreshing dish, originating from Taiwan, is perfect for a hot summer day. The mung bean noodles and fresh vegetables are lightly tossed in a delightful hot soy sauce.

Ingredients:

  • 200g dried mung bean noodles
  • 1 cucumber, julienned
  • 2 bell peppers, julienned
  • 1 quarter avocado, sliced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 inches ginger, minced
  • 1 tablespoon fermented tofu (optional)
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon honey
  • 1 tablespoon rice vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon chili oil

Instructions:

  1. Cook the mung bean noodles in boiling water for 2 to 3 minutes, rinse with cold water, and drain. Set aside.
  2. In a small bowl, combine soy sauce, honey, rice vinegar, and fermented tofu. Set aside.
  3. In a large bowl, mix the noodles, cucumber, bell peppers, ginger, garlic, and avocado. Drizzle the hot soy sauce mixture over the noodles and vegetables, and toss to combine.
  4. Top with a drizzle of chili oil and serve chilled or at room temperature.

3. Braised Eggplant with Garlic Sauce

Braised eggplant with garlic sauce is a popular Chinese dish that is not only rich in flavor but also incredibly easy and quick to prepare. Perfect for a weeknight dinner, this dish can be served over rice or noodles.

Ingredients:

  • 500g eggplant, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 1 onion, sliced
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon rice vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon oyster sauce (use vegetarian version)
  • 2 tablespoons water
  • 1 teaspoon white sugar
  • 1 teaspoon cornstarch mixed with 1 tablespoon water

Instructions:

  1. In a saucepan, heat a splash of oil and sauté the onion and garlic until fragrant.
  2. Add the eggplant, soy sauce, rice vinegar, oyster sauce, white sugar, and water. Simmer for 10 minutes, or until the eggplant is tender.
  3. Stir in the cornstarch mixture and cook until the sauce thickens. Serve immediately.

4. Lotus Root Salad

Fresh lotus root is used in various Chinese dishes, including stews and stir-fries. This refreshing lotus root salad is a fantastic way to showcase the unique texture and flavor in a healthier format.

Ingredients:

  • 1 fresh lotus root, peeled and julienned
  • ½ red bell pepper, julienned
  • ¼ cucumber, julienned
  • 2 green onions, chopped
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1 teaspoon sesame oil
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon rice vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon sugar
  • 1 teaspoon sesame seeds

Instructions:

  1. Boil the lotus root in salted water for 2 minutes, then drain and rinse under cold water. Set aside.
  2. In a large bowl, combine lotus root, bell pepper, cucumber, and green onions.
  3. In a separate small bowl, whisk together vegetable oil, sesame oil, soy sauce, rice vinegar, and sugar.
  4. Pour the dressing over the vegetables and gently toss to combine. Sprinkle with sesame seeds before serving.

5. Pan-Fried Assorted Mushrooms Buns

These delectable, soft, fluffy buns filled with a mixture of mushrooms are perfect for a satisfying vegetarian meal. These buns can be made with any assortment of mushrooms for a flavorful filling.

Ingredients (Buns):

  • 500g all-purpose flour
  • 200ml warm water
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • 2 tablespoons cooking oil
  • ¼ teaspoon baking powder

Ingredients (Filling):

  • 200g assorted mushrooms, finely chopped
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon oyster sauce (use vegetarian version)
  • 1 tablespoon rice wine
  • 1 tablespoon cornstarch mixed with 2 tablespoons water
  • Green onions, chopped (for topping)

Instructions:

  1. To prepare the dough, combine all the bun ingredients in a mixing bowl and knead until smooth. Let the dough rise for 1 hour.
  2. Meanwhile, prepare the filling by sautéing mushrooms, onion, and garlic in a pan until soft. Season with soy sauce, oyster sauce, and rice wine. Stir in the cornstarch mixture and cook until thickened.
  3. Roll out the dough into small buns and fill them with the mushroom mixture. Seal the edges and place them on a greased pan. Brush the tops with cooking oil.
  4. Proof the buns for another 20 minutes, then bake at 190°C for 20 minutes or until golden brown. Top with chopped green onions before serving.

These Chinese vegetarian recipes are not only delicious but also packed with essential nutrients for a healthier lifestyle. From breakfast to dinner, each dish provides a perfect mix of flavors and textures, making every meal a unique and delightful culinary experience.
