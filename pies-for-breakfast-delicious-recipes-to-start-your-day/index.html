Pies for Breakfast: Delicious Recipes to Start Your Day


Pies aren’t just for dessert anymore! Transform your morning routine with these delightful breakfast pie recipes that are sure to impress your taste buds and nourish your body. From savory to sweet, these pies are perfect for any breakfast lover looking to elevate their morning meal. Let’s dive into a variety of flavors and fillings that will make waking up a little easier.

The Allure of Breakfast Pies

Breakfast pies offer a unique twist on traditional morning meals. They can be packed with nutritious ingredients, allowing you to kickstart your day with energy and satisfaction. Plus, they are versatile; you can make them ahead of time, freeze them, and reheat them for those busy mornings. The flaky crust, combined with savory or sweet fillings, creates a satisfying experience that can be enjoyed by everyone in the family.

Savory Breakfast Pies

1. Spinach and Feta Breakfast Pie

Ingredients:

  • 1 pre-made pie crust
  • 2 cups fresh spinach, chopped
  • 1 cup feta cheese, crumbled
  • 4 large eggs
  • 1 cup milk
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. In a skillet, heat olive oil over medium heat. Add chopped spinach and sauté until wilted, about 3-4 minutes.
  3. In a mixing bowl, whisk together the eggs, milk, garlic powder, salt, and pepper.
  4. Lay the pie crust in a pie dish and distribute the sautéed spinach evenly across the bottom. Sprinkle feta cheese over the spinach.
  5. Pour the egg mixture over the spinach and feta, ensuring even coverage.
  6. Bake for 30-35 minutes or until the filling is set and lightly golden on top.
  7. Allow to cool for a few minutes before slicing. Serve warm.

2. Breakfast Quiche with Bacon and Cheddar

Ingredients:

  • 1 pie crust
  • 6 slices of bacon, cooked and crumbled
  • 1 cup sharp cheddar cheese, shredded
  • 4 large eggs
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1/2 cup onion, finely chopped
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C).
  2. In a mixing bowl, whisk together eggs, cream, salt, and pepper.
  3. Place the pie crust in a pie dish, and sprinkle the crumbled bacon, chopped onion, and cheddar cheese evenly across the bottom.
  4. Pour the egg mixture over the filling, ensuring it’s well distributed.
  5. Bake for 35-40 minutes or until the quiche is puffed and golden.
  6. Let cool for a few minutes before slicing. Perfect for brunch gatherings!

3. Mushroom and Swiss Breakfast Pie

Ingredients:

  • 1 pie crust
  • 2 cups mushrooms, sliced
  • 1 cup Swiss cheese, shredded
  • 3 large eggs
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 tablespoon butter
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 teaspoon thyme (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. In a skillet, melt butter over medium heat. Add mushrooms and sauté until they are browned and moisture has evaporated.
  3. In a mixing bowl, whisk together eggs, milk, salt, pepper, and thyme.
  4. Place the pie crust in a pie dish. Layer the sautéed mushrooms and Swiss cheese in the pie crust.
  5. Pour the egg mixture over the filling.
  6. Bake for 30-35 minutes until the filling is set and the top is golden.
  7. Allow cooling slightly before slicing. Enjoy warm or at room temperature.

Sweet Breakfast Pies

4. Blueberry Cream Cheese Breakfast Pie

Ingredients:

  • 1 pie crust
  • 1 cup cream cheese, softened
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 2 cups fresh blueberries
  • 1 tablespoon cornstarch
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 1 egg (for egg wash)

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. In a mixing bowl, beat cream cheese, sugar, and vanilla until smooth.
  3. Spread the cream cheese mixture evenly on the bottom of the pie crust.
  4. In another bowl, combine blueberries, cornstarch, and lemon juice. Gently fold to coat the blueberries.
  5. Pour the blueberry mixture over the cream cheese layer.
  6. Brush the edges of the crust with beaten egg for a golden finish.
  7. Bake for 35-40 minutes or until the blueberries are bubbly and the crust is golden brown.
  8. Let cool before slicing. Serve with a drizzle of honey or a dollop of whipped cream.

5. Apple Cinnamon Breakfast Pie

Ingredients:

  • 1 pie crust
  • 3 cups apples, peeled and sliced (Granny Smith or Honeycrisp)
  • 1/2 cup brown sugar
  • 1 teaspoon cinnamon
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 1 tablespoon flour (to thicken)
  • 1 egg (for egg wash)

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 425°F (220°C).
  2. In a large bowl, mix sliced apples, brown sugar, cinnamon, lemon juice, and flour until well combined.
  3. Pour the apple mixture into the pie crust, spreading evenly.
  4. Brush the edges with beaten egg for a nice golden crust.
  5. Bake for 30-35 minutes or until the apples are tender and the crust is golden.
  6. Allow cooling slightly before serving. This pie pairs wonderfully with a scoop of vanilla ice cream.

6. Chocolate Chip Banana Breakfast Pie

Ingredients:

  • 1 premade pie crust
  • 2 ripe bananas, mashed
  • 1 cup chocolate chips
  • 3 large eggs
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/2 cup flour
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. In a bowl, whisk together eggs, sugar, and vanilla. Stir in the mashed bananas.
  3. Add flour and salt, mixing until just combined. Fold in chocolate chips.
  4. Pour the mixture into the pie crust.
  5. Bake for 25-30 minutes or until the center is set and a toothpick comes out clean.
  6. Allow cooling before slicing. This sweet breakfast pie is a great way to use up overripe bananas!

Tips for Perfect Breakfast Pies

  • Choose the Right Crust: Whether you opt for a homemade or store-bought crust, make sure it’s sturdy enough to hold the filling. Blind baking the crust for a few minutes can help prevent sogginess.

  • Experiment with Fillings: Don’t hesitate to mix and match your favorite ingredients. Go for seasonal fruits or vegetables, and try different cheeses and proteins to create your own signature breakfast pie.

  • Make It Ahead: Many breakfast pies can be made ahead of time and stored in the refrigerator or freezer. Just reheat them in the oven or microwave for a quick and satisfying morning meal.

  • Presentation Matters: Serve your breakfast pies with fresh fruit, yogurt, or a sprinkle of powdered sugar to enhance their visual appeal and flavor.

Conclusion

Breakfast pies are a delightful way to start your day, offering both convenience and flavor. From savory quiches to sweet fruit pies, there’s a breakfast pie to meet every palate. They’re perfect for family breakfasts, brunch gatherings, or even as a treat for yourself on busy mornings. With these recipes in hand, you can now enjoy the comfort and joy that breakfast pies bring to the table. So, roll up your sleeves, gather your ingredients, and let the pie-making adventure begin!
