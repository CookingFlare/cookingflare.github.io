Quick and Easy Garlic Butter Rice with Scallops


If you’re looking for a delicious and impressive dish that’s quick and easy to make, then you’ve come to the right place. This recipe for Garlic Butter Rice with Scallops is perfect for a special dinner or a weeknight treat. The combination of tender scallops and flavorful garlic butter rice will leave your taste buds dancing. Plus, the dish comes together in no time, making it perfect for those busy days when you still want to enjoy a gourmet meal.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of jasmine rice
  • 1 3/4 cups of chicken broth
  • 1/4 cup of unsalted butter
  • 4 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 pound of sea scallops, patted dry
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley for garnish

Instructions

  1. Start by rinsing the jasmine rice under cold water until the water runs clear. This will help remove any excess starch and prevent the rice from becoming too sticky when cooked.

  2. In a medium saucepan, combine the rinsed rice and chicken broth. Bring the mixture to a boil over medium-high heat. Once boiling, reduce the heat to low, cover the saucepan with a lid, and let the rice simmer for about 15-20 minutes, or until all the liquid is absorbed and the rice is tender.

  3. While the rice is cooking, prepare the scallops. In a large skillet, melt the unsalted butter over medium heat. Add the minced garlic and sauté for about 1-2 minutes, or until the garlic becomes fragrant.

  4. Pat the scallops dry with a paper towel and season them with salt and pepper. Once the garlic is fragrant, increase the heat to medium-high and add the scallops to the skillet. Cook the scallops for 2-3 minutes on each side, or until they are golden brown and slightly caramelized on the outside, and opaque in the center.

  5. Once the rice is cooked, fluff it with a fork and transfer it to the skillet with the scallops. Gently toss the rice with the garlic butter and evenly distribute the scallops throughout the rice.

  6. Remove the skillet from the heat and allow the flavors to meld together for a minute or two. Garnish with fresh parsley for a pop of color and a hint of freshness.

  7. Serve the Garlic Butter Rice with Scallops immediately, and enjoy the delightful combination of flavors and textures.

Tips and Variations

  • For an extra kick of flavor, consider adding a splash of white wine to the skillet after cooking the garlic, before adding the scallops.
  • If you’re not a fan of scallops, this recipe can easily be adapted to use shrimp or even chicken for a different twist.
  • Feel free to add some freshly grated Parmesan cheese on top just before serving for an extra layer of richness.

Final Thoughts

This Quick and Easy Garlic Butter Rice with Scallops is a fantastic dish that’s sure to impress. The tender scallops pair beautifully with the fragrant garlic butter rice, creating a symphony of flavors in every bite. Whether you’re cooking for a special occasion or just craving something special for dinner, this recipe is a winner. And the best part? It’s so easy to make that you’ll want to add it to your regular meal rotation. So, give this recipe a try and savor the deliciousness of this delightful dish!
