Mummy Dogs with Cheese and Bacon: A Fun and Easy Halloween Snack


As Halloween approaches, the quest for the perfect festive snack begins. Whether you’re hosting a spooky gathering or simply looking for a fun treat to enjoy with your family, Mummy Dogs with Cheese and Bacon are the perfect solution! These adorable little snacks bring together the deliciousness of hot dogs, gooey cheese, and crispy bacon, all wrapped up in a fun mummy disguise. In this post, we’ll explore how to create these delightful Mummy Dogs, the best tips for presentation, and some creative variations to keep things exciting.

What Are Mummy Dogs?

Mummy Dogs are a playful twist on the classic pig in a blanket. Traditionally, pig in a blanket consists of hot dogs wrapped in dough and baked until golden brown. The Mummy Dogs take this concept a step further by adding cheese and bacon into the mix, creating a savory, cheesy filling that’s hard to resist. With their spooky appearance and delicious flavor, Mummy Dogs are sure to be a hit at any Halloween celebration.

Ingredients You’ll Need

To get started on your Mummy Dogs with Cheese and Bacon, gather the following ingredients:

  • Hot Dogs: Choose your favorite brand or type of hot dog, whether beef, pork, turkey, or a plant-based alternative.
  • Cheese: Opt for a cheese that melts well, such as cheddar, mozzarella, or a cheese blend.
  • Bacon: Use crispy bacon to add an extra layer of flavor. You can also use bacon bits if you’re looking for a quicker option.
  • Puff Pastry or Crescent Roll Dough: This will serve as the mummy wrap.
  • Mustard or Ketchup: For serving and adding a little extra flavor.
  • Egg Wash: (Optional) This is made by whisking one egg with a bit of water or milk, which helps create a beautiful golden finish when baked.

Step-by-Step Instructions

Step 1: Preheat the Oven

Begin by preheating your oven to 375°F (190°C). This ensures that your Mummy Dogs will bake evenly and come out perfectly crispy.

Step 2: Prepare the Dough

If you are using puff pastry or crescent roll dough, roll it out on a lightly floured surface. If using crescent roll dough, press the seams together to create a solid sheet. Cut the dough into thin strips, about 1/2 inch wide. These strips will be used to wrap around the hot dogs, creating the mummy effect.

Step 3: Assemble the Mummy Dogs

  1. Cut the Hot Dogs: If you’d like, you can cut the hot dogs in half to make smaller bites, or leave them whole for a more substantial snack.

  2. Add Cheese: Take a piece of cheese and cut it into thin strips. Place a strip of cheese inside each hot dog. You can use a knife to make a small slit in the hot dog to insert the cheese, or you can wrap the cheese around the hot dog if it fits well.

  3. Wrap in Bacon: Next, take your crispy bacon and wrap it around the hot dog, covering the cheese as much as possible. You can use one or two pieces of bacon, depending on how much bacon flavor you want.

  4. Wrap with Dough: Now it’s time to wrap your mummies! Take the prepared dough strips and start wrapping them around the hot dog, bacon, and cheese combo. Leave a small gap for the “face” of the mummy, and make sure to crisscross the dough strips for the classic mummy look.

Step 4: Brush with Egg Wash

Once all the Mummy Dogs are wrapped, place them on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper. If desired, brush the exposed dough with an egg wash to achieve a beautiful golden color when baked.

Step 5: Bake

Place the baking sheet in the preheated oven and bake for 15-20 minutes or until the dough is golden brown and crispy. Keep an eye on them to ensure they don’t overbake.

Step 6: Create the Mummy Faces

After baking, remove the Mummy Dogs from the oven and let them cool slightly. Using mustard or ketchup, dot little eyes on the exposed hot dog parts to create a spooky face. Get creative with your designs!

Step 7: Serve and Enjoy

Your Mummy Dogs with Cheese and Bacon are now ready to be served! Arrange them on a platter and serve with extra mustard or ketchup for dipping. These snacks are sure to be a crowd-pleaser!

Tips for the Perfect Mummy Dogs

  • Choose Quality Ingredients: The better the quality of your hot dogs, cheese, and bacon, the more delicious your Mummy Dogs will be.
  • Experiment with Cheese: Don’t be afraid to try different types of cheese. Pepper jack can add a spicy kick, while cream cheese can provide a rich, creamy texture.
  • Make it Vegetarian: Substitute hot dogs for veggie dogs and bacon for a plant-based alternative to create a vegetarian version of Mummy Dogs.
  • Try Different Dough: Although puff pastry and crescent roll dough are popular choices, you can also experiment with biscuit dough or even homemade dough if you’re feeling adventurous.
  • Add Spices: For an extra layer of flavor, sprinkle some garlic powder, onion powder, or Italian herbs on the wrapped mummies before baking.

Creative Variations

While Mummy Dogs with Cheese and Bacon are delicious as is, you can easily customize them to suit your tastes or dietary needs. Here are a few creative variations to consider:

  1. Spooky Veggie Mummies: For a healthier option, use veggie dogs and wrap them in whole wheat dough. You can also add thin slices of bell peppers or zucchini for a colorful twist.

  2. Halloween-Themed Dipping Sauces: Create themed dipping sauces by mixing ketchup with a little bit of food coloring to create spooky colors or flavors. For instance, a black olive tapenade can serve as a “witch’s brew” dip.

  3. Add Veggies: Incorporate thinly sliced jalapeños or other veggies inside the hot dog before wrapping to add some crunch and flavor.

  4. Mini Mummy Dogs: Use cocktail sausages instead of hot dogs for bite-sized mummies that are perfect for parties.

  5. Mummy Pizza Dogs: Instead of cheese, try stuffing the hot dogs with pizza sauce and mozzarella cheese before wrapping them up. This will create a fun pizza-inspired treat!

Conclusion

Mummy Dogs with Cheese and Bacon are a fun and easy Halloween snack that will delight guests of all ages. Their playful appearance and delicious flavors make them a perfect addition to any Halloween party or family gathering. With simple ingredients and creative variations, you can customize your Mummy Dogs to suit your tastes while enjoying the spirit of the season. So, gather your family in the kitchen, and get ready to create some spooky snacks that are sure to be a hit! Happy Halloween!
