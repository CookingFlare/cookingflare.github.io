Flavorful Nut-Free Basil Pesto Pasta Recipe


If you’re a fan of pesto but have a nut allergy or simply prefer a nut-free version, this flavorful nut-free basil pesto pasta recipe is the perfect solution. Pesto is a classic Italian sauce that traditionally contains pine nuts, but this recipe replaces them with a combination of seeds and cheese to create a delicious, allergy-friendly alternative. The vibrant green color and fresh, aromatic flavor of this pesto will elevate your pasta dish to a whole new level.

Ingredients

For the nut-free basil pesto:

  • 2 cups fresh basil leaves
  • 3 cloves garlic
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/4 cup sunflower seeds
  • 1/4 cup pumpkin seeds (pepitas)
  • 1/2 cup extra virgin olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

For the pasta:

  • 1 pound of your favorite pasta (spaghetti, fettuccine, or penne)
  • Salt for boiling the pasta
  • Additional Parmesan cheese for serving

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Pesto:

    • In a food processor, combine the basil leaves, garlic, Parmesan cheese, sunflower seeds, and pumpkin seeds. Pulse until the ingredients are finely chopped.
    • While the food processor is running, slowly drizzle in the olive oil until the mixture is smooth and well combined.
    • Season the pesto with salt and pepper to taste. Set it aside.

  2. Cook the Pasta:

    • Bring a large pot of water to a boil. Season the water generously with salt.
    • Add the pasta to the boiling water and cook according to the package instructions until al dente.
    • Once cooked, drain the pasta, reserving a small amount of the cooking water.

  3. Combine the Pasta and Pesto:

    • Return the drained pasta to the pot.
    • Add the nut-free basil pesto to the pasta and toss until the pasta is evenly coated with the pesto. If the pesto is too thick, add a small amount of the reserved pasta cooking water to loosen it up.

  4. Serve and Enjoy:

    • Divide the pesto-coated pasta among serving plates or bowls.
    • Sprinkle additional Parmesan cheese over each portion.
    • Serve immediately and enjoy the vibrant flavors of this nut-free basil pesto pasta.

Tips and Variations

  • If you prefer a smoother pesto, you can process the ingredients for a longer time in the food processor.
  • For a dairy-free version, you can substitute the Parmesan cheese with nutritional yeast or a dairy-free cheese alternative.
  • Feel free to add a handful of halved cherry tomatoes or a handful of steamed green beans to the pasta for added freshness and color.

This nut-free basil pesto pasta is a versatile dish that can be served as a main course or as a side dish alongside grilled chicken or fish. Its bright, herbaceous flavor makes it a perfect choice for a summertime meal or a quick and satisfying weeknight dinner. Whether you’re catering to nut allergies or simply looking for a delightful twist on a classic recipe, this nut-free basil pesto pasta is sure to become a new favorite in your culinary repertoire. Enjoy!
