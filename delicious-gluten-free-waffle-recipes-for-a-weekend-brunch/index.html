Delicious Gluten-Free Waffle Recipes for a Weekend Brunch


Are you ready to elevate your weekend brunch game with some delicious gluten-free waffle recipes? Whether you’re gluten intolerant or simply looking to explore new culinary horizons, these recipes are sure to delight your taste buds and impress your brunch guests. From classic flavors to innovative twists, these gluten-free waffle recipes are a must-try for anyone who loves a good weekend brunch. So, dust off your waffle maker and let’s get cooking!

Classic Gluten-Free Waffles

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups gluten-free flour blend
  • 1 tablespoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 3/4 cups milk
  • 1/2 cup vegetable oil
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Instructions:

  1. In a large bowl, whisk together the gluten-free flour blend, baking powder, salt, and sugar.
  2. In a separate bowl, beat the eggs and then stir in the milk, vegetable oil, and vanilla extract.
  3. Gradually add the wet ingredients to the dry ingredients, stirring until just combined.
  4. Preheat your waffle maker and lightly grease it with oil or non-stick cooking spray.
  5. Pour the batter into the waffle maker and cook according to the manufacturer’s instructions.
  6. Serve the classic gluten-free waffles with your favorite toppings such as fresh fruit, maple syrup, or whipped cream.

Chocolate Chip Banana Waffles

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups gluten-free flour blend
  • 1 tablespoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 3/4 cups milk
  • 1/2 cup vegetable oil
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 2 ripe bananas, mashed
  • 1/2 cup chocolate chips

Instructions:

  1. Follow the same instructions as for the classic gluten-free waffles, adding the mashed bananas and chocolate chips to the batter before cooking in the waffle maker.
  2. Once cooked, top the chocolate chip banana waffles with additional banana slices, a drizzle of chocolate sauce, and a sprinkle of powdered sugar for a decadent brunch treat.

Savory Spinach and Feta Waffles

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups gluten-free flour blend
  • 1 tablespoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 3/4 cups milk
  • 1/2 cup vegetable oil
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 cup fresh spinach, chopped
  • 1/2 cup crumbled feta cheese

Instructions:

  1. In a large bowl, combine the gluten-free flour blend, baking powder, and salt.
  2. In a separate bowl, whisk together the eggs, milk, vegetable oil, and garlic powder.
  3. Gradually add the wet ingredients to the dry ingredients, stirring until smooth.
  4. Fold in the chopped spinach and crumbled feta cheese.
  5. Preheat the waffle maker and pour the batter into the hot waffle iron, cooking until golden and crisp.
  6. Serve the savory spinach and feta waffles with a dollop of Greek yogurt and a sprinkle of fresh herbs for a delightful savory brunch option.

Conclusion

There you have it – three delicious gluten-free waffle recipes to elevate your weekend brunch experience. Whether you prefer classic flavors, indulgent chocolate chip banana waffles, or savory spinach and feta waffles, there’s something for everyone to enjoy. These gluten-free waffle recipes are not only delicious but also versatile, allowing you to get creative with your favorite toppings and accompaniments. So, gather your friends and family, fire up the waffle maker, and savor the joy of a leisurely weekend brunch with these delightful gluten-free waffles!
