One-Pot Beef and Broccoli Ramen Recipe


If you’re looking for a quick and delicious meal that’s easy to make and requires minimal cleanup, then this One-Pot Beef and Broccoli Ramen recipe is perfect for you. This dish combines the rich flavors of tender beef, vibrant broccoli, and savory ramen noodles in a single pot, creating a satisfying and comforting meal that’s sure to become a staple in your recipe collection.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb (450g) flank steak, thinly sliced
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon fresh ginger, grated
  • 1/2 cup (120ml) soy sauce
  • 1/4 cup (60ml) oyster sauce
  • 1/4 cup (60ml) beef broth
  • 2 tablespoons brown sugar
  • 4 cups (950ml) water
  • 2 (3oz) packages of ramen noodles
  • 2 cups (300g) broccoli florets
  • 2 green onions, thinly sliced
  • Sesame seeds for garnish (optional)

Instructions

  1. In a large pot or deep skillet, heat a small amount of oil over medium-high heat. Add the thinly sliced flank steak and cook until browned, then remove the beef from the pot and set it aside.

  2. In the same pot, add the minced garlic and grated ginger, and sauté for a minute until fragrant.

  3. Pour in the soy sauce, oyster sauce, beef broth, and brown sugar, stirring to combine the ingredients and create a flavorful sauce.

  4. Add the water to the pot and bring it to a boil. Once boiling, carefully add the ramen noodles and cook for 2-3 minutes until they start to soften.

  5. Stir in the broccoli florets and the cooked beef, allowing them to simmer in the flavorful broth until the broccoli is tender-crisp and the beef is cooked to your preference.

  6. Once the beef and broccoli are cooked, taste the broth and adjust the seasoning if necessary.

  7. Serve the One-Pot Beef and Broccoli Ramen in bowls, garnished with thinly sliced green onions and a sprinkle of sesame seeds if desired.

Tips and Variations

  • For a spicy kick, add a pinch of red pepper flakes or a drizzle of chili oil.
  • Substitute the flank steak with thinly sliced chicken breast or tofu for a different protein option.
  • Feel free to customize the vegetables with your favorites, such as snap peas, bell peppers, or mushrooms.

Why You’ll Love This Recipe

This One-Pot Beef and Broccoli Ramen recipe is a fantastic option for busy weeknights when you crave a satisfying meal without spending hours in the kitchen. The combination of tender beef, vibrant broccoli, and comforting ramen noodles in a delicious savory sauce makes it a crowd-pleaser for both adults and kids. Plus, the convenience of cooking everything in one pot means minimal cleanup, allowing you to spend more time enjoying your meal and less time washing dishes.

Whether you’re a ramen enthusiast or simply looking for a new twist on a classic dish, this recipe is a must-try. Its versatility allows for personalization, making it suitable for various dietary preferences and flavor profiles. With its simple yet bold flavors, this One-Pot Beef and Broccoli Ramen is bound to become a regular feature on your dinner table. So, gather your ingredients, grab a pot, and get ready to enjoy a delightful and stress-free cooking experience with this delightful recipe!
