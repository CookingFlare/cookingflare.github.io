Delightful Allergy-Friendly Blueberry Muffins for a Healthy Snack


Are you looking for a scrumptious, allergy-friendly snack that’s perfect for any time of the day? Look no further than these delightful blueberry muffins! Packed with juicy blueberries and wholesome ingredients, these muffins are not only delicious but also suitable for those with various dietary restrictions. Whether you’re allergic to dairy, eggs, or gluten, you can still indulge in these moist and flavorful muffins without worry. Let’s dive into the recipe and discover how to create these irresistible treats in your own kitchen.

Ingredients

Dry Ingredients:

  • 2 cups gluten-free flour
  • 1/2 cup almond flour
  • 1/2 cup coconut sugar
  • 1 tablespoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt

Wet Ingredients:

  • 1 ripe banana, mashed
  • 1/4 cup unsweetened applesauce
  • 1/4 cup coconut oil, melted
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 cup dairy-free milk (such as almond or oat milk)
  • 1 tablespoon apple cider vinegar

Additional Ingredients:

  • 1 1/2 cups fresh blueberries
  • 1 tablespoon gluten-free flour (for coating the blueberries)

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C) and line a muffin tin with paper liners or lightly grease the cups.

  2. In a small bowl, toss the fresh blueberries with 1 tablespoon of gluten-free flour until they are evenly coated. This will prevent the blueberries from sinking to the bottom of the muffins during baking.

  3. In a large mixing bowl, combine the gluten-free flour, almond flour, coconut sugar, baking powder, baking soda, and salt. Mix well to ensure all the dry ingredients are thoroughly combined.

  4. In another bowl, whisk together the mashed banana, unsweetened applesauce, melted coconut oil, vanilla extract, dairy-free milk, and apple cider vinegar until smooth and well blended.

  5. Gradually add the wet ingredients to the dry ingredients, stirring gently until just combined. Be careful not to overmix the batter, as this can result in dense muffins.

  6. Gently fold the flour-coated blueberries into the batter, being careful not to break the blueberries.

  7. Spoon the batter into the prepared muffin tin, filling each cup about two-thirds full.

  8. Bake the muffins in the preheated oven for 20-25 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center of a muffin comes out clean.

  9. Once baked, remove the muffins from the oven and allow them to cool in the tin for 5 minutes before transferring them to a wire rack to cool completely.

Serving Suggestions

These allergy-friendly blueberry muffins are perfect for any occasion. Whether you’re looking for a quick breakfast on the go, a satisfying afternoon snack, or a delightful treat to accompany your afternoon tea, these muffins fit the bill. Enjoy them warm or at room temperature, and store any leftovers in an airtight container for up to 3 days.

Final Thoughts

With their tender crumb and burst of juicy blueberries in every bite, these allergy-friendly blueberry muffins are sure to become a favorite in your household. Their simple preparation and wholesome ingredients make them a guilt-free indulgence that everyone can enjoy. So, go ahead and whip up a batch of these delightful muffins to satisfy your cravings and share the joy of allergy-friendly baking with your loved ones!
