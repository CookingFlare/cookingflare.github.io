Vegan Coconut Lime Cupcakes: A Tropical Treat


As the summer sun beats down, our taste buds crave something refreshing and cooling. What better way to quench that thirst than with a sweet treat that combines the tropical flavors of coconut and lime? These Vegan Coconut Lime Cupcakes are a game-changer, offering a delightful escape from the heat and a taste of the exotic.

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/2 cup unsweetened shredded coconut
  • 1/2 cup coconut oil
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 2 teaspoons lime zest
  • 2 tablespoons freshly squeezed lime juice
  • 2 large eggs, replaced by 2 flax eggs*
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • Confectioners’ sugar, for dusting

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (180°C). Line a 12-cup muffin tin with cupcake liners.
  2. In a medium bowl, whisk together the flour, sugar, and unsweetened shredded coconut.
  3. In a large bowl, whisk together the coconut oil, baking powder, and salt.
  4. Add the lime zest, lime juice, flax eggs, and vanilla extract to the wet ingredients. Whisk until smooth.
  5. Gradually add the dry ingredients to the wet ingredients, whisking until just combined.
  6. Divide the batter evenly among the cupcake liners.
  7. Bake for 18-20 minutes or until a toothpick inserted in the center comes out clean.
  8. Allow the cupcakes to cool completely in the pan before frosting.

Coconut Lime Buttercream Frosting

  • 1/2 cup unsalted vegan butter, softened
  • 1 1/2 cups powdered sugar
  • 2 tablespoons unsweetened coconut cream
  • 1 teaspoon lime juice
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
  1. Beat the softened butter until creamy.
  2. Gradually add the powdered sugar, beating until smooth and creamy.
  3. Add the coconut cream, lime juice, and vanilla extract. Beat until combined.

Assembly and Decoration

  1. Once the cupcakes are completely cool, frost each cupcake with the Coconut Lime Buttercream Frosting.
  2. Dust the top of each cupcake with confectioners’ sugar.
  3. Garnish with toasted coconut flakes or chopped fresh lime zest, if desired.

Tips and Variations

  • For an extra burst of lime flavor, drizzle the cupcakes with a lime glaze made by whisking together 1 tablespoon powdered sugar and 1 tablespoon freshly squeezed lime juice.
  • Substitute the flax eggs with mashed banana or applesauce for a different texture.
  • Add a sprinkle of shredded coconut or chopped nuts to the batter for added texture.
  • Use these cupcakes as a base for a tropical cake by layering them with whipped coconut cream and toasted coconut flakes.

Conclusion

These Vegan Coconut Lime Cupcakes are a refreshing and decadent treat that’s perfect for hot summer days. The combination of coconut and lime is a match made in heaven, and the flax eggs provide a moist and tender crumb. Whether you’re a vegan or just looking for a unique dessert option, these cupcakes are sure to impress. So go ahead, indulge in a tropical treat and let the sweet escape begin!
