Savory Mexican Chorizo Breakfast Burritos


Wake up to the flavors of Mexico with these delicious and satisfying Savory Mexican Chorizo Breakfast Burritos. Packed with savory chorizo, fluffy scrambled eggs, melted cheese, and a medley of vibrant vegetables, these burritos are the perfect way to start your day. Whether you’re looking for a quick breakfast on the go or a leisurely brunch, this recipe will surely satisfy your cravings.

Ingredients

  • 8 large flour tortillas
  • 1 pound Mexican chorizo
  • 8 large eggs
  • 1 bell pepper, diced
  • 1 small onion, diced
  • 1 jalapeno pepper, seeded and finely chopped
  • 1 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • Fresh cilantro, for garnish
  • Salsa, for serving

Instructions

  1. Heat a large skillet over medium heat and add the chorizo. Cook, breaking it up with a spoon, until browned and cooked through, about 5-7 minutes. Remove the chorizo from the skillet and set aside.

  2. In the same skillet, add the diced onion, bell pepper, and jalapeno pepper. Sauté until the vegetables are tender, about 5 minutes.

  3. In a separate bowl, whisk the eggs until well beaten. Pour the eggs into the skillet with the sautéed vegetables. Cook, stirring occasionally, until the eggs are scrambled and cooked to your desired consistency.

  4. Warm the flour tortillas in a dry skillet or microwave until pliable. This will make them easier to roll.

  5. To assemble the burritos, place a spoonful of the cooked chorizo onto each tortilla. Top with a scoop of the scrambled eggs, a sprinkle of shredded cheddar cheese, and a pinch of salt and pepper.

  6. Fold the sides of the tortilla inwards, then roll it up tightly to form a burrito. Repeat with the remaining ingredients.

  7. If desired, you can heat the assembled burritos in a skillet or microwave for a few seconds to melt the cheese and warm them through.

  8. Garnish the burritos with fresh cilantro and serve with salsa on the side for dipping.

Tips and Variations

  • Feel free to customize your breakfast burritos by adding your favorite toppings such as diced tomatoes, avocado, or sour cream.
  • If you prefer a spicier kick, you can use spicy chorizo or add some hot sauce to the scrambled eggs.
  • These burritos can be made ahead of time and stored in the refrigerator or freezer for a quick and convenient breakfast option.
  • If you’re looking to make a vegetarian version, you can substitute the chorizo with vegetarian chorizo or black beans.

Conclusion

Savory Mexican Chorizo Breakfast Burritos are a flavorful and hearty way to start your day. With the perfect balance of savory chorizo, fluffy scrambled eggs, and melted cheese, these burritos are sure to satisfy your taste buds. Whether you’re enjoying them on the go or savoring them during a leisurely brunch, these burritos are a delicious and satisfying breakfast option. Give this recipe a try and experience the flavors of Mexico in the comfort of your own kitchen.

Remember to experiment with different toppings and fillings to make these breakfast burritos your own. Enjoy and buen provecho!
