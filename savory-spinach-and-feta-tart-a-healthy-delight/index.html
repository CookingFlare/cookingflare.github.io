Savory Spinach and Feta Tart: A Healthy Delight


Are you looking for a delectable and healthy dish to impress your friends and family? Look no further than this savory spinach and feta tart recipe. Packed with nutritious ingredients and bursting with flavor, this tart is sure to become a favorite in your recipe repertoire. Whether you’re hosting a brunch, a casual dinner, or simply looking for a satisfying meal, this savory spinach and feta tart will not disappoint.

Ingredients:

  • 1 sheet of store-bought puff pastry
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 6 cups fresh spinach, roughly chopped
  • 1/2 cup crumbled feta cheese
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 4 large eggs
  • 1/2 cup milk or heavy cream
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Begin by preheating your oven to 375°F (190°C) and lightly greasing a tart pan or pie dish.

  2. Roll out the puff pastry sheet to fit the tart pan, then gently press it into the pan, trimming any excess pastry from the edges.

  3. In a skillet, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the chopped onion and minced garlic, sautéing until the onion is soft and translucent.

  4. Add the chopped spinach to the skillet, stirring gently until it wilts down. Remove the skillet from the heat and allow the spinach mixture to cool slightly.

  5. In a mixing bowl, whisk together the eggs and milk or heavy cream. Season with salt and pepper to taste.

  6. Spread the spinach mixture evenly over the puff pastry in the tart pan. Sprinkle the crumbled feta and grated Parmesan cheese over the spinach.

  7. Carefully pour the egg mixture over the spinach and cheese, ensuring that it is evenly distributed.

  8. Place the tart in the preheated oven and bake for 25-30 minutes, or until the pastry is golden brown and the filling is set.

  9. Once baked, remove the tart from the oven and allow it to cool for a few minutes before slicing and serving.

Serving Suggestions:

This savory spinach and feta tart pairs beautifully with a crisp, green salad dressed with a light vinaigrette. It’s also a fantastic addition to a brunch spread, alongside fresh fruit and a selection of pastries. For a heartier meal, serve the tart with roasted vegetables or a side of creamy mashed potatoes.

Health Benefits:

Not only is this savory spinach and feta tart incredibly delicious, but it also boasts a range of health benefits. Spinach is a rich source of vitamins and minerals, including iron, calcium, and vitamin K. Feta cheese adds a creamy, tangy flavor while providing a dose of protein and calcium. Paired with the lightness of puff pastry, this tart offers a balanced and nutritious option for any meal.

Conclusion:

In conclusion, this savory spinach and feta tart is a delightful combination of wholesome ingredients and indulgent flavors. Whether you’re a seasoned cook or a novice in the kitchen, this recipe is straightforward and rewarding. Impress your loved ones with this healthy and delicious dish that’s perfect for any occasion. So, what are you waiting for? It’s time to roll up your sleeves, preheat that oven, and get ready to savor every bite of this delightful spinach and feta tart. Enjoy!
