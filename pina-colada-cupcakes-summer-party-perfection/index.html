Pina Colada Cupcakes: Summer Party Perfection


Summer is the ideal season to bring out the playful and adventurous side of cooking. Swap out those traditional lemonades and cookies for our scrumptious Pina Colada Cupcakes - a refreshing and exotic twist on your usual dessert fare. These delightful treats are perfect for a summer party, or even an afternoon snack while you bask in the sunshine. So, take a sip of the tropics and let’s dive into the delicious world of Pina Colada Cupcakes.

Ingredients:

  • 1 and 1/4 cups all-purpose flour (188g)
  • 1 cup granulated sugar (200g)
  • 1 and 1/2 teaspoons baking powder (7g)
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt (3g)
  • 1 stick unsalted butter, softened (115g)
  • 1/2 cup vegetable oil (118ml)
  • 2 large eggs (100g with shell)
  • 1 and 1/2 teaspoons pure vanilla extract (7ml)
  • 1/2 cup milk (118ml)
  • 3/4 cup crushed pineapple in juice (drained), reserve 2 tablespoons of juice (177g)
  • 2 cups shredded coconut (80g), divided
  • 1 and 1/4 cups unsalted butter, softened (140g)
  • 3 and 1/2 cups powdered sugar (420g)
  • 1/2 cup reserved pineapple juice (118ml)
  • 1 teaspoon pure vanilla extract (5ml)
  • 1/2 cup crushed pineapple in juice (drained), to mix (50g)
  • Additional shredded coconut for garnishing (optional)

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (177°C). Line a muffin tin with 12 cupcake liners and set aside.

  2. In a medium bowl, whisk together the flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt. In a large bowl, beat the softened butter, oil, eggs, and vanilla extract until creamy. Gradually add the dry ingredients while alternating between additions of milk and reserved pineapple juice. Fold in the crushed pineapple and 1 and 3/4 cups (40g) of shredded coconut until evenly blended.

  3. Distribute the batter evenly among the cupcake liners, filling them about 2/3 full. Bake in the preheated oven for 18-20 minutes or until a toothpick inserted in the center of a cupcake comes out clean. Once done, let the cupcakes cool in the pan for a few minutes before transferring them to a cooling rack to cool completely.

  4. For the frosting, beat the softened butter until creamy in a large bowl. Gradually add the powdered sugar and vanilla extract, alternating between additions of the reserved pineapple juice. Fold in the remaining 1/4 cup (10g) of shredded coconut and 1/2 cup of crushed pineapple until fully incorporated.

  5. Once the cupcakes are cooled, spread or pipe the frosting evenly on top of each cupcake. Garnish with additional shredded coconut if desired.

Voila! Your Pina Colada Cupcakes are ready to be indulged in. Share them with your friends, family, or keep them all to yourself (we won’t judge!). Enjoy these delightfulsummer treats!

Conclusion

Whether you’re hosting a beach party, a tropical-themed wedding shower, or simply enjoying a warm summer evening, these Pina Colada Cupcakes will certainly infuse your gatherings with excitement and flavor. The cool and refreshing taste of pineapple and coconut transport you directly to an island getaway, making these cupcakes an absolute must-try for the summer season. So grab a cup of your favorite summer drink, put on your favorite Hawaiian shirt, and let’s get baking!
