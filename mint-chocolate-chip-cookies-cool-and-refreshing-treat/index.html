Mint Chocolate Chip Cookies: Cool and Refreshing Treat


If you’re looking for a delightful treat to satisfy your sweet tooth, look no further than these refreshing mint chocolate chip cookies. The combination of cool mint and rich chocolate chips creates a flavor explosion that will leave you wanting more. Whether you’re hosting a summer gathering or simply craving a cool and delicious treat, these cookies are sure to impress. So, let’s dive right into the recipe!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 cup brown sugar, packed
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 2 and 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 and 1/2 cups chocolate chips
  • 1/2 teaspoon peppermint extract
  • Green food coloring (optional)

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C) and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.

  2. In a large mixing bowl, cream together the softened butter, granulated sugar, and brown sugar until light and fluffy. You can use a stand mixer or handheld mixer for this step.

  3. Add the eggs one at a time, beating well after each addition. Stir in the vanilla extract.

  4. In a separate bowl, whisk together the flour, baking powder, baking soda, and salt. Gradually add this dry mixture to the wet ingredients, mixing until just combined. Be careful not to overmix, as this can result in tough cookies.

  5. Fold in the chocolate chips, making sure they are evenly distributed throughout the dough.

  6. If desired, add the peppermint extract for an extra burst of mint flavor. For a vibrant green color, add a few drops of green food coloring and mix until the desired shade is achieved. This step is optional but adds a fun touch to the cookies.

  7. Using a cookie scoop or tablespoon, drop rounded portions of dough onto the prepared baking sheet, spacing them about 2 inches apart. Flatten each portion slightly with the back of a spoon or your fingertips.

  8. Bake for 10-12 minutes, or until the edges are golden brown. The centers may appear slightly undercooked, but they will firm up as the cookies cool.

  9. Remove the baking sheet from the oven and let the cookies cool for a few minutes before transferring them to a wire rack to cool completely.

  10. Once cooled, these mint chocolate chip cookies are ready to be enjoyed! Serve them with a glass of cold milk or alongside a scoop of mint chocolate chip ice cream for an extra indulgent treat.

Tips and Variations

  • For a more intense mint flavor, you can add an additional 1/2 teaspoon of peppermint extract.
  • If you prefer a softer texture, reduce the baking time by 1-2 minutes.
  • Feel free to experiment with different types of chocolate chips, such as dark chocolate or white chocolate, to customize the flavor.
  • To store the cookies, place them in an airtight container at room temperature for up to 5 days. You can also freeze them for longer storage.

Enjoy the refreshing combination of mint and chocolate with every bite of these delightful cookies. They are perfect for any occasion, from summer parties to cozy winter nights by the fireplace. So, gather your ingredients and get ready to indulge in this cool and refreshing treat. Happy baking!
