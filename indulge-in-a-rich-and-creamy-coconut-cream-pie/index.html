Indulge in a Rich and Creamy Coconut Cream Pie


If you’re a fan of the tropical flavor of coconut, then you’re in for a treat with this indulgent and creamy coconut cream pie recipe. This delightful dessert is a perfect blend of rich coconut flavor, velvety smooth custard, and a buttery, flaky crust. It’s a classic dessert that will transport you to a sunny beach with just one bite.

Ingredients

For the Crust:

  • 1 1/2 cups of graham cracker crumbs
  • 1/3 cup of granulated sugar
  • 1/2 cup of unsalted butter, melted

For the Filling:

  • 1 cup of coconut milk
  • 1 cup of whole milk
  • 1 cup of sweetened shredded coconut
  • 1/2 cup of granulated sugar
  • 1/4 cup of cornstarch
  • 4 large egg yolks
  • 2 tablespoons of unsalted butter
  • 1 teaspoon of vanilla extract

For the Topping:

  • 1 1/2 cups of heavy cream
  • 3 tablespoons of powdered sugar
  • 1/2 cup of sweetened shredded coconut, toasted

Instructions

Step 1: Make the Crust

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. In a medium bowl, combine the graham cracker crumbs, sugar, and melted butter. Mix until the crumbs are evenly moistened.
  3. Press the crumb mixture into the bottom and up the sides of a 9-inch pie dish.
  4. Bake the crust for 10 minutes, then remove from the oven and let it cool completely.

Step 2: Prepare the Filling

  1. In a medium saucepan, combine the coconut milk, whole milk, and sweetened shredded coconut. Heat over medium heat until it just begins to simmer, then remove from the heat and let it steep for 30 minutes.
  2. In a separate bowl, whisk together the sugar, cornstarch, and egg yolks until smooth.
  3. Gradually whisk the egg mixture into the coconut milk mixture in the saucepan.
  4. Place the saucepan back on the stove over medium heat and cook, stirring constantly, until the mixture thickens and comes to a boil.
  5. Remove from the heat and stir in the butter and vanilla extract.
  6. Pour the filling into the cooled pie crust, cover with plastic wrap directly on the surface of the filling to prevent a skin from forming, and refrigerate for at least 4 hours or until set.

Step 3: Make the Topping

  1. In a large bowl, whip the heavy cream and powdered sugar until stiff peaks form.
  2. Spread the whipped cream over the chilled pie filling.
  3. Sprinkle the toasted coconut over the whipped cream.

Serving

Once the coconut cream pie is assembled, it’s ready to be served. This luscious dessert is perfect for any occasion, whether it’s a summer barbecue, a holiday feast, or simply a weeknight treat. The creamy coconut filling, combined with the light and airy whipped cream, is a match made in dessert heaven.

Conclusion

Indulge in the tropical flavors of coconut with this rich and creamy coconut cream pie. Whether you’re a coconut enthusiast or just looking to add a taste of the tropics to your dessert table, this recipe is sure to impress. The velvety smooth custard filling, the buttery graham cracker crust, and the fluffy whipped cream topping all come together to create a truly irresistible treat. So go ahead, treat yourself and your loved ones to a slice of paradise with this delightful coconut cream pie!
