Homemade Apple Cinnamon Bars: A Sweet and Spiced Treat


Who doesn’t love the comforting flavors of apple and cinnamon? These Homemade Apple Cinnamon Bars are the perfect sweet treat to satisfy your dessert cravings. Packed with the goodness of fresh apples and warm spices, these bars are easy to make and even easier to devour. So, let’s get baking!

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground nutmeg
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 3/4 cup brown sugar
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 2 cups peeled, cored, and diced apples (about 2 medium-sized apples)
  • 1/2 cup chopped walnuts (optional)

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease a 9x13-inch baking pan and set aside.

  2. In a medium-sized bowl, whisk together the flour, baking powder, baking soda, salt, cinnamon, and nutmeg. Set aside.

  3. In a separate large bowl, cream together the softened butter, brown sugar, and granulated sugar until light and fluffy. You can use an electric mixer or do this by hand.

  4. Beat in the eggs, one at a time, followed by the vanilla extract. Mix until well combined.

  5. Gradually add the dry ingredients to the wet ingredients, mixing just until everything is well incorporated. Be careful not to overmix.

  6. Fold in the diced apples and chopped walnuts (if using). The apples will add moisture and sweetness to the bars, while the walnuts will provide a delightful crunch.

  7. Pour the batter into the prepared baking pan and spread it evenly with a spatula.

  8. Bake in the preheated oven for 25-30 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean. The bars should be golden brown and slightly firm to the touch.

  9. Once baked, remove the pan from the oven and allow the bars to cool completely before cutting them into squares or rectangles.

  10. Serve these delectable Homemade Apple Cinnamon Bars as a delightful dessert or enjoy them with a cup of tea or coffee for a cozy afternoon treat. They also make a fantastic addition to any brunch or potluck gathering.

Tips and Variations

  • For an extra burst of flavor, consider adding a teaspoon of lemon zest to the batter. The hint of citrus will complement the apple and cinnamon beautifully.
  • If you prefer a softer texture, you can replace half of the all-purpose flour with whole wheat flour.
  • Feel free to adjust the amount of cinnamon and nutmeg to suit your taste preferences. If you’re a cinnamon lover, go ahead and add an extra pinch!
  • These bars can be stored in an airtight container at room temperature for up to 3 days. If you want to keep them for longer, refrigerate them for up to a week or freeze them for up to 3 months.

So, there you have it! A simple and scrumptious recipe for Homemade Apple Cinnamon Bars. Enjoy the delightful combination of sweet apples, warm spices, and buttery goodness. These bars are sure to become a favorite among family and friends. Happy baking!
