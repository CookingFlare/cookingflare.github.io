Slow Cooker Mediterranean Chicken Wraps for Healthy Lunches


Are you looking for a delicious and healthy lunch option that is both easy to prepare and full of flavor? Look no further than these Slow Cooker Mediterranean Chicken Wraps! Packed with Mediterranean-inspired ingredients and cooked to perfection in a slow cooker, these wraps are a great choice for busy individuals who want a nutritious and satisfying meal on the go.

Ingredients

To make these mouthwatering Mediterranean Chicken Wraps, you will need the following ingredients:

  • 2 pounds of boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1 cup of cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1 cucumber, sliced
  • 1 red onion, thinly sliced
  • 1 cup of mixed greens
  • 1/4 cup of Kalamata olives, pitted and sliced
  • 1/4 cup of feta cheese, crumbled
  • 1/4 cup of Greek yogurt
  • 2 tablespoons of lemon juice
  • 2 tablespoons of olive oil
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon of dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Whole wheat tortillas or pita bread for wrapping

Instructions

  1. In a slow cooker, place the chicken breasts and season them with salt, pepper, minced garlic, and dried oregano. Pour in the olive oil and lemon juice, ensuring the chicken is evenly coated with the marinade.

  2. Cover the slow cooker and cook on low heat for 6-8 hours or on high heat for 3-4 hours, or until the chicken is tender and cooked through.

  3. Once the chicken is cooked, remove it from the slow cooker and shred it using two forks. Return the shredded chicken to the slow cooker and mix it with the remaining juices to ensure it stays moist and flavorful.

  4. Prepare the toppings for your wraps by slicing the cherry tomatoes, cucumber, and red onion. Crumble the feta cheese and pit and slice the Kalamata olives.

  5. To assemble the wraps, warm the whole wheat tortillas or pita bread in a dry skillet or in the microwave for a few seconds.

  6. Spread a spoonful of Greek yogurt onto each tortilla or pita bread, then layer on the mixed greens.

  7. Add a generous portion of the slow-cooked Mediterranean chicken on top of the greens, followed by the sliced cherry tomatoes, cucumber, red onion, Kalamata olives, and crumbled feta cheese.

  8. Fold the sides of the tortilla or pita bread over the filling, then roll it tightly to create a wrap.

  9. Repeat the process with the remaining tortillas or pita bread and fillings.

  10. Serve the Slow Cooker Mediterranean Chicken Wraps immediately or pack them for a nutritious and satisfying lunch on the go. These wraps can be refrigerated for up to three days, making them perfect for meal prepping.

Conclusion

These Slow Cooker Mediterranean Chicken Wraps are a fantastic option for those seeking a healthy and flavorful lunch. The slow-cooked chicken is tender and infused with Mediterranean flavors, while the fresh vegetables, tangy feta cheese, and creamy Greek yogurt add a delightful combination of textures and tastes. Whether you’re a busy professional, a student, or simply someone who enjoys a delicious lunch, these wraps are sure to satisfy your cravings while keeping you energized throughout the day. Give them a try and enjoy the taste of the Mediterranean in every bite!
