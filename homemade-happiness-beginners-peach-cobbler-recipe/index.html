Homemade Happiness: Beginner's Peach Cobbler Recipe


Welcome to the ultimate guide on creating your own slice of homemade happiness with a delightful peach cobbler. This beginner-friendly recipe is the perfect way to indulge in the sweet, comforting flavors of summer. Whether you’re a seasoned baker or just starting out on your culinary journey, this peach cobbler recipe is sure to bring warmth and joy to your kitchen. So, let’s dive in and uncover the secrets to creating this classic dessert!

Ingredients:

  • 4 cups fresh or canned peaches, sliced
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, melted
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C) and grease a baking dish with butter.
  2. In a large mixing bowl, combine the peaches, 1/2 cup sugar, and cinnamon. Spread the mixture evenly into the prepared baking dish.
  3. In another bowl, whisk together the flour, remaining 1/2 cup sugar, baking powder, and salt.
  4. Add the melted butter, milk, and vanilla extract to the dry ingredients, and stir until just combined.
  5. Pour the batter over the peaches, spreading it out evenly.
  6. Bake the peach cobbler for 40-45 minutes, or until the top is golden brown and the filling is bubbly.
  7. Once done, remove the cobbler from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes before serving.

Tips for Success:

  • For a twist, consider adding a handful of blueberries or raspberries to the peach filling for an extra burst of flavor.
  • Serve the peach cobbler warm with a scoop of vanilla ice cream or a dollop of whipped cream for a decadent treat.
  • Store any leftover cobbler in the refrigerator and reheat it in the oven for a delightful dessert the next day.
