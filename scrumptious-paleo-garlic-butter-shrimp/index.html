Scrumptious Paleo Garlic Butter Shrimp


If you’re looking for a delicious and healthy dish that’s quick and easy to prepare, then this Scrumptious Paleo Garlic Butter Shrimp recipe is just what you need. Packed with flavor and nutrients, this dish is perfect for anyone following a paleo lifestyle or simply looking for a mouthwatering seafood option. Let’s dive into the recipe and discover how to create this delectable dish in the comfort of your own kitchen.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound of large shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 4 tablespoons of ghee or clarified butter
  • 4 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon of paprika
  • 1 teaspoon of dried oregano
  • 1/2 teaspoon of sea salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon of black pepper
  • Fresh parsley, chopped, for garnish
  • Cauliflower rice or zucchini noodles, for serving

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Shrimp: Begin by patting the shrimp dry with paper towels. This helps ensure that they cook evenly and develop a nice sear.

  2. Melt the Butter: In a large skillet over medium heat, melt the ghee or clarified butter. Once melted, add the minced garlic to the skillet and sauté for about 1 minute, or until fragrant.

  3. Cook the Shrimp: Add the prepared shrimp to the skillet, arranging them in a single layer. Sprinkle the paprika, dried oregano, sea salt, and black pepper over the shrimp. Cook for 2-3 minutes on each side, or until they turn pink and opaque.

  4. Garnish and Serve: Once the shrimp are cooked through, remove the skillet from the heat. Sprinkle the chopped parsley over the shrimp for a fresh and vibrant finish. Serve the garlic butter shrimp over a bed of cauliflower rice or zucchini noodles for a complete and satisfying meal.

Why Choose Paleo Garlic Butter Shrimp?

This Scrumptious Paleo Garlic Butter Shrimp recipe offers a plethora of benefits. Firstly, shrimp is a fantastic source of lean protein, making it an excellent choice for those looking to maintain a healthy and balanced diet. Additionally, when cooked with ghee or clarified butter, this dish becomes a rich source of healthy fats, which can support brain function and overall well-being.

The use of garlic and herbs not only enhances the flavor of the shrimp but also provides numerous health benefits. Garlic is known for its immune-boosting properties and can aid in reducing inflammation, while oregano is packed with antioxidants that contribute to overall health.

Furthermore, this recipe is entirely grain-free and low in carbohydrates, making it suitable for those following a paleo or low-carb diet. By serving the shrimp with cauliflower rice or zucchini noodles, you can enjoy a satisfying and filling meal without the need for traditional grains.

In Summary

This Scrumptious Paleo Garlic Butter Shrimp recipe is a true delight for the taste buds and a nourishing option for those seeking a wholesome and flavorful meal. Whether you’re cooking for yourself or entertaining guests, this dish is sure to impress. The simplicity of the ingredients and the ease of preparation make it a go-to recipe for busy weeknights or leisurely weekends. So, why not give it a try and savor the delightful combination of succulent shrimp, aromatic garlic, and rich butter in every mouthful?
