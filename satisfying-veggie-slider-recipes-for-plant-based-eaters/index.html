Satisfying Veggie Slider Recipes for Plant-Based Eaters


If you’re a plant-based eater looking for a delicious and satisfying meal, look no further than veggie sliders. These bite-sized wonders are packed with flavor, texture, and all the nutrients you need. Whether you’re hosting a party, craving a snack, or want a fun twist on a classic burger, these veggie slider recipes are sure to satisfy your taste buds. Get ready to take your plant-based eating to the next level!

1. Portobello Mushroom Sliders

Ingredients:

  • 4 Portobello mushroom caps
  • 2 tablespoons balsamic vinegar
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 4 whole wheat slider buns
  • Toppings of your choice (lettuce, tomato, onion, avocado, etc.)

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your grill or grill pan over medium heat.
  2. In a small bowl, whisk together the balsamic vinegar, olive oil, garlic powder, salt, and pepper.
  3. Brush the mushroom caps on both sides with the marinade.
  4. Place the mushrooms on the grill and cook for about 4-5 minutes on each side, until they are tender and slightly charred.
  5. Remove the mushrooms from the grill and let them cool slightly.
  6. Assemble your sliders by placing a mushroom cap on each bun and adding your desired toppings.
  7. Serve immediately and enjoy!

2. Lentil Walnut Sliders

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup cooked lentils
  • 1/2 cup walnuts, chopped
  • 1/4 cup breadcrumbs
  • 1/4 cup finely chopped onion
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 1 teaspoon smoked paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon cumin
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 4 whole wheat slider buns
  • Toppings of your choice (spinach, red onion, pickles, vegan mayo, etc.)

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C) and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
  2. In a large bowl, mash the cooked lentils with a fork or potato masher until they are mostly broken down.
  3. Add the chopped walnuts, breadcrumbs, onion, garlic, soy sauce, smoked paprika, cumin, salt, and pepper to the bowl. Mix well to combine.
  4. Shape the mixture into small patties, about the size of your slider buns, and place them on the prepared baking sheet.
  5. Bake the sliders for 20-25 minutes, flipping them halfway through, until they are golden brown and crispy.
  6. Remove the sliders from the oven and let them cool slightly before assembling.
  7. Spread your desired toppings on the buns, add a lentil walnut patty, and enjoy your flavorful sliders!

3. Quinoa Black Bean Sliders

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup cooked quinoa
  • 1 cup cooked black beans, mashed
  • 1/4 cup finely chopped red bell pepper
  • 1/4 cup finely chopped red onion
  • 2 tablespoons chopped fresh cilantro
  • 2 tablespoons breadcrumbs
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • 1/2 teaspoon chili powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 4 whole wheat slider buns
  • Toppings of your choice (guacamole, salsa, vegan cheese, etc.)

Instructions:

  1. In a large bowl, combine the cooked quinoa, mashed black beans, red bell pepper, red onion, cilantro, breadcrumbs, cumin, chili powder, salt, and pepper. Mix well to combine.
  2. Shape the mixture into small patties, about the size of your slider buns.
  3. Heat a non-stick skillet over medium heat and lightly grease it with oil.
  4. Cook the sliders for 3-4 minutes on each side, until they are crispy and heated through.
  5. Remove the sliders from the skillet and let them cool slightly.
  6. Assemble your sliders by placing a quinoa black bean patty on each bun and adding your desired toppings.
  7. Serve immediately and savor the deliciousness!

Conclusion

These satisfying veggie slider recipes are proof that plant-based eating can be exciting, flavorful, and downright delicious. Whether you choose the umami-rich Portobello mushroom sliders, the protein-packed lentil walnut sliders, or the zesty quinoa black bean sliders, you’re in for a treat. So gather your ingredients, fire up the grill or stove, and get ready to enjoy these mouthwatering plant-based creations. Your taste buds and your body will thank you!
