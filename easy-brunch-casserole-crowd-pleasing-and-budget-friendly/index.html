Easy Brunch Casserole: Crowd-Pleasing and Budget-Friendly


Brunch casseroles are a fantastic option for gatherings, holidays, and simply treating yourself on a lazy weekend morning. This versatile and hearty dish offers endless customizations, making it an incredibly budget-friendly way to feed a crowd. Not to mention, it comes together remarkably easily, making it a foolproof win for anyone looking to impress their guests.

Ingredients

  • For the base:
    • 1 pound of cooked and crumbled bacon
    • 1 cup of shredded cheddar cheese
    • 1 cup of shredded Mozzarella cheese
    • Half a cup of diced onions
    • 2 minced garlic cloves
    • 1 cup of all-purpose flour
    • 2 large eggs
    • 2 cups of milk
    • 2 tablespoons butter
    • 2 teaspoons baking powder
    • Half a teaspoon of salt
  • For the topping:
    • 2 cups of fresh spinach, roughly chopped
    • 1 bell pepper, finely diced
    • 1 avocado, cubed
    • 1 tomato, sliced
    • 1 tablespoon chopped fresh chives
    • 4 eggs, sunny side up or poached (optional)

Instructions

First, cook and crumble your bacon and set aside. While your bacon cooks, dice your onions and mince your garlic - have these ready before proceeding.

Next, preheat your oven to 375 degrees Fahrenheit.

In a large bowl, combine your flour, baking powder, and salt. Add in your eggs, milk, and melted butter, stirring until well combined. Then, fold in the cooked bacon, 3/4 cups of cheddar, and 3/4 cups of Mozzarella. Reserve the remaining cheese for topping the casserole.

Prepare a 9x13 inch baking dish by greasing with non-stick spray. Spread half of the bacon mixture into an even layer in your baking dish. Layer on half of the diced onions and minced garlic. Add a layer of spinach on top of this and sprinkle it generously with the remaining cheddar and mozzarella cheeses.

Pour remaining bacon mixture over the spinach, creating an even layer. Top with remaining onions, garlic, and diced bell pepper.

Place your casserole in the oven and bake for approximately 40 minutes, or until the center is set and the edges start to pull away from the sides of the dish.

While your casserole bakes, prepare your toppings. Finely dice your avocado and tomato, chop your chives, and get your sunny side up or poached eggs ready.

Once your casserole has finished baking, remove it from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes. Garnish with your freshly prepared toppings, enjoy, and prepare for compliments!

A Winning Brunch

By making this Easy Brunch Casserole, you’ll set yourself up for a winning meal. Combining budget-friendly ingredients with generous portions, you’ll have an effortlessly impressive yet hearty brunch option.

Don’t limit this casseroles use to weekends. With its make-ahead possibilities and customizable toppings, it can easily accommodate vegan, vegetarian, and special dietary needs. Given the plethora of combinations, your creative options for this casserole are endless!

In conclusion, the Easy Brunch Casserole is not just a mouth-watering, crowd-pleasing dish, but it’s also a versatile, inexpensive, and easy-to-make option for any occasion, making it perfect for everyone to enjoy!
