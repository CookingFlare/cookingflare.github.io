Savory Instant Pot Beef and Broccoli Recipe for a Tasty Dinner


If you’re looking for a quick and delicious dinner idea, look no further than this savory Instant Pot Beef and Broccoli recipe. With the magic of the Instant Pot, you can have a flavorful and tender beef dish ready in no time. The combination of juicy beef, crisp broccoli, and a delectable sauce will leave your taste buds begging for more. Let’s get started!

Ingredients

  • 1 pound of beef, thinly sliced
  • 2 cups of broccoli florets
  • 1/2 cup of beef broth
  • 1/4 cup of soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons of brown sugar
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon of ginger, grated
  • 1 tablespoon of cornstarch
  • 2 tablespoons of water
  • Optional: sesame seeds and sliced green onions for garnish

Instructions

  1. Start by preparing your beef. Slice it thinly against the grain to ensure tenderness. Set it aside while you prepare the sauce.

  2. In a small bowl, whisk together the beef broth, soy sauce, brown sugar, minced garlic, and grated ginger. This sauce will be the star of the dish, providing a perfect balance of sweet and savory flavors.

  3. Preheat your Instant Pot by selecting the “Saute” function. Once hot, add a drizzle of oil and sear the beef slices in batches until browned on both sides. This step helps to lock in the flavors and gives the beef a nice caramelized exterior.

  4. Once all the beef is seared, pour in the prepared sauce and give it a good stir. Make sure to scrape any browned bits from the bottom of the pot to prevent burning.

  5. Secure the lid on the Instant Pot and set the valve to the “Sealing” position. Select the “Manual” or “Pressure Cook” function and set the timer for 10 minutes. The Instant Pot will work its magic, tenderizing the beef and infusing it with the flavors of the sauce.

  6. While the beef is cooking, steam the broccoli florets. You can either use a steamer basket on the stovetop or the Instant Pot’s steam function. Cook the broccoli until it’s vibrant green and tender-crisp.

  7. Once the beef is done cooking, perform a quick release of the pressure by carefully turning the valve to the “Venting” position. Once the pressure has been released, carefully remove the lid.

  8. In a small bowl, whisk together the cornstarch and water to create a slurry. Stir the slurry into the beef and sauce mixture in the Instant Pot. Select the “Saute” function again and let the sauce thicken for a few minutes, stirring occasionally.

  9. Add the steamed broccoli to the Instant Pot and gently stir to combine. Let the dish simmer for another minute or two to allow the flavors to meld together.

  10. Serve the savory Instant Pot Beef and Broccoli over steamed rice or noodles. Garnish with sesame seeds and sliced green onions for an extra pop of flavor and visual appeal.

Now, sit back, relax, and enjoy this delicious and fuss-free dinner that you’ve effortlessly prepared using your trusty Instant Pot. This recipe is sure to become a family favorite, perfect for those busy weeknights when you crave a satisfying and tasty meal. Bon appétit!

Note: The cooking times and settings may vary depending on the model and size of your Instant Pot. Always refer to the manufacturer’s instructions for precise cooking guidelines.
