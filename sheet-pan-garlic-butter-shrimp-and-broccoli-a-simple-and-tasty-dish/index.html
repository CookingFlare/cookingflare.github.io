Sheet Pan Garlic Butter Shrimp and Broccoli: A Simple and Tasty Dish


Are you looking for a quick and delicious meal that requires minimal effort and clean-up? Look no further than Sheet Pan Garlic Butter Shrimp and Broccoli! This simple and tasty dish is perfect for busy weeknights when you want something flavorful and nutritious without spending hours in the kitchen.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound of shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 1 head of broccoli, cut into florets
  • 4 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 4 tablespoons of butter, melted
  • 2 tablespoons of olive oil
  • Juice of 1 lemon
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C) and line a baking sheet with parchment paper or aluminum foil for easy clean-up.

  2. In a small bowl, combine the melted butter, minced garlic, olive oil, lemon juice, salt, and pepper. Mix well to create a flavorful garlic butter sauce.

  3. Place the shrimp and broccoli florets on the prepared baking sheet. Drizzle the garlic butter sauce over the shrimp and broccoli, making sure everything is evenly coated.

  4. Gently toss the shrimp and broccoli to ensure they are well coated with the sauce. Spread them out in an even layer on the baking sheet, making sure they are not overcrowded.

  5. Place the baking sheet in the preheated oven and bake for about 15-20 minutes, or until the shrimp are pink and cooked through, and the broccoli is tender-crisp. Be careful not to overcook the shrimp, as they can become rubbery.

  6. Once cooked, remove the sheet pan from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes before serving. The garlic butter sauce will have caramelized slightly, adding an extra depth of flavor to the dish.

  7. Serve the Sheet Pan Garlic Butter Shrimp and Broccoli as a main course with a side of rice or quinoa, or as a light and flavorful appetizer. Garnish with freshly chopped parsley for a pop of color and freshness.

Tips and Variations

  • You can customize this dish by adding other vegetables such as bell peppers, cherry tomatoes, or zucchini. Just make sure to adjust the cooking time accordingly, as different vegetables may require different cooking times.

  • For a spicy twist, add a pinch of red pepper flakes to the garlic butter sauce before drizzling it over the shrimp and broccoli. This will give the dish a subtle kick of heat.

  • If you prefer a vegetarian version, you can substitute the shrimp with tofu or tempeh. Just make sure to adjust the cooking time accordingly, as vegetarian alternatives may require less time in the oven.

  • This dish is also great for meal prepping. You can easily double or triple the recipe and divide it into individual meal-prep containers for easy grab-and-go lunches or dinners throughout the week.

Sheet Pan Garlic Butter Shrimp and Broccoli is a versatile and flavorful dish that is sure to impress your family and friends. Whether you’re a seafood lover or simply looking for a delicious and hassle-free meal, this recipe ticks all the boxes. Give it a try and savor the delightful combination of juicy shrimp, tender-crisp broccoli, and the aromatic flavors of garlic butter. Enjoy!
