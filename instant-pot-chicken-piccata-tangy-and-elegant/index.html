Instant Pot Chicken Piccata: Tangy and Elegant


If you’re looking for a delicious and elegant dish that’s bursting with flavor, Instant Pot Chicken Piccata is the answer. This classic Italian dish features tender chicken breasts bathed in a tangy and buttery lemon sauce, creating a harmony of flavors that will impress your family and guests. The best part? It’s incredibly easy to make in your Instant Pot, saving you time and effort without compromising on taste.

Ingredients

  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • Salt and black pepper, to taste
  • 1/2 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup chicken broth
  • 1/2 cup freshly squeezed lemon juice
  • 1/4 cup capers, drained
  • 1/4 cup unsalted butter
  • Fresh parsley, chopped, for garnish

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Chicken: Season the chicken breasts with salt and black pepper. Dredge the chicken in flour, shaking off any excess.

  2. Sauté the Chicken: Select the “Sauté” function on your Instant Pot and heat the olive oil. Add the chicken breasts and cook for 2-3 minutes on each side, or until golden brown. Remove the chicken from the Instant Pot and set aside.

  3. Prepare the Sauce: In the same pot, add the minced garlic and sauté for about 30 seconds. Pour in the chicken broth and lemon juice, then use a wooden spoon to deglaze the bottom of the pot, scraping up any browned bits. Stir in the capers.

  4. Pressure Cook the Chicken: Return the chicken to the Instant Pot, ensuring it’s submerged in the sauce. Secure the lid and set the Instant Pot to “Manual” or “Pressure Cook” for 5 minutes. Once done, perform a quick pressure release.

  5. Finish the Dish: Carefully remove the chicken from the Instant Pot and transfer it to a serving platter. Stir the unsalted butter into the sauce in the pot, allowing it to melt and create a rich, velvety texture.

  6. Serve and Garnish: Pour the luscious lemon-caper sauce over the chicken and garnish with fresh chopped parsley. Serve the Instant Pot Chicken Piccata with your choice of pasta, rice, or steamed vegetables for a complete and satisfying meal.

Tips and Tricks

  • For an extra touch of elegance, you can slice the chicken breasts before serving and fan them out on the platter.
  • If you prefer a thicker sauce, you can mix 1 tablespoon of cornstarch with 2 tablespoons of water and stir it into the sauce after pressure cooking, then select the “Sauté” function to simmer and thicken the sauce.

Final Thoughts

Instant Pot Chicken Piccata is a versatile dish that can elevate a weeknight dinner or be the star of a special occasion. Its vibrant flavors and effortless preparation make it a go-to recipe for both seasoned cooks and kitchen beginners. The tangy and elegant combination of lemon, capers, and tender chicken is sure to delight your taste buds and leave you craving for more. So, fire up your Instant Pot and treat yourself to a restaurant-quality meal right in the comfort of your own home.
