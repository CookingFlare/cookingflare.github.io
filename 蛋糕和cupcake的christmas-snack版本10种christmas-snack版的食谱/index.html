蛋糕和cupcake的christmas snack版本：10种christmas snack版的食谱


Christmas is just around the corner, and the festive atmosphere is already palpable. As we gear up for the holiday season, our thoughts turn to what delicious treats we can whip up to share with loved ones. In this post, we’ll explore 10 Christmas snack recipes that blend the magic of cookies and cupcakes to create a truly unforgettable experience.

Recipe 1: Cranberry White Chocolate Chip Cookies

Combine the warmth of white chocolate chips with the tanginess of cranberries in these delightful cookies. Simply mix 2 cups of all-purpose flour, 1 teaspoon of baking soda, and 1 teaspoon of salt with 1 cup of unsalted butter, softened. Add 3/4 cup of white granulated sugar, 1/2 cup of brown sugar, and 2 large eggs, then stir in 1 cup of dried cranberries and 1 cup of white chocolate chips. Scoop onto a baking sheet and bake at 375°F for 12-15 minutes.

Recipe 2: Gingerbread Cupcakes with Cream Cheese Frosting

These moist cupcakes are infused with the warmth of ginger and spices, while the cream cheese frosting adds a tangy and creamy touch. Preheat your oven to 350°F and prepare a cupcake tin. Mix 1 1/2 cups of all-purpose flour, 1 teaspoon of baking powder, 1 teaspoon of ground cinnamon, 1/2 teaspoon of ground ginger, and 1/2 teaspoon of salt with 1/2 cup of unsalted butter, softened. Add 1 cup of granulated sugar, 2 large eggs, and 2 teaspoons of vanilla extract, then stir in 1 cup of milk. Divide the batter evenly among the cupcake liners and bake for 18-20 minutes. Allow to cool before frosting with a cream cheese frosting made from 8 ounces of cream cheese, softened, 1/2 cup of unsalted butter, softened, and 1 teaspoon of vanilla extract.

Recipe 3: Snickerdoodle Cookies with Cinnamon Sugar

These classic snickerdoodle cookies are elevated by the addition of cinnamon sugar, creating a sweet and spicy treat. Combine 2 1/4 cups of all-purpose flour, 1 teaspoon of baking soda, and 1 teaspoon of cream of tartar with 1 cup of unsalted butter, softened. Add 1 3/4 cups of white granulated sugar and 2 large eggs, then stir in 2 teaspoons of ground cinnamon and 1/2 teaspoon of salt. Scoop onto a baking sheet and bake at 375°F for 10-12 minutes. While still warm, toss the cookies in a mixture of 1/4 cup granulated sugar and 1 tablespoon of ground cinnamon.

Recipe 4: Peppermint Mocha Cupcakes with Chocolate Ganache

These decadent cupcakes combine the richness of chocolate with the freshness of peppermint, perfect for the holiday season. Preheat your oven to 350°F and prepare a cupcake tin. Mix 1 1/2 cups of all-purpose flour, 1 teaspoon of baking powder, 1 teaspoon of instant coffee powder, and 1/2 teaspoon of salt with 1/2 cup of unsalted butter, softened. Add 1 cup of granulated sugar, 2 large eggs, and 2 teaspoons of peppermint extract, then stir in 1 cup of milk. Divide the batter evenly among the cupcake liners and bake for 18-20 minutes. Allow to cool before topping with a chocolate ganache made from 1 cup of semisweet chocolate chips and 1 cup of heavy cream.

Recipe 5: Shortbread Cookies with Lemon Zest

These buttery shortbread cookies are elevated by the brightness of lemon zest, creating a refreshing and citrusy treat. Combine 2 1/4 cups of all-purpose flour, 1/2 cup of confectioners’ sugar, and 1/4 teaspoon of salt with 1/2 cup of unsalted butter, softened. Add 1 tablespoon of lemon zest and 1 tablespoon of lemon juice, then stir until a dough forms. Roll out the dough to 1/4 inch thickness and cut into desired shapes. Bake at 300°F for 18-20 minutes or until lightly golden.

Recipe 6: Eggnog Cheesecake Cupcakes with Caramel Sauce

These creamy cheesecake cupcakes are infused with the warmth of eggnog and topped with a rich caramel sauce, perfect for the holiday season. Preheat your oven to 350°F and prepare a cupcake tin. Mix 1 1/2 cups of all-purpose flour, 1 teaspoon of baking powder, and 1/2 teaspoon of salt with 1/2 cup of unsalted butter, softened. Add 1 cup of granulated sugar, 2 large eggs, and 2 teaspoons of eggnog, then stir in 1 cup of milk. Divide the batter evenly among the cupcake liners and bake for 18-20 minutes. Allow to cool before topping with a caramel sauce made from 1 cup of heavy cream, 1/2 cup of granulated sugar, and 1 tablespoon of unsalted butter.

Recipe 7: Chocolate Crinkle Cookies with Crushed Peppermint

These chewy cookies are elevated by the addition of crushed peppermint, creating a refreshing and chocolatey treat. Combine 2 1/4 cups of all-purpose flour, 1 teaspoon of baking soda, and 1 teaspoon of salt with 1 cup of unsalted butter, softened. Add 3/4 cup of granulated sugar, 1/2 cup of brown sugar, and 2 large eggs, then stir in 1 cup of semisweet chocolate chips and 1/2 cup of crushed peppermint candies. Scoop onto a baking sheet and bake at 375°F for 10-12 minutes.

Recipe 8: Gingerbread Men with Royal Icing

These classic gingerbread men are elevated by the addition of royal icing, creating a sweet and festive treat. Preheat your oven to 350°F and prepare a gingerbread man cookie cutter. Mix 2 cups of all-purpose flour, 1 teaspoon of baking soda, and 1 teaspoon of ground cinnamon with 1/2 cup of unsalted butter, softened. Add 3/4 cup of granulated sugar, 1/4 cup of brown sugar, and 2 large eggs, then stir in 1/4 cup of molasses. Scoop onto a baking sheet and bake for 10-12 minutes. Allow to cool before decorating with royal icing made from 1 cup of powdered sugar, 2 tablespoons of meringue powder, and 2 tablespoons of water.

Recipe 9: Peanut Butter Blossoms with Hershey’s Kisses

These classic peanut butter blossoms are elevated by the addition of Hershey’s Kisses, creating a sweet and indulgent treat. Combine 2 1/4 cups of all-purpose flour, 1 teaspoon of baking soda, and 1 teaspoon of salt with 1/2 cup of unsalted butter, softened. Add 1/2 cup of peanut butter, 1/4 cup of granulated sugar, and 1/4 cup of brown sugar, then stir in 1 egg. Scoop onto a baking sheet and bake at 375°F for 10-12 minutes. While still warm, press a Hershey’s Kiss into the center of each cookie.

Recipe 10: Chocolate Dipped Shortbread Cookies with Crushed Candy Canes

These buttery shortbread cookies are elevated by the addition of crushed candy canes and chocolate, creating a festive and indulgent treat. Combine 2 1/4 cups of all-purpose flour, 1/2 cup of confectioners’ sugar, and 1/4 teaspoon of salt with 1/2 cup of unsalted butter, softened. Add 1 tablespoon of crushed candy canes and 1 tablespoon of vanilla extract, then stir until a dough forms. Roll out the dough to 1/4 inch thickness and cut into desired shapes. Bake at 300°F for 18-20 minutes or until lightly golden. Allow to cool before dipping in melted chocolate and sprinkling with crushed candy canes.

These 10 Christmas snack recipes are sure to impress your friends and family with their unique flavor combinations and festive presentation. Whether you’re a cookie or cupcake enthusiast, there’s something for everyone in this collection. So go ahead, get baking, and make this holiday season one to remember!
