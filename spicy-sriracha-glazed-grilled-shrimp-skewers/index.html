Spicy Sriracha Glazed Grilled Shrimp Skewers


If you’re looking to spice up your grilling game, look no further than these Spicy Sriracha Glazed Grilled Shrimp Skewers. Perfectly marinated shrimp, infused with the fiery kick of Sriracha, come together beautifully on the grill for a dish that is sure to impress your guests at your next barbecue. In this post, we’ll explore not just the recipe, but also tips on how to select the best shrimp, the art of grilling, and ideas for serving these delectable skewers.

Why Shrimp is the Perfect Grill Choice

Shrimp is a fantastic choice for grilling thanks to its quick cooking time and ability to absorb flavors from marinades. Not only are shrimp low in calories and high in protein, but they also cook in a matter of minutes, making them ideal for those impromptu gatherings or busy weeknight dinners. The delicate texture of shrimp pairs perfectly with bold flavors, making them an excellent canvas for the spicy Sriracha glaze.

Selecting the Best Shrimp

When it comes to buying shrimp, freshness is key. Here are some tips to ensure you select the best:

  1. Type of Shrimp: You can choose between wild-caught and farm-raised shrimp. Wild-caught shrimp often have a more robust flavor, while farm-raised shrimp can be more readily available and sometimes more affordable.

  2. Size Matters: For skewers, medium to large shrimp (21/25 count per pound) work best. They hold up well to grilling and provide a satisfying bite.

  3. Shell-On vs. Shell-Off: While shell-on shrimp offer a richer flavor, shell-off shrimp are easier to eat. For this recipe, we recommend using peeled and deveined shrimp for convenience.

  4. Frozen vs. Fresh: If you can’t find fresh shrimp, frozen shrimp is a great alternative. Just ensure they are properly thawed by placing them in the refrigerator overnight or running them under cold water for a quicker thaw.

Ingredients

Here’s what you’ll need for the Spicy Sriracha Glazed Grilled Shrimp Skewers:

For the Skewers:

  • 1 pound of large shrimp (peeled and deveined)
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons lime juice (freshly squeezed)
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon onion powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Bamboo or metal skewers (soaked in water if using bamboo)

For the Sriracha Glaze:

  • ¼ cup Sriracha sauce
  • 2 tablespoons honey (or agave syrup)
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce (low sodium preferred)
  • 1 tablespoon rice vinegar
  • 1 teaspoon sesame oil
  • 1 clove garlic (minced)
  • 1 teaspoon fresh ginger (grated)
  • Optional: 1 tablespoon sesame seeds for garnish
  • Optional: Chopped cilantro for garnish

Marinade Time

To get the maximum flavor from your shrimp, marinate them ahead of time. Here’s how to do it:

  1. Prepare the Marinade: In a medium bowl, whisk together olive oil, lime juice, garlic powder, onion powder, salt, and pepper. Add the shrimp to the bowl and toss until they are well coated.

  2. Marinate: Cover the bowl with plastic wrap and let the shrimp marinate in the refrigerator for at least 30 minutes. For best results, marinate for 1-2 hours.

  3. Prepare the Sriracha Glaze: In a separate bowl, combine Sriracha, honey, soy sauce, rice vinegar, sesame oil, minced garlic, and grated ginger. Mix well and set aside.

Skewering the Shrimp

Once the shrimp have marinated, it’s time to prepare them for grilling:

  1. Skewer the Shrimp: Thread the marinated shrimp onto the skewers, leaving a little space between each shrimp to allow for even cooking. If you’re using bamboo skewers, ensure they have been soaked in water for at least 30 minutes to prevent burning.

  2. Brush with Glaze: Before placing the skewers on the grill, brush the shrimp lightly with the Sriracha glaze. Reserve some glaze for basting during cooking.

Grilling the Shrimp

Now comes the exciting part: grilling! Follow these steps for perfectly grilled shrimp:

  1. Preheat the Grill: Heat your grill to medium-high (about 400°F to 450°F). If using a charcoal grill, ensure the coals are evenly distributed.

  2. Oil the Grill Grates: To prevent sticking, use a paper towel dipped in oil to lightly coat the grill grates.

  3. Grill the Skewers: Place the shrimp skewers on the grill. Cook for 2-3 minutes on one side, then brush with more Sriracha glaze before flipping them over. Grill for an additional 2-3 minutes on the other side until the shrimp are opaque and have grill marks.

  4. Watch Closely: Shrimp cook quickly, so keep a close eye to avoid overcooking, which can lead to a rubbery texture.

Serving Suggestions

Once your Spicy Sriracha Glazed Grilled Shrimp Skewers are done, it’s time to serve them up! Here are a few ideas to elevate your dish:

  1. Accompaniments: Serve the skewers with a side of coconut rice or jasmine rice to balance out the heat. A simple salad with a citrus vinaigrette also complements the dish well.

  2. Toppings: Sprinkle with sesame seeds and chopped cilantro for added flavor and presentation.

  3. Dipping Sauce: Offer a side of extra Sriracha glaze for dipping or a cooling yogurt sauce made with Greek yogurt, lemon juice, and fresh herbs.

  4. Beverage Pairing: Pair your skewers with a cold beer, a zesty mojito, or a refreshing iced tea to wash down the spicy kick.

Variations to Try

Feel free to get creative with this recipe! Here are some delicious variations:

  1. Vegetable Additions: Alternate shrimp with colorful bell peppers, cherry tomatoes, or zucchini on the skewers for added flavor and nutrition.

  2. Different Heat Levels: Adjust the amount of Sriracha to suit your taste. You can also mix in other spicy elements like cayenne pepper or chili flakes.

  3. Marinade Alternatives: Experiment with different marinades, such as a tequila-lime marinade or a coconut curry marinade, for a unique twist.

  4. Add Fruits: Grilled pineapple or mango can add a sweet touch that pairs beautifully with the spicy shrimp.

Final Thoughts

These Spicy Sriracha Glazed Grilled Shrimp Skewers are not only easy to make but also packed with flavor that will tantalize your taste buds. Whether you’re hosting a summer barbecue or just want to impress your family with a delicious weeknight meal, this recipe is sure to become a favorite. So fire up the grill, gather your ingredients, and enjoy the delightful combination of heat and sweetness that these shrimp skewers bring to the table! Happy grilling!
