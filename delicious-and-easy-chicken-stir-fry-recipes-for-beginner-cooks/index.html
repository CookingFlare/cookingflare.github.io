Delicious and Easy Chicken Stir-Fry Recipes for Beginner Cooks


Are you a beginner cook looking to impress your friends and family with a delicious and easy chicken stir-fry? Look no further! In this blog post, we will explore three mouthwatering chicken stir-fry recipes that are perfect for beginners. These recipes are not only simple to make but also bursting with flavor. So, grab your apron and let’s get cooking!

1. Classic Chicken Stir-Fry

Ingredients:

  • 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts, sliced into thin strips
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1 onion, thinly sliced
  • 1 bell pepper, thinly sliced
  • 1 carrot, julienned
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon oyster sauce
  • 1 teaspoon sesame oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Heat the vegetable oil in a large skillet or wok over medium-high heat.
  2. Add the chicken strips and cook until they are browned and cooked through. Remove from the skillet and set aside.
  3. In the same skillet, add the onion, bell pepper, carrot, and garlic. Cook until the vegetables are tender-crisp.
  4. Return the chicken to the skillet and add the soy sauce, oyster sauce, and sesame oil. Stir well to coat the chicken and vegetables.
  5. Cook for an additional 2-3 minutes, or until everything is heated through.
  6. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  7. Serve hot with steamed rice or noodles.

2. Sweet and Spicy Chicken Stir-Fry

Ingredients:

  • 2 boneless, skinless chicken thighs, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1 red onion, thinly sliced
  • 1 red bell pepper, thinly sliced
  • 1 cup pineapple chunks
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons honey
  • 1 tablespoon sriracha sauce
  • 1 tablespoon rice vinegar
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Heat the vegetable oil in a large skillet or wok over medium-high heat.
  2. Add the chicken pieces and cook until they are browned and cooked through. Remove from the skillet and set aside.
  3. In the same skillet, add the red onion, red bell pepper, and pineapple chunks. Cook until the vegetables are tender-crisp.
  4. Return the chicken to the skillet and add the soy sauce, honey, sriracha sauce, and rice vinegar. Stir well to coat the chicken and vegetables.
  5. Cook for an additional 2-3 minutes, or until everything is heated through.
  6. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  7. Serve hot with steamed rice or quinoa.

3. Garlic Ginger Chicken Stir-Fry

Ingredients:

  • 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts, sliced into thin strips
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon fresh ginger, grated
  • 1 broccoli head, cut into florets
  • 1 cup snap peas
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon hoisin sauce
  • 1 tablespoon cornstarch
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Heat the vegetable oil in a large skillet or wok over medium-high heat.
  2. Add the chicken strips and cook until they are browned and cooked through. Remove from the skillet and set aside.
  3. In the same skillet, add the garlic and ginger. Cook for about 1 minute, or until fragrant.
  4. Add the broccoli florets and snap peas to the skillet. Cook until the vegetables are tender-crisp.
  5. Return the chicken to the skillet and add the soy sauce, hoisin sauce, and cornstarch. Stir well to coat the chicken and vegetables.
  6. Cook for an additional 2-3 minutes, or until everything is heated through and the sauce has thickened.
  7. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  8. Serve hot with steamed rice or noodles.

There you have it - three delicious and easy chicken stir-fry recipes that even beginner cooks can master. These recipes are versatile, allowing you to customize them with your favorite vegetables or sauces. So, get creative in the kitchen and enjoy the flavorful wonders of chicken stir-fry!
