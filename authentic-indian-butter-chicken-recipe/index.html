Authentic Indian Butter Chicken Recipe


If there’s one dish that epitomizes the rich and flavorsome essence of Indian cuisine, it has to be the iconic Butter Chicken. This luxurious and creamy dish has won hearts around the globe with its velvety tomato-based sauce and tender chicken pieces infused with a myriad of spices. Today, we’ll dive into the secrets of creating an authentic Indian Butter Chicken right in your own kitchen.

Ingredients:

  • 1.5 lbs boneless chicken, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 1 cup plain yogurt
  • 1 tablespoon ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 teaspoon garam masala
  • 1 teaspoon chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric
  • 1 teaspoon cumin powder
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 tablespoons oil
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 1 cinnamon stick
  • 3-4 green cardamom pods
  • 1 cup tomato puree
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1 tablespoon kasuri methi (dried fenugreek leaves)
  • Fresh cilantro for garnish

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, mix the yogurt, ginger-garlic paste, garam masala, chili powder, turmeric, cumin powder, and salt. Add the chicken pieces to this marinade, ensuring they are well-coated. Let it marinate for at least 30 minutes.

  2. Heat oil in a pan and add the marinated chicken. Cook until the chicken is tender and cooked through. Set aside.

  3. In another pan, melt butter and add the cinnamon stick and cardamom pods. Sauté for a minute to release the flavors.

  4. Add the tomato puree and cook until the oil separates from the mixture.

  5. Pour in the heavy cream and stir well. Let it simmer for a few minutes.

  6. Add the cooked chicken to the sauce. Crush the kasuri methi between your palms and sprinkle it over the dish. Mix well.

  7. Allow the Butter Chicken to simmer for a few more minutes, allowing the flavors to meld together.

  8. Garnish with fresh cilantro before serving.

Serving Suggestions:

  • Butter Chicken pairs perfectly with steamed basmati rice or hot naan bread.
  • A side of cucumber raita or crispy papadums complements the dish beautifully.

Tips for Perfection:

  • For a smoky flavor, you can finish the dish with a quick fume of charcoal smoke.
  • Adjust the spice levels according to your preference.
  • Freshly prepared ginger-garlic paste enhances the dish’s aroma significantly.

Final Thoughts:

Indulge in the decadence of Authentic Indian Butter Chicken with this recipe that promises to transport your taste buds to the vibrant streets of India. Whether you’re a seasoned chef or a home cook eager to explore new flavors, this dish is sure to become a favorite at your dining table. Enjoy the creamy, buttery goodness of this classic Indian delicacy that celebrates the art of spices and flavors. Happy cooking!
