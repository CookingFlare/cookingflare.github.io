How to Prepare Mexican Sweet Potato Tacos

If you’re looking for a mouthwatering and wholesome dish that’s bursting with flavor, look no further than these Mexican Sweet Potato Tacos. This recipe combines the rich and earthy sweetness of sweet potatoes with a medley of vibrant Mexican spices, creating a dish that’s as satisfying to the taste buds as it is nourishing for the body. Ingredients For the Sweet Potato Filling: 2 large sweet potatoes, peeled and diced 1 tablespoon olive oil 1 teaspoon chili powder 1 teaspoon cumin 1/2 teaspoon paprika Salt and pepper to taste For the Black Bean Salsa: 1 can black beans, rinsed and drained 1/2 red onion, finely chopped 1 tomato, diced 1/4 cup fresh cilantro, chopped Juice of 1 lime Salt to taste For the Avocado Crema: 1 ripe avocado 1/4 cup Greek yogurt 1 clove garlic, minced Juice of 1 lime Salt and pepper to taste Additional Ingredients: 8 small corn tortillas Shredded lettuce, for serving Crumbled queso fresco, for serving (optional) Instructions Step 1: Prepare the Sweet Potato Filling Preheat the oven to 400°F (200°C). In a large bowl, toss the diced sweet potatoes with olive oil, chili powder, cumin, paprika, salt, and pepper until evenly coated. Spread the seasoned sweet potatoes in a single layer on a baking sheet and roast for 25-30 minutes, or until tender and slightly caramelized. Step 2: Make the Black Bean Salsa In a medium bowl, combine the black beans, red onion, tomato, cilantro, lime juice, and salt. Mix well and set aside. Step 3: Prepare the Avocado Crema In a blender or food processor, combine the avocado, Greek yogurt, garlic, lime juice, salt, and pepper. Blend until smooth and creamy. If the mixture is too thick, add a splash of water to achieve the desired consistency. Step 4: Assemble the Tacos Warm the corn tortillas in a dry skillet over medium heat until soft and pliable. Spoon a generous portion of the roasted sweet potato filling onto each tortilla. Top the sweet potatoes with the black bean salsa, shredded lettuce, and a drizzle of the avocado crema. If desired, sprinkle crumbled queso fresco over the assembled tacos for an extra touch of creaminess. Serving Suggestions These Mexican Sweet Potato Tacos are incredibly versatile and can be customized to suit your taste. You can add a kick of heat with some sliced jalapeños, or include a refreshing crunch with diced radishes. Serve the tacos with a side of Mexican rice, or a simple green salad for a complete and satisfying meal. ...
