Rice and Chicken Recipes for Easy Family Meals


Rice and chicken are two of the most versatile and budget-friendly ingredients that can be turned into a wide variety of delicious meals. Whether you’re cooking for a large family, planning a weeknight dinner, or looking for ways to incorporate healthy ingredients into your meals, rice and chicken are the perfect pairing. In this post, we’ll explore a range of rice and chicken recipes that are simple to make, full of flavor, and guaranteed to satisfy even the pickiest eaters in your home.

1. Classic Chicken and Rice Casserole

One of the easiest ways to bring rice and chicken together is by making a comforting chicken and rice casserole. This dish combines tender chicken, creamy sauce, and fluffy rice in one pan, making it an ideal option for busy weeknights or when you need to prepare a meal ahead of time.

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups of cooked rice (white or brown rice)
  • 2 chicken breasts, boneless and skinless
  • 1 can of cream of mushroom soup (or any preferred cream soup)
  • 1 cup of chicken broth
  • 1 medium onion, chopped
  • 1 cup of shredded cheddar cheese
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 tablespoon of olive oil
  • Fresh parsley for garnish (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. In a large skillet, heat olive oil over medium heat. Season the chicken breasts with salt and pepper and cook them in the skillet until golden brown and cooked through, about 6-7 minutes per side. Remove the chicken from the pan and set aside.
  3. In the same skillet, sauté the chopped onion until soft, about 3 minutes.
  4. In a large mixing bowl, combine the cooked rice, cream of mushroom soup, chicken broth, and sautéed onions. Stir until well combined.
  5. Cut the cooked chicken into bite-sized pieces and add it to the rice mixture.
  6. Transfer the mixture into a greased 9x13 baking dish. Top with shredded cheddar cheese.
  7. Bake the casserole in the preheated oven for 20-25 minutes, or until the cheese is melted and bubbly.
  8. Garnish with fresh parsley and serve.

This chicken and rice casserole is creamy, cheesy, and filling, making it a go-to dinner that’s easy to prepare and even easier to enjoy.

2. One-Pot Chicken and Rice

If you’re looking for a no-fuss, one-pot meal that’s quick and easy to prepare, a one-pot chicken and rice dish is the answer. This recipe combines tender chicken, savory rice, and vegetables all in one pot, making cleanup a breeze.

Ingredients:

  • 4 chicken thighs (bone-in, skin-on)
  • 1 cup of long-grain white rice
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 1 bell pepper, chopped
  • 1 garlic clove, minced
  • 2 cups of chicken broth
  • 1 teaspoon of paprika
  • 1 teaspoon of thyme
  • 1 tablespoon of olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a large skillet or Dutch oven, heat olive oil over medium heat. Season the chicken thighs with salt, pepper, paprika, and thyme.
  2. Brown the chicken thighs in the skillet for 4-5 minutes per side, until the skin is crispy and golden. Remove the chicken from the skillet and set it aside.
  3. In the same skillet, sauté the chopped onion, bell pepper, and minced garlic until softened, about 3 minutes.
  4. Add the rice to the skillet and stir it into the vegetable mixture for 1-2 minutes to lightly toast the rice.
  5. Pour in the chicken broth, scraping the bottom of the pan to release any flavorful bits stuck to the bottom.
  6. Return the chicken thighs to the skillet, skin-side up, and bring the mixture to a simmer.
  7. Cover the skillet, reduce the heat to low, and cook for 20-25 minutes, or until the rice is tender and the chicken is fully cooked.
  8. Serve the chicken and rice with a drizzle of the flavorful broth.

This one-pot dish is full of flavor and requires minimal effort. It’s a fantastic option for busy nights when you want a hearty meal with minimal cleanup.

3. Chicken and Rice Stir-Fry

For a quick and healthy dinner, chicken and rice stir-fry is a perfect choice. The combination of sautéed chicken, vegetables, and rice with a savory sauce makes for a delicious and satisfying meal that can be customized to your preferences.

Ingredients:

  • 2 chicken breasts, thinly sliced
  • 2 cups of cooked rice (preferably cold, leftover rice works best)
  • 1 carrot, julienned
  • 1 bell pepper, sliced
  • 1/2 cup of frozen peas
  • 2 tablespoons of soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon of oyster sauce
  • 1 teaspoon of sesame oil
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 2 tablespoons of vegetable oil
  • 1 egg (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Heat 1 tablespoon of vegetable oil in a large skillet or wok over medium-high heat.
  2. Add the sliced chicken to the skillet and cook until browned and cooked through, about 5-7 minutes. Remove the chicken from the skillet and set it aside.
  3. In the same skillet, add the remaining tablespoon of vegetable oil. Stir-fry the carrots, bell pepper, and peas for 3-4 minutes until they are tender but still crisp.
  4. Push the vegetables to one side of the skillet and crack the egg into the empty space. Scramble the egg and cook it through, then mix it with the vegetables.
  5. Add the cold, cooked rice to the skillet, breaking up any clumps. Stir-fry the rice for 2-3 minutes to heat it through and allow it to absorb the flavors of the pan.
  6. Return the cooked chicken to the skillet and add the soy sauce, oyster sauce, and sesame oil. Stir everything together to combine and cook for another 2 minutes.
  7. Serve the stir-fry immediately, garnished with sliced green onions if desired.

This stir-fry is a great way to use up leftover rice while incorporating a variety of colorful vegetables. It’s a quick and healthy meal that the whole family will enjoy.

4. Lemon Garlic Chicken with Rice

For a lighter and refreshing take on the classic chicken and rice combination, try making lemon garlic chicken with rice. The bright, zesty flavor of lemon pairs perfectly with the savory chicken and rice, creating a dish that feels fresh yet satisfying.

Ingredients:

  • 2 chicken breasts, boneless and skinless
  • 1 cup of long-grain white rice
  • 2 cups of chicken broth
  • 1 lemon (zested and juiced)
  • 3 garlic cloves, minced
  • 2 tablespoons of olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon of dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley for garnish

Instructions:

  1. In a medium saucepan, cook the rice in chicken broth according to the package instructions.
  2. While the rice is cooking, heat olive oil in a skillet over medium heat. Season the chicken breasts with salt, pepper, and oregano.
  3. Cook the chicken breasts for 6-7 minutes per side, until they are golden brown and fully cooked through.
  4. In a small bowl, mix the lemon juice, lemon zest, and minced garlic.
  5. Once the chicken is cooked, remove it from the skillet and set it aside. In the same skillet, pour the lemon-garlic mixture and cook for 2 minutes, allowing the sauce to thicken slightly.
  6. Serve the chicken over a bed of cooked rice, spooning the lemon-garlic sauce over the top. Garnish with fresh parsley.

This dish offers a light yet flavorful meal, with the freshness of lemon and garlic enhancing the chicken and rice combination.

5. Chicken Biryani

Biryani is a flavorful, spiced rice dish that originated in India and has become beloved worldwide. Chicken biryani is a hearty and aromatic dish that combines tender chicken, basmati rice, and a blend of spices, making it a perfect family meal for special occasions or when you want to treat yourself to something delicious.

Ingredients:

  • 2 chicken breasts or thighs, cut into pieces
  • 1 1/2 cups of basmati rice
  • 2 cups of chicken broth
  • 1 onion, thinly sliced
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 teaspoon of cumin
  • 1 teaspoon of coriander
  • 1 teaspoon of garam masala
  • 1/2 teaspoon of turmeric
  • 1/4 teaspoon of cinnamon
  • 1/4 teaspoon of cloves
  • 2 tablespoons of vegetable oil
  • 1/4 cup of yogurt
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh cilantro for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Rinse the basmati rice under cold water until the water runs clear. Set aside.
  2. Heat vegetable oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Add the sliced onion and sauté until golden brown, about 5 minutes. Remove half of the onions and set aside for garnish.
  3. Add the minced garlic and chopped tomatoes to the skillet. Cook for 3 minutes until the tomatoes soften.
  4. Stir in the chicken pieces and cook until browned on all sides, about 6 minutes.
  5. Add the cumin, coriander, garam masala, turmeric, cinnamon, and cloves. Stir well to coat the chicken and cook for another 2 minutes to release the spices’ flavors.
  6. Add the yogurt and mix it into the chicken and spice mixture. Pour in the chicken broth and bring the mixture to a simmer.
  7. Add the rinsed rice to the pot and stir gently. Cover the pot and reduce the heat to low. Cook for 15-20 minutes, or until the rice is tender and the chicken is fully cooked.
  8. Garnish with fresh cilantro and the reserved fried onions before serving.

Chicken biryani is a perfect dish to serve for family gatherings or when you want to enjoy a flavorful and hearty meal that is both comforting and exotic.

Conclusion

Rice and chicken are the foundation of so many delicious and easy meals that can be enjoyed by families of all sizes. From comforting casseroles to flavorful stir-fries and global-inspired dishes like biryani, the possibilities are endless when it comes to creating meals with these two ingredients. Try out these recipes for easy family meals that will make dinner time a little less stressful and a lot more delicious. Whether you’re cooking for a crowd or just preparing a quick dinner for yourself, rice and chicken will always come to the rescue with flavor, versatility, and ease.
