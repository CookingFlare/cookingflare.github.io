Greek Stuffed Bell Peppers: A Colorful and Flavorful Dish


If you’re looking for a vibrant and delicious dish to impress your friends and family, Greek Stuffed Bell Peppers are the perfect choice. This Mediterranean-inspired recipe combines the freshness of bell peppers with a flavorful filling, creating a wholesome and visually stunning meal. Whether you’re a seasoned chef or a novice cook, this recipe is sure to elevate your culinary skills and delight your taste buds.

Ingredients

To create these Greek Stuffed Bell Peppers, you’ll need the following ingredients:

  • 6 large bell peppers, any color
  • 1 cup of uncooked rice
  • 1 pound of ground lamb or beef
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 can (14 ounces) of diced tomatoes
  • 1/2 cup of crumbled feta cheese
  • 1/4 cup of chopped fresh parsley
  • 1/4 cup of chopped fresh mint
  • 1 teaspoon of dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Olive oil for cooking

Instructions

Step 1: Prepare the Bell Peppers

  • Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
  • Cut the tops off the bell peppers and remove the seeds and membranes from the insides.
  • Place the hollowed-out bell peppers in a baking dish, standing upright.

Step 2: Cook the Filling

  • Cook the rice according to the package instructions and set it aside.
  • In a large skillet, heat a drizzle of olive oil over medium heat.
  • Add the chopped onion and minced garlic, and sauté until softened and fragrant.
  • Add the ground lamb or beef to the skillet, breaking it up with a spoon, and cook until browned.
  • Stir in the diced tomatoes, cooked rice, crumbled feta cheese, fresh parsley, fresh mint, and dried oregano.
  • Season the filling with salt and pepper to taste and cook for a few more minutes to let the flavors meld together.

Step 3: Fill the Bell Peppers

  • Spoon the filling mixture into the hollowed-out bell peppers, pressing down gently to pack the filling in.
  • Place the pepper tops back on each filled bell pepper.

Step 4: Bake and Serve

  • Drizzle a bit of olive oil over the stuffed peppers to help them brown in the oven.
  • Cover the baking dish with foil and bake the stuffed peppers for 30 minutes.
  • Remove the foil and continue baking for an additional 15-20 minutes, or until the peppers are tender and the filling is heated through.
  • Once done, remove the stuffed bell peppers from the oven and let them cool for a few minutes before serving.

Serving Suggestions

These Greek Stuffed Bell Peppers are delicious on their own, but you can elevate the meal by serving them with a side of tzatziki sauce, a fresh Greek salad, or some crusty bread. The vibrant colors and flavors of this dish make it an ideal centerpiece for a summer dinner party or a cozy family meal.

Final Thoughts

Greek Stuffed Bell Peppers are a delightful combination of wholesome ingredients and Mediterranean flavors. The tender bell peppers, savory filling, and aromatic herbs come together to create a dish that’s as visually appealing as it is delicious. Whether you’re a fan of Greek cuisine or simply enjoy exploring new flavors, this recipe is a must-try. So, gather your ingredients, roll up your sleeves, and embark on a culinary journey to the sunny shores of Greece with this delightful and colorful dish.
