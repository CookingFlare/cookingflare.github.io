Quick and Easy Beef and Broccoli Recipe


If you’re looking for a delicious and quick meal that’s packed with flavor, then look no further than this amazing beef and broccoli recipe. This dish is a classic Chinese takeout favorite, but you can easily recreate it at home in just a few simple steps. The combination of tender beef, crisp broccoli, and savory sauce is sure to be a hit with your family and friends. Plus, the best part is that it can be ready in no time, making it perfect for busy weeknights or lazy weekends.

Ingredients

To make this quick and easy beef and broccoli recipe, you’ll need the following ingredients:

  • 1 pound flank steak, thinly sliced
  • 1/2 cup low-sodium soy sauce
  • 1/4 cup brown sugar
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon fresh ginger, grated
  • 1/2 cup beef broth
  • 2 tablespoons cornstarch
  • 3 cups broccoli florets
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • Cooked rice, for serving

Instructions

Step 1: Marinate the Beef

Start by marinating the thinly sliced flank steak in a mixture of low-sodium soy sauce, brown sugar, minced garlic, and grated ginger. Let the beef marinate for at least 30 minutes to allow the flavors to infuse.

Step 2: Prepare the Sauce

While the beef is marinating, you can prepare the sauce. In a small bowl, whisk together the beef broth and cornstarch until smooth. This will help thicken the sauce and give it a delicious, glossy finish.

Step 3: Cook the Beef

In a large skillet or wok, heat the vegetable oil over medium-high heat. Once the oil is hot, add the marinated beef slices and cook until browned and cooked through, about 2-3 minutes per side. Remove the beef from the skillet and set it aside.

Step 4: Cook the Broccoli

In the same skillet, add the broccoli florets and stir-fry for 2-3 minutes, or until they are bright green and slightly tender.

Step 5: Combine and Serve

Return the cooked beef to the skillet with the broccoli. Pour in the prepared sauce and stir everything together. Cook for an additional 1-2 minutes, allowing the sauce to thicken and coat the beef and broccoli.

Step 6: Serve

Once the beef and broccoli are coated in the savory sauce, remove the skillet from the heat. Serve the beef and broccoli over a bed of cooked rice for a complete and satisfying meal.

Tips and Variations

  • Feel free to add some extra heat to this dish by tossing in a pinch of red pepper flakes or a drizzle of sriracha.
  • For a healthier twist, you can use low-sodium soy sauce and brown rice instead of white rice.
  • If you’re vegetarian, you can easily substitute the beef with tofu or seitan for a plant-based version of this classic dish.

Conclusion

In just a few simple steps, you can create a flavorful and satisfying beef and broccoli dish that’s perfect for any occasion. Whether you’re looking for a quick weeknight meal or a crowd-pleasing dinner for guests, this recipe is sure to become a favorite in your cooking repertoire. The tender beef, crisp broccoli, and savory sauce come together to create a dish that’s as delicious as it is easy to make. So, next time you’re craving Chinese takeout, why not try making it at home with this quick and easy beef and broccoli recipe? Your taste buds will thank you!
