Irresistible BBQ Ribs Recipes for Your Next Cookout


Is there anything better than sinking your teeth into a perfectly cooked, juicy rack of BBQ ribs? If you’re like me, the answer is a resounding no! Ribs are a staple at cookouts and backyard gatherings, and for good reason. They are flavorful, tender, and oh-so-satisfying. Whether you prefer them sticky and sweet or spicy and smoky, I’ve got you covered with three irresistible BBQ ribs recipes that will take your cookout to the next level.

1. Classic Smoked BBQ Ribs

Ingredients:

  • 2 racks of baby back ribs
  • 1/4 cup brown sugar
  • 2 tablespoons paprika
  • 1 tablespoon chili powder
  • 1 tablespoon garlic powder
  • 1 tablespoon onion powder
  • 1 tablespoon salt
  • 1 tablespoon black pepper
  • BBQ sauce of your choice

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your smoker to 225°F (107°C) and prepare it for indirect cooking.
  2. In a small bowl, mix together the brown sugar, paprika, chili powder, garlic powder, onion powder, salt, and black pepper to create a dry rub.
  3. Rub the dry rub all over the ribs, ensuring they are evenly coated.
  4. Place the ribs on the smoker, bone side down, and smoke for approximately 4-5 hours or until the meat is tender and the internal temperature reaches 190°F (88°C).
  5. Brush the ribs with your favorite BBQ sauce during the last 30 minutes of cooking.
  6. Remove the ribs from the smoker and let them rest for 10 minutes before slicing and serving.

2. Honey Sriracha Glazed Ribs

Ingredients:

  • 2 racks of St. Louis-style ribs
  • 1/4 cup honey
  • 2 tablespoons sriracha sauce
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons rice vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon minced garlic
  • 1 tablespoon minced ginger
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your grill to medium-high heat.
  2. Season the ribs with salt and pepper.
  3. In a small saucepan, combine the honey, sriracha sauce, soy sauce, rice vinegar, minced garlic, and minced ginger. Heat over medium heat until the sauce is well combined and slightly reduced.
  4. Place the ribs on the grill, bone side down, and cook for approximately 1 hour or until the meat starts to pull away from the bones.
  5. Brush the ribs with the honey sriracha glaze during the last 15 minutes of cooking, turning and brushing every few minutes.
  6. Remove the ribs from the grill and let them rest for 10 minutes before slicing and serving.

3. Bourbon Maple Glazed Ribs

Ingredients:

  • 2 racks of spare ribs
  • 1/2 cup maple syrup
  • 1/4 cup bourbon
  • 2 tablespoons Dijon mustard
  • 2 tablespoons apple cider vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 teaspoon smoked paprika
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 275°F (135°C).
  2. Season the ribs with salt, pepper, smoked paprika, and garlic powder.
  3. Place the ribs on a baking sheet and cover tightly with aluminum foil.
  4. Bake the ribs in the oven for approximately 2-3 hours or until they are tender.
  5. In a small saucepan, combine the maple syrup, bourbon, Dijon mustard, apple cider vinegar, and Worcestershire sauce. Heat over medium heat until the glaze is thickened and reduced.
  6. Remove the ribs from the oven and brush them generously with the bourbon maple glaze.
  7. Preheat your grill to medium-high heat and grill the ribs for 5-7 minutes on each side, brushing with more glaze as desired.
  8. Remove the ribs from the grill and let them rest for 10 minutes before slicing and serving.

Conclusion

There you have it – three irresistible BBQ ribs recipes that will make your next cookout a memorable one. Whether you prefer the classic smoky flavor, a sweet and spicy kick, or a touch of bourbon-infused goodness, these recipes have got you covered. So fire up the grill or smoker, gather your friends and family, and get ready to indulge in some finger-licking goodness. Happy cooking!
