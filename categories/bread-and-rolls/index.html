Caramel Pecan Sticky Buns: A Sweet Indulgence

There’s something truly magical about the aroma of freshly baked goods wafting through the kitchen, especially when it involves the irresistible combination of caramel, pecans, and sticky buns. If you’re looking to treat yourself and your loved ones to a decadent sweet delight, then look no further than these heavenly Caramel Pecan Sticky Buns. Ingredients: 1 cup warm milk 2 1/4 tsp active dry yeast 1/2 cup granulated sugar 2 large eggs 1/3 cup unsalted butter, softened 4 1/2 cups all-purpose flour 1 tsp salt 1 cup packed brown sugar 1/2 cup unsalted butter 1/4 cup corn syrup 1 1/2 cups chopped pecans Instructions: Activate the Yeast: In a bowl, combine warm milk and yeast. Let it sit for about 5 minutes until foamy. Prepare the Dough: In a separate bowl, mix sugar, eggs, and softened butter. Add in the yeast mixture. Gradually mix in flour and salt until a dough forms. Knead the dough for about 5-7 minutes until smooth. First Rise: Place the dough in a greased bowl, cover with a damp towel, and let it rise in a warm place for about 1-1.5 hours until doubled in size. Make the Caramel Sauce: In a saucepan, melt brown sugar, butter, and corn syrup until smooth. Pour the caramel into the bottom of a greased baking pan and sprinkle with chopped pecans. Assemble the Buns: Roll out the dough into a rectangle. Spread softened butter over the dough and sprinkle with a mixture of brown sugar and cinnamon. Roll and Cut: Roll up the dough tightly and slice into buns. Place the buns over the caramel-pecan mixture in the baking pan. Second Rise: Cover the buns and let them rise for another 30-45 minutes. Bake: Preheat the oven and bake the buns for 25-30 minutes until golden brown. Enjoy: Once baked, invert the sticky buns onto a serving platter and relish the gooey caramel, crunchy pecans, and fluffy buns.
