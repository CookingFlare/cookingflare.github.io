Savory Chicken Flautas: A Crunchy Mexican Delight


If you’re a fan of Mexican cuisine, then you’re in for a treat with this delicious recipe for Savory Chicken Flautas. These crispy, golden-brown delights are packed with flavor and are sure to become a family favorite. Whether you’re hosting a party or simply craving a flavorful snack, these flautas are the perfect choice. So, let’s dive into the recipe and discover how to create this crunchy Mexican delight in your own kitchen.

Ingredients

For the Filling:

  • 2 cups cooked and shredded chicken
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • 1 teaspoon chili powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon paprika
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil

For the Assembly:

  • 12-14 corn tortillas
  • Vegetable oil for frying
  • Toothpicks

For Serving:

  • Shredded lettuce
  • Diced tomatoes
  • Sour cream
  • Guacamole
  • Salsa

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Filling:

    • In a skillet, heat the vegetable oil over medium heat. Add the chopped onion and minced garlic, and sauté until the onion is translucent.
    • Add the shredded chicken, cumin, chili powder, paprika, salt, and pepper to the skillet. Stir well to combine all the ingredients. Cook for 5-7 minutes, allowing the flavors to meld together. Once done, remove the filling from the heat and set it aside.

  2. Assemble the Flautas:

    • Warm the corn tortillas in a dry skillet for a few seconds on each side to make them pliable.
    • Place a spoonful of the chicken filling on one end of each tortilla, then roll it tightly to form a cigar shape. Secure the seam with a toothpick. Repeat this process for all the tortillas.

  3. Fry the Flautas:

    • In a large skillet, heat about half an inch of vegetable oil over medium-high heat.
    • Carefully place the rolled flautas, seam side down, into the hot oil. Fry them in batches until they are golden and crispy, turning them occasionally to ensure even cooking. Once done, transfer the flautas to a plate lined with paper towels to drain any excess oil.

  4. Serve and Enjoy:

    • Once all the flautas are fried and ready, it’s time to serve them. Remove the toothpicks from each flauta and arrange them on a platter.
    • Serve with shredded lettuce, diced tomatoes, sour cream, guacamole, and salsa for dipping. These toppings add a wonderful freshness and creaminess that perfectly complement the crunchy flautas.

Tips and Variations

  • To make this recipe vegetarian, substitute the chicken with sautéed vegetables or black beans.
  • If you prefer a spicier kick, add some chopped jalapeños or a dash of hot sauce to the filling mixture.
  • For a healthier alternative, you can bake the flautas in a preheated oven at 400°F for 20-25 minutes, turning them halfway through the cooking time.

Savory Chicken Flautas are a fantastic appetizer, snack, or even a main course. Their crispy exterior and flavorful filling make them a crowd-pleaser at any gathering. You can also customize the toppings and fillings to suit your taste preferences, making this recipe incredibly versatile. So, roll up your sleeves and get ready to enjoy the delightful crunch of these Mexican-inspired flautas!
