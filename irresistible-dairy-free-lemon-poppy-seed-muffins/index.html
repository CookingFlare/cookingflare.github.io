Irresistible Dairy-Free Lemon Poppy Seed Muffins

3 min · 517 words

Who doesn’t love a delightful, moist muffin bursting with the zesty flavor of lemon and the delightful crunch of poppy seeds? If you’re a fan of citrusy treats and are looking for a dairy-free option, you’re in for a treat. These Irresistible Dairy-Free Lemon Poppy Seed Muffins are the perfect blend of tangy and sweet, and they’re incredibly easy to make. Whether you’re hosting a brunch, packing a lunchbox treat, or simply craving a homemade baked good, these muffins are sure to impress.

Ingredients

Dry Ingredients:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 tablespoon poppy seeds
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt

Wet Ingredients:

  • 3/4 cup unsweetened almond milk (or any dairy-free milk of your choice)
  • 1/2 cup vegetable oil
  • 1/4 cup fresh lemon juice
  • Zest of 2 lemons
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Glaze (optional):

  • 1/2 cup powdered sugar
  • 2-3 tablespoons fresh lemon juice

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C) and line a muffin tin with paper liners or grease it with a bit of oil to prevent sticking.

  2. In a large mixing bowl, whisk together the dry ingredients: flour, sugar, poppy seeds, baking powder, baking soda, and salt.

  3. In a separate bowl, combine the wet ingredients: almond milk, vegetable oil, lemon juice, lemon zest, and vanilla extract. Mix until well combined.

  4. Pour the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients and gently fold them together until just combined. Be careful not to overmix, as this can result in dense muffins.

  5. Spoon the batter into the prepared muffin tin, filling each cup about two-thirds full.

  6. Place the muffin tin in the preheated oven and bake for 18-20 minutes, or until the muffins are golden brown and a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.

  7. While the muffins are baking, you can prepare the optional glaze. In a small bowl, whisk together the powdered sugar and lemon juice until smooth. Adjust the consistency by adding more sugar or lemon juice as needed.

  8. Once the muffins are done, remove them from the oven and allow them to cool in the tin for a few minutes before transferring them to a wire rack to cool completely.

  9. If desired, drizzle the glaze over the cooled muffins for an extra burst of lemony sweetness.

Serving and Storing

These Dairy-Free Lemon Poppy Seed Muffins are best enjoyed fresh, but they can be stored in an airtight container at room temperature for up to 3 days. You can also freeze the muffins for up to 3 months; simply thaw them at room temperature or warm them in the microwave before serving.

Serve these muffins alongside a cup of hot tea or coffee for a delightful breakfast or snack. Their bright, citrusy flavor makes them a perfect addition to any spring or summer gathering, and the fact that they’re dairy-free means they can be enjoyed by those with dietary restrictions or preferences.

Final Thoughts

Baking dairy-free treats doesn’t mean sacrificing flavor or texture, and these Irresistible Dairy-Free Lemon Poppy Seed Muffins are a testament to that. Their moist crumb, vibrant lemon flavor, and delightful crunch from the poppy seeds make them a must-try for any baking enthusiast, whether they follow a dairy-free diet or not. So, grab your ingredients and get ready to fill your kitchen with the tantalizing aroma of freshly baked muffins.

Enjoy the process, savor the results, and share the joy of these delightful muffins with your loved ones. Happy baking!
