Irresistible Peach Cobbler Pancakes


Are you ready to take your pancake game to a whole new level? Look no further than these irresistible Peach Cobbler Pancakes! This delightful twist on a classic breakfast favorite will have your taste buds singing with joy. With the perfect balance of fluffy pancakes and sweet, juicy peaches, this recipe is sure to become a family favorite. So grab your apron and let’s get cooking!

Ingredients

For the Pancakes:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons granulated sugar
  • 1 tablespoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 3/4 cups milk
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1/4 cup unsalted butter, melted
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

For the Peach Cobbler Topping:

  • 4 cups fresh peaches, peeled and sliced
  • 1/2 cup brown sugar
  • 1/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon cinnamon
  • 1/4 teaspoon nutmeg
  • 1/4 cup unsalted butter, melted

Instructions

  1. In a large mixing bowl, whisk together the flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt. Set aside.

  2. In a separate bowl, whisk together the milk, eggs, melted butter, and vanilla extract. Pour the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients and stir until just combined. Be careful not to overmix; a few lumps are okay.

  3. Preheat a non-stick skillet or griddle over medium heat. Lightly grease with butter or cooking spray.

  4. Pour 1/4 cup of batter onto the skillet for each pancake. Cook until bubbles form on the surface, then flip and cook for another 1-2 minutes, or until golden brown. Repeat with the remaining batter.

  5. While the pancakes are cooking, prepare the peach cobbler topping. In a medium bowl, combine the sliced peaches, brown sugar, flour, cinnamon, nutmeg, and melted butter. Stir until the peaches are evenly coated.

  6. Heat a large skillet over medium heat. Add the peach mixture and cook for 5-7 minutes, or until the peaches are tender and the sauce has thickened slightly.

  7. To serve, stack the pancakes on a plate and spoon the warm peach cobbler topping over the top. You can also add a dollop of whipped cream or a sprinkle of powdered sugar for extra indulgence.

  8. Enjoy your irresistible Peach Cobbler Pancakes!

Tips and Variations

  • If fresh peaches are not available, you can use frozen peaches. Just make sure to thaw and drain them before using.
  • For an extra touch of sweetness, drizzle maple syrup over the pancakes before adding the peach cobbler topping.
  • Feel free to experiment with other fruits such as apples or berries in the cobbler topping for a different flavor experience.
  • These pancakes can be made in advance and stored in an airtight container in the refrigerator. To reheat, simply pop them in the toaster or microwave until warm.

Now that you have the recipe for these irresistible Peach Cobbler Pancakes, it’s time to gather your ingredients and start flipping those pancakes! Whether you’re enjoying them for breakfast, brunch, or even dessert, these pancakes are sure to impress. So go ahead, indulge in the sweet and comforting flavors of a classic peach cobbler in pancake form. Your taste buds will thank you!
