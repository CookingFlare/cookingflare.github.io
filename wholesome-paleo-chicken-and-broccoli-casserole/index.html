Wholesome Paleo Chicken and Broccoli Casserole


There’s something incredibly comforting about a warm, hearty casserole that brings everyone to the table. Today, we’re diving into a delightful recipe for a wholesome Paleo Chicken and Broccoli Casserole. This dish is not only bursting with flavor but also adheres to the principles of the Paleo diet, making it a perfect choice for those looking to eat clean and stay healthy.

The combination of tender chicken, fresh broccoli, and creamy sauce creates a satisfying meal that’s sure to become a family favorite. Let’s get cooking!

Ingredients

Before we jump into the step-by-step instructions, let’s gather all the ingredients you’ll need for this delicious casserole:

  • Chicken:
    • 2 pounds of boneless, skinless chicken breasts
    • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • Vegetables:
    • 4 cups of fresh broccoli florets
    • 1 large onion, finely chopped
    • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • Sauce:
    • 1 cup of full-fat coconut milk
    • 1 cup of chicken broth
    • 1 tablespoon of Dijon mustard
    • 1 tablespoon of nutritional yeast (optional, for a cheesy flavor)
    • 1 teaspoon of paprika
    • 1 teaspoon of dried thyme
    • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • Topping:
    • 1/2 cup of almond flour
    • 1/4 cup of shredded coconut (unsweetened)
    • 2 tablespoons of olive oil or melted coconut oil
    • Fresh parsley, chopped (for garnish)

Preparation

Step 1: Preheat Your Oven

Begin by preheating your oven to 375°F (190°C). This will ensure it’s ready to go once your casserole is assembled.

Step 2: Cook the Chicken

Season the chicken breasts with salt and pepper on both sides. Heat a large skillet over medium-high heat and add a drizzle of olive oil or coconut oil. Once hot, add the chicken breasts and cook until golden brown on both sides and fully cooked through, about 5-7 minutes per side. Remove the chicken from the skillet and set aside to cool.

Step 3: Prepare the Vegetables

In the same skillet, add the chopped onion and cook until translucent, about 3-4 minutes. Add the minced garlic and cook for another minute until fragrant. Steam the broccoli florets until they are just tender but still bright green, about 4-5 minutes. Set aside.

Step 4: Assemble the Sauce

In a medium-sized mixing bowl, combine the coconut milk, chicken broth, Dijon mustard, nutritional yeast (if using), paprika, dried thyme, salt, and pepper. Whisk until well combined.

Step 5: Shred the Chicken

Once the chicken has cooled enough to handle, shred it into bite-sized pieces using two forks.

Step 6: Combine Everything Together

In a large mixing bowl, combine the shredded chicken, sautéed onions and garlic, steamed broccoli, and the prepared sauce. Mix until everything is well coated with the sauce.

Step 7: Transfer to Baking Dish

Transfer the mixture to a greased 9x13 inch baking dish, spreading it out evenly.

Step 8: Prepare the Topping

In a small mixing bowl, combine the almond flour, shredded coconut, and olive oil or melted coconut oil. Mix until the topping resembles coarse crumbs. Sprinkle this evenly over the top of the casserole.

Step 9: Bake

Place the casserole in the preheated oven and bake for 25-30 minutes, or until the top is golden brown and the filling is bubbly.

Step 10: Serve

Remove the casserole from the oven and let it sit for about 5 minutes before serving. Garnish with freshly chopped parsley for a pop of color and added freshness.

Tips and Variations

  • Vegetable Substitutions: Feel free to substitute or add other veggies like cauliflower, spinach, or bell peppers to the casserole.
  • Spice It Up: For a bit of heat, add a pinch of cayenne pepper or red pepper flakes to the sauce.
  • Creamy Texture: If you prefer a thicker sauce, you can add a tablespoon of arrowroot powder or tapioca starch to the sauce mixture before combining it with the chicken and vegetables.
  • Storage: This casserole stores well in the refrigerator for up to 4 days. Simply reheat individual portions in the microwave or oven.

Nutritional Benefits

This Paleo Chicken and Broccoli Casserole is not only delicious but also packed with nutrients. Chicken is an excellent source of lean protein, while broccoli provides fiber, vitamins (especially Vitamin C and K), and antioxidants. The coconut milk adds healthy fats, making it a well-rounded meal that supports various dietary needs.

Conclusion

There you have it – a wholesome, satisfying Paleo Chicken and Broccoli Casserole that’s perfect for any occasion. Whether you’re following a Paleo diet or simply looking for a nutritious and delicious meal, this casserole is sure to hit the spot. Gather your ingredients, follow the steps, and enjoy a dish that’s as good for your body as it is for your taste buds. Happy cooking!
