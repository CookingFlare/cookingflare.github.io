Indulge in the Creaminess of Indian Matar Paneer Curry


If there’s one dish that captures the essence of Indian cuisine, it’s the rich and creamy Matar Paneer Curry. This classic vegetarian dish combines the delicate flavors of peas and paneer (Indian cottage cheese) with a luscious, spiced tomato-based gravy. Whether you’re a seasoned cook or a novice in the kitchen, this recipe is a must-try for anyone looking to indulge in the vibrant and aromatic world of Indian cooking.

Ingredients

For the Matar Paneer

  • 250g paneer, cut into cubes
  • 1 cup green peas (fresh or frozen)
  • 2 large tomatoes, finely chopped
  • 1 large onion, finely chopped
  • 2-3 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1-inch piece of ginger, grated
  • 2-3 green chilies, finely chopped
  • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 teaspoon red chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon coriander powder
  • 1 teaspoon garam masala
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • 2 tablespoons cooking oil
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh cilantro leaves for garnish

For the Spice Blend

  • 1 cinnamon stick
  • 3-4 green cardamom pods
  • 3-4 cloves

Instructions

Step 1: Prepare the Spice Blend

  1. In a small pan, dry roast the cinnamon, cardamom pods, and cloves until fragrant. Remove from heat and allow to cool.
  2. Grind the roasted spices into a fine powder using a spice grinder or mortar and pestle. Set aside.

Step 2: Sauté the Aromatics

  1. Heat the cooking oil in a large pan over medium heat.
  2. Add the cumin seeds and allow them to sizzle for a few seconds.
  3. Add the chopped onions, garlic, and green chilies. Sauté until the onions are golden brown.

Step 3: Create the Flavorful Base

  1. Add the grated ginger and sauté for another minute.
  2. Stir in the chopped tomatoes and cook until they soften and release their juices.
  3. Sprinkle the turmeric, red chili, and coriander powders. Mix well and cook for a few minutes until the oil starts to separate from the spice mixture.

Step 4: Introduce the Paneer and Peas

  1. Gently add the paneer cubes and green peas to the aromatic base. Stir carefully to coat them with the spice mixture.
  2. Pour in the heavy cream and gently mix to combine.
  3. Season with salt to taste and let the curry simmer for 5-7 minutes, allowing the flavors to meld together.

Step 5: Infuse the Fragrant Spice Blend

  1. Sprinkle the homemade spice blend and garam masala over the curry. Stir gently to incorporate the spices.
  2. Let the Matar Paneer simmer for an additional 2-3 minutes, allowing the spices to infuse into the curry.

Step 6: Garnish and Serve

  1. Remove the pan from the heat and garnish the Matar Paneer with fresh cilantro leaves.
  2. Serve the creamy and aromatic Matar Paneer hot with fluffy basmati rice or buttery naan bread.

Conclusion

Indulge in the creaminess of Indian Matar Paneer Curry, a dish that tantalizes the taste buds with its velvety texture and robust flavors. Whether you’re preparing a special dinner or simply craving a comforting meal, this recipe is a delightful addition to your culinary repertoire. Embrace the art of Indian cooking and treat yourself to the luxurious experience of savoring this iconic dish. Enjoy the rich, aromatic journey that awaits with every bite of Matar Paneer Curry.
