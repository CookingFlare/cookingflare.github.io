Savory Onion and Sage Gravy Recipe to Enhance Your Thanksgiving Spread


Thanksgiving is a time for family, gratitude, and of course, indulgent feasts. As you prepare to gather around the table with your loved ones, it’s essential to have a show-stopping centerpiece for your meal. That’s where our savory onion and sage gravy comes in. This delectable addition to your Thanksgiving spread will elevate your meal to new heights, leaving your guests craving for more. Let’s dive into the recipe and discover how you can create this mouthwatering gravy from scratch.

Ingredients

  • 4 large yellow onions, thinly sliced
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter
  • 1/2 cup all-purpose flour
  • 4 cups low-sodium chicken or vegetable broth
  • 1/2 cup dry white wine
  • 2 tablespoons fresh sage, chopped
  • Salt and black pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Caramelize the Onions: In a large skillet, melt the butter over medium heat. Add the thinly sliced onions and cook, stirring occasionally, until they turn a deep, golden brown color. This process will take about 30-40 minutes, but the flavor it adds to the gravy is well worth the time.

  2. Create the Roux: Once the onions are caramelized, sprinkle the flour over them and stir continuously for about 2-3 minutes. This will create a thick, golden-brown mixture known as a roux, which will serve as the base for our gravy.

  3. Incorporate the Liquid: Slowly pour in the chicken or vegetable broth, stirring constantly to prevent lumps from forming. Add the dry white wine and continue to stir until the mixture thickens and comes to a gentle simmer.

  4. Infuse with Sage: Stir in the freshly chopped sage, allowing the fragrant herb to infuse the gravy with its earthy aroma. Season with salt and black pepper to taste, adjusting the flavors to your preference.

  5. Simmer and Serve: Let the gravy simmer for an additional 5-10 minutes, allowing all the flavors to meld together. Once it reaches your desired consistency, remove it from the heat and transfer it to a serving dish.

Serving Suggestions

This savory onion and sage gravy pairs perfectly with traditional Thanksgiving dishes such as roasted turkey, mashed potatoes, and stuffing. Its rich, velvety texture and complex flavors will complement the main course and leave your guests reaching for seconds. Additionally, consider drizzling the gravy over roasted vegetables or using it as a dipping sauce for dinner rolls to fully appreciate its savory goodness.

Conclusion

With its deep, caramelized onion base and the aromatic essence of fresh sage, this savory onion and sage gravy is a must-have addition to your Thanksgiving table. Not only does it bring a burst of flavor to your meal, but it also adds a touch of elegance to the occasion. So, as you prepare to host your Thanksgiving gathering, consider incorporating this delectable gravy into your menu. Your guests will be grateful for the extra touch of indulgence, and your feast will be remembered for years to come.

Embrace the warmth of the holiday season and elevate your Thanksgiving spread with this irresistible savory onion and sage gravy. Cheers to good food, cherished moments, and the joy of sharing a memorable meal with those you hold dear.
