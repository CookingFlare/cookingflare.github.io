Savory BBQ Pulled Chicken Sandwich: Perfect for a Summer BBQ


When it comes to summer BBQs, nothing quite hits the spot like a delicious, savory BBQ pulled chicken sandwich. Whether you’re hosting a backyard cookout or simply craving a taste of summer, this recipe is sure to please. The smoky, tangy flavors of the BBQ sauce combined with tender, juicy chicken make for a mouthwatering experience that will have your guests coming back for seconds. Without further ado, let’s dive into the recipe and tips for making the perfect BBQ pulled chicken sandwich.

Ingredients

  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1 cup of your favorite BBQ sauce
  • 1/4 cup brown sugar
  • 1/4 cup apple cider vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 teaspoon smoked paprika
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon onion powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Hamburger buns or sandwich rolls
  • Coleslaw (optional, for topping)

Instructions

  1. Prep the Chicken: Start by seasoning the chicken breasts with salt, pepper, smoked paprika, and garlic powder. Place the seasoned chicken in a slow cooker.

  2. Mix the Sauce: In a bowl, combine the BBQ sauce, brown sugar, apple cider vinegar, Worcestershire sauce, and onion powder. Mix well to ensure the brown sugar is fully dissolved.

  3. Pour the Sauce: Pour the sauce mixture over the chicken in the slow cooker, ensuring that the chicken is evenly coated.

  4. Cook: Cover the slow cooker and cook on low for 6-8 hours or on high for 3-4 hours, until the chicken is tender and easily shreds with a fork.

  5. Shred the Chicken: Once the chicken is cooked, use two forks to shred it directly in the slow cooker, allowing it to soak up the flavorful sauce.

  6. Assemble the Sandwiches: Toast the hamburger buns or sandwich rolls, then spoon the BBQ pulled chicken onto the bottom half of each bun. If desired, top the chicken with a generous helping of coleslaw for added crunch and freshness. Add the top bun and serve.

Tips for Perfect BBQ Pulled Chicken

  • Choose the Right BBQ Sauce: The key to a delicious pulled chicken sandwich is the BBQ sauce. Experiment with different flavors and find one that suits your taste preferences, whether it’s smoky, spicy, or tangy.

  • Slow and Low: Cooking the chicken low and slow is essential for achieving that melt-in-your-mouth tenderness. This method allows the flavors to meld together and ensures the chicken stays juicy.

  • Customize with Coleslaw: While the classic BBQ pulled chicken sandwich is fantastic on its own, adding a scoop of coleslaw on top can take it to the next level. The crisp, creamy texture pairs beautifully with the saucy chicken.

  • Batch Cooking: This recipe is perfect for batch cooking. Make a larger quantity and freeze the leftovers for quick and easy meals on busy days.

Conclusion

There you have it – a simple yet irresistible recipe for savory BBQ pulled chicken sandwiches, perfect for a summer BBQ or any casual gathering. With minimal effort and a burst of flavor in every bite, this dish is a true crowd-pleaser. So fire up the grill, gather your friends and family, and savor the taste of summer with these delectable sandwiches. Happy cooking!
