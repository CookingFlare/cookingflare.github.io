Bacon Guacamole Burger: An Irresistible Combination


If you’re a fan of burgers, guacamole, and bacon, then you’re in for a treat! The Bacon Guacamole Burger is an irresistible combination that will tantalize your taste buds and leave you craving for more. This mouthwatering burger is the perfect fusion of smoky, savory bacon, creamy and flavorful guacamole, and a juicy beef patty, all sandwiched between soft, toasted buns. In this blog post, we’ll guide you through the process of creating this delicious masterpiece in the comfort of your own kitchen.

Ingredients

For the Burger Patties:

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon onion powder

For the Guacamole:

  • 2 ripe avocados
  • 1/4 cup diced red onion
  • 1 small tomato, diced
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon fresh lime juice
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh cilantro

Additional Ingredients:

  • 4 hamburger buns
  • 8 slices of crispy bacon
  • Lettuce and tomato for garnish

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Burger Patties:

    • In a large mixing bowl, combine the ground beef, salt, pepper, garlic powder, and onion powder. Gently mix the ingredients together, being careful not to overwork the meat. Form the mixture into 4 equal-sized patties.

  2. Cook the Burger Patties:

    • Preheat your grill or a skillet over medium-high heat. Cook the burger patties for about 4-5 minutes on each side, or until they reach your desired level of doneness.

  3. Make the Guacamole:

    • Cut the avocados in half and remove the pits. Scoop the flesh into a bowl and mash it with a fork. Add the diced onion, tomato, minced garlic, lime juice, salt, pepper, and chopped cilantro. Mix until well combined.

  4. Prepare the Bacon:

    • Cook the bacon in a skillet over medium heat until it’s crispy and golden brown. Once done, transfer the bacon to a paper towel-lined plate to drain off any excess grease.

  5. Assemble the Burger:

    • Toast the hamburger buns until they are golden brown. Spread a generous amount of guacamole on the bottom half of each bun. Place a cooked burger patty on top of the guacamole, followed by two slices of crispy bacon. Add lettuce and tomato on top, then cover with the other half of the bun.

  6. Serve and Enjoy:

    • Your Bacon Guacamole Burger is now ready to be devoured! Serve it alongside some crispy fries or a fresh salad for a complete meal.

Tips and Variations

  • For a spicy kick, consider adding some diced jalapenos to the guacamole.
  • Experiment with different types of cheese such as pepper jack or cheddar for an extra flavor boost.
  • If you prefer a lighter option, you can use turkey bacon or veggie bacon as a substitute for traditional bacon.

Conclusion

The Bacon Guacamole Burger is a flavor-packed indulgence that brings together the richness of bacon, the creaminess of guacamole, and the satisfaction of a classic burger. Whether you’re hosting a backyard barbecue or simply craving a hearty meal, this recipe is sure to impress. So, gather your ingredients and get ready to savor every delectable bite of this irresistible combination!
