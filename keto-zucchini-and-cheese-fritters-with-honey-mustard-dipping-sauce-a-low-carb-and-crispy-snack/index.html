Keto Zucchini and Cheese Fritters with Honey Mustard Dipping Sauce: A Low-Carb and Crispy Snack


If you’re looking for a delicious and healthy snack that’s low in carbs and high in flavor, then look no further than these Keto Zucchini and Cheese Fritters with Honey Mustard Dipping Sauce. These crispy fritters are the perfect way to satisfy your cravings while sticking to your low-carb lifestyle. Plus, the zesty honey mustard dipping sauce adds a delightful tang that takes these fritters to the next level. Let’s dive into the recipe and learn how to make these irresistible fritters!

Ingredients:

For the Fritters:

  • 2 medium zucchinis, grated
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup almond flour
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Olive oil for frying

For the Honey Mustard Dipping Sauce:

  • 1/4 cup mayonnaise
  • 2 tablespoons Dijon mustard
  • 1 tablespoon honey
  • 1 teaspoon lemon juice
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Start by grating the zucchinis and then using a clean kitchen towel to squeeze out any excess moisture. This step is crucial to ensure that the fritters turn out crispy.

  2. In a large bowl, combine the grated zucchinis, shredded mozzarella cheese, almond flour, eggs, Parmesan cheese, minced garlic, dried oregano, salt, and pepper. Mix everything together until well combined.

  3. Heat a few tablespoons of olive oil in a large non-stick skillet over medium heat.

  4. Once the oil is hot, scoop about 1/4 cup of the zucchini mixture for each fritter and carefully place it in the skillet, flattening it slightly with a spatula. Make sure not to overcrowd the skillet.

  5. Cook the fritters for 3-4 minutes on each side, or until they are golden brown and crispy. You may need to work in batches, adding more oil to the skillet as needed.

  6. While the fritters are cooking, prepare the honey mustard dipping sauce by combining mayonnaise, Dijon mustard, honey, lemon juice, salt, and pepper in a small bowl. Mix until smooth and well combined.

  7. Once the fritters are cooked, transfer them to a plate lined with paper towels to drain any excess oil.

  8. Serve the Keto Zucchini and Cheese Fritters warm with the honey mustard dipping sauce on the side.

Tips:

  • For an extra kick of flavor, consider adding a dash of cayenne pepper or red pepper flakes to the fritter mixture.
  • If you prefer a different type of cheese, feel free to experiment with options like cheddar or pepper jack.

These Keto Zucchini and Cheese Fritters with Honey Mustard Dipping Sauce are not only a delightful snack but also a versatile dish that can be enjoyed as an appetizer or a side dish. Whether you’re following a keto diet or simply looking for a tasty and nutritious treat, these fritters are sure to impress. The combination of crispy texture, cheesy goodness, and zesty dipping sauce makes this recipe a winner for any occasion. So, why not give it a try and savor the flavors of this low-carb delight? Enjoy!
