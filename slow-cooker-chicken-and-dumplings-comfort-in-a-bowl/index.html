Slow Cooker Chicken and Dumplings: Comfort in a Bowl


There’s something truly satisfying about a warm and comforting bowl of chicken and dumplings. The tender chicken, the fluffy dumplings, and the rich broth all come together to create a dish that warms the soul. And with a slow cooker, you can easily achieve that comforting flavor without spending hours in the kitchen.

Ingredients

  • 2 pounds of boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 2 cups of chicken broth
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 3 carrots, peeled and sliced
  • 3 celery stalks, sliced
  • 3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon of dried thyme
  • 1 bay leaf
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 cup of frozen peas
  • 1 cup of all-purpose flour
  • 2 teaspoons of baking powder
  • ½ teaspoon of salt
  • ½ cup of milk
  • 2 tablespoons of melted butter
  • Fresh parsley, chopped (for garnish)

Instructions

  1. Place the chicken breasts in the slow cooker and add the chicken broth, diced onion, sliced carrots, sliced celery, minced garlic, dried thyme, bay leaf, salt, and pepper. Give it a gentle stir to combine all the ingredients.

  2. Cover the slow cooker and cook on low for 6-8 hours or on high for 4-6 hours, until the chicken is cooked through and tender.

  3. Once the chicken is cooked, remove it from the slow cooker and shred it using two forks. Return the shredded chicken to the slow cooker.

  4. Add the frozen peas to the slow cooker and stir to combine. Cook for an additional 30 minutes on low.

  5. While the peas are cooking, prepare the dumplings. In a mixing bowl, combine the all-purpose flour, baking powder, salt, milk, and melted butter. Stir until just combined, being careful not to overmix.

  6. Drop spoonfuls of the dumpling batter onto the surface of the chicken and broth mixture in the slow cooker. Cover and cook for an additional 30 minutes on high, or until the dumplings are cooked through and fluffy.

  7. Once the dumplings are cooked, remove the bay leaf from the slow cooker. Serve the chicken and dumplings in bowls, garnished with fresh chopped parsley.

Tips and Variations

  • For a creamier broth, you can add ½ cup of heavy cream to the slow cooker along with the frozen peas.
  • If you prefer a thicker broth, you can mix 2 tablespoons of cornstarch with ¼ cup of water and add it to the slow cooker 30 minutes before the end of the cooking time.
  • Feel free to add other vegetables like green beans or corn to the slow cooker for additional flavor and texture.
  • If you don’t have a slow cooker, you can still make this recipe on the stovetop. Simply follow the same steps, but cook the chicken and vegetables in a large pot over low heat for 1-2 hours, until the chicken is tender.

Conclusion

Slow Cooker Chicken and Dumplings is the ultimate comfort food that can be enjoyed any time of the year. The convenience of a slow cooker makes this recipe a breeze to prepare, and the end result is a delicious bowl of tender chicken, fluffy dumplings, and flavorful broth. So the next time you’re craving a comforting meal, give this recipe a try. Your taste buds will thank you!
