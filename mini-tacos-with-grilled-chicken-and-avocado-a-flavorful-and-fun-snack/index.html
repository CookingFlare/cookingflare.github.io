Mini Tacos with Grilled Chicken and Avocado: A Flavorful and Fun Snack


If you’re looking for a delicious and fun snack to serve at your next gathering, mini tacos with grilled chicken and avocado are the perfect choice. These bite-sized treats are bursting with flavor and are sure to be a hit with your friends and family. Not only are they tasty, but they are also easy to make and can be customized to suit your personal preferences. In this blog post, we’ll guide you through the process of creating these delectable mini tacos step by step.

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound boneless, skinless chicken breast
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 12 small corn tortillas
  • 2 avocados, diced
  • 1/2 cup diced tomatoes
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh cilantro
  • 1/4 cup finely chopped red onion
  • 1 lime, cut into wedges
  • Hot sauce (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Start by marinating the chicken. In a bowl, mix together the olive oil, chili powder, cumin, garlic powder, salt, and pepper. Add the chicken breast to the bowl and coat it evenly with the marinade. Cover the bowl and refrigerate for at least 30 minutes to allow the flavors to meld.

  2. Preheat your grill or grill pan over medium-high heat. Once hot, place the marinated chicken breast on the grill and cook for 6-7 minutes on each side, or until the chicken is fully cooked through and has beautiful grill marks. Remove the chicken from the grill and let it rest for a few minutes before slicing it into small, bite-sized pieces.

  3. While the chicken is resting, warm the corn tortillas on the grill for about 30 seconds on each side, until they are soft and pliable. Stack the tortillas on a plate and cover them with a clean kitchen towel to keep them warm.

  4. Assemble the mini tacos by placing a few pieces of the grilled chicken onto each tortilla. Top the chicken with diced avocado, tomatoes, cilantro, and red onion. Squeeze a wedge of lime over the top of each taco for a burst of citrusy freshness. If you like a bit of heat, drizzle some hot sauce over the tacos.

  5. Serve the mini tacos with grilled chicken and avocado immediately and watch them disappear in no time!

Tips for Customization:

  • Add a dollop of sour cream or Greek yogurt for a creamy element.
  • Incorporate shredded cheese for an extra layer of richness.
  • Experiment with different types of hot sauce to find your favorite flavor combination.
  • For a vegetarian option, swap the grilled chicken for grilled vegetables like bell peppers, zucchini, and onions.

Conclusion:

These mini tacos with grilled chicken and avocado are not only delicious, but they also make for a visually appealing and satisfying snack. The combination of tender grilled chicken, creamy avocado, and zesty lime creates a flavor explosion in every bite. Whether you’re hosting a party or simply looking for a fun and flavorful snack, these mini tacos are sure to be a crowd-pleaser. So, gather your ingredients and get ready to impress your guests with this delightful treat!
