Savory Breakfast Sushi Rolls for a Unique and Tasty Morning


Who says sushi is just for lunch or dinner? If you’re looking to shake up your morning routine and add a touch of creativity to your breakfast, then these savory breakfast sushi rolls are just what you need. Not only are they visually appealing, but they’re also incredibly delicious and versatile. Whether you’re a sushi aficionado or a breakfast enthusiast, this recipe is sure to become a new favorite. So, let’s roll up our sleeves and dive into the art of making savory breakfast sushi rolls!

Ingredients

For the Sushi Rice:

  • 2 cups sushi rice
  • 2 1/2 cups water
  • 1/2 cup rice vinegar
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 teaspoon salt

For the Filling:

  • 6 eggs
  • 1 tablespoon butter
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 4 slices of cooked bacon
  • 1 ripe avocado, sliced
  • 1 small cucumber, julienned
  • 4 nori seaweed sheets

For the Dipping Sauce:

  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon rice vinegar
  • 1 teaspoon sesame oil
  • 1 teaspoon finely grated ginger
  • 1 teaspoon honey

Instructions

Step 1: Prepare the Sushi Rice

  1. Rinse the sushi rice in a fine-mesh strainer until the water runs clear.
  2. In a medium saucepan, combine the rice and water. Bring to a boil, then reduce the heat to low, cover, and simmer for 20 minutes.
  3. In a small saucepan, combine the rice vinegar, sugar, and salt. Heat over medium heat, stirring occasionally, until the sugar and salt are dissolved. Remove from heat and let it cool.
  4. Once the rice is cooked, transfer it to a large bowl. Gently fold in the seasoned rice vinegar until well combined. Let the rice cool to room temperature.

Step 2: Prepare the Filling

  1. In a bowl, beat the eggs and season with salt and pepper. In a non-stick skillet, melt the butter over medium heat. Pour in the beaten eggs and cook, stirring occasionally, until soft scrambled. Remove from heat and let it cool.
  2. Lay a bamboo sushi mat on a clean, flat surface. Place a sheet of plastic wrap on top of the mat. Lay a nori sheet, shiny side down, on the plastic wrap.

Step 3: Assemble the Sushi Rolls

  1. With wet hands, spread a thin layer of sushi rice evenly over the nori sheet, leaving a 1-inch border at the top.
  2. Arrange the scrambled eggs, bacon, avocado slices, and cucumber strips in a line across the center of the rice.
  3. Using the bamboo mat, carefully roll the sushi away from you, tucking the filling in as you go. Roll it tightly and seal the edge with a little water.
  4. Repeat the process with the remaining nori sheets and filling ingredients.

Step 4: Slice and Serve

  1. Using a sharp knife, slice each sushi roll into 6-8 pieces. Wipe the knife with a damp cloth between each cut to keep it clean.
  2. In a small bowl, whisk together the soy sauce, rice vinegar, sesame oil, ginger, and honey to make the dipping sauce.
  3. Serve the savory breakfast sushi rolls with the dipping sauce on the side and enjoy!

Tips and Variations

  • Get creative with the fillings! Try adding smoked salmon, cream cheese, or sautéed mushrooms for a different flavor profile.
  • For a vegetarian option, swap the bacon for grilled tofu or marinated tempeh.
  • Use a sushi rolling mat to make the rolling process easier and more precise.
  • If you’re short on time, prepare the sushi rice and filling the night before to streamline the assembly process in the morning.

There you have it – a unique and tasty way to start your morning with savory breakfast sushi rolls. Whether you enjoy them on their own or as part of a brunch spread, these rolls are bound to impress. So, why not give this recipe a try and elevate your breakfast game? Happy rolling!
