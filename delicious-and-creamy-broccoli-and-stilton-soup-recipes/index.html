Delicious and Creamy Broccoli and Stilton Soup Recipes


Broccoli and Stilton soup is a classic comfort dish that perfectly marries the earthy flavors of fresh broccoli with the rich, tangy notes of Stilton cheese. This creamy soup is not only delightful to the palate but also packs a nutritional punch, making it a favorite among health-conscious foodies. In this post, we’ll explore several different recipes for making delicious and creamy broccoli and Stilton soup, ensuring you have various options to suit your taste and dietary preferences.

The Benefits of Broccoli and Stilton

Before diving into the recipes, let’s take a moment to appreciate the star ingredients of this soup. Broccoli is renowned for its health benefits; it’s rich in vitamins C and K, fiber, and various antioxidants that contribute to overall health. Meanwhile, Stilton cheese, known for its characteristic blue veins, adds a creamy texture and complex flavor that elevates any dish.

Why Choose Broccoli and Stilton Soup?

  1. Nutritional Powerhouse: Combining broccoli with Stilton cheese creates a soup rich in nutrients and flavor.
  2. Comfort Food: The creamy texture and warm flavors make this soup perfect for cozy evenings.
  3. Versatile: This soup can be enjoyed on its own, paired with crusty bread, or served as a starter for a dinner party.

Classic Creamy Broccoli and Stilton Soup

Ingredients

  • 500g fresh broccoli, chopped
  • 1 medium onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 medium potato, peeled and diced
  • 1 liter vegetable stock
  • 200g Stilton cheese, crumbled
  • 200ml double cream
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley for garnish

Instructions

  1. Sauté the Vegetables: In a large pot, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the chopped onion and garlic, sautéing until they become translucent.

  2. Cook the Broccoli and Potato: Add the diced potato and chopped broccoli to the pot. Stir for a few minutes, allowing the vegetables to soften slightly.

  3. Add Stock: Pour in the vegetable stock and bring to a boil. Lower the heat and let it simmer for about 20 minutes, or until the broccoli and potatoes are tender.

  4. Blend the Soup: Using an immersion blender, puree the soup until smooth. If you prefer a chunkier texture, blend only half of the soup and mix it back in.

  5. Stir in the Cheese and Cream: Add the crumbled Stilton cheese and double cream to the pot, stirring until the cheese has melted and combined with the soup. Season with salt and pepper to taste.

  6. Serve: Ladle the soup into bowls and garnish with fresh parsley. Enjoy your delicious and creamy broccoli and Stilton soup with a slice of crusty bread!

Variations of Broccoli and Stilton Soup

While the classic version is hard to beat, here are some variations to spice things up and cater to different preferences.

1. Broccoli and Stilton Soup with Caramelized Onions

Ingredients:

  • All ingredients from the classic recipe, plus 2 extra onions for caramelization.

Instructions:

  1. Caramelize Onions: In a separate pan, thinly slice the extra onions and cook them over low heat with a bit of olive oil until they are golden brown and caramelized.
  2. Follow the classic recipe: Once the soup is blended, stir in the caramelized onions before serving. This adds a sweet, rich flavor that complements the tanginess of the Stilton.

2. Spicy Broccoli and Stilton Soup

Ingredients:

  • All classic ingredients plus 1 teaspoon of chili flakes or a fresh red chili, chopped.

Instructions:

  1. Add Spice: When sautéing the onions and garlic, add the chili flakes or chopped chili to infuse the soup with heat.
  2. Blend and Serve: Follow the classic recipe instructions, and be prepared for a delightful kick in your creamy soup!

3. Vegan Broccoli and Cashew “Stilton” Soup

Ingredients:

  • 500g fresh broccoli, chopped
  • 1 medium onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 medium potato, peeled and diced
  • 1 liter vegetable stock
  • 200g cashews, soaked for 4 hours
  • 200ml coconut cream
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Nutritional yeast for flavor, optional
  • Fresh parsley for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Prepare Cashew Cream: Drain and rinse the soaked cashews. Blend them with a little water until smooth and creamy. Add nutritional yeast for a cheesy flavor if desired.
  2. Follow the classic recipe: Replace the Stilton cheese with your cashew cream after blending the soup. Stir in the coconut cream for added richness before serving.

Tips for Perfect Broccoli and Stilton Soup

  • Use Fresh Ingredients: Fresh broccoli will give your soup the best flavor and texture. Avoid frozen broccoli if possible.
  • Adjust Creaminess: For a lighter soup, reduce the amount of cream. You can also substitute with low-fat yogurt for a tangy twist.
  • Experiment with Flavors: Incorporate additional herbs such as thyme or rosemary for added depth. A splash of lemon juice can brighten the flavors beautifully.

Storage and Reheating

This soup can be made in advance and stored in the refrigerator for up to 3 days. It also freezes well for up to 3 months. To reheat, gently warm on the stove, adding a splash of water or stock if the soup is too thick.

Conclusion

Broccoli and Stilton soup is a delightful dish that combines nutrition with comfort food. Whether you stick with the classic recipe or try one of the delicious variations, you’re sure to create a warming bowl of soup that will satisfy and nourish. Perfect for a chilly evening, a starter for a dinner party, or a light lunch, this soup is versatile and easy to make.

So gather your ingredients, unleash your inner chef, and enjoy the creamy goodness of broccoli and Stilton soup in your kitchen today!
