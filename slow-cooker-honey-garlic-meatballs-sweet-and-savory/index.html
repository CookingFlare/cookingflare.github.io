Slow Cooker Honey Garlic Meatballs: Sweet and Savory


Slow cookers are a game-changer when it comes to easy, flavorful meals that require minimal effort. One of my favorite recipes to make in a slow cooker is Honey Garlic Meatballs. This dish combines the sweetness of honey with savory garlic and tender meatballs, creating a delicious blend of flavors that will leave everyone at the table asking for more.

Ingredients

For the Meatballs:

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1/2 cup breadcrumbs
  • 1/4 cup milk
  • 1 egg
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil

For the Sauce:

  • 1/2 cup honey
  • 1/2 cup soy sauce
  • 1/4 cup ketchup
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon ginger, minced
  • 1/4 teaspoon red pepper flakes (optional)
  • 1/4 cup water
  • 1 tablespoon cornstarch

For Serving:

  • Cooked rice
  • Sesame seeds (optional)
  • Chopped green onions (optional)

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Meatballs:

    • In a large bowl, combine ground beef, breadcrumbs, milk, egg, minced garlic, salt, and pepper. Mix until well combined.
    • Shape the mixture into meatballs, about 1 inch in diameter.

  2. Brown the Meatballs:

    • Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat.
    • Add meatballs in batches and cook until browned on all sides, about 4-5 minutes per batch. Transfer browned meatballs to a plate.

  3. Make the Sauce:

    • In a medium bowl, whisk together honey, soy sauce, ketchup, minced garlic, ginger, and red pepper flakes (if using).
    • In a separate small bowl, mix water and cornstarch until cornstarch is dissolved.

  4. Combine Everything in the Slow Cooker:

    • Place the browned meatballs in the slow cooker.
    • Pour the honey garlic sauce over the meatballs, making sure they are well coated.
    • Cover and cook on low for 2-3 hours, or until the meatballs are cooked through and the sauce has thickened.

  5. Serve and Enjoy:

    • Once done, serve the Honey Garlic Meatballs over cooked rice.
    • Garnish with sesame seeds and chopped green onions if desired.
    • Enjoy the sweet and savory flavors of this delicious dish!

Tips for Success

  • Choosing Ground Meat: You can use ground beef, pork, chicken, or turkey for the meatballs, depending on your preference.
  • Adjusting Sweetness: If you prefer a less sweet sauce, you can reduce the amount of honey slightly or balance it with a bit more soy sauce.
  • Thickening the Sauce: If the sauce needs further thickening after cooking, mix a little more cornstarch with cold water and stir it into the slow cooker. Cook on high for 15-20 minutes, stirring occasionally, until thickened.

Why Slow Cooker Honey Garlic Meatballs?

This recipe is perfect for busy days when you want a homemade meal without spending hours in the kitchen. The slow cooker does all the work of infusing the meatballs with the rich flavors of garlic and honey, ensuring they are tender and juicy by the time you’re ready to eat. Plus, it’s a crowd-pleaser! Whether you’re serving a family dinner or hosting a party, these meatballs are sure to be a hit.

By using simple ingredients and letting the slow cooker do its magic, you can create a dish that’s both satisfying and impressive. The combination of sweet honey and savory garlic makes these meatballs irresistible, and the addition of rice and garnishes completes the meal perfectly.

So, the next time you’re looking for a delicious and stress-free dinner idea, give these Slow Cooker Honey Garlic Meatballs a try. Your taste buds will thank you!
