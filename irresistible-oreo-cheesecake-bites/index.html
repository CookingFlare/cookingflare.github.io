Irresistible Oreo Cheesecake Bites


If you’re a fan of Oreo cookies and cheesecake, then you’re in for a treat with these irresistible Oreo Cheesecake Bites. These delightful little desserts are the perfect combination of creamy cheesecake and chocolatey Oreo goodness, all in a convenient bite-sized form. Whether you’re hosting a party, looking for a sweet treat, or just craving something indulgent, these Oreo Cheesecake Bites are sure to satisfy your sweet tooth.

Ingredients

For the Oreo Crust:

  • 24 Oreo cookies
  • 4 tablespoons unsalted butter, melted

For the Cheesecake Filling:

  • 16 oz (2 packages) cream cheese, softened
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/2 cup sour cream
  • 12 Oreo cookies, coarsely chopped

For the Topping (optional):

  • Whipped cream
  • Additional Oreo cookies, crushed

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Oreo Crust:

    • Preheat the oven to 325°F (160°C). Line a 9x9 inch baking pan with parchment paper, leaving an overhang on the sides for easy removal.
    • In a food processor, pulse the Oreo cookies until they are finely crushed. Alternatively, you can place the cookies in a zip-top bag and crush them with a rolling pin.
    • In a bowl, mix the crushed Oreo cookies with melted butter until well combined.
    • Press the Oreo mixture into the bottom of the prepared baking pan in an even layer. Bake for 10 minutes, then remove from the oven and set aside.

  2. Prepare the Cheesecake Filling:

    • In a large bowl, beat the softened cream cheese and sugar together until smooth and creamy.
    • Add the eggs, one at a time, mixing well after each addition. Stir in the vanilla extract.
    • Gently fold in the sour cream until fully combined, then stir in the coarsely chopped Oreo cookies.
    • Pour the cheesecake filling over the baked Oreo crust and spread it into an even layer.

  3. Bake and Chill:

    • Bake the cheesecake for 35-40 minutes, or until the edges are set and the center is slightly jiggly.
    • Remove from the oven and let it cool to room temperature, then refrigerate for at least 3-4 hours, or until completely chilled and set.

  4. Cut and Serve:

    • Once the cheesecake is thoroughly chilled, use the parchment paper overhang to lift it out of the baking pan.
    • Cut the cheesecake into bite-sized squares and, if desired, top each square with a dollop of whipped cream and a sprinkle of crushed Oreo cookies.

Tips and Variations

  • For a twist, try using different flavors of Oreo cookies such as mint, peanut butter, or golden Oreos.
  • You can also customize the toppings by drizzling chocolate sauce or caramel over the cheesecake bites.
  • These Oreo Cheesecake Bites can be made ahead of time and stored in the refrigerator for up to 3 days, making them a convenient dessert option for entertaining.

Final Thoughts

These Oreo Cheesecake Bites are a delightful marriage of two beloved treats – Oreo cookies and creamy cheesecake. The crunchy Oreo crust provides the perfect contrast to the luscious cheesecake filling, making each bite a symphony of flavors and textures. Whether you’re a seasoned baker or a novice in the kitchen, this recipe is approachable and rewarding. So, gather your ingredients, preheat your oven, and get ready to indulge in these irresistible Oreo Cheesecake Bites. Your taste buds will thank you!
