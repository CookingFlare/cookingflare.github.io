Buttery and Delicious Butterscotch Chip Cookies


If you’re a fan of classic cookies with a twist, then you’re in for a treat with these buttery and delicious butterscotch chip cookies. This delightful recipe combines the rich, caramel-like flavor of butterscotch with the comforting buttery goodness of a traditional cookie, creating a perfect balance of sweetness and warmth in every bite. Whether you’re baking for a special occasion or simply craving a sweet indulgence, these cookies are sure to become a favorite in your recipe collection.

Ingredients

For the Butterscotch Chip Cookies:

  • 1 cup (2 sticks) unsalted butter, softened
  • 1 cup light brown sugar, packed
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 2 cups butterscotch chips

Instructions

Step 1: Preparing the Dough

  1. In a large mixing bowl, cream together the softened butter, brown sugar, and granulated sugar until light and fluffy.
  2. Add the eggs, one at a time, beating well after each addition. Stir in the vanilla extract.
  3. In a separate bowl, whisk together the flour, baking soda, and salt. Gradually add the dry ingredients to the creamed mixture and mix until well combined.
  4. Gently fold in the butterscotch chips until evenly distributed throughout the dough.

Step 2: Chilling the Dough

  1. Cover the cookie dough and refrigerate for at least 1 hour, or until firm. Chilling the dough helps the flavors meld together and prevents the cookies from spreading too much during baking.

Step 3: Baking the Cookies

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C) and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
  2. Using a cookie scoop or spoon, portion the chilled dough into rounded balls and place them on the prepared baking sheet, leaving some space between each cookie for spreading.
  3. Bake the cookies for 10-12 minutes, or until the edges are golden brown and the centers are set.
  4. Remove the cookies from the oven and allow them to cool on the baking sheet for a few minutes before transferring them to a wire rack to cool completely.

Step 4: Enjoying the Deliciousness

Once the cookies have cooled, pour yourself a glass of cold milk or brew a fresh cup of coffee, and indulge in the buttery, butterscotch-infused goodness of these delightful cookies. Share them with family and friends or savor them as a well-deserved treat just for yourself.

Tips and Variations

  • For an extra indulgent twist, sprinkle a pinch of sea salt on top of each cookie before baking to enhance the sweet-salty contrast.
  • Experiment with different types of butterscotch chips, such as dark or white chocolate butterscotch chips, to customize the flavor profile of the cookies.
  • Store any leftover cookies in an airtight container at room temperature for up to a week, though they are likely to disappear much sooner!

So, the next time you’re in the mood for a comforting and indulgent sweet treat, give these buttery and delicious butterscotch chip cookies a try. With their rich butterscotch flavor and irresistible buttery texture, they are sure to become a beloved addition to your baking repertoire. Happy baking!
