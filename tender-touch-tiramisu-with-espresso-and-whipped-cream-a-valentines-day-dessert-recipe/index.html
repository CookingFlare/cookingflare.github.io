Tender Touch Tiramisu with Espresso and Whipped Cream: A Valentine's Day Dessert Recipe


Valentine’s Day is just around the corner, and what better way to celebrate than with a decadent and indulgent dessert? This Tender Touch Tiramisu with Espresso and Whipped Cream is the perfect treat to impress your loved one. With layers of delicate ladyfingers soaked in rich espresso, creamy mascarpone cheese, and a hint of cocoa, this classic Italian dessert will leave you craving for more.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup strong brewed espresso, cooled
  • 24 ladyfingers
  • 4 large egg yolks
  • 3/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 2/3 cup milk
  • 1 1/2 cups heavy cream
  • 8 ounces mascarpone cheese
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 2 tablespoons cocoa powder

Instructions

  1. In a shallow bowl, pour the cooled espresso. Dip each ladyfinger into the espresso, making sure to coat both sides, but not soak them completely. Line the bottom of a 9x9-inch baking dish with half of the soaked ladyfingers.

  2. In a medium-sized bowl, whisk together the egg yolks and sugar until well combined and slightly thickened. Add the milk and whisk until smooth. Transfer the mixture to a saucepan and cook over low heat, stirring constantly, until it thickens into a custard-like consistency. Remove from heat and let it cool completely.

  3. In a separate bowl, whip the heavy cream until soft peaks form. Add the mascarpone cheese and vanilla extract to the cooled custard and mix until well combined. Gently fold in the whipped cream until the mixture is smooth and creamy.

  4. Spoon half of the mascarpone mixture over the layer of soaked ladyfingers in the baking dish. Use a spatula to spread it evenly. Place another layer of soaked ladyfingers on top, followed by the remaining mascarpone mixture. Smooth the top with the spatula.

  5. Cover the baking dish with plastic wrap and refrigerate for at least 4 hours or overnight to allow the flavors to meld together and the tiramisu to set.

  6. Just before serving, sift the cocoa powder over the top of the tiramisu. This will add a touch of bitterness to balance the sweetness of the dessert. Cut into squares and serve chilled.

Tips and Variations

  • For an extra indulgent touch, you can drizzle some chocolate sauce or sprinkle grated chocolate over the top of the tiramisu before serving.
  • If you prefer a stronger coffee flavor, you can add a splash of your favorite liqueur, such as Kahlua or Amaretto, to the espresso.
  • To make individual portions, assemble the tiramisu in serving glasses or small bowls instead of a baking dish. Layer the soaked ladyfingers and mascarpone mixture alternately, ending with a layer of the mascarpone mixture on top. Chill as instructed before serving.

So there you have it, a delightful and romantic dessert to share with your special someone on Valentine’s Day or any occasion that calls for a touch of sweetness. The combination of espresso-soaked ladyfingers, creamy mascarpone, and a dusting of cocoa creates a heavenly dessert that will leave you and your loved one swooning. Enjoy!
