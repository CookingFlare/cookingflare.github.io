Mouthwatering Nut-Free Banana Bread Recipe


Are you a fan of banana bread but have a nut allergy? Don’t worry, we’ve got you covered! This mouthwatering nut-free banana bread recipe is the perfect solution for those who can’t enjoy the traditional version. With its moist and flavorful texture, you won’t even miss the nuts. So let’s get baking!

Ingredients

  • 3 ripe bananas, mashed
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, melted
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup plain yogurt
  • 1/4 cup milk

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C) and grease a 9x5 inch loaf pan with butter or cooking spray.

  2. In a large mixing bowl, combine the mashed bananas, melted butter, and sugar. Mix well until the ingredients are fully incorporated.

  3. Add the eggs one at a time, mixing well after each addition. Stir in the vanilla extract.

  4. In a separate bowl, whisk together the flour, baking soda, and salt. Gradually add the dry ingredients to the banana mixture, alternating with the yogurt and milk. Begin and end with the dry ingredients, mixing until just combined. Be careful not to overmix, as this can result in a dense banana bread.

  5. Pour the batter into the prepared loaf pan and smooth the top with a spatula.

  6. Bake for 55-60 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean. If the top begins to brown too quickly, you can loosely cover it with aluminum foil.

  7. Once baked, remove the banana bread from the oven and let it cool in the pan for 10 minutes. Then transfer it to a wire rack to cool completely before slicing.

Serving Suggestions

This nut-free banana bread is delicious on its own, but you can take it to the next level with a few serving suggestions. Here are some ideas to inspire you:

  • Spread a generous layer of cream cheese on a slice of banana bread for a creamy and tangy twist.
  • Drizzle some honey or maple syrup over a warm slice to add a touch of sweetness.
  • Serve it with a scoop of vanilla ice cream for a delightful dessert.
  • Enjoy a slice with your morning coffee or tea for a comforting breakfast.

Storage Tips

If you have any leftovers (which is rare because it’s so delicious!), you can store them in an airtight container at room temperature for up to 3 days. For longer storage, wrap the banana bread tightly in plastic wrap and keep it in the refrigerator for up to a week. You can also freeze individual slices in freezer-safe bags for up to 3 months. Just thaw them at room temperature or pop them in the toaster for a quick and easy snack.

Conclusion

We hope you enjoy this mouthwatering nut-free banana bread recipe as much as we do. It’s the perfect treat for any occasion, whether you’re hosting a brunch or simply craving a homemade snack. So go ahead, grab those ripe bananas, and start baking this delightful treat. Your taste buds will thank you!

Note: This recipe can easily be customized to suit your preferences. Feel free to add chocolate chips, chopped dried fruits, or even a sprinkle of cinnamon for an extra burst of flavor. Get creative and make it your own!
