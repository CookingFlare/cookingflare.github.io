Mouthwatering Low Carb Garlic Parmesan Shrimp and Zoodles Recipe


If you’re on the lookout for a delicious, healthy meal that doesn’t compromise on flavor, look no further than this low carb garlic parmesan shrimp and zoodles recipe. This dish perfectly combines the succulent taste of shrimp with the freshness of zucchini noodles, making it an ideal choice for anyone following a low-carb or keto diet.

Why Zoodles?

Zoodles, or zucchini noodles, have gained immense popularity, especially among health-conscious food lovers. They serve as a fantastic substitute for traditional pasta, offering a fraction of the calories and carbohydrates. Zucchini is low in calories and high in essential nutrients, making it a perfect vehicle for the rich and savory garlic parmesan sauce in this recipe. Not only do zoodles provide an excellent texture, but they also add a subtle sweetness that complements the dish beautifully.

Benefits of Shrimp

Shrimp is another star ingredient in this recipe. Packed with protein and low in calories, shrimp is a fantastic option for those looking to maintain a healthy lifestyle. They are rich in omega-3 fatty acids, which are known for their anti-inflammatory properties and heart health benefits. Additionally, shrimp cooks quickly, making it a convenient protein choice for busy weeknights.

Ingredients

To whip up this mouthwatering low carb garlic parmesan shrimp and zoodles dish, you will need the following ingredients:

  • 1 pound large shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 4 medium zucchinis, spiralized into noodles
  • 4 tablespoons unsalted butter
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon red pepper flakes (adjust according to your spice preference)
  • 1 teaspoon dried Italian seasoning
  • ½ cup grated Parmesan cheese, plus extra for serving
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley, chopped (for garnish)
  • Lemon wedges (for serving)

Kitchen Tools You’ll Need

Before we dive into the cooking process, let’s gather some essential kitchen tools that will make your cooking experience smooth and enjoyable:

  • Spiralizer or vegetable peeler (for zoodles)
  • Large skillet or frying pan
  • Tongs (for tossing the shrimp and zoodles)
  • Measuring cups and spoons
  • Wooden spoon or spatula

Cooking Instructions

Step 1: Prepare the Zoodles

Start by spiralizing the zucchinis to create zoodles. If you don’t have a spiralizer, you can use a vegetable peeler to make thin strips. Once you have your zoodles, set them aside. It’s important not to overcook the zoodles, as they can become mushy. We want them to maintain a slight crunch for the best texture.

Step 2: Sauté the Shrimp

Heat 2 tablespoons of butter in a large skillet over medium heat. Once the butter is melted and bubbling, add the minced garlic and sauté for about 30 seconds until fragrant. Be careful not to burn the garlic, as it can turn bitter.

Next, add the shrimp to the skillet in a single layer. Season with salt, pepper, red pepper flakes, and Italian seasoning. Cook the shrimp for about 2-3 minutes on one side, then flip them over and cook for an additional 2-3 minutes until they turn pink and opaque. Shrimp cooks quickly, so keep an eye on them to avoid overcooking.

Step 3: Combine the Ingredients

Once the shrimp are cooked through, remove them from the skillet and set them aside on a plate. In the same skillet, melt the remaining 2 tablespoons of butter. Add the spiralized zucchini and toss gently to combine with the butter and leftover flavors from the shrimp. Sauté for about 2-3 minutes until the zoodles are just tender yet still crisp.

Step 4: Create the Sauce

Lower the heat and return the cooked shrimp to the skillet with the zoodles. Add the grated Parmesan cheese and toss everything together until the cheese melts and coats the shrimp and zoodles. If the mixture seems too dry, you can add a splash of water or chicken broth to create a creamy texture.

Step 5: Serve

Remove the skillet from heat and taste for seasoning. Adjust with more salt, pepper, or cheese as desired. Serve the garlic parmesan shrimp and zoodles in bowls, garnished with freshly chopped parsley and additional Parmesan cheese. For a zesty kick, serve with lemon wedges on the side to squeeze over the dish.

Tips for the Perfect Dish

  • Choose Fresh Shrimp: When selecting shrimp, opt for wild-caught if possible, as they tend to have better flavor and texture.
  • Don’t Overcook the Zoodles: To maintain their crunch, add the zoodles to the skillet just before serving and cook them for only a couple of minutes.
  • Experiment with Flavors: Feel free to customize the recipe by adding your favorite vegetables like cherry tomatoes, bell peppers, or spinach for extra nutrition and flavor.
  • Make it Creamy: For an even richer sauce, incorporate a splash of heavy cream or cream cheese when combining the shrimp and zoodles.

Nutritional Information

This low carb garlic parmesan shrimp and zoodles recipe is not only delicious but also nutritious. Here’s a rough breakdown of the nutritional content per serving:

  • Calories: 350
  • Protein: 30g
  • Fat: 20g
  • Carbohydrates: 8g
  • Fiber: 2g
  • Net Carbs: 6g

Final Thoughts

This mouthwatering low carb garlic parmesan shrimp and zoodles recipe is sure to become a staple in your kitchen. It’s quick to prepare, bursting with flavor, and perfect for weeknight dinners or special occasions. The combination of shrimp and zoodles creates a satisfying meal that will leave you feeling full without the heavy carb load.

Try this recipe today and treat yourself to a delightful dining experience that’s both healthy and indulgent. Whether you’re following a low carb lifestyle or simply looking for a delicious meal, this dish is guaranteed to impress. Enjoy your culinary adventure and savor every bite of this delectable shrimp and zoodles dish!
