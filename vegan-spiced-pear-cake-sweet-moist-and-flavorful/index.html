Vegan Spiced Pear Cake: Sweet, Moist, and Flavorful


Few desserts capture the warmth of autumn like a spiced pear cake. This vegan spiced pear cake is a celebration of rich flavors, moist texture, and natural sweetness—all without any dairy or eggs. Whether you’re planning dessert for a family gathering or simply want a cozy treat to go with your favorite warm drink, this plant-based cake is a showstopper.

Dive into the details below, and you’ll discover how simple ingredients and seasonal produce can work together in perfect harmony.

Why Pears Make the Perfect Ingredient

Pears are one of nature’s sweetest fruits, delivering a soft texture and juicy sweetness that feels luxurious in baked goods. In this recipe, ripe pears not only enhance the flavor but also contribute moisture and richness to the cake. If chosen carefully, fresh pears can make all the difference. Look for pears that are slightly soft to the touch but not overly ripe; this ensures they’re easy to work with without becoming mushy. Varieties like Bartlett or Bosc work wonderfully for baking.

When paired with warm spices like cinnamon, ginger, and nutmeg, pears create a flavor profile that dances between earthy and sweet. This delicate balance makes spiced pear cake an irresistible option for those seeking a vegan dessert that feels decadent yet wholesome.

What Makes This Cake Moist and Vegan-Friendly

Some people worry that vegan cakes might turn out dry without the richness of eggs or dairy. In reality, nothing could be further from the truth! In this recipe, we use ingredients like unsweetened applesauce and plant-based milk to achieve a moist texture that’s just as soft as (if not better than) traditional cakes. Applesauce acts as both an egg replacer and a natural sweetener, while plant-based milk helps bind the ingredients together.

For structure, we’ll use all-purpose flour and a small amount of almond flour to give the cake its signature texture, which is dense yet tender—just the way a spiced cake should be.

Vegan Spiced Pear Cake Recipe

Let’s get straight into the kitchen. This recipe is easy to follow, even for novice bakers, and the results are truly magical.

Ingredients

Dry Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/3 cup almond flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon fine sea salt
  • 2 teaspoons ground cinnamon
  • 1 teaspoon ground ginger
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground nutmeg

Wet Ingredients

  • 1 cup unsweetened applesauce
  • 1/2 cup brown sugar (or coconut sugar for less refined sweetness)
  • 1/3 cup neutral oil (e.g., sunflower, avocado, or vegetable oil)
  • 3/4 cup unsweetened plant-based milk (such as almond or oat milk)
  • 2 teaspoons pure vanilla extract

Pears

  • 2 medium-sized ripe pears, diced (approximately 1 1/2 cups)

Optional Topping

  • A sprinkle of turbinado sugar
  • A handful of chopped walnuts or pecans

Instructions

Step 1: Prep and Preheat
Start by preheating your oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease a 9-inch round cake pan or line it with parchment paper for easier removal later.

Step 2: Combine Dry Ingredients
In a large bowl, whisk together the all-purpose flour, almond flour, baking powder, baking soda, salt, cinnamon, ginger, and nutmeg. Ensure the spices are evenly distributed throughout the flour mixture.

Step 3: Mix Wet Ingredients
In a separate bowl, combine the applesauce, brown sugar, oil, plant-based milk, and vanilla extract. Whisk until smooth and well-incorporated.

Step 4: Incorporate Pears
Peel and dice the pears into small, bite-sized chunks. Be sure to remove any seeds or tough parts. Add the diced pears to the wet ingredient mixture, stirring gently to coat the fruit.

Step 5: Combine Wet and Dry Mixtures
Create a well in the center of the dry ingredients, then pour in the wet mixture. Using a spatula or wooden spoon, gently fold the ingredients together until no large clumps of flour remain. Be careful not to overmix; stop once the batter is smooth and combined.

Step 6: Transfer to Cake Pan
Pour the batter into the prepared cake pan, spreading it out evenly with your spatula. If desired, sprinkle turbinado sugar or chopped nuts on top for added texture and crunch.

Step 7: Bake
Place the cake in the preheated oven and bake for 45–50 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean. If the top of the cake begins to brown too quickly, lightly cover it with aluminum foil during the last 10 minutes of baking.

Step 8: Cool and Serve
Once baked, remove the cake from the oven and allow it to cool in the pan for about 10 minutes. Then transfer it to a wire rack and let it cool completely before slicing. This step ensures the cake sets properly and retains its structure.

Pro Tips for Enhancing Your Cake

  1. Adjust the Spices: If you’re a fan of bolder flavors, consider adding a pinch of ground cloves or increasing the cinnamon for a more pronounced spice profile.
  2. Serve it Warm: Pair a slice with vegan whipped cream or a scoop of dairy-free vanilla ice cream for an indulgent dessert.
  3. Add Texture: If you enjoy extra crunch, fold in a handful of chopped walnuts or pecans into the batter before baking.
  4. Make it Gluten-Free: Substitute the all-purpose flour with a gluten-free 1:1 baking flour for a gluten-free option.

Why You’ll Love This Cake

The combination of spiced warmth and juicy pears makes this vegan spiced pear cake a comforting dessert for any occasion. Its moist texture ensures every bite is satisfying, while the natural sweetness from the pears and applesauce provides a wholesome feel. Best of all, this recipe is entirely plant-based—you won’t find any dairy or eggs here—making it a perfect option for vegan households or anyone looking to try something new in their baking routine.

Whether you’re a seasoned baker or experimenting with vegan desserts for the first time, this cake is sure to delight your taste buds. With minimal effort and maximum flavor, you’ll find that this spiced pear cake is a regular addition to your dessert lineup.

If you’re ready to embrace seasonal flavors and treat yourself to a slice of moist, flavorful cake, there’s no need to wait. Gather your ingredients and start baking today—you’ll be glad you did. Happy baking!
