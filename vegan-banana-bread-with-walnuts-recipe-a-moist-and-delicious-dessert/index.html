Vegan Banana Bread with Walnuts Recipe: A Moist and Delicious Dessert


Are you looking for a delectable vegan dessert that will satisfy your sweet cravings and leave you wanting more? Look no further than this Vegan Banana Bread with Walnuts recipe. This moist and flavorful treat is perfect for any occasion, whether you’re hosting a brunch, enjoying a leisurely afternoon, or simply craving a delightful homemade dessert.

Ingredients

  • 3 ripe bananas, mashed
  • 1/3 cup melted coconut oil
  • 1/2 cup organic brown sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • A pinch of salt
  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup chopped walnuts

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C). Lightly grease a 9x5-inch loaf pan.

  2. In a large mixing bowl, mash the ripe bananas with a fork until smooth. Add the melted coconut oil, organic brown sugar, and vanilla extract. Mix well.

  3. Sprinkle the baking soda and salt over the banana mixture and gently stir until combined.

  4. Add the all-purpose flour to the mixture and stir until just combined. Be careful not to overmix, as this can result in a dense banana bread.

  5. Gently fold in the chopped walnuts, reserving a few for sprinkling on top.

  6. Pour the batter into the prepared loaf pan and spread it out evenly. Sprinkle the remaining chopped walnuts over the top of the batter.

  7. Bake in the preheated oven for 55-60 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.

  8. Once baked, remove the banana bread from the oven and allow it to cool in the pan for 10 minutes. Then, transfer it to a wire rack to cool completely before slicing.

Serving Suggestions

This Vegan Banana Bread with Walnuts is a versatile dessert that can be enjoyed in various ways. Serve it warm with a dollop of vegan whipped cream or a drizzle of maple syrup for a delightful indulgence. Alternatively, pair it with a cup of hot tea or coffee for a cozy and satisfying treat.

Tips and Variations

  • For an extra burst of flavor, consider adding a teaspoon of ground cinnamon or a dash of nutmeg to the batter.
  • If you prefer a sweeter banana bread, increase the amount of brown sugar according to your taste preferences.
  • Experiment with different mix-ins such as dairy-free chocolate chips, dried fruits, or shredded coconut to customize the recipe to your liking.

Final Thoughts

In conclusion, this Vegan Banana Bread with Walnuts recipe is a delightful dessert that embodies the perfect balance of moistness, sweetness, and nutty goodness. Whether you’re a vegan, a baking enthusiast, or simply someone with a sweet tooth, this recipe is sure to become a favorite in your kitchen. Embrace the simplicity and wholesome flavors of this delectable banana bread, and treat yourself to a slice of pure delight.

So, what are you waiting for? Gather your ingredients, preheat your oven, and embark on a culinary journey to create this irresistible Vegan Banana Bread with Walnuts. Your taste buds will thank you!
