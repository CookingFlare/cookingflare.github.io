Homemade Easter Candies and Confections to Indulge In


Easter, a time for family, celebration, and sweets! Indulge in these delightful homemade Easter candies and confections that are sure to impress your loved ones. From classic chocolate treats to unique and modern creations, there’s something for everyone.

Chocolate-Covered Marshmallow Eggs

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup of marshmallows
  • 2 cups of milk chocolate chips
  • 1 tablespoon of vegetable shortening

Directions:

  1. Start by creating a double boiler. Fill a large pot halfway with water and place a heatproof bowl on top. Ensure that the bowl rests on the pot without touching the water.
  2. In the heatproof bowl, combine the marshmallows and milk chocolate chips.
  3. Heat the water in the pot over medium heat until it simmers, then slowly stir the mixture until the chocolate and marshmallows are completely melted.
  4. Remove the bowl from the heat and stir in the vegetable shortening to help thicken the chocolate.
  5. Let the mixture cool slightly, then use a melon baller or spoon to scoop mixture and form eggs.
  6. Chill for about 30 minutes, then dip in melted chocolate and add decorations like sprinkles, coconut, or crushed nuts.
  7. Allow the chocolate to cool and harden before serving.

Coconut-Laced Easter Bark

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups of milk chocolate chips
  • 1 cup of sweetened coconut flakes
  • 1/2 cup of chopped dried apricots
  • 1/2 cup of chopped almonds

Directions:

  1. Line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
  2. In a microwave-safe bowl or using a double boiler, melt the milk chocolate chips, stirring until smooth.
  3. On the prepared baking sheet, spread the melted chocolate to form a rectangular shape, about 1/8 inch thick.
  4. To the melted chocolate, add the coconut flakes, dried apricots, and almonds, pressing them gently into the chocolate to ensure they stay in place.
  5. Refrigerate for about 1 hour, or until the chocolate is firm.
  6. Wearing gloves, break the bark into shards and serve.

Peeps-Infused Bunny Cookies

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup of butter, softened
  • 1 cup of powdered sugar
  • 2 teaspoons of vanilla extract
  • 3 cups of all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon of baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon of salt
  • 2 cups of colored marshmallow Peeps

Directions:

  1. In a large bowl, cream the softened butter and powdered sugar together until fluffy.
  2. Add the vanilla extract and mix well.
  3. In a separate bowl, whisk together the all-purpose flour, baking powder, and salt.
  4. Gradually blend the dry ingredients into the wet ingredients, being sure not to overmix.
  5. Wrap the dough in plastic wrap and refrigerate for about 1 hour.
  6. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C) and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
  7. Roll the chilled dough into small balls, about 1-inch in diameter, then flatten them slightly with your hand, leaving an indentation for the Peeps.
  8. Place the cookies onto the prepared baking sheet and press 1-2 Peeps into the center of each cookie.
  9. Bake for about 10-12 minutes, then remove and let cool completely on a wire rack.
  10. Serve and enjoy the delightful combination of fluffy Peeps and tender cookies.

Homemade Easter candies and confections add a personal touch to your holiday celebrations. Fill the Easter baskets of your loved ones with these delightful treats and create lasting memories. Happy baking!
