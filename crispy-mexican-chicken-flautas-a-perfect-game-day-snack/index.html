Crispy Mexican Chicken Flautas: A Perfect Game Day Snack


If you’re looking for a crowd-pleasing snack to enjoy during game day or any casual get-together, look no further than these delicious Crispy Mexican Chicken Flautas. These flavor-packed rolled tacos are the perfect combination of crispy, savory, and satisfying. Whether you’re a fan of Mexican cuisine or simply enjoy a good finger food, this recipe is sure to become a favorite in your household.

Ingredients

For the Filling:

  • 2 cups shredded cooked chicken
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon chili powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon paprika
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1/2 cup chicken broth
  • 1 cup shredded cheese (cheddar or Monterey Jack)

For the Assembly:

  • 12-16 corn tortillas
  • Vegetable oil, for frying

For Serving:

  • Sour cream
  • Salsa
  • Guacamole
  • Chopped fresh cilantro
  • Lime wedges

Instructions

Step 1: Prepare the Filling

  1. In a skillet, sauté the chopped onion and garlic until softened.
  2. Add the shredded chicken, cumin, chili powder, paprika, salt, and pepper. Stir to combine.
  3. Pour in the chicken broth and simmer until the liquid has reduced and the mixture is well seasoned.
  4. Remove the skillet from the heat and stir in the shredded cheese until melted and well mixed.

Step 2: Assemble the Flautas

  1. Warm the corn tortillas in a dry skillet until soft and pliable.
  2. Spoon a generous portion of the chicken filling onto each tortilla and roll it tightly.
  3. Secure the flautas with toothpicks to prevent them from unraveling during frying.

Step 3: Fry the Flautas

  1. In a deep skillet or frying pan, heat about 1 inch of vegetable oil over medium-high heat.
  2. Carefully place the rolled flautas in the hot oil, seam side down.
  3. Fry the flautas in batches, turning them occasionally, until they are golden brown and crispy all over.
  4. Transfer the fried flautas to a paper towel-lined plate to drain any excess oil.

Step 4: Serve and Enjoy

  1. Once all the flautas are fried, remove the toothpicks and arrange them on a serving platter.
  2. Serve the crispy flautas with sides of sour cream, salsa, guacamole, chopped cilantro, and lime wedges for squeezing.
  3. Encourage your guests to dip the flautas in the accompanying sauces and garnishes for a burst of flavor in every bite.

Tips and Variations

  • For a vegetarian option, substitute the chicken with sautéed vegetables and black beans.
  • Make a large batch of flautas and freeze them before frying. When ready to enjoy, simply fry them from frozen for a quick and convenient snack.
  • Experiment with different cheese blends or add a spicy kick with diced jalapeños in the filling.

Enjoy the crispy goodness of these Mexican Chicken Flautas while cheering on your favorite team or simply savoring a flavorful snack. This recipe is versatile, easy to customize, and guaranteed to be a hit with friends and family. So, roll up your sleeves and get ready to indulge in the irresistible crunch of these delectable flautas!
