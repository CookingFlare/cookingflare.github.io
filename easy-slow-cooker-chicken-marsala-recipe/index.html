Easy Slow Cooker Chicken Marsala Recipe


If you’re looking for a delicious and hassle-free dinner option, then this easy slow cooker Chicken Marsala recipe is a must-try. The slow cooker does all the work for you, infusing the chicken with rich flavors and ensuring a tender, succulent result. This classic Italian dish is perfect for cozy family dinners or entertaining guests, and the best part is that it requires minimal effort on your part. Let’s dive into the recipe and discover how to create this mouthwatering Chicken Marsala in your own kitchen.

Ingredients:

  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 8 ounces sliced mushrooms
  • 1 cup Marsala wine
  • 1 cup chicken broth
  • 2 tablespoons unsalted butter
  • Fresh parsley for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Begin by preparing the chicken breasts. In a shallow dish, combine the all-purpose flour, salt, pepper, dried oregano, and dried thyme. Dredge the chicken breasts in the seasoned flour mixture, ensuring they are evenly coated.

  2. Heat the olive oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat. Once the oil is hot, add the chicken breasts and cook until they are golden brown on both sides, but not fully cooked through. This should take about 3-4 minutes per side. Transfer the partially cooked chicken to the slow cooker.

  3. In the same skillet, add the sliced mushrooms and sauté them until they are golden and tender. This should take around 5 minutes. Transfer the mushrooms to the slow cooker, arranging them around the chicken breasts.

  4. Pour the Marsala wine and chicken broth into the skillet, using a wooden spoon to scrape up any browned bits from the bottom of the pan. Let the mixture simmer for a few minutes to reduce slightly, then pour it over the chicken and mushrooms in the slow cooker.

  5. Cover the slow cooker and cook the Chicken Marsala on low for 4-5 hours, or until the chicken is cooked through and tender. Once the cooking time is complete, transfer the chicken and mushrooms to a serving platter and cover them to keep warm.

  6. In a small saucepan, melt the unsalted butter over medium heat. Once melted, pour the butter over the chicken and mushrooms in the serving platter.

  7. Garnish the Chicken Marsala with fresh parsley and serve it hot, accompanied by your favorite side dishes such as mashed potatoes, pasta, or steamed vegetables.

Tips for Success:

  • For a deeper flavor, consider adding a splash of balsamic vinegar to the Marsala wine and chicken broth mixture.
  • If you prefer a thicker sauce, you can create a slurry by mixing a tablespoon of cornstarch with a tablespoon of cold water, then adding it to the slow cooker during the last 30 minutes of cooking.
  • Ensure the chicken breasts are similar in size to ensure even cooking in the slow cooker.
  • Feel free to customize the dish by adding ingredients such as shallots, garlic, or fresh herbs to the slow cooker for additional flavor.

Conclusion:

With its rich, savory flavors and tender chicken, this easy slow cooker Chicken Marsala recipe is sure to become a favorite in your household. The convenience of the slow cooker makes it a perfect option for busy weeknights or when you simply want to enjoy a delicious homemade meal without spending hours in the kitchen. Give this recipe a try and savor the delightful taste of Chicken Marsala with minimal effort and maximum flavor.

So, what are you waiting for? Gather your ingredients, set up your slow cooker, and get ready to indulge in a delightful culinary experience with this easy slow cooker Chicken Marsala recipe!
