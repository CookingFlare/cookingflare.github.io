Roasted Garlic and Rosemary Mashed Cauliflower: A Healthy Fall Side

3 min · 536 words

As the weather starts to cool down and the leaves begin to change colors, it’s the perfect time to cozy up with some comforting and wholesome fall dishes. One such dish that captures the essence of fall is Roasted Garlic and Rosemary Mashed Cauliflower. This delightful and healthy side dish is a great alternative to traditional mashed potatoes, offering a lighter and more nutritious option for your autumn meals.

Ingredients

  • 1 head of cauliflower, chopped into florets
  • 4 cloves of garlic, peeled
  • 2 tablespoons of olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon of fresh rosemary, finely chopped
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1/4 cup of low-fat milk or vegetable broth
  • 2 tablespoons of grated Parmesan cheese (optional)

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C).

  2. In a baking dish, toss the cauliflower florets and peeled garlic cloves with olive oil, chopped rosemary, salt, and pepper until well coated.

  3. Roast the cauliflower and garlic in the preheated oven for 25-30 minutes, or until the cauliflower is tender and lightly browned, stirring occasionally.

  4. Once roasted, remove the cauliflower and garlic from the oven and let them cool for a few minutes.

  5. In a food processor or blender, combine the roasted cauliflower, garlic, and milk or vegetable broth. Blend until smooth and creamy. You may need to scrape down the sides of the processor or blender to ensure everything is well combined.

  6. If desired, stir in the grated Parmesan cheese for an extra flavor boost.

  7. Season the mashed cauliflower with additional salt and pepper to taste.

  8. Transfer the mashed cauliflower to a serving dish and garnish with a sprig of fresh rosemary for a pop of color.

Health Benefits

Mashed cauliflower is a fantastic alternative to mashed potatoes for those looking to cut back on carbohydrates or add more vegetables to their diet. Cauliflower is rich in nutrients such as vitamin C, vitamin K, and fiber, making it a nutritious choice for a fall side dish. The addition of roasted garlic not only imparts a deliciously mellow flavor but also brings its immune-boosting properties to the table. Rosemary, with its aromatic and earthy notes, not only enhances the flavor of the dish but also offers antioxidants and anti-inflammatory benefits.

Serving Suggestions

This Roasted Garlic and Rosemary Mashed Cauliflower pairs beautifully with a variety of fall main dishes such as roasted chicken, grilled salmon, or a hearty stew. It can also be a wonderful addition to your Thanksgiving or holiday spread, bringing a touch of elegance and healthfulness to the table.

As you savor the creamy texture and savory flavors of this mashed cauliflower, you’ll find yourself embracing the essence of fall with every bite. So, why not elevate your autumn meals with this nutritious and delightful side dish? Let the comforting aroma of roasted garlic and rosemary fill your kitchen, and enjoy the wholesome goodness of this healthy fall favorite.
