Easy Christmas Cookies: Simple Recipes for Festive Baking


‘Tis the season to be jolly and indulge in the sweet delights of Christmas cookies! This festive time of year calls for delicious treats that bring joy to our taste buds and warm our hearts. Whether you’re an experienced baker or a novice in the kitchen, these easy Christmas cookie recipes are sure to impress your family, friends, and even Santa himself. So, grab your apron and get ready to spread some holiday cheer with these simple and delightful recipes!

1. Classic Sugar Cookies

Prep Time: 20 minutes Cook Time: 10 minutes Total Time: 30 minutes Yield: 24 cookies

Ingredients:

  • 2 3/4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1 1/2 cups white sugar
  • 1 egg
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C). Line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
  2. In a medium bowl, whisk together the flour, baking soda, and baking powder. Set aside.
  3. In a large bowl, cream together the softened butter and sugar until light and fluffy. Beat in the egg and vanilla extract.
  4. Gradually add the dry ingredients to the wet ingredients, mixing until well combined.
  5. Roll out the dough on a lightly floured surface to about 1/4 inch thickness. Use Christmas-themed cookie cutters to cut out desired shapes.
  6. Place the cookies onto the prepared baking sheet and bake for 8 to 10 minutes, or until lightly golden around the edges.
  7. Allow the cookies to cool on a wire rack before decorating with icing, sprinkles, or other festive toppings.

2. Peppermint Chocolate Chip Cookies

Prep Time: 15 minutes Cook Time: 12 minutes Total Time: 27 minutes Yield: 36 cookies

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 cup brown sugar, packed
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 2 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup semi-sweet chocolate chips
  • 1 cup crushed peppermint candies or candy canes

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C). Line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
  2. In a large bowl, cream together the softened butter, granulated sugar, and brown sugar until light and fluffy. Beat in the eggs, one at a time, followed by the vanilla extract.
  3. In a separate bowl, whisk together the flour, baking soda, and salt. Gradually add the dry ingredients to the wet ingredients, mixing until just combined.
  4. Stir in the chocolate chips and crushed peppermint candies.
  5. Drop rounded tablespoons of dough onto the prepared baking sheet, spacing them about 2 inches apart.
  6. Bake for 10 to 12 minutes, or until golden brown around the edges.
  7. Allow the cookies to cool on the baking sheet for a few minutes before transferring them to a wire rack to cool completely.

3. Gingerbread Cookies

Prep Time: 30 minutes Cook Time: 10 minutes Total Time: 40 minutes Yield: 24 cookies

Ingredients:

  • 3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons ground ginger
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons ground cinnamon
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cloves
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 3/4 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 3/4 cup packed brown sugar
  • 1/2 cup molasses
  • 1 large egg

Instructions:

  1. In a medium bowl, whisk together the flour, ginger, cinnamon, cloves, baking soda, and salt. Set aside.
  2. In a large bowl, cream together the softened butter and brown sugar until light and fluffy. Beat in the molasses and egg until well combined.
  3. Gradually add the dry ingredients to the wet ingredients, mixing until a dough forms.
  4. Divide the dough in half and wrap each portion in plastic wrap. Refrigerate for at least 1 hour, or until firm.
  5. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C). Line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
  6. On a lightly floured surface, roll out the dough to about 1/4 inch thickness. Use gingerbread-shaped cookie cutters to cut out the cookies.
  7. Place the cookies onto the prepared baking sheet and bake for 8 to 10 minutes, or until firm to the touch.
  8. Allow the cookies to cool on a wire rack before decorating with icing, candy, or other decorative elements.

Spread the Christmas Cheer!

These easy Christmas cookie recipes are perfect for creating memorable moments in the kitchen during the holiday season. From the classic sugar cookies that can be decorated in countless ways to the delightful combination of peppermint and chocolate in the peppermint chocolate chip cookies, and the warm and spicy flavors of the gingerbread cookies, these recipes are sure to become family favorites.

So, gather your loved ones, put on some holiday music, and let the sweet aromas of freshly baked cookies fill your home. Whether you’re baking as a gift, for a holiday gathering, or simply to enjoy with a warm cup of cocoa, these easy Christmas cookies are a delicious way to celebrate the most wonderful time of the year. Happy baking and Merry Christmas!
