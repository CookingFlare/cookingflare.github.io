Lemon Poppy Seed Loaf: A Zesty and Moist Dessert


If you’re looking for a refreshing and flavorful dessert that will leave your taste buds tingling, look no further than this Lemon Poppy Seed Loaf. Bursting with the zesty tang of lemons and the subtle crunch of poppy seeds, this moist and delectable treat is sure to impress your family and friends. Plus, it’s incredibly easy to make!

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 ½ teaspoons baking powder
  • ½ teaspoon baking soda
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • 1 tablespoon poppy seeds
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • ½ cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 tablespoon lemon zest
  • ½ cup fresh lemon juice
  • ½ cup buttermilk

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease and flour a standard-sized loaf pan.

  2. In a medium bowl, whisk together the flour, baking powder, baking soda, salt, and poppy seeds. Set aside.

  3. In a large mixing bowl, cream together the granulated sugar and softened butter until light and fluffy. This step can be done using an electric mixer or by hand.

  4. Beat in the eggs, one at a time, ensuring each is fully incorporated before adding the next.

  5. Stir in the vanilla extract, lemon zest, and lemon juice. The mixture may appear slightly curdled, but don’t worry, this is normal.

  6. Gradually add the dry ingredients to the wet ingredients, alternating with buttermilk. Begin and end with the dry ingredients, mixing until just combined. Be careful not to overmix, as this can result in a dense loaf.

  7. Pour the batter into the prepared loaf pan, smoothing the top with a spatula for an even finish.

  8. Bake in the preheated oven for approximately 50-60 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center of the loaf comes out clean.

  9. Once baked, remove the loaf from the oven and allow it to cool in the pan for about 10 minutes. Then, transfer the loaf to a wire rack to cool completely.

Serving Suggestions

This Lemon Poppy Seed Loaf is delicious on its own, but you can take it to the next level with a simple glaze or icing. Here are a few ideas to consider:

  • Lemon Glaze: Whisk together powdered sugar and fresh lemon juice until you achieve a pourable consistency. Drizzle the glaze over the cooled loaf for an extra burst of citrus flavor.

  • Cream Cheese Frosting: Beat together cream cheese, powdered sugar, and a splash of lemon juice until smooth. Spread the frosting generously over the loaf for a creamy and tangy twist.

  • Fresh Berries: Serve each slice of Lemon Poppy Seed Loaf with a handful of fresh berries, such as raspberries or blueberries, for a pop of color and added fruity goodness.

Conclusion

With its vibrant lemon flavor, delicate texture, and delightful poppy seed crunch, this Lemon Poppy Seed Loaf is the perfect dessert for any occasion. Whether you’re hosting a brunch, celebrating a special event, or simply craving a sweet treat, this recipe is sure to satisfy. So grab your ingredients and get baking – your taste buds will thank you!
