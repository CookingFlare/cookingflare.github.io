Easy Italian Recipes: Delicious Meals for Busy Weeknights


Are you craving a taste of Italy but short on time? Look no further! This post is filled with quick, delicious, and authentic Italian recipes that will delight your taste buds and satisfy your hunger. Impress your family and friends with these easy-to-make dishes that require minimal ingredients and preparation.

1. Spaghetti alla Carbonara

Prep Time: 10 minutes
Cook Time: 15 minutes
Servings: 4

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound spaghetti
  • 4 large eggs
  • 1 cup chopped pancetta or bacon
  • 1/2 cup grated Pecorino Romano cheese
  • Salt
  • Ground black pepper

Directions:

  1. Cook spaghetti according to package instructions.
  2. In a large bowl, whisk together eggs, cheese, salt, and black pepper.
  3. In a large skillet, cook pancetta or bacon until crispy. Remove from heat.
  4. When spaghetti is done, drain and add to the skillet with the pancetta or bacon.
  5. Immediately add the egg mixture and toss quickly to combine. Serve hot.

2. Margherita Pizza

Prep Time: 10 minutes
Cook Time: 10 minutes
Servings: 2

Ingredients:

  • 1 pre-made pizza dough
  • 1 cup prepared marinara sauce
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/4 cup fresh basil leaves
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese

Directions:

  1. Preheat oven to 450°F (232°C).
  2. Roll out pizza dough and place on a baking sheet.
  3. Evenly spread marinara sauce over the dough, leaving a small border around the edges.
  4. Sprinkle mozzarella cheese over the sauce, followed by the fresh basil leaves.
  5. Bake for 10-12 minutes, or until the crust is golden and the cheese is melted.
  6. Remove from oven and sprinkle with Parmesan cheese. Serve hot.

3. Chicken Parmesan

Prep Time: 15 minutes
Cook Time: 30 minutes
Servings: 4

Ingredients:

  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/2 cup breadcrumbs
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • Salt and ground black pepper
  • 1/2 cup olive oil
  • 1/2 cup marinara sauce
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese

Directions:

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Season chicken with salt and pepper. Dredge in flour, then dip in eggs, and finally coat with breadcrumbs mixed with Parmesan cheese.
  3. Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Cook chicken on both sides until golden brown. Remove from skillet and place on a baking sheet.
  4. Bake in the oven for 20-25 minutes, or until the chicken is cooked through.
  5. Spoon marinara sauce over the chicken and top with mozzarella cheese. Return to the oven for 5 minutes, or until the cheese is melted.
  6. Serve hot with a side of pasta or vegetables.

4. Minestrone Soup

Prep Time: 15 minutes
Cook Time: 30 minutes
Servings: 6

Ingredients:

  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 carrots, chopped
  • 2 celery stalks, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 6 cups vegetable broth
  • 1 can (14.5 ounces) crushed tomatoes
  • 1 can (15 ounces) cannelloni beans, drained and rinsed
  • 1 cup small pasta (such as ditalini)
  • 2 cups fresh spinach leaves
  • Salt and ground black pepper

Directions:

  1. In a large pot, heat olive oil over medium heat. Add onion, carrots, and celery, cooking until softened.
  2. Add garlic and cook for an additional 1-2 minutes.
  3. Pour in vegetable broth, crushed tomatoes, and cannelloni beans. Bring to a boil.
  4. Add small pasta and cook for 8-10 minutes, or until the pasta is al dente.
  5. Stir in fresh spinach leaves and reduce heat to a simmer. Cook for an additional 5-10 minutes, or until the spinach is wilted.
  6. Season with salt and ground black pepper to taste. Serve hot, accompanied by crusty bread.

Enjoy these easy-to-make Italian dishes that will have you dining like a true Italian in no time! While they may not be traditional recipes, they are a great way to get that Italian flavor without hours of preparation. Buon appetito!
