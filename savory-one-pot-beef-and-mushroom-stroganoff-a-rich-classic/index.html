Savory One-Pot Beef and Mushroom Stroganoff: A Rich Classic


Are you in the mood for a hearty and comforting meal that will satisfy your cravings? Look no further than this Savory One-Pot Beef and Mushroom Stroganoff recipe! This classic dish is packed with flavors that will transport you to a cozy evening by the fireplace. Best of all, it can be prepared in just one pot, making clean-up a breeze.

Ingredients

  • 1.5 pounds of beef stew meat, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 8 ounces of mushrooms, sliced
  • 2 tablespoons of butter
  • 2 tablespoons of all-purpose flour
  • 2 cups of beef broth
  • 1 cup of sour cream
  • 1 tablespoon of Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 teaspoon of Dijon mustard
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • Fresh parsley, chopped (for garnish)
  • Egg noodles or rice (for serving)

Instructions

  1. In a large pot or Dutch oven, melt the butter over medium heat. Add the beef stew meat and cook until browned on all sides. Remove the beef from the pot and set aside.

  2. In the same pot, add the chopped onion and minced garlic. Sauté until the onion becomes translucent and the garlic is fragrant.

  3. Add the sliced mushrooms to the pot and cook until they release their moisture and start to brown.

  4. Sprinkle the flour over the mushrooms, onions, and garlic, and stir to coat everything evenly. Cook for an additional minute to cook out the raw flour taste.

  5. Slowly pour in the beef broth while stirring constantly to avoid any lumps. Bring the mixture to a simmer and cook until the sauce thickens slightly.

  6. Return the beef to the pot and add the Worcestershire sauce and Dijon mustard. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Stir everything together.

  7. Reduce the heat to low and cover the pot. Let the stroganoff simmer for about 1.5 to 2 hours, or until the beef is tender and the flavors have melded together.

  8. Just before serving, stir in the sour cream until well combined. Taste and adjust the seasoning if needed.

  9. Serve the Savory One-Pot Beef and Mushroom Stroganoff over egg noodles or rice. Garnish with fresh chopped parsley for a pop of color and added freshness.

Tips and Variations

  • For a creamier stroganoff, you can add more sour cream or even some heavy cream. Adjust the amount according to your preference.
  • If you’re short on time, you can use pre-sliced mushrooms to save some prep work.
  • This recipe can easily be adapted to vegetarian or vegan diets by substituting the beef stew meat with plant-based protein alternatives and using vegetable broth instead of beef broth.
  • Feel free to experiment with different types of mushrooms for added depth of flavor. Cremini, shiitake, or portobello mushrooms are all excellent choices.

There you have it – a delicious and satisfying Savory One-Pot Beef and Mushroom Stroganoff recipe that will become a family favorite. The rich flavors and tender beef will make this dish a standout at any dinner table. Give it a try and let your taste buds be delighted!
