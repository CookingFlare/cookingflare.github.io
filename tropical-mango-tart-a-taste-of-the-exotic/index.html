Tropical Mango Tart: A Taste of the Exotic


In a medium bowl, combine the graham cracker crumbs and melted butter. Mix well until the crumbs are evenly coated. Press the mixture onto the bottom and up the sides of a 9-inch tart pan. Place the crust in the refrigerator to chill while you prepare the filling.

  1. Make the Mango Filling

    In a small saucepan, combine the diced mangoes, granulated sugar, cornstarch, water, and lime juice. Cook over medium heat, stirring constantly, until the mixture thickens and the mangoes are soft, about 10 minutes. Remove from heat and let it cool completely.

  2. Prepare the Whipped Cream

    In a mixing bowl, combine the heavy cream, powdered sugar, and vanilla extract. Using an electric mixer, beat the mixture on high speed until stiff peaks form.

  3. Assemble the Tart

    Take the tart crust out of the refrigerator and spread the mango filling evenly over the crust. Top it with the prepared whipped cream, spreading it out to cover the mango filling completely. Sprinkle with toasted coconut flakes if desired.

  4. Chill and Serve

    Place the tart in the refrigerator and let it chill for at least 4 hours or overnight. This will allow the flavors to meld together and the tart to set. When ready to serve, remove from the refrigerator, slice, and enjoy!

Serving Suggestions

  • Serve the Tropical Mango Tart chilled for a refreshing dessert on a hot summer day.
  • Garnish with fresh mint leaves or additional diced mangoes for an extra pop of color and flavor.
  • Pair with a scoop of coconut or vanilla ice cream to create a truly indulgent treat.

Why You’ll Love This Tropical Mango Tart

Indulge your taste buds with this exotic and refreshing Tropical Mango Tart. Bursting with the vibrant flavors of ripe mangoes and zesty lime, this dessert is like a tropical getaway in every bite. The combination of the buttery graham cracker crust, sweet mango filling, and fluffy whipped cream creates a symphony of textures and tastes that will leave you craving for more.

What makes this tart truly special is the use of fresh, ripe mangoes. Their natural sweetness shines through, complemented by a hint of tanginess from the lime juice. The creamy whipped cream adds a lightness to the tart, balancing out the richness of the mango filling. And if you’re a fan of coconut, the toasted coconut flakes on top add a delightful crunch and tropical flair.

Not only is this Tropical Mango Tart a feast for the taste buds, but it’s also a feast for the eyes. The vibrant yellow hue of the mango filling against the creamy white whipped cream creates a visually stunning dessert that is sure to impress your family and friends.

Whether you’re hosting a summer barbecue, celebrating a special occasion, or simply craving a taste of the exotic, this Tropical Mango Tart is the perfect dessert to elevate any meal. So go ahead, transport yourself to a tropical paradise with every delicious bite of this delightful tart.
