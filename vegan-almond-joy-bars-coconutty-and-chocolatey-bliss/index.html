Vegan Almond Joy Bars: Coconutty and Chocolatey Bliss


As the sun sets on a warm summer evening, there’s nothing quite like indulging in a sweet treat that’s both refreshing and satisfying. For those who follow a vegan lifestyle, finding a delicious and guilt-free dessert option can be a challenge. But fear not, dear readers, for today we’re going to dive into the world of vegan baking and create a dessert that’s sure to become a new favorite: Vegan Almond Joy Bars.

These chewy, coconutty, and chocolatey bars are a game-changer for anyone looking for a plant-based alternative to traditional treats. With just a few simple ingredients, you’ll be able to create a batch of bars that are not only vegan but also gluten-free and refined sugar-free.

Ingredients

Before we dive into the recipe, let’s take a look at the ingredients you’ll need:

  • 1 1/2 cups rolled oats
  • 1 cup unsweetened shredded coconut
  • 1/2 cup almond butter
  • 1/4 cup maple syrup
  • 1/4 cup coconut oil
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup dairy-free chocolate chips
  • 1 cup chopped almonds
  • 1 tablespoon coconut flakes

Instructions

Now that we have our ingredients, let’s move on to the instructions. These bars are surprisingly easy to make, and with just a few simple steps, you’ll be enjoying your delicious treats in no time.

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (180°C). Line an 8x8 inch baking dish with parchment paper.
  2. In a large mixing bowl, combine the oats, coconut, almond butter, maple syrup, coconut oil, vanilla extract, and salt. Mix until well combined.
  3. Stir in the dairy-free chocolate chips and chopped almonds until they’re evenly distributed throughout the mixture.
  4. Press the mixture into the prepared baking dish.
  5. Bake for 20-25 minutes, or until the edges are lightly golden brown.
  6. Remove the bars from the oven and let them cool completely.
  7. Once the bars have cooled, melt the coconut flakes in a small saucepan over low heat. Stir until smooth and creamy.
  8. Pour the melted coconut flakes over the bars and spread evenly.
  9. Refrigerate the bars for at least 30 minutes to allow the coconut flakes to set.
  10. Cut the bars into desired size and serve.

Tips and Variations

As with any recipe, there are always ways to customize and make it your own. Here are a few tips and variations to consider:

  • If you prefer a chewier bar, bake for 15-20 minutes. If you prefer a firmer bar, bake for 25-30 minutes.
  • You can also add a sprinkle of sea salt on top of the bars before baking for an extra burst of flavor.
  • If you’re not a fan of coconut, you can substitute it with chopped nuts or seeds.
  • You can also use different types of non-dairy milk instead of coconut oil to create a creamier texture.

Conclusion

And there you have it, dear readers, a delicious and easy-to-make vegan dessert recipe that’s sure to satisfy your sweet tooth. With its combination of chewy oats, crunchy coconut, and rich chocolate, these bars are the perfect treat for anyone looking for a guilt-free indulgence. So go ahead, give them a try, and let me know what you think!
