Thai Green Curry Recipe with Chicken and Vegetables


If you’re looking to add a burst of flavor to your dinner repertoire, Thai green curry is a perfect choice. This aromatic and creamy dish is a staple in Thai cuisine, known for its balance of spicy, sweet, and savory flavors. In this recipe, we’ll guide you through creating an authentic Thai green curry with tender chicken and an assortment of fresh vegetables. Let’s dive into the culinary delights of Thailand and bring the vibrant flavors to your kitchen!

Ingredients:

For the Green Curry Paste:

  • 4-6 green Thai chilies (depending on your spice preference)
  • 1 shallot, chopped
  • 4 cloves of garlic
  • 1 stalk of lemongrass, thinly sliced
  • 1 thumb-sized piece of galangal, chopped
  • 1 teaspoon of coriander seeds
  • 1 teaspoon of cumin seeds
  • A handful of fresh cilantro roots and stems
  • 1 teaspoon of shrimp paste
  • Zest of 1 lime
  • 1 tablespoon of fish sauce
  • 1 tablespoon of vegetable oil

For the Curry:

  • 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts, thinly sliced
  • 1 can (14 oz) of coconut milk
  • 1 cup of chicken stock
  • 1 red bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 small eggplant, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 1 cup of bamboo shoots
  • 2 kaffir lime leaves
  • 1 tablespoon of palm sugar
  • Fish sauce, to taste
  • Fresh Thai basil leaves, for garnish
  • Steamed jasmine rice, to serve

Instructions:

Step 1: Prepare the Green Curry Paste

  1. In a mortar and pestle or a food processor, combine the green Thai chilies, shallot, garlic, lemongrass, galangal, coriander seeds, cumin seeds, cilantro roots and stems, shrimp paste, and lime zest.
  2. Grind or process the ingredients into a smooth paste.
  3. Add the fish sauce and vegetable oil to the paste and mix well. Set the green curry paste aside.

Step 2: Cook the Curry:

  1. In a large pan or wok, heat some vegetable oil over medium heat.
  2. Add the green curry paste to the pan and sauté for a few minutes until fragrant.
  3. Add the sliced chicken to the pan and cook until it’s no longer pink on the outside.
  4. Pour in the coconut milk and chicken stock, stirring to combine.
  5. Bring the curry to a gentle simmer and let it cook for about 10 minutes, allowing the flavors to meld together.
  6. Add the red bell pepper, eggplant, bamboo shoots, kaffir lime leaves, and palm sugar to the curry. Let it simmer for another 5-7 minutes until the vegetables are tender.
  7. Season the curry with fish sauce to taste.

Step 3: Serve and Enjoy:

  1. Once the curry is ready, remove it from the heat.
  2. Ladle the fragrant Thai green curry into serving bowls.
  3. Garnish with fresh Thai basil leaves for an aromatic finish.
  4. Serve the curry hot alongside steamed jasmine rice, allowing the creamy sauce to mingle with the fluffy grains.

Embrace the Flavors of Thailand

With this delightful Thai green curry recipe, you can transport your taste buds to the bustling streets of Thailand. The aromatic blend of herbs, spices, and creamy coconut milk creates a harmony of flavors that will leave you craving more. Whether you’re a fan of bold spices or prefer a milder kick, this dish can be tailored to suit your palate. So, gather your ingredients, embrace the culinary adventure, and savor the rich and vibrant flavors of authentic Thai green curry!
