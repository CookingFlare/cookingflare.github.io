Crispy Baked Sweet Potato Fries for a Healthy Snack


If you’re looking for a guilt-free and delicious snack, look no further than crispy baked sweet potato fries. These fries are not only a healthier alternative to traditional fries, but they are also incredibly easy to make and packed with flavor. Whether you’re craving a savory snack or looking for a side dish to accompany your meal, these crispy baked sweet potato fries are sure to satisfy your taste buds.

Ingredients

  • 2 large sweet potatoes
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon onion powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper

Instructions

Step 1: Preheat the Oven

Preheat your oven to 425°F (220°C) and line a baking sheet with parchment paper. This will ensure that the sweet potato fries cook evenly and become crispy without sticking to the pan.

Step 2: Prepare the Sweet Potatoes

Wash the sweet potatoes thoroughly and pat them dry with a paper towel. Then, using a sharp knife, carefully cut the sweet potatoes into evenly-sized fries. For a crispier texture, leave the skin on, as it adds a nice crunch to the fries.

Step 3: Season the Fries

In a large bowl, combine the olive oil, paprika, garlic powder, onion powder, salt, and black pepper. Toss the sweet potato fries in the seasoned oil mixture, ensuring that each fry is evenly coated. The combination of spices adds a delightful flavor and a hint of smokiness to the fries.

Step 4: Arrange on the Baking Sheet

Spread the seasoned sweet potato fries in a single layer on the prepared baking sheet. Make sure to leave some space between the fries to allow for even browning. This will help the fries crisp up nicely in the oven.

Step 5: Bake to Perfection

Place the baking sheet in the preheated oven and bake the sweet potato fries for 25-30 minutes, flipping them halfway through the cooking time. Keep an eye on them to prevent burning, and remove them from the oven once they are golden brown and crispy. The edges will be slightly caramelized, adding a delightful sweetness to each bite.

Step 6: Serve and Enjoy

Once the sweet potato fries are done baking, remove them from the oven and let them cool for a few minutes. Then, transfer them to a serving dish and sprinkle with a pinch of additional salt if desired. These crispy baked sweet potato fries are best enjoyed hot and fresh out of the oven, paired with your favorite dipping sauce or enjoyed on their own.

Health Benefits

Sweet potatoes are a nutritional powerhouse, packed with vitamins, minerals, and fiber. They are an excellent source of vitamin A, vitamin C, and potassium, making them a nutritious choice for a satisfying snack. Baking the sweet potatoes instead of frying them reduces the added calories and unhealthy fats, making this snack a healthier alternative to traditional fries.

Conclusion

Crispy baked sweet potato fries are a delightful and wholesome snack that’s simple to prepare and bursting with flavor. Whether you’re hosting a gathering, looking for a satisfying snack, or seeking a healthier alternative to traditional fries, these crispy baked sweet potato fries are a surefire hit. With their crispy exterior and tender interior, these fries are the perfect guilt-free indulgence. So, grab some sweet potatoes and get ready to enjoy a delightful and nutritious snack that will leave you craving more!
