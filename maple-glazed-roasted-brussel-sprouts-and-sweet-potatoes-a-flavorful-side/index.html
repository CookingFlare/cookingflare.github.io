Maple Glazed Roasted Brussel Sprouts and Sweet Potatoes: A Flavorful Side


If you’re looking for a delicious and colorful side dish to complement your main course, look no further than this Maple Glazed Roasted Brussels Sprouts and Sweet Potatoes recipe. Packed with flavor and nutrients, this dish is sure to impress your family and friends. The combination of the tender Brussels sprouts, sweet potatoes, and the irresistible maple glaze creates a harmonious balance of sweet and savory flavors that will leave everyone craving for more.

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound Brussels sprouts, trimmed and halved
  • 2 medium-sized sweet potatoes, peeled and cut into cubes
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons maple syrup
  • 1 teaspoon Dijon mustard
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon smoked paprika
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 425°F (220°C) and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.

  2. In a large mixing bowl, combine the Brussels sprouts, sweet potatoes, olive oil, maple syrup, Dijon mustard, garlic powder, smoked paprika, salt, and pepper. Toss well to ensure that all the vegetables are evenly coated with the glaze.

  3. Transfer the coated Brussels sprouts and sweet potatoes onto the prepared baking sheet, spreading them out in a single layer. This will allow them to roast evenly and develop a caramelized exterior.

  4. Place the baking sheet in the preheated oven and roast for approximately 25-30 minutes, or until the vegetables are tender and slightly browned. Make sure to toss the vegetables halfway through the cooking time to ensure even browning.

  5. Once the Brussels sprouts and sweet potatoes are cooked to perfection, remove them from the oven and let them cool for a few minutes. This will allow the flavors to develop further and the glaze to thicken slightly.

  6. Transfer the roasted vegetables to a serving dish and garnish with a sprinkle of fresh parsley or a drizzle of additional maple syrup if desired. Serve immediately and enjoy!

Tips:

  • To save time, you can prep the Brussels sprouts and sweet potatoes in advance by trimming and halving the sprouts, and peeling and cutting the sweet potatoes into cubes. Store them separately in airtight containers in the refrigerator until you are ready to use them.

  • Feel free to customize this dish by adding your favorite seasonings or spices. You can experiment with adding a pinch of cayenne pepper for a spicy kick or a dash of cinnamon for a touch of warmth.

  • This recipe can easily be doubled or halved to accommodate the number of servings you require. Just adjust the quantities of the ingredients accordingly.

  • Leftovers can be stored in an airtight container in the refrigerator for up to 3 days. To reheat, simply place them in a preheated oven at 350°F (175°C) for about 10 minutes, or until heated through.

So there you have it, a simple yet flavorful side dish that will elevate any meal. The combination of the roasted Brussels sprouts and sweet potatoes, along with the irresistible maple glaze, will make this dish a favorite among your family and friends. Give it a try and let your taste buds thank you!
