Satisfying Nut-Free Chicken Enchiladas Recipe


If you’re looking for a delicious and satisfying dinner that’s free from nuts, then this Chicken Enchiladas recipe is perfect for you. Packed with flavor and easy to make, these enchiladas will become a family favorite in no time. The combination of tender chicken, flavorful spices, and savory sauce wrapped in warm tortillas creates a dish that’s sure to please everyone at the table. Let’s dive into the recipe and get ready to enjoy a delightful meal!

Ingredients

  • 2 cups shredded cooked chicken
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon chili powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 can (14 ounces) tomato sauce
  • 1 cup chicken broth
  • 1/2 cup sour cream
  • 1 can (4 ounces) diced green chilies
  • 10-12 corn tortillas
  • 2 cups shredded Monterey Jack cheese
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh cilantro, for garnish

Instructions

Step 1: Prepare the Filling

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. In a large skillet, heat the olive oil over medium heat.
  3. Add the chopped onion and sauté until translucent.
  4. Stir in the minced garlic, ground cumin, chili powder, paprika, and dried oregano. Cook for another minute until fragrant.
  5. Add the shredded chicken to the skillet and mix well to combine with the spices.
  6. Season the filling with salt and pepper to taste. Set aside.

Step 2: Prepare the Enchilada Sauce

  1. In a medium saucepan, combine the tomato sauce and chicken broth.
  2. Bring the mixture to a simmer over medium heat.
  3. Remove the saucepan from the heat and stir in the sour cream and diced green chilies. Set aside.

Step 3: Assemble the Enchiladas

  1. Pour a small amount of the enchilada sauce into the bottom of a 9x13 inch baking dish, spreading it evenly.
  2. Warm the corn tortillas in the microwave for a few seconds to make them pliable.
  3. Spoon a portion of the chicken filling onto each tortilla, then roll it up and place it seam-side down in the prepared baking dish.
  4. Once all the enchiladas are assembled, pour the remaining enchilada sauce over the top.
  5. Sprinkle the shredded Monterey Jack cheese over the enchiladas, covering them evenly.

Step 4: Bake and Serve

  1. Cover the baking dish with aluminum foil and bake the enchiladas in the preheated oven for 20 minutes.
  2. Remove the foil and continue baking for an additional 5-10 minutes, or until the cheese is melted and bubbly.
  3. Garnish the enchiladas with fresh cilantro before serving.

Enjoy Your Nut-Free Chicken Enchiladas!

Now that your nut-free chicken enchiladas are ready, it’s time to savor the delightful flavors and enjoy a comforting meal. Serve the enchiladas with your favorite sides such as Spanish rice, refried beans, or a crisp green salad. Whether it’s a weeknight dinner or a gathering with friends and family, this recipe is sure to impress and satisfy. So, gather your ingredients and get ready to delight your taste buds with these delicious nut-free chicken enchiladas!
