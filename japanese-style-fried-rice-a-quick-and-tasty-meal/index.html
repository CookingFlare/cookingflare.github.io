Japanese Style Fried Rice: A Quick and Tasty Meal


If you’re looking for a quick and delicious meal that’s bursting with flavor, then Japanese Style Fried Rice is the perfect choice. This dish offers a unique twist on the traditional fried rice recipe, incorporating a variety of ingredients that are commonly found in Japanese cuisine. Whether you’re a fan of Japanese food or simply looking to try something new, this recipe is sure to impress.

Ingredients

To make Japanese Style Fried Rice, you’ll need the following ingredients:

  • 2 cups of cooked short-grain rice
  • 2 tablespoons of vegetable oil
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 carrot, finely diced
  • 1 cup of frozen peas
  • 2 tablespoons of soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon of oyster sauce
  • 2 eggs, beaten
  • 2 green onions, thinly sliced
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Heat the vegetable oil in a large wok or skillet over medium heat. Add the chopped onion and minced garlic, and sauté until fragrant and translucent.

  2. Add the diced carrot to the wok and continue to cook for another 2-3 minutes, or until the carrot is tender.

  3. Push the vegetables to one side of the wok, creating a space in the center. Pour the beaten eggs into the center of the wok and scramble them until cooked through.

  4. Once the eggs are cooked, mix them with the vegetables in the wok.

  5. Add the frozen peas to the wok and stir-fry for another minute, or until the peas are heated through.

  6. Add the cooked rice to the wok, breaking up any clumps with a spatula. Stir-fry the rice and vegetables together for 2-3 minutes, ensuring that everything is well combined.

  7. Drizzle the soy sauce and oyster sauce over the rice and continue to stir-fry for another minute, allowing the flavors to meld together.

  8. Season with salt and pepper to taste, and give the fried rice one final toss to evenly distribute the seasonings.

  9. Remove the wok from the heat and garnish the fried rice with thinly sliced green onions.

Serving Suggestions

Japanese Style Fried Rice can be enjoyed on its own as a satisfying meal, or it can be served as a side dish alongside your favorite Japanese main course. For an extra touch of authenticity, consider serving the fried rice with a side of pickled ginger and a drizzle of sesame oil.

Final Thoughts

Japanese Style Fried Rice is a quick and tasty meal that brings the flavors of Japan right to your plate. With its colorful combination of vegetables, savory soy sauce, and fluffy rice, this dish is sure to become a family favorite. So, the next time you’re craving a simple yet delicious meal, give this recipe a try. Your taste buds will thank you!
