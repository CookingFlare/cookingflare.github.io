Spring Sauces and Dips Using Seasonal Herbs


As the chill of winter gives way to the fresh warmth of spring, our taste buds begin to crave lighter, fresher flavors that reflect the season’s vibrant bounty. One of the easiest and most exciting ways to incorporate the essence of spring into your meals is by making use of seasonal herbs. These fresh, aromatic plants can elevate any dish, providing depth and complexity with just a few simple ingredients.

This blog post will explore a variety of sauces and dips that are perfect for spring. Packed with seasonal herbs, these recipes will help you celebrate the flavors of the season. From tangy herb-infused vinaigrettes to creamy herb dips, there’s something for every palate. Whether you’re looking for a flavorful addition to salads, grilled meats, or even a light snack, these spring-inspired recipes are sure to impress.

1. Herb-Infused Lemon Vinaigrette

Ingredients:

  • 1/4 cup fresh lemon juice
  • 1/2 cup extra virgin olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons Dijon mustard
  • 1 teaspoon honey
  • 1 garlic clove, finely minced
  • 2 tablespoons fresh parsley, chopped
  • 1 tablespoon fresh chives, chopped
  • 1 tablespoon fresh tarragon, chopped
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a small bowl, whisk together the lemon juice, Dijon mustard, honey, and minced garlic.
  2. Gradually add the olive oil while continuing to whisk, creating an emulsion.
  3. Once the vinaigrette is well combined, stir in the chopped herbs â€” parsley, chives, and tarragon.
  4. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Adjust the balance of acidity and sweetness if necessary by adding more lemon juice or honey.
  5. Serve immediately or store in an airtight container in the refrigerator for up to a week.

Why It Works:

Lemon vinaigrette is a quintessential spring sauce that brings brightness and zest to any salad or roasted vegetable dish. The combination of parsley, chives, and tarragon enhances the vinaigrette with fresh, vibrant herbal notes that perfectly complement the tangy lemon. This vinaigrette is ideal for drizzling over tender greens, asparagus, or even grilled chicken.

2. Creamy Avocado and Basil Dip

Ingredients:

  • 2 ripe avocados, peeled and pitted
  • 1/4 cup fresh basil leaves, chopped
  • 2 tablespoons Greek yogurt
  • 1 tablespoon lime juice
  • 1 garlic clove, minced
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a food processor, combine the avocados, basil, Greek yogurt, lime juice, and garlic.
  2. Blend until smooth and creamy, scraping down the sides of the bowl as needed.
  3. Taste the dip and adjust the seasoning with salt, pepper, or more lime juice if desired.
  4. Serve with crunchy vegetables like carrots, cucumbers, or bell peppers, or as a dip for tortilla chips.

Why It Works:

This creamy avocado and basil dip is a fantastic way to showcase the fresh, herbal flavors of spring. The richness of the avocado pairs beautifully with the aromatic, slightly sweet basil, while the Greek yogurt adds a tangy creaminess. This dip is a refreshing change from traditional guacamole, perfect for dipping or spreading on toast for a light spring snack.

3. Mint and Yogurt Sauce for Lamb

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup Greek yogurt
  • 1/4 cup fresh mint leaves, finely chopped
  • 2 tablespoons lemon juice
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1/2 teaspoon cumin
  • 1 garlic clove, minced
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, combine the Greek yogurt, chopped mint, lemon juice, olive oil, cumin, and minced garlic.
  2. Stir well to combine and season with salt and pepper.
  3. Let the sauce sit in the refrigerator for at least 30 minutes to allow the flavors to meld together.
  4. Serve alongside roasted or grilled lamb chops, lamb kebabs, or grilled vegetables.

Why It Works:

Mint is a classic herb to pair with lamb, and this cooling, creamy yogurt sauce is a perfect complement to the richness of the meat. The tangy yogurt balances the earthy flavors of the lamb, while the mint adds a refreshing and aromatic element. The cumin adds a subtle, warm spice that ties everything together beautifully. This sauce is a must-have for your next spring barbecue or dinner party.

4. Garlic and Rosemary Chimichurri

Ingredients:

  • 1/4 cup red wine vinegar
  • 1/2 cup extra virgin olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons fresh rosemary, finely chopped
  • 2 tablespoons fresh parsley, chopped
  • 1 garlic clove, minced
  • 1/2 teaspoon red pepper flakes (optional)
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a small bowl, whisk together the red wine vinegar, olive oil, garlic, and red pepper flakes.
  2. Stir in the chopped rosemary and parsley, and season with salt and pepper.
  3. Let the chimichurri sit for at least 15 minutes to allow the flavors to combine.
  4. Serve with grilled meats, such as steak or chicken, or drizzle over roasted vegetables for an extra punch of flavor.

Why It Works:

Chimichurri is a vibrant, herb-packed sauce from Argentina, and the addition of fresh rosemary gives it a distinctly springtime flair. The tanginess of the red wine vinegar pairs beautifully with the robust garlic and the aromatic rosemary, creating a versatile sauce that enhances the flavors of grilled meats or roasted vegetables. It’s a perfect accompaniment for springtime barbecues and outdoor gatherings.

5. Dijon and Tarragon Mustard Sauce

Ingredients:

  • 1/4 cup Dijon mustard
  • 2 tablespoons fresh tarragon, chopped
  • 1 tablespoon white wine vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon honey
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a small bowl, whisk together the Dijon mustard, white wine vinegar, olive oil, and honey.
  2. Stir in the freshly chopped tarragon, and season with salt and pepper.
  3. Taste and adjust the seasoning as needed.
  4. Serve with roasted or grilled vegetables, fish, or as a tangy dipping sauce for crusty bread.

Why It Works:

Tarragon is a wonderfully aromatic herb that pairs beautifully with mustard, creating a tangy yet refined sauce. This Dijon and tarragon mustard sauce has a perfect balance of acidity and sweetness, making it ideal for drizzling over roasted spring vegetables, grilled fish, or even as a dip for fresh bread. The fresh tarragon adds a sophisticated note that elevates the mustard without overpowering it.

6. Herbed Cream Cheese Dip

Ingredients:

  • 8 ounces cream cheese, softened
  • 1/4 cup sour cream
  • 2 tablespoons fresh parsley, chopped
  • 1 tablespoon fresh dill, chopped
  • 1 tablespoon fresh chives, chopped
  • 1 teaspoon lemon zest
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a mixing bowl, combine the cream cheese and sour cream, and beat until smooth and creamy.
  2. Stir in the chopped parsley, dill, chives, and lemon zest.
  3. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  4. Serve with fresh vegetables, crackers, or as a spread for bagels.

Why It Works:

This herbed cream cheese dip is a simple and indulgent spring treat that highlights the fresh flavors of herbs like parsley, dill, and chives. The creamy texture is balanced by the bright, zesty lemon, making it a great addition to a spring brunch or picnic. It pairs wonderfully with fresh veggies, making it an easy and crowd-pleasing option for gatherings.

7. Basil Pesto

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups fresh basil leaves
  • 1/4 cup pine nuts, toasted
  • 1/2 cup Parmesan cheese, grated
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1/2 cup extra virgin olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a food processor, combine the basil leaves, pine nuts, Parmesan cheese, and garlic.
  2. Pulse until everything is finely chopped.
  3. With the processor running, slowly drizzle in the olive oil until the pesto reaches your desired consistency.
  4. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  5. Serve with pasta, spread on toast, or as a topping for grilled vegetables or meats.

Why It Works:

Basil pesto is a timeless sauce that truly shines in spring when basil is in full bloom.
