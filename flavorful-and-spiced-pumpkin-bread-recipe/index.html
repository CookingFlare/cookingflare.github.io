Flavorful and Spiced Pumpkin Bread Recipe


Are you ready to indulge in a delicious treat that will fill your home with the delightful aroma of fall? Look no further than this Flavorful and Spiced Pumpkin Bread recipe. Bursting with warm spices and the rich flavors of pumpkin, this bread is the perfect accompaniment to a cozy afternoon or a delightful breakfast option. Let’s dive into the recipe!

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground nutmeg
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cloves
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground ginger
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/2 cup brown sugar, packed
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 cup canned pumpkin puree
  • 1/2 cup milk

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease and flour a 9x5-inch loaf pan and set it aside.

  2. In a large bowl, whisk together the all-purpose flour, baking soda, baking powder, salt, cinnamon, nutmeg, cloves, and ginger. Set the dry mixture aside.

  3. In another bowl, cream together the softened butter, granulated sugar, and brown sugar until light and fluffy. This will take about 3-4 minutes.

  4. Beat in the eggs, one at a time, ensuring each is fully incorporated before adding the next. Stir in the vanilla extract.

  5. Add the canned pumpkin puree to the wet mixture and mix until well combined.

  6. Gradually add the dry mixture to the wet mixture, alternating with the milk. Begin and end with the dry mixture, mixing until just combined after each addition. Be careful not to overmix, as this can result in a dense bread.

  7. Pour the batter into the prepared loaf pan and smooth the top with a spatula.

  8. Bake for 60-70 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.

  9. Once baked, remove the bread from the oven and let it cool in the pan for 10 minutes. Then, transfer it to a wire rack to cool completely before slicing.

Serving Suggestions

This Flavorful and Spiced Pumpkin Bread is delicious on its own, but it can be taken to the next level with a few additional serving suggestions:

  • Spread a layer of cream cheese frosting on top for a decadent twist.
  • Serve it warm with a scoop of vanilla ice cream for a delightful dessert.
  • Enjoy it with a cup of hot apple cider for a cozy fall treat.

Storage Tips

If you have any leftovers (which is rare!), store the pumpkin bread in an airtight container at room temperature for up to 3 days. It can also be frozen for longer storage. Simply wrap individual slices in plastic wrap and place them in a ziplock bag before freezing.

Conclusion

This Flavorful and Spiced Pumpkin Bread recipe is a true celebration of the flavors of fall. It’s a delightful treat that will have your taste buds dancing with joy. Whether you’re enjoying it as a breakfast option or as a dessert, this bread is sure to become a favorite in your baking repertoire. So, grab your apron and get ready to bake up a storm of pumpkin goodness. Happy baking!
