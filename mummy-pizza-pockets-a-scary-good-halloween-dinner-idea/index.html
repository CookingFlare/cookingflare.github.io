Mummy Pizza Pockets: A Scary Good Halloween Dinner Idea


Halloween is just around the corner, and what better way to celebrate than with some spooktacular treats? If you’re looking for a deliciously eerie dinner idea that will delight both kids and adults, look no further than Mummy Pizza Pockets. These creepy yet tasty snacks are perfect for Halloween parties or a ghoulish family dinner. With their flaky crust and gooey cheese filling, these Mummy Pizza Pockets are sure to be a hit!

Ingredients

  • 1 package of pre-made pizza dough
  • 1 cup of pizza sauce
  • 2 cups of shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup of sliced black olives
  • Sliced pepperoni
  • 1 egg, beaten

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C) and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.

  2. Roll out the pre-made pizza dough on a lightly floured surface to about 1/4 inch thickness.

  3. Using a round cookie cutter or a glass, cut out circles from the pizza dough. These will be the bases for your Mummy Pizza Pockets.

  4. Place a tablespoon of pizza sauce onto each dough circle, leaving a small border around the edges.

  5. Sprinkle shredded mozzarella cheese over the sauce, followed by a few sliced black olives for the spooky eyes.

  6. Next, take a slice of pepperoni and cut it into thin strips. Lay the strips diagonally across the cheese to create the mummy’s bandages.

  7. Take another circle of pizza dough and place it on top of the filling. Use your fingers to press the edges of the dough together, sealing the pocket.

  8. Brush the tops of the Mummy Pizza Pockets with the beaten egg. This will give them a golden brown color when baked.

  9. Place the Mummy Pizza Pockets onto the prepared baking sheet and bake for about 15-20 minutes, or until the crust is golden and the cheese is melted and bubbly.

  10. Allow the Mummy Pizza Pockets to cool for a few minutes before serving. They can be enjoyed on their own or served with additional pizza sauce for dipping.

Tips and Tricks

  • Get creative with your Mummy Pizza Pockets! You can add other toppings like diced bell peppers, mushrooms, or even mini meatballs to create different flavors and textures.

  • If you want to make your Mummy Pizza Pockets extra spooky, you can use edible food coloring to dye the pizza sauce green or red. This will give them a truly haunting appearance.

  • Don’t worry about making your Mummy Pizza Pockets look perfect. The more imperfect they are, the scarier they’ll look!

  • These Mummy Pizza Pockets can be made in advance and stored in the refrigerator. When you’re ready to serve them, simply reheat them in the oven for a few minutes until warmed through.

Conclusion

Mummy Pizza Pockets are a frightfully fun and delicious Halloween dinner idea that will have everyone howling with delight. With their flaky crust, gooey cheese filling, and spooky mummy bandages, these snacks are sure to be a hit at any Halloween gathering. So, gather your little monsters and get ready to enjoy this scary good treat that’s perfect for the spookiest night of the year!
