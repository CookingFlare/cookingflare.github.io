Instant Pot Pulled Pork Recipe: Fall-Off-The-Bone Tender and Delicious


If you’re a fan of tender, juicy pulled pork but don’t have the time to babysit a smoker or slow cooker, then this Instant Pot pulled pork recipe is for you. With the magic of pressure cooking, you can achieve that irresistible fall-off-the-bone texture and mouthwatering flavor in a fraction of the time. Whether you’re hosting a summer barbecue, a game day gathering, or simply craving a comforting meal, this recipe is sure to impress your taste buds and your guests.

Ingredients

  • 4-5 pounds pork shoulder or pork butt, trimmed of excess fat
  • 1 tablespoon smoked paprika
  • 1 tablespoon garlic powder
  • 1 tablespoon onion powder
  • 1 teaspoon cayenne pepper
  • 2 teaspoons ground cumin
  • 2 teaspoons salt
  • 1 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1 cup chicken or beef broth
  • 1/2 cup apple cider vinegar
  • 1/4 cup your favorite barbecue sauce

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Pork: In a small bowl, mix the smoked paprika, garlic powder, onion powder, cayenne pepper, cumin, salt, and black pepper. Rub this spice mixture all over the pork shoulder, ensuring it’s evenly coated.

  2. Sear the Meat: Set your Instant Pot to “Sauté” mode and add a splash of oil. Once the pot is hot, sear the pork shoulder on all sides until nicely browned, about 3-4 minutes per side. This step adds depth of flavor to the final dish.

  3. Pressure Cook: Pour the broth and apple cider vinegar into the Instant Pot, then place the seared pork on top. Lock the lid in place and set the Instant Pot to cook at high pressure for 90 minutes. Allow the pressure to release naturally for 15 minutes before using the quick release to vent any remaining pressure.

  4. Shred the Pork: Carefully remove the pork from the Instant Pot and transfer it to a cutting board. Using two forks, shred the pork into succulent, juicy pieces.

  5. Add the Finishing Touch: Mix in your favorite barbecue sauce with the pulled pork, adjusting the amount to your taste preference. The residual heat will warm the sauce and infuse the pork with its sweet and tangy flavors.

  6. Serve and Enjoy: Your Instant Pot pulled pork is now ready to be enjoyed! Serve it on a soft, toasted bun with a side of coleslaw, pickles, or any other fixings you love. It’s also fantastic on its own, paired with some roasted vegetables or a fresh green salad.

Tips for Success

  • For even more flavor, you can marinate the pork shoulder in the spice rub overnight before cooking.
  • Feel free to customize the spice blend to suit your palate. Add a dash of cinnamon for warmth or a pinch of cayenne for extra heat.
  • If you prefer a thicker sauce, you can simmer the cooking liquid in the Instant Pot on the “Sauté” mode after removing the pork until it reduces to your desired consistency.

Final Thoughts

With this Instant Pot pulled pork recipe, you can savor the rich, smoky essence of slow-cooked barbecue without spending hours tending to a traditional smoker or slow cooker. The Instant Pot’s ingenious pressure cooking technology transforms tough cuts of pork into a tender, flavor-packed delight in a fraction of the time. Whether you’re feeding a crowd or simply treating yourself to a satisfying meal, this recipe is a game-changer. So, fire up your Instant Pot and prepare to be amazed by the delectable results!

So, give this recipe a try and let the succulent aroma of perfectly cooked pulled pork fill your kitchen. Your taste buds will thank you, and your guests will be begging for the recipe. Enjoy!
