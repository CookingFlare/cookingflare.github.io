5 Easy and Delicious Breakfast Burrito Recipes for Beginners


Breakfast burritos - the ultimate morning meal that combines the convenience of a wrap with the excitement of a flavorful filling. Whether you’re a busy professional on-the-go or a weekend warrior looking for a satisfying start to the day, breakfast burritos are the perfect solution. In this post, we’ll explore 5 easy and delicious breakfast burrito recipes that are perfect for beginners. From classic combinations to creative twists, these recipes will have you wrapping up a delicious breakfast in no time.

Recipe 1: Classic Scrambled Eggs and Sausage

Ingredients:

  • 4 eggs
  • 1/2 cup diced sausage (such as chorizo or breakfast sausage)
  • 1/2 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1/4 cup chopped cilantro
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 4 tortillas

Instructions:

  1. Scramble the eggs in a bowl and set aside.
  2. Cook the sausage in a pan over medium-high heat, breaking it up into small pieces as it cooks.
  3. Add the cooked sausage to the scrambled eggs and stir to combine.
  4. Warm the tortillas by wrapping them in a damp paper towel and microwaving for 20-30 seconds.
  5. Assemble the burritos by spooning the egg and sausage mixture onto a tortilla, followed by a sprinkle of cheese and a sprinkle of cilantro.

Recipe 2: Avocado and Black Bean

Ingredients:

  • 1 ripe avocado, diced
  • 1/2 cup cooked black beans
  • 1/2 cup shredded Monterey Jack cheese
  • 1/4 cup chopped cilantro
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 4 tortillas

Instructions:

  1. Mash the avocado in a bowl and set aside.
  2. Cook the black beans according to package instructions.
  3. Warm the tortillas by wrapping them in a damp paper towel and microwaving for 20-30 seconds.
  4. Assemble the burritos by spooning the mashed avocado onto a tortilla, followed by a spoonful of black beans, a sprinkle of cheese, and a sprinkle of cilantro.

Recipe 3: Bacon and Cheddar

Ingredients:

  • 6 slices of bacon, cooked and crumbled
  • 1/2 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1/4 cup chopped scallions
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 4 tortillas

Instructions:

  1. Cook the bacon in a pan over medium heat until crispy, then crumble into small pieces.
  2. Warm the tortillas by wrapping them in a damp paper towel and microwaving for 20-30 seconds.
  3. Assemble the burritos by spooning the crumbled bacon onto a tortilla, followed by a sprinkle of cheese and a sprinkle of scallions.

Recipe 4: Spinach and Feta

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup fresh spinach leaves
  • 1/2 cup crumbled feta cheese
  • 1/4 cup chopped red onion
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 4 tortillas

Instructions:

  1. Chop the spinach leaves into small pieces and set aside.
  2. Warm the tortillas by wrapping them in a damp paper towel and microwaving for 20-30 seconds.
  3. Assemble the burritos by spooning the chopped spinach onto a tortilla, followed by a sprinkle of feta cheese and a sprinkle of red onion.

Recipe 5: Sweet Potato and Black Bean

Ingredients:

  • 1 large sweet potato, cooked and diced
  • 1/2 cup cooked black beans
  • 1/4 cup shredded Monterey Jack cheese
  • 1/4 cup chopped cilantro
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 4 tortillas

Instructions:

  1. Cook the sweet potato according to package instructions, then dice into small pieces.
  2. Cook the black beans according to package instructions.
  3. Warm the tortillas by wrapping them in a damp paper towel and microwaving for 20-30 seconds.
  4. Assemble the burritos by spooning the diced sweet potato onto a tortilla, followed by a spoonful of black beans, a sprinkle of cheese, and a sprinkle of cilantro.

Conclusion

Breakfast burritos are a delicious and convenient way to start your day. With these 5 easy and delicious recipes, you’ll be well on your way to becoming a breakfast burrito master. Whether you’re in the mood for classic scrambled eggs and sausage or something a little more adventurous like sweet potato and black beans, there’s a breakfast burrito recipe on this list for everyone. So go ahead, get creative, and wrap up a delicious breakfast today!
