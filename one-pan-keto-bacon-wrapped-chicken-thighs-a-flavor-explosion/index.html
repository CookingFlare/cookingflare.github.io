One-Pan Keto Bacon-Wrapped Chicken Thighs: A Flavor Explosion


If you’re looking for a delicious and easy keto-friendly dish that’s bursting with flavor, then look no further! This one-pan recipe for bacon-wrapped chicken thighs is a game-changer. Not only is it incredibly tasty, but it’s also a breeze to make, making it the perfect meal for busy weeknights or casual gatherings with friends and family.

Ingredients

  • 6 boneless, skinless chicken thighs
  • 12 slices of bacon
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley for garnish

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C).
  2. Season the chicken thighs with garlic powder, paprika, dried thyme, salt, and black pepper, ensuring that each piece is evenly coated with the flavorful seasoning.
  3. Carefully wrap each seasoned chicken thigh with two slices of bacon, securing them with toothpicks if necessary.
  4. In a large oven-safe skillet, heat the olive oil over medium-high heat. Once hot, add the bacon-wrapped chicken thighs to the skillet, searing them for 2-3 minutes on each side until the bacon is slightly crispy.
  5. Transfer the skillet to the preheated oven and bake for 20-25 minutes, or until the chicken is cooked through and the bacon is golden and crispy.
  6. Once done, remove the skillet from the oven and let the chicken thighs rest for a few minutes before serving.
  7. Garnish with freshly chopped parsley for a pop of color and an extra burst of freshness.

Why This Recipe Is a Must-Try

Keto-Friendly

This recipe is perfect for those following a keto lifestyle, as it’s low in carbs and high in flavor. The combination of juicy chicken thighs and savory bacon creates a satisfying and indulgent dish that won’t derail your low-carb eating plan.

Easy Preparation

With just a handful of simple ingredients and minimal prep work, this one-pan dish is a breeze to prepare. Whether you’re a seasoned home cook or just starting out in the kitchen, this recipe is approachable and stress-free.

Crowd-Pleaser

Whether you’re hosting a dinner party or simply cooking for your family, these bacon-wrapped chicken thighs are sure to be a hit. The rich, smoky flavor of the bacon pairs perfectly with the tender, juicy chicken, making it a crowd-pleasing dish for any occasion.

Versatile

While this recipe is fantastic on its own, you can easily customize it to suit your taste preferences. Consider adding a sprinkle of your favorite cheese before baking or experimenting with different seasonings to create your own signature flavor profile.

Time-Saving

With the convenience of using just one pan, this recipe minimizes cleanup and makes for a fuss-free cooking experience. Spend less time doing dishes and more time enjoying a delicious, home-cooked meal.

In Conclusion

These one-pan keto bacon-wrapped chicken thighs are a true flavor explosion, offering a perfect balance of simplicity and indulgence. Whether you’re following a keto diet or simply looking for a mouthwatering dish to add to your recipe repertoire, this recipe is a winner. Give it a try and savor the deliciousness of this easy, crowd-pleasing meal.
