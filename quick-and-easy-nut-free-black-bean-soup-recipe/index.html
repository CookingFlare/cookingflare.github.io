Quick and Easy Nut-Free Black Bean Soup Recipe


If you’re in the mood for a comforting and hearty meal that’s also quick and easy to make, then this Nut-Free Black Bean Soup recipe is just what you need. Packed with protein, fiber, and flavor, this soup is a perfect option for busy weeknights or lazy weekends. Plus, it’s nut-free, making it a safe choice for those with nut allergies or dietary restrictions. Let’s dive into the recipe and get cooking!

Ingredients:

  • 2 cans (15 oz each) black beans, drained and rinsed
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 red bell pepper, diced
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon chili powder
  • 4 cups vegetable broth
  • 1 can (14.5 oz) diced tomatoes
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Optional toppings: chopped cilantro, avocado, sour cream, lime wedges

Instructions:

  1. Heat the olive oil in a large pot over medium heat. Add the chopped onion, minced garlic, and diced red bell pepper. Sauté for 5-7 minutes, or until the vegetables are soft and fragrant.

  2. Add the ground cumin and chili powder to the pot, stirring to coat the vegetables. Let the spices toast for a minute to release their flavors.

  3. Pour in the vegetable broth, drained and rinsed black beans, and diced tomatoes. Bring the soup to a simmer, then reduce the heat and let it cook for 15-20 minutes, allowing the flavors to meld together.

  4. Once the soup has simmered and the flavors have developed, use an immersion blender to partially blend the soup to your desired consistency. If you don’t have an immersion blender, carefully transfer a portion of the soup to a blender and pulse until smooth, then return it to the pot.

  5. Season the soup with salt and pepper to taste, adjusting the seasoning as needed. If the soup is too thick, you can add a bit more vegetable broth to reach your desired consistency.

  6. Ladle the Nut-Free Black Bean Soup into bowls and serve with your choice of toppings, such as chopped cilantro, avocado slices, a dollop of sour cream, or a squeeze of fresh lime juice. Enjoy!

Why You’ll Love This Recipe:

  • Quick and Easy: This recipe comes together in under 30 minutes, making it a perfect option for busy days when you need a satisfying meal without spending hours in the kitchen.
  • Nut-Free: With the increasing prevalence of nut allergies, it’s important to have delicious and safe recipes on hand. This Nut-Free Black Bean Soup is a great option for individuals with nut allergies or those cooking for someone with a nut allergy.
  • Versatile: You can easily customize this soup to your liking by adding different toppings or adjusting the seasoning to suit your taste preferences.
  • Healthy and Filling: Packed with protein and fiber from the black beans, this soup is both nutritious and satisfying, making it a great option for a wholesome meal.

So, next time you’re craving a nourishing and flavorful soup, give this Nut-Free Black Bean Soup recipe a try. Whether you’re cooking for yourself, your family, or guests, this recipe is sure to be a hit. Enjoy the simplicity and deliciousness of this quick and easy soup!
