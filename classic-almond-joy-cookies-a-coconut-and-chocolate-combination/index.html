Classic Almond Joy Cookies: A Coconut and Chocolate Combination


Who can resist the delectable combination of coconut, chocolate, and almonds? If you’re a fan of Almond Joy candy bars, then you’re in for a treat with this Classic Almond Joy Cookies recipe. These cookies are a delightful twist on the classic chocolate chip cookie, packed with the tropical flavors of coconut and the crunchy goodness of almonds. Get ready to indulge in a heavenly delight that will satisfy your sweet tooth cravings!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 cup brown sugar, packed
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 1/2 cups sweetened shredded coconut
  • 1 1/2 cups semi-sweet chocolate chips
  • 1 cup sliced almonds

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C). Line a baking sheet with parchment paper or lightly grease it.

  2. In a large mixing bowl, cream together the softened butter, granulated sugar, and brown sugar until light and fluffy. This will take about 2-3 minutes using an electric mixer on medium speed.

  3. Add the eggs, one at a time, beating well after each addition. Stir in the vanilla extract.

  4. In a separate bowl, whisk together the all-purpose flour, baking soda, and salt. Gradually add the dry ingredients to the butter mixture, mixing until just combined.

  5. Stir in the sweetened shredded coconut, semi-sweet chocolate chips, and sliced almonds. Ensure that the ingredients are evenly distributed throughout the dough.

  6. Using a cookie scoop or a tablespoon, drop rounded balls of dough onto the prepared baking sheet, spacing them about 2 inches apart. Gently press down on each cookie dough ball to slightly flatten it.

  7. Bake for 10-12 minutes, or until the edges are golden brown. The centers may still look slightly undercooked, but they will set as the cookies cool.

  8. Remove the baking sheet from the oven and allow the cookies to cool on the sheet for 5 minutes. Then, transfer them to a wire rack to cool completely.

  9. Once cooled, store the Classic Almond Joy Cookies in an airtight container at room temperature for up to a week. Enjoy them with a glass of milk or a hot cup of coffee!

Tips and Variations

  • For an extra burst of coconut flavor, you can use coconut extract instead of vanilla extract.
  • Swap out the semi-sweet chocolate chips for milk chocolate or dark chocolate chips if desired.
  • If you prefer a chewier texture, reduce the baking time by a minute or two.
  • Feel free to add a drizzle of melted chocolate on top of each cookie for an extra touch of sweetness and visual appeal.

Now that you have the recipe for Classic Almond Joy Cookies, it’s time to gather your ingredients, preheat your oven, and get baking! These cookies are perfect for sharing with family and friends or enjoying as a sweet treat just for yourself. So, put on your apron and let the aroma of coconut and chocolate fill your kitchen. Happy baking!
