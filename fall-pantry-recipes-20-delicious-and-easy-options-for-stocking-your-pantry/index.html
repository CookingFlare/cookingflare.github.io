Fall Pantry Recipes: 20 Delicious and Easy Options for Stocking Your Pantry


As the leaves start to change color and the air turns crisp, it’s time to embrace the flavors of fall. And what better way to do that than by stocking your pantry with delicious and easy recipes that celebrate the season? In this blog post, we’ll explore 20 fall pantry recipes that are sure to satisfy your taste buds and keep you warm on those chilly autumn nights. So grab your apron and let’s get cooking!

1. Pumpkin Spice Pancakes

Start your day off right with these fluffy and flavorful pumpkin spice pancakes. Made with pantry staples like flour, baking powder, and canned pumpkin, these pancakes are a cozy way to kickstart your mornings.

2. Butternut Squash Soup

Nothing says fall quite like a warm bowl of butternut squash soup. With just a handful of ingredients, including butternut squash, onions, and vegetable broth, this recipe is a breeze to make. Top it off with a dollop of sour cream and some crispy bacon for an extra touch of indulgence.

3. Apple Cider Glazed Chicken

Add a touch of sweetness to your dinner with this apple cider glazed chicken recipe. With a simple glaze made from apple cider, brown sugar, and Dijon mustard, this dish is bursting with fall flavors. Serve it alongside some roasted vegetables for a complete and satisfying meal.

4. Maple Roasted Brussels Sprouts

Brussels sprouts get a bad rap, but this recipe is sure to change your mind. Tossed in a maple syrup and Dijon mustard glaze, these roasted Brussels sprouts are caramelized and delicious. It’s the perfect side dish to accompany any fall meal.

5. Cranberry Pecan Salad

Freshen up your fall menu with this vibrant cranberry pecan salad. Made with dried cranberries, toasted pecans, mixed greens, and a tangy vinaigrette, this salad is a refreshing addition to any meal. It’s the perfect balance of sweet, tart, and crunchy.

6. Sweet Potato Casserole

No fall pantry is complete without a classic sweet potato casserole. Made with mashed sweet potatoes, brown sugar, and a crunchy pecan topping, this dish is a must-have for any holiday gathering. It’s the ultimate comfort food that will leave you feeling warm and satisfied.

7. Pumpkin Bread

Indulge your sweet tooth with this moist and flavorful pumpkin bread. Made with canned pumpkin, warm spices, and a hint of vanilla, this bread is perfect for breakfast or as a snack with a cup of tea. It’s a fall favorite that will have your kitchen smelling heavenly.

8. Harvest Stuffed Acorn Squash

Fill your kitchen with the aroma of fall with this harvest stuffed acorn squash recipe. Filled with a savory mixture of wild rice, mushrooms, and dried cranberries, these roasted acorn squash halves are a show-stopping main course. It’s a dish that’s as beautiful as it is delicious.

9. Cinnamon Apple Crisp

Warm up your kitchen with the comforting scent of cinnamon apple crisp. Made with fresh apples, cinnamon, and a buttery oat topping, this dessert is the epitome of fall. Serve it with a scoop of vanilla ice cream for the ultimate indulgence.

10. Spiced Hot Chocolate

Cozy up by the fire with a mug of spiced hot chocolate. Made with cocoa powder, warm spices, and a splash of vanilla extract, this drink is the perfect way to end a chilly fall day. Top it off with some whipped cream and a sprinkle of cinnamon for an extra treat.

These are just a few of the many fall pantry recipes you can enjoy this season. From comforting soups to indulgent desserts, there’s something for everyone to love. So stock up your pantry with these delicious and easy options, and embrace the flavors of fall like never before. Happy cooking!
