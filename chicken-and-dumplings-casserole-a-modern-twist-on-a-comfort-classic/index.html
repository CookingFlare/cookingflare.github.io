Chicken and Dumplings Casserole: A Modern Twist on a Comfort Classic


If you’re a fan of the comforting flavors of chicken and dumplings but want to try a modern twist on this classic dish, then you’re in for a treat. This Chicken and Dumplings Casserole recipe takes the beloved flavors of traditional chicken and dumplings and transforms them into a convenient and delicious casserole. It’s the perfect way to enjoy the heartwarming taste of chicken and dumplings in a new and exciting format.

Ingredients

For the Chicken Mixture:

  • 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 carrots, diced
  • 2 celery stalks, diced
  • 1 teaspoon thyme
  • 1 teaspoon rosemary
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 cup chicken broth
  • 1 cup frozen peas
  • 1 cup frozen corn
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream

For the Dumplings:

  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 cup unsalted butter, chilled and cut into small pieces
  • 3/4 cup milk

For the Topping:

  • 1/4 cup unsalted butter, melted
  • 1/2 cup breadcrumbs
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 tablespoon fresh parsley, chopped

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Chicken Mixture:

    • Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
    • In a large skillet, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the chicken breasts and cook until browned on both sides. Remove the chicken from the skillet and set aside.
    • In the same skillet, add the onion, garlic, carrots, and celery. Sauté until the vegetables are tender.
    • Stir in the thyme, rosemary, salt, and pepper. Pour in the chicken broth, scraping up any browned bits from the bottom of the skillet.
    • Shred the cooked chicken and return it to the skillet. Add the peas, corn, and heavy cream. Simmer for a few minutes until the mixture has thickened slightly. Remove from heat.

  2. Make the Dumplings:

    • In a mixing bowl, combine the flour, baking powder, and salt. Cut in the chilled butter until the mixture resembles coarse crumbs.
    • Stir in the milk until just combined. The dough will be sticky.

  3. Assemble the Casserole:

    • Transfer the chicken mixture to a greased 9x13 inch baking dish.
    • Drop spoonfuls of the dumpling dough on top of the chicken mixture, covering it as evenly as possible.

  4. Add the Topping:

    • In a small bowl, combine the melted butter, breadcrumbs, Parmesan cheese, and parsley. Sprinkle this mixture over the dumplings.

  5. Bake the Casserole:

    • Cover the baking dish with foil and bake for 30 minutes. Then, remove the foil and bake for an additional 15-20 minutes, or until the dumplings are golden brown and cooked through.

  6. Serve and Enjoy!

    • Let the casserole cool for a few minutes before serving. This modern twist on chicken and dumplings is a comforting and satisfying dish that’s perfect for family dinners or potlucks.

Conclusion

This Chicken and Dumplings Casserole recipe offers a fresh take on a beloved classic, blending the familiar flavors of chicken and dumplings with the convenience of a casserole. Whether you’re looking for a cozy weeknight meal or a crowd-pleasing dish for gatherings, this modern twist on a comfort classic is sure to impress. Try it out and savor the delightful combination of tender chicken, hearty vegetables, fluffy dumplings, and a golden, crunchy topping. It’s a dish that brings together the best of tradition and innovation in every comforting bite.
