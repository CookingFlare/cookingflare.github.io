Caramel Macchiato Cupcakes: A Coffee Lover's Delight


If you’re a coffee lover with a sweet tooth, you’re in for a treat! These Caramel Macchiato Cupcakes are the perfect blend of rich coffee flavor and decadent caramel sweetness. Whether you’re hosting a brunch, a coffee date, or just craving a delicious homemade dessert, these cupcakes are sure to impress. With a moist coffee-infused cake, a creamy caramel filling, and a luscious coffee-flavored frosting, these cupcakes will elevate your baking game to a whole new level.

Ingredients

For the Cupcakes:

  • 1 ¾ cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 ½ teaspoons baking powder
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • ½ cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • ½ cup brewed strong coffee, cooled
  • ½ cup milk

For the Caramel Filling:

  • 1 cup caramel sauce

For the Coffee Frosting:

  • 1 ½ cups unsalted butter, softened
  • 4 cups confectioners’ sugar
  • 2-3 tablespoons brewed strong coffee, cooled
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Instructions

Step 1: Prepare the Cupcakes

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C) and line a muffin tin with cupcake liners.
  2. In a medium bowl, whisk together the flour, baking powder, and salt.
  3. In a separate large bowl, cream the butter and sugar together until light and fluffy.
  4. Add the eggs, one at a time, beating well after each addition. Stir in the vanilla extract.
  5. In a small bowl, combine the brewed coffee and milk.
  6. Gradually add the dry ingredients to the creamed mixture, alternating with the coffee mixture. Mix until just combined.
  7. Fill each cupcake liner two-thirds full with the batter.
  8. Bake for 18-20 minutes or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.
  9. Remove from the oven and let the cupcakes cool completely.

Step 2: Make the Caramel Filling

  1. Once the cupcakes are cooled, use a sharp knife to cut out a small cone-shaped piece from the center of each cupcake.
  2. Fill each cavity with caramel sauce, then replace the cut-out piece on top of the caramel.

Step 3: Prepare the Coffee Frosting

  1. In a large bowl, beat the softened butter until creamy.
  2. Gradually add the confectioners’ sugar, one cup at a time, beating well after each addition.
  3. Add the brewed coffee and vanilla extract, and beat until smooth and fluffy.

Step 4: Frost the Cupcakes

  1. Transfer the coffee frosting to a piping bag fitted with a decorative tip.
  2. Pipe the frosting onto each cupcake, covering the caramel-filled cavity.

Serving and Enjoyment

These Caramel Macchiato Cupcakes are a delightful indulgence for any coffee enthusiast. Serve them at your next gathering, or simply enjoy them with a cup of your favorite coffee.
