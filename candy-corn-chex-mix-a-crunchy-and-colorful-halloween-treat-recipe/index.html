Candy Corn Chex Mix: A Crunchy and Colorful Halloween Treat Recipe


Halloween is just around the corner, and it’s time to whip up some spooktacular treats for your friends and family. If you’re looking for a delicious, crunchy, and colorful snack that captures the spirit of the season, then you’re in luck! This Candy Corn Chex Mix recipe is the perfect combination of sweet and salty, making it an irresistible treat for Halloween parties, movie nights, or simply snacking on a cozy autumn evening.

Ingredients

  • 3 cups Corn Chex cereal
  • 3 cups Rice Chex cereal
  • 1 cup pretzel twists
  • 1 cup candy corn
  • 1 cup white chocolate chips
  • 1/4 cup unsalted butter
  • 1/2 cup powdered sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • Orange and yellow food coloring

Instructions

  1. Start by preheating your oven to 250°F (120°C) and line a large baking sheet with parchment paper.

  2. In a large mixing bowl, combine the Corn Chex cereal, Rice Chex cereal, pretzels, and candy corn. Gently toss the ingredients together to create an even mixture.

  3. In a microwave-safe bowl, melt the white chocolate chips and butter together in 30-second intervals, stirring in between, until smooth and fully melted. Be careful not to overheat the chocolate.

  4. Once the chocolate and butter are melted, stir in the vanilla extract. Then, pour the mixture over the cereal and candy corn, making sure to coat the dry ingredients evenly.

  5. Toss the mixture gently until all the pieces are coated with the chocolate mixture. This is where the magic happens – the candy corn will start to stick to the Chex mix, creating a delightful blend of flavors and textures.

  6. Spread the mixture in an even layer on the prepared baking sheet. Place it in the preheated oven and bake for 45 minutes, stirring every 15 minutes to ensure even coating and to prevent the mixture from sticking to the parchment paper.

  7. While the Chex mix is baking, prepare the colored powdered sugar. Divide the powdered sugar into two separate bowls and add a few drops of orange food coloring to one bowl and yellow food coloring to the other. Use a fork to mix the food coloring into the powdered sugar until you achieve vibrant orange and yellow colors.

  8. Once the Chex mix is done baking, remove it from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes. Then, sprinkle the colored powdered sugar over the mixture and gently toss to coat evenly. The powdered sugar adds a delightful sweetness and a pop of Halloween colors to the mix.

  9. Allow the Chex mix to cool completely before serving. Once cooled, it’s ready to be enjoyed! You can store any leftovers in an airtight container for up to a week, but let’s be honest – it’s so delicious that it probably won’t last that long.

Final Thoughts

With its vibrant colors and irresistible combination of flavors, this Candy Corn Chex Mix is a must-have for your Halloween celebrations. Whether you’re hosting a party or simply indulging in some seasonal snacking, this recipe is sure to be a hit with kids and adults alike. So, get ready to spread some Halloween cheer with this crunchy and colorful treat that captures the essence of the spookiest holiday of the year!
