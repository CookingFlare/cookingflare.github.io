Recipe 1: Spicy Mango Jalapeno Pickles

Ingredients:

  • 4 ripe mangoes, peeled and sliced
  • 4-6 fresh jalapenos, sliced
  • 1 cup white vinegar
  • 1 cup water
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1 tablespoon salt
  • 2 cloves of garlic, crushed
  • 1 teaspoon mustard seeds
  • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric

Instructions:

  1. In a saucepan, combine the white vinegar, water, sugar, salt, garlic, mustard seeds, cumin seeds, and turmeric. Bring the mixture to a boil, then reduce the heat and simmer for 5 minutes.
  2. Place the sliced mangoes and jalapenos in a clean, sterilized jar.
  3. Pour the hot brine over the mangoes and jalapenos, making sure they are completely submerged.
  4. Allow the pickles to cool to room temperature, then cover the jar with a lid and store it in the refrigerator for at least 24 hours before enjoying.

These Spicy Mango Jalapeno Pickles strike the perfect balance between sweet and heat, making them a versatile condiment for everything from grilled meats to cheese platters.

Recipe 2: Turmeric and Ginger Carrot Pickles

Ingredients:

  • 6-8 large carrots, peeled and cut into sticks
  • 1 tablespoon fresh ginger, grated
  • 1 teaspoon ground turmeric
  • 1 cup rice vinegar
  • 1 cup water
  • 1/4 cup honey
  • 1 tablespoon salt
  • 2 teaspoons black peppercorns
  • 2 bay leaves

Instructions:

  1. In a saucepan, combine the rice vinegar, water, honey, salt, ginger, turmeric, black peppercorns, and bay leaves. Bring the mixture to a boil, then remove it from the heat.
  2. Pack the carrot sticks into a clean, sterilized jar.
  3. Pour the hot brine over the carrots, ensuring they are fully immersed.
  4. Allow the pickles to cool to room temperature, then seal the jar and refrigerate for at least 48 hours before serving.

These vibrant Turmeric and Ginger Carrot Pickles are not only visually stunning but also pack a punch of earthy, spicy flavor that pairs beautifully with salads and sandwiches.
