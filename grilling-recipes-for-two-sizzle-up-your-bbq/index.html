Grilling Recipes for Two: Sizzle Up Your BBQ


Are you looking for a fun and delicious way to spend quality time with your loved one? Why not fire up the grill and whip up some mouthwatering dishes for two? Grilling is not only a great way to cook flavorful meals, but it also allows you to enjoy the outdoors while creating memorable moments together. In this blog post, we will share some amazing grilling recipes specifically designed for couples. So, grab your aprons and get ready to sizzle up your BBQ!

Grilled Lemon Herb Chicken Skewers

Ingredients:

  • 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 2 lemons, juiced and zested
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon fresh thyme, chopped
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your grill to medium-high heat.
  2. Cut the chicken breasts into 1-inch cubes and place them in a bowl.
  3. In a separate bowl, whisk together the lemon juice, lemon zest, olive oil, minced garlic, fresh thyme, salt, and pepper.
  4. Pour the marinade over the chicken cubes and toss to coat evenly. Let it marinate for at least 30 minutes.
  5. Thread the chicken cubes onto skewers and grill for 10-12 minutes, flipping halfway through, until the chicken is cooked through and slightly charred.
  6. Serve the grilled lemon herb chicken skewers with a side of grilled vegetables or a fresh salad.

Grilled Shrimp Tacos with Mango Salsa

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 pound large shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon chili powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon cumin
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 4 small tortillas

For the Mango Salsa:

  • 1 ripe mango, peeled and diced
  • 1/4 cup red onion, finely chopped
  • 1/4 cup fresh cilantro, chopped
  • Juice of 1 lime

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your grill to medium heat.
  2. In a bowl, toss the shrimp with olive oil, chili powder, cumin, salt, and pepper.
  3. Thread the shrimp onto skewers and grill for 2-3 minutes per side, until they turn pink and opaque.
  4. While the shrimp is grilling, prepare the mango salsa by combining the diced mango, red onion, cilantro, and lime juice in a bowl. Mix well.
  5. Warm the tortillas on the grill for about 30 seconds on each side.
  6. To assemble the tacos, place a few grilled shrimp on each tortilla and top with the mango salsa.
  7. Serve the grilled shrimp tacos with a side of guacamole and sour cream.

Grilled Vegetable Skewers

Ingredients:

  • 1 zucchini, sliced into 1/2-inch rounds
  • 1 yellow squash, sliced into 1/2-inch rounds
  • 1 red bell pepper, cut into 1-inch pieces
  • 1 red onion, cut into wedges
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your grill to medium-high heat.
  2. In a large bowl, combine the zucchini, yellow squash, red bell pepper, red onion, olive oil, minced garlic, dried oregano, salt, and pepper. Toss to coat the vegetables evenly.
  3. Thread the vegetables onto skewers, alternating between different types for a colorful presentation.
  4. Grill the vegetable skewers for 8-10 minutes, turning occasionally, until the vegetables are tender and slightly charred.
  5. Remove the skewers from the grill and serve as a side dish or as a vegetarian main course.

Grilled Pineapple with Honey Lime Glaze

Ingredients:

  • 1 ripe pineapple, peeled and cut into rings
  • 2 tablespoons honey
  • Juice of 1 lime
  • Fresh mint leaves for garnish (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your grill to medium heat.
  2. In a small bowl, whisk together the honey and lime juice.
  3. Brush the honey lime glaze onto both sides of the pineapple rings.
  4. Grill the pineapple rings for 2-3 minutes per side until they are caramelized and slightly charred.
  5. Remove the grilled pineapple from the grill and garnish with fresh mint leaves if desired.
  6. Serve the grilled pineapple as a refreshing dessert or as a topping for ice cream.

There you have it! Four delectable grilling recipes for two that will surely sizzle up your BBQ and create unforgettable moments with your loved one. Enjoy the flavors, embrace the warmth of the grill, and cherish the time spent together. Happy grilling!
