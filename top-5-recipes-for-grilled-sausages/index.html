Top 5 Recipes for Grilled Sausages


Grilled sausages are the ultimate comfort food, perfect for any outdoor barbecue or a cozy weeknight dinner. Whether you’re a seasoned grilling expert or a beginner, grilling sausages is easy, quick, and delicious. The best part is that sausages come in many varieties, from spicy Italian to savory bratwurst, ensuring there’s a flavor to satisfy every palate.

In this post, we will explore five unique and mouthwatering recipes for grilled sausages that will elevate your grilling game. From classic combinations to bold, creative twists, these recipes will impress your guests and add a new dimension to your sausage grilling repertoire. So fire up that grill and get ready to enjoy some sizzling sausages like never before!

1. Classic Grilled Bratwurst with Sauerkraut and Mustard

There’s nothing quite like a classic bratwurst, especially when it’s paired with tangy sauerkraut and spicy mustard. This recipe is simple, yet incredibly satisfying, making it an all-time favorite for sausage lovers. Whether you’re tailgating, hosting a summer party, or enjoying a quiet evening with family, this dish is sure to please.

Ingredients:

  • 4 bratwurst sausages
  • 1 cup sauerkraut, drained and warmed
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons yellow mustard
  • 4 hearty buns (preferably bratwurst buns)
  • Salt and pepper, to taste

Directions:

  1. Preheat your grill: Heat the grill to medium-high heat, ensuring the grates are clean and well-oiled to prevent sticking.
  2. Grill the bratwurst: Place the bratwurst sausages on the grill and cook for 7-10 minutes, turning occasionally to ensure even cooking. Bratwurst should be golden brown on the outside and cooked through on the inside (an internal temperature of 160°F).
  3. Warm the sauerkraut: While the sausages are grilling, heat the sauerkraut in a small pan over medium heat. Season it with a pinch of salt and pepper to taste.
  4. Assemble the bratwurst: Once the sausages are grilled to perfection, place them in the buns. Top each bratwurst with a generous spoonful of sauerkraut and a dollop of yellow mustard.
  5. Serve and enjoy: Serve immediately and enjoy with your favorite side dishes like potato salad or coleslaw.

This simple yet satisfying recipe is a tribute to classic German flavors and is guaranteed to be a crowd-pleaser.

2. Spicy Italian Sausages with Peppers and Onions

For those who love a bit of heat, this recipe for grilled spicy Italian sausages with sautéed peppers and onions is a must-try. The smoky, savory sausages pair perfectly with the sweetness of bell peppers and the slight bitterness of caramelized onions. The combination creates a flavorful, spicy, and slightly sweet bite with every forkful.

Ingredients:

  • 4 spicy Italian sausages
  • 2 red bell peppers, sliced
  • 2 green bell peppers, sliced
  • 1 large yellow onion, sliced
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon balsamic vinegar
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • Crusty baguette or Italian rolls for serving

Directions:

  1. Prepare the grill: Preheat your grill to medium-high heat, ensuring it is well-oiled to prevent the sausages from sticking.
  2. Grill the sausages: Place the spicy Italian sausages on the grill and cook for 8-10 minutes, turning frequently to ensure they are evenly grilled. They should have a nice brown crust and be cooked through (internal temperature of 160°F).
  3. Sauté the peppers and onions: While the sausages are grilling, heat olive oil in a skillet over medium heat. Add the sliced bell peppers and onions and sauté them for about 5-7 minutes, or until they are tender and slightly caramelized. Stir in the balsamic vinegar and cook for another 2 minutes to blend the flavors. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  4. Assemble the sausages: Once the sausages are grilled, place them on a serving platter or in individual rolls. Top with the sautéed peppers and onions.
  5. Serve and enjoy: Serve these spicy Italian sausages with your favorite sides, such as a fresh green salad or roasted potatoes. The rich, savory flavors of the sausages and the sweetness of the peppers and onions are a perfect combination.

This recipe brings a zesty flair to your grilling sessions and is perfect for those who love a little extra spice.

3. BBQ Bacon-Wrapped Sausages

There’s no such thing as too much bacon, especially when it’s wrapped around a savory sausage and grilled to crispy perfection. This BBQ bacon-wrapped sausage recipe takes things to the next level by combining the smoky flavors of bacon with the savory richness of the sausage, all coated in a tangy barbecue sauce. It’s the perfect treat for bacon lovers and barbecue enthusiasts alike.

Ingredients:

  • 4 breakfast sausages (or your preferred sausage variety)
  • 8 strips of bacon
  • ½ cup barbecue sauce (your favorite brand)
  • Salt and pepper, to taste

Directions:

  1. Prepare the grill: Preheat your grill to medium heat, ensuring the grates are well-oiled.
  2. Wrap the sausages: Take two strips of bacon and wrap them around each sausage, securing the ends with toothpicks if needed. You want the bacon to cover the entire sausage for maximum flavor.
  3. Grill the sausages: Place the bacon-wrapped sausages on the grill and cook for 10-12 minutes, turning frequently to ensure even cooking. The bacon should be crispy and golden brown, while the sausage should be cooked through (internal temperature of 160°F).
  4. Glaze with barbecue sauce: During the last few minutes of grilling, brush the sausages with a generous amount of barbecue sauce. Grill for an additional 2-3 minutes to allow the sauce to caramelize slightly.
  5. Serve and enjoy: Remove the toothpicks and serve these juicy BBQ bacon-wrapped sausages hot off the grill. They pair wonderfully with grilled corn, baked beans, or a fresh coleslaw.

This recipe is a showstopper that will have everyone coming back for more. The crispy bacon combined with the rich, savory sausage and sweet barbecue sauce is irresistible.

4. Grilled Chicken Sausage with Fresh Herb Pesto

For a lighter, healthier take on grilled sausages, try this grilled chicken sausage recipe with a vibrant herb pesto. The savory chicken sausages are perfectly complemented by the fresh, zesty pesto made with basil, garlic, and Parmesan. This combination is a refreshing twist on the traditional grilled sausage and will add a burst of flavor to your meal.

Ingredients:

  • 4 chicken sausages (preferably with herbs or garlic)
  • 1 cup fresh basil leaves
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • ¼ cup Parmesan cheese, grated
  • ¼ cup olive oil
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • Lemon wedges, for serving

Directions:

  1. Preheat the grill: Heat your grill to medium-high heat, making sure the grates are clean and well-oiled.
  2. Grill the chicken sausages: Place the chicken sausages on the grill and cook for 6-8 minutes, turning occasionally to ensure they are evenly cooked. The sausages should reach an internal temperature of 165°F.
  3. Make the pesto: While the sausages are grilling, prepare the pesto. In a food processor, combine the fresh basil, garlic, Parmesan, and olive oil. Blend until smooth, then season with salt and pepper to taste.
  4. Serve with pesto: Once the sausages are grilled, place them on a serving plate and drizzle with the fresh herb pesto. Serve with lemon wedges on the side for an extra burst of flavor.
  5. Serve and enjoy: These grilled chicken sausages with pesto are light and refreshing, making them a great option for a summer dinner. Pair them with a side of grilled vegetables or a quinoa salad for a healthy and satisfying meal.

This recipe is perfect for those who prefer a leaner option without sacrificing flavor. The fresh pesto gives the sausage a bright, herby note that elevates the entire dish.

5. Cajun Sausages with Grilled Corn and Avocado Salsa

If you’re looking for a bold, zesty recipe, these Cajun-spiced sausages with grilled corn and avocado salsa are the perfect choice. The spicy sausage is complemented by the smoky sweetness of grilled corn and the creamy richness of avocado salsa. This recipe is bursting with flavor and will make your taste buds dance.

Ingredients:

  • 4 Cajun sausages
  • 2 ears of corn, husked
  • 1 ripe avocado, diced
  • 1 medium tomato, diced
  • 1 small red onion, diced
  • 1 tablespoon fresh cilantro, chopped
  • 1 tablespoon lime juice
  • Salt and pepper, to taste

Directions:

  1. Preheat the grill: Heat your grill to medium-high, ensuring the grates are well-oiled.
  2. Grill the sausages and corn: Place the Cajun sausages on the grill and cook for 8-10 minutes, turning frequently. At the same time, place the corn on the grill and cook, turning every 2-3 minutes, until it’s charred on all sides.
  3. Prepare the salsa: While the sausages and corn are grilling, make the avocado salsa. In a bowl, combine the diced avocado, tomato, onion, cilantro, lime juice, salt, and pepper. Toss gently to combine.
  4. Assemble the dish: Once the sausages are grilled to perfection, remove the corn from the grill. Cut the corn kernels off the cob and mix them into the avocado salsa.
  5. Serve and enjoy: Serve the Cajun sausages with a generous helping of the grilled corn and avocado salsa on top. This dish is perfect for a backyard barbecue or a casual get-together.

The bold flavors of Cajun seasoning paired with the smoky sweetness of grilled corn and the creamy avocado salsa make this dish a standout.

Conclusion

Grilled sausages are a versatile and delicious meal option for any occasion. Whether you prefer classic bratwurst or a more adventurous Cajun sausage, these recipes offer a variety of flavors that will keep everyone coming back for more. The key to perfect grilled sausages lies in choosing high-quality ingredients and experimenting with different toppings and sides to create a complete and satisfying meal.

So next time you fire up the grill, try one (or all!) of these fantastic sausage recipes. With these dishes, you’re guaranteed to impress your family and friends while enjoying some of the most flavorful sausages around!
