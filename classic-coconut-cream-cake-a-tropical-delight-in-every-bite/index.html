Classic Coconut Cream Cake: A Tropical Delight in Every Bite


Coconut lovers, rejoice! We have a treat for you that will transport your taste buds to a tropical paradise. Introducing the Classic Coconut Cream Cake, a dessert that combines the rich and creamy flavor of coconut with a light and fluffy cake base. Get ready to indulge in a slice of heaven!

Ingredients

For the cake:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 ½ cups granulated sugar
  • 1 cup shredded coconut
  • 1 ½ teaspoons baking powder
  • ½ teaspoon baking soda
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • ½ cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1 cup coconut milk
  • 3 large eggs
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

For the coconut cream filling:

  • 1 can (14 ounces) coconut milk
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • ½ cup powdered sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

For the frosting:

  • 2 cups heavy cream
  • ½ cup powdered sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 cup shredded coconut, toasted

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease and flour two 9-inch round cake pans.

  2. In a large mixing bowl, combine the flour, sugar, shredded coconut, baking powder, baking soda, and salt. Mix well to ensure all the ingredients are evenly distributed.

  3. Add the softened butter, coconut milk, eggs, and vanilla extract to the dry ingredients. Using an electric mixer, beat the mixture on medium speed for 2 minutes, until the batter is smooth and well combined.

  4. Divide the batter equally between the prepared cake pans. Smooth the tops with a spatula to create an even surface.

  5. Bake the cakes in the preheated oven for 25-30 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean. Remove the cakes from the oven and let them cool in the pans for 10 minutes. Then, transfer them to a wire rack to cool completely.

  6. While the cakes are cooling, prepare the coconut cream filling. In a mixing bowl, combine the coconut milk, heavy cream, powdered sugar, and vanilla extract. Beat the mixture on high speed until soft peaks form.

  7. Once the cakes are completely cooled, carefully slice each cake horizontally to create four layers in total.

  8. Place one cake layer on a serving plate or cake stand. Spread a generous amount of the coconut cream filling evenly over the layer. Repeat this step with the remaining layers, making sure to spread the filling evenly between each layer.

  9. In a separate mixing bowl, prepare the frosting by combining the heavy cream, powdered sugar, and vanilla extract. Whip the mixture on high speed until stiff peaks form.

  10. Frost the entire cake with the whipped cream frosting, covering the sides and top. Sprinkle the toasted shredded coconut over the frosting, gently pressing it into the sides of the cake for a beautiful and delicious finishing touch.

  11. Refrigerate the Classic Coconut Cream Cake for at least 2 hours to allow the flavors to meld together and the frosting to set.

  12. Serve chilled and enjoy the tropical delight in every bite! This cake is perfect for any occasion, whether it’s a summer barbecue, birthday celebration, or simply a treat for yourself.

Conclusion

The Classic Coconut Cream Cake is a true tropical delight that will transport you to a sunny beach with each bite. The combination of moist coconut cake, creamy coconut filling, and fluffy whipped cream frosting creates a harmonious blend of flavors and textures that will leave you craving more. Whether you’re a coconut lover or simply looking for a unique dessert to impress your guests, this cake is sure to be a hit. So, grab your apron and get ready to embark on a culinary journey to paradise with this irresistible coconut creation!
