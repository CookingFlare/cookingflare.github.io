Mediterranean-Style Fish Tacos with Slaw


Are you tired of the same old tacos? Look no further. This Mediterranean-Style Fish Tacos with Slaw recipe combines the vibrant flavors of the Mediterranean with the convenience of a taco. This dish is perfect for a weeknight dinner or a quick and easy meal. The key to this recipe is using a flavorful fish, such as salmon or tilapia, and marinating it with a mix of Mediterranean flavors, including lemon juice, olive oil, garlic, and oregano. The slaw, made with shredded cabbage, carrots, red onion, cilantro, lemon juice, and tahini, provides a cool and creamy contrast to the warm crispy fish. Servings: 4 - 6 Prep Time: 20 minutes Cook Time: 10 - 12 minutes Total Time: 30 - 32 minutes Ingredients For the Fish:

1 pound fish (such as salmon or tilapia) 1/4 cup lemon juice 2 tablespoons olive oil 2 cloves garlic, minced 1 tablespoon chopped fresh oregano Salt and pepper, to taste

For the Slaw:

1 cup shredded cabbage 1 cup shredded carrots 1/4 cup thinly sliced red onion 1/4 cup chopped fresh cilantro 2 tablespoons lemon juice 2 tablespoons tahini 1/4 cup olive oil Salt and pepper, to taste

For the Tacos:

4 - 6 corn tortillas Lemon wedges, for serving

Instructions Step 1: Prepare the Fish In a large bowl, whisk together lemon juice, olive oil, garlic, and oregano. Add the fish and toss to coat. Cover and refrigerate for at least 10 minutes. Step 2: Prepare the Slaw In a large bowl, whisk together lemon juice, tahini, and olive oil. Add the shredded cabbage, carrots, red onion, and cilantro. Toss to coat and season with salt and pepper to taste. Set aside. Step 3: Cook the Fish Heat a large skillet over medium-high heat. Remove the fish from the marinade, letting any excess liquid drip off. Cook the fish for 4 - 5 minutes per side, until it's cooked through and flakes easily with a fork. Step 4: Assemble the Tacos Warm the tortillas by wrapping them in a damp paper towel and microwaving for 20 - 30 seconds. Cut the cooked fish into small pieces and divide among the tortillas. Top each taco with a spoonful of slaw and a squeeze of lemon juice. Serve immediately. Notes

This recipe can be made with any type of fish, but salmon and tilapia are our top recommendations. Make sure to pat the fish dry before cooking to prevent it from steaming instead of searing. To make this recipe more substantial, add some crispy grilled halloumi cheese or crunchy capers to the tacos.

Tips and Variations

If you want to add some heat to your slaw, add some diced jalapeño or serrano peppers. Swap out the cabbage for shredded kale or napa cabbage for a different flavor. For a vegan version, omit the fish and add some sliced grilled portobello mushrooms or eggplant to the tacos.

Nutrition Information

Calories: 350 Fat: 20g Saturated Fat: 3.5g Cholesterol: 20mg Carbohydrates: 15g Fiber: 2g Protein: 30g

This Mediterranean-Style Fish Tacos with Slaw recipe is a delicious and easy meal that will transport your taste buds to the Mediterranean coast. With its vibrant flavors and healthy ingredients, this recipe is perfect for a quick and delicious dinner.
