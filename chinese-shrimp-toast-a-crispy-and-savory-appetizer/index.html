Chinese Shrimp Toast: A Crispy and Savory Appetizer


If you’re a fan of Chinese cuisine, you’ve likely come across the delightful dish known as Shrimp Toast. This crispy and savory appetizer has been a staple in Chinese restaurants for decades, and for good reason. It’s a perfect combination of crunchy, savory, and satisfying flavors that are sure to impress your guests. In this recipe, we’ll explore the art of making Chinese Shrimp Toast at home, allowing you to enjoy this delicious appetizer any time you desire.

Ingredients

To make Chinese Shrimp Toast, you’ll need the following ingredients:

  • 1 pound of fresh shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon of grated fresh ginger
  • 2 green onions, finely chopped
  • 1 tablespoon of soy sauce
  • 1 teaspoon of sesame oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 egg
  • 6 slices of white bread, crusts removed
  • Cornstarch for coating
  • Vegetable oil for frying

Instructions

  1. Begin by preparing the shrimp. In a food processor, combine the fresh shrimp, minced garlic, grated ginger, and chopped green onions. Pulse the mixture until it forms a coarse paste.

  2. Transfer the shrimp mixture to a bowl and add the soy sauce, sesame oil, salt, and pepper. Mix well to ensure that the seasonings are evenly distributed.

  3. In a separate bowl, lightly beat the egg. Take each slice of bread and dip it into the beaten egg, ensuring that both sides are coated.

  4. Spread an even layer of the shrimp mixture onto one side of each slice of bread. Be generous with the shrimp mixture, ensuring that it covers the bread evenly.

  5. Once the bread is coated with the shrimp mixture, sprinkle a thin layer of cornstarch over the top, gently pressing it into the shrimp mixture to ensure it adheres.

  6. In a large skillet, heat vegetable oil over medium-high heat. Carefully place the shrimp toasts, shrimp side down, into the hot oil. Fry until golden brown and crispy, then flip and fry the other side until golden as well.

  7. Once the shrimp toasts are golden and crispy on both sides, remove them from the oil and place them on a paper towel to drain any excess oil.

  8. Allow the shrimp toasts to cool slightly before cutting them into triangles or rectangles, and they’re ready to be served!

Serving Suggestions

Chinese Shrimp Toast is best served hot and crispy, accompanied by a dipping sauce of your choice. Some popular options include sweet chili sauce, plum sauce, or even a simple soy sauce and vinegar mixture. The crispy exterior and succulent shrimp interior make for a delightful combination of textures and flavors that will leave your guests wanting more.

Conclusion

With its crispy exterior and savory shrimp filling, Chinese Shrimp Toast is a delightful appetizer that is sure to impress. By following this recipe, you can recreate this classic dish in the comfort of your own kitchen, allowing you to savor its delicious flavors any time you desire. Whether you’re hosting a dinner party or simply craving a taste of Chinese cuisine, this recipe for Chinese Shrimp Toast is bound to become a favorite in your culinary repertoire. Enjoy!
