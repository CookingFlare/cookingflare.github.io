Caramel Apple Hand Pies: A Portable and Sweet Fall Treat


Fall is the perfect time to indulge in all things apple and cinnamon, and what better way to do so than with a batch of delicious caramel apple hand pies? These delightful treats are not only easy to make but also portable, making them the ideal sweet treat for picnics, lunchboxes, or simply enjoying on the go. In this blog post, we’ll walk you through a step-by-step guide on how to create these delectable hand pies, complete with a flaky crust, a gooey caramel apple filling, and a touch of cinnamon that will have your taste buds singing with delight.

Ingredients

For the crust:

  • 2 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup unsalted butter, cold and cut into small cubes
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • 1/2 cup ice water

For the filling:

  • 4 medium apples, peeled, cored, and diced
  • 1/2 cup brown sugar
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 teaspoon nutmeg
  • 1/4 cup caramel sauce
  • 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice

For assembly:

  • 1 egg, beaten (for egg wash)
  • Granulated sugar (for sprinkling)

Instructions

Step 1: Prepare the Crust

  1. In a large mixing bowl, combine the flour, salt, and sugar.
  2. Add the cold, cubed butter to the flour mixture and use a pastry cutter or your fingertips to work the butter into the flour until it resembles coarse crumbs.
  3. Gradually add the ice water, a few tablespoons at a time, and mix until the dough starts to come together.
  4. Divide the dough in half, shape each half into a disc, and wrap them in plastic wrap. Refrigerate for at least 1 hour.

Step 2: Make the Filling

  1. In a medium bowl, combine the diced apples, brown sugar, cinnamon, nutmeg, caramel sauce, flour, and lemon juice. Toss until the apples are evenly coated.

Step 3: Assemble the Hand Pies

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C) and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
  2. On a lightly floured surface, roll out one disc of the chilled dough to about 1/8 inch thickness. Use a round cutter to cut out circles of dough (approximately 5 inches in diameter).
  3. Place a spoonful of the apple filling in the center of each dough circle.
  4. Brush the edges of the dough with the beaten egg, fold the dough over the filling to create a half-moon shape, and crimp the edges with a fork to seal.
  5. Place the assembled hand pies on the prepared baking sheet, brush the tops with the remaining beaten egg, and sprinkle with granulated sugar.

Step 4: Bake and Enjoy

  1. Bake the hand pies for 20-25 minutes, or until the crust is golden brown and the filling is bubbling.
  2. Allow the hand pies to cool for a few minutes before serving.

Conclusion

These caramel apple hand pies are a wonderful way to celebrate the flavors of fall. Whether you’re hosting a gathering or simply looking for a delightful treat to enjoy on a crisp autumn day, these portable pies are sure to be a hit. The combination of buttery, flaky crust, sweet caramel, and tender apples will have you reaching for seconds. So, roll up your sleeves, preheat the oven, and get ready to savor the deliciousness of caramel apple hand pies!
