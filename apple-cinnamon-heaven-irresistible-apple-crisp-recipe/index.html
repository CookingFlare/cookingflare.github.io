Apple Cinnamon Heaven: Irresistible Apple Crisp Recipe


Nothing beats the comforting aroma of warm apples and cinnamon wafting through the kitchen. If you’re looking for a delightful dessert that will satisfy your sweet tooth, look no further than this irresistible Apple Crisp recipe. With its perfect balance of sweetness and tartness, combined with a crunchy oat topping, this dessert is sure to become a family favorite. So grab some fresh apples and let’s get baking!

Ingredients

For the Apple Filling:

  • 6 cups of peeled, cored, and thinly sliced apples (about 6 medium-sized apples)
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground nutmeg
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice

For the Crisp Topping:

  • 1 cup old-fashioned oats
  • 1/2 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup packed light brown sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, cold and cut into small pieces

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C). Grease a 9x9-inch baking dish and set it aside.

  2. In a large bowl, combine the sliced apples, granulated sugar, flour, cinnamon, nutmeg, salt, and lemon juice. Toss until the apples are evenly coated with the mixture. Transfer the apple filling into the prepared baking dish and spread it out evenly.

  3. In another bowl, prepare the crisp topping. Combine the oats, flour, brown sugar, cinnamon, and salt. Mix well. Add the cold pieces of butter and use your fingers or a pastry cutter to work the butter into the dry ingredients until the mixture resembles coarse crumbs.

  4. Sprinkle the crisp topping evenly over the apple filling in the baking dish.

  5. Place the baking dish in the preheated oven and bake for about 40-45 minutes, or until the topping is golden brown and the apples are tender when pierced with a fork.

  6. Once the Apple Crisp is done baking, remove it from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes before serving. This will allow the juices to thicken slightly.

  7. Serve warm, either on its own or with a scoop of vanilla ice cream or a dollop of whipped cream for an extra indulgence. Enjoy!

Tips and Variations

  • For a twist on the classic recipe, try adding a handful of chopped walnuts or pecans to the crisp topping for an extra crunch.

  • If you prefer a sweeter filling, you can increase the amount of sugar in the apple filling by an additional 1/4 cup.

  • This recipe can easily be doubled or halved depending on your needs. Simply adjust the quantities accordingly and use a larger or smaller baking dish.

  • Leftovers can be stored in an airtight container in the refrigerator for up to 3 days. Reheat in the oven or microwave before serving.

Conclusion

Indulge in the heavenly combination of apples, cinnamon, and a crisp oat topping with this irresistible Apple Crisp recipe. Whether you’re hosting a dinner party or simply craving a comforting dessert, this recipe is a winner. The warm, fragrant scent of apples and cinnamon will fill your home, making everyone eagerly await the moment they can dig into a spoonful of this delightful treat. So go ahead, bring a taste of Apple Cinnamon Heaven to your table and create lasting memories with this irresistible Apple Crisp recipe.
