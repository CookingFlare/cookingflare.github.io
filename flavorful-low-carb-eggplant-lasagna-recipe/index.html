Flavorful Low Carb Eggplant Lasagna Recipe


If you’re looking for a delicious low-carb alternative to traditional lasagna, this flavorful eggplant lasagna recipe is just what you need. Packed with layers of savory flavors and wholesome ingredients, this dish is a perfect choice for those seeking a healthy and satisfying meal. Whether you’re following a low-carb diet or simply looking to incorporate more veggies into your meals, this eggplant lasagna is sure to impress your taste buds and leave you feeling fully satisfied.

Ingredients

For the Eggplant Layers:

  • 2 large eggplants, sliced lengthwise
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper

For the Meat Sauce:

  • 1 pound ground turkey or beef
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can (14 ounces) crushed tomatoes
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

For the Cheese Filling:

  • 1 1/2 cups ricotta cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 1/4 cup fresh basil, chopped
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Additional:

  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Fresh basil leaves for garnish

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Eggplant:

    • Preheat the oven to 400°F (200°C).
    • Place the eggplant slices on a baking sheet and drizzle with olive oil. Sprinkle with salt and pepper. Bake for 15-20 minutes, or until the eggplant is tender. Remove from the oven and set aside.

  2. Make the Meat Sauce:

    • In a large skillet, cook the ground turkey or beef over medium heat until browned. Add the chopped onion and garlic, and cook until softened. Stir in the crushed tomatoes, oregano, basil, salt, and pepper. Simmer for 10-15 minutes, then remove from heat.

  3. Prepare the Cheese Filling:

    • In a bowl, combine the ricotta cheese, Parmesan cheese, egg, chopped basil, salt, and pepper. Mix until well combined.

  4. Assemble the Lasagna:

    • In a greased 9x13 inch baking dish, spread a thin layer of the meat sauce. Arrange a layer of eggplant slices on top of the sauce, then spread a layer of the cheese filling. Sprinkle with mozzarella cheese. Repeat the layers, finishing with a final layer of meat sauce and mozzarella cheese on top.

  5. Bake the Lasagna:

    • Cover the baking dish with foil and bake for 25 minutes. Remove the foil and bake for an additional 10-15 minutes, or until the cheese is bubbly and golden brown.

  6. Serve and Enjoy:

    • Allow the lasagna to cool for a few minutes, then garnish with fresh basil leaves before serving. Slice into portions and savor the flavors of this delicious low-carb eggplant lasagna.

Nutritional Information

  • Serving Size: 1 slice
  • Calories: 280
  • Total Fat: 15g
  • Saturated Fat: 7g
  • Cholesterol: 65mg
  • Sodium: 520mg
  • Total Carbohydrates: 14g
  • Dietary Fiber: 5g
  • Sugars: 7g
  • Protein: 23g

Final Thoughts

With its rich layers of roasted eggplant, flavorful meat sauce, and creamy cheese filling, this low-carb eggplant lasagna is a true crowd-pleaser. Whether you’re cooking for a family dinner or hosting a gathering with friends, this recipe is an excellent choice for a satisfying and nourishing meal. Embrace the flavors of this hearty dish and delight in the fact that you can enjoy a comforting lasagna while staying true to your low-carb lifestyle. Bon appétit!
