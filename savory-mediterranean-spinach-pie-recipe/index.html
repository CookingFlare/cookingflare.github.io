Savory Mediterranean Spinach Pie Recipe


If you're looking for a delicious and nutritious meal option that's perfect for any occasion, you're in luck. Today, we're sharing a mouthwatering Savory Mediterranean Spinach Pie Recipe that's sure to become a staple in your kitchen. This flavorful pie combines the brightness of the Mediterranean with the comforting warmth of a savory pie, making it a perfect dish to serve to both vegetarian and meat-eating friends and family. The Story Behind the Recipe The Mediterranean diet is renowned for its incredible health benefits, which are made all the more delicious with the fresh flavors and aromas of the region. Our Savory Mediterranean Spinach Pie is an innovative fusion of Greek, Italian, and Middle Eastern flavors, as we blend the humble spinach with the sweetness of feta cheese and the boisterous zing of sun-dried tomatoes. This harmonious blend is the perfect canvas for creating a vegetarian masterpiece that is both nutritious and unforgettable. Ingredients Before you get started on this Savory Mediterranean Spinach Pie Recipe, make sure you have the following ingredients ready to go:

1 package of puff pastry dough (homemade or store-bought) 1 package of frozen spinach, thawed and drained 1 medium onion, finely chopped 3 cloves of garlic, minced 1 cup of crumbled feta cheese 1 cup of small, unpeeled cherry tomatoes, halved 1/4 cup of olive oil 1 tsp of dried oregano Salt and pepper, to taste 1 beaten egg, for egg wash

Instructions

Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C). Line a baking sheet with parchment paper or a silicone mat. In a large skillet over medium heat, add the olive oil and sauté the chopped onion and minced garlic until softened and translucent, about 5 minutes. Add the thawed spinach and cook for 2-3 minutes, allowing it to collapse. Season with salt, pepper, and dried oregano. Roll out the puff pastry dough to a thickness of 1/8 inch. Position the dough on the prepared baking sheet. With a knife, create a 1-inch border around the edges of the pastry. In a bowl, mix the cooked spinach mixture with crumbled feta cheese and cherry tomatoes. Spoon the mixture evenly onto the pastry, leaving a 1-inch space from the border. Fold the 1-inch border over the filling, and cut a decorative edge to help hold the filling in. If desired, create some fork pinches to add some visual presentation. Brush the edges with the beaten egg and make a few cross-hatches on the surface to allow for better browning during baking. Carefully slide the pie onto the baking sheet, making sure the border is away from the baking sheet edges. Bake the pie for 35-40 minutes or until the crust is golden brown, and the filling is bubbly.

Functionality Parts: Storage and Decoration

This spinach pie will need to rest and reheat if savored cold for an hour. Wrap the pie tightly in plastic wrap or aluminum foil, or keep refrigerated and rewarm for a few minutes in the oven or microwave before serving. The cooled pie will easily keep at room temperature for up to 5 hours for everyday usage.

The warm, savory flavor of the Savory Mediterranean Spinach Pie is a culinary masterpiece that pairs well with pasta, salads, or roasted vegetables. Perfect for your dinner/happy hour socializing social circle. Store leftovers for the next meal.
