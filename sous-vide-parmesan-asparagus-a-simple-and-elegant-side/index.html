Sous Vide Parmesan Asparagus: A Simple and Elegant Side


When it comes to creating the perfect side dish, asparagus is a timeless favorite. Its delicate flavor and versatile nature make it a wonderful addition to any meal. Today, we’re taking this classic vegetable to the next level with a modern cooking technique – sous vide. By cooking asparagus sous vide, we can achieve the ideal texture and preserve the vibrant green color, resulting in a truly elegant and flavorful dish. Paired with the rich umami of Parmesan cheese, this Sous Vide Parmesan Asparagus is sure to impress your guests and elevate any dining experience.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound (450g) fresh asparagus, woody ends trimmed
  • 2 tablespoons (30ml) olive oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon (5ml) lemon juice
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1/2 cup (50g) grated Parmesan cheese
  • Fresh parsley for garnish

Instructions

Step 1: Preparing the Asparagus

Begin by preheating the sous vide water bath to 185°F (85°C). While the water bath is heating, prepare the asparagus by trimming off the woody ends. This can be done by gently bending each spear until it naturally snaps at the point where the tough part ends. Then, place the asparagus in a large resealable bag.

Step 2: Flavor Infusion

In a small bowl, mix together the olive oil, minced garlic, lemon juice, salt, and pepper. Pour this flavorful mixture over the asparagus in the bag, ensuring that the spears are evenly coated. Seal the bag using the water displacement method to remove any air, allowing the marinade to fully infuse the asparagus.

Step 3: Sous Vide Cooking

Once the water bath has reached the desired temperature, carefully submerge the sealed bag of asparagus and cook for 15-20 minutes. This gentle cooking process will result in perfectly tender asparagus with a vibrant green color and enhanced flavor.

Step 4: Finishing Touches

After the cooking time is complete, carefully remove the bag from the water bath and transfer the asparagus to a serving plate. Sprinkle the grated Parmesan cheese generously over the asparagus, allowing the residual heat to melt the cheese slightly. Garnish with fresh parsley for a pop of color and added freshness.

Serving Suggestions

This Sous Vide Parmesan Asparagus pairs beautifully with a variety of dishes. Serve it alongside a juicy steak for a luxurious steakhouse experience, or incorporate it into a light pasta dish for a refreshing springtime meal. The delicate flavors of the asparagus and the savory richness of the Parmesan cheese make it a versatile side that complements a wide range of main courses.

Conclusion

In conclusion, Sous Vide Parmesan Asparagus is a simple yet elegant side dish that showcases the natural beauty and flavor of this beloved vegetable. By harnessing the precision of sous vide cooking, we can elevate the humble asparagus to a gourmet level, making it a standout addition to any dining occasion. Whether you’re a seasoned home cook or a culinary enthusiast looking to expand your repertoire, this recipe is a must-try for anyone seeking to impress their guests with a sophisticated and delightful side dish.
