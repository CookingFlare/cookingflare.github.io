BBQ Chicken Salad Wraps: Healthy and Flavorful Recipes


Are you looking for a delicious and nutritious meal that’s bursting with flavor? Look no further than BBQ Chicken Salad Wraps! These wraps are not only packed with protein and fresh vegetables, but they also offer a delightful combination of smoky barbecue flavor and crisp salad goodness. Whether you’re hosting a summer gathering or simply want to enjoy a satisfying and wholesome meal, these BBQ Chicken Salad Wraps are sure to impress.

Ingredients

To make these mouthwatering wraps, you’ll need the following ingredients:

  • 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1/4 cup barbecue sauce
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 4 large tortilla wraps
  • 2 cups mixed salad greens
  • 1/2 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1/4 cup red onion, thinly sliced
  • 1/4 cup corn kernels
  • 1/4 cup black beans
  • 1/4 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1/4 cup ranch dressing

Instructions

  1. Preheat your grill or stovetop grill pan over medium-high heat.

  2. In a small bowl, combine the barbecue sauce, olive oil, garlic powder, salt, and pepper. Mix well.

  3. Brush the chicken breasts with the barbecue sauce mixture, ensuring they are evenly coated.

  4. Place the chicken breasts on the preheated grill and cook for approximately 6-8 minutes per side, or until the internal temperature reaches 165°F (74°C). Cooking times may vary depending on the thickness of the chicken breasts.

  5. Once cooked, remove the chicken from the grill and let it rest for a few minutes. Then, slice it into thin strips.

  6. Take a tortilla wrap and layer it with a handful of mixed salad greens, cherry tomatoes, red onion, corn kernels, black beans, and shredded cheddar cheese.

  7. Drizzle ranch dressing over the salad ingredients.

  8. Arrange a few slices of the grilled barbecue chicken on top of the salad.

  9. Tightly roll the tortilla wrap, ensuring all the ingredients are securely enclosed.

  10. Repeat the process for the remaining tortilla wraps.

  11. Once all the wraps are assembled, slice them in half diagonally for a more appealing presentation.

  12. Serve the BBQ Chicken Salad Wraps immediately and enjoy!

Tips and Variations

  • You can customize these wraps by adding your favorite vegetables or toppings. Try adding sliced avocado, cucumber, or bell peppers for an extra crunch and freshness.

  • If you’re vegetarian or prefer a meat-free option, you can substitute the chicken with grilled tofu or tempeh. Marinate the tofu or tempeh in the barbecue sauce mixture and cook it on the grill until heated through.

  • For a low-carb option, you can use lettuce leaves instead of tortilla wraps. Simply wrap the salad ingredients and barbecue chicken in large lettuce leaves, securing them with toothpicks if needed.

  • If you’re hosting a BBQ party, consider serving these wraps alongside other summer favorites like grilled corn on the cob, coleslaw, or potato salad.

  • Leftover BBQ Chicken Salad Wraps can be stored in an airtight container in the refrigerator for up to 2 days. However, for the best taste and freshness, it’s recommended to enjoy them immediately after assembly.

Final Thoughts

BBQ Chicken Salad Wraps are not only a treat for your taste buds but also a satisfying and nutritious meal. Packed with protein, fiber, and a variety of colorful vegetables, these wraps are a perfect balance of flavors and textures. Whether you’re enjoying them for lunch, dinner, or as a party appetizer, these wraps are sure to be a hit. So, fire up the grill, gather your ingredients, and get ready to savor the deliciousness of BBQ Chicken Salad Wraps!
