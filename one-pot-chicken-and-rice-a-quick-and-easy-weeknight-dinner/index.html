One-Pot Chicken and Rice: A Quick and Easy Weeknight Dinner


Are you tired of spending hours in the kitchen after a long day at work? Look no further than this delicious and hassle-free one-pot chicken and rice recipe! With just a few simple ingredients and minimal prep time, you can have a wholesome and flavorful meal on the table in no time.

Ingredients

  • 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1 cup long-grain white rice
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 red bell pepper, diced
  • 1 cup frozen peas
  • 2 cups chicken broth
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley for garnish

Instructions

  1. Heat a large pot or Dutch oven over medium-high heat. Add a tablespoon of oil and brown the chicken breasts on both sides. Remove the chicken from the pot and set aside.

  2. In the same pot, add the diced onion, minced garlic, and diced red bell pepper. Sauté until the vegetables are softened and fragrant, about 5 minutes.

  3. Add the rice to the pot and stir to coat it with the remaining oil and vegetable mixture. Toast the rice for a minute or two until it starts to turn golden brown.

  4. Pour in the chicken broth, paprika, dried thyme, salt, and pepper. Give everything a good stir to combine.

  5. Place the chicken breasts back into the pot, nestling them into the rice mixture. Cover the pot with a lid and reduce the heat to low. Simmer for 15-20 minutes, or until the rice is tender and the chicken is cooked through.

  6. Remove the chicken breasts from the pot and shred them using two forks. Return the shredded chicken to the pot and add the frozen peas. Stir everything together and let it cook for another 5 minutes, or until the peas are heated through.

  7. Serve the one-pot chicken and rice hot, garnished with fresh parsley. Enjoy!

Why Choose One-Pot Chicken and Rice?

This one-pot chicken and rice recipe is perfect for busy weeknights when you want a satisfying meal without spending hours in the kitchen. Here are a few reasons why you should give it a try:

  1. Quick and Easy: With just a few simple steps, you can have a delicious and well-rounded meal on the table in under 30 minutes. It’s perfect for those nights when you’re short on time but still want a homemade dinner.

  2. Minimal Cleanup: Since everything cooks in one pot, you’ll have fewer dishes to wash after dinner. It’s a win-win situation!

  3. Versatile: This recipe serves as a great base that you can customize to your liking. Feel free to add your favorite vegetables, spices, or even swap the chicken for another protein like shrimp or tofu.

  4. Healthy and Nutritious: Chicken is a lean source of protein, while rice provides carbohydrates for energy. This dish also includes vegetables like onions, bell peppers, and peas, adding essential vitamins and minerals to your meal.

So, the next time you’re looking for a quick and easy dinner idea, give this one-pot chicken and rice recipe a try. It’s a simple yet delicious meal that the whole family will love. Enjoy!
