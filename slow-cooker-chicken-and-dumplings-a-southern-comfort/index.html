Slow Cooker Chicken and Dumplings: A Southern Comfort


If there’s one dish that embodies the warmth and comfort of Southern cooking, it’s slow cooker chicken and dumplings. This beloved classic is the epitome of soul-satisfying comfort food, and the best part is that it’s incredibly easy to make. Today, I’m excited to share my take on this timeless recipe, perfect for those cozy family dinners or lazy Sunday afternoons.

Ingredients

For the chicken stew:

  • 2 lbs of boneless, skinless chicken thighs
  • 4 cups of chicken broth
  • 1 cup of diced carrots
  • 1 cup of diced celery
  • 1 medium onion, finely chopped
  • 3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon of dried thyme
  • 1 teaspoon of dried rosemary
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 cup of frozen peas (added later)

For the dumplings:

  • 2 cups of all-purpose flour
  • 1 tablespoon of baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon of salt
  • 1/4 cup of unsalted butter, cold and cut into small pieces
  • 3/4 cup of milk

Instructions

Step 1: Prep the Chicken Stew

  1. Begin by placing the chicken thighs in the slow cooker.
  2. Add the chicken broth, diced carrots, celery, onion, garlic, dried thyme, dried rosemary, salt, and pepper.
  3. Cover and cook on low for 6-8 hours or on high for 3-4 hours, until the chicken is tender and cooked through.

Step 2: Make the Dumpling Dough

  1. In a mixing bowl, whisk together the flour, baking powder, and salt for the dumplings.
  2. Add the cold, cubed butter and use a pastry cutter or fork to cut it into the flour mixture until it resembles coarse crumbs.
  3. Slowly pour in the milk, stirring until just combined. Be careful not to overmix.

Step 3: Add the Dumplings

  1. Once the chicken is cooked, shred it into bite-sized pieces using two forks.
  2. Increase the slow cooker temperature to high if it’s not already on high.
  3. Drop the dumpling dough by spoonfuls onto the top of the hot chicken stew.
  4. Cover and cook on high for 30-40 minutes, or until the dumplings are cooked through and fluffy.

Step 4: Serve and Enjoy

  1. About 10 minutes before the dumplings are done, add the frozen peas to the slow cooker and gently stir.
  2. Once the dumplings are cooked, ladle the chicken and dumplings into bowls and serve hot.

Why Slow Cooker Chicken and Dumplings?

This slow cooker chicken and dumplings recipe is a game-changer for busy home cooks. The slow cooker does all the work, infusing the stew with rich, savory flavors while keeping the chicken tender and juicy. The dumplings, cooked right in the stew, soak up all the delicious broth, creating a perfect balance of textures and flavors in every bite.

The combination of aromatic herbs, tender chicken, and fluffy dumplings in a comforting broth makes this dish an instant family favorite. It’s a versatile recipe that can be enjoyed year-round, whether you’re looking for a warm, hearty meal in the winter or a no-fuss, crowd-pleasing dinner for a summer gathering.

So, next time you’re craving a taste of Southern comfort, give this slow cooker chicken and dumplings recipe a try. It’s the kind of meal that brings everyone to the table and leaves them feeling nourished and content. After all, good food has a way of bringing people together, and this dish is no exception.

Whether you’re a seasoned home cook or just starting your culinary journey, this recipe is a must-try. It’s a delicious reminder that sometimes, the simplest meals are the most memorable.

I hope you enjoy this recipe and find comfort in the hearty flavors of slow cooker chicken and dumplings. Happy cooking!
