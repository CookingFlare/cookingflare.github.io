Tasty Instant Pot Corn Chowder Recipes for a Creamy Delight


If you’re looking for a comforting and flavorful dish to warm you up on a chilly evening, look no further than these delectable Instant Pot corn chowder recipes. Packed with the sweetness of fresh corn, the creaminess of potatoes, and the savory flavors of bacon and herbs, these recipes will surely become a staple in your kitchen. Plus, the ease and speed of using the Instant Pot make these recipes perfect for busy weeknights when you crave a homemade meal without the fuss.

Classic Corn Chowder

Ingredients:

  • 4 slices bacon, chopped
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 4 cups fresh or frozen corn kernels
  • 2 large potatoes, peeled and diced
  • 4 cups chicken or vegetable broth
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1 teaspoon thyme
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Chopped fresh chives for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Set the Instant Pot to sauté mode and cook the bacon until crispy. Remove the bacon and set aside, leaving the bacon fat in the pot.
  2. Sauté the onion and garlic in the bacon fat until softened.
  3. Add the corn, potatoes, broth, and thyme to the pot. Close the lid and set the Instant Pot to high pressure for 5 minutes.
  4. Once the cooking cycle is complete, perform a quick pressure release.
  5. Stir in the heavy cream and season with salt and pepper.
  6. Using an immersion blender, partially blend the chowder to thicken it while still leaving some chunks of corn and potatoes.
  7. Ladle the chowder into bowls and garnish with the crispy bacon and fresh chives.

Spicy Chipotle Corn Chowder

Ingredients:

  • 4 slices bacon, chopped
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 4 cups fresh or frozen corn kernels
  • 2 large potatoes, peeled and diced
  • 4 cups chicken or vegetable broth
  • 1 cup coconut milk
  • 2 chipotle peppers in adobo sauce, chopped
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh cilantro for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Using the sauté function on the Instant Pot, cook the bacon until it becomes crispy. Remove the bacon and set it aside, leaving the bacon fat in the pot.
  2. Sauté the onion and garlic in the bacon fat until softened.
  3. Add the corn, potatoes, broth, chipotle peppers, and cumin to the pot. Close the lid and cook on high pressure for 5 minutes.
  4. After the cooking cycle, perform a quick pressure release.
  5. Stir in the coconut milk and season with salt and pepper.
  6. Use an immersion blender to partially blend the chowder, creating a creamy yet slightly chunky texture.
  7. Serve the chowder in bowls, topping it with the crispy bacon and fresh cilantro.

Vegan Corn Chowder

Ingredients:

  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 4 cups fresh or frozen corn kernels
  • 2 large potatoes, peeled and diced
  • 4 cups vegetable broth
  • 1 cup coconut cream
  • 1 teaspoon smoked paprika
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Sliced green onions for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Set the Instant Pot to sauté and heat the olive oil. Sauté the onion and garlic until softened.
  2. Add the corn, potatoes, vegetable broth, and smoked paprika to the pot. Close the lid and cook on high pressure for 5 minutes.
  3. Once the cooking cycle is complete, perform a quick pressure release.
  4. Stir in the coconut cream and season with salt and pepper.
  5. Use an immersion blender to partially blend the chowder, leaving some texture while creating a creamy base.
  6. Serve the chowder in bowls, garnishing it with sliced green onions.

Conclusion

With these versatile Instant Pot corn chowder recipes, you can easily whip up a soul-warming meal that’s sure to impress your family and friends. Whether you’re craving a classic, a spicy kick, or a vegan-friendly version, these recipes offer something for everyone. So, dust off your Instant Pot and get ready to enjoy a bowl of creamy delight with every spoonful!
