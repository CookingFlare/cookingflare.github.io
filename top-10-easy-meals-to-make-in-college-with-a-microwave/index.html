Top 10 Easy Meals to Make in College with a Microwave


Being a college student often means dealing with hectic schedules, late-night study sessions, and limited access to a full kitchen. But just because you don’t have a fancy stove or oven doesn’t mean you can’t enjoy delicious, nutritious meals. One of the most convenient tools you can use is your trusty microwave. It’s a quick and easy way to make tasty meals without the hassle of traditional cooking methods. In this blog post, we’ll explore the top 10 easy meals that you can prepare in your microwave, perfect for those busy college days.

1. Microwave Mac and Cheese

Mac and cheese is a comfort food favorite, and with the microwave, you can make a creamy, cheesy dish in no time.

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 cup elbow macaroni
  • 1/4 cup milk
  • 1/2 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1 tablespoon butter
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Place the elbow macaroni in a microwave-safe bowl and add enough water to cover the pasta. Microwave on high for about 4-6 minutes or until the pasta is cooked.
  2. Drain the excess water and add the butter and milk to the bowl.
  3. Stir in the shredded cheddar cheese and microwave for another 30 seconds to melt the cheese.
  4. Mix until creamy and add salt and pepper to taste. Enjoy a warm, cheesy mac and cheese in minutes!

This microwave mac and cheese is easy to make and can be customized with your favorite add-ins like bacon, veggies, or extra spices for a unique twist.

2. Microwave Breakfast Burrito

If you’re looking for a breakfast option that’s both filling and quick, a microwave breakfast burrito is a great choice.

Ingredients:

  • 1 large flour tortilla
  • 1/2 cup scrambled eggs
  • 1/4 cup shredded cheese
  • 1/4 cup salsa (optional)
  • 1/4 cup diced avocado (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Start by scrambling eggs in a microwave-safe bowl. Microwave on high for about 1-2 minutes, stirring halfway through to ensure even cooking.
  2. Once the eggs are cooked, place the tortilla on a microwave-safe plate.
  3. Add the scrambled eggs, shredded cheese, salsa, and diced avocado onto the center of the tortilla.
  4. Fold the sides of the tortilla and roll it up tightly.
  5. Microwave the entire burrito for another 30 seconds to melt the cheese and warm the tortilla.

This breakfast burrito is simple, customizable, and perfect for on-the-go mornings.

3. Microwave Rice Bowl

A microwave rice bowl is a fantastic way to enjoy a balanced meal with protein, veggies, and grains. It’s easy to make and can be tailored to your taste.

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 cup instant rice
  • 1/2 cup frozen vegetables (peas, carrots, corn, etc.)
  • 1/2 cup cooked chicken, beef, or tofu
  • Soy sauce or teriyaki sauce (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the instant rice according to the package instructions in the microwave.
  2. While the rice is cooking, microwave the frozen vegetables in a separate bowl for 2-3 minutes or until heated through.
  3. Once the rice and veggies are done, combine them in a large bowl.
  4. Add the cooked protein (chicken, beef, or tofu) and drizzle with soy sauce or teriyaki sauce for added flavor.
  5. Stir well and enjoy a satisfying, wholesome meal in just minutes.

This rice bowl can be made with any leftover proteins or vegetables you have on hand, making it an excellent choice for meal prepping.

4. Microwave Quesadilla

If you’re craving something cheesy and savory, a microwave quesadilla is the way to go. It’s simple and packed with flavor.

Ingredients:

  • 2 flour tortillas
  • 1/2 cup shredded cheese (cheddar, mozzarella, or a blend)
  • 1/4 cup cooked chicken or beans (optional)
  • Salsa and sour cream (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Lay one tortilla flat on a microwave-safe plate.
  2. Sprinkle a generous amount of cheese on top of the tortilla, and add any additional fillings like cooked chicken or beans.
  3. Place the second tortilla on top to form a sandwich.
  4. Microwave on high for 1-2 minutes, or until the cheese is melted and the tortillas are warm.
  5. Slice into wedges and serve with salsa and sour cream for dipping.

This quesadilla is the perfect snack or light meal, and you can easily mix and match ingredients based on what you have in your fridge.

5. Microwave Mug Omelet

Mug meals are a college student’s best friend, and this microwave mug omelet is a quick, protein-packed breakfast or lunch option.

Ingredients:

  • 2 eggs
  • 2 tablespoons milk
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1/4 cup diced vegetables (bell peppers, onions, spinach, etc.)
  • 1/4 cup shredded cheese

Instructions:

  1. In a microwave-safe mug, whisk together the eggs, milk, salt, and pepper.
  2. Add the diced vegetables and shredded cheese to the mug and stir to combine.
  3. Microwave on high for 1-2 minutes, stopping halfway through to stir the mixture.
  4. Continue microwaving until the eggs are fully set, then serve directly in the mug.

This microwave mug omelet is quick, filling, and a great way to start your day or fuel your afternoon study session.

6. Microwave Sweet Potato

Sweet potatoes are packed with nutrients, and they can be easily cooked in the microwave for a healthy meal or snack.

Ingredients:

  • 1 medium sweet potato
  • Olive oil (optional)
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Pierce the sweet potato several times with a fork to allow steam to escape.
  2. Place the sweet potato on a microwave-safe plate and microwave on high for 5-7 minutes, flipping halfway through.
  3. Once the sweet potato is tender, slice it open and fluff the insides with a fork.
  4. Drizzle with olive oil, sprinkle with salt and pepper, or top with your favorite seasonings.

This simple microwave sweet potato is incredibly versatile. You can add toppings like Greek yogurt, black beans, or cheese for a more filling meal.

7. Microwave Pasta with Marinara Sauce

Pasta is another easy meal that can be made in the microwave, and with a jar of marinara sauce, you have a classic dish in no time.

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 cup pasta (penne, spaghetti, or any shape)
  • 1/2 cup marinara sauce
  • 1 tablespoon grated Parmesan cheese (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Place the pasta in a microwave-safe bowl and cover it with water. Microwave on high for 4-6 minutes, stirring halfway through.
  2. Once the pasta is cooked, drain any excess water and stir in the marinara sauce.
  3. Microwave for an additional 1-2 minutes to heat the sauce.
  4. Top with grated Parmesan cheese and serve.

This microwave pasta dish is a quick and satisfying meal, and you can add meatballs, sautéed vegetables, or even grilled chicken for extra protein.

8. Microwave Stir-Fry

If you’re craving a stir-fry but don’t have a wok or stove, don’t worry. You can whip up a quick stir-fry using just your microwave.

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 cup frozen stir-fry vegetables
  • 1/2 cup cooked chicken, beef, or tofu
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil or sesame oil
  • Cooked rice or noodles (optional)

Instructions:

  1. In a microwave-safe bowl, combine the frozen vegetables with the cooked protein.
  2. Drizzle with soy sauce and olive oil, then stir to coat evenly.
  3. Microwave on high for 2-3 minutes or until the vegetables are tender and the protein is heated through.
  4. Serve over cooked rice or noodles for a complete meal.

This stir-fry is simple, healthy, and customizable to your taste, making it an excellent option for a busy college day.

9. Microwave Mug Cake

When you’re craving something sweet, a microwave mug cake is a great way to satisfy your dessert cravings without the need for an oven.

Ingredients:

  • 4 tablespoons flour
  • 3 tablespoons sugar
  • 2 tablespoons cocoa powder
  • 1/8 teaspoon baking powder
  • 3 tablespoons milk
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • A pinch of salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon vanilla extract

Instructions:

  1. In a microwave-safe mug, mix together the dry ingredients: flour, sugar, cocoa powder, baking powder, and salt.
  2. Add the wet ingredients: milk, vegetable oil, and vanilla extract. Stir until smooth.
  3. Microwave on high for 1 minute to 1 minute and 30 seconds, or until the cake has risen and is set in the middle.
  4. Let it cool slightly before enjoying your warm, chocolatey mug cake.

This microwave mug cake is perfect for satisfying a sweet tooth without having to make an entire batch of cookies or cake.

10. Microwave Pizza

Craving pizza but don’t have an oven? You can make a quick and easy pizza in the microwave using a tortilla as the base.

Ingredients:

  • 1 flour tortilla
  • 1/4 cup pizza sauce
  • 1/4 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Toppings of your choice (pepperoni, vegetables, etc.)

Instructions:

  1. Place the tortilla on a microwave-safe plate and spread the pizza sauce evenly over the top.
  2. Sprinkle the shredded mozzarella cheese over the sauce and add your favorite toppings.
  3. Microwave on high for 1-2 minutes or until the cheese is melted and bubbly.
  4. Slice and enjoy!

This microwave pizza is a fun and customizable meal that’s ready in minutes.

These 10 microwave meals prove that you don’t need a full kitchen to eat well in college. Whether you’re in the mood for breakfast, lunch, or a quick snack, these recipes will keep you fueled and satisfied throughout the day. With just a microwave, a few simple ingredients, and a little creativity, you can enjoy delicious and nutritious meals without the hassle of traditional cooking.
