Delicious Blueberry Streusel Coffee Cake: Perfect for Brunch


Are you looking for the perfect dish to impress your guests at brunch? Look no further! This recipe for Delicious Blueberry Streusel Coffee Cake is sure to be a crowd-pleaser. With its moist, tender cake base, bursting with juicy blueberries, and topped with a crunchy streusel topping, this coffee cake is a delightful treat that pairs perfectly with a cup of coffee or tea.

Ingredients

For the cake:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 cup sour cream
  • 1 1/2 cups fresh blueberries

For the streusel topping:

  • 1/2 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/4 cup unsalted butter, chilled and cubed

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease and flour a 9-inch round cake pan.

  2. In a medium bowl, whisk together the flour, baking powder, baking soda, and salt. Set aside.

  3. In a large bowl, cream together the softened butter and granulated sugar until light and fluffy. Add the eggs one at a time, beating well after each addition. Stir in the vanilla extract.

  4. Gradually add the dry ingredients to the butter mixture, alternating with the sour cream. Begin and end with the dry ingredients, mixing just until combined. Be careful not to overmix.

  5. Gently fold in the fresh blueberries, being careful not to crush them.

  6. In a small bowl, combine the flour and granulated sugar for the streusel topping. Add the chilled, cubed butter and mix with a fork or your fingertips until the mixture resembles coarse crumbs.

  7. Pour the cake batter into the prepared pan, spreading it evenly. Sprinkle the streusel topping over the batter, covering it completely.

  8. Bake for 45-50 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean. The streusel topping should be golden brown.

  9. Remove the cake from the oven and let it cool in the pan for 10 minutes. Then, transfer it to a wire rack to cool completely before serving.

Serving Suggestions

This Delicious Blueberry Streusel Coffee Cake is best served at room temperature. Once cooled, slice it into wedges and serve it alongside your favorite hot beverage, such as freshly brewed coffee or a fragrant cup of tea. The combination of the moist cake, bursting blueberries, and the crunchy streusel topping creates a symphony of flavors and textures that will surely leave your taste buds craving more.

Make-Ahead Tip

If you want to prepare this coffee cake in advance, you can bake it the night before and store it tightly covered at room temperature. In the morning, simply bring it out, slice, and serve. It will still taste just as delicious!

Conclusion

With its irresistible combination of blueberries and streusel topping, this Delicious Blueberry Streusel Coffee Cake is a brunch favorite that will have everyone asking for seconds. Its moist and tender cake, dotted with bursts of juicy blueberries, makes it an indulgent treat that is perfect for any occasion. So, why wait? Gather your ingredients and get baking! Your friends and family will thank you for this mouthwatering creation. Enjoy!
