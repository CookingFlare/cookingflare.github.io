Fluffy Strawberry Shortcake Cupcakes: A Summertime Classic


Summer is the perfect time to indulge in the sweet and refreshing flavors of strawberries. What better way to celebrate this delightful fruit than with Fluffy Strawberry Shortcake Cupcakes? These cupcakes are a twist on the classic dessert, combining the light and airy texture of a cupcake with the sweet and juicy taste of fresh strawberries. Get ready to treat your taste buds to a burst of summertime goodness!

Ingredients

For the cupcakes:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 1/2 cups granulated sugar
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 cup buttermilk
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

For the strawberry filling:

  • 2 cups fresh strawberries, hulled and diced
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 2 tablespoons cornstarch
  • 1/4 cup water

For the whipped cream frosting:

  • 2 cups heavy cream
  • 1/4 cup powdered sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C) and line a muffin tin with cupcake liners.

  2. In a large mixing bowl, combine the flour, sugar, baking powder, baking soda, and salt. Mix well.

  3. Add the softened butter to the dry ingredients and mix until the mixture resembles coarse crumbs.

  4. In a separate bowl, whisk together the eggs, buttermilk, and vanilla extract.

  5. Slowly pour the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients, mixing until just combined. Be careful not to overmix.

  6. Fill each cupcake liner about two-thirds full with the batter.

  7. Bake for 18-20 minutes or until a toothpick inserted into the center of a cupcake comes out clean.

  8. While the cupcakes are baking, prepare the strawberry filling. In a saucepan, combine the diced strawberries, sugar, cornstarch, and water. Cook over medium heat, stirring constantly, until the mixture thickens and the strawberries release their juices. Remove from heat and let cool.

  9. Once the cupcakes have cooled completely, use a small knife or spoon to create a hollow in the center of each cupcake. Fill each hollow with a spoonful of the strawberry filling.

  10. To make the whipped cream frosting, whip the heavy cream, powdered sugar, and vanilla extract together until stiff peaks form.

  11. Pipe or spread the whipped cream frosting onto the cupcakes, covering the strawberry filling.

  12. Serve the Fluffy Strawberry Shortcake Cupcakes immediately, or refrigerate until ready to serve. Enjoy!

Summertime Delights

These Fluffy Strawberry Shortcake Cupcakes are the perfect treat for summer gatherings, picnics, or simply indulging in a sweet and fruity dessert. The combination of the light and fluffy cupcake, the burst of fresh strawberry filling, and the creamy whipped cream frosting creates a delightful harmony of flavors and textures. Whether you’re a fan of classic strawberry shortcake or simply looking to try something new, these cupcakes are sure to impress. So go ahead, take a bite, and savor the taste of summer!

Remember to get creative with your decorations. You can garnish the cupcakes with fresh strawberry slices or sprinkle some powdered sugar on top. The possibilities are endless when it comes to personalizing these delicious treats.

So, invite your friends over, whip up a batch of Fluffy Strawberry Shortcake Cupcakes, and let the taste of summer brighten up your day. Happy baking!
