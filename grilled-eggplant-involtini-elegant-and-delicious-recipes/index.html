Grilled Eggplant Involtini: Elegant and Delicious Recipes


If you’re looking for a show-stopping appetizer or side dish that’s as elegant as it is delicious, then grilled eggplant involtini is the perfect choice. This Italian-inspired dish features thinly sliced grilled eggplant filled with a creamy ricotta and herb mixture, then rolled up and baked to perfection. The result is a flavorful, visually stunning dish that is sure to impress your guests.

Ingredients

For the Grilled Eggplant:

  • 2 large eggplants
  • Olive oil
  • Salt and pepper

For the Filling:

  • 1 ½ cups ricotta cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh basil
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • Salt and pepper to taste

For the Tomato Sauce:

  • 2 cups crushed tomatoes
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh basil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

For Garnish:

  • Fresh basil leaves
  • Grated Parmesan cheese

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Eggplant:

    • Preheat the grill to medium-high heat.
    • Slice the eggplants lengthwise into 1/4-inch thick slices.
    • Brush both sides of the eggplant slices with olive oil and season with salt and pepper.
    • Grill the eggplant slices for 2-3 minutes on each side, until they are tender and have grill marks. Remove from the grill and set aside.

  2. Make the Filling:

    • In a medium bowl, combine the ricotta cheese, Parmesan cheese, basil, parsley, and minced garlic. Season with salt and pepper to taste.

  3. Prepare the Tomato Sauce:

    • In a small saucepan, heat a bit of olive oil over medium heat. Add the minced garlic and sauté for 1 minute.
    • Add the crushed tomatoes, basil, salt, and pepper. Simmer for 10-15 minutes, until the sauce has thickened slightly.

  4. Assemble the Involtini:

    • Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
    • Spread a thin layer of the tomato sauce in the bottom of a baking dish.
    • Take a grilled eggplant slice and place a spoonful of the ricotta mixture at one end. Roll up the eggplant slice and place it seam side down in the baking dish. Repeat with the remaining eggplant slices and filling.

  5. Bake and Serve:

    • Spoon the remaining tomato sauce over the top of the rolled eggplant slices.
    • Bake for 20-25 minutes, until the involtini are heated through and the sauce is bubbly.
    • Garnish with fresh basil leaves and grated Parmesan cheese before serving.

Tips and Variations

  • To make this dish vegan, you can substitute the ricotta and Parmesan cheese with a dairy-free alternative.
  • Add a handful of pine nuts to the ricotta mixture for added texture and flavor.
  • Serve the grilled eggplant involtini with a side of mixed greens dressed in balsamic vinaigrette for a complete and satisfying meal.

Grilled eggplant involtini is a versatile dish that can be served as an appetizer, a side dish, or even as a main course for a vegetarian meal. Its elegant presentation and delicious flavors make it a standout dish for any occasion. Whether you’re hosting a dinner party or simply craving something special for dinner, this recipe is sure to delight your taste buds and impress your guests. Enjoy the delightful combination of grilled eggplant, creamy ricotta, and tangy tomato sauce in every bite.
