Lemony One-Pot Chicken Piccata: A Zesty Delight


Chicken Piccata is a classic Italian dish that combines tender chicken with a tangy lemon sauce. It’s a versatile recipe that can be enjoyed on its own or paired with pasta, rice, or vegetables. In this recipe, we’ll show you how to make a delicious Lemony One-Pot Chicken Piccata that’s bursting with flavor and incredibly easy to prepare. Let’s get cooking!

Ingredients

  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1/2 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 4 tablespoons unsalted butter, divided
  • 4 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 cup chicken broth
  • 1/2 cup freshly squeezed lemon juice
  • 1/4 cup capers, drained
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley

Instructions

  1. Season the chicken breasts with salt and pepper on both sides. Dredge the chicken in flour, shaking off any excess.

  2. Heat the olive oil and 2 tablespoons of butter in a large skillet over medium-high heat. Once the butter has melted, add the chicken breasts to the skillet and cook for 4-5 minutes per side, or until golden brown and cooked through. Remove the chicken from the skillet and set aside.

  3. In the same skillet, add the minced garlic and cook for about 1 minute, or until fragrant. Be careful not to burn the garlic.

  4. Pour in the chicken broth and lemon juice, scraping the bottom of the skillet to release any browned bits. Bring the mixture to a boil and let it simmer for 5 minutes, or until the liquid has reduced slightly.

  5. Stir in the capers and the remaining 2 tablespoons of butter. Cook for an additional 2 minutes, or until the sauce has thickened slightly.

  6. Return the chicken breasts to the skillet and spoon the sauce over them. Cook for another 2-3 minutes, or until the chicken is heated through.

  7. Sprinkle the chopped parsley over the chicken before serving. The vibrant green color adds a pop of freshness to the dish.

Serving Suggestions

  • Serve the Lemony One-Pot Chicken Piccata with a side of pasta or buttered noodles. The sauce pairs beautifully with the pasta, creating a delightful combination of flavors.
  • For a lighter option, serve the chicken with steamed vegetables or a fresh green salad. The tangy sauce provides a refreshing contrast to the crispness of the vegetables.
  • If you’re feeling adventurous, try serving the chicken piccata over a bed of creamy mashed potatoes. The creamy texture of the potatoes complements the zesty flavors of the dish.

Final Thoughts

Lemony One-Pot Chicken Piccata is a simple yet elegant dish that will impress your family and friends. The combination of tender chicken, tangy lemon sauce, and briny capers creates a burst of flavors in every bite. Plus, with the convenience of cooking everything in one pot, cleanup is a breeze. So, grab your skillet and get ready to enjoy this zesty delight!

Remember, cooking is all about experimenting and adding your own personal touch. Feel free to adjust the ingredients and measurements to suit your taste. Whether you’re a seasoned cook or just starting out, this Lemony One-Pot Chicken Piccata recipe is sure to become a favorite in your kitchen. Bon appétit!
