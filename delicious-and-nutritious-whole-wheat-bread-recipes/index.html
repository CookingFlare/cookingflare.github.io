Delicious and Nutritious Whole Wheat Bread Recipes


Are you looking for a healthy and delicious alternative to traditional white bread? Look no further! Whole wheat bread is not only tasty but also packed with nutrients that can support your overall well-being. In this blog post, we will explore some mouthwatering whole wheat bread recipes that will leave you wanting more. So, let’s get started!

1. Classic Whole Wheat Bread

Ingredients:

  • 3 cups whole wheat flour
  • 1 ½ cups warm water
  • 2 tablespoons honey
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 teaspoons active dry yeast
  • 1 teaspoon salt

Instructions:

  1. In a large mixing bowl, combine the warm water and honey. Stir until the honey dissolves.
  2. Sprinkle the yeast over the water-honey mixture and let it sit for 5-10 minutes, or until it becomes frothy.
  3. Add the olive oil and salt to the bowl and mix well.
  4. Gradually add the whole wheat flour to the bowl, stirring continuously until a dough forms.
  5. Transfer the dough to a floured surface and knead for about 10 minutes, or until the dough becomes smooth and elastic.
  6. Place the dough in a greased bowl, cover it with a clean kitchen towel, and let it rise in a warm place for about 1 hour, or until it doubles in size.
  7. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  8. Punch down the risen dough and shape it into a loaf. Place the loaf in a greased loaf pan.
  9. Cover the loaf pan with a clean kitchen towel and let it rise again for about 30 minutes.
  10. Bake the bread in the preheated oven for 30-35 minutes, or until it turns golden brown.
  11. Remove the bread from the oven and let it cool completely before slicing.

2. Cinnamon Raisin Whole Wheat Bread

Ingredients:

  • 2 ½ cups whole wheat flour
  • 1 ½ cups warm water
  • ¼ cup honey
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 teaspoons active dry yeast
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 ½ teaspoons ground cinnamon
  • ½ cup raisins

Instructions:

  1. Follow steps 1 to 4 from the Classic Whole Wheat Bread recipe.
  2. In a small bowl, mix together the ground cinnamon and raisins.
  3. Add the cinnamon-raisin mixture to the dough and knead until well incorporated.
  4. Continue with steps 6 to 11 from the Classic Whole Wheat Bread recipe.

3. Multigrain Whole Wheat Bread

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups whole wheat flour
  • 1 cup bread flour
  • ½ cup rolled oats
  • ¼ cup sunflower seeds
  • ¼ cup flaxseeds
  • ¼ cup sesame seeds
  • 2 tablespoons honey
  • 1 ½ cups warm water
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 teaspoons active dry yeast
  • 1 teaspoon salt

Instructions:

  1. In a large mixing bowl, combine the whole wheat flour, bread flour, rolled oats, sunflower seeds, flaxseeds, and sesame seeds.
  2. In a separate bowl, mix together the warm water and honey. Stir until the honey dissolves.
  3. Sprinkle the yeast over the water-honey mixture and let it sit for 5-10 minutes, or until it becomes frothy.
  4. Add the olive oil and salt to the bowl and mix well.
  5. Gradually add the dry ingredients to the wet ingredients, stirring continuously until a dough forms.
  6. Transfer the dough to a floured surface and knead for about 10 minutes, or until the dough becomes smooth and elastic.
  7. Place the dough in a greased bowl, cover it with a clean kitchen towel, and let it rise in a warm place for about 1 hour, or until it doubles in size.
  8. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  9. Punch down the risen dough and shape it into a loaf. Place the loaf in a greased loaf pan.
  10. Cover the loaf pan with a clean kitchen towel and let it rise again for about 30 minutes.
  11. Bake the bread in the preheated oven for 30-35 minutes, or until it turns golden brown.
  12. Remove the bread from the oven and let it cool completely before slicing.

Conclusion

Whole wheat bread is a fantastic choice for those seeking a healthier alternative to refined white bread. With these delicious and nutritious recipes, you can enjoy the goodness of whole grains while savoring the delightful taste of homemade bread. So go ahead, give these recipes a try and experience the satisfaction of baking your own wholesome bread. Your taste buds and body will thank you!
