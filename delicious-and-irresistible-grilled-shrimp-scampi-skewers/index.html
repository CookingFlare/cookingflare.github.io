Delicious and Irresistible: Grilled Shrimp Scampi Skewers


Grilled shrimp scampi skewers are an absolute delight for seafood lovers. The combination of succulent shrimp, zesty lemon, and garlic-infused butter creates a dish that is bursting with flavor. Whether you’re hosting a summer barbecue or simply looking for a quick and easy meal, these skewers are sure to impress. So fire up the grill and get ready to indulge in this irresistible seafood treat!

Ingredients

  • 1 pound of large shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 4 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1/4 cup of fresh lemon juice
  • 1/4 cup of extra-virgin olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons of melted butter
  • 1 teaspoon of red pepper flakes (optional)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Wooden skewers, soaked in water for 30 minutes

Instructions

  1. In a large bowl, combine the minced garlic, lemon juice, olive oil, melted butter, red pepper flakes (if using), salt, and pepper. Mix well to create a flavorful marinade.

  2. Add the peeled and deveined shrimp to the bowl, making sure each shrimp is coated with the marinade. Allow the shrimp to marinate for at least 15 minutes, or up to 1 hour for a more intense flavor.

  3. Preheat your grill to medium-high heat. While the grill is heating, thread the marinated shrimp onto the soaked wooden skewers, ensuring they are secure and evenly spaced.

  4. Once the grill is hot, place the shrimp skewers directly on the grates. Cook the shrimp for 2-3 minutes per side, or until they turn pink and opaque. Be careful not to overcook the shrimp, as they can become tough and rubbery.

  5. Remove the shrimp skewers from the grill and transfer them to a serving platter. Garnish with fresh lemon wedges and chopped parsley for an extra burst of flavor.

  6. Serve the grilled shrimp scampi skewers immediately while they are still hot. They can be enjoyed as a main dish, served over a bed of rice or pasta, or as a flavorful addition to a salad.

  7. Don’t forget to squeeze the grilled lemon wedges over the shrimp for an extra tangy kick. The smoky char from the grill combined with the bright citrus notes will take these skewers to a whole new level of deliciousness!

Tips and Variations

  • For a spicier kick, add more red pepper flakes to the marinade or serve the skewers with a side of hot sauce.
  • Experiment with different herbs and spices to customize the flavor profile of the shrimp skewers. Fresh basil, thyme, or even a sprinkle of Old Bay seasoning can add a unique twist.
  • If you don’t have access to a grill, these shrimp skewers can also be cooked on a stovetop grill pan or under a broiler. Just make sure to adjust the cooking time accordingly.

Grilled shrimp scampi skewers are a versatile and crowd-pleasing dish that is perfect for any occasion. Whether you’re enjoying them as an appetizer, a main course, or even as a topping for a refreshing summer salad, these skewers will never fail to impress. So gather your ingredients, fire up the grill, and prepare to savor the flavors of this delicious and irresistible seafood delight!
