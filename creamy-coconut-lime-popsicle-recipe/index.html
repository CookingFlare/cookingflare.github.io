Creamy Coconut Lime Popsicle Recipe


Popsicles are the perfect treat for hot summer days, and this Creamy Coconut Lime Popsicle recipe is sure to become a favorite among both kids and adults. With its refreshing blend of tropical flavors and smooth, creamy texture, these popsicles are a delightful way to beat the heat. Plus, they’re incredibly easy to make! Let’s dive right into the recipe.

Ingredients

  • 1 can (400 ml) coconut milk
  • 1/2 cup full-fat Greek yogurt
  • 1/4 cup freshly squeezed lime juice
  • 1/4 cup honey
  • Zest of 1 lime
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Instructions

  1. In a mixing bowl, combine the coconut milk, Greek yogurt, lime juice, honey, lime zest, and vanilla extract. Stir well until all the ingredients are fully incorporated.

  2. Pour the mixture into popsicle molds, filling each mold about 3/4 of the way. Leave a little space at the top to allow for expansion during freezing.

  3. Place the popsicle molds in the freezer and let them set for about 1 hour. Then, insert popsicle sticks into each mold. If your molds don’t have built-in sticks, you can cover the molds with plastic wrap and insert the sticks through the plastic wrap. This will help keep the sticks in place.

  4. Return the popsicles to the freezer and let them freeze completely, usually for about 4-6 hours, or overnight.

  5. Once the popsicles are fully frozen, remove them from the molds by running the molds under warm water for a few seconds. This will help loosen the popsicles and make them easier to remove.

  6. Serve the Creamy Coconut Lime Popsicles immediately, or store them in an airtight container in the freezer for up to 2 weeks.

Tips and Variations

  • For an extra tropical twist, you can add some shredded coconut to the popsicle mixture before freezing. This will give the popsicles a delightful texture and added flavor.

  • If you prefer a sweeter popsicle, you can increase the amount of honey or add a few drops of stevia or your preferred sweetener to taste.

  • For a fun and colorful presentation, you can layer the popsicle mixture with fruit purees. Simply pour a layer of the coconut lime mixture into the molds, freeze for about 1 hour, then add a layer of your chosen fruit puree (such as mango or raspberry), freeze again, and repeat until the molds are full. This will create beautiful and tasty layered popsicles.

  • If you don’t have popsicle molds, you can use small paper cups or ice cube trays instead. Just cover the cups or trays with plastic wrap and insert popsicle sticks through the plastic wrap.

Enjoy the Creamy Coconut Lime Popsicles on a sunny afternoon or as a guilt-free dessert after a delicious meal. With their tropical flavors and creamy texture, they are sure to become a hit with everyone who tries them. So go ahead and give this recipe a try. Your taste buds will thank you!
