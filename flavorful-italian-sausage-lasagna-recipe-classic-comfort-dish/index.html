Flavorful Italian Sausage Lasagna Recipe - Classic Comfort Dish


Lasagna is a classic Italian dish that brings comfort and warmth to any table. With layers of pasta, meat, cheese, and sauce, it is the perfect dish for family gatherings or cozy nights in. In this recipe, we will be adding a delightful twist by using flavorful Italian sausage to enhance the taste and take this traditional dish to a whole new level.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound Italian sausage
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 can (28 ounces) crushed tomatoes
  • 1 can (6 ounces) tomato paste
  • 2 teaspoons dried basil
  • 2 teaspoons dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1/2 cup red wine
  • 1 package (16 ounces) lasagna noodles
  • 1 container (15 ounces) ricotta cheese
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • Fresh parsley, for garnish

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).

  2. In a large skillet, cook the Italian sausage over medium heat until browned. Add the chopped onion and minced garlic, and cook until the onion is translucent and fragrant.

  3. Stir in the crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, dried basil, dried oregano, salt, black pepper, and red wine. Simmer the sauce for about 15 minutes, allowing the flavors to meld together.

  4. While the sauce is simmering, cook the lasagna noodles according to the package instructions. Be sure to cook them al dente, as they will continue to cook in the oven.

  5. In a separate bowl, combine the ricotta cheese, 1 cup of shredded mozzarella cheese, and 1/2 cup of grated Parmesan cheese. Mix well until thoroughly combined.

  6. Now it’s time to assemble the lasagna. In a 9x13-inch baking dish, spread a thin layer of the meat sauce on the bottom. Arrange a single layer of cooked lasagna noodles on top of the sauce.

  7. Spread a generous layer of the cheese mixture over the noodles, followed by another layer of meat sauce. Repeat this layering process until all the ingredients are used, ending with a layer of meat sauce on top.

  8. Sprinkle the remaining shredded mozzarella cheese and grated Parmesan cheese over the top layer of meat sauce.

  9. Cover the baking dish with aluminum foil and bake in the preheated oven for 30 minutes. Then, remove the foil and bake for an additional 15 minutes, or until the cheese is melted and bubbly.

  10. Once the lasagna is done baking, let it cool for a few minutes before serving. Garnish with fresh parsley for a pop of color and added freshness.

This flavorful Italian sausage lasagna is a true crowd-pleaser, guaranteed to satisfy even the pickiest eaters. The combination of hearty sausage, rich tomato sauce, and layers of cheesy goodness creates a symphony of flavors that will leave everyone coming back for seconds. Whether you’re hosting a dinner party or simply craving a comforting meal, this classic comfort dish is sure to hit the spot.

So, roll up your sleeves, gather your ingredients, and get ready to indulge in the ultimate Italian comfort food experience. Buon appetito!
