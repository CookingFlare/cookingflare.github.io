Pickling Pairings: Perfect Companions for Your Artisanal Pickles


When it comes to crafts, pickling must be one of the most versatile forms of it. This ancient method of food preservation not only imparts tangy, refreshing flavors, but also boosts up the nutritional value of a myriad of vegetables and fruits, turning them into an exquisite and versatile palette of culinary delights. But, artisanal pickles, though delectable by themselves, truly come into their own when paired with complimentary foods that enhance their inherent flavors. From pickled beets to pickled pears, these Artisanal Pickles have a talismanic way of weaving a symphony of gastronomic alliances that dance on your tongue. Let us delve deeper into some perfect pickling pairings to elevate your culinary experience.

1. Bread & Butter

An assortment of artisanal pickles simply perks up the mundane task of consuming bread and butter. Try soft, warm releases with tangy pickled cucumbers or spice-up your meal with some fiery pickled peppers for a flavour explosion. The interplay of crunchy bread with the sharp, tangy pickles offers an intertwined dance of textures that is sure to delight your senses.

2. Charcuterie Board

Take your charcuterie board to another level by adding a pleasing pickle party! Artisanal pickles, with their sharp tang or sweet slick, serve as a refreshing counterpoint to the richness of meats and cheeses. Try crumbling blue cheese with zesty dill pickles or coupling spicy pickled onions with soft Goat cheese. A charming medley for your eyes and a heavenly blend for your palate!

3. Savory Waffles

Breakfast, brunch or dinner—waffles are versatile enough to serve across these meals. Whisk in a dollop of spicy mustard and add a smear of artisanal pickles on your waffles for a zesty, crunchy combination that sings a sweet melody of flavors. Or, try sweet potato waffles with tangy pickled beets—a match made in flavor utopia!

4. Eggs Galore!

Eggs, versatile as ever, blend well with pickles. Poach or scramble them and add a serve of cider or vinegar-based pickled cucumbers for a tangy embrace. For a bold flavor, try omelets with pickled onions, jalapenos, or peppers, and have your taste buds soaring sky-high!

5. A Glass of Wine

The pairing of wine and pickles accentuates the complex, zesty flavors of each. Viognier or Riesling seems to absorb the tartness of pickled veggie scenarios and portrays nature’s ballet in a glass. Try Lance Winery’s Viognier with pickled beets for an exotic symphony of flavors.

6. Smoked Meat

Smoked meat dishes, such as pulled pork or brisket, find a pickling friendship in mustard-based or vinegar-slathered pickles. The tangy astringency of the pickles adds a lip-smacking touch to the rich smoky taste of meat, converting an ordinary meal into a festive fiesta.

7. Sandwiches

Revive the humdrum sandwich with a wild Elvis flair! Spread peanut butter and banana slices on the bread, add a slice of pickled jalapeno and indulge in the flavorsome fusion that is a far cry from your regular meal.

So, next time you’re whipping up a dish, try exploring one of these delightful pairings and see how your artisanal pickles can elevate your culinary experience. These pairings are sure to offer fresh, innovative ways to enjoy your pickles.

In all aspects of our lives, including cooking, there’s always room for creativity, fun, and experimentation. Go ahead, pickle up your life with these exciting pairings, and revel in the delectable explosion of flavors.
