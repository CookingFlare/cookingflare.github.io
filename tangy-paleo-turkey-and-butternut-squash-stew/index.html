Tangy Paleo Turkey and Butternut Squash Stew


When the weather starts to turn chilly, there’s nothing quite like a hearty stew to warm you up from the inside out. This Tangy Paleo Turkey and Butternut Squash Stew recipe is a flavorful and satisfying dish that’s perfect for those cozy nights in. Packed with protein, healthy fats, and a medley of vibrant vegetables, this stew is not only delicious but also fits into the paleo lifestyle.

Ingredients

  • 1.5 pounds of ground turkey
  • 1 medium butternut squash, peeled and diced
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 red bell pepper, diced
  • 1 green bell pepper, diced
  • 1 can of diced tomatoes
  • 4 cups of chicken or vegetable broth
  • 2 tablespoons of tomato paste
  • 2 tablespoons of apple cider vinegar
  • 1 teaspoon of paprika
  • 1 teaspoon of dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon of dried thyme
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley for garnish

Instructions

  1. In a large pot or Dutch oven, brown the ground turkey over medium heat until fully cooked. Remove the turkey from the pot and set it aside.

  2. In the same pot, add the chopped onion and sauté until translucent. Add the minced garlic and sauté for another minute.

  3. Stir in the diced butternut squash, red and green bell peppers, and continue to cook for 5-7 minutes until the vegetables begin to soften.

  4. Add the can of diced tomatoes, tomato paste, apple cider vinegar, paprika, dried oregano, and thyme to the pot. Stir well to combine all the ingredients.

  5. Pour in the chicken or vegetable broth and bring the stew to a gentle boil. Lower the heat and let the stew simmer for 20-25 minutes, or until the butternut squash is tender.

  6. Once the stew has thickened slightly and the flavors have melded together, return the cooked ground turkey to the pot. Season with salt and pepper to taste.

  7. Allow the stew to simmer for an additional 10-15 minutes, stirring occasionally.

  8. Serve the Tangy Paleo Turkey and Butternut Squash Stew hot, garnished with fresh parsley.

Nutritional Benefits

This Tangy Paleo Turkey and Butternut Squash Stew is not only bursting with flavor, but it also offers a range of nutritional benefits. The turkey provides a lean source of protein, while the butternut squash contributes essential vitamins and minerals, including vitamin A, vitamin C, and potassium. The combination of vegetables adds fiber and antioxidants to the dish, making it a wholesome and nourishing choice for a balanced meal.

Serving Suggestions

This stew can be enjoyed on its own as a satisfying one-pot meal, or paired with a simple side salad for a complete and wholesome dinner. It also makes for excellent leftovers, as the flavors continue to develop over time. Feel free to customize the recipe by adding your favorite herbs or spices to suit your taste preferences.

Conclusion

With its robust flavors and nourishing ingredients, this Tangy Paleo Turkey and Butternut Squash Stew is a must-try for anyone looking to add a touch of warmth and comfort to their paleo cooking repertoire. Whether you’re following a paleo lifestyle or simply seeking a wholesome and delicious meal, this stew is sure to become a staple in your recipe collection. So, gather your ingredients and get ready to savor the rich, tangy goodness of this delightful stew!
