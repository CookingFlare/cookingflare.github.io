Mouthwatering Keto BBQ Pulled Pork Lettuce Wraps


If you’re a fan of barbecue flavors and looking for a delicious low-carb option, these mouthwatering Keto BBQ Pulled Pork Lettuce Wraps are just what you need. Packed with smoky, tender pulled pork and wrapped in crisp lettuce leaves, this recipe offers a satisfying meal that’s perfect for summer gatherings or a quick weeknight dinner.

Ingredients

For the Pulled Pork:

  • 3 lbs pork shoulder
  • 1 cup chicken broth
  • 1/2 cup sugar-free BBQ sauce
  • 2 tsp smoked paprika
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp onion powder
  • 1/2 tsp cayenne pepper
  • Salt and pepper to taste

For the Lettuce Wraps:

  • Large lettuce leaves, such as butter or romaine
  • Sugar-free coleslaw (optional, for topping)
  • Extra BBQ sauce for drizzling

Instructions

  1. Prep the Pork: Season the pork shoulder with smoked paprika, garlic powder, onion powder, cayenne pepper, salt, and pepper. Place the seasoned pork shoulder in a slow cooker and pour in the chicken broth. Cook on low for 8-10 hours until the pork is tender and easily shreds with a fork.

  2. Shred the Pork: Once the pork is cooked, remove it from the slow cooker and shred it using two forks. Discard any excess fat.

  3. Add the BBQ Sauce: In a bowl, mix the shredded pork with the sugar-free BBQ sauce until it’s well coated. Adjust the amount of BBQ sauce to your taste.

  4. Assemble the Lettuce Wraps: Take large, crisp lettuce leaves and spoon a generous amount of the BBQ pulled pork into the center of each leaf. Top with a dollop of sugar-free coleslaw for added crunch and creaminess.

  5. Serve and Enjoy: Drizzle extra BBQ sauce over the assembled lettuce wraps and serve them immediately. These wraps are best enjoyed fresh, allowing the crisp lettuce to contrast with the tender, flavorful pork.

Why Keto BBQ Pulled Pork Lettuce Wraps?

These Keto BBQ Pulled Pork Lettuce Wraps are not only a delicious way to enjoy the flavors of barbecue without the carbs, but they also offer a range of benefits:

  • Low-Carb: By using lettuce instead of traditional buns or wraps, you can enjoy all the flavors of pulled pork without the added carbs.

  • High in Protein: Pork shoulder is a rich source of protein, which is essential for muscle repair and growth.

  • Flavorful and Versatile: The smoky, tangy flavors of the pulled pork combined with the crispness of the lettuce create a satisfying contrast in textures and tastes.

  • Perfect for Gatherings: These lettuce wraps are a fantastic option for picnics, barbecues, or any social gathering where you want to offer a low-carb, crowd-pleasing dish.

Final Thoughts

Whether you’re following a ketogenic lifestyle or simply looking for a lighter alternative to traditional pulled pork sandwiches, these Keto BBQ Pulled Pork Lettuce Wraps are sure to satisfy your cravings. The tender, flavorful pork paired with the freshness of the lettuce makes for a delightful combination that’s both satisfying and guilt-free. Give this recipe a try and enjoy all the deliciousness of barbecue season without compromising your healthy eating goals.

So, fire up your slow cooker, grab some fresh lettuce, and get ready to indulge in these irresistible Keto BBQ Pulled Pork Lettuce Wraps!
