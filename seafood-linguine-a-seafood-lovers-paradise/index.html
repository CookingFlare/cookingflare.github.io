Seafood Linguine: A Seafood Lover's Paradise


Calling all seafood enthusiasts! If you’re passionate about seafood, then this recipe is tailor-made for you. Seafood linguine is a delightful and indulgent dish that combines the flavors of the ocean with the satisfying twirl of perfectly cooked pasta. Whether you’re hosting a dinner party, celebrating a special occasion, or simply craving a luxurious meal, this seafood linguine recipe will surely impress your taste buds and those of your guests.

Ingredients

For the Pasta:

  • 1 pound linguine
  • 2 tablespoons salt
  • Water for boiling

For the Seafood Medley:

  • 1 pound mixed seafood (such as shrimp, scallops, and calamari)
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1/2 cup white wine
  • 1/4 cup fresh parsley, chopped
  • Salt and black pepper to taste

For the Sauce:

  • 4 tablespoons unsalted butter
  • 1/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 cups whole milk
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/4 teaspoon nutmeg
  • Salt and white pepper to taste

Instructions

Step 1: Prepare the Seafood

  1. Thoroughly rinse the mixed seafood under cold water and pat dry with paper towels.
  2. Heat a large skillet over medium heat and add the olive oil.
  3. Add the minced garlic to the skillet and sauté until fragrant, about 1 minute.
  4. Add the mixed seafood to the skillet, season with salt and black pepper, and cook until just opaque. Remove the seafood from the skillet and set aside.

Step 2: Cook the Pasta

  1. In a large pot, bring water to a boil and add 2 tablespoons of salt.
  2. Add the linguine to the boiling water and cook until al dente, according to the package instructions.
  3. Once cooked, drain the linguine and set aside.

Step 3: Prepare the Sauce

  1. In the same skillet used for the seafood, melt the unsalted butter over medium heat.
  2. Whisk in the all-purpose flour to create a roux and cook for 1-2 minutes.
  3. Slowly pour in the whole milk while continuously whisking to avoid lumps.
  4. Add the grated Parmesan cheese and nutmeg, and continue to whisk until the sauce thickens.
  5. Season the sauce with salt and white pepper to taste.

Step 4: Bring It All Together

  1. Return the cooked seafood to the skillet with the sauce.
  2. Add the cherry tomatoes and white wine to the skillet, and gently stir to combine.
  3. Toss in the cooked linguine and mix everything together until the pasta is well coated with the seafood and sauce.
  4. Sprinkle with fresh parsley and give it one final toss.

Serving Suggestion

Serve the seafood linguine in individual pasta bowls, garnished with an extra sprinkle of Parmesan cheese and a twist of freshly ground black pepper. Pair this delectable dish with a crisp white wine and a side of garlic bread to complete the dining experience.

Enjoy Your Seafood Linguine!

This seafood linguine recipe promises to transport your taste buds to a coastal paradise with its harmonious blend of fresh seafood, flavorful sauce, and perfectly cooked pasta. Whether you’re a seasoned chef or a passionate home cook, this dish is sure to become a staple in your culinary repertoire. So, roll up your sleeves, gather your ingredients, and embark on a seafood lover’s journey with this sumptuous seafood linguine recipe!
