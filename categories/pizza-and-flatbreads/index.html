Irresistible Veggie Flatbread Pizza Recipes

If you’re a fan of pizza and looking for a healthier alternative, veggie flatbread pizzas are the perfect solution. They are quick, easy to make, and packed full of delicious, nutritious ingredients. Whether you’re a vegetarian, looking to eat more plant-based meals, or just want to try something new, these irresistible veggie flatbread pizza recipes will surely delight your taste buds. Roasted Vegetable Flatbread Pizza Ingredients 2 whole wheat flatbreads 1 cup marinara sauce 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese 1 zucchini, sliced 1 red bell pepper, sliced 1 yellow bell pepper, sliced 1 red onion, sliced 2 tbsp olive oil 2 cloves garlic, minced Salt and pepper to taste Fresh basil for garnish Instructions Preheat the oven to 425°F (220°C). Place the sliced zucchini, bell peppers, and red onion on a baking sheet. Drizzle with olive oil, sprinkle with minced garlic, salt, and pepper. Toss to coat the vegetables evenly. Roast the vegetables in the preheated oven for 15-20 minutes or until they are tender and slightly caramelized. Spread marinara sauce evenly over each flatbread. Sprinkle shredded mozzarella cheese over the marinara sauce. Arrange the roasted vegetables on top of the cheese. Bake the flatbread pizzas in the oven for 10-12 minutes or until the cheese is melted and bubbly. Garnish with fresh basil before serving. Pesto Veggie Flatbread Pizza Ingredients 2 whole grain flatbreads 1/2 cup pesto sauce 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved 1 cup baby spinach 1/2 cup crumbled feta cheese 1/4 cup pine nuts Salt and pepper to taste Fresh arugula for garnish Instructions Preheat the oven to 400°F (200°C). Spread pesto sauce evenly over each flatbread. Scatter halved cherry tomatoes and baby spinach over the pesto. Sprinkle crumbled feta cheese and pine nuts on top. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Bake the flatbread pizzas in the preheated oven for 8-10 minutes or until the edges are golden and the cheese is melted. Garnish with fresh arugula before serving. Southwest Veggie Flatbread Pizza Ingredients 2 whole wheat flatbreads 1/2 cup black bean dip 1 cup shredded Monterey Jack cheese 1/2 cup corn kernels 1/2 cup black beans, drained and rinsed 1/2 cup diced red onion 1/2 cup diced bell peppers 1/2 tsp ground cumin 1/2 tsp chili powder Fresh cilantro for garnish Instructions Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C). Spread black bean dip evenly over each flatbread. Sprinkle shredded Monterey Jack cheese over the black bean dip. Scatter corn kernels, black beans, diced red onion, and bell peppers over the cheese. Sprinkle ground cumin and chili powder over the toppings. Bake the flatbread pizzas in the oven for 12-15 minutes or until the cheese is melted and bubbly. Garnish with fresh cilantro before serving.
