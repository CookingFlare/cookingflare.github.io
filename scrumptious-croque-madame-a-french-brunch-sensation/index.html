Scrumptious Croque Madame: A French Brunch Sensation


If you’re a fan of brunch, then you’re in for a treat with this Scrumptious Croque Madame recipe. Originating from France, this delectable dish combines the classic flavors of a ham and cheese sandwich with a twist of elegance. With its rich and indulgent flavors, the Croque Madame is the perfect way to start your day or enjoy a leisurely weekend brunch.

Ingredients

For the béchamel sauce:

  • 2 tablespoons unsalted butter
  • 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup whole milk
  • 1/4 teaspoon nutmeg
  • Salt and pepper to taste

For the sandwich:

  • 8 slices of thick-cut bread, preferably brioche or sourdough
  • Dijon mustard, for spreading
  • 8 slices of cooked ham
  • 1 1/2 cups grated Gruyere cheese
  • 4 large eggs
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Chopped fresh parsley, for garnish

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).

  2. To make the béchamel sauce, melt the butter in a saucepan over medium heat. Once melted, whisk in the flour and cook for about 1 minute until smooth. Gradually add in the milk while continuously whisking until the mixture thickens. Stir in the nutmeg, salt, and pepper. Remove from heat and set aside.

  3. Lay out the bread slices on a baking sheet and lightly toast them in the preheated oven for about 5 minutes, or until slightly golden. This step will help prevent the bread from becoming soggy when assembled.

  4. Take the toasted bread slices out of the oven and spread a thin layer of Dijon mustard on one side of each slice. Place a slice of ham on four of the bread slices, followed by a generous amount of grated Gruyere cheese. Top each with another bread slice, mustard side down.

  5. Pour the béchamel sauce evenly over the assembled sandwiches, making sure to cover each one completely. Sprinkle some additional grated Gruyere cheese on top.

  6. Carefully crack an egg onto each sandwich, ensuring that the yolk stays intact. Season with salt and pepper.

  7. Return the baking sheet to the oven and bake for about 15 minutes, or until the eggs are cooked to your desired level of doneness. If you prefer a runny yolk, reduce the baking time slightly.

  8. Once cooked, remove the Croque Madames from the oven and let them cool for a few minutes. Garnish with chopped fresh parsley before serving.

Serving Suggestions

Croque Madame is often served with a side of greens, such as a simple mixed salad or sautéed spinach. The freshness of the greens balances out the richness of the sandwich, making it a well-rounded meal. You can also pair it with a cup of strong coffee or a mimosa for a complete brunch experience.

Final Thoughts

The Scrumptious Croque Madame is a French brunch sensation that will elevate your breakfast game to a whole new level. Its combination of ham, cheese, creamy béchamel sauce, and a perfectly cooked egg creates a harmonious blend of flavors and textures. So why not treat yourself and your loved ones to this indulgent and satisfying dish? Whether it’s a lazy Sunday morning or a special occasion, the Croque Madame is sure to impress. Bon appétit!
