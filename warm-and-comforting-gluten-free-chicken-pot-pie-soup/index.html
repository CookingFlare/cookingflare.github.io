Warm and Comforting: Gluten-Free Chicken Pot Pie Soup


Are you looking for a delicious and comforting meal that’s also gluten-free? Look no further than this amazing recipe for Warm and Comforting Gluten-Free Chicken Pot Pie Soup. It’s the perfect dish for those chilly evenings when you crave something hearty and satisfying. Packed with wholesome ingredients and bursting with flavor, this soup will warm your soul and fill your belly. Let’s dive into the recipe and discover how to make this delightful dish from scratch.

Ingredients

For the Soup:

  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 carrots, chopped
  • 2 celery stalks, chopped
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried rosemary
  • 1/4 cup gluten-free all-purpose flour
  • 4 cups chicken broth
  • 1 cup unsweetened almond milk
  • 2 cups cooked chicken, shredded
  • 1 cup frozen peas
  • Salt and pepper to taste

For the Gluten-Free Biscuit Topping:

  • 1 cup gluten-free all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 cup cold unsalted butter, cubed
  • 1/2 cup unsweetened almond milk
  • 1 tablespoon chopped fresh parsley

Instructions

  1. In a large pot, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the diced onion and cook until translucent. Add the minced garlic, carrots, and celery. Cook for a few minutes until the vegetables start to soften.

  2. Sprinkle the dried thyme, dried rosemary, and gluten-free all-purpose flour over the vegetables. Stir well to coat the vegetables with the flour and cook for 1-2 minutes.

  3. Gradually pour in the chicken broth, stirring constantly to avoid lumps. Add the unsweetened almond milk and bring the mixture to a simmer.

  4. Once the soup base has thickened, add the shredded chicken and frozen peas. Let the soup simmer for 10-15 minutes until the flavors meld together. Season with salt and pepper to taste.

  5. While the soup is simmering, prepare the gluten-free biscuit topping. In a mixing bowl, combine the gluten-free all-purpose flour, baking powder, baking soda, and salt. Cut in the cold cubed butter until the mixture resembles coarse crumbs. Stir in the unsweetened almond milk and chopped fresh parsley until a soft dough forms.

  6. Drop spoonfuls of the biscuit dough on top of the simmering soup. Cover the pot and let the biscuits cook for 15-20 minutes until they are cooked through and golden on top.

  7. Once the biscuits are cooked, serve the Warm and Comforting Gluten-Free Chicken Pot Pie Soup hot in bowls. Enjoy the heartwarming flavors and the comforting aroma of this delightful dish.

Final Thoughts

This Warm and Comforting Gluten-Free Chicken Pot Pie Soup is a true crowd-pleaser. It’s a fantastic way to enjoy all the flavors of a classic chicken pot pie in a comforting soup form. Whether you’re gluten-free or simply looking for a cozy and satisfying meal, this recipe is sure to become a staple in your kitchen. Give it a try and savor the warmth and comfort it brings to your table.

So there you have it, a delightful recipe for Warm and Comforting Gluten-Free Chicken Pot Pie Soup that’s sure to bring joy to your taste buds and warmth to your soul. Enjoy every spoonful of this delicious and wholesome soup, and share it with your loved ones for a heartwarming dining experience. Cheers to good food and great company!
