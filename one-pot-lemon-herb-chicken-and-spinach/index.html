One Pot Lemon Herb Chicken and Spinach


One pot meals are a lifesaver when it comes to weeknight dinners. They are quick, easy, and require minimal cleanup. If you’re looking for a delicious and healthy one pot meal, then you’re in luck! In this recipe, we’ll be making One Pot Lemon Herb Chicken and Spinach. This dish is packed with flavor and loaded with nutrients. So let’s get started!

Ingredients

  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • 1 teaspoon dried rosemary
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1 lemon, sliced
  • 1 cup chicken broth
  • 4 cups fresh spinach

Instructions

  1. Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Add minced garlic and sauté for about 1 minute, until fragrant.

  2. Season chicken breasts with dried thyme, dried rosemary, dried oregano, salt, and black pepper. Place the seasoned chicken breasts in the skillet and cook for 5 minutes on each side, until browned.

  3. Add lemon slices to the skillet, arranging them around the chicken breasts. Pour chicken broth over the chicken and lemon slices.

  4. Cover the skillet with a lid and let it simmer for 15 minutes, or until the chicken is cooked through and no longer pink in the center.

  5. Remove the lid and add fresh spinach to the skillet. Stir the spinach into the sauce until wilted, about 2 minutes.

  6. Serve the One Pot Lemon Herb Chicken and Spinach hot, garnished with additional lemon slices if desired.

Why You’ll Love This Recipe

Not only is this One Pot Lemon Herb Chicken and Spinach recipe incredibly easy to make, but it’s also incredibly flavorful. The combination of herbs, garlic, and lemon infuses the chicken with a bright and zesty taste. The spinach adds a fresh and vibrant touch, making this dish a healthy and nutritious choice.

Health Benefits

Chicken is an excellent source of lean protein, which is essential for building and repairing muscles. It is also low in fat and calories, making it a great option for those watching their weight. Spinach, on the other hand, is packed with vitamins and minerals, including iron, calcium, and vitamins A and C. It is also rich in antioxidants, which help protect your body against free radicals.

Tips and Variations

  • If you don’t have chicken breasts, you can use chicken thighs instead. Just adjust the cooking time accordingly.
  • Feel free to add other vegetables to the dish, such as bell peppers or cherry tomatoes, for an extra burst of flavor and color.
  • Serve this One Pot Lemon Herb Chicken and Spinach over rice, quinoa, or pasta for a more filling meal.

Conclusion

One Pot Lemon Herb Chicken and Spinach is a simple yet delicious meal that will satisfy your taste buds and nourish your body. With just a few ingredients and minimal effort, you can whip up a healthy and flavorful dinner that the whole family will love. So give this recipe a try and enjoy the convenience of a one pot meal without compromising on taste or nutrition. Happy cooking!
