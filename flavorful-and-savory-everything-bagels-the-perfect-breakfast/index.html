Flavorful and Savory Everything Bagels: The Perfect Breakfast


There’s something truly special about the aroma of freshly baked bread wafting through the kitchen, especially when it’s coming from a batch of warm, golden-brown bagels. And when it comes to bagels, few varieties are as beloved as the everything bagel. With its delightful combination of savory flavors, the everything bagel has become a staple in breakfast and brunch menus across the globe. If you’re a fan of this classic treat, or if you’ve never tried one before, get ready to embark on a culinary adventure as we explore the art of crafting flavorful and savory everything bagels right in your own kitchen.

The Allure of Everything Bagels

Everything bagels are a perfect union of textures and flavors, boasting a chewy interior and a crispy, golden crust, all generously coated in a delectable blend of savory toppings. The signature “everything” seasoning typically includes a harmonious mixture of sesame seeds, poppy seeds, dried garlic, dried onion, and coarse salt. This distinctive blend not only adds a delightful crunch to the bagel’s exterior but also infuses each bite with a burst of aromatic and savory goodness.

Crafting the Perfect Everything Bagel

Ingredients

To begin your journey to the perfect everything bagel, you’ll need the following ingredients:

  • 4 cups of bread flour
  • 1 tablespoon of granulated sugar
  • 1 ½ teaspoons of salt
  • 1 tablespoon of instant yeast
  • 1 ½ cups of warm water
  • 1 egg, beaten (for egg wash)
  • 2 tablespoons of each: sesame seeds, poppy seeds, dried garlic, and dried onion
  • 1 tablespoon of coarse salt

Instructions

Step 1: Activate the Yeast

In a small bowl, combine the warm water, sugar, and yeast. Allow the mixture to sit for 5-10 minutes until it becomes frothy, indicating that the yeast is activated.

Step 2: Mix the Dough

In a large mixing bowl, combine the bread flour and salt. Add the activated yeast mixture and stir until a shaggy dough forms. Turn the dough out onto a lightly floured surface and knead for 10-12 minutes until the dough becomes smooth and elastic.

Step 3: Let the Dough Rise

Place the dough in a lightly oiled bowl, cover it with a clean kitchen towel, and let it rise in a warm, draft-free place for about 1 hour or until it doubles in size.

Step 4: Shape the Bagels

Once the dough has risen, punch it down and divide it into 8 equal pieces. Shape each piece into a smooth ball, then poke a hole in the center and gently stretch it to form the classic bagel shape.

Step 5: Boil the Bagels

In a large pot, bring water to a gentle boil. Preheat your oven to 425°F (220°C). Carefully place the bagels in the boiling water, 2 at a time, and boil for 1 minute on each side. Remove the bagels from the water and place them on a parchment-lined baking sheet.

Step 6: Add the Everything Topping

Brush each bagel with the beaten egg wash, then generously sprinkle the sesame seeds, poppy seeds, dried garlic, dried onion, and coarse salt on top of each bagel.

Step 7: Bake to Perfection

Bake the bagels in the preheated oven for 20-25 minutes, or until they turn golden brown and sound hollow when tapped on the bottom.

Serving Suggestions

Once your everything bagels are fresh out of the oven, the possibilities for serving them are endless. Whether you choose to enjoy them simply toasted and smeared with cream cheese, or as the foundation for a gourmet breakfast sandwich, the rich flavors and chewy texture of these homemade everything bagels are sure to elevate your breakfast experience to new heights.

In Conclusion

Crafting your own batch of everything bagels from scratch is not only a deeply satisfying culinary endeavor but also a wonderful way to infuse your mornings with the irresistible aroma and flavors of a bakery-fresh breakfast. So, roll up your sleeves, preheat your oven, and get ready to savor the delight of homemade everything bagels. Your taste buds will thank you!

Remember, the key to perfecting this recipe lies in patience and attention to detail. Embrace the process, experiment with different toppings, and savor the reward of a truly delectable breakfast treat that you can proudly call your own.
