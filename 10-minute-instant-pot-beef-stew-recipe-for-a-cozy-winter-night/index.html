10-Minute Instant Pot Beef Stew Recipe for a Cozy Winter Night


There’s nothing quite like a hearty beef stew to warm you up on a chilly winter evening. The Instant Pot has revolutionized the way we cook, making it possible to create rich and flavorful stews in a fraction of the time it would take using traditional methods. In this recipe, I’ll show you how to make a delicious beef stew in just 10 minutes of active cooking time, perfect for those nights when you crave a comforting meal without spending hours in the kitchen.

Ingredients

  • 1.5 pounds of beef stew meat, cut into chunks
  • 2 tablespoons of olive oil
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 3 garlic cloves, minced
  • 4 carrots, cut into chunks
  • 3 potatoes, cut into chunks
  • 2 cups of beef broth
  • 1 cup of red wine (optional)
  • 2 tablespoons of tomato paste
  • 1 teaspoon of dried thyme
  • 1 teaspoon of dried rosemary
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 tablespoons of cornstarch
  • 2 tablespoons of water
  • Chopped fresh parsley for garnish

Instructions

  1. Turn on the Instant Pot and select the “Sauté” function. Once hot, add the olive oil and brown the beef stew meat in batches. This will help develop the flavor of the stew. Remove the browned meat and set it aside.
  2. In the same pot, add the chopped onion and garlic. Sauté for a few minutes until they are softened and fragrant.
  3. Add the carrots and potatoes to the pot, stirring them for a couple of minutes to coat them with the flavors in the pot.
  4. Pour in the beef broth and red wine (if using), scraping the bottom of the pot to release any browned bits. This step adds incredible depth to the stew.
  5. Stir in the tomato paste, dried thyme, and dried rosemary. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  6. Return the browned beef stew meat to the pot, along with any juices that may have accumulated.
  7. Seal the Instant Pot, set the valve to the sealing position, and select the “Pressure Cook” or “Manual” setting for 10 minutes.
  8. Once the cooking time is complete, allow for a natural pressure release for 10 minutes before carefully switching the valve to the venting position to release any remaining pressure.
  9. In a small bowl, mix the cornstarch and water to create a slurry. Stir the slurry into the stew to thicken the sauce. Select the “Sauté” function again and cook for a few minutes until the stew has thickened to your liking.
  10. Serve the beef stew hot, garnished with chopped fresh parsley for a burst of color and freshness.

Tips for Perfection

  • For an extra depth of flavor, you can sear the beef in the Instant Pot using the “Sauté” function before starting the stew.
  • If you’re short on time, you can skip the red wine and still achieve a delicious result.
  • This beef stew is even better the next day, so consider making a double batch for leftovers.

Final Thoughts

With the Instant Pot, you can enjoy a rich and comforting beef stew in just 10 minutes of active cooking time. The tender beef, aromatic vegetables, and savory broth come together to create a dish that’s perfect for a cozy winter night. Whether you’re cooking for your family or hosting a dinner party, this recipe is sure to impress. So, grab your Instant Pot, gather your ingredients, and get ready to savor the delicious flavors of this quick and easy beef stew.
