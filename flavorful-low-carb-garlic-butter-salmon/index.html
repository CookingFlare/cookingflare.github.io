Flavorful Low Carb Garlic Butter Salmon


If you’re looking for a delicious and healthy dinner option, this flavorful low carb garlic butter salmon recipe is a must-try. Not only is salmon a great source of protein and omega-3 fatty acids, but it’s also incredibly versatile and easy to cook. Paired with a rich garlic butter sauce, this dish is sure to become a favorite in your household. Let’s dive into the recipe and discover how to create this mouthwatering dish in just a few simple steps.

Ingredients

  • 4 salmon fillets
  • 4 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1/4 cup unsalted butter
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon fresh lemon juice
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley for garnish

Instructions

  1. Preheat the Oven: Start by preheating your oven to 375°F (190°C). Line a baking sheet with parchment paper and set it aside.

  2. Prepare the Salmon: Pat the salmon fillets dry with a paper towel and season them with salt and pepper on both sides. Place the fillets on the prepared baking sheet and set aside.

  3. Make the Garlic Butter Sauce: In a small saucepan, melt the butter over medium heat. Add the minced garlic and cook for 1-2 minutes, stirring frequently, until the garlic becomes fragrant. Stir in the olive oil and lemon juice, then remove the saucepan from the heat.

  4. Coat the Salmon: Pour the garlic butter sauce over the salmon fillets, making sure to evenly coat each piece.

  5. Bake the Salmon: Place the baking sheet in the preheated oven and bake the salmon for 12-15 minutes, or until it is cooked to your desired level of doneness. The salmon should flake easily with a fork when it’s ready.

  6. Garnish and Serve: Once the salmon is cooked, remove it from the oven and garnish with fresh parsley. Serve the garlic butter salmon hot, accompanied by your favorite low carb side dish or a crisp garden salad.

Tips for Success

  • Ensure the salmon is patted dry before seasoning to help achieve a crispy skin.
  • Adjust the amount of garlic to suit your personal taste preferences.
  • For an extra burst of flavor, consider adding a sprinkle of freshly grated parmesan cheese over the salmon before baking.

Why You’ll Love This Recipe

Not only is this low carb garlic butter salmon recipe incredibly easy to make, but it’s also packed with flavor. The combination of rich butter, aromatic garlic, and zesty lemon juice creates a truly irresistible sauce that complements the natural taste of the salmon. Additionally, this dish is low in carbohydrates, making it a perfect option for those following a keto or low carb lifestyle.

Salmon is also known for its health benefits, including being an excellent source of high-quality protein and essential omega-3 fatty acids. Incorporating this nutritious fish into your diet can support heart health, brain function, and overall well-being.

Whether you’re cooking for a special occasion or simply looking for a satisfying weeknight meal, this flavorful low carb garlic butter salmon is sure to impress. It’s a dish that proves healthy eating can be both indulgent and delicious.

So, next time you’re in the mood for a delectable seafood dish, give this recipe a try and savor the delightful flavors of perfectly cooked salmon with a luscious garlic butter sauce. Enjoy!
