Delicious Pork Tenderloin with Roasted Veggies: Family Dinner Recipe


Are you looking for a delicious and satisfying meal to enjoy with your family? Look no further! This recipe for Delicious Pork Tenderloin with Roasted Veggies is not only flavorful but also incredibly easy to make. With tender pork and a medley of colorful roasted vegetables, this dish is sure to become a family favorite.

Ingredients

  • 1 pork tenderloin (approximately 1.5 pounds)
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • 1 teaspoon dried rosemary
  • 1 red bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 yellow bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 zucchini, sliced
  • 1 red onion, sliced
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes
  • Fresh parsley for garnish

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C).

  2. In a small bowl, combine the paprika, garlic powder, dried thyme, dried rosemary, salt, and pepper. This will be the seasoning for the pork tenderloin.

  3. Rub the pork tenderloin all over with the olive oil, ensuring it is evenly coated. Then, sprinkle the seasoning mixture over the pork, pressing it gently to adhere.

  4. Place the seasoned pork tenderloin on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper. Surround the pork with the sliced bell peppers, zucchini, red onion, and cherry tomatoes. Drizzle the vegetables with olive oil and season with salt and pepper.

  5. Roast the pork and vegetables in the preheated oven for about 25-30 minutes, or until the internal temperature of the pork reaches 145°F (63°C). Remember to flip the vegetables halfway through for even cooking.

  6. Once cooked, remove the pork tenderloin from the oven and let it rest for about 5 minutes before slicing. This will ensure that the juices redistribute, resulting in a tender and juicy pork.

  7. While the pork rests, transfer the roasted vegetables to a serving platter. Sprinkle with fresh parsley for a pop of color and added freshness.

  8. Slice the pork tenderloin into medallions and arrange it on the platter alongside the roasted veggies. Serve hot and enjoy!

Tips and Variations

  • For an extra kick of flavor, you can marinate the pork tenderloin overnight in a mixture of olive oil, garlic, and your choice of herbs and spices. This will infuse the meat with even more deliciousness.

  • Feel free to customize the roasted vegetable medley based on your preferences or what you have on hand. You can add mushrooms, carrots, or even sweet potatoes for a heartier dish.

  • If you prefer your vegetables to be more caramelized, you can increase the oven temperature to 425°F (220°C) and roast them for an additional 5-10 minutes.

  • Leftovers can be stored in an airtight container in the refrigerator for up to 3 days. Simply reheat in the oven or microwave for a quick and easy meal.

Now that you have this scrumptious recipe in your arsenal, gather your loved ones around the dinner table and indulge in the flavors of this Delicious Pork Tenderloin with Roasted Veggies. It’s a surefire way to create precious family memories over a warm and satisfying meal. Enjoy!
