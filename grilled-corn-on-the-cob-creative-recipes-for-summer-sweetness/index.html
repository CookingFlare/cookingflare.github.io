Grilled Corn on the Cob: Creative Recipes for Summer Sweetness


Summer is here, and with it comes the perfect excuse to fire up the grill and indulge in some delicious grilled corn on the cob. Whether you’re a fan of classic butter and salt or looking to try something new, we’ve got you covered with these creative recipes that will take your grilled corn game to the next level.

Classic Grilled Corn with Herbs

  • 4 ears of corn, husked and silked
  • 2 tablespoons of unsalted butter, softened
  • 2 tablespoons of chopped fresh parsley
  • 2 tablespoons of chopped fresh chives
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Preheat your grill to medium-high heat. In a small bowl, mix together the softened butter, chopped parsley, and chopped chives. Brush the mixture evenly onto each ear of corn. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Grill the corn for 10-12 minutes, turning every 2-3 minutes, until slightly charred and tender.

Spicy Grilled Corn with Lime and Cotija

  • 4 ears of corn, husked and silked
  • 2 tablespoons of unsalted butter, softened
  • 1 tablespoon of freshly squeezed lime juice
  • 1 teaspoon of ground cumin
  • 1/2 teaspoon of smoked paprika
  • 1/4 teaspoon of cayenne pepper
  • 1/4 cup of crumbled Cotija cheese
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Preheat your grill to medium-high heat. In a small bowl, mix together the softened butter, lime juice, cumin, smoked paprika, and cayenne pepper. Brush the mixture evenly onto each ear of corn. Grill the corn for 10-12 minutes, turning every 2-3 minutes, until slightly charred and tender. Sprinkle with crumbled Cotija cheese and serve.

Korean-Style Grilled Corn with Gochujang and Sesame

  • 4 ears of corn, husked and silked
  • 2 tablespoons of unsalted butter, softened
  • 2 tablespoons of gochujang (Korean chili paste)
  • 1 tablespoon of soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon of brown sugar
  • 1/4 cup of toasted sesame seeds
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Preheat your grill to medium-high heat. In a small bowl, mix together the softened butter, gochujang, soy sauce, and brown sugar. Brush the mixture evenly onto each ear of corn. Grill the corn for 10-12 minutes, turning every 2-3 minutes, until slightly charred and tender. Sprinkle with toasted sesame seeds and serve.

Grilled Corn with Garlic and Parmesan

  • 4 ears of corn, husked and silked
  • 2 tablespoons of unsalted butter, softened
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1/4 cup of grated Parmesan cheese
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Preheat your grill to medium-high heat. In a small bowl, mix together the softened butter, minced garlic, and grated Parmesan cheese. Brush the mixture evenly onto each ear of corn. Grill the corn for 10-12 minutes, turning every 2-3 minutes, until slightly charred and tender. Serve hot.

Mexican-Style Grilled Corn with Chili Powder and Lime

  • 4 ears of corn, husked and silked
  • 2 tablespoons of unsalted butter, softened
  • 1 tablespoon of chili powder
  • 1 tablespoon of freshly squeezed lime juice
  • 1/4 cup of crumbled queso fresco (Mexican cheese)
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Preheat your grill to medium-high heat. In a small bowl, mix together the softened butter, chili powder, and lime juice. Brush the mixture evenly onto each ear of corn. Grill the corn for 10-12 minutes, turning every 2-3 minutes, until slightly charred and tender. Sprinkle with crumbled queso fresco and serve.

Whether you’re in the mood for something classic and simple or adventurous and bold, these grilled corn recipes are sure to please even the pickiest of eaters. So fire up the grill, grab some corn, and get creative with these delicious recipes. Happy grilling!
