Wholesome Low Carb Green Bean and Almond Salad Recipe


Are you looking for a refreshing and nutritious salad that’s not only low in carbs but also bursting with flavor? Look no further than this Wholesome Low Carb Green Bean and Almond Salad! Packed with vibrant green beans, crunchy almonds, and a zesty dressing, this salad is a celebration of fresh, wholesome ingredients that will leave you feeling satisfied and energized. Whether you’re following a low-carb diet or simply seeking a delicious and nutritious dish, this recipe is sure to become a staple in your meal rotation.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound fresh green beans, trimmed
  • 1/2 cup slivered almonds
  • 1/4 cup extra virgin olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons red wine vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon Dijon mustard
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley for garnish

Instructions

  1. Blanch the Green Beans: Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Add the green beans and cook for 2-3 minutes, or until they are bright green and crisp-tender. Immediately transfer the green beans to a bowl of ice water to stop the cooking process. Once cooled, drain the green beans and pat them dry with a paper towel.

  2. Toast the Almonds: In a dry skillet over medium heat, toast the slivered almonds, stirring frequently, until they are golden and fragrant. This should take about 3-5 minutes. Keep a close eye on the almonds to prevent them from burning. Once toasted, remove the almonds from the skillet and set them aside to cool.

  3. Prepare the Dressing: In a small bowl, whisk together the extra virgin olive oil, red wine vinegar, Dijon mustard, minced garlic, salt, and pepper. Taste the dressing and adjust the seasoning as needed.

  4. Assemble the Salad: In a large bowl, combine the blanched green beans and toasted almonds. Drizzle the dressing over the salad and gently toss to coat the ingredients evenly.

  5. Chill and Garnish: Cover the salad and refrigerate for at least 30 minutes to allow the flavors to meld. Before serving, garnish the salad with fresh parsley for a pop of color and added freshness.

Serving Suggestions

This Wholesome Low Carb Green Bean and Almond Salad pairs beautifully with grilled chicken, fish, or tofu for a complete and satisfying meal. It also makes a delightful side dish for barbecues, picnics, and potlucks. Feel free to customize the salad by adding cherry tomatoes, red onion, or crumbled feta cheese for an extra burst of flavor and texture.

Nutritional Benefits

Green beans are rich in vitamins A, C, and K, as well as folate and fiber, making them a nutrient-dense addition to your diet. Almonds provide heart-healthy fats, protein, and essential nutrients like vitamin E and magnesium. With this salad, you can nourish your body with wholesome ingredients while savoring every bite.

Conclusion

In conclusion, this Wholesome Low Carb Green Bean and Almond Salad is a testament to the fact that healthy eating can be both delicious and satisfying. Whether you enjoy it as a light lunch, a side dish, or a potluck contribution, this salad is a versatile and flavorful option for any occasion. Embrace the vibrant flavors of fresh green beans and crunchy almonds, and elevate your culinary repertoire with this delightful recipe. Bon appétit!
