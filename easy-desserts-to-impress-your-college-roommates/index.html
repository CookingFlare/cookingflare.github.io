Easy Desserts to Impress Your College Roommates


Are you tired of the same old store-bought desserts? Want to impress your college roommates with your culinary skills? Look no further! In this blog post, we will share some easy dessert recipes that are sure to wow your roommates and leave them begging for more. These desserts are not only delicious but also simple to make, perfect for any college student with limited time and resources.

1. Chocolate Lava Mug Cake

Who can resist a gooey chocolate cake? This chocolate lava mug cake is the ultimate dessert to satisfy your sweet tooth. With just a few ingredients and a microwave, you can whip up this decadent treat in no time.

Ingredients:

  • 4 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • 3 tablespoons granulated sugar
  • 2 tablespoons unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1/8 teaspoon baking powder
  • Pinch of salt
  • 3 tablespoons milk
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1/4 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 tablespoon chocolate chips

Instructions:

  1. In a microwave-safe mug, whisk together the flour, sugar, cocoa powder, baking powder, and salt.
  2. Add the milk, vegetable oil, and vanilla extract to the mug. Stir until well combined.
  3. Stir in the chocolate chips.
  4. Microwave the mug on high for 60 to 70 seconds, or until the cake has risen and the center is still slightly gooey.
  5. Allow the cake to cool for a minute before digging in.

2. Berry Parfait

If you’re looking for a dessert that’s both refreshing and healthy, this berry parfait is the perfect choice. Packed with vibrant berries and creamy yogurt, it’s a guilt-free treat that will leave your roommates impressed.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup Greek yogurt
  • 1 cup mixed berries (strawberries, blueberries, raspberries)
  • 2 tablespoons honey
  • 1/4 cup granola

Instructions:

  1. In a glass or bowl, layer the Greek yogurt, mixed berries, and honey.
  2. Repeat the layers until all the ingredients are used, ending with a dollop of Greek yogurt on top.
  3. Sprinkle the granola over the yogurt.
  4. Serve chilled and enjoy!

3. Peanut Butter Banana Bites

If you’re a fan of the classic combination of peanut butter and banana, you’ll love these bite-sized treats. They’re easy to make and perfect for snacking or as a quick dessert.

Ingredients:

  • 2 ripe bananas
  • 1/4 cup peanut butter
  • 1/4 cup chocolate chips

Instructions:

  1. Peel the bananas and cut them into bite-sized pieces.
  2. Spread a small amount of peanut butter on one side of each banana piece.
  3. Place another banana piece on top to create a mini sandwich.
  4. Melt the chocolate chips in the microwave or on the stovetop, stirring until smooth.
  5. Dip each banana bite halfway into the melted chocolate, allowing any excess to drip off.
  6. Place the chocolate-covered banana bites on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper.
  7. Place the baking sheet in the refrigerator for about 30 minutes, or until the chocolate has hardened.
  8. Serve chilled and enjoy!

Conclusion

With these easy dessert recipes, you can impress your college roommates and satisfy your sweet cravings without spending hours in the kitchen. Whether it’s a chocolate lava mug cake, a refreshing berry parfait, or peanut butter banana bites, these desserts are sure to leave a lasting impression.

So, grab your apron, gather your ingredients, and get ready to amaze your college roommates with these delicious and easy-to-make treats. Happy cooking!
