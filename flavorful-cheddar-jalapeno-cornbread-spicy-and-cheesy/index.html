Flavorful Cheddar Jalapeno Cornbread: Spicy and Cheesy


If you’re a fan of cornbread and love a little kick in your food, then this recipe for Flavorful Cheddar Jalapeno Cornbread is perfect for you. Packed with the bold flavors of sharp cheddar cheese and spicy jalapenos, this cornbread will add a delicious twist to any meal. Whether you’re serving it as a side dish or enjoying it on its own, this recipe is sure to satisfy your taste buds.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup cornmeal
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 tablespoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 cup buttermilk
  • 1/4 cup unsalted butter, melted
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 cup shredded sharp cheddar cheese
  • 1/4 cup diced jalapenos (seeds and membranes removed for less heat)

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C) and grease a 9-inch square baking dish.
  2. In a large mixing bowl, combine the cornmeal, flour, baking powder, salt, and sugar. Mix well to ensure all the dry ingredients are evenly distributed.
  3. In a separate bowl, whisk together the buttermilk, melted butter, and eggs until well combined.
  4. Pour the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients and gently stir until just combined. Be careful not to overmix, as this can result in a dense cornbread.
  5. Fold in the shredded cheddar cheese and diced jalapenos, ensuring they are evenly distributed throughout the batter.
  6. Pour the batter into the greased baking dish and spread it out evenly.
  7. Bake in the preheated oven for 20-25 minutes, or until the cornbread is golden brown on top and a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.
  8. Remove from the oven and allow the cornbread to cool slightly before slicing and serving.

Serving Suggestions

This Flavorful Cheddar Jalapeno Cornbread is incredibly versatile and can be enjoyed in a variety of ways. Here are a few serving suggestions to get you started:

  • Serve alongside a bowl of chili for a hearty and comforting meal.
  • Cut into bite-sized pieces and serve as an appetizer at your next gathering.
  • Toast slices of cornbread and top with avocado, salsa, and sour cream for a delicious twist on avocado toast.
  • Enjoy a slice on its own as a snack or light lunch.

Tips and Variations

  • For an extra burst of flavor, try adding some chopped cooked bacon to the batter.
  • If you prefer a milder cornbread, reduce the amount of diced jalapenos or remove the seeds and membranes before dicing.
  • Experiment with different types of cheese, such as pepper jack or smoked Gouda, to customize the flavor to your liking.
  • To make this recipe gluten-free, simply substitute the all-purpose flour with a gluten-free flour blend.

Now that you have this amazing recipe for Flavorful Cheddar Jalapeno Cornbread, it’s time to get in the kitchen and start baking. With its spicy kick and cheesy goodness, this cornbread is sure to become a new favorite. So gather your ingredients, preheat your oven, and get ready to enjoy a slice of this delicious and satisfying treat. Happy baking!
