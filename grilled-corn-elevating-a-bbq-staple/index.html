Grilled Corn: Elevating a BBQ Staple


Grilled corn is a quintessential BBQ staple that brings a burst of smoky flavor and a delightful crunch to any summer gathering. Whether you’re hosting a backyard cookout or simply craving a tasty side dish, grilled corn is a versatile and delicious addition to your menu. In this blog post, we will explore some unique and flavorful ways to elevate this classic dish, taking your BBQ experience to new heights.

Ingredients:

  • Fresh corn on the cob (as many as desired)
  • Butter, softened
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • Various seasonings and toppings (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the grill: Before starting, preheat your grill to medium-high heat. This will ensure that the corn cooks evenly and develops those beautiful charred grill marks.

  2. Prepare the corn: Peel back the husks of each ear of corn, leaving them attached at the base. Remove the silk strands and then fold the husks back up, covering the corn. Soak the corn in cold water for about 10 minutes. This step helps to prevent the husks from burning during grilling and keeps the corn moist.

  3. Grill the corn: Remove the corn from the water and shake off any excess. Place the corn on the preheated grill, directly over the flame, and close the lid. Grill for about 10-15 minutes, turning occasionally, until the corn is tender and lightly charred. Keep an eye on the corn to prevent it from burning.

  4. Add flavor: Once the corn is grilled to perfection, carefully remove it from the grill using tongs. Peel back the husks and brush each ear of corn with softened butter. Sprinkle with salt and pepper to taste. This simple seasoning enhances the natural sweetness of the corn and adds a burst of flavor.

  5. Get creative with toppings: Now comes the fun part – adding unique and flavorful toppings to take your grilled corn to the next level. Here are a few ideas to get you started:

    • Mexican Street Corn: Spread a layer of mayonnaise over each ear of corn, then roll it in crumbled cotija cheese. Sprinkle with chili powder and a squeeze of lime juice for an authentic Mexican twist.

    • Parmesan Herb Corn: Mix together grated Parmesan cheese, chopped fresh herbs (such as parsley, cilantro, or basil), and a pinch of garlic powder. Roll each ear of corn in the mixture to coat it with the cheesy herb goodness.

    • Spicy Sriracha Corn: Combine melted butter with sriracha sauce and a squeeze of lime juice. Brush this spicy mixture onto the grilled corn and sprinkle with chopped fresh cilantro for an irresistible kick.

    • BBQ Bacon Corn: Wrap each ear of grilled corn with a slice of crispy bacon and secure it with toothpicks. Brush with your favorite BBQ sauce and enjoy the smoky and savory combination.

  6. Serve and enjoy: Arrange the grilled corn on a platter and garnish with additional toppings or herbs, if desired. Serve it alongside your favorite BBQ dishes and watch as your guests delight in the elevated flavors of this classic summer side.

Grilled corn is a versatile canvas that allows you to experiment with various seasonings and toppings, making it a standout dish at any BBQ. Whether you prefer a sweet and savory combination or crave a spicy kick, there’s a grilled corn variation to suit every palate. So fire up the grill, gather your friends and family, and savor the taste of summer with this elevated BBQ staple.
