Cloves Unveiled: From Savory to Sweet Creations


When it comes to adding warmth, depth, and a touch of sweetness to dishes, cloves are an essential ingredient that often takes center stage in both savory and sweet recipes. These aromatic flower buds from the clove tree are not only known for their distinctive flavor but also for their numerous health benefits. In this blog post, we will explore the versatility of cloves and unveil a range of savory and sweet creations that will elevate your culinary repertoire.

Savory Creations

1. Clove-Spiced Pulled Pork

Ingredients:

  • 3 lbs pork shoulder
  • 2 tsp ground cloves
  • 1 tsp ground black pepper
  • 1 tbsp brown sugar
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 cup apple cider vinegar
  • 1 cup chicken broth
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced

Instructions:

  1. In a small bowl, mix the ground cloves, black pepper, brown sugar, and salt to create a dry rub.
  2. Rub the mixture evenly over the pork shoulder.
  3. In a slow cooker, combine the apple cider vinegar, chicken broth, and minced garlic.
  4. Place the seasoned pork shoulder in the slow cooker and cook on low for 8 hours or until the meat is tender and easily pulls apart.
  5. Once cooked, shred the pork and serve as a filling for sandwiches or tacos.

2. Clove-Spiced Butternut Squash Soup

Ingredients:

  • 1 medium butternut squash, peeled and cubed
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 4 cups vegetable broth
  • 1 tsp ground cloves
  • 1/2 tsp ground nutmeg
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1/4 cup heavy cream (optional)
  • Fresh parsley for garnish

Instructions:

  1. In a large pot, sauté the chopped onion and minced garlic until softened.
  2. Add the cubed butternut squash, ground cloves, and nutmeg to the pot.
  3. Pour in the vegetable broth and bring the mixture to a boil.
  4. Reduce the heat and let the soup simmer for 20-25 minutes or until the squash is tender.
  5. Use an immersion blender to puree the soup until smooth.
  6. Stir in the heavy cream, if using, and season with salt and pepper.
  7. Ladle the soup into bowls and garnish with fresh parsley before serving.

Sweet Creations

1. Clove-Spiced Apple Pie

Ingredients:

  • 6 cups sliced apples
  • 3/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 2 tbsp all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp ground cinnamon
  • 1/2 tsp ground cloves
  • 1/4 tsp ground nutmeg
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • 2 9-inch pie crusts

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 425°F (220°C).
  2. In a large bowl, combine the sliced apples, granulated sugar, flour, ground cinnamon, ground cloves, ground nutmeg, and lemon juice.
  3. Place one pie crust in a pie dish and pour the apple mixture into the crust.
  4. Cover the pie with the second crust, crimp the edges, and cut slits in the top crust to allow steam to escape.
  5. Bake the pie for 45-50 minutes or until the crust is golden brown and the filling is bubbling.

2. Clove-Spiced Hot Chocolate

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups milk
  • 1/4 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/4 tsp ground cloves
  • 1/4 tsp ground cinnamon
  • 1/2 tsp vanilla extract
  • Whipped cream and ground cinnamon for garnish

Instructions:

  1. In a saucepan, heat the milk over medium heat until warmed through.
  2. Whisk in the cocoa powder, sugar, ground cloves, and ground cinnamon until the mixture is smooth and heated.
  3. Remove the saucepan from the heat and stir in the vanilla extract.
  4. Pour the hot chocolate into mugs and top with whipped cream and a sprinkle of ground cinnamon.

Cloves, with their warm, aromatic, and slightly sweet flavor, add a delightful twist to both savory and sweet dishes. Whether you’re simmering a hearty stew, baking a delectable dessert, or crafting a cozy beverage, the addition of cloves can truly transform your culinary creations. Embrace the versatility of this humble spice and let its essence infuse your kitchen with a symphony of flavors.
