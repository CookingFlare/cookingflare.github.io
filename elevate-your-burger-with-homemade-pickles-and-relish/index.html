Elevate Your Burger with Homemade Pickles and Relish


Everybody loves a good burger, but what sets an ordinary burger apart from an extraordinary one? The answer lies in the details, and one often-overlooked detail that can take your burger to the next level is homemade pickles and relish. These tangy and flavorful condiments add a burst of freshness and complexity to your burger, making each bite a delightful experience. In this blog post, we will explore how to make your own pickles and relish from scratch, elevating your burger game to new heights.

Homemade Pickles

Making your own pickles may seem like a daunting task, but I assure you, it’s easier than you think. Plus, the satisfaction of biting into a crunchy pickle that you made yourself is unparalleled. Here’s a simple and delicious recipe for homemade pickles:

Ingredients:

  • 4-5 pickling cucumbers
  • 1 cup water
  • 1 cup distilled white vinegar
  • 2 tablespoons kosher salt
  • 1 tablespoon sugar
  • 2 cloves of garlic, smashed
  • 1 tablespoon whole black peppercorns
  • 1 teaspoon dill seeds
  • 1 teaspoon mustard seeds

Instructions:

  1. Wash the cucumbers thoroughly and slice them into thin rounds. Set aside.
  2. In a saucepan, combine water, vinegar, salt, and sugar. Bring it to a boil, stirring until the salt and sugar dissolve completely.
  3. In sterilized jars, divide the garlic, peppercorns, dill seeds, and mustard seeds evenly.
  4. Pack the cucumber slices tightly into the jars, leaving about half an inch of headspace at the top.
  5. Pour the hot vinegar mixture into the jars, covering the cucumbers completely.
  6. Seal the jars tightly and let them cool to room temperature. Then, refrigerate the pickles for at least 24 hours before enjoying them.

Homemade Relish

Now that you have your homemade pickles, let’s move on to making a relish that will take your burger to new heights. This tangy and sweet relish is the perfect complement to the smoky flavors of a burger.

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups chopped pickles (homemade or store-bought)
  • 1/2 cup finely chopped onion
  • 1/4 cup diced red bell pepper
  • 1/4 cup diced green bell pepper
  • 1/4 cup apple cider vinegar
  • 2 tablespoons granulated sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon mustard seeds
  • 1/4 teaspoon celery seeds
  • 1/4 teaspoon turmeric
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a medium-sized bowl, combine the chopped pickles, onion, red bell pepper, and green bell pepper.
  2. In a small saucepan, heat the apple cider vinegar, sugar, mustard seeds, celery seeds, turmeric, salt, and pepper over medium heat. Stir until the sugar dissolves and the mixture comes to a simmer.
  3. Pour the hot vinegar mixture over the pickle mixture and stir to combine.
  4. Let the relish cool to room temperature, then transfer it to a jar or airtight container.
  5. Refrigerate the relish for at least 2 hours before serving to allow the flavors to meld together.

Elevating Your Burger

Now that you have your homemade pickles and relish, it’s time to assemble the ultimate burger. Here are a few ideas to help you elevate your burger game:

  1. Classic Cheeseburger: Top your juicy patty with a slice of your favorite cheese, a generous dollop of relish, and a few slices of homemade pickles. The combination of flavors will have your taste buds dancing.

  2. Spicy Burger: Add a kick to your burger by spreading a thin layer of spicy mayo on the bun. Top it with a patty, melted pepper jack cheese, pickles, and a spoonful of relish. This burger is not for the faint of heart!

  3. Mediterranean Burger: Take your taste buds on a trip to the Mediterranean by topping your patty with feta cheese, sliced cucumbers, Kalamata olives, and a spoonful of relish. The flavors will transport you to sunny shores.

  4. Barbecue Bacon Burger: Who can resist the smoky flavor of bacon? Top your patty with crispy bacon, a slice of cheddar cheese, pickles, and a drizzle of tangy barbecue sauce. This burger is a crowd-pleaser.

Remember, the key to a great burger is to balance the flavors and textures. Experiment with different combinations of toppings, and don’t be afraid to get creative. With homemade pickles and relish in your arsenal, the possibilities are endless. So, fire up that grill and get ready to elevate your burger game to new heights. Enjoy!
