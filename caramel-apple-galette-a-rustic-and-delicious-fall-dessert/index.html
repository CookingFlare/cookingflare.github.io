Caramel Apple Galette: A Rustic and Delicious Fall Dessert


Fall is the perfect season for indulging in warm and comforting desserts, and there’s no better way to embrace the flavors of autumn than with a delicious Caramel Apple Galette. This rustic dessert combines the sweetness of caramel with the tartness of apples, all wrapped in a buttery, flaky crust. It’s the perfect treat to enjoy on a chilly evening, paired with a scoop of vanilla ice cream or a dollop of whipped cream.

Ingredients

For the crust:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup cold unsalted butter, cut into small cubes
  • 1/4 cup ice water

For the filling:

  • 4-5 medium-sized apples, peeled, cored, and thinly sliced
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground nutmeg
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 cup caramel sauce

For the caramel sauce:

  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/4 cup water
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • 2 tablespoons unsalted butter
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • Pinch of salt

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C) and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.

  2. In a large mixing bowl, combine the flour, sugar, and salt for the crust. Add the cold butter cubes and use a pastry cutter or your fingers to cut the butter into the flour mixture until it resembles coarse crumbs.

  3. Gradually add the ice water, a tablespoon at a time, mixing until the dough comes together. Be careful not to overmix. Shape the dough into a disk, wrap it in plastic wrap, and refrigerate for at least 30 minutes.

  4. In another bowl, combine the sliced apples, sugar, cinnamon, nutmeg, salt, and flour for the filling. Toss everything together until the apples are evenly coated.

  5. On a lightly floured surface, roll out the chilled dough into a rough circle, about 12 inches in diameter. Transfer the dough onto the prepared baking sheet.

  6. Arrange the apple slices in the center of the dough, leaving a 2-inch border around the edges. Fold the edges of the dough over the apples, pleating as you go, to create a rustic crust.

  7. Drizzle the caramel sauce over the apples, reserving a little for serving. Brush the crust with some melted butter for a golden finish.

  8. Bake the galette in the preheated oven for 35-40 minutes, or until the crust is golden and the apples are tender.

  9. While the galette is baking, prepare the caramel sauce. In a medium saucepan, combine the sugar and water. Cook over medium-high heat, swirling the pan occasionally, until the sugar dissolves and turns amber in color.

  10. Remove the pan from heat and carefully whisk in the heavy cream, followed by the butter, vanilla extract, and salt. Continue whisking until the caramel sauce is smooth and well combined. Set aside to cool.

  11. Once the galette is done baking, remove it from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes. Drizzle some of the remaining caramel sauce over the top.

  12. Serve the Caramel Apple Galette warm or at room temperature, and enjoy the delightful combination of sweet caramel and tart apples in every bite.

Conclusion

Indulge in the flavors of fall with this delicious Caramel Apple Galette. This rustic dessert is easy to make and will impress your family and friends with its beautiful presentation and mouthwatering taste. With a flaky crust, tender apples, and a drizzle of homemade caramel sauce, this galette is the perfect dessert for any autumn gathering. So, go ahead and treat yourself to this delightful fall dessert that will warm your heart and satisfy your sweet tooth.
