Mouthwatering Instant Pot Teriyaki Chicken Recipe


Are you craving a delicious and flavorful chicken dish that is quick and easy to make? Look no further! This mouthwatering Instant Pot Teriyaki Chicken recipe is sure to satisfy your taste buds. With the help of your trusty Instant Pot, you can have a tasty meal on the table in no time. So, let’s dive right into the recipe!

Ingredients

  • 2 pounds of boneless, skinless chicken thighs
  • 1/2 cup of soy sauce
  • 1/4 cup of honey
  • 1/4 cup of rice vinegar
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon of grated fresh ginger
  • 1 tablespoon of cornstarch
  • 1 tablespoon of water
  • Optional garnish: sesame seeds and sliced green onions

Instructions

  1. Start by preparing the chicken. Trim any excess fat and cut the chicken thighs into bite-sized pieces.

  2. In a small bowl, whisk together the soy sauce, honey, rice vinegar, minced garlic, and grated ginger. This will be the teriyaki sauce for your chicken.

  3. Preheat your Instant Pot by selecting the “Sauté” function. Once the pot is hot, add the chicken and cook until it is browned on all sides. This will help seal in the flavors and keep the chicken juicy.

  4. Pour the prepared teriyaki sauce over the chicken in the Instant Pot. Give it a gentle stir to ensure that all the chicken pieces are coated with the sauce.

  5. Secure the lid of the Instant Pot and set the valve to the “Sealing” position. Select the “Manual” or “Pressure Cook” function and set the timer for 10 minutes.

  6. While the chicken is cooking, prepare a slurry by combining the cornstarch and water in a small bowl. This will help thicken the sauce.

  7. Once the cooking time is complete, allow the pressure to release naturally for 5 minutes. Then, carefully perform a quick release by turning the valve to the “Venting” position.

  8. Remove the lid of the Instant Pot and select the “Sauté” function once again. Add the cornstarch slurry to the pot and stir well. Cook for an additional 2-3 minutes, or until the sauce has thickened to your desired consistency.

  9. Serve the mouthwatering Instant Pot Teriyaki Chicken over steamed rice or noodles. Garnish with sesame seeds and sliced green onions for an extra burst of flavor and visual appeal.

Tips and Variations

  • For an extra touch of sweetness, you can add a tablespoon of brown sugar to the teriyaki sauce mixture.
  • If you prefer a spicier kick, you can add some red pepper flakes or Sriracha sauce to the teriyaki sauce.
  • Feel free to customize this recipe with your favorite vegetables. Simply add them to the Instant Pot along with the chicken before cooking.
  • Leftover teriyaki chicken can be stored in an airtight container in the refrigerator for up to 3 days. It can be reheated in the microwave or on the stovetop.

Now you have a delicious Instant Pot Teriyaki Chicken recipe at your fingertips. This flavorful dish is perfect for busy weeknights when you want a satisfying meal without spending hours in the kitchen. Give it a try and let your taste buds thank you! Enjoy!
