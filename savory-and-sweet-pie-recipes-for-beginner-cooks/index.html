Savory and Sweet Pie Recipes for Beginner Cooks


Pies are a classic dish loved by many, and they come in a variety of flavors and fillings. Whether you’re a beginner cook or someone looking to expand their culinary skills, making pies can be a fun and rewarding experience. In this blog post, we will explore both savory and sweet pie recipes that are perfect for beginner cooks. So let’s roll up our sleeves and get baking!

Savory Pie Recipes

Chicken Pot Pie

Preparation time: 20 minutes

Cooking time: 40 minutes

Servings: 4

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups cooked chicken, shredded
  • 1 cup frozen mixed vegetables
  • 1 cup chicken broth
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 cup butter
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 package store-bought pie crust

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. In a saucepan, melt the butter over medium heat. Add the flour and stir until well combined.
  3. Slowly pour in the chicken broth and milk, stirring constantly until the mixture thickens.
  4. Add the shredded chicken, mixed vegetables, thyme, salt, and pepper to the saucepan. Stir until well combined.
  5. Remove the saucepan from heat and set aside.
  6. Line a pie dish with one of the pie crusts. Pour the chicken mixture into the dish.
  7. Cover the filling with the second pie crust, seal the edges, and cut slits on the top to allow steam to escape.
  8. Bake for 40 minutes or until the crust is golden brown.
  9. Allow the pie to cool for a few minutes before serving.

Spinach and Feta Pie

Preparation time: 15 minutes

Cooking time: 30 minutes

Servings: 6

Ingredients:

  • 1 package frozen spinach, thawed and drained
  • 1 cup crumbled feta cheese
  • 1/2 cup ricotta cheese
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/4 teaspoon nutmeg
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 package store-bought puff pastry sheets

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C).
  2. In a mixing bowl, combine the spinach, feta cheese, ricotta cheese, Parmesan cheese, garlic, nutmeg, salt, and pepper. Mix well.
  3. Roll out the puff pastry sheets and line a pie dish with one of the sheets.
  4. Pour the spinach mixture into the dish and spread it evenly.
  5. Cover the filling with the second puff pastry sheet, seal the edges, and cut slits on the top.
  6. Bake for 30 minutes or until the pastry is golden brown.
  7. Allow the pie to cool slightly before serving.

Sweet Pie Recipes

Apple Pie

Preparation time: 30 minutes

Cooking time: 50 minutes

Servings: 8

Ingredients:

  • 6 cups peeled and sliced apples
  • 3/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground nutmeg
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 1 package store-bought pie crust

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 425°F (220°C).
  2. In a large mixing bowl, combine the sliced apples, sugar, flour, cinnamon, nutmeg, and lemon juice. Mix well.
  3. Line a pie dish with one of the pie crusts. Pour the apple mixture into the dish.
  4. Cover the filling with the second pie crust, seal the edges, and cut slits on the top.
  5. Bake for 50 minutes or until the crust is golden brown and the filling is bubbling.
  6. Allow the pie to cool for at least 1 hour before serving.

Chocolate Cream Pie

Preparation time: 25 minutes

Cooking time: 10 minutes

Chilling time: 4 hours

Servings: 8

Ingredients:

  • 1 9-inch pre-baked pie crust
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/4 cup cornstarch
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 3 cups whole milk
  • 4 large egg yolks
  • 6 ounces semisweet chocolate, chopped
  • 2 tablespoons unsalted butter
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • Whipped cream for garnish

Instructions:

  1. In a saucepan, combine the sugar, cornstarch, and salt. Gradually whisk in the milk until smooth.
  2. Place the saucepan over medium heat and cook, stirring constantly, until the mixture thickens.
  3. In a separate bowl, whisk the egg yolks. Slowly pour a small amount of the hot milk mixture into the egg yolks, whisking constantly.
  4. Pour the egg yolk mixture back into the saucepan and cook for an additional 2 minutes, stirring constantly.
  5. Remove the saucepan from heat and stir in the chopped chocolate, butter, and vanilla extract until smooth.
  6. Pour the chocolate mixture into the pre-baked pie crust and smooth the top.
  7. Refrigerate for at least 4 hours or until set.
  8. Garnish with whipped cream before serving.

Conclusion

These savory and sweet pie recipes are perfect for beginner cooks who want to try their hand at creating delicious and impressive dishes. From the comforting Chicken Pot Pie to the indulgent Chocolate Cream Pie, there’s something for everyone to enjoy. So gather your ingredients, preheat your oven, and let the aroma of freshly baked pies fill your kitchen. Happy baking!
