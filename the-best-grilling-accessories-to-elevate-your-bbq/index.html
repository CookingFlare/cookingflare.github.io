The Best Grilling Accessories to Elevate Your BBQ


When it comes to outdoor cooking, a grill is just the beginning. While a quality grill is essential for achieving that perfect char and smoky flavor, the right accessories can make the difference between a good barbecue and an extraordinary one. Whether you’re a seasoned grill master or just starting out, investing in the right tools and gear will take your BBQ game to the next level. From precision thermometers to multifunctional grilling mats, the right accessories not only make grilling more enjoyable but also ensure consistent, professional-level results every time. Let’s explore the must-have grilling accessories that can transform your backyard cookout into an unforgettable culinary experience.

1. Grill Tongs and Spatula Set – Your Everyday Grill Companions

First, every grill enthusiast needs a reliable set of tongs and a sturdy spatula. These tools allow you to maneuver steaks, chicken, vegetables, or burgers without damaging their structure. Opt for tongs with a long handle, heat-resistant grips, and locking hinges for effortless management and storage. A high-quality spatula should feature a wide surface for flipping and a beveled edge to slide easily under delicate foods like fish fillets. Look for stainless steel options to ensure durability and easy cleaning.

It’s worth noting that a set with an additional fork can be helpful, but be cautious not to pierce meats too often, as it causes those flavorful juices to escape.

2. Instant-Read Meat Thermometer for Precise Cooking

Achieving the perfect level of doneness for different meats is what separates the amateurs from the pros. An instant-read meat thermometer is an essential tool for ensuring your steak is perfectly medium-rare or your chicken is cooked thoroughly but still juicy. A digital model with a fast response time is ideal, as it minimizes the time you need to leave your grill open.

Some advanced thermometers even come equipped with Bluetooth or Wi-Fi capabilities, so you can monitor the temperature of your meat from your smartphone while mingling with your guests or preparing sides in the kitchen.

3. Grill Baskets for Small or Delicate Foods

Grill baskets are a lifesaver when cooking small or delicate items such as shrimp, diced vegetables, or fish that might otherwise slip through the grates. These baskets come in various sizes, and many feature non-stick coatings for easy release. A high-quality basket ensures even heat distribution and allows you to flip an entire batch with one swift motion.

Consider perforated options if you prefer a little char and smoky flavor without burning or sticking. Grill baskets are also great for toasting bread or warming tortillas—it’s a versatile must-have for any BBQ lover.

4. Heat-Resistant BBQ Gloves for Safety

Protecting your hands while grilling is non-negotiable. Heat-resistant BBQ gloves allow you to handle hot tools, food, or even move around coals without fear of burns. Look for gloves made from silicone or aramid fiber, which can withstand high temperatures and provide a non-slip grip.

These gloves are especially important if you’re working with heavy cast iron pans or grilling over a fire pit. You’ll often find that high-quality gloves are water- and stain-resistant, which is an added bonus.

5. Grill Mats – The Secret to Clean Grilling

Grill mats are a game-changer for anyone who loves doing a variety of foods on the grill. These mats sit on top of the grates and prevent small pieces of food from falling into the fire while still giving you those perfect grill marks. They are perfect for delicate items like eggs or pancakes and sticky foods such as marinated fish or fruit.

Because they’re reusable, grill mats are also eco-friendly and reduce cleanup time. Just wipe them down or toss them in the dishwasher after use. Be sure to buy a set that is PFOA-free and safe for high-heat cooking to ensure both durability and food safety.

6. Chimney Starter for Effortless and Eco-Friendly Charcoal Lighting

Using lighter fluid can alter the flavor of your grilled foods, not to mention its impact on the environment. A chimney starter eliminates the need for harmful chemicals and makes starting a charcoal grill a breeze. Simply load it with lump charcoal or briquettes, place it over a few crumpled pieces of newspaper, and light the paper. Within 15–20 minutes, you’ll have evenly fired coals ready to use.

The chimney starter is especially useful for low-and-slow BBQ sessions, as it allows you to add lit coals gradually to maintain steady heat.

7. Rotisserie Kit for Impressive Meals

Rotisserie cooking transforms your grill into a powerhouse capable of producing tender, juicy meats with perfectly crispy skin. From whole chickens to roasts, a rotisserie kit spins your food slowly over the fire, ensuring even cooking and exceptional flavor.

Most grill brands offer rotisserie attachment kits designed to fit their models, so you’ll want to choose one compatible with your setup. Pair this with a quality basting brush and your favorite marinade for show-stopping results.

8. Cast Iron Griddle for Versatility

Expand your grill’s functionality with a cast iron griddle. These heavy-duty accessories are perfect for cooking foods that typically don’t work well directly over grates, such as bacon, fried eggs, pancakes, or sautéed vegetables. Cast iron retains heat exceptionally well, so you’ll achieve even cooking with no cold spots.

Many griddles come reversible, with one side flat and the other featuring a ridged design, giving you flexibility depending on what you’re preparing. Season your griddle regularly to maintain its non-stick surface and prevent rusting.

9. Skewers for Kabobs and More

Skewers are a classic grilling accessory that every BBQ enthusiast should own. Whether you’re making kabobs, grilling marshmallows, or crafting fruit skewers for dessert, high-quality skewers make the process easier. Stainless steel skewers are reusable and more durable than their bamboo counterparts, and their flat design ensures that food doesn’t spin as you turn them.

Look for memory-wire or adjustable-length skewers for added convenience. If you prefer disposable ones, soak bamboo skewers in water for 30 minutes before grilling to prevent them from burning.

10. Smoker Box for Infusing Flavor

If you love the smoky flavor of BBQ but don’t own a dedicated smoker, a smoker box is the perfect accessory for adding that rich, authentic taste to your food. Fill the box with wood chips—popular varieties include hickory, applewood, and cherry—and place it on the grill. The chips will slowly smolder, releasing aromatic smoke that permeates your meats, vegetables, and seafood.

Smoker boxes are compatible with both gas and charcoal grills and allow you to experiment with an array of wood flavors to discover your favorite combinations.

11. BBQ Sauce Mop and Basting Tools

When it’s time to glaze your ribs or add a final touch of marinade to your chicken, a good basting brush or BBQ mop makes all the difference. Look for brushes with heat-resistant silicone bristles that can handle sticky sauces and high temperatures without melting or shedding.

Traditional cotton or wool mops are also excellent for thin basting liquids like vinegars and broths commonly used in slow-smoking recipes.

12. Wire Grill Brush for Hassle-Free Cleaning

A clean grill is vital for safe and efficient cooking. A sturdy wire grill brush removes stuck-on debris, ensuring your food stays flavorful and free from remnants of past cookouts. Opt for a brush with stainless steel bristles and a long handle to keep your hands away from the heat.

For safety, inspect your brush regularly to ensure no bristles come loose, as these can become hazards if ingested. Alternatively, you can explore bristle-free options that use a coiled metal design for scraping grates.

13. Grill Light for Night Grilling

For evening barbecues that extend past sundown, a grill light is an absolute necessity. These lights clip onto your grill lid or handle, illuminating your cooking area so you can see exactly what you’re doing without a flashlight or phone light. Many models come with adjustable angles and LED bulbs, ensuring maximum visibility and energy efficiency.

Grill lights are especially helpful during the fall and winter months when daylight fades earlier, allowing you to enjoy barbecue year-round.

14. Pizza Stone for Wood-Fired Flavor

A pizza stone is a fantastic way to diversify what you can prepare on your grill. Whether you’re making traditional pizzas, flatbreads, or even cookies, a stone provides an evenly heated surface that mimics a wood-fired oven. Place the stone on the grates, preheat it thoroughly, and enjoy perfectly crisp crusts paired with your favorite toppings.

Choose a stone that fits your grill and have a pizza peel on hand to easily slide pies on and off the grill.

Elevating your barbecue experience doesn’t require a complete overhaul of your equipment—just a few strategic additions can take your grilling adventures to the next level. With these versatile and functional accessories, you’ll be better equipped to impress your family and friends with bold flavors, perfect textures, and unforgettable meals every time the grill is fired up. So, stock up on these essentials and get ready to elevate your BBQ game like never before!
