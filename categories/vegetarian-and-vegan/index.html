Blend on high speed until all the ingredients are well combined and you have a smooth and creamy consistency. Garnish with a handful of fresh blueberries or a sprinkle of cinnamon on top, if desired. In this blog post, I’ll share some mouthwatering recipes for vegan chocolate desserts that are sure to satisfy your cravings and impress even the most skeptical non-vegan friends. Vegan Chocolate Mousse Ingredients: 1 can of full-fat coconut milk 1/2 cup of dairy-free dark chocolate chips 2 tablespoons of maple syrup 1 teaspoon of vanilla extract Pinch of salt Instructions: Chill the can of coconut milk in the refrigerator overnight. Open the can and scoop out the solidified coconut cream into a mixing bowl, leaving the coconut water behind. In a small saucepan, melt the dark chocolate chips over low heat, stirring constantly. Once melted, remove the saucepan from the heat and let it cool for a few minutes. Add the melted chocolate, maple syrup, vanilla extract, and salt to the coconut cream. Use a hand mixer to whip the mixture until smooth and creamy. Divide the mousse into serving glasses and refrigerate for at least 2 hours before serving. Garnish with fresh berries, shaved chocolate, or a dollop of coconut whipped cream.
