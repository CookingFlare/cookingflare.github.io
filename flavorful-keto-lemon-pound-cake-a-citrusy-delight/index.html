Flavorful Keto Lemon Pound Cake: A Citrusy Delight


Are you craving a delicious dessert that won’t derail your keto diet? Look no further than this Flavorful Keto Lemon Pound Cake recipe. Bursting with tangy lemon flavor and perfectly moist, this cake will satisfy your sweet tooth while keeping you on track with your low-carb lifestyle. Plus, it’s incredibly easy to make! So, let’s get started.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups almond flour
  • 1/2 cup coconut flour
  • 1/4 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1/4 cup coconut oil, melted
  • 4 large eggs
  • 1 cup granulated erythritol (or your preferred keto-friendly sweetener)
  • 1/4 cup fresh lemon juice
  • 2 tablespoons lemon zest
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 cup unsweetened almond milk

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 325°F (165°C) and grease a loaf pan with butter or cooking spray.

  2. In a large mixing bowl, combine the almond flour, coconut flour, baking powder, and salt. Mix well to ensure the dry ingredients are evenly distributed.

  3. In a separate bowl, beat the softened butter, melted coconut oil, and granulated erythritol together until creamy and well combined.

  4. Add the eggs, one at a time, to the butter mixture, beating well after each addition. This will ensure a smooth and airy texture for your pound cake.

  5. Stir in the fresh lemon juice, lemon zest, and unsweetened almond milk. Mix until all the wet ingredients are fully incorporated.

  6. Gradually add the dry ingredient mixture to the wet ingredients, stirring gently until a thick batter forms. Be careful not to overmix, as this can lead to a dense cake.

  7. Pour the batter into the greased loaf pan, smoothing the top with a spatula to create an even surface.

  8. Bake the pound cake in the preheated oven for 50-60 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.

  9. Once baked, remove the cake from the oven and let it cool in the pan for 10 minutes. Then, transfer it to a wire rack to cool completely.

  10. Once cooled, slice and serve your Flavorful Keto Lemon Pound Cake as is, or dust it with powdered erythritol for an extra touch of sweetness.

Tips and Variations

  • For a more intense lemon flavor, you can add a few drops of lemon extract to the batter.
  • Feel free to experiment with different keto-friendly sweeteners to suit your taste preferences. Just make sure to adjust the measurements accordingly.
  • To add a touch of elegance, serve your pound cake with a dollop of whipped cream and a sprinkle of fresh lemon zest.

Enjoy this delightful keto-friendly treat as a dessert or a snack any time of the day. The tangy lemon flavor combined with the moist texture of the pound cake will surely leave you craving more. Plus, with its low-carb profile, you can indulge guilt-free! So, grab your ingredients and get baking. Your taste buds will thank you!

Note: This recipe yields approximately 12 servings.
