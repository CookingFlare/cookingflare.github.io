Satisfying Thai Basil Tofu Stir Fry: A Spicy Vegan Option


If you’re looking for a flavorful, plant-based dish that packs a punch, then this Satisfying Thai Basil Tofu Stir Fry is the perfect recipe for you. This spicy vegan option is a delightful combination of tender tofu, crisp vegetables, and aromatic Thai basil, all stir-fried to perfection. Whether you’re a seasoned chef or a novice in the kitchen, this recipe is sure to impress your taste buds and satisfy your craving for bold flavors.

Ingredients

For the Sauce:

  • 3 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons hoisin sauce
  • 1 tablespoon rice vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon maple syrup
  • 1 tablespoon sriracha sauce
  • 1 teaspoon cornstarch
  • 1/4 cup vegetable broth

For the Stir Fry:

  • 14 oz (400g) firm tofu, drained and cubed
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons cornstarch
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 red chili, thinly sliced
  • 1 bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 onion, sliced
  • 1 cup snow peas
  • 1 cup Thai basil leaves
  • Cooked rice, for serving

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Tofu: Start by pressing the tofu to remove excess moisture. Once drained, cut the tofu into cubes and toss them in 2 tablespoons of soy sauce. Then, coat the tofu cubes in 2 tablespoons of cornstarch.

  2. Make the Sauce: In a small bowl, whisk together the soy sauce, hoisin sauce, rice vinegar, maple syrup, sriracha sauce, cornstarch, and vegetable broth. Set the sauce aside for later use.

  3. Stir Fry: Heat 2 tablespoons of vegetable oil in a large pan or wok over medium-high heat. Add the tofu cubes and cook until they are golden and crispy. Remove the tofu from the pan and set it aside.

  4. In the same pan, add the minced garlic and sliced red chili. Sauté for a minute until fragrant.

  5. Add the sliced bell pepper, onion, and snow peas to the pan. Stir-fry the vegetables until they are tender-crisp.

  6. Return the crispy tofu to the pan and pour the prepared sauce over the tofu and vegetables. Stir well to coat everything with the sauce.

  7. Once the sauce has thickened and coats the tofu and vegetables evenly, add the Thai basil leaves to the pan. Toss everything together until the basil leaves are wilted.

  8. Serve: Remove the pan from the heat and serve the Thai Basil Tofu Stir Fry over cooked rice.

Tips and Variations

  • To make this dish gluten-free, use tamari instead of soy sauce.
  • Feel free to add other vegetables such as broccoli, carrots, or mushrooms for extra texture and flavor.
  • If you prefer a milder version, you can reduce the amount of chili or omit it altogether.

Final Thoughts

This Satisfying Thai Basil Tofu Stir Fry is a wonderful way to incorporate bold flavors and wholesome ingredients into your vegan cooking repertoire. The combination of crispy tofu, vibrant vegetables, and fragrant Thai basil creates a harmonious dish that is both satisfying and nutritious. Whether you’re a vegan enthusiast or simply looking to expand your culinary horizons, this recipe is a must-try for anyone who appreciates the vibrant and spicy flavors of Thai cuisine. Enjoy this delightful stir fry as a standalone meal or as part of a spread in a Thai-inspired feast.
