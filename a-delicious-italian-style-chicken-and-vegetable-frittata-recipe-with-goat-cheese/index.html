A Delicious Italian-Style Chicken and Vegetable Frittata Recipe with Goat Cheese


Are you in the mood for a hearty and flavorful dish that is both easy to make and bursting with Italian flavors? Look no further than this delicious Italian-style chicken and vegetable frittata recipe with goat cheese. Frittatas are a versatile and satisfying dish that can be enjoyed for breakfast, lunch, or dinner. They are also a great way to use up leftover ingredients and add a touch of elegance to any meal. So, let’s dive into this mouthwatering recipe!

Ingredients

  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1 red bell pepper, diced
  • 1 yellow bell pepper, diced
  • 1 zucchini, diced
  • 1 small onion, diced
  • 6 large eggs
  • 1/4 cup milk
  • 1/2 cup crumbled goat cheese
  • 2 tablespoons chopped fresh basil
  • 2 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Olive oil for cooking

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease a 9-inch oven-safe skillet or baking dish with olive oil.

  2. Season the chicken breasts with salt and pepper. Heat a tablespoon of olive oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat. Cook the chicken until it is cooked through, about 6-8 minutes per side. Remove the chicken from the skillet and let it cool. Once cooled, shred the chicken into bite-sized pieces.

  3. In the same skillet, add another tablespoon of olive oil and sauté the diced bell peppers, zucchini, and onion over medium heat until they are tender, about 5-6 minutes. Remove from heat and set aside.

  4. In a large bowl, whisk together the eggs and milk until well combined. Stir in the shredded chicken, sautéed vegetables, crumbled goat cheese, chopped basil, and parsley. Season with salt and pepper to taste.

  5. Pour the egg mixture into the greased skillet or baking dish, making sure the ingredients are evenly distributed. Gently shake the skillet or dish to help the mixture settle.

  6. Place the skillet or baking dish in the preheated oven and bake for 25-30 minutes, or until the frittata is set and the top is golden brown.

  7. Once cooked, remove the frittata from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes. Slice it into wedges and serve warm.

Serving Suggestions

This Italian-style chicken and vegetable frittata pairs well with a fresh green salad or a side of roasted potatoes. It can also be enjoyed on its own as a satisfying and nutritious meal. The creamy goat cheese adds a tangy richness to the dish, while the combination of chicken and vegetables provides a delightful burst of flavors.

Conclusion

This delicious Italian-style chicken and vegetable frittata recipe with goat cheese is the perfect dish to add to your repertoire. It’s simple to make, packed with wholesome ingredients, and guaranteed to satisfy even the pickiest of eaters. Whether you serve it for breakfast, lunch, or dinner, this frittata is sure to become a new family favorite. So, gather your ingredients, preheat your oven, and get ready to enjoy a taste of Italy right in your own kitchen!
