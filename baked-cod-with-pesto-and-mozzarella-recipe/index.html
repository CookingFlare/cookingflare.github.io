Baked Cod with Pesto and Mozzarella Recipe


When it comes to preparing a delicious and healthy meal, baked cod with pesto and mozzarella is a winning combination. This recipe is a wonderful way to enjoy the delicate flavor of cod, complemented by the rich taste of pesto and the gooey goodness of melted mozzarella. Whether you’re cooking for a special occasion or simply craving a flavorful dish, this recipe is sure to impress.

Ingredients

To begin, gather the following ingredients:

  • 4 cod fillets
  • 1 cup of basil pesto
  • 1 cup of cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1 cup of shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 2 tablespoons of olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh basil leaves for garnish

Instructions

Step 1: Preparing the Cod

Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C). Place the cod fillets on a lined baking sheet and drizzle them with olive oil. Season the fillets with salt and pepper, ensuring that they are evenly coated.

Step 2: Adding the Pesto

Next, spread a generous layer of basil pesto over each cod fillet. The vibrant green color of the pesto will not only add visual appeal but also infuse the fish with a burst of flavor.

Step 3: Topping with Mozzarella and Tomatoes

Sprinkle the shredded mozzarella over the pesto-covered cod fillets, making sure to cover them completely. Then, scatter the halved cherry tomatoes on top of the mozzarella. The tomatoes will add a touch of sweetness and acidity to balance the richness of the dish.

Step 4: Baking the Cod

Place the baking sheet in the preheated oven and bake the cod for 15-20 minutes, or until the fish is opaque and flakes easily with a fork. The mozzarella should be melted and bubbly, creating a golden crust on top of the cod.

Step 5: Serving

Once the cod is baked to perfection, remove it from the oven and allow it to cool for a few minutes. Garnish the fillets with fresh basil leaves for a pop of color and an extra hint of herbal freshness.

Serving Suggestions

This delectable baked cod with pesto and mozzarella pairs wonderfully with a side of fluffy quinoa or a vibrant mixed green salad. The mild, buttery flavor of the cod allows the pesto and mozzarella to shine, making it a versatile dish that can be complemented by a variety of sides.

Final Thoughts

Baked cod with pesto and mozzarella is an elegant yet straightforward dish that is perfect for both weeknight dinners and special gatherings. The combination of flaky cod, zesty pesto, and creamy mozzarella creates a symphony of flavors that will leave your taste buds singing. This recipe is a fantastic way to incorporate more seafood into your diet while treating yourself to a truly indulgent meal.

So, the next time you’re in the mood for a culinary adventure, consider preparing this delightful baked cod dish. Your family and friends are sure to be impressed by the sophisticated flavors and stunning presentation. Enjoy!
