Baked Apples with Cinnamon and Honey


Apple season is here, and what better way to enjoy this delightful fruit than with a simple yet scrumptious baked apples recipe? This mouthwatering dessert is perfect for warm, cozy evenings, and can be easily prepared with ingredients found in any kitchen. In this culinary journey, we will master the magic of transforming ordinary apples into a divine treat, all thanks to the wondrous combination of cinnamon and honey.

Ingredients

  • 6 medium-sized tart apples
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 2 tablespoons honey
  • Quarter teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • Optional: vanilla extract, whipped cream, or cinnamon ice cream for serving

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Rinse and dry your apples thoroughly, ensuring the skins are spotless. Pat them dry with a clean kitchen towel or paper towels. This step is essential for obtaining a perfectly crisp texture in your baked apples.
  3. Prepare a small bowl for the filling. In it, combine honey and cinnamon, mixing well until a thick, fragrant paste forms.
  4. Using an apple corer or a sharp knife, carefully cut a core out of each apple, removing the center but leaving the bottom to keep the apples upright. Be gentle and try not to slice all the way through the bottom; this will help maintain the shape and prevent a messy oven.
  5. Fill each apple’s core with the honey-cinnamon filling, packing it tightly to maximize flavor.
  6. Melt the butter in a small bowl. Brush each apple’s top and sides with the melted butter, ensuring that the entire outer surface is covered.
  7. Place the apples in a baking dish or on a baking sheet to prevent leakage and to promote even heat distribution. Fill the dish with approximately ½ inch (1.3 cm) of water, as the apples may release some moisture while baking.
  8. Transfer the baking dish to your preheated oven for approximately 35-40 minutes. The apples will be done when a fork can easily slide into their core and the filling appears bubbling and caramelized.
  9. Remove your delightful baked apples from the oven and let them rest briefly to solidify the filling.
  10. Serve your apples as is or with an additional drizzle of honey, a sprinkle of ground cinnamon, a dollop of whipped cream, or a scoop of cinnamon ice cream. Make it a family affair by adding your favorite toppings or experimenting with other flavors like caramel or nutmeg!

Tips and Tricks

  • Tailor the sweetness. Opt for sweeter apples if you prefer more natural flavors and less honey. Conversely, choose tarter apples for a zestier dish if you prefer reduced sweetness.
  • Adding a dash of vanilla extract to the filling create a depth of flavor that beautifully complements the spices.
  • Making baked apples in advance? Store your apples uncovered in the refrigerator to prevent over-sogginess and retain a lovely crunch.
  • For an extra-crisp crust, sprinkle the apples with a bit of sugar before baking.

Embrace the simple elegance of baked apples with cinnamon and honey, a perfect union of flavor that will elevate your culinary game and warm your heart.
