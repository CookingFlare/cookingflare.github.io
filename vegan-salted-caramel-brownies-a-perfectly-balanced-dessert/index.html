Vegan Salted Caramel Brownies: A Perfectly Balanced Dessert


If you’re a fan of decadent desserts but are looking for a vegan option that doesn’t compromise on flavor, then these Vegan Salted Caramel Brownies are just what you need. Rich, fudgy, and perfectly balanced with a hint of salted caramel, these brownies are a delightful treat for any occasion. Whether you’re a committed vegan or simply looking to explore plant-based baking, this recipe is sure to become a staple in your dessert repertoire.

Ingredients

For the Brownie Base:

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1 cup coconut sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup unsweetened applesauce
  • 1/4 cup coconut oil, melted
  • 1/4 cup almond milk
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

For the Salted Caramel Swirl:

  • 1/2 cup coconut cream
  • 1/2 cup coconut sugar
  • 2 tablespoons coconut oil
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/2 teaspoon sea salt

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C) and line a square baking dish with parchment paper.

  2. In a large mixing bowl, whisk together the flour, cocoa powder, coconut sugar, baking powder, and salt until well combined.

  3. In a separate bowl, combine the applesauce, melted coconut oil, almond milk, and vanilla extract. Mix until smooth.

  4. Gradually add the wet ingredients to the dry ingredients, stirring until a thick, smooth batter forms.

  5. In a small saucepan over medium heat, combine the coconut cream, coconut sugar, and coconut oil for the salted caramel swirl. Bring the mixture to a gentle boil, then reduce the heat and simmer for 5-7 minutes, stirring constantly, until the caramel thickens. Remove from heat and stir in the vanilla extract and sea salt.

  6. Pour half of the brownie batter into the prepared baking dish, then drizzle half of the salted caramel over the top. Use a knife to gently swirl the caramel into the batter. Repeat with the remaining batter and caramel.

  7. Bake the brownies for 25-30 minutes, or until the edges are set and a toothpick inserted into the center comes out with moist crumbs.

  8. Allow the brownies to cool in the pan for at least 15 minutes before transferring to a wire rack to cool completely.

  9. Once cooled, slice the brownies into squares and serve.

Tips and Variations

  • For an extra indulgent touch, sprinkle a pinch of flaky sea salt over the top of the brownies before baking.

  • Feel free to add chopped nuts or dairy-free chocolate chips to the brownie batter for added texture and flavor.

  • Store any leftover brownies in an airtight container at room temperature for up to 3 days, or in the refrigerator for up to a week.

Final Thoughts

These Vegan Salted Caramel Brownies are a testament to the fact that plant-based desserts can be just as indulgent and satisfying as their traditional counterparts. The combination of rich, chocolatey brownie and luscious salted caramel creates a perfectly balanced flavor profile that will leave you coming back for more. Whether you’re baking for a special occasion or simply craving a sweet treat, give this recipe a try and experience the magic of vegan baking at its finest.
