Wholesome Gluten-Free Oatmeal Cookies: A Chewy Delight


Oatmeal cookies are a timeless classic, loved by many for their chewy texture and comforting flavors. However, for those who follow a gluten-free diet, enjoying these treats can be a bit challenging. That’s why I’m excited to share with you my recipe for Wholesome Gluten-Free Oatmeal Cookies that are not only delicious but also easy to make. Get ready to indulge in a chewy delight!

Ingredients

  • 1 ½ cups gluten-free rolled oats
  • 1 cup gluten-free flour blend
  • ½ teaspoon baking soda
  • ½ teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • ¼ teaspoon salt
  • ½ cup unsalted butter, softened
  • ½ cup coconut sugar
  • ¼ cup pure maple syrup
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 large egg
  • ½ cup raisins
  • ½ cup chopped walnuts (optional)

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C) and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.

  2. In a medium-sized bowl, whisk together the gluten-free rolled oats, gluten-free flour blend, baking soda, ground cinnamon, and salt. Set aside.

  3. In a separate large bowl, cream together the softened unsalted butter, coconut sugar, pure maple syrup, and vanilla extract until light and fluffy. You can use an electric mixer or do this by hand.

  4. Add the egg to the butter-sugar mixture and beat until well combined.

  5. Gradually add the dry ingredients to the wet ingredients, mixing until just incorporated. Be careful not to overmix as this can result in tough cookies.

  6. Fold in the raisins and chopped walnuts (if using) until evenly distributed throughout the dough.

  7. Using a cookie scoop or tablespoon, drop rounded portions of dough onto the prepared baking sheet, spacing them about 2 inches apart. Flatten each portion slightly with the back of a spoon or your fingertips.

  8. Bake the cookies for 10-12 minutes or until golden brown around the edges. The centers may still be slightly soft, but they will firm up as they cool.

  9. Remove the baking sheet from the oven and let the cookies cool on the sheet for 5 minutes. Then, transfer them to a wire rack to cool completely.

  10. Once cooled, store the Wholesome Gluten-Free Oatmeal Cookies in an airtight container at room temperature for up to one week. Enjoy them with a glass of milk or a cup of tea for a delightful treat any time of the day!

Final Thoughts

These Wholesome Gluten-Free Oatmeal Cookies are a true delight for anyone who craves a chewy and flavorful treat. The combination of gluten-free rolled oats, warm cinnamon, sweet raisins, and crunchy walnuts (if added) creates a perfect balance of flavors and textures. Plus, they are incredibly easy to make, allowing you to satisfy your cravings in no time.

Whether you follow a gluten-free diet or simply want to try something new, these cookies are a must-try. Share them with your loved ones, bring them to potlucks, or enjoy them as a sweet snack whenever you need a pick-me-up. Wholesome and delicious, these cookies will surely become a favorite in your household!

So, what are you waiting for? Gather your ingredients, preheat your oven, and let’s bake some Wholesome Gluten-Free Oatmeal Cookies together. Get ready to savor the chewy delight that awaits you!
