Vegan Caramel Apple Crisp: A Perfect Fall Dessert


When the leaves start to turn vibrant shades of red and gold, and there’s a slight chill in the air, it can only mean one thing - fall is here! And what better way to celebrate the season than with a warm, comforting dessert like Vegan Caramel Apple Crisp? This delectable treat combines the sweetness of caramel, the tartness of apples, and the crunch of a crisp topping, all without using any animal products. Whether you’re a vegan, looking to reduce your dairy consumption, or simply a lover of delicious desserts, this recipe is sure to become a staple in your fall baking repertoire.

Ingredients

For the Apple Filling:

  • 6 medium apples, peeled, cored, and sliced
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground nutmeg
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt

For the Caramel Sauce:

  • 1 cup coconut milk
  • 1 cup brown sugar
  • 1/4 cup vegan butter
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt

For the Crisp Topping:

  • 1 cup old-fashioned oats
  • 1/2 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup brown sugar
  • 1/4 cup chopped pecans
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup vegan butter, melted

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C) and lightly grease a 9x13-inch baking dish.

  2. In a large bowl, combine the sliced apples, granulated sugar, flour, cinnamon, nutmeg, and salt. Toss until the apples are evenly coated, then transfer the mixture to the prepared baking dish, spreading it out into an even layer.

  3. In a saucepan over medium heat, combine the coconut milk, brown sugar, vegan butter, vanilla extract, and salt for the caramel sauce. Bring the mixture to a gentle boil, then reduce the heat and simmer for 5-7 minutes, stirring occasionally, until the sauce has thickened slightly. Remove from heat and let it cool for a few minutes.

  4. Pour the caramel sauce over the apple mixture in the baking dish, ensuring it’s evenly distributed.

  5. In a separate bowl, combine the oats, flour, brown sugar, chopped pecans, cinnamon, and salt for the crisp topping. Stir in the melted vegan butter until the mixture is crumbly.

  6. Sprinkle the crisp topping evenly over the caramel-coated apples.

  7. Bake in the preheated oven for 40-45 minutes, or until the topping is golden brown, and the apples are tender.

  8. Remove from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes before serving.

Serving Suggestions

This Vegan Caramel Apple Crisp is best enjoyed warm, either on its own or topped with a scoop of dairy-free vanilla ice cream or a dollop of coconut whipped cream. The contrast of the warm, gooey caramel with the cold, creamy topping is simply irresistible.

Conclusion

With its aromatic spices, luscious caramel, and tender apples, this Vegan Caramel Apple Crisp is the epitome of fall comfort food. Whether you’re hosting a dinner party or craving a cozy night in, this dessert is guaranteed to impress. The best part? It’s entirely vegan, so everyone can indulge in its delightful flavors without compromising their dietary choices. So, when the crisp autumn air beckons, whip up a batch of this irresistible dessert and savor the flavors of the season.
