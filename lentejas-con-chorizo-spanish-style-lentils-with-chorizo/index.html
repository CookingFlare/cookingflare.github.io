Lentejas con Chorizo: Spanish-Style Lentils with Chorizo


There’s something deeply comforting and satisfying about a steaming bowl of lentejas con chorizo, a traditional Spanish dish that brings together the earthy flavors of lentils with the smoky, spicy goodness of chorizo. This hearty, nutritious, and flavorful meal is a staple in Spanish households, and for good reason. Whether you’re looking for a wholesome weeknight dinner or a dish to impress your guests, lentejas con chorizo is sure to hit the spot.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup dried green or brown lentils
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 carrots, diced
  • 2 stalks celery, diced
  • 8 oz Spanish chorizo, sliced
  • 1 teaspoon smoked paprika
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 4 cups chicken or vegetable broth
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley for garnish
  • Crusty bread for serving

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Lentils: Start by rinsing the lentils under cold water and picking out any debris. Set them aside.

  2. Sauté the Aromatics: In a large pot or Dutch oven, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the chopped onion, minced garlic, diced carrots, and celery. Sauté for about 5 minutes until the vegetables begin to soften and the onion turns translucent.

  3. Add the Chorizo: Push the sautéed vegetables to the side of the pot and add the sliced chorizo. Let it cook for a few minutes until it releases its flavorful oils and starts to brown.

  4. Infuse with Flavor: Stir in the smoked paprika and bay leaf, allowing the spices to bloom and fill the air with their aromatic essence.

  5. Bring It All Together: Add the rinsed lentils to the pot, followed by the chicken or vegetable broth. Bring the mixture to a gentle boil, then reduce the heat and let it simmer for about 25-30 minutes, or until the lentils are tender but not mushy.

  6. Season and Serve: Once the lentils are cooked to perfection, season the dish with salt and pepper to taste. Garnish with freshly chopped parsley and serve the lentejas con chorizo hot, accompanied by a slice of crusty bread to soak up the savory broth.

Tips and Tricks

  • For an extra kick of heat, consider adding a pinch of cayenne pepper or a splash of hot sauce to the lentejas con chorizo.
  • If you prefer a smoother texture, you can partially blend the cooked lentils before serving.
  • Leftovers can be refrigerated and reheated the next day, allowing the flavors to meld even further for an even more delicious meal.

The Magic of Lentejas con Chorizo

Lentejas con chorizo is a celebration of simple, wholesome ingredients coming together to create a dish that’s so much more than the sum of its parts. The lentils provide a rich, earthy base, while the chorizo infuses every spoonful with its robust, smoky flavor. The marriage of these elements, along with the aromatic blend of vegetables and spices, results in a dish that warms the soul and satisfies the senses.

In Conclusion

Whether you’re a seasoned cook or just starting your culinary journey, lentejas con chorizo is a recipe that’s both approachable and deeply rewarding. It’s a testament to the beauty of rustic, traditional cooking, where a handful of humble ingredients can be transformed into a meal that brings people together and creates lasting memories. So, gather your ingredients, set a pot on the stove, and let the magic of lentejas con chorizo unfold in your kitchen.
