Grilled Vegetable Pasta Salad: Fresh and Flavorful Fourth of July Side


The Fourth of July is just around the corner, and that means it’s time to fire up the grill and celebrate with some delicious food. One dish that never fails to impress is a Grilled Vegetable Pasta Salad. Not only is it bursting with flavor, but it’s also a refreshing and healthy addition to any summer spread. In this recipe, we’ll show you how to create a mouthwatering pasta salad that will be the star of your Fourth of July barbecue.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound of your favorite pasta (penne or fusilli work well)
  • 2 cups of mixed vegetables (such as bell peppers, zucchini, and red onion)
  • 1/4 cup of olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons of balsamic vinegar
  • 1 clove of garlic, minced
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 cup of cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1/2 cup of crumbled feta cheese
  • Fresh basil leaves, torn

Instructions

  1. Preheat your grill to medium-high heat. While the grill is heating up, cook the pasta according to the package instructions. Once cooked, drain and set aside.

  2. In a large mixing bowl, combine the mixed vegetables, olive oil, balsamic vinegar, minced garlic, salt, and pepper. Toss until the vegetables are well coated.

  3. Place the vegetables on the grill and cook for about 5-7 minutes, or until they are slightly charred and tender. Make sure to turn them occasionally to ensure even cooking.

  4. Once the vegetables are grilled to perfection, remove them from the grill and let them cool slightly. Then, chop them into bite-sized pieces.

  5. In a large serving bowl, combine the cooked pasta, grilled vegetables, cherry tomatoes, crumbled feta cheese, and torn basil leaves. Toss gently to combine all the ingredients.

  6. Serve the Grilled Vegetable Pasta Salad immediately, or refrigerate for a couple of hours to let the flavors meld together. This salad tastes great both chilled or at room temperature.

  7. Enjoy your Fresh and Flavorful Fourth of July side dish!

Tips and Variations

  • Feel free to customize this recipe by adding or substituting your favorite grilled vegetables. Asparagus, eggplant, and mushrooms are all great options.
  • If you don’t have a grill, you can achieve similar results by roasting the vegetables in the oven at 425°F for about 15-20 minutes.
  • For added protein, you can toss in some grilled chicken or shrimp.
  • If you prefer a creamier salad, you can mix in some mayonnaise or Greek yogurt.
  • Don’t forget to season your pasta cooking water with salt to enhance the overall flavor of the dish.

Conclusion

Grilled Vegetable Pasta Salad is a fantastic addition to any Fourth of July celebration. Its vibrant colors, fresh flavors, and healthy ingredients make it the perfect side dish to accompany your grilled meats and burgers. Whether you’re hosting a backyard barbecue or attending a potluck, this salad is sure to impress your friends and family. So fire up that grill, gather your favorite vegetables, and get ready to savor the taste of summer with this Fresh and Flavorful Fourth of July side.
