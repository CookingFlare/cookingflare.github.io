Fresh Catch: Seafood Recipes to Enjoy in the Spring


As the weather warms up and the days become longer, there’s no better time to indulge in the fresh and vibrant flavors of seafood. Spring brings an abundance of delicious catches, from succulent shrimp to delicate fish, offering a plethora of opportunities to create mouthwatering dishes that celebrate the season. Whether you’re a seafood enthusiast or looking to explore new culinary horizons, these seafood recipes are sure to delight your taste buds and bring a taste of the ocean to your table.

Grilled Lemon Garlic Shrimp Skewers

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound of large shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • Zest and juice of 2 lemons
  • 2 tablespoons of olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon of dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Wooden skewers, soaked in water

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, combine the minced garlic, lemon zest, lemon juice, olive oil, oregano, salt, and pepper.
  2. Add the shrimp to the marinade, ensuring they are well coated. Allow the shrimp to marinate for 20-30 minutes.
  3. Preheat the grill to medium-high heat.
  4. Thread the marinated shrimp onto the soaked skewers.
  5. Grill the shrimp for 2-3 minutes on each side, or until they are pink and opaque.
  6. Serve the shrimp skewers with a squeeze of fresh lemon juice and a sprinkle of chopped parsley.

Citrusy Baked Cod with Asparagus

Ingredients:

  • 4 cod fillets
  • 1 lemon, thinly sliced
  • 1 orange, thinly sliced
  • 1 bunch of asparagus, trimmed
  • 3 tablespoons of olive oil
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh dill for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 400°F (200°C).
  2. Place the cod fillets in a baking dish and season with salt and pepper.
  3. Arrange the lemon and orange slices on top of the cod fillets.
  4. In a bowl, toss the asparagus with olive oil, minced garlic, salt, and pepper.
  5. Arrange the asparagus around the cod in the baking dish.
  6. Bake for 15-20 minutes, or until the fish is cooked through and the asparagus is tender.
  7. Garnish with fresh dill before serving.

Spring Pea and Mint Risotto with Seared Scallops

Ingredients:

  • 1 1/2 cups of Arborio rice
  • 4 cups of vegetable or seafood broth
  • 1 cup of fresh or frozen peas
  • 1/4 cup of fresh mint leaves, chopped
  • 1/2 cup of grated Parmesan cheese
  • 12 large sea scallops
  • 2 tablespoons of butter
  • 2 tablespoons of olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Lemon wedges for serving

Instructions:

  1. In a large pot, bring the broth to a simmer.
  2. In a separate pan, heat 1 tablespoon of butter and 1 tablespoon of olive oil over medium heat.
  3. Add the scallops to the pan and sear for 2-3 minutes on each side, or until they are golden brown and opaque. Set aside.
  4. In the same pan, add the remaining butter and olive oil. Add the Arborio rice and cook for 1-2 minutes, stirring constantly.
  5. Ladle in a spoonful of the hot broth and stir until the liquid is absorbed. Continue adding the broth, stirring frequently, until the rice is creamy and al dente.
  6. Stir in the peas, mint, and Parmesan cheese. Season with salt and pepper.
  7. Divide the risotto among serving plates and top each portion with seared scallops.
  8. Serve with a wedge of lemon for a burst of freshness.

Conclusion

With the arrival of spring, the bounty of the sea comes alive, offering an array of delectable seafood options to elevate your culinary repertoire. These recipes are designed to capture the essence of the season, with bright and refreshing flavors that perfectly complement the freshness of spring. Embrace the abundance of the ocean and savor the delights of seafood in the comfort of your own kitchen. Whether you’re hosting a gathering or simply treating yourself to a special meal, these seafood recipes are a delightful way to welcome the flavors of spring into your home.
