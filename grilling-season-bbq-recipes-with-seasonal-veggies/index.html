Grilling Season: BBQ Recipes with Seasonal Veggies


Summer is the perfect time to fire up the grill and indulge in some delicious barbeque recipes. And what better way to make your grilling sessions even more exciting than by incorporating seasonal veggies into your BBQ dishes? Not only do they add a burst of freshness and vibrant colors to your plate, but they also provide essential nutrients to keep you feeling healthy and satisfied. In this blog post, we’ll explore some mouthwatering BBQ recipes that highlight the best of seasonal veggies. So, let’s get grilling!

Grilled Vegetable Skewers

Skewers are a classic grilling staple, and they offer a fantastic opportunity to showcase the flavors of seasonal veggies. Start by selecting a variety of colorful vegetables such as bell peppers, zucchini, cherry tomatoes, red onions, and mushrooms. Cut them into bite-sized pieces and thread them onto skewers. Drizzle with olive oil, sprinkle with salt and pepper, and add a pinch of your favorite herbs and spices. Grill the skewers over medium heat until the veggies are tender and slightly charred. Serve them as a delightful appetizer or side dish at your next BBQ gathering.

Grilled Stuffed Peppers

Stuffed peppers are a crowd-pleasing favorite, and they can be easily adapted to include seasonal veggies. Begin by hollowing out bell peppers and removing the seeds. Fill each pepper with a mixture of cooked quinoa, sautéed onions, corn kernels, and diced zucchini. Top with a sprinkle of cheese and place the stuffed peppers on the grill. Cook until the peppers are tender and the cheese has melted to perfection. These grilled stuffed peppers make a satisfying main course for vegetarian guests or can be served as a delicious side dish alongside grilled meats.

Grilled Veggie Pizza

Who doesn’t love pizza? Take your pizza game to the next level by grilling your pizza dough and topping it with a medley of seasonal veggies. Start by rolling out your favorite pizza dough and brush it with olive oil. Place the dough directly on the grill and cook until it becomes golden brown and crispy. Remove the dough from the grill and spread a layer of tomato sauce or pesto. Top with a mix of grilled veggies such as eggplant, asparagus, and artichoke hearts. Sprinkle with cheese and return the pizza to the grill until the cheese has melted and the veggies are tender. Slice it up and enjoy a unique and delicious grilled veggie pizza!

Grilled Corn on the Cob with Herb Butter

No BBQ is complete without some juicy and flavorful grilled corn on the cob. To elevate this classic side dish, prepare a delicious herb butter. Mix softened butter with freshly chopped herbs like basil, parsley, and chives. Add a squeeze of lemon juice, salt, and pepper, and blend well. Spread the herb butter generously over the corn cobs and wrap them in aluminum foil. Grill the corn on medium heat for about 15 minutes, turning occasionally, until the kernels are tender and slightly charred. Unwrap and serve the corn on the cob with a sprinkle of additional herbs for an extra burst of flavor.

Grilled Veggie Burger

For all the burger lovers out there, a grilled veggie burger is a must-try recipe. Create your own veggie burger patties using a combination of grated seasonal veggies such as carrots, zucchini, and sweet potatoes. Mix in cooked quinoa, breadcrumbs, and your choice of spices. Form the mixture into patties and grill them until they develop a nice char. Serve these delicious veggie burgers on toasted buns with your favorite toppings and condiments. They are an excellent alternative to traditional meat burgers and will surely impress your guests with their flavor and texture.

Conclusion

Embrace the grilling season by incorporating the vibrant flavors of seasonal veggies into your BBQ recipes. From grilled vegetable skewers to stuffed peppers, there are endless possibilities to explore. Get creative with your favorite veggies, experiment with different herbs and spices, and don’t forget to enjoy the smoky and delicious results. So, fire up your grill, gather your friends and family, and savor the taste of summer with these fantastic BBQ recipes. Happy grilling!

Remember to always make sure your grill is clean, properly heated, and you follow safe grilling practices to ensure a successful and enjoyable grilling experience.
