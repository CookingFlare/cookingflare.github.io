Gooey Chocolate Chip Cookie Recipe for Every Occasion


Who doesn’t love a warm, gooey chocolate chip cookie straight from the oven? Whether you’re celebrating a special occasion or simply craving a sweet treat, this recipe is sure to satisfy your cookie cravings. With its soft and chewy texture, melty chocolate chips, and a hint of vanilla, these cookies are perfect for any occasion. So, let’s dive right into the recipe!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 cup brown sugar, packed
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 2 cups chocolate chips

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C) and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.

  2. In a large mixing bowl, cream together the softened butter, granulated sugar, and brown sugar until light and fluffy. This should take about 2-3 minutes.

  3. Add the eggs one at a time, beating well after each addition. Stir in the vanilla extract.

  4. In a separate bowl, whisk together the flour, baking soda, and salt. Gradually add the dry ingredients to the wet ingredients, mixing until just combined. Be careful not to overmix, as this can result in tough cookies.

  5. Fold in the chocolate chips until evenly distributed throughout the dough. For an extra gooey texture, you can reserve a handful of chocolate chips to press onto the top of each cookie before baking.

  6. Using a cookie scoop or a tablespoon, drop rounded portions of dough onto the prepared baking sheet, spacing them about 2 inches apart.

  7. Bake for 10-12 minutes, or until the edges are lightly golden. The centers may still appear slightly undercooked, but they will firm up as the cookies cool.

  8. Remove from the oven and allow the cookies to cool on the baking sheet for a few minutes before transferring them to a wire rack to cool completely.

  9. Once cooled, enjoy these irresistible, gooey chocolate chip cookies with a glass of cold milk or a scoop of your favorite ice cream. They are perfect for indulging in alone or sharing with loved ones.

Tips and Variations

  • For an extra decadent twist, consider adding chopped nuts, such as walnuts or pecans, to the cookie dough.
  • If you prefer a more pronounced vanilla flavor, you can use a vanilla bean instead of vanilla extract. Simply scrape the seeds from the vanilla bean and add them to the wet ingredients.
  • Don’t have chocolate chips on hand? No problem! You can substitute them with chunks of your favorite chocolate bar or even white chocolate chips for a different flavor profile.
  • For an ooey-gooey treat, slightly underbake the cookies by reducing the baking time by a minute or two. This will result in a softer, more melty center.

So there you have it – a simple and delicious recipe for gooey chocolate chip cookies that are versatile enough for any occasion. Whether you’re baking for a birthday party, a cozy night in, or simply because you deserve a sweet pick-me-up, these cookies are sure to please. So grab your apron, preheat that oven, and get ready to indulge in a batch of homemade goodness. Happy baking!
