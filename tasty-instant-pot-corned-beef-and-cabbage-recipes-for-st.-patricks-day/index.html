Tasty Instant Pot Corned Beef and Cabbage Recipes for St. Patrick's Day


St. Patrick’s Day is just around the corner, and what better way to celebrate than with a delicious and hearty meal? Instant Pot corned beef and cabbage is a classic dish that is not only easy to make but also packed with flavor. In this blog post, we will share with you two mouthwatering recipes that will impress your friends and family.

Recipe 1: Traditional Corned Beef and Cabbage

Ingredients:

  • 3 pounds of corned beef brisket
  • 1 onion, quartered
  • 3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 6 cups of water
  • 1 head of cabbage, cut into wedges
  • 4 carrots, peeled and cut into chunks
  • 6 small red potatoes, halved
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Place the corned beef brisket in the Instant Pot and add the onion, garlic, and water.
  2. Close the lid and set the Instant Pot to high pressure for 90 minutes.
  3. Once the cooking time is complete, allow the pressure to release naturally for 10 minutes, then manually release the remaining pressure.
  4. Remove the corned beef from the Instant Pot and cover it with foil to keep it warm.
  5. Add the cabbage, carrots, and potatoes to the Instant Pot and close the lid.
  6. Set the Instant Pot to high pressure for 5 minutes.
  7. Once the cooking time is complete, manually release the pressure.
  8. Serve the corned beef sliced, alongside the vegetables, and season with salt and pepper to taste.

Recipe 2: Guinness-Glazed Corned Beef and Cabbage

Ingredients:

  • 3 pounds of corned beef brisket
  • 1 onion, quartered
  • 3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 bottle of Guinness beer
  • 1 cup of brown sugar
  • 1 tablespoon of Dijon mustard
  • 1 head of cabbage, cut into wedges
  • 4 carrots, peeled and cut into chunks
  • 6 small red potatoes, halved
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Place the corned beef brisket in the Instant Pot and add the onion, garlic, Guinness beer, brown sugar, and Dijon mustard.
  2. Close the lid and set the Instant Pot to high pressure for 90 minutes.
  3. Once the cooking time is complete, allow the pressure to release naturally for 10 minutes, then manually release the remaining pressure.
  4. Remove the corned beef from the Instant Pot and brush it with the glaze formed in the pot.
  5. Preheat your broiler to high and place the glazed corned beef on a baking sheet.
  6. Broil the corned beef for 5-7 minutes, or until the glaze is caramelized and bubbly.
  7. While the corned beef is broiling, add the cabbage, carrots, and potatoes to the Instant Pot and close the lid.
  8. Set the Instant Pot to high pressure for 5 minutes.
  9. Once the cooking time is complete, manually release the pressure.
  10. Serve the glazed corned beef sliced, alongside the vegetables, and season with salt and pepper to taste.

Conclusion

These Instant Pot corned beef and cabbage recipes are perfect for St. Patrick’s Day or any day you’re craving a hearty and flavorful meal. Whether you prefer the traditional version or the Guinness-glazed twist, these recipes are sure to delight your taste buds and impress your guests. So don your greenest outfit, pour yourself a pint of stout, and enjoy a delicious feast that celebrates the luck of the Irish!
