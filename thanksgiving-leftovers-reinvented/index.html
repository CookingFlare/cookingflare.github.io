Thanksgiving Leftovers Reinvented


Ever wondered what to do with those Thanksgiving leftovers? Instead of the same old sandwich or soup, why not transform your turkey, stuffing, and vegetables into exciting new dishes? Here are some creative and surprising ways to reinvent your holiday leftovers.

Leftover Turkey Tacos

Preheat your oven or stovetop grill, and get ready for a zesty twist on traditional turkey fare. Shred your leftover turkey and mix it with your favorite taco-seasoned salsa, chopped cilantro, and lime juice. Warm up taco shells or lettuce wraps and fill with the spicy turkey mixture.

Fancy Up Your Leftover Stuffing

Don’t let your stuffing go to waste! Turn it into a savory stuffing frittata or stuffing croquettes. Mix the leftovers with beaten eggs, milk, and a handful of cheese. Pour the mixture into a greased oven-safe dish and bake until golden brown, or form the mixture into bite-sized balls, roll in breadcrumbs, and fry until crispy. Both options are delicious served with a cool cranberry sauce.

Turkey Tetrazzini

The Thanksgiving feast doesn’t have to end when the holiday is over. Transform leftover turkey into a luxurious pasta dish by combining cooked turkey with cooked spaghetti, sautéed mushrooms and onions, a splash of white wine, and cream. Finish the dish with grated Parmesan cheese and a sprinkle of paprika for garnish.

Turkey Pot Pie

Why not make a comforting turkey pot pie? Simply mix your leftover turkey with peas, carrots, and onions, and pour the mixture into a greased pie dish. Top the concoction with a homemade lattice crust and bake until the crust turns golden brown. Serve this cozy meal with a side salad or garlic bread for added flavor.

Cranberry Sips

Give your cranberry sauce a second chance in the spotlight and transform it into a refreshing cocktail! Combine cranberry sauce with a dash of orange juice, vodka, and a handful of ice in a shaker or blender. Strain the mixture into glasses and garnish with orange slices and a sprig of mint.

Thanksgiving Leftovers Salad

Turn your leftover vegetables into a vibrant salad by sautéing them with a touch of olive oil and garlic. Combine the sautéed vegetables with a can of black beans, corn kernels, and toss in a tangy lime-cilantro dressing. Serve as a colorful side or main dish on a bed of greens.

Leftover Turkey Chili

Transform your turkey leftovers into a hearty standalone meal by making turkey chili. Heat up a pot with a bit of oil, add chopped onions, garlic, and your leftover turkey. Stir in crushed tomatoes, chicken stock, and any remaining veggies. Let simmer for a few hours, and enjoy your homemade chili with cornbread or crackers on the side.

Do not let your Thanksgiving leftovers go to waste - transform them into exciting and unique dishes beyond the classic turkey sandwich. Get creative and enjoy your holiday remnants in new ways to delight your taste buds long after the Thanksgiving gathering is over. Enjoy!
