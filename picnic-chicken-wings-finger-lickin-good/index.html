Picnic Chicken Wings: Finger-Lickin' Good


Who doesn’t love a picnic? The sun shining, the grass beneath your feet, and the tantalizing aroma of delicious food wafting through the air. And what better way to enjoy a picnic than with some finger-lickin’ good chicken wings? In this recipe, we’ll show you how to make the perfect picnic chicken wings that will have everyone begging for seconds.

Ingredients

  • 2 pounds of chicken wings
  • 1/2 cup of soy sauce
  • 1/4 cup of honey
  • 3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons of olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon of Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 teaspoon of smoked paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon of black pepper
  • 1/2 teaspoon of salt

Instructions

  1. Start by preheating your grill to medium-high heat. While the grill is heating up, prepare the chicken wings by trimming off any excess fat and separating them into wingettes and drumettes.

  2. In a large bowl, whisk together the soy sauce, honey, minced garlic, olive oil, Worcestershire sauce, smoked paprika, black pepper, and salt. This flavorful marinade will be the secret to our delicious picnic chicken wings.

  3. Add the chicken wings to the marinade, making sure to coat them evenly. Let the wings marinate for at least 30 minutes, allowing the flavors to penetrate the meat.

  4. Once the grill is hot, place the chicken wings on the grill grates, making sure to leave enough space between each wing. This will ensure that they cook evenly and develop a nice char.

  5. Grill the wings for about 12-15 minutes, flipping them halfway through, until they are cooked through and have a beautiful golden brown color. The internal temperature of the wings should reach 165°F (74°C) to ensure they are fully cooked.

  6. Remove the wings from the grill and let them rest for a few minutes before serving. This will allow the juices to redistribute, resulting in tender and juicy chicken.

  7. Serve the picnic chicken wings with your favorite dipping sauces and sides. Classic choices include barbecue sauce, ranch dressing, or even a homemade spicy aioli. Pair them with some fresh coleslaw, corn on the cob, or a simple green salad for a complete picnic feast.

Tips and Tricks

  • For extra flavor, you can add a squeeze of lemon or lime juice to the marinade. This will give the chicken wings a zesty kick.

  • If you prefer a spicier version, you can add a dash of cayenne pepper or hot sauce to the marinade. Adjust the amount according to your heat tolerance.

  • To ensure juicy wings, avoid overcrowding the grill. Cook them in batches if necessary.

  • If you don’t have a grill, you can also bake the wings in the oven at 400°F (200°C) for about 25-30 minutes, flipping them halfway through.

  • Don’t forget to pack some wet wipes or napkins for easy clean-up during your picnic adventure.

So there you have it, a simple yet delicious recipe for picnic chicken wings that will elevate your outdoor dining experience. Whether you’re enjoying a sunny day at the park or heading to the beach, these finger-lickin’ good wings are sure to be a hit. So grab your picnic blanket, gather your loved ones, and get ready to savor the flavors of summer with these mouthwatering chicken wings. Happy picnicking!
