Citrus Mint Cooler: Lemon Lime Mint Mocktail


A Splash of Sunshine, A Touch of Cool: Introducing Citrus Mint Cooler, a lemon lime mint mocktail, a delightful concoction that is as versatile as it is refreshing. A mouthwatering blend of vibrant citrus flavors, combined with the invigorating taste of mint, creates the perfect accompaniment for casual get-togethers, festive gatherings or a relaxing day by the pool.

Ingredients:

  • 4 cups club soda or seltzer water
  • 2 tablespoons lemon juice
  • 2 tablespoons lime juice
  • 2 tablespoons mint syrup
  • 1 cup chopped fresh mint leaves
  • 2 cups slices of fresh lemon and lime
  • 8 dashes of Angostura bitters, optional

Instructions:

  1. In a large pitcher, combine the club soda or seltzer water with lemon juice, lime juice, and mint syrup.
  2. Stir well, ensuring all ingredients are evenly incorporated.
  3. Add the chopped fresh mint leaves and mix gently. Allow the mixture to sit for at least 30 minutes, giving the mint time to release its fragrant oils.
  4. Before serving, add the fresh lemon and lime slices to the pitcher, ensuring they are distributed evenly.
  5. If desired, add a few dashes of Angostura bitters for an extra layer of complexity and depth.
  6. Serve chilled over ice in tall glasses. Garnish with a sprig of fresh mint and a slice of lemon or lime for a visually appealing and enticing presentation.
