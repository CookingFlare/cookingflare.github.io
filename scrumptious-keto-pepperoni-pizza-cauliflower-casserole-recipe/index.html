Scrumptious Keto Pepperoni Pizza Cauliflower Casserole Recipe


If you’re a fan of pizza but looking for a low-carb, keto-friendly alternative, then this Scrumptious Keto Pepperoni Pizza Cauliflower Casserole is the perfect dish for you. Packed with flavor and wholesome ingredients, this recipe is a delightful twist on the classic pepperoni pizza, without the guilt of traditional crust. Let’s dive into the delicious details of this mouthwatering cauliflower casserole.

Ingredients

For the Cauliflower Base:

  • 1 head cauliflower, cut into florets
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste

For the Topping:

  • 1/2 cup sugar-free marinara sauce
  • 1 1/2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 20 slices pepperoni
  • 1 teaspoon Italian seasoning
  • Fresh basil leaves for garnish (optional)

Instructions

  1. Preheat the Oven: Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C) and grease a baking dish with olive oil or cooking spray.

  2. Prepare the Cauliflower: In a large bowl, toss the cauliflower florets with olive oil, garlic powder, salt, and pepper until evenly coated. Spread the cauliflower in a single layer on the prepared baking dish.

  3. Roast the Cauliflower: Roast the cauliflower in the preheated oven for 20-25 minutes, or until it’s tender and slightly golden brown around the edges. Remove from the oven and let it cool slightly.

  4. Assemble the Casserole: Spread the sugar-free marinara sauce over the roasted cauliflower. Sprinkle the shredded mozzarella and grated Parmesan cheese over the sauce. Arrange the pepperoni slices on top and sprinkle with Italian seasoning.

  5. Bake to Perfection: Return the casserole to the oven and bake for an additional 12-15 minutes, or until the cheese is melted and bubbly.

  6. Garnish and Serve: Once out of the oven, let the casserole rest for a few minutes. Garnish with fresh basil leaves if desired, then slice and serve hot.

Nutritional Information

  • Serving Size: 1 slice
  • Calories: 180
  • Total Fat: 12g
  • Saturated Fat: 6g
  • Cholesterol: 35mg
  • Sodium: 430mg
  • Total Carbohydrates: 5g
  • Dietary Fiber: 2g
  • Sugars: 2g
  • Protein: 12g

Why You’ll Love This Recipe

This Scrumptious Keto Pepperoni Pizza Cauliflower Casserole is a game-changer for anyone craving the flavors of pizza while following a keto lifestyle. Here’s why this recipe is a must-try:

Keto-Friendly and Low-Carb:

With cauliflower as the base, this casserole is low in carbs, making it a great option for those following a keto diet. It’s a wonderful way to satisfy pizza cravings without sacrificing your dietary goals.

Easy to Make:

This recipe is simple and straightforward, requiring just a few basic ingredients and minimal prep time. It’s a fantastic option for a quick weeknight dinner or a casual get-together with friends.

Customizable and Versatile:

Feel free to customize the toppings to suit your preferences. Add extra veggies, swap the pepperoni for other meats, or incorporate different cheeses to create your own signature cauliflower casserole.

Family-Friendly:

Final Thoughts

This Scrumptious Keto Pepperoni Pizza Cauliflower Casserole is a delightful combination of savory flavors and wholesome ingredients. Whether you’re a keto enthusiast or simply looking for a delicious, low-carb meal, this recipe is a winner. So, preheat that oven and get ready to savor every bite of this mouthwatering cauliflower casserole!
