Easy Low-Carb Keto Chicken Enchilada Casserole


If you’re looking for a delicious and easy low-carb keto recipe that the whole family will love, then you’ve come to the right place. This Easy Low-Carb Keto Chicken Enchilada Casserole is a flavor-packed dish that’s perfect for weeknight dinners or meal prepping for the week ahead. Packed with protein, healthy fats, and vibrant flavors, this casserole is sure to become a staple in your keto recipe collection.

Ingredients

  • 2 lbs boneless, skinless chicken breasts, cooked and shredded
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 medium onion, diced
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 red bell pepper, diced
  • 1 teaspoon chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1/2 teaspoon smoked paprika
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 1/2 cups enchilada sauce
  • 1/2 cup sour cream
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh cilantro
  • 2 cups shredded Mexican blend cheese
  • 1/2 cup sliced black olives (optional)

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. In a large skillet, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the diced onion and cook until softened, about 5 minutes.
  3. Add the minced garlic and diced red bell pepper to the skillet, and cook for an additional 2-3 minutes.
  4. Stir in the chili powder, cumin, oregano, smoked paprika, salt, and pepper. Cook for another 1-2 minutes until fragrant.
  5. Add the cooked and shredded chicken to the skillet, stirring to combine with the onion and pepper mixture.
  6. Pour in the enchilada sauce and mix well, allowing the flavors to meld together for a few minutes.
  7. Remove the skillet from the heat and stir in the sour cream and chopped cilantro until well combined.
  8. Transfer the chicken mixture to a greased 9x13 inch baking dish, spreading it out evenly.
  9. Sprinkle the shredded cheese over the top of the chicken mixture, covering it completely.
  10. If desired, add the sliced black olives on top of the cheese.
  11. Place the casserole in the preheated oven and bake for 20-25 minutes, or until the cheese is melted and bubbly.
  12. Once done, remove the casserole from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes before serving.

Serving Suggestions

This Easy Low-Carb Keto Chicken Enchilada Casserole is delicious on its own, but you can take it to the next level with some tasty toppings. Serve it with a dollop of sour cream, a sprinkle of fresh cilantro, and a side of crisp lettuce for a refreshing contrast. You can also add some sliced avocado or a squeeze of lime juice for added zing.

Meal Prep Tip

This casserole is perfect for meal prepping. Once baked, you can divide it into individual portions and store them in airtight containers in the fridge for up to 4 days. Simply reheat in the microwave for a quick and satisfying keto-friendly meal.

Final Thoughts

With its rich, savory flavors and satisfying textures, this Easy Low-Carb Keto Chicken Enchilada Casserole is a must-try for anyone following a keto lifestyle or simply looking for a delicious and wholesome meal. It’s a versatile dish that can be customized with your favorite keto-friendly ingredients, making it a go-to recipe for any occasion.

So, the next time you’re craving the comforting flavors of enchiladas without the extra carbs, give this recipe a try and enjoy a hearty and satisfying meal that’s sure to please your taste buds and keep you on track with your low-carb keto goals. Happy cooking!
