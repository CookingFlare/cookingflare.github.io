Savory Crab and Corn Fritters with Spicy Remoulade


Crab lovers rejoice! These Savory Crab and Corn Fritters with Spicy Remoulade are the perfect appetizer or snack for any occasion. Packed with tender crab meat, sweet corn, and a kick of spice, these fritters are sure to impress your taste buds. And don’t worry, the Spicy Remoulade sauce is here to take these fritters to a whole new level of deliciousness. So let’s dive into the recipe and get cooking!

Ingredients

  • 1 pound crab meat, picked over for shells
  • 1 cup corn kernels, fresh or frozen
  • 1/2 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 cup cornmeal
  • 1/4 cup green onions, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon Old Bay seasoning
  • 1/2 teaspoon paprika
  • 1/4 teaspoon cayenne pepper (adjust to taste)
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 2 large eggs, beaten
  • Vegetable oil, for frying

Spicy Remoulade Sauce

  • 1/2 cup mayonnaise
  • 2 tablespoons Dijon mustard
  • 2 tablespoons hot sauce
  • 1 tablespoon capers, drained and chopped
  • 1 tablespoon fresh parsley, finely chopped
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon Worcestershire sauce
  • 1/2 teaspoon paprika
  • 1/4 teaspoon cayenne pepper
  • Salt and black pepper, to taste

Instructions

  1. In a large bowl, combine the crab meat, corn kernels, flour, cornmeal, green onions, minced garlic, Old Bay seasoning, paprika, cayenne pepper, salt, and black pepper. Mix well until all the ingredients are evenly incorporated.

  2. Add the beaten eggs to the crab mixture and stir until everything is well combined. The mixture should be moist but still hold its shape. If it’s too dry, add a little more beaten egg; if it’s too wet, add a little more flour.

  3. Heat vegetable oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat. You want enough oil to cover the bottom of the skillet with about 1/2 inch depth.

  4. Scoop about 1/4 cup of the crab mixture and gently pat it into a small patty. Repeat with the remaining mixture, making sure not to overcrowd the skillet. Carefully place the fritters into the hot oil and cook for about 3-4 minutes per side, or until golden brown and crispy. You may need to cook them in batches, depending on the size of your skillet.

  5. While the fritters are cooking, prepare the Spicy Remoulade sauce. In a small bowl, combine the mayonnaise, Dijon mustard, hot sauce, capers, fresh parsley, minced garlic, Worcestershire sauce, paprika, cayenne pepper, salt, and black pepper. Mix well until all the ingredients are fully incorporated. Adjust the seasoning according to your taste preferences.

  6. Once the fritters are cooked, transfer them to a paper towel-lined plate to drain any excess oil. Serve the Savory Crab and Corn Fritters hot with a generous dollop of the Spicy Remoulade sauce on top.

Enjoy these delectable fritters as a party appetizer, game day snack, or even as a light lunch. The combination of the tender crab meat, sweet corn, and the spicy kick from the remoulade sauce is simply irresistible. These fritters will surely be a hit at your next gathering. So go ahead and give this recipe a try, and let the flavors transport you to seafood heaven!
