Crazy about Chicory: 6 Chicory-rooted Juice & Smoothie Recipes


Chicory, ah the oft-overlooked, under-appreciated root that’s been lurking in the shadows of your local farmer’s market for far too long! Don’t let its humble exterior fool you, for within this unassuming bud lies a powerful ally when it comes to incorporating a healthy punch into your morning routine. To help uncover its tremendous potential, we’ve whipped up 6 delightful juice and smoothie concoctions, each packed with enough energy and nutrients to fuel your wildest dreams.

Rise and Shine Breakfast Blender

For the always-on-the-go types:

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup chicory roots, chopped
  • 1 medium banana, sliced
  • 2 tablespoons almond butter
  • 1 cup unsweetened almond milk
  • 1 tablespoon honey
  • 1/2 teaspoon cinnamon

Instructions:

  1. Toss chicory roots, banana slices, almond butter, almond milk, honey, and cinnamon into a high-speed blender.
  2. Blend until smooth and creamy.
  3. Pour into your favorite mug, grab your favorite snack, and conquer the day!

Glowing Skin Green Goddess

Vitamins, minerals, and all things pretty.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup chicory roots, chopped
  • 1 medium apple, cored
  • 1 cup parsley, fresh leaves
  • 1 cup spinach
  • 1 lemon, juiced
  • 1 inch piece of ginger, peeled

Instructions:

  1. Add chicory roots, apple, parsley, spinach, lemon juice, and ginger to your blender.
  2. Blitz until you’ve reached a dreamy, frothy texture.
  3. Pour yourself a glass, sip back and revel in your newfound beauty prowess.

Immunity-Boosting Evergreen

With just a touch of sweetness, this concoction comes with a kick from its verdant roots!

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup chicory roots, chopped
  • 1 medium beetroot, trimmed
  • 1 cup kale
  • 1/2 lemon, juiced
  • 1 inch piece of ginger, peeled
  • 1 small knob of turmeric (optional)
  • 1 tablespoon agave nectar

Instructions:

  1. Throw chicory roots, beetroot, kale, lemon juice, ginger, turmeric (if using), and agave nectar into your blender.
  2. Puree until thoroughly mixed.
  3. Raise a glass to a healthier you and chug this concoction straight into your veins.

Berrylicious Honeyed Chicory Twist

A balanced harmony of sweet and earthy flavors.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup chicory roots, chopped
  • 1/2 cup blueberries
  • 1/2 cup mixed berries (raspberries, strawberries)
  • 1/2 lemon, juiced
  • 1 tablespoon honey
  • 1 cup cold water

Instructions:

  1. Dump chicory roots, blueberries, mixed berries, lemon juice, honey, and water into your blender.
  2. Blend until smooth and silky.
  3. Taste, and maybe add a few more berries. Shh… no one will judge you for that.

Chicory-Rooted Sunrise Breeze

A morning recipe with energy to spare.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup chicory roots, chopped
  • 1 cup pineapple, tops cut off
  • 1 cup spinach
  • 1 tablespoon lime juice
  • 1-inch piece of ginger, peeled
  • Optional: 1-2 sprigs of mint

Instructions:

  1. Combine chicory roots, pineapple, spinach, lime juice, ginger, and mint into the blender.
  2. Blend until a beautiful sunrise hue is achieved.
  3. Grab a moment of Zen with each sip.

Midnight Magic Moonbeam

Don’t let the name intimidate you, this calming concoction tucks you in without ever kvetching you out.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup chicory roots
  • 1 medium carrot, trimmed
  • 1 stalk of celery
  • 1 cup cilantro
  • 1/2 lemon, juiced
  • 1 tablespoon coconut oil

Instructions:

  1. Pour chicory roots, carrot, celery, cilantro, lemon juice, and coconut oil into your blender.
  2. Whirl until emerald moonlight hue resembles.
  3. Soothe away any lingering tensions with a nightly elixir.

Well there you go, the chicory universe unlocked! Now you’re ready and equipped to go beyond your routine greens and make the most out of this humble root. So, put your blending skills to the test, release your culinary creativeness, and don’t forget to hashtag #ChicoryLover on Instagram – and may the world get a taste of what you’ve brewed up! Happy sipping!
