Crispy Crunch: Baked Parmesan Chicken Tenders Recipe


There’s something irresistible about the combination of crispy, crunchy, and cheesy flavors in a dish. And when it comes to chicken tenders, nothing beats the satisfaction of biting into a perfectly cooked piece of meat with a golden, flavorful coating. In this recipe, we’re taking the classic chicken tender and giving it a delicious twist with the addition of parmesan cheese. Get ready to indulge in the ultimate crispy crunch!

Ingredients

  • 1 pound of chicken tenders
  • 1 cup of breadcrumbs
  • 1/2 cup of grated parmesan cheese
  • 1 teaspoon of garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon of paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon of salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon of black pepper
  • 2 eggs, beaten
  • Cooking spray

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 425°F (220°C) and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.

  2. In a shallow dish, mix together the breadcrumbs, grated parmesan cheese, garlic powder, paprika, salt, and black pepper. This flavorful mixture will be the coating for the chicken tenders.

  3. Dip each chicken tender into the beaten eggs, making sure to coat it thoroughly. Then, roll the tender in the breadcrumb mixture, pressing gently to ensure the coating sticks to the chicken.

  4. Place the coated chicken tenders onto the prepared baking sheet, leaving a little space between each tender. This will allow the heat to circulate evenly and ensure crispy results.

  5. Lightly spray the chicken tenders with cooking spray. This will help them achieve that golden brown color and crispy texture.

  6. Bake the chicken tenders in the preheated oven for 15-20 minutes, or until they are cooked through and golden brown. The internal temperature should reach 165°F (74°C).

  7. Once the chicken tenders are done, remove them from the oven and let them rest for a few minutes before serving. This will help retain their juiciness and ensure maximum flavor.

  8. Serve your Crispy Crunch: Baked Parmesan Chicken Tenders with your favorite dipping sauce, such as marinara, honey mustard, or ranch dressing. These tenders are perfect as an appetizer, a main course, or even as a snack.

Tips and Variations

  • For an extra kick of flavor, add a pinch of cayenne pepper to the breadcrumb mixture. This will give your chicken tenders a spicy twist.

  • If you prefer a gluten-free option, you can use gluten-free breadcrumbs or crushed cornflakes instead. The result will still be deliciously crispy.

  • Experiment with different seasonings and spices to customize the flavor of your chicken tenders. Try adding dried herbs like oregano or thyme, or even some grated lemon zest for a citrusy twist.

  • If you want to make a larger batch of these chicken tenders, simply double or triple the ingredients accordingly. They are great for feeding a crowd or for meal prepping.

  • Leftovers can be stored in an airtight container in the refrigerator for up to three days. To reheat, simply place them in a preheated oven at 350°F (175°C) for about 10 minutes, or until heated through.

Enjoy the Crispy Crunch!

There you have it – a simple yet incredibly delicious recipe for Crispy Crunch: Baked Parmesan Chicken Tenders. With their golden, cheesy coating and tender, juicy meat, these chicken tenders are sure to become a favorite in your household. Whether you’re serving them as an appetizer, a main course, or just as a snack, they are guaranteed to satisfy your craving for that irresistible crispy crunch. So go ahead, give this recipe a try and enjoy the deliciousness!
