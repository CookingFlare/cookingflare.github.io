Thai Basil Tofu Noodle Soup: A Fragrant and Comforting Bowl


There’s nothing quite like a warm bowl of fragrant Thai basil tofu noodle soup to comfort your soul and tantalize your taste buds. This delightful dish is a harmonious blend of aromatic herbs, savory tofu, and slurp-worthy noodles, all swimming in a rich and flavorful broth. Whether you’re a seasoned home cook or a culinary novice, this recipe is sure to impress and satisfy.

Ingredients

For the Soup:

  • 1 block of firm tofu, cubed
  • 8 cups vegetable broth
  • 1 can of coconut milk
  • 3 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons fresh ginger, grated
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 red chilies, thinly sliced
  • 1 tablespoon lemongrass, finely chopped
  • 1 cup shiitake mushrooms, sliced
  • 1 red bell pepper, thinly sliced
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes
  • 1 tablespoon coconut sugar
  • 1 cup fresh basil leaves
  • 8 oz rice noodles
  • Salt and pepper to taste

For Garnish:

  • Fresh basil leaves
  • Sliced red chilies
  • Bean sprouts
  • Lime wedges

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Tofu: In a large pot, heat some oil over medium-high heat. Add the cubed tofu and cook until golden brown on all sides. Remove the tofu from the pot and set aside.

  2. Create the Flavor Base: In the same pot, add a bit more oil if needed. Sauté the garlic, ginger, red chilies, and lemongrass until fragrant.

  3. Build the Broth: Pour in the vegetable broth and coconut milk. Stir in the soy sauce and coconut sugar. Bring the mixture to a gentle simmer.

  4. Add the Veggies: Toss in the shiitake mushrooms, red bell pepper, and cherry tomatoes. Let the vegetables cook until they are tender yet still vibrant.

  5. Infuse with Aromatics: Introduce the fresh basil leaves to the pot and allow them to infuse their aromatic essence into the broth.

  6. Cook the Noodles: While the broth is simmering, cook the rice noodles according to the package instructions. Drain and set aside.

  7. Bringing it All Together: Once the broth is ready, gently add the cooked tofu and noodles to the pot. Season with salt and pepper to taste.

  8. Serve and Garnish: Ladle the fragrant Thai basil tofu noodle soup into bowls. Garnish with fresh basil leaves, sliced red chilies, bean sprouts, and a wedge of lime for a burst of citrus.
