Easy and Delicious: One-Pan Chicken Alfredo Pasta


Are you craving a creamy, comforting dish that’s easy to make and doesn’t require a pile of dishes to clean up afterward? Look no further than this delightful One-Pan Chicken Alfredo Pasta recipe! This dish is a perfect combination of tender chicken, al dente pasta, and a rich, velvety Alfredo sauce, all cooked together in one pan for a hassle-free cooking experience.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound (450g) boneless, skinless chicken breasts, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon Italian seasoning
  • 8 oz (225g) fettuccine pasta
  • 2 1/2 cups (600ml) chicken broth
  • 1 1/2 cups (360ml) heavy cream
  • 1 cup (100g) freshly grated Parmesan cheese
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley for garnish

Instructions

  1. Sear the Chicken: Heat the olive oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat. Add the chicken pieces to the skillet and season with salt, pepper, and Italian seasoning. Cook the chicken until it’s golden brown on all sides, then remove it from the skillet and set it aside.

  2. Cook the Aromatics: In the same skillet, add the minced garlic and sauté for about 30 seconds, until fragrant.

  3. Add the Pasta and Liquid: Break the fettuccine pasta in half and add it to the skillet. Pour in the chicken broth and heavy cream, then give everything a gentle stir to combine. Bring the mixture to a simmer.

  4. Simmer and Stir: Reduce the heat to medium-low and let the pasta simmer in the sauce for about 15 minutes, or until the pasta is cooked and the sauce has thickened, stirring occasionally to prevent sticking.

  5. Finish with the Chicken and Cheese: Once the pasta is cooked, return the seared chicken to the skillet. Add the freshly grated Parmesan cheese and stir until the cheese is melted and the chicken is heated through.

  6. Garnish and Serve: Season with additional salt and pepper if needed. Garnish with fresh parsley for a pop of color and flavor.

Tips for Success

  • Use a wide skillet or pan to ensure even cooking and to prevent the pasta from sticking together.
  • Freshly grated Parmesan cheese will melt more smoothly and give your sauce a better texture compared to pre-grated cheese.
  • If the sauce seems too thick, you can add a splash of chicken broth to thin it out to your desired consistency.

Enjoy Your One-Pan Wonder!

This One-Pan Chicken Alfredo Pasta is a fantastic dish for busy weeknights when you want a homemade meal with minimal effort and cleanup. The creamy Alfredo sauce coats the pasta and chicken beautifully, creating a luxurious and satisfying meal that will have everyone asking for seconds. Serve it with a side of garlic bread and a crisp salad for a complete and delightful dinner experience.

So, the next time you’re in the mood for a comforting and indulgent meal, give this recipe a try! It’s a wonderful way to bring the family together around the dinner table without spending hours in the kitchen. Happy cooking!
