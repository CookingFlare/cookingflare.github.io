Healthy Paleo Beef and Cabbage Soup Recipe


Looking for a hearty and nutritious meal that’s perfect for a cozy night in or a comforting family dinner? This Healthy Paleo Beef and Cabbage Soup is a delicious option that’s not only satisfying but also packed with wholesome ingredients. Whether you’re following a paleo diet or simply looking for a nourishing dish, this recipe is sure to become a favorite in your kitchen.

Ingredients

For the Soup:

  • 1 pound grass-fed ground beef
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried thyme
  • 1/4 teaspoon cayenne pepper (optional)
  • 6 cups beef or vegetable broth
  • 1 head green cabbage, cored and chopped
  • 2 carrots, peeled and sliced
  • 2 celery stalks, sliced
  • 1 can diced tomatoes
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley for garnish

Instructions

Browning the Beef:

  1. Sauté the Beef: Heat a large soup pot over medium-high heat. Add the ground beef and cook, breaking it apart with a spoon, until browned. Remove the beef from the pot and set it aside.

  2. Sauté the Aromatics: In the same pot, add the olive oil, diced onion, and minced garlic. Sauté for a few minutes until the onions are translucent and fragrant.

Building the Soup:

  1. Combine Ingredients: Return the browned beef to the pot. Add the paprika, dried oregano, dried thyme, and cayenne pepper. Stir everything together to coat the beef and aromatics with the spices.

  2. Add Broth and Vegetables: Pour in the beef or vegetable broth, chopped cabbage, sliced carrots, sliced celery, and diced tomatoes. Season with salt and black pepper to taste.

  3. Simmer: Bring the soup to a boil, then reduce the heat to low. Let the soup simmer for about 20-25 minutes, or until the vegetables are tender and the flavors have melded together.

Serving and Enjoying:

  1. Garnish and Serve: Ladle the hot soup into bowls and garnish with freshly chopped parsley. The vibrant green color of the parsley adds a beautiful contrast to the rich soup.

  2. Enjoy! Serve the Healthy Paleo Beef and Cabbage Soup hot and savor every spoonful of its comforting flavors.

Tips for Success

  • Use Quality Beef: Opt for grass-fed beef to enhance the flavor and nutritional value of the soup.
  • Adjust Spices: Feel free to adjust the amount of cayenne pepper based on your preference for spiciness.
  • Make It Ahead: This soup tastes even better the next day, so consider making a batch ahead of time for a quick, satisfying meal.

Pairing Suggestions

This Healthy Paleo Beef and Cabbage Soup pairs wonderfully with a variety of sides. Here are a few ideas to complete your meal:

  • Crusty Bread: A slice of grain-free or paleo-friendly bread is perfect for dipping into the savory broth.
  • Side Salad: A light and refreshing side salad with mixed greens, cherry tomatoes, and a simple vinaigrette provides a delightful contrast to the rich soup.
  • Roasted Vegetables: A medley of roasted root vegetables adds depth and variety to the meal.

Why This Recipe Works

  • Nutrient-Dense Ingredients: This soup is packed with wholesome vegetables and high-quality beef, providing a wide array of essential nutrients.
  • Paleo-Friendly: Adhering to paleo diet principles, this recipe avoids processed ingredients and focuses on natural, whole foods.
  • Flavorful and Comforting: The combination of tender beef, vibrant vegetables, and aromatic herbs creates a soup that’s both flavorful and comforting.

Health Benefits

  • Protein-Rich: With grass-fed beef as the star ingredient, this soup offers a generous amount of high-quality protein.
  • Vitamins and Minerals: The assortment of vegetables in the soup contributes essential vitamins and minerals for overall well-being.
  • Gut-Friendly: Cabbage is known for its gut-health benefits, making this soup not only delicious but also supportive of digestive wellness.

Conclusion

With its nourishing ingredients and rich, savory flavors, this Healthy Paleo Beef and Cabbage Soup is a wonderful addition to any meal rotation. Whether you’re seeking a wholesome paleo-friendly dish or simply craving a bowl of comforting soup, this recipe delivers on both taste and nutrition. So, gather your ingredients, prepare your soup pot, and get ready to enjoy a bowl of this delightful, satisfying soup.
