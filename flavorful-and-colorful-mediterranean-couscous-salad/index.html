Flavorful and Colorful Mediterranean Couscous Salad


If you’re looking for a dish that’s bursting with vibrant colors and bold flavors, then a Mediterranean couscous salad is the perfect choice. This dish is not only visually stunning but also a delightful explosion of Mediterranean-inspired ingredients that will tantalize your taste buds. Whether you’re looking for a refreshing side dish or a satisfying main course, this salad has got you covered. Let’s dive into the recipe and explore how to create this delightful dish in your own kitchen.

Ingredients

For the Couscous:

  • 1 ½ cups couscous
  • 1 ¾ cups vegetable or chicken broth
  • 2 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon salt

For the Dressing:

  • 1/3 cup extra virgin olive oil
  • 1/4 cup freshly squeezed lemon juice
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon honey
  • Salt and pepper to taste

For the Salad:

  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1 cup cucumber, diced
  • 1 cup red bell pepper, diced
  • 1/2 cup red onion, thinly sliced
  • 1/2 cup Kalamata olives, pitted and halved
  • 1/2 cup fresh parsley, chopped
  • 1/4 cup fresh mint, chopped
  • 4 ounces feta cheese, crumbled

Instructions

Step 1: Prepare the Couscous

  1. In a medium saucepan, bring the vegetable or chicken broth, olive oil, and salt to a boil.
  2. Stir in the couscous, cover the saucepan with a lid, and remove it from the heat.
  3. Let the couscous sit for 5 minutes, then fluff it with a fork to separate the grains.

Step 2: Make the Dressing

  1. In a small bowl, whisk together the olive oil, lemon juice, minced garlic, honey, salt, and pepper until well combined.

Step 3: Assemble the Salad

  1. In a large bowl, combine the cooked couscous, cherry tomatoes, cucumber, red bell pepper, red onion, Kalamata olives, parsley, and mint.
  2. Drizzle the dressing over the salad and gently toss to coat all the ingredients.
  3. Sprinkle the crumbled feta cheese on top of the salad before serving.

Serving Suggestions

This Mediterranean couscous salad is a versatile dish that can be served in various ways. It makes for a delightful side dish alongside grilled chicken, fish, or lamb. You can also turn it into a complete meal by adding grilled shrimp or tofu for a protein boost. Additionally, this salad is perfect for picnics, potlucks, or as a make-ahead lunch option for busy weekdays.

Why You’ll Love This Salad

The combination of fresh vegetables, flavorful herbs, tangy feta cheese, and zesty lemon dressing creates a symphony of tastes and textures in every bite. The couscous provides a light and fluffy base that beautifully absorbs the flavors of the dressing and other ingredients. This salad is not only a treat for your taste buds but also a feast for the eyes, thanks to its vibrant colors and appealing presentation.

Conclusion

In conclusion, this Mediterranean couscous salad is a celebration of the bountiful flavors and colors of the Mediterranean region. It’s a dish that embodies freshness, healthfulness, and a delightful blend of textures and tastes. Whether you’re hosting a gathering or simply craving a nourishing and visually stunning meal, this salad is sure to impress. So, gather your ingredients and embark on a culinary journey to the Mediterranean with this delightful couscous salad.
