Sicilian Cannoli with Pistachios: A Crunchy Italian Sweet


Welcome to our latest culinary adventure! Today, we’re going to explore the delightful world of Sicilian Cannoli with Pistachios. These crunchy Italian sweets are a true indulgence for anyone with a sweet tooth. With a crispy shell and a creamy, sweet filling, Sicilian Cannoli are a treat that will transport you to the vibrant streets of Sicily.

Ingredients

To make these delicious Sicilian Cannoli, you will need:

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons granulated sugar
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 2 tablespoons unsalted butter, cold and cut into small pieces
  • 1/2 cup sweet Marsala wine
  • 1 egg white, lightly beaten
  • Vegetable oil, for frying
  • 1 cup ricotta cheese
  • 1/2 cup powdered sugar
  • 1/4 cup chopped pistachios
  • 1/4 cup chocolate chips
  • 1/4 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • Zest of 1 orange

Instructions

  1. In a large mixing bowl, combine the flour, sugar, and salt. Add the cold butter pieces and mix with your fingers until the mixture resembles coarse crumbs.

  2. Gradually add the Marsala wine to the flour mixture, stirring until the dough comes together. Knead the dough on a lightly floured surface for about 5 minutes, or until smooth and elastic.

  3. Divide the dough into two equal portions. Cover one portion with a damp cloth to prevent drying out. Roll out the other portion of dough as thinly as possible, to about 1/8 inch thickness.

  4. Using a round cookie cutter or a glass, cut out circles from the rolled-out dough. Wrap each circle of dough around a cannoli tube, sealing the edges with a little beaten egg white.

  5. Heat vegetable oil in a deep fryer or a heavy-bottomed pot to 375°F (190°C). Carefully lower the cannoli tubes into the hot oil and fry until golden brown and crispy, about 3-4 minutes. Remove the cannoli shells from the oil and drain on paper towels.

  6. Once the cannoli shells have cooled, gently slide them off the tubes. They should easily slip off, leaving you with a crispy shell ready to be filled.

  7. In a separate bowl, combine the ricotta cheese, powdered sugar, chopped pistachios, chocolate chips, vanilla extract, and orange zest. Mix well until all the ingredients are fully incorporated.

  8. Using a piping bag or a small spoon, fill each cannoli shell with the ricotta mixture. Start from one end and pipe the filling all the way through to the other end. Repeat with the remaining cannoli shells.

  9. For an extra touch of indulgence, you can dip the ends of the filled cannoli into melted chocolate and sprinkle some extra chopped pistachios on top.

  10. Serve your Sicilian Cannoli with Pistachios immediately or refrigerate for a couple of hours to allow the flavors to meld together. These delightful treats are best enjoyed within a day or two for the ultimate freshness.

Conclusion

Congratulations! You’ve successfully created your very own batch of Sicilian Cannoli with Pistachios. The combination of the crunchy shell, creamy filling, and the rich flavors of pistachios and chocolate make these treats a true delight for the senses.

Whether you’re hosting a dinner party or simply looking to indulge in something special, Sicilian Cannoli with Pistachios are sure to impress. So go ahead, take a bite, and savor the deliciousness of this iconic Italian sweet. Buon appetito!
