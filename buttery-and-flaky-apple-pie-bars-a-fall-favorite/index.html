Buttery and Flaky Apple Pie Bars: A Fall Favorite


Fall is the perfect time to indulge in the cozy flavors of apple pie. But why settle for a traditional pie when you can enjoy all the deliciousness in a convenient and portable bar form? These Buttery and Flaky Apple Pie Bars are a delightful twist on the classic dessert, offering a buttery crust, a luscious apple filling, and a crumbly streusel topping. Get ready to fall in love with these irresistible treats.

Ingredients

For the crust and streusel topping:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 3/4 cup unsalted butter, cold and cubed
  • 1 large egg, beaten
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

For the apple filling:

  • 4 cups apples, peeled, cored, and thinly sliced
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground nutmeg
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 2 tablespoons lemon juice

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C) and line a 9x13-inch baking dish with parchment paper.

  2. In a large bowl, whisk together the flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt for the crust and streusel topping. Add the cold cubed butter and use a pastry cutter or your fingertips to cut the butter into the dry ingredients until the mixture resembles coarse crumbs.

  3. Reserve 1 1/2 cups of the mixture for the streusel topping. To the remaining mixture, add the beaten egg and vanilla extract. Mix until the dough comes together. Press the dough evenly into the bottom of the prepared baking dish.

  4. In another bowl, combine the sliced apples, sugar, flour, cinnamon, nutmeg, salt, and lemon juice for the apple filling. Toss everything together until the apples are evenly coated.

  5. Spread the apple filling over the crust in the baking dish. Sprinkle the reserved streusel topping evenly over the apples.

  6. Bake in the preheated oven for 40-45 minutes, or until the crust and streusel topping turn golden brown and the apples are tender.

  7. Remove from the oven and let the apple pie bars cool completely in the baking dish before cutting into squares.

Serving Suggestions

These Buttery and Flaky Apple Pie Bars are delightful on their own, but you can take them to the next level with a scoop of vanilla ice cream or a dollop of whipped cream. They are perfect for fall gatherings, potlucks, or simply enjoying as a sweet treat with a cup of hot apple cider.

Tips and Variations

  • Feel free to experiment with different types of apples for the filling. Granny Smith, Honeycrisp, or Golden Delicious apples work well in this recipe.
  • For an extra touch of indulgence, drizzle caramel sauce over the cooled bars before serving.
  • If you want to make these bars in advance, store them in an airtight container at room temperature for up to 3 days or refrigerate for up to a week.

Get ready to embrace the flavors of fall with these Buttery and Flaky Apple Pie Bars. With their buttery crust, sweet apple filling, and crumbly streusel topping, they are sure to become a favorite treat during the autumn season. So grab your apron and get baking – your taste buds will thank you!
