Savory Skewers: 8 Grilled Appetizers for Summer BBQs


Summer is finally here, and with it comes the perfect excuse to fire up the grill and host a backyard BBQ. While burgers and hot dogs are always a crowd-pleaser, why not mix things up with some savory skewers? Not only are they easy to make, but they’re also packed with flavor and can be customized to suit any taste. In this post, we’ll share 8 delicious grilled appetizer recipes that are sure to impress your guests.

Chicken Satay

One of the most popular skewer recipes, chicken satay is a staple of Southeast Asian cuisine. Marinate chicken breast in a mixture of coconut milk, lime juice, garlic, and coriander, then grill until cooked through. Serve with peanut sauce, fresh cucumber slices, and toasted bread for a refreshing snack.

Shrimp and Pineapple Skewers

These colorful skewers are perfect for a summer BBQ. Alternate chunks of succulent shrimp with pineapple chunks and cherry tomatoes, then brush with olive oil and grill until the shrimp are pink and the pineapple is caramelized. Serve with a squeeze of fresh lime juice and a sprinkle of chopped cilantro.

Beef and Onion Skewers

For a heartier skewer option, try these beef and onion skewers. Marinate thinly sliced beef in a mixture of soy sauce, olive oil, and Worcestershire sauce, then thread onto skewers with sliced onions and bell peppers. Grill until the beef is cooked to your liking, then serve with a side of roasted potatoes and steamed broccoli.

Mushroom and Spinach Skewers

For a vegetarian option, these mushroom and spinach skewers are a great choice. Alternate button mushrooms with fresh spinach leaves and cherry tomatoes, then brush with olive oil and grill until the mushrooms are tender. Serve with a side of crusty bread and a sprinkle of feta cheese.

Lemon and Herb Skewers

These skewers are perfect for a light and refreshing summer snack. Alternate chunks of lemon with fresh herbs like parsley, basil, and dill, then brush with olive oil and grill until the lemon is caramelized. Serve with a side of crudités and hummus.

Pepper and Onion Skewers

These colorful skewers are a great way to use up any leftover bell peppers and onions. Alternate chunks of bell peppers with sliced onions and mushrooms, then brush with olive oil and grill until the vegetables are tender. Serve with a side of crusty bread and a dollop of sour cream.

Prosciutto and Melon Skewers

For a sweet and savory skewer option, try these prosciutto and melon skewers. Alternate slices of prosciutto with chunks of fresh melon, then drizzle with honey and serve with a side of crackers and cheese.

Bacon-Wrapped Dates

These sweet and savory skewers are perfect for a summer BBQ. Stuff dates with crumbled blue cheese and wrap with bacon, then grill until the bacon is crispy. Serve with a side of toasted almonds and a drizzle of honey.

These savory skewers are sure to be a hit at your next summer BBQ. With a variety of flavors and textures to choose from, there’s something for everyone. So fire up the grill, gather your friends and family, and get ready to enjoy the perfect summer afternoon.
