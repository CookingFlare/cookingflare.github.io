Satisfy Your Cravings: Easy Enchilada Recipes


Enchiladas, a beloved Mexican dish, has been winning hearts worldwide with its mouth-watering flavors and textures. Typically consisting of a tortilla rolled around a filling, then coated in sauce, the beauty of enchiladas lies in their versatility. You can customize them with an array of ingredients to cater to your specific cravings and dietary requirements.

Before diving into the recipe details, let’s understand some crucial components that make a delicious enchilada:

  1. Enchilada sauce: A rich, flavorful sauce that brings alive the Mexican spice in your mouth. Homemade or store-bought, pick your preferred base – whether it’s Grandma’s recipe or a grocery store find. An enchilada sauce generally has a bold, savory taste that complements the filling harmoniously.
  2. Filling: The heart of an enchilada, the filling should be invigorating and delectable to entice your taste buds. Popular and easy fillings include chicken, beans, cheese, vegetables, and much more.
  3. Tortillas: Flour or corn, tender and adaptable, these are essential for creating that iconic rolled texture.

Now that we have our foundations down, let’s explore various easy enchilada recipes that range from traditional fare to modern twists:

Classic Chicken Enchiladas

Ingredients:

  • 3 large chicken breasts, cooked and shredded
  • 1 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1 can (10 oz) green chilies, drained
  • 1 bottle (16 oz) enchilada sauce
  • 1 pack (8-10) small flour tortillas
  • 1 cup sour cream
  • 2 cups shredded lettuce
  • Cilantro leaves, for garnishing

Instructions:

  1. Cook chicken breasts and shred them. Set aside.
  2. Spread a layer of sour cream over each tortilla and add a spoonful of shredded chicken, chilies, and cheese to the center.
  3. Roll up the tortilla tightly around the filling.
  4. Heat a layer of enchilada sauce in a baking dish and place the rolled tortillas, seam side down, in the sauce.
  5. Pour the remaining sauce evenly over the enchiladas.
  6. Sprinkle cheese all over the enchiladas.
  7. Bake in an oven preheated to 350°F (177°C) for 20-25 minutes or until heated through.
  8. Garnish with shredded lettuce and cilantro. Serve.

Vegetarian Bean Enchiladas

Ingredients:

  • 1 can (15 oz) black beans, drained and rinsed
  • 1 can (15 oz) pinto beans, drained and rinsed
  • 1 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1 can (10 oz) green chilies, chopped
  • 1 bottle (16 oz) enchilada sauce
  • 1 pack (8-10) small corn tortillas
  • 1 cup sour cream
  • 2 cups shredded lettuce
  • Cilantro leaves, for garnishing

Instructions:

  1. Mix the beans with half the chilies, cheese, and a dollop of sour cream.
  2. Dip a tortilla into the enchilada sauce and layer the filling at the center.
  3. Roll the tortilla just like before.
  4. Place rolled tortillas in a baking dish, where the seam side is down.
  5. Spoon the leftover sauce over the enchiladas.
  6. Reserve a portion of cheese to sprinkle on top and slide the dish into the oven.
  7. Bake at 350°F (177°C) for 20-25 minutes or until heated through.
  8. Splash of fresh cilantro over the enchiladas and serve.

Modern Spin on Enchiladas - Sweet Potato Enchiladas

Ingredients:

  • 2 large sweet potatoes
  • 1 cup cooked black beans
  • 1 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1 can (10 oz) green chilies
  • 1 bottle (16 oz) enchilada sauce
  • 1 pack (8-10) small corn tortillas
  • 1 tbsp olive oil

Instructions:

  1. Roast sweet potatoes in an oven preheated to 400°F (204°C) for approximately 50 minutes or until exceptionally tender.
  2. Cool sweet potatoes for a few minutes before peeling and mashing them in a large bowl. Season it with salt to taste.
  3. Stir in black beans, chilies, and ¾ of the cheese.
  4. Make enchiladas following steps 2-5 from the Classic Chicken Enchiladas recipe.
  5. Pour enchilada sauce throughout the dish, then sprinkle the rest of the cheese.
  6. Bake at 350°F (177°C) for 20-25 minutes, and that’s it!
  7. Your cheesy, wholesome sweet potato enchiladas are ready to be devoured.

Regardless of which easy enchilada recipe you choose, the end result is sure to tantalize your taste buds and satisfy your cravings. So, gather your ingredients, and let’s get cooking! From your kitchen to your plate, bon appétit!
