Spaghetti and Meatballs: A Classic Italian Comfort Food Dish


Spaghetti and meatballs is a timeless dish that brings warmth, comfort, and a taste of Italy to your table. This classic Italian recipe has been passed down through generations, and its popularity remains unwavering. The combination of tender meatballs, flavorful tomato sauce, and al dente spaghetti creates a harmonious symphony of flavors that will leave you craving for more. In this blog post, we’ll explore the secrets to making the perfect spaghetti and meatballs, so get ready to unleash your inner Italian chef!

Ingredients

To make the meatballs, you will need:

  • 500g ground beef
  • 1/2 cup breadcrumbs
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1/4 cup fresh parsley, finely chopped
  • 1 egg, lightly beaten
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper

For the tomato sauce, gather:

  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 can (400g) crushed tomatoes
  • 1 can (200g) tomato sauce
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried basil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

For serving, you will need:

  • 400g spaghetti
  • Fresh basil leaves, torn
  • Grated Parmesan cheese

Instructions

Let’s dive into the step-by-step process of creating this mouthwatering dish:

  1. In a large mixing bowl, combine the ground beef, breadcrumbs, Parmesan cheese, minced garlic, chopped parsley, beaten egg, salt, and black pepper. Mix well until all the ingredients are evenly incorporated.

  2. Shape the mixture into small, round meatballs, approximately 1 inch in diameter. You should be able to make around 20 meatballs.

  3. Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Add the finely chopped onion and minced garlic, sautéing until they become translucent and fragrant.

  4. Add the crushed tomatoes and tomato sauce to the skillet, stirring until well combined with the onions and garlic. Season the sauce with dried oregano, dried basil, salt, and pepper. Allow the sauce to simmer for about 15 minutes, allowing the flavors to meld together.

  5. While the sauce is simmering, bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Cook the spaghetti according to the package instructions until al dente. Drain and set aside.

  6. In a separate skillet, heat a tablespoon of olive oil over medium-high heat. Add the meatballs to the skillet and cook until they are browned on all sides and cooked through, approximately 8-10 minutes.

  7. Once the meatballs are cooked, transfer them to the simmering tomato sauce and let them cook together for an additional 5-10 minutes, allowing the flavors to infuse.

  8. Serve the spaghetti and meatballs hot, topped with the tomato sauce and meatballs. Garnish with torn basil leaves and grated Parmesan cheese for an extra burst of flavor.

Tips and Variations

  • For a healthier alternative, you can use ground turkey or chicken instead of beef for the meatballs.
  • Experiment with different herbs and spices to personalize the tomato sauce. Add a pinch of red pepper flakes for a spicy kick or a dash of sugar for a touch of sweetness.
  • If you prefer a smoother sauce, you can blend the cooked tomato sauce in a food processor or blender before adding the meatballs.
  • To make the dish more indulgent, melt some mozzarella cheese on top of the meatballs and spaghetti before serving.

Conclusion

Spaghetti and meatballs is a classic Italian comfort food dish that never fails to satisfy. With its rich flavors and hearty combination of tender meatballs and al dente spaghetti, it’s no wonder this dish holds a special place in the hearts of many. By following this recipe and adding your own personal touch, you can create a memorable meal that will transport you to the streets of Italy. So gather your ingredients, roll up your sleeves, and get ready to experience the magic of spaghetti and meatballs in your own kitchen. Buon appetito!
