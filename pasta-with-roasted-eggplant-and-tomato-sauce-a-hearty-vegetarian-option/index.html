Pasta with Roasted Eggplant and Tomato Sauce: A Hearty Vegetarian Option


Are you in the mood for a delicious and hearty vegetarian pasta dish? Look no further! Our Pasta with Roasted Eggplant and Tomato Sauce is sure to satisfy your cravings. This recipe combines the rich flavors of roasted eggplant and tangy tomato sauce, resulting in a mouthwatering dish that will leave you wanting more.

Ingredients

To make this delectable pasta dish, you will need the following ingredients:

  • 1 large eggplant
  • 3 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 can (400g) diced tomatoes
  • 2 tablespoons tomato paste
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • 500g pasta of your choice
  • Fresh basil leaves, for garnish
  • Grated Parmesan cheese, for serving (optional)

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 200°C (400°F).
  2. Slice the eggplant into 1-inch thick rounds and place them on a baking tray lined with parchment paper. Drizzle the eggplant slices with 2 tablespoons of olive oil and season with salt and pepper. Roast the eggplant in the preheated oven for about 25 minutes, or until golden brown and tender. Set aside.
  3. In the meantime, heat the remaining tablespoon of olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Add the chopped onion and minced garlic, and sauté until the onion is translucent and fragrant.
  4. Add the diced tomatoes, tomato paste, dried basil, and dried oregano to the skillet. Stir well to combine all the ingredients. Reduce the heat to low and let the sauce simmer for about 15-20 minutes, allowing the flavors to meld together.
  5. While the sauce is simmering, cook the pasta according to the package instructions until al dente. Drain the pasta and set aside.
  6. Once the sauce has thickened and the flavors have developed, remove it from the heat and set aside.
  7. Now it’s time to assemble the dish. In a large serving bowl, combine the cooked pasta, roasted eggplant slices, and tomato sauce. Gently toss everything together until the pasta is evenly coated with the sauce.
  8. Serve the Pasta with Roasted Eggplant and Tomato Sauce garnished with fresh basil leaves. For an extra touch of flavor, sprinkle some grated Parmesan cheese on top (optional).

Final Thoughts

This Pasta with Roasted Eggplant and Tomato Sauce is a fantastic vegetarian option that doesn’t compromise on taste or satisfaction. The roasted eggplant adds a delicious smoky flavor, while the tangy tomato sauce brings a burst of freshness to the dish. The combination of these flavors, along with the perfectly cooked pasta, creates a truly hearty and satisfying meal.

Whether you’re a vegetarian or simply looking to incorporate more plant-based meals into your diet, this recipe is a winner. It’s easy to make, packed with flavor, and guaranteed to impress your family and friends. So, why wait? Head to your kitchen and give this Pasta with Roasted Eggplant and Tomato Sauce a try. Your taste buds will thank you!
