Family-Friendly Chicken Wing Recipes for Game Day


Game day is always an exciting time for family and friends to come together, cheer for their favorite team, and of course, enjoy delicious food. And what better way to elevate the game day experience than with some finger-licking chicken wing recipes? Whether you like them spicy, sweet, tangy, or savory, there’s a chicken wing recipe for every palate. In this blog post, we’ll explore some family-friendly chicken wing recipes that are sure to be a hit on game day.

Classic Crispy Chicken Wings

Ingredients:

  • 2 lbs chicken wings
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp black pepper
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 1/2 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 cup unsalted butter, melted

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 400°F (200°C).
  2. In a large bowl, toss the chicken wings with salt, black pepper, and garlic powder.
  3. Coat the seasoned wings with flour and place them on a greased baking sheet.
  4. Bake for 45-50 minutes or until the wings are golden and crispy.
  5. In a separate bowl, mix the melted butter with your favorite hot sauce.
  6. Toss the baked wings in the hot sauce mixture until evenly coated.
  7. Serve with celery sticks and ranch dressing.

Honey BBQ Chicken Wings

Ingredients:

  • 2 lbs chicken wings
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp paprika
  • 1/2 tsp garlic powder
  • 1/2 cup honey
  • 1/4 cup BBQ sauce
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 2 tbsp apple cider vinegar

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the grill to medium-high heat.
  2. Season the chicken wings with salt, paprika, and garlic powder.
  3. In a small saucepan, combine honey, BBQ sauce, soy sauce, and apple cider vinegar. Simmer for 5 minutes to make the glaze.
  4. Grill the chicken wings for 15-20 minutes, turning occasionally, until they are cooked through and golden brown.
  5. During the last few minutes of grilling, brush the wings with the honey BBQ glaze.
  6. Serve with extra glaze on the side for dipping.

Lemon Pepper Chicken Wings

Ingredients:

  • 2 lbs chicken wings
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp lemon pepper seasoning
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 tbsp lemon juice
  • 1 tbsp chopped fresh parsley

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 425°F (220°C).
  2. Season the chicken wings with salt and lemon pepper seasoning.
  3. Drizzle olive oil over the seasoned wings and toss to coat.
  4. Place the wings on a baking sheet and bake for 30-35 minutes until crispy and golden.
  5. In a small bowl, mix lemon juice and chopped parsley.
  6. Drizzle the lemon-parsley mixture over the baked wings and toss to coat.
  7. Serve with lemon wedges for an extra citrusy kick.

Teriyaki Chicken Wings

Ingredients:

  • 2 lbs chicken wings
  • 1/2 cup soy sauce
  • 1/4 cup brown sugar
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tsp grated fresh ginger
  • 2 tbsp honey
  • 2 green onions, chopped (for garnish)

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, combine soy sauce, brown sugar, garlic, ginger, and honey to make the teriyaki sauce.
  2. Marinate the chicken wings in the teriyaki sauce for at least 1 hour.
  3. Preheat the grill to medium heat.
  4. Grill the marinated wings for 15-20 minutes, turning and basting with the remaining sauce.
  5. Once the wings are cooked through and caramelized, remove them from the grill.
  6. Garnish with chopped green onions before serving.

These family-friendly chicken wing recipes are easy to make and are perfect for game day gatherings. Whether you’re hosting a small get-together or a big game day party, these recipes are sure to be a crowd-pleaser. So, get ready to score big with these delicious chicken wing recipes and make your game day extra special!
