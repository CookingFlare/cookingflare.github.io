Satisfying Keto Pepperoni and Cheese Stuffed Chicken Breast


Are you looking for a delicious and satisfying keto-friendly meal that will leave you wanting more? Look no further! Our Satisfying Keto Pepperoni and Cheese Stuffed Chicken Breast is the perfect dish to satisfy your cravings while keeping you on track with your low-carb lifestyle. This recipe is packed with flavor and will surely become a staple in your kitchen. Let’s get started!

Ingredients

  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup sliced pepperoni
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 tablespoons Italian seasoning
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • Olive oil, for cooking

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).

  2. Using a sharp knife, carefully slice a pocket into each chicken breast. Be sure not to cut all the way through. You want to create a pocket to stuff with the delicious filling.

  3. In a bowl, combine the shredded mozzarella cheese, sliced pepperoni, grated Parmesan cheese, and Italian seasoning. Mix well until all the ingredients are evenly distributed.

  4. Open up the pocket of each chicken breast and stuff them with the cheese and pepperoni mixture. Press down gently to ensure the filling is evenly distributed.

  5. Season the outside of the chicken breasts with salt and pepper to taste.

  6. Heat a large oven-safe skillet over medium-high heat. Add a drizzle of olive oil to the skillet and swirl it around to coat the bottom.

  7. Carefully place the stuffed chicken breasts in the skillet, making sure not to overcrowd the pan. Cook for 3-4 minutes on each side, or until the chicken is golden brown.

  8. Transfer the skillet to the preheated oven and bake for 20-25 minutes, or until the chicken is cooked through and the cheese is melted and bubbly.

  9. Remove the skillet from the oven and let the chicken breasts rest for a few minutes before serving. This will allow the juices to redistribute and ensure a moist and flavorful dish.

  10. Serve your Satisfying Keto Pepperoni and Cheese Stuffed Chicken Breast with a side of steamed vegetables or a fresh salad for a complete and nutritious meal.

Tips and Variations

  • If you’re not following a keto diet, you can experiment with different types of cheese and fillings. Try using cheddar, Swiss, or even blue cheese for a different flavor profile.
  • Feel free to add some chopped fresh herbs, such as basil or parsley, to the cheese and pepperoni mixture for added freshness and aroma.
  • If you don’t have an oven-safe skillet, you can transfer the stuffed chicken breasts to a baking dish before placing them in the oven.
  • Leftovers can be stored in an airtight container in the refrigerator for up to 3 days. Simply reheat in the oven or microwave before enjoying.

Conclusion

Our Satisfying Keto Pepperoni and Cheese Stuffed Chicken Breast is a flavorful and filling dish that will satisfy your cravings without derailing your keto lifestyle. The combination of melted cheese, savory pepperoni, and juicy chicken creates a mouthwatering experience that is sure to become a favorite. Give this recipe a try and let us know what you think. Happy cooking!
