15-Minute Chicken and Vegetable Stir-Fry Recipe


Looking for a delicious and healthy meal that you can whip up in no time? Look no further! This 15-minute chicken and vegetable stir-fry is the perfect solution for busy weeknights when you want something quick, nutritious, and packed with flavor.

Ingredients

  • 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts, thinly sliced
  • 2 cups of mixed vegetables (such as bell peppers, snap peas, carrots, and broccoli)
  • 3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon of fresh ginger, grated
  • 2 tablespoons of soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon of oyster sauce
  • 1 tablespoon of sesame oil
  • 2 tablespoons of vegetable oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Cooked rice or noodles for serving

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Ingredients: Start by thinly slicing the chicken breasts and chopping the mixed vegetables into bite-sized pieces. Mince the garlic and grate the fresh ginger. Having all the ingredients ready to go will make the stir-frying process quick and seamless.

  2. Heat the Wok or Skillet: Place a wok or large skillet over medium-high heat and add the vegetable oil. Once the oil is hot, add the sliced chicken to the pan. Stir-fry the chicken for 3-4 minutes or until it’s no longer pink and has a nice golden color.

  3. Add the Aromatics: Push the chicken to one side of the pan and add the minced garlic and grated ginger to the empty space. Stir-fry for about 30 seconds until fragrant, then mix it with the chicken.

  4. Incorporate the Vegetables: Toss in the mixed vegetables and stir-fry for an additional 2-3 minutes until they are slightly tender but still crisp.

  5. Season the Stir-Fry: Drizzle the soy sauce, oyster sauce, and sesame oil over the chicken and vegetables. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Continue stir-frying for another minute to allow the flavors to meld together.

  6. Serve and Enjoy: Once everything is cooked to perfection, remove the stir-fry from the heat and serve it immediately over cooked rice or noodles. Garnish with some sliced green onions or sesame seeds for an extra pop of flavor and texture.

Tips for Success

  • Prep Ahead: To make this recipe even quicker, consider prepping the ingredients ahead of time, such as slicing the chicken and chopping the vegetables the night before.
  • Customize Your Veggies: Feel free to use your favorite vegetables in this stir-fry. It’s a great way to clean out the fridge and use up any produce that’s on the verge of going bad.
  • Add Some Heat: If you like a bit of spice, add a sprinkle of red pepper flakes or a drizzle of sriracha to the stir-fry for a kick of heat.

This 15-minute chicken and vegetable stir-fry is not only a time-saver, but it’s also a versatile dish that can be easily customized to suit your taste preferences. Whether you’re a busy parent, a working professional, or just someone who appreciates a quick and delicious meal, this recipe is sure to become a go-to in your culinary repertoire. So, the next time you’re pressed for time but still want a home-cooked meal, give this recipe a try and enjoy a satisfying dish in no time!
