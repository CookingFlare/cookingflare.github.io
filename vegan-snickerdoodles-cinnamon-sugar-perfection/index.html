Vegan Snickerdoodles: Cinnamon-Sugar Perfection


Are you ready to sink your teeth into a mouthwatering treat that is both vegan and irresistibly delicious? Look no further than these Vegan Snickerdoodles! Bursting with warm cinnamon and coated in a sweet sugary crust, these cookies are the epitome of cinnamon-sugar perfection. Whether you’re a seasoned vegan or simply looking to explore the world of plant-based desserts, these Snickerdoodles are sure to satisfy your sweet tooth. So, let’s dive right in and learn how to whip up these delectable delights!

Ingredients

To create these vegan Snickerdoodles, you’ll need the following ingredients:

  • 1 ½ cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon cream of tartar
  • ½ teaspoon baking soda
  • ¼ teaspoon salt
  • ½ cup vegan butter, softened
  • ¾ cup granulated sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 2 tablespoons plant-based milk (such as almond or soy milk)

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C) and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.

  2. In a medium-sized bowl, whisk together the all-purpose flour, cream of tartar, baking soda, and salt. Set aside.

  3. In a separate large mixing bowl, cream together the softened vegan butter, granulated sugar, and vanilla extract until light and fluffy. This can be done using an electric mixer or by hand.

  4. Gradually add the dry ingredient mixture to the creamed butter and sugar, mixing well to combine. The dough should come together and form a soft, cohesive ball.

  5. If the dough feels a bit dry, add the plant-based milk, one tablespoon at a time, until the dough reaches the desired consistency.

  6. In a small bowl, combine 2 tablespoons of granulated sugar with 1 tablespoon of ground cinnamon. Mix well to create the cinnamon-sugar coating for the cookies.

  7. Using your hands or a cookie scoop, portion out small balls of dough, approximately 1 inch in diameter. Roll each ball in the cinnamon-sugar mixture, ensuring it is coated evenly.

  8. Place the coated dough balls onto the prepared baking sheet, leaving enough space between each cookie to allow for spreading during baking.

  9. Bake the Snickerdoodles for 10-12 minutes, or until the edges are lightly golden brown. The centers may still appear slightly soft, but they will firm up as the cookies cool.

  10. Remove the baking sheet from the oven and allow the cookies to cool on the sheet for 5 minutes. Transfer the cookies to a wire rack to cool completely.

  11. Once cooled, these Vegan Snickerdoodles are ready to be devoured! Serve them alongside a glass of plant-based milk or enjoy them on their own. They also make a delightful gift or party favor.

Tips and Variations

  • For an extra kick of flavor, try adding a dash of ground nutmeg or cardamom to the cinnamon-sugar coating mixture.
  • If you prefer a chewier texture, slightly underbake the Snickerdoodles by reducing the baking time by 1-2 minutes.
  • Feel free to experiment with different types of plant-based milk in this recipe. Each variety can impart its own unique flavor to the cookies.
  • To keep these Snickerdoodles fresh, store them in an airtight container at room temperature for up to 5 days. They can also be frozen for longer storage.

Now that you have the recipe for these heavenly Vegan Snickerdoodles, it’s time to get baking! With their irresistibly soft centers, delightful cinnamon-sugar coating, and vegan-friendly ingredients, these cookies are bound to become a staple in your dessert repertoire. So grab your apron, gather your ingredients, and embark on a journey to cinnamon-sugar perfection. Enjoy!
