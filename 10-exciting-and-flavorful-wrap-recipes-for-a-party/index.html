10 Exciting and Flavorful Wrap Recipes for a Party


Wraps are the ultimate party foodâ€”they’re easy to prepare, convenient to eat, and can be packed with a variety of flavors that cater to everyone’s tastes. Whether you’re hosting a casual get-together, a birthday bash, or a game night with friends, these 10 exciting wrap recipes are sure to be a hit. From savory to spicy, and everything in between, these wraps offer a burst of flavors that will keep your guests coming back for more.

1. Tex-Mex Beef Fajita Wrap

Ingredients:

  • Large flour tortillas
  • Thinly sliced beef steak
  • Bell peppers, thinly sliced
  • Red onion, thinly sliced
  • Tex-Mex seasoning blend
  • Shredded cheese (cheddar or Mexican blend)
  • Sour cream
  • Salsa
  • Fresh cilantro, chopped

Instructions:

  1. Season beef steak with Tex-Mex seasoning and grill until cooked to your preference.
  2. Heat tortillas and layer with beef steak, bell peppers, red onion, shredded cheese, sour cream, salsa, and fresh cilantro.
  3. Roll up tightly, slice into halves, and serve.

This wrap packs a punch with bold Tex-Mex flavors and is perfect for those who love a hearty and satisfying meal.

2. Asian Sesame Chicken Wrap

Ingredients:

  • Large spinach or whole wheat tortillas
  • Grilled chicken breast, thinly sliced
  • Asian sesame dressing
  • Shredded carrots
  • Cucumber, julienned
  • Red cabbage, thinly sliced
  • Green onions, chopped
  • Sesame seeds

Instructions:

  1. Toss grilled chicken breast in Asian sesame dressing until well coated.
  2. Lay out tortillas and layer with chicken breast, shredded carrots, cucumber, red cabbage, green onions, and sesame seeds.
  3. Roll up tightly, slice into pinwheels, and secure with toothpicks for easy serving.

This wrap offers a refreshing blend of Asian-inspired flavors and crunchy vegetables, making it a delightful addition to any party spread.

3. Mediterranean Veggie Wrap

Ingredients:

  • Large whole wheat wraps
  • Hummus (flavor of your choice)
  • Baby spinach leaves
  • Cherry tomatoes, halved
  • Kalamata olives, sliced
  • Red onion, thinly sliced
  • Feta cheese, crumbled
  • Fresh basil leaves, chopped
  • Balsamic glaze

Instructions:

  1. Spread a generous layer of hummus over the wraps.
  2. Layer with baby spinach leaves, cherry tomatoes, Kalamata olives, red onion, feta cheese, fresh basil leaves, and drizzle with balsamic glaze.
  3. Roll tightly, slice into rounds, and serve chilled.

This Mediterranean-inspired wrap is bursting with colors and flavors, perfect for a light and healthy party option.

4. BBQ Pulled Pork Wrap

Ingredients:

  • Large flour tortillas
  • BBQ pulled pork
  • Coleslaw mix (cabbage and carrots)
  • Pickles, sliced
  • Red onion, thinly sliced
  • BBQ sauce
  • Fresh parsley, chopped

Instructions:

  1. Warm tortillas and spread a layer of BBQ sauce.
  2. Layer with BBQ pulled pork, coleslaw mix, sliced pickles, red onion, and fresh parsley.
  3. Roll up tightly, slice into halves, and serve with extra BBQ sauce for dipping.

This wrap combines the smoky goodness of BBQ pulled pork with crunchy coleslaw, creating a satisfying and flavorful party treat.

5. Caprese Party Wraps

Ingredients:

  • Large spinach or sun-dried tomato tortillas
  • Fresh mozzarella cheese, sliced
  • Cherry tomatoes, halved
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • Balsamic glaze
  • Olive oil
  • Salt and pepper

Instructions:

  1. Drizzle olive oil over tortillas and layer with fresh mozzarella cheese, cherry tomatoes, and fresh basil leaves.
  2. Season with salt and pepper, drizzle with balsamic glaze.
  3. Roll up tightly, slice into pinwheels, and secure with toothpicks for serving.

These Caprese wraps offer a taste of Italy with creamy mozzarella, sweet tomatoes, and aromatic basil, perfect for a sophisticated party.

6. Smoked Salmon and Cream Cheese Wraps

Ingredients:

  • Large whole wheat wraps
  • Smoked salmon slices
  • Cream cheese
  • Cucumber, thinly sliced
  • Red onion, thinly sliced
  • Capers
  • Fresh dill, chopped
  • Lemon juice
  • Salt and pepper

Instructions:

  1. Spread a layer of cream cheese over the wraps.
  2. Layer with smoked salmon slices, cucumber, red onion, capers, fresh dill, and a squeeze of lemon juice.
  3. Roll up tightly, slice into rounds, and serve chilled.

These elegant wraps are perfect for seafood lovers, offering a creamy and refreshing flavor with each bite.

7. Spicy Buffalo Chicken Wrap

Ingredients:

  • Large flour tortillas
  • Buffalo chicken tenders, cooked and sliced
  • Romaine lettuce leaves
  • Celery, thinly sliced
  • Blue cheese dressing
  • Hot sauce (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Lay out tortillas and spread a layer of blue cheese dressing.
  2. Layer with buffalo chicken tenders, romaine lettuce, celery, and a drizzle of hot sauce if desired.
  3. Roll up tightly, slice into halves, and serve with extra blue cheese dressing on the side.

This wrap brings the heat with spicy buffalo chicken and cool, crisp veggiesâ€”a guaranteed crowd-pleaser at any party.

8. Teriyaki Steak Wrap

Ingredients:

  • Large spinach or whole wheat tortillas
  • Thinly sliced steak, marinated in teriyaki sauce
  • Bell peppers, thinly sliced
  • Red onion, thinly sliced
  • Pineapple chunks
  • Fresh cilantro, chopped

Instructions:

  1. Grill steak slices until cooked to your preference and toss in teriyaki sauce.
  2. Warm tortillas and layer with teriyaki steak, bell peppers, red onion, pineapple chunks, and fresh cilantro.
  3. Roll up tightly, slice into pinwheels, and serve warm.

This wrap offers a fusion of sweet and savory flavors with tender teriyaki steak and juicy pineapple, perfect for a tropical-themed party.

9. Greek Chicken Gyro Wrap

Ingredients:

  • Large pita bread or Greek-style flatbreads
  • Grilled chicken breast, thinly sliced
  • Tzatziki sauce
  • Cherry tomatoes, halved
  • Cucumber, thinly sliced
  • Red onion, thinly sliced
  • Kalamata olives, sliced
  • Fresh parsley, chopped

Instructions:

  1. Warm pita bread and spread a layer of tzatziki sauce.
  2. Layer with grilled chicken breast, cherry tomatoes, cucumber, red onion, Kalamata olives, and fresh parsley.
  3. Roll up tightly, slice into halves, and serve with extra tzatziki sauce on the side.

This Greek-inspired wrap offers a burst of Mediterranean flavors with tender chicken and tangy tzatziki sauce, perfect for a festive gathering.

  • Large whole wheat wraps
