Savory Seafood Stuffed Portobello Mushrooms Recipe


If you’re looking for a sophisticated yet easy-to-make dish to impress your guests or simply treat yourself to a gourmet meal, this savory seafood stuffed portobello mushrooms recipe is just what you need. Bursting with flavors and textures, this dish combines the earthy richness of portobello mushrooms with the delicate sweetness of seafood, creating a truly indulgent culinary experience. Whether you’re a seafood lover or simply enjoy experimenting with new flavors, this recipe is sure to become a favorite in your repertoire.

Ingredients

  • 4 large portobello mushrooms
  • 1 pound mixed seafood (such as shrimp, crab, and scallops)
  • 1/2 cup breadcrumbs
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley
  • 1/4 cup chopped sun-dried tomatoes
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Mushrooms:

    • Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
    • Gently clean the portobello mushrooms with a damp paper towel to remove any dirt. Carefully remove the stems and scoop out the gills to create a hollow cavity for the stuffing.

  2. Prepare the Seafood Filling:

    • In a large skillet, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the minced garlic and sauté for a minute until fragrant.
    • Add the mixed seafood to the skillet and cook for 3-4 minutes until just opaque. Be careful not to overcook the seafood as it will continue to cook in the oven.
    • Remove the skillet from the heat and stir in the breadcrumbs, Parmesan cheese, chopped parsley, and sun-dried tomatoes. Season with salt and pepper to taste.

  3. Stuff the Mushrooms:

    • Place the cleaned portobello mushrooms on a baking sheet, cavity side up. Divide the seafood filling evenly among the mushrooms, pressing gently to pack the filling.

  4. Bake to Perfection:

    • Transfer the baking sheet to the preheated oven and bake the stuffed mushrooms for 15-18 minutes, or until the mushrooms are tender and the filling is golden brown.

  5. Serve and Enjoy:

    • Once done, remove the mushrooms from the oven and let them cool for a few minutes before serving. Garnish with additional chopped parsley or a drizzle of balsamic glaze for an extra touch of elegance.

Tips and Variations

  • For a spicy kick, add a pinch of red pepper flakes to the seafood filling.
  • Feel free to experiment with different seafood combinations, such as lobster or chopped clams, to customize the filling to your liking.
  • If you’re a cheese enthusiast, consider adding a sprinkle of crumbled feta or creamy goat cheese on top of the seafood filling before baking.

Final Thoughts

These savory seafood stuffed portobello mushrooms are a versatile dish that can be served as an appetizer, a side dish, or even a light main course. The robust umami flavors of the portobello mushrooms perfectly complement the sweetness of the seafood filling, creating a harmonious blend of tastes and textures. Whether you’re hosting a dinner party or simply craving a gourmet treat, this recipe is a must-try for anyone who appreciates the marriage of seafood and earthy mushrooms. Bon appétit!
