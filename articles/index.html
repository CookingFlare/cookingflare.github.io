Pomegranate Pecan Salad with a Tangy Dressing

Are you looking for a refreshing and delicious salad that’s bursting with flavor? Look no further than this Pomegranate Pecan Salad with a tangy dressing! This vibrant salad is not only visually appealing but also packed with nutrients. The combination of juicy pomegranate seeds, crunchy pecans, and tangy dressing creates a perfect balance of sweet and savory flavors. Let’s dive into the recipe and get started! Ingredients 1 head of lettuce (preferably Romaine or your favorite greens) 1 cup of pomegranate seeds 1/2 cup of pecans, toasted and roughly chopped 1/4 cup of crumbled feta cheese 1/4 cup of red onion, thinly sliced Fresh parsley, chopped (for garnish) Tangy Dressing 3 tablespoons of olive oil 2 tablespoons of fresh lemon juice 1 tablespoon of honey 1 teaspoon of Dijon mustard Salt and pepper to taste Instructions Start by washing and drying the lettuce. Tear the leaves into bite-sized pieces and place them in a large salad bowl. Add the pomegranate seeds, pecans, crumbled feta cheese, and red onion to the salad bowl. Toss gently to combine all the ingredients. In a separate small bowl, whisk together the olive oil, lemon juice, honey, Dijon mustard, salt, and pepper. Adjust the seasonings according to your taste preferences. Drizzle the tangy dressing over the salad and toss gently to ensure all the ingredients are coated evenly. Sprinkle some fresh parsley on top for a pop of color and added freshness. Serve immediately and enjoy!
