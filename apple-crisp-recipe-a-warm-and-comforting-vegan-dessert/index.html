Apple Crisp Recipe: A Warm and Comforting Vegan Dessert


There’s something magical about the combination of apples, spices, and a crispy topping that makes apple crisp a beloved dessert. And if you’re a fan of vegan desserts or looking to explore plant-based options, you’re in for a treat! In this recipe, we’ll show you how to make a delicious apple crisp that is not only vegan but also incredibly comforting. So let’s get started!

Ingredients

For the apple filling, you’ll need:

  • 6 medium-sized apples, peeled, cored, and sliced
  • 1/4 cup of maple syrup
  • 2 tablespoons of lemon juice
  • 1 teaspoon of ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 teaspoon of ground nutmeg

For the crisp topping, gather:

  • 1 cup of rolled oats
  • 1/2 cup of almond flour
  • 1/4 cup of coconut sugar
  • 1/4 cup of melted coconut oil
  • 1/2 teaspoon of ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 teaspoon of salt

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease a baking dish with coconut oil or use a non-stick cooking spray.

  2. In a large mixing bowl, combine the sliced apples, maple syrup, lemon juice, cinnamon, and nutmeg. Toss everything together until the apples are evenly coated with the mixture.

  3. Transfer the apple filling to the greased baking dish, spreading it out evenly.

  4. In another bowl, mix together the rolled oats, almond flour, coconut sugar, melted coconut oil, cinnamon, and salt. Stir until the ingredients are well combined and form a crumbly texture.

  5. Sprinkle the crisp topping evenly over the apple filling, covering it completely.

  6. Place the baking dish in the preheated oven and bake for 40-45 minutes, or until the apples are tender and the topping is golden brown.

  7. Once the apple crisp is done baking, remove it from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes before serving. This will allow the flavors to meld together and make it easier to handle.

  8. Serve the warm and comforting apple crisp on its own or with a scoop of vegan vanilla ice cream for an extra indulgence. Enjoy!

Tips and Variations

  • Experiment with different types of apples for varying flavors and textures. Granny Smith apples are tart and hold their shape well, while Honeycrisp apples are sweet and juicy.

  • For added crunch, sprinkle some chopped nuts, such as almonds or walnuts, on top of the crisp before baking.

  • If you prefer a gluten-free version, substitute the rolled oats with gluten-free oats and ensure that all other ingredients are certified gluten-free.

  • Feel free to adjust the sweetness of the apple filling by adding more or less maple syrup, depending on your taste preferences.

  • Leftovers can be stored in an airtight container in the refrigerator for up to three days. Reheat in the oven or microwave before serving.

Conclusion

This apple crisp recipe is not only vegan but also incredibly delicious and satisfying. The combination of warm, tender apples with the sweet and crispy topping is sure to please both vegans and non-vegans alike. Whether you’re serving it as a dessert for a cozy family dinner or bringing it to a potluck, this warm and comforting apple crisp is bound to be a hit. So give it a try and let the flavors of fall fill your kitchen with warmth and joy!
