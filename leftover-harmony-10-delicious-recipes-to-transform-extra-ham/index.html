Leftover Harmony: 10 Delicious Recipes to Transform Extra Ham


Ham is a versatile and flavorful ingredient that can be used in a variety of dishes. Whether you have leftover ham from a holiday feast or simply want to change things up in your meal planning, these 10 delicious recipes will help you transform that extra ham into something truly amazing. From hearty soups to comforting casseroles and savory sandwiches, let’s dive into the world of leftover ham and discover the harmony it brings to your kitchen.

1. Ham and Potato Soup

Warm up on a chilly day with a comforting bowl of ham and potato soup. This creamy and hearty soup is the perfect way to use up leftover ham. Simply combine diced ham, potatoes, onions, carrots, and celery in a pot with chicken broth and simmer until the vegetables are tender. Finish off with a touch of cream, salt, and pepper for a soul-soothing meal.

2. Ham and Cheese Quiche

Quiche is a classic dish that can be enjoyed for breakfast, brunch, or even dinner. Combine diced ham, shredded cheese, eggs, milk, and your favorite vegetables like spinach or mushrooms. Pour the mixture into a pre-baked pie crust and bake until golden and set. Serve with a side salad for a complete and satisfying meal.

3. Ham and Pineapple Skewers

Get creative with your leftover ham by making sweet and savory ham and pineapple skewers. Alternate chunks of ham with pineapple pieces on skewers and grill until the ham is slightly caramelized and the pineapple is juicy. These skewers are perfect for summer barbecues or as a fun appetizer at your next gathering.

4. Ham and Swiss Sliders

Sliders are always a hit, and this ham and Swiss version is no exception. Slice Hawaiian rolls in half, layer sliced ham and Swiss cheese on top, and brush the tops with a mixture of melted butter, mustard, and poppy seeds. Bake until the cheese is melted and the rolls are golden brown. These mini sandwiches are great for parties or as a quick and easy weeknight dinner.

5. Ham and Broccoli Casserole

Combine leftover ham with steamed broccoli, cooked pasta, and a creamy cheese sauce for a delicious and filling casserole. Top with breadcrumbs and bake until bubbly and golden. This comforting dish is a great way to sneak in some veggies and make good use of that leftover ham.

6. Ham and Egg Breakfast Burrito

Start your day off right with a protein-packed ham and egg breakfast burrito. Scramble eggs with diced ham, cheese, and your favorite veggies like bell peppers or onions. Wrap the mixture in a warm tortilla and top with salsa or hot sauce for an extra kick. These burritos are portable and can be made ahead of time for busy mornings.

7. Ham and Potato Hash

Turn your leftover ham into a hearty and flavorful hash. Sauté diced ham with diced potatoes, onions, and bell peppers until everything is golden and crispy. Serve with a fried egg on top for a satisfying breakfast or brunch option.

8. Ham and Cheese Stuffed Chicken Breast

Elevate your chicken dinner by stuffing it with leftover ham and cheese. Make a pocket in the chicken breast and stuff it with diced ham and your favorite cheese. Season with salt, pepper, and herbs, then bake until the chicken is cooked through and the cheese is melted. Serve with roasted vegetables for a complete and impressive meal.

9. Ham and Spinach Pasta

Combine diced ham with cooked pasta, sautéed spinach, and a creamy garlic sauce for a quick and delicious pasta dish. The flavors of the ham and spinach complement each other perfectly, making this a satisfying and nutritious meal that can be ready in no time.

10. Ham and Apple Panini

Take your sandwich game to the next level with a ham and apple panini. Layer sliced ham, thinly sliced apples, and your favorite cheese on bread. Grill the sandwich in a panini press until the cheese is melted and the bread is toasted. The combination of savory ham and sweet apples is a match made in heaven.

There you have it – 10 delicious recipes to transform your leftover ham into culinary masterpieces. From soups and casseroles to sandwiches and pasta dishes, these recipes are sure to bring harmony to your kitchen and delight your taste buds. So next time you find yourself with extra ham, don’t let it go to waste. Embrace the possibilities and create something truly delicious. Bon appétit!
