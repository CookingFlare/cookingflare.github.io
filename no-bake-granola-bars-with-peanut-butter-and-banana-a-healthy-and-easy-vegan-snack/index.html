No-Bake Granola Bars with Peanut Butter and Banana: A Healthy and Easy Vegan Snack


Are you looking for a delicious and nutritious vegan snack that requires no baking? Look no further! These No-Bake Granola Bars with Peanut Butter and Banana are the perfect combination of sweet, salty, and crunchy, making them an ideal on-the-go snack or a quick energy boost during the day. Packed with wholesome ingredients, these bars are not only tasty but also provide a good source of fiber, healthy fats, and natural sugars. Plus, they are super easy to make!

Ingredients

  • 2 cups rolled oats
  • 1 cup pitted dates, soaked in warm water for 10 minutes and drained
  • 1 ripe banana, mashed
  • 1/2 cup natural peanut butter
  • 1/4 cup maple syrup
  • 1/4 cup chopped nuts (such as almonds or walnuts)
  • 1/4 cup dried fruit (such as cranberries or raisins)
  • 1/4 cup unsweetened shredded coconut
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract

Instructions

  1. In a large mixing bowl, combine the rolled oats, mashed banana, peanut butter, maple syrup, chopped nuts, dried fruit, shredded coconut, salt, and vanilla extract. Mix well until all the ingredients are evenly combined.

  2. Line a square baking dish with parchment paper, leaving some overhang on the sides for easy removal. This will make it easier to cut the bars later.

  3. Transfer the mixture into the lined baking dish and press it down firmly using the back of a spoon or your hands. Make sure the mixture is evenly spread and tightly packed.

  4. Place the baking dish in the refrigerator for at least 2 hours, or until the mixture has set and become firm.

  5. Once the bars are set, remove them from the refrigerator and lift them out of the baking dish using the parchment paper overhang. Place them on a cutting board.

  6. Using a sharp knife, slice the mixture into rectangular bars of your desired size. You can make them as small or as large as you like!

  7. Serve immediately or store the bars in an airtight container in the refrigerator for up to one week. These bars also freeze well, so you can make a big batch and enjoy them for longer.

Tips and Variations

  • Feel free to customize these granola bars to your liking by adding your favorite nuts, seeds, or spices. You can also drizzle melted dark chocolate over the top for an extra indulgent touch.
  • If you prefer a sweeter taste, you can add a tablespoon or two of honey or agave syrup to the mixture.
  • For an extra protein boost, you can add a scoop of your favorite plant-based protein powder to the mixture. Just make sure to adjust the amount of liquid ingredients accordingly.
  • These granola bars can be easily tailored to fit your dietary needs. They are naturally vegan, gluten-free, and dairy-free!

So, the next time you’re in need of a quick and healthy snack, give these No-Bake Granola Bars with Peanut Butter and Banana a try. They are not only simple to make but also packed with wholesome ingredients that will keep you satisfied and energized throughout the day. Enjoy!
