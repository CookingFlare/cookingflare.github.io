Spicy One Pot Cajun Chicken Pasta


If you’re a fan of bold flavors and easy weeknight dinners, then this Spicy One Pot Cajun Chicken Pasta is the perfect recipe for you. Packed with smoky Cajun spices, tender chicken, and creamy sauce, this dish will satisfy your cravings and impress your taste buds. Plus, it’s all cooked in one pot, making cleanup a breeze. Let’s get started!

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 pound boneless, skinless chicken breasts, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 1 tablespoon Cajun seasoning
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 1 bell pepper, diced
  • 3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 can (14.5 ounces) diced tomatoes
  • 1 cup chicken broth
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 8 ounces penne pasta
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • Fresh parsley, for garnish

Instructions

  1. Heat the olive oil in a large pot over medium-high heat. Add the chicken and sprinkle with Cajun seasoning. Cook until browned, about 5 minutes. Remove the chicken from the pot and set aside.

  2. In the same pot, add the diced onion, bell pepper, and minced garlic. Cook until the vegetables are softened, about 5 minutes.

  3. Add the diced tomatoes (including the liquid) to the pot and stir to combine. Bring the mixture to a simmer.

  4. Stir in the chicken broth and heavy cream. Bring the mixture back to a simmer.

  5. Add the penne pasta to the pot and stir to combine. Reduce the heat to medium-low and cover. Cook for about 15 minutes, or until the pasta is tender, stirring occasionally.

  6. Return the chicken to the pot and stir to combine. Cook for an additional 5 minutes, or until the chicken is heated through.

  7. Season with salt and pepper, to taste.

  8. Serve the Spicy One Pot Cajun Chicken Pasta hot, garnished with fresh parsley.

Why You’ll Love This Recipe

This Spicy One Pot Cajun Chicken Pasta is not only delicious but also incredibly convenient. With just one pot, you can have a flavorful and satisfying dinner on the table in no time. The smoky Cajun seasoning adds a kick of heat, while the creamy sauce balances it out perfectly. The combination of tender chicken, sautéed vegetables, and al dente pasta creates a harmonious blend of flavors and textures.

What sets this recipe apart is the use of Cajun seasoning. This spice blend typically includes paprika, cayenne pepper, garlic powder, and other aromatic ingredients. It adds depth and complexity to the dish, making it truly stand out.

Another great thing about this recipe is its versatility. You can easily customize it to suit your taste preferences. If you prefer a milder version, simply adjust the amount of Cajun seasoning used. You can also add other ingredients like shrimp or sausage to make it even heartier.

Not only is this Spicy One Pot Cajun Chicken Pasta delicious, but it’s also a great option for meal prep. You can easily double the recipe and have leftovers for the next day. It reheats well and tastes just as good the next day.

So, the next time you’re in the mood for a spicy and comforting meal, give this Spicy One Pot Cajun Chicken Pasta a try. It’s a crowd-pleaser that will leave you wanting more. Enjoy!
