Slow Cooker Mexican Shredded Chicken: Versatile and Delicious

4 min · 656 words

Are you looking for a delicious and easy-to-make recipe that can be used in a variety of dishes? Look no further than slow cooker Mexican shredded chicken! This recipe is a game-changer for anyone who loves Mexican cuisine, as it can be used to make tacos, burritos, quesadillas, and more.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1 can of diced tomatoes with green chilies
  • 1 can of black beans, drained and rinsed
  • 1 can of diced green chilies
  • 1 tablespoon of olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon of cumin
  • 1 teaspoon of chili powder
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • 1 cup of chicken broth
  • Optional: jalapenos, sour cream, shredded cheese, and cilantro for topping

Instructions

  1. Place the chicken breasts in the slow cooker.
  2. In a separate bowl, mix together the diced tomatoes with green chilies, black beans, diced green chilies, olive oil, cumin, chili powder, salt, and pepper.
  3. Pour the mixture over the chicken breasts in the slow cooker.
  4. Pour in the chicken broth and stir to combine.
  5. Cook on low for 6-8 hours or high for 3-4 hours.
  6. When the chicken is cooked through and shreds easily with a fork, it is done.
  7. Shred the chicken with two forks and return it to the slow cooker.
  8. Taste and adjust the seasoning as needed.
  9. Serve the chicken hot, garnished with your desired toppings.

Uses for Slow Cooker Mexican Shredded Chicken

This recipe is incredibly versatile and can be used in a variety of dishes. Here are a few ideas to get you started:

  • Tacos: Serve the shredded chicken in tacos with your favorite toppings, such as jalapenos, sour cream, shredded cheese, and cilantro.
  • Burritos: Use the shredded chicken to fill burritos with beans, cheese, and veggies.
  • Quesadillas: Fill tortillas with the shredded chicken, cheese, and peppers for a delicious and easy meal.
  • Salad: Add the shredded chicken to a salad with mixed greens, cherry tomatoes, and avocado for a healthy and tasty meal.
  • Stuffed peppers: Fill bell peppers with the shredded chicken, rice, and cheese for a flavorful and nutritious meal.

Tips and Variations

  • To add some heat to your dish, add some diced jalapenos or serrano peppers to the slow cooker with the chicken.
  • For extra flavor, add some chopped onions, garlic, or bell peppers to the slow cooker with the chicken.
  • To make this recipe more substantial, add some cooked rice, beans, or roasted vegetables to the slow cooker with the chicken.
  • For a spicy kick, add some cayenne pepper or red pepper flakes to the chicken as it cooks.

Conclusion

Slow cooker Mexican shredded chicken is a delicious and versatile recipe that can be used in a variety of dishes. With its mild flavor and tender texture, it’s a great option for anyone who loves Mexican cuisine. Try it out and see how you can incorporate it into your favorite meals!
