Grilling Scallops: Elegant Recipes for Seafood Lovers


If you’re a seafood enthusiast, then grilling scallops is a culinary delight you won’t want to miss. Scallops, with their delicate flavor and tender texture, are perfect for grilling, and they make an elegant addition to any meal. In this blog post, we’ll explore some exquisite recipes for grilling scallops that will elevate your seafood game and impress your guests.

Grilled Scallops with Lemon and Herb Butter

Ingredients:

  • Fresh large sea scallops
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • Zest of 1 lemon
  • 2 tablespoons fresh parsley, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your grill to medium-high heat.
  2. In a small bowl, combine the softened butter, lemon zest, chopped parsley, minced garlic, salt, and pepper. Mix until well combined.
  3. Pat the scallops dry with paper towels and season them with salt and pepper.
  4. Place the scallops on the preheated grill and cook for 2-3 minutes on each side, or until they are opaque and have grill marks.
  5. Once the scallops are cooked, transfer them to a serving platter and top each one with a generous dollop of the lemon and herb butter mixture.
  6. Serve immediately and enjoy the succulent flavors of the grilled scallops with the zesty, herb-infused butter.

Grilled Scallop and Mango Skewers

Ingredients:

  • Fresh large sea scallops
  • Ripe mango, cut into chunks
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons fresh lime juice
  • 1 teaspoon honey
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh cilantro, for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Soak wooden skewers in water for at least 30 minutes to prevent them from burning on the grill.
  2. Thread the scallops and mango chunks onto the skewers, alternating between the two.
  3. In a small bowl, whisk together the olive oil, lime juice, honey, ground cumin, salt, and pepper to create a marinade.
  4. Brush the marinade over the scallop and mango skewers, ensuring they are well coated.
  5. Preheat the grill to medium heat and place the skewers on the grill.
  6. Grill for 2-3 minutes on each side, or until the scallops are opaque and the mango is lightly caramelized.
  7. Once done, remove the skewers from the grill and garnish with fresh cilantro before serving.

Grilled Scallops with Bacon and Balsamic Glaze

Ingredients:

  • Fresh large sea scallops
  • Thinly sliced bacon
  • Balsamic glaze
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Toothpicks

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the grill to medium-high heat.
  2. Wrap each scallop with a slice of bacon and secure it with a toothpick.
  3. Season the bacon-wrapped scallops with salt and pepper.
  4. Place the scallops on the grill and cook for 3-4 minutes on each side, or until the bacon is crispy and the scallops are cooked through.
  5. Once done, transfer the scallops to a serving platter and drizzle them with balsamic glaze for a delightful sweet and tangy finish.

Grilling scallops opens up a world of culinary possibilities, allowing you to experiment with different flavors and textures. Whether you prefer the simplicity of lemon and herb butter, the exotic combination of scallops and mango, or the indulgent pairing of bacon and balsamic glaze, these recipes are sure to delight your taste buds and impress your guests. So fire up the grill, gather your ingredients, and get ready to savor the elegance of grilled scallops.
