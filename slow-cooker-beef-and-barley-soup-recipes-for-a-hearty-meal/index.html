Slow Cooker Beef and Barley Soup Recipes for a Hearty Meal


Are you looking for a comforting dish that will warm your soul and satisfy your hunger? Look no further than these delicious slow cooker beef and barley soup recipes! Packed with flavor, hearty ingredients, and cooked to perfection in a slow cooker, these recipes are guaranteed to become a family favorite. So, grab your apron and let’s get cooking!

Recipe 1: Classic Beef and Barley Soup

Ingredients:

  • 1.5 pounds beef stew meat, cubed
  • 1 cup barley
  • 4 carrots, sliced
  • 3 celery stalks, chopped
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 6 cups beef broth
  • 2 cups water
  • 2 bay leaves
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a skillet over medium heat, brown the beef stew meat until it’s nicely seared on all sides. This will help enhance the flavor of the soup.

  2. Transfer the beef to the slow cooker and add the barley, carrots, celery, onion, garlic, beef broth, water, bay leaves, dried thyme, salt, and pepper. Stir everything together to combine.

  3. Cover the slow cooker and cook on low for 8-10 hours or on high for 4-6 hours, until the beef is tender and the barley is cooked through.

  4. Once the soup is ready, remove the bay leaves and season with additional salt and pepper if needed.

  5. Serve the soup hot and garnish with fresh herbs or croutons for an extra touch of flavor.

Recipe 2: Spicy Beef and Barley Soup

Ingredients:

  • 1.5 pounds beef stew meat, cubed
  • 1 cup barley
  • 4 carrots, sliced
  • 3 celery stalks, chopped
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 6 cups beef broth
  • 2 cups water
  • 1 can diced tomatoes with green chilies
  • 2 teaspoons chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a skillet over medium heat, brown the beef stew meat until it’s nicely seared on all sides. This will help enhance the flavor of the soup.

  2. Transfer the beef to the slow cooker and add the barley, carrots, celery, onion, garlic, beef broth, water, diced tomatoes with green chilies, chili powder, paprika, cumin, salt, and pepper. Stir everything together to combine.

  3. Cover the slow cooker and cook on low for 8-10 hours or on high for 4-6 hours, until the beef is tender and the barley is cooked through.

  4. Once the soup is ready, taste and adjust the seasonings according to your preference.

  5. Serve the soup hot and garnish with a dollop of sour cream and a sprinkle of fresh cilantro for a burst of freshness.

Recipe 3: Mushroom and Beef Barley Soup

Ingredients:

  • 1.5 pounds beef stew meat, cubed
  • 1 cup barley
  • 8 ounces mushrooms, sliced
  • 4 carrots, sliced
  • 3 celery stalks, chopped
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 6 cups beef broth
  • 2 cups water
  • 1 tablespoon Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a skillet over medium heat, brown the beef stew meat until it’s nicely seared on all sides. This will help enhance the flavor of the soup.

  2. Transfer the beef to the slow cooker and add the barley, mushrooms, carrots, celery, onion, garlic, beef broth, water, Worcestershire sauce, dried thyme, salt, and pepper. Stir everything together to combine.

  3. Cover the slow cooker and cook on low for 8-10 hours or on high for 4-6 hours, until the beef is tender and the barley is cooked through.

  4. Once the soup is ready, adjust the seasonings according to your preference.

  5. Serve the soup hot and garnish with a sprinkle of freshly chopped parsley for a pop of color.

Conclusion

There you have it, three scrumptious slow cooker beef and barley soup recipes that are perfect for a hearty meal. Whether you prefer the classic version, a spicy kick, or the earthy flavors of mushrooms, these recipes offer something for everyone. Plus, the convenience of using a slow cooker allows you to have a delicious meal ready with minimal effort. So, why wait? Give these recipes a try and enjoy the comforting flavors of a homemade soup that will warm both your body and soul.
