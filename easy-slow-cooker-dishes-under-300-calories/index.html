Easy Slow Cooker Dishes Under 300 Calories


Are you tired of rummaging through your cupboards for ingredients to whip up a quick and healthy meal? Look no further! Slow cookers are a game-changer for busy individuals who want to enjoy nutritious food without the hassle of constant cooking. In this post, we'll delve into the world of easy slow cooker recipes that are not only mouth-watering but also pack under 300 calories per serving. The Importance of Slow Cooking Slow cooking is an incredibly versatile cooking method that allows you to prepare a variety of dishes with minimal effort. By cooking food over low heat for an extended period, you can break down tough fibers, infuse flavors, and retain essential nutrients. This technique is perfect for busy individuals, as it allows you to prepare meals ahead of time and come home to a delicious, ready-to-eat meal. 10 Easy Slow Cooker Dishes Under 300 Calories Here are ten mouth-watering slow cooker recipes that are under 300 calories per serving. Each dish is carefully crafted to maximize flavor while keeping calorie counts in check.

  1. Chicken Fajitas

Serves: 4-6 Calories: 252 Ingredients: 1 lb boneless chicken breasts 1 bell pepper, sliced 1 small onion, sliced 1 packet of fajita seasoning 6 small flour tortillas Optional toppings: avocado, sour cream, salsa, shredded cheese

Instructions: Place chicken, bell pepper, onion, and fajita seasoning in the slow cooker. Cook on low for 6-8 hours. Serve with warm tortillas and desired toppings.

  1. Vegetable Lentil Stew

Serves: 6-8 Calories: 238 Ingredients: 1 cup dried green or brown lentils 2 medium carrots, sliced 2 medium zucchinis, sliced 2 small potatoes, sliced 4 cups vegetable broth 1 can diced tomatoes 1 tsp dried thyme

Instructions: Combine lentils, carrots, zucchinis, potatoes, vegetable broth, diced tomatoes, and thyme in the slow cooker. Cook on low for 8-10 hours. Serve hot and garnish with fresh herbs.

  1. Chicken Tikka Masala

Serves: 4-6 Calories: 266 Ingredients: 1 lb boneless chicken breasts 1 cup plain Greek yogurt 2 tsp curry powder 1 can diced tomatoes 1 cup chicken broth 1 tsp garam masala

Instructions: Marinate chicken in yogurt, curry powder, and garam masala for at least 30 minutes. Place chicken in the slow cooker and surround with diced tomatoes and chicken broth. Cook on low for 4-6 hours. Serve over basmati rice and garnish with fresh cilantro.

  1. Black Bean Chili

Serves: 6-8 Calories: 278 Ingredients: 1 can black beans, drained and rinsed 1 medium onion, diced 2 cloves garlic, minced 2 cups diced tomatoes 1 cup vegetable broth 1 tsp cumin 1 tsp chili powder

Instructions: Cook onion and garlic in a pan until softened, then transfer to the slow cooker. Add black beans, diced tomatoes, vegetable broth, cumin, and chili powder. Cook on low for 6-8 hours. Serve hot, topped with desired toppings.

  1. Honey Garlic Shrimp

Serves: 2-4 Calories: 202 Ingredients: 1 lb large shrimp, peeled and deveined 2 cloves garlic, minced 1 tbsp honey 1 tbsp olive oil 1 tsp soy sauce

Instructions: Mix garlic, honey, olive oil, and soy sauce in a bowl. Place shrimp in the slow cooker and pour the garlicky mixture over them. Cook on low for 2-4 hours. Serve with steamed vegetables and garnish with chopped green onions.

  1. Kidney Bean Rice Bowl

Serves: 4-6 Calories: 270 Ingredients: 1 cup uncooked brown rice 2 cups water 1 can kidney beans, drained and rinsed 1 tsp ground cumin 1 tsp curry powder

Instructions: Cook rice according to package instructions. Mix kidney beans, cumin, and curry powder in a bowl. Place cooked rice and bean mixture in the slow cooker. Cook on low for 2-4 hours. Serve hot and garnish with chopped cilantro.

  1. Chicken and Quinoa Bowl

Serves: 4-6 Calories: 284 Ingredients: 1 lb boneless chicken breasts 1 cup uncooked quinoa 2 cups water 1 cup chopped fresh parsley 1 tsp lemon zest 1 tsp olive oil

Instructions: Cook quinoa according to package instructions. Place chicken, parsley, lemon zest, and olive oil in the slow cooker. Cook on low for 2-4 hours. Serve with cooked quinoa and garnish with chopped parsley.

  1. Spinach and Feta Stuffed Chicken

Serves: 4-6 Calories: 247 Ingredients: 1 lb boneless chicken breasts 1 cup frozen spinach, thawed and drained 1 cup crumbled feta cheese 1 cup chicken broth 1 tsp dried thyme Salt and pepper, to taste

Instructions: Mix spinach, feta cheese, chicken broth, and thyme in a bowl. Slice chicken breasts horizontally and fill with spinach mixture. Place chicken in the slow cooker and cook on low for 6-8 hours. Serve hot and garnish with chopped parsley.

  1. Jamaican Jerk Chicken

Serves: 4-6 Calories: 263 Ingredients: 1 lb boneless chicken breasts 2 tbsp jerk seasoning 1 onion, sliced 2 cloves garlic, minced 1 cup chicken broth

Instructions: Mix jerk seasoning, onion, and garlic in a bowl. Place chicken in the slow cooker and surround with jerk seasoning mixture and chicken broth. Cook on low for 6-8 hours. Serve hot and garnish with chopped cilantro.

  1. Vegetable Curry

Serves: 6-8 Calories: 278 Ingredients: 2 cups mixed vegetables (carrots, zucchinis, potatoes, and bell peppers) 1 can diced tomatoes 2 cups vegetable broth 1 tsp curry powder 1 tsp turmeric Salt and pepper, to taste

Instructions: Combine mixed vegetables, diced tomatoes, vegetable broth, curry powder, and turmeric in the slow cooker. Cook on low for 8-10 hours. Serve hot and garnish with chopped fresh cilantro.

Tips and Variations To personalize your slow cooker recipes, try the following variations:

Add protein: Experiment with adding chicken, beef, pork, tofu, or tempeh to your dishes for added protein. Spice it up: Don't be afraid to add more spices, herbs, or seasonings to give your dishes a unique flavor. Veggie power: Choose your favorite vegetables and add them to the slow cooker for added nutrition. Make it a meal prep: Prepare individual portions of your slow cooker recipes and store them in the fridge or freezer for a quick and easy meal.

By incorporating these easy slow cooker recipes into your diet, you'll enjoy nutritious meals with minimal effort. So why not give these recipes a try and discover the delicious world of slow cooking?
