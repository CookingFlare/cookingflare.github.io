Tasty Dairy-Free Mediterranean Pasta Salad


When it comes to refreshing summer meals, a colorful and flavorful pasta salad is always a winner. This dairy-free Mediterranean pasta salad is not only delicious but also packed with the vibrant flavors of the Mediterranean region. Whether you’re looking for a satisfying lunch, a side dish for a barbecue, or a light dinner option, this recipe has got you covered.

Ingredients

  • 12 ounces of fusilli pasta (or any pasta of your choice)
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1 cup cucumber, diced
  • 1/2 cup Kalamata olives, pitted and halved
  • 1/4 cup red onion, thinly sliced
  • 1/4 cup fresh parsley, chopped
  • 1/4 cup fresh mint, chopped
  • 1/3 cup extra virgin olive oil
  • 1/4 cup red wine vinegar
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Cook the Pasta: Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Add the pasta and cook according to the package instructions until al dente. Drain the pasta and rinse it under cold water to stop the cooking process. Transfer the pasta to a large mixing bowl.

  2. Prepare the Dressing: In a small bowl, whisk together the extra virgin olive oil, red wine vinegar, minced garlic, dried oregano, salt, and pepper. This zesty dressing will infuse the pasta salad with Mediterranean flavors.

  3. Combine the Ingredients: Add the cherry tomatoes, cucumber, Kalamata olives, red onion, fresh parsley, and fresh mint to the bowl with the cooked pasta. Gently toss the ingredients together to distribute them evenly.

  4. Add the Dressing: Pour the prepared dressing over the pasta salad and toss everything together until the salad is well coated with the dressing.

  5. Chill and Serve: Cover the bowl with plastic wrap and refrigerate the pasta salad for at least 1 hour to allow the flavors to meld together. Before serving, give the salad a final toss and adjust the seasoning if necessary.

Tips for Customization

  • Protein Boost: If you’d like to add some protein to the salad, consider incorporating grilled chicken, chickpeas, or marinated tofu.
  • Extra Vegetables: Feel free to customize the salad by adding roasted red peppers, artichoke hearts, or sun-dried tomatoes for a burst of additional flavors.
  • Dairy-Free Cheese: If you’re a fan of dairy-free cheese, crumbled tofu feta or a sprinkle of nutritional yeast can be a delightful addition to this pasta salad.

Serving and Storage

This dairy-free Mediterranean pasta salad is best enjoyed fresh, but it can be stored in an airtight container in the refrigerator for up to 2 days. Just give it a good toss before serving the leftovers to freshen it up.

Final Thoughts

With its vibrant colors, refreshing flavors, and simple preparation, this dairy-free Mediterranean pasta salad is a versatile dish that’s perfect for any occasion. Whether you’re planning a picnic, a potluck, or simply want to elevate your everyday meals, this pasta salad is sure to impress. Embrace the essence of the Mediterranean with every forkful of this delightful, dairy-free creation.
