Creamy Low Carb Tomato Basil Soup


Are you craving a comforting and delicious soup that won’t derail your low-carb lifestyle? Look no further than this creamy low carb tomato basil soup! Packed with rich flavors and velvety textures, this soup is a perfect choice for a cozy night in or a quick and satisfying meal. Best of all, it’s simple to make and can be customized to suit your taste preferences. Let’s dive into the recipe and discover the magic of low-carb indulgence.

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 medium onion, chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 14-ounce cans of whole peeled tomatoes
  • 1 cup chicken or vegetable broth
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • 1/4 cup fresh basil, chopped
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Optional: grated Parmesan cheese for garnish

Instructions

  1. Sauté the Aromatics: In a large pot, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the chopped onion and sauté until translucent, then add the minced garlic and cook for another minute until fragrant.

  2. Simmer the Tomatoes: Add the whole peeled tomatoes to the pot, breaking them up with a spoon. Allow the tomatoes to simmer for about 10 minutes, allowing the flavors to meld together.

  3. Blend the Mixture: Using an immersion blender or transferring the mixture to a blender, puree the tomato mixture until smooth. Be cautious with hot liquids in a blender and blend in batches if necessary.

  4. Add the Broth and Cream: Return the pureed mixture to the pot if using a blender, then stir in the chicken or vegetable broth. Allow the soup to come to a gentle simmer, then reduce the heat and stir in the heavy cream until fully incorporated.

  5. Season and Garnish: Add the chopped basil to the soup and stir to combine. Season with salt and pepper to taste, and simmer for an additional 5 minutes to allow the flavors to come together. Serve the soup hot, garnished with a sprinkle of grated Parmesan cheese if desired.

Customization Options

  • Spice it Up: If you enjoy a bit of heat, consider adding a pinch of red pepper flakes or a dash of hot sauce to the soup for a spicy kick.

  • Vegetable Add-Ins: Feel free to add diced zucchini, bell peppers, or spinach to the soup for added texture and nutrients.

  • Protein Boost: Enhance the soup’s heartiness by stirring in cooked and shredded chicken or crispy bacon pieces.

Benefits of Low Carb Tomato Basil Soup

This creamy low carb tomato basil soup offers more than just a delicious taste. With the absence of starchy ingredients, it’s a great choice for those following a low-carb or keto lifestyle. The healthy fats from the olive oil and heavy cream add richness to the soup while keeping the carb count low. Additionally, the antioxidant-rich tomatoes and fragrant basil provide essential nutrients and vibrant flavors.

Final Thoughts

Whether you’re seeking a soul-warming meal on a chilly evening or a satisfying lunch option, this creamy low carb tomato basil soup checks all the boxes. Its simplicity and versatility make it a staple in any low-carb kitchen. So, grab a bowl, savor the velvety texture, and indulge in every spoonful of this comforting creation.

Enjoy your creamy low carb tomato basil soup, and let its nourishing warmth envelop you in pure delight.
