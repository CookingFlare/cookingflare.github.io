Vegan Strawberry Shortcake: A Light and Refreshing Dessert


As the weather warms up and the days grow longer, there’s something undeniably refreshing about a dessert that combines sweet fruit, light and fluffy layers, and a smooth, creamy texture. If you’re looking for a dessert that’s both satisfying and cruelty-free, vegan strawberry shortcake is the perfect choice. This twist on the classic dessert not only embraces the natural sweetness of strawberries but also provides a light, dairy-free alternative that everyone will enjoy.

In this post, we’ll guide you step-by-step through making a delicious vegan strawberry shortcake, highlighting the ingredients, preparation, and tips for getting the perfect texture and taste. Whether you’re hosting a summer gathering or simply indulging in a comforting treat, this dessert will leave your guests delighted. So, let’s dive into this wonderful recipe!

Why Choose Vegan Strawberry Shortcake?

Vegan strawberry shortcake is an excellent option for those looking to reduce their animal product consumption without compromising on taste. This dessert is made without dairy, eggs, or any animal-derived ingredients, yet it’s just as delicious (if not more!) than the traditional version. With fresh, juicy strawberries as the star, it’s the perfect way to enjoy a light, seasonal dessert that’s both healthy and indulgent.

Health Benefits of Vegan Strawberry Shortcake

While indulgent, this vegan version of strawberry shortcake can still be a healthier alternative to the traditional recipe. By swapping out heavy dairy products for plant-based ingredients, you’re reducing the overall fat content. Strawberries, one of the key ingredients, are full of antioxidants, vitamins, and fiber, making this dessert a guilt-free treat. Plus, using plant-based milk and cream options ensures that it’s dairy-free and suitable for those who are lactose intolerant or following a plant-based lifestyle.

Ingredients for Vegan Strawberry Shortcake

Making vegan strawberry shortcake is a breeze with the right ingredients. The following are the essential components of this dessert:

For the Strawberry Topping:

  • Fresh Strawberries: 4 cups, hulled and sliced
  • Sugar: 2 tablespoons (optional, depending on the sweetness of the strawberries)
  • Lemon Juice: 1 tablespoon (for extra brightness)

For the Vegan Shortcake Biscuits:

  • All-Purpose Flour: 2 cups
  • Baking Powder: 2 teaspoons
  • Baking Soda: 1/2 teaspoon
  • Sugar: 1/4 cup (optional)
  • Salt: 1/2 teaspoon
  • Vegan Butter: 1/2 cup (cold and cut into cubes)
  • Unsweetened Almond Milk (or any plant-based milk): 3/4 cup
  • Apple Cider Vinegar: 1 tablespoon (to activate the baking soda)
  • Vanilla Extract: 1 teaspoon

For the Vegan Whipped Cream:

  • Coconut Cream: 1 can (refrigerated overnight, scoop out the thick cream)
  • Powdered Sugar: 2 tablespoons
  • Vanilla Extract: 1 teaspoon

Instructions for Making Vegan Strawberry Shortcake

Step 1: Prepare the Strawberry Topping

The first step in making your vegan strawberry shortcake is preparing the strawberries. This gives the fruit time to release its juices and become syrupy, adding to the delicious flavor of your dessert.

  1. Hull and slice the strawberries into thin slices. Place them in a bowl.
  2. Sprinkle the sliced strawberries with sugar and drizzle the lemon juice on top. Toss everything together gently to combine.
  3. Cover the bowl and let it sit at room temperature for about 30 minutes. During this time, the strawberries will release their natural juices, creating a sweet, syrupy coating.

Step 2: Make the Vegan Shortcake Biscuits

Now that the strawberries are macerating, you can move on to the biscuits, which form the base of your shortcake.

  1. Preheat your oven to 425°F (220°C) and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
  2. In a large mixing bowl, whisk together the flour, baking powder, baking soda, sugar (if using), and salt.
  3. Add the cold vegan butter cubes to the flour mixture. Using a pastry cutter or your fingertips, work the butter into the flour until the mixture resembles coarse crumbs. If you don’t have a pastry cutter, you can also use a fork or your hands. The key is to incorporate the butter evenly without melting it.
  4. In a small bowl, combine the almond milk and apple cider vinegar. Let it sit for a minute to thicken slightly.
  5. Pour the wet ingredients into the flour mixture and stir gently until just combined. Be careful not to overmix, as this can lead to dense biscuits.
  6. Turn the dough out onto a floured surface and gently knead it a few times until it comes together. Pat the dough into a 1-inch thick rectangle.
  7. Use a round biscuit cutter or a glass to cut out biscuits, placing them on the prepared baking sheet.
  8. Bake the biscuits for about 12-15 minutes or until they’re golden brown on top and a toothpick comes out clean when inserted into the center.
  9. Once baked, remove the biscuits from the oven and allow them to cool slightly.

Step 3: Make the Vegan Whipped Cream

While the biscuits are cooling, it’s time to prepare the vegan whipped cream. This easy and decadent topping is made with coconut cream for a rich, creamy texture.

  1. Open the can of chilled coconut cream and scoop out the thick, solidified cream (you can discard or save the coconut water for another use).
  2. Transfer the coconut cream to a mixing bowl and beat it with a hand mixer or stand mixer until it becomes light and fluffy. This should take about 3-5 minutes.
  3. Add the powdered sugar and vanilla extract, continuing to beat until well combined and smooth.

Step 4: Assemble the Vegan Strawberry Shortcake

Now that everything is ready, it’s time to assemble your vegan strawberry shortcake!

  1. Slice the cooled shortcake biscuits in half horizontally.
  2. Spoon a generous amount of the macerated strawberries onto the bottom half of each biscuit, ensuring that the syrup from the strawberries soaks into the biscuit slightly.
  3. Add a dollop of whipped coconut cream on top of the strawberries.
  4. Place the top half of the biscuit on top, pressing gently to create a sandwich-like structure.
  5. If desired, add extra strawberries and whipped cream on top for a more indulgent presentation.

Serve immediately for the best experience, and enjoy the fresh, light flavors of this vegan strawberry shortcake!

Tips for the Perfect Vegan Strawberry Shortcake

To make sure your vegan strawberry shortcake turns out perfectly every time, consider these helpful tips:

  • Fresh Strawberries are Key: Using fresh, ripe strawberries is crucial for the best flavor. If strawberries are out of season, you can also use frozen strawberries, but make sure to thaw and drain them properly.
  • Chill the Coconut Cream: Refrigerating the coconut cream overnight ensures that it solidifies and becomes easier to whip.
  • Don’t Overmix the Biscuit Dough: Overworking the dough can result in tough biscuits, so be gentle and mix until just combined.
  • Customize the Sweetness: If you prefer a less sweet shortcake, reduce the sugar in the biscuits or whipped cream. The strawberries themselves provide a natural sweetness.
  • Try Different Milk Options: While almond milk is a great option for vegan shortcakes, feel free to use other plant-based milk like oat milk, soy milk, or cashew milk for a slightly different flavor.

Variations on the Classic Vegan Strawberry Shortcake

While the classic vegan strawberry shortcake is delicious on its own, you can experiment with different flavors and ingredients to create your own variations. Here are some ideas:

  1. Add Other Berries: In addition to strawberries, consider adding blueberries, raspberries, or blackberries to the topping for a mixed berry shortcake.
  2. Coconut Shortcake: Add shredded coconut to the biscuit dough for a coconut-flavored variation that complements the coconut cream.
  3. Chocolate Lovers: For a rich twist, drizzle melted dark chocolate over the whipped cream or mix cocoa powder into the biscuit dough.
  4. Lemon Zest: Add a touch of lemon zest to the whipped cream or biscuit dough to infuse a refreshing citrus flavor.

Conclusion

Vegan strawberry shortcake is a perfect dessert for warm weather, offering a light and refreshing treat that’s both indulgent and healthy. The combination of sweet strawberries, flaky vegan biscuits, and creamy coconut whipped cream creates a dessert that’s as delightful to look at as it is to eat. Whether you’re preparing it for a special occasion or enjoying it on a sunny afternoon, this dessert is sure to satisfy everyone at the table. With its simple ingredients and easy preparation, it’s a dessert that you’ll come back to again and again. Enjoy!
