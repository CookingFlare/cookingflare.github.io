Irresistible Chocolate Hazelnut Muffins: A Nutty Morning Treat


There’s nothing quite like starting your morning with a warm, freshly baked muffin. And when that muffin is infused with the irresistible combination of rich chocolate and crunchy hazelnuts, you know you’re in for a treat. These Chocolate Hazelnut Muffins are the perfect way to indulge your taste buds and kickstart your day with a burst of flavor. So, let’s get baking!

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup cocoa powder
  • 1 tablespoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, melted
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 cup chopped hazelnuts

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C). Line a muffin tin with paper liners or grease it lightly with butter.

  2. In a large bowl, whisk together the flour, cocoa powder, baking powder, and salt. Set aside.

  3. In another bowl, cream together the melted butter and granulated sugar until light and fluffy. You can use a hand mixer or do this by hand.

  4. Add the eggs, one at a time, beating well after each addition. Stir in the vanilla extract.

  5. Gradually add the dry ingredients to the wet ingredients, alternating with the milk. Begin and end with the dry ingredients, stirring just until combined. Be careful not to overmix, as this can result in dense muffins.

  6. Gently fold in the chopped hazelnuts, reserving a handful for topping the muffins.

  7. Spoon the batter into the prepared muffin tin, filling each cup about three-quarters full. Sprinkle the reserved hazelnuts on top of each muffin.

  8. Bake for 18-20 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center of a muffin comes out clean. Allow the muffins to cool in the tin for a few minutes before transferring them to a wire rack to cool completely.

  9. Serve these delectable Chocolate Hazelnut Muffins warm or at room temperature. They’re perfect on their own, but you can also enjoy them with a dollop of whipped cream or a drizzle of chocolate sauce.

Tips and Variations

  • To enhance the nutty flavor, toast the hazelnuts before chopping them. Simply spread them on a baking sheet and bake at 350°F (175°C) for about 10 minutes, or until fragrant. Let them cool before using.

  • If you prefer a more intense chocolate flavor, you can use dark cocoa powder instead of regular cocoa powder.

  • Add a twist of citrus by incorporating the zest of an orange or lemon into the batter. This will add a refreshing element to the rich chocolate and nutty combination.

  • For an extra touch of indulgence, you can top each muffin with a drizzle of melted chocolate or a dusting of powdered sugar.

  • These muffins can be stored in an airtight container at room temperature for up to three days. To enjoy them warm, simply pop them in the microwave for a few seconds before serving.

Enjoy the delightful combination of chocolate and hazelnuts with these irresistible Chocolate Hazelnut Muffins. They’re perfect for breakfast, brunch, or even as a sweet treat any time of the day. So, grab your apron, preheat your oven, and let the aroma of these delicious muffins fill your kitchen. Happy baking!
