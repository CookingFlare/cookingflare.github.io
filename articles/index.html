BBQ Shrimp Tacos: Easy and Flavorful Recipes

If you’re a fan of tacos and seafood, then you’re in for a treat! BBQ shrimp tacos are the perfect combination of smoky flavors, succulent shrimp, and fresh toppings. They’re easy to make, bursting with flavor, and guaranteed to impress your friends and family. So, let’s dive right into these mouthwatering recipes! Classic BBQ Shrimp Tacos Ingredients: 1 pound large shrimp, peeled and deveined 2 tablespoons olive oil 2 cloves garlic, minced 1 teaspoon paprika 1/2 teaspoon cayenne pepper Salt and pepper, to taste 8 small corn tortillas Toppings: shredded lettuce, diced tomatoes, chopped cilantro, lime wedges Instructions: Preheat your grill or stovetop grill pan to medium-high heat. In a bowl, combine the olive oil, minced garlic, paprika, cayenne pepper, salt, and pepper. Mix well. Add the shrimp to the bowl and toss until they are evenly coated with the marinade. Thread the shrimp onto skewers, piercing them through the tail and head to keep them secure. Grill the shrimp for 2-3 minutes on each side, until they turn pink and opaque. Warm the corn tortillas on the grill for about 30 seconds on each side. Remove the shrimp from the skewers and divide them evenly among the tortillas. Top the shrimp with shredded lettuce, diced tomatoes, chopped cilantro, and a squeeze of fresh lime juice. Serve the BBQ shrimp tacos immediately and enjoy! Spicy Chipotle BBQ Shrimp Tacos Ingredients: 1 pound large shrimp, peeled and deveined 2 tablespoons olive oil 2 cloves garlic, minced 1 chipotle pepper in adobo sauce, minced 1 tablespoon adobo sauce (from the can of chipotle peppers) 1 tablespoon lime juice Salt and pepper, to taste 8 small flour tortillas Toppings: sliced avocado, thinly sliced red onion, chopped fresh cilantro Instructions: Preheat your grill or stovetop grill pan to medium-high heat. In a bowl, combine the olive oil, minced garlic, minced chipotle pepper, adobo sauce, lime juice, salt, and pepper. Mix well. Add the shrimp to the bowl and toss until they are evenly coated with the marinade. Thread the shrimp onto skewers, piercing them through the tail and head to keep them secure. Grill the shrimp for 2-3 minutes on each side, until they turn pink and opaque. Warm the flour tortillas on the grill for about 30 seconds on each side. Remove the shrimp from the skewers and divide them evenly among the tortillas. Top the shrimp with sliced avocado, thinly sliced red onion, and chopped fresh cilantro. Serve the spicy chipotle BBQ shrimp tacos immediately and enjoy! Sweet and Tangy Pineapple BBQ Shrimp Tacos Ingredients: 1 pound large shrimp, peeled and deveined 2 tablespoons olive oil 2 cloves garlic, minced 1 cup pineapple juice 2 tablespoons soy sauce 2 tablespoons honey Salt and pepper, to taste 8 small flour tortillas Toppings: diced pineapple, sliced jalapenos, chopped green onions Instructions: Preheat your grill or stovetop grill pan to medium-high heat. In a bowl, combine the olive oil, minced garlic, pineapple juice, soy sauce, honey, salt, and pepper. Mix well. Add the shrimp to the bowl and toss until they are evenly coated with the marinade. Thread the shrimp onto skewers, piercing them through the tail and head to keep them secure. Grill the shrimp for 2-3 minutes on each side, until they turn pink and opaque. Warm the flour tortillas on the grill for about 30 seconds on each side. Remove the shrimp from the skewers and divide them evenly among the tortillas. Top the shrimp with diced pineapple, sliced jalapenos, and chopped green onions. Serve the sweet and tangy pineapple BBQ shrimp tacos immediately and enjoy! Conclusion BBQ shrimp tacos are a delicious twist on the classic taco dish. Whether you prefer the classic version, the spicy chipotle variation, or the sweet and tangy pineapple twist, these recipes are sure to please your taste buds.
