Prosciutto and Arugula Pizza: A Classic Italian Combination


If you’re a fan of Italian cuisine, then you’re in for a treat with this delicious Prosciutto and Arugula Pizza recipe. The combination of the salty prosciutto and the peppery arugula creates a harmonious blend of flavors that will leave your taste buds begging for more. In this blog post, we’ll walk you through the step-by-step process of creating this classic Italian dish. So, roll up your sleeves and get ready to embark on a culinary journey!

Ingredients

To make this mouthwatering Prosciutto and Arugula Pizza, you’ll need the following ingredients:

  • 1 pizza dough (store-bought or homemade)
  • 1/4 cup tomato sauce
  • 8 slices of prosciutto
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1 cup fresh arugula
  • Extra virgin olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 450°F (230°C). If you have a pizza stone, place it in the oven while it preheats to ensure a crispy crust.

  2. Roll out the pizza dough on a floured surface to your desired thickness. Transfer the rolled-out dough onto a baking sheet or pizza peel lined with parchment paper.

  3. Spread the tomato sauce evenly over the dough, leaving a small border around the edges for the crust.

  4. Sprinkle the shredded mozzarella cheese evenly over the sauce.

  5. Tear the slices of prosciutto into smaller pieces and distribute them over the cheese.

  6. Season with salt and pepper to taste.

  7. Carefully transfer the pizza onto the preheated pizza stone or directly onto the oven rack.

  8. Bake for approximately 12-15 minutes, or until the crust is golden brown and the cheese is melted and bubbly.

  9. Once the pizza is out of the oven, let it cool for a few minutes. This will make it easier to handle and prevent any burns.

  10. In the meantime, toss the fresh arugula with a drizzle of extra virgin olive oil in a mixing bowl. This will help to enhance the flavor of the arugula and give it a glossy appearance.

  11. Once the pizza has cooled slightly, evenly distribute the dressed arugula over the top.

  12. Slice the pizza into desired portions and serve immediately.

Tips and Variations

  • For a gourmet twist, drizzle some balsamic glaze over the arugula before serving. The sweet and tangy flavors of the glaze will complement the salty prosciutto perfectly.

  • If you’re a cheese lover, sprinkle some crumbled goat cheese or feta cheese over the pizza before baking. This will add an extra layer of creaminess and tanginess to the dish.

  • For an added crunch, sprinkle some toasted pine nuts or walnuts over the arugula.

  • Feel free to experiment with different types of cheese. Gorgonzola, fontina, or even a blend of cheeses can take this pizza to the next level of deliciousness.

Conclusion

Prosciutto and Arugula Pizza is a classic Italian combination that never fails to impress. The delicate flavors of the prosciutto, paired with the peppery arugula, create a well-balanced and satisfying dish. Whether you’re hosting a dinner party or simply craving a taste of Italy, this pizza recipe is sure to please. So, gather your ingredients, fire up that oven, and indulge in a slice of pizza perfection. Buon appetito!
