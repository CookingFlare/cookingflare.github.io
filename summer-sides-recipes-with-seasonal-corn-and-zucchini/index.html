Summer Sides: Recipes with Seasonal Corn and Zucchini


As summer rolls in with its abundance of fresh produce, there’s no better time to explore delicious sides that highlight the flavors of the season. Corn and zucchini, two staples of summer gardens and farmers’ markets, offer endless possibilities for creating vibrant and nutritious dishes that complement any meal. Whether you’re hosting a backyard barbecue or simply enjoying a weeknight dinner al fresco, these recipes are sure to elevate your summer dining experience.

Grilled Corn Salad with Avocado and Lime Dressing

Ingredients:

  • 4 ears of corn, husked
  • 1 avocado, diced
  • 1 pint cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1/4 cup red onion, finely chopped
  • 1/4 cup fresh cilantro, chopped
  • Juice of 2 limes
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat grill to medium-high heat.
  2. Grill the corn until kernels are slightly charred, turning occasionally, about 10 minutes. Let cool.
  3. Cut kernels off the cob and place in a large bowl.
  4. Add diced avocado, cherry tomatoes, red onion, and cilantro to the bowl with corn.
  5. In a small bowl, whisk together lime juice, olive oil, salt, and pepper.
  6. Pour dressing over the salad and toss gently to combine.
  7. Serve immediately or refrigerate until ready to serve.

This Grilled Corn Salad is a refreshing side bursting with flavors of sweet corn, creamy avocado, and tangy lime—a perfect accompaniment to grilled meats or seafood.

Zucchini Fritters with Herb Yogurt Sauce

Ingredients:

  • 2 medium zucchinis, grated
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 egg, lightly beaten
  • 2 tablespoons chopped fresh dill
  • 2 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • Vegetable oil for frying

Herb Yogurt Sauce:

  • 1 cup Greek yogurt
  • 1 tablespoon chopped fresh dill
  • 1 tablespoon chopped fresh parsley
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Place grated zucchini in a colander and sprinkle with salt. Let sit for 10 minutes, then squeeze out excess moisture using a clean kitchen towel.
  2. In a large bowl, combine grated zucchini, flour, Parmesan cheese, egg, dill, parsley, and garlic. Mix until well combined.
  3. Heat vegetable oil in a large skillet over medium heat.
  4. Drop spoonfuls of zucchini mixture into the skillet, flattening with a spatula to form fritters. Cook until golden brown and crispy, about 3-4 minutes per side. Transfer to a paper towel-lined plate.
  5. In a small bowl, mix together Greek yogurt, dill, parsley, lemon juice, salt, and pepper to make the herb yogurt sauce.
  6. Serve zucchini fritters warm with herb yogurt sauce on the side.

These Zucchini Fritters are a delightful way to enjoy summer squash, with a crispy exterior and tender interior complemented by the cool and creamy herb yogurt sauce.

Corn and Zucchini Casserole

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups fresh corn kernels (about 3-4 ears)
  • 2 medium zucchinis, diced
  • 1/2 cup diced red bell pepper
  • 1/2 cup diced onion
  • 1 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1/2 cup mayonnaise
  • 1/2 cup sour cream
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/4 teaspoon garlic powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 cup breadcrumbs
  • 2 tablespoons butter, melted

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease a 9x13-inch baking dish.
  2. In a large bowl, combine corn kernels, diced zucchini, red bell pepper, onion, cheddar cheese, mayonnaise, sour cream, Parmesan cheese, garlic powder, salt, and pepper.
  3. Spread mixture evenly into the prepared baking dish.
  4. In a small bowl, mix breadcrumbs with melted butter. Sprinkle evenly over the top of the casserole.
  5. Bake for 30-35 minutes, or until bubbly and golden brown on top.
  6. Remove from oven and let cool slightly before serving.

This Corn and Zucchini Casserole is a comforting and cheesy dish that showcases the sweetness of corn and the mild flavor of zucchini, perfect for family gatherings or potluck dinners.

Grilled Zucchini with Parmesan and Basil

Ingredients:

  • 4 medium zucchinis, sliced lengthwise into 1/4-inch thick strips
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh basil

Instructions:

  1. Preheat grill to medium-high heat.
  2. Brush zucchini slices with olive oil and season with salt and pepper.
  3. Grill zucchini slices until tender and slightly charred, about 3-4 minutes per side.
  4. Remove from grill and place on a serving platter.
  5. Sprinkle grilled zucchini with grated Parmesan cheese and chopped fresh basil.
  6. Serve immediately as a flavorful side dish or appetizer.

These Grilled Zucchini slices are enhanced by the nutty Parmesan cheese and aromatic basil, making them a simple yet elegant addition to any summer meal.

Conclusion

Explore the bounty of summer with these flavorful recipes featuring seasonal corn and zucchini. Whether you prefer the sweetness of corn in a refreshing salad or the versatility of zucchini in crispy fritters and comforting casseroles, these dishes are sure to delight your taste buds. Embrace the freshness of summer produce and savor these sides alongside grilled meats, seafood, or as standalone vegetarian delights. Enjoy the flavors of the season with every bite and make the most of summer’s vibrant offerings. Cheers to delicious summer dining!
