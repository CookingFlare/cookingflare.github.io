Irresistible Peanut Butter Cup Cheesecake: A Match Made in Heaven


If there’s one dessert that can bring pure joy to any occasion, it’s a decadent cheesecake. And when you combine the creamy richness of cheesecake with the irresistible allure of peanut butter cups, you get a dessert that is truly a match made in heaven. This recipe for Irresistible Peanut Butter Cup Cheesecake is a delightful fusion of two classic treats, guaranteed to leave your taste buds singing with delight.

Ingredients

For the Crust:

  • 1 1/2 cups chocolate cookie crumbs
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, melted

For the Filling:

  • 24 oz cream cheese, softened
  • 1 cup creamy peanut butter
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 3 large eggs
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1/2 cup sour cream
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • 12 peanut butter cups, chopped

For the Topping:

  • 1/2 cup semi-sweet chocolate chips
  • 1/4 cup heavy cream
  • 6 peanut butter cups, halved

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Crust:

    • Preheat the oven to 325°F (165°C).
    • In a medium bowl, combine the chocolate cookie crumbs, sugar, and melted butter. Press the mixture into the bottom of a 9-inch springform pan. Bake for 10 minutes, then set aside to cool.

  2. Make the Filling:

    • In a large bowl, beat the cream cheese and peanut butter until smooth. Add the sugar and beat until combined.
    • Add the eggs, one at a time, beating well after each addition.
    • Stir in the vanilla extract, sour cream, and heavy cream until the mixture is smooth and creamy.
    • Gently fold in the chopped peanut butter cups.

  3. Assemble the Cheesecake:

    • Pour the filling over the prepared crust and smooth the top with a spatula.
    • Place the springform pan on a large piece of aluminum foil and fold the foil up around the sides of the pan. This will prevent water from seeping into the cheesecake during the baking process.
    • Place the wrapped springform pan in a large roasting pan. Fill the roasting pan with hot water until it reaches halfway up the sides of the springform pan. This water bath will ensure the cheesecake bakes evenly and stays creamy.

  4. Bake and Chill:

    • Bake the cheesecake in the preheated oven for 1 hour and 15 minutes, or until the edges are set and the center is slightly jiggly.
    • Turn off the oven and let the cheesecake cool in the oven with the door propped open for 1 hour.
    • Remove the cheesecake from the oven and run a knife around the edge of the pan to loosen the cheesecake.
    • Refrigerate the cheesecake for at least 4 hours or overnight to set.

  5. Add the Topping:

    • In a small saucepan, heat the heavy cream until it just begins to simmer.
    • Remove from heat and add the chocolate chips. Let it sit for a minute, then stir until smooth.
    • Pour the chocolate ganache over the chilled cheesecake and spread it evenly.
    • Arrange the halved peanut butter cups on top of the ganache.

  6. Serve and Enjoy:

    • Carefully remove the cheesecake from the springform pan before slicing and serving.
    • Each luscious bite of this Irresistible Peanut Butter Cup Cheesecake is a delightful harmony of velvety cheesecake, creamy peanut butter, and rich chocolate, all coming together to create a symphony of flavors that will leave your guests in awe.

Whether it’s a special celebration or a simple gathering with loved ones, this Peanut Butter Cup Cheesecake is a show-stopping dessert that will undoubtedly make a lasting impression. The marriage of silky cheesecake and indulgent peanut butter cups is a match made in dessert heaven, and this recipe is your ticket to experiencing pure bliss with every forkful. So, gather your ingredients, preheat your oven, and get ready to create a dessert masterpiece that will elevate any occasion to a whole new level of sweetness.
