Meat Lovers Unite: Carnivorous Recipes for Two


Are you and your significant other meat lovers through and through? Do you revel in the sizzle of a perfectly seared steak or the aroma of a slowly roasted rack of ribs? If the answer is a resounding “yes,” then you’ve come to the right place. In this carnivorous culinary adventure, we’ll explore a delightful array of meat-centric recipes designed for two, perfect for those intimate dinner dates or cozy nights in. From succulent steaks to tantalizing lamb chops, get ready to indulge in a symphony of flavors that will leave you and your partner craving for more.

Appetizers

1. Bacon-Wrapped Scallops

Prep Time: 10 minutes | Cook Time: 15 minutes | Total Time: 25 minutes

Ingredients:

  • 8 sea scallops
  • 8 slices of bacon
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 400°F (200°C).
  2. Wrap each scallop with a slice of bacon and secure with a toothpick.
  3. Drizzle olive oil over the bacon-wrapped scallops and season with salt and pepper.
  4. Place the scallops on a baking sheet and bake for 15 minutes, or until the bacon is crispy and the scallops are cooked through.
  5. Serve hot and enjoy the smoky, savory goodness.

Main Course

2. Filet Mignon with Red Wine Reduction

Prep Time: 10 minutes | Cook Time: 20 minutes | Total Time: 30 minutes

Ingredients:

  • 2 6-ounce filet mignon steaks
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1/2 cup red wine
  • 1/4 cup beef broth
  • 2 tablespoons unsalted butter

Instructions:

  1. Season the filet mignon steaks generously with salt and pepper.
  2. In a skillet, heat the olive oil over medium-high heat.
  3. Sear the steaks for 3-4 minutes on each side for medium-rare, or to your desired level of doneness.
  4. Remove the steaks from the skillet and let them rest.
  5. In the same skillet, pour in the red wine and beef broth, scraping the browned bits from the bottom.
  6. Let the sauce simmer and reduce by half.
  7. Stir in the butter until it melts and the sauce thickens.
  8. Pour the red wine reduction over the filet mignon steaks and serve with your favorite sides.

Dessert

3. Molten Chocolate Lava Cake

Prep Time: 15 minutes | Cook Time: 12 minutes | Total Time: 27 minutes

Ingredients:

  • 4 ounces bittersweet chocolate, chopped
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter
  • 1 cup powdered sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 egg yolks
  • 6 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • Pinch of salt

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 425°F (220°C).
  2. Grease two ramekins with butter and dust with cocoa powder.
  3. In a microwave-safe bowl, melt the chocolate and butter together in 30-second intervals, stirring until smooth.
  4. Whisk in the powdered sugar until well combined.
  5. Add the eggs and egg yolks, one at a time, mixing well after each addition.
  6. Gently fold in the flour and salt until just combined.
  7. Divide the batter between the prepared ramekins.
  8. Bake for 12 minutes, or until the edges are set but the center is still soft.
  9. Let the cakes cool for a minute, then run a knife around the edges and invert onto plates.
  10. Serve immediately and revel in the oozy, decadent center.
