Mediterranean Wrap: Hummus, Feta, and Greek Veggie Medley


If you’re looking for a flavorful and satisfying meal that’s bursting with Mediterranean flavors, then look no further than this delicious Mediterranean wrap recipe. Packed with creamy hummus, tangy feta cheese, and a vibrant Greek veggie medley, this wrap is a perfect combination of fresh ingredients and bold flavors. Whether you’re planning a quick and healthy lunch or a delightful picnic spread, this Mediterranean wrap is sure to impress your taste buds.

Ingredients

For the Mediterranean Wrap, you will need the following ingredients:

  • 1 large whole wheat or spinach wrap
  • 1/4 cup of hummus
  • 1/4 cup of crumbled feta cheese
  • 1/2 cup of diced cucumber
  • 1/2 cup of diced tomatoes
  • 1/4 cup of sliced red onion
  • 1/4 cup of sliced Kalamata olives
  • 1/2 cup of mixed salad greens
  • 1 tablespoon of extra virgin olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon of red wine vinegar
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

Step 1: Prepare the Greek Veggie Medley

Start by preparing the Greek veggie medley. In a medium bowl, combine the diced cucumber, diced tomatoes, sliced red onion, sliced Kalamata olives, and mixed salad greens. Drizzle the mixture with extra virgin olive oil and red wine vinegar. Season with salt and pepper to taste, and toss gently to combine. Set the veggie medley aside while you prepare the wrap.

Step 2: Assemble the Wrap

Lay the whole wheat or spinach wrap on a clean, flat surface. Spread the hummus evenly over the wrap, leaving a small border around the edges. Sprinkle the crumbled feta cheese over the hummus, ensuring an even distribution.

Step 3: Add the Greek Veggie Medley

Spoon the prepared Greek veggie medley onto the center of the wrap, spreading it out to cover the surface evenly. Be generous with the veggie medley, as it’s the star of this Mediterranean wrap.

Step 4: Roll and Serve

Once the wrap is assembled, it’s time to roll it up. Start by folding in the sides of the wrap, then roll it up tightly from the bottom, ensuring that the filling is secure. Slice the wrap in half at a slight angle for a beautiful presentation.

Step 5: Enjoy!

Your Mediterranean Wrap: Hummus, Feta, and Greek Veggie Medley is now ready to be enjoyed. Whether you’re serving it for a quick lunch or packing it for a picnic, this wrap is a delightful and satisfying meal that captures the essence of Mediterranean cuisine.

Conclusion

This Mediterranean wrap recipe is a celebration of fresh, wholesome ingredients and bold Mediterranean flavors. With creamy hummus, tangy feta cheese, and a vibrant Greek veggie medley, this wrap is a perfect embodiment of the vibrant and healthy Mediterranean diet. It’s a versatile dish that can be enjoyed on its own or paired with a refreshing side salad or some crispy pita chips.

So, the next time you’re craving a taste of the Mediterranean, whip up this delightful wrap and transport your taste buds to the sunny shores of Greece or the charming streets of Cyprus. With its enticing flavors and vibrant colors, this Mediterranean wrap is a true culinary delight that’s bound to become a favorite in your recipe collection.

Enjoy the delightful flavors of the Mediterranean with this simple and delicious wrap recipe!
