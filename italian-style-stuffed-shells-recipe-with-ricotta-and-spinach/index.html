Italian-Style Stuffed Shells Recipe with Ricotta and Spinach


If you’re a fan of Italian cuisine and are looking for a delicious and comforting dish to add to your recipe repertoire, look no further than this Italian-style stuffed shells recipe with ricotta and spinach. This dish is a perfect combination of creamy, cheesy, and savory flavors that will have your taste buds singing. Whether you’re cooking for a special occasion or simply craving some hearty comfort food, these stuffed shells are sure to be a hit.

Ingredients

To make this delectable dish, you’ll need the following ingredients:

  • 1 box of jumbo pasta shells
  • 1 15-ounce container of ricotta cheese
  • 1 10-ounce package of frozen chopped spinach, thawed and drained
  • 1 cup of grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 egg, lightly beaten
  • 1 teaspoon of garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon of dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 jar of your favorite marinara sauce
  • 2 cups of shredded mozzarella cheese

Instructions

Step 1: Prepare the Shells

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook the jumbo shells according to the package instructions. Be sure to cook them al dente, as they will continue to cook in the oven later. Once cooked, drain the shells and set them aside to cool.

Step 2: Make the Filling

  1. In a large mixing bowl, combine the ricotta cheese, drained spinach, Parmesan cheese, beaten egg, garlic powder, dried oregano, salt, and pepper. Mix everything together until well combined.

Step 3: Stuff the Shells

  1. Take each cooked shell and carefully spoon the ricotta and spinach mixture into it, ensuring that each shell is generously filled.

Step 4: Assemble the Dish

  1. Spread a thin layer of marinara sauce on the bottom of a large baking dish.
  2. Place the stuffed shells in the baking dish, arranging them in a single layer.
  3. Once all the shells are in the dish, pour the remaining marinara sauce over the top, making sure to cover each shell generously.
  4. Sprinkle the shredded mozzarella cheese over the top of the shells, covering them evenly.

Step 5: Bake and Serve

  1. Cover the baking dish with aluminum foil and bake in the preheated oven for 25 minutes.
  2. After 25 minutes, remove the foil and continue baking for an additional 10-15 minutes, or until the cheese is melted and bubbly.
  3. Once done, remove the dish from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes before serving.

Enjoy Your Italian-Style Stuffed Shells!

These Italian-style stuffed shells with ricotta and spinach are a comforting and satisfying dish that’s perfect for a cozy family dinner or a gathering with friends. The creamy ricotta filling combined with the savory marinara sauce and gooey melted mozzarella cheese creates a flavor explosion that will have everyone coming back for seconds. Serve these delicious stuffed shells with a side salad and some crusty bread for a complete and memorable meal.

So, next time you’re in the mood for a classic Italian dish with a twist, give this recipe a try and savor the flavors of Italy right in your own kitchen. Buon appetito!
