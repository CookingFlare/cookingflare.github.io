Spicy Thai Beef Salad for a Flavorful Dinner


If you’re craving a vibrant and zesty dish for dinner, look no further than this Spicy Thai Beef Salad. Packed with bold flavors and fresh ingredients, this salad is a perfect balance of heat, tanginess, and crunch. Whether you’re a fan of Thai cuisine or simply looking to spice up your dinner routine, this recipe is sure to impress your taste buds and leave you feeling satisfied.

Ingredients

For the Salad:

  • 1 lb (450g) beef steak, thinly sliced
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1 cucumber, thinly sliced
  • 1 red onion, thinly sliced
  • 1 cup mixed salad greens
  • 1/2 cup fresh cilantro leaves
  • 1/4 cup fresh mint leaves
  • 1/4 cup roasted peanuts, roughly chopped

For the Dressing:

  • 3 tablespoons fish sauce
  • 2 tablespoons lime juice
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon brown sugar
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1-2 red chilies, thinly sliced
  • 1 teaspoon grated ginger

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Beef: In a bowl, combine the sliced beef with a pinch of salt and pepper. Heat a skillet over high heat and sear the beef for 2-3 minutes on each side, or until it reaches your desired level of doneness. Once cooked, set the beef aside to rest.

  2. Make the Dressing: In a small bowl, whisk together the fish sauce, lime juice, soy sauce, brown sugar, minced garlic, sliced red chilies, and grated ginger. Adjust the seasoning to your taste preferences, adding more lime juice for extra tang or more brown sugar for sweetness.

  3. Assemble the Salad: In a large mixing bowl, combine the cherry tomatoes, cucumber, red onion, mixed salad greens, cilantro, and mint leaves. Toss the salad with half of the prepared dressing, ensuring the ingredients are well coated.

  4. Slice the Beef: Once the beef has rested, slice it thinly against the grain. Add the sliced beef to the salad and drizzle the remaining dressing over the top.

  5. Finish with Peanuts: Sprinkle the chopped roasted peanuts over the salad to add a delightful crunch and nutty flavor.

  6. Serve and Enjoy: Divide the Spicy Thai Beef Salad onto serving plates and garnish with extra cilantro and mint leaves if desired. Serve immediately and relish in the delicious blend of flavors.

Tips for Success

  • For a spicier kick, leave the seeds in the red chilies when preparing the dressing.
  • Adjust the level of heat by adding more or fewer chilies according to your preference.
  • To make it a complete meal, consider serving the Spicy Thai Beef Salad with steamed jasmine rice or rice noodles.

Final Thoughts

This Spicy Thai Beef Salad is a celebration of vibrant and aromatic Thai flavors. The tender beef, crisp vegetables, and aromatic herbs come together harmoniously, creating a dish that is both satisfying and refreshing. The balance of heat, tang, and crunch makes this salad an ideal choice for a flavorful dinner that will leave your taste buds tingling with delight.

So, the next time you’re in the mood for a culinary adventure, consider whipping up this Spicy Thai Beef Salad. It’s a delightful way to bring the exotic flavors of Thailand to your dinner table and create a memorable dining experience for yourself and your loved ones. Enjoy!
