Zesty Mediterranean Pasta Primavera Recipe


Are you looking for a dish that embodies the flavors and spirit of the Mediterranean diet? The Zesty Mediterranean Pasta Primavera is a perfect fit for pasta lovers who crave a refreshing twist on traditional spring dishes. This vibrant recipe bursts with a medley of sautéed vegetables, tangy herbs, creamy ricotta cheese, and the brightness of lemon. So, let's dive in and explore the magical world of fresh flavors. What is Pasta Primavera? Pasta primavera originated in the 1970s and literally translates to "spring pasta" in Italian, representing the explosion of colorful vegetables commonly found during the spring season. The dainty, flavorful, and edible flowers commonly used in this dish are shredded and sautéed, turning this pasta creation into an artistic masterpiece. Prepare your taste buds for a nutritious, plant-based dish infused with flooding colors and diverse taste. Prepare Your Ingredients Before diving into this scrumptious Italian dish, gather your kitchen arsenal, alongside the artery-friendly required ingredients:

12 oz whole wheat pasta of choice - spaghetti, fettuccine, or angel hair works best 2 cups fresh spring mix, monthly-ending baby carrots, eggplant, peppers, zucchini, cabbage, cherry tomatoes 1/2 cup ricotta cheese 2 cloves garlic, minced 1/4 cup raisins 1 cup water packed artichokes hearts 1/2 cup liquid concentrated chicken size caber call olives and stuff chopped basil reduced balsamic vinegar, aamt entire carved gran wine salt and pepper to taste Quick-pour smoothasting olive oil
