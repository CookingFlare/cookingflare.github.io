Mouthwatering Christmas Pudding Recipe with Brandy Sauce


The holiday season is upon us, and what better way to celebrate than with a mouthwatering Christmas pudding? This traditional dessert is a staple in many households and is sure to impress your family and friends. In this recipe, we will guide you through the process of creating a rich and flavorful Christmas pudding, complete with a decadent brandy sauce. Get ready to indulge in a festive treat that will leave everyone craving for more!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup raisins
  • 1 cup currants
  • 1 cup sultanas
  • 1 cup chopped dates
  • 1 cup chopped candied peel
  • 1 cup chopped almonds
  • 1 cup grated carrot
  • 1 cup grated apple
  • 1 cup breadcrumbs
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup brown sugar
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1 teaspoon ground nutmeg
  • 1 teaspoon ground ginger
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 cup melted butter
  • 3 eggs
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1/2 cup brandy (for soaking the fruit)

Instructions

  1. In a large mixing bowl, combine the raisins, currants, sultanas, chopped dates, candied peel, chopped almonds, grated carrot, and grated apple. Pour in the brandy and mix well. Cover the bowl with plastic wrap and let the fruits soak for at least 24 hours, preferably longer. This will allow the flavors to meld together and create a deliciously moist pudding.

  2. After the soaking period, preheat your oven to 325°F (165°C). Grease a 2-quart pudding basin and set aside.

  3. In a separate bowl, combine the breadcrumbs, all-purpose flour, brown sugar, ground cinnamon, ground nutmeg, ground ginger, and baking powder. Mix well to ensure the spices are evenly distributed.

  4. In another bowl, beat the eggs and add the melted butter and milk. Whisk until well combined.

  5. Gradually add the dry ingredients to the egg mixture, stirring gently after each addition. Once the mixture is well combined, fold in the soaked fruits and any remaining brandy.

  6. Pour the pudding batter into the greased pudding basin, ensuring it is evenly distributed. Smooth the top with a spatula.

  7. Cover the basin tightly with a double layer of aluminum foil, making sure it is completely sealed. This will prevent any moisture from entering the pudding during the cooking process.

  8. Place the pudding basin in a large roasting pan and fill the pan with boiling water until it reaches halfway up the sides of the basin. This water bath will help the pudding cook evenly and maintain its moisture.

  9. Carefully transfer the roasting pan to the preheated oven and bake for approximately 6 hours. Check the water level occasionally and top up with boiling water if needed.

  10. Once the cooking time is complete, remove the pudding from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes. Remove the foil and invert the pudding onto a serving plate.

Brandy Sauce

No Christmas pudding is complete without a luscious brandy sauce. This creamy accompaniment adds a delightful touch to the rich flavors of the pudding.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 2 tablespoons brandy

Instructions

  1. In a saucepan, heat the heavy cream, granulated sugar, and butter over medium heat. Stir continuously until the sugar has dissolved and the mixture is hot but not boiling.

  2. Remove the saucepan from the heat and stir in the brandy. Be careful, as the brandy may cause the mixture to splatter slightly.

  3. Return the saucepan to low heat and cook for an additional 2-3 minutes, stirring constantly, until the sauce has thickened slightly.

  4. Remove from heat and let the brandy sauce cool for a few minutes before serving.

Serving Suggestions

To serve, slice the Christmas pudding into thick wedges and drizzle each slice with the warm brandy sauce. You can also garnish with a sprinkle of powdered sugar or a sprig of fresh mint for an extra festive touch. This delightful dessert pairs perfectly with a cup of hot tea or a glass of mulled wine.

So there you have it, a mouthwatering Christmas pudding recipe with brandy sauce that will leave your taste buds dancing with joy. Enjoy this holiday treat with your loved ones and create lasting memories. Happy holidays and happy cooking!
