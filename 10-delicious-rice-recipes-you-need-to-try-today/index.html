10 Delicious Rice Recipes You Need to Try Today


Rice is an essential ingredient in kitchens worldwide, celebrated for its versatility, simplicity, and ability to pair perfectly with an array of flavors. Whether you’re in the mood for something hearty, spicy, or comforting, rice can serve as the foundation for a meal that pleases the palate and satisfies the soul. We’ve compiled 10 must-try rice recipes that elevate this pantry staple to an extraordinary dining experience. These dishes are easy to prepare, packed with flavor, and suitable for a variety of occasions.

1. Classic Spanish Paella

Enjoy a vibrant taste of Spain with this iconic rice dish brimming with bold flavors and rich textures. Traditionally prepared with saffron-infused rice, tender chicken, smoky chorizo, and fresh seafood, paella is a symphony of taste and color.

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups of Bomba or Arborio rice
  • 1/2 tsp of saffron threads
  • 4 cups of chicken broth
  • 1 lb of chicken thighs (cubed)
  • 1/2 lb of chorizo (sliced)
  • 1/2 lb of shrimp
  • 1 red bell pepper (sliced)
  • 1 onion (chopped)
  • 2 tomatoes (diced)
  • 1 tsp smoked paprika
  • Olive oil, salt, and pepper to taste

Directions:

  1. Heat olive oil in a wide pan and sauté chicken and chorizo until browned. Remove them and set aside.
  2. In the same pan, sauté onion, tomatoes, and bell pepper. Add paprika and saffron threads.
  3. Stir in rice, ensuring it’s well-coated with the seasonings before adding the broth. Let it simmer.
  4. Return chicken and chorizo to the pan, then layer shrimp on top. Cover and cook until the rice absorbs the broth.

Enjoy this rich, one-pan meal that brings a slice of Mediterranean cuisine into your kitchen.

2. Creamy Mushroom Risotto

Perfect for dinner parties or comfort food cravings, this creamy mushroom risotto dazzles with its velvety texture and earthy flavor.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup Arborio rice
  • 4 cups chicken or vegetable stock
  • 1/2 cup dry white wine
  • 2 cups mixed mushrooms (sliced)
  • 1 small onion (finely chopped)
  • 2 cloves garlic (minced)
  • 1/2 cup Parmesan cheese (grated)
  • 3 tbsp butter
  • 2 tbsp olive oil

Directions:

  1. Heat olive oil and sauté mushrooms in a pan until tender. Remove mushrooms and set aside.
  2. In the same pan, sauté onions and garlic until translucent. Add Arborio rice and toast lightly.
  3. Pour in the white wine, stirring until the rice absorbs it. Then gradually add the warm stock, one ladle at a time, stirring continuously.
  4. When the rice is creamy and tender, stir in mushrooms, butter, and Parmesan for an irresistibly creamy texture.

Serve warm and savor the luxurious flavors.

3. Thai Pineapple Fried Rice

Transport yourself to Southeast Asia with this sweet and savory Thai pineapple fried rice.

Ingredients:

  • 3 cups cooked jasmine rice (refrigerated overnight)
  • 1 cup fresh pineapple (diced)
  • 1/2 cup cashews
  • 2 eggs (beaten)
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 2 tbsp fish sauce
  • 2 garlic cloves (minced)
  • 1 red chili (sliced)
  • Green onions, lime wedges, and cilantro for garnish

Directions:

  1. Heat oil in a large wok or skillet. Scramble the eggs and set them aside.
  2. Sauté garlic, chili, and pineapple until caramelized. Add rice, soy sauce, and fish sauce, stirring to combine.
  3. Mix in the scrambled eggs and cashews, then garnish with green onions, lime wedges, and fresh cilantro.

This fragrant and colorful dish hits all the right notes.

4. Jollof Rice

This West African classic is full of smoky, spicy goodness that’s hard to resist.

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups long-grain rice
  • 3 tomatoes (blended)
  • 2 red bell peppers (blended)
  • 1 onion (blended)
  • 1/4 cup tomato paste
  • 3 cups chicken or vegetable stock
  • 1 tsp thyme
  • 1 tsp curry powder
  • 1/2 tsp paprika
  • Olive oil and salt to taste

Directions:

  1. Heat olive oil in a pot. Add tomato paste and cook until it darkens.
  2. Mix in the blended tomato, pepper, and onion mix, simmering until thickened.
  3. Add spices, stock, and rice. Stir well, cover, and let it cook over low heat until the rice is tender.

Pair with grilled chicken or plantains for a complete meal.

5. Mexican Rice

This lively side dish features bold seasoning and zesty flavors.

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups of long-grain white rice
  • 4 cups chicken broth
  • 1/2 cup tomato sauce
  • 1 small onion (chopped)
  • 1 clove garlic (minced)
  • 1 tsp cumin
  • Olive oil and cilantro for garnish

Directions:

  1. Toast the rice in olive oil until golden.
  2. Add onion, garlic, and cumin. Stir until fragrant.
  3. Stir in tomato sauce and broth. Cover and simmer until the rice is tender.

Serve alongside tacos or grilled meats for a complete fiesta.
