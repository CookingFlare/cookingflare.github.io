BBQ Chicken Tacos: Easy and Delicious Recipes for Taco Night


Are you looking for a simple yet flavorful recipe to spice up your taco nights? Look no further than these mouthwatering BBQ chicken tacos! Packed with smoky, tangy, and savory flavors, these tacos are sure to become a family favorite. Plus, they are incredibly easy to make, making them a perfect go-to meal for busy weeknights or casual gatherings with friends. Let’s dive into the recipe and discover how to create these irresistible BBQ chicken tacos.

Ingredients

For the BBQ Chicken:

  • 1 pound boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1 cup of your favorite BBQ sauce
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon smoked paprika
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste

For the Taco Assembly:

  • 8-10 small flour or corn tortillas
  • 1 cup shredded lettuce
  • 1 cup diced tomatoes
  • 1/2 cup diced red onion
  • 1/2 cup chopped fresh cilantro
  • 1 cup shredded cheddar or Monterey Jack cheese
  • Sour cream, for serving
  • Lime wedges, for serving

Instructions

  1. Prepare the BBQ Chicken:

    • Preheat your grill or stovetop grill pan over medium-high heat.
    • In a small bowl, mix the BBQ sauce, olive oil, smoked paprika, garlic powder, salt, and pepper.
    • Brush the chicken breasts with the BBQ sauce mixture, ensuring they are evenly coated.
    • Grill the chicken for 6-7 minutes on each side, or until fully cooked and slightly charred. Once cooked, remove the chicken from the grill and let it rest for a few minutes before slicing into strips.

  2. Assemble the Tacos:

    • Warm the tortillas on the grill or in a separate skillet for a few seconds on each side.
    • Place a few slices of the BBQ chicken onto each tortilla.
    • Top the chicken with shredded lettuce, diced tomatoes, red onion, cilantro, and a sprinkle of cheese.
    • Finish off with a dollop of sour cream and a squeeze of fresh lime juice.

  3. Serve and Enjoy:

    • Arrange the assembled BBQ chicken tacos on a platter and serve them with extra lime wedges on the side.
    • Let everyone customize their tacos with their favorite toppings and enjoy the explosion of flavors!

Tips for Customization

  • Feel free to customize your BBQ chicken tacos with additional toppings such as sliced avocado, pickled jalapenos, or a drizzle of hot sauce for an extra kick.
  • If you prefer a lighter option, you can use lettuce wraps or whole-grain tortillas instead of traditional flour or corn tortillas.

Why BBQ Chicken Tacos?

BBQ chicken tacos are a fantastic way to bring together the smoky flavors of BBQ with the beloved tradition of taco night. The combination of tender, juicy chicken and the vibrant, fresh toppings creates a delightful contrast of textures and tastes in every bite. Whether you’re hosting a casual dinner with friends or simply craving a hassle-free yet satisfying meal, these BBQ chicken tacos are a surefire crowd-pleaser.

In Conclusion

These BBQ chicken tacos are a prime example of how simple ingredients can come together to create a memorable and delicious dish. With minimal prep and maximum flavor, they are an ideal addition to your recipe repertoire. So, gather your ingredients, fire up the grill, and get ready to savor the irresistible appeal of BBQ chicken tacos on your next taco night! Cheers to easy and flavorful meals that never fail to impress. Happy cooking!
