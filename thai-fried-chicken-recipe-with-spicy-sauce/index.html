Thai Fried Chicken Recipe with Spicy Sauce


Thailand is famous for its delicious street food, and one of the most popular dishes is Thai fried chicken. The crispy exterior and juicy interior of the chicken, combined with the spicy and sweet flavors of the sauce, make this dish a must-try for anyone who loves Thai cuisine. In this recipe, we will show you how to make Thai fried chicken with spicy sauce using a few simple ingredients and some basic cooking techniques.

Ingredients:

  • 2 lbs boneless, skinless chicken thighs, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup cornstarch
  • 1/2 cup panko breadcrumbs
  • 1/4 cup vegetable oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon grated ginger
  • 1 tablespoon fish sauce
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon lime juice
  • 1/4 cup water
  • 1/4 cup Thai red curry paste
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh cilantro
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Spicy sauce (see below for recipe)

Spicy Sauce:

  • 1/2 cup Thai red curry paste
  • 1/4 cup fish sauce
  • 1/4 cup lime juice
  • 1/4 cup water
  • 2 tablespoons palm sugar
  • 1 tablespoon grated ginger
  • 1 tablespoon chopped fresh cilantro
  • 1-2 teaspoons Thai chili peppers, seeded and chopped
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a large bowl, combine the flour, cornstarch, and panko breadcrumbs.
  2. Add the chicken pieces to the bowl and toss to coat evenly with the flour mixture.
  3. Heat the vegetable oil in a large skillet or wok over medium-high heat.
  4. Add the chicken pieces to the skillet and cook until golden brown, about 5-7 minutes.
  5. Remove the chicken from the skillet and set aside.
  6. In the same skillet, add the minced garlic and grated ginger and cook until fragrant, about 1 minute.
  7. Add the fish sauce, soy sauce, and lime juice to the skillet and stir to combine.
  8. Add the water to the skillet and bring the mixture to a boil.
  9. Reduce the heat to medium-low and simmer the sauce for 5-7 minutes, or until it has thickened slightly.
  10. Add the Thai red curry paste to the skillet and stir to combine.
  11. Add the cooked chicken back to the skillet and toss to coat with the spicy sauce.
  12. Season the chicken with salt and pepper to taste.
  13. Garnish with chopped fresh cilantro and serve hot.

Tips and Variations:

  • For an extra crispy coating, you can chill the chicken pieces in the refrigerator for 30 minutes before frying.
  • To make the spicy sauce more intense, you can add more Thai chili peppers or use hotter peppers like habaneros.
  • You can also add other ingredients to the spicy sauce, such as chopped bell peppers or onions, to give it more flavor and texture.
  • For a vegetarian version, you can use tofu or tempeh instead of chicken.

Conclusion:

Thai fried chicken with spicy sauce is a delicious and easy-to-make dish that is perfect for any occasion. With its crispy exterior and juicy interior, combined with the spicy and sweet flavors of the sauce, this dish is sure to please even the pickiest of eaters. Whether you are a fan of Thai cuisine or just looking for a new recipe to try, this dish is definitely worth a shot. So go ahead and give it a try, and enjoy the delicious flavors of Thailand!
