Instant Pot Chicken and Rice: A Complete Meal in One Pot


Are you looking for a quick and easy one-pot meal that is both delicious and nutritious? Look no further than Instant Pot Chicken and Rice! This recipe is perfect for busy weeknights when you don’t have much time to spend in the kitchen but still want a home-cooked meal that the whole family will love.

Ingredients

To make this Instant Pot Chicken and Rice dish, you will need the following ingredients:

  • 1 pound of boneless, skinless chicken breasts, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 1 cup of long-grain white rice
  • 1 small onion, diced
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon of dried thyme
  • 1 teaspoon of paprika
  • 1 teaspoon of salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon of black pepper
  • 2 cups of chicken broth
  • 1 cup of frozen peas
  • Fresh parsley for garnish

Instructions

  1. Start by setting your Instant Pot to sauté mode. Add a drizzle of oil and sauté the diced onion and minced garlic until they become fragrant and translucent.

  2. Next, add the bite-sized chicken pieces to the Instant Pot and cook until they are browned on all sides. This will help to seal in the juices and add flavor to the final dish.

  3. Once the chicken is browned, add the long-grain white rice to the Instant Pot. Stir the rice in with the chicken and onions, coating it with the flavorful oils.

  4. Sprinkle the dried thyme, paprika, salt, and black pepper over the chicken and rice mixture. Stir everything together to ensure that the spices are evenly distributed.

  5. Pour the chicken broth into the Instant Pot, making sure that the rice and chicken are completely submerged. This will ensure that the rice cooks evenly and absorbs all of the delicious flavors.

  6. Secure the lid on the Instant Pot and set the valve to the sealing position. Cook the chicken and rice mixture on high pressure for 8 minutes.

  7. Once the cooking time is complete, allow the Instant Pot to naturally release pressure for 10 minutes. After 10 minutes, carefully release any remaining pressure by turning the valve to the venting position.

  8. Remove the lid from the Instant Pot and stir in the frozen peas. The residual heat from the chicken and rice mixture will thaw the peas perfectly.

  9. Let the Instant Pot Chicken and Rice sit for a few minutes to allow the flavors to meld together. Serve hot, garnished with fresh parsley for added freshness.

The Benefits of One-Pot Meals

One-pot meals like Instant Pot Chicken and Rice are not only convenient and time-saving, but they also offer a range of benefits. Firstly, they require minimal cleanup, as all the ingredients are cooked together in a single pot. This means less time spent washing dishes and more time enjoying your meal.

Additionally, one-pot meals are a great way to incorporate a variety of ingredients into one dish. In this Instant Pot Chicken and Rice recipe, you have the protein from the chicken, the carbohydrates from the rice, and the vegetables from the peas. It’s a complete meal that provides a balance of nutrients.

Customize Your Instant Pot Chicken and Rice

One of the great things about this recipe is its versatility. You can easily customize it to suit your taste preferences and dietary restrictions. Here are a few ideas to get you started:

  • Swap the chicken breasts for boneless, skinless chicken thighs for a richer and more tender result.
  • Add your favorite vegetables to the mix, such as diced carrots or bell peppers, to increase the nutritional value and add more color to the dish.
  • Experiment with different spices and herbs to create unique flavor profiles. Try adding a pinch of cumin or a sprinkle of Italian seasoning for a twist.

Conclusion

Instant Pot Chicken and Rice is a simple, yet satisfying meal that will become a family favorite in no time. With the convenience of the Instant Pot and the delicious flavors of the chicken, rice, and spices, this recipe is a winner. So the next time you’re in need of a quick and easy dinner, give this one-pot wonder a try!
