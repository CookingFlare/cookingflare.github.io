Cheesy Scalloped Potatoes: A Comforting Easter Side Dish

3 min · 573 words

Scalloped potatoes are a classic comfort food that never fails to impress. With their creamy texture and cheesy goodness, they make the perfect side dish for any occasion. And what better time to indulge in this delicious treat than Easter? Whether you’re hosting a festive gathering or simply looking for a delightful dish to complement your Easter feast, these Cheesy Scalloped Potatoes are sure to be a hit.

Ingredients

  • 2 pounds of Russet potatoes, thinly sliced
  • 1 cup of shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1 cup of shredded Gruyere cheese
  • 2 cups of heavy cream
  • 4 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons of butter
  • 2 tablespoons of all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon of salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon of black pepper
  • 1/4 teaspoon of ground nutmeg

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).

  2. In a saucepan over medium heat, melt the butter. Add the minced garlic and sauté until fragrant, about 1 minute.

  3. Add the flour to the saucepan and whisk continuously for 2 minutes, allowing the flour to cook and form a roux.

  4. Slowly pour in the heavy cream while whisking constantly, ensuring there are no lumps. Continue whisking until the mixture thickens and coats the back of a spoon.

  5. Stir in the salt, black pepper, and ground nutmeg, combining the flavors into a velvety sauce.

  6. In a greased baking dish, layer half of the sliced potatoes, overlapping them slightly. Pour half of the creamy sauce over the potatoes, spreading it evenly.

  7. Sprinkle half of the shredded cheddar and Gruyere cheese on top of the sauce, allowing it to melt and create a golden crust.

  8. Repeat the layers with the remaining potatoes, sauce, and cheese.

  9. Cover the baking dish with aluminum foil and bake in the preheated oven for 40 minutes.

  10. Remove the foil and continue baking for an additional 20 minutes, or until the potatoes are tender and the cheese is bubbly and golden.

  11. Allow the Cheesy Scalloped Potatoes to cool for a few minutes before serving. This will help the dish set and make it easier to portion.

  12. Serve the potatoes as a comforting Easter side dish alongside roasted ham, tender lamb, or any other main course of your choice.

Tips and Variations

  • To add a touch of freshness and color, sprinkle some chopped parsley or chives over the finished dish before serving.

  • For a twist on the classic recipe, consider adding crispy bacon or caramelized onions between the layers of potatoes.

  • If you prefer a lighter version of this dish, you can substitute half of the heavy cream with chicken or vegetable broth.

  • Leftovers can be stored in an airtight container in the refrigerator for up to 3 days. Reheat them in the oven or microwave until heated through.

Conclusion

Cheesy Scalloped Potatoes are a timeless Easter side dish that brings comfort and joy to any gathering. With their creamy texture, layers of cheesy goodness, and delicate flavors, they are the perfect accompaniment to your holiday feast. Whether you’re celebrating Easter or simply craving a delicious and satisfying dish, give this recipe a try and watch the smiles light up around the table.
