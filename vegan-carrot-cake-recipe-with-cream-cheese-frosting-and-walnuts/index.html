Vegan Carrot Cake Recipe with Cream Cheese Frosting and Walnuts


Carrot cake is a classic dessert loved by many, but what if you could enjoy all the deliciousness without any animal products? This vegan carrot cake recipe with cream cheese frosting and walnuts is sure to satisfy your cravings while still aligning with your dietary preferences. So let’s get baking!

Ingredients

For the cake:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground nutmeg
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground ginger
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/2 cup vegetable oil
  • 1/2 cup unsweetened applesauce
  • 1/4 cup almond milk
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 2 cups grated carrots
  • 1 cup crushed pineapple, drained
  • 1/2 cup chopped walnuts

For the cream cheese frosting:

  • 1/2 cup vegan butter, softened
  • 8 oz vegan cream cheese, softened
  • 4 cups powdered sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C) and grease a 9-inch round cake pan.

  2. In a large mixing bowl, whisk together the flour, baking powder, baking soda, cinnamon, nutmeg, ginger, and salt. Set aside.

  3. In another bowl, combine the granulated sugar, vegetable oil, applesauce, almond milk, and vanilla extract. Mix well until all the ingredients are fully incorporated.

  4. Gradually add the wet ingredients to the dry ingredients, stirring until just combined. Be careful not to overmix as it can result in a dense cake.

  5. Fold in the grated carrots, crushed pineapple, and chopped walnuts. These ingredients will add texture and flavor to your cake.

  6. Pour the batter into the prepared cake pan and smooth the top with a spatula.

  7. Bake for 35-40 minutes or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.

  8. While the cake is baking, prepare the cream cheese frosting. In a separate bowl, beat the vegan butter and cream cheese until smooth and creamy.

  9. Gradually add the powdered sugar, one cup at a time, and continue to beat until the frosting is fluffy. Stir in the vanilla extract.

  10. Once the cake has cooled completely, carefully remove it from the pan and transfer it to a serving plate.

  11. Spread a generous layer of the cream cheese frosting over the top and sides of the cake, using a spatula or a butter knife to create a smooth finish.

  12. Optional: Garnish the cake with additional chopped walnuts for an extra crunch and visual appeal.

  13. Slice and serve your vegan carrot cake, and enjoy the delightful combination of flavors and textures!

Conclusion

This vegan carrot cake recipe with cream cheese frosting and walnuts is a delightful treat that proves you don’t need animal products to enjoy a delicious dessert. The moist and flavorful cake, combined with the creamy and tangy frosting, will satisfy even the most discerning taste buds. Whether you follow a vegan lifestyle or simply want to try something new, this recipe is a must-try. So, gather your ingredients, preheat your oven, and get ready to indulge in a slice of vegan heaven!
