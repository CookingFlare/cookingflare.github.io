Greek Lemon Potatoes: A Zesty and Crispy Side Dish


If you’re looking for a delectable side dish to elevate your meals, look no further than Greek Lemon Potatoes. These zesty and crispy potatoes are a staple in Greek cuisine and are sure to become a favorite in your kitchen. With a perfect balance of tangy lemon, aromatic herbs, and crispy edges, Greek Lemon Potatoes are a delightful addition to any meal, from weeknight dinners to special occasions.

Ingredients

To make these mouthwatering Greek Lemon Potatoes, you’ll need the following ingredients:

  • 4 large potatoes, peeled and cut into wedges
  • 1/3 cup olive oil
  • 1/2 cup chicken or vegetable broth
  • 1/4 cup fresh lemon juice
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley for garnish

Instructions

Step 1: Preparing the Potatoes

Start by preheating your oven to 400°F (200°C). While the oven is preheating, peel the potatoes and cut them into wedges. Rinse the potato wedges under cold water to remove excess starch, then pat them dry with a paper towel.

Step 2: Creating the Flavorful Mixture

In a large bowl, whisk together the olive oil, chicken or vegetable broth, fresh lemon juice, minced garlic, dried oregano, dried thyme, salt, and pepper. This flavorful mixture will be the key to infusing the potatoes with tangy and aromatic flavors.

Step 3: Coating the Potatoes

Place the potato wedges in the bowl with the flavorful mixture and toss them until they are well coated. Ensure that each potato wedge is covered with the zesty mixture to guarantee maximum flavor.

Step 4: Baking to Perfection

Transfer the coated potato wedges to a baking dish, arranging them in a single layer. Pour any remaining mixture from the bowl over the potatoes. This will ensure that the potatoes absorb all the delicious flavors as they bake.

Place the baking dish in the preheated oven and bake the potatoes for approximately 45-50 minutes, or until they are golden and crispy on the outside, and tender on the inside. Remember to flip the potatoes halfway through the baking time to ensure even crispiness.

Step 5: Garnish and Serve

Once the Greek Lemon Potatoes are perfectly golden and crispy, remove them from the oven. Garnish the potatoes with freshly chopped parsley for a burst of color and freshness. These zesty and crispy potatoes are now ready to be served alongside your favorite main dishes.

Serving Suggestions

Greek Lemon Potatoes pair wonderfully with a variety of main dishes. They are the perfect accompaniment to grilled chicken, roasted lamb, or Mediterranean-style fish. You can also serve them alongside a fresh Greek salad for a complete and satisfying meal.

Final Thoughts

With their irresistible combination of zesty lemon, fragrant herbs, and crispy texture, Greek Lemon Potatoes are a delightful side dish that will impress your family and guests. Whether you’re hosting a gathering or simply preparing a cozy dinner at home, these flavorful potatoes are sure to steal the show.

So, the next time you’re craving a side dish that’s both zesty and crispy, give Greek Lemon Potatoes a try. Your taste buds will thank you for it!

Enjoy your culinary adventure with these delightful Greek Lemon Potatoes!
