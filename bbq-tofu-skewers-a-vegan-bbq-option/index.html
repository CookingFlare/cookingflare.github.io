BBQ Tofu Skewers: A Vegan BBQ Option


Have you been searching for a delicious plant-based meal option to bring to your next BBQ? Look no further than these mouth-watering BBQ tofu skewers! Not only are they packed with protein and flavor, but they also delight the taste buds of both vegetarians and meat-lovers alike.

Ingredients:

  • 1 pack of firm tofu (14 oz)
  • 1 cup of cornstarch
  • 1/2 cup of your favorite BBQ sauce
  • 1/2 cup of chopped mixed peppers (red, yellow, and green)
  • 1/2 cup of chopped red onion
  • 1/2 cup of cherry tomatoes
  • 1 cup of cilantro leaves
  • 12 wooden skewers
  • Additional BBQ sauce, for serving

Instructions:

  1. Begin by pressing the tofu to remove any excess liquid. Place the tofu on a clean dish towel or paper towels and put something heavy on top, such as a cutting board or cookbook, to weigh it down. Let it rest for around 30 minutes.
  2. While the tofu is pressing, prepare the vegetables. Rinse and chop the mixed peppers, red onion, and cilantro. Be sure to handle the chopping with care to avoid any accidents in the kitchen ðŸ”ª.
  3. After the tofu has finished pressing, cut the tofu into chunks that are approximately 1-inch in size.
  4. In a shallow dish or bowl, whisk together the cornstarch and BBQ sauce. Mix until thoroughly combined.
  5. Take the tofu cubes and coat them in the cornstarch and BBQ sauce mixture. Make sure to evenly cover each piece.
  6. Prepare the wooden skewers by randomly grouping combinations of tofu, peppers, onion, and cherry tomatoes on each one. Sprinkling a bit of cilantro atop the skewers adds extra flair and flavor ðŸŒ¿.
  7. Heat up your grill to medium-high heat. No grill available? No problem! You can also use an oven broiler or even a stovetop grill pan.
  8. Once the grill is hot, place the skewers directly on the grate. Cook for approximately 5-6 minutes on each side, or until the tofu is nicely charred and the vegetables are tender.
  9. Remove the skewers from the grill, and serve them immediately with additional BBQ sauce on the side for dipping.

FAQ

Q: What are the health benefits of BBQ tofu skewers?

A: BBQ tofu skewers are a wonderful vegan option that is packed with nutritional benfits like high protein, low-fat, and high-fiber content. Plus, tofu is a fantastic source of iron, calcium, and sulfate. Don’t believe us? Give it a try!

Q: How do you keep tofu from falling off the skewers?

A: To prevent your delectable tofu from dislodging from the skewers during grilling or handling, simply press the tofu before applying the cornstarch and BBQ sauce. This step ensures the cubes will hold together well and creates a scrumptious, crispy coating.
