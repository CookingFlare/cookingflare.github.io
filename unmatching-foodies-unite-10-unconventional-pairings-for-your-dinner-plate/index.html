Unmatching Foodies Unite: 10 Unconventional Pairings for Your Dinner Plate


Have you ever been dared to pair a pickle with peanut butter or a banana with soy sauce? If you’re like most people, your instincts probably urged you to cringe in horror. But, you know what they say: don’t knock it till you’ve tried it. This blog post is for the foodies who dare to challenge the status quo, the unconventionally daring souls who are ready to jump into the world of the bizarrely brilliant. Today, we’ve assembled a selection of daring pairings for your dinner plate, sure to tantalize your taste buds and make jaws drop all around. So put on your chef’s hat and prepare to dive into the realm of the avant-garde - it’s time for Unmatching Foodies to unite!

1. Chocolate Fountain with Pineapples and Marshmallows

While your gut is screaming “contradiction in terms!”, this playful fusion of sweet and fruity will sweep you off your feet. Set up a chocolate fountain next to a bowl of pineapple chunks and marshmallows and watch as your guests help themselves to a warmed-up version of night and day. Bonus points if you add strawberries, bananas, and raspberries to the mix.

2. Sweet Potato Fries with Honey Mustard Dip

Who would have thought that the humble, square-cut fry can be zested up to an entirely new level? The sweetness of the fries coupled with the tangy, piquant fuse of honey mustard makes for a truly finger-licking combination. And if your guests want to take it even further, feel free to drizzle some truffle oil on top and watch the conversation spark.

3. Crispy Fried Chicken with Waffles

Brace yourself, as this Southern classic has transcended its comfort-food boundaries and spread its wings (as it were) all the way to the world of the unconventional. Fried chicken topped with fruit salsa and drizzled with a sweet maple syrup - talk about eclectic!

4. Ham and Pineapple Pizza

For all the Hawaiians at heart, the combination of sweet pineapple chunks and salty ham is a match made in the processing chambers of your taste buds. Its tanginess and sweet taste create a symphony of flavors that exist perfectly on a classic Margherita pizza.

5. Peanut Butter Sandwich with Pickles

Hold onto your hats because things are about to get interesting. The sweet, creamy, nutty flavor of peanut butter blends surprisingly well with the zesty tanginess of a dill pickle. Grab your trusty mayo or any other spread of choice to saturate the bread - and voilà, the world’s most ‘unmatched’ sandwich.

6. Strawberry and Balsamic Reduction Drizzle

Who said dessert can’t push the boundaries? This combination sneaks up on you, with its first bite, you’re welcomed by strawberries’ rich, sweet flavor. The balsamic reduction, then, comes swooping in with its rich, sweet-sour characteristics, that complement the strawberries and balance the sweetness.

7. Grilled Cheese with a Tomato Soup Twist

This everlasting classic transcends to new heights with a twist on the classic tomato soup - chipotle and cheese infused. This twist provides a warmth that complements the gooey, melty cheese between crunch bread - a hearty amalgamation that’s truly worth trying.

8. Apple and Brie Grilled Cheese

Switch things up and try grilled cheese lovers - this Apple and Brie Grilled Cheese will tickle your taste buds mistaken. Sandwich minced apples between soft slices brie and grilled bread. The tangy, savory flavor of brie pairs perfectly with sweet apples caramelization, creating a delightful balance.

9. Kimchi and Peanut Butter on Toast

Don’t let the unconventional combination fool you; the heat of the kimchi and the nuttiness of the peanut butter make a surprisingly successful pairing. The spiciness of kimchi is complemented nicely by the earthy crunch of peanut butter.

10. Campfire Concoction: S’mores with Spicy Chocolate Mole

Why stick to plain chocolate for the classic campfire toasted marshmallow treat when you can experiment with the spicy, earthy notes of mole chocolate sauce? The complex layering of flavors in mole combines beautifully with the gooey marshmallow and crisp graham cracker, making for a truly revelatory dessert.

And there you have it - ten unconventional pairings that will have people buzzing not just about your culinary skills but also your daring spirit in the kitchen. So feast on fried chicken with waffles, revel in the saccharine tanginess of pickle-infused peanut butter sandwiches, and let the gustatory journey into the uncharted territory unfold. Bon appétit!
