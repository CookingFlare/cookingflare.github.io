Instant Pot Chicken and Sweet Potato Gnocchi Recipe: A Delicious and Nutritious Option


If you’re looking for a comforting and flavorful meal that’s also packed with nutrition, this Instant Pot Chicken and Sweet Potato Gnocchi recipe is just what you need. The combination of tender chicken, pillowy gnocchi, and sweet potato in a savory broth makes for a satisfying dish that’s perfect for any occasion. Plus, the convenience of using an Instant Pot ensures that you can have this wholesome meal on the table in no time. Let’s dive into the details of this delectable recipe!

Ingredients

  • 1 lb boneless, skinless chicken breasts, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 medium onion, finely chopped
  • 3 garlic cloves, minced
  • 2 medium sweet potatoes, peeled and diced
  • 4 cups chicken broth
  • 1 cup water
  • 1 tsp dried thyme
  • 1 tsp dried rosemary
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/4 tsp black pepper
  • 16 oz store-bought gnocchi
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • Fresh parsley, chopped (for garnish)

Instructions

  1. Sear the Chicken: Set the Instant Pot to “Saute” mode and add the olive oil. Once hot, add the chicken pieces and cook until browned. Transfer the chicken to a plate and set aside.

  2. Saute the Aromatics: In the same pot, add the chopped onion and minced garlic. Cook for a few minutes until the onions are soft and translucent.

  3. Add the Sweet Potatoes and Spices: Stir in the diced sweet potatoes, dried thyme, dried rosemary, salt, and black pepper.

  4. Pressure Cook: Pour in the chicken broth and water, then add the seared chicken back into the pot. Close the Instant Pot lid and set the valve to the sealing position. Cook on high pressure for 5 minutes, then do a quick release.

  5. Cook the Gnocchi: Open the lid and stir in the gnocchi. Set the Instant Pot to “Saute” mode and cook the gnocchi in the broth for a few minutes until they are tender.

  6. Add the Cream: Pour in the heavy cream and stir gently to combine all the ingredients. Let the mixture simmer for a couple of minutes to allow the flavors to meld together.

  7. Serve: Ladle the chicken, sweet potato, and gnocchi mixture into bowls. Garnish with chopped fresh parsley for a pop of color and freshness.

Why This Recipe Rocks

This Instant Pot Chicken and Sweet Potato Gnocchi recipe is a winner for several reasons. First and foremost, it’s incredibly tasty. The tender chicken, soft sweet potatoes, and pillowy gnocchi soak up the savory broth, creating a symphony of flavors in every spoonful. The addition of aromatic herbs like thyme and rosemary elevates the dish to a whole new level of deliciousness.

Moreover, this recipe is a nutritional powerhouse. Sweet potatoes are rich in vitamins, fiber, and antioxidants, while chicken provides lean protein. This makes it a balanced meal that not only satisfies your taste buds but also nourishes your body.

Another reason to love this recipe is its convenience. Thanks to the Instant Pot, you can achieve the same rich, slow-cooked flavors in a fraction of the time. This means you can enjoy a hearty, homemade meal even on the busiest of days.

Final Thoughts

The Instant Pot Chicken and Sweet Potato Gnocchi recipe is a fantastic option for anyone seeking a wholesome, flavorful, and convenient meal. Whether you’re cooking for your family or hosting a dinner party, this dish is sure to impress. So, grab your Instant Pot and get ready to savor every spoonful of this delightful creation!
