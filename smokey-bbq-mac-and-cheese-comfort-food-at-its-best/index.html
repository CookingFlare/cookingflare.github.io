Smokey BBQ Mac and Cheese: Comfort Food at Its Best


Who doesn’t love a good, creamy mac and cheese dish? It’s a culinary crowd-pleaser, one that never fails to bring a smile to anyone’s face. But why stop at the classic recipe when you can take it to a whole new level with a touch of smoky barbecue flavor?

Today, we’re diving deep into the world of mouth-watering, perfectly gooey Smokey BBQ Mac and Cheese. This dish combines the savoury tang of barbecue sauce with the irresistible creaminess of melted cheese, and the result is nothing short of scrumptious.

Ingredients

  • 4 tablespoons unsalted butter
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 4 cups whole milk
  • 1/2 cup The BBQ Lab’s Smoky Barbecue Sauce (or your favorite BBQ sauce)
  • 1/4 cup Dijon mustard
  • 1/4 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon onion powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon smoked paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon cracked black pepper
  • 4 cups shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1 cup bread crumbs
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 1/2 cups elbow macaroni

Instructions

Step 1: Cook the pasta

Begin by cooking your elbow macaroni in boiling, salted water as per the package instructions. You’ll want it to be cooked al dente, meaning it’s tender but still slightly firm to the bite. It’s important to start this process first, as it will take the longest to cook.

Step 2: Start the sauce

While the pasta cooks, melt 4 tablespoons of butter in a large saucepan over medium heat. Once melted, add 1 cup of flour to create a roux, stirring constantly so that it doesn’t burn. This roux will eventually be the thickening agent for your sauce.

Step 3: Add the milk

Slowly pour in your 4 cups of whole milk, whisking constantly to avoid lumps. Add 1/2 cup of your favorite barbecue sauce, as well as 1/4 cup Dijon mustard, 1/4 teaspoon garlic powder, 1/4 teaspoon onion powder, 1/4 teaspoon smoked paprika, 1/2 teaspoon salt, and 1/4 teaspoon cracked black pepper. Stir everything well and let it simmer until it thickens to your desired consistency.

Step 4: Combine pasta and cheese

Drain the cooked elbow macaroni and add it to the sauce. Toss the macaroni in the sauce until fully coated. Next, add your 4 cups of shredded cheddar cheese and your cup of shredded mozzarella cheese. Stir until all the cheese is melted and well-distributed in your mac and cheese.

Step 5: Add crispy crunch

Sprinkle 1 cup of bread crumbs and 1/2 cup of grated Parmesan cheese over the top of your mac and cheese in a large baking dish. Finally, bake at 350 degrees Fahrenheit for 30 minutes, or until the top is golden brown and crispy.

Your Smokey BBQ Mac and Cheese is now ready to serve! It’s a meat-lovers dream come true, and the ideal comfort food for a casual get-together with family or friends.

This dish is so versatile that you can play around with different barbecue sauces, cheeses, and variations, catering to individual preferences. But there’s no denying that this delicious fusion of barbecue sauce, creamy mac and cheese, and smoky undertones is a winning combination. Enjoy a rich, comforting Smokey BBQ Mac and Cheese tonight and see for yourself!
