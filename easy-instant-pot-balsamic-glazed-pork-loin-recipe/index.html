Easy Instant Pot Balsamic Glazed Pork Loin Recipe


Are you looking for a delicious and easy-to-make pork loin recipe that’s perfect for a weeknight dinner or a special occasion? Look no further! This Easy Instant Pot Balsamic Glazed Pork Loin Recipe is a game-changer. With just a few simple ingredients and a few minutes of prep time, you’ll have a mouth-watering, fall-apart tender pork loin that’s infused with the rich flavors of balsamic glaze.

Ingredients

  • 1 (1-2 pound) pork loin
  • 1/4 cup balsamic vinegar
  • 1/4 cup brown sugar
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1/4 cup chicken broth

Instructions

Step 1: Prep the Pork Loin

Preheat the Instant Pot to sauté mode. Add the olive oil and swirl it around to coat the bottom of the pot. Sear the pork loin on all sides until it’s browned, about 2-3 minutes per side. Remove the pork loin from the pot and set it aside.

Step 2: Make the Balsamic Glaze

In the same pot, add the balsamic vinegar, brown sugar, garlic, thyme, salt, and pepper. Stir until the sugar has dissolved, then bring the mixture to a boil. Reduce the heat to low and simmer for about 5 minutes, or until the glaze has thickened slightly.

Step 3: Cook the Pork Loin

Add the chicken broth to the pot and stir to combine. Return the pork loin to the pot and close the lid. Make sure the valve is set to “sealing”. Cook on high pressure for 10-12 minutes, or until the pork loin reaches an internal temperature of 145°F.

Step 4: Glaze the Pork Loin

After the cooking time has elapsed, allow the pressure to release naturally for 5 minutes, then quick-release any remaining pressure. Remove the pork loin from the pot and brush it with the balsamic glaze. Return the pork loin to the pot and close the lid. Cook for an additional 2-3 minutes, or until the glaze is caramelized and sticky.

Step 5: Serve

Remove the pork loin from the pot and let it rest for 5-10 minutes before slicing it thinly. Serve with your favorite sides, such as roasted vegetables, mashed potatoes, or a simple green salad.

Tips and Variations

  • To make this recipe more substantial, serve the pork loin with a side of roasted Brussels sprouts or caramelized onions.
  • If you prefer a crisper crust on your pork loin, broil it for an additional 2-3 minutes after glazing.
  • Experiment with different types of vinegar, such as apple cider vinegar or white wine vinegar, to create a unique flavor profile.

Conclusion

This Easy Instant Pot Balsamic Glazed Pork Loin Recipe is a crowd-pleaser that’s perfect for any occasion. With its rich, caramelized glaze and tender, fall-apart texture, it’s sure to become a new favorite in your household. So why wait? Get cooking and enjoy the delicious flavors of this recipe!
