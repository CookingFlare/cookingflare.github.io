Wholesome and Delicious: Middle Eastern Lentil and Rice Soup


There’s something truly comforting about a warm bowl of soup, especially when it’s packed with wholesome ingredients and bursting with flavor. Middle Eastern cuisine is known for its rich and aromatic dishes, and this Middle Eastern Lentil and Rice Soup is no exception. This hearty and nutritious soup is a staple in many Middle Eastern households, and for good reason - it’s simple to make, incredibly satisfying, and full of nourishing ingredients.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup brown lentils, rinsed and drained
  • 1/2 cup long-grain white rice
  • 1 large onion, finely chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 carrots, diced
  • 2 celery stalks, diced
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon ground coriander
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground turmeric
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 teaspoon cayenne pepper
  • 6 cups vegetable or chicken broth
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • Fresh lemon juice for serving
  • Fresh parsley for garnish

Instructions

  1. In a large pot, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the chopped onions and sauté until translucent, then add the minced garlic and cook for another minute.

  2. Stir in the carrots and celery, and cook for a few minutes until they begin to soften.

  3. Add the ground cumin, coriander, turmeric, cinnamon, and cayenne pepper to the pot, and stir to coat the vegetables in the spices.

  4. Pour in the rinsed lentils and white rice, and then add the broth. Bring the soup to a boil, then reduce the heat and let it simmer for about 30-40 minutes, or until the lentils and rice are tender.

  5. Season the soup with salt and black pepper to taste. If the soup is too thick, you can add more broth or water to reach your desired consistency.

  6. Serve the soup hot, garnished with a squeeze of fresh lemon juice and a sprinkle of chopped parsley.

Why You’ll Love It

This Middle Eastern Lentil and Rice Soup is a celebration of earthy flavors and warm spices. The combination of lentils and rice provides a hearty base, while the aromatic spices add depth and complexity to the dish. The carrots and celery bring a subtle sweetness and crunch, balancing out the rich flavors of the soup.

Not only is this soup incredibly delicious, but it’s also a nutritional powerhouse. Lentils are an excellent source of protein, fiber, and essential nutrients, making them a perfect addition to any wholesome meal. Combined with the nourishing properties of the vegetables and the comfort of the rice, this soup is a complete and balanced dish that will leave you feeling satisfied and nourished.

Final Thoughts

Whether you’re looking for a hearty weeknight dinner or a comforting meal to warm you up on a chilly day, this Middle Eastern Lentil and Rice Soup is the perfect choice. With its simple preparation and incredible flavors, it’s a dish that will quickly become a favorite in your recipe repertoire. So, grab a spoon, savor the aroma of warm spices, and indulge in a bowl of this wholesome and delicious Middle Eastern soup.
