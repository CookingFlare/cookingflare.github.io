Delicious Mango Coconut Rice Pudding: A Tropical Twist


If you’re looking for a sweet and tropical dessert to satisfy your taste buds, look no further than this delicious Mango Coconut Rice Pudding recipe. With the perfect combination of creamy coconut, fragrant mango, and tender rice, this dessert is a delightful twist on the classic rice pudding. It’s easy to make, and the flavors will transport you straight to a tropical paradise. So, let’s dive in and create this delectable treat!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of jasmine rice
  • 2 cups of water
  • 1 can (14 ounces) of coconut milk
  • 1/2 cup of sugar
  • 1/4 teaspoon of salt
  • 1 ripe mango, diced
  • 1/4 cup of shredded coconut, toasted
  • Fresh mint leaves, for garnish

Instructions

  1. In a medium saucepan, bring the water to a boil. Add the jasmine rice and stir. Reduce the heat to low, cover, and let it simmer for about 15-20 minutes, or until the rice is tender and has absorbed the water.

  2. In a separate saucepan, combine the coconut milk, sugar, and salt. Heat the mixture over medium heat, stirring occasionally, until the sugar has dissolved and the mixture is warm.

  3. Once the rice is cooked, transfer it to the saucepan with the coconut milk mixture. Stir well to combine, and let it simmer over low heat for another 10-15 minutes, or until the mixture thickens slightly.

  4. Remove the saucepan from the heat and let the rice pudding cool to room temperature. It will continue to thicken as it cools.

  5. While the rice pudding cools, prepare the mango by peeling it and dicing it into small cubes.

  6. Once the rice pudding has cooled, gently fold in the diced mango. Make sure to keep some mango cubes aside for garnish.

  7. Divide the mango coconut rice pudding into serving bowls and sprinkle with toasted shredded coconut. Top each bowl with a few cubes of mango and garnish with fresh mint leaves.

  8. Serve the delicious Mango Coconut Rice Pudding chilled and enjoy the tropical flavors!

Optional Variations

If you want to add an extra layer of flavor to your Mango Coconut Rice Pudding, here are a few optional variations you can try:

  • Mango Puree: Instead of adding diced mango directly to the rice pudding, blend a ripe mango into a smooth puree and swirl it through the pudding for a vibrant burst of mango flavor.

  • Coconut Flakes: If you’re a fan of coconut, sprinkle some extra toasted coconut flakes on top of the pudding for added texture and taste.

  • Lime Zest: For a tangy twist, grate some lime zest over the finished pudding. The citrusy aroma will elevate the tropical flavors even further.

Final Thoughts

This Delicious Mango Coconut Rice Pudding is a delightful dessert that brings the taste of the tropics straight to your table. The creamy coconut milk, fragrant mango, and fluffy rice create a heavenly combination that will leave you craving more. Whether you’re hosting a summer gathering or simply want to treat yourself to a tropical indulgence, this recipe is sure to impress. So, grab your ingredients, follow the simple steps, and get ready to savor every spoonful of this tropical twist on a classic dessert. Enjoy!
