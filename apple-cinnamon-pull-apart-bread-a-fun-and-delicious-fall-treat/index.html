Apple Cinnamon Pull-Apart Bread: A Fun and Delicious Fall Treat


There’s something magical about the arrival of fall—the crisp air, vibrant foliage, and the comforting aroma of freshly baked treats. And what better way to celebrate the season than with a warm and indulgent Apple Cinnamon Pull-Apart Bread? This delightful recipe combines the sweet tartness of apples with the warm spice of cinnamon, creating a treat that is both fun to make and impossible to resist. So, roll up your sleeves, preheat your oven, and let’s dive into the world of this delectable fall treat!

Ingredients

For the bread:

  • 3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 2 1/4 teaspoons instant yeast
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, melted
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1/4 cup water
  • 2 large eggs

For the apple filling:

  • 2 medium-sized apples, peeled, cored, and finely diced
  • 1/4 cup brown sugar
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground nutmeg
  • 1/4 cup unsalted butter, melted

For the cinnamon sugar coating:

  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 2 teaspoons ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 cup unsalted butter, melted

Instructions

  1. Prepare the dough: In a large mixing bowl, combine the all-purpose flour, granulated sugar, instant yeast, and salt. In a separate microwave-safe bowl, melt the butter. Add the milk and water to the melted butter and stir until combined. Pour the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients and add the eggs. Mix well until a soft dough forms.

  2. Knead the dough: Transfer the dough onto a lightly floured surface and knead for about 5 minutes, or until the dough becomes smooth and elastic. Place the dough in a greased bowl, cover it with a clean kitchen towel, and let it rise in a warm place for 1 hour, or until doubled in size.

  3. Prepare the apple filling: In a medium-sized bowl, combine the diced apples, brown sugar, ground cinnamon, ground nutmeg, and melted butter. Toss until the apples are evenly coated with the mixture. Set aside.

  4. Assemble the bread: Punch down the risen dough and transfer it onto a lightly floured surface. Roll the dough into a large rectangle, about 12x18 inches. Spread the apple filling evenly over the dough, leaving a small border around the edges. Starting from the long side, tightly roll up the dough into a log.

  5. Slice the dough: Using a sharp knife, cut the log into 12 equal-sized pieces. Arrange the slices in a greased 9x5-inch loaf pan, placing them cut-side up. Cover the pan with a clean kitchen towel and let the dough rise for an additional 30 minutes.

  6. Bake the bread: Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C). Once the dough has risen, place the loaf pan in the preheated oven and bake for 35-40 minutes, or until the bread is golden brown and cooked through.

  7. Coat the bread: While the bread is baking, prepare the cinnamon sugar coating. In a small bowl, combine the granulated sugar and ground cinnamon. Once the bread is out of the oven and still warm, brush the melted butter over the top, then generously sprinkle the cinnamon sugar mixture on top.

  8. Serve and enjoy: Allow the Apple Cinnamon Pull-Apart Bread to cool slightly before serving. The bread can be enjoyed warm or at room temperature. Pull apart the sweet, cinnamon-infused layers and savor the delightful combination of apples and spice. This fall treat is perfect for breakfast, brunch, or a cozy afternoon snack with a cup of hot apple cider.

Conclusion

Apple Cinnamon Pull-Apart Bread is a delightful and indulgent treat that captures the essence of fall. The combination of tart apples, warm cinnamon, and sweet dough creates a satisfying flavor profile that will leave you craving more. Whether you’re enjoying it on a crisp autumn morning or sharing it with friends and family, this recipe is sure to bring joy and comfort. So, embrace the season, gather your ingredients, and let the aroma of freshly baked bread fill your home. Happy baking!
