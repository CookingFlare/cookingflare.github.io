Peach Cobbler Cupcakes: Summer's Finest Fruit Tossed in Cake Form


Ah, summer! The season of sunshine, warm rays, and the vibrant taste of peaches. With the abundance of this incredible fruit during these sunny months, it’s only fitting that we find a scrumptious way to enjoy it. And what better way to savour the sweet, juicy peach than in cupcake form? Welcome to the magical world of Peach Cobbler Cupcakes – a delightful fusion of warm peach cobbler and sweet, fluffy cake. Let’s dive into this harvesting adventure and whip up our very own fruity, cakey delight!

Ingredients:

Before we get our hands dirty (and mouths watering), let’s organize our ingredients. We’ll need:

For the Cake:

  • 1 1/3 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/2 cup buttermilk
  • 1 tablespoon peach puree

For the Peach Cobbler Filling:

  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter
  • 3/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 cup brown sugar
  • 2 peaches, peeled and diced

For the Peach Buttercream Frosting:

  • 1 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 3 1/2 cups powdered sugar
  • 1/4 cup peach puree
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/4 teaspoon peach extract
  • Pinch of salt

Instructions:

Now that we have our ingredients, it’s time to get our hands dirty. Let’s bake these Peach Cobbler Cupcakes.

Step 1: Preheat Oven & Prepare Parchment Paper

Start by preheating your oven to 350°F (175°C) and lining a 12-cup muffin tin with cupcake liners. This will ensure a moist and perfectly shaped cupcake.

Step 2: Mix the Peach Cobbler Filling

In a medium-sized pot, melt the 1/2 cup of butter. Once melted, add the 3/4 cup of sugar, 1/4 cup flour, 1/4 teaspoon cinnamon, and 1/4 cup brown sugar. Stir it all together over low heat until the mixture becomes bubbly and thick. Now set it aside to cool.

Step 3: Make the Peach Puree

To ensure that each cupcake is packed with peachy goodness, we’ll need some peach puree. The simplest way to do this is by blending 3-4 peaches. Set aside 1/4 cup of this puree for use in the cake and keep the rest for the frosting.

Step 4: Create the Cake Batter

In a large mixing bowl, beat the softened 1/2 cup of unsalted butter and 1 cup granulated sugar until light and fluffy. Add the 2 large eggs, one at a time, ensuring each is well incorporated. Then, whisk in 1 teaspoon of vanilla extract and 1/2 cup buttermilk.

Using a separate bowl, combine 1 1/3 cups of flour, 1/2 teaspoon baking powder, 1/2 teaspoon baking soda, and 1/4 teaspoon salt. Fold these dry ingredients into the wet mixture a little at a time. Finally, gently stir in the 1/4 cup peach puree.

Step 5: Assemble the Cupcakes

Once the peach cobbler filling has cooled, it’s time to assemble our Peach Cobbler Cupcakes. Using a small cookie scoop, distribute the cake batter evenly into the cupcake liners, filling each about halfway.

Now, spoon the peach cobbler filling on top of the cake batter, being careful not to mix. A little peek-a-boo of peach cobbler should be visible when you put them in the oven.

Step 6: Bake Perfection

Place the cupcake-filled muffin tin in the oven and let them bake for 18-20 minutes, or until the toothpick inserted in the center comes out clean.

Step 7: Frost the Cupcakes

After the cupcakes have cooled, it’s time for the pièce de résistance—the peach buttercream frosting. Beat the softened 1 cup of unsalted butter to achieve a smooth, creamy texture. Gradually add 3 1/2 cups powdered sugar, alternating between additions of 1/4 cup of our reserved peach puree and 1 teaspoon vanilla extract.

Once the frosting is perfect, frosting a cupcake might seem intimidating, but trust me; you’ll have it down in no time. Start by using an off-set spatula to spread a generous layer of frosting on each cupcake. This will create a deliciously irresistible juxtaposition between soft cake and the rich frosting.

Step 8: Enjoy!

Now, gather your friends or family, arrange these delightful desserts on a platter, and prepare to be showered with compliments. Savor the summer in every bite of these Peach Cobbler Cupcakes—packed with peaches tossed in a heavenly cake. Quite simply the epitome of summer, feel the warmth radiate with every cupcake!

We have now successfully completed our little Peach Cobbler Cupcake adventure. The end result will be a memorable burst of summer in each scrumptious bite. Now, go ahead and indulge yourself and those around you in this treasure trove of fruity festivity. May your memories made during this peachy endeavor be as delicious as these cupcakes!
