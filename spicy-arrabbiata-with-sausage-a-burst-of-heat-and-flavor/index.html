Spicy Arrabbiata with Sausage: A Burst of Heat and Flavor


If you’re a fan of bold and fiery flavors, then you’re in for a treat with this Spicy Arrabbiata with Sausage recipe. This classic Italian dish is known for its rich tomato sauce and a kick of heat that will leave your taste buds tingling with delight. And with the addition of savory sausage, it takes the dish to a whole new level of flavor. So, let’s dive right in and discover how to create this burst of heat and flavor in your own kitchen!

Ingredients

  • 1 pound of spicy Italian sausage
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 4 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 can (28 ounces) of crushed tomatoes
  • 1 can (14 ounces) of diced tomatoes
  • 2 tablespoons of tomato paste
  • 1/2 cup of red wine (optional)
  • 1 teaspoon of dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon of dried basil
  • 1/2 teaspoon of red pepper flakes (adjust according to your preferred level of spiciness)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh basil leaves, for garnish
  • Grated Parmesan cheese, for serving

Instructions

  1. In a large skillet, cook the spicy Italian sausage over medium heat until browned and cooked through. Remove the sausage from the skillet and set it aside, leaving any rendered fat in the pan.

  2. In the same skillet, add the chopped onion and minced garlic. Sauté until the onion becomes translucent and the garlic becomes fragrant, about 3-4 minutes.

  3. Add the crushed tomatoes, diced tomatoes, and tomato paste to the skillet. Stir well to combine. If desired, add the red wine to enhance the flavor of the sauce. Bring the sauce to a simmer and let it cook for about 15-20 minutes, allowing the flavors to meld together.

  4. While the sauce is simmering, slice the cooked sausage into bite-sized pieces.

  5. Add the sliced sausage, dried oregano, dried basil, red pepper flakes, salt, and pepper to the skillet. Stir well to incorporate all the ingredients. Let the sauce continue to simmer for an additional 10 minutes to allow the flavors to develop.

  6. Taste the sauce and adjust the seasonings according to your preference. If you prefer a spicier arrabbiata, add more red pepper flakes. If you want a milder version, reduce the amount of red pepper flakes.

  7. Once the sauce is ready, it’s time to serve! Ladle the Spicy Arrabbiata with Sausage over your favorite pasta, such as penne or rigatoni. Garnish with fresh basil leaves and sprinkle with grated Parmesan cheese for an extra burst of flavor.

Tips and Variations

  • If you can’t handle too much heat, you can substitute mild Italian sausage for the spicy version.
  • For a vegetarian version, omit the sausage and add sautéed mushrooms or bell peppers for added texture and flavor.
  • If you prefer a smoother sauce, you can use an immersion blender or a regular blender to puree the sauce until it reaches your desired consistency.
  • This Spicy Arrabbiata with Sausage can be made ahead of time and stored in the refrigerator for up to three days. The flavors will continue to develop and intensify over time.

Now that you have the recipe for this Spicy Arrabbiata with Sausage, it’s time to bring some heat and flavor to your next dinner. Whether you’re cooking for yourself or entertaining guests, this dish is sure to impress with its bold and vibrant flavors. So, go ahead and channel your inner Italian chef and enjoy this burst of heat and flavor!
