Grilled Arancini: Italian Rice Balls on the BBQ


Are you looking for a unique and mouthwatering dish to impress your friends and family at your next barbecue? Look no further than Grilled Arancini: Italian Rice Balls on the BBQ! These crispy, cheesy, and flavorful rice balls are a perfect combination of Italian cuisine and barbecue goodness. In this blog post, we will guide you through the step-by-step process of making these delicious grilled arancini, ensuring that your taste buds are in for a treat.

Ingredients

To make Grilled Arancini, you will need the following ingredients:

  • 2 cups of cooked risotto rice
  • 1 cup of mozzarella cheese, cut into small cubes
  • 1/2 cup of grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/4 cup of chopped fresh basil
  • 1/4 cup of chopped fresh parsley
  • 2 eggs, beaten
  • 1 cup of bread crumbs
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Olive oil for brushing

Instructions

  1. Preheat your barbecue grill to medium heat.

  2. In a large bowl, combine the cooked risotto rice, mozzarella cheese, Parmesan cheese, chopped basil, chopped parsley, and beaten eggs. Mix well until all the ingredients are evenly incorporated. Season with salt and pepper to taste.

  3. Take a small portion of the rice mixture and flatten it in the palm of your hand. Place a cube of mozzarella cheese in the center and shape the rice mixture into a ball, ensuring that the cheese is completely covered. Repeat this process until all the rice mixture is used.

  4. Roll each rice ball in bread crumbs, ensuring that they are evenly coated. This will give the arancini a crispy texture when grilled.

  5. Brush each arancini with olive oil to prevent sticking to the grill.

  6. Place the arancini on the preheated barbecue grill and cook for about 10-12 minutes, turning occasionally, until they are golden brown and crispy on all sides.

  7. Once cooked, remove the arancini from the grill and let them cool for a few minutes before serving. This will allow the cheese to set and prevent any burns.

  8. Serve the grilled arancini hot with your favorite dipping sauce. Marinara sauce or aioli make excellent accompaniments to these delectable rice balls.

Tips and Variations

  • Experiment with different fillings by adding cooked ground meat, sautéed vegetables, or even a touch of truffle oil to the rice mixture before shaping the arancini.
  • If you don’t have a barbecue grill, you can also bake the arancini in a preheated oven at 375°F (190°C) for about 20-25 minutes, or until they are golden brown and crispy.
  • Make a big batch of arancini and freeze them before grilling. This way, you can have a quick and delicious appetizer ready whenever you have guests over.

Conclusion

Grilled Arancini: Italian Rice Balls on the BBQ are a delightful twist on traditional Italian arancini, giving them a smoky and grilled flavor that will surely impress your taste buds. Whether you’re hosting a barbecue party or simply looking for a unique and flavorful appetizer, these grilled arancini are a must-try. So fire up your barbecue grill and get ready to indulge in these crispy and cheesy delights that will leave everyone craving for more. Enjoy!
