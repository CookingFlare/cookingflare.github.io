Greek Lemon Orzo Salad: A Fresh and Zesty Pasta Dish


Are you looking for a light and refreshing pasta salad that bursts with flavor? Look no further than this Greek Lemon Orzo Salad! Packed with vibrant ingredients and zesty flavors, this dish is perfect for picnics, potlucks, or as a side dish for any summer meal. Let’s dive right into the recipe and discover how to create this delightful dish.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup orzo pasta
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons freshly squeezed lemon juice
  • 1 teaspoon lemon zest
  • 1 small cucumber, diced
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1/2 cup Kalamata olives, pitted and sliced
  • 1/4 cup red onion, finely chopped
  • 1/4 cup fresh parsley, chopped
  • 1/4 cup fresh dill, chopped
  • 1/4 cup crumbled feta cheese
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Cook the orzo pasta according to the package instructions until al dente. Drain and rinse under cold water to cool it down.
  2. In a large mixing bowl, combine the cooked orzo pasta, olive oil, lemon juice, and lemon zest. Toss gently to coat the pasta evenly with the dressing.
  3. Add the diced cucumber, cherry tomatoes, Kalamata olives, red onion, parsley, and dill to the bowl. Mix well to combine all the ingredients.
  4. Sprinkle the crumbled feta cheese over the salad and season with salt and pepper to taste. Give it a final toss to ensure everything is well incorporated.
  5. Cover the bowl and refrigerate for at least 30 minutes to let the flavors meld together.
  6. When ready to serve, give the salad a quick stir and adjust the seasoning if needed. Garnish with some extra fresh herbs if desired.
  7. Enjoy this fresh and zesty Greek Lemon Orzo Salad as a standalone meal or as a side dish to complement your main course. It pairs well with grilled chicken, fish, or even lamb.

Why You’ll Love This Recipe

  1. Flavorful and zesty: The combination of lemon juice, lemon zest, and fresh herbs adds a burst of freshness and tanginess to the salad, making it incredibly flavorful.
  2. Light and refreshing: This pasta salad is perfect for hot summer days when you crave something light and refreshing. It won’t weigh you down but will still satisfy your taste buds.
  3. Quick and easy: With minimal cooking involved, this recipe comes together in no time. You can have a delicious salad ready to go within 30 minutes.
  4. Versatile: While this Greek Lemon Orzo Salad is fantastic on its own, you can also customize it to your liking. Feel free to add grilled vegetables, roasted chicken, or even swap out the feta cheese for goat cheese.
  5. Perfect for gatherings: Whether you’re hosting a picnic or attending a potluck, this salad is a crowd-pleaser. Its vibrant colors and enticing flavors will make it the star of any gathering.

So, the next time you’re in the mood for a refreshing pasta salad, give this Greek Lemon Orzo Salad a try. Its zesty flavors and vibrant ingredients will transport you straight to the Mediterranean. Enjoy the taste of summer with every bite!
