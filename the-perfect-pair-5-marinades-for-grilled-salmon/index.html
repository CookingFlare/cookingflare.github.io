The Perfect Pair: 5 Marinades for Grilled Salmon


Salmon is a versatile and delicious fish that is loved by seafood enthusiasts around the world. Its rich flavor and delicate texture make it an ideal choice for grilling. One way to elevate your grilled salmon to new heights is by marinating it. Marinating not only adds flavor but also helps to tenderize the fish, resulting in a juicy and succulent dish. In this blog post, we will explore five mouthwatering marinades that perfectly complement grilled salmon. So fire up your grill and get ready to tantalize your taste buds with these delectable creations.

1. Lemon and Dill Marinade

Ingredients:

  • 1/4 cup fresh lemon juice
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons chopped fresh dill
  • 1 teaspoon minced garlic
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a small bowl, whisk together the lemon juice, olive oil, dill, minced garlic, salt, and pepper.
  2. Place the salmon fillets in a shallow dish and pour the marinade over them, ensuring they are evenly coated.
  3. Cover the dish and refrigerate for at least 30 minutes to allow the flavors to meld.
  4. Preheat your grill to medium-high heat and lightly oil the grates.
  5. Remove the salmon from the marinade, allowing any excess to drip off, and place them on the grill.
  6. Grill the salmon for about 4-5 minutes per side, or until it flakes easily with a fork.
  7. Serve hot and garnish with fresh dill sprigs for an extra touch of elegance.

2. Honey Mustard Marinade

Ingredients:

  • 3 tablespoons Dijon mustard
  • 2 tablespoons honey
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon apple cider vinegar
  • 1 teaspoon minced garlic
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, whisk together the Dijon mustard, honey, soy sauce, apple cider vinegar, minced garlic, salt, and pepper.
  2. Place the salmon fillets in a resealable bag and pour the marinade over them, ensuring they are well-coated.
  3. Seal the bag and refrigerate for at least 1 hour, allowing the flavors to penetrate the fish.
  4. Preheat your grill to medium heat and lightly oil the grates.
  5. Remove the salmon from the marinade, reserving the marinade for basting.
  6. Place the salmon on the grill and cook for about 4-6 minutes per side, basting occasionally with the reserved marinade.
  7. The salmon should be opaque and flake easily when done.
  8. Serve hot and drizzle with any remaining marinade for an extra burst of flavor.

3. Teriyaki Glaze Marinade

Ingredients:

  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons honey
  • 1 tablespoon rice vinegar
  • 1 teaspoon minced ginger
  • 1 teaspoon minced garlic
  • 1/2 teaspoon sesame oil
  • Sesame seeds for garnish

Instructions:

  1. In a small saucepan, combine the soy sauce, honey, rice vinegar, minced ginger, minced garlic, and sesame oil.
  2. Cook over medium heat until the mixture thickens slightly, stirring occasionally.
  3. Remove from heat and let it cool.
  4. Place the salmon fillets in a shallow dish and pour the teriyaki glaze over them, making sure they are well-coated.
  5. Cover the dish and refrigerate for at least 1 hour for the flavors to meld.
  6. Preheat your grill to medium-high heat and lightly oil the grates.
  7. Remove the salmon from the marinade, allowing any excess to drip off, and place them on the grill.
  8. Grill the salmon for about 4-5 minutes per side, or until it reaches your desired level of doneness.
  9. Sprinkle with sesame seeds and serve with steamed rice for a complete meal.

4. Maple Soy Glaze Marinade

Ingredients:

  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons maple syrup
  • 1 tablespoon rice vinegar
  • 1 teaspoon minced garlic
  • 1/2 teaspoon grated fresh ginger
  • 1/2 teaspoon sesame oil
  • Chopped green onions for garnish

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, whisk together the soy sauce, maple syrup, rice vinegar, minced garlic, grated ginger, and sesame oil.
  2. Place the salmon fillets in a resealable bag and pour the marinade over them, ensuring they are well-coated.
  3. Seal the bag and refrigerate for at least 1 hour to allow the flavors to infuse the fish.
  4. Preheat your grill to medium heat and lightly oil the grates.
  5. Remove the salmon from the marinade, reserving the marinade for basting.
  6. Place the salmon on the grill and cook for about 4-6 minutes per side, basting occasionally with the reserved marinade.
  7. The salmon should be flaky and opaque when done.
  8. Sprinkle with chopped green onions and serve with a side of roasted vegetables for a balanced meal.

5. Citrus Herb Marinade

Ingredients:

  • 1/4 cup orange juice
  • 2 tablespoons lemon juice
  • 2 tablespoons lime juice
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 teaspoons chopped fresh thyme
  • 1 teaspoon chopped fresh rosemary
  • 1 teaspoon minced garlic
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a small bowl, whisk together the orange juice, lemon juice, lime juice, olive oil, chopped thyme, chopped rosemary, minced garlic, salt, and pepper.
  2. Place the salmon fillets in a shallow dish and pour the marinade over them, ensuring they are evenly coated.
  3. Cover the dish and refrigerate for at least 30 minutes to allow the flavors to mingle.
  4. Preheat your grill to medium-high heat and lightly oil the grates.
  5. Remove the salmon from the marinade, allowing any excess to drip off, and place them on the grill.
  6. Grill the salmon for about 4-5 minutes per side, or until it flakes easily with a fork.
  7. Serve hot and garnish with a sprig of fresh thyme or rosemary for a pop of color.

Conclusion

Grilled salmon is a delightful dish on its own, but marinating it takes it to a whole new level. With these five tantalizing marinades, you can effortlessly transform your grilled salmon into a gourmet masterpiece. Whether you prefer the bright flavors of lemon and dill, the sweet tang of honey mustard, the savory notes of teriyaki glaze, the richness of maple soy glaze, or the refreshing zest of citrus herb, there’s a marinade here to suit every palate. So next time you fire up the grill, don’t forget to marinate your salmon for an unforgettable dining experience. Happy grilling!
