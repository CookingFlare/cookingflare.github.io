Pumpkin Cheesecake Bars: A Creamy and Spiced Dessert Recipe


If you’re a fan of pumpkin-flavored treats, then these Pumpkin Cheesecake Bars are a must-try! With a creamy, spiced filling and a buttery graham cracker crust, these bars are the perfect combination of pumpkin pie and cheesecake. Whether you’re looking for a dessert to impress your guests at a fall gathering or simply want to indulge in a delightful treat, these bars are sure to satisfy your cravings. Let’s dive into the recipe and discover how to make these delectable Pumpkin Cheesecake Bars!

Ingredients

For the Crust:

  • 1 ½ cups graham cracker crumbs
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, melted

For the Cheesecake Filling:

  • 16 oz cream cheese, softened
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 cup canned pumpkin puree
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 tsp ground cinnamon
  • 1/2 tsp ground nutmeg
  • 1/4 tsp ground cloves
  • 1/4 tsp ground ginger

For the Topping (Optional):

  • Whipped cream
  • Ground cinnamon for dusting

Instructions

Step 1: Preparing the Crust

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C) and line a 9x13-inch baking pan with parchment paper, leaving an overhang on the sides for easy removal.
  2. In a medium bowl, combine the graham cracker crumbs, sugar, and melted butter. Mix until the crumbs are moistened and press the mixture firmly into the bottom of the prepared pan. Bake for 10 minutes, then remove from the oven and set aside.

Step 2: Making the Cheesecake Filling

  1. In a large bowl, beat the softened cream cheese and sugar together until smooth and creamy.
  2. Add the pumpkin puree, eggs, vanilla extract, and spices (cinnamon, nutmeg, cloves, and ginger) to the cream cheese mixture. Beat until well combined and smooth.
  3. Pour the filling over the pre-baked crust and spread it evenly.

Step 3: Baking and Chilling

  1. Bake the Pumpkin Cheesecake Bars for 35-40 minutes, or until the edges are set and the center is slightly jiggly.
  2. Once baked, remove the bars from the oven and allow them to cool at room temperature for 1 hour. Then, transfer the pan to the refrigerator and chill the bars for at least 4 hours, or preferably overnight, to set.

Step 4: Serving

  1. Once the bars are thoroughly chilled and set, use the parchment overhang to lift them out of the pan.
  2. Cut the bars into squares, and if desired, top each square with a dollop of whipped cream and a sprinkle of ground cinnamon before serving.

Conclusion

These Pumpkin Cheesecake Bars are a delightful fusion of fall flavors and creamy indulgence. The warm spices combined with the richness of the cream cheese and the earthy sweetness of pumpkin create a dessert that is both comforting and elegant. Whether you’re hosting a holiday gathering or simply want to treat yourself to something special, these bars are the perfect choice. So, roll up your sleeves, gather your ingredients, and embark on a culinary adventure to create these irresistible Pumpkin Cheesecake Bars. Enjoy every creamy and spiced bite!
