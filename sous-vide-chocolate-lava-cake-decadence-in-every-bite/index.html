Sous Vide Chocolate Lava Cake: Decadence in Every Bite


Indulge your senses with the ultimate chocolate dessert - Sous Vide Chocolate Lava Cake. This velvety, rich, and decadent treat is sure to satisfy your sweet tooth cravings. With the precise cooking technique of sous vide, you can achieve the perfect texture and oozy center that will leave you wanting more. So, grab your apron and let’s dive into the world of chocolatey goodness!

Ingredients

  • 200g dark chocolate (70% cocoa solids), chopped
  • 200g unsalted butter
  • 4 large eggs
  • 150g granulated sugar
  • 100g all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • Optional: powdered sugar or ice cream for serving

Instructions

  1. Preheat your sous vide water bath to 135°F (57°C). This temperature will give you a perfectly molten center with a tender cake exterior.

  2. In a microwave-safe bowl, melt the dark chocolate and butter together in short bursts, stirring in between, until smooth and fully melted. Set aside to cool slightly.

  3. In a separate mixing bowl, whisk the eggs and sugar together until well combined and slightly pale in color. This will help create a light and airy texture in the final cake.

  4. Slowly pour the melted chocolate mixture into the egg mixture, whisking continuously to prevent the eggs from scrambling. Add the vanilla extract and mix until smooth.

  5. Gradually fold in the flour and salt until just combined. Be careful not to overmix, as this can result in a dense cake.

  6. Divide the batter evenly among individual ramekins or silicone molds, filling them about three-quarters full. This recipe makes approximately 6 servings.

  7. Place the ramekins into a large zipper-lock bag, removing as much air as possible before sealing. Alternatively, you can use vacuum-sealed bags for a more precise cooking process.

  8. Submerge the bagged ramekins into the preheated water bath and cook for 45 minutes. The long cooking time ensures the cake is evenly cooked and the center remains delightfully molten.

  9. After the cooking time is up, carefully remove the ramekins from the water bath and allow them to cool for a few minutes. Gently unmold the cakes onto serving plates.

  10. Serve the Sous Vide Chocolate Lava Cakes warm, dusted with powdered sugar, and accompanied by a scoop of your favorite ice cream. The combination of warm, gooey cake and cold, creamy ice cream is simply irresistible.

Tips and Variations

  • Experiment with different types of chocolate to customize the flavor of your lava cakes. Milk chocolate will result in a sweeter, milder taste, while dark chocolate with higher cocoa content adds a bolder, more intense flavor.
  • For an extra indulgent twist, try adding a spoonful of Nutella or peanut butter in the center of each cake before cooking. This will create a delightful surprise when you take your first bite.
  • If you don’t have ramekins or silicone molds, you can use oven-safe mugs or small glass jars instead. Just make sure they are heatproof and won’t crack under the sous vide cooking temperature.

Now that you’ve discovered the magic of Sous Vide Chocolate Lava Cake, you can impress your family and friends with this show-stopping dessert. The combination of the perfectly cooked cake and the luscious molten center will make every bite a truly decadent experience. So, go ahead and treat yourself to this indulgent delight - you deserve it!
