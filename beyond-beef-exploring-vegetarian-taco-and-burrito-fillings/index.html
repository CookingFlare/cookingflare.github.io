Beyond Beef: Exploring Vegetarian Taco and Burrito Fillings


Tacos and burritos are classic Mexican dishes that have become beloved all over the world. Traditionally, these dishes are made with meat fillings, but with the rise in popularity of vegetarian and vegan diets, there has been a demand for delicious plant-based alternatives. That’s where Beyond Beef comes in. In this blog post, we will explore some creative and mouth-watering vegetarian taco and burrito fillings using Beyond Beef.

1. Beyond Beef and Black Bean Tacos

Ingredients:

  • 1 package of Beyond Beef
  • 1 can of black beans, rinsed and drained
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 bell pepper, diced
  • 1 teaspoon of cumin
  • 1 teaspoon of paprika
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Taco shells or tortillas
  • Toppings of your choice (salsa, guacamole, shredded lettuce, etc.)

Instructions:

  1. Heat some oil in a pan over medium heat. Add the diced onion and minced garlic, and sauté until fragrant.
  2. Add the diced bell pepper and sauté for a few more minutes until softened.
  3. Crumble the Beyond Beef into the pan and cook until browned.
  4. Add the black beans, cumin, paprika, salt, and pepper. Mix well and let it simmer for a few minutes to allow the flavors to meld together.
  5. Warm up the taco shells or tortillas in a separate pan or in the oven.
  6. Fill the taco shells or tortillas with the Beyond Beef and black bean mixture.
  7. Top with your favorite toppings and enjoy!

2. Spicy Beyond Beef and Corn Burritos

Ingredients:

  • 1 package of Beyond Beef
  • 1 cup of corn kernels
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 jalapeño pepper, seeded and diced
  • 1 teaspoon of chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon of cayenne pepper (adjust according to your spice preference)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Burrito tortillas
  • Toppings of your choice (sour cream, shredded cheese, diced tomatoes, etc.)

Instructions:

  1. Heat some oil in a pan over medium heat. Add the diced onion, minced garlic, and diced jalapeño pepper. Sauté until fragrant.
  2. Crumble the Beyond Beef into the pan and cook until browned.
  3. Add the corn kernels, chili powder, cayenne pepper, salt, and pepper. Mix well and let it simmer for a few minutes.
  4. Warm up the burrito tortillas in a separate pan or in the oven.
  5. Spoon the Beyond Beef and corn mixture onto the tortillas, leaving some space at the edges.
  6. Fold the sides of the tortillas over the filling, then roll them up tightly.
  7. Optional: Heat the burritos in a pan for a few minutes on each side to make them crispy.
  8. Serve with your favorite toppings and enjoy!

Conclusion

Beyond Beef provides a delicious and satisfying alternative to traditional meat fillings in tacos and burritos. With its meaty texture and rich flavor, it’s perfect for those looking to incorporate more plant-based options into their diet. Whether you choose to make Beyond Beef and black bean tacos or spicy Beyond Beef and corn burritos, these recipes are sure to impress both vegetarians and meat-eaters alike. So go ahead, get creative in the kitchen, and enjoy these mouth-watering vegetarian taco and burrito fillings with Beyond Beef!
