Aromatic and Creamy Chicken Korma Recipe


If you’re a fan of rich and aromatic Indian cuisine, then you’re in for a treat with this Aromatic and Creamy Chicken Korma recipe. This dish is a perfect blend of flavors, with tender chicken pieces simmered in a luscious, creamy sauce that’s infused with fragrant spices. It’s a dish that’s sure to impress your family and friends, and the best part is, it’s easier to make than you might think.

Ingredients

Before we dive into the cooking process, let’s take a look at the ingredients you’ll need to recreate this mouthwatering Chicken Korma:

  • 1.5 lbs (680g) boneless, skinless chicken thighs, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 1 cup (240ml) plain yogurt
  • 2 large onions, finely chopped
  • 4 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1-inch piece of ginger, grated
  • 1 cup (240ml) coconut milk
  • 1/2 cup (120ml) chicken broth
  • 1/2 cup (120ml) heavy cream
  • 1/4 cup (60g) almond meal
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 2 tablespoons ghee
  • 2 teaspoons ground coriander
  • 2 teaspoons ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cardamom
  • 1/2 teaspoon cayenne pepper
  • Salt to taste
  • Chopped fresh cilantro for garnish

Instructions

Marinating the Chicken

  1. In a large bowl, combine the yogurt and a teaspoon each of ground coriander, cumin, and turmeric.
  2. Add the chicken pieces to the bowl and toss them until they are well coated in the marinade.
  3. Cover the bowl with plastic wrap and refrigerate for at least 1 hour, allowing the flavors to infuse into the chicken.

Making the Korma Sauce

  1. Heat the vegetable oil and ghee in a large, deep skillet over medium heat.
  2. Add the chopped onions and cook until they are soft and translucent.
  3. Stir in the garlic, ginger, and the remaining ground spices (coriander, cumin, cinnamon, cardamom, and cayenne pepper).
  4. Cook the mixture for a few minutes until it becomes fragrant.
  5. Add the marinated chicken pieces to the skillet and cook until they are golden brown on all sides.
  6. Pour in the coconut milk and chicken broth, then bring the mixture to a gentle simmer.
  7. Cover the skillet and let the chicken cook in the sauce for about 15-20 minutes, or until it is tender and fully cooked.
  8. Stir in the heavy cream and almond meal, allowing the sauce to thicken slightly.
  9. Season the korma with salt to taste, and let it simmer for a few more minutes to allow the flavors to meld together.

Serving the Chicken Korma

Once the Chicken Korma is ready, spoon it onto a serving dish and garnish it with freshly chopped cilantro. This dish pairs beautifully with steamed basmati rice, naan bread, or even a side of fragrant saffron rice. The creamy, aromatic flavors of the Korma will surely leave everyone at the table satisfied and craving more.

Conclusion

There you have it – a delightful Chicken Korma recipe that’s brimming with aromatic spices and creamy goodness. Whether you’re hosting a dinner party or simply craving a taste of India at home, this recipe is a must-try. The combination of tender chicken and a rich, flavorful sauce makes this dish a standout favorite. So, roll up your sleeves, gather your ingredients, and get ready to impress your taste buds with this Aromatic and Creamy Chicken Korma!
