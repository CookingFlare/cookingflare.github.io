Vegan Chocolate Chip Muffins Recipe with a Crunchy Oat Topping


Are you craving a delicious and satisfying treat that’s vegan-friendly and bursting with chocolatey goodness? Look no further than these delectable Vegan Chocolate Chip Muffins with a delightful crunchy oat topping. These muffins are perfect for breakfast, a snack on the go, or as a sweet indulgence with your afternoon tea. They are easy to make and will leave your kitchen smelling like a cozy bakery. Let’s dive into the recipe and bring these irresistible muffins to life!

Ingredients:

For the Muffins:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/4 cup brown sugar
  • 1 tablespoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup almond milk (or any plant-based milk of your choice)
  • 1/3 cup melted coconut oil
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/2 cup dairy-free chocolate chips

For the Crunchy Oat Topping:

  • 1/2 cup old-fashioned oats
  • 1/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 cup brown sugar
  • 1/4 cup melted coconut oil

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the Oven: Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C) and line a muffin tin with paper liners or lightly grease the cups.

  2. Prepare the Muffin Batter: In a large mixing bowl, whisk together the flour, granulated sugar, brown sugar, baking powder, and salt. In a separate bowl, mix the almond milk, melted coconut oil, and vanilla extract. Pour the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients and stir until just combined. Gently fold in the chocolate chips.

  3. Make the Oat Topping: In a small bowl, combine the oats, flour, brown sugar, and melted coconut oil. Mix until the ingredients are well combined and the mixture has a crumbly texture.

  4. Fill the Muffin Cups: Spoon the muffin batter into the prepared muffin tin, filling each cup about two-thirds full. Sprinkle the oat topping evenly over the muffin batter, pressing it gently to adhere.

  5. Bake to Perfection: Place the muffin tin in the preheated oven and bake for 18-20 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center of a muffin comes out clean.

  6. Cool and Enjoy: Once baked, remove the muffins from the oven and allow them to cool in the tin for 5 minutes before transferring them to a wire rack to cool completely. Now, it’s time to indulge in these irresistible vegan chocolate chip muffins!

Tips and Variations:

  • For a nutty flavor, add a handful of chopped walnuts or almonds to the muffin batter.
  • If you prefer a sweeter topping, sprinkle a little extra brown sugar on the oat topping before baking.
  • These muffins can be stored in an airtight container at room temperature for up to 3 days, or frozen for longer storage.

The Final Word:

These Vegan Chocolate Chip Muffins with a Crunchy Oat Topping are a delightful way to satisfy your sweet tooth while embracing a plant-based lifestyle. The combination of moist, chocolate-studded muffins and the delightful crunch of the oat topping is simply irresistible. Whether you’re a seasoned baker or just beginning to explore the world of vegan baking, this recipe is sure to become a favorite in your collection. So, roll up your sleeves, preheat your oven, and get ready to create magic in your kitchen with these scrumptious vegan muffins!
