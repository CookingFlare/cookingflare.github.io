Delicious and Healthy Paleo-Friendly Beet Recipes


Are you looking for some creative and delicious ways to incorporate beets into your paleo-friendly diet? Look no further! Beets are not only vibrant and beautiful, but they are also incredibly nutritious and versatile. Packed with essential nutrients and antioxidants, beets are a fantastic addition to any paleo diet. In this blog post, we will explore a variety of mouthwatering beet recipes that are not only paleo-friendly but also incredibly delicious and easy to prepare.

Roasted Beet and Arugula Salad

Ingredients

  • 3 medium beets, peeled and sliced
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 4 cups fresh arugula
  • 1/4 cup chopped walnuts
  • 1/4 cup crumbled paleo-friendly feta cheese (optional)

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 400°F (200°C).
  2. Place the sliced beets on a baking sheet, drizzle with olive oil, and season with salt and pepper.
  3. Roast the beets for 30-35 minutes or until tender.
  4. In a large bowl, combine the roasted beets, arugula, walnuts, and feta cheese.
  5. Drizzle with your favorite paleo-friendly vinaigrette and toss to combine.

This vibrant and refreshing salad is a perfect combination of earthy beets, peppery arugula, and crunchy walnuts. The creamy feta cheese, if using, adds a delightful tangy flavor that complements the sweetness of the beets.

Beet and Sweet Potato Hash

Ingredients

  • 2 medium beets, peeled and grated
  • 1 large sweet potato, peeled and grated
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 2 tablespoons ghee or coconut oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley for garnish

Instructions

  1. In a large skillet, heat the ghee or coconut oil over medium heat.
  2. Add the chopped onion and sauté until translucent.
  3. Add the grated beets and sweet potatoes to the skillet, stirring to combine.
  4. Season with salt and pepper, then cover and cook for 10-15 minutes, stirring occasionally, until the vegetables are tender.
  5. Garnish with fresh parsley before serving.

This colorful and hearty beet and sweet potato hash is a delightful dish that can be enjoyed for breakfast, brunch, or as a satisfying side dish with your favorite protein.

Beet and Coconut Milk Soup

Ingredients

  • 4 medium beets, peeled and diced
  • 1 can (13.5 oz) full-fat coconut milk
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 4 cups vegetable or bone broth
  • 1 tablespoon fresh lemon juice
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh cilantro for garnish

Instructions

  1. In a large pot, sauté the chopped onion and garlic until fragrant.
  2. Add the diced beets and sauté for a few minutes.
  3. Pour in the coconut milk and broth, then bring to a gentle boil.
  4. Reduce the heat and simmer for 20-25 minutes, or until the beets are tender.
  5. Using an immersion blender, blend the soup until smooth.
  6. Stir in the fresh lemon juice and season with salt and pepper.
  7. Ladle the soup into bowls and garnish with fresh cilantro.

This velvety beet and coconut milk soup is a comforting and nourishing dish that is perfect for cooler days. The natural sweetness of the beets pairs beautifully with the creamy coconut milk, creating a delightful flavor combination.

Conclusion

Beets are truly a versatile and nutritious vegetable that can be enjoyed in a variety of ways on a paleo diet. From vibrant salads to hearty soups, these recipes showcase the delicious and healthy possibilities of incorporating beets into your meals. Whether you are a beet enthusiast or looking to explore new flavors, these paleo-friendly beet recipes are sure to delight your taste buds and nourish your body. Give them a try and discover the wonderful world of beet-centric dishes!
