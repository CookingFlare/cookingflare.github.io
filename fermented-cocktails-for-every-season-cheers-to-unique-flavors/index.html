Fermented Cocktails for Every Season: Cheers to Unique Flavors


As the seasons change, so do our cravings for refreshing and unique cocktails. While traditional cocktails have their charm, why not take your mixology skills to the next level by experimenting with fermented ingredients? Fermented cocktails offer a delightful twist of flavors, along with the added health benefits of probiotics. In this blog post, we will explore a selection of fermented cocktails that are perfect for every season. So, grab your shaker and let’s get started!

Spring: Hibiscus Kombucha Margarita

Ingredients:

  • 2 oz tequila
  • 1 oz lime juice
  • 1 oz hibiscus kombucha
  • 1/2 oz agave syrup
  • Ice cubes
  • Lime wedge, for garnish

Instructions:

  1. In a cocktail shaker, combine tequila, lime juice, hibiscus kombucha, and agave syrup.
  2. Add a handful of ice cubes and shake vigorously for about 30 seconds.
  3. Strain the mixture into a glass filled with ice.
  4. Garnish with a lime wedge.
  5. Serve and enjoy this refreshing spring cocktail!

Summer: Watermelon Basil Kefir Cooler

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups fresh watermelon chunks
  • 1 cup basil kefir
  • 1 oz vodka (optional)
  • 1/2 oz simple syrup
  • Fresh basil leaves, for garnish
  • Watermelon slice, for garnish

Instructions:

  1. In a blender, combine fresh watermelon chunks and basil kefir.
  2. Blend until smooth.
  3. Add vodka (if desired) and simple syrup. Blend again to combine.
  4. Fill a glass with ice cubes and pour the mixture over it.
  5. Garnish with fresh basil leaves and a watermelon slice.
  6. Sip and savor the flavors of summer with this cooling kefir cocktail!

Fall: Apple Ginger Kombucha Mule

Ingredients:

  • 2 oz vodka
  • 4 oz apple ginger kombucha
  • 1/2 oz lime juice
  • 1/2 oz ginger syrup
  • Apple slices, for garnish
  • Fresh mint leaves, for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Fill a copper mug or glass with ice cubes.
  2. Add vodka, apple ginger kombucha, lime juice, and ginger syrup.
  3. Stir gently to combine.
  4. Garnish with apple slices and fresh mint leaves.
  5. Sip and embrace the cozy flavors of fall with this apple-infused mule!

Winter: Cranberry Orange Juniper Fizz

Ingredients:

  • 2 oz gin
  • 1 oz cranberry juice
  • 1/2 oz orange juice
  • 1/2 oz juniper berry syrup
  • Club soda, to top
  • Fresh cranberries, for garnish
  • Rosemary sprig, for garnish

Instructions:

  1. In a cocktail shaker, combine gin, cranberry juice, orange juice, and juniper berry syrup.
  2. Shake well with ice.
  3. Strain the mixture into a glass filled with ice cubes.
  4. Top with club soda.
  5. Garnish with fresh cranberries and a rosemary sprig.
  6. Sip and embrace the festive flavors of winter with this cranberry-infused fizz!

These fermented cocktails are the perfect way to elevate your mixology game and surprise your guests with unique flavors. Whether it’s the vibrant hibiscus kombucha margarita in spring or the cozy cranberry orange juniper fizz in winter, each season brings its own fermented twist. So, gather your ingredients, get creative, and raise a glass to the wonderful world of fermented cocktails. Cheers!
