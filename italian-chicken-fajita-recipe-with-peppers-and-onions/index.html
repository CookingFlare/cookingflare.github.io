Italian Chicken Fajita Recipe with Peppers and Onions


Are you looking for a delicious and flavorful recipe that combines the best of Italian and Mexican cuisine? Look no further! Today, we’re going to share with you a mouth-watering Italian Chicken Fajita Recipe with Peppers and Onions that’s sure to become a new favorite.

##Ingredients

  • 1 pound boneless, skinless chicken breasts, cut into thin strips
  • 1 large onion, sliced
  • 2 large bell peppers (any color), sliced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1/4 teaspoon red pepper flakes
  • 8 small flour tortillas
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley
  • 2 tablespoons chopped fresh cilantro

##Instructions

###Step 1: Prepare the Chicken and Vegetables

In a large bowl, whisk together olive oil, oregano, basil, salt, black pepper, and red pepper flakes. Add the chicken strips and toss to coat. Cover and refrigerate for at least 30 minutes or up to 2 hours.

In a separate bowl, toss together sliced onions and bell peppers. Set aside.

###Step 2: Cook the Chicken and Vegetables

Heat a large skillet over medium-high heat. Remove the chicken from the marinade, letting any excess liquid drip off. Add the chicken to the skillet and cook until browned and cooked through, about 5-7 minutes. Transfer the chicken to a plate and cover with foil to keep warm.

In the same skillet, add the sliced onions and bell peppers. Cook, stirring occasionally, until the vegetables are tender and lightly charred, about 10-12 minutes.

###Step 3: Assemble the Fajitas

Warm the tortillas by wrapping them in a damp paper towel and microwaving for 20-30 seconds.

To assemble the fajitas, slice the cooked chicken into thin strips. Add a portion of the chicken to a tortilla, followed by a spoonful of the sautéed onions and bell peppers. Top with shredded mozzarella cheese, chopped parsley, and cilantro.

###Step 4: Serve and Enjoy!

Serve immediately and enjoy the delicious flavors of Italy and Mexico combined!

##Tips and Variations

  • For an extra kick, add some diced jalapeños or serrano peppers to the skillet with the onions and bell peppers.
  • Substitute chicken breasts with chicken thighs or chicken sausage for a different flavor profile.
  • Add some diced tomatoes or tomato sauce to the skillet with the chicken and vegetables for extra moisture and flavor.
  • Serve with your favorite toppings, such as sour cream, salsa, or avocado.

##Conclusion

This Italian Chicken Fajita Recipe with Peppers and Onions is a game-changer for anyone looking to spice up their meal routine. With its bold flavors and textures, it’s sure to become a new favorite in your household. So go ahead, give it a try, and enjoy the delicious combination of Italian and Mexican cuisine!
