10 Quick Ground Meat Recipes for Busy Weekdays


When life gets hectic, cooking a delicious meal doesn’t need to be a time-consuming task. Ground meat is a versatile and affordable option that can be transformed into a wide variety of flavorful dishes in no time. Whether you’re juggling work, family, or other responsibilities, these quick ground meat recipes will help you get dinner on the table with minimal effort, all while satisfying your cravings for hearty, savory meals.

1. One-Pan Ground Beef Stir-Fry

This one-pan ground beef stir-fry is a quick and healthy meal that combines the savory goodness of ground beef with a rainbow of vegetables. You can customize it with whatever veggies you have on hand, and it’s ready in under 30 minutes.

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon oyster sauce
  • 1 teaspoon ginger, minced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 bell pepper, thinly sliced
  • 1 zucchini, sliced
  • 1 cup snap peas
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Cooked rice, to serve

Instructions:

  1. Heat olive oil in a large skillet or wok over medium-high heat.
  2. Add the ground beef and cook, breaking it apart with a wooden spoon until browned.
  3. Add garlic and ginger, cooking for another 1-2 minutes until fragrant.
  4. Stir in soy sauce, oyster sauce, and vegetables. Cook for 5-7 minutes, or until vegetables are tender but still crisp.
  5. Season with salt and pepper, and serve over rice for a complete meal.

2. Ground Turkey Tacos

Tacos are a favorite in many households for their speed and versatility. This ground turkey taco recipe is a lighter alternative to traditional beef tacos, and it’s packed with flavor. With just a few ingredients, you can have a taco night on the table in less than 30 minutes.

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb ground turkey
  • 1 packet taco seasoning (or homemade)
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 8 taco shells or tortillas
  • 1 cup shredded lettuce
  • 1 cup diced tomatoes
  • 1/2 cup shredded cheese
  • 1/4 cup sour cream

Instructions:

  1. In a skillet, cook ground turkey over medium heat until browned, breaking it apart as it cooks.
  2. Stir in the taco seasoning and water, cooking until the sauce thickens, about 5 minutes.
  3. Heat the taco shells in the oven or microwave according to package instructions.
  4. Assemble tacos by spooning the turkey mixture into the shells, then topping with lettuce, tomatoes, cheese, and a dollop of sour cream.
  5. Serve with your favorite salsa or guacamole for an extra burst of flavor.

3. Ground Pork and Cabbage Stir-Fry

Ground pork is an often-overlooked ingredient but it’s perfect for stir-fries. This ground pork and cabbage stir-fry is quick, healthy, and full of flavor. The cabbage adds crunch, while the pork gives it a satisfying richness.

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb ground pork
  • 1 small head of cabbage, shredded
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon sesame oil
  • 1 teaspoon rice vinegar
  • 1 teaspoon honey
  • 1/4 teaspoon crushed red pepper flakes (optional)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Green onions for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Heat sesame oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat.
  2. Add ground pork and cook until browned, breaking it up into small pieces.
  3. Add garlic, soy sauce, rice vinegar, and honey, stirring to combine.
  4. Add shredded cabbage and cook for 5-7 minutes, until tender.
  5. Season with salt, pepper, and crushed red pepper flakes.
  6. Garnish with green onions and serve immediately.

4. Ground Beef and Sweet Potato Skillet

A hearty, healthy meal that combines the richness of ground beef with the sweetness of roasted sweet potatoes. This skillet meal is simple, nutritious, and can be on your table in under 30 minutes.

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 2 medium sweet potatoes, diced
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon smoked paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon cumin
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Add diced sweet potatoes and cook, stirring occasionally, until softened and slightly browned, about 10 minutes.
  2. Remove the sweet potatoes from the skillet and set them aside.
  3. In the same skillet, add ground beef, onion, and garlic. Cook until the beef is browned and the onion is soft.
  4. Add smoked paprika, cumin, salt, and pepper. Stir to combine.
  5. Return the sweet potatoes to the skillet, mixing everything together and cooking for an additional 5 minutes.
  6. Garnish with fresh parsley before serving.

5. Ground Chicken Lettuce Wraps

If you’re looking for a low-carb dinner option, ground chicken lettuce wraps are the way to go. These wraps are light, fresh, and packed with flavor. They make a great weeknight dinner or appetizer for a gathering.

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb ground chicken
  • 1/2 cup water chestnuts, chopped
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon hoisin sauce
  • 1 tablespoon rice vinegar
  • 1 teaspoon grated ginger
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 head of iceberg lettuce, leaves separated
  • Chopped green onions for garnish
  • Crushed peanuts for garnish (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Heat a skillet over medium heat and cook the ground chicken until browned, breaking it apart as it cooks.
  2. Add garlic, ginger, soy sauce, hoisin sauce, and rice vinegar. Stir well and cook for 2-3 minutes.
  3. Add chopped water chestnuts and cook for another 2 minutes.
  4. Remove from heat and let it cool slightly.
  5. Spoon the ground chicken mixture into lettuce leaves and top with green onions and crushed peanuts.
  6. Serve as a healthy, flavorful appetizer or light dinner.

6. Spaghetti with Ground Beef Bolognese Sauce

A classic spaghetti Bolognese is always a family favorite. This version uses ground beef to create a rich, savory sauce that pairs perfectly with spaghetti. This dish is simple to make and is ready in under 30 minutes.

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1 jar marinara sauce
  • 1/2 cup onion, diced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 lb spaghetti
  • Fresh basil for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Cook spaghetti according to package instructions.
  2. Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Add onion and garlic, sautéing until soft.
  3. Add ground beef, breaking it up as it cooks. Once browned, stir in marinara sauce and oregano.
  4. Simmer for 10-15 minutes to allow the flavors to meld together.
  5. Drain the pasta and serve it topped with the Bolognese sauce. Garnish with fresh basil.

7. Ground Beef and Rice Stuffed Peppers

Stuffed peppers are a great way to use ground beef and create a complete meal in one dish. This recipe combines ground beef, rice, and seasonings, all stuffed into colorful bell peppers. It’s a filling dinner that’s both easy and satisfying.

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1 cup cooked rice
  • 4 bell peppers, tops cut off and seeds removed
  • 1 can diced tomatoes
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon onion powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1/2 cup shredded cheese (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. In a skillet, cook ground beef until browned. Add garlic powder, onion powder, salt, and pepper.
  3. Stir in the cooked rice and diced tomatoes, cooking for 5 minutes.
  4. Stuff the bell peppers with the beef and rice mixture.
  5. Place the stuffed peppers in a baking dish and bake for 25 minutes.
  6. Top with cheese and bake for an additional 5 minutes until melted.

8. Ground Lamb Meatballs

Ground lamb meatballs are a flavorful twist on the classic meatball. They’re perfectly seasoned with spices and herbs and cook quickly. Serve them with couscous, over pasta, or in a pita for a delicious meal.

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb ground lamb
  • 1/4 cup breadcrumbs
  • 1/4 cup parsley, chopped
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • 1 teaspoon coriander
  • 1 egg
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Olive oil for frying

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, combine ground lamb, breadcrumbs, parsley, cumin, coriander, egg, salt, and pepper. Mix well.
  2. Shape the mixture into meatballs, about 1 inch in diameter.
  3. Heat olive oil in a pan over medium heat. Fry the meatballs for about 8 minutes, turning them occasionally, until browned and cooked through.
  4. Serve with your favorite side dish.

9. Ground Beef Shepherd’s Pie

Shepherd’s pie is a comforting, classic dish that’s perfect for busy nights. This recipe uses ground beef as the filling and is topped with creamy mashed potatoes. It’s a filling dinner that’s sure to please everyone at the table.

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1 cup frozen peas and carrots
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 cups mashed potatoes
  • 1 cup beef broth
  • 1 tablespoon flour
  • 1 teaspoon Worcestershire sauce
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. In a skillet, cook ground beef with onions and garlic until browned. Add frozen peas and carrots.
  3. Stir in flour and Worcestershire sauce. Pour in beef broth, and let the mixture simmer until thickened.
  4. Spread the beef mixture into a baking dish and top with mashed potatoes.
  5. Bake for 20 minutes, until the top is golden brown.

10. Ground Beef and Spinach Pasta Bake

This easy pasta bake combines ground beef, spinach, and cheese for a comforting, cheesy dish that’s perfect for busy weeknights. It’s a simple, delicious way to get dinner on the table quickly.

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1 package pasta (penne or rigatoni)
  • 1 jar marinara sauce
  • 2 cups spinach, chopped
  • 1/2 cup ricotta cheese
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook pasta according to package directions.
  3. In a skillet, brown ground beef. Stir in marinara sauce and spinach. Let it simmer for 5 minutes.
  4. In a baking dish, combine the pasta, beef mixture, ricotta cheese, and half of the mozzarella.
  5. Top with the remaining mozzarella and Parmesan, then bake for 15-20 minutes until bubbly and golden.

These 10 quick ground meat recipes are a lifesaver for busy weekdays. With just a few ingredients and minimal prep time, you can whip up delicious, satisfying meals that your whole family will love. Whether you’re in the mood for tacos, stir-fries, or a comforting pasta bake, there’s something for everyone to enjoy. Keep these recipes in your back pocket for those hectic days when you need a quick dinner solution. Happy cooking!
