Spicy Nut-Free Buffalo Cauliflower Wings Recipe


Craving a spicy, tangy snack that’s also healthy and nut-free? Look no further than these delicious Spicy Nut-Free Buffalo Cauliflower Wings. This recipe is perfect for game day, parties, or simply when you want to enjoy a guilt-free treat. Our buffalo cauliflower wings are crispy, flavorful, and pack just the right amount of heat. Let’s dive into making this crowd-pleasing appetizer.

Ingredients

For the Cauliflower Wings:

  • 1 large head of cauliflower, cut into bite-sized florets
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour (or gluten-free flour)
  • 1 cup water
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon onion powder
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper

For the Buffalo Sauce:

  • 1/2 cup hot sauce (such as Frank’s RedHot)
  • 1/4 cup unsalted butter (or a vegan alternative)
  • 1 tablespoon white vinegar
  • 1/4 teaspoon Worcestershire sauce (ensure it’s nut-free and vegan if needed)
  • 1/4 teaspoon cayenne pepper (optional for extra heat)
  • 1/4 teaspoon garlic powder

For Serving:

  • Celery sticks
  • Carrot sticks
  • Ranch or blue cheese dressing (ensure it’s nut-free)

Instructions

Step 1: Preheat Your Oven

  1. Preheat your oven to 450°F (230°C).
  2. Line a baking sheet with parchment paper or lightly grease it with oil to prevent sticking.

Step 2: Prepare the Cauliflower

  1. Wash and cut the cauliflower into bite-sized florets.
  2. In a large bowl, combine the flour, water, garlic powder, onion powder, paprika, salt, and black pepper. Stir until a smooth batter forms. The consistency should be similar to pancake batter.

Step 3: Coat and Bake the Cauliflower

  1. Dip each cauliflower floret into the batter, ensuring it is well coated. Allow any excess batter to drip off.
  2. Place the coated florets onto the prepared baking sheet in a single layer, making sure they do not touch.
  3. Bake in the preheated oven for 20 minutes, turning the florets halfway through to ensure even baking.

Step 4: Prepare the Buffalo Sauce

  1. While the cauliflower is baking, prepare the buffalo sauce. In a small saucepan over medium heat, combine the hot sauce, butter, white vinegar, Worcestershire sauce, cayenne pepper (if using), and garlic powder.
  2. Stir the mixture until the butter has melted completely and the ingredients are well combined. Remove from heat and set aside.

Step 5: Coat the Baked Cauliflower

  1. After the initial 20 minutes of baking, remove the cauliflower from the oven.
  2. Using a brush or spoon, generously coat each cauliflower floret with the prepared buffalo sauce.
  3. Return the coated cauliflower to the oven and bake for an additional 15-20 minutes, or until the cauliflower is crispy and the sauce is slightly caramelized.

Step 6: Serve and Enjoy

  1. Once the cauliflower wings are done baking, remove them from the oven and let them cool for a few minutes.
  2. Serve the buffalo cauliflower wings with celery sticks, carrot sticks, and your choice of ranch or blue cheese dressing for dipping.

Tips and Variations

  • Gluten-Free Option: Substitute all-purpose flour with a gluten-free flour blend to make this recipe suitable for those with gluten sensitivities.
  • Vegan Option: Use a plant-based butter alternative and ensure your Worcestershire sauce is vegan.
  • Extra Crispy Wings: For a crunchier texture, you can double-bake the cauliflower. After the initial 20 minutes, allow the cauliflower to cool slightly before applying the buffalo sauce and baking again.
  • Air Fryer Method: If you prefer using an air fryer, cook the coated cauliflower at 400°F (200°C) for about 12-15 minutes, shaking the basket halfway through. Apply the sauce and cook for an additional 5 minutes.
  • Milder Version: If you’re sensitive to heat, reduce the amount of hot sauce and omit the cayenne pepper for a milder flavor.

Nutritional Information

Here’s a general overview per serving (assuming 4 servings):

  • Calories: ~150
  • Protein: 3g
  • Carbohydrates: 15g
  • Fat: 10g
  • Fiber: 3g
  • Sugar: 2g
  • Sodium: 600mg

Why You’ll Love This Recipe

These buffalo cauliflower wings are not only a fantastic alternative to traditional chicken wings, but they’re also incredibly versatile. They’re perfect for vegetarians, those with nut allergies, and anyone looking for a lighter, healthier snack option. The combination of crispy baked cauliflower and tangy buffalo sauce provides a satisfying crunch and a delightful burst of flavor.

Perfect for Any Occasion

They’re easy to make, require minimal ingredients, and can be customized to suit various dietary preferences. Plus, their vibrant color and bold flavors make them a standout dish at any gathering.

With their crispy texture, tangy sauce, and easy preparation, they’re sure to become a favorite in your household.
