Thai Minced Pork Salad: A Spicy and Savory Salad


If you’re a fan of Thai cuisine, you’re in for a treat with this flavorful and aromatic Thai Minced Pork Salad. Also known as “Larb Moo,” this dish is a perfect combination of spicy, savory, and refreshing flavors that will tantalize your taste buds. The balance of heat, tanginess, and umami makes this salad a crowd-pleaser and a delightful addition to any meal. Whether you’re hosting a dinner party or simply craving a taste of Thailand, this recipe is sure to impress.

Ingredients

For the Pork Marinade:

  • 500g ground pork
  • 2 tablespoons fish sauce
  • 2 tablespoons lime juice
  • 1 tablespoon palm sugar
  • 1 teaspoon chili flakes
  • 2 shallots, finely sliced
  • 2 spring onions, finely chopped
  • 2 tablespoons roasted rice powder
  • Fresh cilantro, chopped

For the Salad:

  • 1 cup fresh mint leaves
  • 1 cup fresh cilantro leaves
  • 1 cup fresh basil leaves
  • 1/2 cup chopped green beans
  • 1/2 cup sliced shallots
  • 1/4 cup sliced spring onions
  • 2-3 fresh red chilies, sliced
  • 2 tablespoons toasted rice powder

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Pork:

    • In a bowl, combine the ground pork, fish sauce, lime juice, palm sugar, and chili flakes. Mix well and let it marinate for at least 15 minutes to allow the flavors to meld together.

  2. Cook the Pork:

    • In a skillet over medium heat, cook the marinated pork until it’s browned and fully cooked. Set aside to cool slightly.

  3. Assemble the Salad:

    • In a large mixing bowl, combine the cooked pork, fresh mint, cilantro, basil, green beans, shallots, spring onions, and red chilies.

  4. Add the Final Touches:

    • Sprinkle the toasted rice powder over the salad and gently toss everything together to ensure the flavors are well distributed.

  5. Serve and Enjoy:

    • Transfer the Thai Minced Pork Salad to a serving platter and garnish with additional cilantro and mint leaves. This salad is best enjoyed with a side of fresh lettuce leaves and sticky rice.

Tips and Tricks

  • For an extra kick of heat, add more chili flakes or fresh chilies to suit your spice preference.
  • Feel free to adjust the level of sweetness and tanginess by adding more palm sugar or lime juice, according to your taste.

The Flavorful Delight

Thai Minced Pork Salad is a delightful combination of textures and flavors. The aromatic blend of fresh herbs, zesty lime juice, and the subtle sweetness of palm sugar creates a harmonious symphony for your taste buds. The tender minced pork, accented by the toasted rice powder, adds a satisfying depth to the dish. This salad is not only a feast for the senses but also a celebration of Thai culinary artistry.

Whether you’re exploring Thai cuisine for the first time or seeking to expand your cooking repertoire, this recipe is a must-try. Its vibrant flavors and simple preparation make it an ideal dish for both casual gatherings and special occasions. So, roll up your sleeves, gather your ingredients, and embark on a culinary journey to Thailand with this tantalizing Thai Minced Pork Salad.
