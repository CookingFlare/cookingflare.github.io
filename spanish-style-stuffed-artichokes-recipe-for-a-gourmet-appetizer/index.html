Spanish Style Stuffed Artichokes: Recipe for a Gourmet Appetizer


Artichokes are a versatile and delicious vegetable that can be transformed into a variety of mouthwatering dishes. One popular way to enjoy artichokes is by stuffing them with a delectable mixture of ingredients, creating a gourmet appetizer that is sure to impress your guests. In this recipe, we will explore how to make Spanish Style Stuffed Artichokes, a flavorful dish that combines the richness of chorizo, the tanginess of Manchego cheese, and the freshness of herbs.

Ingredients

  • 4 large artichokes
  • 1 lemon, halved
  • 200g chorizo, finely chopped
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 100g Manchego cheese, grated
  • 2 tablespoons fresh parsley, chopped
  • 2 tablespoons fresh thyme, chopped
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • Olive oil, for drizzling

Instructions

  1. Start by preparing the artichokes. Trim the stem and remove any tough outer leaves. Cut off the top third of each artichoke and rub the cut surfaces with a lemon half to prevent discoloration. Use a spoon to remove the furry choke from the center of each artichoke, creating a hollow cavity. Place the artichokes in a large pot of salted boiling water, cover, and cook for about 20 minutes, or until tender. Drain and set aside.

  2. In a skillet, heat a drizzle of olive oil over medium heat. Add the chorizo and cook until browned and crispy, about 5 minutes. Remove the chorizo from the skillet and set aside.

  3. In the same skillet, add the onion and garlic. Sauté until translucent and fragrant, about 3 minutes. Remove from heat and let cool slightly.

  4. In a mixing bowl, combine the cooked chorizo, sautéed onion and garlic, grated Manchego cheese, chopped parsley, and thyme. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Mix well until all the ingredients are evenly combined.

  5. Preheat your oven to 180°C (350°F).

  6. Take each cooked artichoke and gently spread the leaves apart to create space for the stuffing. Spoon the chorizo mixture into the center of each artichoke, packing it tightly. Place the stuffed artichokes in a baking dish and drizzle with a little olive oil.

  7. Bake the artichokes in the preheated oven for about 15 minutes, or until the cheese is melted and the tops are golden brown.

  8. Once cooked, remove the artichokes from the oven and let them cool slightly before serving. These Spanish Style Stuffed Artichokes are best enjoyed warm, allowing the flavors to meld together. Serve as a sophisticated appetizer at your next dinner party or enjoy them as a delightful snack.

Conclusion

Spanish Style Stuffed Artichokes are a gourmet appetizer that combines the unique flavors of chorizo, Manchego cheese, and fresh herbs. The tender artichokes provide the perfect vessel for the rich and savory stuffing, creating a dish that is as visually stunning as it is delicious. Whether you’re hosting a dinner party or looking to elevate your next snack, give this recipe a try and impress your guests with this Spanish-inspired creation. Enjoy!
