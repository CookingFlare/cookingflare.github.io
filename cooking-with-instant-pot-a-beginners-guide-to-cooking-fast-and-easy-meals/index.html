Cooking with Instant Pot: A Beginner's Guide to Cooking Fast and Easy Meals


Welcome to our beginner’s guide to cooking with Instant Pot! If you’re new to this magical kitchen appliance, you’re in for a treat. The Instant Pot is a multi-functional pressure cooker that can help you whip up delicious meals in a fraction of the time compared to traditional cooking methods. In this guide, we’ll walk you through the basics of using an Instant Pot and share some mouthwatering recipes to get you started.

Getting Started with Your Instant Pot

Before we dive into the recipes, let’s go over the basics of using an Instant Pot. Here are a few key things to keep in mind:

  1. Safety First: Always ensure that the Instant Pot is properly sealed and the pressure valve is in the correct position before starting any cooking process. Follow the manufacturer’s instructions carefully to avoid any accidents.

  2. Understanding the Buttons: Instant Pots come with a variety of buttons, each serving a specific purpose. Become familiar with the different functions such as sauté, pressure cook, steam, and slow cook. This will allow you to make the most of your Instant Pot’s capabilities.

  3. Cooking Times: One of the best features of the Instant Pot is its ability to cook food quickly. However, it’s important to note that the cooking times may vary depending on the recipe and the quantity of ingredients. Experimentation and practice will help you determine the perfect cooking time for your favorite dishes.

  4. Natural Pressure Release vs. Quick Release: After cooking, you have the option to release the pressure naturally or quickly. Natural pressure release involves allowing the Instant Pot to cool down on its own, while quick release involves manually manipulating the pressure valve to release the pressure. Different recipes may require different release methods, so be sure to follow the instructions provided.

Now that you’re acquainted with the basics, it’s time to put your Instant Pot to work! Here are three delicious recipes to get you started:

Recipe 1: Creamy Chicken and Mushroom Pasta

Ingredients:

  • 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 8 ounces of mushrooms, sliced
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 cup of chicken broth
  • 1 cup of heavy cream
  • 8 ounces of pasta (your choice)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Set your Instant Pot to sauté mode and heat some oil. Sauté the chicken until browned on both sides. Remove and set aside.
  2. In the same pot, add the mushrooms and garlic. Sauté until the mushrooms are golden brown.
  3. Add the chicken broth and deglaze the pot, scraping up any browned bits from the bottom.
  4. Add the chicken back to the pot, along with the pasta. Pour in the heavy cream and season with salt and pepper.
  5. Close the Instant Pot lid and set it to pressure cook for 5 minutes.
  6. Once the cooking is complete, perform a quick release and carefully open the lid.
  7. Give everything a good stir and let it sit for a few minutes to thicken.
  8. Serve hot, garnished with fresh parsley.

Recipe 2: Beef Stew

Ingredients:

  • 1.5 pounds of beef stew meat, cubed
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 carrots, sliced
  • 2 potatoes, cubed
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 2 cups of beef broth
  • 1 tablespoon of tomato paste
  • 1 teaspoon of dried thyme
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Set your Instant Pot to sauté mode and heat some oil. Brown the beef cubes on all sides. Remove and set aside.
  2. In the same pot, add the onions, carrots, and potatoes. Sauté until the onions are translucent.
  3. Add the garlic, tomato paste, and dried thyme. Stir to combine.
  4. Pour in the beef broth and season with salt and pepper.
  5. Add the beef back to the pot and give everything a good stir.
  6. Close the Instant Pot lid and set it to pressure cook for 35 minutes.
  7. Once the cooking is complete, perform a natural pressure release.
  8. Carefully open the lid and give the stew a good stir before serving.

Recipe 3: Vegetarian Chili

Ingredients:

  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 bell peppers, chopped
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 can of kidney beans, drained and rinsed
  • 1 can of black beans, drained and rinsed
  • 1 can of diced tomatoes
  • 1 cup of vegetable broth
  • 1 tablespoon of chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon of cumin
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Set your Instant Pot to sauté mode and heat some oil. Sauté the onions, bell peppers, and garlic until softened.
  2. Add the kidney beans, black beans, diced tomatoes, vegetable broth, chili powder, cumin, salt, and pepper. Stir to combine.
  3. Close the Instant Pot lid and set it to pressure cook for 10 minutes.
  4. Once the cooking is complete, perform a quick release and carefully open the lid.
  5. Give the chili a good stir and let it sit for a few minutes to thicken.
  6. Serve hot, topped with your favorite garnishes such as shredded cheese, sour cream, or chopped cilantro.

Congratulations! You’ve completed the beginner’s guide to cooking with an Instant Pot. With these recipes and tips, you’ll be able to create fast, easy, and delicious meals in no time. Experiment with different ingredients and flavors to make the recipes your own. Happy cooking!
