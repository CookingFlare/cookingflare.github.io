Start Your Day Right: Beginner's Breakfast Burrito Recipe


Are you tired of eating the same old cereal or toast for breakfast? Spice up your mornings with this delicious and easy-to-make Beginner’s Breakfast Burrito recipe. Packed with protein, veggies, and a burst of flavors, this breakfast burrito will leave you energized and ready to tackle the day ahead. So, let’s get started!

Ingredients

  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 tortilla wraps
  • 1/4 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1/4 cup diced bell peppers
  • 1/4 cup diced onions
  • 2 tablespoons diced tomatoes
  • 2 tablespoons chopped fresh cilantro
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Olive oil for cooking

Instructions

  1. Heat a drizzle of olive oil in a skillet over medium heat. Add the diced bell peppers and onions, and sauté until they become tender and slightly caramelized.

  2. In a bowl, whisk the eggs with salt and pepper until well beaten. Pour the beaten eggs into the skillet with the sautéed bell peppers and onions. Cook, stirring occasionally, until the eggs are fully cooked and scrambled.

  3. Warm the tortilla wraps in a separate skillet or in the microwave for a few seconds to make them more pliable.

  4. Divide the scrambled eggs equally between the two tortilla wraps, spreading them out in a line down the center of each wrap.

  5. Sprinkle the shredded cheddar cheese, diced tomatoes, and chopped cilantro evenly over the scrambled eggs.

  6. Fold one side of the tortilla wrap over the filling, then fold in the two opposite sides to enclose the filling. Roll the burrito tightly, starting from the filled side, until it is completely wrapped.

  7. Heat a clean skillet over medium heat and lightly coat it with olive oil. Place the wrapped burrito in the skillet, seam-side down, and cook for 2-3 minutes on each side, or until the tortilla is golden brown and crispy.

  8. Once cooked, remove the burrito from the skillet and let it cool slightly before slicing it in half diagonally.

Tips and Variations

  • Feel free to customize your breakfast burrito by adding other ingredients like cooked bacon, diced avocado, or a dollop of sour cream.
  • If you prefer a spicier kick, you can add some chopped jalapeños or a sprinkle of hot sauce to the filling before rolling the burrito.
  • To make this recipe even quicker, you can prepare the scrambled eggs and sautéed veggies in advance and store them in the refrigerator. When you’re ready to assemble the burrito, simply reheat the filling and proceed with the remaining steps.

Enjoy your homemade Beginner’s Breakfast Burrito with a side of fresh fruit or a steaming cup of coffee. This versatile recipe is perfect for breakfast on the go or a lazy weekend brunch. Give it a try and start your day right with a delicious and satisfying meal!
