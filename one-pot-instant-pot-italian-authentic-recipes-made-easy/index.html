One-Pot Instant Pot Italian: Authentic Recipes Made Easy


Are you a fan of Italian cuisine but find the thought of making intricate dishes daunting? Fear not, because with the magic of the Instant Pot and the simplicity of one-pot recipes, you can enjoy delicious and authentic Italian flavors with ease. From hearty pasta dishes to flavorful risottos, we’ve got you covered with these mouthwatering recipes that will transport your taste buds to Italy in no time.

Classic Spaghetti Bolognese

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can crushed tomatoes
  • 2 cups beef broth
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 12 oz spaghetti
  • Fresh basil leaves for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Set the Instant Pot to sauté mode and brown the ground beef.
  2. Add the onions and garlic, sauté until translucent.
  3. Pour in the crushed tomatoes, beef broth, oregano, salt, and pepper. Stir well.
  4. Break the spaghetti in half and layer it on top of the mixture.
  5. Close the Instant Pot lid, set to manual high pressure for 8 minutes.
  6. Once done, do a quick pressure release, stir well, and serve hot garnished with fresh basil.

Enjoy the rich and meaty flavors of this classic Italian dish without the fuss of multiple pots and pans!

Creamy Mushroom Risotto

Ingredients:

  • 1 ½ cups Arborio rice
  • 4 cups chicken or vegetable broth
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 8 oz mushrooms, sliced
  • ½ cup white wine
  • ½ cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Turn on the Instant Pot to sauté mode and sauté the onions and garlic until soft.
  2. Add the mushrooms and cook until they release their juices.
  3. Stir in the Arborio rice until well coated.
  4. Pour in the white wine and let it simmer for a few minutes.
  5. Add the broth, salt, and pepper. Close the lid and cook on manual high pressure for 6 minutes.
  6. Once done, do a quick pressure release, stir in the Parmesan cheese, and let it sit for a few minutes before serving with a sprinkle of fresh parsley.

Indulge in the creamy and luxurious texture of this comforting Mushroom Risotto that will surely impress your family and friends.

Tuscan Chicken Pasta

Ingredients:

  • 2 chicken breasts, diced
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1 can diced tomatoes
  • 2 cups chicken broth
  • 8 oz penne pasta
  • ½ cup heavy cream
  • Fresh spinach leaves
  • Grated Pecorino Romano cheese

Instructions:

  1. Season the chicken with basil, thyme, and paprika. Sauté in the Instant Pot until browned.
  2. Add the diced tomatoes, chicken broth, and penne pasta.
  3. Close the lid and cook on manual high pressure for 4 minutes.
  4. Quick release the pressure, stir in the heavy cream and spinach until wilted.
  5. Serve hot with a generous sprinkle of Pecorino Romano cheese on top.

This Tuscan Chicken Pasta is a delightful combination of tender chicken, flavorful tomatoes, and creamy sauce that will have you coming back for seconds.

Whether you’re a busy parent, a cooking enthusiast, or just someone who appreciates the convenience of one-pot meals, these Instant Pot Italian recipes are sure to satisfy your cravings for authentic flavors without the hassle. From the comforting Creamy Mushroom Risotto to the classic Spaghetti Bolognese and the flavorful Tuscan Chicken Pasta, these dishes are perfect for busy weeknights or cozy weekends. Let the Instant Pot work its magic and indulge in these authentic Italian flavors made easy. Happy cooking!
