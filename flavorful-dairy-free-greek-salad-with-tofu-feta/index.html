Flavorful Dairy-Free Greek Salad with Tofu Feta


Are you craving a refreshing and nutritious dish that’s bursting with Mediterranean flavors? Look no further than this delightful Dairy-Free Greek Salad with Tofu Feta! This recipe offers a vegan twist on the classic Greek salad, featuring a harmonious blend of fresh vegetables, herbs, and a tangy tofu feta that will tantalize your taste buds. Whether you’re following a dairy-free diet, looking to incorporate more plant-based meals into your routine, or simply seeking a delicious and wholesome dish, this Greek salad is sure to become a staple in your recipe collection.

Ingredients

For the Tofu Feta

  • 1 block extra-firm tofu
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons lemon juice
  • 2 tablespoons apple cider vinegar
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon onion powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste

For the Salad

  • 4 large ripe tomatoes, chopped
  • 1 English cucumber, diced
  • 1 red onion, thinly sliced
  • 1 green bell pepper, seeded and chopped
  • 1/2 cup Kalamata olives, pitted
  • 1/4 cup fresh parsley, chopped
  • 1/4 cup fresh mint, chopped

For the Dressing

  • 1/4 cup extra-virgin olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons red wine vinegar
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

Tofu Feta Preparation

  1. Begin by pressing the block of tofu to remove excess moisture. Once pressed, cut the tofu into small cubes and place them in a shallow dish.
  2. In a small bowl, whisk together the olive oil, lemon juice, apple cider vinegar, dried oregano, garlic powder, onion powder, salt, and pepper.
  3. Pour the marinade over the tofu cubes, ensuring they are well coated. Cover the dish and refrigerate for at least 2 hours to allow the flavors to meld.

Salad Preparation

  1. In a large bowl, combine the chopped tomatoes, diced cucumber, sliced red onion, chopped green bell pepper, and pitted Kalamata olives.
  2. Toss the vegetables together gently to mix.
  3. Add the fresh parsley and mint to the bowl and toss again to distribute the herbs evenly.

Dressing Preparation

  1. In a small bowl, whisk together the extra-virgin olive oil, red wine vinegar, minced garlic, dried oregano, salt, and pepper until emulsified.

Assembly

  1. Once the tofu feta has marinated, remove it from the refrigerator and drain off any excess marinade.
  2. Gently fold the tofu feta into the prepared salad, ensuring it is evenly distributed.
  3. Drizzle the dressing over the salad and toss gently to coat the ingredients with the flavorful dressing.

Serving

This Dairy-Free Greek Salad with Tofu Feta is best served immediately to enjoy the vibrant flavors and textures at their peak. However, it can also be refrigerated for up to 2 hours before serving, allowing the flavors to further develop. This salad makes a delightful side dish for a summer barbecue, a light lunch option, or a colorful addition to a mezze platter.

Conclusion

With its vibrant colors, fresh flavors, and dairy-free twist, this Greek salad is a true celebration of the Mediterranean culinary tradition. The tofu feta adds a creamy, tangy element that beautifully complements the crisp vegetables and zesty dressing. Whether you’re a seasoned chef or a novice cook, this recipe is a wonderful introduction to the world of dairy-free and plant-based cooking. So, embrace the flavors of Greece and indulge in this tantalizing Dairy-Free Greek Salad with Tofu Feta!
