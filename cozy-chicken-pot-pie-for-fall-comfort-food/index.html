Cozy Chicken Pot Pie for Fall Comfort Food


As the leaves turn golden and the air becomes crisp, there’s nothing quite like a comforting, hearty dish to warm the soul. And what better way to embrace the cozy vibes of fall than with a delicious homemade chicken pot pie? This classic dish is the epitome of comfort food, with its flaky crust, creamy filling, and tender chunks of chicken. Whether you’re hosting a gathering with loved ones or simply craving a satisfying meal, this cozy chicken pot pie is sure to hit the spot.

Ingredients

For the Crust:

  • 2 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup unsalted butter, cold and cubed
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 6-8 tablespoons ice water

For the Filling:

  • 1 1/2 lbs chicken breast, cooked and cubed
  • 1 cup carrots, diced
  • 1 cup peas
  • 1 cup celery, diced
  • 1/2 cup onion, chopped
  • 1/3 cup unsalted butter
  • 1/2 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 1/2 cups chicken broth
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

Step 1: Prepare the Crust

  1. In a large mixing bowl, combine the flour and salt.
  2. Add the cold, cubed butter to the flour mixture.
  3. Use a pastry cutter or two forks to cut the butter into the flour until the mixture resembles coarse crumbs.
  4. Gradually add the ice water, 1 tablespoon at a time, and mix until the dough comes together.
  5. Divide the dough in half, shape into discs, wrap in plastic wrap, and refrigerate for at least 1 hour.

Step 2: Make the Filling

  1. In a large skillet, melt the butter over medium heat.
  2. Add the carrots, peas, celery, and onion to the skillet and cook until the vegetables are tender.
  3. Stir in the flour and cook for 1-2 minutes.
  4. Gradually whisk in the chicken broth and milk until the mixture is smooth and thickened.
  5. Add the cooked chicken, dried thyme, salt, and pepper, and stir to combine. Remove from heat and set aside.

Step 3: Assemble and Bake

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Roll out one of the refrigerated dough discs on a floured surface to fit a 9-inch pie dish.
  3. Pour the chicken filling into the pie crust.
  4. Roll out the second dough disc and place it over the filling. Trim and crimp the edges, then cut slits in the top crust for ventilation.
  5. Place the pot pie on a baking sheet and bake for 45-50 minutes, or until the crust is golden brown and the filling is bubbling.

Serving Suggestions

This cozy chicken pot pie pairs wonderfully with a simple green salad dressed with a light vinaigrette. For a heartier meal, serve it alongside mashed potatoes or roasted vegetables. The creamy, savory filling and flaky crust make it a perfect centerpiece for a comforting fall dinner.

Conclusion

With its irresistible aroma and comforting flavors, this cozy chicken pot pie is the ultimate way to embrace the warm, inviting spirit of fall. Whether you’re enjoying it with family or savoring it as a solo treat, this classic dish is sure to evoke feelings of nostalgia and contentment. So, roll up your sleeves, gather your ingredients, and get ready to savor the delightful satisfaction of a homemade chicken pot pie this fall. Embrace the season and indulge in the simple joy of good food and cherished company.
