Savory Mexican Chicken Enchilada Casserole: A Crowd-Pleasing Dish


If you’re craving the flavors of Mexico in a comforting, crowd-pleasing dish, look no further than this savory Mexican Chicken Enchilada Casserole. This recipe brings together the vibrant and bold flavors of Mexican cuisine in a hearty and satisfying casserole that is perfect for family dinners, potlucks, or casual gatherings. Packed with tender chicken, flavorful enchilada sauce, gooey cheese, and layers of tortillas, this casserole is sure to become a staple in your recipe collection.

Ingredients

For the Enchilada Sauce:

  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • 4 tablespoons chili powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon cumin
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 2 cups chicken broth

For the Casserole:

  • 3 cups shredded cooked chicken
  • 12 corn tortillas
  • 2 cups shredded Monterey Jack cheese
  • 1 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1/2 cup chopped fresh cilantro
  • 1/2 cup diced red onion
  • 1 can (15 ounces) black beans, drained and rinsed
  • 1 can (15 ounces) corn kernels, drained
  • 1 can (4 ounces) diced green chilies
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Enchilada Sauce:

    • In a saucepan, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Stir in the flour and cook for 1 minute.
    • Add the chili powder, cumin, garlic powder, oregano, salt, and black pepper. Cook for 1 more minute.
    • Gradually whisk in the chicken broth, and bring the mixture to a simmer. Cook for 10-15 minutes, or until the sauce has thickened.

  2. Assemble the Casserole:

    • Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C). Grease a 9x13 inch baking dish.
    • Spread a thin layer of the enchilada sauce on the bottom of the baking dish.
    • Layer 6 tortillas on top of the sauce, overlapping to cover the bottom of the dish.
    • In a large bowl, combine the shredded chicken, black beans, corn, green chilies, cilantro, and red onion. Season with salt and pepper.
    • Spread half of the chicken mixture over the tortillas, then top with 1/3 of the remaining enchilada sauce and 1/3 of the Monterey Jack and cheddar cheeses.
    • Repeat the layers with the remaining tortillas, chicken mixture, sauce, and cheese.
    • Cover the dish with foil and bake for 20 minutes. Remove the foil and bake for an additional 10 minutes, or until the cheese is melted and bubbly.

  3. Serve and Enjoy:

    • Let the casserole cool for a few minutes before slicing.
    • Garnish with fresh cilantro, diced red onion, and a dollop of sour cream, if desired.
    • Serve the Mexican Chicken Enchilada Casserole hot and enjoy the delicious flavors of Mexico in every bite!

Conclusion

This Savory Mexican Chicken Enchilada Casserole is a delightful fusion of flavors and textures that will have your family and friends coming back for seconds. Whether you’re hosting a casual dinner or simply want to treat your loved ones to a special meal, this dish is a surefire way to impress. With its layers of tender chicken, zesty enchilada sauce, and gooey cheese, this casserole captures the essence of Mexican cuisine in every bite. So, gather your ingredients, preheat your oven, and get ready to savor the irresistible aromas of this crowd-pleasing dish.
