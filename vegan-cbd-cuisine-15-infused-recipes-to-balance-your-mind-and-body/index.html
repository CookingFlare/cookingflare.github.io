2. CBD-infused Chickpea Curry

A perfect blend of spices, hearty chickpeas, and a hint of CBD, for a perfect meal that calms the soul.

Ingredients:

  • 1 can of chickpeas, drained and rinsed
  • 1 large onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon of freshly grated ginger
  • 1 tablespoon of curry powder
  • 1 can of diced tomatoes
  • 1 ½ cups of vegetable broth
  • 2 tablespoons of CBD-infused coconut oil
  • ½ teaspoon of turmeric
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Heat CBD-infused coconut oil in a large pan over medium heat.
  2. Add onion and cook until softened.
  3. Stir in garlic and ginger and cook for a minute.
  4. Add curry powder, turmeric, and cook for another minute.
  5. Add chickpeas, diced tomatoes, and vegetable broth, and bring to simmer.
  6. Allow the curry to cook uncovered for at least 20 minutes to let flavors meld. Season with salt and pepper to your taste.
  7. Serve your CBD-infused chickpea curry with a side of steamed rice for a beautiful symphony of flavors and a relaxed body!

3. Edible CBD Chocolate Truffles

Indulge in the excellent taste of these Vegan CBD-infused Chocolate Truffles to pamper your body and soul.

Ingredients:

  • 8 ounces of dark chocolate (vegan and organic)
  • 1/3 cup of almond milk
  • 1 tablespoon of CBD isolate
  • ½ teaspoon of vanilla extract
  • Almond flour, for rolling (around ½ cup)

Instructions:

  1. In a double boiler, melt the dark chocolate with almond milk, stirring frequently until smooth.
  2. Remove from heat and add CBD isolate and vanilla extract, and stir until well combined.
  3. Refrigerate the mixture for at least 1 hour, or until fully hardened.
  4. Once hardened, scoop out small portions using a teaspoon and roll them in almond flour to coat.
  5. Store your CBD-infused Chocolate Truffles in the refrigerator and enjoy these heavenly treats!

4. CBD-Infused Vegan Pasta with Walnut Pesto

A dipping fork into this smooth, creamy pesto waiting at the base of a plate, dessert rather than dinner!

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup of pasta (such as penne or fusilli)
  • 1 cup of walnuts
  • 2 cups of fresh basil leaves
  • 4 cloves of garlic
  • 1 cup of hemp milk
  • ¼ cup of olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • CBD-infused olive oil (1 tablespoon)

Instructions:

  1. Cook pasta according to the package instructions until al dente.
  2. While the pasta is cooking, place walnuts, basil leaves, and garlic in a food processor and pulse until coarsely chopped.
  3. Add hemp milk and olive oil, pulse until smooth. Mix in CBD-infused olive oil and season with salt and pepper.
  4. Toss the cooked pasta with the CBD-infused walnut pesto until well coated, and enjoy a fantastic burst of flavors!

5. CBD Lemon Bars

Put a twist on a classic dessert and soothe your senses with lemon bars infused with the goodness of CBD!

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups of almond flour
  • 1/3 cup of coconut oil
  • ½ cup of maple syrup
  • 1 tablespoon of CBD isolate
  • 1 cup of lemon juice
  • 1 cup of maple syrup
  • ½ cup of coconut oil

Line a baking dish with parchment paper and proceed as follows:

For the crust:

  1. Combine almond flour, melted coconut oil, maple syrup, and CBD isolate in a mixing bowl.
  2. Press the crust mixture evenly into the prepared baking dish.
  3. Bake for 20 minutes until lightly golden and set aside.

For the filling:

  1. Whisk together lemon juice, maple syrup, and melted coconut oil until smooth.
  2. Pour the mixture over the baked crust and bake the bars for another 20 minutes.
  3. Allow the bars to cool completely before slicing and serving.
