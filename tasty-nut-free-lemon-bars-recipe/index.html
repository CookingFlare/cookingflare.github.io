Tasty Nut-Free Lemon Bars Recipe


Are you looking for a refreshing and delightful dessert that’s perfect for any occasion? Look no further than these delectable nut-free lemon bars! The combination of tangy lemon filling and buttery crust makes these bars a crowd-pleaser, and the best part is that they are completely nut-free, making them a safe option for those with nut allergies. Whether you’re hosting a summer gathering, planning a picnic, or simply craving a zesty treat, these lemon bars are sure to hit the spot.

Ingredients

For the Crust:

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1/4 cup powdered sugar
  • A pinch of salt

For the Lemon Filling:

  • 4 large eggs
  • 1 1/2 cups granulated sugar
  • 1/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2/3 cup freshly squeezed lemon juice
  • Zest of 2 lemons
  • Powdered sugar for dusting

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Crust:

    • Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C) and line an 8x8 inch baking pan with parchment paper.
    • In a mixing bowl, cream together the softened butter and powdered sugar until light and fluffy.
    • Gradually add the flour and salt to the butter mixture, mixing until a dough forms.
    • Press the dough into the prepared baking pan, ensuring an even layer across the bottom.
    • Bake the crust for 15-20 minutes or until lightly golden. Remove from the oven and set aside to cool.

  2. Make the Lemon Filling:

    • In a separate bowl, whisk together the eggs and granulated sugar until well combined.
    • Add the flour, lemon juice, and lemon zest to the egg mixture, whisking until smooth.
    • Pour the lemon filling over the cooled crust, spreading it into an even layer.
    • Return the pan to the oven and bake for an additional 20-25 minutes, or until the filling is set and the edges are lightly golden.

  3. Chill and Serve:

    • Once baked, remove the lemon bars from the oven and allow them to cool to room temperature.
    • Refrigerate the bars for at least 2 hours to allow the filling to set completely.
    • Once chilled, dust the top of the bars with powdered sugar and cut into squares.

  4. Enjoy!

    • Serve these nut-free lemon bars chilled and savor the bright, citrusy flavor with every bite.

Tips and Variations

  • For a decorative touch, garnish the lemon bars with a twist of lemon zest or a dollop of whipped cream.
  • If you prefer a sweeter crust, you can increase the amount of powdered sugar in the crust recipe.
  • Experiment with different citrus flavors by substituting the lemon juice and zest with lime or orange for a unique twist on this classic recipe.

These nut-free lemon bars are a delightful combination of sweet and tangy flavors, making them a perfect treat for any occasion. Whether you’re catering to nut allergies or simply craving a burst of citrusy goodness, this recipe is sure to become a favorite in your dessert repertoire. So, gather your ingredients and get ready to delight your taste buds with these irresistible nut-free lemon bars!
