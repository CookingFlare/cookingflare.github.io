Grilled Desserts: Sweet Treats for the BBQ Enthusiast


Who says the grill is only for savory dishes? Take your barbecue game to the next level by exploring the realm of grilled desserts. From smoky caramelized fruits to indulgent sweet treats, grilled desserts offer a unique and delicious twist to your summer gatherings. In this blog post, we’ll explore some mouthwatering grilled dessert recipes that are sure to impress your guests and satisfy your sweet tooth.

Grilled Pineapple with Cinnamon and Honey

Let’s start with a classic favorite - grilled pineapple. This tropical fruit takes on a whole new level of flavor when kissed by the flames of a hot grill. To make this simple yet delightful dessert, follow these steps:

  1. Preheat your grill to medium-high heat.
  2. Slice a fresh pineapple into rings, about 1/2 inch thick.
  3. Brush the pineapple slices with a mixture of melted butter, cinnamon, and honey.
  4. Place the pineapple slices directly on the grill grates and cook for about 2-3 minutes on each side, or until they develop grill marks and become slightly caramelized.
  5. Remove from the grill and serve warm. You can enjoy them as is or pair them with a scoop of vanilla ice cream for an extra indulgence.

Grilled Banana S’mores

S’mores are a classic campfire treat, but have you ever considered grilling them? Grilling bananas adds a delightful caramelized flavor that takes this dessert to a whole new level. Here’s how to make grilled banana s’mores:

  1. Preheat your grill to medium heat.
  2. Cut a small slit lengthwise in each banana, making sure not to cut all the way through.
  3. Stuff the slit with mini marshmallows and chocolate chips.
  4. Wrap the bananas tightly in aluminum foil, ensuring they are completely sealed.
  5. Place the foil-wrapped bananas on the grill and cook for about 5-7 minutes, or until the marshmallows and chocolate have melted.
  6. Carefully remove from the grill, unwrap the foil, and let them cool for a few minutes before enjoying this gooey, chocolatey delight.

Grilled Pound Cake with Berries and Whipped Cream

Grilled pound cake takes a classic dessert to new heights. The heat from the grill caramelizes the cake, creating a slightly crispy exterior while keeping the inside moist and tender. Pair it with fresh berries and whipped cream for a delightful summer treat. Here’s how to make grilled pound cake with berries and whipped cream:

  1. Preheat your grill to medium heat.
  2. Slice a store-bought or homemade pound cake into thick slices.
  3. Brush both sides of the cake slices with melted butter.
  4. Place the cake slices directly on the grill grates and cook for about 2-3 minutes on each side, or until they develop grill marks and become slightly crispy.
  5. Remove from the grill and let them cool for a few minutes.
  6. Serve the grilled pound cake slices with a generous spoonful of fresh berries (such as strawberries, blueberries, or raspberries) and a dollop of whipped cream.

Grilled Watermelon with Feta and Mint

Grilled watermelon may sound unconventional, but trust us, it’s a game-changer. The heat of the grill enhances the sweetness of the watermelon while adding a smoky char that pairs perfectly with the tangy feta cheese and refreshing mint. Here’s how to make grilled watermelon with feta and mint:

  1. Preheat your grill to medium-high heat.
  2. Slice a seedless watermelon into thick wedges.
  3. Lightly brush the watermelon wedges with olive oil.
  4. Place the watermelon wedges directly on the grill grates and cook for about 2-3 minutes on each side, or until they develop grill marks and become slightly caramelized.
  5. Remove from the grill and let them cool for a few minutes.
  6. Sprinkle crumbled feta cheese and chopped fresh mint over the grilled watermelon wedges.
  7. Serve as a refreshing and unexpected appetizer or as a side dish to complement your grilled main course.

Grilled Desserts: A Sweet Finale

Grilled desserts are the perfect way to end a barbecue feast. Whether you’re looking to impress your guests or simply indulge in something sweet, these grilled dessert recipes are sure to satisfy. So fire up your grill, get creative, and enjoy the delicious flavors that can only be achieved through the magic of grilling. Happy grilling and happy dessert-ing!

Note: Remember to always exercise caution when grilling and use proper grilling tools to ensure your safety.
