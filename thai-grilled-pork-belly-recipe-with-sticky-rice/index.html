Thai Grilled Pork Belly Recipe with Sticky Rice


Grilled pork belly is a beloved dish in Thai cuisine, famous for its succulent and flavorful meat. Paired with sticky rice, it creates a perfect balance of textures and flavors that will leave you craving for more. In this recipe, we will guide you through the process of making this delectable Thai grilled pork belly with sticky rice.

Ingredients

For the pork belly marinade:

  • 1.5 pounds of pork belly, skin-on
  • 3 tablespoons of fish sauce
  • 2 tablespoons of soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons of oyster sauce
  • 1 tablespoon of honey
  • 1 tablespoon of sesame oil
  • 4 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon of ground black pepper

For the sticky rice:

  • 2 cups of Thai sticky rice
  • 2 cups of water
  • 1 teaspoon of salt

For serving:

  • Fresh cilantro leaves
  • Thai chili sauce

Instructions

Preparing the Pork Belly

  1. In a bowl, combine the fish sauce, soy sauce, oyster sauce, honey, sesame oil, minced garlic, and ground black pepper. Mix well to create the marinade.

  2. Place the pork belly in a shallow dish and pour the marinade over the meat. Make sure to coat the pork belly thoroughly with the marinade. Cover the dish with plastic wrap and let it marinate in the refrigerator for at least 2 hours, or overnight for a more intense flavor.

Grilling the Pork Belly

  1. Preheat your grill to medium-high heat.

  2. Remove the pork belly from the marinade and pat it dry with paper towels. This step will ensure a crispy skin when grilling.

  3. Place the pork belly on the preheated grill, skin-side down. Grill for about 10 minutes, or until the skin is crispy and golden brown. Flip the pork belly over and continue grilling for an additional 10-15 minutes, or until the meat is cooked through and tender.

  4. Once the pork belly is cooked, remove it from the grill and let it rest for a few minutes. This step allows the juices to redistribute, resulting in a more flavorful and moist meat.

Making the Sticky Rice

  1. Rinse the sticky rice under cold water until the water runs clear. This step removes any excess starch from the rice.

  2. In a pot, combine the rinsed sticky rice, water, and salt. Bring the mixture to a boil over medium heat. Once boiling, reduce the heat to low and cover the pot with a tight-fitting lid. Let the rice simmer for 15-20 minutes, or until the rice is tender and all the water has been absorbed.

  3. Once the sticky rice is cooked, remove it from the heat and let it sit, covered, for an additional 5 minutes. This step allows the rice to steam and become even stickier.

Serving

  1. Slice the grilled pork belly into thin strips.

  2. Serve the sliced pork belly on a plate with a side of sticky rice. Garnish with fresh cilantro leaves for a burst of freshness.

  3. For an extra kick of flavor, serve the Thai grilled pork belly with Thai chili sauce on the side. The spicy and tangy sauce complements the richness of the pork belly and adds a delightful heat to the dish.

Enjoy this delicious Thai grilled pork belly with sticky rice as a main course or as part of a Thai-inspired feast. The combination of tender pork belly and sticky rice will transport your taste buds to the vibrant streets of Thailand. So fire up your grill and get ready to indulge in this mouthwatering dish!
