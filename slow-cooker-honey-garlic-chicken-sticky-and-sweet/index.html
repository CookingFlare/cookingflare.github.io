Slow Cooker Honey Garlic Chicken: Sticky and Sweet


If you’re looking for a mouthwatering and easy-to-make dish that will have your taste buds dancing with delight, then this Slow Cooker Honey Garlic Chicken recipe is a must-try! Bursting with sticky and sweet flavors, this dish is a crowd-pleaser that will leave everyone asking for seconds. The best part? It’s made in a slow cooker, which means minimal effort and maximum flavor!

Ingredients

  • 2 pounds of boneless, skinless chicken thighs
  • 1/2 cup of low-sodium soy sauce
  • 1/2 cup of honey
  • 1/4 cup of ketchup
  • 4 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon of dried basil
  • 1/2 teaspoon of dried oregano
  • 1/4 teaspoon of red pepper flakes (optional, for a hint of heat)
  • 2 tablespoons of cornstarch
  • 2 tablespoons of water
  • Sesame seeds and sliced green onions, for garnish

Instructions

  1. In a bowl, whisk together the soy sauce, honey, ketchup, minced garlic, dried basil, dried oregano, and red pepper flakes (if using). This flavorful sauce will be the key to achieving that sticky and sweet goodness.

  2. Place the chicken thighs in the slow cooker, ensuring they are in a single layer. Pour the sauce over the chicken, making sure each piece is coated evenly. Cover the slow cooker and cook on low heat for 4-5 hours, or until the chicken is tender and cooked through.

  3. Once the chicken is cooked, carefully remove it from the slow cooker and set it aside. In a small bowl, whisk together the cornstarch and water until smooth. Slowly pour the cornstarch mixture into the sauce in the slow cooker, stirring well to combine. This will help thicken the sauce and give it that irresistible sticky texture.

  4. Return the chicken to the slow cooker and gently toss it in the thickened sauce, ensuring each piece is coated. Cover the slow cooker again and cook on low heat for an additional 30 minutes to allow the flavors to meld together.

  5. Serve the Slow Cooker Honey Garlic Chicken hot, garnished with sesame seeds and sliced green onions for an added touch of freshness. This dish pairs perfectly with steamed rice or noodles, allowing you to soak up every bit of that delicious sauce.

Tips and Variations

  • For an extra burst of flavor, marinate the chicken in the sauce for at least 30 minutes before cooking. This will allow the flavors to penetrate the meat and create an even more delectable dish.
  • If you prefer a spicier kick, increase the amount of red pepper flakes or add a dash of hot sauce to the sauce mixture. Adjust the heat level to your liking.
  • Don’t have chicken thighs? No problem! You can easily substitute them with boneless, skinless chicken breasts. Just keep in mind that the cooking time may vary slightly, as chicken breasts tend to cook faster than thighs.
  • To make this dish even more nutritious, feel free to add your favorite vegetables to the slow cooker. Bell peppers, broccoli, and snap peas are all great options that will complement the flavors of the honey garlic sauce.

With its sticky and sweet glaze, tender chicken, and rich flavors, this Slow Cooker Honey Garlic Chicken is sure to become a family favorite. It’s the perfect dish for busy weeknights or lazy weekends when you want something delicious without spending hours in the kitchen. So go ahead, give this recipe a try, and prepare to impress your loved ones with a meal that will leave them begging for more!
