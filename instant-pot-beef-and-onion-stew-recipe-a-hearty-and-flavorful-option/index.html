Instant Pot Beef and Onion Stew Recipe: A Hearty and Flavorful Option


There’s nothing quite like a warm and comforting stew to satisfy your cravings on a chilly day. And when it comes to convenience and flavor, it’s hard to beat the Instant Pot. This versatile kitchen appliance allows you to create delicious meals in a fraction of the time, and today, we have a recipe that will surely become a favorite in your household: Instant Pot Beef and Onion Stew.

Ingredients

  • 2 pounds beef stew meat, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 2 large onions, thinly sliced
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 4 carrots, peeled and sliced
  • 3 celery stalks, diced
  • 1 pound potatoes, cubed
  • 4 cups beef broth
  • 1 cup red wine (optional)
  • 2 tablespoons tomato paste
  • 2 tablespoons Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 tablespoon dried thyme
  • 1 tablespoon dried rosemary
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil

Instructions

  1. Start by setting your Instant Pot to the sauté function. Add the olive oil and allow it to heat up.
  2. Once the oil is hot, add the beef stew meat and brown it on all sides. This step will help seal in the flavor and keep the meat tender.
  3. Remove the beef from the Instant Pot and set it aside. Add the onions and garlic to the pot and sauté until they become translucent and fragrant.
  4. Next, add the carrots, celery, and potatoes to the pot, and give them a quick stir to combine everything.
  5. Return the beef to the Instant Pot, along with any accumulated juices. Pour in the beef broth and red wine (if using), and stir in the tomato paste and Worcestershire sauce.
  6. Add the dried thyme and rosemary, and season with salt and pepper to taste. Give everything a good stir to ensure the flavors are well-distributed.
  7. Secure the lid on the Instant Pot, making sure the valve is set to the sealing position. Select the “Pressure Cook” function and set the timer for 30 minutes.
  8. Once the cooking time is up, allow the pressure to release naturally for about 10 minutes, and then carefully perform a quick release to release any remaining pressure.
  9. Give the stew a gentle stir, and if desired, you can thicken the sauce by selecting the “Sauté” function and simmering the stew for a few minutes until it reaches your desired consistency.
  10. Serve the Instant Pot Beef and Onion Stew hot, garnished with fresh parsley if desired. Pair it with crusty bread or fluffy mashed potatoes for a complete and satisfying meal.

Why Choose Instant Pot Beef and Onion Stew?

There are several reasons why this Instant Pot Beef and Onion Stew recipe deserves a spot in your regular meal rotation. Firstly, the Instant Pot allows you to achieve tender and flavorful meat in a fraction of the time it would take using traditional cooking methods. This means you can enjoy a hearty and satisfying stew even on busy weeknights.

The combination of beef, onions, and aromatic herbs creates a rich and comforting flavor profile that will warm your soul. The addition of carrots, celery, and potatoes not only adds texture but also boosts the nutritional value of the dish. With a generous amount of beef broth and a splash of red wine, this stew is packed with mouthwatering flavors that will leave you craving for more.

The Instant Pot Beef and Onion Stew is also a versatile dish that you can customize to suit your preferences. Feel free to add other vegetables like mushrooms or peas, or even experiment with different herbs and spices to create your unique twist.

So, the next time you’re in the mood for a hearty and flavorful stew, give this Instant Pot Beef and Onion Stew recipe a try. With minimal effort and maximum taste, it’s a meal that will warm your heart and satisfy your taste buds.
