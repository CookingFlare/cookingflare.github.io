Deliciously Unique Lavender Honey Shortbread Cookies


If you’re looking for a delightful twist on the classic shortbread cookie, look no further than these deliciously unique lavender honey shortbread cookies. Fragrant lavender combined with the sweetness of honey creates a heavenly flavor that will have your taste buds dancing. These cookies are perfect for afternoon tea, dessert, or as a thoughtful homemade gift for friends and family.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1/2 cup powdered sugar
  • 2 tablespoons honey
  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tablespoon culinary lavender
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Lavender: In a small bowl, combine the culinary lavender with the honey. Allow it to sit for at least 30 minutes to infuse the honey with the delicate floral flavor of the lavender.

  2. Cream the Butter and Sugar: In a large mixing bowl, cream together the softened butter and powdered sugar until light and fluffy.

  3. Add the Lavender Honey: Once the lavender has infused the honey, add the honey mixture to the creamed butter and sugar. Mix until well combined.

  4. Incorporate the Dry Ingredients: Sift the flour and salt into the butter mixture. Gently fold the dry ingredients into the wet ingredients until a soft dough forms.

  5. Chill the Dough: Shape the dough into a disc, wrap it in plastic wrap, and refrigerate for at least 1 hour. Chilling the dough will help the cookies hold their shape during baking.

  6. Preheat the Oven: Preheat your oven to 325°F (165°C) and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.

  7. Roll and Cut: On a lightly floured surface, roll out the chilled dough to about 1/4 inch thickness. Use cookie cutters to cut the dough into your desired shapes.

  8. Bake to Perfection: Carefully transfer the cut cookies to the prepared baking sheet. Bake for 12-15 minutes, or until the edges are lightly golden. Be mindful not to overbake the cookies to preserve their delicate flavor.

  9. Cool and Enjoy: Allow the cookies to cool on the baking sheet for a few minutes before transferring them to a wire rack to cool completely.

Tips for Success

  • Use culinary lavender specifically labeled for culinary use, as it is free from any chemicals or pesticides.
  • If you prefer a more subtle lavender flavor, you can reduce the amount of lavender used in the recipe.
  • These cookies can be stored in an airtight container at room temperature for up to a week, making them a perfect make-ahead treat for any occasion.

These delightful lavender honey shortbread cookies are a delightful way to introduce a touch of elegance to your baking repertoire. Their delicate flavor and buttery, crumbly texture make them a true delight for the senses. Whether you’re a seasoned baker or new to the world of baking, these unique cookies are sure to impress and become a staple in your collection of go-to recipes. Enjoy the delicate floral notes and sweetness of these charming cookies with a cup of tea or as a standalone treat. Happy baking!
