Sweet and Tangy Lemon Bread: A Refreshing Treat


Are you looking for a delightful treat that perfectly balances sweetness and tanginess? Look no further than this scrumptious Sweet and Tangy Lemon Bread recipe! Bursting with zesty lemon flavor and a moist, tender crumb, this delightful bread is a perfect accompaniment to your morning coffee or afternoon tea. Whether you’re an experienced baker or a novice in the kitchen, this recipe is simple to follow and guarantees a delicious outcome every time.

Ingredients

For the Lemon Bread:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1/2 cup Greek yogurt
  • 1/4 cup fresh lemon juice
  • Zest of 2 lemons
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

For the Lemon Glaze:

  • 1 cup powdered sugar
  • 2-3 tablespoons fresh lemon juice

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease a 9x5-inch loaf pan and set it aside.

  2. In a medium bowl, whisk together the flour, baking powder, baking soda, and salt. Set aside.

  3. In a large mixing bowl, cream together the softened butter and granulated sugar until light and fluffy.

  4. Beat in the eggs, one at a time, ensuring each is fully incorporated.

  5. Add the Greek yogurt, fresh lemon juice, lemon zest, and vanilla extract to the wet mixture. Mix until well combined.

  6. Gradually add the dry ingredients to the wet ingredients, mixing until just combined. Be careful not to overmix, as this can result in a dense bread.

  7. Pour the batter into the prepared loaf pan, spreading it out evenly.

  8. Bake for 50-60 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean. If the top begins to brown too quickly, tent the loaf pan with aluminum foil.

  9. While the bread is baking, prepare the lemon glaze by whisking together the powdered sugar and fresh lemon juice until smooth.

  10. Once the bread is done, remove it from the oven and allow it to cool in the pan for 10-15 minutes.

  11. Transfer the bread to a wire rack, then drizzle the lemon glaze over the warm bread, allowing it to soak in.

  12. Allow the bread to cool completely before slicing and serving.

Serving Suggestions

This Sweet and Tangy Lemon Bread is delicious on its own, but you can take it to the next level by serving it with a dollop of whipped cream or a scoop of vanilla ice cream. The bright, citrusy flavor pairs beautifully with fresh berries, making it a delightful dessert option for any occasion.

Storage

To keep your lemon bread fresh, store it in an airtight container at room temperature for up to 3 days. For longer storage, wrap it tightly in plastic wrap and freeze for up to 2 months. Thaw at room temperature before serving.

Final Thoughts

With its vibrant lemon flavor and tender texture, this Sweet and Tangy Lemon Bread is a true crowd-pleaser. Whether you’re hosting a brunch, attending a potluck, or simply craving a delightful homemade treat, this recipe is sure to impress. Embrace the bright flavors of citrus with every bite of this delightful lemon bread – it’s a refreshing treat that will leave you yearning for more. Enjoy!
