Baked Ziti with Italian Sausage: A Cheesy and Savory Pleasure


Welcome to our kitchen, where today we’re diving into the world of Italian cuisine with a mouthwatering recipe for Baked Ziti with Italian Sausage. This cheesy and savory delight is sure to please even the pickiest of eaters. So, let’s roll up our sleeves and get cooking!

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ziti pasta
  • 1 pound Italian sausage, casings removed
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can (28 ounces) crushed tomatoes
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1 cup ricotta cheese
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • Fresh basil leaves, for garnish

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C). Grease a 9x13-inch baking dish with cooking spray and set it aside.

  2. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Cook the ziti according to the package instructions until al dente. Drain and set aside.

  3. In a large skillet, cook the Italian sausage over medium heat, breaking it up into small pieces with a wooden spoon, until browned and cooked through. Transfer the cooked sausage to a plate lined with paper towels to drain excess grease.

  4. In the same skillet, add the chopped onion and minced garlic. Sauté until the onion becomes translucent and the garlic becomes fragrant, about 3-4 minutes.

  5. Pour in the crushed tomatoes, dried basil, dried oregano, salt, and black pepper. Stir well to combine. Bring the mixture to a simmer and let it cook for about 10 minutes, allowing the flavors to meld together.

  6. In a large mixing bowl, combine the cooked ziti, cooked Italian sausage, and tomato sauce mixture. Mix until everything is evenly coated.

  7. In a separate bowl, stir together the ricotta cheese, shredded mozzarella cheese, and grated Parmesan cheese.

  8. Spread half of the ziti mixture in the prepared baking dish. Dollop half of the cheese mixture evenly over the ziti. Repeat the layers with the remaining ziti mixture and cheese mixture.

  9. Cover the baking dish with foil and bake in the preheated oven for 25 minutes. Remove the foil and bake for an additional 10 minutes, or until the cheese is melted and bubbly.

  10. Once baked, remove the dish from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes. Garnish with fresh basil leaves before serving.

Serving Suggestions

This Baked Ziti with Italian Sausage pairs perfectly with a fresh green salad and a crusty garlic bread. It’s a hearty and satisfying meal that will surely impress your family and friends.

In Summary

With its irresistible combination of cheesy goodness and flavorful Italian sausage, this Baked Ziti recipe is a true crowd-pleaser. The ziti pasta, cooked to perfection, is enveloped in a rich tomato sauce infused with herbs and spices. The layers of ricotta, mozzarella, and Parmesan cheese add a creamy and indulgent touch.

Whether you’re hosting a dinner party or simply craving comfort food, this Baked Ziti with Italian Sausage is a recipe worth adding to your repertoire. So, grab your apron, gather your ingredients, and let the aromas of this delectable dish fill your kitchen. Buon appetito!
