Garlic Parmesan Focaccia Bread: A Flavorful Option


Looking for a delicious and easy bread recipe to elevate your dining experience? Look no further than Garlic Parmesan Focaccia Bread! This flavorful bread is a perfect blend of savory garlic, aromatic herbs, and rich Parmesan cheese, making it a versatile option for any meal. Whether you’re a seasoned baker or a beginner in the kitchen, this recipe is sure to impress your taste buds and those of your loved ones.

Ingredients:

  • 4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tablespoon sugar
  • 1 tablespoon salt
  • 1 tablespoon instant yeast
  • 1 ½ cups warm water
  • ½ cup olive oil
  • 4 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon dried oregano
  • 1 tablespoon dried rosemary
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the Dough:

    • In a large mixing bowl, combine the flour, sugar, salt, and instant yeast. Mix well.
    • Slowly add the warm water and ¼ cup of olive oil to the dry ingredients. Stir until a dough forms.
    • Knead the dough on a floured surface for about 5-7 minutes until it becomes smooth and elastic.

  2. First Rise:

    • Place the dough in a greased bowl, cover it with a damp towel, and let it rise in a warm place for about 1 hour or until it doubles in size.

  3. Prepare the Topping:

    • In a small bowl, mix the minced garlic, dried oregano, dried rosemary, and the remaining olive oil.

  4. Assembly:

    • Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C) and grease a baking sheet.
    • Transfer the risen dough to the baking sheet and gently press it out to fit the pan.
    • Using your fingertips, make dimples all over the dough.

  5. Final Steps:

    • Brush the herb and garlic mixture over the dough, ensuring it covers the surface evenly.
    • Sprinkle the grated Parmesan cheese generously on top.
    • Let the dough rest for another 20 minutes to slightly rise again.

  6. Baking:

    • Bake the focaccia bread in the preheated oven for 20-25 minutes or until golden brown on top.

  7. Enjoy:

    • Once baked, remove the bread from the oven and let it cool slightly.
    • Slice the Garlic Parmesan Focaccia Bread into squares or strips.
    • Serve warm and enjoy the delightful flavors!

This Garlic Parmesan Focaccia Bread is perfect for serving as a side to soups, salads, or enjoyed on its own as a tasty snack. The combination of garlic, herbs, and Parmesan creates a beautiful harmony of flavors that will leave you craving more. Try this recipe today and indulge in the irresistible aroma and taste of freshly baked bread. Happy baking!

Remember, the key to a successful focaccia bread lies in allowing the dough to rise adequately and infusing it with the flavorful garlic-herb mixture. Experiment with different herbs and cheeses to create your unique twist on this classic recipe. Dive into the world of homemade bread-making and savor the satisfaction of creating something truly special in your kitchen. Cheers to delicious moments shared over a slice of warm Garlic Parmesan Focaccia Bread!
