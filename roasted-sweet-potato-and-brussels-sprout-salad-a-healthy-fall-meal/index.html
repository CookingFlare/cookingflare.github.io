Roasted Sweet Potato and Brussels Sprout Salad: A Healthy Fall Meal


Fall is the perfect time to indulge in hearty and nutritious salads that celebrate the flavors of the season. This Roasted Sweet Potato and Brussels Sprout Salad is a delightful combination of sweet and savory, packed with nutrients, and bursting with flavors that will leave you craving for more. Whether you’re looking for a satisfying lunch or a delicious side dish for dinner, this salad ticks all the boxes.

Ingredients

  • 2 medium-sized sweet potatoes, peeled and cubed
  • 1 pound fresh Brussels sprouts, trimmed and halved
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons balsamic vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon honey
  • 1 teaspoon Dijon mustard
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • 1/2 cup dried cranberries
  • 1/2 cup chopped pecans
  • 4 cups baby spinach or mixed greens
  • 1/4 cup crumbled goat cheese (optional)

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 425°F (220°C). Line a baking sheet with parchment paper or aluminum foil for easy cleanup.

  2. Roast the sweet potatoes: In a large bowl, toss the sweet potato cubes with 2 tablespoons of olive oil, salt, and pepper. Spread them out in a single layer on the prepared baking sheet. Roast in the preheated oven for 25-30 minutes, or until the sweet potatoes are tender and slightly caramelized. Make sure to toss them halfway through cooking for even browning.

  3. Roast the Brussels sprouts: In the same bowl, toss the halved Brussels sprouts with 1 tablespoon of olive oil, salt, and pepper. Add them to the baking sheet with the sweet potatoes, making sure they are spread out in a single layer. Roast for an additional 15-20 minutes, or until the Brussels sprouts are tender and lightly browned.

  4. Prepare the dressing: In a small bowl, whisk together the balsamic vinegar, honey, Dijon mustard, remaining olive oil, salt, and pepper. Set aside.

  5. Assemble the salad: In a large salad bowl, combine the roasted sweet potatoes, Brussels sprouts, dried cranberries, chopped pecans, and baby spinach or mixed greens. Drizzle the dressing over the salad and toss gently to coat all the ingredients.

  6. Serve and enjoy: Transfer the salad to individual plates or a large serving bowl. If desired, sprinkle crumbled goat cheese on top for an extra creamy and tangy touch. Serve immediately and savor the wholesome flavors of this fall-inspired salad.

This Roasted Sweet Potato and Brussels Sprout Salad is not only a feast for the taste buds but also a nutritional powerhouse. Sweet potatoes are rich in fiber, vitamins A and C, and are known for their antioxidant properties. Brussels sprouts, on the other hand, are packed with vitamin K, folate, and are an excellent source of dietary fiber. Combined with the crunch of pecans and the sweetness of dried cranberries, this salad offers a perfect balance of flavors and textures.

So, the next time you’re looking for a healthy and satisfying meal during the fall season, give this Roasted Sweet Potato and Brussels Sprout Salad a try. It’s a simple yet impressive dish that will leave you feeling nourished and satisfied. Enjoy the flavors of fall with every bite!
