Cinnamon Apple Crumble Recipe: A Warm and Comforting Vegan Treat


Autumn is here, and with it comes the delightful aroma of cinnamon, apples, and warmth. What better way to celebrate the season than with a comforting vegan treat that’s sure to become a new favorite? Today, we’re sharing a recipe for a scrumptious Cinnamon Apple Crumble that’s perfect for snuggling up on a chilly evening.

The Magic of Cinnamon

Cinnamon is more than just a spice; it’s a symbol of warmth, comfort, and nostalgia. Its sweet, aromatic flavor has been a staple in many cuisines for centuries, and for good reason. Not only does it add depth and complexity to baked goods, but it also has numerous health benefits, from reducing inflammation to improving blood sugar levels.

The Perfect Apple

For this recipe, you’ll need a mix of sweet and tart apples. We recommend using a combination of Granny Smith and Gala apples, as they provide the perfect balance of flavors and textures. Slice the apples thinly, making sure to remove any seeds or cores, and set them aside for later use.

The Crumble Topping

The crumble topping is where the magic happens. A blend of rolled oats, almond flour, brown sugar, and cinnamon creates a crunchy, buttery topping that’s simply irresistible. To make the crumble topping, combine 1 1/2 cups rolled oats, 1/2 cup almond flour, 1/2 cup brown sugar, and 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon in a bowl. Add 1/4 cup vegan butter, melted, and mix until the topping is crumbly and well combined.

Assembling the Crumble

Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C). In a 9x9-inch baking dish, arrange the sliced apples in a single layer. In a separate bowl, mix together 2 tablespoons cornstarch, 1 tablespoon lemon juice, and 1/4 cup maple syrup. Pour the mixture over the apples, making sure they’re evenly coated.

The Final Touches

Sprinkle the crumble topping evenly over the apples, making sure to cover the entire surface. If desired, add a sprinkle of cinnamon on top for an extra boost of flavor.

Baking and Serving

Bake the crumble for 40-45 minutes, or until the topping is golden brown and the apples are tender. Allow the crumble to cool for at least 30 minutes before serving. Serve warm, topped with a scoop of vegan vanilla ice cream or a dollop of whipped cream, if desired.

Tips and Variations

  • For an extra crunchy topping, bake the crumble for an additional 5-10 minutes.
  • Substitute the rolled oats with gluten-free oats for a gluten-free option.
  • Add a sprinkle of nutmeg or allspice to the crumble topping for an extra boost of flavor.
  • Use a combination of sweet and tart apples, such as Granny Smith and Fuji, for a unique flavor profile.

Conclusion

This Cinnamon Apple Crumble recipe is a true delight, perfect for snuggling up on a chilly evening or serving at your next gathering. With its warm, comforting flavors and crunchy, buttery topping, it’s sure to become a new favorite in your household. So go ahead, indulge in a slice (or two), and let the magic of cinnamon and apples transport you to a cozy autumn evening.
