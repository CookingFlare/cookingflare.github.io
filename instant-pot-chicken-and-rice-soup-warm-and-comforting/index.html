Instant Pot Chicken and Rice Soup: Warm and Comforting


There’s nothing quite like a warm bowl of chicken soup to comfort your body and soul. And when it comes to convenience and speed, the Instant Pot is your best friend in the kitchen. In this recipe, we’ll show you how to make a delicious and hearty Instant Pot Chicken and Rice Soup that will warm you up from the inside out.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1 cup diced carrots
  • 1 cup diced celery
  • 1 cup diced onion
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • 1 teaspoon dried rosemary
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 6 cups chicken broth
  • 1 cup long-grain white rice
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley for garnish

Instructions

  1. Start by preparing the ingredients. Dice the carrots, celery, and onion. Mince the garlic and set everything aside.

  2. Turn on your Instant Pot and select the sauté function. Add a drizzle of olive oil and let it heat up.

  3. Once the Instant Pot is hot, add the diced onion and minced garlic. Sauté for 2-3 minutes until the onions are translucent and the garlic is fragrant.

  4. Add the diced carrots and celery to the pot. Sauté for another 2 minutes until the vegetables begin to soften.

  5. Now it’s time to add the star of the show: the chicken breasts. Place them in the Instant Pot, making sure they are submerged in the vegetable mixture.

  6. Sprinkle the dried thyme and rosemary over the chicken breasts. Add the bay leaf for extra flavor.

  7. Pour in the chicken broth, making sure to cover the chicken completely. The liquid will help infuse the chicken with flavor and keep it moist during cooking.

  8. Secure the lid on the Instant Pot and set the cooking time to 10 minutes on high pressure.

  9. While the soup is cooking, rinse the rice under cold water until the water runs clear. This step removes excess starch and prevents the rice from becoming mushy.

  10. Once the cooking time is up, do a quick release of the pressure. Remove the chicken breasts from the Instant Pot and shred them using two forks. Return the shredded chicken to the pot.

  11. Add the rinsed rice to the soup. Stir well to combine all the ingredients.

  12. Place the lid back on the Instant Pot and set the cooking time to 8 minutes on high pressure.

  13. After the cooking time is complete, let the pressure release naturally for 5 minutes, then do a quick release to release any remaining pressure.

  14. Give the soup a good stir and season with salt and pepper to taste.

  15. Ladle the Instant Pot Chicken and Rice Soup into bowls and garnish with fresh parsley.

Enjoy the Warmth and Comfort

There you have it – a delicious and comforting Instant Pot Chicken and Rice Soup ready in no time. The flavors of the tender chicken, aromatic vegetables, and fluffy rice come together to create a bowl of warmth that will soothe your soul on a chilly day. The convenience of the Instant Pot makes this recipe a breeze, allowing you to enjoy a homemade soup without spending hours in the kitchen. So grab your Instant Pot, gather the ingredients, and let the comforting aroma fill your home.
