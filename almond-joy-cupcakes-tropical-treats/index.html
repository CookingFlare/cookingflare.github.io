Almond Joy Cupcakes: Tropical Treats


There’s nothing quite like the warm embrace of a cupcake, especially if that cupcake is an Almond Joy-inspired treat. Drenched with flavors reminiscent of the quintessential Almond Joy candy bar, these cupcakes will whisk you away to a tropical island paradise.

Curious to find out how to recreate this tropical delight in your very own kitchen? Well, strap in because we are about to embark on a fantastic, almond-filled adventure!

Ingredients:

For the Almond Joy Cupcakes:

  • 2 1/4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/2 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons baking soda
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1/2 cup vegetable oil
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 1 tablespoon almond extract
  • 1 cup boiling water

For the Almond Joy Buttercream Frosting:

  • 1 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 4 1/2 cups powdered sugar
  • 1/4 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1/4 cup almond flour
  • 1/4 cup coconut flakes
  • 2 tablespoons heavy cream
  • 1 teaspoon almond extract
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract

Instructions:

Let’s begin our journey by crafting the cupcakes!

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C). Line your muffin tins with cupcake liners or spray them with non-stick cooking spray to prevent sticking.

  2. Create the Almond Joy Cupcake Batter: In a large bowl, whisk together the flour, granulated sugar, cocoa powder, baking powder, baking soda, and salt. Now, you’re concocting a magical blend of dry ingredients!

  3. Pour in the milk, vegetable oil, eggs, vanilla extract, and almond extract. A good mix of wet ingredients will help bring your cupcakes to life.

  4. Slowly stir in the boiling water to the batter. Remember that lumpy batter is perfectly fine, so do not overmix! Now your batter is ready to fill the muffin liners 2/3 of the way up.

  5. Bake those scrumptious cupcakes in the oven for approximately 15-18 minutes. You’ll know they’re done when you see them spring back when gently touched in the centers.

  6. Once baked, remove the cupcakes from the oven and allow them to cool on a wire rack.

While those cupcakes are cooling down and taking their final formation, let’s create the Almond Joy Buttercream Frosting!

  1. Beat the Unsalted Butter: In a large bowl, beat the softened butter until it’s creamy and smooth.

  2. Gradually add the powdered sugar, cocoa powder, almond flour, and coconut flakes. Continue mixing until everything is well incorporated.

  3. Stir in the heavy cream, followed by the almond and vanilla extracts. This will help to create a light and delectable frosting.

Assembly Time:

Now that both the cupcakes and frosting have cooled, it’s time to bring all the tropical goodness together.

  1. Frost: Pipe or spread the Almond Joy Buttercream Frosting on each cooled cupcake.

  2. You can get creative with the frosting by adding extra coconut flakes and a splash of almond extract on top for an extra bit of Almond Joy enthusiasm.

The Almond Joy Cupcakes are ready to serve!

These Almond Joy cupcakes will charm your taste buds with their harmonious blend of chocolate, coconut, and almond. The generous portion of these tropical treats makes them the perfect dessert for parties, potlucks, or any joyous gathering! Prepare to immerse yourself in the island vibes that comes with every scrumptious Almond Joy Cupcake. Enjoy!

To the kitchen, adventurers, and happy Almond Joy Cupcake baking!
