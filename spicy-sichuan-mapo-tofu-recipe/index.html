Spicy Sichuan Mapo Tofu Recipe


Get ready to tantalize your taste buds with a fiery explosion of flavors in this Spicy Sichuan Mapo Tofu recipe. Originating from the Sichuan province of China, this dish is renowned for its tongue-tingling spiciness and numbing sensation. The combination of bold spices, fragrant aromatics, and silky tofu creates a dish that is both comforting and incredibly satisfying. Let’s dive into the world of Sichuan cuisine and learn how to master this iconic Mapo Tofu recipe.

Ingredients:

  • 1 block of silken tofu
  • 200g minced pork
  • 2 tablespoons Sichuan peppercorns
  • 3 tablespoons spicy doubanjiang (chili bean paste)
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon black bean paste
  • 4 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1-inch piece of ginger, minced
  • 2 green onions, finely sliced
  • 1 cup chicken or vegetable broth
  • 1 tablespoon cornstarch mixed with 2 tablespoons of water
  • 2 tablespoons cooking oil
  • Salt and sugar to taste

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the Tofu:

    • Carefully cut the silken tofu into small cubes and set aside.

  2. Toast the Sichuan Peppercorns:

    • In a dry pan, toast the Sichuan peppercorns over low heat until fragrant. Remove from heat and crush them coarsely using a mortar and pestle.

  3. Cook the Pork:

    • Heat oil in a wok or deep skillet over medium-high heat. Add minced garlic and ginger, and sauté until fragrant.
    • Add the minced pork and cook until it starts to brown.

  4. Add Spices:

    • Stir in the spicy doubanjiang and black bean paste, and cook for a minute to release their flavors.

  5. Create the Sauce:

    • Pour in the soy sauce and chicken or vegetable broth. Bring to a simmer.

  6. Thicken the Sauce:

    • Slowly pour in the cornstarch mixture while stirring continuously until the sauce thickens.

  7. Incorporate Tofu:

    • Gently add the cubed tofu into the sauce, being careful not to break the pieces. Let it simmer for a few minutes to soak up the flavors.

  8. Final Touches:

    • Taste the sauce and adjust the seasoning with salt and sugar if needed.
    • Sprinkle the toasted Sichuan peppercorns and green onions over the dish.

  9. Serve and Enjoy:

    • Transfer the Spicy Sichuan Mapo Tofu to a serving dish and pair it with steamed rice for a complete meal.

Tips:

  • Adjust the level of spiciness by increasing or decreasing the amount of chili bean paste.
  • For a vegetarian version, replace the minced pork with chopped mushrooms or textured vegetable protein.
  • Sichuan peppercorns are essential for the authentic flavor of this dish, so try not to skip them.

Get ready to embark on a culinary journey to Sichuan with this Spicy Mapo Tofu recipe. The bold flavors and aromatic spices will transport your taste buds to the bustling streets of Chengdu. So, roll up your sleeves, gather your ingredients, and let the magic of Sichuan cuisine unfold in your kitchen!

Remember, the key to a perfect Mapo Tofu lies in finding the delicate balance between spice, heat, and numbing sensation. Embrace the bold flavors, and don’t be afraid to experiment to suit your palate. Enjoy every bite of this iconic Sichuan dish!
