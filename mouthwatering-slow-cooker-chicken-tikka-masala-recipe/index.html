Mouthwatering Slow Cooker Chicken Tikka Masala Recipe


If you’re looking for a dish that marries convenience with incredible flavor, look no further than this mouthwatering Slow Cooker Chicken Tikka Masala recipe. This beloved Indian dish is known for its rich, creamy sauce and tender chicken pieces, making it a perfect choice for family dinners or meal prep. What makes this recipe even better? You can set it and forget it!

What is Chicken Tikka Masala?

Chicken Tikka Masala is a popular dish originating from Indian cuisine, characterized by marinated chicken pieces cooked in a spiced tomato sauce. Its origins are often debated, with both Indian and British claims to its invention. Regardless of its roots, the dish has gained global popularity and has become a staple in many households.

Why Use a Slow Cooker?

Using a slow cooker for Chicken Tikka Masala has several advantages:

  1. Flavor Development: Slowly cooking the chicken allows the spices to meld beautifully, resulting in a depth of flavor that’s hard to achieve with quicker cooking methods.

  2. Tender Chicken: The gentle, low heat ensures that the chicken becomes incredibly tender and juicy.

  3. Convenience: Simply toss in your ingredients, set the timer, and walk away. This is perfect for busy days when you want a homemade meal without the fuss.

Ingredients You’ll Need

To make this Slow Cooker Chicken Tikka Masala, gather the following ingredients:

  • For the Marinade:

    • 1 pound (450g) boneless, skinless chicken thighs or breasts, cut into bite-sized pieces
    • 1 cup plain yogurt
    • 2 tablespoons lemon juice
    • 4 cloves garlic, minced
    • 1 tablespoon fresh ginger, grated
    • 2 teaspoons garam masala
    • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
    • 1 teaspoon ground coriander
    • 1 teaspoon turmeric
    • 1 teaspoon paprika
    • 1 teaspoon salt
    • ½ teaspoon black pepper

  • For the Sauce:

    • 1 medium onion, finely chopped
    • 1 can (14 oz) crushed tomatoes
    • 1 cup coconut milk or heavy cream
    • 2 tablespoons tomato paste
    • 1 tablespoon garam masala
    • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
    • 1 teaspoon ground coriander
    • 1 teaspoon paprika
    • ½ teaspoon cayenne pepper (optional, for heat)
    • Salt and pepper to taste
    • Fresh cilantro for garnish

Step-by-Step Instructions

Step 1: Marinate the Chicken

  1. In a large bowl, combine the yogurt, lemon juice, garlic, ginger, garam masala, cumin, coriander, turmeric, paprika, salt, and black pepper. Mix well.
  2. Add the chicken pieces to the marinade, ensuring they are fully coated. Cover and refrigerate for at least 1 hour, or overnight for deeper flavor.

Step 2: Prepare the Slow Cooker

  1. After marinating, spray the inside of your slow cooker with cooking spray for easy cleanup.
  2. Add the chopped onion to the bottom of the slow cooker.

Step 3: Cook the Chicken

  1. Place the marinated chicken on top of the onions in the slow cooker.
  2. Pour the crushed tomatoes, coconut milk or heavy cream, and tomato paste over the chicken.
  3. Sprinkle the additional garam masala, cumin, coriander, paprika, and cayenne pepper over the top. Stir gently to combine all ingredients, but be careful not to disturb the chicken too much.

Step 4: Slow Cook

  1. Cover the slow cooker and set it to cook on low for 6-8 hours or on high for 3-4 hours. The chicken should be tender and cooked through, reaching an internal temperature of 165°F (74°C).

Step 5: Finishing Touches

  1. Once cooked, taste the sauce and adjust the seasoning with salt and pepper if needed. If you prefer a thicker sauce, you can remove the lid and let it cook on high for an additional 30 minutes.
  2. Before serving, garnish with freshly chopped cilantro for a burst of color and flavor.

Serving Suggestions

To truly enjoy your Slow Cooker Chicken Tikka Masala, consider serving it with:

  • Basmati Rice: Fluffy, fragrant basmati rice serves as the perfect base to soak up the delicious sauce.
  • Naan Bread: Soft, pillowy naan is ideal for dipping into the sauce.
  • Raita: A cooling yogurt sauce with cucumber and herbs can help balance the spices.
  • Steamed Vegetables: For a healthier option, serve alongside a medley of steamed vegetables.

Storing and Reheating

If you have leftovers, they can be stored in an airtight container in the refrigerator for up to 3 days. To reheat, simply warm in the microwave or on the stovetop until heated through. You can also freeze the Chicken Tikka Masala for up to 3 months. Just make sure to thaw it overnight in the refrigerator before reheating.

Tips for the Best Slow Cooker Chicken Tikka Masala

  1. Use Quality Ingredients: Fresh spices and high-quality chicken make a significant difference in flavor.
  2. Adjust Spice Levels: If you prefer a milder dish, reduce the amount of cayenne pepper or omit it entirely.
  3. Experiment with Variations: Feel free to add vegetables like bell peppers or spinach to the slow cooker for added nutrition.
  4. Customize Your Creaminess: If you want a richer dish, add more coconut milk or cream to the sauce.

Conclusion

This Slow Cooker Chicken Tikka Masala recipe is a delightful way to bring the flavors of Indian cuisine into your home with minimal effort. Perfect for family dinners, meal prep, or impressing guests, it offers a satisfying and aromatic dish that everyone will love. With its tender chicken, creamy sauce, and aromatic spices, it’s bound to become a favorite in your weekly rotation. So, set your slow cooker, kick back, and enjoy the mouthwatering results of your culinary adventure!
