Creamy One-Pot Tuscan Chicken Pasta: A Family Favorite


Welcome to our kitchen, where we’re about to whip up a mouthwatering dish that will satisfy even the pickiest eaters. Creamy One-Pot Tuscan Chicken Pasta is a family favorite that combines the rustic flavors of Tuscany with the creamy goodness of pasta. This recipe is not only delicious but also incredibly easy to make, thanks to its one-pot cooking method. So, let’s grab our aprons and get ready to create a culinary masterpiece that will have everyone asking for seconds!

Ingredients

  • 1 pound boneless, skinless chicken breasts, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 onion, thinly sliced
  • 1 red bell pepper, thinly sliced
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1/2 teaspoon red pepper flakes (adjust according to your spice preference)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 cup chicken broth
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 8 ounces penne pasta
  • 2 cups fresh spinach
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • Fresh basil leaves, for garnish

Instructions

  1. In a large, deep skillet or pot, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the chicken pieces and cook until they are browned on all sides. Remove the chicken from the skillet and set it aside.

  2. In the same skillet, add the minced garlic, sliced onion, and red bell pepper. Sauté until the vegetables are softened and fragrant.

  3. Add the cherry tomatoes, dried oregano, dried basil, red pepper flakes, salt, and pepper to the skillet. Cook for an additional 2 minutes, allowing the flavors to meld together.

  4. Pour in the chicken broth and heavy cream, stirring well to combine. Bring the mixture to a simmer.

  5. Add the penne pasta to the skillet, ensuring that it is fully submerged in the liquid. Cover the skillet and cook for about 15 minutes, or until the pasta is al dente, stirring occasionally to prevent sticking.

  6. Once the pasta is cooked, remove the lid and stir in the fresh spinach. Allow the spinach to wilt for a minute or two.

  7. Return the cooked chicken to the skillet and sprinkle the grated Parmesan cheese over the top. Stir everything together until the cheese has melted and the sauce is creamy and delicious.

  8. Serve the Creamy One-Pot Tuscan Chicken Pasta hot, garnished with fresh basil leaves for an extra burst of flavor.

Tips and Variations

  • If you want to add more vegetables to this dish, feel free to experiment with your favorites. Mushrooms, zucchini, or even artichoke hearts would make great additions.
  • For a lighter version, you can use half-and-half or milk instead of heavy cream. The sauce will be slightly thinner but still incredibly tasty.
  • To make this dish gluten-free, simply swap out the regular penne pasta for a gluten-free alternative.

Conclusion

Creamy One-Pot Tuscan Chicken Pasta is the epitome of comfort food. It’s creamy, flavorful, and incredibly satisfying. The best part? It can be prepared in just one pot, minimizing the cleanup and maximizing the time you can spend enjoying your meal with your loved ones. So, the next time you’re craving a hearty pasta dish that will please the whole family, give this recipe a try. We guarantee it will become a staple in your kitchen in no time. Happy cooking!
