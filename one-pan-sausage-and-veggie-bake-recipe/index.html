One-Pan Sausage and Veggie Bake Recipe


If you’re looking for a delicious and hassle-free dinner idea, then this one-pan sausage and veggie bake recipe is just what you need. It’s a simple, yet flavorful dish that brings together the savory goodness of sausage with the wholesome freshness of assorted vegetables. The best part? It’s all made in one pan, making cleanup a breeze. Let’s dive into the recipe and discover how to create this mouthwatering meal.

Ingredients

  • 4 Italian sausages, sliced
  • 1 red bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 yellow bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 green bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 red onion, sliced
  • 2 cups cherry tomatoes
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley for garnish

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C).

  2. In a large bowl, combine the sliced sausages, bell peppers, red onion, cherry tomatoes, and minced garlic.

  3. Drizzle the olive oil over the mixture and sprinkle with dried oregano, dried thyme, salt, and pepper. Toss everything together until the ingredients are evenly coated with the seasonings and oil.

  4. Transfer the sausage and veggie mixture to a large baking sheet or roasting pan, spreading it out into an even layer.

  5. Place the pan in the preheated oven and bake for 25-30 minutes, or until the sausages are cooked through and the vegetables are tender and slightly caramelized.

  6. Once done, remove the pan from the oven and garnish the dish with freshly chopped parsley for a burst of color and added freshness.

  7. Serve the one-pan sausage and veggie bake hot, straight from the oven, and enjoy the delightful combination of flavors.

Why You’ll Love This Recipe

This one-pan sausage and veggie bake recipe is a true winner for several reasons. Firstly, it’s incredibly convenient, requiring minimal prep and cleanup thanks to the all-in-one pan approach. Secondly, it’s a versatile dish that allows you to customize the ingredients based on your preferences. You can swap out the bell peppers for zucchini, add mushrooms, or experiment with different types of sausages to suit your taste.

From a nutritional standpoint, this recipe is a powerhouse of vitamins and nutrients. The colorful array of vegetables provides an abundance of antioxidants, while the sausage infuses the dish with rich, savory flavors. It’s a balanced meal that’s satisfying and wholesome at the same time.

Lastly, the aroma that fills your kitchen as this dish bakes in the oven is simply irresistible. The combination of herbs, garlic, and sizzling sausage will have your appetite in overdrive before you even take the first bite.

Final Thoughts

The one-pan sausage and veggie bake is a fantastic go-to recipe for busy weeknights, casual gatherings, or even a laid-back weekend meal. It’s a fuss-free way to bring together the goodness of fresh ingredients and hearty flavors without spending hours in the kitchen.

So, the next time you’re in need of a satisfying and effortless dinner option, give this recipe a try. It’s a surefire way to please your taste buds and simplify your cooking routine. Enjoy!
