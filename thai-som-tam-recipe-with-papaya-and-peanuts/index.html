Thai Som Tam Recipe with Papaya and Peanuts


If you’re a fan of Thai cuisine, you’ve probably come across the refreshing and flavorful dish known as Som Tam. This vibrant salad, also known as green papaya salad, is a staple in Thai street food culture and has gained popularity worldwide for its bold flavors and healthful ingredients. In this recipe, we’ll explore how to create an authentic and delicious Som Tam with green papaya and crunchy peanuts, bringing a taste of Thailand to your kitchen.

Ingredients

For the Salad:

  • 1 medium green papaya, peeled and shredded
  • 2-3 cloves of garlic
  • 2-3 Thai bird’s eye chilies, chopped
  • 2 small tomatoes, cut into wedges
  • 1 cup long beans, cut into 1-inch pieces
  • 2 tablespoons dried shrimp, optional
  • ½ cup roasted peanuts, coarsely crushed
  • 2-3 tablespoons palm sugar or brown sugar
  • 2-3 tablespoons fish sauce
  • 3-4 tablespoons lime juice
  • 1-2 tablespoons tamarind juice

For Garnish:

  • Lime wedges
  • Cabbage leaves
  • Additional roasted peanuts

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Papaya: Start by peeling the green papaya and shredding it using a grater or a mandoline slicer. Place the shredded papaya in a large mixing bowl and set aside.

  2. Make the Dressing: In a mortar and pestle, pound the garlic and Thai chilies into a coarse paste. If you don’t have a mortar and pestle, you can use a food processor. Add the tomatoes and long beans to the mortar and lightly pound to bruise the vegetables.

  3. Combine the Ingredients: Add the shredded papaya to the mortar and gently pound to incorporate the flavors. Add the dried shrimp, if using, and continue to mix.

  4. Season the Salad: Add the palm sugar, fish sauce, lime juice, and tamarind juice to the salad. Toss everything together until well combined, ensuring that the flavors are balanced.

  5. Add the Peanuts: Toss in the coarsely crushed roasted peanuts and mix them into the salad, adding a delightful crunch to every bite.

  6. Serve and Enjoy: Transfer the Som Tam to a serving plate lined with cabbage leaves. Garnish with additional roasted peanuts and lime wedges.

Tips for the Perfect Som Tam

  • Adjust the Heat: The level of spiciness in Som Tam can be adjusted by adding or reducing the amount of Thai bird’s eye chilies.
  • Balance the Flavors: Taste the salad as you go along and adjust the sweetness, saltiness, and sourness to your preference.
  • Texture Matters: The key to a great Som Tam is achieving the perfect balance of textures, from the crispness of the papaya to the crunch of the peanuts.

Final Thoughts

Thai Som Tam with green papaya and peanuts is a dish that perfectly encapsulates the vibrant flavors of Thai cuisine. Whether you’re a fan of spicy food or just enjoy exploring new taste sensations, this salad is sure to delight your palate. With its blend of sweet, sour, spicy, and savory flavors, Som Tam offers a culinary adventure that is both refreshing and satisfying. So, gather your ingredients and embark on a journey to Thailand right from your own kitchen!
