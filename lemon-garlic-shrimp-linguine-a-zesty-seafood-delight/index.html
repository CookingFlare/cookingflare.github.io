Lemon Garlic Shrimp Linguine: A Zesty Seafood Delight


If you’re a fan of seafood and love the zesty flavors of lemon and garlic, then this Lemon Garlic Shrimp Linguine recipe is sure to become a favorite in your culinary repertoire. This dish perfectly combines the succulent taste of shrimp with the bright, citrusy notes of lemon and the aromatic richness of garlic, all served atop a bed of perfectly cooked linguine. Whether you’re hosting a dinner party or simply craving a restaurant-quality meal at home, this recipe is a surefire way to impress your guests or yourself.

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound of linguine
  • 1 pound of large shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 4 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1/4 cup of fresh lemon juice
  • Zest of 1 lemon
  • 1/2 cup of chicken broth
  • 1/4 cup of dry white wine
  • 1/4 cup of chopped fresh parsley
  • 3 tablespoons of olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Red pepper flakes (optional, for a hint of heat)

Instructions:

  1. Begin by cooking the linguine according to the package instructions. Once al dente, drain the pasta and set it aside, reserving a small amount of the pasta water.

  2. In a large skillet, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the minced garlic and sauté for about a minute, being careful not to let it brown.

  3. Increase the heat to medium-high and add the shrimp to the skillet. Cook for 2-3 minutes on each side until they turn pink and opaque.

  4. Remove the shrimp from the skillet and set them aside. In the same skillet, pour in the white wine and chicken broth, scraping up any browned bits from the bottom of the pan.

  5. Allow the liquid to simmer and reduce by half, then add the lemon juice, lemon zest, and chopped parsley. Season with salt, pepper, and red pepper flakes if desired.

  6. Return the shrimp to the skillet, coating them in the lemon garlic sauce. Let everything simmer together for a couple of minutes to allow the flavors to meld.

  7. Finally, add the cooked linguine to the skillet, tossing everything together to ensure the pasta is well-coated with the flavorful sauce. If needed, add a small amount of the reserved pasta water to loosen the sauce.

  8. Serve the Lemon Garlic Shrimp Linguine in individual bowls, garnished with an extra sprinkle of fresh parsley and a wedge of lemon for an extra burst of citrus.

Tips and Variations:

  • For a lighter version, you can use whole wheat or gluten-free linguine.
  • Feel free to add in some fresh cherry tomatoes or baby spinach for an extra pop of color and flavor.
  • If you prefer a creamier sauce, you can stir in a couple of tablespoons of heavy cream or grated Parmesan cheese at the end.

This Lemon Garlic Shrimp Linguine recipe is a celebration of fresh, vibrant flavors that come together to create a truly delightful seafood dish. The combination of tender shrimp, tangy lemon, and aromatic garlic makes this meal a standout favorite for any occasion. Whether you’re a seasoned home cook or just starting your culinary journey, this recipe is an excellent way to elevate your cooking skills and treat yourself to a restaurant-quality meal in the comfort of your own kitchen. Enjoy this zesty seafood delight with family and friends, and savor every mouthwatering bite!
