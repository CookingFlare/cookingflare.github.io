5-Ingredient Caprese Stuffed Chicken Skillet Recipe


If you’re looking for a delicious and easy-to-make chicken dish that is sure to impress, then this 5-Ingredient Caprese Stuffed Chicken Skillet recipe is for you. It combines the classic flavors of Caprese salad with tender and juicy chicken, all cooked in a single skillet. With just five simple ingredients, you can have a gourmet meal on the table in no time.

Ingredients

  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 8 ounces fresh mozzarella cheese, sliced
  • 1/4 cup fresh basil leaves
  • 2 tablespoons balsamic glaze

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).

  2. Using a sharp knife, make a horizontal slit in each chicken breast, creating a pocket for the stuffing. Be careful not to cut all the way through.

  3. Stuff each chicken breast with a few cherry tomato halves, a slice of mozzarella cheese, and a few basil leaves. Press the edges of the chicken together to seal the pocket.

  4. Heat a large oven-safe skillet over medium-high heat. Add a drizzle of olive oil and swirl to coat the bottom of the skillet.

  5. Carefully place the stuffed chicken breasts in the skillet, seam side down. Cook for 4-5 minutes, or until golden brown.

  6. Flip the chicken breasts over and transfer the skillet to the preheated oven. Bake for 15-20 minutes, or until the chicken is cooked through and the cheese is melted and bubbly.

  7. Remove the skillet from the oven and let the chicken rest for a few minutes before serving.

  8. Drizzle the balsamic glaze over the stuffed chicken breasts just before serving. Garnish with extra basil leaves, if desired.

Serving Suggestions

This Caprese Stuffed Chicken Skillet pairs well with a variety of side dishes. Here are a few ideas to complete your meal:

  • Roasted Vegetables: Serve the chicken with a side of roasted vegetables, such as asparagus, Brussels sprouts, or zucchini. Simply toss the vegetables with olive oil, salt, and pepper, and roast them in the oven alongside the chicken.

  • Mashed Potatoes: Creamy mashed potatoes make a comforting and satisfying side dish. Whip up a batch of your favorite mashed potatoes and serve them alongside the stuffed chicken.

  • Garlic Bread: For a carb-filled indulgence, serve the chicken with a side of garlic bread. Toast some slices of French bread and spread them with a mixture of butter, minced garlic, and parsley.

Conclusion

With just five ingredients and a single skillet, you can create a flavorful and impressive dish that will have your guests thinking you spent hours in the kitchen. This 5-Ingredient Caprese Stuffed Chicken Skillet recipe is perfect for busy weeknights or special occasions when you want to make a memorable meal without a lot of fuss. Enjoy the combination of juicy chicken, melted mozzarella, fresh tomatoes, and fragrant basil in every bite. Don’t forget to drizzle the balsamic glaze for that extra touch of tangy sweetness. Give this recipe a try and prepare to be amazed by the simplicity and deliciousness it brings to your table.
