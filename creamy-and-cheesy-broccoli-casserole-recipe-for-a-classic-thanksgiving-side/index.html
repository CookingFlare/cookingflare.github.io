Creamy and Cheesy Broccoli Casserole Recipe for a Classic Thanksgiving Side


Thanksgiving is a time for indulging in delicious and comforting dishes that bring families together. While the turkey is often the star of the show, the side dishes play a crucial role in creating a memorable Thanksgiving feast. One classic side dish that never fails to impress is the Creamy and Cheesy Broccoli Casserole. This dish combines tender broccoli florets with a rich and creamy cheese sauce, creating a mouthwatering combination that will have your guests coming back for seconds.

Ingredients

To make this delectable Creamy and Cheesy Broccoli Casserole, you will need the following ingredients:

  • 4 cups of fresh broccoli florets
  • 1 cup of shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1 cup of grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 cup of heavy cream
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1/4 cup of butter
  • 2 tablespoons of all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon of salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon of black pepper

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C). Grease a 9x13-inch baking dish with butter or cooking spray to prevent sticking.

  2. In a large pot, bring water to a boil. Add the broccoli florets and cook for 3-4 minutes, or until they turn bright green and become slightly tender. Drain the broccoli and set aside.

  3. In a separate saucepan, melt the butter over medium heat. Add the minced garlic and sauté for 1-2 minutes, until fragrant.

  4. Gradually whisk in the flour until it forms a smooth paste. Cook for an additional 1-2 minutes, stirring constantly.

  5. Slowly pour in the heavy cream, whisking continuously to prevent lumps from forming. Continue cooking and stirring until the mixture thickens and comes to a simmer.

  6. Reduce the heat to low and stir in the shredded cheddar cheese and grated Parmesan cheese. Continue stirring until the cheeses melt and the sauce becomes smooth and creamy. Season with salt and black pepper to taste.

  7. Add the cooked broccoli florets to the cheese sauce, gently folding them in until they are well-coated.

  8. Transfer the broccoli mixture to the greased baking dish, spreading it evenly. Top with an additional sprinkle of shredded cheddar cheese and grated Parmesan cheese.

  9. Bake in the preheated oven for 20-25 minutes, or until the casserole is bubbly and the cheese on top turns golden brown.

  10. Remove from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes before serving. The Creamy and Cheesy Broccoli Casserole is best enjoyed warm.

Serving Suggestions

This Creamy and Cheesy Broccoli Casserole is a versatile side dish that pairs well with a variety of main courses. Serve it alongside your Thanksgiving turkey, roasted chicken, or even as a standalone vegetarian option.

For those who enjoy an extra kick of flavor, you can sprinkle some crushed red pepper flakes on top before baking for a hint of spice. Additionally, you can garnish the casserole with fresh herbs like chopped parsley or chives for a pop of freshness.

Conclusion

The Creamy and Cheesy Broccoli Casserole is a beloved classic that adds a touch of elegance and comfort to any Thanksgiving table. With its creamy cheese sauce and tender broccoli florets, it is sure to be a crowd-pleaser that will have your guests asking for the recipe. So, this Thanksgiving, elevate your side dish game and treat your loved ones to this irresistible casserole.
