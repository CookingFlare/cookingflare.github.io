Rice and Grain Tarts: Sweet and Savory Tarts Featuring Nutritious Grains


Tarts are a versatile and delicious dish that can be enjoyed any time of the day. Whether you prefer a sweet or savory flavor profile, tarts offer endless possibilities to satisfy your cravings. In this blog post, we will explore the world of rice and grain tarts, where we combine the goodness of nutritious grains with the indulgence of a tart. Get ready to tantalize your taste buds with these delectable recipes that are both wholesome and flavorful.

Sweet Rice and Grain Tart

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup cooked brown rice
  • 1 cup cooked quinoa
  • 1/2 cup almond flour
  • 1/4 cup maple syrup
  • 2 tablespoons coconut oil
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/2 teaspoon cinnamon
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup mixed berries (strawberries, blueberries, raspberries)
  • 1/4 cup sliced almonds

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C).

  2. In a large bowl, combine the cooked brown rice, quinoa, almond flour, maple syrup, coconut oil, vanilla extract, cinnamon, and salt. Mix well until all the ingredients are thoroughly combined.

  3. Press the mixture into a greased tart pan, ensuring an even layer on the bottom and sides of the pan.

  4. Bake the crust in the preheated oven for 10 minutes, or until lightly golden.

  5. Remove the crust from the oven and let it cool completely.

  6. Once the crust has cooled, spread the mixed berries evenly over the crust.

  7. Sprinkle the sliced almonds on top of the berries.

  8. Place the tart back in the oven and bake for an additional 15 minutes, or until the berries are soft and the almonds are golden.

  9. Remove the tart from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes before serving.

  10. Enjoy a slice of this sweet rice and grain tart as a delightful dessert or a satisfying afternoon snack.

Savory Rice and Grain Tart

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup cooked white rice
  • 1 cup cooked farro
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh basil
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 garlic clove, minced
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1/4 cup sliced black olives
  • 1/4 cup crumbled feta cheese

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C).

  2. In a large bowl, combine the cooked white rice, farro, grated Parmesan cheese, chopped fresh basil, olive oil, minced garlic, dried oregano, salt, and black pepper. Mix well until all the ingredients are thoroughly combined.

  3. Press the mixture into a greased tart pan, ensuring an even layer on the bottom and sides of the pan.

  4. Bake the crust in the preheated oven for 10 minutes, or until lightly golden.

  5. Remove the crust from the oven and let it cool completely.

  6. Once the crust has cooled, arrange the cherry tomatoes and sliced black olives on top of the crust.

  7. Sprinkle the crumbled feta cheese evenly over the tomatoes and olives.

  8. Place the tart back in the oven and bake for an additional 15 minutes, or until the cheese is melted and the tomatoes are slightly roasted.

  9. Remove the tart from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes before serving.

  10. Serve a slice of this savory rice and grain tart as a delightful appetizer or a light lunch option.

Conclusion

Rice and grain tarts are a delightful way to incorporate nutritious grains into your diet while enjoying a tasty treat. Whether you have a sweet tooth or prefer savory flavors, these recipes offer something for everyone. From the sweet berry-filled tart to the savory tomato and feta tart, these creations will surely impress your family and friends. So why not give them a try and experience the deliciousness of rice and grain tarts today? Enjoy!
