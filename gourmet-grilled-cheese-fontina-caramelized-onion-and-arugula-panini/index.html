Gourmet Grilled Cheese: Fontina, Caramelized Onion, and Arugula Panini


There’s something undeniably comforting about a perfectly grilled cheese sandwich. The combination of gooey, melted cheese and crispy bread is a timeless classic that transcends age and culinary preferences. In this recipe, we take the traditional grilled cheese to a whole new level by incorporating the rich flavors of Fontina cheese, the sweetness of caramelized onions, and the peppery bite of arugula. The result? A gourmet grilled cheese panini that’s sure to elevate your sandwich game to new heights.

Ingredients

  • 8 slices of sourdough bread
  • 8 oz. Fontina cheese, thinly sliced
  • 2 large onions, thinly sliced
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 1 cup fresh arugula
  • 2 tbsp balsamic glaze
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

Caramelized Onions

  1. Heat a skillet over medium heat and add the butter.
  2. Add the thinly sliced onions to the skillet and cook, stirring occasionally, until they turn golden brown and caramelized, about 20-25 minutes.
  3. Season the onions with a pinch of salt and set them aside.

Assembling the Panini

  1. Brush one side of each sourdough bread slice with olive oil.
  2. Place the bread slices, oiled side down, on a clean surface.
  3. Layer the Fontina cheese, caramelized onions, and arugula on half of the bread slices.
  4. Drizzle the balsamic glaze over the arugula.
  5. Top with the remaining bread slices, oiled side up.

Grilling the Panini

  1. Preheat a panini press or a non-stick skillet over medium heat.
  2. Carefully transfer the assembled panini to the panini press or skillet.
  3. If using a skillet, place a heavy pan on top of the panini to press it down.
  4. Cook the panini for about 3-4 minutes on each side, or until the bread is golden brown and the cheese is melted.

Serving

  1. Remove the panini from the panini press or skillet and let it cool for a minute.
  2. Carefully slice the panini in half and serve immediately.

Why This Recipe Works

The use of Fontina cheese in this recipe brings a rich and nutty flavor to the panini, complemented by the sweet and savory notes of the caramelized onions. The peppery arugula adds a refreshing contrast, while the balsamic glaze ties all the flavors together with a hint of sweetness and acidity. The combination of these high-quality ingredients creates a sophisticated and indulgent grilled cheese experience that’s perfect for a leisurely lunch or a quick, yet impressive, dinner.

Tips for Success

  • Use a good quality sourdough bread for the best texture and flavor.
  • Be patient when caramelizing the onions to achieve that deep, sweet flavor.
  • Don’t skimp on the Fontina cheese - its meltability and flavor are essential to this recipe.

In Conclusion

The Fontina, Caramelized Onion, and Arugula Panini takes the humble grilled cheese to a whole new level of sophistication. Whether you’re looking to impress guests at a casual get-together or simply treat yourself to a gourmet lunch, this recipe is a surefire way to elevate the classic comfort of a grilled cheese sandwich. Give it a try and savor the delicious combination of flavors that this gourmet panini has to offer.
