10 Delicious Wrap Recipes for Family Lunches


Wraps are the perfect solution for busy families looking for quick, delicious, and versatile meals. Whether you’re packing lunches for school or work, or sitting down for a casual midday meal, wraps bring a mix of flavors and textures to the table. They’re easy to customize with ingredients you have on hand and can be as healthy or indulgent as you please. Letâ€™s dive into ten mouthwatering wrap recipes that are bound to become family favorites.

1. Classic Turkey and Avocado Wrap

Simple yet satisfying, this timeless turkey and avocado wrap is a hit with kids and adults alike.

Ingredients:

  • Whole grain tortillas
  • Sliced turkey breast
  • Fresh avocado slices
  • Crisp romaine lettuce
  • Light mayonnaise or plain Greek yogurt
  • Salt and pepper

Instructions:

  1. Spread a thin layer of mayonnaise or Greek yogurt on the tortilla.
  2. Lay down a few slices of turkey breast evenly.
  3. Add avocado slices and romaine lettuce for a fresh crunch.
  4. Sprinkle lightly with salt and pepper.
  5. Roll the tortilla tightly, slice it in half, and serve.

2. Spicy Buffalo Chicken Wrap

For those who love bold flavors, this spicy buffalo chicken wrap delivers the perfect kick.

Ingredients:

  • Flour tortillas
  • Shredded rotisserie chicken
  • Buffalo sauce
  • Crumbled blue cheese
  • Shredded carrots
  • Ranch dressing

Instructions:

  1. Toss the shredded chicken in buffalo sauce until fully coated.
  2. Place the chicken on the tortilla and top it with blue cheese crumbles.
  3. Add shredded carrots for texture and drizzle ranch dressing over the top.
  4. Roll it up and enjoy the spice!

3. Mediterranean Veggie Wrap

Packed with fresh veggies and Mediterranean flavors, this wrap is light yet satisfying.

Ingredients:

  • Spinach tortillas
  • Hummus (any flavor you like)
  • Sliced cucumbers
  • Cherry tomatoes (halved)
  • Kalamata olives (pitted and sliced)
  • Baby spinach leaves
  • Feta cheese

Instructions:

  1. Spread a generous layer of hummus on the spinach tortilla.
  2. Layer with cucumber slices, cherry tomatoes, olives, and baby spinach.
  3. Sprinkle feta cheese over the veggies.
  4. Fold the tortilla and take a bite of Mediterranean bliss.

4. Southwestern Black Bean Wrap

A vegetarian wrap loaded with protein and zesty southwestern flavors.

Ingredients:

  • Whole-wheat tortillas
  • Cooked black beans
  • Corn kernels
  • Diced red bell peppers
  • Shredded Mexican cheese blend
  • Sour cream or plain yogurt
  • Fresh cilantro

Instructions:

  1. Heat black beans and corn kernels together, seasoned with a pinch of salt.
  2. Spread a dollop of sour cream or yogurt on the tortilla.
  3. Add the black bean and corn mixture.
  4. Sprinkle red bell peppers, shredded cheese, and finish with fresh cilantro.
  5. Wrap it up tightly for a wholesome meal.

5. Grilled Chicken Caesar Wrap

A creamy Caesar salad stuffed into a wrap for a portable lunch option.

Ingredients:

  • Soft tortillas
  • Grilled chicken breast (sliced)
  • Shredded romaine lettuce
  • Grated Parmesan cheese
  • Caesar dressing
  • Croutons (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Toss romaine lettuce with Caesar dressing in a bowl.
  2. Layer the grilled chicken onto the tortilla.
  3. Add dressed lettuce on top of the chicken.
  4. Sprinkle Parmesan cheese and croutons for added crunch.
  5. Roll up the tortilla and savor the Caesar goodness.

6. Sweet and Smoky BBQ Beef Wrap

A hearty wrap featuring smoky barbecue flavors for meat lovers.

Ingredients:

  • Large flour tortillas
  • Slow-cooked beef (shredded)
  • Barbecue sauce
  • Pickled red onions
  • Shredded cheddar cheese
  • Baby arugula

Instructions:

  1. Mix shredded beef with barbecue sauce until coated.
  2. Layer the beef onto the tortilla and add pickled red onions.
  3. Sprinkle shredded cheddar cheese and top with baby arugula.
  4. Roll tightly and dig in!

7. Asian Chicken Lettuce Wraps

These lettuce-based wraps provide a low-carb option with bold Asian-inspired flavors.

Ingredients:

  • Large romaine lettuce leaves
  • Ground chicken or turkey
  • Soy sauce
  • Hoisin sauce
  • Minced garlic
  • Grated ginger
  • Shredded carrots

Instructions:

  1. Cook ground chicken with garlic, ginger, soy sauce, and hoisin sauce.
  2. Let the mixture cool slightly before spooning it into romaine lettuce leaves.
  3. Add shredded carrots for crunch and serve as a handheld wrap.

8. Tuna Salad Wrap

A classic tuna salad reinvented into a portable lunch option.

Ingredients:

  • Whole-grain tortillas
  • Canned tuna (drained)
  • Plain Greek yogurt or mayonnaise
  • Diced celery
  • Sliced red onion
  • Lemon juice
  • Black pepper

Instructions:

  1. Combine tuna, Greek yogurt, celery, red onion, lemon juice, and pepper in a bowl.
  2. Spread the tuna salad mixture evenly on the tortilla.
  3. Roll tightly and pack for an easy grab-and-go lunch.

9. Caprese Chicken Wrap

A fresh take on the Italian classic, perfect for lovers of mozzarella and tomatoes.

Ingredients:

  • Sun-dried tomato tortillas
  • Grilled chicken breast (sliced)
  • Fresh mozzarella slices
  • Sliced tomatoes
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • Balsamic glaze

Instructions:

  1. Layer the grilled chicken, mozzarella, tomato slices, and basil onto the tortilla.
  2. Drizzle with balsamic glaze for extra flavor.
  3. Roll up and enjoy the freshness in every bite.

10. Breakfast Burrito Wrap

Who says wraps are just for lunch? This breakfast burrito is great any time of day.

Ingredients:

  • Large tortillas
  • Scrambled eggs
  • Crumbled cooked bacon or sausage
  • Shredded cheese
  • Diced potatoes or hashbrowns
  • Salsa

Instructions:

  1. Layer scrambled eggs, bacon or sausage, and shredded cheese onto the tortilla.
  2. Add cooked diced potatoes or hashbrowns for heartiness.
  3. Spoon a bit of salsa over the fillings.
  4. Wrap it up and enjoy a family-friendly breakfast treat!
