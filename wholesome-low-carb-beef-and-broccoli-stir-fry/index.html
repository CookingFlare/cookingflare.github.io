Wholesome Low Carb Beef and Broccoli Stir-Fry


If you’re looking for a quick, delicious, and healthy dinner option, look no further than this Wholesome Low Carb Beef and Broccoli Stir-Fry recipe. Packed with flavor and nutrients, this stir-fry is the perfect way to satisfy your cravings without compromising your dietary goals. Plus, it’s easy to make and can be ready in just under 30 minutes!

Ingredients

  • 1 pound of lean beef, thinly sliced
  • 2 cups of broccoli florets
  • 1 red bell pepper, thinly sliced
  • 1 yellow bell pepper, thinly sliced
  • 1 medium onion, thinly sliced
  • 3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon of fresh ginger, grated
  • 3 tablespoons of low-sodium soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons of oyster sauce
  • 1 tablespoon of sesame oil
  • 1 tablespoon of olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Optional: red pepper flakes for a spicy kick

Instructions

  1. In a large skillet or wok, heat the olive oil over medium-high heat. Add the sliced beef and cook until browned, about 3-4 minutes. Remove the beef from the skillet and set it aside.

  2. In the same skillet, add the sesame oil, minced garlic, and grated ginger. Cook for about 1 minute until fragrant.

  3. Add the sliced onions, bell peppers, and broccoli florets to the skillet. Stir-fry for about 5 minutes until the vegetables are slightly tender but still crisp.

  4. In a small bowl, whisk together the low-sodium soy sauce and oyster sauce. Pour the sauce mixture over the vegetables in the skillet and stir to coat evenly.

  5. Return the cooked beef to the skillet and toss everything together. Cook for an additional 2-3 minutes until the beef is heated through and the flavors have melded together.

  6. Season with salt and pepper to taste. If you like it spicy, sprinkle some red pepper flakes on top.

  7. Serve hot and enjoy your Wholesome Low Carb Beef and Broccoli Stir-Fry! You can enjoy it as is or pair it with cauliflower rice or steamed brown rice for a more filling meal.

Health Benefits

This Wholesome Low Carb Beef and Broccoli Stir-Fry is not only delicious but also packed with health benefits. Here are some of the reasons why you should incorporate this dish into your regular meal rotation:

  • Low in carbs: By using lean beef and plenty of fresh vegetables, this stir-fry is low in carbohydrates, making it ideal for those following a low-carb or keto diet.

  • High in protein: Beef is an excellent source of protein, which is essential for building and repairing muscles, as well as supporting overall health and wellbeing.

  • Rich in vitamins and minerals: Broccoli and bell peppers are loaded with essential vitamins and minerals, including vitamin C, vitamin A, and potassium. These nutrients play a crucial role in maintaining a healthy immune system and promoting optimal bodily functions.

  • Fiber-rich: Thanks to the broccoli and other veggies, this stir-fry is also a good source of dietary fiber. Fiber aids in digestion, helps regulate blood sugar levels, and promotes a feeling of fullness.

  • Heart-healthy: The use of lean beef and minimal added fats in this recipe makes it heart-healthy. Lean beef is low in saturated fats and rich in omega-3 fatty acids, which can help reduce the risk of heart disease.

So, what are you waiting for? Give this Wholesome Low Carb Beef and Broccoli Stir-Fry a try and savor the deliciousness while nourishing your body with wholesome ingredients. Your taste buds and your health will thank you!
