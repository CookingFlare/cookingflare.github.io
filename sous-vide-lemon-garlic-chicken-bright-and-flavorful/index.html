Sous Vide Lemon Garlic Chicken: Bright and Flavorful


Welcome to another exciting recipe on our blog! Today, we’re going to explore the wonderful world of sous vide cooking and create a dish that is bursting with bright flavors – Sous Vide Lemon Garlic Chicken. This recipe is perfect for those looking to elevate their chicken game and impress their taste buds. So, let’s dive right in!

Ingredients

  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 4 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 2 lemons, juiced and zested
  • 2 tablespoons of olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon of salt
  • 1 teaspoon of black pepper
  • 1 tablespoon of fresh parsley, chopped

Instructions

  1. Preheat your sous vide water bath to 145°F (63°C). This temperature will ensure that the chicken is perfectly cooked and retains its moisture.

  2. In a small bowl, combine the minced garlic, lemon zest, olive oil, salt, and black pepper. Mix well to create a flavorful marinade.

  3. Place each chicken breast in a separate vacuum-sealed bag. Pour the marinade evenly over each chicken breast, ensuring that they are well-coated. Seal the bags using a vacuum sealer or the water displacement method.

  4. Gently place the sealed bags into the preheated water bath. Make sure that the chicken is fully submerged. Allow the chicken to cook for 1 to 2 hours, depending on the thickness of the breasts. The longer cooking time will result in a more tender and flavorful chicken.

  5. While the chicken is cooking, prepare a bowl of ice water. Once the cooking time is done, carefully remove the bags from the water bath and immediately place them in the ice bath. This will stop the cooking process and chill the chicken.

  6. After the chicken has cooled down, remove it from the bags and pat dry with a paper towel. This step is crucial to ensure that the chicken develops a beautiful golden crust when seared.

  7. Heat a skillet over medium-high heat and add a tablespoon of olive oil. Once the oil is hot, add the chicken breasts and sear them for 1 to 2 minutes on each side until they develop a golden brown crust.

  8. Remove the chicken from the skillet and let it rest for a few minutes. This resting period allows the juices to redistribute, resulting in a more tender and succulent chicken.

  9. Sprinkle the freshly chopped parsley over the chicken breasts for a vibrant burst of freshness and color.

Serving Suggestions

Sous Vide Lemon Garlic Chicken pairs well with a variety of side dishes. Here are a few suggestions to complete your meal:

  • Roasted vegetables: Toss some seasonal vegetables with olive oil, salt, and pepper. Roast them in the oven until they are tender and slightly caramelized.

  • Garlic mashed potatoes: Boil some potatoes until they are fork-tender. Drain and mash them with butter, garlic, and a splash of milk. Season with salt and pepper to taste.

  • Lemon herb rice: Cook some fragrant basmati rice according to the instructions on the package. Once cooked, fluff it with a fork and stir in some fresh lemon juice, chopped herbs, and a drizzle of olive oil.

Conclusion

Congratulations! You have successfully created a bright and flavorful Sous Vide Lemon Garlic Chicken. This cooking technique ensures that the chicken is cooked to perfection, tender and juicy on the inside, with a beautiful golden crust on the outside. The combination of zesty lemon and aromatic garlic creates a burst of flavor that will tantalize your taste buds. So, go ahead and impress your family and friends with this delicious and impressive dish. Happy cooking!
