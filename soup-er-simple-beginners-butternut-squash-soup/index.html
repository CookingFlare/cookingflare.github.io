Soup-er Simple: Beginner's Butternut Squash Soup


Butternut squash soup is a classic fall dish that is both comforting and delicious. With its creamy texture and subtle sweetness, it’s no wonder why this soup has become a favorite among many. If you’re a beginner in the kitchen or simply looking for a quick and easy recipe, this soup is perfect for you. In this post, we’ll walk you through the steps to make a Soup-er Simple Butternut Squash Soup that will warm your soul.

Ingredients

  • 1 medium-sized butternut squash, peeled, seeded, and cut into chunks
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 carrot, peeled and chopped
  • 1 apple, peeled, cored, and chopped
  • 4 cups vegetable broth
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground nutmeg
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Olive oil for cooking
  • Optional toppings: roasted pumpkin seeds, a drizzle of cream, or a sprinkle of fresh herbs

Instructions

  1. Heat a large pot over medium heat and add a drizzle of olive oil. Once the oil is hot, add the diced onion and minced garlic. Sauté for a few minutes until the onion becomes translucent and the garlic becomes fragrant.

  2. Add the chopped carrot and apple to the pot and cook for another few minutes until they start to soften.

  3. Now, it’s time to add the star of the show – the butternut squash. Add the squash chunks to the pot and give everything a good stir.

  4. Pour in the vegetable broth, making sure it covers all the ingredients in the pot. If needed, add more broth or water to ensure everything is submerged.

  5. Season the soup with ground cinnamon, ground nutmeg, salt, and pepper. Stir well to distribute the flavors evenly.

  6. Bring the soup to a boil, then reduce the heat to low. Cover the pot and let the soup simmer for about 20-25 minutes or until the butternut squash is tender when pierced with a fork.

  7. Once the squash is cooked, remove the pot from the heat and let it cool slightly.

  8. Using an immersion blender or a regular blender, carefully puree the soup until smooth and creamy. If using a regular blender, blend the soup in batches, making sure to vent the lid to prevent any accidents.

  9. Return the pureed soup to the pot and heat it over low heat, stirring occasionally, until it is heated through.

  10. Taste the soup and adjust the seasonings if needed. If the soup is too thick, you can add a little more vegetable broth or water to thin it out.

  11. Ladle the Soup-er Simple Butternut Squash Soup into bowls and serve hot. If desired, top with roasted pumpkin seeds, a drizzle of cream, or a sprinkle of fresh herbs for an extra touch of flavor and presentation.

Enjoy your homemade Soup-er Simple Butternut Squash Soup and savor the warm flavors of fall. This recipe is perfect for beginners or anyone looking for a quick and easy meal. It’s a great way to introduce yourself to the joys of cooking and explore the wonderful world of soups. So grab your apron, gather your ingredients, and let’s get cooking!

Remember, cooking is all about experimentation, so don’t be afraid to make this recipe your own. Add a pinch of cayenne pepper for some heat or a squeeze of lemon juice for a tangy twist. The possibilities are endless, and the result will always be delicious. Happy cooking!
