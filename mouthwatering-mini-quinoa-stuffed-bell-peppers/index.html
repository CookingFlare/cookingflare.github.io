Mouthwatering Mini Quinoa Stuffed Bell Peppers


If you’re looking for a delicious and healthy appetizer or side dish, these mouthwatering mini quinoa stuffed bell peppers are just what you need. Packed with flavor and nutrient-rich ingredients, they are sure to impress your family and friends. Plus, they are gluten-free and vegetarian, making them a versatile option for any dietary preference.

Ingredients

  • 12 mini bell peppers, any color
  • 1 cup cooked quinoa
  • 1/2 cup black beans, rinsed and drained
  • 1/2 cup corn kernels
  • 1/4 cup diced red onion
  • 1/4 cup diced tomatoes
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh cilantro
  • 1/2 teaspoon cumin
  • 1/2 teaspoon chili powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon garlic powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Optional toppings: shredded cheese, avocado, sour cream

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).

  2. Cut the tops off the mini bell peppers and remove the seeds and membranes. Set aside.

  3. In a large mixing bowl, combine the cooked quinoa, black beans, corn kernels, red onion, tomatoes, cilantro, cumin, chili powder, garlic powder, salt, and pepper. Mix well until all the ingredients are evenly distributed.

  4. Stuff each mini bell pepper with the quinoa mixture, pressing it down gently to ensure it fills the pepper completely.

  5. Place the stuffed bell peppers on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper or a silicone baking mat.

  6. Bake in the preheated oven for 20-25 minutes, or until the peppers are tender and slightly charred.

  7. Remove from the oven and let cool for a few minutes before serving.

  8. Serve the mini quinoa stuffed bell peppers as is, or add your favorite toppings such as shredded cheese, avocado slices, or a dollop of sour cream.

Tips and Variations

  • To save time, you can cook the quinoa ahead of time and store it in the refrigerator until you’re ready to make the stuffed bell peppers.

  • If you prefer a spicier flavor, add a dash of hot sauce or sprinkle some crushed red pepper flakes into the quinoa mixture.

  • For a heartier meal, serve these mini quinoa stuffed bell peppers on a bed of mixed greens or alongside grilled chicken or fish.

  • Feel free to get creative with the ingredients! You can add diced jalapenos, chopped olives, or even crumbled feta cheese to the quinoa mixture for extra flavor.

  • Leftovers can be stored in an airtight container in the refrigerator for up to 3 days. They make a great grab-and-go lunch option.

Conclusion

These mouthwatering mini quinoa stuffed bell peppers are a delightful addition to any meal or gathering. Whether you’re hosting a party or simply looking for a nutritious side dish, these peppers are sure to please. The combination of quinoa, black beans, and spices creates a flavorful filling that perfectly complements the sweetness of the bell peppers. So, give this recipe a try and enjoy a burst of flavors in every bite!

Remember, cooking is all about experimentation and making it your own. Feel free to adjust the ingredients and spices to suit your taste preferences. And most importantly, have fun in the kitchen!
