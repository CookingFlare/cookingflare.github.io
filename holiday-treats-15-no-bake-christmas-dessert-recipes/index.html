Holiday Treats: 15 No-Bake Christmas Dessert Recipes


The holiday season is a magical time filled with warmth, joy, and delicious treats. As we gather around with family and friends, sweet delights become the centerpiece of our celebrations. However, not everyone has the time or desire to spend hours in the kitchen baking. That’s where no-bake desserts come to the rescue! These quick and easy recipes allow you to whip up delicious holiday treats without ever turning on the oven. In this blog post, we’ll dive into 15 delightful no-bake Christmas dessert recipes that will impress your guests and satisfy your sweet tooth.

1. Peppermint Bark

Ingredients:

  • 12 oz white chocolate
  • 12 oz dark chocolate
  • 1 tsp peppermint extract
  • Crushed candy canes for topping

Instructions:

  1. Melt dark chocolate in a microwave-safe bowl in 30-second increments, stirring until smooth.
  2. Spread the melted dark chocolate into a thin layer on a parchment-lined baking sheet.
  3. Refrigerate until set, about 30 minutes.
  4. Melt white chocolate in a similar manner and stir in peppermint extract.
  5. Pour the white chocolate over the set dark chocolate layer and spread evenly.
  6. Sprinkle crushed candy canes on top and refrigerate until completely firm. Break into pieces to serve.

2. Chocolate-Covered Pretzel Rods

Ingredients:

  • 1 bag of pretzel rods
  • 12 oz of chocolate (milk or dark)
  • Assorted sprinkles or crushed nuts

Instructions:

  1. Melt chocolate in a microwave or double boiler until smooth.
  2. Dip each pretzel rod into the chocolate, allowing excess to drip off.
  3. Roll the chocolate-covered pretzel in sprinkles or nuts while still wet.
  4. Place on parchment paper to set. Allow to cool completely before serving.

3. No-Bake Cheesecake Cups

Ingredients:

  • 8 oz cream cheese, softened
  • 1 cup powdered sugar
  • 1 cup whipped cream
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • Graham cracker crumbs
  • Fresh berries for topping

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, beat the cream cheese and powdered sugar until smooth.
  2. Fold in the whipped cream and vanilla extract.
  3. In serving cups, layer graham cracker crumbs, cheesecake mixture, and top with fresh berries.
  4. Refrigerate for at least 2 hours before serving for a chilled treat.

4. Coconut Snowballs

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups shredded coconut
  • 1 cup sweetened condensed milk
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • Extra shredded coconut for rolling

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, mix shredded coconut, sweetened condensed milk, and vanilla extract until well combined.
  2. Form the mixture into small balls and roll in additional shredded coconut.
  3. Place in the refrigerator for about 30 minutes to firm up before serving.

5. Chocolate Rice Crispy Treats

Ingredients:

  • 4 cups rice cereal
  • 10 oz chocolate chips
  • 1 cup mini marshmallows
  • ¼ cup butter

Instructions:

  1. Melt butter in a large saucepan over low heat. Add chocolate chips and stir until melted.
  2. Stir in rice cereal and mini marshmallows until well coated.
  3. Press the mixture into a greased 9x13-inch pan and let sit until set. Cut into squares to serve.

6. Pumpkin Chocolate Chip Energy Bites

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup rolled oats
  • ½ cup pumpkin puree
  • ¼ cup honey
  • ½ cup chocolate chips
  • 1 tsp pumpkin pie spice

Instructions:

  1. In a mixing bowl, combine all ingredients and mix until well combined.
  2. Roll the mixture into small balls and place on a baking sheet.
  3. Refrigerate for at least 30 minutes before serving.

7. Nutty Chocolate Truffles

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup chocolate chips
  • ½ cup heavy cream
  • 1 cup chopped nuts (walnuts, almonds, or pecans)
  • Cocoa powder for dusting

Instructions:

  1. Heat heavy cream until simmering, then pour over chocolate chips. Let sit for a few minutes and stir until smooth.
  2. Refrigerate the mixture until firm, about 1-2 hours.
  3. Scoop out small portions and roll into balls. Roll in chopped nuts or cocoa powder before serving.

8. No-Bake Peanut Butter Bars

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup peanut butter
  • 1 cup powdered sugar
  • 1 cup graham cracker crumbs
  • 1 cup chocolate chips

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, mix peanut butter, powdered sugar, and graham cracker crumbs until well combined.
  2. Press the mixture into a greased 8x8-inch pan.
  3. Melt chocolate chips and pour over the peanut butter layer. Spread evenly.
  4. Refrigerate for at least an hour before cutting into squares.

9. Fruit and Yogurt Parfaits

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups Greek yogurt
  • 2 cups mixed berries (strawberries, blueberries, raspberries)
  • Honey or maple syrup (to taste)
  • Granola for topping

Instructions:

  1. In serving glasses, layer Greek yogurt, mixed berries, and a drizzle of honey or maple syrup.
  2. Top with granola for added crunch.
  3. Serve immediately or refrigerate for a refreshing treat later.

10. Oreo Truffles

Ingredients:

  • 1 package Oreo cookies
  • 8 oz cream cheese, softened
  • 12 oz chocolate for coating

Instructions:

  1. Crush Oreo cookies into fine crumbs and mix with cream cheese until well combined.
  2. Roll the mixture into small balls and place on a baking sheet.
  3. Freeze for about 30 minutes to firm up.
  4. Melt chocolate and dip each truffle, then place back on the baking sheet to set.

11. No-Bake Chocolate Mousse

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1 cup chocolate chips
  • 2 eggs, separated
  • 2 tbsp sugar

Instructions:

  1. Melt chocolate chips in a microwave or double boiler. Let cool slightly.
  2. Whip heavy cream until soft peaks form. Set aside.
  3. In a bowl, beat egg whites until soft peaks form, gradually adding sugar until glossy.
  4. Stir egg yolks into the cooled chocolate, then fold in whipped cream and egg whites gently.
  5. Spoon into serving dishes and refrigerate for at least 2 hours before serving.

12. Gingerbread Cookie Dough Bites

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup almond flour
  • 1 tsp ground ginger
  • 1 tsp cinnamon
  • ¼ cup honey
  • ¼ cup mini chocolate chips

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, combine almond flour, ginger, cinnamon, and honey. Mix until a dough forms.
  2. Fold in mini chocolate chips.
  3. Roll into small balls and refrigerate for 30 minutes before serving.

13. No-Bake Tiramisu Cups

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup mascarpone cheese
  • 1 cup whipped cream
  • ½ cup brewed coffee, cooled
  • 1 package ladyfinger cookies
  • Cocoa powder for dusting

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, mix mascarpone cheese and whipped cream until smooth.
  2. Dip ladyfingers in cooled coffee and layer them in serving cups.
  3. Add a layer of the mascarpone mixture, then repeat layers.
  4. Dust with cocoa powder before serving. Chill for at least 2 hours.

14. Chocolate-Covered Marshmallows

Ingredients:

  • 1 bag of large marshmallows
  • 12 oz chocolate (milk or dark)
  • Assorted toppings (sprinkles, crushed nuts, coconut)

Instructions:

  1. Melt chocolate in a microwave or double boiler until smooth.
  2. Dip each marshmallow into the chocolate, allowing excess to drip off.
  3. Roll in your choice of toppings while the chocolate is still wet.
  4. Set on parchment paper to cool completely before serving.

15. No-Bake S’mores Bars

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups graham cracker crumbs
  • 1 cup mini marshmallows
  • 1 cup chocolate chips
  • ½ cup butter, melted

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, mix graham cracker crumbs, melted butter, and half the mini marshmallows.
  2. Press the mixture into a greased 9x13-inch pan.
  3. Sprinkle remaining mini marshmallows and chocolate chips on top.
  4. Refrigerate until firm, then cut into squares to serve.

Conclusion

No-bake desserts are a fantastic way to bring holiday cheer to your table without the fuss of baking. These 15 no-bake Christmas dessert recipes offer a delightful variety of flavors and textures to satisfy every palate. From the crunch of chocolate-covered pretzel rods to the creaminess of no-bake cheesecake cups, there’s something for everyone to enjoy. So gather your ingredients, invite your loved ones, and create sweet memories this holiday season with these easy and delicious treats! Happy holidays!
