Decadent Tarte au Chocolat: A French Chocolate Tart


If you’re a chocolate lover, then you’re in for a treat with this decadent Tarte au Chocolat recipe. This French chocolate tart is the epitome of indulgence, with its rich and velvety chocolate filling encased in a buttery, flaky pastry shell. It’s the perfect dessert to impress your guests or satisfy your own sweet cravings.

Ingredients

For the pastry:

  • 200g all-purpose flour
  • 100g unsalted butter, cold and cubed
  • 1 tablespoon granulated sugar
  • Pinch of salt
  • 1 egg yolk
  • 2-3 tablespoons cold water

For the chocolate filling:

  • 200g dark chocolate, chopped
  • 200ml heavy cream
  • 50g unsalted butter, at room temperature
  • 3 large eggs
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • Pinch of salt

Instructions

Step 1: Preparing the Pastry

  1. In a large mixing bowl, combine the flour, sugar, and salt. Add the cold cubed butter and use your fingertips to rub it into the flour until the mixture resembles coarse crumbs.
  2. Create a well in the center of the mixture and add the egg yolk. Gradually add the cold water, a tablespoon at a time, while mixing with a fork until the dough comes together.
  3. Transfer the dough onto a lightly floured surface and knead it gently until smooth. Shape it into a disc, wrap it in plastic wrap, and refrigerate for at least 30 minutes.

Step 2: Pre-baking the Pastry Shell

  1. Preheat the oven to 180°C (350°F). Remove the pastry dough from the refrigerator and let it sit at room temperature for a few minutes.
  2. On a lightly floured surface, roll out the dough into a circle large enough to fit your tart pan. Transfer the dough to the pan, pressing it gently into the corners and trimming any excess.
  3. Prick the bottom of the pastry with a fork, then line it with parchment paper and fill it with baking weights or dried beans.
  4. Bake the pastry shell for 15 minutes, then remove the weights and parchment paper and bake for an additional 5 minutes, or until the crust is golden brown. Set aside to cool.

Step 3: Making the Chocolate Filling

  1. In a heatproof bowl, melt the chopped dark chocolate over a pot of simmering water, making sure the bowl doesn’t touch the water. Stir until smooth, then remove from heat and let it cool slightly.
  2. In a separate bowl, whip the heavy cream until soft peaks form. Set aside.
  3. In another bowl, cream the room temperature butter until smooth. Add the eggs one at a time, beating well after each addition. Stir in the vanilla extract and salt.
  4. Gradually add the melted chocolate to the butter mixture, whisking until well combined. Gently fold in the whipped cream until the mixture is smooth and glossy.

Step 4: Assembling and Baking the Tart

  1. Pour the chocolate filling into the pre-baked pastry shell, smoothing the top with a spatula.
  2. Place the tart on a baking sheet and bake for 25-30 minutes, or until the filling is set around the edges but still slightly wobbly in the center.
  3. Remove the tart from the oven and let it cool completely at room temperature. Once cooled, refrigerate for at least 2 hours to allow the filling to set.

Serving and Enjoying

When you’re ready to serve, remove the tart from the refrigerator and let it sit at room temperature for a few minutes. You can dust it with cocoa powder or powdered sugar for a finishing touch. Slice it into wedges and savor the velvety smoothness of the chocolate filling against the buttery pastry. This Tarte au Chocolat is sure to delight your taste buds and leave you craving for more.

So, the next time you’re in the mood for a truly indulgent dessert, give this Decadent Tarte au Chocolat recipe a try. Your guests will be impressed, and you’ll have a new go-to dessert for any occasion. Bon appétit!
