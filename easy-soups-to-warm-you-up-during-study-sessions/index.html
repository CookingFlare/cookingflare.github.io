Easy Soups to Warm You Up During Study Sessions


Studying can be mentally taxing, especially during those long, chilly nights. What better way to fuel your brain and warm your soul than with a comforting bowl of soup? Easy to prepare and packed with nutrients, these soups will keep you cozy and focused during your study sessions. Let’s dive into some delicious and simple soup recipes that are perfect for those late-night study marathons.

1. Classic Chicken Noodle Soup

Preparation time: 30 minutes | Serves: 4

Ingredients:

  • 2 chicken breasts, cooked and shredded
  • 8 cups chicken broth
  • 2 carrots, diced
  • 2 celery stalks, diced
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 2 cups egg noodles
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley for garnish

Instructions:

  1. In a large pot, sauté the carrots, celery, onion, and garlic until fragrant.
  2. Add the chicken broth and bring to a boil.
  3. Add the shredded chicken and egg noodles, then simmer until the noodles are tender.
  4. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  5. Garnish with fresh parsley before serving.

2. Creamy Tomato Basil Soup

Preparation time: 40 minutes | Serves: 6

Ingredients:

  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 3 garlic cloves, minced
  • 2 cans (28 oz each) whole tomatoes
  • 1 cup chicken broth
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1/4 cup fresh basil, chopped
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Croutons for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Heat the olive oil in a pot and sauté the onion and garlic until soft.
  2. Add the whole tomatoes and chicken broth, then simmer for 20 minutes.
  3. Use an immersion blender to puree the soup until smooth.
  4. Stir in the heavy cream and fresh basil, then season with salt and pepper.
  5. Simmer for an additional 10 minutes.
  6. Serve with a sprinkle of fresh basil and croutons on top.

3. Hearty Vegetable Minestrone

Preparation time: 45 minutes | Serves: 8

Ingredients:

  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 3 garlic cloves, minced
  • 2 carrots, diced
  • 2 celery stalks, diced
  • 1 zucchini, diced
  • 1 cup green beans, chopped
  • 1 can (14 oz) diced tomatoes
  • 8 cups vegetable broth
  • 1 cup ditalini pasta
  • 1 can (15 oz) kidney beans, drained and rinsed
  • 2 cups spinach
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Grated Parmesan cheese for garnish

Instructions:

  1. In a large pot, heat the olive oil and sauté the onion and garlic until translucent.
  2. Add the carrots, celery, zucchini, and green beans, and cook for 5 minutes.
  3. Pour in the diced tomatoes and vegetable broth, then bring to a boil.
  4. Add the ditalini pasta and simmer until al dente.
  5. Stir in the kidney beans and spinach, then season with salt and pepper.
  6. Serve with a sprinkle of grated Parmesan cheese on top.

Conclusion

These easy and nourishing soup recipes are perfect for keeping you warm and focused during your study sessions. Whether you prefer a classic chicken noodle soup, a creamy tomato basil soup, or a hearty vegetable minestrone, there’s a comforting bowl of soup for everyone. So, the next time you’re hitting the books, take a break and treat yourself to one of these delicious soups. Your mind and body will thank you for it!
