10 Salad Recipes for a Light and Refreshing Dinner


When the weather heats up and you crave something light yet satisfying for dinner, salads are the perfect go-to option. Packed with fresh ingredients, vibrant colors, and delicious flavors, salads can be incredibly versatile. Whether you’re looking for a quick weekday meal or planning a leisurely weekend dinner, here are 10 salad recipes that will delight your taste buds and leave you feeling refreshed.

1. Greek Salad with Lemon Herb Vinaigrette

Ingredients:

  • Romaine lettuce, chopped
  • Cherry tomatoes, halved
  • Cucumber, sliced
  • Red onion, thinly sliced
  • Kalamata olives
  • Feta cheese, crumbled
  • For the dressing: Olive oil, lemon juice, garlic, oregano, salt, and pepper

Instructions:

  1. In a large bowl, combine the lettuce, tomatoes, cucumber, red onion, olives, and feta cheese.
  2. In a small jar, shake together the olive oil, lemon juice, minced garlic, oregano, salt, and pepper until well combined.
  3. Drizzle the dressing over the salad and toss gently to coat. Serve immediately.

2. Thai Mango Salad with Peanut Dressing

Ingredients:

  • Mixed greens
  • Fresh mango, sliced or diced
  • Red bell pepper, thinly sliced
  • Carrots, shredded
  • Red cabbage, shredded
  • Roasted peanuts, chopped
  • For the dressing: Peanut butter, soy sauce, lime juice, honey, sesame oil, ginger, and garlic

Instructions:

  1. Arrange the mixed greens on a serving platter. Top with mango, red bell pepper, carrots, red cabbage, and roasted peanuts.
  2. In a small bowl, whisk together the peanut butter, soy sauce, lime juice, honey, sesame oil, minced ginger, and garlic until smooth.
  3. Drizzle the dressing over the salad just before serving. Garnish with extra peanuts if desired.

3. Caprese Salad with Balsamic Glaze

Ingredients:

  • Fresh mozzarella cheese, sliced
  • Tomatoes, sliced
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • Balsamic glaze
  • Extra virgin olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Arrange alternating slices of mozzarella cheese and tomatoes on a serving platter.
  2. Tuck fresh basil leaves between the slices.
  3. Drizzle with balsamic glaze and extra virgin olive oil.
  4. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Serve immediately.

4. Quinoa Salad with Roasted Vegetables

Ingredients:

  • Quinoa, cooked and cooled
  • Bell peppers (red, yellow, and green), diced
  • Zucchini, diced
  • Red onion, diced
  • Cherry tomatoes, halved
  • Fresh parsley, chopped
  • For the dressing: Olive oil, lemon juice, Dijon mustard, honey, garlic, salt, and pepper

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 400°F (200°C). Toss the diced bell peppers, zucchini, and red onion with olive oil, salt, and pepper. Spread on a baking sheet and roast for 20-25 minutes until tender and slightly caramelized.
  2. In a large bowl, combine the cooked quinoa, roasted vegetables, cherry tomatoes, and chopped parsley.
  3. In a small bowl, whisk together the olive oil, lemon juice, Dijon mustard, honey, minced garlic, salt, and pepper.
  4. Pour the dressing over the salad and toss gently to combine. Serve warm or chilled.

5. Cobb Salad with Creamy Avocado Dressing

Ingredients:

  • Mixed greens
  • Grilled chicken breast, sliced
  • Hard-boiled eggs, sliced
  • Avocado, diced
  • Cherry tomatoes, halved
  • Crispy bacon, crumbled
  • Blue cheese, crumbled
  • For the dressing: Avocado, Greek yogurt, lime juice, cilantro, garlic, salt, and pepper

Instructions:

  1. Arrange the mixed greens on a large plate. Top with grilled chicken breast, sliced hard-boiled eggs, diced avocado, cherry tomatoes, crumbled crispy bacon, and blue cheese.
  2. In a blender or food processor, combine the avocado, Greek yogurt, lime juice, chopped cilantro, minced garlic, salt, and pepper. Blend until smooth and creamy.
  3. Drizzle the dressing over the salad just before serving. Garnish with additional cilantro if desired.

6. Mediterranean Orzo Salad

Ingredients:

  • Orzo pasta, cooked and cooled
  • Cucumber, diced
  • Cherry tomatoes, halved
  • Kalamata olives, sliced
  • Red onion, finely chopped
  • Fresh parsley, chopped
  • Feta cheese, crumbled
  • For the dressing: Olive oil, red wine vinegar, lemon juice, dried oregano, salt, and pepper

Instructions:

  1. In a large bowl, combine the cooked and cooled orzo pasta, diced cucumber, halved cherry tomatoes, sliced Kalamata olives, finely chopped red onion, chopped parsley, and crumbled feta cheese.
  2. In a small bowl, whisk together the olive oil, red wine vinegar, lemon juice, dried oregano, salt, and pepper.
  3. Pour the dressing over the salad and toss gently to coat. Serve chilled or at room temperature.

7. Asian Sesame Chicken Salad

Ingredients:

  • Mixed greens
  • Grilled chicken breast, sliced
  • Edamame, shelled
  • Red bell pepper, thinly sliced
  • Carrots, shredded
  • Cucumber, sliced
  • Roasted peanuts, chopped
  • Sesame seeds
  • For the dressing: Soy sauce, rice vinegar, sesame oil, honey, ginger, garlic, and sriracha (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Arrange the mixed greens on a serving platter. Top with grilled chicken breast, shelled edamame, thinly sliced red bell pepper, shredded carrots, sliced cucumber, chopped roasted peanuts, and sesame seeds.
  2. In a small bowl, whisk together the soy sauce, rice vinegar, sesame oil, honey, minced ginger, minced garlic, and sriracha (if using).
  3. Drizzle the dressing over the salad just before serving. Garnish with extra sesame seeds and chopped peanuts.

8. Spinach and Strawberry Salad with Poppy Seed Dressing

Ingredients:

  • Baby spinach leaves
  • Fresh strawberries, sliced
  • Red onion, thinly sliced
  • Goat cheese, crumbled
  • Toasted almonds, sliced
  • For the dressing: Greek yogurt, honey, apple cider vinegar, poppy seeds, salt, and pepper

Instructions:

  1. In a large bowl, combine the baby spinach leaves, sliced fresh strawberries, thinly sliced red onion, crumbled goat cheese, and toasted sliced almonds.
  2. In a small bowl, whisk together the Greek yogurt, honey, apple cider vinegar, poppy seeds, salt, and pepper until smooth.
  3. Drizzle the dressing over the salad just before serving. Toss gently to coat all ingredients.

9. Shrimp and Avocado Salad

Ingredients:

  • Mixed greens
  • Cooked shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • Avocado, diced
  • Grape tomatoes, halved
  • Cucumber, sliced
  • Red onion, thinly sliced
  • For the dressing: Olive oil, lime juice, cilantro, garlic, salt, and pepper

Instructions:

  1. Arrange the mixed greens on a serving platter. Top with cooked shrimp, diced avocado, halved grape tomatoes, sliced cucumber, and thinly sliced red onion.
  2. In a small bowl, whisk together the olive oil, lime juice, chopped cilantro, minced garlic, salt, and pepper.
  3. Drizzle the dressing over the salad just before serving. Serve immediately.

10. Watermelon and Feta Salad

Ingredients:

  • Watermelon, cubed
  • Feta cheese, cubed or crumbled
  • Fresh mint leaves, chopped
  • Red onion, thinly sliced
  • Arugula or mixed greens
  • Balsamic glaze
  • Extra virgin olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a large bowl, combine the cubed watermelon, cubed or crumbled feta cheese, chopped fresh mint leaves, thinly sliced red onion, and arugula or mixed greens.
  2. Drizzle with balsamic glaze and extra virgin olive oil.
  3. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Serve immediately for a refreshing summer salad.

These 10 salad recipes are not only delicious and satisfying but also easy to customize with your favorite ingredients. Whether you’re in the mood for a classic Greek salad or something more adventurous like an Asian sesame chicken salad, there’s a recipe here to suit every palate. Enjoy these light and refreshing salads for a dinner that’s both healthy and flavorful!
