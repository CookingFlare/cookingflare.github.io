10 Easy Chicken Breasts Recipes for Any Night of the Week


Chicken breasts are a go-to choice for a healthy and versatile protein, making them the perfect ingredient for weeknight dinners. Whether you’re feeding a family, cooking for one, or looking for meal prep ideas, chicken breasts can be transformed into countless delicious dishes. In this blog post, we’ll walk you through 10 easy chicken breast recipes that are quick, flavorful, and guaranteed to make any night of the week special.

1. Lemon Herb Grilled Chicken Breasts

Grilled chicken breasts are a classic choice, and when paired with a refreshing lemon herb marinade, they become a mouthwatering dinner option. This recipe is perfect for summer barbecues or a simple weeknight meal.

Ingredients:

  • 2 chicken breasts, boneless and skinless
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons lemon juice
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Directions:

  1. In a bowl, combine olive oil, lemon juice, minced garlic, oregano, thyme, salt, and pepper. Whisk to combine.
  2. Place the chicken breasts in a resealable plastic bag or shallow dish and pour the marinade over them. Seal and refrigerate for at least 30 minutes (or up to 4 hours).
  3. Preheat the grill to medium-high heat.
  4. Grill the chicken for about 6-8 minutes per side or until the internal temperature reaches 165°F.
  5. Let the chicken rest for a few minutes before slicing and serving with your favorite sides.

2. Baked Parmesan Crusted Chicken Breasts

Crispy on the outside, juicy on the inside – these baked Parmesan-crusted chicken breasts make a satisfying meal without the need for frying. This recipe uses a combination of breadcrumbs and Parmesan cheese for a golden, cheesy crust.

Ingredients:

  • 2 chicken breasts, boneless and skinless
  • 1/2 cup breadcrumbs
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon dried Italian seasoning
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1 egg, beaten
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil

Directions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C). Line a baking sheet with parchment paper or lightly grease it.
  2. In a shallow bowl, combine the breadcrumbs, Parmesan cheese, garlic powder, Italian seasoning, salt, and pepper.
  3. Dip each chicken breast into the beaten egg, then coat with the breadcrumb mixture.
  4. Place the coated chicken breasts on the prepared baking sheet. Drizzle with olive oil.
  5. Bake for 20-25 minutes, or until the chicken reaches an internal temperature of 165°F and the crust is golden brown.
  6. Serve with a side of vegetables or a fresh salad.

3. Honey Mustard Chicken Breasts

Honey mustard sauce is a crowd-pleasing combination of sweet and tangy flavors, making it the perfect glaze for chicken breasts. This dish is simple to make, yet bursting with flavor.

Ingredients:

  • 2 chicken breasts, boneless and skinless
  • 3 tablespoons honey
  • 2 tablespoons Dijon mustard
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon apple cider vinegar
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Directions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. In a small bowl, whisk together honey, Dijon mustard, olive oil, apple cider vinegar, salt, and pepper.
  3. Season the chicken breasts with salt and pepper, then place them in a baking dish.
  4. Pour the honey mustard sauce over the chicken breasts, ensuring they are evenly coated.
  5. Bake for 25-30 minutes, or until the chicken reaches 165°F in the center.
  6. Serve with roasted potatoes or steamed vegetables for a complete meal.

4. Garlic Butter Chicken Breasts

Garlic butter chicken breasts are rich, flavorful, and incredibly easy to make. The buttery sauce infuses the chicken with amazing flavor, making this recipe an all-time favorite.

Ingredients:

  • 2 chicken breasts, boneless and skinless
  • 4 tablespoons butter
  • 4 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 tablespoon fresh parsley, chopped
  • 1 teaspoon lemon juice
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Directions:

  1. Heat a large skillet over medium-high heat and melt 2 tablespoons of butter.
  2. Season the chicken breasts with salt and pepper, then add them to the skillet. Cook for 6-7 minutes per side, or until the chicken reaches an internal temperature of 165°F.
  3. Remove the chicken from the skillet and set aside. In the same skillet, melt the remaining butter and sauté the minced garlic until fragrant (about 1 minute).
  4. Stir in the lemon juice and fresh parsley, and then return the chicken to the skillet. Spoon the garlic butter sauce over the chicken.
  5. Serve with mashed potatoes or rice to soak up the delicious sauce.

5. Chicken Breast Stir-Fry

Stir-fry is a quick and healthy way to prepare chicken breasts. With colorful vegetables and a savory sauce, this dish is packed with flavor and nutrition. It’s a perfect meal for busy weeknights.

Ingredients:

  • 2 chicken breasts, boneless and skinless, thinly sliced
  • 1 bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 carrot, julienned
  • 1/2 onion, sliced
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon oyster sauce
  • 1 tablespoon sesame oil
  • 1 tablespoon cornstarch
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • Cooked rice, for serving

Directions:

  1. In a small bowl, whisk together soy sauce, oyster sauce, sesame oil, and cornstarch. Set aside.
  2. Heat 1 tablespoon of vegetable oil in a large skillet or wok over medium-high heat. Add the sliced chicken and cook for 5-6 minutes, or until browned and cooked through. Remove the chicken from the skillet and set aside.
  3. In the same skillet, add the remaining tablespoon of vegetable oil. Stir-fry the bell pepper, carrot, and onion for about 3-4 minutes, or until tender-crisp.
  4. Add the minced garlic and cook for an additional 30 seconds until fragrant.
  5. Return the chicken to the skillet, pour in the sauce, and toss everything together to coat.
  6. Serve the stir-fry over cooked rice for a complete meal.

6. Chicken Parmesan

This classic Italian dish combines crispy breaded chicken with marinara sauce and melted cheese. Chicken Parmesan is always a hit at dinner parties and makes for a hearty family meal.

Ingredients:

  • 2 chicken breasts, boneless and skinless
  • 1 cup breadcrumbs
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1/2 cup marinara sauce
  • 1/2 cup mozzarella cheese, shredded
  • 1 egg, beaten
  • 1/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • Olive oil for frying

Directions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Season the chicken breasts with salt and pepper. Dredge each chicken breast in flour, dip in the beaten egg, and coat with a mixture of breadcrumbs, Parmesan cheese, and dried oregano.
  3. Heat a couple of tablespoons of olive oil in a skillet over medium heat. Fry the chicken breasts for 4-5 minutes per side until golden brown.
  4. Place the fried chicken on a baking sheet. Spoon marinara sauce over each breast and sprinkle with shredded mozzarella cheese.
  5. Bake for 10 minutes, or until the cheese is melted and bubbly.
  6. Serve with pasta or a side salad for a complete meal.

7. Crispy Chicken Tenders

Chicken tenders are a fun and easy option for family dinners, especially when you make them from scratch. This recipe delivers crispy, golden chicken tenders that everyone will love.

Ingredients:

  • 2 chicken breasts, cut into strips
  • 1 cup breadcrumbs
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1 egg, beaten
  • Olive oil for frying

Directions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C). Line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
  2. In a shallow bowl, combine the breadcrumbs, Parmesan cheese, paprika, garlic powder, salt, and pepper.
  3. Dip each chicken strip into the beaten egg, then coat in the breadcrumb mixture.
  4. Heat a couple of tablespoons of olive oil in a skillet over medium-high heat. Fry the chicken tenders for 2-3 minutes per side until golden and crispy.
  5. Transfer the tenders to the prepared baking sheet and bake for 8-10 minutes, or until the chicken reaches an internal temperature of 165°F.
  6. Serve with your favorite dipping sauces.

8. Coconut Chicken Breasts

If you’re craving something a little more exotic, try these coconut chicken breasts. The coconut adds a sweet, nutty flavor that pairs beautifully with the tender chicken.

Ingredients:

  • 2 chicken breasts, boneless and skinless
  • 1/2 cup shredded coconut
  • 1/4 cup breadcrumbs
  • 1/4 teaspoon cumin
  • 1/4 teaspoon paprika
  • 1 egg, beaten
  • 2 tablespoons coconut oil for frying

Directions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. In a shallow bowl, combine shredded coconut, breadcrumbs, cumin, and paprika.
  3. Dip each chicken breast in the beaten egg, then coat with the coconut mixture.
  4. Heat coconut oil in a skillet over medium heat. Fry the chicken for 4-5 minutes per side until golden brown.
  5. Transfer the chicken to a baking sheet and bake for an additional 10 minutes, or until the chicken reaches an internal temperature of 165°F.
  6. Serve with rice or a tropical fruit salad for a complete meal.

9. Chicken Alfredo

Creamy, comforting, and rich, chicken Alfredo is a dish that everyone loves. It’s a perfect way to enjoy chicken breasts while indulging in a luxurious, velvety sauce.

Ingredients:

  • 2 chicken breasts, boneless and skinless
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 cups heavy cream
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 8 oz fettuccine pasta
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Directions:

  1. Cook the fettuccine pasta according to package directions. Drain and set aside.
  2. Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat. Season the chicken breasts with salt and pepper, then cook for 6-7 minutes per side until golden brown and cooked through. Remove the chicken and slice it.
  3. In the same skillet, add minced garlic and sauté for 1 minute. Pour in the heavy cream and bring to a simmer.
  4. Stir in the Parmesan cheese and cook until the sauce thickens.
  5. Toss the cooked pasta with the Alfredo sauce. Top with sliced chicken and serve.

10. Spicy Cajun Chicken Breasts

For a bold, flavorful dinner, try these spicy Cajun chicken breasts. The Cajun seasoning adds heat and depth of flavor, making this a dish you’ll crave again and again.

Ingredients:

  • 2 chicken breasts, boneless and skinless
  • 1 tablespoon Cajun seasoning
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1/2 teaspoon paprika
  • 1/4 teaspoon cayenne pepper
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Directions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Rub the chicken breasts with Cajun seasoning, paprika, cayenne pepper, salt, and pepper.
  3. Heat olive oil in an oven-safe skillet over medium-high heat. Sear the chicken for 3-4 minutes per side until golden brown.
  4. Transfer the skillet to the preheated oven and bake for 15-20 minutes, or until the chicken reaches an internal temperature of 165°F.
  5. Serve with a side of roasted vegetables or over a bed of rice.

These 10 easy chicken breast recipes provide a variety of flavors and cooking methods to suit any night of the week. From savory garlic butter chicken to spicy Cajun chicken, these dishes are sure to please everyone at the dinner table. Plus, most of them are quick and simple to make, leaving you with more time to relax and enjoy your meal. Give them a try and elevate your weeknight dinners to the next level!
