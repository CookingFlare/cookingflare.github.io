Juicy and Flavorful Herb-Roasted Cornish Game Hens with Citrus Glaze Recipe for a Unique Thanksgiving Entree


Thanksgiving is a time for gathering with loved ones and indulging in delicious food. While the classic turkey may be the star of the show, why not switch things up this year with a unique and flavorful alternative? Enter the Juicy and Flavorful Herb-Roasted Cornish Game Hens with Citrus Glaze recipe. These individual game hens are packed with aromatic herbs and coated in a tangy citrus glaze, creating a mouthwatering dish that is sure to impress your guests.

Ingredients

  • 4 Cornish game hens
  • 4 sprigs of fresh rosemary
  • 4 sprigs of fresh thyme
  • 4 sprigs of fresh sage
  • 1 lemon, sliced
  • 1 orange, sliced
  • 4 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1/4 cup of olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • For the Citrus Glaze:
    • 1/2 cup of orange juice
    • 1/4 cup of lemon juice
    • 1/4 cup of honey
    • 2 tablespoons of Dijon mustard

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 425°F (220°C).

  2. Start by preparing the herb mixture. In a small bowl, combine the minced garlic, olive oil, and a pinch of salt and pepper. Mix well to form a paste.

  3. Take each Cornish game hen and gently loosen the skin from the breast and thighs. Be careful not to tear the skin.

  4. Rub the herb mixture under the skin of each game hen, spreading it evenly over the meat. This will infuse the hens with a burst of flavor and keep them moist during cooking.

  5. Stuff each game hen with a sprig of rosemary, thyme, and sage, along with a few slices of lemon and orange.

  6. Place the game hens on a wire rack set inside a roasting pan, breast-side up. This will allow the hens to cook evenly and ensure crispy skin all around.

  7. Season the hens with salt and pepper to taste.

  8. In a small saucepan, combine the orange juice, lemon juice, honey, and Dijon mustard. Bring the mixture to a simmer over medium heat, then reduce the heat to low and let it cook for about 5 minutes, until the glaze thickens slightly.

  9. Brush the glaze generously over the game hens, making sure to coat them evenly.

  10. Roast the hens in the preheated oven for about 45-50 minutes, or until the internal temperature reaches 165°F (74°C).

  11. Baste the hens with the glaze every 15 minutes to enhance the flavor and keep them moist.

  12. Once cooked, remove the game hens from the oven and let them rest for a few minutes before serving.

  13. Serve the herb-roasted Cornish game hens with the remaining glaze on the side for dipping. Garnish with fresh herbs and citrus slices for an extra touch of elegance.

Conclusion

This Juicy and Flavorful Herb-Roasted Cornish Game Hens with Citrus Glaze recipe is a delightful twist on the traditional Thanksgiving entree. The combination of aromatic herbs, tangy citrus, and succulent game hens creates a dish that is both flavorful and visually appealing. Your guests will be impressed by the unique flavors and the tender, juicy meat. So why not break away from tradition this year and bring something new and exciting to your Thanksgiving table? Give this recipe a try and let your taste buds experience a whole new level of deliciousness. Happy cooking and happy Thanksgiving!
