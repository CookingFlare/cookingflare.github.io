Classic Mojito Scones: A Refreshing and Boozy Breakfast Treat


Scones are a beloved breakfast treat that never fails to bring a smile to our faces. The classic combination of buttery goodness and a hint of sweetness is hard to resist. But what if we told you there was a way to take your scones to a whole new level? Introducing Classic Mojito Scones – a refreshing and boozy twist on a breakfast favorite.

The Perfect Blend of Refreshment and Indulgence

Imagine biting into a scone that is not only soft and crumbly but also infused with the invigorating flavors of lime and mint. The zingy citrus notes combined with the cool freshness of mint create a truly unique flavor profile that will transport you to a tropical paradise with every bite.

Ingredients You’ll Need

To get started on your Classic Mojito Scones, gather the following ingredients:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 tablespoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, cold and cubed
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • 1 large egg
  • Zest of 1 lime
  • 2 tablespoons freshly squeezed lime juice
  • 2 tablespoons fresh mint leaves, finely chopped

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 425°F (220°C). Line a baking sheet with parchment paper and set aside.

  2. In a large mixing bowl, combine the flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt. Mix well to ensure all the dry ingredients are evenly distributed.

  3. Add the cold, cubed butter to the dry ingredients. Using your fingertips or a pastry cutter, cut the butter into the flour mixture until it resembles coarse crumbs.

  4. In a separate bowl, whisk together the heavy cream, egg, lime zest, lime juice, and chopped mint leaves until well combined.

  5. Make a well in the center of the dry ingredients and pour in the wet mixture. Gently stir until the dough comes together, being careful not to overmix.

  6. Transfer the dough onto a lightly floured surface and shape it into a circle, about 1 inch thick. Using a sharp knife or a scone cutter, cut the dough into 8 wedges.

  7. Place the scones on the prepared baking sheet, leaving a little space between each one. Brush the tops with a little extra heavy cream for a golden finish.

  8. Bake in the preheated oven for 12-15 minutes or until the scones are lightly golden brown on top.

  9. Remove from the oven and allow the scones to cool on a wire rack for a few minutes. Serve warm or at room temperature.

Serving Suggestions

Classic Mojito Scones are delicious on their own, but they can be taken to the next level with a few simple additions. Serve them with a dollop of freshly whipped cream and a sprig of fresh mint for an extra touch of elegance. Or, if you’re feeling adventurous, pair them with a mojito-inspired glaze made with powdered sugar, lime juice, and a splash of rum.

The Perfect Start to Your Day

Whether you’re looking to indulge in a boozy breakfast treat or simply want to add a refreshing twist to your morning routine, Classic Mojito Scones are the answer. Their bright flavors and buttery texture make them a delightful way to start your day. So why not give this recipe a try and treat yourself to a taste of paradise?

Remember, moderation is key when it comes to enjoying boozy treats. Enjoy responsibly and savor each bite of these delightful Classic Mojito Scones. Cheers to a breakfast that’s both refreshing and indulgent!
