The Best Gluten-Free Bread Recipe: Soft and Tasty


Are you tired of dense, dry gluten-free bread that tastes more like cardboard than a delicious homemade loaf? Look no further! This gluten-free bread recipe will exceed your expectations with its soft, fluffy texture and incredible flavor. Whether you have celiac disease, a gluten sensitivity, or simply want to explore gluten-free baking, this recipe is a game-changer. Let’s dive into the details of creating the best gluten-free bread you’ve ever tasted.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups gluten-free all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup almond flour
  • 1/2 cup tapioca flour
  • 1/4 cup ground flaxseed
  • 1 tablespoon active dry yeast
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon xanthan gum
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 tablespoons honey
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon apple cider vinegar
  • 1 1/2 cups warm water (110°F)

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Yeast: In a small bowl, combine the warm water and honey. Sprinkle the yeast over the mixture and let it sit for 5-10 minutes, or until foamy.

  2. Mix the Dry Ingredients: In a large mixing bowl, whisk together the gluten-free all-purpose flour, almond flour, tapioca flour, ground flaxseed, salt, and xanthan gum.

  3. Combine Wet Ingredients: In another bowl, whisk together the eggs, olive oil, and apple cider vinegar. Add the proofed yeast mixture and stir to combine.

  4. Mix the Dough: Pour the wet ingredients into the bowl of dry ingredients. Use a stand mixer or hand mixer to beat the dough at medium speed for 3-4 minutes, scraping down the sides of the bowl as needed. The dough should be smooth and slightly sticky.

  5. Let the Dough Rise: Transfer the dough to a greased loaf pan, cover it with a clean kitchen towel, and let it rise in a warm place for 45-60 minutes, or until it has doubled in size.

  6. Bake the Bread: Preheat the oven to 350°F. Once the dough has risen, place the loaf pan in the oven and bake for 35-40 minutes, or until the bread is golden brown and sounds hollow when tapped on the bottom.

  7. Cool and Enjoy: Remove the bread from the oven and let it cool in the pan for 10 minutes. Then transfer the loaf to a wire rack to cool completely before slicing. Enjoy your delicious, soft gluten-free bread with your favorite toppings!

Tips for Success

  • Use warm water to activate the yeast, but ensure it’s not too hot to avoid killing the yeast.
  • For the best texture, allow the dough to rise until it has visibly doubled in size before baking.
  • Experiment with adding seeds or nuts to the dough for extra flavor and nutrition.
  • Store the bread in an airtight container or resealable bag to maintain its freshness.

Final Thoughts

With this gluten-free bread recipe, you can say goodbye to disappointing store-bought loaves and embrace the joy of homemade bread. Whether you’re making sandwiches, enjoying toast with your morning coffee, or simply savoring a warm slice with butter, this bread will elevate your gluten-free baking experience. Embrace the art of gluten-free bread making and savor the soft, tasty results of this delightful recipe. Happy baking!
