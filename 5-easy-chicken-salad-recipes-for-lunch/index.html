5 Easy Chicken Salad Recipes for Lunch


Chicken salad is one of those versatile dishes that can be made in countless ways, offering a delicious and healthy option for lunch. Whether you’re looking for something light, hearty, or creamy, there’s a chicken salad recipe that fits every taste preference. Here are five easy and flavorful chicken salad recipes to keep your lunches exciting and nutritious.

1. Classic Chicken Salad with Mayo

A timeless favorite, the classic chicken salad is a perfect combination of tender chicken, crunchy vegetables, and creamy mayo. This recipe is simple to make and incredibly satisfying.

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups cooked, shredded chicken (rotisserie chicken works great)
  • 1/2 cup mayonnaise
  • 1 tablespoon Dijon mustard
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 1 celery stalk, finely chopped
  • 1/4 cup red onion, finely chopped
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 teaspoon dried dill (optional)

Instructions:

  1. In a large mixing bowl, combine the shredded chicken, celery, and red onion.
  2. In a separate bowl, whisk together the mayonnaise, Dijon mustard, lemon juice, and dried dill if using.
  3. Pour the dressing over the chicken mixture and stir until everything is evenly coated.
  4. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  5. Chill for at least 30 minutes before serving to let the flavors meld together.

This classic version is perfect for those who love creamy textures and fresh ingredients. Serve it in a sandwich, on a bed of greens, or with crackers for a light meal.

2. Avocado Chicken Salad

For a fresh and creamy twist on the traditional recipe, avocado chicken salad is an excellent option. The creamy texture of the avocado replaces mayonnaise, making this version a healthier and equally satisfying alternative.

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups cooked, shredded chicken
  • 1 ripe avocado, mashed
  • 1/4 cup Greek yogurt
  • 1 tablespoon lime juice
  • 1/4 cup red onion, finely chopped
  • 1/4 cup cilantro, chopped
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, combine the shredded chicken, red onion, and cilantro.
  2. In a separate bowl, mash the avocado and mix it with the Greek yogurt and lime juice until smooth.
  3. Add the avocado mixture to the chicken and stir until well combined.
  4. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  5. Chill for at least 30 minutes before serving to allow the flavors to meld.

This avocado chicken salad is packed with healthy fats and is perfect for a light, protein-packed meal. Serve it in a lettuce wrap, on whole-grain bread, or alongside a fresh salad for a satisfying lunch.

3. Curried Chicken Salad

If you love bold flavors, a curried chicken salad will spice up your lunch routine. The addition of curry powder gives the salad a warm, aromatic flavor that pairs wonderfully with the sweetness of dried fruits like raisins or cranberries.

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups cooked, shredded chicken
  • 1/2 cup plain Greek yogurt
  • 1 tablespoon mayonnaise
  • 1 tablespoon curry powder
  • 1 tablespoon honey
  • 1/4 cup raisins or dried cranberries
  • 1/4 cup chopped almonds or cashews
  • 1/4 cup celery, chopped
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a large bowl, mix the shredded chicken, raisins or cranberries, celery, and chopped nuts.
  2. In a separate bowl, whisk together the Greek yogurt, mayonnaise, curry powder, and honey.
  3. Pour the dressing over the chicken mixture and stir to combine.
  4. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  5. Chill in the fridge for at least 30 minutes to allow the flavors to develop.

This curried chicken salad offers a delightful combination of sweet, savory, and spicy flavors. It’s perfect for serving as a sandwich filling or as a topping for a mixed greens salad.

4. Mediterranean Chicken Salad

For a fresh and flavorful chicken salad inspired by Mediterranean cuisine, this recipe incorporates ingredients like feta cheese, olives, and cucumbers. The dressing is light yet zesty, making it a refreshing option for a hot day.

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups cooked, shredded chicken
  • 1/4 cup Kalamata olives, pitted and chopped
  • 1/4 cup crumbled feta cheese
  • 1/2 cup cucumber, diced
  • 1/4 cup red onion, thinly sliced
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon red wine vinegar
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a large bowl, combine the shredded chicken, olives, feta, cucumber, and red onion.
  2. In a small bowl, whisk together the olive oil, red wine vinegar, dried oregano, salt, and pepper.
  3. Pour the dressing over the chicken mixture and toss gently to combine.
  4. Chill for 30 minutes before serving.

This Mediterranean chicken salad is perfect for those who enjoy bright, tangy flavors with a bit of Mediterranean flair. Serve it in a pita pocket, over a bed of greens, or as a side dish to grilled meats.

5. Waldorf Chicken Salad

The Waldorf chicken salad is a fruity, nutty variation that adds a touch of sweetness and crunch to the mix. With ingredients like apples, grapes, and walnuts, this chicken salad is as nutritious as it is delicious.

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups cooked, shredded chicken
  • 1 apple, diced (any variety you prefer)
  • 1/2 cup red grapes, halved
  • 1/4 cup walnuts, chopped
  • 1/2 cup Greek yogurt
  • 1 tablespoon honey
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a large bowl, combine the shredded chicken, diced apple, grapes, and walnuts.
  2. In a small bowl, whisk together the Greek yogurt, honey, and lemon juice until smooth.
  3. Pour the dressing over the chicken mixture and stir gently to combine.
  4. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  5. Chill for 30 minutes before serving to allow the flavors to meld.

This sweet and crunchy Waldorf chicken salad is an excellent choice for those who enjoy a mix of fruit and savory flavors. It pairs beautifully with whole-grain crackers or can be enjoyed as a stand-alone dish.

Tips for Making the Perfect Chicken Salad

No matter which chicken salad recipe you choose, here are some general tips to elevate your salad-making game:

  • Use Cooked, Shredded Chicken: The texture of shredded chicken provides a nice balance to the creamy dressing. You can use leftover chicken or purchase pre-cooked rotisserie chicken for a quick and easy option.
  • Add Fresh Herbs: Fresh herbs like parsley, cilantro, or dill can brighten up the flavors of your chicken salad and add a fresh, aromatic touch.
  • Customize Your Dressing: If you prefer a lighter dressing, try substituting the mayonnaise or Greek yogurt with a vinaigrette or olive oil-based dressing for a healthier option.
  • Experiment with Add-ins: Feel free to mix in additional ingredients like avocado, roasted vegetables, or nuts to create your own unique version of chicken salad.

Conclusion

Chicken salad is a versatile dish that can be tailored to suit your tastes, dietary preferences, and available ingredients. Whether you prefer the classic creamy version, a fresh and zesty avocado salad, or a bold curried option, these five recipes are easy to make and sure to satisfy. Try them all and find your favorite!

From simple weekday lunches to picnics and potlucks, chicken salad is an ideal meal for busy days. Make ahead, store in the fridge, and enjoy a delicious, protein-packed meal all week long.
