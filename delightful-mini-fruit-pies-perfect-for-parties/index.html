Delightful Mini Fruit Pies: Perfect for Parties


Are you looking for a sweet treat that’s perfect for parties? Look no further than these delightful mini fruit pies! They are not only delicious but also easy to make, making them a great choice for any occasion. Whether you’re hosting a birthday party, a bridal shower, or just having friends over for dessert, these mini fruit pies are sure to impress your guests. So, let’s get baking!

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup unsalted butter, chilled and cut into small cubes
  • 1/4 cup ice water
  • Assorted fresh fruits of your choice (such as strawberries, blueberries, raspberries, and peaches)
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 tablespoon cornstarch
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Instructions

  1. In a large bowl, combine the flour and salt. Add the chilled butter cubes and use a pastry cutter or your fingertips to work the butter into the flour until the mixture resembles coarse crumbs.

  2. Slowly add the ice water, a tablespoon at a time, and mix until the dough comes together. Be careful not to overmix. Divide the dough into two equal portions, shape each into a disk, and wrap them in plastic wrap. Refrigerate for at least 1 hour.

  3. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).

  4. In a separate bowl, combine the granulated sugar, cornstarch, lemon juice, and vanilla extract. Mix well until the sugar has dissolved and the mixture is smooth.

  5. Roll out one portion of the chilled dough on a lightly floured surface to a thickness of about 1/8 inch. Use a round cookie cutter or a glass to cut out circles of dough, approximately 3 inches in diameter. Repeat with the remaining dough.

  6. Place the dough circles onto a baking sheet lined with parchment paper. Spoon a small amount of the sugar mixture onto the center of each circle, leaving a border around the edges.

  7. Wash and prepare your fresh fruits. Cut larger fruits into smaller pieces if necessary. Arrange the fruit pieces on top of the sugar mixture, keeping them within the borders of the dough circles.

  8. Fold the edges of the dough circles over the fruit, creating a rustic-looking crust. Press lightly to seal the edges.

  9. Bake the mini fruit pies in the preheated oven for 20-25 minutes, or until the crust is golden brown and the fruit is bubbly.

  10. Remove from the oven and let the pies cool for a few minutes before serving. These mini fruit pies are best enjoyed warm, but they can also be served at room temperature.

  11. Serve your delightful mini fruit pies as is or with a scoop of vanilla ice cream or a dollop of freshly whipped cream. Enjoy!

Conclusion

These delightful mini fruit pies are not only visually appealing but also bursting with the fresh flavors of your favorite fruits. They are the perfect bite-sized treat for any party or gathering. With a buttery crust and a sweet, juicy fruit filling, these mini pies are sure to be a hit with your guests. So, why wait? Gather your ingredients, roll up your sleeves, and start baking these amazing mini fruit pies today. Your taste buds will thank you!
