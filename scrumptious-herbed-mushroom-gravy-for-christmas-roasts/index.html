Scrumptious Herbed Mushroom Gravy for Christmas Roasts


Are you looking for the perfect gravy to complement your Christmas roast? Look no further than this Scrumptious Herbed Mushroom Gravy recipe! Bursting with rich flavors and aromatic herbs, this gravy will take your holiday meal to the next level. Whether you’re serving turkey, ham, or beef, this versatile gravy is sure to impress your guests. So, let’s dive right into the recipe!

Ingredients

  • 1 pound fresh mushrooms, sliced
  • 4 tablespoons unsalted butter
  • 1/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • 3 cups vegetable or chicken broth
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 2 tablespoons fresh parsley, chopped
  • 1 tablespoon fresh thyme leaves
  • 1 teaspoon dried rosemary
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. In a large skillet, melt the butter over medium heat. Add the sliced mushrooms and sauté until they release their moisture and turn golden brown, about 8-10 minutes. Remove half of the mushrooms and set aside for later.

  2. Sprinkle the flour over the remaining mushrooms in the skillet and stir well to coat. Cook for an additional 2 minutes to cook off the raw flour taste.

  3. Slowly pour in the broth, whisking constantly to prevent lumps from forming. Bring the mixture to a simmer and cook for 5 minutes, or until the gravy thickens.

  4. Add the heavy cream, reserved mushrooms, parsley, thyme, and rosemary to the skillet. Stir well to combine. Season with salt and pepper to taste.

  5. Reduce the heat to low and let the gravy simmer for an additional 5 minutes, allowing the flavors to meld together.

  6. Remove from heat and let the gravy cool slightly before serving. The gravy will thicken further as it cools.

  7. Serve this Scrumptious Herbed Mushroom Gravy alongside your Christmas roast for a truly unforgettable meal. The earthy flavors of the mushrooms combined with the aromatic herbs create a delightful harmony that will have your guests coming back for seconds.

Tips and Variations

  • For an extra depth of flavor, you can add a splash of red or white wine to the gravy before adding the broth.
  • If you prefer a smoother gravy, you can blend the cooked mushrooms with an immersion blender or in a regular blender before adding them back to the skillet.
  • Feel free to adjust the herbs according to your taste preferences. Fresh rosemary or thyme sprigs can also be added for a visual appeal.
  • This gravy can be made in advance and reheated before serving. Simply store it in an airtight container in the refrigerator for up to 3 days.
  • If you have any leftovers, this Scrumptious Herbed Mushroom Gravy makes a fantastic accompaniment to sandwiches, mashed potatoes, or even pasta dishes.

Final Thoughts

There you have it - a recipe for Scrumptious Herbed Mushroom Gravy that will elevate your Christmas roast to new heights. The combination of sautéed mushrooms, aromatic herbs, and creamy texture will leave your taste buds singing with joy. Don’t forget to pour this gravy generously over your roast and savor each delicious bite. Your guests will be impressed, and your Christmas feast will be one to remember. Happy cooking and happy holidays!
