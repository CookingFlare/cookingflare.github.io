How to Make Korean Spicy Pork Belly (Samgyeopsal) at Home


Korean cuisine is renowned for its bold flavors, vibrant colors, and unique cooking techniques. One dish that stands out in the realm of Korean barbecue is Samgyeopsal, or Korean spicy pork belly. This dish is not only a favorite among locals but has also gained immense popularity worldwide. The combination of tender, juicy pork belly marinated in a spicy sauce, grilled to perfection, and served with an array of side dishes makes it a delightful culinary experience. In this blog post, we will guide you through the process of making Korean spicy pork belly at home, ensuring that you can enjoy this delicious dish anytime you crave it.

Ingredients

To make authentic Korean spicy pork belly, you will need the following ingredients:

For the Pork Belly Marinade:

  • 1 pound (450g) pork belly, sliced into 1/4 inch thick pieces
  • 3 tablespoons gochujang (Korean red chili paste)
  • 2 tablespoons gochugaru (Korean red chili flakes)
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon sesame oil
  • 1 tablespoon honey or sugar
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1-inch piece of ginger, grated
  • 1 tablespoon rice vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon sesame seeds
  • 1 green onion, finely chopped
  • Salt and pepper to taste

For Grilling:

  • Lettuce leaves (for wrapping)
  • Sliced garlic
  • Sliced green chili peppers
  • Sliced onions
  • Cooked rice (for serving)
  • Ssamjang (Korean dipping sauce)

Equipment Needed

  • Grill or grill pan
  • Mixing bowl
  • Tongs
  • Knife and cutting board
  • Serving plates

Step-by-Step Instructions

Step 1: Prepare the Marinade

  1. Combine Ingredients: In a mixing bowl, combine gochujang, gochugaru, soy sauce, sesame oil, honey (or sugar), minced garlic, grated ginger, rice vinegar, sesame seeds, and chopped green onion. Mix well until all ingredients are thoroughly combined.

  2. Season the Marinade: Taste the marinade and adjust the seasoning with salt and pepper according to your preference. The marinade should have a balance of spicy, sweet, and savory flavors.

Step 2: Marinate the Pork Belly

  1. Add Pork Belly: Place the sliced pork belly into the bowl with the marinade. Ensure that each piece is well-coated with the marinade.

  2. Marination Time: Cover the bowl with plastic wrap and let the pork belly marinate in the refrigerator for at least 30 minutes. For best results, marinate for 2-4 hours or overnight to allow the flavors to penetrate the meat.

Step 3: Prepare the Grill

  1. Preheat the Grill: If you are using an outdoor grill, preheat it to medium-high heat. For an indoor grill pan, heat it over medium-high heat until hot.

  2. Oil the Grill: Lightly oil the grill grates or the grill pan to prevent the pork belly from sticking.

Step 4: Grill the Pork Belly

  1. Grill the Pork: Once the grill is hot, place the marinated pork belly slices on the grill. Cook for about 3-4 minutes on each side, or until the pork is nicely charred and cooked through. The fat should render, creating a crispy texture.

  2. Add Aromatics: During the last minute of grilling, add sliced garlic, green chili peppers, and onions to the grill. This will infuse additional flavors into the dish.

Step 5: Serve and Enjoy

  1. Prepare the Table: While the pork belly is grilling, prepare your serving area with lettuce leaves, cooked rice, and ssamjang.

  2. Assemble the Wraps: Once the pork belly is cooked, remove it from the grill and let it rest for a minute. To eat, take a lettuce leaf, place a piece of grilled pork belly on top, add some grilled garlic and onions, and a dollop of ssamjang. Wrap it up and enjoy!

  3. Pair with Sides: Samgyeopsal is often served with various side dishes (banchan) such as kimchi, pickled radish, and steamed vegetables. These sides complement the rich flavors of the pork belly and enhance the overall dining experience.

Tips for the Perfect Samgyeopsal

  • Quality of Meat: Choose high-quality pork belly with a good balance of meat and fat. This will ensure a juicy and flavorful result.
  • Marination Time: The longer you marinate the pork belly, the more flavorful it will be. If you have time, marinate overnight for the best results.
  • Grilling Technique: Avoid overcrowding the grill.
