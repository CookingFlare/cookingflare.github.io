Sous Vide Garlic Parmesan Green Beans: A Tasty and Healthy Side


Green beans are a versatile and nutritious vegetable that can be prepared in a variety of ways. One delicious and healthy option is to cook them using the sous vide method, which ensures they retain their vibrant color, crisp texture, and incredible flavor. In this recipe, we will be infusing the green beans with garlic and Parmesan cheese, elevating them to a whole new level of deliciousness. This dish makes for a perfect side that pairs well with a wide range of main courses.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound fresh green beans, ends trimmed
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Preheat your sous vide water bath to 185°F (85°C).

  2. In a large bowl, toss the green beans with minced garlic, olive oil, Parmesan cheese, salt, and pepper. Ensure that the green beans are evenly coated with the garlic and Parmesan mixture.

  3. Transfer the seasoned green beans into a vacuum-sealable bag. Seal the bag using a vacuum sealer or the water displacement method.

  4. Immerse the sealed bag of green beans into the preheated water bath, ensuring that the beans are fully submerged. Cook for 1 hour to allow the flavors to meld together and the green beans to become tender yet still crisp.

  5. Once the cooking time is complete, carefully remove the bag from the water bath and transfer the green beans to a serving dish.

  6. Optional: For an added touch of flavor and presentation, you can quickly sear the cooked green beans in a hot skillet with a little olive oil until they develop a slight char.

  7. Serve the sous vide garlic Parmesan green beans immediately as a delicious and healthy side dish that will surely impress your guests.

Tips and Variations

  • If you prefer your green beans to have a softer texture, increase the cooking time in the sous vide water bath by 15-30 minutes.

  • Experiment with different seasonings to customize the flavors. Consider adding a pinch of red pepper flakes for some heat or a squeeze of lemon juice for brightness.

  • Feel free to adjust the amount of garlic and Parmesan cheese to suit your taste preferences. You can also sprinkle some additional grated Parmesan cheese on top of the cooked green beans before serving.

  • This recipe can easily be doubled or halved to accommodate different serving sizes.

  • Leftover sous vide garlic Parmesan green beans can be stored in an airtight container in the refrigerator for up to 3 days. They can be enjoyed cold as a refreshing snack or reheated in the microwave or on the stovetop.

Conclusion

Sous vide garlic Parmesan green beans offer a flavorful and nutritious twist on a classic side dish. The sous vide method ensures that the green beans are perfectly cooked, retaining their vibrant color and crisp texture. The garlic and Parmesan cheese add a delightful savory note that complements the natural sweetness of the green beans. Whether you’re looking for a healthy side dish for a family dinner or an impressive addition to your next dinner party, these sous vide green beans are sure to please. Give this recipe a try and discover a new favorite way to enjoy this versatile vegetable!
