Fluffy and Flavorful Waffle Recipes for a Lazy Sunday Morning


Sunday mornings are made for relaxation, and what better way to start the day than with a delicious and satisfying breakfast? Waffles are the perfect treat to enjoy on a lazy Sunday morning, and with these fluffy and flavorful recipes, you’ll be in for a real treat.

Classic Buttermilk Waffle Recipe

For a traditional waffle flavor, you can’t go wrong with a classic buttermilk recipe. This recipe yields a light and airy waffle with a subtle tang from the buttermilk.

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 4 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 cup buttermilk
  • 4 tablespoons unsalted butter, melted

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your waffle iron according to the manufacturer’s instructions.
  2. In a large bowl, whisk together the flour, baking powder, salt, and sugar.
  3. In a separate bowl, whisk together the eggs, buttermilk, and melted butter.
  4. Pour the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients and stir until just combined. Do not overmix.
  5. The batter should still be slightly lumpy.
  6. Pour approximately 1/4 cup of batter onto the center of the waffle iron and spread it evenly to the edges.
  7. Cook for 3-5 minutes, or until the waffles are golden brown and crispy.
  8. Repeat with the remaining batter, greasing the waffle iron with cooking spray as needed.

Blueberry Waffle Recipe with Fresh Berries

Add a burst of fresh flavor to your Sunday morning with these blueberry waffles. The sweetness of the blueberries pairs perfectly with the crispy waffle exterior.

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 4 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 cup buttermilk
  • 4 tablespoons unsalted butter, melted
  • 1 cup fresh or frozen blueberries

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your waffle iron according to the manufacturer’s instructions.
  2. In a large bowl, whisk together the flour, baking powder, salt, and sugar.
  3. In a separate bowl, whisk together the eggs, buttermilk, and melted butter.
  4. Pour the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients and stir until just combined. Do not overmix.
  5. Gently fold in the blueberries.
  6. Pour approximately 1/4 cup of batter onto the center of the waffle iron and spread it evenly to the edges.
  7. Cook for 3-5 minutes, or until the waffles are golden brown and crispy.
  8. Repeat with the remaining batter, greasing the waffle iron with cooking spray as needed.

Chocolate Chip Waffle Recipe with a Crunchy Twist

For a sweet and indulgent treat, try these chocolate chip waffles with a crunchy twist. The chopped nuts add a delightful texture to the crispy waffles.

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 4 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 cup buttermilk
  • 4 tablespoons unsalted butter, melted
  • 1 cup semi-sweet chocolate chips
  • 1/2 cup chopped walnuts

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your waffle iron according to the manufacturer’s instructions.
  2. In a large bowl, whisk together the flour, baking powder, salt, and sugar.
  3. In a separate bowl, whisk together the eggs, buttermilk, and melted butter.
  4. Pour the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients and stir until just combined. Do not overmix.
  5. Fold in the chocolate chips and chopped walnuts.
  6. Pour approximately 1/4 cup of batter onto the center of the waffle iron and spread it evenly to the edges.
  7. Cook for 3-5 minutes, or until the waffles are golden brown and crispy.
  8. Repeat with the remaining batter, greasing the waffle iron with cooking spray as needed.

Tips and Variations

  • For a crisper waffle, cook the batter for an additional minute or until the waffles are darker golden brown.
  • Experiment with different flavors by adding a teaspoon of vanilla extract, cinnamon, or nutmeg to the batter.
  • Try using Greek yogurt or sour cream instead of buttermilk for a tangier flavor.
  • Add a sprinkle of powdered sugar or a drizzle of honey to the waffles for an extra touch of sweetness.

With these fluffy and flavorful waffle recipes, you’ll be well on your way to creating a delicious and satisfying breakfast for your lazy Sunday morning. Whether you’re in the mood for something classic and traditional or something new and exciting, there’s a waffle recipe here for you. So go ahead, get cooking, and start your day off right with a warm and crispy waffle!
