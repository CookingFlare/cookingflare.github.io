Spiced Pumpkin Bread: A Fall Favorite for Breakfast or Dessert


Fall is in the air, and that means it’s time to indulge in all things pumpkin! Whether you’re looking for a delicious breakfast treat or a delectable dessert, Spiced Pumpkin Bread is the perfect choice. This moist and flavorful bread is packed with warm spices and the comforting taste of pumpkin, making it a fall favorite for all.

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground nutmeg
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground ginger
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground cloves
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/2 cup brown sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 cup pumpkin puree

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C) and grease a 9x5-inch loaf pan.

  2. In a medium bowl, whisk together the flour, baking soda, baking powder, salt, cinnamon, nutmeg, ginger, and cloves. Set aside.

  3. In a large bowl, cream together the softened butter, granulated sugar, and brown sugar until light and fluffy. This can be done using an electric mixer or by hand.

  4. Add the eggs one at a time, beating well after each addition. Stir in the vanilla extract.

  5. Gradually add the dry ingredients to the wet ingredients, alternating with the pumpkin puree. Begin and end with the dry ingredients, mixing until just combined. Be careful not to overmix, as this can result in a dense bread.

  6. Pour the batter into the prepared loaf pan and smooth the top with a spatula.

  7. Bake in the preheated oven for 60-70 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean. If the top of the bread starts to brown too quickly, you can cover it loosely with aluminum foil.

  8. Once baked, remove the loaf from the oven and let it cool in the pan for 10 minutes. Then transfer it to a wire rack to cool completely before slicing.

Serving Suggestions:

  • Enjoy a slice of Spiced Pumpkin Bread for breakfast with a hot cup of coffee or tea. The warm, fragrant spices will kickstart your day with a cozy fall vibe.

  • For a delightful dessert, serve a slice of this delicious bread with a dollop of whipped cream or a scoop of vanilla ice cream. The creamy sweetness complements the spiced pumpkin flavor perfectly.

Storage Tips:

  • If you have any leftovers (which is rare!), store them in an airtight container at room temperature for up to 3 days. You can also freeze individual slices for longer storage. Just make sure to wrap them tightly in plastic wrap or place them in a freezer bag.

Final Thoughts:

Spiced Pumpkin Bread is a versatile and scrumptious treat that can be enjoyed for breakfast or dessert. Its rich pumpkin flavor and warm spices capture the essence of fall in every bite. Whether you’re baking it for a cozy morning at home or a gathering with friends, this recipe is sure to become a fall favorite in your household. So go ahead, embrace the flavors of the season, and indulge in the delightful goodness of Spiced Pumpkin Bread!
