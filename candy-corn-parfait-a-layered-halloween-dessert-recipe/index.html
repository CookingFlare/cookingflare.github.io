Candy Corn Parfait: A Layered Halloween Dessert Recipe


Halloween is just around the corner, and what better way to celebrate than with a fun and festive dessert? Enter the Candy Corn Parfait – a visually stunning and delicious treat that’s perfect for any Halloween gathering. This layered dessert is not only eye-catching but also incredibly easy to make, making it a hit with both kids and adults alike. So, let’s dive into the recipe and learn how to create this show-stopping Halloween dessert!

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup of orange gelatin
  • 1 cup of pineapple gelatin
  • 1 cup of whipped cream
  • 1/2 cup of candy corn
  • 1/2 cup of vanilla yogurt
  • 1/4 cup of sugar
  • 1/4 cup of water
  • 1 teaspoon of vanilla extract

Instructions:

Step 1: Prepare the Gelatin Layers

  1. In a small bowl, prepare the orange gelatin according to the package instructions. Pour a thin layer of the orange gelatin into the bottom of each parfait glass.

  2. Place the glasses in the refrigerator for about 15-20 minutes or until the gelatin is set.

  3. Once the orange gelatin has set, prepare the pineapple gelatin in another small bowl. Carefully pour the pineapple gelatin over the set orange gelatin layer in each glass.

  4. Place the glasses back in the refrigerator to allow the pineapple gelatin layer to set.

Step 2: Whip the Cream

  1. While the gelatin layers are setting, take a mixing bowl and whip the cream until it forms stiff peaks.

  2. Once the cream is whipped, set it aside in the refrigerator.

Step 3: Prepare the Candy Corn Topping

  1. In a small saucepan, combine the sugar and water. Heat the mixture over medium heat, stirring constantly, until the sugar has completely dissolved.

  2. Remove the saucepan from the heat and stir in the vanilla extract.

  3. Add the candy corn to the saucepan and stir until the candy corn is evenly coated with the sugar mixture.

  4. Allow the candy corn to cool and harden on a parchment-lined baking sheet.

Step 4: Assemble the Parfaits

  1. Once the gelatin layers have set, remove the glasses from the refrigerator.

  2. Carefully spoon a layer of whipped cream on top of the set gelatin layers in each glass.

  3. Sprinkle a generous amount of the candy corn topping over the whipped cream layer.

  4. Finish off the parfaits with a dollop of vanilla yogurt on top of the candy corn.

  5. Garnish with a few pieces of candy corn for an extra festive touch.

Step 5: Serve and Enjoy!

  1. Chill the parfaits in the refrigerator until ready to serve.

  2. When it’s time to serve, bring out the parfaits and watch as your guests marvel at the colorful layers and delightful presentation.

  3. Enjoy this sweet and festive treat with your loved ones and celebrate the Halloween season in style!

Conclusion

The Candy Corn Parfait is a delightful and visually appealing dessert that captures the spirit of Halloween. With its vibrant layers and playful presentation, it’s sure to be a hit at any Halloween party or gathering. This easy-to-make recipe allows you to impress your guests with minimal effort, making it a perfect choice for busy hosts. So, this Halloween, treat yourself and your loved ones to this delicious and fun Candy Corn Parfait!
