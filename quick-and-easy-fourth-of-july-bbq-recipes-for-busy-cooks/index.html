Quick and Easy Fourth of July BBQ Recipes for Busy Cooks


Are you a busy cook looking for quick and easy recipes to spice up your Fourth of July BBQ? Look no further! We’ve got you covered with a selection of mouthwatering dishes that will impress your guests without keeping you stuck in the kitchen all day. From grilled meats to refreshing salads, these recipes will make your Fourth of July celebration a hit!

Grilled Citrus Chicken Skewers

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound chicken breast, cut into cubes
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • Juice of 1 lemon
  • Juice of 1 lime
  • Zest of 1 orange
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Wooden skewers, soaked in water for 30 minutes

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your grill to medium-high heat.
  2. In a bowl, combine the olive oil, lemon juice, lime juice, orange zest, minced garlic, salt, and pepper. Mix well.
  3. Add the chicken cubes to the marinade and toss until evenly coated. Let it marinate for at least 30 minutes.
  4. Thread the chicken onto the soaked skewers.
  5. Grill the skewers for about 8-10 minutes, turning occasionally, until the chicken is cooked through and slightly charred.
  6. Serve hot and garnish with fresh herbs and a squeeze of lemon juice.

Grilled Vegetable Kabobs

Ingredients:

  • 1 red bell pepper, cut into chunks
  • 1 yellow bell pepper, cut into chunks
  • 1 zucchini, sliced into rounds
  • 1 red onion, cut into wedges
  • 8-10 cherry tomatoes
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons balsamic vinegar
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your grill to medium heat.
  2. In a large bowl, combine the olive oil, balsamic vinegar, dried oregano, salt, and pepper. Mix well.
  3. Add the vegetables to the marinade and toss until evenly coated. Let them marinate for about 15-20 minutes.
  4. Thread the vegetables onto skewers, alternating between different colors for an appealing presentation.
  5. Grill the vegetable skewers for about 10-12 minutes, turning occasionally, until they are tender and lightly charred.
  6. Serve hot and drizzle with any remaining marinade for added flavor.

Watermelon and Feta Salad

Ingredients:

  • 4 cups cubed watermelon
  • 1 cup crumbled feta cheese
  • 1/4 cup fresh mint leaves, torn
  • 1 tablespoon extra-virgin olive oil
  • Juice of 1 lime
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a large bowl, combine the watermelon cubes, crumbled feta cheese, and torn mint leaves.
  2. Drizzle the olive oil and lime juice over the salad.
  3. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  4. Gently toss all the ingredients together until well combined.
  5. Let the salad sit for a few minutes to allow the flavors to meld together.
  6. Serve chilled and enjoy the refreshing combination of sweet watermelon, tangy feta cheese, and fresh mint.

Grilled Pineapple with Cinnamon

Ingredients:

  • 1 ripe pineapple, peeled and cored
  • 1 tablespoon brown sugar
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • Vanilla ice cream (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your grill to medium-high heat.
  2. Cut the pineapple into rings, about 1/2-inch thick.
  3. In a small bowl, combine the brown sugar and ground cinnamon.
  4. Sprinkle the cinnamon sugar mixture over the pineapple rings, coating both sides.
  5. Grill the pineapple rings for about 2-3 minutes per side, until they are caramelized and slightly charred.
  6. Serve the grilled pineapple as is or with a scoop of vanilla ice cream for a delightful dessert.

These quick and easy Fourth of July BBQ recipes will surely make your gathering a memorable one. With minimal effort and maximum flavor, you can spend more time enjoying the festivities and less time in the kitchen. So fire up the grill, gather your loved ones, and celebrate the Fourth of July with these delicious dishes. Happy Independence Day!
