Unique Fourth of July BBQ Pizza Recipes for a Memorable Celebration


Are you looking to add a twist to your Fourth of July barbecue this year? Look no further than these unique BBQ pizza recipes that are sure to impress your guests and make your celebration truly memorable. From smoky flavors to patriotic toppings, these recipes will take your pizza game to the next level. So fire up your grill and get ready to indulge in these delicious creations!

1. Grilled BBQ Chicken Pizza

Ingredients:

  • 1 store-bought pizza dough
  • 1 cup cooked and shredded chicken
  • 1/2 cup barbecue sauce
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/4 cup red onion, thinly sliced
  • 1/4 cup fresh cilantro, chopped
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your grill to medium-high heat.
  2. Roll out the pizza dough into your desired shape and thickness.
  3. Place the dough directly on the grill grates and cook for about 2-3 minutes on each side, or until lightly charred.
  4. Remove the dough from the grill and set aside.
  5. In a bowl, mix the shredded chicken with barbecue sauce until well coated.
  6. Spread the barbecue sauce-coated chicken evenly over the grilled pizza dough.
  7. Sprinkle the mozzarella cheese, red onion, and cilantro on top.
  8. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  9. Carefully place the topped pizza back on the grill and cook for an additional 5-7 minutes, or until the cheese is melted and bubbly.
  10. Remove from the grill, let it cool for a few minutes, and then slice and serve.

2. Smoked Pulled Pork Pizza

Ingredients:

  • 1 store-bought pizza dough
  • 1 cup smoked pulled pork
  • 1/2 cup barbecue sauce
  • 1 cup shredded smoked Gouda cheese
  • 1/4 cup pickled jalapenos, sliced
  • 1/4 cup red onion, thinly sliced
  • Fresh cilantro for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your grill to medium-high heat.
  2. Roll out the pizza dough into your desired shape and thickness.
  3. Place the dough directly on the grill grates and cook for about 2-3 minutes on each side, or until lightly charred.
  4. Remove the dough from the grill and set aside.
  5. In a bowl, mix the smoked pulled pork with barbecue sauce until well coated.
  6. Spread the barbecue sauce-coated pulled pork evenly over the grilled pizza dough.
  7. Sprinkle the shredded smoked Gouda cheese, pickled jalapenos, and red onion on top.
  8. Carefully place the topped pizza back on the grill and cook for an additional 5-7 minutes, or until the cheese is melted and bubbly.
  9. Remove from the grill, let it cool for a few minutes, and then garnish with fresh cilantro before serving.

3. Caprese Grilled Pizza

Ingredients:

  • 1 store-bought pizza dough
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1 cup fresh mozzarella cheese, sliced
  • 1/4 cup fresh basil leaves, torn
  • 2 tablespoons balsamic glaze
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your grill to medium-high heat.
  2. Roll out the pizza dough into your desired shape and thickness.
  3. Place the dough directly on the grill grates and cook for about 2-3 minutes on each side, or until lightly charred.
  4. Remove the dough from the grill and set aside.
  5. Arrange the sliced mozzarella cheese evenly over the grilled pizza dough.
  6. Scatter the cherry tomato halves on top of the cheese.
  7. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  8. Carefully place the topped pizza back on the grill and cook for an additional 5-7 minutes, or until the cheese is melted and the tomatoes are slightly roasted.
  9. Remove from the grill, let it cool for a few minutes, and then drizzle with balsamic glaze.
  10. Sprinkle torn basil leaves over the top before serving.

4. Red, White, and Blue Fruit Pizza

Ingredients:

  • 1 store-bought pizza dough
  • 1 cup cream cheese, softened
  • 2 tablespoons powdered sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 cup strawberries, sliced
  • 1/2 cup blueberries
  • 1/2 cup raspberries

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your grill to medium-high heat.
  2. Roll out the pizza dough into your desired shape and thickness.
  3. Place the dough directly on the grill grates and cook for about 2-3 minutes on each side, or until lightly charred.
  4. Remove the dough from the grill and set aside.
  5. In a bowl, mix the softened cream cheese, powdered sugar, and vanilla extract until smooth.
  6. Spread the cream cheese mixture evenly over the grilled pizza dough.
  7. Arrange the sliced strawberries, blueberries, and raspberries on top in a patriotic pattern.
  8. Carefully place the topped pizza back on the grill and cook for an additional 5-7 minutes, or until the cream cheese is slightly melted.
  9. Remove from the grill, let it cool for a few minutes, and then slice and serve.

These unique BBQ pizza recipes are sure to be a hit at your Fourth of July celebration. With their smoky flavors, patriotic toppings, and grilled perfection, they will take your barbecue to the next level. So gather your ingredients, fire up the grill, and get ready to impress your guests with these mouthwatering creations. Happy Fourth of July!
