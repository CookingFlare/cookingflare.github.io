Savory Slow Cooker Pork Recipes the Whole Family Will Love


Pork is a versatile and flavorful meat that can be easily transformed into mouthwatering dishes, especially when cooked in a slow cooker. The slow cooking process allows the pork to become tender and infused with the rich flavors of the ingredients, making it a perfect option for busy families looking for delicious and convenient meals. In this blog post, we’ll explore some savory slow cooker pork recipes that are sure to become family favorites.

Slow Cooker Pulled Pork Tacos

Ingredients:

  • 3 lbs pork shoulder
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup chicken broth
  • 1 cup barbecue sauce
  • 1 tbsp brown sugar
  • 1 tbsp chili powder
  • 1 tsp cumin
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Corn tortillas
  • Toppings: shredded lettuce, diced tomatoes, sour cream, shredded cheese

Instructions:

  1. Place the chopped onion and minced garlic in the bottom of the slow cooker.
  2. Rub the pork shoulder with the brown sugar, chili powder, cumin, salt, and pepper.
  3. Place the pork shoulder on top of the onions and garlic in the slow cooker.
  4. Pour the chicken broth and barbecue sauce over the pork.
  5. Cook on low for 8 hours, or until the pork is tender and easily shreds with a fork.
  6. Remove the pork from the slow cooker and shred it using two forks.
  7. Serve the pulled pork in corn tortillas with your favorite toppings.

Slow Cooker Honey Garlic Pork Chops

Ingredients:

  • 4 boneless pork chops
  • 1/2 cup honey
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tsp ginger, grated
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper
  • 1/4 cup water
  • 1 tbsp cornstarch
  • Green onions for garnish

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, whisk together the honey, soy sauce, minced garlic, grated ginger, and black pepper.
  2. Place the pork chops in the slow cooker and pour the honey garlic sauce over them.
  3. Cook on low for 4-5 hours, or until the pork chops are tender.
  4. In a small bowl, whisk together the water and cornstarch to create a slurry.
  5. Pour the slurry into the slow cooker and stir to combine with the sauce. Cook for an additional 30 minutes, or until the sauce has thickened.
  6. Serve the pork chops with the sauce and garnish with sliced green onions.

Slow Cooker Pork and Potato Stew

Ingredients:

  • 2 lbs pork loin, cut into cubes
  • 4 potatoes, peeled and diced
  • 2 carrots, sliced
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 4 cups chicken broth
  • 1 tsp thyme
  • 1 tsp rosemary
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1/4 cup flour
  • 1/4 cup water

Instructions:

  1. Place the pork loin, potatoes, carrots, onion, and garlic in the slow cooker.
  2. Season with thyme, rosemary, salt, and pepper.
  3. Pour the chicken broth over the ingredients.
  4. Cook on low for 6-8 hours, or until the pork is cooked through and the vegetables are tender.
  5. In a small bowl, whisk together the flour and water to create a slurry.
  6. Pour the slurry into the slow cooker and stir to combine with the stew. Cook for an additional 30 minutes, or until the stew has thickened.
  7. Serve the pork and potato stew hot, garnished with fresh herbs if desired.

These savory slow cooker pork recipes are not only delicious but also incredibly easy to prepare, making them perfect for busy weeknights or lazy weekends. The slow cooker does all the work, allowing you to enjoy tender and flavorful pork dishes without spending hours in the kitchen. Try these recipes and watch as your family falls in love with the irresistible flavors of slow-cooked pork.
