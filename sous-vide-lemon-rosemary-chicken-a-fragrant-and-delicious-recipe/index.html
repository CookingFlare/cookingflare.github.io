Sous Vide Lemon Rosemary Chicken: A Fragrant and Delicious Recipe


Are you tired of the same old chicken recipes? Looking for something that will tantalize your taste buds and impress your dinner guests? Look no further than this mouthwatering Sous Vide Lemon Rosemary Chicken recipe. With its fragrant blend of citrus and herbs, this dish is sure to become a favorite in your kitchen.

Ingredients

  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 2 lemons, sliced
  • 4 sprigs of fresh rosemary
  • 4 cloves of garlic, minced
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Olive oil

Instructions

  1. Preheat your sous vide water bath to 145°F (63°C).

  2. Season the chicken breasts generously with salt and pepper on both sides.

  3. Place each chicken breast in a separate vacuum-sealed bag.

  4. Divide the minced garlic evenly among the bags, sprinkling it over the chicken breasts.

  5. Lay a sprig of fresh rosemary on top of each chicken breast.

  6. Arrange a few slices of lemon on top of the rosemary.

  7. Drizzle a small amount of olive oil over each chicken breast to keep them moist during cooking.

  8. Seal the bags using a vacuum sealer or the water displacement method.

  9. Gently place the bags in the preheated water bath and cook for 1.5 to 2 hours, depending on the thickness of the chicken breasts.

  10. Once the chicken is cooked, remove the bags from the water bath and carefully open them, being mindful of the hot steam.

  11. Heat a skillet over medium-high heat and add a drizzle of olive oil.

  12. Remove the chicken breasts from the bags and pat them dry with paper towels.

  13. Sear the chicken breasts in the hot skillet for about 1 minute on each side, or until they develop a golden brown crust.

  14. Remove the chicken breasts from the skillet and let them rest for a few minutes before slicing.

  15. Serve the Sous Vide Lemon Rosemary Chicken with a side of roasted vegetables or a fresh salad for a complete and satisfying meal.

Why Sous Vide?

Sous vide is a cooking technique that involves sealing food in a vacuum-sealed bag and cooking it in a water bath at a precise temperature. This method ensures that the food is cooked evenly and retains its moisture, resulting in tender and succulent meat. By cooking the chicken breasts at a low temperature for an extended period, the flavors of the lemon and rosemary infuse into the meat, creating a truly delectable dish.

Tips for Success

  • When sealing the bags, make sure to remove as much air as possible to ensure proper cooking.

  • If you don’t have a vacuum sealer, you can use the water displacement method. Simply place the seasoned chicken breasts in a resealable plastic bag and slowly lower it into the water, allowing the water to push out the air. Seal the bag just before it is fully submerged.

  • Experiment with different herbs and seasonings to customize the flavor of your Sous Vide Lemon Rosemary Chicken. Thyme, oregano, or even a hint of chili powder can add a unique twist to this classic recipe.

  • If you prefer a crispier skin, you can sear the chicken breasts in a hot skillet before placing them in the water bath. This will give the chicken a nice crust while still benefiting from the sous vide cooking method.

So there you have it – a fragrant and delicious Sous Vide Lemon Rosemary Chicken recipe that will elevate your dinner game. Whether you’re cooking for a special occasion or simply treating yourself to a gourmet meal, this recipe is sure to impress. Give it a try and experience the magic of sous vide cooking for yourself. Bon appétit!
