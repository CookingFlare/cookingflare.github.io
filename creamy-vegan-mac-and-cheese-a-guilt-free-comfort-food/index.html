Creamy Vegan Mac and Cheese: A Guilt-Free Comfort Food


Who doesn’t love a bowl of creamy, cheesy mac and cheese? It’s the ultimate comfort food that warms your soul and brings back childhood memories. But what if you’re following a vegan diet? Can you still indulge in this classic dish without compromising on taste and texture? The answer is a resounding YES! In this recipe, we’ll show you how to create a creamy vegan mac and cheese that will satisfy your cravings and leave you guilt-free.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound of macaroni pasta
  • 2 cups of raw cashews, soaked overnight
  • 1 cup of unsweetened almond milk
  • 1/4 cup of nutritional yeast
  • 2 tablespoons of lemon juice
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon of onion powder
  • 1 teaspoon of dijon mustard
  • 1/2 teaspoon of turmeric powder (optional, for color)
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Cook the macaroni pasta according to the package instructions until al dente. Drain and set aside.

  2. In a blender, combine the soaked cashews, almond milk, nutritional yeast, lemon juice, minced garlic, onion powder, dijon mustard, turmeric powder (if using), salt, and pepper. Blend until smooth and creamy.

  3. In a large saucepan, heat the sauce over medium heat, stirring constantly, until it thickens. This should take about 5 minutes. Adjust the seasoning according to your taste preferences.

  4. Add the cooked macaroni to the saucepan and stir until the pasta is well coated with the creamy sauce.

  5. Serve the creamy vegan mac and cheese immediately, garnished with some freshly chopped parsley or a sprinkle of paprika for an extra pop of color.

Tips and Variations

  • If you don’t have cashews on hand, you can substitute them with blanched almonds or even silken tofu for a nut-free version.

  • Feel free to add some sautéed vegetables like mushrooms, spinach, or bell peppers to the dish for extra flavor and nutrients.

  • For a crispy topping, transfer the mac and cheese to a baking dish, sprinkle some breadcrumbs or vegan cheese on top, and broil it in the oven until golden and bubbly.

  • Leftovers can be stored in an airtight container in the refrigerator for up to 3 days. Simply reheat it in a saucepan with a splash of almond milk to bring back the creamy consistency.

Final Thoughts

Creamy vegan mac and cheese is a game-changer for those who are following a plant-based lifestyle or simply looking for a healthier alternative to the traditional dish. With the right combination of ingredients, you can achieve a velvety smooth sauce that rivals its dairy-based counterpart. So go ahead, grab a bowl, and indulge in this guilt-free comfort food that will leave you feeling satisfied and nourished.

Enjoy!
