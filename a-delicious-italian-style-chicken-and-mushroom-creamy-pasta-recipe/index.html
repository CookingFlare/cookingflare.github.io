A Delicious Italian-Style Chicken and Mushroom Creamy Pasta Recipe


If you’re in the mood for a comforting and flavorful meal that transports you to the heart of Italy, look no further than this delicious Italian-style chicken and mushroom creamy pasta recipe. This dish combines tender chicken, earthy mushrooms, al dente pasta, and a rich, creamy sauce to create a truly satisfying dining experience. Whether you’re cooking for a special occasion or simply craving a hearty, homemade meal, this recipe is sure to impress your taste buds and leave you feeling fully satisfied.

Ingredients

To make this mouthwatering Italian-style chicken and mushroom creamy pasta, you’ll need the following ingredients:

  • 1 pound of boneless, skinless chicken breasts, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 8 ounces of cremini or button mushrooms, sliced
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 cup of chicken broth
  • 1 cup of heavy cream
  • 1/2 cup of grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 teaspoon of dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon of dried basil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 12 ounces of your favorite pasta, such as fettuccine or penne
  • Fresh parsley for garnish

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Ingredients: Begin by preparing all the ingredients as listed above. Chop the chicken into bite-sized pieces, slice the mushrooms, finely chop the onion, and mince the garlic. Having everything ready will make the cooking process smooth and efficient.

  2. Cook the Chicken and Mushrooms: In a large skillet, heat a tablespoon of olive oil over medium-high heat. Add the chicken pieces and cook until they are golden brown and cooked through. Remove the chicken from the skillet and set it aside. In the same skillet, add a bit more oil if needed and sauté the mushrooms until they are golden and tender. Add the chopped onion and garlic, and cook until the onion is translucent and fragrant.

  3. Prepare the Sauce: Once the mushrooms, onion, and garlic are cooked, pour in the chicken broth and bring it to a simmer. Allow the broth to reduce slightly before adding the heavy cream. Stir in the dried oregano, dried basil, and grated Parmesan cheese. Season the sauce with salt and pepper to taste, and let it simmer until it thickens slightly.

  4. Cook the Pasta: While the sauce is simmering, bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Cook the pasta according to the package instructions until it is al dente. Drain the pasta and set it aside.

  5. Combine and Serve: Add the cooked chicken to the sauce, stirring to coat the chicken pieces evenly. Then, add the cooked pasta to the skillet, tossing everything together until the pasta is well coated with the creamy sauce and the chicken and mushrooms are distributed throughout. If the sauce seems too thick, you can add a splash of chicken broth or cream to loosen it up.

  6. Garnish and Enjoy: Once everything is well combined, garnish the Italian-style chicken and mushroom creamy pasta with freshly chopped parsley. Serve the dish hot, and enjoy the delightful flavors and aromas of this comforting Italian-inspired meal.

Conclusion

This Italian-style chicken and mushroom creamy pasta recipe is a perfect choice for a cozy dinner at home or a gathering with loved ones. The combination of tender chicken, earthy mushrooms, and a luscious creamy sauce creates a dish that is both indulgent and satisfying. The flavors and textures of this pasta dish are sure to transport you to the rustic kitchens of Italy, where hearty and comforting meals are a way of life. So, roll up your sleeves, gather your ingredients, and treat yourself to this delightful Italian-inspired culinary experience. Buon appetito!
