Delightful One Pot Lemon Garlic Butter Shrimp


If you’re a seafood lover, then this recipe is bound to become one of your favorites. Delightful One Pot Lemon Garlic Butter Shrimp is not only easy to make, but it also packs a punch of flavor that will leave your taste buds craving for more. This recipe is perfect for a quick weeknight dinner or even for entertaining guests. So, let’s dive right into it!

Ingredients

  • 1 pound of fresh shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 4 tablespoons of unsalted butter
  • 4 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 lemon, juiced and zested
  • 1/2 teaspoon of red pepper flakes (optional)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley, chopped for garnish

Instructions

  1. In a large skillet or frying pan, melt the butter over medium heat. Once the butter has melted, add the minced garlic and sauté for about a minute until fragrant.

  2. Add the shrimp to the skillet and cook for about 2-3 minutes on each side until they turn pink and opaque. Be careful not to overcook the shrimp as they can become rubbery.

  3. Squeeze the juice of one lemon over the shrimp and sprinkle the lemon zest on top. If you like a little heat, add the red pepper flakes at this point. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Give everything a good stir to coat the shrimp evenly with the lemon garlic butter sauce.

  4. Reduce the heat to low and let the shrimp simmer in the sauce for another minute or so to allow the flavors to meld together.

  5. Remove the skillet from the heat and garnish with freshly chopped parsley. The bright green color of the parsley adds a pop of freshness to the dish and complements the flavors perfectly.

  6. Serve the Delightful One Pot Lemon Garlic Butter Shrimp immediately while it’s still hot. You can enjoy it as it is or pair it with some crusty bread to soak up all the delicious sauce.

  7. And there you have it – a quick and easy recipe for Delightful One Pot Lemon Garlic Butter Shrimp that will surely impress your family and friends.

Tips and Variations

  • If you prefer a creamier sauce, you can add a splash of heavy cream or coconut milk to the skillet after adding the lemon juice. This will give the dish a rich and velvety texture.

  • Feel free to customize the recipe by adding your favorite vegetables. Bell peppers, cherry tomatoes, or even zucchini work well with the flavors of the lemon garlic butter sauce.

  • For an extra burst of citrus, you can add some lemon slices to the skillet while cooking the shrimp. This will infuse the dish with even more lemony goodness.

  • If you’re not a fan of spicy food, you can omit the red pepper flakes. The dish will still be flavorful without the heat.

  • Don’t forget to serve the Delightful One Pot Lemon Garlic Butter Shrimp with some lemon wedges on the side. Squeezing a little extra lemon juice over the shrimp right before eating adds a refreshing tang that enhances the overall taste.

So, there you have it – a simple yet impressive recipe for Delightful One Pot Lemon Garlic Butter Shrimp. With its bright flavors and minimal cleanup, this dish is a winner in every way. Whether you’re cooking for yourself or entertaining guests, this recipe is sure to satisfy. Give it a try and let your taste buds dance with delight!
