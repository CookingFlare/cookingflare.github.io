Garlic Butter Shrimp Pasta: A Quick and Easy Weeknight Dinner


If you’re looking for a delicious and satisfying meal that can be whipped up in no time, then this Garlic Butter Shrimp Pasta recipe is perfect for you. Packed with flavors and textures, this dish is sure to become a family favorite. Best of all, it requires minimal ingredients and can be prepared in just under 30 minutes. So let’s dive right into the recipe!

Ingredients

  • 8 ounces of linguine or your pasta of choice
  • 1 pound of shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 4 tablespoons of unsalted butter
  • 4 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1/2 teaspoon of red pepper flakes (adjust according to your spice preference)
  • 1/4 cup of chicken or vegetable broth
  • 1/4 cup of freshly grated Parmesan cheese
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley, chopped (for garnish)

Instructions

  1. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Cook the pasta according to the package instructions until al dente. Drain and set aside.

  2. While the pasta is cooking, melt the butter in a large skillet over medium heat. Add the minced garlic and red pepper flakes, and sauté until the garlic becomes fragrant and slightly golden, about 2 minutes.

  3. Increase the heat to medium-high and add the shrimp to the skillet. Cook for 2-3 minutes per side, or until the shrimp turn pink and opaque. Be careful not to overcook the shrimp, as they can become rubbery.

  4. Remove the cooked shrimp from the skillet and set aside. In the same skillet, pour in the chicken or vegetable broth and bring it to a simmer. Allow the broth to reduce slightly, about 2 minutes.

  5. Add the cooked pasta to the skillet and toss it in the garlic butter sauce. Make sure to coat the pasta evenly.

  6. Return the cooked shrimp to the skillet and toss it with the pasta. This will allow the flavors to meld together.

  7. Sprinkle the freshly grated Parmesan cheese over the pasta and gently toss to combine. The cheese will melt slightly, creating a creamy and flavorful sauce.

  8. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Remember, the Parmesan cheese adds a salty element, so adjust accordingly.

  9. Remove the skillet from the heat and garnish the Garlic Butter Shrimp Pasta with freshly chopped parsley.

  10. Serve the pasta immediately while it’s still hot and enjoy the delicious flavors and textures that this quick and easy weeknight dinner has to offer.

Conclusion

Garlic Butter Shrimp Pasta is a versatile dish that can be customized to suit your taste preferences. You can add vegetables like spinach or cherry tomatoes for an extra burst of flavor and nutrition. Feel free to experiment and make it your own.

This recipe is not only quick and easy to prepare, but it also delivers on taste and satisfaction. Whether you’re cooking for yourself, your family, or even guests, this Garlic Butter Shrimp Pasta is sure to impress. So, the next time you’re in need of a delicious weeknight dinner, give this recipe a try and enjoy the fantastic flavors that come together in every bite.
