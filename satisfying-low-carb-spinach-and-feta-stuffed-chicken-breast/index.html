Satisfying Low Carb Spinach and Feta Stuffed Chicken Breast


If you’re looking for a delicious and satisfying low-carb meal, this spinach and feta stuffed chicken breast recipe is sure to hit the spot. Packed with flavor and nutrients, this dish is perfect for a cozy dinner at home or for entertaining guests. The combination of tender chicken, savory spinach, and creamy feta cheese creates a mouthwatering experience that will have everyone asking for seconds.

Ingredients

For the Chicken:

  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil

For the Filling:

  • 2 cups fresh spinach, chopped
  • 1/2 cup crumbled feta cheese
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

For the Coating:

  • 1/2 cup almond flour
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 2 eggs, beaten

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).

  2. In a skillet, heat 1 tablespoon of olive oil over medium heat. Add the chopped spinach and minced garlic, and sauté until the spinach is wilted and the garlic is fragrant, about 3-4 minutes. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Remove the spinach mixture from the heat and let it cool slightly.

  3. In a mixing bowl, combine the sautéed spinach, crumbled feta cheese, and mix well to create the filling.

  4. On a clean surface, lay out the chicken breasts and carefully slice a pocket into each one, being careful not to cut all the way through. Season the chicken breasts with salt and pepper, then stuff each one with the spinach and feta filling.

  5. In a shallow dish, combine the almond flour, paprika, garlic powder, salt, and pepper. In another dish, place the beaten eggs.

  6. Dip each stuffed chicken breast into the beaten eggs, ensuring it’s well coated, then dredge it in the almond flour mixture, pressing gently to adhere.

  7. In a large skillet, heat 1 tablespoon of olive oil over medium-high heat. Add the coated chicken breasts and cook for 2-3 minutes on each side until golden brown.

  8. Transfer the browned chicken breasts to a baking dish and bake in the preheated oven for 20-25 minutes, or until the chicken is cooked through and the filling is hot and bubbly.

  9. Once cooked, remove the chicken from the oven and let it rest for a few minutes before serving.

Serving Suggestions

This spinach and feta stuffed chicken breast pairs beautifully with a crisp green salad dressed with a light vinaigrette or a side of roasted vegetables. The flavors of the spinach and feta complement a variety of side dishes, making it a versatile and impressive meal for any occasion.

Final Thoughts

This low-carb spinach and feta stuffed chicken breast recipe is a fantastic way to elevate your weeknight dinners or impress your guests with a flavorful and healthy dish. By combining simple ingredients with a few easy cooking techniques, you can create a restaurant-quality meal right in your own kitchen. Whether you’re following a low-carb lifestyle or simply looking for a delicious new recipe to try, this dish is a must-try for anyone who loves the combination of tender chicken, earthy spinach, and tangy feta cheese. Enjoy!
