The Best Cookie Recipes for Summer Parties


Summer is here, and with it comes the perfect excuse to throw a party and indulge in some delicious treats. What better way to do so than with a platter of freshly baked cookies? In this post, we’ll explore the best cookie recipes for summer parties, guaranteed to impress your guests and satisfy their sweet tooth.

夏季派对饼干的基本原则

Before we dive into the recipes, let’s talk about the basic principles of making the perfect summer party cookies. Here are a few key takeaways:

  • Choose seasonal ingredients: Summer is the perfect time to incorporate fresh fruits, herbs, and nuts into your cookie dough. This not only adds flavor but also a pop of color to your baked goods.
  • Keep it light and refreshing: Summer cookies should be light, airy, and refreshing. Avoid using heavy or rich ingredients that can weigh down your cookies.
  • Consider dietary restrictions: Summer parties often attract a diverse range of guests. Make sure to include cookie recipes that cater to different dietary needs, such as gluten-free, vegan, or sugar-free options.

1. 蜜桃和牛奶饼干

The combination of sweet peaches and creamy milk is a match made in heaven. These cookies are perfect for a summer party and are sure to be a hit with your guests.

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 cup diced peaches
  • 1/4 cup chopped pecans
  • Salt to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C). Line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
  2. In a medium bowl, whisk together flour, sugar, and salt.
  3. In a large bowl, cream together butter and milk until smooth. Beat in eggs and vanilla extract.
  4. Gradually add dry ingredients to wet ingredients and mix until a dough forms. Fold in diced peaches and chopped pecans.
  5. Drop rounded tablespoonfuls of dough onto prepared baking sheet, leaving about 2 inches of space between each cookie.
  6. Bake for 12-15 minutes or until lightly golden.

2. 连续柠檬和新奶饼干

These lemon and new milk cookies are a refreshing twist on the classic cookie. The combination of bright citrus and creamy milk is sure to brighten up any summer party.

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1/2 cup new milk
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 teaspoon lemon zest
  • 2 tablespoons freshly squeezed lemon juice
  • 1/4 cup chopped pistachios
  • Salt to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C). Line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
  2. In a medium bowl, whisk together flour, sugar, and salt.
  3. In a large bowl, cream together butter and new milk until smooth. Beat in eggs and lemon zest.
  4. Gradually add dry ingredients to wet ingredients and mix until a dough forms. Fold in lemon juice and chopped pistachios.
  5. Drop rounded tablespoonfuls of dough onto prepared baking sheet, leaving about 2 inches of space between each cookie.
  6. Bake for 12-15 minutes or until lightly golden.

3. 棕榈和椰子油饼干

These palm and coconut oil cookies are a tropical twist on the classic cookie. The combination of sweet palm sugar and creamy coconut oil is sure to transport your guests to a tropical paradise.

  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1/4 cup coconut oil
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

  • 1/4 cup shredded coconut
  • Salt to taste

4. 香蕉和咖啡饼干

These banana and coffee cookies are a unique twist on the classic cookie. The combination of sweet banana and rich coffee is sure to satisfy your guests’ sweet tooth.

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1/2 cup mashed banana
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 teaspoon instant coffee powder
  • 1/4 cup chopped walnuts
  • Salt to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C). Line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
  2. In a medium bowl, whisk together flour, sugar, and salt.
  3. In a large bowl, cream together butter and mashed banana until smooth. Beat in eggs and coffee powder.
  4. Gradually add dry ingredients to wet ingredients and mix until a dough forms. Fold in chopped walnuts.
  5. Drop rounded tablespoonfuls of dough onto prepared baking sheet, leaving about 2 inches of space between each cookie.
  6. Bake for 12-15 minutes or until lightly golden.

These four cookie recipes are sure to impress your guests at your next summer party. With their unique flavor combinations and refreshing twists, they’re perfect for the warm weather season. So go ahead, get baking, and make some unforgettable memories with your friends and family.
