Classic Easter Ham Recipes for a Perfect Holiday Meal


Easter is a time for joy, celebration, and of course, delicious food. One of the most iconic dishes that grace Easter dinner tables is a succulent, glazed ham. Whether you’re hosting a large gathering or having an intimate family meal, a classic Easter ham is a must-have centerpiece. In this blog post, we’ll explore traditional and creative ways to prepare and serve Easter ham to make your holiday meal truly memorable.

Honey Glazed Ham

Ingredients:

  • 1 spiral-cut ham
  • 1 cup honey
  • 1/2 cup brown sugar
  • 1/4 cup Dijon mustard
  • 2 tablespoons apple cider vinegar
  • Cloves for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 325°F (165°C).
  2. Place the ham in a roasting pan.
  3. In a small saucepan, combine honey, brown sugar, mustard, and vinegar. Heat over low heat until sugar dissolves.
  4. Brush the ham with the glaze, making sure to get into the slices.
  5. Insert cloves into the ham for added flavor.
  6. Cover the ham with foil and bake for about 1.5 hours, basting with the glaze every 30 minutes.
  7. Remove the foil in the last 20 minutes of baking for a caramelized finish.
  8. Let the ham rest before slicing and serving.

Pineapple Glazed Ham

Ingredients:

  • 1 fully cooked ham
  • 1 can pineapple rings
  • 1/2 cup brown sugar
  • 1/4 cup pineapple juice
  • 1 tablespoon Dijon mustard
  • Maraschino cherries for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 325°F (165°C).
  2. Score the ham and place it in a roasting pan.
  3. Arrange pineapple rings and cherries on top of the ham using toothpicks.
  4. In a bowl, mix brown sugar, pineapple juice, and mustard to create the glaze.
  5. Brush the glaze over the ham, making sure to coat evenly.
  6. Cover with foil and bake for about 1.5 hours, basting occasionally.
  7. Remove the foil for the last 20 minutes to caramelize the glaze.
  8. Let the ham rest before slicing and serving.

Bourbon Glazed Ham

Ingredients:

  • 1 bone-in ham
  • 1/2 cup bourbon
  • 1/2 cup brown sugar
  • 1/4 cup honey
  • 2 tablespoons Dijon mustard
  • 1 teaspoon ground cloves

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 325°F (165°C).
  2. Place the ham in a roasting pan.
  3. In a saucepan, combine bourbon, brown sugar, honey, mustard, and cloves. Simmer until slightly thickened.
  4. Brush the glaze over the ham, ensuring it covers the entire surface.
  5. Bake the ham uncovered for about 2 hours, basting with the glaze every 30 minutes.
  6. Let the ham rest before slicing thinly and serving.

Conclusion

Easter ham is a truly classic dish that brings warmth and flavor to any holiday gathering. Whether you prefer a sweet honey glaze, a tangy pineapple twist, or a rich bourbon infusion, there’s a ham recipe to suit every taste. Experiment with different glazes and garnishes to make your Easter meal a feast to remember. Serve with your favorite sides and enjoy the company of loved ones around the table. Wishing you a happy and delicious Easter celebration!

Remember, the key to a perfect holiday meal is not just the taste but also the love and effort you put into preparing it. Enjoy the process, savor the flavors, and create lasting memories with your Easter ham masterpiece!
