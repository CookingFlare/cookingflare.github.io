25 Festive Christmas Cookie Recipes to Sweeten Your Holiday


Christmas is nearly here, and that means the holiday spirit is alive in kitchens everywhere! Families are donning aprons, preheating ovens, and reaching for flour and sugar to create cookies worthy of the season. The aroma of spices, extracts, and sweet dough is the essence of a festive home. Whether you’re hosting a Christmas party, gifting homemade treats to loved ones, or baking just for yourself, these 25 festive Christmas cookie recipes will add a special touch to the celebrations!

1. Classic Sugar Cookies

An iconic choice, sugar cookies are beautifully simple yet versatile. Make them crisp or soft depending on your preference. Decorate them with royal icing, colorful sprinkles, and festive shapes like snowflakes or candy canes to make them a perfect holiday treat.

Pro tip: Chill the dough before rolling it out for easier handling and consistent shapes.

2. Gingerbread Men Cookies

No list of Christmas cookies is complete without gingerbread men. Packed with bold spices like ginger, cinnamon, and cloves, these cookies are fun to decorate with icing and candies. They also pair perfectly with eggnog or mulled cider!

3. Peppermint Bark Cookies

A mashup of cookies and candy! These buttery sugar cookies are topped with white chocolate, crushed peppermint candies, and mini chocolate chips. Every bite is a burst of holiday cheer and winter freshness.

4. Thumbprint Cookies with Jam

Often associated with joyful gatherings, thumbprint cookies are a nostalgic favorite. The buttery dough is pressed at the center and filled with sweet or tart jams like raspberry, apricot, or strawberry. Simple yet sophisticated!

5. Chocolate Snowball Cookies

These are rich chocolate cookies rolled generously in powdered sugar for a snowy appearance. The fudge-like center makes them a decadent addition to any cookie platter. Perfect for chocolate lovers.

6. Spice Molasses Cookies

Molasses cookies bring old-fashioned charm to the holiday season. Their deep, warm flavors are enhanced by a blend of spices. They’re soft and chewy—ideal for dunking into a hot cup of cocoa or coffee.

7. Linzer Cookies

Linzer cookies are a Christmas classic from Austria. Made with almond flour and filled with fruit preserves, their delicate lattice design is both elegant and inviting. Dusting them with powdered sugar elevates their festive flair.

8. White Chocolate Cranberry Cookies

Cranberries add tangy sweetness, while white chocolate provides a creamy counterbalance. These cookies are eye-catching thanks to the red and white colors, making them a bright addition to your holiday table.

9. Snowflake Shortbread Cookies

Shortbread is a buttery and crumbly favorite, especially when shaped like snowflakes for the holidays. Dip them halfway into melted chocolate or sprinkle with coarse sugar for added flair and holiday sweetness.

10. Double Chocolate Mint Cookies

Mint and chocolate were made for the holidays! These cookies combine cocoa powder and chocolate chips for richness, then add a cooling hint of mint extract. A sprinkle of crushed candy canes completes the festive look.

11. Eggnog Cookies

Eggnog isn’t just for sipping—it’s a wonderful ingredient for cookies! These soft cookies feature the distinct, spiced flavor of eggnog and are finished with a creamy nutmeg frosting for the ultimate holiday vibe.

12. Christmas Lights Sugar Cookies

Decorating Christmas tree sugar cookies with “lights” made of colorful candies or icing is a joyful experience for bakers of all ages. A little creativity goes a long way in making these cookies stand out.

13. Chocolate Orange Cookies

Zesty orange and rich chocolate come together for a sophisticated flavor pairing. These cookies are perfect for gifting since their balance of sweet and citrus is universally enjoyed.

14. Almond Snowball Cookies

Almond snowballs have a light, nutty flavor and an irresistible texture. Rolled in powdered sugar, they mimic little snowballs to match the season. Enhance the festive presentation by using silver dragees or edible snowflake decorations.

15. Cinnamon Roll Cookies

If you love cinnamon rolls, these cookies are a dream come true. The dough is rolled with cinnamon sugar, sliced into mini spirals, and baked to perfection. They’re soft, swirly, and indulgently festive.

16. Chocolate Chip Candy Cane Cookies

A twist on the classic chocolate chip cookie, these add crushed candy canes to the mix. The result is a chewy, minty delight that’s great for sharing.

17. Pistachio Cherry Cookies

These eye-catching cookies feature the festive colors of red and green from cherries and pistachios. The buttery dough complements the nutty and fruity flavors beautifully.

18. Raspberry Cheesecake Cookies

Creamy cheesecake-like centers surrounded by buttery cookie dough and topped with raspberry preserves make these cookies decadent and festive. They’re guaranteed to be the star of your holiday cookie tray.

19. Orange Cardamom Cookies

Exotic cardamom paired with vibrant orange zest creates a unique cookie that’s aromatic and bright. These cookies bring warmth and sunshine to winter gatherings.

20. Holiday M&M Cookies

Bright red and green M&Ms transform a classic cookie into a holiday masterpiece. The chocolate and colorful candy combination creates a dessert kids and adults will love.

21. Gluten-Free Christmas Cookies

Using almond flour or gluten-free all-purpose flour, you can create tender cookies that are perfect for guests with dietary restrictions. Decorate with icing and sprinkles for the same cheerful charm as traditional cookies.

22. Chocolate Cherry Cookies

Cherries and chocolate marry beautifully in this festive treat. The tangy cherries balance the richness of the cocoa for a sophisticated yet crowd-pleasing cookie.

23. Nutty Biscotti

Biscotti are a great addition to your cookie lineup, especially for coffee lovers. Add festive nuts like almonds or hazelnuts, and jazz them up by dipping edges in white or dark chocolate.

24. Candy-Cane Twists

Bring the classic peppermint candy cane to cookie form with fun red and white swirls. These buttery cookies are flavored with peppermint extract and are sure to impress with their unique and festive look.

25. Salted Caramel Cookies

The luxurious combination of sweet caramel and a touch of sea salt makes these cookies impossible to resist. Add a drizzle of caramel over the baked cookies for extra gooey decadence.

Tips for Creating the Perfect Batch of Christmas Cookies

  1. Prep Ahead: Always read the recipe thoroughly and prepare ingredients ahead of time. It saves time and ensures a smooth process.
  2. Don’t Skip Chilling: Chill dough as directed—this prevents spreading and helps maintain the intended shapes.
  3. Experiment with Flavors: From extracts to spices, don’t be afraid to innovate and personalize your cookies!
  4. Decorate Creatively: The more fun you have decorating, the more magical your cookies will become.
  5. Store Properly: Use airtight containers to preserve freshness and flavors, especially if gifting cookies.

Spread the joy of the season through sweet creations that delight hearts and taste buds alike. Whether you’re baking with loved ones or surprising friends with edible gifts, these festive Christmas cookies will brighten your holidays and remind you why this season truly is the most wonderful time of the year!
