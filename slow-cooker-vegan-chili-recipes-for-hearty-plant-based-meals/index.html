Slow Cooker Vegan Chili Recipes for Hearty Plant-Based Meals


Are you in the mood for a delicious and hearty plant-based meal? Look no further! In this blog post, we will explore some mouthwatering slow cooker vegan chili recipes that are sure to satisfy your cravings. These recipes are not only packed with flavor but also incredibly easy to make. So, grab your slow cooker, gather your ingredients, and let’s get cooking!

Classic Vegan Chili

Preparation time: 15 minutes
Cooking time: 4-6 hours
Serves: 6

Ingredients

  • 1 can (15 ounces) of kidney beans, drained and rinsed
  • 1 can (15 ounces) of black beans, drained and rinsed
  • 1 can (15 ounces) of diced tomatoes
  • 1 cup of vegetable broth
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 red bell pepper, diced
  • 1 green bell pepper, diced
  • 1 tablespoon of chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon of cumin
  • 1 teaspoon of paprika
  • Salt and pepper, to taste

Instructions

  1. In your slow cooker, combine the kidney beans, black beans, diced tomatoes, vegetable broth, onion, garlic, red bell pepper, green bell pepper, chili powder, cumin, paprika, salt, and pepper. Mix well to combine all the flavors.
  2. Cover the slow cooker and cook on low heat for 4-6 hours or on high heat for 2-3 hours, until the chili is thick and the flavors have melded together.
  3. Once the chili is ready, taste and adjust the seasoning if needed. Serve hot with your favorite toppings, such as avocado slices, chopped cilantro, or a dollop of vegan sour cream.

Spicy Quinoa Chili

Preparation time: 15 minutes
Cooking time: 6-8 hours
Serves: 8

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of quinoa, rinsed
  • 1 can (15 ounces) of kidney beans, drained and rinsed
  • 1 can (15 ounces) of black beans, drained and rinsed
  • 1 can (15 ounces) of corn, drained
  • 1 can (15 ounces) of diced tomatoes
  • 1 cup of vegetable broth
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 red bell pepper, diced
  • 1 green bell pepper, diced
  • 2 tablespoons of chili powder
  • 1 tablespoon of cumin
  • 1 teaspoon of paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon of cayenne pepper (adjust to taste)
  • Salt and pepper, to taste

Instructions

  1. In your slow cooker, combine the quinoa, kidney beans, black beans, corn, diced tomatoes, vegetable broth, onion, garlic, red bell pepper, green bell pepper, chili powder, cumin, paprika, cayenne pepper, salt, and pepper. Stir well to combine all the ingredients.
  2. Cover the slow cooker and cook on low heat for 6-8 hours or on high heat for 3-4 hours, until the quinoa is tender and the chili has thickened.
  3. Before serving, taste the chili and adjust the seasoning according to your preference. Serve hot and garnish with chopped green onions or vegan cheese, if desired.

White Bean and Sweet Potato Chili

Preparation time: 20 minutes
Cooking time: 6-8 hours
Serves: 6

Ingredients

  • 2 cans (15 ounces each) of white beans, drained and rinsed
  • 2 sweet potatoes, peeled and diced
  • 1 can (15 ounces) of diced tomatoes
  • 1 cup of vegetable broth
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 jalapeno pepper, seeded and diced
  • 1 tablespoon of chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon of cumin
  • 1 teaspoon of smoked paprika
  • Salt and pepper, to taste

Instructions

  1. In your slow cooker, combine the white beans, sweet potatoes, diced tomatoes, vegetable broth, onion, garlic, jalapeno pepper, chili powder, cumin, smoked paprika, salt, and pepper. Mix well to ensure all the ingredients are evenly coated with the spices.
  2. Cover the slow cooker and cook on low heat for 6-8 hours or on high heat for 3-4 hours, until the sweet potatoes are tender and the chili has thickened.
  3. Once the chili is done, give it a taste and adjust the seasoning if needed. Serve hot and garnish with fresh cilantro or a squeeze of lime juice for an extra burst of flavor.

Conclusion

There you have it, three delicious slow cooker vegan chili recipes that are perfect for those seeking a hearty plant-based meal. Whether you prefer the classic flavors, a spicy kick, or a unique twist with white beans and sweet potatoes, these recipes are sure to impress your taste buds. So, dust off your slow cooker and give these recipes a try. Your stomach will thank you!

Note: Remember to always adjust the seasoning according to your preference and feel free to experiment with additional vegetables or spices to make these recipes your own.
