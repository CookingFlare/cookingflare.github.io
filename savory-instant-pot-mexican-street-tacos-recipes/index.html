Savory Instant Pot Mexican Street Tacos Recipes


If you’re a fan of Mexican street food, then you’re in for a treat with these savory Instant Pot Mexican street tacos. The Instant Pot makes it easy to achieve that slow-cooked flavor in a fraction of the time, resulting in tender, flavorful meat that’s perfect for stuffing into warm corn tortillas. Whether you’re hosting a gathering or simply craving a delicious taco dinner, this recipe is sure to become a favorite. Let’s dive into the details and bring the vibrant flavors of Mexican street food right into your kitchen.

Ingredients

For the Meat:

  • 2 pounds of beef (such as chuck roast or flank steak)
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 4 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 cup beef broth
  • 1 tablespoon cumin
  • 1 tablespoon chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1 teaspoon oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste

For Serving:

  • Corn tortillas
  • Chopped onion
  • Chopped cilantro
  • Salsa verde
  • Lime wedges

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Meat:

    • Cut the beef into chunks and season with salt and pepper.
    • Set your Instant Pot to sauté mode and add a little oil.
    • Sear the beef on all sides until browned, then remove from the pot and set aside.

  2. Sauté Aromatics:

    • In the same pot, add the chopped onion and garlic. Sauté until softened and fragrant.

  3. Pressure Cook:

    • Return the beef to the pot and add the beef broth, cumin, chili powder, paprika, and oregano.
    • Close the lid and set the Instant Pot to high pressure for 30 minutes.

  4. Release Pressure and Shred:

    • Once the cooking time is up, allow the pressure to release naturally for 10 minutes, then do a quick pressure release.
    • Remove the beef from the pot and shred it using two forks.

  5. Broil for Crispy Edges (Optional):

    • For an added layer of flavor and texture, you can spread the shredded beef on a baking sheet and broil in the oven for a few minutes until it gets crispy edges.

  6. Assemble the Tacos:

    • Warm the corn tortillas and fill them with the tender, flavorful beef.
    • Top with chopped onion, cilantro, and a drizzle of salsa verde.
    • Serve with lime wedges on the side for an extra pop of freshness.

Tips for the Perfect Instant Pot Mexican Street Tacos

  • Use a cut of beef with some marbling, like chuck roast or flank steak, for the best flavor and texture.
  • For a smoky kick, consider adding a chipotle pepper in adobo sauce to the cooking liquid.
  • Don’t skip the step of browning the meat before pressure cooking. It adds depth of flavor to the dish.
  • If you prefer a spicier flavor, you can adjust the amount of chili powder to suit your taste.

Final Thoughts

These savory Instant Pot Mexican street tacos are a celebration of bold flavors and vibrant colors. The tender beef, fragrant with spices, pairs perfectly with the freshness of the toppings. Whether it’s a weeknight dinner or a festive gathering, these tacos are sure to be a hit. So, grab your Instant Pot and get ready to savor the irresistible taste of authentic Mexican street food right at home. Enjoy!

Remember, the secret to a great taco is in the details – from the tender meat to the perfect balance of flavors. With this recipe, you’ll have a crowd-pleasing dish that’s sure to become a regular on your menu. Happy cooking!
