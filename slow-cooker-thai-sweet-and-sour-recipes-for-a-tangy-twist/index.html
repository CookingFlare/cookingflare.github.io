Slow Cooker Thai Sweet and Sour Recipes for a Tangy Twist


If you’re a fan of Thai cuisine and love the sweet and sour flavor profile, then you’re in for a treat with these slow cooker Thai sweet and sour recipes. The slow cooker, also known as a crock-pot, is a fantastic tool for creating flavorful and tender dishes with minimal effort. By combining the bold and vibrant flavors of Thai cuisine with the convenience of a slow cooker, you can elevate your culinary skills and impress your friends and family with these mouthwatering dishes.

Slow Cooker Thai Sweet and Sour Chicken

Ingredients:

  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1 red bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 green bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 onion, sliced
  • 1 cup pineapple chunks
  • 1/2 cup chicken broth
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 1/4 cup rice vinegar
  • 3 tablespoons brown sugar
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon ginger, grated
  • 1 tablespoon cornstarch
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Cooked rice for serving
  • Fresh cilantro for garnish

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, whisk together the chicken broth, soy sauce, rice vinegar, brown sugar, garlic, and ginger.
  2. Place the chicken breasts in the slow cooker and season with salt and pepper.
  3. Arrange the sliced bell peppers, onion, and pineapple chunks around the chicken.
  4. Pour the sauce mixture over the chicken and vegetables.
  5. Cover and cook on low for 4-5 hours or until the chicken is tender.
  6. Once the chicken is cooked, remove it from the slow cooker and shred it using two forks.
  7. In a small bowl, mix the cornstarch with 2 tablespoons of water to create a slurry. Stir the slurry into the sauce in the slow cooker to thicken it.
  8. Return the shredded chicken to the slow cooker and stir to coat it in the sweet and sour sauce.
  9. Serve the sweet and sour chicken over cooked rice and garnish with fresh cilantro.

Slow Cooker Thai Sweet and Sour Pork

Ingredients:

  • 2 pounds pork tenderloin, cut into 1-inch cubes
  • 1 red onion, sliced
  • 1 cup pineapple chunks
  • 1/2 cup chicken broth
  • 1/4 cup fish sauce
  • 1/4 cup palm sugar or brown sugar
  • 3 tablespoons lime juice
  • 2 tablespoons tamarind paste
  • 2 tablespoons tomato paste
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon chili flakes
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Cooked jasmine rice for serving
  • Chopped peanuts for garnish

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, combine the chicken broth, fish sauce, palm sugar, lime juice, tamarind paste, tomato paste, garlic, and chili flakes.
  2. Place the pork cubes in the slow cooker and season with salt and pepper.
  3. Add the sliced red onion and pineapple chunks to the slow cooker.
  4. Pour the sauce mixture over the pork and vegetables.
  5. Cover and cook on low for 6-7 hours or until the pork is fork-tender.
  6. Once the pork is cooked, remove it from the slow cooker and set it aside.
  7. Transfer the sauce from the slow cooker to a saucepan and simmer over medium heat until it thickens.
  8. Return the pork to the slow cooker and pour the thickened sauce over it.
  9. Serve the sweet and sour pork over cooked jasmine rice and garnish with chopped peanuts.

Conclusion

These slow cooker Thai sweet and sour recipes are a fantastic way to infuse your kitchen with the exotic flavors of Thailand while enjoying the convenience of using a slow cooker. Whether you’re a busy professional, a parent with a hectic schedule, or simply someone who loves delicious food, these recipes are sure to become a staple in your culinary repertoire. So, grab your slow cooker and get ready to tantalize your taste buds with the tantalizing tang of Thai sweet and sour dishes!
