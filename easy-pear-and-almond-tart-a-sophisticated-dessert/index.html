Easy Pear and Almond Tart: A Sophisticated Dessert


If you’re in the mood for a dessert that’s both elegant and effortlessly delicious, look no further than this easy pear and almond tart. This sophisticated dessert combines the delicate sweetness of ripe pears with the rich, nutty flavor of almonds, all nestled in a buttery, flaky crust. Whether you’re hosting a dinner party or simply craving a special treat, this pear and almond tart is sure to impress. Let’s dive into the recipe and discover how to create this culinary masterpiece.

Ingredients

For the Crust:

  • 1 1/4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 cup almond flour
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, chilled and cubed
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1 large egg yolk
  • 2 tablespoons ice water

For the Almond Filling:

  • 1 cup almond flour
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 teaspoon almond extract

For the Pears:

  • 3 ripe but firm pears, peeled, cored, and thinly sliced
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 1/4 cup apricot preserves

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Crust:

    • In a food processor, pulse together the all-purpose flour, almond flour, granulated sugar, and salt. Add the chilled butter and pulse until the mixture resembles coarse crumbs.
    • In a small bowl, whisk together the egg yolk and ice water. Drizzle the egg mixture into the food processor and pulse until the dough comes together.
    • Turn the dough out onto a lightly floured surface and knead gently to bring it together. Shape the dough into a disk, wrap it in plastic wrap, and refrigerate for at least 1 hour.

  2. Make the Almond Filling:

    • In a mixing bowl, combine the almond flour, granulated sugar, and salt. Add the softened butter and beat until smooth.
    • Beat in the eggs, one at a time, and then stir in the almond extract. Set the almond filling aside.

  3. Prepare the Pears:

    • In a bowl, toss the sliced pears with lemon juice to prevent browning.

  4. Assemble the Tart:

    • Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C). On a lightly floured surface, roll out the chilled dough into a circle about 12 inches in diameter. Transfer the dough to a 9-inch tart pan with a removable bottom, pressing it gently into the corners and trimming any excess.
    • Spread the almond filling evenly over the bottom of the tart crust. Arrange the sliced pears in a decorative pattern over the almond filling.

  5. Bake the Tart:

    • Place the tart on a baking sheet and bake for 40-45 minutes, or until the crust is golden and the filling is set.
    • In a small saucepan, heat the apricot preserves over low heat until melted. Gently brush the warm preserves over the pears to create a beautiful glaze.

  6. Serve and Enjoy:

    • Allow the tart to cool slightly before slicing. Serve the pear and almond tart at room temperature, optionally with a dollop of lightly sweetened whipped cream or a scoop of vanilla ice cream.

Conclusion

This easy pear and almond tart is a delightful combination of flavors and textures, making it the perfect dessert for any occasion. The buttery crust provides a satisfying crunch, the almond filling offers a luxurious nuttiness, and the tender pears add a natural sweetness that’s simply irresistible. Whether you’re an experienced baker or just starting out, this recipe is approachable and rewarding, allowing you to create a stunning dessert that will leave a lasting impression. So, indulge in the decadence of this sophisticated pear and almond tart and treat yourself to a slice of pure bliss.
