Creamy One Pot Chicken and Bacon Pasta


Are you looking for a comforting and satisfying meal that’s easy to make and requires minimal cleanup? Look no further than this creamy one pot chicken and bacon pasta recipe! This dish is a perfect combination of tender chicken, smoky bacon, and creamy pasta that will leave your taste buds singing. Plus, with only one pot to clean, it’s a winner for busy weeknights or lazy weekends. Let’s dive into the details of this mouthwatering recipe.

Ingredients

  • 2 chicken breasts, diced
  • 8 slices of bacon, chopped
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 2 cups of chicken broth
  • 2 cups of heavy cream
  • 12 oz of penne pasta
  • 1 cup of grated Parmesan cheese
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley for garnish

Instructions

  1. Cook the Bacon: In a large pot or deep skillet, cook the chopped bacon over medium heat until it’s crispy. Once done, remove the bacon from the pot and set it aside on a paper towel-lined plate to drain.

  2. Sear the Chicken: In the same pot with the bacon fat, add the diced chicken. Season it with salt and pepper, then cook until it’s golden brown and no longer pink inside. Once cooked, remove the chicken from the pot and set it aside.

  3. Saute the Aromatics: Using the same pot, saute the finely chopped onion and minced garlic until they are soft and fragrant, adding extra flavor to the dish.

  4. Add the Pasta and Liquid: Pour the penne pasta into the pot, followed by the chicken broth and heavy cream. Stir everything together, then bring the mixture to a gentle boil. Reduce the heat and let it simmer, stirring occasionally, until the pasta is tender and the sauce has thickened.

  5. Incorporate the Chicken and Bacon: Once the pasta is cooked, add the cooked chicken and crispy bacon back into the pot, combining them with the creamy pasta.

  6. Finish with Parmesan and Seasoning: Sprinkle the grated Parmesan cheese over the dish and stir it in until it’s melted and the sauce is creamy. Taste the pasta and adjust the seasoning with salt and pepper as needed.

  7. Garnish and Serve: To finish, sprinkle the dish with fresh parsley for a pop of color and added freshness. Serve the creamy one pot chicken and bacon pasta hot, and enjoy the delicious flavors!

Tips for Success

  • Use Quality Ingredients: Since this recipe doesn’t require many ingredients, using high-quality chicken, bacon, and Parmesan cheese will elevate the flavors of the dish.

  • Don’t Overcook the Pasta: Keep an eye on the pasta as it simmers in the creamy sauce to ensure it doesn’t overcook and become mushy. The perfect al dente texture is key to a successful pasta dish.

  • Customize with Vegetables: Feel free to add some vegetables like spinach, peas, or sun-dried tomatoes to add a nutritional and colorful twist to the dish.

  • Make Ahead: This creamy one pot chicken and bacon pasta is great for meal prepping. Simply store any leftovers in an airtight container in the refrigerator for up to 3 days, and reheat it when you’re ready to enjoy it again.

Final Thoughts

Creamy one pot chicken and bacon pasta is a fantastic option for anyone looking for a fuss-free yet indulgent meal. The combination of savory chicken, smoky bacon, and creamy pasta creates a dish that’s both comforting and satisfying. Whether you’re cooking for yourself, your family, or guests, this recipe is sure to impress with its rich flavors and minimal cleanup. Give it a try and experience the joy of a delicious homemade meal without the hassle of multiple pots and pans. Happy cooking!
