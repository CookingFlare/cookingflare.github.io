The Bee's Knees: A Classic Gin Cocktail with a Sweet Honey Infusion


The Bee’s Knees is a classic gin cocktail that combines the refreshing flavor of gin with the sweetness of honey. This cocktail is the perfect balance of citrus and sweetness, making it a delightful drink for any occasion. In this recipe, we will show you how to infuse honey with the flavors of lemon and orange, creating a unique twist on this beloved cocktail.

Ingredients

  • 2 ounces of gin
  • 3/4 ounce of freshly squeezed lemon juice
  • 1/2 ounce of freshly squeezed orange juice
  • 1/2 ounce of honey syrup (made by combining equal parts honey and warm water)

Instructions

  1. In a cocktail shaker, combine the gin, lemon juice, orange juice, and honey syrup.
  2. Add ice to the shaker and shake vigorously for about 15 seconds, allowing the ingredients to blend together and chill.
  3. Strain the mixture into a chilled cocktail glass.
  4. Optional: Garnish with a lemon twist or a slice of orange for an added touch of elegance.
  5. Serve and enjoy!

The Honey Infusion

To take this classic gin cocktail to the next level, we will create a honey infusion that adds a subtle depth of flavor. Here’s how to make it:

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of honey
  • Zest of 1 lemon
  • Zest of 1 orange

Instructions

  1. In a small saucepan, combine the honey, lemon zest, and orange zest.
  2. Heat the mixture over low heat, stirring occasionally, until the honey becomes thin and fragrant. This process should take about 5 minutes.
  3. Remove the saucepan from heat and allow the honey infusion to cool completely.
  4. Strain the honey infusion into a clean glass jar, discarding the lemon and orange zest.
  5. Store the honey infusion in the refrigerator for up to one month.

Why The Bee’s Knees?

The Bee’s Knees has a long history that dates back to the Prohibition era. This classic cocktail gained popularity during a time when the taste of homemade gin was often masked by other ingredients, including honey and citrus. The combination of gin, lemon, and honey creates a harmonious balance of flavors, making it a timeless drink that continues to be enjoyed today.

Tips and Variations

  • Experiment with different types of gin to find your preferred flavor profile. London dry gin is a popular choice, but feel free to try other varieties such as Plymouth or Old Tom gin.
  • For a sweeter version of The Bee’s Knees, you can increase the amount of honey syrup used in the recipe.
  • If you prefer a stronger citrus flavor, adjust the amount of lemon and orange juice to suit your taste.
  • To make a large batch of The Bee’s Knees for a party or gathering, simply multiply the ingredients by the desired number of servings and mix in a pitcher.

Conclusion

The Bee’s Knees is a classic gin cocktail that truly lives up to its name. With its refreshing citrus notes and the subtle sweetness of honey, this drink is a true crowd-pleaser. Whether you’re hosting a cocktail party or simply looking to enjoy a delicious and well-balanced beverage, The Bee’s Knees is a must-try. Cheers!
