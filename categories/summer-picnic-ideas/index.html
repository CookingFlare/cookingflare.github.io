Picnic-Ready Chicken Skewer Recipes

When it comes to picnics, one of the most convenient and delicious dishes to prepare is chicken skewers. These portable and flavorful bites are perfect for outdoor gatherings, offering a convenient way to enjoy a hearty and satisfying meal without the need for utensils or plates. In this blog post, we’ll explore three tantalizing chicken skewer recipes that are sure to elevate your picnic experience. Classic Lemon Herb Chicken Skewers Ingredients: 1.5 lbs boneless, skinless chicken breasts, cut into 1-inch cubes 1/4 cup olive oil 3 tbsp fresh lemon juice 2 cloves garlic, minced 2 tsp fresh thyme, chopped 2 tsp fresh rosemary, chopped Salt and pepper to taste Wooden skewers, soaked in water for 30 minutes Instructions: In a bowl, combine the olive oil, lemon juice, minced garlic, thyme, rosemary, salt, and pepper. Add the chicken cubes to the marinade and toss to coat evenly. Cover and refrigerate for at least 1 hour. Preheat the grill to medium-high heat. Thread the marinated chicken cubes onto the soaked skewers. Grill the skewers for 6-8 minutes, turning occasionally, until the chicken is cooked through and lightly charred. Serve the lemon herb chicken skewers with a fresh green salad and a side of tzatziki for a refreshing picnic meal. Spicy Honey Sriracha Chicken Skewers Ingredients: 1.5 lbs boneless, skinless chicken thighs, cut into 1-inch cubes 1/4 cup soy sauce 3 tbsp honey 2 tbsp Sriracha sauce 2 cloves garlic, minced 1 tsp fresh ginger, grated Wooden skewers, soaked in water for 30 minutes Instructions: In a bowl, whisk together the soy sauce, honey, Sriracha sauce, minced garlic, and grated ginger. Add the chicken cubes to the marinade and let it sit for 30 minutes at room temperature. Preheat the grill to medium-high heat. Thread the marinated chicken cubes onto the soaked skewers. Grill the skewers for 5-7 minutes per side, basting with the remaining marinade, until the chicken is caramelized and cooked through. These spicy honey Sriracha chicken skewers are perfect for those who enjoy a kick of heat in their picnic dishes. Teriyaki Pineapple Chicken Skewers Ingredients: 1.5 lbs boneless, skinless chicken thighs, cut into 1-inch cubes 1 cup teriyaki sauce 1/4 cup pineapple juice 1 red bell pepper, cut into chunks 1 green bell pepper, cut into chunks 1 red onion, cut into chunks Wooden skewers, soaked in water for 30 minutes Instructions: In a bowl, combine the teriyaki sauce and pineapple juice. Add the chicken cubes to the marinade and refrigerate for 2 hours. Preheat the grill to medium heat. Thread the marinated chicken cubes, bell peppers, and red onion onto the soaked skewers, alternating the ingredients. Grill the skewers for 10-12 minutes, turning occasionally, until the chicken is fully cooked and the vegetables are tender-crisp. These teriyaki pineapple chicken skewers bring a sweet and savory flavor to your picnic spread and are a hit with both kids and adults. Whether you prefer the zesty freshness of lemon herb, the fiery kick of spicy honey Sriracha, or the sweet tang of teriyaki pineapple, these chicken skewer recipes are bound to impress at any picnic gathering. Prepare them in advance, pack them up in a secure container, and head outdoors to enjoy a delightful picnic feast with family and friends. ...
