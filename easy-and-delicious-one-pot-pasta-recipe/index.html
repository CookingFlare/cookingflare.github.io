Easy and Delicious One Pot Pasta Recipe


Are you tired of spending hours in the kitchen, cooking and cleaning up after a delicious pasta dinner? Well, I have the perfect solution for you - an easy and delicious one pot pasta recipe! With just a few simple ingredients and a single pot, you can create a mouthwatering pasta dish that will leave your taste buds begging for more. So, let’s get cooking!

Ingredients

  • 1 pound of your favorite pasta
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 bell pepper, diced
  • 1 cup of cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1 cup of sliced mushrooms
  • 2 cups of vegetable or chicken broth
  • 1 cup of heavy cream
  • 1 teaspoon of dried basil
  • 1 teaspoon of dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Grated Parmesan cheese for garnish

Instructions

  1. In a large pot, heat some olive oil over medium heat. Add the chopped onion and minced garlic, and sauté until fragrant and translucent.

  2. Next, add the bell pepper, cherry tomatoes, and mushrooms to the pot. Sauté for a few minutes until the vegetables are slightly softened.

  3. Now it’s time to add the pasta. Pour it into the pot, followed by the vegetable or chicken broth and the heavy cream. Stir everything together to combine.

  4. Season the mixture with dried basil, dried oregano, salt, and pepper. Give it another stir to evenly distribute the flavors.

  5. Bring the pot to a boil, then reduce the heat to low and cover it with a lid. Let the pasta simmer for about 10-12 minutes, or until the pasta is cooked to your desired level of tenderness. Remember to stir occasionally to prevent sticking.

  6. Once the pasta is cooked, remove the pot from the heat. Give it a final stir to incorporate any remaining liquid.

  7. Serve the one pot pasta hot, garnished with grated Parmesan cheese. You can also sprinkle some fresh herbs, such as basil or parsley, for an extra burst of flavor.

Tips and Variations

  • Feel free to customize this recipe with your favorite vegetables, such as zucchini, spinach, or broccoli. Just make sure to adjust the cooking time accordingly.

  • If you prefer a lighter version, you can substitute the heavy cream with half-and-half or milk. The result will still be creamy and delicious.

  • For an added protein boost, you can toss in some cooked chicken, shrimp, or sausage. Simply add them to the pot during the sautéing step.

  • This one pot pasta is great for meal prepping. Cook a large batch and divide it into individual portions for easy lunches or dinners throughout the week.

  • Don’t limit yourself to just Italian flavors. Experiment with different herbs and spices to create unique and exciting flavor combinations. Think Mexican-inspired with cumin and chili powder, or Mediterranean-inspired with lemon zest and olives.

Now that you have this easy and delicious one pot pasta recipe in your arsenal, you can say goodbye to the hassle of multiple pots and pans. With just a single pot and a handful of ingredients, you can whip up a satisfying and flavorful pasta dish in no time. So, get ready to impress your family and friends with your culinary skills, and enjoy a stress-free cooking experience. Happy cooking!
