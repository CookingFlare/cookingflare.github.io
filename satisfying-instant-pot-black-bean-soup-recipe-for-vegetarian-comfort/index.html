Satisfying Instant Pot Black Bean Soup Recipe for Vegetarian Comfort


There’s nothing quite as comforting as a warm bowl of soup on a chilly day. And when it comes to vegetarian comfort food, this Satisfying Instant Pot Black Bean Soup is a winner. Packed with flavor, protein, and nutrients, this recipe will leave you feeling satisfied and nourished. Plus, it’s incredibly easy to make in your Instant Pot, saving you time and effort in the kitchen.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound dried black beans, rinsed and soaked overnight
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 bell pepper, diced
  • 2 carrots, diced
  • 2 stalks celery, diced
  • 1 jalapeno pepper, seeded and diced
  • 1 tablespoon cumin
  • 1 tablespoon chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon smoked paprika
  • 1 teaspoon oregano
  • 4 cups vegetable broth
  • 2 cups water
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • Optional toppings: chopped cilantro, diced avocado, sour cream, lime wedges

Instructions

  1. Drain and rinse the soaked black beans. Set aside.
  2. Select the sauté function on your Instant Pot and heat a tablespoon of oil. Add the diced onion, garlic, bell pepper, carrots, celery, and jalapeno pepper. Sauté for 5 minutes until the vegetables are softened.
  3. Add the cumin, chili powder, smoked paprika, and oregano to the Instant Pot. Stir to coat the vegetables in the spices.
  4. Pour in the vegetable broth and water, followed by the soaked black beans. Give everything a good stir.
  5. Secure the lid on your Instant Pot and set the pressure release valve to the sealing position. Cook on high pressure for 25 minutes.
  6. Once the cooking time is up, allow the pressure to release naturally for 15 minutes, then carefully quick-release any remaining pressure.
  7. Use an immersion blender to partially blend the soup, leaving some whole beans for texture. If you don’t have an immersion blender, carefully transfer a portion of the soup to a blender and blend until smooth, then return it to the pot.
  8. Taste the soup and season with salt and pepper as needed.
  9. Ladle the soup into bowls and top with your favorite garnishes, such as chopped cilantro, diced avocado, sour cream, and a squeeze of lime juice.
  10. Serve hot and enjoy the comforting flavors of this Instant Pot Black Bean Soup!

Why We Love This Recipe

This Satisfying Instant Pot Black Bean Soup is a vegetarian’s dream come true. Not only does it offer a hearty and filling meal, but it’s also packed with plant-based protein from the black beans. The combination of spices, including cumin, chili powder, and smoked paprika, adds a depth of flavor that will keep you coming back for more.

Using the Instant Pot to cook the black beans not only saves you time but also infuses the soup with a rich and satisfying taste. The pressure cooking process helps to break down the beans, resulting in a velvety texture that’s incredibly comforting.

This soup is incredibly versatile. You can enjoy it as a standalone meal, or pair it with a fresh salad or a slice of crusty bread for a more substantial lunch or dinner. The optional toppings, such as chopped cilantro, diced avocado, sour cream, and lime wedges, add a burst of freshness and tanginess that complements the flavors of the soup perfectly.

Whether you’re a vegetarian looking for a comforting and nutritious meal or simply someone who appreciates a good bowl of soup, this Satisfying Instant Pot Black Bean Soup recipe is sure to become a favorite in your kitchen. Give it a try and experience the comfort and satisfaction it brings with every spoonful.
