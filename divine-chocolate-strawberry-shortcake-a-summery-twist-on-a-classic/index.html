Divine Chocolate Strawberry Shortcake: A Summery Twist on a Classic


Indulge in the perfect summer treat with this mouthwatering recipe for Divine Chocolate Strawberry Shortcake. This delightful twist on the classic shortcake combines the rich flavors of chocolate with the refreshing sweetness of strawberries. Whether you’re hosting a backyard barbecue or simply craving a sweet treat, this recipe is sure to impress.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 tablespoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup cold unsalted butter, cubed
  • 1 cup milk
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 2 cups fresh strawberries, hulled and sliced
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 2 cups heavy cream
  • 1/4 cup powdered sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • Chocolate shavings, for garnish

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 425°F (220°C) and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.

  2. In a large mixing bowl, combine the flour, cocoa powder, granulated sugar, baking powder, and salt. Mix well to ensure all the dry ingredients are evenly incorporated.

  3. Add the cold cubed butter to the dry ingredient mixture. Using a pastry cutter or your fingertips, cut the butter into the dry mixture until it resembles coarse crumbs.

  4. Pour in the milk and vanilla extract. Stir until the dough comes together and forms a sticky mass.

  5. Turn the dough out onto a lightly floured surface and knead it gently a few times until it becomes smooth. Be careful not to overwork the dough.

  6. Roll out the dough to about 1/2-inch thickness. Using a round biscuit cutter, cut out individual shortcakes and place them on the prepared baking sheet.

  7. Bake the shortcakes in the preheated oven for 12-15 minutes, or until they are firm to the touch and slightly golden.

  8. While the shortcakes are baking, prepare the strawberry filling. In a medium bowl, combine the sliced strawberries, granulated sugar, and lemon juice. Toss gently to coat the strawberries in the sweet syrup. Let the mixture sit for about 10 minutes to allow the flavors to meld.

  9. In a separate mixing bowl, beat the heavy cream, powdered sugar, and vanilla extract until soft peaks form.

  10. Once the shortcakes have cooled slightly, split them in half horizontally using a serrated knife.

  11. Place a dollop of the whipped cream on the bottom half of each shortcake, followed by a generous spoonful of the strawberry mixture.

  12. Top with the remaining half of the shortcake and garnish with additional whipped cream, strawberry slices, and chocolate shavings.

  13. Serve immediately and enjoy this divine chocolate strawberry shortcake with friends and family!

Conclusion

The Divine Chocolate Strawberry Shortcake is a delightful dessert that combines the richness of chocolate with the freshness of strawberries. The soft and crumbly shortcakes provide the perfect base for the sweet strawberry filling and creamy whipped cream. This summery twist on a classic will surely satisfy your sweet tooth and make any occasion feel special. So, why not treat yourself and your loved ones to this heavenly dessert? Try this recipe today and let the flavors of chocolate and strawberries elevate your summer celebrations!
