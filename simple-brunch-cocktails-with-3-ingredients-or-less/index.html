Simple Brunch Cocktails with 3 Ingredients or Less


Brunch is a delightful occasion that brings friends and family together, offering the perfect opportunity to unwind over delicious food and refreshing drinks. While brunch is often associated with elaborate meals, it can also be a time to enjoy simple, elegant cocktails. The best part? You can whip up these cocktails with just three ingredients or less! In this post, we’ll explore a variety of simple brunch cocktails that are not only easy to make but also pack a punch of flavor. Let’s dive in!

1. Classic Mimosa

Ingredients:

  • Champagne or sparkling wine
  • Fresh orange juice
  • Orange slice (for garnish)

Instructions:

  1. Fill a flute glass halfway with chilled champagne or sparkling wine.
  2. Top off the glass with fresh orange juice.
  3. Garnish with an orange slice for a touch of flair.

The mimosa is a timeless brunch favorite, celebrated for its light and refreshing taste. The combination of bubbly and citrus makes it a perfect complement to any brunch spread. Plus, it’s incredibly easy to adjust the ratios based on personal preference!

2. Bellini

Ingredients:

  • Prosecco
  • Peach puree (fresh or canned)
  • Peach slice (for garnish)

Instructions:

  1. Pour about two parts Prosecco into a flute glass.
  2. Add one part peach puree.
  3. Stir gently and garnish with a slice of peach.

The Bellini, originating from Italy, is a fruity and sophisticated option that’s sure to impress. The sweetness of the peach puree blends beautifully with the sparkling wine, creating a cocktail that feels both indulgent and refreshing.

3. Vodka Cranberry Spritzer

Ingredients:

  • Vodka
  • Cranberry juice
  • Club soda

Instructions:

  1. Fill a glass with ice.
  2. Pour in one part vodka and two parts cranberry juice.
  3. Top with club soda to add fizz and stir gently.

This Vodka Cranberry Spritzer is a lively and vibrant choice for your brunch table. The tartness of the cranberry juice balances perfectly with the smoothness of the vodka, and the club soda adds a refreshing sparkle. It’s a great option if you’re looking for something light and invigorating.

4. Gin and Tonic

Ingredients:

  • Gin
  • Tonic water
  • Lime wedge

Instructions:

  1. Fill a glass with ice.
  2. Pour in one part gin.
  3. Top with tonic water and squeeze in a wedge of lime.

The Gin and Tonic is a classic cocktail that’s both simple and refreshing. The botanical flavors of the gin combined with the crispness of the tonic make it a versatile option that can be enjoyed any time of the day. For an extra twist, try using flavored tonic water!

5. Aperol Spritz

Ingredients:

  • Aperol
  • Prosecco
  • Soda water

Instructions:

  1. Fill a wine glass with ice.
  2. Add three parts Prosecco, two parts Aperol, and a splash of soda water.
  3. Stir gently and garnish with an orange slice.

The Aperol Spritz is a vibrant and trendy cocktail that has gained popularity in recent years. Its bittersweet flavor profile and beautiful orange hue make it an Instagram-worthy addition to your brunch table. It’s light enough to enjoy with your favorite brunch dishes while still providing a refreshing kick.

6. Dark and Stormy

Ingredients:

  • Dark rum
  • Ginger beer
  • Lime wedge

Instructions:

  1. Fill a glass with ice.
  2. Add one part dark rum.
  3. Top with ginger beer and squeeze in a lime wedge.

For those who prefer a bolder flavor, the Dark and Stormy is a fantastic choice. The spiciness of the ginger beer perfectly complements the rich, caramel notes of the dark rum. This cocktail is a great way to add a bit of excitement to your brunch menu!

7. Tequila Sunrise

Ingredients:

  • Tequila
  • Orange juice
  • Grenadine

Instructions:

  1. Fill a glass with ice.
  2. Pour in one part tequila and two parts orange juice.
  3. Slowly drizzle grenadine over the top to create a beautiful sunrise effect.

The Tequila Sunrise is not only visually stunning but also deliciously sweet. The layers of orange juice and grenadine create a striking presentation that will brighten up any brunch gathering. It’s fruity and refreshing, making it a great option for a sunny morning.

8. Whiskey Sour

Ingredients:

  • Whiskey
  • Lemon juice
  • Simple syrup (or sugar)

Instructions:

  1. In a shaker, combine one part whiskey, half part lemon juice, and half part simple syrup.
  2. Shake well and strain into a glass with ice.

The Whiskey Sour is a classic cocktail that brings a punch of flavor and a hint of tartness. It’s simple yet sophisticated, making it a great addition to your brunch lineup. The balance of sweet and sour will appeal to a wide range of palates.

9. Champagne Cocktail

Ingredients:

  • Champagne or sparkling wine
  • Sugar cube
  • Angostura bitters

Instructions:

  1. Place a sugar cube in the bottom of a flute glass.
  2. Douse the sugar cube with a few dashes of Angostura bitters.
  3. Slowly pour champagne or sparkling wine over the sugar cube.

The Champagne Cocktail is an elegant and timeless choice that’s perfect for special occasions. The bitters add a complex flavor to the bubbly, making it feel extra luxurious. Serve this cocktail for a touch of sophistication at your brunch gathering!

10. Pineapple Mule

Ingredients:

  • Vodka
  • Pineapple juice
  • Ginger beer

Instructions:

  1. Fill a glass with ice.
  2. Pour in one part vodka and one part pineapple juice.
  3. Top with ginger beer and stir gently.

The Pineapple Mule is a tropical twist on the classic Moscow Mule. The sweetness of the pineapple juice, combined with the spiciness of the ginger beer, creates a refreshing cocktail that transports you to a beachy paradise. It’s perfect for a laid-back brunch atmosphere.

Conclusion

Brunch cocktails don’t have to be complicated! With just three ingredients or less, you can create a variety of delicious and refreshing drinks that will elevate your brunch experience. Whether you prefer the classic mimosa or are looking to try something new like the Pineapple Mule, these cocktails are sure to please your guests and make your brunch memorable.

Next time you’re planning a brunch gathering, consider these simple yet delightful cocktails. They’re easy to make, taste amazing, and will add a festive touch to your meal. Cheers to simple brunch cocktails that are as enjoyable to make as they are to sip!
