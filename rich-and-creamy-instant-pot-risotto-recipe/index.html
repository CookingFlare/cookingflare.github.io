Rich and Creamy Instant Pot Risotto Recipe


If you’re a fan of creamy, luxurious risotto but don’t have the time to stand by the stove stirring for hours, then this Instant Pot risotto recipe is about to become your new best friend. With the magic of the Instant Pot, you can achieve the same rich and velvety texture of traditional risotto in a fraction of the time and with minimal effort. This recipe is a game-changer for busy weeknights or when you want to impress your guests with a gourmet dish without breaking a sweat.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups Arborio rice
  • 4 cups chicken or vegetable broth
  • 1 cup dry white wine
  • 1 shallot, finely chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • 4 tablespoons unsalted butter
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley, chopped (for garnish)

Instructions

  1. Sauté the Aromatics: Set your Instant Pot to “Sauté” mode and melt 2 tablespoons of butter. Add the chopped shallot and minced garlic, and sauté for 2-3 minutes until they become fragrant.

  2. Toast the Rice: Add the Arborio rice to the pot and stir to coat it with the butter and aromatics. Let it cook for 1-2 minutes, stirring occasionally, until the rice turns slightly translucent.

  3. Deglaze with Wine: Pour in the white wine and stir, scraping the bottom of the pot to release any browned bits. Allow the wine to simmer for a minute or two, allowing the alcohol to cook off.

  4. Pressure Cook: Add the chicken or vegetable broth to the pot and give everything a good stir. Secure the lid on the Instant Pot, set the valve to the sealing position, and cook on high pressure for 6 minutes.

  5. Natural Release: Once the cooking time is up, allow the pressure to release naturally for 5 minutes. Then, carefully release any remaining pressure by turning the valve to the venting position.

  6. Add the Cream and Cheese: Stir in the heavy cream, remaining butter, and grated Parmesan cheese until everything is well combined and the risotto becomes rich and creamy. Season with salt and pepper to taste.

  7. Serve and Garnish: Spoon the Instant Pot risotto onto serving plates, garnish with chopped fresh parsley, and serve immediately.

Tips for Success

  • Use good quality Arborio rice for the best results. Its high starch content is what gives risotto its signature creaminess.
  • Don’t skip the step of toasting the rice before pressure cooking. This helps to create a nutty flavor and ensures the grains maintain their structure during cooking.
  • Experiment with different add-ins such as sautéed mushrooms, crispy pancetta, or fresh herbs to customize your risotto to your liking.

Final Thoughts

With this Instant Pot risotto recipe, you can enjoy the decadent flavors and creamy texture of traditional risotto without the time-consuming stirring process. Whether you’re cooking for your family or hosting a dinner party, this rich and creamy dish is sure to impress. Embrace the convenience of the Instant Pot and elevate your cooking game with this foolproof risotto recipe.
