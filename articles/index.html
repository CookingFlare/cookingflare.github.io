Flavor-Packed Low Carb Greek Salad with Grilled Chicken

Are you looking for a delicious and healthy meal that will satisfy your taste buds without compromising your low carb diet? Look no further than this Flavor-Packed Low Carb Greek Salad with Grilled Chicken recipe! Packed with fresh ingredients and bursting with Mediterranean flavors, this salad is a surefire way to elevate your lunch or dinner. Ingredients 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts 2 tablespoons olive oil 2 teaspoons dried oregano Salt and pepper to taste 4 cups mixed salad greens 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved 1 cup cucumber, diced 1/2 cup red onion, thinly sliced 1/2 cup Kalamata olives, pitted and halved 1/2 cup crumbled feta cheese Directions Preheat your grill to medium-high heat. Season the chicken breasts with olive oil, dried oregano, salt, and pepper. Make sure to coat both sides evenly. Grill the chicken for about 6-8 minutes per side, or until the internal temperature reaches 165°F (74°C). Once cooked, remove from the grill and let it rest for a few minutes before slicing. While the chicken is resting, prepare the salad by combining the mixed salad greens, cherry tomatoes, cucumber, red onion, Kalamata olives, and crumbled feta cheese in a large bowl. Slice the grilled chicken breasts into thin strips and add them to the salad bowl. Toss the salad gently to combine all the ingredients and distribute the flavors evenly. Serve immediately and enjoy the explosion of flavors in every bite! Why Choose This Salad?
