Sizzling Spicy Garlic Butter Shrimp Pasta Recipe


If you’re a fan of bold flavors and crave a dish that will tantalize your taste buds, then this sizzling spicy garlic butter shrimp pasta is the perfect recipe for you. This mouthwatering dish combines succulent shrimp, al dente pasta, and a spicy garlic butter sauce that will leave you craving for more. Whether you’re cooking for a special occasion or simply treating yourself to a delicious meal, this recipe is sure to impress. Let’s dive into the details and get cooking!

Ingredients

  • 1 pound of large shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 12 ounces of linguine pasta
  • 4 tablespoons of unsalted butter
  • 4 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon of red pepper flakes (adjust to taste)
  • 1/2 cup of chicken broth
  • 1/4 cup of dry white wine
  • 1/4 cup of freshly chopped parsley
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • Grated Parmesan cheese for garnish

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Shrimp: In a large skillet, melt 2 tablespoons of butter over medium-high heat. Add the shrimp to the skillet and season with salt and black pepper. Sauté the shrimp for 2-3 minutes on each side until they are pink and opaque. Once cooked, transfer the shrimp to a plate and set aside.

  2. Cook the Pasta: In a large pot of salted boiling water, cook the linguine pasta according to the package instructions until al dente. Once cooked, drain the pasta and set it aside.

  3. Make the Spicy Garlic Butter Sauce: In the same skillet used to cook the shrimp, melt the remaining 2 tablespoons of butter over medium heat. Add the minced garlic and red pepper flakes to the skillet, and sauté for 1-2 minutes until the garlic becomes fragrant. Be careful not to let the garlic brown.

  4. Deglaze the Pan: Pour the white wine and chicken broth into the skillet, stirring to combine all the flavors and to deglaze the pan. Allow the sauce to simmer for 3-4 minutes until it reduces slightly.

  5. Combine the Pasta and Shrimp: Add the cooked pasta and shrimp back to the skillet with the spicy garlic butter sauce. Toss everything together until the pasta is evenly coated with the sauce and the shrimp are heated through.

  6. Finish and Serve: Sprinkle the freshly chopped parsley over the pasta and toss to incorporate. Taste and adjust the seasoning if necessary. Serve the sizzling spicy garlic butter shrimp pasta in individual bowls, garnished with grated Parmesan cheese.

Tips for Success

  • For an extra kick of heat, increase the amount of red pepper flakes in the sauce.
  • Use fresh and high-quality shrimp for the best flavor and texture.
  • If you prefer a thicker sauce, you can add a tablespoon of heavy cream at the end and simmer for an additional 2-3 minutes.

Final Thoughts

This sizzling spicy garlic butter shrimp pasta recipe is a delightful fusion of flavors that will elevate your dinner table to new heights. The succulent shrimp, perfectly cooked pasta, and the fiery garlic butter sauce come together to create a dish that is both comforting and indulgent. Whether you’re a seafood lover or simply enjoy a bit of spice in your meals, this recipe is guaranteed to become a favorite in your culinary repertoire. So, gather your ingredients, roll up your sleeves, and get ready to savor every sizzling bite of this delectable dish!
