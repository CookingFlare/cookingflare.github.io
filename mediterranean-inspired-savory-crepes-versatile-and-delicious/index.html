Mediterranean-Inspired Savory Crepes: Versatile and Delicious


When it comes to Mediterranean cuisine, the flavors are vibrant, the ingredients are fresh, and the dishes are versatile. One such dish that perfectly embodies the essence of Mediterranean cooking is savory crepes. These delicate, thin pancakes are not only incredibly versatile but also a delightful canvas for a wide range of Mediterranean-inspired fillings. In this blog post, we’ll explore the art of making savory crepes and some mouthwatering Mediterranean-inspired fillings that will elevate your crepe game to a whole new level.

The Perfect Savory Crepe Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 1/2 cups milk
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 tablespoons melted butter
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley (optional)
  • Olive oil for cooking

Instructions:

  1. In a large mixing bowl, whisk together the flour, milk, eggs, melted butter, salt, and pepper until the batter is smooth. If desired, add the chopped fresh parsley for an extra burst of flavor.
  2. Heat a non-stick skillet over medium heat and lightly brush it with olive oil.
  3. Pour a small ladleful of the batter into the skillet, swirling it around to spread the batter evenly.
  4. Cook the crepe for about 2 minutes, until the edges start to lift from the pan and the bottom is golden brown.
  5. Carefully flip the crepe using a spatula and cook the other side for an additional 1-2 minutes.
  6. Transfer the cooked crepe to a plate and repeat the process with the remaining batter.

Now that you have mastered the art of making the perfect savory crepes, it’s time to explore some delectable Mediterranean-inspired fillings to take your crepes to the next level.

Mediterranean-Inspired Savory Crepe Fillings

1. Spinach and Feta Filling

  • Sauté fresh spinach with a touch of olive oil, garlic, and a pinch of nutmeg until wilted.
  • Fill the crepes with the sautéed spinach and crumbled feta cheese.
  • Fold the crepes and serve with a dollop of Greek yogurt on top.

2. Mediterranean Vegetable and Hummus Filling

  • Roast a mixture of colorful Mediterranean vegetables such as bell peppers, zucchini, and eggplant with a drizzle of olive oil and a sprinkle of oregano.
  • Spread a generous layer of hummus on the crepes and top with the roasted vegetables.
  • Roll up the crepes and garnish with a sprinkle of fresh parsley.

3. Lemon Garlic Shrimp Filling

  • Sauté shrimp with minced garlic, lemon zest, and a splash of white wine until the shrimp are pink and cooked through.
  • Place the lemon garlic shrimp in the center of the crepes and fold them into neat parcels.
  • Drizzle with a squeeze of fresh lemon juice before serving.

4. Sun-Dried Tomato and Pesto Filling

  • Spread a layer of basil pesto on the crepes and sprinkle with chopped sun-dried tomatoes.
  • Add a handful of baby arugula for a peppery kick and roll up the crepes.
  • Serve with an extra drizzle of pesto on top for an added burst of flavor.

5. Greek Chicken Souvlaki Filling

  • Toss grilled chicken pieces in a mixture of Greek yogurt, lemon juice, garlic, and oregano.
  • Fill the crepes with the marinated chicken and top with diced tomatoes, cucumbers, and a sprinkle of crumbled feta cheese.
  • Fold the crepes and serve with a side of tzatziki sauce.

With these tantalizing Mediterranean-inspired savory crepe fillings, you can effortlessly bring the flavors of the Mediterranean to your table. Whether you’re hosting a brunch, preparing a light lunch, or indulging in a flavorful dinner, these savory crepes are sure to impress your taste buds and those of your loved ones.

So, the next time you’re craving a taste of the Mediterranean, whip up a batch of savory crepes and let your creativity run wild with these delightful fillings. Bon appétit!
