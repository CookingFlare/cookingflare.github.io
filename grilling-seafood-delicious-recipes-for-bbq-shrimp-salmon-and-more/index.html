Grilling Seafood: Delicious Recipes for BBQ Shrimp, Salmon, and More


Grilling seafood is a fantastic way to add a touch of smoky flavor and enhance the natural flavors of fish and shellfish. Whether you’re hosting a backyard barbecue or simply craving a delicious seafood meal, these recipes are sure to satisfy your cravings. From succulent BBQ shrimp to perfectly grilled salmon, let’s dive into some mouthwatering seafood recipes that will elevate your grilling game.

Grilled BBQ Shrimp Skewers

If you’re a fan of shrimp, you’re in for a treat with these delectable BBQ shrimp skewers. With a tangy and slightly sweet marinade, these skewers are bursting with flavor. Here’s how you can prepare them:

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound of large shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • ¼ cup of olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons of honey
  • 2 tablespoons of soy sauce
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon of paprika
  • ½ teaspoon of cayenne pepper (adjust according to your spice preference)
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • Bamboo skewers, soaked in water for 30 minutes

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, whisk together the olive oil, honey, soy sauce, minced garlic, paprika, cayenne pepper, salt, and black pepper.
  2. Add the peeled and deveined shrimp to the marinade, ensuring they are well coated. Let them marinate for at least 30 minutes in the refrigerator.
  3. Preheat your grill to medium-high heat.
  4. Thread the marinated shrimp onto the soaked bamboo skewers.
  5. Place the skewers on the preheated grill and cook for 2-3 minutes per side, or until the shrimp turn pink and opaque.
  6. Remove the skewers from the grill and serve hot. Garnish with chopped parsley, if desired.

Grilled Salmon with Lemon and Dill

Salmon is a popular choice for grilling due to its rich, buttery flavor and tender flesh. This recipe combines the freshness of lemon and the aromatic dill herb to create a truly delightful dish. Let’s get started:

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound of salmon fillets
  • 2 lemons, sliced
  • 2 tablespoons of fresh dill, chopped
  • 2 tablespoons of olive oil
  • Salt and black pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your grill to medium heat.
  2. Brush the salmon fillets with olive oil and season them with salt and black pepper.
  3. Place the salmon fillets on a piece of aluminum foil or a grilling mat.
  4. Lay the lemon slices on top of the salmon fillets and sprinkle them with chopped dill.
  5. Carefully transfer the foil or grilling mat with the salmon onto the preheated grill.
  6. Close the grill and cook for approximately 10-12 minutes, or until the salmon is cooked through and flakes easily with a fork.
  7. Remove the salmon from the grill and serve hot. Squeeze fresh lemon juice over the top for an extra burst of flavor.

Grilled Garlic Butter Lobster Tails

If you’re looking to indulge in a luxurious seafood treat, grilled garlic butter lobster tails are the way to go. The succulent lobster meat combined with the rich garlic butter creates a truly unforgettable dish. Here’s how you can prepare it:

Ingredients:

  • 4 lobster tails
  • ½ cup of unsalted butter, melted
  • 4 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons of fresh parsley, chopped
  • Salt and black pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your grill to medium-high heat.
  2. Using kitchen shears, carefully cut the top shell of each lobster tail lengthwise.
  3. Gently lift the meat out of the shell, keeping it attached at the base.
  4. In a small bowl, mix together the melted butter, minced garlic, chopped parsley, salt, and black pepper.
  5. Brush the garlic butter mixture onto the exposed lobster meat.
  6. Place the lobster tails on the preheated grill, meat side down.
  7. Grill for approximately 5-7 minutes, or until the lobster meat is opaque and cooked through.
  8. Carefully remove the lobster tails from the grill and serve hot. Drizzle with additional garlic butter, if desired.

Conclusion

Grilling seafood is a fantastic way to bring out the best flavors in fish and shellfish. Whether you choose to grill shrimp, salmon, lobster, or any other seafood, these recipes are sure to please your taste buds. Experiment with different marinades, herbs, and spices to create your own unique grilled seafood creations. So fire up your grill, gather your favorite seafood, and get ready to enjoy a delicious and memorable meal. Happy grilling!
