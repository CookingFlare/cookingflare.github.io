BBQ Chicken Stir-Fry: Quick and Easy Recipes for a Grilled Dinner


Looking for a delicious and easy grilled dinner idea? Look no further than this BBQ Chicken Stir-Fry recipe! It’s the perfect combination of smoky, savory, and sweet flavors, all coming together in a quick and easy stir-fry. Whether you’re hosting a backyard barbecue or just craving a flavorful meal, this recipe is sure to be a hit. Let’s dive into the details of creating this mouthwatering dish.

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound boneless, skinless chicken breasts, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 1 red bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 green bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 small onion, sliced
  • 1 cup broccoli florets
  • 1 cup snap peas
  • 1 cup pineapple chunks (fresh or canned)
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Cooked rice or quinoa for serving

For the BBQ Sauce:

  • 1 cup ketchup
  • 1/4 cup brown sugar
  • 2 tablespoons apple cider vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 teaspoon smoked paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon onion powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a small bowl, whisk together all the BBQ sauce ingredients until well combined. Set aside.

  2. Heat the olive oil in a large skillet or wok over medium-high heat. Add the chicken pieces and season with salt and pepper. Cook for 5-7 minutes, or until the chicken is cooked through and nicely browned. Remove the chicken from the skillet and set aside.

  3. In the same skillet, add a little more oil if needed, then add the sliced bell peppers, onion, broccoli, snap peas, and garlic. Stir-fry for 5-7 minutes, or until the vegetables are tender-crisp.

  4. Return the cooked chicken to the skillet and add the pineapple chunks. Pour the BBQ sauce over the chicken and vegetables, stirring to coat everything evenly. Cook for an additional 2-3 minutes, allowing the sauce to thicken and the flavors to meld together.

  5. Once everything is heated through and well coated with the BBQ sauce, remove the skillet from the heat.

  6. Serve the BBQ chicken stir-fry over cooked rice or quinoa, and garnish with chopped green onions or sesame seeds for an extra pop of flavor.

Tips and Tricks:

  • For a spicier kick, add a dash of hot sauce or a pinch of red pepper flakes to the BBQ sauce.
  • Feel free to customize the vegetables in this stir-fry based on your preferences or what’s in season.
  • If you’re using wooden skewers for grilling, soak them in water for at least 30 minutes before threading the chicken and vegetables to prevent them from burning.

Final Thoughts:

This BBQ Chicken Stir-Fry is a fantastic way to enjoy the flavors of a barbecue in a quick and easy stir-fry format. The smoky sweetness of the BBQ sauce pairs perfectly with the tender chicken and vibrant vegetables, creating a dish that’s both satisfying and full of flavor. Whether you’re grilling outdoors or cooking indoors, this recipe is a must-try for anyone who loves the taste of grilled goodness. So fire up the grill or heat up the skillet, and get ready to enjoy a delightful BBQ chicken stir-fry!
