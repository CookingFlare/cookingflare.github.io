Tasty Slow Cooker Honey Garlic Chicken Recipe


Are you looking for a delicious and hassle-free dinner option that will leave your taste buds tingling? Look no further than this mouthwatering Tasty Slow Cooker Honey Garlic Chicken recipe. This recipe is not only incredibly flavorful but also incredibly easy to prepare, making it the perfect choice for busy weeknights or lazy weekends.

Ingredients:

  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1/2 cup honey
  • 1/2 cup low-sodium soy sauce
  • 1/4 cup hoisin sauce
  • 2 tablespoons ketchup
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1/4 teaspoon red pepper flakes (optional)
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • 2 tablespoons cornstarch
  • 3 tablespoons water
  • Sesame seeds and chopped green onions for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Begin by placing the chicken breasts in the slow cooker. Season them with salt and black pepper to taste.

  2. In a mixing bowl, whisk together the honey, soy sauce, hoisin sauce, ketchup, minced garlic, dried basil, dried oregano, and red pepper flakes (if using).

  3. Pour this flavorful mixture over the chicken in the slow cooker, ensuring that the chicken is evenly coated with the sauce.

  4. Cover the slow cooker and cook the chicken on low heat for 3-4 hours or on high heat for 1.5-2 hours. The chicken should be tender and thoroughly cooked through.

  5. Once the chicken is cooked, carefully remove it from the slow cooker and transfer it to a serving platter. Keep the slow cooker on.

  6. In a small bowl, mix the cornstarch and water together to create a slurry. Pour this slurry into the sauce in the slow cooker, stirring well to combine.

  7. Cover the slow cooker again and cook the sauce on high heat for 15-20 minutes, or until it has thickened to your desired consistency.

  8. While the sauce is thickening, use two forks to shred the cooked chicken.

  9. Once the sauce has thickened, return the shredded chicken to the slow cooker and toss it in the thickened honey garlic sauce until it is well coated.

  10. Serve the Tasty Slow Cooker Honey Garlic Chicken over a bed of fluffy white rice, garnished with sesame seeds and chopped green onions for an extra pop of flavor and color.

Tips and Tricks:

  • For an extra burst of freshness, squeeze some fresh lemon juice over the chicken just before serving.
  • If you prefer a spicier kick, increase the amount of red pepper flakes or add a dash of hot sauce to the sauce mixture.
  • Leftovers can be stored in an airtight container in the refrigerator for up to 3 days, and they make for a delicious lunch the next day.

This Tasty Slow Cooker Honey Garlic Chicken recipe is sure to become a regular in your dinner rotation. It’s the perfect combination of sweet and savory, and the tender, juicy chicken practically melts in your mouth. Plus, the convenience of using a slow cooker means you can come home to a house filled with the irresistible aroma of this delectable dish. So, gather your ingredients, set up your slow cooker, and get ready to enjoy a meal that will have everyone asking for seconds!
