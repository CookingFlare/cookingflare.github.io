Hearty Low Carb Beef and Brussels Sprouts Skillet


When it comes to hearty and healthy meals, this Low Carb Beef and Brussels Sprouts Skillet recipe is a game-changer. Packed with protein, fiber, and essential nutrients, this dish is not only delicious but also perfect for those looking to maintain a low-carb lifestyle. The combination of tender beef, flavorful Brussels sprouts, and aromatic seasonings makes this skillet a standout addition to your weekly meal plan. Let’s dive into the details of this satisfying and nutritious recipe.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound of lean ground beef
  • 1 tablespoon of olive oil
  • 1 medium onion, diced
  • 3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 pound of Brussels sprouts, trimmed and halved
  • 1 teaspoon of paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon of dried thyme
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1/2 cup of beef broth
  • 1 tablespoon of Worcestershire sauce
  • Fresh parsley for garnish

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Ingredients: Start by gathering and preparing all the ingredients. Trim and halve the Brussels sprouts, dice the onion, and mince the garlic. Having everything ready will make the cooking process smooth and enjoyable.

  2. Brown the Ground Beef: Heat a large skillet over medium-high heat and add the lean ground beef. Break it up with a spoon and cook until it’s browned and no longer pink. Once cooked, transfer the beef to a plate and set it aside.

  3. Sauté the Aromatics: In the same skillet, add a tablespoon of olive oil and sauté the diced onion and minced garlic until they become fragrant and translucent. This will create a flavorful base for the dish.

  4. Add the Brussels Sprouts and Seasonings: Incorporate the halved Brussels sprouts into the skillet and sprinkle them with paprika, dried thyme, salt, and pepper. Stir the ingredients together, allowing the Brussels sprouts to absorb the aromatic flavors.

  5. Simmer with Broth and Worcestershire Sauce: Pour in the beef broth and Worcestershire sauce, stirring gently to combine everything. Let the mixture simmer for a few minutes, allowing the Brussels sprouts to become tender while soaking up the savory liquid.

  6. Combine with Cooked Ground Beef: Once the Brussels sprouts are tender to your liking, reintroduce the cooked ground beef back into the skillet. Stir everything together, ensuring that the flavors meld beautifully.

  7. Garnish and Serve: Before serving, garnish the skillet with freshly chopped parsley for a pop of color and added freshness. This hearty and low-carb beef and Brussels sprouts skillet is ready to be enjoyed.

Nutritional Benefits

This recipe is not only a treat for your taste buds but also a nutritious addition to your diet. Brussels sprouts are a rich source of vitamins K and C, as well as fiber, while lean ground beef provides high-quality protein. The combination of these ingredients makes this skillet a well-rounded meal that will leave you feeling satisfied and energized.

Conclusion

In conclusion, this Hearty Low Carb Beef and Brussels Sprouts Skillet recipe is a must-try for anyone seeking a flavorful, low-carb dish that doesn’t compromise on heartiness. Whether you’re following a low-carb diet or simply looking for a delicious way to incorporate more vegetables and lean protein into your meals, this recipe has you covered. The simplicity of preparation, the robust flavors, and the nutritional benefits make this skillet a winner in every aspect. Give it a try and savor the wholesome goodness of this satisfying meal.

So there you have it, a delightful and nutritious Low Carb Beef and Brussels Sprouts Skillet recipe that’s sure to become a staple in your culinary repertoire. Enjoy!
