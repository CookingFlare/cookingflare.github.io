Irresistible Bacon-Wrapped Tater Tots: A Crowd-Pleasing Snack


Who doesn’t love the combination of crispy bacon and perfectly cooked tater tots? This recipe takes the classic tater tots to a whole new level by wrapping them in delicious bacon. Irresistible and easy to make, these bacon-wrapped tater tots are sure to be a hit at your next gathering. Whether you’re hosting a party, game night, or simply enjoying a movie marathon, this crowd-pleasing snack will leave everyone wanting more.

Ingredients

  • 1 package of frozen tater tots
  • 1 pound of bacon
  • 1/2 cup of brown sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon of garlic powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon of paprika
  • 1/4 teaspoon of black pepper
  • Toothpicks

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C). Line a baking sheet with parchment paper or aluminum foil for easy cleanup.

  2. In a shallow dish, combine the brown sugar, garlic powder, paprika, and black pepper. Mix well to ensure the spices are evenly distributed.

  3. Take a slice of bacon and cut it in half. Wrap each tater tot with a half slice of bacon, securing it with a toothpick. Repeat until all the tater tots are wrapped.

  4. Roll each bacon-wrapped tater tot in the brown sugar mixture, coating it evenly. Place the coated tater tots on the prepared baking sheet, leaving space between each one to allow for even cooking.

  5. Bake the bacon-wrapped tater tots in the preheated oven for 20-25 minutes, or until the bacon is crispy and the tater tots are golden brown. Remember to flip them halfway through cooking to ensure they cook evenly.

  6. Once cooked, remove the bacon-wrapped tater tots from the oven and let them cool slightly before serving. Remove the toothpicks before serving to avoid any accidents.

  7. Serve the irresistible bacon-wrapped tater tots as a standalone snack or with your favorite dipping sauce. They pair perfectly with ranch dressing, ketchup, or even a spicy sriracha mayo.

  8. Enjoy the crunchy exterior of the bacon, the softness of the tater tots, and the burst of flavors in every bite. These crowd-pleasing snacks will disappear fast, so make sure to make plenty!

Tips and Variations

  • For a spicy twist, add a pinch of cayenne pepper or chili powder to the brown sugar mixture.
  • Experiment with different types of bacon, such as maple-flavored or applewood-smoked, to add extra depth of flavor.
  • If you want to make these bacon-wrapped tater tots in advance, you can assemble them up to a day ahead and refrigerate them. Just pop them in the oven when you’re ready to serve.
  • This recipe can easily be doubled or tripled to accommodate larger gatherings. Just make sure to adjust the cooking time accordingly.
  • If you’re looking for a vegetarian alternative, you can use vegetarian bacon or even strips of roasted bell peppers instead.

Conclusion

Bacon-wrapped tater tots are a surefire way to impress your guests and keep them coming back for more. With their crispy bacon exterior and fluffy tater tot interior, these snacks are simply irresistible. Whether you’re entertaining a crowd or enjoying a cozy night in, these crowd-pleasing treats are guaranteed to be a hit. So go ahead, whip up a batch of these bacon-wrapped tater tots, and get ready to be the star of the party!
