One Pot Lemon Garlic Butter Salmon and Broccoli


If you’re looking for a quick, flavorful, and healthy dinner option, look no further than this One Pot Lemon Garlic Butter Salmon and Broccoli recipe. It’s a perfect dish for those busy weeknights when you want something delicious without spending hours in the kitchen. Not only is this recipe easy to make, but it also delivers a burst of fresh flavors that will leave your taste buds delighted.

Ingredients

  • 4 salmon fillets
  • 2 cups broccoli florets
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/4 cup chicken or vegetable broth
  • 3 tablespoons unsalted butter
  • 2 tablespoons fresh lemon juice
  • 1 teaspoon lemon zest
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley for garnish

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Salmon: Season the salmon fillets with salt and pepper on both sides. Set them aside while you prepare the rest of the ingredients.

  2. Sauté the Garlic: In a large skillet or pan, melt 2 tablespoons of butter over medium heat. Add the minced garlic and sauté for about 1 minute until fragrant.

  3. Cook the Salmon: Place the seasoned salmon fillets in the skillet, skin side down. Cook for 3-4 minutes until the skin is crispy and golden. Then, flip the fillets and cook for an additional 2-3 minutes until the salmon is just cooked through. Remove the salmon from the skillet and set it aside.

  4. Prepare the Broccoli: In the same skillet, add the broccoli florets and the remaining tablespoon of butter. Sauté the broccoli for 2-3 minutes until it starts to become tender.

  5. Combine the Ingredients: Pour the chicken or vegetable broth into the skillet with the broccoli. Add the lemon juice and zest, then season with a pinch of salt and pepper. Stir to combine all the flavors.

  6. Simmer and Finish: Place the cooked salmon back into the skillet, nestled among the broccoli. Let the entire dish simmer for 2-3 minutes, allowing the flavors to meld together.

  7. Serve: Once the salmon is heated through and the broccoli is tender yet crisp, remove the skillet from the heat. Garnish with fresh parsley and an extra drizzle of lemon juice if desired.

Tips and Tricks

  • Freshness is Key: When it comes to salmon, always opt for the freshest fillets you can find. This ensures that your dish will have the best flavor and texture.
  • Don’t Overcook the Salmon: Salmon can quickly become dry if overcooked, so keep an eye on it while it’s in the skillet.
  • Customize Your Veggies: Feel free to add other vegetables like cherry tomatoes, bell peppers, or asparagus to the dish for extra color and nutrients.

This One Pot Lemon Garlic Butter Salmon and Broccoli dish is not only a feast for the taste buds but also a feast for the eyes. The vibrant colors of the salmon and broccoli, combined with the zesty aroma of lemon and garlic, create a meal that’s as visually appealing as it is delicious. Plus, with the convenience of only using one pot, cleanup is a breeze, making this recipe a win-win for any home cook.

So, the next time you’re in need of a hassle-free yet impressive meal, give this recipe a try. Whether you’re cooking for yourself, your family, or even for guests, this One Pot Lemon Garlic Butter Salmon and Broccoli is sure to become a go-to favorite in your recipe repertoire. Enjoy!
