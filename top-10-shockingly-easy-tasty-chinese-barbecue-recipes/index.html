Top 10 Shockingly Easy & Tasty Chinese Barbecue Recipes


Barbecue food in China might not be as popular as in the West, but that doesn’t mean Chinese cuisine isn’t filled with an array of delicious and flavorful barbecue dishes. Thanks to their unique marinating techniques, spices, and cooking methods, Chinese food offers a scrumptious variety of dishes that can satiate your taste buds. In this blog post, we’ll explore ten of the most easy-to-make and mouthwatering Chinese barbecue recipes, putting your nearest take-out menu to shame!

  1. Hakka-styled Barbecued Chicken What could be more appetizing than tender, juicy chicken with delectable flavors impregnated through every fiber? This Hakka-inspired recipe features a mixture of dark soy sauce, hoisin sauce, and red fermented bean curd for an electric burst of taste. All you need is some chicken drumsticks or thighs, and a few hours to marinate before getting them grilled for a perfect barbecue dinner.

  2. Mapo Tofu Skewers Love tofu or not, Mapo Tofu skewers are not to be missed. Made with the famous Sichuan dish’s secret ingredients—doubanjiang (Chili Bean Paste), fermenting beans, and tofu blocks—these skewers will be a knockout hit at your barbecue party. Mix the tofu with the paste and let them marinate for a few hours, then throw them on the grill for some smoky flavors.

  3. Chinese Barbecue Pork (Char Siu) Char Siu is a classic Chinese dish that everybody should know how to make. Thick slices of tender pork belly are marinated in a blend of soy sauce, honey, and Five-Spice powder, which contributes to a sweet, tangy, and aromatic sauce. Baste the pork with this delectable marinade and let them cook until perfectly soft and tender over the grill.

  4. Spicy Sichuan Pork skewers Loaded with flavors from Sichuan spices, this dish is as hot as it’s tasty! Made with ground pork, dried chilies, Sichuan peppercorns, and other mouth-watering spices, this skewer recipe will give your taste buds a journey they’ll never forget. Enjoy these fiery and delectable treats on top of a bed of freshly steamed rice.

  5. Shanghai Smoked Fish Bring a taste of Shanghai to your backyard: this unique barbecued smoked fish recipe takes smoky flavors to another level. A well-cured fish belly, marinated with soy sauce, sugar, and kelp, will give you a sumptuous smoked delicacy, perfect for seafood lovers.

  6. Chinese Style Beef Satay Impress your friends with this upscale twist on a classic Southeast Asian dish: Chinese Beef Satay. Tender chunks of beef are marinated in a mixture of soy sauce, oyster sauce, and sesame oil, and then grilled until just right. Finally, serve them with Soya sauce or Hoisin sauce for additional taste.

  7. Tangyuan (Glazed Sesame Balls) Dessert lovers will be blown away by this delicious Chinese delicacy. Tangyuan is a sweet dumpling filled with sweet red bean paste and coated with sesame seeds. After boiling or frying, the snack gets a delicious glaze made from sugar, honey, and sesame oil. Warm and satisfying, these dessert skewers are a delectable finish to any barbecue feast.

  8. Lamb Kebabs with Hoisin Sauce Spicy, bold, and incredibly delicious, these grilled lamb skewers marinated in hoisin sauce are a must-try for meat lovers. Their succulent texture and unique flavor, combined with the smoky touch of the grill, make it a crowd-pleaser at any barbecue event.

  9. Barbecued Pork Ribs in Honey-Grape Sauce Perfect for a summer barbecue, these slightly sweet, succulent pork ribs are cooked in a luscious grape and honey sauce. The combination yields an excellent amalgamation of sweet and savory flavors that dance in your mouth and make every bite unforgettable.

  10. Shrimp Skewers with Spicy Salt and Pepper Close your barbecue event on a high note - zesty, crunchy, and packed with flavors, these shrimp skewers marry perfectly cooked shrimp with a mix of salt, pepper, and aromatics, such as garlic and onion powder. These simple yet satisfying skewers would make any barbecue summer a success.

Conclusion

That wraps up our list of ten deliciously easy and savory Chinese barbecue recipes. No more summer BBQs can proceed without a little twist of Chinese inspiration. Enjoy your backyard grilling with these mouthwatering, authentic dishes that will have your taste buds thanking you.
