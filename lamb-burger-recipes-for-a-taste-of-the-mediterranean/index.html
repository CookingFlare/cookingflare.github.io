Lamb Burger Recipes for a Taste of the Mediterranean


If you’re tired of the same old beef burgers and want to add a twist to your grilling game, look no further than lamb burgers. Bursting with flavor and reminiscent of the Mediterranean, these juicy and aromatic burgers will transport your taste buds to a whole new level.

Classic Greek Lamb Burger

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound ground lamb
  • 1/4 cup finely chopped red onion
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • 4 burger buns
  • Toppings: crumbled feta cheese, sliced cucumber, sliced red onion, lettuce, tzatziki sauce

Instructions:

  1. In a mixing bowl, combine the ground lamb, red onion, garlic, oregano, cumin, salt, and pepper.
  2. Gently shape the mixture into four patties, making sure not to overwork the meat.
  3. Preheat your grill or skillet over medium-high heat.
  4. Cook the lamb burgers for about 4-5 minutes per side, or until they reach your desired level of doneness.
  5. Toast the burger buns on the grill or in a toaster.
  6. Assemble the burgers by placing the cooked lamb patties on the buns and topping them with crumbled feta cheese, sliced cucumber, red onion, lettuce, and a generous dollop of tzatziki sauce.
  7. Serve immediately and enjoy the burst of Mediterranean flavors!

Moroccan Spiced Lamb Burger

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound ground lamb
  • 1/4 cup finely chopped red bell pepper
  • 1/4 cup finely chopped fresh cilantro
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon ground coriander
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • 4 burger buns
  • Toppings: sliced tomato, sliced red onion, harissa mayo, fresh mint leaves

Instructions:

  1. In a mixing bowl, combine the ground lamb, red bell pepper, cilantro, garlic, cumin, coriander, cinnamon, salt, and pepper.
  2. Shape the mixture into four patties, ensuring they are evenly sized for even cooking.
  3. Preheat your grill or skillet over medium-high heat.
  4. Cook the lamb burgers for about 4-5 minutes per side, or until they are cooked to your liking.
  5. Toast the burger buns on the grill or in a toaster.
  6. Assemble the burgers by placing the cooked lamb patties on the buns and topping them with sliced tomato, red onion, a dollop of harissa mayo, and fresh mint leaves.
  7. Serve immediately and savor the exotic blend of Moroccan spices.

Mediterranean Lamb Burger with Tzatziki Sauce

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound ground lamb
  • 1/4 cup finely chopped fresh parsley
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cumin
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • 4 burger buns
  • Toppings: sliced tomato, sliced red onion, lettuce, tzatziki sauce

Instructions:

  1. In a mixing bowl, combine the ground lamb, parsley, garlic, oregano, cumin, salt, and pepper.
  2. Shape the mixture into four patties, ensuring they are compact and hold their shape.
  3. Preheat your grill or skillet over medium-high heat.
  4. Cook the lamb burgers for about 4-5 minutes per side, or until they are cooked to your desired level of doneness.
  5. Toast the burger buns on the grill or in a toaster.
  6. Assemble the burgers by placing the cooked lamb patties on the buns and topping them with sliced tomato, red onion, lettuce, and a generous drizzle of tzatziki sauce.
  7. Serve immediately and experience the refreshing flavors of the Mediterranean.

These lamb burger recipes are a delightful departure from the ordinary. Whether you choose the classic Greek, Moroccan spiced, or Mediterranean variation, each bite will transport you to sun-kissed shores and vibrant flavors. So fire up the grill and embark on a culinary journey with these mouthwatering lamb burgers. Enjoy!
