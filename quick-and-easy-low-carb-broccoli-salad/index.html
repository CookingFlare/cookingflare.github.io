Quick and Easy Low Carb Broccoli Salad


Are you looking for a delicious and healthy salad that’s quick to make and low in carbs? Look no further! This quick and easy low carb broccoli salad is the perfect dish to satisfy your cravings while keeping you on track with your healthy eating goals. Packed with flavor and nutrients, this salad is a must-try for anyone looking to add more greens to their diet.

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups broccoli florets
  • 1/4 cup diced red onion
  • 1/4 cup crumbled feta cheese
  • 1/4 cup chopped walnuts
  • 1/4 cup dried cranberries
  • 2 tablespoons sliced almonds
  • 2 tablespoons fresh lemon juice
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Start by preparing the broccoli. Cut the broccoli florets into bite-sized pieces and rinse them thoroughly under cold water. Drain and set aside.

  2. In a large mixing bowl, combine the diced red onion, crumbled feta cheese, chopped walnuts, dried cranberries, and sliced almonds. Toss to mix well.

  3. Add the broccoli to the mixing bowl and gently toss to combine with the other ingredients.

  4. In a separate small bowl, whisk together the fresh lemon juice and olive oil. Season with salt and pepper to taste.

  5. Pour the dressing over the broccoli salad and toss until all the ingredients are evenly coated.

  6. Let the salad sit for about 10 minutes to allow the flavors to meld together. This will also help to slightly soften the broccoli florets, making them more tender and enjoyable to eat.

  7. Serve the low carb broccoli salad as a side dish or as a light lunch on its own. It pairs well with grilled chicken or fish for a complete and satisfying meal.

Health Benefits:

This low carb broccoli salad is not only delicious but also packed with nutritional benefits. Here are some key health benefits of the ingredients used in this recipe:

  • Broccoli: Broccoli is a cruciferous vegetable that is rich in vitamins, minerals, and fiber. It is known for its anti-inflammatory properties and is a great source of antioxidants.

  • Red Onion: Red onions are not only flavorful but also provide health benefits. They contain antioxidants and compounds that may help reduce the risk of chronic diseases.

  • Feta Cheese: Feta cheese is lower in fat and calories compared to other types of cheese. It is also a good source of protein and calcium.

  • Walnuts: Walnuts are a great source of omega-3 fatty acids, which are beneficial for heart health. They also provide fiber, protein, and various other vitamins and minerals.

  • Dried Cranberries: Dried cranberries add a touch of sweetness to this salad while providing antioxidants and fiber.

  • Almonds: Almonds are nutrient-rich and provide healthy fats, protein, fiber, and essential vitamins and minerals.

Conclusion:

This quick and easy low carb broccoli salad is a perfect option for those who are following a low carb or keto diet, or simply looking for a healthy and delicious salad to enjoy. Packed with flavor and nutrients, it’s a great way to incorporate more vegetables into your diet. Whether you’re looking for a side dish or a light lunch, this salad is sure to satisfy your taste buds and keep you feeling satisfied. Give it a try and let us know how you like it!
