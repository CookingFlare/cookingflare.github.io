Homemade Cinnamon Rolls for Cooking for Two: Sweet and Gooey


When it comes to baking, few things are as comforting and indulgent as a warm, gooey cinnamon roll fresh out of the oven. The aroma of cinnamon and sugar wafting through your kitchen can evoke cherished memories of family gatherings and cozy mornings. But what if you’re cooking for just two? You don’t need a dozen rolls; a smaller batch can provide the same delightful experience without overwhelming your kitchen. This recipe for homemade cinnamon rolls is designed specifically for two, ensuring that you can enjoy this sweet treat without the guilt of leftovers.

Ingredients

For the Dough:

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons granulated sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 3 tablespoons unsalted butter, melted
  • 1/3 cup buttermilk
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract

For the Filling:

  • 1/4 cup brown sugar, packed
  • 1 tablespoon ground cinnamon
  • 1 tablespoon unsalted butter, softened
  • 1/8 teaspoon salt

For the Cream Cheese Glaze:

  • 2 ounces cream cheese, softened
  • 1/4 cup powdered sugar
  • 1 tablespoon milk
  • 1/4 teaspoon vanilla extract

Equipment Needed

  • Mixing bowls
  • Rolling pin
  • Baking dish (8-inch square or similar)
  • Measuring cups and spoons
  • Whisk
  • Spatula
  • Plastic wrap or a clean kitchen towel

Instructions

Step 1: Prepare the Dough

  1. Mix Dry Ingredients
    In a medium bowl, whisk together 1 cup of all-purpose flour, 2 tablespoons of granulated sugar, 1/2 teaspoon of baking powder, 1/4 teaspoon of baking soda, and 1/4 teaspoon of salt. This combination will help create a light and fluffy dough.

  2. Add Wet Ingredients
    Create a well in the center and add 3 tablespoons of melted butter, 1/3 cup of buttermilk, and 1/2 teaspoon of vanilla extract. Gently stir until the dough starts to come together. It should be slightly sticky but manageable.

  3. Knead the Dough
    On a lightly floured surface, knead the dough for about 1-2 minutes until it becomes smooth. Be careful not to over-knead; you want the dough to remain soft and tender.

  4. Let It Rest
    Place the dough in a bowl and cover it with plastic wrap or a clean kitchen towel. Let it rest for about 10 minutes. This allows the gluten to relax, making it easier to roll out.

Step 2: Make the Filling

While the dough rests, prepare the filling.

  1. Combine Filling Ingredients
    In a small bowl, mix together 1/4 cup of packed brown sugar, 1 tablespoon of ground cinnamon, 1 tablespoon of softened butter, and 1/8 teaspoon of salt. Use a fork to combine until you achieve a crumbly texture. Set aside.

Step 3: Roll Out the Dough

  1. Roll the Dough
    After the dough has rested, place it on a lightly floured surface. Roll it out into a rectangle about 1/4-inch thick, approximately 10x6 inches.

  2. Spread the Filling
    Evenly spread the cinnamon filling over the rolled-out dough, leaving a small border around the edges.

Step 4: Form the Rolls

  1. Roll It Up
    Starting from the long side, tightly roll the dough into a log. Ensure that you roll it as evenly as possible to create uniform rolls.

  2. Slice the Rolls
    Using a sharp knife or a piece of dental floss, slice the log into 4 equal pieces. This will give you two cinnamon rolls for each person, perfect for a cozy breakfast or brunch.

Step 5: Bake the Rolls

  1. Prepare the Baking Dish
    Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C). Grease an 8-inch square baking dish with cooking spray or a little butter.

  2. Arrange the Rolls
    Place the sliced rolls in the prepared baking dish, cut side up. Leave a little space between each roll to allow for expansion.

  3. Bake
    Bake in the preheated oven for about 15-18 minutes, or until the rolls are golden brown and cooked through. Keep an eye on them to prevent over-baking.

Step 6: Make the Cream Cheese Glaze

While the rolls are baking, prepare the delightful cream cheese glaze.

  1. Combine Ingredients
    In a small bowl, mix together 2 ounces of softened cream cheese, 1/4 cup of powdered sugar, 1 tablespoon of milk, and 1/4 teaspoon of vanilla extract. Use a whisk to blend until smooth and creamy.

Step 7: Glaze and Serve

  1. Glaze the Rolls
    Once the cinnamon rolls are out of the oven, allow them to cool for about 5 minutes. Drizzle the cream cheese glaze generously over the warm rolls for that sweet, gooey finish.

  2. Serve Warm
    Enjoy your homemade cinnamon rolls while they’re still warm. Pair them with a cup of coffee or tea for a perfect morning treat.

Tips for Perfect Cinnamon Rolls

  • Use Room Temperature Ingredients: For the best texture, ensure your butter and cream cheese are at room temperature before using them. This allows for easier mixing and a smoother glaze.

  • Don’t Skip the Resting Time: Allowing the dough to rest not only makes it easier to roll but also contributes to a softer final product.

  • Experiment with Flavors: Feel free to add nuts, raisins, or even chocolate chips to the filling for a different twist. A splash of maple syrup in the glaze can also enhance the sweetness.

  • Storage: If you happen to have leftovers (which is rare!), store them in an airtight container at room temperature for up to two days. Reheat in the microwave for a few seconds to enjoy that fresh-baked taste again.

Conclusion

Homemade cinnamon rolls for two are a sweet and gooey delight that brings warmth and joy to any morning. Perfectly portioned for a couple, this recipe allows you to indulge without the hassle of a large batch. Baking these rolls is not just about satisfying your sweet tooth; it’s also about creating a cherished moment shared between you and someone special. So gather your ingredients, roll up your sleeves, and get ready to enjoy the delightful experience of making and devouring your very own homemade cinnamon rolls!
