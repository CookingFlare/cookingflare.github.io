Healthy Nut-Free Black Bean Burger Recipe


Who said burgers can’t be healthy? This delicious and nutritious Nut-Free Black Bean Burger recipe is perfect for those looking for a plant-based alternative packed with flavor. Whether you follow a vegetarian or vegan lifestyle or simply want to incorporate more plant-based meals into your diet, these burgers are a must-try!

Ingredients

  • 2 cans (15 ounces each) black beans, drained and rinsed
  • 1/2 cup finely chopped red bell pepper
  • 1/2 cup finely chopped onion
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh cilantro
  • 1 tablespoon ground flaxseed mixed with 3 tablespoons water (flaxseed egg substitute)
  • 1/2 cup breadcrumbs (gluten-free if desired)
  • 2 teaspoons ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon chili powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon smoked paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil

Instructions

  1. In a large mixing bowl, mash the black beans using a fork or potato masher until they form a thick paste-like consistency.

  2. Add the chopped red bell pepper, onion, minced garlic, and fresh cilantro to the mashed black beans. Mix well to combine.

  3. In a small bowl, prepare the flaxseed egg substitute by mixing the ground flaxseed with water. Allow it to sit for a few minutes until it becomes gelatinous.

  4. Add the flaxseed egg substitute, breadcrumbs, ground cumin, chili powder, smoked paprika, salt, and black pepper to the black bean mixture. Stir until all the ingredients are thoroughly combined.

  5. Shape the mixture into burger patties. This recipe should make approximately 6 patties, depending on your desired size.

  6. Heat the olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Once hot, add the burger patties and cook for 4-5 minutes on each side, or until they are golden brown and crispy.

  7. Serve the Nut-Free Black Bean Burgers on your favorite bun or lettuce wrap, and top with your favorite burger toppings such as lettuce, tomato, onion, pickles, or avocado. Feel free to get creative with your toppings to suit your taste preferences!

  8. Enjoy your homemade Nut-Free Black Bean Burgers while they’re still warm and juicy. These burgers are also great for meal prep, as they can be stored in the refrigerator for up to 5 days or frozen for future use.

Why Choose Nut-Free Black Bean Burgers?

There are several reasons why you should give these Nut-Free Black Bean Burgers a try. Firstly, they are a great source of plant-based protein, fiber, and essential nutrients. Black beans are rich in antioxidants and can help lower cholesterol levels, promote healthy digestion, and support heart health.

Secondly, this recipe is nut-free, making it suitable for individuals with nut allergies or dietary restrictions. With the absence of nuts, you can enjoy these burgers without worrying about any potential allergens.

Lastly, these burgers are incredibly versatile. You can customize them to your liking by adding different spices, herbs, or even vegetables to the mixture. Feel free to experiment and make them your own!

Final Thoughts

In conclusion, these Healthy Nut-Free Black Bean Burgers are a delicious and nutritious alternative to traditional meat-based burgers. They are easy to make, packed with flavor, and provide a satisfying meal for everyone at the table.

So, why not give this recipe a try? Your taste buds and your body will thank you!
