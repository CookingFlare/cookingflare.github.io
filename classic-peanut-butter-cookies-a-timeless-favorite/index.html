Classic Peanut Butter Cookies: A Timeless Favorite


Who doesn’t love the warm, nutty aroma of freshly baked cookies? And when it comes to classic cookie recipes, it’s hard to beat the timeless favorite – peanut butter cookies! These delectable treats are a staple in many households, and it’s not hard to see why. With their rich, buttery flavor and melt-in-your-mouth texture, peanut butter cookies are a crowd-pleaser for all ages.

Ingredients

For this recipe, you’ll need:

  • 1 cup of unsalted butter, softened
  • 1 cup of smooth peanut butter
  • 1 cup of granulated sugar
  • 1 cup of packed brown sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 teaspoons of pure vanilla extract
  • 2 and 1/2 cups of all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon of baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon of baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon of salt

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C) and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.

  2. In a large mixing bowl, cream together the softened butter, peanut butter, granulated sugar, and brown sugar until light and fluffy.

  3. Add the eggs one at a time, beating well after each addition. Stir in the vanilla extract.

  4. In a separate bowl, whisk together the all-purpose flour, baking powder, baking soda, and salt. Gradually add this dry mixture to the wet ingredients, mixing until just combined. Be careful not to overmix, as it can result in a tougher cookie.

  5. Roll the dough into small balls, about 1 inch in diameter. Place the balls onto the prepared baking sheet, leaving a few inches of space between each cookie.

  6. Using a fork, gently press down on each cookie, creating a crisscross pattern. This not only adds a decorative touch but also helps the cookies bake evenly.

  7. Bake the cookies in the preheated oven for 10-12 minutes, or until the edges are golden brown. The centers may still appear slightly undercooked, but they will firm up as the cookies cool.

  8. Remove the baking sheet from the oven and allow the cookies to cool on the sheet for 5 minutes. Then, transfer them to a wire rack to cool completely.

  9. Once cooled, store the cookies in an airtight container to maintain their freshness. They can be enjoyed for up to a week, if they last that long!

Tips and Variations

  • For an extra burst of flavor, you can add chocolate chips, chopped peanuts, or even a sprinkle of sea salt on top of each cookie before baking.

  • If you prefer a chewier texture, you can replace some of the all-purpose flour with quick oats. This will add a subtle nuttiness and a delightful chewiness to the cookies.

  • If you’re a fan of crunchy cookies, simply bake them for a couple of minutes longer to achieve a crispier texture.

  • Peanut butter cookies also make a perfect base for ice cream sandwiches. Simply sandwich a scoop of your favorite ice cream between two cookies and enjoy!

Conclusion

Classic peanut butter cookies are a beloved treat that has stood the test of time. With their simple ingredients and irresistible flavor, these cookies are sure to bring joy to your taste buds and warmth to your home. So, grab your apron, preheat that oven, and indulge in the timeless delight of homemade peanut butter cookies. Your family and friends will thank you!
