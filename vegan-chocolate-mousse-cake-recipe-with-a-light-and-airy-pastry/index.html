Vegan Chocolate Mousse Cake Recipe with a Light and Airy Pastry


If you’re a fan of chocolate desserts but also follow a vegan lifestyle, then this Vegan Chocolate Mousse Cake with a Light and Airy Pastry is the perfect treat for you. Indulge in the rich and velvety chocolate mousse layered between light and fluffy pastry, all made without any animal products. This recipe is not only delicious but also easy to make, so let’s get started!

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup cocoa powder
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup coconut oil, melted
  • 1 cup almond milk
  • 1 tablespoon apple cider vinegar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

For the Vegan Chocolate Mousse:

  • 1 cup vegan chocolate chips
  • 1 can coconut cream, chilled overnight
  • 1/4 cup powdered sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Instructions

Step 1: Preparing the Pastry

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. In a large mixing bowl, whisk together the flour, cocoa powder, granulated sugar, baking powder, and salt.
  3. In a separate bowl, combine the melted coconut oil, almond milk, apple cider vinegar, and vanilla extract.
  4. Pour the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients and mix until a dough forms.
  5. Transfer the dough onto a lightly floured surface and knead it gently for a minute.
  6. Roll out the dough into a circle that fits the size of your cake tin.
  7. Carefully transfer the pastry into the cake tin, making sure it covers the bottom and sides evenly.
  8. Bake the pastry for 15-20 minutes or until it turns golden brown. Once baked, set it aside to cool completely.

Step 2: Making the Vegan Chocolate Mousse

  1. Melt the vegan chocolate chips in a heatproof bowl over a pot of simmering water. Stir until smooth and set aside to cool slightly.
  2. In a separate mixing bowl, scoop out the solid part of the chilled coconut cream, leaving behind the liquid.
  3. Using an electric mixer, whip the coconut cream until it becomes fluffy and forms soft peaks.
  4. Gradually add the powdered sugar and vanilla extract to the whipped coconut cream, and continue whipping until well combined.
  5. Gently fold the melted chocolate into the whipped coconut cream until fully incorporated.

Step 3: Assembling the Cake

  1. Once the pastry has completely cooled, spread a generous layer of the vegan chocolate mousse over the bottom.
  2. Place the cake in the refrigerator for at least 2 hours to allow the mousse to set.
  3. After the mousse has set, repeat the process with another layer of pastry and mousse until you have used up all the ingredients.
  4. Finish the cake with a final layer of pastry on top.
  5. Refrigerate the cake for at least 4 hours or overnight to allow all the layers to set and the flavors to meld together.

Step 4: Serving and Enjoying

  1. Remove the Vegan Chocolate Mousse Cake from the refrigerator and carefully remove it from the cake tin.
  2. Slice the cake into desired portions and serve chilled.
  3. Garnish with fresh berries, mint leaves, or a dusting of cocoa powder for an added touch.

Now that you have this delectable Vegan Chocolate Mousse Cake with a Light and Airy Pastry recipe, you can impress your friends and family with a delicious vegan dessert that doesn’t compromise on taste or texture. Enjoy this rich and indulgent treat guilt-free, knowing that it’s made with love and compassion for both animals and your taste buds. Happy baking!
