Crunchy Coconut Shrimp Tacos with Pineapple Salsa


Tired of the same old taco recipes? Want to add a tropical twist to your taco night? Look no further than these delicious Crunchy Coconut Shrimp Tacos with Pineapple Salsa! This recipe combines the crispy goodness of coconut shrimp with the refreshing flavors of pineapple salsa, creating a mouthwatering dish that is sure to impress your family and friends. So, let’s dive right in and get cooking!

Ingredients

For the Shrimp:

  • 1 pound large shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 1 cup shredded coconut
  • 1 cup panko breadcrumbs
  • 1/2 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 eggs, beaten
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • Vegetable oil, for frying

For the Pineapple Salsa:

  • 1 cup diced fresh pineapple
  • 1/2 cup diced red bell pepper
  • 1/4 cup diced red onion
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh cilantro
  • Juice of 1 lime
  • Salt and pepper, to taste

For the Tacos:

  • 8 small flour tortillas
  • Shredded lettuce
  • Lime wedges, for serving

Instructions

  1. In a shallow dish, combine the shredded coconut and panko breadcrumbs. Set aside.

  2. In another shallow dish, place the all-purpose flour. Season with salt and pepper.

  3. Dip each shrimp into the flour, shaking off any excess. Then, dip the shrimp into the beaten eggs, allowing any excess to drip off. Finally, coat the shrimp in the coconut and breadcrumb mixture, pressing gently to adhere.

  4. Heat vegetable oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat. Fry the coated shrimp in batches until golden brown and crispy, about 2-3 minutes per side. Transfer the cooked shrimp to a paper towel-lined plate to drain excess oil.

  5. In a medium bowl, combine the diced pineapple, red bell pepper, red onion, chopped cilantro, lime juice, salt, and pepper. Mix well to combine and let the flavors meld together while you prepare the tacos.

  6. Warm the flour tortillas in a dry skillet or microwave until soft and pliable.

  7. To assemble the tacos, place a handful of shredded lettuce in the center of each tortilla. Top with a few pieces of the crispy coconut shrimp and a spoonful of the pineapple salsa. Squeeze a lime wedge over each taco for an extra burst of flavor.

  8. Serve the Crunchy Coconut Shrimp Tacos with Pineapple Salsa immediately and enjoy the explosion of textures and flavors in every bite!

Tips and Variations

  • For an extra kick of heat, add some diced jalapenos to the pineapple salsa.
  • If you prefer a healthier alternative, you can bake the coconut shrimp instead of frying them. Simply place the coated shrimp on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper and bake in a preheated oven at 425°F (220°C) for about 10-12 minutes, flipping halfway through.
  • Feel free to experiment with different toppings and garnishes for your tacos. Some suggestions include avocado slices, diced tomatoes, or a drizzle of sriracha mayo.

Conclusion

These Crunchy Coconut Shrimp Tacos with Pineapple Salsa are a delightful fusion of flavors that will transport your taste buds to a tropical paradise. With their crispy coconut coating, juicy shrimp, and tangy pineapple salsa, these tacos are sure to become a new family favorite. So, why wait? Gather the ingredients, follow the simple steps, and enjoy a delicious meal that will leave everyone craving for more. Happy cooking!
