Pumpkin Bread Pudding with Caramel Sauce: A Decadent Fall Dessert


Indulge in the flavors of fall with this irresistible Pumpkin Bread Pudding with Caramel Sauce. This delightful dessert combines the warm, comforting taste of pumpkin with the rich sweetness of caramel. Perfect for autumn gatherings or a cozy night in, this recipe will surely satisfy your sweet tooth and leave you wanting more.

Ingredients

  • 1 loaf of day-old bread, preferably brioche or challah
  • 2 cups whole milk
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1 cup pumpkin puree
  • 3/4 cup brown sugar
  • 4 large eggs
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground nutmeg
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground cloves
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt

For the Caramel Sauce:

  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 6 tablespoons unsalted butter
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease a 9x13-inch baking dish and set aside.

  2. Cut the day-old bread into 1-inch cubes and spread them evenly in the prepared baking dish.

  3. In a medium-sized saucepan, combine the milk, cream, pumpkin puree, brown sugar, eggs, vanilla extract, cinnamon, nutmeg, cloves, and salt. Whisk until well combined and the sugar has dissolved.

  4. Pour the pumpkin mixture over the bread cubes, making sure all the bread is soaked. Press down gently with a spatula to ensure even absorption.

  5. Allow the bread pudding to sit for 15-20 minutes to allow the bread to soak up the pumpkin mixture.

  6. Place the baking dish in the preheated oven and bake for 45-50 minutes, or until the bread pudding is set and golden brown on top.

  7. While the bread pudding is baking, prepare the caramel sauce. In a medium-sized saucepan over medium heat, melt the granulated sugar, stirring constantly. Cook until the sugar has melted and turned a golden amber color.

  8. Add the butter to the saucepan and continue stirring until melted and well combined with the caramelized sugar.

  9. Slowly pour in the heavy cream and continue stirring until the caramel sauce is smooth and creamy. Remove from heat and stir in the salt.

  10. Once the bread pudding is done baking, remove it from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes. Serve warm, drizzled with the caramel sauce.

Enjoy the Delights of Fall

This Pumpkin Bread Pudding with Caramel Sauce is the epitome of a decadent fall dessert. The combination of moist bread soaked in a pumpkin-infused custard, topped with a luscious caramel sauce, is simply irresistible. Serve it warm, and savor each bite as the flavors of fall dance on your taste buds.

Whether you’re hosting a fall gathering or simply want to treat yourself to a cozy dessert, this recipe is sure to impress. The creamy texture, the fragrant spices, and the sweet caramel sauce come together to create a dessert that embodies the essence of autumn.

So, gather your loved ones, light some candles, and indulge in the ultimate fall treat. Pumpkin Bread Pudding with Caramel Sauce is a dessert that will warm your heart and make memories that last a lifetime.
