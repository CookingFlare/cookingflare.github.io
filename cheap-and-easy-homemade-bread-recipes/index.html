Cheap and Easy Homemade Bread Recipes


Are you looking to fill your home with the inviting aroma of freshly baked bread without breaking the bank? Look no further! In this blog post, we’ll explore some incredibly simple and cost-effective homemade bread recipes that will have you savoring the satisfaction of baking your own bread in no time.

Classic White Bread

Ingredients:

  • 4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 teaspoons instant yeast
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • 1 ½ teaspoons salt
  • 1 ½ cups warm water
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil

Instructions:

  1. In a large mixing bowl, combine the flour, instant yeast, sugar, and salt.
  2. Gradually add the warm water and vegetable oil to the dry ingredients, mixing until a dough forms.
  3. Knead the dough on a floured surface for about 10 minutes until it becomes smooth and elastic.
  4. Place the dough in a greased bowl, cover it with a kitchen towel, and let it rise in a warm place for about an hour or until doubled in size.
  5. Punch down the dough to release the air, then shape it into a loaf and place it in a greased loaf pan.
  6. Cover the pan and let the dough rise for another 30 minutes.
  7. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C) and bake the bread for 30-35 minutes or until golden brown.
  8. Allow the bread to cool before slicing and enjoying.

Whole Wheat Honey Bread

Ingredients:

  • 3 cups whole wheat flour
  • 1 ½ cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 ¼ teaspoons instant yeast
  • 1 ¼ teaspoons salt
  • 1 ¼ cups warm water
  • ¼ cup honey
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil

Instructions:

  1. In a large bowl, combine the whole wheat flour, all-purpose flour, instant yeast, and salt.
  2. Add the warm water, honey, and vegetable oil to the dry ingredients, mixing until a dough forms.
  3. Knead the dough on a floured surface for about 8-10 minutes until it becomes smooth and elastic.
  4. Place the dough in a greased bowl, cover it with a kitchen towel, and let it rise in a warm place for about 1 hour or until doubled in size.
  5. Punch down the dough, then shape it into a loaf and place it in a greased loaf pan.
  6. Cover the pan and let the dough rise for another 30 minutes.
  7. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C) and bake the bread for 30-35 minutes or until it sounds hollow when tapped.
  8. Let the bread cool before slicing and savoring every bite.

Quick No-Yeast Beer Bread

Ingredients:

  • 3 cups self-rising flour
  • 3 tablespoons granulated sugar
  • 12 oz. beer (room temperature)
  • ¼ cup melted butter

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C) and grease a 9x5-inch loaf pan.
  2. In a mixing bowl, combine the self-rising flour and sugar.
  3. Pour in the beer and stir until just combined.
  4. Pour the batter into the prepared loaf pan and drizzle the melted butter over the top.
  5. Bake for 45-55 minutes or until the top is golden brown and a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.
  6. Let the beer bread cool for a few minutes before slicing and enjoying its delightful simplicity.

Conclusion

With these easy and affordable homemade bread recipes, you can elevate your baking skills and treat yourself to the delightful taste of freshly baked bread whenever you desire. Whether you prefer the classic simplicity of white bread, the wholesome goodness of whole wheat honey bread, or the quick and effortless no-yeast beer bread, there’s a recipe here for everyone. Enjoy the process of creating your own bread from scratch and relish the delicious results of your culinary creativity. Happy baking!
