Slow Cooker Italian Sausage and Peppers Recipes for Classic Flavors


If you’re a fan of hearty, flavorful Italian dishes but don’t have the time to spend hours in the kitchen, then this Slow Cooker Italian Sausage and Peppers recipe is perfect for you. It’s a classic dish that brings together the savory goodness of Italian sausage with the sweet and tangy flavors of bell peppers, all simmered to perfection in a slow cooker. This recipe is not only delicious but also incredibly easy to prepare, making it a go-to option for busy weeknights or lazy weekends.

Ingredients:

  • 1.5 pounds Italian sausage links
  • 3 large bell peppers (red, yellow, and green), sliced
  • 1 large onion, sliced
  • 4 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 can (14.5 ounces) diced tomatoes
  • 1 can (6 ounces) tomato paste
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1/2 teaspoon red pepper flakes (optional)
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley for garnish
  • Crusty Italian bread or cooked pasta for serving

Instructions:

  1. Brown the Sausage: Begin by browning the Italian sausage links in a skillet over medium heat. Once they are browned on all sides, transfer them to the slow cooker.

  2. Add the Vegetables: In the same skillet, add the sliced bell peppers, onions, and minced garlic. Sauté them for a few minutes until they start to soften. Transfer the sautéed vegetables to the slow cooker, arranging them around the sausage.

  3. Create the Sauce: In a bowl, mix together the diced tomatoes, tomato paste, dried oregano, dried basil, red pepper flakes (if using), salt, and black pepper. Pour this flavorful mixture over the sausage and vegetables in the slow cooker.

  4. Cook Slow and Low: Cover the slow cooker and cook the Italian sausage and peppers on low for 6-8 hours or on high for 3-4 hours. The slow cooking process allows the flavors to meld together, resulting in a rich and aromatic dish.

  5. Serve and Enjoy: Once the sausage and peppers are tender and the flavors have developed, it’s time to serve this delicious dish. You can enjoy it on its own, or for a heartier meal, serve it over cooked pasta or with crusty Italian bread. Garnish with fresh parsley for a pop of color and freshness.

Tips and Variations:

  • Spice it Up: If you prefer a spicier kick, you can use hot Italian sausage or increase the amount of red pepper flakes in the recipe.
  • Make it a Sandwich: Spoon the sausage and peppers into a crusty Italian roll for a mouthwatering sandwich.
  • Add Some Wine: For an extra depth of flavor, you can add a splash of red wine to the sauce mixture before pouring it over the sausage and peppers.

Final Thoughts:

This Slow Cooker Italian Sausage and Peppers recipe is a true crowd-pleaser, perfect for feeding a hungry family or entertaining guests. The combination of tender Italian sausage, sweet bell peppers, and aromatic herbs creates a comforting and satisfying meal that will transport you to the heart of Italy with every bite. Plus, the convenience of using a slow cooker means that you can set it and forget it, allowing you to savor the delightful aromas as the dish cooks to perfection.

So, the next time you’re craving classic Italian flavors without the fuss, give this slow cooker recipe a try. It’s a timeless favorite that never fails to impress and is sure to become a staple in your cooking repertoire. Buon appetito!
