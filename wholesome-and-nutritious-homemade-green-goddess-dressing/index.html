Wholesome and Nutritious: Homemade Green Goddess Dressing


Are you looking to add a burst of freshness and flavor to your salads, sandwiches, or veggie bowls? Look no further than this homemade green goddess dressing! Packed with vibrant herbs and wholesome ingredients, this dressing is not only delicious but also incredibly nutritious. In this recipe, we’ll explore how to create this green goddess dressing from scratch, and delve into the health benefits of the key ingredients.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup fresh parsley
  • 1/2 cup fresh basil leaves
  • 1/4 cup fresh tarragon leaves
  • 2 green onions, chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 cup Greek yogurt
  • 1/4 cup mayonnaise
  • 2 tablespoons lemon juice
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Prep the Herbs: Start by washing the fresh herbs thoroughly and patting them dry. Remove the stems from the parsley, basil, and tarragon, and finely chop the leaves.

  2. Blend: In a blender or food processor, combine the chopped herbs, green onions, minced garlic, Greek yogurt, mayonnaise, and lemon juice. Blend until the mixture is smooth and well combined.

  3. Season: Add salt and pepper to taste, and blend again to incorporate the seasoning evenly into the dressing.

  4. Chill: Transfer the dressing to a glass jar or airtight container and refrigerate for at least 30 minutes to allow the flavors to meld together.

  5. Serve: Once chilled, drizzle this vibrant green goddess dressing over your favorite salads, use it as a dip for fresh veggies, or spread it on sandwiches and wraps for an extra burst of flavor.

Health Benefits

Fresh Herbs

Parsley, basil, and tarragon are not only flavorful but also packed with essential nutrients. Parsley is a good source of vitamin K, while basil provides a healthy dose of vitamin A and tarragon offers antioxidants like vitamin C and B-complex vitamins.

Greek Yogurt

Rich in protein, calcium, and probiotics, Greek yogurt adds a creamy texture to the dressing while boosting its nutritional value.

Lemon Juice

The addition of lemon juice not only enhances the tangy flavor but also provides a dose of vitamin C and beneficial plant compounds.

By making your own green goddess dressing at home, you have full control over the quality and freshness of the ingredients, ensuring that you’re consuming a wholesome and nutritious dressing without any unwanted additives or preservatives. Plus, the vibrant green color of this dressing is a visual indicator of its healthful, natural ingredients.

Whether you’re hosting a summer barbecue, preparing a quick weekday meal, or simply looking to elevate your salads, this homemade green goddess dressing is a versatile and nutritious addition to your culinary repertoire. Enjoy the process of creating this vibrant dressing, and savor the fresh and wholesome flavors it brings to your dishes!

So, what are you waiting for? Gather your fresh herbs, Greek yogurt, and a few other simple ingredients, and embark on a culinary adventure with this homemade green goddess dressing. Your taste buds and your body will thank you for it!
