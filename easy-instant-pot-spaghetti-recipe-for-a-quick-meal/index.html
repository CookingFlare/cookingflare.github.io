Easy Instant Pot Spaghetti Recipe for a Quick Meal


Are you tired of spending hours in the kitchen to prepare a delicious meal? Do you crave the taste of homemade spaghetti but don’t have the time to cook it from scratch? Look no further! This easy Instant Pot spaghetti recipe is a game-changer for busy home cooks who want to enjoy a quick and satisfying meal.

The Benefits of Using an Instant Pot

The Instant Pot is a pressure cooker that has taken the world by storm. Its versatility and ease of use make it a staple in many kitchens. With an Instant Pot, you can cook a variety of dishes, from soups to stews, and even desserts. But what makes it particularly useful is its ability to cook pasta quickly and efficiently.

The Ingredients

To make this easy Instant Pot spaghetti recipe, you will need the following ingredients:

  • 1 pound spaghetti
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 3 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 can (28 oz) crushed tomatoes
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • 1 pound ground beef or sausage, optional

The Instructions

  1. Press the “saute” button on the Instant Pot and heat the olive oil until shimmering.
  2. Add the diced onion and cook until translucent, about 3-4 minutes.
  3. Add the minced garlic and cook for an additional minute, until fragrant.
  4. Add the crushed tomatoes, dried basil, and dried oregano. Stir well to combine.
  5. Add the spaghetti to the Instant Pot, breaking it in half if necessary to fit.
  6. Add 4 cups of water to the Instant Pot, making sure the spaghetti is fully submerged.
  7. Close the lid of the Instant Pot and make sure the valve is set to “sealing”.
  8. Press the “manual” or “pressure cook” button and set the cooking time to 5 minutes at high pressure.
  9. Let the pressure release naturally for 10 minutes, then quick-release any remaining pressure.
  10. Open the lid and check the spaghetti for doneness. If it’s not al dente, close the lid and cook for an additional 2-3 minutes.
  11. Season the spaghetti with salt and pepper to taste.
  12. Serve hot, garnished with grated Parmesan cheese and chopped fresh parsley, if desired.

Optional: Adding Ground Beef or Sausage

If you want to add some extra flavor and protein to your spaghetti, you can cook ground beef or sausage in the Instant Pot before adding the tomatoes and spaghetti. Simply brown the ground beef or sausage in the Instant Pot, breaking it up with a spoon as it cooks, before adding the remaining ingredients.

Tips and Variations

  • Use fresh tomatoes instead of canned, if you prefer. Simply chop them up and add them to the Instant Pot along with the other ingredients.
  • Add some red wine to the sauce for a richer flavor.
  • Use different types of pasta, such as penne or rigatoni, if you prefer.
  • Add some roasted vegetables, such as broccoli or bell peppers, to the spaghetti for added nutrition and flavor.

Conclusion

This easy Instant Pot spaghetti recipe is a quick and delicious solution for a busy weeknight dinner. With its ease of use and versatility, the Instant Pot is a must-have for any home cook. Try this recipe tonight and enjoy a satisfying and flavorful meal in no time!
