Savory Breakfast Tostada Recipes for a Mexican-Inspired Brunch


If you’re looking to spice up your weekend brunch routine, why not try something a little different? Savory breakfast tostadas are the perfect way to add a Mexican-inspired twist to your morning meal. With their crispy tortillas, flavorful toppings, and bold spices, these tostadas are sure to impress your guests and leave you craving more.

Classic Huevos Rancheros Tostada

Let’s start with a classic: Huevos Rancheros. To make this delicious tostada, you’ll need the following ingredients:

  • Corn tortillas
  • Refried beans
  • Eggs
  • Salsa
  • Avocado
  • Cheese (optional)
  • Cilantro (optional)

To begin, heat a frying pan over medium heat and lightly toast your corn tortillas until they become crispy. Set them aside.

Next, heat up your refried beans in a small saucepan. Once warm, spread a generous layer of beans onto each tortilla.

In the same pan, fry your eggs to your desired doneness. Place one egg on top of each tortilla.

Now it’s time to add the toppings. Spoon salsa over the eggs, followed by slices of avocado. If you’re a cheese lover, sprinkle some grated cheese on top. Finally, garnish with fresh cilantro for an added burst of flavor.

Chorizo and Egg Tostada

If you’re in the mood for something a little spicier, try this Chorizo and Egg Tostada. Here’s what you’ll need:

  • Corn tortillas
  • Chorizo
  • Eggs
  • Black beans
  • Pico de gallo
  • Sour cream (optional)
  • Lime wedges

Start by cooking the chorizo in a skillet over medium heat until it’s fully cooked and slightly crispy. Remove from the pan and set aside.

In the same skillet, crack your eggs and cook them to your liking. For an extra kick, you can scramble the eggs with the cooked chorizo.

Meanwhile, warm up your black beans in a small saucepan.

To assemble the tostadas, toast your corn tortillas until they’re nice and crispy. Spread a layer of black beans onto each tortilla, followed by the chorizo and egg mixture.

Top it off with a spoonful of pico de gallo and a dollop of sour cream, if desired. Serve with lime wedges for an added zing.

Avocado and Black Bean Tostada

For a vegetarian option that’s still bursting with flavor, try this Avocado and Black Bean Tostada. Here’s what you’ll need:

  • Corn tortillas
  • Avocado
  • Black beans
  • Red onion
  • Cilantro
  • Lime juice
  • Salt and pepper to taste

To start, mash your avocado in a bowl and season it with lime juice, salt, and pepper. Set aside.

Next, warm up your black beans in a small saucepan. Once heated, mash them slightly with a fork to create a creamy texture.

In a separate bowl, combine finely chopped red onion and cilantro. This will be your fresh topping.

To assemble the tostadas, toast your corn tortillas until they’re crispy. Spread a layer of mashed avocado onto each tortilla, followed by a layer of black beans.

Finish it off with a sprinkle of the red onion and cilantro mixture. Serve with a side of salsa if desired.

Conclusion

There you have it, three delicious savory breakfast tostada recipes that will take your brunch game to the next level. Whether you prefer the classic flavors of Huevos Rancheros, the spicy kick of Chorizo and Egg, or the freshness of Avocado and Black Bean, these tostadas are sure to satisfy your Mexican-inspired cravings. So gather your ingredients, fire up the stove, and get ready to enjoy a brunch that’s anything but ordinary. Buen provecho!
