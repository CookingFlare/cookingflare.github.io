Paleo Chicken and Rice Recipes: 5 Delicious and Grain-Free Options


If you’re following a paleo lifestyle or simply looking for grain-free alternatives, chicken and rice dishes can still be a part of your culinary repertoire. In this post, we’ll explore five delectable and grain-free Paleo chicken and rice recipes that are not only delicious but also easy to prepare. These recipes offer a creative twist on traditional favorites, giving you the opportunity to savor the flavors of chicken and rice while staying true to your dietary preferences.

1. Cauliflower “Rice” Chicken Stir-Fry

Ingredients:

  • 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts, thinly sliced
  • 1 head of cauliflower, riced
  • 1 red bell pepper, thinly sliced
  • 1 yellow bell pepper, thinly sliced
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon coconut aminos
  • 1 teaspoon grated fresh ginger
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 tablespoons coconut oil

Instructions:

  1. In a large skillet or wok, heat coconut oil over medium-high heat.
  2. Add the sliced chicken and cook until no longer pink.
  3. Push the chicken to one side of the skillet and add the onions, bell peppers, and garlic. Cook until the vegetables are tender-crisp.
  4. Stir in the riced cauliflower and coconut aminos. Cook for an additional 3-4 minutes, stirring frequently.
  5. Add the grated ginger, salt, and pepper. Stir to combine and cook for another 2 minutes.
  6. Remove from heat and serve hot.

This flavorful stir-fry offers the satisfying texture of “rice” without the grains, making it a perfect Paleo alternative to traditional chicken and rice stir-fry.

2. Turmeric Chicken with Broccoli Rice

Ingredients:

  • 4 chicken thighs, bone-in and skin-on
  • 3 cups broccoli florets
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon ground turmeric
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 400°F (200°C).
  2. In a small bowl, mix the olive oil, turmeric, paprika, garlic powder, salt, and pepper to create a marinade.
  3. Place the chicken thighs in a baking dish and coat them evenly with the marinade.
  4. Bake for 25-30 minutes or until the chicken is cooked through and golden brown.
  5. While the chicken is baking, pulse the broccoli florets in a food processor until they resemble rice.
  6. In a skillet, heat a tablespoon of olive oil over medium heat. Add the broccoli “rice” and sauté for 3-4 minutes until tender.
  7. Serve the turmeric chicken over the broccoli rice and enjoy.

This vibrant dish not only offers a pop of color but also provides a nourishing and satisfying meal that’s free from grains.

3. Lemon Garlic Chicken with Parsnip “Rice”

Ingredients:

  • 4 bone-in, skin-on chicken drumsticks
  • 2 parsnips, peeled and grated
  • Zest and juice of 1 lemon
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. In a small bowl, combine the olive oil, lemon zest, minced garlic, thyme, salt, and pepper.
  3. Place the chicken drumsticks in a baking dish and brush them with the lemon garlic mixture.
  4. Bake for 35-40 minutes or until the chicken is golden and fully cooked.
  5. In the meantime, heat a tablespoon of olive oil in a skillet over medium heat. Add the grated parsnips and sauté for 5-6 minutes until tender.
  6. Drizzle the parsnip “rice” with lemon juice and toss to combine.
  7. Serve the lemon garlic chicken alongside the parsnip “rice” for a citrusy and aromatic Paleo delight.

This refreshing dish showcases the versatility of parsnips as a grain-free alternative to rice, adding a subtle sweetness to the meal.
