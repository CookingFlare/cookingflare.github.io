Tomato Basil Bisque: A Rich and Creamy Delight


If you’re a fan of rich and creamy soups, then you’re in for a treat with this Tomato Basil Bisque recipe. This classic dish is a delightful combination of ripe tomatoes, aromatic basil, and velvety smooth cream. Whether you’re looking for a comforting meal on a chilly evening or an elegant starter for a dinner party, this flavorful bisque is sure to impress.

Ingredients

  • 2 lbs ripe tomatoes, quartered
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/4 cup fresh basil leaves, chopped
  • 4 cups vegetable broth
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 teaspoon sugar (optional, to balance the acidity of the tomatoes)

Instructions

  1. Preheat the Oven: Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C).

  2. Roast the Tomatoes: Place the quartered tomatoes on a baking sheet, drizzle with olive oil, and season with salt and pepper. Roast in the preheated oven for 30-40 minutes, or until the tomatoes are soft and slightly caramelized.

  3. Sauté the Aromatics: In a large pot, melt the butter over medium heat. Add the chopped onion and garlic, and sauté until softened and aromatic.

  4. Blend the Ingredients: Add the roasted tomatoes, sautéed onion and garlic, and fresh basil to a blender. Blend until smooth, then transfer the mixture back to the pot.

  5. Simmer the Bisque: Pour in the vegetable broth and bring the mixture to a gentle simmer. Let it simmer for 20-25 minutes to allow the flavors to meld together.

  6. Add the Cream: Stir in the heavy cream, and let the bisque simmer for an additional 5-10 minutes. Be careful not to let it boil.

  7. Season to Perfection: Taste the bisque and adjust the seasoning with salt, pepper, and sugar if desired. The sugar can help balance the acidity of the tomatoes for a well-rounded flavor.

  8. Serve and Enjoy: Ladle the tomato basil bisque into bowls, garnish with a drizzle of cream or a sprinkle of fresh basil, and serve with crusty bread or a side salad.

Tips and Variations

  • Vegan Version: For a vegan-friendly option, substitute the heavy cream with coconut milk or a non-dairy cream alternative.
  • Grilled Cheese Pairing: Tomato basil bisque pairs beautifully with a classic grilled cheese sandwich for a comforting and nostalgic meal.
  • Garnish Galore: Get creative with your garnishes! Croutons, a swirl of balsamic glaze, or a sprinkle of grated Parmesan cheese can take this bisque to the next level.

Final Thoughts

Tomato basil bisque is a timeless classic that never fails to delight the senses. Its velvety texture, vibrant color, and harmonious flavors make it a versatile dish that suits any occasion. Whether you’re a seasoned home cook or a beginner in the kitchen, this recipe is a must-try for anyone who appreciates the simple pleasures of a well-made soup. So, gather your ingredients, embrace the aroma of roasting tomatoes, and savor the comforting luxury of a bowl of homemade tomato basil bisque.
