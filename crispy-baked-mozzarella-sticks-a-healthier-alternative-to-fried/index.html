Crispy Baked Mozzarella Sticks: A Healthier Alternative to Fried


Who doesn’t love the crispy, gooey goodness of mozzarella sticks? These classic appetizers are a crowd-pleaser at any gathering, but the traditional fried version can be heavy on the calories and unhealthy fats. However, fear not, because I have the perfect solution – crispy baked mozzarella sticks! This healthier alternative delivers all the flavor and crunch you crave, without the guilt. Let’s dive into this delicious recipe and discover how to create a mouthwatering snack that’s both wholesome and satisfying.

Ingredients

  • 12 sticks of string cheese
  • 1 cup seasoned breadcrumbs
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • Cooking spray
  • Marinara sauce, for dipping

Instructions

  1. Preheat the Oven: Begin by preheating your oven to 400°F (200°C). This ensures that it’s nice and hot for baking the mozzarella sticks to golden perfection.

  2. Prepare the Breading Station: In three separate shallow dishes, set up a breading station. Place the flour in one, the beaten eggs in another, and combine the breadcrumbs and Parmesan cheese in the third dish.

  3. Coat the Cheese Sticks: Take each stick of string cheese and coat it in the flour, then dip it in the beaten eggs, and finally coat it with the breadcrumb mixture. Be sure to press the breadcrumbs onto the cheese sticks so that they are evenly coated.

  4. Repeat for Extra Crispness: For an extra crispy coating, you can repeat the egg and breadcrumb coating process a second time.

  5. Arrange on Baking Sheet: Once all the cheese sticks are coated, place them on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper. Make sure to leave a little space between each stick so they bake evenly.

  6. Bake to Perfection: Lightly spray the coated cheese sticks with cooking spray. This will help them achieve that irresistible golden crispness. Bake in the preheated oven for about 8-10 minutes, or until the coating is golden brown and the cheese is soft and gooey.

  7. Serve and Enjoy: Once they are done, remove the mozzarella sticks from the oven and allow them to cool for a few minutes. Serve them with a side of marinara sauce for dipping, and watch them disappear in no time.

Healthier and Equally Delicious

By opting to bake rather than fry these mozzarella sticks, you significantly reduce the amount of added fats and calories without sacrificing the irresistible crunch and cheesy goodness. The result is a healthier snack that doesn’t compromise on taste. These crispy baked mozzarella sticks are perfect for satisfying your cravings while still maintaining a balanced diet.

Conclusion

Whether you’re hosting a party, looking for a tasty snack, or simply indulging in a savory treat, crispy baked mozzarella sticks are the answer. With this recipe, you can enjoy the classic comfort food with a healthier twist. So go ahead, whip up a batch of these irresistible snacks and treat yourself to the perfect combination of gooey, cheesy, and crispy goodness!

So, what are you waiting for? Get ready to savor the deliciousness of these healthier mozzarella sticks, and share the joy of guilt-free indulgence with your friends and family. Happy baking!
