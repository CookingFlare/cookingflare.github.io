Quick and Healthy Chicken Salad Recipes: 5 Meals Under 30 Minutes


Are you looking for delicious and nutritious chicken salad recipes that can be prepared in under 30 minutes? Look no further! In this blog post, we’ve curated five quick and healthy chicken salad recipes that are not only easy to make but also bursting with flavor. Whether you’re a busy professional, a parent on-the-go, or simply someone who wants to enjoy a satisfying meal without spending hours in the kitchen, these recipes are perfect for you. So grab your apron and let’s get started!

1. Greek Chicken Salad

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups cooked chicken breast, shredded
  • 1 cup cucumber, diced
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1/2 cup red onion, thinly sliced
  • 1/4 cup Kalamata olives, pitted and chopped
  • 1/4 cup feta cheese, crumbled
  • 2 tablespoons fresh lemon juice
  • 2 tablespoons extra-virgin olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a large bowl, combine the shredded chicken, cucumber, cherry tomatoes, red onion, Kalamata olives, and feta cheese.
  2. In a small bowl, whisk together the lemon juice, olive oil, dried oregano, salt, and pepper.
  3. Pour the dressing over the chicken salad and toss gently to combine.
  4. Serve immediately and enjoy!

2. Asian Chicken Salad

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups cooked chicken breast, diced
  • 1 cup shredded cabbage
  • 1 cup shredded carrots
  • 1/2 cup edamame beans
  • 1/4 cup green onions, chopped
  • 1/4 cup cilantro, chopped
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons rice vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon sesame oil
  • 1 teaspoon honey
  • 1/2 teaspoon grated ginger
  • Sesame seeds for garnish (optional)

Instructions:

  1. In a large bowl, combine the diced chicken, shredded cabbage, shredded carrots, edamame beans, green onions, and cilantro.
  2. In a small bowl, whisk together the soy sauce, rice vinegar, sesame oil, honey, and grated ginger.
  3. Pour the dressing over the chicken salad and toss gently to combine.
  4. Sprinkle with sesame seeds, if desired, and serve immediately.

3. Southwest Chicken Salad

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups cooked chicken breast, shredded
  • 1 cup black beans, rinsed and drained
  • 1 cup corn kernels
  • 1/2 cup diced red bell pepper
  • 1/4 cup diced red onion
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh cilantro
  • Juice of 1 lime
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1/2 teaspoon chili powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Tortilla chips for serving (optional)

Instructions:

  1. In a large bowl, combine the shredded chicken, black beans, corn kernels, red bell pepper, red onion, and cilantro.
  2. In a small bowl, whisk together the lime juice, olive oil, ground cumin, chili powder, salt, and pepper.
  3. Pour the dressing over the chicken salad and toss gently to combine.
  4. Serve the salad with tortilla chips on the side, if desired.

4. Mediterranean Chicken Salad

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups cooked chicken breast, diced
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1/2 cup cucumber, diced
  • 1/4 cup red onion, thinly sliced
  • 1/4 cup Kalamata olives, pitted and chopped
  • 1/4 cup crumbled feta cheese
  • 2 tablespoons fresh lemon juice
  • 2 tablespoons extra-virgin olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a large bowl, combine the diced chicken, cherry tomatoes, cucumber, red onion, Kalamata olives, and feta cheese.
  2. In a small bowl, whisk together the lemon juice, olive oil, dried oregano, salt, and pepper.
  3. Pour the dressing over the chicken salad and toss gently to combine.
  4. Serve immediately and enjoy the Mediterranean flavors!

5. BBQ Chicken Salad

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups cooked chicken breast, shredded
  • 1 cup mixed salad greens
  • 1/2 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1/4 cup diced red onion
  • 1/4 cup corn kernels
  • 1/4 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • 2 tablespoons BBQ sauce
  • 2 tablespoons ranch dressing
  • Tortilla strips for garnish (optional)

Instructions:

  1. In a large bowl, combine the shredded chicken, mixed salad greens, cherry tomatoes, red onion, corn kernels, and shredded cheddar cheese.
  2. In a small bowl, mix together the BBQ sauce and ranch dressing.
  3. Pour the dressing over the chicken salad and toss gently to combine.
  4. Garnish with tortilla strips, if desired, and serve immediately.

These quick and healthy chicken salad recipes are perfect for those busy days when you need a satisfying meal in a pinch. They’re packed with protein and nutrients, and can be easily customized to suit your taste preferences. So next time you’re short on time but still want to enjoy a delicious and nutritious meal, give these recipes a try. Your taste buds will thank you!

Remember, it’s important to adapt the recipes to your dietary needs and preferences. Feel free to experiment with different ingredients and flavors to make these recipes your own. Happy cooking!
