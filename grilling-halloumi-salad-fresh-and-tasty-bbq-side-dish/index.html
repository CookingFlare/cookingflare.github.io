Grilling Halloumi Salad: Fresh and Tasty BBQ Side Dish


Are you looking for a fresh and tasty side dish to accompany your BBQ feast? Look no further than this delightful Grilling Halloumi Salad. This dish combines the smoky flavors of grilled halloumi cheese with the freshness of crisp greens and juicy tomatoes, all tied together with a zesty dressing. Whether you’re hosting a summer cookout or simply craving a flavorful addition to your meal, this salad is sure to impress your taste buds and those of your guests.

Ingredients

For the Salad:

  • 1 block of halloumi cheese, sliced
  • 4 cups mixed salad greens
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1/2 red onion, thinly sliced
  • 1/4 cup fresh basil leaves

For the Dressing:

  • 1/4 cup extra virgin olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons balsamic vinegar
  • 1 teaspoon Dijon mustard
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Grilled Halloumi

    • Preheat your grill to medium-high heat.
    • Brush the halloumi slices lightly with olive oil.
    • Grill the halloumi for 2-3 minutes on each side, until grill marks appear and the cheese is slightly softened.

  2. Assemble the Salad

    • In a large bowl, combine the mixed salad greens, cherry tomatoes, red onion, and fresh basil leaves.

  3. Make the Dressing

    • In a small bowl, whisk together the extra virgin olive oil, balsamic vinegar, Dijon mustard, salt, and pepper until well combined.

  4. Combine and Serve

    • Add the grilled halloumi to the bowl of salad ingredients.
    • Drizzle the dressing over the salad and halloumi.
    • Gently toss the salad until the ingredients are evenly coated with the dressing.

  5. Serve and Enjoy

    • Transfer the Grilling Halloumi Salad to a serving platter or individual plates.
    • Serve immediately and savor the delicious combination of flavors and textures.

Tips for Perfecting Your Grilling Halloumi Salad

  • Choosing the Right Halloumi: Look for a high-quality halloumi cheese that is suitable for grilling. The cheese should hold its shape when grilled and develop a golden crust while remaining soft and gooey inside.

  • Grilling Time: Keep a close eye on the halloumi while grilling to prevent it from becoming too soft or overly charred. Aim for a perfect balance of grill marks and a slightly softened texture.

  • Dressing Variation: Feel free to customize the salad dressing to your preference. You can add a touch of honey for sweetness or incorporate fresh herbs like oregano or thyme for an extra burst of flavor.

  • Serving Suggestions: This Grilling Halloumi Salad pairs beautifully with grilled meats, seafood, or as a standalone dish. It also makes a delightful addition to a picnic or potluck spread.

Final Thoughts

Grilling Halloumi Salad is a celebration of summery flavors and textures that are perfect for any BBQ gathering or outdoor meal. The salty, savory halloumi cheese, paired with the vibrant salad greens and tangy dressing, creates a harmonious and satisfying dish that will leave your guests asking for seconds. Whether you’re a seasoned grill master or a novice cook, this recipe is a wonderful way to elevate your BBQ experience with a refreshing and delicious side dish. So fire up the grill, gather your ingredients, and get ready to delight your taste buds with this Fresh and Tasty Grilling Halloumi Salad!
