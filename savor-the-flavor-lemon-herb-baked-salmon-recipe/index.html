Savor the Flavor: Lemon Herb Baked Salmon Recipe


If you’re looking for a delicious and nutritious meal that is both easy to prepare and packed with flavor, then you’ve come to the right place. This Lemon Herb Baked Salmon recipe is sure to tickle your taste buds and leave you craving for more. With the perfect balance of zesty lemon and aromatic herbs, this dish is a crowd-pleaser that will impress even the most discerning palates.

Ingredients

To create this mouthwatering dish, you will need the following ingredients:

  • 1 pound of fresh salmon fillets
  • 2 tablespoons of olive oil
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon of fresh lemon zest
  • 1 tablespoon of fresh lemon juice
  • 1 tablespoon of fresh dill, chopped
  • 1 tablespoon of fresh parsley, chopped
  • Salt and pepper, to taste

Instructions

Now, let’s dive into the step-by-step process of making this delectable Lemon Herb Baked Salmon:

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C) and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.

  2. Place the salmon fillets on the prepared baking sheet, skin-side down. Drizzle the olive oil over the salmon, ensuring it is evenly coated.

  3. In a small bowl, combine the minced garlic, lemon zest, lemon juice, dill, parsley, salt, and pepper. Mix well to create a flavorful herb mixture.

  4. Spoon the herb mixture over the salmon fillets, spreading it evenly to cover the surface. The aromatic blend of herbs and lemon will infuse the salmon with a burst of freshness.

  5. Once the salmon is coated with the herb mixture, transfer the baking sheet to the preheated oven. Bake for approximately 15-20 minutes, or until the salmon is cooked through and flakes easily with a fork.

  6. Remove the salmon from the oven and let it rest for a few minutes. This will allow the flavors to meld together, resulting in a perfectly seasoned dish.

  7. Serve the Lemon Herb Baked Salmon hot and garnish with additional fresh herbs, if desired. This dish pairs well with roasted vegetables or a light salad, making it a complete and satisfying meal.

Health Benefits

Not only does this Lemon Herb Baked Salmon recipe tantalize your taste buds, but it also provides a host of health benefits. Salmon is a rich source of omega-3 fatty acids, which are essential for heart health and brain function. The fresh herbs add a dose of antioxidants and anti-inflammatory properties, further enhancing the nutritional value of this dish.

Conclusion

In conclusion, the Lemon Herb Baked Salmon recipe is a true delight for both the palate and the body. Its vibrant flavors, combined with the healthful properties of salmon and fresh herbs, make it a must-try dish for any seafood lover. Whether you’re preparing a special meal for guests or enjoying a cozy dinner at home, this recipe is guaranteed to impress. So, savor the flavor and indulge in the goodness of this Lemon Herb Baked Salmon recipe today!

Note: The mentioned cooking times are approximate and may vary depending on the thickness of the salmon fillets. Always ensure that the salmon is cooked to a safe internal temperature of 145°F (63°C) before consuming.
