Valentine's Day BBQ for Two: Grilled Steak and Potato Recipe


Valentine’s Day is the perfect occasion to show your loved one how much you care, and what better way to do that than with a romantic BBQ for two? This Grilled Steak and Potato recipe is sure to impress your significant other and create a memorable dining experience right in the comfort of your own backyard. So, fire up the grill and get ready to create a delicious meal that will make this Valentine’s Day one to remember.

Ingredients

For the Steak:

  • 2 high-quality ribeye steaks
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons of olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

For the Potatoes:

  • 4 medium-sized potatoes
  • 2 tablespoons of butter
  • 1 tablespoon of fresh rosemary, chopped
  • Salt and pepper to taste

For the Salad:

  • Mixed greens
  • Cherry tomatoes
  • Balsamic vinaigrette

For the Romantic Ambiance:

  • Candlelit table setting
  • Soft music
  • A bottle of your favorite wine

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Steak:

    • Remove the steaks from the refrigerator and let them sit at room temperature for 30 minutes.
    • In a small bowl, mix the minced garlic, olive oil, salt, and pepper.
    • Rub the garlic and oil mixture onto both sides of the steaks, ensuring they are evenly coated.
    • Preheat the grill to high heat.

  2. Grill the Steak:

    • Place the steaks on the hot grill and cook for 4-5 minutes on each side for medium-rare, or to your desired level of doneness.
    • Once cooked, remove the steaks from the grill and let them rest for 5 minutes before serving.

  3. Prepare the Potatoes:

    • Wash and dry the potatoes, then cut them into wedges.
    • In a small bowl, melt the butter and mix in the chopped rosemary, salt, and pepper.
    • Toss the potato wedges in the butter mixture until evenly coated.

  4. Grill the Potatoes:

    • Place the potato wedges on a piece of aluminum foil and fold the edges to create a packet.
    • Grill the potato packet over medium heat for 20-25 minutes, or until the potatoes are tender and lightly browned.

  5. Assemble the Salad:

    • In a large bowl, toss the mixed greens and cherry tomatoes with balsamic vinaigrette.

  6. Set the Ambiance:

    • Prepare a beautifully decorated table with a candlelit setting and soft music to create a romantic atmosphere.
    • Pour a glass of wine for you and your loved one to enjoy while dining.

  7. Serve and Enjoy:

    • Place the grilled steaks and potatoes on a plate, and serve with the mixed green salad on the side.
    • Enjoy your romantic Valentine’s Day BBQ for two, savoring each bite and the special moments shared with your loved one.

Conclusion

This Grilled Steak and Potato recipe is a delightful way to celebrate Valentine’s Day with your significant other. The sizzling steaks, flavorful potatoes, and fresh salad create a well-rounded meal that is both delicious and heartwarming. By taking the time to prepare this meal and setting a romantic ambiance, you are sure to make this Valentine’s Day a truly memorable and special occasion for you and your loved one. Cheers to love, good food, and cherished moments together!
