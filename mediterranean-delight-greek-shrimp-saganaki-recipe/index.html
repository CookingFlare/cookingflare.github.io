Mediterranean Delight: Greek Shrimp Saganaki Recipe


If you’re a seafood lover looking to add a Mediterranean twist to your dinner table, then this Greek Shrimp Saganaki recipe is just what you need. Bursting with flavors and rich in tradition, this dish is sure to transport your taste buds to the sunny shores of Greece. With a combination of succulent shrimp, tangy tomatoes, and a hint of feta cheese, this recipe is a true Mediterranean delight.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound large shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 red bell pepper, diced
  • 1 yellow bell pepper, diced
  • 1 can (14 ounces) diced tomatoes
  • 1/2 cup tomato sauce
  • 1/2 cup white wine (optional)
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried thyme
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 cup crumbled feta cheese
  • Fresh parsley, chopped (for garnish)

Instructions

  1. In a large skillet, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the chopped onion and minced garlic, and sauté until they become fragrant and translucent.

  2. Add the diced red and yellow bell peppers to the skillet and cook for another 3-4 minutes, until they soften slightly.

  3. Increase the heat to medium-high and add the shrimp to the skillet. Cook for about 2 minutes on each side, until they turn pink and are just cooked through. Remove the shrimp from the skillet and set aside.

  4. In the same skillet, add the diced tomatoes, tomato sauce, and white wine (if using). Stir in the dried oregano, dried thyme, salt, and pepper. Reduce the heat to low and simmer for about 10 minutes, allowing the flavors to meld together.

  5. Return the cooked shrimp to the skillet and stir gently to coat them with the tomato sauce. Cook for an additional 2 minutes, until the shrimp are heated through.

  6. Sprinkle the crumbled feta cheese over the top of the shrimp mixture and cover the skillet. Allow the cheese to melt for a minute or two.

  7. Remove the skillet from the heat and garnish with fresh chopped parsley. Serve the Greek Shrimp Saganaki hot, accompanied by crusty bread or cooked rice.

Why You’ll Love This Recipe

The Greek Shrimp Saganaki is a perfect example of the vibrant and healthy Mediterranean cuisine. The combination of fresh seafood, aromatic herbs, and tangy tomatoes creates a burst of flavors that will leave you craving for more. The shrimp is cooked to perfection, tender and juicy, while the feta cheese adds a creamy and salty element that complements the dish beautifully.

Not only is this recipe delicious, but it is also incredibly versatile. You can serve it as an appetizer, a main course, or even as part of a mezze platter. It pairs well with a variety of side dishes, such as roasted vegetables, Greek salad, or tzatziki sauce.

In addition to being a tasty treat, the Greek Shrimp Saganaki also offers numerous health benefits. Shrimp is an excellent source of lean protein and is low in calories. Tomatoes are rich in antioxidants and vitamins, while olive oil provides heart-healthy monounsaturated fats. So, you can indulge in this dish guilt-free.

Final Thoughts

The Greek Shrimp Saganaki recipe is a true Mediterranean delight that will transport you to the sunny shores of Greece with every bite. It’s a simple yet flavorful dish that is sure to impress your family and friends. So, why not bring a taste of the Mediterranean to your dinner table tonight? Give this recipe a try and discover the pleasure of Greek cuisine! Enjoy!
