Delicious Italian Sausage Soup Recipes to Warm You Up


Winter is the perfect time to curl up with a steaming bowl of soup, and what better way to warm your soul than with a delicious Italian sausage soup? Bursting with flavor and hearty ingredients, these soups are sure to satisfy your cravings and keep you cozy on those chilly nights. From classic recipes to unique twists, here are some mouthwatering Italian sausage soup recipes to try.

1. Classic Italian Sausage and Tomato Soup

Prep Time: 10 minutes
Cook Time: 30 minutes
Total Time: 40 minutes
Servings: 4

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound Italian sausage, casings removed
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can diced tomatoes
  • 4 cups chicken broth
  • 2 cups chopped spinach
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a large pot, cook the Italian sausage over medium heat until browned. Remove the sausage from the pot and set aside.
  2. In the same pot, sauté the onion and garlic until fragrant and translucent.
  3. Add the diced tomatoes, chicken broth, dried basil, salt, and pepper to the pot. Bring to a simmer.
  4. Return the cooked sausage to the pot and let the soup simmer for 15 minutes to allow the flavors to meld together.
  5. Stir in the chopped spinach and cook for an additional 5 minutes.
  6. Serve hot and enjoy!

2. Creamy Italian Sausage and Tortellini Soup

Prep Time: 15 minutes
Cook Time: 40 minutes
Total Time: 55 minutes
Servings: 6

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound Italian sausage, casings removed
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 4 cups chicken broth
  • 2 cups half-and-half
  • 1 can diced tomatoes
  • 1 package cheese tortellini
  • 2 cups chopped kale
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a large pot, cook the Italian sausage over medium heat until browned. Remove the sausage from the pot and set aside.
  2. In the same pot, sauté the onion and garlic until fragrant and translucent.
  3. Add the chicken broth, half-and-half, diced tomatoes, dried oregano, salt, and pepper to the pot. Bring to a simmer.
  4. Return the cooked sausage to the pot and let the soup simmer for 30 minutes.
  5. Meanwhile, cook the cheese tortellini according to the package instructions.
  6. Add the cooked tortellini and chopped kale to the soup. Cook for an additional 5 minutes.
  7. Serve hot and enjoy the creamy goodness!

3. Spicy Italian Sausage and White Bean Soup

Prep Time: 10 minutes
Cook Time: 40 minutes
Total Time: 50 minutes
Servings: 6

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound Italian sausage, casings removed
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 4 cups chicken broth
  • 2 cans white beans, drained and rinsed
  • 1 can diced tomatoes
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • 1 teaspoon red pepper flakes (adjust to taste)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley for garnish

Instructions:

  1. In a large pot, cook the Italian sausage over medium heat until browned. Remove the sausage from the pot and set aside.
  2. In the same pot, sauté the onion and garlic until fragrant and translucent.
  3. Add the chicken broth, white beans, diced tomatoes, dried thyme, red pepper flakes, salt, and pepper to the pot. Bring to a simmer.
  4. Return the cooked sausage to the pot and let the soup simmer for 30 minutes.
  5. Serve hot, garnished with fresh parsley.

Final Thoughts

Italian sausage soup is a comforting and flavorful dish that will warm you up from the inside out. Whether you prefer a classic tomato-based soup, a creamy tortellini delight, or a spicy white bean creation, these recipes are sure to please your taste buds. So, grab your apron, gather the ingredients, and get ready to indulge in the deliciousness of Italian sausage soup. Stay warm and enjoy!
