Thai Massaman Curry with Beef and Potatoes Recipe


If you’re a fan of Thai cuisine, then you’re in for a treat with this delicious and aromatic Thai Massaman Curry with Beef and Potatoes recipe. Bursting with flavors and spices, this hearty dish is sure to impress your taste buds and leave you wanting more. The combination of tender beef, creamy coconut milk, and fragrant spices creates a symphony of flavors that will transport you straight to the bustling streets of Thailand.

Ingredients

To create this mouthwatering dish, you will need the following ingredients:

  • 500g beef, thinly sliced
  • 2 large potatoes, peeled and cubed
  • 1 can (400ml) coconut milk
  • 2 tablespoons massaman curry paste
  • 1 onion, thinly sliced
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • 2 tablespoons fish sauce
  • 1 tablespoon tamarind paste
  • 1 tablespoon palm sugar (or brown sugar)
  • 1 cinnamon stick
  • 3 cardamom pods
  • 3 bay leaves
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon ground coriander
  • 1/2 cup roasted peanuts, roughly chopped
  • Fresh cilantro, for garnish

Instructions

Now, let’s dive into the step-by-step process of creating this tantalizing Thai Massaman Curry with Beef and Potatoes:

  1. Heat the vegetable oil in a large pot over medium heat. Add the onion and garlic, and cook until they become translucent and fragrant.

  2. Add the massaman curry paste, ground cumin, ground coriander, cinnamon stick, cardamom pods, and bay leaves to the pot. Stir well to combine and allow the spices to release their flavors.

  3. Add the thinly sliced beef to the pot and cook until it browns on all sides. This will help seal in the flavors of the meat.

  4. Pour in the coconut milk, fish sauce, tamarind paste, and palm sugar. Stir everything together until well combined.

  5. Add the cubed potatoes to the pot, ensuring they are submerged in the curry mixture. Reduce the heat to low, cover the pot, and let it simmer for about 1 to 1.5 hours, or until the beef is tender and the potatoes are cooked through.

  6. While the curry is simmering, take this time to roast the peanuts in a dry pan over medium heat until they become golden brown. Once roasted, roughly chop them and set aside for garnishing.

  7. Once the beef is tender and the potatoes are cooked, remove the cinnamon stick, cardamom pods, and bay leaves from the pot.

  8. Serve the Massaman Curry hot, garnished with the roasted peanuts and fresh cilantro. It pairs perfectly with fragrant jasmine rice or steamed noodles.

Enjoy the Flavors of Thailand!

There you have it - a tantalizing Thai Massaman Curry with Beef and Potatoes recipe that will transport you straight to the vibrant streets of Thailand. The aromatic spices, creamy coconut milk, and tender beef create a harmonious blend of flavors that is sure to satisfy your cravings for authentic Thai cuisine.

So, gather your ingredients, follow the simple steps, and get ready to savor this mouthwatering dish. Whether you’re cooking for yourself or entertaining guests, this Thai Massaman Curry with Beef and Potatoes is guaranteed to be a crowd-pleaser. Enjoy the exotic flavors and embrace the culinary journey that Thailand has to offer!

Note: This recipe serves 4 people, so feel free to adjust the quantities based on your needs.
