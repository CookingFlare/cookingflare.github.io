Pumpkin Spice Latte Cupcakes: A Fall-Inspired Treat


As the leaves turn golden and the air becomes crisp, it’s time to indulge in the flavors of fall. What better way to celebrate the season than with a delightful fusion of pumpkin, spice, and a hint of coffee? Pumpkin Spice Latte Cupcakes are the perfect treat to cozy up with on a chilly autumn day. This recipe takes the beloved flavors of a pumpkin spice latte and transforms them into a moist and flavorful cupcake that will surely become a seasonal favorite.

Ingredients

For the Cupcakes:

  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground ginger
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground nutmeg
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground cloves
  • 1 cup pumpkin puree
  • 1/2 cup brown sugar
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/2 cup vegetable oil
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/2 cup strong brewed coffee, cooled

For the Coffee Frosting:

  • 1 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 3-4 cups powdered sugar
  • 2-3 tablespoons strong brewed coffee, cooled
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • Pinch of salt

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C) and line a muffin tin with cupcake liners.

  2. In a medium bowl, whisk together the flour, baking powder, baking soda, salt, cinnamon, ginger, nutmeg, and cloves.

  3. In a separate large bowl, whisk together the pumpkin puree, brown sugar, granulated sugar, vegetable oil, eggs, and vanilla extract until smooth.

  4. Gradually add the dry ingredients to the wet ingredients, mixing until just combined. Be careful not to overmix.

  5. Stir in the cooled brewed coffee until the batter is smooth and well combined.

  6. Divide the batter evenly among the prepared cupcake liners, filling each about two-thirds full.

  7. Bake for 18-20 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center of a cupcake comes out clean.

  8. Allow the cupcakes to cool in the pan for a few minutes before transferring them to a wire rack to cool completely.

  9. While the cupcakes are cooling, prepare the coffee frosting. In a large bowl, beat the softened butter until creamy.

  10. Gradually add the powdered sugar, 1 cup at a time, until the frosting reaches your desired sweetness and consistency.

  11. Add the brewed coffee, vanilla extract, and a pinch of salt, and beat until light and fluffy.

  12. Once the cupcakes are completely cool, frost them generously with the coffee frosting.

Serving Suggestions

These Pumpkin Spice Latte Cupcakes are best enjoyed with a steaming mug of your favorite coffee or a spiced chai latte. Serve them at autumn gatherings, Halloween parties, or as a delightful mid-morning treat with friends and family.

Conclusion

Pumpkin Spice Latte Cupcakes are a wonderful way to capture the essence of fall in a single bite. The warm spices, rich pumpkin flavor, and subtle hint of coffee come together to create a truly indulgent treat that embodies the spirit of the season. Whether you’re a fan of pumpkin spice lattes or simply love the flavors of fall, these cupcakes are sure to become a beloved addition to your autumn baking repertoire. So, embrace the fall vibes and get ready to savor every delightful bite of these delectable Pumpkin Spice Latte Cupcakes!
