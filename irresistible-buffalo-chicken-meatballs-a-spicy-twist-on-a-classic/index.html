Irresistible Buffalo Chicken Meatballs: A Spicy Twist on a Classic


If you’re a fan of buffalo chicken, then get ready to take your love for this spicy dish to the next level with these Irresistible Buffalo Chicken Meatballs. This recipe puts a tasty twist on the classic buffalo chicken flavor by transforming it into bite-sized, juicy meatballs that are perfect for any occasion. So, fire up your taste buds and let’s get cooking!

Ingredients

For the meatballs:

  • 1 pound ground chicken
  • 1/2 cup breadcrumbs
  • 1/4 cup finely chopped onion
  • 1/4 cup finely chopped celery
  • 1/4 cup crumbled blue cheese
  • 1/4 cup hot sauce
  • 1/4 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon onion powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper

For the buffalo sauce:

  • 1/2 cup hot sauce
  • 1/4 cup unsalted butter
  • 1 tablespoon honey
  • 1 teaspoon Worcestershire sauce

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C) and line a baking sheet with parchment paper. Set aside.

  2. In a large mixing bowl, combine the ground chicken, breadcrumbs, onion, celery, blue cheese, hot sauce, garlic powder, onion powder, salt, and black pepper. Mix well until all the ingredients are evenly incorporated.

  3. Using clean hands, shape the mixture into meatballs, about 1-inch in diameter, and place them on the prepared baking sheet.

  4. Bake the meatballs in the preheated oven for 15-20 minutes, or until they are cooked through and golden brown on the outside.

  5. While the meatballs are baking, prepare the buffalo sauce. In a small saucepan, melt the butter over medium heat. Once melted, add the hot sauce, honey, and Worcestershire sauce. Stir well until the sauce is smooth and combined. Remove from heat.

  6. Once the meatballs are cooked, transfer them to a large mixing bowl and pour the buffalo sauce over them. Gently toss the meatballs in the sauce until they are fully coated.

  7. Serve the Irresistible Buffalo Chicken Meatballs hot with your favorite dipping sauce, celery sticks, and extra blue cheese crumbles for added flavor. Enjoy!

Tips and Variations

  • For an extra kick of heat, add a pinch of cayenne pepper to the meatball mixture.
  • If you prefer a milder flavor, reduce the amount of hot sauce in both the meatballs and the buffalo sauce.
  • These meatballs can be made ahead of time and reheated in the oven or microwave before serving.
  • Serve the meatballs as an appetizer, a main course with a side of rice or mashed potatoes, or even in a sub sandwich for a delicious twist.

Final Thoughts

These Irresistible Buffalo Chicken Meatballs are a fantastic way to enjoy the classic buffalo chicken flavor in a unique and bite-sized form. The combination of juicy ground chicken, tangy hot sauce, and creamy blue cheese creates an explosion of flavors in every mouthful. Whether you’re hosting a party, looking for a crowd-pleasing appetizer, or simply craving a spicy treat, these meatballs are sure to impress. So, gather your ingredients, follow the simple steps, and get ready to savor the irresistible taste of buffalo chicken meatballs!
