A Taste of the Tropics: Gluten-Free Pineapple Fried Rice


Welcome to another delightful recipe on our blog! Today, we’re taking your taste buds on a tropical journey with our mouthwatering Gluten-Free Pineapple Fried Rice. Bursting with flavors and vibrant colors, this dish is perfect for those who crave a taste of the tropics. Whether you’re gluten intolerant or simply looking for a gluten-free option, this recipe will surely satisfy your cravings. So, let’s dive right in!

Ingredients

To create this delectable dish, you’ll need the following ingredients:

  • 2 cups of cooked jasmine rice (cooled)
  • 1 small pineapple, diced
  • 2 tablespoons of gluten-free soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon of sesame oil
  • 1 tablespoon of olive oil
  • 1 red bell pepper, diced
  • 1 yellow bell pepper, diced
  • 1 small onion, diced
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 cup of peas
  • 1 cup of diced carrots
  • 1 cup of diced zucchini
  • 1 cup of diced broccoli
  • 1/4 cup of cashews, chopped
  • Fresh cilantro for garnish

Instructions

  1. In a large skillet or wok, heat the olive oil and sesame oil over medium heat.

  2. Add the diced onion and minced garlic to the skillet, and sauté until the onion becomes translucent and the garlic becomes fragrant.

  3. Next, add the diced bell peppers, peas, carrots, zucchini, and broccoli to the skillet. Stir-fry the vegetables for about 5-7 minutes, until they become tender-crisp.

  4. Push the vegetables to one side of the skillet, and add the beaten eggs to the other side. Scramble the eggs until they are fully cooked.

  5. Now, add the diced pineapple to the skillet and stir-fry for an additional 2-3 minutes, until the pineapple becomes slightly caramelized.

  6. Add the cooked jasmine rice to the skillet, along with the gluten-free soy sauce. Stir everything together until the rice is evenly coated with the sauce and the ingredients are well combined.

  7. Continue cooking for another 5 minutes, stirring occasionally, until the rice is heated through.

  8. Finally, sprinkle the chopped cashews over the fried rice and gently toss them in.

  9. Remove the skillet from the heat and garnish with fresh cilantro for an added burst of flavor and vibrant presentation.

  10. Serve the Gluten-Free Pineapple Fried Rice hot and enjoy the tropical flavors!

Conclusion

Congratulations! You’ve successfully created a tantalizing dish that brings the taste of the tropics to your table. The combination of sweet pineapple, colorful veggies, and aromatic spices makes this Gluten-Free Pineapple Fried Rice a delightful and healthy meal option. It’s a perfect dish to impress your family and friends at gatherings or simply to treat yourself to a taste of paradise. So, go ahead and indulge in this flavorful creation. Bon appétit!

Remember to experiment with different variations by adding protein of your choice, such as shrimp, chicken, or tofu, to make it even more satisfying. Feel free to adjust the spice level according to your preference, and don’t forget to share your delicious creations with us. Stay tuned for more exciting recipes on our blog. Happy cooking!
