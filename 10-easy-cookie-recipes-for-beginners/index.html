10 Easy Cookie Recipes for Beginners


Cookies are one of the most rewarding treats to bake, especially when you’re just getting started in the kitchen. They’re simple, tasty, and bring a sense of accomplishment when that first batch comes out of the oven just right. Whether you’re craving classic chocolate chip cookies or branching out to new flavors, getting the hang of cookie-making doesn’t have to be intimidating. In this guide, we’ll explore 10 foolproof cookie recipes for beginners that will have you baking like a pro in no time.

1. Classic Chocolate Chip Cookies

Starting with a fan-favorite, chocolate chip cookies are iconic for a reason. They’re easy to make, and the result is a chewy, sweet treat that’s universally loved.

Ingredients:

  • 2 ¼ cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • ½ tsp salt
  • 1 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • ¾ cup granulated sugar
  • ¾ cup packed brown sugar
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 cups semisweet chocolate chips

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C) and prepare a baking sheet with parchment paper.
  2. In a bowl, whisk together flour, baking soda, and salt.
  3. In a separate bowl, cream together butter, granulated sugar, and brown sugar until smooth. Add vanilla extract and eggs, then mix until incorporated.
  4. Gradually mix in the dry ingredients until a soft dough forms. Stir in the chocolate chips.
  5. Scoop dough onto the baking sheet, spacing each cookie 2 inches apart.
  6. Bake for 8-10 minutes or until golden brown. Let cool before serving.

2. Sugar Cookies

Sugar cookies are incredibly versatile. They’re perfect on their own or can be decorated for holidays, special occasions, or just because!

Ingredients:

  • 2 ¾ cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • ½ tsp baking powder
  • 1 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1 ½ cups granulated sugar
  • 1 large egg
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C) and line a baking sheet.
  2. Combine flour, baking soda, and baking powder in a bowl.
  3. Cream together butter and sugar until light and fluffy. Add egg and vanilla extract, mixing thoroughly.
  4. Slowly stir in the dry ingredients to form a soft dough.
  5. Roll the dough into 1-inch balls and place them evenly on the baking sheet.
  6. Bake for 8-10 minutes until the edges are lightly golden. Cool before decorating or serving.

3. Peanut Butter Cookies

With their rich, nutty flavor and signature crisscross pattern, peanut butter cookies are a nostalgic treat for kids and adults alike.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup creamy peanut butter
  • ½ cup granulated sugar
  • ½ cup packed brown sugar
  • 1 large egg
  • ½ tsp vanilla extract
  • Optional: A pinch of salt

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C). Line your baking sheet.
  2. In a bowl, mix together peanut butter, sugars, egg, and vanilla extract until smooth. Add salt if desired.
  3. Roll dough into small balls and place them on the baking sheet. Flatten each ball with a fork, making a crisscross pattern.
  4. Bake for 9-11 minutes and allow them to cool completely before enjoying.

4. Oatmeal Raisin Cookies

For those who love hearty and flavorful desserts, oatmeal raisin cookies are a nutritious choice.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1 cup packed brown sugar
  • ½ cup granulated sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 ½ cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1 tsp cinnamon
  • ½ tsp salt
  • 3 cups rolled oats
  • 1 cup raisins

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C) and prepare a baking sheet.
  2. Cream butter and sugars until smooth. Add eggs and vanilla extract, mixing thoroughly.
  3. In a separate bowl, combine flour, baking soda, cinnamon, and salt. Gradually add to the wet mixture.
  4. Fold in oats and raisins to complete the dough.
  5. Drop rounded spoonfuls of dough onto the baking sheet. Bake for 10-12 minutes. Cool and enjoy!

5. Snickerdoodles

Snickerdoodles are soft, cinnamon-spiced cookies that boast a delightful tang from cream of tartar.

Ingredients:

  • 2 ¾ cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp cream of tartar
  • ½ tsp baking soda
  • ¼ tsp salt
  • 1 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1 ½ cups sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 3 tbsp sugar mixed with 1 tbsp cinnamon (for rolling)

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Mix flour, cream of tartar, baking soda, and salt in a bowl.
  3. Cream butter and 1 ½ cups sugar in a separate bowl, then add eggs and combine. Stir in the dry ingredients.
  4. Roll dough into balls and coat each in the cinnamon-sugar mixture.
  5. Place on baking sheet and bake for 10 minutes. Cool before serving.

6. No-Bake Chocolate Cookies

No oven? No problem! These chocolate cookies are ready in minutes and require zero baking skills.

Ingredients:

  • ½ cup unsalted butter
  • 2 cups sugar
  • ½ cup milk
  • 4 tbsp cocoa powder
  • ½ cup peanut butter
  • 3 cups rolled oats
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract

Instructions:

  1. In a saucepan, melt butter, sugar, milk, and cocoa powder over medium heat. Bring to a boil for 1 minute.
  2. Remove from heat and stir in peanut butter, oats, and vanilla extract.
  3. Drop spoonfuls of mixture onto wax paper and let cool until set.

7. Coconut Macaroons

If you’re craving something unique, coconut macaroons deliver a chewy, tropical bite and require minimal ingredients.

Ingredients:

  • 2 ½ cups shredded coconut
  • ¾ cup sweetened condensed milk
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 325°F (165°C).
  2. Mix shredded coconut, condensed milk, and vanilla extract in a bowl until combined.
  3. Scoop small mounds of the mixture onto a baking sheet.
  4. Bake for 12-15 minutes until golden on top. Cool and serve.

8. Double Chocolate Cookies

Chocolate lovers will rejoice with this chewy, fudgy recipe that’s packed with cocoa flavor.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1 egg
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • ½ cup cocoa powder
  • ½ tsp baking powder
  • Pinch of salt
  • 1 cup chocolate chips

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. Cream butter and sugar until smooth, then add the egg and mix well.
  3. Stir in flour, cocoa powder, baking powder, and salt. Fold in chocolate chips.
  4. Roll dough into balls, place on a baking sheet, and bake for 8-10 minutes.

9. Lemon Cookies

Bright and citrusy, lemon cookies offer a refreshing twist to your typical dessert lineup.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup sugar
  • Zest of 1 lemon
  • ½ cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1 large egg
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • 1 ½ cups all-purpose flour
  • ½ tsp baking soda
  • ¼ tsp salt

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. Rub sugar and lemon zest together in a bowl to release citrus oils, then cream with butter until fluffy. Add egg and lemon juice.
  3. Combine flour, baking soda, and salt, mixing into the wet ingredients.
  4. Scoop dough onto a baking sheet and bake for 10-12 minutes.

10. M&M Cookies

Fun and colorful, M&M cookies are perfect for parties, kids, and gifting.

Ingredients:

  • ½ cup unsalted butter, softened
  • ½ cup brown sugar
  • ¼ cup granulated sugar
  • 1 large egg
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 ¼ cups all-purpose flour
  • ½ tsp baking soda
  • ½ tsp salt
  • 1 cup M&M candies

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. Cream butter and sugars together, then add egg and vanilla extract.
  3. Mix in flour, baking soda, and salt. Fold in M&M candies.
  4. Scoop onto a baking sheet and bake for 8-10 minutes.

Final Thoughts

Starting your cookie-baking journey doesn’t have to be overwhelming. With these 10 easy cookie recipes for beginners, you’ll have plenty of delicious options to choose from, whether you’re celebrating a weekend win or treating yourself to a sweet indulgence. Dive in, have fun, and let the aroma of freshly baked cookies transform your kitchen!
