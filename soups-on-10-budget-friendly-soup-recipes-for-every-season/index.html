Soup's On: 10 Budget-Friendly Soup Recipes for Every Season


There’s nothing quite like a warm and comforting bowl of soup. Whether it’s a cold winter’s day or a breezy summer evening, soup has a way of soothing the soul and satisfying the appetite. Plus, it’s a great way to stretch your budget and make the most of what you have in your pantry. In this blog post, we’ll explore 10 budget-friendly soup recipes that are perfect for every season. From hearty stews to light broths, these recipes are sure to become staples in your kitchen.

1. Classic Chicken Noodle Soup

Ingredients:

  • 1 whole chicken, cut into pieces
  • 2 carrots, sliced
  • 2 celery stalks, diced
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 6 cups of chicken broth
  • 2 cups of egg noodles
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a large pot, heat some oil over medium heat. Add the chicken pieces and cook until browned.
  2. Add the carrots, celery, onion, and garlic. Cook until the vegetables are tender.
  3. Pour in the chicken broth and bring to a boil. Reduce heat and simmer for 30 minutes.
  4. Add the egg noodles and cook until tender. Season with salt and pepper.
  5. Serve hot and enjoy!

2. Creamy Tomato Basil Soup

Ingredients:

  • 4 cups of canned tomatoes
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 cup of vegetable broth
  • 1/2 cup of heavy cream
  • Fresh basil leaves, chopped
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a saucepan, sauté the onion and garlic until fragrant.
  2. Add the canned tomatoes and vegetable broth. Bring to a boil and simmer for 15 minutes.
  3. Use an immersion blender to puree the soup until smooth.
  4. Stir in the heavy cream and chopped basil leaves. Season with salt and pepper.
  5. Simmer for an additional 5 minutes, then serve hot.

3. Minestrone Soup

Ingredients:

  • 1 tablespoon of olive oil
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 2 carrots, sliced
  • 2 celery stalks, diced
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 can of diced tomatoes
  • 4 cups of vegetable broth
  • 1 can of kidney beans, rinsed and drained
  • 1 cup of small pasta shells
  • Italian seasoning, to taste
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Heat olive oil in a large pot over medium heat. Add the onion, carrots, celery, and garlic. Cook until the vegetables are tender.
  2. Stir in the diced tomatoes and vegetable broth. Bring to a boil and simmer for 15 minutes.
  3. Add the kidney beans and pasta shells. Cook until the pasta is al dente.
  4. Season with Italian seasoning, salt, and pepper.
  5. Serve hot and enjoy a hearty bowl of minestrone soup.

4. Butternut Squash Soup

Ingredients:

  • 1 butternut squash, peeled and diced
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 4 cups of vegetable broth
  • 1/2 cup of coconut milk
  • 1 teaspoon of curry powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a large pot, sauté the onion and garlic until fragrant.
  2. Add the diced butternut squash and vegetable broth. Bring to a boil and simmer until the squash is tender.
  3. Use an immersion blender to puree the soup until smooth.
  4. Stir in the coconut milk and curry powder. Season with salt and pepper.
  5. Simmer for an additional 5 minutes, then serve hot.

5. Lentil Soup

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup of dried lentils
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 carrots, sliced
  • 2 celery stalks, diced
  • 3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 4 cups of vegetable broth
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 1 teaspoon of cumin
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Rinse the lentils under cold water.
  2. In a large pot, sauté the onion, carrots, celery, and garlic until tender.
  3. Add the lentils, vegetable broth, bay leaf, and cumin. Bring to a boil and simmer for 30 minutes.
  4. Season with salt and pepper.
  5. Serve hot and enjoy a nutritious bowl of lentil soup.

6. Thai Coconut Curry Soup

Ingredients:

  • 1 tablespoon of vegetable oil
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons of red curry paste
  • 1 can of coconut milk
  • 4 cups of vegetable broth
  • 1 cup of diced tofu
  • 1 cup of mixed vegetables (bell peppers, broccoli, carrots)
  • Fresh cilantro, chopped
  • Lime wedges for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Heat vegetable oil in a large pot over medium heat. Add the onion and garlic. Cook until fragrant.
  2. Stir in the red curry paste and cook for 1 minute.
  3. Add the coconut milk and vegetable broth. Bring to a boil and simmer for 10 minutes.
  4. Add the tofu and mixed vegetables. Cook until tender.
  5. Garnish with fresh cilantro and serve with lime wedges.

7. Potato Leek Soup

Ingredients:

  • 4 potatoes, peeled and diced
  • 2 leeks, white and light green parts only, sliced
  • 3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 4 cups of vegetable broth
  • 1 cup of heavy cream
  • Fresh chives, chopped
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a large pot, sauté the leeks and garlic until tender.
  2. Add the diced potatoes and vegetable broth. Bring to a boil and simmer until the potatoes are soft.
  3. Use an immersion blender to puree the soup until smooth.
  4. Stir in the heavy cream and season with salt and pepper.
  5. Simmer for an additional 5 minutes, then serve hot. Garnish with fresh chives.

8. Mexican Tortilla Soup

Ingredients:

  • 1 tablespoon of olive oil
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 can of diced tomatoes
  • 4 cups of vegetable broth
  • 1 cup of frozen corn
  • 1 cup of black beans, rinsed and drained
  • 1 teaspoon of chili powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Tortilla chips for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Heat olive oil in a large pot over medium heat. Add the onion and garlic. Cook until fragrant.
  2. Stir in the diced tomatoes and vegetable broth. Bring to a boil and simmer for 15 minutes.
  3. Add the frozen corn and black beans. Cook until heated through.
  4. Season with chili powder, salt, and pepper.
  5. Serve hot and garnish with crushed tortilla chips.

9. Italian Wedding Soup

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound of ground chicken
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 carrots, sliced
  • 2 celery stalks, diced
  • 3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 6 cups of chicken broth
  • 1 cup of small pasta (such as orzo or ditalini)
  • Fresh spinach leaves
  • Parmesan cheese for garnish
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a large pot, cook the ground chicken until browned. Remove from the pot and set aside.
  2. In the same pot, sauté the onion, carrots, celery, and garlic until tender.
  3. Pour in the chicken broth and bring to a boil. Reduce heat and simmer for 15 minutes.
  4. Add the cooked ground chicken and pasta. Cook until the pasta is al dente.
  5. Stir in the fresh spinach leaves and season with salt and pepper.
  6. Serve hot and garnish with grated Parmesan cheese.

10. Creamy Mushroom Soup

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound of mushrooms, sliced
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 4 cups of vegetable broth
  • 1/2 cup of heavy cream
  • Fresh thyme leaves
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a large pot, sauté the mushrooms, onion, and garlic until tender.
  2. Add the vegetable broth and bring to a boil. Simmer for 15 minutes.
  3. Use an immersion blender to puree the soup until smooth.
  4. Stir in the heavy cream and fresh thyme leaves. Season with salt and pepper.
  5. Simmer for an additional 5 minutes, then serve hot.

Conclusion

Soup is a versatile dish that can be enjoyed all year round. With these 10 budget-friendly soup recipes, you can whip up a delicious and satisfying meal without breaking the bank. From classic chicken noodle soup to creamy mushroom soup, there’s something for everyone’s taste buds. So, grab your soup pot and get cooking! Remember to experiment with different flavors and ingredients to make these recipes your own. Happy soup-making!
