Savory One Pot Mediterranean Quinoa


If you’re looking for a healthy, flavorful, and easy-to-make one-pot dish, then this Savory One Pot Mediterranean Quinoa is the perfect recipe for you. Packed with the vibrant flavors of the Mediterranean, this dish combines the nuttiness of quinoa with an array of wholesome ingredients to create a satisfying and nutritious meal that will surely become a new favorite in your recipe collection.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup quinoa
  • 2 cups vegetable broth
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 small red onion, finely chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1 can (14 oz) diced tomatoes, drained
  • 1 cup chopped spinach
  • 1 cup chopped roasted red peppers
  • 1/2 cup sliced Kalamata olives
  • 1/4 cup crumbled feta cheese
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley for garnish

Instructions

  1. Rinse the Quinoa: Before starting the cooking process, rinse the quinoa under cold water to remove any bitterness.

  2. Sauté the Aromatics: In a large pot or skillet, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the chopped red onion and minced garlic, and sauté until the onion is soft and translucent.

  3. Add the Quinoa and Spices: Add the rinsed quinoa to the pot, and stir it around for a couple of minutes to lightly toast it. Then, add the dried oregano, dried basil, and paprika, stirring to coat the quinoa evenly with the spices.

  4. Pour in the Vegetable Broth and Tomatoes: Pour in the vegetable broth and add the drained diced tomatoes. Stir everything together and bring the mixture to a gentle simmer.

  5. Simmer and Add the Veggies: Cover the pot and let the quinoa simmer for about 15 minutes, or until most of the liquid has been absorbed. Then, add the chopped spinach, roasted red peppers, and Kalamata olives, stirring them into the quinoa.

  6. Finish and Serve: Once the quinoa has absorbed all the liquid and the veggies are tender, remove the pot from the heat. Season with salt and pepper to taste, and sprinkle the crumbled feta cheese over the top. Let the dish sit for a few minutes to allow the flavors to meld together. Finally, garnish with fresh parsley before serving.

Serving Suggestions

This savory one-pot Mediterranean quinoa is a versatile dish that can be enjoyed on its own as a wholesome vegetarian meal, or paired with grilled chicken, fish, or tofu for added protein. It also makes a delicious filling for stuffed bell peppers or tomatoes, and can be served warm or at room temperature, making it perfect for meal prep and packed lunches.

Conclusion

With its rich flavors, vibrant colors, and nutrient-packed ingredients, this Savory One Pot Mediterranean Quinoa is a delightful representation of the healthy and delicious cuisine of the Mediterranean region. Whether you’re a fan of quinoa or looking to incorporate more wholesome grains into your diet, this recipe is a must-try for anyone seeking a simple, yet satisfying meal that celebrates the bountiful flavors of the Mediterranean.

So, gather your ingredients, set aside a pot, and embark on a culinary journey to the Mediterranean with this delightful one-pot quinoa dish. Enjoy the flavors and the health benefits that this recipe has to offer, and share the joy of good food with your friends and family!
