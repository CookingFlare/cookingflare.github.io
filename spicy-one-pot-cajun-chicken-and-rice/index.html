Spicy One Pot Cajun Chicken and Rice


If you’re looking for a delicious and flavorful one-pot meal that will satisfy your taste buds, look no further than this Spicy One Pot Cajun Chicken and Rice recipe. Packed with bold Cajun flavors and tender chicken, this dish is sure to become a family favorite. Plus, the best part is that it can be made in just one pot, making cleanup a breeze!

Ingredients

  • 1 pound chicken thighs, boneless and skinless
  • 2 tablespoons Cajun seasoning
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 1 green bell pepper, diced
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup long-grain rice
  • 1 can diced tomatoes, undrained
  • 2 cups chicken broth
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1/2 teaspoon paprika
  • 1/4 teaspoon cayenne pepper (adjust according to spice preference)
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • Fresh parsley, for garnish

Instructions

  1. Start by seasoning the chicken thighs with Cajun seasoning, ensuring they are evenly coated. Set aside.

  2. Place a large pot or Dutch oven over medium heat and add the olive oil. Once the oil is hot, add the seasoned chicken thighs and cook until browned on both sides, about 5 minutes per side. Remove the chicken from the pot and set aside.

  3. In the same pot, add the diced onion, green bell pepper, and minced garlic. Sauté until the vegetables are softened, about 5 minutes.

  4. Add the rice to the pot and stir to coat it with the vegetable mixture. Cook for an additional 2 minutes, allowing the rice to slightly toast.

  5. Pour in the diced tomatoes, chicken broth, dried thyme, dried oregano, paprika, cayenne pepper, salt, and pepper. Stir well to combine all the ingredients.

  6. Return the chicken thighs to the pot, nestling them into the rice mixture. Bring the pot to a boil, then reduce the heat to low and cover with a lid. Let the dish simmer for 20-25 minutes, or until the rice is cooked and the chicken is tender.

  7. Once cooked, remove the pot from the heat and let it sit for 5 minutes before removing the lid. This will allow the flavors to meld together.

  8. Serve the Spicy One Pot Cajun Chicken and Rice hot, garnished with fresh parsley for a touch of freshness.

Tips and Variations

  • For an extra kick of heat, add additional cayenne pepper or a few dashes of hot sauce.
  • Feel free to customize this dish by adding your favorite vegetables such as corn, peas, or diced zucchini.
  • If you prefer a milder flavor, reduce the amount of Cajun seasoning or omit the cayenne pepper altogether.
  • Leftovers can be stored in an airtight container in the refrigerator for up to 3 days. Simply reheat in the microwave or on the stovetop.

Enjoy this Spicy One Pot Cajun Chicken and Rice recipe as a hearty and satisfying meal that will transport your taste buds to the flavors of Louisiana. Its simplicity and bold flavors make it perfect for a weeknight dinner or a casual get-together with friends and family. Give it a try and let your senses indulge in the comforting and spicy goodness of this one-pot wonder!
