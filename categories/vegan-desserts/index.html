Vegan Peanut Butter and Jelly Bars Recipe with a Graham Cracker Crust and a Crunchy Topping

If you’re a fan of the classic peanut butter and jelly combination, then you’re in for a treat with this vegan twist on a beloved favorite. These Vegan Peanut Butter and Jelly Bars with a Graham Cracker Crust and a Crunchy Topping are the perfect blend of sweet, salty, and crunchy, making them an irresistible dessert or snack option for vegans and non-vegans alike. Ingredients For the Graham Cracker Crust: 2 cups vegan graham cracker crumbs 1/4 cup coconut sugar 1/2 cup melted coconut oil 1/4 teaspoon salt For the Peanut Butter Filling: 1 1/2 cups creamy peanut butter 1/2 cup maple syrup 1/4 cup coconut oil, melted 1 teaspoon vanilla extract 1/4 teaspoon salt For the Jelly Layer: 1 1/2 cups of your favorite fruit jelly or jam For the Crunchy Topping: 1 cup rolled oats 1/2 cup chopped peanuts 1/4 cup maple syrup 2 tablespoons coconut oil, melted 1/4 teaspoon salt Instructions Step 1: Prepare the Graham Cracker Crust Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C) and line a 9x9-inch baking pan with parchment paper. In a mixing bowl, combine the vegan graham cracker crumbs, coconut sugar, melted coconut oil, and salt. Mix until the crumbs are evenly coated. Press the crumb mixture firmly into the bottom of the prepared baking pan. Bake the crust for 10 minutes, then remove from the oven and set aside to cool. Step 2: Make the Peanut Butter Filling In a microwave-safe bowl, heat the creamy peanut butter, maple syrup, melted coconut oil, vanilla extract, and salt until smooth and well combined. Pour the peanut butter filling over the cooled graham cracker crust and spread it into an even layer. Step 3: Add the Jelly Layer Gently warm the fruit jelly or jam in a small saucepan over low heat until it becomes easier to spread. Carefully spread the jelly over the peanut butter layer, creating an even layer of jelly. Step 4: Prepare the Crunchy Topping In a mixing bowl, combine the rolled oats, chopped peanuts, maple syrup, melted coconut oil, and salt. Stir until all the ingredients are well combined. Sprinkle the crunchy topping evenly over the jelly layer. Step 5: Bake and Cool Bake the assembled bars for 25-30 minutes, or until the topping is golden brown. Remove the pan from the oven and allow the bars to cool completely at room temperature, then transfer to the refrigerator to chill for at least 2 hours before slicing. Serving and Storing Once chilled, lift the bars out of the pan using the parchment paper overhang and transfer them to a cutting board. Use a sharp knife to slice the bars into squares or rectangles. These Vegan Peanut Butter and Jelly Bars can be stored in an airtight container in the refrigerator for up to 1 week, but they’re so delicious, they probably won’t last that long! ...
