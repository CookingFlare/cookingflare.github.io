Savory and Spicy Thai Pineapple Fried Rice


If you’re looking to add a burst of flavor to your dinner table, this Savory and Spicy Thai Pineapple Fried Rice recipe is just the thing you need. The combination of sweet pineapple, savory spices, and a hint of heat creates a mouthwatering dish that will transport your taste buds to the bustling streets of Thailand. This recipe is perfect for a weeknight dinner or for impressing guests at a dinner party. Let’s dive into the details of this delectable dish.

Ingredients:

  • 3 cups of cooked jasmine rice
  • 1 cup of fresh pineapple, diced
  • 1 red bell pepper, diced
  • 1 yellow bell pepper, diced
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 2 eggs, lightly beaten
  • 1/2 cup of roasted cashew nuts
  • 1/2 cup of green peas
  • 3 tablespoons of soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons of fish sauce
  • 1 tablespoon of curry powder
  • 1 teaspoon of chili flakes
  • Fresh cilantro for garnish
  • Lime wedges for serving

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the Ingredients: Start by cooking the jasmine rice according to the package instructions. Once cooked, allow it to cool completely. Meanwhile, dice the pineapple, bell peppers, and onion. Mince the garlic and beat the eggs in a small bowl.

  2. Stir-fry the Aromatics: Heat a large wok or skillet over medium-high heat. Add a splash of oil and sauté the minced garlic and chopped onion until fragrant.

  3. Add the Bell Peppers and Pineapple: Toss in the diced red and yellow bell peppers, and the fresh pineapple. Stir-fry for a few minutes until the peppers are slightly tender and the pineapple caramelizes.

  4. Push the Ingredients to the Side: Push the ingredients to one side of the wok, making room for the beaten eggs. Pour the eggs into the empty side of the wok and scramble them until just set.

  5. Incorporate the Rice: Add the cooled jasmine rice to the wok, breaking up any clumps as you stir-fry.

  6. Season the Rice: Drizzle the soy sauce and fish sauce over the rice, then sprinkle the curry powder and chili flakes. Stir everything together until the rice is evenly coated and heated through.

  7. Add the Final Touches: Toss in the green peas and roasted cashew nuts, stirring until everything is well combined.

  8. Serve and Garnish: Transfer the fragrant Thai Pineapple Fried Rice to a serving platter. Garnish with fresh cilantro and serve with lime wedges on the side for a bright, citrusy finish.

Enjoy the Burst of Flavors

This Savory and Spicy Thai Pineapple Fried Rice is a delightful explosion of flavors and textures. The sweetness of the pineapple, the crunch of the cashew nuts, and the hint of spice from the chili flakes come together to create a truly satisfying dish. Whether you’re a fan of Thai cuisine or new to its flavors, this recipe is sure to become a favorite in your culinary repertoire. So, roll up your sleeves, gather your ingredients, and embark on a culinary adventure with this delightful Thai-inspired dish. Bon appétit!
