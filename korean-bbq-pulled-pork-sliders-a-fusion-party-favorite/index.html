Korean BBQ Pulled Pork Sliders: A Fusion Party Favorite


If you’re looking to elevate your party food game, look no further than these Korean BBQ pulled pork sliders. This fusion dish combines the smoky, savory flavors of traditional American BBQ pulled pork with the bold, spicy, and slightly sweet flavors of Korean BBQ. The result? A mouthwatering, finger-licking slider that will have your guests coming back for more.

Ingredients

For the Pulled Pork:

  • 3 lbs pork shoulder
  • 1 cup soy sauce
  • 1/2 cup brown sugar
  • 1/4 cup rice vinegar
  • 1/4 cup sesame oil
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tbsp grated ginger
  • 1/4 cup gochujang (Korean chili paste)
  • 1/4 cup ketchup
  • 1/4 cup honey
  • 1 tsp black pepper
  • Slider buns

For the Slaw:

  • 2 cups shredded cabbage
  • 1 carrot, julienned
  • 1/4 cup mayo
  • 1 tbsp rice vinegar
  • Salt and pepper to taste

For the Sauce:

  • Reserved cooking liquid
  • 1/4 cup gochujang
  • 2 tbsp brown sugar
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp sesame oil

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Pork: Start by seasoning the pork shoulder with salt and pepper. In a slow cooker, combine the soy sauce, brown sugar, rice vinegar, sesame oil, onion, garlic, ginger, gochujang, ketchup, honey, and black pepper. Place the pork shoulder in the slow cooker and cook on low for 8 hours or until the pork is tender and easily shreddable.

  2. Make the Slaw: In a bowl, mix together the shredded cabbage, julienned carrot, mayo, rice vinegar, salt, and pepper. Refrigerate until ready to use.

  3. Shred the Pork: Once the pork is cooked, remove it from the slow cooker and shred it using two forks. Reserve the cooking liquid for the sauce.

  4. Make the Sauce: In a saucepan, combine the reserved cooking liquid, gochujang, brown sugar, soy sauce, and sesame oil. Simmer until the sauce thickens slightly.

  5. Assemble the Sliders: Toast the slider buns and layer each with a generous amount of the Korean BBQ pulled pork, a spoonful of the sauce, and a dollop of the slaw.

  6. Serve and Enjoy: Arrange the sliders on a platter and watch as your guests devour this delicious fusion creation.

Why Korean BBQ Pulled Pork Sliders?

Fusion of Flavors

The marriage of American and Korean BBQ flavors creates a unique and irresistible taste experience. The smoky richness of the pulled pork pairs perfectly with the spicy, tangy, and sweet notes of the Korean BBQ sauce.

Perfect Party Food

These sliders are perfect for any gathering, from game day to backyard barbecues. They are easy to eat, packed with flavor, and sure to be a hit with your guests.

Versatility

Not only are these sliders great for parties, but they also make a satisfying weeknight dinner. The leftover pulled pork can be used in tacos, rice bowls, or even on top of a pizza for a fun twist.

Crowd Pleaser

The combination of tender, juicy pulled pork, zesty slaw, and flavorful sauce is a guaranteed crowd-pleaser. Your guests will be raving about these sliders long after the party is over.

So, the next time you’re planning a gathering or simply craving a flavor-packed meal, consider whipping up a batch of these Korean BBQ pulled pork sliders. They’re a delicious fusion of two culinary worlds that is sure to leave a lasting impression on your taste buds.
