Blueberry Hand Pies: A Portable Pastry Delight


Hand pies are a delightful treat that combines the goodness of pie with the convenience of a handheld snack. Perfect for picnics, lunchboxes, or simply enjoying on-the-go, these blueberry hand pies are sure to satisfy your cravings for something sweet and satisfying. With a flaky, buttery crust and a burst of juicy blueberry filling, they are a true pastry lover’s dream.

Ingredients

For the Crust:

  • 2 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tablespoon granulated sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup unsalted butter, cold and cut into cubes
  • 6-8 tablespoons ice water

For the Filling:

  • 2 cups fresh or frozen blueberries
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 tablespoon cornstarch
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 1/2 teaspoon lemon zest (optional)
  • 1/4 teaspoon cinnamon (optional)

For Assembly:

  • 1 egg, beaten (for egg wash)
  • Coarse sugar (for sprinkling)

Instructions

1. Prepare the Blueberry Filling

In a medium saucepan, combine the blueberries, sugar, cornstarch, lemon juice, lemon zest (if using), and cinnamon (if using). Stir gently to coat the blueberries with the sugar and cornstarch mixture.

Cook over medium heat, stirring frequently, until the mixture begins to thicken and the blueberries release their juices, about 8-10 minutes. Remove from heat and let the filling cool completely before using.

2. Make the Pie Crust

In a large mixing bowl, whisk together the flour, sugar, and salt. Add the cold cubed butter to the flour mixture.

Using a pastry cutter or your fingers, work the butter into the flour until the mixture resembles coarse crumbs with some larger pea-sized pieces of butter remaining.

Gradually add the ice water, 1 tablespoon at a time, mixing with a fork or your hands until the dough begins to come together. You may not need to use all the water.

Turn the dough out onto a lightly floured surface and knead it gently until it comes together into a cohesive ball. Divide the dough in half, shape each half into a disk, wrap them tightly in plastic wrap, and refrigerate for at least 1 hour.

3. Assemble the Hand Pies

Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C). Line a baking sheet with parchment paper.

On a lightly floured surface, roll out one disk of chilled dough to about 1/8 inch thickness. Using a 4-5 inch round cookie cutter or a small bowl as a guide, cut out circles from the dough. Gather and reroll any scraps to make more circles.

Place about 1-2 tablespoons of the cooled blueberry filling onto the center of each dough circle. Fold the dough over the filling to create a half-moon shape. Press the edges together firmly with your fingers or a fork to seal the pies. Repeat with the remaining dough and filling.

4. Bake the Hand Pies

Transfer the assembled hand pies to the prepared baking sheet. Brush the tops of the pies with the beaten egg and sprinkle with coarse sugar.

Using a sharp knife, make a small slit or two on the tops of each pie to allow steam to escape during baking.

Bake in the preheated oven for 20-25 minutes, or until the pies are golden brown and the filling is bubbly. Remove from the oven and let cool on the baking sheet for 5-10 minutes before transferring to a wire rack to cool completely.

5. Enjoy!

These blueberry hand pies are best enjoyed fresh on the day they are made, but they can be stored in an airtight container at room temperature for up to 2 days. They can also be frozen for longer storage; just thaw at room temperature or reheat gently in the oven before serving.

Whether you’re looking for a portable dessert for a summer outing or a sweet treat to enjoy with your afternoon tea, these blueberry hand pies are sure to delight. The combination of flaky crust and juicy blueberry filling makes them irresistible to pastry lovers of all ages. Try making a batch today and share them with friends and family—they’re guaranteed to disappear quickly!

This recipe for blueberry hand pies is a testament to the joy of homemade baking. With simple ingredients and straightforward instructions, you can create a batch of these delicious pastries to enjoy anytime. Treat yourself to the satisfaction of baking from scratch and savoring the fruits of your labor with every bite.
