Savory Pork Stir Fry Recipes for Meat Lovers


If you’re a meat lover and a fan of quick and delicious meals, then pork stir fry recipes are a must-try. The combination of tender pork, vibrant vegetables, and flavorful sauces makes for a satisfying and savory dish that is sure to tantalize your taste buds. In this blog post, we’ll explore three mouthwatering pork stir fry recipes that are perfect for meat lovers looking to add some excitement to their dinner menu.

Classic Pork and Vegetable Stir Fry

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound pork tenderloin, thinly sliced
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon oyster sauce
  • 1 tablespoon hoisin sauce
  • 1 tablespoon cornstarch
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1 red bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 green bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 cup snap peas
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon fresh ginger, grated
  • Cooked white rice, for serving

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, combine the sliced pork with soy sauce, oyster sauce, hoisin sauce, and cornstarch. Allow the pork to marinate for 15 minutes.
  2. In a large skillet or wok, heat the vegetable oil over high heat. Add the marinated pork and stir-fry until browned and cooked through. Remove the pork from the skillet and set aside.
  3. In the same skillet, add the bell peppers, snap peas, garlic, and ginger. Stir-fry for 2-3 minutes until the vegetables are tender-crisp.
  4. Return the cooked pork to the skillet and toss to combine with the vegetables.
  5. Serve the pork and vegetable stir fry over cooked white rice.

Spicy Garlic Pork Stir Fry

Ingredients:

  • 1 ½ pounds pork loin, thinly sliced
  • 3 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons rice vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon honey
  • 2 teaspoons cornstarch
  • 3 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1 onion, thinly sliced
  • 1 red chili, thinly sliced
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 small head broccoli, cut into florets
  • 1 teaspoon sesame seeds, for garnish
  • Cooked noodles, for serving

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, mix the sliced pork with soy sauce, rice vinegar, honey, and cornstarch. Let the pork marinate for 20 minutes.
  2. Heat 2 tablespoons of vegetable oil in a large skillet or wok over medium-high heat. Add the marinated pork and stir-fry until golden brown and cooked through. Remove the pork from the skillet and set aside.
  3. In the same skillet, add the remaining tablespoon of vegetable oil. Sauté the onion, red chili, and garlic until fragrant.
  4. Add the broccoli florets to the skillet and stir-fry for 3-4 minutes until tender.
  5. Return the cooked pork to the skillet and toss to combine with the vegetables.
  6. Serve the spicy garlic pork stir fry over cooked noodles, garnished with sesame seeds.

Honey Sesame Pork and Snow Pea Stir Fry

Ingredients:

  • 1 ½ pounds pork shoulder, thinly sliced
  • ¼ cup soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons honey
  • 1 tablespoon sesame oil
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon grated fresh ginger
  • 2 cups snow peas, trimmed
  • 2 green onions, sliced
  • 2 tablespoons sesame seeds
  • Cooked brown rice, for serving

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, combine the sliced pork with soy sauce, honey, sesame oil, garlic, and ginger. Let the pork marinate for 30 minutes.
  2. Heat a large skillet or wok over medium-high heat. Add the marinated pork and stir-fry until caramelized and cooked through. Remove the pork from the skillet and set aside.
  3. In the same skillet, add the snow peas and green onions. Stir-fry for 2-3 minutes until the vegetables are tender-crisp.
  4. Return the cooked pork to the skillet and toss to combine with the snow peas and green onions.
  5. Sprinkle the honey sesame pork and snow pea stir fry with sesame seeds and serve over cooked brown rice.

These savory pork stir fry recipes are a fantastic way to elevate your cooking game and satisfy your cravings for a hearty and flavorful meal. Whether you’re a seasoned chef or a novice cook, these recipes are easy to prepare and are guaranteed to impress your family and friends. So, grab your ingredients and get ready to embark on a culinary adventure with these delectable pork stir fry dishes. Happy cooking!
