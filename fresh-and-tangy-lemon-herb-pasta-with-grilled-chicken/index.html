Fresh and Tangy Lemon Herb Pasta with Grilled Chicken


Are you looking for a delicious and refreshing dish to satisfy your taste buds? Look no further! This Fresh and Tangy Lemon Herb Pasta with Grilled Chicken recipe is the perfect combination of flavors and textures that will leave you wanting more. With the bright and zesty flavors of lemon and the aromatic herbs, this dish is sure to be a crowd-pleaser.

Ingredients

  • 8 ounces of pasta (linguine or spaghetti works well)
  • 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 2 tablespoons of olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • Zest and juice of 1 lemon
  • 1 tablespoon of fresh thyme, chopped
  • 1 tablespoon of fresh parsley, chopped
  • 1 tablespoon of fresh basil, chopped
  • 1/4 cup of grated parmesan cheese

Instructions

  1. Cook the pasta according to the package instructions until al dente. Drain and set aside.

  2. Preheat your grill to medium-high heat. Season the chicken breasts with salt and pepper, then brush them with olive oil. Place the chicken on the grill and cook for about 6-8 minutes per side, or until the internal temperature reaches 165°F. Remove from the grill and let the chicken rest for a few minutes before slicing it into thin strips.

  3. In a large skillet, heat the remaining olive oil over medium heat. Add the minced garlic and sauté for about a minute, or until fragrant.

  4. Add the cooked pasta to the skillet and toss to coat it with the garlic-infused oil. Stir in the lemon zest and juice, followed by the chopped thyme, parsley, and basil. Season with salt and pepper to taste.

  5. Finally, add the sliced grilled chicken to the pasta and gently toss to combine. Cook for another 2-3 minutes, or until everything is heated through.

  6. Remove the skillet from the heat and sprinkle the grated parmesan cheese on top. Give it a final toss to melt the cheese slightly and distribute the flavors evenly.

  7. Serve the Fresh and Tangy Lemon Herb Pasta with Grilled Chicken in shallow bowls, garnished with a sprig of fresh basil or parsley. Enjoy!

Tips and Variations

  • To make this recipe vegetarian-friendly, simply omit the chicken and substitute with grilled vegetables like zucchini, bell peppers, and mushrooms.
  • Feel free to experiment with different herbs, such as rosemary or oregano, to add your own twist to the dish.
  • For an extra burst of freshness, squeeze some additional lemon juice over the pasta just before serving.
  • If you prefer a creamier sauce, you can add a splash of heavy cream or a dollop of Greek yogurt to the skillet when tossing the pasta.

Conclusion

This Fresh and Tangy Lemon Herb Pasta with Grilled Chicken recipe is a delightful combination of flavors that will transport you to a sunny Mediterranean paradise. The tangy lemon, fragrant herbs, and perfectly grilled chicken come together to create a dish that is both refreshing and satisfying. Whether you’re looking for a quick weeknight meal or planning a dinner party, this recipe is sure to impress. Give it a try and let your taste buds rejoice!
