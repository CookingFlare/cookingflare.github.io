Decadent Chocolate Covered Caramel Apples


Who doesn’t love the combination of sweet and tart flavors? When it comes to indulgent treats, it doesn’t get much better than decadent chocolate covered caramel apples. These delightful treats are not only delicious, but they also make for a fun and interactive dessert that the whole family can enjoy. From the crisp and juicy apple to the gooey caramel and rich chocolate coating, each bite is a heavenly experience. In this recipe, we’ll take you through the steps to create your own mouthwatering chocolate covered caramel apples that are sure to impress.

Ingredients:

  • 6 medium-sized apples (preferably Granny Smith)
  • 1 package of caramel candies
  • 1 cup of semi-sweet chocolate chips
  • 1 tablespoon of vegetable oil
  • Optional toppings: crushed nuts, sprinkles, or shredded coconut

Instructions:

  1. Start by washing and thoroughly drying the apples. This step is crucial as it ensures the caramel adheres properly to the apple surface.

  2. Remove the stems from the apples and insert wooden skewers or popsicle sticks into each apple. This will serve as the handle for dipping and eating the caramel apples.

  3. Unwrap the caramel candies and place them in a microwave-safe bowl. Add a tablespoon of water to the bowl to help melt the caramel evenly.

  4. Microwave the caramel in 30-second intervals, stirring each time, until the candies have completely melted and formed a smooth caramel sauce. Be careful as the caramel can become very hot.

  5. Holding the skewer or popsicle stick, dip each apple into the melted caramel, swirling it around to ensure an even coating. Allow any excess caramel to drip off before placing the coated apple on a parchment-lined baking sheet.

  6. Repeat the dipping process for all the apples and let them cool in the refrigerator for about 30 minutes, or until the caramel has set.

  7. In the meantime, prepare the chocolate coating by melting the semi-sweet chocolate chips with a tablespoon of vegetable oil. This will create a smooth and glossy chocolate shell.

  8. Once the caramel has set, remove the apples from the refrigerator. Holding the skewer or popsicle stick, dip each caramel-coated apple into the melted chocolate, again swirling it around to ensure an even coating.

  9. If desired, sprinkle the freshly dipped apples with crushed nuts, sprinkles, or shredded coconut while the chocolate is still wet. This will add extra flavor and texture to your decadent treat.

  10. Place the chocolate covered caramel apples back on the parchment-lined baking sheet and refrigerate them for another 30 minutes, or until the chocolate has hardened.

  11. Once the chocolate has set, your decadent chocolate covered caramel apples are ready to be enjoyed! Serve them as a delightful dessert at parties, gatherings, or simply as a special treat for yourself.

Tips:

  • Experiment with different apple varieties to find your favorite flavor combination. Granny Smith apples work well due to their tartness, which pairs perfectly with the sweet caramel and chocolate.
  • Get creative with the toppings! From crushed cookies to drizzles of white chocolate, there are endless possibilities to customize your chocolate covered caramel apples.
  • Store any leftover apples in an airtight container in the refrigerator for up to two days. Just remember to bring them to room temperature before enjoying to fully appreciate the flavors.

Now that you have the recipe for these decadent chocolate covered caramel apples, it’s time to gather your ingredients and get started. Whether you’re looking for a fun activity to do with the kids or a delicious treat to satisfy your sweet tooth, these apples are a fantastic choice. Enjoy the perfect balance of flavors and textures in every bite, and don’t forget to share this delectable recipe with your friends and family. Happy dipping!
