Creamy and Cheesy Butternut Squash and Sage Risotto


Risotto is a classic Italian dish that has been loved by food enthusiasts for generations. Its creamy texture and rich flavors make it a comforting and satisfying meal, perfect for any occasion. In this recipe, we will be adding a delightful twist to the traditional risotto by incorporating the sweet and nutty flavor of butternut squash, complemented by the earthy aroma of sage. The result is a Creamy and Cheesy Butternut Squash and Sage Risotto that is sure to impress your taste buds and leave you craving for more.

Ingredients

  • 1 small butternut squash, peeled, seeded, and diced
  • 5-6 cups chicken or vegetable broth
  • 1 ½ cups Arborio rice
  • 1 cup dry white wine
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 3-4 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 tablespoon fresh sage, finely chopped
  • ½ cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 4 tablespoons unsalted butter
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • Olive oil

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Butternut Squash: Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C). Place the diced butternut squash on a baking sheet, drizzle with olive oil, and season with salt and pepper. Roast in the preheated oven for 20-25 minutes, or until the squash is tender and lightly caramelized. Set aside.

  2. Simmer the Broth: In a saucepan, heat the chicken or vegetable broth over medium heat. Once it reaches a gentle simmer, reduce the heat to low to keep it warm.

  3. Sauté the Aromatics: In a large, heavy-bottomed pot, heat 2 tablespoons of butter over medium heat. Add the chopped onion and cook until translucent. Stir in the minced garlic and fresh sage, cooking for an additional minute until fragrant.

  4. Toast the Rice: Add the Arborio rice to the pot and stir to coat the grains with the butter and aromatics. Let the rice toast for a couple of minutes until it becomes slightly translucent around the edges.

  5. Deglaze with Wine: Pour in the dry white wine and stir the rice constantly until the wine is absorbed.

  6. Add the Broth: Begin adding the warm broth to the rice, one ladleful at a time, stirring frequently. Allow the rice to absorb the broth before adding more. This process will take about 18-20 minutes, and the rice should be creamy and slightly al dente when done.

  7. Incorporate the Butternut Squash: Gently fold in the roasted butternut squash, being careful not to mash it completely. This will add a beautiful golden hue and a rich, sweet flavor to the risotto.

  8. Finish with Cheese and Butter: Stir in the grated Parmesan cheese and remaining 2 tablespoons of butter, allowing them to melt and create a luscious, creamy texture. Season with salt and black pepper to taste.

  9. Serve and Enjoy: Spoon the Creamy and Cheesy Butternut Squash and Sage Risotto into warm bowls, garnish with a sprinkle of fresh sage, and an extra grating of Parmesan cheese if desired. Serve immediately and savor the wonderful flavors.

This Creamy and Cheesy Butternut Squash and Sage Risotto is a true celebration of the season’s bounty, combining the sweetness of the butternut squash, the earthiness of sage, and the richness of Parmesan cheese into a harmonious and satisfying dish. Whether you’re looking to impress guests at a dinner party or simply indulge in a comforting meal at home, this risotto is bound to become a favorite in your culinary repertoire. Enjoy the process of creating this delicious dish, and savor every spoonful of its creamy goodness.

So there you have it, a delightful twist on the classic risotto that will elevate your cooking game and leave you with a satisfied smile. Try out this recipe and let the creamy, cheesy, and comforting flavors transport you to a culinary paradise!
