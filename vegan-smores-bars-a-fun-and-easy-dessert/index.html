Vegan S'mores Bars: A Fun and Easy Dessert


S’mores are a classic treat that everyone knows and loves. The combination of graham crackers, gooey marshmallows, and rich chocolate creates an irresistible flavor profile that’s synonymous with campfires and outdoor adventures. But what if you could enjoy this comforting dessert year-round, without the need for a campfire? Enter Vegan S’mores Bars—a fun and easy way to bring the flavors of s’mores to your kitchen, while accommodating plant-based diets.

These Vegan S’mores Bars are a delightful twist on the traditional recipe, using simple plant-based ingredients that everyone can enjoy. Whether you’re vegan, gluten-free, or just looking for a delicious new dessert, these bars are sure to satisfy your sweet tooth. Best of all, they’re quick to make, with no baking required!

Why Vegan S’mores Bars?

If you’re wondering why you should try a vegan version of the classic s’mores dessert, there are several reasons:

  1. Inclusive for All Diets: Vegan s’mores bars cater to those who follow plant-based diets, those who have lactose intolerance, or anyone looking to reduce animal product consumption. They’re also ideal for guests with dietary restrictions.

  2. Easy to Make: Unlike traditional s’mores, which require a campfire and can be a bit messy to prepare, these bars can be made quickly and with minimal effort in your own kitchen. The recipe requires no special equipment, just a few basic ingredients.

  3. No Baking: This vegan dessert recipe doesn’t require turning on the oven, making it a perfect summer treat when you want to avoid the heat. It’s a no-bake dessert that’s as easy as mixing and chilling!

  4. Full of Flavor: These vegan s’mores bars offer the same nostalgic flavor of the original s’mores, with a smooth and creamy marshmallow layer, crunchy graham cracker crust, and rich chocolate topping.

Ingredients for Vegan S’mores Bars

To create these vegan s’mores bars, you’ll need just a handful of ingredients. Each component can be easily swapped with alternatives if you have specific dietary needs. Here’s what you’ll need:

For the Crust:

  • Graham crackers: Traditional graham crackers provide the perfect texture and flavor for the base. Look for vegan-friendly graham crackers (most store-bought varieties are vegan, but check the labels to be sure).
  • Vegan butter: Instead of regular butter, use a plant-based butter substitute to keep the crust rich and buttery.
  • Brown sugar: This adds a subtle sweetness and helps bind the crust together. You can use coconut sugar or maple sugar if you prefer a different sweetness option.
  • Salt: A pinch of salt enhances the sweetness and adds a layer of depth to the crust.

For the Marshmallow Layer:

  • Vegan marshmallows: Not all marshmallows are vegan, as traditional marshmallows are made with gelatin. Be sure to choose vegan marshmallows, which are made with plant-based alternatives like agar-agar or konjac.
  • Coconut cream: The richness of coconut cream helps create a creamy, luscious texture for the marshmallow layer.
  • Vanilla extract: A splash of vanilla adds a warm, aromatic flavor to the marshmallow cream.

For the Chocolate Layer:

  • Vegan chocolate chips: Choose a high-quality vegan chocolate, preferably dark chocolate with a cocoa content of 70% or higher. This ensures a rich, deep chocolate flavor.
  • Coconut oil: Adding a little coconut oil helps the chocolate layer set beautifully, giving it a smooth, glossy finish. You can also use vegetable oil or another neutral oil if you prefer.

How to Make Vegan S’mores Bars

Making these bars is a simple and fun process that even beginners can tackle. All you need to do is assemble the layers, chill, and enjoy!

Step 1: Prepare the Crust

  1. Crush the graham crackers: Start by breaking the graham crackers into small pieces. You can do this by placing them in a zip-top bag and using a rolling pin or your hands to crush them into crumbs. Aim for fine crumbs to create a smooth crust.
  2. Mix the crust ingredients: In a bowl, combine the crushed graham crackers with melted vegan butter, brown sugar, and a pinch of salt. Stir everything together until it forms a dough-like texture.
  3. Press the crust into the pan: Transfer the mixture into a lined 9x9-inch baking dish or a similar-sized pan. Press the crumbs firmly into the bottom to form an even layer. You can use the back of a spoon or your fingers to press it down tightly.
  4. Chill the crust: Place the crust in the fridge or freezer for 10-15 minutes to help it firm up while you prepare the next layers.

Step 2: Make the Marshmallow Layer

  1. Melt the marshmallows: In a saucepan, combine the coconut cream and vegan marshmallows over medium heat. Stir continuously until the marshmallows begin to melt and the mixture becomes smooth and creamy. This can take a few minutes, so be patient.
  2. Add vanilla extract: Once the marshmallow mixture is fully melted and smooth, remove it from the heat and stir in the vanilla extract.
  3. Spread the marshmallow layer: Remove the crust from the fridge and pour the melted marshmallow mixture over the graham cracker crust. Use a spatula to spread it evenly across the entire surface.

Step 3: Prepare the Chocolate Layer

  1. Melt the chocolate: In a heatproof bowl, combine the vegan chocolate chips and coconut oil. Melt them together in the microwave in 30-second intervals, stirring in between, until the chocolate is fully melted and smooth.
  2. Pour the chocolate: Once the chocolate is melted, pour it over the marshmallow layer, spreading it evenly with a spatula. Allow the chocolate to cover the entire surface for a beautiful, glossy finish.
  3. Chill the bars: Once the chocolate is spread out, place the pan back in the fridge or freezer to let everything set. This will take about 30 minutes to an hour, depending on how cold your fridge is.

Step 4: Slice and Serve

After the bars have chilled and set, take them out of the fridge and allow them to sit at room temperature for a few minutes. This makes it easier to cut through the chocolate and marshmallow layers. Use a sharp knife to slice them into squares or bars. Serve immediately and enjoy!

Tips for Perfect Vegan S’mores Bars

To make sure your vegan s’mores bars turn out perfect every time, here are a few helpful tips:

  1. Use high-quality ingredients: Since these bars are made with a few simple ingredients, using high-quality graham crackers, chocolate, and marshmallows will make a big difference in flavor.
  2. Let them cool completely: Allow the bars to cool fully before slicing. This helps the layers set and prevents the chocolate from becoming too gooey when you cut into them.
  3. Make them ahead of time: Vegan s’mores bars can be made in advance. In fact, they taste even better after sitting for a day or two in the fridge, as the flavors have more time to meld together.
  4. Vary the chocolate: If you’re a fan of milk chocolate, feel free to swap out dark chocolate for your favorite dairy-free milk chocolate.
  5. Get creative with toppings: If you want to make your s’mores bars extra special, try adding a sprinkle of crushed nuts, a dash of sea salt, or a drizzle of caramel on top before serving.

Why You’ll Love These Vegan S’mores Bars

These Vegan S’mores Bars are the ultimate indulgence. With a perfectly crunchy graham cracker crust, a creamy marshmallow layer, and a rich chocolate topping, every bite is a satisfying combination of textures and flavors. Whether you’re hosting a vegan-friendly gathering, craving a sweet treat, or simply looking for a new dessert to add to your recipe rotation, these bars will hit the spot.

They’re easy to prepare, customizable to your preferences, and guaranteed to impress anyone who tries them. Plus, they’re the perfect way to bring the spirit of s’mores into your home—even if you don’t have a campfire nearby.

Conclusion

Vegan S’mores Bars are a delicious, fun, and easy dessert that will delight anyone with a sweet tooth. Whether you’re vegan, gluten-free, or simply looking for a new dessert to try, this recipe is perfect for all. With no baking required and minimal ingredients, it’s an accessible treat that’s full of nostalgic flavors. So next time you’re in the mood for something sweet, try making these vegan s’mores bars—they’re sure to be a hit!
