Hearty Low Carb Lemon Rosemary Roasted Chicken


If you’re looking for a delicious and satisfying low-carb meal, this Hearty Low Carb Lemon Rosemary Roasted Chicken recipe is a must-try. Bursting with flavors of zesty lemon and aromatic rosemary, this dish is not only healthy but also incredibly flavorful. Plus, it’s easy to make, making it a perfect choice for a busy weeknight dinner or a special weekend meal with friends and family.

Ingredients

  • 1 whole chicken (about 4-5 pounds)
  • 2 lemons, sliced
  • 4 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons of fresh rosemary, finely chopped
  • 2 tablespoons of olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 425°F (220°C).

  2. Begin by preparing the chicken. Make sure it is properly cleaned and pat dry with paper towels. This will help the skin crisp up nicely during roasting.

  3. In a small bowl, combine the minced garlic, chopped rosemary, olive oil, salt, and pepper. Mix well to create a flavorful marinade.

  4. Carefully loosen the skin of the chicken by gently sliding your fingers between the skin and the meat. Be careful not to tear the skin.

  5. Rub the marinade mixture all over the chicken, both on the outside and under the skin. Make sure to evenly distribute the marinade for maximum flavor.

  6. Stuff the cavity of the chicken with the sliced lemons. This will infuse the meat with a vibrant citrus aroma and keep it juicy during roasting.

  7. Place the chicken on a roasting pan or a baking dish with a wire rack. This will allow the heat to circulate evenly around the chicken, ensuring it cooks through and the skin becomes crispy.

  8. Roast the chicken in the preheated oven for approximately 1 hour and 30 minutes, or until the internal temperature reaches 165°F (74°C). If you have a meat thermometer, insert it into the thickest part of the thigh without touching the bone to check the temperature.

  9. Once the chicken is cooked through, remove it from the oven and let it rest for about 10 minutes before carving. This resting time will allow the juices to redistribute, resulting in a moist and tender chicken.

  10. Carve the chicken into desired pieces and serve hot. You can garnish it with additional lemon slices and fresh rosemary for an extra touch of flavor and presentation.

Serving Suggestions

This Hearty Low Carb Lemon Rosemary Roasted Chicken pairs well with a variety of side dishes. Here are a few suggestions to complete your meal:

  • Roasted vegetables: Toss some Brussels sprouts, carrots, or cauliflower with olive oil, salt, and pepper, then roast them alongside the chicken for a wholesome and nutritious side dish.

  • Cauliflower mash: Replace traditional mashed potatoes with a low-carb alternative by steaming cauliflower florets and blending them until smooth. Season with salt, pepper, and a pat of butter for a creamy and guilt-free side.

  • Green salad: Serve the roasted chicken on a bed of fresh mixed greens, cherry tomatoes, and cucumber slices for a light and refreshing accompaniment.

Conclusion

With its vibrant flavors and simple preparation, this Hearty Low Carb Lemon Rosemary Roasted Chicken is a winner for any occasion. Whether you’re following a low-carb diet or simply looking to enjoy a delicious and healthy meal, this recipe is sure to satisfy your taste buds. So, gather your ingredients, preheat your oven, and get ready to indulge in a dish that will leave you craving for more. Enjoy!
