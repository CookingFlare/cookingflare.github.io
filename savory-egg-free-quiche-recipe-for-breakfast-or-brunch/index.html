Savory Egg-Free Quiche Recipe for Breakfast or Brunch


If you’re looking for a delicious and satisfying breakfast or brunch dish that’s egg-free, then you’re in the right place. This savory egg-free quiche is packed with flavor, protein, and a medley of vegetables that will surely delight your taste buds. Whether you’re catering to dietary restrictions, allergies, or simply want to try something different, this recipe is a winner. Let’s dive into the ingredients and steps to create this delightful dish.

Ingredients

For the Crust:

  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, chilled and cubed
  • 1/4 cup ice water

For the Filling:

  • 1 cup firm tofu, drained and crumbled
  • 1/2 cup unsweetened almond milk (or any non-dairy milk of your choice)
  • 2 tablespoons nutritional yeast
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon onion powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric (for color)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 bell pepper, diced
  • 1 cup spinach, chopped
  • 1/2 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1/4 cup fresh basil, chopped

Instructions

Step 1: Prepare the Crust

  1. In a large bowl, combine the flour and salt. Add the chilled, cubed butter and use a pastry cutter or your fingers to work the butter into the flour until the mixture resembles coarse crumbs.
  2. Gradually add the ice water, a tablespoon at a time, and mix until the dough comes together. Shape the dough into a disk, wrap it in plastic wrap, and refrigerate for at least 30 minutes.

Step 2: Make the Filling

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. In a blender, combine the crumbled tofu, almond milk, nutritional yeast, garlic powder, onion powder, turmeric, salt, and pepper. Blend until smooth and set aside.
  3. In a skillet, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the chopped onion and garlic, and sauté until fragrant. Add the bell pepper and cook until softened. Then, add the spinach and cook until wilted. Remove from heat and let it cool slightly.

Step 3: Assemble and Bake

  1. Roll out the chilled dough on a floured surface to fit a 9-inch pie dish. Press the dough into the dish and trim any excess.
  2. Arrange the cherry tomatoes and fresh basil in the pie crust.
  3. Pour the tofu mixture over the vegetables in the crust, making sure it’s evenly distributed.
  4. Bake the quiche in the preheated oven for 40-45 minutes, or until the filling is set and the crust is golden brown.
  5. Allow the quiche to cool for a few minutes before slicing and serving.

Serving Suggestions

This savory egg-free quiche pairs well with a side salad, fresh fruit, or even some roasted potatoes. It’s a versatile dish that can be enjoyed warm or at room temperature, making it perfect for brunch gatherings or a leisurely weekend breakfast.

So, there you have it - a flavorful and satisfying savory egg-free quiche that’s sure to impress. Whether you follow a plant-based diet, have egg allergies, or simply want to try something new, this recipe is a must-try. Get creative with your choice of vegetables and enjoy a delightful breakfast or brunch with this delicious dish!
