BBQ Chicken Drumsticks: Finger-Licking Recipes for Summer Parties


Summer is the perfect time for outdoor gatherings and delicious barbecues. And what better way to satisfy your taste buds than with some finger-licking BBQ chicken drumsticks? These flavorful and juicy drumsticks are a crowd-pleaser and will have your guests coming back for more. So fire up the grill, grab your apron, and let’s dive into some mouthwatering BBQ chicken drumstick recipes!

Classic BBQ Chicken Drumsticks

Ingredients:

  • 8 chicken drumsticks
  • 1 cup BBQ sauce
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your grill to medium-high heat.
  2. In a small bowl, mix together the olive oil, minced garlic, paprika, salt, and black pepper.
  3. Brush the chicken drumsticks with the olive oil mixture, ensuring they are evenly coated.
  4. Place the drumsticks on the grill and cook for about 15-20 minutes, turning occasionally, until they reach an internal temperature of 165°F (74°C).
  5. During the last 5 minutes of grilling, brush the drumsticks with your favorite BBQ sauce, and cook until the sauce becomes slightly caramelized.
  6. Remove the drumsticks from the grill and let them rest for a few minutes before serving. Enjoy!

Spicy Honey BBQ Chicken Drumsticks

Ingredients:

  • 8 chicken drumsticks
  • 1/2 cup BBQ sauce
  • 2 tablespoons hot sauce
  • 2 tablespoons honey
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon chili powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon cayenne pepper
  • Salt and black pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your grill to medium heat.
  2. In a mixing bowl, combine the BBQ sauce, hot sauce, honey, soy sauce, olive oil, minced garlic, chili powder, cayenne pepper, salt, and black pepper. Mix well.
  3. Place the chicken drumsticks in a large resealable bag and pour the marinade over them. Seal the bag and massage the marinade into the drumsticks. Let them marinate in the refrigerator for at least 1 hour or overnight for maximum flavor.
  4. Remove the drumsticks from the marinade and discard any excess marinade.
  5. Grill the drumsticks for about 20-25 minutes, turning occasionally, until they are cooked through and reach an internal temperature of 165°F (74°C).
  6. Brush the drumsticks with additional BBQ sauce during the last 5 minutes of grilling for an extra burst of flavor.
  7. Remove from the grill and let them rest for a few minutes before serving. Enjoy the spicy and sweet goodness!

Sticky Asian BBQ Chicken Drumsticks

Ingredients:

  • 8 chicken drumsticks
  • 1/2 cup hoisin sauce
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons honey
  • 2 tablespoons rice vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon sesame oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon ginger, grated
  • 1/2 teaspoon red pepper flakes
  • Chopped green onions and sesame seeds for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your grill to medium-high heat.
  2. In a bowl, whisk together the hoisin sauce, soy sauce, honey, rice vinegar, sesame oil, minced garlic, grated ginger, and red pepper flakes.
  3. Place the chicken drumsticks in a large resealable bag and pour the marinade over them. Seal the bag and massage the marinade into the drumsticks. Let them marinate in the refrigerator for at least 1 hour or overnight.
  4. Remove the drumsticks from the marinade and reserve the marinade for basting.
  5. Grill the drumsticks for about 15-20 minutes, turning occasionally, until they are cooked through and reach an internal temperature of 165°F (74°C).
  6. Baste the drumsticks with the reserved marinade during the last 5 minutes of grilling to create a sticky glaze.
  7. Remove from the grill and sprinkle with chopped green onions and sesame seeds for an extra touch of flavor and presentation.
  8. Let them rest for a few minutes before serving. Dive into the irresistible Asian-inspired BBQ chicken drumsticks!

Conclusion

BBQ chicken drumsticks are a must-have for any summer party. Whether you prefer the classic BBQ flavor, a spicy kick, or an Asian twist, these recipes will satisfy your cravings and impress your guests. Don’t forget to pair them with your favorite sides, like coleslaw, corn on the cob, or potato salad. So fire up the grill, enjoy the warm weather, and indulge in the finger-licking goodness of BBQ chicken drumsticks! Happy grilling!
