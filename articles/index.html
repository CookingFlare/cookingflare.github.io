Grilled Caesar Pizza: A Fusion of Flavors

Are you ready to take your pizza game to the next level? Look no further than the mouthwatering fusion of flavors that is Grilled Caesar Pizza. This unique twist on a classic Caesar salad meets the beloved pizza, creating a culinary masterpiece that will tantalize your taste buds. Ingredients For the Dough: 2 1/4 teaspoons active dry yeast 1 1/2 cups warm water 3 1/2 cups all-purpose flour 2 tablespoons olive oil 1 teaspoon salt For the Caesar Dressing: 1/2 cup mayonnaise 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese 2 cloves garlic, minced 2 tablespoons lemon juice 1 teaspoon Dijon mustard Salt and pepper to taste For the Toppings: 2 cups shredded cooked chicken 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese 1/2 cup shredded Parmesan cheese 1 cup chopped romaine lettuce 1/4 cup croutons Anchovy fillets (optional) Instructions Step 1: Prepare the Dough In a large bowl, dissolve the yeast in warm water and let it sit for about 5 minutes until it becomes frothy. Stir in the flour, olive oil, and salt until a dough starts to form. Knead the dough on a floured surface for about 5 minutes until it becomes smooth and elastic. Place the dough in a greased bowl, cover it with a damp cloth, and let it rise in a warm place for about 1 hour or until doubled in size. Step 2: Make the Caesar Dressing In a small bowl, whisk together the mayonnaise, grated Parmesan cheese, minced garlic, lemon juice, Dijon mustard, salt, and pepper until well combined. Set aside. Step 3: Grill the Pizza Preheat your grill to medium-high heat. Divide the dough in half and roll each half into a 12-inch circle. Carefully transfer the dough to the preheated grill and cook for 2-3 minutes on each side until it starts to bubble and grill marks appear. Step 4: Assemble the Pizza Remove the grilled dough from the grill and spread a generous amount of the prepared Caesar dressing over each crust. Evenly distribute the shredded chicken and mozzarella cheese over the dressing. Return the pizzas to the grill and cook for an additional 5-7 minutes or until the cheese is melted and bubbly. Step 5: Garnish and Serve Sprinkle the shredded Parmesan cheese over the hot pizzas. Top with chopped romaine lettuce, croutons, and anchovy fillets if desired. Slice the pizzas and serve immediately. The Perfect Fusion of Flavors Grilled Caesar Pizza brings together the creamy, tangy flavors of Caesar dressing with the crispy, smoky goodness of grilled pizza crust. The addition of shredded chicken and a medley of cheeses takes this culinary creation to new heights, while the fresh romaine lettuce and crunchy croutons provide the perfect finishing touch.
