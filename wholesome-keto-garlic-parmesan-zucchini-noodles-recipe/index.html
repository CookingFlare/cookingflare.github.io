Wholesome Keto Garlic Parmesan Zucchini Noodles Recipe


Are you looking for a delicious and healthy low-carb meal that will satisfy your cravings for pasta? Look no further than this Wholesome Keto Garlic Parmesan Zucchini Noodles recipe. Zucchini noodles, also known as “zoodles,” are a fantastic alternative to traditional pasta, especially for those following a ketogenic diet. They are not only nutritious but also incredibly versatile, making them the perfect canvas for a variety of flavors. In this recipe, we will combine the freshness of zucchini noodles with the savory goodness of garlic and parmesan to create a dish that is both wholesome and satisfying.

Ingredients

  • 4 medium zucchinis, spiralized
  • 3 tablespoons olive oil
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/2 teaspoon red pepper flakes
  • 1/2 cup grated parmesan cheese
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley, chopped (for garnish)

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Zucchini Noodles: Using a spiralizer, create zucchini noodles from the medium zucchinis. If you don’t have a spiralizer, you can use a vegetable peeler to create long, thin strips resembling noodles.

  2. Sauté the Garlic: In a large pan, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the minced garlic and red pepper flakes, and sauté for 1-2 minutes, or until the garlic becomes fragrant.

  3. Cook the Zucchini Noodles: Add the zucchini noodles to the pan, tossing them gently to coat them with the garlic-infused oil. Sauté the noodles for 2-3 minutes, just until they are tender but still have a slight crunch.

  4. Add Parmesan Cheese: Sprinkle the grated parmesan cheese over the zucchini noodles, and toss them gently to incorporate the cheese evenly. The heat of the noodles will melt the cheese slightly, creating a creamy and flavorful coating.

  5. Season to Taste: Season the noodles with salt and pepper according to your taste preferences. Remember that parmesan cheese can be salty, so adjust the seasoning accordingly.

  6. Garnish and Serve: Transfer the zucchini noodles to a serving dish, and garnish with freshly chopped parsley for a pop of color and added freshness.

Nutritional Information

  • Calories: 180
  • Total Fat: 14g
  • Cholesterol: 10mg
  • Sodium: 280mg
  • Total Carbohydrates: 6g
  • Dietary Fiber: 2g
  • Protein: 8g

Why Zucchini Noodles?

Zucchini noodles are a fantastic alternative to traditional pasta for several reasons. Firstly, they are incredibly low in carbohydrates, making them suitable for those following a ketogenic diet or anyone looking to reduce their carb intake. Secondly, they are packed with essential nutrients such as vitamins A and C, potassium, and antioxidants, making them a highly nutritious choice. Finally, zucchini noodles are incredibly versatile and can be paired with a wide range of sauces and flavors, allowing for endless creativity in the kitchen.

Flavor Variations

While this Wholesome Keto Garlic Parmesan Zucchini Noodles recipe is delicious on its own, feel free to get creative and customize it to suit your taste preferences. You can add protein such as grilled chicken, shrimp, or tofu to make it a complete meal. Additionally, you can incorporate other ingredients such as sun-dried tomatoes, spinach, or roasted vegetables to add depth and complexity to the dish. The beauty of zucchini noodles lies in their ability to adapt to various flavors, so don’t be afraid to experiment and make this recipe your own.

Final Thoughts

With its simplicity, nutrient density, and delightful flavors, this Wholesome Keto Garlic Parmesan Zucchini Noodles recipe is a must-try for anyone seeking a healthy and satisfying meal. Whether you are following a ketogenic lifestyle or simply looking to incorporate more nutritious vegetables into your diet, zucchini noodles are an excellent choice. Embrace the versatility of zucchini noodles, and let your creativity run wild as you explore the multitude of flavors and combinations that can elevate this humble vegetable into a culinary masterpiece. Enjoy!
