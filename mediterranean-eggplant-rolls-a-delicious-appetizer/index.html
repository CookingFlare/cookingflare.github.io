Mediterranean Eggplant Rolls: A Delicious Appetizer


If you’re looking for a flavorful and elegant appetizer to impress your guests, look no further than these delectable Mediterranean Eggplant Rolls. This dish combines the rich flavors of eggplant, tangy feta cheese, and fragrant herbs, all rolled into a delightful bite-sized treat. Whether you’re hosting a dinner party or simply craving a taste of the Mediterranean, these eggplant rolls are sure to be a hit. Let’s dive into the recipe and discover how to create this delightful appetizer.

Ingredients

For the Eggplant Rolls:

  • 2 medium eggplants
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

For the Filling:

  • 1 cup crumbled feta cheese
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh mint
  • 1/4 cup chopped sun-dried tomatoes
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • Zest of 1 lemon
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

For the Lemon Yogurt Sauce:

  • 1 cup Greek yogurt
  • Zest and juice of 1 lemon
  • 1 tablespoon honey
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Eggplant:

    • Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
    • Slice the eggplants lengthwise into thin strips, about 1/4 inch thick.
    • Brush the eggplant slices with olive oil and season with salt and pepper.
    • Place the slices on a baking sheet and roast in the oven for 15-20 minutes, until they are tender and slightly golden.

  2. Make the Filling:

    • In a bowl, combine the crumbled feta cheese, chopped parsley, mint, sun-dried tomatoes, minced garlic, lemon zest, and olive oil.
    • Season the filling with salt and pepper, and mix until well combined.

  3. Assemble the Rolls:

    • Take a roasted eggplant slice and place a spoonful of the filling at one end.
    • Roll the eggplant slice around the filling, creating a neat little roll.
    • Repeat with the remaining eggplant slices and filling.

  4. Prepare the Lemon Yogurt Sauce:

    • In a small bowl, whisk together the Greek yogurt, lemon zest, lemon juice, honey, salt, and pepper until smooth.

  5. Serve and Enjoy:

    • Arrange the eggplant rolls on a serving platter and drizzle with the lemon yogurt sauce.
    • Garnish with a sprinkle of fresh herbs or a drizzle of olive oil, if desired.
    • Serve the rolls as a delightful appetizer and watch as your guests savor the delicious flavors of the Mediterranean.

Tips for Success

  • If you prefer a smokier flavor, you can grill the eggplant slices instead of roasting them in the oven.
  • Feel free to customize the filling with your favorite herbs and additional ingredients such as pine nuts or olives.
  • These eggplant rolls can be made ahead of time and stored in the refrigerator until ready to serve, making them a convenient choice for entertaining.

Final Thoughts

With their vibrant colors and bold flavors, these Mediterranean Eggplant Rolls are a true celebration of the Mediterranean cuisine. The creamy feta cheese, aromatic herbs, and tender eggplant come together to create a dish that is both visually stunning and irresistibly delicious. Whether you’re a fan of Mediterranean flavors or simply looking for a unique appetizer to add to your repertoire, these eggplant rolls are a must-try. So, gather your ingredients and embark on a culinary journey to the Mediterranean with this delightful appetizer. Cheers to good food and great company!
