Juicy Grilled Chicken Recipes for Every Occasion


Grilled chicken is a versatile and delicious dish that can be enjoyed in a variety of ways. Whether you’re hosting a backyard barbecue, preparing a quick weeknight dinner, or looking for a healthy lunch option, grilled chicken always comes to the rescue. In this blog post, we’ll explore a selection of juicy grilled chicken recipes that are perfect for every occasion. So fire up your grill and get ready to tantalize your taste buds!

1. Lemon Herb Grilled Chicken

Ingredients:

  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • Juice of 2 lemons
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon fresh thyme, chopped
  • 1 tablespoon fresh rosemary, chopped
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, combine the lemon juice, olive oil, minced garlic, thyme, rosemary, salt, and pepper.
  2. Place the chicken breasts in a resealable plastic bag and pour the marinade over them. Seal the bag and refrigerate for at least 30 minutes.
  3. Preheat the grill to medium-high heat.
  4. Remove the chicken from the marinade and discard the excess liquid.
  5. Grill the chicken for 6-8 minutes per side or until cooked through and juices run clear.
  6. Let the chicken rest for a few minutes before serving. Slice and enjoy!

2. Teriyaki Grilled Chicken Skewers

Ingredients:

  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken thighs, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons honey
  • 2 tablespoons rice vinegar
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon fresh ginger, grated
  • Wooden skewers, soaked in water for 30 minutes

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, whisk together the soy sauce, honey, rice vinegar, minced garlic, and grated ginger.
  2. Add the chicken pieces to the marinade and toss to coat. Let it marinate in the refrigerator for at least 1 hour.
  3. Preheat the grill to medium heat.
  4. Thread the marinated chicken pieces onto the soaked wooden skewers.
  5. Grill the skewers for 10-12 minutes, turning occasionally, until the chicken is cooked through and slightly charred.
  6. Serve the teriyaki chicken skewers with steamed rice and a sprinkle of sesame seeds for added flavor.

3. Spicy Cajun Grilled Chicken

Ingredients:

  • 4 bone-in, skin-on chicken thighs
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons Cajun seasoning
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon onion powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a small bowl, combine the olive oil, Cajun seasoning, paprika, garlic powder, onion powder, salt, and pepper to make a marinade.
  2. Rub the marinade all over the chicken thighs, ensuring they are evenly coated.
  3. Preheat the grill to medium-high heat.
  4. Place the chicken thighs on the grill, skin side down, and cook for 6-8 minutes.
  5. Flip the chicken thighs and continue grilling for another 6-8 minutes or until the internal temperature reaches 165°F (74°C).
  6. Remove the chicken from the grill and let it rest for a few minutes before serving. The spicy Cajun flavors will make your taste buds dance!

4. Greek-style Grilled Chicken Salad

Ingredients:

  • 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Mixed salad greens
  • Cherry tomatoes, halved
  • Cucumber, sliced
  • Red onion, thinly sliced
  • Kalamata olives
  • Feta cheese, crumbled

Instructions:

  1. In a small bowl, whisk together the olive oil, lemon juice, minced garlic, dried oregano, salt, and pepper.
  2. Place the chicken breasts in a resealable plastic bag and pour the marinade over them. Seal the bag and let it marinate in the refrigerator for at least 1 hour.
  3. Preheat the grill to medium-high heat.
  4. Remove the chicken from the marinade and discard the excess liquid.
  5. Grill the chicken breasts for 6-8 minutes per side or until cooked through and juices run clear.
  6. Let the chicken rest for a few minutes before slicing.
  7. In a large bowl, combine the salad greens, cherry tomatoes, cucumber, red onion, Kalamata olives, and feta cheese.
  8. Add the sliced grilled chicken on top of the salad and drizzle with your favorite dressing. Toss to combine and enjoy a refreshing and flavorful Greek-style grilled chicken salad!

Grilled chicken is a crowd-pleasing favorite that can be enjoyed on various occasions. These juicy grilled chicken recipes offer a range of flavors and styles to suit everyone’s taste buds. From the zesty tang of lemon herb chicken to the bold spiciness of Cajun grilled chicken, there’s something for everyone. So, fire up the grill and impress your family and friends with these mouthwatering grilled chicken recipes. Happy grilling!
