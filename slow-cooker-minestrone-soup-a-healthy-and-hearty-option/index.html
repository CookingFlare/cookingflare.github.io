Slow Cooker Minestrone Soup: A Healthy and Hearty Option


Are you looking for a delicious and nutritious meal that requires minimal effort? Look no further than Slow Cooker Minestrone Soup! Packed with vegetables, beans, and flavorful herbs, this soup is not only easy to make but also a perfect option for those seeking a healthy and hearty meal. Let’s dive into the recipe and discover how you can create this wholesome dish in the comfort of your own kitchen.

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 2 carrots, sliced
  • 2 celery stalks, sliced
  • 1 zucchini, diced
  • 1 red bell pepper, diced
  • 4 cups vegetable broth
  • 1 can (14 oz) diced tomatoes
  • 1 can (14 oz) kidney beans, drained and rinsed
  • 1 can (14 oz) cannellini beans, drained and rinsed
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 bay leaf
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • 2 cups spinach, chopped
  • Grated Parmesan cheese, for garnish (optional)

Instructions

  1. Heat the olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Add the diced onion and minced garlic, and sauté until fragrant and translucent. Transfer the mixture to the slow cooker.

  2. To the slow cooker, add the sliced carrots, celery, zucchini, and red bell pepper. Pour in the vegetable broth and diced tomatoes, followed by the kidney beans and cannellini beans. Stir in the dried basil, dried oregano, bay leaf, salt, and pepper.

  3. Cover the slow cooker and cook on low heat for 6-8 hours or on high heat for 3-4 hours. This slow cooking process allows the flavors to meld together and develop a rich taste.

  4. Once the soup has cooked, remove the bay leaf and stir in the chopped spinach. The heat from the soup will wilt the spinach perfectly. Taste and adjust the seasonings if needed.

  5. Ladle the Slow Cooker Minestrone Soup into bowls and garnish with grated Parmesan cheese, if desired. Serve hot with crusty bread or a side salad for a complete meal.

Tips and Variations

  • Feel free to customize this recipe to suit your taste preferences and dietary needs. You can add other vegetables like diced potatoes or butternut squash for added texture and flavor.
  • Don’t be afraid to experiment with different herbs and spices. Fresh basil and thyme are great additions to enhance the aroma of the soup.
  • To make this recipe vegan, simply swap the Parmesan cheese garnish for a dairy-free alternative or omit it altogether.
  • Leftovers can be stored in an airtight container in the refrigerator for up to 3 days. This soup also freezes well, so you can make a larger batch and save some for later.

In Conclusion

Slow Cooker Minestrone Soup is a fantastic option for those busy days when you want a wholesome meal without spending hours in the kitchen. By using your slow cooker, you can effortlessly create a delicious and nutritious dish that will warm your soul. With its abundance of vegetables, beans, and aromatic herbs, this soup is not just healthy but also incredibly satisfying. So grab your ingredients, set up your slow cooker, and let this Slow Cooker Minestrone Soup become a staple in your recipe collection. Enjoy!
