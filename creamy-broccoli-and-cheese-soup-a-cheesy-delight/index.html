Creamy Broccoli and Cheese Soup: A Cheesy Delight


There’s something truly comforting about a warm bowl of creamy broccoli and cheese soup. It’s the perfect combination of wholesome broccoli, rich cheese, and flavorful seasonings. Whether you’re looking for a quick weeknight meal or a cozy dish to enjoy on a chilly evening, this recipe is sure to hit the spot. So, let’s dive into the delightful world of creamy broccoli and cheese soup and learn how to make this cheesy goodness right in your own kitchen.

Ingredients

  • 4 cups of fresh broccoli florets
  • 1/2 cup of diced onion
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 4 tablespoons of butter
  • 1/4 cup of all-purpose flour
  • 4 cups of chicken or vegetable broth
  • 2 cups of whole milk
  • 2 cups of shredded cheddar cheese
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1/2 teaspoon of paprika
  • Croutons for garnish (optional)

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Broccoli: Start by blanching the broccoli florets in boiling water for 2-3 minutes, then immediately transfer them to an ice bath to stop the cooking process. This will help the broccoli retain its vibrant green color.

  2. Sauté the Aromatics: In a large pot, melt the butter over medium heat. Add the diced onion and minced garlic, and sauté until they are soft and fragrant.

  3. Create the Roux: Sprinkle the flour over the sautéed aromatics and stir to form a paste. Cook the roux for a couple of minutes to get rid of the raw flour taste.

  4. Add the Broth and Milk: Slowly pour in the chicken or vegetable broth while continuously stirring to incorporate the roux. Once the broth is fully mixed in, pour in the whole milk and continue stirring until the mixture is smooth.

  5. Simmer and Add Broccoli: Bring the soup to a gentle simmer, then add the blanched broccoli florets. Let the soup simmer for about 10-15 minutes, or until the broccoli is tender.

  6. Cheesy Goodness: Reduce the heat to low and gradually stir in the shredded cheddar cheese. Continue stirring until the cheese is fully melted and the soup is smooth and creamy.

  7. Season and Serve: Season the soup with salt, pepper, and paprika according to your taste preferences. Ladle the creamy broccoli and cheese soup into bowls, and if desired, top with some crunchy croutons for an extra bit of texture.

Tips for Success

  • Use freshly shredded cheddar cheese for the best melting and flavor.
  • If you prefer a smoother soup, you can use an immersion blender to puree the soup to your desired consistency.
  • Experiment with different cheese varieties such as Gruyère or smoked Gouda for a unique twist on the classic recipe.

Enjoy Your Cheesy Delight

Now that you’ve mastered the art of making creamy broccoli and cheese soup, it’s time to savor the fruits of your labor. This comforting and flavorful soup is perfect for family dinners, casual gatherings, or simply as a treat for yourself. So, grab a ladle, fill up a bowl, and indulge in the cheesy delight that is creamy broccoli and cheese soup. Bon appétit!
