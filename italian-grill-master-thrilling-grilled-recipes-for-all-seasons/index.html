Italian Grill-master: Thrilling Grilled Recipes for All Seasons


Summer is in full swing, and it’s the perfect time to bring out that barbecue grill and indulge in the flavors of Italy! Grilling is an excellent way to cook sumptuous Italian dishes that everyone will love. From tasty Caprese Salad to mouth-watering Grilled Chicken with Lemon Herb Marinade, we’ve compiled some thrilling recipes that are sure to enhance your backyard get-togethers or feasts. So, grab your apron, preheat that grill, and let the Italian Grill-master tuition commence!

1. Classic Grilled Margherita Pizza

Who doesn’t love a good pizza? Grilling Margherita pizza brings a whole new level of flavor to this traditional favorite. Here’s what you’ll need for this delightful treat:

Ingredients:

  • 1 ready-to-cook pizza dough
  • 1 cup of tomato sauce or diced tomatoes
  • Fresh mozzarella cheese slices
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • Olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Spread the pizza dough on a lightly-floured surface and knead it for a couple of minutes until it’s smooth and elastic.
  2. Preheat your grill to medium heat and brush the grates with oil to avoid sticking.
  3. Roll out the pizza dough into a flat circle, about 1/4 inch thickness.
  4. Place the dough on a piece of aluminum foil, spread the tomato sauce or tomatoes evenly over the dough, and add mozzarella slices as desired.
  5. Fold the edges of the aluminum foil over the pizza, creating a pocket to hold in the ingredients while grilling.
  6. Place the foil-wrapped pizza on the grill and cook for about 8-10 minutes, rotating occasionally to ensure even cooking, and until the crust turns golden brown.
  7. Carefully remove the pizza from the grill on a heatproof surface, open the foil, garnish with fresh basil leaves, a drizzle of olive oil, and season with salt and pepper.
  8. Slice and serve hot!

2. Grilled Chicken with Lemon Herb Marinade

Nothing says “Italian grill” like a deliciously tender and juicy grilled chicken. Here’s a fantastic recipe for you to bring to your next outdoor event:

Ingredients:

  • 4 boneless chicken breasts
  • Juice and zest from 1 lemon
  • 2 minced garlic cloves
  • 1 tsp. chopped fresh rosemary
  • ½ tsp. dried oregano
  • 2 tbsp. olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a small bowl, combine the lemon juice, garlic, rosemary, oregano, and olive oil to create your marinade.
  2. Rub the marinade onto both sides of the chicken breasts and place them in a dish or resealable bag. Cover and refrigerate for at least 30 minutes or up to 3 hours for extra flavor.
  3. Preheat your grill to medium-high heat, and once hot, grill the chicken for about 6-7 minutes on each side or until cooked through and no longer pink in the center.
  4. Remove the chicken from the grill and let it rest for a few minutes. Slice and serve with your favorite side dishes!

3. Grilled Eggplant, Zucchini, and Tomatoes

A light and refreshing grilled vegetable dish that pairs elegantly with your grilled chicken or pizza. Let’s get started:

Ingredients:

  • 2 medium-sized eggplants, sliced lengthwise
  • 3 zucchinis, sliced lengthwise
  • 4 medium-sized tomatoes, halved
  • 2 tbsp. olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • ½ tsp. dried oregano
  • Fresh basil leaves for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your grill to medium heat and brush the grates with oil.
  2. In a bowl, gently toss the sliced eggplants, zucchinis, and tomatoes with olive oil, salt, pepper, and dried oregano.
  3. Place the vegetables on the grill and cook, rotating occasionally and adjusting heat as necessary to prevent burning, until the vegetables are softened and lightly charred (about 6-8 minutes for eggplants and zucchinis, and 3-4 minutes for tomatoes).
  4. Transfer the cooked vegetables to a serving plate, drizzle with additional olive oil, and garnish with fresh basil leaves.

4. Grilled Caprese Salad

A summertime classic, this Caprese salad is a refreshing burst of flavors that will leave you longing for more!

Ingredients:

  • 1 large package of fresh mozzarella balls
  • 3-4 medium-sized fresh tomatoes, sliced
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • 2 tbsp. balsamic vinegar
  • Olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your grill to medium-high heat and lightly oil the grates.
  2. Place the mozzarella balls and tomatoes on the grill, and cook until lightly charred and warmed through (about 1-2 minutes per side for tomatoes and slightly longer for the mozzarella).
  3. Arrange the grilled mozzarella and tomatoes on a serving plate, layering them with fresh basil leaves in between.
  4. Drizzle balsamic vinegar and olive oil on top, and season with salt and pepper to taste.

Bring the Tastes of Italy to Your Backyard

These thrilling grilled recipes for all seasons will bring a taste of a sunny Italian summer to your backyard gatherings. Gather your family and friends around your grill, share these delightful dishes, and enjoy every incredible bite. Buon Appetito!
