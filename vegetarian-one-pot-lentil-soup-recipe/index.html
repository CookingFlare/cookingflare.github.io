Vegetarian One Pot Lentil Soup Recipe


Are you looking for a comforting and nutritious meal that’s easy to make and perfect for any season? Look no further than this delicious Vegetarian One Pot Lentil Soup recipe! Packed with protein, fiber, and a medley of veggies, this soup is not only satisfying but also incredibly easy to prepare. Let’s dive into the details and explore how you can whip up this wholesome dish in your own kitchen.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup of dry green or brown lentils
  • 1 large onion, diced
  • 3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 2 carrots, chopped
  • 2 celery stalks, chopped
  • 1 medium sweet potato, peeled and diced
  • 1 can of diced tomatoes
  • 6 cups of vegetable broth
  • 1 teaspoon of ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon of smoked paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon of turmeric
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the Lentils: Rinse the lentils under cold water and set them aside.

  2. Sauté the Aromatics: In a large pot or Dutch oven, heat a tablespoon of olive oil over medium heat. Add the diced onion and sauté until translucent. Then, add the minced garlic and cook for another minute until fragrant.

  3. Add the Veggies: Stir in the chopped carrots, celery, and sweet potato. Cook for a few minutes until the vegetables begin to soften.

  4. Season the Soup: Sprinkle the ground cumin, smoked paprika, and turmeric over the vegetables. Stir well to coat the veggies with the aromatic spices.

  5. Simmer the Soup: Pour in the vegetable broth and add the rinsed lentils and diced tomatoes. Bring the soup to a gentle boil, then reduce the heat and let it simmer for about 25-30 minutes, or until the lentils and vegetables are tender.

  6. Season to Perfection: Season the soup with salt and pepper to your liking. Taste and adjust the seasonings as needed.

  7. Serve and Enjoy: Ladle the hot lentil soup into bowls and garnish with freshly chopped parsley. Serve with a slice of crusty bread or a sprinkle of grated Parmesan cheese if desired.

Why You’ll Love This Recipe:

Nutritious and Filling

With the combination of lentils, vegetables, and flavorful spices, this soup is a powerhouse of nutrition. Lentils are rich in protein and fiber, making them a great addition to a vegetarian diet. The addition of sweet potatoes adds a touch of natural sweetness and a dose of beta-carotene.

Easy and Convenient

This one pot lentil soup recipe is perfect for busy weeknights or lazy weekends. With minimal prep and cooking time, you can have a hearty and wholesome meal on the table without much effort. Plus, it’s a great way to use up any leftover vegetables you have in the fridge.

Versatile and Customizable

Feel free to customize this recipe to suit your taste preferences. You can add in other vegetables such as spinach, kale, or bell peppers. For a spicy kick, a dash of cayenne pepper or a drizzle of hot sauce can elevate the flavors.

Budget-Friendly

Lentils are an affordable source of plant-based protein, making this recipe not only nutritious but also budget-friendly. Whether you’re cooking for a crowd or meal-prepping for the week, this soup is a cost-effective option that doesn’t compromise on taste or nutrition.

In Conclusion

Whether you’re a dedicated vegetarian, trying to incorporate more meatless meals into your diet, or simply craving a comforting bowl of soup, this Vegetarian One Pot Lentil Soup recipe is sure to become a staple in your kitchen. Its simplicity, nutritional value, and robust flavor make it a winner for all occasions. Give it a try and savor the goodness of this wholesome and satisfying soup!
