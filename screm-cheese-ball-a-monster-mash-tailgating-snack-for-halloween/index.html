Screm' Cheese Ball: A Monster Mash Tailgating Snack for Halloween


Who says tailgating and Halloween can’t combine? This year, skip the classic spooky snacks and bring a creature-infused twist to your tailgate with the Screm’ Cheese Ball. This fun and fearsome monster-themed cheese ball recipe will have your friends and family howling for more! So, grab your kitchen tools, and let’s dive into this ghoulishly delicious snack.

Ingredients:

  • 8 oz cream cheese, softened
  • 8 oz shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1/2 cup finely chopped green onions
  • 1/4 cup mayonnaise
  • 2 tbsp Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp onion powder
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • 2 cups panko bread crumbs
  • Food coloring (green and black)
  • Plastic wrap
  • Aluminum foil
  • Wooden skewers or lollipop sticks

Instructions:

  1. In a large bowl, combine the softened cream cheese, shredded cheddar cheese, green onions, mayonnaise, Worcestershire sauce, garlic powder, onion powder, salt, and pepper. Mix well until a smooth and creamy consistency is achieved. You can use a hand mixer for this step if desired.

  2. Divide your cheese ball mixture into three equal parts. In one part, add a generous amount of green food coloring to make your monster slime green. In another part, add black food coloring to create a spooky shade of black or dark brown. Keep the last part untinted for the monster’s belly.

  3. Take a square or round piece of plastic wrap and place the green colored cheese ball in the center. Wrap tightly, forming the cheese into a log shape, approximating the size of a small pumpkin. Using aluminum foil, cover the plastic-wrapped cheese ball to secure it, and place it in the fridge for at least 30 minutes to allow it to set.

  4. In a shallow dish, mix the panko bread crumbs with a pinch of black food coloring. This colored bread crumb mixture will be used for the creature’s scales.

  5. Remove the chilled green cheese ball from the fridge, and unwrap it. Dip the entire ball into the panko mixture, gently pressing the bread crumbs onto the surface to firmly adhere. Inspect the ball for any exposed parts and re-dip it if necessary.

  6. To create eyes and a mouth for your Screm’ Cheese Ball monster, carve out small sections on opposite sides of the cheese ball, measuring roughly the size of the wooden skewers. Insert skewers or lollipop sticks into these openings, leaving enough space for the black cheese ball to adhere.

  7. Now it’s time for the last part of the creature - the black, snaking maw! Roll the remaining dark-colored cheese ball into a long, thin, and snakelike shape, and carefully place it around one of the skewers, treating it like a licorice twirl. Affix the black cheese into the mouth hole you previously carved out, allowing it to overflow slightly for a sinister look.

  8. Finally, gather the untinted cheese ball portion and roll it into a smaller-sized ball for the creature’s belly. Place it between the skewers, nestled against the green cheese ball.

  9. Time to serve your hauntingly delicious Screm’ Cheese Ball! With Halloween-themed decorations or even on top of a carved-out pumpkin, this appetizer will make your tailgating spread stand out from the rest.

Now, your monster mash is complete with a wickedly yum Screm’ Cheese Ball! This gory yet delectable snack is sure to become the talk of the tailgate, delighting gruesome food lovers and Halloween enthusiasts alike. Whether you’re a foodie or simply a fan of all things spooky, this cheese ball is the perfect treat to celebrate the Halloween season. So, gather your family and friends, and dig in to this horrifyingly tasty appetizer – if you dare! Happy tailing and haunting!
