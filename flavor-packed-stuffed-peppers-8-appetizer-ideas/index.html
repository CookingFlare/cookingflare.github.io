Flavor-Packed Stuffed Peppers: 8 Appetizer Ideas


Stuffed peppers are a classic appetizer that never goes out of style. They are easy to make, versatile, and can be tailored to suit any taste or occasion. In this post, we’ll explore 8 different flavor-packed stuffed pepper recipes that are sure to impress your guests.

Recipe 1: Mediterranean Stuffed Peppers

  • 4 large bell peppers, any color
  • 1 cup cooked rice
  • 1/2 cup crumbled feta cheese
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C). Cut the tops off the peppers and remove the seeds and membranes. In a large bowl, combine the rice, feta cheese, parsley, garlic, and olive oil. Stuff each pepper with the mixture and cover with aluminum foil. Bake for 25 minutes, then remove the foil and bake for an additional 10-15 minutes, or until the peppers are tender.
