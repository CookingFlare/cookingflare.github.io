Apple Cinnamon Swirl Bread Recipe: A Warm and Comforting Vegan Treat


Indulge in the delightful aroma of freshly baked apple cinnamon swirl bread. This vegan recipe is a warm and comforting treat that is sure to satisfy your cravings. With its soft and fluffy texture, paired with the sweet and tangy flavors of apple and cinnamon, this bread is perfect for breakfast, brunch, or as a delightful afternoon snack. Follow this simple recipe to create your own delicious loaf of Apple Cinnamon Swirl Bread.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 tablespoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1 cup unsweetened almond milk
  • 1/4 cup coconut oil, melted
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 apple, peeled, cored, and diced

For the Cinnamon Swirl

  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 tablespoon ground cinnamon

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease a 9x5-inch loaf pan and set aside.

  2. In a large mixing bowl, combine the all-purpose flour, granulated sugar, baking powder, salt, and ground cinnamon. Mix well.

  3. In a separate bowl, whisk together the almond milk, melted coconut oil, and vanilla extract until well combined.

  4. Pour the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients and stir until just combined. Be careful not to overmix, as this can result in a dense loaf.

  5. Gently fold in the diced apple, ensuring that it is evenly distributed throughout the batter.

  6. In a small bowl, mix together the granulated sugar and ground cinnamon for the cinnamon swirl.

  7. Pour half of the batter into the greased loaf pan. Sprinkle half of the cinnamon sugar mixture over the batter.

  8. Pour the remaining batter over the cinnamon sugar layer, and then sprinkle the remaining cinnamon sugar mixture on top.

  9. Use a butter knife to gently swirl the cinnamon sugar mixture into the batter, creating a marbled effect.

  10. Bake in the preheated oven for 50-60 minutes or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.

  11. Once baked, remove the bread from the oven and let it cool in the pan for 10 minutes. Then, transfer the bread to a wire rack to cool completely before slicing.

  12. Slice and serve the Apple Cinnamon Swirl Bread on its own or with a smear of vegan butter or cream cheese. Enjoy it warm or at room temperature.

Tips and Variations

  • For an extra burst of flavor, add a handful of chopped walnuts or raisins to the batter before baking.
  • If you prefer a sweeter bread, drizzle a simple glaze made with powdered sugar and almond milk over the cooled loaf.
  • This bread can be stored in an airtight container at room temperature for up to 3 days. It can also be frozen for longer storage.

Indulge in the cozy flavors of apple and cinnamon with this vegan Apple Cinnamon Swirl Bread. Whether enjoyed as a morning treat or an afternoon indulgence, this bread is sure to bring warmth and comfort to your day. Give this recipe a try and savor the delightful combination of flavors in every slice. Happy baking!
