Fresh Herb Sandwiches: Delicious Recipes for Lunch


When it comes to lunch, sandwiches often steal the spotlight. They are versatile, quick to prepare, and can be packed with a variety of flavors and textures. One of the best ways to elevate your sandwich game is by incorporating fresh herbs. Fresh herbs not only add a burst of flavor but also bring a vibrant touch to your meals. In this post, we will explore several delicious fresh herb sandwich recipes that are perfect for a satisfying lunch.

Why Fresh Herbs?

Fresh herbs can transform an ordinary sandwich into an extraordinary culinary experience. They offer distinct aromas and flavors that dry herbs simply cannot replicate. Herbs like basil, cilantro, parsley, and dill can brighten up your lunch and even provide health benefits, such as antioxidants and anti-inflammatory properties. Adding fresh herbs to your sandwiches is an easy way to incorporate more freshness and nutrition into your diet.

Classic Herb Butter Spread

Before we dive into the sandwich recipes, let’s create a simple yet flavorful herb butter spread that can be used in many of the sandwiches to follow. This spread can be made in advance and stored in the refrigerator for several days.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1/4 cup fresh parsley, finely chopped
  • 2 tablespoons fresh chives, finely chopped
  • 2 tablespoons fresh dill, finely chopped
  • 1 garlic clove, minced
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a mixing bowl, combine the softened butter, parsley, chives, dill, and minced garlic.
  2. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  3. Mix until all ingredients are well combined.
  4. Transfer the herb butter to a piece of plastic wrap, shape it into a log, and refrigerate until firm.
  5. Slice and spread on your favorite bread when ready to assemble your sandwiches.

1. Herbed Chicken Sandwich

This herbed chicken sandwich is packed with flavor and is perfect for a filling lunch. The herb butter spread adds a rich and aromatic touch that complements the grilled chicken beautifully.

Ingredients:

  • 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 4 slices of whole grain bread
  • 1 cup mixed greens
  • 1/2 cup sliced tomatoes
  • Herb butter spread (from the recipe above)

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your grill or grill pan over medium-high heat.
  2. Drizzle the chicken breasts with olive oil and season with salt and pepper.
  3. Grill the chicken for 6-7 minutes on each side or until fully cooked. Remove from the grill and let it rest for a few minutes before slicing.
  4. Toast the whole grain bread slices lightly on the grill.
  5. Spread a generous amount of herb butter on one side of each slice of toasted bread.
  6. Layer the sliced grilled chicken, mixed greens, and tomatoes between two slices of bread.
  7. Top with the remaining slices of bread, slice in half, and enjoy!

2. Caprese Herb Sandwich

Inspired by the classic Caprese salad, this sandwich is fresh, savory, and utterly delicious. The combination of mozzarella, tomatoes, basil, and a drizzle of balsamic glaze makes it a delightful option for lunch.

Ingredients:

  • 4 slices of ciabatta bread
  • 8 ounces fresh mozzarella cheese, sliced
  • 2 medium tomatoes, sliced
  • 1/2 cup fresh basil leaves
  • Balsamic glaze, for drizzling
  • Olive oil, for drizzling
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. Arrange the ciabatta slices on a baking sheet and drizzle with olive oil.
  3. Layer the mozzarella slices, followed by the tomato slices, and fresh basil leaves on two slices of ciabatta.
  4. Season with salt and pepper, then drizzle with balsamic glaze.
  5. Top with the remaining slices of ciabatta.
  6. Bake in the preheated oven for about 10-12 minutes, or until the cheese is melted and the bread is golden.
  7. Remove from the oven, slice, and serve warm.

3. Mediterranean Veggie Sandwich

This Mediterranean veggie sandwich is a colorful and nutritious option that showcases a variety of fresh herbs and vegetables. It’s perfect for a light lunch or a quick snack.

Ingredients:

  • 4 slices of whole wheat bread
  • 1/2 cup hummus
  • 1 cup cucumber, thinly sliced
  • 1 cup bell peppers (red, yellow, or orange), thinly sliced
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1/4 cup red onion, thinly sliced
  • 1/2 cup olives (black or green), pitted and sliced
  • 1/4 cup fresh mint leaves
  • 1/4 cup fresh parsley, chopped
  • Feta cheese, for topping (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Spread a generous layer of hummus on each slice of whole wheat bread.
  2. Layer the cucumber, bell peppers, cherry tomatoes, red onion, and olives on two of the slices.
  3. Sprinkle the fresh mint and parsley over the veggies.
  4. If using, crumble feta cheese on top for extra flavor.
  5. Top with the remaining slices of bread.
  6. Slice in half and serve immediately.

4. Turkey and Pesto Sandwich

This turkey and pesto sandwich is a quick and flavorful option that’s perfect for busy lunch hours. The herbaceous pesto adds a delightful twist to the classic turkey sandwich.

Ingredients:

  • 4 slices of sourdough bread
  • 1/2 cup basil pesto
  • 8 ounces sliced turkey breast
  • 1/2 cup arugula or spinach
  • 1/2 cup roasted red peppers, sliced
  • Fresh basil leaves, for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Spread a generous layer of basil pesto on two slices of sourdough bread.
  2. Layer the sliced turkey breast, arugula or spinach, and roasted red peppers on top of the pesto.
  3. Garnish with fresh basil leaves.
  4. Top with the remaining slices of bread.
  5. Optional: Grill the sandwich on a panini press or in a skillet until golden brown and the cheese is melted.
  6. Slice and serve warm.

5. Tuna Salad with Fresh Herbs Sandwich

For a twist on a classic tuna salad, this recipe incorporates fresh herbs for a refreshing flavor. It’s a great way to enjoy a protein-packed lunch.

Ingredients:

  • 1 can (5 oz) tuna, drained
  • 2 tablespoons mayonnaise
  • 1 tablespoon Dijon mustard
  • 1/4 cup celery, finely chopped
  • 1/4 cup red onion, finely chopped
  • 1/4 cup fresh dill, chopped
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 4 slices of whole grain bread
  • Lettuce leaves, for serving

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, combine the drained tuna, mayonnaise, Dijon mustard, celery, red onion, and fresh dill.
  2. Mix well and season with salt and pepper to taste.
  3. Spread the tuna salad mixture onto two slices of whole grain bread.
  4. Top with lettuce leaves and the remaining slices of bread.
  5. Slice in half and serve immediately.

Conclusion

Fresh herb sandwiches are a delightful way to enjoy lunch while incorporating vibrant flavors and nutritious ingredients. From herbed chicken to Mediterranean veggies, these recipes showcase the versatility and freshness that herbs bring to the table. Whether you’re packing a lunch for work or enjoying a leisurely meal at home, these sandwiches are sure to satisfy your cravings and leave you feeling nourished.

Experiment with different herbs and ingredients to make each sandwich your own. With endless possibilities, you can transform a simple lunch into a gourmet experience right at home. So, gather your fresh herbs and let your culinary creativity flow! Enjoy your delicious herb-infused sandwiches!
