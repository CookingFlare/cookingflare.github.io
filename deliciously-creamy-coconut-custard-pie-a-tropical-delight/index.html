Deliciously Creamy Coconut Custard Pie: A Tropical Delight


If you’re a fan of tropical flavors and creamy desserts, then this Deliciously Creamy Coconut Custard Pie is sure to become a new favorite in your recipe collection. This pie brings together the lusciousness of coconut and the richness of custard in a delightful marriage of flavors that will transport you to a sunny beach with every bite.

Ingredients

For the Crust:

  • 1 1/2 cups of graham cracker crumbs
  • 1/3 cup of granulated sugar
  • 1/2 cup of unsalted butter, melted

For the Filling:

  • 1 cup of coconut milk
  • 1 cup of whole milk
  • 1 cup of sweetened shredded coconut
  • 4 large eggs
  • 1/2 cup of granulated sugar
  • 1/4 cup of cornstarch
  • 1/4 teaspoon of salt
  • 1 teaspoon of vanilla extract

For the Topping (Optional):

  • Whipped cream
  • Toasted coconut flakes

Instructions

  1. Preheat the Oven: Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C).

  2. Make the Crust: In a medium bowl, combine the graham cracker crumbs, sugar, and melted butter. Press the mixture into the bottom and up the sides of a 9-inch pie dish. Bake the crust for 10 minutes, then set it aside to cool.

  3. Prepare the Filling: In a saucepan, combine the coconut milk, whole milk, and sweetened shredded coconut. Heat the mixture over medium heat until it is just about to simmer. Remove the pan from the heat and let the mixture steep for 5-10 minutes to infuse the coconut flavor into the milk.

  4. Whisk the Eggs: In a large bowl, whisk together the eggs, sugar, cornstarch, and salt until well combined.

  5. Combine the Mixtures: Gradually pour the warm coconut milk mixture into the bowl with the eggs, whisking constantly to temper the eggs. Once combined, pour the mixture back into the saucepan.

  6. Cook the Custard: Place the saucepan over medium heat and cook the custard, stirring constantly, until it thickens and coats the back of a spoon. This should take about 5-7 minutes. Be careful not to let the mixture boil.

  7. Add Vanilla: Once the custard has thickened, remove the saucepan from the heat and stir in the vanilla extract.

  8. Assemble the Pie: Pour the coconut custard filling into the prepared pie crust and smooth the top with a spatula.

  9. Bake the Pie: Place the pie in the preheated oven and bake for 25-30 minutes, or until the center is set but still slightly jiggly.

  10. Cool and Chill: Remove the pie from the oven and let it cool to room temperature. Once cooled, refrigerate the pie for at least 4 hours, or until it is thoroughly chilled and set.

  11. Serve and Enjoy: If desired, top the pie with a dollop of whipped cream and a sprinkle of toasted coconut flakes before serving. Slice the pie and savor the creamy, tropical flavors!

Conclusion

This Deliciously Creamy Coconut Custard Pie is a true tropical delight that will elevate any gathering or special occasion. Its velvety smooth custard filling and the essence of coconut nestled in a buttery graham cracker crust will transport you to a breezy island paradise with every forkful. Whether you’re hosting a summer soirée or simply craving a taste of the tropics, this pie is the perfect sweet treat to indulge in.

So, why not bring a taste of the tropics into your kitchen with this delightful recipe? Whether you’re a seasoned baker or new to the world of pies, this recipe is approachable and rewarding. From the first bite to the last, this Coconut Custard Pie is a slice of paradise that you’ll want to enjoy again and again.
