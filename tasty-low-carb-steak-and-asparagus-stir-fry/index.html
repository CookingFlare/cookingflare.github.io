Tasty Low Carb Steak and Asparagus Stir-Fry


Are you looking for a delicious and healthy low-carb meal that’s quick and easy to prepare? Look no further! This Tasty Low Carb Steak and Asparagus Stir-Fry recipe is the perfect choice for a flavorful and satisfying dish that won’t weigh you down. Packed with protein, vitamins, and minerals, this stir-fry is a fantastic option for anyone looking to maintain a balanced and nutritious diet.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound of flank steak, thinly sliced
  • 1 pound of fresh asparagus, trimmed and cut into 2-inch pieces
  • 3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon of fresh ginger, grated
  • 1/4 cup of low-sodium soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons of oyster sauce
  • 1 tablespoon of sesame oil
  • 2 tablespoons of olive oil
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • Sesame seeds for garnish (optional)
  • Sliced green onions for garnish (optional)

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Steak: Season the thinly sliced flank steak with salt and black pepper. In a large skillet or wok, heat 1 tablespoon of olive oil over high heat. Add the steak slices and cook for 2-3 minutes until browned. Remove the steak from the skillet and set aside.

  2. Cook the Asparagus: In the same skillet, add the remaining tablespoon of olive oil. Toss in the minced garlic and grated ginger, and sauté for 1 minute until fragrant. Add the asparagus pieces to the skillet and stir-fry for 4-5 minutes until they are tender-crisp.

  3. Combine the Ingredients: Return the cooked steak to the skillet with the asparagus. Pour in the low-sodium soy sauce and oyster sauce, and drizzle with sesame oil. Toss everything together to ensure the steak and asparagus are coated with the flavorful sauce.

  4. Finish and Serve: Cook for an additional 2-3 minutes, allowing the flavors to meld together. Taste the stir-fry and adjust the seasoning if necessary. Once ready, transfer the stir-fry to a serving dish. If desired, garnish with sesame seeds and sliced green onions for an extra pop of flavor and visual appeal.

Why You’ll Love This Recipe

  • Health Benefits: Asparagus is a nutrient-dense vegetable that’s high in fiber, vitamins A, C, and K, and folate. Paired with lean flank steak, this stir-fry provides a healthy dose of protein and essential nutrients.

  • Flavorful and Aromatic: The combination of fresh garlic, ginger, and savory sauces creates a mouthwatering aroma and a burst of umami flavor in every bite.

  • Quick and Easy: This recipe comes together in under 30 minutes, making it an ideal option for busy weeknights when you want a wholesome meal without spending hours in the kitchen.

  • Versatile: Feel free to customize this stir-fry with your favorite vegetables such as bell peppers, snap peas, or broccoli. You can also swap the flank steak for chicken, shrimp, or tofu for a different twist.

In Conclusion

This Tasty Low Carb Steak and Asparagus Stir-Fry is a fantastic addition to your collection of go-to recipes. Whether you’re following a low-carb diet or simply looking for a delicious and nutritious meal, this stir-fry ticks all the boxes. With its tender-crisp asparagus, savory steak, and delightful blend of seasonings, it’s a dish that’s sure to become a family favorite. Try it out and savor the delightful flavors of this delightful stir-fry!
