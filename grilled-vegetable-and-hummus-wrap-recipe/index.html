Grilled Vegetable and Hummus Wrap Recipe


Wraps are a versatile and delicious meal option that can be enjoyed any time of the day. This Grilled Vegetable and Hummus Wrap recipe is a perfect combination of flavors and textures that will surely satisfy your taste buds. Packed with nutrients and bursting with Mediterranean flavors, this wrap is not only healthy but also incredibly tasty.

Ingredients:

  • 1 zucchini, sliced
  • 1 yellow bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 red bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 red onion, sliced
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 4 large whole wheat wraps
  • 1 cup hummus
  • 1/2 cup crumbled feta cheese
  • Fresh parsley, chopped (for garnish)

Instructions:

  1. Grill the Vegetables: Preheat the grill to medium-high heat. In a large bowl, toss the zucchini, bell peppers, red onion, and cherry tomatoes with olive oil, salt, and pepper. Grill the vegetables until they are tender and slightly charred, about 8-10 minutes.

  2. Assemble the Wraps: Warm the whole wheat wraps slightly to make them more pliable. Spread a generous amount of hummus on each wrap, leaving a border around the edges.

  3. Add the Grilled Vegetables: Divide the grilled vegetables evenly among the wraps, placing them in the center of each wrap on top of the hummus.

  4. Sprinkle with Feta: Sprinkle the crumbled feta cheese over the grilled vegetables on each wrap.

  5. Wrap it Up: Fold in the sides of each wrap, then roll them up tightly from the bottom, ensuring all the ingredients are enclosed.

  6. Serve and Enjoy: Slice the wraps in half diagonally and serve garnished with chopped fresh parsley. These wraps can be enjoyed immediately or packed for a delicious on-the-go meal.

Health Benefits:

  • Vegetables: The grilled vegetables are rich in vitamins, minerals, and antioxidants, promoting overall health and well-being.
  • Whole Wheat Wraps: Using whole wheat wraps adds fiber to your meal, aiding digestion and keeping you full for longer.
  • Hummus: Hummus is a good source of plant-based protein and healthy fats, making this wrap a satisfying and nutritious option.
  • Feta Cheese: While feta cheese adds a creamy and tangy flavor, it also provides calcium and protein to support bone health.

Conclusion:

This Grilled Vegetable and Hummus Wrap recipe is a delightful way to incorporate more vegetables into your diet while enjoying a flavorful and satisfying meal. Whether you’re looking for a quick lunch, a light dinner, or a portable meal for picnics or outings, these wraps are sure to please your palate. Experiment with different vegetables or add a drizzle of balsamic glaze for a unique twist. Healthy, tasty, and easy to make, these wraps are a must-try for any food enthusiast.

So, go ahead, fire up the grill, gather your ingredients, and indulge in these delectable Grilled Vegetable and Hummus Wraps. Happy cooking!
