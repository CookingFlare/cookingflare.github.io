Cooking with Chives: 10 Easy Spring Recipes to Try


As spring unfurls its vibrant colors, it’s the perfect time to embrace seasonal ingredients that bring freshness and flavor to your kitchen. One such ingredient is chives—those slender, green herbs that add a mild onion flavor and a pop of color to any dish. Beyond their culinary charm, chives are easy to grow, making them a delightful addition to your herb garden. In this blog post, we’ll explore ten easy spring recipes that highlight the versatility of chives, from appetizers to main courses and even desserts.

1. Chive and Cheese Stuffed Mini Peppers

Ingredients:

  • 12 mini bell peppers
  • 1 cup cream cheese, softened
  • 1/2 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1/4 cup chives, finely chopped
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cut the mini peppers in half lengthwise and remove the seeds.
  3. In a bowl, mix the cream cheese, shredded cheddar, chives, salt, and pepper until well combined.
  4. Fill each pepper half with the cheese mixture.
  5. Place the stuffed peppers on a baking sheet and bake for 15-20 minutes or until the cheese is bubbly and golden.
  6. Serve warm as an appetizer or party snack.

These chive and cheese stuffed mini peppers are a delightful way to showcase chives while adding a splash of color to your table.

2. Chive Potato Salad

Ingredients:

  • 2 pounds baby potatoes
  • 1/2 cup sour cream
  • 1/4 cup mayonnaise
  • 1/4 cup chives, finely chopped
  • 1 tablespoon Dijon mustard
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Boil the baby potatoes in salted water until tender, about 15-20 minutes. Drain and let cool.
  2. In a large bowl, whisk together the sour cream, mayonnaise, chives, Dijon mustard, salt, and pepper.
  3. Cut the cooled potatoes in half and add them to the bowl. Gently toss to coat.
  4. Refrigerate for at least 30 minutes before serving to allow the flavors to meld.

This chive potato salad is a creamy and flavorful twist on a classic side dish, perfect for spring picnics and barbecues.

3. Chive and Mushroom Risotto

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup Arborio rice
  • 4 cups vegetable broth
  • 1 cup mushrooms, sliced
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1/2 cup white wine
  • 1/2 cup chives, chopped
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a saucepan, heat the vegetable broth and keep warm over low heat.
  2. In a large skillet, heat olive oil over medium heat. Add the onions and garlic and sauté until translucent.
  3. Add the mushrooms and cook until softened.
  4. Stir in the Arborio rice and cook for 1-2 minutes until lightly toasted.
  5. Pour in the white wine and cook until absorbed.
  6. Gradually add the warm vegetable broth, one ladle at a time, stirring frequently until the rice is creamy and al dente.
  7. Stir in the chopped chives and season with salt and pepper before serving.

This chive and mushroom risotto is a rich and hearty dish that exemplifies the comfort of spring meals.

4. Chive-Infused Butter

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1/4 cup chives, finely chopped
  • 1 teaspoon lemon juice
  • Salt to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, combine the softened butter, chives, lemon juice, and salt.
  2. Mix until well incorporated.
  3. Transfer the mixture onto a piece of parchment paper and roll it into a log shape. Refrigerate until firm.
  4. Slice into rounds and use to top grilled meats, vegetables, or bread.

Chive-infused butter is a versatile condiment that can elevate any dish, making it a must-have in your spring cooking repertoire.

5. Chive and Egg Breakfast Tacos

Ingredients:

  • 4 large eggs
  • 1/4 cup milk
  • 1/4 cup chives, chopped
  • 4 small corn tortillas
  • 1 avocado, sliced
  • Salsa for serving
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, whisk together the eggs, milk, chives, salt, and pepper.
  2. Heat a non-stick skillet over medium heat and pour in the egg mixture. Scramble until cooked through.
  3. Warm the corn tortillas in another skillet.
  4. Fill each tortilla with the scrambled eggs, avocado slices, and salsa.

These chive and egg breakfast tacos are a delightful way to kick off your day with fresh flavors and satisfying ingredients.

6. Creamy Chive and Lemon Pasta

Ingredients:

  • 8 ounces pasta of your choice
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1/2 cup chives, chopped
  • 1 lemon, zested and juiced
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Cook the pasta according to package instructions; drain and set aside.
  2. In a saucepan, heat the heavy cream over medium heat. Stir in the lemon zest, juice, chives, and Parmesan cheese.
  3. Toss the cooked pasta in the creamy chive sauce and season with salt and pepper.

This creamy chive and lemon pasta is a bright and satisfying dish, perfect for a spring evening.

7. Chive and Spinach Quiche

Ingredients:

  • 1 pre-made pie crust
  • 6 large eggs
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 cup fresh spinach, chopped
  • 1/2 cup chives, chopped
  • 1 cup shredded cheese (cheddar or Swiss)
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. In a bowl, whisk together the eggs, milk, salt, and pepper.
  3. Stir in the spinach, chives, and cheese.
  4. Pour the mixture into the pie crust and bake for 35-40 minutes, or until the filling is set and lightly golden.
  5. Let cool slightly before slicing and serving.

This chive and spinach quiche makes for a delightful brunch option or a light lunch paired with a salad.

8. Chive and Tomato Bruschetta

Ingredients:

  • 1 baguette, sliced
  • 2 cups cherry tomatoes, diced
  • 1/4 cup chives, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons balsamic vinegar
  • Olive oil for drizzling
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C).
  2. Arrange the baguette slices on a baking sheet and drizzle with olive oil. Bake for 5-7 minutes until toasted.
  3. In a bowl, combine the diced tomatoes, chives, garlic, balsamic vinegar, salt, and pepper.
  4. Spoon the tomato mixture onto the toasted baguette slices and serve immediately.

Chive and tomato bruschetta is a fresh and vibrant appetizer that beautifully showcases the flavors of spring.

9. Chive and Feta Stuffed Chicken Breasts

Ingredients:

  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1 cup feta cheese, crumbled
  • 1/2 cup chives, chopped
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. In a bowl, mix together the feta cheese, chives, salt, and pepper.
  3. Cut a pocket into each chicken breast and fill with the feta and chive mixture.
  4. Heat olive oil in an oven-safe skillet over medium-high heat. Sear the chicken breasts on both sides until golden.
  5. Transfer the skillet to the oven and bake for 20-25 minutes, or until the chicken is cooked through.

These chive and feta stuffed chicken breasts are a savory main course that will impress your family and guests alike.

10. Chive-Infused Lemonade

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup fresh lemon juice
  • 1/4 cup chives, chopped
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 4 cups water
  • Ice for serving
  • Lemon slices for garnish

Instructions:

  1. In a saucepan, combine 1 cup of water with the sugar and chives. Heat until the sugar dissolves, then let cool.
  2. Strain the chive mixture to remove solids.
  3. In a pitcher, combine the remaining water, lemon juice, and the chive-infused syrup. Stir well.
  4. Serve over ice with lemon slices for garnish.

This chive-infused lemonade is a refreshing drink that adds a unique twist to your typical spring beverages.

Conclusion

Chives are a fantastic addition to your culinary repertoire, offering a mild onion flavor that enhances a variety of dishes. From appetizers to main courses and even beverages, these ten easy spring recipes showcase the versatility of chives while celebrating the freshness of the season. So, gather your ingredients and start cooking with chives today—your taste buds will thank you!

Whether you’re hosting a spring gathering or looking for new ways to enjoy your meals, these recipes are sure to impress. Happy cooking!
