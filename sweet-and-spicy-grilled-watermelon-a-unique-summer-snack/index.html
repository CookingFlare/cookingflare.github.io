Sweet and Spicy Grilled Watermelon: A Unique Summer Snack


When we think of grilling, the usual suspects like burgers, steaks, and veggies often come to mind. But have you ever considered grilling fruit? Grilled watermelon is a delightful surprise that combines the sweetness of this juicy fruit with a hint of smokiness and a kick of spice. It’s a unique summer snack that will elevate your grilling game and impress your guests. In this blog post, we’ll explore the magic of sweet and spicy grilled watermelon and how you can easily prepare this mouthwatering treat at home.

The Magic of Grilled Watermelon

Watermelon is a staple of summer, known for its refreshing and hydrating properties. When grilled, watermelon takes on a whole new dimension of flavor. The heat caramelizes the natural sugars in the fruit, intensifying its sweetness and creating a subtle smoky undertone. Paired with a touch of spice, grilled watermelon becomes a complex and irresistible treat that will tantalize your taste buds.

Ingredients You’ll Need

To prepare sweet and spicy grilled watermelon, you’ll need the following ingredients:

  • 1 medium seedless watermelon, cut into wedges
  • 2 tablespoons of honey
  • 1 teaspoon of chili powder
  • 1 lime, juiced
  • A pinch of salt
  • Fresh mint leaves for garnish

Instructions

  1. Preheat the Grill: Start by preheating your grill to medium-high heat. Make sure the grates are clean and lightly oiled to prevent the watermelon from sticking.

  2. Prepare the Watermelon: Cut the watermelon into wedges, leaving the rind on for easy handling on the grill.

  3. Create the Glaze: In a small bowl, mix together the honey, chili powder, lime juice, and a pinch of salt. This sweet and spicy glaze will be brushed onto the watermelon during grilling.

  4. Grill the Watermelon: Carefully place the watermelon wedges on the preheated grill. Let them cook for about 2-3 minutes on each side, or until grill marks appear and the fruit begins to caramelize.

  5. Glaze the Watermelon: Using a basting brush, generously coat each side of the watermelon wedges with the prepared glaze. This step infuses the fruit with the perfect balance of sweetness and heat.

  6. Serve and Garnish: Once the watermelon is nicely grilled and glazed, remove it from the grill and arrange it on a platter. Garnish with fresh mint leaves for a pop of color and an extra layer of freshness.

Serving Suggestions

Sweet and spicy grilled watermelon can be enjoyed in various ways. Serve it as a standalone snack at your next summer barbecue, or get creative with these serving suggestions:

  • Salad Topping: Chop the grilled watermelon into cubes and add it to a fresh summer salad for a burst of flavor and texture.
  • Savory Pairing: Pair the grilled watermelon with grilled halloumi cheese for a delightful contrast of sweet, salty, and smoky flavors.
  • Cocktail Garnish: Use grilled watermelon wedges as a unique and eye-catching garnish for your favorite summer cocktails.

Final Thoughts

Grilled watermelon is a true summer sensation that defies expectations and delivers a symphony of flavors on your palate. The combination of sweetness, smokiness, and spice creates a memorable snacking experience that will have your guests asking for more. Experiment with different spice blends and serving ideas to make this unique summer snack your own. Embrace the unexpected and savor the magic of sweet and spicy grilled watermelon this summer!
