Dairy-Free Pizza and Pasta Recipes for Italian Night at Home


Welcome to our delicious dairy-free recipes for Italian night at home! Whether you have dietary restrictions or simply want to explore new flavors, these dairy-free pizza and pasta recipes will surely satisfy your cravings. In this blog post, we will share three mouthwatering recipes that are easy to make and bursting with Italian flavors.

1. Dairy-Free Margherita Pizza

Ingredients:

  • 1 pizza crust (store-bought or homemade)
  • 1/2 cup tomato sauce
  • 1 cup dairy-free mozzarella cheese
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • Extra-virgin olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to the temperature specified on the pizza crust package or your homemade recipe.
  2. Roll out the pizza crust on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper.
  3. Spread the tomato sauce evenly over the crust, leaving a small border around the edges.
  4. Sprinkle the dairy-free mozzarella cheese on top of the sauce.
  5. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  6. Bake in the preheated oven until the crust is golden and the cheese has melted and bubbled, usually 10-15 minutes.
  7. Remove from the oven and garnish with fresh basil leaves.
  8. Drizzle with extra-virgin olive oil for added flavor.
  9. Slice and serve hot. Buon appetito!

2. Dairy-Free Pesto Pasta

Ingredients:

  • 8 ounces gluten-free pasta (or any pasta of your choice)
  • 2 cups fresh basil leaves
  • 1/2 cup pine nuts
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 1/4 cup nutritional yeast
  • 1/4 cup extra-virgin olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Cook the pasta according to the package instructions until al dente. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a food processor, combine the basil leaves, pine nuts, garlic cloves, nutritional yeast, and a pinch of salt and pepper.
  3. Pulse until the ingredients are well combined and form a thick paste.
  4. Gradually add the extra-virgin olive oil while pulsing, until the mixture reaches a creamy consistency.
  5. In a large pan, heat a tablespoon of olive oil over medium heat.
  6. Add the cooked pasta to the pan and toss it with the dairy-free pesto sauce until well coated.
  7. Cook for an additional 2-3 minutes to heat the pasta and allow the flavors to meld together.
  8. Serve hot and garnish with fresh basil leaves, if desired. Enjoy!

3. Dairy-Free Eggplant Parmesan

Ingredients:

  • 1 large eggplant, sliced into 1/2-inch rounds
  • 1 cup gluten-free breadcrumbs
  • 1/2 cup almond milk (or any dairy-free milk)
  • 1/4 cup nutritional yeast
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon onion powder
  • 1 cup marinara sauce
  • Fresh basil leaves for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C).
  2. In a shallow bowl, combine the breadcrumbs, nutritional yeast, dried oregano, dried basil, garlic powder, and onion powder.
  3. Dip each eggplant slice into the almond milk, ensuring it is fully coated.
  4. Dredge the eggplant slice in the breadcrumb mixture, pressing gently to adhere the crumbs to the eggplant.
  5. Place the coated eggplant slices on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper.
  6. Bake in the preheated oven for 20-25 minutes or until the eggplant is golden and crisp.
  7. Remove the eggplant from the oven and top each slice with marinara sauce.
  8. Return the baking sheet to the oven and bake for an additional 5-10 minutes or until the sauce is heated through.
  9. Garnish with fresh basil leaves and serve hot. Delizioso!

We hope you enjoy these dairy-free pizza and pasta recipes for your Italian night at home. By using dairy-free alternatives, you can still indulge in the rich flavors of Italian cuisine while accommodating your dietary needs. So gather your loved ones, put on some Italian music, and create a memorable Italian feast right in your own kitchen. Buon appetito!
