Sweet and Savory Bacon Biscuits Recipe


Imagine waking up to the delightful aroma of freshly baked sweet and savory bacon biscuits wafting through your kitchen. These delectable treats are a perfect combination of crispy bacon, flaky biscuits, and a hint of sweetness. Whether you’re a fan of savory breakfasts or enjoy a touch of sweetness with your meal, these bacon biscuits are sure to become a family favorite. Let’s dive into the recipe and learn how to create these mouthwatering delights from scratch.

Ingredients:

  • 8 slices of bacon
  • 2 cups of all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 cup of granulated sugar
  • 1 tablespoon of baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon of salt
  • 1/2 cup of unsalted butter (cold and cut into small pieces)
  • 1/2 cup of whole milk
  • 1 large egg
  • 1/4 cup of maple syrup

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the Bacon:

    • Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C).
    • Place the bacon slices on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper.
    • Bake the bacon in the preheated oven for 15-20 minutes until crispy.
    • Remove the bacon from the oven, let it cool, then chop it into small pieces.

  2. Make the Biscuit Dough:

    • In a large mixing bowl, combine the flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt.
    • Add the cold butter pieces to the dry ingredients and use a pastry cutter or fork to cut the butter into the flour mixture until it resembles coarse crumbs.
    • In a separate bowl, whisk together the milk, egg, and maple syrup.
    • Pour the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients and stir until just combined.
    • Gently fold in the chopped bacon.

  3. Shape and Bake the Biscuits:

    • Turn the dough out onto a floured surface and knead it gently a few times until it comes together.
    • Roll out the dough to about 1-inch thickness and use a biscuit cutter to cut out biscuits.
    • Place the biscuits on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper.
    • Bake the biscuits in the preheated oven for 15-18 minutes or until they are golden brown.

  4. Serve and Enjoy:

    • Once the bacon biscuits are baked to perfection, remove them from the oven and let them cool slightly.
    • Serve the biscuits warm with a drizzle of maple syrup on top for an extra touch of sweetness.
    • Enjoy these sweet and savory bacon biscuits with a hot cup of coffee or tea for a truly comforting breakfast experience.

These sweet and savory bacon biscuits are a wonderful twist on the classic biscuit recipe. The combination of crispy bacon bits and a hint of maple syrup adds a unique flavor profile that will leave your taste buds craving more. Whether you enjoy them for breakfast, brunch, or as a snack, these biscuits are sure to impress your family and friends.

So, why not surprise your loved ones with a batch of these delicious bacon biscuits? They are easy to make, incredibly flavorful, and perfect for any occasion. Give this recipe a try and treat yourself to a delightful culinary experience that combines the best of both sweet and savory flavors. Your taste buds will thank you!

Remember, good food is meant to be shared and enjoyed with the ones you love. Happy baking and savor every bite of these sweet and savory bacon biscuits!
