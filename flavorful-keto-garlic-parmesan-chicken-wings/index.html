Flavorful Keto Garlic Parmesan Chicken Wings


If you’re searching for a delicious and keto-friendly appetizer or main dish, look no further than these Flavorful Keto Garlic Parmesan Chicken Wings. This recipe combines the crispy texture of perfectly baked chicken wings with the savory flavors of garlic and parmesan, making it a crowd-pleaser for any occasion. Whether you’re hosting a game night, a family gathering, or simply craving a flavorful low-carb meal, these chicken wings are sure to impress. Let’s dive into the details of creating this mouthwatering dish.

Ingredients

  • 2 lbs of chicken wings
  • 1/2 cup grated parmesan cheese
  • 4 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper
  • 1/4 cup fresh parsley, chopped
  • 2 tbsp unsalted butter, melted

Instructions

Step 1: Preparing the Chicken Wings

  1. Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C).
  2. Line a baking sheet with parchment paper and set it aside.
  3. In a large bowl, toss the chicken wings with olive oil, salt, and black pepper until they are evenly coated.

Step 2: Baking the Chicken Wings

  1. Place the seasoned chicken wings on the prepared baking sheet in a single layer, ensuring they are not touching each other.
  2. Bake the chicken wings in the preheated oven for 40-45 minutes, or until they are golden brown and crispy.

Step 3: Making the Garlic Parmesan Coating

  1. In a small bowl, combine the grated parmesan cheese, minced garlic, dried oregano, and paprika.
  2. Once the chicken wings are cooked, remove them from the oven and transfer them to a clean large bowl.
  3. Pour the melted butter over the hot chicken wings and toss them until they are evenly coated.
  4. Sprinkle the garlic parmesan mixture over the wings and toss them again to ensure even distribution.

Step 4: Serving the Chicken Wings

  1. Transfer the coated chicken wings to a serving platter and sprinkle the chopped parsley over the top for a pop of color and freshness.
  2. Serve the Flavorful Keto Garlic Parmesan Chicken Wings with your favorite dipping sauce or alongside a crisp salad for a complete meal.

Nutritional Information

  • Serving size: 6 wings
  • Calories: 320
  • Total Fat: 22g
  • Saturated Fat: 8g
  • Cholesterol: 110mg
  • Sodium: 420mg
  • Total Carbohydrates: 2g
  • Dietary Fiber: 0g
  • Sugars: 0g
  • Protein: 28g

Final Thoughts

These Flavorful Keto Garlic Parmesan Chicken Wings are a perfect combination of crispy, savory, and indulgent flavors without the guilt of carbs. Whether you’re following a keto lifestyle or simply looking for a new way to enjoy chicken wings, this recipe is a must-try. The blend of garlic, parmesan, and a hint of herbs creates a tantalizing flavor profile that will leave your taste buds craving more. So, gather your ingredients, preheat your oven, and get ready to savor every bite of these delectable chicken wings. Enjoy!
