Coconut Cream Pie Recipe: A Creamy and Delicious Vegan Dessert


If you’re in the mood for a luscious and indulgent dessert that’s not only vegan but also bursting with tropical flavors, then this Vegan Coconut Cream Pie is just the ticket. This recipe combines the richness of coconut with a velvety smooth cream filling, all nestled in a crisp, flaky crust. Whether you’re a seasoned vegan or simply looking to explore plant-based desserts, this Coconut Cream Pie will surely delight your taste buds and impress your guests. So, let’s dive into the recipe and bring a taste of paradise to your dessert table.

Ingredients

  • For the Crust:

    • 1 1/2 cups of almond flour
    • 1/4 cup of coconut oil, melted
    • 2 tablespoons of maple syrup
    • A pinch of salt

  • For the Coconut Cream Filling:

    • 2 cans of full-fat coconut milk, chilled
    • 1/2 cup of coconut sugar
    • 1/4 cup of cornstarch
    • 1 teaspoon of vanilla extract
    • 1/2 teaspoon of coconut extract (optional)

  • For the Whipped Topping:

    • 1 can of coconut cream, chilled
    • 2 tablespoons of powdered sugar
    • 1 teaspoon of vanilla extract

  • For Garnish (Optional):

    • Toasted coconut flakes
    • Fresh berries

Instructions

Making the Crust:

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. In a bowl, combine the almond flour, melted coconut oil, maple syrup, and a pinch of salt.
  3. Press the mixture into a 9-inch pie dish, making sure to evenly coat the bottom and sides.
  4. Bake the crust for 10-12 minutes or until it turns golden brown. Allow it to cool completely.

Preparing the Coconut Cream Filling:

  1. Open the chilled coconut milk cans and scoop out the thick cream that has risen to the top, leaving the watery portion behind. Place the cream in a mixing bowl.
  2. In a saucepan, whisk together the coconut sugar, cornstarch, vanilla extract, and coconut extract (if using).
  3. Gradually pour in the coconut cream, whisking constantly to avoid lumps.
  4. Place the saucepan over medium heat and bring the mixture to a gentle boil. Let it simmer for 2-3 minutes or until it thickens.
  5. Remove the pan from the heat and pour the coconut cream filling into the cooled pie crust. Smooth out the top with a spatula. Refrigerate the pie for at least 4 hours or until set.

Making the Whipped Topping:

  1. Open the chilled can of coconut cream and scoop out the thickened cream into a mixing bowl, leaving any watery residue behind.
  2. Add the powdered sugar and vanilla extract to the cream.
  3. Whip the mixture with a hand mixer until it becomes light and fluffy.

Assembling the Pie:

  1. Once the pie has set, spread the whipped coconut cream over the top of the pie.
  2. If desired, sprinkle toasted coconut flakes and arrange fresh berries on top for a decorative touch.

Serving and Storing

This Vegan Coconut Cream Pie is best served chilled. Slice it with a sharp knife and savor each creamy bite. Any leftovers can be stored in the refrigerator for up to 3-4 days, though it’s unlikely there will be any left once your guests taste this delectable dessert!

Final Thoughts

With its dreamy coconut-infused filling and buttery almond crust, this Vegan Coconut Cream Pie is a true celebration of tropical flavors. Whether it’s for a special occasion or a casual gathering, this dessert is sure to leave a lasting impression.
