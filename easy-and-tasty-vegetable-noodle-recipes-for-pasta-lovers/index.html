Easy and Tasty Vegetable Noodle Recipes for Pasta Lovers


Are you a pasta lover looking for a healthier alternative? Look no further than these easy and tasty vegetable noodle recipes! Packed with flavor and loaded with nutrients, these recipes are perfect for those who want to enjoy their favorite pasta dishes without the guilt. Whether you’re a vegetarian, vegan, or simply looking to incorporate more vegetables into your diet, these recipes are sure to satisfy your pasta cravings. So grab your spiralizer and let’s get cooking!

1. Zucchini Noodle Carbonara

Ingredients:

  • 2 large zucchinis
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 cup of mushrooms, sliced
  • 4 slices of bacon, cooked and crumbled
  • 1/2 cup of grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 eggs, beaten
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Spiralize the zucchinis to create long, thin noodles.
  2. Heat a large skillet over medium heat and add a drizzle of olive oil.
  3. Sauté the garlic and mushrooms until the mushrooms are tender.
  4. Add the zucchini noodles to the skillet and cook for 2-3 minutes, until they are slightly softened.
  5. In a separate bowl, whisk together the beaten eggs, Parmesan cheese, salt, and pepper.
  6. Pour the egg mixture over the zucchini noodles and toss gently to combine.
  7. Continue cooking for 1-2 minutes, until the eggs are fully cooked and the noodles are coated in the sauce.
  8. Serve immediately, garnished with crumbled bacon.

2. Sweet Potato Noodle Bolognese

Ingredients:

  • 2 large sweet potatoes
  • 1 pound of ground beef or plant-based alternative
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 carrot, diced
  • 1 celery stalk, diced
  • 1 can of crushed tomatoes
  • 1 tablespoon of tomato paste
  • 1 teaspoon of dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Using a spiralizer, turn the sweet potatoes into noodles.
  2. In a large skillet, cook the ground beef (or plant-based alternative) until browned. Remove from the skillet and set aside.
  3. In the same skillet, sauté the onion, garlic, carrot, and celery until softened.
  4. Add the ground beef back to the skillet and stir in the crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, oregano, salt, and pepper.
  5. Simmer the sauce for 15-20 minutes, until it has thickened.
  6. While the sauce is simmering, cook the sweet potato noodles in boiling water for 5-7 minutes, until tender.
  7. Drain the noodles and divide them among plates. Top with the bolognese sauce and serve hot.

3. Carrot Noodle Pad Thai

Ingredients:

  • 4 large carrots
  • 2 tablespoons of vegetable oil
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 cup of tofu, diced
  • 2 eggs, beaten (optional for vegan version)
  • 1/4 cup of chopped peanuts
  • 2 tablespoons of soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon of honey or agave syrup
  • 1 tablespoon of lime juice
  • 1/4 cup of fresh cilantro, chopped

Instructions:

  1. Peel the carrots and use a spiralizer to create carrot noodles.
  2. Heat the vegetable oil in a large skillet over medium heat.
  3. Add the garlic and tofu to the skillet and cook until the tofu is golden brown.
  4. Push the tofu to one side of the skillet and pour the beaten eggs into the other side. Scramble the eggs until cooked, then mix them with the tofu.
  5. Add the carrot noodles to the skillet and stir-fry for 2-3 minutes, until they are slightly softened.
  6. In a small bowl, whisk together the soy sauce, honey (or agave syrup), and lime juice.
  7. Pour the sauce over the carrot noodles and tofu, tossing to coat evenly.
  8. Remove from heat and garnish with chopped peanuts and fresh cilantro.
  9. Serve hot and enjoy!

These easy and tasty vegetable noodle recipes are a great way to satisfy your pasta cravings while still enjoying the benefits of a vegetable-rich diet. From zucchini carbonara to sweet potato bolognese and carrot pad Thai, there’s something for everyone to enjoy. So why not give these recipes a try and experience the deliciousness of vegetable noodles for yourself? Happy cooking!
