Scrumptious Keto Pepperoni Pizza Cauliflower Casserole


Are you craving the flavors of a classic pepperoni pizza but want to keep it keto-friendly? Look no further than this Scrumptious Keto Pepperoni Pizza Cauliflower Casserole! Packed with savory flavors, cheesy goodness, and a low-carb cauliflower base, this casserole is a delightful twist on a beloved dish. Whether you’re following a keto lifestyle or simply want to try a delicious new recipe, this casserole is sure to become a favorite in your household.

Ingredients:

For the Cauliflower Base:

  • 1 large head of cauliflower, cut into florets
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste

For the Pizza Topping:

  • 1 cup sugar-free marinara sauce
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1 cup sliced pepperoni
  • 1/2 cup sliced black olives
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • Fresh basil for garnish (optional)

Instructions:

1. Preparing the Cauliflower Base:

  • Preheat the oven to 400°F (200°C).
  • In a large bowl, toss the cauliflower florets with olive oil, garlic powder, salt, and pepper.
  • Spread the seasoned cauliflower on a baking sheet and roast for 20-25 minutes, or until tender and slightly golden. Remove from the oven and set aside.

2. Assembling the Casserole:

  • Grease a baking dish with olive oil or non-stick cooking spray.
  • Spread the roasted cauliflower in an even layer at the bottom of the baking dish.
  • Pour the sugar-free marinara sauce over the cauliflower and spread it evenly.
  • Sprinkle the shredded mozzarella cheese over the marinara sauce, covering the cauliflower completely.
  • Arrange the pepperoni slices and black olive slices on top of the cheese.
  • Sprinkle the dried oregano over the pepperoni and olives.

3. Baking the Casserole:

  • Place the assembled casserole in the preheated oven and bake for 20-25 minutes, or until the cheese is melted and bubbly, and the edges are golden brown.
  • Once done, remove the casserole from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes.
  • Garnish with fresh basil, if desired, before serving.

Serving Suggestions:

This Scrumptious Keto Pepperoni Pizza Cauliflower Casserole is delightful on its own, but you can elevate the experience by serving it with a side salad dressed with balsamic vinaigrette or a refreshing cucumber and tomato salad. The bright acidity of the salads complements the richness of the casserole, creating a well-balanced meal.

Final Thoughts:

This keto-friendly twist on a classic pepperoni pizza is a testament to how creative and satisfying low-carb cooking can be. The cauliflower base provides a hearty foundation, while the combination of marinara sauce, cheese, and pepperoni delivers all the flavors you love in a pizza. Whether you’re following a keto diet or simply looking for a delicious and wholesome meal, this Scrumptious Keto Pepperoni Pizza Cauliflower Casserole is a must-try dish that will delight your taste buds and leave you feeling satisfied.

Give this recipe a try, and savor the comforting flavors of pepperoni pizza in a wholesome, keto-friendly casserole. Enjoy!
