Pasta with Creamy Lemon Herb Chicken: A Bright and Flavorful Dish


If you’re looking for a bright and flavorful dish that’s perfect for any occasion, then look no further than this Pasta with Creamy Lemon Herb Chicken recipe. This dish is an absolute crowd-pleaser, combining the zesty freshness of lemon and herbs with the rich creaminess of a classic pasta dish. It’s a delightful fusion of flavors that will leave your taste buds singing!

Ingredients

For the Chicken:

  • 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • 1 teaspoon dried rosemary
  • 1/2 cup chicken broth
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • Zest of 1 lemon
  • Juice of 1 lemon
  • 2 tablespoons fresh parsley, chopped

For the Pasta:

  • 12 oz fettuccine or your pasta of choice
  • Salt for boiling water
  • Freshly grated Parmesan cheese for serving
  • Fresh parsley for garnish

Instructions

Step 1: Cook the Chicken

  1. Season the chicken breasts with salt and pepper on both sides.
  2. In a large skillet, heat olive oil over medium-high heat.
  3. Add the chicken breasts to the skillet and cook for about 6-7 minutes on each side, or until they are golden brown and cooked through.
  4. Remove the chicken from the skillet and set aside.

Step 2: Prepare the Sauce

  1. In the same skillet, add minced garlic, dried oregano, dried thyme, and dried rosemary. Sauté for about 1 minute until fragrant.
  2. Pour in the chicken broth, scraping up any browned bits from the bottom of the skillet.
  3. Let the broth simmer for 2-3 minutes.
  4. Stir in the heavy cream, lemon zest, and lemon juice. Simmer for an additional 5 minutes until the sauce thickens slightly.
  5. Return the cooked chicken to the skillet, coating it with the creamy lemon herb sauce.
  6. Sprinkle fresh parsley over the chicken.

Step 3: Cook the Pasta

  1. In a large pot, bring salted water to a boil.
  2. Cook the pasta according to the package instructions until al dente.
  3. Drain the pasta, reserving a cup of the pasta water.

Step 4: Combine the Pasta and Chicken

  1. Add the cooked pasta to the skillet with the creamy lemon herb chicken.
  2. Toss the pasta and chicken together, adding a splash of the reserved pasta water if needed to loosen the sauce.
  3. Taste and adjust the seasoning with salt and pepper if necessary.

Step 5: Serve and Enjoy

  1. Divide the pasta into serving plates.
  2. Garnish with freshly grated Parmesan cheese and chopped parsley.
  3. Serve immediately and enjoy the bright and flavorful dish!

Conclusion

This Pasta with Creamy Lemon Herb Chicken recipe is a true celebration of fresh flavors and creamy indulgence. The zing of lemon and the fragrant herbs elevate the tender chicken and perfectly coat the pasta, creating a dish that’s both comforting and exciting. Whether you’re hosting a dinner party or simply craving a special meal at home, this recipe is sure to impress. So, gather your ingredients, let the aromas fill your kitchen, and savor every delightful bite of this bright and flavorful dish.
