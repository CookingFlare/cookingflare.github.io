Instant Pot Lemon Chicken: Tangy and Savory


Are you looking for a quick and delicious chicken recipe that bursts with tangy and savory flavors? Look no further, because Instant Pot Lemon Chicken is here to satisfy your taste buds! This mouthwatering dish combines the brightness of lemon with the richness of chicken, creating a perfect balance of flavors that will leave you craving for more. And the best part? It’s incredibly easy to make in your trusty Instant Pot!

Ingredients

To make this delightful Instant Pot Lemon Chicken, you’ll need the following ingredients:

  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 4 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • Zest of 2 lemons
  • Juice of 2 lemons
  • 1 cup chicken broth
  • 1 tablespoon cornstarch (optional, for thickening)

Instructions

  1. Start by seasoning the chicken breasts with salt, black pepper, dried oregano, and dried thyme. Ensure that the chicken is evenly coated with the spices.

  2. Set your Instant Pot to sauté mode and heat the olive oil. Once the oil is hot, add the seasoned chicken breasts and cook them for 2-3 minutes on each side until they are lightly browned. This step helps to seal in the flavors and gives the chicken a beautiful golden color.

  3. After browning the chicken, remove it from the Instant Pot and set it aside. Add the minced garlic to the pot and sauté for about 1 minute until fragrant.

  4. Deglaze the pot by adding the lemon juice and using a wooden spoon to scrape up any browned bits from the bottom. This step will infuse the chicken with the bright and tangy flavor of lemon.

  5. Add the lemon zest and chicken broth to the pot, and give it a good stir to combine all the ingredients. Return the chicken breasts to the pot, making sure they are partially submerged in the liquid.

  6. Place the lid on the Instant Pot and set it to manual high pressure for 8 minutes. Once the cooking time is complete, allow the pressure to naturally release for 5 minutes, then carefully do a quick release of the remaining pressure.

  7. If you prefer a thicker sauce, you can mix 1 tablespoon of cornstarch with a little water, and add it to the pot. Switch the Instant Pot to sauté mode and cook for an additional 2-3 minutes until the sauce thickens.

  8. Serve the Instant Pot Lemon Chicken hot, garnished with fresh parsley or lemon slices if desired. This tangy and savory delight pairs perfectly with steamed rice, roasted vegetables, or a crisp green salad.

Conclusion

With its zesty lemon flavor and tender chicken, Instant Pot Lemon Chicken is a winner in both taste and convenience. This recipe allows you to create a restaurant-quality dish in the comfort of your own home, with minimal effort and maximum flavor. The Instant Pot does all the hard work, infusing the chicken with the tangy essence of lemon, while keeping it moist and tender. So, next time you’re in the mood for a delicious and hassle-free meal, give this recipe a try. Your taste buds will thank you!

That’s it for our Instant Pot Lemon Chicken recipe! We hope you enjoy making and savoring this tangy and savory dish as much as we do. Let us know in the comments below how it turns out for you. Happy cooking!
