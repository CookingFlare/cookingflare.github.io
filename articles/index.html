Ingredients 2 pounds of large shrimp, peeled and deveined 4 cloves of garlic, minced 1/4 cup of extra virgin olive oil Zest of 1 lemon Juice of 2 lemons 1 teaspoon of dried oregano 1 teaspoon of dried thyme Salt and black pepper to taste Fresh parsley for garnish Instructions Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C) and line a baking sheet with parchment paper. In a large bowl, combine the shrimp, minced garlic, olive oil, lemon zest, lemon juice, oregano, thyme, salt, and black pepper. Toss everything together until the shrimp are evenly coated with the marinade. Spread the marinated shrimp in a single layer on the prepared baking sheet. Roast the shrimp in the preheated oven for 8-10 minutes, or until they are pink and cooked through. Be careful not to overcook the shrimp to keep them tender and juicy. Once done, remove the shrimp from the oven and garnish with freshly chopped parsley. Serve the Greek-style Lemon Garlic Roasted Shrimp immediately, either as an appetizer or a main dish. You can pair it with a refreshing Greek salad, some crusty bread, or over a bed of fluffy couscous.
