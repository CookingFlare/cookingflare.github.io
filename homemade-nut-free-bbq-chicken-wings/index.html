Homemade Nut-Free BBQ Chicken Wings


When it comes to comfort food, few dishes can rival the delight of BBQ chicken wings. Crispy on the outside, succulent on the inside, and coated with a rich, tangy sauce, these wings are a crowd-pleaser for any gathering. Whether you’re hosting a backyard barbecue or enjoying a cozy night in, nut-free BBQ chicken wings are a must-try. This recipe will guide you through the process of creating the perfect wings that everyone will love.

Why Nut-Free?

In recent years, the awareness of food allergies has grown significantly. For families dealing with nut allergies, finding safe and delicious recipes can be a challenge. Our nut-free BBQ chicken wings ensure that everyone can indulge without worry. The flavors in this dish come from the sauce and spices, allowing you to enjoy a mouthwatering treat without compromising safety.

Ingredients

For the Chicken Wings:

  • 2 pounds of chicken wings
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon onion powder
  • 1 teaspoon smoked paprika
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil

For the BBQ Sauce:

  • 1 cup ketchup
  • 1/4 cup apple cider vinegar
  • 1/4 cup brown sugar
  • 1 tablespoon Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 tablespoon honey
  • 1 teaspoon mustard powder
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon onion powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon smoked paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon cayenne pepper (optional, for heat)

Equipment Needed

  • Large mixing bowl
  • Baking sheet
  • Parchment paper or aluminum foil
  • Saucepan
  • Whisk
  • Basting brush
  • Instant-read thermometer

Instructions

Step 1: Preparing the Chicken Wings

  1. Preheat Your Oven: Begin by preheating your oven to 425°F (220°C). A high temperature will help achieve that perfect crispiness on the wings.

  2. Clean and Dry the Wings: Rinse the chicken wings under cold water and pat them dry with paper towels. Removing excess moisture is crucial for crispiness.

  3. Season the Wings: In a large mixing bowl, combine the salt, black pepper, garlic powder, onion powder, and smoked paprika. Add the chicken wings to the bowl and drizzle with olive oil. Toss everything together until the wings are well-coated with the seasoning.

Step 2: Baking the Wings

  1. Prepare the Baking Sheet: Line a baking sheet with parchment paper or aluminum foil for easy cleanup. Place a wire rack on top of the baking sheet to allow air circulation around the wings.

  2. Arrange the Wings: Lay the seasoned wings out in a single layer on the wire rack, ensuring they are not touching. This helps them cook evenly and become crispy.

  3. Bake the Wings: Place the baking sheet in the preheated oven and bake for 40-45 minutes, flipping the wings halfway through. Use an instant-read thermometer to check for doneness; the internal temperature should reach 165°F (75°C).

Step 3: Making the BBQ Sauce

  1. Combine Ingredients: While the wings are baking, prepare the BBQ sauce. In a saucepan, whisk together ketchup, apple cider vinegar, brown sugar, Worcestershire sauce, honey, mustard powder, garlic powder, onion powder, smoked paprika, black pepper, salt, and cayenne pepper (if using).

  2. Simmer the Sauce: Heat the saucepan over medium heat and bring the mixture to a gentle simmer. Allow it to cook for about 10-15 minutes, stirring occasionally until the sauce thickens slightly. Remove from heat and set aside.

Step 4: Saucing the Wings

  1. Coat the Wings: Once the chicken wings are cooked to perfection, remove them from the oven. Place the wings in a large mixing bowl and pour half of the BBQ sauce over them. Toss the wings to ensure they are evenly coated.

  2. Broil for Extra Crispiness: For an extra touch, return the coated wings to the oven on the broiler setting for an additional 5-7 minutes. Keep a close eye on them to prevent burning.

  3. Serve with Remaining Sauce: Transfer the wings to a serving platter and drizzle the remaining BBQ sauce on top. You can also serve additional sauce on the side for dipping.

Tips for Perfect BBQ Chicken Wings

  • Choose the Right Wings: Look for fresh, high-quality chicken wings. You can buy them whole and separate them into flats and drumettes, or purchase them pre-cut for convenience.

  • Marinate for Flavor: For an even deeper flavor, consider marinating the wings in the seasoning mixture for a couple of hours or overnight in the refrigerator.

  • Experiment with Spices: Feel free to adjust the seasonings and spices in the BBQ sauce to match your taste preferences. You can add a splash of hot sauce for extra heat or a hint of liquid smoke for a smoky flavor.

  • Grilling Option: For those who prefer a grilled flavor, you can also cook the wings on a grill. Simply preheat the grill to medium-high heat and cook the wings, turning occasionally, until they reach an internal temperature of 165°F.

Pairing Suggestions

These nut-free BBQ chicken wings are incredibly versatile and can be paired with a variety of side dishes to create a complete meal. Here are a few ideas:

  • Coleslaw: A refreshing side of coleslaw complements the smoky flavors of the wings perfectly.
  • Corn on the Cob: Grilled or boiled corn on the cob slathered in butter is a classic summer side.
  • French Fries: Crispy French fries or sweet potato fries make for a delightful pairing.
  • Vegetable Platter: Serve with a platter of fresh vegetables and ranch or blue cheese dressing for dipping.

Storing and Reheating Leftovers

If you happen to have any leftover wings (though that’s unlikely!), you can store them in an airtight container in the refrigerator for up to three days. To reheat, simply place the wings on a baking sheet and bake at 350°F (175°C) for about 10-15 minutes until heated through and crispy.

Conclusion

Homemade nut-free BBQ chicken wings are not only easy to make but also incredibly flavorful and satisfying. Whether you’re preparing them for a family gathering or just a quiet night at home, these wings are sure to impress. With a crunchy exterior, juicy meat, and a tangy BBQ sauce, they are bound to become a staple in your recipe repertoire. Enjoy the process of making these delicious wings, and don’t forget to share with friends and family—because great food is meant to be enjoyed together!
