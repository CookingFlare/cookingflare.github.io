Delicious and Nutritious Indian Spinach Dal


Indian cuisine is known for its rich flavors and vibrant colors. One classic dish that perfectly embodies the essence of Indian cooking is Spinach Dal. Packed with nutrients and bursting with flavor, this traditional lentil and spinach stew is a true delight for the senses. In this recipe, we will explore how to make a delicious and nutritious Indian Spinach Dal that will leave you craving for more.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup yellow split lentils (moong dal)
  • 2 cups fresh spinach leaves, washed and chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, finely chopped
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1-inch piece of ginger, grated
  • 2 green chilies, slit lengthwise
  • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 teaspoon red chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon garam masala
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 tablespoons ghee or vegetable oil
  • Fresh cilantro leaves, for garnish

Instructions

  1. Rinse the yellow split lentils thoroughly under cold running water until the water runs clear. Soak the lentils in water for about 30 minutes.

  2. In a large pot, heat the ghee or vegetable oil over medium heat. Add the cumin seeds and let them sizzle for a few seconds until fragrant.

  3. Add the minced garlic, grated ginger, and slit green chilies to the pot. Sauté for a minute until the garlic turns golden brown and aromatic.

  4. Add the finely chopped onions and cook until they turn translucent. Stir occasionally to prevent burning.

  5. Once the onions are cooked, add the chopped tomatoes to the pot. Cook until the tomatoes become soft and mushy, about 5 minutes.

  6. Drain the soaked lentils and add them to the pot. Stir well to combine the lentils with the onion and tomato mixture.

  7. Add turmeric powder, red chili powder, and salt to taste. Mix well to coat the lentils with the spices.

  8. Pour in enough water to cover the lentils completely. Bring the mixture to a boil, then reduce the heat to low and cover the pot. Let the dal simmer for about 20 minutes or until the lentils are cooked through and tender.

  9. Once the lentils are cooked, add the chopped spinach leaves to the pot. Stir well and let the spinach wilt in the hot dal for a few minutes.

  10. Sprinkle garam masala over the dal and give it a final stir. Adjust the seasoning according to your taste preferences.

  11. Remove the pot from heat and transfer the Spinach Dal to a serving bowl. Garnish with fresh cilantro leaves.

Serve the Delicious and Nutritious Indian Spinach Dal hot with steamed rice or naan bread for a complete and satisfying meal. The creamy texture of the lentils combined with the earthiness of the spinach and aromatic spices creates a harmonious blend of flavors that will transport you to the streets of India. This comforting dish not only pleases your taste buds but also nourishes your body with its abundance of vitamins, minerals, and fiber.

So, next time you’re in the mood for a wholesome and flavorsome Indian meal, give this Spinach Dal recipe a try. It’s a true culinary masterpiece that will leave you coming back for more. Enjoy!
