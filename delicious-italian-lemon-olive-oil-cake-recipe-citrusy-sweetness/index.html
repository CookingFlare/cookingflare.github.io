Delicious Italian Lemon Olive Oil Cake Recipe - Citrusy Sweetness


Are you a fan of citrusy desserts that are both refreshing and indulgent? If so, you’re in for a treat! Today, I’m sharing with you a mouthwatering recipe for a Delicious Italian Lemon Olive Oil Cake - a delightful dessert that perfectly balances the tanginess of lemons with the subtle sweetness of olive oil.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 3 large eggs
  • 1 1/4 cups granulated sugar
  • 1/2 cup extra virgin olive oil
  • 1/2 cup whole milk
  • Zest of 2 lemons
  • Juice of 1 lemon
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • Powdered sugar, for dusting

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C) and grease a 9-inch round cake pan.

  2. In a medium bowl, whisk together the flour, baking powder, baking soda, and salt. Set aside.

  3. In a large bowl, beat the eggs and granulated sugar until pale and fluffy. This should take about 3-4 minutes.

  4. Slowly add the olive oil to the egg mixture while continuously whisking. Make sure the oil is fully incorporated.

  5. Add the lemon zest, lemon juice, and vanilla extract to the mixture. Mix well.

  6. Gradually add the dry ingredients to the wet ingredients, alternating with the milk. Begin and end with the dry ingredients, mixing until just combined. Be careful not to overmix the batter.

  7. Pour the batter into the prepared cake pan and smooth the top with a spatula.

  8. Bake for approximately 35-40 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.

  9. Remove the cake from the oven and let it cool in the pan for 10 minutes. Then, transfer it to a wire rack to cool completely.

  10. Once the cake has cooled, dust the top with powdered sugar for an elegant finishing touch.

Serving Suggestions

This Italian Lemon Olive Oil Cake is perfect for any occasion. It pairs beautifully with a cup of tea or coffee, making it an ideal treat for afternoon gatherings or brunches. For an extra touch of indulgence, serve it with a dollop of freshly whipped cream or a scoop of creamy vanilla ice cream.

Storage Tips

If you have any leftovers (which might be rare, considering how delicious this cake is!), you can store them in an airtight container at room temperature for up to three days. The cake will remain moist and flavorful, allowing you to enjoy its citrusy sweetness even after a few days.

Final Thoughts

The combination of tangy lemons and fragrant olive oil creates a unique and delightful flavor profile in this Italian Lemon Olive Oil Cake. Its moist and tender crumb, coupled with the refreshing citrus notes, will leave you craving for more. So, why not give this recipe a try and indulge in a slice of citrusy sweetness? We guarantee it will become a favorite in your dessert repertoire!

Remember, baking is all about experimentation and enjoyment. Feel free to customize this recipe to suit your taste preferences. Add a hint of lemon glaze or sprinkle some crushed pistachios on top for an extra crunch. The possibilities are endless!

Happy baking!
