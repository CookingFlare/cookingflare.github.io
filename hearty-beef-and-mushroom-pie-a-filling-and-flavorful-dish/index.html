Hearty Beef and Mushroom Pie: A Filling and Flavorful Dish


Are you craving a comforting and savory dish that will warm your soul? Look no further than our Hearty Beef and Mushroom Pie recipe. This delicious pie combines tender beef, earthy mushrooms, and flavorful herbs, all encased in a buttery pastry crust. With its rich and satisfying flavors, this dish is perfect for cozy family dinners or gatherings with friends. So, roll up your sleeves and let’s get cooking!

Ingredients

  • 500g beef stew meat, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 200g button mushrooms, sliced
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • 250ml beef broth
  • 250ml red wine
  • 2 sprigs of fresh thyme
  • 1 bay leaf
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • 1 sheet of store-bought puff pastry, thawed
  • 1 egg, beaten (for egg wash)

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 180°C (350°F).

  2. In a large, oven-safe skillet or Dutch oven, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the beef stew meat and cook until browned on all sides. Remove the meat from the skillet and set it aside.

  3. In the same skillet, add the chopped onion and minced garlic. Sauté until the onion becomes translucent and fragrant.

  4. Add the sliced mushrooms to the skillet and cook until they start to soften and release their moisture.

  5. Sprinkle the flour over the mushrooms and onions, stirring well to coat. This will help thicken the sauce.

  6. Slowly pour in the beef broth and red wine, stirring constantly to ensure there are no lumps. Add the thyme sprigs and bay leaf to the skillet.

  7. Return the browned beef to the skillet and season everything with salt and pepper to taste. Stir well to combine all the ingredients.

  8. Cover the skillet with a lid or aluminum foil and transfer it to the preheated oven. Allow the beef and mushroom mixture to cook for 2-2.5 hours, or until the beef is tender and the flavors have melded together.

  9. While the beef is cooking, roll out the puff pastry sheet on a lightly floured surface. Cut it into a circle slightly larger than the diameter of your skillet.

  10. Once the beef is done cooking, remove the skillet from the oven and discard the thyme sprigs and bay leaf. Give the mixture a final stir.

  11. Carefully place the puff pastry circle over the skillet, tucking in the edges to seal the pie. Brush the pastry with the beaten egg, which will give it a beautiful golden color when baked.

  12. Using a sharp knife, make a few small slits in the center of the pastry to allow steam to escape while baking.

  13. Return the skillet to the oven and bake for 25-30 minutes, or until the pastry is puffed and golden.

  14. Remove the pie from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes before serving. This will allow the filling to set slightly.

  15. Slice the pie into generous portions and serve it alongside your favorite sides, such as mashed potatoes or steamed vegetables.

Enjoy the Perfect Comfort Food

Our Hearty Beef and Mushroom Pie is a true crowd-pleaser. The succulent beef, earthy mushrooms, and rich gravy come together harmoniously to create a filling and flavorful dish that will leave everyone satisfied. Whether it’s a chilly winter’s night or a Sunday family gathering, this pie is sure to bring warmth and comfort to your table.

So, gather your ingredients, preheat your oven, and get ready to indulge in a slice of pure comfort. This Hearty Beef and Mushroom Pie is bound to become a staple in your recipe repertoire. Happy cooking!
