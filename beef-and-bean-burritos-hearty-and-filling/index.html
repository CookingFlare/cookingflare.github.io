Beef and Bean Burritos: Hearty and Filling


Burritos are a classic Mexican dish that never fails to satisfy. The combination of tender beef, flavorful beans, and a variety of toppings make for a delicious and filling meal. In this recipe, we will guide you through the steps of creating homemade Beef and Bean Burritos that are sure to impress your family and friends.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 can (15 ounces) of refried beans
  • 1 teaspoon chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • 8 large flour tortillas
  • Shredded cheese (cheddar or Mexican blend)
  • Toppings of your choice: salsa, guacamole, sour cream, etc.

Instructions

  1. In a large skillet, cook the ground beef over medium heat until browned. Make sure to break up the meat into small crumbles as it cooks.
  2. Add the diced onion and minced garlic to the skillet with the beef. Cook for an additional 2-3 minutes, or until the onion becomes translucent and fragrant.
  3. Stir in the refried beans, chili powder, cumin, salt, and pepper. Mix well to combine all the ingredients.
  4. Reduce the heat to low and let the mixture simmer for about 10 minutes, stirring occasionally. This will allow the flavors to meld together and create a rich and savory filling.
  5. While the beef and bean mixture is simmering, warm the flour tortillas in a dry skillet over medium heat. This will make the tortillas more pliable and easier to roll.
  6. Once the tortillas are warmed, spoon a generous amount of the beef and bean mixture onto the center of each tortilla. Top with a sprinkle of shredded cheese.
  7. Fold the sides of the tortilla over the filling, then roll it tightly from bottom to top, creating a burrito shape. Repeat this process with the remaining tortillas and filling.
  8. Serve the Beef and Bean Burritos immediately with your choice of toppings. Salsa, guacamole, and sour cream are all great options to enhance the flavors of the burritos.
  9. Enjoy your homemade Beef and Bean Burritos while they’re still warm and the cheese is gooey!

Tips and Variations

  • For an extra kick of spice, add a diced jalapeno pepper to the beef and bean mixture.
  • Experiment with different types of beans, such as black beans or pinto beans, to create unique flavor combinations.
  • If you’re looking to add some freshness to your burritos, consider topping them with diced tomatoes, shredded lettuce, or chopped cilantro.
  • To make this recipe vegetarian-friendly, simply omit the ground beef and increase the amount of beans.

Now that you have the recipe for Beef and Bean Burritos, it’s time to gather your ingredients and get cooking! These burritos are perfect for a quick weeknight dinner or a casual gathering with friends. The hearty and filling combination of beef and beans, along with your favorite toppings, will leave everyone satisfied. So roll up your sleeves and prepare to indulge in a taste of Mexico with these delicious homemade burritos. Enjoy!
