Vegan Cherry Almond Cake: A Nutty and Fruity Delight


If you’re a fan of delicious vegan desserts that are packed with flavor, then this Vegan Cherry Almond Cake is a must-try! Bursting with the natural sweetness of cherries and the nuttiness of almonds, this cake is a delightful treat that will satisfy your cravings. Whether you’re a vegan or simply looking to incorporate more plant-based recipes into your diet, this recipe is sure to impress.

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup almond flour
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup almond milk
  • 1/2 cup vegetable oil
  • 2 teaspoons almond extract
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 cup fresh cherries, pitted and halved

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C) and grease a 9-inch round cake pan with vegan butter or cooking spray.
  2. In a large mixing bowl, combine the all-purpose flour, almond flour, sugar, baking powder, baking soda, and salt. Mix well to ensure all the dry ingredients are evenly combined.
  3. In a separate bowl, whisk together the almond milk, vegetable oil, almond extract, and vanilla extract. Pour the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients and stir until just combined. Be careful not to overmix, as this can result in a dense cake.
  4. Gently fold in the fresh cherries, ensuring they are evenly distributed throughout the batter.
  5. Pour the batter into the prepared cake pan and smooth the top with a spatula.
  6. Bake in the preheated oven for 40-45 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center of the cake comes out clean.
  7. Once baked, remove the cake from the oven and allow it to cool in the pan for 10 minutes. Then, transfer the cake to a wire rack to cool completely.
  8. Once the cake has cooled, you can enjoy it as is, or you can dust it with powdered sugar for an extra touch of sweetness. Serve slices of this delicious Vegan Cherry Almond Cake with a dollop of vegan whipped cream or a scoop of dairy-free ice cream for an indulgent treat.

The Perfect Vegan Dessert

This Vegan Cherry Almond Cake is the perfect dessert for any occasion. Its light and fluffy texture combined with the natural sweetness of cherries and the nuttiness of almonds create a harmonious flavor profile that will leave you wanting more. The use of almond flour adds a unique twist to this classic cake, making it suitable for those who are looking for gluten-free options as well.

Not only is this cake incredibly delicious, but it’s also easy to make. With just a few simple ingredients and straightforward instructions, you’ll have a homemade vegan dessert that will impress your friends and family. Plus, the addition of fresh cherries adds a pop of vibrant color and juicy sweetness that elevates the cake to a whole new level.

Whether you’re a vegan or simply looking to explore more plant-based recipes, this Vegan Cherry Almond Cake is a must-try. Its delightful combination of flavors and textures will make it a favorite among both vegans and non-vegans alike. So go ahead and indulge in this nutty and fruity delight – you won’t be disappointed!
