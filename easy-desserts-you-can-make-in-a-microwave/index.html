Easy Desserts You Can Make in a Microwave


When it comes to satisfying your sweet tooth, nothing beats the convenience of quick and easy desserts that you can whip up in a matter of minutes. With the microwave as your trusty sidekick, you can create delicious treats without the need for extensive prep work or long baking times. From mug cakes to gooey brownies, this guide covers a variety of easy microwave desserts that are perfect for any occasion. Grab your favorite mug, and let’s dive into some delightful recipes!

1. Chocolate Mug Cake

Ingredients:

  • 4 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • 4 tablespoons granulated sugar
  • 2 tablespoons unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1/8 teaspoon baking powder
  • A pinch of salt
  • 3 tablespoons milk
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1/4 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • Optional: chocolate chips or nuts for added texture

Instructions:

  1. In a microwave-safe mug, combine the flour, sugar, cocoa powder, baking powder, and salt. Mix well to ensure there are no lumps.
  2. Add the milk, vegetable oil, and vanilla extract to the dry ingredients. Stir until the batter is smooth and well combined.
  3. If you’re feeling indulgent, fold in some chocolate chips or nuts.
  4. Microwave the mug on high for 1 minute and 30 seconds. Start checking for doneness at the 1-minute mark. The cake should rise and look set but still be moist.
  5. Allow the mug cake to cool for a minute before digging in. Enjoy it plain or top it with a scoop of ice cream!

2. Microwave Peanut Butter Cookies

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 cup peanut butter
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/4 cup brown sugar
  • 1/4 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 egg
  • A pinch of salt

Instructions:

  1. In a mixing bowl, combine the peanut butter, granulated sugar, brown sugar, vanilla extract, and egg. Mix until the ingredients are well incorporated.
  2. Scoop tablespoon-sized portions of the dough and place them on a microwave-safe plate lined with parchment paper. Leave enough space between each cookie, as they will spread slightly.
  3. Using a fork, flatten each cookie and create a crisscross pattern on top.
  4. Microwave the cookies for about 30-40 seconds. They should look slightly undercooked; they will firm up as they cool.
  5. Let the cookies cool for a few minutes before enjoying them with a glass of milk!

3. Banana Bread in a Mug

Ingredients:

  • 1 ripe banana, mashed
  • 4 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • 1 tablespoon sugar
  • 1/8 teaspoon baking powder
  • A pinch of salt
  • 1 egg
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • Optional: walnuts or chocolate chips for added flavor

Instructions:

  1. In a microwave-safe mug, mash the ripe banana until smooth.
  2. Add the flour, sugar, baking powder, salt, egg, and vegetable oil to the mug. Stir until well combined.
  3. If desired, mix in walnuts or chocolate chips for extra flavor.
  4. Microwave the mug on high for 1 minute and 30 seconds, or until the banana bread is cooked through.
  5. Allow it to cool slightly before digging in. This moist banana bread is perfect for breakfast or a quick snack!

4. Microwave S’mores

Ingredients:

  • 2 graham crackers
  • 1 chocolate bar (or chocolate chips)
  • 1 marshmallow

Instructions:

  1. On a microwave-safe plate, break one graham cracker in half and place the two halves on the plate.
  2. Place a piece of chocolate on one half of the graham cracker, and top it with a marshmallow.
  3. Microwave the plate for about 10-15 seconds, or until the marshmallow puffs up.
  4. Carefully remove the plate from the microwave (it may be hot!). Top the melted marshmallow and chocolate with the other graham cracker half and press down gently.
  5. Let it cool for a moment before enjoying this gooey treat!

5. Microwave Chocolate Lava Cake

Ingredients:

  • 1/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 2 tablespoons cocoa powder
  • 1/8 teaspoon baking powder
  • A pinch of salt
  • 3 tablespoons milk
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1/4 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 tablespoon chocolate chips

Instructions:

  1. In a microwave-safe mug, mix together the flour, sugar, cocoa powder, baking powder, and salt.
  2. Add the milk, vegetable oil, and vanilla extract to the dry ingredients, mixing until smooth.
  3. Gently fold in the chocolate chips, which will create the molten center.
  4. Microwave on high for about 40-60 seconds. The cake should rise, and the center should remain soft and gooey.
  5. Allow it to cool for a minute before enjoying. You can serve it with a scoop of vanilla ice cream for a delightful contrast!

6. Microwave Berry Crisp

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup mixed berries (fresh or frozen)
  • 2 tablespoons granulated sugar
  • 1 tablespoon cornstarch
  • 1/4 cup rolled oats
  • 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons brown sugar
  • 1 tablespoon butter, melted
  • A pinch of cinnamon

Instructions:

  1. In a microwave-safe bowl, combine the mixed berries, granulated sugar, and cornstarch. Stir gently to coat the berries and set aside.
  2. In another bowl, mix the oats, flour, brown sugar, melted butter, and cinnamon until crumbly.
  3. Layer the berry mixture at the bottom of a microwave-safe dish, then sprinkle the oat topping over the berries.
  4. Microwave on high for about 2-3 minutes, or until the berries are bubbly and the topping is golden.
  5. Let it cool slightly before serving. This crisp is a great way to enjoy the fresh flavors of berries!

7. Mug Cheesecake

Ingredients:

  • 2 oz cream cheese, softened
  • 2 tablespoons granulated sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 egg yolk
  • 2 tablespoons graham cracker crumbs
  • 1 tablespoon butter, melted

Instructions:

  1. In a mixing bowl, combine the softened cream cheese, granulated sugar, vanilla extract, and egg yolk. Beat until smooth and creamy.
  2. In a separate bowl, mix the graham cracker crumbs and melted butter until crumbly.
  3. Spoon the graham cracker mixture into the bottom of a microwave-safe mug, pressing it down slightly to form a crust.
  4. Pour the cheesecake batter over the crust and smooth the top.
  5. Microwave on high for about 40-60 seconds, or until the cheesecake is set but still slightly jiggly in the center.
  6. Allow it to cool before enjoying. This mini cheesecake is a delightful way to end your meal!

8. Microwave Rice Pudding

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 cup cooked rice
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1/4 cup sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • A pinch of salt
  • Optional: cinnamon or raisins for flavor

Instructions:

  1. In a microwave-safe bowl, combine the cooked rice, milk, sugar, vanilla extract, and salt. Stir well.
  2. Microwave the mixture on high for about 2-3 minutes, stirring halfway through, until it thickens and becomes creamy.
  3. If desired, add a sprinkle of cinnamon or a handful of raisins for added flavor.
  4. Allow it to cool slightly before serving. This comforting rice pudding is perfect for any time of day!

Conclusion

With these easy microwave dessert recipes, satisfying your cravings has never been simpler. Whether you’re looking for a quick treat after dinner or a sweet snack during the day, these delicious options are sure to hit the spot. The best part? Most of these recipes take only a few minutes to prepare, allowing you to indulge in your favorite sweets without the fuss of traditional baking. So, the next time you find yourself in need of a sweet fix, remember these delightful microwave desserts, and enjoy the sweet satisfaction they bring!
