Sizzling Steak Burrito Bowls: A Hearty Meal


When it comes to satisfying our cravings for a hearty meal, few dishes can rival the sizzling steak burrito bowl. This mouthwatering creation combines the tender juiciness of grilled steak with the vibrant flavors of Mexican-inspired ingredients, all wrapped up in a bed of fluffy rice. In this recipe, we’ll guide you through the process of crafting the perfect steak burrito bowl, complete with all the fixings.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb flank steak
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon lime juice
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon chili powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon paprika
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • 1 large onion, sliced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 red bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 can (14.5 oz) diced tomatoes, drained
  • 1 cup cooked white rice
  • 1 cup black beans, cooked
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh cilantro
  • 2 tablespoons sour cream
  • 1 tablespoon salsa
  • 1 lime, cut into wedges
  • 6-8 corn tortillas
  • Shredded cheese, for serving (optional)

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Steak Preheat a grill or grill pan to medium-high heat. In a small bowl, whisk together olive oil, lime juice, cumin, chili powder, and paprika. Brush the mixture evenly onto both sides of the flank steak. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Grill the steak for 5-7 minutes per side, or until it reaches your desired level of doneness. Let the steak rest for 5 minutes before slicing it thinly against the grain.

  2. Sauté the Vegetables In a large skillet, heat 1 tablespoon of olive oil over medium-high heat. Add the sliced onion and cook for 3-4 minutes, or until it’s translucent and starting to caramelize. Add the minced garlic and cook for an additional minute, stirring constantly to prevent burning. Add the sliced red bell pepper and cook for 2-3 minutes, or until it’s tender.

  3. Assemble the Burrito Bowls To assemble the burrito bowls, place a scoop of cooked white rice into the bottom of a large bowl. Add a portion of the sautéed vegetables, followed by a few slices of the grilled steak. Top with a spoonful of black beans, a sprinkle of chopped cilantro, and a dollop of sour cream. Drizzle with salsa and serve with a lime wedge on the side.

Tips and Variations

  • For added crunch, top your burrito bowl with toasted tortilla strips or crispy tortilla chips.
  • Substitute the flank steak with chicken or carnitas for a different protein option.
  • Add some heat to your burrito bowl by sprinkling diced jalapeños or sliced hot peppers on top.
  • Experiment with different types of cheese, such as cheddar or Monterey Jack, for a unique flavor profile.

Conclusion

The sizzling steak burrito bowl is a hearty meal that’s sure to satisfy even the most discerning palates. With its combination of tender steak, vibrant vegetables, and flavorful spices, this dish is perfect for a weeknight dinner or a special occasion. Whether you’re a fan of Mexican cuisine or just looking for a new twist on a classic meal, the sizzling steak burrito bowl is sure to become a new favorite.
