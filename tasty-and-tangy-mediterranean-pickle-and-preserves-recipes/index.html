Tasty and Tangy: Mediterranean Pickle and Preserves Recipes


Welcome to the tantalizing world of Mediterranean pickles and preserves! The Mediterranean region is renowned for its vibrant and flavorful cuisine, and its pickles and preserves are no exception. From zesty olives to tangy pickled vegetables, these recipes are a celebration of bold flavors and culinary creativity. In this blog post, we’ll explore a variety of mouthwatering recipes that will add a burst of Mediterranean flair to your dining table.

Classic Greek Feta and Olive Tapenade

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup pitted Kalamata olives
  • 1 cup crumbled feta cheese
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon fresh lemon juice
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a food processor, combine the olives, feta cheese, garlic, olive oil, lemon juice, and oregano.
  2. Pulse the mixture until it reaches your desired consistency, whether chunky or smooth.
  3. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  4. Transfer the tapenade to a serving bowl and drizzle with a touch of extra virgin olive oil.
  5. Serve with pita bread, crackers, or as a topping for grilled meats and vegetables.

Tangy Moroccan Preserved Lemons

Ingredients:

  • 4-6 large lemons
  • 1/2 cup coarse sea salt
  • Extra lemon juice, if needed
  • Sterilized glass jar with a tight-fitting lid

Instructions:

  1. Begin by washing and thoroughly drying the lemons.
  2. Cut off the stem end of each lemon, and then make a deep crosswise cut, slicing almost to the bottom but leaving the quarters attached at the base.
  3. Generously sprinkle salt inside the cuts of each lemon.
  4. Pack the lemons into the jar, pressing them down to release their juices and adding more salt as you go.
  5. If necessary, top off the jar with additional lemon juice to cover the lemons completely.
  6. Seal the jar tightly and let the lemons sit at room temperature for at least 30 days, turning the jar upside down occasionally to distribute the juices.

Spicy Italian Giardiniera

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup cauliflower florets
  • 1 cup carrot, sliced into rounds
  • 1 cup celery, sliced
  • 1 cup red bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 cup green olives
  • 1 cup pepperoncini peppers
  • 4 cloves garlic, crushed
  • 1 1/2 cups white vinegar
  • 1 1/2 cups water
  • 2 tablespoons salt
  • 1 tablespoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon red pepper flakes

Instructions:

  1. In a large bowl, combine the cauliflower, carrot, celery, bell pepper, olives, pepperoncini, and garlic.
  2. In a saucepan, bring the vinegar, water, salt, oregano, and red pepper flakes to a boil.
  3. Pour the hot brine over the vegetable mixture, ensuring that the vegetables are fully submerged.
  4. Allow the giardiniera to cool to room temperature, and then transfer it to a sterilized jar.
  5. Seal the jar tightly and refrigerate for at least 24 hours before enjoying.

Whether you’re a fan of the bold flavors of the Mediterranean or simply looking to add a touch of tanginess to your meals, these pickle and preserve recipes are sure to delight your taste buds. From the robust Greek tapenade to the piquant Moroccan preserved lemons and the fiery Italian giardiniera, each recipe offers a unique culinary experience that captures the essence of Mediterranean cuisine. So, roll up your sleeves, gather your ingredients, and embark on a flavorful journey through the Mediterranean with these delectable pickle and preserve recipes!
