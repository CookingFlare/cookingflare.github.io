Ultimate Grilled Chicken Sandwich: Perfect for a BBQ


Who doesn’t love a good barbecue? The sizzle of the grill, the mouthwatering aroma of the food, and the joy of gathering with friends and family. One classic dish that never fails to impress is the Ultimate Grilled Chicken Sandwich. Packed with flavor and easy to make, this sandwich will be the star of your next BBQ. So, grab your apron and let’s get grilling!

Ingredients

  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 4 burger buns
  • 4 slices of cheddar cheese
  • 4 lettuce leaves
  • 4 tomato slices
  • 1 red onion, thinly sliced
  • Pickles, for serving

Marinade

  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons lemon juice
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper

Sauce

  • 1/2 cup mayonnaise
  • 2 tablespoons Dijon mustard
  • 1 tablespoon honey
  • 1 tablespoon apple cider vinegar
  • 1/2 teaspoon smoked paprika
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. In a bowl, whisk together all the marinade ingredients. Place the chicken breasts in a resealable bag and pour the marinade over them. Seal the bag and massage the marinade into the chicken. Refrigerate for at least 1 hour, or overnight for maximum flavor.

  2. Preheat your grill to medium-high heat. Remove the chicken from the marinade and discard the excess marinade. Grill the chicken for about 6-8 minutes per side, or until the internal temperature reaches 165°F (75°C). Remove from the grill and let it rest for a few minutes.

  3. While the chicken is resting, prepare the sauce. In a small bowl, whisk together the mayonnaise, Dijon mustard, honey, apple cider vinegar, smoked paprika, salt, and pepper. Set aside.

  4. Toast the burger buns on the grill for a minute or until lightly golden. Remove from the grill and set aside.

  5. Assemble the sandwiches. Spread a generous amount of the sauce on the bottom half of each bun. Top with a grilled chicken breast, a slice of cheddar cheese, lettuce, tomato, red onion, and pickles. Place the top half of the bun on top.

  6. Serve the Ultimate Grilled Chicken Sandwiches immediately and watch your guests’ faces light up with delight. Enjoy!

Tips and Variations

  • For an extra kick of flavor, add some sliced jalapenos or hot sauce to the sandwich.
  • Experiment with different types of cheese, such as pepper jack or Swiss, to personalize your sandwich.
  • If you prefer a spicier sauce, add a pinch of cayenne pepper or a few dashes of hot sauce to the mayonnaise mixture.
  • Serve the sandwich with a side of coleslaw, potato salad, or crispy french fries for a complete meal.

Now that you have the recipe for the Ultimate Grilled Chicken Sandwich, it’s time to fire up the grill and create a memorable BBQ experience. Whether you’re hosting a backyard gathering or simply enjoying a delicious meal at home, this sandwich is sure to be a crowd-pleaser. So, gather your loved ones, soak up the sunshine, and indulge in the irresistible flavors of this barbecue classic. Happy grilling!
