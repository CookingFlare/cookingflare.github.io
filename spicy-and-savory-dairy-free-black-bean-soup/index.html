Spicy and Savory Dairy-Free Black Bean Soup


Looking for a hearty and flavorful soup that’s both spicy and dairy-free? Look no further! This Spicy and Savory Dairy-Free Black Bean Soup is packed with bold flavors, protein-rich black beans, and a zesty kick that will leave you craving for more. Whether you’re a vegan, lactose intolerant, or simply looking to add more plant-based meals to your diet, this soup is a winner!

Ingredients

  • 2 cups dried black beans
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 jalapeno pepper, seeded and minced
  • 2 teaspoons cumin
  • 2 teaspoons chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 4 cups vegetable broth
  • 1 can diced tomatoes
  • 1 tablespoon lime juice
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • Fresh cilantro, for garnish

Instructions

  1. Rinse the black beans thoroughly and soak them in water overnight. This will help soften the beans and reduce cooking time. Drain and set aside.

  2. In a large pot, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the diced onion, minced garlic, and jalapeno pepper. Sauté until the onions are translucent and fragrant.

  3. Add the cumin, chili powder, and paprika to the pot. Stir well to coat the onions and peppers with the spices. Cook for an additional minute to enhance the flavors.

  4. Add the soaked black beans to the pot, along with the vegetable broth and diced tomatoes. Bring the mixture to a boil, then reduce the heat to low and simmer for about 1 ½ to 2 hours, or until the beans are tender and cooked through.

  5. Using an immersion blender or a regular blender, carefully puree about half of the soup until smooth. This will give the soup a creamy texture while still maintaining some chunkiness.

  6. Stir in the lime juice and season with salt and pepper to taste. Adjust the spices according to your preference, adding more chili powder for extra heat or more cumin for a smokier flavor.

  7. Serve the Spicy and Savory Dairy-Free Black Bean Soup hot, garnished with fresh cilantro. You can also add a dollop of dairy-free sour cream or sprinkle some crushed tortilla chips for added crunch.

Why You’ll Love This Soup

  • Dairy-Free Delight: This black bean soup is completely dairy-free, making it a great option for those with lactose intolerance or a dairy-free lifestyle. The creamy texture comes from pureeing a portion of the soup, giving it a rich and comforting consistency.

  • Protein-Packed: Black beans are not only delicious but also a great source of plant-based protein. They’re loaded with fiber, vitamins, and minerals, making this soup a nutritious and satisfying meal.

  • Flavor Explosion: The combination of spices, including cumin, chili powder, and paprika, gives this soup a robust and smoky flavor. The kick from the jalapeno pepper adds just the right amount of heat, elevating the taste to a whole new level.

  • Budget-Friendly: Using dried black beans instead of canned ones not only saves you money but also allows you to control the sodium content. Plus, you can easily store any leftovers for future meals.

So, if you’re in the mood for a delicious and spicy dairy-free soup, give this Spicy and Savory Dairy-Free Black Bean Soup a try. It’s perfect for cozy nights in or for impressing guests at your next dinner party. Enjoy the bold flavors and the satisfaction of a nourishing bowl of soup that will warm your heart and soul.
