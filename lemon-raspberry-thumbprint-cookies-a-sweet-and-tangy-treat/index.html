Lemon Raspberry Thumbprint Cookies: A Sweet and Tangy Treat

3 min · 620 words

Thumbprint cookies are a classic favorite, and there’s nothing quite like the combination of zesty lemon and fruity raspberry. These Lemon Raspberry Thumbprint Cookies are the perfect treat for any occasion. The tanginess of the lemon perfectly balances the sweetness of the raspberry jam, creating a delightful flavor explosion in every bite. So, put on your apron and get ready to bake these mouthwatering cookies!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 2/3 cup granulated sugar
  • 2 large egg yolks
  • 2 teaspoons lemon zest
  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup raspberry jam

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C) and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.

  2. In a large mixing bowl, cream together the softened butter and granulated sugar until light and fluffy. This can be done with an electric mixer or by hand with a sturdy spoon.

  3. Add the egg yolks and lemon zest to the creamed butter and sugar mixture. Mix well until everything is fully combined.

  4. In a separate bowl, whisk together the all-purpose flour and salt. Gradually add the flour mixture to the wet ingredients, mixing until a soft dough forms.

  5. Take tablespoon-sized portions of the dough and roll them into balls. Place the dough balls onto the prepared baking sheet, leaving about 2 inches of space between each cookie.

  6. Using your thumb or the back of a teaspoon, make an indentation in the center of each cookie. Be careful not to press too hard and flatten the cookie.

  7. Fill each indentation with approximately 1/2 teaspoon of raspberry jam. The jam will spread during baking, so be mindful not to overfill.

  8. Bake the cookies in the preheated oven for 12-15 minutes, or until the edges are lightly golden.

  9. Once baked, remove the cookies from the oven and allow them to cool on the baking sheet for a few minutes. Then, transfer them to a wire rack to cool completely.

  10. Serve and enjoy these sweet and tangy Lemon Raspberry Thumbprint Cookies with a cup of tea or coffee. They make a wonderful addition to any dessert table or as a homemade gift for friends and family.

Tips and Variations

  • For an extra burst of citrus flavor, you can add a teaspoon of lemon juice to the dough along with the lemon zest.
  • If you prefer a different fruit filling, feel free to experiment with different flavors. Strawberry or apricot jam would work wonderfully in these cookies as well.
  • To make these cookies even more festive, dust them with powdered sugar before serving.
  • Store the cookies in an airtight container at room temperature for up to a week. They also freeze well if you want to make a larger batch and save some for later.

So, there you have it – a simple yet delightful recipe for Lemon Raspberry Thumbprint Cookies. The combination of tangy lemon and sweet raspberry jam is sure to satisfy your taste buds and leave you wanting more. Whether you’re looking for a tasty treat to enjoy with your afternoon tea or a homemade gift to share with loved ones, these cookies are the perfect choice. Happy baking!
