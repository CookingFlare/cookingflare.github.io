Cinnamon Swirl Bundt Cake: Cozy Comfort


If you’re in search of a scrumptious treat to warm your heart and your home, look no further than the ever-delightful Cinnamon Swirl Bundt Cake. This delightful dessert is nothing short of a culinary masterpiece, combining the heavenly flavors of cinnamon and sugar with the tender crumb of a fluffy sponge cake. Today, we’ll dive into a mouthwatering world of cozy comfort with this stellar recipe that will surely become a treasured crowd-pleaser.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup (225g) unsalted butter, softened
  • 2 cups (400g) granulated sugar
  • 4 large eggs, room temperature
  • 1 3/4 cups (220g) all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon pure vanilla extract
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon

For the Cinnamon Swirl:

  • 1/2 cup (100g) granulated sugar
  • 1 tablespoon ground cinnamon

For the Streusel Topping:

  • 1/4 cup (60g) unsalted butter, melted
  • 1 cup (200g) packed brown sugar
  • 1 teaspoons ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt

Instructions:

First, let’s prepare our bundt pan. Grease a 12-cup bundt pan, ensuring every nook and cranny is coated. Then, dust lightly with flour, shaking out any excess.

Next, navigate towards the realm of the dry ingredients. In a medium-sized mixing bowl, whisk together 1 3/4 cups of all-purpose flour, 1/2 teaspoon baking powder, 1/4 teaspoon baking soda, and 1/4 teaspoon salt. This step is where we ensure our cake will have the perfect structure for a splendid cinnamon swirl.

Over on the wet side of the kitchen, beat 1 cup (225g) of softened unsalted butter and 2 cups (400g) of granulated sugar together in a large mixing bowl. The batter should be smooth and creamy - it may even look like whipped marshmallow fluff!

Stir in 4 large eggs, one at a time, followed by 1 teaspoon of pure vanilla extract and 1 teaspoon of ground cinnamon. Now we’ve got the taste buds covered!

Divide the batter right down the middle and spread half onto the bottom of your prepared bundt pan. Swirl in 1/2 cup of granulated sugar and 1 tablespoon of ground cinnamon over the batter in the pan. In a mesmerizing dance, pour the remaining batter on top and smooth it out.

To make our luscious streusel topping, whisk together 1/4 cup of melted unsalted butter, 1 cup of packed brown sugar, 1 teaspoon of ground cinnamon, and 1/4 teaspoon of salt. Sprinkle this delectable mixture over our cinnamon-swirled batter.

The final step is easy - bake! Preheat your oven to 350°F (177°C) and allow your creation to spend 45-50 minutes in the oven, or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.

Let the cake cool for about 10 minutes in the pan. Then, gently coax your cinnamon swirl bundt cake from its cozy dwelling. Allow it to cool completely on a wire rack before diving in.

This cake is a marvel of mingling flavors and is best enjoyed with good friends, family, or perhaps a warm cup of tea or cozy cup of milking. The cinnamon swirl bundt cake is here to remind us that there’s no better time than now to embrace the coziness. So gather your ingredients, and enter a world of Cinnamon Swirl Bundt Cake - the epitome of cozy comfort.
