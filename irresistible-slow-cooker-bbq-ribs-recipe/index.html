Irresistible Slow Cooker BBQ Ribs Recipe


Are you craving juicy, tender, fall-off-the-bone BBQ ribs? Look no further! With this irresistible slow cooker BBQ ribs recipe, you can enjoy mouthwatering ribs without the hassle of firing up the grill. Let your slow cooker do all the work while you sit back and relax.

Ingredients

  • 2 racks of baby back ribs
  • 1 cup BBQ sauce
  • 1/4 cup brown sugar
  • 2 tablespoons paprika
  • 1 tablespoon garlic powder
  • 1 tablespoon onion powder
  • 1 tablespoon chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon cayenne pepper
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Start by preparing the ribs. Remove the membrane from the back of the ribs by sliding a butter knife under the thin, translucent membrane and lifting it away. This step will ensure that your ribs turn out tender and delicious.

  2. In a small bowl, mix together the brown sugar, paprika, garlic powder, onion powder, chili powder, cayenne pepper, salt, and pepper. This flavorful rub will give your ribs a delicious crust and enhance their smoky taste.

  3. Rub the spice mixture all over the ribs, making sure to coat both sides evenly. This will infuse the ribs with a rich blend of spices that will complement the BBQ sauce.

  4. Place the ribs in the slow cooker, standing them up on their sides with the meaty side facing the wall of the slow cooker. This way, the ribs will cook evenly and the meat will become incredibly tender.

  5. Pour the BBQ sauce over the ribs, making sure to cover them completely. You can choose any BBQ sauce you like, whether it’s sweet, tangy, or spicy. The choice is yours!

  6. Set the slow cooker to low heat and cook the ribs for 6-8 hours. Slow cooking allows the flavors to meld together and the meat to become tender and juicy. The long cooking time will result in ribs that are simply irresistible.

  7. Once the cooking time is up, carefully remove the ribs from the slow cooker and place them on a baking sheet lined with aluminum foil. The ribs will be incredibly tender, so handle them gently to avoid them falling apart.

  8. Preheat your oven to broil. Brush the ribs with some additional BBQ sauce to give them a beautiful glaze and caramelization.

  9. Broil the ribs in the oven for 5-7 minutes, or until the sauce begins to bubble and caramelize. Keep a close eye on them to prevent burning.

  10. Remove the ribs from the oven and let them rest for a few minutes before serving. This resting time allows the juices to redistribute, resulting in even more flavorful and succulent ribs.

  11. Slice the ribs between the bones and serve them with your favorite sides, such as coleslaw, cornbread, or baked beans. Get ready to indulge in the most irresistible slow cooker BBQ ribs you’ve ever tasted!

Conclusion

With this irresistible slow cooker BBQ ribs recipe, you can enjoy tender, flavorful ribs any day of the week. The slow cooker does all the work, allowing you to savor the deliciousness without the hassle. Whether you’re hosting a backyard barbecue or simply craving a hearty meal, these fall-off-the-bone ribs will satisfy your every craving. So fire up your slow cooker and get ready for a finger-licking good time!
