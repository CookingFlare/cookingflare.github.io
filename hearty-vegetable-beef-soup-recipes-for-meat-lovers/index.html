Hearty Vegetable Beef Soup Recipes for Meat Lovers


Nothing beats a warm and comforting bowl of soup, especially when it’s packed with hearty vegetables and tender beef. If you’re a meat lover looking for a delicious and satisfying soup recipe, you’re in luck! In this blog post, we’ll be sharing three mouthwatering vegetable beef soup recipes that are sure to tantalize your taste buds and keep you coming back for more.

1. Classic Beef and Vegetable Soup

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound beef stew meat, cubed
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 carrots, sliced
  • 2 celery stalks, sliced
  • 1 can diced tomatoes
  • 4 cups beef broth
  • 2 cups water
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a large pot, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the beef stew meat and brown on all sides. Remove the meat from the pot and set aside.
  2. In the same pot, add the diced onion and minced garlic. Sauté until fragrant and translucent.
  3. Add the sliced carrots and celery to the pot and cook for a few minutes until slightly softened.
  4. Return the beef stew meat to the pot and add the diced tomatoes, beef broth, water, bay leaf, dried thyme, salt, and pepper. Stir well to combine.
  5. Bring the soup to a boil, then reduce the heat to low and simmer for 1 to 2 hours, or until the beef is tender and the flavors have melded together.
  6. Remove the bay leaf and season with additional salt and pepper if needed.
  7. Serve hot and enjoy!

2. Spicy Beef and Vegetable Soup

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound beef sirloin, thinly sliced
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 red bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 green bell pepper, sliced
  • 2 carrots, sliced
  • 2 zucchinis, sliced
  • 1 can diced tomatoes with green chilies
  • 4 cups beef broth
  • 2 cups water
  • 1 tablespoon chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Heat the vegetable oil in a large pot over medium-high heat. Add the sliced beef sirloin and cook until browned. Remove the beef from the pot and set aside.
  2. In the same pot, add the diced onion and minced garlic. Sauté until fragrant and translucent.
  3. Add the sliced red and green bell peppers, carrots, and zucchinis to the pot. Cook for a few minutes until slightly softened.
  4. Return the beef to the pot and add the diced tomatoes with green chilies, beef broth, water, chili powder, cumin, salt, and pepper. Stir well to combine.
  5. Bring the soup to a boil, then reduce the heat to low and simmer for 30 to 45 minutes, or until the beef is tender and the vegetables are cooked through.
  6. Adjust the seasonings if necessary and serve hot. Sprinkle with chopped fresh cilantro for an extra burst of flavor.

3. Beef Barley Vegetable Soup

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound beef chuck roast, cut into small cubes
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 carrots, sliced
  • 2 celery stalks, sliced
  • 1 cup sliced mushrooms
  • 1 cup barley
  • 4 cups beef broth
  • 2 cups water
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • 1 teaspoon dried rosemary
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a large pot, melt the butter over medium heat. Add the beef cubes and brown on all sides. Remove the beef from the pot and set aside.
  2. In the same pot, add the diced onion and minced garlic. Sauté until fragrant and translucent.
  3. Add the sliced carrots, celery, and mushrooms to the pot. Cook for a few minutes until slightly softened.
  4. Return the beef to the pot and add the barley, beef broth, water, dried thyme, dried rosemary, salt, and pepper. Stir well to combine.
  5. Bring the soup to a boil, then reduce the heat to low and simmer for 1 to 1.5 hours, or until the beef is tender and the barley is cooked through.
  6. Adjust the seasonings if necessary and serve hot. Enjoy with a crusty bread for a complete meal.

Conclusion

These hearty vegetable beef soup recipes are perfect for meat lovers who crave a warm and comforting meal. Whether you prefer a classic beef and vegetable soup, a spicy version with a kick, or a beef barley soup with a rich and earthy flavor, there’s something for everyone to enjoy. So grab your pot, gather your ingredients, and get ready to savor the deliciousness of these hearty soups. Your taste buds will thank you!
