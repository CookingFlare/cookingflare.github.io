Vegan Oreo Cheesecake: A Cookie Lover's Dream


If you’re a cookie lover and a fan of cheesecake, then this Vegan Oreo Cheesecake is about to become your new favorite dessert. It’s rich, creamy, and bursting with the irresistible flavors of chocolate and cookies. And the best part? It’s completely plant-based!

Ingredients

For the crust:

  • 2 cups of vegan chocolate cookie crumbs (such as Oreos)
  • 1/4 cup of melted coconut oil

For the filling:

  • 2 cups of raw cashews, soaked overnight and drained
  • 1/2 cup of coconut cream
  • 1/2 cup of maple syrup
  • 1/4 cup of melted coconut oil
  • 1/4 cup of fresh lemon juice
  • 1 teaspoon of vanilla extract
  • 1 cup of crushed vegan chocolate cookies (such as Oreos)

For the topping:

  • Vegan whipped cream (optional)
  • Crushed vegan chocolate cookies (such as Oreos)

Instructions

  1. Start by preparing the crust. In a food processor, pulse the vegan chocolate cookie crumbs until they resemble fine crumbs. Add the melted coconut oil and pulse again until the mixture is well combined and sticks together when pressed between your fingers.

  2. Press the crust mixture into the bottom of a 9-inch springform pan, making sure to evenly distribute it. Use the back of a spoon or the bottom of a glass to press it down firmly. Place the pan in the refrigerator to set while you prepare the filling.

  3. In the same food processor (no need to wash it), combine the soaked and drained cashews, coconut cream, maple syrup, melted coconut oil, lemon juice, and vanilla extract. Blend until smooth and creamy, scraping down the sides of the processor as needed.

  4. Add the crushed vegan chocolate cookies to the filling mixture and pulse a few times to incorporate them. You want to have small cookie chunks throughout the filling for added texture and flavor.

  5. Pour the filling mixture over the prepared crust in the springform pan. Smooth the top with a spatula or the back of a spoon. Tap the pan gently on the counter to remove any air bubbles.

  6. Cover the pan with plastic wrap and place it in the refrigerator to set for at least 6 hours, or preferably overnight. This will allow the cheesecake to firm up and develop its flavors.

  7. Once chilled and set, remove the cheesecake from the refrigerator. Carefully remove the sides of the springform pan, leaving the cheesecake on the bottom.

  8. If desired, garnish the top of the cheesecake with vegan whipped cream and crushed vegan chocolate cookies. This will add an extra layer of indulgence and visual appeal.

  9. Serve chilled and enjoy every bite of this decadent Vegan Oreo Cheesecake. It’s the perfect dessert for any occasion, whether you’re hosting a dinner party or simply treating yourself to a well-deserved sweet treat.

Final Thoughts

This Vegan Oreo Cheesecake is a true delight for cookie lovers and dessert enthusiasts alike. It combines the classic flavors of chocolate and cookies with a creamy, dairy-free twist. Plus, it’s incredibly easy to make and doesn’t require any fancy ingredients.

So go ahead and indulge in this cookie lover’s dream. Your taste buds will thank you, and you’ll impress your friends and family with your vegan baking skills. Enjoy!
