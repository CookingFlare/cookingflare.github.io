Whip Up a Flavorful Paleo-Friendly Curry for Dinner


Curry is a versatile and delicious dish that can be enjoyed by people following various dietary preferences. If you’re on a paleo diet and craving a flavorful curry for dinner, you’re in luck! In this recipe, we’ll show you how to whip up a delicious and paleo-friendly curry that will satisfy your taste buds and keep you feeling nourished. So, let’s get started!

Ingredients

  • 1 pound of boneless chicken, cut into cubes
  • 2 tablespoons of coconut oil
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon of fresh ginger, grated
  • 1 tablespoon of curry powder
  • 1 teaspoon of turmeric powder
  • 1 teaspoon of cumin powder
  • 1 teaspoon of paprika
  • 1 can of coconut milk
  • 1 cup of chicken broth
  • 2 cups of mixed vegetables (such as bell peppers, carrots, and broccoli)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh cilantro for garnish

Instructions

  1. Heat the coconut oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Add the chopped onion and sauté until it becomes translucent.

  2. Add the minced garlic and grated ginger to the skillet. Sauté for another minute until fragrant.

  3. Add the chicken cubes to the skillet and cook until they are browned on all sides.

  4. In a small bowl, mix together the curry powder, turmeric powder, cumin powder, and paprika. Add this spice mixture to the skillet and stir well to coat the chicken evenly.

  5. Pour in the coconut milk and chicken broth. Stir to combine all the ingredients.

  6. Add the mixed vegetables to the skillet and season with salt and pepper to taste. Stir well and bring the mixture to a simmer.

  7. Reduce the heat to low, cover the skillet, and let the curry simmer for about 15-20 minutes, or until the chicken is cooked through and the vegetables are tender.

  8. Once the curry is cooked, remove it from the heat and let it sit for a few minutes to allow the flavors to meld together.

  9. Serve the paleo-friendly curry over cauliflower rice or enjoy it on its own. Garnish with fresh cilantro for an extra burst of flavor.

Tips and Variations

  • Feel free to customize this recipe by adding or substituting vegetables of your choice. You can also use different proteins like shrimp, beef, or tofu instead of chicken.
  • If you prefer a spicier curry, you can add a dash of cayenne pepper or chili flakes to the spice mixture.
  • For a creamier curry, you can add a spoonful of almond butter or coconut cream towards the end of the cooking process.

Final Thoughts

This paleo-friendly curry is not only delicious but also packed with nutrients from the chicken, vegetables, and aromatic spices. It’s a perfect dish to enjoy on a cozy evening or impress your friends and family with its vibrant flavors. So go ahead and whip up this flavorful curry for dinner tonight. Your taste buds will thank you!

Remember, cooking is all about experimentation and making the recipe your own. Feel free to adjust the spices and ingredients according to your taste preferences. Happy cooking!
