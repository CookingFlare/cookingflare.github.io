Sweet and Savory Tart Recipes for Brunch Delights


Tarts are a versatile and delicious dish that can be enjoyed throughout the day. Whether you’re hosting a brunch or simply want to indulge in a delightful treat, sweet and savory tarts are the perfect choice. In this blog post, we’ll explore some mouthwatering tart recipes that are sure to impress your guests and satisfy your cravings. So let’s dive right in!

Spinach and Feta Tart

Preparation time: 20 minutes | Cooking time: 40 minutes | Serves: 6

Ingredients:

  • 1 sheet of puff pastry
  • 2 cups of fresh spinach, chopped
  • 1 cup of crumbled feta cheese
  • 1/4 cup of diced red onion
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 3 eggs
  • 1/2 cup of milk
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C). Roll out the puff pastry and line a tart pan with it.
  2. In a pan, sauté the spinach, red onion, and garlic until the spinach is wilted. Remove from heat and set aside.
  3. In a bowl, whisk together the eggs, milk, salt, and pepper. Add the sautéed spinach mixture and feta cheese, and mix well.
  4. Pour the mixture into the tart pan lined with puff pastry.
  5. Bake for 35-40 minutes, or until the tart is golden brown and set in the center.
  6. Allow the tart to cool for a few minutes before serving. Slice and enjoy!

Blueberry Lemon Tart

Preparation time: 15 minutes | Cooking time: 30 minutes | Serves: 8

Ingredients:

  • 1 pre-baked tart shell
  • 2 cups of fresh blueberries
  • Zest of 1 lemon
  • 1/2 cup of granulated sugar
  • 1/4 cup of cornstarch
  • 1/4 teaspoon of salt
  • 1 cup of water

Instructions:

  1. In a saucepan, combine the blueberries, lemon zest, sugar, cornstarch, salt, and water. Stir well to combine.
  2. Cook the mixture over medium heat, stirring constantly until it thickens and comes to a boil.
  3. Reduce the heat and simmer for 2 minutes, stirring continuously.
  4. Remove from heat and let the mixture cool slightly.
  5. Pour the blueberry filling into the pre-baked tart shell and spread it evenly.
  6. Place the tart in the refrigerator for at least 2 hours to set.
  7. Serve chilled and garnish with additional blueberries and lemon zest, if desired.

Caramelized Onion and Goat Cheese Tart

Preparation time: 15 minutes | Cooking time: 1 hour | Serves: 4

Ingredients:

  • 1 sheet of shortcrust pastry
  • 3 large onions, thinly sliced
  • 2 tablespoons of olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon of balsamic vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon of brown sugar
  • 4 ounces of goat cheese, crumbled
  • Fresh thyme leaves for garnish
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C). Roll out the shortcrust pastry and line a tart pan with it.
  2. In a large pan, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the sliced onions and cook until they are soft and caramelized, about 30 minutes.
  3. Stir in the balsamic vinegar and brown sugar, and continue cooking for another 5 minutes.
  4. Spread the caramelized onion mixture evenly onto the pastry-lined tart pan.
  5. Crumble the goat cheese over the top of the onions. Season with salt and pepper.
  6. Bake for 25-30 minutes, or until the tart is golden brown.
  7. Garnish with fresh thyme leaves before serving.

Conclusion

These sweet and savory tart recipes are perfect for brunch delights or any occasion that calls for a delicious and impressive dish. Whether you prefer the tangy combination of spinach and feta, the fruity goodness of blueberries and lemon, or the rich flavors of caramelized onion and goat cheese, there’s a tart for every palate. So go ahead, get creative in the kitchen, and enjoy these delightful treats with your loved ones. Bon appétit!
