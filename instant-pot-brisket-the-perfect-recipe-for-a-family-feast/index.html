Instant Pot Brisket: The Perfect Recipe for a Family Feast


When it comes to family dinners, few meals can match the comfort and richness of a tender, flavorful brisket. Whether you’re preparing a holiday feast or just looking to impress your loved ones, brisket is a dish that brings everyone together around the table. But cooking a brisket traditionally can take hours—leaving you with limited time to enjoy the day with your family. That’s where the Instant Pot comes in. With its fast cooking capabilities, the Instant Pot allows you to make a mouthwatering brisket in a fraction of the time it would take using conventional methods. This Instant Pot brisket recipe will ensure you get a tender, juicy brisket every time, packed with bold flavors that your family will rave about.

Why Use an Instant Pot for Brisket?

Before diving into the recipe, let’s take a moment to appreciate the Instant Pot. This multi-functional appliance has revolutionized home cooking by drastically reducing cooking times while retaining flavor and texture. When making brisket, the Instant Pot works wonders by tenderizing the meat quickly while sealing in all the savory juices. The pressure cooking method ensures that every bite is tender and juicy, with just the right balance of flavors. Using an Instant Pot also minimizes the need for constant attention, allowing you to go about other tasks while your brisket cooks to perfection.

Another great benefit of using an Instant Pot is that it allows you to infuse your brisket with rich, deep flavors in a short amount of time. Traditional methods of slow cooking brisket can sometimes lead to overcooking the outside of the meat, but with the Instant Pot, you can achieve the perfect tenderness while locking in the smoky and savory notes of your rub and braising liquids.

Now that we’ve covered the benefits, let’s get into the recipe itself.

Ingredients for Instant Pot Brisket

To create the ultimate Instant Pot brisket, you’ll need a few key ingredients. The beauty of this recipe is that it requires minimal effort but yields incredible results. Here’s what you’ll need:

For the Brisket:

  • 3 to 4 lbs of beef brisket (flat cut or point cut, your choice)
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 large onion, sliced
  • 4 garlic cloves, minced
  • 2 cups beef broth (or a mixture of beef broth and red wine for extra richness)
  • 1 tablespoon apple cider vinegar (adds a touch of acidity to balance the richness)
  • 1 tablespoon Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce (adds umami flavor)
  • 2 teaspoons ground paprika
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon onion powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt (adjust to taste)
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried thyme (optional, but recommended for depth of flavor)
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried rosemary (optional, for a fragrant touch)
  • 1 tablespoon brown sugar (for a hint of sweetness)
  • 1 tablespoon tomato paste (for a bit of tang and richness)
  • 1 teaspoon liquid smoke (optional, but gives an authentic BBQ flavor)

For the Finishing Sauce:

  • 1 tablespoon cornstarch (to thicken the sauce)
  • 2 tablespoons cold water

For Garnishing:

  • Fresh parsley (chopped, for garnish)
  • Chopped green onions (optional)

Step-by-Step Instructions for Making Instant Pot Brisket

Step 1: Prepare the Brisket

Start by trimming the excess fat from your brisket. While some fat is necessary for flavor and moisture, you don’t want too much as it may prevent the rub from properly sticking to the meat. Pat the brisket dry with paper towels to ensure a good sear.

Step 2: Season the Brisket

In a small bowl, combine the paprika, cumin, garlic powder, onion powder, salt, black pepper, thyme, rosemary, and brown sugar. Rub this spice mixture all over the brisket, making sure to coat every side. This dry rub will help develop a deep, flavorful crust on the meat as it cooks.

Step 3: Sear the Brisket

Set your Instant Pot to the “Sauté” function and heat the olive oil. Once the oil is hot, carefully place the brisket in the pot and sear it on all sides for 3 to 4 minutes per side until a rich brown crust forms. This step is crucial for locking in the flavors and developing that savory, caramelized exterior.

Once the brisket is seared, remove it from the pot and set it aside.

Step 4: Sauté the Aromatics

With the Instant Pot still on the “Sauté” function, add the sliced onion and minced garlic to the pot. Sauté for 2 to 3 minutes, or until the onion becomes translucent and the garlic is fragrant. This will help release the natural sweetness of the onion and infuse the cooking liquid with extra flavor.

Step 5: Deglaze the Pot

Add the beef broth (or beef broth and red wine) to the pot to deglaze it, scraping up any browned bits left from searing the brisket. These bits, known as “fond,” contain tons of flavor and are essential for building a rich sauce.

Step 6: Add the Remaining Ingredients

Once the pot is deglazed, add the apple cider vinegar, Worcestershire sauce, soy sauce, tomato paste, and liquid smoke (if using). Stir everything together to combine.

Step 7: Return the Brisket to the Pot

Place the seared brisket back into the Instant Pot, fat side up. This will allow the fat to melt and infuse the meat with flavor as it cooks. Pour any leftover rub or juices over the brisket.

Step 8: Pressure Cook the Brisket

Secure the Instant Pot lid and set the valve to “Sealing.” Select the “Manual” or “Pressure Cook” function and cook on high pressure for 90 minutes. This should give you a perfectly tender brisket that falls apart with a fork. For an even more tender result, you can cook it for up to 120 minutes, but be mindful that it may fall apart a bit more.

Step 9: Natural Release

Once the cooking time is up, let the pressure release naturally for 10 to 15 minutes. After the natural release, carefully turn the valve to “Venting” to release any remaining pressure. Open the lid and check the brisket—it should be incredibly tender and juicy.

Step 10: Make the Finishing Sauce

Remove the brisket from the Instant Pot and set it aside to rest for 10 minutes. While the brisket is resting, you can make the finishing sauce. To thicken the cooking liquid, mix the cornstarch with cold water in a small bowl to create a slurry. Turn the Instant Pot back to “Sauté” and add the slurry to the pot. Stir the sauce for a few minutes until it thickens into a rich, glossy gravy.

Step 11: Slice and Serve

After resting, slice the brisket against the grain to ensure tender, melt-in-your-mouth slices. Pour some of the thickened sauce over the brisket, and garnish with chopped parsley and green onions for a fresh touch.

Serve your Instant Pot brisket with mashed potatoes, roasted vegetables, or your favorite side dishes. The rich flavors and tenderness of the brisket will surely make it the star of your family feast.

Tips for Perfect Instant Pot Brisket

  • Choosing the Right Brisket Cut: Brisket comes in two main cuts: the flat cut and the point cut. The flat cut is leaner, while the point cut has more marbling, which makes it more flavorful and tender. Both cuts work well in the Instant Pot, but the point cut will give you a juicier result.

  • Allow the Brisket to Rest: After cooking, it’s important to let the brisket rest for at least 10 minutes before slicing. This allows the juices to redistribute throughout the meat, ensuring each bite is flavorful and moist.

  • Use the Leftover Liquid: Don’t discard the leftover cooking liquid! You can serve it as a gravy or store it to use in soups, stews, or sauces.

  • Make It Ahead: The beauty of brisket is that it tastes even better the next day. You can make it ahead of time and store it in the fridge for up to 3 days. Just reheat it gently when you’re ready to serve.

Conclusion

This Instant Pot brisket recipe is the perfect way to create a tender, flavorful family feast without the long hours of traditional cooking. With minimal effort, you can have a succulent brisket ready in just a couple of hours, packed with bold flavors that everyone will love. Whether you’re hosting a holiday gathering, celebrating a special occasion, or simply enjoying a cozy family dinner, this brisket recipe will become a go-to favorite. Try it out, and prepare to be amazed at how easy it is to create a memorable meal in your Instant Pot.
