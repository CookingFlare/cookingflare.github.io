5 Cheap and Flavorful Homemade Salsa Verde Recipes


Looking for a way to add some zing to your meals without breaking the bank? Look no further than homemade salsa verde! This tangy and flavorful green sauce is not only versatile but also incredibly easy to make. With just a few simple ingredients, you can whip up a batch of salsa verde that will elevate your dishes to a whole new level. In this blog post, we’ll share five budget-friendly and delicious homemade salsa verde recipes that are sure to impress. Let’s get started!

1. Classic Salsa Verde

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound tomatillos, husked and rinsed
  • 2 cloves garlic, peeled
  • 1 small onion, chopped
  • 1 jalapeno pepper, seeds removed
  • 1/4 cup fresh cilantro, chopped
  • 2 tablespoons lime juice
  • Salt, to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a saucepan, bring water to a boil. Add the tomatillos and cook for about 5 minutes, until they become soft.
  2. Drain the tomatillos and transfer them to a blender or food processor.
  3. Add the garlic, onion, jalapeno pepper, cilantro, lime juice, and a pinch of salt to the blender.
  4. Blend until the mixture reaches a smooth consistency.
  5. Taste and adjust the seasoning with more salt or lime juice if needed.
  6. Allow the salsa verde to cool before serving. It can be stored in an airtight container in the refrigerator for up to a week.

2. Roasted Salsa Verde

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound tomatillos, husked and rinsed
  • 2 cloves garlic, peeled
  • 1 small onion, quartered
  • 1 jalapeno pepper, seeds removed
  • 1/4 cup fresh cilantro, chopped
  • 2 tablespoons lime juice
  • Salt, to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C).
  2. Place the tomatillos, garlic, onion, and jalapeno pepper on a baking sheet.
  3. Roast the vegetables in the oven for about 15 minutes, or until they become slightly charred.
  4. Remove the baking sheet from the oven and let the vegetables cool.
  5. Transfer the roasted vegetables to a blender or food processor.
  6. Add the cilantro, lime juice, and a pinch of salt to the blender.
  7. Blend until the mixture reaches a smooth consistency.
  8. Taste and adjust the seasoning with more salt or lime juice if needed.
  9. Allow the salsa verde to cool before serving. It can be stored in an airtight container in the refrigerator for up to a week.

3. Avocado Salsa Verde

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound tomatillos, husked and rinsed

Instructions:

  2. Add the cilantro, lime juice, and a pinch of salt to the blender.

  4. Taste and adjust the seasoning with more salt or lime juice if needed.
  5. Allow the avocado salsa verde to cool before serving. It can be stored in an airtight container in the refrigerator for up to a week.

4. Mango Salsa Verde

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound tomatillos, husked and rinsed
  • 1 ripe mango, peeled and chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, peeled
  • 1 small onion, chopped
  • 1 jalapeno pepper, seeds removed
  • 1/4 cup fresh cilantro, chopped
  • 2 tablespoons lime juice
  • Salt, to taste

5. Grilled Corn Salsa Verde

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound tomatillos, husked and rinsed
  • 2 ears of corn, husked
  • 2 cloves garlic, peeled
  • 1 small onion, chopped
  • 1 jalapeno pepper, seeds removed
  • 1/4 cup fresh cilantro, chopped
  • 2 tablespoons lime juice
  • Salt, to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your grill to medium-high heat.
  2. Place the tomatillos, corn, garlic, onion, and jalapeno pepper on the grill.
  3. Grill the vegetables for about 10 minutes, or until they become slightly charred.
  4. Remove the vegetables from the grill and let them cool.
  5. Cut the kernels off the grilled corn and transfer them to a blender or food processor.
  6. Add the grilled tomatillos, garlic, onion, jalapeno pepper, cilantro, lime juice, and a pinch of salt to the blender.
  7. Blend until the mixture reaches a smooth consistency.
  8. Taste and adjust the seasoning with more salt or lime juice if needed.
  9. Allow the grilled corn salsa verde to cool before serving. It can be stored in an airtight container in the refrigerator for up to a week.

There you have it - five cheap and flavorful homemade salsa verde recipes that are sure to impress your taste buds. Experiment with these recipes, and feel free to adjust the ingredients to suit your preferences. Whether you’re adding a dollop to your tacos, serving it with grilled meats, or using it as a zesty dip, salsa verde is the perfect way to add a burst of flavor to your meals without breaking the bank. Enjoy!
