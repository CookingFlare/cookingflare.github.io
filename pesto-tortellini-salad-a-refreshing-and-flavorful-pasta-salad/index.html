Pesto Tortellini Salad: A Refreshing and Flavorful Pasta Salad


When it comes to summer meals, nothing beats a refreshing and flavorful pasta salad. And if you’re looking for a dish that’s quick, easy, and bursting with delicious flavors, then look no further than the Pesto Tortellini Salad. This delightful dish combines cheesy tortellini, vibrant pesto, and an assortment of fresh vegetables to create a mouthwatering salad that’s perfect for picnics, potlucks, or a light weeknight dinner.

Ingredients

To make this tantalizing Pesto Tortellini Salad, you’ll need the following ingredients:

  • 1 pound cheese tortellini
  • 1/2 cup classic basil pesto
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1/2 cup black olives, sliced
  • 1/4 cup red onion, finely chopped
  • 1/2 cup fresh mozzarella balls, halved
  • 1/4 cup fresh basil, chopped
  • 1/4 cup pine nuts, toasted
  • Salt and black pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Cook the Tortellini: In a large pot of salted boiling water, cook the cheese tortellini according to the package instructions. Once cooked, drain the tortellini and rinse it under cold water to stop the cooking process.

  2. Prepare the Pesto: In a small bowl, mix the classic basil pesto with a splash of olive oil to loosen it up and enhance the flavors.

  3. Combine the Ingredients: In a large mixing bowl, combine the cooked tortellini, cherry tomatoes, black olives, red onion, fresh mozzarella balls, and chopped fresh basil. Gently toss the ingredients together until evenly distributed.

  4. Add the Pesto: Pour the prepared pesto over the tortellini and vegetable mixture. Using a spatula, gently fold the pesto into the salad until everything is well coated.

  5. Season and Garnish: Season the Pesto Tortellini Salad with salt and black pepper to taste. Sprinkle the toasted pine nuts over the top for a delightful crunch and nutty flavor.

  6. Chill and Serve: Cover the salad with plastic wrap and refrigerate for at least 30 minutes to allow the flavors to meld together. When ready to serve, give the salad a final toss and garnish with additional fresh basil.

Tips and Variations

  • For a protein boost, consider adding grilled chicken or shrimp to the salad.
  • Feel free to swap the classic basil pesto for sun-dried tomato pesto or arugula pesto for a unique twist.
  • This salad can be made ahead of time and stored in the refrigerator for up to two days.

Conclusion

With its vibrant colors, bold flavors, and simple preparation, the Pesto Tortellini Salad is a must-try for any pasta salad enthusiast. Whether you’re hosting a summer gathering or simply craving a light and satisfying meal, this salad is sure to impress. So, grab your ingredients, mix up a batch of this delightful salad, and savor every forkful of this refreshing and flavorful dish. Cheers to good food and great company!
