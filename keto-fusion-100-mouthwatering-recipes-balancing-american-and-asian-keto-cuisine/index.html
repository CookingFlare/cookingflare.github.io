Keto Fusion: 100 Mouthwatering Recipes Balancing American and Asian Keto Cuisine


Ah, Keto Fusion! A culinary adventure where two globally adored cuisines come together like an exquisite dance. Imagine succulent American BBQ and spicy Asian dishes sharing space on the same plate, all while ensuring each dish adheres to the Keto protocol. Are you ready for a gastronomic journey that’s both refreshing and familiar? Let’s dive into 100 mouthwatering recipes, balancing American comfort food with the zest and heat of Asian cuisine.

1. Spicy Sriracha Honey Chicken Skewers

If you’re craving the sweet and fiery, these Sriracha Honey Chicken Skewers are for you.

Ingredients:

  • 1 ½ lbs chicken thighs, cut into chunks
  • 8 wooden skewers, soaked in water
  • 1/4 cup sriracha
  • 1/4 cup honey
  • Salt and pepper, to taste

Instructions:

  1. Thread the marinated chicken onto the soaked skewers.
  2. Grill over medium heat for 15-20 minutes, rotating skewers for even cooking.
  3. Mix the sriracha and honey together for the glaze. Brush it onto the chicken.
  4. Serve immediately with a side of steamed broccoli.

2. Keto Cauliflower Mac and Cheese

Love Mac and Cheese? This fusion dish will win your heart.

Ingredients:

  • 1 head of cauliflower, cut into florets
  • 4 tablespoons of ghee or butter
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1 heaping cup of shredded cheddar cheese
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Steam the cauliflower florets until they’re easily pierced with a fork.
  2. Puree the steamed cauliflower in a food processor to create “cauliflower rice.”
  3. Melt ghee in a saucepan over medium heat. Add cauliflower “rice.” Cook for a minute.
  4. Stir in heavy cream, followed by the cheddar cheese. Cook until the cheese melts.
  5. Season with salt and pepper.

Bon Appétit!

3. Kung Pao Cauliflower Bites

Faux wings that are crunchy, saucy, and drizzled with sesame, these cauliflower bites will transport you to an Asian bistro.

Ingredients:

  • 1lb cauliflower florets
  • 2 eggs, beaten
  • 1 cup almond flour
  • Salt, pepper, garlic powder to taste
  • 2 tablespoons peanut oil, plus 1/4 cup
  • 1 cup keto broccoli slaw
  • 1/3 cup peans, no sugar added
  • 1 tablespoon rice vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 1 teaspoon chili paste
  • 2 green onions, sliced
  • Chopped peanuts, for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Pat dry cauliflower and toss with egg, then coat with almond flour, garlic powder, salt, and pepper.
  2. Heat 2 tablespoons of oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat. Cook until golden, turned occasionally, for about 10 minutes.
  3. Add remaining oil to the skillet. Sauté broccoli slaw for about 3 minutes.
  4. In a small bowl, whisk together peans, rice vinegar, soy sauce, and chili paste. Pour over cauliflower.
  5. Toss, then garnish with green onions and peanuts. Serve hot!

4. Sushi Stuffed Avocado Boats

This elegant and playful dish makes a statement on any Keto table.

Ingredients:

  • 4 ripe but firm avocados
  • 1 pound sushi-grade tuna, thinly sliced
  • ½ cup keto version of mayonnaise
  • 1 tablespoon mustard
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • ½ teaspoon sesame oil
  • 2 tablespoons sesame seeds
  • Microgreens, for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Slice avocados in half and remove the pits. Scoop out some of the flesh to make room for the tuna.
  2. In a bowl, mix mayo, mustard, soy sauce, and sesame oil.
  3. Stir in the tuna.
  4. Spoon the tuna mixture into the avocado boats and sprinkle sesame seeds on top.
  5. Serve immediately with microgreens!

5. Bacon-Wrapped Jalapeño Poppers with Sweet Chili Dipping Sauce

A cross-cultural love affair like no other…

Ingredients:

  • 24 jalapeño peppers, halved and seeded
  • 24 slices of bacon
  • 1 cup cream cheese, softened
  • 1/2 cup cheddar cheese, shredded
  • 1/4 cup crumbled blue cheese
  • 1/4 cup almond flour
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Sweet Chili Dipping Sauce:

  • 1/2 cup peans, no sugar added
  • 1/4 cup rice vinegar
  • 1/4 cup lime juice
  • 1 tablespoon garlic powder
  • 1 tablespoon ginger powder
  • 1 teaspoon red pepper flakes (or more, if you like heat!)

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 400°F.
  2. Mix cream cheese, cheddar, and blue cheese in a bowl. Stuff each jalapeño half with the mixture.
  3. Wrap each jalapeño half with a slice of bacon. Secure with a toothpick.
  4. Coat with almond flour and bake for 25-30 minutes until the bacon is crispy.
  5. For the sauce, mix all ingredients in a bowl.
  6. Serve these delicious poppers with the sweet chili sauce on the side. Enjoy!
