Chocolate Raspberry Cupcakes: A Perfect Pair


There’s something undeniably delightful about the combination of chocolate and raspberry. The rich, indulgent flavor of chocolate paired with the bright, tangy sweetness of raspberries creates a perfect harmony that tantalizes the taste buds. If you’re a fan of this classic flavor duo, then you’re in for a treat because today, we’re diving into the world of Chocolate Raspberry Cupcakes - a decadent dessert that’s sure to impress.

Ingredients

For the Cupcakes:

  • 1 3/4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 cups granulated sugar
  • 3/4 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1 1/2 tsp baking powder
  • 1 1/2 tsp baking soda
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 cup whole milk
  • 1/2 cup vegetable oil
  • 2 tsp pure vanilla extract
  • 1 cup boiling water

For the Raspberry Filling:

  • 1 1/2 cups fresh raspberries
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 2 tsp cornstarch
  • 1/4 cup water

For the Chocolate Frosting:

  • 1 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 3 1/2 cups powdered sugar
  • 1/2 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • 1 tsp pure vanilla extract
  • Pinch of salt

Instructions

  1. Preheat: Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C) and line a muffin tin with cupcake liners.

  2. Prepare the Cupcake Batter: In a large bowl, combine the flour, sugar, cocoa powder, baking powder, baking soda, and salt. Add the eggs, milk, oil, and vanilla extract, and beat on medium speed for 2 minutes. Stir in the boiling water until the batter is well combined.

  3. Bake the Cupcakes: Fill each cupcake liner two-thirds full with batter. Bake for 20-22 minutes or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean. Allow the cupcakes to cool completely.

  4. Make the Raspberry Filling: In a small saucepan, combine the raspberries, sugar, cornstarch, and water. Cook over medium heat, stirring constantly until the mixture thickens. Remove from heat and let it cool.

  5. Core the Cupcakes: Using a small knife or cupcake corer, remove the center of each cupcake to create a well for the raspberry filling.

  6. Fill the Cupcakes: Spoon the raspberry filling into the center of each cupcake.

  7. Prepare the Chocolate Frosting: In a large bowl, beat the butter until creamy. Gradually add the powdered sugar and cocoa powder, beating until well combined. Add the heavy cream, vanilla extract, and salt, and beat until smooth and fluffy.

  8. Frost the Cupcakes: Pipe or spread the chocolate frosting onto the cupcakes, covering the raspberry filling.

  9. Enjoy: Indulge in the delightful combination of chocolate and raspberry with every bite of these heavenly cupcakes.

Tips and Variations

  • For an extra burst of raspberry flavor, consider adding a tablespoon of raspberry liqueur to the filling.
  • Garnish the cupcakes with fresh raspberries or a sprinkle of chocolate shavings for a beautiful presentation.
  • If you prefer a lighter frosting, consider using a whipped chocolate ganache instead.

These Chocolate Raspberry Cupcakes are the perfect indulgence for any occasion, from birthday celebrations to afternoon tea parties. The rich, moist chocolate cake pairs beautifully with the vibrant raspberry filling and decadent chocolate frosting, creating a symphony of flavors that will leave everyone craving for more. So, roll up your sleeves, gather your ingredients, and treat yourself to the delightful experience of baking and savoring these irresistible cupcakes.
