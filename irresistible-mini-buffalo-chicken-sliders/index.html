Irresistible Mini Buffalo Chicken Sliders


Are you a fan of spicy, tangy flavors? Do you love finger foods that pack a punch? If so, then you’re going to fall head over heels for these Irresistible Mini Buffalo Chicken Sliders! These bite-sized sliders are the perfect blend of tender chicken, zesty buffalo sauce, and creamy toppings, all nestled between soft slider buns. They’re easy to make, crowd-pleasing, and guaranteed to leave your taste buds craving more. So, let’s dive right into the recipe!

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1/2 cup buffalo sauce
  • 1/4 cup mayonnaise
  • 1/4 cup sour cream
  • 1 tablespoon ranch seasoning
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon onion powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • 12 mini slider buns
  • Lettuce leaves, for garnish
  • Sliced tomatoes, for garnish
  • Sliced red onions, for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C). Line a baking sheet with parchment paper and set aside.

  2. In a medium mixing bowl, combine the buffalo sauce, mayonnaise, sour cream, ranch seasoning, garlic powder, onion powder, salt, and black pepper. Stir well until all the ingredients are fully combined, creating a creamy buffalo sauce mixture.

  3. Trim any excess fat from the chicken breasts and slice them into small, bite-sized pieces. Place the chicken pieces into the bowl with the buffalo sauce mixture. Toss to coat the chicken evenly with the sauce.

  4. Heat a large skillet over medium-high heat. Once hot, add the chicken pieces to the skillet, ensuring they are in a single layer. Cook for 2-3 minutes on each side, or until the chicken is cooked through and slightly charred.

  5. Transfer the cooked chicken pieces to the prepared baking sheet, arranging them in a single layer. Place the baking sheet in the preheated oven and bake for an additional 5-7 minutes, or until the chicken is fully cooked and the sauce is bubbling.

  6. While the chicken is baking, slice the mini slider buns in half horizontally. Toast the buns if desired for added crunch and flavor.

  7. Once the chicken is done baking, remove it from the oven and allow it to cool slightly. Using two forks, shred the chicken into smaller pieces.

  8. To assemble the sliders, spread a dollop of the creamy buffalo sauce mixture onto the bottom half of each slider bun. Top with a generous portion of the shredded buffalo chicken. Garnish with lettuce leaves, sliced tomatoes, and sliced red onions, or any other toppings you desire.

  9. Place the top half of each slider bun onto the assembled sliders, pressing down slightly to secure all the ingredients together.

  10. Serve these Irresistible Mini Buffalo Chicken Sliders immediately and watch as your friends and family devour them with delight. They’re perfect for game nights, parties, or even as a delicious weeknight dinner option.

Final Thoughts:

These Irresistible Mini Buffalo Chicken Sliders are a flavor-packed sensation that will leave your taste buds begging for more. The tender chicken, zesty buffalo sauce, and creamy toppings create a harmony of flavors that’s incredibly satisfying. Whether you’re hosting a get-together or simply looking for a delicious meal to enjoy, these sliders are sure to impress. So, get your ingredients ready, follow the simple steps, and indulge in these mouthwatering sliders that will leave you craving for another bite. Enjoy!
