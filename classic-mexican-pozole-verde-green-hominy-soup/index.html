Classic Mexican Pozole Verde: Green Hominy Soup


Pozole Verde, also known as Green Hominy Soup, is a classic Mexican dish that is rich in flavors and traditions. This vibrant and savory soup is perfect for any occasion, whether you’re hosting a dinner party or simply craving a comforting bowl of goodness. In this recipe, we’ll guide you through the steps to create a delicious Pozole Verde that will transport your taste buds to Mexico.

Ingredients

  • 2 pounds of pork shoulder, cut into chunks
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 4 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons of vegetable oil
  • 1 pound of fresh tomatillos, husked and rinsed
  • 2 jalapeno peppers, stemmed and seeded
  • 1 bunch of fresh cilantro, stems removed
  • 1 can (29 ounces) of hominy, drained and rinsed
  • 6 cups of chicken broth
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. In a large pot, heat the vegetable oil over medium heat. Add the pork shoulder chunks and cook until browned on all sides. Remove the pork from the pot and set it aside.

  2. In the same pot, add the chopped onion and minced garlic. Sauté until the onion becomes translucent and the garlic becomes fragrant.

  3. While the onion and garlic are cooking, prepare the tomatillos by removing the husks and rinsing them thoroughly. Cut the tomatillos into quarters.

  4. Add the tomatillos, jalapeno peppers, and cilantro to a blender or food processor. Blend until you have a smooth and vibrant green sauce.

  5. Return the browned pork shoulder to the pot with the onion and garlic. Pour in the green sauce from the blender and stir to coat the pork evenly.

  6. Add the drained and rinsed hominy to the pot, followed by the chicken broth. Stir well to combine all the ingredients.

  7. Bring the soup to a boil, then reduce the heat to low. Cover the pot and let the soup simmer for at least 2 hours, or until the pork is tender and the flavors have melded together.

  8. Season the Pozole Verde with salt and pepper to taste. Adjust the seasoning as needed.

  9. Serve the Pozole Verde hot in bowls, garnished with fresh cilantro leaves and sliced radishes. Accompany the soup with warm tortillas or crusty bread for a complete and satisfying meal.

Tips

  • For a spicier version of Pozole Verde, leave the seeds and ribs in the jalapeno peppers or add an extra pepper.
  • If you prefer a thicker soup, you can blend a portion of the hominy along with the green sauce before adding it to the pot.
  • Pozole Verde is traditionally served with a variety of toppings, such as shredded cabbage, diced avocado, and lime wedges. Feel free to customize your soup with your favorite garnishes.

Conclusion

Pozole Verde is a beloved Mexican dish that showcases the vibrant flavors and culinary heritage of the country. This Green Hominy Soup is a delicious and comforting option for those seeking a taste of Mexico in their own kitchen. With its tender pork, tangy tomatillos, and hearty hominy, Pozole Verde is a true crowd-pleaser. So gather your ingredients, follow the recipe, and immerse yourself in the rich flavors of this classic Mexican dish. ¡Buen provecho!
