Tex-Mex Black Bean and Corn Salad Recipe


Are you in the mood for a refreshing and flavorful salad that captures the essence of Tex-Mex cuisine? Look no further than this delicious Tex-Mex Black Bean and Corn Salad recipe. Packed with protein-rich black beans, sweet corn, and a medley of colorful vegetables, this salad is bursting with vibrant flavors and textures that will leave your taste buds craving for more.

Ingredients

  • 1 can (15 ounces) black beans, rinsed and drained
  • 1 cup frozen corn, thawed
  • 1 red bell pepper, diced
  • 1 green bell pepper, diced
  • 1 small red onion, finely chopped
  • 1 jalapeño pepper, seeded and finely chopped
  • 1/4 cup fresh cilantro, chopped
  • Juice of 2 limes
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. In a large mixing bowl, combine the black beans, corn, red bell pepper, green bell pepper, red onion, jalapeño pepper, and cilantro. Mix well to combine.

  2. In a separate small bowl, whisk together the lime juice, olive oil, ground cumin, salt, and pepper. Pour the dressing over the salad mixture and toss gently to coat all the ingredients evenly.

  3. Cover the salad and refrigerate for at least 30 minutes to allow the flavors to meld together. This salad can be made ahead of time and stored in the refrigerator for up to 24 hours.

  4. Before serving, give the salad a quick stir to redistribute the dressing. Taste and adjust the seasonings if needed. You can add more lime juice, salt, or pepper according to your preference.

  5. Serve the Tex-Mex Black Bean and Corn Salad chilled as a side dish or as a main course. It pairs perfectly with grilled chicken, fish, or even as a filling for tacos or burritos.

Why You’ll Love This Tex-Mex Salad

This Tex-Mex Black Bean and Corn Salad is not only delicious but also incredibly nutritious. It’s loaded with fiber, plant-based protein, and a variety of vitamins and minerals. The combination of black beans and corn provides a satisfying and filling meal that will keep you energized throughout the day.

The colorful assortment of bell peppers adds a delightful crunch and a pop of vibrant flavor to the salad. The red onion and jalapeño pepper contribute a mild heat and a hint of spiciness, while the fresh cilantro adds a refreshing herbaceous note.

The zesty lime dressing, with its tangy and citrusy flavor, brings all the ingredients together and enhances the overall taste of the salad. The subtle smokiness from the ground cumin adds a touch of warmth and depth to the dish.

Not only is this Tex-Mex Black Bean and Corn Salad a crowd-pleaser, but it’s also incredibly versatile. You can customize it by adding your favorite ingredients such as diced avocado, cherry tomatoes, or even a squeeze of hot sauce for an extra kick.

So, whether you’re hosting a summer barbecue, attending a potluck, or simply looking for a quick and healthy meal, this Tex-Mex Black Bean and Corn Salad is a must-try recipe. It’s easy to make, bursting with flavors, and guaranteed to impress both your family and friends.

Try it out today and savor the bold and refreshing taste of Tex-Mex cuisine in every bite!
