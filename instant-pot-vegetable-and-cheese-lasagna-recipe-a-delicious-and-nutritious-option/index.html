Instant Pot Vegetable and Cheese Lasagna Recipe: A Delicious and Nutritious Option


Are you looking for a hearty and flavorful vegetarian dish to satisfy your lasagna cravings? Look no further than this Instant Pot Vegetable and Cheese Lasagna recipe! Packed with wholesome vegetables, gooey cheese, and perfectly cooked lasagna noodles, this dish is a delightful twist on the classic lasagna, all made conveniently in your Instant Pot. Whether you’re a seasoned chef or a cooking novice, this recipe is sure to impress your taste buds and leave you feeling satisfied. Let’s dive into the details of creating this mouthwatering dish.

Ingredients:

  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 3 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 zucchini, sliced
  • 1 yellow squash, sliced
  • 1 red bell pepper, diced
  • 1 cup sliced mushrooms
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1/2 teaspoon red pepper flakes
  • 1 (24-ounce) jar of marinara sauce
  • 1 (15-ounce) container of ricotta cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 9 lasagna noodles, uncooked
  • 1 1/2 cups water
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh basil leaves for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Set your Instant Pot to the sauté function and add the olive oil. Once the pot is hot, add the chopped onion and sauté for 2-3 minutes until translucent. Add the minced garlic and sauté for another 30 seconds.

  2. Add the zucchini, yellow squash, red bell pepper, and mushrooms to the pot. Sauté the vegetables for 5-7 minutes until they begin to soften.

  3. Sprinkle the dried oregano, dried basil, and red pepper flakes over the vegetables. Stir to combine and cook for an additional minute.

  4. Pour the marinara sauce over the sautéed vegetables and stir well to incorporate. Turn off the sauté function.

  5. In a medium bowl, combine the ricotta cheese, grated Parmesan cheese, and 1 cup of shredded mozzarella cheese. Mix until well combined.

  6. To assemble the lasagna, spread a thin layer of the vegetable marinara sauce on the bottom of the Instant Pot. Place 3 lasagna noodles on top of the sauce, breaking them if necessary to fit.

  7. Spread half of the ricotta cheese mixture over the noodles, followed by a layer of the vegetable marinara sauce. Repeat the layers with 3 more lasagna noodles, the remaining ricotta cheese mixture, and the remaining vegetable marinara sauce.

  8. Place the final 3 lasagna noodles on top of the sauce. Pour the water over the noodles, making sure to moisten them evenly. Top with the remaining 1 cup of shredded mozzarella cheese.

  9. Close the lid of the Instant Pot and set the vent to the sealing position. Cook on high pressure for 25 minutes, then allow for a natural pressure release for 10 minutes before carefully venting any remaining pressure.

  10. Carefully remove the lid and let the lasagna sit for a few minutes to set. Garnish with fresh basil leaves before serving.

Serving:

This Instant Pot Vegetable and Cheese Lasagna is a delightful one-pot meal that can be served as a main course for a family dinner or as a crowd-pleasing dish at gatherings. The combination of tender vegetables, savory cheeses, and perfectly cooked noodles creates a delightful harmony of flavors that will leave everyone reaching for seconds. Pair it with a crisp green salad and a glass of your favorite wine for a complete dining experience.

Conclusion:

With this Instant Pot Vegetable and Cheese Lasagna recipe, you can enjoy the rich and comforting flavors of traditional lasagna with the added bonus of wholesome vegetables. The convenience of using the Instant Pot makes this dish a practical option for busy weeknights when you crave a satisfying homemade meal. Whether you’re a vegetarian or simply looking to incorporate more plant-based dishes into your diet, this recipe is a winner. Give it a try and savor the deliciousness of this nutritious lasagna!
