Slow Cooker BBQ Chicken Sliders: Perfect for Parties


Are you hosting a party and looking for a delicious and hassle-free recipe that will impress your guests? Look no further than these mouthwatering Slow Cooker BBQ Chicken Sliders! This easy-to-make dish is the perfect addition to any party spread, offering a savory and satisfying option for both kids and adults alike.

Ingredients

  • 2 lbs boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1 cup of your favorite BBQ sauce
  • 1/4 cup brown sugar
  • 1/4 cup apple cider vinegar
  • 1/4 cup chicken broth
  • 1 tbsp Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 tsp smoked paprika
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 1/2 tsp onion powder
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • Slider buns
  • Optional toppings: coleslaw, pickles, red onions

Instructions

  1. Place the chicken breasts in the slow cooker.

  2. In a bowl, whisk together the BBQ sauce, brown sugar, apple cider vinegar, chicken broth, Worcestershire sauce, smoked paprika, garlic powder, onion powder, black pepper, and salt.

  3. Pour the BBQ sauce mixture over the chicken in the slow cooker, ensuring that the chicken is evenly coated.

  4. Cover and cook on low for 6-7 hours, or on high for 3-4 hours, until the chicken is tender and easily shreds with a fork.

  5. Once the chicken is cooked, use two forks to shred it directly in the slow cooker, allowing it to soak up the flavorful sauce.

  6. To assemble the sliders, place a generous amount of the BBQ chicken onto each slider bun.

  7. Add your favorite toppings such as coleslaw, pickles, or red onions to customize each slider to your liking.

  8. Serve the sliders on a platter and watch them disappear as your guests savor the irresistible flavors of the Slow Cooker BBQ Chicken Sliders.

Tips for Success

  • Use a high-quality BBQ sauce to ensure rich and robust flavor.
  • If you prefer a spicier kick, add a dash of cayenne pepper or hot sauce to the BBQ sauce mixture.
  • For a tangy twist, consider adding a squeeze of fresh lime juice to the shredded chicken before assembling the sliders.

Why Slow Cooker BBQ Chicken Sliders are Perfect for Parties

The beauty of Slow Cooker BBQ Chicken Sliders lies not only in their delicious taste but also in the convenience they offer for hosts. By utilizing a slow cooker, you can effortlessly prepare the chicken ahead of time, allowing you to focus on other party preparations while the flavors meld together to perfection. The sliders are also versatile, as they can be served as a main dish or as part of a larger buffet, making them an ideal choice for any gathering, from game day festivities to backyard barbecues.

Conclusion

With their tender, succulent chicken and bold, smoky flavors, Slow Cooker BBQ Chicken Sliders are sure to be a hit at your next party. Whether you’re hosting a crowd or simply looking for a stress-free meal option, these sliders are a delicious and satisfying choice that will leave your guests coming back for more. So fire up that slow cooker, gather your ingredients, and get ready to impress with this crowd-pleasing recipe that’s perfect for parties!
