Zesty Lemon Garlic Shrimp Scampi Linguine: A Burst of Citrus Flavor


If you’re a fan of seafood and crave a burst of citrus flavor in your meals, then you’re in for a treat. This recipe for Zesty Lemon Garlic Shrimp Scampi Linguine is a delightful marriage of succulent shrimp, tangy lemon, and aromatic garlic, all nestled on a bed of perfectly cooked linguine. It’s a dish that not only tantalizes the taste buds but also brings a refreshing zing to your dining table.

Ingredients

To start off this culinary adventure, gather the following ingredients:

  • 1 pound of linguine
  • 1 pound of large shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 4 tablespoons of unsalted butter
  • 4 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1/2 cup of chicken broth
  • 1/4 cup of freshly squeezed lemon juice
  • Zest of 1 lemon
  • 1/4 cup of chopped fresh parsley
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Red pepper flakes (optional)
  • Grated Parmesan cheese for garnish

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Linguine: Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil and cook the linguine according to the package instructions until al dente. Once cooked, drain the linguine and set it aside.

  2. Cook the Shrimp: In a large skillet over medium heat, melt 2 tablespoons of butter. Add the shrimp to the skillet and season with salt, pepper, and a pinch of red pepper flakes if you desire a hint of heat. Cook the shrimp for 2-3 minutes on each side until they turn pink and opaque. Once cooked, transfer the shrimp to a plate and cover to keep warm.

  3. Prepare the Sauce: In the same skillet, melt the remaining 2 tablespoons of butter. Add the minced garlic and sauté for about a minute until fragrant. Be careful not to let the garlic brown. Pour in the chicken broth, lemon juice, and lemon zest. Allow the mixture to simmer for 2-3 minutes to reduce slightly.

  4. Combine the Components: Return the cooked shrimp to the skillet and toss to coat them in the lemon garlic sauce. Add the cooked linguine to the skillet and gently toss everything together until the linguine is well coated with the sauce. If the sauce seems too dry, you can add a splash of pasta water to loosen it up.

  5. Finish and Serve: Sprinkle the chopped parsley over the dish and give it a final toss. Taste and adjust the seasoning if necessary. Once ready, transfer the Zesty Lemon Garlic Shrimp Scampi Linguine to a serving platter or individual plates. Finish with a generous sprinkle of grated Parmesan cheese for an extra savory touch.

Enjoy the Burst of Citrus Flavor

This Zesty Lemon Garlic Shrimp Scampi Linguine is a celebration of vibrant flavors that come together to create a memorable dining experience. The succulent shrimp, infused with the zing of lemon and the warmth of garlic, pairs beautifully with the tender linguine, resulting in a dish that is both comforting and invigorating.

Next time you’re looking to impress your guests or simply treat yourself to a special meal, consider whipping up this delightful recipe. Whether it’s a cozy dinner for two or a gathering with friends, this dish is sure to leave a lasting impression. So, roll up your sleeves, gather your ingredients, and embark on a culinary journey that promises a burst of citrus flavor in every mouthful. Cheers to good food and unforgettable flavors!
