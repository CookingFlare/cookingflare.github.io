Brunch Cocktails for Vegans and Vegetarians


Brunch, a delightful combination of breakfast and lunch, is all about good company, delicious food, and, of course, refreshing drinks. While many people look forward to mimosas, bloody marys, and other classic cocktails at brunch, those following a vegan or vegetarian diet might find themselves in need of options that align with their lifestyle choices. Fortunately, there are plenty of plant-based cocktail options that are just as delicious and satisfying as their traditional counterparts. Whether you’re hosting a brunch gathering or simply looking for a refreshing drink to enjoy with your morning meal, these vegan and vegetarian-friendly brunch cocktails are the perfect choice.

1. Vegan Mimosa

The mimosa is perhaps the most iconic brunch cocktail, and it’s an easy drink to adapt for a vegan lifestyle. Traditionally made with orange juice and champagne, this simple and effervescent drink is naturally vegan-friendly. But to ensure you’re sticking to plant-based principles, it’s essential to check the label on the champagne. Some sparkling wines use animal-derived fining agents like egg whites or gelatin in the production process.

Ingredients:

  • 3 ounces of chilled orange juice (freshly squeezed is best)
  • 3 ounces of vegan-friendly champagne or sparkling wine
  • Optional: A slice of orange for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Pour the orange juice into a champagne flute.
  2. Top with the chilled sparkling wine, pouring gently to preserve the bubbles.
  3. Garnish with a slice of orange for a burst of citrusy aroma and color.

The vegan mimosa is both simple and refreshing, making it the perfect drink to kick off any brunch. You can also mix things up by experimenting with different fruit juices like grapefruit, pineapple, or pomegranate for unique variations.

2. Bloody Mary (Vegan-Friendly)

The classic Bloody Mary is a brunch favorite with its bold flavors, savory spices, and invigorating tang. While the original recipe contains a splash of Worcestershire sauce, which often includes anchovies, it’s easy to make a vegan-friendly version with a few simple substitutions.

Ingredients:

  • 4 ounces of tomato juice
  • 1 ounce of fresh lemon juice
  • 1 teaspoon of vegan Worcestershire sauce (make sure it’s free of fish)
  • 1/2 teaspoon of hot sauce (adjust to taste)
  • 1/2 teaspoon of celery salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon of black pepper
  • 1-2 ounces of vodka (optional for extra kick)
  • Celery stalk, lemon wedge, and olives for garnish

Instructions:

  1. In a cocktail shaker, combine tomato juice, lemon juice, Worcestershire sauce, hot sauce, celery salt, and black pepper.
  2. Shake well to combine all the ingredients.
  3. If desired, add vodka and shake again to mix.
  4. Pour the mixture into a glass filled with ice.
  5. Garnish with a celery stalk, lemon wedge, and olives.

This vegan-friendly Bloody Mary will bring all the zesty flavors you love, without any animal-derived ingredients. Feel free to adjust the seasonings to your preference, adding more spice, acidity, or salt as needed.

3. Cucumber Mint Cooler

For those looking for something light and refreshing, the cucumber mint cooler is an excellent choice. This drink is crisp, clean, and full of fresh flavors—perfect for a spring or summer brunch. With cucumber and mint at the forefront, it’s both hydrating and invigorating.

Ingredients:

  • 4-5 slices of cucumber
  • 6-8 fresh mint leaves
  • 1 ounce of lime juice
  • 1 teaspoon of agave syrup or simple syrup
  • 4 ounces of sparkling water or club soda
  • Crushed ice
  • Cucumber and mint sprig for garnish

Instructions:

  1. In a cocktail shaker, muddle the cucumber slices and mint leaves together to release their juices and aromas.
  2. Add lime juice and agave syrup to the shaker.
  3. Fill the shaker with ice and shake vigorously for about 10 seconds.
  4. Strain the mixture into a glass filled with crushed ice.
  5. Top with sparkling water or club soda.
  6. Garnish with a cucumber slice and a sprig of mint.

The cucumber mint cooler is an incredibly refreshing, low-calorie option that’s naturally vegan and vegetarian-friendly. The freshness of the cucumber combined with the aromatic mint makes it the ideal drink for a leisurely brunch with friends.

4. Tropical Rum Punch (Vegan-Friendly)

If you’re in the mood for a tropical cocktail that transports you straight to paradise, look no further than the vegan-friendly tropical rum punch. Bursting with the sweetness of pineapple and orange juice, this cocktail is perfect for a laid-back brunch that feels like a vacation.

Ingredients:

  • 2 ounces of light rum
  • 3 ounces of pineapple juice
  • 2 ounces of orange juice
  • 1/2 ounce of lime juice
  • 1/2 ounce of grenadine
  • Pineapple slices and maraschino cherries for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Fill a cocktail shaker with ice.
  2. Add the rum, pineapple juice, orange juice, lime juice, and grenadine to the shaker.
  3. Shake well until the mixture is chilled.
  4. Strain into a glass filled with ice.
  5. Garnish with a slice of pineapple and a maraschino cherry for a vibrant, tropical touch.

This rum punch is an easy-to-make, crowd-pleasing option that’s both colorful and delicious. Plus, it’s completely vegan, as the grenadine is typically made from pomegranate juice rather than any animal-derived ingredients.

5. Vegan Bellini

For those who enjoy a sweet and fruity brunch cocktail, the Bellini is a wonderful choice. Traditionally made with Prosecco and peach purée, this classic Italian drink can easily be made vegan by choosing a Prosecco or sparkling wine that’s certified vegan.

Ingredients:

  • 2 ounces of peach purée (fresh or frozen peaches blended)
  • 4 ounces of chilled vegan-friendly Prosecco or sparkling wine
  • Peach slices for garnish (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Pour the peach purée into a champagne flute.
  2. Slowly pour the chilled Prosecco or sparkling wine into the flute, gently stirring to combine.
  3. Garnish with a slice of fresh peach.

The peach flavor in this cocktail pairs beautifully with the bubbles of the sparkling wine, creating a light, refreshing drink that’s perfect for a warm afternoon brunch.

6. Pineapple Basil Smash

The pineapple basil smash is an unexpected yet delightful brunch cocktail, combining the sweetness of pineapple with the earthy flavors of basil. It’s a bit more adventurous than a traditional mimosa, making it the perfect drink for those who enjoy a unique twist on their brunch beverages.

Ingredients:

  • 4-5 fresh basil leaves
  • 2 ounces of pineapple juice
  • 1 ounce of lime juice
  • 1/2 ounce of simple syrup or agave nectar
  • 1 ounce of white rum (optional)
  • Crushed ice
  • Pineapple and basil leaves for garnish

Instructions:

  1. In a cocktail shaker, muddle the basil leaves with pineapple juice and lime juice.
  2. Add simple syrup and rum (if using) to the shaker and fill with ice.
  3. Shake well for about 10 seconds.
  4. Strain the mixture into a glass filled with crushed ice.
  5. Garnish with a pineapple wedge and a few basil leaves.

The pineapple basil smash is a unique brunch cocktail that offers a harmonious balance of sweet and herbal flavors. It’s a great choice for anyone looking to impress their guests with something a little different.

7. Spicy Grapefruit Margarita

For those who enjoy a little heat with their cocktails, the spicy grapefruit margarita is a perfect fit. The tanginess of grapefruit juice is beautifully complemented by the smoky, spicy notes of jalapeño, creating a vibrant and flavorful drink that’s perfect for a lively brunch gathering.

Ingredients:

  • 2 ounces of tequila (make sure it’s 100% agave)
  • 3 ounces of fresh grapefruit juice
  • 1 ounce of lime juice
  • 1/2 ounce of agave syrup or simple syrup
  • 1-2 slices of jalapeño (adjust to your spice preference)
  • Salt or chili powder for rimming the glass (optional)
  • Grapefruit slice for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Rim your glass with salt or chili powder by rubbing a lime wedge around the rim and dipping it into the seasoning.
  2. In a cocktail shaker, muddle the jalapeño slices with lime juice and agave syrup.
  3. Add tequila and grapefruit juice to the shaker, and fill with ice.
  4. Shake well to combine.
  5. Strain into the prepared glass, filled with ice.
  6. Garnish with a slice of grapefruit.

The spicy grapefruit margarita is a bold and exciting cocktail with a perfect balance of tart, sweet, and spicy. It’s sure to become a favorite at your next brunch!

Conclusion

Whether you prefer a classic mimosa, a savory Bloody Mary, or something more adventurous like the spicy grapefruit margarita, there’s no shortage of delicious vegan and vegetarian brunch cocktails to enjoy. With a little creativity, you can easily swap out animal-derived ingredients and still enjoy the flavors you love. So, the next time you’re hosting a brunch or simply relaxing with friends, whip up one of these delightful drinks and toast to a good time, plant-based style!

Each of these cocktails is easy to make and packed with fresh, vibrant flavors that will make your brunch unforgettable. Cheers!
