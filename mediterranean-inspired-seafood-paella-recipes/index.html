Mediterranean-Inspired Seafood Paella Recipes


If you’re looking to transport your taste buds to the sunny shores of the Mediterranean, then look no further than these delicious seafood paella recipes. Bursting with vibrant flavors and rich in seafood goodness, these recipes are perfect for any occasion, whether you’re hosting a dinner party or simply treating yourself to a special meal.

Classic Seafood Paella

Ingredients:

  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 red bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 yellow bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 cup Arborio rice
  • 1/2 teaspoon saffron threads
  • 1 teaspoon smoked paprika
  • 1 cup white wine
  • 3 cups seafood stock
  • 1 pound fresh mussels, cleaned and debearded
  • 1 pound large shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 1/2 cup frozen peas
  • Fresh parsley, chopped
  • Lemon wedges, for serving

Instructions:

  1. Heat the olive oil in a large, shallow pan over medium heat. Add the onion and garlic, and sauté until fragrant and translucent.
  2. Add the bell peppers to the pan and cook until they start to soften.
  3. Stir in the Arborio rice, saffron threads, and smoked paprika, ensuring that the rice is well-coated with the spices.
  4. Pour in the white wine and cook until it has evaporated.
  5. Gradually add the seafood stock, stirring occasionally, and cook until the rice is almost tender, about 15 minutes.
  6. Nestle the mussels and shrimp into the rice mixture, cover the pan, and cook until the seafood is cooked through and the mussels have opened, about 5 minutes.
  7. Stir in the frozen peas and cook for an additional 2 minutes.
  8. Remove the pan from the heat and let it sit, covered, for 5 minutes to allow the flavors to meld.
  9. Garnish with fresh parsley and serve with lemon wedges for that extra zing.

Grilled Seafood Paella

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound large prawns, peeled and deveined
  • 1 pound squid, cleaned and cut into rings
  • 1 pound mussels, cleaned and debearded
  • 1 pound clams, cleaned
  • 1 red onion, finely chopped
  • 4 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 red bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 yellow bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 cup Arborio rice
  • 1/2 teaspoon smoked paprika
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • 1 lemon, zested and juiced
  • 3 cups seafood stock
  • Fresh parsley, chopped
  • Olive oil
  • Salt and pepper, to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your grill to medium-high heat.
  2. In a large bowl, combine the prawns, squid, mussels, clams, red onion, garlic, bell peppers, lemon zest, dried oregano, dried thyme, and a drizzle of olive oil. Season with salt and pepper, and toss to coat everything evenly.
  3. In a large paella pan or a heatproof skillet, heat some olive oil over medium heat. Add the Arborio rice and smoked paprika, and toast the rice until it turns slightly golden, about 2 minutes.
  4. Pour in the seafood stock and bring it to a simmer. Cook for 5 minutes, allowing the flavors to meld together.
  5. Carefully transfer the paella pan or skillet to the preheated grill. Close the grill lid and cook for about 15 minutes, or until the rice is tender and has absorbed the liquid.
  6. Place the marinated seafood and vegetables on the grill, and cook until the seafood is opaque and cooked through, and the vegetables are tender, about 5 minutes.
  7. Remove the paella from the grill and let it rest for a few minutes. Squeeze fresh lemon juice over the top and garnish with fresh parsley.
  8. Serve hot and enjoy the smoky flavors of the grilled seafood paella.

Spicy Seafood Paella

Ingredients:

  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 red bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 yellow bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 cup Arborio rice
  • 1/2 teaspoon smoked paprika
  • 1/4 teaspoon cayenne pepper
  • 1 cup white wine
  • 3 cups seafood stock
  • 1 pound fresh mussels, cleaned and debearded
  • 1 pound large shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 1/2 cup frozen corn kernels
  • Fresh cilantro, chopped
  • Lime wedges, for serving

Instructions:

  1. Heat the olive oil in a large, shallow pan over medium heat. Add the onion and garlic, and sauté until fragrant and translucent.
  2. Add the bell peppers to the pan and cook until they start to soften.
  3. Stir in the Arborio rice, smoked paprika, and cayenne pepper, ensuring that the rice is well-coated with the spices.
  4. Pour in the white wine and cook until it has evaporated.
  5. Gradually add the seafood stock, stirring occasionally, and cook until the rice is almost tender, about 15 minutes.
  6. Nestle the mussels and shrimp into the rice mixture, cover the pan, and cook until the seafood is cooked through and the mussels have opened, about 5 minutes.
  7. Stir in the frozen corn kernels and cook for an additional 2 minutes.
  8. Remove the pan from the heat and let it sit, covered, for 5 minutes to allow the flavors to meld.
  9. Garnish with fresh cilantro and serve with lime wedges for that extra kick.

Enjoy these Mediterranean-inspired seafood paella recipes that will transport you to sun-kissed shores with every bite. Whether you prefer the classic version, the smoky flavors of the grilled variation, or the spicy twist, these recipes are sure to impress and satisfy your cravings. So gather your ingredients, get cooking, and indulge in the flavors of the Mediterranean today!
