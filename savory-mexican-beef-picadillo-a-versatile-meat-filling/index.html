Savory Mexican Beef Picadillo: A Versatile Meat Filling


Are you looking for a delicious and versatile meat filling that can elevate your Mexican dishes? Look no further than savory Mexican beef picadillo! This flavorful and easy-to-make dish is a staple in Mexican cuisine, and it can be used in a variety of recipes, from tacos and burritos to empanadas and enchiladas. In this blog post, we will explore the traditional recipe for Mexican beef picadillo and provide you with some ideas on how to incorporate this versatile meat filling into your favorite dishes.

Ingredients

To make the savory Mexican beef picadillo, you will need the following ingredients:

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 onion, finely diced
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 bell pepper, finely diced
  • 1 tomato, diced
  • 1 potato, peeled and diced
  • 1 carrot, peeled and diced
  • 1/4 cup green olives, sliced
  • 2 tablespoons raisins
  • 2 tablespoons tomato paste
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • 1 teaspoon chili powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil

Instructions

  1. Heat the olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Add the diced onion and minced garlic, and cook until the onion is translucent and fragrant.

  2. Add the ground beef to the skillet, and cook until browned, breaking it up with a spoon as it cooks.

  3. Once the beef is browned, add the diced bell pepper, tomato, potato, carrot, green olives, raisins, tomato paste, cumin, and chili powder. Stir well to combine all the ingredients.

  4. Reduce the heat to low, cover the skillet, and let the picadillo simmer for about 20 minutes, or until the potatoes and carrots are tender.

  5. Season with salt and pepper to taste, and give it a final stir.

  6. Remove the picadillo from the heat and let it cool slightly before using it as a filling in your favorite dishes.

Ideas for Using Mexican Beef Picadillo

Now that you have a delicious batch of savory Mexican beef picadillo, let’s explore some creative ways to incorporate it into your favorite dishes:

Tacos

Fill soft or crispy taco shells with a generous spoonful of picadillo, and top it with your favorite taco toppings, such as shredded cheese, lettuce, tomatoes, and salsa. The savory and flavorful beef filling will take your tacos to the next level.

Burritos

Spread a layer of picadillo down the center of a tortilla, and add some rice, beans, and cheese. Roll it up tightly, tucking in the sides, and you have a satisfying and hearty burrito that will make your taste buds dance.

Empanadas

Use the picadillo as a filling for homemade empanadas. Simply spoon a small amount of the filling onto a circle of dough, fold it over, and seal the edges. Bake until golden brown, and you have a delicious and portable snack or appetizer.

Enchiladas

Roll the picadillo in corn tortillas, and place them in a baking dish. Top with enchilada sauce and shredded cheese, and bake until the cheese is melted and bubbly. Serve with rice and beans for a complete and satisfying meal.

Stuffed Peppers

Hollow out bell peppers and stuff them with the picadillo. Bake until the peppers are tender and the filling is heated through. The combination of the sweet bell peppers and the savory beef filling is simply irresistible.

Conclusion

Savory Mexican beef picadillo is a versatile meat filling that can transform your favorite Mexican dishes into culinary delights. With its combination of flavors and textures, it adds depth and richness to tacos, burritos, empanadas, enchiladas, and stuffed peppers. So next time you’re craving Mexican cuisine, whip up a batch of picadillo and let your creativity soar. Your taste buds will thank you!
