Maple Glazed Brussels Sprouts and Bacon: A Flavorful Side Dish


If you’re looking for a delicious and unique side dish to complement your meals, look no further than these Maple Glazed Brussels Sprouts with Bacon. This recipe combines the earthy flavor of Brussels sprouts with the savory goodness of bacon, all tied together with a sweet and tangy maple glaze. It’s a dish that’s sure to be a hit at any gathering, whether it’s a casual weeknight dinner or a special holiday feast.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound Brussels sprouts, trimmed and halved
  • 6 slices bacon, chopped
  • 2 tablespoons maple syrup
  • 1 tablespoon balsamic vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Preheat the Oven: Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C) and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.

  2. Cook the Bacon: In a large skillet over medium heat, cook the chopped bacon until it’s browned and crispy. Use a slotted spoon to transfer the cooked bacon to a plate lined with paper towels to drain excess grease. Leave the bacon drippings in the skillet.

  3. Prepare the Brussels Sprouts: Toss the halved Brussels sprouts in the bacon drippings in the skillet, coating them evenly. Season with salt and pepper to taste.

  4. Roast the Brussels Sprouts: Arrange the Brussels sprouts in a single layer on the prepared baking sheet and roast in the preheated oven for 20-25 minutes, or until they are tender and caramelized.

  5. Make the Maple Glaze: While the Brussels sprouts are roasting, whisk together the maple syrup, balsamic vinegar, and olive oil in a small bowl.

  6. Combine and Glaze: Once the Brussels sprouts are done roasting, transfer them back to the skillet with the cooked bacon. Pour the maple glaze over the sprouts and bacon, tossing everything together until the glaze evenly coats the sprouts and bacon.

  7. Serve and Enjoy: Transfer the Maple Glazed Brussels Sprouts and Bacon to a serving dish and serve immediately. The combination of sweet, savory, and tangy flavors will surely impress your taste buds and leave your guests wanting more.

Tips and Variations

  • For a vegetarian version, you can omit the bacon and sauté the Brussels sprouts in olive oil instead.
  • Add a sprinkle of grated Parmesan cheese over the finished dish for an extra flavor boost.
  • Experiment with different types of vinegar, such as apple cider vinegar or red wine vinegar, to customize the glaze to your liking.

In Conclusion

Maple Glazed Brussels Sprouts and Bacon is a side dish that offers a delightful interplay of flavors and textures. The rich umami of the bacon, the slight bitterness of the Brussels sprouts, and the sweet and tangy notes of the maple glaze come together to create a truly memorable dish. Whether you’re a Brussels sprouts enthusiast or looking to convert skeptics, this recipe is a must-try. It’s a versatile dish that pairs well with a wide range of main courses, making it a valuable addition to your culinary repertoire. So, next time you’re in the mood for a side that’s anything but ordinary, give this recipe a whirl and savor the delightful combination of Maple Glazed Brussels Sprouts and Bacon.

So, what are you waiting for? Get those Brussels sprouts roasting and let the aroma of maple-glazed goodness fill your kitchen!
