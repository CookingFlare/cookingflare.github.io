Decadent Oreo Cake: Cookies and Cream Heaven

If you’re a fan of Oreo cookies and all things creamy, then this recipe is a must-try for you! Our Decadent Oreo Cake is a true delight, combining the rich flavors of chocolate and cream in every bite. Whether you’re celebrating a special occasion or simply treating yourself to a sweet indulgence, this cake is guaranteed to satisfy your cravings. Ingredients 2 cups all-purpose flour 1 and 1/2 cups granulated sugar 3/4 cup unsweetened cocoa powder 1 and 1/2 teaspoons baking powder 1 and 1/2 teaspoons baking soda 1 teaspoon salt 1 cup buttermilk 1/2 cup vegetable oil 2 large eggs 2 teaspoons vanilla extract 1 cup boiling water 1 package Oreo cookies (regular or Double Stuf) 2 cups heavy cream 1/4 cup powdered sugar 1 teaspoon vanilla extract Instructions Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C) and grease two 9-inch round cake pans. In a large mixing bowl, combine the flour, sugar, cocoa powder, baking powder, baking soda, and salt. Stir until well combined. Add the buttermilk, vegetable oil, eggs, and vanilla extract to the dry ingredients. Mix until the batter is smooth and well incorporated. Carefully pour in the boiling water and mix until the batter becomes thin. Don’t worry, it’s supposed to be like this! Divide the batter equally between the prepared cake pans. Take your Oreo cookies and gently twist them apart, separating the cookies from the cream filling. Place the cream filling in a separate bowl. Place a layer of Oreo cookies on top of the batter in each cake pan, pressing them lightly into the batter. Pour the remaining batter over the Oreo cookies, making sure they’re fully covered. Bake in the preheated oven for 30-35 minutes or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean. Allow the cakes to cool completely before frosting. Frosting In a mixing bowl, combine the heavy cream, powdered sugar, and vanilla extract. Beat the mixture until stiff peaks form. Be careful not to overbeat, as it may turn into butter! Take your reserved Oreo cream filling and fold it into the whipped cream mixture. This will give the frosting an extra Oreo flavor. Once the cakes have cooled, remove them from the pans and place one cake layer on a serving plate or cake stand. Spread a generous amount of the Oreo whipped cream frosting on top of the cake layer. Carefully place the second cake layer on top and frost the entire cake with the remaining whipped cream frosting. For an added touch, crumble some Oreo cookies and sprinkle them on top of the cake as a garnish. Refrigerate the cake for at least one hour before serving to allow the flavors to meld together. Enjoy! Slice into this decadent Oreo Cake and let the layers of chocolatey goodness and creamy Oreo filling transport you to dessert heaven. The combination of the moist chocolate cake and the luscious Oreo whipped cream frosting is a match made in culinary paradise. Serve it at your next gathering or enjoy it as a special treat for yourself. Either way, you’re in for a cookies and cream extravaganza that will leave everyone wanting more! ...
