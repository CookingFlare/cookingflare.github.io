Easy and Elegant Seafood Paella Recipe for Home Cooks


If you’re looking to impress your guests with a stunning and delicious dish, look no further than this easy and elegant seafood paella recipe. Paella is a traditional Spanish dish that’s perfect for special occasions or even just a cozy dinner at home. This recipe brings together a delightful mix of seafood, flavorful rice, and aromatic spices that will transport your taste buds to the sunny shores of Spain.

Ingredients

For the Seafood Stock:

  • 4 cups of water
  • Shells and heads from 1 pound of shrimp
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves, smashed
  • 1 bay leaf
  • Salt to taste

For the Paella:

  • 1/4 cup of olive oil
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 4 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 red bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 yellow bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 cup of diced tomatoes
  • 2 cups of Spanish paella rice
  • 1 teaspoon of saffron threads
  • 1 teaspoon of smoked paprika
  • 4 cups of seafood stock
  • 1 pound of jumbo shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 1 pound of mussels, cleaned and debearded
  • 1 pound of clams, cleaned
  • 1/2 cup of frozen peas
  • Lemon wedges for serving
  • Chopped fresh parsley for garnish

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Seafood Stock:

    • In a large pot, bring the water to a boil. Add the shrimp shells and heads, chopped onion, smashed garlic cloves, bay leaf, and a pinch of salt. Reduce the heat and let the stock simmer for 30 minutes. Strain the stock and set aside.

  2. Cook the Paella:

    • In a large paella pan or skillet, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the chopped onion and minced garlic, sautéing until they become translucent.
    • Add the sliced red and yellow bell peppers, and cook until they start to soften.
    • Stir in the diced tomatoes and cook for a few minutes until they release their juices.
    • Add the paella rice to the pan, stirring to coat the grains with the flavorful mixture.
    • Sprinkle the saffron threads and smoked paprika over the rice, then pour in the seafood stock. Gently stir to distribute the ingredients evenly.

  3. Add the Seafood:

    • Arrange the jumbo shrimp, mussels, and clams on top of the rice mixture. Cook for about 15-20 minutes, or until the rice is tender and the seafood is cooked through.
    • Scatter the frozen peas over the paella and cook for an additional 5 minutes.

  4. Serve and Enjoy:

    • Once the seafood is cooked and the rice has absorbed the delicious flavors, remove the paella from the heat.
    • Garnish with chopped fresh parsley and serve with lemon wedges on the side.

Tips for Success

  • Use high-quality saffron for the best flavor and color.
  • Ensure the seafood is fresh and cleaned thoroughly before adding it to the paella.
  • Allow the paella to rest for a few minutes before serving to let the flavors meld together.

Final Thoughts

This easy and elegant seafood paella recipe is a show-stopping entrée that will leave your guests impressed and satisfied. The combination of tender seafood, aromatic rice, and vibrant spices makes for a truly memorable dining experience. Whether you’re hosting a dinner party or simply craving a taste of Spain, this paella is sure to become a favorite in your culinary repertoire. So, don your apron, gather your ingredients, and get ready to create a culinary masterpiece in the comfort of your own kitchen!
