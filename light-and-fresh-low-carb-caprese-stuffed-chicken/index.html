Light and Fresh Low Carb Caprese Stuffed Chicken


Looking for a delightful and healthy dinner option that’s bursting with flavor? This Light and Fresh Low Carb Caprese Stuffed Chicken recipe is a must-try! It’s a simple yet elegant dish that brings together the classic flavors of Caprese salad and tender chicken breast. Not only is it low in carbs, but it’s also a feast for the senses, combining juicy tomatoes, fragrant basil, creamy mozzarella, and succulent chicken. Let’s dive into the recipe and learn how to create this delectable dish right in your own kitchen!

Ingredients:

  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 8 oz fresh mozzarella, sliced into 1/4-inch rounds
  • 1/4 cup fresh basil leaves
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tbsp balsamic vinegar
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

1. Preparing the Chicken:

Begin by preheating your oven to 375°F (190°C). While the oven is heating, prepare the chicken breasts by slicing a pocket into each one. Be careful not to cut all the way through. Season the inside and outside of the chicken breasts with salt and pepper to taste.

2. Stuffing the Chicken:

Next, it’s time to stuff the chicken breasts. Place 2-3 slices of mozzarella, a few halved cherry tomatoes, and a couple of basil leaves inside the pocket of each chicken breast. Press the edges of the chicken together to seal in the filling. Use toothpicks to secure, if necessary.

3. Searing the Chicken:

In a large oven-safe skillet, heat 1 tablespoon of olive oil over medium-high heat. Add the stuffed chicken breasts and sear for 2-3 minutes on each side, or until golden brown. This will help lock in the juices and flavors.

4. Finishing in the Oven:

Once the chicken is seared, transfer the skillet to the preheated oven. Bake for 20-25 minutes or until the chicken is cooked through and the cheese is melted and bubbly.

5. Making the Balsamic Glaze:

While the chicken is baking, prepare the balsamic glaze. In a small saucepan, heat the remaining olive oil over medium heat. Add the minced garlic and sauté for 1 minute until fragrant. Stir in the balsamic vinegar and let it simmer for 2-3 minutes until it has slightly thickened.

6. Serving:

Once the chicken is done, remove it from the oven and let it rest for a few minutes. Drizzle the balsamic glaze over the stuffed chicken breasts. Garnish with fresh basil leaves and additional cherry tomatoes if desired.

Enjoying the Dish:

Serve this delightful Caprese Stuffed Chicken with a side of roasted vegetables, a crisp green salad, or a light quinoa pilaf for a complete and satisfying meal. The combination of flavors and textures in this dish is sure to impress your family and friends, making it perfect for a casual weeknight dinner or a special weekend gathering.

This Light and Fresh Low Carb Caprese Stuffed Chicken recipe is a wonderful way to elevate a simple chicken breast into a gourmet experience. Not only is it low in carbs, but it’s also packed with protein and essential nutrients, making it a healthy choice for anyone looking to indulge in a delicious meal without the guilt. Try this recipe today and savor the taste of summer with every bite!
