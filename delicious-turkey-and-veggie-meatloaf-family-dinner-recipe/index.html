Delicious Turkey and Veggie Meatloaf: Family Dinner Recipe


Are you tired of the same old meatloaf recipe? Looking for a healthier, more flavorful alternative that the whole family will love? Look no further! This Delicious Turkey and Veggie Meatloaf recipe is the perfect way to add a nutritious twist to a classic comfort food. Packed with lean ground turkey and a variety of colorful vegetables, this meatloaf is not only delicious but also a great way to sneak in some extra nutrients for picky eaters. Let’s dive into the details of this mouthwatering recipe!

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground turkey
  • 1/2 cup grated zucchini
  • 1/2 cup grated carrot
  • 1/2 cup finely chopped onion
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/2 cup breadcrumbs
  • 1/4 cup ketchup
  • 1 tablespoon Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley
  • 2 eggs, beaten

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C). Lightly grease a loaf pan and set it aside.

  2. In a large mixing bowl, combine the ground turkey, grated zucchini, grated carrot, chopped onion, minced garlic, breadcrumbs, ketchup, Worcestershire sauce, dried oregano, dried thyme, salt, black pepper, chopped parsley, and beaten eggs. Use your hands or a spoon to mix the ingredients until well combined.

  3. Transfer the meatloaf mixture into the prepared loaf pan, and gently press it down to form an even loaf shape.

  4. Place the loaf pan in the preheated oven and bake for 45-55 minutes, or until the meatloaf is cooked through and the top is golden brown.

  5. Once cooked, remove the meatloaf from the oven and let it rest for a few minutes before slicing.

  6. Serve slices of the Delicious Turkey and Veggie Meatloaf with your favorite sides, such as mashed potatoes, steamed vegetables, or a fresh salad.

Tips and Variations

  • Feel free to customize this recipe by adding other vegetables such as bell peppers, mushrooms, or spinach.
  • For a gluten-free option, use gluten-free breadcrumbs or crushed gluten-free crackers.
  • You can also experiment with different herbs and spices to suit your taste preferences.

Why You’ll Love This Recipe

This Delicious Turkey and Veggie Meatloaf is a fantastic way to incorporate more vegetables into your family’s diet without sacrificing flavor. The combination of lean ground turkey with zucchini, carrots, and onions creates a moist and flavorful meatloaf that’s sure to be a hit with both kids and adults. Plus, it’s a great make-ahead dish that can be enjoyed for dinner and then repurposed for sandwiches or salads the next day.

Final Thoughts

Whether you’re a meatloaf enthusiast or looking for a healthier dinner option, this Delicious Turkey and Veggie Meatloaf is a must-try recipe. It’s a versatile dish that’s perfect for busy weeknights or a cozy family dinner. So, roll up your sleeves, gather your ingredients, and get ready to savor the delicious flavors of this wholesome meatloaf. Enjoy!
