Instant Pot Recipes with Ground Beef: Versatile and Delicious


If you’re looking for a way to make dinner time easier, more efficient, and tastier, you’re in the right place. The Instant Pot has revolutionized home cooking, offering a speedy way to prepare meals without sacrificing flavor. One of the most versatile ingredients you can work with in your Instant Pot is ground beef. Whether you’re craving a comforting stew, a savory soup, or a quick taco night, ground beef in the Instant Pot makes for a quick, satisfying meal that can please everyone at the table.

In this blog post, we’ll explore several delicious and simple Instant Pot recipes featuring ground beef. From hearty chili to flavorful pasta dishes, these recipes are perfect for busy weeknights or meal prep. So, grab your Instant Pot, and let’s dive into some scrumptious ground beef recipes that will become staples in your kitchen!

1. Instant Pot Ground Beef Chili

Chili is the ultimate comfort food, and when made in the Instant Pot, it comes together in a fraction of the time it takes to make on the stovetop. This hearty and flavorful chili uses ground beef as its base and is packed with beans, tomatoes, and spices for a rich, savory flavor. Best of all, the Instant Pot helps infuse all those flavors quickly, so you’ll have a warm, satisfying meal in no time.

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can (14 oz) diced tomatoes
  • 1 can (15 oz) kidney beans, drained and rinsed
  • 1 can (15 oz) black beans, drained and rinsed
  • 1 cup beef broth
  • 2 tbsp chili powder
  • 1 tsp cumin
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • 1/2 tsp cayenne pepper (optional, for heat)
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • Optional toppings: shredded cheese, sour cream, green onions

Instructions:

  1. Set your Instant Pot to “Sauté” mode. Once hot, add the ground beef and cook until browned, breaking it apart with a spoon as it cooks.
  2. Add the diced onion and garlic, and sauté for another 2-3 minutes until softened.
  3. Stir in the chili powder, cumin, paprika, and cayenne pepper. Cook for 1 minute to let the spices bloom.
  4. Add the diced tomatoes, beans, and beef broth. Stir well to combine.
  5. Cancel the “Sauté” mode and secure the lid on the Instant Pot. Set it to cook on high pressure for 10 minutes.
  6. Once the cooking time is up, allow the pressure to naturally release for 10 minutes, then quick release the remaining pressure.
  7. Stir the chili and season with salt and pepper to taste. Serve with your favorite toppings like shredded cheese or sour cream.

2. Instant Pot Ground Beef Tacos

Tacos are a quick and easy weeknight dinner, and with the Instant Pot, you can have perfectly seasoned ground beef ready in just minutes. The Instant Pot locks in the flavors, making your taco filling incredibly juicy and flavorful. Whether you’re hosting Taco Tuesday or just craving a delicious, customizable meal, these Instant Pot ground beef tacos will not disappoint.

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 packet taco seasoning (or homemade seasoning)
  • 1/2 cup beef broth
  • Soft taco shells
  • Toppings: shredded lettuce, diced tomatoes, cheese, sour cream, salsa

Instructions:

  1. Set the Instant Pot to “Sauté” mode. Once hot, add the ground beef and cook until browned, breaking it apart as it cooks.
  2. Add the diced onion and garlic to the pot, sautéing for an additional 2 minutes until softened.
  3. Stir in the taco seasoning and beef broth, mixing everything together to coat the meat in the seasoning.
  4. Cancel the “Sauté” mode and lock the Instant Pot lid. Set it to cook on high pressure for 5 minutes.
  5. When the cooking time is up, quickly release the pressure.
  6. Open the Instant Pot and give the beef mixture a good stir. Spoon the flavorful beef into taco shells and top with your favorite toppings.

3. Instant Pot Ground Beef Stroganoff

Beef Stroganoff is a classic, rich dish that’s often slow-cooked to bring out its deep, comforting flavors. With the Instant Pot, you can make this creamy, indulgent meal in a fraction of the time, without losing any of the richness. The ground beef creates a wonderful, easy-to-eat version of the classic dish, perfect for busy weeknights.

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 cups beef broth
  • 8 oz egg noodles
  • 1 cup sour cream
  • 1 tbsp Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 tsp Dijon mustard
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • 2 tbsp flour (optional, for thickening)

Instructions:

  1. Set the Instant Pot to “Sauté” mode. Brown the ground beef, breaking it apart as it cooks. Drain any excess fat.
  2. Add the diced onion and garlic, sautéing until softened, about 3 minutes.
  3. Stir in the flour (if using) and cook for 1 minute to form a roux.
  4. Add the beef broth, Worcestershire sauce, and Dijon mustard. Stir to combine.
  5. Stir in the egg noodles, making sure they’re submerged in the liquid.
  6. Cancel the “Sauté” mode, seal the Instant Pot lid, and set it to cook on high pressure for 4 minutes.
  7. When the timer goes off, quick-release the pressure and carefully open the lid.
  8. Stir in the sour cream and season with salt and pepper to taste. Serve hot and enjoy a creamy, comforting dish!

4. Instant Pot Ground Beef and Rice Casserole

A one-pot meal that combines ground beef, rice, and savory seasonings, this casserole-style dish is hearty, satisfying, and simple to make. The Instant Pot ensures that the rice cooks perfectly every time, soaking up the flavors from the beef and seasonings. It’s the perfect comfort food for a busy weeknight.

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup long-grain white rice
  • 1 can (14 oz) diced tomatoes
  • 1 cup beef broth
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • 1/2 tsp paprika
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • Shredded cheese (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Set the Instant Pot to “Sauté” mode. Brown the ground beef, breaking it apart as it cooks. Drain any excess fat.
  2. Add the diced onion and garlic, and sauté for 2-3 minutes until softened.
  3. Stir in the rice, diced tomatoes, beef broth, oregano, paprika, salt, and pepper.
  4. Cancel the “Sauté” mode, secure the lid, and set the Instant Pot to cook on high pressure for 8 minutes.
  5. Once the cooking time is up, let the pressure release naturally for 5 minutes, then quick-release any remaining pressure.
  6. Stir the casserole, then top with shredded cheese if desired. Serve immediately for a delicious, one-pot meal.

5. Instant Pot Ground Beef Meatballs

Meatballs are a universal favorite, whether served with spaghetti or as an appetizer. Using ground beef in the Instant Pot makes the process incredibly fast while still delivering tender, juicy meatballs. With a flavorful marinara sauce, these meatballs are a crowd-pleaser at any table.

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1/4 cup breadcrumbs
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tsp dried basil
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • 1 cup marinara sauce

Instructions:

  1. In a mixing bowl, combine the ground beef, breadcrumbs, Parmesan, egg, garlic, basil, oregano, salt, and pepper. Mix until just combined.
  2. Form the mixture into meatballs, about 1-inch in diameter.
  3. Pour the marinara sauce into the Instant Pot and gently place the meatballs into the sauce.
  4. Seal the Instant Pot lid and set it to cook on high pressure for 10 minutes.
  5. After the cooking time is up, perform a quick release of the pressure.
  6. Carefully remove the meatballs and serve them with extra marinara sauce over pasta or as a sandwich filling.

Conclusion

Ground beef is a fantastic ingredient for the Instant Pot, as it cooks quickly and absorbs the flavors of any seasoning or broth you add. These recipes are just the beginning of the many ways you can enjoy ground beef in your Instant Pot. Whether you’re in the mood for a classic chili, creamy stroganoff, or easy tacos, these dishes prove that with a little bit of ground beef and an Instant Pot, you can create delicious meals in no time. Try them out and enjoy the ease and flavor that comes with cooking ground beef in the Instant Pot!
