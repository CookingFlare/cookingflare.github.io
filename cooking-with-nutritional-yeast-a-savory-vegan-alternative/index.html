Cooking with Nutritional Yeast: A Savory Vegan Alternative


Nutritional yeast is a versatile ingredient that has gained popularity in the vegan community for its unique flavor and nutritional benefits. It is a deactivated form of yeast that is often used as a cheese substitute due to its cheesy and nutty taste. Not only does it add a delicious umami flavor to your dishes, but it is also packed with essential nutrients, making it a great addition to any vegan diet. In this blog post, we will explore some exciting and delicious ways to incorporate nutritional yeast into your cooking.

1. Nutritional Yeast “Cheese” Sauce

One of the most popular uses of nutritional yeast is in a vegan “cheese” sauce. This creamy and flavorful sauce is perfect for drizzling over pasta, nachos, or even steamed vegetables. Here’s how you can make it:

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup raw cashews, soaked overnight
  • 3 tablespoons nutritional yeast
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 1 clove garlic
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon paprika
  • 1/4 teaspoon onion powder
  • 1 cup water

Instructions:

  1. Drain the soaked cashews and add them to a blender.
  2. Add the nutritional yeast, lemon juice, garlic, salt, paprika, onion powder, and water to the blender.
  3. Blend on high speed until smooth and creamy.
  4. Pour the mixture into a saucepan and heat over medium heat until warmed through.
  5. Serve over your favorite dishes and enjoy!

2. Nutritional Yeast “Parmesan” Sprinkle

If you’re looking for a vegan alternative to parmesan cheese, nutritional yeast can be a game-changer. This simple “parmesan” sprinkle adds a cheesy flavor and a touch of texture to your dishes. Here’s how to make it:

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 cup nutritional yeast
  • 1/4 cup raw almonds
  • 1/4 cup raw cashews
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon onion powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt

Instructions:

  1. Add the nutritional yeast, almonds, cashews, garlic powder, onion powder, and salt to a food processor.
  2. Pulse until the mixture resembles a coarse crumb texture.
  3. Sprinkle this “parmesan” over pasta, salads, roasted vegetables, or anything else you desire.

3. Nutritional Yeast “Popcorn” Seasoning

Looking for a healthy and flavorful alternative to buttered popcorn? Look no further than nutritional yeast! This savory seasoning adds a deliciously cheesy flavor to your popcorn without any added fat. Here’s how you can make it:

Ingredients:

  • 1/4 cup nutritional yeast
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon onion powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt

Instructions:

  1. In a small bowl, combine the nutritional yeast, garlic powder, onion powder, paprika, and salt.
  2. Sprinkle this mixture over freshly popped popcorn and toss to coat evenly.
  3. Enjoy a guilt-free and delicious snack!

4. Nutritional Yeast “Breading” for Tofu or Vegetables

Nutritional yeast can also be used as a flavorful coating for tofu or vegetables before baking or frying. This “breading” adds a crispy and savory layer that elevates the overall taste and texture of your dish. Here’s how to use it:

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup panko breadcrumbs
  • 1/4 cup nutritional yeast
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon paprika
  • 1/4 teaspoon dried thyme

Instructions:

  1. In a shallow bowl, combine the panko breadcrumbs, nutritional yeast, garlic powder, salt, paprika, and dried thyme.
  2. Dip tofu or vegetables into your choice of liquid (such as plant-based milk or aquafaba) and then coat them in the nutritional yeast mixture.
  3. Bake or fry until golden and crispy.
  4. Serve as a delicious and protein-packed main dish or side.

Conclusion

Nutritional yeast is a versatile and nutritious ingredient that adds a savory and cheesy flavor to vegan dishes. Whether you’re looking for a creamy “cheese” sauce, a sprinkle of “parmesan,” a popcorn seasoning, or a crispy breading, nutritional yeast has got you covered. Experiment with these recipes and discover the endless possibilities of cooking with nutritional yeast. Enjoy the flavors and benefits it brings to your vegan meals!
