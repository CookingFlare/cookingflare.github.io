Fresh Herb Sweets: Indulgent Desserts with a Hint of Herbaceousness


Who said herbs are only meant for savory dishes? When it comes to desserts, incorporating fresh herbs can take your sweet treats to a whole new level. The subtle herbaceousness adds a unique twist to classic desserts, creating indulgent creations that are both refreshing and surprising. In this blog post, we will explore a variety of recipes that showcase the delightful combination of fresh herbs and sweets. Get ready to tantalize your taste buds with these herb-infused desserts!

1. Lemon Verbena Tart

Ingredients:

  • 1 pre-made tart shell
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1/4 cup lemon juice
  • 2 tablespoons lemon zest
  • 2 tablespoons chopped fresh lemon verbena leaves
  • Fresh berries for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. In a saucepan, combine the heavy cream, sugar, lemon juice, lemon zest, and chopped lemon verbena leaves.
  3. Heat the mixture over medium heat until it just begins to simmer. Remove from heat and let it steep for 10 minutes.
  4. Strain the mixture to remove the lemon verbena leaves and any zest.
  5. Pour the mixture into the pre-made tart shell and bake for 15-20 minutes, or until the filling is set.
  6. Allow the tart to cool completely before garnishing with fresh berries.
  7. Serve and enjoy this tangy and herbaceous delight!

2. Basil Chocolate Chip Cookies

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1 1/2 cups sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup chocolate chips
  • 1/4 cup finely chopped fresh basil leaves

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. In a large mixing bowl, cream together the softened butter and sugar until light and fluffy.
  3. Beat in the eggs, one at a time, followed by the vanilla extract.
  4. In a separate bowl, whisk together the flour, baking soda, and salt. Gradually add the dry ingredients to the butter mixture, mixing until just combined.
  5. Fold in the chocolate chips and finely chopped fresh basil leaves.
  6. Drop rounded tablespoons of dough onto a baking sheet, spacing them about 2 inches apart.
  7. Bake for 10-12 minutes, or until the edges are golden brown.
  8. Allow the cookies to cool on the baking sheet for a few minutes before transferring them to a wire rack to cool completely.
  9. Enjoy these unique and aromatic basil-infused chocolate chip cookies!

3. Lavender Panna Cotta

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups heavy cream
  • 1/4 cup sugar
  • 1 tablespoon dried culinary lavender
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 2 teaspoons powdered gelatin
  • 2 tablespoons cold water
  • Fresh lavender sprigs for garnish

Instructions:

  1. In a saucepan, heat the heavy cream, sugar, dried lavender, and vanilla extract over medium heat until it starts to simmer. Remove from heat and let it steep for 10 minutes.
  2. In a small bowl, sprinkle the gelatin over the cold water. Allow it to bloom for 5 minutes.
  3. Strain the steeped cream mixture to remove the dried lavender.
  4. Return the strained mixture to the saucepan and heat it over low heat. Add the bloomed gelatin and stir until dissolved.
  5. Remove from heat and let the mixture cool slightly.
  6. Pour the mixture into individual serving glasses or ramekins.
  7. Refrigerate for at least 4 hours, or until set.
  8. Garnish with fresh lavender sprigs before serving.
  9. Relish in the delicate flavors of lavender in this creamy and elegant dessert!

These are just a few examples of the enchanting desserts you can create by incorporating fresh herbs. The combination of herbs and sweets may seem unconventional, but once you try these herb-infused treats, you’ll be hooked! Experiment with different herbs like mint, rosemary, or thyme to discover your own unique herbaceous desserts. Don’t be afraid to embrace the unexpected in your culinary creations. Happy herb-infused baking!
