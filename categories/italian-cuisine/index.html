Italian-Style Baked Ziti with Meatballs and Ricotta Recipe

If you’re looking for a hearty and comforting Italian dish that will impress your family and friends, this Italian-style baked ziti with meatballs and ricotta recipe is just what you need. This classic dish combines tender meatballs, zesty marinara sauce, al dente ziti pasta, and creamy ricotta cheese, all baked to perfection. It’s a crowd-pleaser that’s perfect for any occasion, from casual weeknight dinners to special gatherings. Ingredients: 1 pound ziti pasta 1 pound ground beef 1/2 cup breadcrumbs 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese 1 egg 1 teaspoon garlic powder 1 teaspoon dried oregano 1 teaspoon dried basil Salt and pepper to taste 4 cups marinara sauce 1 1/2 cups ricotta cheese 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese Fresh basil for garnish Instructions: Step 1: Prepare the Meatballs Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C). In a large bowl, combine the ground beef, breadcrumbs, Parmesan cheese, egg, garlic powder, dried oregano, dried basil, salt, and pepper. Mix until well combined. Shape the mixture into meatballs, about 1 inch in diameter, and place them on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper. Bake the meatballs in the preheated oven for 15-20 minutes or until they are cooked through. Step 2: Cook the Ziti While the meatballs are baking, bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Add the ziti pasta and cook according to the package instructions until al dente. Drain the pasta and set it aside. Step 3: Assemble the Baked Ziti In a large bowl, mix the cooked ziti with the marinara sauce until the pasta is well coated. In a greased 9x13 inch baking dish, spread half of the ziti and marinara mixture evenly. Place the cooked meatballs on top of the ziti mixture. Spoon dollops of ricotta cheese over the meatballs. Sprinkle half of the mozzarella cheese over the ricotta. Add the remaining ziti and marinara mixture on top of the cheese layer. Sprinkle the rest of the mozzarella cheese over the ziti. Step 4: Bake the Dish Cover the baking dish with aluminum foil and bake in the preheated oven for 25 minutes. Remove the foil and bake for an additional 10-15 minutes, or until the cheese is bubbly and golden brown. Step 5: Serve and Enjoy Once the baked ziti is done, remove it from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes. Garnish with fresh basil before serving. Serve the Italian-style baked ziti with meatballs and ricotta hot, and watch it disappear in no time! This Italian-style baked ziti with meatballs and ricotta recipe is a delightful combination of flavors and textures that will surely become a favorite in your recipe collection. Whether you’re hosting a casual dinner or a festive gathering, this dish is sure to be a hit. So gather your ingredients, roll up your sleeves, and get ready to create a delicious masterpiece that will have everyone asking for seconds. Buon appetito! ...

Creamy Italian Gorgonzola Sauce Recipe - Bold Cheese Delight

If you’re a cheese lover, then this Creamy Italian Gorgonzola Sauce recipe is a must-try! This bold and flavorful sauce will elevate any dish, whether you’re serving it with pasta, grilled meats, or roasted vegetables. Made with rich and creamy Gorgonzola cheese, this sauce is a true delight for cheese enthusiasts. So, let’s dive right into the recipe and discover the magic of this creamy Italian sauce! Ingredients 1 cup Gorgonzola cheese, crumbled 1 cup heavy cream 2 tablespoons unsalted butter 1 small onion, finely chopped 2 cloves garlic, minced 1/4 cup dry white wine Salt and pepper to taste Instructions In a medium-sized saucepan, melt the butter over medium heat. Add the chopped onion and minced garlic, and sauté until they become soft and translucent. Pour in the white wine and let it simmer for a couple of minutes. This will help to deglaze the pan and infuse the sauce with a delicate flavor. Reduce the heat to low and add the crumbled Gorgonzola cheese to the saucepan. Stir continuously until the cheese melts and blends with the other ingredients. Slowly pour in the heavy cream, stirring constantly to incorporate it into the sauce. Continue cooking over low heat until the sauce thickens and coats the back of a spoon. This will take around 5-7 minutes. Season the sauce with salt and pepper according to your taste preferences. Remember that Gorgonzola cheese has a naturally salty flavor, so be cautious with the amount of salt you add. Remove the sauce from heat and let it cool for a few minutes before serving. The sauce will continue to thicken as it cools down. Serving Suggestions Pasta: Toss this creamy Gorgonzola sauce with your favorite pasta and garnish it with some fresh herbs like thyme or parsley. The combination of the creamy sauce and al dente pasta will create a heavenly dish. Grilled Meats: Drizzle this sauce over grilled chicken, steak, or pork chops to add a burst of flavor. The richness of the Gorgonzola perfectly complements the smoky char from the grill. Roasted Vegetables: Pour the sauce over roasted vegetables like cauliflower, Brussels sprouts, or butternut squash. The creamy and tangy notes of the Gorgonzola will transform simple vegetables into a gourmet side dish. Conclusion Creamy Italian Gorgonzola Sauce is a versatile and indulgent addition to your culinary repertoire. Its bold flavors and creamy texture will elevate any dish to new heights. Whether you’re a cheese aficionado or simply looking to tantalize your taste buds, this sauce is a must-try.
