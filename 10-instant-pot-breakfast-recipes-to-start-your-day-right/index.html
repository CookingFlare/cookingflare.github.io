10 Instant Pot Breakfast Recipes to Start Your Day Right


Mornings can often feel rushed, and finding the time to prepare a hearty, nutritious breakfast that’s both satisfying and delicious can be challenging. Enter the Instant Pot — a kitchen gadget that makes cooking faster, easier, and more convenient. With its pressure-cooking capabilities, the Instant Pot is a fantastic tool for whipping up a variety of breakfast dishes in no time. Whether you’re in the mood for something sweet, savory, or a combination of both, these 10 Instant Pot breakfast recipes will help you start your day the right way.

1. Instant Pot Steel-Cut Oats

There’s no better way to kickstart your morning than with a warm, comforting bowl of oatmeal. Steel-cut oats are hearty, healthy, and full of fiber, but they often take a while to cook on the stove. Luckily, the Instant Pot makes them quick and easy.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup steel-cut oats
  • 4 cups water or milk (or a mix of both)
  • 1 tablespoon butter or coconut oil
  • Pinch of salt
  • Sweetener of choice (maple syrup, honey, or brown sugar)

Instructions:

  1. Add steel-cut oats, water or milk, butter, and salt into the Instant Pot.
  2. Secure the lid and set the valve to “sealing.”
  3. Set the Instant Pot to cook on high pressure for 4 minutes.
  4. Once cooking is complete, allow the pressure to release naturally for about 10 minutes before manually releasing any remaining pressure.
  5. Stir in your sweetener and any other toppings like fresh fruit, nuts, or cinnamon for extra flavor.

This creamy and filling dish will keep you energized throughout the morning.

2. Instant Pot Scrambled Eggs

Scrambled eggs are a classic breakfast option, and using your Instant Pot for them ensures they’re cooked perfectly every time. These eggs come out fluffy and tender, and the best part is that they take mere minutes to make.

Ingredients:

  • 6 large eggs
  • 1/4 cup milk or cream
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 tablespoon butter or oil

Instructions:

  1. Crack the eggs into a mixing bowl and whisk with milk, salt, and pepper until well combined.
  2. Add butter to the Instant Pot insert, then set the Instant Pot to “Sauté” mode. Let the butter melt and coat the bottom of the pot.
  3. Pour the egg mixture into the pot. Stir gently and cook for 1-2 minutes until the eggs start to set.
  4. Turn off the Instant Pot and let the residual heat finish cooking the eggs. Stir occasionally for a smooth texture.

These eggs are an excellent base for adding your favorite mix-ins, such as cheese, spinach, or diced ham.

3. Instant Pot Breakfast Casserole

This breakfast casserole is a one-pot wonder that brings together eggs, sausage, potatoes, cheese, and more. It’s the perfect dish for a busy family morning or meal prepping for the week ahead.

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound breakfast sausage
  • 1/2 onion, diced
  • 2 cups frozen hashbrowns
  • 8 large eggs
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1 1/2 cups shredded cheese (cheddar or your choice)
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Turn the Instant Pot to “Sauté” mode and brown the sausage with diced onion until cooked through.
  2. Layer the frozen hashbrowns over the sausage mixture.
  3. In a separate bowl, whisk together eggs, milk, salt, and pepper. Pour the egg mixture over the hashbrowns.
  4. Sprinkle cheese on top.
  5. Secure the lid and set the Instant Pot to cook on high pressure for 15 minutes. Allow the pressure to release naturally for 10 minutes before releasing any remaining pressure.
  6. Cut into slices and serve hot.

This casserole can be stored in the fridge for up to 4 days, making it an excellent option for meal prepping.

4. Instant Pot Quinoa Porridge

If you’re looking for a gluten-free breakfast option, quinoa porridge is an excellent choice. It’s packed with protein, fiber, and essential vitamins, making it a nutritious way to start the day.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup quinoa, rinsed
  • 2 cups almond milk (or your preferred milk)
  • 1 tablespoon maple syrup or honey
  • 1 teaspoon cinnamon
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • Toppings: fresh fruit, nuts, or seeds

Instructions:

  1. Add quinoa, almond milk, maple syrup, cinnamon, and vanilla extract to the Instant Pot.
  2. Seal the lid and set the Instant Pot to cook on high pressure for 1 minute.
  3. Let the pressure naturally release for 10 minutes before releasing any remaining pressure manually.
  4. Stir the quinoa and serve with your favorite toppings.

This creamy, nutrient-dense porridge is a delightful alternative to oatmeal.

5. Instant Pot French Toast Casserole

French toast is a beloved breakfast dish, but making it in casserole form in the Instant Pot is a great way to serve a crowd with minimal effort. This recipe combines fluffy bread, eggs, and a rich cinnamon-sugar topping that’s bound to please everyone.

Ingredients:

  • 1 loaf of challah or brioche bread, cubed
  • 6 large eggs
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • 1/4 cup maple syrup
  • 1 teaspoon cinnamon
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/2 cup brown sugar

Instructions:

  1. Grease the Instant Pot insert with butter or cooking spray. Layer the bread cubes in the pot.
  2. In a mixing bowl, whisk together eggs, milk, heavy cream, maple syrup, cinnamon, and vanilla extract. Pour the mixture evenly over the bread cubes.
  3. Sprinkle brown sugar over the top.
  4. Secure the lid and set the Instant Pot to cook on high pressure for 25 minutes. Allow for a natural pressure release for about 10 minutes.
  5. Remove the casserole, serve hot, and drizzle with extra maple syrup if desired.

6. Instant Pot Breakfast Burrito Filling

Breakfast burritos are a versatile and filling option, and the Instant Pot makes it easy to prepare a flavorful filling in no time. This recipe combines scrambled eggs, sausage, and vegetables, ready to be wrapped in a warm tortilla.

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound breakfast sausage
  • 6 large eggs
  • 1/2 bell pepper, diced
  • 1/2 onion, diced
  • 1/2 cup shredded cheese (cheddar or Monterey Jack)
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Turn the Instant Pot to “Sauté” mode and cook the sausage until browned. Remove the sausage and set aside.
  2. Sauté the bell pepper and onion in the Instant Pot until soft.
  3. Add the sausage back into the pot, then whisk the eggs and pour them in.
  4. Stir gently until the eggs are cooked through.
  5. Sprinkle cheese on top and let it melt before serving.

Use this filling to create breakfast burritos with your favorite toppings like salsa, sour cream, or avocado.

7. Instant Pot Sweet Potato Breakfast Hash

This savory breakfast hash is the perfect blend of sweet and savory flavors, with tender sweet potatoes, savory sausage, and eggs. It’s an easy, one-pot breakfast that’s both nutritious and delicious.

Ingredients:

  • 2 medium sweet potatoes, peeled and diced
  • 1/2 pound breakfast sausage
  • 1/2 onion, diced
  • 4 large eggs
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Turn the Instant Pot to “Sauté” mode and cook the sausage until browned.
  2. Add the diced sweet potatoes and onion to the pot, and sauté for 5-7 minutes until the sweet potatoes start to soften.
  3. Season with paprika, salt, and pepper.
  4. Create small wells in the hash and crack the eggs into each well.
  5. Seal the lid and set the Instant Pot to cook on high pressure for 5 minutes.
  6. Release the pressure and serve hot.

This is a hearty, flavorful breakfast that’s perfect for fueling your day.

8. Instant Pot Banana Bread

If you’re craving something a bit sweeter for breakfast, banana bread made in the Instant Pot is the perfect treat. This moist, delicious bread is perfect with a cup of coffee.

Ingredients:

  • 3 ripe bananas, mashed
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 2 cups flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Instructions:

  1. Grease a 7-inch cake pan that fits inside your Instant Pot.
  2. Mix the mashed bananas, eggs, sugar, milk, flour, baking soda, salt, and vanilla extract in a bowl.
  3. Pour the batter into the prepared cake pan.
  4. Add 1 cup of water to the Instant Pot insert and place a trivet in the pot.
  5. Set the pan on top of the trivet, secure the lid, and set the Instant Pot to cook on high pressure for 35 minutes.
  6. Allow the pressure to release naturally before removing the banana bread from the pot.

9. Instant Pot Chia Pudding

Chia pudding is a great make-ahead breakfast option that’s both nutritious and satisfying. With the Instant Pot, you can quickly prepare the chia seeds and let them soak up the liquid to create a creamy, pudding-like texture.

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 cup chia seeds
  • 2 cups almond milk
  • 1 tablespoon maple syrup
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • Toppings: berries, nuts, or coconut flakes

Instructions:

  1. Add chia seeds, almond milk, maple syrup, and vanilla extract to the Instant Pot.
  2. Stir well and secure the lid.
  3. Set the Instant Pot to “Sauté” mode for 2-3 minutes to heat the mixture.
  4. Let the pudding cool slightly before refrigerating overnight.
  5. Serve with fresh berries and your choice of toppings.

This make-ahead breakfast is ideal for busy mornings when you need something quick and healthy.

10. Instant Pot Baked Apples

For a warm, comforting breakfast that feels like dessert, baked apples in the Instant Pot are a great option. They’re sweet, soft, and full of cinnamon and spice, making them the perfect treat to enjoy in the morning.

Ingredients:

  • 4 medium apples, cored
  • 1/4 cup maple syrup
  • 1 teaspoon cinnamon
  • 1/4 teaspoon nutmeg
  • 1/4 cup water

Instructions:

  1. Place the apples in the Instant Pot insert.
  2. Drizzle the maple syrup over the apples and sprinkle with cinnamon and nutmeg.
  3. Pour the water into the bottom of the pot.
  4. Set the Instant Pot to cook on high pressure for 5 minutes.
  5. Let the pressure release naturally for 5 minutes before manually releasing the remaining pressure.

Serve these baked apples with a dollop of yogurt or a sprinkle of granola for an extra special touch.

Conclusion

The Instant Pot is an incredibly versatile kitchen appliance that can transform your breakfast routine. From sweet oatmeal to savory scrambles, casseroles, and even baked goods, it offers endless possibilities for quick and easy morning meals. These 10 recipes are a great starting point for your Instant Pot breakfast adventures, helping you save time and effort while still enjoying nutritious, flavorful dishes.
