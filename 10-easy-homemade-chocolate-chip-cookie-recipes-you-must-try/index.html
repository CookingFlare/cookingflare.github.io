10 Easy Homemade Chocolate Chip Cookie Recipes You Must Try


Chocolate chip cookies are a beloved classic that never goes out of style. Whether you’re baking for a family gathering, a holiday celebration, or just because you’re craving something sweet, nothing beats a warm, fresh batch of homemade cookies. The chewy texture, the melt-in-your-mouth chocolate, and the perfect balance of sweetness make these cookies an irresistible treat for all ages. If you’re looking for a few creative and easy recipes to elevate your cookie game, you’re in the right place.

Here are 10 delightful chocolate chip cookie recipes that you can make in the comfort of your kitchen. From traditional to unique variations, these recipes will bring out the baker in you.

1. Classic Chocolate Chip Cookies

Ingredients:

  • 2 1/4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 cup packed brown sugar
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 cups semisweet chocolate chips
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C). Line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
  2. In a medium bowl, whisk together the flour, baking soda, and salt. Set aside.
  3. In a large bowl, cream the softened butter with the granulated sugar and brown sugar until light and fluffy.
  4. Beat in the eggs one at a time, then stir in the vanilla extract.
  5. Gradually mix the dry ingredients into the wet ingredients until combined.
  6. Fold in the chocolate chips.
  7. Drop rounded tablespoons of dough onto the prepared baking sheet, spacing them about 2 inches apart.
  8. Bake for 10-12 minutes, or until the edges are golden brown but the centers are soft.
  9. Cool on the baking sheet for a few minutes before transferring to a wire rack to cool completely.

This recipe is a timeless favorite, and it delivers the perfect balance of crispy edges and chewy centers that make these cookies so special.

2. Chewy Chocolate Chip Cookies

Ingredients:

  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 3/4 cup brown sugar, packed
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 large egg
  • 1 1/2 cups semi-sweet chocolate chips

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C). Line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
  2. In a medium bowl, combine the flour, baking soda, and salt.
  3. In a separate large bowl, cream together the butter, brown sugar, and granulated sugar until smooth and creamy.
  4. Beat in the egg and vanilla extract.
  5. Gradually mix in the dry ingredients until just combined.
  6. Stir in the chocolate chips.
  7. Drop rounded tablespoons of dough onto the prepared baking sheet.
  8. Bake for 9-11 minutes, or until golden brown around the edges but still soft in the center.
  9. Let the cookies cool on the baking sheet for a few minutes before transferring to a wire rack.

For those who prefer their cookies soft and chewy with a slightly underdone center, this recipe is a winner!

3. Gluten-Free Chocolate Chip Cookies

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups gluten-free all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1/2 cup brown sugar
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 cups gluten-free chocolate chips

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C). Line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
  2. In a medium bowl, whisk together the gluten-free flour, baking soda, and salt.
  3. In a large bowl, beat together the softened butter, brown sugar, and granulated sugar until creamy.
  4. Add the eggs and vanilla extract, mixing until fully combined.
  5. Gradually add the dry ingredients to the wet ingredients and stir until well incorporated.
  6. Fold in the chocolate chips.
  7. Drop rounded tablespoons of dough onto the baking sheet, spacing them apart.
  8. Bake for 10-12 minutes, or until golden brown.
  9. Allow the cookies to cool on the baking sheet before transferring them to a wire rack.

This gluten-free version proves that you don’t need gluten to make delicious, soft chocolate chip cookies. It’s a great alternative for those with dietary restrictions without compromising on taste.

4. Double Chocolate Chip Cookies

Ingredients:

  • 1 3/4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 3/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 cup brown sugar, packed
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 1/2 cups milk chocolate chips
  • 1/2 cup dark chocolate chips

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C) and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
  2. In a medium bowl, whisk together the flour, cocoa powder, baking soda, and salt.
  3. In a large mixing bowl, cream together the butter, granulated sugar, and brown sugar.
  4. Add the eggs one at a time, then stir in the vanilla extract.
  5. Gradually add the dry ingredients to the wet mixture until well incorporated.
  6. Fold in the milk and dark chocolate chips.
  7. Drop rounded tablespoons of dough onto the prepared baking sheet.
  8. Bake for 10-12 minutes, or until the cookies are set around the edges but still soft in the middle.
  9. Allow the cookies to cool on the baking sheet for a few minutes before transferring them to a wire rack.

For chocolate lovers, this double chocolate chip cookie recipe is an indulgent treat. The combination of cocoa powder and two types of chocolate chips takes these cookies to the next level.

5. Peanut Butter Chocolate Chip Cookies

Ingredients:

  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1/2 cup peanut butter
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/2 cup brown sugar, packed
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 large egg
  • 1 1/2 cups chocolate chips

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C). Line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
  2. In a small bowl, whisk together the flour, baking soda, and salt.
  3. In a large bowl, beat together the butter, peanut butter, granulated sugar, and brown sugar until smooth and creamy.
  4. Add the egg and vanilla extract and mix until fully combined.
  5. Gradually add the dry ingredients to the wet mixture and mix until just incorporated.
  6. Stir in the chocolate chips.
  7. Drop rounded tablespoons of dough onto the prepared baking sheet.
  8. Bake for 9-11 minutes, or until golden around the edges.
  9. Cool the cookies on the baking sheet for a few minutes before transferring to a wire rack.

The peanut butter and chocolate combination is a match made in heaven. These cookies are rich, flavorful, and perfect for those who enjoy a little extra indulgence in their dessert.

7. Salted Caramel Chocolate Chip Cookies

Ingredients:

  • 1 3/4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 cup brown sugar, packed
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 1/2 cups chocolate chips
  • 1/2 cup caramel bits
  • 1/4 teaspoon sea salt (for sprinkling)

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C). Line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
  2. In a medium bowl, whisk together the flour, baking soda, and salt.
  3. In a large bowl, cream the butter, granulated sugar, and brown sugar together until smooth.
  4. Beat in the eggs one at a time and then stir in the vanilla extract.
  5. Gradually add the dry ingredients to the wet ingredients and mix until well combined.
  6. Stir in the chocolate chips and caramel bits.
  7. Drop rounded tablespoons of dough onto the baking sheet.
  8. Sprinkle each cookie with a pinch of sea salt.
  9. Bake for 10-12 minutes, or until golden brown.
  10. Cool the cookies on the baking sheet for a few minutes before transferring them to a wire rack.

The touch of sea salt combined with rich caramel and chocolate creates a sophisticated yet comforting cookie. These salted caramel chocolate chip cookies are a unique twist on the classic.

8. Vegan Chocolate Chip Cookies

Ingredients:

  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup coconut oil, melted
  • 1/2 cup brown sugar, packed
  • 1/4 cup maple syrup
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/4 cup non-dairy milk (such as almond milk)
  • 1 1/2 cups dairy-free chocolate chips

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C). Line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
  2. In a medium bowl, whisk together the flour, baking soda, and salt.
  3. In a large bowl, whisk together the melted coconut oil, brown sugar, maple syrup, vanilla extract, and non-dairy milk.
  4. Gradually add the dry ingredients to the wet mixture and stir until combined.
  5. Fold in the dairy-free chocolate chips.
  6. Drop rounded tablespoons of dough onto the baking sheet.
  7. Bake for 9-11 minutes, or until the edges are golden brown.
  8. Let the cookies cool on the baking sheet before transferring to a wire rack.

This vegan chocolate chip cookie recipe is just as delicious as the traditional version, without any dairy. Perfect for those following a plant-based lifestyle!

9. White Chocolate Macadamia Nut Cookies

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 cup brown sugar, packed
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 1/2 cups white chocolate chips
  • 1 cup macadamia nuts, coarsely chopped

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C). Line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
  2. In a medium bowl, whisk together the flour, baking soda, and salt.
  3. In a large bowl, beat the butter, granulated sugar, and brown sugar until creamy.
  4. Add the eggs and vanilla extract, mixing until combined.
  5. Gradually add the dry ingredients and stir until just mixed.
  6. Fold in the white chocolate chips and chopped macadamia nuts.
  7. Drop rounded tablespoons of dough onto the baking sheet.
  8. Bake for 10-12 minutes, or until the cookies are golden brown.
  9. Allow the cookies to cool on the baking sheet before transferring them to a wire rack.

This decadent cookie features the creamy sweetness of white chocolate paired with the rich, buttery flavor of macadamia nuts. A wonderful twist for anyone looking for a different take on chocolate chip cookies.

  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda

  4. Add the eggs and vanilla extract, mixing well.
  5. Gradually add the dry ingredients, mixing until combined.
  6. Fold in the chocolate chips and rainbow sprinkles.
  7. Drop rounded tablespoons of dough onto the baking sheet.
  8. Bake for 10-12 minutes, or until the edges are golden brown.
  9. Cool on the baking sheet for a few minutes before transferring to a wire rack.

These colorful cookies are perfect for celebrations or any time you want a cheerful treat. The combination of chocolate chips and sprinkles makes these cookies as fun as they are tasty.
