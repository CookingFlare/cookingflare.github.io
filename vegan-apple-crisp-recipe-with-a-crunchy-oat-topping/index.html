Vegan Apple Crisp Recipe with a Crunchy Oat Topping


Are you craving a warm and comforting dessert that’s vegan-friendly and bursting with the flavors of fresh apples? Look no further than this delicious Vegan Apple Crisp recipe with a Crunchy Oat Topping. It’s the perfect dessert to enjoy during the cozy autumn months or any time you want a sweet treat that’s both satisfying and guilt-free.

Ingredients

For the Apple Filling:

  • 6 medium-sized apples, peeled, cored, and sliced
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 1/4 cup maple syrup
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground nutmeg

For the Crunchy Oat Topping:

  • 1 cup rolled oats
  • 1/2 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup brown sugar
  • 1/4 cup coconut oil, melted
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).

  2. In a large bowl, combine the sliced apples, lemon juice, maple syrup, cinnamon, and nutmeg. Toss gently until the apples are well coated in the mixture.

  3. Transfer the apple mixture to a 9x9-inch baking dish or any similar-sized oven-safe dish.

  4. In a separate bowl, mix together the rolled oats, all-purpose flour, brown sugar, melted coconut oil, cinnamon, and salt. Stir until the ingredients are well combined and form a crumbly texture.

  5. Sprinkle the oat topping evenly over the apple mixture, covering it completely.

  6. Place the baking dish in the preheated oven and bake for 40-45 minutes, or until the apples are tender and the topping is golden brown.

  7. Remove the apple crisp from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes before serving.

  8. Serve the Vegan Apple Crisp warm, either on its own or with a scoop of dairy-free vanilla ice cream or a dollop of coconut whipped cream. Enjoy the perfect balance of sweet, tart, and crunchy flavors!

Tips and Variations

  • Feel free to experiment with different types of apples for this recipe. Tart apples like Granny Smith or Honeycrisp work well, but you can also use sweeter varieties like Gala or Fuji.

  • For an extra touch of flavor, add a handful of raisins, dried cranberries, or chopped nuts to the apple filling before baking.

  • If you prefer a gluten-free version of this recipe, simply use a gluten-free all-purpose flour blend instead of regular flour.

  • To make this Vegan Apple Crisp ahead of time, assemble the apple filling and oat topping separately. Store them in separate airtight containers in the refrigerator. When you’re ready to bake, simply combine the two and follow the baking instructions as usual.

Conclusion

This Vegan Apple Crisp recipe with a Crunchy Oat Topping is a delightful dessert that will satisfy your sweet tooth and leave you feeling warm and cozy. It’s a perfect combination of tender apples, aromatic spices, and a crispy oat topping that will make your taste buds dance with joy. Whether you’re vegan or not, this dessert is sure to become a favorite in your recipe collection. So go ahead, grab some fresh apples, and get ready to indulge in this delicious treat!
