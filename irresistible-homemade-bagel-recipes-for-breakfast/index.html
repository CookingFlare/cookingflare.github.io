Irresistible Homemade Bagel Recipes for Breakfast


There’s nothing quite like the smell of fresh bagels wafting through the kitchen in the morning. Whether you prefer them plain, topped with seeds, or smothered in cream cheese, homemade bagels are a delightful treat for breakfast. In this blog post, we’ll explore some irresistible homemade bagel recipes that will elevate your breakfast game and have your family and friends coming back for more.

Classic Plain Bagels

Ingredients:

  • 4 cups bread flour
  • 1 tablespoon sugar
  • 1 ½ teaspoons salt
  • 1 tablespoon instant yeast
  • 1 ¼ cups warm water
  • 1 tablespoon honey
  • 1 egg white
  • Toppings of your choice (poppy seeds, sesame seeds, etc.)

Instructions:

  1. In a large bowl, mix the flour, sugar, salt, and yeast.
  2. Add the warm water and honey to the dry ingredients and mix until a dough forms.
  3. Knead the dough on a floured surface for about 10 minutes, or until it becomes smooth and elastic.
  4. Divide the dough into 8 equal parts and shape each into a ball. Use your finger to poke a hole through the center and stretch it to form a ring.
  5. Let the bagels rise for about 20 minutes.
  6. Preheat your oven to 425°F (220°C).
  7. Bring a large pot of water to a boil and carefully place the bagels in the water for 1 minute, flipping them halfway through.
  8. Remove the bagels from the water using a slotted spoon and place them on a baking sheet. Brush each bagel with egg white and sprinkle with your desired toppings.
  9. Bake for 20-25 minutes, or until golden brown.

Cinnamon Raisin Bagels

Ingredients:

  • 4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 ¼ teaspoons instant yeast
  • 1 ½ teaspoons salt
  • 1 ½ cups warm water
  • ¼ cup brown sugar
  • 1 ½ teaspoons ground cinnamon
  • ¾ cup raisins

Instructions:

  1. In a large bowl, combine the flour, yeast, and salt.
  2. Add the warm water and brown sugar to the dry ingredients and mix until a shaggy dough forms.
  3. Knead the dough on a floured surface for 10-15 minutes, incorporating the raisins and cinnamon as you go.
  4. Divide the dough into 12 equal parts and shape each into a ball. Use your finger to poke a hole through the center and stretch it to form a ring.
  5. Let the bagels rise for about 30 minutes.
  6. Preheat your oven to 425°F (220°C).
  7. Bring a large pot of water to a boil and carefully place the bagels in the water for 1 minute, flipping them halfway through.
  8. Remove the bagels from the water using a slotted spoon and place them on a baking sheet.
  9. Bake for 20-25 minutes, or until golden brown.

Everything Bagels

Ingredients:

  • 4 cups bread flour
  • 1 tablespoon sugar
  • 1 ½ teaspoons salt
  • 1 tablespoon instant yeast
  • 1 ¼ cups warm water
  • 1 tablespoon honey
  • 1 egg white
  • Toppings: poppy seeds, sesame seeds, dried minced garlic, dried minced onion, and coarse salt

Instructions:

  1. Follow the same instructions as for the classic plain bagels recipe, but instead of using a single topping, combine all the toppings in a shallow dish and dip the bagels into the mixture after brushing with egg white.

Conclusion

Homemade bagels are a labor of love, but the results are undeniably worth it. The aroma of fresh bagels baking in the oven is enough to make anyone’s morning brighter. Whether you stick to the classic plain bagels, indulge in the sweetness of cinnamon raisin, or opt for the savory delight of everything bagels, the satisfaction of biting into a warm, homemade bagel is unmatched. So, roll up your sleeves, put on your apron, and get ready to enjoy the delightful process of making your own irresistible bagels for breakfast.
