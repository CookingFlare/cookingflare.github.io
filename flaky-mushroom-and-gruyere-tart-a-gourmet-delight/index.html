Flaky Mushroom and Gruyere Tart: A Gourmet Delight


If you’re looking for a show-stopping appetizer or a delicious main course, then look no further than this Flaky Mushroom and Gruyere Tart. With its buttery, flaky crust and a rich, savory filling, this tart is sure to impress even the most discerning palates.

Ingredients

For the crust:

  • 2 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup unsalted butter, chilled and cut into small cubes
  • 1/4 to 1/2 cup ice water

For the filling:

  • 1 pound mushrooms, sliced
  • 2 tablespoons unsalted butter
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon fresh thyme leaves
  • 1 cup Gruyere cheese, grated
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Prepare the crust

    • In a large mixing bowl, whisk together the flour and salt.
    • Add the cold butter cubes to the flour mixture and use a pastry cutter or your fingertips to cut the butter into the flour until it resembles coarse crumbs.
    • Gradually add the ice water, 1 tablespoon at a time, and mix until the dough comes together. Be careful not to overmix.
    • Divide the dough in half, shape each half into a disk, and wrap them in plastic wrap. Refrigerate for at least 30 minutes.

  2. Prepare the filling

    • In a large skillet, melt the butter over medium heat. Add the sliced mushrooms and sauté until they are golden brown and any liquid has evaporated, about 10 minutes.
    • Add the minced garlic and thyme leaves to the skillet and sauté for an additional 2 minutes.
    • Remove the skillet from heat and let the mushroom mixture cool slightly.
    • In a separate bowl, combine the grated Gruyere cheese and heavy cream. Season with salt and pepper to taste.

  3. Assemble the tart

    • Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
    • On a lightly floured surface, roll out one of the dough disks to fit a 9-inch tart pan. Press the dough into the pan, ensuring it covers the bottom and sides evenly.
    • Spread the cooled mushroom mixture evenly over the tart crust.
    • Pour the Gruyere and cream mixture over the mushrooms, spreading it out to cover the filling completely.
    • Roll out the second dough disk and cut it into thin strips. Create a lattice pattern over the top of the tart.
    • Trim any excess dough and crimp the edges to seal the tart.
    • Place the tart on a baking sheet and bake for 35-40 minutes, or until the crust is golden brown and the filling is set.

  4. Serve and enjoy

    • Allow the tart to cool for a few minutes before slicing and serving.
    • This Flaky Mushroom and Gruyere Tart can be served warm or at room temperature, making it a versatile dish for any occasion.
    • Garnish with fresh thyme leaves or a sprinkle of grated Gruyere for an extra touch of elegance.
    • Pair it with a crisp green salad or a glass of your favorite wine for a complete and satisfying meal.

Conclusion

With its buttery, flaky crust, earthy mushrooms, and creamy Gruyere filling, this Flaky Mushroom and Gruyere Tart is a gourmet delight that will impress your family and friends. Whether you serve it as an appetizer or a main course, this tart is sure to become a favorite in your culinary repertoire. So go ahead, unleash your inner chef, and indulge in this delicious and elegant dish. Bon appétit!
