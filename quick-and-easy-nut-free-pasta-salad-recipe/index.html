Quick and Easy Nut-Free Pasta Salad Recipe


Are you looking for a delicious and nutritious pasta salad recipe that is not only quick and easy but also nut-free? Look no further! This recipe is perfect for those who have nut allergies or simply want to enjoy a nut-free meal. Packed with fresh vegetables, flavorful herbs, and a tangy dressing, this pasta salad will surely become your go-to dish for picnics, potlucks, or even a light lunch.

Ingredients

  • 8 ounces of pasta (your choice of shape)
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1 cup cucumber, diced
  • 1/2 cup red bell pepper, diced
  • 1/2 cup yellow bell pepper, diced
  • 1/4 cup red onion, finely chopped
  • 1/4 cup black olives, sliced
  • 1/4 cup feta cheese, crumbled
  • 2 tablespoons fresh parsley, chopped
  • 2 tablespoons fresh basil, chopped

Dressing

  • 3 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons red wine vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon Dijon mustard
  • 1 garlic clove, minced
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Cook the pasta according to the package instructions until al dente. Drain and rinse with cold water to stop the cooking process. Set aside.

  2. In a large bowl, combine the cherry tomatoes, cucumber, red and yellow bell peppers, red onion, black olives, feta cheese, parsley, and basil. Toss gently to mix the ingredients evenly.

  3. In a small bowl, whisk together the olive oil, red wine vinegar, Dijon mustard, minced garlic, salt, and pepper until well combined. The dressing should have a tangy and slightly creamy consistency.

  4. Pour the dressing over the vegetable mixture and toss gently to coat all the ingredients with the dressing.

  5. Add the cooked pasta to the bowl and toss again to combine. Make sure the pasta is well coated with the dressing and mixed with the vegetables.

  6. Let the pasta salad sit in the refrigerator for at least 30 minutes to allow the flavors to meld together. This will also give the pasta salad a refreshing chill.

  7. Once chilled, give the pasta salad a final toss before serving. You can sprinkle some additional fresh herbs on top for garnish, if desired.

Serving Suggestions

This nut-free pasta salad is incredibly versatile and can be enjoyed on its own as a light and refreshing meal. However, if you’re looking to make it a heartier dish, feel free to add grilled chicken, shrimp, or even roasted vegetables.

You can also serve this pasta salad as a side dish alongside grilled meats or fish. It pairs perfectly with barbecued chicken, steak, or salmon.

For an extra burst of flavor, you can squeeze some fresh lemon juice over the pasta salad just before serving. It adds a tangy and citrusy kick that complements the vegetables and herbs beautifully.

Conclusion

This quick and easy nut-free pasta salad recipe is a winner in terms of taste, convenience, and allergy-friendliness. With its vibrant colors, refreshing flavors, and simple preparation, it’s bound to become a staple in your recipe repertoire. So go ahead and give it a try – your taste buds will thank you!
