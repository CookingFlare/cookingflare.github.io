A Twist on Tradition: Homemade Roasted Garlic Dressing

Are you tired of the same old dressings? Looking for a way to add a twist to your salads? Look no further! In this recipe, we’ll show you how to make a delicious homemade roasted garlic dressing that will elevate your salads to a whole new level. With its rich and robust flavor, this dressing is sure to become a staple in your kitchen. Ingredients 1 whole head of garlic 2 tablespoons olive oil 1 cup plain Greek yogurt 2 tablespoons lemon juice 1 tablespoon Dijon mustard 1 tablespoon honey Salt and pepper to taste Instructions Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C). Take the whole head of garlic and cut off the top to expose the cloves. Place the garlic on a sheet of aluminum foil and drizzle with olive oil. Wrap the garlic in the foil and place it in the preheated oven. Roast for about 40 minutes, or until the garlic cloves are soft and golden brown. Once roasted, remove the garlic from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes. Squeeze the roasted garlic cloves out of their skins into a small bowl. In a separate bowl, whisk together the Greek yogurt, lemon juice, Dijon mustard, and honey until well combined. Add the roasted garlic to the yogurt mixture and mix well. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Transfer the dressing to a jar or airtight container and refrigerate for at least 1 hour to allow the flavors to meld together. Serve over your favorite salad and enjoy! Why Roasted Garlic Dressing? Roasted garlic adds a depth of flavor to this dressing that you won’t find in store-bought options. The roasting process brings out the natural sweetness of the garlic, resulting in a mellow and slightly caramelized flavor. Combined with the tanginess of the Greek yogurt, the brightness of the lemon juice, and the subtle kick of Dijon mustard, this dressing is a flavor explosion in every bite. ...
