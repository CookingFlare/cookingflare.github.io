5 Easy Steps to Perfect Chocolate Chip Ice Cream


Who doesn’t love a creamy and delicious scoop of homemade ice cream? And when it comes to classic flavors, chocolate chip ice cream is hard to beat. The combination of rich chocolate and smooth vanilla is a match made in dessert heaven. In this recipe, I’ll walk you through five easy steps to create the perfect batch of chocolate chip ice cream right in your own kitchen.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups heavy cream
  • 1 cup whole milk
  • 3/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 4 egg yolks
  • 2 teaspoons pure vanilla extract
  • 1/2 cup chocolate chips

Step 1: Prepare the Base

In a medium saucepan, combine the heavy cream and milk. Heat the mixture over medium heat until it begins to steam, but make sure it doesn’t boil. While the cream and milk are heating up, whisk together the sugar and egg yolks in a separate bowl until well combined.

Step 2: Temper the Eggs

Once the cream and milk mixture is steaming, slowly pour about 1/2 cup of it into the bowl with the sugar and egg yolks, whisking constantly. This step is called tempering the eggs, and it ensures that they don’t scramble when added to the hot liquid. Continue to whisk the egg mixture while gradually adding the remaining hot cream and milk.

Step 3: Cook the Base

Pour the mixture back into the saucepan and place it over medium-low heat. Stir the base constantly with a wooden spoon or heatproof spatula until it thickens slightly and coats the back of the spoon. This should take about 5-7 minutes. Be careful not to let the mixture boil.

Step 4: Chill and Chill

Remove the saucepan from the heat and stir in the vanilla extract. Transfer the base to a heatproof bowl and cover it with plastic wrap, making sure the wrap touches the surface of the base to prevent a skin from forming. Refrigerate the mixture for at least 4 hours or preferably overnight to allow the flavors to meld and the base to chill completely.

Step 5: Churn and Mix

Once the base is thoroughly chilled, it’s time to churn the ice cream. Follow the instructions on your ice cream maker to churn the base until it reaches a soft-serve consistency. This usually takes about 20-25 minutes. During the last few minutes of churning, add the chocolate chips and let them mix in.

Final Touch: Freeze and Enjoy

Transfer the churned ice cream to an airtight container, making sure to distribute the chocolate chips evenly throughout. Place the container in the freezer for at least 4 hours or until the ice cream is firm. Now, scoop out a generous portion of your homemade chocolate chip ice cream, grab a spoon, and indulge in every creamy bite!

Conclusion

Creating your own batch of chocolate chip ice cream is easier than you might think. With just a few simple steps, you can enjoy a velvety smooth and delicious treat that will rival any store-bought version. So why not give it a try? Gather your ingredients, follow the steps, and savor the sweet rewards of your homemade chocolate chip ice cream. Your taste buds will thank you!
