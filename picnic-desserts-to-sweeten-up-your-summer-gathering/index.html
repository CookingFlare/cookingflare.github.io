Picnic Desserts to Sweeten Up Your Summer Gathering


As the warm summer days roll in, there’s no better way to enjoy the sunshine than with a delightful picnic outdoors. And what’s a picnic without some delicious desserts to sweeten up your gathering? Whether you’re planning a picnic in the park, at the beach, or in your backyard, these mouthwatering dessert recipes are sure to be a hit with your family and friends.

Lemon Blueberry Bars

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup flour
  • 1/2 cup powdered sugar
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • Zest of 1 lemon
  • 2 tablespoons lemon juice
  • 1 cup fresh blueberries

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C) and grease a 9x9-inch baking pan.
  2. In a bowl, combine the flour, powdered sugar, and butter to form a crumbly mixture. Press into the prepared pan and bake for 15 minutes.
  3. In another bowl, whisk together the sugar, eggs, baking powder, salt, lemon zest, and lemon juice until well combined.
  4. Gently fold in the blueberries and pour the mixture over the baked crust.
  5. Bake for an additional 25-30 minutes until the top is golden brown. Let it cool before slicing into bars.

Strawberry Shortcake Skewers

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound fresh strawberries, hulled
  • 1 cup whipped cream
  • 1 package store-bought pound cake, cut into cubes
  • Wooden skewers

Instructions:

  1. Thread a strawberry, a piece of pound cake, and another strawberry onto each skewer.
  2. Repeat the process for the remaining skewers.
  3. Serve the strawberry shortcake skewers with a dollop of whipped cream for dipping.

Watermelon Lime Sorbet

Ingredients:

  • 4 cups cubed seedless watermelon
  • 1/2 cup fresh lime juice
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1/4 cup water
  • Mint leaves for garnish

Instructions:

  1. In a blender, combine the watermelon, lime juice, sugar, and water. Blend until smooth.
  2. Pour the mixture into a shallow dish and freeze for 4-6 hours, stirring every hour to break up ice crystals.
  3. Scoop the sorbet into bowls, garnish with mint leaves, and serve chilled.

No-Bake Chocolate Peanut Butter Bars

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup creamy peanut butter
  • 1/2 cup honey
  • 1/2 cup coconut oil
  • 2 cups rolled oats
  • 1/4 cup cocoa powder
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • Pinch of salt

Instructions:

  1. In a saucepan, melt the peanut butter, honey, and coconut oil over low heat.
  2. Stir in the oats, cocoa powder, vanilla extract, and salt until well combined.
  3. Press the mixture into a lined pan and refrigerate for 2-3 hours until set. Cut into bars and enjoy!

These delightful picnic desserts are not only easy to make but also perfect for enjoying under the sun. Whether you’re a fan of fruity treats or indulgent chocolatey delights, there’s something for everyone to savor at your summer gathering. So pack your picnic basket, grab a blanket, and treat yourself to these sweet delights that will surely make your outdoor experience even more enjoyable!
