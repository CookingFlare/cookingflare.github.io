Delectable Thanksgiving Cornbread Recipes


Thanksgiving is a time for gathering with loved ones and celebrating gratitude, and what better way to do that than with a delectable cornbread dish? Cornbread has long been a staple at Thanksgiving dinners, adding a warm and comforting touch to the feast. In this blog post, we will explore three mouthwatering cornbread recipes that are sure to impress your guests. So, roll up your sleeves, put on your apron, and let’s get cooking!

1. Classic Cornbread

Preparation time: 15 minutes Cooking time: 25 minutes Total time: 40 minutes Serves: 8

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup cornmeal
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 tablespoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 cup sugar
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1/4 cup butter, melted
  • 2 large eggs

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C) and grease a baking dish.
  2. In a large bowl, whisk together the cornmeal, flour, baking powder, salt, and sugar.
  3. In a separate bowl, mix together the milk, melted butter, and eggs.
  4. Pour the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients and stir until just combined.
  5. Pour the batter into the prepared baking dish and smooth the top.
  6. Bake for 20-25 minutes or until golden brown and a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.
  7. Allow the cornbread to cool for a few minutes before serving.

2. Jalapeno and Cheddar Cornbread Muffins

Preparation time: 15 minutes Cooking time: 20 minutes Total time: 35 minutes Serves: 12

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup cornmeal
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 tablespoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 cup sugar
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1/4 cup butter, melted
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • 2 jalapeno peppers, seeded and finely chopped

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C) and line a muffin tin with liners or grease it.
  2. In a large bowl, whisk together the cornmeal, flour, baking powder, salt, and sugar.
  3. In a separate bowl, mix together the milk, melted butter, and eggs.
  4. Pour the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients and stir until just combined.
  5. Fold in the shredded cheddar cheese and chopped jalapeno peppers.
  6. Spoon the batter into the prepared muffin tin, filling each cup about 3/4 full.
  7. Bake for 15-20 minutes or until golden brown and a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.
  8. Allow the muffins to cool for a few minutes before serving.

3. Maple Pecan Cornbread

Preparation time: 15 minutes Cooking time: 25 minutes Total time: 40 minutes Serves: 8

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup cornmeal
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 tablespoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 cup sugar
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1/4 cup butter, melted
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1/2 cup chopped pecans
  • 1/4 cup maple syrup

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C) and grease a baking dish.
  2. In a large bowl, whisk together the cornmeal, flour, baking powder, salt, and sugar.
  3. In a separate bowl, mix together the milk, melted butter, and eggs.
  4. Pour the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients and stir until just combined.
  5. Fold in the chopped pecans.
  6. Pour half of the batter into the prepared baking dish.
  7. Drizzle the maple syrup over the batter, then pour the remaining batter on top.
  8. Bake for 20-25 minutes or until golden brown and a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.
  9. Allow the cornbread to cool for a few minutes before serving.

Conclusion

Cornbread is a versatile and delicious addition to any Thanksgiving feast. Whether you prefer the classic version, spicy jalapeno and cheddar muffins, or the sweet and nutty maple pecan cornbread, there’s a recipe here to satisfy everyone’s taste buds. So, this Thanksgiving, take your cornbread game to the next level and impress your guests with these delectable recipes. Happy cooking and happy Thanksgiving!
