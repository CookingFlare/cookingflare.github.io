Spring Brunch: Recipes with Seasonal Herbs and Cheese


Springtime brings a burst of fresh flavors, and what better way to celebrate than with a delightful spring brunch? Incorporating seasonal herbs and cheese into your dishes adds a unique twist that will impress your guests and tantalize your taste buds. In this blog post, we will explore three mouth-watering recipes that showcase the vibrant flavors of spring. So grab your apron and let’s get cooking!

1. Herb and Cheese Frittata

Ingredients:

  • 6 large eggs
  • 1/4 cup milk
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 cup mixed fresh herbs (such as dill, parsley, and chives), chopped
  • 1 cup shredded cheese (such as Gruyere or cheddar)

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. In a large bowl, whisk together the eggs, milk, salt, and pepper until well combined.
  3. Heat the olive oil in an oven-safe skillet over medium heat. Add the mixed herbs and sauté for 2-3 minutes, until fragrant.
  4. Pour the egg mixture into the skillet, ensuring that the herbs are evenly distributed.
  5. Sprinkle the shredded cheese over the top of the frittata.
  6. Transfer the skillet to the preheated oven and bake for 15-20 minutes, or until the eggs are set in the center.
  7. Remove from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes before slicing and serving.

Serve this herb and cheese frittata alongside a fresh salad or some crusty bread for a satisfying and protein-packed brunch option.

2. Spring Vegetable Tart

Ingredients:

  • 1 sheet of puff pastry, thawed
  • 1 cup ricotta cheese
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 tablespoon fresh thyme leaves
  • 1 cup mixed spring vegetables (such as asparagus, peas, and leeks), thinly sliced
  • Salt and pepper, to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C).
  2. Roll out the puff pastry on a lightly floured surface to fit a tart pan.
  3. In a small bowl, mix together the ricotta cheese, Parmesan cheese, and fresh thyme leaves. Season with salt and pepper.
  4. Spread the cheese mixture evenly over the puff pastry, leaving a small border around the edges.
  5. Arrange the sliced spring vegetables on top of the cheese mixture, creating an attractive pattern.
  6. Season the vegetables with salt and pepper.
  7. Bake in the preheated oven for 20-25 minutes, or until the puff pastry is golden brown and the vegetables are tender.
  8. Allow the tart to cool slightly before slicing and serving.

This spring vegetable tart is a showstopper that combines the earthy flavors of fresh vegetables with the creaminess of ricotta and the flakiness of puff pastry.

3. Cheesy Herb Scones

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tablespoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, cold and cubed
  • 1/2 cup mixed fresh herbs (such as rosemary, thyme, and basil), chopped
  • 1 cup shredded cheese (such as Gouda or mozzarella)
  • 3/4 cup milk

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 425°F (220°C).
  2. In a large bowl, whisk together the flour, baking powder, and salt.
  3. Cut in the cold butter using a pastry cutter or your fingertips, until the mixture resembles coarse crumbs.
  4. Stir in the chopped herbs and shredded cheese.
  5. Gradually add the milk, stirring until the dough comes together.
  6. Turn the dough out onto a lightly floured surface and knead gently a few times to bring it together.
  7. Roll out the dough to a thickness of about 1 inch and cut into desired shapes using a biscuit cutter or knife.
  8. Place the scones on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper.
  9. Bake for 12-15 minutes, or until the scones are golden brown.
  10. Allow the scones to cool slightly before serving.

These savory cheesy herb scones are perfect for brunch, served warm with a dollop of butter or cream cheese.

Conclusion

Spring brunches are an excellent opportunity to embrace the flavors of the season. By incorporating seasonal herbs and cheese into your recipes, you can elevate your dishes to new heights. Whether you choose to make a herb and cheese frittata, a spring vegetable tart, or cheesy herb scones, your guests will be impressed by the fresh and vibrant flavors. So gather your ingredients, get creative in the kitchen, and enjoy a delicious spring brunch like no other!
