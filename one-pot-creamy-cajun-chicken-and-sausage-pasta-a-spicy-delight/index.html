One-Pot Creamy Cajun Chicken and Sausage Pasta: A Spicy Delight


If you’re looking for a delicious and hearty one-pot meal that packs a punch of flavor, look no further than this One-Pot Creamy Cajun Chicken and Sausage Pasta recipe. Bursting with Cajun spices, tender chicken, savory sausage, and a creamy sauce, this dish is a spicy delight that will satisfy even the pickiest of eaters.

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 pound boneless, skinless chicken breasts, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 1 pound smoked sausage, sliced
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 1 bell pepper, diced
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons Cajun seasoning
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried thyme
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1/4 teaspoon cayenne pepper (optional, for extra heat)
  • 1 can diced tomatoes, undrained
  • 3 cups chicken broth
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 12 ounces penne pasta
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • Fresh parsley, chopped (for garnish)

Instructions

  1. In a large pot or Dutch oven, heat the olive oil over medium-high heat. Add the chicken and sausage, and cook until browned. Remove from the pot and set aside.

  2. In the same pot, add the diced onion, bell pepper, and garlic. Cook until the vegetables are soft and fragrant, about 5 minutes.

  3. Add the Cajun seasoning, paprika, dried thyme, dried oregano, and cayenne pepper (if using) to the pot. Stir well to coat the vegetables in the spices.

  4. Pour in the diced tomatoes, chicken broth, and heavy cream. Stir to combine, then bring the mixture to a boil.

  5. Once boiling, add the penne pasta to the pot. Reduce the heat to medium-low and cover. Cook for about 12-15 minutes, or until the pasta is tender, stirring occasionally to prevent sticking.

  6. Return the chicken and sausage to the pot. Stir well to combine all the ingredients. Cook for an additional 5 minutes to heat the chicken and sausage through.

  7. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Garnish with freshly chopped parsley.

  8. Serve hot and enjoy the creamy, spicy goodness of this One-Pot Creamy Cajun Chicken and Sausage Pasta.

Why this Recipe is a Winner

This One-Pot Creamy Cajun Chicken and Sausage Pasta recipe is a winner for several reasons. First, the combination of Cajun spices, chicken, and sausage creates a flavor profile that is bold, smoky, and satisfying. The spices add a kick of heat, which can be adjusted to your taste by adding more or less cayenne pepper.

Second, the one-pot cooking method makes this dish incredibly convenient. With just a single pot, you can create a complete meal that requires minimal clean-up. This recipe is perfect for busy weeknights when you want a delicious and filling dinner without spending hours in the kitchen.

Lastly, the creamy sauce adds a luxurious touch to the dish. The combination of diced tomatoes, chicken broth, and heavy cream creates a velvety sauce that coats the pasta and brings all the flavors together. The result is a comforting and indulgent meal that will leave you wanting more.

So, if you’re in the mood for a spicy delight that’s easy to make and full of flavor, give this One-Pot Creamy Cajun Chicken and Sausage Pasta recipe a try. It’s a crowd-pleasing dish that will satisfy your cravings and impress your family and friends. Enjoy!
