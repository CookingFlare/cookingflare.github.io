Delicious Slow Cooker Chicken Tortilla Soup Recipe


Are you looking for a hearty and flavorful soup that practically cooks itself? Look no further than this delicious slow cooker chicken tortilla soup recipe. Packed with tender chicken, savory spices, and a variety of tasty toppings, this soup is a crowd-pleaser that will warm you up from the inside out. Plus, the convenience of using a slow cooker means you can simply set it and forget it, allowing the flavors to meld together for a truly satisfying meal. Let’s dive into the details of creating this comforting dish.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1 can (15 ounces) diced tomatoes
  • 1 can (10 ounces) red enchilada sauce
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 1 can (4 ounces) chopped green chilies
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 4 cups chicken broth
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 1 cup frozen corn
  • 1 tablespoon lime juice
  • Fresh cilantro, for garnish
  • Tortilla strips or chips
  • Shredded cheese
  • Avocado slices
  • Sour cream

Instructions

  1. Begin by placing the chicken breasts in the bottom of the slow cooker.

  2. In a large bowl, combine the diced tomatoes, enchilada sauce, chopped onion, green chilies, minced garlic, chicken broth, cumin, chili powder, salt, pepper, and bay leaf. Stir the mixture well to combine all the ingredients.

  3. Pour the mixture over the chicken in the slow cooker, ensuring that the chicken is fully submerged in the liquid.

  4. Cover and cook on low heat for 6-8 hours, or on high heat for 3-4 hours, until the chicken is tender and fully cooked.

  5. Once the chicken is cooked, remove it from the slow cooker and shred it using two forks. Return the shredded chicken to the slow cooker.

  6. Add the frozen corn and lime juice to the soup, stirring to combine. Allow the soup to cook for an additional 30 minutes to allow the flavors to meld together.

  7. Before serving, taste the soup and adjust the seasonings if necessary.

  8. When ready to serve, ladle the soup into bowls and top with fresh cilantro, tortilla strips or chips, shredded cheese, avocado slices, and a dollop of sour cream.

Tips and Tricks

  • To add an extra kick of heat, consider adding a diced jalapeno pepper to the soup mixture.
  • For a time-saving option, use a rotisserie chicken and shred the meat to use in place of cooking the chicken breasts in the slow cooker.
  • This soup can be stored in an airtight container in the refrigerator for up to 3 days, making it a great option for meal prep.

Conclusion

This slow cooker chicken tortilla soup is a fantastic way to enjoy the flavors of Mexican cuisine with minimal effort. Whether you’re entertaining a crowd or simply looking for a comforting meal on a chilly day, this recipe is sure to become a favorite in your household. The combination of tender chicken, zesty spices, and a variety of toppings makes for a soup that is both satisfying and versatile. Give this recipe a try and savor the delicious flavors of this slow cooker chicken tortilla soup. Happy cooking!
