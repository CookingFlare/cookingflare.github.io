Fluffy Banana Nut Muffins: A Delicious and Nutty Breakfast


Are you looking for a delicious and nutty breakfast option that will leave you feeling satisfied and energized? Look no further than these fluffy banana nut muffins! Packed with the goodness of ripe bananas and crunchy nuts, these muffins are the perfect way to start your day on a flavorful note.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 3 ripe bananas, mashed
  • 1/2 cup buttermilk
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 cup chopped walnuts or pecans (or a mix of both)

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C) and line a muffin tin with paper liners.

  2. In a medium-sized bowl, whisk together the flour, baking powder, baking soda, and salt. Set aside.

  3. In a large mixing bowl, cream together the softened butter and granulated sugar until light and fluffy. You can use an electric mixer for this step, or simply mix vigorously with a wooden spoon.

  4. Add the eggs, one at a time, beating well after each addition. Make sure to scrape down the sides of the bowl to ensure everything is well incorporated.

  5. Stir in the mashed bananas, buttermilk, and vanilla extract until well combined.

  6. Gradually add the dry ingredients to the wet ingredients, mixing until just combined. Be careful not to overmix, as this can result in dense muffins.

  7. Gently fold in the chopped nuts, reserving a small handful to sprinkle on top of the muffins.

  8. Using an ice cream scoop or a spoon, divide the batter evenly among the prepared muffin cups. Fill each cup about 3/4 full.

  9. Sprinkle the reserved nuts on top of each muffin, pressing them lightly into the batter.

  10. Bake in the preheated oven for 18-20 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center of a muffin comes out clean.

  11. Remove the muffins from the oven and let them cool in the tin for 5 minutes. Then, transfer them to a wire rack to cool completely.

  12. Once cooled, serve and enjoy! These muffins are best enjoyed fresh, but you can store them in an airtight container at room temperature for up to 3 days or freeze them for longer storage.

Final Thoughts

These fluffy banana nut muffins are a true delight to the senses. The moist and tender crumb, combined with the natural sweetness of the bananas and the satisfying crunch of the nuts, make them a breakfast treat worth waking up for.

Not only are these muffins delicious, but they are also a great way to use up overripe bananas that might otherwise go to waste. Plus, the nuts add a dose of healthy fats and protein, making these muffins a wholesome and nutritious choice to kick-start your day.

So, why not treat yourself and your loved ones to a batch of these fluffy banana nut muffins? Whether enjoyed with a cup of coffee or as a grab-and-go snack, they are sure to bring a smile to your face and make your mornings a little brighter.
