Hearty Low Carb Beef and Mushroom Stir-Fry


If you’re looking for a hearty and flavorful low-carb meal, this beef and mushroom stir-fry is the perfect option. Packed with protein, healthy fats, and plenty of vegetables, this dish is not only delicious but also satisfying and nutritious. Whether you’re following a low-carb diet or simply want to incorporate more wholesome meals into your weeknight rotation, this recipe is sure to become a favorite.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound of beef sirloin, thinly sliced
  • 2 cups of sliced mushrooms
  • 1 onion, thinly sliced
  • 3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 red bell pepper, thinly sliced
  • 1/4 cup of soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons of sesame oil
  • 1 tablespoon of grated fresh ginger
  • 1 teaspoon of red pepper flakes
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 green onions, chopped (for garnish)
  • 2 tablespoons of olive oil

Instructions

  1. In a small bowl, whisk together the soy sauce, sesame oil, grated ginger, and red pepper flakes. Set aside.

  2. Heat 1 tablespoon of olive oil in a large skillet or wok over medium-high heat. Add the sliced beef and stir-fry for 2-3 minutes, until browned. Remove the beef from the skillet and set aside.

  3. In the same skillet, add the remaining tablespoon of olive oil. Add the sliced onions and minced garlic, and stir-fry for 2-3 minutes until the onions are soft and translucent.

  4. Add the sliced mushrooms and red bell pepper to the skillet and stir-fry for an additional 3-4 minutes, until the vegetables are tender.

  5. Return the cooked beef to the skillet and pour the prepared sauce over the beef and vegetables. Stir well to coat everything evenly. Cook for another 2-3 minutes, allowing the flavors to meld together.

  6. Season with salt and pepper to taste.

  7. Remove the skillet from the heat and garnish with chopped green onions.

  8. Serve the beef and mushroom stir-fry hot, either on its own or over a bed of cauliflower rice for an extra low-carb twist.

Nutritional Information

  • Serving Size: 1/4 of the recipe
  • Calories: 320
  • Total Fat: 18g
  • Saturated Fat: 4g
  • Cholesterol: 80mg
  • Sodium: 800mg
  • Total Carbohydrate: 8g
  • Dietary Fiber: 2g
  • Sugars: 4g
  • Protein: 32g

Tips and Variations

  • Feel free to add other low-carb vegetables to the stir-fry, such as broccoli, snow peas, or zucchini.
  • For a different flavor profile, you can substitute the beef with chicken or tofu.
  • If you prefer a spicier stir-fry, add an extra pinch of red pepper flakes or a drizzle of sriracha sauce.
  • This dish can be prepared ahead of time and stored in an airtight container in the refrigerator for up to 3 days.

Conclusion

This hearty low-carb beef and mushroom stir-fry is a versatile and satisfying dish that’s perfect for a quick and flavorful weeknight dinner. With its tender beef, savory mushrooms, and vibrant peppers, this stir-fry is a celebration of bold flavors and wholesome ingredients. Whether you’re following a low-carb lifestyle or simply looking to add more variety to your meals, this recipe is a delicious and nutritious choice. Enjoy the simplicity and heartiness of this stir-fry, and feel free to customize it with your favorite low-carb vegetables and proteins.
