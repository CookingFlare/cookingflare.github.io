Delicious and Irresistible: Grilled Bacon-Wrapped Shrimp


If you’re a fan of succulent seafood and savory bacon, then you’re in for a treat with this mouthwatering recipe for Grilled Bacon-Wrapped Shrimp. This dish is a perfect combination of flavors and textures, showcasing the natural sweetness of shrimp and the rich, smoky essence of bacon. Whether you’re hosting a backyard barbecue or looking for a show-stopping appetizer, these grilled bacon-wrapped shrimp are sure to impress your guests and tantalize your taste buds.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound large shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 12 slices of bacon, cut in half
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon cayenne pepper
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • Wooden skewers, soaked in water for 30 minutes

Instructions

  1. Preheat your grill to medium-high heat, around 375°F to 400°F.

  2. In a small bowl, combine the olive oil, minced garlic, paprika, cayenne pepper, black pepper, and salt. Mix well to create a flavorful marinade.

  3. Place the peeled and deveined shrimp in a large resealable plastic bag or a shallow dish. Pour the marinade over the shrimp, ensuring that each one is evenly coated. Allow the shrimp to marinate in the refrigerator for at least 15 minutes to let the flavors infuse.

  4. While the shrimp is marinating, take the halved bacon slices and wrap each piece around a shrimp, securing it with a wooden skewer to keep the bacon in place.

  5. Once all the shrimp are wrapped in bacon, place them on the preheated grill. Cook for 2-3 minutes on each side, or until the bacon is crispy and the shrimp turns a beautiful pink color.

  6. Carefully remove the grilled bacon-wrapped shrimp from the grill and transfer them to a serving platter. Allow them to cool for a few minutes before serving.

  7. Garnish with a sprinkle of fresh parsley or a squeeze of lemon juice for an extra pop of flavor.

Serving Suggestions

These grilled bacon-wrapped shrimp are a versatile dish that can be served in various ways. They make for an irresistible appetizer when paired with a zesty cocktail sauce or a creamy avocado dip. You can also turn them into a main course by serving them over a bed of seasoned rice or a crisp, refreshing salad.

Tips and Tricks

  • If you’re using wooden skewers, be sure to soak them in water for at least 30 minutes before threading the shrimp to prevent them from burning on the grill.
  • To add a hint of citrusy brightness to the shrimp, consider adding a splash of fresh lime juice to the marinade.
  • For an extra kick of heat, sprinkle a pinch of red pepper flakes over the grilled bacon-wrapped shrimp before serving.

Conclusion

Grilled Bacon-Wrapped Shrimp is a culinary delight that brings together the best of land and sea. The succulent shrimp, enveloped in smoky, crispy bacon, create a flavor explosion that’s hard to resist. Whether you’re grilling up a storm for a summer cookout or simply craving a delectable seafood dish, these grilled bacon-wrapped shrimp are guaranteed to be a hit. So fire up the grill and get ready to savor every bite of this irresistible creation!
