Grilled Halloumi Skewers with Tzatziki: Greek-inspired Delights


Are you ready to take your taste buds on a Mediterranean journey? Look no further than these Grilled Halloumi Skewers with Tzatziki! Bursting with flavors of Greece, this dish is a delightful combination of smoky grilled halloumi cheese and refreshing tzatziki sauce. Whether you’re hosting a backyard barbecue or simply craving a flavorful snack, these skewers are sure to impress.

Ingredients

To make these Greek-inspired delights, you will need the following ingredients:

  • 1 block of halloumi cheese, cut into cubes
  • 1 red bell pepper, cut into chunks
  • 1 yellow bell pepper, cut into chunks
  • 1 red onion, cut into chunks
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Wooden skewers, soaked in water for 30 minutes

Tzatziki Sauce

For the tzatziki sauce, gather the following ingredients:

  • 1 cup Greek yogurt
  • 1/2 cucumber, grated and drained
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 1 tablespoon fresh dill, chopped
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Preheat your grill to medium-high heat.

  2. In a bowl, combine the halloumi cheese, bell peppers, red onion, olive oil, dried oregano, salt, and pepper. Toss gently to coat the ingredients evenly.

  3. Thread the halloumi cheese, bell peppers, and red onion onto the soaked wooden skewers, alternating the ingredients to create colorful skewers.

  4. Place the skewers on the preheated grill and cook for about 2-3 minutes per side, or until the halloumi cheese is nicely charred and the vegetables are tender.

  5. While the skewers are grilling, prepare the tzatziki sauce. In a separate bowl, combine the Greek yogurt, grated cucumber, minced garlic, lemon juice, fresh dill, salt, and pepper. Mix well until all the ingredients are incorporated.

  6. Once the skewers are done, remove them from the grill and let them cool for a few minutes.

  7. Serve the grilled halloumi skewers with a generous dollop of tzatziki sauce on the side.

Flavorful Tips

  • For an extra burst of flavor, squeeze some fresh lemon juice over the grilled halloumi skewers before serving.

  • Feel free to get creative with your skewers by adding cherry tomatoes, mushrooms, or any other vegetables you enjoy.

  • If you don’t have a grill, you can also cook the skewers in a grill pan or even under the broiler.

  • Serve these skewers as an appetizer, main course, or even as a delicious addition to your salad.

Conclusion

Grilled Halloumi Skewers with Tzatziki are the perfect embodiment of Greek cuisine. The smoky and salty halloumi cheese, combined with the vibrant flavors of the grilled peppers and onions, creates a truly mouthwatering experience. And the cool and creamy tzatziki sauce adds a refreshing touch that complements the dish perfectly. So, whether you’re hosting a summer gathering or simply looking to elevate your everyday meals, these Greek-inspired delights are a must-try. Enjoy the flavors of Greece right in your own backyard!
