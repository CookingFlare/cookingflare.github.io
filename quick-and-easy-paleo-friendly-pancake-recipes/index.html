Quick and Easy Paleo-Friendly Pancake Recipes


If you’re following a paleo diet, you might think that enjoying pancakes is off the menu. But fear not, because I’ve got some fantastic paleo-friendly pancake recipes that are not only delicious but also easy to make. Whether you’re craving a classic pancake stack or something a bit more adventurous, these recipes have got you covered. So, let’s dive into the world of paleo-friendly pancakes and get ready to whip up some mouthwatering breakfast treats!

Classic Paleo Pancakes

Ingredients:

  • 2 large ripe bananas
  • 4 eggs
  • 1/2 cup almond flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon cinnamon
  • Coconut oil for cooking

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, mash the ripe bananas until smooth.
  2. Add the eggs to the mashed bananas and whisk together until well combined.
  3. Stir in the almond flour, baking powder, and cinnamon until the batter is smooth.
  4. Heat a non-stick skillet over medium heat and add a small amount of coconut oil.
  5. Pour 1/4 cup of batter onto the skillet for each pancake.
  6. Cook for 2-3 minutes on each side or until golden brown.
  7. Serve with a drizzle of pure maple syrup and fresh berries.

Blueberry Coconut Pancakes

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup almond flour
  • 1/4 cup coconut flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 4 eggs
  • 1/2 cup coconut milk
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/2 cup fresh blueberries
  • Coconut oil for cooking

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, mix together the almond flour, coconut flour, and baking soda.
  2. In a separate bowl, whisk the eggs, coconut milk, and vanilla extract.
  3. Gradually add the dry ingredients to the wet ingredients, stirring until well combined.
  4. Gently fold in the fresh blueberries.
  5. Heat a skillet over medium heat and add a small amount of coconut oil.
  6. Pour 1/4 cup of batter onto the skillet for each pancake.
  7. Cook for 2-3 minutes on each side or until lightly golden.
  8. Serve with a sprinkle of shredded coconut and a dollop of coconut yogurt.

Pumpkin Spice Pancakes

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup pumpkin puree
  • 4 eggs
  • 1/4 cup almond flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon pumpkin pie spice
  • Coconut oil for cooking

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, whisk together the pumpkin puree and eggs until smooth.
  2. Stir in the almond flour, baking powder, and pumpkin pie spice until well combined.
  3. Heat a non-stick skillet over medium heat and add a small amount of coconut oil.
  4. Pour 1/4 cup of batter onto the skillet for each pancake.
  5. Cook for 2-3 minutes on each side or until golden brown.
  6. Serve with a dusting of cinnamon and a drizzle of honey.

Conclusion

There you have it – three delicious and easy paleo-friendly pancake recipes that are sure to satisfy your breakfast cravings. Whether you’re a fan of classic flavors, fruity twists, or seasonal delights, these recipes offer something for everyone. So, grab your ingredients, fire up the stove, and get ready to enjoy a stack of paleo pancakes that are as wholesome as they are tasty!
