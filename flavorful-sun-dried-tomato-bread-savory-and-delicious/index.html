Flavorful Sun-Dried Tomato Bread: Savory and Delicious


Are you a bread lover? Do you enjoy experimenting with different flavors and textures in your homemade bread? If so, then you’re in for a treat! In this recipe, we will be exploring the wonderful world of sun-dried tomatoes and how they can elevate your bread to a whole new level of deliciousness.

Ingredients

  • 3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup warm water
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon honey
  • 2 teaspoons active dry yeast
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup chopped sun-dried tomatoes (drained if packed in oil)
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh basil
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese

Instructions

  1. In a small bowl, dissolve the yeast in warm water and let it sit for about 5 minutes until it becomes frothy.

  2. In a large mixing bowl, combine the flour, salt, sun-dried tomatoes, fresh basil, and grated Parmesan cheese. Give it a good mix to evenly distribute the ingredients.

  3. Make a well in the center of the dry ingredients and pour in the yeast mixture, olive oil, and honey. Using a wooden spoon or your hands, mix everything together until a rough dough forms.

  4. Transfer the dough onto a lightly floured surface and knead for about 10 minutes, or until the dough becomes smooth and elastic. If the dough feels too sticky, add a little more flour as needed.

  5. Place the dough in a greased bowl, cover it with a clean kitchen towel or plastic wrap, and let it rise in a warm place for about 1 hour, or until it doubles in size.

  6. Once the dough has risen, punch it down to release any air bubbles. Turn it out onto a lightly floured surface and shape it into a loaf.

  7. Transfer the shaped loaf onto a baking sheet lined with parchment paper. Cover it with the kitchen towel or plastic wrap and let it rise for another 30 minutes.

  8. Meanwhile, preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).

  9. Just before baking, lightly score the top of the loaf with a sharp knife or make a few diagonal slashes.

  10. Bake the bread in the preheated oven for about 30-35 minutes, or until it turns golden brown and sounds hollow when tapped on the bottom.

  11. Once baked, remove the bread from the oven and let it cool on a wire rack before slicing.

Serving Suggestions

This flavorful sun-dried tomato bread is perfect on its own, but it also pairs beautifully with a variety of dishes. Here are a few serving suggestions to get you started:

  • Enjoy a slice of warm sun-dried tomato bread with a generous spread of creamy goat cheese.
  • Turn it into a delicious sandwich by layering slices of roast beef, provolone cheese, and arugula.
  • Toast a slice and top it with avocado, cherry tomatoes, and a sprinkle of sea salt for a quick and satisfying breakfast.

Conclusion

There you have it – a recipe for flavorful sun-dried tomato bread that is sure to impress your family and friends. The combination of tangy sun-dried tomatoes, fragrant basil, and savory Parmesan cheese creates a bread that is both delicious and unique. So go ahead, put on your apron, and let your taste buds embark on a delightful journey with this savory bread recipe. Happy baking!
