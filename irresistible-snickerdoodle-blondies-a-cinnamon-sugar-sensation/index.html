Irresistible Snickerdoodle Blondies: A Cinnamon-Sugar Sensation


If you’re a fan of the classic snickerdoodle cookies and crave something a little more indulgent, then you’re in for a treat with these irresistible Snickerdoodle Blondies. This recipe takes all the delightful flavors of a snickerdoodle cookie and transforms them into a soft, chewy, and buttery blondie that’s simply irresistible. With a crackly cinnamon-sugar crust and a buttery vanilla base, these blondies are the perfect combination of sweet, spicy, and downright delicious.

Ingredients

For the Blondies:

  • 1 cup unsalted butter, melted
  • 1 3/4 cups light brown sugar, packed
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 tablespoon pure vanilla extract
  • 2 1/3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt

For the Cinnamon-Sugar Topping:

  • 2 tablespoons granulated sugar
  • 2 teaspoons ground cinnamon

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C) and line a 9x13-inch baking pan with parchment paper, leaving an overhang on the sides for easy removal.

  2. In a large mixing bowl, whisk together the melted butter and brown sugar until well combined.

  3. Add the eggs and vanilla extract to the butter-sugar mixture, and whisk until smooth and creamy.

  4. In a separate bowl, whisk together the flour, baking powder, and salt. Gradually add the dry ingredients to the wet ingredients, mixing until just combined. Be careful not to overmix the batter.

  5. Transfer the blondie batter to the prepared baking pan and spread it out evenly with a spatula.

  6. In a small bowl, mix together the granulated sugar and ground cinnamon for the topping. Sprinkle the cinnamon-sugar mixture evenly over the blondie batter.

  7. Bake in the preheated oven for 25-30 minutes, or until the top is golden brown and a toothpick inserted into the center comes out with a few moist crumbs.

  8. Allow the blondies to cool completely in the pan before lifting them out using the parchment paper overhang. This makes it easier to cut them into neat squares.

  9. Once cooled, slice the blondies into squares and serve. Enjoy the wonderful aroma and the delightful taste of these cinnamon-sugar sensations!

Tips and Variations

  • For an extra indulgent twist, fold in a cup of white chocolate chips or chopped pecans into the blondie batter before baking.
  • Drizzle a simple vanilla or cream cheese glaze over the cooled blondies for an added touch of sweetness.
  • Store any leftover blondies in an airtight container at room temperature for up to 3 days, or in the refrigerator for up to a week.

Final Thoughts

These Snickerdoodle Blondies are a delightful twist on the beloved snickerdoodle cookie. With their soft, chewy texture and the perfect balance of cinnamon and sugar, they are sure to become a favorite in your baking repertoire. Whether you’re serving them at a gathering, sharing them with friends, or simply indulging in a sweet treat for yourself, these blondies are a guaranteed crowd-pleaser. So, roll up your sleeves, preheat that oven, and get ready to savor the cinnamon-sugar sensation of these irresistible Snickerdoodle Blondies!
