Grilled Vegetable Wrap: A Light and Healthy Fourth of July Lunch


As the Fourth of July approaches, it’s time to start thinking about delicious, healthy, and refreshing meals to enjoy with friends and family. Grilled Vegetable Wrap is a fantastic choice for a light and satisfying lunch that perfectly embodies the spirit of summer. Packed with colorful veggies and flavorful accents, this recipe is sure to be a hit at your Fourth of July gathering.

Ingredients

For the Grilled Vegetables:

  • 2 medium zucchinis, sliced lengthwise
  • 1 large red bell pepper, deseeded and quartered
  • 1 large yellow bell pepper, deseeded and quartered
  • 1 large red onion, peeled and cut into thick slices
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • Salt and black pepper to taste

For the Hummus Spread:

  • 1 can chickpeas, drained and rinsed
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 3 tablespoons tahini
  • Juice of 1 lemon
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • Salt to taste

Additional Ingredients:

  • 4 large whole wheat or spinach tortillas
  • 1 cup mixed salad greens
  • 1/2 cup crumbled feta cheese (optional)
  • Fresh basil leaves for garnish

Instructions

Grilling the Vegetables:

  1. Preheat the grill to medium-high heat.
  2. In a large bowl, toss the zucchinis, bell peppers, red onion, and cherry tomatoes with olive oil, salt, and black pepper.
  3. Place the vegetables on the grill and cook for 4-5 minutes on each side, or until they are tender and have beautiful grill marks.
  4. Once the vegetables are grilled to perfection, remove them from the grill and set aside.

Making the Hummus Spread:

  1. In a food processor, combine the chickpeas, minced garlic, tahini, lemon juice, and olive oil.
  2. Blend until the mixture becomes smooth and creamy. Add salt to taste and adjust the consistency by adding a little water if needed.

Assembling the Wraps:

  1. Lay out the tortillas on a clean work surface.
  2. Spread a generous layer of the prepared hummus spread over each tortilla.
  3. Arrange a handful of mixed salad greens on top of the hummus spread.
  4. Place a generous portion of the grilled vegetables on the greens.
  5. If desired, sprinkle some crumbled feta cheese over the vegetables for an extra burst of flavor.
  6. Add a few fresh basil leaves for a pop of color and freshness.

Rolling the Wraps:

  1. Fold the sides of the tortilla inwards, then roll it up tightly from the bottom to enclose the filling.
  2. Repeat the process with the remaining tortillas and fillings.

Serving and Enjoying:

  1. Slice each wrap in half diagonally and arrange on a serving platter.
  2. These wraps can be served immediately or wrapped in parchment paper for a picnic or outdoor celebration.
  3. Pair them with your favorite Fourth of July beverages and enjoy the vibrant flavors of summer.

Nutrition and Benefits

Grilled Vegetable Wraps are not only delicious but also packed with nutrients. Zucchinis, bell peppers, and cherry tomatoes are rich in vitamins and antioxidants, while hummus provides a good source of plant-based protein and healthy fats. This recipe is also a great way to incorporate more vegetables into your diet, making it a wonderful choice for those looking to enjoy a balanced and wholesome meal.

Conclusion

The Grilled Vegetable Wrap is a delightful and nutritious option for a Fourth of July lunch, offering a wonderful combination of flavors and textures. Whether you’re hosting a gathering or simply enjoying a relaxing day outdoors, these wraps are a fantastic addition to your menu. Embrace the summer season and celebrate with this light and healthy dish that is sure to please everyone at the table. Happy Fourth of July!
