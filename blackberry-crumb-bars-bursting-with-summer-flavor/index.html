Blackberry Crumb Bars: Bursting with Summer Flavor


There’s nothing quite like the taste of fresh blackberries during the summer months. Their sweet and tart flavors combine to create a burst of deliciousness that can elevate any dessert. One of my favorite ways to showcase these vibrant berries is by making Blackberry Crumb Bars. These bars are not only easy to make but also incredibly satisfying to eat. So, let’s dive right into this recipe and get ready to embrace the flavors of summer!

Ingredients

For the Crust and Crumb Topping:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup unsalted butter, cold and cubed
  • 1 large egg
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

For the Blackberry Filling:

  • 4 cups fresh blackberries
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 2 tablespoons cornstarch
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C) and line a 9x13-inch baking pan with parchment paper.

  2. In a large mixing bowl, combine the flour, granulated sugar, baking powder, and salt. Add the cold cubed butter and use a pastry cutter or your fingers to cut the butter into the dry ingredients until the mixture resembles coarse crumbs.

  3. In a small bowl, beat the egg and vanilla extract together. Pour the egg mixture into the crumb mixture and stir until a dough forms.

  4. Reserve about 1 1/2 cups of the dough for the crumb topping. Press the remaining dough evenly into the bottom of the prepared baking pan to form the crust.

  5. In another bowl, gently toss together the blackberries, granulated sugar, cornstarch, and lemon juice until well coated. Spread the blackberry mixture evenly over the crust.

  6. Take the reserved dough and crumble it over the top of the blackberry filling, covering it as evenly as possible.

  7. Bake the bars in the preheated oven for about 45-50 minutes, or until the crust and crumb topping are golden brown and the blackberry filling is bubbly.

  8. Once baked, remove the pan from the oven and allow the bars to cool completely before cutting into squares.

Serving Suggestions

These Blackberry Crumb Bars are delicious on their own, but you can take them to the next level by serving them with a scoop of vanilla ice cream or a dollop of whipped cream. The creamy and cold accompaniment beautifully complements the sweet and tangy flavors of the bars.

Storage

If you have any leftovers (which is rare!), you can store the Blackberry Crumb Bars in an airtight container at room temperature for up to 3 days. However, I doubt they will last that long!

Final Thoughts

Blackberry Crumb Bars are a delightful treat that captures the essence of summer. The juicy blackberries, buttery crust, and crumbly topping create a balanced and irresistible dessert. Whether you’re hosting a summer gathering, enjoying a picnic, or simply treating yourself, these bars are sure to impress. So, grab some fresh blackberries and get ready to indulge in the flavors of the season with these Bursting with Summer Flavor Blackberry Crumb Bars!

Remember to enjoy every bite and savor the taste of summer. Happy baking!
