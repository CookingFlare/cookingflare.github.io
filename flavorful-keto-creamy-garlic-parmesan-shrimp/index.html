Flavorful Keto Creamy Garlic Parmesan Shrimp


When it comes to keto-friendly dishes, it’s important to find recipes that are not only low in carbs but also bursting with flavor. If you’re a seafood lover looking for a creamy and indulgent dish that aligns with your keto lifestyle, then this Flavorful Keto Creamy Garlic Parmesan Shrimp recipe is a must-try. Packed with succulent shrimp, rich cream, and savory Parmesan cheese, this dish is a delightful combination of flavors that will leave your taste buds singing.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound of large shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 2 tablespoons of olive oil
  • 4 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 cup of heavy cream
  • 1/2 cup of grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 teaspoon of Italian seasoning
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley for garnish
  • Cauliflower rice or zucchini noodles for serving

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Shrimp: In a large skillet, heat the olive oil over medium-high heat. Add the minced garlic and sauté for about 1 minute until fragrant.

  2. Cook the Shrimp: Add the shrimp to the skillet and season with salt and black pepper. Cook the shrimp for 2-3 minutes on each side until they turn pink and opaque. Once cooked, remove the shrimp from the skillet and set them aside.

  3. Make the Creamy Sauce: In the same skillet, pour in the heavy cream and bring it to a gentle simmer. Add the grated Parmesan cheese and Italian seasoning, stirring constantly until the cheese has melted and the sauce has thickened slightly.

  4. Combine the Shrimp and Sauce: Return the cooked shrimp to the skillet, coating them with the creamy garlic Parmesan sauce. Allow the shrimp to simmer in the sauce for an additional 2-3 minutes to absorb the flavors.

  5. Serve and Garnish: Serve the flavorful creamy garlic Parmesan shrimp over a bed of cauliflower rice or zucchini noodles. Garnish with freshly chopped parsley for a pop of color and added freshness.

Why You’ll Love This Dish

  • Keto-Friendly: With minimal carbs and a good dose of healthy fats, this dish is perfect for those following a keto lifestyle.

  • Rich and Creamy: The combination of heavy cream and Parmesan cheese creates a luscious, creamy sauce that perfectly coats the succulent shrimp.

  • Garlic Infusion: The addition of minced garlic infuses the dish with robust, aromatic flavors that elevate the overall taste.

  • Quick and Easy: This recipe comes together in under 30 minutes, making it a convenient option for a weeknight dinner.

  • Versatile: The creamy garlic Parmesan sauce pairs well with not only shrimp but also with chicken or even roasted vegetables for a versatile keto-friendly option.

Whether you’re following a keto diet or simply appreciate a deliciously indulgent dish, this Flavorful Keto Creamy Garlic Parmesan Shrimp recipe is a winner. Indulge in the creamy, garlicky goodness without compromising your dietary goals. It’s a dish that’s sure to become a regular feature on your menu, satisfying cravings and impressing guests with its rich flavors and elegant presentation.
