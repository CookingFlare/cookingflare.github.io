Coffee and Walnut Cake: A Perfect Pairing of Flavors


There’s something truly magical about the combination of coffee and walnuts. The rich, deep flavor of coffee complements the earthy, nutty taste of walnuts in a way that creates a harmonious blend of flavors. If you’re a fan of both coffee and cake, then this Coffee and Walnut Cake recipe is sure to become a favorite in your repertoire.

Ingredients

For the Cake:

  • 200g unsalted butter, softened
  • 200g caster sugar
  • 4 eggs
  • 200g self-raising flour
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 2 tbsp instant coffee granules, dissolved in 1 tbsp hot water
  • 100g walnuts, chopped

For the Coffee Buttercream:

  • 250g unsalted butter, softened
  • 500g icing sugar
  • 2 tbsp instant coffee granules, dissolved in 2 tbsp hot water

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 180°C (350°F) and grease and line two 20cm (8-inch) round cake tins with parchment paper.

  2. In a large mixing bowl, cream together the softened butter and caster sugar until pale and fluffy.

  3. Beat in the eggs, one at a time, adding a spoonful of flour with each egg to prevent the mixture from curdling.

  4. Sift in the remaining flour and baking powder, then gently fold the dry ingredients into the wet mixture until just combined.

  5. Stir in the dissolved coffee and chopped walnuts until evenly distributed throughout the batter.

  6. Divide the batter evenly between the prepared cake tins and smooth the tops with a spatula.

  7. Bake in the preheated oven for 25-30 minutes or until a skewer inserted into the center of the cakes comes out clean.

  8. While the cakes are baking, prepare the coffee buttercream by beating together the softened butter, icing sugar, and dissolved coffee until smooth and creamy.

  9. Once the cakes are baked, remove them from the oven and allow them to cool in their tins for 10 minutes before transferring to a wire rack to cool completely.

  10. Once the cakes are completely cool, spread half of the coffee buttercream over the top of one cake, then carefully place the second cake on top.

  11. Use the remaining coffee buttercream to frost the top and sides of the cake, creating a smooth, even finish.

  12. For a decorative touch, sprinkle some chopped walnuts on top of the cake before serving.

The Perfect Pairing

This Coffee and Walnut Cake is a delightful treat for any occasion. The robust flavor of coffee infuses the light and fluffy cake layers, while the crunch of walnuts adds a delightful texture. The smooth and creamy coffee buttercream ties everything together, creating a truly indulgent experience for coffee and cake lovers alike.

Whether you’re enjoying a slice with a cup of freshly brewed coffee in the morning or serving it as a dessert at a dinner party, this Coffee and Walnut Cake is a versatile and delicious addition to any menu.

So, why not indulge in the perfect pairing of flavors with this delightful coffee-infused cake? It’s a simple yet elegant treat that will leave everyone craving for more. Happy baking!
