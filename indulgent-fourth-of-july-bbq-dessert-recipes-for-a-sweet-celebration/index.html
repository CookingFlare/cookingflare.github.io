Indulgent Fourth of July BBQ Dessert Recipes for a Sweet Celebration


The Fourth of July is a time for celebration, and what better way to celebrate than with a delicious BBQ and indulgent desserts? This year, take your Fourth of July party to the next level with these mouthwatering BBQ dessert recipes that are sure to satisfy your sweet tooth. From fruity delights to chocolatey treats, these desserts are perfect for a sweet celebration under the summer sun.

1. Grilled Strawberry Shortcake Skewers

Ingredients:

  • Fresh strawberries
  • Pound cake, cut into cubes
  • Wooden skewers
  • Whipped cream

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your grill to medium heat.
  2. Thread the strawberries and pound cake cubes alternately onto the skewers.
  3. Place the skewers on the grill and cook for about 2-3 minutes on each side, until grill marks appear.
  4. Remove the skewers from the grill and let them cool slightly.
  5. Serve the grilled strawberry shortcake skewers with a dollop of whipped cream on top.

2. S’mores Stuffed Bananas

Ingredients:

  • Ripe bananas
  • Mini marshmallows
  • Chocolate chips
  • Aluminum foil

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your grill to medium heat.
  2. Slice each banana lengthwise, leaving the peel intact.
  3. Stuff the bananas with mini marshmallows and chocolate chips.
  4. Wrap each banana tightly in aluminum foil.
  5. Place the foil-wrapped bananas on the grill and cook for about 5-7 minutes, until the marshmallows and chocolate have melted.
  6. Carefully remove the bananas from the grill and let them cool slightly before serving.

3. Patriotic Berry Parfait

Ingredients:

  • Fresh strawberries, sliced
  • Fresh blueberries
  • Greek yogurt
  • Honey
  • Granola

Instructions:

  1. In a glass or a bowl, layer the sliced strawberries, blueberries, Greek yogurt, honey, and granola.
  2. Repeat the layers until you reach the top.
  3. Finish with a dollop of Greek yogurt, a drizzle of honey, and a sprinkle of granola on top.
  4. Serve chilled.

4. Red, White, and Blue Popsicles

Ingredients:

  • Fresh strawberries, pureed
  • Fresh blueberries, pureed
  • Coconut milk
  • Honey

Instructions:

  1. In a popsicle mold, pour the strawberry puree into one-third of each mold.
  2. Freeze for about 30 minutes or until slightly firm.
  3. Pour coconut milk into the molds, filling another one-third of each mold.
  4. Freeze for another 30 minutes or until slightly firm.
  5. Pour the blueberry puree into the remaining one-third of each mold.
  6. Insert popsicle sticks into each mold and freeze until solid.
  7. To remove the popsicles, run the molds under warm water for a few seconds.

5. Chocolate-Dipped Watermelon Slices

Ingredients:

  • Watermelon, sliced into wedges
  • Dark chocolate chips
  • Chopped nuts or sprinkles (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
  2. Melt the dark chocolate chips in a microwave-safe bowl, stirring every 30 seconds until smooth.
  3. Dip each watermelon wedge into the melted chocolate, allowing any excess chocolate to drip off.
  4. Place the chocolate-dipped watermelon slices onto the prepared baking sheet.
  5. Sprinkle chopped nuts or sprinkles over the chocolate, if desired.
  6. Refrigerate for about 15 minutes or until the chocolate sets.
  7. Serve chilled.

These indulgent Fourth of July BBQ dessert recipes will surely impress your guests and add a sweet touch to your celebration. Whether you’re grilling strawberries, stuffing bananas with s’mores, or creating patriotic parfaits, these recipes are guaranteed to be a hit. So fire up the grill, gather your loved ones, and enjoy these delicious treats as you celebrate Independence Day. Happy Fourth of July!
