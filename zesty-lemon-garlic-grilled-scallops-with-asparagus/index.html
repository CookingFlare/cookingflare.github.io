Zesty Lemon Garlic Grilled Scallops with Asparagus


Scallops are a delicacy that many seafood lovers enjoy. Their tender texture and sweet flavor make them a perfect choice for grilling. When paired with zesty lemon and garlic, and served alongside fresh asparagus, you have a dish that is bursting with flavor and perfect for a summer cookout. In this recipe, we’ll show you how to create mouthwatering grilled scallops with a tangy twist, and a side of grilled asparagus that complements the flavors beautifully.

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound of fresh scallops
  • 1 bunch of asparagus
  • 2 lemons
  • 4 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons of olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your grill to medium-high heat. If you’re using a charcoal grill, make sure the coals are hot and ashy.

  2. Rinse the scallops under cold water and pat them dry with a paper towel. This step is crucial to ensure that the scallops cook evenly and develop a nice sear.

  3. In a small bowl, combine the juice of one lemon, minced garlic, olive oil, salt, and pepper. Mix well to create a marinade.

  4. Place the scallops in a ziplock bag or a shallow dish and pour the marinade over them. Toss the scallops gently to ensure they are evenly coated. Allow them to marinate for about 15 minutes to let the flavors infuse.

  5. While the scallops are marinating, prepare the asparagus. Trim the woody ends from the asparagus spears and place them in a separate dish. Drizzle with olive oil, sprinkle with salt and pepper, and toss to coat.

  6. Once the grill is hot, place the asparagus spears directly on the grates. Grill for about 5-7 minutes, turning occasionally, until they are tender and slightly charred. Remove from the grill and set aside.

  7. Now it’s time to grill the scallops. Make sure the grill grates are clean and lightly oiled to prevent sticking. Place the scallops on the grill, making sure to leave space between them. Grill for about 2-3 minutes on each side until they are opaque and slightly firm to the touch. Be careful not to overcook them as they can become tough and rubbery.

  8. While the scallops are grilling, cut the remaining lemon into wedges for serving.

  9. Once the scallops are cooked, remove them from the grill and transfer them to a serving platter. Squeeze some fresh lemon juice over the scallops for an extra burst of flavor.

  10. Arrange the grilled asparagus alongside the scallops on the platter. Garnish with lemon wedges and a sprinkle of fresh herbs, such as parsley or chives, if desired.

  11. Serve the zesty lemon garlic grilled scallops with asparagus immediately, while they are still hot. The combination of flavors and textures will tantalize your taste buds and impress your guests.

Enjoy this delightful dish as a main course or as part of a seafood feast. The zesty lemon and garlic marinade adds a refreshing kick to the succulent scallops, while the grilled asparagus provides a satisfying crunch. Whether you’re hosting a backyard barbecue or simply craving a flavorful meal, these grilled scallops with asparagus are sure to satisfy your culinary desires.

So fire up the grill, gather your ingredients, and get ready to indulge in a sensational seafood dish that will leave you craving more. Happy grilling!
