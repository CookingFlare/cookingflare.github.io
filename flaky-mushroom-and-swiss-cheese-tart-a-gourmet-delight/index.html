Flaky Mushroom and Swiss Cheese Tart: A Gourmet Delight


If you’re a fan of savory tarts, then you’re in for a treat with this Flaky Mushroom and Swiss Cheese Tart recipe. With its buttery, flaky crust and rich, cheesy filling, this tart is a gourmet delight that will impress even the most discerning palates. Whether you’re preparing it for a special occasion or simply indulging in a culinary adventure, this recipe is sure to satisfy your cravings.

Ingredients

For the crust:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup unsalted butter, cold and cubed
  • 6 tablespoons ice water

For the filling:

  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 pound mushrooms, sliced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 cup Swiss cheese, grated
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • 2 eggs

Instructions

Step 1: Prepare the Crust

  1. In a large mixing bowl, combine the flour and salt. Add the cold, cubed butter and use a pastry cutter or your fingertips to cut the butter into the flour until the mixture resembles coarse crumbs.

  2. Slowly add the ice water, one tablespoon at a time, mixing until the dough comes together. Be careful not to overmix.

  3. Transfer the dough onto a lightly floured surface and shape it into a disc. Wrap it in plastic wrap and refrigerate for at least 30 minutes.

Step 2: Prepare the Filling

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).

  2. Heat the olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Add the sliced mushrooms and cook until they have released their moisture and are golden brown, about 8-10 minutes.

  3. Add the minced garlic, dried thyme, salt, and pepper to the skillet. Cook for an additional 2 minutes, stirring occasionally.

  4. In a separate bowl, whisk together the grated Swiss cheese, heavy cream, and eggs until well combined.

Step 3: Assemble and Bake

  1. On a floured surface, roll out the chilled dough to fit a 9-inch tart pan. Press the dough into the pan, trimming any excess.

  2. Spread the cooked mushrooms evenly over the tart crust.

  3. Pour the cheese mixture over the mushrooms, ensuring it covers the entire surface.

  4. Place the tart on a baking sheet and bake in the preheated oven for 30-35 minutes, or until the crust is golden brown and the filling is set.

  5. Allow the tart to cool for a few minutes before slicing and serving.

Serving Suggestions

This Flaky Mushroom and Swiss Cheese Tart pairs beautifully with a fresh green salad for a light lunch or dinner. It can also be served as an appetizer at your next gathering or as a delightful addition to a brunch spread. The earthy flavors of the mushrooms and the creamy richness of the Swiss cheese create a harmonious balance that will leave your taste buds craving more.

Conclusion

With its flaky crust and irresistible filling, this Flaky Mushroom and Swiss Cheese Tart is a gourmet delight that is sure to impress. Whether you’re a seasoned cook or a beginner in the kitchen, this recipe is straightforward and easy to follow, making it accessible to all. So why wait? Gather your ingredients, put on your apron, and embark on a culinary adventure that will leave you with a delectable tart that’s perfect for any occasion. Enjoy!
