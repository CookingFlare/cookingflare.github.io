BBQ Chicken Burritos: Sweet and Smoky Goodness


Who doesn’t love a good barbecue? The smoky flavors, the juicy meats, and the mouthwatering aromas that waft through the air—it’s a feast for the senses. And when it comes to barbecue, there’s one dish that never fails to impress: BBQ Chicken Burritos. These delectable wraps combine tender, smoky chicken with a medley of flavors that will leave you craving for more. So fire up your grill, gather your ingredients, and get ready to indulge in some sweet and smoky goodness!

Ingredients

  • 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1 cup of your favorite barbecue sauce
  • 1 teaspoon of smoked paprika
  • 1 teaspoon of garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon of onion powder
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • 4 large flour tortillas
  • 1 cup of cooked rice
  • 1 cup of black beans, rinsed and drained
  • 1 cup of corn kernels
  • 1 cup of shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1/2 cup of diced red onion
  • 1/2 cup of diced tomatoes
  • Fresh cilantro, for garnish

Instructions

  1. Preheat your grill to medium-high heat.

  2. In a small bowl, combine the barbecue sauce, smoked paprika, garlic powder, onion powder, salt, and pepper. Mix well to combine.

  3. Season the chicken breasts with salt and pepper, then brush them generously with the barbecue sauce mixture.

  4. Place the chicken breasts on the preheated grill and cook for about 6-8 minutes per side, or until they are cooked through and reach an internal temperature of 165°F (75°C). Remove the chicken from the grill and let it rest for a few minutes before slicing it into thin strips.

  5. While the chicken is resting, warm the flour tortillas on the grill for about 30 seconds on each side, or until they are pliable and slightly charred.

  6. To assemble the burritos, spread a layer of cooked rice onto each tortilla, leaving a border around the edges. Top the rice with a scoop of black beans, a scoop of corn kernels, a handful of sliced chicken, a sprinkle of diced red onion and tomatoes, and a generous amount of shredded cheddar cheese.

  7. Fold the sides of the tortilla over the filling, then roll it up tightly from the bottom to create a burrito. Repeat with the remaining tortillas and filling.

  8. Serve the BBQ Chicken Burritos immediately, garnished with fresh cilantro. You can also serve them with additional barbecue sauce, sour cream, or your favorite salsa for dipping.

Tips and Variations

  • Feel free to customize your BBQ Chicken Burritos with your favorite toppings and fillings. You can add sliced avocado, jalapenos, or even a drizzle of ranch dressing for extra flavor.

  • If you don’t have a grill, you can also cook the chicken in a skillet or bake it in the oven. Simply brush the chicken with the barbecue sauce mixture and cook it until it reaches an internal temperature of 165°F (75°C).

  • Want to make your burritos ahead of time? Simply assemble them without the toppings, wrap them tightly in foil or plastic wrap, and refrigerate them for up to 24 hours. When you’re ready to serve, just heat them in the oven or on the grill until they are warmed through.

  • Don’t have flour tortillas? You can use corn tortillas or even lettuce leaves for a lighter option.

So there you have it—BBQ Chicken Burritos: Sweet and Smoky Goodness! These mouthwatering wraps are perfect for a casual weeknight dinner, a backyard barbecue party, or even a quick and delicious lunch. The combination of tender, smoky chicken with the flavorful medley of fillings is sure to satisfy your cravings and leave you wanting more. So fire up that grill and give this recipe a try. Trust me, your taste buds will thank you!
