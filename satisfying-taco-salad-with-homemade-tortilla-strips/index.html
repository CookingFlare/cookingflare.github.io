Satisfying Taco Salad with Homemade Tortilla Strips


Taco salads are a crowd-pleasing dish that combines the flavors of a classic taco with the freshness of a salad. Today, I’m sharing with you a recipe for a satisfying taco salad with homemade tortilla strips that will take your salad game to the next level. This recipe is perfect for a quick lunch, a light dinner, or even for entertaining guests. Let’s dive right into it!

Ingredients

For the taco salad:

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 cup canned black beans, drained and rinsed
  • 1 cup corn kernels
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1 avocado, diced
  • 1 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh cilantro

For the homemade tortilla strips:

  • 4 small flour tortillas
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • Salt to taste

For the dressing:

  • 1/4 cup sour cream
  • 2 tablespoons mayonnaise
  • 1 tablespoon lime juice
  • 1 teaspoon honey
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. In a large skillet, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the diced onion and minced garlic and sauté until the onion is translucent and fragrant.
  3. Add the ground beef to the skillet and cook until browned, breaking it up with a spoon as it cooks. Drain any excess fat.
  4. Sprinkle the chili powder, cumin, paprika, salt, and pepper over the cooked ground beef. Stir well to coat the beef with the spices.
  5. Add the black beans and corn to the skillet and cook for an additional 5 minutes, until heated through.
  6. While the beef mixture is cooking, prepare the tortilla strips. Cut the flour tortillas into thin strips and place them on a baking sheet. Drizzle with olive oil and sprinkle with salt. Toss to coat evenly.
  7. Bake the tortilla strips in the preheated oven for 10-12 minutes, or until golden and crispy.
  8. In a small bowl, whisk together the sour cream, mayonnaise, lime juice, honey, salt, and pepper to make the dressing.
  9. Assemble the taco salad by layering the lettuce, beef mixture, cherry tomatoes, avocado, shredded cheddar cheese, and chopped cilantro in a large bowl or individual serving bowls.
  10. Drizzle the dressing over the salad and toss gently to combine.
  11. Top the salad with the homemade tortilla strips for an extra crunch.
  12. Serve immediately and enjoy!

Final Thoughts

This satisfying taco salad with homemade tortilla strips is a complete meal in itself. The combination of flavors and textures will leave you craving for more. The homemade tortilla strips add a delightful crunch to the salad, while the dressing ties everything together with a creamy and tangy note. Feel free to customize this recipe by adding your favorite toppings like diced bell peppers, sliced olives, or even some jalapeños for an extra kick. So, the next time you’re in the mood for a delicious and healthy salad, give this taco salad a try. Your taste buds will thank you!

Remember to get creative in the kitchen and have fun with your cooking. Don’t be afraid to experiment and make this recipe your own. Enjoy!
