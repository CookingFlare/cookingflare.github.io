Easy BBQ Chicken Quesadillas Recipe


If you’re a fan of quick, flavorful, and satisfying meals, then this Easy BBQ Chicken Quesadillas recipe is the perfect addition to your repertoire. With tender shredded chicken, gooey melted cheese, and a touch of smoky barbecue flavor, these quesadillas are sure to become a family favorite. Best of all, they can be whipped up in no time, making them a fantastic option for busy weeknights or laid-back weekend lunches.

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups cooked and shredded chicken
  • 1 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup your favorite BBQ sauce
  • 1/4 cup diced red onion
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh cilantro
  • 4 large flour tortillas
  • Cooking spray or olive oil for pan-frying

Instructions:

  1. In a mixing bowl, combine the shredded chicken and BBQ sauce. Toss until the chicken is evenly coated with the sauce.

  2. Place a large skillet or griddle over medium heat. Lightly grease the surface with cooking spray or a thin layer of olive oil.

  3. Lay a flour tortilla flat in the skillet. Spread a layer of the shredded cheddar and mozzarella cheese over half of the tortilla.

  4. Spoon a portion of the BBQ-coated shredded chicken over the cheese layer. Sprinkle some diced red onion and chopped cilantro on top.

  5. Fold the empty half of the tortilla over the filling to create a half-moon shape.

  6. Cook the quesadilla for 2-3 minutes on each side, or until the tortilla turns golden brown and the cheese melts to a gooey consistency.

  7. Remove the quesadilla from the skillet and transfer it to a cutting board. Allow it to cool for a minute before slicing it into wedges.

  8. Repeat the process with the remaining tortillas and filling ingredients.

  9. Serve the BBQ chicken quesadillas warm, with additional BBQ sauce, sour cream, or guacamole on the side for dipping.

Tips and Variations:

  • Experiment with different types of cheese, such as pepper jack or gouda, to add a unique twist to the flavor profile.
  • For a spicy kick, consider adding a sprinkle of crushed red pepper flakes or a few dashes of hot sauce to the chicken and BBQ sauce mixture.
  • If you’re short on time, you can use a store-bought rotisserie chicken and pre-shredded cheese to streamline the preparation process.

Conclusion:

These Easy BBQ Chicken Quesadillas are a fantastic way to elevate your quesadilla game with minimal effort. Whether you’re cooking for a crowd or simply craving a comforting meal, this recipe is a winner in terms of flavor and simplicity. So, next time you’re in the mood for a fuss-free yet delicious dish, give these BBQ chicken quesadillas a try and prepare to be delighted!

Enjoy!
