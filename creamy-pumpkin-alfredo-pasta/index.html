Creamy Pumpkin Alfredo Pasta


Are you ready for a mouth-watering fusion of flavors that will lift the spirits of every gathering, no matter the occasion? Look no further! Today, we’re going to share an original and scrumptious recipe with you – Creamy Pumpkin Alfredo Pasta!

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound of your favorite pasta
  • 2 tablespoons of unsalted butter
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 small onion (diced)
  • 2 cloves garlic (minced)
  • 1 cup pumpkin puree
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • Salt and ground black pepper (to taste)
  • Fresh chopped parsley (for garnishing)

Instructions:

  1. Cook the Pasta:

Start by cooking the pasta according to the package instructions in boiling, salted water until it’s al dente. Once done, drain it and set it aside.

  1. Prepare the Pumpkin Purée:

You can either use a fresh pumpkin or a canned one for this recipe. If opting for a homemade pumpkin purée, bake a small pumpkin in an oven at 375°F (190°C) for about an hour. Once cooked, remove the seeds, skin, and dice the flesh. Allow the diced pumpkin to cool, then blend it into a smooth purée.

  1. Saute the Onions and Garlic:

In a large pan over medium heat, melt the two tablespoons of unsalted butter. Add two tablespoons of olive oil and allow it to warm up. Sauté the diced onion until it softens and turns translucent, then add the minced garlic, cooking briefly until you get a pleasant aroma.

  1. Add the Pumpkin and Thickener:

Pour in the pumpkin purée, stirring thoroughly to combine with the onion-garlic mixture. Simmer for about ten minutes to reduce and intensify the pumpkin flavors. If the mixture is looking too runny, dissolve one to two tablespoons of cornstarch in a small amount of water and add it to the pan, stirring continuously until the desired thickness is reached.

  1. **Combine the Pasta and Cream:

Once the pumpkin mixture has reached your preferred consistency, remove it from the heat and pour in the heavy cream. Mix well until everything is homogeneous. Grate the Parmesan cheese and add it in as well, stirring until fully combined. At this point, you may taste the sauce and add salt and ground black pepper according to your preferences.

  1. Combine the Pasta and Sauce:

Add your cooked pasta into the pan, tossing it gently to ensure an even coating. If you’d like a more robust alfredo sauce, consider adding an extra tablespoon or two of butter or some reserved cooking water for a silkier texture. The choice is yours!

  1. Garnish and Serve:

Once your pasta is perfectly coated, garnish with fresh chopped parsley for an eye-catching pop of green. Serve your Creamy Pumpkin Alfredo Pasta immediately with some toasted bread or a side salad for a well-rounded meal.

Now that you have this delightful recipe ready, gather your family and friends for an unforgettable dining experience. Not only will you show off your culinary prowess, but you’ll also delight your guests with the unique fusion of pumpkin and alfredo flavors. Bon appétit!
