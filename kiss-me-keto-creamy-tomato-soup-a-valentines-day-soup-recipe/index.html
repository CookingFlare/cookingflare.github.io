Kiss Me Keto Creamy Tomato Soup: A Valentine's Day Soup Recipe


Valentine’s Day is just around the corner, and what better way to celebrate love than with a warm and comforting bowl of soup? This Kiss Me Keto Creamy Tomato Soup is not only delicious but also fits perfectly into a low-carb and ketogenic diet. With its rich flavor and velvety texture, this soup is sure to impress your loved one and make your Valentine’s Day extra special.

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 can (14 ounces) diced tomatoes
  • 1 can (14 ounces) tomato sauce
  • 1 cup vegetable broth
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • Fresh basil leaves, for garnish

Instructions

  1. In a large pot, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the chopped onion and minced garlic, and sauté until the onion becomes translucent and fragrant.

  2. Add the diced tomatoes, tomato sauce, vegetable broth, dried basil, dried oregano, salt, and black pepper to the pot. Stir well to combine all the ingredients.

  3. Bring the mixture to a boil, then reduce the heat to low and let it simmer for about 20 minutes, allowing the flavors to meld together.

  4. Using an immersion blender or a regular blender, puree the soup until smooth and creamy. Be careful when blending hot liquids, as they can splash. If using a regular blender, blend in batches and return the soup to the pot.

  5. Pour in the heavy cream and stir until well incorporated. Heat the soup over low heat until warmed through, but avoid boiling it.

  6. Taste the soup and adjust the seasoning if necessary. You can add more salt, pepper, or herbs according to your preference.

  7. Ladle the creamy tomato soup into bowls and garnish with fresh basil leaves. Serve it alongside some low-carb bread or a salad for a complete and satisfying meal.

Tips and Variations

  • For an extra kick of flavor, you can add a pinch of red pepper flakes or a dash of hot sauce to the soup while it simmers.

  • If you prefer a chunkier texture, you can skip the blending step and enjoy the soup with the diced tomatoes as they are.

  • To make this soup even more romantic, you can serve it in heart-shaped bowls or garnish it with heart-shaped croutons made from low-carb bread.

  • Feel free to customize this recipe by adding other keto-friendly ingredients such as cooked bacon, grated cheese, or even some cooked shrimp for a seafood twist.

Conclusion

This Kiss Me Keto Creamy Tomato Soup is the perfect dish to share with your loved one on Valentine’s Day. Its smooth and luscious texture, combined with the comforting flavors of tomatoes and herbs, will warm your hearts and nourish your bodies. Whether you’re following a ketogenic lifestyle or simply looking for a delicious soup recipe, this dish is sure to delight both your taste buds and your sweetheart. So, grab a spoon, cozy up with your loved one, and enjoy a bowl of this creamy tomato goodness. Happy Valentine’s Day!
