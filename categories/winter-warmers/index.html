Roasted Vegetable Lasagna: Winter Vegetarian Delight

Are you looking for a hearty and comforting dish to warm you up during the cold winter months? Look no further than this delicious Roasted Vegetable Lasagna! Packed with roasted vegetables, layers of gooey cheese, and a rich tomato sauce, this vegetarian lasagna is sure to become a family favorite. Ingredients To make this winter vegetarian delight, you will need the following ingredients: 2 eggplants, sliced 2 zucchinis, sliced 1 red bell pepper, sliced 1 yellow bell pepper, sliced 1 red onion, sliced 4 cloves of garlic, minced 2 tablespoons olive oil Salt and pepper, to taste 9 lasagna noodles, cooked according to package instructions 2 cups ricotta cheese 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese 2 cups tomato sauce Fresh basil leaves, for garnish Instructions Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C). In a large baking sheet, spread out the sliced eggplants, zucchinis, red and yellow bell peppers, and red onion. Drizzle with olive oil, sprinkle with minced garlic, salt, and pepper. Toss to coat evenly. Roast the vegetables in the preheated oven for about 20-25 minutes, or until they are tender and slightly caramelized. Set aside. In a 9x13-inch baking dish, spread a thin layer of tomato sauce on the bottom. Place 3 lasagna noodles on top of the sauce, making sure they don’t overlap. Spread a layer of roasted vegetables on top of the noodles, followed by dollops of ricotta cheese and a sprinkling of shredded mozzarella cheese. Repeat the layers until all the ingredients are used, ending with a layer of tomato sauce and a generous sprinkling of Parmesan cheese on top. Cover the baking dish with aluminum foil and bake in the preheated oven for 30 minutes. Remove the foil and continue baking for an additional 10-15 minutes, or until the cheese is golden and bubbly. Remove the lasagna from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes before slicing. Garnish with fresh basil leaves and serve hot. Why Roasted Vegetable Lasagna? This Roasted Vegetable Lasagna is the perfect dish to enjoy during the winter season. It’s not only delicious but also packed with nutrients from the roasted vegetables.
