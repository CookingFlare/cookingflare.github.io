10 Creative Fall Sandwiches for Lunch Lovers


As the crisp autumn air sets in, there’s no better way to enjoy the season than with a warm, hearty sandwich. Fall brings with it an abundance of flavors—roasted squash, apples, and rich cheeses—that pair perfectly between slices of bread. Whether you’re packing your lunch for work, looking to try something new at home, or just craving a cozy meal, these creative fall sandwiches will elevate your lunchtime experience.

Here are 10 unique and satisfying fall-inspired sandwiches to try this season:

1. Roasted Butternut Squash and Goat Cheese Panini

There’s nothing like the rich, velvety texture of roasted butternut squash paired with the tangy creaminess of goat cheese. This sandwich is the ultimate fall comfort food, offering a perfect balance of sweet and savory flavors.

Ingredients:

  • 1 medium butternut squash, peeled and cubed
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 4 slices sourdough bread
  • 4 oz goat cheese, softened
  • 1 tbsp honey
  • Fresh thyme leaves for garnish
  • 2 tbsp butter for grilling

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C). Toss the butternut squash cubes in olive oil, salt, and pepper. Roast in the oven for 25-30 minutes, or until golden and tender.
  2. Spread a thin layer of goat cheese on two slices of sourdough bread.
  3. Once the squash is roasted, layer it onto the cheese-covered bread. Drizzle with honey and sprinkle fresh thyme leaves on top.
  4. Place the second slice of sourdough bread on top to form the sandwich.
  5. Heat a skillet over medium heat and butter the outside of each sandwich. Grill each side until golden brown and crispy, about 3-4 minutes per side.
  6. Slice and serve warm. The sweet squash combined with the tangy goat cheese is an autumn delight.

2. Apple, Brie, and Prosciutto Croissant

This sandwich combines the fresh, crisp taste of autumn apples with the creaminess of brie cheese and the savory richness of prosciutto, all nestled inside a buttery croissant. It’s the perfect sandwich for a brunch or a light lunch.

Ingredients:

  • 2 croissants, halved
  • 1 apple, thinly sliced (preferably Granny Smith or Honeycrisp)
  • 4 oz brie cheese, sliced
  • 4 slices prosciutto
  • Arugula for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Cut the croissants in half lengthwise.
  2. Layer slices of brie cheese on the bottom half of each croissant.
  3. Add the apple slices on top of the cheese, followed by a couple of slices of prosciutto.
  4. Garnish with a handful of fresh arugula for a peppery contrast.
  5. Close the croissant and enjoy! The combination of sweet apples, salty prosciutto, and creamy brie will make your taste buds dance.

3. Turkey, Cranberry, and Brie Melt

This Thanksgiving-inspired sandwich brings together the traditional flavors of turkey, cranberry sauce, and brie cheese in a melty, toasted masterpiece. It’s the perfect way to enjoy those Thanksgiving leftovers, or simply embrace the season’s best flavors.

Ingredients:

  • 2 slices multigrain bread
  • 4 oz roasted turkey breast
  • 2 tbsp cranberry sauce
  • 2 slices brie cheese
  • 1 tbsp butter for grilling

Instructions:

  1. Spread cranberry sauce on one slice of bread and layer turkey on top.
  2. Add brie cheese slices and place the other slice of bread on top.
  3. Butter the outside of the sandwich and grill on medium heat until the bread is golden brown and the cheese is melted.
  4. Slice and serve with a side of sweet potato fries or a green salad.

4. Pumpkin and Hummus Veggie Wrap

For a vegetarian-friendly fall sandwich, this pumpkin and hummus wrap is a great choice. The roasted pumpkin pairs beautifully with the creamy hummus and crunchy veggies, making it a filling and nutritious meal.

Ingredients:

  • 1 small pumpkin, peeled, seeded, and cubed
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 4 whole wheat wraps
  • ½ cup hummus
  • 1 cup spinach leaves
  • 1 small carrot, julienned
  • ½ cucumber, thinly sliced
  • ¼ cup feta cheese, crumbled (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 400°F (200°C). Toss the pumpkin cubes in olive oil, salt, and pepper, and roast for 25 minutes, until tender.
  2. Spread hummus over the center of each wrap.
  3. Add a layer of roasted pumpkin, spinach leaves, julienned carrot, and cucumber slices.
  4. Sprinkle with feta cheese for added richness (if desired).
  5. Roll up the wraps tightly, slice, and serve. The earthy pumpkin combined with fresh veggies and creamy hummus makes this wrap a fall favorite.

5. BBQ Pulled Pork and Apple Slaw Sandwich

Sweet and smoky BBQ pulled pork paired with crunchy apple slaw makes for an unforgettable sandwich. This hearty meal combines the comforting flavors of slow-cooked pork with the lightness of fresh apples and tangy slaw.

Ingredients:

  • 2 sandwich rolls (preferably brioche or potato rolls)
  • 2 cups pulled pork (cooked with your favorite BBQ sauce)
  • 1 cup apple slaw (shredded apples, cabbage, and a tangy vinaigrette)
  • Pickled onions (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Warm the pulled pork in a saucepan until heated through.
  2. Prepare the apple slaw by tossing shredded cabbage and apples with a simple vinaigrette (olive oil, apple cider vinegar, salt, and pepper).
  3. Toast the sandwich rolls lightly, then pile on the pulled pork.
  4. Top with apple slaw and pickled onions (if desired).
  5. Close the sandwich, cut in half, and serve with a side of crispy chips. This sandwich is a smoky, sweet, and tangy treat.

6. Roasted Vegetable and Pesto Ciabatta

Roasted vegetables and pesto make a deliciously savory fall sandwich, perfect for lunch or a light dinner. The earthy flavors of the roasted veggies pair wonderfully with the fresh, herby pesto.

Ingredients:

  • 2 ciabatta rolls
  • 1 cup mixed roasted vegetables (zucchini, bell peppers, eggplant, or sweet potatoes)
  • 2 tbsp pesto sauce
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 small red onion, thinly sliced

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 400°F (200°C). Toss the vegetables in olive oil, salt, and pepper, then roast for 25-30 minutes.
  2. Slice the ciabatta rolls in half and toast lightly.
  3. Spread pesto sauce on each side of the ciabatta rolls.
  4. Layer the roasted vegetables and thinly sliced red onions onto the rolls.
  5. Close the sandwich, slice, and enjoy the rich flavors of autumn vegetables combined with the aromatic pesto.

7. Grilled Cheese with Sweet Potato and Sage

Sweet potato and sage come together in this classic grilled cheese sandwich with a fall twist. The sweetness of the roasted sweet potato balances perfectly with the savory cheese and fragrant sage.

Ingredients:

  • 2 slices whole grain bread
  • 1 medium sweet potato, peeled and cubed
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp fresh sage, chopped
  • 2 slices cheddar cheese
  • 1 tbsp butter for grilling

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 400°F (200°C). Toss the sweet potato cubes with olive oil and roast for 25 minutes, until tender.
  2. Mash the roasted sweet potato and stir in fresh sage.
  3. Place a slice of cheddar cheese on each piece of bread and spread the mashed sweet potato mixture in between.
  4. Butter the outside of the sandwich and grill on medium heat until the bread is golden and the cheese is melted.
  5. Serve warm for a perfect fall comfort meal.

8. Mushroom and Swiss Melt

This sandwich features earthy mushrooms sautéed with garlic and onions, topped with Swiss cheese and melted to perfection. It’s a great savory option for a cool autumn day.

Ingredients:

  • 2 slices sourdough or rye bread
  • 1 cup mushrooms, sliced
  • 1 small onion, thinly sliced
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 2 slices Swiss cheese
  • 1 tbsp Dijon mustard (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Heat butter in a skillet over medium heat. Add onions and sauté until translucent.
  2. Add mushrooms and garlic and cook until the mushrooms are tender, about 5-7 minutes.
  3. Spread Dijon mustard (if using) on one slice of bread and layer with the sautéed mushrooms and onions.
  4. Top with Swiss cheese and place the second slice of bread on top.
  5. Grill the sandwich in a skillet until golden brown and the cheese is melted, about 3-4 minutes per side.

9. Fall Harvest Veggie and Avocado Toast

This open-faced sandwich is a vibrant way to enjoy the season’s produce. With roasted vegetables and creamy avocado on top of crusty bread, it’s a healthy yet filling lunch option.

Ingredients:

  • 2 slices multigrain bread, toasted
  • 1 small butternut squash, peeled and cubed
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 avocado, mashed
  • A handful of arugula

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 400°F (200°C). Toss the butternut squash cubes in olive oil, salt, and pepper, and roast for 25 minutes.
  2. Mash the avocado with a pinch of salt and spread it on the toasted bread.
  3. Top with roasted squash and a handful of fresh arugula for added flavor.
  4. Serve as an open-faced sandwich and enjoy the nutritious, fall-inspired flavors.

10. Smoked Salmon and Cream Cheese Bagel

The smoky richness of salmon combined with the tangy cream cheese creates a savory sandwich that’s perfect for any time of day. Add a few capers, red onions, and fresh dill for a delicious touch.

Ingredients:

  • 2 bagels, split in half
  • 4 oz smoked salmon
  • 2 tbsp cream cheese
  • 2 tbsp red onion, thinly sliced
  • Fresh dill for garnish
  • 1 tbsp capers (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Spread a layer of cream cheese on each half of the bagel.
  2. Top with smoked salmon, red onion slices, and capers.
  3. Garnish with fresh dill for a burst of flavor.
  4. Serve immediately as a satisfying and flavorful sandwich.

With these 10 creative fall sandwiches, you can enjoy the season’s harvest in every bite. Whether you prefer warm, cheesy melts or crisp, fresh wraps, there’s something for everyone to enjoy. Embrace the flavors of autumn and elevate your lunchtime routine with these delicious and inventive sandwiches!
