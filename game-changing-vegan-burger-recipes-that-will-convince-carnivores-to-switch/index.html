Game-Changing Vegan Burger Recipes That Will Convince Carnivores to Switch


Have you ever had one of those days where your carnivorous friend shows up at your doorstep with a burger in their hands, expecting a heartwarming welcome? And then you tell them you follow a vegan diet? Now’s your chance to make them drool with envy with these game-changing vegan burger recipes that will cleverly, stealthily, and utterly deliciously bring them over to the plant-side.

  1. Avocado and Black Bean Burgers

Avocado and Black Bean Burgers Ingredients:

  • 1 avocado
  • 1 can black beans, drained and rinsed
  • ½ cup rolled oats
  • ½ cup bread crumbs
  • 1 tsp cumin
  • 1 large egg (or 1 flax egg for vegan)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Burger buns
  • Toppings of your choosing (lettuce, tomato, onions, etc.)

Instructions:

  1. Start by mashing the avocado in a bowl, and mix it with the black beans in a separate large bowl.

  2. In a separate small bowl, combine rolled oats, bread crumbs, cumin, and a pinch of salt and pepper.

  3. If you’re using a flax egg, mix 1 tbsp ground flaxseed with 3 tbsp water and let it sit for 5 mins.

  4. Add the oat mixture to the mashed avocado and black bean mixture. If using a regular egg, add it now. If using a flax egg, add it after it’s thicken. Mix well to combine.

  5. Heat a large pan with a little oil over medium heat. Form patties using your hands, and gently place them onto the pan. Fry for around 5-6 minutes on each side or until browned.

  6. Serve with your favourite bun and toppings. Trust me, your carnivore friends will be left begging for more!

  7. Chickpea and Sweet Potato Burgers

Chickpea and Sweet Potato Burgers Ingredients:

  • 1 large sweet potato, cooked and mashed
  • 1 can chickpeas, drained and rinsed
  • ½ cup rolled oats
  • 1 tbsp paprika
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 large egg (or 1 flax egg for vegan)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Burger buns
  • Toppings of your choosing

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C). Peel the sweet potato and cut it into large cubes. Roast on a baking tray for about 30 minutes or until tender. Let it cool and then mash it in a bowl.

  2. In a separate large bowl, mix chickpeas, mashed sweet potato, rolled oats, paprika, garlic powder, a pinch of salt and pepper.

  3. If you’re using a flax egg, mix 1 tbsp ground flaxseed with 3 tbsp water and let it sit for 5 minutes.

  4. Add the oat mixture to the mashed sweet potato and chickpea mixture. If using a regular egg, add it now. If using a flax egg, add it after it has thickened. Mix well to combine.

  5. Follow the same cooking process as the Avocado and Black Bean Burgers. These patties make a hearty meal that will even satisfy the strongest carnivore cravings!

  6. Quinoa and Lentil Burger Patties

Quinoa and Lentil Burger Patties Ingredients:

  • ½ cup quinoa, cooked
  • ½ cup cooked green lentils
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup breadcrumbs
  • 1 tsp smoked paprika
  • 1 large egg (or 1 flax egg for vegan)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Burger buns
  • Toppings of your choosing

Instructions:

  1. In a large bowl, combine the cooked quinoa, lentils, chopped onion, and minced garlic. Allow the mixture to cool down.
  2. Once cooled, add the breadcrumbs, smoked paprika, a pinch of salt and pepper, and mix well.
  3. If you’re using a flax egg, mix 1 tbsp ground flaxseed with 3 tbsp water and let it sit for 5 minutes.
  4. Add the egg, if using, to the mixture and mix well. If using a flax egg, add it after it has thickened.
  5. Form patties using your hands, and gently place them onto a preheated pan with a little oil over medium heat. Fry for 4-5 minutes on each side or until browned.
  6. Serve these Quinoa and Lentil Burger Patties on a burger bun with your favorite toppings and watch as your unsuspecting carnivore friends are won over by the scrumptious taste of these healthy vegan burgers!

Conclusion

So, the next time your carnivorous friends decide to pay you an impromptu visit, surprise them with these mouth-watering vegan burgers that are packed with flavor and texture, truly game-changers for anyone daring enough to give them a try. Get ready for belly-rumbling praise and for your culinary skills to be put to the ultimate test - convincing your carnivore friends to switch to a plant-based diet. Who knows, maybe, just maybe, these delicious burgers will plant the first seed of change in their minds!
