Flavorful Instant Pot Chicken Curry Recipes for Indian Food Lovers


If you’re a fan of Indian cuisine and are looking to add some authentic and flavorful dishes to your recipe repertoire, then you’re in for a treat! In this post, we’ll be exploring some mouthwatering Instant Pot chicken curry recipes that are sure to tantalize your taste buds and transport you to the vibrant streets of India.

Why Choose Instant Pot for Chicken Curry?

The Instant Pot has revolutionized the way we cook, making it easier and faster to prepare delicious meals, especially when it comes to Indian curries. The pressure cooking function of the Instant Pot helps to infuse the flavors of the spices and herbs into the chicken, resulting in a tender and succulent dish that would typically take hours to achieve using traditional cooking methods.

Instant Pot Butter Chicken Curry

Ingredients:

  • 2 lbs. chicken thighs, boneless and skinless
  • 1 large onion, finely chopped
  • 4 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1-inch ginger, grated
  • 1 cup tomato puree
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 2 tbsp ghee
  • 1 tbsp garam masala
  • 1 tsp ground turmeric
  • 1 tsp ground cumin
  • 1 tsp ground coriander
  • 1 tsp Kashmiri chili powder
  • Salt, to taste
  • Fresh cilantro leaves, for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Turn on the Instant Pot to the sauté mode and heat the ghee. Add the chopped onions and sauté until they turn translucent.
  2. Add the minced garlic and grated ginger, and cook for another minute.
  3. Add the chicken thighs to the pot and brown them on all sides.
  4. Stir in the garam masala, turmeric, cumin, coriander, and Kashmiri chili powder. Cook for a minute to toast the spices.
  5. Pour in the tomato puree and season with salt. Stir well to combine all the ingredients.
  6. Close the Instant Pot lid and set the pressure cook mode for 10 minutes.
  7. Once the cooking cycle is complete, allow the pressure to release naturally for 10 minutes, then do a quick pressure release.
  8. Stir in the heavy cream and let the curry simmer for a few minutes until it thickens.
  9. Garnish with fresh cilantro leaves and serve hot with steamed rice or naan.

Instant Pot Chicken Tikka Masala

Ingredients:

  • 2 lbs. chicken breast, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 1 large onion, diced
  • 4 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1-inch ginger, grated
  • 1 cup plain yogurt
  • 1 cup tomato puree
  • 1 cup chicken broth
  • 2 tbsp ghee
  • 1 tbsp garam masala
  • 1 tsp ground cumin
  • 1 tsp ground coriander
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • 1 tsp ground turmeric
  • Salt, to taste
  • Fresh cilantro leaves, for garnish

Instructions:

  1. In the Instant Pot, heat the ghee using the sauté mode. Add the diced onions and sauté until they turn golden brown.
  2. Add the minced garlic and grated ginger, and cook for another minute.
  3. Stir in the garam masala, cumin, coriander, paprika, and turmeric. Cook for a minute to toast the spices.
  4. Add the chicken pieces to the pot and coat them well with the spice mixture.
  5. Pour in the tomato puree, chicken broth, and plain yogurt. Season with salt and mix everything thoroughly.
  6. Close the Instant Pot lid and set the pressure cook mode for 8 minutes.
  7. Once the cooking cycle is complete, allow the pressure to release naturally for 10 minutes, then do a quick pressure release.
  8. Garnish with fresh cilantro leaves and serve hot with steamed rice or naan.

Final Thoughts

These Instant Pot chicken curry recipes are a delightful way to bring the rich and aromatic flavors of Indian cuisine into your home kitchen. Whether you’re hosting a dinner party or simply craving a comforting meal, these recipes are guaranteed to impress and satisfy your craving for authentic Indian flavors. So, dust off your Instant Pot and let’s embark on a culinary journey to India without leaving the comfort of your own home!
