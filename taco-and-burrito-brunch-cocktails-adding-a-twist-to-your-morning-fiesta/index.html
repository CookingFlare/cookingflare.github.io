Taco and Burrito Brunch Cocktails: Adding a Twist to Your Morning Fiesta


Who says cocktails are just for the evening? If you’re a fan of brunch and love to add a little extra fiesta to your weekend mornings, then you’re in for a treat. In this blog post, we’re going to take your brunch game to the next level with some tantalizing taco and burrito-inspired cocktails. These drinks are not only delicious but also a perfect complement to your favorite brunch dishes. So, roll up your sleeves and get ready to mix up some magic!

Spicy Margarita Twist

Let’s start with a classic with a twist – the Spicy Margarita. This zesty concoction brings the vibrant flavors of a traditional margarita and adds a spicy kick to wake up your taste buds. To make this fiesta in a glass, you’ll need:

  • 2 oz tequila
  • 1 oz lime juice
  • 1/2 oz agave syrup
  • A splash of orange liqueur
  • Sliced jalapenos for garnish

To prepare, simply combine the tequila, lime juice, agave syrup, and orange liqueur in a shaker with ice. Shake vigorously and strain into a salt-rimmed glass filled with ice. Garnish with a slice of jalapeno for an extra fiery touch. This spicy margarita is the perfect partner for your brunch burrito and will definitely add a zing to your morning fiesta.

Breakfast Burrito Bloody Mary

Next up, we have a savory sensation that combines the heartiness of a breakfast burrito with the tangy goodness of a Bloody Mary. This Breakfast Burrito Bloody Mary is a meal in itself and is sure to become a brunch favorite. Here’s what you’ll need:

  • 2 oz vodka
  • 4 oz tomato juice
  • 1/2 oz lemon juice
  • A dash of hot sauce
  • Pinch of salt and pepper
  • Mini breakfast burrito for garnish

Start by mixing the vodka, tomato juice, lemon juice, hot sauce, salt, and pepper in a shaker with ice. Shake well and strain into a glass filled with ice. Garnish with a mini breakfast burrito – yes, you read that right! The combination of the savory cocktail with the mini burrito garnish is a delightful twist on the classic Bloody Mary and will make your brunch stand out from the rest.

Chilaquiles Tequila Sunrise

Last but not least, we have a vibrant and refreshing cocktail inspired by the flavors of chilaquiles – the Chilaquiles Tequila Sunrise. This cocktail brings together the sunny flavors of a tequila sunrise and the vibrant essence of chilaquiles, creating a drink that’s as visually stunning as it is delicious. Here’s what you’ll need:

  • 2 oz tequila
  • 4 oz orange juice
  • 1/2 oz grenadine
  • Lime wedge and tortilla chip for garnish

To create this masterpiece, pour the tequila and orange juice into a glass filled with ice. Slowly pour the grenadine over the back of a spoon to create the sunrise effect. Garnish with a lime wedge and a small tortilla chip for that extra crunch. This Chilaquiles Tequila Sunrise is a perfect accompaniment to your brunch taco and will bring a burst of flavor to your morning fiesta.

Time to Fiesta!

There you have it – three tantalizing taco and burrito brunch cocktails that are guaranteed to take your morning fiesta to the next level. Whether you’re hosting a brunch party or simply want to add a little extra flair to your weekend mornings, these cocktails are sure to impress. So, gather your friends, put on some lively music, and get ready to fiesta like never before with these creative and delicious brunch cocktails. Cheers to a morning filled with flavor and fun!
