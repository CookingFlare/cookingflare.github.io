Flavorful Low Carb Teriyaki Salmon

4 min · 685 words

Salmon is one of the most popular types of fish, and for good reason. It’s rich in omega-3 fatty acids, protein, and various essential vitamins and minerals. However, many people may shy away from cooking salmon due to its high fat content, which can make it seem like a less-than-ideal choice for those following a low-carb diet. Fear not, dear readers, for we have a solution that will satisfy your cravings and keep your carb intake in check: Flavorful Low Carb Teriyaki Salmon!

Ingredients

  • 4 salmon fillets (6 oz each)
  • 1/4 cup low-sugar soy sauce
  • 1/4 cup low-carb teriyaki sauce
  • 2 tbsp brown sugar
  • 2 tbsp grated fresh ginger
  • 2 tbsp garlic, minced
  • 1 tsp sesame oil
  • 1 tsp black pepper
  • 1/4 cup chopped green onions for garnish
  • 1/4 cup toasted sesame seeds for garnish

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C).
  2. In a small saucepan, combine soy sauce, teriyaki sauce, brown sugar, ginger, garlic, and sesame oil. Bring the mixture to a boil over medium-high heat, then reduce the heat to medium-low and simmer for 5-7 minutes, or until the sauce has thickened slightly.
  3. Line a baking sheet with parchment paper or aluminum foil. Place the salmon fillets on the prepared baking sheet.
  4. Brush the teriyaki sauce evenly over both sides of the salmon fillets. Season with black pepper to taste.
  5. Bake the salmon for 12-15 minutes, or until it reaches an internal temperature of 145°F (63°C).
  6. Remove the salmon from the oven and garnish with chopped green onions and toasted sesame seeds.
  7. Serve immediately and enjoy!

Health Benefits

Salmon is an excellent source of omega-3 fatty acids, which have been shown to improve heart health, reduce inflammation, and even alleviate symptoms of depression and anxiety. The low-carb teriyaki sauce used in this recipe is a healthier alternative to traditional teriyaki sauces, which are often high in sugar and refined carbohydrates. By using a low-sugar soy sauce and reducing the amount of sugar in the sauce, we can enjoy the flavors of teriyaki without sacrificing our dietary goals.

Variations

While this recipe is designed to be a low-carb and flavorful way to prepare salmon, there are several variations you can try to mix things up. For example, you could add some sliced bell peppers or mushrooms to the baking sheet with the salmon for added flavor and nutrients. Alternatively, you could substitute the soy sauce with tamari or coconut aminos for a gluten-free option.

Conclusion

Flavorful Low Carb Teriyaki Salmon is a delicious and healthy recipe that’s perfect for those following a low-carb diet. By using a low-sugar soy sauce and reducing the amount of sugar in the teriyaki sauce, we can enjoy the flavors of this popular dish without sacrificing our dietary goals. Whether you’re a seasoned cook or a beginner in the kitchen, this recipe is sure to become a staple in your household. So go ahead, give it a try, and indulge in the rich flavors and health benefits of this incredible dish!
