Taste of Italy: 5 Marinades for Grilled Chicken Parmesan


Are you craving the bold flavors of Italy? Look no further than these five mouthwatering marinades for grilled chicken Parmesan. With a perfect balance of herbs, spices, and Italian flair, these marinades will take your grilled chicken to a whole new level. Whether you’re hosting a backyard barbecue or simply looking to elevate your weeknight dinner, these recipes are sure to impress. So, fire up the grill and get ready to indulge in the taste of Italy!

1. Classic Italian Herb Marinade

Ingredients:

  • 1/4 cup extra virgin olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons balsamic vinegar
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 tablespoon dried oregano
  • 1 tablespoon dried basil
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • Salt and pepper, to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, whisk together the olive oil, balsamic vinegar, minced garlic, dried oregano, dried basil, dried thyme, salt, and pepper.
  2. Place the chicken breasts in a resealable plastic bag and pour the marinade over them.
  3. Seal the bag and refrigerate for at least 2 hours, or overnight for maximum flavor.
  4. Preheat the grill to medium-high heat. Remove the chicken from the marinade and discard the excess.
  5. Grill the chicken for 6-8 minutes per side, or until cooked through.
  6. Serve the grilled chicken Parmesan with your favorite marinara sauce and melted mozzarella cheese.

2. Lemon Garlic Marinade

Ingredients:

  • Juice of 2 lemons
  • Zest of 1 lemon
  • 4 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1/4 cup extra virgin olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon chopped fresh parsley
  • Salt and pepper, to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, combine the lemon juice, lemon zest, minced garlic, olive oil, chopped parsley, salt, and pepper.
  2. Place the chicken breasts in a resealable plastic bag and pour the marinade over them.
  3. Seal the bag and refrigerate for at least 1 hour, or up to 4 hours.
  4. Preheat the grill to medium-high heat. Remove the chicken from the marinade and discard the excess.
  5. Grill the chicken for 6-8 minutes per side, or until cooked through.
  6. Top the grilled chicken Parmesan with a squeeze of fresh lemon juice before serving.

3. Sun-Dried Tomato and Basil Marinade

Ingredients:

  • 1/4 cup sun-dried tomatoes, packed in oil, drained and chopped
  • 2 tablespoons chopped fresh basil
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1/4 cup extra virgin olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons balsamic vinegar
  • Salt and pepper, to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a blender or food processor, combine the sun-dried tomatoes, chopped basil, minced garlic, olive oil, balsamic vinegar, salt, and pepper. Blend until smooth.
  2. Place the chicken breasts in a resealable plastic bag and pour the marinade over them.
  3. Seal the bag and refrigerate for at least 2 hours, or overnight for maximum flavor.
  4. Preheat the grill to medium-high heat. Remove the chicken from the marinade and discard the excess.
  5. Grill the chicken for 6-8 minutes per side, or until cooked through.
  6. Sprinkle some fresh basil on top of the grilled chicken Parmesan for an extra burst of flavor.

4. Spicy Arrabbiata Marinade

Ingredients:

  • 1/4 cup extra virgin olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons red wine vinegar
  • 2 tablespoons tomato paste
  • 1 teaspoon crushed red pepper flakes
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • Salt and pepper, to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, whisk together the olive oil, red wine vinegar, tomato paste, crushed red pepper flakes, dried oregano, dried basil, salt, and pepper.
  2. Place the chicken breasts in a resealable plastic bag and pour the marinade over them.
  3. Seal the bag and refrigerate for at least 2 hours, or overnight for maximum flavor.
  4. Preheat the grill to medium-high heat. Remove the chicken from the marinade and discard the excess.
  5. Grill the chicken for 6-8 minutes per side, or until cooked through.
  6. Serve the grilled chicken Parmesan with a spicy arrabbiata sauce for an extra kick.

5. Pesto and Mozzarella Marinade

Ingredients:

  • 1/4 cup prepared pesto
  • 2 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 4 slices fresh mozzarella cheese
  • Salt and pepper, to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, combine the pesto, olive oil, minced garlic, salt, and pepper.
  2. Place the chicken breasts in a resealable plastic bag and pour the marinade over them.
  3. Seal the bag and refrigerate for at least 1 hour, or up to 4 hours.
  4. Preheat the grill to medium-high heat. Remove the chicken from the marinade and discard the excess.
  5. Grill the chicken for 6-8 minutes per side, or until cooked through.
  6. Top each grilled chicken breast with a slice of fresh mozzarella cheese and let it melt before serving.

There you have it - five delicious marinades for grilled chicken Parmesan that will transport your taste buds to Italy. Each marinade offers a unique flavor profile, allowing you to experiment and find your favorite combination. So, gather your ingredients, fire up the grill, and savor the authentic taste of Italy right in your own backyard. Buon appetito!
