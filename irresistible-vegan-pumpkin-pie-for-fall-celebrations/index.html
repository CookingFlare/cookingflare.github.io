Irresistible Vegan Pumpkin Pie for Fall Celebrations


As the leaves start to change and the air becomes crisp, it’s time to embrace the flavors of fall. One iconic treat that instantly comes to mind is pumpkin pie. This classic dessert evokes feelings of warmth and comfort, making it the perfect addition to any fall celebration. Whether you’re hosting a cozy gathering with friends or simply want to treat yourself to a delightful dessert, this vegan pumpkin pie recipe is a must-try.

Ingredients

For the Crust:

  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup coconut oil, solid
  • 1/4 cup cold water
  • 1 tablespoon maple syrup
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt

For the Filling:

  • 1 can (15 ounces) pumpkin puree
  • 3/4 cup full-fat coconut milk
  • 1/2 cup brown sugar
  • 1/4 cup cornstarch
  • 2 teaspoons pumpkin pie spice
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt

Instructions

Step 1: Prepare the Crust

  1. In a large bowl, combine the all-purpose flour and salt.
  2. Add the solid coconut oil to the flour mixture and use a pastry cutter or fork to cut the oil into the flour until it resembles coarse crumbs.
  3. In a small bowl, mix the cold water and maple syrup. Gradually add the water mixture to the flour and coconut oil, stirring until the dough comes together.
  4. Form the dough into a ball, wrap it in plastic wrap, and refrigerate for at least 30 minutes.

Step 2: Preheat the Oven

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C).

Step 3: Roll Out the Dough

  1. Once the dough has chilled, remove it from the refrigerator and place it on a lightly floured surface.
  2. Roll the dough into a circle large enough to fit into a 9-inch pie dish.

Step 4: Assemble the Pie

  1. Carefully transfer the rolled-out dough to the pie dish and press it firmly into the bottom and sides.
  2. Trim any excess dough hanging over the edge of the pie dish.

Step 5: Make the Filling

  1. In a large bowl, whisk together the pumpkin puree, coconut milk, brown sugar, cornstarch, pumpkin pie spice, vanilla extract, and salt until smooth and well combined.

Step 6: Bake the Pie

  1. Pour the pumpkin filling into the prepared pie crust and smooth the top with a spatula.
  2. Place the pie in the preheated oven and bake for 60-70 minutes, or until the filling is set and the crust is golden brown.

Step 7: Cool and Serve

  1. Once baked, remove the pie from the oven and allow it to cool to room temperature before slicing.
  2. Serve the vegan pumpkin pie with a dollop of coconut whipped cream or a sprinkle of cinnamon for an extra touch of indulgence.

Embracing the Flavors of Fall

This vegan pumpkin pie captures the essence of fall in every bite. The creamy, spiced filling paired with a flaky, golden crust is a delightful combination that will please vegans and non-vegans alike. Whether you’re celebrating the season with loved ones or simply savoring a quiet evening at home, this irresistible dessert is the perfect way to welcome fall.

So, as the air turns crisp and the leaves begin to change, take a moment to indulge in the comforting flavors of the season with this delectable vegan pumpkin pie. It’s a treat that embodies the spirit of fall and is sure to become a beloved addition to your autumn traditions.
