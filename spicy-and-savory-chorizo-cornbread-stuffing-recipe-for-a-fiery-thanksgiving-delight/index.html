Spicy and Savory Chorizo Cornbread Stuffing Recipe for a Fiery Thanksgiving Delight


Thanksgiving is a time for indulging in delicious, comforting dishes that warm the soul. If you’re looking to spice up your traditional Thanksgiving feast this year, look no further than this Spicy and Savory Chorizo Cornbread Stuffing recipe. Packed with bold flavors and a fiery kick, this stuffing will take your taste buds on a thrilling adventure.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound chorizo, casing removed
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 2 stalks celery, diced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 red bell pepper, diced
  • 1 jalapeño pepper, seeded and finely chopped
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon smoked paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1/2 teaspoon chili powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1 cup chicken broth
  • 8 cups cornbread, cubed and dried
  • 1/2 cup chopped fresh cilantro
  • 2 tablespoons unsalted butter, melted

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C).

  2. In a large skillet over medium heat, cook the chorizo, breaking it up into crumbles, until browned and cooked through. Remove the chorizo from the skillet and set aside.

  3. In the same skillet, sauté the onion, celery, garlic, red bell pepper, and jalapeño pepper until softened, about 5 minutes.

  4. Add the cooked chorizo back into the skillet, along with the cumin, smoked paprika, dried oregano, chili powder, salt, and black pepper. Stir well to combine and cook for an additional 2 minutes to allow the flavors to meld together.

  5. Pour in the chicken broth and bring the mixture to a simmer. Cook for 5 minutes, or until the liquid has reduced slightly.

  6. In a large mixing bowl, combine the dried cornbread cubes, chorizo mixture, and fresh cilantro. Toss gently to ensure all the ingredients are evenly distributed.

  7. Drizzle the melted butter over the cornbread mixture and toss again to coat everything in the buttery goodness.

  8. Transfer the stuffing to a greased baking dish, spreading it out evenly.

  9. Bake in the preheated oven for 30-35 minutes, or until the top is golden brown and the stuffing is heated through.

  10. Remove from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes before serving.

Serving Suggestions

This Spicy and Savory Chorizo Cornbread Stuffing is the perfect accompaniment to your Thanksgiving turkey, adding a fiery twist to the holiday table. Serve it alongside cranberry sauce, mashed potatoes, and roasted vegetables for a well-rounded Thanksgiving feast.

Final Thoughts

With its bold flavors and irresistible spiciness, this Chorizo Cornbread Stuffing will surely become a Thanksgiving favorite. The combination of savory chorizo, aromatic spices, and fluffy cornbread creates a harmony of taste and texture that will leave your guests craving for more. So, this year, dare to bring a fiery delight to your Thanksgiving table and enjoy the deliciousness of this unique stuffing recipe. Happy cooking and happy Thanksgiving!

Note: Remember to adjust the spice level according to your preference. If you prefer a milder stuffing, reduce the amount of jalapeño pepper or omit it entirely.
