Thai Peanut Quinoa Bowl: A Nutty and Satisfying Asian Inspired Meal


If you’re looking for a delicious and nutritious meal that combines the flavors of Thailand with the health benefits of quinoa, look no further than this Thai Peanut Quinoa Bowl. Packed with nutty goodness and inspired by Asian cuisine, this bowl is a perfect option for those seeking a satisfying and flavorful meal.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup quinoa, rinsed
  • 2 cups water
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 red bell pepper, thinly sliced
  • 1 cup shredded carrots
  • 1 cup sliced cucumber
  • 1 cup shredded purple cabbage
  • 1 cup chopped fresh cilantro
  • 1 cup chopped peanuts

For the Thai Peanut Sauce:

  • 1/4 cup creamy peanut butter
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons rice vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon honey
  • 1 tablespoon sesame oil
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon grated ginger
  • Juice of 1 lime

Instructions

  1. In a medium saucepan, combine the quinoa and water. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat to low, cover, and simmer for 15-20 minutes, or until the quinoa is tender and the water is absorbed. Fluff with a fork and set aside.

  2. While the quinoa is cooking, heat the olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Add the sliced red bell pepper and cook for 2-3 minutes, until slightly softened. Remove from heat and set aside.

  3. In a small bowl, whisk together all the ingredients for the Thai Peanut Sauce until well combined.

  4. In a large mixing bowl, combine the cooked quinoa, sliced red bell pepper, shredded carrots, sliced cucumber, shredded purple cabbage, chopped fresh cilantro, and chopped peanuts. Pour the Thai Peanut Sauce over the bowl and toss until everything is well coated.

  5. Serve the Thai Peanut Quinoa Bowl in individual bowls and garnish with additional chopped peanuts and cilantro, if desired. Enjoy!

Why You’ll Love This Thai Peanut Quinoa Bowl

  1. Nutty and Satisfying: The combination of quinoa and peanuts in this bowl adds a delightful nuttiness that satisfies your taste buds and leaves you feeling full and satisfied.

  2. Asian Inspired Flavors: The Thai Peanut Sauce brings together the flavors of soy sauce, sesame oil, garlic, and ginger, creating a deliciously authentic Asian taste that will transport you to the streets of Thailand.

  3. Healthy and Nutritious: Quinoa is a complete protein, meaning it contains all nine essential amino acids. It’s also rich in fiber, vitamins, and minerals, making it a great choice for those looking to incorporate more nutritious ingredients into their diet.

  4. Customizable: This Thai Peanut Quinoa Bowl is highly customizable. Feel free to add or substitute any vegetables or toppings of your choice to make it your own.

  5. Quick and Easy: With simple ingredients and straightforward instructions, this recipe is easy to whip up, even on busy weeknights.

So, what are you waiting for? Give this Thai Peanut Quinoa Bowl a try and indulge in the nutty and satisfying flavors of Asia. Your taste buds and your body will thank you!
