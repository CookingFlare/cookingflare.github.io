Creamy Parmesan and Garlic Mashed Cauliflower Recipe for a Low-Carb Side Dish


Looking for a delicious low-carb alternative to traditional mashed potatoes? Look no further! This Creamy Parmesan and Garlic Mashed Cauliflower recipe is the perfect side dish to elevate any meal. With its rich, velvety texture and savory flavors, it’s sure to become a new favorite in your recipe collection.

Ingredients

  • 1 head of cauliflower, cut into florets
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1/4 cup of grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 tablespoons of unsalted butter
  • 1/4 cup of heavy cream
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh chives for garnish (optional)

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Cauliflower: Start by bringing a large pot of water to a boil. Add the cauliflower florets and cook for about 10-15 minutes, or until they are tender. Drain the cauliflower and pat dry with a paper towel to remove excess moisture.

  2. Sauté the Garlic: In a small skillet, melt the butter over medium heat. Add the minced garlic and sauté for 1-2 minutes, or until it becomes fragrant. Be careful not to let the garlic brown.

  3. Blend the Mixture: In a food processor or blender, combine the cooked cauliflower, sautéed garlic, grated Parmesan cheese, and heavy cream. Blend until the mixture is smooth and creamy. If necessary, you can add more cream to achieve your desired consistency.

  4. Season to Perfection: Transfer the cauliflower mixture to a mixing bowl and season with salt and pepper to taste. Stir well to ensure the seasonings are evenly distributed throughout the mash.

  5. Garnish and Serve: Once seasoned, transfer the mashed cauliflower to a serving dish. If desired, garnish with freshly chopped chives for a pop of color and added freshness.

Tips and Tricks

  • For a vegan-friendly version, you can use a plant-based butter and substitute the Parmesan cheese with nutritional yeast for a cheesy flavor.
  • Experiment with different herbs and spices to customize the flavor profile of the mashed cauliflower. Thyme, rosemary, or a dash of cayenne pepper can add an extra dimension to the dish.
  • If you prefer a chunkier texture, you can use a potato masher instead of a blender to mash the cauliflower.

Why Choose Mashed Cauliflower?

Mashed cauliflower is an excellent low-carb alternative to traditional mashed potatoes. Cauliflower is rich in vitamins, minerals, and antioxidants, making it a nutritious choice for your side dish. Additionally, this recipe is keto-friendly and gluten-free, making it suitable for a variety of dietary preferences.

The creamy texture of the mashed cauliflower, combined with the savory notes of garlic and Parmesan, creates a satisfying dish that will leave you feeling satisfied without the guilt of consuming excess carbs. Whether you’re following a low-carb lifestyle or simply looking to incorporate more vegetables into your diet, this recipe is a versatile and flavorful option.

So, next time you’re planning a meal, consider adding this Creamy Parmesan and Garlic Mashed Cauliflower to your menu. Its simplicity, delicious taste, and healthful qualities make it a winning choice for any occasion.

Enjoy your culinary adventure and savor every creamy, flavorful bite of this delightful low-carb side dish!
