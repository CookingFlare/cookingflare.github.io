Whip Up a Quick and Easy Stir Fry with Beef and Broccoli


Stir-fries are a fantastic way to create a delicious and nutritious meal in a short amount of time. One of the most classic and beloved stir-fry recipes is the Beef and Broccoli stir-fry. This dish is not only quick and easy to make, but it’s also packed with flavor and healthy ingredients. If you’re looking for a satisfying meal that you can whip up in no time, this recipe is perfect for you.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound of flank steak, thinly sliced
  • 3 cups of broccoli florets
  • 1 red bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 yellow bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 small onion, sliced
  • 3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon of fresh ginger, grated
  • 1/2 cup of low-sodium soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons of oyster sauce
  • 1 tablespoon of brown sugar
  • 1 tablespoon of cornstarch
  • 1/2 cup of beef broth
  • 2 tablespoons of vegetable oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Cooked rice for serving

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Beef: In a bowl, mix the soy sauce, oyster sauce, brown sugar, and cornstarch. Add the sliced flank steak to the bowl and toss to coat. Let it marinate for at least 15 minutes.

  2. Cook the Broccoli: In a large skillet or wok, heat 1 tablespoon of vegetable oil over medium-high heat. Add the broccoli florets and stir-fry for 3-4 minutes, or until they are tender-crisp. Remove the broccoli from the skillet and set aside.

  3. Stir-fry the Beef: In the same skillet, add the remaining tablespoon of vegetable oil. Add the marinated flank steak and stir-fry for 2-3 minutes, or until it’s browned and cooked through. Remove the beef from the skillet and set aside.

  4. Sauté the Aromatics: In the same skillet, add the sliced bell peppers, onion, garlic, and ginger. Sauté for 2-3 minutes, or until the vegetables are tender.

  5. Combine and Simmer: Return the beef and broccoli to the skillet with the sautéed vegetables. Pour in the beef broth and stir to combine. Allow the mixture to simmer for 2-3 minutes, or until the sauce has thickened and the beef and vegetables are heated through. Season with salt and pepper to taste.

  6. Serve: Serve the beef and broccoli stir-fry over cooked rice and garnish with sesame seeds or sliced green onions if desired.

Tips for Success

  • Prep Ahead: To make this stir-fry even quicker, you can prep the ingredients ahead of time. Slice the beef, chop the vegetables, and prepare the sauce the night before, so all you have to do is cook when you’re ready to eat.

  • Customize the Veggies: Feel free to add or substitute other vegetables such as snap peas, carrots, or mushrooms to suit your taste.

  • Perfect Pairing: This stir-fry pairs perfectly with a side of steamed jasmine rice or noodles for a complete and satisfying meal.

Conclusion

This Quick and Easy Beef and Broccoli Stir Fry is a fantastic go-to recipe for busy weeknights when you want something flavorful and healthy on the table fast. With a handful of ingredients and simple cooking techniques, you can create a dish that’s sure to become a family favorite. Whether you’re a stir-fry enthusiast or a beginner, this recipe is a great way to explore the world of Asian-inspired cuisine in the comfort of your own kitchen. Enjoy the delightful flavors and the satisfaction of creating a delicious meal in no time!
