Healthy and Fresh: Greek Salad Wrap


If you’re looking for a delicious, healthy, and refreshing meal, look no further than this Greek Salad Wrap recipe. Packed with crisp vegetables, creamy feta cheese, and tangy dressing, this wrap is a perfect combination of flavors and textures. Whether you’re planning a picnic, a quick lunch, or a light dinner, this Greek Salad Wrap is sure to satisfy your cravings. Let’s dive into the recipe and discover how to make this mouthwatering dish.

Ingredients

For the Salad:

  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1 cucumber, diced
  • 1/2 red onion, thinly sliced
  • 1 bell pepper, diced
  • 1/2 cup Kalamata olives, pitted and halved
  • 1/2 cup crumbled feta cheese
  • 1/4 cup fresh parsley, chopped

For the Dressing:

  • 1/4 cup extra virgin olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons red wine vinegar
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste

For the Wrap:

  • 4 large whole wheat or spinach tortillas

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Salad:

    • In a large bowl, combine the cherry tomatoes, cucumber, red onion, bell pepper, Kalamata olives, feta cheese, and fresh parsley. Toss gently to mix the ingredients evenly.

  2. Make the Dressing:

    • In a small bowl, whisk together the extra virgin olive oil, red wine vinegar, dried oregano, salt, and pepper until well combined.

  3. Assemble the Wrap:

    • Lay out the whole wheat or spinach tortillas on a clean surface. Divide the prepared Greek salad evenly among the tortillas, placing the salad mixture in the center of each tortilla.

  4. Roll the Wrap:

    • Fold the sides of the tortilla over the filling, then roll it up tightly from the bottom, tucking the edges in as you go. Secure the wrap with a toothpick if necessary.

  5. Serve and Enjoy:

    • Slice the wraps in half diagonally and serve immediately. These wraps can also be wrapped in parchment paper or foil for a portable meal on the go.

Nutritional Benefits

This Greek Salad Wrap is not only delicious but also packed with nutrients. The fresh vegetables provide a healthy dose of vitamins and minerals, while the feta cheese adds a creamy texture and a good source of calcium. The olive oil in the dressing offers heart-healthy monounsaturated fats, making this wrap a wholesome and satisfying choice for a well-balanced meal.

Tips and Variations

  • For a protein boost, add grilled chicken or tofu to the wrap.
  • Experiment with different types of olives for a unique flavor profile.
  • Substitute the feta cheese with crumbled goat cheese or a vegan cheese alternative for a dairy-free option.
  • Serve the wraps with a side of hummus or tzatziki for an extra Mediterranean touch.

Final Thoughts

This Greek Salad Wrap is a versatile and nutritious dish that can be enjoyed at any time of the day. Whether you’re following a vegetarian diet, looking for a light meal, or simply craving the fresh flavors of the Mediterranean, this recipe is a must-try. Embrace the vibrant colors and vibrant flavors of this wrap, and savor every bite of this healthy and fresh creation.

So, why wait? Gather the ingredients, roll up your sleeves, and indulge in the delightful experience of making and savoring these Greek Salad Wraps. Your taste buds and your body will thank you for it!
