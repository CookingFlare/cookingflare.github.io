The Perfect BBQ Chicken Pizza: Tangy and Delicious


Who doesn’t love the mouthwatering combination of tangy barbecue sauce, tender chicken, and gooey cheese on a crispy pizza crust? If you’re craving a flavor-packed meal that’s perfect for a cozy night in or a casual get-together with friends, then look no further. This recipe for the Perfect BBQ Chicken Pizza will satisfy your taste buds and leave you craving more.

Ingredients

For the Pizza Dough:

  • 2 ¼ teaspoons of active dry yeast
  • 1 ½ cups of warm water (110°F)
  • 1 tablespoon of sugar
  • 3 ½ cups of all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons of olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon of salt

For the Toppings:

  • 1 cup of your favorite BBQ sauce
  • 2 cups of cooked, shredded chicken
  • 1 ½ cups of shredded mozzarella cheese
  • ½ cup of thinly sliced red onions
  • ¼ cup of chopped fresh cilantro
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

Step 1: Prepare the Pizza Dough

  1. In a small bowl, dissolve the sugar in the warm water. Sprinkle the yeast over the water and let it stand for about 5 minutes, until it becomes frothy.
  2. In a large mixing bowl, combine the flour and salt. Make a well in the center and pour in the yeast mixture and olive oil.
  3. Stir the dough until it comes together, then turn it out onto a floured surface and knead for about 5 minutes, until it becomes smooth and elastic.
  4. Place the dough in a greased bowl, cover it with a clean kitchen towel, and let it rise in a warm place for about 1 hour, until it doubles in size.

Step 2: Preheat the Oven and Prepare the Toppings

  1. Preheat your oven to 475°F and place a pizza stone or inverted baking sheet in the oven to heat up.
  2. In a small bowl, toss the shredded chicken with a few tablespoons of BBQ sauce until it’s well coated. Set it aside.
  3. Roll out the pizza dough on a lightly floured surface to your desired thickness and transfer it to a parchment-lined baking sheet.

Step 3: Assemble and Bake the Pizza

  1. Spread the remaining BBQ sauce over the pizza dough, leaving a small border around the edges.
  2. Sprinkle half of the shredded mozzarella cheese over the BBQ sauce, then top it with the BBQ chicken, sliced red onions, and the remaining cheese.
  3. Carefully transfer the pizza to the preheated oven and bake for 12-15 minutes, or until the crust is golden and the cheese is bubbly and melted.
  4. Remove the pizza from the oven, sprinkle the chopped cilantro over the top, and let it cool for a few minutes before slicing and serving.

Enjoy the Tangy and Delicious Flavor!

This Perfect BBQ Chicken Pizza is a delightful marriage of flavors and textures that’s sure to impress your family and friends. The tangy BBQ sauce, tender chicken, and melted cheese create a symphony of taste with every bite. Whether you’re hosting a casual dinner party or simply treating yourself to a comforting meal, this pizza is a guaranteed crowd-pleaser.

So, next time you’re in the mood for a homemade pizza with a twist, give this recipe a try and savor the delightful combination of flavors. Happy cooking and even happier eating!
