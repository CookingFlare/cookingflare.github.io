One Pot Creamy Mushroom Pasta


If you’re a fan of quick and easy meals that are packed with flavor, then this one-pot creamy mushroom pasta is a dish you’ll want to add to your recipe collection. Not only is it a time-saver in the kitchen, but it’s also a crowd-pleaser, making it perfect for busy weeknights or casual dinner parties. The creamy sauce, infused with the earthy flavors of mushrooms, creates a luxurious and satisfying dish that will have everyone coming back for seconds.

Ingredients

  • 12 oz (340g) of pasta (such as fettuccine or penne)
  • 2 tablespoons of olive oil
  • 1 pound (450g) of sliced mushrooms (such as cremini or button mushrooms)
  • 4 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cups (480ml) of vegetable or chicken broth
  • 1 cup (240ml) of heavy cream
  • 1/2 cup (120ml) of grated Parmesan cheese
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley for garnish

Instructions

  1. Sauté the Mushrooms: Heat the olive oil in a large pot over medium-high heat. Add the sliced mushrooms and cook until they are golden brown and have released their juices. This should take about 5-7 minutes. Remove the mushrooms from the pot and set them aside.

  2. Cook the Aromatics: In the same pot, add the chopped onion and minced garlic. Sauté them until they are soft and fragrant, which should take around 2-3 minutes.

  3. Add the Pasta and Liquid: Once the aromatics are ready, add the dry pasta to the pot, along with the vegetable or chicken broth. Bring the liquid to a boil, then reduce the heat to a simmer. Cook the pasta according to the package instructions, stirring occasionally to prevent sticking.

  4. Finish the Dish: When the pasta is al dente and most of the liquid has been absorbed, stir in the heavy cream and the sautéed mushrooms. Let the mixture simmer for a few more minutes until the sauce thickens slightly. Then, add the grated Parmesan cheese and stir until it’s melted and fully incorporated. Season the dish with salt and pepper to taste.

  5. Garnish and Serve: Once the sauce has reached the desired consistency, remove the pot from the heat. Garnish the creamy mushroom pasta with fresh parsley for a pop of color and a hint of freshness. Serve the dish hot, and watch it disappear in no time!

Tips and Variations

  • For a touch of indulgence, you can add a splash of white wine to the sauce just before adding the cream.
  • If you’re a fan of heat, consider adding a pinch of red pepper flakes or a dash of hot sauce to spice things up.
  • To make this dish vegetarian-friendly, use vegetable broth instead of chicken broth.
  • Feel free to experiment with different types of mushrooms, such as shiitake or oyster mushrooms, to add depth to the dish.

The One-Pot Magic

The beauty of this one-pot creamy mushroom pasta goes beyond its rich and velvety sauce. It’s a dish that embodies convenience and comfort, allowing you to create a memorable meal with minimal cleanup. The marriage of savory mushrooms, creamy sauce, and perfectly cooked pasta makes for a delightful dining experience that will leave everyone at the table satisfied.

So, the next time you’re in need of a hassle-free yet impressive meal, turn to this recipe for a flavorful and fuss-free solution. Whether you’re cooking for your family or entertaining guests, this one-pot wonder is sure to become a staple in your culinary repertoire. Enjoy the simplicity and satisfaction of a dish that proves that great flavor doesn’t have to come with great effort.
