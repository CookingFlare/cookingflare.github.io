Keto-Friendly Spinach and Artichoke Stuffed Chicken Breast


Are you looking for a delicious and healthy keto-friendly recipe? Look no further! This Spinach and Artichoke Stuffed Chicken Breast is not only packed with flavor but also fits perfectly into your low-carb lifestyle. This recipe is easy to make and will leave you feeling satisfied and nourished. Let’s dive into the details and get cooking!

Ingredients:

  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1 cup of spinach, chopped
  • 1 cup of artichoke hearts, drained and chopped
  • 1/2 cup of cream cheese, softened
  • 1/4 cup of grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon of dried basil
  • 1/2 teaspoon of dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C) and line a baking dish with parchment paper.

  2. In a medium-sized bowl, combine the chopped spinach, artichoke hearts, cream cheese, Parmesan cheese, minced garlic, dried basil, dried oregano, salt, and pepper. Mix well until all the ingredients are evenly incorporated.

  3. Lay the chicken breasts flat on a cutting board and make a horizontal slit in the center of each breast, creating a pocket for the stuffing. Be careful not to cut all the way through.

  4. Generously stuff each chicken breast with the spinach and artichoke mixture, dividing it equally among the four breasts. Use toothpicks to secure the openings and keep the stuffing in place.

  5. Place the stuffed chicken breasts in the prepared baking dish and season the tops with a sprinkle of salt and pepper.

  6. Bake the chicken in the preheated oven for 25-30 minutes, or until the chicken is cooked through and no longer pink in the center. The internal temperature should reach 165°F (74°C).

  7. Once cooked, remove the chicken from the oven and let it rest for a few minutes before serving. This will allow the juices to redistribute and ensure moist and tender chicken.

  8. Serve the Spinach and Artichoke Stuffed Chicken Breast as a main dish alongside a fresh green salad or roasted vegetables. The creamy spinach and artichoke filling pairs perfectly with the juicy chicken, creating a mouthwatering combination of flavors.

Tips:

  • If you prefer a crispier exterior, you can broil the stuffed chicken breasts for a couple of minutes at the end of the cooking time. This will give them a golden brown crust.

  • Feel free to customize the stuffing by adding other keto-friendly ingredients like sun-dried tomatoes, feta cheese, or even cooked bacon. Get creative and experiment with flavors!

  • Leftovers can be stored in an airtight container in the refrigerator for up to 3 days. Simply reheat in the oven or microwave for a quick and easy meal.

Conclusion:

This Keto-Friendly Spinach and Artichoke Stuffed Chicken Breast recipe is a winner for anyone following a low-carb or ketogenic diet. It combines the goodness of chicken with the creamy and flavorful stuffing, making it a satisfying and nutritious meal option. Whether you’re cooking for yourself or entertaining guests, this dish is sure to impress. Give it a try and let your taste buds thank you!
