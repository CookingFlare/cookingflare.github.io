Classic American Chicken Alfredo Recipe for Pasta Lovers


If you’re a pasta lover, there’s nothing quite like a classic American Chicken Alfredo. This rich, creamy dish is a favorite in many households and for a good reason. The combination of tender chicken, al dente pasta, and a velvety Alfredo sauce is simply irresistible. Whether you’re cooking for a special occasion or just craving some comfort food, this recipe is sure to hit the spot. So, let’s dive into the delicious world of Classic American Chicken Alfredo!

Ingredients

For the Chicken:

  • 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano

For the Alfredo Sauce:

  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter
  • 2 cups heavy cream
  • 2 cups grated Parmesan cheese
  • Salt and white pepper to taste
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon onion powder

Additional:

  • 1 pound fettuccine pasta
  • Fresh parsley for garnish

Instructions

Step 1: Prepare the Chicken

  1. Season the chicken breasts with salt, black pepper, garlic powder, and dried oregano.
  2. In a large skillet, heat olive oil over medium-high heat.
  3. Add the seasoned chicken breasts and cook for 6-7 minutes per side, or until golden brown and cooked through.
  4. Remove the chicken from the skillet and let it rest for a few minutes. Then, slice it into thin strips.

Step 2: Make the Alfredo Sauce

  1. In the same skillet used for the chicken, melt the butter over medium heat.
  2. Pour in the heavy cream and bring it to a simmer.
  3. Gradually add the grated Parmesan cheese, stirring constantly until the sauce is smooth and creamy.
  4. Season the sauce with salt, white pepper, garlic powder, and onion powder.

Step 3: Cook the Pasta

  1. Cook the fettuccine pasta according to the package instructions until al dente.
  2. Drain the pasta, reserving a cup of the cooking water.

Step 4: Combine and Serve

  1. Add the cooked pasta to the skillet with the Alfredo sauce.
  2. Toss the pasta in the sauce, adding a little of the reserved cooking water if needed to loosen the sauce.
  3. Gently fold in the sliced chicken until everything is well coated.
  4. Serve the Chicken Alfredo hot, garnished with fresh parsley.

Tips for the Perfect Chicken Alfredo

  • Use freshly grated Parmesan cheese for the best flavor and texture.
  • For an extra touch of elegance, sprinkle some additional Parmesan and freshly ground black pepper over the plated dish.
  • If the Alfredo sauce thickens too much, simply add a splash of milk or cream to adjust the consistency.

In Conclusion

The Classic American Chicken Alfredo is a timeless pasta dish that never fails to impress. Its indulgent flavors and comforting qualities make it a go-to recipe for many. Whether you’re cooking for a family dinner, a date night, or simply treating yourself, this Chicken Alfredo will always be a crowd-pleaser. So, gather your ingredients and get ready to delight your taste buds with this beloved American favorite!
