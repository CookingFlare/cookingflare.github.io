Bold and Spicy: Chipotle Chicken Burrito Recipe


If you’re in the mood for a bold and spicy meal that is sure to tantalize your taste buds, then look no further than this Chipotle Chicken Burrito recipe. Packed with flavor and heat, this dish is perfect for those who love a little kick in their meals. Whether you’re cooking for yourself, your family, or hosting a dinner party, this recipe is bound to impress.

Ingredients

  • 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 2 tablespoons of olive oil
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 red bell pepper, diced
  • 1 can of black beans, drained and rinsed
  • 1 cup of cooked rice
  • 1 teaspoon of ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon of chili powder
  • 1 chipotle pepper in adobo sauce, minced
  • 1/2 cup of chicken broth
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 4 large flour tortillas
  • 1 cup of shredded cheese
  • 1/2 cup of sour cream
  • 1/4 cup of chopped fresh cilantro
  • Lime wedges for serving

Instructions

  1. Start by seasoning the chicken breasts with salt, pepper, and a pinch of chili powder. In a large skillet over medium-high heat, warm 1 tablespoon of olive oil. Once hot, add the chicken breasts and cook until golden brown and cooked through, about 6-7 minutes per side. Remove the chicken from the skillet and let it rest for a few minutes before slicing it into strips.

  2. In the same skillet, heat the remaining tablespoon of olive oil over medium heat. Add the diced onion, minced garlic, and red bell pepper. Sauté until the vegetables are tender, about 5 minutes.

  3. Stir in the black beans, cooked rice, ground cumin, chili powder, and minced chipotle pepper. Pour in the chicken broth and let the mixture simmer for 5-7 minutes, allowing the flavors to meld together. Season with salt and pepper to taste.

  4. To assemble the burritos, lay out the flour tortillas and divide the chicken and bean mixture among them. Sprinkle each with shredded cheese, a dollop of sour cream, and a sprinkle of fresh cilantro.

  5. Fold in the sides of the tortillas and then roll them up tightly, creating a burrito shape.

  6. In a clean skillet over medium heat, warm each burrito for 2-3 minutes on each side until they are golden and crispy.

  7. Serve the burritos with lime wedges on the side for a burst of citrusy freshness.

Tips and Tricks

  • For an extra smoky flavor, you can grill the chicken breasts instead of pan-searing them.
  • If you prefer a milder heat, you can reduce the amount of chipotle pepper used in the recipe.
  • Feel free to customize the burritos with your favorite toppings such as avocado, salsa, or pickled jalapeños.

Final Thoughts

This Chipotle Chicken Burrito recipe is a celebration of bold flavors and spices. From the smoky chipotle pepper to the zesty lime, every bite is a delight for your senses. Whether you’re a fan of spicy food or just looking to add some excitement to your dinner table, this recipe is a winner. So, roll up your sleeves and get ready to savor the bold and spicy goodness of these Chipotle Chicken Burritos!
