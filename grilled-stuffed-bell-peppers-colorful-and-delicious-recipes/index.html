Grilled Stuffed Bell Peppers: Colorful and Delicious Recipes


Are you looking for a vibrant and flavorful dish that will impress your family and friends at your next barbecue? Look no further than grilled stuffed bell peppers! These colorful and delicious recipes are not only visually appealing but also packed with a medley of flavors that will leave your taste buds craving for more. Whether you’re a vegetarian or a meat lover, there’s a stuffed bell pepper recipe for everyone. So, fire up your grill and get ready to indulge!

Classic Italian Stuffed Bell Peppers

Ingredients:

  • 4 bell peppers (any color)
  • 1 cup cooked rice
  • 1/2 pound ground beef
  • 1/2 cup marinara sauce
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh basil
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your grill to medium-high heat.
  2. Cut the tops off the bell peppers and remove the seeds and membranes.
  3. In a large bowl, combine the cooked rice, ground beef, marinara sauce, Parmesan cheese, basil, parsley, dried oregano, salt, and pepper. Mix well until all the ingredients are evenly incorporated.
  4. Stuff each bell pepper with the meat and rice mixture, packing it tightly.
  5. Place the stuffed bell peppers on the preheated grill and cook for about 15-20 minutes, or until the peppers are tender and the filling is cooked through.
  6. Remove the bell peppers from the grill and let them cool for a few minutes before serving.

Vegetarian Quinoa Stuffed Bell Peppers

Ingredients:

  • 4 bell peppers (any color)
  • 1 cup cooked quinoa
  • 1 cup chopped mixed vegetables (such as carrots, zucchini, and mushrooms)
  • 1/2 cup crumbled feta cheese
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your grill to medium-high heat.
  2. Cut the tops off the bell peppers and remove the seeds and membranes.
  3. In a large skillet, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the minced garlic and cook for 1 minute, until fragrant.
  4. Add the chopped vegetables to the skillet and cook for about 5 minutes, or until they are slightly tender.
  5. In a large bowl, combine the cooked quinoa, cooked vegetables, feta cheese, chopped parsley, dried thyme, salt, and pepper. Mix well to combine all the ingredients.
  6. Stuff each bell pepper with the quinoa and vegetable mixture, packing it tightly.
  7. Place the stuffed bell peppers on the preheated grill and cook for about 15-20 minutes, or until the peppers are tender and the filling is heated through.
  8. Remove the bell peppers from the grill and let them cool for a few minutes before serving.

Spicy Mexican Stuffed Bell Peppers

Ingredients:

  • 4 bell peppers (any color)
  • 1 cup cooked chicken, shredded
  • 1 cup cooked black beans
  • 1/2 cup cooked corn kernels
  • 1/2 cup salsa
  • 1/4 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh cilantro
  • 1 teaspoon chili powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon cumin
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your grill to medium-high heat.
  2. Cut the tops off the bell peppers and remove the seeds and membranes.
  3. In a large bowl, combine the cooked chicken, black beans, corn kernels, salsa, cheddar cheese, chopped cilantro, chili powder, cumin, salt, and pepper. Mix well to incorporate all the flavors.
  4. Stuff each bell pepper with the chicken and bean mixture, packing it tightly.
  5. Place the stuffed bell peppers on the preheated grill and cook for about 15-20 minutes, or until the peppers are tender and the filling is heated through.
  6. Remove the bell peppers from the grill and let them cool for a few minutes before serving.

Conclusion

Grilled stuffed bell peppers are a fantastic addition to any summer cookout or weeknight dinner. The possibilities are endless, allowing you to experiment with different flavors and ingredients to suit your taste. Whether you prefer the classic Italian, vegetarian, or spicy Mexican version, these recipes are sure to please both your eyes and your palate. So, fire up the grill, get creative, and enjoy these colorful and delicious stuffed bell peppers!
