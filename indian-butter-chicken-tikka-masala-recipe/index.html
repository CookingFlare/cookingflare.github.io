Indian Butter Chicken Tikka Masala Recipe


If you’re a fan of Indian cuisine, then you’re in for a treat! Today, we’re going to dive into the delicious world of Indian Butter Chicken Tikka Masala. This dish is a perfect blend of rich flavors and spices, creating a mouthwatering experience that will leave you craving more. So, let’s get started!

Ingredients

For the marinade:

  • 500g boneless chicken, cut into cubes
  • 1 cup plain yogurt
  • 2 tablespoons lemon juice
  • 2 teaspoons ginger garlic paste
  • 1 teaspoon red chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon garam masala
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • Salt to taste

For the sauce:

  • 4 tablespoons butter
  • 1 large onion, finely chopped
  • 2 teaspoons ginger garlic paste
  • 2 teaspoons red chili powder
  • 2 teaspoons garam masala
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 can (400g) tomato puree
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander leaves for garnish

Instructions

Step 1: Marinating the Chicken

  1. In a large mixing bowl, combine the yogurt, lemon juice, ginger garlic paste, red chili powder, garam masala, turmeric powder, and salt.
  2. Add the chicken cubes to the marinade and mix well, ensuring that each piece is coated. Let it marinate for at least 2 hours, or overnight for best results.

Step 2: Grilling the Chicken

  1. Preheat your grill or oven to 200°C (400°F).
  2. Thread the marinated chicken onto skewers and place them on the grill or oven rack.
  3. Cook the chicken for about 12-15 minutes, turning occasionally, until it is charred and cooked through.
  4. Remove the chicken from the skewers and set aside.

Step 3: Preparing the Sauce

  1. In a large pan, melt the butter over medium heat.
  2. Add the chopped onion and sauté until it turns golden brown.
  3. Add the ginger garlic paste and sauté for another minute.
  4. Add the red chili powder, garam masala, turmeric powder, and salt. Mix well and cook for 2 minutes.
  5. Pour in the tomato puree and cook for an additional 5 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  6. Reduce the heat to low and gradually add the heavy cream, stirring continuously. Simmer for 5 minutes until the sauce thickens.

Step 4: Combining the Chicken and Sauce

  1. Add the grilled chicken to the sauce and mix gently, ensuring that each piece is coated.
  2. Cover the pan and simmer for another 10 minutes, allowing the flavors to meld together.
  3. Garnish with fresh coriander leaves.

Serving Suggestions

Indian Butter Chicken Tikka Masala is best enjoyed with steamed basmati rice or naan bread. You can also pair it with a side of cucumber raita or a fresh salad for a complete and satisfying meal. Don’t forget to serve it hot and savor every bite!

Conclusion

Congratulations! You’ve just mastered the art of preparing Indian Butter Chicken Tikka Masala. With its aromatic spices and creamy texture, this dish is a true delight for your taste buds. Impress your family and friends with this flavorful recipe and create a memorable dining experience. Happy cooking!
