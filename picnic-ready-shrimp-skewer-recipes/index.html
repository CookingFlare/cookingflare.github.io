Picnic-Ready Shrimp Skewer Recipes

4 min · 733 words

Summer is the perfect time for outdoor picnics, and what better way to enjoy the warm weather than with delicious shrimp skewers? These easy-to-make recipes are not only packed with flavor but also convenient for picnics. So grab your skewers and let’s get cooking!

1. Lemon Garlic Shrimp Skewers

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound of large shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons of olive oil
  • Juice of 1 lemon
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley, chopped (for garnish)

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, combine the minced garlic, olive oil, lemon juice, salt, and pepper. Mix well.
  2. Add the shrimp to the bowl and toss until they are well coated with the marinade. Let it marinate for at least 30 minutes.
  3. Preheat your grill to medium-high heat.
  4. Thread the marinated shrimp onto skewers, making sure to leave a little space between each shrimp.
  5. Grill the skewers for about 2-3 minutes per side or until the shrimp turns pink and opaque.
  6. Remove the skewers from the grill and garnish with chopped parsley.
  7. Serve the lemon garlic shrimp skewers hot or at room temperature. They are perfect as a standalone dish or served with a side of grilled vegetables or a refreshing salad.

2. Spicy Cajun Shrimp Skewers

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound of large shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 2 tablespoons of olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon of Cajun seasoning
  • 1 teaspoon of paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon of cayenne pepper (adjust according to your spice preference)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh cilantro, chopped (for garnish)

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, combine the olive oil, Cajun seasoning, paprika, cayenne pepper, salt, and pepper. Mix well to create a marinade.
  2. Add the shrimp to the bowl and toss until they are evenly coated with the marinade. Let it marinate for about 15-20 minutes.
  3. Preheat your grill to medium-high heat.
  4. Thread the marinated shrimp onto skewers, making sure to leave a little space between each shrimp.
  5. Grill the skewers for approximately 2-3 minutes per side or until the shrimp turns pink and opaque.
  6. Remove the skewers from the grill and garnish with chopped cilantro.
  7. These spicy Cajun shrimp skewers are bursting with flavor and pair well with a side of rice or a fresh salad. They are sure to add a kick to your picnic spread!

3. Teriyaki Glazed Shrimp Skewers

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound of large shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 1/4 cup of soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons of honey
  • 1 tablespoon of rice vinegar
  • 1 clove of garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon of grated ginger
  • Sesame seeds (for garnish)

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, whisk together the soy sauce, honey, rice vinegar, minced garlic, and grated ginger to create the teriyaki glaze.
  2. Add the shrimp to the bowl and toss until they are well coated with the glaze. Let it marinate for about 30 minutes.
  3. Preheat your grill to medium-high heat.
  4. Thread the marinated shrimp onto skewers, making sure to leave a little space between each shrimp.
  5. Grill the skewers for approximately 2-3 minutes per side or until the shrimp turns pink and opaque, basting with the remaining teriyaki glaze as you grill.
  6. Remove the skewers from the grill and sprinkle with sesame seeds for an added crunch and nutty flavor.
  7. These teriyaki glazed shrimp skewers are a crowd-pleaser and can be enjoyed on their own or paired with steamed rice and stir-fried vegetables.

Conclusion

These picnic-ready shrimp skewer recipes are not only easy to prepare but also full of flavor. Whether you prefer a tangy lemon garlic marinade, a spicy Cajun kick, or a sweet and savory teriyaki glaze, these recipes have got you covered. So the next time you plan a picnic or an outdoor gathering, make sure to include these delicious shrimp skewers on your menu. Happy grilling!
