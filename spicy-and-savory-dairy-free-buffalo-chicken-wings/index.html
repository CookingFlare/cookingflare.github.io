Spicy and Savory Dairy-Free Buffalo Chicken Wings


Are you ready to elevate your game day or party spread with a delicious and dairy-free twist on a classic favorite? Look no further than these Spicy and Savory Dairy-Free Buffalo Chicken Wings! This recipe takes the traditional buffalo wing and gives it a flavorful kick without the need for dairy products. Whether you’re catering to a dairy-free diet or simply looking to try something new, these wings are sure to be a hit with your guests.

Ingredients:

  • 2 lbs of chicken wings
  • 1/2 cup hot sauce
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons apple cider vinegar
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon onion powder
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon cayenne pepper (adjust to taste for desired level of spiciness)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh chopped cilantro for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C) and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.

  2. In a large bowl, whisk together the hot sauce, olive oil, apple cider vinegar, garlic powder, onion powder, paprika, cayenne pepper, salt, and pepper until well combined.

  3. Add the chicken wings to the bowl and toss them in the spicy marinade until they are evenly coated.

  4. Place the coated wings on the prepared baking sheet in a single layer, ensuring they are not touching each other.

  5. Bake the wings in the preheated oven for 45-50 minutes, or until they are golden brown and crispy, turning them over halfway through the cooking time to ensure even browning.

  6. Once the wings are cooked through and crispy, remove them from the oven and let them cool for a few minutes.

  7. Transfer the wings to a serving platter, sprinkle with fresh chopped cilantro for a burst of flavor, and serve them with your favorite dairy-free dipping sauce, such as a zesty avocado ranch or a tangy garlic aioli.

Why Dairy-Free?

While traditional buffalo wings often incorporate butter or other dairy products in the sauce, this dairy-free version offers a lighter and equally flavorful alternative. By using a combination of hot sauce, olive oil, and aromatic spices, you can achieve the same bold buffalo flavor without the need for dairy. This makes the wings suitable for individuals with lactose intolerance or dairy allergies, as well as those following a dairy-free or vegan diet.

Serving Suggestions:

These Spicy and Savory Dairy-Free Buffalo Chicken Wings are versatile and can be served in various ways to suit different occasions. Whether you’re hosting a game day gathering, a backyard barbecue, or a casual get-together, these wings are a crowd-pleaser. Pair them with crunchy celery sticks and carrot strips alongside a cooling dairy-free dip for a classic buffalo wing experience. Alternatively, serve them over a bed of crisp lettuce for a lighter meal option that still packs a punch of flavor.

Conclusion:

With their bold and zesty flavor, these Spicy and Savory Dairy-Free Buffalo Chicken Wings are a must-try for anyone looking to add a touch of spice to their culinary repertoire. Whether you’re a seasoned cook or a novice in the kitchen, this recipe is straightforward and delivers impressive results. So, gather your ingredients, preheat your oven, and get ready to savor the irresistible combination of heat and savory goodness with every bite of these delectable dairy-free buffalo wings.
