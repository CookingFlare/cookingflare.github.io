Hearty Slow Cooker Chili Recipe Perfect for Game Day

4 min · 656 words

As the weather starts to cool down, there’s nothing like a warm and comforting bowl of chili to satisfy your cravings. And what better way to enjoy it than with a group of friends and family on game day? In this recipe, we’ll show you how to make a hearty slow cooker chili that’s perfect for your next gathering.

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1 large onion, diced
  • 3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 2 medium bell peppers, diced
  • 2 tbsp chili powder
  • 1 tsp ground cumin
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • 1/2 tsp cayenne pepper
  • 1 can (14.5 oz) diced tomatoes
  • 1 can (15 oz) red kidney beans, drained and rinsed
  • 1 can (16 oz) pinto beans, drained and rinsed
  • 1 can (16 oz) diced green chilies
  • 1 tsp smoked paprika
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • 1/4 cup water
  • Optional toppings: shredded cheese, sour cream, diced onions, and crushed tortilla chips

Instructions:

  1. Brown the Ground Beef: In a large skillet, cook the ground beef over medium-high heat, breaking it up with a spoon as it cooks, until it’s fully browned. Drain the excess fat and set it aside.
  2. Soften the Onions: In the same skillet, add the diced onion and cook until it’s translucent and starting to caramelize. Add the minced garlic and cook for an additional minute.
  3. Add the Bell Peppers: Add the diced bell peppers to the skillet and cook until they’re tender. Add the chili powder, cumin, oregano, and cayenne pepper, and cook for 1-2 minutes, until the spices are fragrant.
  4. Add the Remaining Ingredients: Add the browned ground beef, diced tomatoes, red kidney beans, pinto beans, diced green chilies, smoked paprika, salt, and pepper to the slow cooker. Stir to combine.
  5. Add the Water: Add the 1/4 cup of water to the slow cooker and stir to combine.
  6. Cook the Chili: Cook the chili on low for 6-8 hours or high for 3-4 hours. The longer it cooks, the thicker and more flavorful it becomes.
  7. Serve and Enjoy: Serve the chili hot, topped with your choice of optional toppings.

Tips and Variations:

  • For a vegetarian option, simply omit the ground beef and add an additional can of black beans or pinto beans.
  • For a vegan option, omit the ground beef and add a can of black beans or pinto beans, as well as a splash of vegetable broth.
  • To make the chili more substantial, add some cooked ground turkey or chicken to the pot.
  • Experiment with different types of peppers, such as jalapeños or Anaheim peppers, for added heat and flavor.
  • Serve the chili with some crusty bread or cornbread for a delicious and filling meal.

Conclusion:

This hearty slow cooker chili recipe is perfect for your next game day gathering. With its rich, bold flavors and tender texture, it’s sure to be a hit with your friends and family. And with its easy-to-make instructions and customizable options, it’s a recipe that’s perfect for any occasion. So gather your ingredients, fire up your slow cooker, and get ready to enjoy a delicious and comforting bowl of chili.
