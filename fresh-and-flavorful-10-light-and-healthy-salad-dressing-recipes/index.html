Fresh and Flavorful: 10 Light and Healthy Salad Dressing Recipes


Are you tired of the same old salad dressings that are loaded with calories and unhealthy ingredients? If so, you’re in luck! In this blog post, we’re going to share with you 10 light and healthy salad dressing recipes that are not only delicious but also good for you. These dressings will add a burst of flavor to your salads without compromising on nutrition. So, let’s dive right in!

1. Lemon Herb Vinaigrette

Ingredients:

  • 2 tablespoons fresh lemon juice
  • 1 tablespoon Dijon mustard
  • 1 teaspoon honey
  • 1/4 cup extra virgin olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon chopped fresh herbs (such as basil, parsley, or dill)
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a small bowl, whisk together the lemon juice, Dijon mustard, and honey.
  2. Slowly drizzle in the olive oil while whisking continuously until well combined.
  3. Stir in the chopped herbs and season with salt and pepper.
  4. Serve over your favorite salad greens and enjoy!

2. Creamy Avocado Dressing

Ingredients:

  • 1 ripe avocado
  • 2 tablespoons plain Greek yogurt
  • 2 tablespoons fresh lime juice
  • 2 tablespoons chopped fresh cilantro
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a blender or food processor, combine the avocado, Greek yogurt, lime juice, cilantro, and minced garlic.
  2. Blend until smooth and creamy.
  3. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  4. Drizzle over your salad and savor the creamy goodness!

3. Balsamic Dijon Dressing

Ingredients:

  • 2 tablespoons balsamic vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon Dijon mustard
  • 1 teaspoon honey
  • 1/4 cup extra virgin olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a small bowl, whisk together the balsamic vinegar, Dijon mustard, and honey.
  2. Slowly drizzle in the olive oil while whisking continuously until well combined.
  3. Season with salt and pepper.
  4. Pour over your salad and enjoy the tangy and sweet flavors!

4. Ginger Sesame Dressing

Ingredients:

  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon rice vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon sesame oil
  • 1 teaspoon grated fresh ginger
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon honey

Instructions:

  1. In a small bowl, whisk together the soy sauce, rice vinegar, sesame oil, grated ginger, minced garlic, and honey.
  2. Drizzle over your salad for an Asian-inspired twist!

5. Greek Yogurt Ranch Dressing

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 cup plain Greek yogurt
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh dill
  • 2 tablespoons chopped fresh chives
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a small bowl, combine the Greek yogurt, fresh dill, chives, minced garlic, and lemon juice.
  2. Season with salt and pepper.
  3. Mix well and enjoy a healthier version of the classic ranch dressing!

6. Honey Mustard Dressing

Ingredients:

  • 2 tablespoons Dijon mustard
  • 2 tablespoons honey
  • 2 tablespoons apple cider vinegar
  • 2 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a small bowl, whisk together the Dijon mustard, honey, apple cider vinegar, olive oil, salt, and pepper.
  2. Drizzle over your salad for a sweet and tangy flavor!

7. Cilantro Lime Dressing

Ingredients:

  • 1/4 cup fresh lime juice
  • 2 tablespoons chopped fresh cilantro
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 1/4 cup extra virgin olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a small bowl, whisk together the lime juice, chopped cilantro, minced garlic, olive oil, salt, and pepper.
  2. Pour over your salad and enjoy the refreshing taste of cilantro and lime!

8. Raspberry Vinaigrette

Ingredients:

  • 1/4 cup fresh raspberries
  • 2 tablespoons balsamic vinegar
  • 2 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon honey
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a blender or food processor, blend the fresh raspberries until smooth.
  2. In a small bowl, whisk together the raspberry puree, balsamic vinegar, olive oil, honey, salt, and pepper.
  3. Drizzle over your salad for a fruity and tangy dressing!

9. Orange Almond Dressing

Ingredients:

  • 1/4 cup fresh orange juice
  • 2 tablespoons almond butter
  • 1 tablespoon rice vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon honey
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a blender or food processor, combine the fresh orange juice, almond butter, rice vinegar, honey, salt, and pepper.
  2. Blend until smooth and creamy.
  3. Pour over your salad for a nutty and citrusy dressing!

10. Creamy Tahini Dressing

Ingredients:

  • 2 tablespoons tahini
  • 2 tablespoons lemon juice
  • 2 tablespoons water
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a small bowl, whisk together the tahini, lemon juice, water, minced garlic, salt, and pepper.
  2. Drizzle over your salad and enjoy the creamy and nutty flavors!

There you have it - 10 light and healthy salad dressing recipes that will take your salads to the next level. These dressings are not only delicious but also packed with nutrients. So, go ahead and try them out. Your taste buds and your body will thank you!
