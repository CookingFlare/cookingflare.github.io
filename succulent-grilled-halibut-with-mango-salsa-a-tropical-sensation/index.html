Succulent Grilled Halibut with Mango Salsa: A Tropical Sensation

3 min · 600 words

If you’re looking for a dish that perfectly captures the flavors of summer, then look no further. Succulent grilled halibut with mango salsa is a tropical sensation that will transport your taste buds to a sun-soaked paradise. This recipe combines the delicate, flaky texture of halibut with the vibrant, sweet-tangy flavors of fresh mango salsa. Whether you’re hosting a backyard barbecue or simply craving a taste of the tropics, this dish is sure to impress.

Ingredients

For the Grilled Halibut:

  • 4 halibut fillets
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • Salt and pepper to taste

For the Mango Salsa:

  • 2 ripe mangoes, diced
  • 1/2 red onion, finely chopped
  • 1 red bell pepper, diced
  • 1 jalapeno, seeded and minced
  • 1/4 cup fresh cilantro, chopped
  • Juice of 2 limes
  • Salt to taste

For Garnish:

  • Fresh cilantro leaves
  • Lime wedges

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Grilled Halibut:

    • Preheat your grill to medium-high heat.
    • In a small bowl, mix the olive oil, garlic powder, paprika, salt, and pepper.
    • Brush the halibut fillets with the seasoned oil.
    • Grill the halibut for 4-5 minutes on each side, or until the fish is opaque and flakes easily with a fork.

  2. Make the Mango Salsa:

    • In a medium bowl, combine the diced mangoes, red onion, red bell pepper, jalapeno, and cilantro.
    • Squeeze the lime juice over the salsa and gently toss to combine.
    • Season with salt to taste.

  3. Assemble and Serve:

    • Place the grilled halibut fillets on a serving platter.
    • Spoon the mango salsa over the top of each fillet.
    • Garnish with fresh cilantro leaves and serve with lime wedges on the side.

Tips for Success

  • Choose ripe, fragrant mangoes for the salsa to ensure a sweet and juicy flavor.
  • Adjust the level of spiciness in the salsa by adding more or less jalapeno, according to your preference.
  • For a smoky flavor, you can grill the halibut on a cedar plank instead of directly on the grill grates.

Why You’ll Love This Dish

The succulent grilled halibut is the perfect canvas for the bright and zesty mango salsa. The flaky, mild flavor of the halibut pairs beautifully with the sweet, tangy, and slightly spicy salsa, creating a harmonious and refreshing combination. This dish is not only a feast for the taste buds but also a visual delight, with its vibrant colors and enticing aroma.

Final Thoughts

Succulent grilled halibut with mango salsa is a celebration of summertime flavors that will elevate any gathering or meal. The lightness of the fish, combined with the tropical freshness of the mango salsa, makes this dish a standout choice for warm-weather dining. Whether you’re enjoying it at a beachside picnic or a cozy family dinner, this tropical sensation is guaranteed to leave a lasting impression. So, fire up the grill and get ready to savor the taste of summer with this irresistible recipe.
