Summer Squash Ribbon Salad: A Fresh and Elegant Side Dish


When the heat of summer is in full swing, there’s nothing quite like a light and refreshing salad to complement a meal or serve as a standalone dish. This Summer Squash Ribbon Salad is a delightful blend of flavors and textures, making it the perfect addition to any summer gathering. The combination of vibrant summer squash, zesty lemon, and fragrant herbs creates a dish that is not only visually stunning but also bursting with fresh, seasonal flavors.

Ingredients

  • 2 medium-sized yellow summer squash
  • 2 medium-sized zucchini
  • 1/4 cup extra virgin olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons fresh lemon juice
  • 1 teaspoon honey
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh basil
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh mint
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1/4 cup crumbled feta cheese (optional)

Instructions

  1. Begin by washing the summer squash and zucchini, then use a vegetable peeler to create long, thin ribbons. You can also use a mandoline slicer if you have one, but a regular vegetable peeler works just as well. Place the ribbons in a large mixing bowl and set aside.

  2. In a small bowl, whisk together the extra virgin olive oil, fresh lemon juice, honey, and a pinch of salt and pepper. This simple vinaigrette will serve as the perfect dressing for the salad, providing a bright and tangy flavor that complements the mild sweetness of the squash.

  3. Pour the vinaigrette over the squash ribbons and gently toss to coat. Be careful not to break the delicate ribbons as you mix. The goal is to ensure that each ribbon is lightly coated with the dressing, allowing the flavors to meld together.

  4. Once the ribbons are dressed, add the chopped fresh basil and mint to the bowl. These aromatic herbs will infuse the salad with a burst of freshness, adding depth and complexity to the dish.

  5. If you’d like to add a touch of creamy saltiness to the salad, sprinkle the crumbled feta cheese over the top. The feta brings a lovely contrast to the crisp, tender squash ribbons, elevating the salad to a new level of indulgence.

  6. Give the salad a final gentle toss to incorporate all the ingredients, then taste and adjust the seasoning as needed. The salad should be vibrant and well-balanced, with the flavors of the summer squash shining through.

  7. To serve, arrange the dressed squash ribbons on a platter, allowing them to cascade in an elegant, natural manner. This visually stunning presentation adds to the appeal of the dish, making it a feast for the eyes as well as the palate.

Serving Suggestions

This Summer Squash Ribbon Salad is a versatile dish that pairs beautifully with a wide range of main courses. Its light and fresh profile makes it an ideal side dish for grilled chicken, fish, or steak. It also makes a lovely addition to a picnic or potluck spread, as it can be served at room temperature and doesn’t require reheating.

For a complete summer meal, consider serving the salad alongside a crisp white wine or a refreshing citrus-infused cocktail. The bright, herbaceous flavors of the salad are complemented by the citrusy notes of these beverages, creating a harmonious dining experience.

Conclusion

With its simple preparation and vibrant flavors, this Summer Squash Ribbon Salad is a celebration of the season’s bounty. Whether enjoyed at a casual backyard barbecue or a sophisticated dinner party, this salad is sure to impress with its elegant presentation and delightful taste. Embrace the abundance of summer produce and create this fresh and elegant side dish to elevate your summer dining experience. Cheers to the joys of summer and the pleasures of good food!
