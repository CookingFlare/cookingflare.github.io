Bountiful Easter Brunch Buffet: Recipes for a Festive Spread


Easter is a time of joy and celebration, and what better way to celebrate than with a bountiful brunch buffet? Whether you’re hosting a gathering for family and friends or simply looking to indulge in some delicious dishes, this collection of recipes will help you create a festive spread that will impress everyone.

Menu

  1. Eggs Benedict Casserole

    • This twist on the classic brunch favorite combines all the flavors of Eggs Benedict in a convenient casserole form. Layers of English muffins, Canadian bacon, and poached eggs are smothered in a creamy hollandaise sauce. It’s a crowd-pleaser that can be prepared ahead of time, making it perfect for a stress-free Easter brunch.

  2. Spring Vegetable Frittata

    • Celebrate the season with a vibrant and flavorful spring vegetable frittata. Packed with fresh asparagus, peas, and cherry tomatoes, this colorful dish is as visually appealing as it is delicious. The addition of goat cheese adds a creamy tang that complements the vegetables perfectly.

  3. Honey Glazed Ham

    • No Easter brunch is complete without a succulent honey glazed ham. This recipe takes a classic approach, with a sweet and sticky glaze made from a combination of honey, Dijon mustard, and brown sugar. The result is a beautifully caramelized ham that will be the centerpiece of your buffet table.

  4. Smoked Salmon Platter

    • Elevate your brunch spread with a stunning smoked salmon platter. Arrange thinly sliced smoked salmon on a platter and serve it with an assortment of accompaniments such as capers, red onion, dill, and lemon wedges. Provide a variety of bagels or artisan bread for guests to build their own delicious smoked salmon sandwiches.

  5. Hot Cross Buns

    • No Easter brunch would be complete without hot cross buns. These spiced and fruit-filled buns are traditionally enjoyed on Good Friday but are equally delightful on Easter Sunday. Serve them warm with a generous slather of butter for a sweet and comforting treat.

  6. Spring Salad with Lemon Vinaigrette

    • Lighten up the brunch buffet with a refreshing spring salad. Toss together a mix of fresh greens, crisp radishes, sugar snap peas, and thinly sliced fennel. Drizzle with a zesty lemon vinaigrette for a burst of citrusy flavor. The vibrant colors and crisp textures will add a touch of freshness to your spread.

  7. Carrot Cake Pancakes

    • Combine the flavors of a classic carrot cake with fluffy pancakes for a delightful Easter brunch treat. Grated carrots, warm spices, and a touch of sweetness create a pancake batter that is transformed into a stack of delectable carrot cake pancakes. Serve them with a dollop of cream cheese frosting for the ultimate indulgence.

  8. Mimosa Bar

    • Elevate your brunch drinks with a DIY mimosa bar. Provide a selection of chilled sparkling wine, fruit juices, and fresh fruit garnishes. Guests can mix and match their own mimosas, creating personalized flavor combinations to suit their taste buds. It’s a fun and interactive addition to your Easter brunch buffet.

Conclusion

A bountiful Easter brunch buffet is the perfect way to celebrate the holiday with loved ones. With these recipes, you can create a festive spread that will impress your guests and leave them craving more. From savory dishes like Eggs Benedict Casserole and Spring Vegetable Frittata to sweet treats like Hot Cross Buns and Carrot Cake Pancakes, there’s something for everyone to enjoy. So gather your loved ones, set the table with beautiful decorations, and indulge in a memorable Easter brunch that will create lasting memories.
