Indulgent Nutella Stuffed French Toast Recipe


Are you ready to take your breakfast game to the next level? Look no further than this indulgent Nutella stuffed French toast recipe. With a rich and creamy Nutella filling sandwiched between two slices of fluffy French toast, this dish is a decadent treat that’s perfect for a leisurely weekend brunch or a special occasion. Let’s dive into the delightful world of indulgent breakfasts with this mouthwatering recipe.

Ingredients

For the Nutella Filling:

  • 1 cup Nutella
  • 1/2 cup cream cheese
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 2 tablespoons powdered sugar

For the French Toast:

  • 8 slices thick-cut bread (brioche or challah work best)
  • 4 large eggs
  • 1 cup whole milk
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 2 tablespoons unsalted butter

Instructions

  1. In a mixing bowl, combine the Nutella, cream cheese, vanilla extract, and powdered sugar. Mix until smooth and well combined. Set aside.

  2. Lay out 4 slices of bread and spread a generous amount of the Nutella filling on each slice. Top each Nutella-covered slice with another slice of bread to create 4 Nutella-stuffed sandwiches.

  3. In a shallow dish, whisk together the eggs, milk, vanilla extract, cinnamon, and salt until well combined.

  4. Heat a large skillet or griddle over medium heat and add a tablespoon of butter. Once the butter has melted and the skillet is hot, carefully dip each Nutella-stuffed sandwich into the egg mixture, making sure to coat both sides evenly.

  5. Place the coated sandwiches onto the skillet and cook for 2-3 minutes on each side, or until golden brown and the Nutella filling is warm and gooey.

  6. Once cooked, remove the French toast from the skillet and place it on a serving plate. Repeat the dipping and cooking process with the remaining Nutella-stuffed sandwiches.

  7. Serve the Nutella stuffed French toast warm, dusted with powdered sugar, and drizzled with maple syrup or a dollop of whipped cream for an extra touch of indulgence.

Tips and Tricks

  • For an extra decadent twist, consider adding a sprinkle of chopped hazelnuts or a scoop of vanilla ice cream on top of the French toast.
  • If you’re feeling adventurous, try experimenting with different types of bread, such as cinnamon swirl or sourdough, for a unique flavor profile.
  • To make this recipe ahead of time, assemble the Nutella-stuffed sandwiches and store them in the refrigerator overnight. In the morning, simply dip, cook, and enjoy!

Final Thoughts

Indulgent Nutella stuffed French toast is a delightful way to treat yourself and your loved ones to a special breakfast experience. The combination of rich Nutella, creamy filling, and golden French toast creates a symphony of flavors and textures that’s sure to impress. Whether you’re celebrating a special occasion or simply craving a luxurious breakfast, this recipe is guaranteed to elevate your morning routine. So, roll up your sleeves, grab your ingredients, and get ready to indulge in the ultimate breakfast delight!
