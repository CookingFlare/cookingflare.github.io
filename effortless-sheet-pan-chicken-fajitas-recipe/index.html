Effortless Sheet Pan Chicken Fajitas Recipe


Are you tired of spending hours in the kitchen, slaving away over a hot stove, only to end up with a mediocre meal? Do you wish there was a way to cook a delicious, flavorful dish with minimal effort and cleanup? Look no further! Today, we’re going to share with you a game-changing recipe for Effortless Sheet Pan Chicken Fajitas that will revolutionize the way you cook.

What You Need

  • 1 1/2 pounds boneless, skinless chicken breasts, cut into 1/2-inch strips
  • 1 large onion, sliced
  • 2 large bell peppers (any color), sliced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon smoked paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 8 small flour tortillas
  • Optional toppings: avocado, sour cream, salsa, shredded cheese, cilantro

The Magic Happens

Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C). Line a large sheet pan with aluminum foil or parchment paper for easy cleanup. In a large bowl, whisk together olive oil, cumin, smoked paprika, salt, and pepper. Add the chicken, onion, bell peppers, and garlic to the bowl, and toss to combine until everything is well coated.

The Sheet Pan Trick

Spread the chicken and vegetables out in a single layer on the prepared sheet pan. You may need to do this in batches depending on the size of your sheet pan. The key is to make sure everything is in contact with the pan, so that it cooks evenly.

The Wait

Place the sheet pan in the oven and bake for 25-30 minutes, or until the chicken is cooked through and the vegetables are tender. You can check for doneness by cutting into one of the chicken strips or bell peppers. If they’re not quite cooked, give them a few more minutes.

The Fun Part

Once the chicken and vegetables are done, remove the sheet pan from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes. Warm the tortillas by wrapping them in a damp paper towel and microwaving for 20-30 seconds.

Assemble and Serve

Slice the chicken and vegetables into bite-sized pieces. Assemble the fajitas by placing a portion of the chicken and vegetables onto a tortilla, and adding your desired toppings. Serve immediately and enjoy!

The Best Part

This recipe is not only delicious and easy to make, but it’s also incredibly versatile. Feel free to customize it to your liking by adding your favorite toppings or using different types of protein, such as steak or shrimp. The sheet pan cooking method is also a great way to cook a variety of vegetables, so don’t be afraid to get creative and add your favorite veggies to the mix.

Conclusion

Effortless Sheet Pan Chicken Fajitas is a game-changing recipe that will revolutionize the way you cook. With minimal effort and cleanup, you can create a delicious, flavorful dish that’s perfect for a weeknight dinner or a special occasion. So go ahead, give it a try, and enjoy the benefits of sheet pan cooking!
