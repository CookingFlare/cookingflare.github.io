Mouthwatering Chicken Stir Fry Recipes That Will Leave You Craving More


Are you in the mood for a delicious and satisfying meal that can be prepared in a flash? Look no further than these mouthwatering chicken stir fry recipes! Packed with flavor and loaded with nutritious ingredients, these recipes are sure to leave you craving more. So grab your wok and get ready to whip up a feast!

1. Teriyaki Chicken Stir Fry

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound boneless, skinless chicken breasts, thinly sliced
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons teriyaki sauce
  • 1 tablespoon honey
  • 1 tablespoon sesame oil
  • 1 bell pepper, thinly sliced
  • 1 cup broccoli florets
  • 1 cup snap peas
  • 1 carrot, julienned
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon ginger, grated
  • 2 green onions, sliced

Instructions:

  1. In a small bowl, whisk together soy sauce, teriyaki sauce, honey, and sesame oil. Set aside.
  2. Heat a tablespoon of oil in a wok or large skillet over medium-high heat. Add chicken and stir fry until cooked through, about 5 minutes. Remove chicken from the wok and set aside.
  3. In the same wok, add another tablespoon of oil and stir fry the bell pepper, broccoli, snap peas, carrot, garlic, and ginger for 3-4 minutes, until vegetables are crisp-tender.
  4. Return the chicken to the wok and pour the teriyaki sauce over the mixture. Stir fry for an additional 2 minutes, until everything is well coated and heated through.
  5. Serve hot, garnished with green onions. Enjoy!

2. Spicy Basil Chicken Stir Fry

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound boneless, skinless chicken thighs, thinly sliced
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon fish sauce
  • 1 tablespoon oyster sauce
  • 1 tablespoon sriracha sauce
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • 1 bell pepper, thinly sliced
  • 1 onion, thinly sliced
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup basil leaves

Instructions:

  1. In a small bowl, combine soy sauce, fish sauce, oyster sauce, and sriracha sauce. Set aside.
  2. Heat vegetable oil in a wok or large skillet over medium-high heat. Add chicken and stir fry until cooked through, about 5 minutes. Remove chicken from the wok and set aside.
  3. In the same wok, add the bell pepper, onion, and garlic. Stir fry for 3-4 minutes, until vegetables are crisp-tender.
  4. Return the chicken to the wok and pour the sauce over the mixture. Stir fry for an additional 2 minutes, until everything is well coated and heated through.
  5. Remove from heat and stir in basil leaves until wilted. Serve hot and enjoy the spicy goodness!

3. Honey Garlic Chicken Stir Fry

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound boneless, skinless chicken breasts, thinly sliced
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 3 tablespoons honey
  • 2 tablespoons rice vinegar
  • 2 tablespoons hoisin sauce
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • 1 bell pepper, thinly sliced
  • 1 zucchini, thinly sliced
  • 1 cup sugar snap peas
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon sesame oil
  • Sesame seeds, for garnish

Instructions:

  1. In a small bowl, whisk together soy sauce, honey, rice vinegar, and hoisin sauce. Set aside.
  2. Heat vegetable oil in a wok or large skillet over medium-high heat. Add chicken and stir fry until cooked through, about 5 minutes. Remove chicken from the wok and set aside.
  3. In the same wok, add the bell pepper, zucchini, sugar snap peas, and garlic. Stir fry for 3-4 minutes, until vegetables are crisp-tender.
  4. Return the chicken to the wok and pour the sauce over the mixture. Stir fry for an additional 2 minutes, until everything is well coated and heated through.
  5. Remove from heat and drizzle with sesame oil. Garnish with sesame seeds and serve hot. Enjoy the sweet and savory flavors!

Conclusion

These mouthwatering chicken stir fry recipes are quick, easy, and guaranteed to satisfy your taste buds. Whether you prefer a tangy teriyaki sauce, a spicy basil kick, or a sweet honey garlic glaze, there’s a recipe here that will leave you craving more. So why wait? Grab your ingredients and start stir frying your way to a delicious meal that will have everyone asking for seconds. Happy cooking!
