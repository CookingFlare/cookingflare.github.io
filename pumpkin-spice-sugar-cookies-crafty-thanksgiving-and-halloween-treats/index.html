Pumpkin Spice Sugar Cookies: Crafty Thanksgiving and Halloween Treats


Prepare to be bewitched by these delightful pumpkin spice sugar cookies! Combining the allure of pumpkin with the sweetness of sugar cookies, these treats are bound to be a hit at any Halloween or Thanksgiving gathering. What’s more, they’re better than basic pumpkin recipes – they boast a delightful baker’s twist. So, put on your apron, preheat that oven, and let’s get baking!

ingredients:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground ginger
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground cloves
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1 3/4 cups granulated sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 teaspoon pure vanilla extract
  • 1/2 cup pumpkin puree
  • 1 1/2 cups powdered sugar
  • 2 tablespoons pumpkin spice blend

instructions:

Step 1: Prep the Equipment & Ingredients

Ensure all your ingredients are at room temperature. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C) and line two baking sheets with parchment paper to prevent sticking.

Step 2: The Dry Ingredients

In a medium-sized bowl, whisk together 2 cups of all-purpose flour, 1 teaspoon of baking powder, 1/2 teaspoon of baking soda, 1/2 teaspoon of ground cinnamon, 1/2 teaspoon of ground ginger, 1/4 teaspoon of ground cloves, and 1/4 teaspoon of salt.

Step 3: Cream the Butter & Sugar

In a mixing bowl, beat 1 cup of softened butter and 1 3/4 cups of granulated sugar with an electric mixer until light and fluffy. This should take about 3-5 minutes.

Step 4: Mix in Eggs & Vanilla

Gradually incorporate 2 large eggs and 1 teaspoon of pure vanilla extract into the butter-sugar mixture. Continue to mix until fully combined.

Step 5: Pumpkin Puree

Gently fold in 1/2 cup of pumpkin puree without overmixing to retain the air bubbles that will make your cookies soft and fluffy.

Step 6: Combine the Dry & Wet Ingredients

Gradually mix in the dry ingredients to the wet ingredients. Fold everything together until completely combined.

Step 7: Make the Pumpkin Spice Sugar Cookies

Using an ice cream scoop or spoon, portion out the dough into 1-inch-sized balls and arrange them on the prepared baking sheets, leaving about 2 inches of space between each cookie to allow for spreading. Flatten each cookie slightly with the palm of your hand or the back of a spoon, creating thin and delightful cookies.

Step 8: Bake Those Bad Boys

Bake for 10-12 minutes, or until the edges are lightly golden. It is crucial not to overbake the cookies, as they should remain soft and chewy.

Step 9: Cool the Cookies

Allow the cookies to cool on the baking sheets for about 5 minutes before transferring them to a wire rack to cool completely.

Step 10: Frost the Cookies

In a bowl, mix 1 1/2 cups of powdered sugar with 2 tablespoons of pumpkin spice blend. Give it a good stir until smooth.

Step 11: Frost & Decorate

Once the cookies have cooled completely, dip the top of each cookie into the pumpkin spice sugar glaze. Allow some of the glaze to drip down the sides if desired for a rustic look.

Step 12: Let the Magic Happen

Let the glaze set for an hour, and then grab a warm mug of spiced pumpkin latte and indulge in these scrumptious pumpkin spice sugar cookies!

That’s it! Your delightful pumpkin spice sugar cookies are complete and ready to bewitch your guests! These unique treats are perfect for incorporating into your Halloween and Thanksgiving celebrations, and their enchanting aroma will make your home smell like a cozy autumn wonderland.

Don’t forget – the best part about baking is sharing your creations. So, wrap up a few of these bewitching pumpkin spice sugar cookies in lovely packages to deliver a magical treat to friends and neighbors. Their taste buds will thank you! Happy baking!
