Mediterranean-inspired Quinoa Salad with Roasted Chickpeas


Looking for a fresh and flavorful dish that’s both healthy and satisfying? Look no further than this Mediterranean-inspired quinoa salad with roasted chickpeas. Packed with protein, fiber, and a medley of vibrant flavors, this salad is perfect for a light lunch or a colorful side dish at dinner. Let’s dive into the recipe and discover how to create this delicious and nutritious dish.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup quinoa
  • 1 can chickpeas, drained and rinsed
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1 cucumber, diced
  • 1/2 red onion, finely chopped
  • 1/4 cup Kalamata olives, sliced
  • 1/4 cup fresh parsley, chopped
  • 1/4 cup fresh mint, chopped
  • 1/3 cup feta cheese, crumbled
  • 1/4 cup extra virgin olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons red wine vinegar
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

Step 1: Prepare the Quinoa

Start by rinsing the quinoa under cold water to remove any bitterness. In a saucepan, combine the quinoa with 2 cups of water and a pinch of salt. Bring to a boil, then reduce the heat to low, cover, and simmer for 15-20 minutes, or until the quinoa is tender and the water is absorbed. Once cooked, fluff the quinoa with a fork and let it cool to room temperature.

Step 2: Roast the Chickpeas

Preheat the oven to 400°F (200°C). Pat the chickpeas dry with a paper towel and spread them out on a baking sheet. Drizzle with olive oil, sprinkle with salt, pepper, and a pinch of oregano, then toss to coat. Roast in the oven for 20-25 minutes, or until the chickpeas are golden and crispy. Remove from the oven and let them cool.

Step 3: Assemble the Salad

In a large bowl, combine the cooked quinoa, roasted chickpeas, cherry tomatoes, cucumber, red onion, Kalamata olives, parsley, and mint. Toss gently to mix all the ingredients.

Step 4: Make the Dressing

In a small bowl, whisk together the extra virgin olive oil, red wine vinegar, and dried oregano. Season with salt and pepper to taste.

Step 5: Final Touches

Pour the dressing over the salad and toss to coat everything evenly. Sprinkle the crumbled feta cheese on top and give it a final gentle toss.

Step 6: Serve and Enjoy!

Once everything is combined, your Mediterranean-inspired quinoa salad with roasted chickpeas is ready to be served. This salad can be enjoyed immediately or chilled in the refrigerator for a couple of hours to allow the flavors to meld together.

Conclusion

This Mediterranean-inspired quinoa salad with roasted chickpeas is a celebration of fresh, wholesome ingredients and bold flavors. Whether you’re a fan of Mediterranean cuisine or simply looking to add more nutrient-packed meals to your repertoire, this salad is sure to impress. With its protein-rich quinoa, crispy roasted chickpeas, and a colorful array of vegetables, it’s a feast for both the eyes and the taste buds. So, gather your ingredients and let’s embark on a culinary journey to the sunny shores of the Mediterranean with this delightful salad. Enjoy!
