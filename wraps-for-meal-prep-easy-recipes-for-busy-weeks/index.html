Wraps for Meal Prep: Easy Recipes for Busy Weeks


In today’s fast-paced world, finding time to prepare healthy meals can be a challenge. Meal prepping is a fantastic solution that not only saves time but also ensures you have nutritious options ready to go. One of the most versatile and convenient meal prep options is wraps. They are easy to make, customizable, and perfect for on-the-go lunches or dinners. In this blog post, we’ll explore a variety of delicious wrap recipes that are perfect for meal prep, ensuring you stay nourished throughout your busy week.

Why Choose Wraps for Meal Prep?

Wraps are an excellent choice for meal prep for several reasons:

  1. Versatility: You can fill wraps with a variety of ingredients, from proteins and vegetables to spreads and sauces, making them suitable for any dietary preference.
  2. Portability: Wraps are easy to transport, making them ideal for lunches at work, picnics, or road trips.
  3. Quick to Prepare: Most wraps can be assembled in under 15 minutes, allowing you to whip up a week’s worth of meals in no time.
  4. Storage Friendly: Wraps can be stored in the refrigerator for several days without losing their flavor or texture, making them perfect for meal prep.

Essential Ingredients for Wraps

Before diving into the recipes, let’s discuss some essential ingredients you’ll need for your wraps:

  • Wraps/Tortillas: Choose from whole wheat, spinach, or gluten-free options based on your preference.
  • Proteins: Grilled chicken, turkey, tofu, beans, or hummus are great choices.
  • Vegetables: Fresh veggies like lettuce, spinach, bell peppers, cucumbers, and carrots add crunch and nutrition.
  • Cheese: Feta, cheddar, or mozzarella can enhance the flavor of your wraps.
  • Spreads and Sauces: Consider using hummus, tzatziki, mustard, or a vinaigrette to add moisture and flavor.

Easy Wrap Recipes for Meal Prep

1. Grilled Chicken Caesar Wrap

Ingredients:

  • 2 whole wheat tortillas
  • 1 cup grilled chicken, sliced
  • 1 cup romaine lettuce, chopped
  • 1/4 cup Caesar dressing
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, combine the grilled chicken, romaine lettuce, Caesar dressing, and Parmesan cheese. Mix well.
  2. Lay the tortillas flat and divide the chicken mixture evenly between them.
  3. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  4. Roll the tortillas tightly, tucking in the ends as you go.
  5. Wrap in foil or parchment paper and store in the refrigerator for up to 4 days.

2. Mediterranean Veggie Wrap

Ingredients:

  • 2 spinach tortillas
  • 1 cup hummus
  • 1/2 cup cucumber, sliced
  • 1/2 cup bell pepper, sliced
  • 1/4 cup red onion, thinly sliced
  • 1/4 cup feta cheese, crumbled
  • 1/4 cup olives, sliced

Instructions:

  1. Spread a generous layer of hummus on each tortilla.
  2. Layer the cucumber, bell pepper, red onion, feta cheese, and olives on top of the hummus.
  3. Roll the tortillas tightly, ensuring all ingredients are enclosed.
  4. Wrap in plastic wrap or foil and refrigerate for up to 5 days.

3. Turkey and Avocado Wrap

Ingredients:

  • 2 large flour tortillas
  • 1 cup sliced turkey breast
  • 1 avocado, sliced
  • 1 cup mixed greens
  • 1/4 cup ranch dressing
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. On each tortilla, layer the turkey slices, avocado, and mixed greens.
  2. Drizzle ranch dressing over the top and season with salt and pepper.
  3. Roll the tortillas tightly, tucking in the sides as you go.
  4. Store in an airtight container in the refrigerator for up to 4 days.

4. Spicy Black Bean and Quinoa Wrap

Ingredients:

  • 2 whole wheat tortillas
  • 1 cup cooked quinoa
  • 1 cup black beans, rinsed and drained
  • 1/2 cup corn, canned or frozen
  • 1/2 cup salsa
  • 1/4 cup cilantro, chopped
  • 1 avocado, diced

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, combine the cooked quinoa, black beans, corn, salsa, and cilantro.
  2. Lay the tortillas flat and divide the quinoa mixture evenly between them.
  3. Top with diced avocado.
  4. Roll the tortillas tightly and wrap in foil or parchment paper.
  5. Store in the refrigerator for up to 5 days.
