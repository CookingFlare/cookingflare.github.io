Creamy Mexican Shrimp Enchiladas: A Seafood Sensation


If you’re a seafood lover and a fan of Mexican cuisine, then you’re in for a treat with this recipe for Creamy Mexican Shrimp Enchiladas. Packed with bold flavors and a creamy twist, these enchiladas are a seafood sensation that will leave your taste buds craving for more.

Ingredients

For the enchiladas:

  • 1 pound of shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon chili powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon smoked paprika
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 8-10 corn tortillas

For the creamy sauce:

  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • 2 cups chicken or vegetable broth
  • 1 cup sour cream
  • 1 cup shredded Monterey Jack cheese
  • Salt and pepper to taste

For garnish:

  • Chopped fresh cilantro
  • Sliced avocado
  • Lime wedges

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C).

  2. In a large skillet, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the chopped onion and minced garlic, and sauté until fragrant and translucent.

  3. Add the shrimp to the skillet and cook until they turn pink and opaque. This should take about 3-4 minutes. Remove the shrimp from the skillet and set aside.

  4. In the same skillet, add the ground cumin, chili powder, smoked paprika, salt, and pepper. Stir them into the onion and garlic mixture, allowing the spices to bloom for a minute.

  5. While the spices are cooking, prepare the creamy sauce. In a separate saucepan, melt the butter over medium heat. Whisk in the flour until well combined, creating a roux. Slowly pour in the chicken or vegetable broth, whisking continuously to avoid lumps. Bring the mixture to a simmer and cook until it thickens slightly.

  6. Remove the saucepan from the heat and stir in the sour cream and shredded Monterey Jack cheese until the sauce is smooth and creamy. Season with salt and pepper to taste.

  7. Pour half of the creamy sauce into the skillet with the cooked shrimp. Stir to combine, ensuring the shrimp are coated evenly.

  8. Warm the corn tortillas in the oven or on a hot skillet until they are pliable.

  9. Spoon a generous amount of the shrimp and creamy sauce mixture onto each tortilla. Roll them up and place them seam-side down in a baking dish.

  10. Pour the remaining creamy sauce over the enchiladas, spreading it evenly.

  11. Bake the enchiladas in the preheated oven for about 20-25 minutes, or until the sauce is bubbly and the edges of the tortillas are golden brown.

  12. Remove the enchiladas from the oven and let them cool slightly before serving.

  13. Garnish the enchiladas with chopped fresh cilantro, sliced avocado, and a squeeze of lime juice for an extra burst of flavor.

  14. Serve your Creamy Mexican Shrimp Enchiladas hot and enjoy the delicious combination of creamy sauce, succulent shrimp, and vibrant Mexican flavors.

Conclusion

Creamy Mexican Shrimp Enchiladas are a seafood sensation that brings together the best of Mexican cuisine and the richness of creamy sauces. This dish is perfect for a special dinner or when you want to impress your family and friends with a unique twist on traditional enchiladas. With its bold flavors and satisfying combination of ingredients, these enchiladas are sure to become a favorite in your household. Give this recipe a try and indulge in the seafood sensation that is Creamy Mexican Shrimp Enchiladas.
