Sizzling Supper: Skillet BBQ Chicken and Potatoes


Are you looking for a delicious and hassle-free dinner idea that will satisfy the whole family? Look no further than this mouthwatering Skillet BBQ Chicken and Potatoes recipe! This one-pan wonder is perfect for those busy weeknights when you want a hearty meal without spending hours in the kitchen. Let’s dive into the details of this sizzling supper that will have everyone coming back for seconds.

Ingredients

  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 4 medium potatoes, diced
  • 1 cup of your favorite BBQ sauce
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Chopped fresh parsley for garnish

Instructions

  1. Prep the Chicken: Start by seasoning the chicken breasts with garlic powder, paprika, salt, and pepper. This will infuse the meat with flavor and create a tantalizing crust when seared in the skillet.

  2. Sear the Chicken: Heat the olive oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat. Once the oil is shimmering, add the seasoned chicken breasts to the skillet. Sear them for about 6-7 minutes on each side or until they develop a golden-brown crust. This step locks in the juices and ensures a juicy, tender chicken.

  3. Add the Potatoes: After searing the chicken, remove them from the skillet and set them aside. In the same skillet, add the diced potatoes. Let them cook for about 5 minutes, stirring occasionally, until they start to brown and become tender.

  4. Combine and Simmer: Once the potatoes are slightly golden, return the seared chicken to the skillet, arranging it on top of the potatoes. Pour the BBQ sauce over the chicken and potatoes, ensuring they are well-coated. Reduce the heat to medium-low, cover the skillet, and let the flavors meld together for 15-20 minutes. This step allows the chicken to absorb the smoky sweetness of the BBQ sauce while the potatoes soak up all the savory goodness.

  5. Serve and Garnish: Once the chicken is cooked through and the potatoes are fork-tender, it’s time to plate up this delightful dish. Sprinkle some freshly chopped parsley over the skillet for a pop of color and freshness.

Tips and Variations

  • Spice it Up: If you like a bit of heat, consider adding a pinch of cayenne pepper or a splash of hot sauce to the BBQ sauce for an extra kick.
  • Cheesy Twist: For a comforting twist, sprinkle some shredded cheddar or Monterey Jack cheese over the skillet during the last few minutes of cooking. The cheese will melt and create a gooey, irresistible topping.
  • Veggie Boost: Feel free to toss in some bell peppers, onions, or any other veggies of your choice to amp up the nutritional value and add more depth to the flavors.

Final Thoughts

With minimal prep and cleanup, this Skillet BBQ Chicken and Potatoes recipe is a game-changer for busy cooks who crave a satisfying and flavorful meal. The combination of tender chicken, smoky BBQ sauce, and hearty potatoes creates a symphony of flavors that will have everyone reaching for seconds. Plus, the convenience of cooking everything in one skillet means fewer dishes to wash – a win-win situation! Whether you’re hosting a casual dinner or simply want to treat yourself to a comforting meal, this recipe is sure to become a household favorite. So, roll up your sleeves, fire up the skillet, and get ready to savor every sizzling bite!
