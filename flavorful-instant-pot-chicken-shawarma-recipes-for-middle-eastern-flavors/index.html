Flavorful Instant Pot Chicken Shawarma Recipes for Middle Eastern Flavors


If you’re a fan of Middle Eastern cuisine, you’ll love the rich and aromatic flavors of Chicken Shawarma. Traditionally cooked on a vertical spit and shaved off for serving, Chicken Shawarma is a dish that’s deeply rooted in the culinary traditions of the Middle East. In this blog post, we’ll explore how to create a delicious and flavorful Instant Pot Chicken Shawarma that captures the essence of Middle Eastern cooking while making the process convenient and time-saving.

Ingredients

For the Chicken Marinade:

  • 2 lbs of boneless, skinless chicken thighs
  • 1/4 cup of plain yogurt
  • 3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 2 teaspoons of ground cumin
  • 2 teaspoons of paprika
  • 1 teaspoon of ground turmeric
  • 1 teaspoon of ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 teaspoon of cayenne pepper
  • Salt and black pepper to taste

For Cooking:

  • 1 tablespoon of olive oil
  • 1 cup of chicken broth

For Serving:

  • Pita bread or flatbread
  • Sliced tomatoes
  • Sliced cucumbers
  • Chopped lettuce
  • Tahini sauce or garlic sauce
  • Pickles

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Chicken: In a bowl, mix the yogurt, minced garlic, cumin, paprika, turmeric, cinnamon, cayenne pepper, salt, and black pepper. Add the chicken thighs to the marinade, ensuring they are well coated. Cover the bowl and refrigerate for at least 1 hour, allowing the flavors to infuse.

  2. Sear the Chicken: Set the Instant Pot to sauté mode and add the olive oil. Once hot, sear the marinated chicken thighs for 2-3 minutes on each side until golden brown. This will help lock in the flavors.

  3. Pressure Cook: Pour the chicken broth into the Instant Pot, then lock the lid in place. Set the Instant Pot to high pressure and cook for 10 minutes. Once done, allow for a natural pressure release for 5 minutes before performing a quick pressure release.

  4. Shred and Serve: Using two forks, shred the cooked chicken directly in the Instant Pot. This will allow the chicken to soak up any remaining juices and flavors.

  5. Assemble the Shawarma: Warm the pita bread or flatbread. Place a generous portion of the shredded chicken on the bread, then add the sliced tomatoes, cucumbers, lettuce, and any desired sauce. Roll the bread into a wrap or sandwich and serve with pickles on the side.

Tips for Extra Flavor

  • For an extra depth of flavor, consider adding a pinch of ground cloves or allspice to the marinade.
  • If you prefer a smokier taste, you can add a dash of liquid smoke to the marinade.
  • Experiment with different toppings and sauces to find your perfect combination of textures and flavors.

Conclusion

With the help of an Instant Pot, you can enjoy the tantalizing flavors of Chicken Shawarma with minimal effort and time. The combination of aromatic spices, tender chicken, and vibrant toppings makes this dish a true celebration of Middle Eastern cuisine. Whether you’re hosting a gathering or simply craving a taste of the exotic, this Instant Pot Chicken Shawarma recipe is sure to satisfy your culinary desires. So, gather your ingredients, fire up your Instant Pot, and embark on a flavorful journey to the heart of the Middle East right in your own kitchen.
