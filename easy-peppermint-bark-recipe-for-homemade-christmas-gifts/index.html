Easy Peppermint Bark Recipe for Homemade Christmas Gifts


Are you looking for a simple yet delicious treat to gift your loved ones this Christmas? Look no further than this easy peppermint bark recipe! With just a few ingredients and minimal effort, you can create a delightful homemade gift that is sure to impress. The combination of rich dark chocolate and refreshing peppermint is a classic holiday flavor that everyone will love.

Ingredients

  • 12 ounces of high-quality dark chocolate, chopped
  • 12 ounces of white chocolate, chopped
  • 1 teaspoon of peppermint extract
  • 1/2 cup of crushed candy canes

Instructions

  1. Line a baking sheet with parchment paper and set aside.

  2. In a microwave-safe bowl, melt the dark chocolate in 30-second intervals, stirring in between, until smooth and fully melted.

  3. Once the dark chocolate is melted, stir in half of the peppermint extract until well combined.

  4. Pour the dark chocolate onto the prepared baking sheet and spread it evenly with a spatula.

  5. Place the baking sheet in the refrigerator for about 15 minutes to allow the dark chocolate to set.

  6. While the dark chocolate is setting, melt the white chocolate in a separate microwave-safe bowl using the same method as before.

  7. Once the white chocolate is melted, stir in the remaining peppermint extract.

  8. Take the baking sheet out of the refrigerator and pour the white chocolate over the dark chocolate layer, spreading it evenly with a spatula.

  9. Sprinkle the crushed candy canes on top of the white chocolate layer, gently pressing them into the chocolate.

  10. Return the baking sheet to the refrigerator and let it set for at least 1 hour, or until completely firm.

  11. Once the peppermint bark is firm, remove it from the refrigerator and break it into small, irregular pieces.

  12. Package the peppermint bark in festive containers or gift bags, and share the joy of homemade Christmas treats with your friends and family!

Tips and Variations

  • Use high-quality chocolate for the best flavor and texture.
  • Feel free to experiment with different types of chocolate, such as milk or semi-sweet, to suit your taste.
  • Add a pinch of salt to the crushed candy canes for a contrasting flavor.
  • Sprinkle additional toppings, such as chopped nuts or festive sprinkles, on top of the white chocolate layer for added visual appeal.
  • Customize the packaging by adding ribbons, tags, or personalized labels to make your homemade gift extra special.

Peppermint bark is not only delicious but also versatile. It can be enjoyed on its own, added to hot chocolate for a minty twist, or used as a topping for ice cream or holiday desserts. Whether you’re looking for a quick and easy homemade gift or a delightful treat to indulge in during the holiday season, this easy peppermint bark recipe is a perfect choice.

So, gather your ingredients, put on your favorite Christmas music, and get ready to spread some holiday cheer with this delightful peppermint bark recipe. Your loved ones will be thrilled to receive such a thoughtful and delicious homemade gift. Happy holidays and happy baking!
