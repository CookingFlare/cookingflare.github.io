Cauldron Cupcakes: Bake Up Your Broomstick Magic for Halloween


Are you ready to unleash the powerful force of pumpkin spice, sugar, and whimsy in your kitchen this Halloween? Spellbinding these exceptionally delicious cauldron cupcakes is a guaranteed way to enthrall your guests! These magical treats combine the classic tastes of fall with an enchanting touch straight from your favorite witch’s book of spells.

Warm up your oven and prepare your mixing bowls, because it’s time to take a crash potion making chemistry course! These captivating cauldron cupcakes will bewitch any festive occasion with their delightful simplicity.

The Spell Ingredients:

  • 1 and 3/4 cups (210g) all-purpose flour
  • 1 and 1/2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1 and 1/2 teaspoons baking soda
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup (225g) unsalted butter, room temperature
  • 1 and 1/2 cups (300g) granulated sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1/2 cup (120ml) whole milk
  • 1/2 cup (120ml) vegetable oil
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 1/2 cup (120ml) unsweetened pumpkin puree
  • 1/2 cup (120ml) green food coloring (for a vibrant witchy color)
  • 4 cups powdered sugar
  • 6 tablespoons milk
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/8 teaspoon black gel food coloring

The Alchemy Steps

For the Cauldron Cupcakes:

  1. In a small bowl, whisk together the flour, baking powder, baking soda, and salt. Set aside.
  2. Using a stand or hand mixer, cream together the butter and sugar until light and fluffy.
  3. Beat in the eggs one at a time, followed by the milk, oil, pumpkin puree, and vanilla extract.
  4. Gradually mix in the dry ingredients on low speed until well combined.
  5. Batter can be additionally colored using green food coloring if desired. Multiply your magical powers by scooping about 2 tablespoons of batter into each cavity of a silicone cauldron or aluminum cupcake molds (greased if metal).
  6. Bake in a preheated oven at 350°F (180°C) for 15-20 minutes or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean. Cool for 5 minutes in the cups before transferring to wire racks to cool completely.

For the Potion Glaze:

  1. Sift the powdered sugar into a medium-sized bowl.
  2. In a separate bowl, whisk the milk and vanilla extract together.
  3. Gradually pour the liquid ingredients into the powdered sugar, stirring with a spoon or whisk until smooth. For an added cauldron touch, add a few drops of black gel food coloring until you reach the dark potion shade you desire.
  4. Let the glaze thicken to the right consistency. It should be pourable but thick enough to coat the cupcakes.

Assembly Time:

  1. Place your cooled cauldron cupcakes on a wire rack over a baking sheet or large piece of parchment paper to protect your work surface from glaze drips.
  2. Spoon the potion glaze over each cupcake, working slowly and pour as you would a magical brew. You may use a spoon or a piping bag to create different patterns and add intrigue.
  3. Allow the glaze to harden a bit, and just like that, your cauldron cupcakes are ready for the great Halloween feast!

You’ve brewed up a delightful potion with these enchanting cauldron cupcakes. These spooktacular treats are guaranteed to put a spell on anyone at your Halloween gathering. Save the recipe and double your magical talents by tantalizing your senses with a second batch. Happy brewing!
