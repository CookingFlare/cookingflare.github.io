Low-Carb Instant Pot Recipes: Satisfying Meals for Keto Dieters


When you’re following a low-carb or ketogenic lifestyle, finding quick, satisfying, and delicious meals can sometimes feel like a challenge. Between meal prepping, counting carbs, and ensuring you’re hitting your macros, it’s easy to get overwhelmed. This is where the Instant Pot comes in—a versatile, time-saving kitchen tool that makes creating keto-friendly dishes a breeze. Whether you’re a beginner or an experienced keto enthusiast, the Instant Pot can be a game changer when it comes to whipping up nutrient-dense meals without spending hours in the kitchen.

Below, we’ve put together a collection of mouthwatering, low-carb Instant Pot recipes that are guaranteed to keep you on track with your goals while catering to your taste buds. From hearty soups to tender meats and even decadent side dishes, there’s something here for everyone.

Garlic Butter Chicken Thighs

Juicy, flavorful chicken thighs are perfect for anyone following a low-carb lifestyle. This recipe pairs tender chicken with a rich garlic butter sauce, all made in your Instant Pot in under 30 minutes.

Ingredients:

  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 6 bone-in, skin-on chicken thighs
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons unsalted butter
  • 1 cup chicken broth
  • 1 teaspoon fresh thyme leaves (or ½ teaspoon dried thyme)
  • ½ teaspoon paprika
  • ¼ teaspoon cayenne pepper (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Set your Instant Pot to sauté mode. Add the olive oil and let it heat up.
  2. Season the chicken thighs generously with salt, pepper, paprika, and cayenne (if using). Sear the thighs, skin side down, until golden brown—about 3-4 minutes per side. Remove and set aside.
  3. Add minced garlic to the pot and sauté until fragrant, about 30 seconds.
  4. Deglaze the pot with chicken broth, making sure to scrape up any browned bits from the bottom.
  5. Add butter and thyme to the broth, stirring to combine, then place the chicken thighs back into the pot.
  6. Cover and set the Instant Pot to high pressure for 12 minutes. Once finished, do a quick release.
  7. Serve the chicken thighs with the garlic butter sauce spooned over the top. This pairs perfectly with steamed broccoli or mashed cauliflower for a complete low-carb meal.

Creamy Chicken Alfredo Zoodles

No noodles? No problem! Zucchini noodles (or “zoodles”) are the star of this creamy, keto-friendly Alfredo dish. With the Instant Pot, you can easily create a rich and flavorful sauce to elevate this meal.

Ingredients:

  • 2 large zucchinis, spiralized into noodles
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 pound boneless, skinless chicken breast, cut into strips
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • ½ cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • ½ teaspoon onion powder
  • ½ teaspoon Italian seasoning
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Chopped parsley for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Set your Instant Pot to sauté mode and add the olive oil. Cook chicken strips seasoned with garlic powder, onion powder, Italian seasoning, salt, and pepper until lightly browned. Remove the chicken and set aside.
  2. Add minced garlic to the pot and sauté until aromatic.
  3. Stir in heavy cream and Parmesan cheese, whisking constantly to dissolve. Let it simmer for a minute until the sauce starts to thicken.
  4. Return the cooked chicken to the pot and stir to coat it in the creamy Alfredo sauce. Turn off the sauté function.
  5. Place the prepared zucchini noodles in a large bowl and pour the chicken Alfredo mixture on top. Toss gently to combine.
  6. Serve warm, garnished with fresh parsley. For a little extra crunch, sprinkle some toasted almond slivers on top.

Low-Carb Beef Chili

Chili might traditionally be a carb-heavy dish, but this Instant Pot recipe skips the beans while keeping all the bold flavors intact. It’s hearty, satisfying, and perfect for meal prep.

Ingredients:

  • 2 tablespoons avocado oil
  • 1 pound ground beef (80/20 preferred)
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 small green bell pepper, diced
  • 1 can (14 ounces) diced tomatoes
  • 1 tablespoon tomato paste
  • 1 cup beef broth
  • 1 tablespoon chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon smoked paprika
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • 1 teaspoon oregano
  • ½ teaspoon cayenne pepper (optional)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Chopped cilantro, sour cream, and shredded cheese for toppings

Instructions:

  1. With your Instant Pot on sauté mode, heat the avocado oil and add ground beef. Cook until browned, breaking it up with a wooden spoon. Remove the meat and set aside.
  2. Add the chopped onion, bell pepper, and garlic to the pot. Sauté until softened.
  3. Stir in the tomatoes, tomato paste, and beef broth. Add the chili powder, smoked paprika, cumin, oregano, cayenne, salt, and pepper. Mix well.
  4. Return the browned ground beef to the pot and stir to combine.
  5. Cover and cook on high pressure for 15 minutes. Perform a quick release when finished.
  6. Serve the chili hot, topped with sour cream, shredded cheese, and a sprinkle of cilantro. This dish is great on its own or served with low-carb tortilla chips.

Egg Bite Muffins

Finding breakfast options on a low-carb diet can be tricky, but these fluffy egg bites are a lifesaver! Made right in your Instant Pot, they’re portable, customizable, and keto-friendly.

Ingredients:

  • 6 large eggs
  • ½ cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • ¼ cup heavy cream
  • ⅓ cup cooked bacon bits
  • 2 tablespoons chopped green onions
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Cooking spray

Instructions:

  1. In a mixing bowl, whisk together the eggs, heavy cream, cheese, salt, and pepper until well combined.
  2. Lightly grease a silicone egg-bite mold with cooking spray. Divide the bacon bits and chopped green onions among the compartments.
  3. Pour the egg mixture evenly into the molds.
  4. Add 1 cup of water to the bottom of your Instant Pot and place the steamer rack inside. Position the egg-bite mold on the rack.
  5. Seal the lid and set the Instant Pot to high pressure for 8 minutes. Allow for a natural release.
  6. Remove the silicone mold carefully and let the egg bites cool before removing. Store extras in the fridge for a quick grab-and-go breakfast throughout the week.

Cauliflower Mash with Garlic and Chives

This creamy, buttery cauliflower mash is a fantastic low-carb replacement for traditional mashed potatoes. The Instant Pot simplifies the process for a velvety result every time.

Ingredients:

  • 1 medium head of cauliflower, chopped into florets
  • ⅓ cup chicken broth
  • 2 tablespoons unsalted butter
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • ¼ cup heavy cream
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 tablespoons chopped chives (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Add the cauliflower florets and chicken broth to your Instant Pot.
  2. Seal the lid and cook on high pressure for 5 minutes. Perform a quick release when done.
  3. Drain any excess liquid and transfer the cauliflower to a food processor or blender.
  4. Add butter, garlic, heavy cream, salt, and pepper. Blend until smooth and creamy.
  5. Stir in fresh chives, if desired, and serve hot as a comforting side dish.

The Instant Pot proves that eating low-carb doesn’t have to mean sacrificing flavor or convenience. These recipes are not only delicious but also packed with the nutrients and healthy fats that support a keto-friendly lifestyle. Whether you’re cooking for the week ahead or preparing a hearty dinner for your family, these satisfying dishes will keep you motivated on your low-carb journey. Happy cooking!
