Creamy Garlic Parmesan Brussels Sprouts: A Quick and Delicious Side


Creamy Garlic Parmesan Brussels Sprouts are a delightful side dish that will elevate any meal. Bursting with flavor and packed with nutrients, these Brussels sprouts are the perfect addition to your dinner table. Whether you’re a Brussels sprouts aficionado or a skeptic, this recipe will surely win you over.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound Brussels sprouts, trimmed and halved
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. In a large skillet, melt the butter over medium heat. Add the minced garlic and sauté until fragrant, about 1 minute.

  2. Add the Brussels sprouts to the skillet and season with salt and pepper. Cook for 5-7 minutes, stirring occasionally, until the sprouts are tender and lightly browned.

  3. Reduce the heat to low and pour in the heavy cream. Stir well to combine and let the cream simmer for 2-3 minutes, allowing it to thicken slightly.

  4. Sprinkle the grated Parmesan cheese over the Brussels sprouts and stir until the cheese has melted and coated the sprouts evenly.

  5. Remove from heat and transfer the creamy garlic Parmesan Brussels sprouts to a serving dish.

  6. Serve immediately and enjoy!

Why Choose Creamy Garlic Parmesan Brussels Sprouts?

  1. Flavorful and Nutritious: Brussels sprouts are known for their unique taste and high nutritional value. They are rich in vitamins C and K, as well as fiber and antioxidants. The creamy garlic Parmesan sauce enhances their natural flavors, making them even more irresistible.

  2. Quick and Easy: This recipe requires minimal preparation and cooking time, making it a perfect choice for busy weeknights. In just under 20 minutes, you’ll have a flavorful and satisfying side dish ready to serve.

  3. Versatile: Creamy Garlic Parmesan Brussels Sprouts can complement a wide range of main dishes. They pair well with roasted chicken, grilled steak, or even vegetarian options like tofu or tempeh. Feel free to experiment and find your favorite combination.

  4. Crowd-Pleaser: Even if you have Brussels sprouts skeptics at the table, this recipe has the power to convert them into fans. The creamy garlic Parmesan sauce adds a luxurious touch, making these sprouts irresistible to even the pickiest eaters.

Tips and Variations

  • To add some crunch to the dish, you can top the creamy garlic Parmesan Brussels sprouts with toasted breadcrumbs or crispy bacon bits.
  • For a healthier alternative, you can substitute the heavy cream with Greek yogurt or coconut milk.
  • If you prefer a stronger garlic flavor, you can increase the number of garlic cloves or add some minced shallots to the skillet.
  • Experiment with different herbs and spices to customize the flavor profile. Fresh thyme, rosemary, or red pepper flakes can all add a unique twist to this classic recipe.

In Conclusion

Creamy Garlic Parmesan Brussels Sprouts are a fantastic addition to any meal. With their creamy texture, bold flavors, and nutritional benefits, they are sure to become a favorite side dish. Whether you’re cooking for your family or hosting a dinner party, this recipe is a guaranteed crowd-pleaser. Give it a try and savor the deliciousness!
