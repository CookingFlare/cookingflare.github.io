Savory Low Carb Bacon and Cheese Stuffed Mushrooms


If you’re looking for a delicious and low-carb appetizer or side dish, these savory bacon and cheese stuffed mushrooms are a must-try. Packed with flavor and easy to make, these stuffed mushrooms are sure to be a hit at your next gathering or family dinner. Plus, they’re keto-friendly, making them a great option for those following a low-carb lifestyle.

Ingredients:

  • 20 large mushrooms, stems removed
  • 6 slices of bacon, diced
  • 1/2 cup finely chopped onion
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 4 ounces cream cheese, softened
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/4 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1/4 teaspoon paprika
  • 1/4 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon onion powder
  • 2 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley
  • Salt to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C) and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.

  2. In a large skillet, cook the diced bacon over medium heat until it becomes crispy. Remove the bacon from the skillet and set it aside, leaving the bacon drippings in the pan.

  3. In the same skillet with the bacon drippings, add the chopped onion and minced garlic. Sauté until the onion becomes translucent and the garlic is fragrant.

  4. In a mixing bowl, combine the softened cream cheese, grated Parmesan cheese, shredded mozzarella cheese, black pepper, paprika, garlic powder, onion powder, and chopped parsley. Mix until well combined.

  5. Add the sautéed onion, garlic, and crispy bacon to the cheese mixture. Stir until all the ingredients are evenly incorporated.

  6. Season the mushroom caps with a sprinkle of salt, then stuff each mushroom generously with the bacon and cheese mixture, mounding it on top.

  7. Place the stuffed mushrooms on the prepared baking sheet and bake in the preheated oven for 20-25 minutes, or until the mushrooms are tender and the filling is golden and bubbly.

  8. Once done, remove the stuffed mushrooms from the oven and allow them to cool for a few minutes before serving.

Serving Suggestion:

These savory low carb bacon and cheese stuffed mushrooms are best served warm, making them a perfect appetizer for any occasion. You can also pair them with a fresh green salad for a light and satisfying meal. The combination of savory bacon, creamy cheese, and earthy mushrooms makes these stuffed mushrooms a crowd-pleaser.

Whether you’re hosting a party or simply looking for a tasty low-carb dish, these bacon and cheese stuffed mushrooms are sure to impress. With their rich flavor and satisfying texture, they are a delightful addition to any menu. Enjoy making and savoring these delectable stuffed mushrooms with family and friends!

This recipe for savory low carb bacon and cheese stuffed mushrooms is a delightful combination of flavors and textures. Whether you’re following a low-carb diet or simply looking for a delicious appetizer, these stuffed mushrooms are a fantastic choice. Enjoy the rich, savory taste of bacon and cheese in every bite!
