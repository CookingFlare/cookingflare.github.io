Slow Cooker Italian Recipes That Transport You to Italy


Italy is known for its rich culinary heritage, and there’s no better way to experience the essence of Italian cuisine than through slow-cooked recipes. The slow cooker, or crockpot, is a versatile kitchen tool that allows you to create delicious, comforting Italian meals with minimal effort. In this post, we’ll explore a variety of slow cooker Italian recipes that will transport your taste buds to the sun-drenched hills of Tuscany and the bustling streets of Rome.

Classic Slow Cooker Bolognese

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 can of crushed tomatoes
  • 1/2 cup beef broth
  • 1/4 cup red wine
  • 2 tablespoons tomato paste
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 pound pasta of your choice, cooked al dente

Instructions:

  1. In a skillet, brown the ground beef with the chopped onion and garlic until the meat is no longer pink. Drain any excess fat.
  2. Transfer the browned beef, onion, and garlic to the slow cooker.
  3. Add the crushed tomatoes, beef broth, red wine, tomato paste, dried oregano, dried basil, salt, and pepper to the slow cooker. Stir to combine.
  4. Cover and cook on low for 6-8 hours or on high for 3-4 hours.
  5. Serve the Bolognese sauce over the cooked pasta and garnish with freshly grated Parmesan cheese.

Tuscan Slow Cooker Chicken

Ingredients:

  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1 can of diced tomatoes
  • 1/2 cup chicken broth
  • 1/4 cup balsamic vinegar
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh basil for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Place the chicken breasts in the slow cooker.
  2. In a bowl, mix together the diced tomatoes, chicken broth, balsamic vinegar, minced garlic, dried basil, dried oregano, salt, and pepper.
  3. Pour the tomato mixture over the chicken in the slow cooker.
  4. Cover and cook on low for 6-7 hours or on high for 3-4 hours.
  5. Garnish with fresh basil before serving.

Slow Cooker Minestrone Soup

Ingredients:

  • 4 cups chicken or vegetable broth
  • 1 can of diced tomatoes
  • 2 carrots, diced
  • 2 stalks of celery, diced
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 zucchini, diced
  • 1 cup small pasta, such as ditalini or elbow macaroni
  • 1 can of kidney beans, drained and rinsed
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Freshly grated Parmesan cheese for serving

Instructions:

  1. In the slow cooker, combine the broth, diced tomatoes, carrots, celery, onion, garlic, zucchini, pasta, kidney beans, dried oregano, dried basil, salt, and pepper.
  2. Cover and cook on low for 6-8 hours or on high for 3-4 hours.
  3. Serve the minestrone soup hot, garnished with freshly grated Parmesan cheese.

Conclusion

With these slow cooker Italian recipes, you can bring the flavors of Italy into your home with ease. Whether it’s a rich and hearty Bolognese sauce, tender Tuscan chicken, or a comforting minestrone soup, the slow cooker allows you to savor the essence of Italian cuisine with minimal preparation. Embrace the simplicity and depth of flavor that these recipes offer, and let your taste buds embark on a journey to Italy with every delicious bite. Buon appetito!
