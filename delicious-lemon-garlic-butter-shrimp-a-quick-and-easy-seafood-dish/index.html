Delicious Lemon Garlic Butter Shrimp: A Quick and Easy Seafood Dish


If you’re a fan of seafood, then you’re in for a treat with this delicious Lemon Garlic Butter Shrimp recipe. Not only is it quick and easy to make, but it’s also bursting with flavor, making it the perfect dish for any occasion. Whether you’re cooking for a family dinner or hosting a dinner party, this recipe is sure to impress. So, let’s dive right in and discover how to create this mouthwatering dish.

Ingredients

First, let’s gather all the ingredients we need to make this delectable Lemon Garlic Butter Shrimp.

  • 1 pound of large shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 4 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1/4 cup of fresh lemon juice
  • 1/4 cup of chicken or vegetable broth
  • 4 tablespoons of unsalted butter
  • 2 tablespoons of olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons of freshly chopped parsley
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

Now that we have all the ingredients assembled, it’s time to get cooking!

  1. Prepare the Shrimp: Start by patting the shrimp dry with paper towels and then seasoning them with salt and pepper.

  2. Sauté the Shrimp: In a large skillet, heat the olive oil over medium-high heat. Add the shrimp to the skillet and cook for about 2 minutes on each side until they turn pink and opaque. Once cooked, transfer the shrimp to a plate and set them aside.

  3. Make the Garlic Butter Sauce: In the same skillet, melt the butter over medium heat. Add the minced garlic and sauté for about 1 minute until fragrant. Be careful not to let the garlic brown.

  4. Add the Lemon Juice and Broth: Pour in the fresh lemon juice and chicken or vegetable broth, stirring to combine. Let the mixture simmer for a couple of minutes until it slightly reduces.

  5. Combine the Shrimp and Sauce: Return the cooked shrimp to the skillet, tossing them in the garlic butter sauce until they are well coated.

  6. Garnish and Serve: Sprinkle the freshly chopped parsley over the shrimp, giving it a vibrant pop of color and flavor. Then, it’s ready to be served!

Serving Suggestions

This Lemon Garlic Butter Shrimp is incredibly versatile and can be served in various ways. You can enjoy it over a bed of rice, pasta, or quinoa for a satisfying and complete meal. For a lighter option, pair it with a crisp garden salad or steamed vegetables. The bright and zesty flavors of the dish also make it a perfect appetizer for any gathering.

Final Thoughts

In just a few simple steps, you’ve created a restaurant-quality seafood dish that is sure to impress your family and friends. The combination of zesty lemon, savory garlic, and rich butter creates a symphony of flavors that will tantalize your taste buds. Plus, the quick and easy preparation makes it an ideal choice for busy weeknights or lazy weekends.

So, the next time you’re craving a special seafood treat, remember this delightful Lemon Garlic Butter Shrimp recipe. It’s a true crowd-pleaser that will elevate any mealtime into a memorable dining experience. Enjoy!
