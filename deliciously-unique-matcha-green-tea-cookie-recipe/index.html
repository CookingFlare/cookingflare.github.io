Deliciously Unique Matcha Green Tea Cookie Recipe


Are you a fan of matcha green tea? Do you enjoy experimenting with new and unique cookie recipes? If so, you’re in for a treat! In this blog post, we’re going to explore a deliciously unique matcha green tea cookie recipe that will tantalize your taste buds and impress your friends and family. These cookies are not only visually stunning with their vibrant green color, but they also have a delightful earthy flavor that sets them apart from traditional cookies. So, let’s dive into the recipe and discover how to create these delectable treats!

Ingredients:

  • 1 ½ cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 tbsp matcha green tea powder
  • ½ tsp baking powder
  • ½ cup unsalted butter, softened
  • ¾ cup granulated sugar
  • 1 egg
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • Pinch of salt
  • Optional: white chocolate chips, for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the Oven: Start by preheating your oven to 350°F (175°C) and lining a baking sheet with parchment paper.

  2. Combine Dry Ingredients: In a medium-sized bowl, sift together the all-purpose flour, matcha green tea powder, and baking powder. Set aside.

  3. Cream the Butter and Sugar: In a large mixing bowl, cream together the softened unsalted butter and granulated sugar until light and fluffy.

  4. Add Wet Ingredients: Beat in the egg, vanilla extract, and a pinch of salt until well combined.

  5. Incorporate Dry Ingredients: Gradually add the dry ingredient mixture to the wet ingredients, mixing until a smooth cookie dough forms.

  6. Form Cookie Dough Balls: Using a spoon or cookie scoop, portion out the cookie dough and roll it into small balls. Place the dough balls onto the prepared baking sheet, leaving some space between each one.

  7. Optional: If desired, gently press a few white chocolate chips onto the tops of the cookie dough balls for an extra touch of sweetness.

  8. Bake the Cookies: Place the baking sheet in the preheated oven and bake the cookies for 10-12 minutes, or until the edges are slightly golden.

  9. Cool and Enjoy: Once baked, remove the cookies from the oven and allow them to cool on the baking sheet for a few minutes before transferring them to a wire rack to cool completely.

Serving Suggestions:

These delicious matcha green tea cookies are perfect for enjoying with a cup of hot green tea or as a delightful addition to any dessert platter. Their unique flavor and beautiful green color make them a standout treat for any occasion.

Conclusion:

In conclusion, this deliciously unique matcha green tea cookie recipe is a must-try for anyone who appreciates the distinct flavor of matcha green tea. Whether you’re a seasoned baker or just beginning your culinary journey, these cookies are a fun and impressive addition to your repertoire. So, roll up your sleeves, gather your ingredients, and get ready to indulge in the delightful world of matcha green tea cookies. Happy baking!

With their vibrant green color and delightful earthy flavor, these matcha green tea cookies are sure to be a hit at any gathering. Enjoy the process of creating these unique treats, and savor every bite as you share them with your loved ones. Happy baking!
