Instant Pot Beef Stir-Fry: Quick and Flavorful


There’s nothing quite like the aroma of a sizzling stir-fry wafting through the kitchen. The combination of tender beef, crisp vegetables, and a savory sauce is simply irresistible. And with the help of an Instant Pot, you can have this delicious dish on the table in no time. Whether you’re a busy professional, a parent juggling multiple responsibilities, or simply someone who loves a hearty meal without the fuss, this Instant Pot Beef Stir-Fry recipe is sure to become a favorite in your culinary repertoire.

Ingredients

  • 1 ½ pounds flank steak, thinly sliced against the grain
  • 1 tablespoon cornstarch
  • 3 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons oyster sauce
  • 2 tablespoons hoisin sauce
  • 1 tablespoon rice vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon brown sugar
  • 1 teaspoon sesame oil
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon fresh ginger, grated
  • 1 onion, sliced
  • 2 bell peppers, sliced
  • 1 cup snow peas
  • 1 cup beef broth
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 2 green onions, chopped (for garnish)
  • Cooked rice or noodles (for serving)

Instructions

  1. In a bowl, toss the sliced beef with cornstarch until well coated. Set aside.

  2. In another bowl, mix together the soy sauce, oyster sauce, hoisin sauce, rice vinegar, brown sugar, and sesame oil. Set aside.

  3. Preheat the Instant Pot by selecting the “Sauté” function. Once hot, add the vegetable oil, garlic, and ginger. Sauté for 1 minute until fragrant.

  4. Add the beef to the Instant Pot and cook until browned on all sides, about 3-4 minutes.

  5. Stir in the sliced onion, bell peppers, and snow peas. Cook for an additional 2 minutes.

  6. Pour the prepared sauce and beef broth over the beef and vegetables, stirring to combine.

  7. Secure the lid and set the Instant Pot to “Manual” or “Pressure Cook” on high for 10 minutes.

  8. Once the cooking cycle is complete, perform a quick release of the pressure.

  9. Carefully remove the lid and give the stir-fry a good stir. If the sauce needs thickening, select the “Sauté” function and cook for a few more minutes until the desired consistency is reached.

  10. Serve the Instant Pot Beef Stir-Fry over cooked rice or noodles, garnished with chopped green onions.

Tips for Success

  • For an extra kick of heat, add a teaspoon of chili flakes to the sauce.
  • Feel free to customize the vegetables in this stir-fry based on your preferences or what you have on hand.
  • To make this dish gluten-free, use tamari in place of soy sauce and ensure that the oyster and hoisin sauces are also gluten-free.

Final Thoughts

The beauty of this Instant Pot Beef Stir-Fry recipe lies in its simplicity and speed. By harnessing the power of the Instant Pot, you can achieve tender, flavorful beef and perfectly cooked vegetables without the need for multiple pans and a lengthy cooking process. This recipe is a game-changer for those hectic weeknights when you crave a wholesome, homemade meal without the hassle.

So, gather your ingredients, fire up your Instant Pot, and get ready to savor a dish that seamlessly combines speed and flavor. Your taste buds – and your schedule – will thank you for it!
