Simple and Delicious Chicken and Broccoli Casserole Recipe


Looking for a comforting, hearty meal that’s easy to make and loved by the whole family? This simple and delicious chicken and broccoli casserole is the answer. Packed with tender chicken, vibrant broccoli, and a creamy, cheesy sauce, this casserole is sure to become a regular in your dinner rotation. Plus, it’s a great way to sneak in some veggies for the picky eaters in your life. Let’s dive into the recipe and get cooking!

Ingredients:

  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1 head of broccoli, cut into florets
  • 1 cup of chicken broth
  • 1 cup of heavy cream
  • 1 cup of grated cheddar cheese
  • 1/2 cup of grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 teaspoon of garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon of onion powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 tablespoons of olive oil
  • 1/2 cup of breadcrumbs

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven: Start by preheating your oven to 375°F (190°C). Grease a 9x13 inch baking dish and set it aside.

  2. Cook the chicken: In a large skillet, heat the olive oil over medium-high heat. Season the chicken breasts with salt, pepper, garlic powder, and onion powder. Cook the chicken for about 6-7 minutes on each side, or until golden brown and cooked through. Once done, transfer the chicken to a cutting board and let it rest for a few minutes. Then, chop the chicken into bite-sized pieces.

  3. Blanch the broccoli: While the chicken is cooking, bring a pot of salted water to a boil. Add the broccoli florets and cook for 2-3 minutes, or until bright green and slightly tender. Drain the broccoli and set it aside.

  4. Make the sauce: In the same skillet used for the chicken, pour in the chicken broth and heavy cream. Bring the mixture to a simmer, then reduce the heat to low. Stir in the cheddar cheese and Parmesan cheese until melted and smooth. Season the sauce with salt and pepper to taste.

  5. Assemble the casserole: Arrange the chopped chicken and blanched broccoli in the greased baking dish. Pour the creamy cheese sauce over the top, ensuring everything is well coated.

  6. Bake and finish: Sprinkle the breadcrumbs evenly over the casserole. Place the dish in the preheated oven and bake for 25-30 minutes, or until the casserole is bubbly and the breadcrumbs are golden brown.

  7. Serve and enjoy: Once the casserole is done, remove it from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes. Serve this delicious chicken and broccoli casserole with a side salad or some crusty bread for a complete and satisfying meal.

Tips and Variations:

  • Add some heat: For a spicy kick, sprinkle some red pepper flakes into the cheese sauce.
  • Swap the protein: Not a fan of chicken? You can easily substitute it with cooked turkey or even ham.
  • Make it gluten-free: Use gluten-free breadcrumbs and double-check that your chicken broth is gluten-free if you need to avoid gluten in your diet.
  • Bulk it up: Add cooked pasta, such as penne or rotini, to the casserole for an even heartier dish.

This chicken and broccoli casserole is a versatile and crowd-pleasing dish that’s perfect for any occasion. Whether you’re looking for a quick weeknight meal or a comforting potluck contribution, this recipe has got you covered. Give it a try and watch it become a staple in your recipe collection!
