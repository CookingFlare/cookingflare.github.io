Grilled Zucchini Rolls: Light and Flavorful Fourth of July Appetizer


The Fourth of July is a time for celebration, and what better way to kick off the festivities than with a light and flavorful appetizer that will leave your guests craving for more? Grilled zucchini rolls are the perfect addition to any Independence Day gathering, offering a fresh and vibrant burst of flavors that will tantalize the taste buds. This simple yet elegant dish not only looks impressive but also packs a punch in the flavor department, making it an ideal choice for a summer soirée.

Ingredients

To create this delectable dish, you will need the following ingredients:

  • 3 large zucchinis, thinly sliced lengthwise
  • 1 cup of ricotta cheese
  • 1/4 cup of grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 tablespoons of fresh basil, finely chopped
  • 1 clove of garlic, minced
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Olive oil for grilling
  • Balsamic glaze for drizzling

Instructions

Step 1: Prepare the Zucchini Slices

Begin by slicing the zucchinis lengthwise into thin strips. A mandoline slicer can be a handy tool for achieving consistent thickness. Once sliced, lightly brush the zucchini strips with olive oil and season with salt and pepper.

Step 2: Grill the Zucchini

Preheat the grill to medium-high heat and place the zucchini slices directly on the grates. Grill for 2-3 minutes on each side until they are tender and have prominent grill marks. Remove the zucchini from the grill and set aside to cool.

Step 3: Prepare the Filling

In a mixing bowl, combine the ricotta cheese, Parmesan cheese, chopped basil, minced garlic, and a pinch of salt. Stir the mixture until well combined and smooth.

Step 4: Assemble the Zucchini Rolls

Lay the grilled zucchini slices out flat and spoon a small amount of the ricotta filling onto each slice. Gently roll up each slice to create a neat little zucchini roll.

Step 5: Serve and Enjoy

Place the zucchini rolls on a serving platter, drizzle them with balsamic glaze, and garnish with additional fresh basil if desired. These delightful grilled zucchini rolls are now ready to be savored and shared with your guests.

Tips for Success

  • Use a sharp knife or mandoline slicer to ensure uniform zucchini slices.
  • Don’t overfill the zucchini rolls to prevent the filling from spilling out.
  • If balsamic glaze is not available, a sprinkle of balsamic vinegar can be used as an alternative.

Why Grilled Zucchini Rolls?

Grilled zucchini rolls are a fantastic choice for a Fourth of July appetizer for several reasons. Firstly, they embody the colors of the American flag with their vibrant green zucchini and creamy white filling, making them a visually appealing addition to the table. Secondly, the combination of smoky grilled zucchini and the creamy, herby filling creates a delightful contrast of textures and flavors that will surely impress your guests. Lastly, this appetizer is light and refreshing, making it a perfect start to a summer celebration without leaving your guests feeling overly full before the main course.

In Conclusion

With their simplicity, elegance, and burst of flavors, grilled zucchini rolls are an ideal appetizer to kick off your Fourth of July festivities. Whether you’re hosting a backyard barbecue, a picnic, or a potluck, these delightful little rolls are sure to be a hit. So, fire up the grill, gather your ingredients, and get ready to impress your guests with this light and flavorful appetizer that captures the essence of summer.

So, what are you waiting for? It’s time to add a refreshing twist to your Fourth of July menu with these delightful grilled zucchini rolls. Happy cooking and happy Fourth of July!
