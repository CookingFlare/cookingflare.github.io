Deliciously Soft and Buttery Brioche Bread Recipes


Brioche bread is a French pastry that is known for its rich, buttery flavor and soft, tender texture. It’s a versatile bread that can be enjoyed on its own, used as a base for sandwiches, or even transformed into delectable French toast. In this blog post, we’ll explore three deliciously soft and buttery brioche bread recipes that will take your baking skills to the next level. So, let’s get started!

Classic Brioche Bread

Ingredients:

  • 500g all-purpose flour
  • 10g salt
  • 50g sugar
  • 10g instant yeast
  • 250ml milk, lukewarm
  • 4 large eggs
  • 200g unsalted butter, softened

Instructions:

  1. In a large mixing bowl, combine the flour, salt, sugar, and instant yeast. Mix well.
  2. Add the lukewarm milk and eggs to the dry ingredients. Mix until a sticky dough forms.
  3. Knead the dough on a lightly floured surface for about 10 minutes, until it becomes smooth and elastic.
  4. Gradually add the softened butter to the dough, kneading it in until fully incorporated.
  5. Place the dough in a greased bowl, cover it with a clean kitchen towel, and let it rise in a warm place for about 2 hours, or until doubled in size.
  6. Once the dough has risen, gently punch it down to release any air bubbles. Divide the dough into equal portions and shape them into balls.
  7. Place the dough balls in a greased baking pan, leaving some space between them.
  8. Cover the pan with a kitchen towel and let the dough rise for another hour.
  9. Preheat your oven to 180°C (350°F).
  10. Bake the brioche bread for 25-30 minutes, or until golden brown.
  11. Remove the bread from the oven and let it cool on a wire rack before serving.

Chocolate Chip Brioche Bread

Ingredients:

  • 500g all-purpose flour
  • 10g salt
  • 50g sugar
  • 10g instant yeast
  • 250ml milk, lukewarm
  • 4 large eggs
  • 200g unsalted butter, softened
  • 200g chocolate chips

Instructions:

  1. Follow the instructions for the classic brioche bread recipe until step 4.
  2. Once the softened butter is fully incorporated into the dough, gently fold in the chocolate chips.
  3. Continue with steps 5-11 from the classic brioche bread recipe.

Cinnamon Swirl Brioche Bread

Ingredients:

  • 500g all-purpose flour
  • 10g salt
  • 50g sugar
  • 10g instant yeast
  • 250ml milk, lukewarm
  • 4 large eggs
  • 200g unsalted butter, softened
  • 100g brown sugar
  • 2 tablespoons ground cinnamon

Instructions:

  1. Follow the instructions for the classic brioche bread recipe until step 4.
  2. Once the softened butter is fully incorporated into the dough, combine the brown sugar and ground cinnamon in a small bowl.
  3. Roll out the dough on a lightly floured surface into a rectangular shape.
  4. Sprinkle the cinnamon sugar mixture evenly over the dough.
  5. Starting from one of the longer sides, tightly roll up the dough into a log.
  6. Cut the log in half lengthwise, exposing the layers of cinnamon sugar.
  7. Twist the two halves together, creating a beautiful swirl effect.
  8. Place the twisted dough in a greased baking pan.
  9. Follow steps 8-11 from the classic brioche bread recipe.

Enjoy the heavenly aroma and the melt-in-your-mouth goodness of these deliciously soft and buttery brioche bread recipes. Whether you choose the classic version, the chocolate chip variation, or the cinnamon swirl creation, one thing is for sure – you won’t be able to resist a second slice. So grab your apron, preheat your oven, and let the baking adventure begin! Happy baking!
