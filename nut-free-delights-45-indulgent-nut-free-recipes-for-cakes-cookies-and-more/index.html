Nut-Free Delights: 45 Indulgent Nut-Free Recipes for Cakes, Cookies, and More


Are you tired of missing out on sweet treats because of a nut allergy? Worry no more! We’ve gathered a selection of 45 indulgent nut-free recipes to satisfy your cravings for cakes, cookies, and more. No nut allergies should discourage you from enjoying life’s little pleasures, so let’s dive into the world of nut-free delights!

1. Flourless Chocolate Cake

Ingredients:

  • 8 oz semi-sweet chocolate
  • 3 cups cream cheese, at room temperature
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/2 tsp. vanilla extract
  • 1/4 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1 tbsp. instant coffee powder
  • 1 tsp. baking powder

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven at 350°F (175°C) and grease a 9-inch springform pan.
  2. Melt chocolate in a double boiler, stirring constantly. Set aside.
  3. Whip cream cheese smoothly, then gradually add sugar, mixing until light and fluffy.
  4. Add vanilla extract and mix well.
  5. Alternate blending in melted chocolate and cocoa powder mixture.
  6. Thoroughly combine coffee and baking powder, then gently fold into mixture.
  7. Pour batter into prepared pan and bake for 35-45 minutes, or until it pulls away from the sides.
  8. Cool completely before serving. Enjoy!

2. Peanut Butter Cookie Cups

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup creamy peanut butter
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 large egg
  • 1 tsp. baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp. baking soda
  • 1/4 tsp. salt
  • 1 cup semisweet chocolate chips

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C) and line a muffin tin with paper liners.
  2. Mix peanut butter, sugar, and egg together.
  3. Stir in baking powder, baking soda, and salt.
  4. Drop about 1 tbsp. of dough into each muffin liner, pressing down slightly.
  5. Bake for 10-12 minutes, then remove from oven.
  6. Press chocolate chips into the warm cookies.
  7. Cool completely before enjoying your delicious peanut butter treats!

3. Zucchini Bread Muffins

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups grated zucchini
  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp. baking powder
  • 1 tsp. baking soda
  • 1/2 tsp. cinnamon
  • 1/2 tsp. salt
  • 1/2 cup vegetable oil
  • 1 cup brown sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 tsp. vanilla extract
  • 1 cup raisins (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C) and grease 24 muffin cups.
  2. Squeeze grated zucchini to remove excess moisture.
  3. Mix flour, baking powder, baking soda, cinnamon, and salt.
  4. Beat vegetable oil, brown sugar, eggs, and vanilla extract in a separate bowl.
  5. Combine wet and dry mixtures; fold in zucchini and raisins.
  6. Fill greased muffin cups 2/3 full.
  7. Bake for 25-30 minutes, or until a toothpick comes out clean.
  8. Serve warm, and enjoy these moist and delicious zucchini bread muffins!

4. Carrot Cake Cupcakes

Ingredients:

  • 1 3/4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp. baking powder
  • 1 tsp. baking soda
  • 1/2 tsp. ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 tsp. ground ginger
  • 1/4 tsp. ground cloves
  • 1/2 tsp. salt
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/2 cup vegetable oil
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup grated carrots
  • 1/2 cup raisins

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C) and line a 12-cup muffin tin with liners.
  2. In a medium bowl, combine flour, baking powder, baking soda, cinnamon, ginger, cloves, and salt.
  3. Beat sugar, oil, and eggs in a separate large bowl.
  4. Stir half of the dry mixture into the wet mixture, then fold in carrots and raisins.
  5. Add the remaining dry ingredients and mix until just combined.
  6. Fill each muffin liner 2/3 full and bake for 20 minutes.
  7. Cool cupcakes completely before frosting with your favorite nut-free cream cheese frosting.

5. Cinnamon Sugar Donut Holes

Ingredients:

  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/3 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/2 tsp. baking powder
  • 1/4 tsp. baking soda
  • 1/4 tsp. ground cinnamon
  • 1/2 tsp. salt
  • 6 tbsp. unsalted butter, melted
  • 3/4 cup milk
  • 1 tbsp. granulated sugar
  • 2 tsp. ground cinnamon

Instructions:

  1. In a large bowl, combine flour, sugar, baking powder, baking soda, cinnamon, and salt.
  2. Whisk in melted butter and milk until smooth, no lumps remain.
  3. Heat oil to 375°F (190°C) in a deep pot or frying pan.
  4. Using an ice cream scoop, drop batter into hot oil.
  5. Fry 6-8 donut holes at a time, turning occasionally, until golden brown.
  6. Using a slotted spoon, carefully transfer donut holes to paper towels to drain.
  7. Combine sugars and cinnamon in a shallow dish and roll hot donut holes to coat.
  8. Serve warm and enjoy these deliciously sweet, nut-free treats!
