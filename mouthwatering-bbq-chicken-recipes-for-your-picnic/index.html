Mouthwatering BBQ Chicken Recipes for Your Picnic


Summer is in full swing, and that means it’s the perfect time to fire up the grill and enjoy some delicious BBQ chicken. Whether you’re planning a picnic in the park or a backyard cookout, these mouthwatering BBQ chicken recipes are sure to impress your friends and family. From tangy and spicy to sweet and savory, there’s a recipe here to suit every palate. So grab your apron and get ready to elevate your picnic with these delectable dishes.

1. Honey Mustard BBQ Chicken Skewers

Prep time: 15 minutes
Cook time: 10 minutes
Serves: 4

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound boneless, skinless chicken breasts, cut into cubes
  • ¼ cup Dijon mustard
  • 2 tablespoons honey
  • 2 tablespoons apple cider vinegar
  • 1 teaspoon smoked paprika
  • Salt and pepper, to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a small bowl, whisk together the Dijon mustard, honey, apple cider vinegar, smoked paprika, salt, and pepper.
  2. Thread the chicken cubes onto skewers, then brush the honey mustard mixture onto the chicken.
  3. Preheat the grill to medium-high heat and lightly oil the grates.
  4. Grill the skewers for 4-5 minutes per side, or until the chicken is cooked through and slightly charred.
  5. Serve hot and enjoy!

2. Spicy Chipotle BBQ Chicken Drumsticks

Prep time: 10 minutes
Cook time: 40 minutes
Serves: 6

Ingredients:

  • 6 chicken drumsticks
  • ½ cup BBQ sauce
  • 2 tablespoons chipotle peppers in adobo sauce, minced
  • 2 tablespoons lime juice
  • 1 tablespoon honey
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • Salt and pepper, to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the grill to medium heat.
  2. In a small bowl, mix together the BBQ sauce, chipotle peppers, lime juice, honey, garlic powder, salt, and pepper.
  3. Season the chicken drumsticks with salt and pepper, then brush them generously with the chipotle BBQ sauce mixture.
  4. Place the drumsticks on the grill and cook for 20 minutes, turning occasionally and basting with more sauce.
  5. Continue grilling for another 20 minutes, or until the chicken is cooked through and the skin is crispy.
  6. Remove from the grill and let rest for a few minutes before serving.

3. Pineapple BBQ Chicken Burgers

Prep time: 20 minutes
Cook time: 15 minutes
Serves: 4

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound ground chicken
  • ½ cup BBQ sauce
  • ¼ cup breadcrumbs
  • ¼ cup finely chopped red onion
  • 2 tablespoons chopped fresh cilantro
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • 4 pineapple rings
  • 4 burger buns
  • Lettuce, tomato, and red onion slices, for serving

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the grill to medium-high heat.
  2. In a large bowl, combine the ground chicken, BBQ sauce, breadcrumbs, red onion, cilantro, garlic powder, salt, and pepper. Mix until well combined.
  3. Divide the mixture into 4 equal portions and shape into patties.
  4. Grill the patties for 6-7 minutes per side, or until cooked through.
  5. During the last few minutes of grilling, place the pineapple rings on the grill and cook for 1-2 minutes per side, or until lightly charred.
  6. Toast the burger buns on the grill for a minute or two.
  7. Assemble the burgers by placing a chicken patty on each bun, then top with a pineapple ring, lettuce, tomato, and red onion slices.
  8. Serve immediately and enjoy the tropical flavors!

Conclusion

There you have it, three mouthwatering BBQ chicken recipes that are perfect for your picnic. Whether you prefer the tangy sweetness of honey mustard, the fiery kick of chipotle, or the tropical twist of pineapple, these recipes are sure to please. So fire up the grill, gather your loved ones, and enjoy a delicious outdoor feast. Happy grilling!
