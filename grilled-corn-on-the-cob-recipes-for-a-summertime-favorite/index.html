Grilled Corn on the Cob: Recipes for a Summertime Favorite


Grilled corn on the cob is a classic summertime favorite that brings back memories of backyard barbecues and lazy afternoons by the pool. There’s something about the smoky char and sweet, juicy kernels that make it a must-have dish for any outdoor gathering. In this blog post, we’ll explore three delicious recipes that will take your grilled corn on the cob to the next level. So fire up the grill and get ready to impress your friends and family with these mouthwatering creations!

Recipe 1: Classic Grilled Corn on the Cob

Ingredients:

  • 4 ears of corn
  • Olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Butter (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your grill to medium-high heat.
  2. Peel back the husks of the corn, but leave them attached at the base. Remove the silk strands.
  3. Brush each ear of corn with olive oil and sprinkle with salt and pepper.
  4. Place the corn directly on the grill grates and cook for about 10-12 minutes, turning occasionally, until the kernels are tender and lightly charred.
  5. Remove from the grill and let cool for a few minutes.
  6. Serve as is or slather with butter for an extra indulgent treat.

Recipe 2: Mexican Street Corn (Elote)

Ingredients:

  • 4 ears of corn
  • ¼ cup mayonnaise
  • ¼ cup sour cream
  • ½ cup crumbled cotija cheese
  • 1 teaspoon chili powder
  • 1 tablespoon lime juice
  • Fresh cilantro, chopped (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your grill to medium-high heat.
  2. Peel back the husks of the corn, but leave them attached at the base. Remove the silk strands.
  3. Grill the corn for about 10-12 minutes, turning occasionally, until the kernels are tender and lightly charred.
  4. In a small bowl, mix together the mayonnaise, sour cream, lime juice, and chili powder.
  5. Once the corn is cooked, brush each ear with the mayonnaise mixture.
  6. Sprinkle the crumbled cotija cheese on top of the corn, followed by a sprinkle of chili powder.
  7. Garnish with fresh cilantro, if desired.
  8. Serve immediately and enjoy the irresistible flavors of Mexican street corn.

Recipe 3: Herb and Garlic Grilled Corn on the Cob

Ingredients:

  • 4 ears of corn
  • 4 tablespoons butter, softened
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon fresh parsley, chopped
  • 1 tablespoon fresh thyme, chopped
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your grill to medium-high heat.
  2. Peel back the husks of the corn, but leave them attached at the base. Remove the silk strands.
  3. In a small bowl, combine the softened butter, minced garlic, chopped parsley, and thyme. Mix well.
  4. Spread the herb and garlic butter evenly over each ear of corn.
  5. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  6. Place the corn on the grill and cook for about 10-12 minutes, turning occasionally, until the kernels are tender and lightly charred.
  7. Remove from the grill and let cool for a few minutes.
  8. Serve and savor the aromatic blend of herbs and garlic with every bite.

Grilled corn on the cob is a versatile and crowd-pleasing dish that can be prepared in various ways to suit your taste buds. Whether you prefer the simplicity of classic grilled corn, the bold flavors of Mexican street corn, or the aromatic herb and garlic combination, these recipes will elevate your grilling game and make your summertime gatherings even more memorable. So grab some fresh corn, fire up the grill, and indulge in the deliciousness of grilled corn on the cob. Happy grilling!
