Vegan Chocolate Chip Cookie Dough Bars Recipe for a Treat


If you’re searching for a delectable dessert that combines the delightful taste of cookie dough with a wholesome vegan twist, look no further! These Vegan Chocolate Chip Cookie Dough Bars are not only easy to make, but they’re also a treat that everyone will love, whether they follow a vegan lifestyle or just enjoy a good dessert. Packed with rich flavors and a chewy texture, these bars are perfect for satisfying your sweet tooth while adhering to a plant-based diet.

Ingredients You’ll Need

To whip up a batch of these scrumptious Vegan Chocolate Chip Cookie Dough Bars, you will need the following ingredients:

For the Cookie Dough Layer:

  • 1 cup almond flour
    Almond flour gives the bars a rich, nutty flavor while keeping them gluten-free.

  • 1 cup oat flour
    Oat flour adds a chewy texture and is also naturally gluten-free. You can make your own by blending rolled oats until fine.

  • ½ teaspoon baking soda
    This helps the bars rise just a bit for a fluffy texture.

  • ½ teaspoon salt
    A pinch of salt enhances the flavors of the sweet ingredients.

  • ½ cup coconut oil, melted
    Coconut oil provides healthy fats and a subtle sweetness.

  • ¾ cup maple syrup
    This natural sweetener keeps the bars moist and adds a lovely flavor profile.

  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
    Vanilla extract elevates the cookie dough flavor, making it irresistible.

  • ¾ cup vegan chocolate chips
    Use your favorite brand of dairy-free chocolate chips for a gooey texture.

For the Chocolate Topping:

  • ½ cup dairy-free chocolate chips
    These will be melted to create a luscious topping.

  • 1 tablespoon coconut oil
    Adding coconut oil to the chocolate helps it melt smoothly and creates a glossy finish.

Instructions

Step 1: Prepare Your Baking Dish

Start by lining an 8x8-inch baking dish with parchment paper. This will make it easier to remove the bars later and ensure they don’t stick to the pan.

Step 2: Mix the Dry Ingredients

In a large mixing bowl, combine the almond flour, oat flour, baking soda, and salt. Whisk these dry ingredients together until they are well incorporated. This helps to ensure an even distribution of the leavening agent and salt.

Step 3: Combine the Wet Ingredients

In a separate bowl, combine the melted coconut oil, maple syrup, and vanilla extract. Whisk the ingredients together until they are smooth and fully blended. The warmth of the coconut oil helps the mixture to become creamy.

Step 4: Create the Cookie Dough Mixture

Pour the wet ingredients into the bowl with the dry ingredients. Stir until just combined; be careful not to overmix. Gently fold in the vegan chocolate chips, ensuring they’re evenly distributed throughout the dough.

Step 5: Press the Dough into the Baking Dish

Transfer the cookie dough mixture to your prepared baking dish. Using a spatula or your hands, press the dough down evenly until it fills the bottom of the dish. Make sure it’s compact to ensure it holds together once baked.

Step 6: Bake the Cookie Dough Bars

Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C). Bake the cookie dough layer for about 20-25 minutes, or until the edges are lightly golden and the center is set. Keep an eye on them so that they do not overbake, as you want a soft, chewy texture.

Step 7: Cool Down

Once baked, remove the baking dish from the oven and let it cool in the pan for about 10 minutes. After that, transfer it to a wire rack to cool completely. This step is crucial as it allows the bars to firm up, making them easier to cut.

Step 8: Prepare the Chocolate Topping

While the cookie dough layer is cooling, melt the remaining dairy-free chocolate chips and coconut oil together. You can do this in a microwave-safe bowl in 30-second intervals, stirring in between, until melted and smooth.

Step 9: Drizzle the Chocolate

Once the cookie dough layer has cooled completely, pour the melted chocolate mixture over the top. Spread it evenly with a spatula to cover the entire surface. For a decorative touch, you can sprinkle a few extra chocolate chips on top.

Step 10: Chill the Bars

To set the chocolate topping, place the baking dish in the refrigerator for at least 30 minutes. This will help the chocolate firm up and make the bars easier to slice.

Step 11: Slice and Serve

After chilling, remove the bars from the refrigerator. Lift them out of the baking dish using the parchment paper, and place them on a cutting board. Use a sharp knife to cut the bars into squares or rectangles, depending on your preference.

Storage Tips

Store any leftover Vegan Chocolate Chip Cookie Dough Bars in an airtight container in the refrigerator for up to a week. Alternatively, you can freeze them for longer storage. Just make sure to wrap them tightly in plastic wrap or aluminum foil before placing them in a freezer-safe container.

Why You’ll Love These Bars

These Vegan Chocolate Chip Cookie Dough Bars are the perfect balance of indulgence and healthiness. They are:

  • No-Bake Option: If you prefer not to bake, you can also make a no-bake version by simply refrigerating the cookie dough mixture for a few hours before slicing.

  • Customizable: Feel free to customize the recipe by adding different mix-ins, such as nuts, dried fruits, or even a sprinkle of sea salt on top of the chocolate.

  • Kid-Friendly: These bars are a fantastic treat for kids. They’ll love the sweet, chewy cookie dough flavor, and you’ll love that they’re made with wholesome ingredients.

  • Perfect for Sharing: Whether it’s a potluck, a picnic, or just a gathering with friends, these bars are an excellent dessert to share. They’re easy to transport and always a hit!

Conclusion

The Vegan Chocolate Chip Cookie Dough Bars are not just a treat; they are a delightful way to enjoy a classic dessert while sticking to a vegan diet. With their rich flavors and soft, chewy texture, these bars are sure to become a favorite in your home. The best part? They come together in just a few simple steps, making them perfect for any occasion.

Next time you’re in the mood for something sweet, give this recipe a try. You’ll be amazed at how satisfying and delightful these vegan treats can be! Enjoy them on their own, or pair them with a scoop of vegan ice cream for an extra indulgent experience. Happy baking!
