Vegan Chocolate Cream Puffs Recipe with a Light and Airy Pastry


If you’re looking for a decadent yet vegan dessert that will impress your friends and family, look no further than this Vegan Chocolate Cream Puffs recipe. These delightful treats are filled with a rich and creamy chocolate filling and encased in a light and airy pastry that will leave you craving for more. Whether you’re a seasoned vegan chef or just dipping your toes into the world of plant-based cooking, this recipe is sure to become a staple in your dessert repertoire.

Ingredients

For the Pastry:

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup water
  • 1/2 cup vegan butter
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1 tablespoon sugar

For the Chocolate Filling:

  • 1 can (13.5 oz) full-fat coconut milk
  • 8 oz dairy-free dark chocolate, chopped
  • 1/4 cup maple syrup
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Instructions

Making the Pastry:

  1. In a medium saucepan, combine water, vegan butter, salt, and sugar. Bring to a boil over medium heat.

  2. Once boiling, reduce the heat to low and add the flour all at once. Stir vigorously with a wooden spoon until the mixture forms a ball and pulls away from the sides of the pan.

  3. Remove the pan from the heat and let the dough cool for 5-10 minutes.

  4. Preheat the oven to 400°F (200°C) and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.

  5. Transfer the dough to a piping bag fitted with a large round tip. Pipe the dough onto the prepared baking sheet, about 1 1/2 inches in diameter.

  6. Bake for 15 minutes, then reduce the heat to 350°F (175°C) and bake for an additional 20 minutes, or until the pastries are golden brown and puffed.

  7. Remove from the oven and transfer to a wire rack to cool completely.

Making the Chocolate Filling:

  1. In a small saucepan, heat the coconut milk over medium heat until it just begins to simmer.

  2. Place the chopped chocolate in a heatproof bowl. Pour the hot coconut milk over the chocolate and let it sit for 1-2 minutes to melt the chocolate.

  3. Add the maple syrup and vanilla extract to the chocolate mixture. Stir until smooth and creamy.

  4. Let the chocolate filling cool to room temperature, then transfer it to a piping bag fitted with a small round tip.

Assembling the Cream Puffs:

  1. Once the pastry shells are completely cooled, use a sharp knife to slice off the top third of each shell.

  2. Pipe the chocolate filling into the bottom two-thirds of each shell.

  3. Place the tops back on the filled shells and dust with powdered sugar, if desired.

  4. Serve and enjoy!

Tips and Tricks

  • Be sure to use full-fat coconut milk for the filling to achieve a rich and creamy texture.
  • When piping the pastry dough, leaving some space between each puff to allow for expansion while baking.
  • If you don’t have a piping bag, you can use a zip-top bag with the corner snipped off to pipe the pastry and filling.

Conclusion

These Vegan Chocolate Cream Puffs are a delightful indulgence that will satisfy any sweet tooth. The combination of the light and airy pastry with the rich and decadent chocolate filling is sure to impress even the most discerning dessert connoisseurs. Whether you’re hosting a dinner party or simply craving a sweet treat, this recipe is a must-try for anyone looking to indulge in a guilt-free, plant-based dessert. So, roll up your sleeves, preheat your oven, and get ready to create a dessert that will have everyone coming back for seconds!
