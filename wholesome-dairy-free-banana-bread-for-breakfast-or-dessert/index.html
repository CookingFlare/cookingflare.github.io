Wholesome Dairy-Free Banana Bread for Breakfast or Dessert


Are you looking for a delicious and nutritious treat that can be enjoyed for breakfast or dessert? Look no further than this wholesome dairy-free banana bread recipe. Packed with flavor and goodness, this recipe is not only easy to make but also caters to those with dietary restrictions. Whether you follow a dairy-free lifestyle or simply want to try something different, this banana bread is sure to become a favorite.

Ingredients

To make this wholesome dairy-free banana bread, you will need the following ingredients:

  • 3 ripe bananas
  • 1/2 cup of coconut oil
  • 1/2 cup of maple syrup
  • 2 teaspoons of vanilla extract
  • 1 3/4 cups of whole wheat flour
  • 1 teaspoon of baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon of salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon of cinnamon
  • 1/4 teaspoon of nutmeg
  • Optional: 1/2 cup of chopped walnuts or chocolate chips for added texture and flavor

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C) and grease a loaf pan with coconut oil or line it with parchment paper.

  2. In a large mixing bowl, mash the ripe bananas until smooth. Add the coconut oil, maple syrup, and vanilla extract. Mix well until all the ingredients are combined.

  3. In a separate bowl, whisk together the whole wheat flour, baking soda, salt, cinnamon, and nutmeg. Gradually add the dry ingredients to the wet ingredients, stirring until just combined. Be careful not to overmix, as this can result in a dense bread.

  4. If desired, fold in the chopped walnuts or chocolate chips for an extra burst of flavor and texture.

  5. Pour the batter into the prepared loaf pan, smoothing the top with a spatula.

  6. Bake for approximately 50-60 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean. The baking time may vary, so keep an eye on the bread and adjust accordingly.

  7. Once baked, remove the banana bread from the oven and allow it to cool in the pan for 10 minutes. Then transfer it to a wire rack to cool completely before slicing.

Serving Suggestions

This wholesome dairy-free banana bread is incredibly versatile and can be enjoyed in various ways. Here are a few serving suggestions:

  • Fresh out of the oven: Slice the warm banana bread and enjoy it as is. The soft texture and comforting flavors are perfect for a cozy breakfast or a delightful dessert.

  • Toasted with a spread: Take your banana bread to the next level by toasting slices and spreading them with almond butter, peanut butter, or your favorite dairy-free spread. This adds a deliciously creamy and nutty element to the bread.

  • Topped with fruit: For a refreshing twist, top your banana bread with fresh berries, sliced bananas, or a drizzle of honey. The combination of the sweet bread and vibrant fruits creates a delightful contrast of flavors.

Conclusion

With its wholesome ingredients and dairy-free nature, this banana bread recipe is a true winner. Whether enjoyed as a satisfying breakfast or a sweet treat after a meal, this versatile bread is sure to please. So gather your ingredients, preheat your oven, and get ready to savor the deliciousness of this wholesome dairy-free banana bread.
