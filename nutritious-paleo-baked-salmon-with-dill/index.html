Nutritious Paleo Baked Salmon with Dill


Salmon is not only a delicious and versatile fish, but it is also packed with essential nutrients that promote overall health. In this recipe, we will be combining the flavors of salmon with the fragrant herb, dill, to create a nutritious and mouthwatering dish that adheres to the principles of the Paleo diet. Let’s dive right into the recipe!

Ingredients

  • 1 pound of fresh salmon fillets
  • 2 tablespoons of olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon of lemon juice
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon of fresh dill, chopped
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).

  2. In a small bowl, combine the olive oil, lemon juice, minced garlic, chopped dill, salt, and pepper. Mix well until the ingredients are thoroughly combined.

  3. Place the salmon fillets on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper.

  4. Drizzle the olive oil mixture over the salmon fillets, making sure to coat them evenly on all sides. Use a brush or your hands to spread the mixture evenly.

  5. Place the baking sheet with the salmon in the preheated oven and bake for 12-15 minutes, or until the salmon is cooked through and flakes easily with a fork.

  6. Once cooked, remove the salmon from the oven and allow it to cool for a few minutes before serving.

  7. Serve the salmon hot, garnished with a sprig of fresh dill for an added touch of freshness.

Why Choose Paleo Baked Salmon with Dill?

The Paleo diet focuses on consuming whole, unprocessed foods that our ancestors would have eaten. This means eliminating grains, dairy, and processed sugars from your diet. By choosing this Nutritious Paleo Baked Salmon with Dill recipe, you are not only adhering to the principles of the Paleo diet but also enjoying a dish that is rich in essential nutrients.

Salmon is an excellent source of omega-3 fatty acids, which are known for their heart-healthy benefits. These fatty acids help reduce inflammation, lower blood pressure, and improve overall cardiovascular health. Additionally, salmon is a great source of high-quality protein, making it an ideal choice for those following a Paleo diet.

Dill, the star herb of this recipe, adds a burst of flavor to the salmon. It is not only delicious but also has several health benefits. Dill is known for its antimicrobial properties and its ability to aid digestion. It is also rich in antioxidants, which help protect the body against oxidative stress.

Final Thoughts

This Nutritious Paleo Baked Salmon with Dill recipe is a perfect choice for those looking to incorporate more nutrient-dense foods into their diet. The combination of fresh salmon, fragrant dill, and the simplicity of the Paleo cooking method creates a dish that is both delicious and nourishing. Give it a try and experience the wholesome flavors and health benefits for yourself!

Remember, cooking is a creative process, so feel free to experiment with different herbs and spices to customize this recipe to your liking. Enjoy the journey of discovering new flavors and nourishing your body with every bite. Happy cooking!
