Exotic Edamame and Mango Salad Recipe


Are you tired of the same old boring salads? Looking to add a burst of flavor and excitement to your meal? Look no further! Our Exotic Edamame and Mango Salad recipe is here to tantalize your taste buds and take your salad game to the next level.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup frozen edamame, thawed
  • 1 ripe mango, peeled and diced
  • 1 small red bell pepper, diced
  • 1/2 red onion, thinly sliced
  • 1/4 cup fresh cilantro, chopped
  • 1/4 cup unsalted cashews, roughly chopped
  • 1 tablespoon sesame seeds

Dressing

  • 2 tablespoons lime juice
  • 2 tablespoons rice vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon honey
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 1 teaspoon sesame oil
  • 1/2 teaspoon grated ginger
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. In a large mixing bowl, combine the thawed edamame, diced mango, red bell pepper, red onion, cilantro, cashews, and sesame seeds. Mix well to combine.

  2. In a separate small bowl, whisk together the lime juice, rice vinegar, honey, soy sauce, sesame oil, grated ginger, salt, and pepper. This will be the dressing for your salad.

  3. Pour the dressing over the salad mixture and toss gently until all the ingredients are coated evenly.

  4. Once the salad is well-dressed, cover the bowl with plastic wrap and refrigerate for at least 30 minutes to allow the flavors to meld together.

  5. When you’re ready to serve, give the salad one final toss to ensure everything is well-mixed. You can garnish with additional cilantro and sesame seeds if desired.

  6. Serve the Exotic Edamame and Mango Salad as a refreshing side dish, or add some grilled chicken or shrimp to make it a complete meal.

Why You’ll Love This Salad

This Exotic Edamame and Mango Salad is a perfect blend of sweet and savory flavors. The juicy mango provides a burst of tropical sweetness, while the edamame adds a satisfying crunch. The red bell pepper and red onion add a vibrant color and a touch of spiciness, while the cashews give it a delightful nutty flavor. The dressing, with its tangy lime juice and fragrant sesame oil, ties all the ingredients together beautifully.

Not only is this salad incredibly delicious, but it’s also packed with nutrients. Edamame is a great source of plant-based protein and is rich in fiber, vitamins, and minerals. Mangoes are a good source of vitamin C and antioxidants, while the bell pepper and red onion provide additional vitamins and minerals. The cashews add healthy fats and protein, making this salad a wholesome and satisfying choice.

Conclusion

Say goodbye to boring salads and hello to a burst of exotic flavors with our Exotic Edamame and Mango Salad recipe. The combination of sweet mangoes, crunchy edamame, and zesty dressing will leave your taste buds craving for more. Serve it as a side dish or make it a complete meal by adding some protein. This salad is not only delicious but also packed with nutrients. So, why wait? Gather your ingredients and get ready to embark on a culinary adventure with this exotic salad!
