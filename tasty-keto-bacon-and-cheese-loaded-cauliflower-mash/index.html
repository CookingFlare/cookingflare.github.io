Tasty Keto Bacon and Cheese Loaded Cauliflower Mash


If you’re on a keto diet and missing the comforting flavors of mashed potatoes, then you’re in for a treat! This Tasty Keto Bacon and Cheese Loaded Cauliflower Mash is a delicious and low-carb alternative that will satisfy your cravings without the guilt. Packed with savory bacon, gooey cheese, and creamy cauliflower, this dish is sure to become a favorite in your keto recipe collection.

Ingredients

  • 1 medium head of cauliflower
  • 4 slices of bacon, cooked and crumbled
  • 1 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1/4 cup heavy cream
  • 2 tablespoons unsalted butter
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Chopped chives for garnish (optional)

Instructions

  1. Start by preparing the cauliflower. Remove the leaves and stem, and chop the cauliflower into florets. Rinse them under cold water and pat dry.

  2. In a large pot, bring water to a boil. Add a pinch of salt and the cauliflower florets. Cook for about 10-12 minutes, or until the cauliflower is fork-tender.

  3. While the cauliflower is cooking, heat a skillet over medium heat. Add the butter and minced garlic, and sauté until fragrant, about 1-2 minutes.

  4. Once the cauliflower is cooked, drain well and transfer it to a large mixing bowl. Use a potato masher or a fork to mash the cauliflower until smooth and creamy.

  5. Add the sautéed garlic, heavy cream, and half of the shredded cheddar cheese to the cauliflower mash. Mix well until the cheese is melted and fully incorporated.

  6. Season the mixture with salt and pepper to taste. Remember that the bacon will add some saltiness, so be mindful of the amount of salt you add.

  7. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C). Grease a baking dish with butter or cooking spray.

  8. Transfer the cauliflower mash to the prepared baking dish, spreading it evenly. Top with the remaining shredded cheddar cheese and crumbled bacon.

  9. Bake in the preheated oven for about 20-25 minutes, or until the cheese is bubbly and golden brown.

  10. Remove from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes before serving. Garnish with chopped chives if desired.

Serving Suggestions

This Tasty Keto Bacon and Cheese Loaded Cauliflower Mash is a versatile dish that can be enjoyed on its own or as a side. Here are some serving suggestions to elevate your meal:

  • Serve alongside grilled chicken or steak for a complete keto-friendly dinner.
  • Top with a dollop of sour cream and sprinkle with extra bacon for an extra indulgent treat.
  • Pair with a side of sautéed vegetables for a well-rounded and nutritious meal.

Final Thoughts

Tasty Keto Bacon and Cheese Loaded Cauliflower Mash is a delicious and satisfying dish that proves you don’t have to sacrifice flavor on a keto diet. With its creamy texture, savory bacon, and gooey cheese, it’s a perfect alternative to traditional mashed potatoes. Whether you’re following a keto lifestyle or simply looking for a tasty side dish, give this recipe a try and enjoy the comforting flavors guilt-free!

Remember to experiment with different toppings and additions to make it your own. Get creative and have fun in the kitchen. Happy cooking!
