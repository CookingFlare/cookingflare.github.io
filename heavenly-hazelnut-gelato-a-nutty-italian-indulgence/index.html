Heavenly Hazelnut Gelato: A Nutty Italian Indulgence


If you’re a fan of rich, creamy desserts with a nutty twist, then you’re in for a treat. Today, we’re diving into the world of heavenly hazelnut gelato, a luscious Italian indulgence that will have you craving for more. This velvety, smooth gelato boasts the irresistible flavor of toasted hazelnuts, creating a delightful harmony of nuttiness and sweetness that will captivate your taste buds. Whether you’re a gelato aficionado or just looking to elevate your dessert game, this recipe is sure to impress. So, let’s roll up our sleeves and embark on a gelato-making adventure that will transport you to the streets of Italy with every spoonful.

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups whole milk
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 3/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 cup toasted hazelnuts, finely ground
  • 4 large egg yolks
  • 1 teaspoon pure vanilla extract
  • A pinch of salt

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the Hazelnuts:

    • Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C). Spread the hazelnuts in a single layer on a baking sheet and roast them for about 12-15 minutes, or until they turn golden and fragrant. Let them cool, then rub the hazelnuts with a clean kitchen towel to remove the skins. Once they are cool, finely grind the hazelnuts in a food processor until they reach a sand-like consistency.

  2. Infuse the Milk and Cream:

    • In a saucepan, combine the whole milk, heavy cream, and half of the sugar. Gently warm the mixture over medium heat, stirring occasionally, until it reaches a simmer. Once it simmers, remove it from the heat and stir in the ground hazelnuts. Cover the saucepan and let the mixture steep for 1 hour to infuse the flavors.

  3. Prepare the Base:

    • After the steeping time, strain the hazelnut-infused mixture through a fine-mesh sieve into a clean saucepan, pressing down on the hazelnuts to extract all the flavorful liquid. Discard the ground hazelnuts. In a separate bowl, whisk together the egg yolks, remaining sugar, and a pinch of salt until the mixture becomes pale and slightly thick. Gradually pour the warm hazelnut-infused liquid into the egg mixture, whisking constantly.

  4. Cook the Custard:

    • Place the saucepan over medium heat and cook the mixture, stirring constantly with a wooden spoon, until it thickens enough to coat the back of the spoon. Be careful not to let it boil. Once the custard reaches the right consistency, remove it from the heat and strain it through a fine-mesh sieve into a clean bowl. Stir in the vanilla extract.

  5. Chill and Churn:

    • Cover the bowl with plastic wrap, ensuring it touches the surface of the custard to prevent a skin from forming. Chill the custard in the refrigerator for at least 4 hours or preferably overnight. Once the custard is thoroughly chilled, pour it into your ice cream maker and churn according to the manufacturer’s instructions until it reaches a soft, creamy consistency.

  6. Freeze and Enjoy:

    • Transfer the churned hazelnut gelato into a freezer-safe container, smoothing the top with a spatula. Cover the container with a lid and freeze the gelato for at least 4 hours, or until it firms up. When ready to serve, let the gelato sit at room temperature for a few minutes to soften slightly, then scoop into bowls and savor every spoonful of this nutty Italian delight.

Tips and Variations:

  • For an extra indulgent touch, drizzle the hazelnut gelato with a luscious chocolate sauce and sprinkle with chopped hazelnuts.
  • Experiment with different nut varieties, such as almonds or pistachios, to create your own custom nutty gelato flavors.
  • If you don’t have an ice cream maker, you can still make this heavenly hazelnut gelato by pouring the custard into a freezer-safe container and stirring it every 30 minutes until it reaches the desired consistency.

There you have it – a heavenly hazelnut gelato recipe that promises to transport you to a blissful state of nutty indulgence. Whether you’re savoring it on a warm summer day or treating yourself to a cozy night in, this Italian-inspired dessert is an exquisite way to elevate your culinary repertoire. So, gather your ingredients, embrace the art of gelato-making, and get ready to experience the luxurious delight of homemade hazelnut gelato. Buon appetito!
