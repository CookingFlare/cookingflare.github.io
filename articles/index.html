Chipotle Cranberry Sauce Recipe Ingredients: 1 cup fresh or frozen cranberries 1/2 cup orange juice 1/4 cup honey 1 chipotle pepper in adobo sauce, minced 1 teaspoon adobo sauce 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon Pinch of salt Instructions: In a saucepan, combine the cranberries, orange juice, and honey. Bring the mixture to a simmer over medium heat, stirring occasionally. Add the minced chipotle pepper, adobo sauce, cinnamon, and salt to the saucepan. Continue to cook the sauce until the cranberries burst and the mixture thickens, stirring occasionally. Remove from heat and let the sauce cool before serving.
