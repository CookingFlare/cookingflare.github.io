Comforting Soup Recipes for Beginner Cooks


Are you a beginner cook looking for delicious and comforting soup recipes? Look no further! In this blog post, we will share five easy-to-make soup recipes that are perfect for beginners. These recipes are not only tasty but also require minimal cooking skills. So, let’s get started!

1. Creamy Tomato Soup

Ingredients:

  • 1 can of tomato soup
  • 1 cup of milk
  • 1 teaspoon of dried basil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a medium-sized saucepan, heat the tomato soup over medium heat.
  2. Stir in the milk and dried basil.
  3. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  4. Cook for 5 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  5. Serve hot and garnish with fresh basil leaves if desired.

2. Chicken Noodle Soup

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups of chicken broth
  • 1 cup of cooked chicken, shredded
  • 1 cup of egg noodles
  • 1 carrot, sliced
  • 1 celery stalk, diced
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a large pot, bring the chicken broth to a boil.
  2. Add the cooked chicken, egg noodles, carrot, and celery.
  3. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  4. Simmer for 15 minutes or until the vegetables are tender.
  5. Serve hot and enjoy this classic comfort food.

3. Butternut Squash Soup

Ingredients:

  • 1 butternut squash, peeled and cubed
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 4 cups of vegetable broth
  • 1 teaspoon of dried thyme
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a large pot, sauté the onion and garlic until fragrant.
  2. Add the butternut squash, vegetable broth, dried thyme, salt, and pepper.
  3. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat and simmer for 20 minutes or until the squash is tender.
  4. Use an immersion blender or a regular blender to puree the soup until smooth.
  5. Serve hot and savor the rich flavors of this comforting soup.

4. Lentil Soup

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup of dried lentils
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 carrots, diced
  • 2 celery stalks, diced
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 4 cups of vegetable broth
  • 1 teaspoon of cumin
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Rinse the lentils under cold water and drain.
  2. In a large pot, sauté the onion, carrots, celery, and garlic until tender.
  3. Add the lentils, vegetable broth, cumin, salt, and pepper.
  4. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat and simmer for 30-40 minutes or until the lentils are cooked.
  5. Serve hot and enjoy the hearty goodness of this lentil soup.

5. Potato Leek Soup

Ingredients:

  • 4 potatoes, peeled and diced
  • 2 leeks, sliced
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 4 cups of vegetable broth
  • 1 cup of heavy cream
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a large pot, sauté the leeks and garlic until softened.
  2. Add the potatoes, vegetable broth, salt, and pepper.
  3. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat and simmer for 20 minutes or until the potatoes are tender.
  4. Use an immersion blender or a regular blender to puree the soup until smooth.
  5. Stir in the heavy cream and heat gently until warmed through.
  6. Serve hot and indulge in the creamy goodness of this potato leek soup.

There you have it - five comforting soup recipes that are perfect for beginner cooks. These recipes are simple, delicious, and will warm your soul. Give them a try and enjoy the cozy feeling of a homemade bowl of soup. Happy cooking!
