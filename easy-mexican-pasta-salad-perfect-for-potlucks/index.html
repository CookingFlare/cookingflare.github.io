Easy Mexican Pasta Salad: Perfect for Potlucks


Potlucks are a fantastic way to bring people together over delicious food, and what better dish to bring than an Easy Mexican Pasta Salad? This vibrant and flavorful salad is a perfect addition to any potluck spread. Packed with colorful vegetables, zesty flavors, and a creamy dressing, it’s sure to be a hit with everyone. Plus, it’s incredibly easy to make and can be prepared ahead of time, making it a convenient option for busy hosts. Let’s dive into this mouthwatering recipe and get ready to impress your friends and family at the next potluck!

Ingredients

  • 1 pound pasta (such as rotini or penne)
  • 1 can black beans, drained and rinsed
  • 1 cup corn kernels (fresh, canned, or thawed if frozen)
  • 1 red bell pepper, diced
  • 1 orange bell pepper, diced
  • 1/2 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1/4 cup red onion, finely chopped
  • 1/4 cup fresh cilantro, chopped
  • 1 avocado, diced
  • 1/2 cup crumbled cotija cheese (or feta cheese for a substitute)
  • 1/3 cup sour cream
  • 1/4 cup mayonnaise
  • 2 tablespoons lime juice
  • 1 teaspoon chili powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon cumin
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Cook the Pasta: Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Add the pasta and cook according to the package instructions until al dente. Drain the pasta and rinse it under cold water to stop the cooking process. Transfer the pasta to a large mixing bowl.

  2. Prepare the Dressing: In a small bowl, whisk together the sour cream, mayonnaise, lime juice, chili powder, cumin, salt, and pepper until well combined. Adjust the seasoning to taste.

  3. Assemble the Salad: Add the black beans, corn, bell peppers, cherry tomatoes, red onion, and cilantro to the bowl with the pasta. Gently toss to combine. Pour the dressing over the pasta salad and toss until everything is evenly coated.

  4. Chill and Garnish: Cover the salad with plastic wrap and refrigerate for at least 1 hour to allow the flavors to meld together. Before serving, gently fold in the diced avocado and crumbled cotija cheese.

Tips and Variations

  • For a spicy kick, add a finely chopped jalapeno pepper to the salad.
  • This salad can be made a day in advance, but wait to add the avocado and cheese until just before serving to keep them fresh.
  • Feel free to customize the vegetables based on your preferences. Diced cucumber, green onions, or even grilled corn would be delightful additions.
  • To make it a complete meal, add grilled chicken or shrimp to the salad.

Final Thoughts

This Easy Mexican Pasta Salad is not only a feast for the taste buds but also a feast for the eyes with its vibrant colors and fresh ingredients. Its versatility and crowd-pleasing appeal make it an ideal dish for potlucks, picnics, or even a simple weeknight dinner. Whether you’re a seasoned cook or just starting out in the kitchen, this recipe is a winner. So, grab your favorite serving bowl, whip up this delightful salad, and get ready to bask in the compliments at your next gathering. Happy cooking!
