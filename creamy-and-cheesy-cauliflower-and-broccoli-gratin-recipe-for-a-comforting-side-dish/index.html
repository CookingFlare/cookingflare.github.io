Creamy and Cheesy Cauliflower and Broccoli Gratin Recipe for a Comforting Side Dish


Looking for a delicious and comforting side dish that will leave your taste buds satisfied? Look no further than this Creamy and Cheesy Cauliflower and Broccoli Gratin recipe! Packed with flavor, this dish combines the creaminess of cauliflower and the earthiness of broccoli, all topped off with a cheesy crust that will have you coming back for seconds.

Ingredients

  • 1 large head of cauliflower, cut into florets
  • 1 medium-sized head of broccoli, cut into florets
  • 3 tablespoons butter
  • 3 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • 2 cups milk
  • 1 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried thyme
  • Salt and pepper, to taste

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).

  2. In a large pot of boiling water, blanch the cauliflower and broccoli florets for 3-4 minutes, until they are slightly tender. Drain well and set aside.

  3. In a separate saucepan over medium heat, melt the butter. Once melted, whisk in the flour to create a roux. Cook for 1-2 minutes until the mixture turns slightly golden.

  4. Slowly pour in the milk while continuously whisking to avoid any lumps. Cook the mixture for about 5 minutes, until it thickens and coats the back of a spoon.

  5. Remove the saucepan from heat and stir in the shredded cheddar cheese, grated Parmesan cheese, garlic powder, dried thyme, salt, and pepper. Stir until the cheeses have melted and the sauce is smooth.

  6. Add the blanched cauliflower and broccoli to the cheese sauce, gently folding them in until they are well coated.

  7. Transfer the mixture to a baking dish and spread it out evenly.

  8. Bake in the preheated oven for 20-25 minutes, until the top is golden brown and bubbling.

  9. Remove from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes before serving.

Serving Suggestions

This Creamy and Cheesy Cauliflower and Broccoli Gratin is the perfect side dish for any occasion. It pairs well with grilled chicken, roasted meats, or even as a vegetarian main course. Serve it alongside a fresh salad or steamed vegetables for a complete and satisfying meal.

Tips and Variations

  • For an extra kick of flavor, sprinkle some breadcrumbs on top before baking.
  • Feel free to experiment with different types of cheese, such as Gruyere or Fontina, to create your own unique twist.
  • If you prefer a lighter version, you can substitute the whole milk with low-fat milk or even almond milk.
  • Add some cooked bacon or diced ham to the dish for a more indulgent and savory flavor.

Conclusion

Creamy and Cheesy Cauliflower and Broccoli Gratin is the ultimate comfort food that will surely become a family favorite. Its creamy texture, cheesy goodness, and the combination of cauliflower and broccoli make it a nutritious and satisfying side dish. Whether it’s for a holiday dinner or a simple weeknight meal, this recipe is guaranteed to impress. So, go ahead and give it a try. Your taste buds will thank you!
