Tropical Paradise Tart: Mango, Pineapple, and Coconut in Every Bite


Are you ready to embark on a culinary journey to a tropical paradise? Look no further than this irresistible Tropical Paradise Tart. Bursting with the vibrant flavors of mango, pineapple, and coconut, this tart is a true delight for your taste buds. Whether you’re hosting a summer gathering or simply craving a taste of the tropics, this recipe is sure to transport you to a sun-soaked beach with every bite.

Ingredients

For the crust:

  • 1 ½ cups graham cracker crumbs
  • 1/3 cup melted butter
  • ¼ cup granulated sugar

For the filling:

  • 1 can (14 ounces) sweetened condensed milk
  • 1 cup fresh mango, diced
  • 1 cup fresh pineapple, diced
  • 1 cup shredded coconut
  • 1 tablespoon lime juice
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

For the topping:

  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 2 tablespoons powdered sugar
  • Additional shredded coconut, for garnish

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C).

  2. In a medium-sized bowl, combine the graham cracker crumbs, melted butter, and granulated sugar. Mix well until the crumbs are evenly coated with the butter and sugar.

  3. Press the crumb mixture into the bottom and sides of a tart pan, firmly and evenly. Make sure to create a sturdy crust that will hold the filling.

  4. Bake the crust in the preheated oven for about 8-10 minutes or until it turns golden brown. Remove from the oven and let it cool completely.

  5. In a large mixing bowl, combine the sweetened condensed milk, diced mango, diced pineapple, shredded coconut, lime juice, and vanilla extract. Stir well to ensure all the ingredients are thoroughly mixed.

  6. Pour the filling mixture into the cooled tart crust, spreading it evenly.

  7. Place the tart in the refrigerator to chill for at least 2 hours, allowing the flavors to meld together and the filling to set.

  8. In the meantime, prepare the topping by whipping the heavy cream and powdered sugar together until stiff peaks form.

  9. Once the tart has chilled, spread the whipped cream over the top, covering the entire surface.

  10. Sprinkle additional shredded coconut over the whipped cream for a delightful tropical touch.

  11. Slice and serve chilled, savoring each and every bite of this Tropical Paradise Tart.

Tips and Variations

  • For a twist on the recipe, you can experiment with different tropical fruits such as papaya, passion fruit, or even kiwi.

  • If you prefer a more intense coconut flavor, you can toast the shredded coconut before adding it to the filling.

  • For an added crunch, you can sprinkle crushed macadamia nuts on top of the whipped cream.

  • This tart can be made ahead of time and stored in the refrigerator for up to 3 days. Just make sure to keep it covered to retain its freshness.

  • Serve this refreshing tart with a dollop of whipped cream and a sprinkle of lime zest for an extra burst of citrusy goodness.

Indulge in the flavors of the tropics with this Tropical Paradise Tart. Its luscious combination of mango, pineapple, and coconut will transport you to a beachside paradise with every bite. This recipe is an absolute crowd-pleaser and will leave your guests begging for seconds. So, gather your ingredients, put on some tropical tunes, and get ready to savor the taste of paradise.
