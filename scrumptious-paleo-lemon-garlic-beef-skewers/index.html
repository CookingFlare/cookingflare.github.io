Scrumptious Paleo Lemon Garlic Beef Skewers


Are you ready to take your grilling game to the next level? Look no further than these mouthwatering Paleo Lemon Garlic Beef Skewers! Packed with flavor, these skewers are a perfect addition to your summer grilling menu. The combination of zesty lemon, pungent garlic, and savory beef will have your taste buds dancing with delight. Plus, this recipe is Paleo-friendly, making it a great option for those looking to enjoy a delicious meal while sticking to their dietary preferences.

Ingredients

  • 2 lbs grass-fed beef, cut into 1-inch cubes
  • 1/4 cup extra virgin olive oil
  • Zest and juice of 2 lemons
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon sea salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • Wooden skewers, soaked in water for at least 30 minutes

Instructions

  1. In a large bowl, combine the extra virgin olive oil, lemon zest, lemon juice, minced garlic, dried oregano, sea salt, and black pepper. Mix well to create the marinade.

  2. Add the cubed beef to the bowl with the marinade. Toss the beef until it is well coated with the marinade. Cover the bowl and let the beef marinate in the refrigerator for at least 2 hours, or preferably overnight for the best flavor.

  3. Once the beef has marinated, preheat your grill to medium-high heat.

  4. Thread the marinated beef cubes onto the soaked wooden skewers, making sure to leave a little space between each piece of beef.

  5. Place the beef skewers on the preheated grill and cook for 3-4 minutes per side, or until the beef reaches your desired level of doneness.

  6. Remove the skewers from the grill and let them rest for a few minutes before serving.

  7. Serve the Paleo Lemon Garlic Beef Skewers with your favorite grilled vegetables or a fresh salad for a complete and satisfying meal.

Why You’ll Love These Skewers

The bright and zesty flavors of lemon and garlic perfectly complement the rich, savory taste of the beef. The marinade not only infuses the beef with incredible flavor but also helps to tenderize the meat, resulting in juicy and succulent skewers every time. Plus, by using grass-fed beef, you’re not only getting a delicious meal but also reaping the benefits of its higher levels of omega-3 fatty acids and antioxidants.

A Perfect Paleo Option

Following a Paleo lifestyle does not mean sacrificing taste, and these Paleo Lemon Garlic Beef Skewers are a prime example of that. They are free from grains, dairy, and processed ingredients, making them a great choice for those looking to stick to a Paleo diet. The ingredients are simple, wholesome, and packed with flavor, proving that eating Paleo can be both nutritious and delicious.

Final Thoughts

Whether you’re hosting a summer barbecue, planning a family dinner, or simply craving a flavorful meal, these Scrumptious Paleo Lemon Garlic Beef Skewers are sure to impress. With minimal prep and maximum flavor, this recipe is a great addition to your collection of go-to grilling favorites. So fire up the grill, gather your ingredients, and get ready to enjoy a dish that is not only healthy and satisfying but also bursting with irresistible flavors.
