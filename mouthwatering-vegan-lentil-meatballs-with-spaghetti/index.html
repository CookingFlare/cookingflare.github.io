Mouthwatering Vegan Lentil Meatballs with Spaghetti

3 min · 598 words

Are you ready for a culinary adventure that will tantalize your taste buds and leave you craving for more? Look no further, because today we are going to dive into the world of mouthwatering vegan lentil meatballs with spaghetti. This plant-based twist on a classic Italian dish will not only satisfy your cravings but also nourish your body with wholesome ingredients. So, tie your apron strings and let’s get cooking!

Ingredients

For the lentil meatballs:

  • 1 cup cooked lentils
  • 1/2 cup breadcrumbs
  • 1/4 cup finely chopped onion
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons ground flaxseeds mixed with 6 tablespoons water (flax egg)
  • 2 tablespoons nutritional yeast
  • 1 tablespoon tomato paste
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1/2 teaspoon smoked paprika
  • Salt and pepper to taste

For the spaghetti:

  • 8 ounces spaghetti
  • 2 cups marinara sauce
  • Fresh basil leaves for garnish

Instructions

  1. In a large mixing bowl, combine the cooked lentils, breadcrumbs, chopped onion, minced garlic, flax egg, nutritional yeast, tomato paste, dried oregano, dried basil, smoked paprika, salt, and pepper. Mix well until the ingredients are evenly incorporated.

  2. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C) and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.

  3. Shape the lentil mixture into meatballs, about 1-2 tablespoons each, and place them on the prepared baking sheet. Repeat until all the mixture is used.

  4. Bake the lentil meatballs in the preheated oven for 25-30 minutes, or until they are golden brown and firm to the touch.

  5. While the lentil meatballs are baking, cook the spaghetti according to the package instructions until al dente. Drain and set aside.

  6. In a large skillet, heat the marinara sauce over medium heat until warmed through.

  7. Once the lentil meatballs are done, carefully transfer them to the skillet with the marinara sauce. Gently stir to coat the meatballs with the sauce, being careful not to break them.

  8. Allow the meatballs to simmer in the sauce for 5-10 minutes, allowing the flavors to meld together.

  9. Serve the lentil meatballs and marinara sauce over the cooked spaghetti. Garnish with fresh basil leaves for an extra burst of flavor.

Tips and Variations

  • If you prefer a gluten-free option, replace the breadcrumbs with gluten-free breadcrumbs or ground oats.
  • These lentil meatballs can be made ahead of time and stored in the refrigerator for up to 3 days or frozen for up to 1 month. Simply reheat them in the marinara sauce before serving.
  • Feel free to experiment with different herbs and spices to suit your taste preferences. Adding a pinch of red pepper flakes will give the dish a spicy kick.
  • For a heartier meal, serve the lentil meatballs with a side of garlic bread and a fresh green salad.

Final Thoughts

There you have it, a delectable vegan twist on the classic meatball and spaghetti dish. These lentil meatballs are not only bursting with flavor but also packed with plant-based protein and fiber. Whether you’re a vegan, vegetarian, or simply looking to incorporate more plant-based meals into your diet, this recipe is sure to become a staple in your kitchen. So, grab your fork and dig into a plateful of mouthwatering vegan lentil meatballs with spaghetti. Buon appetito!
