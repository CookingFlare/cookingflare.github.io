10 Easy Bread Recipes You Can Make at Home


Making bread at home can be an incredibly rewarding experience. There’s something special about the aroma of freshly baked bread filling your kitchen, and it’s even better when you can enjoy a slice that you made yourself. If you’ve never baked bread before or are just looking for simple, easy recipes to try, you’re in the right place.

Bread baking doesn’t have to be difficult or time-consuming. With the right ingredients and a little bit of patience, you can create delicious loaves, rolls, and more from the comfort of your home. In this post, we’ll walk you through 10 easy bread recipes that anyone can make, whether you’re a seasoned baker or a beginner.

1. Classic White Bread

Let’s start with the classic white bread—simple, fluffy, and perfect for sandwiches or as a side to any meal.

Ingredients:

  • 3 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 packet active dry yeast
  • 1 tablespoon sugar
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons salt
  • 1 1/4 cups warm water
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil

Instructions:

  1. In a large bowl, dissolve sugar in warm water. Sprinkle the yeast over the top and let it sit for about 5 minutes until it becomes foamy.
  2. Add flour and salt to the yeast mixture. Stir until the dough starts to come together.
  3. Turn the dough onto a floured surface and knead for about 5-7 minutes, until it’s smooth and elastic.
  4. Grease a bowl and place the dough inside, turning it once to coat with oil. Cover with a damp towel and let it rise for about 1 hour or until doubled in size.
  5. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
  6. Punch down the dough and shape it into a loaf. Place it in a greased loaf pan.
  7. Bake for 25-30 minutes, or until golden brown. Let it cool on a wire rack before slicing.

2. No-Knead Bread

If you’re short on time or don’t want to do the kneading yourself, no-knead bread is a fantastic option. It requires minimal effort and the results are incredible.

Ingredients:

  • 3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 1/4 teaspoons salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon active dry yeast
  • 1 1/2 cups warm water

Instructions:

  1. In a large bowl, combine the flour, salt, and yeast. Add warm water and stir until a shaggy dough forms.
  2. Cover the bowl with plastic wrap and let the dough sit at room temperature for 12-18 hours, or until it has doubled in size.
  3. Preheat the oven to 450°F (230°C). Place a Dutch oven inside the oven while it heats up.
  4. Once preheated, carefully remove the Dutch oven, and place your dough inside. Cover with the lid and bake for 30 minutes.
  5. After 30 minutes, remove the lid and bake for another 10-15 minutes, until the bread is golden brown.
  6. Let the bread cool before slicing.

3. Whole Wheat Bread

Whole wheat bread is a healthier alternative to white bread. It’s dense, hearty, and perfect for those looking to boost their fiber intake.

Ingredients:

  • 3 cups whole wheat flour
  • 1 packet active dry yeast
  • 1 tablespoon honey
  • 1 1/4 cups warm water
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons salt

Instructions:

  1. In a large bowl, dissolve honey in warm water. Sprinkle the yeast over the top and let it sit for about 5 minutes until foamy.
  2. Add the olive oil, salt, and whole wheat flour to the yeast mixture. Stir to combine.
  3. Turn the dough onto a floured surface and knead for about 5-7 minutes.
  4. Place the dough in a greased bowl, cover it, and let it rise for about 1 hour.
  5. Punch down the dough, shape it into a loaf, and place it in a greased loaf pan.
  6. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C) and bake for 25-30 minutes, until the top is golden brown. Let it cool before slicing.

4. Garlic Herb Bread

This savory garlic herb bread is perfect for pairing with soups, salads, or pasta dishes. The combination of garlic and fresh herbs will fill your kitchen with an irresistible aroma.

Ingredients:

  • 3 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 packet active dry yeast
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 1/4 cups warm water
  • 1 tablespoon sugar
  • 2 teaspoons garlic powder
  • 1 tablespoon dried rosemary
  • 1 tablespoon dried thyme
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons salt

Instructions:

  1. In a small bowl, dissolve sugar in warm water and sprinkle yeast over the top. Let it sit for 5 minutes until it becomes foamy.
  2. In a large bowl, combine flour, garlic powder, rosemary, thyme, and salt.
  3. Add the yeast mixture and olive oil to the dry ingredients. Stir until combined.
  4. Knead the dough on a floured surface for about 5-7 minutes, until it’s smooth and elastic.
  5. Place the dough in a greased bowl, cover it, and let it rise for 1 hour.
  6. Punch down the dough, shape it into a loaf, and place it in a greased pan.
  7. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C) and bake for 25-30 minutes, until the bread is golden brown. Let it cool before slicing.

5. Soft Dinner Rolls

These soft and fluffy dinner rolls are perfect for any meal, from family dinners to holiday feasts.

Ingredients:

  • 3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 packet active dry yeast
  • 1/2 cup warm milk
  • 1/4 cup sugar
  • 1/4 cup butter, melted
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 egg

Instructions:

  1. In a small bowl, dissolve sugar in warm milk. Sprinkle yeast over the top and let it sit for 5 minutes until foamy.
  2. In a large bowl, combine flour, salt, and melted butter. Add the yeast mixture and egg, stirring until the dough comes together.
  3. Knead the dough on a floured surface for 5-7 minutes, until smooth.
  4. Place the dough in a greased bowl, cover, and let it rise for 1 hour.
  5. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
  6. Punch down the dough, divide it into 12 pieces, and roll each into a ball. Place the balls in a greased 9x13-inch baking dish.
  7. Bake for 15-20 minutes, until the rolls are golden brown. Let them cool slightly before serving.

6. Cheese Bread

Cheese lovers will adore this cheesy bread, with gooey cheese baked right into the dough.

Ingredients:

  • 3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 packet active dry yeast
  • 1 tablespoon sugar
  • 1 1/4 cups warm water
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 1/2 cups shredded cheese (cheddar, mozzarella, or a blend)

Instructions:

  1. In a small bowl, dissolve sugar in warm water and sprinkle yeast over the top. Let it sit for about 5 minutes until foamy.
  2. In a large bowl, combine flour and salt. Add the yeast mixture and stir to combine.
  3. Knead the dough for about 5-7 minutes, adding the cheese halfway through.
  4. Let the dough rise for about 1 hour in a greased bowl.
  5. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C). Punch down the dough, shape it into a loaf, and place it in a greased loaf pan.
  6. Bake for 25-30 minutes, until the bread is golden brown. Let it cool before slicing.

7. Cinnamon Swirl Bread

This sweet, cinnamon-infused bread is perfect for breakfast or as a dessert. The swirls of cinnamon sugar create a deliciously sweet and aromatic loaf.

Ingredients:

  • 3 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 packet active dry yeast
  • 1/2 cup warm milk
  • 1/4 cup sugar
  • 1/4 cup butter, melted
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 2 teaspoons ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 cup sugar (for cinnamon sugar mixture)

Instructions:

  1. In a small bowl, dissolve sugar in warm milk. Sprinkle yeast over the top and let it sit for 5 minutes until foamy.
  2. In a large bowl, combine flour, salt, and melted butter. Add the yeast mixture and stir until combined.
  3. Knead the dough for 5-7 minutes, until smooth and elastic. Let it rise for 1 hour.
  4. Punch down the dough and roll it out on a floured surface into a rectangle.
  5. Sprinkle cinnamon sugar over the dough and roll it up tightly.
  6. Place the dough into a greased loaf pan and let it rise for another 30 minutes.
  7. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C) and bake for 25-30 minutes. Let it cool before slicing.

8. Irish Soda Bread

Irish soda bread is a quick and easy bread that doesn’t require yeast. The combination of baking soda and buttermilk makes it rise and gives it a slightly tangy flavor.

Ingredients:

  • 4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 1/4 cups buttermilk

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 425°F (220°C). Grease a baking sheet or line it with parchment paper.
  2. In a large bowl, combine flour, baking soda, and salt.
  3. Gradually add the buttermilk, stirring until the dough comes together.
  4. Turn the dough out onto a floured surface and knead gently for about 1 minute.
  5. Shape the dough into a round loaf and place it on the baking sheet. Use a sharp knife to cut a deep cross on the top.
  6. Bake for 30-40 minutes, until the bread sounds hollow when tapped on the bottom.
  7. Let it cool before slicing.

9. Focaccia Bread

Focaccia is an Italian flatbread that’s incredibly versatile. It’s soft, slightly chewy, and can be topped with a variety of herbs, olives, or other ingredients.

Ingredients:

  • 3 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 packet active dry yeast
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 1/4 cups warm water
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil (for topping)
  • Fresh rosemary (optional)

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, combine warm water, yeast, and sugar. Let it sit for about 5 minutes until foamy.
  2. Add flour, salt, and olive oil. Stir until the dough forms.
  3. Knead the dough on a floured surface for about 5-7 minutes, until smooth.
  4. Place the dough in a greased bowl and let it rise for 1 hour.
  5. Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C). Punch down the dough and transfer it to a greased baking sheet.
  6. Use your fingers to dimple the dough and drizzle with olive oil. Add rosemary if desired.
  7. Bake for 20-25 minutes, until golden brown.

10. Sweet Potato Bread

Sweet potato bread is a slightly sweet and moist bread that’s perfect for a snack or as a side dish.

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups mashed sweet potatoes
  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1 teaspoon cinnamon
  • 1/2 teaspoon nutmeg
  • 1/4 cup butter, melted
  • 2 eggs

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease a loaf pan.
  2. In a large bowl, combine mashed sweet potatoes, sugar, and melted butter.
  3. In a separate bowl, whisk together flour, baking powder, baking soda, cinnamon, and nutmeg.
  4. Gradually add the dry ingredients to the wet ingredients, mixing well.
  5. Pour the batter into the greased loaf pan and bake for 55-60 minutes, until a toothpick comes out clean.
  6. Let the bread cool before slicing.

Conclusion

Whether you’re a beginner baker or a seasoned pro, these 10 bread recipes are easy to follow and yield delicious results. From classic white bread to sweet potato bread, there’s something for everyone. Each of these recipes can be customized with your favorite ingredients, so feel free to experiment and add your personal touch.

Baking bread at home doesn’t have to be intimidating. With a little time, effort, and a few simple ingredients, you can make your own bakery-quality bread right in your kitchen. So, why not give one (or all) of these recipes a try today?
