Easy Cookie Recipes for Busy Weeknights


Life can be hectic, especially during busy weeknights when the to-do list seems endless. Between work, school, chores, and family activities, finding time to prepare a delicious dessert might seem impossible. But what if you could whip up warm, homemade cookies in under 30 minutes? Yes, it’s entirely possible! Whether you crave classic chocolate chip, peanut butter, or something more unique, these easy cookie recipes will satisfy your sweet tooth without taking up too much time. Let’s dive into a variety of quick and simple cookie recipes that are perfect for busy weeknights.

1. Classic Chocolate Chip Cookies

Chocolate chip cookies are a timeless treat that never goes out of style. The comforting aroma of warm cookies right out of the oven is enough to make anyone smile. This recipe is simple, quick, and yields soft, chewy cookies every time.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 3/4 cup white sugar
  • 3/4 cup packed brown sugar
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 1/4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 2 cups semisweet chocolate chips

Directions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C). Line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
  2. In a large bowl, cream the butter, white sugar, and brown sugar together until light and fluffy.
  3. Beat in the eggs one at a time, followed by the vanilla extract.
  4. In a separate bowl, whisk the flour, baking soda, and salt together. Gradually add the dry ingredients to the wet mixture, mixing until just combined.
  5. Stir in the chocolate chips.
  6. Drop spoonfuls of cookie dough onto the prepared baking sheet, spacing them about 2 inches apart.
  7. Bake for 8-10 minutes or until golden brown around the edges.
  8. Let the cookies cool on the baking sheet for a few minutes before transferring them to a wire rack to cool completely.

These cookies are perfect for any time of day, whether you’re winding down after a long day or need a quick treat for an unexpected guest.

2. Peanut Butter Cookies

Peanut butter cookies are another favorite that comes together in a flash. With just a few ingredients, you can create a batch of soft, chewy cookies that are packed with peanut flavor.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup peanut butter (creamy or chunky)
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 egg
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1/2 tsp baking soda

Directions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C). Line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
  2. In a large mixing bowl, combine the peanut butter, sugar, egg, vanilla extract, and baking soda. Stir until smooth and well combined.
  3. Roll the dough into tablespoon-sized balls and place them on the prepared baking sheet, spacing them about 2 inches apart.
  4. Gently press each cookie with a fork in a crisscross pattern.
  5. Bake for 8-10 minutes or until the edges are golden brown.
  6. Allow the cookies to cool on the baking sheet for a few minutes before transferring to a wire rack.

These peanut butter cookies are soft, slightly crumbly, and full of rich peanut butter flavor. They’re a quick way to satisfy your cravings without spending too much time in the kitchen.

3. Oatmeal Raisin Cookies

Oatmeal raisin cookies are perfect for those who prefer a heartier cookie. The oats provide a nice texture, and the raisins add a natural sweetness that pairs beautifully with the cinnamon and nutmeg in the dough. These cookies are also a great way to sneak in some fiber!

Ingredients:

  • 3/4 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1 cup packed brown sugar
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1/2 tsp ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 tsp ground nutmeg
  • 3 cups old-fashioned rolled oats
  • 1 1/2 cups raisins

Directions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C). Line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
  2. In a large bowl, cream the butter, brown sugar, and granulated sugar together until smooth and fluffy.
  3. Beat in the eggs one at a time, followed by the vanilla extract.
  4. In a separate bowl, whisk the flour, baking soda, cinnamon, and nutmeg together. Gradually mix this into the wet ingredients.
  5. Stir in the oats and raisins until evenly combined.
  6. Drop spoonfuls of dough onto the prepared baking sheet, spacing them about 2 inches apart.
  7. Bake for 10-12 minutes, or until the edges are golden and the center is set.
  8. Let the cookies cool on the baking sheet for a few minutes before transferring them to a wire rack to cool completely.

These oatmeal raisin cookies are soft, chewy, and satisfying, with just the right balance of sweetness and spice.

4. Snickerdoodle Cookies

Snickerdoodles are a fun, cinnamon-sugar-coated cookie that’s a crowd-pleaser for all ages. Their soft, chewy texture combined with the cinnamon-sugar coating makes them irresistible.

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 2 3/4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp cream of tartar
  • 1/2 tsp baking soda
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 2 tbsp granulated sugar (for rolling)
  • 2 tsp ground cinnamon (for rolling)

Directions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C). Line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
  2. In a large bowl, cream the butter and sugar together until light and fluffy.
  3. Beat in the eggs one at a time, followed by the vanilla extract.
  4. In a separate bowl, whisk the flour, cream of tartar, baking soda, and salt together.
  5. Gradually add the dry ingredients to the wet ingredients, mixing until fully combined.
  6. In a small bowl, combine the 2 tablespoons of sugar with the cinnamon.
  7. Roll the cookie dough into tablespoon-sized balls and then roll each ball in the cinnamon-sugar mixture.
  8. Place the dough balls on the prepared baking sheet, spacing them about 2 inches apart.
  9. Bake for 8-10 minutes or until the cookies are slightly cracked on top.
  10. Let the cookies cool on the baking sheet for a few minutes before transferring to a wire rack to cool completely.

These snickerdoodles are simple, flavorful, and a great choice for when you want something easy yet delicious.

5. Salted Caramel Chocolate Cookies

For those who crave a combination of sweet and salty, these salted caramel chocolate cookies are an indulgent treat. The rich chocolate flavor is balanced perfectly with the gooey caramel and a touch of sea salt.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 3/4 cup packed brown sugar
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 large egg
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1 cup semi-sweet chocolate chips
  • 1/2 cup caramel bits or chopped caramel candies
  • Sea salt for sprinkling

Directions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C). Line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
  2. In a large bowl, cream the butter, brown sugar, and granulated sugar together until light and fluffy.
  3. Beat in the egg and vanilla extract.
  4. In a separate bowl, whisk the flour, baking soda, and salt together.
  5. Gradually add the dry ingredients to the wet ingredients, mixing until just combined.
  6. Stir in the chocolate chips and caramel bits.
  7. Drop tablespoon-sized portions of dough onto the prepared baking sheet, spacing them about 2 inches apart.
  8. Sprinkle each cookie with a pinch of sea salt before baking.
  9. Bake for 10-12 minutes or until the edges are golden and the center is set.
  10. Let the cookies cool on the baking sheet for a few minutes before transferring them to a wire rack.

These cookies have the perfect balance of rich chocolate, gooey caramel, and a sprinkle of salt that makes them truly unforgettable.

6. 3-Ingredient Nutella Cookies

When you’re truly pressed for time but still want something sweet, these Nutella cookies come to the rescue. With just three ingredients, you can have a batch of soft, melt-in-your-mouth cookies ready in no time.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup Nutella
  • 1 egg
  • 3/4 cup all-purpose flour

Directions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C). Line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
  2. In a medium bowl, mix the Nutella, egg, and flour together until fully combined.
  3. Drop tablespoon-sized portions of dough onto the baking sheet, spacing them about 2 inches apart.
  4. Bake for 8-10 minutes, or until the cookies are firm around the edges.
  5. Let the cookies cool on the baking sheet for a few minutes before transferring to a wire rack.

These Nutella cookies are rich, soft, and incredibly easy to make, perfect for when you need a quick dessert or snack.

Final Thoughts

Baking cookies doesn’t have to be complicated, and it doesn’t require hours in the kitchen. With these easy recipes, you can bake a batch of homemade cookies in under 30 minutes, even on the busiest of weeknights. Whether you love classic chocolate chip, crave peanut butter, or are looking for something a bit more indulgent, these recipes will help you create delicious treats without the stress. So, next time you’re feeling the need for a sweet snack, try one of these easy cookie recipes – they’re sure to bring comfort to your evening and a smile to your face!
