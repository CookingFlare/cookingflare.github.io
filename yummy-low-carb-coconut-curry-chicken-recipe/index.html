Yummy Low Carb Coconut Curry Chicken Recipe


If you’re looking for a delicious and healthy low carb meal that’s bursting with flavor, then you’re in for a treat! Our Yummy Low Carb Coconut Curry Chicken Recipe is the perfect dish to satisfy your cravings without compromising on taste. Packed with tender chicken, aromatic spices, and creamy coconut milk, this curry is a true delight for the taste buds.

Ingredients

  • 2 lbs chicken breast, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 1 tablespoon coconut oil
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon ginger, grated
  • 2 tablespoons curry powder
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 teaspoon cumin powder
  • 1 teaspoon coriander powder
  • 1 can (14 oz) coconut milk
  • 1 cup chicken broth
  • 1 cup broccoli florets
  • 1 red bell pepper, sliced
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • Fresh cilantro, for garnish

Instructions

  1. Heat the coconut oil in a large pan over medium heat. Add the chopped onion and sauté until translucent.

  2. Add the minced garlic and grated ginger to the pan and cook for another minute until fragrant.

  3. In a small bowl, combine the curry powder, turmeric powder, cumin powder, and coriander powder. Mix well.

  4. Push the onion mixture to one side of the pan and add the chicken pieces to the other side. Cook the chicken until browned on all sides.

  5. Sprinkle the spice mixture over the chicken and onion mixture. Stir well to coat the chicken evenly with the spices.

  6. Pour in the coconut milk and chicken broth. Stir to combine everything together.

  7. Bring the mixture to a boil, then reduce the heat to low and let it simmer for about 15 minutes, or until the chicken is cooked through and tender.

  8. Add the broccoli florets and sliced red bell pepper to the pan. Cook for an additional 5 minutes, or until the vegetables are crisp-tender.

  9. Season with salt and pepper to taste.

  10. Serve the Yummy Low Carb Coconut Curry Chicken hot, garnished with fresh cilantro.

Why Choose Low Carb?

Low carb diets have gained popularity in recent years due to their effectiveness in aiding weight loss and improving overall health. By reducing your carbohydrate intake, you can regulate your blood sugar levels, increase your energy levels, and promote fat burning.

Coconut milk is a great alternative to heavy cream or dairy-based sauces in low carb recipes. It adds a rich and creamy texture to this curry without adding unnecessary carbs. Plus, coconut milk is rich in healthy fats that can keep you feeling satisfied and full for longer.

Health Benefits of Curry

Aside from being incredibly flavorful, this Yummy Low Carb Coconut Curry Chicken Recipe also offers a range of health benefits. The spices used in curry, such as turmeric and cumin, are known for their anti-inflammatory properties. They can help reduce inflammation in the body and support a healthy immune system.

Additionally, the combination of chicken and vegetables in this recipe provides a good dose of lean protein and essential nutrients. The broccoli and red bell pepper add a vibrant touch of color and provide vitamins, fiber, and antioxidants.

Serving Suggestions

This Yummy Low Carb Coconut Curry Chicken Recipe is incredibly versatile and can be enjoyed on its own or paired with your favorite low carb side dishes. Here are a few serving suggestions to get you started:

  • Serve over cauliflower rice for a complete low carb meal.
  • Garnish with toasted coconut flakes for an extra touch of flavor and crunch.
  • Add a squeeze of lime juice before serving to brighten the flavors.
  • Serve alongside a fresh green salad for a light and refreshing meal.

Conclusion

Our Yummy Low Carb Coconut Curry Chicken Recipe is a true winner when it comes to low carb cooking. It’s packed with flavor, easy to make, and offers a range of health benefits. Whether you’re following a low carb diet or simply looking for a delicious and nutritious meal, this recipe is sure to impress. So go ahead, give it a try and indulge in a bowl of creamy, aromatic curry goodness!
