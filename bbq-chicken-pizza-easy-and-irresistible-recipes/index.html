BBQ Chicken Pizza: Easy and Irresistible Recipes


Who doesn’t love a good old BBQ chicken pizza? The combination of tangy barbecue sauce, tender chicken, gooey cheese, and crispy crust is simply irresistible. Whether you’re hosting a casual get-together or just craving a delicious homemade pizza, this BBQ chicken pizza recipe is sure to become a favorite. In this blog post, we’ll explore not just one, but three different ways to create mouthwatering BBQ chicken pizzas at home. Let’s dive in and discover the secrets to making these easy and irresistible recipes.

Classic BBQ Chicken Pizza

Ingredients:

  • 1 pre-made pizza dough
  • 1 cup cooked and shredded chicken breast
  • 1/2 cup barbecue sauce
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/4 red onion, thinly sliced
  • 2 tablespoons chopped fresh cilantro
  • Olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 475°F (245°C).
  2. Roll out the pizza dough on a lightly floured surface to your desired thickness.
  3. Place the dough on a pizza stone or baking sheet.
  4. Brush the dough with a thin layer of olive oil.
  5. In a small bowl, mix the shredded chicken with barbecue sauce until well coated.
  6. Spread the barbecue chicken evenly over the pizza dough.
  7. Top with shredded mozzarella cheese, sliced red onions, and a sprinkle of salt and pepper.
  8. Bake in the preheated oven for 12-15 minutes, or until the crust is golden and the cheese is bubbly.
  9. Once out of the oven, sprinkle with chopped cilantro for a fresh finish.
  10. Slice and serve hot!

Grilled BBQ Chicken Pizza

Ingredients:

  • 1 pre-made pizza dough
  • 1 cup cooked and shredded chicken breast
  • 1/2 cup barbecue sauce
  • 1 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1/4 cup corn kernels
  • 2 green onions, thinly sliced
  • Olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your grill to medium-high heat.
  2. Roll out the pizza dough on a lightly floured surface.
  3. Brush one side of the dough with olive oil.
  4. Place the dough, oiled side down, directly on the grill grates.
  5. Grill for 2-3 minutes with the lid closed, or until the dough has grill marks and is firm enough to flip.
  6. Carefully remove the dough from the grill and place it cooked-side up on a cutting board or baking sheet.
  7. Spread barbecue sauce evenly over the grilled side of the dough.
  8. Toss the shredded chicken in barbecue sauce and spread it over the pizza dough.
  9. Sprinkle with shredded cheddar cheese, corn kernels, green onions, salt, and pepper.
  10. Return the pizza to the grill and cook for an additional 5-7 minutes, or until the cheese is melted and bubbly.
  11. Remove from the grill, slice, and enjoy your delicious grilled BBQ chicken pizza!

BBQ Chicken Pizza Stuffed Peppers

Ingredients:

  • 4 large bell peppers, halved and seeds removed
  • 1 cup cooked and shredded chicken breast
  • 1/2 cup barbecue sauce
  • 1 cup shredded pepper jack cheese
  • 1/4 cup diced red bell pepper
  • 1/4 cup diced red onion
  • Olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. In a bowl, mix the shredded chicken with barbecue sauce until well coated.
  3. Lightly brush the outside of the bell pepper halves with olive oil and sprinkle with salt and pepper.
  4. Fill each pepper half with the barbecue chicken mixture.
  5. Top with shredded pepper jack cheese, diced red bell pepper, and red onion.
  6. Place the stuffed peppers on a baking sheet and bake in the preheated oven for 25-30 minutes, or until the peppers are tender and the cheese is golden and bubbly.
  7. Remove from the oven and let them cool for a few minutes.
  8. Serve the BBQ chicken pizza stuffed peppers as a unique and delicious twist on traditional pizza!

These three variations of BBQ chicken pizza are not only easy to make, but they are also incredibly delicious. Whether you prefer the classic oven-baked version, the smoky flavor of a grilled pizza, or a creative twist with stuffed peppers, there’s a BBQ chicken pizza for every occasion. So, gather your ingredients, get creative in the kitchen, and treat yourself to the irresistible flavors of homemade BBQ chicken pizza. Your taste buds will thank you!
