Fluffy Vanilla Cupcakes with Salted Caramel Frosting: A Sweet and Salty Combination


There’s something truly magical about the combination of sweet and salty flavors. It’s like a symphony in your mouth, where each bite brings a perfect harmony of taste and texture. And when it comes to cupcakes, nothing beats the classic pairing of fluffy vanilla cake with rich and indulgent salted caramel frosting. So, let’s dive into the world of sweet and salty delights with these Fluffy Vanilla Cupcakes with Salted Caramel Frosting.

Ingredients

For the Cupcakes:

  • 1 ½ cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 ½ teaspoons baking powder
  • ¼ teaspoon salt
  • ¾ cup unsalted butter, at room temperature
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • ½ cup whole milk

For the Salted Caramel Frosting:

  • 1 cup unsalted butter, at room temperature
  • 2 cups powdered sugar
  • ½ cup salted caramel sauce (homemade or store-bought)
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • ¼ teaspoon salt

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C) and line a cupcake pan with paper liners.

  2. In a medium-sized bowl, whisk together the flour, baking powder, and salt. Set aside.

  3. In a separate large bowl, cream together the butter and sugar until light and fluffy. This should take about 3-4 minutes using an electric mixer.

  4. Add the eggs, one at a time, making sure to beat well after each addition. Stir in the vanilla extract.

  5. Gradually add the flour mixture to the butter mixture, alternating with the milk. Start and end with the flour mixture, mixing just until combined. Be careful not to overmix, as this can result in tough cupcakes.

  6. Divide the batter evenly among the prepared cupcake liners, filling each about two-thirds full.

  7. Bake for 18-20 minutes or until a toothpick inserted into the center of a cupcake comes out clean. Remove from the oven and let the cupcakes cool in the pan for 5 minutes. Then transfer them to a wire rack to cool completely.

  8. While the cupcakes are cooling, let’s prepare the salted caramel frosting. In a large bowl, beat the butter until creamy and smooth.

  9. Gradually add the powdered sugar, mixing well after each addition. This will ensure a smooth and fluffy frosting.

  10. Add the salted caramel sauce, vanilla extract, and salt. Beat until everything is well combined and the frosting is light and airy.

  11. Once the cupcakes are completely cooled, generously frost each cupcake with the salted caramel frosting, using a piping bag or a spatula.

  12. For an extra touch of sweetness, drizzle some additional salted caramel sauce over the frosted cupcakes.

And voila! Your Fluffy Vanilla Cupcakes with Salted Caramel Frosting are ready to be enjoyed. The light and airy vanilla cupcakes perfectly complement the rich and decadent salted caramel frosting, creating a delightful balance of flavors. These cupcakes are perfect for special occasions, afternoon tea, or whenever you’re in need of a sweet and salty treat. So, go ahead and indulge in this irresistible combination!

Remember, the key to achieving the best results is to follow the instructions closely and use high-quality ingredients. With a little bit of effort and a whole lot of love, you’ll have a batch of Fluffy Vanilla Cupcakes with Salted Caramel Frosting that will leave everyone craving for more. Enjoy!
