Healthy and Gluten-Free Almond Flour Chocolate Chip Cookies


Who doesn’t love a warm, gooey chocolate chip cookie straight from the oven? But for those who follow a gluten-free or healthy lifestyle, finding a delicious cookie that meets their dietary requirements can be a challenge. Well, fret no more! I have the perfect recipe for you – Healthy and Gluten-Free Almond Flour Chocolate Chip Cookies.

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups almond flour
  • 1/4 cup coconut flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/3 cup coconut oil, melted
  • 1/4 cup pure maple syrup
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 1/2 cup dark chocolate chips
  • Optional: 1/2 cup chopped walnuts or pecans for added crunch

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C) and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.

  2. In a large mixing bowl, whisk together the almond flour, coconut flour, baking soda, and salt. Make sure they are well combined.

  3. In a separate bowl, mix together the melted coconut oil, maple syrup, and vanilla extract until smooth and well incorporated.

  4. Pour the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients and stir until a thick dough forms. Fold in the dark chocolate chips (and nuts, if desired) until evenly distributed throughout the dough.

  5. Take about 1 tablespoon of dough and roll it into a ball. Place the cookie dough balls onto the prepared baking sheet, leaving enough space between each cookie for spreading.

  6. Gently press down on each cookie dough ball with the back of a spoon to flatten them slightly.

  7. Bake the cookies in the preheated oven for 10-12 minutes or until the edges turn golden brown. The cookies will still be soft in the center, but they will firm up as they cool.

  8. Remove the baking sheet from the oven and let the cookies cool on the sheet for 5 minutes. Then, transfer them to a wire rack to cool completely.

  9. Once the cookies have cooled, store them in an airtight container at room temperature for up to 5 days. Enjoy!

Why Almond Flour?

Almond flour is an excellent alternative to traditional wheat flour for those following a gluten-free or low-carb diet. It is made from ground almonds and provides a nutty flavor and a soft, moist texture to baked goods. Almond flour is also rich in healthy fats, protein, and fiber, making it a nutritious choice for your cookies.

Health Benefits of Dark Chocolate

Aside from its indulgent taste, dark chocolate has a range of health benefits. It is rich in antioxidants, which can help reduce inflammation and protect against oxidative stress. Dark chocolate also contains flavonoids, which have been shown to support heart health and improve brain function. So, you can feel good about enjoying these chocolate chip cookies!

Conclusion

These Healthy and Gluten-Free Almond Flour Chocolate Chip Cookies are a delicious treat that you can enjoy guilt-free. With the perfect blend of almond flour, coconut oil, and dark chocolate chips, these cookies are not only gluten-free but also packed with nutrients. So, go ahead and satisfy your sweet tooth while staying true to your healthy lifestyle. Happy baking!
