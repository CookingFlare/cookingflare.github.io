Feeling Lucky? Try Chinese Fortune Cookies Recipes at Home


Everybody loves Chinese food, be it the tasty stir-fries, the rich dumplings or crispy fortune cookies to end a meal with a twist. As much as we enjoy ordering those sweet and crunchy fortune cookies at our favorite Chinese restaurant, nothing compares to the excitement of making them at home, especially when you finally get to read the scroll of paper tucked inside that says “You’re going to have a great day!” Now, let’s find out how to make these delightful treats right in your kitchen.

Ingredients

For approximately 50 fortune cookies, you’ll need:

  • 1 cup (100g) all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons (25g) sugar
  • 1/4 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 3 tablespoons (45ml) water
  • 20 fortune cookies papers (available at Asian grocery stores or online)
  • 2-3 tablespoons (30-45ml) melting wafers or chocolate (like candy melts or almond bark, you can use dark, milk, or white chocolate)

Instructions

Step 1: Preheat oven and Prepare Tray

Begin by preheating your oven to 300°F (150°C). Grease a baking sheet or line it with a silicon mat to prevent your cookies from sticking.

Step 2: Mix Dry Ingredients

In a large mixing bowl, combine the all-purpose flour, sugar, baking soda, and a pinch of salt. Mix well to evenly distribute the ingredients.

Step 3: Combine Wet Ingredients

Gradually add in the water, mixing gently until a smooth, thick batter forms. The batter should have a consistency similar to pancake batter, neither too thick nor too thin.

Step 4: Resting the Batter

Cover your mixing bowl with a kitchen towel or plastic wrap and let the batter rest for 15-20 minutes. This allows the baking soda to activate, resulting in a better rise.

Step 5: Forming and Baking Fortune Cookies

Carefully line a work surface with a silicone baking mat or parchment paper to prevent sticking.

Using a tablespoon or cookie scoop, measure out the batter and drop it onto the prepared surface. Use the bottom of the scoop or a spoon to spread a scant 1 tablespoon of batter into a very thin, approximately 5-inch (12.5cm) circle. It takes practice, but try to make them as uniform in size as possible.

Place a fortune in the center of each circle. Then, fold the cookie dough over the fortune into a semi-circle, pressing the edges together gently to seal. Place the formed cookie on the prepared baking sheet.

Repeat this process with the remaining batter.

When the baking sheet is full, bake it in the preheated oven for 12-15 minutes, or until the cookies turn a light golden color. Midway through, rotate the tray to ensure even baking.

Step 6: Shaping Fortune Cookies

As soon as the fortune cookies come out of the oven, use a spatula to transfer them to the prepared work surface. While they are still warm and pliable, use your hands or the edge of a plate to slightly press and shape the cookies into a cup-like shape. Make sure you fold them right away or the cookies will harden too quickly.

Step 7: Finishing Touch

For decoration, melt the melting wafers or chocolate following the package instructions. Using a fork or skewer, quickly and carefully dip the edges of the cookies into the melted wafers, placing them on a silicone mat or parchment paper to set.

Once the wafers have hardened, fill the cookies with your choice of filling, like nuts, dried fruit, or chocolate chips if desired.

Tips to Remember

  • Be quick and efficient when handling the warm cookies. They can become brittle rather fast.
  • If the cookies crack when trying to fold them, return them to the oven for another minute or two.
  • If you’re unable to find fortune cookie papers, you can use wax or parchment paper. However, remember that when you fold them, a regular fold creates two cookies.

Conclusion

Making fortune cookies at home is a fun and engaging activity. Plus, you can surprise your friends and family with your homemade Chinese takeout ending. The next time you’re craving that sweet and satisfying crunch of fortune cookies, give this recipe a try — your taste buds and your fortune will thank you!
