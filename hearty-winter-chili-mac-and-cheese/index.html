Hearty Winter Chili Mac and Cheese


When the winter chill sets in, there’s nothing quite like a comforting bowl of hearty chili mac and cheese to warm you up from the inside out. This wholesome dish brings together the best of two classic comfort foods – chili and mac and cheese – to create a flavorful and satisfying meal that’s perfect for cozy nights in. Whether you’re hosting a gathering or simply craving a soul-warming dinner, this recipe is sure to please.

Ingredients

For the Chili:

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 large onion, diced
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can (14 ounces) diced tomatoes
  • 1 can (15 ounces) kidney beans, drained and rinsed
  • 1 can (15 ounces) black beans, drained and rinsed
  • 1 cup beef broth
  • 2 tablespoons chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil

For the Mac and Cheese:

  • 12 ounces elbow macaroni
  • 4 tablespoons butter
  • 1/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 cups milk
  • 2 cups shredded cheddar cheese
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Chili:

    • In a large pot, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the diced onions and garlic, and sauté until fragrant.
    • Add the ground beef to the pot and cook until browned, breaking it up with a spoon as it cooks.
    • Stir in the diced tomatoes, kidney beans, black beans, beef broth, chili powder, cumin, salt, and pepper. Bring the mixture to a simmer and let it cook for 20-25 minutes, stirring occasionally.

  2. Cook the Macaroni:

    • While the chili is simmering, cook the elbow macaroni according to the package instructions. Drain and set aside.

  3. Prepare the Cheese Sauce:

    • In a separate saucepan, melt the butter over medium heat. Whisk in the flour to create a roux, cooking for a minute until it turns golden.
    • Gradually pour in the milk, whisking constantly to avoid lumps. Cook until the mixture thickens.
    • Stir in the shredded cheddar cheese until smooth and creamy. Season with salt and pepper to taste.

  4. Combine the Chili and Mac and Cheese:

    • Once the chili is ready, stir in the cooked macaroni until well combined.
    • Pour the cheese sauce over the chili and macaroni mixture, gently folding it in until everything is coated in the luscious cheese sauce.

  5. Serve and Enjoy:

    • Ladle the hearty winter chili mac and cheese into bowls and garnish with a sprinkle of shredded cheese, chopped green onions, or a dollop of sour cream, if desired.

Tips and Variations

  • For a vegetarian version, simply omit the ground beef and use vegetable broth instead of beef broth for the chili.
  • Add a kick of heat by incorporating diced jalapeños or a dash of hot sauce into the chili.
  • Experiment with different types of cheese, such as pepper jack or smoked gouda, to customize the flavor profile of the mac and cheese.

There you have it – a delightful fusion of flavors and textures that will undoubtedly become a favorite in your winter recipe repertoire. The combination of savory chili, creamy mac and cheese, and a medley of comforting spices makes this dish a standout winner for any occasion. So, gather your ingredients, embrace the winter vibes, and treat yourself to a steaming bowl of homemade hearty winter chili mac and cheese.
