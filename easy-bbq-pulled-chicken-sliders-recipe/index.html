Easy BBQ Pulled Chicken Sliders Recipe


If you’re looking for a crowd-pleasing dish that’s perfect for any gathering, these Easy BBQ Pulled Chicken Sliders are just the ticket. Packed with flavor and simple to make, they’re a fantastic addition to your summer barbecue menu or a quick and satisfying weeknight dinner. The best part? You can whip up a batch in no time, leaving you more time to enjoy with your guests or simply relax. Let’s dive into the details of this mouthwatering recipe.

Ingredients

For the BBQ Pulled Chicken:

  • 2 lbs boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1 cup BBQ sauce (use your favorite flavor)
  • 1/2 cup chicken broth
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1 teaspoon onion powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste

For the Sliders:

  • 12 slider buns
  • 1 cup coleslaw (optional, for topping)

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Chicken: Place the chicken breasts in the bottom of your slow cooker. In a bowl, mix together the BBQ sauce, chicken broth, minced garlic, paprika, onion powder, salt, and pepper. Pour the mixture over the chicken, ensuring it’s evenly coated. Cover and cook on low for 6-7 hours, or on high for 3-4 hours, until the chicken is tender and easily shreds with a fork.

  2. Shred the Chicken: Once the chicken is cooked, use two forks to shred it directly in the slow cooker, mixing it into the sauce.

  3. Assemble the Sliders: Toast the slider buns in the oven or on a skillet. Place a generous amount of the BBQ pulled chicken onto the bottom half of each bun. If desired, top with a spoonful of coleslaw for added crunch and freshness. Finish with the top half of the bun.

  4. Serve and Enjoy: Arrange the sliders on a platter and watch them disappear as your guests savor every delectable bite. These sliders pair perfectly with a side of crispy fries, a fresh salad, or even some corn on the cob.

Tips and Tricks

  • Customize the Flavor: Experiment with different BBQ sauce flavors to find your favorite combination. Whether you prefer a smoky, spicy, or sweet profile, there’s a BBQ sauce out there to suit your taste.

  • Make it Ahead: This recipe is perfect for meal prep. Prepare the BBQ pulled chicken ahead of time and store it in the refrigerator for up to 3 days. When you’re ready to serve, simply reheat the chicken and assemble the sliders for a quick and satisfying meal.

  • Feeding a Crowd: If you’re hosting a larger gathering, simply double or triple the recipe to accommodate the number of guests. You can’t go wrong with a plentiful supply of these irresistible sliders.

  • Sides and Accompaniments: Elevate the sliders by offering a variety of condiments and toppings such as pickles, sliced onions, or even a drizzle of extra BBQ sauce.

There you have it—easy, flavorful, and sure to be a hit at your next get-together. Whether you’re celebrating with friends, planning a family picnic, or simply craving a delicious meal, these BBQ Pulled Chicken Sliders are a go-to option that will keep everyone coming back for more. Happy cooking and enjoy every bite!
