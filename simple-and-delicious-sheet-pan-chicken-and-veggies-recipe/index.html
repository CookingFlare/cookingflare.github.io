Simple and Delicious: Sheet Pan Chicken and Veggies Recipe


If you’re looking for a fuss-free, delicious, and nutritious meal that requires minimal effort and cleanup, then you’re in the right place. Sheet pan meals have been gaining popularity for all the right reasons - they are incredibly easy to prepare, versatile, and offer a perfect balance of flavors. Today, I’m excited to share with you a simple and delicious sheet pan chicken and veggies recipe that will become a regular in your meal rotation.

Ingredients

  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1 pound baby potatoes, halved
  • 2 bell peppers, sliced
  • 1 red onion, cut into wedges
  • 2 cups broccoli florets
  • 3 tablespoons olive oil
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • 1 teaspoon dried rosemary
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley for garnish

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C).

  2. In a small bowl, mix together the olive oil, minced garlic, dried thyme, dried rosemary, salt, and pepper.

  3. Place the chicken breasts in a large resealable plastic bag and pour half of the prepared marinade over the chicken. Seal the bag and massage the marinade into the chicken until well coated. Set aside to marinate while you prepare the vegetables.

  4. On a large sheet pan, arrange the halved baby potatoes, sliced bell peppers, onion wedges, and broccoli florets. Drizzle the remaining marinade over the vegetables and toss to coat evenly.

  5. Move the vegetables to the sides of the sheet pan, creating space in the center for the marinated chicken breasts.

  6. Place the marinated chicken breasts in the center of the sheet pan, ensuring they are not overlapping.

  7. Transfer the sheet pan to the preheated oven and roast for 25-30 minutes, or until the chicken is cooked through and the vegetables are tender, with crispy edges.

  8. Once done, remove the sheet pan from the oven and let it rest for a few minutes.

  9. Garnish with fresh parsley and serve the succulent sheet pan chicken and veggies hot, straight from the oven.

Why This Recipe Works

The beauty of this sheet pan chicken and veggies recipe lies in its simplicity and the harmonious combination of flavors. The chicken breasts absorb the aromatic marinade, resulting in juicy, flavorful meat, while the vegetables caramelize and develop a delightful char, adding depth to the dish.

This recipe is not only a time-saver but also a great way to ensure you’re getting a healthy and balanced meal on the table. The variety of colorful vegetables provides an array of essential nutrients, making this dish as nutritious as it is delicious.

Customization Options

One of the best things about sheet pan meals is their versatility. You can easily customize this recipe to suit your preferences and dietary requirements. Consider experimenting with different vegetable combinations, such as zucchini, cherry tomatoes, or asparagus. Additionally, you can swap the chicken breasts for bone-in thighs or even salmon fillets for a delightful variation.

Cleanup Made Easy

As if the ease of preparation and the delectable outcome weren’t enough, the real beauty of sheet pan meals is the minimal cleanup involved. A single sheet pan means less time spent washing up, allowing you to savor your meal and spend more time enjoying the company of your loved ones.

So, the next time you’re in need of a quick and satisfying meal that ticks all the boxes, give this sheet pan chicken and veggies recipe a try. With its simplicity, versatility, and fantastic flavors, it’s sure to become a regular feature on your dining table. Enjoy!
