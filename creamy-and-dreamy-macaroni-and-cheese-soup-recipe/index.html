Creamy and Dreamy: Macaroni and Cheese Soup Recipe


Are you ready for a cozy and comforting dish that will warm you from the inside out? Look no further than this indulgent macaroni and cheese soup recipe. This delightful twist on the classic mac and cheese will have your taste buds singing with joy. Creamy, cheesy, and oh-so-satisfying, this soup is perfect for chilly evenings or whenever you need a little pick-me-up.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups elbow macaroni
  • 4 tablespoons unsalted butter
  • 1/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • 3 cups whole milk
  • 2 cups chicken or vegetable broth
  • 2 cups shredded sharp cheddar cheese
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon onion powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Optional toppings: crispy bacon bits, chopped scallions, extra shredded cheese

Instructions

  1. Cook the Macaroni: In a large pot of boiling salted water, cook the elbow macaroni according to the package instructions. Drain and set aside.

  2. Prepare the Soup Base: In the same pot, melt the butter over medium heat. Whisk in the flour and cook for about 1 minute, stirring constantly. Slowly pour in the milk and broth, whisking until smooth. Allow the mixture to simmer for 5 minutes, stirring occasionally.

  3. Add the Cheeses: Reduce the heat to low and gradually stir in the shredded cheddar and mozzarella cheeses until melted and creamy. Be patient and keep stirring to achieve a smooth consistency.

  4. Season and Incorporate: Stir in the garlic powder, onion powder, and season with salt and pepper to taste. Add the cooked macaroni to the pot and stir until well combined.

  5. Serve and Enjoy: Ladle the macaroni and cheese soup into bowls and garnish with your favorite toppings such as crispy bacon bits, chopped scallions, or extra shredded cheese. Serve hot and savor every creamy spoonful.

Tips and Variations

  • For an extra flavor boost, consider adding a dash of hot sauce or a pinch of cayenne pepper to the soup.
  • Experiment with different cheese combinations such as Gouda, gruyere, or fontina for a unique twist on the recipe.
  • To make the soup even heartier, mix in some cooked and crumbled Italian sausage or diced ham.

Why You’ll Love This Macaroni and Cheese Soup

This macaroni and cheese soup is the ultimate comfort food with a delightful creamy texture and rich, cheesy flavor. It’s a fantastic way to elevate the classic mac and cheese into a soul-soothing soup that will be a hit with the whole family. Whether you’re hosting a casual get-together or simply craving a bowl of warmth on a chilly day, this recipe is sure to become a favorite in your kitchen.

So, what are you waiting for? Gather your ingredients, bring out your trusty pot, and get ready to indulge in a bowl of creamy and dreamy macaroni and cheese soup that will leave you wanting more. Enjoy every spoonful and share the love with those around you!
