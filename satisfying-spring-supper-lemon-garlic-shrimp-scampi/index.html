Satisfying Spring Supper: Lemon Garlic Shrimp Scampi


Spring is the perfect time to indulge in light and refreshing dishes that celebrate the season’s fresh flavors. One dish that never fails to impress is the delightful Lemon Garlic Shrimp Scampi. This classic Italian-American dish is a true crowd-pleaser, featuring succulent shrimp, aromatic garlic, zesty lemon, and a hint of white wine. Whether you’re hosting a dinner party or simply craving a satisfying meal, this Lemon Garlic Shrimp Scampi recipe is sure to delight your taste buds.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound large shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 8 oz linguine or spaghetti
  • 4 tablespoons unsalted butter
  • 4 tablespoons olive oil
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/2 teaspoon red pepper flakes
  • 1/2 cup dry white wine
  • Zest of 1 lemon
  • Juice of 1 lemon
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • 1/4 cup fresh parsley, chopped
  • Grated Parmesan cheese for garnish

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Shrimp: Begin by patting the shrimp dry with paper towels and seasoning them with salt and black pepper.

  2. Cook the Pasta: Cook the linguine or spaghetti according to the package instructions until al dente. Drain and set aside.

  3. Sauté the Garlic: In a large skillet, heat the olive oil and butter over medium heat. Add the minced garlic and red pepper flakes, and sauté for about 1 minute until the garlic becomes fragrant.

  4. Cook the Shrimp: Increase the heat to medium-high and add the seasoned shrimp to the skillet. Cook for 2-3 minutes on each side until they turn pink and opaque. Be careful not to overcook the shrimp.

  5. Add the Wine and Lemon: Pour in the white wine and let it simmer for 2 minutes, allowing the alcohol to cook off. Add the lemon zest and lemon juice, then season with additional salt and pepper to taste.

  6. Combine the Pasta and Shrimp: Toss the cooked pasta into the skillet with the shrimp and sauce. Gently mix everything together until the pasta is well-coated with the flavorful sauce.

  7. Finish and Serve: Remove the skillet from the heat and sprinkle chopped parsley over the dish. Serve the Lemon Garlic Shrimp Scampi in individual plates, and garnish with grated Parmesan cheese for an extra burst of flavor.

Tips for Success

  • Fresh Ingredients: Use fresh, high-quality ingredients for the best flavor. Fresh shrimp and newly squeezed lemon juice will elevate the dish.

  • Don’t Overcook the Shrimp: Shrimp cook quickly, so be mindful not to overcook them, as this can result in a rubbery texture.

  • Adjust Seasoning: Taste the sauce before combining it with the pasta and adjust the seasoning as needed. The balance of flavors is key to a perfect Lemon Garlic Shrimp Scampi.

  • Presentation: Garnish the finished dish with a sprinkle of fresh parsley and grated Parmesan cheese for a beautiful presentation and added depth of flavor.

In Conclusion

Lemon Garlic Shrimp Scampi is a delightful dish that embodies the essence of spring with its bright, citrusy flavors and succulent shrimp. This recipe is perfect for a cozy dinner at home or a special gathering with loved ones. With its simple yet elegant preparation, it’s a wonderful way to welcome the season and savor the fresh, vibrant tastes of spring. So, gather your ingredients and prepare to delight your senses with this Satisfying Spring Supper!
