Dairy-Free Soup and Stew Recipes for a Cozy Night In


Nothing beats a cozy night in, curled up on the couch with a warm bowl of soup or stew. And if you’re following a dairy-free diet, you might think that your options are limited when it comes to creamy and flavorful recipes. But fear not! We’ve got you covered with these delicious dairy-free soup and stew recipes that are sure to satisfy your cravings and keep you warm all night long.

1. Creamy Butternut Squash Soup

Preparation time: 30 minutes
Cooking time: 45 minutes
Serves: 4

Ingredients:

  • 1 medium butternut squash, peeled, seeded, and cubed
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 2 carrots, peeled and chopped
  • 3 cups vegetable broth
  • 1 tsp dried thyme
  • 1 tsp ground cumin
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley for garnish

Instructions:

  1. In a large pot, heat some olive oil over medium heat. Add the onion, garlic, and carrots, and sauté until they become tender.
  2. Add the butternut squash cubes, vegetable broth, thyme, and cumin to the pot. Bring to a boil, then reduce the heat and let it simmer for about 30 minutes, or until the squash is soft and easily mashed.
  3. Using an immersion blender or a regular blender, puree the soup until smooth and creamy. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  4. Serve hot, garnished with fresh parsley.

2. Hearty Lentil Stew

Preparation time: 15 minutes
Cooking time: 1 hour
Serves: 6

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup dried green or brown lentils, rinsed
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 2 carrots, peeled and chopped
  • 2 celery stalks, chopped
  • 1 can diced tomatoes
  • 4 cups vegetable broth
  • 1 tsp ground cumin
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh cilantro for garnish

Instructions:

  1. In a large pot, heat some olive oil over medium heat. Add the onion, garlic, carrots, and celery, and sauté until they become tender.
  2. Add the lentils, diced tomatoes, vegetable broth, cumin, paprika, salt, and pepper to the pot. Bring to a boil, then reduce the heat and let it simmer for about 45 minutes to 1 hour, or until the lentils are cooked and the stew has thickened.
  3. Taste and adjust the seasonings if needed.
  4. Serve hot, garnished with fresh cilantro.

3. Chunky Vegetable Chowder

Preparation time: 20 minutes
Cooking time: 40 minutes
Serves: 4

Ingredients:

  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 2 carrots, peeled and chopped
  • 2 potatoes, peeled and cubed
  • 2 celery stalks, chopped
  • 2 cups vegetable broth
  • 1 cup unsweetened almond milk
  • 1 cup corn kernels (fresh or frozen)
  • 1 cup green beans, trimmed and chopped
  • 1 tsp dried thyme
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley for garnish

Instructions:

  1. In a large pot, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the onion, garlic, carrots, potatoes, and celery, and sauté until they become tender.
  2. Add the vegetable broth, almond milk, corn kernels, green beans, thyme, salt, and pepper to the pot. Bring to a boil, then reduce the heat and let it simmer for about 30 minutes, or until the vegetables are cooked through.
  3. Taste and adjust the seasonings if needed.
  4. Serve hot, garnished with fresh parsley.

Conclusion

These dairy-free soup and stew recipes are perfect for a cozy night in. Whether you’re in the mood for a creamy butternut squash soup, a hearty lentil stew, or a chunky vegetable chowder, these recipes will satisfy your cravings and warm you up from the inside out. So grab a bowl, snuggle up on the couch, and enjoy a delicious and dairy-free meal.
