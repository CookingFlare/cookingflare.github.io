Flavorful and Savory Herb Bread Recipes


Are you tired of plain and boring bread? Are you looking to add some excitement and flavor to your homemade loaves? Look no further! In this blog post, we will explore a variety of flavorful and savory herb bread recipes that will take your baking skills to the next level. From rosemary-infused focaccia to garlic and chive dinner rolls, these recipes are sure to impress your family and friends.

Rosemary Focaccia

Ingredients:

  • 4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 teaspoons instant yeast
  • 2 teaspoons salt
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons fresh rosemary, chopped
  • 1 teaspoon sea salt

Instructions:

  1. In a large mixing bowl, combine the flour, yeast, and salt.
  2. Add 2 cups of warm water and olive oil to the dry ingredients. Mix until a soft dough forms.
  3. Knead the dough on a lightly floured surface for about 5 minutes, or until it becomes smooth and elastic.
  4. Place the dough in a greased bowl and cover it with a clean kitchen towel. Let it rise in a warm place for about 1 hour, or until it doubles in size.
  5. Preheat your oven to 425°F (220°C).
  6. Punch down the dough and transfer it to a greased baking sheet.
  7. Gently press your fingers into the dough, creating small indentations.
  8. Drizzle olive oil over the top of the dough and sprinkle it with fresh rosemary and sea salt.
  9. Bake the focaccia for 20-25 minutes, or until it turns golden brown.
  10. Let it cool for a few minutes before slicing and serving.

Garlic and Chive Dinner Rolls

Ingredients:

  • 3 cups bread flour
  • 2 1/4 teaspoons instant yeast
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 2 tablespoons butter, melted
  • 1 cup warm milk
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/4 cup fresh chives, chopped

Instructions:

  1. In a large bowl, combine the bread flour, yeast, sugar, and salt.
  2. Add the melted butter, warm milk, minced garlic, and chopped chives to the dry ingredients. Mix until a soft dough forms.
  3. Knead the dough on a lightly floured surface for about 7-10 minutes, or until it becomes smooth and elastic.
  4. Place the dough in a greased bowl and cover it with a clean kitchen towel. Let it rise in a warm place for about 1 hour, or until it doubles in size.
  5. Punch down the dough and divide it into 12 equal pieces.
  6. Shape each piece into a small ball and place them in a greased baking dish.
  7. Cover the dish with a clean kitchen towel and let the rolls rise for another 30 minutes.
  8. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
  9. Bake the rolls for 15-20 minutes, or until they turn golden brown.
  10. Brush the tops of the rolls with melted butter and sprinkle them with additional minced garlic and chopped chives, if desired.

Sage and Parmesan Breadsticks

Ingredients:

  • 2 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon instant yeast
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 cup warm water
  • 2 tablespoons fresh sage, chopped
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese

Instructions:

  1. In a large mixing bowl, combine the flour, yeast, and salt.
  2. Add the olive oil and warm water to the dry ingredients. Mix until a soft dough forms.
  3. Knead the dough on a lightly floured surface for about 5 minutes, or until it becomes smooth and elastic.
  4. Place the dough in a greased bowl and cover it with a clean kitchen towel. Let it rise in a warm place for about 1 hour, or until it doubles in size.
  5. Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C).
  6. Punch down the dough and divide it into 12 equal pieces.
  7. Roll each piece into a thin rope and place them on a greased baking sheet.
  8. Sprinkle the chopped sage and grated Parmesan cheese over the breadsticks.
  9. Bake for 15-20 minutes, or until they turn golden brown.
  10. Allow the breadsticks to cool for a few minutes before serving.

Conclusion

These flavorful and savory herb bread recipes are perfect for any occasion. Whether you’re serving them as an appetizer, alongside a delicious soup, or as a side dish to a hearty main course, they are sure to delight your taste buds. So, put on your apron, gather your ingredients, and let’s get baking! Your homemade herb bread will have everyone asking for seconds. Enjoy!
