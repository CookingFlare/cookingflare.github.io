Roasted Mediterranean Cauliflower with Tahini Sauce


Getting creative with vegetables is a great way to stay healthy and experiment with new flavors. In this article, we will explore the delicious and nutritious Roasted Mediterranean Cauliflower with Tahini Sauce. This dish is a great alternative to traditional vegetables side dishes, offering a flavor-packed method to add more nutrients to your meals. What is Cauliflower? Cauliflower is a cruciferous vegetable that linguistically belongs to the cabbage family. It contains a wide variety of vitamins, including vitamin C, vitamin K, and folate. Cauliflower boasts antioxidant properties due to its rich content of various phytochemicals. Importance of Roasting Roasting brings out the best in vegetables. The Maillard reaction, a chemical reaction that occurs when food is cooked, converts the carbohydrates and amino acids into new compounds with unique flavors and aromas. Roasting enhances the texture, creating a tender yet crispy exterior that pairs perfectly with a variety of sauces. Roasted Mediterranean Cauliflower Recipe Preparation Time: 15 minutes Cooking Time: 25 minutes Servings: 4-6 Ingredients:

1 head of cauliflower 2 tablespoons olive oil 1/2 teaspoon overground cumin 1/4 teaspoon ground cumin 1/2 teaspoon paprika 1/4 cup chicken or vegetable broth Salt and pepper, to taste 2 cloves of garlic, minced 1/4 cup fresh parsley, chopped 2 tablespoons Tahini sauce (see below for preparation)

Instructions: Preheat the oven to 425°F (220°C).

Rinse the cauliflower and divide it into florets. Place it on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper in a single layer. Brush the cauliflower with olive oil and sprinkle cumin, paprika, salt, and pepper evenly over the florets. Roast in the oven for 15 minutes or until slightly tender. While the cauliflower is roasting, mix the chicken or vegetable broth, garlic, and parsley in a small bowl. Remove the cauliflower from the oven and pour the broth mixture over the florets. Toss to glaze. Return the cauliflower to the oven and roast for another 10 minutes, or until the florets are tender and slightly caramelized. Remove from the oven and let it cool slightly.

Tahini Sauce Preparation

1/4 cup tahini

1/4 cup water

2 cloves of garlic, minced

2 tablespoons fresh parsley, chopped

1 tablespoon lemon juice

Salt and pepper to taste Combine the tahini, water, garlic, parsley, and lemon juice in a blender. Blend until smooth, adding more water if necessary to achieve desired consistency. Season with salt and pepper to taste.

Serving and Garnishing Spoon the roasted cauliflower onto a serving platter or individual plates. Drizzle the tahini sauce over the cauliflower. Garnish with fresh parsley and serve immediately.

Tips and Variations For added crunch, sprinkle some roasted almonds or pistachios over the dish. For an extra kick, add a pinch of red pepper flakes to the cumin mixture. This dish can be served as a side, light lunch, or main course, depending on your preference. For a vegan version, use vegetable broth instead of chicken broth. Try substituting lemon juice with lime or orange juice for a flavorful twist. Conclusion Roasted Mediterranean Cauliflower with Tahini Sauce is a flavorful and nutritious dish that packs a punch of health benefits. Cauliflower, rich in vitamins and antioxidants, pairs well with the creamy and savory tahini sauce. This recipe is an excellent choice for maintaining a balanced diet and exploring new flavors.
