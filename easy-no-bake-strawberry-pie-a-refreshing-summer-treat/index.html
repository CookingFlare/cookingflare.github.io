Easy No-Bake Strawberry Pie: A Refreshing Summer Treat


As the heat of summer rolls in, there’s nothing quite like a refreshing and delightful dessert to cool you down. This easy no-bake strawberry pie is the perfect answer to those warm weather cravings. With a buttery graham cracker crust and a luscious strawberry filling, this pie is a celebration of the sweet, juicy flavors of summer. The best part? It requires no baking, making it an ideal treat for those lazy, hazy days when you’d rather not turn on the oven.

Ingredients:

For the crust:

  • 1 ½ cups graham cracker crumbs
  • ⅓ cup granulated sugar
  • ½ cup unsalted butter, melted

For the filling:

  • 1 pound fresh strawberries, hulled and sliced
  • ¾ cup granulated sugar
  • 2 tablespoons cornstarch
  • ½ cup water
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 8 oz cream cheese, softened
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 cup heavy cream, whipped

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the Crust:

    • In a medium bowl, combine the graham cracker crumbs, sugar, and melted butter. Stir until the crumbs are evenly moistened.
    • Press the mixture into the bottom and up the sides of a 9-inch pie dish. Use the back of a spoon or a flat-bottomed glass to press the crust firmly into place.
    • Place the crust in the refrigerator to chill while you prepare the filling.

  2. Make the Filling:

    • In a small bowl, mash half of the sliced strawberries with a fork or potato masher. Set aside.
    • In a saucepan, combine the sugar, cornstarch, water, and lemon juice. Cook over medium heat, stirring constantly until the mixture thickens and becomes clear.
    • Remove the saucepan from the heat and add the mashed strawberries. Stir until well combined. Allow the mixture to cool to room temperature.

  3. Prepare the Cream Cheese Filling:

    • In a mixing bowl, beat the softened cream cheese and vanilla extract until smooth and creamy.
    • Gently fold in the whipped cream until well combined.

  4. Assemble the Pie:

    • Spread the cream cheese filling evenly over the chilled graham cracker crust.
    • Arrange the remaining fresh strawberry slices on top of the cream cheese filling.
    • Pour the cooled strawberry glaze over the strawberries, ensuring they are evenly coated.

  5. Chill and Serve:

    • Refrigerate the pie for at least 4 hours, or until set.
    • Slice and serve the chilled no-bake strawberry pie, garnishing with additional whipped cream and fresh strawberries if desired.

Tips and Variations:

  • For a lighter version, you can use a pre-made graham cracker crust from the store.
  • Feel free to experiment with different berries or a combination of berries for the filling.
  • Add a sprinkle of lemon zest over the top of the pie for an extra pop of citrus flavor.

This easy no-bake strawberry pie is a delightful way to make the most of the summer season’s bounty of fresh strawberries. It’s a fuss-free dessert that’s sure to impress your friends and family at any summer gathering. So, go ahead, whip up this delightful pie, and savor every sweet and tangy bite of summer!

Enjoy the flavors of the season with this simple and delicious no-bake strawberry pie – the perfect addition to your summer dessert repertoire.
