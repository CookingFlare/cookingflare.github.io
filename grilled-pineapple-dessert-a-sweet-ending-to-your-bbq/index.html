Grilled Pineapple Dessert: A Sweet Ending to Your BBQ


There’s nothing quite like a delightful dessert to round off an evening of barbecuing and delicious summertime dining. And what could be more refreshing than a sweet grilled pineapple dessert? Made with just three ingredients, this delightful treat is sure to impress your guests and satisfy their sweet tooth cravings. It’s a unique, flavorful, and versatile ending to any barbecue meal, and the process is straightforward and easy to follow.

Ingredients:

  • 2 ripe pineapples
  • 2 tablespoons brown sugar
  • 2 tablespoons melted butter

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your grill to medium heat, around 350°F (175°C). Cut your pineapples into quarter-slices, removing the core in the process.

  2. In a small bowl, mix together the melted butter and brown sugar until well combined.

  3. Use a pastry brush to spread the butter-sugar mixture evenly on both sides of the pineapple slices.

  4. Place the marinated pineapple slices on the preheated grill. Cook for about 5 to 6 minutes per side, depending on your preferred level of caramelization and doneness.

  5. As you are grilling the pineapples, they will start to caramelize and develop beautiful grill marks. Keep an eye on them and make sure not to overcook or burn the fruit.

  6. Once nicely grilled, remove the pineapples from the heat and let them cool slightly on a serving platter, allowing the contrasting flavors of the hot caramelized exterior to meld with the cool inner fruit.

  7. Serve your grilled pineapple dessert on its own or with a scoop of your favorite ice cream or frozen dessert. You can also garnish with mint leaves for a refreshing contrast in flavors and a pop of color on the plate.

This grilled pineapple dessert can be easily customized and adapted according to your personal taste preferences. For example, you could add a drizzle of dark rum or a pinch of cinnamon to the butter-sugar mixture for an extra touch of flavor.

If you’re the type of cook who likes to make use of your outdoor grill year-round, this dessert is an excellent option for wintertime barbecues or indoor-smoked meals. The process is effortless, and you’ll impress your friends and family with this unique and spectacular grilled pineapple dessert. It’ll become your go-to dessert recipe in no time!

From everyone at the BBQ gathering back to the kitchen, the beautified plates of grilled pineapple dessert creates a spectacular view. This simple yet satisfying treat brings the perfect ending to a barbecue meal, leaving you with a peaceful, full belly as the sun sets on your memorable backyard gathering.

Keep experimenting with your BBQ techniques and explore more fruits to try in this delightful fashion! There are several fruits suitable for grilling, including peaches and apples, which also complement the savory grilled flavors of your barbecue meal. Incorporating different desserts like these into your barbecuing routine is guaranteed to elevate your culinary expertise and entertain scores with satisfaction.
