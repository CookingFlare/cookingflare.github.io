Banana Cupcakes with Cream Cheese Frosting: A Moist and Delightful Treat

3 min · 538 words

There’s something incredibly comforting about the combination of ripe bananas and cream cheese frosting. The sweet, slightly tangy flavor of the frosting paired with the moist, tender crumb of banana cupcakes is a match made in dessert heaven. Whether you’re a baking enthusiast or a novice in the kitchen, this recipe for Banana Cupcakes with Cream Cheese Frosting is sure to become a favorite in your repertoire.

Ingredients

For the Banana Cupcakes:

  • 3 ripe bananas, mashed
  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 3/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/4 cup buttermilk

For the Cream Cheese Frosting:

  • 8 oz cream cheese, softened
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 3-4 cups powdered sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C) and line a muffin tin with paper liners.

  2. In a medium bowl, whisk together the flour, baking powder, baking soda, and salt.

  3. In a separate large bowl, cream together the softened butter and granulated sugar until light and fluffy. Add the eggs, one at a time, beating well after each addition. Stir in the mashed bananas and vanilla extract.

  4. Gradually add the dry ingredients to the banana mixture, alternating with the buttermilk, beginning and ending with the dry ingredients.

  5. Divide the batter evenly among the prepared muffin cups, filling each about two-thirds full.

  6. Bake for 20-25 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center of a cupcake comes out clean.

  7. Allow the cupcakes to cool in the pan for 5 minutes, then transfer them to a wire rack to cool completely.

  8. While the cupcakes are cooling, prepare the cream cheese frosting. In a large bowl, beat together the softened cream cheese and butter until smooth. Gradually add the powdered sugar, 1 cup at a time, until the frosting reaches your desired sweetness and consistency. Beat in the vanilla extract.

  9. Once the cupcakes are completely cool, generously frost them with the cream cheese frosting.

Tips for Success

  • Use ripe bananas for the best flavor. The bananas should be heavily speckled with brown spots, indicating that they are at their sweetest and most flavorful.

  • Be sure to properly measure your flour using the spoon and level method to avoid dense cupcakes.

  • For an extra touch of indulgence, sprinkle some chopped nuts or a dash of cinnamon on top of the cream cheese frosting.

Whether you’re making these banana cupcakes for a special occasion or simply to satisfy a sweet craving, they are sure to be a hit. The natural sweetness of the bananas combined with the richness of the cream cheese frosting creates a delightful balance of flavors that will keep you coming back for more. Enjoy these moist and delightful treats with a cup of coffee or as a sweet ending to any meal. Happy baking!
