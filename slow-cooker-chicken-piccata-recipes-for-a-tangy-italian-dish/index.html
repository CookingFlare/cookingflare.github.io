Slow Cooker Chicken Piccata Recipes for a Tangy Italian Dish


Are you craving a taste of Italy but don’t have hours to spend in the kitchen? Look no further than this slow cooker chicken piccata recipe! Bursting with zesty flavors and tender chicken, this dish will transport your taste buds to the sunny shores of the Amalfi Coast. Whether you’re a seasoned chef or a novice in the kitchen, this recipe is sure to impress and delight. Let’s dive into the tantalizing world of slow cooker chicken piccata!

Ingredients:

  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1/2 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1/2 cup chicken broth
  • 1/4 cup fresh lemon juice
  • 1/4 cup capers, drained
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/2 cup dry white wine
  • 1/4 cup unsalted butter, cut into pieces
  • Sliced lemon for garnish
  • Cooked pasta or rice for serving

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the Chicken: In a shallow dish, combine the flour, salt, and black pepper. Dredge the chicken breasts in the flour mixture, shaking off any excess.

  2. Brown the Chicken: In a large skillet over medium-high heat, warm the olive oil. Add the chicken breasts and cook until golden brown on both sides, about 3-4 minutes per side. Transfer the chicken to the slow cooker.

  3. Create the Sauce: In the same skillet, add the chicken broth, lemon juice, capers, parsley, garlic, and white wine. Bring the mixture to a simmer, scraping up any browned bits from the bottom of the pan.

  4. Slow Cook: Pour the sauce over the chicken in the slow cooker. Cover and cook on low for 4-5 hours, or until the chicken is tender and cooked through.

  5. Finish with Butter: Once the chicken is cooked, stir in the pieces of butter until melted and the sauce is smooth and glossy.

  6. Serve and Enjoy: Serve the chicken piccata over cooked pasta or rice, spooning the luscious sauce over the top. Garnish with sliced lemon and additional chopped parsley, if desired.

Tips for Success:

  • For a lighter version, you can use boneless, skinless chicken thighs instead of breasts.
  • If you prefer a thicker sauce, you can mix 1 tablespoon of cornstarch with 2 tablespoons of cold water and stir it into the slow cooker during the last 30 minutes of cooking.
  • Freshly squeezed lemon juice will give the dish a vibrant, tangy flavor that is unmatched.

Why Slow Cooker Chicken Piccata?

The slow cooker method infuses the chicken with the flavors of the zesty sauce, resulting in tender, succulent meat that practically melts in your mouth. The convenience of the slow cooker allows you to set it and forget it, making it perfect for busy weeknights or lazy weekends. Plus, the tantalizing aroma that fills your home as it cooks will have everyone eagerly awaiting dinnertime.

Conclusion:

With its bright, tangy flavors and effortless preparation, slow cooker chicken piccata is a must-try Italian dish for any home cook. Elevate your culinary repertoire with this simple yet sophisticated recipe that is sure to become a family favorite. So, grab your slow cooker and get ready to savor the irresistible flavors of Italy right in your own kitchen! Buon appetito!
