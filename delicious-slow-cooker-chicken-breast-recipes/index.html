Delicious Slow Cooker Chicken Breast Recipes


If you’re looking for easy, flavorful, and tender chicken breast recipes, then look no further than your slow cooker. Slow cooking chicken breast not only ensures that the meat stays juicy and tender, but it also allows the flavors to meld together beautifully. Whether you’re a busy parent, a working professional, or just someone who loves a hassle-free cooking experience, these slow cooker chicken breast recipes are sure to become staples in your kitchen.

1. Slow Cooker Lemon Garlic Chicken

Ingredients:

  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • Juice of 2 lemons
  • 1/2 cup chicken broth
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley

Instructions:

  1. Place the chicken breasts in the slow cooker.
  2. In a bowl, mix together the minced garlic, oregano, thyme, salt, pepper, lemon juice, chicken broth, and olive oil.
  3. Pour the mixture over the chicken breasts.
  4. Cover and cook on low for 6-7 hours or on high for 3-4 hours.
  5. Once cooked, sprinkle fresh parsley over the chicken before serving.

2. Slow Cooker Honey Sesame Chicken

Ingredients:

  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1/2 cup honey
  • 1/2 cup low-sodium soy sauce
  • 1/4 cup ketchup
  • 2 tablespoons sesame oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon cornstarch
  • 2 tablespoons water
  • Sesame seeds and sliced green onions for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Place the chicken breasts in the slow cooker.
  2. In a bowl, mix together the honey, soy sauce, ketchup, sesame oil, and minced garlic.
  3. Pour the mixture over the chicken breasts.
  4. Cover and cook on low for 5-6 hours or on high for 2-3 hours.
  5. In a small bowl, mix the cornstarch and water to create a slurry. Stir the slurry into the slow cooker and cook for an additional 30 minutes to thicken the sauce.
  6. Garnish with sesame seeds and sliced green onions before serving.

3. Slow Cooker Creamy Mushroom Chicken

Ingredients:

  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1 cup sliced mushrooms
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • 1 teaspoon dried rosemary
  • 1 cup chicken broth
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Chopped fresh parsley for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Place the chicken breasts in the slow cooker.
  2. Add the sliced mushrooms, chopped onion, minced garlic, thyme, rosemary, and chicken broth.
  3. Cover and cook on low for 6-7 hours or on high for 3-4 hours.
  4. In a small bowl, whisk together the heavy cream and flour. Stir the mixture into the slow cooker and cook for an additional 30 minutes to thicken the sauce.
  5. Season with salt and pepper to taste and garnish with chopped fresh parsley before serving.

Conclusion

These delicious slow cooker chicken breast recipes are not only convenient but also incredibly versatile. Whether you’re craving something zesty, sweet and savory, or rich and creamy, there’s a slow cooker chicken breast recipe here for you. Embrace the simplicity and deliciousness of slow cooking, and treat yourself to these mouthwatering chicken dishes. Enjoy!
