Pappardelle with Wild Mushroom Sauce: A Rustic and Earthy Dish


If you’re looking for a hearty and comforting pasta dish that’s full of flavor, then you’ve come to the right place. Today, we’re going to talk about Pappardelle with Wild Mushroom Sauce. This rustic and earthy dish is perfect for those cozy nights when you want something warm and satisfying.

Ingredients

To make this delicious dish, you’ll need the following ingredients:

  • 8 ounces of pappardelle pasta
  • 1 pound of mixed wild mushrooms (such as cremini, shiitake, and oyster), sliced
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 shallot, finely chopped
  • 2 tablespoons of butter
  • 1 cup of heavy cream
  • 1/2 cup of grated Parmesan cheese
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley, chopped (for garnish)

Instructions

  1. Start by cooking the pappardelle pasta according to the package instructions. Make sure to cook it al dente, as it will continue to cook in the sauce later on. Once cooked, drain and set aside.

  2. In a large skillet, melt the butter over medium heat. Add the minced garlic and chopped shallot, and sauté until fragrant and translucent.

  3. Next, add the sliced wild mushrooms to the skillet. Cook them until they start to release their moisture and become golden brown. This process will take about 8-10 minutes. Season with salt and pepper to taste.

  4. Reduce the heat to low and pour in the heavy cream. Stir well to combine the cream with the mushrooms, garlic, and shallots. Let the mixture simmer for about 5 minutes, allowing the flavors to meld together.

  5. Add the cooked pappardelle pasta to the skillet and toss gently to coat it evenly with the mushroom sauce. Cook for an additional 2-3 minutes to ensure that the pasta is fully heated through.

  6. Remove the skillet from the heat and sprinkle the grated Parmesan cheese over the pasta. Stir until the cheese is melted and forms a creamy sauce that coats the pasta.

  7. Serve the Pappardelle with Wild Mushroom Sauce in bowls, garnished with fresh parsley. The earthy flavors of the mushrooms combined with the rich creaminess of the sauce make this dish truly irresistible.

Tips and Variations

  • If you’re feeling adventurous, you can experiment with different types of mushrooms to add depth and complexity to the dish. Porcini mushrooms, in particular, have a robust and nutty flavor that pairs well with the creamy sauce.

  • For a touch of freshness, you can squeeze a bit of lemon juice over the finished dish. The acidity will help cut through the richness of the sauce and enhance the flavors.

  • If you prefer a vegetarian version, you can substitute vegetable broth for the chicken broth. This will still give the sauce a savory depth of flavor without the use of meat.

  • To make the dish even heartier, you can add some cooked and crumbled Italian sausage or crispy bacon to the sauce. This will add a smoky and savory element that complements the earthiness of the mushrooms.

Final Thoughts

Pappardelle with Wild Mushroom Sauce is a dish that embodies comfort and warmth. Its rustic flavors and creamy texture make it a perfect choice for a cozy dinner at home. Whether you’re a mushroom lover or just looking to try something new, this recipe is sure to delight your taste buds. So, gather your ingredients and get ready to enjoy a delicious and satisfying meal. Bon appétit!
