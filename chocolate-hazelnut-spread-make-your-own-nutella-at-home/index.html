Chocolate Hazelnut Spread: Make Your Own Nutella at Home


Who doesn’t love the creamy, chocolaty goodness of Nutella? This indulgent chocolate hazelnut spread has become a breakfast staple for many around the world. But did you know that you can make your own Nutella at home? Not only is it easy and fun, but it also allows you to customize the flavors and ingredients to suit your taste. In this recipe, I will guide you through the process of creating your very own homemade Nutella.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups of roasted hazelnuts
  • 1 cup of powdered sugar
  • 1/2 cup of unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1/4 cup of vegetable oil
  • 1 teaspoon of vanilla extract
  • 1/2 teaspoon of salt

Instructions

  1. Start by preheating your oven to 350°F (175°C). Spread the hazelnuts evenly on a baking sheet and roast them for about 10-12 minutes, or until fragrant and golden brown. Let them cool completely before proceeding.

  2. Once the hazelnuts have cooled, transfer them to a food processor or high-powered blender. Pulse them for a few minutes until they are finely ground and start to release their natural oils. You should have a smooth hazelnut butter consistency.

  3. Next, add the powdered sugar, cocoa powder, vegetable oil, vanilla extract, and salt to the hazelnut butter. Blend everything together until well combined and smooth. You may need to scrape down the sides of the processor or blender a few times to ensure everything is mixed evenly.

  4. Taste the mixture and adjust the sweetness and flavor according to your preference. You can add more powdered sugar for a sweeter spread or more cocoa powder for a richer chocolate taste.

  5. Once you are satisfied with the flavor, transfer the homemade Nutella to a clean, airtight container. It can be stored at room temperature for up to two weeks, or you can refrigerate it to extend its shelf life.

  6. Now comes the best part – enjoying your homemade Nutella! Spread it on toast, pancakes, waffles, or use it as a dip for fruits. Get creative and use it in your favorite baked goods or even as a filling for homemade pastries.

Tips and Variations

  • For an extra smooth and creamy texture, you can strain the hazelnut butter after grinding the roasted hazelnuts. This step is optional but will result in a more refined spread.

  • If you want to enhance the hazelnut flavor, you can add a few drops of hazelnut extract to the mixture. This will give it an extra nutty kick.

  • To make a vegan version of homemade Nutella, simply substitute the powdered sugar with a vegan-friendly alternative like coconut sugar or maple syrup.

  • Experiment with different types of nuts to create your own unique spreads. Almonds, cashews, or even a mix of nuts can be used instead of hazelnuts.

  • Feel free to add other flavorings like cinnamon, espresso powder, or even a pinch of chili powder for a spicy twist.

Making your own Nutella at home allows you to control the quality of ingredients and customize the flavors to your liking. Plus, it’s a fun and rewarding experience that will impress your family and friends. So why not give it a try? Whip up a batch of homemade Nutella and indulge in the irresistible combination of chocolate and hazelnuts.
