Chili Relleno Burritos: A Cheesy and Spicy Delight


If you’re a fan of Mexican cuisine, you’re in for a treat with this delectable recipe for Chili Relleno Burritos. This dish combines the rich, smoky flavors of roasted poblano peppers, the creaminess of melted cheese, and the satisfying heartiness of a burrito. It’s a perfect fusion of flavors and textures that will leave your taste buds dancing with delight. Whether you’re hosting a dinner party or simply craving a comforting meal, these Chili Relleno Burritos are sure to impress.

Ingredients

Before we dive into the cooking process, let’s take a look at the ingredients you’ll need to prepare these mouthwatering burritos:

  • 4 large poblano peppers
  • 2 cups of shredded Monterey Jack cheese
  • 1 cup of crumbled queso fresco
  • 1 cup of cooked rice
  • 1 can of black beans, drained and rinsed
  • 1 cup of corn kernels (fresh or frozen)
  • 1 teaspoon of ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon of smoked paprika
  • 1 teaspoon of garlic powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 4 large flour tortillas
  • Salsa and sour cream for serving

Instructions

Step 1: Roast the Poblano Peppers

The first step in preparing Chili Relleno Burritos is to roast the poblano peppers. You can do this by placing the peppers directly over a gas flame or under a broiler. Allow the skin to char and blister, turning the peppers occasionally to ensure even roasting. Once the peppers are charred all over, transfer them to a bowl and cover with plastic wrap. Let them steam for about 10 minutes, then peel off the skin, remove the seeds, and cut the peppers into strips.

Step 2: Prepare the Filling

In a large mixing bowl, combine the shredded Monterey Jack cheese, crumbled queso fresco, cooked rice, black beans, corn kernels, ground cumin, smoked paprika, garlic powder, and salt and pepper to taste. Mix everything together until well combined.

Step 3: Assemble the Burritos

Lay out the flour tortillas on a clean surface, then divide the filling equally among the tortillas. Place a portion of the roasted poblano strips on top of the filling. Fold in the sides of the tortillas, then roll them up tightly to form burritos.

Step 4: Cook the Burritos

Heat a large skillet over medium heat and lightly grease it with cooking spray or oil. Place the burritos seam-side down in the skillet and cook for 3-4 minutes on each side, or until they are golden brown and the cheese is melted.

Step 5: Serve and Enjoy

Once the burritos are cooked, remove them from the skillet and place them on a serving platter. You can slice them in half for a beautiful presentation. Serve the Chili Relleno Burritos with your favorite salsa and a dollop of sour cream on the side.

Final Thoughts

Chili Relleno Burritos are a delightful combination of flavors and textures that make for a satisfying and indulgent meal. The smokiness of the roasted poblanos, the creaminess of the melted cheese, and the earthy goodness of the black beans and rice all come together to create a truly memorable dining experience. Whether you’re a fan of spicy food or simply enjoy exploring new flavors, these burritos are guaranteed to become a favorite in your recipe collection. So, gather your ingredients, roll up your sleeves, and treat yourself to the cheesy and spicy delight that is Chili Relleno Burritos!
