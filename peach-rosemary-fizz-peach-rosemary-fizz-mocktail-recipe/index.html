Peach Rosemary Fizz: Peach Rosemary Fizz Mocktail Recipe


Looking for a refreshing and elegant drink to elevate your next gathering? This Peach Rosemary Fizz mocktail is the perfect blend of sweet, herbaceous, and bubbly flavors that will delight your taste buds and impress your guests. Whether you’re hosting a summer brunch, a bridal shower, or simply craving a sophisticated non-alcoholic beverage, this mocktail is sure to be a hit. Let’s dive into the recipe and learn how to craft this delightful Peach Rosemary Fizz.

Ingredients

  • 2 ripe peaches, peeled and diced
  • 1/4 cup of granulated sugar
  • 1/4 cup of water
  • 4-5 fresh rosemary sprigs
  • 1 cup of sparkling water
  • 1/4 cup of fresh lemon juice
  • Ice cubes
  • Fresh rosemary sprigs for garnish
  • Sliced peaches for garnish

Instructions

Step 1: Prepare the Peach Rosemary Syrup

  1. In a small saucepan, combine the diced peaches, granulated sugar, water, and 2-3 rosemary sprigs.
  2. Bring the mixture to a gentle boil over medium heat, stirring occasionally.
  3. Once the sugar has dissolved, reduce the heat and let the mixture simmer for 10-15 minutes, allowing the peaches to soften and the flavors to meld.
  4. Remove the saucepan from the heat and let the syrup cool to room temperature.
  5. Once cooled, strain the syrup through a fine-mesh sieve, pressing the peaches to extract all the flavorful liquid. Discard the solids and reserve the peach rosemary syrup.

Step 2: Assemble the Mocktail

  1. In a large pitcher, combine the peach rosemary syrup, sparkling water, and fresh lemon juice.
  2. Stir the mixture gently to ensure the flavors are well combined.
  3. Add ice cubes to serving glasses and pour the mocktail over the ice, dividing it evenly among the glasses.

Step 3: Garnish and Serve

  1. Garnish each glass with a sprig of fresh rosemary and a slice of peach for a touch of elegance.
  2. Serve the Peach Rosemary Fizz immediately and enjoy the delightful combination of peach, rosemary, and effervescence.

Tips and Variations

  • For a stronger rosemary flavor, you can infuse the syrup with additional rosemary sprigs and let it steep for a longer period.
  • Adjust the sweetness of the mocktail by adding more or less peach rosemary syrup to suit your taste preferences.
  • To make a larger batch for a party, simply double or triple the recipe and prepare the mocktail in a punch bowl for easy serving.

Final Thoughts

The Peach Rosemary Fizz mocktail is a wonderful way to celebrate the flavors of the season and treat yourself and your guests to a sophisticated and alcohol-free beverage. The combination of juicy peaches, fragrant rosemary, and the effervescence of sparkling water creates a drink that is both visually stunning and deliciously satisfying. So, whether you’re sipping this mocktail on a warm afternoon or serving it at your next soiree, it’s sure to be a crowd-pleaser. Cheers to the art of mocktail mixology and the joy of savoring every sip!

Give this Peach Rosemary Fizz mocktail recipe a try and let its delightful flavors become a highlight of your next gathering. Cheers to good company and refreshing libations!
