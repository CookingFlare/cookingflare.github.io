Quick and Easy Margherita Chicken Skillet Recipe


If you’re looking for a delicious and hassle-free dinner idea, look no further than this quick and easy Margherita Chicken Skillet recipe. This dish combines the classic flavors of a Margherita pizza with tender, juicy chicken, creating a mouthwatering meal that’s perfect for any night of the week. Plus, it’s ready in under 30 minutes, making it ideal for busy weeknights or lazy weekends.

Ingredients

To start off, gather the following ingredients:

  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 cups cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/4 teaspoon red pepper flakes
  • 8 ounces fresh mozzarella, sliced
  • Fresh basil leaves for garnish

Instructions

  1. Season the Chicken: Begin by seasoning the chicken breasts with dried oregano, salt, and black pepper. This will infuse the chicken with the aromatic flavors of the herbs and spices.

  2. Sear the Chicken: In a large skillet, heat the olive oil over medium-high heat. Once the oil is hot, add the seasoned chicken breasts to the skillet and sear them for about 6-7 minutes on each side, or until they are golden brown and fully cooked through. Remove the chicken from the skillet and set it aside.

  3. Prepare the Tomato Mixture: In the same skillet, add the halved cherry tomatoes, minced garlic, and red pepper flakes. Sauté the mixture for 3-4 minutes, or until the tomatoes start to soften and release their juices.

  4. Assemble the Dish: Return the seared chicken breasts to the skillet, nestling them among the tomato mixture. Top each chicken breast with slices of fresh mozzarella.

  5. Broil the Chicken: Preheat the broiler in your oven. Once hot, place the skillet under the broiler and cook for 2-3 minutes, or until the mozzarella is melted and bubbly.

  6. Garnish and Serve: Remove the skillet from the oven and garnish the dish with fresh basil leaves. This adds a pop of color and a hint of freshness to the dish. Serve the Margherita Chicken Skillet hot, with your choice of sides.

Why You’ll Love This Recipe

This Margherita Chicken Skillet recipe is a fantastic way to elevate a simple chicken dinner into something truly special. The combination of juicy chicken, sweet cherry tomatoes, gooey melted mozzarella, and aromatic basil creates a flavor profile that’s reminiscent of a classic Margherita pizza, but with a protein-packed twist. Plus, the entire dish comes together in just one skillet, minimizing the cleanup and making it a convenient option for busy days.

Final Thoughts

Whether you’re cooking for yourself, your family, or entertaining guests, this Margherita Chicken Skillet recipe is sure to impress. It’s a quick and easy dish that doesn’t compromise on flavor, and it’s a great way to add a touch of Italian-inspired cuisine to your weekly menu. So, next time you’re in need of a simple yet satisfying dinner idea, give this recipe a try and savor the delightful flavors of a Margherita pizza in the form of a delicious chicken skillet meal. Enjoy!
