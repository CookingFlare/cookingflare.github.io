10 Cookie Recipes That Are Perfect for Sharing


When it comes to treats that bring people together, few things measure up to the universal appeal of cookies. Whether you’re baking for a family gathering, contributing to a potluck, or simply planning to surprise your friends, cookies are a surefire way to spread warmth and joy. Below, you’ll find ten cookie recipes that are as delightful to make as they are to share. From classics to creative twists, these recipes are guaranteed to impress every crowd.

1. Chewy Chocolate Chip Cookies

No cookie list is complete without the ultimate classic: chocolate chip cookies. This recipe creates soft and chewy cookies with gooey pockets of melted chocolate and golden edges for balance. Perfect for sharing at gatherings or surprising your coworkers on a Friday.

Ingredients:

  • 2 ¼ cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • ½ tsp salt
  • 1 cup unsalted butter, melted
  • 1 cup brown sugar
  • ½ cup granulated sugar
  • 2 tsp pure vanilla extract
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 cups semi-sweet chocolate chips

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C). Line baking sheets with parchment paper.
  2. In a medium-sized bowl, mix flour, baking soda, and salt. Set aside.
  3. Combine melted butter, brown sugar, and granulated sugar in a large bowl. Add vanilla and eggs, beating well.
  4. Slowly mix dry ingredients into the wet mixture until just combined. Stir in chocolate chips.
  5. Scoop dough onto prepared sheets and bake for 10–12 minutes, until the edges are golden. Cool before sharing.

2. Peanut Butter Blossoms

Soft and sweet peanut butter cookies topped with a chocolate kiss are the ultimate treat for peanut butter lovers. These cookies are perfect for sharing during holidays or adding to care packages.

Ingredients:

  • 1 ¾ cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • ½ tsp salt
  • ½ cup unsalted butter, softened
  • ½ cup granulated sugar
  • ½ cup packed brown sugar
  • ½ cup creamy peanut butter
  • 1 egg
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • Hershey’s chocolate kisses

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C). Line baking sheets with parchment paper.
  2. Whisk together flour, baking soda, and salt in a bowl. Set aside.
  3. Cream butter, sugar, and brown sugar in another bowl. Add peanut butter, egg, and vanilla, mixing until smooth.
  4. Gradually add dry ingredients to wet ingredients, stirring until combined.
  5. Roll the dough into balls and bake for 8–10 minutes. Remove from oven, immediately press a chocolate kiss into each cookie, and cool.

3. Snickerdoodles

With their tangy cinnamon-sugar coating, snickerdoodles are as cozy as they are crowd-pleasing. These cookies are perfect for sharing over coffee or packaging in decorative tins for gifts.

Ingredients:

  • 2 ¾ cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 tsp cream of tartar
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • ¼ tsp salt
  • 1 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1 ½ cups granulated sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • ¼ cup granulated sugar mixed with 2 tsp cinnamon

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C). Line baking sheets with parchment paper.
  2. Combine flour, cream of tartar, baking soda, and salt in a bowl. Set aside.
  3. Cream butter and sugar until fluffy. Add eggs and vanilla, mixing well.
  4. Gradually incorporate dry ingredients into the wet mixture.
  5. Roll dough into balls, coat in cinnamon sugar, and bake for 10–12 minutes. Cool on wire racks.

4. Oatmeal Raisin Cookies

For a sweet yet wholesome treat, oatmeal raisin cookies offer the perfect balance of chewy oats and juicy raisins. These are sure to be a crowd favorite at brunch gatherings or casual picnics.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1 cup packed brown sugar
  • ½ cup granulated sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 ½ cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1 tsp ground cinnamon
  • ½ tsp salt
  • 3 cups rolled oats
  • 1 cup raisins

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C). Line baking sheets with parchment paper.
  2. Cream together butter, brown sugar, and granulated sugar. Add eggs and vanilla, mixing well.
  3. In another bowl, combine flour, baking soda, cinnamon, and salt. Gradually mix this into the wet ingredients.
  4. Stir in oats and raisins. Scoop dough onto baking sheets and bake for 12–15 minutes. Cool thoroughly before sharing.

5. Double Chocolate Cookies

Chocolate lovers rejoice! These rich and indulgently chewy cookies combine cocoa powder and chocolate chips for the ultimate dessert experience.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1 cup brown sugar
  • ½ cup granulated sugar
  • 1 large egg
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • ½ cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • ¼ tsp salt
  • 1 ½ cups semi-sweet chocolate chips

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C). Line baking sheets with parchment paper.
  2. Cream butter and sugars until fluffy. Add egg and vanilla, mixing thoroughly.
  3. In a separate bowl, combine the flour, cocoa powder, baking soda, and salt. Gradually mix this into the wet ingredients.
  4. Fold in chocolate chips. Drop spoonfuls of dough onto the baking sheets.
  5. Bake for 9–11 minutes, cool, and share the chocolate joy.

6. Lemon Sugar Cookies

Bright and zesty lemon sugar cookies offer a refreshing twist compared to other traditional cookies. These citrus-infused delights are ideal for tea parties or sunny afternoon picnics.

Ingredients:

  • 2 ½ cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • ½ tsp salt
  • 1 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1 ½ cups granulated sugar
  • 1 egg
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • 1 tbsp lemon zest

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C). Line baking sheets with parchment paper.
  2. Mix flour, baking powder, and salt in a bowl. Set aside.
  3. Cream butter and sugar until light and fluffy. Add egg, lemon juice, and zest, mixing well.
  4. Gradually incorporate dry ingredients into wet ingredients.
  5. Scoop dough onto baking sheets and bake for 12–14 minutes. Let cookies cool before serving.

7. White Chocolate Cranberry Cookies

These sweet and tangy cookies are a festive favorite that work wonderfully for holiday parties or gifts. The tart cranberries and creamy white chocolate create an unbeatable combo.

Ingredients:

  • 2 ¼ cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • ½ tsp salt
  • ¾ cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1 cup packed brown sugar
  • ½ cup granulated sugar
  • 1 large egg
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 cup dried cranberries
  • 1 cup white chocolate chips

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C). Line baking sheets with parchment paper.
  2. Mix flour, baking soda, and salt in a bowl. Set aside.
  3. Cream butter, brown sugar, and granulated sugar until fluffy. Add egg and vanilla, mixing well.
  4. Slowly mix dry ingredients into the wet mixture. Stir in cranberries and white chocolate chips.
  5. Scoop dough onto baking sheets and bake for 10–12 minutes. Cool before sharing.

8. Salted Caramel Cookies

Decadent salted caramel cookies are sure to make a lasting impression. The buttery caramel notes pair perfectly with a light sprinkle of sea salt.
