Oozing Monster Pizzas: A Scary-Haunted Feast for Halloween


Halloween is here, and it’s time to dig into your spookiest recipes and share some monstrous fun with your family and friends! One incredibly chilling centerpiece for your Halloween meal has to be the Oozing Monster Pizza. This post will take you through the delightful journey of creating your own frighteningly delicious pizzas with a mysterious surprise in every bite.

Consider Your Dough

The spine-chilling foundation of our Oozing Monster Pizza is the nine-inch pre-made pizza crust. You can make your dough from scratch, or save time by picking up fresh vegan crusts from your local grocery store. Store-bought dough is not just convenient but also packed with flavor and a wonderful texture. You can even find crusts that are conveniently pre-baked, allowing for a quicker preparation time.

Total dough time: 10 minutes (for pre-made & pre-baked crusts)

Creeping with a Green Pest: Pesto Spread

Lay down the pesto spread, creating the groundwork for our spooky creature. Fresh basil pesto adds a delightful freshness to the pizza and forms the perfect green background for our monster. Making your own pesto from scratch is as simple as blending basil leaves, garlic cloves, pine nuts, grated Parmigiano-Reggiano or Nutritional yeast, and lemon juice with a little bit of extra-virgin olive oil. Alternatively, looking for a no-cook pesto spread option, many grocery stores carry fresh ready-to-use pesto.

Total pesto time: 5 minutes (for homemade & store-bought pesto)

Creating the Oozing Monster Eyes

For the oozy, gory center of our monster, let’s use a combination of melted vegan cheese (like mozzarella), black olives, and olive tapenade. Simply slice the black olives in half and place them around the pizza in pairs to form the eyes of our monster. Then, thinly spread the black olive tapenade around the olives to imitate oozing blood.

Total eyes time: 15 minutes ( preparation & baking time for cheese)

Merging the Monster’s Parts: Laying Out the Creature’s Features

For the tongue, use vibrant red pepper strips laid out in a snake-like formation. For claws, lay down some pre-cooked and crumbled bacon or vegan bacon bits around the edges of the pizza. For the sharp teeth, combine thinly-sliced red onions and thinly-sliced dried chili peppers.

Expanding the Monster’s Habitat: Surrounding Accents

Finally, it’s time to bring the nooks and crannies of our monster’s craggy lair. Line basil leaves and fresh cherry tomatoes around the edge of the pizza, creating a border of dark forest. Sprinkle fresh thyme and rosemary over the pizza, providing depth and an earthy aroma to the pizza.

Total accents time: 5 minutes (preparation)

Fire up your Oven and Bake that Spooky Abode

Now that your monstrous creation is ready, preheat your oven to 475°F (245°C) and bake the Oozing Monster Pizza for around 10-12 minutes, or until the crust is crisp and crust edges are lightly golden.

Finishing Touches: Garnishing the Horrifying Delight

Before serving, allow your pizza to cool slightly. Then scatter some chopped parsley over the monster’s creepy crust to give a tender, fresh touch. Your Oozing Monster Pizza is now ready to serve and be enjoyed by your hungry and spooked guests this Halloween.

The Oozing Monster Pizza is not just a Halloween treat - it’s an incredibly fun recipe to make (and eat!). You can easily customize the features of your monster by choosing from an array of veggies and toppings like mushrooms, roasted red pepper, or even slices of pickled jalapenos for a spicy surprise. The creativity for your monstrous creations is only limited by your imagination and the ingredients available to you. Happy haunting!
