5-Ingredient Caprese Stuffed Portobello Mushrooms Skillet Recipe


If you’re a fan of quick and easy recipes that are bursting with flavor, then this 5-ingredient Caprese stuffed portobello mushrooms skillet recipe is a must-try. This dish is a delightful combination of juicy portobello mushrooms, gooey mozzarella, vibrant cherry tomatoes, fresh basil, and balsamic glaze. It’s perfect for a light lunch, a flavorful appetizer, or even a satisfying side dish. Let’s dive into the details of this simple yet impressive recipe.

Ingredients:

  • 4 large portobello mushrooms
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 8 oz fresh mozzarella, sliced
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • Balsamic glaze
  • Olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

Step 1: Prepare the Portobello Mushrooms

Preheat your skillet over medium heat and drizzle a bit of olive oil. Clean the portobello mushrooms and remove the stems. Carefully scrape out the gills using a spoon to create more space for the filling. Place the mushrooms gill-side up in the skillet.

Step 2: Stuff with Caprese Goodness

Layer each portobello mushroom with slices of fresh mozzarella, followed by the halved cherry tomatoes. Sprinkle a pinch of salt and pepper over the tomatoes for extra flavor. Top it off with a few fresh basil leaves for a pop of color and herbaceous aroma.

Step 3: Let It Cook

Cover the skillet and let the mushrooms cook for about 10-12 minutes or until the cheese is melted and bubbly, and the mushrooms are tender. Keep an eye on them to prevent burning and ensure even cooking.

Step 4: Finish with Balsamic Glaze

Once the mushrooms are cooked to perfection, drizzle a generous amount of balsamic glaze over the top. The sweet and tangy glaze will complement the savory flavors of the mushrooms, cheese, and tomatoes, adding a delightful finishing touch.

Step 5: Serve and Enjoy

Carefully transfer the stuffed portobello mushrooms to a serving platter or individual plates. Garnish with a few more fresh basil leaves for an elegant presentation. These flavorful delights are best enjoyed warm, so serve them immediately and savor every bite.

Tips and Variations:

  • Feel free to customize this recipe by adding a sprinkle of Italian seasoning or a dash of red pepper flakes for a hint of heat.
  • For a richer flavor, you can brush the portobello mushrooms with balsamic glaze before adding the filling.
  • Experiment with different types of cheese, such as creamy burrata or tangy goat cheese, for a unique twist on the classic Caprese combination.

Final Thoughts:

This 5-ingredient Caprese stuffed portobello mushrooms skillet recipe is a testament to the fact that simplicity can be incredibly satisfying. With just a handful of wholesome ingredients, you can create a dish that looks and tastes gourmet. Whether you’re cooking for a special occasion or simply craving a flavorful meal, these stuffed portobello mushrooms are sure to impress. Give this recipe a try and let the delightful flavors speak for themselves!
