Instant Pot Baked Potatoes Recipe: Perfect for a Quick and Easy Side Dish


If you’re looking for a quick and easy side dish that’s both delicious and versatile, look no further than the Instant Pot baked potatoes. This simple recipe will show you how to achieve perfectly cooked, fluffy potatoes in a fraction of the time it takes to bake them in the oven. Whether you’re serving them alongside a juicy steak, a hearty stew, or a fresh salad, these Instant Pot baked potatoes are sure to be a hit at your next meal.

Why Choose Instant Pot Baked Potatoes?

The Instant Pot has revolutionized the way many of us cook, and for good reason. It’s a multi-functional kitchen appliance that can efficiently handle a wide range of cooking tasks, including perfectly baking potatoes. Using the Instant Pot for this recipe not only saves time but also ensures that the potatoes are evenly cooked and incredibly tender.

Ingredients

  • 4 medium-sized russet potatoes
  • 1 cup of water
  • Salt (to taste)
  • Olive oil (optional)
  • Toppings of your choice (e.g., butter, sour cream, chives, bacon bits, shredded cheese)

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Potatoes: Start by scrubbing the potatoes under running water to remove any dirt or debris. Once clean, pat them dry with a paper towel.

  2. Poke the Potatoes: Using a fork or a sharp knife, carefully poke each potato a few times. This will allow steam to escape while the potatoes cook, preventing them from bursting.

  3. Add Water to the Instant Pot: Pour one cup of water into the Instant Pot and place the trivet inside.

  4. Pressure Cook the Potatoes: Arrange the potatoes on the trivet, making sure they are not stacked on top of each other. Sprinkle a little salt over the potatoes for added flavor. Close the lid and set the Instant Pot to cook on high pressure for 12-15 minutes, depending on the size of the potatoes.

  5. Natural Release: Once the cooking time is complete, allow the pressure to release naturally for 10 minutes. Afterward, carefully perform a quick pressure release by turning the valve to the venting position.

  6. Serve and Enjoy: Using tongs, carefully remove the potatoes from the Instant Pot. If desired, lightly brush the potatoes with olive oil and sprinkle with a little more salt. Then, slice each potato open and fluff the insides with a fork. Add your favorite toppings and serve immediately.

Tips and Serving Suggestions

  • For extra crispy skins, you can transfer the cooked potatoes to a baking sheet and broil them in the oven for a few minutes.
  • These Instant Pot baked potatoes are delicious on their own, but they also make a fantastic base for a variety of toppings. Consider setting up a “potato bar” for a fun and customizable meal.

Final Thoughts

With this Instant Pot baked potatoes recipe, you can enjoy the comforting goodness of perfectly cooked potatoes in a fraction of the time. Whether you’re a busy parent, a working professional, or simply someone who loves good food, this recipe is a game-changer. Embrace the simplicity and versatility of the Instant Pot and elevate your side dish game with these delectable baked potatoes.
