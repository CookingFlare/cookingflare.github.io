Rigatoni with Sausage and Peas: A Hearty Pasta Dish


If you’re on the lookout for a comforting and flavorful pasta dish, look no further than rigatoni with sausage and peas. This hearty and satisfying meal is perfect for a cozy family dinner or a gathering with friends. The combination of savory Italian sausage, tender peas, and perfectly al dente rigatoni creates a dish that is sure to please even the most discerning palates. In this recipe, we’ll guide you through the steps to create this delectable dish in your own kitchen.

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound (450g) rigatoni pasta
  • 1 pound (450g) Italian sausage, casings removed
  • 1 cup (150g) frozen peas
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can (14 oz/400g) diced tomatoes
  • 1/2 cup (120ml) chicken broth
  • 1/2 cup (120ml) heavy cream
  • 1/2 teaspoon red pepper flakes
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Grated Parmesan cheese for serving
  • Fresh basil for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Cook the Rigatoni: Begin by bringing a large pot of salted water to a boil. Add the rigatoni and cook according to the package instructions until al dente. Drain the pasta, reserving a cup of the cooking water, and set aside.

  2. Brown the Sausage: In a large skillet over medium heat, cook the Italian sausage, breaking it into crumbles with a wooden spoon, until it is browned and cooked through. Transfer the cooked sausage to a plate lined with paper towels to drain any excess grease.

  3. Saute the Aromatics: In the same skillet, add the chopped onion and garlic. Cook for a few minutes until the onion is soft and translucent.

  4. Combine the Ingredients: Add the diced tomatoes, chicken broth, and red pepper flakes to the skillet, stirring to combine. Allow the mixture to simmer for about 5 minutes to let the flavors meld together.

  5. Add the Peas and Cream: Stir in the frozen peas and heavy cream, allowing the mixture to come to a gentle simmer. Let it cook for an additional 5 minutes until the peas are tender and the sauce has thickened slightly.

  6. Bring It All Together: Add the cooked rigatoni to the skillet, tossing it gently with the sauce to coat the pasta evenly. If the sauce is too thick, you can add some of the reserved pasta cooking water to loosen it up.

  7. Serve and Enjoy: Once everything is well combined, season the dish with salt and pepper to taste. Serve the rigatoni with sausage and peas in individual bowls, garnishing each portion with grated Parmesan cheese and fresh basil for an extra burst of flavor.

This rigatoni with sausage and peas recipe is a delightful marriage of hearty flavors and comforting textures. The savory sausage, vibrant peas, and rich tomato cream sauce come together to create a dish that is both satisfying and incredibly delicious. Whether you’re cooking for a crowd or simply craving a comforting meal at home, this recipe is sure to become a favorite in your culinary repertoire.

So, the next time you’re in the mood for a hearty pasta dish that’s bursting with flavor, give this rigatoni with sausage and peas recipe a try. Your taste buds will thank you for it!
