Satisfying Winter Chicken and Broccoli Casserole


When the cold winter weather sets in, there’s nothing quite like a warm and comforting casserole to satisfy your cravings. This Satisfying Winter Chicken and Broccoli Casserole is the perfect dish to warm you up from the inside out. Packed with tender chicken, vibrant broccoli, and a creamy, cheesy sauce, this casserole is a crowd-pleaser that will leave everyone coming back for seconds.

Ingredients

For the Casserole:

  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts, cooked and shredded
  • 4 cups fresh broccoli florets, blanched
  • 1 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1 cup chicken broth
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • 4 tablespoons unsalted butter
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • Salt and pepper to taste

For the Topping:

  • 1 cup panko breadcrumbs
  • 2 tablespoons melted butter
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C). Grease a 9x13 inch baking dish and set aside.

  2. In a large skillet, melt the butter over medium heat. Add the minced garlic and sauté until fragrant.

  3. Sprinkle the flour over the butter and garlic, whisking constantly to form a roux. Cook the roux for 1-2 minutes until it turns a light golden color.

  4. Slowly pour in the chicken broth and heavy cream, whisking continuously until the sauce thickens and becomes smooth.

  5. Stir in the grated Parmesan cheese, cheddar cheese, and mozzarella cheese until melted and well combined. Season the sauce with dried thyme, salt, and pepper to taste.

  6. In the prepared baking dish, layer the shredded chicken and blanched broccoli. Pour the creamy cheese sauce over the chicken and broccoli, ensuring that everything is evenly coated.

  7. In a small bowl, combine the panko breadcrumbs, melted butter, and grated Parmesan cheese for the topping. Sprinkle the breadcrumb mixture over the casserole.

  8. Cover the baking dish with foil and bake for 20 minutes. Then, remove the foil and continue baking for an additional 10-15 minutes, or until the casserole is bubbly and the top is golden brown.

  9. Once done, remove the casserole from the oven and let it rest for a few minutes before serving.

Serve and Enjoy!

This Satisfying Winter Chicken and Broccoli Casserole is best enjoyed hot out of the oven, with its rich and creamy sauce complementing the tender chicken and crisp broccoli. Whether you’re sharing it with family, friends, or enjoying a cozy night in, this casserole is sure to be a hit. Pair it with a side of crusty bread or a simple green salad for a complete and satisfying meal.

So, next time you’re craving a comforting dish to warm you up during the chilly winter months, give this recipe a try. It’s a delightful way to bring a little warmth and cheer to the season!

With the rich blend of flavors and the heartiness of the ingredients, this Satisfying Winter Chicken and Broccoli Casserole is a delightful addition to your winter recipe collection. Enjoy the process of cooking and savor the wonderful aroma that fills your kitchen as this casserole bakes to perfection.
