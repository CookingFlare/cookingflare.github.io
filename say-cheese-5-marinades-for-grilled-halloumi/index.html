Say Cheese! 5 Marinades for Grilled Halloumi


Grilled halloumi cheese is a delicious and versatile ingredient that can be enjoyed in a variety of ways. Its firm texture and salty flavor make it the perfect cheese for grilling. With just a few simple ingredients, you can create mouthwatering marinades that will elevate your grilled halloumi to a whole new level. Here are five irresistible marinades that will make your taste buds sing:

1. Herbed Lemon Marinade

This refreshing marinade combines the zesty flavors of lemon and the aromatic notes of fresh herbs. To make this marinade, whisk together the juice of one lemon, a tablespoon of olive oil, a teaspoon of chopped fresh thyme, a teaspoon of chopped fresh rosemary, and a pinch of salt. Place the halloumi cheese in a shallow dish and pour the marinade over it. Allow the cheese to marinate for at least 30 minutes before grilling. The tanginess of the lemon and the earthiness of the herbs will complement the rich flavor of the halloumi perfectly.

2. Spicy Honey Marinade

For those who enjoy a little heat, this spicy honey marinade is a must-try. In a small bowl, mix together a tablespoon of honey, a teaspoon of chili powder, a pinch of cayenne pepper, and a tablespoon of olive oil. Brush the marinade onto the halloumi cheese, making sure to coat both sides. Let it marinate for about 15 minutes before grilling. The sweetness of the honey combined with the spiciness of the chili powder will create a tantalizing flavor profile that will leave you craving more.

3. Garlic and Herb Marinade

If you’re a fan of garlic, this marinade is for you. In a blender or food processor, combine three cloves of garlic, a tablespoon of fresh parsley, a tablespoon of fresh oregano, a tablespoon of olive oil, and a pinch of salt. Blend until smooth. Place the halloumi cheese in a zip-top bag and pour the marinade over it. Massage the marinade into the cheese, ensuring that it is evenly coated. Allow the cheese to marinate for at least one hour before grilling. The garlic and herb flavors will infuse into the cheese, creating a savory and aromatic delight.

4. Balsamic Glaze Marinade

For a touch of sweetness and tanginess, try this balsamic glaze marinade. In a small saucepan, heat a quarter cup of balsamic vinegar over medium heat until it starts to bubble. Reduce the heat and simmer for about 10 minutes or until the vinegar has thickened into a glaze-like consistency. Remove from heat and let it cool. Brush the balsamic glaze onto the halloumi cheese, making sure to coat both sides. Let it marinate for about 20 minutes before grilling. The rich and slightly sweet taste of the balsamic glaze will add depth and complexity to the halloumi.

5. Smoky Paprika Marinade

For a smoky and slightly spicy kick, this paprika marinade is a winner. In a small bowl, mix together a tablespoon of smoked paprika, a teaspoon of ground cumin, a teaspoon of ground coriander, a tablespoon of olive oil, and a pinch of salt. Rub the marinade onto the halloumi cheese, ensuring that it is evenly coated. Let it marinate for about 30 minutes before grilling. The smoky flavor of the paprika combined with the warmth of the cumin and coriander will give your halloumi a bold and irresistible taste.

Grilled halloumi cheese is a versatile ingredient that can be enjoyed as a main course, added to salads, or used as a filling in sandwiches. With these five marinades, you can take your grilled halloumi to the next level by infusing it with a burst of flavors. Whether you prefer tangy and refreshing, sweet and spicy, or savory and smoky, there’s a marinade here to suit every palate. So fire up the grill, grab some halloumi, and let the marinades work their magic. Say cheese and savor the deliciousness!
