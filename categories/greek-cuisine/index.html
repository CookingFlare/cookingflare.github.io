Greek Lemon Orzo Salad: A Fresh and Zesty Pasta Dish

Are you looking for a light and refreshing pasta salad that bursts with flavor? Look no further than this Greek Lemon Orzo Salad! Packed with vibrant ingredients and zesty flavors, this dish is perfect for picnics, potlucks, or as a side dish for any summer meal. Let’s dive right into the recipe and discover how to create this delightful dish. Ingredients 1 cup orzo pasta 2 tablespoons olive oil 2 tablespoons freshly squeezed lemon juice 1 teaspoon lemon zest 1 small cucumber, diced 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved 1/2 cup Kalamata olives, pitted and sliced 1/4 cup red onion, finely chopped 1/4 cup fresh parsley, chopped 1/4 cup fresh dill, chopped 1/4 cup crumbled feta cheese Salt and pepper to taste Instructions Cook the orzo pasta according to the package instructions until al dente. Drain and rinse under cold water to cool it down. In a large mixing bowl, combine the cooked orzo pasta, olive oil, lemon juice, and lemon zest. Toss gently to coat the pasta evenly with the dressing. Add the diced cucumber, cherry tomatoes, Kalamata olives, red onion, parsley, and dill to the bowl. Mix well to combine all the ingredients. Sprinkle the crumbled feta cheese over the salad and season with salt and pepper to taste. Give it a final toss to ensure everything is well incorporated. Cover the bowl and refrigerate for at least 30 minutes to let the flavors meld together. When ready to serve, give the salad a quick stir and adjust the seasoning if needed. Garnish with some extra fresh herbs if desired. Enjoy this fresh and zesty Greek Lemon Orzo Salad as a standalone meal or as a side dish to complement your main course. It pairs well with grilled chicken, fish, or even lamb. Why You’ll Love This Recipe Flavorful and zesty: The combination of lemon juice, lemon zest, and fresh herbs adds a burst of freshness and tanginess to the salad, making it incredibly flavorful. Light and refreshing: This pasta salad is perfect for hot summer days when you crave something light and refreshing. It won’t weigh you down but will still satisfy your taste buds. Quick and easy: With minimal cooking involved, this recipe comes together in no time. You can have a delicious salad ready to go within 30 minutes. Versatile: While this Greek Lemon Orzo Salad is fantastic on its own, you can also customize it to your liking. Feel free to add grilled vegetables, roasted chicken, or even swap out the feta cheese for goat cheese. Perfect for gatherings: Whether you’re hosting a picnic or attending a potluck, this salad is a crowd-pleaser. Its vibrant colors and enticing flavors will make it the star of any gathering. Greek Tzatziki Chicken Salad: A Refreshing and Protein-Packed Meal

When it comes to wholesome and flavorful meals, Greek cuisine never fails to impress. This Greek Tzatziki Chicken Salad is a perfect example of how simple ingredients can come together to create a refreshing and protein-packed dish that’s perfect for any occasion. Whether you’re looking for a light lunch, a satisfying dinner, or a crowd-pleasing potluck contribution, this recipe has got you covered. Ingredients For the Chicken: 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts 2 cloves of garlic, minced 1 tablespoon olive oil 1 teaspoon dried oregano Salt and pepper to taste For the Tzatziki Sauce: 1 cup Greek yogurt 1 cucumber, grated and drained 2 cloves of garlic, minced 2 tablespoons fresh dill, chopped 1 tablespoon lemon juice Salt and pepper to taste For the Salad: 4 cups mixed greens 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved 1/2 red onion, thinly sliced 1/2 cup Kalamata olives 1/4 cup crumbled feta cheese 1/4 cup fresh mint leaves, chopped Instructions Step 1: Prepare the Chicken In a bowl, combine the minced garlic, olive oil, dried oregano, salt, and pepper. Add the chicken breasts to the bowl and coat them evenly with the marinade. Cover and refrigerate for at least 30 minutes to allow the flavors to infuse. Step 2: Make the Tzatziki Sauce Grate the cucumber and place it in a fine-mesh sieve. Sprinkle with a little salt and let it sit for 10 minutes to drain excess moisture. In a bowl, combine the Greek yogurt, minced garlic, chopped dill, lemon juice, and the drained cucumber. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Refrigerate until ready to use. Step 3: Grill the Chicken Preheat a grill or grill pan over medium-high heat. Grill the marinated chicken breasts for 6-7 minutes on each side, or until fully cooked and nicely charred. Remove from the grill and let them rest for a few minutes before slicing. Step 4: Assemble the Salad In a large salad bowl, combine the mixed greens, cherry tomatoes, red onion, Kalamata olives, and mint leaves. Toss the salad with half of the prepared Tzatziki sauce. Top the salad with the sliced grilled chicken, crumbled feta cheese, and an extra drizzle of Tzatziki sauce. Serving Suggestions This Greek Tzatziki Chicken Salad is best served immediately, allowing the flavors to meld together beautifully.
