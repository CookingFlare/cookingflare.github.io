15 Ground Beef Recipes You Need for Weeknight Dinners


Ground beef is one of the most versatile and budget-friendly ingredients you can have in your kitchen. Whether you’re whipping up quick dinners after long days or trying to feed a hungry family, ground beef can deliver delicious, hearty meals with surprisingly minimal effort. Here are 15 creative and satisfying ground beef recipes to add to your weeknight rotation.

1. Classic Beef Tacos

Few meals come together faster than a batch of beef tacos. Brown and season the ground beef with garlic powder, onion powder, paprika, and cumin, then spoon it into warm tortillas. Add toppings like shredded lettuce, diced tomatoes, cheese, avocado, and sour cream for a customizable fiesta perfect for any night of the week.

2. Cheesy Beef and Pasta Casserole

Transform ground beef into the ultimate comfort food with this cheesy pasta bake. Start by cooking ground beef with onions, garlic, and Italian seasoning. Mix it into cooked pasta, stir in marinara sauce, top with shredded mozzarella, and bake until bubbly. Pair with a simple green salad for a complete meal.

3. Beef and Vegetable Stir-Fry

Ground beef isn’t just for traditional recipes—it shines in stir-fries, too. Sauté ground beef with carrots, bell peppers, broccoli, and snap peas, then toss with a sauce made of soy sauce, hoisin, and ginger. Serve over steamed rice or noodles for a quick dinner that’s as satisfying as takeout.

4. Stuffed Bell Peppers

For a healthy yet filling meal, try ground beef stuffed peppers. Halve and hollow out bell peppers, then fill with cooked ground beef mixed with rice, diced onions, marinara sauce, and seasonings. Bake until the peppers are soft, then sprinkle with cheese. This colorful dish is packed with flavor and nutrients.

5. Sloppy Joe Sandwiches

Bring a little nostalgia to the table with homemade sloppy Joes. Cook ground beef with ketchup, mustard, Worcestershire sauce, and brown sugar for a sweet-and-savory filling. Serve the mixture on toasted hamburger buns and watch your family devour this classic recipe in no time.

6. One-Pan Beef and Rice Skillet

Keep cleanup to a minimum with this one-pan dinner. Sauté ground beef with onions and garlic, add uncooked rice, and pour in chicken or beef broth. Toss in diced vegetables of your choice, cover, and simmer until the rice is tender. A weeknight dinner that’s easy and satisfying!

7. Homemade Beef Meatballs

Making meatballs from scratch is surprisingly simple and far tastier than frozen ones. Combine ground beef with breadcrumbs, Parmesan cheese, egg, garlic, and herbs, then shape them into balls. Bake or pan-fry the meatballs, and serve with marinara sauce over pasta or on sliders for a fun twist.

8. Beef Enchiladas

Ground beef enchiladas are a surefire hit. Cook the beef with taco seasoning, then roll it into flour tortillas along with cheese. Arrange them in a baking dish, pour enchilada sauce over the top, and bake until the cheese melts. Garnish with cilantro and serve with a dollop of sour cream.

9. Beef and Black Bean Chili

Chili is the ultimate hearty weeknight dinner. Sauté ground beef with onions, garlic, and chili powder, then stir in black beans, diced tomatoes, and a touch of beef broth. Simmer until thickened and serve with toppings like shredded cheddar, sour cream, and jalapeños—or scoop it over roasted potatoes for a creative twist.

10. Ground Beef Stroganoff

Put a modern spin on a classic dish with this creamy stroganoff. Brown ground beef with mushrooms, onions, and garlic, then stir in a creamy sauce made from beef broth, sour cream, and Dijon mustard. Serve over egg noodles for a comforting dish that everyone will love.

11. Beef Lettuce Wraps

For low-carb nights, opt for lettuce wraps. Cook ground beef with garlic, ginger, soy sauce, and a splash of sesame oil, then spoon it into crisp butter lettuce leaves. Add shredded carrots, scallions, and a sprinkle of chopped peanuts for a crunchy, satisfying meal.

12. Hamburger Steak with Gravy

Bring Southern-style comfort to your table with hamburger steak. Form ground beef into patties and cook until browned. Serve the patties smothered in rich brown gravy made from beef broth and onions. Pair with mashed potatoes and green beans for a homestyle dinner that’s sure to impress.

13. Taco Stuffed Sweet Potatoes

Combine sweet and savory flavors by stuffing baked sweet potatoes with seasoned ground beef. Top the filling with diced tomatoes, shredded cheese, and a drizzle of lime crema. The contrast of flavors makes this dish unique, tasty, and perfect for weeknight dining.

14. Shepherd’s Pie

A traditional shepherd’s pie is always welcomed at the dinner table. Cook ground beef with onions, peas, and carrots, and layer the mixture in a baking dish. Top with creamy mashed potatoes and bake until golden-brown. This hearty dish is great for warming up on cooler nights.

15. Spicy Beef and Potato Hash

For a dish with a kick, try spicy ground beef hash. Cook the beef with diced potatoes, onions, and your favorite chili seasoning. Add chopped jalapeños for extra heat, and finish with a sprinkle of shredded cheese. Serve as is or with fried eggs on top for an unforgettable dinner.

Ground beef is more than just a quick-fix solution for dinner; it’s a canvas for creativity in the kitchen. These 15 recipes will breathe new life into your weeknight cooking with flavors, textures, and techniques your family will love. Whether you’re craving comfort food classics or experimenting with global cuisines, these ground beef dishes will ensure every bite is delicious—and every dinner is hassle-free.
