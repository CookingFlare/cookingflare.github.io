Summer Squash and Tomato Tart Recipe


Are you looking for a delightful dish that perfectly captures the essence of summer? Look no further than this Summer Squash and Tomato Tart recipe. This savory tart combines the freshness of summer squash and the vibrant flavors of ripe tomatoes to create a dish that will impress your family and friends. Whether you’re hosting a weekend brunch or simply craving a light and flavorful meal, this recipe is sure to become a favorite in your kitchen.

Ingredients:

  • 1 sheet of store-bought puff pastry
  • 2 medium-sized summer squash, thinly sliced
  • 2 large ripe tomatoes, thinly sliced
  • 1 cup of shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/4 cup of grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons of olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon of fresh thyme leaves
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh basil leaves for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven: Start by preheating your oven to 400°F (200°C) and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.

  2. Prepare the puff pastry: Roll out the puff pastry sheet on the prepared baking sheet. Using a sharp knife, lightly score a border about 1 inch from the edges of the pastry.

  3. Layer the cheeses: Sprinkle the shredded mozzarella and grated Parmesan cheeses evenly over the puff pastry within the border.

  4. Add the squash and tomatoes: Arrange the sliced summer squash and tomatoes on top of the cheese in an overlapping pattern. Sprinkle the minced garlic over the vegetables.

  5. Drizzle with olive oil: Drizzle the olive oil over the vegetables and season with salt, pepper, and fresh thyme leaves.

  6. Bake the tart: Place the tart in the preheated oven and bake for 25-30 minutes or until the pastry is puffed and golden and the vegetables are tender.

  7. Serve and garnish: Once baked, remove the tart from the oven and let it cool slightly. Garnish with fresh basil leaves before slicing and serving.

Tips for Success:

  • Use fresh produce: Opt for ripe, seasonal summer squash and tomatoes for the best flavor.
  • Customize your toppings: Feel free to add your favorite herbs, such as oregano or rosemary, to enhance the taste.
  • Experiment with cheeses: Mix and match different cheeses like goat cheese or Gruyère for a unique twist.
  • Serve with a side salad: Pair the tart with a fresh green salad dressed with balsamic vinaigrette for a well-rounded meal.

This Summer Squash and Tomato Tart is a versatile dish that can be enjoyed for any meal of the day. Whether served warm out of the oven or at room temperature, the flavors of the roasted vegetables and cheesy goodness will surely leave you craving more. So, gather your ingredients, preheat your oven, and get ready to savor the taste of summer in every bite!

Remember, cooking is all about creativity, so don’t be afraid to make this recipe your own by adding your personal touch. Enjoy every moment in the kitchen, and have fun experimenting with different flavor combinations. Happy cooking!
