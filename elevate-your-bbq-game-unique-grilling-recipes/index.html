Elevate Your BBQ Game: Unique Grilling Recipes


Welcome to our blog post on unique grilling recipes that will take your BBQ game to the next level! We all love the classic burgers and hotdogs, but why not shake things up a bit and surprise your guests with these innovative and delicious dishes? From mouthwatering grilled watermelon to savory bacon-wrapped asparagus, we’ve got you covered. Let’s dive right in!

Grilled Watermelon with Feta and Mint

Who says watermelon is just for slicing? Grilling watermelon brings out its natural sweetness and adds a smoky flavor that pairs perfectly with the tanginess of feta cheese and the freshness of mint. Here’s how to make this refreshing and unexpected dish:

Ingredients:

  • 1 small watermelon, cut into thick slices
  • 1 cup crumbled feta cheese
  • Fresh mint leaves, chopped
  • Olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your grill to medium-high heat.
  2. Brush both sides of the watermelon slices with olive oil and season with salt and pepper.
  3. Place the watermelon slices on the grill and cook for about 2-3 minutes per side, or until grill marks appear.
  4. Remove the watermelon from the grill and let it cool for a few minutes.
  5. Sprinkle the crumbled feta cheese and chopped mint leaves over the grilled watermelon slices.
  6. Drizzle with a little more olive oil and season with salt and pepper to taste.
  7. Serve immediately and enjoy the unique combination of flavors!

Bacon-Wrapped Asparagus Skewers

Asparagus is a versatile vegetable that becomes even more irresistible when wrapped in crispy bacon. These skewers make for a fantastic appetizer or side dish that will impress your guests. Here’s how to make them:

Ingredients:

  • Fresh asparagus spears, trimmed
  • Bacon slices (1 slice per 2 asparagus spears)
  • Olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your grill to medium heat.
  2. Drizzle the asparagus spears with olive oil and season with salt and pepper.
  3. Take one slice of bacon and wrap it around 2 asparagus spears, securing the ends with toothpicks.
  4. Repeat with the remaining asparagus and bacon.
  5. Place the bacon-wrapped asparagus skewers on the grill and cook for about 8-10 minutes, turning occasionally, until the bacon is crispy and the asparagus is tender.
  6. Remove from the grill and let them cool slightly before serving.
  7. Enjoy the delightful combination of smoky bacon and tender asparagus!

Grilled Pizza with Prosciutto and Arugula

Why order pizza when you can make it on the grill? This unique twist on a classic favorite will leave your taste buds craving for more. Try this grilled pizza with prosciutto and arugula recipe:

Ingredients:

  • Pizza dough (store-bought or homemade)
  • Olive oil
  • Tomato sauce
  • Mozzarella cheese, shredded
  • Prosciutto slices
  • Fresh arugula
  • Balsamic glaze (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your grill to medium-high heat.
  2. Roll out the pizza dough into your desired shape and thickness.
  3. Brush one side of the dough with olive oil and place it on the grill, oiled side down.
  4. Cook for about 3-4 minutes, or until the bottom is golden brown and grill marks appear.
  5. Remove the pizza crust from the grill and flip it over.
  6. Spread tomato sauce over the grilled side of the crust, leaving a border around the edges.
  7. Sprinkle shredded mozzarella cheese over the sauce.
  8. Top with prosciutto slices.
  9. Place the pizza back on the grill and cook for another 3-4 minutes, or until the cheese has melted and the crust is crispy.
  10. Remove from the grill and let it cool for a few minutes.
  11. Garnish with fresh arugula and drizzle with balsamic glaze if desired.
  12. Slice and serve this unique grilled pizza to wow your guests!

Conclusion

These unique grilling recipes are sure to elevate your BBQ game and impress your friends and family. From the smoky sweetness of grilled watermelon to the irresistible combination of bacon-wrapped asparagus and the gourmet flair of grilled pizza, these dishes will take your outdoor cooking to a whole new level. So fire up the grill, get creative, and enjoy the fantastic flavors of summer!
