Easy and Delicious Cinnamon Apple Crumble: A Sweet and Crunchy Dessert


Are you in the mood for a warm and comforting dessert that’s both easy to make and incredibly delicious? Look no further than this mouthwatering Cinnamon Apple Crumble recipe. Packed with the perfect blend of sweet and tart flavors, and topped with a crunchy crumble, this dessert is sure to become a family favorite. Let’s dive right in!

Ingredients

To make this scrumptious Cinnamon Apple Crumble, you’ll need:

  • 4 large apples (any variety you prefer), peeled, cored, and thinly sliced
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground nutmeg
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt

For the crumble topping, you’ll need:

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup rolled oats
  • 1/2 cup packed brown sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, cold and cubed

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C). Grease a 9-inch (23 cm) baking dish and set aside.

  2. In a large mixing bowl, combine the sliced apples, lemon juice, granulated sugar, ground cinnamon, ground nutmeg, and salt. Toss everything together until the apples are well coated with the mixture.

  3. Transfer the apple mixture to the greased baking dish, spreading it out evenly.

  4. In a separate bowl, prepare the crumble topping. Combine the all-purpose flour, rolled oats, packed brown sugar, and ground cinnamon. Mix well. Add the cold, cubed butter and use your fingers or a pastry cutter to incorporate the butter into the dry ingredients until the mixture resembles coarse crumbs.

  5. Sprinkle the crumble topping evenly over the apple mixture in the baking dish.

  6. Place the dish in the preheated oven and bake for 35-40 minutes, or until the apples are tender and the crumble topping is golden brown.

  7. Remove the dish from the oven and allow it to cool for a few minutes before serving.

  8. Serve the Cinnamon Apple Crumble warm, either on its own or with a scoop of vanilla ice cream for an extra indulgent treat. Enjoy!

Tips and Variations

  • For added texture and flavor, you can mix in some chopped nuts, such as walnuts or pecans, into the crumble topping before baking.
  • If you prefer a sweeter dessert, you can increase the amount of granulated sugar in the apple mixture or sprinkle some additional sugar on top of the crumble before baking.
  • Experiment with different apple varieties to find your favorite combination. Granny Smith apples provide a tartness that pairs well with the sweetness of the crumble, while Honeycrisp or Gala apples offer a sweeter flavor.

Conclusion

There you have it – a simple and delightful recipe for Easy and Delicious Cinnamon Apple Crumble. This dessert is perfect for any occasion, whether it’s a cozy family dinner or a gathering with friends. The warm, cinnamon-infused apples combined with the crunchy crumble make for a heavenly treat that will leave everyone asking for seconds. So, grab your apron and get ready to enjoy a taste of pure bliss!
