Budget-Friendly Mexican-Inspired Enchilada Recipes


Enchiladas are a beloved Mexican dish that offers a delectable combination of flavors and textures. From the tender tortillas to the savory fillings, enchiladas are a crowd-pleaser that can be enjoyed by everyone. In this blog post, we will explore budget-friendly Mexican-inspired enchilada recipes that are easy to make and will satisfy your taste buds without breaking the bank.

Classic Cheese Enchiladas

Ingredients:

  • 8 corn tortillas
  • 2 cups shredded cheese (cheddar, Monterey Jack, or a blend)
  • 1 cup enchilada sauce
  • Optional toppings: diced onions, sliced jalapenos, chopped cilantro

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. Warm the corn tortillas in a skillet or microwave until they are pliable.
  3. Place a handful of shredded cheese in the center of each tortilla and roll it tightly.
  4. Place the rolled tortillas seam-side down in a baking dish.
  5. Pour the enchilada sauce over the rolled tortillas, making sure to cover them completely.
  6. Sprinkle additional cheese on top if desired.
  7. Bake in the preheated oven for 20-25 minutes, or until the cheese is melted and bubbly.
  8. Remove from the oven and let cool for a few minutes before serving.
  9. Garnish with diced onions, sliced jalapenos, and chopped cilantro if desired.

Chicken Enchiladas Verdes

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups cooked and shredded chicken
  • 8 corn tortillas
  • 1 cup salsa verde
  • 1 cup sour cream
  • 1 cup shredded cheese
  • Optional toppings: diced tomatoes, sliced avocado, chopped cilantro

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. In a bowl, mix the shredded chicken with half of the salsa verde and half of the sour cream.
  3. Warm the corn tortillas in a skillet or microwave until they are pliable.
  4. Spoon a generous amount of the chicken mixture onto each tortilla and roll it tightly.
  5. Place the rolled tortillas seam-side down in a baking dish.
  6. Pour the remaining salsa verde and sour cream over the rolled tortillas, making sure to cover them completely.
  7. Sprinkle shredded cheese on top.
  8. Bake in the preheated oven for 20-25 minutes, or until the cheese is melted and bubbly.
  9. Remove from the oven and let cool for a few minutes before serving.
  10. Garnish with diced tomatoes, sliced avocado, and chopped cilantro if desired.

Vegetarian Black Bean Enchiladas

Ingredients:

  • 8 corn tortillas
  • 1 can black beans, rinsed and drained
  • 1 cup corn kernels
  • 1 cup diced bell peppers
  • 1 cup enchilada sauce
  • 1 cup shredded cheese
  • Optional toppings: sliced black olives, chopped green onions, sour cream

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. In a bowl, combine the black beans, corn kernels, and diced bell peppers.
  3. Warm the corn tortillas in a skillet or microwave until they are pliable.
  4. Spoon a generous amount of the bean mixture onto each tortilla and roll it tightly.
  5. Place the rolled tortillas seam-side down in a baking dish.
  6. Pour the enchilada sauce over the rolled tortillas, making sure to cover them completely.
  7. Sprinkle shredded cheese on top.
  8. Bake in the preheated oven for 20-25 minutes, or until the cheese is melted and bubbly.
  9. Remove from the oven and let cool for a few minutes before serving.
  10. Garnish with sliced black olives, chopped green onions, and sour cream if desired.

Conclusion

These budget-friendly Mexican-inspired enchilada recipes are perfect for a family dinner or a casual gathering with friends. They are easy to make, delicious, and won’t put a dent in your wallet. Whether you prefer classic cheese, chicken, or vegetarian options, enchiladas offer a satisfying meal that will transport you to the vibrant flavors of Mexico. So, gather your ingredients, roll up your sleeves, and get ready to enjoy a taste of Mexico with these budget-friendly enchilada recipes.
