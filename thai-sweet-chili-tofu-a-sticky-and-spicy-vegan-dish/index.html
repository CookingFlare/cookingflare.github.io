Thai Sweet Chili Tofu: A Sticky and Spicy Vegan Dish


If you’re looking for a delicious and satisfying vegan dish that packs a punch of flavor, Thai Sweet Chili Tofu is the perfect choice. This dish combines the savory richness of tofu with the sweet and spicy flavors of Thai sweet chili sauce, creating a mouthwatering experience that will leave you craving more. Whether you’re a seasoned vegan cook or just starting to explore plant-based cuisine, this recipe is sure to become a staple in your kitchen.

Ingredients

To make Thai Sweet Chili Tofu, you’ll need the following ingredients:

  • 1 block of extra firm tofu
  • 1/4 cup cornstarch
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1/4 cup Thai sweet chili sauce
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon rice vinegar
  • 1 teaspoon sesame oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon fresh ginger, grated
  • 1/4 cup green onions, chopped
  • Cooked rice or noodles for serving

Instructions

Step 1: Preparing the Tofu

Start by pressing the tofu to remove excess water. Place the block of tofu between two paper towels and set a heavy object, such as a cast-iron skillet, on top. Let the tofu press for 15-20 minutes to ensure it becomes firm and holds its shape.

Once the tofu is pressed, cut it into bite-sized cubes and toss them in cornstarch until evenly coated. This will create a crispy exterior when the tofu is cooked.

Step 2: Pan-Frying the Tofu

In a large skillet, heat the vegetable oil over medium-high heat. Add the coated tofu cubes to the skillet and cook for 5-7 minutes, turning occasionally, until all sides are golden brown and crispy. Once the tofu is cooked, transfer it to a plate lined with paper towels to drain any excess oil.

Step 3: Making the Sweet Chili Sauce

In a small bowl, whisk together the Thai sweet chili sauce, soy sauce, rice vinegar, sesame oil, garlic, and ginger. This flavorful sauce will be the star of the dish, providing the perfect balance of sweetness and heat.

Step 4: Bringing It All Together

Return the skillet to medium heat and add the chopped green onions. Sauté for a minute, then add the pan-fried tofu back to the skillet. Pour the sweet chili sauce over the tofu and stir gently to coat each piece.

Continue cooking for an additional 2-3 minutes, allowing the tofu to absorb the flavors of the sauce and become sticky and delicious.

Step 5: Serving

Once the tofu is coated and heated through, remove the skillet from the heat. Serve the Thai Sweet Chili Tofu over cooked rice or noodles, garnished with additional green onions if desired.

Conclusion

Thai Sweet Chili Tofu is a delightful combination of crispy, tender tofu and a sticky, spicy-sweet sauce that will tantalize your taste buds. This dish is not only vegan and packed with protein, but it’s also incredibly satisfying and easy to make. Whether you’re preparing it for a quick weeknight dinner or a special gathering with friends, this recipe is sure to impress and leave everyone wanting more.

Give this Thai Sweet Chili Tofu recipe a try and experience the delightful fusion of flavors that will elevate your vegan cooking game to new heights. Enjoy every bite of this sticky and spicy creation that brings the vibrant tastes of Thailand right to your table.
