Delicious and Filling Rice and Black Bean Burritos with Homemade Salsa


Burritos are a popular choice for a quick and satisfying meal. They are versatile, allowing you to fill them with an array of delicious ingredients. In this recipe, we will be making Rice and Black Bean Burritos with Homemade Salsa. This combination of flavors and textures will leave you craving for more. So let’s get started!

Ingredients

  • 2 cups cooked rice
  • 1 cup black beans, rinsed and drained
  • 1 small onion, diced
  • 1 bell pepper, diced
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • 1 teaspoon chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 4 large flour tortillas
  • Homemade salsa (recipe below)
  • Optional toppings: shredded cheese, sour cream, guacamole, chopped cilantro

Instructions

  1. Heat the olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Add the diced onion, bell pepper, and minced garlic. Sauté until the vegetables are tender and fragrant.

  2. Add the black beans to the skillet and cook for a few minutes, until heated through. Season with cumin, chili powder, paprika, salt, and pepper. Stir well to combine the flavors.

  3. Add the cooked rice to the skillet and mix everything together. Cook for another few minutes, allowing the rice to absorb the flavors of the spices.

  4. Warm the flour tortillas in a dry skillet or in the microwave until pliable. This will make them easier to roll.

  5. Place a generous scoop of the rice and black bean mixture onto the center of each tortilla. Add a spoonful of homemade salsa on top. If desired, sprinkle some shredded cheese, add a dollop of sour cream, a spoonful of guacamole, and a sprinkle of chopped cilantro.

  6. Fold the sides of the tortilla over the filling, then roll it up tightly from the bottom. Repeat with the remaining tortillas and filling.

  7. Serve the Rice and Black Bean Burritos with Homemade Salsa immediately. They can be enjoyed as is, or you can pair them with a side of Mexican rice or a fresh green salad.

Homemade Salsa

Making your own salsa is incredibly easy and adds a burst of fresh flavor to any dish. Here’s a simple recipe for homemade salsa that pairs perfectly with the Rice and Black Bean Burritos.

Ingredients

  • 4 ripe tomatoes, diced
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 1 jalapeño pepper, seeds and ribs removed, finely chopped
  • 1/4 cup fresh cilantro, chopped
  • Juice of 1 lime
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. In a bowl, combine the diced tomatoes, chopped onion, chopped jalapeño pepper, and fresh cilantro.

  2. Squeeze the juice of one lime over the mixture and season with salt and pepper to taste. Stir well to combine.

  3. Allow the salsa to sit for at least 10 minutes before serving, to allow the flavors to meld together.

  4. Serve the homemade salsa alongside the Rice and Black Bean Burritos. It adds a vibrant freshness that complements the savory filling perfectly.

Conclusion

These Delicious and Filling Rice and Black Bean Burritos with Homemade Salsa are a fantastic option for a quick and satisfying meal. The combination of rice, black beans, and spices creates a flavorful filling, while the homemade salsa adds a burst of freshness. Customize your burritos with your favorite toppings and enjoy every delicious bite. Whether you’re cooking for yourself or a crowd, these burritos are sure to be a hit.
