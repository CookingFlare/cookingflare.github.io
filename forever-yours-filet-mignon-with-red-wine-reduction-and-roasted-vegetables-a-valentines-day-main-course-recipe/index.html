Forever Yours Filet Mignon with Red Wine Reduction and Roasted Vegetables: A Valentine's Day Main Course Recipe

4 min · 730 words

Valentine’s Day is a special occasion to celebrate love, and what better way to do so than by preparing a romantic and delicious meal for your significant other? Our Forever Yours Filet Mignon with Red Wine Reduction and Roasted Vegetables is the perfect main course for a memorable Valentine’s Day dinner. This recipe combines the tenderness of filet mignon with the rich flavors of a red wine reduction, accompanied by perfectly roasted vegetables, creating a dish that is sure to impress your loved one.

Ingredients:

For the Filet Mignon:

  • 2 filet mignon steaks, about 8 ounces each
  • Salt and freshly ground black pepper
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons unsalted butter
  • 2 sprigs of fresh thyme
  • 2 cloves of garlic, smashed

For the Red Wine Reduction:

  • 1 cup red wine
  • 1/2 cup beef broth
  • 2 tablespoons shallots, finely chopped
  • 1 tablespoon unsalted butter
  • Salt and freshly ground black pepper

For the Roasted Vegetables:

  • 1 pound baby potatoes, halved
  • 2 carrots, peeled and cut into sticks
  • 1 red bell pepper, seeded and sliced
  • 1 yellow bell pepper, seeded and sliced
  • 1 red onion, cut into wedges
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • Salt and freshly ground black pepper

Instructions:

Cooking the Filet Mignon:

  1. Preheat the oven to 400°F (200°C).
  2. Season the filet mignon steaks generously with salt and freshly ground black pepper.
  3. In a large skillet, heat the olive oil over high heat until it shimmers.
  4. Add the steaks to the skillet and sear for 3 minutes on each side, or until well-browned.
  5. Add the butter, thyme, and smashed garlic to the skillet. Spoon the melted butter over the steaks for an additional 1-2 minutes to infuse the flavors.
  6. Transfer the skillet to the preheated oven and roast for 5-7 minutes for medium-rare, or to your desired level of doneness.
  7. Remove the steaks from the skillet and let them rest for 5 minutes before serving.

Making the Red Wine Reduction:

  1. In the same skillet used for the steaks, over medium heat, add the shallots and sauté until softened, about 2 minutes.
  2. Pour in the red wine and beef broth, scraping up any browned bits from the bottom of the skillet.
  3. Simmer the mixture until it has reduced by half, about 10 minutes.
  4. Whisk in the butter until the sauce is glossy and well combined. Season with salt and freshly ground black pepper to taste.

Roasting the Vegetables:

  1. In a large bowl, toss the baby potatoes, carrots, bell peppers, and red onion with olive oil, minced garlic, dried thyme, salt, and freshly ground black pepper.
  2. Spread the vegetables in a single layer on a baking sheet.
  3. Roast in the preheated oven for 25-30 minutes, or until the vegetables are tender and golden brown, stirring once halfway through.

Serving:

Place the filet mignon on a serving plate, drizzle with the red wine reduction, and serve alongside the roasted vegetables for a stunning presentation. Pair this exquisite dish with your favorite red wine to elevate the dining experience.

This Forever Yours Filet Mignon with Red Wine Reduction and Roasted Vegetables recipe is a labor of love that will surely make your Valentine’s Day celebration a memorable one. The tender, melt-in-your-mouth filet mignon paired with the luxurious red wine reduction and perfectly roasted vegetables creates a symphony of flavors that will delight your taste buds and captivate your heart. Enjoy this special dish with your beloved, and cherish the moments shared over a delightful meal made with love.
