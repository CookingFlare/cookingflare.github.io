South African Malva Pudding Cake Recipe with Apricot and Caramel


If you’re in the mood for a delightful and indulgent dessert, look no further than the South African Malva Pudding Cake with a twist of apricot and caramel. This rich and moist sponge cake, soaked in a sweet and sticky sauce, is a beloved South African classic that is sure to impress your taste buds and those of your guests.

Ingredients

For the Pudding Cake:

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1 pinch of salt
  • 1 tablespoon apricot jam
  • 1 tablespoon vinegar
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1 large egg
  • 1 tablespoon butter
  • 1 teaspoon apricot vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon smooth apricot jam
  • 1/2 cup full cream milk

For the Apricot and Caramel Sauce:

  • 1 cup apricot jam
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 1/2 cup cream

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (180°C) and grease a medium-sized baking dish.

  2. In a bowl, sift the flour, baking soda, and salt together.

  3. In a separate bowl, mix the apricot jam and vinegar.

  4. In another bowl, cream the sugar and egg together, then add the butter and mix until creamy.

  5. Add the apricot vinegar to the egg mixture and mix well.

  6. Gradually add the dry ingredients to the egg mixture, alternating with the milk, and mix until smooth.

  7. Pour the batter into the greased baking dish and bake for 30-40 minutes, or until the pudding cake is golden and a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.

  8. While the cake is baking, prepare the apricot and caramel sauce. In a saucepan, combine the apricot jam, water, and sugar, and bring to a boil. Reduce the heat and simmer for 5 minutes.

  9. Add the butter and cream to the saucepan and stir until the sauce is smooth and well combined. Remove from heat.

  10. Once the pudding cake is baked, remove it from the oven and immediately pour the warm apricot and caramel sauce over the hot cake, allowing it to soak in.

  11. Let the cake stand for a few minutes to allow the sauce to penetrate the cake, then serve warm.

Serving Suggestions

This indulgent South African Malva Pudding Cake with Apricot and Caramel is best enjoyed warm, perhaps with a dollop of whipped cream or a scoop of vanilla ice cream. The combination of the moist, flavorful cake and the sweet, tangy apricot and caramel sauce creates a truly irresistible treat that is perfect for any special occasion or simply as a comforting dessert after a hearty meal.

Conclusion

The South African Malva Pudding Cake with Apricot and Caramel is a delightful twist on a traditional favorite, combining the rich, decadent flavors of the classic dessert with the fruity sweetness of apricot and the lusciousness of caramel. Whether you’re a seasoned baker or just starting out, this recipe is sure to impress and satisfy. So why not bring a taste of South Africa to your home with this tantalizing dessert? Enjoy!

This South African Malva Pudding Cake recipe with a twist of apricot and caramel is a delightful fusion of flavors that will surely captivate your taste buds. Whether you’re planning a special dinner or simply craving a comforting dessert, this indulgent treat is a perfect choice.
