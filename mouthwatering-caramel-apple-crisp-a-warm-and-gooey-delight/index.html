Mouthwatering Caramel Apple Crisp: A Warm and Gooey Delight


There’s something about the combination of sweet, juicy apples and rich, buttery caramel that just screams comfort and indulgence. When you take those classic flavors and turn them into a warm, gooey, and oh-so-satisfying dessert, you get the mouthwatering delight that is Caramel Apple Crisp. This dessert is perfect for any time of the year but especially shines during the fall when apples are at their prime. The combination of tender apples, a buttery oat crumble, and a luscious caramel sauce creates a dessert that’s impossible to resist. Whether you’re serving it at a family gathering, a holiday feast, or simply craving a cozy treat, this Caramel Apple Crisp is sure to steal the show.

Ingredients

For the Apple Filling:

  • 6 large Granny Smith apples, peeled, cored, and sliced
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground nutmeg
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

For the Oat Crumble Topping:

  • 1 cup old-fashioned rolled oats
  • 3/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup packed light brown sugar
  • 1/4 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, melted

For the Caramel Sauce:

  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 6 tablespoons unsalted butter, cut into pieces
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven: Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease a 9x13 inch baking dish and set it aside.

  2. Prepare the Apple Filling: In a large bowl, combine the sliced apples, granulated sugar, flour, cinnamon, nutmeg, salt, and vanilla extract. Toss gently until the apples are evenly coated, then transfer the mixture to the prepared baking dish, spreading it out into an even layer.

  3. Make the Oat Crumble Topping: In a separate bowl, mix together the oats, flour, brown sugar, baking powder, and baking soda. Pour the melted butter over the oat mixture and stir until everything is well combined and the mixture resembles coarse crumbs.

  4. Assemble the Crisp: Sprinkle the oat crumble topping evenly over the apple filling, covering it completely.

  5. Bake the Crisp: Place the baking dish in the preheated oven and bake for 40-45 minutes, or until the topping is golden brown and the apple filling is bubbling around the edges.

  6. Prepare the Caramel Sauce: While the crisp is baking, make the caramel sauce. In a saucepan over medium heat, melt the granulated sugar, stirring constantly until it turns a deep amber color. Add the butter and stir until melted. Slowly pour in the heavy cream while stirring constantly. Allow the mixture to boil for 1 minute, then remove it from the heat. Stir in the vanilla extract and salt. Let the caramel sauce cool slightly.

  7. Serve and Enjoy: Once the Caramel Apple Crisp is done baking, remove it from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes. Drizzle the warm caramel sauce over the crisp just before serving. Serve the crisp warm, with a scoop of vanilla ice cream or a dollop of whipped cream if desired.

Tips and Variations

  • For a twist, consider adding a handful of chopped pecans or walnuts to the oat crumble topping for added crunch and nutty flavor.
  • You can use a mix of apple varieties for the filling to add complexity to the flavor profile.
  • Leftovers can be stored in the refrigerator and reheated in the oven for a delicious treat the next day.

Conclusion

Caramel Apple Crisp is the epitome of comfort and warmth in dessert form. The tender, spiced apples, the buttery oat crumble, and the decadent caramel sauce come together to create a dessert that’s both familiar and irresistibly indulgent. Whether you’re entertaining guests or simply treating yourself to a cozy evening in, this Caramel Apple Crisp is guaranteed to delight and satisfy. With its inviting aroma and gooey, comforting texture, it’s a dessert that’s sure to become a favorite in your recipe collection. So, roll up your sleeves, gather your ingredients, and get ready to indulge in the irresistible deliciousness of Caramel Apple Crisp.
