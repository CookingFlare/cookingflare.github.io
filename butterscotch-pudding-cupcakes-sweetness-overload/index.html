Butterscotch Pudding Cupcakes: Sweetness Overload


Are you in search of the perfect dessert that will simultaneously satisfy your sweet tooth and keep your taste buds begging for more? Look no further than the delightful, delectable Butterscotch Pudding Cupcakes! These delightful treats are the perfect combination of velvety smooth butterscotch pudding and rich, moist cupcakes - an absolute necessity for any dessert table, party, or simply when you’re craving something exceptional. Let’s dive into the wonderful world of Butterscotch Pudding Cupcakes and learn how to make this heavenly dessert.

Ingredients:

  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 1/2 cup buttermilk
  • 1 (3.4 oz) box instant butterscotch pudding mix
  • 1/2 cup butterscotch chips

For the Butterscotch Frosting:

  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1 (5.9 oz) tub of whipped mascarpone cheese
  • 1 (8 oz) tub of whipped cream cheese
  • 1 1/2 cups powdered sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/2 cup butterscotch chips, melted

First things first, begin by preheating your over to 350°F (175°C). Be sure to prep a 12-cup muffin tin by lightly greasing or adding cupcake liners. If you choose to use cupcake liners, you can skip the greasing process!

In a medium-sized bowl, whisk together the all-purpose flour, baking powder, baking soda, and salt. You can set this aside for a bit, we’ll come back to it, along with the flour, later.

In a separate, large mixing bowl, cream together the softened unsalted butter and granulated sugar until light and fluffy. To achieve that perfect texture, make sure to beat them together for at least five minutes.

Once you’ve reached the desired texture, add in the eggs one at a time, ensuring each egg is fully combined before adding the next. Following this, mix in the 2 teaspoons of vanilla extract.

Stir in half of the dry ingredients to the wet mix, followed by the buttermilk, and finally the remaining dry ingredients. As for mixing, there’s no need to overdo it - gently fold everything together until just combined.

At this point, fold in the butterscotch chips, and then divide the batter among the prepared muffin cups, filling each about halfway. To bake, place the muffin tin in the oven and let the cupcakes bake for 18-20 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center of a cupcake comes out clean.

Just before the cupcakes finish baking, you can begin preparing the dreamy butterscotch frosting. In a large mixing bowl, beat together the softened unsalted butter, whipped mascarpone cheese, and whipped cream cheese. Gradually add in the powdered sugar, blending until smooth.

Once combined, stir in the teaspoon of vanilla extract, and then finally, the melted butterscotch chips. Once the cupcakes have finished baking, let them cool in the tin for a couple of minutes before transferring them to a wire rack to cool completely. Go ahead and frost the cooled cupcakes and indulge in the sweet, silky butterscotch frosting.

And voila! The exquisite Butterscotch Pudding Cupcakes are ready to be served and enjoyed. Be warned though; they’re so irresistible that they might become an addiction. So, gather your friends and family around the table, and for a taste experience they will never forget, share these scrumptious Butterscotch Pudding Cupcakes with your loved ones. No doubt, these cupcakes will be a hit at your next gathering and a heavenly indulgence you’ll return to time and again.

Treat yourself to a slice of heaven with these Butterscotch Pudding Cupcakes - an extraordinary fusion of smooth and creamy butterscotch pudding that you just wouldn’t want to pass up. Happy baking!
