Keto BBQ Battle: On the Grill with Low-Carb Goodness


Fire up your grill, roll out the keto red carpet (because, well, we keto-d kind of love this lifestyle), and get ready for a backyard BBQ battle featuring mouth-watering low-carb recipes! From rib-sticking keto burgers to zesty grilled veggies – this post is all about bringing the heat and flavor, the low-carb way.

First Stop: Savory Bacon-Wrapped Brands!

BAcon-WrapPEDIAs: Ingredients:

  • 8 slices center-cut bacon
  • 8 thin-cut pork belly slices (about 2-3 oz each)
  • Salt and freshly ground black pepper
  • 2 tablespoons brown keto sugar (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your grill to medium-high heat.
  2. Season the pork belly slices with salt, pepper, and a dash of brown keto sugar (if using).
  3. Place a slice of bacon on your cutting board, and lay the marinated pork belly slice in the middle. Roll the bacon around the pork belly, trimming any excess fat if needed.
  4. Secure each Bacon-Wrapped Brand with a toothpick, and place on the grill.
  5. Cook for 8-10 minutes, turning occasionally, until thoroughly cooked and temperature reaches about 145°F.
  6. Remove toothpicks, let sit for a few minutes, then slice and serve.

Making Waves with Keto BBQ Shrimp Skewers:

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb peeled and deveined large shrimp
  • Salt, pepper, and paprika
  • Olive oil
  • 3 cloves minced garlic
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your grill on medium-high heat.
  2. In a large bowl, toss shrimp with salt, pepper, paprika, and olive oil. Mix in minced garlic.
  3. Thread shrimp onto skewers, leaving a bit of space between each piece.
  4. Grill for 2-3 minutes per side, until pink and cooked through.
  5. Sprinkle with chopped parsley before serving.

In the weCARIOOUS Zone: Keto BBQ Cauliflower Steaks:

Ingredients:

  • 1 large head of cauliflower
  • Salt and pepper
  • Olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon honey sriracha (adjust to taste)
  • Fresh chopped parsley

Instructions:

  1. Preheat grill to medium heat.
  2. Trim and cut the cauliflower head into 1-inch thick “steaks.”
  3. Grill steaks for 6-8 minutes per side, brushing with honey sriracha sauce during the last few minutes, until tender and caramelized.
  4. Season with salt, pepper, and a light drizzle of olive oil. Sprinkle with fresh chopped parsley before serving.

Fight Fire with Fire: Keto BBQ Ribs

Ingredients:

  • 2 slabs of spareribs or pork back ribs
  • Salt, pepper, and a dash of garlic powder
  • 1 cup of your favorite keto BBQ sauce
  • Cola or apple cider vinegar (for steaming)

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your grill on medium-low heat.
  2. Season both sides of ribs with salt, pepper, and garlic powder.
  3. Arrange the ribs in a grill basket or on a steamer rack, with an aluminum foil-covered drip pan beneath.
  4. Brush with a thin layer of keto BBQ sauce.
  5. Pour cola or apple cider vinegar into the drip pan to create steam and help ribs cook evenly.
  6. Place the ribs directly on the grill, allowing fat to drip into the pan for flavorful cooking.
  7. Grill for 2 to 2-1/2 hours, brushing frequently with additional sauce to achieve that glossy glazed finish.

Keep the Party Rolling with Keto Caribbean-Glazed Chicken Thighs:

Ingredients:

  • 1 tablespoon honey
  • 1 tablespoon lime juice
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon ground allspice
  • 1 cup mango salsa or mango chutney (ensure keto-friendly)

Instructions:

  1. Rinse and pat dry chicken thighs.
  2. In a bowl, whisk together honey, lime juice, garlic, allspice, and mango salsa.
  3. Reserve extra sauce for brushing on chicken during grilling.
  4. Marinate chicken thighs for at least 30 minutes in the refrigerator.
  5. Preheat grill to medium-high heat.
  6. Grill chicken thighs for 8-10 minutes per side, brushing occasionally with extra sauce.
  7. Internal temperature should reach 165°F before removing from heat.
