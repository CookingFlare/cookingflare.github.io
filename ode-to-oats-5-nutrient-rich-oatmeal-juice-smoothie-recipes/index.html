Ode to Oats: 5 Nutrient-rich Oatmeal Juice & Smoothie Recipes


Ah, oats – the unassuming hero of breakfast tables everywhere. Most often celebrated in their cozy, hot and creamy form, oats have long been valued for their hearty, nutty flavor and impressive nutritional profile. But oats go far beyond bowls or even cookies – when blended into juices and smoothies, they elevate the natural sweetness of your favorite fruits, lend a satisfying texture, and pack your beverages with chunky bits of goodness.

Today, we’re raising a glass to oats in a whole new way, with these 5 nutrient-rich oatmeal juice and smoothie recipes. Whether you need a pre-workout boost, a post-workout recovery sip, or a quick and wholesome meal on-the-go, these recipes will keep your taste buds dancing and your body nourished all day long.

1. Berry Oat Pancake Power Blast Smoothie

A twist on your classic flapjack, this power-packed smoothie bursts with fruity zest and satisfying oats. Best of all, it’s entirely guilt-free!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup rolled oats
  • 1 banana
  • 1 cup mixed berries (fresh or frozen)
  • ¼ cup almond milk
  • 1 tbsp chia seeds
  • 2 tsp honey
  • ½ tsp cinnamon
  • Ice cubes (optional)

Instructions

  1. Blend oats in a high-powered blender until a flour-like consistency forms.
  2. Add banana, mixed berries, almond milk, chia seeds, honey, and cinnamon to blender.
  3. Blend everything together until smooth and creamy. Add ice cubes if you’re on team frosty.
  4. Pour your power blast smoothie into a glass and enjoy.

2. Oat-Zucchini Berry Green Juice

Ever heard of a green juice that makes you full until lunchtime? The zucchini and oats in this recipe make it possible!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup rolled oats
  • 1 large zucchini
  • 1 cucumber
  • 3 leaves kale
  • 1 apple
  • 1 cup mixed berries
  • ½ lemon
  • 1-inch ginger (peeled)

Instructions

  1. Rinse the kale and prepare your produce by washing, peeling, and chopping it into smaller portions.
  2. Blend oats in a blender until a fine powder forms.
  3. Add oats, zucchini, cucumber, apple, mixed berries, lemon, and ginger to your juicer.
  4. Collect the juice in a pitcher, stir well and pour into glass. Cheers to your green goodness!

3. Pumpkin Spice Oatmeal Latte Smoothie

Autumn may be over, but there’s no stopping the irresistible sensation of pumpkin spice. Rekindle the magic in this robust, velvety smoothie, perfect for cool mornings or pick-me-ups.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup rolled oats
  • 1 cup unsweetened almond milk
  • ½ cup canned pumpkin puree
  • 1 tbsp pumpkin seeds
  • 1 tsp honey
  • ½ tsp cinnamon
  • ¼ tsp nutmeg
  • 1 scoop vanilla protein powder (optional)

Instructions

  1. With a high-speed blender, process oats until they reach a fine powder.
  2. Add almond milk, pumpkin puree, pumpkin seeds, honey, cinnamon, nutmeg, and protein powder (if using) to blender.
  3. Blend until perfectly smooth and let the silky latte magic happen!
  4. Pour and sip your autumn in a glass, reminiscing about falling leaves and cozy sweaters.

4. Tropical Turmeric-Ginger Oat Smoothie

Turmeric and ginger might sound intense, but take it from us: together with juicy tropical fruits, they create a mouthwatering and nutritious treat.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup rolled oats
  • 1 frozen mango
  • 1 frozen banana
  • 1 cup coconut water
  • 1 tbsp coconut oil
  • ½ tsp turmeric
  • 1-inch ginger (peeled)
  • 1 tbsp lime juice

Instructions

  1. Blend oats in a blender until powdered.
  2. Add mango, banana, coconut water, coconut oil, turmeric, ginger, and lime juice into blender.
  3. Mix and blend until creamy and smooth. Grab your blender lid tight – this blend packs a punch!
  4. Pour into a glass and enjoy this sunshine in a glass as you picture your next beach vacay.

5. Cinnamon-Maple Oatmeal-Peanut Butter Smoothie

The latest trend? Savory smoothies. Ditch the sweet and hello to your newest obsession: a protein-packed smoothie that leaves you full and with a hangover cure.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup rolled oats
  • 2 cups unsweetened almond milk
  • 2 scoops of your favorite protein powder
  • 1 tbsp peanut butter
  • 1 tbsp maple syrup
  • ½ tsp cinnamon

Instructions

  1. Blend oats until a fine powder forms.
  2. Add almond milk, protein powder, peanut butter, maple syrup, and cinnamon to blender.
  3. Blend until smooth, mimicking a milkshake – but trust, it’s the unsung hero of hearty meals.
  4. Pour into a glass and enjoy, relishing the peculiar flavor combination.

There you have it – the oatmeal renaissance! No longer are oats reserved for just breakfast; these creative oatmeal juice and smoothie recipes let oats shine beyond our morning bowls. Indulge your palate with sweet and savory combinations and feel the nourishing benefits for yourself. Regardless of your taste preference, these 5 concoctions are bound to bring joy into your life – one oat-powered sip at a time. Happy blending!
