One Pot Teriyaki Beef and Rice


If you’re looking for a delicious and easy dinner recipe that requires minimal cleanup, then look no further than this One Pot Teriyaki Beef and Rice. This flavorful dish combines tender beef, aromatic rice, and a savory teriyaki sauce all cooked together in one pot for a convenient and satisfying meal. Not only is it a time-saver, but it’s also a crowd-pleaser, making it perfect for busy weeknights or casual gatherings. Let’s dive into the recipe and discover how to make this mouthwatering One Pot Teriyaki Beef and Rice!

Ingredients

  • 1 pound of beef (such as flank steak or sirloin), thinly sliced
  • 1 cup of white rice
  • 2 cups of beef or vegetable broth
  • 1/2 cup of low-sodium soy sauce
  • 1/4 cup of mirin
  • 2 tablespoons of brown sugar
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon of grated ginger
  • 1 tablespoon of cornstarch
  • 2 green onions, thinly sliced (for garnish)
  • Sesame seeds (for garnish)
  • Vegetable oil
  • Salt and pepper

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Beef: Season the thinly sliced beef with salt and pepper. In a large pot or deep skillet, heat a tablespoon of vegetable oil over medium-high heat. Add the seasoned beef and cook until browned. Once the beef is cooked, transfer it to a plate and set it aside.

  2. Cook the Aromatics: In the same pot, add the minced garlic and grated ginger. Sauté for a minute until fragrant.

  3. Add the Rice: Pour in the white rice and stir to coat it with the aromatics. Let the rice toast for a minute.

  4. Make the Teriyaki Sauce: In a bowl, whisk together the soy sauce, mirin, brown sugar, and beef or vegetable broth. Pour the teriyaki sauce mixture into the pot with the rice.

  5. Simmer: Bring the mixture to a boil, then reduce the heat to low. Cover the pot and let the rice cook for about 15-20 minutes, or until the rice is tender and has absorbed the liquid.

  6. Thicken the Sauce: In a small bowl, mix the cornstarch with a few tablespoons of water to create a slurry. Pour the slurry into the pot and stir until the sauce thickens.

  7. Add the Beef: Once the sauce has thickened, return the cooked beef to the pot. Stir to combine the beef with the rice and sauce. Let it simmer for a few more minutes to allow the flavors to meld together.

  8. Serve: Once everything is heated through and well combined, remove the pot from the heat. Garnish the One Pot Teriyaki Beef and Rice with sliced green onions and sesame seeds for a pop of freshness and nuttiness.

Tips and Variations

  • For a twist, you can add vegetables such as bell peppers, snap peas, or broccoli to the One Pot Teriyaki Beef and Rice for added color and nutrition.
  • If you prefer a different protein, you can substitute the beef with chicken or tofu for a versatile variation.
  • Feel free to adjust the sweetness or saltiness of the teriyaki sauce to suit your taste preferences.

Conclusion

This One Pot Teriyaki Beef and Rice recipe is a lifesaver for those busy days when you crave a homemade meal without the fuss of multiple pots and pans. The tender beef, fragrant rice, and savory teriyaki sauce come together harmoniously in one pot, creating a dish that’s both convenient and satisfying. Whether you’re cooking for yourself, your family, or guests, this recipe is sure to impress with its bold flavors and minimal cleanup. Give it a try and savor the simplicity and deliciousness of this One Pot Teriyaki Beef and Rice!
