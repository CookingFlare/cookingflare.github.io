Coconut Cream Cake: A Tropical Escape in Every Bite


If you’re looking for a dessert that can transport you to a tropical paradise with every mouthful, look no further than the luscious Coconut Cream Cake. This delightful cake is a perfect blend of moist coconut-flavored layers, velvety cream filling, and a generous sprinkle of toasted coconut on top. Whether you’re hosting a summer gathering, planning a luau-themed party, or simply craving a taste of the tropics, this cake is sure to delight your taste buds and leave you yearning for more.

Ingredients

For the Cake

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 cup unsweetened shredded coconut
  • 1 cup coconut milk
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 3 large eggs
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

For the Cream Filling

  • 1 1/2 cups heavy cream
  • 1/2 cup powdered sugar
  • 1 teaspoon coconut extract

For the Frosting

  • 1 1/2 cups whipped cream
  • 1/2 cup powdered sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/2 cup toasted coconut, for garnish

Instructions

Step 1: Prepare the Cake

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease and flour two 9-inch round cake pans.
  2. In a bowl, whisk together the flour, shredded coconut, baking powder, baking soda, and salt.
  3. In another bowl, cream the butter and sugar until light and fluffy. Add the eggs one at a time, beating well after each addition. Stir in the vanilla extract.
  4. Gradually add the dry ingredients to the creamed mixture, alternating with the coconut milk. Mix until just combined.
  5. Divide the batter evenly between the prepared cake pans. Smooth the tops with a spatula.
  6. Bake for 25-30 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean. Allow the cakes to cool in the pans for 10 minutes before transferring to wire racks to cool completely.

Step 2: Make the Cream Filling

  1. In a chilled bowl, whip the heavy cream until it begins to thicken. Add the powdered sugar and coconut extract, and continue whipping until stiff peaks form.

Step 3: Assemble the Cake

  1. Once the cake layers have cooled, place one layer on a serving plate. Spread the cream filling evenly over the top.
  2. Carefully place the second cake layer on top of the filling.

Step 4: Prepare the Frosting

  1. In a bowl, combine the whipped cream, powdered sugar, and vanilla extract. Beat until smooth and spreadable.

Step 5: Frost and Garnish the Cake

  1. Frost the top and sides of the cake with the whipped cream frosting.
  2. Sprinkle the toasted coconut over the top of the cake, gently pressing it into the frosting.

Serving and Storage Tips

  • Serve the Coconut Cream Cake chilled for the best flavor and texture.
  • Store any leftovers in the refrigerator, covered, for up to 3 days.

Final Thoughts

The Coconut Cream Cake is a true celebration of the exotic flavors of coconut, and every bite is a decadent escape to the tropics. Its airy layers, dreamy cream filling, and the delightful crunch of toasted coconut make it a dessert that’s impossible to resist. Whether you’re a coconut enthusiast or simply in need of a dessert that exudes sunshine and happiness, this cake is guaranteed to become a favorite in your recipe collection. So, go ahead, whisk yourself away to a tropical paradise with this delightful Coconut Cream Cake!
