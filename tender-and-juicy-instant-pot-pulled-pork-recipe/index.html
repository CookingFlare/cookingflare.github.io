Tender and Juicy Instant Pot Pulled Pork Recipe


Pulled pork is a classic dish that never fails to impress. The tender and juicy meat, combined with flavorful seasonings, makes it a crowd favorite. And with an Instant Pot, you can achieve that perfect melt-in-your-mouth texture in a fraction of the time. In this recipe, we’ll show you how to make tender and juicy Instant Pot pulled pork that will have your taste buds singing.

Ingredients

  • 3 pounds of pork shoulder
  • 1 tablespoon of paprika
  • 1 tablespoon of brown sugar
  • 1 tablespoon of garlic powder
  • 1 tablespoon of onion powder
  • 1 teaspoon of cayenne pepper
  • 1 teaspoon of salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon of black pepper
  • 1 cup of chicken broth
  • 1/2 cup of your favorite barbecue sauce

Instructions

  1. Start by preparing the pork shoulder. Trim off any excess fat and cut it into manageable pieces, about 2-3 inches in size.

  2. In a small bowl, combine the paprika, brown sugar, garlic powder, onion powder, cayenne pepper, salt, and black pepper. Mix well to create a flavorful rub.

  3. Rub the spice mixture all over the pork shoulder, making sure to coat it evenly. Let it sit for about 15 minutes to allow the flavors to penetrate the meat.

  4. Preheat your Instant Pot by selecting the “Saute” function. Once hot, add a splash of oil and sear the pork shoulder on all sides until nicely browned. This step will help lock in the juices and add extra flavor to the meat.

  5. Once the pork is seared, remove it from the Instant Pot and set it aside.

  6. Pour the chicken broth into the Instant Pot and use a wooden spoon to scrape off any browned bits from the bottom. This will prevent any potential burn warnings during pressure cooking.

  7. Return the seared pork shoulder to the Instant Pot, making sure it is submerged in the broth. Secure the lid and set the vent to the sealing position.

  8. Select the “Manual” or “Pressure Cook” function, depending on your Instant Pot model, and set the cooking time to 70 minutes on high pressure. The Instant Pot will take some time to come to pressure before the cooking time starts counting down.

  9. Once the cooking time is complete, allow the pressure to release naturally for at least 15 minutes. Then, carefully switch the vent to the venting position to release any remaining pressure.

  10. Remove the pork shoulder from the Instant Pot and transfer it to a cutting board. Use two forks to shred the meat into bite-sized pieces.

  11. In a separate bowl, mix together the barbecue sauce and a few tablespoons of the cooking liquid from the Instant Pot. This will help infuse the pork with even more flavor.

  12. Pour the sauce over the shredded pork and toss to coat it evenly. Feel free to adjust the amount of sauce to your preference.

  13. Serve the tender and juicy Instant Pot pulled pork on buns or as a filling for tacos, nachos, or even as a topping for baked potatoes. The possibilities are endless!

Enjoy this mouthwatering pulled pork recipe that will have your family and friends begging for seconds. The Instant Pot makes it so easy to achieve that perfect tender and juicy texture, making it a go-to recipe for any occasion. Whether you’re hosting a backyard barbecue or simply craving a comforting meal, this Instant Pot pulled pork will never disappoint. Happy cooking!
