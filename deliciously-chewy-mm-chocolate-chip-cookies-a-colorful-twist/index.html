Deliciously Chewy M&M Chocolate Chip Cookies: A Colorful Twist


There’s something undeniably delightful about a warm, freshly baked chocolate chip cookie. The aroma that fills the air, the satisfying crunch as you take a bite, and the comforting sweetness that lingers on your taste buds. But what if we took this classic treat and added a colorful twist? Enter Deliciously Chewy M&M Chocolate Chip Cookies!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 cup brown sugar, packed
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 1/2 cups M&M’s candies
  • 1 cup semisweet chocolate chips

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C). Line a baking sheet with parchment paper and set aside.

  2. In a large mixing bowl, cream together the softened butter, granulated sugar, and brown sugar until light and fluffy. This should take about 2-3 minutes.

  3. Add the eggs, one at a time, mixing well after each addition. Stir in the vanilla extract.

  4. In a separate bowl, whisk together the all-purpose flour, baking soda, and salt. Gradually add the dry ingredients to the butter mixture, mixing until just combined.

  5. Gently fold in the M&M’s candies and semisweet chocolate chips. The colorful M&M’s will not only add a burst of vibrant hues to your cookies but also a delightful crunch in every bite.

  6. Using a cookie scoop or tablespoon, drop rounded portions of dough onto the prepared baking sheet, spacing them about 2 inches apart.

  7. Bake for 10-12 minutes, or until the edges are lightly golden. The centers may appear slightly undercooked, but they will continue to set as the cookies cool.

  8. Remove from the oven and allow the cookies to cool on the baking sheet for 5 minutes before transferring them to a wire rack to cool completely. This will ensure that they maintain their irresistibly chewy texture.

  9. Once cooled, grab a cookie, take a bite, and let the explosion of chocolatey goodness and colorful M&M’s take you on a flavor journey like no other.

Tips and Variations

  • For an extra chewy texture, refrigerate the cookie dough for at least 1 hour before baking. This will also allow the flavors to meld together, resulting in an even more delicious cookie.

  • Feel free to experiment with different flavors of M&M’s. From peanut butter to caramel, there’s a wide range of options to suit your taste preferences.

  • If you want to make these cookies even more indulgent, sprinkle a pinch of sea salt on top before baking. The combination of sweet and salty will elevate these treats to a whole new level.

  • These cookies also make fantastic gifts. Package them in a cute jar or wrap them up with a ribbon for a heartfelt present that will surely bring a smile to anyone’s face.

So there you have it, a colorful twist on the classic chocolate chip cookie. The Deliciously Chewy M&M Chocolate Chip Cookies are sure to be a hit at any gathering or simply enjoyed as a delightful treat for yourself. Get baking and let the vibrant colors and delicious flavors bring joy to your taste buds!
