Pasta with Lemon Butter Shrimp: A Tangy and Savory Delight


If you’re a fan of seafood and pasta, then this recipe is a match made in culinary heaven. Pasta with Lemon Butter Shrimp is a dish that combines the tanginess of lemon with the savory flavors of shrimp, creating a delightful and satisfying meal. Whether you’re looking to impress your dinner guests or simply treat yourself to a special dinner, this recipe is sure to hit the spot.

Ingredients

To create this tantalizing dish, you will need the following ingredients:

  • 8 ounces of linguine pasta
  • 1 pound of large shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 2 tablespoons of butter
  • 3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1/2 cup of chicken broth
  • Juice and zest of 1 lemon
  • 1/4 cup of chopped parsley
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Cook the linguine pasta according to the package instructions until al dente. Drain and set aside.

  2. In a large skillet, melt the butter over medium heat. Add the minced garlic and sauté for about 1 minute until fragrant.

  3. Add the shrimp to the skillet and cook for 2-3 minutes on each side until they turn pink and opaque. Remove the shrimp from the skillet and set aside.

  4. In the same skillet, pour in the chicken broth and bring it to a simmer. Add in the lemon juice and zest, stirring well to combine.

  5. Return the shrimp to the skillet and toss them in the lemon-butter sauce. Cook for an additional 2 minutes until the shrimp are fully coated in the sauce.

  6. Add the cooked linguine pasta to the skillet and toss it with the shrimp and sauce until everything is well combined. Cook for another minute to heat through.

  7. Remove the skillet from the heat and sprinkle the chopped parsley over the pasta. Season with salt and pepper to taste.

  8. Serve the Pasta with Lemon Butter Shrimp immediately, garnished with extra lemon zest if desired. Pair it with a fresh green salad or garlic bread for a complete and satisfying meal.

Tips and Variations

  • For an extra burst of flavor, you can add some red pepper flakes to the sauce while cooking the garlic. This will give the dish a subtle spicy kick.

  • If you prefer a creamier sauce, you can add a splash of heavy cream or half-and-half to the skillet along with the chicken broth. This will create a rich and velvety texture.

  • Experiment with different types of pasta to switch up the dish. Try using fettuccine, spaghetti, or even penne for a unique twist.

  • If you’re not a fan of shrimp, you can substitute it with chicken or scallops. The lemon butter sauce pairs well with a variety of proteins.

  • Don’t forget to zest the lemon before juicing it. The zest adds a bright and citrusy flavor to the dish.

In Conclusion

Pasta with Lemon Butter Shrimp is a dish that combines the tanginess of lemon with the savory flavors of shrimp, creating a delightful and satisfying meal. With its simple yet elegant preparation, this recipe is perfect for both weeknight dinners and special occasions. So, why not treat yourself and your loved ones to this tangy and savory delight?
