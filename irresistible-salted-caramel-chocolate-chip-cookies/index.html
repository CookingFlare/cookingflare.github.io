Irresistible Salted Caramel Chocolate Chip Cookies


If you’re a cookie lover like me, then you’re in for a treat with these irresistible salted caramel chocolate chip cookies. These cookies combine the classic flavors of sweet caramel, rich chocolate chips, and a hint of saltiness to create a truly delightful treat. They are soft, chewy, and guaranteed to satisfy your sweet tooth. So grab your apron and let’s get baking!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 cup brown sugar, packed
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 2 cups chocolate chips
  • 1 cup caramel bits
  • Sea salt, for sprinkling

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C) and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.

  2. In a large mixing bowl, cream together the softened butter, granulated sugar, and brown sugar until light and fluffy.

  3. Add the eggs one at a time, mixing well after each addition. Stir in the vanilla extract.

  4. In a separate bowl, whisk together the all-purpose flour, baking soda, and salt. Gradually add the dry ingredients to the wet ingredients, mixing until just combined.

  5. Fold in the chocolate chips and caramel bits. The dough will be thick and chunky, which is exactly what you want for those gooey caramel surprises in every bite!

  6. Using a cookie scoop or a tablespoon, drop rounded portions of dough onto the prepared baking sheet, spacing them about 2 inches apart.

  7. Sprinkle a pinch of sea salt on top of each cookie for that perfect sweet and salty balance.

  8. Bake for 10-12 minutes, or until the edges are golden brown. The centers may look slightly undercooked, but they will continue to set as the cookies cool.

  9. Allow the cookies to cool on the baking sheet for 5 minutes, then transfer them to a wire rack to cool completely.

  10. Once the cookies are completely cooled, store them in an airtight container to keep them soft and chewy.

Serving Suggestions

These salted caramel chocolate chip cookies are delicious on their own, but they can also be paired with a glass of cold milk or a scoop of vanilla ice cream for an extra indulgent treat. They are perfect for bake sales, parties, or simply enjoying with a cup of coffee on a cozy afternoon.

Tips and Variations

  • To intensify the caramel flavor, you can drizzle some caramel sauce on top of the cookies before serving.
  • If you prefer a crunchier texture, you can bake the cookies for a few minutes longer.
  • Experiment with different types of chocolate chips, such as dark chocolate or white chocolate, to create different flavor combinations.
  • You can also add chopped nuts, such as walnuts or pecans, for some extra crunch and flavor.

So there you have it! A delectable recipe for irresistible salted caramel chocolate chip cookies that will surely become a favorite among your family and friends. Get ready to indulge in the perfect balance of sweet and salty flavors in every bite. Happy baking!
