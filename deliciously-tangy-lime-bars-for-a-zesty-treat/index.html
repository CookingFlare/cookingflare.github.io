Deliciously Tangy Lime Bars for a Zesty Treat


Are you looking for a refreshing dessert that will tantalize your taste buds and leave you wanting more? Look no further than these deliciously tangy lime bars! Made with a shortbread crust, a zesty lime filling, and a sprinkle of powdered sugar on top, these bars are the perfect treat for any occasion. Whether you’re hosting a dinner party, a baby shower, or just need a sweet pick-me-up, these lime bars are sure to impress.

Ingredients

To make these scrumptious bars, you’ll need the following ingredients:

  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/4 cup confectioners’ sugar
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 teaspoons grated lime zest
  • 2 tablespoons freshly squeezed lime juice
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 cup powdered sugar

Instructions

To start, preheat your oven to 350°F (180°C). Line an 8-inch square baking dish with parchment paper or aluminum foil, leaving some overhang on the sides for easy removal.

In a medium bowl, whisk together the flour, granulated sugar, and confectioners’ sugar. Add the softened butter and mix until the mixture resembles coarse crumbs. Press the mixture evenly into the prepared baking dish.

Bake the crust for 20-25 minutes, or until it is lightly golden brown. Remove from the oven and set aside to cool slightly.

While the crust is baking, prepare the lime filling. In a large bowl, whisk together the eggs, lime zest, lime juice, and vanilla extract until well combined.

Once the crust has cooled, pour the lime filling over the top and smooth out the surface. Return the dish to the oven and bake for an additional 20-25 minutes, or until the filling is set and the edges are slightly puffed.

Remove the dish from the oven and let it cool completely on a wire rack.

Assembly and Decoration

Once the bars have cooled, use the overhanging parchment paper or foil to lift them out of the baking dish. Place the bars on a cutting board and cut them into squares or rectangles.

Sprinkle the top of each bar with powdered sugar and serve.

Tips and Variations

These lime bars are a versatile dessert that can be customized to suit your taste preferences. Here are a few tips and variations to keep in mind:

  • To enhance the lime flavor, use freshly squeezed lime juice instead of bottled juice.
  • For a more intense lime flavor, add an additional tablespoon or two of lime juice to the filling.
  • To make these bars more festive, top them with a sprinkle of edible gold dust or a dollop of whipped cream.
  • To make these bars more substantial, add a layer of chopped nuts or shredded coconut to the crust before baking.

Conclusion

These deliciously tangy lime bars are the perfect dessert for any occasion. With their shortbread crust, zesty lime filling, and sprinkle of powdered sugar on top, they’re sure to impress your friends and family. So go ahead, give them a try, and experience the tangy taste of lime in a whole new way!
