Spicy Slow Cooker Buffalo Chicken Sliders Recipe


If you’re looking for a game-day treat or a quick and delicious weeknight dinner, you’ve stumbled upon the perfect recipe! Spicy Slow Cooker Buffalo Chicken Sliders are not only bursting with flavor but are incredibly easy to prepare. Just toss everything into your slow cooker and let it do the work while you sit back and relax. These sliders are sure to be a hit at your next gathering or family meal. Let’s dive into this mouthwatering recipe that will have everyone coming back for seconds.

Ingredients

Before we get started, let’s gather all the ingredients you’ll need for this scrumptious recipe:

For the Buffalo Chicken

  • 2 lbs boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1 cup buffalo sauce (choose your favorite brand or make your own!)
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon onion powder
  • 1 teaspoon smoked paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon cayenne pepper (adjust according to your heat preference)
  • Salt and pepper to taste

For the Sliders

  • 12 slider buns (mini brioche or whole wheat)
  • 1 cup shredded cheddar cheese (or your cheese of choice)
  • 1/2 cup ranch dressing (or blue cheese dressing, for that classic buffalo flavor)
  • 1/4 cup green onions, chopped (for garnish)
  • Lettuce leaves (optional, for freshness)
  • Pickles (optional, for an extra crunch)

Instructions

Step 1: Prepare the Chicken

  1. Season the Chicken: Begin by seasoning your chicken breasts with garlic powder, onion powder, smoked paprika, cayenne pepper, salt, and pepper. Rub the spices in well to coat the chicken thoroughly.

  2. Add to Slow Cooker: Place the seasoned chicken breasts into your slow cooker. Pour the buffalo sauce over the chicken, ensuring that each piece is generously coated.

  3. Cook: Cover the slow cooker and cook on low for 6-8 hours or on high for 3-4 hours. The chicken is done when it is cooked through and easily shreds with a fork.

Step 2: Shred the Chicken

  1. Shred the Chicken: Once the chicken is fully cooked, remove it from the slow cooker and place it on a cutting board. Use two forks to shred the chicken into bite-sized pieces.

  2. Mix with Sauce: Return the shredded chicken to the slow cooker and stir it well to combine with any remaining buffalo sauce. Let it sit on low for another 15-30 minutes to absorb all the flavors.

Step 3: Assemble the Sliders

  1. Prepare the Buns: While the chicken is absorbing the flavors, slice your slider buns in half. If you’d like, you can lightly toast the buns under the broiler for added texture.

  2. Add Chicken: Spoon a generous amount of the spicy buffalo chicken mixture onto the bottom half of each slider bun.

  3. Top with Cheese: Sprinkle shredded cheddar cheese over the chicken on each slider. If you prefer your cheese melted, you can return the sliders to the oven for a few minutes to achieve that gooey texture.

  4. Dress It Up: Drizzle ranch or blue cheese dressing over the chicken, and add any desired toppings like chopped green onions or lettuce for added crunch and freshness.

  5. Complete Your Sliders: Place the top bun on each slider and secure with a toothpick if necessary.

Step 4: Serve and Enjoy!

Your Spicy Slow Cooker Buffalo Chicken Sliders are now ready to be served! Arrange them on a platter and watch as your guests dive in. These sliders pair wonderfully with homemade potato chips, celery sticks, or a simple green salad.

Tips for the Perfect Sliders

  • Adjust the Heat: If you like it spicy, feel free to add more cayenne pepper or even some diced jalapeños to the slow cooker. Conversely, if you prefer a milder flavor, reduce the amount of buffalo sauce or choose a milder brand.

  • Make it Ahead: These sliders are perfect for meal prep! You can cook the buffalo chicken in advance and store it in the refrigerator for up to 4 days. Simply reheat when you’re ready to assemble the sliders.

  • Freezing: Leftover buffalo chicken can be frozen for up to three months. Just make sure to store it in an airtight container. When ready to use, thaw in the fridge overnight and reheat in the slow cooker or on the stove.

  • Creative Variations: Feel free to customize your sliders with different toppings! Sliced avocado, jalapeños, or even coleslaw can add a delightful twist to the traditional buffalo flavor.

Nutritional Information

While these sliders are undeniably delicious, they also pack a punch in terms of flavor and nutrition. Here’s a rough breakdown of what you can expect per slider, assuming 12 sliders are made:

  • Calories: 250
  • Protein: 20g
  • Carbohydrates: 25g
  • Fat: 10g
  • Fiber: 1g

These figures can vary based on the exact ingredients and portion sizes used.

Conclusion

Spicy Slow Cooker Buffalo Chicken Sliders are the ultimate crowd-pleaser that brings the heat and flavor to any gathering. Not only are they easy to prepare, but they also offer a comforting and satisfying meal that everyone will love. Whether you’re hosting a game night, preparing for a family dinner, or simply craving something delicious, these sliders are sure to impress. Give this recipe a try, and don’t be surprised when they become a regular on your meal rotation!

Enjoy your cooking, and happy eating!
