Instant Pot Mexican Shredded Chicken: Tacos Galore!


If you’re craving a taste of Mexico right in your own kitchen, then you’re in for a treat! This Instant Pot Mexican Shredded Chicken recipe is a game-changer when it comes to creating flavorful, tender, and versatile shredded chicken that can be used in a variety of dishes. From tacos and burritos to salads and nachos, this recipe will become a staple in your meal rotation.

Ingredients

Before we dive into the cooking process, let’s take a look at the ingredients you’ll need to make this mouthwatering Instant Pot Mexican Shredded Chicken:

  • 2 pounds of boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1 cup of chicken broth
  • 1 can (14 ounces) of diced tomatoes
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 4 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon of chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon of cumin
  • 1 teaspoon of paprika
  • 1 teaspoon of oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 tablespoons of olive oil

Instructions

Step 1: Sauté the Aromatics

Start by setting your Instant Pot to the sauté function. Add the olive oil, chopped onion, and minced garlic to the pot. Sauté for a few minutes until the onions turn translucent and the garlic becomes fragrant.

Step 2: Add the Chicken and Spices

Once the aromatics are ready, add the chicken breasts to the Instant Pot. Season the chicken with chili powder, cumin, paprika, oregano, salt, and pepper. Give everything a good stir to ensure the chicken is coated with the flavorful spices.

Step 3: Pour in the Liquid

Next, pour in the chicken broth and add the can of diced tomatoes. The liquid will infuse the chicken with moisture and enhance its overall tenderness and flavor.

Step 4: Pressure Cook

Secure the lid on the Instant Pot and set the vent to the sealing position. Cook on high pressure for 10 minutes. Once the cooking time is complete, allow for a natural pressure release for 10 minutes before manually releasing any remaining pressure.

Step 5: Shred the Chicken

Carefully remove the lid from the Instant Pot and use two forks to shred the tender Mexican-spiced chicken. The chicken should effortlessly fall apart, soaking up all the delicious flavors from the broth and spices.

Step 6: Serve and Enjoy!

Your Instant Pot Mexican Shredded Chicken is now ready to be enjoyed in a variety of dishes. Whether you’re assembling tacos, burritos, salads, or nachos, this versatile shredded chicken will elevate any meal with its bold flavors and juicy texture.

Serving Suggestions

Now that you have a batch of mouthwatering shredded chicken, here are a few ideas on how to serve it:

  • Tacos: Fill warm tortillas with the shredded chicken, and top with your favorite taco toppings such as salsa, guacamole, and shredded cheese.
  • Burritos: Wrap the shredded chicken in a large tortilla along with rice, beans, and other fillings of your choice.
  • Salads: Add the shredded chicken to a bed of fresh greens and vegetables for a hearty and flavorful salad.
  • Nachos: Create a loaded plate of nachos by layering tortilla chips with the shredded chicken and a variety of toppings, then baking until the cheese is melted and bubbly.

Conclusion

With this Instant Pot Mexican Shredded Chicken recipe in your culinary arsenal, you’ll be able to whip up a wide array of delicious and satisfying meals that celebrate the vibrant flavors of Mexican cuisine. Whether you’re cooking for a crowd or simply enjoying a cozy dinner at home, these tender and flavorful shredded chicken tacos are sure to impress. So, grab your Instant Pot and get ready to embark on a culinary journey to Mexico, right in your own kitchen!
