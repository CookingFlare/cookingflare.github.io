Indulgent Lobster and Shrimp Ravioli: A Seafood Lover's Dream


Are you a seafood lover? If so, you’re in for a treat with this indulgent recipe for Lobster and Shrimp Ravioli. This dish combines the delicate flavors of lobster and shrimp with the rich creaminess of a homemade pasta sauce, resulting in a truly decadent meal that will leave you craving for more.

Ingredients

For the Pasta:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 3 large eggs
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon salt

For the Filling:

  • 1 lobster tail, cooked and chopped
  • 1/2 pound shrimp, cooked and chopped
  • 1/2 cup ricotta cheese
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley
  • Salt and pepper to taste

For the Sauce:

  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Start by making the pasta dough. In a large mixing bowl, combine the flour and salt. Create a well in the center of the flour and add the eggs and olive oil. Using a fork, gradually mix the eggs and oil into the flour until a dough forms.

  2. Transfer the dough onto a floured surface and knead for about 5 minutes until smooth and elastic. Wrap the dough in plastic wrap and let it rest for 30 minutes.

  3. While the dough is resting, prepare the filling. In a bowl, combine the chopped lobster tail, shrimp, ricotta cheese, Parmesan cheese, parsley, salt, and pepper. Mix well until all the ingredients are evenly incorporated.

  4. Roll out the pasta dough using a pasta machine or a rolling pin until it is thin but not transparent. Cut the dough into 3-inch squares.

  5. Spoon a tablespoon of the filling onto the center of each square. Fold the dough over the filling to form a triangle, and press the edges to seal the ravioli. Repeat this step for the remaining dough and filling.

  6. In a large pot of salted boiling water, cook the ravioli for about 4-5 minutes until they float to the surface. Remove them with a slotted spoon and set aside.

  7. To make the sauce, melt the butter in a skillet over medium heat. Add the minced garlic and sauté for a minute until fragrant. Pour in the heavy cream and bring it to a simmer. Stir in the grated Parmesan cheese and season with salt and pepper to taste.

  8. Add the cooked ravioli to the sauce and gently toss them to coat evenly. Cook for an additional 2-3 minutes until the ravioli are heated through and the sauce thickens slightly.

  9. Serve the Lobster and Shrimp Ravioli hot, garnished with some freshly grated Parmesan cheese and chopped parsley. Pair it with a crisp white wine or a light salad for a complete and satisfying meal.

Conclusion

Indulge in the flavors of the sea with this exquisite recipe for Lobster and Shrimp Ravioli. The combination of succulent lobster and shrimp, paired with a creamy homemade sauce, creates a dish that is truly a seafood lover’s dream. With simple ingredients and easy-to-follow steps, you’ll be able to create a restaurant-quality meal in the comfort of your own kitchen. So why wait? Treat yourself to this indulgent delight and savor every bite of the rich, flavorful ravioli.
