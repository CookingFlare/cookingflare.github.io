Fresh and Zesty Lemon Herb Pasta Salad: Perfect for Outdoor Gatherings


Are you looking for a refreshing and flavorful dish to serve at your next outdoor gathering? Look no further than this Fresh and Zesty Lemon Herb Pasta Salad! Packed with vibrant colors, tangy flavors, and a delightful combination of herbs, this salad is sure to be a crowd-pleaser.

Ingredients

To make this delicious pasta salad, you will need the following ingredients:

  • 12 ounces of pasta (we recommend using fusilli or rotini)
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1 cup cucumber, diced
  • 1/2 cup red onion, thinly sliced
  • 1/4 cup Kalamata olives, pitted and halved
  • 1/4 cup fresh parsley, chopped
  • 2 tablespoons fresh basil, chopped
  • 2 tablespoons fresh dill, chopped
  • 1/4 cup feta cheese, crumbled

For the Lemon Herb Dressing, you will need:

  • 1/4 cup extra virgin olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons lemon juice
  • 1 tablespoon Dijon mustard
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Cook the pasta according to the package instructions until al dente. Drain and rinse with cold water to cool it down.
  2. In a large mixing bowl, combine the cooked pasta, cherry tomatoes, cucumber, red onion, Kalamata olives, parsley, basil, dill, and feta cheese.
  3. In a separate small bowl, whisk together the olive oil, lemon juice, Dijon mustard, minced garlic, salt, and pepper until well combined.
  4. Pour the dressing over the pasta salad and toss gently to coat all the ingredients evenly.
  5. Cover the bowl with plastic wrap and refrigerate for at least 30 minutes to allow the flavors to blend together.
  6. Before serving, give the salad a quick toss to redistribute the dressing. Taste and adjust the seasoning if necessary.
  7. Transfer the pasta salad to a serving bowl or platter, and garnish with some extra fresh herbs for an added pop of color.

Serving Suggestions

This Fresh and Zesty Lemon Herb Pasta Salad is perfect for outdoor gatherings such as picnics, barbecues, or even a casual lunch in the backyard. Serve it as a side dish alongside grilled chicken or fish, or enjoy it as a light and refreshing main course.

Tips

  • You can customize this recipe by adding or substituting your favorite vegetables. Bell peppers, artichoke hearts, or even roasted corn would be delicious additions.
  • If you prefer a creamier dressing, you can mix in a spoonful of Greek yogurt or mayonnaise.
  • Make sure to rinse the cooked pasta with cold water to stop the cooking process and prevent the pasta from becoming mushy.
  • For a vegan version of this salad, simply omit the feta cheese or substitute it with a vegan cheese alternative.

Conclusion

With its vibrant colors, tangy flavors, and fresh herbs, this Fresh and Zesty Lemon Herb Pasta Salad is a true crowd-pleaser. Whether you’re hosting a backyard gathering or simply looking for a light and refreshing meal, this salad is sure to impress. So, grab your ingredients and get ready to enjoy this delicious dish under the sun!
