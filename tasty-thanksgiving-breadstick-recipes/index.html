Tasty Thanksgiving Breadstick Recipes


Welcome to our Thanksgiving breadstick extravaganza! Thanksgiving is a time for family, friends, and, of course, delicious food. If you’re looking to add a special touch to your Thanksgiving feast this year, why not try your hand at making some tasty breadsticks? Whether you prefer traditional flavors or want to experiment with something new, we’ve got you covered with these scrumptious breadstick recipes that are sure to impress your guests.

1. Classic Garlic Parmesan Breadsticks

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound pizza dough
  • 3 tablespoons unsalted butter, melted
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 400°F (200°C).
  2. Roll out the pizza dough on a lightly floured surface and cut it into strips.
  3. In a small bowl, mix the melted butter, minced garlic, and oregano.
  4. Brush the butter mixture over the dough strips and sprinkle with Parmesan cheese, salt, and pepper.
  5. Twist each strip and place them on a baking sheet.
  6. Bake for 15-20 minutes, or until golden brown.
  7. Serve warm and enjoy the irresistible aroma of garlic and Parmesan!

2. Rosemary and Sea Salt Breadsticks

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound pizza dough
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons fresh rosemary, finely chopped
  • 1 teaspoon sea salt

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 425°F (220°C).
  2. Roll out the pizza dough and cut it into sticks.
  3. Brush the breadsticks with olive oil and sprinkle with chopped rosemary and sea salt.
  4. Place the breadsticks on a baking sheet and bake for 12-15 minutes, or until crisp and golden.
  5. These fragrant and savory breadsticks will add a touch of elegance to your Thanksgiving table.

3. Cheesy Bacon Breadsticks

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound pizza dough
  • 4 slices bacon, cooked and crumbled
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 tablespoons unsalted butter, melted
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon onion powder

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Roll out the pizza dough and sprinkle half of the mozzarella and Parmesan cheese over it.
  3. Top with the crumbled bacon and the remaining cheese.
  4. Roll the dough into a log and cut it into sticks.
  5. Place the sticks on a baking sheet and brush with melted butter mixed with garlic and onion powder.
  6. Bake for 20-25 minutes, or until the breadsticks are golden and the cheese is bubbly.
  7. These indulgent breadsticks are a perfect combination of crispy, cheesy, and smoky flavors.

4. Cinnamon Sugar Breadsticks

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound pizza dough
  • 3 tablespoons unsalted butter, melted
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 tablespoon ground cinnamon

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Roll out the pizza dough and cut it into sticks.
  3. Brush the breadsticks with melted butter and sprinkle with a mixture of sugar and cinnamon.
  4. Bake for 15-18 minutes, or until golden and fragrant.
  5. These sweet and aromatic breadsticks are a delightful way to end your Thanksgiving meal.

Conclusion

These delightful breadstick recipes are a fantastic addition to your Thanksgiving menu. Whether you’re a fan of savory or sweet flavors, there’s a breadstick here for everyone. So, roll up your sleeves, preheat your oven, and get ready to impress your loved ones with these tantalizing treats. Happy Thanksgiving and happy baking!
