Easy and Quick Chicken Wrap Recipes


If you’re looking for a delicious and satisfying meal that’s easy to make and perfect for any occasion, chicken wraps are the way to go. Whether you’re preparing a quick lunch, a wholesome dinner, or a tasty snack, these versatile wraps are sure to hit the spot. The best part? They can be customized to suit your taste preferences, making them a go-to option for even the pickiest eaters.

Classic Grilled Chicken Caesar Wrap

Ingredients:

  • 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • Salt and pepper
  • Olive oil
  • 4 large flour tortillas
  • Romaine lettuce, chopped
  • Caesar dressing
  • Shredded Parmesan cheese

Instructions:

  1. Season the chicken breasts with salt, pepper, and a drizzle of olive oil.
  2. Grill the chicken over medium heat until fully cooked, about 6-7 minutes per side. Let it rest for a few minutes, then slice into strips.
  3. Warm the flour tortillas in a dry skillet for a few seconds on each side.
  4. Lay the tortillas flat and layer with chopped romaine lettuce, grilled chicken strips, a drizzle of Caesar dressing, and a sprinkle of shredded Parmesan cheese.
  5. Roll up the tortillas, tucking in the sides as you go, and secure with toothpicks if needed.

Serve immediately and enjoy the classic flavors of a Grilled Chicken Caesar Wrap.

Spicy Buffalo Chicken Wrap

Ingredients:

  • 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • Salt and pepper
  • Olive oil
  • 4 large flour tortillas
  • Shredded lettuce
  • Chopped tomatoes
  • Blue cheese dressing
  • Hot sauce

Instructions:

  1. Season the chicken breasts with salt, pepper, and a drizzle of olive oil.
  2. Grill the chicken over medium-high heat until fully cooked, about 6-7 minutes per side. Let it rest for a few minutes, then slice into strips.
  3. Warm the flour tortillas in a dry skillet for a few seconds on each side.
  4. Lay the tortillas flat and layer with shredded lettuce, chopped tomatoes, grilled chicken strips, a drizzle of blue cheese dressing, and a drizzle of hot sauce.
  5. Roll up the tortillas, tucking in the sides as you go, and secure with toothpicks if needed.

Get ready to spice up your meal with the tantalizing flavors of a Spicy Buffalo Chicken Wrap.
