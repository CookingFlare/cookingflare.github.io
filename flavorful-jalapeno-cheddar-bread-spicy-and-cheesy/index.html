Flavorful Jalapeno Cheddar Bread: Spicy and Cheesy


Are you ready to tantalize your taste buds with a delightful combination of spice and cheese? Look no further than this incredible recipe for Flavorful Jalapeno Cheddar Bread! This bread is the perfect blend of spicy jalapenos and rich cheddar cheese, creating a savory treat that is sure to become a favorite in your household.

Ingredients

To begin your flavorful journey, gather the following ingredients:

  • 3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 1/4 teaspoons active dry yeast
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons salt
  • 1 cup warm water
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1/2 cup pickled jalapeno slices, diced
  • 1/4 cup diced red onion
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh cilantro
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon smoked paprika

Instructions

Step 1: Activate the Yeast

In a small bowl, combine the warm water and active dry yeast. Allow the mixture to sit for about 5 minutes, or until the yeast becomes frothy.

Step 2: Prepare the Dough

In a large mixing bowl, combine the flour and salt. Create a well in the center of the dry ingredients and pour in the activated yeast mixture and olive oil. Stir the ingredients together until a shaggy dough forms.

Step 3: Knead the Dough

Transfer the dough to a floured surface and knead it for about 8-10 minutes, or until the dough becomes smooth and elastic.

Step 4: Add the Flavor

Return the dough to the mixing bowl and add the shredded cheddar cheese, diced jalapenos, red onion, cilantro, garlic powder, and smoked paprika. Gently fold the ingredients into the dough until they are evenly distributed.

Step 5: Let the Dough Rise

Cover the bowl with a clean kitchen towel and allow the dough to rise in a warm, draft-free place for about 1 hour, or until it has doubled in size.

Step 6: Shape the Bread

After the dough has risen, punch it down to release the air. Shape it into a round loaf and place it on a parchment-lined baking sheet.

Step 7: Final Rise

Cover the loaf with a kitchen towel and let it rise for an additional 30 minutes.

Step 8: Bake to Perfection

Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C). Once the dough has completed its final rise, bake the bread for 30-35 minutes, or until it is golden brown and sounds hollow when tapped on the bottom.

Step 9: Cool and Enjoy

Allow the bread to cool on a wire rack for a few minutes before slicing into it. Serve the jalapeno cheddar bread warm, and revel in the spicy, cheesy goodness!

Conclusion

This Flavorful Jalapeno Cheddar Bread is a delightful fusion of flavors that will surely elevate any meal. Whether enjoyed on its own, as a side to a hearty soup, or as part of a delicious sandwich, this bread is bound to impress. So, roll up your sleeves, gather your ingredients, and embark on a culinary adventure that will leave your taste buds singing!
