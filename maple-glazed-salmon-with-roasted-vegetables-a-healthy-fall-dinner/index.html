Maple-Glazed Salmon with Roasted Vegetables: A Healthy Fall Dinner


As the crisp autumn air sets in, it’s the perfect time to cozy up with a nourishing and flavorful meal. This Maple-Glazed Salmon with Roasted Vegetables recipe is a delightful combination of seasonal ingredients that will tantalize your taste buds and provide a boost of essential nutrients. Not only is this dish a feast for the senses, but it’s also a fantastic way to incorporate heart-healthy omega-3 fatty acids and an array of vitamins and minerals into your diet.

Ingredients

For the Maple-Glazed Salmon:

  • 4 salmon fillets
  • 1/4 cup pure maple syrup
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon grated fresh ginger
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • Salt and black pepper to taste

For the Roasted Vegetables:

  • 2 cups Brussels sprouts, halved
  • 2 cups butternut squash, cubed
  • 1 red onion, cut into wedges
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • Salt and black pepper to taste

Instructions

Maple-Glazed Salmon:

  1. In a small bowl, whisk together the maple syrup, soy sauce, minced garlic, and grated ginger.
  2. Season the salmon fillets with salt and black pepper, then brush the maple glaze over the fillets, ensuring they are evenly coated.
  3. In a large skillet, heat the olive oil over medium-high heat. Place the salmon fillets in the skillet and cook for 4-5 minutes on each side, or until the salmon is cooked through and the glaze has caramelized.

Roasted Vegetables:

  1. Preheat the oven to 400°F (200°C).
  2. In a large mixing bowl, toss the Brussels sprouts, butternut squash, and red onion with olive oil, minced garlic, dried thyme, salt, and black pepper.
  3. Spread the seasoned vegetables in a single layer on a baking sheet and roast for 25-30 minutes, or until they are tender and lightly caramelized.

Serving

Arrange the Maple-Glazed Salmon fillets on a serving platter and accompany them with a generous serving of the Roasted Vegetables. The combination of the sweet and savory glaze on the salmon, paired with the earthy, caramelized flavors of the roasted vegetables, creates a harmonious and satisfying meal.

Nutritional Benefits

Salmon is packed with omega-3 fatty acids, which are essential for heart health and brain function. It’s also an excellent source of high-quality protein and a variety of vitamins and minerals. The Brussels sprouts, butternut squash, and red onion in the roasted vegetables provide an abundance of fiber, vitamins, and antioxidants, making this dish a nutritional powerhouse.

Conclusion

This Maple-Glazed Salmon with Roasted Vegetables recipe is a delightful way to celebrate the flavors of fall while nourishing your body with wholesome ingredients. Whether you’re cooking for a special occasion or simply craving a comforting and nutritious meal, this dish is sure to become a seasonal favorite. Embrace the bountiful harvest of autumn with this delectable and healthful dinner option.
