Mini Tacos with Carnitas and Slaw: A Flavorful and Fun Snack for Any Time


Craving a delicious and satisfying snack that’s bursting with flavor? Look no further than these Mini Tacos with Carnitas and Slaw! These bite-sized treats are perfect for any occasion, whether you’re hosting a party or simply looking to elevate your snacking game. Packed with tender and juicy carnitas, crunchy slaw, and a variety of mouthwatering toppings, these mini tacos are sure to impress your taste buds and leave you wanting more.

Ingredients

For the carnitas:

  • 1.5 lbs pork shoulder, cut into small cubes
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon onion powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste

For the slaw:

  • 2 cups shredded cabbage
  • 1/4 cup mayonnaise
  • 1 tablespoon lime juice
  • 1 teaspoon honey
  • Salt and pepper to taste

For the tacos:

  • Mini tortillas
  • Sliced avocado
  • Fresh cilantro leaves
  • Lime wedges for serving

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 325°F (163°C).

  2. In a large skillet, heat the olive oil over medium-high heat. Add the pork shoulder cubes and season with chili powder, cumin, garlic powder, onion powder, salt, and pepper. Sear the pork on all sides until browned and crispy, about 5 minutes.

  3. Once the pork is seared, transfer it to a baking dish and cover with foil. Place in the preheated oven and cook for 2-3 hours, or until the meat is tender and easily shreds with a fork.

  4. While the carnitas are cooking, prepare the slaw. In a medium bowl, combine the shredded cabbage, mayonnaise, lime juice, honey, salt, and pepper. Mix well until the cabbage is coated in the dressing. Set aside in the refrigerator to chill.

  5. Once the carnitas are done, remove them from the oven and let them cool slightly. Use two forks to shred the meat into bite-sized pieces.

  6. To assemble the mini tacos, warm the mini tortillas in a dry skillet or microwave for a few seconds. Place a spoonful of the shredded carnitas onto each tortilla, followed by a spoonful of the slaw. Top with sliced avocado, fresh cilantro leaves, and a squeeze of lime juice.

  7. Serve the mini tacos immediately and enjoy!

Tips and Variations

  • For an extra kick of flavor, add some diced jalapenos or a sprinkle of hot sauce to your mini tacos.
  • Want to make it a meal? Serve these mini tacos alongside some Mexican rice and refried beans for a complete and satisfying dinner.
  • Feel free to customize the toppings to your liking. Add some diced tomatoes, sliced radishes, or a dollop of sour cream for additional freshness and creaminess.

Conclusion

These Mini Tacos with Carnitas and Slaw are a delightful and flavorful snack that will satisfy your cravings and impress your guests. The tender and juicy carnitas combined with the crunchy slaw and a variety of toppings create a harmonious blend of textures and flavors. Whether you’re planning a party or simply looking to elevate your snacking game, these mini tacos are the perfect choice. So go ahead, give this recipe a try, and indulge in a truly enjoyable snack experience.
