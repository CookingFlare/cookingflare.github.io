Gluten-Free Apple Cinnamon Pancakes: A Cozy Breakfast Option


Are you in the mood for a warm and comforting breakfast that’s both gluten-free and delicious? Look no further than these fluffy Gluten-Free Apple Cinnamon Pancakes! Packed with the flavors of autumn, these pancakes are the perfect way to start your day on a cozy note. Whether you follow a gluten-free diet or simply want to try something new, these pancakes are sure to become a favorite in your breakfast rotation.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup gluten-free all-purpose flour
  • 1 tablespoon brown sugar
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup unsweetened applesauce
  • 1/2 cup almond milk (or any non-dairy milk)
  • 1 tablespoon melted coconut oil (or any neutral oil)
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 medium apple, peeled and grated

Instructions

  1. In a large mixing bowl, whisk together the gluten-free all-purpose flour, brown sugar, baking powder, baking soda, ground cinnamon, and salt. Ensure all the dry ingredients are well combined.

  2. In a separate bowl, combine the unsweetened applesauce, almond milk, melted coconut oil, and vanilla extract. Whisk until everything is well incorporated.

  3. Slowly pour the wet mixture into the dry mixture, stirring gently with a spatula or wooden spoon until just combined. Be careful not to overmix, as this can lead to dense pancakes.

  4. Gently fold in the grated apple, ensuring it is evenly distributed throughout the batter.

  5. Preheat a non-stick skillet or griddle over medium heat. Lightly grease the surface with cooking spray or a small amount of oil.

  6. Using a 1/4 cup measuring cup, scoop the pancake batter onto the skillet, leaving enough space between each pancake to allow for spreading.

  7. Cook the pancakes for 2-3 minutes on one side, or until the edges start to look set and small bubbles form on the surface. Flip the pancakes and cook for an additional 1-2 minutes, or until golden brown.

  8. Remove the pancakes from the skillet and keep them warm on a plate covered with aluminum foil while you cook the remaining batter.

  9. Serve the Gluten-Free Apple Cinnamon Pancakes warm, topped with a drizzle of maple syrup, a sprinkle of cinnamon, and some fresh apple slices for an extra burst of flavor.

Why Choose Gluten-Free?

Gluten-free diets have gained popularity in recent years, whether due to celiac disease, gluten sensitivity, or personal choice. These pancakes offer a delicious gluten-free alternative to traditional pancakes without compromising on taste or texture. Using gluten-free all-purpose flour ensures that everyone can enjoy a cozy breakfast option without worrying about dietary restrictions.

The Magic of Apples and Cinnamon

The combination of apples and cinnamon is a classic flavor pairing that evokes feelings of warmth and comfort. The grated apple adds both moisture and natural sweetness to the pancakes, while the cinnamon infuses the batter with a cozy aroma that will fill your kitchen as you cook. These pancakes are the perfect way to celebrate the flavors of fall all year round.

Conclusion

Gluten-Free Apple Cinnamon Pancakes are a delightful twist on a breakfast classic. With their fluffy texture, warm apple flavor, and comforting cinnamon undertones, these pancakes are sure to become a family favorite. Plus, being gluten-free means they can be enjoyed by everyone at the breakfast table. So, why not treat yourself to a cozy and delicious start to your day with these gluten-free pancakes?
