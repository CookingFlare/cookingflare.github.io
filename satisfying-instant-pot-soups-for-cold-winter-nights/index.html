Satisfying Instant Pot Soups for Cold Winter Nights


As the cold winter nights draw in, there’s nothing quite like a warm, hearty bowl of soup to comfort the soul and warm the body. The Instant Pot has revolutionized the way we cook, making it easier and faster to create delicious meals, including soups. In this blog post, we’ll explore some satisfying Instant Pot soup recipes that are perfect for those chilly winter evenings. From classic chicken noodle soup to creamy tomato bisque, these recipes are sure to become staples in your winter meal rotation.

Chicken Noodle Soup

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound boneless, skinless chicken thighs
  • 6 cups chicken broth
  • 2 carrots, sliced
  • 2 celery stalks, chopped
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 2 cups egg noodles
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Place the chicken thighs, chicken broth, carrots, celery, onion, and garlic in the Instant Pot.
  2. Close the lid and set the Instant Pot to high pressure for 10 minutes.
  3. Once the timer goes off, allow for a natural pressure release for 10 minutes.
  4. Open the lid and remove the chicken thighs. Shred the chicken using two forks and return it to the pot.
  5. Add the egg noodles and thyme to the pot, then close the lid and set the Instant Pot to high pressure for 4 minutes.
  6. Once the noodles are cooked, season the soup with salt and pepper, and serve hot.

Butternut Squash Soup

Ingredients:

  • 1 medium butternut squash, peeled, seeded, and diced
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 4 cups vegetable broth
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground nutmeg
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Place the butternut squash, onion, garlic, vegetable broth, cinnamon, and nutmeg in the Instant Pot.
  2. Close the lid and set the Instant Pot to high pressure for 10 minutes.
  3. Once the timer goes off, use the quick release function to release the pressure.
  4. Use an immersion blender to puree the soup until smooth.
  5. Stir in the heavy cream and season the soup with salt and pepper.
  6. Serve the soup hot, garnished with a drizzle of cream and a sprinkle of nutmeg.

Beef and Barley Soup

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound stewing beef, cubed
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 2 carrots, sliced
  • 2 celery stalks, chopped
  • 1 cup pearl barley
  • 6 cups beef broth
  • 2 bay leaves
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Place the stewing beef, onion, carrots, celery, pearl barley, beef broth, and bay leaves in the Instant Pot.
  2. Close the lid and set the Instant Pot to high pressure for 20 minutes.
  3. Once the timer goes off, allow for a natural pressure release for 10 minutes.
  4. Open the lid, season the soup with salt and pepper, and serve hot.

Creamy Tomato Bisque

Ingredients:

  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 cans (28 ounces each) whole tomatoes
  • 1 cup vegetable broth
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Select the sauté function on the Instant Pot and heat the olive oil.
  2. Add the onion and garlic, sautéing until softened.
  3. Add the whole tomatoes (with their juice) and vegetable broth to the pot.
  4. Close the lid and set the Instant Pot to high pressure for 10 minutes.
  5. Once the timer goes off, use the quick release function to release the pressure.
  6. Use an immersion blender to puree the soup until smooth.
  7. Stir in the heavy cream, dried basil, salt, and pepper.
  8. Serve the soup hot, garnished with a drizzle of cream and a sprinkle of basil.

These satisfying Instant Pot soups are not only delicious but also a breeze to make, thanks to the efficiency of the Instant Pot. Whether you’re craving a classic chicken noodle soup or a comforting creamy tomato bisque, these recipes are sure to warm you up on those cold winter nights. So, grab your Instant Pot and get ready to enjoy a cozy evening in with a steaming bowl of homemade soup!
