Vegan Coconut Macaroons: Chewy and Coconutty Goodness


Are you craving a sweet treat that’s not only delicious but also vegan-friendly? Look no further than these delightful Vegan Coconut Macaroons! These chewy, coconutty morsels are a perfect indulgence for anyone with a sweet tooth, and the best part is that they’re entirely plant-based. Whether you follow a vegan lifestyle or simply want to try something new, these macaroons are sure to become a favorite in your recipe collection.

Ingredients

  • 3 cups shredded coconut
  • 1/2 cup coconut cream
  • 1/2 cup maple syrup
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 cup coconut oil, melted
  • 1 tablespoon cornstarch
  • 1/4 cup almond flour
  • 1/2 cup dairy-free chocolate chips (optional)

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 325°F (165°C) and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.

  2. In a large bowl, combine the shredded coconut, coconut cream, maple syrup, vanilla extract, and salt. Mix well until the ingredients are thoroughly combined.

  3. In a separate small bowl, whisk together the melted coconut oil and cornstarch until smooth. Add this mixture to the large bowl of coconut mixture and stir until everything is well incorporated.

  4. Gently fold in the almond flour until it’s evenly distributed in the mixture. If you’re a chocolate lover, this is the perfect time to add the dairy-free chocolate chips for an extra indulgent twist.

  5. Using a cookie scoop or your hands, form the mixture into small mounds and place them onto the prepared baking sheet. Make sure to leave some space between each macaroon to allow for spreading.

  6. Bake the macaroons for 20-25 minutes, or until they turn golden brown on the edges. Keep an eye on them to prevent over-browning.

  7. Once they’re done, remove the macaroons from the oven and let them cool on the baking sheet for a few minutes before transferring them to a wire rack to cool completely.

  8. Store the macaroons in an airtight container at room temperature for up to a week, although they’re so delicious, they probably won’t last that long!

Tips and Tricks

  • For an extra decorative touch, drizzle melted dairy-free chocolate over the cooled macaroons and let them set before serving.
  • If you prefer a sweeter flavor, you can adjust the amount of maple syrup to your liking.
  • Experiment with different extracts such as almond or coconut for a unique flavor profile.

Final Thoughts

These Vegan Coconut Macaroons are a testament to the fact that vegan desserts can be just as delectable as their traditional counterparts. Their chewy texture and rich coconut flavor make them a hit with vegans and non-vegans alike. Whether you enjoy them as an afternoon snack or as a sweet ending to a meal, these macaroons are sure to bring a smile to your face. So, gather your ingredients and get ready to savor the chewy and coconutty goodness of these delightful treats!
