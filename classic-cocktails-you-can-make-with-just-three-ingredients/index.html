Classic Cocktails You Can Make with Just Three Ingredients


With the rise of craft cocktails and artisanal spirits, it’s easy to get overwhelmed by the sheer number of ingredients and techniques required to create a truly unique drink. But what if you could make a classic cocktail with just three ingredients? Sounds too good to be true, right? Well, it’s not. In this post, we’ll explore some of the most iconic cocktails that can be made with just three ingredients, and show you how to do it.

The Old Fashioned

The Old Fashioned is a classic cocktail that has been around for over a century. It’s made with just three ingredients: bourbon or rye whiskey, sugar, and bitters. Here’s a simple recipe to get you started:

  • 2 oz bourbon or rye whiskey
  • 1/2 tsp sugar
  • 2 dashes Angostura bitters

Combine the whiskey, sugar, and bitters in a mixing glass filled with ice. Stir for about 30 seconds to chill and dilute the ingredients. Strain into a rocks glass filled with ice and garnish with an orange peel, if desired.

The Daiquiri

The Daiquiri is a refreshing cocktail that’s perfect for hot summer days. It’s made with just three ingredients: white rum, lime juice, and simple syrup. Here’s a simple recipe to get you started:

  • 2 oz white rum
  • 1 oz fresh lime juice
  • 1/2 oz simple syrup

Combine the rum, lime juice, and simple syrup in a shaker filled with ice. Shake for about 15 seconds to combine and chill the ingredients. Strain into a chilled coupe glass and garnish with a lime wheel, if desired.

The Whiskey Sour

The Whiskey Sour is a classic cocktail that’s been around for over a century. It’s made with just three ingredients: whiskey, lemon juice, and simple syrup. Here’s a simple recipe to get you started:

  • 2 oz whiskey
  • 3/4 oz fresh lemon juice
  • 1/2 oz simple syrup

Combine the whiskey, lemon juice, and simple syrup in a shaker filled with ice. Shake for about 15 seconds to combine and chill the ingredients. Strain into a chilled rocks glass and garnish with a lemon wheel, if desired.

The Gin Rickey

The Gin Rickey is a classic cocktail that’s perfect for those who prefer a lighter, more refreshing drink. It’s made with just three ingredients: gin, lime juice, and soda water. Here’s a simple recipe to get you started:

  • 2 oz gin
  • 1 oz fresh lime juice
  • 4 oz soda water

Combine the gin and lime juice in a highball glass filled with ice. Top with soda water and stir gently to combine. Garnish with a lime wheel, if desired.

The Mai Tai

The Mai Tai is a classic cocktail that’s perfect for those who love tropical flavors. It’s made with just three ingredients: rum, orange curaçao, and lime juice. Here’s a simple recipe to get you started:

  • 2 oz dark rum
  • 1 oz orange curaçao
  • 1 oz fresh lime juice

Combine the rum, orange curaçao, and lime juice in a shaker filled with ice. Shake for about 15 seconds to combine and chill the ingredients. Strain into a chilled coupe glass and garnish with a lime wheel and a sprig of mint, if desired.

The Moscow Mule

The Moscow Mule is a classic cocktail that’s perfect for those who love spicy drinks. It’s made with just three ingredients: vodka, ginger beer, and lime juice. Here’s a simple recipe to get you started:

  • 2 oz vodka
  • 4 oz ginger beer
  • 1 oz fresh lime juice

Combine the vodka and lime juice in a copper mug filled with ice. Top with ginger beer and stir gently to combine. Garnish with a lime wheel, if desired.

Conclusion

As you can see, making classic cocktails with just three ingredients is easier than you might think. With a little creativity and experimentation, you can create a wide range of delicious and iconic cocktails using just a few simple ingredients. So next time you’re looking to impress your friends or try something new, remember that simplicity is often the key to greatness.
