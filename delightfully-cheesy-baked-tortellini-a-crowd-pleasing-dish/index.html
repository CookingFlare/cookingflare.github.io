Delightfully Cheesy Baked Tortellini: A Crowd-Pleasing Dish


If there’s one dish that never fails to impress a crowd, it’s a hearty, cheesy, and oh-so-satisfying baked tortellini. This classic Italian pasta dish is a true crowd-pleaser, combining the rich flavors of cheese, savory tomato sauce, and perfectly cooked pasta. Whether you’re hosting a family gathering, a dinner party, or simply craving a comforting meal, this baked tortellini recipe is sure to become a staple in your cooking repertoire. So, gather your ingredients and let’s dive into the delightful world of cheesy baked tortellini!

Ingredients

Before we get started, let’s gather all the ingredients we need to bring this delicious dish to life:

  • 1 pound of cheese tortellini
  • 1 24-ounce jar of marinara sauce
  • 1 cup of shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup of grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/2 cup of ricotta cheese
  • 1/4 cup of chopped fresh basil
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon of dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Olive oil for greasing the baking dish

Instructions

Step 1: Preheat the Oven

Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C) and lightly grease a 9x13 inch baking dish with olive oil.

Step 2: Cook the Tortellini

Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil and cook the tortellini according to the package instructions. Once cooked, drain the tortellini and set it aside.

Step 3: Prepare the Cheese Mixture

In a large mixing bowl, combine the shredded mozzarella cheese, grated Parmesan cheese, ricotta cheese, chopped fresh basil, minced garlic, dried oregano, salt, and pepper. Mix everything together until well combined.

Step 4: Assemble the Dish

Pour half of the marinara sauce into the bottom of the prepared baking dish. Spread the cooked tortellini evenly over the sauce. Pour the remaining marinara sauce over the tortellini, making sure it’s well-covered. Finally, spoon the cheese mixture over the top, spreading it out to cover the entire dish.

Step 5: Bake to Perfection

Cover the baking dish with aluminum foil and bake in the preheated oven for 25 minutes. Then, remove the foil and continue baking for an additional 10-15 minutes, or until the cheese is golden and bubbly.

Step 6: Serve and Enjoy

Once the baked tortellini is beautifully golden and bubbling, remove it from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes. Garnish with extra fresh basil and a sprinkle of grated Parmesan cheese before serving. This dish pairs perfectly with a crisp green salad and a glass of your favorite wine.

Why You’ll Love This Dish

Delightfully cheesy and irresistibly comforting, this baked tortellini dish is a winner for so many reasons. The combination of three different cheeses creates a luxurious, gooey texture that perfectly coats each tender tortellini. The savory marinara sauce adds a zesty kick, while the aromatic blend of fresh basil and garlic elevates the flavors to new heights.

What’s more, this dish is incredibly versatile. You can customize it to your heart’s content by adding cooked Italian sausage, sautéed mushrooms, or even some baby spinach for an extra nutritional boost. It’s a fantastic way to sneak in some extra veggies for picky eaters or to elevate the dish for a special occasion.

In Conclusion

Whether you’re a seasoned home cook or just starting your culinary journey, this delightfully cheesy baked tortellini recipe is a must-try. It’s a comforting, crowd-pleasing dish that will earn you rave reviews every time. So, next time you’re looking for a dish that’s as easy to make as it is delicious, turn to this baked tortellini recipe and watch as it becomes a beloved favorite in your household. Buon appetito!
