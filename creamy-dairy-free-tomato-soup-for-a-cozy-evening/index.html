Creamy Dairy-Free Tomato Soup for a Cozy Evening


There’s nothing quite like a warm bowl of tomato soup to bring comfort on a chilly evening. This dairy-free version of the classic tomato soup is not only creamy and delicious but also suitable for those with lactose intolerance or a dairy-free lifestyle. Made with wholesome ingredients and bursting with flavor, this creamy dairy-free tomato soup is the perfect dish to cozy up with at the end of a long day.

Ingredients:

  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 medium onion, diced
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons tomato paste
  • 1 can (28 oz) crushed tomatoes
  • 1 can (14 oz) coconut milk
  • 2 cups vegetable broth
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh basil leaves for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Sauté the Aromatics: In a large pot, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the diced onion and sauté until translucent, then add the minced garlic and cook for another minute until fragrant.

  2. Add the Tomato Paste: Stir in the tomato paste and cook for a couple of minutes to caramelize it slightly, enhancing the depth of flavor in the soup.

  3. Simmer with Tomatoes: Pour in the crushed tomatoes and stir to combine. Let the mixture simmer for about 10 minutes, allowing the flavors to meld together.

  4. Blend and Creamify: Using an immersion blender, carefully blend the soup until smooth. Alternatively, transfer the soup to a blender in batches and blend until creamy.

  5. Incorporate the Creaminess: Return the smooth soup to the pot if using a blender, then pour in the coconut milk and vegetable broth. Stir well to combine and bring the soup to a gentle simmer.

  6. Season and Simmer: Add the dried basil, dried oregano, salt, and pepper to the soup, adjusting the seasonings to your taste. Let the soup simmer for another 10-15 minutes, allowing the flavors to develop.

  7. Serve and Garnish: Ladle the creamy dairy-free tomato soup into bowls and garnish with fresh basil leaves for a pop of color and added freshness.

Why This Recipe Works:

  • Creamy and Dairy-Free: The addition of coconut milk lends a luxurious creaminess to the soup without the need for dairy, making it suitable for those with lactose intolerance or following a dairy-free diet.

  • Rich Flavor Profile: Caramelizing the tomato paste and allowing the soup to simmer with the aromatics and seasonings results in a rich and robust flavor profile that is sure to satisfy your taste buds.

  • Comfort in Every Spoonful: Whether you’re curled up on the couch with a good book or hosting a casual gathering, this creamy dairy-free tomato soup brings warmth and comfort with every spoonful.

  • Versatile and Customizable: This recipe serves as a fantastic base for customization. Feel free to add a pinch of red pepper flakes for a hint of heat or toss in some cooked pasta or rice for a heartier meal.

In Conclusion:

This creamy dairy-free tomato soup is a delightful twist on a beloved classic, offering a velvety texture and a burst of tomato goodness in every spoonful. Whether you’re seeking a quick and satisfying weeknight dinner or a soothing meal to unwind with, this recipe is sure to become a staple in your culinary repertoire. So, grab a bowl, snuggle up, and savor the comforting embrace of this creamy dairy-free tomato soup on a cozy evening.
