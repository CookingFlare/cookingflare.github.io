Savory Shrimp Tacos: A Seafood Lover's Delight

If you’re a seafood lover looking for a delicious and satisfying meal, look no further than savory shrimp tacos. Bursting with flavors and packed with nutrients, these tacos are a seafood delight that will leave you craving for more. Whether you’re hosting a dinner party or simply want to treat yourself to a tasty meal, this recipe will surely impress your taste buds. Ingredients 1 pound of fresh shrimp, peeled and deveined 2 tablespoons of olive oil 1 teaspoon of paprika 1 teaspoon of cumin 1/2 teaspoon of chili powder 1/2 teaspoon of garlic powder Salt and pepper to taste 8 small flour tortillas 1 cup of shredded lettuce 1 cup of diced tomatoes 1/2 cup of diced red onion 1/4 cup of chopped fresh cilantro Lime wedges for serving Instructions In a large bowl, combine the shrimp, olive oil, paprika, cumin, chili powder, garlic powder, salt, and pepper. Toss well to coat the shrimp evenly with the spices. Heat a skillet over medium-high heat and add the shrimp. Cook for 2-3 minutes on each side, until they turn pink and opaque. Remove from heat and set aside. Warm the flour tortillas in a dry skillet or microwave for a few seconds until pliable. To assemble the tacos, place a small amount of shredded lettuce on each tortilla. Top with cooked shrimp, diced tomatoes, diced red onion, and chopped cilantro. Squeeze fresh lime juice over the tacos for a burst of citrusy flavor. Serve the savory shrimp tacos immediately and enjoy! Tips and Variations For an extra kick of heat, add sliced jalapeños or a drizzle of hot sauce to your tacos. Experiment with different toppings such as avocado slices, sour cream, or crumbled queso fresco. If you prefer a gluten-free option, use corn tortillas instead of flour tortillas. Grilling the shrimp instead of cooking them on the stovetop adds a smoky flavor to the tacos. Feel free to marinate the shrimp for a few hours or overnight to enhance the flavor even more.
