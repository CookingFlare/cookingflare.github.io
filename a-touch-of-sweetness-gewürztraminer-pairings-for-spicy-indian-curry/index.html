A Touch of Sweetness: Gewürztraminer Pairings for Spicy Indian Curry


When it comes to pairing wine with spicy Indian curry, many of us tend to reach for a crisp, dry white wine, hoping to cut through the heat. But what if I told you there’s a wine that can actually enhance the flavors of your curry, rather than just neutralizing the heat? Enter Gewürztraminer, a sweet and aromatic white wine that’s perfect for pairing with spicy Indian dishes.

Gewürztraminer, a varietal from the Alsace region of France, is known for its distinctive floral and spicy aromas, which make it an ideal match for the complex flavors found in Indian cuisine. The sweetness of the wine helps to balance the heat of the curry, while its acidity cuts through the richness of the sauce. And with its off-dry style, Gewürztraminer can even complement the sweetness of the spices and sauces used in Indian cooking.

But don’t just take my word for it – let’s explore some specific pairing options for Gewürztraminer and spicy Indian curry.

Pairing 1: Gewürztraminer and Chicken Tikka Masala

Chicken Tikka Masala is a classic Indian dish that’s both creamy and spicy. To pair it with Gewürztraminer, look for a wine with a slightly sweet and floral nose, such as the Trimbach Gewürztraminer from Alsace. The sweetness of the wine will help to balance the richness of the sauce, while its acidity will cut through the creaminess. And with its off-dry style, the Gewürztraminer will complement the sweetness of the spices and the tomato sauce.

Pairing 2: Gewürztraminer and Beef Vindaloo

Beef Vindaloo is a spicy and flavorful Indian dish that’s perfect for pairing with Gewürztraminer. Look for a wine with a slightly sweet and spicy nose, such as the Hugel Gewürztraminer from Alsace. The sweetness of the wine will help to balance the heat of the curry, while its acidity will cut through the richness of the sauce. And with its off-dry style, the Gewürztraminer will complement the sweetness of the spices and the vinegar used in the dish.

Pairing 3: Gewürztraminer and Vegetable Korma

Vegetable Korma is a mild and creamy Indian dish that’s perfect for pairing with Gewürztraminer. Look for a wine with a sweet and floral nose, such as the Domaine Weinbach Gewürztraminer from Alsace. The sweetness of the wine will help to balance the richness of the sauce, while its acidity will cut through the creaminess. And with its off-dry style, the Gewürztraminer will complement the sweetness of the spices and the vegetables used in the dish.

In conclusion, Gewürztraminer is a wine that’s perfect for pairing with spicy Indian curry. Its sweetness and acidity make it an ideal match for the complex flavors found in Indian cuisine, and its off-dry style allows it to complement the sweetness of the spices and sauces used in Indian cooking. Whether you’re pairing it with Chicken Tikka Masala, Beef Vindaloo, or Vegetable Korma, Gewürztraminer is a wine that’s sure to elevate your Indian cooking experience.

Additional Tips for Pairing Gewürztraminer with Indian Curry

  • When pairing Gewürztraminer with Indian curry, look for a wine with a sweet and floral nose, as this will help to balance the heat of the curry.
  • The acidity of the wine is also important, as it will help to cut through the richness of the sauce.
  • The off-dry style of Gewürztraminer makes it an ideal match for Indian cuisine, as it will complement the sweetness of the spices and sauces used in Indian cooking.
  • When pairing Gewürztraminer with Indian curry, consider the level of spiciness in the dish. If the curry is very spicy, you may want to opt for a wine with a slightly sweeter nose, such as the Trimbach Gewürztraminer. If the curry is less spicy, you may want to opt for a wine with a slightly drier nose, such as the Hugel Gewürztraminer.
  • Don’t be afraid to experiment with different Gewürztraminer wines to find the one that pairs best with your favorite Indian dishes.
