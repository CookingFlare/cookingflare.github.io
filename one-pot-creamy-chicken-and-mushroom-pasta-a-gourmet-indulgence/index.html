One-Pot Creamy Chicken and Mushroom Pasta: A Gourmet Indulgence


Are you craving a delicious and indulgent meal that’s also easy to make? Look no further than this One-Pot Creamy Chicken and Mushroom Pasta recipe. With its rich flavors and creamy sauce, it’s the perfect dish to satisfy your taste buds and impress your guests. Plus, it requires only one pot, which means less time spent on cleanup and more time enjoying your meal!

Ingredients

  • 1 lb chicken breast, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 8 oz mushrooms, sliced
  • 1 medium onion, finely chopped
  • 3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 2 cups chicken broth
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • 12 oz fettuccine pasta
  • Fresh parsley, for garnish

Instructions

  1. In a large pot or Dutch oven, heat the olive oil and butter over medium heat. Add the chicken pieces and cook until browned on all sides. Remove the chicken from the pot and set it aside.

  2. In the same pot, add the chopped onion and sliced mushrooms. Cook until the mushrooms are golden brown and the onions are soft and translucent. Add the minced garlic and cook for an additional minute, stirring constantly.

  3. Return the chicken to the pot and add the dried thyme and oregano. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Stir well to combine all the flavors.

  4. Add the chicken broth and bring it to a boil. Once boiling, add the fettuccine pasta and cook according to the package instructions, stirring occasionally to prevent sticking.

  5. Once the pasta is cooked al dente, reduce the heat to low and pour in the heavy cream. Stir in the grated Parmesan cheese until it’s melted and incorporated into the sauce. Allow the sauce to simmer for a few minutes, stirring occasionally, until it thickens slightly.

  6. Remove the pot from the heat and let it sit for a couple of minutes to allow the sauce to thicken further. Give it a final stir, and your One-Pot Creamy Chicken and Mushroom Pasta is ready to be served.

  7. To serve, divide the pasta into bowls or plates. Garnish with fresh parsley for a pop of color and added freshness. You can also sprinkle some additional Parmesan cheese on top, if desired.

Tips and Variations

  • For added flavor, consider marinating the chicken in a mixture of olive oil, garlic, and herbs for a few hours before cooking.

  • If you prefer a vegetarian option, you can easily omit the chicken and use vegetable broth instead of chicken broth.

  • Feel free to experiment with different types of mushrooms, such as cremini or shiitake, to add extra depth to the dish.

  • If you’re looking to make this recipe healthier, you can substitute heavy cream with half-and-half or a lighter cream alternative. Additionally, using whole wheat or gluten-free pasta is a great option for those with dietary restrictions.

  • Leftovers can be stored in an airtight container in the refrigerator for up to three days. Simply reheat in a pan with a splash of cream or broth to bring back the creaminess.

So there you have it, a delightful One-Pot Creamy Chicken and Mushroom Pasta recipe that’s sure to become a favorite in your household. Its gourmet flavors and simplicity make it a perfect choice for both casual weeknight dinners and special occasions. Enjoy the creamy goodness and savor every bite!
