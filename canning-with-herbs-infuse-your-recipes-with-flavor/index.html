Canning with Herbs: Infuse Your Recipes with Flavor


Canning is a great way to preserve the flavors and nutrients of fresh herbs, allowing you to enjoy them year-round. When done correctly, canned herbs can be a valuable addition to a variety of dishes, from soups and stews to sauces and marinades. In this article, we will explore the basics of canning with herbs and provide some tips and recipes to get you started.

基础知识

Before we dive into the world of canning with herbs, it’s essential to understand the basics of canning. Canning involves filling a clean, sterilized jar with food, and then sealing it in a hot water bath or pressure canner to kill off any bacteria and create a vacuum seal.

  • 选择适合罐头的食材：When selecting herbs for canning, choose fresh, fragrant, and healthy plants with no signs of disease or pests. Popular herbs for canning include basil, oregano, thyme, and rosemary.
  • 准备罐头材料：Before canning, prepare your jars, lids, and rings by washing them in hot soapy water, rinsing thoroughly, and sterilizing them in a pot of boiling water for 10-15 minutes.
  • 选择适合罐头的方法：There are two common methods for canning herbs: hot water bath canning and pressure canning. Hot water bath canning is suitable for high-acid foods like pickles and jams, while pressure canning is better for low-acid foods like meats and vegetables.

罐头方法

热水浴罐头

Hot water bath canning is a simple and effective method for canning herbs. Here’s a step-by-step guide:

  1. Prepare the herbs：Chop or tear the herbs into small pieces and pack them tightly into a clean, sterilized jar.
  2. Add liquid：Add a hot liquid, such as water or oil, to cover the herbs and leave about 1/4 inch of headspace at the top of the jar.
  3. Seal the jar：Place a lid and ring on the jar and tighten until they are just finger-tight.
  4. Process the jar：Place the jar in a large pot of boiling water, making sure that the water covers the jar by at least 1 inch. Process for 10-15 minutes.
  5. Let it cool：Remove the jar from the pot and let it cool to room temperature, undisturbed, for 12-24 hours.

压力罐头

Pressure canning is a more complex method that requires special equipment, but it’s necessary for canning low-acid foods like meats and vegetables. Here’s a step-by-step guide:

  1. Prepare the herbs：Chop or tear the herbs into small pieces and pack them tightly into a clean, sterilized jar.
  2. Add liquid：Add a hot liquid, such as water or oil, to cover the herbs and leave about 1/4 inch of headspace at the top of the jar.
  3. Seal the jar：Place a lid and ring on the jar and tighten until they are just finger-tight.
  4. Process the jar：Place the jar in a pressure canner and process at 10-15 pounds of pressure for 10-15 minutes.
  5. Let it cool：Remove the jar from the pressure canner and let it cool to room temperature, undisturbed, for 12-24 hours.

罐头配方

Here are some delicious and easy-to-make recipes for canning with herbs:

罐头 Basil

  • Ingredients：2 cups fresh basil leaves, 1 cup water, 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • Instructions：Pack the basil leaves into a clean, sterilized jar, leaving about 1/4 inch of headspace at the top. Add the water and olive oil. Seal the jar and process in a hot water bath for 10-15 minutes.

罐头 Oregano

  • Ingredients：2 cups fresh oregano leaves, 1 cup water, 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • Instructions：Pack the oregano leaves into a clean, sterilized jar, leaving about 1/4 inch of headspace at the top. Add the water and olive oil. Seal the jar and process in a hot water bath for 10-15 minutes.

罐头 Thyme

  • Ingredients：2 cups fresh thyme leaves, 1 cup water, 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • Instructions：Pack the thyme leaves into a clean, sterilized jar, leaving about 1/4 inch of headspace at the top. Add the water and olive oil. Seal the jar and process in a hot water bath for 10-15 minutes.

罐头 Rosemary

  • Ingredients：2 cups fresh rosemary leaves, 1 cup water, 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • Instructions：Pack the rosemary leaves into a clean, sterilized jar, leaving about 1/4 inch of headspace at the top. Add the water and olive oil. Seal the jar and process in a hot water bath for 10-15 minutes.

使用罐头

Once your canned herbs are ready, you can use them in a variety of dishes, from soups and stews to sauces and marinades. Here are some tips for using canned herbs:

  • Add flavor to soups and stews：Add a spoonful of canned herbs to your favorite soups and stews for a burst of fresh flavor.
  • Make sauces and marinades：Use canned herbs to make delicious sauces and marinades for meats, vegetables, and grains.
  • Enhance baked goods：Add a pinch of canned herbs to your favorite baked goods, such as breads, cakes, and cookies, for a subtle hint of flavor.

By following these tips and recipes, you can enjoy the flavors of fresh herbs year-round, and add a touch of elegance to your favorite dishes. Happy canning!
