Easy One Pot Garlic Butter Shrimp Pasta


If you’re looking for a quick and delicious meal that will impress your family or guests, look no further than this easy one pot garlic butter shrimp pasta recipe. This dish is a perfect combination of succulent shrimp, al dente pasta, and a creamy garlic butter sauce that will have everyone coming back for seconds. The best part? It can be made in just one pot, minimizing the cleanup and maximizing the flavor. Let’s dive into the details of this mouthwatering recipe.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound of large shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 12 ounces of linguine pasta
  • 4 tablespoons of unsalted butter
  • 4 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon of red pepper flakes (adjust to your spice preference)
  • 1 1/2 cups of chicken broth
  • 1 cup of heavy cream
  • 1/2 cup of freshly grated Parmesan cheese
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley, chopped, for garnish

Instructions

  1. Start by seasoning the shrimp with salt and black pepper. In a large pot or skillet, melt 2 tablespoons of butter over medium-high heat. Add the seasoned shrimp and cook for 1-2 minutes on each side until they turn pink and opaque. Once cooked, remove the shrimp from the pot and set aside.

  2. In the same pot, add the remaining 2 tablespoons of butter and minced garlic. Sauté the garlic for about 1 minute until fragrant, being careful not to let it burn. Sprinkle in the red pepper flakes for a hint of heat.

  3. Next, add the linguine to the pot, breaking the pasta in half if needed to fit. Pour in the chicken broth and heavy cream. Bring the mixture to a gentle boil, then reduce the heat to a simmer. Cook the pasta, stirring occasionally, for about 10-12 minutes or until it is al dente and the sauce has thickened.

  4. Once the pasta is cooked, add the cooked shrimp back into the pot. Stir in the freshly grated Parmesan cheese, allowing it to melt into the creamy sauce. Season with additional salt and pepper to taste.

  5. To finish, garnish the dish with chopped fresh parsley for a burst of color and freshness.

  6. Serve the garlic butter shrimp pasta immediately, and enjoy the delightful combination of flavors and textures.

Tips and Variations

  • For an extra burst of freshness, squeeze a bit of lemon juice over the finished dish.
  • If you prefer a lighter version, you can substitute the heavy cream with half-and-half for a slightly less rich sauce.
  • Feel free to customize the level of spice by adjusting the amount of red pepper flakes to suit your taste.

Conclusion

This easy one pot garlic butter shrimp pasta is a fantastic option for a satisfying weeknight dinner or a special occasion. With the convenience of cooking everything in one pot and the irresistible flavors of garlic, butter, and succulent shrimp, this dish is sure to become a family favorite. Whether you’re a novice cook or a seasoned chef, this recipe is a must-try for anyone who appreciates a simple yet elegant meal. So, roll up your sleeves and get ready to create a memorable dining experience with this delightful dish. Cheers to good food and great company!
