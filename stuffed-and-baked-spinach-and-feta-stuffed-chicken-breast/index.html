Stuffed and Baked: Spinach and Feta Stuffed Chicken Breast


If you’re looking for a dish that is both elegant and comforting, look no further than spinach and feta stuffed chicken breast. This delightful recipe combines the wholesome flavors of fresh spinach, tangy feta cheese, and juicy chicken breast, creating a meal that is not only delicious but also visually appealing. Perfect for a weeknight dinner or a special occasion, this dish is sure to impress your family and friends.

In this blog post, we will take you through the step-by-step process of making spinach and feta stuffed chicken breast, along with tips and tricks to ensure perfect results every time. From preparing the stuffing to baking the chicken to perfection, you’ll find everything you need to create this delectable dish right at home.

Ingredients

To make spinach and feta stuffed chicken breast, you will need the following ingredients:

For the Stuffing:

  • 2 cups fresh spinach, chopped (you can use frozen spinach, but make sure to thaw and drain it well)
  • 1 cup feta cheese, crumbled
  • 1/4 cup cream cheese, softened
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/4 teaspoon red pepper flakes (optional)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil

For the Chicken:

  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon onion powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil (for drizzling)

For Garnish:

  • Fresh parsley, chopped
  • Lemon wedges

Preparing the Stuffing

  1. Sauté the Spinach: Heat 1 tablespoon of olive oil in a skillet over medium heat. Add the minced garlic and sauté for about 30 seconds until fragrant. Add the chopped spinach and cook until wilted, about 2-3 minutes. If using frozen spinach, cook until heated through and any excess moisture has evaporated.

  2. Mix the Ingredients: In a mixing bowl, combine the sautéed spinach, crumbled feta cheese, softened cream cheese, grated Parmesan cheese, red pepper flakes, salt, and pepper. Mix until all the ingredients are well incorporated. The stuffing should be creamy and flavorful, with the spinach and feta providing a delicious contrast.

Preparing the Chicken Breasts

  1. Preheat the Oven: Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C). This ensures that the chicken cooks evenly and thoroughly.

  2. Prepare the Chicken: Place the chicken breasts on a cutting board. Using a sharp knife, carefully cut a pocket into each chicken breast. Make sure not to cut all the way through; you want to create a space to hold the stuffing.

  3. Season the Chicken: In a small bowl, mix together paprika, garlic powder, onion powder, salt, and pepper. Rub this seasoning mix all over the chicken breasts, including inside the pockets. This will add flavor to the chicken itself.

Stuffing the Chicken

  1. Fill the Pockets: Spoon the prepared spinach and feta stuffing into each chicken breast pocket. Be generous, but ensure that you do not overstuff, as the filling may leak out during baking.

  2. Secure the Filling: To keep the stuffing inside, use toothpicks to secure the openings of the chicken breasts. Alternatively, you can use kitchen twine to tie the chicken breasts closed.

Baking the Chicken

  1. Prepare the Baking Dish: Lightly grease a baking dish with cooking spray or a little olive oil to prevent sticking. Place the stuffed chicken breasts in the baking dish.

  2. Drizzle with Olive Oil: Drizzle the tops of the stuffed chicken breasts with olive oil to help them brown and create a crispy exterior.

  3. Bake: Place the baking dish in the preheated oven and bake for about 30-35 minutes, or until the chicken is cooked through and reaches an internal temperature of 165°F (75°C). If you want a golden-brown top, you can broil the chicken for the last 2-3 minutes of cooking, keeping a close eye to avoid burning.

Serving Suggestions

Once your spinach and feta stuffed chicken breasts are done baking, remove them from the oven and let them rest for about 5 minutes. This allows the juices to redistribute, ensuring a moist and flavorful chicken.

Plating

  1. Slice and Serve: Carefully remove the toothpicks or twine from the chicken breasts. Slice them in half to reveal the beautiful stuffing inside. Arrange the sliced chicken on a serving platter.

  2. Garnish: Sprinkle freshly chopped parsley over the top for a pop of color and freshness. Serve with lemon wedges on the side for an extra burst of flavor.

Pairing Ideas

Spinach and feta stuffed chicken breast pairs wonderfully with a variety of side dishes. Here are some ideas to complement your meal:

  • Roasted Vegetables: Toss seasonal vegetables like zucchini, bell peppers, and carrots in olive oil, salt, and pepper, and roast them in the oven alongside your chicken for a colorful and nutritious side.

  • Rice or Quinoa: Serve the chicken over a bed of fluffy rice or quinoa to soak up the delicious juices from the chicken. You can also add herbs or spices to the grains for added flavor.

  • Salad: A light garden salad with mixed greens, cherry tomatoes, and a simple vinaigrette offers a refreshing contrast to the rich flavors of the stuffed chicken.

Tips for Success

  • Choose the Right Chicken: When selecting chicken breasts, look for ones that are uniform in size to ensure even cooking. If you can find organic or free-range chicken, it will enhance the flavor of your dish.

  • Don’t Rush the Stuffing: Take your time when preparing the stuffing. Sautéing the spinach properly and mixing the cheeses thoroughly will ensure a delicious filling.

  • Check for Doneness: Always use a meat thermometer to check the internal temperature of the chicken. This prevents undercooking or overcooking, which can result in dry chicken.

  • Experiment with Flavors: Feel free to customize the stuffing to your liking. Add sun-dried tomatoes, olives, or different herbs to create your own unique flavor combinations.

Conclusion

Spinach and feta stuffed chicken breast is a delightful dish that brings together the goodness of fresh ingredients and the satisfying flavors of cheese and chicken. Whether you’re cooking for your family or hosting a dinner party, this recipe is sure to become a favorite.

With its beautiful presentation and mouthwatering taste, it’s a meal that celebrates the art of home cooking. So gather your ingredients, roll up your sleeves, and enjoy the process of creating this delicious dish. Your taste buds will thank you!

Take your time to savor each step and enjoy the journey of cooking. Happy cooking!
