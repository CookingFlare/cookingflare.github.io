Instant Pot Comfort Food Classics: Mac and Cheese, Pot Roast, and More


When hunger calls for a hearty, soul-warming meal, few options bring as much satisfaction as classic comfort foods. From creamy mac and cheese to melt-in-your-mouth pot roast, these dishes transcend trends, speaking to the desire for meals that feel like a warm hug. What makes the experience even better is the Instant Pot—a versatile kitchen tool that saves time while delivering exceptional flavors. If you’re looking to elevate your cooking game without spending hours in the kitchen, this guide is for you. Let’s dive into some timeless comfort food recipes that you can master with the Instant Pot.

Creamy Instant Pot Mac and Cheese

Is there any dish more universally loved than mac and cheese? Whether you’re feeding kids, entertaining guests, or treating yourself to a cozy evening at home, this creamy, gooey delight always hits the spot. The Instant Pot makes it faster and easier than ever to whip up a batch that’s rich and flavorful with minimal effort.

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound elbow macaroni
  • 4 cups water or chicken broth (for added flavor)
  • 2 tablespoons unsalted butter
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon ground mustard
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 2 cups sharp cheddar cheese, shredded
  • 1 cup parmesan cheese, grated
  • 1 cup whole milk
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • Freshly ground pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Cook the Pasta: Pour the macaroni, water (or broth), salt, mustard, and garlic powder into the Instant Pot. Stir to prevent sticking. Seal the lid, ensuring the vent is set to “Sealing,” and cook on high pressure for 4 minutes. Once the timer beeps, perform a quick release.
  2. Add Cream and Cheese: Open the lid, stir the pasta, and turn on the sauté function. Add the butter, milk, heavy cream, and cheeses a little at a time, stirring until they melt into a luscious sauce. Season with pepper for an extra kick.
  3. Serve Warm: Serve immediately and watch everyone fall in love with your creamy, cheesy creation.

This recipe is endlessly customizable. Add cooked bacon, breadcrumbs, spinach, or even a sprinkle of hot sauce to suit your taste.

Instant Pot Pot Roast: A Classic, Simplified

Few dishes define comfort like pot roast. The combination of tender beef, rich gravy, and hearty vegetables creates a medley of flavors that feels timeless. Traditionally, pot roast takes hours to cook, but with the Instant Pot, you can achieve the same depth of flavor in a fraction of the time.

Ingredients:

  • 3-4 pound chuck roast
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • 1 teaspoon smoked paprika
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 onion, roughly chopped
  • 4 carrots, peeled and chopped
  • 3 potatoes, peeled and cut into large cubes
  • 2 cups beef broth
  • 1 cup red wine (optional but highly recommended)
  • 2 tablespoons tomato paste
  • 2 tablespoons cornstarch mixed with 2 tablespoons cold water (for thickening)

Instructions:

  1. Sear the Meat: Set your Instant Pot to sauté mode and heat the olive oil. Season the roast with salt, pepper, thyme, and smoked paprika. Sear the beef on all sides until browned, about 4-5 minutes per side, then remove it and set aside.
  2. Sauté the Aromatics: Add the garlic and onion to the pot, stirring until fragrant. Add the tomato paste, cooking for another minute.
  3. Deglaze the Pot: Gradually pour in the beef broth and wine, scraping the bottom of the pot to loosen any browned bits. This adds depth to your gravy.
  4. Pressure Cook: Return the roast to the pot and add the carrots and potatoes. Seal the lid and set to high pressure for 60 minutes. Allow a natural release for 15 minutes before opening the lid.
  5. Make the Gravy: Remove the roast and vegetables, leaving the liquid in the pot. Turn the Instant Pot to sauté mode and thicken the liquid with the cornstarch mixture, whisking until it turns into a rich gravy.
  6. Serve: Slice the roast, surround it with the veggies, and drizzle with gravy for the ultimate comfort meal.

Round out the meal with buttery dinner rolls or a side salad.

Instant Pot Chicken and Dumplings

No comfort food roundup is complete without chicken and dumplings. This classic dish combines tender chunks of chicken in a creamy broth topped with pillowy soft dumplings. It’s a one-pot meal that delivers true home-cooked nostalgia.

Ingredients:

  • 1 tablespoon unsalted butter
  • 1 pound boneless, skinless chicken thighs or breasts, cubed
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 2 celery stalks, chopped
  • 2 carrots, chopped
  • 2 teaspoons fresh thyme (or 1 teaspoon dried)
  • 4 cups chicken broth
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • 1 cup frozen peas
  • Salt and pepper to taste

For Dumplings:

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 tablespoon baking powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup whole milk
  • 2 tablespoons unsalted butter, melted

Instructions:

  1. Sauté the Chicken: Turn the Instant Pot to sauté mode and melt the butter. Add the chicken pieces, cooking until lightly browned on all sides. Remove and set aside.
  2. Build the Base: Add the onion, celery, and carrots to the pot and sauté for 2-3 minutes until fragrant. Add the thyme and pour in the chicken broth, scraping the bottom to deglaze. Return the chicken to the pot.
  3. Pressure Cook: Seal the lid and cook on high pressure for 8 minutes. Perform a quick release and carefully open the lid.
  4. Add Cream and Peas: Turn the pot back to sauté mode and stir in the heavy cream and peas, allowing the mixture to simmer gently.
  5. Make the Dumplings: In a separate bowl, whisk together the dumpling ingredients until you have a thick batter. Drop spoonfuls of the batter directly into the simmering broth.
  6. Steam the Dumplings: Cover the Instant Pot with its lid (not sealed) and let the dumplings steam for 10-12 minutes until fluffy and cooked through.
  7. Serve Warm: Ladle the chicken, vegetables, broth, and dumplings into bowls for a comforting, hearty meal.

Instant Pot Mashed Potatoes

Perfect as a side dish to any of the above recipes, mashed potatoes are another beloved classic. With an Instant Pot, you can enjoy smooth, creamy potatoes in less time and with less effort.

Ingredients:

  • 2 pounds Yukon Gold or Russet potatoes, peeled and cubed
  • 1 cup water
  • 1/2 cup whole milk
  • 4 tablespoons unsalted butter
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Cook the Potatoes: Place the potatoes and water in the Instant Pot. Seal the lid and cook on high pressure for 8 minutes. Perform a quick release once the timer ends.
  2. Mash and Season: Drain the potatoes, then mash them directly in the pot. Stir in milk, butter, salt, and pepper until creamy and smooth.
  3. Serve Fresh: Serve immediately for a delicious, no-fail accompaniment to any comfort meal.

Whether you’re craving mac and cheese, pot roast, or chicken and dumplings, the Instant Pot is your ticket to creating mouthwatering comfort food favorites with ease. These recipes bring together the essence of cozy, home-cooked meals while streamlining the process so you can spend less time in the kitchen and more time enjoying the warmth of these timeless dishes.
