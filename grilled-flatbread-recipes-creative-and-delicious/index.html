Grilled Flatbread Recipes: Creative and Delicious


Grilled flatbread is a versatile and delicious option for any meal. Its crispy exterior and soft, chewy interior make it the perfect canvas for a wide range of flavors. Whether you’re looking for a simple appetizer, a hearty main course, or a delightful dessert, grilled flatbread has got you covered. In this post, we’ll explore some creative and mouthwatering grilled flatbread recipes that will elevate your cooking game and impress your family and friends.

Grilled Flatbread with Pesto and Cherry Tomatoes

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound pizza dough
  • 1/2 cup store-bought or homemade pesto
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1/2 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Olive oil, for brushing
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh basil leaves for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your grill to medium-high heat.
  2. Divide the pizza dough into four equal portions and roll each portion into a thin circle.
  3. Brush one side of each flatbread with olive oil and place them on the grill, oiled side down.
  4. Grill for 2-3 minutes until the flatbread develops grill marks.
  5. Brush the top side of the flatbread with olive oil, then flip them over.
  6. Spread a generous dollop of pesto on the grilled side of each flatbread.
  7. Sprinkle the mozzarella cheese evenly over the pesto, then top with the cherry tomatoes.
  8. Close the grill lid and cook for an additional 3-4 minutes until the cheese is melted and bubbly.
  9. Remove the flatbread from the grill, season with salt and pepper, and garnish with fresh basil leaves.
  10. Slice and serve immediately.

Grilled Flatbread with Fig, Prosciutto, and Arugula

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound pizza dough
  • 1/4 cup fig jam
  • 4 slices prosciutto
  • 1 cup arugula
  • Balsamic glaze for drizzling
  • Olive oil, for brushing

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your grill to medium-high heat.
  2. Divide the pizza dough into two equal portions and roll each portion into a thin oval shape.
  3. Brush one side of each flatbread with olive oil and place them on the grill, oiled side down.
  4. Grill for 2-3 minutes until the flatbread develops grill marks.
  5. Brush the top side of the flatbread with olive oil, then flip them over.
  6. Spread a thin layer of fig jam on the grilled side of each flatbread.
  7. Tear the prosciutto into smaller pieces and distribute them evenly over the fig jam.
  8. Close the grill lid and cook for an additional 3-4 minutes until the prosciutto crisps up.
  9. Remove the flatbread from the grill and top with a handful of arugula.
  10. Drizzle with balsamic glaze, slice, and serve immediately.

Grilled Flatbread with Nutella and Fresh Berries

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound pizza dough
  • 1/2 cup Nutella
  • 1 cup fresh berries (strawberries, raspberries, blueberries)
  • Powdered sugar for dusting

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your grill to medium-high heat.
  2. Divide the pizza dough into four equal portions and roll each portion into a thin circle.
  3. Brush one side of each flatbread with olive oil and place them on the grill, oiled side down.
  4. Grill for 2-3 minutes until the flatbread develops grill marks.
  5. Brush the top side of the flatbread with olive oil, then flip them over.
  6. Spread a generous layer of Nutella on the grilled side of each flatbread.
  7. Arrange the fresh berries on top of the Nutella.
  8. Close the grill lid and cook for an additional 2-3 minutes until the Nutella is warmed and the berries are slightly softened.
  9. Remove the flatbread from the grill, dust with powdered sugar, slice, and serve immediately.

These grilled flatbread recipes are not only delicious but also a fun way to get creative in the kitchen. Whether you’re hosting a summer barbecue or simply craving a unique and flavorful meal, these recipes are sure to delight your taste buds. Experiment with different toppings and flavors to create your own signature grilled flatbread masterpiece. Happy grilling!
