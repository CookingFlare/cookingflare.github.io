Slow Cooker Sweet and Sour Meatballs: A Tangy Delight


Are you in the mood for a delicious and tangy dish that will leave your taste buds dancing? Look no further than these Slow Cooker Sweet and Sour Meatballs! This recipe is perfect for those busy days when you want a flavorful meal without spending hours in the kitchen. With just a few simple ingredients and your trusty slow cooker, you can create a mouthwatering dish that will impress both family and friends. So, let’s get started!

Ingredients

To make these delectable Sweet and Sour Meatballs, you will need:

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1/2 cup breadcrumbs
  • 1/4 cup milk
  • 1/4 cup finely chopped onion
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 1 egg, beaten
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon pepper
  • 1 cup pineapple chunks, drained (reserve the juice)
  • 1 green bell pepper, diced
  • 1 red bell pepper, diced
  • 1/2 cup brown sugar
  • 1/4 cup apple cider vinegar
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon cornstarch
  • 1 tablespoon cold water

Instructions

  1. In a large bowl, combine the ground beef, breadcrumbs, milk, onion, garlic, egg, salt, and pepper. Mix until well combined. Shape the mixture into bite-sized meatballs, approximately 1 inch in diameter.

  2. Heat a large skillet over medium-high heat and add the meatballs. Cook until browned on all sides, about 5 minutes. Transfer the meatballs to your slow cooker.

  3. In a separate bowl, whisk together the reserved pineapple juice, brown sugar, apple cider vinegar, and soy sauce. Pour the mixture over the meatballs in the slow cooker. Add the pineapple chunks, diced green bell pepper, and diced red bell pepper. Gently stir to combine.

  4. Cover the slow cooker and cook on low heat for 4-6 hours, or until the meatballs are cooked through and the flavors have melded together.

  5. In the last 30 minutes of cooking, combine the cornstarch and cold water in a small bowl to make a slurry. Stir the slurry into the slow cooker and continue cooking until the sauce has thickened.

  6. Serve the Sweet and Sour Meatballs over cooked rice or noodles, and garnish with chopped green onions, if desired. Enjoy the tangy delight that is sure to satisfy your cravings!

Tips and Variations

  • If you prefer a spicier version, add a dash of hot sauce or red pepper flakes to the sauce mixture.
  • For a healthier twist, you can use ground turkey or chicken instead of ground beef.
  • Feel free to customize the vegetables to your liking. You can add carrots, snap peas, or even water chestnuts for extra crunch.
  • If you’re short on time, you can skip browning the meatballs and simply place them directly in the slow cooker. However, browning them adds a nice texture and flavor.
  • Leftovers can be stored in an airtight container in the refrigerator for up to 3 days. They can also be frozen for up to 3 months.

Now that you have the recipe for these Slow Cooker Sweet and Sour Meatballs, it’s time to gather your ingredients and get cooking! Whether you’re serving them as an appetizer at a party or as a main course for a weeknight dinner, these tangy meatballs are sure to be a hit. So, grab your slow cooker and let the flavors meld together to create a truly delightful dish. Enjoy!
