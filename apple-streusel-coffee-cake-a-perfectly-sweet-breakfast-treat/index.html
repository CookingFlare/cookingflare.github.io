Apple Streusel Coffee Cake: A Perfectly Sweet Breakfast Treat


Are you looking for a delightful and indulgent breakfast treat that will fill your home with the warm, comforting aroma of cinnamon and apples? Look no further than this delicious recipe for Apple Streusel Coffee Cake! Whether you’re hosting a brunch, celebrating a special occasion, or simply craving a cozy morning indulgence, this delightful cake is sure to please.

Ingredients

For the Cake:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 cup sour cream
  • 2 cups chopped apples (such as Granny Smith or Honeycrisp)

For the Streusel Topping:

  • 1/2 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup packed brown sugar
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 cup unsalted butter, melted

For the Glaze (optional):

  • 1/2 cup powdered sugar
  • 1-2 tablespoons milk
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease and flour a 9-inch round springform pan.

  2. In a medium bowl, whisk together the flour, baking powder, baking soda, and salt for the cake. Set aside.

  3. In a large mixing bowl, cream together the softened butter and granulated sugar until light and fluffy. Add the eggs, one at a time, beating well after each addition. Stir in the vanilla extract.

  4. Gradually add the dry ingredients to the creamed mixture, alternating with the sour cream. Mix well after each addition.

  5. Gently fold in the chopped apples until evenly distributed throughout the batter.

  6. In a separate bowl, prepare the streusel topping by combining the flour, brown sugar, and cinnamon. Drizzle the melted butter over the mixture and stir until crumbly.

  7. Spread half of the cake batter into the prepared pan. Sprinkle half of the streusel topping over the batter. Repeat with the remaining batter and streusel topping.

  8. Bake for 45-50 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center of the cake comes out clean. Allow the cake to cool in the pan for 10 minutes before removing the sides of the springform pan.

  9. If desired, prepare the glaze by whisking together the powdered sugar, milk, and vanilla extract. Drizzle the glaze over the warm coffee cake before serving.

Serving Suggestions

This Apple Streusel Coffee Cake is best enjoyed fresh and warm, paired with a steaming cup of your favorite coffee or tea. The tender crumb, juicy apple pieces, and irresistible streusel topping come together to create a delightful symphony of flavors and textures.

Conclusion

Whether you’re a seasoned baker or just starting your culinary journey, this Apple Streusel Coffee Cake is a fantastic recipe to add to your collection. Its simplicity, versatility, and irresistible appeal make it a go-to choice for any breakfast or brunch gathering. So, roll up your sleeves, preheat your oven, and get ready to savor the sweet, comforting goodness of this delightful coffee cake!
