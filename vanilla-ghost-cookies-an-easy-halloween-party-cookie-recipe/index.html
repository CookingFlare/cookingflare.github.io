Vanilla Ghost Cookies: An Easy Halloween Party Cookie Recipe


Looking for a fun and easy Halloween cookie recipe that’s sure to impress your guests? Look no further than these delightful Vanilla Ghost Cookies! With just a few simple ingredients and minimal preparation time, these tasty treats will be a hit at any spooky Halloween party. Feast your eyes on this simple recipe and whip up a ghostly spread in no time!

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1 1/2 cups granulated sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 1/2 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • Red food coloring (optional)
  • Black icing or cookie icing (for the ghostly eyes)

Instructions:

  1. In a large bowl, cream together the softened butter and granulated sugar until light and fluffy, using an electric mixer. This step ensures that the butter and sugar are well combined, creating moist and delicious cookies.

  2. Add the eggs one at a time, beating well after each addition. Incorporating the eggs gradually ensures even distribution and a smooth mixture.

  3. In a separate bowl, combine the flour, baking powder, and salt. Gradually add this dry mixture to the wet ingredients, stirring to combine until just incorporated. Be careful not to overmix, as this can result in tough cookies.

  4. Add the vanilla extract to the cookie dough and mix until well combined. If you want your cookies to have a more pronounced ghostly appearance, add a few drops of red food coloring to the dough. Mix until the desired color is reached, being cautious not to add too much as it can alter the cookies’ taste.

  5. Wrap the dough in plastic wrap and refrigerate for at least 1 hour. Chilling the dough makes it easier to work with and prevents the cookies from spreading too much in the oven.

  6. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C) and line two baking sheets with parchment paper.

  7. Once chilled, scoop out the dough in teaspoon-sized portions and roll them into balls with your hands. Place the dough balls on the prepared baking sheets, about 2 inches apart.

  8. Bake the cookies for 10-12 minutes or until the edges are lightly golden. Rotate the baking sheets halfway through the baking time for even baking.

  9. Remove the cookies from the oven and let them cool on the baking sheets for 5 minutes before transferring them to a wire rack to cool completely.

  10. Once the cookies have cooled, it’s time to get creative with your ghostly decorations! You can use black icing or cookie icing to create ghostly eyes. Draw two small ovals slightly off-center for each cookie to achieve that eerie, ghostly appearance.

  11. Allow the icing to set, preferably for about 30 minutes, before enjoying your ghostly Vanilla Ghost Cookies. This ensures your decorations hold their shape as you indulge in these delicious Halloween treats.

Your Vanilla Ghost Cookies are now ready to be served at your Halloween shindig! Watch in delight as your guests whip up endless “oohs” and “aahs” as they bite into these spooky, sweet, and easy-to-make Halloween cookies. Enjoy!

The Perfect Presentation:

To make your Vanilla Ghost Cookies an instant showstopper at your party, consider placing them in clear cellophane bags tied with black or orange ribbon. You can also arrange them on a black or orange plate surrounded by artificial spiderwebs and small plastic skeletons for an added touch of Halloween charm. By showcasing these appealing treats, you’re sure to make your Halloween celebration one that’s positively frightful with ghoulish delight!

Party planning and cooking can often feel overwhelming, but with this simple and straightforward Vanilla Ghost Cookies recipe, you’re now one step closer to hosting a Halloween bash that’s not only enjoyable but stress-free. Treat your friends and family to these delightful ghostly sweets this Halloween season, and don’t forget to capture the mouthwatering memories for a year of spooky sweet nostalgia. Happy baking!
