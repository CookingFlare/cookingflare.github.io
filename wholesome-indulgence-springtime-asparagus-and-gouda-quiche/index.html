Wholesome Indulgence: Springtime Asparagus and Gouda Quiche


Are you ready to treat your taste buds to a delightful springtime indulgence? Look no further than this exquisite Springtime Asparagus and Gouda Quiche. Bursting with the fresh flavors of seasonal asparagus and the creamy richness of Gouda cheese, this quiche is the perfect addition to a brunch spread or a light dinner. Let’s dive into the recipe and discover how to create this wholesome indulgence in your own kitchen.

Ingredients

  • 1 9-inch pie crust
  • 1 pound fresh asparagus, trimmed and cut into 1-inch pieces
  • 1 ½ cups shredded Gouda cheese
  • 5 large eggs
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1 teaspoon olive oil
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley for garnish

Instructions

  1. Preheat and Prep: Start by preheating your oven to 375°F (190°C). Place the pie crust in a 9-inch pie dish and set it aside.

  2. Sauté the Asparagus: Heat the olive oil in a skillet over medium heat. Add the chopped onion and minced garlic, and sauté until they are soft and fragrant. Then, add the asparagus pieces and sauté for an additional 5-7 minutes, or until the asparagus is tender-crisp. Season with salt and pepper to taste.

  3. Prepare the Custard Filling: In a mixing bowl, whisk together the eggs and heavy cream until well combined. Stir in the shredded Gouda cheese and the sautéed asparagus mixture.

  4. Assemble and Bake: Pour the custard filling into the prepared pie crust. Gently shake the dish to distribute the filling evenly. Place the quiche in the preheated oven and bake for 35-40 minutes, or until the filling is set and the top is golden brown.

  5. Serve and Enjoy: Once the quiche is done, remove it from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes. Garnish with fresh parsley before slicing and serving.

Tips for Success

  • Fresh is Best: When it comes to asparagus, using fresh, seasonal spears will elevate the flavor of the quiche. Look for firm, bright green asparagus with tightly closed tips.

  • Customize Your Quiche: Feel free to add your own personal touch to this recipe. Consider incorporating other seasonal vegetables like spinach or leeks for added depth of flavor.

  • Make-Ahead Option: This quiche can be made ahead of time and refrigerated. Simply reheat individual slices in the microwave for a quick and satisfying meal.

Final Thoughts

With its buttery crust, tender asparagus, and melty Gouda, this Springtime Asparagus and Gouda Quiche is a celebration of the season’s bounty. Whether you’re hosting a spring brunch or craving a light and flavorful dinner, this quiche is sure to impress. Embrace the freshness of spring and savor each delightful bite of this wholesome indulgence.

So, gather your ingredients, preheat your oven, and let the aroma of this delightful quiche fill your kitchen. It’s time to elevate your springtime dining experience with a dish that embodies the essence of wholesome indulgence. Enjoy every moment of creating and savoring this delectable Springtime Asparagus and Gouda Quiche.
