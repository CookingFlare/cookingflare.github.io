Creamy Slow Cooker Chicken Alfredo: A Pasta Lover's Dream


There’s something undeniably comforting about a creamy, indulgent plate of chicken alfredo. The combination of tender chicken, rich alfredo sauce, and perfectly cooked pasta is a classic favorite for good reason. But what if I told you that you could achieve that same level of decadence with minimal effort, thanks to your trusty slow cooker? That’s right – today, we’re diving into the world of slow cooker chicken alfredo, a dish that will elevate your pasta game to new heights.

Slow Cooker Magic

One of the best things about using a slow cooker for this recipe is the hands-off approach it allows. Simply toss in a few key ingredients, set it, and forget it until you’re ready to indulge in a delicious, homemade meal. The slow cooker works its magic by infusing the chicken with flavor and creating a luscious, creamy sauce that clings to every strand of pasta.

The Perfect Pairing

When it comes to choosing the right pasta for your slow cooker chicken alfredo, opt for fettuccine or linguine. These thicker pasta shapes are ideal for standing up to the rich sauce and hearty chicken, ensuring that every bite is a perfect balance of flavors and textures. Don’t forget to generously salt the water when boiling the pasta – this is your chance to infuse the noodles with flavor from the inside out.

Ingredients:

  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 2 cups heavy cream
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • ½ cup unsalted butter
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 12 ounces fettuccine or linguine
  • Fresh parsley, for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Place the chicken breasts in the slow cooker.
  2. In a saucepan over medium heat, combine the heavy cream, Parmesan cheese, butter, minced garlic, dried oregano, and dried basil. Cook, stirring occasionally, until the sauce is smooth and heated through.
  3. Pour the sauce over the chicken in the slow cooker. Season with salt and pepper.
  4. Cover and cook on low for 4-5 hours, or until the chicken is cooked through and tender.
  5. About 30 minutes before serving, cook the fettuccine or linguine according to the package instructions. Drain well.
  6. Using two forks, shred the chicken in the slow cooker. Return the shredded chicken to the slow cooker and stir to coat with the sauce.
  7. Serve the creamy slow cooker chicken alfredo over the cooked pasta, garnished with fresh parsley.

Tips for Success

To take your slow cooker chicken alfredo to the next level, consider adding a handful of fresh spinach or a sprinkle of red pepper flakes for a pop of color and flavor. Additionally, if you prefer a thicker sauce, you can mix a tablespoon of cornstarch with a tablespoon of water and stir it into the slow cooker during the last 30 minutes of cooking.

A Crowd-Pleasing Favorite

Whether you’re hosting a casual weeknight dinner or entertaining a crowd, creamy slow cooker chicken alfredo is a guaranteed crowd-pleaser. The rich, velvety sauce and tender chicken will have everyone coming back for seconds, and the best part is that you’ll have plenty of time to mingle with your guests instead of being tied to the stove.

So, the next time you’re craving a comforting bowl of pasta, let your slow cooker do the heavy lifting and treat yourself to a bowl of creamy slow cooker chicken alfredo. It’s a pasta lover’s dream come true.
