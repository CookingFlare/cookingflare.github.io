Smoothies, Bowls, and Popsicles: 20 Fresh Vegan Recipes to Cool Down This Summer


As the mercury soars during these hot summer months, there’s nothing more refreshing than indulging in a cool, creamy, and nutrient-packed treat to satiate your cravings. What better way to quench your thirst than by making your own homemade vegan smoothies, bowls, and popsicles?

In this post, we present you with 20 mouth-watering vegan recipes that are perfect for cooling down on warm days. Ranging from invigorating blue spirulina smoothies to luscious acai bowls and delightful mango-raspberry popsicles, these treats are as good for your body as they are for your soul. Give them a try and elevate your summer!

Green Goddess Smoothie Bowl

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup spinach
  • 1/2 cup unsweetened almond milk
  • 1/2 cup frozen pineapple chunks
  • 1/2 avocado
  • 1 tbsp chia seeds
  • 1 tbsp almond butter
  • Toppings: shredded coconut, goji berries, cacao nibs

Instructions:

  1. To a blender, add the spinach and almond milk, blend until smooth.
  2. Add the pineapple, avocado, and blend again until creamy and uniform.
  3. Transfer the mixture into a bowl, stir in the chia seeds and almond butter until thoroughly mixed.
  4. Garnish with shredded coconut, goji berries, and cacao nibs before serving.

Peach Mango Matcha Smoothie

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup frozen sliced peaches
  • 1 cup frozen mango chunks
  • 1 tsp matcha green tea powder
  • 1/2 cup unsweetened coconut milk
  • 1 banana
  • 1 tbsp agave syrup

Instructions:

  1. Blend the frozen peaches, mango chunks, matcha, coconut milk, banana, and agave syrup in a blender until smooth.
  2. Pour the mixture into glasses and enjoy immediately.

Açaí Coconut Bowl

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup frozen açaí berries
  • 1/2 cup unsweetened almond milk
  • 1/2 cup unsweetened coconut flakes
  • 1/2 avocado
  • 1 banana
  • Toppings: granola, sliced almonds, dried banana chips, shredded coconut

Instructions:

  1. Blend the açaí berries and almond milk in a blender until smooth. Pour the mixture into a bowl.
  2. Mash the avocado and add it on top of the açaí puree.
  3. Slice the banana lengthwise and place the slices around the bowl.
  4. Cover the bowl generously with coconut flakes, then sprinkle with granola, sliced almonds, dried banana chips, and shredded coconut.

Mango Raspberry Burst Popsicles

Ingredients:

  • 4 cups frozen mango chunks
  • 2 cups frozen raspberries
  • 2 ripe avocados
  • 1/2 cup freshly-squeezed orange juice
  • 1/2 cup coconut water

Instructions:

  1. In a blender, add the frozen mango chunks, frozen raspberries, avocado, orange juice, and coconut water.
  2. Blend until completely smooth. Pour the mixture into popsicle molds.
  3. Freeze for at least 4 hours or until solid. Enjoy your refreshing vegan popsicles!

Ultimately, the world of vegan summer treats is extensive and mind-blowing, with limitless variations of flavors, textures, and ingredient combinations. Experiment and experience the incredible diversity of raw vegan cuisine in your own kitchen with these 20 fresh and scrumptious recipes. Cheers to a cool and nourishing summer season!
