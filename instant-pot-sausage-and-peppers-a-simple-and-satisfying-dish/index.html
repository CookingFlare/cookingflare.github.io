Instant Pot Sausage and Peppers: A Simple and Satisfying Dish


Few meals combine simplicity and bold flavors quite like sausage and peppers. This timeless combination resonates with food enthusiasts who crave hearty, satisfying meals that don’t require hours of effort. With the Instant Pot taking center stage in kitchens everywhere, it’s time to reimagine this classic dish with the magic of pressure cooking. From the savory richness of the sausage to the tender sweetness of the peppers, this recipe is a go-to for busy weeknights or cozy family dinners.

Why Make Sausage and Peppers in the Instant Pot?

The Instant Pot has revolutionized home cooking, and its versatility shines with dishes like sausage and peppers. Traditional preparation often involves stovetop monitoring, lengthy oven times, and constant stirring. The Instant Pot eliminates the guesswork while locking in all the flavors.

Cooking sausage and peppers in a pressure cooker ensures juicy sausages and beautifully softened veggies, infused with aromatic seasonings. Whether you’re serving it with crusty bread, over polenta, or tossed with pasta, this Instant Pot version is quick, flavorful, and far less messy than classic cooking methods.

Key Ingredients for a Flavorful Dish

While sausage and peppers have countless regional and personal variations, it’s worth curating the best ingredients to elevate this dish. Here’s a breakdown of what you’ll need:

Sausages

Choose sausages based on your desired flavor profile. Sweet or mild Italian sausage is a classic choice, but spicy Italian sausages can add a fiery kick. If you prefer a healthier option, chicken sausage works beautifully, retaining a light yet satisfying touch.

Bell Peppers

The dish transforms with the vibrant trio of red, yellow, and green bell peppers. Their sweetness and texture complement the savory sausage. Feel free to add orange peppers for an extra layer of color and taste.

Onions

Thinly sliced onions add depth and aroma to your dish. A mix of red or yellow onions works wonders, as they take on a lovely caramelized note when cooked under pressure.

Garlic

Fresh garlic cloves, minced or sliced, are essential for rich undertones. Garlic infuses into both the vegetables and sausage, amplifying every bite.

Tomatoes

Canned diced tomatoes or crushed tomatoes are your source of saucy goodness. They create a luscious base that mingles perfectly with the other ingredients.

Seasonings

To savor that signature Italian flair, use a mix of dried oregano, basil, and thyme. A pinch of red pepper flakes adds subtle heat, while salt and pepper bring balance to the overall taste.

Broth or Wine

For lush flavor, deglaze your Instant Pot with chicken broth or dry white wine. These liquids expand the flavor profile while preventing food from sticking during the cooking process.

Step-by-Step Recipe: Instant Pot Sausage and Peppers

Let’s dive into this simple, delicious recipe that’s bound to become a staple in your kitchen.

Prep Time: 15 minutes

Cook Time: 30 minutes

Total Time: 45 minutes

Servings: 4–6

Ingredients:

  • 1-2 tbsp olive oil
  • 5–6 Italian sausages (sweet, spicy, or chicken)
  • 1 large green bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 large red bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 large yellow bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 medium red onion, thinly sliced
  • 3–4 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 can (14 oz) crushed or diced tomatoes
  • ½ cup chicken broth or dry white wine
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • 1 tsp dried basil
  • ½ tsp dried thyme
  • ¼ tsp red pepper flakes (optional)
  • Salt and freshly ground black pepper, to taste

Instructions:

1. Brown the Sausages

Set your Instant Pot to sauté mode and heat olive oil for 1–2 minutes. Add the sausages, allowing them to brown evenly for about 2–3 minutes per side. This step seals in the juices while creating crispy edges. Once browned, remove the sausages and set them aside.

2. Sauté the Vegetables

Add the sliced onions and garlic to the pot, stirring occasionally until they’re soft and fragrant. Incorporate the bell peppers and cook for another 2–3 minutes. This brief sauté process heightens the flavor of the vegetables.

3. Deglaze the Pot

Pour chicken broth or white wine into the Instant Pot to deglaze. Scrape off any browned bits stuck to the bottom of the pot using a wooden spoon—these bits hold the secret to deep, rich flavors.

4. Layer Ingredients

Return the browned sausages to the pot, nestling them among the sautéed vegetables. Pour the canned tomatoes over the top. Sprinkle oregano, basil, thyme, and red pepper flakes (if using). Season everything with salt and black pepper.

5. Pressure Cook

Seal the Instant Pot lid and ensure the vent is set to the “sealing” position. Select the manual or pressure cook button and set it to 6 minutes on high pressure.

6. Quick Release

Once the cooking cycle ends, do a quick release by carefully turning the vent to “venting.” Open the lid once the pressure has fully released.

7. Serve and Enjoy

Give the dish a good stir to ensure the sausages and vegetables are evenly coated in the flavorful tomato sauce. Serve immediately with a side of crusty bread, creamy polenta, or al dente pasta.

Pairing Suggestions and Serving Ideas

Instant Pot sausage and peppers are brimming with versatility. Here are some ideas for serving and pairing this simple dish to cater to different preferences:

  • With Pasta: Toss the sausages, peppers, and sauce into freshly cooked spaghetti or rigatoni for a classic Italian feel.
  • Over Rice: Serve it over fluffy white rice or saffron-infused rice for a filling yet simple meal.
  • In Sandwiches: Stuff the sausage and peppers into toasted hoagie rolls for an irresistible sausage sub.
  • On Polenta: Pair it with creamy polenta or even grits to add a soft, comforting base.
  • Standalone Dish: Serve it in bowls for those who enjoy meals that don’t require any carb pairing.

Tips for Perfect Instant Pot Sausage and Peppers

  • Searing is Essential: Don’t skip browning the sausages. Even though they’ll cook under pressure, searing adds an irreplaceable depth.
  • Mind the Liquid: To avoid the dreaded “burn warning” in the Instant Pot, make sure to scrape up all the browned bits after deglazing and ensure enough liquid is in the pot.
  • Adjust Spice Levels: If you’re making this for kids or guests who prefer milder flavors, stick to sweet sausages and omit the red pepper flakes.
  • Add Fresh Herbs: If you have fresh basil or parsley on hand, sprinkle it over the finished dish for a pop of color and flavor.
  • Double the Batch: This recipe doubles beautifully! Use an 8-quart Instant Pot and increase the ingredients proportionally to serve a larger crowd or freeze leftovers.

Why You’ll Love This Dish

Instant Pot sausage and peppers ticks all the boxes for a busy home chef. It’s quick, simple, and requires minimal cleanup, making it ideal for both weeknight dinners and weekend meals. The fusion of smoky sausage, sweet peppers, tangy tomatoes, and aromatic herbs culminates in a dish that feels both indulgent and wholesome.

The Instant Pot captures flavors in ways that traditional methods can sometimes struggle to replicate. Tender sausages and perfectly cooked vegetables bathed in a soul-warming sauce offer comfort in every bite. Whether you’re cooking for family, hosting friends, or meal prepping for the week, this recipe is guaranteed to impress—and best of all, it’s as approachable as it is delicious.

So grab your Instant Pot, gather your ingredients, and dive into the world of sausage and peppers. You’re just 45 minutes away from a dinner that will have everyone asking for seconds!
