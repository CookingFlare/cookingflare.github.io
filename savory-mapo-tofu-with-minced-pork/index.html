Savory Mapo Tofu with Minced Pork


Mapo Tofu is a beloved classic in Sichuan cuisine, known for its bold flavors and spicy kick. This version of Mapo Tofu with minced pork offers a delicious twist to the traditional recipe, combining the richness of pork with the signature spicy and numbing Sichuan peppercorns. Ready to embark on a culinary adventure? Let’s dive into the recipe!

Ingredients:

  • 1 block of firm tofu, cubed
  • 200g minced pork
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1-inch piece of ginger, grated
  • 2 green onions, finely chopped
  • 2 tablespoons doubanjiang (chili bean paste)
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • 1 cup chicken or vegetable broth
  • 1 teaspoon cornstarch mixed with 2 tablespoons water
  • 1 teaspoon Sichuan peppercorns, toasted and ground
  • Salt to taste
  • Steamed rice, to serve

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the Tofu: Start by cubing the firm tofu and set it aside. This will ensure that the tofu maintains its shape during cooking.
  2. Cook the Minced Pork: In a pan, heat the vegetable oil over medium heat. Add the minced garlic, ginger, and white parts of the green onions. Sauté until fragrant.
  3. Add the Minced Pork: Add the minced pork to the pan and cook until it’s no longer pink, breaking it apart with a spoon as it cooks.
  4. Incorporate the Flavor: Stir in the doubanjiang (chili bean paste) and soy sauce, allowing the mixture to cook for a minute to release its flavors.
  5. Introduce the Tofu: Gently add the cubed tofu to the pan, being careful not to break it. Stir gently to coat the tofu with the pork mixture.
  6. Pour in the Broth: Add the chicken or vegetable broth to the pan, ensuring it covers the tofu. Let the mixture simmer for about 10-15 minutes to allow the flavors to meld.
  7. Thicken the Sauce: Slowly drizzle in the cornstarch-water mixture while stirring the tofu gently. This will help thicken the sauce to a silky consistency.
  8. Spice it Up: Sprinkle in the ground Sichuan peppercorns for that signature numbing heat. Adjust the salt according to your taste preference.
  9. Finish and Serve: Garnish the Mapo Tofu with the green parts of the chopped green onions for a pop of color and freshness. Serve hot with steamed rice.

Enjoy the Flavor Explosion!

This savory Mapo Tofu with minced pork is a flavor explosion in every bite. The combination of tender tofu, rich minced pork, and fiery Sichuan peppercorns creates a dish that is sure to tantalize your taste buds. Whether you’re a fan of spicy food or looking to explore the world of Sichuan cuisine, this recipe is a must-try. Serve it up for a cozy dinner at home or impress your friends with your culinary skills. Get ready to experience the bold flavors of this classic dish!

Remember, the key to a perfect Mapo Tofu lies in balancing the heat, saltiness, and numbing sensation of the Sichuan peppercorns. Feel free to adjust the spice level to suit your palate and make this recipe your own. So, gather your ingredients, put on your apron, and let’s cook up a storm with this delectable Savory Mapo Tofu with Minced Pork recipe!
