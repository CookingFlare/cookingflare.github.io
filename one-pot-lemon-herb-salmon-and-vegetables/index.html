One Pot Lemon Herb Salmon and Vegetables


If you’re looking for a simple yet impressive dish that’s bursting with flavor, then look no further than this One Pot Lemon Herb Salmon and Vegetables recipe. This dish not only makes for a beautiful presentation but also delivers on taste and nutrition. The combination of succulent salmon, fresh herbs, and vibrant vegetables creates a delightful harmony of flavors that will leave your taste buds dancing.

Ingredients

  • 4 salmon fillets
  • 1 pound baby potatoes, halved
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes
  • 1 bunch asparagus, trimmed
  • 1 lemon, sliced
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons fresh parsley, chopped
  • 1 tablespoon fresh thyme leaves
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Prep the Ingredients: Start by preheating your oven to 400°F (200°C). Season the salmon fillets with salt and pepper, and set them aside. In a large bowl, toss the halved baby potatoes, cherry tomatoes, and trimmed asparagus with olive oil, minced garlic, fresh parsley, and thyme. Season the vegetables with salt and pepper, then mix until well combined.

  2. Assemble the Dish: Place the seasoned vegetables in an even layer at the bottom of a large oven-proof skillet or baking dish. Lay the seasoned salmon fillets on top of the vegetables. Tuck lemon slices around the salmon for an extra burst of citrus flavor.

  3. Bake to Perfection: Transfer the skillet or baking dish to the preheated oven and bake for 15-20 minutes, or until the salmon is cooked through and the vegetables are tender. For a golden brown crust on the salmon, you can broil it for 2-3 minutes at the end.

  4. Serve and Enjoy: Once the salmon is cooked to perfection and the vegetables are tender, remove the dish from the oven. Serve the One Pot Lemon Herb Salmon and Vegetables directly from the skillet or transfer to a serving platter. Garnish with additional fresh herbs if desired and enjoy!

Why You’ll Love This Recipe

  • Effortless Preparation: With just one pot and minimal prep, this recipe is perfect for busy weeknights or casual entertaining.
  • Healthy and Nutritious: Packed with omega-3 fatty acids from the salmon and an array of vitamins and minerals from the vegetables, this dish is as nutritious as it is delicious.
  • Vibrant and Fresh Flavors: The combination of lemon, garlic, and fresh herbs infuses the salmon and vegetables with bright, zesty flavors that are sure to tantalize your palate.
  • Easy Cleanup: Since this dish is made in one pot, cleanup is a breeze, allowing you to savor the meal without worrying about a pile of dishes afterward.

So, whether you’re a seafood lover, a fan of vibrant vegetables, or simply in search of a hassle-free yet impressive dish, this One Pot Lemon Herb Salmon and Vegetables recipe is sure to become a favorite in your culinary repertoire. Try it out for your next meal and delight in the delightful fusion of flavors and the ease of preparation. Cheers to simple yet sensational cooking!
