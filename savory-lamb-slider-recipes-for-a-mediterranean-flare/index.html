Savory Lamb Slider Recipes for a Mediterranean Flare


If you’re looking to add a touch of Mediterranean flavor to your next gathering or simply want to try something new in your kitchen, look no further than these savory lamb slider recipes. These bite-sized delights are packed with bold flavors, tender lamb, and a blend of aromatic spices that will transport your taste buds to the shores of the Mediterranean.

Classic Mediterranean Lamb Sliders

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound ground lamb
  • 1/2 cup breadcrumbs
  • 1/4 cup finely chopped fresh parsley
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon ground coriander
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • Slider buns
  • Toppings: tzatziki sauce, sliced tomatoes, sliced red onion, lettuce

Instructions:

  1. In a large bowl, combine the ground lamb, breadcrumbs, parsley, minced garlic, cumin, coriander, dried oregano, salt, and pepper. Mix until well combined.
  2. Shape the lamb mixture into small patties, about 2 inches in diameter.
  3. Heat a grill or grill pan over medium-high heat. Cook the lamb sliders for about 3-4 minutes per side, or until they reach your desired level of doneness.
  4. Toast the slider buns lightly on the grill.
  5. Assemble the sliders by placing a lamb patty on each bun and adding your desired toppings, such as tzatziki sauce, sliced tomatoes, sliced red onion, and lettuce.
  6. Serve immediately and enjoy the flavors of the Mediterranean in every bite!

Spicy Harissa Lamb Sliders

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound ground lamb
  • 2 tablespoons harissa paste
  • 1/4 cup finely chopped fresh cilantro
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1/2 teaspoon smoked paprika
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • Slider buns
  • Toppings: spicy mayo, crumbled feta cheese, pickled jalapeños, arugula

Instructions:

  1. In a large bowl, mix together the ground lamb, harissa paste, cilantro, minced garlic, cumin, smoked paprika, salt, and pepper until well combined.
  2. Form the lamb mixture into small patties, approximately 2 inches in diameter.
  3. Heat a grill or grill pan over medium-high heat. Cook the lamb sliders for about 3-4 minutes per side, or until they are cooked to your desired level of doneness.
  4. Lightly toast the slider buns on the grill.
  5. Assemble the sliders by placing a lamb patty on each bun and adding your desired toppings, such as spicy mayo, crumbled feta cheese, pickled jalapeños, and arugula.
  6. Serve immediately and savor the spicy kick of the harissa paired with the succulent lamb.

Greek-Inspired Lamb Sliders

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound ground lamb
  • 1/4 cup crumbled feta cheese
  • 1/4 cup finely chopped Kalamata olives
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon chopped fresh dill
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • Slider buns
  • Toppings: tzatziki sauce, sliced cucumbers, sliced red onion, lettuce

Instructions:

  1. In a large bowl, combine the ground lamb, crumbled feta cheese, chopped Kalamata olives, minced garlic, chopped dill, dried oregano, salt, and pepper. Mix until well incorporated.
  2. Shape the lamb mixture into small patties, around 2 inches in diameter.
  3. Preheat a grill or grill pan over medium-high heat. Cook the lamb sliders for approximately 3-4 minutes per side, or until they reach your desired level of doneness.
  4. Lightly toast the slider buns on the grill.
  5. Assemble the sliders by placing a lamb patty on each bun and adding your desired toppings, such as tzatziki sauce, sliced cucumbers, sliced red onion, and lettuce.
  6. Serve immediately and enjoy the taste of Greece in every bite!

These savory lamb slider recipes are perfect for any occasion, whether you’re hosting a backyard barbecue, planning a casual dinner, or simply want to tantalize your taste buds with the bold flavors of the Mediterranean. Get creative with your toppings and sauces to customize these sliders to your liking. So, fire up the grill, gather your friends and family, and indulge in these delicious bites that will transport you to the sunny shores of the Mediterranean.
