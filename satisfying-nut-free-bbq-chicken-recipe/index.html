Satisfying Nut-Free BBQ Chicken Recipe


Are you looking for a delicious and satisfying BBQ chicken recipe that is also nut-free? Look no further! This nut-free BBQ chicken recipe is perfect for those with nut allergies or anyone looking for a flavorful and easy-to-make dish for their next barbecue. With a few simple ingredients and some easy-to-follow steps, you can create a mouthwatering BBQ chicken that will have everyone coming back for seconds.

Ingredients

  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1 cup of your favorite BBQ sauce
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon cayenne pepper (adjust to taste)
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Prep the Chicken: Start by prepping the chicken breasts. Trim off any excess fat and pat them dry with a paper towel.

  2. Marinate the Chicken: In a bowl, mix the BBQ sauce, olive oil, minced garlic, paprika, cayenne pepper, salt, and pepper. This flavorful marinade will infuse the chicken with a rich, tangy taste. Place the chicken breasts in the marinade, making sure to coat them evenly. Cover the bowl and let the chicken marinate in the refrigerator for at least 30 minutes. For even more flavor, you can marinate the chicken for up to 24 hours.

  3. Preheat the Grill: While the chicken is marinating, preheat your grill to medium-high heat. Make sure the grill grates are clean and lightly oiled to prevent the chicken from sticking.

  4. Grill the Chicken: Once the grill is hot, remove the chicken from the marinade and place it on the grill. Discard any remaining marinade. Grill the chicken for about 6-7 minutes on each side, or until the internal temperature reaches 165°F (75°C). Cooking times may vary depending on the thickness of the chicken breasts.

  5. Rest and Serve: Once the chicken is cooked through, remove it from the grill and let it rest for a few minutes. This allows the juices to redistribute, ensuring a juicy and flavorful result. Slice the chicken and serve it with your favorite sides, such as corn on the cob, coleslaw, or a fresh green salad.

Tips and Variations

  • Oven Option: If you don’t have a grill or prefer to cook indoors, you can bake the marinated chicken in a preheated oven at 375°F (190°C) for 25-30 minutes, or until fully cooked.
  • Flavor Boost: Add a squeeze of fresh lime juice to the marinade for a zesty twist.
  • Spice Level: Adjust the amount of cayenne pepper to suit your heat preference.

Conclusion

This nut-free BBQ chicken recipe is a crowd-pleaser and a great addition to any summer cookout or weeknight dinner. With its bold flavors and simple preparation, it’s sure to become a favorite in your recipe collection. Whether you’re grilling outdoors or cooking indoors, this recipe is a versatile and satisfying option for chicken lovers everywhere. Enjoy the smoky, savory goodness of this nut-free BBQ chicken, and savor the compliments from your family and friends at your next gathering!
