Classic Kung Pao Chicken Stir Fry


Are you craving a delicious and savory Chinese dish that packs a punch of flavors? Look no further than the classic Kung Pao Chicken Stir Fry! This iconic dish is a perfect blend of tender chicken, crunchy peanuts, and a medley of vegetables, all coated in a mouthwatering sauce that will leave your taste buds tingling with delight. Whether you are a fan of spicy dishes or simply enjoy exploring new cuisines, this recipe is sure to become a favorite in your kitchen.

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound boneless, skinless chicken breasts, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 1/2 cup roasted peanuts
  • 1 red bell pepper, diced
  • 1 green bell pepper, diced
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 3-4 dried red chilies
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1-inch piece of ginger, minced
  • 2 green onions, chopped (for garnish)
  • Cooking oil

For the Marinade:

  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon rice wine or dry sherry
  • 1 tablespoon cornstarch

For the Sauce:

  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon hoisin sauce
  • 1 tablespoon rice vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon sugar
  • 1 teaspoon sesame oil
  • 1 teaspoon cornstarch

Instructions:

  1. Marinate the Chicken: In a bowl, combine the chicken pieces with the marinade ingredients - soy sauce, rice wine, and cornstarch. Mix well and let it marinate for about 30 minutes.

  2. Prepare the Sauce: In another bowl, whisk together the soy sauce, hoisin sauce, rice vinegar, sugar, sesame oil, and cornstarch. Set aside.

  3. Heat the Wok: Heat a wok or large skillet over high heat. Add cooking oil and swirl to coat the pan.

  4. Stir Fry Chicken: Add the marinated chicken to the hot wok and stir-fry until it is no longer pink. Remove the chicken from the wok and set it aside.

  5. Cook Vegetables: In the same wok, add a little more oil if needed. Stir fry the dried red chilies, garlic, and ginger until fragrant. Add the diced bell peppers and onion, and continue to stir fry until the vegetables are slightly tender.

  6. Combine Ingredients: Return the cooked chicken to the wok. Pour in the prepared sauce and mix everything together until well coated. Let it simmer for a few minutes until the sauce thickens.

  7. Add Peanuts: Toss in the roasted peanuts and stir to combine. Ensure everything is heated through.

  8. Garnish and Serve: Garnish the Kung Pao Chicken with chopped green onions for a pop of freshness and color. Serve hot over steamed rice or noodles.

Enjoy Your Homemade Kung Pao Chicken!

There you have it - a simple yet flavorful recipe for Classic Kung Pao Chicken Stir Fry that you can easily recreate in your own kitchen. The combination of tender chicken, crunchy peanuts, and vibrant vegetables in a delicious sauce makes this dish a standout favorite. Whether you are cooking for yourself or entertaining guests, this dish is sure to impress with its bold flavors and satisfying textures. So, roll up your sleeves, grab your wok, and get ready to enjoy a taste of authentic Chinese cuisine right at home. Happy cooking!
