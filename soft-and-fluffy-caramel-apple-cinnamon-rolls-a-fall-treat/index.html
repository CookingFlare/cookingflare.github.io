Soft and Fluffy Caramel Apple Cinnamon Rolls: A Fall Treat


Fall is in the air, and with it comes the warm, comforting aromas of spices and sweet treats. There’s nothing quite like the scent of cinnamon and caramel wafting from the oven to evoke feelings of coziness and nostalgia. In this recipe, we’ll combine the classic flavors of caramel and cinnamon with the tender sweetness of apples to create a truly unforgettable fall treat: soft and fluffy caramel apple cinnamon rolls.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup warm water
  • 2 teaspoons active dry yeast
  • 3 tablespoons granulated sugar
  • 4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 cup unsalted butter, melted
  • 1/2 cup whole milk
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground nutmeg
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground cardamom
  • 1/2 cup caramel sauce (homemade or store-bought)
  • 1/2 cup diced apples (Granny Smith or Honeycrisp work well)
  • Confectioners’ sugar, for dusting

Instructions

First, let’s start by activating the yeast. In a small bowl, combine the warm water and yeast. Stir to dissolve, then let it sit for 5-10 minutes, or until the mixture becomes frothy and bubbly.

In a large mixing bowl, whisk together the flour, sugar, and salt. Add the melted butter, whole milk, eggs, cinnamon, nutmeg, and cardamom. Mix until a shaggy dough forms.

Knead the dough on a floured surface for 10-12 minutes, until it becomes smooth and elastic. Place the dough in a greased bowl, cover it with plastic wrap, and let it rise in a warm, draft-free place for about 1 hour, or until it has doubled in size.

Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C). Line a large baking sheet with parchment paper.

Punch down the dough and roll it out into a large rectangle, about 1/4-inch thick. Brush the dough with the caramel sauce, leaving a 1-inch border around the edges. Sprinkle the diced apples over the caramel sauce.

Roll the dough into a tight log, starting from the long side. Cut the log into 12 equal pieces. Place the rolls on the prepared baking sheet, leaving about 1 inch of space between each roll.

Cover the rolls with plastic wrap and let them rise for another 30-40 minutes, or until they have puffed up slightly.

Bake the rolls for 18-20 minutes, or until they are golden brown. While the rolls are baking, prepare the glaze by whisking together the caramel sauce and a tablespoon of heavy cream.

Remove the rolls from the oven and let them cool for 5 minutes. Drizzle the glaze over the rolls and sprinkle with confectioners’ sugar. Serve warm and enjoy!

Tips and Variations

  • To make the caramel sauce from scratch, combine 1 cup heavy cream, 1/2 cup granulated sugar, and 1/4 teaspoon sea salt in a saucepan. Bring to a boil, then reduce the heat and simmer for 5-7 minutes, or until the sauce thickens slightly.
  • Substitute chopped walnuts or pecans for the caramel sauce for a crunchy twist.
  • Add a sprinkle of sea salt or flaky salt to the glaze for a touch of savory flavor.
  • Make the rolls ahead of time and freeze them for up to 2 months. Thaw overnight at room temperature and bake as directed.

These soft and fluffy caramel apple cinnamon rolls are the perfect treat for a crisp fall morning or a cozy evening gathering. The combination of sweet caramel, tender apples, and warm spices is sure to become a new favorite. So go ahead, indulge in the flavors of the season, and make this recipe your own!
