Modern Vietnamese Fusion: Cook with a Twist of Tradition


Vietnamese cuisine is both light and flavorful, highlighting fresh herbs and a balance of sweet, salty, sour, and spicy flavors. Inspired by these elements, here are a few innovative recipes that combine traditional Vietnamese dishes with a creative twist, perfect for an adventurous palate. Get ready to impress your friends and family with these unique fusion dishes that celebrate both classic and modern culinary techniques.

Phenom Bo Kho (Spicy Beef Stew with Mushrooms and Kombu)

Soft tender beef, aromatic spices, and a umami bomb from the addition of mushrooms and kombu is a perfect example of blending the old with the new in this Vietnamese beef stew recipe.

Ingredients:

  • 2 lbs beef for stewing, cut into large chunks
  • 2 stalks lemongrass, bruised and cut into 3-inch pieces
  • 2 tsp salt
  • 1 tbsp pepper
  • 1/4 cup Vietnamese fish sauce (nước mắm)
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 1/2 cup oyster sauce
  • 1 tbsp sugar
  • 2 lbs mixed mushrooms, such as shiitake, oyster, and cremini, sliced
  • 1 large kombu strip
  • 1 onion, sliced
  • 4 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 thumb-sized piece of ginger, minced
  • 1 cup beef broth

Instructions:

  1. Season the beef chunks with salt and pepper.
  2. In a large stewing pot, heat some oil over medium-high heat, then brown the beef on all sides. Remove from the pot and set aside.
  3. In the same pot, add lemongrass, onion, garlic, ginger, and sauté until aromatic.
  4. Add the oyster sauce, fish sauce, water, sugar, beef broth, and kombu to the pot. Bring it to a boil.
  5. Once boiling, reduce the heat, place the beef chunks into the pot with the liquid concoction. Add mixed mushrooms and simmer over low heat for about 1.5-2 hours or until beef is tender.
  6. Remove the kombu, serve the beef stew in bowls and garnish with extra whole leaf mushrooms and some fresh cilantro.

Bun Chay Xao (Vegan Salad Roll with Tofu and Vegetables)

These Vietnamese salad rolls get a modern twist with the addition of seasoned tofu and a vegan peanut dipping sauce. It’s an elegant yet simple dish that will leave your guests satisfied and wanting more.

Ingredients:

Salad Rolls:

  • 8 rice paper rounds
  • 1 block of firm tofu, pressed and sliced into triangles
  • 1 cup bean sprouts
  • 1 cup shredded carrots
  • 1 cup julienned red bell pepper
  • 1 cup julienned cucumber
  • 1/2 cup torn basil leaves
  • 1/2 cup torn mint leaves
  • 1/2 cup torn cilantro leaves
  • Peanut Dipping Sauce (recipe below)

Peanut Dipping Sauce:

  • 3 tbsp peanut butter
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp lime juice
  • 2 tbsp water
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • 1 small garlic clove, minced
  • Pinch of red pepper flakes

Instructions:

  1. For the tofu: Season the tofu triangles with salt, pepper, and a teaspoon of lime juice. Pan-fry in a non-stick skillet over medium heat until golden brown on both sides.
  2. Soak the rice paper rounds in a shallow dish of warm water for about 10 seconds until softened.
  3. Assemble the salad rolls: on a softened wrapper, place a few ingredients such as tofu, carrot, bell pepper, cucumber, basil, mint, and cilantro, and fold the wrapper tightly.
  4. Repeat the process until all ingredients are used.
  5. For the dipping sauce: whisk together peanut butter, soy sauce, lime juice, water, sugar, garlic, and red pepper flakes in a small bowl.
  6. Serve vegan salad rolls with the peanut dipping sauce and enjoy!

Vietnamese-inspired BBQ Pork Chops with dismantled Fresh Spring Roll Ingredients

Barbecue pork chops are given an Asian twist in this recipe where they are served with dismantled fresh spring roll ingredients, offering a light and refreshing complement to the smoky, savory grilled pork.

Ingredients:

Pork Chops:

  • 4 pork chops, about 1-inch thick
  • 2 tbsp fish sauce
  • 1 tbsp sugar
  • 1 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp salt
  • ½ tsp ground black pepper

Dismantled Spring Roll Ingredients:

  • 1 cup sliced cucumber
  • 1 cup shredded red cabbage
  • 1 cup shredded carrots
  • 1 cup fresh mint leaves
  • 1 cup fresh basil leaves
  • 1 cup fresh cilantro leaves
  • 1 cup bean sprouts
  • 1 cup crushed peanuts

BBQ Marinade:

  • 3 tbsp fish sauce
  • 2 tbsp brown sugar
  • 1 tbsp oyster sauce
  • 1 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tsp Sriracha sauce
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper
  • 1/2 tsp garlic powder
  • 1/2 tsp ground cumin

Instructions:

  1. In a small bowl, whisk together the marinade ingredients. Place the pork chops in a sealed container and marinate for at least 2 hours or overnight.
  2. Preheat the grill to medium-high heat.
  3. Remove the pork chops from the marinade and discard the leftover liquid. Grill the pork chops for about 4 to 5 minutes on each side, until fully cooked and slightly charred.
  4. While the pork chops are on the grill, disassemble the salad roll elements into separate bowls.
  5. Slice the cooked BBQ pork chops and serve with the dismantled spring roll ingredients, allowing your guests to compose their own fresh spring roll-inspired bites.

This Vietnamese fusion-inspired menu is perfect for those looking for a fun and exciting culinary adventure. Try these creative recipes with friends and family for a memorable meal. Your taste buds will thank you for this exploration of traditional and modern flavors. Happy cooking!
