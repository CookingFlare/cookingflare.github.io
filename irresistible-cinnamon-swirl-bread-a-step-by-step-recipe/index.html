Irresistible Cinnamon Swirl Bread: A Step-by-Step Recipe


If you’re a fan of cinnamon and bread, then this recipe is a match made in heaven. The irresistible aroma of freshly baked cinnamon swirl bread wafting through your kitchen is enough to make anyone’s mouth water. With its soft, fluffy texture and gooey cinnamon filling, this bread is sure to become a favorite in your household.

Ingredients

Before we dive into the recipe, let’s gather all the ingredients you’ll need:

  • 3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 2 1/4 teaspoons instant yeast
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1/4 cup water
  • 1/4 cup unsalted butter, melted
  • 2 large eggs, beaten
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

For the cinnamon swirl filling:

  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 3/4 cup brown sugar
  • 2 tablespoons ground cinnamon

Instructions

Now that we have everything ready, let’s get started with the recipe:

  1. In a large mixing bowl, combine the flour, sugar, instant yeast, and salt. Mix well.

  2. In a small saucepan, heat the milk, water, and melted butter over low heat until the mixture reaches a warm temperature, around 110°F (43°C). Remove from heat.

  3. Pour the warm milk mixture into the dry ingredients. Add the beaten eggs and vanilla extract. Stir until a soft dough forms.

  4. Transfer the dough onto a lightly floured surface and knead for about 5-7 minutes, or until the dough becomes smooth and elastic.

  5. Place the dough in a greased bowl, cover it with a clean kitchen towel, and let it rise in a warm place for about 1-2 hours, or until it doubles in size.

  6. While the dough is rising, prepare the cinnamon swirl filling. In a small bowl, mix together the softened butter, brown sugar, and ground cinnamon until well combined.

  7. Once the dough has doubled in size, punch it down to release any air bubbles. Roll out the dough into a rectangle shape, about 12x16 inches.

  8. Spread the cinnamon swirl filling evenly over the dough, leaving a small border around the edges.

  9. Starting from the long side, tightly roll up the dough into a log. Pinch the seam to seal.

  10. Place the rolled dough into a greased loaf pan, seam side down. Cover it with a kitchen towel and let it rise for another 30-45 minutes, or until it reaches the top of the pan.

  11. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C). Bake the bread for 30-35 minutes, or until it turns golden brown and sounds hollow when tapped on the bottom.

  12. Remove the bread from the oven and let it cool in the pan for about 10 minutes. Then transfer it to a wire rack to cool completely before slicing.

Enjoy the Deliciousness

Congratulations! You’ve just created a mouthwatering masterpiece – a loaf of irresistible cinnamon swirl bread. The warm, comforting flavors of cinnamon and freshly baked bread will make it hard to resist having a second (or third) slice.

This bread is perfect for breakfast, brunch, or simply as a sweet treat any time of the day. Serve it toasted with a pat of butter or enjoy it as is. You can also get creative and use this bread to make French toast or bread pudding.

So, what are you waiting for? Gather your ingredients, follow the steps, and indulge in the heavenly delight of homemade cinnamon swirl bread. Your taste buds will thank you!
