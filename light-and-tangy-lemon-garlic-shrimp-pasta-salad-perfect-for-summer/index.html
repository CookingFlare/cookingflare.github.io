Light and Tangy Lemon Garlic Shrimp Pasta Salad: Perfect for Summer


As the temperatures rise, our cravings for light, refreshing meals increase, and what better way to satisfy those cravings than with a delicious and vibrant pasta salad? This Light and Tangy Lemon Garlic Shrimp Pasta Salad is a perfect blend of flavors and textures, making it an ideal dish for summer gatherings, picnics, or a simple weeknight dinner. Packed with succulent shrimp, al dente pasta, and a zesty dressing, this salad is sure to become a staple in your recipe collection.

Ingredients

For the Salad:

  • 1 pound large shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 8 ounces fusilli pasta
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1/2 English cucumber, diced
  • 1/4 cup red onion, finely chopped
  • 1/4 cup fresh parsley, chopped
  • Salt and pepper to taste

For the Dressing:

  • 1/4 cup extra virgin olive oil
  • Zest of 1 lemon
  • Juice of 2 lemons
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon honey
  • 1/2 teaspoon Dijon mustard
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Cook the Pasta: In a large pot of boiling salted water, cook the fusilli pasta according to the package instructions. Once al dente, drain the pasta and rinse it under cold water to stop the cooking process. Set aside.

  2. Prepare the Shrimp: Season the shrimp with salt and pepper. In a large skillet over medium-high heat, heat a drizzle of olive oil. Add the shrimp and cook for 2-3 minutes on each side until pink and opaque. Remove from the skillet and set aside to cool.

  3. Make the Dressing: In a small bowl, whisk together the extra virgin olive oil, lemon zest, lemon juice, minced garlic, honey, Dijon mustard, salt, and pepper until well combined.

  4. Assemble the Salad: In a large mixing bowl, combine the cooked pasta, cherry tomatoes, diced cucumber, red onion, and chopped parsley. Add the cooked shrimp to the bowl. Pour the dressing over the salad and gently toss to coat all the ingredients evenly.

  5. Chill and Serve: Cover the salad and refrigerate for at least 30 minutes to allow the flavors to meld together. Before serving, give the salad a gentle toss and adjust the seasoning if necessary. Serve chilled and enjoy!

Tips and Variations

  • For an extra kick of flavor, add a pinch of red pepper flakes to the dressing.
  • Substitute the shrimp with grilled chicken or tofu for a different protein option.
  • Feel free to add additional vegetables such as bell peppers, snap peas, or baby spinach to customize the salad to your liking.

Why It’s Perfect for Summer

This Light and Tangy Lemon Garlic Shrimp Pasta Salad is a delightful choice for summer dining for several reasons. Firstly, the zesty lemon and garlic dressing provides a refreshing and tangy flavor that complements the lightness of the shrimp and pasta. Additionally, the salad can be made ahead of time, allowing you to spend less time in the kitchen and more time enjoying the sunshine with your loved ones. The vibrant colors and flavors of this dish make it an eye-catching addition to any summer spread, and its versatility makes it suitable for various occasions, from casual picnics to elegant al fresco dinners.

Whether you’re hosting a barbecue, attending a potluck, or simply craving a satisfying yet light meal, this Lemon Garlic Shrimp Pasta Salad is a must-try recipe for the summer season. Its simplicity, bright flavors, and adaptability make it a standout dish that is sure to leave a lasting impression on your taste buds and those of your guests. Enjoy the summer vibes with this delightful and refreshing pasta salad!
