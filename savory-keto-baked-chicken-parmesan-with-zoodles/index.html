Savory Keto Baked Chicken Parmesan with Zoodles


As the keto diet continues to gain popularity, many people are looking for delicious and satisfying meals that fit within the diet’s guidelines. One classic Italian-American dish that can be easily adapted to a keto-friendly version is Chicken Parmesan. By substituting traditional breadcrumbs with a crispy almond flour crust and pairing it with zucchini noodles, you can enjoy a savory and flavorful meal that’s low in carbs and high in protein.

Ingredients

  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1 cup almond flour
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 large egg
  • 1 cup marinara sauce (make sure it’s sugar-free and keto-friendly)
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese (make sure it’s a keto-friendly brand)
  • 2 medium zucchinis
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C).
  2. In a shallow dish, mix together the almond flour, Parmesan cheese, parsley, garlic, salt, and pepper.
  3. In a separate dish, whisk together the egg and olive oil.
  4. Dip each chicken breast in the egg mixture, coating it completely, then roll it in the almond flour mixture to coat. Place the coated chicken on a plate or tray.
  5. Drizzle the marinara sauce on the bottom of a 9x13-inch baking dish. Arrange the coated chicken breasts on top of the sauce.
  6. Sprinkle the shredded mozzarella cheese over the chicken breasts.
  7. Bake the chicken in the preheated oven for 25-30 minutes, or until it reaches an internal temperature of 165°F (74°C).
  8. While the chicken is baking, spiralize the zucchinis into noodles. Heat the olive oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat. Add the zucchini noodles and cook for 3-4 minutes, or until they’re tender but still crisp. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  9. Once the chicken is done, remove it from the oven and let it rest for a few minutes before slicing it into strips.
  10. Serve the sliced chicken with the zucchini noodles and marinara sauce. Enjoy!

Tips and Variations

  • To make this recipe more substantial, you can add some sautéed spinach or mushrooms to the zucchini noodles.
  • If you prefer a crisper crust on your chicken, you can broil it for an additional 2-3 minutes after baking. Keep an eye on it to make sure it doesn’t burn.
  • You can also use different seasonings in the almond flour mixture, such as dried oregano or basil, to give the chicken a unique flavor.
  • If you’re short on time, you can use pre-cooked chicken breast or pre-made zucchini noodles to speed up the cooking process.

Conclusion

This Savory Keto Baked Chicken Parmesan with Zoodles is a delicious and satisfying meal that’s perfect for a weeknight dinner or a special occasion. By using almond flour and zucchini noodles, you can enjoy a low-carb and high-protein version of this classic Italian-American dish. Give it a try and enjoy the flavors of Italy without compromising your keto diet!
