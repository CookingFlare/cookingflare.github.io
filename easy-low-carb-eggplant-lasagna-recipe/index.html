Easy Low Carb Eggplant Lasagna Recipe


Eggplant lasagna is a delicious and healthy alternative to traditional lasagna that is packed with flavor and nutrients. This low-carb version swaps out traditional pasta sheets for thinly sliced eggplant, making it a perfect option for those following a low-carb or keto diet. Not only is it easy to make, but it’s also a great way to incorporate more vegetables into your diet. So, let’s get started!

Ingredients

  • 2 large eggplants, sliced lengthwise into thin sheets
  • 1 pound ground beef (or ground turkey for a leaner option)
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 cup marinara sauce
  • 1 cup ricotta cheese
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).

  2. In a large skillet, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the diced onion and minced garlic, and sauté until fragrant and translucent.

  3. Add the ground beef to the skillet and cook until browned. Break up the meat with a spatula as it cooks to ensure it’s evenly cooked.

  4. Once the meat is browned, add the marinara sauce, dried oregano, dried basil, salt, and pepper. Stir well to combine, and let the sauce simmer for about 5 minutes to allow the flavors to meld together.

  5. While the sauce is simmering, prepare the eggplant by slicing it lengthwise into thin sheets. You can use a mandoline slicer or a sharp knife for this step. Make sure the slices are thin but still sturdy enough to hold the lasagna layers.

  6. In a separate bowl, combine the ricotta cheese and grated Parmesan cheese. Mix well to create a creamy cheese filling.

  7. Now it’s time to assemble the lasagna. Start by spreading a thin layer of the meat sauce at the bottom of a baking dish. Layer the eggplant slices on top, followed by a layer of the ricotta cheese mixture. Repeat this process until all the ingredients are used, finishing with a layer of meat sauce on top.

  8. Sprinkle the shredded mozzarella cheese evenly over the top of the lasagna.

  9. Cover the baking dish with aluminum foil and place it in the preheated oven. Bake for 30 minutes.

  10. After 30 minutes, remove the foil and continue baking for an additional 10-15 minutes, or until the cheese is golden and bubbly.

  11. Once the lasagna is done, remove it from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes before serving. This will allow the layers to set and make it easier to slice.

  12. Serve the low carb eggplant lasagna hot and enjoy!

Conclusion

This easy low carb eggplant lasagna recipe is a fantastic option for those looking to cut down on carbs while still enjoying a delicious and satisfying meal. The eggplant slices provide a hearty and flavorful base, while the meat sauce and cheese layers add depth and richness. Whether you’re following a low-carb or keto diet or simply looking to incorporate more vegetables into your meals, this recipe is a winner. Give it a try and impress your family and friends with this healthy twist on a classic dish!
