Pesto and Sun-Dried Tomato Bread Recipe


If you’re looking to elevate your bread-baking game with a burst of flavor, this Pesto and Sun-Dried Tomato Bread recipe is a must-try. The combination of vibrant pesto and tangy sun-dried tomatoes creates a delightful taste experience that is perfect for any occasion. Let’s dive into the recipe and bring some Mediterranean flair to your kitchen!

Ingredients:

  • 3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 packet active dry yeast
  • 1 cup warm water
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1/4 cup store-bought or homemade pesto
  • 1/2 cup chopped sun-dried tomatoes
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder

Instructions:

  1. Activate the Yeast: In a small bowl, combine the warm water, sugar, and yeast. Let it sit for about 5-10 minutes until it becomes frothy.

  2. Mix the Dough: In a large mixing bowl, combine the flour, salt, garlic powder, olive oil, activated yeast mixture, pesto, and sun-dried tomatoes. Mix until a dough forms.

  3. Knead the Dough: Transfer the dough to a floured surface and knead for about 8-10 minutes until it becomes smooth and elastic.

  4. First Rise: Place the dough in a lightly oiled bowl, cover it with a damp towel, and let it rise in a warm place for about 1 hour or until it doubles in size.

  5. Shape the Bread: Once the dough has risen, punch it down and shape it into a loaf. Place the loaf on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper.

  6. Second Rise: Cover the loaf with a damp towel and let it rise for another 30-45 minutes.

  7. Preheat the Oven: Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).

  8. Bake the Bread: Bake the bread in the preheated oven for 25-30 minutes or until it turns golden brown and sounds hollow when tapped on the bottom.

  9. Cool and Enjoy: Once baked, transfer the bread to a wire rack to cool. Slice and serve it warm with a spread of butter or as a side to your favorite dishes.

Tips:

  • Feel free to customize the recipe by adding other ingredients like olives, feta cheese, or pine nuts for extra flavor.
  • Ensure the sun-dried tomatoes are well-drained and pat them dry before adding them to the dough to prevent excess moisture.
  • Store any leftover bread in an airtight container or freeze for future use.

With its aromatic pesto swirls and bursts of sun-dried tomatoes, this bread is a delightful treat for your taste buds. Whether you enjoy it as a snack, with a bowl of soup, or as part of a picnic spread, this Pesto and Sun-Dried Tomato Bread is sure to impress. Give this recipe a try and savor the Mediterranean flavors in every bite!
