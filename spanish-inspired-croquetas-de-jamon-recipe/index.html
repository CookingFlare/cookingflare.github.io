Spanish Inspired Croquetas de Jamon Recipe


Croquetas de Jamon, or Ham Croquettes, are a classic Spanish dish that is loved for its crispy exterior and creamy, flavorful filling. These bite-sized delights are perfect as an appetizer or a snack, and they are sure to impress your guests at any gathering. In this recipe, I will guide you through the steps to create these delicious Spanish inspired Croquetas de Jamon.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups of milk
  • 1/2 cup of all-purpose flour
  • 4 tablespoons of unsalted butter
  • 1/2 cup of finely chopped cooked ham
  • 1/4 cup of finely chopped onion
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1/4 teaspoon of nutmeg
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 cup of breadcrumbs
  • Vegetable oil for frying

Instructions

  1. In a medium saucepan, heat the milk over medium heat until it comes to a simmer. Remove from heat and set aside.

  2. In a separate large saucepan, melt the butter over medium heat. Add the chopped onion and minced garlic, and cook until they are soft and translucent.

  3. Stir in the flour and cook for about 2 minutes, stirring constantly to avoid any lumps. This will create a roux, which will thicken the mixture.

  4. Slowly pour in the warm milk, whisking continuously to combine. Cook the mixture for about 5 minutes, or until it thickens and becomes smooth and creamy.

  5. Add the chopped ham, nutmeg, salt, and pepper to the saucepan. Stir well to incorporate all the ingredients evenly. Cook for an additional 2 minutes to allow the flavors to meld together.

  6. Remove the mixture from the heat and transfer it to a bowl. Cover it with plastic wrap, making sure the plastic touches the surface of the mixture to prevent a skin from forming. Refrigerate for at least 2 hours, or until the mixture is firm.

  7. Once the mixture has chilled and firmed up, it’s time to shape the croquettes. Take a small amount of the mixture and roll it into a small cylinder shape, about 2 inches long. Repeat this process until all the mixture is used.

  8. Place the breadcrumbs in a shallow dish. Roll each croquette in the breadcrumbs, making sure they are fully coated. Set them aside on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper.

  9. In a deep saucepan or a deep fryer, heat the vegetable oil to 350°F (175°C). Carefully fry the croquettes in small batches for about 3 minutes, or until they turn golden brown and crispy. Use a slotted spoon to remove them from the oil and transfer them to a paper towel-lined plate to drain any excess oil.

  10. Serve the Croquetas de Jamon warm, as they are best enjoyed fresh out of the fryer. You can serve them with a side of aioli or a tangy tomato sauce for dipping.

Conclusion

With their crispy exterior and creamy, flavorful filling, Spanish Inspired Croquetas de Jamon are sure to be a hit at any gathering. Whether you’re hosting a party or simply looking for a delicious snack, this recipe will not disappoint. The combination of tender ham, aromatic spices, and a crispy coating creates a mouthwatering bite-sized treat that is hard to resist. So, why not bring a taste of Spain to your kitchen and give these Croquetas de Jamon a try? Your taste buds will thank you!
