Savory Garlic Butter Shrimp Scampi: A Quick and Easy Weeknight Meal


Looking for a delicious and satisfying meal that comes together in a flash? Look no further than this Savory Garlic Butter Shrimp Scampi recipe. Bursting with flavors of garlic, butter, and tender shrimp, this dish is perfect for busy weeknights when you want something special without spending hours in the kitchen.

Ingredients

To make this savory shrimp scampi, you’ll need:

  • Shrimp: Fresh or thawed, peeled, and deveined.
  • Garlic: Fresh cloves minced finely.
  • Butter: Unsalted butter adds richness.
  • Olive Oil: For sautéing the shrimp and garlic.
  • White Wine: Adds depth and flavor (optional).
  • Lemon: Freshly squeezed lemon juice for brightness.
  • Parsley: Chopped fresh parsley for garnish.
  • Salt and Pepper: To season the shrimp scampi to taste.
  • Red Pepper Flakes: Optional for a hint of heat.

Instructions

Step 1: Prepare the Shrimp

Start by patting dry the shrimp with paper towels to ensure they cook evenly. Season lightly with salt and pepper according to your taste preferences.

Step 2: Sauté the Garlic

In a large skillet or pan, heat olive oil over medium heat. Add minced garlic and sauté for about 1 minute until fragrant, being careful not to burn it.

Step 3: Cook the Shrimp

Increase the heat to medium-high and add the shrimp to the skillet in a single layer. Cook for 2-3 minutes, stirring occasionally, until the shrimp turn pink and opaque. Remove the shrimp from the skillet and set aside.

Step 4: Make the Garlic Butter Sauce

Reduce the heat to medium-low. Add butter to the skillet and let it melt, stirring occasionally. Once the butter is melted, add a splash of white wine (if using) and let it simmer for about 1 minute to cook off the alcohol.

Step 5: Combine and Finish

Return the cooked shrimp to the skillet. Add freshly squeezed lemon juice and chopped parsley. Toss everything together gently until the shrimp are coated evenly with the garlic butter sauce. Season with additional salt and pepper to taste.

Step 6: Serve and Enjoy

Serve the savory garlic butter shrimp scampi immediately, garnished with more fresh parsley if desired. This dish pairs wonderfully with crusty bread, pasta, or a fresh green salad.

Tips for Success

  • Fresh Ingredients: Use fresh shrimp and garlic for the best flavor.
  • Don’t Overcook: Shrimp cook quickly, so be attentive to avoid overcooking.
  • Customize: Feel free to add more garlic or adjust the amount of butter and lemon juice based on your preference.

Variations

  • Creamy Shrimp Scampi: Add a splash of cream or half-and-half to the butter sauce for a richer version.
  • Spicy Shrimp Scampi: Increase the amount of red pepper flakes or add a dash of hot sauce for a spicy kick.
  • Vegetable Addition: Include sautéed vegetables like cherry tomatoes or spinach for added texture and flavor.

Why You’ll Love It

This garlic butter shrimp scampi recipe is not only quick and easy but also incredibly flavorful. The combination of garlic, butter, and fresh herbs creates a luxurious sauce that coats each tender shrimp perfectly. Whether you’re cooking for yourself or entertaining guests, this dish is sure to impress with its simplicity and deliciousness.

Final Thoughts

Next time you’re looking for a fuss-free dinner that doesn’t compromise on taste, give this savory garlic butter shrimp scampi a try. With minimal ingredients and maximum flavor, it’s a weeknight winner that will have everyone coming back for seconds. Enjoy the satisfaction of a homemade meal that feels like a restaurant-quality dish, right in the comfort of your own kitchen. Cheers to good food and memorable meals!
