Simple Fourth of July BBQ Side Dish Recipes


The Fourth of July is a time for celebration, and what better way to celebrate than with a delicious BBQ? While the main course may steal the show, it’s the side dishes that truly complete the meal. Whether you’re hosting a backyard gathering or attending a potluck, these simple Fourth of July BBQ side dish recipes are sure to impress your guests. From creamy coleslaw to zesty pasta salad, there’s something for everyone to enjoy.

1. Classic Coleslaw

Ingredients:

  • 1 small head of cabbage, finely shredded
  • 1 carrot, grated
  • 1/2 cup mayonnaise
  • 2 tablespoons white vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon sugar
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a large bowl, combine the shredded cabbage and grated carrot. Mix well.
  2. In a separate bowl, whisk together the mayonnaise, white vinegar, sugar, salt, and pepper until well combined.
  3. Pour the dressing over the cabbage and carrot mixture. Toss until the vegetables are evenly coated.
  4. Cover and refrigerate for at least 1 hour before serving to allow the flavors to meld together.

2. Grilled Corn on the Cob

Ingredients:

  • 4 ears of corn, husks removed
  • 2 tablespoons butter, softened
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your grill to medium-high heat.
  2. Rub each ear of corn with the softened butter, making sure to coat all sides.
  3. Season the corn with salt and pepper to taste.
  4. Place the corn directly on the grill grates and cook for 10-12 minutes, turning occasionally, until the kernels are tender and lightly charred.
  5. Remove from the grill and serve immediately.

3. Caprese Skewers

Ingredients:

  • 1 pint cherry tomatoes
  • 8 ounces fresh mozzarella balls
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • Balsamic glaze, for drizzling

Instructions:

  1. Thread a cherry tomato, a mozzarella ball, and a basil leaf onto a skewer. Repeat with the remaining ingredients.
  2. Arrange the skewers on a platter and drizzle with balsamic glaze.
  3. Serve immediately or refrigerate until ready to serve.

4. Zesty Pasta Salad

Ingredients:

  • 8 ounces rotini pasta
  • 1/2 cup mayonnaise
  • 2 tablespoons Dijon mustard
  • 1 tablespoon red wine vinegar
  • 1/4 cup chopped red onion
  • 1/4 cup chopped bell pepper
  • 1/4 cup chopped cucumber
  • 1/4 cup chopped black olives
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Cook the rotini pasta according to package instructions. Drain and rinse with cold water.
  2. In a large bowl, whisk together the mayonnaise, Dijon mustard, red wine vinegar, salt, and pepper until well combined.
  3. Add the cooked pasta, red onion, bell pepper, cucumber, and black olives to the bowl. Toss until the pasta and vegetables are evenly coated with the dressing.
  4. Cover and refrigerate for at least 1 hour before serving to allow the flavors to meld together.

5. Watermelon Salad

Ingredients:

  • 4 cups cubed watermelon
  • 1 cup crumbled feta cheese
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh mint
  • 2 tablespoons lime juice
  • 1 tablespoon honey

Instructions:

  1. In a large bowl, combine the watermelon, feta cheese, and fresh mint.
  2. In a small bowl, whisk together the lime juice and honey until well combined.
  3. Pour the dressing over the watermelon mixture and toss gently to combine.
  4. Serve immediately or refrigerate until ready to serve.

These simple Fourth of July BBQ side dish recipes are guaranteed to add a burst of flavor to your Independence Day celebration. From the creamy coleslaw to the zesty pasta salad, each dish is packed with fresh ingredients and vibrant flavors. So fire up the grill, invite your friends and family over, and get ready to indulge in these delicious side dishes. Happy Fourth of July!
