Lip-Smacking Low Carb Lemon Garlic Butter Shrimp


If you’re looking for a quick, flavorful, and low-carb dish that will impress your taste buds, then this Lip-Smacking Low Carb Lemon Garlic Butter Shrimp recipe is just what you need. This dish is not only easy to prepare, but it’s also bursting with zesty flavors that will leave you craving for more. Whether you’re following a low-carb diet or simply want to enjoy a delicious and healthy meal, this recipe is a winner. Let’s dive into the details of creating this delightful dish.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound of large shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 4 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1/4 cup of fresh lemon juice
  • 1/4 cup of unsalted butter
  • 1 tablespoon of olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon of lemon zest
  • 1/2 teaspoon of red pepper flakes
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • Chopped fresh parsley for garnish

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Shrimp: Start by patting the shrimp dry with paper towels. Season them with a pinch of salt and black pepper, ensuring they are evenly coated.

  2. Sauté the Shrimp: In a large skillet, heat the olive oil over medium-high heat. Add the shrimp to the skillet and cook for 1-2 minutes on each side until they turn pink and opaque. Once cooked, remove the shrimp from the skillet and set them aside.

  3. Make the Garlic Butter Sauce: In the same skillet, melt the butter over medium heat. Add the minced garlic and red pepper flakes, and sauté for about 1 minute until the garlic becomes fragrant.

  4. Add Lemon Flavor: Pour in the fresh lemon juice and lemon zest, stirring to combine all the flavors. Let the sauce simmer for 1-2 minutes, allowing it to thicken slightly.

  5. Combine the Shrimp and Sauce: Return the cooked shrimp to the skillet, tossing them in the lemon garlic butter sauce until they are well coated. Cook for an additional 1-2 minutes to ensure the shrimp are heated through.

  6. Garnish and Serve: Once the shrimp are coated in the flavorful sauce, remove the skillet from the heat. Garnish with chopped fresh parsley, and serve the Lip-Smacking Low Carb Lemon Garlic Butter Shrimp immediately.

Nutritional Information

This Lip-Smacking Low Carb Lemon Garlic Butter Shrimp dish is not only delicious but also low in carbohydrates, making it a great option for those following a low-carb lifestyle. Here’s a brief overview of the nutritional content:

  • Calories: Approximately 270 per serving
  • Protein: 24g
  • Fat: 17g
  • Carbohydrates: 4g
  • Fiber: 1g

Serving Suggestions

This delightful dish pairs wonderfully with a variety of sides, making it a versatile option for any occasion. Consider serving the Lip-Smacking Low Carb Lemon Garlic Butter Shrimp over a bed of zucchini noodles, cauliflower rice, or a crisp green salad for a complete and satisfying meal.

Final Thoughts

There you have it, a simple yet sensational recipe for Lip-Smacking Low Carb Lemon Garlic Butter Shrimp that’s sure to become a favorite in your household. The combination of zesty lemon, savory garlic butter, and succulent shrimp creates a symphony of flavors that will leave you wanting more. Whether you’re cooking for a special occasion or simply craving a delectable meal, this recipe is a must-try. Enjoy!
