Delicious Spinach and Feta Quiche Recipes for a Vegetarian Brunch


If you’re looking for a show-stopping dish to serve at your next brunch, look no further than these mouth-watering spinach and feta quiches. Packed with flavor, protein, and nutrients, these vegetarian quiches are sure to impress your guests and satisfy even the most discerning palates.

Spinach and Feta Quiche Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1 9-inch pie crust
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 4 cups fresh spinach, roughly chopped
  • 4 large eggs
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1/2 cup crumbled feta cheese
  • 1/2 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Heat the olive oil in a skillet over medium heat. Add the chopped onion and garlic, and sauté until the onion is translucent.
  3. Add the chopped spinach to the skillet and cook until wilted, about 3-4 minutes. Remove from heat and set aside to cool.
  4. In a mixing bowl, whisk together the eggs and milk. Season with salt and pepper.
  5. Place the pie crust in a pie dish and crimp the edges. Spread the spinach mixture evenly over the bottom of the pie crust.
  6. Sprinkle the crumbled feta and shredded mozzarella over the spinach.
  7. Pour the egg mixture over the spinach and cheese.
  8. Bake in the preheated oven for 35-40 minutes or until the quiche is set and the crust is golden brown.
  9. Allow the quiche to cool for a few minutes before slicing and serving.

Spinach and Feta Mini Quiche Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1 12-cup muffin tin
  • Cooking spray
  • 1 9-inch pie crust
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 4 cups fresh spinach, roughly chopped
  • 4 large eggs
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1/2 cup crumbled feta cheese
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C). Lightly grease the muffin tin with cooking spray.
  2. Using a round cookie cutter, cut out circles from the pie crust that are slightly larger than the muffin tin cups.
  3. Press the pie crust circles into the muffin tin, forming mini pie crusts.
  4. Heat the olive oil in a skillet over medium heat. Add the chopped onion and garlic, and sauté until the onion is translucent.
  5. Add the chopped spinach to the skillet and cook until wilted, about 3-4 minutes. Remove from heat and set aside to cool.
  6. In a mixing bowl, whisk together the eggs and milk. Season with salt and pepper.
  7. Divide the spinach mixture evenly among the mini pie crusts.
  8. Sprinkle the crumbled feta over the spinach in each mini quiche.
  9. Pour the egg mixture over the spinach and feta in each mini quiche, filling to just below the top of the crust.
  10. Bake in the preheated oven for 20-25 minutes or until the mini quiches are set and the crust is golden brown.
  11. Allow the mini quiches to cool for a few minutes before removing them from the muffin tin.

Conclusion

These spinach and feta quiche recipes are versatile, delicious, and perfect for a vegetarian brunch. Whether you opt for the classic pie-sized quiche or the adorable mini versions, you’re sure to impress your guests with these savory delights. So, roll up your sleeves, preheat the oven, and get ready to create a brunch masterpiece that will have everyone coming back for seconds!
