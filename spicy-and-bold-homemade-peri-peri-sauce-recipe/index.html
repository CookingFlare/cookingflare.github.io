Spicy and Bold: Homemade Peri-Peri Sauce Recipe


If you’re a fan of spicy and bold flavors, homemade peri-peri sauce is a must-try for your culinary adventures. This fiery and flavorful sauce, originating from Portuguese and African cuisines, has gained popularity around the world for its intense heat and rich, complex taste. Whether you want to add a kick to your grilled meats, marinate chicken, or simply spice up your favorite dishes, this homemade peri-peri sauce will elevate your culinary creations to a whole new level.

Ingredients

  • 10-12 red bird’s eye chilies
  • 4 cloves of garlic
  • 1 small red bell pepper
  • 1 small onion
  • 1 lemon (juiced)
  • 2 tablespoons red wine vinegar
  • 1 teaspoon smoked paprika
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup olive oil

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Chilies: Start by preparing the red bird’s eye chilies. Remove the stems and roughly chop them. Be cautious while handling the chilies, as they can be extremely spicy. Use gloves if needed and avoid touching your face or eyes.

  2. Roast the Vegetables: Cut the red bell pepper into large chunks and peel the garlic cloves. Halve the onion. Place the red bell pepper, garlic, and onion on a baking sheet and drizzle with a little olive oil. Roast in the oven at 400°F (200°C) for 20-25 minutes or until the vegetables are charred and softened.

  3. Blend the Ingredients: Once the roasted vegetables have cooled, place them in a blender or food processor. Add the chopped chilies, lemon juice, red wine vinegar, smoked paprika, dried oregano, and salt. Blend until the mixture becomes a coarse paste.

  4. Emulsify the Sauce: With the blender running, slowly drizzle in the olive oil until the sauce becomes smooth and emulsified. The olive oil will help balance the heat of the chilies and create a luscious texture.

  5. Adjust the Seasoning: Taste the peri-peri sauce and adjust the seasoning to your preference. You can add more salt, lemon juice, or a touch of honey for a hint of sweetness if desired.

  6. Store the Sauce: Transfer the peri-peri sauce to a sterilized glass jar or airtight container. It can be stored in the refrigerator for up to two weeks, allowing the flavors to meld and intensify.

Serving Suggestions

  • Marinade: Use the peri-peri sauce as a marinade for grilled chicken, shrimp, or tofu. Let the protein sit in the sauce for a few hours or overnight to absorb the intense flavors before grilling or roasting.
  • Dipping Sauce: Pair the peri-peri sauce with crispy french fries, roasted vegetables, or grilled halloumi for a spicy and tangy dipping experience.
  • Condiment: Drizzle the peri-peri sauce over burgers, sandwiches, or wraps to add a fiery kick and depth of flavor to your favorite meals.

Conclusion

Homemade peri-peri sauce is a versatile and fiery condiment that will awaken your taste buds with its bold flavors. By following this simple recipe, you can create a batch of this sensational sauce to elevate a wide range of dishes. Embrace the heat and savor the complexity of this homemade peri-peri sauce as you explore the world of spicy and bold culinary delights.
