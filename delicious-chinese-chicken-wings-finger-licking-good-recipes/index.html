Delicious Chinese Chicken Wings: Finger-Licking Good Recipes


If you’re a fan of Chinese cuisine and love indulging in some finger-licking good chicken wings, then you’re in for a treat! Chinese chicken wings are known for their bold flavors, crispy skin, and tender meat that falls off the bone. In this blog post, we’ll explore a variety of delicious Chinese chicken wing recipes that will leave you craving for more. So, let’s dive right into it!

1. Sweet and Spicy Soy Glazed Chicken Wings

These sticky and flavorful wings are a crowd-pleaser at any gathering. To make this recipe, you’ll need the following ingredients:

  • 2 pounds of chicken wings
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons honey
  • 1 tablespoon sriracha sauce
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon grated ginger
  • 1 tablespoon sesame oil
  • 1 tablespoon rice vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon cornstarch
  • 2 green onions, chopped (for garnish)

To prepare the wings, start by preheating your oven to 425°F (220°C). In a mixing bowl, combine the soy sauce, honey, sriracha sauce, minced garlic, grated ginger, sesame oil, and rice vinegar. Mix well until the ingredients are fully combined.

Next, place the chicken wings on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper. Pour the sauce over the wings, making sure to coat them evenly. Bake the wings for 25-30 minutes or until they are golden brown and cooked through.

While the wings are baking, prepare the glaze. In a small saucepan, combine the cornstarch with a tablespoon of water and mix until smooth. Heat the saucepan over medium heat and add the remaining sauce from the baking sheet. Cook the glaze until it thickens, stirring constantly.

Once the wings are cooked, remove them from the oven and brush the glaze over them. Garnish with chopped green onions for an extra pop of flavor. Serve hot and enjoy!

2. Crispy Szechuan Pepper Chicken Wings

If you’re a fan of bold and spicy flavors, these Szechuan pepper chicken wings are a must-try. Here’s what you’ll need:

  • 2 pounds of chicken wings
  • 1/4 cup cornstarch
  • 1 tablespoon Szechuan peppercorns, crushed
  • 1 tablespoon five-spice powder
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • Vegetable oil (for frying)

To begin, pat dry the chicken wings with paper towels to remove any excess moisture. In a large mixing bowl, combine the cornstarch, crushed Szechuan peppercorns, five-spice powder, salt, and black pepper. Toss the chicken wings in the mixture until they are well-coated.

Heat vegetable oil in a deep fryer or large pot to 375°F (190°C). Fry the wings in batches for about 8-10 minutes or until they are crispy and golden brown. Make sure to turn them occasionally for even cooking.

Once the wings are cooked, remove them from the oil and place them on a paper towel-lined plate to drain any excess oil. Serve them hot and enjoy the spicy kick of the Szechuan pepper!

3. Honey Garlic Ginger Glazed Chicken Wings

For a sweet and tangy twist on Chinese chicken wings, try this mouthwatering recipe. Here’s what you’ll need:

  • 2 pounds of chicken wings
  • 1/4 cup honey
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon grated ginger
  • 1 tablespoon rice vinegar
  • 1 teaspoon sesame oil
  • 1/2 teaspoon red pepper flakes (optional)
  • 2 green onions, chopped (for garnish)

Start by preheating your oven to 400°F (200°C). In a mixing bowl, combine the honey, soy sauce, minced garlic, grated ginger, rice vinegar, sesame oil, and red pepper flakes (if using). Mix well until all the ingredients are thoroughly combined.

Place the chicken wings on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper. Pour the sauce over the wings, making sure to coat them evenly. Bake for 25-30 minutes or until the wings are crispy and cooked through.

Once the wings are ready, remove them from the oven and garnish them with chopped green onions for a fresh and vibrant touch. Serve them hot and watch them disappear within minutes!

Conclusion

Chinese chicken wings are a delightful and versatile dish that can be enjoyed on various occasions. Whether you prefer sweet and spicy flavors or bold and tangy ones, these recipes are sure to satisfy your cravings. Experiment with different sauces, spices, and cooking methods to create your own unique twist on these finger-licking good Chinese chicken wings. So, grab your apron and get ready to impress your family and friends with these mouthwatering recipes!
