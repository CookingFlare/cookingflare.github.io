Mini Quiches Lorraine: A Classic and Easy Appetizer for Brunch or Dinner


Are you looking for a delicious and versatile appetizer that’s perfect for brunch or dinner? Look no further than Mini Quiches Lorraine! These bite-sized treats are a classic dish that never goes out of style. With their buttery crust, creamy filling, and savory bacon, they are sure to be a hit at your next gathering. Plus, they are incredibly easy to make, making them a go-to recipe for both novice and experienced cooks.

Ingredients:

  • 1 package of pre-made pie crusts
  • 6 slices of bacon, cooked and crumbled
  • 1 cup of shredded Swiss cheese
  • 1/2 cup of chopped onion
  • 4 large eggs
  • 1 cup of heavy cream
  • 1/2 teaspoon of salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon of black pepper
  • 1/4 teaspoon of nutmeg

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C). Grease a mini muffin pan or line it with paper liners.

  2. Roll out the pre-made pie crusts on a floured surface. Using a round cookie cutter or a glass, cut out circles that are slightly larger than the wells of the mini muffin pan. Press the circles into the wells, making sure they come up the sides of each well.

  3. In a skillet, cook the bacon until crispy. Remove from the skillet and let it cool. Once cooled, crumble the bacon into small pieces.

  4. In the same skillet, sauté the chopped onion until translucent and fragrant. Remove from heat and set aside.

  5. In a medium-sized bowl, whisk together the eggs, heavy cream, salt, black pepper, and nutmeg until well combined.

  6. Distribute the crumbled bacon, sautéed onion, and shredded Swiss cheese evenly among the pie crusts in the mini muffin pan.

  7. Pour the egg mixture over the filling in each well, being careful not to overfill.

  8. Bake in the preheated oven for 15-20 minutes or until the quiches are golden brown and set in the center.

  9. Remove from the oven and let cool for a few minutes before carefully removing the mini quiches from the pan. Serve warm or at room temperature.

Serving Suggestions:

Mini Quiches Lorraine are incredibly versatile and can be served in a variety of ways. Here are a few serving suggestions to get you started:

  • Brunch: Serve these mini quiches as part of a brunch spread along with fresh fruit, pastries, and a side salad.
  • Dinner: Pair the mini quiches with a crisp green salad and roasted vegetables for a light and satisfying dinner.
  • Party appetizer: These bite-sized treats are perfect for parties and can be easily enjoyed with one hand. Place them on a platter and watch them disappear!

Tips and Variations:

  • Make ahead: You can prepare the mini quiches in advance and store them in the refrigerator. Simply reheat in the oven or microwave before serving.
  • Vegetarian option: Omit the bacon and add sautéed mushrooms or spinach for a delicious vegetarian twist.
  • Experiment with different cheeses: Swiss cheese is traditional for Quiches Lorraine, but feel free to try other cheeses like Gruyere or cheddar for a different flavor.

So there you have it, a classic and easy recipe for Mini Quiches Lorraine that will impress your friends and family. Whether you serve them for brunch, dinner, or as a party appetizer, these savory treats are sure to be a crowd-pleaser. Give them a try and enjoy the deliciousness!
