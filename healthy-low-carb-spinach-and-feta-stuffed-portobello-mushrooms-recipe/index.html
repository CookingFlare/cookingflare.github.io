Healthy Low Carb Spinach and Feta Stuffed Portobello Mushrooms Recipe


If you’re looking for a delicious and healthy low-carb meal that’s also easy to prepare, then you’re in for a treat! This spinach and feta stuffed portobello mushrooms recipe is not only mouthwatering but also packed with nutrients. Whether you’re a vegetarian, a fan of Mediterranean flavors, or just someone who enjoys a good dish, this recipe is sure to satisfy your taste buds.

Ingredients:

  • 4 large portobello mushrooms
  • 2 cups of fresh spinach, chopped
  • 1/2 cup of crumbled feta cheese
  • 1/4 cup of grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/4 cup of chopped red onion
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons of olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon of dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the Portobello Mushrooms:

    • Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C). Gently wipe the portobello mushrooms with a damp cloth to clean them. Remove the stems and scrape out the gills using a spoon.

  2. Prepare the Filling:

    • In a skillet, heat 1 tablespoon of olive oil over medium heat. Add the chopped red onion and minced garlic, and sauté for 2-3 minutes until fragrant.
    • Add the chopped spinach to the skillet and cook until wilted, about 2-3 minutes. Season with salt, pepper, and dried oregano.
    • Remove the skillet from the heat and allow the spinach mixture to cool slightly.

  3. Assemble the Stuffed Mushrooms:

    • Place the portobello mushrooms on a baking sheet. Drizzle the remaining olive oil over the mushrooms and season with salt and pepper.
    • Divide the spinach mixture evenly among the mushrooms, pressing it down gently to fill the caps.
    • Sprinkle the crumbled feta cheese and grated Parmesan cheese over the stuffed mushrooms.

  4. Bake and Serve:

    • Bake the stuffed mushrooms in the preheated oven for 15-20 minutes, or until the cheese is golden and bubbly.
    • Once done, remove the mushrooms from the oven and let them cool for a few minutes. Garnish with fresh parsley before serving.

Nutritional Benefits:

This healthy and flavorful dish offers an array of nutritional benefits. Portobello mushrooms are low in calories and high in fiber, making them an excellent choice for those looking to reduce their carbohydrate intake. Spinach is a rich source of vitamins and minerals, including vitamin A, vitamin C, and iron. Feta cheese adds a creamy and tangy flavor while providing a dose of calcium and protein. The combination of these ingredients makes this recipe a well-rounded and nourishing option for a low-carb diet.

Conclusion:

This Healthy Low Carb Spinach and Feta Stuffed Portobello Mushrooms recipe is a fantastic way to enjoy a satisfying meal without compromising on flavor or nutrition. Whether you’re cooking for yourself or entertaining guests, these stuffed mushrooms are sure to impress. The simplicity of the ingredients and the ease of preparation make this recipe a go-to option for anyone seeking a wholesome and delicious dish. Try it out today and savor the delightful flavors of this Mediterranean-inspired creation!
