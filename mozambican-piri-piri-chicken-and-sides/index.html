Mozambican Piri Piri Chicken and Sides


If you’re looking to spice up your dinner routine with a taste of Africa, look no further than Mozambican Piri Piri Chicken. This vibrant and fiery dish is a staple in Mozambique and is sure to tantalize your taste buds. In this blog post, we’ll explore the rich history of Piri Piri Chicken, share a traditional recipe, and suggest some delicious sides to complement this flavorful main dish.

The History of Piri Piri Chicken

Piri Piri Chicken traces its roots back to Portugal, where it was introduced by African colonizers. The term “Piri Piri” refers to a type of chili pepper that is commonly used to season the chicken. Over time, the dish evolved and became a beloved part of Mozambican cuisine, known for its bold flavors and unique spiciness.

Traditional Mozambican Piri Piri Chicken Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1 whole chicken, cut into pieces
  • 4-6 Piri Piri chili peppers, finely chopped
  • 4 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons of olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons of lemon juice
  • 1 teaspoon of paprika
  • 1 teaspoon of salt
  • 1 teaspoon of black pepper
  • Fresh cilantro, for garnish

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, combine the Piri Piri chili peppers, minced garlic, olive oil, lemon juice, paprika, salt, and black pepper. Mix well to create a marinade.

  2. Place the chicken pieces in a shallow dish and pour the marinade over them. Make sure each piece is fully coated. Cover the dish and refrigerate for at least 2 hours, allowing the flavors to penetrate the chicken.

  3. Preheat your grill to medium-high heat. Remove the chicken from the marinade and place it on the grill. Cook for about 10-15 minutes on each side, or until the chicken is cooked through and has a nicely charred exterior.

  4. Once the chicken is cooked, remove it from the grill and let it rest for a few minutes. Garnish with fresh cilantro.

Delicious Sides to Complement Piri Piri Chicken

Now that you have your flavorful Piri Piri Chicken ready, it’s time to think about some delicious sides to complete your meal. Here are a few ideas:

  1. Coconut Rice: This fragrant and slightly sweet rice is the perfect accompaniment to the spicy Piri Piri Chicken. Simply cook your rice as usual, but replace some of the water with coconut milk for a tropical twist.

  2. Grilled Corn: Add a touch of smokiness to your meal by grilling some fresh corn on the cob. Brush the corn with melted butter and sprinkle with salt before grilling until charred and tender.

  3. Cucumber and Tomato Salad: Light and refreshing, this salad provides a cool contrast to the fiery flavors of the chicken. Slice cucumbers and tomatoes, and toss them with olive oil, lemon juice, salt, and pepper.

  4. Mozambican Piri Piri Sauce: For those who like it extra spicy, serve your Piri Piri Chicken with a side of Mozambican Piri Piri sauce. This fiery condiment is made from Piri Piri peppers, vinegar, and spices, and adds an extra kick to any dish.

Conclusion

Mozambican Piri Piri Chicken is a truly unique and flavorful dish that will transport you to the sunny shores of Mozambique. With its fiery marinade and delicious sides, this meal is sure to impress your family and friends. So why not bring the taste of Africa to your dinner table tonight? Fire up the grill, prepare your sides, and enjoy the bold and vibrant flavors of Piri Piri Chicken.
