Irresistible Dairy-Free Blueberry Muffins for a Sweet Treat

3 min · 490 words

If you’re a fan of muffins, but prefer to avoid dairy, then this recipe is for you! These dairy-free blueberry muffins are not only irresistibly delicious, but they also offer a guilt-free sweet treat. Packed with juicy blueberries and a tender, moist texture, these muffins are perfect for breakfast, brunch, or as a delightful snack anytime during the day.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 tablespoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup vegetable oil
  • 1 cup dairy-free milk (such as almond or oat milk)
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 1/2 cups fresh or frozen blueberries

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C). Line a muffin tin with paper liners or grease the cups lightly with oil.

  2. In a large mixing bowl, whisk together the flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt until well combined. This will ensure that your muffins have a consistent texture throughout.

  3. In a separate bowl, combine the vegetable oil, dairy-free milk, and vanilla extract. Mix well until the ingredients are fully incorporated.

  4. Gradually pour the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients, stirring gently until just combined. Be careful not to overmix, as this can lead to dense muffins. A few lumps in the batter are perfectly fine.

  5. Gently fold in the blueberries, making sure they are evenly distributed throughout the batter. This will ensure that every bite is bursting with juicy blueberry goodness.

  6. Fill each muffin cup about 3/4 full with the batter. You can use an ice cream scoop or a spoon to make this process easier and more consistent.

  7. Bake the muffins in the preheated oven for 20 to 25 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean. The tops should be golden brown and slightly firm to the touch.

  8. Once baked, remove the muffins from the oven and let them cool in the tin for a few minutes. Then, transfer them to a wire rack to cool completely.

  9. Serve these irresistible dairy-free blueberry muffins warm or at room temperature. They are perfect on their own, but you can also enjoy them with a dollop of dairy-free yogurt or a spread of your favorite nut butter.

Tips and Variations

  • For an extra burst of flavor, you can add a teaspoon of lemon zest to the batter. The citrusy notes will complement the sweetness of the blueberries beautifully.
  • If you prefer a sweeter muffin, you can sprinkle a little bit of sugar on top of each muffin before baking. This will give them a delightful crunch and added sweetness.
  • Feel free to experiment with different types of berries or fruits. This recipe works well with raspberries, blackberries, or even diced peaches.
  • If you have any leftovers, you can store these muffins in an airtight container at room temperature for up to 3 days. They also freeze well, so you can enjoy them later by simply thawing them at room temperature or warming them in the oven.

Conclusion

These irresistible dairy-free blueberry muffins are a delightful treat that everyone can enjoy, regardless of dietary restrictions. They are easy to make, bursting with juicy blueberries, and have a tender, moist texture that will keep you coming back for more. Whether you’re hosting a brunch, looking for a quick breakfast option, or simply craving something sweet, these muffins are sure to satisfy your taste buds. So go ahead, whip up a batch of these delectable dairy-free blueberry muffins and indulge in a guilt-free sweet treat!
