5-Ingredient Caprese Chicken Salad Recipe


If you’re looking for a quick and delicious salad recipe that’s bursting with flavor, look no further than this 5-Ingredient Caprese Chicken Salad. With just a handful of ingredients, you can create a refreshing and satisfying dish that’s perfect for any occasion.

Ingredients

  • 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 8 ounces fresh mozzarella, sliced
  • 1/4 cup fresh basil leaves, torn
  • 2 tablespoons balsamic glaze

Instructions

  1. Preheat your grill or stovetop grill pan to medium-high heat.

  2. Season the chicken breasts with salt and pepper on both sides.

  3. Place the chicken breasts on the grill and cook for about 6-8 minutes per side, or until the internal temperature reaches 165°F (74°C).

  4. Remove the chicken from the grill and let it rest for a few minutes before slicing it into thin strips.

  5. In a large bowl, combine the cherry tomatoes, fresh mozzarella, and torn basil leaves. Toss gently to combine.

  6. Add the sliced chicken to the bowl and drizzle the balsamic glaze over the top.

  7. Toss everything together until well coated.

  8. Serve immediately and enjoy!

Why You’ll Love This Recipe

This 5-Ingredient Caprese Chicken Salad is not only incredibly easy to make, but it’s also packed with flavor. The combination of juicy cherry tomatoes, creamy mozzarella, and fragrant basil creates a classic Caprese salad base. Adding the grilled chicken takes this dish to a whole new level, providing a satisfying protein boost.

The balsamic glaze adds a tangy and slightly sweet note to the salad, tying all the flavors together perfectly. It’s a versatile dressing that complements the freshness of the ingredients, making every bite a delight.

This recipe is ideal for those busy days when you want something delicious without spending hours in the kitchen. With just five simple ingredients, you can whip up this salad in no time, leaving you more time to enjoy it with your loved ones.

Tips and Variations

  • Be sure to let the chicken rest after grilling before slicing it. This allows the juices to redistribute, resulting in tender and juicy meat.

  • Feel free to customize this salad to your liking. Add some sliced avocado, roasted red peppers, or even some toasted pine nuts for extra texture and flavor.

  • If you don’t have balsamic glaze on hand, you can make your own by simmering balsamic vinegar until it thickens and becomes syrupy.

  • This salad is perfect on its own, but you can also serve it with some crusty bread or as a side dish alongside grilled meats or fish.

  • If you want to make this recipe vegetarian, simply omit the chicken and add some cooked quinoa or chickpeas for added protein.

Final Thoughts

The 5-Ingredient Caprese Chicken Salad is a simple yet flavorful dish that will impress your family and friends. With just a handful of ingredients, you can create a satisfying meal that’s perfect for any occasion. Whether you’re looking for a quick lunch or a light dinner, this recipe has got you covered. Give it a try and prepare to be amazed by the burst of flavors in every bite. Enjoy!
