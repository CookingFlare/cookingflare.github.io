One-Pan Keto Bacon-Wrapped Chicken Thighs: A Flavor Explosion

If you’re looking for a delicious and easy keto-friendly dish that’s bursting with flavor, then look no further! This one-pan recipe for bacon-wrapped chicken thighs is a game-changer. Not only is it incredibly tasty, but it’s also a breeze to make, making it the perfect meal for busy weeknights or casual gatherings with friends and family. Ingredients 6 boneless, skinless chicken thighs 12 slices of bacon 2 tablespoons olive oil 1 teaspoon garlic powder 1 teaspoon paprika 1 teaspoon dried thyme Salt and black pepper to taste Fresh parsley for garnish Instructions Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C). Season the chicken thighs with garlic powder, paprika, dried thyme, salt, and black pepper, ensuring that each piece is evenly coated with the flavorful seasoning. Carefully wrap each seasoned chicken thigh with two slices of bacon, securing them with toothpicks if necessary. In a large oven-safe skillet, heat the olive oil over medium-high heat. Once hot, add the bacon-wrapped chicken thighs to the skillet, searing them for 2-3 minutes on each side until the bacon is slightly crispy. Transfer the skillet to the preheated oven and bake for 20-25 minutes, or until the chicken is cooked through and the bacon is golden and crispy. Once done, remove the skillet from the oven and let the chicken thighs rest for a few minutes before serving. Garnish with freshly chopped parsley for a pop of color and an extra burst of freshness. Easy Low-Carb Keto Chicken Enchilada Casserole

If you’re looking for a delicious and easy low-carb keto recipe that the whole family will love, then you’ve come to the right place. This Easy Low-Carb Keto Chicken Enchilada Casserole is a flavor-packed dish that’s perfect for weeknight dinners or meal prepping for the week ahead. Packed with protein, healthy fats, and vibrant flavors, this casserole is sure to become a staple in your keto recipe collection. Ingredients 2 lbs boneless, skinless chicken breasts, cooked and shredded 1 tablespoon olive oil 1 medium onion, diced 3 cloves garlic, minced 1 red bell pepper, diced 1 teaspoon chili powder 1 teaspoon ground cumin 1/2 teaspoon dried oregano 1/2 teaspoon smoked paprika Salt and pepper to taste 1 1/2 cups enchilada sauce 1/2 cup sour cream 1/4 cup chopped fresh cilantro 2 cups shredded Mexican blend cheese 1/2 cup sliced black olives (optional) Instructions Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C). In a large skillet, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the diced onion and cook until softened, about 5 minutes. Add the minced garlic and diced red bell pepper to the skillet, and cook for an additional 2-3 minutes. Stir in the chili powder, cumin, oregano, smoked paprika, salt, and pepper. Cook for another 1-2 minutes until fragrant. Add the cooked and shredded chicken to the skillet, stirring to combine with the onion and pepper mixture. Pour in the enchilada sauce and mix well, allowing the flavors to meld together for a few minutes. Remove the skillet from the heat and stir in the sour cream and chopped cilantro until well combined. Transfer the chicken mixture to a greased 9x13 inch baking dish, spreading it out evenly. Sprinkle the shredded cheese over the top of the chicken mixture, covering it completely. If desired, add the sliced black olives on top of the cheese. Place the casserole in the preheated oven and bake for 20-25 minutes, or until the cheese is melted and bubbly. Once done, remove the casserole from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes before serving. Serving Suggestions This Easy Low-Carb Keto Chicken Enchilada Casserole is delicious on its own, but you can take it to the next level with some tasty toppings. Serve it with a dollop of sour cream, a sprinkle of fresh cilantro, and a side of crisp lettuce for a refreshing contrast. You can also add some sliced avocado or a squeeze of lime juice for added zing. ...
