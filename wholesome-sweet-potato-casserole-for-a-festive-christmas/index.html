Wholesome Sweet Potato Casserole for a Festive Christmas


The holiday season is a time for gathering with loved ones, sharing stories, and indulging in delicious food. One dish that never fails to make an appearance at the Christmas table is the sweet potato casserole. This Wholesome Sweet Potato Casserole recipe is a delightful combination of comforting flavors and festive cheer. Whether you’re hosting a grand feast or contributing a dish to a potluck, this recipe is sure to impress and leave everyone craving for more.

Ingredients

For the Sweet Potato Base:

  • 4 large sweet potatoes, peeled and cubed
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1/3 cup milk (or dairy-free alternative)
  • 1/2 cup brown sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt

For the Pecan Streusel Topping:

  • 1 cup chopped pecans
  • 1/2 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup brown sugar
  • 1/4 cup unsalted butter, melted

Instructions

Step 1: Preparing the Sweet Potatoes

  1. Begin by preheating your oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Place the peeled and cubed sweet potatoes in a large pot, cover with water, and bring to a boil.
  3. Cook the sweet potatoes until they are tender, then drain and transfer them to a large mixing bowl.

Step 2: Making the Sweet Potato Base

  1. Using a potato masher or electric mixer, mash the cooked sweet potatoes until smooth.
  2. Add the softened butter, milk, brown sugar, eggs, vanilla extract, and salt to the bowl.
  3. Mix the ingredients thoroughly until well combined and smooth.

Step 3: Assembling the Casserole

  1. Grease a baking dish with butter or cooking spray.
  2. Transfer the sweet potato mixture into the prepared baking dish, spreading it out evenly.

Step 4: Creating the Pecan Streusel Topping

  1. In a separate bowl, combine the chopped pecans, flour, brown sugar, and melted butter.
  2. Sprinkle the pecan streusel topping evenly over the sweet potato mixture in the baking dish.

Step 5: Baking the Casserole

  1. Place the assembled casserole in the preheated oven and bake for 25-30 minutes, or until the topping is golden brown and the sweet potato base is set.

Step 6: Serving and Enjoying

  1. Once the casserole is done baking, remove it from the oven and allow it to cool slightly before serving.
  2. This Wholesome Sweet Potato Casserole is best enjoyed warm, and it pairs wonderfully with savory main dishes or as a delightful standalone treat.

Why This Recipe Is Perfect for Christmas

The Wholesome Sweet Potato Casserole is a holiday favorite for several reasons. Its vibrant orange color and comforting aroma evoke the spirit of Christmas, while the decadent pecan streusel topping adds a delightful crunch that contrasts beautifully with the creamy sweet potato base. Moreover, the warm and inviting flavors of this dish make it a perfect complement to the festive atmosphere of a Christmas gathering.

Whether you’re looking to add a new twist to your traditional Christmas menu or simply want to savor a heartwarming dish, this Wholesome Sweet Potato Casserole is a must-try. It’s a versatile recipe that can easily be adapted to accommodate various dietary preferences, ensuring that everyone around the table can partake in the joy of the holiday feast.

So, this Christmas, delight your family and friends with the comforting flavors of this Wholesome Sweet Potato Casserole. From its velvety smooth base to its irresistible pecan topping, this dish is a true celebration of the season’s abundance and warmth.

I hope you enjoy this Wholesome Sweet Potato Casserole recipe and that it becomes a cherished addition to your festive Christmas traditions. Wishing you a joyous and delicious holiday season!
