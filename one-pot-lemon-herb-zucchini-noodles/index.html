One Pot Lemon Herb Zucchini Noodles


Are you looking for a quick and healthy meal that’s bursting with flavor? Look no further than this One Pot Lemon Herb Zucchini Noodles recipe. Not only is it delicious, but it’s also packed with nutrients and perfect for those following a low-carb or gluten-free diet.

Ingredients

  • 4 medium zucchini
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried thyme
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • Juice of 1 lemon
  • Zest of 1 lemon
  • Grated Parmesan cheese, for garnish (optional)

Instructions

  1. Start by spiralizing the zucchini into noodles using a spiralizer or julienne peeler. Set the zucchini noodles aside.

  2. In a large skillet, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the minced garlic and cook for about 1 minute until fragrant.

  3. Add the zucchini noodles to the skillet and toss them gently with tongs to coat them in the garlic-infused oil. Cook for about 2-3 minutes, stirring occasionally, until the zucchini noodles have softened slightly.

  4. Sprinkle the dried oregano, dried basil, dried thyme, salt, and black pepper over the zucchini noodles. Toss everything together to evenly distribute the herbs and seasonings.

  5. Pour the lemon juice and lemon zest over the zucchini noodles. Stir well to combine, ensuring that the noodles are coated in the lemony goodness.

  6. Continue cooking the zucchini noodles for another 2-3 minutes, or until they have reached your desired level of tenderness. Be careful not to overcook them, as they can become mushy.

  7. Once the zucchini noodles are done, remove them from the heat and transfer them to a serving dish. Garnish with grated Parmesan cheese if desired, for an extra burst of flavor.

  8. Serve the One Pot Lemon Herb Zucchini Noodles immediately, while they’re still hot. They can be enjoyed as a light and refreshing main dish or as a side dish to accompany grilled chicken or fish.

Tips and Variations

  • If you don’t have a spiralizer or julienne peeler, you can also use a vegetable peeler to create thin ribbons of zucchini, resembling fettuccine noodles.

  • Feel free to customize this recipe by adding your favorite protein, such as grilled shrimp or chicken, for a heartier meal.

  • For a touch of freshness and color, you can toss in some cherry tomatoes or baby spinach leaves along with the zucchini noodles.

  • To make this dish vegan, simply omit the Parmesan cheese garnish or use a dairy-free alternative.

  • Experiment with different herbs and spices to suit your taste preferences. Fresh herbs like basil, parsley, or mint can also be used to enhance the flavors.

  • Leftovers can be stored in an airtight container in the refrigerator for up to 2 days. Reheat gently in a skillet or enjoy cold as a refreshing salad.

Conclusion

This One Pot Lemon Herb Zucchini Noodles recipe is a fantastic option for those seeking a lighter and healthier alternative to traditional pasta dishes. With its zesty lemon flavor and aromatic herbs, it’s a dish that will leave you feeling satisfied and nourished. Plus, it’s incredibly easy to make, taking just minutes to prepare. So why not give it a try and add some zest to your next meal? Enjoy!
