Moist and Flavorful Apple-Stuffed Roast Pork Recipe for a Unique Thanksgiving Experience


Thanksgiving is a time for indulging in delicious, comforting meals with loved ones. While turkey tends to steal the spotlight, why not switch things up this year with a show-stopping apple-stuffed roast pork? This recipe promises moist, flavorful meat with a hint of sweetness, making it the perfect centerpiece for your Thanksgiving feast. Let’s dive into the details of this delightful dish and how to prepare it to perfection.

Ingredients

For the Pork:

  • 1 boneless pork loin (about 4-5 pounds)
  • Salt and pepper
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon fresh rosemary, chopped
  • 1 tablespoon fresh thyme, chopped

For the Apple Stuffing:

  • 2 large apples, such as Honeycrisp or Granny Smith, peeled, cored, and diced
  • 1/2 cup chopped pecans
  • 1/4 cup dried cranberries
  • 1/4 cup brown sugar
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground nutmeg
  • Pinch of salt

For the Glaze:

  • 1/2 cup apple cider
  • 2 tablespoons maple syrup
  • 1 tablespoon Dijon mustard
  • 1 tablespoon apple cider vinegar

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Pork:

    • Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
    • Butterfly the pork loin by making a lengthwise cut down the center, leaving about 1/2 inch uncut.
    • Season the inside and outside of the pork loin generously with salt and pepper.
    • In a skillet over medium heat, warm the olive oil. Add the minced garlic, rosemary, and thyme, and sauté for about 2 minutes. Remove from heat and let it cool slightly.

  2. Prepare the Apple Stuffing:

    • In a bowl, combine the diced apples, chopped pecans, dried cranberries, brown sugar, cinnamon, nutmeg, and a pinch of salt. Mix well.

  3. Assemble the Roast:

    • Spread the apple stuffing evenly over the butterflied pork loin.
    • Roll the loin tightly, starting with the long side, and secure it with kitchen twine at 1 1/2-inch intervals.

  4. Roast the Pork:

    • Place the stuffed pork loin on a roasting pan, and roast in the preheated oven for 1 1/2 to 2 hours, or until the internal temperature reaches 145°F (63°C). Baste the pork with the glaze every 20 minutes during the last hour of cooking.

  5. Prepare the Glaze:

    • In a small saucepan, combine the apple cider, maple syrup, Dijon mustard, and apple cider vinegar. Bring to a simmer over medium heat and cook for about 10 minutes, or until the glaze has slightly thickened.

  6. Serve and Enjoy:

    • Once the roast pork is cooked to perfection, remove it from the oven and let it rest for 15 minutes before slicing. Serve the apple-stuffed roast pork with the remaining glaze on the side for an extra burst of flavor.

Conclusion

This apple-stuffed roast pork recipe is a delightful departure from the traditional Thanksgiving turkey, offering a unique and flavorful alternative. The combination of juicy pork, sweet apples, and aromatic spices is sure to impress your guests and elevate your holiday spread. So, this Thanksgiving, dare to be different and treat your loved ones to a memorable and mouthwatering meal with this delectable apple-stuffed roast pork recipe. Cheers to a unique and delicious Thanksgiving experience!
