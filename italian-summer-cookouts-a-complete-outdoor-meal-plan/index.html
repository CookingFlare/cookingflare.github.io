Italian Summer Cookouts: A Complete Outdoor Meal Plan


Summer in Italy is nothing short of magical. The warm breeze, the inviting smell of grilling, and the sound of clinking wine glasses in the background create a perfect outdoor dining atmosphere. To capture the essence of an authentic Italian summer cookout, we’ve put together a complete outdoor meal plan filled with delicious, easy-to-prepare dishes that are perfect for sharing with your friends and family.

Antipasti

Start the meal with a platter of delectable antipasti that will tantalize your taste buds and set the stage for a delightful feast. Our Italian-style antipasti platter features:

  1. Marinated Artichoke Hearts: Artichoke hearts marinated in olive oil, garlic, lemon juice, and oregano. This simple yet flavorful side dish is a staple in every Italian cookout.

  2. Morbidella Olive Crostini: Mini toasts topped with sliced olives, cherry tomatoes, and a drizzle of balsamic glaze. A perfect balance of flavors for even the pickiest appetizer enthusiasts.

  3. Roasted Red Pepper Hummus: A creamy blend of roasted red peppers, tahini, lemon juice, and garlic, perfect for scooping with fresh veggies or warm pita bread.

Primo Piatti

Next up, we’ve prepared a few primo piatti (first courses) dishes to kick off your meal:

  1. Grilled Vegetable Caprese: A spin on the classic caprese salad, featuring grilled zucchini, eggplant, and summer tomatoes, beautifully drizzled with balsamic glaze, and topped with fresh basil and buffalo mozzarella.

  2. Pesto Spaghetti: Sautee spaghetti with a generous amount of fresh pesto made from basil, garlic, pine nuts, Parmigiano-Reggiano, and extra virgin olive oil. Add some cherry tomatoes and you’ve got a pesto spaghetti that will make anyone’s mouth water.

Secondo Piatti

For the heartiest part of the meal, we’ve put together a classic Italian secondo piatti (second courses) dish:

  1. Grilled Lamb Chops with Mint Pesto: Tender, juicy lamb chops marinated in a mixture of mint, garlic, parmesan, and extra virgin olive oil. Pair it with crusty bread or a side of farro salad for a perfect balance.

Contorni

No Italian summer cookout is complete without a range of contorni (side dishes). Here are a few to add to your outdoor feast:

  1. Grilled Asparagus with Lemon and Garlic: Season thin asparagus spears with lemon, garlic, salt, and pepper, then grill until tender and slightly charred.

  2. Lemony Orzo Salad: A refreshing orzo salad with cherry tomatoes, cucumber, fresh mint, and a tangy lemon vinaigrette. This versatile side dish adds a bright pop of flavor to your cookout spread.

Dolci

End the meal on a sweet note with a selection of Italian dolci (desserts):

  1. Tiramisu Cups: Classic Italian tiramisu in a portable cup form. Layers of coffee-soaked ladyfinger, creamy ricotta mascarpone, and freshly brewed espresso ensure that your guests will beg for seconds.

  2. Sicilian Cannoli Casserole: A simplified version of the famous Sicilian pastry, featuring a crowd-pleasing casserole that combines ricotta, pistachios, mini cannoli shells, and a drizzle of chocolate sauce.

Beverages

No Italian cookout is complete without a drink or two, so why not finish off the meal with an Italian-inspired beverage?

  1. Aperol Spritz: A refreshing, citrus-based drink made with Aperol, Prosecco, and a splash of soda water. It’s the perfect summer drink to share with friends over good conversation or music.

With this Italian Summer Cookouts meal plan, you’re all set to enjoy the best of Italy right in your very own backyard. Say ciao to the mundane and hello to an unforgettable outdoor feast with your nearest and dearest.
