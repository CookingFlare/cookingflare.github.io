A French Twist on Pizza: Tarte Flambée


If you’re a pizza lover looking to add a touch of French flair to your culinary repertoire, then Tarte Flambée is the recipe for you. This delicious dish originates from the Alsace region of France, where it is often referred to as “Flammekueche.” Tarte Flambée is a thin, crispy crust topped with a creamy white sauce, caramelized onions, and crispy bacon. It’s a perfect combination of flavors and textures that will transport your taste buds to the heart of France.

Ingredients

For the dough:

  • 2 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • 1 cup lukewarm water
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil

For the topping:

  • 2 large onions, thinly sliced
  • 8 ounces bacon, thinly sliced
  • 1 cup crème fraîche
  • Salt and black pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 500°F (260°C).

  2. In a large mixing bowl, combine the flour, salt, and sugar. Gradually add the lukewarm water and olive oil, mixing until the dough comes together.

  3. Transfer the dough to a floured surface and knead for about 5 minutes until smooth and elastic. Shape the dough into a ball and let it rest for 15 minutes.

  4. Meanwhile, heat a skillet over medium heat and cook the bacon until crispy. Remove the bacon from the pan and set it aside.

  5. In the same skillet, add the thinly sliced onions and cook them over medium-low heat until soft and golden brown, stirring occasionally. This process may take around 15-20 minutes. Once caramelized, remove the onions from the heat and set them aside.

  6. Roll out the dough into a thin rectangle or circle, depending on your preference. Place the rolled-out dough on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper.

  7. Spread a generous layer of crème fraîche over the dough, leaving a small border around the edges. The crème fraîche acts as a creamy base for the toppings.

  8. Sprinkle the caramelized onions evenly over the crème fraîche. Then, distribute the crispy bacon slices on top.

  9. Season with salt and black pepper to taste.

  10. Carefully transfer the baking sheet to the preheated oven and bake the Tarte Flambée for about 12-15 minutes, or until the edges of the crust are golden brown and the topping is bubbling and slightly browned.

  11. Remove the Tarte Flambée from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes before cutting it into slices.

  12. Serve the Tarte Flambée hot and enjoy the harmonious blend of flavors that will transport you to the charming streets of Alsace.

Conclusion

Tarte Flambée is a delightful twist on traditional pizza that brings the flavors of France to your table. The crispy crust, creamy white sauce, caramelized onions, and crispy bacon create a symphony of tastes and textures that will please even the most discerning palate. Serve this delicious dish as an appetizer, a main course, or even as a snack during social gatherings. It’s versatile, easy to make, and guaranteed to impress your guests. So, why not add a French twist to your pizza night with Tarte Flambée? Bon appétit!
