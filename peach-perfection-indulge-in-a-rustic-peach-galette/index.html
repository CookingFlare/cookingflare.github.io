Peach Perfection: Indulge in a Rustic Peach Galette


If you’re a fan of the sweet, juicy goodness of peaches, then you’re in for a treat. Today, we’re going to take your love for peaches to a whole new level with a delicious and rustic Peach Galette. This simple yet elegant dessert will impress your family and friends with its flaky crust and luscious peach filling. So let’s roll up our sleeves and get baking!

Ingredients

For the crust:

  • 2 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tablespoon granulated sugar
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup unsalted butter, cold and cut into small cubes
  • 1/2 cup ice water

For the peach filling:

  • 4 cups fresh peaches, peeled and sliced
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 2 tablespoons cornstarch
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon

For the egg wash:

  • 1 egg, beaten
  • 1 tablespoon milk

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).

  2. In a large mixing bowl, whisk together the flour, sugar, and salt for the crust. Add the cold butter cubes, and using a pastry cutter or your fingertips, cut the butter into the flour mixture until it resembles coarse crumbs.

  3. Gradually add the ice water, a few tablespoons at a time, and mix until the dough comes together. Be careful not to overmix. Form the dough into a disk, wrap it in plastic wrap, and refrigerate for at least 30 minutes.

  4. While the dough is chilling, prepare the peach filling. In a separate bowl, combine the sliced peaches, sugar, cornstarch, vanilla extract, and cinnamon. Gently toss everything together until the peaches are coated evenly. Set aside.

  5. Remove the chilled dough from the refrigerator and place it on a lightly floured surface. Roll it out into a 12-inch circle, about 1/4 inch thick. Transfer the rolled-out dough onto a parchment-lined baking sheet.

  6. Spoon the peach filling onto the center of the dough, leaving a 2-inch border around the edges. Gently fold the edges of the dough over the peaches, overlapping as needed. Press down lightly to secure the folds.

  7. In a small bowl, whisk together the beaten egg and milk to make the egg wash. Brush the edges of the galette with the egg wash.

  8. Bake the galette in the preheated oven for about 40-45 minutes, or until the crust is golden brown and the peaches are tender. Remove from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes before serving.

  9. Serve the Peach Galette warm or at room temperature. You can enjoy it as is, or if you’re feeling extra indulgent, serve it with a scoop of vanilla ice cream or a dollop of whipped cream. The choice is yours!

Tips and Variations

  • Feel free to experiment with different fruits for the filling. You can try using a combination of peaches and berries, or even swap the peaches for apples or pears.
  • For an extra touch of flavor, sprinkle some sliced almonds or a dusting of powdered sugar on top of the galette before baking.
  • If you’re short on time, you can use store-bought pie crust instead of making your own. Just roll it out and follow the same instructions for assembling the galette.

Final Thoughts

There you have it – a delightful Peach Galette that celebrates the natural sweetness of peaches in a rustic and charming way. This dessert is perfect for summer gatherings, afternoon tea, or simply as a treat for yourself. So go ahead, grab those fresh peaches and create your own Peach Perfection! Your taste buds will thank you.
