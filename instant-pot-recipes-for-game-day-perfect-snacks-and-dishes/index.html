Instant Pot Recipes for Game Day: Perfect Snacks and Dishes


When game day rolls around, the pressure is on to serve up delicious, crowd-pleasing snacks and dishes that will keep your guests energized and satisfied. Whether you’re hosting a watch party, tailgating with friends, or simply indulging in a cozy night in, Instant Pot recipes are a game-changer for creating flavorful dishes in no time. With an Instant Pot, you can whip up everything from savory dips to hearty mains, all while enjoying the convenience of minimal preparation and clean-up.

In this post, we’ll walk you through a collection of irresistible Instant Pot recipes that are perfect for game day. These dishes are not only easy to make, but they’re also designed to please a variety of tastes, ensuring everyone is covered. From crispy chicken wings to creamy dips, there’s something for everyone!

Why Choose Instant Pot for Game Day?

Before diving into the recipes, let’s quickly explore why the Instant Pot is your best friend when it comes to game day cooking.

Speed and Efficiency

Game day is all about having fun, not slaving away in the kitchen. With the Instant Pot, you can cut cooking time drastically. The pressure cooking function reduces the time it takes to cook stews, meats, and even beans, so you can spend less time cooking and more time cheering on your team.

Set It and Forget It

Many Instant Pot recipes allow you to simply set the machine, walk away, and come back to perfectly cooked dishes. This is ideal when you’re juggling other tasks or want to enjoy the game without being glued to the stove.

Flavor Infusion

The Instant Pot locks in all the flavors of your ingredients, making your dishes richer and more flavorful. Whether you’re making a chili, stew, or even a dessert, the pressure cooking process helps ingredients meld together beautifully.

Now, let’s get to the fun part—the recipes!

1. Instant Pot Chicken Wings: Crispy and Delicious

Chicken wings are a game day classic, but getting them crispy can be a challenge. The Instant Pot is your solution for cooking tender wings quickly, while a quick broil in the oven will give them that crispy texture we all crave.

Ingredients:

  • 2 lbs chicken wings
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon onion powder
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1/4 cup chicken broth
  • 1/2 cup buffalo sauce (or your favorite wing sauce)

Instructions:

  1. Prep the Wings: Pat the chicken wings dry with paper towels. This step is key for achieving a crispy exterior.
  2. Season the Wings: In a bowl, toss the wings with olive oil, salt, garlic powder, onion powder, paprika, and black pepper until evenly coated.
  3. Pressure Cook: Pour the chicken broth into the Instant Pot and place the wings on the trivet inside the pot. Secure the lid, set the vent to sealing, and cook on high pressure for 10 minutes. Once the cooking time is up, allow a natural release for 5 minutes, then perform a quick release for any remaining pressure.
  4. Crisp the Wings: Preheat your broiler to high. Transfer the wings to a baking sheet and brush with buffalo sauce (or your sauce of choice). Broil for 3-5 minutes, flipping once, until they’re golden and crispy.
  5. Serve: Serve the wings hot with celery, carrot sticks, and dipping sauce.

2. Instant Pot Pulled Pork Sliders

Pulled pork sliders are always a hit at game day gatherings. The Instant Pot makes cooking this dish incredibly simple while ensuring the pork is tender, juicy, and packed with flavor.

Ingredients:

  • 3 lbs pork shoulder (or pork butt)
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup barbecue sauce (plus extra for serving)
  • 1/2 cup apple cider vinegar
  • 1/2 cup chicken broth
  • 1 tablespoon brown sugar
  • 1 teaspoon smoked paprika
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Slider buns
  • Pickles (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Sear the Pork: Set your Instant Pot to the “Sauté” function and add olive oil. Sear the pork shoulder on all sides until golden brown. This step adds a depth of flavor to the meat.
  2. Sauté the Aromatics: Remove the pork from the pot and set it aside. In the same pot, add the diced onion and minced garlic. Sauté for 2-3 minutes until fragrant.
  3. Pressure Cook: Add the barbecue sauce, apple cider vinegar, chicken broth, brown sugar, smoked paprika, cumin, salt, and pepper to the pot. Stir to combine. Return the pork shoulder to the pot, ensuring it’s submerged in the liquid. Secure the lid and set the Instant Pot to high pressure for 60 minutes. Once done, let the pressure release naturally for 10 minutes, then do a quick release for any remaining pressure.
  4. Shred the Pork: Remove the pork from the Instant Pot and shred it using two forks. Return the shredded pork to the pot and stir it in the sauce.
  5. Assemble the Sliders: Spoon the pulled pork onto slider buns, top with pickles if desired, and serve with extra barbecue sauce.

3. Instant Pot Chili: A Hearty Crowd-Pleaser

Chili is another classic dish that’s perfect for game day. The Instant Pot’s pressure cooking function ensures that all the flavors meld together quickly, creating a rich and hearty chili that will warm you up on even the chilliest game days.

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb ground beef or turkey
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 1 bell pepper, diced
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can (14.5 oz) diced tomatoes
  • 1 can (15 oz) kidney beans, drained and rinsed
  • 1 can (15 oz) black beans, drained and rinsed
  • 1 can (6 oz) tomato paste
  • 1 cup beef or vegetable broth
  • 1 tablespoon chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • 1/2 teaspoon smoked paprika
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Shredded cheese (for topping)
  • Sour cream (for topping)
  • Jalapeños (for topping)

Instructions:

  1. Sauté the Meat and Veggies: Set the Instant Pot to “Sauté” and cook the ground beef (or turkey), breaking it up with a spoon, until browned. Remove the meat and set it aside. In the same pot, sauté the onion, bell pepper, and garlic until softened, about 3-4 minutes.
  2. Add the Ingredients: Add the browned meat back into the pot along with the diced tomatoes, beans, tomato paste, broth, chili powder, cumin, smoked paprika, salt, and pepper. Stir to combine.
  3. Pressure Cook: Secure the lid and set the Instant Pot to high pressure for 15 minutes. After the cooking time is up, allow a natural release for 10 minutes, then quick release any remaining pressure.
  4. Serve: Ladle the chili into bowls and top with shredded cheese, sour cream, and jalapeños for an extra kick.

4. Instant Pot Queso Dip

No game day spread is complete without a warm and creamy dip, and this Instant Pot queso dip is sure to be the star of the show. It’s cheesy, flavorful, and incredibly easy to make!

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb Velveeta cheese, cubed
  • 1 can (10 oz) diced tomatoes with green chilies, undrained
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1/2 teaspoon cumin
  • 1/4 teaspoon chili powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Combine the Ingredients: Add the cubed Velveeta cheese, diced tomatoes with green chilies, milk, cumin, chili powder, salt, and pepper to the Instant Pot.
  2. Cook: Set the Instant Pot to “Sauté” and cook, stirring occasionally, until the cheese is completely melted and smooth. You can also set it to low pressure for 5 minutes if you want a more hands-off approach.
  3. Serve: Pour the dip into a serving bowl and serve with tortilla chips, crackers, or fresh veggies for dipping.

5. Instant Pot Meatball Subs

Meatball subs are the ultimate comfort food, and the Instant Pot makes preparing these savory sandwiches a breeze. The meatballs come out tender and juicy, while the marinara sauce is perfectly seasoned and rich.

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1/2 cup breadcrumbs
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon Italian seasoning
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 jar marinara sauce
  • 4 sub rolls
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese

Instructions:

  1. Make the Meatballs: In a large bowl, mix together the ground beef, breadcrumbs, Parmesan cheese, egg, garlic, Italian seasoning, salt, and pepper. Form the mixture into small meatballs (about 1 inch in diameter).
  2. Cook the Meatballs: Pour marinara sauce into the Instant Pot and gently place the meatballs into the sauce. Secure the lid and cook on high pressure for 10 minutes. Allow a quick release of the pressure once the cooking time is up.
  3. Assemble the Subs: Toast the sub rolls in the oven or on a grill pan. Spoon the meatballs and sauce into the rolls, then top with shredded mozzarella cheese.
  4. Serve: Serve the meatball subs warm with a side of chips or salad.

Conclusion

With these Instant Pot recipes, game day cooking becomes effortless and fun. Whether you’re in the mood for tender chicken wings, savory pulled pork sliders, hearty chili, or cheesy dips, the Instant Pot allows you to prepare these dishes quickly and easily. These crowd-pleasing recipes are perfect for serving a large group, ensuring that everyone has something to enjoy while watching the game. So, fire up your Instant Pot, gather your friends and family, and get ready for a delicious game day feast!
