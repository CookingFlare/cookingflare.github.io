Grilled Halloumi Salad: A Mediterranean Delight


Are you looking for a refreshing and flavorful salad that will transport you straight to the sunny shores of the Mediterranean? Look no further than this Grilled Halloumi Salad! Packed with vibrant colors, bold flavors, and a perfect balance of textures, this salad is a true culinary delight. Whether you’re a vegetarian or simply looking to add more veggies to your diet, this recipe is a winner.

Ingredients

  • 250g Halloumi cheese, cut into thick slices
  • 2 cups mixed salad greens
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1 cucumber, thinly sliced
  • 1 red onion, thinly sliced
  • 1/4 cup Kalamata olives, pitted
  • 2 tablespoons fresh mint leaves, chopped
  • 2 tablespoons fresh parsley, chopped
  • Juice of 1 lemon
  • 3 tablespoons extra-virgin olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Preheat your grill or grill pan to medium-high heat. While the grill is heating up, prepare the salad ingredients.

  2. In a large mixing bowl, combine the salad greens, cherry tomatoes, cucumber, red onion, Kalamata olives, mint leaves, and parsley. Toss gently to combine.

  3. In a separate small bowl, whisk together the lemon juice, olive oil, salt, and pepper. Set aside.

  4. Place the Halloumi cheese slices on the preheated grill or grill pan. Cook for about 2-3 minutes on each side, or until the cheese develops grill marks and becomes slightly golden brown.

  5. Remove the grilled Halloumi slices from the grill and let them cool for a minute. Then, cut the slices into bite-sized pieces.

  6. Add the grilled Halloumi pieces to the salad bowl and pour the prepared lemon dressing over the top. Gently toss the salad to evenly coat the ingredients with the dressing.

  7. Serve immediately and enjoy the burst of flavors in every bite!

Tips and Variations

  • Halloumi is a unique cheese that holds its shape when grilled or pan-fried, making it the perfect addition to salads. If you can’t find Halloumi, you can substitute it with a firm and salty cheese like feta or paneer.

  • To add a touch of sweetness to your salad, consider adding some fresh pomegranate seeds or a drizzle of honey to the dressing.

  • For an extra crunch, sprinkle some toasted pine nuts or slivered almonds over the salad before serving.

  • If you prefer a warmer salad, you can grill the cherry tomatoes, cucumber, and red onion alongside the Halloumi. Just toss them with a little olive oil, salt, and pepper before grilling.

  • This Grilled Halloumi Salad makes a fantastic side dish for grilled meats or a satisfying vegetarian main course. You can also serve it as a light lunch or dinner on its own.

In Conclusion

With its combination of smoky grilled Halloumi, crisp vegetables, and zesty lemon dressing, this Mediterranean-inspired salad is sure to become a favorite in your kitchen. It’s a celebration of fresh, vibrant flavors and the perfect way to enjoy the bountiful produce of the season. So, fire up the grill, gather your ingredients, and dive into the deliciousness of this Grilled Halloumi Salad. Bon appétit!
