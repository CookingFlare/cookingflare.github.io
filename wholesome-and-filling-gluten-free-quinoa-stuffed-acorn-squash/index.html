Wholesome and Filling: Gluten-Free Quinoa Stuffed Acorn Squash


Are you looking for a delicious and nutritious gluten-free recipe that will leave you feeling satisfied? Look no further! This Wholesome and Filling: Gluten-Free Quinoa Stuffed Acorn Squash recipe is the perfect dish to add to your repertoire. Packed with wholesome ingredients and bursting with flavor, this recipe is sure to become a household favorite.

Ingredients

  • 2 acorn squash
  • 1 cup quinoa
  • 2 cups vegetable broth
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup mushrooms, sliced
  • 1 cup spinach, chopped
  • 1/2 cup dried cranberries
  • 1/2 cup pecans, chopped
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C).

  2. Cut the acorn squash in half lengthwise and scoop out the seeds. Place the squash halves cut-side down on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper. Bake for 30-35 minutes, or until the squash is tender.

  3. While the squash is baking, rinse the quinoa under cold water. In a medium saucepan, bring the vegetable broth to a boil. Add the quinoa, reduce the heat to low, cover, and let it simmer for about 15 minutes or until all the liquid is absorbed.

  4. In a large skillet, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the chopped onion and minced garlic and sauté until fragrant and translucent.

  5. Add the sliced mushrooms to the skillet and cook until they have released their moisture and are golden brown. Stir in the chopped spinach and cook until wilted.

  6. In a large bowl, combine the cooked quinoa, sautéed vegetables, dried cranberries, chopped pecans, dried thyme, and season with salt and pepper to taste. Mix well until all the ingredients are evenly distributed.

  7. Remove the baked acorn squash from the oven and carefully flip them over. Fill each squash half with the quinoa stuffing, pressing it down gently to make sure it’s tightly packed.

  8. Return the stuffed squash to the oven and bake for an additional 10-15 minutes, or until the stuffing is heated through and slightly golden.

  9. Serve the Gluten-Free Quinoa Stuffed Acorn Squash as a wholesome main dish or a hearty side. The combination of flavors and textures will leave you feeling satisfied and nourished.

Final Thoughts

This Wholesome and Filling: Gluten-Free Quinoa Stuffed Acorn Squash recipe is a perfect option for those following a gluten-free diet or simply looking to incorporate more nutritious ingredients into their meals. The combination of the tender acorn squash, fluffy quinoa, sautéed vegetables, and crunchy pecans creates a delightful contrast of flavors and textures.

Not only is this recipe delicious, but it’s also packed with nutrients. Quinoa is a complete protein, providing all nine essential amino acids, while the acorn squash offers a good dose of dietary fiber, vitamins, and minerals. The addition of mushrooms, spinach, and dried cranberries adds extra depth of flavor and nutritional value.

Whether you’re cooking for yourself or a crowd, this recipe is sure to impress. So, why not give it a try and see for yourself how delicious and satisfying a gluten-free meal can be? Enjoy!
