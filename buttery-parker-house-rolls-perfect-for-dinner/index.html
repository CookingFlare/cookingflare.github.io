Buttery Parker House Rolls: Perfect for Dinner


Parker House rolls, known for their rich buttery flavor and tender texture, have been a classic dinner favorite for generations. Over time, the rolls have remained popular in various social gatherings, from family dinners to cocktail parties. Join us as we explore the world of Parker House rolls and share the best recipes to perfect your homemade dinner staple.

Recipe: Traditional Buttery Parker House Rolls

Ingredients

  • 1 cup warm water (110 degrees F/45 degrees C)
  • 2 1/4 teaspoons active dry yeast
  • 1/4 cup white sugar
  • 2 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons room temperature butter
  • 1 teaspoon salt

Instructions

  1. In a bowl, mix together warm water, yeast, and sugar. Let the mixture sit for about 5 minutes until it becomes frothy.
  2. In a separate bowl, combine the flour and softened butter. Mix well then add the yeast mixture and salt. Stir until the dough comes together.
  3. Knead the dough until smooth and elastic. Cover the bowl and allow the dough to rise in a warm place for about 1 hour or until it doubles in size.
  4. Preheat the oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C). Grease a baking sheet.
  5. Gently punch down the dough and divide it into equal portions. Shape each portion into a ball, then flatten slightly into a disc.
  6. Place the discs on the prepared baking sheet about 2 inches apart, cover, and let rest for another 10 minutes.
  7. Bake in a preheated oven for 15-20 minutes until golden brown.
  8. Enjoy your warm and buttery Parker House rolls with your favorite dinner dishes.

Recipe: Savory Garlic and Herb Parker House Rolls

Ingredients

  • 1 cup warm water (110 degrees F/45 degrees C)
  • 2 1/4 teaspoons active dry yeast
  • 1/4 cup white sugar
  • 2 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons room temperature butter
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 2 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley
  • 1 tablespoon dried oregano
  • 1 tablespoon minced garlic

Instructions

For these rolls, follow the same steps from the traditional recipe (steps 1-7) and add in the following:

  1. While kneading the dough, mix in parsley and oregano. Ensure to evenly distribute the herbs throughout the dough.
  2. Flatten the dough portions into discs and spread minced garlic evenly on top.

Enjoy these savory garlic and herb Parker House rolls as a delightfully versatile dinner accompaniment. Goes wonderfully with soups, stews, and roasted meats.

Recipe: Sweet Cinnamon Parker House Rolls

Ingredients

  • 1 cup warm water (110 degrees F/45 degrees C)
  • 2 1/4 teaspoons active dry yeast
  • 1/4 cup white sugar
  • 2 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons room temperature butter
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 2 tablespoons cinnamon sugar mixture

Instructions

For this recipe, follow steps 1-7 from the traditional recipe and add in the following:

  1. While kneading the dough, knead in the cinnamon sugar mixture. Make sure cinnamon sugar is evenly distributed throughout the dough.
  2. Flatten the dough portions into discs and sprinkle a bit of cinnamon sugar on top.

These sweet cinnamon Parker House rolls are the perfect addition to dessert or as a breakfast treat. Pair them with butter or cream cheese for a delicious treat.

Dive into the world of Parker House rolls by trying out these recipes and surprise your loved ones with the warmth and flavor of homemade dinner rolls. Whether you prefer them savory or sweet, the buttery Parker House rolls remain a timeless dinner classic. Happy cooking!
