Simple and Fun Family Pancake Recipes for Breakfast


Pancakes are a beloved breakfast staple in many households, and for good reason. They are not only delicious and versatile but also simple to prepare. Whether you’re cooking for a large family or just want to indulge in a sweet treat, pancakes offer a canvas for creativity. In this blog post, we will explore a variety of simple and fun family pancake recipes that will bring joy to your breakfast table. From classic buttermilk pancakes to exciting flavor combinations, there’s something here for everyone. So, grab your apron, and let’s get cooking!

The Classic Buttermilk Pancake

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons granulated sugar
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup buttermilk
  • 1 large egg
  • 2 tablespoons melted butter
  • Cooking spray or additional butter for the pan

Instructions:

  1. In a large mixing bowl, whisk together the flour, sugar, baking powder, baking soda, and salt.
  2. In a separate bowl, mix together the buttermilk, egg, and melted butter until fully combined.
  3. Pour the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients and stir gently until just combined. Be careful not to overmix; a few lumps are okay.
  4. Preheat a non-stick skillet or griddle over medium heat and lightly grease with cooking spray or butter.
  5. Pour about 1/4 cup of batter onto the skillet for each pancake. Cook until bubbles form on the surface, then flip and cook until golden brown on the other side.
  6. Serve warm with maple syrup, fresh fruit, or your favorite toppings.

Fluffy Banana Pancakes

Banana pancakes are a great way to use up ripe bananas while adding natural sweetness to your breakfast.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons granulated sugar
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup buttermilk
  • 1 large egg
  • 1 ripe banana, mashed
  • 2 tablespoons melted butter
  • Sliced bananas for topping

Instructions:

  1. In a mixing bowl, combine the flour, sugar, baking powder, baking soda, and salt.
  2. In another bowl, mix the buttermilk, egg, mashed banana, and melted butter until well blended.
  3. Add the wet ingredients to the dry ingredients and gently mix until just combined.
  4. Heat a skillet over medium heat and grease lightly.
  5. Pour 1/4 cup of batter for each pancake, and cook until bubbles appear. Flip and cook until golden brown.
  6. Serve with sliced bananas and a drizzle of honey or syrup.

Chocolate Chip Pancakes

For the chocolate lovers in the family, these pancakes are a must-try!

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons granulated sugar
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup buttermilk
  • 1 large egg
  • 2 tablespoons melted butter
  • 1/2 cup chocolate chips

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, whisk together the flour, sugar, baking powder, baking soda, and salt.
  2. In another bowl, combine the buttermilk, egg, and melted butter.
  3. Mix the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients and stir in the chocolate chips.
  4. Heat your skillet and grease it before pouring the batter.
  5. Cook each pancake until bubbles form and then flip to finish cooking.
  6. Serve warm with additional chocolate chips or whipped cream.

Savory Spinach and Feta Pancakes

For those who prefer a savory breakfast, these spinach and feta pancakes are a delicious option packed with nutrients.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup buttermilk
  • 1 large egg
  • 1 cup fresh spinach, chopped
  • 1/2 cup feta cheese, crumbled
  • Olive oil for cooking

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, combine flour, baking powder, baking soda, and salt.
  2. In another bowl, mix the buttermilk, egg, chopped spinach, and crumbled feta.
  3. Combine the wet and dry ingredients until just mixed.
  4. Heat olive oil in a skillet over medium heat.
  5. Pour the batter and cook until golden brown on both sides.
  6. Serve with a dollop of Greek yogurt or tzatziki sauce.

Apple Cinnamon Pancakes

Apple cinnamon pancakes are perfect for fall but delightful year-round. The combination of warm spices and sweet apples is irresistible.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons brown sugar
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1 cup buttermilk
  • 1 large egg
  • 1 apple, peeled and diced
  • 2 tablespoons melted butter

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, whisk together flour, brown sugar, baking powder, baking soda, salt, and cinnamon.
  2. In another bowl, mix buttermilk, egg, and melted butter.
  3. Combine the wet and dry ingredients and fold in the diced apple.
  4. Preheat your skillet and grease it.
  5. Pour the batter and cook until bubbles form, then flip to cook through.
  6. Serve with warm maple syrup and extra cinnamon if desired.

Blueberry Lemon Pancakes

Brighten up your breakfast with the refreshing flavors of blueberries and lemon.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons granulated sugar
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup buttermilk
  • 1 large egg
  • 2 tablespoons melted butter
  • Zest of 1 lemon
  • 1/2 cup fresh blueberries

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, mix flour, sugar, baking powder, baking soda, and salt.
  2. In another bowl, whisk together buttermilk, egg, melted butter, and lemon zest.
  3. Combine the wet and dry ingredients, then fold in the blueberries.
  4. Heat a skillet and grease it lightly.
  5. Pour the batter and cook until bubbles appear, then flip to finish.
  6. Serve with lemon curd or syrup.
