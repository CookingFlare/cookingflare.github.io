10 Explosive Juice & Smoothie Recipes for the Chocolate Lover


Are you a chocolate aficionado looking for a new way to incorporate your favorite treat into your daily routine? Look no further! Here are 10 jaw-dropping juice and smoothie recipes that will satisfy your chocolate cravings without weighing you down.

1. Chocolate Avocado Smoothie

Creamy, chocolaty, and packed with healthy fats, this smoothie is perfect for breakfast or a mid-day snack.

Ingredients:

  • 1 ripe avocado
  • 1 cup unsweetened almond milk
  • 1/4 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1 pitted Medjool date
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • Pinch of salt
  • Ice cubes

Instructions:

  1. Add ingredients into a blender and blend until smooth.
  2. Pour into a glass and serve immediately.

2. Chocolate Spinach Smoothie

This velvety smoothie is packed with iron and fiber, making it a great addition to your breakfast routine. Kids will love it too!

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups fresh spinach leaves
  • 1 ripe banana
  • 1/2 cup unsweetened almond milk
  • 1/4 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1 pitted Medjool date
  • 1 teaspoon chia seeds
  • 1/4 cup ice cubes

Instructions:

  1. Add ingredients into a blender and blend until smooth.
  2. Pour into a glass and serve immediately.

3. Blackberry Chocolate Smoothie Bowl

A luscious blend of dark chocolate and tangy blackberries, this smoothie bowl is served chilled for a refreshing treat.

Ingredients:

  • 1 frozen banana
  • 1/2 cup unsweetened almond milk
  • 2 tablespoons unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1/2 cup frozen blackberries
  • 1 tablespoon almond butter
  • 1/4 cup rolled oats

Instructions:

  1. Add rolled oats to a blender and blend until fine.
  2. Add remaining ingredients and blend until smooth.
  3. Pour into a bowl and top with extra blackberries and a drizzle of almond butter.

4. Chocolate Coffee Smoothie

A classic combo with a chocolate twist, this smoothie is perfect for mornings when you need an extra caffeine boost.

Ingredients:

  • 1 frozen banana
  • 1/2 cup unsweetened almond milk
  • 1/4 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1/2 cup brewed cold coffee
  • 1 tablespoon hazelnut butter
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/4 teaspoon cinnamon

Instructions:

  1. Add all ingredients into a blender and blend until smooth.
  2. Pour into a glass and serve immediately.

5. Chocolate Avocado Mousse

A guilt-free treat that tastes like a decadent chocolate mousse, this recipe is sure to impress your dinner guests.

Ingredients:

  • 2 ripe avocados
  • 1/2 cup unsweetened almond milk
  • 1/4 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1 pitted Medjool date
  • 1 tablespoon chia seeds
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • Pinch of salt

Instructions:

  1. Scoop avocado flesh into a blender.
  2. Add remaining ingredients and blend until smooth.
  3. Spoon into a bowl and serve chilled.

6. Chocolate Banana Juice

This sweet and creamy juice is a delicious twist on the classic banana smoothie.

Ingredients:

  • 2 ripe bananas
  • 1 cup unsweetened almond milk
  • 1/4 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 1 pitted Medjool date

Instructions:

  1. Add all ingredients into a blender and blend until smooth.
  2. Pour into a glass and serve immediately.

7. Chocolate Blueberry Smoothie

A delightful combination of dark chocolate and tart blueberries - perfect for a mid-day pick-me-up!

Ingredients:

  • 1 frozen banana
  • 1/2 cup unsweetened almond milk
  • 1/4 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1/2 cup frozen blueberries
  • 1 tablespoon almond butter

Instructions:

  1. Add all ingredients into a blender and blend until smooth.
  2. Pour into a glass and serve immediately.

8. Chocolate Cherry Smoothie

A festive blend of dark chocolate and cherries, this smoothie is a delightful treat that’s perfect for the holiday season.

Ingredients:

  • 1 frozen banana
  • 1/2 cup unsweetened almond milk
  • 1/4 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1/2 cup frozen dark cherries
  • 1 tablespoon cashew butter

Instructions:

  1. Add all ingredients into a blender and blend until smooth.
  2. Pour into a glass and serve immediately.

9. Chocolate Ginger Smoothie

A unique twist on a classic chocolate smoothie, this warm and spicy blend is perfect for those chilly days.

Ingredients:

  • 1 frozen banana
  • 1/2 cup unsweetened almond milk
  • 1/4 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1/2 inch ginger, peeled and chopped
  • 1 tablespoon coconut oil
  • 1/2 teaspoon cinnamon

Instructions:

  1. Add all ingredients into a blender and blend until smooth.
  2. Pour into a glass and serve immediately.

10. Chocolate Matcha Smoothie

An antioxidant powerhouse, this green chocolate smoothie is perfect for those looking for a boost in energy throughout the day.

Ingredients:

  • 1 frozen banana
  • 1/2 cup unsweetened almond milk
  • 1/4 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1 teaspoon matcha powder
  • 1 tablespoon almond butter
  • 1/2 cup ice cubes

Instructions:

  1. Add all ingredients into a blender and blend until smooth.
  2. Pour into a glass and serve immediately.

Now that you have a handful of delicious, chocolate-infused juice and smoothie recipes to try, you can satisfy your cravings without sacrificing your health. Happy blending!
