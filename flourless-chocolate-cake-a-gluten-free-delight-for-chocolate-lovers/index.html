Flourless Chocolate Cake: A Gluten-Free Delight for Chocolate Lovers


If you’re a chocolate lover with a gluten intolerance or simply looking for a decadent dessert that’s free from flour, then this Flourless Chocolate Cake recipe is the answer to your cravings. Rich, moist, and intensely chocolatey, this indulgent cake is a showstopper for any occasion. Whether you’re hosting a dinner party, celebrating a special milestone, or simply treating yourself to a delightful dessert, this Flourless Chocolate Cake is sure to impress. Let’s dive into the recipe and unleash the chocolatey goodness!

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup (225g) unsalted butter, plus extra for greasing
  • 1 1/2 cups (300g) granulated sugar
  • 1 cup (240ml) strong hot coffee
  • 10 oz (280g) dark chocolate, chopped
  • 6 large eggs
  • 1 cup (100g) unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • Pinch of salt
  • Confectioners’ sugar, for dusting (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Preheat and Prepare:

    • Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C).
    • Grease a 9-inch (23cm) springform pan with butter and line the bottom with parchment paper.

  2. Melt the Chocolate:

    • In a heatproof bowl, combine the chopped dark chocolate and hot coffee.
    • Let it sit for a minute, then stir until the chocolate has melted and the mixture is smooth. Set aside to cool slightly.

  3. Whisk the Eggs:

    • In a large bowl, whisk the eggs, granulated sugar, and vanilla extract until the mixture is pale and thick.

  4. Add the Butter and Chocolate Mixture:

    • In a saucepan, melt the butter over low heat.
    • Once melted, gradually pour the butter into the egg mixture, whisking constantly.
    • Then, gradually pour in the melted chocolate and coffee mixture, whisking until well combined.

  5. Fold in the Cocoa Powder:

    • Sift the cocoa powder into the batter and gently fold until no streaks of cocoa remain.

  6. Bake the Cake:

    • Pour the batter into the prepared springform pan and smooth the top.
    • Bake in the preheated oven for 35-40 minutes, or until the cake has set and a toothpick inserted into the center comes out with moist crumbs attached.

  7. Cool and Serve:

    • Allow the cake to cool in the pan for 10 minutes, then run a knife around the edge to loosen it before releasing the sides of the pan.
    • Transfer the cake to a serving plate and let it cool completely.
    • Dust with confectioners’ sugar, if desired, before slicing and serving.

Serving Suggestions:

This Flourless Chocolate Cake is a divine treat on its own, but you can elevate the experience by serving it with a dollop of freshly whipped cream or a scoop of vanilla bean ice cream. For a touch of elegance, decorate the cake with a sprinkle of cocoa powder, a drizzle of chocolate ganache, or a scattering of fresh berries. The richness of this cake pairs beautifully with a cup of freshly brewed coffee or a glass of velvety red wine.

Conclusion:

Whether you’re following a gluten-free lifestyle or simply love the intense allure of chocolate, this Flourless Chocolate Cake is a must-try. The simplicity of the ingredients combined with the luxurious taste and texture makes it a winning dessert for any chocolate enthusiast. So, gather your ingredients, preheat your oven, and get ready to savor a slice of chocolate heaven with this Flourless Chocolate Cake recipe. Happy baking and indulging!
