Delicious Italian Stuffed Peppers Recipe - Mediterranean Flavors

3 min · 539 words

If you’re looking for a flavorful and satisfying dish that captures the essence of the Mediterranean, then this Italian Stuffed Peppers recipe is just what you need. Bursting with vibrant colors and robust flavors, these stuffed peppers are a delightful fusion of Italian and Mediterranean cuisine. Whether you’re planning a family dinner or hosting a gathering with friends, these stuffed peppers are sure to impress even the most discerning palates. Let’s dive into the recipe and discover how to create this culinary masterpiece.

Ingredients

For the Stuffed Peppers:

  • 6 large bell peppers (any color will do, but a mix of red, yellow, and green adds a colorful touch)
  • 1 cup cooked quinoa
  • 1 pound ground Italian sausage
  • 1 cup diced tomatoes
  • 1/2 cup chopped Kalamata olives
  • 1/2 cup crumbled feta cheese
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh basil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste

For the Mediterranean Sauce:

  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can (28 ounces) crushed tomatoes
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

Step 1: Preparing the Peppers

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Slice the tops off the bell peppers and remove the seeds and membranes from the inside.
  3. Place the peppers in a baking dish, cut side up, and set aside.

Step 2: Making the Filling

  1. In a large skillet over medium heat, cook the Italian sausage until browned, breaking it into crumbles as it cooks.
  2. Add the diced tomatoes, cooked quinoa, Kalamata olives, feta cheese, basil, garlic, and dried oregano to the skillet. Stir the mixture until well combined. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  3. Spoon the filling into the prepared bell peppers, mounding it slightly on top.

Step 3: Creating the Mediterranean Sauce

  1. In a separate saucepan, heat the olive oil over medium heat.
  2. Add the chopped onion and garlic, and sauté until softened and fragrant.
  3. Stir in the crushed tomatoes, fresh parsley, and dried oregano. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  4. Simmer the sauce for 10-15 minutes, allowing the flavors to meld together.

Step 4: Baking the Stuffed Peppers

  1. Pour the Mediterranean sauce over the stuffed peppers in the baking dish.
  2. Cover the dish with foil and bake for 45-50 minutes, or until the peppers are tender.
  3. Remove the foil during the last 10 minutes of baking to allow the tops of the peppers to lightly brown.

Serving

Once the stuffed peppers are done, remove them from the oven and let them cool for a few minutes. Garnish with additional fresh basil or parsley, if desired. Serve the stuffed peppers warm, accompanied by a crisp green salad or garlic bread for a complete Mediterranean-inspired meal.

Conclusion

This Italian Stuffed Peppers recipe brings together the vibrant flavors of the Mediterranean in a wholesome and satisfying dish. Whether you’re a fan of Italian cuisine or simply love exploring new flavors, these stuffed peppers are a must-try. The combination of tender bell peppers, savory Italian sausage, tangy feta cheese, and aromatic herbs creates a harmonious medley of tastes and textures that will leave your taste buds yearning for more. So, gather your ingredients, embrace the spirit of the Mediterranean, and delight in the creation of these delectable stuffed peppers. Buon appetito!
