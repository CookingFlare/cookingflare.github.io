Tasty Nut-Free Stuffed Chicken Parmesan


If you’re looking for a delicious and satisfying dinner that’s also nut-free, then this Tasty Nut-Free Stuffed Chicken Parmesan recipe is perfect for you. This dish combines the classic flavors of chicken parmesan with a savory and cheesy stuffing, making it a true crowd-pleaser. Whether you have a nut allergy or just prefer to avoid nuts in your cooking, this recipe will surely become a favorite in your household.

Ingredients

  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1 cup Italian seasoned breadcrumbs
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup marinara sauce
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon onion powder
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

Step 1: Prepare the Stuffing

  1. In a bowl, mix together the grated Parmesan cheese, shredded mozzarella cheese, garlic powder, onion powder, and dried oregano. Set aside.

Step 2: Butterfly the Chicken Breasts

  1. Lay the chicken breasts flat on a cutting board.
  2. Using a sharp knife, carefully slice each chicken breast horizontally, but not all the way through, creating a pocket for the stuffing.

Step 3: Stuff the Chicken Breasts

  1. Divide the cheese mixture evenly and stuff it into the pockets of each chicken breast.

Step 4: Bread the Chicken

  1. In a shallow dish, beat the eggs.
  2. In another shallow dish, mix together the Italian seasoned breadcrumbs, garlic powder, onion powder, and a pinch of salt and pepper.
  3. Dip each stuffed chicken breast into the beaten eggs, then coat them with the breadcrumb mixture, pressing gently to adhere the breadcrumbs.

Step 5: Cook the Chicken

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. In a large skillet, heat the olive oil over medium-high heat.
  3. Place the breaded chicken breasts in the skillet and cook for 3-4 minutes on each side, until golden brown.
  4. Transfer the browned chicken breasts to a baking dish and top each one with a spoonful of marinara sauce.

Step 6: Bake

  1. Sprinkle the remaining mozzarella cheese over the top of each chicken breast.
  2. Bake in the preheated oven for 25-30 minutes, or until the chicken is cooked through and the cheese is melted and bubbly.

Step 7: Serve and Enjoy

  1. Once the chicken is cooked, remove it from the oven and let it rest for a few minutes.
  2. Serve the Tasty Nut-Free Stuffed Chicken Parmesan with your favorite side dish, such as pasta or a crisp green salad.

Conclusion

This Tasty Nut-Free Stuffed Chicken Parmesan recipe is a wonderful way to enjoy the flavors of a classic Italian dish without any nuts. The combination of tender chicken, gooey cheese, and zesty marinara sauce makes for a truly satisfying meal. Whether you’re cooking for your family or entertaining guests, this recipe is sure to impress. Plus, it’s a great way to accommodate those with nut allergies without sacrificing flavor. Give it a try and savor the deliciousness of this nut-free twist on a beloved favorite!
