The Aviation: A Classic Gin Cocktail with a Touch of Violet


If you’re a fan of classic cocktails with a modern twist, then The Aviation is a must-try for your mixology repertoire. This elegant and refreshing drink has a captivating violet hue, thanks to the addition of crème de violette, and a delightful balance of botanical flavors that make it a timeless favorite. In this recipe, we’ll guide you through the steps to create this sophisticated libation and elevate your cocktail game.

Ingredients

  • 2 oz gin
  • ½ oz maraschino liqueur
  • ¼ oz crème de violette
  • ¾ oz fresh lemon juice
  • Ice cubes
  • Luxardo maraschino cherry, for garnish

Instructions

  1. Chill Your Glassware: Begin by chilling a coupe or martini glass in the freezer. This will ensure that your Aviation stays crisp and refreshing until the last sip.

  2. Prepare Your Ingredients: Gather all your ingredients and equipment. Fresh lemon juice is key to achieving the bright and tangy flavor profile of The Aviation, so make sure to use freshly squeezed juice for the best results.

  3. Shake, Shake, Shake: Fill a cocktail shaker with ice cubes. Add the gin, maraschino liqueur, crème de violette, and freshly squeezed lemon juice. Shake the mixture vigorously for about 15-20 seconds to properly chill and dilute the cocktail.

  4. Strain and Serve: Once you’ve achieved the perfect chill, strain the cocktail into your chilled glass. The beautiful violet hue of The Aviation will immediately capture your attention, inviting you to savor its unique visual appeal.

  5. Garnish with Elegance: For the finishing touch, garnish your Aviation with a Luxardo maraschino cherry. The deep red color of the cherry provides a striking contrast to the cocktail’s delicate violet shade and adds a hint of sweetness to the overall drinking experience.

  6. Enjoy: Now, it’s time to savor the fruit of your labor. Take a moment to appreciate the exquisite balance of floral, citrus, and herbal notes in this classic gin cocktail. Each sip is a journey through time, as The Aviation has been enchanting cocktail enthusiasts for decades with its timeless allure.

The History of The Aviation

The Aviation cocktail has a rich and storied history that dates back to the early 20th century. Its origins can be traced to Hugo Ensslin’s 1916 recipe book, “Recipes for Mixed Drinks,” where the classic combination of gin, maraschino liqueur, and crème de violette was first documented. Over the years, the recipe underwent variations and adaptations, with the crème de violette being omitted due to its scarcity.

In recent years, there has been a resurgence of interest in classic cocktails, leading to a revival of The Aviation. Bartenders and cocktail enthusiasts alike have embraced the return of crème de violette, allowing the cocktail to reclaim its original, enchanting hue and flavor profile. Today, The Aviation continues to hold its status as a timeless libation that pays homage to the golden age of cocktails.

Elevate Your Home Bar Experience

By mastering The Aviation, you not only add a sophisticated and visually stunning cocktail to your repertoire but also gain insight into the art of balancing flavors and creating a multisensory drinking experience. The interplay of floral, citrus, and herbal elements in The Aviation sets it apart as a cocktail that appeals to both the palate and the eyes.

So, the next time you find yourself yearning for a libation that transcends the ordinary, consider mixing up The Aviation. Its classic charm and contemporary appeal make it a fitting choice for both intimate gatherings and elegant soirées. Embrace the timeless allure of this classic gin cocktail with a touch of violet, and let its captivating flavors transport you to an era of sophistication and refinement.

In conclusion, The Aviation is not just a cocktail; it’s a testament to the enduring legacy of classic mixology. Its vibrant violet hue and harmonious blend of flavors make it a standout choice for those with a discerning palate and an appreciation for the art of cocktail craftsmanship. Whether you’re a seasoned mixologist or a curious newcomer to the world of cocktails, The Aviation invites you to embark on a journey through time and taste, leaving an indelible impression with each sip. Cheers to the timeless elegance of The Aviation!
