Roll with It: Fabulous Dumplings, PIZZAS, & More Asian & Italian Sushi for Kids!


Introducing the most mouth-watering, family-friendly fusion recipes that your kids will absolutely adore! Who says you can’t mix two thrilling culinary worlds? In this blog post, we’re about to show you how to roll with it and create some magical creations such as dumplings, pizzas, and even some Asian & Italian sushi that your little ones will be begging for more.

Dumplings with an Italian Twist: Mac & Cheese Ravioli

Kids adore mac & cheese, and this spin elevates the beloved childhood favorite to a whole new level of fun and flavor.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup elbow macaroni
  • 2 cups shredded sharp cheddar cheese
  • 1 cup whole milk
  • 3 tablespoons unsalted butter
  • 1/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/8 teaspoon ground mustard
  • 1/4 teaspoon garlic powder
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • 1 package of wonton wrappers
  • Water
  • Olive oil

Instructions:

  1. Cook macaroni according to package instructions and set aside.
  2. In a separate saucepan, melt the butter and add the flour to create a roux, stirring constantly.
  3. Slowly whisk in the milk until the sauce begins to thicken.
  4. Add in the ground mustard, garlic powder, salt, and pepper, then fold in the shredded cheddar cheese until melted and creamy.
  5. Combine the cheese sauce with the cooked macaroni, and allow it to cool for a few minutes.
  6. Lay out the wonton wrappers on a clean surface, and spoon a small amount of mac & cheese mixture in the center of each.
  7. Wet the edges of the wonton wrapper with water, fold in half, making sure to seal the edges, and crimp with a fork. Repeat the process until all wrappers are used.
  8. In a deep pan, bring water to a boil; reduce heat to a simmer and add olive oil to prevent sticking.
  9. Place the ravioli in the simmering water, a few at a time, allowing them to cook for about 3-5 minutes or until they float to the surface.
  10. Serve these delightful treats warm, garnished with additional cheese or diced vegetables.

Pizzadilla: The Quesadilla-Pizza Hybrid Your Kids Will Love

This fusion creation will dazzle their taste buds while giving their creativity a boost to pick their favorite toppings.

Ingredients:

  • 8 large flour tortillas
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 2 cups shredded rotisserie chicken, lean turkey, or tofu for a vegetarian option
  • 1 cup pizza sauce
  • Desired pizza toppings such as pepperoni slices, sliced bell peppers, crumbled cooked bacon, or black olives

Instructions:

  1. Preheat a large non-stick skillet or griddle over medium heat.
  2. Place two tortillas on a flat surface, and spread pizza sauce evenly over each, leaving a small border around the edges.
  3. Sprinkle a generous layer of mozzarella cheese, followed by the chosen protein, and your desired pizza toppings.
  4. Top with the remaining 2 tortillas, sauce-side down.
  5. Carefully transfer the tortilla stack to the preheated skillet or griddle, cooking each side for approximately 4-5 minutes or until the cheese has melted and the tortillas have turned golden brown and slightly crispy.
  6. Using a large spatula, transfer the Pizzadilla to a cutting board, and let it cool for a minute before slicing it into quarters or smaller triangular slices.
  7. Serve with marinara or ranch dipping sauce for the ultimate twist on a classic.

Asian & Italian Sushi Magic: Fusion Sushi Rolls

Embrace the creativity with these sushi rolls that could be an exciting fusion of Asian and Italian cuisine to satisfy your kid’s sushi cravings.

Ingredients:

  • 4 cups cooked sushi rice
  • 1 cup sushi vinegar
  • 1/4 cup of your kid’s favorite pasta sauce
  • 8 large rice paper sheets
  • Assorted lean protein, vegetables, fruits, and cheese
  • Sesame oil for cooking

Instructions:

  1. Combine sushi rice with sushi vinegar and set aside to cool, spreading the rice onto a baking tray lined with parchment paper to prevent it from clumping.
  2. Cook your choice of proteins, vegetables, and fruits, ensuring they’re slightly undercooked so they’re easier to slice thinly.
  3. In a shallow dish, pour a layer of pasta sauce.
  4. Rehydrate rice paper sheets in a bowl of warm water, following the manufacturer’s recommendation.
  5. Place a rehydrated rice paper sheet on a flat surface, spoon a thin layer of sushi rice in the center, and roll up your chosen protein, vegetables, fruits, and cheese, along with a drizzle of pasta sauce and sesame oil.
  6. Continue rolling tightly, ensuring a neat and even surface; no rice or filling should be spilling out.
  7. Seal the roll with a bit of pasta sauce, ensuring that you’ve tuck the roll under itself and press it gently to hold it together.
  8. With a sharp, wet knife, slice your rainbow fusion sushi rolls into bite-sized pieces.
  9. Arrange your creations on a serving platter and let your kids enjoy the marvels of your culinary magic.

Lastly, while cooking with kids can be messy, the memories created are priceless. Encourage them to get involved, and it might just become their favorite family time activity. Create delicious fusion dishes and enjoy the smiles and laughter of their excited faces while indulging in their well-deserved culinary masterpieces!
