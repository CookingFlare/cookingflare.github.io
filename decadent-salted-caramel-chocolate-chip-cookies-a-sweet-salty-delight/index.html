Decadent Salted Caramel Chocolate Chip Cookies: A Sweet & Salty Delight

3 min · 496 words

If you’re a fan of the classic chocolate chip cookie but also crave an extra indulgent twist, then these Decadent Salted Caramel Chocolate Chip Cookies are for you. Packed with gooey caramel, rich chocolate chips, and a sprinkle of sea salt, these cookies are the perfect balance of sweet and salty flavors. Get ready to satisfy your sweet tooth and impress your friends and family with this irresistible treat!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup (2 sticks) unsalted butter, softened
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 cup brown sugar, packed
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 2 cups semisweet chocolate chips
  • 1 cup caramel bits or chopped soft caramels
  • Sea salt, for sprinkling

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C) and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.

  2. In a large mixing bowl, cream together the softened butter, granulated sugar, and brown sugar until light and fluffy. This may take a few minutes, so be patient!

  3. Add the eggs, one at a time, mixing well after each addition. Stir in the vanilla extract.

  4. In a separate bowl, whisk together the flour, baking soda, and salt. Gradually add the dry ingredients to the wet ingredients, mixing until just combined. Be careful not to overmix, as this can result in tough cookies.

  5. Fold in the chocolate chips and caramel bits until evenly distributed throughout the dough.

  6. Using a cookie scoop or tablespoon, drop rounded dough balls onto the prepared baking sheet, spacing them about 2 inches apart. If desired, gently press a few additional chocolate chips and caramel bits onto the tops of the dough balls for an extra visual appeal.

  7. Sprinkle a pinch of sea salt over each dough ball, ensuring that the salt is evenly distributed.

  8. Bake for 10-12 minutes or until the edges are golden brown. The centers may still appear slightly undercooked, but they will firm up as the cookies cool.

  9. Remove the baking sheet from the oven and allow the cookies to cool on the sheet for 5 minutes before transferring them to a wire rack to cool completely. This allows the cookies to set and become chewy.

  10. Once cooled, store the cookies in an airtight container at room temperature for up to 5 days. Though, with their irresistible taste, they probably won’t last that long!

Tips and Variations

  • For an extra gooey caramel center, try freezing small pieces of caramel and adding them to the cookie dough before baking.
  • If you prefer a softer cookie, reduce the baking time by a minute or two.
  • Experiment with different types of chocolate chips, such as dark chocolate or white chocolate, to customize the flavor of your cookies.
  • Serve these cookies warm with a scoop of vanilla ice cream for a decadent dessert.

So there you have it, a recipe for Decadent Salted Caramel Chocolate Chip Cookies that will surely satisfy your sweet and salty cravings. Whether you’re baking for a special occasion or simply treating yourself, these cookies are a delightful indulgence. Enjoy!
