Delicious and Easy BBQ Chicken Recipes


When it comes to meals that bring people together, there’s nothing quite like the aroma of BBQ chicken sizzling on the grill. Whether you’re hosting a summer cookout, planning a family dinner, or simply craving smoky, tender chicken infused with bold flavor, BBQ chicken is an all-time classic that never fails to impress.

If you’re looking for easy and creative BBQ chicken recipes to add to your repertoire, you’ve come to the right place. In this guide, we’ll walk you through a variety of methods, marinades, rubs, and cooking techniques that will elevate your BBQ chicken game. The best part? These recipes are simple enough for beginners but delicious enough to impress even the most seasoned chefs. Let’s dive in!

1. Classic BBQ Chicken with Homemade Sauce

There’s something special about the classic BBQ chicken, slathered in a sweet, tangy, and smoky homemade sauce. Here’s how to make the ultimate classic version:

Ingredients:

  • 4 bone-in, skin-on chicken thighs
  • 4 bone-in chicken drumsticks
  • 1 cup ketchup
  • 1/4 cup apple cider vinegar
  • 1/4 cup brown sugar
  • 2 tablespoons Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 teaspoon smoked paprika
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon onion powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Cooking oil for grilling

Instructions:

  1. Prep the chicken: Pat the chicken pieces dry with paper towels. Season both sides with salt, pepper, and a light drizzle of oil to keep them moist while grilling.
  2. Make the BBQ sauce: In a small saucepan, combine ketchup, apple cider vinegar, brown sugar, Worcestershire sauce, smoked paprika, garlic powder, and onion powder. Simmer over low heat for 10-15 minutes, stirring occasionally, until the sauce thickens.
  3. Grill the chicken: Preheat your grill to medium heat. Place the chicken skin-side down and cook for about 5-7 minutes per side, or until grill marks appear.
  4. Baste with BBQ sauce: Brush the chicken generously with the homemade BBQ sauce and move it to indirect heat. Continue grilling for another 15-20 minutes, flipping occasionally and basting with more sauce.
  5. Serve and enjoy: Let the chicken rest for 5 minutes before serving. Garnish with fresh parsley, and serve with potato salad or coleslaw for the perfect summer meal.

2. Honey Lime BBQ Chicken

This recipe combines the perfect balance of sweetness and acidity, creating an irresistible caramelized glaze on the chicken. The honey and lime work together beautifully, delivering a refreshing twist on traditional BBQ flavors.

Ingredients:

  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • Juice and zest of 2 limes
  • 3 tablespoons honey
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon chili powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon cumin
  • Fresh cilantro for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Marinate the chicken: In a resealable plastic bag, combine lime juice, lime zest, honey, soy sauce, olive oil, chili powder, and cumin. Add the chicken breasts, seal the bag, and refrigerate for at least 1 hour (overnight for best results).
  2. Preheat the grill: Heat the grill to medium-high. Lightly grease the grates to prevent sticking.
  3. Grill the chicken: Remove the chicken from the marinade and grill for about 5-6 minutes per side, or until the internal temperature reaches 165°F. Discard the remaining marinade.
  4. Garnish and serve: Sprinkle chopped fresh cilantro over the grilled chicken and serve with a side of Mexican-style corn or fresh green salad.

3. Spicy Chipotle BBQ Chicken Wings

For those who love a bit of heat, these chipotle BBQ chicken wings pack a punch. They’re perfect for game days or casual gatherings with friends.

Ingredients:

  • 2 pounds chicken wings, separated into flats and drumettes
  • 1/3 cup chipotle peppers in adobo sauce
  • 1/3 cup honey
  • 2 tablespoons apple cider vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon smoked paprika
  • Ranch or blue cheese dressing for dipping

Instructions:

  1. Make the chipotle BBQ sauce: In a blender, combine chipotle peppers in adobo, honey, apple cider vinegar, soy sauce, garlic powder, and smoked paprika. Blend until smooth.
  2. Bake the wings: Preheat the oven to 400°F. Place the wings on a wire rack over a baking sheet and bake for 30-35 minutes, flipping halfway through.
  3. Toss in sauce: Once baked and crispy, toss the wings in the chipotle BBQ sauce until fully coated.
  4. Finish on the grill (optional): For extra charred flavor, grill the coated wings for 2-3 minutes per side.
  5. Serve: Plate the wings with celery sticks and your favorite dipping sauce.

4. Slow-Cooker BBQ Pulled Chicken

Don’t have a grill? No problem! This slow-cooker BBQ pulled chicken is packed with flavor and perfect for sandwiches, tacos, or rice bowls.

Ingredients:

  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken thighs
  • 1 cup BBQ sauce (store-bought or homemade)
  • 1/4 cup chicken broth
  • 1 tablespoon Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon smoked paprika
  • Burger buns or tortillas for serving

Instructions:

  1. Assemble the slow cooker: Place chicken thighs in the slow cooker. Add BBQ sauce, chicken broth, Worcestershire sauce, garlic powder, and smoked paprika. Stir to coat the chicken evenly.
  2. Cook low and slow: Cover and cook on low for 6-7 hours or high for 3-4 hours, until the chicken is tender and easily shreds with a fork.
  3. Shred the chicken: Remove the chicken from the slow cooker and shred it using two forks. Return the shredded chicken to the sauce and mix well.
  4. Serve: Pile the pulled chicken onto burger buns, top with coleslaw, and enjoy!

5. Pineapple Teriyaki Grilled Chicken

Add a tropical flair to your BBQ with a pineapple teriyaki glaze that’s sweet, savory, and oh-so-addictive.

Ingredients:

  • 4 chicken thighs, bone-in and skin-on
  • 1/2 cup pineapple juice
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons brown sugar
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 teaspoon grated ginger
  • Pineapple slices for grilling

Instructions:

  1. Marinate the chicken: In a bowl, whisk together pineapple juice, soy sauce, brown sugar, garlic, and ginger. Place the chicken in the marinade, cover, and refrigerate for at least 2 hours.
  2. Preheat the grill: Heat the grill to medium-high and oil the grates.
  3. Grill the chicken and pineapple: Remove the chicken from the marinade and grill for 6-7 minutes per side, until fully cooked. Grill pineapple slices for 2-3 minutes per side until caramelized.
  4. Serve: Top the grilled chicken with pineapple slices and serve with rice or a light cucumber salad.

Bonus Tips for Perfect BBQ Chicken

No matter which recipe you choose, keep these tips in mind for flawless BBQ chicken every time:

  • Use a meat thermometer: This ensures your chicken is perfectly cooked without being over- or under-done. For chicken breasts, aim for 165°F; for thighs, 175°F.
  • Preheat your grill: A hot grill prevents the chicken from sticking and helps develop those lovely grill marks.
  • Keep it moist: Baste the chicken with sauce or brush it with oil while grilling to prevent it from drying out.
  • Rest your chicken: Let your chicken rest for a few minutes after grilling to allow the juices to redistribute.

With these BBQ chicken recipes and tips, you’re well on your way to mastering the art of backyard barbecues. From classic smoky flavors to bold and unique twists, there’s something here for every taste. So fire up the grill, grab your favorite ingredients, and get ready to wow your friends and family with delicious, easy BBQ chicken dishes. Happy grilling!
