Vietnamese Pho Ga Recipe with Chicken and Rice Noodle Soup


If you’re a fan of Vietnamese cuisine, you’ve likely heard of Pho, the aromatic and flavorful noodle soup that has gained international popularity. While Pho Bo (beef pho) is the more well-known version, Pho Ga (chicken pho) is equally delicious and offers a lighter alternative. Today, we’re going to explore the art of making a comforting and authentic Vietnamese Pho Ga with chicken and rice noodle soup.

Ingredients:

For the broth:

  • 1 whole chicken (about 3-4 pounds)
  • 1 large onion, halved
  • 4-inch piece of ginger, sliced
  • 2 cinnamon sticks
  • 3 star anise
  • 4 cloves
  • 1 cardamom pod
  • 1 teaspoon coriander seeds
  • 1 tablespoon salt
  • 2 tablespoons fish sauce
  • 1 tablespoon sugar

For the soup:

  • 1 pound dried rice noodles
  • 2 cups shredded cooked chicken
  • 4 green onions, thinly sliced
  • 1 cup bean sprouts
  • Fresh cilantro, basil, and lime wedges for serving
  • Hoisin sauce and Sriracha for garnish

Instructions:

Step 1: Prepare the Broth

  1. Rinse the chicken and place it in a large pot. Add enough water to cover the chicken.
  2. Bring the water to a boil, then reduce the heat to a gentle simmer. Skim off any foam that rises to the surface.
  3. Add the halved onion, ginger, cinnamon sticks, star anise, cloves, cardamom pod, and coriander seeds to the pot.
  4. Simmer the broth for 1.5 to 2 hours, occasionally skimming off any impurities that rise to the surface.
  5. Once the chicken is cooked through and the broth is fragrant, remove the chicken from the pot and set it aside to cool.
  6. Strain the broth through a fine-mesh sieve to remove the solids. Return the broth to the pot and season with salt, fish sauce, and sugar. Keep the broth warm on the stove.

Step 2: Prepare the Noodles and Garnishes

  1. Cook the rice noodles according to the package instructions. Drain and rinse under cold water.
  2. Shred the cooked chicken into bite-sized pieces.
  3. Prepare the green onions, bean sprouts, cilantro, basil, and lime wedges for serving.

Step 3: Assemble the Pho Ga

  1. Divide the cooked rice noodles among serving bowls.
  2. Top the noodles with shredded chicken and sliced green onions.
  3. Ladle the hot broth over the noodles and chicken.
  4. Serve the Pho Ga with bean sprouts, cilantro, basil, lime wedges, hoisin sauce, and Sriracha on the side for garnishing.
