Vegan Cherry Almond Granola Bars: A Healthy Snack


Granola bars are a perfect on-the-go snack that can keep you energized throughout the day. But with the increasing popularity of veganism, finding a vegan-friendly granola bar can be a challenge. That’s why we’ve come up with a delicious recipe for Vegan Cherry Almond Granola Bars. Packed with wholesome ingredients and bursting with flavor, these bars are not only vegan but also gluten-free and refined sugar-free. So without further ado, let’s dive into the recipe!

Ingredients

  • 2 cups rolled oats
  • 1 cup almonds, roughly chopped
  • 1 cup dried cherries
  • 1/2 cup almond butter
  • 1/2 cup maple syrup
  • 1/4 cup coconut oil, melted
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/2 teaspoon cinnamon
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C). Line a baking dish with parchment paper, leaving some overhang for easy removal later.

  2. In a large mixing bowl, combine the rolled oats, chopped almonds, and dried cherries. Mix well to evenly distribute the ingredients.

  3. In a separate bowl, whisk together the almond butter, maple syrup, melted coconut oil, vanilla extract, cinnamon, and salt until smooth and well combined.

  4. Pour the wet mixture over the dry ingredients and stir until everything is fully coated and well incorporated.

  5. Transfer the mixture to the lined baking dish and press it down firmly with the back of a spoon or your hands to create an even layer.

  6. Bake in the preheated oven for 20-25 minutes or until the edges turn golden brown.

  7. Once baked, remove the dish from the oven and let it cool completely. This will allow the bars to firm up and hold their shape.

  8. Once cooled, lift the parchment paper to remove the entire slab of granola from the dish. Place it on a cutting board and use a sharp knife to cut it into individual bars of your desired size.

  9. Store the bars in an airtight container at room temperature for up to a week, or refrigerate them for a longer shelf life.

Why Choose Vegan Cherry Almond Granola Bars?

These vegan cherry almond granola bars are not only delicious but also offer several health benefits. Here’s why they make a great choice for a healthy snack:

  1. Wholesome ingredients: The combination of rolled oats, almonds, and dried cherries provides a good balance of carbohydrates, healthy fats, and fiber. These ingredients help keep you full and satisfied.

  2. Gluten-free: For those with gluten sensitivities or celiac disease, these bars are a safe and delicious option. They are made with gluten-free oats and do not contain any gluten-containing ingredients.

  3. Refined sugar-free: Traditional granola bars often contain refined sugars, which can cause energy crashes and are generally unhealthy. Our vegan cherry almond granola bars are sweetened with natural ingredients like maple syrup, which provides a more sustained source of energy.

  4. Portable and convenient: These bars are perfect for those busy days when you need a quick and nutritious snack. They can be easily packed in your bag or lunchbox, making them a convenient choice on the go.

So there you have it—a simple yet tasty recipe for Vegan Cherry Almond Granola Bars. Give them a try and enjoy a delicious and healthy snack that will keep you fueled throughout the day. Happy snacking!
