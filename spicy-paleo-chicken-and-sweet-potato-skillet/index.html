Spicy Paleo Chicken and Sweet Potato Skillet


Are you in search of a delicious and healthy meal that’s easy to prepare? Look no further! This Spicy Paleo Chicken and Sweet Potato Skillet recipe is the perfect combination of spicy, savory, and satisfying. Packed with protein, fiber, and flavor, this dish will surely become a favorite in your weekly meal rotation.

Ingredients

  • 2 large chicken breasts, diced
  • 2 medium sweet potatoes, peeled and diced
  • 1 red bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 yellow bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • 1 teaspoon chili powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon red pepper flakes
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • Fresh cilantro for garnish

Instructions

  1. Heat 1 tablespoon of olive oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat. Add the diced chicken breasts and cook until browned and cooked through. Remove the cooked chicken from the skillet and set aside.

  2. In the same skillet, add the remaining 1 tablespoon of olive oil. Add the diced sweet potatoes and cook for about 5 minutes, stirring occasionally.

  3. Add the chopped onion and minced garlic to the skillet with the sweet potatoes. Cook for an additional 2-3 minutes until the onions are translucent and the garlic is fragrant.

  4. Incorporate the sliced red and yellow bell peppers into the skillet, and continue to cook for another 3-4 minutes until the peppers are slightly softened.

  5. Season the vegetable mixture with paprika, cumin, chili powder, red pepper flakes, salt, and pepper. Stir well to ensure the spices are evenly distributed.

  6. Return the cooked chicken to the skillet, and stir to combine all the ingredients. Allow the dish to cook for an additional 5-7 minutes, or until the sweet potatoes are tender and the flavors have melded together.

  7. Once the dish is cooked to perfection, remove the skillet from the heat and garnish with fresh cilantro.

  8. Serve the spicy Paleo chicken and sweet potato skillet hot, and enjoy the delicious blend of flavors and textures!

Why Choose This Recipe?

This Spicy Paleo Chicken and Sweet Potato Skillet recipe is not only a flavorful and satisfying meal, but it also aligns with a Paleo lifestyle. The combination of lean protein from the chicken, complex carbohydrates from the sweet potatoes, and the array of colorful vegetables makes this dish a well-rounded and nutritious option.

In addition to being a wholesome meal, this recipe is also incredibly versatile. You can customize the level of spiciness to suit your taste preferences by adjusting the amount of chili powder and red pepper flakes. Furthermore, you can easily add or substitute other vegetables such as broccoli, zucchini, or spinach to accommodate your personal favorites or what’s in season.

Moreover, this recipe is perfect for meal prepping. You can prepare a large batch of the Spicy Paleo Chicken and Sweet Potato Skillet ahead of time and portion it out for convenient and healthy lunches or dinners throughout the week. It reheats beautifully and can be enjoyed hot or cold, making it an ideal option for those with busy schedules.

Final Thoughts

In conclusion, this Spicy Paleo Chicken and Sweet Potato Skillet recipe is a wonderful addition to any cooking repertoire. It’s a one-pan dish that’s bursting with flavor, nutrients, and appeal. Whether you’re following a Paleo diet, looking to incorporate more wholesome ingredients into your meals, or simply seeking a satisfying and tasty dish, this recipe is sure to please your palate and nourish your body. Give it a try and savor the delightful combination of spicy chicken, sweet potatoes, and vibrant vegetables in every bite!
