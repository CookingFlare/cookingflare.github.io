Decadent Double Chocolate Ice Cream Cake Recipe


If you’re a fan of chocolate, ice cream, and cake, then this Decadent Double Chocolate Ice Cream Cake is set to be your new favorite indulgence. This recipe combines the richness of chocolate cake with the creaminess of ice cream, resulting in a dessert that’s guaranteed to impress your guests and satisfy your sweet tooth. Whether you’re celebrating a special occasion or simply craving a luxurious treat, this ice cream cake is the perfect choice.

Ingredients:

For the Chocolate Cake Layer:

  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 1/2 cups granulated sugar
  • 3/4 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons baking soda
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 2 large eggs
  • 3/4 cup whole milk
  • 1/2 cup vegetable oil
  • 2 teaspoons pure vanilla extract
  • 3/4 cup boiling water

For the Ice Cream Layer:

  • 1.5 quarts of chocolate ice cream
  • 1/2 cup chocolate fudge sauce

For the Chocolate Ganache:

  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 8 ounces semi-sweet chocolate, chopped
  • 1 teaspoon pure vanilla extract

Instructions:

Chocolate Cake Layer:

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease and flour two 9-inch round cake pans.
  2. In a large bowl, combine the flour, sugar, cocoa, baking powder, baking soda, and salt. Add eggs, milk, oil, and vanilla, and beat on medium speed for 2 minutes.
  3. Stir in the boiling water. The batter will be thin. Pour evenly into the prepared pans.
  4. Bake for 30 to 35 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean. Allow the cakes to cool in the pans for 10 minutes, then transfer to a wire rack to cool completely.

Assembly:

  1. Once the cakes are completely cooled, place one cake layer into the bottom of a 9-inch springform pan.
  2. Spread the chocolate ice cream over the cake layer, smoothing it out to create an even layer.
  3. Drizzle the chocolate fudge sauce over the ice cream layer.
  4. Place the second cake layer on top of the ice cream and fudge sauce, pressing down gently.
  5. Cover the springform pan with plastic wrap and freeze for at least 4 hours or overnight.

Chocolate Ganache:

  1. In a small saucepan, heat the heavy cream over medium heat until it just begins to simmer.
  2. Place the chopped chocolate in a heatproof bowl and pour the hot cream over the chocolate.
  3. Let it sit for 2-3 minutes, then stir until the chocolate has fully melted and the mixture is smooth.
  4. Stir in the vanilla extract. Let the ganache cool for about 10 minutes.

Final Assembly:

  1. Remove the cake from the freezer and release it from the springform pan.
  2. Pour the chocolate ganache over the top of the cake, allowing it to drip down the sides.
  3. Return the cake to the freezer for 15-20 minutes to allow the ganache to set.
  4. Once the ganache is set, the ice cream cake is ready to be served.

Serving:

Slice the Decadent Double Chocolate Ice Cream Cake into wedges and serve with a dollop of whipped cream or a scoop of vanilla ice cream for an extra indulgent experience.

This Decadent Double Chocolate Ice Cream Cake is a show-stopping dessert that will leave everyone wanting more. With its layers of moist chocolate cake, creamy chocolate ice cream, and rich chocolate ganache, it’s the ultimate treat for chocolate lovers. Whether you’re hosting a dinner party or simply treating yourself, this recipe is sure to impress and delight.

So, go ahead and indulge in the irresistible decadence of this Double Chocolate Ice Cream Cake. Your taste buds will thank you!
