Savory Chicken Shawarma Wrap: A Taste of the Middle East


In the heart of the Middle East, Shawarma is a beloved street food that has captured the taste buds of people from all over the world. This flavorful dish is made with juicy chicken, crispy pita bread, and a medley of aromatic spices. In this recipe, we will take you on a culinary journey to the Middle East, where you can experience the authentic taste of Shawarma in the comfort of your own home.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound boneless, skinless chicken breast or thighs
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon smoked paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 4 pita breads
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh cilantro
  • 2 tablespoons tahini
  • 2 tablespoons yogurt
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • Vegetable oil for brushing

Instructions

Marinating the Chicken

In a large bowl, whisk together olive oil, garlic, lemon juice, cumin, smoked paprika, salt, and black pepper. Add the chicken and mix until the meat is well coated with the marinade. Cover the bowl with plastic wrap and refrigerate for at least 2 hours or overnight.

Grilling the Chicken

Preheat a grill or grill pan to medium-high heat. Remove the chicken from the marinade, letting any excess liquid drip off. Grill the chicken for 5-7 minutes per side, or until it is cooked through. Transfer the chicken to a plate and let it rest for 5 minutes.

Preparing the Tahini Sauce

In a small bowl, whisk together tahini, yogurt, lemon juice, and salt until smooth. Taste and adjust the seasoning as needed.

Assembling the Shawarma Wrap

Slice the grilled chicken into thin strips. Warm the pita breads by wrapping them in foil and heating them in the oven for 5 minutes. Brush the pita breads with vegetable oil and spread a layer of tahini sauce on each bread. Add a few slices of the grilled chicken, some chopped parsley and cilantro, and a sprinkle of salt. Serve immediately and enjoy!

Tips and Variations

  • For a more authentic Shawarma experience, use a vertical spit or a rotisserie to cook the chicken. This will give the meat a crispy exterior and a juicy interior.
  • Add some crispy falafel or crispy fried onions to the wrap for extra texture and flavor.
  • Replace the tahini sauce with a garlic mayo or a tzatziki sauce for a different flavor profile.
  • Experiment with different spices and seasonings to give the chicken a unique flavor. For example, try adding some cayenne pepper or some ground coriander to the marinade.

Conclusion

Savory Chicken Shawarma Wrap is a delicious and flavorful dish that is sure to become a favorite in your household. With its crispy pita bread, juicy chicken, and aromatic spices, this recipe is a perfect representation of the Middle Eastern cuisine. Whether you are a foodie or a busy professional, this recipe is easy to make and will satisfy your cravings. So, gather your ingredients and start cooking!
