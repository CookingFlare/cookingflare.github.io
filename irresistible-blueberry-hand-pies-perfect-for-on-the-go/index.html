Irresistible Blueberry Hand Pies: Perfect for On-the-Go


Who doesn’t love the convenience of a handheld treat that’s bursting with juicy, sweet blueberries? These irresistible blueberry hand pies are the perfect on-the-go snack or dessert, and they’re easier to make than you might think. Whether you’re planning a picnic, packing a lunch, or simply craving a delicious pastry, these hand pies are a delightful option that everyone will love.

Ingredients

  • For the Dough:

    • 2 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
    • 1 tablespoon granulated sugar
    • 1 teaspoon salt
    • 1 cup unsalted butter, cold and cut into cubes
    • 1/2 cup ice water

  • For the Blueberry Filling:

    • 2 cups fresh blueberries
    • 1/3 cup granulated sugar
    • 1 tablespoon cornstarch
    • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
    • 1/2 teaspoon lemon zest
    • 1/4 teaspoon cinnamon
    • 1/8 teaspoon salt

  • Egg Wash:

    • 1 large egg
    • 1 tablespoon water

  • Optional:

    • Coarse sugar for sprinkling

Instructions

Step 1: Prepare the Dough

  1. In a large bowl, whisk together the flour, sugar, and salt.
  2. Add the cold cubed butter to the flour mixture and use a pastry cutter or two forks to cut the butter into the flour until the mixture resembles coarse crumbs.
  3. Drizzle the ice water over the mixture and stir with a fork until the dough begins to come together.
  4. Turn the dough out onto a clean surface and gently knead it together. Divide the dough in half, shape each half into a disk, wrap them in plastic wrap, and refrigerate for at least 1 hour.

Step 2: Make the Blueberry Filling

  1. In a medium saucepan, combine the blueberries, sugar, cornstarch, lemon juice, lemon zest, cinnamon, and salt.
  2. Cook the mixture over medium heat, stirring occasionally, until the blueberries burst and the mixture thickens. Remove from heat and let it cool to room temperature.

Step 3: Assemble the Hand Pies

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C) and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
  2. On a lightly floured surface, roll out one disk of dough to about 1/8 inch thickness. Use a 5-inch round cutter to cut out circles of dough.
  3. Place about 1-2 tablespoons of the blueberry filling in the center of each dough circle.
  4. In a small bowl, whisk together the egg and water to make the egg wash. Brush the edges of the dough circles with the egg wash.
  5. Fold the dough over the filling to create half-moon shapes and crimp the edges with a fork to seal.
  6. Place the hand pies on the prepared baking sheet and brush the tops with the remaining egg wash. Sprinkle with coarse sugar if desired.

Step 4: Bake and Enjoy

  1. Bake the hand pies for 20-25 minutes, or until they are golden brown.
  2. Let the hand pies cool on the baking sheet for a few minutes before transferring them to a wire rack to cool completely.
  3. These hand pies are best enjoyed the day they are made, but they can be stored in an airtight container at room temperature for up to 2 days.

Conclusion

With their flaky crust and bursting blueberry filling, these hand pies are a delightful treat that’s perfect for any occasion. Whether you’re headed out for a day of adventure or simply want to indulge in a portable dessert, these irresistible blueberry hand pies are sure to satisfy. So, gather your ingredients, roll up your sleeves, and get ready to bake up a batch of these delicious hand pies that are as convenient as they are delicious!
