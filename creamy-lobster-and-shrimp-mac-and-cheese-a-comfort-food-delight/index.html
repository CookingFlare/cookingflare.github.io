Creamy Lobster and Shrimp Mac and Cheese: A Comfort Food Delight


Are you ready to elevate your mac and cheese game? Look no further than this indulgent and luxurious recipe for Creamy Lobster and Shrimp Mac and Cheese. This dish takes the classic comfort food to a whole new level with the addition of succulent lobster and tender shrimp, all enveloped in a rich and creamy cheese sauce. Whether you’re hosting a special dinner or simply craving a decadent meal, this recipe is sure to impress and satisfy.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound elbow macaroni
  • 1/2 pound cooked lobster meat, chopped
  • 1/2 pound cooked shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter
  • 1/2 cup all-purpose flour
  • 4 cups whole milk
  • 2 cups shredded Gruyère cheese
  • 2 cups shredded sharp cheddar cheese
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon onion powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 cup panko breadcrumbs
  • Fresh parsley for garnish

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C) and lightly grease a 9x13 inch baking dish.

  2. Cook the elbow macaroni according to the package instructions until al dente. Drain and set aside.

  3. In a large pot, melt the butter over medium heat. Once melted, whisk in the flour to create a roux. Cook the roux for a few minutes until it turns a light golden color.

  4. Gradually pour in the milk, whisking constantly to avoid lumps. Continue to cook the sauce until it thickens and coats the back of a spoon.

  5. Reduce the heat to low and stir in the shredded Gruyère and cheddar cheese until melted and smooth. Season the cheese sauce with garlic powder, onion powder, salt, and pepper.

  6. Add the cooked macaroni, chopped lobster, and shrimp to the cheese sauce, stirring until everything is well combined.

  7. Transfer the mac and cheese mixture to the prepared baking dish, spreading it out evenly.

  8. In a small bowl, combine the panko breadcrumbs with a drizzle of melted butter. Sprinkle the breadcrumb mixture over the top of the mac and cheese.

  9. Bake the mac and cheese in the preheated oven for 25-30 minutes, or until the top is golden and the cheese is bubbly.

  10. Once done, remove the mac and cheese from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes. Garnish with fresh parsley before serving.

Serving Suggestions

This Creamy Lobster and Shrimp Mac and Cheese is a show-stopping dish on its own, but you can take it to the next level by serving it alongside a crisp green salad dressed with a light vinaigrette. For a heartier meal, pair it with a side of roasted vegetables or garlic bread. This dish also makes a delightful addition to any special occasion or holiday spread.

Conclusion

Creamy, decadent, and utterly irresistible, this Creamy Lobster and Shrimp Mac and Cheese is a comfort food delight that will leave your taste buds singing. Whether you’re a seafood lover or simply enjoy indulging in a luxurious meal, this recipe is a must-try. So, gather your ingredients, preheat your oven, and get ready to savor every creamy, cheesy bite of this elevated mac and cheese masterpiece.
