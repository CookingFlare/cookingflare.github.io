5 Vibrant Spring Pasta Salads Perfect for Picnics


Spring is the perfect time to enjoy the outdoors and indulge in some delicious and vibrant pasta salads. Whether you’re planning a picnic with friends or simply want to add some color to your lunch routine, these 5 pasta salad recipes will not disappoint. Packed with fresh ingredients and bursting with flavors, these salads are not only visually appealing but also incredibly satisfying. So, grab your picnic blanket and get ready to savor these delightful creations!

1. Caprese Pasta Salad

Ingredients:

  • 8 oz fusilli pasta
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1 cup fresh mozzarella balls, halved
  • ½ cup fresh basil leaves, torn
  • 2 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon balsamic vinegar
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Cook the fusilli pasta according to the package instructions until al dente. Drain and let it cool.
  2. In a large bowl, combine the cooled pasta, cherry tomatoes, mozzarella balls, and torn basil leaves.
  3. Drizzle with olive oil and balsamic vinegar. Season with salt and pepper.
  4. Toss gently until all the ingredients are well coated. Let it sit for a few minutes to allow the flavors to meld.
  5. Serve chilled and enjoy the fresh and tangy flavors of this classic Caprese Pasta Salad.

2. Greek Orzo Salad

Ingredients:

  • 8 oz orzo pasta
  • 1 cup cucumber, diced
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • ½ cup Kalamata olives, pitted and halved
  • ½ cup crumbled feta cheese
  • ¼ cup red onion, thinly sliced
  • 2 tablespoons lemon juice
  • 2 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon fresh dill, chopped
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Cook the orzo pasta according to the package instructions until al dente. Drain and let it cool.
  2. In a large bowl, combine the cooled orzo pasta, cucumber, cherry tomatoes, Kalamata olives, crumbled feta cheese, and red onion.
  3. In a separate small bowl, whisk together the lemon juice, olive oil, chopped dill, salt, and pepper.
  4. Pour the dressing over the pasta salad and toss gently to coat all the ingredients.
  5. Let it chill in the refrigerator for at least 30 minutes to allow the flavors to develop.
  6. Serve cold and enjoy the refreshing taste of this Greek-inspired Orzo Salad.

3. Lemon Asparagus Pasta Salad

Ingredients:

  • 8 oz bowtie pasta
  • 1 bunch asparagus, trimmed and cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 1 cup frozen peas, thawed
  • ½ cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • Zest of 1 lemon
  • ¼ cup lemon juice
  • 2 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon honey
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Cook the bowtie pasta according to the package instructions until al dente. Drain and let it cool.
  2. Bring a pot of salted water to a boil and blanch the asparagus for 2 minutes. Remove and transfer to an ice bath to stop the cooking process. Drain and pat dry.
  3. In a large bowl, combine the cooled bowtie pasta, blanched asparagus, thawed peas, grated Parmesan cheese, and lemon zest.
  4. In a separate small bowl, whisk together the lemon juice, olive oil, honey, salt, and pepper.
  5. Pour the dressing over the pasta salad and toss gently to coat all the ingredients.
  6. Allow the salad to chill in the refrigerator for at least an hour to let the flavors mingle.
  7. Serve chilled and enjoy the zesty and vibrant flavors of this Lemon Asparagus Pasta Salad.

4. Southwest Pasta Salad

Ingredients:

  • 8 oz rotini pasta
  • 1 cup corn kernels, cooked and cooled
  • 1 cup black beans, rinsed and drained
  • ½ cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • ½ cup red bell pepper, diced
  • ½ cup green bell pepper, diced
  • ¼ cup red onion, diced
  • 2 tablespoons lime juice
  • 2 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon fresh cilantro, chopped
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Cook the rotini pasta according to the package instructions until al dente. Drain and let it cool.
  2. In a large bowl, combine the cooled rotini pasta, corn kernels, black beans, cherry tomatoes, red bell pepper, green bell pepper, and red onion.
  3. In a separate small bowl, whisk together the lime juice, olive oil, chopped cilantro, ground cumin, salt, and pepper.
  4. Pour the dressing over the pasta salad and toss gently to coat all the ingredients.
  5. Let it sit for about 15 minutes to allow the flavors to meld together.
  6. Serve at room temperature and enjoy the bold and zesty flavors of this Southwest Pasta Salad.

5. Pesto Tortellini Salad

Ingredients:

  • 8 oz cheese tortellini
  • ½ cup sun-dried tomatoes, chopped
  • ½ cup roasted red peppers, chopped
  • ½ cup black olives, sliced
  • ¼ cup pine nuts, toasted
  • 2 tablespoons pesto sauce
  • 2 tablespoons lemon juice
  • 2 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Cook the cheese tortellini according to the package instructions until al dente. Drain and let it cool.
  2. In a large bowl, combine the cooled cheese tortellini, sun-dried tomatoes, roasted red peppers, black olives, and toasted pine nuts.
  3. In a separate small bowl, whisk together the pesto sauce, lemon juice, olive oil, salt, and pepper.
  4. Pour the dressing over the pasta salad and toss gently to coat all the ingredients.
  5. Let it chill in the refrigerator for at least 1 hour to allow the flavors to meld together.
  6. Serve cold and enjoy the delightful combination of flavors in this Pesto Tortellini Salad.

These 5 Vibrant Spring Pasta Salads are not only easy to make but also a feast for the senses. Bursting with fresh ingredients and vibrant colors, they are perfect for picnics, potlucks, or even as a refreshing side dish for any meal. So, get creative in the kitchen, mix and match ingredients, and let your taste buds dance with joy as you enjoy these delightful pasta salads. Happy spring picnicking!
