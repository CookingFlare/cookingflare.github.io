Homemade Salted Caramel Brownie Chunk Ice Cream: A Decadent Delight


There’s something truly magical about homemade ice cream. The creamy texture, the rich flavors, and the satisfaction of knowing that you made it yourself. And when it comes to indulgent ice cream flavors, it’s hard to beat the combination of salted caramel and brownie chunks. This Homemade Salted Caramel Brownie Chunk Ice Cream recipe is sure to delight your taste buds and leave you craving more.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups heavy cream
  • 1 cup whole milk
  • 3/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 4 large egg yolks
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/2 cup salted caramel sauce
  • 1 cup brownie chunks (homemade or store-bought)

Instructions

  1. In a saucepan, heat the heavy cream and milk over medium heat until it begins to steam. Be careful not to let it boil.
  2. In a separate bowl, whisk together the sugar and egg yolks until well combined.
  3. Slowly pour the hot cream mixture into the egg mixture, whisking constantly to prevent the eggs from scrambling.
  4. Pour the mixture back into the saucepan and cook over low heat, stirring constantly, until the mixture thickens and coats the back of a spoon. This should take about 5-7 minutes.
  5. Remove the saucepan from the heat and stir in the vanilla extract.
  6. Transfer the mixture to a bowl and cover it with plastic wrap, making sure the plastic wrap is touching the surface of the custard to prevent a skin from forming. Refrigerate for at least 4 hours, or overnight.
  7. Once the custard has chilled, pour it into an ice cream maker and churn according to the manufacturer’s instructions.
  8. While the ice cream is churning, prepare the salted caramel sauce if you haven’t already.
  9. Once the ice cream reaches a soft-serve consistency, gently fold in the salted caramel sauce and brownie chunks.
  10. Transfer the ice cream to a lidded container and freeze for at least 4 hours, or until firm.
  11. When ready to serve, remove the ice cream from the freezer and let it sit at room temperature for a few minutes to soften slightly. Scoop into bowls or cones, and enjoy!

Tips and Variations

  • For an extra indulgent treat, drizzle additional salted caramel sauce over the top of each scoop before serving.
  • Feel free to get creative with your add-ins! Try adding crushed pretzels, chopped nuts, or even a swirl of fudge sauce for extra flavor and texture.
  • If you don’t have an ice cream maker, you can still make this recipe. After step 6, transfer the custard to a lidded container and freeze for about 2 hours, or until partially frozen. Remove from the freezer and use a fork to break up any ice crystals that have formed. Return to the freezer and repeat this process every hour for about 4-6 hours, until the ice cream reaches the desired consistency.

Conclusion

Homemade Salted Caramel Brownie Chunk Ice Cream is the ultimate decadent treat. The creamy, luscious base combined with the sweet and salty caramel sauce and fudgy brownie chunks creates a flavor combination that is simply irresistible. Whether enjoyed on a hot summer day or as a comforting dessert on a cozy evening, this ice cream is sure to satisfy your sweet tooth and leave you wanting more. So why not give this recipe a try and indulge in a homemade delight that will have you coming back for seconds?
