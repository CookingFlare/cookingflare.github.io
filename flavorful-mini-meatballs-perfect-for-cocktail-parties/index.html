Flavorful Mini Meatballs: Perfect for Cocktail Parties


If you’re hosting a cocktail party and looking for a delicious and crowd-pleasing appetizer, look no further than these flavorful mini meatballs. These bite-sized treats are packed with savory flavors and are sure to be a hit among your guests. Plus, they’re easy to make and can be prepared in advance, making your party planning a breeze.

Ingredients

For the meatballs:

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1/2 pound ground pork
  • 1/4 cup breadcrumbs
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/4 cup finely chopped onion
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1/4 teaspoon red pepper flakes (optional)

For the sauce:

  • 1 cup ketchup
  • 1/4 cup brown sugar
  • 1/4 cup apple cider vinegar
  • 2 tablespoons Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 tablespoon Dijon mustard
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon onion powder

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C) and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.

  2. In a large bowl, combine the ground beef, ground pork, breadcrumbs, Parmesan cheese, chopped onion, minced garlic, parsley, oregano, salt, black pepper, and red pepper flakes (if using). Mix well until all the ingredients are evenly incorporated.

  3. Shape the meat mixture into small, bite-sized meatballs, about 1 inch in diameter. Place the meatballs on the prepared baking sheet, leaving a little space between each one.

  4. Bake the meatballs in the preheated oven for 15-20 minutes, or until they are cooked through and slightly browned.

  5. While the meatballs are baking, prepare the sauce. In a small saucepan, combine the ketchup, brown sugar, apple cider vinegar, Worcestershire sauce, Dijon mustard, garlic powder, and onion powder. Cook over medium heat, stirring occasionally, until the sauce thickens slightly, about 5 minutes.

  6. Once the meatballs are cooked, transfer them to a serving dish and pour the sauce over them. Gently toss the meatballs in the sauce until they are coated evenly.

  7. Serve the flavorful mini meatballs with toothpicks or cocktail skewers for easy grabbing. They can be enjoyed hot or at room temperature, making them ideal for a cocktail party.

Tips and Variations

  • For an extra kick of flavor, add some finely chopped jalapeno peppers or a dash of hot sauce to the meatball mixture.
  • If you prefer a tangier sauce, increase the amount of apple cider vinegar or add a splash of lemon juice.
  • To save time on the day of your party, you can prepare the meatballs in advance and freeze them. When you’re ready to serve, simply thaw them and bake as directed.
  • These mini meatballs can also be served as a main course with pasta or rice, or as a topping for sliders or sandwiches.

So there you have it - a delicious and easy recipe for flavorful mini meatballs that are perfect for cocktail parties. Your guests will love these bite-sized treats, and you’ll love how simple they are to make. Enjoy!
