Deliciously Simple 3-Ingredient Cookie Recipes


Looking for a quick and easy way to satisfy your sweet tooth? Look no further! In this article, we’ll be sharing some deliciously simple 3-ingredient cookie recipes that are sure to delight your taste buds. These recipes are perfect for beginner bakers or anyone who wants to whip up a batch of tasty treats in no time. So, let’s get started!

1. Peanut Butter Chocolate Chip Cookies

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup peanut butter
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1 egg
  • 1/2 cup chocolate chips (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C) and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
  2. In a mixing bowl, combine the peanut butter, sugar, and egg until well-blended.
  3. If desired, fold in the chocolate chips for an extra burst of flavor.
  4. Roll the dough into small balls and place them on the prepared baking sheet.
  5. Flatten each ball with a fork, creating a crisscross pattern.
  6. Bake for 10-12 minutes or until the edges are golden brown.
  7. Allow the cookies to cool on the baking sheet for a few minutes before transferring them to a wire rack to cool completely.

These peanut butter chocolate chip cookies are a classic combination that never fails to please. The creamy peanut butter blends perfectly with the sweetness of the sugar, while the optional chocolate chips add a delightful twist. Enjoy these cookies with a glass of milk or simply on their own for a delectable treat!

2. Oatmeal Raisin Cookies

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups oats
  • 1 cup ripe bananas, mashed
  • 1/2 cup raisins

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C) and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
  2. In a mixing bowl, combine the oats, mashed bananas, and raisins.
  3. Mix well until all the ingredients are evenly incorporated.
  4. Using a spoon or cookie scoop, drop small portions of the dough onto the prepared baking sheet.
  5. Flatten each portion slightly with the back of the spoon.
  6. Bake for 15-18 minutes or until the edges are golden brown.
  7. Allow the cookies to cool on the baking sheet before transferring them to a wire rack to cool completely.

These oatmeal raisin cookies are a healthier alternative to traditional cookies, thanks to the natural sweetness of ripe bananas and the chewiness of raisins. They make for a perfect snack or a quick breakfast on the go!

3. Coconut Macaroons

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups shredded coconut
  • 1/2 cup sweetened condensed milk
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 325°F (160°C) and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
  2. In a mixing bowl, combine the shredded coconut, sweetened condensed milk, and vanilla extract.
  3. Mix well until all the ingredients are thoroughly combined.
  4. Using a spoon or cookie scoop, drop small portions of the mixture onto the prepared baking sheet.
  5. Bake for 15-20 minutes or until the edges are golden brown.
  6. Allow the macaroons to cool on the baking sheet for a few minutes before transferring them to a wire rack to cool completely.

These coconut macaroons are a delightful treat for coconut lovers. The shredded coconut gives them a wonderful texture, while the sweetened condensed milk binds everything together. Enjoy these macaroons as a light and indulgent dessert!

Conclusion

There you have it! Three deliciously simple 3-ingredient cookie recipes that are sure to satisfy your sweet cravings. Whether you prefer the classic combination of peanut butter and chocolate, the wholesome goodness of oatmeal and raisins, or the tropical taste of coconut, these recipes are guaranteed to be a hit. So, why not give them a try and indulge in some homemade goodness? Happy baking!
