BBQ Chicken Flatbread: Quick and Flavorful Recipes


Are you looking for a quick and flavorful meal that will satisfy your cravings for BBQ chicken? Look no further! This BBQ chicken flatbread recipe is the perfect combination of smoky, tangy, and savory flavors, all packed into a convenient and easy-to-make flatbread. Whether you’re hosting a casual dinner with friends or craving a delicious homemade meal, this recipe is sure to become a favorite in your collection.

Ingredients

For the Flatbread:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup Greek yogurt
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil

For the BBQ Chicken Topping:

  • 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1 cup BBQ sauce
  • 1/2 cup red onion, thinly sliced
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/4 cup fresh cilantro, chopped
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

Step 1: Preparing the Flatbread

  1. In a large bowl, combine the all-purpose flour, baking powder, and salt.
  2. Add the Greek yogurt and olive oil to the flour mixture. Mix until a dough forms.
  3. On a lightly floured surface, knead the dough for a few minutes until smooth.
  4. Divide the dough into two equal portions and roll each portion into a thin oval shape to form the flatbreads.

Step 2: Cooking the Chicken

  1. Preheat the grill or a grill pan over medium-high heat.
  2. Season the chicken breasts with salt and pepper.
  3. Grill the chicken for 6-7 minutes on each side or until fully cooked.
  4. Remove the chicken from the grill and let it rest for a few minutes before slicing it into thin strips.

Step 3: Assembling the Flatbread

  1. Preheat the oven to 425°F (220°C).
  2. Place the prepared flatbreads on a baking sheet.
  3. Spread a layer of BBQ sauce over each flatbread, leaving a small border around the edges.
  4. Arrange the sliced chicken and red onion over the BBQ sauce.
  5. Sprinkle the shredded mozzarella cheese evenly over the top of each flatbread.

Step 4: Baking the Flatbread

  1. Place the baking sheet in the preheated oven and bake for 12-15 minutes, or until the edges of the flatbread are golden brown and the cheese is bubbly.
  2. Remove the flatbreads from the oven and let them cool for a few minutes.
  3. Sprinkle the chopped cilantro over the hot flatbreads for a burst of freshness.

Serving Suggestions

This BBQ chicken flatbread is best enjoyed fresh out of the oven, with a side of crisp, mixed greens or a refreshing cucumber and tomato salad. For a delightful contrast, pair it with a glass of chilled lemonade or your favorite craft beer.

Conclusion

With its simple preparation and explosive flavors, this BBQ chicken flatbread recipe is a versatile crowd-pleaser that is guaranteed to impress. Whether it’s a laid-back weeknight dinner or a casual gathering with loved ones, this recipe is a testament to the joy of simple, yet delicious, homemade food. So, go ahead, fire up the grill, and treat yourself to the wonderful aromas and flavors of this BBQ chicken flatbread. Your taste buds will thank you!
