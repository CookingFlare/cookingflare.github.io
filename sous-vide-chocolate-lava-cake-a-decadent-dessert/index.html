Sous Vide Chocolate Lava Cake: A Decadent Dessert


If you’re a chocolate lover, you’re in for a treat with this sous vide chocolate lava cake recipe. This indulgent dessert is a perfect way to satisfy your sweet tooth and impress your guests with its rich, velvety texture and delightful molten center. The sous vide cooking method ensures that the cake is evenly cooked to perfection, creating a luscious lava center that will have everyone swooning with delight.

Ingredients:

  • 8 oz (226g) high-quality dark chocolate, chopped
  • 1/2 cup (113g) unsalted butter
  • 3 large eggs
  • 3/4 cup (150g) granulated sugar
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1/4 cup (30g) all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp pure vanilla extract

Instructions:

Step 1: Prepare the Sous Vide Bath

Preheat your sous vide water bath to 185°F (85°C).

Step 2: Melt the Chocolate and Butter

In a heatproof bowl, combine the chopped dark chocolate and unsalted butter. Place the bowl over a pot of simmering water, creating a double boiler. Stir the chocolate and butter mixture occasionally until melted and smooth. Remove from heat and let it cool slightly.

Step 3: Prepare the Cake Batter

In a large mixing bowl, whisk together the eggs, granulated sugar, and salt until well combined. Add the melted chocolate and butter mixture, along with the vanilla extract, and gently fold until smooth. Sift in the all-purpose flour and fold until just combined.

Step 4: Fill the Mason Jars

Divide the batter evenly among four 4-ounce mason jars, leaving some space at the top for the cake to rise. Secure the lids on the jars, but not too tightly.

Step 5: Sous Vide Cooking

Carefully place the mason jars into the preheated sous vide water bath, ensuring that they are fully submerged. Cook for 45 minutes to 1 hour, depending on your desired lava cake consistency. Remove the jars from the water bath and let them cool for a few minutes.

Step 6: Serve and Enjoy

Carefully remove the lids from the mason jars and run a knife around the edge of the cakes to loosen them. Invert the cakes onto serving plates and garnish with a dusting of powdered sugar, a scoop of vanilla ice cream, or fresh berries. Serve immediately and enjoy the luxurious, molten center of the sous vide chocolate lava cake.

Tips and Variations:

  • For a flavor twist, add a splash of liqueur such as Grand Marnier or Amaretto to the cake batter before cooking.
  • Experiment with different types of chocolate, such as milk chocolate or a combination of dark and milk chocolate, to customize the flavor profile of the lava cake.
  • If you don’t have mason jars, you can use sous vide-safe resealable bags or silicone molds for cooking the lava cakes.

Conclusion:

Sous vide chocolate lava cake is a show-stopping dessert that elevates the classic chocolate cake to a whole new level of decadence. With the precise temperature control of sous vide cooking, you can achieve the perfect molten center every time, making this dessert a delightful treat for any special occasion or a luxurious indulgence for a cozy night in. Try this recipe and savor the irresistible allure of a velvety chocolate lava cake that will leave your taste buds longing for more.
