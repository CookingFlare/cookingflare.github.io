Delicious Bacon-Wrapped Meatloaf: A Tasty Twist on a Classic


If you’re a fan of comfort food and looking for a delicious twist on a classic dish, then you’re in for a treat! Our Bacon-Wrapped Meatloaf recipe is sure to satisfy your cravings and leave you wanting more. This mouthwatering dish combines the rich flavors of ground beef, savory bacon, and a blend of herbs and spices, resulting in a meatloaf that is moist, flavorful, and simply irresistible.

Ingredients

  • 1.5 pounds ground beef
  • 6 slices bacon
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/2 cup breadcrumbs
  • 1/4 cup milk
  • 1/4 cup ketchup
  • 2 tablespoons Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 tablespoon Dijon mustard
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).

  2. In a large mixing bowl, combine the ground beef, chopped onion, minced garlic, breadcrumbs, milk, ketchup, Worcestershire sauce, Dijon mustard, dried thyme, dried oregano, salt, and black pepper. Mix well until all the ingredients are evenly incorporated.

  3. On a clean work surface, lay out the bacon slices side by side, slightly overlapping each other. This will create a “bacon blanket” to wrap around the meatloaf.

  4. Place the meatloaf mixture on top of the bacon slices and shape it into a loaf shape. Carefully wrap the bacon slices around the meatloaf, tucking the ends underneath to secure them.

  5. Transfer the bacon-wrapped meatloaf to a baking dish or a loaf pan, making sure it fits snugly. This will help the meatloaf retain its shape during cooking.

  6. Place the baking dish in the preheated oven and bake for approximately 1 hour, or until the internal temperature reaches 160°F (71°C). This ensures that the meatloaf is cooked through and safe to eat.

  7. Once the meatloaf is cooked, remove it from the oven and let it rest for a few minutes before slicing. This allows the juices to redistribute, resulting in a moist and tender meatloaf.

  8. Serve your delicious bacon-wrapped meatloaf with your favorite sides, such as mashed potatoes and steamed vegetables. You can also drizzle some additional ketchup or barbecue sauce on top for extra flavor.

Tips and Variations

  • For a smoky twist, you can use smoked bacon instead of regular bacon.
  • Feel free to customize the seasoning by adding herbs and spices of your choice. Fresh herbs like parsley or basil can add a burst of freshness to the meatloaf.
  • If you prefer a glaze on top of the meatloaf, you can mix together ketchup, brown sugar, and Dijon mustard, and spread it over the bacon before baking.
  • Leftover bacon-wrapped meatloaf makes for delicious sandwiches the next day. Simply slice the meatloaf and place it between two slices of bread with your favorite condiments.

So there you have it, a scrumptious and flavorful twist on the classic meatloaf. With its crispy bacon exterior and moist, tender interior, this bacon-wrapped meatloaf is sure to be a hit at your dinner table. Give it a try and enjoy the mouthwatering goodness!
