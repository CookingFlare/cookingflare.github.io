Delightful Mini Apple Pies: Bite-Sized Fall Treats


Fall is finally here, and what better way to embrace the season than with Delightful Mini Apple Pies? These bite-sized treats are the perfect combination of sweet apples, warm spices, and flaky pastry, making them the ultimate fall dessert. Whether you’re hosting a cozy gathering or simply craving a delicious treat, these mini apple pies are sure to impress. So grab your apron and let’s get baking!

Ingredients

For the crust:

  • 2 ½ cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup unsalted butter, cold and cubed
  • 6-8 tablespoons ice water

For the filling:

  • 4 cups apples, peeled, cored, and diced
  • ½ cup granulated sugar
  • ¼ cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon cinnamon
  • ¼ teaspoon nutmeg
  • 2 tablespoons lemon juice

For the topping:

  • ¼ cup granulated sugar
  • 1 teaspoon cinnamon

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C) and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.

  2. In a large mixing bowl, combine the flour and salt for the crust. Add the cubed butter and use a pastry cutter or your fingers to cut the butter into the flour until it resembles coarse crumbs.

  3. Gradually add the ice water, one tablespoon at a time, and mix until the dough comes together. Be careful not to overmix. Divide the dough in half, shape each half into a disk, and wrap them in plastic wrap. Refrigerate for at least 30 minutes.

  4. In another bowl, combine the diced apples, granulated sugar, flour, cinnamon, nutmeg, and lemon juice. Toss until the apples are evenly coated.

  5. On a lightly floured surface, roll out one of the dough disks to a thickness of about 1/8 inch. Use a round cookie cutter or a glass to cut out circles that are about 3 inches in diameter. Repeat with the second dough disk.

  6. Place a spoonful of the apple filling onto the center of each dough circle. Fold the edges of the dough over the filling, pinching them together to seal the pies. Use a fork to crimp the edges for a decorative touch.

  7. In a small bowl, mix together the granulated sugar and cinnamon for the topping. Sprinkle the mixture over the mini apple pies.

  8. Transfer the pies to the prepared baking sheet and bake for 20-25 minutes, or until the crust is golden brown and the filling is bubbling.

  9. Remove from the oven and let the mini apple pies cool for a few minutes before serving. These delicious treats can be enjoyed warm or at room temperature.

Tips and Variations

  • Feel free to add a sprinkle of powdered sugar or a dollop of whipped cream on top of each mini apple pie for an extra touch of sweetness.
  • You can use a combination of different apple varieties for the filling, such as Granny Smith, Honeycrisp, or Golden Delicious, to add a depth of flavor.
  • If you prefer a more rustic look, you can skip the crimping and simply fold the edges of the dough over the filling without sealing them completely.
  • These mini apple pies can be stored in an airtight container at room temperature for up to 3 days. They can also be frozen for longer storage.

Enjoy the delightful flavors of fall with these bite-sized mini apple pies. With their flaky crust and spiced apple filling, they are the perfect treat to savor during the autumn season. Whether you’re baking for a special occasion or simply indulging in a sweet treat, these mini apple pies are guaranteed to bring joy and warmth to your kitchen. So gather your ingredients and start baking today!
