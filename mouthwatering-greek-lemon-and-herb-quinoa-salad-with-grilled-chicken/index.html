Mouthwatering Greek Lemon and Herb Quinoa Salad with Grilled Chicken


Are you ready to tantalize your taste buds with a vibrant Mediterranean dish that’s not only healthy but also bursting with flavor? This Greek Lemon and Herb Quinoa Salad with Grilled Chicken is a culinary delight that combines the nutty essence of quinoa with the zesty notes of fresh herbs and juicy grilled chicken. Whether you’re looking for a wholesome lunch, a light dinner, or a satisfying meal prep option, this salad fits the bill perfectly. Let’s dive into the details of this delectable recipe that will leave you craving more!

Ingredients

For the Quinoa Salad

  • 1 cup quinoa, rinsed
  • 2 cups water or low-sodium vegetable broth
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1 cucumber, diced
  • 1 bell pepper (red or yellow), diced
  • 1/2 red onion, finely chopped
  • 1/2 cup Kalamata olives, pitted and sliced
  • 1/2 cup feta cheese, crumbled
  • 1/4 cup fresh parsley, chopped
  • 1/4 cup fresh mint, chopped
  • Zest of 1 lemon
  • Salt and pepper to taste

For the Grilled Chicken

  • 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • Juice of 1 lemon
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste

For the Lemon Herb Dressing

  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • Juice of 1 lemon
  • 1 teaspoon Dijon mustard
  • 1 teaspoon honey or maple syrup
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

Step 1: Prepare the Quinoa

  1. Begin by rinsing the quinoa under cold water to remove its natural coating, known as saponin, which can give it a bitter taste.
  2. In a medium saucepan, combine the rinsed quinoa and water or vegetable broth. Bring it to a boil over medium-high heat.
  3. Once boiling, reduce the heat to low, cover the saucepan, and let it simmer for about 15 minutes or until all the liquid is absorbed.
  4. Remove from heat and let it sit for 5 minutes. Fluff the quinoa with a fork and set it aside to cool.

Step 2: Marinate the Chicken

  1. While the quinoa is cooking, prepare the marinade for the chicken. In a bowl, whisk together the olive oil, lemon juice, minced garlic, dried oregano, salt, and pepper.
  2. Place the chicken breasts in a resealable plastic bag or shallow dish, pour the marinade over them, and ensure they are well coated. Let them marinate for at least 30 minutes (or up to 2 hours in the refrigerator for deeper flavor).

Step 3: Grill the Chicken

  1. Preheat your grill or grill pan over medium-high heat.
  2. Remove the chicken from the marinade and discard the remaining marinade. Grill the chicken for about 6-7 minutes on each side or until the internal temperature reaches 165°F (75°C).
  3. Once cooked, transfer the chicken to a cutting board and let it rest for a few minutes before slicing it into strips.

Step 4: Assemble the Salad

  1. In a large mixing bowl, combine the cooled quinoa, cherry tomatoes, cucumber, bell pepper, red onion, Kalamata olives, feta cheese, parsley, mint, and lemon zest.
  2. Drizzle the salad with the lemon herb dressing, and gently toss to combine. Season with salt and pepper to taste.

Step 5: Serve

  1. Divide the salad into serving bowls or plates. Top each serving with slices of grilled chicken.
  2. Garnish with extra herbs or a sprinkle of feta cheese if desired. Serve immediately or refrigerate for later enjoyment.

Tips for the Perfect Greek Lemon and Herb Quinoa Salad

  • Customize Your Ingredients: Feel free to add or substitute other vegetables such as artichokes, radishes, or spinach based on your preferences. This salad is versatile and can accommodate various seasonal ingredients.
  • Make it Vegan: To enjoy a vegan version, simply omit the grilled chicken and feta cheese. You can add roasted chickpeas for added protein and texture.
  • Meal Prep Friendly: This salad is perfect for meal prep! Store the quinoa salad and grilled chicken separately in airtight containers in the refrigerator. It can last up to 4 days and makes for an easy grab-and-go lunch or dinner option.

Nutritional Benefits of Quinoa and Greek Ingredients

Quinoa is often hailed as a superfood, and for good reason. It is a complete protein, meaning it contains all nine essential amino acids that our bodies cannot produce on their own. Additionally, quinoa is high in fiber and packed with vitamins and minerals such as magnesium, iron, and B vitamins. Combined with the fresh vegetables and herbs in this salad, you’ll enjoy a nutrient-dense meal that supports overall health.

The Mediterranean diet, known for its focus on fresh, whole ingredients, promotes heart health and has been associated with numerous health benefits, including reduced inflammation and lower risks of chronic diseases. The use of olive oil, fresh herbs, and citrus not only adds flavor but also contributes to the antioxidant properties of the dish.

Conclusion

This Greek Lemon and Herb Quinoa Salad with Grilled Chicken is a delightful representation of the Mediterranean’s vibrant culinary scene. With its refreshing flavors and wholesome ingredients, it’s a dish that brings joy and nourishment to your table. Whether you’re hosting a summer barbecue, looking for a nutritious work lunch, or simply wanting to enjoy a delicious meal, this quinoa salad is sure to impress.

So, gather your ingredients and experience the mouthwatering taste of Greece right in your kitchen. Bon appétit!
