Paleo Chicken and Rice Bowl Recipes: 5 Delicious and Grain-Free Options


As the Paleo diet continues to gain popularity, many of us are looking for new and exciting ways to incorporate its principles into our daily meals. One of the most challenging aspects of following a Paleo diet is finding grain-free alternatives to traditional staples like rice. However, with a little creativity and experimentation, it’s easy to create delicious and satisfying Paleo chicken and rice bowl recipes that are both healthy and flavorful.

In this post, we’ll explore five different Paleo chicken and rice bowl recipes that are sure to become new favorites in your household. From classic combinations to more adventurous flavor profiles, these recipes showcase the versatility and simplicity of Paleo cooking.

Recipe 1: Spicy Chicken and Cauliflower Rice Bowl

  • 1 lb boneless, skinless chicken breast or thighs, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 2 cups cauliflower florets
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp ground cumin
  • 1 tsp smoked paprika
  • 1/2 tsp cayenne pepper
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • 2 cups mixed greens
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh cilantro
  • 2 tbsp fresh lime juice

Preheat oven to 400°F (200°C). Toss chicken with olive oil, cumin, smoked paprika, cayenne pepper, salt, and pepper on a baking sheet. Roast for 20-25 minutes, or until cooked through. Meanwhile, pulse cauliflower florets in a food processor until they resemble rice. Sauté in a skillet with a little olive oil until tender. To assemble the bowl, place cooked chicken on top of cauliflower “rice,” then add mixed greens, cilantro, and a squeeze of lime juice.

Recipe 2: Korean-Style Chicken and Zucchini Noodle Bowl

  • 1 lb boneless, skinless chicken breast or thighs, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 2 medium zucchinis
  • 2 tbsp coconut oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tsp ground ginger
  • 1 tsp soy sauce
  • 1 tsp honey
  • 1/4 tsp red pepper flakes
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • 2 cups mixed greens
  • 1/4 cup chopped toasted sesame seeds
  • 2 tbsp chopped green onions

Preheat oven to 400°F (200°C). Toss chicken with coconut oil, garlic, ginger, soy sauce, honey, red pepper flakes, salt, and pepper on a baking sheet. Roast for 20-25 minutes, or until cooked through. Meanwhile, spiralize zucchinis into noodles. Sauté in a skillet with a little coconut oil until tender. To assemble the bowl, place cooked chicken on top of zucchini noodles, then add mixed greens, sesame seeds, and green onions.

Recipe 3: Indian-Inspired Chicken and Sweet Potato Bowl

  • 1 lb boneless, skinless chicken breast or thighs, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 2 medium sweet potatoes
  • 2 tbsp coconut oil
  • 1 tsp ground cumin
  • 1 tsp curry powder
  • 1/2 tsp ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 tsp ground cayenne pepper
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • 2 cups mixed greens
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh cilantro
  • 2 tbsp plain Greek yogurt

Preheat oven to 400°F (200°C). Toss chicken with coconut oil, cumin, curry powder, cinnamon, cayenne pepper, salt, and pepper on a baking sheet. Roast for 20-25 minutes, or until cooked through. Meanwhile, pierce sweet potatoes with a fork several times and roast in the oven for 45-50 minutes, or until tender. To assemble the bowl, place cooked chicken on top of roasted sweet potato, then add mixed greens, cilantro, and a dollop of Greek yogurt.

Recipe 4: Mediterranean Chicken and Quinoa-Free Bowl

  • 1 lb boneless, skinless chicken breast or thighs, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 2 cups chopped eggplant
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • 1 tsp lemon zest
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • 2 cups mixed greens
  • 1/4 cup crumbled feta cheese
  • 2 tbsp chopped fresh parsley

Preheat oven to 400°F (200°C). Toss chicken with olive oil, garlic, oregano, lemon zest, salt, and pepper on a baking sheet. Roast for 20-25 minutes, or until cooked through. Meanwhile, slice eggplant into 1/2-inch thick rounds and brush with olive oil. Roast in the oven for 20-25 minutes, or until tender. To assemble the bowl, place cooked chicken on top of roasted eggplant, then add mixed greens, feta cheese, and parsley.

Recipe 5: Thai-Style Chicken and Green Bean Bowl

  • 1 lb boneless, skinless chicken breast or thighs, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 2 cups trimmed green beans
  • 2 tbsp coconut oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tsp grated ginger
  • 1 tsp Thai red curry paste
  • 1/4 tsp red pepper flakes
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • 2 cups mixed greens
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh cilantro
  • 2 tbsp chopped peanuts

Preheat oven to 400°F (200°C). Toss chicken with coconut oil, garlic, ginger, curry paste, red pepper flakes, salt, and pepper on a baking sheet. Roast for 20-25 minutes, or until cooked through. Meanwhile, steam green beans in a steamer basket until tender. To assemble the bowl, place cooked chicken on top of green beans, then add mixed greens, cilantro, and peanuts.

These Paleo chicken and rice bowl recipes offer a range of flavors and textures that are sure to satisfy even the pickiest of eaters. Whether you’re a seasoned Paleo enthusiast or just looking for a new way to approach mealtime, these recipes are a great place to start. So go ahead, get creative, and enjoy the delicious and grain-free goodness of Paleo cooking!
