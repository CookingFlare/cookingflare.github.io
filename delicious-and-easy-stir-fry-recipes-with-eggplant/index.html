Delicious and Easy Stir Fry Recipes with Eggplant


Stir fry recipes are a quick and delicious way to make a satisfying meal, and when it comes to stir fry, eggplant is a versatile and flavorful ingredient. In this blog post, we’ll explore some delicious and easy stir fry recipes that feature eggplant as the star of the dish. So grab your wok and let’s get cooking!

1. Eggplant and Tofu Stir Fry

Preparation time: 15 minutes Cooking time: 20 minutes Serves: 4

Ingredients:

  • 1 large eggplant, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 1 block of tofu, cut into cubes
  • 1 red bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 yellow bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 onion, sliced
  • 3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons oyster sauce
  • 1 tablespoon sesame oil
  • 1 teaspoon cornstarch
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Optional: chili flakes for added spice

Instructions:

  1. In a small bowl, whisk together the soy sauce, oyster sauce, sesame oil, cornstarch, and a pinch of salt and pepper. Set aside.
  2. Heat a tablespoon of oil in a wok or large skillet over medium-high heat. Add the tofu cubes and cook until golden brown on all sides. Remove the tofu from the pan and set aside.
  3. In the same pan, add another tablespoon of oil and sauté the garlic until fragrant. Add the eggplant and cook until it starts to soften, about 5 minutes.
  4. Add the bell peppers and onion to the pan and continue to cook for another 3-4 minutes, or until the vegetables are tender-crisp.
  5. Return the tofu to the pan and pour in the sauce mixture. Stir well to coat everything evenly.
  6. Cook for an additional 2-3 minutes, or until the sauce has thickened slightly.
  7. Serve the stir fry hot with steamed rice or noodles. Enjoy!

2. Spicy Eggplant and Chicken Stir Fry

Preparation time: 20 minutes Cooking time: 15 minutes Serves: 4

Ingredients:

  • 2 medium eggplants, cut into cubes
  • 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts, thinly sliced
  • 1 red bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 green bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 onion, sliced
  • 3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons hoisin sauce
  • 1 tablespoon chili garlic sauce
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a small bowl, whisk together the soy sauce, hoisin sauce, and chili garlic sauce. Set aside.
  2. Heat the vegetable oil in a wok or large skillet over medium-high heat. Add the chicken slices and cook until browned and cooked through. Remove the chicken from the pan and set aside.
  3. In the same pan, add the garlic and sauté until fragrant. Add the eggplant cubes and cook until they start to soften, about 5 minutes.
  4. Add the bell peppers and onion to the pan and continue to cook for another 3-4 minutes, or until the vegetables are tender-crisp.
  5. Return the chicken to the pan and pour in the sauce mixture. Stir well to coat everything evenly.
  6. Cook for an additional 2-3 minutes, or until the sauce has thickened slightly.
  7. Serve the stir fry hot with steamed rice or noodles. Enjoy the spicy kick!

3. Eggplant and Mushroom Stir Fry

Preparation time: 15 minutes Cooking time: 15 minutes Serves: 4

Ingredients:

  • 2 medium eggplants, cut into cubes
  • 8 ounces mushrooms, sliced
  • 1 red bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 yellow bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 onion, sliced
  • 3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons balsamic vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon honey
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a small bowl, whisk together the soy sauce, balsamic vinegar, honey, and a pinch of salt and pepper. Set aside.
  2. Heat the vegetable oil in a wok or large skillet over medium-high heat. Add the garlic and sauté until fragrant.
  3. Add the eggplant cubes and cook until they start to soften, about 5 minutes. Add the mushrooms and cook for an additional 3-4 minutes, or until they release their juices.
  4. Add the bell peppers and onion to the pan and continue to cook for another 3-4 minutes, or until the vegetables are tender-crisp.
  5. Pour in the sauce mixture and stir well to coat everything evenly.
  6. Cook for an additional 2-3 minutes, or until the sauce has thickened slightly.
  7. Serve the stir fry hot with steamed rice or noodles. Enjoy the earthy flavors of the mushrooms!

Conclusion

Eggplant is a versatile and delicious ingredient that adds a unique flavor and texture to stir fry dishes. Whether you prefer it paired with tofu, chicken, or mushrooms, these easy stir fry recipes are sure to satisfy your taste buds. So why not give them a try and discover the wonders of eggplant in your stir fry creations? Happy cooking!
