Satisfying Vegetarian Sliders for Meatless Mondays


If you’re looking for a delicious and satisfying option for Meatless Mondays, these vegetarian sliders are sure to hit the spot. Packed with flavor and made with wholesome ingredients, these sliders are the perfect way to enjoy a meatless meal without compromising on taste. Whether you’re a vegetarian or simply looking to incorporate more plant-based meals into your diet, these sliders are a must-try.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup cooked quinoa
  • 1 can black beans, drained and rinsed
  • ½ cup breadcrumbs
  • ½ cup grated cheddar cheese
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 1 teaspoon smoked paprika
  • ½ teaspoon cumin
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Slider buns
  • Toppings of your choice (lettuce, tomato, avocado, etc.)

Instructions

  1. In a large bowl, mash the black beans using a fork or potato masher until they are partially mashed but still have some texture.

  2. Add the cooked quinoa, breadcrumbs, grated cheddar cheese, chopped onion, minced garlic, soy sauce, smoked paprika, cumin, salt, and pepper to the bowl. Mix well until all the ingredients are evenly combined.

  3. Shape the mixture into small patties, about the size of your slider buns. You should be able to make around 8-10 patties, depending on the size you prefer.

  4. Heat a non-stick skillet over medium heat and add a drizzle of oil. Cook the patties for 3-4 minutes on each side, or until they are golden brown and crispy.

  5. Once the patties are cooked, remove them from the skillet and let them cool slightly.

  6. Assemble your sliders by placing a patty on each slider bun and adding your desired toppings. You can opt for classic toppings like lettuce, tomato, and mayo, or get creative with avocado, pickles, or spicy sauces.

  7. Serve your vegetarian sliders alongside a side salad or some sweet potato fries for a complete and satisfying meal.

Why Choose Vegetarian Sliders?

These vegetarian sliders are not only delicious but also offer numerous health benefits. By opting for a plant-based meal, you’re reducing your intake of saturated fats and cholesterol found in meat, while increasing your consumption of nutrient-rich vegetables and legumes. The combination of quinoa and black beans in these sliders provides a good source of protein, fiber, and essential vitamins and minerals.

Additionally, choosing vegetarian options can have a positive impact on the environment. Livestock farming is a significant contributor to greenhouse gas emissions and deforestation. By incorporating more meatless meals into your diet, you’re helping to reduce your carbon footprint and support a more sustainable food system.

Conclusion

These satisfying vegetarian sliders are a tasty and wholesome alternative to traditional meat sliders. Packed with flavor, protein, and nutrients, these sliders are perfect for Meatless Mondays or any day of the week. Whether you’re a vegetarian or simply looking to reduce your meat consumption, these sliders are sure to satisfy your cravings and leave you feeling nourished and satisfied. Give them a try and discover the joys of meatless eating. Enjoy!
