Deliciously Simple No-Bake S'mores Bars: A Campfire-Inspired Treat


If you’re a fan of the classic campfire treat, S’mores, then get ready to indulge in a new twist on this beloved favorite. These Deliciously Simple No-Bake S’mores Bars are the perfect combination of gooey marshmallows, rich chocolate, and crunchy graham crackers, all without the need for a campfire. Whether you’re hosting a summer BBQ, planning a cozy movie night at home, or just craving something sweet, these bars are sure to be a hit with both kids and adults alike.

Ingredients

For the Crust

  • 2 cups crushed graham crackers
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, melted
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar

For the Filling

  • 3 cups mini marshmallows
  • 2 cups milk chocolate chips
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • Pinch of salt

For the Topping

  • 1 cup milk chocolate chips
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • 1 cup mini marshmallows

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Crust

    • In a large bowl, combine the crushed graham crackers, melted butter, and granulated sugar. Mix until the ingredients are well combined and the mixture resembles wet sand.

  2. Press the Crust

    • Press the graham cracker mixture firmly and evenly into the bottom of a 9x13-inch baking dish. Use the back of a spoon or a flat-bottomed glass to ensure the crust is compacted.

  3. Prepare the Filling

    • In a microwave-safe bowl, combine the milk chocolate chips and heavy cream. Microwave in 30-second intervals, stirring in between, until the chocolate is completely melted and smooth. Stir in the vanilla extract and salt.

  4. Add the Marshmallows

    • Gently fold in the mini marshmallows into the chocolate mixture until they are evenly distributed.

  5. Assemble the Bars

    • Pour the chocolate and marshmallow filling over the graham cracker crust, spreading it into an even layer with a spatula.

  6. Prepare the Topping

    • In another microwave-safe bowl, combine the milk chocolate chips and heavy cream. Microwave in 30-second intervals, stirring in between, until the chocolate is melted and smooth.

  7. Add the Final Touch

    • Pour the chocolate topping over the marshmallow layer, spreading it into an even layer. Sprinkle the mini marshmallows on top of the chocolate layer, gently pressing them in.

  8. Chill and Serve

    • Refrigerate the S’mores Bars for at least 2 hours, or until the bars are set. Once chilled, cut into squares and serve.

Tips and Tricks

  • For a gooey and decadent texture, allow the bars to come to room temperature for a few minutes before serving.
  • You can customize the bars by adding chopped nuts, shredded coconut, or even a drizzle of caramel sauce to the filling.
  • Store any leftover bars in an airtight container in the refrigerator for up to 5 days.

These Deliciously Simple No-Bake S’mores Bars are a delightful way to enjoy the flavors of a classic campfire treat without the need for open flames. Whether you’re whipping them up for a special occasion or just to satisfy a sweet tooth, these bars are sure to become a new favorite in your dessert repertoire. So, gather your ingredients and get ready to indulge in the irresistible combination of graham crackers, marshmallows, and chocolate with these delightful no-bake S’mores Bars!
