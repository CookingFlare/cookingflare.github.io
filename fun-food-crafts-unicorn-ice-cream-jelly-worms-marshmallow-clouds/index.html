Fun Food Crafts: Unicorn Ice Cream, Jelly worms & Marshmallow Clouds


Are you ready to embark on a delectable and enchanting culinary adventure? Feast your eyes and taste buds on this dazzling trio of fun food crafts that are sure to make any occasion even more magical. Dive right in and let’s create a fantastical world of sweet delights with: Unicorn Ice Cream, Jelly Worms, and Marshmallow Clouds.

Unicorn Ice Cream

The first course in our enchanting feast is none other than the iconic Unicorn Ice Cream. This whimsical dessert pays homage to the beloved mythical creature while indulging our sweet tooth. Here’s how you can create it in your own kitchen:

Ingredients:

  • One pint of your favorite ice cream flavor (vanilla, strawberry, or green apple work best for that unicorn vibe!)
  • One drop of food coloring (use pink, blue, or purple to create a multi-colored swirl)
  • A handful of rainbow sprinkles and edible glitter

Instructions:

  1. Here’s where things get urgen-ly fun! Start by scooping a generous portion of ice cream into a microwave-safe bowl. If you wish to create a spectrum of colors, split your ice cream into separate bowls and dye each portion with a different color.
  2. Gently swirl your colored ice cream with a knife or spoon to form ribbons of color.
  3. Sprinkle with a handful of colorful goodies while your ice cream is still soft. As it returns to the freezer, the rainbow sprinkles and edible glitter will get embedded in the ice cream for a majestic appearance.
  4. Freeze your masterpiece for 15 minutes before digging in. Make sure you serve it on a pretty plate or in a glass to fully appreciate the magic of your Undicorn Ice Cream!

Jelly Worms

Ấe Next up, we have a creepily delightful dessert that adds a squiggly surprise to any sweet spread. Introducing Jelly Worms! These squishy treats can easily be customized to fit any color scheme or theme party.

Ingredients:

  • A pack of strawberry, lime, and berry-flavored gelatin mix
  • One cup of boiling water, divided
  • A handful of edible food paint or gel food coloring
  • Tray lined with silicone or wax paper

Instructions:

  1. Stir each flavor of gelatin mix into a separate cup containing one-third cup of boiling water. Ensure you add a drop or two of food paint per cup to color your gelatin. Let each mixture sit for a minute to let it bloom.
  2. Add the remaining boiling water to each cup, stirring until the gelatin completely dissolves. Pour the colored gelatin into individual zip-lock bags, removing as much air as possible, and sealing them tightly.
  3. Lay the bags flat on the tray, and use your hands or a rolling pin to help flatten out the gelatin mixture into long, thin worms (about the thickness of spaghetti).
  4. As the gelatin begins to thicken, hang it up vertically by the corner of the zip-lock bag or onto a skewer. Allow your worms to dry and set. Once set to your desired formation and firmness, you can peel the worms out of the bag and enjoy!

Marshmallow Clouds

Finally, we have the pièce de résistance: Marshmallow Clouds! Gentle and airy, these yummy clouds are the perfect finishing touch to our enchanting feast.

Ingredients:

  • A bag of large marshmallows
  • Edible white glitter or snow sparkles
  • A straw for each marshmallow

Instructions:

  1. The beauty of this cloud is in its simplicity. Start by giving each marshmallow a light spritz of edible shimmer spray, or gently roll them in the edible glitter to create the illusion of snowflakes.
  2. Now, the finale: take a marshmallow and insert a straw through its center. Twist the straw around to create a wispy cloud-like appearance.
  3. Display your marshmallow clouds artfully on a dessert table or serve alongside your delicious concoctions from before.

So gather your friends, family, or whipped cream– it’s time to unveil this fantastical feast. Treat yourself to an array of flamboyant ice cream, gelatin worms, and marshmallow clouds. Get ready for an unexpectedly delightful adventure that will tickle your taste buds and ignite your culinary creativity! Enjoy fulfilling your dream of becoming a dessert wizard in this whimsical world that’s full of sweet surprises.
