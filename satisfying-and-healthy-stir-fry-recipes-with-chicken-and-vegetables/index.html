Satisfying and Healthy Stir Fry Recipes with Chicken and Vegetables


Looking for a quick, delicious, and healthy meal? Look no further than these satisfying stir fry recipes with chicken and vegetables. Stir fry is a versatile cooking technique that allows you to mix and match your favorite ingredients while keeping the flavors fresh and vibrant. Plus, it’s a great way to incorporate more vegetables into your diet. So let’s dive in and discover some mouthwatering stir fry recipes!

1. Classic Chicken and Vegetable Stir Fry

Ingredients:

  • 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts, thinly sliced
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon cornstarch
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1 bell pepper, thinly sliced
  • 1 broccoli crown, cut into florets
  • 1 carrot, thinly sliced
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon grated ginger
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • Optional garnish: sesame seeds, chopped green onions

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, combine the soy sauce and cornstarch. Add the sliced chicken and toss to coat. Set aside for 10 minutes to marinate.
  2. Heat the vegetable oil in a large skillet or wok over medium-high heat. Add the marinated chicken and stir fry until cooked through, about 5-6 minutes. Remove the chicken from the skillet and set aside.
  3. In the same skillet, add the bell pepper, broccoli, carrot, garlic, and ginger. Stir fry for 3-4 minutes, or until the vegetables are crisp-tender.
  4. Return the cooked chicken to the skillet and stir fry for an additional 2 minutes, allowing the flavors to meld. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  5. Serve the stir fry over steamed rice or noodles. Garnish with sesame seeds and chopped green onions, if desired.

2. Spicy Thai Basil Chicken Stir Fry

Ingredients:

  • 2 boneless, skinless chicken thighs, thinly sliced
  • 2 tablespoons oyster sauce
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon fish sauce
  • 1 tablespoon hoisin sauce
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • 1 onion, thinly sliced
  • 1 red bell pepper, thinly sliced
  • 1 cup snap peas
  • 2 Thai chilies, thinly sliced (adjust to taste)
  • Handful of fresh basil leaves
  • Optional garnish: lime wedges, chopped peanuts

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, whisk together the oyster sauce, soy sauce, fish sauce, and hoisin sauce. Add the sliced chicken and toss to coat. Let it marinate for 15 minutes.
  2. Heat the vegetable oil in a large skillet or wok over medium-high heat. Add the marinated chicken and stir fry until cooked through, about 5-6 minutes. Remove the chicken from the skillet and set aside.
  3. In the same skillet, add the onion, red bell pepper, snap peas, and Thai chilies. Stir fry for 3-4 minutes until the vegetables are tender-crisp.
  4. Return the cooked chicken to the skillet and stir fry for an additional minute. Add the fresh basil leaves and stir until wilted.
  5. Serve the spicy Thai basil chicken stir fry over steamed rice. Garnish with lime wedges and chopped peanuts, if desired.

3. Teriyaki Chicken and Vegetable Stir Fry

Ingredients:

  • 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts, thinly sliced
  • 1/4 cup teriyaki sauce
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • 1 zucchini, thinly sliced
  • 1 yellow squash, thinly sliced
  • 1 cup sliced mushrooms
  • 1 cup snow peas
  • 1/2 cup sliced carrots
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • Optional garnish: sesame seeds, sliced green onions

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, combine the sliced chicken with the teriyaki sauce. Allow it to marinate for 15 minutes.
  2. Heat the vegetable oil in a large skillet or wok over medium-high heat. Add the marinated chicken and stir fry until cooked through, about 5-6 minutes. Remove the chicken from the skillet and set aside.
  3. In the same skillet, add the zucchini, yellow squash, mushrooms, snow peas, carrots, and garlic. Stir fry for 3-4 minutes until the vegetables are tender.
  4. Return the cooked chicken to the skillet and stir fry for an additional minute, allowing the flavors to meld.
  5. Serve the teriyaki chicken and vegetable stir fry over steamed rice or noodles. Garnish with sesame seeds and sliced green onions, if desired.

Conclusion

These satisfying and healthy stir fry recipes with chicken and vegetables are sure to become your go-to meals. They are quick, easy to make, and packed with flavor. Whether you prefer a classic stir fry, a spicy Thai basil version, or a teriyaki twist, there’s a recipe here to suit your taste buds. So grab your skillet, chop up some fresh ingredients, and get ready to savor the deliciousness of homemade stir fry!
