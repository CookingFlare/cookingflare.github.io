The Corpse Reviver No. 1 2.0: A Modern Twist on a Classic Cocktail


The Corpse Reviver No. 1 is a classic cocktail that has been enjoyed for decades. With its refreshing taste and intriguing mix of ingredients, it has become a favorite among cocktail enthusiasts. But what if we told you that there’s a modern twist to this beloved drink? Introducing the Corpse Reviver No. 1 2.0, a reinvented version that will take your taste buds on a wild ride.

The Origins of the Corpse Reviver No. 1

Before we dive into the modern twist, let’s take a moment to appreciate the origins of the Corpse Reviver No. 1. This cocktail first appeared in the 1930s and was touted as a “hair of the dog” remedy for hangovers. The original recipe called for equal parts cognac, apple brandy, and sweet vermouth, with a dash of absinthe for an added kick.

The Modern Twist: A Flavor Explosion

To create the Corpse Reviver No. 1 2.0, we’ve taken inspiration from the original recipe and added a few unexpected ingredients that will elevate your drinking experience. Here’s what you’ll need:

  • 1 ½ ounces of gin
  • ¾ ounce of yellow Chartreuse
  • ¾ ounce of Lillet Blanc
  • ½ ounce of freshly squeezed lemon juice
  • A splash of absinthe
  • Lemon peel, for garnish

To start, fill a cocktail shaker with ice and add the gin, yellow Chartreuse, Lillet Blanc, and lemon juice. Shake vigorously until well chilled. In a separate glass, rinse the inside with a splash of absinthe to add that distinctive flavor. Discard any excess absinthe.

Next, strain the cocktail into the absinthe-rinsed glass and garnish with a twist of lemon peel. The citrusy notes from the lemon juice complement the herbal undertones of the Chartreuse and absinthe, creating a harmonious blend of flavors.

Exploring the Flavor Profile

The Corpse Reviver No. 1 2.0 offers a complex and vibrant flavor profile that will keep you coming back for more. The gin provides a crisp and botanical base, while the yellow Chartreuse adds a touch of sweetness and a hint of spice. The Lillet Blanc contributes a subtle fruitiness, balancing out the other ingredients perfectly. The addition of absinthe adds an intriguing anise flavor that lingers on the palate, making each sip an adventure.

Serving Suggestions and Variations

This modern twist on the classic Corpse Reviver No. 1 is best enjoyed in a chilled cocktail glass. For an extra touch of elegance, consider rimming the glass with sugar or garnishing with a sprig of fresh herbs, such as mint or thyme.

If you’re feeling adventurous, feel free to experiment with variations of the Corpse Reviver No. 1 2.0. You can try substituting the gin with your favorite spirit, such as vodka or tequila, to create a unique twist. Additionally, you can play around with the ratios of the ingredients to find the perfect balance that suits your taste buds.

Final Thoughts

The Corpse Reviver No. 1 2.0 is a modern take on a classic cocktail that pays homage to its roots while introducing exciting new flavors. Whether you’re a seasoned cocktail enthusiast or someone looking to explore the world of mixology, this drink is sure to impress. So gather your ingredients, shake up a glass of this delightful concoction, and let the Corpse Reviver No. 1 2.0 transport you to a world of taste and sophistication. Cheers!
