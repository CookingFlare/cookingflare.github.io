Sizzling Spicy Garlic Butter Shrimp and Pasta Recipe


Are you tired of the same old pasta dishes and looking for something new and exciting to spice up your dinner routine? Look no further! Today, we’re going to dive into a recipe that’s sure to tantalize your taste buds and leave you wanting more - Sizzling Spicy Garlic Butter Shrimp and Pasta. This mouth-watering dish combines the flavors of succulent shrimp, aromatic garlic, and spicy kick of red pepper flakes with the comfort of al dente pasta.

Ingredients

For this recipe, you’ll need the following ingredients:

  • 12 large shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 12 oz pasta of your choice (we recommend spaghetti or linguine)
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tbsp unsalted butter
  • 1 tsp red pepper flakes
  • 1 tsp lemon zest
  • 2 tbsp freshly squeezed lemon juice
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley, chopped (optional)

Instructions

Step 1: Cook the Pasta

Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil and cook your chosen pasta according to the package instructions until al dente. Reserve 1 cup of pasta water before draining the spaghetti.

Step 2: Prepare the Shrimp

Rinse the shrimp under cold water and pat them dry with paper towels. Season with salt and pepper.

Step 3: Cook the Shrimp

Heat 1 tablespoon of butter in a large skillet over medium-high heat. Add the shrimp and cook for 2-3 minutes per side, or until they’re pink and cooked through. Transfer the shrimp to a plate and set aside.

Step 4: Make the Garlic Butter Sauce

In the same skillet, add the remaining 1 tablespoon of butter. Once melted, add the minced garlic and cook for 1-2 minutes, or until fragrant.

Step 5: Add the Red Pepper Flakes and Lemon Zest

Add the red pepper flakes and lemon zest to the skillet and cook for an additional 30 seconds, stirring constantly.

Step 6: Combine the Shrimp and Garlic Butter Sauce

Add the cooked shrimp back into the skillet and toss with the garlic butter sauce until well coated.

Step 7: Combine the Pasta and Shrimp

Add the cooked pasta to the skillet and toss with the shrimp and garlic butter sauce. If the sauce seems too thick, add a little of the reserved pasta water.

Step 8: Season and Serve

Season the pasta with salt and pepper to taste. Serve hot, garnished with chopped parsley if desired.

Tips and Variations

  • For an extra spicy kick, add more red pepper flakes or use hot sauce to taste.
  • Substitute the shrimp with chicken or scallops for a different protein option.
  • Add some cherry tomatoes or bell peppers to the garlic butter sauce for added flavor and color.
  • Experiment with different types of pasta, such as linguine or fettuccine, for a change of pace.

Conclusion

And there you have it - a Sizzling Spicy Garlic Butter Shrimp and Pasta recipe that’s sure to become a new favorite. With its perfect balance of spicy and savory flavors, this dish is sure to please even the pickiest of eaters. So go ahead, give it a try, and get ready to impress your family and friends with this mouth-watering recipe!
