One-Pot Mediterranean Pasta: Easy and Flavorful Recipes


Are you looking for a delicious and hassle-free dinner option that brings the flavors of the Mediterranean right to your table? Look no further than these easy and flavorful one-pot Mediterranean pasta recipes. Packed with fresh ingredients, aromatic herbs, and rich olive oil, these dishes are sure to transport your taste buds to the sunny shores of the Mediterranean.

Recipe 1: Classic Mediterranean Veggie Pasta

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1 zucchini, sliced
  • 1 yellow bell pepper, diced
  • 1 red onion, thinly sliced
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1/2 teaspoon red pepper flakes
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 8 oz penne pasta
  • 3 cups vegetable broth
  • 1/4 cup black olives, sliced
  • 2 tablespoons capers
  • Fresh basil leaves for garnish

Instructions:

  1. In a large pot, combine cherry tomatoes, zucchini, bell pepper, red onion, garlic, oregano, basil, red pepper flakes, salt, and pepper.
  2. Add penne pasta and vegetable broth to the pot. Bring to a simmer over medium heat.
  3. Cover and cook for about 15-20 minutes, stirring occasionally until the pasta is cooked and most of the liquid is absorbed.
  4. Stir in black olives and capers. Adjust seasoning if needed.
  5. Serve hot, garnished with fresh basil leaves.

Recipe 2: Lemon and Herb Shrimp Mediterranean Pasta

Ingredients:

  • 8 oz spaghetti
  • 1 lb shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 1 lemon, zested and juiced
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • 1 teaspoon dried rosemary
  • 1/2 teaspoon paprika
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 cups cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1/2 cup feta cheese, crumbled
  • Fresh parsley for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Cook spaghetti according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  2. In the same pot, sauté shrimp until pink and cooked through. Remove from the pot and set aside.
  3. In the pot, add lemon zest, lemon juice, garlic, thyme, rosemary, paprika, salt, and pepper. Cook for a minute.
  4. Add cherry tomatoes and cook until slightly softened.
  5. Return shrimp to the pot, along with cooked spaghetti. Toss everything together.
  6. Sprinkle feta cheese over the pasta.
  7. Serve hot, garnished with fresh parsley.

Recipe 3: Sun-Dried Tomato and Artichoke Mediterranean Pasta

Ingredients:

  • 10 oz fusilli pasta
  • 1/2 cup sun-dried tomatoes, chopped
  • 1 can artichoke hearts, drained and quartered
  • 1/4 cup pine nuts
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried thyme
  • Red pepper flakes to taste
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • Fresh basil leaves for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Cook fusilli pasta according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a large skillet, toast pine nuts until lightly browned. Remove from the skillet and set aside.
  3. In the same skillet, sauté garlic until fragrant. Add sun-dried tomatoes, artichoke hearts, basil, thyme, and red pepper flakes.
  4. Toss in the cooked pasta and pine nuts. Mix well.
  5. Sprinkle Parmesan cheese over the pasta.
  6. Serve hot, garnished with fresh basil leaves.

These one-pot Mediterranean pasta recipes are not only easy to make but also bursting with Mediterranean flavors. Whether you prefer a vegetarian option, a seafood delight, or a pasta loaded with sun-dried tomatoes and artichokes, there’s a recipe here for everyone to enjoy. Try these recipes for a taste of the Mediterranean right in your own kitchen! Enjoy your culinary journey!
