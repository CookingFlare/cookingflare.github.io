The Boulevardier 4.0: A Modern Take on a Classic Whiskey Cocktail


If you’re a whiskey lover looking for a cocktail that combines the richness of bourbon with the complexity of Campari, then The Boulevardier 4.0 is the drink for you. This modern twist on a classic whiskey cocktail brings together the best of both worlds, creating a delightful and sophisticated drink that will impress your guests at any gathering.

Ingredients

To make The Boulevardier 4.0, you’ll need the following ingredients:

  • 2 ounces of bourbon
  • 1 ounce of sweet vermouth
  • 1 ounce of Campari
  • 2 dashes of orange bitters
  • Orange peel, for garnish

Instructions

  1. In a mixing glass filled with ice, add the bourbon, sweet vermouth, Campari, and orange bitters. Stir gently until well combined and chilled.

  2. Strain the mixture into a chilled cocktail glass. If you prefer a stronger drink, you can serve it over a large ice cube in an old-fashioned glass.

  3. Garnish The Boulevardier 4.0 with a twist of orange peel. This will not only add a pop of color but also enhance the aroma of the cocktail.

  4. Serve and enjoy!

The Boulevardier 4.0: A Modern Twist

The Boulevardier 4.0 takes inspiration from the classic cocktail, Boulevardier, which was created in the 1920s. Traditionally, the Boulevardier is made with bourbon, sweet vermouth, and Campari, but The Boulevardier 4.0 adds a touch of modernity with the addition of orange bitters.

The combination of bourbon and Campari creates a harmonious balance of flavors. The bourbon brings warmth and depth, while the Campari adds a bitter and herbal complexity. The sweet vermouth adds a touch of sweetness and rounds out the flavors.

The addition of orange bitters in The Boulevardier 4.0 takes this cocktail to the next level. The bitters add a citrusy and aromatic note, enhancing the overall taste experience. It adds a modern twist to the classic recipe, making it a drink that stands out from the crowd.

Serving Suggestions

The Boulevardier 4.0 is a versatile cocktail that can be enjoyed on various occasions. Whether you’re hosting a cocktail party or simply want to unwind after a long day, this drink is an excellent choice.

For a more festive touch, consider serving The Boulevardier 4.0 at your next holiday gathering. The rich colors of the cocktail and the aromatic orange peel garnish will add a touch of elegance to any celebration.

If you’re a fan of whiskey-based cocktails, you can also experiment with different bourbons to create your own unique version of The Boulevardier 4.0. Each bourbon has its own distinct flavor profile, which can add depth and complexity to the drink.

Conclusion

The Boulevardier 4.0 is a modern twist on a classic whiskey cocktail that will delight your taste buds and impress your guests. With its rich flavors, balanced ingredients, and elegant presentation, it’s a drink that deserves a spot on your cocktail menu.

So, the next time you’re in the mood for a sophisticated and memorable drink, reach for The Boulevardier 4.0. Cheers to the perfect blend of tradition and innovation!
