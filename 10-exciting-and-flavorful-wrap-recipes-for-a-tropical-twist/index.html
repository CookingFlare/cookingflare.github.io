10 Exciting and Flavorful Wrap Recipes for a Tropical Twist


Are you tired of the same old sandwich for lunch? Looking for something fresh and exciting to spice up your mealtime routine? Look no further! In this blog post, we bring you 10 exciting and flavorful wrap recipes that will transport your taste buds to a tropical paradise. These wraps are not only delicious but also easy to make, making them perfect for a quick and satisfying meal. So, without further ado, let’s dive into these mouthwatering recipes!

1. Hawaiian BBQ Chicken Wrap

Ingredients:

  • Grilled chicken breast
  • Pineapple chunks
  • Red onion slices
  • Cilantro leaves
  • BBQ sauce
  • Tortilla wrap

Instructions:

  1. Lay the tortilla wrap flat on a clean surface.
  2. Spread a generous amount of BBQ sauce over the wrap.
  3. Place the grilled chicken breast, pineapple chunks, red onion slices, and cilantro leaves on top of the sauce.
  4. Roll the wrap tightly, tucking in the sides as you go.
  5. Slice the wrap into bite-sized pieces and enjoy!

2. Tropical Shrimp and Mango Wrap

Ingredients:

  • Cooked shrimp
  • Mango slices
  • Avocado slices
  • Red bell pepper strips
  • Fresh spinach leaves
  • Lime juice
  • Tortilla wrap

Instructions:

  1. Place the tortilla wrap on a flat surface.
  2. Layer the cooked shrimp, mango slices, avocado slices, red bell pepper strips, and fresh spinach leaves on top of the wrap.
  3. Squeeze lime juice over the ingredients for a tangy kick.
  4. Roll the wrap tightly, ensuring all the ingredients are securely wrapped inside.
  5. Cut the wrap into sections and savor the burst of tropical flavors!

3. Caribbean Jerk Chicken Wrap

Ingredients:

  • Grilled jerk chicken
  • Sliced plantains

  • Tortilla wrap

Instructions:

  4. Slice the wrap into smaller pieces and dive into the Caribbean-inspired goodness!

4. Pineapple Teriyaki Tofu Wrap

Ingredients:

  • Marinated tofu
  • Grilled pineapple slices
  • Cucumber slices
  • Carrot ribbons
  • Teriyaki sauce
  • Tortilla wrap

Instructions:

  1. Lay the tortilla wrap flat on a clean surface.
  2. Arrange the marinated tofu, grilled pineapple slices, cucumber slices, and carrot ribbons on top of the wrap.
  3. Drizzle teriyaki sauce over the ingredients for extra flavor.
  4. Roll the wrap tightly, ensuring all the ingredients are snugly wrapped inside.
  5. Cut the wrap into bite-sized portions and relish the tropical fusion of flavors!

5. Tropical Black Bean Wrap

  • Black beans (cooked or canned)
  • Sliced avocado
  • Diced tomatoes
  • Red onion slices
  • Fresh cilantro leaves
  • Lime juice
  • Tortilla wrap

Instructions:

  2. Layer the black beans, sliced avocado, diced tomatoes, red onion slices, and fresh cilantro leaves onto the wrap.
  3. Squeeze lime juice over the ingredients for a zesty taste.
  4. Roll the wrap tightly, ensuring all the ingredients are well-packed.
  5. Slice the wrap into smaller pieces and immerse yourself in the tropical goodness!
