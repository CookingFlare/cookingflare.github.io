Easy Blueberry and Lavender Frozen Yogurt


If you’re looking to indulge in a dessert that’s both light and bursting with flavor, this Blueberry and Lavender Frozen Yogurt recipe is exactly what you need. It’s a delightful blend of sweet, tangy blueberries with the subtle, floral notes of lavender—a perfect balance that makes this frozen yogurt incredibly refreshing. Whether you’re cooling off on a hot summer day or craving a healthy yet satisfying dessert any time of year, this recipe is straightforward and quick to make, requiring only a handful of ingredients and minimal prep time.

Why Blueberry and Lavender?

Blueberries are packed with antioxidants, vitamins, and fiber. Their natural sweetness and vibrant color make them an excellent choice for frozen desserts. Lavender, on the other hand, offers a unique, aromatic flavor that elevates the yogurt’s profile without overpowering it. The subtle floral hints complement the fruity blueberries beautifully, creating a sophisticated yet accessible frozen treat.

This combination not only tastes great but also looks stunning in its pale purple hue speckled with deep-blue berries—an enticing dessert for both the eyes and the palate.

Ingredients You’ll Need

  • 2 cups fresh or frozen blueberries
  • 2 cups plain Greek yogurt (full-fat or low-fat, depending on preference)
  • 1/2 cup honey or agave nectar (adjust sweetness to taste)
  • 1 tablespoon culinary lavender buds (dried)
  • 1 teaspoon lemon juice (for brightness)
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • Pinch of salt

Using Greek yogurt provides the creamy texture and protein that make this dessert more filling and nutritious compared to standard frozen yogurt. Adjust the honey or agave nectar if you prefer less sweetness.

Step-by-Step Preparation

Step 1: Infuse the Lavender

Lavender’s delicate flavor shines best when infused gently. Start by bringing 1/2 cup of water to a boil, then remove from heat. Add the dried lavender buds and cover, allowing the mixture to steep for about 10 minutes. Strain the lavender-water infusion through a fine mesh sieve to remove the buds and set the liquid aside to cool completely.

Step 2: Prepare the Blueberries

If you are using fresh blueberries, give them a quick rinse and pat dry. For frozen blueberries, allow them to thaw slightly to release some juice, which will intensify the flavor and help blend smoothly.

Next, blend the blueberries in a food processor or blender until you achieve a puree with a few small chunks left for texture. Reserve a handful of whole berries to fold in later if you prefer bursts of whole blueberry flavor throughout the frozen yogurt.

Step 3: Mix the Yogurt Base

In a large mixing bowl, combine the Greek yogurt, honey, lemon juice, vanilla extract, pinch of salt, and the chilled lavender infusion. Stir well to incorporate all the flavors evenly.

Step 4: Combine Blueberry Puree and Yogurt

Add the blueberry puree to the yogurt mixture and fold gently to create a marbled effect or blend fully for a uniform color and flavor, depending on your aesthetic preference. If you saved some whole blueberries, now is the time to fold them in for added texture and visual appeal.

Step 5: Freeze the Mixture

Pour the mixture into an ice cream maker and churn according to the manufacturer’s instructions, usually about 20-30 minutes until it reaches a soft-serve consistency.

If you don’t have an ice cream maker, pour the mixture into a shallow freezer-safe container, cover, and freeze. Every 30 minutes, stir the mixture vigorously with a fork or whisk to break up ice crystals until it freezes evenly. This manual churning will take around 3-4 hours.

Step 6: Serve and Enjoy

Once the frozen yogurt has set, serve it immediately for a soft-serve experience or transfer it to an airtight container and freeze for an additional 1-2 hours for a more scoopable texture.

Garnish each serving with a sprinkle of dried lavender buds or a few fresh blueberries for elegance and extra aroma.

Tips for Perfect Frozen Yogurt Every Time

  • Choosing Yogurt: Full-fat Greek yogurt creates a creamier texture, but low-fat versions work fine if you want fewer calories.
  • Adjusting Sweetness: Blueberries vary in sweetness. Taste the mixture before freezing and add more honey or agave nectar as desired.
  • Lavender Selection: Use culinary-grade lavender buds. Avoid garden varieties treated with pesticides.
  • Swirl Effects: For a more visually appealing dessert, try swirling the blueberry puree in after mixing the yogurt and lavender infusion, rather than blending all together.
  • Storage: Store frozen yogurt in a sealed container to avoid ice crystals. Press a piece of parchment paper on the surface before sealing to prevent freezer burn.

Variations to Experiment With

  • Lemon Blueberry Lavender: Boost the lemon juice to 2 teaspoons and add lemon zest for a sharper citrus tang.
  • Honey Almond Crunch: Add toasted almond slices on top just before serving for texture contrast.
  • Creamy Coconut Twist: Substitute half the Greek yogurt with coconut milk yogurt to add a tropical flair.
  • Lavender Vanilla Beans: Instead of extract, scrape a vanilla bean pod into the mixture for deeper vanilla flavor and tiny vanilla specks.
  • Berry Medley: Mix in other berries like raspberries or blackberries for more complex fruity layers.

Pairing Suggestions

This frozen yogurt pairs beautifully with light summer salads, grilled fruit, or a simple shortbread cookie on the side. It’s also a sophisticated palate cleanser between courses featuring richer dishes such as goat cheese tartlets or herb-crusted chicken.

Nutritional Benefit at a Glance

  • High in probiotics supporting gut health (from yogurt)
  • Rich in antioxidants and vitamins (from blueberries)
  • Lower in fat and calories compared to traditional ice cream
  • Natural sweeteners used rather than refined sugar

Final Thoughts

This Blueberry and Lavender Frozen Yogurt hits the sweet spot between indulgence and nutrition. Its ease of preparation, combination of fresh, wholesome ingredients, and delicate floral twist make it a standout dessert for anyone who loves vibrant, fresh flavors.

Whether enjoyed as a solo treat or shared with family and friends at a dinner party, it’s bound to impress and delight.

Try this recipe tonight and discover how surprisingly easy it is to make your own gourmet frozen yogurt at home—your taste buds will thank you!
