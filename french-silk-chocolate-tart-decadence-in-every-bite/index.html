French Silk Chocolate Tart: Decadence in Every Bite


French Silk Chocolate Tart is a rich and indulgent dessert that is sure to satisfy any sweet tooth. This decadent treat is made with a velvety smooth chocolate filling, nestled in a buttery pastry crust. The combination of textures and flavors is simply divine, making it a perfect dessert for any special occasion.

Ingredients

For the Pastry Crust:

  • 2 1/4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup confectioners’ sugar
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup cold unsalted butter, cut into small pieces
  • 1/4 cup ice-cold water

For the Chocolate Filling:

  • 1 cup (200g) high-quality dark chocolate chips (at least 70% cocoa)
  • 1/2 cup (120ml) heavy cream
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 2 large egg yolks
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Instructions

Pastry Crust

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. In a large mixing bowl, combine the flour, confectioners’ sugar, and salt.
  3. Add the cold butter and use a pastry blender or your fingertips to work it into the flour mixture until it resembles coarse crumbs.
  4. Gradually add the ice-cold water, stirring with a fork until the dough comes together in a ball.
  5. Turn the dough out onto a lightly floured surface and knead a few times until it becomes smooth and pliable.
  6. Wrap the dough in plastic wrap and refrigerate for at least 30 minutes.
  7. On a lightly floured surface, roll out the chilled dough to a thickness of about 1/8 inch (3mm).
  8. Transfer the dough to a 9-inch (23cm) tart pan with a removable bottom.
  9. Trim the edges of the dough and press it into the corners of the pan.
  10. Chill the crust in the refrigerator for 15 minutes.

Chocolate Filling

  1. Melt the chocolate chips in a double boiler or in the microwave in 30-second increments, stirring between each interval until smooth.
  2. In a separate bowl, whisk together the heavy cream, granulated sugar, and egg yolks until well combined.
  3. Add the melted chocolate to the cream mixture and whisk until smooth.
  4. Stir in the vanilla extract.
  5. Pour the chocolate filling into the chilled pastry crust.
  6. Smooth the top of the filling with a spatula.

Baking

  1. Bake the tart for 40-45 minutes, or until the edges of the crust are golden brown and the filling is set.
  2. Remove the tart from the oven and let it cool completely on a wire rack.

Tips and Variations

  • To ensure a smooth and creamy filling, make sure to not overheat the chocolate or the cream mixture.
  • If you want a more intense chocolate flavor, use a higher percentage of cocoa in your chocolate chips.
  • You can also add a sprinkle of sea salt or a dollop of whipped cream on top of the tart for added texture and flavor.

Conclusion

French Silk Chocolate Tart is a show-stopping dessert that is sure to impress your guests. With its rich and velvety chocolate filling, buttery pastry crust, and smooth texture, it’s a perfect treat for any special occasion. Whether you’re a chocolate lover or just looking for a decadent dessert to satisfy your sweet tooth, this recipe is sure to be a hit. So go ahead, indulge in the richness and decadence of French Silk Chocolate Tart, and treat yourself to a little bit of heaven.
