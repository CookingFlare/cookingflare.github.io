Instant Pot Lentil Soup Recipe: A Nutritious and Filling Option


Are you looking for a delicious and healthy meal that can be prepared in no time? Look no further than this Instant Pot Lentil Soup recipe. Packed with nutrients and bursting with flavor, this soup is the perfect option for a quick and nutritious meal.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup dried lentils
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 2 carrots, chopped
  • 2 celery stalks, diced
  • 3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 4 cups vegetable broth
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Rinse the lentils under cold water and set them aside.
  2. Turn on the Instant Pot and select the sauté function. Add a drizzle of olive oil and sauté the onion, carrots, celery, and garlic until they become tender and fragrant.
  3. Add the cumin, paprika, and bay leaf to the Instant Pot and stir well to combine the flavors.
  4. Pour in the vegetable broth and add the rinsed lentils. Give it a good stir and season with salt and pepper to taste.
  5. Secure the lid of the Instant Pot and set the pressure release valve to the sealing position. Select the manual or pressure cook function and set the timer for 15 minutes.
  6. Once the cooking time is complete, allow the pressure to release naturally for about 10 minutes. Then, carefully turn the pressure release valve to the venting position to release any remaining pressure.
  7. Open the Instant Pot lid and give the soup a stir. The lentils should be tender and the flavors well combined.
  8. Serve the soup hot and garnish with fresh herbs, such as parsley or cilantro, if desired.

Why Choose Instant Pot Lentil Soup?

Not only is this Instant Pot Lentil Soup recipe incredibly easy to make, but it also offers a host of health benefits. Lentils are a great source of plant-based protein, fiber, and essential minerals. They are low in fat and cholesterol, making them a heart-healthy option.

The combination of vegetables and spices in this soup not only adds flavor but also provides a variety of vitamins and antioxidants. The cumin and paprika add a warm and earthy taste, while the bay leaf infuses a subtle herbal note.

With the Instant Pot, you can have this delicious and nutritious soup ready in no time. The pressure cooking function ensures that the lentils become tender and the flavors meld together perfectly, all without the need for constant monitoring.

Tips and Variations

  • You can customize this Instant Pot Lentil Soup recipe by adding your favorite vegetables. Consider adding diced tomatoes, bell peppers, or even spinach for an extra nutritional boost.
  • If you prefer a creamier soup, you can blend a portion of the soup using an immersion blender or regular blender. Just be sure to cool the soup slightly before blending.
  • Leftovers can be stored in the refrigerator for up to 3 days or frozen for longer storage. Simply reheat the soup on the stovetop or in the microwave when you’re ready to enjoy it again.

Final Thoughts

This Instant Pot Lentil Soup recipe is not only quick and easy but also a fantastic option for those looking for a nutritious and filling meal. Packed with protein, fiber, and a medley of vegetables, it’s a wholesome choice that will leave you satisfied and nourished.

So next time you’re in need of a satisfying and healthy meal, give this Instant Pot Lentil Soup recipe a try. Your taste buds and your body will thank you!
