Creamy Scalloped Potatoes: A Comforting Side Dish


Creamy scalloped potatoes are a classic comfort food that never fails to impress. This rich and indulgent side dish is perfect for any occasion, whether it’s a cozy family dinner or a festive holiday feast. With layers of tender potatoes bathed in a velvety cheese sauce, this dish is sure to please even the pickiest eaters. So, let’s dive right into the recipe and learn how to make these heavenly scalloped potatoes!

Ingredients

  • 2 pounds of potatoes, preferably Russet or Yukon Gold
  • 2 cups of heavy cream
  • 1 cup of grated cheddar cheese
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons of butter
  • 2 tablespoons of all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon of dried thyme
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C). Grease a baking dish with butter or cooking spray.

  2. Peel the potatoes and slice them into thin rounds, about 1/8-inch thick. You can use a mandoline slicer or a sharp knife for this step.

  3. In a saucepan, melt the butter over medium heat. Add the chopped onion and minced garlic, and sauté until they turn translucent and fragrant.

  4. Sprinkle the flour over the butter and onion mixture, stirring constantly to create a roux. Cook for a minute or two to remove any raw flour taste.

  5. Gradually pour in the heavy cream while whisking continuously to prevent lumps from forming. Keep whisking until the sauce thickens and coats the back of a spoon.

  6. Remove the saucepan from the heat and stir in the grated cheddar cheese until it melts and incorporates into the sauce. Season with dried thyme, salt, and pepper to taste.

  7. Arrange a layer of sliced potatoes in the greased baking dish, slightly overlapping each other. Pour a portion of the cheese sauce over the potatoes, making sure to cover them evenly.

  8. Repeat the process, layering the remaining potatoes and cheese sauce until all the ingredients are used. Make sure to finish with a layer of cheese sauce on top.

  9. Cover the baking dish with aluminum foil and bake in the preheated oven for about 45 minutes. Then, remove the foil and bake for an additional 15-20 minutes, or until the potatoes are tender and the top is golden and bubbly.

  10. Once cooked, remove the scalloped potatoes from the oven and let them rest for a few minutes before serving. This will allow the sauce to thicken slightly.

  11. Serve the creamy scalloped potatoes as a delightful side dish alongside roasted meats, grilled chicken, or as a standalone vegetarian option. Enjoy the creamy, cheesy goodness!

Conclusion

Creamy scalloped potatoes are a timeless dish that brings comfort and satisfaction to any meal. With its layers of tender potatoes and velvety cheese sauce, it’s a side dish that will have everyone coming back for seconds. Whether you’re hosting a dinner party or simply craving a comforting meal, this recipe is a must-try. So, gather your ingredients, preheat your oven, and get ready to indulge in the creamy deliciousness of scalloped potatoes. Bon appétit!
