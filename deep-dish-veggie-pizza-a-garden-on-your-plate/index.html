Deep Dish Veggie Pizza: A Garden on Your Plate


Are you a pizza lover looking for a healthier, veggie-packed alternative to satisfy your cravings? Look no further than our mouthwatering Deep Dish Veggie Pizza recipe. This pizza is not only a delight for your taste buds but also a feast for your eyes, with a colorful array of fresh vegetables that will make your plate resemble a beautiful garden. So, roll up your sleeves and let’s dive into this delicious and nutritious creation!

Ingredients

For the dough:

  • 2 1/4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 package (2 1/4 teaspoons) active dry yeast
  • 1 cup warm water
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil

For the sauce:

  • 1 can (14 ounces) crushed tomatoes
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper

For the toppings:

  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1 medium red bell pepper, thinly sliced
  • 1 medium yellow bell pepper, thinly sliced
  • 1 small red onion, thinly sliced
  • 1 medium zucchini, thinly sliced
  • 1 cup sliced mushrooms
  • 1/2 cup sliced black olives
  • Fresh basil leaves, torn

Instructions

  1. Prepare the dough: In a large bowl, combine the flour, sugar, and salt. In a separate small bowl, dissolve the yeast in warm water and let it sit for about 5 minutes until it becomes foamy. Make a well in the center of the dry ingredients and pour in the yeast mixture and olive oil. Mix until a soft, slightly sticky dough forms. Transfer the dough to a floured surface and knead for about 5 minutes until smooth and elastic. Place the dough in a greased bowl, cover with a clean kitchen towel, and let it rise in a warm place for about 1 hour or until doubled in size.

  2. Prepare the sauce: In a small saucepan, combine the crushed tomatoes, minced garlic, dried oregano, salt, and black pepper. Simmer over low heat for about 10 minutes, stirring occasionally. Remove from heat and set aside.

  3. Preheat the oven: Preheat your oven to 425°F (220°C).

  4. Assemble the pizza: Grease a deep-dish pizza pan or a 9x13-inch baking dish. Punch down the risen dough and transfer it to the prepared pan. Press the dough evenly into the pan, making sure to cover the bottom and sides. Spread the tomato sauce evenly over the dough. Sprinkle the shredded mozzarella cheese over the sauce, followed by the sliced bell peppers, red onion, zucchini, mushrooms, and black olives.

  5. Bake and enjoy: Place the pizza in the preheated oven and bake for 25-30 minutes, or until the crust is golden brown and the cheese is bubbly and melted. Remove from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes. Garnish with torn fresh basil leaves before serving.

Tips and Variations

  • Feel free to experiment with different vegetable toppings based on your personal preferences. Some delicious additions could include cherry tomatoes, spinach, artichoke hearts, or even roasted eggplant.
  • For an extra burst of flavor, sprinkle some grated Parmesan cheese or crumbled feta cheese on top of the pizza before baking.
  • If you prefer a thinner crust, you can divide the dough in half and make two medium-sized pizzas instead of one deep dish pizza.
  • This pizza is perfect for sharing with friends and family. Serve it as a main course for a vegetarian dinner or cut it into smaller squares for a delightful appetizer at your next gathering.

Now that you have this incredible Deep Dish Veggie Pizza recipe, you can bring a taste of the garden to your plate. With its vibrant colors, fresh flavors, and wholesome ingredients, this pizza will make you fall in love with healthy eating. So, gather your ingredients, put on your apron, and get ready to embark on a culinary adventure that will leave you craving for more!
