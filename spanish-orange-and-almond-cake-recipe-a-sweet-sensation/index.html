Spanish Orange and Almond Cake Recipe: A Sweet Sensation


If you’re looking for a delightful dessert that will transport your taste buds to the sunny streets of Spain, look no further than the Spanish Orange and Almond Cake. This traditional Spanish delicacy is a sweet sensation that combines the vibrant flavors of oranges and the nutty goodness of almonds.

Ingredients

To create this mouthwatering cake, you will need the following ingredients:

  • 3 large oranges
  • 6 eggs
  • 250 grams of ground almonds
  • 250 grams of granulated sugar
  • 1 teaspoon of baking powder
  • A pinch of salt

Instructions

  1. Begin by preheating your oven to 180°C (350°F). Grease a round cake tin and line the bottom with parchment paper.

  2. Wash the oranges thoroughly and place them in a saucepan. Cover them with cold water and bring to a boil. Allow them to simmer for about 2 hours until they become tender. Drain the oranges and set them aside to cool.

  3. Once the oranges have cooled, cut them into quarters and remove any seeds. Place the oranges, including the peel, into a blender or food processor and blend until smooth.

  4. In a large mixing bowl, beat the eggs and sugar together until well combined. Add in the almond flour, baking powder, and a pinch of salt. Mix until everything is evenly incorporated.

  5. Now, it’s time to add the pureed oranges to the mixture. Gently fold them into the batter until fully combined.

  6. Pour the batter into the prepared cake tin and smooth the top with a spatula. Place the tin in the preheated oven and bake for approximately 50-60 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.

  7. Once the cake is baked, remove it from the oven and let it cool in the tin for about 10 minutes. Then, transfer it to a wire rack to cool completely.

  8. When the cake has cooled, you can dust it with powdered sugar for an extra touch of sweetness. Slice and serve this delectable Spanish Orange and Almond Cake with a dollop of whipped cream or a scoop of vanilla ice cream.

Why You’ll Love It

This Spanish Orange and Almond Cake is a true delight for the senses. The combination of the citrusy aroma from the oranges and the nutty undertones from the almonds creates a flavor profile that is both refreshing and comforting. The moist and tender texture of the cake adds to its appeal, making it a perfect treat for any occasion.

Moreover, this cake is gluten-free, making it suitable for those with dietary restrictions. The use of fresh oranges not only infuses the cake with a burst of citrus flavor but also provides a natural sweetness that is both vibrant and satisfying.

Whether you’re hosting a dinner party or simply treating yourself to a special dessert, the Spanish Orange and Almond Cake is a recipe that is sure to impress. So, go ahead and indulge in this sweet sensation that will transport you to the beautiful streets of Spain with every bite.

Enjoy!
