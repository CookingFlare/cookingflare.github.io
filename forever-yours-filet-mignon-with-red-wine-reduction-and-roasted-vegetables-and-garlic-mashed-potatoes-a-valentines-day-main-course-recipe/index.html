Forever Yours Filet Mignon with Red Wine Reduction and Roasted Vegetables and Garlic Mashed Potatoes: A Valentine's Day Main Course Recipe


Forever Yours Filet Mignon with Red Wine Reduction and Roasted Vegetables and Garlic Mashed Potatoes: A Valentine’s Day Main Course Recipe

Valentine’s Day is the perfect occasion to create a special meal for your loved one, and what better way to show your affection than with a delicious homemade dinner? This recipe for Forever Yours Filet Mignon with Red Wine Reduction and Roasted Vegetables and Garlic Mashed Potatoes is sure to impress and delight your significant other. The combination of tender filet mignon, rich red wine reduction, flavorful roasted vegetables, and creamy garlic mashed potatoes creates a luxurious and indulgent main course that is perfect for celebrating love.

Ingredients

For the Filet Mignon:

  • 2 filet mignon steaks
  • Salt and black pepper
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons unsalted butter
  • 2 sprigs of fresh thyme
  • 2 cloves of garlic, crushed

For the Red Wine Reduction:

  • 1 cup red wine
  • 1/2 cup beef broth
  • 1 tablespoon balsamic vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon unsalted butter
  • Salt and black pepper

For the Roasted Vegetables:

  • 1 red bell pepper, cut into chunks
  • 1 yellow bell pepper, cut into chunks
  • 1 zucchini, sliced
  • 1 red onion, cut into wedges
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • Salt and black pepper
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme

For the Garlic Mashed Potatoes:

  • 4 large potatoes, peeled and quartered
  • 4 cloves of garlic
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • 4 tablespoons unsalted butter
  • Salt and black pepper

Instructions

Filet Mignon:

  1. Preheat the oven to 400°F (200°C).
  2. Season the filet mignon steaks generously with salt and black pepper on both sides.
  3. Heat the olive oil in a large ovenproof skillet over medium-high heat.
  4. Add the steaks to the skillet and sear for 2-3 minutes on each side until golden brown.
  5. Add the butter, thyme, and crushed garlic to the skillet.
  6. Transfer the skillet to the preheated oven and roast for 5-7 minutes for medium-rare, or until desired doneness is reached.
  7. Remove the steaks from the skillet and let them rest for a few minutes before serving.

Red Wine Reduction:

  1. In a saucepan, bring the red wine to a simmer over medium heat.
  2. Add the beef broth and balsamic vinegar, and continue to simmer until the liquid is reduced by half.
  3. Remove the saucepan from the heat and whisk in the butter until the sauce is smooth.
  4. Season with salt and black pepper to taste.

Roasted Vegetables:

  1. In a large bowl, toss the red bell pepper, yellow bell pepper, zucchini, and red onion with olive oil, minced garlic, salt, black pepper, and dried thyme.
  2. Spread the vegetables in a single layer on a baking sheet.
  3. Roast in the preheated oven for 20-25 minutes, or until the vegetables are tender and caramelized.

Garlic Mashed Potatoes:

  1. Place the potatoes and garlic cloves in a large pot and cover with cold water.
  2. Bring to a boil, then reduce the heat and simmer for 15-20 minutes, or until the potatoes are tender.
  3. Drain the potatoes and garlic, then return them to the pot.
  4. Add the heavy cream and butter to the pot.
  5. Mash the potatoes and garlic until smooth and creamy. Season with salt and black pepper to taste.

Serving

To serve, place a filet mignon steak on each plate and drizzle with the red wine reduction. Serve the roasted vegetables and garlic mashed potatoes on the side. Pair this exquisite dish with a bottle of your favorite red wine for a truly memorable Valentine’s Day dinner.

This Forever Yours Filet Mignon with Red Wine Reduction and Roasted Vegetables and Garlic Mashed Potatoes recipe is a labor of love that will surely impress your loved one and make them feel cherished. The combination of succulent steak, flavorful red wine reduction, vibrant roasted vegetables, and creamy mashed potatoes creates a symphony of flavors and textures that is perfect for celebrating love and romance. So, this Valentine’s Day, treat your significant other to a homemade meal that speaks volumes of your affection.

Embrace the joy of cooking and the art of creating memorable moments with the one you love. Happy Valentine’s Day!
