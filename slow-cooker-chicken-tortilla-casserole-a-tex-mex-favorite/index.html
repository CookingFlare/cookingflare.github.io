Slow Cooker Chicken Tortilla Casserole: A Tex-Mex Favorite


If you’re a fan of Tex-Mex cuisine and enjoy the convenience of slow cooker meals, then you’re in for a treat with this delicious and easy Slow Cooker Chicken Tortilla Casserole recipe. Packed with flavor, this comforting casserole is perfect for family dinners, potlucks, or casual gatherings with friends. The best part? You can simply set it and forget it, allowing the slow cooker to work its magic while you go about your day. Let’s dive into the details of this mouthwatering Tex-Mex dish.

Ingredients

  • 2 lbs boneless, skinless chicken breasts, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 1 red bell pepper, diced
  • 1 can (15 oz) black beans, drained and rinsed
  • 1 can (15 oz) corn kernels, drained
  • 1 can (10 oz) diced tomatoes with green chilies, undrained
  • 1 cup chicken broth
  • 1 teaspoon chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 6-8 corn tortillas, cut into quarters
  • 2 cups shredded Mexican blend cheese
  • Fresh cilantro, chopped (for garnish)
  • Sour cream (for serving)
  • Sliced jalapeños (optional, for extra heat)

Instructions

  1. In a large bowl, combine the diced chicken, onion, red bell pepper, black beans, corn, diced tomatoes with green chilies, chicken broth, chili powder, cumin, paprika, garlic powder, salt, and pepper. Mix well to ensure the seasoning is evenly distributed.

  2. Lightly grease the slow cooker insert with cooking spray. Place a layer of the chicken mixture at the bottom of the slow cooker.

  3. Arrange a layer of the quartered corn tortillas over the chicken mixture, followed by a layer of shredded cheese. Repeat the layers until all the ingredients are used, finishing with a layer of shredded cheese on top.

  4. Cover the slow cooker and cook on low for 6-7 hours or on high for 3-4 hours, or until the chicken is cooked through and the casserole is bubbly around the edges.

  5. Once the casserole is cooked, carefully remove the slow cooker insert and let it sit for a few minutes to cool slightly. Sprinkle the chopped cilantro over the top for a burst of freshness.

  6. Serve the Slow Cooker Chicken Tortilla Casserole hot, garnished with a dollop of sour cream and sliced jalapeños for those who like it spicy.

Tips and Variations

  • To add a smoky flavor, consider using fire-roasted diced tomatoes with green chilies.
  • For a vegetarian version, swap the chicken for extra black beans and add more veggies like zucchini or mushrooms.
  • Experiment with different types of cheese such as pepper jack or cheddar for a unique flavor profile.
  • Leftovers can be stored in an airtight container in the refrigerator for up to 3 days. Simply reheat in the microwave or oven until heated through.

Final Thoughts

The Slow Cooker Chicken Tortilla Casserole is a versatile and crowd-pleasing dish that captures the essence of Tex-Mex cuisine. Its layers of tender chicken, hearty beans, sweet corn, and zesty spices are a celebration of vibrant flavors. Whether you’re a seasoned cook or a kitchen novice, this recipe is a fantastic introduction to the world of slow cooker casseroles. So, gather your ingredients, set up your slow cooker, and get ready to savor the irresistible aroma of this Tex-Mex favorite as it simmers to perfection. Enjoy!
