Homemade Blackberry Lemon Bars: A Burst of Citrus and Berry Flavor


If you’re looking for a delightful dessert that combines the zing of citrus with the sweetness of berries, look no further than these homemade blackberry lemon bars. This recipe is a perfect balance of tart and sweet, with a buttery crust that complements the luscious blackberry lemon filling. Whether you’re hosting a summer gathering, craving a refreshing treat, or simply want to indulge in a burst of flavor, these blackberry lemon bars are sure to impress.

Ingredients

For the Crust:

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1/4 cup powdered sugar
  • A pinch of salt

For the Blackberry Lemon Filling:

  • 1 1/2 cups fresh blackberries
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 3 large eggs
  • 1/3 cup fresh lemon juice
  • Zest of 1 lemon
  • 1/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking powder

For the Topping:

  • Powdered sugar for dusting

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Crust:

    • Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease an 8x8-inch baking pan.
    • In a mixing bowl, cream together the softened butter and powdered sugar until light and fluffy.
    • Gradually add the flour and salt, mixing until a dough forms.
    • Press the dough into the bottom of the prepared baking pan, creating an even layer.
    • Bake the crust for 15-18 minutes, or until lightly golden. Remove from the oven and set aside.

  2. Make the Blackberry Lemon Filling:

    • In a blender or food processor, puree the fresh blackberries until smooth. Strain the puree through a fine-mesh sieve to remove the seeds, yielding about 3/4 cup of blackberry puree.
    • In a separate bowl, whisk together the blackberry puree, granulated sugar, eggs, lemon juice, lemon zest, flour, and baking powder until well combined.
    • Pour the blackberry lemon mixture over the baked crust, spreading it out evenly.

  3. Bake and Chill:

    • Return the pan to the oven and bake for 25-30 minutes, or until the filling is set and the edges are lightly golden.
    • Once baked, remove the pan from the oven and allow the blackberry lemon bars to cool to room temperature.
    • Refrigerate the bars for at least 2 hours to chill and set.

  4. Serve and Enjoy:

    • Once chilled, dust the top of the bars with powdered sugar.
    • Cut the blackberry lemon bars into squares and serve.

These homemade blackberry lemon bars are a delightful combination of tangy lemon and sweet blackberries, making them a perfect summer dessert or a treat for any time of the year. The buttery crust provides a delicious contrast to the fruity filling, creating a dessert that is as visually appealing as it is delicious. Whether you’re an experienced baker or new to the kitchen, this recipe is straightforward and rewarding, allowing you to savor the flavors of fresh blackberries and zesty lemon in every bite.

So, why not treat yourself and your loved ones to a taste of summer with these delightful homemade blackberry lemon bars? Whip up a batch, share them with friends, or simply enjoy them as a special indulgence. Let the vibrant colors and flavors of blackberry and lemon brighten your day with every bite of these delectable bars.
