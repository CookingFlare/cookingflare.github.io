Hearty and Delicious Beef and Barley Stew Recipes


If you’re craving a warm and comforting meal that will satisfy your hunger and warm your soul, look no further than beef and barley stew. This hearty dish is packed with tender beef, wholesome barley, and a medley of vegetables that come together to create a flavor explosion in every bite. Today, I’m sharing not just one, but two delicious beef and barley stew recipes that are sure to become staples in your kitchen.

Classic Beef and Barley Stew

Ingredients:

  • 1.5 pounds beef stew meat, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 1 cup pearl barley
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 3 carrots, peeled and sliced
  • 3 celery stalks, sliced
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 4 cups beef broth
  • 2 cups water
  • 2 bay leaves
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley, chopped (for garnish)

Instructions:

  1. In a large pot or Dutch oven, heat some oil over medium heat. Add the beef stew meat and brown it on all sides. This step adds depth of flavor to the stew.
  2. Once the beef is browned, remove it from the pot and set it aside. In the same pot, add the diced onion, sliced carrots, sliced celery, and minced garlic. Sauté until the vegetables are tender and fragrant.
  3. Return the beef to the pot and add the pearl barley, beef broth, water, bay leaves, dried thyme, salt, and pepper. Give everything a good stir to combine.
  4. Bring the stew to a boil, then reduce the heat to low and cover the pot. Let it simmer for about 1.5 to 2 hours, or until the beef is tender and the barley is cooked through. Stir occasionally to prevent sticking.
  5. Once the stew is done, remove the bay leaves and discard them. Taste and adjust the seasoning if needed.
  6. Serve the classic beef and barley stew hot, garnished with chopped parsley for a pop of freshness.

Spicy Mexican Beef and Barley Stew

Ingredients:

  • 1.5 pounds beef chuck roast, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 1 cup pearl barley
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 3 bell peppers (red, yellow, and green), seeded and sliced
  • 2 jalapeños, seeded and finely chopped
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can (14 ounces) diced tomatoes
  • 4 cups beef broth
  • 2 cups water
  • 2 teaspoons chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon smoked paprika
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh cilantro, chopped (for garnish)

Instructions:

  1. In a large pot or Dutch oven, heat some oil over medium heat. Add the beef chuck roast and brown it on all sides. Remove the beef from the pot and set it aside.
  2. In the same pot, add the diced onion, sliced bell peppers, chopped jalapeños, and minced garlic. Sauté until the vegetables are tender.
  3. Return the beef to the pot and add the pearl barley, diced tomatoes, beef broth, water, chili powder, ground cumin, smoked paprika, salt, and pepper. Give everything a good stir to combine.
  4. Bring the stew to a boil, then reduce the heat to low and cover the pot. Let it simmer for about 2 to 2.5 hours, or until the beef is tender and the barley is cooked through. Stir occasionally to prevent sticking.
  5. Once the stew is done, taste and adjust the seasoning if needed.
  6. Serve the spicy Mexican beef and barley stew hot, garnished with chopped cilantro for a burst of freshness and vibrant flavors.

Conclusion

Beef and barley stew is the perfect dish to enjoy on a chilly day or whenever you’re in need of some comfort food. Whether you choose the classic version or the spicy Mexican twist, both recipes are guaranteed to warm your heart and leave you feeling satisfied. So go ahead, gather your ingredients, and get ready to savor the rich flavors and textures of these hearty and delicious beef and barley stew recipes.
