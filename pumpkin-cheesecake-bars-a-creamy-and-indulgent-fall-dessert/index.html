Pumpkin Cheesecake Bars: A Creamy and Indulgent Fall Dessert


As the leaves start to turn and the air becomes crisp, it’s time to embrace the flavors of fall. What better way to do that than with a creamy and indulgent dessert that captures the essence of the season? These Pumpkin Cheesecake Bars are the perfect treat for cozy autumn evenings, combining the rich, spiced flavor of pumpkin with the luscious creaminess of cheesecake. Whether you’re hosting a gathering or simply craving a decadent sweet treat, these bars are sure to delight your taste buds and impress your guests.

Ingredients

For the Crust:

  • 1 1/2 cups graham cracker crumbs
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, melted

For the Cheesecake Filling:

  • 16 oz cream cheese, softened
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 cup canned pumpkin puree
  • 3 large eggs
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 1/2 tsp pumpkin pie spice
  • 1/4 tsp salt

For the Topping:

  • 1 cup sour cream
  • 2 tbsp granulated sugar
  • 1/2 tsp vanilla extract

Instructions

  1. Preheat the Oven: Preheat your oven to 325°F (160°C). Line a 9x13-inch baking pan with parchment paper, leaving an overhang on the sides for easy removal.

  2. Prepare the Crust: In a medium bowl, combine the graham cracker crumbs, sugar, and melted butter. Press the mixture firmly into the bottom of the prepared pan. Bake for 10 minutes, then remove from the oven and set aside.

  3. Make the Cheesecake Filling: In a large bowl, beat the softened cream cheese and sugar until smooth and creamy. Add the pumpkin puree, eggs, vanilla extract, pumpkin pie spice, and salt. Beat until well combined and smooth.

  4. Bake the Bars: Pour the cheesecake filling over the pre-baked crust, spreading it evenly. Place the pan in the oven and bake for 35-40 minutes, or until the edges are set and the center is slightly jiggly.

  5. Prepare the Topping: In a small bowl, whisk together the sour cream, sugar, and vanilla extract. Carefully spread the mixture over the hot cheesecake layer and return the pan to the oven for an additional 5 minutes.

  6. Chill and Serve: Allow the Pumpkin Cheesecake Bars to cool at room temperature for about 1 hour, then refrigerate for at least 3 hours or until completely chilled and set. Once chilled, use the parchment overhang to lift the bars out of the pan and onto a cutting board. Cut into squares and serve.

Tips and Variations

  • For an extra indulgent touch, drizzle the bars with caramel sauce before serving.
  • Sprinkle a dash of cinnamon or nutmeg on top for a festive finish.
  • Serve with a dollop of freshly whipped cream and a sprinkle of crushed graham crackers for added texture.

These Pumpkin Cheesecake Bars are a delightful marriage of creamy, spiced pumpkin and velvety cheesecake, making them an irresistible dessert for any fall occasion. Whether you’re a seasoned baker or a novice in the kitchen, these bars are a fantastic way to celebrate the flavors of the season and treat yourself to a little slice of autumnal bliss. Enjoy the warmth and comfort of fall with every heavenly bite of these delectable bars!
