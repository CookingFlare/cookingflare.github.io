Savory and Smoky: BBQ Bacon-Wrapped Meatloaf


If you’re a fan of comfort food with a smoky twist, then this BBQ bacon-wrapped meatloaf recipe is a must-try. It’s a savory and satisfying dish that combines the classic flavors of meatloaf with the irresistible smokiness of barbecue and the indulgent richness of bacon. Whether you’re hosting a backyard barbecue, a family dinner, or simply craving a hearty meal, this recipe is sure to impress and satisfy. Let’s dive into the delicious details of creating this mouthwatering dish.

Ingredients

For the Meatloaf:

  • 1 1/2 pounds ground beef
  • 1/2 cup breadcrumbs
  • 1/4 cup milk
  • 1/2 cup diced onion
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/4 cup ketchup
  • 2 tablespoons Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 teaspoon Dijon mustard
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1/2 teaspoon smoked paprika
  • 1/4 teaspoon cayenne pepper
  • 2 eggs

For the BBQ Glaze:

  • 1/2 cup barbecue sauce
  • 2 tablespoons brown sugar
  • 1 tablespoon apple cider vinegar

For the Wrap:

  • 8-10 slices bacon

Instructions

  1. Preheat the Oven: Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).

  2. Prepare the Meatloaf Mixture: In a large bowl, combine the ground beef, breadcrumbs, milk, diced onion, minced garlic, ketchup, Worcestershire sauce, Dijon mustard, salt, black pepper, smoked paprika, cayenne pepper, and eggs. Mix the ingredients until well combined.

  3. Shape the Meatloaf: On a baking sheet or a loaf pan, shape the meatloaf mixture into a loaf shape.

  4. Wrap with Bacon: Carefully drape the bacon slices over the shaped meatloaf, tucking the ends underneath the loaf.

  5. Prepare the BBQ Glaze: In a small bowl, mix the barbecue sauce, brown sugar, and apple cider vinegar. Brush the glaze over the bacon-wrapped meatloaf, ensuring it’s evenly coated.

  6. Bake the Meatloaf: Place the meatloaf in the preheated oven and bake for 45-55 minutes, or until the internal temperature reaches 160°F (71°C) and the bacon is crispy.

  7. Rest and Serve: Once cooked, allow the meatloaf to rest for a few minutes before slicing. Serve it with additional barbecue sauce on the side for dipping.

Tips and Variations

  • For a smokier flavor, you can add a touch of liquid smoke to the meatloaf mixture.
  • Consider adding a layer of sliced cheese inside the meatloaf for a gooey surprise in every bite.
  • If you prefer a spicy kick, mix some hot sauce into the barbecue glaze for an extra zing.

Why You’ll Love This Recipe

This BBQ bacon-wrapped meatloaf is a delightful combination of rich, savory, and smoky flavors that will leave your taste buds singing. The tender, juicy meatloaf is complemented by the crispy, caramelized bacon and the sweet tang of the barbecue glaze. It’s a versatile dish that can be the centerpiece of a family dinner or a star attraction at a social gathering. Plus, the aroma of this meatloaf as it bakes will have everyone eagerly anticipating the moment it comes out of the oven.

So, whether you’re a meatloaf enthusiast or simply looking to elevate your comfort food game, give this recipe a try and savor the delicious fusion of barbecue and bacon-wrapped goodness.

Enjoy creating and savoring this delectable BBQ bacon-wrapped meatloaf!
