30-Minute Lemon Garlic Shrimp Skewers Recipe


If you’re looking for a quick and delicious seafood dish that’s bursting with flavor, look no further than these 30-Minute Lemon Garlic Shrimp Skewers. This recipe is perfect for those busy weeknights when you want to whip up something tasty without spending hours in the kitchen.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound of large shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons of fresh lemon juice
  • 2 tablespoons of olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon of lemon zest
  • 1/2 teaspoon of salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon of black pepper
  • 1/4 teaspoon of red pepper flakes (optional)
  • Wooden skewers, soaked in water for at least 30 minutes

Instructions

  1. In a small bowl, combine the minced garlic, lemon juice, olive oil, lemon zest, salt, black pepper, and red pepper flakes. Stir well to combine all the ingredients and create a flavorful marinade.

  2. Place the peeled and deveined shrimp in a shallow dish or a resealable plastic bag. Pour the marinade over the shrimp, making sure they are well-coated. Allow the shrimp to marinate for at least 15 minutes, but no more than 30 minutes. This will allow the flavors to infuse into the shrimp without overpowering them.

  3. While the shrimp are marinating, preheat your grill or grill pan to medium-high heat. If using wooden skewers, make sure to soak them in water for at least 30 minutes to prevent them from burning on the grill.

  4. Thread the marinated shrimp onto the skewers, making sure to leave a little space between each piece. This will ensure even cooking and prevent the shrimp from sticking together.

  5. Place the skewers on the preheated grill and cook for approximately 2-3 minutes per side, or until the shrimp are pink and opaque. Be careful not to overcook the shrimp, as they can become tough and rubbery.

  6. Once cooked, remove the skewers from the grill and let them rest for a few minutes. This will allow the juices to redistribute, resulting in tender and juicy shrimp.

  7. Serve the Lemon Garlic Shrimp Skewers hot with a squeeze of fresh lemon juice, if desired. They pair perfectly with a side of rice or a crisp salad for a complete and satisfying meal.

Tips and Variations

  • For additional flavor, you can add some fresh herbs like chopped parsley or cilantro to the marinade.
  • If you prefer a spicier kick, increase the amount of red pepper flakes or add a dash of hot sauce to the marinade.
  • If you don’t have a grill or grill pan, you can also cook the shrimp skewers in a hot skillet or under the broiler in your oven. Just make sure to adjust the cooking time accordingly.

These 30-Minute Lemon Garlic Shrimp Skewers are a fantastic option for seafood lovers who crave a quick and flavorful meal. The tangy lemon and garlic marinade perfectly complements the sweetness of the shrimp, creating a dish that is both refreshing and satisfying. With just a few simple ingredients and minimal prep time, you can enjoy restaurant-quality shrimp skewers right in the comfort of your own home. So fire up the grill and get ready to impress your family and friends with this delicious recipe!
