Vegan Pumpkin Desserts: Autumn-Inspired Recipes


As the crisp breeze of autumn rolls in and the leaves transform into a vibrant tapestry of colors, it is the perfect time to indulge in the warm, comforting flavors of pumpkin. This versatile ingredient is not only a staple in many traditional fall recipes but also shines brightly in vegan desserts, making them a delightful treat for everyone to enjoy. In this blog post, we will explore a variety of vegan pumpkin dessert recipes that capture the essence of autumn while ensuring that they are plant-based and delicious. From pies to cakes and everything in between, get ready to dive into the world of vegan pumpkin desserts!

The Allure of Pumpkin

Pumpkin is more than just a seasonal flavor; it is packed with nutrients, including vitamin A, fiber, and antioxidants. Its natural sweetness and creamy texture make it a fantastic substitute for dairy and eggs in desserts. Whether you’re a seasoned vegan or simply looking to incorporate more plant-based meals into your diet, these recipes will satisfy your autumn cravings while keeping things healthy and delicious.

Classic Vegan Pumpkin Pie

Ingredients

  • 1 ½ cups pumpkin puree (canned or homemade)
  • 1 cup coconut milk (full-fat for creaminess)
  • ¾ cup brown sugar
  • ½ cup maple syrup
  • 3 tablespoons cornstarch
  • 2 teaspoons pumpkin pie spice
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 prepared vegan pie crust (store-bought or homemade)

Instructions

  1. Preheat the Oven: Begin by preheating your oven to 350°F (175°C).

  2. Mix the Filling: In a large mixing bowl, combine the pumpkin puree, coconut milk, brown sugar, maple syrup, cornstarch, pumpkin pie spice, and vanilla extract. Whisk until smooth and well combined.

  3. Prepare the Crust: Place your prepared vegan pie crust into a 9-inch pie dish. If using a homemade crust, ensure it is chilled for at least 30 minutes before baking.

  4. Pour the Filling: Pour the pumpkin filling into the prepared crust, spreading it evenly.

  5. Bake: Bake for 45-50 minutes, or until the filling is set and a toothpick inserted in the center comes out clean.

  6. Cool and Serve: Allow the pie to cool at room temperature for at least one hour before slicing. Serve with a dollop of coconut whipped cream for an extra treat.

Pumpkin Spice Muffins

Ingredients

  • 1 ½ cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup pumpkin puree
  • ½ cup coconut sugar
  • ½ cup almond milk
  • ¼ cup coconut oil, melted
  • 1 tablespoon apple cider vinegar
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1 teaspoon pumpkin pie spice
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • Optional: ½ cup chopped walnuts or chocolate chips

Instructions

  1. Preheat the Oven: Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C) and line a muffin tin with paper liners.

  2. Combine Wet Ingredients: In a large bowl, whisk together the pumpkin puree, almond milk, melted coconut oil, and apple cider vinegar.

  3. Mix Dry Ingredients: In another bowl, mix the flour, coconut sugar, baking powder, baking soda, pumpkin pie spice, and salt.

  4. Combine: Gradually add the dry ingredients to the wet ingredients, stirring until just combined. If desired, fold in the walnuts or chocolate chips.

  5. Bake: Spoon the batter into the muffin tin, filling each cup about ¾ full. Bake for 20-25 minutes or until a toothpick inserted comes out clean.

  6. Cool and Enjoy: Allow the muffins to cool in the tin for 5 minutes before transferring them to a wire rack to cool completely.

Vegan Pumpkin Cheesecake

Ingredients

  • For the crust:

    • 1 ½ cups graham cracker crumbs (or nut-based alternative for gluten-free)
    • ½ cup coconut oil, melted
    • ¼ cup maple syrup

  • For the filling:

    • 2 cups soaked cashews (soaked in water for at least 4 hours)
    • 1 cup pumpkin puree
    • ½ cup maple syrup
    • ½ cup coconut milk
    • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
    • 1 teaspoon pumpkin pie spice
    • 1 tablespoon lemon juice

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Crust: In a bowl, combine the graham cracker crumbs, melted coconut oil, and maple syrup. Press the mixture into the bottom of a springform pan to form the crust. Refrigerate while preparing the filling.

  2. Blend the Filling: Drain and rinse the soaked cashews, then place them in a high-speed blender or food processor. Add the pumpkin puree, maple syrup, coconut milk, vanilla extract, pumpkin pie spice, and lemon juice. Blend until smooth and creamy.

  3. Assemble: Pour the filling over the prepared crust and smooth the top with a spatula.

  4. Chill: Refrigerate the cheesecake for at least 4 hours or until set.

  5. Serve: Once set, carefully remove the cheesecake from the springform pan. Slice and enjoy as a decadent dessert.

Pumpkin Chocolate Chip Cookies

Ingredients

  • 1 cup pumpkin puree
  • 1 cup almond flour
  • ½ cup oats
  • ½ cup coconut sugar
  • ½ cup coconut oil, melted
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • ½ teaspoon baking soda
  • ½ teaspoon pumpkin pie spice
  • ½ cup dairy-free chocolate chips

Instructions

  1. Preheat the Oven: Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C) and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.

  2. Mix Wet Ingredients: In a bowl, combine the pumpkin puree, melted coconut oil, and vanilla extract.

  3. Combine Dry Ingredients: In another bowl, whisk together the almond flour, oats, coconut sugar, baking powder, baking soda, and pumpkin pie spice.

  4. Combine the Mixtures: Gradually add the dry mixture to the wet mixture, stirring until just combined. Fold in the chocolate chips.

  5. Scoop and Bake: Using a cookie scoop, drop spoonfuls of dough onto the prepared baking sheet. Bake for 15-18 minutes or until the edges are lightly golden.

  6. Cool and Enjoy: Allow the cookies to cool on the baking sheet for a few minutes before transferring them to a wire rack to cool completely.

Pumpkin Bread Pudding

Ingredients

  • 4 cups cubed bread (preferably stale)
  • 1 cup pumpkin puree
  • 1 cup almond milk
  • ½ cup maple syrup
  • ½ cup coconut sugar
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 1 teaspoon pumpkin pie spice
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • Optional: nuts or raisins for added texture

Instructions

  1. Preheat the Oven: Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C) and grease a baking dish.

  2. Combine the Base: In a large bowl, whisk together the pumpkin puree, almond milk, maple syrup, coconut sugar, vanilla extract, pumpkin pie spice, and salt.

  3. Mix with Bread: Add the cubed bread to the mixture, ensuring all pieces are well-coated. Let it sit for about 10 minutes to absorb the liquid.

  4. Add Extras: If desired, fold in nuts or raisins for added texture.

  5. Bake: Pour the mixture into the prepared baking dish and bake for 35-40 minutes, or until the top is golden and the center is set.

  6. Serve Warm: Serve warm, optionally topped with a drizzle of coconut cream or a scoop of vegan ice cream.

Conclusion

As the leaves change and the air turns crisp, these vegan pumpkin desserts provide the perfect way to embrace the flavors of autumn. From classic pies to innovative puddings, these recipes celebrate the rich and comforting taste of pumpkin while being kind to the planet. Whether you’re hosting a fall gathering or simply indulging in a cozy night in, these desserts are sure to impress friends and family alike. So grab your pumpkin puree, preheat the oven, and let the baking begin! Enjoy the warmth and joy that these vegan pumpkin desserts bring to your autumn celebrations.
