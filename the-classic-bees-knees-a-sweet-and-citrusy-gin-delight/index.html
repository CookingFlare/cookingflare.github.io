The Classic Bee's Knees: A Sweet and Citrusy Gin Delight


If you’re a fan of classic cocktails with a refreshing twist, then the Bee’s Knees is the perfect drink for you. This delightful concoction combines the bold flavors of gin with the sweetness of honey and the tangy brightness of fresh lemon juice. It’s a timeless cocktail that has stood the test of time and continues to be a favorite among mixologists and cocktail enthusiasts.

The History of the Bee’s Knees

The Bee’s Knees has a fascinating history that dates back to the Prohibition era in the United States. During this time, the illegal production and consumption of alcohol were rampant, leading to the creation of cocktails that masked the harsh taste of bootlegged spirits. The Bee’s Knees was born out of the necessity to make the rough gin more palatable by combining it with honey and lemon, resulting in a smooth and flavorful drink that quickly gained popularity.

Ingredients

To make the perfect Bee’s Knees, you’ll need the following ingredients:

  • 2 oz. gin
  • 3/4 oz. fresh lemon juice
  • 3/4 oz. honey syrup (made by mixing equal parts honey and hot water)

Instructions

  1. Start by preparing your honey syrup. In a small bowl, mix equal parts honey and hot water until the honey is fully dissolved. Set it aside to cool.

  2. Once the honey syrup is ready, fill a cocktail shaker with ice and add the gin, fresh lemon juice, and honey syrup.

  3. Shake the ingredients vigorously for about 15 seconds to ensure they are well combined and chilled.

  4. Strain the mixture into a chilled coupe glass or a martini glass to remove the ice and any citrus pulp, leaving you with a smooth and elegant cocktail.

  5. If desired, garnish the cocktail with a twist of lemon or a lemon wheel for a decorative and citrusy touch.

  6. Serve and enjoy your refreshing and sophisticated Bee’s Knees cocktail!

Tips for a Perfect Bee’s Knees

  • Use a high-quality gin with botanical flavors that complement the honey and lemon. Avoid overly juniper-heavy gins that may overpower the other ingredients.
  • Freshly squeezed lemon juice is essential for achieving the bright, zesty flavor that defines the Bee’s Knees. Avoid using bottled lemon juice for the best results.
  • Adjust the sweetness of the cocktail to your preference by varying the amount of honey syrup. Some prefer a slightly sweeter drink, while others enjoy a more tart and citrus-forward profile.

The Perfect Occasions for a Bee’s Knees

The Bee’s Knees is a versatile cocktail that suits various occasions. Whether you’re hosting a sophisticated dinner party, enjoying a casual evening with friends, or simply unwinding after a long day, the Bee’s Knees is an ideal choice. Its balanced flavors and timeless appeal make it a go-to option for those who appreciate a well-crafted cocktail.

In Conclusion

The Bee’s Knees is a classic cocktail that continues to captivate cocktail enthusiasts with its harmonious blend of sweet, citrusy, and botanical flavors. With its rich history and timeless appeal, it’s no wonder that this delightful drink has stood the test of time and remains a beloved choice for those seeking a refreshing and elegant libation. So, the next time you’re in the mood for a sophisticated yet approachable cocktail, mix up a Bee’s Knees and savor its delightful charm. Cheers!
