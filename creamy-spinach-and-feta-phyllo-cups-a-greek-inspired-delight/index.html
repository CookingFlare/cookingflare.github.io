Creamy Spinach and Feta Phyllo Cups: A Greek-inspired Delight


If you’re a fan of Greek cuisine and looking for a delightful appetizer or snack, then you’re in for a treat! These creamy spinach and feta phyllo cups are the perfect combination of flavors and textures, making them a crowd-pleaser at any gathering.

Ingredients

To make these delicious phyllo cups, you’ll need the following ingredients:

  • 1 package of phyllo dough
  • 2 cups of fresh spinach, chopped
  • 1 cup of feta cheese, crumbled
  • 1/2 cup of cream cheese
  • 1/4 cup of grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/4 cup of chopped fresh dill
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1/4 teaspoon of salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon of black pepper
  • Olive oil for brushing

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C) and prepare a baking sheet lined with parchment paper.

  2. In a large bowl, combine the chopped spinach, feta cheese, cream cheese, Parmesan cheese, chopped dill, minced garlic, salt, and black pepper. Mix well until all the ingredients are evenly incorporated.

  3. Carefully unfold the phyllo dough and cut it into square or rectangular pieces, about 3x3 inches in size. Keep the phyllo dough covered with a damp kitchen towel to prevent it from drying out.

  4. Take one piece of phyllo dough and brush it lightly with olive oil. Place another piece on top and brush it with oil as well. Repeat this process with a total of 4-5 layers of phyllo dough.

  5. Spoon a generous amount of the spinach and feta mixture onto the center of each phyllo dough square. Fold the edges of the phyllo dough over the filling, creating a small cup shape. Repeat this process with the remaining phyllo dough and filling.

  6. Place the filled phyllo cups onto the prepared baking sheet and brush the tops with olive oil. This will help the phyllo dough turn golden brown and crispy in the oven.

  7. Bake the phyllo cups in the preheated oven for about 15-20 minutes, or until the phyllo dough is golden brown and the filling is hot and bubbly.

  8. Once the phyllo cups are done, remove them from the oven and let them cool for a few minutes before serving. These creamy spinach and feta phyllo cups are best enjoyed warm, but they can also be served at room temperature.

Serving Suggestions

These Greek-inspired phyllo cups make a fantastic appetizer for parties, potlucks, or any gathering where you want to impress your guests. They can also be served as a light lunch or snack alongside a fresh salad.

To add some extra flair, you can garnish the phyllo cups with a sprinkle of fresh dill or a drizzle of tzatziki sauce. The tanginess of the feta cheese pairs perfectly with the earthy flavors of the spinach, creating a harmonious blend of taste and texture.

Conclusion

Creamy spinach and feta phyllo cups are a mouthwatering Greek-inspired delight that will leave your taste buds dancing with joy. With their crispy phyllo dough shells and creamy, flavorful filling, these appetizers are sure to be a hit at any occasion. So go ahead, give this recipe a try, and let the flavors of Greece transport you to a culinary paradise. Enjoy!
