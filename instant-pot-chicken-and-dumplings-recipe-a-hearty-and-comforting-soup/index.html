Instant Pot Chicken and Dumplings Recipe: A Hearty and Comforting Soup


Instant Pot chicken and dumplings, a classic comfort food recipe that never fails to warm the heart and soul. This hearty soup is a staple of many cuisines, and for good reason - it’s easy to make, packed with flavor, and sure to satisfy even the biggest appetites.

The Perfect Comfort Food

There’s something special about a warm, steamy bowl of chicken and dumplings on a chilly day. It’s a dish that evokes memories of childhood, of family gatherings, and of cozy nights spent snuggled up by the fireplace. And with this Instant Pot recipe, you can experience that same comfort and warmth in the comfort of your own home.

The Best Part: It’s Easy to Make!

One of the best things about this recipe is just how easy it is to make. With the Instant Pot, you can throw all the ingredients together and let the pressure cooker do the work for you. No more standing over the stove, stirring and monitoring the soup - just set it and forget it!

The Recipe

Here’s what you’ll need:

  • 1 lb boneless, skinless chicken breast or thighs
  • 4 cups chicken broth
  • 1 large onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 carrots, peeled and chopped
  • 2 celery stalks, chopped
  • 1 tsp dried thyme
  • 1 tsp dried basil
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/4 tsp black pepper
  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 1 cup heavy cream (optional)

And here’s how you make it:

  1. Press the “Saute” button on the Instant Pot and melt the butter. Add the chopped onion and cook until softened, about 5 minutes.
  2. Add the garlic, carrots, and celery and cook for an additional 2-3 minutes, until the vegetables are tender.
  3. Add the chicken, chicken broth, thyme, basil, salt, and pepper. Stir to combine.
  4. Close the lid of the Instant Pot and set the valve to “Sealing”. Press the “Manual” or “Pressure Cook” button and set the cooking time to 10 minutes at high pressure.
  5. When the cooking time is up, allow the pressure to release naturally for 10 minutes, then quick-release any remaining pressure.
  6. Open the lid and stir in the flour to thicken the soup.
  7. If desired, stir in the heavy cream to add a rich and creamy texture.
  8. Serve hot, garnished with chopped fresh herbs if desired.

Tips and Variations

  • For an extra boost of flavor, add 1/4 cup of dry white wine to the pot with the chicken and vegetables.
  • If you prefer a lighter broth, use low-sodium chicken broth or add some water to the pot.
  • For a spicy kick, add some diced jalapenos or red pepper flakes to the pot.
  • To make the dumplings from scratch, simply mix 2 cups of all-purpose flour with 1/2 cup of warm water and 1/4 tsp of salt. Drop the dough by spoonfuls into the soup and cook for an additional 5-7 minutes, or until the dumplings are cooked through.

Conclusion

There you have it - a hearty and comforting Instant Pot chicken and dumplings recipe that’s sure to become a family favorite. With its rich and flavorful broth, tender chicken, and fluffy dumplings, this soup is the perfect way to warm up on a chilly day. So go ahead, give it a try, and experience the comfort and joy of a homemade meal, Instant Pot-style!
