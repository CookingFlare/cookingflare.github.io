Quick and Easy Instant Pot Chicken Recipes for Busy Weeknights


If you’re a busy person trying to get a healthy, tasty dinner on the table, you know how challenging it can be to juggle work, family, and other commitments. When time is tight, the Instant Pot can be your best friend in the kitchen. With its ability to cook meals quickly while infusing them with deep, rich flavors, it makes preparing delicious chicken dishes easier than ever. Whether you’re craving a comforting chicken stew or a zesty chicken curry, the Instant Pot has you covered with minimal effort.

In this blog post, we’re diving into quick and easy Instant Pot chicken recipes that will save you time and stress on those hectic weeknights. These dishes are not only simple to prepare but also incredibly versatile, allowing you to adjust flavors and ingredients to suit your taste. Let’s explore these incredible meals that will have your family asking for more!

Why Choose Instant Pot for Chicken?

The Instant Pot is a game-changer when it comes to preparing meals that would typically take hours. It works as a pressure cooker, slow cooker, rice cooker, and even a yogurt maker, all in one. For chicken dishes, this means tender, juicy meat that’s cooked in a fraction of the time compared to traditional methods. You can go from frozen chicken breasts to a fully cooked meal in under 30 minutes. Plus, the Instant Pot helps lock in flavors, ensuring your meals are rich and satisfying.

1. Instant Pot Chicken and Rice

One of the easiest and most comforting dishes you can make in an Instant Pot is chicken and rice. This dish is perfect for busy weeknights because it’s a one-pot meal that requires minimal prep and delivers a filling, flavorful dinner.

Ingredients:

  • 2 lbs chicken breasts or thighs, boneless and skinless
  • 1 cup long-grain white rice, rinsed
  • 1 ½ cups chicken broth
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • 1 tsp dried thyme
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 tbsp olive oil

Instructions:

  1. Set your Instant Pot to sauté mode. Add olive oil, and once it’s hot, sauté the onion and garlic for about 2 minutes until fragrant.
  2. Season the chicken with paprika, thyme, salt, and pepper. Place the chicken into the Instant Pot and sear each side for 2-3 minutes.
  3. Add the rice, chicken broth, and any additional seasoning you like. Stir to combine.
  4. Close the Instant Pot lid, set it to manual high pressure, and cook for 10 minutes.
  5. Allow the Instant Pot to naturally release pressure for 5 minutes, then quick release the remaining pressure.
  6. Fluff the rice with a fork, and your creamy, flavorful chicken and rice is ready to serve!

2. Instant Pot Chicken Alfredo

Craving a rich and creamy pasta dish without spending hours in the kitchen? This Instant Pot chicken Alfredo recipe is a quick solution. The best part is that everything – chicken, pasta, and sauce – gets cooked together in one pot, making cleanup a breeze.

Ingredients:

  • 2 lbs chicken breasts, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 12 oz fettuccine pasta, broken in half
  • 4 cups chicken broth
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Set your Instant Pot to sauté mode and melt the butter. Add the garlic and cook for 1 minute until fragrant.
  2. Add the chicken pieces, season with salt and pepper, and cook for 4-5 minutes until lightly browned.
  3. Pour in the chicken broth and stir to combine. Add the broken fettuccine pasta, ensuring it’s submerged in the liquid.
  4. Close the Instant Pot lid, set it to manual high pressure, and cook for 7 minutes.
  5. Once the cooking time is up, quick release the pressure.
  6. Stir in the heavy cream and Parmesan cheese until the sauce is creamy and smooth.
  7. Serve hot with extra grated Parmesan on top, and enjoy this comforting dish!

3. Instant Pot Lemon Garlic Chicken

If you’re looking for something fresh, bright, and packed with flavor, this lemon garlic chicken is perfect. The Instant Pot seals in the tangy citrus flavor, making it a perfect dish for a light but filling dinner.

Ingredients:

  • 2 lbs chicken breasts or thighs
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • Zest and juice of 2 lemons
  • 1 cup chicken broth
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Set the Instant Pot to sauté mode. Heat the olive oil, then sauté the garlic for about 1 minute.
  2. Season the chicken with salt, pepper, and oregano, then place the chicken into the Instant Pot. Sear each side for 3 minutes.
  3. Add the lemon zest, lemon juice, and chicken broth to the pot. Stir to combine.
  4. Close the Instant Pot lid, set it to manual high pressure, and cook for 10 minutes.
  5. After the cooking time is up, allow the pressure to naturally release for 5 minutes, then quick release the remaining pressure.
  6. Serve the chicken garnished with fresh parsley and a side of your favorite veggies or a salad.

4. Instant Pot BBQ Chicken

BBQ chicken is a crowd-pleaser, and the Instant Pot makes it so easy to cook. This dish comes together in under 30 minutes and is perfect for serving on a busy weeknight when you’re craving smoky, tangy flavors.

Ingredients:

  • 2 lbs chicken breasts or thighs
  • 1 cup BBQ sauce (your favorite kind)
  • 1 cup chicken broth
  • 1 onion, sliced
  • 1 tsp smoked paprika
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Set the Instant Pot to sauté mode and add the onion slices. Sauté for 2-3 minutes until softened.
  2. Season the chicken with smoked paprika, salt, and pepper. Place the chicken in the Instant Pot with the onions.
  3. Pour in the BBQ sauce and chicken broth, making sure the chicken is evenly coated.
  4. Close the Instant Pot lid, set it to manual high pressure, and cook for 12 minutes.
  5. Once the cooking time is up, quick release the pressure. Remove the chicken from the pot and shred it using two forks.
  6. Stir the shredded chicken back into the sauce, and serve over rice, in a sandwich, or alongside roasted vegetables.

5. Instant Pot Chicken Curry

This Instant Pot chicken curry is a flavorful, aromatic dish that will warm you up in no time. The curry sauce is rich and creamy, infused with the perfect blend of spices, and the Instant Pot helps the chicken become incredibly tender.

Ingredients:

  • 2 lbs chicken thighs, boneless and skinless, cut into chunks
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1-inch piece of ginger, grated
  • 2 tbsp curry powder
  • 1 can (14 oz) coconut milk
  • 1 cup chicken broth
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh cilantro for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Set the Instant Pot to sauté mode and heat olive oil. Add the onion, garlic, and ginger, sautéing for 2 minutes until fragrant.
  2. Add the chicken pieces, curry powder, salt, and pepper. Sauté for an additional 3-4 minutes to lightly brown the chicken.
  3. Pour in the coconut milk and chicken broth, stirring to combine.
  4. Close the Instant Pot lid, set it to manual high pressure, and cook for 10 minutes.
  5. After the cooking time is up, allow the pressure to naturally release for 5 minutes, then quick release the remaining pressure.
  6. Garnish with fresh cilantro, and serve over rice or with naan bread.

6. Instant Pot Chicken Tacos

Taco night just got easier with this Instant Pot chicken taco recipe. With minimal prep time, you can have tender, flavorful chicken ready in no time, perfect for tacos, burrito bowls, or salads.

Ingredients:

  • 2 lbs chicken breasts
  • 1 packet taco seasoning (or homemade taco seasoning)
  • 1 cup chicken broth
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 lime, cut into wedges
  • Toppings: shredded lettuce, cheese, salsa, sour cream, cilantro, tortillas

Instructions:

  1. Season the chicken breasts with taco seasoning, salt, and pepper.
  2. Set the Instant Pot to sauté mode and heat olive oil. Sauté the chicken breasts for 2-3 minutes on each side until slightly browned.
  3. Add the chicken broth to the pot, scraping up any browned bits from the bottom of the pot.
  4. Close the Instant Pot lid, set it to manual high pressure, and cook for 10 minutes.
  5. Once the cooking time is up, quick release the pressure.
  6. Shred the chicken with two forks, and serve in warm tortillas with your favorite toppings, such as shredded lettuce, cheese, salsa, sour cream, and fresh cilantro.

Conclusion

When you’re short on time and need to make dinner quickly, the Instant Pot is your best tool for preparing delicious and hearty chicken dishes. The recipes outlined here are perfect for busy weeknights, providing a variety of flavors and cooking methods to suit your preferences. Whether you’re craving something creamy, zesty, smoky, or comforting, the Instant Pot can deliver a meal that’s sure to satisfy the entire family. Try these recipes and enjoy a stress-free and flavorful dinner!
