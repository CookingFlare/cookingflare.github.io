Vegan Sweet Potato Casserole with Pecan Crumble: A Festive Side Dish


Are you looking for a delicious and festive side dish that will impress your guests? Look no further than this Vegan Sweet Potato Casserole with Pecan Crumble! Not only is it packed with flavor, but it’s also made with wholesome ingredients that everyone can enjoy. Whether you’re hosting a holiday gathering or simply want to elevate your weeknight dinner, this recipe is sure to be a hit.

Ingredients

  • 4 large sweet potatoes, peeled and cubed
  • 1/2 cup unsweetened almond milk
  • 1/4 cup maple syrup
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground nutmeg
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt

For the pecan crumble topping:

  • 1 cup rolled oats
  • 1/2 cup chopped pecans
  • 1/4 cup coconut sugar
  • 2 tablespoons coconut oil, melted
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).

  2. In a large pot, bring water to a boil. Add the cubed sweet potatoes and cook until fork-tender, about 15 minutes. Drain the sweet potatoes and transfer them to a mixing bowl.

  3. Using a potato masher or fork, mash the sweet potatoes until smooth. Add the almond milk, maple syrup, vanilla extract, ground cinnamon, ground nutmeg, and salt. Mix well until all the ingredients are fully incorporated.

  4. Transfer the sweet potato mixture to a greased 9x13-inch baking dish, spreading it out evenly.

  5. In a separate mixing bowl, combine the rolled oats, chopped pecans, coconut sugar, melted coconut oil, ground cinnamon, and salt. Mix well until the ingredients are evenly distributed.

  6. Sprinkle the pecan crumble topping over the sweet potato mixture, covering it completely.

  7. Place the baking dish in the preheated oven and bake for 25-30 minutes, or until the pecan crumble is golden brown and crispy.

  8. Remove from the oven and let the casserole cool for a few minutes before serving.

Serving Suggestions

This Vegan Sweet Potato Casserole with Pecan Crumble is perfect as a side dish for any occasion. Serve it alongside your favorite main course, such as roasted vegetables, grilled tofu, or a hearty salad. The creamy sweet potato base pairs beautifully with the crunchy pecan crumble, creating a delightful contrast of textures.

Make-Ahead Tips

If you’re planning to serve this dish for a special occasion, you can easily make it ahead of time. Prepare the sweet potato mixture and the pecan crumble topping separately, but don’t assemble them. Store the sweet potato mixture in an airtight container in the refrigerator for up to two days, and the pecan crumble topping in a separate container at room temperature. When you’re ready to serve, simply assemble the casserole and bake according to the instructions.

Conclusion

This Vegan Sweet Potato Casserole with Pecan Crumble is a true crowd-pleaser. With its creamy sweet potato base and crunchy pecan crumble topping, it’s the perfect festive side dish for any occasion. Not only is it vegan, but it’s also made with wholesome ingredients that will leave you feeling satisfied and nourished. So go ahead and give this recipe a try – your taste buds will thank you!
