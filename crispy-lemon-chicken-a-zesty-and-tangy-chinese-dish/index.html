Crispy Lemon Chicken: A Zesty and Tangy Chinese Dish


If you’re a fan of Chinese cuisine, you’ve probably encountered the delightful flavors of Crispy Lemon Chicken. This dish is a perfect combination of crispy, succulent chicken pieces coated in a tangy and zesty lemon sauce. The contrast of textures and the burst of citrusy flavors make it a popular choice for both casual meals and special occasions. In this blog post, we’ll guide you through the process of creating your own restaurant-quality Crispy Lemon Chicken at home, so you can savor the flavors of China in your own kitchen.

Ingredients

For the Chicken:

  • 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1 cup cornstarch
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • Vegetable oil for frying

For the Lemon Sauce:

  • 1/2 cup chicken broth
  • 1/4 cup freshly squeezed lemon juice
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon cornstarch
  • Zest of one lemon
  • Sliced green onions for garnish

Instructions

  1. Start by preparing the chicken. Cut the chicken breasts into bite-sized pieces and season them with salt and pepper.

  2. In one bowl, beat the eggs. In another bowl, place the cornstarch.

  3. Dip each piece of chicken into the beaten eggs, then coat them thoroughly with cornstarch.

  4. Heat the vegetable oil in a wok or deep skillet over medium-high heat. Once the oil is hot, carefully add the coated chicken pieces in batches, ensuring they are not overcrowded. Fry the chicken until it turns golden brown and crispy. Remove the chicken from the oil and place it on a paper towel to drain excess oil.

  5. In a separate saucepan, combine the chicken broth, lemon juice, sugar, soy sauce, and lemon zest. Bring the mixture to a simmer over medium heat.

  6. In a small bowl, mix the cornstarch with a little water to create a slurry. Slowly pour the slurry into the simmering lemon sauce while continuously stirring. Allow the sauce to thicken, then remove it from the heat.

  7. Place the fried chicken pieces in a large bowl and pour the lemon sauce over them. Gently toss the chicken in the sauce until it is evenly coated.

  8. Transfer the Crispy Lemon Chicken to a serving platter and garnish with sliced green onions for a pop of color and freshness.

Serving Suggestions

Crispy Lemon Chicken pairs wonderfully with steamed white rice or fried rice. You can also serve it alongside stir-fried vegetables for a complete and satisfying meal. For a touch of heat, consider adding a sprinkle of red pepper flakes or a drizzle of chili oil.

Conclusion

Crispy Lemon Chicken is a delightful dish that brings together the crispiness of fried chicken with the bright and tangy flavors of lemon. By following this recipe, you can recreate this classic Chinese favorite in your own kitchen. Whether you’re hosting a dinner party or simply craving a taste of the exotic, this dish is sure to impress and satisfy. So, roll up your sleeves, gather your ingredients, and embark on a culinary journey to discover the irresistible allure of Crispy Lemon Chicken.
