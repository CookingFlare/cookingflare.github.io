Juicy Bison Burger Recipes for Gourmet Grilling


Grilling season is here, and what better way to impress your guests than with a gourmet twist on the classic burger? Bison meat, with its rich flavor and lean profile, is the perfect choice for creating juicy and flavorful burgers that will elevate your grilling game. In this post, we’ll explore two mouthwatering bison burger recipes that are sure to tantalize your taste buds and leave you craving more.

Bison Blue Cheese Burger

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound ground bison meat
  • 1/2 cup crumbled blue cheese
  • 1/4 cup finely chopped red onion
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon Worcestershire sauce
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Brioche buns
  • Arugula for garnish

Instructions:

  1. In a large mixing bowl, gently combine the ground bison, crumbled blue cheese, chopped red onion, minced garlic, Worcestershire sauce, salt, and pepper.
  2. Form the bison mixture into evenly sized patties, making an impression in the center of each patty with your thumb to prevent bulging during grilling.
  3. Preheat your grill to medium-high heat and brush the grates with oil to prevent sticking.
  4. Place the bison patties on the grill and cook for approximately 4-5 minutes per side, or until they reach your desired level of doneness.
  5. Lightly toast the brioche buns on the grill for a minute or two.
  6. Assemble the burgers by placing the grilled bison patties on the brioche buns, topping with a handful of arugula, and any additional condiments of your choice.

The combination of the bold, earthy flavor of bison meat with the creamy, tangy notes of blue cheese creates a burger that is truly unforgettable. The hint of sweetness from the brioche buns and the peppery bite of arugula provide the perfect balance of flavors and textures.

Bison Bacon BBQ Burger

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound ground bison meat
  • 6 strips of crispy bacon
  • 1/2 cup BBQ sauce
  • 1/4 cup caramelized onions
  • 1 teaspoon smoked paprika
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Pretzel buns
  • Sliced pickles for garnish

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, combine the ground bison, smoked paprika, salt, and pepper. Mix gently to incorporate the seasonings.
  2. Form the bison mixture into patties, keeping them slightly thicker in the center to account for shrinkage during grilling.
  3. Grill the bison patties over medium heat, allowing them to cook for about 4-5 minutes on each side.
  4. While the patties are grilling, baste them with BBQ sauce, ensuring they are coated evenly.
  5. Place the crispy bacon strips on the grill to warm them through and enhance their smoky flavor.
  6. Lightly toast the pretzel buns on the grill until they are golden brown.
  7. Assemble the burgers by placing the grilled bison patties on the pretzel buns, topping with caramelized onions, crispy bacon, and sliced pickles.

The marriage of smoky bacon, sweet and tangy BBQ sauce, and the robust flavor of bison meat creates a burger that is a true celebration of summer grilling. The pretzel buns add a delightful crunch, while the caramelized onions provide a touch of sweetness that complements the savory elements perfectly.

Whether you opt for the indulgent Blue Cheese Bison Burger or the irresistible Bison Bacon BBQ Burger, these gourmet bison burger recipes are guaranteed to take your grilling experience to new heights. Impress your friends and family with these delicious creations, and savor the rich, juicy flavor of bison meat in every bite. Happy grilling!
