Individual Chicken and Leek Pies: Comfort Food at its Best

If there’s one thing that can instantly bring a sense of comfort and warmth, it’s a delicious, homemade chicken and leek pie. The combination of tender chicken, flavorful leeks, and buttery pastry is a culinary delight that never fails to satisfy. In this blog post, we’ll explore a recipe for individual chicken and leek pies that will surely become a favorite in your recipe collection. Ingredients For the Filling: 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts, diced 2 leeks, washed and thinly sliced 2 tablespoons butter 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour 1 cup chicken stock 1/2 cup heavy cream Salt and pepper to taste Fresh thyme leaves For the Pastry: 2 1/2 cups all-purpose flour 1 cup unsalted butter, chilled and diced 1/2 teaspoon salt 1/2 cup ice water Instructions Making the Filling: In a large skillet, melt the butter over medium heat. Add the leeks and cook until they are soft and translucent. Add the diced chicken to the skillet and cook until it’s no longer pink. Sprinkle the flour over the chicken and leeks, stirring to coat everything evenly. Pour in the chicken stock and heavy cream, stirring constantly until the mixture thickens. Season the filling with salt, pepper, and fresh thyme leaves. Remove from heat and set aside to cool. Making the Pastry: In a large bowl, combine the flour and salt. Add the chilled, diced butter and use a pastry cutter or your fingers to work the butter into the flour until the mixture resembles coarse crumbs. Gradually add the ice water, mixing until the dough comes together. Divide the dough into 6 equal portions and shape each into a disk. Wrap the disks in plastic wrap and refrigerate for at least 30 minutes. Assembling the Pies: Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C). Roll out each pastry disk on a floured surface to about 1/8 inch thickness. Line 6 individual pie dishes with the pastry, leaving any excess hanging over the edges. Divide the cooled chicken and leek filling among the pie dishes. Roll out the remaining pastry to create the pie crusts. Place them over the filled pies, pressing the edges to seal. Cut a few slits in the top of each pie to allow steam to escape. Brush the tops of the pies with an egg wash for a golden finish. Baking the Pies: Place the pies on a baking sheet and bake for 30-35 minutes, or until the pastry is golden brown and the filling is bubbly. Allow the pies to cool for a few minutes before serving. Enjoy the Comforting Deliciousness There you have it – a delightful recipe for individual chicken and leek pies that’s perfect for a cozy dinner or a special gathering. The creamy filling, tender chicken, and flaky pastry come together to create a dish that embodies comfort food at its best. Serve these pies with a side of your favorite greens or a fresh salad for a complete and satisfying meal. ...
