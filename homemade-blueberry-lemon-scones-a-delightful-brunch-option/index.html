Homemade Blueberry Lemon Scones: A Delightful Brunch Option


There’s something truly magical about the combination of blueberries and lemon. The tangy citrus flavor of lemon perfectly complements the sweet burst of blueberries, creating a delightful symphony of flavors. And what better way to showcase this magical pairing than in a batch of homemade blueberry lemon scones? These scones are not only easy to make but also make for a delightful brunch option that will impress your family and friends.

Ingredients

To make these scrumptious blueberry lemon scones, you will need the following ingredients:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 tablespoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • Zest of 1 lemon
  • 1/2 cup cold unsalted butter, cut into small cubes
  • 1/2 cup buttermilk (or regular milk with 1 teaspoon lemon juice)
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 cup fresh blueberries

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 425°F (220°C) and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.

  2. In a large mixing bowl, whisk together the flour, sugar, baking powder, salt, and lemon zest. This will ensure that all the dry ingredients are well combined and evenly distributed.

  3. Add the cold cubed butter to the dry ingredients. Using a pastry cutter or your fingertips, cut the butter into the flour mixture until it resembles coarse crumbs. The key here is to work quickly and avoid melting the butter with the heat from your hands.

  4. In a separate small bowl, whisk together the buttermilk and vanilla extract. Pour the wet mixture into the dry ingredients and gently mix until just combined. Be careful not to overmix, as this can result in tough scones.

  5. Gently fold in the fresh blueberries, being careful not to crush them. The burst of juicy blueberries in every bite is what makes these scones truly special.

  6. Transfer the dough onto a lightly floured surface and gently pat it into a 1-inch thick rectangle. Using a sharp knife or a bench scraper, cut the dough into 8 equal triangles.

  7. Place the scones onto the prepared baking sheet, leaving some space between them to allow for expansion. Brush the tops of the scones with a little bit of buttermilk for a beautiful golden color.

  8. Bake the scones in the preheated oven for about 15-18 minutes, or until they are golden brown on the outside and cooked through in the center. You can test for doneness by inserting a toothpick into the center of a scone – if it comes out clean, they’re ready!

  9. Once baked, remove the scones from the oven and let them cool on a wire rack for a few minutes. These scones are best enjoyed warm, so grab a cup of tea or coffee and indulge in the delightful flavors of homemade blueberry lemon scones.

Conclusion

These homemade blueberry lemon scones are a true delight and make for a perfect brunch option. The combination of tangy lemon zest and juicy blueberries creates a burst of flavors that will leave you craving for more. Whether you’re hosting a brunch with friends or simply treating yourself to a special breakfast, these scones are sure to impress. So, gather your ingredients, preheat that oven, and get ready to indulge in the magic of homemade blueberry lemon scones. Happy baking!
