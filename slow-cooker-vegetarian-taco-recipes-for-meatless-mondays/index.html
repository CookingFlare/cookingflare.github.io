Slow Cooker Vegetarian Taco Recipes for Meatless Mondays


If you’re looking for a delicious and easy way to enjoy Meatless Mondays, look no further than these slow cooker vegetarian taco recipes. Packed with flavor and nutrients, these recipes will satisfy your taco cravings without the need for meat. Plus, the slow cooker does all the work, making them perfect for busy weekdays. So grab your tortillas and let’s get cooking!

1. Slow Cooker Black Bean Tacos

Ingredients:

  • 1 can (15 ounces) black beans, drained and rinsed
  • 1 cup corn kernels
  • 1 bell pepper, diced
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 can (14.5 ounces) diced tomatoes
  • 1 tablespoon chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Tortillas and toppings of your choice (suggested: avocado, salsa, cilantro)

Instructions:

  1. In a slow cooker, combine the black beans, corn, bell pepper, onion, garlic, diced tomatoes, chili powder, cumin, paprika, salt, and pepper. Stir well to combine.
  2. Cook on low heat for 6-8 hours or on high heat for 3-4 hours, until the flavors have melded together and the vegetables are tender.
  3. Serve the black bean mixture in warm tortillas and top with your favorite toppings. Enjoy!

2. Slow Cooker Lentil Tacos

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup dried lentils
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 can (14.5 ounces) diced tomatoes
  • 1 tablespoon chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • 1/2 teaspoon smoked paprika
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Tortillas and toppings of your choice (suggested: guacamole, sour cream, lettuce)

Instructions:

  1. Rinse the lentils and place them in a slow cooker. Add the onion, garlic, diced tomatoes, chili powder, cumin, smoked paprika, salt, and pepper. Mix well.
  2. Cook on low heat for 6-8 hours or on high heat for 3-4 hours, until the lentils are tender and the flavors have combined.
  3. Serve the lentil mixture in warm tortillas and top with your desired toppings. Delicious!

3. Slow Cooker Sweet Potato Tacos

Ingredients:

  • 2 sweet potatoes, peeled and diced
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 can (14.5 ounces) black beans, drained and rinsed
  • 1 can (14.5 ounces) diced tomatoes
  • 1 tablespoon chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • 1/2 teaspoon cinnamon
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Tortillas and toppings of your choice (suggested: Greek yogurt, cilantro, lime wedges)

Instructions:

  1. In a slow cooker, combine the sweet potatoes, onion, garlic, black beans, diced tomatoes, chili powder, cumin, cinnamon, salt, and pepper. Stir well to mix the ingredients.
  2. Cook on low heat for 6-8 hours or on high heat for 3-4 hours, until the sweet potatoes are tender and the flavors have melded.
  3. Serve the sweet potato mixture in warm tortillas and top with your favorite toppings. Enjoy the burst of flavors!

Conclusion

These slow cooker vegetarian taco recipes are a fantastic way to enjoy Meatless Mondays. Whether you’re a die-hard taco lover or simply looking to incorporate more plant-based meals into your diet, these recipes are sure to satisfy your taste buds. The best part? The slow cooker does all the work, leaving you with a delicious and fuss-free meal. So go ahead, give these recipes a try, and discover the joy of meatless tacos!
