Soft and Chewy Sugar Cookies: Simple Elegance in Every Bite


There’s something truly delightful about a perfectly baked sugar cookie – its soft and chewy texture, the delicate sweetness that melts in your mouth, and the sheer simplicity that brings joy to every bite. In this recipe, we’ll be sharing our secrets to creating soft and chewy sugar cookies that are sure to impress.

Ingredients

  • 2 ¾ cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • ½ teaspoon baking powder
  • 1 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1 ½ cups granulated sugar
  • 1 large egg
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • ¼ cup granulated sugar (for rolling)

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C). Line a baking sheet with parchment paper and set aside.

  2. In a medium bowl, whisk together the flour, baking soda, and baking powder. Set aside.

  3. In a large mixing bowl, cream together the softened butter and granulated sugar until light and fluffy. This should take about 2-3 minutes.

  4. Add the egg and vanilla extract to the butter mixture, and beat until well combined.

  5. Gradually add the dry ingredients to the wet ingredients, mixing until just combined. Be careful not to overmix, as this can result in a tougher cookie.

  6. Using a cookie scoop or a tablespoon, portion out the dough and roll it into 1-inch balls.

  7. Roll each ball of dough in the additional granulated sugar until evenly coated. This will give the cookies their signature sparkle and sweetness.

  8. Place the sugar-coated dough balls onto the prepared baking sheet, spacing them about 2 inches apart to allow for spreading.

  9. Bake in the preheated oven for 10-12 minutes, or until the edges are lightly golden. The cookies will appear slightly underbaked in the center, but they will firm up as they cool.

  10. Remove the baking sheet from the oven and let the cookies cool on the sheet for 5 minutes. Then transfer them to a wire rack to cool completely.

  11. Once cooled, store the soft and chewy sugar cookies in an airtight container at room temperature. They will stay fresh for up to 5 days, but we doubt they’ll last that long!

Tips and Variations

  • For an extra touch of elegance, you can add a sprinkle of colored sugar or festive sprinkles to the tops of the cookies before baking.
  • If you prefer a slightly crisper cookie, you can increase the baking time by 1-2 minutes.
  • To customize your sugar cookies, you can add mix-ins such as chocolate chips, chopped nuts, or dried fruit. Just fold them into the dough before portioning and rolling.
  • These cookies are also a fantastic base for sandwich cookies. Simply spread a layer of your favorite frosting or jam between two cookies for a delightful treat.

Conclusion

Soft and chewy sugar cookies are a timeless classic that never fails to bring joy and happiness to any occasion. With this simple recipe, you’ll be able to create a batch of these elegant cookies that are sure to impress your family and friends. So go ahead, indulge in the simple pleasure of a soft and chewy sugar cookie – it’s a treat that will never go out of style.

Enjoy!
