Wholesome Nut-Free Sweet and Sour Chicken Recipe


If you’re craving a delicious and wholesome Asian-inspired dish that’s free from nuts, then you’re in for a treat with this Nut-Free Sweet and Sour Chicken recipe. It’s a perfect balance of sweet, tangy, and savory flavors that will leave your taste buds dancing with delight. This recipe is not only nut-free but also simple to prepare, making it a great choice for a family dinner or a gathering with friends.

Ingredients

For the Chicken:

  • 1.5 lbs boneless, skinless chicken breasts, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 1/2 cup cornstarch
  • 2 large eggs, beaten
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Vegetable oil for frying

For the Sweet and Sour Sauce:

  • 1 cup pineapple juice
  • 1/2 cup ketchup
  • 1/4 cup rice vinegar
  • 1/4 cup brown sugar
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon cornstarch
  • 1 red bell pepper, diced
  • 1 green bell pepper, diced
  • 1 cup pineapple chunks

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Chicken:

    • In a large bowl, season the chicken pieces with salt and pepper. Then, coat the chicken pieces in cornstarch, shaking off any excess.
    • Dip the cornstarch-coated chicken pieces into the beaten eggs, ensuring they are well coated.
    • In a large skillet, heat the vegetable oil over medium-high heat. Fry the chicken in batches until golden and cooked through. Transfer the cooked chicken to a paper towel-lined plate to drain any excess oil.

  2. Make the Sweet and Sour Sauce:

    • In a small bowl, whisk together the pineapple juice, ketchup, rice vinegar, brown sugar, soy sauce, and cornstarch until smooth.
    • In the same skillet used to cook the chicken, add the diced bell peppers and pineapple chunks. Sauté for a few minutes until slightly softened.
    • Pour the sweet and sour sauce over the peppers and pineapple. Bring the mixture to a simmer, stirring constantly, until the sauce thickens and coats the peppers and pineapple.

  3. Combine the Chicken and Sauce:

    • Add the cooked chicken to the skillet with the sweet and sour sauce. Gently toss the chicken, peppers, and pineapple until everything is well coated with the sauce.

  4. Serve and Enjoy:

    • Transfer the Nut-Free Sweet and Sour Chicken to a serving dish and garnish with sliced green onions, if desired. Serve the dish over steamed white rice or your choice of grain. Enjoy the delightful blend of flavors and textures!

Nutritional Information

  • Calories: 350 per serving
  • Protein: 25g
  • Carbohydrates: 40g
  • Fat: 10g
  • Sodium: 800mg
  • Fiber: 3g

Final Thoughts

This Nut-Free Sweet and Sour Chicken recipe is a fantastic option for those with nut allergies or for anyone looking to enjoy a wholesome and flavorful meal. The combination of tender chicken, vibrant bell peppers, and juicy pineapple, all coated in a luscious sweet and sour sauce, creates a dish that’s sure to become a family favorite. Whether you’re cooking for a special occasion or a casual weeknight dinner, this recipe is a winner. Try it out and savor the delightful flavors of this nut-free culinary delight!
