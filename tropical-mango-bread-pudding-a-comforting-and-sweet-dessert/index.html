Tropical Mango Bread Pudding: A Comforting and Sweet Dessert


Are you looking for a delightful dessert that captures the essence of the tropics? Look no further than this Tropical Mango Bread Pudding recipe! This luscious and comforting dessert combines the rich, creamy texture of bread pudding with the sweet, tropical flavor of mango, resulting in a treat that’s perfect for any occasion. Whether you’re entertaining guests or simply craving a taste of the exotic, this recipe is sure to impress.

Ingredients

Before we dive into the preparation, let’s take a look at the ingredients you’ll need to create this delectable dessert:

  • 6 cups of cubed bread (such as brioche or challah)
  • 2 ripe mangoes, peeled and diced
  • 4 large eggs
  • 1 cup of whole milk
  • 1 cup of coconut milk
  • 1/2 cup of granulated sugar
  • 1 teaspoon of vanilla extract
  • 1/2 teaspoon of ground cinnamon
  • A pinch of salt
  • 1/2 cup of shredded coconut (optional, for garnish)
  • Whipped cream (optional, for serving)

Instructions

Step 1: Prepare the Bread

Begin by preheating your oven to 350°F (175°C). Then, spread the cubed bread on a baking sheet and toast it in the oven for about 10-15 minutes, or until the bread is golden and slightly crispy. This step will help the bread hold its texture when mixed with the custard mixture.

Step 2: Make the Custard Mixture

In a large bowl, whisk together the eggs, whole milk, coconut milk, granulated sugar, vanilla extract, ground cinnamon, and a pinch of salt until well combined. This luscious custard will be the foundation of our bread pudding, infusing each bite with creamy sweetness and a hint of tropical flavor.

Step 3: Assemble the Bread Pudding

Once the custard mixture is ready, gently fold in the toasted bread cubes and diced mangoes, ensuring that the bread is well-coated with the custard. Allow the mixture to sit for about 15-20 minutes, allowing the bread to soak up the flavors of the custard and mango. This step is crucial for achieving a moist and flavorful bread pudding.

Step 4: Bake to Perfection

Transfer the bread pudding mixture into a greased baking dish, spreading it out evenly. Sprinkle the shredded coconut on top for an extra touch of tropical goodness. Then, place the baking dish in the preheated oven and bake the pudding for 45-50 minutes, or until the top is golden brown and the custard is set.

Step 5: Serve and Enjoy

Once the bread pudding is beautifully golden and fragrant, remove it from the oven and allow it to cool for a few minutes. You can choose to serve it warm, allowing the creamy custard and sweet mango to envelop your senses. For an extra indulgence, top each serving with a dollop of whipped cream.

Conclusion

With its velvety texture, aromatic flavors, and tropical allure, this Tropical Mango Bread Pudding is a dessert that’s bound to captivate your taste buds and transport you to a sun-kissed paradise. Whether you’re savoring it on a balmy summer evening or seeking a bit of warmth during the colder months, this delightful treat is a true celebration of tropical indulgence. So, gather your ingredients and embark on a culinary journey to create this comforting and sweet dessert that’s sure to leave a lasting impression on all who indulge in its deliciousness.
