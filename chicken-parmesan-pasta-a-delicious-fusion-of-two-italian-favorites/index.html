Chicken Parmesan Pasta: A Delicious Fusion of Two Italian Favorites


If you’re a fan of Italian cuisine, you’re in for a treat with this delicious recipe that combines the best of two Italian classics – Chicken Parmesan and Pasta. This Chicken Parmesan Pasta dish is the perfect fusion of flavors, bringing together the crispy goodness of breaded chicken and the comforting appeal of pasta, all smothered in rich marinara sauce and melted cheese. It’s a hearty and satisfying meal that’s sure to become a family favorite.

Ingredients

To make this mouthwatering Chicken Parmesan Pasta, you’ll need the following ingredients:

  • 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 cup Italian breadcrumbs
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 pound penne or spaghetti pasta
  • 2 cups marinara sauce
  • 1 1/2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Fresh basil leaves for garnish

Instructions

Step 1: Prepare the Chicken

Start by preheating your oven to 375°F (190°C). While the oven is heating up, prepare the chicken breasts by pounding them to an even thickness. Season the chicken with salt and pepper.

Step 2: Breading the Chicken

Set up three shallow dishes – one with flour, one with beaten eggs, and one with a mixture of Italian breadcrumbs and grated Parmesan cheese. Dredge each chicken breast in the flour, then dip into the beaten eggs, and finally coat with the breadcrumb mixture, pressing the breadcrumbs gently to adhere to the chicken.

Step 3: Cook the Chicken

In a large skillet, heat some olive oil over medium-high heat. Once the oil is hot, add the breaded chicken breasts and cook until golden brown on both sides, about 3-4 minutes per side. Transfer the browned chicken to a baking dish.

Step 4: Cook the Pasta

While the chicken is cooking, bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Cook the pasta according to the package instructions until al dente. Drain the pasta and set aside.

Step 5: Assemble the Dish

Pour the marinara sauce over the browned chicken breasts, making sure to cover them completely. Sprinkle the shredded mozzarella cheese over the top of the chicken and sauce.

Step 6: Bake

Place the baking dish in the preheated oven and bake for 25-30 minutes, or until the cheese is bubbly and golden brown.

Step 7: Serve

Once the Chicken Parmesan Pasta is out of the oven, let it rest for a few minutes. Then, serve the chicken and sauce over the cooked pasta, garnished with fresh basil leaves.

Enjoy the Fusion of Flavors

The result is a delightful combination of crispy, tender chicken, savory marinara sauce, and gooey melted cheese, all perfectly paired with al dente pasta. This Chicken Parmesan Pasta dish is a celebration of Italian flavors that will have everyone at the table asking for seconds.

Conclusion

With its satisfying textures and rich flavors, Chicken Parmesan Pasta is a comforting and indulgent dish that brings together the best of both worlds – the beloved Chicken Parmesan and the timeless appeal of pasta. Whether you’re cooking for a special occasion or simply craving a hearty, flavorful meal, this recipe is an excellent choice. So, gather your ingredients, roll up your sleeves, and get ready to savor the delicious fusion of two Italian favorites in this Chicken Parmesan Pasta dish. Buon appetito!
