Herbs and More: 5 Marinades to Elevate Grilled Vegetables


Grilled vegetables are a delightful addition to any summer gathering or backyard barbecue. They bring vibrant colors, smoky flavors, and a healthy touch to the table. But why settle for plain grilled vegetables when you can take them to the next level with a burst of fresh herbs and other flavorful ingredients? In this post, we’ll explore five mouthwatering marinades that will elevate your grilled vegetables and impress your guests. So fire up the grill and get ready to tantalize your taste buds!

1. Lemon and Herb Marinade

The combination of zesty lemon and aromatic herbs creates a tantalizing flavor profile that complements a variety of vegetables. To make this marinade, whisk together the juice of two lemons, a generous amount of chopped fresh herbs such as parsley, thyme, and rosemary, a drizzle of olive oil, and a pinch of salt and pepper. Pour the marinade over your vegetables and let them soak up the flavors for at least 30 minutes before grilling. The result? Tender, tangy vegetables with a refreshing twist.

2. Asian-Inspired Ginger and Soy Marinade

For an Asian-inspired twist on grilled vegetables, try this ginger and soy marinade. In a bowl, combine minced fresh ginger, soy sauce, honey, sesame oil, and a splash of rice vinegar. Whisk until well combined, then pour the marinade over your vegetables. Allow them to marinate for at least an hour, preferably overnight, to let the flavors penetrate the vegetables. When grilled, the ginger and soy marinade imparts a beautiful umami flavor that pairs perfectly with the charred smokiness of the vegetables.

3. Spicy Cajun Marinade

If you’re craving a bit of heat, this spicy Cajun marinade is sure to please your taste buds. In a bowl, mix together paprika, cayenne pepper, garlic powder, onion powder, dried thyme, dried oregano, salt, and black pepper. Add a generous splash of olive oil and mix until it forms a paste. Coat your vegetables with the marinade and let them sit for at least 15 minutes before grilling. The vibrant combination of spices in this marinade adds a bold and fiery kick to your grilled vegetables.

4. Mediterranean-inspired Balsamic and Herb Marinade

For a taste of the Mediterranean, try this balsamic and herb marinade. In a bowl, whisk together balsamic vinegar, minced garlic, chopped fresh basil, dried oregano, olive oil, salt, and pepper. Pour the marinade over your vegetables and allow them to marinate for at least 30 minutes. This marinade adds a tangy sweetness from the balsamic vinegar, combined with the earthiness of the herbs, resulting in a flavor that will transport you straight to the shores of the Mediterranean.

5. Smoky Chipotle Marinade

For those who love bold, smoky flavors, this chipotle marinade is a must-try. In a blender, combine chipotle peppers in adobo sauce, garlic, lime juice, honey, olive oil, cumin, and a pinch of salt. Blend until smooth, then pour the marinade over your vegetables. Allow them to marinate for at least 1 hour to infuse the smoky chipotle flavor. The combination of heat and smokiness in this marinade creates a unique and irresistible taste that will leave your guests coming back for more.

Grilling Tips

Before you start grilling, here are a few tips to ensure your vegetables turn out perfect every time:

  • Preheat your grill to medium-high heat to achieve those beautiful grill marks and a smoky flavor.
  • Make sure to cut your vegetables into uniform sizes to ensure even cooking.
  • Brush your grill grates with oil to prevent sticking.
  • Don’t overcrowd the grill. Leave some space between the vegetables to allow for even cooking and those coveted grill marks.
  • Keep a close eye on your vegetables and turn them frequently to prevent burning.

With these marinades and grilling tips, you’ll be well on your way to creating a feast of delicious, elevated grilled vegetables that will impress even the most discerning palates. So gather your favorite herbs, spices, and condiments, and let your creativity shine as you experiment with different combinations. Happy grilling!
