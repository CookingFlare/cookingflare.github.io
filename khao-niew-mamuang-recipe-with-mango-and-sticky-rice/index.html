Khao Niew Mamuang Recipe with Mango and Sticky Rice


If you’re a fan of Thai desserts, then you’re in for a treat! Today, we’re going to learn how to make the famous Khao Niew Mamuang, a delightful Thai dessert made with mango and sticky rice. This sweet and creamy dish is perfect for satisfying your cravings and impressing your friends and family with your culinary skills.

Ingredients

To make this delicious dessert, you’ll need the following ingredients:

  • 1 cup of sticky rice
  • 1 cup of coconut milk
  • 1/2 cup of sugar
  • 1/4 teaspoon of salt
  • 2 ripe mangoes

Instructions

  1. Soak the sticky rice in water for at least 4 hours or overnight. This will help soften the rice and make it easier to cook.

  2. After soaking, drain the rice and transfer it to a steamer basket lined with a cheesecloth. Steam the rice over medium heat for about 25-30 minutes, or until it becomes tender and translucent.

  3. While the rice is steaming, prepare the coconut sauce by combining the coconut milk, sugar, and salt in a saucepan. Heat the mixture over medium heat until the sugar has dissolved, stirring occasionally.

  4. Once the rice is cooked, transfer it to a mixing bowl and pour half of the coconut sauce over it. Mix well to ensure that the sauce is evenly distributed and the rice is fully coated.

  5. Cover the bowl with a clean kitchen towel and let it sit for about 10-15 minutes to allow the rice to absorb the flavors.

  6. While the rice is resting, peel and slice the mangoes into thin, bite-sized pieces.

  7. To serve, divide the sticky rice into individual serving bowls or plates. Top each portion with a generous amount of sliced mangoes.

  8. Drizzle the remaining coconut sauce over the mangoes and rice, allowing it to seep into the crevices and enhance the flavors.

  9. Garnish with a sprinkle of toasted sesame seeds or a sprig of fresh mint, if desired.

  10. Serve the Khao Niew Mamuang while the rice is still warm for the best taste and texture.

And there you have it - a simple yet irresistible Khao Niew Mamuang recipe that will transport you to the vibrant streets of Thailand. This dessert is a harmonious combination of flavors and textures, with the sticky rice providing a chewy base for the sweet and juicy mangoes. The creamy coconut sauce adds a luscious touch that ties everything together.

Now, you can enjoy this delightful Thai dessert in the comfort of your own home. Whether it’s for a special occasion or just a sweet indulgence, the Khao Niew Mamuang recipe is sure to impress. So, gather your ingredients, follow the steps, and get ready to savor the taste of Thailand!

Conclusion

In conclusion, Khao Niew Mamuang is a traditional Thai dessert that combines the richness of coconut milk, the sweetness of mangoes, and the stickiness of rice. This dessert is not only a treat for the taste buds but also a feast for the eyes. Its vibrant colors and delightful flavors make it a popular choice among locals and tourists alike.

Whether you’re a fan of Thai cuisine or simply looking to explore new flavors, Khao Niew Mamuang is a must-try dessert that will leave you craving for more.
