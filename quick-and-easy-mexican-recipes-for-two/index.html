Quick and Easy Mexican Recipes for Two


Looking for delicious Mexican recipes that are quick and easy to prepare? Look no further! In this blog post, we’ll share some mouthwatering Mexican dishes that are perfect for a cozy dinner for two. Whether you’re cooking for a date night or simply want to enjoy a flavorful meal with your loved one, these recipes are sure to impress.

1. Chicken Enchiladas Verdes

Ingredients:

  • 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1 cup salsa verde
  • 1/2 cup sour cream
  • 1 cup shredded Monterey Jack cheese
  • 4 large flour tortillas
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Chopped cilantro for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. Season the chicken breasts with salt and pepper, then grill or pan-fry until cooked through. Shred the chicken and set aside.
  3. In a small bowl, mix the salsa verde and sour cream together.
  4. In a separate bowl, combine the shredded chicken and half of the salsa verde mixture.
  5. Warm the tortillas in a dry skillet until pliable.
  6. Divide the chicken mixture evenly among the tortillas and roll them up tightly.
  7. Place the enchiladas in a baking dish and pour the remaining salsa verde mixture over the top.
  8. Sprinkle the shredded cheese evenly over the enchiladas.
  9. Bake in the preheated oven for 20 minutes, or until the cheese is melted and bubbly.
  10. Garnish with chopped cilantro and serve hot.

2. Shrimp Tacos with Mango Salsa

Ingredients:

  • 8 small flour tortillas
  • 1 pound shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon chili powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon cumin
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 cup diced mango
  • 1/2 cup diced red onion
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh cilantro
  • Juice of 1 lime

Instructions:

  1. Preheat a grill or grill pan over medium-high heat.
  2. In a bowl, toss the shrimp with olive oil, chili powder, cumin, salt, and pepper.
  3. Grill the shrimp for 2-3 minutes on each side, or until they are pink and cooked through.
  4. Warm the tortillas in a dry skillet or on the grill.
  5. In a separate bowl, combine the diced mango, red onion, cilantro, and lime juice to make the salsa.
  6. Fill each tortilla with a few grilled shrimp and top with the mango salsa.
  7. Serve immediately and enjoy!

3. Mexican Street Corn Salad

Ingredients:

  • 4 ears of corn, husked
  • 1/4 cup mayonnaise
  • 1/4 cup sour cream
  • Juice of 1 lime
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh cilantro
  • 1/4 teaspoon chili powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon paprika
  • 1/4 cup crumbled cotija cheese
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat a grill or grill pan over medium-high heat.
  2. Grill the corn for 8-10 minutes, turning occasionally, until the kernels are charred and tender.
  3. Cut the kernels off the cob and transfer them to a large bowl.
  4. In a separate bowl, whisk together the mayonnaise, sour cream, lime juice, cilantro, chili powder, and paprika.
  5. Pour the dressing over the corn and toss to coat.
  6. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  7. Sprinkle the crumbled cotija cheese over the top and serve at room temperature.

Conclusion

These quick and easy Mexican recipes for two are perfect for those nights when you want a flavorful meal without spending hours in the kitchen. From the zesty Chicken Enchiladas Verdes to the refreshing Shrimp Tacos with Mango Salsa, and the creamy Mexican Street Corn Salad, there’s something to satisfy every craving. So grab your apron, head to the kitchen, and enjoy a delicious Mexican feast with your loved one. Buen provecho!
