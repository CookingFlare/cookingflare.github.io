Sizzling BBQ Chicken Wrap: Perfect for Summer Cookouts


Summer is the perfect time for firing up the grill and enjoying delicious, flavorful meals with family and friends. If you’re looking for a mouthwatering dish that’s easy to make and perfect for a summer cookout, look no further than the Sizzling BBQ Chicken Wrap. This recipe combines the smoky flavors of barbecue chicken with fresh, crisp vegetables, all wrapped up in a warm tortilla. It’s a versatile dish that can be served as a main course or sliced into smaller pieces for a delightful appetizer. Let’s dive into the details of how to create this delectable summer treat.

Ingredients

For the BBQ Chicken:

  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1 cup of your favorite barbecue sauce
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

For the Wrap:

  • 4 large flour tortillas
  • 1 cup shredded lettuce
  • 1 cup diced tomatoes
  • 1/2 cup diced red onions
  • 1 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1/2 cup ranch dressing

Instructions

Step 1: Prepare the BBQ Chicken

Start by preheating your grill to medium-high heat. While the grill is heating up, brush the chicken breasts with olive oil and season them with salt and pepper. This simple seasoning will enhance the natural flavors of the chicken and create a perfect base for the barbecue sauce.

Once the grill is hot, place the seasoned chicken breasts on the grill and cook for 6-7 minutes on each side, or until the internal temperature reaches 165°F (75°C). As the chicken cooks, baste it generously with your favorite barbecue sauce, allowing the flavors to caramelize and create a mouthwatering glaze.

Step 2: Assemble the Wrap

While the chicken is grilling, it’s time to prepare the other ingredients for the wrap. Lay out the flour tortillas and evenly distribute the shredded lettuce, diced tomatoes, and red onions over each tortilla. Sprinkle the shredded cheddar cheese on top of the vegetables, creating a colorful and flavorful base for the wrap.

Step 3: Slice and Wrap the Chicken

Once the chicken is fully cooked, remove it from the grill and allow it to rest for a few minutes. This resting period will help the juices redistribute, ensuring that each bite of the wrap is moist and flavorful. After resting, slice the chicken into thin strips and distribute them evenly over the prepared tortillas.

Step 4: Drizzle with Ranch Dressing

To add a creamy, tangy element to the wrap, drizzle each assembled wrap with ranch dressing. The coolness of the ranch dressing will complement the smoky flavors of the barbecue chicken, creating a perfect balance of flavors and textures.

Step 5: Wrap and Serve

To complete the Sizzling BBQ Chicken Wrap, carefully fold the sides of each tortilla over the filling and then roll it up tightly, creating a neat, compact wrap. Once wrapped, the chicken wrap is ready to be served. For a delightful presentation, you can slice the wraps into smaller pieces and arrange them on a platter, or serve them whole for a heartier meal.

Enjoy the Flavors of Summer

The Sizzling BBQ Chicken Wrap is a delightful combination of smoky, savory, and fresh flavors that capture the essence of summer. Whether you’re hosting a backyard cookout, a picnic in the park, or simply looking for a delicious meal to enjoy with loved ones, this recipe is sure to satisfy. The versatility of the wrap makes it an ideal choice for any occasion, and its vibrant colors and bold flavors will impress guests and family alike. So fire up the grill, gather your ingredients, and treat yourself to the irresistible taste of summer with this fantastic Sizzling BBQ Chicken Wrap recipe. Happy cooking!
