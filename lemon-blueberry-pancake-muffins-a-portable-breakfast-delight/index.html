Lemon Blueberry Pancake Muffins: A Portable Breakfast Delight


Who doesn’t love the smell of freshly baked muffins in the morning? Imagine waking up to the delightful aroma of Lemon Blueberry Pancake Muffins wafting through your kitchen, promising a delicious start to your day. These muffins combine the best of two breakfast classics—pancakes and muffins—into a portable treat that’s perfect for busy mornings or leisurely brunches.

Ingredients

Dry Ingredients:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 tablespoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt

Wet Ingredients:

  • 1 cup buttermilk
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, melted and slightly cooled
  • 2 large eggs
  • Zest of 1 lemon
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Blueberry Compote:

  • 1 cup fresh or frozen blueberries
  • 2 tablespoons granulated sugar
  • 1 tablespoon water
  • 1 teaspoon lemon juice

Streusel Topping:

  • 1/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons granulated sugar
  • 2 tablespoons unsalted butter, cold and cubed

Instructions

Making the Blueberry Compote:

  1. In a small saucepan, combine the blueberries, sugar, water, and lemon juice.
  2. Cook over medium heat, stirring occasionally, until the mixture comes to a simmer.
  3. Reduce the heat to low and continue cooking for 8-10 minutes, or until the blueberries burst and the mixture thickens slightly.
  4. Remove from heat and let cool completely.

Preparing the Streusel Topping:

  1. In a small bowl, combine the flour and sugar for the streusel topping.
  2. Cut in the cold cubed butter using a pastry cutter or your fingers until the mixture resembles coarse crumbs.
  3. Set aside.

Making the Muffin Batter:

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C). Line a muffin tin with paper liners or grease with non-stick cooking spray.
  2. In a large mixing bowl, whisk together the flour, sugar, baking powder, baking soda, and salt.
  3. In another bowl, whisk together the buttermilk, melted butter, eggs, lemon zest, lemon juice, and vanilla extract until well combined.
  4. Pour the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients and stir until just combined. Do not overmix; the batter should be slightly lumpy.
  5. Gently fold in 3/4 cup of the prepared blueberry compote, reserving the rest for topping.

Assembling the Muffins:

  1. Divide the batter evenly among the muffin cups, filling each about 3/4 full.
  2. Spoon a small amount of the reserved blueberry compote over the top of each muffin.
  3. Sprinkle the streusel topping evenly over the muffins, pressing lightly to adhere.

Baking the Muffins:

  1. Bake for 18-20 minutes, or until the muffins are golden brown and a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.
  2. Remove from the oven and let cool in the pan for 5 minutes.
  3. Transfer the muffins to a wire rack to cool completely.

Serving Suggestions

These Lemon Blueberry Pancake Muffins are best enjoyed warm, straight from the oven, or at room temperature. Serve them with a dollop of whipped cream or a drizzle of maple syrup for an extra indulgent treat. Pair them with a hot cup of coffee or tea for a cozy breakfast experience, or pack them into lunchboxes for a delightful mid-morning snack.

Storage Tips

Store any leftover muffins in an airtight container at room temperature for up to 3 days. You can also freeze the muffins for longer storage; simply wrap them individually in plastic wrap and place them in a freezer-safe container or bag. To reheat, microwave thawed muffins for 20-30 seconds or until warmed through.

Final Thoughts

Whether you’re looking for a quick breakfast on the go or planning a leisurely brunch with friends, these Lemon Blueberry Pancake Muffins are sure to impress. With their fluffy texture, burst of blueberry flavor, and hint of citrusy zest, they offer a delightful twist on traditional pancakes and muffins. Try baking a batch today and discover why these muffins are a beloved favorite for any occasion. Enjoy the simple pleasure of homemade baking with every bite of these delicious muffins!
