One Pot Beef and Black Bean Chili


Chili is the ultimate comfort food, and there’s nothing quite like a warm bowl of chili on a chilly evening. Today, I’m sharing a delicious recipe for One Pot Beef and Black Bean Chili that is sure to satisfy your taste buds and warm your soul.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 bell pepper, diced
  • 1 can black beans, drained and rinsed
  • 1 can diced tomatoes
  • 1 cup beef broth
  • 2 tablespoons chili powder
  • 1 tablespoon cumin
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon cayenne pepper (optional, for those who like it spicy)
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • Fresh cilantro, for garnish
  • Sour cream, for serving

Instructions

  1. Heat a large pot over medium heat and add the ground beef. Cook until browned, breaking it up with a spoon as it cooks. Drain any excess fat.

  2. Add the diced onion, minced garlic, and diced bell pepper to the pot. Cook for 5 minutes, until the vegetables have softened.

  3. Stir in the black beans, diced tomatoes, beef broth, chili powder, cumin, paprika, cayenne pepper (if using), salt, and pepper. Bring the mixture to a boil, then reduce the heat and simmer for 30 minutes, allowing the flavors to meld together.

  4. Serve the chili hot, garnished with fresh cilantro and a dollop of sour cream. Enjoy!

Why One Pot Beef and Black Bean Chili?

This One Pot Beef and Black Bean Chili recipe is perfect for busy weeknights when you don’t have a lot of time to spend in the kitchen. With just a few simple ingredients, you can create a hearty and flavorful meal that the whole family will love.

The combination of ground beef, black beans, and aromatic spices creates a rich and satisfying chili that is packed with protein and fiber. The black beans not only add a creamy texture to the chili but also provide a nutritional boost. They are a great source of plant-based protein, fiber, and essential vitamins and minerals.

One of the best things about this recipe is that it can be customized to suit your taste preferences. If you like it spicy, feel free to add more cayenne pepper or even some diced jalapenos. If you prefer a milder flavor, simply reduce the amount of chili powder and cayenne pepper. You can also top your chili with your favorite garnishes, such as shredded cheese, diced avocado, or sliced green onions.

Not only is this One Pot Beef and Black Bean Chili delicious and easy to make, but it also requires minimal cleanup. Since everything is cooked in one pot, you won’t have a sink full of dirty dishes to tackle after dinner. It’s a win-win!

So, the next time you’re craving a warm and comforting meal, give this One Pot Beef and Black Bean Chili recipe a try. It’s sure to become a family favorite!
