Greek Feta and Olive Stuffed Chicken Breast: A Mediterranean Twist


When it comes to Mediterranean cuisine, the flavors are vibrant, the ingredients are fresh, and the dishes are often a celebration of simplicity. One dish that perfectly captures the essence of Mediterranean cooking is the Greek feta and olive stuffed chicken breast. This recipe combines tender chicken breast with the tangy richness of feta cheese and the briny, savory flavor of olives, resulting in a mouthwatering dish that will transport your taste buds to the sun-drenched shores of Greece.

Ingredients

  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1 cup crumbled feta cheese
  • 1/2 cup pitted Kalamata olives, chopped
  • 2 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley
  • 1 tablespoon chopped fresh oregano
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Toothpicks

Instructions

Step 1: Prepare the Filling

In a mixing bowl, combine the crumbled feta cheese, chopped Kalamata olives, chopped parsley, chopped oregano, minced garlic, and olive oil. Mix the ingredients together until they are well combined. The filling should have a crumbly texture with the flavors of the feta and olives evenly distributed.

Step 2: Butterfly the Chicken Breasts

Lay the chicken breasts on a clean, flat surface. Using a sharp knife, carefully slice each chicken breast horizontally, but not all the way through, to create a pocket. Be sure not to cut all the way to the other side. Open up the chicken breasts to form a butterfly shape.

Step 3: Stuff the Chicken Breasts

Divide the feta and olive filling evenly among the butterflied chicken breasts, spooning it into the pockets you created. Gently press the edges of the chicken together to encase the filling. Secure the openings with toothpicks to ensure the filling stays inside during cooking.

Step 4: Season the Chicken

Season the stuffed chicken breasts with salt and pepper to taste. The salt will help to enhance the flavors of the feta and olives, while the pepper will add a subtle kick to the dish.

Step 5: Cook the Chicken

Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C). Heat a skillet over medium-high heat and add a drizzle of olive oil. Once the skillet is hot, place the stuffed chicken breasts in the skillet and cook for 3-4 minutes on each side, or until they are golden brown. This initial searing will lock in the flavors and juices of the chicken.

Transfer the seared chicken breasts to a baking dish and bake in the preheated oven for 20-25 minutes, or until the chicken is cooked through and the filling is hot and bubbly.

Step 6: Serve and Enjoy

Once the chicken is cooked, remove the toothpicks and let the chicken rest for a few minutes before slicing. Serve the Greek feta and olive stuffed chicken breasts with a side of roasted vegetables, a fresh Greek salad, or a simple lemon and herb couscous. The combination of the tangy feta, briny olives, and tender chicken is sure to delight your taste buds and transport you to the Mediterranean with every bite.

Conclusion

This Greek feta and olive stuffed chicken breast recipe is a delightful way to bring the flavors of the Mediterranean to your dinner table. Whether you’re hosting a dinner party or simply treating yourself to a special meal, this dish is a perfect choice. The combination of feta and olives adds a burst of flavor to the tender chicken, creating a dish that is both elegant and comforting. So, gather your ingredients, embrace the spirit of Mediterranean cooking, and indulge in this delightful culinary journey.
