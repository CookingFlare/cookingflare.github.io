Roasted Pumpkin Seeds Three Ways


The smell of pumpkin seeds roasting on the oven brings back countless cozy and heartwarming memories. Whether you are excitedly carving jack-o’-lanterns or preparing your Thanksgiving feast, pumpkin seeds are a must-have snack. This year, take your pumpkin seed snacking up a notch with three unique and flavorful twists. From sweet to savory, there’s something for everyone to enjoy.

Method:

  1. Cleaning and Drying the Pumpkin Seeds Before diving into the three flavor variations, let’s go over the essential steps for preparing pumpkin seeds to roast. Begin by carving or scooping out your pumpkin, and save the seeds for roasting later. Rinse pumpkin seeds thoroughly under cool water to remove any pumpkin residue and debris. Once clean, spread the seeds evenly on a clean, damp towel and allow them to dry completely for approximately two hours or until completely free of moisture.

  2. Flavoring the Pumpkin Seeds With your pumpkin seeds ready for roasting, let’s dive into the deliciously versatile recipes.

Flavor Variant 1: Honey, Cinnamon, and Pumpkin Seed Mix

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups of Pumpkin Seeds
  • 2 tablespoons of honey
  • 1 teaspoon of cinnamon
  • Salt, to taste

Instructions: After drying the seeds, toss them gently in a bowl with honey, cinnamon, and a dash of salt. Mix well to ensure even coating. For the best results, let the pumpkin seeds sit in the honey-cinnamon mixture for at least an hour before roasting.

Flavor Variant 2: Hot and Spicy Roasted Pumpkin Seeds

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups of Pumpkin Seeds
  • 1 tablespoon of olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon of paprika
  • 1 teaspoon of garlic powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon of cayenne pepper
  • Salt, to taste

Instructions: Heat olive oil in a pan over medium heat, add pumpkin seeds and stir frequently. Add paprika, garlic powder, cayenne pepper, and a pinch of salt. Mix well, reduce heat and let them cook slowly. Eventually, you’ll have a fragrant and slightly spicy treat.

Flavor Variant 3: Classic Roasted and Salty Pumpkin Seeds

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups of Pumpkin Seeds
  • 1 tablespoon of olive oil
  • Salt, to taste

Instructions: In a lined baking tray, combine the pumpkin seeds with olive oil and a generous helping of salt. Stir until all seeds are lightly coated. Place them in the oven at 180°C (350°F) for approximately 15 minutes, stirring occasionally. Once golden brown, your classic roasted and salty pumpkin seeds will be ready to serve.

  1. Oven Roasting and Serving Suggestions

Preheat your oven to 180°C (350°F). Place pumpkin seeds on a lined baking tray in a single layer, ensuring there is space between seeds for even toasting. Roast for approximately 15 minutes or until seeds are fragrant and golden brown, ensuring to stir the seeds frequently to avoid over toasting.

Conclusion:

Add a touch of flair to the classic pumpkin seed snack this season. By incorporating these easy and delicious recipes, your friends and family will beg for more. Plus, the versatility of these recipes allows you to tailor your snacks to the crowd’s taste buds, making pumpkin seeds an accessible option for any event. So do not hesitations, pick your favorite, roast them and enjoy!
