Delightful Low Carb Zucchini and Parmesan Baked Chips Recipe

Are you looking for a healthy and delicious snack that won’t derail your low carb diet? Look no further than these delightful low carb zucchini and Parmesan baked chips! Packed with flavor and crunch, these chips are the perfect guilt-free treat. Plus, they are incredibly easy to make. So grab your zucchinis and let’s get started! Ingredients 2 medium zucchinis 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese 1/4 cup almond flour 1 teaspoon garlic powder 1/2 teaspoon paprika 1/2 teaspoon salt 1/4 teaspoon black pepper Cooking spray Instructions Preheat your oven to 425°F (220°C). Line a baking sheet with parchment paper. Wash the zucchinis and cut them into thin slices, about 1/8 inch thick. A mandoline slicer works great for this, but a sharp knife will do the job too. In a shallow bowl, combine the Parmesan cheese, almond flour, garlic powder, paprika, salt, and black pepper. Mix well to ensure all the ingredients are evenly distributed. Take each zucchini slice and dip it into the Parmesan mixture, pressing down gently to make sure it sticks to both sides. Place the coated slice on the prepared baking sheet. Repeat this process until all the zucchini slices are coated. Lightly spray the coated zucchini slices with cooking spray. This will help them crisp up nicely in the oven. Bake the zucchini chips for 15-20 minutes, or until they are golden brown and crispy. Keep an eye on them as they can burn quickly. Once the chips are done, remove them from the oven and let them cool for a few minutes. They will continue to crisp up as they cool. Serve your delightful low carb zucchini and Parmesan baked chips as a snack or as a side dish. They pair perfectly with your favorite dip or salsa. Store any leftovers in an airtight container at room temperature for up to 3 days. Although, let’s be honest, they probably won’t last that long! Why Choose Low Carb Zucchini and Parmesan Baked Chips? These zucchini and Parmesan chips are not only delicious but also low in carbs, making them a great option for those following a low carb or keto diet. Zucchinis are a fantastic vegetable to incorporate into your diet as they are low in calories and high in fiber, vitamins, and minerals. Light and Fresh Low Carb Mediterranean Chicken Salad

Are you in the mood for a light and refreshing meal that’s bursting with Mediterranean flavors? Look no further than this delightful Low Carb Mediterranean Chicken Salad. Packed with protein, healthy fats, and vibrant vegetables, this salad is not only delicious but also perfect for those following a low-carb lifestyle. Let’s dive into the recipe and discover how to create this culinary masterpiece in your own kitchen. Ingredients For the Chicken: 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts 1 tablespoon olive oil 1 teaspoon dried oregano Salt and pepper to taste For the Salad: 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved 1 cucumber, diced 1/2 red onion, thinly sliced 1/2 cup Kalamata olives, pitted and halved 1/4 cup crumbled feta cheese 1/4 cup fresh parsley, chopped For the Dressing: 1/4 cup extra virgin olive oil 2 tablespoons red wine vinegar 1 clove garlic, minced 1 teaspoon dried oregano Salt and pepper to taste Instructions Step 1: Prepare the Chicken Preheat your grill or grill pan over medium-high heat. In a small bowl, combine the olive oil, dried oregano, salt, and pepper. Rub this mixture evenly over the chicken breasts. Grill the chicken for 6-7 minutes per side, or until cooked through. Once done, set the chicken aside to cool. Step 2: Assemble the Salad In a large bowl, combine the cherry tomatoes, cucumber, red onion, Kalamata olives, feta cheese, and fresh parsley. Once the chicken has cooled, slice it into bite-sized pieces and add it to the bowl with the salad ingredients. Step 3: Prepare the Dressing In a small jar, combine the extra virgin olive oil, red wine vinegar, minced garlic, dried oregano, salt, and pepper. Shake the jar vigorously until the dressing is well combined. Step 4: Toss and Serve Drizzle the dressing over the salad and gently toss everything together until the salad is evenly coated with the dressing. Divide the salad into individual serving bowls and garnish with an extra sprinkle of feta cheese and a few fresh parsley leaves. Why You’ll Love This Salad This Low Carb Mediterranean Chicken Salad is a celebration of fresh, wholesome ingredients that come together to create a symphony of flavors and textures. Scrumptious Low Carb Coconut Flour Pancakes

These scrumptious low carb coconut flour pancakes are the perfect way to start your day. Packed with coconut goodness and light on carbs, these pancakes are a guilt-free treat that will leave you satisfied and energized. Ingredients 1/4 cup coconut flour 1/2 teaspoon baking powder 1/4 teaspoon salt 4 large eggs 1/4 cup coconut milk 2 tablespoons coconut oil, melted 1 teaspoon vanilla extract Optional: sugar-free maple syrup, fresh berries, or unsweetened shredded coconut for topping Instructions In a large bowl, whisk together the coconut flour, baking powder, and salt. In a separate bowl, beat the eggs until they are frothy. Add the coconut milk, melted coconut oil, and vanilla extract to the eggs and mix until well combined. Gradually add the wet ingredients to the dry ingredients, stirring until smooth and well combined. Let the batter sit for a few minutes to thicken up. Heat a non-stick skillet or griddle over medium heat and lightly grease with coconut oil. Pour 1/4 cup of batter onto the skillet for each pancake. Cook for 2-3 minutes, or until bubbles form on the surface of the pancake. Carefully flip the pancake and cook for an additional 2-3 minutes, or until golden brown. Repeat with the remaining batter, greasing the skillet as needed. Serve the pancakes warm, topped with a drizzle of sugar-free maple syrup, fresh berries, or a sprinkle of unsweetened shredded coconut. Why Coconut Flour?
