Easy Tuna Pasta Bake with Crunchy Topping


If you’re looking for a simple yet delicious comfort food recipe, this Easy Tuna Pasta Bake with Crunchy Topping is the perfect dish to add to your weekly meal plan. It’s a crowd-pleaser that combines the heartiness of pasta with the rich flavors of tuna and a crispy, golden topping. Whether you’re cooking for a family dinner, meal prepping for the week, or entertaining friends, this recipe will be a hit. It’s not only tasty but also quick and easy to prepare, making it ideal for both beginners and seasoned cooks alike.

Why You’ll Love This Tuna Pasta Bake

Tuna pasta bake is a classic British dish that combines the simple ingredients of pasta, tuna, and a cheesy sauce for an unbeatable flavor. However, this recipe takes things up a notch by adding a crunchy topping that gives it a satisfying texture and extra layers of flavor. This dish is a one-pan meal that brings together pantry staples with fresh ingredients to create a hearty and comforting meal.

Here are just a few reasons why you’ll love making this tuna pasta bake:

  • Quick and Easy: The preparation and cooking time are both under 1 hour. You’ll spend only about 15 minutes preparing the ingredients, and the rest of the time is spent baking.
  • Budget-Friendly: Tuna, pasta, and a few basic ingredients are all you need to create this tasty dish. It’s easy on the wallet, making it a perfect choice for tight budgets.
  • Versatile: You can make it your own by adding vegetables or changing up the cheese. Feel free to get creative with the ingredients!
  • Kid-Friendly: With its creamy texture and cheesy goodness, even picky eaters will love it.

Ingredients for Tuna Pasta Bake with Crunchy Topping

To make this delicious tuna pasta bake, you’ll need the following ingredients:

For the Pasta Bake:

  • 250g (9oz) pasta (penne, fusilli, or macaroni works best)
  • 2 cans of tuna in oil or brine, drained (around 160g each)
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 200ml (7 fl oz) milk
  • 100ml (3.4 fl oz) double cream
  • 200g (7oz) grated cheese (cheddar is best, but you can also use mozzarella or a blend)
  • 1 tablespoon Dijon mustard (optional for a little tang)
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • Salt and freshly ground black pepper, to taste
  • Fresh parsley or basil for garnish (optional)

For the Crunchy Topping:

  • 50g (1.7oz) breadcrumbs (panko works best for extra crunch)
  • 2 tablespoons grated cheese (cheddar or Parmesan)
  • 1 tablespoon butter, melted
  • Fresh herbs (optional for garnish)

How to Make Tuna Pasta Bake with Crunchy Topping

Now, let’s dive into the step-by-step process to make this easy tuna pasta bake.

Step 1: Cook the Pasta

Start by cooking the pasta. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil, then add the pasta. Cook according to the package instructions until al dente. Once cooked, drain the pasta, reserving a cup of pasta water just in case you need it later for the sauce. Set the pasta aside.

Step 2: Prepare the Tuna Sauce

While the pasta is cooking, heat a tablespoon of olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Add the finely chopped onion and cook for about 5 minutes until soft and translucent. Stir in the minced garlic and cook for another 30 seconds until fragrant.

Now, add the drained tuna to the pan. Break it up with a spoon and stir it into the onions and garlic. Cook for 2-3 minutes to allow the tuna to heat through. Once done, add the milk, double cream, and Dijon mustard (if using) to the pan. Stir everything together and bring to a gentle simmer. Allow the sauce to thicken slightly for about 5-6 minutes. Season with dried oregano, salt, and freshly ground black pepper to taste.

Once the sauce has thickened, remove from the heat and stir in the grated cheese until it melts into the sauce. If the sauce looks too thick, add a little bit of the reserved pasta water to loosen it up.

Step 3: Combine the Pasta and Sauce

In a large baking dish, combine the cooked pasta and the tuna sauce. Stir everything together, ensuring the pasta is evenly coated in the creamy sauce. If you’d like, you can also mix in some peas, sweetcorn, or chopped vegetables for extra nutrition and flavor at this stage.

Step 4: Make the Crunchy Topping

In a small bowl, mix the breadcrumbs with the grated cheese. Add the melted butter and stir until the breadcrumbs are well coated and the mixture looks crumbly. This will form the crispy, golden topping that everyone loves.

Step 5: Assemble the Bake

Spread the breadcrumb mixture evenly over the top of the pasta and sauce. This topping will bake to a crispy, golden perfection in the oven, adding the perfect crunch to the creamy dish below.

Step 6: Bake in the Oven

Preheat your oven to 180°C (350°F). Place the baking dish in the oven and bake for 25-30 minutes, or until the topping is golden brown and the pasta bake is bubbling around the edges. The cheese will melt and form a beautiful, creamy layer beneath the crunchy topping.

Step 7: Serve and Enjoy!

Once the tuna pasta bake is out of the oven, let it cool for a few minutes before serving. Garnish with fresh parsley or basil for a touch of color and flavor. Serve it with a side salad or some garlic bread for the perfect meal. Enjoy the warm, comforting flavors with family and friends!

Tips for the Best Tuna Pasta Bake

  1. Use Quality Tuna: The quality of the tuna you use will make a big difference in the flavor. Choose tuna packed in oil for a richer taste, or tuna packed in brine for a lighter option.

  2. Customizing the Cheese: While cheddar is the most common cheese used in a tuna pasta bake, feel free to experiment with different cheeses. Mozzarella adds a lovely meltiness, while Parmesan gives a nutty, salty kick.

  3. Add Veggies: If you want to increase the nutritional value, feel free to add some cooked vegetables to the mix. Frozen peas, sweetcorn, or chopped spinach are all great additions that will blend perfectly with the creamy sauce.

  4. Leftovers: This dish reheats beautifully, making it a great option for meal prepping. Store leftovers in an airtight container in the fridge for up to 3 days. You can also freeze it for up to 3 months. Just make sure to let it cool before storing.

  5. Make It Your Own: Experiment with different herbs and spices in the sauce. Adding a pinch of chili flakes will give the dish a bit of heat, while fresh basil or thyme can elevate the flavor profile.
