Taste the Sunshine with a Fresh Niçoise Salad

3 min · 574 words

Are you ready to take your taste buds on a journey to the sunny shores of the Mediterranean? Look no further than this delightful Fresh Niçoise Salad recipe! Bursting with vibrant colors, bold flavors, and wholesome ingredients, this salad is a true celebration of summer. So, grab your apron and get ready to savor the taste of sunshine!

Ingredients

  • 2 cups baby potatoes, halved
  • 4 eggs
  • 1 pound fresh green beans, trimmed
  • 2 cups cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1 cup Kalamata olives, pitted
  • 1 small red onion, thinly sliced
  • 2 cans tuna, drained
  • ¼ cup fresh parsley, chopped
  • ¼ cup fresh basil, chopped

Dressing

  • ¼ cup olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons red wine vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon Dijon mustard
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. In a large pot, bring salted water to a boil. Add the baby potatoes and cook until tender, about 10-12 minutes. Drain and set aside to cool.

  2. Meanwhile, place the eggs in a small saucepan and cover with water. Bring to a boil over medium heat, then reduce the heat and simmer for 8 minutes. Remove from heat, drain, and transfer the eggs to a bowl of ice water to cool. Once cooled, peel and quarter the eggs.

  3. In the same pot used for the potatoes, bring salted water to a boil again. Add the green beans and cook for 3-4 minutes, until crisp-tender. Drain and rinse with cold water to stop the cooking process.

  4. In a large serving bowl, combine the cooked potatoes, green beans, cherry tomatoes, Kalamata olives, red onion, and tuna. Toss gently to mix.

  5. In a small bowl, whisk together the olive oil, red wine vinegar, Dijon mustard, minced garlic, salt, and pepper until well combined. Drizzle the dressing over the salad and toss gently to coat.

  6. Sprinkle the fresh parsley and basil over the salad for an extra burst of flavor and a pop of green.

  7. Serve the Fresh Niçoise Salad chilled or at room temperature, and enjoy the vibrant flavors that will transport you to a sun-soaked beachside café in the French Riviera.

Tips and Variations

  • Feel free to add other ingredients to customize your Fresh Niçoise Salad. Some popular additions include anchovies, capers, roasted red peppers, or even grilled chicken or shrimp for a heartier meal.
  • For a vegetarian version, simply omit the tuna and add extra vegetables like artichoke hearts or roasted beets.
  • If you prefer a creamier dressing, you can mix in a tablespoon of mayonnaise or Greek yogurt.
  • This salad can be prepared in advance and stored in the refrigerator for up to two days. Just wait to add the dressing and fresh herbs until ready to serve to keep the flavors fresh and vibrant.

So, there you have it! A delicious and refreshing Fresh Niçoise Salad that will transport you to the sunny shores of the Mediterranean with every bite. It’s a perfect dish for picnics, gatherings, or simply a light and healthy meal on a hot summer day. So, go ahead and taste the sunshine with this vibrant salad!
