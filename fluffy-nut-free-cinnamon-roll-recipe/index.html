Fluffy Nut-Free Cinnamon Roll Recipe


Are you a fan of cinnamon rolls but have a nut allergy? Look no further! In this recipe, I’ll show you how to make fluffy and delicious nut-free cinnamon rolls that will satisfy your craving. These rolls are perfect for breakfast, brunch, or a sweet treat any time of the day. So let’s get started!

Ingredients

For the dough:

  • 2 ¾ cups all-purpose flour
  • ¼ cup granulated sugar
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 package (2 ¼ teaspoons) instant yeast
  • ½ cup milk, warmed
  • ¼ cup unsalted butter, melted
  • 2 large eggs

For the filling:

  • ½ cup unsalted butter, softened
  • ¾ cup brown sugar
  • 2 tablespoons ground cinnamon

For the icing:

  • 1 cup powdered sugar
  • 2 tablespoons milk
  • ½ teaspoon vanilla extract

Instructions

  1. Start by making the dough. In a large mixing bowl, combine the flour, sugar, salt, and yeast. Mix well.

  2. In another bowl, whisk together the warm milk, melted butter, and eggs. Pour the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients and mix until a soft dough forms.

  3. Transfer the dough to a lightly floured surface and knead for about 5 minutes, or until the dough is smooth and elastic.

  4. Place the dough in a greased bowl, cover with a clean kitchen towel, and let it rise in a warm place for about 1 hour, or until it doubles in size.

  5. While the dough is rising, prepare the filling. In a small bowl, mix together the softened butter, brown sugar, and ground cinnamon until well combined. Set aside.

  6. Once the dough has risen, punch it down to release any air bubbles. Transfer it to a lightly floured surface and roll it out into a 12x18-inch rectangle.

  7. Spread the cinnamon filling evenly over the dough, leaving a small border around the edges. Starting from the long end, tightly roll up the dough into a log.

  8. Cut the log into 12 equal slices and place them in a greased baking dish, leaving a little space between each roll. Cover the dish with a kitchen towel and let the rolls rise for another 30 minutes.

  9. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C). Once the rolls have risen, bake them for 20-25 minutes, or until they are golden brown and cooked through.

  10. While the rolls are baking, prepare the icing. In a small bowl, whisk together the powdered sugar, milk, and vanilla extract until smooth. Set aside.

  11. Once the rolls are done, remove them from the oven and let them cool for a few minutes. Drizzle the icing over the warm rolls, allowing it to seep into the crevices.

  12. Serve the fluffy nut-free cinnamon rolls warm and enjoy!

Tips and Variations

  • For an extra touch of flavor, you can add a teaspoon of vanilla extract to the dough.
  • If you prefer a more gooey filling, you can mix in some raisins or chopped dates with the cinnamon filling.
  • These cinnamon rolls are best enjoyed fresh, but you can store any leftovers in an airtight container at room temperature for up to 2 days.

That’s it! With this recipe, you can indulge in fluffy, nut-free cinnamon rolls that are sure to please everyone. Give it a try and let me know how it turns out. Happy baking!
