Meatball Spaghetti Soup: A Hearty and Comforting Pasta Soup


Are you looking for a comforting and satisfying meal that will warm you up on a chilly evening? Look no further than Meatball Spaghetti Soup! This delicious soup combines the flavors of tender meatballs, al dente spaghetti, and a rich tomato broth to create a hearty and comforting dish that will leave you feeling satisfied.

Ingredients

For the meatballs:

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1/2 cup breadcrumbs
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley
  • 1/4 cup chopped onion
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 1 egg, beaten
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper

For the soup:

  • 8 cups chicken or vegetable broth
  • 1 can (28 ounces) crushed tomatoes
  • 1 can (6 ounces) tomato paste
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried thyme
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 8 ounces spaghetti, broken into small pieces

Instructions

  1. In a large bowl, combine all the meatball ingredients. Mix well until everything is evenly combined.
  2. Roll the mixture into small meatballs, about 1 inch in diameter. Place them on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper.
  3. Preheat your oven to 400°F and bake the meatballs for 15-20 minutes or until they are browned and cooked through. Set aside.
  4. In a large pot, heat some olive oil over medium heat. Add the diced onion and minced garlic, and cook until they are softened and fragrant.
  5. Add the crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, dried basil, dried oregano, dried thyme, salt, and black pepper to the pot. Stir well to combine.
  6. Pour in the chicken or vegetable broth and bring the mixture to a boil. Reduce the heat and let it simmer for about 15 minutes to allow the flavors to meld together.
  7. While the soup is simmering, cook the broken spaghetti according to the package instructions until it is al dente. Drain and set aside.
  8. Add the cooked meatballs to the soup and let them simmer for an additional 5 minutes to heat through.
  9. Finally, add the cooked spaghetti to the pot and stir gently to combine. Let the soup simmer for a few more minutes to ensure everything is heated through.
  10. Serve the Meatball Spaghetti Soup hot, garnished with some freshly grated Parmesan cheese and chopped fresh parsley if desired.

Tips and Variations

  • You can use a mix of ground beef and ground pork for the meatballs for added flavor.
  • Feel free to add some chopped vegetables like carrots, celery, or bell peppers to the soup for extra nutrition and texture.
  • If you prefer a spicier soup, add some crushed red pepper flakes or a dash of hot sauce to the broth.
  • This soup is even more delicious the next day as the flavors continue to develop, so consider making a larger batch and saving some for leftovers.
  • If you’re vegetarian or vegan, you can easily make this soup by substituting the ground meat with plant-based meat alternatives or using meatless meatballs.

Conclusion

Meatball Spaghetti Soup is the perfect dish to warm you up and provide a comforting and satisfying meal. With its tender meatballs, al dente spaghetti, and flavorful tomato broth, this soup is sure to become a family favorite. Give it a try and enjoy the heartiness and comfort it brings to your table.
