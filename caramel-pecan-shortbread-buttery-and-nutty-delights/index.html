Caramel Pecan Shortbread: Buttery and Nutty Delights


Welcome to our delightful recipe blog, where we explore the world of mouth-watering treats! Today, we have a special treat for you: Caramel Pecan Shortbread. These buttery and nutty delights are guaranteed to satisfy your cravings and leave you wanting more. So, let’s dive right in!

Ingredients

For the Shortbread:

  • 1 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt

For the Caramel Pecan Topping:

  • 1 cup caramel sauce (homemade or store-bought)
  • 1 1/2 cups pecans, roughly chopped

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C). Line a 9x9-inch baking pan with parchment paper, leaving some overhang for easy removal later.

  2. In a large mixing bowl, cream together the softened butter and granulated sugar until light and fluffy. This usually takes about 2-3 minutes.

  3. Gradually add the flour and salt to the butter mixture, mixing well after each addition. The dough should come together and form a crumbly texture.

  4. Transfer the dough to the prepared baking pan. Press it down firmly and evenly, using the back of a spoon or your fingertips.

  5. Bake the shortbread crust in the preheated oven for 20-25 minutes, or until it turns golden brown around the edges. Remove from the oven and let it cool completely.

  6. Once the shortbread has cooled, evenly spread the caramel sauce over the top. Make sure to cover the entire surface.

  7. Sprinkle the chopped pecans over the caramel layer. Press them gently into the caramel, ensuring they adhere well.

  8. Place the pan back into the oven and bake for an additional 10-15 minutes, or until the caramel is bubbly and the pecans are lightly toasted.

  9. Remove from the oven and allow the caramel pecan shortbread to cool completely in the pan. This will help the caramel set and the flavors to meld together.

  10. Once cooled, use the parchment paper overhang to lift the shortbread out of the pan. Transfer it to a cutting board and slice into small squares or bars.

  11. Serve and enjoy these buttery and nutty delights with a cup of hot tea or coffee. They are perfect for indulging yourself or as a delicious treat for family and friends.

Tips and Variations

  • For an extra indulgent twist, drizzle melted chocolate over the caramel pecan shortbread before cutting into squares.
  • Feel free to experiment with different types of nuts, such as almonds or walnuts, to add your own unique touch to this recipe.
  • To make homemade caramel sauce, combine 1 cup of granulated sugar and 1/4 cup of water in a saucepan over medium heat. Stir until the sugar dissolves, then increase the heat to high and let it boil without stirring until it turns amber in color. Remove from heat and carefully whisk in 1/2 cup of heavy cream, followed by 2 tablespoons of unsalted butter. Allow the caramel sauce to cool before using.
