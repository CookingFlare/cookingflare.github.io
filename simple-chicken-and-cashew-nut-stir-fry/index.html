Simple Chicken and Cashew Nut Stir Fry


Are you looking for a quick, delicious, and nutritious meal to whip up for dinner? Look no further than this Simple Chicken and Cashew Nut Stir Fry recipe! Packed with protein, fiber, and flavors that will tantalize your taste buds, this dish is sure to become a favorite in your recipe rotation.

Ingredients:

  • 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 1 cup cashew nuts
  • 1 red bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 yellow bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 onion, sliced
  • 3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1-inch piece of ginger, grated
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon oyster sauce
  • 1 tablespoon hoisin sauce
  • 1 teaspoon sesame oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Cooking oil

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the Chicken: Season the chicken pieces with salt and pepper. In a large skillet or wok, heat some cooking oil over medium-high heat. Add the chicken and cook until browned and cooked through. Remove the chicken from the skillet and set aside.

  2. Toast the Cashews: In the same skillet, toast the cashew nuts until they are lightly browned and fragrant. Remove them from the skillet and set aside.

  3. Stir Fry Veggies: Add a bit more oil to the skillet if needed. Sauté the garlic and ginger until aromatic. Add the sliced onions and bell peppers. Stir fry until the vegetables are slightly tender yet still crisp.

  4. Combine Ingredients: Return the cooked chicken and cashew nuts to the skillet with the vegetables. Mix everything together.

  5. Add Sauces: Pour in the soy sauce, oyster sauce, hoisin sauce, and sesame oil. Stir well to coat all the ingredients evenly. Adjust the seasoning with salt and pepper according to your taste.

  6. Finish and Serve: Cook for another minute or two, allowing all the flavors to meld together. Once everything is heated through, remove from heat.

  7. Garnish and Enjoy: Garnish with some chopped green onions or cilantro for an extra burst of freshness. Serve your Simple Chicken and Cashew Nut Stir Fry hot over steamed rice or noodles.

Why You’ll Love This Recipe:

This Simple Chicken and Cashew Nut Stir Fry recipe is a fantastic option for busy weeknights when you want a homemade meal without spending hours in the kitchen. The combination of tender chicken, crunchy cashews, and colorful vegetables creates a dish that is both visually appealing and satisfying to eat.

The beauty of stir fry dishes lies in their versatility. Feel free to customize this recipe by adding your favorite vegetables like broccoli, snap peas, or mushrooms. You can also adjust the level of spice by incorporating chili flakes or sriracha for an extra kick.

Not only is this dish a treat for your taste buds, but it also provides a good balance of protein, healthy fats, and essential nutrients. It’s a great way to ensure you’re getting a well-rounded meal that will keep you feeling energized and satiated.

Next time you’re in the mood for a flavorful and fuss-free dinner option, give this Simple Chicken and Cashew Nut Stir Fry a try. It’s a simple yet satisfying dish that is sure to become a regular on your menu rotation. Happy cooking and bon appétit!
