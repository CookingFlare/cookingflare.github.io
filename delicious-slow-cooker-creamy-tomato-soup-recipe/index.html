Delicious Slow Cooker Creamy Tomato Soup Recipe


If you’re a fan of comforting, creamy soups, then you’re in for a treat with this delicious slow cooker creamy tomato soup recipe. This recipe is perfect for those cozy evenings when you crave a warm and satisfying meal without spending hours in the kitchen. The best part? It’s incredibly easy to make and will fill your home with the irresistible aroma of tomatoes and herbs. Let’s dive into the details of this delectable dish.

Ingredients

  • 1 can (28 ounces) of crushed tomatoes
  • 1 can (14 ounces) of diced tomatoes
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 4 cups of vegetable broth
  • 1 teaspoon of dried basil
  • 1 teaspoon of dried oregano
  • 1/2 teaspoon of red pepper flakes (adjust to taste)
  • 1/4 cup of all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup of unsalted butter
  • 1 cup of heavy cream
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh basil leaves for garnish

Instructions

  1. In a slow cooker, combine the crushed tomatoes, diced tomatoes, diced onion, minced garlic, vegetable broth, dried basil, dried oregano, and red pepper flakes. Stir to mix the ingredients evenly.

  2. Cover the slow cooker and cook the mixture on low heat for 6-8 hours, allowing the flavors to meld together and the vegetables to soften.

  3. About 30 minutes before the end of the cooking time, prepare the creamy base for the soup. In a small saucepan, melt the butter over medium heat. Once melted, whisk in the flour to create a roux. Cook the roux for 2-3 minutes, stirring constantly to prevent burning.

  4. Gradually pour in the heavy cream, whisking continuously to create a smooth, creamy mixture. Allow the mixture to simmer for a few minutes until it thickens slightly.

  5. Carefully transfer the creamy mixture from the saucepan into the slow cooker. Stir well to combine the creamy base with the tomato mixture. Season the soup with salt and pepper to taste.

  6. Continue cooking the soup for the remaining 30 minutes, allowing the flavors to fully meld and the soup to thicken slightly.

  7. Once the soup is ready, ladle it into bowls and garnish with fresh basil leaves for a pop of color and added freshness.

Serving Suggestions

This slow cooker creamy tomato soup pairs beautifully with a variety of accompaniments. Consider serving it with a slice of crusty bread, a sprinkle of grated Parmesan cheese, or a dollop of sour cream for added richness. For a heartier meal, you can also pair the soup with a grilled cheese sandwich or a simple green salad.

Final Thoughts

There’s something truly special about a bowl of creamy tomato soup that warms both the body and soul. With this slow cooker recipe, you can enjoy the rich, velvety texture and vibrant flavors of tomato soup without the fuss. Whether it’s a busy weeknight or a laid-back weekend, this recipe promises to deliver a bowl of comfort and satisfaction with every spoonful. So, gather your ingredients, set up your slow cooker, and let the magic of creamy tomato soup unfold in your kitchen.
