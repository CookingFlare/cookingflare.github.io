Satisfying Beef and Cheddar Sliders: Perfect for Game Day


Are you ready to elevate your game day spread with a mouthwatering dish that will have your friends and family cheering for more? Look no further than these satisfying beef and cheddar sliders. Packed with flavor and easy to eat, these sliders are the perfect addition to any game day gathering. Whether you’re hosting a big game party or just enjoying a casual get-together, these sliders are sure to be a hit.

Ingredients

Before we dive into the recipe, let’s take a look at the ingredients you’ll need to create these delectable sliders:

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon onion powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 12 slider buns
  • 12 slices cheddar cheese
  • 1/4 cup unsalted butter, melted
  • 1 tablespoon Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 tablespoon Dijon mustard
  • 1 tablespoon brown sugar
  • 1 tablespoon sesame seeds
  • Optional toppings: lettuce, tomato, pickles, and your favorite condiments

Instructions

Step 1: Prepare the Beef

In a large mixing bowl, combine the ground beef, garlic powder, onion powder, salt, and pepper. Mix the ingredients thoroughly to ensure the seasonings are evenly distributed throughout the beef.

Step 2: Form the Patties

Divide the seasoned beef mixture into 12 equal portions and shape them into small patties, slightly larger than the size of the slider buns. Press your thumb into the center of each patty to prevent it from bulging during cooking.

Step 3: Cook the Patties

Preheat a skillet or grill pan over medium-high heat. Place the beef patties on the heated surface and cook for 3-4 minutes on each side, or until they reach your desired level of doneness. During the last minute of cooking, place a slice of cheddar cheese on each patty and allow it to melt.

Step 4: Assemble the Sliders

While the patties are cooking, prepare the slider buns by slicing them in half. In a small bowl, whisk together the melted butter, Worcestershire sauce, Dijon mustard, and brown sugar. Brush the butter mixture onto the cut sides of the slider buns.

Step 5: Toast the Buns

Place the buttered slider buns, cut side down, on the skillet or grill pan and toast them until they are golden brown and slightly crisp.

Step 6: Build the Sliders

To assemble the sliders, place a cheddar-topped beef patty on the bottom half of each toasted bun. Add any optional toppings, such as lettuce, tomato, pickles, or condiments, according to your preference. Top each slider with the remaining half of the bun.

Step 7: Serve and Enjoy!

Once the sliders are assembled, transfer them to a serving platter and sprinkle the tops with sesame seeds for an extra touch of flavor and visual appeal. These sliders are best enjoyed hot and fresh, so serve them immediately and watch them disappear in no time.

Conclusion

With their savory beef, gooey cheddar cheese, and flavorful toppings, these beef and cheddar sliders are guaranteed to be a game day winner. Whether you’re rooting for your favorite team or simply enjoying good company, these sliders are the perfect bite-sized treat to keep everyone satisfied. So, fire up the grill, gather your ingredients, and get ready to score big with these delicious sliders on game day!
