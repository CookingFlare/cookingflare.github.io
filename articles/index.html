Healthy One Pot Quinoa and Vegetable Stir Fry

Are you looking for a delicious and nutritious meal that can be prepared quickly and with minimal cleanup? Look no further than this Healthy One Pot Quinoa and Vegetable Stir Fry recipe! Packed with vibrant colors, flavors, and nutrients, this dish is a perfect option for those looking to incorporate more plant-based meals into their diet. Ingredients 1 cup quinoa 2 cups water 2 tablespoons olive oil 1 onion, thinly sliced 2 cloves garlic, minced 1 red bell pepper, thinly sliced 1 yellow bell pepper, thinly sliced 1 zucchini, thinly sliced 1 cup broccoli florets 1 cup snap peas 1 cup mushrooms, sliced 1 tablespoon soy sauce 1 tablespoon sesame oil 1 tablespoon rice vinegar Salt and pepper to taste Instructions Rinse the quinoa under cold water and drain well. In a large pot, bring the water to a boil. Add the quinoa and reduce the heat to low. Cover and simmer for about 15 minutes, or until the quinoa is tender and the water is absorbed. Remove from heat and set aside. In the same pot, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the onion and garlic and sauté until the onion is translucent and fragrant. Add the bell peppers, zucchini, broccoli, snap peas, and mushrooms to the pot. Stir fry for about 5 minutes, or until the vegetables are tender-crisp. In a small bowl, whisk together the soy sauce, sesame oil, and rice vinegar. Pour the sauce over the vegetables in the pot and stir well to coat. Add the cooked quinoa to the pot and toss everything together until well combined. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Cover the pot and let the stir fry sit for a few minutes to allow the flavors to meld together. Serve hot and enjoy! One-Pot Mediterranean Pasta: Easy and Flavorful Recipes

Are you looking for a delicious and hassle-free dinner option that brings the flavors of the Mediterranean right to your table? Look no further than these easy and flavorful one-pot Mediterranean pasta recipes. Packed with fresh ingredients, aromatic herbs, and rich olive oil, these dishes are sure to transport your taste buds to the sunny shores of the Mediterranean. Recipe 1: Classic Mediterranean Veggie Pasta Ingredients: 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved 1 zucchini, sliced 1 yellow bell pepper, diced 1 red onion, thinly sliced 3 cloves garlic, minced 1 teaspoon dried oregano 1 teaspoon dried basil 1/2 teaspoon red pepper flakes Salt and pepper to taste 8 oz penne pasta 3 cups vegetable broth 1/4 cup black olives, sliced 2 tablespoons capers Fresh basil leaves for garnish Instructions: In a large pot, combine cherry tomatoes, zucchini, bell pepper, red onion, garlic, oregano, basil, red pepper flakes, salt, and pepper. Add penne pasta and vegetable broth to the pot. Bring to a simmer over medium heat. Cover and cook for about 15-20 minutes, stirring occasionally until the pasta is cooked and most of the liquid is absorbed. Stir in black olives and capers. Adjust seasoning if needed. Serve hot, garnished with fresh basil leaves. Recipe 2: Lemon and Herb Shrimp Mediterranean Pasta Ingredients: 8 oz spaghetti 1 lb shrimp, peeled and deveined 1 lemon, zested and juiced 4 cloves garlic, minced 1 teaspoon dried thyme 1 teaspoon dried rosemary 1/2 teaspoon paprika Salt and pepper to taste 2 cups cherry tomatoes, halved 1/2 cup feta cheese, crumbled Fresh parsley for garnish Instructions: Cook spaghetti according to package instructions. Drain and set aside. In the same pot, sauté shrimp until pink and cooked through. Remove from the pot and set aside. In the pot, add lemon zest, lemon juice, garlic, thyme, rosemary, paprika, salt, and pepper. Cook for a minute. Add cherry tomatoes and cook until slightly softened. Return shrimp to the pot, along with cooked spaghetti. Toss everything together. Sprinkle feta cheese over the pasta. Serve hot, garnished with fresh parsley. Recipe 3: Sun-Dried Tomato and Artichoke Mediterranean Pasta Ingredients: 10 oz fusilli pasta 1/2 cup sun-dried tomatoes, chopped 1 can artichoke hearts, drained and quartered 1/4 cup pine nuts 2 cloves garlic, minced 1 teaspoon dried basil 1/2 teaspoon dried thyme Red pepper flakes to taste 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese Fresh basil leaves for garnish Instructions: Cook fusilli pasta according to package instructions. Drain and set aside. In a large skillet, toast pine nuts until lightly browned. Remove from the skillet and set aside. In the same skillet, sauté garlic until fragrant. Add sun-dried tomatoes, artichoke hearts, basil, thyme, and red pepper flakes. Toss in the cooked pasta and pine nuts. Mix well. Sprinkle Parmesan cheese over the pasta. Serve hot, garnished with fresh basil leaves. These one-pot Mediterranean pasta recipes are not only easy to make but also bursting with Mediterranean flavors.
