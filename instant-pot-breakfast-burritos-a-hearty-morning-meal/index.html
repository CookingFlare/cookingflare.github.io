Instant Pot Breakfast Burritos: A Hearty Morning Meal


Breakfast is often said to be the most important meal of the day, but it can also be the most rushed. Between getting the kids ready, rushing off to work, or simply waking up on the wrong side of the bed, making a nutritious and filling breakfast can seem like an overwhelming task. Enter the Instant Pot, the kitchen appliance that has revolutionized cooking by reducing cooking times while still delivering delicious, flavorful meals. One of the best ways to use your Instant Pot in the morning is to whip up hearty and satisfying breakfast burritos. Whether you’re feeding a hungry family or meal prepping for a busy week ahead, Instant Pot breakfast burritos are a game-changer.

Why Instant Pot Breakfast Burritos?

You may wonder what makes breakfast burritos such a great choice for a morning meal. Let’s break it down:

  • Quick and Easy: Preparing a hearty breakfast doesn’t need to take hours in the kitchen. The Instant Pot speeds up cooking time, allowing you to create a filling meal in a fraction of the time compared to traditional methods.
  • Customizable: Breakfast burritos are incredibly versatile, allowing you to add a variety of ingredients based on your preferences. Whether you’re a fan of bacon, sausage, or a meatless breakfast, there’s an option for everyone.
  • Meal Prep-Friendly: These burritos freeze well, making them ideal for meal prep. You can prepare a large batch of breakfast burritos and store them in the freezer for a quick grab-and-go breakfast during the week.
  • Hearty and Satisfying: Packed with protein, fiber, and healthy fats, breakfast burritos will keep you full until lunch, providing the energy you need to power through your morning.

Now that we’ve established why breakfast burritos are such a great option, let’s dive into the recipe.

The Ultimate Instant Pot Breakfast Burrito Recipe

This recipe will guide you through making delicious and hearty breakfast burritos using your Instant Pot. The combination of scrambled eggs, sausage, potatoes, and cheese wrapped in a warm tortilla makes for a balanced and satisfying meal.

Ingredients:

For the filling:

  • 1 lb breakfast sausage (or your choice of protein)
  • 1 medium onion, diced
  • 1 bell pepper, diced
  • 1 large potato, diced into small cubes
  • 1 cup shredded cheddar cheese (or cheese of your choice)
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • 8 large eggs
  • 1/4 cup milk (optional for creamier eggs)

For the assembly:

  • 8 large flour tortillas
  • Salsa, guacamole, or sour cream for serving (optional)

Instructions:

Step 1: Prepare the Potatoes

Start by preparing the potatoes in your Instant Pot. The Instant Pot is fantastic for cooking potatoes quickly while retaining their natural flavors.

  1. Sauté the Potatoes: Turn on the Instant Pot and set it to the “Sauté” mode. Add a tablespoon of olive oil or butter to the pot and let it heat up. Once hot, add the diced potatoes. Sauté them for about 5 minutes, stirring occasionally, until they are lightly browned.

  2. Pressure Cook the Potatoes: Add 1/2 cup of water to the Instant Pot and secure the lid. Set the pressure cooker to “Manual” mode for 4 minutes. After the cooking time is up, do a quick release to release the steam.

  3. Set the Potatoes Aside: Carefully remove the potatoes from the Instant Pot and set them aside.

Step 2: Cook the Sausage and Veggies

Now that your potatoes are ready, it’s time to prepare the sausage and vegetables.

  1. Sauté the Sausage: Without draining the Instant Pot, switch back to the “Sauté” mode and add the breakfast sausage. Break it up with a spatula as it cooks. Sauté until the sausage is browned and fully cooked, about 5-7 minutes.

  2. Add the Vegetables: Once the sausage is browned, add the diced onion and bell pepper to the pot. Sauté for an additional 3-4 minutes until the vegetables soften and become fragrant.

  3. Season: Add the garlic powder, paprika, salt, and pepper to the sausage and vegetable mixture. Stir well to coat the ingredients evenly with the spices.

Step 3: Scramble the Eggs

While the sausage and vegetables are cooking, it’s time to scramble the eggs.

  1. Whisk the Eggs: Crack the eggs into a bowl, add the milk (if using), and whisk until well combined. Season with a pinch of salt and pepper.

  2. Scramble in the Instant Pot: Once the sausage and veggies are ready, turn off the Instant Pot and add the scrambled eggs to the pot. Stir everything together until the eggs are cooked through and scrambled to your liking, about 2-3 minutes.

  3. Add the Potatoes: Finally, add the cooked potatoes back into the Instant Pot and mix everything well. The result should be a hearty and flavorful filling for your breakfast burritos.

Step 4: Assemble the Burritos

Now that your filling is ready, it’s time to assemble the burritos.

  1. Warm the Tortillas: Warm the flour tortillas in the microwave for about 15-20 seconds or on a hot griddle for about 30 seconds on each side. This makes the tortillas more pliable and easier to roll.

  2. Assemble: Spoon a generous amount of the breakfast mixture onto each tortilla, and sprinkle with shredded cheddar cheese. Feel free to add extra toppings like salsa, sour cream, or avocado for extra flavor.

  3. Roll the Burritos: Fold in the sides of the tortilla, then roll from the bottom up to form a tight burrito. Repeat for the remaining tortillas.

Step 5: Optional – Toast the Burritos

For an extra crispy touch, you can toast the burritos before serving.

  1. Toast in a Skillet: Heat a skillet over medium heat. Place each burrito seam-side down in the skillet and cook for 1-2 minutes on each side until the tortilla is golden brown and crispy.

  2. Serve and Enjoy: Serve the burritos immediately with your favorite toppings, such as salsa, guacamole, or sour cream. Enjoy!

Variations and Tips

One of the best things about breakfast burritos is their versatility. You can customize them based on your preferences, dietary restrictions, or what you have in your fridge. Here are a few ideas:

  • Vegetarian Option: If you prefer a meatless breakfast, you can easily swap out the sausage for plant-based alternatives, such as veggie sausage or sautéed mushrooms. You can also add spinach, zucchini, or tomatoes for more flavor and nutrition.
  • Cheese Options: While cheddar cheese is a classic choice, you can mix things up with Monterey Jack, pepper jack, or even a creamy blend of cheeses for added richness.
  • Spicy Kick: For a little heat, try adding diced jalapeños or hot sauce to the filling mixture. You can also sprinkle some red pepper flakes over the eggs for an extra layer of spice.
  • Gluten-Free Option: If you’re gluten-free, simply use gluten-free tortillas instead of regular flour tortillas.

Make-Ahead and Freezing Instructions

Breakfast burritos are perfect for meal prepping, especially for busy mornings when you don’t have time to cook. To make-ahead, simply follow the recipe as usual but without the final step of toasting the burritos.

  1. Cool the Burritos: After assembling the burritos, allow them to cool completely before wrapping them in foil or parchment paper.

  2. Freeze: Place the wrapped burritos in a large freezer-safe bag or container. These burritos can be stored in the freezer for up to 3 months.

  3. Reheat: To reheat, remove the foil or parchment paper, wrap the burrito in a damp paper towel, and microwave for 1-2 minutes until heated through. Alternatively, you can heat them in a skillet for a few minutes on each side until warm.

Why You Should Make Instant Pot Breakfast Burritos

There are a few key reasons why you should consider making Instant Pot breakfast burritos part of your morning routine:

  • Saves Time: The Instant Pot’s pressure cooking feature reduces cooking time significantly. What would normally take over an hour to prepare can be completed in under 30 minutes with the Instant Pot.
  • Satisfying and Filling: The combination of protein, vegetables, and fiber-packed potatoes in each burrito makes it a wholesome and filling breakfast option that will keep you energized for hours.
  • Customizable for Every Taste: From vegan-friendly fillings to spicy add-ins, breakfast burritos can be adjusted to suit any taste or dietary need.
  • Great for Meal Prep: Whether you make a batch for the whole week or freeze a few for later, breakfast burritos are ideal for meal prepping. Just grab one in the morning, reheat, and enjoy.

In conclusion, Instant Pot breakfast burritos are a quick, customizable, and satisfying way to start your day. By using the Instant Pot, you can enjoy a delicious, hearty breakfast in no time, even on your busiest mornings. So, why not give this recipe a try and make your mornings easier and tastier?
