Fluffy Blueberry Pancake Recipes for a Burst of Flavor


Are you craving a delicious and fluffy breakfast treat? Look no further than these mouthwatering blueberry pancake recipes that are sure to satisfy your taste buds. Bursting with fresh blueberries and a hint of sweetness, these pancakes are the perfect way to start your day. So grab your apron and get ready to whip up a stack of fluffy goodness!

Classic Blueberry Pancakes

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons granulated sugar
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup buttermilk
  • 1 large egg
  • 2 tablespoons unsalted butter, melted
  • 1 cup fresh blueberries
  • Maple syrup, for serving

Instructions:

  1. In a large bowl, whisk together the flour, sugar, baking powder, baking soda, and salt.
  2. In a separate bowl, whisk together the buttermilk, egg, and melted butter.
  3. Pour the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients and gently stir until just combined. Be careful not to overmix, as this can lead to dense pancakes.
  4. Gently fold in the blueberries.
  5. Heat a non-stick skillet or griddle over medium heat. Drop 1/4 cup portions of batter onto the skillet and spread it out slightly with the back of a spoon.
  6. Cook until bubbles form on the surface of the pancakes, then flip and cook for an additional 1-2 minutes, or until golden brown.
  7. Serve the pancakes warm with a drizzle of maple syrup.

Lemon Blueberry Pancakes

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons granulated sugar
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup buttermilk
  • 1 large egg
  • 2 tablespoons unsalted butter, melted
  • Zest of 1 lemon
  • 1 cup fresh blueberries
  • Powdered sugar, for dusting

Instructions:

  1. In a large bowl, whisk together the flour, sugar, baking powder, baking soda, and salt.
  2. In a separate bowl, whisk together the buttermilk, egg, melted butter, and lemon zest.
  3. Pour the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients and gently stir until just combined.
  4. Gently fold in the blueberries.
  5. Heat a non-stick skillet or griddle over medium heat. Drop 1/4 cup portions of batter onto the skillet and spread it out slightly with the back of a spoon.
  6. Cook until bubbles form on the surface of the pancakes, then flip and cook for an additional 1-2 minutes, or until golden brown.
  7. Dust the pancakes with powdered sugar before serving.

Conclusion

Whether you prefer the classic version, a tangy twist with lemon, or a healthier option with whole wheat, these fluffy blueberry pancake recipes are sure to become a breakfast favorite. The burst of flavor from the blueberries combined with the light and airy texture of the pancakes will leave you craving more. So why wait? Treat yourself to a stack of these delicious pancakes and start your day off right!
