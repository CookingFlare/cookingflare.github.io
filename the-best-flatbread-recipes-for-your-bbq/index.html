The Best Flatbread Recipes for Your BBQ


Flatbreads are a fantastic addition to any BBQ spread. They are versatile, easy to make, and can be paired with a wide variety of toppings, dips, and grilled meats. Whether you’re looking for something to serve alongside your main course or as a fun appetizer, flatbreads can add a unique and flavorful twist to your BBQ feast. In this post, we’ll explore some of the best flatbread recipes that will elevate your BBQ game and impress your guests.

Why Flatbread is Perfect for BBQs

Flatbreads are a great choice for BBQs for several reasons:

  • Quick to cook: Flatbreads cook quickly on the grill, making them ideal for outdoor cooking when you want something fast and flavorful.
  • Customizable: You can top flatbreads with a variety of ingredients, from savory to sweet, depending on your preferences and the theme of your BBQ.
  • Portable: Flatbreads are easy to serve and eat, making them ideal for casual gatherings where guests may be mingling and moving around.
  • Pair well with grilled meats: The soft texture of flatbread pairs perfectly with grilled meats, vegetables, and sauces, making them an excellent side or wrap.

Now, let’s dive into some of the best flatbread recipes you can make for your BBQ.

1. Classic Mediterranean Flatbread

This Mediterranean flatbread recipe is simple and flavorful, featuring a combination of olive oil, fresh herbs, and a dash of sea salt. It’s the perfect side to accompany grilled meats or to enjoy with a tangy yogurt dip.

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon active dry yeast
  • 3/4 cup warm water
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • 1 tablespoon dried oregano
  • Olive oil for brushing
  • Sea salt for sprinkling

Instructions:

  1. Activate the yeast: In a small bowl, mix the warm water and sugar. Sprinkle the active dry yeast on top and let it sit for about 5 minutes until it becomes frothy.
  2. Prepare the dough: In a large bowl, combine the flour and salt. Make a well in the center and pour in the yeast mixture and olive oil. Mix everything together until a dough forms.
  3. Knead the dough: Transfer the dough to a floured surface and knead for about 5–7 minutes, until it becomes smooth and elastic.
  4. Rest the dough: Place the dough in a lightly oiled bowl, cover it with a clean kitchen towel, and let it rise for about 1 hour or until it has doubled in size.
  5. Shape the flatbreads: Once the dough has risen, punch it down and divide it into 4 equal portions. Roll each portion into a ball and then flatten it into a round disk about 1/4 inch thick.
  6. Grill the flatbreads: Preheat the grill to medium-high heat. Brush each flatbread with olive oil and sprinkle with oregano and sea salt. Place the flatbreads on the grill and cook for about 2–3 minutes per side until golden brown with grill marks.
  7. Serve: Remove the flatbreads from the grill and serve warm with your favorite dipping sauce or alongside grilled meats.

This flatbread is soft, aromatic, and full of Mediterranean flavors. It’s perfect for any BBQ, especially when paired with grilled lamb or chicken.

2. Garlic and Herb Flatbread

Garlic and herbs make for a delightful and fragrant topping for flatbread. This recipe infuses the dough with garlic, then brushes it with a buttery, herb-infused oil for an irresistible result.

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon instant yeast
  • 3/4 cup warm water
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 tablespoon sugar
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons fresh rosemary or thyme, chopped
  • 1/4 cup unsalted butter, melted

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the dough: In a large bowl, combine the flour, instant yeast, salt, and sugar. Pour in the warm water and olive oil, mixing until a dough forms. Knead the dough for 5–7 minutes on a floured surface until it becomes smooth and elastic.
  2. Rest the dough: Place the dough in an oiled bowl, cover it with a kitchen towel, and let it rise for about 1 hour.
  3. Make the garlic herb butter: In a small bowl, melt the butter and mix in the minced garlic and chopped herbs.
  4. Shape the dough: Punch down the dough and divide it into 4 portions. Roll each portion into a ball, then flatten it into a round shape about 1/4 inch thick.
  5. Grill the flatbreads: Preheat your grill to medium-high heat. Brush each flatbread with the garlic herb butter, then place them on the grill. Cook for 2–3 minutes per side until golden brown and crispy on the edges.
  6. Serve: Remove the flatbreads from the grill and brush with more garlic herb butter. Serve immediately as a delicious side or appetizer.

The garlic and herb infusion in this flatbread gives it a burst of flavor that’s perfect when paired with grilled steaks, sausages, or even roasted vegetables.

3. Naan Flatbread

Naan is a classic Indian flatbread that’s soft and pillowy, often served with curries and grilled meats. This recipe will show you how to make naan on the grill, giving it a smoky flavor that’s perfect for BBQs.

Ingredients:

  • 3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon instant yeast
  • 3/4 cup warm water
  • 1/4 cup plain yogurt
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 tablespoon sugar
  • 2 tablespoons melted butter (for brushing)

Instructions:

  1. Activate the yeast: Combine the warm water, sugar, and yeast in a small bowl. Let it sit for about 5 minutes until it becomes frothy.
  2. Prepare the dough: In a large bowl, mix together the flour and salt. Make a well in the center and add the yogurt, olive oil, and yeast mixture. Stir until the dough comes together.
  3. Knead the dough: Transfer the dough to a floured surface and knead for 5–7 minutes until it becomes soft and elastic. Place the dough in an oiled bowl, cover it with a towel, and let it rise for 1 hour.
  4. Shape the naan: Punch down the dough and divide it into 6 equal portions. Roll each portion into a ball and flatten into an oval or round shape, about 1/4 inch thick.
  5. Grill the naan: Preheat your grill to medium-high heat. Brush each naan with melted butter and place it on the grill. Grill each side for about 2–3 minutes until golden brown with charred spots.
  6. Serve: Brush the naan with additional melted butter and serve warm with your favorite grilled meats or dips.

This naan is incredibly soft, with a slightly chewy texture that’s perfect for wrapping around grilled chicken or lamb kebabs. It’s a great way to add an exotic touch to your BBQ.

4. Sweet Cinnamon Flatbread

For a sweet twist, try making a cinnamon flatbread. This recipe uses the same dough as the classic Mediterranean flatbread, but with the addition of cinnamon sugar and a touch of honey, it becomes a dessert flatbread that pairs wonderfully with fruit and whipped cream.

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon active dry yeast
  • 3/4 cup warm water
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 tablespoon sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 cup brown sugar
  • 2 tablespoons honey
  • Butter for brushing

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the dough: In a bowl, combine warm water, sugar, and yeast. Let it sit for 5 minutes to activate. In a separate bowl, mix the flour and salt. Add the yeast mixture and olive oil to the flour and mix until a dough forms.
  2. Knead the dough: Knead the dough for 5–7 minutes on a floured surface, until it becomes smooth. Cover and let it rise for 1 hour.
  3. Shape the dough: Divide the dough into 4 portions. Roll each portion into a ball and flatten into a round shape.
  4. Grill the flatbread: Preheat the grill to medium-high heat. Brush each flatbread with melted butter and sprinkle with cinnamon sugar. Grill for 2–3 minutes on each side until golden brown and slightly crispy.
  5. Drizzle with honey: After grilling, drizzle the warm flatbreads with honey and serve.

This sweet flatbread is a perfect treat for BBQs with a dessert option, and it pairs well with fresh berries or vanilla ice cream for an indulgent finish.

5. Veggie Flatbread

For a fresh, healthy option, a veggie flatbread with roasted vegetables is a fantastic choice. You can customize the toppings based on what’s in season, but this recipe features a combination of roasted bell peppers, onions, and zucchini, along with a tangy feta cheese topping.

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon active dry yeast
  • 3/4 cup warm water
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • 1 cup mixed bell peppers, sliced
  • 1/2 cup zucchini, sliced
  • 1/2 red onion, sliced
  • 1/4 cup feta cheese, crumbled
  • Olive oil for brushing
  • Fresh herbs for garnish (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Activate the yeast: Combine warm water, sugar, and yeast in a bowl and let it sit for about 5 minutes.
  2. Prepare the dough: Mix flour, salt, and yeast mixture in a large bowl. Add olive oil and knead for 5–7 minutes until smooth.
  3. Rest the dough: Let the dough rise for 1 hour.
  4. Prepare the veggies: Roast the bell peppers, zucchini, and onion with a drizzle of olive oil and salt at 400°F for 20 minutes until tender.
  5. Shape and top the flatbreads: Roll out the dough and top with roasted veggies and crumbled feta cheese. Grill for 2–3 minutes on each side until golden brown.
  6. Serve: Garnish with fresh herbs and serve warm.

This veggie flatbread is healthy, colorful, and packed with flavors from the roasted vegetables and feta. It makes for a great vegetarian option at any BBQ.

Final Thoughts

Flatbreads are an incredible addition to your BBQ spread, and with these recipes, you can easily impress your guests with delicious, homemade creations. Whether you opt for savory or sweet, Mediterranean or more adventurous flavors, flatbreads are sure to enhance your BBQ experience. Get creative with toppings, and experiment with different flavors to find your perfect flatbread recipe. The possibilities are endless!
