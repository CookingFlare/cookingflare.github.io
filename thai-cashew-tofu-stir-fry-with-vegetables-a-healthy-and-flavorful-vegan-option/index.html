Thai Cashew Tofu Stir-Fry with Vegetables: A Healthy and Flavorful Vegan Option


Are you looking for a delicious and nutritious vegan dish that is packed with flavor? Look no further than this Thai Cashew Tofu Stir-Fry with Vegetables! This vibrant and satisfying stir-fry is not only a treat for your taste buds but also a great way to incorporate more plant-based protein into your diet. With a perfect balance of textures and flavors, this dish is sure to become a family favorite.

Ingredients

  • 1 block of firm tofu, drained and pressed
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce (or tamari for gluten-free option)
  • 2 tablespoons cornstarch
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1 red bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 yellow bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 medium carrot, julienned
  • 1 cup broccoli florets
  • 1/2 cup snow peas
  • 1/2 cup cashews

Sauce

  • 3 tablespoons soy sauce (or tamari for gluten-free option)
  • 2 tablespoons maple syrup
  • 2 tablespoons rice vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon lime juice
  • 1 tablespoon sriracha (adjust to taste)
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon fresh ginger, grated

Instructions

  1. Start by preparing the tofu. Cut the drained and pressed tofu into bite-sized cubes. In a shallow bowl, combine 2 tablespoons of soy sauce and cornstarch. Toss the tofu in the mixture until all the pieces are evenly coated.

  2. Heat 2 tablespoons of vegetable oil in a large skillet or wok over medium-high heat. Add the tofu cubes and cook for 5-7 minutes, flipping occasionally, until they are golden brown and crispy on all sides. Remove the tofu from the skillet and set aside.

  3. In the same skillet, add the sliced bell peppers, julienned carrot, broccoli florets, and snow peas. Stir-fry the vegetables for about 5 minutes until they are tender-crisp.

  4. While the vegetables are cooking, prepare the sauce. In a small bowl, whisk together the soy sauce, maple syrup, rice vinegar, lime juice, sriracha, minced garlic, and grated ginger until well combined.

  5. Once the vegetables are cooked to your liking, add the cooked tofu back to the skillet. Pour the sauce over the tofu and vegetables, stirring gently to coat everything evenly. Cook for an additional 2-3 minutes until the sauce has thickened slightly.

  6. Finally, add the cashews to the skillet and toss everything together. The cashews will add a delightful crunch to the stir-fry.

  7. Serve the Thai Cashew Tofu Stir-Fry with Vegetables hot over a bed of steamed rice or noodles. Garnish with fresh cilantro or chopped green onions for an extra burst of flavor.

Health Benefits

This Thai Cashew Tofu Stir-Fry with Vegetables is not only a tasty and satisfying meal but also a healthy choice for those following a vegan or plant-based diet. Tofu serves as an excellent source of plant-based protein, while the colorful assortment of vegetables provides essential vitamins, minerals, and fiber. The cashews contribute healthy fats, and the stir-fry cooking method helps retain the nutrients in the vegetables.

Conclusion

With its vibrant colors, bold flavors, and wholesome ingredients, this Thai Cashew Tofu Stir-Fry with Vegetables is a must-try recipe for both vegans and non-vegans alike. It’s a versatile dish that can be customized with your favorite vegetables, and the homemade sauce adds a tangy and slightly spicy kick. So why not give it a try and experience the deliciousness of this healthy and flavorful vegan option? Enjoy!
