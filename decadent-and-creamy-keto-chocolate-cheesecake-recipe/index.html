Decadent and Creamy Keto Chocolate Cheesecake Recipe


If you’re a fan of chocolate and cheesecake, then this recipe is for you! This Decadent and Creamy Keto Chocolate Cheesecake is the perfect indulgence for those following a ketogenic lifestyle. With its rich chocolate flavor and velvety smooth texture, it’s hard to believe that this dessert is low in carbs and sugar-free. Get ready to satisfy your sweet tooth without compromising your diet!

Ingredients

For the crust:

  • 1 ½ cups almond flour
  • ¼ cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 2 tablespoons erythritol (or your preferred keto-friendly sweetener)
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • 4 tablespoons melted butter

For the filling:

  • 16 oz cream cheese, softened
  • ¾ cup powdered erythritol (or your preferred keto-friendly sweetener)
  • ½ cup heavy cream
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • ½ cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 4 oz sugar-free dark chocolate, melted and cooled
  • 3 large eggs

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 325°F (163°C).
  2. In a mixing bowl, combine the almond flour, cocoa powder, erythritol, and salt for the crust. Mix well until all the ingredients are evenly combined.
  3. Add the melted butter to the dry ingredients and mix until the mixture resembles coarse crumbs. Press the crust mixture into the bottom of a 9-inch springform pan, evenly covering the surface.
  4. Place the pan in the preheated oven and bake for 10 minutes. Remove from the oven and set aside to cool while you prepare the filling.
  5. In a large mixing bowl, beat the softened cream cheese until smooth and creamy. Add the powdered erythritol, heavy cream, and vanilla extract. Mix until well combined and smooth.
  6. Add the unsweetened cocoa powder to the cream cheese mixture and beat until fully incorporated.
  7. Gradually add the melted dark chocolate to the mixture, mixing well after each addition.
  8. Finally, add the eggs, one at a time, mixing just until combined after each addition. Be careful not to overmix.
  9. Pour the filling mixture over the crust in the springform pan, spreading it evenly.
  10. Bake the cheesecake in the preheated oven for 50-60 minutes, or until the center is set and the edges are slightly puffed.
  11. Once done, turn off the oven and leave the cheesecake inside with the door slightly ajar for 1 hour. This will help prevent cracking.
  12. Remove the cheesecake from the oven and let it cool completely at room temperature. Once cooled, refrigerate for at least 4 hours or overnight before serving.
  13. When ready to serve, carefully remove the sides of the springform pan. Slice and enjoy!

Conclusion

Indulge in the decadence of this Creamy Keto Chocolate Cheesecake without guilt! With its rich chocolate flavor, velvety smooth texture, and low-carb ingredients, this dessert is a perfect treat for those following a ketogenic lifestyle. Make it for special occasions or whenever you’re craving something sweet. Enjoy a slice (or two) of this delicious cheesecake and savor the satisfaction of staying on track with your keto journey. Happy baking!
