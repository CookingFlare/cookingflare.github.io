Easy Fourth of July Dessert Recipes for BBQ


The Fourth of July is a time for celebration, and what better way to round off a delicious BBQ than with some mouthwatering desserts? Whether you’re hosting a big gathering or having a small family get-together, these easy Fourth of July dessert recipes are sure to impress your guests and satisfy their sweet tooth. From classic American treats to refreshing summer delights, these recipes will add a festive touch to your Independence Day celebration.

Red, White, and Blueberry Trifle

Ingredients:

  • 1 store-bought angel food cake, cut into cubes
  • 1 cup fresh blueberries
  • 1 cup fresh strawberries, sliced
  • 2 cups vanilla pudding
  • 1 cup whipped cream
  • Fresh mint leaves for garnish

Instructions:

  1. In a trifle dish or a clear glass bowl, layer the bottom with the angel food cake cubes.
  2. Add a layer of vanilla pudding, followed by a layer of blueberries and strawberries.
  3. Repeat the layers until the trifle dish is filled, finishing with a layer of whipped cream on top.
  4. Garnish with additional blueberries, strawberries, and mint leaves.
  5. Chill for at least 2 hours before serving.

This delightful trifle is not only visually stunning with its patriotic colors but also a refreshing and light dessert that’s perfect for a summer BBQ.

Grilled Peaches with Honey and Yogurt

Ingredients:

  • 4 ripe peaches, halved and pitted
  • 2 tablespoons honey
  • 1 cup Greek yogurt
  • 1/4 cup chopped pecans
  • Fresh mint leaves for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the grill to medium-high heat.
  2. Place the peach halves on the grill, cut side down, and cook for 3-4 minutes until grill marks appear.
  3. Flip the peaches and drizzle the cut side with honey. Grill for an additional 3-4 minutes.
  4. Remove the peaches from the grill and let them cool slightly.
  5. Serve the grilled peaches with a dollop of Greek yogurt, a sprinkle of chopped pecans, and garnish with mint leaves.

The smoky sweetness of the grilled peaches, combined with the creamy yogurt and crunchy pecans, makes this dessert a delightful end to a BBQ meal.

Patriotic Fruit Salad

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups fresh strawberries, hulled and halved
  • 1 cup fresh blueberries
  • 1 cup fresh blackberries
  • 1 cup fresh raspberries
  • 1/4 cup fresh mint leaves, chopped
  • 2 tablespoons honey
  • Juice of 1 lemon

Instructions:

  1. In a large bowl, combine the strawberries, blueberries, blackberries, and raspberries.
  2. In a small bowl, whisk together the honey and lemon juice.
  3. Drizzle the honey and lemon mixture over the fruit and gently toss to coat.
  4. Sprinkle the chopped mint leaves over the fruit salad just before serving.

This vibrant and refreshing fruit salad not only incorporates the colors of the American flag but also provides a healthy and delicious option for dessert.

Firecracker Rice Krispies Treats

Ingredients:

  • 6 cups crispy rice cereal
  • 1/4 cup unsalted butter
  • 1 package (10 ounces) marshmallows
  • Red and blue food coloring
  • White icing
  • Red licorice strings

Instructions:

  1. In a large pot, melt the butter over low heat.
  2. Add the marshmallows and stir until completely melted.
  3. Divide the mixture into three equal portions in separate bowls.
  4. Add red food coloring to one portion and blue food coloring to another, leaving the third portion uncolored.
  5. Gently fold the colored mixtures into the crispy rice cereal until well combined.
  6. Press each colored mixture into separate sections of a greased 9x13-inch baking dish.
  7. Let the treats cool and set, then use the white icing and licorice strings to create “firecracker” designs on top.

These festive and fun Rice Krispies treats are a hit with kids and adults alike and are a great way to add a playful touch to your Fourth of July celebration.

With these easy Fourth of July dessert recipes, you can sweeten up your BBQ and impress your guests with delicious treats that capture the spirit of Independence Day. Whether you choose to make a show-stopping trifle, a refreshing fruit salad, or playful Rice Krispies treats, these desserts are sure to add a memorable finale to your patriotic feast. Happy Fourth of July!
