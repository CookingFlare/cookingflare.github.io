Classic Hawaiian Sweet Rolls Recipe for a Taste of Paradise


Delighting taste buds with their soft texture and mouthwatering flavors, Hawaiian sweet rolls have become a beloved treat worldwide. Rooted in American cuisine, these rolls hold a unique and scrumptious secret: their natural fusion of warm, fluffy texture and the tropical essence of pineapple. Today, we’ll dive into the delightful world of these mystical sweet rolls, unveiling the steps and ingredients that heighten our culinary experience to a haven of flavors. So, get ready to embark on a journey to culinary paradise!

The Story of Hawaiian Sweet Rolls

The history of these irresistible treats dates back to the 1950s when the Depression Biscuit was first introduced by a Hawaiian pastry Maker. Instead of using traditional baking soda or baking powder, this recipe called for a unique component: crushed pineapple. This innovative inclusion resulted in a fascinating amalgamation of the fresh, tropical fruit with the velvety rolls we know and love today.

Ingredients

In crafting the perfect Hawaiian Sweet Roll, a balance of sweet and flavorful components is crucial. Ensure you have the following ingredients on hand:

  1. 2 ½ cups of all-purpose flour
  2. 1/4 cup of sugar
  3. 2 ½ teaspoons of active dry yeast
  4. ½ cup unsalted butter, softened
  5. 1 large egg
  6. 2 cupswarm milk
  7. 1 cup crushed pineapple, well-drained
  8. 1 cup shredded sweetened coconut

The Recipe

Unpacking our ingredients, follow this straightforward process to create these delightful rolls:

  1. Preparation: Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease a 9x13-inch baking dish and set aside.

  2. Combine the dry ingredients: In a large bowl, combine 2 ½ cups of flour, 1/4 cup sugar, and 2 ½ teaspoons of yeast. Mix thoroughly and set aside.

  3. Create the dough: Heat ½ cup of softened butter and 1 cup of crushed pineapple in a saucepan over medium heat. Stir the contents until the butter melts, and combine with the beaten egg. Add this liquid to the flour mix, followed by 2 cups of warm milk quickly. Kneed the mixture until smooth and well-combined.

  4. Add the coconut: Finally, add the 1 cup of shredded coconut, ensuring it’s evenly distributed in the dough. The tropical flavor will elevate the sweet roll taste to new heights!

  5. Roll and rest: Form the dough into a ball and cover it with a clean, damp cloth, which will help the dough “rise” as they rest.

  6. Rolls Assembly: Once rested, cut half of the dough and shape each portion into approximately 15-18 evenly-sized balls. Lay each ball in the prepared baking dish, spacing them evenly.

  7. Proof: Allow the rolls to raise for about 45 minutes.

  8. Bake: Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C). Bake the rolls for 25-30 minutes or until golden brown.
