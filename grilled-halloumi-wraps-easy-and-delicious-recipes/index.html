Grilled Halloumi Wraps: Easy and Delicious Recipes


If you’re a fan of grilling and looking for a quick and tasty meal, look no further than Grilled Halloumi Wraps. These easy and delicious recipes are perfect for any occasion, whether it’s a casual weeknight dinner or a summer barbecue with friends. With their tangy and savory flavors, these wraps are sure to satisfy your taste buds. So, let’s dive right into the recipes!

Recipe 1: Mediterranean Halloumi Wrap

Ingredients:

  • 250g Halloumi cheese, sliced
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 4 large tortilla wraps
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1 cucumber, thinly sliced
  • 1/2 red onion, thinly sliced
  • Handful of fresh basil leaves
  • 4 tablespoons tzatziki sauce

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your grill or grill pan over medium-high heat.
  2. Brush the Halloumi slices with olive oil on both sides.
  3. Place the Halloumi slices on the preheated grill and cook for 2-3 minutes on each side, or until grill marks appear.
  4. Remove the Halloumi from the grill and set aside.
  5. Warm the tortilla wraps on the grill for a few seconds on each side.
  6. Spread a tablespoon of tzatziki sauce on each tortilla wrap.
  7. Layer the grilled Halloumi slices, cherry tomatoes, cucumber slices, red onion slices, and fresh basil leaves on each wrap.
  8. Roll up the wraps tightly, tucking in the sides as you go.
  9. Cut the wraps in half diagonally and serve.

Recipe 2: Spicy Halloumi Wrap

Ingredients:

  • 250g Halloumi cheese, sliced
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 4 large tortilla wraps
  • 1 cup shredded lettuce
  • 1/2 cup thinly sliced bell peppers (red, yellow, or green)
  • 1/2 cup thinly sliced red cabbage
  • 1/4 cup sliced jalapenos
  • 4 tablespoons hot sauce

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your grill or grill pan over medium-high heat.
  2. Brush the Halloumi slices with olive oil on both sides.
  3. Place the Halloumi slices on the preheated grill and cook for 2-3 minutes on each side, or until grill marks appear.
  4. Remove the Halloumi from the grill and set aside.
  5. Warm the tortilla wraps on the grill for a few seconds on each side.
  6. Spread a tablespoon of hot sauce on each tortilla wrap.
  7. Layer the grilled Halloumi slices, shredded lettuce, bell peppers, red cabbage, and sliced jalapenos on each wrap.
  8. Roll up the wraps tightly, tucking in the sides as you go.
  9. Cut the wraps in half diagonally and serve.

Recipe 3: Asian-Inspired Halloumi Wrap

Ingredients:

  • 250g Halloumi cheese, sliced
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons honey
  • 2 tablespoons sesame oil
  • 4 large tortilla wraps
  • 1 cup shredded carrots
  • 1 cup bean sprouts
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh cilantro
  • 4 tablespoons hoisin sauce

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your grill or grill pan over medium-high heat.
  2. In a small bowl, whisk together the soy sauce, honey, and sesame oil.
  3. Brush the Halloumi slices with the soy sauce mixture on both sides.
  4. Place the Halloumi slices on the preheated grill and cook for 2-3 minutes on each side, or until grill marks appear.
  5. Remove the Halloumi from the grill and set aside.
  6. Warm the tortilla wraps on the grill for a few seconds on each side.
  7. Spread a tablespoon of hoisin sauce on each tortilla wrap.
  8. Layer the grilled Halloumi slices, shredded carrots, bean sprouts, and chopped cilantro on each wrap.
  9. Roll up the wraps tightly, tucking in the sides as you go.
  10. Cut the wraps in half diagonally and serve.

There you have it – three different Grilled Halloumi Wrap recipes to satisfy your cravings. Whether you prefer the Mediterranean flavors, a spicy kick, or an Asian-inspired twist, these wraps are versatile and packed with deliciousness. So fire up your grill and enjoy these easy and mouthwatering recipes today!

Note: Grilled Halloumi Wraps are best served immediately after preparation to maintain their texture and flavors. Feel free to customize the recipes by adding your favorite ingredients or condiments. Enjoy!
