10 Quick and Easy Instant Pot Recipes for Busy Weeknights


Life can get hectic, especially when you’re juggling work, family, and other responsibilities. On those busy weeknights when time is short, having a reliable kitchen tool like the Instant Pot can make all the difference. Not only does it speed up cooking time, but it also delivers mouthwatering meals with minimal effort. Whether you’re craving comfort food or a light, healthy dish, the Instant Pot has got you covered. Here are 10 quick and easy Instant Pot recipes that will make your weeknight dinners a breeze.

1. Instant Pot Chicken Alfredo Pasta

This creamy and comforting chicken Alfredo pasta is perfect for a busy weeknight. With the help of the Instant Pot, you’ll have this dish ready in under 30 minutes, without the hassle of boiling noodles separately.

Ingredients:

  • 2 chicken breasts, cubed
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1 teaspoon dried parsley
  • 4 cups chicken broth
  • 12 oz fettuccine pasta
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Set the Instant Pot to “Sauté” mode. Add olive oil and sauté the chicken cubes until golden brown, about 5-6 minutes. Remove and set aside.
  2. In the same pot, add garlic, basil, and parsley. Sauté for about 1 minute until fragrant.
  3. Add chicken broth and deglaze the pot, scraping up any browned bits.
  4. Break the fettuccine in half and layer it on top of the broth. Don’t stir.
  5. Place the chicken back into the pot and seal the Instant Pot lid. Set it to cook on high pressure for 7 minutes.
  6. After the cooking time is up, quick-release the pressure. Stir in the heavy cream and Parmesan cheese. Adjust seasoning with salt and pepper.

This rich and creamy dish is perfect for pasta lovers and can be served with a side salad or garlic bread for a complete meal.

2. Instant Pot Beef Stroganoff

Beef Stroganoff is a classic comfort food that’s often reserved for special occasions. With the Instant Pot, you can enjoy this creamy, savory dish any night of the week without hours of cooking.

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb beef stew meat
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup beef broth
  • 1 tablespoon Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 teaspoon Dijon mustard
  • 8 oz mushrooms, sliced
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1 cup sour cream
  • 2 tablespoons flour
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Egg noodles, for serving

Instructions:

  1. Set the Instant Pot to “Sauté” mode. Add beef and cook until browned on all sides, about 5 minutes.
  2. Add onions, garlic, and mushrooms to the pot, sautéing for an additional 3 minutes.
  3. Stir in beef broth, Worcestershire sauce, Dijon mustard, and paprika. Scrape up any browned bits from the bottom.
  4. Close the lid and set the Instant Pot to cook on high pressure for 20 minutes.
  5. After the cooking time, use a natural release for 10 minutes, then quick-release the remaining pressure.
  6. In a separate bowl, whisk together the sour cream and flour to create a smooth mixture. Stir this into the beef mixture until well combined.
  7. Serve over cooked egg noodles and garnish with fresh parsley.

The creamy, savory sauce and tender beef make this dish a hearty meal that the whole family will love.

3. Instant Pot Vegetarian Chili

This hearty, flavorful vegetarian chili is perfect for those looking for a meatless meal that’s both filling and nutritious. Packed with vegetables, beans, and a kick of spice, it’s a comforting dish that’s easy to make.

Ingredients:

  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 bell peppers, diced
  • 2 zucchinis, diced
  • 1 can (15 oz) black beans, drained and rinsed
  • 1 can (15 oz) kidney beans, drained and rinsed
  • 1 can (15 oz) diced tomatoes
  • 1 cup vegetable broth
  • 2 tablespoons chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Set the Instant Pot to “Sauté” mode and add onions, bell peppers, and zucchinis. Sauté until softened, about 5 minutes.
  2. Add the black beans, kidney beans, diced tomatoes, vegetable broth, chili powder, cumin, and paprika. Stir to combine.
  3. Seal the lid and set the Instant Pot to cook on high pressure for 10 minutes.
  4. After the cooking time, quick-release the pressure.
  5. Stir the chili and adjust seasoning with salt and pepper.

Serve with toppings like sour cream, shredded cheese, or sliced avocado for a complete meal.

4. Instant Pot Honey Garlic Chicken

Sweet and savory, this honey garlic chicken is one of the easiest Instant Pot recipes you’ll ever make. It’s perfect for a busy weeknight when you want a dish that’s both quick and flavorful.

Ingredients:

  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1/4 cup honey
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon rice vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Season the chicken breasts with salt and pepper.
  2. Set the Instant Pot to “Sauté” mode and add olive oil. Brown the chicken breasts for 2-3 minutes on each side.
  3. In a small bowl, whisk together honey, garlic, soy sauce, and rice vinegar.
  4. Pour the honey garlic mixture over the chicken.
  5. Seal the lid and set the Instant Pot to cook on high pressure for 8 minutes.
  6. After the cooking time, quick-release the pressure.
  7. Serve the chicken with the sauce, paired with rice or steamed vegetables.

The balance of honey and garlic creates a delectable glaze that’s irresistible.

5. Instant Pot Pulled Pork Tacos

Pulled pork tacos are a fan favorite, and with the Instant Pot, you can have this delicious dish on the table in no time.

Ingredients:

  • 2 lbs pork shoulder, cut into chunks
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup chicken broth
  • 1 tablespoon chili powder
  • 1 tablespoon cumin
  • 1 teaspoon smoked paprika
  • 1 teaspoon oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Tortillas, for serving
  • Toppings: shredded lettuce, salsa, avocado, cilantro

Instructions:

  1. Season the pork chunks with chili powder, cumin, paprika, oregano, salt, and pepper.
  2. Set the Instant Pot to “Sauté” mode and brown the pork chunks in batches.
  3. Once browned, add onions and garlic to the pot and sauté for another 2-3 minutes.
  4. Add the chicken broth and scrape up any browned bits from the bottom.
  5. Seal the lid and set the Instant Pot to cook on high pressure for 60 minutes.
  6. After the cooking time, let the pressure release naturally for 15 minutes before quick-releasing the remaining pressure.
  7. Shred the pork with two forks and serve in tortillas with your favorite toppings.

These tacos are perfect for a fun, flavorful meal that’s sure to please everyone.

6. Instant Pot Lemon Garlic Salmon

This light and healthy salmon dish is quick and easy, making it perfect for a busy weeknight dinner. The lemon and garlic enhance the natural flavors of the salmon, while the Instant Pot keeps it perfectly tender.

Ingredients:

  • 4 salmon fillets
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 cup water

Instructions:

  1. Season the salmon fillets with olive oil, lemon juice, garlic, thyme, salt, and pepper.
  2. Add the water to the Instant Pot and place the trivet inside.
  3. Place the salmon fillets on the trivet.
  4. Seal the lid and set the Instant Pot to cook on high pressure for 3 minutes.
  5. After the cooking time, quick-release the pressure.
  6. Serve the salmon with steamed vegetables or a side of quinoa for a complete meal.

This dish is light, flavorful, and packed with nutrients.

7. Instant Pot Spaghetti and Meatballs

Spaghetti and meatballs is a classic Italian dish, and the Instant Pot makes it even easier. With everything cooked in one pot, it’s a quick and satisfying meal for any busy night.

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb ground beef or turkey
  • 1/4 cup breadcrumbs
  • 1 egg
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 jar marinara sauce
  • 12 oz spaghetti
  • 2 cups water

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, mix together ground meat, breadcrumbs, egg, Parmesan, garlic powder, and oregano. Roll the mixture into meatballs.
  2. Set the Instant Pot to “Sauté” mode and brown the meatballs in batches.
  3. Once browned, pour in the marinara sauce and water. Stir to combine.
  4. Add the spaghetti, breaking it in half to fit inside the pot. Press the spaghetti down into the sauce.
  5. Seal the lid and set the Instant Pot to cook on high pressure for 8 minutes.
  6. After the cooking time, quick-release the pressure. Stir the pasta and meatballs and serve.

This is a satisfying meal that the entire family will enjoy.

8. Instant Pot Teriyaki Chicken

Sweet and savory teriyaki chicken is always a crowd-pleaser, and the Instant Pot ensures it’s quick and easy to prepare. Serve it with rice and vegetables for a complete meal.

Ingredients:

  • 4 chicken breasts
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 1/4 cup honey
  • 2 tablespoons rice vinegar
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon grated ginger
  • 1 tablespoon cornstarch (optional)

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, whisk together soy sauce, honey, rice vinegar, garlic, and ginger.
  2. Season the chicken breasts with salt and pepper, then pour the teriyaki sauce over them.
  3. Seal the lid and set the Instant Pot to cook on high pressure for 12 minutes.
  4. After the cooking time, quick-release the pressure. Remove the chicken and set aside.
  5. If desired, set the Instant Pot to “Sauté” mode and simmer the sauce to thicken with cornstarch.
  6. Serve the chicken with rice and vegetables, spooning the sauce over the top.

This dish is both quick and full of flavor, making it a perfect addition to your weeknight dinner rotation.

9. Instant Pot Chili Mac

Chili mac is a comforting and filling dish that combines two beloved comfort foods: chili and mac and cheese. This version is made in the Instant Pot, cutting down the cooking time and cleaning.

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can (15 oz) chili beans
  • 1 can (15 oz) diced tomatoes
  • 1 cup beef broth
  • 1 tablespoon chili powder
  • 2 cups elbow macaroni
  • 2 cups shredded cheddar cheese

Instructions:

  1. Set the Instant Pot to “Sauté” mode. Brown the ground beef with the onion and garlic.
  2. Add the chili beans, diced tomatoes, beef broth, chili powder, and macaroni. Stir to combine.
  3. Seal the lid and set the Instant Pot to cook on high pressure for 5 minutes.
  4. After the cooking time, quick-release the pressure.
  5. Stir in shredded cheddar cheese until melted and creamy.

This is a cheesy, hearty dish that will satisfy everyone’s cravings.

10. Instant Pot Chicken and Rice

Chicken and rice is a classic dish, and the Instant Pot makes it incredibly easy to prepare. Tender chicken, seasoned rice, and vegetables come together in one pot for a simple yet delicious meal.

Ingredients:

  • 4 chicken thighs (bone-in, skin-on or boneless)
  • 1 cup long-grain white rice
  • 1 cup chicken broth
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 1 bell pepper, chopped
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Season the chicken thighs with garlic powder, paprika, salt, and pepper.
  2. Set the Instant Pot to “Sauté” mode and brown the chicken thighs on both sides.
  3. Remove the chicken and set aside. Add the onion and bell pepper to the pot, sautéing for 3 minutes.
  4. Add the rice and chicken broth to the pot. Place the chicken thighs on top.
  5. Seal the lid and set the Instant Pot to cook on high pressure for 10 minutes.
  6. After the cooking time, let the pressure release naturally for 5 minutes, then quick-release the remaining pressure.

This dish is a comforting, one-pot meal that’s simple to make and perfect for a busy evening.

These 10 Instant Pot recipes are perfect for busy weeknights when time is limited but you still want a flavorful and satisfying meal. With just a few ingredients and minimal prep, you can enjoy a variety of dishes that cater to every taste. So next time you’re looking for a quick and easy dinner, grab your Instant Pot and let it do the work!
