Slow Cooker Mediterranean Recipes for Healthy Eating

Looking for delicious and healthy meal options that are easy to prepare? Look no further than these flavorful slow cooker Mediterranean recipes! Packed with fresh ingredients, bold flavors, and nutritious elements, these dishes are perfect for anyone looking to embrace the Mediterranean diet and enjoy a taste of the sunny shores of the Mediterranean. Slow Cooker Greek Chicken Pita Wraps Ingredients: 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved 1 red onion, thinly sliced 1/2 cup Kalamata olives, pitted 4 cloves garlic, minced 1 teaspoon dried oregano 1 teaspoon dried thyme 1/2 teaspoon dried rosemary 1/4 cup chicken broth Salt and pepper to taste Whole wheat pita bread Tzatziki sauce for serving Fresh parsley for garnish Instructions: Place the chicken breasts at the bottom of the slow cooker. Add the cherry tomatoes, red onion, Kalamata olives, and minced garlic on top of the chicken. Sprinkle the dried oregano, thyme, and rosemary over the ingredients in the slow cooker. Pour the chicken broth over the mixture and season with salt and pepper. Cover and cook on low for 6-7 hours or on high for 3-4 hours, until the chicken is tender and cooked through. Once cooked, shred the chicken using two forks and mix it with the cooked vegetables. Warm the pita bread and fill it with the chicken and vegetable mixture. Drizzle with tzatziki sauce and garnish with fresh parsley before serving. Mediterranean Lentil Soup Ingredients: 1 cup dried green or brown lentils, rinsed 1 onion, finely chopped 3 carrots, diced 3 celery stalks, diced 4 cloves garlic, minced 1 can (14 oz) diced tomatoes 6 cups vegetable broth 1 teaspoon dried thyme 1 teaspoon dried oregano 1 teaspoon ground cumin Salt and pepper to taste Fresh parsley for garnish Instructions: Place the lentils, onion, carrots, celery, garlic, diced tomatoes, vegetable broth, thyme, oregano, and cumin in the slow cooker. Season with salt and pepper. Cover and cook on low for 7-8 hours or on high for 3-4 hours, until the lentils and vegetables are tender. Once cooked, use an immersion blender to partially blend the soup, leaving some lentils and vegetables whole for texture. Adjust the seasoning if necessary. Ladle the soup into bowls and garnish with fresh parsley before serving. Slow Cooker Mediterranean Stuffed Peppers Ingredients: 4 bell peppers, tops removed and seeds removed 1 cup quinoa, rinsed 1 can (14 oz) chickpeas, drained and rinsed 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved 1/2 cup crumbled feta cheese 1/4 cup Kalamata olives, pitted and chopped 2 cloves garlic, minced 1 teaspoon dried oregano 1 teaspoon dried basil 1/4 cup vegetable broth Salt and pepper to taste Instructions: In a large bowl, combine the quinoa, chickpeas, cherry tomatoes, feta cheese, Kalamata olives, minced garlic, dried oregano, and dried basil. Season with salt and pepper. Stuff the mixture into the bell peppers. Place the stuffed peppers in the slow cooker and pour the vegetable broth into the bottom of the slow cooker. Cover and cook on low for 6-7 hours or on high for 3-4 hours, until the peppers are tender. Once cooked, carefully remove the stuffed peppers from the slow cooker and serve hot. Conclusion These slow cooker Mediterranean recipes are not only delicious and satisfying, but they also offer a host of health benefits. The Mediterranean diet is renowned for its heart-healthy properties, and these recipes are a perfect way to incorporate this beneficial way of eating into your lifestyle. Whether you’re cooking for a weeknight dinner or meal prepping for the week ahead, these flavorful dishes are sure to become staples in your kitchen. Slow Cooker Thai Green Curry Recipes for a Spicy Delight

If you’re a fan of Thai cuisine and love the bold flavors of green curry, then you’re in for a treat! In this blog post, we’ll be sharing not just one, but three delicious and easy-to-make slow cooker Thai green curry recipes that are sure to satisfy your cravings for a spicy delight. So, let’s dive right in! Recipe 1: Slow Cooker Thai Green Curry with Chicken Ingredients: 1 pound boneless, skinless chicken breasts, cut into bite-sized pieces 2 cans (13.5 ounces each) coconut milk 1 cup chicken broth 2 tablespoons green curry paste 1 tablespoon fish sauce 1 tablespoon brown sugar 1 bell pepper, thinly sliced 1 zucchini, sliced 1 cup green beans, trimmed and halved 1 cup bamboo shoots Fresh basil leaves, for garnish Cooked jasmine rice, for serving Instructions: In a slow cooker, combine the coconut milk, chicken broth, green curry paste, fish sauce, and brown sugar. Stir until well combined. Add the chicken, bell pepper, zucchini, green beans, and bamboo shoots to the slow cooker. Stir to coat the ingredients with the curry mixture. Cover and cook on low heat for 4-6 hours or high heat for 2-3 hours, until the chicken is cooked through and the vegetables are tender. Serve the slow cooker Thai green curry over cooked jasmine rice. Garnish with fresh basil leaves. Recipe 2: Slow Cooker Thai Green Curry with Tofu Ingredients: 1 block (14 ounces) firm tofu, drained and cut into cubes 2 cans (13.5 ounces each) coconut milk 1 cup vegetable broth 2 tablespoons green curry paste 1 tablespoon soy sauce 1 tablespoon lime juice 1 tablespoon agave nectar 1 red bell pepper, thinly sliced 1 carrot, thinly sliced 1 cup snow peas Fresh cilantro leaves, for garnish Cooked brown rice, for serving Instructions: In a slow cooker, combine the coconut milk, vegetable broth, green curry paste, soy sauce, lime juice, and agave nectar. Mix well. Add the tofu, bell pepper, carrot, and snow peas to the slow cooker. Stir to combine. Cover and cook on low heat for 4-6 hours or high heat for 2-3 hours, until the tofu is heated through and the vegetables are tender. Serve the slow cooker Thai green curry over cooked brown rice. Garnish with fresh cilantro leaves. Recipe 3: Slow Cooker Thai Green Curry with Shrimp Ingredients: 1 pound shrimp, peeled and deveined 2 cans (13.5 ounces each) coconut milk 1 cup chicken broth 2 tablespoons green curry paste 1 tablespoon fish sauce 1 tablespoon lime juice 1 tablespoon honey 1 onion, thinly sliced 1 red bell pepper, thinly sliced 1 cup sliced mushrooms Fresh mint leaves, for garnish Cooked rice noodles, for serving Instructions: In a slow cooker, combine the coconut milk, chicken broth, green curry paste, fish sauce, lime juice, and honey. Stir until well combined. Add the shrimp, onion, bell pepper, and mushrooms to the slow cooker. Stir to coat the ingredients with the curry mixture. Cover and cook on low heat for 2-3 hours or high heat for 1-2 hours, until the shrimp is pink and cooked through. Serve the slow cooker Thai green curry over cooked rice noodles. Garnish with fresh mint leaves. Now that you have three amazing slow cooker Thai green curry recipes, it’s time to get cooking! Whether you prefer chicken, tofu, or shrimp, these recipes are versatile, flavorful, and perfect for a spicy delight. So, gather your ingredients, set up your slow cooker, and let the magic happen.
