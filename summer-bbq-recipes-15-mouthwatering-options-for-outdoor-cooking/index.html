Summer BBQ Recipes: 15 Mouthwatering Options for Outdoor Cooking


Summer is the perfect time to fire up the grill and enjoy some delicious BBQ. Whether you’re hosting a backyard party or simply craving some flavorful and smoky dishes, we’ve got you covered. In this article, we’ll share 15 mouthwatering BBQ recipes that will take your outdoor cooking to the next level. So, grab your apron, gather your ingredients, and let’s get grilling!

1. Classic Cheeseburger

No BBQ is complete without a classic cheeseburger. Start by seasoning your ground beef with salt, pepper, and your favorite herbs. Shape the patties and grill them to perfection. Top with your favorite cheese, lettuce, tomato, and onions. Serve with a side of crispy fries for the ultimate summer meal.

2. Grilled Chicken Skewers

For a lighter option, try these delicious grilled chicken skewers. Marinate your chicken in a mixture of olive oil, lemon juice, garlic, and herbs. Thread the chicken onto skewers and grill until cooked through. Serve with a tangy yogurt dipping sauce and a side of grilled vegetables.

3. Smoky BBQ Ribs

Fall-off-the-bone BBQ ribs are always a crowd-pleaser. Rub your ribs with a flavorful spice blend, then slow-cook them on the grill until tender. Baste with your favorite BBQ sauce for an extra smoky and sweet flavor. Serve with coleslaw and cornbread for a true Southern BBQ experience.

4. Grilled Shrimp Skewers

Impress your guests with these succulent grilled shrimp skewers. Toss the shrimp in a marinade made with olive oil, garlic, lemon zest, and chili flakes. Thread onto skewers and grill until pink and slightly charred. Serve with a squeeze of fresh lemon juice and a sprinkle of parsley.

5. BBQ Pulled Pork Sandwiches

Transform a humble pork shoulder into tender and flavorful pulled pork sandwiches. Rub the pork with a blend of spices, then slow-cook it on the grill until it falls apart. Shred the meat and mix it with your favorite BBQ sauce. Serve on soft buns with pickles and coleslaw for a finger-licking good meal.

6. Grilled Vegetable Skewers

Vegetarians rejoice! These grilled vegetable skewers are packed with flavor and are a great addition to any BBQ spread. Choose a variety of colorful veggies like bell peppers, zucchini, and cherry tomatoes. Brush with olive oil, sprinkle with salt and pepper, and grill until tender. Serve as a side dish or as a main course for a meatless option.

7. Sticky BBQ Chicken Wings

No BBQ is complete without some finger-licking good chicken wings. Toss your wings in a sticky BBQ sauce made with honey, ketchup, soy sauce, and spices. Grill until crispy and caramelized. Serve with extra sauce on the side and plenty of napkins.

8. Grilled Corn on the Cob

Capture the taste of summer with grilled corn on the cob. Simply peel back the husks, remove the silk, and brush the corn with melted butter and your favorite seasonings. Grill until the kernels are tender and slightly charred. Serve with a sprinkle of salt and a squeeze of lime juice.

9. BBQ Beef Brisket

For a melt-in-your-mouth BBQ experience, try cooking a beef brisket on the grill. Rub the brisket with a flavorful spice mix, then slow-cook it until it’s tender and juicy. Slice thinly and serve with your favorite BBQ sauce. This is a BBQ masterpiece that will impress your guests.

10. Grilled Pineapple

Add some sweetness to your BBQ with grilled pineapple. Slice the pineapple into rings and grill until caramelized and slightly charred. Serve as a side dish or as a topping for burgers and sandwiches. The smoky and tangy flavor of grilled pineapple pairs perfectly with savory dishes.

11. BBQ Salmon

Fish lovers will adore this BBQ salmon recipe. Season your salmon fillets with salt, pepper, and a squeeze of lemon juice. Grill until the fish is flaky and cooked through. Serve with a refreshing cucumber and dill yogurt sauce for a light and delicious meal.

12. BBQ Portobello Mushroom Burger

Vegetarian or not, you’ll love this BBQ portobello mushroom burger. Marinate the mushrooms in a mixture of balsamic vinegar, garlic, and herbs. Grill until tender and juicy. Serve on a bun with your favorite toppings and condiments. It’s a satisfying and flavorful alternative to a traditional burger.

13. Grilled Watermelon Salad

For a refreshing and unique BBQ side dish, try this grilled watermelon salad. Slice the watermelon into thick wedges and grill until slightly charred. Cut into cubes and toss with arugula, feta cheese, mint, and a drizzle of balsamic glaze. It’s a sweet and savory combination that will impress your guests.

14. BBQ Bruschetta

Put a BBQ twist on a classic appetizer with BBQ bruschetta. Grill slices of baguette until crispy, then top with a mixture of diced tomatoes, red onions, garlic, and fresh basil. Drizzle with olive oil and balsamic glaze for a burst of flavor. It’s a crowd-pleasing appetizer that will disappear in no time.

15. Grilled Banana S’mores

End your BBQ on a sweet note with these grilled banana s’mores. Slice a banana lengthwise and stuff it with chocolate chips and mini marshmallows. Wrap in foil and grill until the chocolate is melted and the marshmallows are gooey. Serve with graham crackers for a delicious and indulgent dessert.

These 15 mouthwatering BBQ recipes are sure to elevate your outdoor cooking game this summer. From classic burgers to smoky ribs and grilled desserts, there’s something for everyone to enjoy. So, fire up the grill, gather your loved ones, and savor the flavors of summer. Happy grilling!
