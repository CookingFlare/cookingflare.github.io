Savory Instant Pot Chicken and Rice Soup Recipes


When it comes to comfort food, few dishes can rival the heartwarming goodness of chicken and rice soup. This classic dish, beloved by many, gets a modern twist with the convenience of the Instant Pot. With its ability to tenderize chicken and infuse flavors rapidly, the Instant Pot is your best friend in creating a savory chicken and rice soup that’s perfect for any day of the week. In this post, we’ll explore a variety of savory Instant Pot chicken and rice soup recipes that are sure to become family favorites.

Why Choose Instant Pot for Soup?

The Instant Pot is not just a pressure cooker; it’s a multi-functional kitchen appliance that can sauté, steam, and slow cook. The ability to cook under pressure means your ingredients cook faster while retaining their flavors and nutrients. This is especially beneficial for soups, where the broth needs to absorb all the delicious flavors from the ingredients. Moreover, the Instant Pot simplifies the cooking process, making it easier for novice and experienced cooks alike to whip up a delicious meal in no time.

Basic Ingredients for Chicken and Rice Soup

Before diving into the recipes, let’s discuss the essential ingredients you’ll typically need for chicken and rice soup. These ingredients are versatile, allowing you to customize your soup to suit your taste.

  • Chicken: You can use boneless chicken breasts, thighs, or even a whole chicken cut into pieces.
  • Rice: Long grain white rice, jasmine rice, or brown rice can all be used depending on your preference.
  • Broth: Chicken broth or stock is essential for a rich flavor, but you can also use vegetable broth for a lighter option.
  • Vegetables: Onions, carrots, celery, and garlic are classic choices, but feel free to add any vegetables you love.
  • Herbs and Spices: Fresh or dried herbs like thyme, parsley, or bay leaves can elevate your soup’s flavor profile.
  • Seasoning: Salt and pepper are fundamental, but you can also experiment with other seasonings such as paprika or turmeric.

Now that we have a solid foundation, let’s explore some delectable recipes.

Recipe 1: Classic Instant Pot Chicken and Rice Soup

Ingredients

  • 1 pound boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1 cup long-grain white rice
  • 1 medium onion, diced
  • 2 carrots, sliced
  • 2 celery stalks, diced
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 6 cups chicken broth
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • 1 bay leaf
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • Fresh parsley for garnish

Instructions

  1. Sauté the Vegetables: Set the Instant Pot to the sauté mode. Add olive oil and let it heat. Once hot, add the diced onion, carrots, and celery. Sauté for about 5-7 minutes until the vegetables are softened. Stir in the minced garlic and cook for an additional minute.

  2. Add Chicken and Broth: Layer the chicken breasts on top of the sautéed vegetables. Pour in the chicken broth, then add the thyme, bay leaf, salt, and pepper.

  3. Pressure Cook: Close the lid of the Instant Pot, ensuring the vent is set to sealing. Select the pressure cook setting and set the timer for 10 minutes.

  4. Release Pressure: Once the cooking time is complete, allow the pressure to release naturally for 5 minutes, then carefully do a quick release to let out any remaining steam.

  5. Add Rice: Remove the chicken breasts and shred them using two forks. Set the Instant Pot back to sauté mode. Stir in the rice and let it cook for about 15 minutes or until tender.

  6. Combine and Serve: Return the shredded chicken to the pot. Taste and adjust seasoning if necessary. Serve hot, garnished with fresh parsley.

Recipe 2: Lemon Herb Instant Pot Chicken and Rice Soup

Ingredients

  • 1 pound boneless chicken thighs
  • 1 cup jasmine rice
  • 1 medium onion, chopped
  • 2 carrots, diced
  • 1 celery stalk, chopped
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 6 cups chicken broth
  • Zest and juice of 1 lemon
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • Fresh dill for garnish

Instructions

  1. Sauté the Base: Set the Instant Pot to sauté mode and heat the olive oil. Add the onion, carrots, and celery, cooking until softened. Add garlic and sauté for another minute.

  2. Add Chicken and Broth: Place the chicken thighs into the pot, followed by the chicken broth. Add the lemon zest, juice, oregano, basil, salt, and pepper.

  3. Pressure Cook: Lock the lid and set the pressure cook timer for 12 minutes.

  4. Release Pressure: After cooking, let the pressure release naturally for 5 minutes before doing a quick release.

  5. Incorporate Rice: Remove the chicken, shred it, and set it aside. Stir in the jasmine rice to the broth and select sauté mode again. Cook for about 15 minutes, stirring occasionally.

  6. Finish and Serve: Return the shredded chicken to the soup. Adjust seasoning if needed. Garnish with fresh dill before serving.

Recipe 3: Spicy Instant Pot Chicken and Rice Soup

Ingredients

  • 1 pound boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1 cup brown rice
  • 1 medium onion, chopped
  • 2 jalapeños, diced (seeds removed for less heat)
  • 2 carrots, sliced
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 6 cups chicken broth
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • 1 teaspoon smoked paprika
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • Lime wedges for serving
  • Fresh cilantro for garnish

Instructions

  1. Sauté the Aromatics: In the Instant Pot, heat olive oil on sauté mode. Add the onion, carrots, and jalapeños, cooking until softened. Stir in garlic and cook for an additional minute.

  2. Add Chicken and Broth: Place chicken breasts in the pot and pour in the chicken broth. Add the cumin, smoked paprika, salt, and pepper.

  3. Pressure Cook: Close the lid and set the pressure cook timer for 10 minutes.

  4. Release Pressure: After cooking, allow for a natural pressure release for 5 minutes, then do a quick release.

  5. Cook the Rice: Remove the chicken and shred it. Stir in the brown rice and switch the Instant Pot back to sauté mode. Cook for about 30-35 minutes or until the rice is tender.

  6. Serve: Return the shredded chicken to the soup, taste for seasoning, and serve hot with lime wedges and fresh cilantro.

Tips for Perfect Chicken and Rice Soup

  • Use High-Quality Broth: The broth is the backbone of your soup. Opt for homemade or low-sodium options to control the salt levels.
  • Don’t Skip the Sauté: Sautéing the vegetables first allows the flavors to bloom and enhances the overall taste of your soup.
  • Customize Ingredients: Feel free to add your favorite vegetables or beans for extra nutrition and flavor.
  • Storage: This soup stores well in the refrigerator for up to 3 days or can be frozen for up to 3 months. Just be aware that rice may absorb some broth as it sits.

Conclusion

Instant Pot chicken and rice soup is a versatile dish that can be customized to your liking. Whether you prefer classic flavors, a zesty lemon kick, or a spicy twist, there is a recipe here for everyone. These soups are not only easy to make but also provide a nourishing meal that warms the soul. So grab your Instant Pot, gather your ingredients, and get ready to enjoy a delicious bowl of homemade chicken and rice soup today!
