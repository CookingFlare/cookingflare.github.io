Mouthwatering Lemon Meringue Pie: Step-by-Step Recipe


If you’re looking for a dessert that’s bright, tangy, and utterly irresistible, then this mouthwatering lemon meringue pie is the perfect choice. The combination of zesty lemon filling and fluffy, golden meringue is a match made in dessert heaven. Follow this step-by-step recipe to create a pie that will have your taste buds singing.

Ingredients

For the Crust:

  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, cold and cubed
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 cup ice water

For the Lemon Filling:

  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/4 cup cornstarch
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1 1/2 cups water
  • 1/2 cup fresh lemon juice
  • 2 tablespoons lemon zest
  • 4 large egg yolks
  • 2 tablespoons unsalted butter

For the Meringue:

  • 4 large egg whites
  • 1/4 teaspoon cream of tartar
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract

Instructions

Step 1: Prepare the Crust

  1. In a large mixing bowl, combine the flour, sugar, and salt for the crust.
  2. Add the cold, cubed butter and use a pastry cutter or your fingertips to work the butter into the flour mixture until it resembles coarse crumbs.
  3. Drizzle in the ice water, a little at a time, and mix until the dough comes together.
  4. Shape the dough into a disk, wrap it in plastic wrap, and refrigerate for at least 30 minutes.

Step 2: Roll Out the Crust

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. On a lightly floured surface, roll out the chilled dough into a circle about 12 inches in diameter.
  3. Carefully transfer the dough to a 9-inch pie dish, gently pressing it into the bottom and sides.
  4. Trim any excess dough and crimp the edges decoratively.

Step 3: Blind Bake the Crust

  1. Line the crust with parchment paper and fill it with pie weights or dried beans.
  2. Bake for 15 minutes, then remove the parchment and weights and continue baking for another 10-12 minutes, or until the crust is golden brown.
  3. Remove from the oven and let it cool completely.

Step 4: Make the Lemon Filling

  1. In a medium saucepan, whisk together the sugar, cornstarch, and salt for the filling.
  2. Gradually whisk in the water, lemon juice, and lemon zest until smooth.
  3. Place the saucepan over medium heat and cook, stirring constantly, until the mixture thickens and comes to a boil.
  4. In a separate bowl, lightly beat the egg yolks.
  5. Gradually whisk about 1/2 cup of the hot lemon mixture into the egg yolks to temper them, then pour the egg mixture back into the saucepan.
  6. Cook for another 2 minutes, stirring constantly, until thickened.
  7. Remove from the heat and stir in the butter until melted.
  8. Pour the filling into the cooled pie crust.

Step 5: Prepare the Meringue

  1. In a clean, dry mixing bowl, beat the egg whites and cream of tartar on medium speed until soft peaks form.
  2. Gradually add the sugar, a tablespoon at a time, while beating on high speed until stiff, glossy peaks form.
  3. Beat in the vanilla extract.

Step 6: Assemble and Bake

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. Spread the meringue over the hot lemon filling, making sure to spread it to the edges to seal in the filling.
  3. Use the back of a spoon to create decorative peaks in the meringue.

Step 7: Bake the Pie

  1. Bake the pie for 15-20 minutes, or until the meringue is golden brown.
  2. Cool the pie on a wire rack for 1 hour, then refrigerate for at least 3 hours before serving.

Enjoy Your Mouthwatering Lemon Meringue Pie!

This delightful dessert is a perfect balance of sweet and tart, and it’s sure to impress your family and friends. Whether you’re hosting a special occasion or simply craving a slice of something heavenly, this lemon meringue pie is a show-stopping treat that will leave everyone wanting more. So, roll up your sleeves, gather your ingredients, and get ready to create a pie that will steal the spotlight at any gathering. Happy baking!
