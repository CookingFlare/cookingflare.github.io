Irresistible Nut-Free Chocolate Cake Recipe


Are you looking for a delectable chocolate cake recipe that’s safe for those with nut allergies? Look no further! This nut-free chocolate cake is not only irresistibly delicious but also free from any traces of nuts, making it the perfect treat for anyone with nut sensitivities. In this recipe, I’ll guide you through the steps to create a moist and decadent chocolate cake that will leave everyone coming back for more.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 3/4 cups granulated sugar
  • 3/4 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 2 teaspoons baking soda
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup buttermilk
  • 1/2 cup vegetable oil
  • 3 large eggs
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 1 cup hot water
  • For the frosting:
    • 1 1/2 cups unsalted butter, softened
    • 3/4 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
    • 3 cups powdered sugar
    • 3/4 cup heavy cream
    • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
    • Pinch of salt

Instructions

  1. Preheat the Oven and Prepare the Pans: Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease two 9-inch round cake pans and line the bottoms with parchment paper for easy removal.

  2. Mix the Dry Ingredients: In a large bowl, whisk together the flour, sugar, cocoa powder, baking soda, baking powder, and salt until well combined.

  3. Combine Wet Ingredients: In another bowl, whisk together the buttermilk, vegetable oil, eggs, and vanilla extract until smooth.

  4. Combine and Add Hot Water: Gradually add the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients, mixing until just combined. Then, stir in the hot water until the batter is smooth. The batter will be thin, but that’s perfectly normal.

  5. Bake the Cakes: Divide the batter evenly between the prepared cake pans. Bake for 30 to 35 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean. Allow the cakes to cool in the pans for 10 minutes, then transfer them to a wire rack to cool completely.

  6. Prepare the Frosting: While the cakes are cooling, prepare the chocolate frosting. In a large bowl, beat the butter until creamy. Add the cocoa powder, powdered sugar, heavy cream, vanilla extract, and salt. Beat on low speed until combined, then increase the speed to medium and beat for 2 minutes until creamy and smooth.

  7. Assemble the Cake: Once the cakes are completely cooled, place one layer on a serving plate. Spread a generous layer of frosting on top. Place the second layer on top and frost the top and sides of the cake with the remaining frosting.

  8. Enjoy! Slice and serve this irresistible nut-free chocolate cake to your friends and family. The moist and rich layers of chocolate goodness will surely make this cake a crowd-pleaser!

Conclusion

This nut-free chocolate cake recipe is a must-try for any chocolate lover, especially those with nut allergies. The rich cocoa flavor and moist texture make it a delightful treat for any occasion. Whether it’s for a birthday celebration, a dinner party, or just a sweet indulgence, this cake is sure to impress. So, go ahead, gather your ingredients, and delight your loved ones with this irresistible nut-free chocolate cake!
