One Pot Beef and Bean Chili


Are you in the mood for a comforting and satisfying meal that’s easy to make and bursting with flavor? Look no further than this one pot beef and bean chili recipe! Whether you’re cooking for a crowd or just craving a cozy dinner for yourself, this hearty chili is sure to hit the spot.

Ingredients

Before we dive into the cooking process, let’s gather the essential ingredients for this delicious chili:

  • 1 pound of ground beef
  • 1 large onion, diced
  • 3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 bell pepper, diced
  • 1 can of diced tomatoes
  • 2 cans of kidney beans, drained and rinsed
  • 1 can of tomato sauce
  • 2 cups of beef broth
  • 2 tablespoons of chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon of cumin
  • 1 teaspoon of paprika
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Optional toppings: shredded cheese, sour cream, chopped green onions

Instructions

Now that we have everything we need, let’s get cooking!

  1. Brown the Beef: In a large pot or Dutch oven, brown the ground beef over medium heat until it’s fully cooked. Use a wooden spoon to break it up into crumbles as it cooks. Once the beef is browned, remove any excess fat from the pot.

  2. Add Aromatics: Add the diced onion, minced garlic, and diced bell pepper to the pot with the cooked beef. Sauté the mixture for a few minutes until the vegetables are tender and fragrant.

  3. Combine the Ingredients: Pour in the diced tomatoes, kidney beans, tomato sauce, and beef broth. Stir everything together to ensure that the flavors meld beautifully.

  4. Season the Chili: Sprinkle the chili powder, cumin, paprika, salt, and pepper into the pot. Stir well to distribute the spices evenly throughout the chili.

  5. Simmer to Perfection: Bring the chili to a gentle boil, then reduce the heat to a simmer. Let the chili simmer for about 30 minutes, allowing the flavors to intensify and the liquid to reduce slightly.

  6. Serve and Enjoy: Once the chili has reached your desired consistency, it’s ready to be served! Ladle it into bowls and garnish with your favorite toppings, such as shredded cheese, a dollop of sour cream, or a sprinkle of chopped green onions.

Tips and Variations

  • For a leaner option, you can use ground turkey or chicken instead of beef.
  • If you prefer a spicier kick, add a pinch of cayenne pepper or a diced jalapeño to the chili.
  • This chili can be made in a slow cooker for an even more convenient cooking process. Simply brown the beef and sauté the vegetables before transferring everything to the slow cooker and cooking on low for 6-8 hours.

Final Thoughts

There’s something truly special about a steaming bowl of homemade chili, and this one pot beef and bean chili recipe is a testament to that. Whether it’s a chilly evening or a casual gathering with friends and family, this hearty dish never fails to impress. The best part? It’s all made in just one pot, minimizing the cleanup and maximizing the flavor.

So, the next time you’re craving a cozy and comforting meal, give this recipe a try. You’ll be rewarded with a rich and satisfying chili that’s perfect for any occasion. Enjoy!
