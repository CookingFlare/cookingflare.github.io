Crunchy Asian Ramen Noodle Salad Recipe


Welcome to today’s recipe blog post! In this edition, we will be exploring the delightful world of Crunchy Asian Ramen Noodle Salad. This vibrant and refreshing salad is a perfect blend of crisp vegetables, flavorful dressing, and, of course, the star of the show - crunchy ramen noodles. Whether you’re looking for a quick and easy lunch option or a delightful side dish for your next gathering, this recipe is sure to impress. So let’s dive right in!

Ingredients

For the salad:

  • 2 packages of ramen noodles (discard the seasoning packets)
  • 1 cup shredded purple cabbage
  • 1 cup shredded carrots
  • 1 cup diced red bell pepper
  • 1 cup diced cucumber
  • 1/4 cup chopped green onions
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh cilantro
  • 1/4 cup toasted sesame seeds

For the dressing:

  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 3 tablespoons rice vinegar
  • 2 tablespoons honey
  • 1 tablespoon sesame oil
  • 1 tablespoon freshly squeezed lime juice
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon grated fresh ginger
  • 1/2 teaspoon red pepper flakes (optional for added heat)

Instructions

  1. Start by preparing the dressing. In a small bowl, whisk together the soy sauce, rice vinegar, honey, sesame oil, lime juice, minced garlic, grated ginger, and red pepper flakes. Set the dressing aside to allow the flavors to meld together.

  2. Next, prepare the ramen noodles according to the package instructions. Once cooked, drain the noodles and rinse them under cold water to stop the cooking process. Set aside.

  3. In a large mixing bowl, combine the shredded purple cabbage, shredded carrots, diced red bell pepper, diced cucumber, chopped green onions, and chopped fresh cilantro. Toss the vegetables together to distribute the flavors evenly.

  4. Break the cooked and cooled ramen noodles into small pieces and add them to the vegetable mixture. Note that you can choose to leave the noodles whole for a different texture if desired.

  5. Pour the dressing over the salad and toss everything together until well coated. Make sure all the ingredients are evenly distributed and coated with the flavorful dressing.

  6. Sprinkle the toasted sesame seeds over the salad and gently toss again to incorporate the seeds throughout.

  7. Allow the salad to sit for at least 10 minutes before serving. This will allow the flavors to mingle and the noodles to soak up some of the dressing.

  8. Serve the Crunchy Asian Ramen Noodle Salad chilled or at room temperature. It can be enjoyed on its own as a light meal or served as a side dish alongside grilled chicken or fish.

Conclusion

And there you have it, a delicious and satisfying Crunchy Asian Ramen Noodle Salad recipe! This vibrant salad is not only pleasing to the eyes but also offers a delightful combination of textures and flavors. The crispness of the vegetables, the crunch of the noodles, and the tangy dressing all come together to create a truly memorable dish. So why not give it a try and impress your family and friends with this easy-to-make yet impressive salad? Enjoy!
