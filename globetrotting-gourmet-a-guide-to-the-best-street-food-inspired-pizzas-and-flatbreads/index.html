Globetrotting Gourmet: A Guide to the Best Street Food-inspired Pizzas and Flatbreads


As a globetrotter and a food enthusiast, there’s nothing quite like discovering local street food on your culinary adventures around the world. From Japan’s flavorful takoyaki to Italy’s classic Margherita pizza, the fusion of diverse ingredients, spices, and cooking techniques creates unforgettable culinary experiences. Inspired by these unforgettable tastes, it’s time to bring the world to your kitchen with these street food-inspired pizzas and flatbreads.

Exploring the World through Pizzas

Let’s embark on a delightful culinary journey and explore some of the best street food-inspired pizzas you can create at home.

Malaysian Style Pesto Pizza

This unique twist on a classic Italian dish features fragrant pesto made with Malaysian kaffir lime leaves and topped with succulent grilled chicken, juicy rockets, and a drizzle of tangy passion fruit sauce. A true fusion of flavors!

Brazilian Picanha Flatbread

Experience the smoky flavor of Brazilian churrasco with this delightful flatbread. Marinated picanha (Brazilian-style skirt steak) is piled high with sautéed onions, ripe tomatoes, and spicy pomiso sauce, creating a tantalizing blend of flavors.

Indian Aloo Tikki Pizza

Dive into the joy of Indian street food with this indulgent pizza. Crispy potato cakes (aloo tikki) are layered atop a golden crust, adorned with tangy tamarind chutney, fresh mint, and toasted sesame seeds – a delightful treat for your taste buds.

Greek Feta Famiglia Pizza

Savor the rich flavors of Greece with this family-style pizza. A generous spread of creamy feta, brimming with olives, sun-dried tomatoes, and juicy oregano give this pizza a Mediterranean twist perfect for sharing.

Japanese Takoyaki-inspired Flatbread

A playful twist on the popular Japanese street food, takoyaki, this flatbread offers bold flavors and textures. Soft, chewy flatbread brightens with teriyaki glaze, and features crispy tempura-like bits of octopus, scallions, and a pop of pickled ginger – a playful experience.
