Pumpkin Spice Muffins with a Caramel Glaze and a Sprinkle of Cinnamon Recipe: A Fall-Inspired Vegan Treat


  1. In a large mixing bowl, whisk together the flour, baking powder, baking soda, salt, and pumpkin pie spice. Set aside.

  2. In another bowl, combine the granulated sugar, brown sugar, pumpkin puree, applesauce, vegetable oil, and vanilla extract. Mix until well combined.

  3. Gradually add the wet ingredients to the dry ingredients, stirring until just combined. Be careful not to overmix, as it can result in dense muffins.

  4. Divide the batter evenly among the prepared muffin cups, filling each about 2/3 full.

  5. Bake for 20-25 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.

  6. While the muffins are baking, prepare the caramel glaze. You can use store-bought vegan caramel sauce or make your own by combining 1/2 cup of coconut cream, 1/2 cup of brown sugar, and 1/4 cup of vegan butter in a small saucepan. Cook over medium heat, stirring constantly, until the mixture thickens and coats the back of a spoon.

  7. Once the muffins are done, remove them from the oven and allow them to cool in the pan for 5 minutes. Then transfer them to a wire rack to cool completely.

  8. Drizzle the caramel glaze over the cooled muffins, allowing it to drip down the sides. Sprinkle a pinch of cinnamon on top for an extra burst of flavor.

  9. Serve and enjoy these delicious fall-inspired vegan treats with a hot cup of coffee or tea.

Recipe Notes

  • Feel free to add your favorite nuts or raisins to the batter for added texture and flavor.
  • These muffins can be stored in an airtight container at room temperature for up to 3 days. You can also freeze them for longer storage.

These Pumpkin Spice Muffins with a Caramel Glaze and a Sprinkle of Cinnamon are the perfect treat to enjoy during the fall season. The warm spices and rich caramel glaze make them a decadent indulgence, while the use of vegan ingredients ensures that everyone can enjoy them.

The combination of pumpkin puree, applesauce, and vegetable oil keeps these muffins moist and tender. The pumpkin pie spice adds a delightful blend of cinnamon, nutmeg, ginger, and cloves, giving these muffins a distinct fall flavor. And the homemade caramel glaze takes them to the next level with its smooth and sweet finish.

Whether you’re a vegan or simply looking for a delicious fall-inspired treat, these Pumpkin Spice Muffins are sure to satisfy your cravings. Enjoy them for breakfast, as a snack, or even as a dessert. They’re versatile, easy to make, and guaranteed to be a hit with family and friends.

So why not embrace the flavors of the season and whip up a batch of these delightful Pumpkin Spice Muffins with a Caramel Glaze and a Sprinkle of Cinnamon? They’re the perfect way to celebrate autumn and indulge in a little sweetness.
