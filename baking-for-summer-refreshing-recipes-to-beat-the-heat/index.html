Baking for Summer: Refreshing Recipes to Beat the Heat


As the temperature rises and the sun shines brighter, it’s time to embrace the flavors of summer with some delightful baking recipes that will help you beat the heat. From zesty citrus to juicy berries, these refreshing treats are perfect for bringing a burst of summer into your kitchen. So, let’s roll up our sleeves, preheat the oven, and get ready to bake up some sunshine!

1. Lemon Blueberry Scones

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 tablespoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, cold and cut into cubes
  • 1/2 cup buttermilk
  • 1 large egg
  • Zest of 1 lemon
  • 1 cup fresh blueberries

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C) and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
  2. In a large bowl, whisk together the flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt.
  3. Add the cold butter to the dry ingredients and use a pastry cutter or your fingers to work it into the mixture until it resembles coarse crumbs.
  4. In a separate bowl, whisk together the buttermilk, egg, and lemon zest. Pour this mixture into the dry ingredients and stir until just combined.
  5. Gently fold in the fresh blueberries.
  6. Transfer the dough to a floured surface and pat it into a circle about 1 inch thick. Cut the circle into 8 wedges and place them on the prepared baking sheet.
  7. Bake for 15-18 minutes or until the scones are golden brown. Let them cool slightly before serving.

2. Strawberry-Lime Icebox Pie

Ingredients:

  • 1 1/2 cups graham cracker crumbs
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 6 tablespoons unsalted butter, melted
  • 1 (14-ounce) can sweetened condensed milk
  • 1/2 cup fresh lime juice
  • 1 tablespoon lime zest
  • 1 1/2 cups chopped fresh strawberries
  • 1 cup heavy cream

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, combine the graham cracker crumbs, sugar, and melted butter. Press this mixture into the bottom and up the sides of a pie dish. Chill in the refrigerator for 30 minutes.
  2. In another bowl, whisk together the sweetened condensed milk, lime juice, and lime zest until smooth.
  3. Gently fold in the chopped strawberries and pour the mixture into the prepared crust.
  4. In a separate bowl, whip the heavy cream until stiff peaks form. Spread the whipped cream over the pie filling.
  5. Cover the pie with plastic wrap and refrigerate for at least 4 hours or until set.

3. Mango Coconut Cupcakes

Ingredients:

  • 1 3/4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 3/4 cup canned coconut milk
  • 1/2 cup pureed mango (from 1 large ripe mango)

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C) and line a cupcake tin with paper liners.
  2. In a bowl, whisk together the flour, baking powder, baking soda, and salt.
  3. In another bowl, beat the butter and sugar together until light and fluffy. Add the eggs, one at a time, beating well after each addition. Stir in the vanilla extract.
  4. Combine the coconut milk and mango puree in a small bowl.
  5. Add the dry ingredients to the butter mixture in three additions, alternating with the coconut milk and mango mixture, beginning and ending with the dry ingredients.
  6. Divide the batter evenly among the prepared cupcake liners and bake for 18-20 minutes or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.
  7. Allow the cupcakes to cool before frosting.

These delightful summer baking recipes are sure to bring a smile to your face and a burst of refreshing flavors to your taste buds. Whether you’re hosting a backyard barbecue or simply craving a sweet treat, these recipes are the perfect way to celebrate the season. So, let’s get baking and savor the taste of summer!
