Creamy Garlic Parmesan Chicken: A Comforting Weeknight Dinner


If you’re looking for a delicious and comforting meal to enjoy on a weeknight, look no further than this Creamy Garlic Parmesan Chicken recipe. This dish is a perfect combination of tender chicken, rich and creamy sauce, and savory Parmesan cheese. It’s a simple yet elegant dish that will surely impress your family and friends. Let’s dive into the recipe and discover how to create this indulgent and satisfying meal.

Ingredients

  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup chicken broth
  • 1 1/2 cups heavy cream
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 teaspoon Italian seasoning
  • Fresh parsley for garnish

Instructions

  1. Start by seasoning the chicken breasts with salt and black pepper.

  2. In a large skillet, heat the olive oil over medium-high heat. Once the oil is hot, add the chicken breasts to the skillet and cook for 6-7 minutes on each side, or until they are golden brown and cooked through. Remove the chicken from the skillet and set aside.

  3. In the same skillet, add the minced garlic and sauté for about 1 minute, or until fragrant.

  4. Pour in the chicken broth and bring it to a simmer, scraping up any browned bits from the bottom of the skillet.

  5. Reduce the heat to medium and stir in the heavy cream, Parmesan cheese, and Italian seasoning. Let the sauce simmer for about 2-3 minutes, or until it thickens slightly.

  6. Return the cooked chicken breasts to the skillet, spooning the creamy garlic Parmesan sauce over them. Allow the chicken to heat through in the sauce for a few minutes.

  7. Once the chicken is heated through and the sauce has thickened to your desired consistency, remove the skillet from the heat.

  8. Garnish the Creamy Garlic Parmesan Chicken with freshly chopped parsley for a pop of color and extra flavor.

Serving Suggestions

This Creamy Garlic Parmesan Chicken pairs wonderfully with a variety of sides. You can serve it over cooked pasta, rice, or mashed potatoes for a hearty and satisfying meal. For a lighter option, consider pairing it with a crisp green salad or steamed vegetables. The versatility of this dish allows you to customize it to your liking and create a complete and balanced meal.

Conclusion

Creamy Garlic Parmesan Chicken is a classic and comforting dish that is perfect for any weeknight dinner. Its rich and velvety sauce combined with tender chicken makes it a crowd-pleaser that will leave everyone satisfied. Whether you’re cooking for your family or hosting a dinner party, this recipe is sure to impress. Try it out and savor the delightful flavors of this indulgent dish. Enjoy!
