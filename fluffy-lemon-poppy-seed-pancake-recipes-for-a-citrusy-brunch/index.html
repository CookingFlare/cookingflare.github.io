Fluffy Lemon Poppy Seed Pancake Recipes for a Citrusy Brunch


Who doesn’t love a stack of fluffy pancakes for brunch? And when you add the zesty flavor of lemon and the delightful crunch of poppy seeds, you get a breakfast treat that’s truly citrusy and irresistible. In this recipe blog post, we’ll explore three different variations of fluffy lemon poppy seed pancakes that are sure to impress your taste buds. So, let’s dive right in!

Classic Lemon Poppy Seed Pancakes

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons granulated sugar
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1 tablespoon poppy seeds
  • 1 tablespoon lemon zest
  • 1 cup buttermilk
  • 1 large egg
  • 2 tablespoons unsalted butter, melted
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice

Instructions:

  1. In a large mixing bowl, whisk together the flour, sugar, baking powder, baking soda, salt, poppy seeds, and lemon zest.
  2. In another bowl, whisk together the buttermilk, egg, melted butter, and lemon juice.
  3. Pour the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients and gently fold until just combined. Do not overmix; a few lumps are fine.
  4. Preheat a non-stick skillet or griddle over medium heat. Lightly grease with butter or cooking spray.
  5. Pour 1/4 cup of batter onto the skillet for each pancake. Cook until bubbles form on the surface, then flip and cook for another 1-2 minutes until golden brown.
  6. Serve the pancakes warm with a drizzle of maple syrup and a sprinkle of fresh lemon zest. Enjoy!

Gluten-Free Lemon Poppy Seed Pancakes

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup gluten-free all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons coconut sugar
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1 tablespoon poppy seeds
  • 1 tablespoon lemon zest
  • 1/2 cup almond milk
  • 1 large egg
  • 2 tablespoons coconut oil, melted
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice

Instructions:

  1. In a large mixing bowl, whisk together the gluten-free flour, coconut sugar, baking powder, baking soda, salt, poppy seeds, and lemon zest.
  2. In another bowl, whisk together the almond milk, egg, melted coconut oil, and lemon juice.
  3. Pour the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients and gently fold until just combined.
  4. Follow steps 4-6 from the classic lemon poppy seed pancake recipe.

Vegan Lemon Poppy Seed Pancakes

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup whole wheat flour
  • 2 tablespoons coconut sugar
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1 tablespoon poppy seeds
  • 1 tablespoon lemon zest
  • 1 cup almond milk
  • 2 tablespoons coconut oil, melted
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Instructions:

  1. In a large mixing bowl, whisk together the whole wheat flour, coconut sugar, baking powder, baking soda, salt, poppy seeds, and lemon zest.
  2. In another bowl, whisk together the almond milk, melted coconut oil, lemon juice, and vanilla extract.
  3. Pour the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients and gently fold until just combined.
  4. Follow steps 4-6 from the classic lemon poppy seed pancake recipe.

Now that you have three delicious variations of fluffy lemon poppy seed pancakes, you can choose the one that suits your taste preferences or dietary needs. Whether you go for the classic, gluten-free, or vegan version, these pancakes are guaranteed to add a burst of citrusy goodness to your brunch table. So, gather your ingredients, fire up your stove, and get ready to indulge in a stack of lemony deliciousness. Happy brunching!

Remember, cooking is all about experimentation, so feel free to add your own twists and flavors to these recipes. And if you have any other pancake varieties you’d like us to explore, let us know in the comments below. Until then, happy cooking and bon appétit!
