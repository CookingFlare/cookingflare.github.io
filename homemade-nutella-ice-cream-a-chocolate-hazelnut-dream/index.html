Homemade Nutella Ice Cream: A Chocolate Hazelnut Dream


If you’re a fan of creamy, luscious ice cream and the irresistible combination of chocolate and hazelnut, then you’re in for a real treat with this homemade Nutella ice cream recipe. This indulgent frozen dessert is the perfect way to satisfy your sweet tooth on a hot summer day or any time you’re craving a decadent, frozen delight. Best of all, it’s surprisingly easy to make at home with just a few simple ingredients. So, roll up your sleeves, grab your mixing bowl, and get ready to embark on a journey to chocolate hazelnut paradise!

Ingredients

  • 2 cups heavy cream
  • 1 can (14 ounces) sweetened condensed milk
  • 1 cup Nutella
  • 1 teaspoon pure vanilla extract
  • 1/2 cup chopped hazelnuts (optional, for garnish)

Instructions

  1. In a large mixing bowl, whip the heavy cream until stiff peaks form. This will give your ice cream its creamy, airy texture.

  2. Gently fold in the sweetened condensed milk, Nutella, and vanilla extract until everything is well combined. Be careful not to overmix, as you want to maintain the fluffy consistency.

  3. If you’re feeling adventurous, now is the time to fold in the chopped hazelnuts for a delightful crunch in every bite.

  4. Transfer the mixture to a freezer-safe container, smoothing the top with a spatula for a polished finish.

  5. Cover the container with a lid or plastic wrap to prevent ice crystals from forming on the surface of your ice cream.

  6. Place the container in the freezer and let the magic happen. Allow the ice cream to freeze for at least 6 hours or preferably overnight for the best results.

  7. Once the ice cream is thoroughly frozen, scoop it into bowls or cones, and if you’ve opted for the hazelnut garnish, sprinkle a handful of chopped hazelnuts on top for an extra touch of indulgence.

Serving Suggestions

This velvety Nutella ice cream is a showstopper on its own, but if you’re feeling creative, there are endless ways to take it to the next level. Drizzle a generous amount of warm Nutella over a scoop of ice cream for an extra burst of chocolatey goodness. For a textural contrast, consider topping your ice cream with a sprinkling of crushed wafer cookies or a dollop of whipped cream. If you’re in the mood for a Nutella ice cream sundae, add a swirl of caramel sauce and a maraschino cherry on top for a classic touch.

Final Thoughts

Homemade Nutella ice cream is a luxurious indulgence that’s surprisingly simple to make. With its rich, velvety texture and the unmistakable flavors of chocolate and hazelnut, this frozen delight is sure to become a favorite in your dessert repertoire. Whether you’re serving it at a summer barbecue, a birthday celebration, or simply indulging in a quiet night at home, this chocolate hazelnut dream is bound to captivate the taste buds of everyone who tries it. So, go ahead and treat yourself to a scoop (or two) of this homemade Nutella ice cream and savor every spoonful of this delightful frozen creation.
