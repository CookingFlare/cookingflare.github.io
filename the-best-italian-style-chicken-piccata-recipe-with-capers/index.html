The Best Italian-Style Chicken Piccata Recipe with Capers


Chicken piccata is a classic Italian dish that is loved for its tangy and savory flavors. The combination of tender chicken, zesty lemon, and briny capers creates a dish that is both refreshing and satisfying. In this recipe, we will guide you through the steps to create the best Italian-style chicken piccata with capers.

Ingredients

  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • Salt and black pepper, to taste
  • 1/2 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 4 tablespoons unsalted butter
  • 1/2 cup chicken broth
  • 1/4 cup fresh lemon juice
  • 1/4 cup brined capers, rinsed
  • 1/4 cup fresh parsley, chopped

Instructions

  1. Start by preparing the chicken breasts. Place them between two sheets of plastic wrap and gently pound them to an even thickness. Season each breast with salt and black pepper to taste.

  2. In a shallow dish, combine the all-purpose flour with salt and black pepper. Dredge each chicken breast in the flour mixture, shaking off any excess.

  3. Heat the olive oil and 2 tablespoons of butter in a large skillet over medium-high heat. Once the butter has melted and the pan is hot, add the chicken breasts. Cook for about 3-4 minutes on each side, or until they are golden brown and cooked through. Remove the chicken from the skillet and set aside.

  4. In the same skillet, add the chicken broth, lemon juice, and capers. Bring the mixture to a boil, scraping the bottom of the pan to release any browned bits. Reduce the heat to low and simmer for about 5 minutes, or until the sauce has slightly thickened.

  5. Return the chicken breasts to the skillet, coating them with the sauce. Cook for an additional 2-3 minutes, allowing the flavors to meld together.

  6. Remove the skillet from the heat and add the remaining 2 tablespoons of butter. Swirl the butter around the pan until it melts and forms a glossy sauce.

  7. Sprinkle the fresh parsley over the chicken piccata, and give it a final squeeze of lemon juice for an extra burst of flavor.

  8. Serve the Italian-style chicken piccata with capers hot, alongside your favorite pasta or a bed of fluffy white rice. It pairs perfectly with a crisp green salad or steamed vegetables.

Tips and Variations

  • For an extra touch of richness, you can add a splash of white wine to the sauce before simmering.
  • If you prefer a thicker sauce, you can whisk in a teaspoon of cornstarch mixed with water to the skillet before adding the chicken back in.
  • Experiment with different herbs and spices, such as thyme or garlic powder, to customize the flavor to your liking.
  • If you don’t have capers on hand, you can substitute them with green olives for a slightly different twist.

Enjoy this delicious Italian-style chicken piccata recipe with capers, and savor the flavors of Italy in the comfort of your own home. Buon appetito!
