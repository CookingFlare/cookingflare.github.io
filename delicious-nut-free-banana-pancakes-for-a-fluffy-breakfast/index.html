Delicious Nut-Free Banana Pancakes for a Fluffy Breakfast


Who doesn’t love waking up to a stack of fluffy pancakes? And when those pancakes are made with wholesome ingredients and bursting with the natural sweetness of bananas, you know you’re in for a treat. In this recipe, we’ll show you how to make delicious nut-free banana pancakes that are perfect for a fluffy breakfast. So grab your apron and let’s get cooking!

Ingredients

  • 2 ripe bananas, mashed
  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons granulated sugar
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 1/2 cups milk
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 tablespoons unsalted butter, melted
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Instructions

  1. In a large mixing bowl, combine the mashed bananas, flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt. Mix well until all the ingredients are evenly incorporated.

  2. In a separate bowl, whisk together the milk, eggs, melted butter, and vanilla extract. Pour the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients and stir until just combined. Be careful not to overmix, as this can result in dense pancakes.

  3. Heat a non-stick skillet or griddle over medium heat. Once hot, lightly grease the surface with butter or cooking spray. Pour 1/4 cup of batter onto the skillet for each pancake. Cook until bubbles start to form on the surface, then flip and cook for an additional 1-2 minutes, or until golden brown.

  4. Repeat the process with the remaining batter, adding more butter or cooking spray to the skillet as needed. This recipe should yield around 12 pancakes, depending on the size.

  5. Serve the pancakes warm with your favorite toppings. Fresh fruits, maple syrup, honey, or a sprinkle of powdered sugar are all great options. Get creative and add a dollop of whipped cream or a drizzle of chocolate sauce for an extra indulgence.

Tips and Variations

  • To make these pancakes even more flavorful, you can add a pinch of cinnamon or nutmeg to the batter. These warm spices will complement the sweetness of the bananas perfectly.

  • If you prefer a lighter texture, you can substitute half of the all-purpose flour with whole wheat flour. This will add a nutty flavor and increase the nutritional value of the pancakes.

  • For a gluten-free version, you can use a gluten-free all-purpose flour blend. Make sure to check the label and ensure that the flour is specifically labeled as gluten-free.

  • If you’re feeling adventurous, you can add some mix-ins to the batter. Chopped walnuts, chocolate chips, or blueberries are all excellent choices. Just fold them into the batter gently before cooking.

The Perfect Fluffy Breakfast

These nut-free banana pancakes are not only delicious but also a great way to start your day with a nutritious and satisfying meal. The natural sweetness of the bananas eliminates the need for additional sugar, making these pancakes a healthier option. Plus, they’re so easy to make that you can whip them up whenever you’re in the mood for a fluffy breakfast.

So next time you’re craving pancakes, give this recipe a try. Your taste buds will thank you, and your family and friends will be asking for seconds. Enjoy your fluffy breakfast!
