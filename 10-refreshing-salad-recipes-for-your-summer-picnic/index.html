10 Refreshing Salad Recipes for Your Summer Picnic


Summer is the perfect time to enjoy the great outdoors and indulge in delicious, refreshing salads. Whether you’re planning a picnic at the beach, a hike in the mountains, or a backyard barbecue, these 10 salad recipes will add a burst of flavor and color to your summer spread. From tangy citrus salads to hearty grain bowls, there’s something for everyone to enjoy. So grab your picnic blanket and let’s dive into these refreshing creations!

1. Watermelon Feta Salad

Ingredients:

  • 4 cups cubed watermelon
  • 1 cup crumbled feta cheese
  • 1/2 cup fresh mint leaves, torn
  • 1/4 cup red onion, thinly sliced
  • 2 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon balsamic vinegar
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a large bowl, combine watermelon, feta cheese, mint leaves, and red onion.
  2. Drizzle with olive oil and balsamic vinegar.
  3. Season with salt and pepper.
  4. Toss gently to combine.
  5. Serve chilled.

2. Caprese Skewers

Ingredients:

  • 1 pint cherry tomatoes
  • 8 ounces fresh mozzarella balls
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • Balsamic glaze
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Thread a cherry tomato, a piece of mozzarella, and a basil leaf onto a skewer.
  2. Repeat with remaining ingredients.
  3. Drizzle with balsamic glaze.
  4. Season with salt and pepper.
  5. Serve immediately.

3. Greek Quinoa Salad

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup cooked quinoa
  • 1 cup cucumber, diced
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1/2 cup Kalamata olives, pitted and halved
  • 1/2 cup crumbled feta cheese
  • 1/4 cup red onion, thinly sliced
  • 2 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a large bowl, combine quinoa, cucumber, cherry tomatoes, Kalamata olives, feta cheese, and red onion.
  2. In a small bowl, whisk together olive oil, lemon juice, dried oregano, salt, and pepper.
  3. Pour dressing over the salad and toss gently to combine.
  4. Serve chilled.

4. Taco Salad

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound ground beef or turkey
  • 1 packet taco seasoning
  • 4 cups lettuce, shredded
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1 cup black beans, rinsed and drained
  • 1/2 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1/2 cup tortilla chips, crushed
  • 1/4 cup red onion, diced
  • 1/4 cup cilantro, chopped
  • 1/4 cup sour cream
  • 1/4 cup salsa

Instructions:

  1. In a large skillet, cook ground beef or turkey over medium heat until browned.
  2. Drain excess fat and stir in taco seasoning.
  3. In a large bowl, combine lettuce, cherry tomatoes, black beans, shredded cheddar cheese, tortilla chips, red onion, and cilantro.
  4. Top with cooked meat.
  5. Serve with sour cream and salsa.

5. Asian Slaw

Ingredients:

  • 4 cups shredded cabbage
  • 1 cup shredded carrots
  • 1/2 cup chopped scallions
  • 1/4 cup chopped cilantro
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons rice vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon sesame oil
  • 1 tablespoon honey
  • 1 teaspoon grated ginger
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a large bowl, combine shredded cabbage, shredded carrots, chopped scallions, and chopped cilantro.
  2. In a small bowl, whisk together soy sauce, rice vinegar, sesame oil, honey, grated ginger, garlic powder, salt, and pepper.
  3. Pour dressing over the slaw and toss gently to combine.
  4. Serve chilled.

6. Berry Spinach Salad

Ingredients:

  • 4 cups baby spinach
  • 1 cup strawberries, sliced
  • 1 cup blueberries
  • 1/4 cup crumbled goat cheese
  • 1/4 cup candied pecans
  • 2 tablespoons balsamic glaze

Instructions:

  1. In a large bowl, combine baby spinach, strawberries, blueberries, crumbled goat cheese, and candied pecans.
  2. Drizzle with balsamic glaze.
  3. Toss gently to combine.
  4. Serve immediately.

7. Mediterranean Pasta Salad

Ingredients:

  • 8 ounces cooked pasta (such as fusilli or penne)
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1 cup cucumber, diced
  • 1/2 cup Kalamata olives, pitted and halved
  • 1/4 cup red onion, thinly sliced
  • 1/4 cup crumbled feta cheese
  • 2 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a large bowl, combine cooked pasta, cherry tomatoes, cucumber, Kalamata olives, red onion, and crumbled feta cheese.
  2. In a small bowl, whisk together olive oil, lemon juice, dried oregano, salt, and pepper.
  3. Pour dressing over the salad and toss gently to combine.
  4. Serve chilled.

8. Mexican Street Corn Salad

Ingredients:

  • 4 cups cooked corn kernels
  • 1/2 cup mayonnaise
  • 1/2 cup sour cream
  • 1/2 cup crumbled cotija cheese
  • 1/4 cup chopped cilantro
  • 1 tablespoon lime juice
  • 1 teaspoon chili powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a large bowl, combine cooked corn kernels, mayonnaise, sour cream, cotija cheese, chopped cilantro, lime juice, chili powder, salt, and pepper.
  2. Toss gently to combine.
  3. Serve chilled.

9. Caesar Salad with Grilled Shrimp

Ingredients:

  • 4 cups romaine lettuce, torn
  • 1 cup croutons
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/4 cup Caesar dressing
  • 1 pound grilled shrimp

Instructions:

  1. In a large bowl, combine romaine lettuce, croutons, and grated Parmesan cheese.
  2. Drizzle with Caesar dressing.
  3. Toss gently to combine.
  4. Top with grilled shrimp.
  5. Serve immediately.

10. Avocado and Corn Salad

Ingredients:

  • 4 cups mixed greens
  • 1 avocado, diced
  • 1 cup cooked corn kernels
  • 1/2 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1/4 cup red onion, thinly sliced
  • 2 tablespoons lime juice
  • 2 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon chopped cilantro
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a large bowl, combine mixed greens, diced avocado, cooked corn kernels, cherry tomatoes, and red onion.
  2. In a small bowl, whisk together lime juice, olive oil, chopped cilantro, salt, and pepper.
  3. Pour dressing over the salad and toss gently to combine.
  4. Serve chilled.

These 10 refreshing salad recipes are sure to delight your taste buds and keep you cool during the hot summer months. Whether you’re packing them for a picnic or enjoying them at home, these salads are a delicious and healthy way to embrace the flavors of the season. So gather your friends and family, head outdoors, and savor the taste of summer with these vibrant and satisfying dishes. Happy picnicking!
