Paleo-Friendly Mediterranean Fish Recipes: Fresh and Flavorful


Are you someone who follows a paleo diet but still craves the flavors of the Mediterranean? Look no further! In this blog post, we will explore some delicious and nutritious paleo-friendly Mediterranean fish recipes that are sure to satisfy your taste buds. From light and refreshing salads to hearty and satisfying mains, these recipes will bring the flavors of the Mediterranean to your plate.

Recipe 1: Grilled Lemon Garlic Salmon

Ingredients:

  • 4 salmon fillets
  • 2 lemons, sliced
  • 4 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons of olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the grill to medium heat.
  2. In a small bowl, combine the minced garlic, olive oil, salt, and pepper.
  3. Place each salmon fillet on a sheet of aluminum foil.
  4. Brush the garlic mixture onto the salmon fillets, ensuring they are evenly coated.
  5. Top each fillet with a few slices of lemon.
  6. Wrap the aluminum foil tightly around the salmon, creating a packet.
  7. Place the packets on the grill and cook for 10-12 minutes, or until the salmon flakes easily with a fork.
  8. Carefully open the foil packets and serve the grilled lemon garlic salmon with a side salad or roasted vegetables.

Recipe 2: Mediterranean Baked Cod

Ingredients:

  • 4 cod fillets
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1/2 cup Kalamata olives, pitted and sliced
  • 1/4 cup fresh basil, chopped
  • 2 tablespoons capers
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Place the cod fillets in a baking dish and season with salt and pepper.
  3. In a small bowl, combine the cherry tomatoes, Kalamata olives, basil, capers, and olive oil.
  4. Spoon the tomato mixture over the cod fillets, ensuring they are evenly covered.
  5. Bake in the preheated oven for 15-20 minutes, or until the fish is cooked through and flakes easily with a fork.
  6. Serve the Mediterranean baked cod with a side of steamed vegetables or cauliflower rice.

Recipe 3: Greek-Inspired Tuna Salad

Ingredients:

  • 2 cans of tuna, drained
  • 1/2 cup cucumber, diced
  • 1/2 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1/4 cup red onion, thinly sliced
  • 1/4 cup Kalamata olives, pitted and sliced
  • 2 tablespoons fresh dill, chopped
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • Juice of 1 lemon
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a large bowl, combine the tuna, cucumber, cherry tomatoes, red onion, Kalamata olives, and fresh dill.
  2. In a small bowl, whisk together the olive oil, lemon juice, salt, and pepper.
  3. Pour the dressing over the tuna mixture and toss to combine.
  4. Refrigerate for at least 30 minutes to allow the flavors to meld together.
  5. Serve the Greek-inspired tuna salad on a bed of mixed greens or in lettuce wraps for a light and refreshing meal.

Recipe 4: Zucchini Noodle Shrimp Scampi

Ingredients:

  • 2 medium zucchini, spiralized or julienned
  • 1 pound shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 4 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons ghee or clarified butter
  • Juice of 1 lemon
  • 1/4 cup fresh parsley, chopped
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Heat the ghee or clarified butter in a large skillet over medium heat.
  2. Add the minced garlic and cook until fragrant, about 1 minute.
  3. Add the shrimp to the skillet and cook until pink and opaque, about 3-4 minutes per side.
  4. Remove the shrimp from the skillet and set aside.
  5. In the same skillet, add the zucchini noodles and cook until tender, about 2-3 minutes.
  6. Add the lemon juice, fresh parsley, salt, and pepper to the skillet and toss to combine.
  7. Return the shrimp to the skillet and cook for an additional minute to heat through.
  8. Serve the zucchini noodle shrimp scampi with a sprinkle of fresh parsley on top.

Enjoy these paleo-friendly Mediterranean fish recipes and savor the fresh and flavorful flavors they bring to your table. Whether you’re following a paleo diet or simply looking for healthy and delicious meals, these recipes are sure to become favorites in your kitchen. So go ahead and indulge in the vibrant and nourishing flavors of the Mediterranean!
