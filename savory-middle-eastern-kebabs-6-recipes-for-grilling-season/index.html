Savory Middle Eastern Kebabs: 6 Recipes for Grilling Season

Middle Eastern cuisine offers a delightful array of kebab recipes that are bursting with flavor and perfect for outdoor cooking. From marinated meats to vegetarian options, these six kebab recipes will elevate your grilling game and impress your family and friends.

1. Classic Shish Kebab

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound beef sirloin, cut into 1-inch cubes
  • 1 large onion, finely chopped
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons freshly squeezed lemon juice
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon ground coriander
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Skewers (soaked in water if wooden)

Instructions:

  1. In a large bowl, combine the chopped onion, minced garlic, olive oil, lemon juice, cumin, coriander, paprika, salt, and pepper.
  2. Add the beef cubes to the marinade, ensuring they are well coated. Cover and refrigerate for at least 2 hours, or overnight for the best flavor.
  3. Preheat your grill to medium-high heat. Thread the marinated beef onto the skewers.
  4. Grill the kebabs for about 10-12 minutes, turning occasionally, until they reach your desired level of doneness.
  5. Serve hot with pita bread, hummus, and a fresh salad.

2. Chicken Kofta Kebabs

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound ground chicken
  • 1 small onion, grated
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/4 cup fresh parsley, chopped
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon ground coriander
  • 1/2 teaspoon cayenne pepper
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Skewers

Instructions:

  1. In a mixing bowl, combine the ground chicken, grated onion, minced garlic, parsley, cumin, coriander, cayenne pepper, salt, and pepper. Mix well until all ingredients are thoroughly combined.
  2. Divide the mixture into 8 equal portions and shape them around the skewers into elongated patties.
  3. Preheat your grill to medium heat. Grill the kofta kebabs for about 10-15 minutes, turning until they are cooked through and have a nice char.
  4. Serve with a side of tzatziki sauce and warm pita.

3. Lamb Kebabs with Mint Yogurt Sauce

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound lamb shoulder, cut into 1-inch cubes
  • 4 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons red wine vinegar
  • 2 teaspoons ground cumin
  • 2 teaspoons ground coriander
  • 1 teaspoon cinnamon
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh mint leaves for garnish

For the Mint Yogurt Sauce:

  • 1 cup plain yogurt
  • 1/4 cup fresh mint, finely chopped
  • 1 teaspoon lemon juice
  • Salt to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, mix together the olive oil, red wine vinegar, cumin, coriander, cinnamon, salt, and pepper. Add the lamb cubes and marinate for at least 2 hours, preferably overnight.
  2. For the mint yogurt sauce, combine the yogurt, chopped mint, lemon juice, and salt in a small bowl. Mix well and set aside.
  3. Preheat the grill to medium-high heat. Thread the marinated lamb onto skewers.
  4. Grill the kebabs for about 8-10 minutes, turning occasionally, until they are cooked to your liking.
  5. Serve the lamb kebabs hot, drizzled with mint yogurt sauce and garnished with fresh mint leaves.

4. Spicy Shrimp Kebabs

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound large shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 3 tablespoons Sriracha sauce
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon smoked paprika
  • Juice of 1 lime
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Skewers

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, whisk together olive oil, Sriracha sauce, minced garlic, smoked paprika, lime juice, salt, and pepper.
  2. Add the shrimp to the marinade and toss to coat. Let marinate for 30 minutes.
  3. Preheat your grill to medium-high heat. Thread the shrimp onto skewers.
  4. Grill the shrimp for about 2-3 minutes on each side, until they turn pink and opaque.
  5. Serve immediately with lime wedges and a side of rice or quinoa salad.

5. Grilled Vegetable Kebabs

Ingredients:

  • 1 zucchini, sliced into thick rounds
  • 1 bell pepper, cut into chunks
  • 1 red onion, cut into wedges
  • 8 cherry tomatoes
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 2 teaspoons Italian seasoning
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Skewers

Instructions:

  1. In a large bowl, toss the zucchini, bell pepper, red onion, and cherry tomatoes with olive oil, Italian seasoning, salt, and pepper.
  2. Thread the vegetables onto skewers, alternating the types for a colorful presentation.
  3. Preheat your grill to medium heat. Grill the vegetable kebabs for about 8-10 minutes, turning occasionally until they are tender and slightly charred.
  4. Serve warm as a side dish or over a bed of couscous or quinoa.

6. Falafel Kebabs

Ingredients:

  • 1 can (15 ounces) chickpeas, drained and rinsed
  • 1 small onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/4 cup fresh parsley, chopped
  • 1/4 cup flour
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon ground coriander
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Skewers

Instructions:

  1. In a food processor, combine the chickpeas, onion, garlic, parsley, flour, cumin, coriander, salt, and pepper. Pulse until combined but still slightly chunky.
  2. Form the mixture into small patties or logs that can be easily skewered.
  3. Preheat your grill to medium heat. Thread the falafel onto skewers.
  4. Grill the falafel kebabs for about 5-7 minutes on each side, until they are golden brown and heated through.
  5. Serve with tahini sauce and a side salad.

Conclusion

With a variety of meats and vegetarian options, these kebabs cater to every palate. Enjoy the bold flavors, vibrant colors, and the joy of sharing delicious meals with loved ones. So fire up your grill and get ready for an unforgettable culinary experience!
