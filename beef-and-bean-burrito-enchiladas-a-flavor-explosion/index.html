Beef and Bean Burrito Enchiladas: A Flavor Explosion


  1. Prepare the Beef: In a large skillet, heat the olive oil over medium-high heat. Add the beef and cook until browned, about 3-4 minutes. Remove the beef from the skillet and set aside.
  2. Soften the Tortillas: Warm the tortillas by wrapping them in a damp paper towel and microwaving for 20-30 seconds. This will make them more pliable and easier to roll.
  3. Assemble the Enchiladas: In the same skillet, add the diced onion and cook until translucent, about 3-4 minutes. Add the minced garlic and cook for an additional minute.
  4. Add the Beans and Beef: Add the black beans, beef broth, cumin, paprika, and cayenne pepper to the skillet. Stir to combine. Add the cooked beef back into the skillet and stir to combine.
  5. Roll the Enchiladas: To assemble the enchiladas, lay a tortilla flat and spoon some of the beef and bean mixture onto the center of the tortilla. Roll the tortilla tightly and place seam-side down in a 9x13 inch baking dish. Repeat with the remaining tortillas and filling.
  6. Cover with Enchilada Sauce and Cheese: Pour the enchilada sauce over the rolled tortillas and sprinkle with shredded cheese (if using).
  7. Bake: Cover the dish with aluminum foil and bake in a preheated oven at 375°F (190°C) for 25-30 minutes. Remove the foil and bake for an additional 5-10 minutes, or until the cheese is melted and bubbly.
  8. Garnish: Sprinkle chopped cilantro (if using) over the top of the enchiladas and serve hot.

Tips and Variations

  • To add some crunch to the enchiladas, sprinkle some chopped fresh scallions or chopped pecans on top before baking.
  • For a spicy kick, add some diced jalapeños or serrano peppers to the beef and bean mixture.
  • To make this recipe vegetarian, substitute the beef with sautéed mushrooms or roasted sweet potatoes.
  • To make this recipe vegan, substitute the cheese with a dairy-free alternative and use vegan enchilada sauce.

Conclusion

Beef and Bean Burrito Enchiladas is a recipe that combines the flavors of Mexico and Spain with the comfort of a burrito. With its rich flavors, tender beef, and creamy cheese, this dish is sure to satisfy your taste buds and leave you wanting more. Whether you’re a meat-lover or a vegetarian, this recipe is sure to please. So go ahead, give it a try, and experience the flavor explosion for yourself!
