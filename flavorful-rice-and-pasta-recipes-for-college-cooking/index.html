Flavorful Rice and Pasta Recipes for College Cooking


Welcome, college chefs! Whether you’re a seasoned cook or a newbie in the kitchen, mastering the art of preparing delicious and budget-friendly meals is essential during your college years. Rice and pasta are two versatile ingredients that can form the basis of a wide array of mouthwatering dishes. In this blog post, we’ll explore flavorful rice and pasta recipes that are perfect for college cooking. These recipes are not only easy to make but also packed with flavor, ensuring that you can enjoy a satisfying meal without breaking the bank.

1. Creamy Garlic Parmesan Pasta

Ingredients:

  • 8 oz fettuccine pasta
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Cook the fettuccine pasta according to the package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a large skillet, melt the butter over medium heat. Add the minced garlic and sauté for 1-2 minutes until fragrant.
  3. Pour in the heavy cream and bring to a simmer. Let it cook for 5 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  4. Add the grated Parmesan cheese to the skillet and stir until it has melted and the sauce has thickened.
  5. Season the sauce with salt and pepper to taste.
  6. Add the cooked fettuccine to the skillet and toss until it is well coated with the creamy garlic Parmesan sauce.
  7. Garnish with fresh parsley and serve hot.

2. Spicy Cajun Shrimp and Rice

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb large shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 1 tbsp Cajun seasoning
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 bell pepper, diced
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 2 cups cooked white rice
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh lemon wedges for serving

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, toss the peeled and deveined shrimp with Cajun seasoning until evenly coated.
  2. Heat the olive oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat. Add the seasoned shrimp and cook for 2-3 minutes on each side until pink and cooked through. Remove the shrimp from the skillet and set aside.
  3. In the same skillet, add the diced bell pepper and onion. Sauté until they are tender.
  4. Add the cooked white rice to the skillet and stir to combine with the vegetables.
  5. Return the cooked shrimp to the skillet and toss everything together until heated through.
  6. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  7. Serve the spicy Cajun shrimp and rice with fresh lemon wedges for an extra burst of flavor.

3. One-Pot Chicken Alfredo Rice

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb boneless, skinless chicken breasts, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 cup uncooked white rice
  • 2 cups chicken broth
  • 1 cup Alfredo sauce
  • 1 cup frozen peas
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Freshly grated Parmesan cheese for serving

Instructions:

  1. In a large pot or deep skillet, heat the olive oil over medium-high heat. Add the chicken pieces and cook until they are no longer pink.
  2. Add the uncooked white rice to the pot and stir to combine with the chicken.
  3. Pour in the chicken broth and bring to a boil. Reduce the heat to low, cover, and let it simmer for 18-20 minutes, or until the rice is tender and has absorbed the liquid.
  4. Stir in the Alfredo sauce and frozen peas, allowing them to heat through.
  5. Season the dish with salt and pepper to taste.
  6. Serve the one-pot chicken Alfredo rice hot, topped with freshly grated Parmesan cheese.

These flavorful rice and pasta recipes are perfect for college students looking to prepare satisfying meals without spending hours in the kitchen. Whether you’re craving a creamy pasta dish, a spicy shrimp and rice combo, or a hearty one-pot chicken Alfredo rice, these recipes have got you covered. Get creative with your cooking and enjoy delicious homemade meals that will keep you fueled throughout your college journey!
