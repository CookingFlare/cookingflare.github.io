Flavorful Instant Pot Teriyaki Chicken Recipes


Are you ready to tantalize your taste buds with the sweet and savory flavors of teriyaki chicken? This dish, with its roots in Japanese cuisine, has become a beloved favorite around the world. Thanks to the Instant Pot, creating this delicious meal has never been easier. In this blog post, we will explore a variety of flavorful Instant Pot teriyaki chicken recipes that are not only quick to prepare but also incredibly satisfying. Whether you’re a busy parent, a student, or simply someone who enjoys cooking, these recipes are designed to fit your lifestyle.

What is Teriyaki Chicken?

Teriyaki chicken is a dish that features chicken marinated in a mixture of soy sauce, sake, and mirin, which is then grilled or broiled to perfection. The result is a beautifully glazed, slightly sweet, and savory dish that pairs wonderfully with rice and vegetables. Using the Instant Pot allows us to infuse flavors deeply into the chicken while keeping it moist and tender.

Benefits of Cooking with an Instant Pot

The Instant Pot is a versatile kitchen appliance that combines the functions of a pressure cooker, slow cooker, rice cooker, steamer, sauté pan, and even a yogurt maker. Here are some benefits that make it ideal for cooking teriyaki chicken:

  • Speed: The Instant Pot significantly reduces cooking time, allowing you to whip up meals quickly without sacrificing flavor.
  • Flavor Infusion: Pressure cooking helps to lock in flavors, ensuring that the teriyaki marinade penetrates the chicken thoroughly.
  • Ease of Use: With just a few simple steps and minimal cleanup, you can have a delicious teriyaki chicken meal ready in no time.

Classic Instant Pot Teriyaki Chicken

Ingredients

  • 2 pounds boneless, skinless chicken thighs
  • 1 cup soy sauce (or tamari for gluten-free)
  • 1/2 cup brown sugar
  • 1/4 cup rice vinegar
  • 2 tablespoons sesame oil
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon fresh ginger, grated
  • 1 tablespoon cornstarch
  • 2 tablespoons water
  • Sesame seeds and chopped green onions for garnish

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Marinade: In a bowl, whisk together soy sauce, brown sugar, rice vinegar, sesame oil, garlic, and ginger until the sugar has dissolved.

  2. Marinate the Chicken: Place the chicken thighs in a resealable bag or shallow dish and pour the marinade over them. Let it marinate for at least 30 minutes, or up to overnight in the refrigerator for deeper flavor.

  3. Sauté: Set the Instant Pot to sauté mode. Once hot, add the marinated chicken thighs (reserve the marinade) and sear for about 2-3 minutes on each side until browned.

  4. Pressure Cook: Pour the reserved marinade over the chicken. Close the lid, select the pressure cook setting, and cook on high pressure for 10 minutes. Allow it to naturally release for 5 minutes before switching to a quick release.

  5. Thicken the Sauce: Remove the chicken and set it aside. In a small bowl, mix cornstarch and water to create a slurry. Set the Instant Pot back to sauté mode and stir in the slurry. Cook for a few minutes until the sauce thickens.

  6. Serve: Slice the chicken and serve it over rice, drizzled with the thickened teriyaki sauce. Garnish with sesame seeds and chopped green onions.

Instant Pot Teriyaki Chicken with Pineapple

For a tropical twist, try this delicious Instant Pot teriyaki chicken with pineapple. The sweetness of the pineapple complements the savory teriyaki sauce beautifully.

Ingredients

  • 2 pounds boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1 cup soy sauce
  • 1/2 cup brown sugar
  • 1/4 cup pineapple juice
  • 1 tablespoon rice vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon sesame oil
  • 1 can (20 ounces) pineapple chunks in juice, drained
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon cornstarch
  • 2 tablespoons water
  • Chopped cilantro for garnish

Instructions

  1. Mix the Marinade: In a bowl, whisk together soy sauce, brown sugar, pineapple juice, rice vinegar, sesame oil, and garlic.

  2. Marinate the Chicken: Place the chicken breasts in a resealable bag and pour the marinade over them. Marinate for at least 30 minutes.

  3. Sauté the Chicken: Set the Instant Pot to sauté mode. Once hot, add the marinated chicken (reserve the marinade) and sear for 2-3 minutes on each side.

  4. Add Pineapple: Pour the reserved marinade over the chicken and add the pineapple chunks. Close the lid and cook on high pressure for 10 minutes.

  5. Thicken the Sauce: Remove the chicken and pineapple. Mix cornstarch with water to create a slurry, then add to the pot. Set to sauté and cook until the sauce thickens.

  6. Serve: Slice the chicken and serve with the pineapple on top, drizzled with the thickened sauce. Garnish with chopped cilantro for added freshness.

Instant Pot Teriyaki Chicken Stir-Fry

Take your teriyaki chicken to the next level by adding a medley of colorful vegetables. This stir-fry is not only flavorful but also packed with nutrients.

Ingredients

  • 2 pounds boneless, skinless chicken thighs, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 1 cup soy sauce
  • 1/2 cup brown sugar
  • 1 tablespoon rice vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon sesame oil
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon fresh ginger, grated
  • 2 cups mixed vegetables (bell peppers, broccoli, carrots, snap peas)
  • 1 tablespoon cornstarch
  • 2 tablespoons water
  • Sesame seeds and green onions for garnish

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Marinade: Combine soy sauce, brown sugar, rice vinegar, sesame oil, garlic, and ginger in a bowl.

  2. Marinate the Chicken: Place the chicken pieces in a resealable bag and pour the marinade over them. Marinate for at least 30 minutes.

  3. Sauté the Chicken: Set the Instant Pot to sauté mode. Add the chicken (reserve the marinade) and cook for 3-4 minutes until browned.

  4. Add Vegetables: Pour the reserved marinade over the chicken and add the mixed vegetables. Close the lid and cook on high pressure for 5 minutes.

  5. Thicken the Sauce: Remove the chicken and vegetables. Mix cornstarch with water to create a slurry, then add to the pot. Use sauté mode to thicken the sauce.

  6. Serve: Serve the stir-fry over rice or noodles, drizzled with the thickened teriyaki sauce. Garnish with sesame seeds and chopped green onions.

Tips for Perfect Teriyaki Chicken

  • Use Fresh Ingredients: Fresh garlic and ginger will enhance the flavor profile of your teriyaki chicken. Avoid using bottled versions if possible.
  • Marinate Longer: For the best flavor, marinate your chicken for several hours or overnight.
  • Adjust Sweetness: Feel free to adjust the brown sugar according to your taste preference. You can also substitute it with honey or maple syrup for a different sweetness.
  • Experiment with Vegetables: Add your favorite vegetables or whatever is seasonal for a customized dish.

Conclusion

With these flavorful Instant Pot teriyaki chicken recipes, you can easily bring the taste of Japanese cuisine to your home kitchen. Each recipe is designed to be simple yet packed with flavor, making it perfect for weeknight dinners or meal prep. The versatility of the Instant Pot ensures that you can create a delicious meal in a fraction of the time it would take using traditional methods.

So why not give these recipes a try? Gather your ingredients, fire up the Instant Pot, and treat yourself and your loved ones to a delightful teriyaki chicken dinner. Don’t forget to share your cooking experience and variations with friends and family. Happy cooking!
