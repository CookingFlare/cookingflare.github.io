Caprese Flatbread: A Fresh and Light Option


When it comes to Italian cuisine, there are few dishes more iconic than the Caprese salad. Fresh mozzarella, juicy tomatoes, and fragrant basil come together in a harmonious union that’s both simple and sublime. But why limit yourself to just a salad when you can elevate this classic combination to a whole new level by transforming it into a crispy, cheesy flatbread?

This Caprese flatbread is a game-changer for anyone looking for a fresh and light option that’s perfect for a quick lunch or dinner. The key to its success lies in the quality of the ingredients and the simplicity of the preparation. By using the freshest mozzarella, ripest tomatoes, and most fragrant basil, you’ll be able to capture the essence of the original salad in every bite.

To start, preheat your oven to 425°F (220°C). While it’s heating up, roll out a piece of pizza dough to your desired thickness. You can use a store-bought dough or make your own from scratch – whichever you prefer.

Next, spread a layer of pesto sauce over the dough, leaving a 1/2-inch border around the edges. This will add a rich, herby flavor to the flatbread that complements the other ingredients perfectly.

Now it’s time for the star of the show: the mozzarella. Slice a ball of fresh mozzarella into thin rounds and arrange them on top of the pesto sauce. You should aim for a single layer, but don’t worry if some of the slices overlap slightly.

Once the mozzarella is in place, it’s time to add the tomatoes. Slice a few fresh tomatoes into thin wedges and scatter them over the mozzarella. You can use any variety of tomato you like, but cherry tomatoes or grape tomatoes work particularly well for this recipe.

Finally, sprinkle some fresh basil leaves over the top of the flatbread. This will add a bright, herbaceous flavor that ties the whole dish together.

Place the flatbread in the oven and bake for 15-20 minutes, or until the crust is golden brown and the cheese is melted and bubbly. Remove it from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes before slicing and serving.

This Caprese flatbread is perfect for a quick and easy lunch or dinner, and it’s also a great option for a light snack or appetizer. The best part is that it’s incredibly versatile – you can customize it to your taste by adding other ingredients like prosciutto, arugula, or balsamic glaze.

So the next time you’re in the mood for something fresh and light, give this Caprese flatbread a try. You won’t be disappointed!

Ingredients:

  • 1 piece of pizza dough
  • 1/4 cup pesto sauce
  • 8 oz fresh mozzarella, sliced
  • 2-3 fresh tomatoes, sliced
  • 1/4 cup fresh basil leaves
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 425°F (220°C).
  2. Roll out the pizza dough to your desired thickness.
  3. Spread the pesto sauce over the dough, leaving a 1/2-inch border around the edges.
  4. Arrange the mozzarella slices on top of the pesto sauce.
  5. Add the tomato slices and sprinkle with salt and pepper to taste.
  6. Top with fresh basil leaves.
  7. Bake the flatbread in the oven for 15-20 minutes, or until the crust is golden brown and the cheese is melted and bubbly.
  8. Remove from the oven and let cool for a few minutes before slicing and serving.
