Vegan Sweet Potato Fries: Crispy and Flavorful Oven-Baked Goodness


Who doesn’t love a plate of perfectly crispy and flavorful sweet potato fries? They’re not only delicious but also a healthier alternative to regular fries. Whether you’re a vegan or simply looking to incorporate more plant-based options into your diet, these vegan sweet potato fries are a must-try!

Ingredients:

  • 2 large sweet potatoes
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon onion powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 425°F (220°C). Line a baking sheet with parchment paper or a silicone baking mat.

  2. Start by preparing the sweet potatoes. Wash them thoroughly and pat them dry. Peel the skin off if desired, although leaving the skin on can add extra texture and nutrients.

  3. Cut the sweet potatoes into even-sized fries. Aim for a thickness of about 1/4 inch to ensure they cook evenly.

  4. In a large bowl, combine the olive oil, paprika, garlic powder, onion powder, salt, and black pepper. Mix well to create a flavorful seasoning.

  5. Add the sweet potato fries to the bowl and toss them gently to coat them evenly with the seasoning mixture. Make sure each fry is well coated for maximum flavor.

  6. Transfer the seasoned sweet potato fries to the prepared baking sheet, arranging them in a single layer. This will help them crisp up nicely.

  7. Place the baking sheet in the preheated oven and bake for 20-25 minutes, or until the fries are golden brown and crispy. Make sure to flip them halfway through the cooking time to ensure even browning.

  8. Once the fries are done, remove them from the oven and let them cool for a few minutes before serving. This will allow them to become even crispier.

  9. Serve the vegan sweet potato fries with your favorite dipping sauce or enjoy them as they are. They’re perfect as a side dish, snack, or even as a topping for salads and sandwiches.

Tips:

  • For extra crispiness, you can soak the cut sweet potatoes in cold water for about 30 minutes before seasoning and baking them. This helps remove some of the starch and results in a crunchier texture.

  • Experiment with different seasonings and spices to customize the flavor of your sweet potato fries. Smoked paprika, cumin, or chili powder can add a delightful twist.

  • Don’t overcrowd the baking sheet. If you have a lot of fries, it’s better to use two baking sheets to ensure they cook evenly and become crispy.

  • Keep an eye on the fries towards the end of the cooking time as they can quickly go from golden brown to burnt. Adjust the cooking time if needed.

  • These vegan sweet potato fries are best enjoyed fresh out of the oven. However, if you have leftovers, you can reheat them in the oven at 350°F (175°C) for a few minutes to regain their crispiness.

Enjoy these crispy and flavorful vegan sweet potato fries as a guilt-free indulgence. They’re a crowd-pleasing dish that will satisfy your cravings while nourishing your body with wholesome ingredients. So go ahead, fire up your oven, and treat yourself to a batch of these delicious oven-baked goodies!
