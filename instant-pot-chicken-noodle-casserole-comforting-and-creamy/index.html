Instant Pot Chicken Noodle Casserole: Comforting and Creamy


Are you craving a delicious and comforting meal that is also quick and easy to make? Look no further than this Instant Pot Chicken Noodle Casserole recipe! Packed with tender chicken, hearty noodles, and a creamy sauce, this dish is guaranteed to satisfy your taste buds and warm your soul.

Ingredients

  • 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 8 ounces egg noodles
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 medium onion, diced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup sliced mushrooms
  • 1 cup frozen peas
  • 1 cup diced carrots
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • 1 teaspoon dried parsley
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 2 cups chicken broth
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1 cup shredded cheddar cheese

Instructions

  1. Start by setting your Instant Pot to sauté mode. Add the olive oil, diced onion, and minced garlic. Sauté for a few minutes until the onions become translucent and the garlic is fragrant.

  2. Next, add the sliced mushrooms and diced carrots to the pot. Sauté for an additional 2-3 minutes until the vegetables start to soften.

  3. Turn off the sauté mode and add the chicken breasts to the pot. Sprinkle the dried thyme, dried parsley, salt, and black pepper over the chicken. Pour in the chicken broth.

  4. Secure the lid on the Instant Pot and set the valve to the sealing position. Cook on high pressure for 10 minutes. Once the cooking time is complete, perform a quick release of the pressure.

  5. Carefully remove the chicken breasts from the pot and shred them using two forks. Return the shredded chicken to the Instant Pot.

  6. Add the egg noodles and frozen peas to the pot. Give everything a good stir to combine.

  7. Secure the lid on the Instant Pot again and set the valve to the sealing position. Cook on high pressure for 4 minutes. Once the cooking time is complete, perform a quick release of the pressure.

  8. Open the lid and stir in the heavy cream and shredded cheddar cheese. Continue stirring until the cheese has melted and the sauce is creamy.

  9. Serve your Instant Pot Chicken Noodle Casserole hot and enjoy the comforting flavors and creamy texture. This dish is perfect for a cozy night in or as a crowd-pleasing dinner option.

Tips and Variations

  • Feel free to customize this recipe by adding your favorite vegetables or herbs. You can experiment with different combinations to suit your taste.
  • For an extra kick of flavor, you can add a splash of hot sauce or Worcestershire sauce to the creamy sauce.
  • If you prefer a crusty topping, transfer the casserole to an oven-safe dish, sprinkle with breadcrumbs, and broil for a few minutes until golden brown.

So there you have it – an easy and delicious Instant Pot Chicken Noodle Casserole recipe that will become a family favorite in no time. With the help of your trusty Instant Pot, you can enjoy a comforting and creamy meal without spending hours in the kitchen. Give this recipe a try, and let the warm and satisfying flavors transport you to comfort food heaven.
