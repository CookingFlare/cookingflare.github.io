BBQ Pork Ribs Sliders: Bite-Sized Meaty Goodness


Are you craving a delicious and satisfying meal that packs a punch of flavor? Look no further than these BBQ Pork Ribs Sliders! These bite-sized meaty goodness are perfect for a summer BBQ, game day party, or any occasion where you want to impress your guests with a mouthwatering treat. The combination of tender pork ribs, tangy barbecue sauce, and soft slider buns is simply irresistible. So fire up your grill and get ready to indulge in this finger-licking delight!

Ingredients

To make these BBQ Pork Ribs Sliders, you will need:

  • 2 racks of pork ribs
  • 1 cup of your favorite BBQ sauce
  • 1 tablespoon of brown sugar
  • 1 teaspoon of smoked paprika
  • 1 teaspoon of garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon of onion powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Slider buns
  • Pickles and coleslaw (optional, for serving)

Instructions

  1. Preheat your grill to medium heat. While the grill is heating up, prepare the ribs by removing the membrane from the back of the racks. This will help the flavors penetrate the meat and ensure a tender result.

  2. In a small bowl, mix together the brown sugar, smoked paprika, garlic powder, onion powder, salt, and pepper. This spice rub will add a delicious depth of flavor to the ribs.

  3. Rub the spice mixture all over the pork ribs, making sure to coat them evenly. Let the ribs sit at room temperature for about 15 minutes to allow the flavors to meld.

  4. Once the grill is hot, place the ribs directly on the grates, bone side down. Close the lid and let them cook for about 2 hours, or until the meat is tender and starts to pull away from the bones. Make sure to maintain a consistent temperature of around 275°F (135°C) for optimal results.

  5. While the ribs are cooking, prepare the BBQ sauce by combining your favorite BBQ sauce with a touch of brown sugar, garlic powder, and onion powder. This will enhance the flavors and create a sweet and tangy sauce that complements the smoky ribs perfectly.

  6. After the ribs have cooked for 2 hours, brush them generously with the BBQ sauce, making sure to coat all sides. Close the lid and let them cook for an additional 15-30 minutes, or until the sauce has caramelized and developed a sticky glaze.

  7. Remove the ribs from the grill and let them rest for a few minutes. This will allow the juices to redistribute, resulting in a more flavorful and tender bite.

  8. Once the ribs have rested, slice them into smaller portions that will fit perfectly on the slider buns. Place each rib section on a bun and top with pickles and coleslaw if desired. The combination of the tender meat, tangy sauce, and crunchy toppings will create a flavor explosion in every bite.

Enjoy these BBQ Pork Ribs Sliders with your favorite side dishes and a cold beverage. They are perfect for sharing, but be prepared for them to disappear quickly! The smoky aroma, tender meat, and irresistible flavors will have everyone coming back for more. So gather your friends and family, fire up the grill, and indulge in these bite-sized meaty goodness. Trust me, these sliders will be the star of any gathering. Happy grilling!
