The History of Churros Churros have a fascinating history that dates back to the 16th century. This traditional Spanish pastry is believed to have been inspired by the youtiao, a Chinese fried dough. Spanish shepherds, who were often in remote areas with no access to bakeries, created churros as a convenient and delicious snack that could be easily prepared over an open fire. The name “churro” is thought to derive from the Spanish word for a horn, “churra,” reflecting the shape of these delightful treats. ...
