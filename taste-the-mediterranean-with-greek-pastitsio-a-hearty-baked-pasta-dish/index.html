Taste the Mediterranean with Greek Pastitsio: A Hearty Baked Pasta Dish


If you’re looking to add a taste of the Mediterranean to your dinner table, look no further than Greek Pastitsio. This hearty baked pasta dish is a true crowd-pleaser, with its layers of tender pasta, rich meat sauce, and creamy béchamel topping. It’s a classic comfort food that will transport you straight to the sunny shores of Greece.

Ingredients

For the meat sauce:

  • 500g ground beef
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 can (400g) crushed tomatoes
  • 2 tablespoons tomato paste
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • Salt and pepper to taste

For the pasta:

  • 500g penne rigate or macaroni
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • Salt for boiling water

For the béchamel sauce:

  • 50g butter
  • 1/2 cup all-purpose flour
  • 4 cups milk
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground nutmeg
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 large eggs, beaten

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 180°C (350°F).

  2. To make the meat sauce, heat a large frying pan over medium heat. Add the ground beef and cook until browned, breaking it up with a spoon as it cooks. Remove any excess fat from the pan.

  3. Add the chopped onion and minced garlic to the pan and cook until softened, about 5 minutes.

  4. Stir in the crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, dried oregano, ground cinnamon, salt, and pepper. Let the sauce simmer for about 15 minutes, stirring occasionally.

  5. While the sauce simmers, bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Cook the pasta according to the package instructions until al dente. Drain the pasta and toss it with olive oil to prevent sticking.

  6. To make the béchamel sauce, melt the butter in a saucepan over medium heat. Stir in the flour and cook for about 2 minutes, until the mixture turns a light golden color.

  7. Gradually whisk in the milk, ensuring there are no lumps. Add the ground nutmeg, salt, and pepper. Continue cooking and stirring until the sauce thickens, about 5-7 minutes.

  8. Remove the saucepan from the heat and gradually whisk in the beaten eggs. This will help the sauce set as it bakes.

  9. In a large baking dish, spread half of the cooked pasta evenly across the bottom. Top it with the meat sauce, spreading it out in an even layer. Add the remaining pasta on top of the meat sauce, followed by the béchamel sauce, spreading it out to cover the pasta completely.

  10. Place the baking dish in the preheated oven and bake for 45-50 minutes, or until the top is golden brown and bubbling.

  11. Remove the Pastitsio from the oven and let it rest for 10-15 minutes before serving. This will allow the flavors to meld together and make it easier to slice.

  12. Serve the Greek Pastitsio warm, and enjoy the delicious layers of flavors that will transport you to the Mediterranean with every bite.

Conclusion

Greek Pastitsio is a truly comforting and satisfying dish that captures the essence of Mediterranean cuisine. Its layers of pasta, meat sauce, and creamy béchamel create a harmony of flavors that will have your taste buds singing. Whether you’re cooking for a crowd or enjoying a cozy night in, this hearty baked pasta dish is sure to impress. So, gather your ingredients, embrace the flavors of the Mediterranean, and indulge in the deliciousness of Greek Pastitsio.
