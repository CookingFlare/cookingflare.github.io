Creamy Butternut Squash Mac and Cheese: A Comforting Holiday Twist


The holiday season is the perfect time to indulge in comforting, hearty dishes that bring warmth and joy to the table. One dish that never fails to impress is the classic mac and cheese. However, this year, why not elevate this beloved comfort food with a delightful twist? Introducing Creamy Butternut Squash Mac and Cheese – a luxurious, velvety rendition of the traditional favorite that will leave your guests craving for more.

Ingredients:

  • 1 medium butternut squash, peeled, seeded, and diced
  • 1 pound macaroni pasta
  • 4 tablespoons unsalted butter
  • 1/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • 3 cups whole milk
  • 2 cups sharp cheddar cheese, grated
  • 1 cup Gruyere cheese, grated
  • 1/2 cup Parmesan cheese, grated
  • 1/4 teaspoon nutmeg
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1/2 cup panko breadcrumbs
  • Fresh parsley, chopped (for garnish)

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C). Place the diced butternut squash on a baking sheet, drizzle with olive oil, and season with salt and pepper. Roast in the oven for 25-30 minutes or until the squash is tender.

  2. While the squash is roasting, cook the macaroni pasta according to the package instructions. Drain and set aside.

  3. In a large saucepan, melt the butter over medium heat. Add the flour and whisk continuously for 2-3 minutes to create a roux.

  4. Gradually pour in the milk, whisking constantly until the mixture thickens and becomes smooth.

  5. Add the roasted butternut squash to the sauce, then use an immersion blender to blend until smooth and creamy.

  6. Stir in the cheddar, Gruyere, and Parmesan cheeses, allowing them to melt into the sauce. Season with nutmeg, salt, and pepper.

  7. Add the cooked macaroni to the sauce, stirring until the pasta is evenly coated with the creamy butternut squash mixture.

  8. Transfer the mac and cheese to a greased baking dish. Sprinkle the panko breadcrumbs over the top for a crunchy texture.

  9. Bake in the preheated oven for 20-25 minutes, or until the breadcrumbs are golden brown and the cheese is bubbly.

  10. Once out of the oven, let the mac and cheese cool for a few minutes before serving. Garnish with freshly chopped parsley for a pop of color and freshness.

The Perfect Holiday Indulgence

This Creamy Butternut Squash Mac and Cheese is a delightful departure from the ordinary, offering a rich and creamy texture with a subtle hint of nutty sweetness from the butternut squash. Its vibrant orange hue adds a festive touch to your holiday spread, making it a visually appealing and utterly delicious addition to your table.

Whether you’re hosting a family gathering or a festive dinner party, this comforting holiday twist on mac and cheese is sure to be a crowd-pleaser. The marriage of velvety butternut squash and gooey melted cheese creates a symphony of flavors that will have everyone reaching for seconds.

So, this holiday season, treat yourself and your loved ones to a dish that embodies the spirit of warmth, togetherness, and pure indulgence. Creamy Butternut Squash Mac and Cheese – a holiday classic in the making.
