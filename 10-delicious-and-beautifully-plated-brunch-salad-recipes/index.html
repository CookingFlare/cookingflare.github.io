10 Delicious and Beautifully Plated Brunch Salad Recipes


Brunch, the perfect meal to combine the best of breakfast and lunch. And what better way to start a weekend than with a delicious and beautifully plated brunch salad? In this article, we’ll explore 10 mouthwatering and visually stunning brunch salad recipes that are sure to impress your guests and satisfy your taste buds.

1. Strawberry Spinach Salad with Feta and Balsamic Glaze

This refreshing spring-inspired salad is a perfect way to start a sunny day. The sweetness of the strawberries pairs perfectly with the tanginess of the feta cheese, all wrapped up in a bed of crisp spinach leaves. Simply combine 2 cups of fresh spinach leaves, 1 cup of sliced strawberries, 1/2 cup of crumbled feta cheese, and 1/4 cup of chopped pecans. Drizzle with a balsamic glaze and sprinkle with salt and pepper to taste.

2. Quinoa and Black Bean Salad with Avocado and Lime

This hearty salad is packed with protein and fiber, making it a great option for a weekend brunch. Cook 1 cup of quinoa and let it cool, then combine it with 1 cup of cooked black beans, 1 diced avocado, 1/2 cup of chopped red bell pepper, and 1/4 cup of chopped cilantro. Squeeze a sliver of fresh lime juice over the top and sprinkle with salt and pepper to taste.

3. Grilled Chicken and Strawberry Salad with Feta and Mint

This sweet and savory salad is perfect for a summer brunch. Grill 1 pound of chicken breast and slice it into thin strips, then combine it with 2 cups of mixed greens, 1 cup of sliced strawberries, 1/2 cup of crumbled feta cheese, and 1/4 cup of chopped fresh mint. Drizzle with a balsamic glaze and sprinkle with salt and pepper to taste.

4. Roasted Beet Salad with Goat Cheese and Walnuts

This earthy salad is a great option for a fall or winter brunch. Roast 2 beets in the oven until tender, then peel and dice them. Combine them with 2 cups of mixed greens, 1/2 cup of crumbled goat cheese, 1/4 cup of chopped walnuts, and 1/4 cup of chopped fresh parsley. Drizzle with a balsamic glaze and sprinkle with salt and pepper to taste.

5. Kale and Apple Salad with Cheddar and Pecans

This crunchy salad is a great option for a spring or summer brunch. Combine 2 cups of curly kale leaves, 1 cup of diced apple, 1/2 cup of crumbled cheddar cheese, and 1/4 cup of chopped pecans. Squeeze a sliver of fresh lemon juice over the top and sprinkle with salt and pepper to taste.

6. Shrimp and Avocado Salad with Red Onion and Cilantro

This light and refreshing salad is perfect for a summer brunch. Combine 1 pound of cooked and chilled shrimp, 1 diced avocado, 1/2 cup of diced red onion, and 1/4 cup of chopped cilantro. Squeeze a sliver of fresh lime juice over the top and sprinkle with salt and pepper to taste.

7. Quinoa and Roasted Vegetable Salad with Feta and Lemon

This hearty salad is packed with protein and fiber, making it a great option for a weekend brunch. Cook 1 cup of quinoa and let it cool, then combine it with 1 cup of roasted vegetables (such as zucchini, bell peppers, and eggplant), 1/2 cup of crumbled feta cheese, and 1/4 cup of chopped fresh parsley. Squeeze a sliver of fresh lemon juice over the top and sprinkle with salt and pepper to taste.

8. Spinach and Artichoke Salad with Feta and Sun-dried Tomatoes

This creamy salad is a great option for a fall or winter brunch. Combine 2 cups of fresh spinach leaves, 1 cup of canned artichoke hearts, 1/2 cup of crumbled feta cheese, and 1/4 cup of chopped sun-dried tomatoes. Squeeze a sliver of fresh lemon juice over the top and sprinkle with salt and pepper to taste.

9. Grilled Chicken and Pear Salad with Blue Cheese and Walnuts

This sweet and savory salad is perfect for a summer brunch. Grill 1 pound of chicken breast and slice it into thin strips, then combine it with 2 cups of mixed greens, 1 cup of sliced pear, 1/2 cup of crumbled blue cheese, and 1/4 cup of chopped walnuts. Drizzle with a balsamic glaze and sprinkle with salt and pepper to taste.

10. Caprese Salad with Fresh Mozzarella and Basil

This classic salad is a great option for a summer brunch. Combine 2 cups of sliced fresh mozzarella cheese, 1 cup of sliced tomato, and 1/4 cup of chopped fresh basil. Drizzle with a balsamic glaze and sprinkle with salt and pepper to taste.

These 10 delicious and beautifully plated brunch salad recipes are sure to impress your guests and satisfy your taste buds. Whether you’re looking for a light and refreshing option or a hearty and filling one, there’s something on this list for everyone. So go ahead, get creative, and start cooking up a storm in your kitchen!
