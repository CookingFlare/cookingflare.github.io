Delightful Nut-Free Cinnamon Rolls for a Decadent Breakfast


There’s something truly magical about the warm, comforting aroma of freshly baked cinnamon rolls wafting through the kitchen. These soft, pillowy treats, swirled with cinnamon sugar and topped with luscious cream cheese icing, are a breakfast indulgence that never fails to delight. However, for those with nut allergies, the typical inclusion of nuts in cinnamon roll recipes can be a cause for concern. That’s why I’ve crafted a delightful nut-free cinnamon roll recipe that’s just as delicious and indulgent as the classic version.

Ingredients:

For the dough:

  • 1 cup warm milk
  • 2 1/4 teaspoons active dry yeast
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/3 cup unsalted butter, melted
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 4 1/2 cups all-purpose flour

For the filling:

  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 3/4 cup packed brown sugar
  • 3 tablespoons ground cinnamon

For the icing:

  • 4 ounces cream cheese, softened
  • 1/4 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1 1/2 cups powdered sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/8 teaspoon salt

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the dough:

    • In a large mixing bowl, dissolve the yeast in the warm milk.
    • Add the sugar, melted butter, eggs, salt, and flour.
    • Mix until a soft dough forms.
    • Knead the dough on a lightly floured surface until it is smooth and elastic.

  2. Let the dough rise:

    • Place the dough in a greased bowl, cover it, and let it rise in a warm place until doubled in size, for about 1 hour.

  3. Make the filling:

    • In a small bowl, mix together the softened butter, brown sugar, and cinnamon to create a smooth mixture.

  4. Assemble the rolls:

    • Roll out the dough on a floured surface into a large rectangle.
    • Spread the filling evenly over the dough.
    • Starting from one long end, roll the dough into a log.
    • Cut the log into 12 equal pieces.

  5. Bake the rolls:

    • Place the rolls in a greased 9x13 inch baking pan.
    • Cover them and let them rise in a warm place for 30 minutes.
    • Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C) and bake the rolls for 25-30 minutes until they are golden brown.

  6. Prepare the icing:

    • In a medium bowl, beat together the softened cream cheese, butter, powdered sugar, vanilla extract, and salt until smooth and creamy.

  7. Ice the rolls:

    • Once the rolls are baked and slightly cooled, spread the icing generously over the top of the warm rolls.

Why Nut-Free?

Nut allergies are a serious concern for many individuals and families, and it’s important to provide delicious alternatives that everyone can enjoy without worry. By omitting nuts from this classic cinnamon roll recipe, we ensure that those with nut allergies can savor the decadent pleasure of a freshly baked treat without any compromise.

Final Thoughts

These delightful nut-free cinnamon rolls are a perfect choice for a special breakfast or brunch. Their sweet, aromatic flavor and soft, tender texture make them an irresistible morning treat that will have your family and friends coming back for more. Whether you’re catering to nut allergies or simply looking for a delicious twist on a breakfast classic, these cinnamon rolls are sure to become a beloved favorite in your recipe collection. Enjoy the simple joy of indulging in these delightful, nut-free cinnamon rolls and make your breakfasts truly decadent!
