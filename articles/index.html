Ingredients 2 cups fresh raspberries 3 tablespoons chia seeds 1/4 cup honey or maple syrup (adjust according to your taste) 1 tablespoon lemon juice Instructions Start by washing the fresh raspberries thoroughly under cold water. Pat them dry with a paper towel and set them aside. In a medium-sized bowl, mash the raspberries using a fork or a potato masher until you reach your desired consistency. If you prefer a smoother jam, you can use a blender or food processor. Once mashed, add the chia seeds, honey or maple syrup, and lemon juice to the bowl. Mix everything together until well combined. The chia seeds will absorb the liquid and help thicken the jam. Let the mixture sit at room temperature for about 10-15 minutes to allow the chia seeds to fully absorb the liquid and thicken the jam. Stir occasionally during this time to ensure even distribution of the seeds. After the resting period, transfer the jam into a clean glass jar or an airtight container. Seal the jar tightly and refrigerate for at least 2 hours, or until the jam reaches your desired consistency. Once chilled, your Nut-Free Raspberry Chia Jam is ready to be enjoyed!
