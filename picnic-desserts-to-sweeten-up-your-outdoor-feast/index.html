Picnic Desserts to Sweeten Up Your Outdoor Feast


Planning a picnic and looking for the perfect sweet treats to elevate your outdoor dining experience? Look no further! We’ve rounded up some delightful dessert recipes that are not only delicious but also easy to pack and enjoy al fresco. From fruity delights to decadent chocolatey indulgences, these picnic desserts will surely sweeten up your outdoor feast.

Lemon Blueberry Bars

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1/4 cup powdered sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 2 tablespoons lemon zest
  • 2 tablespoons lemon juice
  • 1 cup fresh blueberries

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C) and grease a baking pan.
  2. In a bowl, combine 1 cup of flour, butter, and powdered sugar to make the crust. Press the mixture into the prepared pan and bake for 15-20 minutes.
  3. Meanwhile, whisk together eggs, granulated sugar, 2 tablespoons of flour, baking powder, salt, lemon zest, and lemon juice.
  4. Gently fold in the blueberries and pour the mixture over the baked crust.
  5. Bake for an additional 25 minutes or until the filling is set. Let it cool before slicing into bars.

Mini Strawberry Shortcakes

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 cup sugar
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 cup unsalted butter, cold and cubed
  • 1/4 cup milk
  • 1 egg
  • 1 cup sliced strawberries
  • Whipped cream

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 425°F (220°C) and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
  2. In a bowl, mix flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt. Cut in the butter until the mixture resembles coarse crumbs.
  3. In a separate bowl, whisk together milk and egg. Add to the flour mixture and stir until just combined.
  4. Drop spoonfuls of the dough onto the baking sheet and bake for 12-15 minutes until golden brown.
  5. Let the shortcakes cool, then slice in half horizontally. Fill with sliced strawberries and whipped cream before serving.

Chocolate Dipped Pretzel Rods

Ingredients:

  • Pretzel rods
  • Dark, milk, and white chocolate chips
  • Assorted sprinkles

Instructions:

  1. Melt each type of chocolate in separate bowls according to package instructions.
  2. Dip each pretzel rod into the melted chocolate of your choice, covering about two-thirds of the rod.
  3. Allow excess chocolate to drip off, then place on a parchment-lined tray.
  4. Decorate with sprinkles before the chocolate sets.
  5. Let the chocolate harden before packing the pretzel rods for your picnic.

These delightful picnic desserts are sure to impress your fellow picnic-goers and add a touch of sweetness to your outdoor gathering. Whether you prefer fruity, creamy, or chocolaty treats, there’s something for everyone to enjoy. So pack your picnic basket, grab a blanket, and indulge in these irresistible sweet creations amidst nature’s beauty. Happy picnicking!
