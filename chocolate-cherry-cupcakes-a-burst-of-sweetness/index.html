Chocolate Cherry Cupcakes: A Burst of Sweetness


If you’re looking for a delightful treat that combines the rich flavors of chocolate with the sweet tang of cherries, then these Chocolate Cherry Cupcakes are just what you need. These moist and decadent cupcakes are a perfect indulgence for any occasion, from afternoon tea to a special celebration. The combination of chocolate and cherry creates a tantalizing flavor profile that will leave your taste buds craving for more. So, let’s dive into the recipe and discover how to create these irresistible sweet delights.

Ingredients:

For the Cupcakes:

  • 1 ½ cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/2 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup warm water
  • 1/3 cup vegetable oil
  • 1 tablespoon white vinegar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/2 cup chopped cherries (fresh or frozen)

For the Cherry Chocolate Frosting:

  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 2 1/2 cups powdered sugar
  • 1/4 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1/4 cup cherry puree (made from fresh or frozen cherries)
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • Pinch of salt

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C) and line a muffin tin with cupcake liners.

  2. In a large mixing bowl, sift together the flour, sugar, cocoa powder, baking soda, and salt.

  3. In a separate bowl, combine the warm water, vegetable oil, vinegar, and vanilla extract.

  4. Gradually add the wet ingredients to the dry ingredients, mixing until just combined.

  5. Gently fold in the chopped cherries.

  6. Fill each cupcake liner about two-thirds full with the batter.

  7. Bake for 18-20 minutes or until a toothpick inserted into the center of a cupcake comes out clean.

  8. While the cupcakes are baking, prepare the cherry chocolate frosting. In a mixing bowl, beat the softened butter until creamy. Gradually add the powdered sugar and cocoa powder, mixing until well combined.

  9. Add the cherry puree, vanilla extract, and a pinch of salt, and continue to beat until the frosting is smooth and fluffy.

  10. Once the cupcakes have cooled completely, frost them generously with the cherry chocolate frosting.

  11. For an extra touch, garnish each cupcake with a fresh cherry on top.

Serving Suggestions:

These Chocolate Cherry Cupcakes are best enjoyed with a hot cup of coffee or a cold glass of milk. They make a delightful addition to any dessert table and are sure to impress your family and friends.

Storage:

Store any leftover cupcakes in an airtight container at room temperature for up to 3 days, or in the refrigerator for up to 5 days.

Final Thoughts:

The combination of rich chocolate and sweet cherries in these cupcakes creates a symphony of flavors that is truly irresistible. Whether you’re a seasoned baker or just starting out, this recipe is simple enough to follow and yields delightful results. So, go ahead and treat yourself to these Chocolate Cherry Cupcakes – a burst of sweetness in every bite!
