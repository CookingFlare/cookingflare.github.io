A Taste of the Aegean: How to Make Greek Style Stuffed Calamari


If you’re looking to bring the flavors of the Aegean Sea to your kitchen, Greek Style Stuffed Calamari is a dish that will transport you to the shores of Greece with every bite. This traditional Greek recipe combines tender calamari with a flavorful stuffing, creating a dish that is both elegant and delicious. Whether you’re hosting a dinner party or simply craving a taste of the Mediterranean, this recipe is sure to impress. So, let’s dive into the art of creating this exquisite Greek delicacy.

Ingredients

For the Calamari:

  • 8 medium-sized whole calamari
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1 red onion, finely chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/4 cup fresh parsley, chopped
  • 1/4 cup fresh mint, chopped
  • 1/2 cup feta cheese, crumbled
  • 1/4 cup Kalamata olives, pitted and chopped
  • 1/4 cup breadcrumbs
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Olive oil for drizzling
  • Lemon wedges for serving

For the Tomato Sauce:

  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can (14 oz) crushed tomatoes
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

Step 1: Prepare the Ingredients

  • Start by cleaning the calamari. Remove the tentacles, clean the insides, and rinse under cold water. Pat them dry with paper towels and set aside.
  • Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).

Step 2: Prepare the Stuffing

  • In a mixing bowl, combine the cherry tomatoes, red onion, garlic, parsley, mint, feta cheese, Kalamata olives, and breadcrumbs. Season the mixture with salt and pepper, and drizzle with a bit of olive oil. Gently toss everything together until well combined.

Step 3: Stuff the Calamari

  • Take each calamari tube and carefully stuff it with the prepared mixture, leaving some space at the top to allow for the stuffing to expand while cooking. Secure the openings with toothpicks to prevent the stuffing from falling out during cooking.

Step 4: Prepare the Tomato Sauce

  • In a saucepan, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the chopped onion and garlic, and sauté until softened and fragrant.
  • Pour in the crushed tomatoes and add the dried oregano. Season with salt and pepper, then let the sauce simmer for about 10 minutes, allowing the flavors to meld together.

Step 5: Assemble and Bake

  • Place the stuffed calamari in a baking dish, and pour the prepared tomato sauce over them. Cover the dish with foil and bake in the preheated oven for 30 minutes.
  • After 30 minutes, remove the foil and continue baking for an additional 10-15 minutes, or until the calamari is tender and the stuffing is golden brown.

Step 6: Serve and Enjoy

  • Once done, carefully transfer the stuffed calamari to a serving platter. Drizzle with a bit of olive oil and garnish with fresh parsley. Serve with lemon wedges on the side for a bright, citrusy touch.

The Taste of the Aegean on Your Plate

With each succulent bite of these Greek Style Stuffed Calamari, you’ll experience the vibrant flavors of the Aegean Sea. The tender calamari paired with the savory stuffing and zesty tomato sauce creates a harmonious blend of Mediterranean tastes that will transport you to the sun-soaked shores of Greece. Whether you’re a seafood enthusiast or simply looking to expand your culinary repertoire, this recipe is a delightful way to savor the essence of Greek cuisine in the comfort of your own home.

So, gather your ingredients, embrace the spirit of the Aegean, and indulge in the incomparable pleasure of preparing and savoring this authentic Greek dish. From the tantalizing aroma that fills your kitchen to the first delightful forkful, Greek Style Stuffed Calamari is a culinary journey that promises to delight your senses and leave a lasting impression on your palate. Cheers to the joy of cooking and the boundless flavors of the Aegean!
