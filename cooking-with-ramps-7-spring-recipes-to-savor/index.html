Cooking with Ramps: 7 Spring Recipes to Savor


Spring is a time for fresh flavors and vibrant dishes, and what better way to celebrate the season than by cooking with ramps? Ramps, also known as wild leeks, are a wild spring vegetable with a short growing season, making them a sought-after ingredient for food enthusiasts. Their delicate garlicky flavor and tender green leaves make them a versatile addition to a variety of dishes. In this blog post, we’ll explore seven creative and delicious recipes that showcase the unique flavors of ramps. From simple pasta dishes to savory tarts, these recipes will help you make the most of ramp season and delight your taste buds with the essence of spring.

1. Ramp Pesto Pasta

This simple yet flavorful pasta dish celebrates the essence of spring. Start by making a vibrant ramp pesto by blending ramps, pine nuts, Parmesan cheese, and olive oil until smooth. Toss the pesto with al dente pasta, adding a splash of pasta water to create a creamy sauce. Finish with a sprinkle of freshly grated Parmesan and a drizzle of extra virgin olive oil for a delightful springtime meal.

2. Grilled Ramp Pizza

Fire up the grill and prepare a show-stopping grilled ramp pizza. Brush pizza dough with olive oil, then top it with a layer of mozzarella cheese, grilled ramps, and a sprinkle of red pepper flakes for a touch of heat. Grill until the crust is golden and the cheese is bubbly, then finish with a drizzle of balsamic glaze for a perfect balance of flavors.

3. Ramp and Goat Cheese Frittata

This elegant frittata is a delightful addition to any spring brunch or lunch. Sauté ramps until tender, then pour in a mixture of beaten eggs and creamy goat cheese. Let the frittata cook gently until the eggs are set, then finish under the broiler for a golden top. The combination of ramps and goat cheese creates a rich and savory dish that’s perfect for sharing.

4. Ramp and Asparagus Risotto

Capture the essence of spring in a comforting bowl of ramp and asparagus risotto. Sauté diced ramps and asparagus in butter until tender, then stir in Arborio rice and white wine. Slowly add warm chicken or vegetable broth, stirring until the rice is creamy and tender. Finish with a generous sprinkle of Parmesan cheese and a squeeze of fresh lemon juice for a bright and flavorful risotto.

5. Ramp and Bacon Quiche

Indulge in a classic quiche with a spring twist by adding ramps to the filling. Combine sautéed ramps, crispy bacon, and sharp cheddar cheese in a buttery pie crust. Pour in a mixture of eggs and cream, then bake until the quiche is golden and set. The combination of savory ramps and smoky bacon creates a quiche that’s perfect for any meal of the day.

6. Ramp and Potato Soup

Warm up with a comforting bowl of ramp and potato soup. Sauté ramps, onions, and garlic until fragrant, then add diced potatoes and chicken or vegetable broth. Simmer until the potatoes are tender, then blend the soup until smooth. Finish with a swirl of cream and a sprinkle of fresh chives for a satisfying soup that celebrates the flavors of spring.

7. Pickled Ramps

Preserve the fleeting flavors of ramps by making pickled ramps. Combine ramps, white wine vinegar, sugar, and spices in a jar, then let the mixture sit until the ramps are lightly pickled. Enjoy pickled ramps as a tangy addition to salads, cheese boards, or as a garnish for grilled meats.

Conclusion

Cooking with ramps is a delightful way to embrace the flavors of spring and add a touch of seasonal flair to your meals. Whether you’re making a simple pasta dish or a show-stopping pizza, ramps bring a unique and fresh flavor to any recipe. With their limited availability, savoring the taste of ramps is truly a celebration of the fleeting beauty of spring. Try these recipes and make the most of ramp season by savoring the essence of spring in every bite.
