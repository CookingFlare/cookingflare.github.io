Mediterranean Lentil and Feta Stuffed Peppers


Are you searching for a delicious and nutritious dish that showcases the fantastic flavors of the Mediterranean? Look no further than this mouthwatering recipe for Mediterranean Lentil and Feta Stuffed Peppers. A symphony of textures and tastes, this recipe combines succulent lentils, crumbled feta cheese, fresh herbs, and crunchy vegetables, all nestled inside vibrant bell peppers. ** Servings: 4-6** ** Prep Time: 45 minutes** ** Cook Time: 35 minutes** ** Total Time: 1 hour 20 minutes** Ingredients

6 bell peppers (any color), seeded and cut in half 1 cup brown or green lentils, rinsed and drained 2 tablespoons olive oil 1 onion, finely chopped 2 cloves garlic, minced 1 cup diced tomatoes (fresh or canned) 1/4 cup fresh parsley, chopped 1/4 cup fresh dill, chopped 2 tablespoons fresh oregano, chopped 1 teaspoon smoked paprika 1/4 teaspoon cayenne pepper (optional) Salt and black pepper, to taste 1/2 cup crumbled feta cheese (reduce or omit for a vegetarian option)

Instructions

Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C). Place the bell peppers on a baking sheet, cut side up. Roast in the oven for 20 minutes, until slightly tender. Remove from the oven and set aside. In a medium saucepan, bring 2 cups of water to a boil. Add the lentils and reduce the heat to a simmer. Cook, covered, for 20-25 minutes, or until the lentils are tender but still retain some firmness. Drain and let cool. In a large skillet, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the onion and garlic and cook, stirring occasionally, for 8-10 minutes, or until the onion is translucent. Add the diced tomatoes, smoked paprika, cayenne pepper (if using), salt, and black pepper to the skillet. Stir to combine. Cook, stirring occasionally, for 3-5 minutes. In a large bowl, combine the cooked lentils, tomato mixture, chopped herbs (parsley, dill, and oregano), and crumbled feta cheese (if using). Stir to combine. To assemble the stuffed peppers, fill each bell pepper half with the lentil mixture, mounding it slightly in the center. Cover the baking sheet with aluminum foil and return it to the oven for 15-20 minutes. Remove the foil and continue to bake for an additional 5-7 minutes, until the peppers are slightly tender and the filling is heated through. Remove the stuffed peppers from the oven and let them cool for a few minutes before serving.

Tips and Variations

For a vegetarian option, omit the feta cheese or substitute it with vegan feta cheese. Replace the bell peppers with other colorful peppers, such as Anaheim or Poblano. Add some heat with diced jalapeños or serrano peppers to the lentil mixture. Experiment with different herbs, such as fresh rosemary or thyme, to change the flavor profile. Serve the stuffed peppers as a main course or as a side dish for any Mediterranean-inspired meal.

Nutritional Information (per serving)

Calories: 360 Protein: 22g Fat: 18g Saturated Fat: 3g Cholesterol: 20mg Carbohydrates: 40g Fiber: 10g

This recipe offers a delightful balance of flavors, textures, and nutrients, making it a fantastic addition to any meal. Don't be afraid to experiment with different herbs and spices to create your own unique twist on this Mediterranean classic. Whether you're a seasoned cook or a culinary novice, Mediterranean Lentil and Feta Stuffed Peppers are sure to become a new favorite dish in your kitchen. So go ahead, get creative, and enjoy the delicious flavors of the Mediterranean!
