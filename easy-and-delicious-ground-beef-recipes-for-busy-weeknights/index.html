Easy and Delicious Ground Beef Recipes for Busy Weeknights


Are you tired of the same old dinner routines? Do you find yourself struggling to come up with quick and tasty meals after a long day at work? Look no further! In this blog post, we’ve rounded up some easy and delicious ground beef recipes that are perfect for busy weeknights. These recipes are not only quick to make but also packed with flavor, making them a hit with both kids and adults. So, let’s dive right in and discover these mouthwatering recipes!

1. Cheesy Stuffed Bell Peppers

Ingredients:

  • 4 bell peppers
  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 cup cooked rice
  • 1 cup shredded cheese
  • 1/2 cup diced onion
  • 1/2 cup diced tomatoes
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cut the tops off the bell peppers and remove the seeds. Place them in a baking dish.
  3. In a skillet, cook the ground beef and diced onions until browned. Drain any excess fat.
  4. Add the cooked rice, diced tomatoes, garlic powder, salt, and pepper to the skillet. Stir well to combine.
  5. Spoon the beef mixture into the bell peppers, filling them to the top.
  6. Sprinkle shredded cheese over the stuffed peppers.
  7. Bake for 25-30 minutes or until the peppers are tender and the cheese is melted and bubbly.
  8. Serve hot and enjoy!

2. One-Pot Beef and Macaroni

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can diced tomatoes
  • 2 cups beef broth
  • 2 cups elbow macaroni
  • 1 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1 teaspoon Italian seasoning
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a large pot, brown the ground beef, onions, and garlic over medium heat.
  2. Drain any excess fat from the pot.
  3. Stir in the diced tomatoes, beef broth, elbow macaroni, Italian seasoning, salt, and pepper.
  4. Bring the mixture to a boil, then reduce the heat to low.
  5. Cover the pot and simmer for 15-20 minutes or until the macaroni is cooked and the liquid is absorbed.
  6. Stir in the shredded cheddar cheese until melted and well combined.
  7. Remove from heat and let it sit for a few minutes before serving.

3. Beef and Broccoli Stir-Fry

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound beef sirloin, thinly sliced
  • 3 cups broccoli florets
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons oyster sauce
  • 1 tablespoon honey
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon ginger, grated
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil

Instructions:

  1. In a small bowl, whisk together the soy sauce, oyster sauce, honey, garlic, and ginger.
  2. Heat the vegetable oil in a large skillet or wok over high heat.
  3. Add the sliced beef and stir-fry for 2-3 minutes until browned.
  4. Add the broccoli florets to the skillet and continue stir-frying for another 2 minutes.
  5. Pour the sauce mixture over the beef and broccoli. Stir well to coat.
  6. Cook for an additional 2 minutes or until the sauce thickens slightly.
  7. Remove from heat and serve hot with steamed rice or noodles.

4. Mini Beef Sliders

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 8 mini hamburger buns
  • 8 slices cheddar cheese
  • Lettuce, tomato, and onion for toppings
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat a grill or stovetop griddle over medium-high heat.
  2. Season the ground beef with salt and pepper, then shape it into small patties.
  3. Place the patties on the grill or griddle and cook for 2-3 minutes per side, or until desired doneness.
  4. Just before removing the patties from the heat, top each one with a slice of cheddar cheese and allow it to melt slightly.
  5. Toast the mini hamburger buns on the grill or griddle until lightly browned.
  6. Assemble the sliders by placing a patty on each bun and topping with lettuce, tomato, and onion.
  7. Serve hot and enjoy these bite-sized delights!

Conclusion

With these easy and delicious ground beef recipes, you’ll never have to worry about what to cook for dinner on busy weeknights. Whether it’s stuffed bell peppers, one-pot beef and macaroni, beef and broccoli stir-fry, or mini beef sliders, these recipes are sure to satisfy your taste buds and make mealtime a breeze. So, why not give them a try and add some excitement to your weeknight dinners? Happy cooking!
