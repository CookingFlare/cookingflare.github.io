Spring Risotto: 5 Recipes to Make the Most of Seasonal Ingredients


As the chill of winter fades and the first blooms of spring appear, our culinary cravings begin to shift. The vibrant colors and fresh flavors of spring vegetables beckon us to the kitchen, inviting us to create delightful dishes that celebrate the season. One of the best ways to showcase these seasonal ingredients is through risotto. This creamy, indulgent dish can be adapted in countless ways, making it a perfect canvas for the bright and fresh tastes of spring.

In this post, we will explore five delightful spring risotto recipes, each highlighting the best seasonal ingredients. From asparagus to peas and radishes to herbs, these recipes are sure to elevate your dining experience and bring the essence of spring to your table.

1. Lemon Asparagus Risotto

Ingredients

  • 1 cup Arborio rice
  • 1 bunch asparagus, trimmed and cut into 1-inch pieces
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 4 cups vegetable broth
  • 1 cup dry white wine
  • Juice and zest of 1 lemon
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley for garnish

Instructions

  1. In a saucepan, heat the vegetable broth and keep it simmering on low heat.
  2. In a large skillet, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the chopped onion and garlic, sautéing until translucent.
  3. Stir in the Arborio rice, coating it with the oil and cooking for about 2 minutes until slightly toasted.
  4. Pour in the white wine, stirring continuously until it is absorbed by the rice.
  5. Gradually add the simmering vegetable broth, one ladle at a time, stirring frequently. Wait until the liquid is absorbed before adding more.
  6. After about 15 minutes, add the asparagus pieces to the risotto.
  7. Continue adding broth and stirring for another 5-7 minutes until the rice is creamy and al dente.
  8. Remove from heat and stir in the lemon juice, zest, and Parmesan cheese. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  9. Serve warm, garnished with fresh parsley.

This Lemon Asparagus Risotto is a perfect representation of spring, with bright flavors that will awaken your taste buds. The creamy texture combined with the crunch of asparagus makes it a delightful dish for any occasion.

2. Pea and Mint Risotto

Ingredients

  • 1 cup Arborio rice
  • 2 cups fresh or frozen peas
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 4 cups vegetable broth
  • 1 cup dry white wine
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1/4 cup fresh mint leaves, chopped
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Heat the vegetable broth in a separate pot and keep it warm.
  2. In a large skillet, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the onion and garlic, sautéing until soft.
  3. Stir in the Arborio rice, cooking for about 2 minutes until it becomes translucent.
  4. Pour in the white wine, stirring until it is absorbed.
  5. Slowly add the vegetable broth, one ladle at a time, stirring frequently until the liquid is absorbed before adding more.
  6. After about 15 minutes, add the peas to the risotto.
  7. Continue stirring and adding broth until the rice is creamy and cooked to your liking, about 5-7 minutes more.
  8. Remove from heat and stir in the chopped mint and Parmesan cheese. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  9. Serve hot, garnished with additional mint if desired.

The Pea and Mint Risotto offers a sweet and refreshing flavor profile, making it an ideal dish for spring gatherings. The combination of sweet peas and fragrant mint creates a harmonious blend that is simply irresistible.

3. Radish and Arugula Risotto

Ingredients

  • 1 cup Arborio rice
  • 1 bunch radishes, thinly sliced
  • 2 cups arugula, roughly chopped
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 4 cups vegetable broth
  • 1 cup dry white wine
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. In a pot, warm the vegetable broth over low heat.
  2. In a large skillet, combine the olive oil and butter over medium heat. Add the chopped onion and garlic, sautéing until soft.
  3. Add the Arborio rice and toast for about 2 minutes, stirring frequently.
  4. Pour in the white wine, stirring until it is absorbed.
  5. Gradually add the vegetable broth, one ladle at a time, stirring continuously.
  6. After about 10 minutes, add the sliced radishes to the risotto.
  7. Continue adding broth and stirring until the rice is creamy and cooked to your preference, about 5-7 minutes longer.
  8. Stir in the arugula and Parmesan cheese, seasoning with salt and pepper to taste.
  9. Serve warm, garnished with additional radish slices if desired.

This Radish and Arugula Risotto delivers a peppery kick, thanks to the fresh arugula, perfectly complemented by the crunch of radishes. It’s a unique and vibrant dish that brings a playful twist to traditional risotto.

4. Spring Vegetable Risotto

Ingredients

  • 1 cup Arborio rice
  • 1 cup green beans, trimmed and cut into 1-inch pieces
  • 1 cup zucchini, diced
  • 1 cup peas (fresh or frozen)
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 4 cups vegetable broth
  • 1 cup dry white wine
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh basil for garnish

Instructions

  1. Heat the vegetable broth in a pot and keep it warm.
  2. In a large skillet, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the onion and garlic, cooking until soft.
  3. Stir in the Arborio rice and toast for a couple of minutes.
  4. Add the white wine, stirring until absorbed.
  5. Gradually add the vegetable broth, one ladle at a time, stirring frequently.
  6. After about 10 minutes, add the green beans and zucchini to the risotto.
  7. Continue adding broth and stirring until the rice is creamy and cooked to your liking, about 5-7 minutes.
  8. Stir in the peas and Parmesan cheese, seasoning with salt and pepper to taste.
  9. Serve warm, garnished with fresh basil.

This Spring Vegetable Risotto is a colorful medley of fresh vegetables that captures the essence of spring in every bite. The combination of textures and flavors offers a delightful experience for the palate.

5. Herb and Lemon Risotto with Feta

Ingredients

  • 1 cup Arborio rice
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 4 cups vegetable broth
  • 1 cup dry white wine
  • Juice and zest of 1 lemon
  • 1/2 cup crumbled feta cheese
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1/4 cup fresh herbs (such as dill, parsley, or chives), chopped
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. In a saucepan, warm the vegetable broth over low heat.
  2. In a large skillet, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the onion and garlic, sautéing until soft.
  3. Stir in the Arborio rice and toast for about 2 minutes.
  4. Pour in the white wine, stirring until it is absorbed.
  5. Gradually add the vegetable broth, one ladle at a time, stirring frequently.
  6. After about 15 minutes, stir in the lemon juice and zest.
  7. Continue adding broth and stirring until the rice is creamy and cooked to your liking.
  8. Remove from heat and fold in the crumbled feta and fresh herbs. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  9. Serve warm, garnished with additional herbs if desired.

The Herb and Lemon Risotto with Feta is a bright and refreshing option that perfectly encapsulates the flavors of spring. The tangy feta and fresh herbs elevate the dish, making it a standout choice for any springtime meal.

Conclusion

Spring is a season of renewal, and what better way to celebrate than by preparing these delightful spring risotto recipes? Each dish showcases seasonal ingredients that are abundant during this vibrant time of year. From the crispness of asparagus to the sweetness of peas, these risottos bring the essence of spring to your plate.

Whether you’re hosting a spring dinner party or simply enjoying a cozy night in, these risottos are sure to impress. With their creamy texture and rich flavors, they invite you to savor the freshness of the season. So grab your apron and get ready to indulge in the delightful world of spring risotto!
