Classic Mojito Waffles: A Refreshing and Boozy Breakfast Treat


When it comes to breakfast, there’s nothing like starting your day with a refreshing and boozy twist. And what better way to do that than with Classic Mojito Waffles? These waffles are a delightful combination of zesty lime, fragrant mint, and a subtle hint of rum. They are the perfect treat to kickstart your morning or enjoy on a lazy weekend brunch. So, grab your waffle maker and let’s get cooking!

Ingredients

To make these delicious Classic Mojito Waffles, you will need the following ingredients:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons granulated sugar
  • 1 tablespoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 3/4 cups milk
  • 1/4 cup vegetable oil
  • 2 large eggs
  • Zest of 2 limes
  • Juice of 1 lime
  • 2 tablespoons finely chopped fresh mint leaves
  • 1 tablespoon rum (optional)
  • Whipped cream and fresh mint leaves, for serving

Instructions

  1. Preheat your waffle maker according to the manufacturer’s instructions.

  2. In a large mixing bowl, whisk together the flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt.

  3. In a separate bowl, whisk together the milk, vegetable oil, eggs, lime zest, lime juice, fresh mint leaves, and rum (if using).

  4. Pour the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients and stir until just combined. Be careful not to overmix; a few lumps in the batter are okay.

  5. Spray the preheated waffle maker with non-stick cooking spray. Pour the batter onto the center of the waffle maker, spreading it evenly with a spatula. Close the lid and cook for about 3-4 minutes, or until the waffles are golden brown and crisp.

  6. Carefully remove the waffles from the waffle maker and transfer them to a wire rack to cool slightly.

  7. Serve the Classic Mojito Waffles warm, topped with a dollop of whipped cream and a few fresh mint leaves. For an extra boozy touch, drizzle some rum over the waffles before serving.

Tips and Variations

  • If you don’t have a waffle maker, you can also make these waffles in a regular pan as pancakes. Simply pour the batter onto a greased, preheated pan and cook until golden brown on both sides.

  • For a non-alcoholic version, you can omit the rum and still enjoy the refreshing flavors of lime and mint in your waffles.

  • Get creative with your toppings! Try adding some fresh berries, a sprinkle of powdered sugar, or even a scoop of vanilla ice cream to take your Classic Mojito Waffles to the next level.

  • If you want to make these waffles ahead of time, you can freeze them in an airtight container. When you’re ready to enjoy them, simply pop them in the toaster or oven to warm them up.

In Conclusion

Classic Mojito Waffles are a unique and delicious twist on traditional breakfast waffles. The combination of zesty lime, fragrant mint, and a hint of rum creates a refreshing and boozy treat that will surely brighten up your mornings. Whether you’re looking for a special breakfast or a fun brunch option, these waffles are sure to impress. So, grab your ingredients and start cooking up a batch of Classic Mojito Waffles today!
