Satisfying Thai Beef Noodle Stir-Fry: A Quick Meal


Thai cuisine is renowned for its bold flavors, aromatics, and spice, and this satisfying Thai beef noodle stir-fry recipe is no exception. With its quick cooking time and ease of preparation, this dish is perfect for a weeknight meal that’s both delicious and nutritious. In this recipe, we’ll explore the combination of tender beef, savory noodles, and a medley of vegetables, all brought together by the rich flavors of Thai cuisine.

Ingredients

  • 200g beef strips (sirloin or ribeye work well)
  • 2 cups mixed vegetables (bell peppers, carrots, broccoli, and onions)
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon grated fresh ginger
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon fish sauce
  • 1 tablespoon brown sugar
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground coriander
  • 1/4 teaspoon cayenne pepper
  • 1/2 cup Thai red curry paste
  • 2 cups cooked rice noodles
  • Salt and black pepper, to taste
  • Fresh cilantro, chopped (optional)

Instructions

  1. Prepare the ingredients: Cut the beef into thin strips and season with salt and black pepper. Cut the mixed vegetables into bite-sized pieces. Grate the ginger and mince the garlic.
  2. Cook the noodles: Cook the rice noodles according to the package instructions and set aside.
  3. Cook the beef: Heat 1 tablespoon of vegetable oil in a large wok or frying pan over medium-high heat. Add the beef and cook for 3-4 minutes, or until browned and cooked through. Remove the beef from the pan and set aside.
  4. Cook the vegetables: In the same pan, add the remaining 1 tablespoon of vegetable oil. Add the mixed vegetables and cook for 3-4 minutes, or until they start to soften.
  5. Add aromatics and spices: Add the minced garlic and grated ginger to the pan and cook for 1 minute, stirring constantly. Add the soy sauce, fish sauce, brown sugar, cumin, coriander, and cayenne pepper to the pan and stir to combine.
  6. Add the curry paste and beef: Add the Thai red curry paste to the pan and cook for 1 minute, stirring constantly. Add the cooked beef back to the pan and stir to combine with the curry paste and spices.
  7. Combine with noodles and vegetables: Add the cooked noodles and mixed vegetables to the pan and stir to combine with the beef and curry paste. Cook for an additional 2-3 minutes, or until the noodles are well coated with the sauce.
  8. Season and serve: Season the dish with salt and black pepper to taste. Garnish with chopped cilantro, if desired. Serve hot and enjoy!

Tips and Variations

  • For an added depth of flavor, add 2 tablespoons of Thai coconut milk to the pan with the curry paste and beef.
  • Substitute the beef with chicken or pork for a different protein option.
  • Add some heat to the dish by increasing the amount of cayenne pepper or adding in some diced jalapeños.
  • Experiment with different vegetables, such as mushrooms, bell peppers, or snow peas, to add variety to the dish.

With its rich and complex flavors, this Thai beef noodle stir-fry is a satisfying and quick meal that’s perfect for a weeknight dinner. The combination of tender beef, savory noodles, and a medley of vegetables, all brought together by the bold flavors of Thai cuisine, is a winning combination that’s sure to become a staple in your household.
