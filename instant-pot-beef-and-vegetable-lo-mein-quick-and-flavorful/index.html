Instant Pot Beef and Vegetable Lo Mein: Quick and Flavorful


Are you craving a delicious and satisfying meal that can be prepared in no time? Look no further than this Instant Pot Beef and Vegetable Lo Mein recipe! Packed with flavorful ingredients and cooked to perfection in the Instant Pot, this dish will surely become a family favorite.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound flank steak, thinly sliced
  • 8 ounces lo mein noodles
  • 1 cup sliced mushrooms
  • 1 cup sliced bell peppers
  • 1 cup sliced carrots
  • 1 cup snow peas
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons oyster sauce
  • 1 tablespoon hoisin sauce
  • 1 tablespoon sesame oil
  • 1/2 cup beef broth
  • 2 tablespoons cornstarch
  • 2 tablespoons water
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • Green onions, for garnish

Instructions

  1. Start by cooking the lo mein noodles according to the package instructions. Drain and set aside.

  2. Turn on the Instant Pot and select the “Saute” function. Heat the sesame oil and add the minced garlic. Cook for about 1 minute or until fragrant.

  3. Add the sliced flank steak to the Instant Pot and cook until browned, stirring occasionally. Season with salt and pepper to taste.

  4. In a small bowl, whisk together the soy sauce, oyster sauce, hoisin sauce, beef broth, cornstarch, and water. Pour the sauce mixture into the Instant Pot, coating the beef evenly.

  5. Add the mushrooms, bell peppers, carrots, and snow peas to the Instant Pot. Stir to combine all the ingredients.

  6. Close the Instant Pot lid and set the valve to the “Sealing” position. Select the “Pressure Cook” function and set the timer for 5 minutes.

  7. Once the cooking time is complete, do a quick release of the pressure. Carefully open the Instant Pot lid.

  8. Stir in the cooked lo mein noodles, ensuring that they are well coated with the flavorful sauce and mixed with the beef and vegetables.

  9. Serve the Instant Pot Beef and Vegetable Lo Mein hot, garnished with chopped green onions for an extra burst of freshness and flavor.

Tips and Variations

  • Feel free to customize this recipe to suit your taste preferences. You can add other vegetables such as broccoli, bok choy, or bean sprouts for added crunch and nutrition.

  • For a spicier kick, add a teaspoon of red pepper flakes or a drizzle of sriracha sauce to the dish.

  • If you prefer a vegetarian version, you can omit the beef and substitute it with tofu or tempeh.

  • Leftovers can be stored in an airtight container in the refrigerator for up to 3 days. Simply reheat in the microwave or on the stovetop for a quick and convenient meal.

Now that you have this amazing Instant Pot Beef and Vegetable Lo Mein recipe in your repertoire, you can enjoy a flavorful and satisfying meal in no time. The Instant Pot makes the cooking process a breeze, allowing you to spend less time in the kitchen and more time enjoying your delicious creation. So fire up your Instant Pot, gather your ingredients, and get ready to impress your family and friends with this quick and flavorful dish. Bon appétit!
