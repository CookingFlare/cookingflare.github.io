Chewy and Spiced Gingerbread Snickerdoodle Cookies: A Holiday Fusion


Who doesn’t love the warm and comforting flavors of gingerbread and the classic snickerdoodle? These two holiday favorites have come together to create the ultimate treat: Chewy and Spiced Gingerbread Snickerdoodle Cookies. This delightful fusion combines the rich spices of gingerbread with the irresistible chewiness of snickerdoodles, making it the perfect addition to your holiday baking repertoire.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1 1/2 cups granulated sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1/4 cup molasses
  • 3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 teaspoons baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 2 teaspoons ground cinnamon
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons ground ginger
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cloves
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground nutmeg
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar (for rolling)

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C) and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.

  2. In a large mixing bowl, cream together the softened butter and granulated sugar until light and fluffy. This should take about 2-3 minutes.

  3. Add the eggs one at a time, mixing well after each addition. Then, stir in the molasses until fully incorporated.

  4. In a separate bowl, whisk together the flour, baking soda, salt, cinnamon, ginger, cloves, and nutmeg. Slowly add the dry ingredients to the wet ingredients, mixing until just combined. Be careful not to overmix the dough.

  5. Place the remaining 1/2 cup of granulated sugar in a small bowl. Take about 1 tablespoon of dough and roll it into a ball. Roll each dough ball in the sugar until fully coated, then place onto the prepared baking sheet. Repeat this process with the remaining dough, spacing the balls about 2 inches apart.

  6. Bake the cookies for 10-12 minutes, or until the edges are set and the centers are still slightly soft. The cookies will firm up as they cool, so be careful not to overbake them.

  7. Allow the cookies to cool on the baking sheet for 5 minutes, then transfer them to a wire rack to cool completely.

  8. Once cooled, store the Chewy and Spiced Gingerbread Snickerdoodle Cookies in an airtight container. They can be enjoyed for up to one week, if they last that long!

The Perfect Holiday Treat

These Chewy and Spiced Gingerbread Snickerdoodle Cookies are the ultimate holiday fusion. The warm spices of gingerbread paired with the soft and chewy texture of snickerdoodles create a cookie that is sure to impress. Whether you’re hosting a holiday party or simply looking for a delicious treat to enjoy with a cup of hot cocoa, these cookies are the answer.

The combination of cinnamon, ginger, cloves, and nutmeg provides a delightful burst of flavor that will transport you to a cozy winter wonderland. The sugary coating adds a touch of sweetness and a satisfying crunch to every bite. These cookies are perfect for sharing with loved ones or gifting to friends and neighbors during the holiday season.

So, why not spice up your holiday baking this year with Chewy and Spiced Gingerbread Snickerdoodle Cookies? They are a festive and delicious twist on two classic treats, and they are sure to become a new favorite in your household. Happy baking!
