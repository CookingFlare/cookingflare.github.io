Creamy and Cheesy Baked Mac and Cheese: A Holiday Favorite


Indulge in the ultimate comfort food this holiday season with our Creamy and Cheesy Baked Mac and Cheese recipe. This classic dish is a crowd-pleaser, perfect for family gatherings and festive celebrations. With its rich, velvety texture and gooey cheese, it’s bound to become a holiday favorite.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound elbow macaroni
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter
  • 1/2 cup all-purpose flour
  • 4 cups milk
  • 2 cups shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 teaspoon Dijon mustard
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1/2 cup breadcrumbs
  • Fresh parsley for garnish

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C) and grease a 9x13-inch baking dish.

  2. Cook the macaroni according to the package instructions until al dente. Drain and set aside.

  3. In a large saucepan, melt the butter over medium heat. Once melted, add the flour and whisk constantly for about 1 minute to create a roux.

  4. Slowly pour in the milk, whisking continuously to prevent lumps from forming. Cook for 5 minutes or until the mixture thickens, stirring occasionally.

  5. Reduce the heat to low and add the cheddar, mozzarella, and Parmesan cheese to the sauce. Stir until the cheese is melted and the sauce is smooth and creamy.

  6. Add the Dijon mustard, garlic powder, salt, and pepper to the sauce. Taste and adjust the seasonings if needed.

  7. Remove the saucepan from the heat and add the cooked macaroni. Stir until the macaroni is evenly coated in the cheese sauce.

  8. Transfer the macaroni and cheese mixture to the greased baking dish, spreading it out evenly.

  9. Sprinkle the breadcrumbs over the top of the macaroni, creating a crispy crust.

  10. Bake in the preheated oven for 25-30 minutes, or until the top is golden brown and the cheese is bubbly.

  11. Remove from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes. Garnish with fresh parsley before serving.

Serving Suggestions

  • Serve this Creamy and Cheesy Baked Mac and Cheese as a main course with a side of steamed vegetables or a fresh salad.
  • It also makes a delicious side dish to accompany roasted meats or grilled chicken.
  • For an extra touch of indulgence, top the baked mac and cheese with crumbled bacon or sautéed mushrooms.

Tips and Tricks

  • Feel free to experiment with different types of cheese to customize the flavors of your mac and cheese. Gouda, Swiss, or Monterey Jack can add a unique twist.
  • To make the dish even creamier, substitute some of the milk with heavy cream or half-and-half.
  • For a slightly tangy flavor, add a squeeze of lemon juice to the cheese sauce.
  • Leftovers can be stored in an airtight container in the refrigerator for up to three days. Reheat in the oven or microwave until heated through.

Conclusion

This Creamy and Cheesy Baked Mac and Cheese recipe is the epitome of comfort food. Its velvety texture and delicious combination of cheeses make it a perfect dish for holiday gatherings. Whether as a main course or a side dish, this mac and cheese will have everyone coming back for seconds. So, grab your apron and get ready to impress your family and friends with this holiday favorite!
