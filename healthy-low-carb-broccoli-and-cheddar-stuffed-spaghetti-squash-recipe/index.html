Healthy Low Carb Broccoli and Cheddar Stuffed Spaghetti Squash Recipe


If you’re looking for a delicious and nutritious low-carb meal that will satisfy your cravings without weighing you down, then you’ve come to the right place. This healthy and flavorful broccoli and cheddar stuffed spaghetti squash recipe is a game-changer. Packed with vitamins, minerals, and the comforting flavors of cheddar cheese, this dish will become a family favorite in no time.

Ingredients

  • 1 medium-sized spaghetti squash
  • 2 cups broccoli florets
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon onion powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • Fresh parsley for garnish

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Spaghetti Squash

    • Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C).
    • Carefully cut the spaghetti squash in half lengthwise and remove the seeds with a spoon.
    • Drizzle the cut sides with olive oil and season with salt and pepper.
    • Place the squash halves cut side down on a baking sheet and roast for 40-50 minutes, or until the flesh is tender and easily pierced with a fork.
    • Once cooked, use a fork to scrape the flesh into spaghetti-like strands and set aside.

  2. Cook the Broccoli

    • While the squash is roasting, steam the broccoli florets until they are tender but still vibrant green. Set aside.

  3. Prepare the Filling

    • In a large bowl, combine the cooked spaghetti squash strands, steamed broccoli, olive oil, garlic powder, onion powder, and half of the shredded cheddar cheese. Mix well.

  4. Assemble and Bake

    • Preheat your broiler.
    • Divide the filling mixture evenly among the spaghetti squash shells.
    • Top each filled squash half with the remaining shredded cheddar cheese.
    • Place the filled squash halves under the broiler for 3-5 minutes, or until the cheese is melted and bubbly.

  5. Serve and Enjoy

    • Carefully transfer the stuffed spaghetti squash to a serving platter.
    • Garnish with fresh parsley for a pop of color and added freshness.
    • Serve hot and enjoy the delicious, healthy goodness!

Nutritional Benefits

This healthy low carb broccoli and cheddar stuffed spaghetti squash recipe is not only a delight for your taste buds but also a powerhouse of nutrients. Spaghetti squash is a great source of fiber, vitamin C, and antioxidants, while broccoli adds a boost of vitamin K, vitamin C, and folate to the mix. The cheddar cheese provides a satisfying richness and a good dose of calcium and protein. This dish is a winning combination of flavor and nutrition.

Conclusion

In conclusion, this healthy low carb broccoli and cheddar stuffed spaghetti squash recipe is a must-try for anyone looking to elevate their low-carb cooking game. It’s a versatile dish that can be enjoyed as a main course or as a side. Whether you’re cooking for yourself or entertaining guests, this recipe is sure to impress. Plus, the leftovers (if there are any) make a delicious next-day lunch. So, gather your ingredients and get ready to savor the wholesome goodness of this delectable dish!
