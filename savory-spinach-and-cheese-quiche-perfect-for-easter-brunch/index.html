Savory Spinach and Cheese Quiche: Perfect for Easter Brunch


This delightful Savory Spinach and Cheese Quiche is the perfect addition to your Easter brunch menu. Bursting with flavor and a creamy texture, this quiche will impress your guests and leave them asking for seconds. With a buttery crust, a filling packed with spinach and cheese, and a hint of aromatic herbs, this quiche is a true crowd-pleaser.

Ingredients

For the crust:

  • 1 ½ cups all-purpose flour
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • ½ cup cold unsalted butter, cubed
  • 4-5 tablespoons ice water

For the filling:

  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 medium onion, finely chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 4 cups fresh spinach, chopped
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • ¼ teaspoon black pepper
  • 4 large eggs
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • ½ cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).

  2. In a large mixing bowl, combine the flour and salt for the crust. Add the cold cubed butter and use a pastry cutter or your fingertips to work the butter into the flour until it resembles coarse crumbs.

  3. Gradually add the ice water, one tablespoon at a time, and mix until the dough comes together. Be careful not to over-mix. Shape the dough into a disk, wrap it in plastic wrap, and refrigerate for at least 30 minutes.

  4. In a skillet, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the chopped onion and minced garlic, and sauté until the onion becomes translucent and fragrant.

  5. Add the chopped spinach to the skillet and cook until wilted. Season with salt and black pepper. Remove from heat and set aside.

  6. In a separate bowl, whisk together the eggs and milk until well combined. Add the shredded cheddar cheese, grated Parmesan cheese, and dried thyme. Mix until evenly incorporated.

  7. Take the chilled dough out of the refrigerator and roll it out on a floured surface to fit a 9-inch pie dish. Carefully transfer the dough to the pie dish, pressing it gently to mold it to the shape of the dish. Trim any excess dough.

  8. Spread the sautéed spinach mixture evenly over the crust. Pour the egg and cheese mixture over the spinach, making sure it covers the entire surface.

  9. Place the quiche in the preheated oven and bake for 35-40 minutes, or until the top is golden brown and the filling is set. To check if it’s done, insert a toothpick into the center of the quiche; if it comes out clean, it’s ready.

  10. Remove the quiche from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes before slicing and serving. This quiche is delicious served warm or at room temperature.

Conclusion

This Savory Spinach and Cheese Quiche is a delightful dish that will elevate your Easter brunch to a whole new level. With its buttery crust, flavorful filling, and perfect balance of spinach and cheese, it’s a recipe that will impress your guests and make your brunch memorable. The aroma of the quiche baking in the oven will fill your home, creating an inviting atmosphere that welcomes everyone to gather around the table. So go ahead and give this recipe a try. Your taste buds will thank you!
