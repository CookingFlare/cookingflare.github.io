Apple Cinnamon Pancake Casserole: A Brunch-Inspired Dessert


Are you looking for a delightful and easy-to-make dessert that can also double as a brunch dish? Look no further than this Apple Cinnamon Pancake Casserole recipe! This mouth-watering casserole combines the comforting flavors of apple and cinnamon with the fluffy goodness of pancakes, creating a treat that’s perfect for any occasion. Whether you’re hosting a brunch with friends or simply craving a cozy dessert, this recipe will surely become a favorite in your kitchen.

Ingredients

For the Pancake Batter:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons granulated sugar
  • 1 tablespoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 2 cups milk
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1/4 cup unsalted butter, melted
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

For the Apple Filling:

  • 4 large apples, peeled, cored, and thinly sliced
  • 1/4 cup brown sugar
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground nutmeg
  • 2 tablespoons unsalted butter

For the Streusel Topping:

  • 1/2 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup brown sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 cup unsalted butter, softened

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Pancake Batter: In a large bowl, whisk together the flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt. In another bowl, whisk together the milk, eggs, melted butter, and vanilla extract. Pour the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients and stir until just combined. Set aside.

  2. Make the Apple Filling: In a skillet, melt 2 tablespoons of butter over medium heat. Add the sliced apples, brown sugar, cinnamon, and nutmeg. Cook, stirring occasionally, until the apples are tender and caramelized, about 8-10 minutes. Remove from heat and set aside.

  3. Prepare the Streusel Topping: In a small bowl, combine the flour, brown sugar, and cinnamon. Cut in the softened butter using a fork or your fingers until the mixture resembles coarse crumbs. Set aside.

  4. Assemble the Casserole: Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease a 9x13-inch baking dish. Pour half of the pancake batter into the prepared dish. Spread the cooked apple filling evenly over the batter. Pour the remaining pancake batter over the apple filling. Sprinkle the streusel topping over the top.

  5. Bake and Serve: Bake the casserole for 30-35 minutes, or until the top is golden brown and the center is set. Allow the casserole to cool for a few minutes before slicing and serving. You can garnish with a dusting of powdered sugar and a drizzle of maple syrup for an extra touch of sweetness.

Tips and Variations

  • For a time-saving option, you can use store-bought pancake mix and prepared apple pie filling, although homemade is always best!
  • Feel free to add chopped nuts, such as pecans or walnuts, to the streusel topping for a delightful crunch.
  • This casserole is delicious on its own, but you can also serve it with a dollop of whipped cream or a scoop of vanilla ice cream for an indulgent treat.

Conclusion

This Apple Cinnamon Pancake Casserole is a delightful fusion of breakfast and dessert that’s sure to impress your family and friends. With its warm, comforting flavors and easy preparation, it’s the perfect addition to any brunch spread or as a sweet treat after dinner. Whether you’re a seasoned home cook or a beginner in the kitchen, this recipe is a wonderful way to showcase your culinary skills and treat yourself to a delicious, homemade dessert. So, gather your ingredients and get ready to savor the delightful aroma of apple and cinnamon as this casserole bakes to perfection in your kitchen!
