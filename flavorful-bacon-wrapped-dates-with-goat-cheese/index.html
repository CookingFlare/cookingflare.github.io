Flavorful Bacon-Wrapped Dates with Goat Cheese


If you’re looking for a mouthwatering appetizer that combines sweet, savory, and creamy flavors, look no further than these Flavorful Bacon-Wrapped Dates with Goat Cheese. This simple yet elegant dish is perfect for entertaining guests or treating yourself to a delicious snack. The combination of crispy bacon, soft dates, and creamy goat cheese creates a flavor explosion that will leave you craving more. Plus, the contrasting textures make each bite a delightful experience.

Ingredients

To make this delectable treat, you will need the following ingredients:

  • 12 Medjool dates, pitted
  • 6 slices of bacon, cut in half
  • 4 ounces of goat cheese
  • Freshly ground black pepper, to taste
  • Toothpicks, for securing the bacon

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C). Line a baking sheet with parchment paper or aluminum foil for easy cleanup.

  2. Take each pitted date and stuff it with a small amount of goat cheese. You can use a spoon or your fingers to gently fill the cavity of each date. The creamy goat cheese adds a tangy note that balances the sweetness of the dates.

  3. Once the dates are stuffed, take a half slice of bacon and wrap it around each date, securing it with a toothpick. Make sure the bacon is tightly wrapped around the date to ensure it cooks evenly and stays in place.

  4. Place the bacon-wrapped dates on the prepared baking sheet, ensuring they are evenly spaced. Sprinkle freshly ground black pepper over the top of each date for an extra kick of flavor.

  5. Transfer the baking sheet to the preheated oven and bake for 15-20 minutes, or until the bacon is crispy and golden brown. The dates should be soft and slightly caramelized.

  6. Once cooked, remove the bacon-wrapped dates from the oven and let them cool for a few minutes. The dates will be hot, so use caution when handling them.

  7. Serve the Flavorful Bacon-Wrapped Dates with Goat Cheese on a platter, removing the toothpicks before serving. These bite-sized treats are perfect for sharing and are sure to impress your guests with their irresistible combination of flavors.

Serving Suggestions

While these Bacon-Wrapped Dates with Goat Cheese are delicious on their own, you can elevate them even further with a few additional ingredients:

  • Balsamic Glaze: Drizzle a tangy balsamic glaze over the top of the dates for a touch of acidity that complements the sweetness of the dates and richness of the goat cheese.
  • Fresh Herbs: Garnish the platter with chopped fresh herbs such as parsley, basil, or chives to add a pop of color and freshness to the dish.
  • Nuts: Crushed toasted nuts like almonds or walnuts can be sprinkled over the top for added texture and nuttiness.

Conclusion

These Flavorful Bacon-Wrapped Dates with Goat Cheese are a crowd-pleasing appetizer that combines sweet, savory, and creamy flavors in every bite. The crispy bacon, soft dates, and tangy goat cheese create an irresistible combination that will leave your taste buds wanting more. Whether you’re hosting a dinner party or simply treating yourself, these bacon-wrapped dates are sure to impress. Enjoy!
