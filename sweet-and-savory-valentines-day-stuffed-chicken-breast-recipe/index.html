Sweet and Savory: Valentine's Day Stuffed Chicken Breast Recipe


Valentine’s Day is just around the corner, and what better way to celebrate than with a special and delicious home-cooked meal? This Sweet and Savory Stuffed Chicken Breast recipe is the perfect dish to impress your loved one with its beautiful presentation and irresistible flavors. So, put on your apron, grab your ingredients, and let’s get cooking!

Ingredients

  • 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 4 slices of prosciutto
  • 1 cup of baby spinach
  • 1/2 cup of feta cheese
  • 1/4 cup of dried cranberries
  • 2 tablespoons of balsamic glaze
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Olive oil for cooking

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).

  2. Lay the chicken breasts flat on a cutting board and carefully butterfly each breast. To do this, make a lengthwise cut through the thickest part of the breast, being careful not to cut all the way through. Open up the chicken breast like a book and flatten it gently with a meat mallet or the palm of your hand.

  3. Season the inside of the chicken breasts with salt and pepper.

  4. Layer two slices of prosciutto on each chicken breast, covering the entire surface.

  5. Spread a handful of baby spinach evenly over the prosciutto.

  6. Sprinkle feta cheese and dried cranberries over the spinach.

  7. Carefully roll up the chicken breasts, starting from one end and tucking in the filling as you go. Secure the rolls with toothpicks.

  8. Heat some olive oil in a large oven-safe skillet over medium-high heat. Add the stuffed chicken breasts and sear them for about 2-3 minutes on each side until golden brown.

  9. Transfer the skillet to the preheated oven and bake for 20-25 minutes, or until the chicken is cooked through and the juices run clear.

  10. Remove the stuffed chicken breasts from the oven and let them rest for a few minutes.

  11. Drizzle the balsamic glaze over the chicken breasts just before serving.

  12. Carefully remove the toothpicks from the chicken breasts and slice them into thick rounds.

  13. Plate the chicken breasts and serve them with your choice of sides, such as roasted veggies or a fresh green salad.

Tips and Variations

  • If you don’t have prosciutto, you can substitute it with thinly sliced ham or bacon.
  • Feel free to experiment with different fillings. Some delicious alternatives include sun-dried tomatoes, goat cheese, or caramelized onions.
  • For an extra touch of sweetness, you can brush the chicken breasts with a honey mustard glaze before baking.
  • If you’re looking to save time, you can prepare the stuffed chicken breasts in advance and refrigerate them until you’re ready to cook. Just make sure to adjust the cooking time accordingly.

Enjoy this Sweet and Savory Stuffed Chicken Breast recipe as a romantic dinner for two or as a special treat for any occasion. The combination of the tender chicken, salty prosciutto, creamy feta cheese, and sweet cranberries will surely delight your taste buds and make your Valentine’s Day celebration extra memorable. Bon appétit!
