Grilled Corn on the Cob: A Summer Staple with a Flavorful Twist


Summer is finally here, and with it comes the perfect excuse to fire up the grill and indulge in some delicious, seasonal treats. One of the most iconic summer staples is grilled corn on the cob, and for good reason. Sweet, smoky, and utterly satisfying, grilled corn is the perfect accompaniment to any summer gathering or backyard BBQ.

But, let’s be real, plain old grilled corn can get a little…boring. That’s why we’re shaking things up with a flavorful twist that will elevate this summer classic to new heights. Say hello to the most epic grilled corn on the cob recipe you’ll ever try!

The Secret to the Best Grilled Corn

So, what makes our grilled corn stand out from the rest? It’s all about the combination of flavors and textures. We’re talking about a sweet and spicy rub, a tangy slaw, and a sprinkle of crispy, crunchy goodness on top. Trust us, this is the perfect combination to take your grilled corn game to the next level.

The Recipe

Here’s what you’ll need:

  • 4-6 ears of corn, husked and silked
  • 2 tablespoons of olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon of brown sugar
  • 1 tablespoon of smoked paprika
  • 1 teaspoon of garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon of salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon of black pepper
  • 1/4 cup of crumbled cotija cheese (optional)
  • 1/4 cup of chopped cilantro (optional)
  • 1/4 cup of lime crema (see below for recipe)
  • 1/4 cup of spicy slaw (see below for recipe)

Lime Crema Recipe

  • 1 cup of sour cream
  • 2 tablespoons of freshly squeezed lime juice
  • 1 tablespoon of honey
  • 1/2 teaspoon of salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon of black pepper

Spicy Slaw Recipe

  • 2 cups of shredded red cabbage
  • 1 cup of shredded carrots
  • 2 tablespoons of mayonnaise
  • 1 tablespoon of apple cider vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon of honey
  • 1/2 teaspoon of salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon of black pepper
  • 1-2 teaspoons of sriracha sauce (depending on desired level of heat)

Instructions

  1. Preheat your grill to medium-high heat.
  2. In a small bowl, whisk together the olive oil, brown sugar, smoked paprika, garlic powder, salt, and black pepper.
  3. Brush the mixture evenly onto each ear of corn.
  4. Place the corn on the grill and cook for 10-12 minutes, turning every 2-3 minutes, until slightly charred and tender.
  5. While the corn is cooking, prepare the lime crema and spicy slaw according to the recipes above.
  6. Once the corn is cooked, brush with lime crema and sprinkle with cotija cheese, cilantro, and spicy slaw.
  7. Serve immediately and enjoy!

Tips and Variations

  • For an extra crispy texture, try grilling the corn for an additional 2-3 minutes on each side.
  • Substitute the cotija cheese with crumbled feta or parmesan for a different flavor profile.
  • Add some diced jalapeños or serrano peppers to the spicy slaw for an extra kick.
  • Use this recipe as a base and add your own favorite toppings, such as grilled shrimp, diced tomatoes, or crumbled bacon.

And there you have it, folks! A summer staple with a flavorful twist that’s sure to become a new favorite. So go ahead, fire up the grill, and get ready to indulge in the most epic grilled corn on the cob of your life. Happy grilling!
