Classic Old Fashioned Cupcakes: A Whiskey-infused Treat

3 min · 489 words

If you’re a fan of classic cocktails and delightful desserts, then you’re in for a treat with these Classic Old Fashioned Cupcakes. Infused with the rich flavors of whiskey, these cupcakes are a sophisticated twist on a beloved classic. With a moist and tender crumb, a hint of citrus, and a boozy kick, these cupcakes are sure to impress at any gathering or special occasion.

Ingredients

For the cupcakes:

  • 1 ½ cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 ½ teaspoons baking powder
  • ¼ teaspoon salt
  • ½ cup unsalted butter, at room temperature
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • Zest of 1 orange
  • 1 tablespoon fresh orange juice
  • ⅓ cup whiskey
  • ½ cup buttermilk

For the whiskey glaze:

  • ¼ cup whiskey
  • ½ cup powdered sugar

For the frosting:

  • 1 ½ cups heavy cream
  • ¼ cup powdered sugar
  • 2 tablespoons whiskey
  • ½ teaspoon vanilla extract

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C). Line a muffin tin with cupcake liners and set aside.

  2. In a medium-sized bowl, whisk together the flour, baking powder, and salt. Set aside.

  3. In a large bowl, cream together the butter and sugar until light and fluffy. Add the eggs one at a time, beating well after each addition. Stir in the orange zest, orange juice, and whiskey.

  4. Gradually add the dry ingredients to the wet ingredients, alternating with the buttermilk. Begin and end with the dry ingredients. Mix until just combined, being careful not to overmix.

  5. Divide the batter evenly among the cupcake liners, filling each about two-thirds full. Bake for 18-20 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.

  6. While the cupcakes are baking, prepare the whiskey glaze. In a small saucepan over medium heat, combine the whiskey and powdered sugar. Stir until the sugar is dissolved and the mixture has slightly thickened. Remove from heat and set aside to cool.

  7. Once the cupcakes are baked, remove them from the oven and let them cool in the pan for 5 minutes. Transfer the cupcakes to a wire rack to cool completely.

  8. Once the cupcakes have cooled, use a toothpick or skewer to poke several small holes in the tops of each cupcake. Brush the whiskey glaze over the cupcakes, allowing it to soak in.

  9. To make the whiskey frosting, in a large bowl, beat the heavy cream until soft peaks form. Add the powdered sugar, whiskey, and vanilla extract. Continue to beat until stiff peaks form.

  10. Pipe or spread the whiskey frosting onto the cooled cupcakes. You can get creative with your frosting designs or simply spread it on with a knife for a rustic look.

  11. Serve these Classic Old Fashioned Cupcakes chilled or at room temperature. They pair perfectly with a glass of whiskey or a cup of coffee.

Enjoy these boozy and indulgent Classic Old Fashioned Cupcakes that bring together the best of two worlds: cocktails and desserts. Whether you’re hosting a party or simply treating yourself, these whiskey-infused treats will leave a lasting impression on your taste buds. So go ahead, savor the flavors and raise a toast to culinary creativity!
