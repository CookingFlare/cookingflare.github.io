Baked Cod with Lemon and Herbs Recipe: A Simple, Healthy Option


If you’re looking for a light and flavorful dish that’s easy to prepare, baked cod with lemon and herbs is the perfect choice. This simple recipe allows the delicate flavor of the cod to shine, enhanced by the brightness of lemon and the earthy aroma of herbs. Not only is this dish delicious, but it’s also a healthy option, making it a great addition to your weekly meal rotation.

Ingredients

To make this delectable baked cod dish, you will need the following ingredients:

  • 4 fresh cod fillets
  • 2 lemons
  • 3 tablespoons of olive oil
  • 4 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon of fresh parsley, chopped
  • 1 teaspoon of fresh thyme, chopped
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

Follow these simple steps to create your baked cod with lemon and herbs:

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Rinse the cod fillets under cold water and pat them dry with a paper towel.
  3. Place the cod fillets in a single layer in a baking dish.
  4. Drizzle the olive oil over the fillets, ensuring they are evenly coated.
  5. Squeeze the juice of one lemon over the fillets, then slice the other lemon and place the slices on top of the fish.
  6. Sprinkle the minced garlic, chopped parsley, and thyme over the fillets.
  7. Season the fillets with salt and pepper to taste.
  8. Cover the baking dish with foil and bake in the preheated oven for 15-20 minutes, or until the fish is opaque and flakes easily with a fork.

Serving Suggestions

This baked cod with lemon and herbs pairs beautifully with a variety of sides. Consider serving it with steamed vegetables, a fresh green salad, or a side of quinoa for a well-balanced meal. The bright flavors of the lemon and herbs complement a wide range of accompaniments, allowing you to customize the dish to suit your preferences.

Health Benefits

Not only is this dish bursting with flavor, but it also offers numerous health benefits. Cod is an excellent source of lean protein and is rich in vitamins and minerals, including vitamin B12, niacin, and phosphorus. Additionally, the use of heart-healthy olive oil and fresh herbs adds an extra nutritional boost to this already wholesome meal.

Final Thoughts

Baked cod with lemon and herbs is a simple yet elegant dish that is sure to impress your family and friends. Whether you’re hosting a dinner party or preparing a weeknight meal, this recipe is a versatile option that can be easily adapted to suit your needs. With its vibrant flavors and healthful ingredients, it’s no wonder that this dish has become a staple in many households.

So why not give this recipe a try and savor the delightful combination of tender cod, zesty lemon, and aromatic herbs? Your taste buds and your body will thank you for choosing this delightful and nutritious option.
