The Last Word: Unlocking the Secrets of a Classic Herbal Cocktail


If you’re a fan of classic cocktails, you’ve probably come across The Last Word at some point. This herbal concoction has a fascinating history and a unique flavor profile that has stood the test of time. In this blog post, we’ll delve into the secrets of The Last Word, exploring its origins, ingredients, and the perfect way to prepare this timeless drink.

The History of The Last Word

The Last Word is a cocktail with a rich history that dates back to the Prohibition era. It was first created at the Detroit Athletic Club in the 1920s, where it remained a well-kept secret for decades. This cocktail eventually fell into obscurity before experiencing a resurgence in popularity in recent years. Its revival is credited to the craft cocktail renaissance and the newfound appreciation for classic recipes.

Unraveling the Ingredients

One of the most intriguing aspects of The Last Word is its unique combination of herbal and citrus flavors. This cocktail calls for equal parts of gin, green Chartreuse, maraschino liqueur, and freshly squeezed lime juice. The gin provides a botanical backbone, while the green Chartreuse contributes a complex herbal sweetness. The maraschino liqueur adds a subtle nuttiness, and the lime juice brings a refreshing citrusy kick. When these ingredients come together, they create a harmonious blend of flavors that is both sophisticated and refreshing.

Crafting the Perfect Last Word

Now that we’ve explored the history and components of The Last Word, let’s dive into the process of making this iconic cocktail. To craft the perfect Last Word, you’ll need the following:

  • 1 ounce gin
  • 1 ounce green Chartreuse
  • 1 ounce maraschino liqueur
  • 1 ounce freshly squeezed lime juice
  • Cocktail shaker
  • Strainer
  • Coupe glass
  1. Begin by filling a cocktail shaker with ice.
  2. Add the gin, green Chartreuse, maraschino liqueur, and lime juice to the shaker.
  3. Shake the mixture vigorously for about 15 seconds to chill the ingredients and achieve proper dilution.
  4. Once the cocktail is well chilled, strain it into a chilled coupe glass.
  5. Optionally, garnish with a twist of lime or a Luxardo cherry for an elegant finishing touch.

The Last Sip

The Last Word is a cocktail that has stood the test of time, and for good reason. Its complex yet balanced flavor profile and intriguing history make it a timeless classic that continues to captivate cocktail enthusiasts worldwide. Whether you’re a seasoned mixologist or a curious cocktail enthusiast, mastering the art of The Last Word is a rewarding endeavor that is sure to impress your guests and elevate your bartending skills.

In conclusion, The Last Word is a cocktail that embodies the artistry and allure of classic mixology. From its clandestine origins to its delightful blend of flavors, this herbal concoction holds a special place in the world of cocktails. So, the next time you’re in the mood for something sophisticated and refreshing, consider unlocking the secrets of The Last Word and savoring every sip of this timeless libation. Cheers!
