Spicy and Tangy Korean BBQ Beef Lettuce Wraps


If you’re a fan of Korean cuisine, you’re in for a treat! These Spicy and Tangy Korean BBQ Beef Lettuce Wraps are a delightful combination of savory, sweet, and spicy flavors, all wrapped up in a crisp lettuce leaf. Whether you’re hosting a dinner party or simply looking to spice up your weeknight dinner routine, this recipe is sure to impress. So, roll up your sleeves and get ready to embark on a culinary adventure!

Ingredients

For the Marinade:

  • 1/2 cup soy sauce
  • 1/4 cup brown sugar
  • 2 tablespoons sesame oil
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon grated fresh ginger
  • 2 tablespoons Korean red pepper paste (gochujang)
  • 1 tablespoon rice vinegar
  • 1 teaspoon black pepper

For the Beef:

  • 1 1/2 pounds beef sirloin, thinly sliced
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil

For the Lettuce Wraps:

  • 1 head of butter or iceberg lettuce, leaves separated and washed
  • 1 cup shredded carrots
  • 1 cup sliced cucumbers
  • 1/2 cup thinly sliced green onions
  • Sesame seeds, for garnish
  • Lime wedges, for serving

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Marinade: In a bowl, whisk together the soy sauce, brown sugar, sesame oil, garlic, ginger, red pepper paste, rice vinegar, and black pepper until the sugar has dissolved.

  2. Marinate the Beef: Place the thinly sliced beef in a shallow dish and pour half of the marinade over the beef, reserving the other half for later. Toss the beef to ensure it’s evenly coated in the marinade. Cover and refrigerate for at least 1 hour, allowing the flavors to infuse.

  3. Sear the Beef: In a large skillet or wok, heat the vegetable oil over high heat. Working in batches, sear the marinated beef for 2-3 minutes per batch, allowing it to caramelize and develop a rich, smoky flavor. Transfer the seared beef to a plate and set aside.

  4. Assemble the Lettuce Wraps: Arrange the lettuce leaves on a platter, and fill each leaf with a small portion of the seared beef. Top the beef with shredded carrots, sliced cucumbers, and green onions. Drizzle the remaining marinade over the assembled wraps and sprinkle with sesame seeds for an added crunch.

  5. Serve and Enjoy: Serve the Spicy and Tangy Korean BBQ Beef Lettuce Wraps with lime wedges on the side for a burst of citrus freshness. Encourage your guests to assemble their own wraps, customizing the fillings to their liking.

Tips for Success

  • For a vegetarian version, swap the beef for marinated and grilled tofu or mushrooms.
  • Adjust the level of spiciness by adding more or less Korean red pepper paste to the marinade.
  • Make it a complete meal by serving the lettuce wraps with steamed rice or kimchi on the side.

In Conclusion

These Spicy and Tangy Korean BBQ Beef Lettuce Wraps are a delightful balance of flavors and textures, making them a perfect choice for a flavorful and interactive dining experience. Whether you’re a seasoned pro or a novice in the kitchen, this recipe is approachable and rewarding, offering a taste of Korea right in your own home. So, gather your loved ones, roll up your sleeves, and savor the vibrant and bold flavors of these delectable lettuce wraps. Bon appétit!
