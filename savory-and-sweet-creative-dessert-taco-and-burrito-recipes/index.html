Savory and Sweet: Creative Dessert Taco and Burrito Recipes


Who says tacos and burritos are just for savory dishes? Get ready to embark on a flavor-packed journey as we explore the world of dessert tacos and burritos. These creative and indulgent treats will satisfy your sweet tooth like never before. So, grab your tortillas and let’s dive into these mouthwatering recipes!

Sweet Strawberry Shortcake Tacos

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 tablespoon sugar
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1/4 cup melted butter
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 pint fresh strawberries, sliced
  • Whipped cream for topping

Instructions:

  1. In a mixing bowl, combine the flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt.
  2. In a separate bowl, whisk together the milk, melted butter, and vanilla extract.
  3. Gradually add the wet ingredients to the dry ingredients, stirring until well combined.
  4. Heat a non-stick pan over medium heat and lightly grease it.
  5. Pour 1/4 cup of the batter onto the pan, spreading it into a thin circle.
  6. Cook for about 2 minutes or until the edges are golden brown, then flip and cook for another minute.
  7. Repeat with the remaining batter.
  8. Once the taco shells are cool, fill them with the sliced strawberries and top with whipped cream.
  9. Serve immediately and enjoy this delightful twist on a classic dessert!

Savory Chocolate Chili Burritos

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can kidney beans, drained and rinsed
  • 1 can diced tomatoes
  • 2 tablespoons chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • 1 teaspoon cocoa powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Flour tortillas

Instructions:

  1. In a large skillet, cook the ground beef, onion, and garlic until the beef is browned and the onion is translucent.
  2. Drain any excess fat from the skillet.
  3. Add the kidney beans, diced tomatoes, chili powder, cumin, cocoa powder, salt, and pepper.
  4. Stir well and let the mixture simmer for about 15 minutes.
  5. Warm the flour tortillas in a dry skillet or microwave.
  6. Spoon the chili mixture onto each tortilla and roll it up like a burrito.
  7. Serve hot and enjoy the unique combination of savory flavors in this chocolate-infused chili burrito!

Sweet and Salty Caramel Pretzel Tacos

Ingredients:

  • Pretzel shells:

    • 1 cup all-purpose flour
    • 1/4 cup unsalted butter, melted
    • 1/4 cup warm water
    • 1 tablespoon sugar
    • 1/2 teaspoon salt

  • Caramel filling:

    • 1 cup granulated sugar
    • 1/4 cup water
    • 1/2 cup heavy cream
    • 2 tablespoons unsalted butter
    • 1/2 teaspoon salt

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. In a mixing bowl, combine the flour, melted butter, warm water, sugar, and salt.
  3. Knead the dough until it forms a smooth ball.
  4. Roll out the dough into a thin sheet and cut it into small circles.
  5. Place the circles on a greased baking sheet and bake for about 10 minutes or until golden brown.
  6. Meanwhile, prepare the caramel filling by combining the sugar and water in a saucepan over medium heat.
  7. Stir until the sugar has dissolved, then let it simmer without stirring for about 10 minutes or until it turns into a golden caramel color.
  8. Remove the saucepan from heat and carefully add the heavy cream, butter, and salt. Stir until well combined.
  9. Let the caramel cool for a few minutes.
  10. Once the pretzel shells have cooled, spoon a generous amount of caramel into each shell.
  11. Serve immediately and savor the delightful combination of sweet and salty flavors in these pretzel tacos.

Conclusion

Dessert tacos and burritos offer a unique way to enjoy your favorite sweet treats with a creative twist. From the classic flavors of strawberry shortcake to the unexpected combination of chocolate and chili, these recipes are sure to impress your taste buds. So, next time you’re craving something sweet, think outside the box and indulge in the deliciousness of dessert tacos and burritos. Trust us, your taste buds will thank you!

Note: Remember to play around with the fillings and toppings to make these recipes your own. Get creative and have fun exploring different flavor combinations!
