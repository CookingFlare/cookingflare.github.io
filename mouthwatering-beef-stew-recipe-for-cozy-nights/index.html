Mouthwatering Beef Stew Recipe for Cozy Nights


There’s nothing quite like a warm and hearty beef stew to satisfy your cravings on a cozy night. This mouthwatering recipe combines tender beef, aromatic vegetables, and flavorful herbs to create a dish that will leave you wanting more. So, grab your apron and let’s get cooking!

Ingredients

  • 2 pounds beef stew meat, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 3 carrots, peeled and sliced
  • 3 celery stalks, sliced
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons tomato paste
  • 4 cups beef broth
  • 1 cup red wine (optional)
  • 2 bay leaves
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • 1 teaspoon dried rosemary
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 cup frozen peas

Instructions

  1. Heat the olive oil in a large pot or Dutch oven over medium-high heat. Add the beef stew meat and brown on all sides. This step helps to develop the rich flavors of the stew.

  2. Once the meat is browned, remove it from the pot and set it aside. In the same pot, add the diced onion, sliced carrots, and sliced celery. Sauté the vegetables until they start to soften, about 5 minutes.

  3. Add the minced garlic and tomato paste to the pot, and cook for an additional 1-2 minutes, stirring constantly. This will help release the flavors of the garlic and tomato paste.

  4. Return the browned beef stew meat to the pot, and pour in the beef broth and red wine (if using). The liquid will help to tenderize the meat and add depth to the stew.

  5. Add the bay leaves, dried thyme, dried rosemary, salt, and pepper to the pot. Stir everything together, then bring the stew to a boil. Once boiling, reduce the heat to low and cover the pot with a lid. Allow the stew to simmer for 2-3 hours, or until the beef is tender and the flavors have melded together.

  6. In the last 10 minutes of cooking, add the frozen peas to the pot. This will add a burst of freshness to the stew and bring a vibrant color to the dish.

  7. Once the stew is ready, remove the bay leaves and discard them. Taste the stew and adjust the seasoning with salt and pepper if needed.

  8. Serve the mouthwatering beef stew hot in bowls, with crusty bread on the side for dipping. The tender beef, flavorful broth, and hearty vegetables will warm you up from the inside out.

Tips and Variations

  • For a thicker stew, you can mix 2 tablespoons of flour with a little water to create a slurry. Stir the slurry into the stew during the last 30 minutes of cooking to thicken the broth.
  • If you prefer a richer flavor, you can sear the beef stew meat in batches instead of browning it all at once. This will create a deeper caramelization on the meat.
  • Feel free to customize the vegetables in this stew based on your preferences. You can add potatoes, parsnips, or even mushrooms for extra depth and texture.

Conclusion

This mouthwatering beef stew recipe is the perfect comfort food for cozy nights. Its rich flavors and tender beef will warm you up and leave you feeling satisfied. So, gather your ingredients, fire up the stove, and indulge in a bowl of this hearty stew. Enjoy!
