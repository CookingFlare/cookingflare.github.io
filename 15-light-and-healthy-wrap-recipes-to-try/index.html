15 Light and Healthy Wrap Recipes to Try


Wraps are a fantastic way to enjoy a nutritious meal without the heaviness of traditional sandwiches. They are versatile, easy to prepare, and can be customized to suit your taste preferences or dietary needs. Whether you’re looking for a quick lunch option, a light dinner, or a portable snack, these 15 light and healthy wrap recipes are sure to satisfy your cravings while keeping your meals wholesome and delicious.

1. Grilled Chicken and Avocado Wrap

Ingredients:

  • 1 whole grain tortilla
  • 4 ounces grilled chicken breast, sliced
  • 1/2 avocado, sliced
  • 1 cup mixed greens
  • 1/4 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1 tablespoon Greek yogurt or light dressing

Instructions:

  1. Lay the whole grain tortilla flat on a clean surface.
  2. Spread Greek yogurt or your choice of dressing over the tortilla.
  3. Layer the grilled chicken, avocado, mixed greens, and cherry tomatoes.
  4. Roll the tortilla tightly, folding in the sides as you go.
  5. Slice in half and enjoy your healthy wrap!

2. Mediterranean Hummus and Veggie Wrap

Ingredients:

  • 1 large spinach tortilla
  • 1/2 cup hummus
  • 1/2 cucumber, thinly sliced
  • 1/2 bell pepper, sliced
  • 1/4 red onion, thinly sliced
  • 1/4 cup feta cheese, crumbled
  • A handful of fresh parsley

Instructions:

  1. Spread the hummus evenly over the spinach tortilla.
  2. Add the cucumber, bell pepper, red onion, feta cheese, and parsley.
  3. Roll the tortilla tightly and slice into bite-sized pieces for a delightful snack or light meal.

3. Turkey and Spinach Wrap

Ingredients:

  • 1 whole wheat tortilla
  • 4 ounces sliced turkey breast
  • 1 cup fresh spinach leaves
  • 1/4 cup shredded carrots
  • 1 tablespoon Dijon mustard

Instructions:

  1. Spread Dijon mustard over the whole wheat tortilla.
  2. Layer sliced turkey, spinach, and shredded carrots on top.
  3. Roll the tortilla tightly and cut in half to serve.

4. Shrimp and Mango Salsa Wrap

Ingredients:

  • 1 large flour tortilla
  • 6 ounces cooked shrimp
  • 1/2 cup mango salsa
  • 1/2 avocado, sliced
  • 1 cup arugula

Instructions:

  1. Place the flour tortilla on a flat surface.
  2. Add cooked shrimp, mango salsa, avocado slices, and arugula.
  3. Roll the tortilla tightly, slice, and enjoy the burst of flavors!

5. Caprese Wrap with Pesto

Ingredients:

  • 1 whole grain tortilla
  • 1/2 cup fresh mozzarella, sliced
  • 1 medium tomato, sliced
  • A handful of fresh basil leaves
  • 2 tablespoons pesto

Instructions:

  1. Spread pesto on the whole grain tortilla.
  2. Layer mozzarella, tomato slices, and basil leaves.
  3. Roll the tortilla tightly, cut in half, and relish the classic Caprese flavors.

6. Quinoa and Black Bean Wrap

Ingredients:

  • 1 large spinach tortilla
  • 1/2 cup cooked quinoa
  • 1/2 cup black beans, rinsed and drained
  • 1/4 cup corn
  • 1/4 cup diced red onion
  • 1 tablespoon lime juice

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, mix quinoa, black beans, corn, red onion, and lime juice.
  2. Spread the mixture on the spinach tortilla.
  3. Roll it up tightly and enjoy a protein-packed meal.

7. Peanut Butter Banana Wrap

Ingredients:

  • 1 whole wheat tortilla
  • 2 tablespoons natural peanut butter
  • 1 banana, sliced
  • 1 tablespoon honey
  • A sprinkle of cinnamon

Instructions:

  1. Spread peanut butter over the whole wheat tortilla.
  2. Layer banana slices, drizzle honey, and sprinkle cinnamon.
  3. Roll up the tortilla and slice for a quick, energizing snack.

8. Roasted Veggie and Feta Wrap

Ingredients:

  • 1 large whole wheat tortilla
  • 1 cup assorted roasted vegetables (zucchini, bell peppers, eggplant)
  • 1/4 cup crumbled feta cheese
  • A handful of spinach

Instructions:

  1. Spread roasted vegetables evenly on the tortilla.
  2. Top with crumbled feta cheese and spinach.
  3. Roll tightly and enjoy the flavors of roasted veggies and feta.

9. Breakfast Wrap with Eggs and Spinach

Ingredients:

  • 1 whole grain tortilla
  • 2 scrambled eggs
  • 1 cup fresh spinach
  • 1/4 avocado, sliced
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Scramble the eggs in a non-stick pan until cooked through.
  2. Lay the tortilla flat and add scrambled eggs, spinach, and avocado.
  3. Season with salt and pepper, roll up, and dig into this healthy breakfast option.

10. Thai Chicken Salad Wrap

Ingredients:

  • 1 large lettuce leaf (romaine or butter lettuce)
  • 4 ounces cooked chicken, shredded
  • 1/4 cup shredded cabbage
  • 1/4 cup grated carrots
  • 2 tablespoons Thai peanut sauce

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, combine shredded chicken, cabbage, carrots, and Thai peanut sauce.
  2. Spoon the mixture onto the lettuce leaf.
  3. Roll it up like a burrito and enjoy a fresh and zesty wrap.

11. Spinach and Feta Wrap

Ingredients:

  • 1 large spinach tortilla
  • 1 cup fresh spinach
  • 1/2 cup crumbled feta cheese
  • 1/4 cup sun-dried tomatoes, sliced
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil

Instructions:

  1. Lay the spinach tortilla flat and drizzle with olive oil.
  2. Add fresh spinach, feta cheese, and sun-dried tomatoes.
  3. Roll tightly, cut in half, and savor the Mediterranean flavors.

12. Grilled Vegetable and Hummus Wrap

Ingredients:

  • 1 whole wheat tortilla
  • 1 cup assorted grilled vegetables (bell peppers, zucchini, onions)
  • 1/2 cup hummus
  • A handful of arugula

Instructions:

  1. Spread hummus over the whole wheat tortilla.
  2. Layer with grilled vegetables and arugula.
  3. Roll tightly and enjoy the smoky flavors of grilled veggies.

13. Sliced Apple and Cheddar Wrap

Ingredients:

  • 1 large flour tortilla
  • 2 ounces sharp cheddar cheese, sliced
  • 1 small apple, thinly sliced
  • A handful of mixed greens
  • Honey mustard for drizzling

Instructions:

  1. Lay the tortilla flat and arrange cheddar cheese, apple slices, and mixed greens.
  2. Drizzle with honey mustard.
  3. Roll up tightly and enjoy the sweet and savory combination.

14. Cilantro Lime Chicken Wrap

Ingredients:

  • 1 large whole grain tortilla
  • 4 ounces grilled chicken, diced
  • 1/4 cup corn
  • 1/4 cup black beans
  • 1 tablespoon chopped cilantro
  • Juice of 1 lime

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, mix grilled chicken, corn, black beans, cilantro, and lime juice.
  2. Spread the mixture on the tortilla.
  3. Roll tightly and enjoy the zesty flavors.

15. Smoked Salmon and Cream Cheese Wrap

Ingredients:

  • 1 whole wheat tortilla
  • 4 ounces smoked salmon
  • 2 tablespoons cream cheese
  • 1/4 cup capers
  • A handful of arugula

Instructions:

  1. Spread cream cheese over the whole wheat tortilla.
  2. Layer smoked salmon, capers, and arugula.
  3. Roll tightly, cut in half, and indulge in this luxurious wrap.

These 15 light and healthy wrap recipes are perfect for anyone looking to enjoy a delicious meal without the extra calories. They can easily be modified to accommodate different dietary preferences, making them suitable for everyone. Wraps are not only a quick and easy meal option but also a great way to use up leftover ingredients from your fridge. The combinations are endless, and the flavors are sure to delight. So grab your favorite tortillas and get creative in the kitchen!
