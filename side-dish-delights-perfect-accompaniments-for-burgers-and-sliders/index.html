Side Dish Delights: Perfect Accompaniments for Burgers and Sliders


When it comes to burgers and sliders, the options for side dishes can be overwhelming. From classic combinations like ketchup and fries to more adventurous options like grilled pineapple and coleslaw, there’s no shortage of delicious accompaniments to pair with your patty. In this post, we’ll explore some of the most popular side dishes that perfectly complement burgers and sliders, and provide you with some inspiration for your next backyard barbecue or casual dinner party.

Classic Combinations

For a timeless twist on the classic combo, try pairing your burger or slider with some crispy, golden-brown onion rings. Simply slice an onion into thin rings, separate them into individual rings, and coat them in a mixture of flour, eggs, and breadcrumbs. Fry them in hot oil until crispy, and season with salt and pepper to taste. Serve them alongside your burger for a satisfying crunch.

Another classic combination is pairing your burger with some creamy, tangy coleslaw. Made with shredded cabbage, mayonnaise, and a splash of vinegar, coleslaw is a refreshing contrast to the richness of the burger. You can also add some chopped carrots or bell peppers to give it a pop of color and added crunch.

Fresh and Fruity Options

For a lighter, fresher take on the classic side dish, try pairing your burger with some grilled or roasted vegetables. Sliced bell peppers, zucchini, and cherry tomatoes are all great options, and can be seasoned with olive oil, salt, and pepper for added flavor. You can also toss them with some chopped fresh herbs like parsley or basil for added brightness.

If you’re looking for something a little sweeter, try pairing your burger with some grilled pineapple rings. Simply brush the pineapple slices with a little bit of honey and grill them until caramelized, then serve them alongside your burger. The sweetness of the pineapple pairs perfectly with the savory flavor of the burger.

Crunchy and Savory Options

For a satisfying crunch, try pairing your burger with some crispy, savory potato chips. You can make your own by slicing potatoes thinly and frying them in hot oil until crispy, or buy them pre-made at your local grocery store. Serve them alongside your burger for a satisfying crunch.

Another crunchy option is pairing your burger with some crispy, flavorful bacon. Simply cook some bacon strips in a pan until crispy, then chop them into small pieces and sprinkle them over your burger. The smoky, savory flavor of the bacon pairs perfectly with the richness of the burger.

Global Inspiration

For a twist on the classic side dish, try pairing your burger with some international flavors. For example, you can try pairing your burger with some spicy Korean-style kimchi slaw, made with shredded cabbage, Korean chili flakes, and a splash of soy sauce. Or, you can try pairing it with some creamy, tangy tzatziki sauce, made with yogurt, cucumber, and garlic.

Another international option is pairing your burger with some crispy, flavorful latkes. Made with shredded potatoes, onions, and eggs, latkes are a classic Jewish dish that pairs perfectly with the savory flavor of the burger. Simply cook them in a pan until crispy, then top them with some sour cream or applesauce for added flavor.

Conclusion

When it comes to side dishes for burgers and sliders, the options are endless. From classic combinations like onion rings and coleslaw to fresh and fruity options like grilled pineapple and roasted vegetables, there’s something for everyone. Whether you’re looking for something crunchy and savory or international and inspired, these side dishes are sure to please. So next time you’re firing up the grill or cooking up a batch of sliders, don’t forget to add one (or two, or three) of these delicious side dishes to the mix. Your taste buds will thank you!
