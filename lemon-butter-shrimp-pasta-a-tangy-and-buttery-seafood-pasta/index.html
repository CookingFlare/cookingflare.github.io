Lemon Butter Shrimp Pasta: A Tangy and Buttery Seafood Pasta


If you’re a fan of seafood and pasta, this Lemon Butter Shrimp Pasta recipe is a must-try! This dish combines the succulent flavors of shrimp with the tangy zest of lemon and the rich, velvety smoothness of butter. It’s a delightful medley of flavors that will tantalize your taste buds and leave you craving for more. Whether you’re cooking for a special occasion or simply treating yourself to a delicious meal, this recipe is sure to impress.

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound of large shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 12 ounces of linguine or spaghetti
  • 4 tablespoons of unsalted butter
  • 4 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1/2 cup of chicken broth
  • 1/4 cup of fresh lemon juice
  • Zest of 1 lemon
  • 1/4 teaspoon of red pepper flakes
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • Chopped fresh parsley for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the Pasta: Cook the linguine or spaghetti according to the package instructions until al dente. Drain the pasta and set it aside.

  2. Cook the Shrimp: In a large skillet, melt 2 tablespoons of butter over medium-high heat. Add the shrimp to the skillet and season with salt and black pepper. Cook the shrimp for 2-3 minutes on each side until they turn pink and opaque. Once cooked, remove the shrimp from the skillet and set them aside.

  3. Make the Lemon Butter Sauce: In the same skillet, melt the remaining 2 tablespoons of butter. Add the minced garlic and red pepper flakes, and sauté for about a minute until the garlic becomes fragrant. Pour in the chicken broth, lemon juice, and lemon zest. Allow the sauce to simmer for 2-3 minutes, allowing the flavors to meld together. Season the sauce with salt and pepper to taste.

  4. Combine the Ingredients: Return the cooked shrimp to the skillet, tossing them in the luscious lemon butter sauce. Add the cooked pasta to the skillet as well, gently tossing everything together until the pasta is well coated with the sauce and the shrimp are evenly distributed.

  5. Serve and Garnish: Once everything is heated through, transfer the Lemon Butter Shrimp Pasta to a serving dish. Garnish with a sprinkle of freshly chopped parsley for a pop of color and an extra burst of freshness.

Tips and Variations:

  • For an extra burst of freshness, add a handful of cherry tomatoes or baby spinach to the skillet when making the lemon butter sauce.
  • If you prefer a bit of heat, increase the amount of red pepper flakes or add a pinch of cayenne pepper to the sauce.
  • You can substitute the shrimp with scallops or even chunks of firm white fish for a delightful variation of this dish.
  • Sprinkle some grated Parmesan cheese over the pasta just before serving for a savory, cheesy finish.

Conclusion:

Lemon Butter Shrimp Pasta is a dish that effortlessly combines the brightness of citrus with the indulgence of butter, resulting in a luxurious and flavorful seafood pasta that’s perfect for any occasion. Whether you’re hosting a dinner party or simply want to elevate your weeknight meal, this recipe is a surefire way to delight your senses and impress your guests. So, grab your apron, gather your ingredients, and get ready to savor the delectable flavors of this tangy and buttery seafood pasta!
