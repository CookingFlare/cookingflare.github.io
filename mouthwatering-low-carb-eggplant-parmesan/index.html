Mouthwatering Low Carb Eggplant Parmesan


Eggplant Parmesan is a classic Italian dish that is loved by many. However, traditional recipes often involve breading and frying the eggplant, which can make it high in carbs and calories. If you’re looking for a healthier, low carb version of this delicious dish, then you’re in luck! This mouthwatering low carb eggplant Parmesan recipe will satisfy your cravings without compromising your dietary goals.

Ingredients

  • 2 medium-sized eggplants
  • 1 cup marinara sauce
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/4 cup fresh basil leaves, torn
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C).

  2. Slice the eggplants into 1/4-inch thick rounds. Sprinkle salt on both sides of the slices and let them sit for about 10 minutes. This helps to remove any bitterness from the eggplant. After 10 minutes, rinse the slices under cold water and pat them dry with a paper towel.

  3. Heat the olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Add the minced garlic and sauté for about 1 minute, until fragrant. Be careful not to burn the garlic.

  4. Add the eggplant slices to the skillet in a single layer. Cook for about 2 minutes on each side, until they are lightly browned. You may need to do this in batches, depending on the size of your skillet.

  5. Remove the eggplant slices from the skillet and set them aside.

  6. In a baking dish, spread a thin layer of marinara sauce on the bottom. Place a single layer of eggplant slices on top of the sauce. Sprinkle some mozzarella cheese and grated Parmesan cheese over the eggplant.

  7. Repeat the layers until all the eggplant slices are used, finishing with a layer of cheese on top.

  8. Bake in the preheated oven for about 20 minutes, or until the cheese is melted and bubbly.

  9. Remove from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes. Garnish with torn basil leaves before serving.

Serving Suggestions

This mouthwatering low carb eggplant Parmesan is delicious on its own, but you can also serve it with a side of zucchini noodles or a fresh green salad for a complete meal. The flavors of the marinara sauce, melted cheese, and tender eggplant combine beautifully to create a satisfying dish that will leave you craving for more.

Tips and Variations

  • If you’re short on time, you can skip the step of salting the eggplant slices. However, salting helps to remove excess moisture and bitterness from the eggplant, resulting in a better texture and taste.

  • Feel free to experiment with different cheese combinations. You can try using a combination of mozzarella, cheddar, and Parmesan for a more flavorful dish.

  • Add some spice to your low carb eggplant Parmesan by sprinkling red pepper flakes on top before baking. This will give it a little kick and enhance the overall flavor.

  • If you’re not a fan of marinara sauce, you can substitute it with a homemade tomato sauce or even a pesto sauce for a different twist.

  • Leftovers can be stored in an airtight container in the refrigerator for up to 3 days. Reheat in the oven or microwave before serving.

Enjoy this mouthwatering low carb eggplant Parmesan as a comforting and satisfying meal that won’t derail your low carb lifestyle. It’s a healthier alternative to the traditional version, without compromising on taste. Give it a try and let your taste buds thank you for this delicious creation!

Note: This recipe serves approximately 4 people.
