Quick and Easy Buffalo Chicken Sliders: A Spicy Snack Idea


If you’re a fan of buffalo chicken and looking for a quick and easy snack idea, then you’re in luck! These Buffalo Chicken Sliders are the perfect combination of spicy, tangy, and savory flavors, all packed into one delicious bite. Whether you’re hosting a game night, having friends over, or simply craving a spicy treat, these sliders are sure to hit the spot.

Ingredients

To make these mouthwatering Buffalo Chicken Sliders, you’ll need the following ingredients:

  • 1 pound of boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1/2 cup of hot sauce (such as Frank’s RedHot)
  • 1/4 cup of melted butter
  • 1/4 cup of ranch dressing
  • 1/2 teaspoon of garlic powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon of onion powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 12 slider buns
  • Optional toppings: lettuce, tomato, blue cheese crumbles

Instructions

  1. Start by preheating your oven to 375°F (190°C).

  2. In a bowl, combine the hot sauce, melted butter, ranch dressing, garlic powder, onion powder, salt, and pepper. Mix well to create a flavorful buffalo sauce.

  3. Next, place the chicken breasts in a baking dish and pour the buffalo sauce over them, making sure to coat them evenly.

  4. Cover the dish with aluminum foil and bake in the preheated oven for 25-30 minutes, or until the chicken is cooked through and tender.

  5. Once the chicken is cooked, remove it from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes.

  6. Using two forks, shred the chicken into small, bite-sized pieces.

  7. Now it’s time to assemble the sliders! Take each slider bun and spread a dollop of ranch dressing on the bottom half.

  8. Place a generous portion of the shredded buffalo chicken on top of the ranch dressing, and add any optional toppings you desire, such as lettuce, tomato, or blue cheese crumbles.

  9. Finally, place the top half of the slider bun on the chicken and toppings, pressing gently to hold everything together.

  10. Repeat this process for the remaining slider buns and chicken.

  11. Serve the Buffalo Chicken Sliders immediately and enjoy the flavorful, spicy goodness!

Tips and Variations

  • If you prefer a milder version of these sliders, you can adjust the amount of hot sauce to your liking. Feel free to add more or less depending on your spice tolerance.

  • For an extra kick of flavor, you can sprinkle some blue cheese crumbles on top of the shredded buffalo chicken before assembling the sliders.

  • If you’re short on time, you can use pre-cooked or rotisserie chicken instead of baking the chicken breasts. Simply shred the cooked chicken and toss it in the buffalo sauce before assembling the sliders.

  • These Buffalo Chicken Sliders make for a great party appetizer or game day snack. You can easily double or triple the recipe to feed a crowd.

So there you have it – a quick and easy Buffalo Chicken Sliders recipe that is sure to satisfy your spicy cravings. With just a handful of ingredients and minimal prep time, you can enjoy these flavorful sliders in no time. Whether you’re hosting a gathering or simply treating yourself to a tasty snack, these Buffalo Chicken Sliders are a winner!
