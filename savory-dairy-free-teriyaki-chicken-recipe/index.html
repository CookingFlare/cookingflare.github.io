Savory Dairy-Free Teriyaki Chicken Recipe


If you’re craving a delicious and flavorful chicken dish that’s dairy-free, then this Savory Teriyaki Chicken recipe is perfect for you. Packed with savory and sweet flavors, this recipe is easy to make and is sure to become a favorite in your household. Whether you’re hosting a dinner party or simply want to treat yourself to a delightful meal, this dairy-free teriyaki chicken is a great choice.

Ingredients

  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1/2 cup soy sauce
  • 1/4 cup water
  • 3 tablespoons honey
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon fresh ginger, grated
  • 2 tablespoons rice vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon cornstarch
  • 1 tablespoon water
  • Sesame seeds and green onions for garnish

Instructions

  1. Begin by preparing the teriyaki sauce. In a small saucepan, combine the soy sauce, 1/4 cup of water, honey, minced garlic, grated ginger, and rice vinegar. Heat the mixture over medium heat, stirring occasionally, until it comes to a simmer. In a separate small bowl, dissolve the cornstarch in 1 tablespoon of water to create a slurry. Once the sauce in the pan is simmering, add the cornstarch slurry to the sauce, stirring constantly. Continue to simmer the sauce for 2-3 minutes, or until it has thickened. Set the teriyaki sauce aside.

  2. Next, prepare the chicken breasts by seasoning them with salt and pepper. In a large skillet over medium-high heat, add a tablespoon of oil. Once the oil is hot, add the chicken breasts to the skillet and cook for 6-7 minutes on each side, or until they are golden brown and fully cooked through.

  3. Once the chicken is cooked, pour the prepared teriyaki sauce over the chicken in the skillet. Allow the chicken to simmer in the sauce for a few minutes, turning the chicken to ensure it is evenly coated with the sauce.

  4. Once the chicken is coated and the sauce has thickened slightly, remove the skillet from the heat. Allow the chicken to rest in the sauce for a couple of minutes.

  5. To serve, slice the teriyaki chicken and arrange it on a serving platter. Drizzle any remaining teriyaki sauce from the skillet over the chicken. Garnish with sesame seeds and chopped green onions for added flavor and visual appeal.

Tips and Serving Suggestions

  • This dairy-free teriyaki chicken pairs beautifully with steamed white rice or quinoa. The flavorful teriyaki sauce is perfect for drizzling over the rice, creating a delightful combination of flavors.
  • For a complete meal, consider serving the teriyaki chicken alongside steamed or stir-fried vegetables. The combination of savory chicken and vibrant vegetables creates a well-balanced and satisfying dish.
  • Leftover teriyaki chicken can be stored in an airtight container in the refrigerator for up to 3 days. Simply reheat the chicken in the microwave or in a skillet until warmed through.

Enjoy this scrumptious Savory Dairy-Free Teriyaki Chicken with your friends and family, and savor the delightful blend of flavors in every bite. Whether you’re a fan of Asian-inspired cuisine or simply appreciate a delicious and wholesome meal, this recipe is sure to impress.
