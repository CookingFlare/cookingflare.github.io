Cut the chicken breasts into bite-sized pieces, approximately 1-inch in size. In a shallow bowl, combine the breadcrumbs, grated Parmesan cheese, garlic powder, paprika, salt, and black pepper. Mix well. Dip each piece of chicken into the beaten eggs, making sure to coat it completely. Next, roll the chicken in the breadcrumb mixture, pressing gently to ensure the breadcrumbs adhere to the chicken. Place the coated chicken nuggets onto the prepared baking sheet, making sure to leave space between each nugget. Spray the tops of the nuggets lightly with cooking spray. This will help them achieve a crispy texture. Bake the chicken nuggets in the preheated oven for 15-20 minutes, or until they are golden brown and cooked through. Homemade Guacamole: Simple Recipe for Kids

Welcome to our easy and delicious homemade guacamole recipe! Guacamole is a versatile and healthy dip that is loved by both kids and adults. It’s perfect for parties, picnics, or simply as a tasty snack. In this recipe, we will guide you through the process of making guacamole from scratch. So, let’s dive right in! Ingredients To make this simple guacamole recipe, you will need the following ingredients: 2 ripe avocados 1 small onion, finely diced 1 tomato, diced 1 garlic clove, minced Juice of 1 lime Salt and pepper to taste Instructions Cut the avocados in half and remove the pits. Scoop out the flesh into a medium-sized bowl. Using a fork, mash the avocados until they reach your desired consistency. Some people prefer chunky guacamole, while others like it smoother. Add the finely diced onion, diced tomato, minced garlic, and lime juice to the bowl with the mashed avocados. Gently mix all the ingredients together until well combined. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Remember, you can always add more seasoning later, so start with a little and adjust to your preference. Tips and Variations If you want to add some heat to your guacamole, you can include a finely chopped jalapeno or a dash of hot sauce. Just be sure to adjust the amount according to your spice tolerance. To add extra flavor, consider mixing in some chopped cilantro or a sprinkle of cumin. Guacamole is best enjoyed fresh, but if you need to store it, cover it tightly with plastic wrap, ensuring the wrap touches the surface of the guacamole to prevent browning. It can be kept in the refrigerator for up to two days. Serving Suggestions Guacamole is incredibly versatile and can be enjoyed in various ways. Here are some serving suggestions: ...
