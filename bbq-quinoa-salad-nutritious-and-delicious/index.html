BBQ Quinoa Salad: Nutritious and Delicious


Quinoa, a nutritious pseudo-cereal native to the Andean region, has quickly gained global popularity for its high protein and dietary fiber content. It’s no wonder that cooked quinoa is a staple in many modern dishes – perfect for vegetarians and non-vegetarians alike. In this blog post, we’ll learn how to create a delicious BBQ quinoa salad perfect for those who want to enjoy a tasty lunch or dinner while keeping their health goals in mind.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup quinoa, rinsed
  • 2 cups water
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 medium bell pepper, diced
  • 1 medium red onion, diced
  • 1 medium cucumber, diced
  • 1 cup fresh corn kernels
  • 1/4 cup freshly chopped cilantro
  • 3 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon liquid smoke (optional)
  • 1 tablespoon apple cider vinegar
  • 1 teaspoon smoked paprika

Instructions

  1. Prepare the quinoa: In a medium saucepan, combine rinsed quinoa with water and 1/2 teaspoon salt. Bring to a boil over medium-high heat.
  2. Once boiling, reduce the temperature to low heat and cover the saucepan. Let it simmer for about 15 to 20 minutes, until all the water has been absorbed, and the quinoa has reached a tender texture.
  3. Meanwhile, prepare the vegetables: Dice the bell pepper, red onion, and cucumber. Remove any corn kernels from the cob or use frozen corn for ease.
  4. In a separate bowl, whisk together olive oil, liquid smoke (if using), apple cider vinegar, and smoked paprika until a smooth marinade is achieved.
  5. Drain the cooked quinoa and transfer it to a large bowl. Add the diced vegetables and cilantro, and mix well.
  6. Drizzle the prepared marinade over the quinoa and vegetable mixture. Toss thoroughly to coat all the ingredients evenly.
  7. Serve the BBQ quinoa salad hot, at room temperature, or cold – your choice! You can accompany it with your favorite protein or enjoy it as a standalone dish. This versatile and tasty salad is guaranteed to satisfy your taste buds and nourish your body.

Variations

Feel free to customize your BBQ quinoa salad as per your preferences or based on available seasonal produce! Consider adding grilled chicken, tofu, beans, or chickpeas for extra protein punch. Substitute the liquid smoke with a teaspoon of soy sauce for a more traditional marinade. You can also add a tablespoon of honey or maple syrup to create a sweetness balance to the smoky flavors.

Benefits of BBQ Quinoa Salad

Aside from the delectable taste, BBQ quinoa salad provides numerous benefits. This salad combines protein-rich quinoa with colorful vegetables, creating a wholesome meal packed with essential nutrients. Furthermore, quinoa boasts important minerals like magnesium, phosphorus, and folate and is naturally gluten-free.

Final Thoughts

Incorporating BBQ quinoa salad into your weekly meal plans is a fantastic, healthy choice. The balanced blend of flavors and textures, along with its versatility, ensures that this dish will quickly become a go-to meal that both your taste buds and your body will thank you for. Enjoy this delicious, nutrient-dense salad and take small steps toward achieving and maintaining optimal health.
