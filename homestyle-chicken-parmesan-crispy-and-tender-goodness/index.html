Homestyle Chicken Parmesan: Crispy and Tender Goodness


If you’re looking for a classic Italian dish that combines the irresistible flavors of crispy breaded chicken and tangy marinara sauce, then look no further than Homestyle Chicken Parmesan. This recipe is the epitome of comfort food, with its crispy exterior, tender chicken, and gooey melted cheese. Whether you’re cooking for a family dinner or hosting a dinner party, this dish is sure to impress. So grab your apron and let’s get cooking!

Ingredients

  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 eggs, beaten
  • 2 cups breadcrumbs
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 cup marinara sauce
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Fresh basil leaves for garnish

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Place the chicken breasts between two sheets of plastic wrap and pound them to an even thickness using a meat mallet or the bottom of a heavy pan. Season the chicken breasts with salt and pepper.
  3. Set up a breading station by placing the flour, beaten eggs, and breadcrumbs in separate shallow dishes.
  4. In a mixing bowl, combine the breadcrumbs, grated Parmesan cheese, dried oregano, dried basil, garlic powder, salt, and pepper. Mix well to ensure the seasonings are evenly distributed.
  5. Dredge each chicken breast in flour, shaking off any excess. Dip the floured chicken breast into the beaten eggs, allowing any excess to drip off. Finally, coat the chicken in the breadcrumb mixture, pressing gently to ensure the breadcrumbs adhere.
  6. Heat a large skillet over medium-high heat and add enough oil to coat the bottom of the pan. Once the oil is hot, carefully add the breaded chicken breasts. Cook for 3-4 minutes on each side or until golden brown and crispy. Transfer the chicken to a baking dish.
  7. Spoon the marinara sauce over each chicken breast, spreading it evenly. Top with the shredded mozzarella cheese.
  8. Bake the chicken in the preheated oven for 20-25 minutes or until the cheese is melted and bubbly, and the chicken is cooked through.
  9. Remove the chicken from the oven and let it rest for a few minutes. Garnish with fresh basil leaves before serving.
  10. Serve the Homestyle Chicken Parmesan alongside your favorite pasta, such as spaghetti or fettuccine, and enjoy!

Tips and Variations

  • For an extra crispy coating, you can double dip the chicken breasts in the egg and breadcrumb mixture.
  • If you prefer a lighter version, you can bake the breaded chicken instead of frying it. Simply place the breaded chicken on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper and bake in a preheated 400°F (200°C) oven for about 20-25 minutes or until cooked through.
  • Feel free to customize the marinara sauce by adding your favorite herbs and spices, such as fresh basil, oregano, or red pepper flakes.
  • For a vegetarian twist, you can substitute the chicken breasts with eggplant slices or tofu and follow the same breading and baking instructions.
  • Serve the Homestyle Chicken Parmesan with a side of garlic bread or a fresh green salad for a complete and satisfying meal.

There you have it - a delicious and comforting recipe for Homestyle Chicken Parmesan. With its crispy breaded exterior, tender chicken, and gooey melted cheese, this dish is a crowd-pleaser that will leave everyone wanting more. So go ahead and give it a try in your own kitchen. Buon appetito!
