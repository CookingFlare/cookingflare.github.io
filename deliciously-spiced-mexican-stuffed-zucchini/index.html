Deliciously Spiced Mexican Stuffed Zucchini


If you’re looking for a flavorful and healthy dish that’s sure to impress, then look no further than these deliciously spiced Mexican stuffed zucchinis. Packed with vibrant flavors and wholesome ingredients, this recipe is a perfect way to elevate your weeknight dinner or impress your guests at a dinner party. Let’s dive into the recipe and discover how to create this mouthwatering dish.

Ingredients

  • 4 medium zucchinis
  • 1 pound ground beef or turkey
  • 1 cup cooked quinoa
  • 1 can black beans, drained and rinsed
  • 1 cup corn kernels
  • 1 red bell pepper, diced
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon chili powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon smoked paprika
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 cup shredded cheese (optional)
  • Fresh cilantro for garnish

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Zucchinis: Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C). Cut the zucchinis in half lengthwise and scoop out the seeds to create a hollow center. Place the zucchini halves on a baking sheet and set aside.

  2. Cook the Filling: In a large skillet, brown the ground beef or turkey over medium heat. Add the diced onion, minced garlic, and red bell pepper, and cook until the vegetables are tender. Stir in the cooked quinoa, black beans, and corn. Season the mixture with ground cumin, chili powder, smoked paprika, salt, and pepper. Allow the filling to simmer for a few minutes to let the flavors meld together.

  3. Stuff the Zucchinis: Spoon the filling generously into each hollowed-out zucchini half, mounding it on top. If desired, sprinkle shredded cheese over the top of each stuffed zucchini for an extra cheesy touch.

  4. Bake to Perfection: Cover the baking sheet with foil and bake the stuffed zucchinis in the preheated oven for 25-30 minutes, or until the zucchinis are tender and the filling is heated through. If you added cheese, you can remove the foil during the last 5 minutes of baking to allow the cheese to melt and bubble.

  5. Serve and Enjoy: Once the zucchinis are cooked to perfection, remove them from the oven and let them cool for a few minutes. Garnish with fresh cilantro and serve the Mexican stuffed zucchinis hot, with a side of salsa, guacamole, or a dollop of sour cream.

Final Thoughts

These deliciously spiced Mexican stuffed zucchinis are a delightful fusion of flavors and textures. Whether you’re a fan of Mexican cuisine or simply looking for a creative way to enjoy zucchinis, this recipe is a winner. The combination of tender zucchini, hearty filling, and aromatic spices makes for a satisfying and wholesome meal that’s perfect for any occasion.

So, why not add a dash of spice to your dinner routine and give these Mexican stuffed zucchinis a try? Whether you’re cooking for your family or hosting a gathering with friends, this recipe is sure to impress and satisfy even the most discerning palates. Enjoy the vibrant flavors of Mexico in every bite, and savor the joy of creating a memorable dish that celebrates fresh and wholesome ingredients.
