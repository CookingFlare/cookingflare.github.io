Pumpkin Spice Muffins with a Caramel Glaze Recipe: A Fall-Inspired Vegan Treat


As the leaves begin to turn and the air becomes crisp, it’s the perfect time to indulge in the warm, comforting flavors of fall. What better way to celebrate the season than with a batch of homemade Pumpkin Spice Muffins with a luscious Caramel Glaze? Not only are these muffins vegan-friendly, but they’re also bursting with the aromatic spices and cozy sweetness that we all love during autumn.

Ingredients

For the Pumpkin Spice Muffins:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons ground cinnamon
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground nutmeg
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground ginger
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground cloves
  • 1 cup canned pumpkin puree
  • 3/4 cup brown sugar
  • 1/2 cup unsweetened almond milk
  • 1/3 cup coconut oil, melted
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

For the Caramel Glaze:

  • 1/2 cup coconut cream
  • 1/2 cup brown sugar
  • 1/4 cup coconut oil
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • Pinch of salt

Instructions

For the Pumpkin Spice Muffins:

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C) and line a muffin tin with paper liners or lightly grease the cups.
  2. In a large bowl, whisk together the flour, baking powder, baking soda, salt, and spices.
  3. In another bowl, combine the pumpkin puree, brown sugar, almond milk, melted coconut oil, and vanilla extract.
  4. Gradually add the wet ingredients to the dry ingredients, stirring until just combined. Be careful not to overmix.
  5. Spoon the batter into the prepared muffin tin, filling each cup about 3/4 full.
  6. Bake for 20-25 minutes or until a toothpick inserted into the center of a muffin comes out clean.
  7. Allow the muffins to cool in the tin for a few minutes before transferring them to a wire rack to cool completely.

For the Caramel Glaze:

  1. In a small saucepan, combine the coconut cream, brown sugar, and coconut oil.
  2. Cook over medium heat, stirring constantly, until the sugar has dissolved and the mixture is smooth.
  3. Remove the saucepan from the heat and stir in the vanilla extract and a pinch of salt.
  4. Let the glaze cool for a few minutes, then drizzle it over the cooled muffins.

Serving and Storage

These Pumpkin Spice Muffins are best enjoyed warm, with a steaming cup of your favorite coffee or tea. The rich, spiced aroma and decadent caramel glaze make them a delightful indulgence on a chilly autumn morning or as an afternoon pick-me-up.

To store any leftovers, place the muffins in an airtight container at room temperature for up to 3 days. For longer storage, you can freeze the muffins (without the glaze) for up to 2 months. Simply thaw at room temperature or reheat in the microwave before serving.

Final Thoughts

Whether you’re a dedicated vegan or simply looking to explore plant-based baking, these Pumpkin Spice Muffins with a Caramel Glaze are a delightful way to savor the flavors of fall. The blend of aromatic spices, creamy pumpkin, and indulgent caramel creates a treat that’s sure to become a seasonal favorite in your home. So, roll up your sleeves, preheat that oven, and let the sweet scents of autumn fill your kitchen with this irresistible recipe!
