How to Make a Flatbread Pizza with Pesto and Chicken


When it comes to creating a delicious and easy-to-make dinner, few dishes hit the mark like a flatbread pizza with pesto and chicken. The combination of fresh, herbaceous pesto, savory chicken, and the crispy, yet tender texture of flatbread makes for a perfect balance of flavors and textures. Whether you are hosting a gathering or looking to treat yourself to a quick and satisfying meal, this flatbread pizza recipe is sure to impress.

In this post, we will walk you through the steps to make this simple yet mouthwatering flatbread pizza, from preparing the ingredients to baking it to perfection. With just a few key ingredients, you can create a dish that rivals any pizzeria but in the comfort of your own kitchen.

Ingredients You’ll Need

Before you get started, make sure you have the following ingredients on hand:

For the Pesto:

  • 2 cups fresh basil leaves: Fresh basil gives the pesto its signature herbal flavor.
  • 2 cloves garlic: Garlic adds a punch of flavor to the pesto, complementing the basil perfectly.
  • 1/4 cup pine nuts: These add a rich, buttery flavor to the pesto. If you can’t find pine nuts, you can substitute them with walnuts or almonds.
  • 1/2 cup Parmesan cheese: This brings a savory, salty depth to the pesto.
  • 1/2 cup extra virgin olive oil: This binds everything together and gives the pesto its smooth consistency.
  • Salt and pepper to taste: These will help balance the flavors.

For the Pizza:

  • 4 pieces of flatbread: Choose flatbread that is thin and slightly crispy for the best texture. You can buy pre-made flatbreads or make your own.
  • 2 cups cooked chicken, shredded: Grilled or roasted chicken works best. Shredded chicken makes it easier to spread across the pizza evenly.
  • 1/2 cup shredded mozzarella cheese: Mozzarella melts beautifully and adds a creamy texture to the pizza.
  • 1/4 cup crumbled goat cheese: Goat cheese adds a tangy flavor that pairs perfectly with the pesto.
  • Fresh arugula: Arugula adds a fresh, peppery crunch that complements the richness of the pesto and chicken.
  • Balsamic glaze (optional): This sweet and tangy glaze adds a finishing touch that elevates the flavors of the pizza.

How to Make the Pesto

Step 1: Prepare the Ingredients

Begin by washing and drying the basil leaves thoroughly. If you’re using whole garlic cloves, peel them. For the nuts, toast them lightly in a dry pan over medium heat for about 2-3 minutes to bring out their flavor. Be careful not to burn them.

Step 2: Blend the Pesto

In a food processor, add the basil leaves, garlic cloves, pine nuts, and Parmesan cheese. Pulse a few times until everything is chopped into small pieces. With the food processor running, slowly drizzle in the olive oil until the pesto reaches a smooth, creamy consistency. Taste the pesto and adjust the seasoning with salt and pepper.

Once your pesto is ready, set it aside. You can make it ahead of time and store it in an airtight container in the fridge for up to a week.

How to Prepare the Flatbread Pizza

Step 1: Preheat Your Oven

Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C). This temperature ensures the flatbread crisps up nicely while melting the cheese and heating the chicken.

Step 2: Prepare the Flatbread

Place the flatbreads on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper or lightly greased with olive oil. If you’re using store-bought flatbreads, make sure they’re spread out evenly on the sheet to ensure they cook uniformly.

Step 3: Spread the Pesto

Using a spoon or a brush, generously spread a layer of pesto over the top of each flatbread. Be sure to cover the entire surface but leave a small border around the edges to form a crust.

Step 4: Add the Chicken

Take your cooked chicken (preferably shredded or chopped into small pieces) and scatter it evenly over the pesto-covered flatbreads. The chicken can be grilled, roasted, or even leftover from a previous meal, making this a great way to repurpose food.

Step 5: Sprinkle the Cheese

Add the shredded mozzarella cheese over the chicken and pesto. Be sure to distribute it evenly so that each bite will be cheesy and delicious. Then, crumble the goat cheese on top of the mozzarella. The creamy goat cheese will add a tangy flavor that balances out the richness of the pesto and chicken.

Step 6: Bake the Pizza

Place the flatbreads in the preheated oven and bake for about 8-10 minutes, or until the edges of the flatbread are golden and crispy, and the cheese has melted and started to bubble. Keep an eye on the pizzas to prevent them from burning, as flatbreads cook faster than traditional pizza dough.

Adding Finishing Touches

Step 1: Garnish with Fresh Arugula

Once the flatbread pizzas are out of the oven, top them with a handful of fresh arugula. The peppery, slightly bitter flavor of the arugula adds a delightful contrast to the richness of the pesto, cheese, and chicken. It also brings a fresh, green element to the dish, lightening up the flavors.

Step 2: Drizzle with Balsamic Glaze (Optional)

For an extra layer of flavor, drizzle some balsamic glaze over the pizzas just before serving. The sweetness of the glaze contrasts beautifully with the savory flavors of the chicken and pesto, giving the pizza a gourmet touch.

Step 3: Slice and Serve

Using a sharp knife or pizza cutter, slice your flatbread pizzas into wedges. Serve immediately, and enjoy the crispy, cheesy, pesto-topped goodness. These pizzas are perfect for a casual weeknight dinner, a party appetizer, or even a light lunch.

Variations to Try

While this recipe is already a crowd-pleaser, you can customize it to suit your taste preferences or dietary needs. Here are a few variations you can try:

  • Add Veggies: If you’re looking to add more vegetables, try adding thin slices of red onion, roasted bell peppers, or cherry tomatoes on top of the pesto before baking. These vegetables complement the pesto’s flavor and add color to your pizza.

  • Swap the Chicken: If you’re a fan of other proteins, you can easily swap the chicken for grilled shrimp, turkey, or even a plant-based alternative like chickpeas or tofu. These substitutions work beautifully with pesto and add variety to the recipe.

  • Use Different Cheeses: While mozzarella and goat cheese are perfect in this recipe, you can experiment with other cheeses like fontina, ricotta, or feta to bring new flavors to the table.

  • Make It Spicy: Add a pinch of red pepper flakes to the pesto for an extra kick of heat. This is a great option for those who love a little spice with their pizza.

Tips for the Best Flatbread Pizza

  1. Use High-Quality Ingredients: The simplicity of this recipe means that the quality of the ingredients really shines through. Use fresh basil, high-quality Parmesan cheese, and extra virgin olive oil for the best flavor.

  2. Preheat Your Oven: Make sure your oven is fully preheated to ensure the flatbreads cook evenly and become crispy. This is particularly important when working with flatbreads, as they cook quickly.

  3. Don’t Overload with Toppings: While it’s tempting to add lots of toppings, be careful not to overload the flatbreads. Too many toppings can make the pizza soggy and prevent the flatbread from crisping up properly.

  4. Serve Immediately: Flatbread pizzas are best enjoyed fresh out of the oven when the cheese is melted, and the crust is crispy. If you let them sit too long, the flatbread may lose its crunch.

Why You’ll Love This Flatbread Pizza with Pesto and Chicken

This flatbread pizza with pesto and chicken is a versatile and quick meal that doesn’t compromise on flavor. It’s easy to make, requires minimal prep, and can be customized to your liking. The pesto offers a burst of fresh, herbaceous flavor, while the chicken adds protein and substance. The combination of mozzarella and goat cheese creates a creamy, savory base, and the arugula adds the perfect touch of freshness.

Whether you’re looking to impress guests with a gourmet appetizer or whip up a quick dinner after a long day, this flatbread pizza recipe will be a go-to in your meal rotation. With the balance of flavors and textures, it’s sure to become a family favorite!

So, the next time you’re craving something light yet satisfying, grab some flatbread and pesto, and enjoy the savory goodness of this chicken pesto flatbread pizza. Your taste buds will thank you!
