7-Minute Spicy Shrimp Ceviche: A Refreshing Appetizer for Summer


When the summer heat is in full swing, there’s nothing quite like a refreshing and zesty appetizer to kick off a meal. Enter the 7-Minute Spicy Shrimp Ceviche, a dish that not only bursts with flavor but also comes together in a flash. Whether you’re hosting a backyard barbecue, a poolside gathering, or simply craving a taste of the coast, this ceviche is the perfect choice. Let’s dive into this quick and easy recipe that will elevate your summer dining experience.

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound of fresh shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 1 cup of fresh lime juice
  • 1 small red onion, finely chopped
  • 1-2 jalapeño peppers, seeded and finely chopped
  • 1 cup of cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1/2 cup of fresh cilantro, chopped
  • 1 avocado, diced
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Tortilla chips or plantain chips for serving

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the Shrimp: In a pot of boiling water, cook the shrimp for just 2-3 minutes until they turn pink and opaque. Be careful not to overcook them, as the texture is key to a perfect ceviche. Once cooked, immediately transfer the shrimp to a bowl of ice water to stop the cooking process. Once cooled, drain the shrimp and chop them into bite-sized pieces.

  2. Marinate the Shrimp: In a non-reactive bowl, combine the chopped shrimp with the fresh lime juice. The acidity of the lime juice will “cook” the shrimp, giving them a tender and flavorful texture. Let the shrimp marinate in the lime juice for about 5 minutes while you prepare the other ingredients.

  3. Mix the Ingredients: After the shrimp has marinated, add the finely chopped red onion, jalapeño peppers, cherry tomatoes, and chopped cilantro to the bowl. Gently toss the ingredients together until the shrimp and vegetables are well combined.

  4. Add the Avocado: Carefully fold in the diced avocado, being mindful not to mash it. The creamy texture of the avocado will complement the tangy and spicy flavors of the ceviche, adding a delightful contrast to each bite.

  5. Season to Perfection: Season the ceviche with salt and pepper to taste, adjusting the seasoning as needed. The balance of flavors is crucial, so don’t skimp on this step.

  6. Chill and Serve: Cover the bowl of ceviche with plastic wrap and refrigerate it for at least 30 minutes to allow the flavors to meld together. When ready to serve, give the ceviche a final stir and dish it into individual serving bowls. Pair it with your favorite tortilla chips or crispy plantain chips for an extra crunch.

Why This Recipe Works:

  • Quick and Easy: This recipe takes just 7 minutes to prepare, making it an ideal choice for busy summer days or last-minute gatherings.
  • Vibrant Flavors: The combination of fresh lime juice, spicy jalapeños, and creamy avocado creates a burst of vibrant flavors that are perfect for summer dining.
  • Light and Refreshing: With no cooking required, this ceviche maintains a light and refreshing quality that’s perfect for hot weather.

In Conclusion:

The 7-Minute Spicy Shrimp Ceviche is a true summer delight that embodies the spirit of coastal dining. With its quick preparation and explosive flavors, it’s an appetizer that’s sure to impress your guests and leave them craving more. So, the next time you’re looking for a refreshing and effortless dish to elevate your summer gatherings, turn to this zesty and satisfying ceviche recipe. Cheers to a summer filled with unforgettable flavors and memorable moments!
