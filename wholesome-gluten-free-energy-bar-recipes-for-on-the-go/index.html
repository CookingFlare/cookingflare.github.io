Wholesome Gluten-Free Energy Bar Recipes for On-the-Go


Are you tired of grabbing unhealthy snacks when you’re on-the-go? Whether you’re heading to work, hitting the gym, or running errands, having a stash of wholesome energy bars can be a game-changer. Not only are they convenient, but they can also provide a nutritious boost to keep you going. And if you’re following a gluten-free diet, fear not! We’ve got some delicious and easy gluten-free energy bar recipes that will satisfy your cravings and fuel your adventures.

Almond Butter & Honey Energy Bars

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup gluten-free rolled oats
  • 1/2 cup almond butter
  • 1/4 cup raw honey
  • 1/2 cup chopped almonds
  • 1/4 cup unsweetened shredded coconut
  • 1/4 cup dried cranberries
  • 1/4 teaspoon sea salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C) and line an 8-inch square baking pan with parchment paper.
  2. In a large bowl, mix together the almond butter, honey, and vanilla extract until well combined.
  3. Add the oats, chopped almonds, shredded coconut, dried cranberries, and sea salt to the almond butter mixture. Stir until everything is evenly coated.
  4. Press the mixture firmly and evenly into the prepared baking pan.
  5. Bake for 12-15 minutes or until the edges turn golden brown.
  6. Allow the bars to cool completely in the pan before cutting into squares or bars.

These almond butter and honey energy bars are packed with protein, healthy fats, and natural sweetness, making them a perfect snack to keep you energized throughout the day.

Chocolate Quinoa Energy Bars

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup cooked quinoa
  • 1/2 cup almond butter
  • 1/4 cup pure maple syrup
  • 1/4 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1/4 cup chopped dark chocolate
  • 1/4 cup unsweetened shredded coconut
  • 1/4 cup pumpkin seeds
  • 1/4 teaspoon sea salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract

Instructions:

  1. In a large bowl, combine the cooked quinoa, almond butter, maple syrup, and vanilla extract until well mixed.
  2. Add the cocoa powder, chopped dark chocolate, shredded coconut, pumpkin seeds, and sea salt to the quinoa mixture. Mix until all ingredients are evenly distributed.
  3. Press the mixture firmly into a parchment-lined 8-inch square baking pan.
  4. Chill the bars in the refrigerator for at least 2 hours before cutting into squares or bars.

These chocolate quinoa energy bars are a delightful blend of rich cocoa, nutty quinoa, and crunchy seeds, providing a satisfying burst of energy with every bite.

Peanut Butter & Banana Energy Bars

Ingredients:

  • 2 ripe bananas, mashed
  • 1/2 cup natural peanut butter
  • 1/4 cup pure maple syrup
  • 1 cup gluten-free crispy rice cereal
  • 1/2 cup chopped peanuts
  • 1/4 cup unsweetened shredded coconut
  • 1/4 cup raisins
  • 1/4 teaspoon cinnamon
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract

Instructions:

  1. Line an 8-inch square baking pan with parchment paper.
  2. In a large bowl, mix together the mashed bananas, peanut butter, maple syrup, cinnamon, and vanilla extract until smooth.
  3. Add the crispy rice cereal, chopped peanuts, shredded coconut, and raisins to the banana mixture. Stir until well combined.
  4. Press the mixture evenly into the prepared baking pan.
  5. Refrigerate the bars for at least 2 hours before cutting into squares or bars.

These peanut butter and banana energy bars offer a delightful blend of flavors and textures, making them a tasty and nutritious snack for any time of day.

Whether you’re craving something nutty, chocolaty, or fruity, these homemade energy bar recipes are sure to keep you fueled and satisfied during your busiest days. Experiment with different ingredients and flavors to create your own customized energy bars that cater to your taste preferences and dietary needs. Enjoy the convenience of having these wholesome gluten-free treats on hand whenever hunger strikes, and say goodbye to unhealthy snacking on-the-go!
