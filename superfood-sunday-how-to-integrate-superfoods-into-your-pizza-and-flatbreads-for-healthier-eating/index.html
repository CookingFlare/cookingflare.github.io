Superfood Sunday: How to Integrate Superfoods into Your Pizza and Flatbreads for Healthier Eating


Are you tired of constantly arguing with yourself between a slice of heavenly pizza and your commitment to a healthier lifestyle? Why choose between the two when you can have your pizza and eat it too with this exciting and nutritious twist? Today, we’re diving into Superfood Sunday and sharing with you some magical ways to integrate the most remarkable superfoods into your beloved pizzas and flatbreads. Break free from the shackles of guilt and embrace these healthier versions of your favorite foods. Let’s continue exploring culinary creativity as we combine nutritious ingredients with rewarding flavors.

First up, let’s start with superfood-packed dough. Quinoa is not only a tasty protein-packed alternative to traditional wheat flour, which will give your dough a nutty flavor, but also increases the fiber content in your creation. To start, simply substitute one-third of the customary wheat flour in your recipe with quinoa flour. For an even more impressive protein punch, try adding a cup of cooked quinoa to your dough mixture as well - just make sure to let your dough rest for about 20 minutes to allow the quinoa’s water to soak up into the other flours before kneading.

We’ll never turn down dark leafy greens, especially kale, and now you don’t have to either. Kale pairs brilliantly with pizza, both in flavor and texture. To introduce these leafy powerhouses into your life, simply toss around a cup of kale, washed and finely chopped, with your other pizza toppings. The subtle earthy flavor of kale complements almost any other toppings, and its tender yet sturdy texture adds a nice contrast to soft cheese.

Another superfood essential is avocado, so don’t hesitate to swirl it lovingly onto your pizza dough for a creamy base prior to adding your toppings. Avocado brings not just a luscious texture to the table but also monounsaturated fats, fiber, vitamins, and a hint of potassium. And as if that’s not enough, the flavors from your other toppings will dance even more deliciously with the avocado base.

Don’t think that beets have no place in your flatbreads! Roasted beets have a sweet yet earthy flavor that will add an unmatched taste to your dough. With a single beet, you’re gaining a substantial amount of fiber, folate, and numerous antioxidants. To incorporate them evenly into the dough, puree cooked beets and spread them onto the rolled-out dough, then gently spread your flour mixture on top before finishing off the usual kneading process. The vibrant hue of the beets will be well camouflaged within the dough but guarantees an enchanting surprise when you cut into your flatbread.

Blueberries not only make your taste buds dance, but they’re also a great source of antioxidants to protect your body from cellular damage. Thankfully, they blend well when pureed so you can whip up a tantalizing and colorful spread for your pizza or flatbread. After you’ve separated the bottom dough from the edges, coat the flattened crust with a layer of pureed blueberries before drizzling it with a bit of honey for added sweetness. You’ve just upped the ante on an already unbeatable combination, ensuring a full-bodied burst of flavors in every bite.

Of course, let’s not forget about chia seeds - the go-to powerhouses with a totally good-looking gelatinous texture that seems to love holding things together. We recommend that you sprinkle a hefty handful of chia seeds on top of your pizza or flatbread just before baking to ensure that they remain crunchy and add that delightful nutty taste to your meal. Plus, you’re packing your food with minerals, fiber, and omega-3 fatty acids. Quite a deal, wouldn’t you say?

Finally, what ends a meal more beautifully than with a hint of dark chocolate? And why not integrate that loving chocolate touch into our culinary endeavors? Sprinkle a dusting of dark chocolate chips or shavings onto your flatbread before adding any other toppings to achieve a rich, antioxidant-filled dessert. The result is a delightful fusion of flavors that’ll leave you wanting more.

There you have it - flavorful and nutritious twists on your favorite pizzas and flatbreads! Now, it’s time for you to branch out into exciting new culinary territories and experience the myriad health benefits of such heavenly innovations. Happy cooking, everyone!
