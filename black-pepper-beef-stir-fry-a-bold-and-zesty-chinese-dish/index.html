Black Pepper Beef Stir Fry: A Bold and Zesty Chinese Dish


If you’re craving a flavorful and satisfying Chinese dish, look no further than Black Pepper Beef Stir Fry. This dish is a perfect balance of bold, zesty flavors and tender beef, making it a favorite among many Chinese food enthusiasts. The combination of savory black pepper, tender beef, and fresh vegetables creates a mouthwatering dish that is sure to impress your family and friends. In this blog post, we’ll guide you through the process of creating this delectable dish in the comfort of your own kitchen.

Ingredients

To create this Black Pepper Beef Stir Fry, you will need the following ingredients:

  • 1 pound of beef steak, thinly sliced
  • 1 tablespoon of soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon of oyster sauce
  • 2 tablespoons of cooking oil
  • 1 onion, sliced
  • 1 bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 cup of broccoli florets
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon of freshly ground black pepper
  • 1 teaspoon of cornstarch
  • 1/2 cup of beef broth
  • 1 tablespoon of hoisin sauce
  • 1 teaspoon of sesame oil
  • Cooked rice, for serving

Instructions

  1. Marinate the Beef: In a bowl, combine the thinly sliced beef with soy sauce and oyster sauce. Allow the beef to marinate for at least 15 minutes to infuse it with flavor.

  2. Prepare the Sauce: In a small bowl, mix the beef broth, hoisin sauce, cornstarch, and freshly ground black pepper to create a flavorful sauce for the stir fry.

  3. Heat the Wok: Heat the cooking oil in a wok or large skillet over high heat. Once the oil is hot, add the marinated beef and stir-fry for 2-3 minutes until it is cooked to your desired level of doneness. Remove the beef from the wok and set it aside.

  4. Stir-fry the Vegetables: In the same wok, add a bit more oil if needed and stir-fry the onion, bell pepper, broccoli, and minced garlic until they are tender-crisp.

  5. Combine the Ingredients: Return the cooked beef to the wok with the stir-fried vegetables. Pour the prepared sauce over the beef and vegetables, and stir-fry for an additional 1-2 minutes until the sauce thickens and coats the ingredients evenly.

  6. Finish and Serve: Drizzle the stir fry with sesame oil for a fragrant finish. Serve the Black Pepper Beef Stir Fry over steamed rice for a satisfying meal that will surely impress your taste buds.

Tips for Success

  • Slice the Beef Thinly: Slicing the beef thinly ensures that it cooks quickly and remains tender in the stir fry.
  • Prepare Ingredients in Advance: Having all your ingredients prepped and ready to go will make the cooking process smooth and efficient.
  • Adjust the Spice Level: If you prefer a spicier dish, feel free to add more freshly ground black pepper to suit your taste.

Enjoy the Bold Flavors of Black Pepper Beef Stir Fry

Black Pepper Beef Stir Fry is a delightful and aromatic dish that brings together the bold flavors of black pepper, tender beef, and crisp vegetables. It’s a versatile dish that can be enjoyed on its own or paired with steamed rice for a satisfying meal. Whether you’re cooking for yourself or entertaining guests, this Chinese favorite is sure to be a hit. So, roll up your sleeves, gather your ingredients, and get ready to savor the bold and zesty flavors of this classic Black Pepper Beef Stir Fry.
