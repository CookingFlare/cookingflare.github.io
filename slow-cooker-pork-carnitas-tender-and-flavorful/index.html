Slow Cooker Pork Carnitas: Tender and Flavorful


If you’re looking for a delicious and hassle-free meal that will impress your family and friends, then look no further than this amazing slow cooker pork carnitas recipe. The beauty of this dish lies in its simplicity and the mouthwatering flavors that develop as the pork slowly cooks to perfection. Whether you’re hosting a casual gathering or simply want to treat yourself to a comforting and satisfying meal, these slow cooker pork carnitas are sure to hit the spot.

Ingredients

  • 3 pounds pork shoulder, cut into chunks
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon pepper
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • 1 teaspoon oregano
  • 1 teaspoon chili powder
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 1 jalapeno, diced (seeds removed for less heat)
  • 1 orange, juiced
  • 1 lime, juiced
  • 1/2 cup fresh cilantro, chopped
  • 1/2 cup chicken broth
  • Corn tortillas, for serving
  • Optional toppings: diced onion, fresh cilantro, avocado, lime wedges

Instructions

  1. Prep the Pork: Begin by seasoning the pork shoulder with salt, pepper, cumin, oregano, and chili powder. Make sure the pork is well coated with the seasoning.

  2. Sear the Pork: In a large skillet over medium-high heat, add the olive oil. Once hot, add the seasoned pork and sear until golden brown on all sides. This step helps to lock in the flavor and create a nice crust on the pork.

  3. Prepare the Slow Cooker: Transfer the seared pork to the slow cooker. Add the minced garlic, diced onion, and jalapeno on top of the pork.

  4. Add Flavor: Squeeze the juice of the orange and lime over the pork. Then, add the fresh cilantro and chicken broth to the slow cooker. These ingredients will infuse the pork with a burst of citrusy and aromatic flavors.

  5. Slow Cook: Cover the slow cooker and cook the pork on low for 8-10 hours, or on high for 4-6 hours. The long, slow cooking process will transform the pork into tender, juicy, and flavorful carnitas.

  6. Shred the Pork: Once the pork is cooked to perfection, use two forks to shred it directly in the slow cooker. The meat should be so tender that it practically falls apart effortlessly.

  7. Crisp the Carnitas (Optional): For an added layer of texture and flavor, you can transfer the shredded pork to a baking sheet and broil it in the oven for a few minutes until the edges become beautifully crispy.

  8. Serve and Enjoy: Warm up some corn tortillas and fill them with the succulent slow cooker pork carnitas. Top with diced onion, fresh cilantro, and avocado for a burst of freshness. Don’t forget to squeeze some extra lime juice for a zesty kick!

Conclusion

There you have it – a sensational slow cooker pork carnitas recipe that will elevate your cooking game to new heights. The tender and flavorful pork paired with the vibrant citrus notes and aromatic spices make this dish a true crowd-pleaser. Whether you’re preparing a festive feast or a laid-back weeknight dinner, these slow cooker pork carnitas are guaranteed to leave everyone craving for more. So, grab your slow cooker, gather your ingredients, and get ready to savor the irresistible delights of homemade pork carnitas!
