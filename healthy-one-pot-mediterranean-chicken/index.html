Healthy One Pot Mediterranean Chicken


If you’re looking for a delicious and healthy meal that’s easy to prepare and requires minimal cleanup, then this Healthy One Pot Mediterranean Chicken recipe is perfect for you. Packed with vibrant flavors and wholesome ingredients, this dish is a delightful fusion of Mediterranean cuisine that will tantalize your taste buds and nourish your body. Let’s dive into the recipe and discover how to create this culinary masterpiece in just a single pot!

Ingredients

  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 red bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 yellow bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes
  • 1 cup Kalamata olives
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1/2 cup low-sodium chicken broth
  • 1/4 cup fresh parsley, chopped
  • Lemon wedges for serving

Instructions

  1. Start by seasoning the chicken breasts with salt, pepper, and paprika.

  2. In a large, deep skillet or pot, heat the olive oil over medium heat.

  3. Add the seasoned chicken breasts to the skillet and cook for 6-7 minutes on each side, or until they are golden brown and cooked through. Once done, transfer the chicken to a plate and set it aside.

  4. In the same skillet, add the diced onion and minced garlic. Sauté for 2-3 minutes until the onions are translucent and the garlic is fragrant.

  5. Next, add the sliced bell peppers, cherry tomatoes, and Kalamata olives to the skillet. Stir the ingredients together and let them cook for 4-5 minutes, allowing the flavors to meld.

  6. Sprinkle the dried oregano and thyme over the vegetable mixture, stirring to incorporate the herbs evenly.

  7. Pour the low-sodium chicken broth into the skillet, then return the cooked chicken breasts to the pot, nestling them into the vegetable mixture.

  8. Cover the skillet and let the dish simmer for 10-12 minutes, allowing the flavors to blend and the chicken to absorb the aromas of the Mediterranean-inspired ingredients.

  9. Once the chicken is tender and infused with the flavors of the dish, garnish with freshly chopped parsley and serve with lemon wedges on the side.

Nutritional Benefits

This Healthy One Pot Mediterranean Chicken recipe is not only a delight for your taste buds but also a nourishing choice for your well-being. The combination of lean protein from the chicken, heart-healthy fats from the olives and olive oil, and an array of vitamins and antioxidants from the vegetables makes this dish a nutritional powerhouse. Additionally, the Mediterranean-inspired ingredients offer a dose of anti-inflammatory properties and are known for their potential to promote heart health.

Final Thoughts

In conclusion, this Healthy One Pot Mediterranean Chicken recipe is a testament to the fact that healthy eating can be both flavorful and convenient. By bringing together the vibrant ingredients of the Mediterranean diet in a single pot, you can create a culinary masterpiece that elevates your dining experience while keeping your health in mind. Whether you’re cooking for a family dinner or meal-prepping for the week, this recipe is a versatile and wholesome option that will leave you feeling satisfied and nourished. So, roll up your sleeves, gather your ingredients, and embark on a culinary journey to the Mediterranean with this delightful one-pot chicken dish. Cheers to good health and great flavors!
