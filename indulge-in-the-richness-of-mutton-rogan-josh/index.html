Indulge in the Richness of Mutton Rogan Josh


Rogan Josh is a classic Kashmiri dish that has gained popularity not only in India but also worldwide. The rich flavors and aromatic spices make it a favorite among food enthusiasts. In this blog post, we will explore the indulgent experience of cooking and savoring Mutton Rogan Josh. So, let’s dive right in!

Ingredients

To make this flavorful dish, you will need the following ingredients:

  • 500 grams of mutton, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 2 onions, finely chopped
  • 3 tomatoes, pureed
  • 4 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1-inch piece of ginger, grated
  • 2 green chilies, slit lengthwise
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 2 teaspoons Kashmiri red chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon cumin powder
  • 1 teaspoon coriander powder
  • 1 teaspoon fennel seeds
  • 4 green cardamom pods
  • 2 black cardamom pods
  • 2 cinnamon sticks
  • 4 cloves
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 1 cup yogurt, whisked
  • 2 tablespoons ghee
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander leaves for garnish

Instructions

  1. Heat the ghee in a heavy-bottomed pan or a pressure cooker. Add the fennel seeds, green and black cardamom pods, cinnamon sticks, cloves, and bay leaf. Sauté for a minute until they release their aroma.

  2. Add the finely chopped onions and cook until they turn golden brown. This will take about 8-10 minutes. Stir occasionally to prevent burning.

  3. Add the minced garlic, grated ginger, and slit green chilies. Sauté for another 2 minutes until the raw smell disappears.

  4. Now, it’s time to add the spice powders - turmeric, Kashmiri red chili, cumin, and coriander. Mix well with the onion mixture and cook for a minute.

  5. Add the pureed tomatoes and cook until the oil starts to separate from the masala. This will take approximately 10 minutes.

  6. Add the mutton pieces to the masala and coat them well with the spices. Cook for 5-7 minutes on medium heat.

  7. Reduce the heat and add the whisked yogurt. Mix well to combine all the flavors. Cover the pan or pressure cooker and let it cook for about 30 minutes, or until the mutton is tender.

  8. Once the mutton is cooked, check for seasoning and add salt according to your taste. If needed, you can also add a little water to adjust the consistency of the gravy.

  9. Garnish with freshly chopped coriander leaves and serve hot with steamed rice, naan, or roti.

Conclusion

Mutton Rogan Josh is a dish that truly indulges your taste buds in the richness of flavors. The combination of perfectly cooked mutton, aromatic spices, and the creaminess of yogurt creates a delightful experience. Whether you are a fan of spicy food or prefer a milder version, this dish can be easily customized to suit your preferences.

Next time you crave something indulgent and packed with flavors, give Mutton Rogan Josh a try. Impress your family and friends with this authentic Kashmiri delicacy and let them savor the richness of this dish. Happy cooking!
