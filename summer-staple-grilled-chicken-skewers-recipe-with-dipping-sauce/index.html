Summer Staple: Grilled Chicken Skewers Recipe with Dipping Sauce


As the sun shines brighter and the days grow longer, there’s nothing quite like the taste of summer grilling. One dish that never fails to impress and satisfy is the classic grilled chicken skewer. Whether you’re hosting a backyard barbecue, enjoying a picnic at the park, or simply looking to whip up something delicious for dinner, grilled chicken skewers are the perfect choice. Today, we’ll explore how to create juicy, flavorful chicken skewers paired with a tantalizing dipping sauce that will elevate your summer gatherings to the next level.

Why Grilled Chicken Skewers?

Grilled chicken skewers are not just delicious; they’re also versatile and easy to prepare. You can customize the flavor profile to suit your taste preferences, experiment with various marinades, and incorporate a variety of vegetables. Plus, they cook quickly on the grill, making them a convenient option for busy summer days.

Choosing the Right Ingredients

To create the most flavorful grilled chicken skewers, selecting high-quality ingredients is essential. Here’s what you’ll need:

For the Chicken Skewers:

  • Chicken Breast or Thighs: Chicken thighs tend to be juicier and more flavorful, while breasts are leaner. Choose according to your preference.
  • Vegetables: Bell peppers, zucchini, cherry tomatoes, and red onions are excellent choices. They add color, flavor, and texture.
  • Marinade Ingredients: A combination of olive oil, garlic, lemon juice, soy sauce, and herbs will infuse the chicken with flavor.

For the Dipping Sauce:

  • Greek Yogurt or Sour Cream: A creamy base for the sauce.
  • Fresh Herbs: Dill, parsley, or cilantro add freshness.
  • Lemon Juice: For brightness and acidity.
  • Garlic: A touch of minced garlic for flavor.
  • Salt and Pepper: To taste.

The Marinade: Infusing Flavor

The key to succulent grilled chicken skewers lies in the marinade. Here’s a simple yet effective marinade recipe that will enhance the flavor of your chicken:

Ingredients for Marinade:

  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 3 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons lemon juice (freshly squeezed)
  • 3 cloves garlic (minced)
  • 1 tablespoon honey (optional for sweetness)
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the Marinade: In a mixing bowl, whisk together the olive oil, soy sauce, lemon juice, minced garlic, honey, oregano, salt, and pepper until well combined.

  2. Marinate the Chicken: Cut the chicken into bite-sized pieces and place them in a resealable plastic bag or a glass dish. Pour the marinade over the chicken, ensuring all the pieces are coated. Seal the bag or cover the dish, then refrigerate for at least 30 minutes, ideally for 2-4 hours. This allows the flavors to penetrate the chicken.

Preparing the Skewers

While the chicken marinates, you can prepare the vegetables. If using wooden skewers, soak them in water for at least 30 minutes to prevent burning on the grill.

Chopping the Vegetables:

  • Bell Peppers: Cut into 1-inch squares.
  • Zucchini: Slice into thick rounds.
  • Red Onion: Cut into wedges.
  • Cherry Tomatoes: Leave whole.

Assembling the Skewers

Once the chicken has marinated, it’s time to assemble your skewers. Alternate between chicken and vegetables for a colorful presentation. Here’s how to do it:

  1. Thread the Skewers: Begin with a piece of chicken, followed by a bell pepper, then chicken again, and repeat until the skewer is filled. Leave a little space between each piece for even cooking.

  2. Repeat: Continue assembling the remaining skewers until all the chicken and vegetables are used.

Grilling the Chicken Skewers

Now that your skewers are ready, it’s time to fire up the grill. Here’s how to achieve perfectly grilled chicken skewers:

Steps for Grilling:

  1. Preheat the Grill: Heat your grill to medium-high heat. If using a charcoal grill, ensure the coals are ashy and glowing.

  2. Oil the Grill Grates: Prevent sticking by oiling the grill grates with a paper towel dipped in oil.

  3. Grill the Skewers: Place the skewers on the grill in a single layer. Grill for about 10-12 minutes, turning occasionally, until the chicken is cooked through and reaches an internal temperature of 165°F (75°C). The vegetables should be tender and slightly charred.

  4. Remove from Grill: Once cooked, remove the skewers from the grill and let them rest for a few minutes before serving.

Creating the Dipping Sauce

While the skewers are grilling, prepare the dipping sauce. This creamy, herby sauce complements the grilled chicken perfectly.

Ingredients for Dipping Sauce:

  • 1 cup Greek yogurt or sour cream
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 1 tablespoon fresh dill (or herb of choice)
  • 1 clove garlic (minced)
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Mix the Ingredients: In a small bowl, combine the Greek yogurt or sour cream, lemon juice, dill, minced garlic, salt, and pepper.

  2. Taste and Adjust: Taste the sauce and adjust seasoning as needed. You can add more lemon juice for acidity or more herbs for flavor.

Serving Suggestions

Once your grilled chicken skewers are ready, it’s time to serve! Here are some ideas to make your summer meal even more delightful:

  • Garnish: Sprinkle some fresh herbs over the skewers for added color and flavor.
  • Side Dishes: Pair your skewers with a refreshing salad, grilled corn on the cob, or a light quinoa dish.
  • Share the Love: Serve the skewers on a large platter, allowing guests to help themselves. The dipping sauce can be served in a small bowl for easy access.

Tips for Perfect Grilled Chicken Skewers

  • Don’t Overcrowd the Skewers: Ensure there’s enough space between each piece for even cooking.
  • Use a Meat Thermometer: To ensure the chicken is fully cooked, use a meat thermometer to check the internal temperature.
  • Experiment with Flavors: Don’t hesitate to try different marinades or vegetables. The beauty of skewers is their versatility!

Storing Leftovers

If you have any leftovers, store them in an airtight container in the refrigerator for up to three days. Reheat in the oven or on the grill for the best results.

Conclusion

Grilled chicken skewers are a summer staple that brings joy to any gathering. Their vibrant flavors, ease of preparation, and ability to please a crowd make them a go-to recipe for any occasion. With a delicious dipping sauce to accompany them, these skewers are sure to become a favorite in your summer repertoire. So fire up that grill, gather your friends and family, and enjoy a delightful meal that captures the essence of summer!
