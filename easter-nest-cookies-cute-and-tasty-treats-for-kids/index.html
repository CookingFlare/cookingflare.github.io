Easter Nest Cookies: Cute and Tasty Treats for Kids


Easter is a time for joy, family, and delicious treats. If you’re looking for a fun and creative activity to do with your kids during the Easter season, making Easter nest cookies is a fantastic choice. These cookies are not only adorable but also scrumptiously tasty. In this blog post, we’ll walk you through a step-by-step guide on how to make these delightful treats that will surely bring smiles to everyone’s faces.

Ingredients

Before we dive into the process of making Easter nest cookies, let’s gather all the ingredients we’ll need for this delightful baking adventure. You’ll need:

  • 2 1/2 cups of all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon of baking soda
  • 1 teaspoon of salt
  • 1 cup of unsalted butter, softened
  • 3/4 cup of granulated sugar
  • 3/4 cup of packed brown sugar
  • 1 teaspoon of vanilla extract
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 cups of semi-sweet chocolate chips
  • 2 cups of chow mein noodles
  • Mini chocolate eggs or jelly beans for decorating

Instructions

Step 1: Preheat the Oven

Begin by preheating your oven to 375°F (190°C) and lining a baking sheet with parchment paper. This will ensure that your cookies bake evenly without sticking to the sheet.

Step 2: Prepare the Dry Ingredients

In a small bowl, whisk together the all-purpose flour, baking soda, and salt. Set this dry mixture aside for later use.

Step 3: Cream the Butter and Sugars

Using a stand mixer or a handheld mixer, cream together the softened unsalted butter, granulated sugar, and packed brown sugar until the mixture is light and fluffy. This will take about 2-3 minutes.

Step 4: Add the Vanilla and Eggs

Once the butter and sugars are creamed, add in the vanilla extract and the eggs, one at a time, mixing well after each addition.

Step 5: Combine the Wet and Dry Ingredients

Gradually add the dry ingredient mixture to the wet ingredients, mixing until just combined. Be careful not to overmix the dough.

Step 6: Add the Chocolate Chips and Chow Mein Noodles

Gently fold in the semi-sweet chocolate chips and chow mein noodles, ensuring that they are evenly distributed throughout the cookie dough.

Step 7: Form the Cookie Nests

Using a tablespoon, scoop out portions of the cookie dough and place them on the prepared baking sheet. Use your fingers to create an indentation in the center of each dough portion, shaping it into a nest-like form.

Step 8: Bake and Decorate

Bake the cookies in the preheated oven for 10-12 minutes or until they are golden brown. Once they are baked, remove the cookies from the oven and immediately press mini chocolate eggs or jelly beans into the center of each nest while the cookies are still warm.

Step 9: Let the Cookies Cool

Allow the cookies to cool on the baking sheet for 5 minutes before transferring them to a wire rack to cool completely. Once cooled, your delightful Easter nest cookies are ready to be enjoyed!

Conclusion

Making Easter nest cookies is a delightful way to celebrate the Easter season with your kids. These cute and tasty treats will not only bring joy to your family but also create lasting memories. So, gather your ingredients, put on your aprons, and get ready to make some delicious Easter nest cookies that will surely be a hit with kids and adults alike. Happy baking and happy Easter!
