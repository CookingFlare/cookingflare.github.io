Classic Baked Macaroni and Cheese: A Cheesy Comfort


Are you craving a warm and comforting dish that will satisfy your cheesy desires? Look no further than Classic Baked Macaroni and Cheese! This timeless recipe is perfect for those cozy nights in or when you’re looking to impress your guests with a classic dish. With its creamy texture and ooey-gooey goodness, this mac and cheese recipe will surely become a family favorite.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound elbow macaroni
  • 4 tablespoons unsalted butter
  • 1/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 cups whole milk
  • 2 cups shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1/4 teaspoon paprika

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C) and grease a 9x13-inch baking dish.

  2. Cook the macaroni according to the package instructions until al dente. Drain and set aside.

  3. In a large saucepan, melt the butter over medium heat. Add the flour and whisk continuously for about 2 minutes, until the mixture becomes smooth and starts bubbling.

  4. Slowly pour in the milk while whisking constantly. Cook for another 2-3 minutes, until the mixture thickens.

  5. Reduce the heat to low and add the cheddar, mozzarella, and Parmesan cheese. Stir until the cheese is completely melted and the sauce is smooth. Remove from heat.

  6. Add the cooked macaroni to the cheese sauce and mix well, ensuring that every noodle is coated with the luscious cheese sauce.

  7. Transfer the macaroni and cheese mixture to the greased baking dish, spreading it evenly.

  8. In a small bowl, combine the salt, black pepper, and paprika. Sprinkle this seasoning mixture evenly over the macaroni and cheese.

  9. Bake in the preheated oven for 25-30 minutes, or until the top is golden brown and the cheese is bubbly.

  10. Remove from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes before serving. This will allow the macaroni and cheese to set slightly, making it easier to serve.

  11. Serve hot and enjoy the cheesy goodness!

Tips and Variations

  • For an extra flavor boost, you can add cooked bacon, sautéed onions, or diced tomatoes to the macaroni and cheese mixture before baking.
  • If you prefer a crispy top, you can sprinkle some breadcrumbs mixed with melted butter over the macaroni and cheese before baking.
  • Feel free to experiment with different types of cheese to create your own unique flavor combination. Gouda, fontina, or gruyere are great options to try.
  • To make this recipe gluten-free, simply use gluten-free macaroni and substitute the all-purpose flour with a gluten-free flour blend.

Conclusion

Classic Baked Macaroni and Cheese is a dish that brings comfort and happiness to the table. Its creamy and cheesy goodness is hard to resist, making it a timeless favorite for both kids and adults. Whether you’re hosting a dinner party or simply craving a cozy night in, this recipe will surely hit the spot. So, grab your ingredients and get ready to indulge in a cheesy delight that will warm your heart and soul.
