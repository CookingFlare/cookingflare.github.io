Teriyaki Tofu Stir Fry: A Vegan Take on a Japanese Classic


If you’re looking for a delicious and healthy vegan recipe that combines the flavors of Japan with the versatility of tofu, then look no further than this Teriyaki Tofu Stir Fry. This dish is a perfect marriage of sweet and savory, with the familiar umami taste that we all love from the traditional teriyaki sauce. It’s quick, easy to make, and packed with nutrients. Let’s dive right into the recipe!

Ingredients:

  • 1 block of firm tofu, pressed and cubed
  • 2 tablespoons of cornstarch
  • 2 tablespoons of vegetable oil
  • 1 tablespoon of minced garlic
  • 1 tablespoon of minced ginger
  • 1 cup of sliced bell peppers (mix of red, green, and yellow for added color)
  • 1 cup of sliced carrots
  • 1 cup of broccoli florets
  • 1 cup of snow peas
  • 1/4 cup of low-sodium soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons of maple syrup
  • 1 tablespoon of rice vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon of sesame oil
  • Optional toppings: sesame seeds, sliced green onions

Instructions:

  1. Start by pressing the tofu to remove excess moisture. Place the tofu block between two plates and put a heavy object on top. Let it sit for about 15 minutes.

  2. While the tofu is being pressed, prepare the vegetables. Slice the bell peppers, carrots, and snow peas. Cut the broccoli into small florets.

  3. In a small bowl, whisk together the low-sodium soy sauce, maple syrup, rice vinegar, and sesame oil. Set aside.

  4. After the tofu has been pressed, cut it into bite-sized cubes. Place the cornstarch in a shallow bowl and coat the tofu cubes with cornstarch, shaking off any excess.

  5. Heat the vegetable oil in a large skillet or wok over medium-high heat. Add the tofu cubes and cook until they are golden brown and crispy on all sides. This should take about 5-7 minutes. Remove the tofu from the skillet and set aside.

  6. In the same skillet, add the minced garlic and ginger. Sauté for about 1 minute until fragrant.

  7. Add the sliced bell peppers, carrots, broccoli, and snow peas to the skillet. Stir-fry for about 5 minutes until the vegetables are crisp-tender.

  8. Return the tofu to the skillet and pour the teriyaki sauce over the tofu and vegetables. Stir gently to coat everything evenly. Cook for an additional 2 minutes until the sauce thickens slightly and coats the tofu and vegetables.

  9. Remove from heat and serve the Teriyaki Tofu Stir Fry hot. You can garnish with sesame seeds and sliced green onions for added flavor and presentation.

Enjoy this vegan take on a Japanese classic! The combination of the crispy tofu, colorful vegetables, and flavorful teriyaki sauce will surely satisfy your taste buds. This dish is not only delicious but also packed with protein, vitamins, and minerals. It’s a great option for a quick weeknight dinner or a weekend meal with family and friends. Give it a try and let us know how it turns out!

Remember, cooking is all about experimentation, so feel free to customize this recipe to suit your taste preferences. You can add other vegetables like mushrooms or zucchini, or even include some cashews or peanuts for added crunch. The possibilities are endless! Happy cooking!
