Black Forest Cake: Layers of Chocolate and Cherries


If you’re a fan of chocolate and cherries, then Black Forest Cake is the perfect dessert for you! This classic German cake is known for its layers of rich chocolate cake, whipped cream, and juicy cherries. It’s a showstopper at any occasion and will leave your guests begging for more. So, let’s dive into the deliciousness and learn how to make this indulgent treat!

Ingredients

For the cake:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 cups granulated sugar
  • 3/4 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons baking soda
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1/2 cup vegetable oil
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 1 cup boiling water

For the filling and frosting:

  • 2 cups heavy cream
  • 1/4 cup powdered sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 2 cans of cherry pie filling

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease and flour two 9-inch round cake pans.

  2. In a large mixing bowl, combine the flour, sugar, cocoa powder, baking powder, baking soda, and salt. Mix well.

  3. Add the eggs, milk, vegetable oil, and vanilla extract to the dry ingredients. Beat on medium speed for about 2 minutes.

  4. Stir in the boiling water. The batter will be thin, but that’s normal.

  5. Pour the batter evenly into the prepared cake pans. Bake for 30 to 35 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.

  6. Remove the cakes from the oven and let them cool in the pans for 10 minutes. Then, transfer them to a wire rack to cool completely.

  7. While the cakes are cooling, prepare the filling and frosting. In a chilled mixing bowl, beat the heavy cream, powdered sugar, and vanilla extract until stiff peaks form.

  8. Once the cakes have cooled, use a serrated knife to level the tops, if needed. Place one cake layer on a serving plate.

  9. Spread a layer of cherry pie filling on top of the first cake layer. Be generous with the cherries!

  10. Next, spread a layer of whipped cream over the cherry filling.

  11. Place the second cake layer on top and repeat the process with another layer of cherry filling and whipped cream.

  12. Finally, frost the sides and the top of the cake with the remaining whipped cream. You can be as creative as you like with the frosting, adding swirls or using a piping bag to decorate.

  13. Once the cake is frosted, refrigerate it for at least 2 hours before serving. This will allow the flavors to meld together and the cake to set.

  14. When you’re ready to serve, slice into the cake and marvel at the layers of chocolate and cherries. Each bite is a heavenly combination of moist cake, sweet cherries, and creamy whipped cream.

Enjoy this decadent Black Forest Cake with your loved ones, and savor the flavors of chocolate and cherries. It’s a dessert that will leave everyone wanting seconds, so be prepared to share the recipe! Happy baking and indulging in this delightful treat!

Note: This recipe makes a traditional Black Forest Cake, but feel free to make it your own by adding your favorite ingredients or twists. Get creative and enjoy the process!
