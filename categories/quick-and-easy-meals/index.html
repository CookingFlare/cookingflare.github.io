Simply thread cherry tomatoes, mozzarella balls, and fresh basil leaves onto toothpicks. Drizzle with olive oil and balsamic glaze, and serve immediately. Recipe 3: Bacon-Wrapped Dates Sweet and savory, these bite-sized treats are sure to impress. Simply wrap dates with bacon slices and bake in the oven at 400°F (200°C) for 10-12 minutes, or until the bacon is crispy. Recipe 4: Mini Quiches These individual quiches are perfect for a crowd and can be made in just 20 minutes. Simply mix 1 cup of shredded cheddar cheese, 1/2 cup of diced ham, and 1/2 cup of diced bell peppers in a bowl. Pour into mini muffin tin cups and bake at 375°F (190°C) for 12-15 minutes, or until the edges are golden brown.
