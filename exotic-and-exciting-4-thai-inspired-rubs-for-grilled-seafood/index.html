Exotic and Exciting: 4 Thai-Inspired Rubs for Grilled Seafood


If you’re a seafood lover and want to add a burst of exotic flavors to your grilled dishes, look no further! In this blog post, we’ll introduce you to four mouthwatering Thai-inspired rubs that will take your grilled seafood to a whole new level. These rubs are easy to make, packed with aromatic spices, and will transport your taste buds to the vibrant streets of Thailand. So fire up your grill and get ready to embark on a culinary adventure!

1. Lemongrass and Ginger Rub

Ingredients:

  • 2 stalks of lemongrass, finely chopped
  • 1 thumb-sized piece of ginger, grated
  • 3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon of soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon of fish sauce
  • 1 tablespoon of brown sugar
  • 1 teaspoon of ground coriander
  • 1 teaspoon of ground cumin

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, combine the lemongrass, ginger, garlic, soy sauce, fish sauce, brown sugar, coriander, and cumin.
  2. Mix well until all the ingredients are evenly distributed.
  3. Rub the mixture generously onto your seafood of choice, such as shrimp or fish fillets.
  4. Allow the rub to marinate the seafood for at least 30 minutes before grilling.
  5. Grill the seafood over medium-high heat until cooked through and slightly charred, approximately 4-6 minutes per side, depending on the thickness of the seafood.

2. Thai Red Curry Rub

Ingredients:

  • 3 tablespoons of Thai red curry paste
  • 2 tablespoons of coconut milk
  • 1 tablespoon of lime juice
  • 1 tablespoon of brown sugar
  • 2 teaspoons of fish sauce
  • 1 teaspoon of ground turmeric
  • 1 teaspoon of ground coriander

Instructions:

  1. In a small bowl, mix together the Thai red curry paste, coconut milk, lime juice, brown sugar, fish sauce, turmeric, and coriander.
  2. Stir until the ingredients are well combined and form a thick paste.
  3. Spread the rub generously over your seafood, ensuring it is evenly coated.
  4. Allow the rub to marinate the seafood for at least 1 hour in the refrigerator.
  5. Preheat your grill to medium heat and cook the seafood for about 4-5 minutes per side, or until it is opaque and flakes easily with a fork.

3. Spicy Basil and Lime Rub

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup of fresh basil leaves, finely chopped
  • Zest and juice of 2 limes
  • 4 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 2 Thai chilies, finely chopped (adjust according to your spice preference)
  • 2 tablespoons of soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon of fish sauce
  • 1 tablespoon of brown sugar
  • 1 teaspoon of ground black pepper

Instructions:

  1. In a mixing bowl, combine the basil leaves, lime zest, lime juice, garlic, Thai chilies, soy sauce, fish sauce, brown sugar, and black pepper.
  2. Stir well until all the ingredients are thoroughly mixed.
  3. Apply the rub generously to your seafood, ensuring it is evenly coated on all sides.
  4. Allow the rub to marinate the seafood for at least 20 minutes.
  5. Grill the seafood over medium-high heat for about 3-4 minutes per side, or until it is cooked through and nicely charred.

4. Sweet and Spicy Tamarind Rub

Ingredients:

  • 3 tablespoons of tamarind paste
  • 2 tablespoons of honey
  • 1 tablespoon of soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon of fish sauce
  • 1 teaspoon of ground chili flakes
  • 1 teaspoon of ground turmeric

Instructions:

  1. In a small bowl, whisk together the tamarind paste, honey, soy sauce, fish sauce, chili flakes, and turmeric.
  2. Ensure all the ingredients are well combined and form a smooth paste.
  3. Apply the rub generously to your seafood, making sure it is evenly coated.
  4. Allow the rub to marinate the seafood for at least 30 minutes in the refrigerator.
  5. Grill the seafood over medium heat for approximately 4-5 minutes per side, or until it is cooked through and caramelized.

Conclusion

With these four Thai-inspired rubs, your grilled seafood will never be the same again. Each rub brings its own unique combination of flavors, from the zesty freshness of lemongrass and ginger to the fiery kick of Thai red curry. Whether you’re hosting a backyard barbecue or simply craving a taste of Thailand, these rubs are guaranteed to impress your taste buds and transport you to the vibrant streets of Southeast Asia. So, get your grill ready, try out these recipes, and prepare to embark on a culinary adventure like no other!
