Savory Sage and Onion Gravy Recipe for a Classic Thanksgiving Accompaniment


Thanksgiving dinner is never complete without a rich and flavorful gravy to complement the star of the show – the turkey. While there are many variations of gravy, we’re going to delve into the timeless classic – Savory Sage and Onion Gravy. This recipe is a perfect balance of savory and aromatic flavors, making it the ideal accompaniment to your Thanksgiving feast. So, let’s dive into the details and master the art of creating this delectable gravy from scratch.

Ingredients

  • 4 tablespoons unsalted butter
  • 1 large onion, finely chopped
  • 4 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • 4 cups chicken or turkey stock
  • 2-3 sprigs of fresh sage
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

Step 1: Sauté the Onions

  • In a saucepan over medium heat, melt the butter.
  • Add the finely chopped onion and cook until it becomes soft and translucent, stirring occasionally. This should take about 8-10 minutes.

Step 2: Add the Flour

  • Sprinkle the flour over the sautéed onions and butter. Stir continuously for 2-3 minutes to cook the flour and form a smooth paste, which will serve as the base for the gravy.

Step 3: Incorporate the Stock

  • Gradually pour in the chicken or turkey stock while whisking continuously to prevent any lumps from forming. Bring the mixture to a gentle simmer, stirring occasionally.

Step 4: Infuse with Sage

  • Toss in the fresh sage sprigs and allow them to infuse their earthy, aromatic essence into the gravy. Let the gravy simmer for about 15-20 minutes to allow the flavors to meld together beautifully.

Step 5: Season and Strain

  • Once the gravy has reached the desired consistency, remove the sage sprigs. Season the gravy with salt and pepper to taste, adjusting according to your preference.

Step 6: Serve and Enjoy

  • Your homemade Savory Sage and Onion Gravy is now ready to take its place alongside the Thanksgiving turkey. Pour it into a gravy boat and delight in its rich, savory goodness as you drizzle it over your roasted turkey, mashed potatoes, and all the other delicious components of your Thanksgiving feast.

Tips for Perfection

  • For a richer flavor, consider using homemade turkey or chicken stock. However, store-bought stock works just as well.
  • If you prefer a smoother texture, you can strain the gravy before serving to remove any onion bits or lumps.
  • Don’t rush the cooking process; allowing the gravy to simmer and meld the flavors will result in a more robust taste.

Conclusion

The Savory Sage and Onion Gravy is a time-honored addition to the Thanksgiving table, bringing warmth and depth to every bite. By following this simple yet flavorful recipe, you can elevate your holiday meal and create lasting memories with family and friends. Whether you’re a seasoned cook or new to the art of gravy-making, this recipe is sure to become a cherished tradition in your Thanksgiving celebrations. So, embrace the aromas, savor the flavors, and indulge in the joy of sharing a homemade meal made with love. Happy Thanksgiving!
