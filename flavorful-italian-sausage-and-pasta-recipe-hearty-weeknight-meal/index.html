Flavorful Italian Sausage and Pasta Recipe - Hearty Weeknight Meal


Are you looking for a delicious and satisfying meal that can be prepared in no time? Look no further than this Flavorful Italian Sausage and Pasta recipe! Packed with robust flavors and wholesome ingredients, this dish is perfect for those busy weeknights when you crave a hearty and comforting meal. Let’s dive right in!

Ingredients

  • 1 pound Italian sausage (mild or hot, depending on your preference)
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 red bell pepper, diced
  • 1 yellow bell pepper, diced
  • 1 can (14 ounces) diced tomatoes
  • 1 can (8 ounces) tomato sauce
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1/2 teaspoon crushed red pepper flakes (optional, for some extra heat)
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh basil
  • 12 ounces penne pasta (or any pasta of your choice)

Instructions

  1. In a large skillet or Dutch oven, cook the Italian sausage over medium heat until browned and cooked through. Remove the sausage from the pan and set aside.

  2. In the same pan, add the diced onion, minced garlic, and diced bell peppers. Sauté until the vegetables are tender and fragrant, about 5 minutes.

  3. Return the cooked sausage to the pan and add the diced tomatoes, tomato sauce, dried basil, dried oregano, and crushed red pepper flakes (if using). Stir well to combine.

  4. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Bring the mixture to a simmer and let it cook for about 10 minutes, allowing the flavors to meld together.

  5. While the sauce is simmering, cook the pasta according to the package instructions until al dente. Drain the pasta and set aside.

  6. Once the sauce has thickened slightly, remove it from the heat and stir in the grated Parmesan cheese. The cheese will melt and create a creamy and flavorful sauce.

  7. Add the cooked pasta to the sauce and toss until the pasta is well coated.

  8. Serve the Flavorful Italian Sausage and Pasta hot, garnished with chopped fresh basil and an extra sprinkle of Parmesan cheese.

Tips and Variations

  • Feel free to customize this recipe by adding your favorite vegetables such as zucchini, mushrooms, or spinach.
  • For a lighter version, you can use turkey sausage or chicken sausage instead of traditional Italian sausage.
  • If you prefer a saucier dish, you can add more tomato sauce or diced tomatoes to the recipe.
  • This recipe can easily be doubled or halved to serve a larger or smaller crowd.
  • Leftovers can be stored in an airtight container in the refrigerator for up to 3 days.

Final Thoughts

This Flavorful Italian Sausage and Pasta recipe is a true crowd-pleaser that will satisfy even the pickiest eaters. With its rich flavors and comforting combination of sausage and pasta, it’s the perfect dish for a quick and easy weeknight meal. So, the next time you’re in need of a hearty and delicious dinner, give this recipe a try. Buon appetito!
