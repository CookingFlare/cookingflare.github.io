Savory Stuffed Bell Peppers Recipe for a Colorful Christmas Meal


The holiday season is a time for celebration, joy, and of course, delicious food! If you’re looking to add a burst of color and flavor to your Christmas feast, then look no further than this Savory Stuffed Bell Peppers recipe. Packed with wholesome ingredients and a delightful combination of flavors, these stuffed bell peppers are sure to impress your guests and make your Christmas meal truly memorable.

Ingredients

To make this delectable dish, you will need the following ingredients:

  • 6 large bell peppers (assorted colors for a vibrant presentation)
  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 cup cooked rice
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 cup diced tomatoes
  • 1 cup corn kernels
  • 1 cup black beans, rinsed and drained
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Shredded cheese (optional, for topping)
  • Fresh parsley or cilantro, for garnish

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C). Prepare a baking dish by lightly greasing it with oil or cooking spray.

  2. Begin by carefully cutting off the tops of the bell peppers and removing the seeds and membranes from the inside. Rinse the peppers under cold water and set them aside.

  3. In a large skillet, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the chopped onion and minced garlic, and sauté until they become fragrant and translucent.

  4. Add the ground beef to the skillet and cook until browned, breaking it up into small pieces with a wooden spoon.

  5. Stir in the diced tomatoes, corn kernels, and black beans. Continue to cook for a few minutes until the flavors meld together.

  6. Add the cooked rice, dried oregano, ground cumin, salt, and pepper to the skillet. Mix well to combine all the ingredients.

  7. Spoon the filling mixture into each bell pepper, packing it tightly until they are completely filled. Place the stuffed peppers upright in the prepared baking dish.

  8. Cover the baking dish with foil and bake in the preheated oven for about 30-35 minutes, or until the peppers are tender.

  9. If desired, remove the foil and sprinkle some shredded cheese on top of each stuffed pepper. Return the dish to the oven for an additional 5 minutes, or until the cheese is melted and bubbly.

  10. Once cooked, remove the stuffed peppers from the oven and let them cool for a few minutes. Garnish with fresh parsley or cilantro before serving.

Serving Suggestions

These savory stuffed bell peppers make a delightful main dish for your Christmas meal. Serve them alongside a fresh green salad or some crusty bread for a complete and satisfying holiday feast. You can also pair them with a tangy tomato sauce or a dollop of sour cream for an extra burst of flavor.

Conclusion

This Savory Stuffed Bell Peppers recipe is not only visually stunning but also incredibly delicious. The combination of tender bell peppers, flavorful filling, and aromatic herbs and spices create a dish that will leave your taste buds dancing with joy. So, why not add a splash of color to your Christmas table this year and give this recipe a try? Your loved ones will be impressed, and your taste buds will thank you!
