Nutty and Sweet Christmas Granola Recipe for a Healthy Start


The holiday season is a time for indulgence, but that doesn’t mean you have to abandon your healthy eating habits. In fact, you can kick off your festive mornings with a nutritious and delicious Nutty and Sweet Christmas Granola. This homemade granola is packed with wholesome ingredients that will energize you for the day ahead, and its delightful combination of flavors will make your Christmas mornings even more special.

Ingredients

  • 3 cups rolled oats
  • 1 cup chopped nuts (such as almonds, pecans, or walnuts)
  • 1 cup mixed seeds (such as pumpkin seeds, sunflower seeds, and flaxseeds)
  • 1 cup shredded coconut
  • 1/2 cup pure maple syrup
  • 1/4 cup coconut oil, melted
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup dried cranberries
  • 1 cup dried apricots, chopped

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 325°F (165°C) and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.

  2. In a large mixing bowl, combine the rolled oats, chopped nuts, mixed seeds, and shredded coconut.

  3. In a separate bowl, whisk together the maple syrup, melted coconut oil, vanilla extract, ground cinnamon, and salt.

  4. Pour the wet mixture over the dry ingredients and stir until everything is well coated.

  5. Spread the granola mixture evenly onto the prepared baking sheet.

  6. Bake for 20-25 minutes, stirring halfway through, until the granola is golden brown and fragrant.

  7. Remove the granola from the oven and allow it to cool completely.

  8. Once the granola has cooled, stir in the dried cranberries and chopped dried apricots.

  9. Transfer the granola to an airtight container and store it at room temperature.

Serving Suggestions

This Nutty and Sweet Christmas Granola is incredibly versatile. Serve it with Greek yogurt and fresh fruit for a wholesome breakfast, sprinkle it over smoothie bowls for added crunch, or simply enjoy it by the handful as a satisfying snack. You can also package it in festive jars and give it as a thoughtful homemade gift to friends and family.

Nutritional Benefits

This granola is not only delicious but also nutritious. Oats are a great source of fiber and essential nutrients, while nuts and seeds provide healthy fats and protein. The dried fruits add natural sweetness and additional vitamins and minerals. With no refined sugars or artificial additives, this granola is a wholesome way to start your day.

Make It Your Own

Feel free to customize this recipe to suit your preferences. You can swap out the nuts and seeds for your favorites, add a sprinkle of warming spices like nutmeg or ginger, or incorporate other dried fruits such as raisins or cherries. The possibilities are endless, so have fun experimenting with different flavor combinations.

Conclusion

This Nutty and Sweet Christmas Granola is a delightful way to nourish your body and treat your taste buds during the holiday season. By preparing this homemade granola, you can savor the festive flavors while staying true to your commitment to healthy eating. Make a batch of this granola, and let it become a cherished part of your Christmas morning traditions. Happy holidays and happy cooking!
