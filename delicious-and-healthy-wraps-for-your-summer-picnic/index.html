Delicious and Healthy Wraps for Your Summer Picnic


The summer season is the perfect time to enjoy a picnic with family and friends. And what better way to elevate your picnic experience than with delicious and healthy wraps? Wraps are not only convenient to pack and carry, but they also offer endless possibilities for flavor combinations. Whether you’re looking for a light and refreshing option or a hearty and satisfying meal, there’s a wrap recipe for everyone. In this blog post, we’ll explore a variety of mouthwatering wrap recipes that are perfect for your summer picnic.

Mediterranean Chicken Wrap

Ingredients:

  • Grilled chicken breast
  • Fresh spinach leaves
  • Sliced cucumbers
  • Diced tomatoes
  • Kalamata olives
  • Feta cheese
  • Whole wheat wraps
  • Tzatziki sauce

Instructions:

  1. Begin by laying out the whole wheat wraps on a clean surface.
  2. Spread a generous amount of tzatziki sauce over each wrap.
  3. Layer the grilled chicken breast, spinach leaves, sliced cucumbers, diced tomatoes, kalamata olives, and crumbled feta cheese on top of the sauce.
  4. Carefully fold the sides of the wrap and then roll it up tightly.
  5. Secure the wraps with toothpicks, if necessary, and slice them in half for easy serving.

Vegan Rainbow Collard Wraps

Ingredients:

  • Large collard green leaves
  • Hummus
  • Shredded carrots
  • Sliced bell peppers (assorted colors)
  • Thinly sliced red cabbage
  • Avocado slices
  • Cooked quinoa

Instructions:

  1. Blanch the collard green leaves in boiling water for a few seconds, then transfer them to an ice bath to cool.
  2. Pat the leaves dry with a clean kitchen towel.
  3. Lay the collard green leaves flat and spread a layer of hummus over each leaf.
  4. Arrange the shredded carrots, sliced bell peppers, red cabbage, avocado slices, and cooked quinoa on top of the hummus.
  5. Carefully fold the bottom of each leaf over the filling, then fold in the sides, and roll them up tightly.
  6. Cut the wraps in half and serve them with your favorite dipping sauce.

Smoked Turkey and Avocado Wrap

Ingredients:

  • Smoked turkey slices
  • Ripe avocado, sliced
  • Crispy bacon strips
  • Sliced red onions
  • Romaine lettuce leaves
  • Whole grain wraps
  • Honey mustard dressing

Instructions:

  1. Lay out the whole grain wraps and drizzle honey mustard dressing over each one.
  2. Layer the smoked turkey slices, sliced avocado, crispy bacon strips, sliced red onions, and romaine lettuce leaves on top of the dressing.
  3. Roll up the wraps tightly and slice them in half before serving.

Tips for Packing Wraps for a Picnic

  1. Use sturdy wraps that won’t easily tear or fall apart when packed.
  2. Wrap the individual wraps tightly in parchment paper or foil to keep them secure and prevent them from unraveling.
  3. Keep the wraps chilled in a cooler or insulated bag to maintain freshness.
  4. Pack any accompanying sauces or dressings separately to prevent the wraps from becoming soggy.

Wrapping Up

These delicious and healthy wrap recipes are sure to be a hit at your summer picnic. Whether you prefer a classic Mediterranean flavor, vibrant vegan options, or a satisfying turkey and avocado combination, there’s a wrap for every palate. With their portability and versatility, wraps are the ideal choice for a picnic meal. So, gather your loved ones, prepare these delightful wraps, and make the most of the sunny days with a memorable picnic experience.
