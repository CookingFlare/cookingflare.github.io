BBQ Pulled Chicken: Recipes for Sandwiches, Tacos, and More


Summer is the perfect time to fire up the grill and indulge in some delicious BBQ treats. One dish that never fails to impress is BBQ pulled chicken. This versatile meat can be used in a variety of ways, from sandwiches to tacos and beyond. In this blog post, we will explore some mouthwatering recipes that will elevate your BBQ game to a whole new level.

Classic BBQ Pulled Chicken Sandwich

Let’s start with the classic: a BBQ pulled chicken sandwich. This crowd-pleasing favorite is a perfect combination of tender chicken, tangy BBQ sauce, and a soft bun. Here’s what you’ll need:

Ingredients:

  • 2 pounds of boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1 cup of your favorite BBQ sauce
  • 1/4 cup of apple cider vinegar
  • 2 tablespoons of brown sugar
  • 1 teaspoon of smoked paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon of garlic powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Hamburger buns

Instructions:

  1. In a slow cooker, combine the BBQ sauce, apple cider vinegar, brown sugar, smoked paprika, garlic powder, salt, and pepper.
  2. Add the chicken breasts to the slow cooker and coat them evenly with the sauce mixture.
  3. Cook on low heat for 6-8 hours or on high heat for 3-4 hours, until the chicken is tender and easily shreds.
  4. Once cooked, remove the chicken from the slow cooker and shred it using two forks.
  5. Return the shredded chicken to the slow cooker and mix it with the remaining sauce.
  6. Serve the BBQ pulled chicken on hamburger buns and enjoy!

BBQ Pulled Chicken Tacos

If you’re in the mood for something a little different, why not try BBQ pulled chicken tacos? These handheld delights are bursting with flavor and are sure to be a hit at your next gathering. Here’s what you’ll need:

Ingredients:

  • 2 pounds of boneless, skinless chicken thighs
  • 1 cup of BBQ sauce
  • 1/4 cup of lime juice
  • 2 teaspoons of chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon of cumin
  • 1/2 teaspoon of garlic powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Tortillas
  • Toppings of your choice (such as shredded lettuce, diced tomatoes, sliced avocado, and chopped cilantro)

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, mix together the BBQ sauce, lime juice, chili powder, cumin, garlic powder, salt, and pepper.
  2. Place the chicken thighs in a zip-top bag and pour the marinade over them. Seal the bag and refrigerate for at least 1 hour, or overnight for maximum flavor.
  3. Preheat your grill to medium-high heat. Remove the chicken from the marinade and discard the excess.
  4. Grill the chicken thighs for 6-8 minutes per side, or until they reach an internal temperature of 165°F (74°C) and have a nice char.
  5. Remove the chicken from the grill and let it rest for a few minutes. Then, shred the meat using two forks.
  6. Warm the tortillas and fill them with the BBQ pulled chicken. Top with your favorite toppings and serve!

BBQ Pulled Chicken Pizza

For a unique twist on a classic dish, why not try BBQ pulled chicken pizza? This combination of smoky chicken, tangy BBQ sauce, and melted cheese is a match made in heaven. Here’s what you’ll need:

Ingredients:

  • Pizza dough (store-bought or homemade)
  • 1 cup of BBQ sauce
  • 2 cups of shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 2 cups of shredded BBQ pulled chicken
  • Red onion, thinly sliced
  • Fresh cilantro, chopped

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to the temperature specified on the pizza dough packaging or to 450°F (230°C) for homemade dough.
  2. Roll out the pizza dough on a floured surface to your desired thickness.
  3. Spread the BBQ sauce evenly over the dough, leaving a small border around the edges.
  4. Sprinkle the shredded mozzarella cheese over the sauce.
  5. Distribute the shredded BBQ pulled chicken evenly over the cheese.
  6. Top with sliced red onion for an extra kick of flavor.
  7. Transfer the pizza to a baking sheet or a pizza stone and bake for 12-15 minutes, or until the crust is golden and the cheese is bubbly.
  8. Remove the pizza from the oven and sprinkle with fresh cilantro.
  9. Slice and serve!

Conclusion

BBQ pulled chicken is a versatile and delicious option that can be enjoyed in various ways. From classic sandwiches to mouthwatering tacos and even on top of a pizza, there’s no shortage of ways to enjoy this flavorful dish. So fire up the grill, gather your friends and family, and get ready to savor the irresistible taste of BBQ pulled chicken in all its mouthwatering glory!

Remember to experiment with different flavors and toppings to make these recipes your own. The possibilities are endless, and with BBQ pulled chicken as the star ingredient, your culinary adventures are sure to be a hit. Enjoy!
