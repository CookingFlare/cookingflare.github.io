Spicy Paleo Turkey and Sweet Potato Hash


If you’re looking for a hearty and satisfying meal that’s packed with flavor and nutrition, look no further than this Spicy Paleo Turkey and Sweet Potato Hash recipe. This dish is not only delicious but also fits into a paleo lifestyle, making it a perfect choice for those looking to eat clean and feel energized.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground turkey
  • 2 medium sweet potatoes, peeled and diced
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 red bell pepper, diced
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • 1/2 teaspoon chili powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh cilantro for garnish
  • Avocado slices for serving
  • Olive oil for cooking

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Sweet Potatoes: In a large skillet, heat a tablespoon of olive oil over medium heat. Add the diced sweet potatoes and cook, stirring occasionally, until they are tender and lightly browned, about 10 minutes. Remove the sweet potatoes from the skillet and set them aside.

  2. Cook the Turkey: In the same skillet, add another tablespoon of olive oil and the diced onion. Cook until the onion is translucent, then add the minced garlic and cook for another minute. Add the ground turkey to the skillet, breaking it apart with a spatula as it cooks. Once the turkey is no longer pink, add the diced red bell pepper and cook until it’s softened.

  3. Season and Combine: Sprinkle the paprika, cumin, chili powder, salt, and pepper over the turkey mixture. Stir well to combine all the ingredients and let the flavors meld together for a few minutes.

  4. Add the Sweet Potatoes: Gently fold the cooked sweet potatoes into the turkey mixture, ensuring they are well incorporated and heated through.

  5. Serve and Enjoy: Once everything is heated through, remove the skillet from the heat. Garnish the hash with fresh cilantro and serve with avocado slices on the side.

Why Choose This Dish?

This Spicy Paleo Turkey and Sweet Potato Hash is a fantastic option for anyone following a paleo diet or simply looking for a satisfying and healthy meal. The combination of lean ground turkey and nutrient-dense sweet potatoes makes this dish a great source of protein, fiber, and essential vitamins and minerals. The spices add a delightful kick, making every bite a burst of flavor.

Moreover, this recipe is versatile and can be enjoyed for breakfast, lunch, or dinner. It’s a great meal prep option, as it reheats beautifully and can be enjoyed throughout the week. Plus, it’s a wonderful way to introduce more vegetables into your diet without sacrificing taste or satisfaction.

So, whether you’re a seasoned paleo enthusiast or someone looking to explore new flavors and ingredients, this Spicy Paleo Turkey and Sweet Potato Hash is sure to become a staple in your recipe collection. Enjoy the delicious combination of savory turkey, sweet potatoes, and aromatic spices in every mouthful.

This Spicy Paleo Turkey and Sweet Potato Hash recipe is a celebration of wholesome ingredients and bold flavors. It’s a testament to the fact that healthy eating can also be incredibly delicious. Whether you’re cooking for yourself, your family, or friends, this dish is bound to impress and satisfy. Give it a try and savor the goodness of paleo eating!
