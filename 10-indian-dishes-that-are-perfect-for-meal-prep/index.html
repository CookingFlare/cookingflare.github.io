10 Indian Dishes That Are Perfect for Meal Prep


Indian cuisine is renowned for its diverse range of flavorful and nutritious dishes. With the increasing popularity of meal prep, it's no surprise that many home cooks are turning to Indian recipes for their convenient, healthy, and versatile meals. Here are 10 tasty and nutritious Indian dishes that are perfect for meal prep, catering to various dietary needs and preferences.

  1. Chana Masala Chana Masala is a popular North Indian dish made with chickpeas cooked in a rich and aromatic tomato-based sauce. This flavorful curry is easy to prepare and can be cooked in large batches for meal prep. Simply soak chickpeas overnight, then sauté onions, ginger, garlic, and spices before adding the chickpeas and simmering the mixture in a slow cooker or Dutch oven. Ingredients:

1 cup dried chickpeas 1 onion 2 inches ginger 3 cloves garlic 1 tsp ground cumin 1 tsp ground coriander 1 tsp ground cumin seeds 1 tsp cayenne pepper 1 can diced tomatoes Salt, to taste

  1. Vegetable Biryani Vegetable Biryani is a famous South Indian one-pot dish made with a medley of vegetables and flavorful basmati rice. It's an excellent dish for meal prep as it can be refrigerated or frozen for up to three days. Cook the basmati rice with sautéed vegetables, spices, and aromatics, then add water and salt to taste. Ingredients:

2 cups basmati rice 1 cup mixed vegetables (carrots, potatoes, peas, onions) 2 green chilies 2 inches ginger 1 tsp ground cumin 1 tsp ground coriander 1 tsp saffron threads 1 tsp salt 4 cups water

  1. Saag Paneer Saag Paneer is a rich and creamy North Indian spinach dish made with paneer and fragrant spices. This hearty dish is perfect for meal prep as it freezes well. Combine sautéed spinach, paneer, and spices in a Dutch oven or slow cooker, and let it simmer until the cheese melts and the spinach is tender. Ingredients:

1 cup paneer 2 cups fresh spinach 2 inches ginger 3 cloves garlic 1 tsp ground cumin 1 tsp ground coriander 1 tsp garam masala Salt, to taste

  1. Chia Seed Roti Chia seed roti is a vegan and gluten-free alternative to traditional flatbreads. It's an excellent option for meal prep as it can be refrigerated for up to three days. Mix chia seeds with flour, water, and spices, then knead the dough into small balls. Flatten the balls into thin disks and cook them in a non-stick pan. Ingredients:

1 cup chia seeds 2 cups all-purpose flour 1/4 cup water 1 tsp ground cumin 1 tsp salt 1 tsp oil

  1. Rajma Masala Rajma Masala is a flavorful and comforting North Indian kidney bean stew. It's a great option for meal prep as it freezes well. Combine sautéed kidney beans, onions, ginger, and spices in a Dutch oven or slow cooker, and let it simmer until the beans are tender. Ingredients:

1 cup kidney beans 1 onion 2 inches ginger 3 cloves garlic 1 tsp ground cumin 1 tsp ground coriander 1 tsp cayenne pepper 1 can diced tomatoes Salt, to taste

  1. Matar Paneer Matar Paneer is a creamy and colorful Indian dish made with paneer, peas, and fragrant spices. It's an excellent option for meal prep as it reheats well. Combine sautéed peas, paneer, and spices in a Dutch oven or slow cooker, and let it simmer until the cheese melts and the peas are tender. Ingredients:

1 cup paneer 1 cup fresh peas 2 inches ginger 3 cloves garlic 1 tsp ground cumin 1 tsp ground coriander 1 tsp garam masala Salt, to taste

  1. Daal Tadka Daal Tadka is a simple and nutritious Indian lentil soup. It's an excellent option for meal prep as it reheats well. Combine lentils, onions, ginger, and spices in a pot, then add water and let it simmer until the lentils are tender. Ingredients:

1 cup red or yellow lentils 1 onion 2 inches ginger 3 cloves garlic 1 tsp ground cumin 1 tsp ground coriander 1 tsp garam masala Salt, to taste

  1. Gobi Matar Gobi Matar is a flavorful and nutritious Indian cauliflower dish made with cauliflower, peas, and fragrant spices. It's an excellent option for meal prep as it freezes well. Combine sautéed cauliflower, peas, and spices in a Dutch oven or slow cooker, and let it simmer until the cauliflower is tender. Ingredients:

1 head cauliflower 1 cup fresh peas 2 inches ginger 3 cloves garlic 1 tsp ground cumin 1 tsp ground coriander 1 tsp garam masala Salt, to taste

  1. Chana Saag Chana Saag is a flavorful and nutritious spinach dish made with chickpeas, spinach, and fragrant spices. It's an excellent option for meal prep as it freezes well. Combine sautéed chickpeas, spinach, and spices in a Dutch oven or slow cooker, and let it simmer until the spinach is tender. Ingredients:

1 cup chickpeas 2 cups fresh spinach 2 inches ginger 3 cloves garlic 1 tsp ground cumin 1 tsp ground coriander 1 tsp garam masala Salt, to taste

  1. Baingan Chana Masala Baingan Chana Masala is a flavorful and nutritious Indian eggplant curry made with eggplant, chickpeas, and fragrant spices. It's an excellent option for meal prep as it reheats well. Combine sautéed eggplant, chickpeas, and spices in a Dutch oven or slow cooker, and let it simmer until the eggplant is tender. Ingredients:

1 eggplant 1 cup chickpeas 1 onion 2 inches ginger 3 cloves garlic 1 tsp ground cumin 1 tsp ground coriander 1 tsp cayenne pepper 1 can diced tomatoes Salt, to taste

Meal Prep Tips To prepare these dishes, start by cooking the protein and rice or roti ahead of time. Then, reheat the dishes as needed or assemble the components of the dish in a meal prep container. You can also customize the dishes to suit your dietary preferences by substituting dairy with non-dairy alternatives or using gluten-free flours. Conclusion Indian meals are incredibly versatile and nutritious, making them perfect for meal prep. With these 10 mouth-watering recipes, you can create a variety of healthy and flavorful meals that cater to various dietary needs. Happy cooking and meal prepping!
