Picnic-Ready Slider Recipes for Easy Snacking


Planning a picnic or outdoor gathering? Look no further! We have some delicious and easy-to-make slider recipes that are perfect for snacking on the go. These bite-sized wonders are not only convenient but also pack a punch of flavor. From classic favorites to unique twists, there’s something for everyone in this collection. So grab your picnic blanket and get ready to indulge in these picnic-ready slider recipes.

1. BBQ Chicken Sliders

Preparation time: 15 minutes | Cooking time: 20 minutes | Total time: 35 minutes | Serves: 4

Ingredients:

  • 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1 cup BBQ sauce
  • 8 slider buns
  • ½ cup coleslaw

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the grill to medium-high heat.
  2. Season the chicken breasts with salt and pepper, then grill for about 10 minutes on each side until cooked through.
  3. Brush the chicken with BBQ sauce and continue grilling for an additional 2 minutes on each side.
  4. Remove the chicken from the grill and let it rest for a few minutes. Then, slice it into thin strips.
  5. Toast the slider buns on the grill for a minute or until slightly crispy.
  6. Assemble the sliders by placing a few slices of BBQ chicken on each bun, topped with a dollop of coleslaw.
  7. Serve immediately and enjoy!

2. Caprese Sliders

Preparation time: 10 minutes | Cooking time: 5 minutes | Total time: 15 minutes | Serves: 4

Ingredients:

  • 8 slider buns
  • 8 fresh mozzarella slices
  • 8 tomato slices
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • Balsamic glaze
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to broil.
  2. Slice the slider buns in half and place them on a baking sheet.
  3. Top each bottom bun with a slice of mozzarella, followed by a tomato slice and a few basil leaves.
  4. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  5. Drizzle balsamic glaze over each slider.
  6. Place the baking sheet in the oven and broil for about 3-5 minutes, until the cheese is melted and the buns are toasted.
  7. Remove from the oven and cover each slider with the top bun.
  8. Serve these delicious Caprese sliders while they’re still warm.

3. Pulled Pork Sliders

Preparation time: 10 minutes | Cooking time: 8 hours | Total time: 8 hours 10 minutes | Serves: 6

Ingredients:

  • 2 pounds pork shoulder
  • 1 cup BBQ sauce
  • ½ cup chicken broth
  • 12 slider buns
  • Pickles (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Place the pork shoulder in a slow cooker and pour in the chicken broth.
  2. Cook on low heat for 8 hours or until the pork is tender and easily shredded.
  3. Remove the pork from the slow cooker and shred it using two forks.
  4. Mix the shredded pork with BBQ sauce until well coated.
  5. Toast the slider buns in the oven or on a grill until slightly crispy.
  6. Assemble the sliders by placing a generous amount of pulled pork on each bun, and if desired, add some pickles for extra tang.
  7. Serve these mouthwatering pulled pork sliders immediately.

4. Turkey and Cranberry Sliders

Preparation time: 15 minutes | Cooking time: 12 minutes | Total time: 27 minutes | Serves: 4

Ingredients:

  • 8 slider buns
  • 8 slices of deli turkey
  • 8 slices of Swiss cheese
  • ½ cup cranberry sauce
  • ½ cup baby spinach leaves

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. Slice the slider buns in half and place them on a baking sheet.
  3. Layer each bottom bun with a slice of Swiss cheese, followed by a slice of turkey and a spoonful of cranberry sauce.
  4. Top with baby spinach leaves.
  5. Cover each slider with the top bun.
  6. Place the baking sheet in the oven and bake for about 10-12 minutes until the cheese is melted and the buns are toasted.
  7. Remove from the oven and serve these delightful turkey and cranberry sliders.

5. Veggie Sliders

Preparation time: 20 minutes | Cooking time: 10 minutes | Total time: 30 minutes | Serves: 4

Ingredients:

  • 8 slider buns
  • 1 cup cooked quinoa
  • 1 cup black beans, mashed
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 1 carrot, grated
  • 1 garlic clove, minced
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Burger toppings of your choice (lettuce, tomato, avocado, etc.)

Instructions:

  1. In a large bowl, combine the cooked quinoa, mashed black beans, onion, carrot, garlic, cumin, paprika, salt, and pepper. Mix well until the ingredients are fully incorporated.
  2. Form the mixture into small patties, about the size of the slider buns.
  3. Heat a skillet over medium heat and lightly grease it with oil.
  4. Cook the veggie patties for about 4-5 minutes on each side until golden brown and crispy.
  5. Toast the slider buns in the oven or on a grill until slightly crispy.
  6. Assemble the sliders by placing a veggie patty on each bun and adding your desired toppings.
  7. Serve these wholesome veggie sliders and enjoy a burst of flavors.

Enjoy these picnic-ready slider recipes that will surely satisfy your cravings while keeping your outdoor snacking experience enjoyable and hassle-free. Whether you’re a meat lover, vegetarian, or simply looking for a quick and tasty bite, these sliders have got you covered. So pack your cooler, grab your favorite sliders, and head out for a delightful picnic adventure!
