Roasted Red Pepper Pasta: A Smoky and Flavorful Pasta Option


Are you looking for a unique and flavorful pasta dish to add to your recipe repertoire? Look no further than this delicious Roasted Red Pepper Pasta recipe. This dish is a perfect fusion of smoky, sweet, and savory flavors, making it a delightful option for any occasion. Whether you’re cooking for a family dinner or entertaining guests, this pasta is sure to impress. Let’s dive into the details of how to create this smoky and flavorful masterpiece.

Ingredients

  • 4 large red bell peppers
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 4 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 pound pasta of your choice (spaghetti or penne work well)
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh basil for garnish

Instructions

Step 1: Roast the Red Peppers

Start by preheating your oven to 400°F (200°C). Place the whole red bell peppers on a baking sheet and roast them in the oven for about 25-30 minutes, or until the skins are charred and blistered. This roasting process will bring out the natural sweetness of the peppers and infuse them with a delightful smoky flavor.

Once the peppers are roasted, carefully transfer them to a bowl and cover it with plastic wrap. Let the peppers steam for about 15 minutes. This will make it easier to remove the skins later.

After steaming, remove the skins, seeds, and stems from the peppers. Cut the flesh into strips and set aside.

Step 2: Prepare the Sauce

In a large skillet, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the chopped onion and sauté until it becomes translucent. Then, add the minced garlic and cook for another minute, stirring constantly to prevent burning.

Next, add the roasted red pepper strips to the skillet and stir to combine with the onion and garlic. Let the mixture cook for a few minutes to meld the flavors together.

Transfer the pepper mixture to a blender or food processor and blend until smooth. Return the mixture to the skillet and pour in the heavy cream. Stir the sauce over low heat until it’s well combined and heated through.

Step 3: Cook the Pasta

While the sauce is simmering, bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Cook the pasta according to the package instructions until it reaches al dente, then drain, reserving a cup of the pasta cooking water.

Step 4: Combine and Serve

Add the cooked pasta to the skillet with the red pepper sauce. Toss the pasta to coat it evenly with the sauce, adding a splash of the reserved pasta cooking water if needed to loosen the sauce.

Sprinkle the grated Parmesan cheese over the pasta and toss again to incorporate. Season with salt and pepper to taste.

Once the pasta is well coated with the sauce and cheese, it’s ready to serve. Divide the pasta among serving plates and garnish with fresh basil leaves for a pop of color and added freshness.

Conclusion

This Roasted Red Pepper Pasta is a delightful combination of smoky, sweet, and savory flavors that will satisfy your craving for a unique and delicious pasta dish. Whether you’re a fan of roasted red peppers or simply looking to try something new, this recipe is sure to become a favorite in your household. The creamy sauce, infused with the essence of roasted red peppers, pairs perfectly with the pasta to create a truly memorable dining experience. Give this recipe a try and discover the delightful world of smoky and flavorful pasta options. Enjoy!
