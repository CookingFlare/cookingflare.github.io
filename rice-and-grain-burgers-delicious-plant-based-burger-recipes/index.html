Rice and Grain Burgers: Delicious Plant-Based Burger Recipes


Are you looking for a tasty and nutritious plant-based alternative to traditional burgers? Look no further! Rice and grain burgers are a fantastic option that will satisfy your cravings and leave you feeling satisfied. These burgers are not only delicious but also packed with vitamins, minerals, and fiber. Plus, they are incredibly versatile, allowing you to experiment with different flavors and textures. Whether you’re a seasoned vegan or just looking to incorporate more plant-based meals into your diet, these rice and grain burgers are sure to impress.

Ingredients

For the burger patties, you will need:

  • 1 cup cooked rice (white or brown)
  • 1 cup cooked grains (quinoa, millet, or bulgur)
  • 1 cup cooked lentils (or canned lentils, drained and rinsed)
  • 1/2 cup breadcrumbs (gluten-free if desired)
  • 1/4 cup finely chopped onion
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon ground flaxseed mixed with 3 tablespoons water (flax egg)
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce or tamari
  • 1 teaspoon smoked paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon cumin
  • Salt and pepper to taste

For the burger toppings, you can use:

  • Burger buns (whole wheat or gluten-free)
  • Lettuce leaves
  • Sliced tomatoes
  • Sliced red onion
  • Avocado slices
  • Vegan mayonnaise
  • Mustard
  • Ketchup

Instructions

  1. In a large mixing bowl, combine the cooked rice, grains, lentils, breadcrumbs, onion, garlic, flax egg, soy sauce or tamari, smoked paprika, cumin, salt, and pepper. Mix well until all the ingredients are evenly distributed.

  2. Using your hands, form the mixture into burger patties of your desired size and thickness. If the mixture is too wet, add more breadcrumbs to bind it together. Place the patties on a plate or baking sheet lined with parchment paper.

  3. Preheat a non-stick skillet or grill pan over medium heat. Lightly grease the surface with oil or cooking spray.

  4. Carefully transfer the burger patties to the preheated skillet or grill pan. Cook for approximately 4-5 minutes on each side, or until golden brown and heated through. You can also grill the patties on an outdoor grill if preferred.

  5. While the burgers are cooking, prepare your desired toppings. Toast the burger buns if desired.

  6. Once the patties are cooked, assemble your rice and grain burgers. Place a patty on the bottom half of each bun, and top with lettuce, tomato slices, red onion slices, avocado slices, vegan mayonnaise, mustard, and ketchup. Feel free to customize your burgers with additional toppings or sauces of your choice.

  7. Serve the rice and grain burgers immediately and enjoy!

Tips and Variations

  • Experiment with different grains and legumes to create unique flavor combinations. Try using quinoa, bulgur, or even chickpeas instead of lentils.
  • Add chopped herbs, such as parsley or cilantro, to the burger mixture for an extra burst of freshness.
  • For a gluten-free option, use gluten-free breadcrumbs or substitute them with almond flour or chickpea flour.
  • If you prefer a firmer texture, refrigerate the burger patties for 30 minutes before cooking.
  • These burgers can be made in advance and stored in the refrigerator for up to 3 days. Simply reheat them in a skillet or microwave before serving.

Final Thoughts

Rice and grain burgers are not only a delicious and healthy alternative to traditional burgers, but they also provide an opportunity to explore new flavors and textures. With a wide range of ingredients and toppings to choose from, you can easily customize these burgers to suit your preferences. Whether you’re a seasoned vegan or simply looking to incorporate more plant-based meals into your diet, these rice and grain burgers are a fantastic choice. So grab your apron, gather your ingredients, and get ready to enjoy a mouthwatering plant-based burger like no other!
