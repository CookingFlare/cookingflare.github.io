Tasty Tart Recipes for a Refreshing Summer Dessert


Summer is the perfect time to enjoy light and refreshing desserts that satisfy your sweet tooth without weighing you down. Tarts are a classic choice for summer, with their buttery crusts and luscious fillings that burst with flavor. In this blog post, we’ll explore some delicious and easy-to-make tart recipes that are sure to impress your family and friends. So put on your apron and let’s get baking!

Lemon Blueberry Tart

Preparation time: 20 minutes
Cooking time: 30 minutes
Servings: 8

Ingredients:

  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, cold and cubed
  • 1/4 cup ice water
  • 3 large eggs
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/2 cup fresh lemon juice
  • Zest of 1 lemon
  • 1/2 cup fresh blueberries
  • Powdered sugar, for dusting

Instructions:

  1. In a food processor, combine the flour, sugar, and salt. Add the cold butter and pulse until the mixture resembles coarse crumbs. Gradually add the ice water and pulse until the dough comes together.
  2. Transfer the dough to a lightly floured surface and shape it into a disk. Wrap it in plastic wrap and refrigerate for 30 minutes.
  3. Preheat the oven to 375째F (190째C).
  4. Roll out the chilled dough on a lightly floured surface to fit a 9-inch tart pan. Press the dough into the pan and trim any excess.
  5. In a bowl, whisk together the eggs, sugar, lemon juice, and lemon zest until well combined.
  6. Sprinkle the blueberries evenly over the tart crust. Pour the lemon mixture over the blueberries.
  7. Bake for 30 minutes or until the filling is set and the crust is golden brown. Allow the tart to cool completely before dusting with powdered sugar.
  8. Serve chilled and enjoy!

Raspberry Almond Tart

Preparation time: 25 minutes
Cooking time: 40 minutes
Servings: 10

Ingredients:

  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, cold and cubed
  • 1/4 cup ice water
  • 1 cup almond flour
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 teaspoon almond extract
  • 1 cup fresh raspberries
  • Sliced almonds, for garnish
  • Confectioners’ sugar, for dusting

Instructions:

  1. In a food processor, combine the flour, sugar, and salt. Add the cold butter and pulse until the mixture resembles coarse crumbs. Gradually add the ice water and pulse until the dough comes together.
  2. Transfer the dough to a lightly floured surface and shape it into a disk. Wrap it in plastic wrap and refrigerate for 30 minutes.
  3. Preheat the oven to 375째F (190째C).
  4. Roll out the chilled dough on a lightly floured surface to fit a 9-inch tart pan. Press the dough into the pan and trim any excess.
  5. In a bowl, cream together the almond flour, sugar, softened butter, eggs, and almond extract until smooth and creamy.
  6. Spread the almond mixture evenly over the tart crust. Arrange the fresh raspberries on top.
  7. Sprinkle sliced almonds over the raspberries for added crunch and visual appeal.
  8. Bake for 40 minutes or until the filling is set and the crust is golden brown. Allow the tart to cool completely before dusting with confectioners’ sugar.
  9. Slice and serve this delightful tart to impress your guests!

Mango Coconut Tart

Preparation time: 15 minutes
Chilling time: 2 hours
Servings: 6

Ingredients:

  • 1 1/2 cups graham cracker crumbs
  • 1/4 cup unsalted butter, melted
  • 2 cups diced mangoes
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 tablespoon cornstarch
  • 1/2 cup coconut milk
  • 1/2 cup shredded coconut
  • Whipped cream, for garnish

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, combine the graham cracker crumbs and melted butter until well combined. Press the mixture into the bottom of a 9-inch tart pan to form the crust. Chill in the refrigerator for 30 minutes.
  2. In a saucepan, combine the diced mangoes, sugar, cornstarch, and coconut milk. Cook over medium heat, stirring constantly, until the mixture thickens.
  3. Remove the mango mixture from heat and let it cool slightly. Pour the mango filling into the chilled crust and spread it evenly.
  4. Sprinkle shredded coconut over the mango filling for an extra tropical touch.
  5. Return the tart to the refrigerator and chill for at least 2 hours or until set.
  6. Before serving, garnish with a dollop of whipped cream and enjoy the tropical flavors!

Conclusion

These tasty tart recipes offer a delightful way to enjoy the flavors of summer. Whether you prefer the tangy combination of lemon and blueberries, the nutty goodness of almonds and raspberries, or the tropical fusion of mango and coconut, there’s a tart for everyone’s taste buds. So why not make the most of the sunny season and indulge in these refreshing desserts? Try out these recipes and let your taste buds embark on a delicious summer adventure!
