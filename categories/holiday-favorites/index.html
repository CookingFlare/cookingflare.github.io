In a bowl, mix the cranberry juice, orange marmalade, brown sugar, Dijon mustard, and ground cloves. Pour the mixture over the ham. Close the Instant Pot and set it to high pressure for 15 minutes. Once done, allow the pressure to release naturally for 10 minutes before quick-releasing the remaining pressure. Transfer the ham to a serving platter, then pour the glaze from the Instant Pot over the ham before serving.
