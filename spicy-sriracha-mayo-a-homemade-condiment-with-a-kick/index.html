Spicy Sriracha Mayo: A Homemade Condiment with a Kick


If you’re a fan of adding a little heat and flavor to your meals, then Spicy Sriracha Mayo is the perfect condiment for you. This homemade sauce combines the creamy richness of mayonnaise with the bold punch of Sriracha, creating a versatile and delectable spread that can elevate any dish. Whether you’re a seasoned home cook or just starting out in the kitchen, making your own Spicy Sriracha Mayo is a simple and rewarding endeavor. In this blog post, we’ll explore the origins of Sriracha, delve into the key ingredients for making this spicy mayo, and provide you with a step-by-step recipe to create your own batch at home.

The Origin of Sriracha

Sriracha, a type of hot sauce or chili sauce made from a paste of chili peppers, distilled vinegar, garlic, sugar, and salt, originated from Thailand. The name “Sriracha” actually comes from Si Racha, a coastal town in Thailand. This iconic sauce has gained popularity around the world for its unique blend of heat and sweetness, making it a beloved condiment for many. Its versatility and ability to enhance a wide variety of dishes have made it a staple in kitchens globally.

Key Ingredients

To make your own Spicy Sriracha Mayo, you’ll need just a few simple ingredients:

  • Mayonnaise: Opt for a good-quality mayonnaise that will provide the creamy base for the sauce.
  • Sriracha: The star of the show, Sriracha brings the heat and distinct flavor to the mayo.
  • Lemon Juice: Adding a splash of fresh lemon juice can help balance the richness of the mayo and the spiciness of the Sriracha.
  • Garlic Powder: This adds an extra layer of savory flavor to the mayo.
  • Salt and Pepper: To taste, to enhance the overall flavor profile.

Recipe: Homemade Spicy Sriracha Mayo

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup mayonnaise
  • 2-3 tablespoons Sriracha
  • 1 tablespoon fresh lemon juice
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a mixing bowl, combine the mayonnaise, Sriracha, fresh lemon juice, and garlic powder.
  2. Whisk the ingredients together until the Sriracha is fully incorporated into the mayonnaise, and the mixture is smooth and uniform in color.
  3. Taste the Spicy Sriracha Mayo and season with salt and pepper according to your preference.
  4. Once seasoned to your liking, transfer the mayo to a clean, airtight container, and refrigerate for at least 30 minutes to allow the flavors to meld together.

Serving Suggestions:

  • Use Spicy Sriracha Mayo as a dip for fries, sweet potato fries, or onion rings.
  • Spread it on sandwiches, burgers, or wraps for an extra kick.
  • Drizzle it over grilled meats, seafood, or roasted vegetables for a spicy flavor boost.

Conclusion

There you have it – a simple yet flavorful recipe for making your own Spicy Sriracha Mayo at home. This versatile condiment is sure to become a favorite in your kitchen, adding a delightful kick to a wide range of dishes. Experiment with the ingredient proportions to create a custom blend that suits your taste preferences. With just a few pantry staples, you can whip up a batch of this zesty mayo and take your culinary creations to the next level. So, get ready to spice up your meals with this homemade condiment that packs a punch!
