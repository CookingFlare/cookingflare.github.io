Berry Mojito Ingredients: 6 fresh strawberries Handful of fresh blueberries 10 fresh mint leaves 2 teaspoons of sugar 2 ounces of white rum 1 ounce of freshly squeezed lime juice Ice cubes Soda water Additional strawberries and mint leaves for garnish Instructions: In a cocktail shaker, muddle the strawberries, blueberries, mint leaves, and sugar until the berries release their juices and the mint becomes fragrant. Add the white rum and lime juice to the shaker, then fill it with ice cubes. Shake the mixture well until chilled. Strain the mixture into a glass filled with ice. Top off the glass with soda water and gently stir. Garnish with a strawberry and a sprig of mint.
