Instant Pot Chicken Tikka Masala: Spicy and Creamy


If you’re a fan of Indian cuisine, then you’re in for a treat with this Instant Pot Chicken Tikka Masala recipe. It’s a dish that’s bursting with flavor, combining tender chicken with a rich, spicy, and creamy tomato-based sauce. Plus, the best part is that you can whip up this culinary delight in a fraction of the time, thanks to the magic of the Instant Pot.

Ingredients

Before we dive into the cooking process, let’s take a look at the ingredients you’ll need to prepare this mouthwatering dish:

  • 2 pounds of boneless, skinless chicken thighs, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 1 cup of plain yogurt
  • 4 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon of grated fresh ginger
  • 2 teaspoons of ground cumin
  • 2 teaspoons of paprika
  • 2 teaspoons of garam masala
  • 1 teaspoon of ground turmeric
  • 1 teaspoon of ground coriander
  • 1/2 teaspoon of cayenne pepper
  • 1/2 teaspoon of ground cinnamon
  • 1/2 teaspoon of salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon of black pepper
  • 1 tablespoon of olive oil
  • 1 large onion, finely chopped
  • 1 can (14 oz) of crushed tomatoes
  • 1 cup of heavy cream
  • Fresh cilantro, for garnish
  • Cooked rice, for serving

Instructions

Marinating the Chicken

  1. In a bowl, combine the plain yogurt, minced garlic, grated ginger, ground cumin, paprika, garam masala, ground turmeric, ground coriander, cayenne pepper, ground cinnamon, salt, and black pepper.
  2. Add the bite-sized chicken pieces to the bowl and ensure they are well coated with the marinade.
  3. Cover the bowl and refrigerate for at least 1 hour, allowing the flavors to meld and infuse the chicken.

Cooking in the Instant Pot

  1. Set the Instant Pot to the sauté function and heat the olive oil.
  2. Add the finely chopped onion to the pot and sauté until it becomes soft and translucent.
  3. Stir in the marinated chicken and cook for about 5 minutes, allowing it to brown slightly.

Adding the Sauce

  1. Pour in the crushed tomatoes, stirring to combine with the chicken and onion.
  2. Secure the lid on the Instant Pot and set the vent to the sealing position.
  3. Cook on high pressure for 10 minutes, then allow for a natural pressure release for 10 minutes. After that, perform a quick pressure release.

Finishing Touches

  1. Stir in the heavy cream, ensuring it’s well incorporated into the sauce.
  2. If desired, adjust the seasoning with salt and pepper to taste.
  3. Serve the Instant Pot Chicken Tikka Masala over cooked rice and garnish with fresh cilantro.

Final Thoughts

The result? A tantalizing fusion of tender chicken in a lusciously creamy and aromatic sauce that will have your taste buds doing a happy dance. The beauty of using the Instant Pot for this recipe is that it not only cuts down the cooking time significantly but also allows the flavors to meld together beautifully.

So, the next time you’re craving a hearty and flavorful meal, consider giving this Instant Pot Chicken Tikka Masala recipe a try. It’s a delightful way to bring the exotic flavors of Indian cuisine right to your dinner table, with minimal effort and maximum satisfaction. Enjoy!
