Sweet and Savory Crepe Recipes for Kids


If you’re looking for a fun and delicious way to get your kids involved in the kitchen, sweet and savory crepes are the perfect option! Crepes are thin, delicate pancakes that can be filled with a variety of sweet or savory fillings, making them a versatile and exciting dish for kids to enjoy. Whether you’re planning a special weekend breakfast or a creative lunch, these crepe recipes are sure to be a hit with the little ones.

Classic Sweet Crepes

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 1/2 cups milk
  • 2 tablespoons melted butter
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Instructions:

  1. In a large mixing bowl, whisk together the flour, eggs, milk, melted butter, salt, sugar, and vanilla extract until smooth.
  2. Heat a lightly greased non-stick pan over medium heat. Pour a small amount of batter into the pan and swirl to spread evenly.
  3. Cook the crepe for about 2 minutes, until the bottom is light golden brown. Flip and cook the other side for 1-2 minutes.
  4. Fill the crepes with your kids’ favorite sweet fillings such as fresh berries, whipped cream, chocolate spread, or sliced bananas.

Ham and Cheese Savory Crepes

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 1/2 cups milk
  • 2 tablespoons melted butter
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup grated cheddar cheese
  • 1/2 cup diced ham
  • 1/4 cup chopped green onions

Instructions:

  1. Follow the same instructions as the sweet crepes to prepare the crepe batter.
  2. Once the crepes are cooked, sprinkle grated cheddar cheese, diced ham, and chopped green onions on one half of the crepe.
  3. Fold the crepe in half to enclose the filling and cook for an additional minute to melt the cheese.

Strawberry and Nutella Crepe Roll-Ups

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 1/2 cups milk
  • 2 tablespoons melted butter
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • Sliced strawberries
  • Nutella

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the sweet crepe batter according to the classic sweet crepe instructions.
  2. Once the crepes are cooked, spread a thin layer of Nutella on each crepe and place sliced strawberries in the center.
  3. Roll the crepes into a tight cylinder and slice into bite-sized pieces for a fun and easy-to-eat treat.

Veggie and Cream Cheese Crepe Wraps

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 1/2 cups milk
  • 2 tablespoons melted butter
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup cream cheese
  • Thinly sliced cucumbers
  • Thinly sliced bell peppers
  • Baby spinach leaves

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the sweet crepe batter according to the classic sweet crepe instructions.
  2. Once the crepes are cooked, spread a thin layer of cream cheese on each crepe and place thinly sliced cucumbers, bell peppers, and baby spinach leaves on one half of the crepe.
  3. Fold the crepe in half to create a wrap and cut into smaller portions for a nutritious and delicious snack.

Conclusion

Crepes are a delightful canvas for culinary creativity, and involving kids in the process of making and filling them can be a fun and educational experience. Whether it’s a special occasion or a regular mealtime, these sweet and savory crepe recipes are sure to bring joy to your kids and the whole family. So, gather your little chefs and let the crepe-making adventure begin!
