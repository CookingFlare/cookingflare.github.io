Heartfelt Valentine's Day Quiche: Quiche Lorraine Recipe for Two


Valentine’s Day is just around the corner, and what better way to celebrate than with a delicious homemade meal? This Heartfelt Valentine’s Day Quiche: Quiche Lorraine Recipe for Two is the perfect way to show your love through the joy of cooking. This classic French dish is not only indulgent and flavorful but also simple to prepare. Whether you’re planning a romantic breakfast in bed or a cozy dinner for two, this quiche is sure to impress your special someone.

Ingredients

For the Crust:

  • 1 1/4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup (1 stick) cold unsalted butter, cut into small cubes
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 cup ice water

For the Filling:

  • 6 slices bacon, cooked until crispy and crumbled
  • 1 cup shredded Gruyère cheese
  • 1/2 cup chopped shallots
  • 4 large eggs
  • 1 1/2 cups heavy cream
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • A pinch of nutmeg

Instructions

Step 1: Prepare the Crust

  1. In a large bowl, combine the flour and salt. Add the cold butter cubes and use a pastry cutter or fork to cut the butter into the flour until the mixture resembles coarse crumbs.
  2. Gradually add the ice water, a tablespoon at a time, mixing gently until the dough comes together.
  3. Shape the dough into a disk, wrap it in plastic wrap, and refrigerate for at least 30 minutes.

Step 2: Preheat the Oven

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).

Step 3: Roll Out the Dough

  1. On a floured surface, roll out the chilled dough into a 12-inch circle. Carefully transfer the dough to a 9-inch pie dish, gently pressing it into the bottom and up the sides. Trim any excess dough and crimp the edges as desired.

Step 4: Prepare the Filling

  1. In a medium bowl, whisk together the eggs, heavy cream, salt, pepper, and nutmeg until well combined.
  2. Sprinkle the bacon, Gruyère cheese, and shallots evenly over the bottom of the prepared crust.
  3. Pour the egg mixture over the filling ingredients.

Step 5: Bake the Quiche

  1. Place the quiche on a baking sheet and bake in the preheated oven for 45-50 minutes, or until the filling is set and the crust is golden brown.
  2. Allow the quiche to cool for a few minutes before slicing and serving.

Serving Suggestions

This Heartfelt Valentine’s Day Quiche: Quiche Lorraine Recipe for Two pairs beautifully with a crisp green salad dressed with a light vinaigrette. For a touch of elegance, consider serving with a side of fresh fruit and a glass of sparkling wine or a mimosa. Whether enjoyed for breakfast, brunch, or dinner, this quiche is a delightful way to celebrate love and good food with your significant other.

Final Thoughts

There’s something undeniably charming about a homemade meal, especially when it’s made with love. This Heartfelt Valentine’s Day Quiche: Quiche Lorraine Recipe for Two is a wonderful way to express your affection through the art of cooking. So, this Valentine’s Day, take the time to create something special for the one you love and savor the joy of sharing a delightful meal together. Cheers to love, laughter, and delicious food!
