Vegan Take 5: 20 Exciting Plant-Based Recipes for Busy Weeknights


Get ready for a burst of flavors and a healthy dose of satisfaction with these 20 delectable plant-based recipes. Perfect for busy weeknights, each of these dishes packs in all the goodness of whole ingredients without sacrificing on taste. Say goodbye to soggy salads and boring vegetarian options, because the recipes we’ve gathered for you are anything but basic. Let’s dive in and discover a world of vegan cooking that even non-vegetarians will love!

  1. Southwestern Sweet Potato Skillet A one-pan wonder that combines the nutty flavor of sweet potatoes, the sass of black beans, and the zest of corn! Simply saut√© onion, garlic, and spices, then stir in the sweet potatoes, black beans, and corn to finish. Garnish with fresh cilantro and enjoy a flavorful and hearty meal in less than 30 minutes!

  2. Quinoa Stuffed Bell Peppers Who said stuffed peppers weren’t vegan-friendly? Load up bell peppers with healthy quinoa, colorful veggies, and exotic spices, bake them to perfection, and witness your taste buds dance to the tune of this delightful meal. Top them with fresh cilantro and lime for an added burst of flavors.

  3. Zucchini Noodles with Avocado Pesto Who wouldn’t want to sink their teeth into zoodles coated in rich, creamy avocado pesto? It’s the perfect replacement for traditional pasta dishes. Easy to make and packed with nutrients, this dish will surely become a staple in your weeknight dinner rotation.

  4. Chickpea Curry with Coconut Rice This protein-packed curry is easy to throw together and undoubtedly satisfying. Serve it with fluffy coconut rice for a complete meal that will transport you to the verdant landscapes of India. Don’t forget to garnish it with fresh cilantro and lime.

  5. Vegan Sushi Rolls Who says sushi is off-limits? Make stunning vegan sushi rolls filled with greens, avocado, cucumber, and bell peppers. Impress your friends or family with a meal that looks fancy but is easy to make with just a few ingredients.

  6. Lentil Shepherd’s Pie This hearty, meatless twist on a classic comfort food is sure to satisfy. Pile nutrient-rich lentils on top of a creamy cauliflower potato mash and bake until golden. A sprinkle of smoked paprika on top adds a touch of delicious smokiness, making it a true winner.

  7. Vegan Potato Salad Leave the mayo behind and embrace this creamy, dairy-free potato salad infused with Greek yogurt, Dijon mustard, and fresh dill. It’s an indulgent version of a summertime favorite that’ll make any barbecue more flavorful.

  8. Roasted Beet and Quinoa Salad Give your palate a delightful makeover with roasted beets and earthy quinoa mingling together to create a memorable Mediterranean salad. Add zing with a lemon-tahini dressing and throw in some fresh herbs to truly make it shine.

  9. Chickpea Pancakes You heard it right! Chickpea flour makes for a scrumptious pancake base that is high in protein and free of gluten. Top them with a variety of colorful veggies, herbs, and spices, and enjoy a filling and satisfying breakfast or brunch meal.

  10. Vegan Gazpacho This summery cold soup is a breeze to make and incredibly refreshing on hot days. Chock-full of juicy tomatoes, zesty cucumbers, and spicy jalapenos, this Spanish classic will have your taste buds dancing in delight.

  11. Jackfruit Carnitas Jackfruit, when shredded and slow-cooked with spices, comes amazingly close to the texture of pulled pork. Use it to make vegan carnitas tacos, or layer it onto a plant-based burrito for a hearty and wholesome meal.

  12. Vegan Stuffed Mushrooms Bring together an appetizing medley of textures in these bite-sized treats. Combination of breadcrumbs, spinach, and aromatic herbs stuffed into juicy mushrooms make for a delectable party starter that vegetarians and non-vegetarians alike will relish.

  13. Spicy Vegan Ramen Whip up an extraordinary bowl of ramen brimming with fiery chili oil, soy-marinated eggs (substitute withalfredoblack beans for a vegan version), and fresh greens. The perfect way to beat the monotony of everyday meals!

  14. Roasted Vegetable and Hummus Wraps Load up your whole-grain tortilla with a mixture of your favorite roasted veggies and zesty hummus. It’s a wrap that’s as nutritious as it is delicious.

  15. Vegan Alfredo Embrace the creamy, dreamy goodness of a vegan alfredo sauce made from cauliflower and nutritional yeast. Pour this over your favorite pasta and watch it become an instant classic.

  16. Butternut Squash Soup Indulge in a comforting bowl of velvety butternut squash soup, made luxurious with coconut milk and fragrant with the warmth of nutmeg and cloves. Perfect for those chilly evenings.

  17. Vegan Lasagna No more whines about missing out on lasagna! This version is just as toothsome, boasting layers of tomatoey sauce, veggie-packed ricotta, and tender noodles. Garnish it with fresh basil for the ultimate experience.

  18. Lentil Meatballs A protein-packed alternative to the classic meatball, these scrumptious lentil meatballs are made even richer with walnuts and breadcrumbs for that indulgent crunch.

  19. Vegan Quesadillas Still pining after cheese-filled quesadillas? Try this vegan version filled with gooey coconut smothered in your favorite veggies. They’re made on the streets of Mexico for a reason - they’r’e just that good!

  20. Vegan Chocolate Ice Cream End your meal with a sweet twist of dairy-free classic: chocolate ice cream made from bananas and almonds. The result is a rich and creamy indulgence that no one could guess is completely plant-based.

These 20 recipes promise to add some excitement to your busy weeknights, all while eating wholesome and delicious plant-based meals. Don’t let going vegan be a lonely road any longer - with these back-pocket staples, you’ll be raring to explore the vast cuisine this incredible diet has to offer. So, gear up to pamper your palate with these lip-smacking vegan delights and transform your cuisine game for the better!
