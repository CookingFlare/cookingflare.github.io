100 Ketofied Classics: Transform Your All-Time Favorite Recipes into Amazing Keto Meals


Keto enthusiasts around the globe rejoice! Introducing one hundred amazing keto classics that promise to transform your all-time favorite recipes into brilliant low-carb, high-fat masterpieces. No more feeling deprived or left out on your delicious culinary adventures. We’re here to help you explore the delightful world of keto-friendly dishes that will have you singing ‘hallelujah’ at your dinner table.

  1. Classic Egg Fried Rice Transform your Chinese takeout favorite into a fluffy, flavorsome keto meal with this delightful recipe.

Ingredients for four servings:

  • 2 tbsp avocado or coconut oil
  • 1 onion, chopped finely
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup cauliflower rice
  • 2 large eggs, beaten
  • 1/2 cup snow peas
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp sesame oil
  • 2 green onions for topping
  1. Heat oil in a large frying pan over medium heat. Sauté the onion and garlic till fragrant.
  2. Add cauliflower rice, cooking for 3-5 minutes until slightly softened.
  3. Push cauliflower rice down and pour the beaten eggs over the pan. Use a spatula to scramble the eggs into the rice mixture.
  4. Stir in snow peas and soy sauce. Cook for 1-2 minutes.
  5. Mix in sesame oil and green onions. Serve immediately.

  1. Shepherd’s Pie This beloved classic dish takes on a new, keto-friendly twist and never fails to please.

Ingredients for six servings:

  • 1 lb ground lamb (can use ground beef)
  • 1 handful mushrooms, chopped
  • 1 cup frozen peas
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup beef broth
  • 2 tbsp tomato paste
  • 1 tbsp Worcestershire sauce
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 3 cups mashed cauliflower (made from 1 head of cauliflower)
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  1. Preheat oven to 400°F (200°C).
  2. In a large skillet, cook lamb or beef till browned. Remove and set aside. Sauté mushrooms, onion, garlic, and lamb/beef in the skillet.
  3. Pour in beef broth, tomato paste, and Worcestershire sauce. Season with salt and pepper. Simmer for 2-3 minutes.
  4. Stir in peas and mix well with the meat mixture. Remove from heat and transfer to a baking dish.
  5. In a separate saucepan, make mashed cauliflower by boiling cauliflower florets for 15 minutes, then draining and mashing with heavy cream and salt to taste.
  6. Spread mashed cauliflower over the meat mixture, creating an even layer.
  7. Bake in the oven for 20-25 minutes or until the top turns golden brown. Serve immediately.

  1. Crispy Fish Tacos Serve a memorable low-carb feast with this delicious, innovative keto fish taco recipe!

Ingredients for four servings:

  • 1/2 lb white fish, like cod, cut into small strips
  • 1/2 avocado, sliced
  • 1/2 red cabbage, shredded
  • 1/2 jalapeno, chopped
  • Sour cream, for topping
  • 1/2 lime, sliced
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Siete Grain Free Tortillas
  1. Season fish with salt, pepper, and olive oil. Cook in a skillet or grill pan over medium-high heat for 5-6 minutes, until the fish flakes easily with a fork. Remove from heat and flake the fish into pieces.
  2. Warm Siete Grain Free Tortillas in the microwave or oven according to package instructions.
  3. Distribute fish strips evenly on tortillas. Top with avocado, cabbage, jalapeño, sour cream, and a wedge of lime. Serve immediately.
