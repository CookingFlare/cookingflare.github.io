Singapore Noodles Recipe with Shrimp and Vegetables


If you’re craving a taste of Singapore but can’t hop on a plane just yet, this Singapore Noodles recipe with shrimp and vegetables is the perfect way to bring the flavors of the vibrant city-state right into your kitchen. Bursting with a harmonious blend of spices and textures, this dish is sure to transport you to the bustling streets of Singapore.

Ingredients

  • 8 ounces rice vermicelli noodles
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • 1/2 pound medium shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 small onion, thinly sliced
  • 1 red bell pepper, thinly sliced
  • 1 carrot, julienned
  • 1 cup bean sprouts
  • 2 green onions, sliced
  • 2 tablespoons curry powder
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon oyster sauce
  • 1 tablespoon fish sauce
  • 1/2 teaspoon sugar
  • Fresh cilantro, for garnish

Instructions

  1. Prepare the rice vermicelli noodles according to the package instructions. Once cooked, rinse with cold water to stop the cooking process and set aside.

  2. In a large skillet or wok, heat the vegetable oil over medium-high heat. Add the shrimp and cook until pink and cooked through, about 2-3 minutes per side. Remove the shrimp from the skillet and set aside.

  3. In the same skillet, add the minced garlic and sliced onion. Sauté until fragrant and the onion turns translucent, about 2-3 minutes.

  4. Add the red bell pepper and carrot to the skillet and cook for an additional 2-3 minutes, or until the vegetables start to soften.

  5. Push the vegetables to one side of the skillet and crack the eggs into the empty space. Scramble the eggs until cooked through, then mix them in with the vegetables.

  6. Add the cooked rice vermicelli noodles to the skillet, along with the bean sprouts and green onions. Toss everything together until well combined.

  7. In a small bowl, whisk together the curry powder, soy sauce, oyster sauce, fish sauce, and sugar. Pour the sauce over the noodles and vegetables, stirring well to evenly coat everything.

  8. Add the cooked shrimp back to the skillet and toss to combine. Cook for an additional 2-3 minutes, or until everything is heated through.

  9. Remove the skillet from the heat and garnish with fresh cilantro.

Enjoy the Flavors of Singapore

Now that you have mastered this Singapore Noodles recipe with shrimp and vegetables, take a moment to savor the delightful blend of spices and textures that make this dish so special. The fragrant curry powder, the tanginess of the sauces, and the tender shrimp will transport your taste buds to the vibrant streets of Singapore.

Feel free to customize this recipe to your liking by adding other vegetables such as bok choy or mushrooms, or even adjusting the level of spiciness to suit your palate. Serve it as a main course or as a side dish alongside your favorite Asian-inspired dishes.

So, gather your ingredients, fire up your stove, and embark on a culinary adventure with this Singapore Noodles recipe. It’s time to bring the exotic flavors of Singapore right into your own home!
