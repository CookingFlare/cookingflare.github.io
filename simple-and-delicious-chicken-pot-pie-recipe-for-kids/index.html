Simple and Delicious Chicken Pot Pie Recipe for Kids


In today’s fast-paced world, finding a recipe that is both simple and delicious can be a challenge. But fear not, because I have the perfect solution for you: a Simple and Delicious Chicken Pot Pie Recipe for Kids! This recipe is not only easy to make, but it is also packed with flavors that will satisfy even the pickiest eaters. So, let’s get started!

Ingredients

For the Pie Crust:

  • 2 ½ cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup unsalted butter, chilled and cubed
  • 6-8 tablespoons ice water

For the Filling:

  • 2 cups cooked chicken, shredded
  • 1 cup mixed vegetables (carrots, peas, corn)
  • 1 medium onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons unsalted butter
  • 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • 1 ½ cups chicken broth
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C).
  2. In a large mixing bowl, combine the flour and salt for the pie crust. Add the cubed butter and use a pastry cutter or your hands to cut the butter into the flour until it resembles coarse crumbs.
  3. Gradually add the ice water, one tablespoon at a time, and mix until the dough comes together. Be careful not to overmix.
  4. Divide the dough in half and shape each half into a disk. Wrap them in plastic wrap and refrigerate for at least 30 minutes.
  5. In a large skillet, melt the butter over medium heat. Add the chopped onion and minced garlic, sautéing until they become translucent.
  6. Add the flour to the skillet and cook for another minute, stirring constantly.
  7. Slowly pour in the chicken broth and milk, stirring continuously until the mixture thickens.
  8. Add the shredded chicken, mixed vegetables, dried thyme, salt, and pepper to the skillet. Stir well to combine all the ingredients.
  9. Remove the skillet from heat and set aside.

Assembling the Pot Pie:

  1. Roll out one of the pie crust disks on a lightly floured surface until it is large enough to cover the bottom and sides of your pie dish.
  2. Carefully transfer the rolled-out crust to the pie dish, gently pressing it against the bottom and sides.
  3. Pour the chicken and vegetable filling into the pie dish, spreading it evenly.
  4. Roll out the second pie crust disk and place it on top of the filling. Trim the excess dough and crimp the edges to seal the pie.
  5. Use a sharp knife to cut a few slits on the top crust to allow steam to escape during baking.
  6. Place the pie dish on a baking sheet and bake in the preheated oven for 35-40 minutes, or until the crust is golden brown.

Serving and Enjoying:

  1. Remove the chicken pot pie from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes before serving.
  2. Slice the pie into portions and serve warm.
  3. This Simple and Delicious Chicken Pot Pie Recipe for Kids pairs well with a fresh green salad or steamed vegetables.

Conclusion

There you have it – a Simple and Delicious Chicken Pot Pie Recipe for Kids that will surely become a family favorite. With its buttery crust and flavorful filling, this recipe is sure to impress even the most discerning little taste buds. So, why not gather your little helpers and start cooking up some delicious memories together? Enjoy!

Remember to always supervise children in the kitchen and follow proper food safety guidelines.
