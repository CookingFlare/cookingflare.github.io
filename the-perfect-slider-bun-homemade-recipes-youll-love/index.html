The Perfect Slider Bun: Homemade Recipes You'll Love


When it comes to creating the perfect slider, one crucial element often gets overlooked—the bun. A slider bun should be soft, flavorful, and able to hold up to the juiciest of fillings. While store-bought buns can do the job, nothing beats the satisfaction of sinking your teeth into a homemade slider bun. In this post, we’ll share three delicious recipes for slider buns that will elevate your sliders to a whole new level.

Classic Slider Bun

Ingredients:

  • 3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • 2 teaspoons active dry yeast
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup warm milk
  • 2 tablespoons unsalted butter, melted
  • 1 egg, beaten
  • Sesame seeds (optional)

Instructions:

  1. In a large mixing bowl, combine the flour, sugar, yeast, and salt.

  2. In a separate bowl, mix together the warm milk, melted butter, and beaten egg.

  3. Gradually pour the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients, stirring until a dough forms.

  4. Transfer the dough to a lightly floured surface and knead for about 5 minutes, or until smooth and elastic.

  5. Place the dough in a greased bowl, cover with a kitchen towel, and let it rise in a warm place for about 1 hour, or until doubled in size.

  6. Punch down the dough and divide it into 12 equal portions. Shape each portion into a small ball and flatten slightly.

  7. Place the buns on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper, leaving enough space between them for expansion. Cover with a kitchen towel and let them rise for another 30 minutes.

  8. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).

  9. If desired, brush the buns with beaten egg and sprinkle sesame seeds on top.

  10. Bake for 12-15 minutes, or until the buns are golden brown.

  11. Remove from the oven and let them cool on a wire rack.

Whole Wheat Slider Bun

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups whole wheat flour
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons honey
  • 1 tablespoon active dry yeast
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup warm water
  • 3 tablespoons olive oil

Instructions:

  1. In a large mixing bowl, combine the whole wheat flour, all-purpose flour, honey, yeast, and salt.

  2. Gradually add the warm water and olive oil, stirring until a dough forms.

  3. Transfer the dough to a lightly floured surface and knead for about 5 minutes, or until smooth and elastic.

  4. Place the dough in a greased bowl, cover with a kitchen towel, and let it rise in a warm place for about 1 hour, or until doubled in size.

  5. Punch down the dough and divide it into 12 equal portions. Shape each portion into a small ball and flatten slightly.

  6. Place the buns on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper, leaving enough space between them for expansion. Cover with a kitchen towel and let them rise for another 30 minutes.

  7. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).

  8. Bake for 12-15 minutes, or until the buns are golden brown.

  9. Remove from the oven and let them cool on a wire rack.

Brioche Slider Bun

Ingredients:

  • 3 cups bread flour
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • 1 tablespoon active dry yeast
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 3 eggs, beaten
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, softened

Instructions:

  1. In a large mixing bowl, combine the bread flour, sugar, yeast, and salt.

  2. Gradually add the beaten eggs and softened butter, stirring until a sticky dough forms.

  3. Transfer the dough to a lightly floured surface and knead for about 10 minutes, or until smooth and elastic.

  4. Place the dough in a greased bowl, cover with a kitchen towel, and let it rise in a warm place for about 2 hours, or until doubled in size.

  5. Punch down the dough and divide it into 12 equal portions. Shape each portion into a small ball and flatten slightly.

  6. Place the buns on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper, leaving enough space between them for expansion. Cover with a kitchen towel and let them rise for another 30 minutes.

  7. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).

  8. Bake for 12-15 minutes, or until the buns are golden brown.

  9. Remove from the oven and let them cool on a wire rack.

Now that you have three fantastic recipes for homemade slider buns, it’s time to get creative with your sliders. Whether you prefer classic cheeseburgers, pulled pork, or vegetarian options, these slider buns will provide the perfect foundation for your culinary creations. So go ahead, roll up your sleeves, and start baking. Your taste buds will thank you!
