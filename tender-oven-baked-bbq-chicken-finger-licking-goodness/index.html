Tender Oven-Baked BBQ Chicken: Finger-Licking Goodness


If you’re looking for a delicious and easy-to-make dish that will have your family and friends licking their fingers, then you’ve come to the right place. This tender oven-baked BBQ chicken recipe is a surefire crowd-pleaser, perfect for a casual weeknight dinner or a weekend barbecue with loved ones. The best part? It’s incredibly simple to prepare, requiring just a few basic ingredients and minimal effort. So, roll up your sleeves, preheat your oven, and get ready to indulge in some finger-licking goodness.

Ingredients:

  • 4 bone-in, skin-on chicken thighs
  • 4 bone-in, skin-on chicken drumsticks
  • 1 cup of your favorite BBQ sauce
  • 2 tablespoons of olive oil
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon of paprika
  • 1 teaspoon of onion powder
  • 1 teaspoon of dried oregano
  • Salt and black pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the Oven: Start by preheating your oven to 400°F (200°C). This will ensure that the chicken cooks evenly and achieves that perfect tender texture.

  2. Prepare the Chicken: While the oven is heating up, it’s time to prepare the star of the show – the chicken. Begin by rinsing the chicken thighs and drumsticks under cold water, then pat them dry with paper towels. Placing the chicken on a clean cutting board, season both sides generously with salt and black pepper. This step is crucial for infusing the meat with flavor and ensuring that each bite is as delicious as the last.

  3. Create the BBQ Marinade: In a small bowl, combine the BBQ sauce, olive oil, minced garlic, paprika, onion powder, and dried oregano. Mix everything together until the ingredients are well incorporated, forming a rich, flavorful marinade.

  4. Coat the Chicken: Next, generously coat each piece of chicken with the prepared BBQ marinade. Using your hands or a basting brush, make sure to cover the chicken evenly, ensuring that every nook and cranny is infused with the mouthwatering flavors of the sauce.

  5. Bake to Perfection: Place the marinated chicken pieces in a single layer on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper. This will prevent the chicken from sticking and make cleanup a breeze. Transfer the baking sheet to the preheated oven and bake for approximately 40-45 minutes, or until the chicken reaches an internal temperature of 165°F (74°C). As the chicken bakes, the aroma of the BBQ sauce will fill your kitchen, building anticipation for the feast to come.

  6. Serve and Enjoy: Once the chicken is perfectly cooked and has developed a beautiful golden-brown hue, remove it from the oven and allow it to rest for a few minutes. This resting period allows the juices to redistribute, ensuring that each succulent bite is bursting with flavor. Serve the tender oven-baked BBQ chicken alongside your favorite sides, such as coleslaw, corn on the cob, or a fresh garden salad.

Tips for Success:

  • Marinate Overnight: For an even more intense flavor, consider marinating the chicken in the BBQ sauce mixture overnight in the refrigerator. This extra time allows the flavors to penetrate the meat, resulting in an even more delectable dish.
  • Check for Doneness: To ensure that the chicken is thoroughly cooked, use a meat thermometer to check the internal temperature. The meat should reach 165°F (74°C) for safe consumption.
  • Broil for Crispy Skin: If you prefer a crispy exterior on your chicken, consider broiling it for the last 5 minutes of cooking. This will add a delightful crunch to each bite, elevating the overall experience.

In Conclusion

In just a few simple steps, you’ve created a mouthwatering masterpiece that is sure to become a staple in your recipe collection. This tender oven-baked BBQ chicken is a celebration of flavor, tenderness, and all-around goodness. Whether you’re hosting a summer cookout, a family gathering, or simply craving a comforting meal, this recipe is guaranteed to deliver satisfaction with every bite. So, gather your ingredients, fire up the oven, and treat yourself to the irresistible delight of homemade BBQ chicken.
