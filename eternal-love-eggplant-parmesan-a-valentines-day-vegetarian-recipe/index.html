Eternal Love Eggplant Parmesan: A Valentine's Day Vegetarian Recipe


Valentine’s Day is a time for expressing love, and what better way to do so than through the art of cooking? This Eternal Love Eggplant Parmesan recipe is a delightful vegetarian dish that embodies the spirit of love and togetherness. Whether you’re celebrating with a special someone, friends, or family, this hearty and flavorful dish is sure to win hearts and satisfy appetites.

Ingredients

  • 2 medium eggplants, sliced into 1/2-inch rounds
  • 2 cups of marinara sauce
  • 1 cup of grated mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup of grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 cup of all-purpose flour
  • 3 large eggs, beaten
  • 2 cups of breadcrumbs
  • 1 teaspoon of dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon of dried basil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Olive oil for frying

Preparation

Step 1: Preparing the Eggplant

  1. Start by preheating your oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Place the eggplant slices on a baking sheet and sprinkle them with salt. Let them sit for about 15 minutes to draw out excess moisture.
  3. After 15 minutes, pat the eggplant slices dry with paper towels to remove the salt and moisture.

Step 2: Breading the Eggplant

  1. Set up a breading station with three shallow dishes. Place the flour in one, beaten eggs in another, and breadcrumbs mixed with dried oregano, dried basil, salt, and pepper in the third dish.
  2. Dredge each eggplant slice in the flour, then dip it into the beaten eggs, and finally coat it with the breadcrumb mixture, pressing gently to adhere the breadcrumbs to the eggplant.

Step 3: Frying the Eggplant

  1. In a large skillet, heat about 1/4 inch of olive oil over medium-high heat.
  2. Working in batches, fry the breaded eggplant slices for 2-3 minutes on each side until golden brown.
  3. Place the fried eggplant slices on a paper towel-lined plate to drain any excess oil.

Step 4: Assembling the Dish

  1. Spread a thin layer of marinara sauce on the bottom of a baking dish.
  2. Arrange a layer of fried eggplant slices over the marinara sauce.
  3. Sprinkle a generous amount of mozzarella and Parmesan cheese over the eggplant layer.
  4. Repeat the layers of marinara sauce, eggplant, and cheese until all the ingredients are used, finishing with a layer of cheese on top.

Step 5: Baking

  1. Cover the baking dish with aluminum foil and bake in the preheated oven for 25 minutes.
  2. After 25 minutes, remove the foil and continue baking for an additional 10-15 minutes, or until the cheese is golden and bubbly.

Serving

Once the Eternal Love Eggplant Parmesan is ready, allow it to cool for a few minutes before slicing and serving. This hearty and aromatic dish pairs beautifully with a crisp green salad and a glass of your favorite wine.

This Valentine’s Day, celebrate love and togetherness with this Eternal Love Eggplant Parmesan recipe. Whether you’re sharing a romantic dinner for two or enjoying a cozy meal with friends and family, this vegetarian delight is a perfect way to express affection through the joy of cooking. The rich flavors and comforting textures of this dish are a true celebration of love that will leave a lasting impression on your loved ones.
