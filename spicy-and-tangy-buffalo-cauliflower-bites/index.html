Spicy and Tangy Buffalo Cauliflower Bites


If you’re a fan of buffalo wings, but you’re looking for a healthier, plant-based alternative, then you’re in for a treat with this recipe for Spicy and Tangy Buffalo Cauliflower Bites. These crispy, flavorful bites are the perfect appetizer, snack, or even a crowd-pleasing party dish. Packed with a kick of spice and a burst of tangy flavor, these cauliflower bites will have everyone reaching for more.

Ingredients

  • 1 head of cauliflower, cut into bite-sized florets
  • 1 cup of all-purpose flour (or chickpea flour for a gluten-free option)
  • 1 cup of water
  • 1 teaspoon of garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon of onion powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon of smoked paprika
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 cup of hot sauce
  • 2 tablespoons of melted butter (or vegan butter for a plant-based option)
  • 1 tablespoon of honey (or maple syrup for a vegan option)
  • 1 tablespoon of apple cider vinegar

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 450°F (230°C) and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.

  2. In a large bowl, whisk together the flour, water, garlic powder, onion powder, smoked paprika, salt, and pepper to create a smooth batter.

  3. Dip each cauliflower floret into the batter, making sure to coat it evenly, then place it on the prepared baking sheet. Repeat this process until all the cauliflower florets are coated.

  4. Bake the cauliflower in the preheated oven for 20-25 minutes, or until they are golden and crispy.

  5. While the cauliflower is baking, prepare the buffalo sauce. In a small saucepan, combine the hot sauce, melted butter, honey, and apple cider vinegar. Cook over medium heat, stirring occasionally, until the sauce begins to simmer. Once it’s simmering, remove it from the heat.

  6. Once the cauliflower is done, remove it from the oven and transfer it to a large bowl. Pour the buffalo sauce over the cauliflower and gently toss until each piece is coated with the spicy and tangy sauce.

  7. Return the coated cauliflower bites to the baking sheet and bake for an additional 10 minutes to allow the sauce to caramelize and the flavors to meld.

  8. Once done, remove the buffalo cauliflower bites from the oven and let them cool for a few minutes before serving.

Serving Suggestions

These Spicy and Tangy Buffalo Cauliflower Bites are delicious on their own, but you can take them to the next level by serving them with a side of creamy ranch or blue cheese dressing, along with some crisp celery and carrot sticks. They also make a great addition to a game day spread or as a tasty topping for salads and wraps.

Final Thoughts

Whether you’re a seasoned pro at cooking with cauliflower or just dipping your toes into the world of plant-based cooking, these buffalo cauliflower bites are sure to win over even the most skeptical of eaters. Their crispy texture, combined with the spicy and tangy flavor, make them an irresistible treat for any occasion. So, the next time you’re craving a spicy snack, give this recipe a try and watch as these buffalo cauliflower bites disappear in no time!
