Fiesta on a Plate: Mexican Street Corn Salad


If you’re looking for a dish that brings the fiesta to your plate, look no further than this vibrant and flavorful Mexican Street Corn Salad. This dish is a celebration of the bold and zesty flavors that are characteristic of Mexican cuisine, and it’s perfect for adding a burst of color and taste to any meal, whether it’s a casual family dinner or a lively gathering with friends.

Ingredients

To create this mouthwatering Mexican Street Corn Salad, you’ll need the following ingredients:

  • 6 ears of fresh corn, husks and silks removed
  • 1/2 cup mayonnaise
  • 1/2 cup sour cream
  • 1/2 cup finely chopped red onion
  • 1/2 cup finely chopped fresh cilantro
  • 1/2 cup crumbled cotija cheese
  • 1 teaspoon chili powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon smoked paprika
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • Juice of 1 lime
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

Step 1: Grilling the Corn

Start by preheating your grill to medium-high heat. Place the ears of corn directly on the grill grates and cook, turning occasionally, until the corn is charred in spots and tender, about 10 minutes. Once the corn is grilled to perfection, remove it from the grill and allow it to cool slightly.

Step 2: Preparing the Dressing

While the grilled corn is cooling, it’s time to prepare the creamy and zesty dressing that will bring all the flavors of this dish together. In a large mixing bowl, combine the mayonnaise, sour cream, minced garlic, lime juice, chili powder, smoked paprika, and salt and pepper. Mix the ingredients until they are well combined and set the dressing aside.

Step 3: Assembling the Salad

Once the grilled corn has cooled, carefully cut the kernels off the cobs and transfer them to a large serving bowl. Add the finely chopped red onion and fresh cilantro to the bowl, and then pour the prepared dressing over the corn mixture. Gently toss the ingredients together until the corn is evenly coated with the dressing.

Step 4: Adding the Final Touches

To complete the dish, sprinkle the crumbled cotija cheese over the salad. The salty and slightly tangy flavor of the cotija cheese adds a delightful contrast to the sweetness of the corn and the creaminess of the dressing. For a final burst of color and flavor, garnish the salad with a little extra chili powder and chopped cilantro.

Serving Suggestions

This Mexican Street Corn Salad is best served immediately, while the corn is still warm from the grill and the flavors are at their peak. It pairs beautifully with grilled meats such as chicken, steak, or shrimp, and it also makes a delightful topping for tacos or a filling for burritos. For a refreshing twist, consider serving the salad alongside a pitcher of icy cold margaritas or a fruity agua fresca.

In Conclusion

With its vibrant colors, bold flavors, and zesty kick, Mexican Street Corn Salad is the perfect dish to add a festive touch to any meal. Whether you’re hosting a summer barbecue, planning a Mexican-themed dinner party, or simply craving a taste of Mexico, this salad is sure to be a hit. So fire up the grill, gather your ingredients, and get ready to savor a fiesta on a plate with this delicious and satisfying dish.

¡Buen provecho!
