Scrumptious S'mores Cookie Bars: A Summer Classic


Welcome to our mouthwatering recipe for Scrumptious S’mores Cookie Bars! This delectable summer treat combines the irresistible flavors of graham crackers, gooey marshmallows, and rich chocolate into a convenient and delicious bar. Perfect for picnics, barbecues, or simply indulging in a sweet treat at home, these cookie bars will leave you craving more.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups crushed graham crackers
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/2 cup packed brown sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 cup chocolate chips
  • 2 cups mini marshmallows

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C) and line a 9x13-inch baking pan with parchment paper. This will make it easier to remove the bars once they are baked.

  2. In a large bowl, combine the crushed graham crackers, flour, baking powder, and salt. Mix well and set aside.

  3. In another bowl, cream together the softened butter, granulated sugar, and brown sugar until light and fluffy. This can be done with a hand mixer or by hand using a wooden spoon.

  4. Beat in the eggs, one at a time, until well combined. Stir in the vanilla extract.

  5. Gradually add the dry ingredients to the wet ingredients, mixing until just combined. Be careful not to overmix, as this can result in tough cookie bars.

  6. Fold in the chocolate chips, ensuring they are evenly distributed throughout the dough.

  7. Press half of the dough into the prepared baking pan, creating an even layer.

  8. Sprinkle the mini marshmallows evenly over the dough, covering the entire surface.

  9. Gently press the remaining dough on top of the marshmallows, covering them completely. This will create a sandwich-like effect, with marshmallows nestled between layers of cookie dough.

  10. Bake in the preheated oven for 25-30 minutes, or until the edges are golden brown and the center is set. The marshmallows will be gooey and slightly toasted.

  11. Remove from the oven and allow the cookie bars to cool completely in the pan before cutting them into squares. This will ensure that they hold their shape and are easy to handle.

  12. Serve these Scrumptious S’mores Cookie Bars at room temperature and enjoy every gooey, chocolatey bite!

Tips and Variations

  • For an extra burst of flavor, consider adding a handful of chopped nuts, such as walnuts or pecans, to the cookie dough.
  • If you prefer a more intense chocolate flavor, substitute dark chocolate chips for the regular chocolate chips.
  • To achieve a campfire-like taste, briefly broil the marshmallows on top of the cookie bars for a toasted effect. Keep a close eye on them to prevent burning.
  • These cookie bars can be stored in an airtight container at room temperature for up to 5 days. If you want to enjoy them warm, simply pop them in the microwave for a few seconds.

Enjoy the nostalgia of a classic campfire treat with a modern twist in these Scrumptious S’mores Cookie Bars. Whether you’re hosting a summer gathering or simply craving a sweet indulgence, these bars are sure to be a hit. So grab your ingredients, preheat the oven, and get ready to embark on a delicious baking adventure!
