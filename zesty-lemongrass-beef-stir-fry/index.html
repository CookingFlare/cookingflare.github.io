Zesty Lemongrass Beef Stir Fry


Craving a burst of flavors in your next meal? Look no further than this irresistible Zesty Lemongrass Beef Stir Fry recipe. Combining the bold and aromatic essence of lemongrass with tender beef and vibrant vegetables, this dish is a surefire way to elevate your culinary skills and impress your taste buds. Let’s dive into the recipe and discover how to create this delectable dish from your own kitchen!

Ingredients:

  • 500g beef, thinly sliced
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon oyster sauce
  • 1 tablespoon fish sauce
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 red bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 yellow bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 onion, sliced
  • 2 stalks lemongrass, white parts only, finely chopped
  • 1 teaspoon chili flakes
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh cilantro for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Marinate the Beef: In a bowl, combine the thinly sliced beef with soy sauce, oyster sauce, and fish sauce. Mix well to ensure the beef is evenly coated. Let it marinate for at least 30 minutes to allow the flavors to penetrate the meat.

  2. Prepare the Lemongrass: While the beef is marinating, finely chop the white parts of the lemongrass stalks. Lemongrass adds a unique citrusy flavor to the dish, enhancing its overall zesty profile.

  3. Sear the Beef: Heat vegetable oil in a wok or large skillet over medium-high heat. Add minced garlic and chopped lemongrass, and sauté until fragrant. Then, add the marinated beef and stir-fry until it is browned and cooked to your desired level of doneness.

  4. Add Vegetables: Incorporate sliced red and yellow bell peppers, along with onion, into the wok. Stir-fry the vegetables with the beef until they are tender yet still crisp, adding a pop of color and freshness to the dish.

  5. Season the Stir Fry: Sprinkle chili flakes over the stir fry for a hint of heat, and season with salt and pepper according to your taste preferences. Toss everything together to ensure the flavors are well combined.

  6. Garnish and Serve: Once the beef and vegetables are cooked to perfection, remove the wok from heat. Garnish the Zesty Lemongrass Beef Stir Fry with fresh cilantro leaves for a final touch of herbaceousness.

  7. Enjoy: Serve the stir fry hot over steamed rice or noodles, and savor the harmonious blend of savory beef, zesty lemongrass, and colorful vegetables in every mouthful.

With its vibrant colors, aromatic fragrance, and tantalizing flavors, this Zesty Lemongrass Beef Stir Fry is a culinary masterpiece that will delight your senses and leave you craving for more. Whether you’re cooking for yourself or entertaining guests, this recipe is sure to be a crowd-pleaser. Give it a try and experience the magic of Asian-inspired cuisine right in your own kitchen!

Remember, the key to a successful stir fry lies in fresh ingredients, precise cooking techniques, and a touch of creativity. Get ready to embark on a flavorful journey with this Zesty Lemongrass Beef Stir Fry recipe and elevate your cooking skills to new heights. Happy cooking!
