Delicious Honey Garlic Chicken Stir Fry: A Sweet and Tangy Treat


When it comes to cooking, there are few things more satisfying than creating a dish that is both sweet and savory. In this recipe, we’ll explore the world of honey garlic chicken stir fry, a dish that combines the rich flavors of honey and garlic with the tender juiciness of chicken. This sweet and tangy treat is sure to become a staple in your household, and with its ease of preparation, it’s perfect for a quick and delicious meal any time of the day.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound boneless, skinless chicken breasts, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons honey
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon grated ginger
  • 1/4 teaspoon red pepper flakes (optional)
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • 1 cup mixed vegetables (bell peppers, carrots, broccoli, etc.)
  • Cooked rice or noodles, for serving

Instructions

  1. In a small bowl, whisk together the honey, soy sauce, and olive oil until well combined.
  2. In a separate bowl, mix together the minced garlic, grated ginger, and red pepper flakes (if using).
  3. Add the chicken pieces to the bowl with the garlic mixture and toss to coat, ensuring the chicken is evenly coated with the garlic and ginger mixture.
  4. Heat a large skillet or wok over medium-high heat and add the chicken. Cook for 5-7 minutes, or until the chicken is cooked through and slightly charred.
  5. Remove the chicken from the skillet and set aside. In the same skillet, add the mixed vegetables and cook until they are tender-crisp, stirring occasionally.
  6. Pour the honey-soy sauce mixture into the skillet with the vegetables and stir to combine. Bring the mixture to a simmer and cook for 1-2 minutes, or until the sauce has thickened slightly.
  7. Add the cooked chicken back into the skillet and toss to coat with the sauce. Cook for an additional 1-2 minutes, or until the chicken is fully coated.
  8. Season the dish with salt and pepper to taste, then serve hot over cooked rice or noodles.

Tips and Variations

  • For an extra boost of flavor, add 1-2 tablespoons of chopped green onions or scallions to the dish before serving.
  • If you prefer a spicier dish, increase the amount of red pepper flakes or add in some diced jalapeños.
  • For a vegetarian option, substitute the chicken with tofu or tempeh and adjust the cooking time accordingly.
  • This dish can be made ahead of time and refrigerated for up to 24 hours before reheating. Simply reheat the dish in the skillet or in the microwave until warmed through.

Conclusion

This honey garlic chicken stir fry is a delicious and easy-to-make dish that is perfect for any occasion. With its sweet and tangy flavors, it’s sure to become a family favorite. Whether you’re looking for a quick weeknight dinner or a special occasion meal, this recipe is sure to impress. So go ahead, give it a try, and enjoy the sweet and savory goodness of this honey garlic chicken stir fry!
