Cinco de Vegan-tos: 20 Flavor-Packed Mexican-Style Recipes for Summer Tastings


Get ready to kick your taste buds into overdrive with these amazing Mexican-inspired recipes that cater to the vegan lifestyle. As the summer heat approaches, you’ll be grateful for these refreshing and delicious plant-based delicacies to tantalize your taste senses. So, drench your empanadas, whip your guacamole, and let’s pico our way through twenty of the most appetizing vegan recipes perfect for your Cinco de Mayo celebration or any summer gathering.

  1. Spicy Tofu Scramble Tacos (Espécial de Tacos de Guacamole) Begin your culinary adventure with these protein-packed tacos. A tofu scramble infused with spices, served with a side of guacamole that brings a thunderstorm of flavor to your palate. The combination of smoky tofu and creamy guac creates a unique Mexican fusion you’ll never forget.

  2. Smoky Tempeh Street Tacos (Tacos de Camarones) Bring those spicy shrimp flavors into your kitchen using smoky tempeh. The mock shrimp tacos are sautéed in a blend of spices and combined with radishes, cilantro, and pineapple salsa for a refreshing kick.

  3. Vegan Enchiladas Suizas (Enchiladas con Salsa Verde) Indulge in these classic Mexican enchiladas with a twist. Filled with tender corn tortillas, creamy cashew sour cream, and jackfruit to mimic shredded chicken, these enchiladas in zesty green salsa will make your taste buds dance.

  4. Fluffy Vegan Chayote Salad (Ensalada de Chayote) This lighter option features a refreshing salad featuring cooked chayote tossed with green apples, corn, avocado, and a citrusy dressing for a burst of summery goodness.

  5. Mushroom and Crushed Pea Tacos (Tacos de Hongo y Chilaquiles con Crema Agria) Allow the earthy tones of mushrooms to mix with freshly crushed peas and zesty Mexican crema, creating a heavenly combination that’s perfect for meat-lovers transitioning to the vegan world.

  6. Cactus and Pickled Onion Quesadillas (Quesadillas de Nopal con Cebolla en Vinagre) Savor juicy bits of cactus and zesty pickled onions enveloped in warm corn tortillas with cheese for an explosion of tangy yet comforting flavors.

  7. Crema con Picante Jícama Agua Fresca (Spicy Jícama and Aloe Cooler) Refresh your senses with this agua fresca made of jícama, aloe vera, and a slick of spicy kick. The perfect drink to cool down while savoring the other flavorful delights.

  8. Flourless Chocolate Avocado Torte with Cinnamon-Dusted Coconut Whipped Cream Give your sweet tooth a treat with this guilt-free dark chocolate avocado cake. Topped with coconut whipped cream dusted with cinnamon, it satisfies your cravings without the ehaviness.

  9. Roasted Root Veggie Fajita Bowls (Bowl de Fajitas de Verduras Asadas) Channel your inner grill master with a hearty bowl filled with roasted root vegetables, bell peppers, and onions, all wrapped in a warm, soft tortilla.

  10. Speckled Basil and Mango Margaritas Sip away the summer heat with this vibrant twist on a classic margarita, blending the fresh flavors of basil and mango with tequila, lime, and agave.

  11. Vegan Pozole with Chayote and Jackfruit Sink your spoon into a steaming bowl of traditional Mexican hominy soup, filled with chunks of jackfruit, chayote, and zesty garnishes such as radishes and avocado.

  12. Chili Lime Corn on the Cob (Elote) Upgrade your BBQ summer offerings with freshly grilled corn on the cob, brushed with a chili-lime mayo and dusted with ancho chili powder and fresh coriander.

  13. Mexican Rice Casserole con Chayote y Verduras A hearty, one-dish meal that combines the creaminess of chayote with colorful bell peppers, and peas in a mouthwatering Mexican rice casserole.

  14. Cauliflower “Chile Rellenos” with Smoky Rod Astier The capers and olives of Niçoise come to life in this mock “chile rellenos,” filled with crispy cauliflower and smoky roasted potatoes.

  15. Vegan Horchata Experience a creamy, refreshing glass of Venezuelan-inspired horchata, featuring rice, cinnamon, and almond milk goodness.

  16. Spicy Kelp Noodles with Creamy Avocado Lime Dressing Deftly toss the slippery eludes of kelp noodles with a luscious avocado-lime dressing for a raw, spicy, and cooling sensation.

  17. Corn and Chia Seed Guacamole Blend vibrant corn and chia seeds with crowd-favorite avocados for a super-power packed guac that has everyone asking for more.

  18. Black Bean, Mango and Jicama Salad Grilled mango lends its sweeestns, while jícama crunches for a tropical slaw that’s as pleasing to the as eyeit is to the taste buds.

  19. Grilled Avocado Salad with Spinach and Spicy Pepitas Let grilled avocados take center stage in this salad, where they join forces with the peppery bite of spinach and the heat of spicy, toasty pepitas.

  20. Vegan Paletas (popsicles) with Hibiscus and Coconut Cap off your feast with a refreshing popsicle that brings together the floral sweetness of hibiscus and the tropical flavors of coconut.

As we celebrate Cinco de Mayo or any summer gathering, make these vegan Mexican recipes the highlight of your meal. They’ve got zest, they’ve got zing, and they’re the perfect accompaniments to all those warm summer nights. Happy cooking, and may your kitchen continue to evolve with brilliant vegan delights!
