Spice Up Your BBQ: 6 Fiery Rubs for Grilled Wings


Are you ready to take your BBQ game to the next level? Look no further than these six fiery rubs that will add a kick of flavor to your grilled wings. Whether you’re hosting a backyard party or simply craving some deliciously spicy wings, these rubs will not disappoint. Get your taste buds ready for a flavor explosion!

1. Smoky Chipotle Rub

Ingredients:

  • 2 tablespoons smoked paprika
  • 1 tablespoon chipotle powder
  • 1 tablespoon brown sugar
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon onion powder
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper

Instructions:

  1. In a small bowl, combine all the ingredients.
  2. Pat the wings dry with a paper towel.
  3. Sprinkle the rub evenly over the wings, making sure to coat them thoroughly.
  4. Let the wings sit in the refrigerator for at least 30 minutes to allow the flavors to meld.
  5. Grill the wings over medium heat until they are cooked through and crispy, about 20-25 minutes, turning occasionally.

The smoky chipotle rub adds a rich and smoky flavor to your wings, with just the right amount of heat. It’s perfect for those who enjoy a balanced combination of smokiness, spiciness, and sweetness.

2. Tangy Sriracha Rub

Ingredients:

  • 2 tablespoons sriracha sauce
  • 1 tablespoon honey
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon lime juice
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper

Instructions:

  1. In a small bowl, whisk together the sriracha sauce, honey, soy sauce, lime juice, garlic powder, salt, and black pepper.
  2. Toss the wings in the sauce, making sure they are evenly coated.
  3. Let the wings marinate in the refrigerator for at least 1 hour, or overnight for a more intense flavor.
  4. Preheat your grill to medium-high heat.
  5. Grill the wings for about 6-8 minutes per side, or until they are golden brown and cooked through.

The tangy sriracha rub brings a delightful balance of sweetness, tanginess, and spiciness to your wings. It’s a crowd-pleaser that will leave everyone wanting more.

3. Sweet and Spicy Korean Rub

Ingredients:

  • 2 tablespoons gochujang (Korean hot pepper paste)
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon honey
  • 1 tablespoon sesame oil
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon ginger powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, combine the gochujang, soy sauce, honey, sesame oil, garlic powder, ginger powder, salt, and black pepper.
  2. Coat the wings with the rub mixture, ensuring they are well-covered.
  3. Allow the wings to marinate in the refrigerator for at least 2 hours, or overnight for maximum flavor.
  4. Preheat your grill to medium heat.
  5. Grill the wings for about 10-12 minutes per side, or until they are crispy and cooked through.

The sweet and spicy Korean rub infuses the wings with a bold and addictive flavor. The combination of gochujang, soy sauce, and honey creates a perfect balance of heat and sweetness that will have your taste buds dancing.

4. Fiery Cajun Rub

Ingredients:

  • 2 tablespoons paprika
  • 1 tablespoon garlic powder
  • 1 tablespoon onion powder
  • 1 tablespoon dried thyme
  • 2 teaspoons cayenne pepper
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper

Instructions:

  1. In a small bowl, mix together the paprika, garlic powder, onion powder, dried thyme, cayenne pepper, salt, and black pepper.
  2. Coat the wings generously with the rub mixture, ensuring they are evenly covered.
  3. Let the wings sit in the refrigerator for at least 1 hour to allow the flavors to meld.
  4. Preheat your grill to medium-high heat.
  5. Grill the wings for about 6-8 minutes per side, or until they are crispy and cooked through.

The fiery Cajun rub brings a taste of the South to your wings. It’s a perfect blend of herbs and spices that will leave your mouth tingling with flavor.

5. Zesty Lemon Pepper Rub

Ingredients:

  • 2 tablespoons lemon zest
  • 1 tablespoon black pepper
  • 1 tablespoon dried thyme
  • 1 tablespoon dried rosemary
  • 1 tablespoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon salt

Instructions:

  1. In a small bowl, combine the lemon zest, black pepper, dried thyme, dried rosemary, garlic powder, and salt.
  2. Rub the mixture all over the wings, making sure to coat them evenly.
  3. Let the wings marinate in the refrigerator for at least 1 hour, or overnight for enhanced flavor.
  4. Preheat your grill to medium heat.
  5. Grill the wings for about 10-12 minutes per side, or until they are golden brown and cooked through.

The zesty lemon pepper rub brings a burst of citrusy freshness to your wings. The combination of lemon zest and black pepper creates a tantalizing flavor that will keep you coming back for more.

6. Spicy Honey Mustard Rub

Ingredients:

  • 2 tablespoons Dijon mustard
  • 2 tablespoons honey
  • 1 tablespoon hot sauce
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper

Instructions:

  1. In a small bowl, whisk together the Dijon mustard, honey, hot sauce, garlic powder, paprika, salt, and black pepper.
  2. Coat the wings with the rub mixture, ensuring they are well-covered.
  3. Allow the wings to marinate in the refrigerator for at least 1 hour, or overnight for maximum flavor.
  4. Preheat your grill to medium-high heat.
  5. Grill the wings for about 6-8 minutes per side, or until they are crispy and cooked through.

The spicy honey mustard rub combines the perfect balance of heat, sweetness, and tanginess. It’s a versatile rub that pairs well with grilled wings and will leave you wanting more.

Now that you have these six fiery rubs in your arsenal, it’s time to heat up your BBQ and wow your guests with some seriously delicious grilled wings. These flavor-packed rubs will take your wings to a whole new level and leave everyone craving for more. So, fire up the grill and get ready for a taste sensation like no other!
