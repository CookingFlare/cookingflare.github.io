Tasty Instant Pot Chicken Marsala Recipe for Italian Flair


If you’re looking for a delicious and flavorful Italian-inspired dish that’s quick and easy to prepare, then this Tasty Instant Pot Chicken Marsala recipe is just what you need. The Instant Pot is a game-changer when it comes to cooking, and it works wonders for creating tender and juicy chicken dishes. This recipe combines the rich and earthy flavors of mushrooms and Marsala wine with succulent chicken breasts, resulting in a dish that’s perfect for a cozy night in or for impressing your guests at a dinner party.

Ingredients:

  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 8 ounces cremini mushrooms, sliced
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup Marsala wine
  • 1 cup chicken broth
  • 2 tablespoons unsalted butter
  • Chopped fresh parsley for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the Chicken: In a shallow dish, mix the flour, salt, pepper, oregano, and thyme. Dredge the chicken breasts in the seasoned flour, shaking off any excess.

  2. Sear the Chicken: Select the “Sauté” function on the Instant Pot and heat the olive oil. Once hot, add the chicken breasts and brown them on both sides, about 3 minutes per side. Transfer the chicken to a plate and set aside.

  3. Cook the Mushrooms and Garlic: Add the sliced mushrooms to the Instant Pot and cook until they release their moisture and begin to brown. Stir in the minced garlic and cook for an additional 30 seconds.

  4. Deglaze the Pot: Pour in the Marsala wine and chicken broth, using a wooden spoon to scrape up any browned bits from the bottom of the pot. This step is crucial for adding depth of flavor to the sauce.

  5. Pressure Cook the Chicken: Return the chicken breasts to the Instant Pot, nestling them into the mushroom mixture. Secure the lid and set the Instant Pot to cook at high pressure for 8 minutes. Once the cooking time is complete, perform a quick pressure release.

  6. Thicken the Sauce: Select the “Sauté” function again and simmer the sauce until it reduces slightly. Stir in the unsalted butter to add richness and shine to the sauce.

  7. Serve and Garnish: Transfer the chicken Marsala to a serving platter, spooning the mushroom sauce over the top. Garnish with chopped fresh parsley for a pop of color and freshness.

Tips for Success:

  • For a richer sauce, you can add a splash of heavy cream or a dollop of crème fraîche at the end of cooking.
  • If you’re not a fan of mushrooms, you can omit them from the recipe or substitute them with another vegetable of your choice, such as spinach or artichokes.
  • Serve the chicken Marsala over a bed of creamy mashed potatoes, buttery polenta, or al dente pasta to soak up the delicious sauce.

Final Thoughts:

This Tasty Instant Pot Chicken Marsala recipe is a fantastic way to bring the flavors of Italy into your kitchen without spending hours over the stove. The Instant Pot streamlines the cooking process, resulting in a dish that tastes like it’s been simmering for hours when, in reality, it comes together in a fraction of the time. Whether you’re cooking for a special occasion or simply craving a comforting and satisfying meal, this chicken Marsala recipe is sure to become a favorite in your recipe collection. Buon appetito!
