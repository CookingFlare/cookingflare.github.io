Crunchy and Creamy Broccoli Salad Recipe


If you’re looking for a refreshing and satisfying salad that’s perfect for summer picnics, barbecues, or just a light lunch, you’ve come to the right place. This Crunchy and Creamy Broccoli Salad recipe is a delightful combination of fresh broccoli, crispy bacon, sweet cranberries, and a creamy dressing that will tantalize your taste buds. Whether you’re a fan of broccoli or not, this salad is sure to win you over with its delicious flavors and textures.

Ingredients

  • 4 cups fresh broccoli florets
  • 1/2 cup diced red onion
  • 1/2 cup crispy cooked bacon, crumbled
  • 1/2 cup dried cranberries
  • 1/2 cup chopped walnuts
  • 1/2 cup mayonnaise
  • 2 tablespoons apple cider vinegar
  • 2 tablespoons honey
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Broccoli: Start by washing the broccoli thoroughly and cutting it into bite-sized florets. Make sure to dry the florets completely to ensure the salad stays crisp and fresh.

  2. Combine Ingredients: In a large mixing bowl, combine the broccoli florets, diced red onion, crispy bacon, dried cranberries, and chopped walnuts. Toss the ingredients together gently to distribute them evenly.

  3. Prepare the Dressing: In a separate bowl, whisk together the mayonnaise, apple cider vinegar, honey, salt, and pepper until the dressing is smooth and well combined.

  4. Combine and Chill: Pour the creamy dressing over the broccoli mixture and toss gently until all the ingredients are coated with the dressing. Cover the bowl with plastic wrap and refrigerate for at least 1 hour to allow the flavors to meld together.

  5. Serve and Enjoy: Once chilled, give the salad a final toss and transfer it to a serving dish. This Crunchy and Creamy Broccoli Salad is ready to be enjoyed as a side dish or a light meal.

Tips for Success

  • Fresh Ingredients: Use the freshest broccoli, red onion, and cranberries you can find to ensure the best flavors in your salad.

  • Make-Ahead: This salad can be made a day in advance, making it a convenient option for parties and gatherings.

  • Customize: Feel free to customize the salad by adding ingredients such as sunflower seeds, shredded cheddar cheese, or a sprinkle of red pepper flakes for a touch of heat.

Why You’ll Love This Recipe

This Crunchy and Creamy Broccoli Salad recipe offers a delightful contrast of textures and flavors. The crispness of the broccoli and walnuts, the sweetness of the cranberries, the savory richness of the bacon, and the creamy dressing come together to create a truly satisfying dish. It’s a versatile recipe that pairs well with grilled meats, sandwiches, or as a standalone dish. Plus, it’s a great way to incorporate more vegetables into your diet while enjoying every bite.

So, the next time you’re in need of a crowd-pleasing salad that’s easy to make and bursting with flavor, give this Crunchy and Creamy Broccoli Salad recipe a try. It’s a wonderful addition to any meal and is guaranteed to become a favorite in your recipe collection.
