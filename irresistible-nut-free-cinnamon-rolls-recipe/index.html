Irresistible Nut-Free Cinnamon Rolls Recipe


If you’re a cinnamon lover, then you’re in for a treat! These Irresistible Nut-Free Cinnamon Rolls are a delicious and indulgent treat that will satisfy your cravings. Bursting with warm cinnamon flavor and topped with a creamy glaze, these cinnamon rolls are perfect for breakfast, brunch, or even as a sweet dessert. The best part? This recipe is nut-free, making it a safe and delicious option for those with nut allergies.

Ingredients

For the dough:

  • 4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 2 1/4 teaspoons active dry yeast
  • 1 cup milk, warmed
  • 1/4 cup unsalted butter, melted
  • 2 large eggs

For the filling:

  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 3/4 cup brown sugar
  • 2 tablespoons ground cinnamon

For the glaze:

  • 1 cup powdered sugar
  • 2 tablespoons milk
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract

Instructions

  1. In a large mixing bowl, combine the flour, sugar, salt, and yeast. Mix well.

  2. In a separate bowl, whisk together the warm milk, melted butter, and eggs.

  3. Pour the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients. Mix until a soft dough forms.

  4. Turn the dough out onto a lightly floured surface and knead for about 5 minutes, or until the dough is smooth and elastic.

  5. Place the dough in a greased bowl and cover with a clean kitchen towel. Allow the dough to rise in a warm, draft-free place for about 1 hour, or until it has doubled in size.

  6. Once the dough has risen, punch it down to release any air bubbles. Roll the dough out into a large rectangle, about 1/4 inch thick.

  7. Spread the softened butter over the surface of the dough. Sprinkle the brown sugar and cinnamon evenly over the butter.

  8. Starting from one long edge, tightly roll the dough into a log. Cut the log into 12 equal slices.

  9. Place the cinnamon rolls in a greased baking dish, leaving a little space between each roll. Cover the dish with a clean kitchen towel and allow the rolls to rise for another 30 minutes.

  10. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C). Bake the cinnamon rolls for 25-30 minutes, or until they are golden brown and cooked through.

  11. While the rolls are baking, prepare the glaze. In a small bowl, whisk together the powdered sugar, milk, and vanilla extract until smooth.

  12. Once the cinnamon rolls are done baking, remove them from the oven and let them cool for a few minutes. Drizzle the glaze over the warm rolls.

  13. Serve the Irresistible Nut-Free Cinnamon Rolls warm and enjoy!

Tips and Variations

  • If you prefer a more gooey cinnamon roll, you can double the filling ingredients and spread it generously over the dough before rolling.
  • For an extra burst of flavor, you can add a handful of raisins or chopped apples to the filling.
  • These cinnamon rolls can be made ahead of time and stored in an airtight container. To reheat, simply pop them in the microwave for a few seconds or warm them in a low-temperature oven.
  • Feel free to experiment with different glazes, such as cream cheese or maple.

There you have it! A mouthwatering recipe for Irresistible Nut-Free Cinnamon Rolls that will leave you wanting more. These cinnamon rolls are perfect for any occasion and are sure to impress your family and friends. So go ahead, indulge in the sweet and comforting flavors of cinnamon, and enjoy every bite!
