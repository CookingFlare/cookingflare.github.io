10 Authentic Italian Pasta Recipes You Must Try


Italian cuisine is celebrated worldwide for its bold flavors, fresh ingredients, and comforting dishes, with pasta holding a special place in the hearts of many. Whether you’re a novice in the kitchen or a seasoned chef, these ten authentic Italian pasta recipes will inspire you to bring a taste of Italy to your table. Each recipe reflects the rich culinary heritage of Italy, showcasing regional ingredients and techniques that guarantee a delightful dining experience. Let’s dive into these mouthwatering dishes!

1. Spaghetti Carbonara

Ingredients:

  • 400g spaghetti
  • 150g guanciale (or pancetta)
  • 3 large eggs
  • 100g Pecorino Romano cheese, grated
  • Black pepper to taste
  • Salt

Instructions:

  1. Cook the Pasta: Boil a large pot of salted water and cook the spaghetti until al dente. Reserve about a cup of pasta water before draining.
  2. Prepare the Sauce: In a bowl, whisk together the eggs and grated Pecorino Romano cheese until well combined.
  3. Sauté the Guanciale: In a large pan, cook the diced guanciale over medium heat until crispy. Remove from heat once cooked.
  4. Combine: Add the drained spaghetti to the guanciale, then quickly mix in the egg and cheese mixture. Stir vigorously, adding reserved pasta water as needed to create a creamy sauce.
  5. Serve: Plate the carbonara and finish with freshly cracked black pepper and an extra sprinkle of cheese.

2. Fettuccine Alfredo

Ingredients:

  • 400g fettuccine
  • 100g unsalted butter
  • 200ml heavy cream
  • 100g Parmigiano-Reggiano, grated
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Parsley for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Cook the Fettuccine: Boil salted water and cook fettuccine until al dente. Reserve some pasta water before draining.
  2. Make the Sauce: In a large skillet, melt the butter over medium heat. Add the heavy cream and simmer for a few minutes until slightly thickened.
  3. Combine: Stir in the grated Parmigiano-Reggiano, mixing until smooth. Add the drained fettuccine and toss well. Use reserved pasta water to achieve a creamy consistency.
  4. Serve: Plate the fettuccine, season with salt and pepper, and garnish with parsley.

3. Pesto Pasta

Ingredients:

  • 400g pasta of choice (trofie or linguine recommended)
  • 100g fresh basil leaves
  • 50g pine nuts
  • 100ml extra virgin olive oil
  • 100g Parmigiano-Reggiano, grated
  • 1 clove of garlic
  • Salt to taste

Instructions:

  1. Cook the Pasta: Prepare the pasta according to package instructions until al dente.
  2. Make the Pesto: In a food processor, combine basil, pine nuts, garlic, and salt. Pulse until finely chopped. Slowly add olive oil while processing until smooth. Stir in the grated cheese.
  3. Combine: Toss the cooked pasta with the pesto sauce, adding a bit of pasta water if needed for creaminess.
  4. Serve: Serve hot, garnished with additional pine nuts and cheese.

4. Lasagna

Ingredients:

  • 12 sheets of lasagna noodles
  • 500g ground beef
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 800g crushed tomatoes
  • 250g ricotta cheese
  • 200g mozzarella cheese, shredded
  • 100g Parmigiano-Reggiano, grated
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • Olive oil, salt, and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat Oven: Preheat your oven to 180°C (350°F).
  2. Prepare the Sauce: In a pan, heat olive oil and sauté onions and garlic until golden. Add ground beef and cook until browned. Stir in crushed tomatoes, salt, pepper, and basil. Simmer for 20 minutes.
  3. Assemble the Lasagna: In a baking dish, spread a layer of meat sauce, followed by lasagna sheets, ricotta, and mozzarella. Repeat layers and finish with sauce and Parmigiano-Reggiano on top.
  4. Bake: Cover with foil and bake for 30 minutes. Remove the foil and bake for an additional 15 minutes until bubbly and golden.
  5. Serve: Let it cool slightly before slicing and serving.

5. Penne Arrabbiata

Ingredients:

  • 400g penne
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 400g canned tomatoes
  • 1 tsp red pepper flakes
  • 50ml extra virgin olive oil
  • Fresh parsley, chopped
  • Salt to taste

Instructions:

  1. Cook the Pasta: Boil the penne in salted water until al dente.
  2. Prepare the Sauce: In a large skillet, heat olive oil and sauté minced garlic until fragrant. Add red pepper flakes, followed by crushed tomatoes and salt. Simmer for 10 minutes.
  3. Combine: Toss the cooked penne in the sauce, ensuring a good coating.
  4. Serve: Finish with freshly chopped parsley and additional red pepper flakes if desired.

6. Orecchiette with Broccoli Rabe

Ingredients:

  • 400g orecchiette
  • 300g broccoli rabe, trimmed and chopped
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 50ml extra virgin olive oil
  • 50g Pecorino Romano, grated
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Cook the Pasta: Boil salted water and add orecchiette. In the last 3 minutes of cooking, add broccoli rabe. Drain together.
  2. Make the Sauce: In a large skillet, heat olive oil and sauté garlic until golden. Toss in the drained pasta and broccoli rabe, mixing well.
  3. Serve: Plate with a sprinkle of Pecorino Romano, salt, and pepper.

7. Ravioli di Ricotta e Spinaci

Ingredients:

  • 250g fresh pasta sheets
  • 250g ricotta cheese
  • 200g spinach, cooked and chopped
  • 1 egg
  • 100g Parmigiano-Reggiano, grated
  • Nutmeg, salt, and pepper to taste
  • Tomato sauce for serving

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the Filling: In a bowl, mix ricotta, spinach, egg, nutmeg, salt, and pepper until well combined.
  2. Fill the Ravioli: Place small mounds of filling on pasta sheets, fold, and seal edges with water to form ravioli.
  3. Cook the Ravioli: Boil until they float (about 3-4 minutes).
  4. Serve: Plate with tomato sauce and sprinkle with Parmigiano-Reggiano.

8. Linguine alle Vongole

Ingredients:

  • 400g linguine
  • 1kg fresh clams (vongole)
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 100ml white wine
  • 50ml extra virgin olive oil
  • Fresh parsley, chopped
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Cook the Linguine: Boil salted water and cook linguine until al dente.
  2. Cook the Clams: In a large pan, heat olive oil and sauté garlic. Add clams and white wine, covering to steam until clams open.
  3. Combine: Toss the cooked linguine with the clams, adding a bit of pasta water for moisture.
  4. Serve: Finish with parsley, salt, and pepper.

9. Tagliatelle al Ragù

Ingredients:

  • 400g tagliatelle
  • 300g ground beef and pork mix
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 carrots, diced
  • 2 celery stalks, diced
  • 400g canned tomatoes
  • 100ml red wine
  • Olive oil, salt, and pepper to taste
  • Grated Parmigiano-Reggiano for serving

Instructions:

  1. Make the Ragù: In a large pot, heat olive oil and sauté onion, carrots, and celery until soft. Add meat, browning it thoroughly. Pour in wine and let it evaporate.
  2. Add Tomatoes: Stir in canned tomatoes, salt, and pepper. Simmer for at least an hour.
  3. Cook the Tagliatelle: Boil the pasta until al dente.
  4. Combine: Toss the pasta with the ragù.
  5. Serve: Plate and top with grated Parmigiano-Reggiano.

10. Spaghetti Aglio e Olio

Ingredients:

  • 400g spaghetti
  • 4 cloves garlic, sliced
  • 100ml extra virgin olive oil
  • 1 tsp red pepper flakes
  • Fresh parsley, chopped
  • Salt to taste

Instructions:

  1. Cook the Spaghetti: Boil salted water and cook spaghetti until al dente.
  2. Sauté Garlic: In a large skillet, heat olive oil and add sliced garlic and red pepper flakes, cooking until garlic is golden.
  3. Combine: Toss the drained spaghetti in the skillet with the garlic oil.
  4. Serve: Garnish with chopped parsley and extra red pepper flakes.

Each of these ten authentic Italian pasta recipes offers a unique taste of Italy, perfect for any occasion. From the creamy richness of Fettuccine Alfredo to the light and vibrant Pesto Pasta, these dishes are guaranteed to impress family and friends. So roll up your sleeves, gather your ingredients, and embark on a flavorful journey through Italy, one pasta dish at a time! Enjoy your culinary adventure!
