Pumpkin Spice Perfection: Homemade Pumpkin Butter Recipes


If you’re a fan of all things pumpkin spice, then homemade pumpkin butter is a must-try delicacy that will elevate your fall flavors to a whole new level. This rich and creamy spread is packed with warm spices and the sweet essence of pumpkin, making it a versatile addition to your kitchen repertoire. Not only is it incredibly easy to make, but it also offers a delicious twist to your breakfast toast, baked goods, or even as a unique dip for savory dishes. Let’s dive into the world of homemade pumpkin butter with these delightful recipes that are sure to impress your taste buds.

Classic Pumpkin Spice Butter

Ingredients:

  • 2 cans (15 oz each) of pumpkin puree
  • 1 cup brown sugar
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/2 cup apple juice
  • 2 tsp cinnamon
  • 1/2 tsp nutmeg
  • 1/2 tsp cloves
  • 1/4 tsp ginger
  • 1/4 tsp allspice
  • Pinch of salt

Instructions:

  1. In a large saucepan, combine the pumpkin puree, brown sugar, granulated sugar, and apple juice.
  2. Stir in the cinnamon, nutmeg, cloves, ginger, allspice, and salt.
  3. Cook the mixture over medium heat, stirring frequently until it comes to a simmer.
  4. Reduce the heat to low and let the mixture simmer for about 30-40 minutes until it thickens.
  5. Remove from heat and let it cool before transferring to sterilized jars.
  6. Store in the refrigerator for up to 2 weeks or freeze for longer storage.

Spiced Maple Pumpkin Butter

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups pumpkin puree
  • 1/2 cup maple syrup
  • 1/4 cup brown sugar
  • 1/4 cup water
  • 1 tsp cinnamon
  • 1/2 tsp nutmeg
  • 1/4 tsp cloves
  • 1/4 tsp allspice
  • Pinch of salt

Instructions:

  1. In a saucepan, combine the pumpkin puree, maple syrup, brown sugar, and water.
  2. Add the cinnamon, nutmeg, cloves, allspice, and salt.
  3. Cook over low heat, stirring occasionally, for about 20-30 minutes until thickened.
  4. Let the pumpkin butter cool before transferring it to jars.
  5. Refrigerate and enjoy within 2 weeks or freeze for longer shelf life.

Vanilla Infused Pumpkin Butter

Ingredients:

  • 3 cups pumpkin puree
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1/2 cup apple cider
  • 1 vanilla bean, split and scraped
  • 1 tsp cinnamon
  • 1/2 tsp nutmeg
  • Pinch of salt

Instructions:

  1. Combine the pumpkin puree, sugar, apple cider, vanilla bean (including the seeds), cinnamon, nutmeg, and salt in a saucepan.
  2. Cook over medium heat, stirring frequently until the mixture thickens, about 25-30 minutes.
  3. Remove the vanilla bean and transfer the pumpkin butter to jars.
  4. Refrigerate for up to 2 weeks or freeze for longer storage.

Whether you spread it on toast, swirl it into oatmeal, or use it as a filling for pastries, homemade pumpkin butter is a delightful way to savor the flavors of the season. Experiment with different spice combinations, sweetness levels, and even add-ins like pecans or cranberries to create your signature pumpkin butter. Embrace the cozy aroma of pumpkin spice in your kitchen with these easy and delicious recipes. Happy cooking and indulging in the pumpkin spice perfection!
