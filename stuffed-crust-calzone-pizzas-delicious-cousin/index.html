Stuffed Crust Calzone: Pizza's Delicious Cousin


If you’re a fan of pizza, then you’re in for a treat with this mouthwatering recipe for stuffed crust calzone. This delightful dish takes all the goodness of a traditional pizza and wraps it up in a warm, golden-brown crust, creating a handheld meal that’s perfect for any occasion. Whether you’re hosting a casual get-together with friends or simply craving a cozy night in, this stuffed crust calzone is sure to satisfy your cravings for cheesy, savory goodness.

Ingredients

For the dough:

  • 3 ½ cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 ¼ cups warm water
  • 2 ¼ teaspoons instant yeast
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon sugar

For the filling:

  • 1 ½ cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • ¾ cup marinara sauce
  • 1 cup sliced pepperoni
  • ½ cup sliced mushrooms
  • ½ cup sliced black olives
  • ½ cup diced bell peppers
  • ½ cup sliced onions
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon red pepper flakes (optional)

Instructions

Step 1: Prepare the Dough

In a large bowl, combine the warm water, yeast, and sugar. Let it sit for 5-10 minutes until the mixture becomes frothy. Add the olive oil and salt, then gradually stir in the flour until a dough forms. Knead the dough on a floured surface for about 5-7 minutes, or until it becomes smooth and elastic. Place the dough in a greased bowl, cover it with a clean kitchen towel, and let it rise in a warm place for 1-2 hours, or until it doubles in size.

Step 2: Prepare the Filling

While the dough is rising, prepare the filling by combining the mozzarella cheese, marinara sauce, pepperoni, mushrooms, black olives, bell peppers, onions, oregano, garlic powder, and red pepper flakes in a large bowl. Mix everything together until well combined.

Step 3: Assemble the Calzone

Once the dough has doubled in size, punch it down to release the air. Divide the dough into four equal portions. Roll out each portion into a circle, about 8-10 inches in diameter. Place a generous amount of the filling on one half of each dough circle, leaving a border around the edges. Fold the other half of the dough over the filling to create a half-moon shape. Press the edges firmly to seal the calzone.

Step 4: Create the Stuffed Crust

To make the stuffed crust, fold the edges of the dough over themselves and press them firmly to seal. This will create a beautiful, cheesy crust that’s bursting with flavor.

Step 5: Bake to Perfection

Preheat your oven to 425°F (220°C). Carefully transfer the assembled calzones to a baking sheet lined with parchment paper. Brush the tops of the calzones with a little olive oil and sprinkle with a pinch of salt. Using a sharp knife, make a few small slits on the top of each calzone to allow steam to escape during baking. Bake for 20-25 minutes, or until the calzones are golden brown and the cheese is bubbly.

Step 6: Enjoy!

Once the calzones are done, remove them from the oven and let them cool for a few minutes. Slice them into portions and serve with extra marinara sauce for dipping. Enjoy the delightful combination of crispy, golden crust and oozing, melty cheese in every bite.

Conclusion

With its crispy exterior, gooey cheese, and flavorful fillings, the stuffed crust calzone is a delightful twist on the classic pizza. Whether you’re a seasoned home cook or just beginning your culinary journey, this recipe is a great way to impress your friends and family with a homemade Italian favorite. So, roll up your sleeves, gather your ingredients, and get ready to indulge in the deliciousness of stuffed crust calzone – pizza’s irresistible cousin.
