In Pinky Promise: 5 Pink Grapefruit-inspired Juice & Smoothie Recipes


Who says healthy sustenance can’t be fun and vibrant? Embrace your inner girly girl with these pink grapefruit-inspired juice and smoothie recipes that are as eye-catching as they are delicious. Their naturally sweet and tart flavors will banish those dull and monotonous smoothies from your life, leaving you with just the best of the best. Here they are - our top 5 pink grapefruit-infused drink combinations that are nothing short of a pinky promise to your taste buds.

1. Rosé Spritz Smoothie

Who wouldn’t want to enjoy a taste of the French Riviera at home? This refreshing Rosé Spritz Smoothie offers a blend of sweet and puckery deliciousness that’s reminiscent of your favorite poolside sip.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup rosé wine (optional)
  • 1 cup fresh grapefruit juice
  • 1/2 cup coconut water
  • 1/2 cup frozen strawberries
  • 1/2 cup ice
  • 1/4 cup lemonade (optional)

Directions:

  1. In a blender, combine all ingredients in the order listed above - if you’re a stickler for blending consistency.
  2. Blend on high until smooth, about 1-2 minutes.
  3. Pour into two glasses and garnish with a strawberry if desired.

2. The Scarlett Sunrise Smoothie Bowl

Don’t be afraid to jump on the smoothie bowl bandwagon with our Scarlett Sunrise Smoothie Bowl. It’s as beautiful as the first light of dawn, and just as invigorating!

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 cup fresh pink grapefruit juice
  • 1/2 cup pineapple chunks
  • 1/2 cup mango chunks
  • 1/2 cup plain Greek yogurt
  • 1/2 avocado
  • 1 cup ice
  • A wide, shallow bowl for serving

Directions:

  1. In your blender, combine the pink grapefruit juice, pineapple, mango, Greek yogurt, and avocado.
  2. Blend on high until completely smooth, scraping down the sides as needed.
  3. In your shallow bowl, scoop the smoothie mixture, and then sprinkle granola and slices of fresh fruit on top.

3. Sunshine in a Glass Juice

Who says a juice needs to be shy and demure? Our Sunshine in a Glass Juice is the epitome of boldness - with all of the vibrancy and vibrato of a sun-kissed California morning.

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 cup fresh pink grapefruit juice
  • 1/2 cup unsweetened almond milk
  • 1 teaspoon honey
  • 1/2 cup freshly squeezed lemon juice
  • 1/2 cup freshly chopped cucumber
  • 1 tablespoon chia seeds
  • A handful of mint leaves

Directions:

  1. Blend together pink grapefruit juice, almond milk, honey, and lemon juice in a blender until smooth.
  2. Transfer the mixture into a pitcher, then add the chopped cucumber and give a gentle stir.
  3. Finally, stir in the chia seeds and mint leaves. Allow the mixture to sit for about 10 minutes, allowing the chia seeds to become gelatinous and thick.
  4. Pour a vibrant dollop of grapefruit sunshine into a glass and serve.

4. Incredible Hulk-ish Kale & Grapefruit Juice

If you’re looking for a juice that’s surprisingly heavy on leafy greens, look no further. This Incredible Hulk-ish Kale & Grapefruit Juice combines nutrient-dense kale with the tart tanginess of pink grapefruit, creating a superhero sip you’ll look forward to each morning.

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups fresh baby kale
  • 1 cup fresh pink grapefruit juice
  • 1/2 cup freshly squeezed orange juice
  • 1/2 avocado
  • 1/2 pineapple
  • A handful of mint leaves

Directions:

  1. Combine all ingredients in a blender, being mindful of your blender’s capacity.
  2. Blend on high for about 1-2 minutes or until a smooth consistency is achieved.
  3. Pour into glasses and relish in your new heroic elixir.

5. Grapefruit & Lavender Lemonade Smoothie

We could always use a moment of serenity, and this smoothie is a zen oasis for the tastebuds. The delicate lavender complements the freshness of the lemon and grapefruit for a crunching floral flavor that will take your taste buds to a euphoric place.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup fresh pink grapefruit juice
  • 1 cup freshly squeezed lemon juice
  • 1/2 cup raw honey
  • 1 cup water
  • A handful of fresh lavender blooms
  • 1/2 cup Greek yogurt
  • 1/2 cup ice
  • Sparkling water to top

Directions:

  1. In a saucepan over low heat, bring lemon juice, honey, and water to a simmer while stirring until the honey dissolves.
  2. Remove from heat and add the fresh lavender blooms, then let it steep for about 10 minutes.
  3. Strain the lavender lemonade into a pitcher: you’ll use this for the smoothie base.
  4. In a blender, combine grapefruit juice, lavender lemonade, Greek yogurt, and ice; blend until smooth.
  5. Pour the smoothie into a glass and top with a splash of sparkling water for natural carbonation.

Embrace your love for all things pink with these thirst-quenching grapefruit-inspired juice and smoothie recipes. Each one is a delightful pinky promise to your tastebuds and the very essence of a flavorful and visually appealing treat.
