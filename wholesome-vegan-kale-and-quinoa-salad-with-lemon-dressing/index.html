Wholesome Vegan Kale and Quinoa Salad with Lemon Dressing


As the seasons change and the weather cools down, our bodies crave nourishing and comforting foods that can provide a boost of energy and vitality. One such dish that fits the bill perfectly is a wholesome vegan kale and quinoa salad with a tangy lemon dressing. This recipe is a symphony of textures and flavors that will leave you feeling satisfied and rejuvenated.

The Ingredients

To make this incredible salad, you’ll need the following ingredients:

  • 1 cup of quinoa, rinsed and drained
  • 2 cups of water or vegetable broth
  • 2 tablespoons of olive oil
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 2 cups of curly kale, stems removed and discarded, leaves torn into bite-sized pieces
  • 1 cup of cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1/2 cup of cucumber, sliced
  • 1/4 cup of chopped fresh parsley
  • 2 tablespoons of freshly squeezed lemon juice
  • 1 teaspoon of apple cider vinegar
  • 1/2 teaspoon of salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon of black pepper

The Preparation

To start, cook the quinoa according to the package instructions using 2 cups of water or vegetable broth. Once cooked, set it aside to cool.

In a large skillet, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the chopped onion and cook until translucent, about 5 minutes. Add the minced garlic and cook for an additional minute, stirring constantly to prevent burning.

Add the torn kale leaves to the skillet and cook until wilted, about 5-7 minutes. You may need to add a splash of water to the pan to help the kale cook evenly.

In a large bowl, combine the cooked quinoa, wilted kale, cherry tomatoes, sliced cucumber, and chopped parsley.

The Lemon Dressing

In a small bowl, whisk together the freshly squeezed lemon juice, apple cider vinegar, salt, and black pepper until well combined.

The Assembly

Pour the lemon dressing over the quinoa and kale mixture, tossing gently to combine.

The Serving

Serve the salad immediately, garnished with additional parsley if desired. You can also refrigerate it for up to 24 hours and serve chilled.

The Benefits

This wholesome vegan kale and quinoa salad is not only delicious, but it’s also packed with nutrients that can provide a range of health benefits. Quinoa is a complete protein, meaning it contains all nine essential amino acids that our bodies need to build and repair tissues. Kale is rich in vitamins A, C, and K, as well as minerals like calcium and iron. The lemon dressing adds a boost of vitamin C and antioxidants to the dish.

Conclusion

This wholesome vegan kale and quinoa salad with lemon dressing is a perfect recipe for a quick and easy lunch or dinner that’s both nourishing and delicious. With its symphony of textures and flavors, it’s a dish that’s sure to become a staple in your kitchen. So go ahead, give it a try, and experience the benefits of a plant-based diet for yourself!
