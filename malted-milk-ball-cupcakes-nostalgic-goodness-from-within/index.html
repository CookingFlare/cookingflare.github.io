Malted Milk Ball Cupcakes: Nostalgic Goodness from Within


Remember those crunchy, creamy, and slightly sweet malted milk balls we all cherish? Well, let’s bring those delightful treats right into our cupcake recipes and transport ourselves back to our childhood. I’m thrilled to introduce you to the indulgent and delightful malted milk ball cupcakes today. Soft, moist and the perfect balance of sweet, salty, and nutty flavors in every bite is bound to make you relive those nostalgic memories of the classic confections.

So, why don’t we get our aprons on and dive into the enchanting world of malted milk ball cupcakes!

The Journey to the Perfect Cupcake

The story of the humble malted milk ball starts way back 1920’s in the United States, a creation masterfully engineered by Frank Mars, the founder of the Mars, Incorporated. Malted milk powder came into play when George Bruce, an English candy maker, earned a British patent for a “Food or Drink Preparations” using it. Finally, our beloved Whopper’s recipe was born in 1968, further popularizing the nostalgic sweetness around the world.

Today, we’ve determined to embrace our love for this classic flavor combination blending it with our heartfelt cupcake recipes.

What You Need

Before we waltz through the guidelines, ensure you stock up your pantry with the key ingredients that’ll elevate these treats, transforming them into scrumptious bites of nostalgic goodness!

  1. Cupcake Ingredients
  • 1 3/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup malted milk powder
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, room temperature
  • 2 large eggs, room temperature
  • 1/2 cup full-fat sour cream
  • 1/2 cup buttermilk
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  1. Malted Milk Chocolate Ganache
  • 8 ounces semi-sweet chocolate, finely chopped
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • 2 tablespoons of the remaining malted milk powder
  • A dash of salt
  1. Cream Cheese Frosting
  • 8 ounces cream cheese, room temperature
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, room temperature
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 2 cups powdered sugar
  • A pinch of salt
  1. Decoration
  • Chopped malted milk balls
  • Edible Sparkles (Optional)
  • A piping bag with a star tip

The Culinary Adventure Begins

The Cupcake

You will first begin by preheating your oven to 350°F. Grease a 12-cup muffin tin, or simply line it with cupcake liners for easy peasy removal!

Conjure a bowl (yes, the mad scientist approach!) and whiz together the flour, malted milk powder, sugar, baking powder, baking soda, and salt.

Next, make sure you beat in the unsalted butter until it resembles a creamy texture. Beat up those large eggs one at a time, followed by adding the sour cream and vanillas.

Now, ignite the flavorful concoction by tossing in the flour mixture, the buttermilk, and a dash of patience. Pour your cupcake batter evenly into each prepared muffin well. Bake until those delectable cupcakes are toothsome yet tender, about 20 minutes, then allow them to cool completely.

Malted Milk Chocolate Ganache

As the cupcakes chill, fret not, as you have the time to bring together the luscious Malted Milk Chocolate Ganache. Cascading a simple yet indulgent ganache, here’s a step to get you started:

In a medium saucepan, breathe life into the heavy cream as it heats through. As it approaches a boil, immediately pour it sluggishly over the finely chopped chocolate, mingled generously with malted milk powder, and a little dash of salt. Whisper a spell by stirring with love until smooth and shiny. As it cools, give it some time to thicken-up!

Heavenly Cream Cheese Frosting

Now, create a magical cream cheese frosting, which will serve as the delightfulfinish to your malted milk ball cupcake. This ethereal frosting blends together the softened cream cheese, unsalted butter, a dusting of powdered sugar, a pinch of salt, and a dash of vanilla, until you have that perfect, fluffybroadcast.

Using your piping bag and star tip, fill it with the ganache, equally distributing its velvety richness. With a swift, steady hand, pipe your luscious cream cheese frosting, repeating the swirl method to create the ideal amount of soft peaks.

Finally, balanced on the tip of the piping, add a smattering of crushed malted milk balls, as well as an edible glitter of yours if you desire.

A Bite of Nostalgia

These Malted Milk Ball Cupcakes are a theme of celebration with each scrumptious bite overflowing with memories of childhood happiness. With a dulcet melody of classic old-fashioned flavors, we knead not look back and question where we’ll find the chime of nostalgic goodness. The truth wafts in the air, mingling gently with the aroma of freshly baked cupcakes. In this moment, let’s knead our hearts with the memories and let our palate dance in the symphony of flavors as we bring forth our childhood sweethearts in the form of Malted Milk Ball Cupcakes, where nostalgia flows like a gentle crescendo.

Bon Appétit!
