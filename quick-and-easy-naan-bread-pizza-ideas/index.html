Quick and Easy Naan Bread Pizza Ideas


If you’re a fan of pizza (and let’s face it, who isn’t?), you’ll love the convenience and deliciousness of naan bread pizza. It’s a quick and easy way to satisfy your pizza cravings without the hassle of making dough from scratch. Naan bread, with its soft texture and slightly charred edges, serves as the perfect base for creating a variety of mouthwatering pizzas. Whether you’re hosting a casual get-together, need a speedy weeknight dinner, or just want to switch up your pizza game, these naan bread pizza ideas are sure to hit the spot.

Classic Margherita Naan Bread Pizza

Ingredients:

  • Naan bread
  • Tomato sauce
  • Fresh mozzarella cheese
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • Olive oil
  • Salt and pepper

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 425°F (220°C).
  2. Place the naan bread on a baking sheet.
  3. Spread a thin layer of tomato sauce over the naan bread, leaving a small border around the edges.
  4. Tear the fresh mozzarella into pieces and arrange it evenly over the tomato sauce.
  5. Season with a pinch of salt and pepper.
  6. Drizzle a little olive oil over the top.
  7. Bake in the preheated oven for 8-10 minutes, or until the cheese is melted and bubbly.
  8. Remove from the oven and scatter fresh basil leaves over the pizza.
  9. Slice and serve immediately.

Spicy Chicken Tikka Naan Bread Pizza

Ingredients:

  • Naan bread
  • Tandoori chicken (cooked and shredded)
  • Red onion (thinly sliced)
  • Green bell pepper (sliced)
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Tikka sauce
  • Fresh cilantro (chopped)

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 425°F (220°C).
  2. Place the naan bread on a baking sheet.
  3. Spread a layer of tikka sauce over the naan bread.
  4. Sprinkle a generous amount of mozzarella cheese over the sauce.
  5. Arrange the shredded chicken, sliced red onion, and green bell pepper over the cheese.
  6. Bake in the preheated oven for 8-10 minutes, or until the cheese is golden and bubbly.
  7. Remove from the oven and garnish with fresh chopped cilantro.
  8. Slice and serve hot.

Pesto, Tomato, and Feta Naan Bread Pizza

Ingredients:

  • Naan bread
  • Pesto sauce
  • Cherry tomatoes (halved)
  • Feta cheese (crumbled)
  • Pine nuts
  • Fresh arugula
  • Balsamic glaze

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 425°F (220°C).
  2. Place the naan bread on a baking sheet.
  3. Spread a layer of pesto sauce over the naan bread.
  4. Scatter the halved cherry tomatoes and crumbled feta cheese over the pesto.
  5. Sprinkle a handful of pine nuts on top.
  6. Bake in the preheated oven for 8-10 minutes, or until the edges are golden and the cheese is melted.
  7. Remove from the oven and top with fresh arugula.
  8. Drizzle with balsamic glaze before serving.

Veggie Supreme Naan Bread Pizza

Ingredients:

  • Naan bread
  • Marinara sauce
  • Shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Sliced mushrooms
  • Sliced bell peppers
  • Sliced black olives
  • Sliced red onion
  • Fresh basil leaves

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 425°F (220°C).
  2. Place the naan bread on a baking sheet.
  3. Spread a thin layer of marinara sauce over the naan bread.
  4. Sprinkle a generous amount of mozzarella cheese over the sauce.
  5. Arrange the sliced mushrooms, bell peppers, black olives, and red onion over the cheese.
  6. Bake in the preheated oven for 8-10 minutes, or until the cheese is bubbly and golden.
  7. Remove from the oven and garnish with fresh basil leaves.
  8. Slice and enjoy the veggie delight.

Naan bread pizza is a versatile canvas for your culinary creativity. With these quick and easy ideas, you can elevate your pizza game and delight your family and friends with delicious flavors that are sure to become favorites. Experiment with different toppings, sauces, and cheeses to create your own signature naan bread pizzas. The next time you’re craving pizza night, consider reaching for naan bread and embark on a culinary adventure that’s as easy as it is delicious.
