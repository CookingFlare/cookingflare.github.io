Spanish Inspired Cinnamon Churro Cupcakes Recipe


If you are a dessert lover on the hunt for a delightful treat with a twist, you’ve stumbled upon a gem! These Spanish Inspired Cinnamon Churro Cupcakes combine the beloved flavors of traditional churros with the tender, moist texture of cupcakes. Imagine the warm aroma of cinnamon and sugar wafting through your kitchen while you whip up this delicious recipe. Perfect for any occasion, these cupcakes are sure to impress your family and friends. Let’s dive into this delightful culinary adventure!

Ingredients

For the Cupcakes:

  • 1 ½ cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • ½ cup unsalted butter, softened
  • ½ cup whole milk
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 ½ teaspoons baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon

For the Cinnamon Sugar Topping:

  • ¼ cup granulated sugar
  • 1 tablespoon ground cinnamon
  • 1 tablespoon unsalted butter, melted

For the Churro Filling:

  • ½ cup unsweetened applesauce (or ½ cup of dulce de leche)
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1 tablespoon granulated sugar

For the Cream Cheese Frosting:

  • 8 oz cream cheese, softened
  • ½ cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 4 cups powdered sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon

Equipment Needed

  • Mixing bowls
  • Cupcake pan
  • Cupcake liners
  • Electric mixer
  • Piping bag with a large star tip
  • Spoon or spatula

Instructions

Step 1: Prepare the Cupcake Batter

  1. Preheat the Oven: Begin by preheating your oven to 350°F (175°C). Line a cupcake pan with cupcake liners to make the removal process easier after baking.

  2. Mix Dry Ingredients: In a medium bowl, whisk together the all-purpose flour, baking powder, salt, and ground cinnamon. This step ensures that all the leavening agents are evenly distributed throughout the flour.

  3. Cream the Butter and Sugar: In a large mixing bowl, use an electric mixer to cream together the softened butter and granulated sugar until light and fluffy, about 3-5 minutes. This process incorporates air into the mixture, giving your cupcakes a light texture.

  4. Add Eggs and Milk: Beat in the eggs, one at a time, making sure each is fully incorporated before adding the next. Then, add the milk and vanilla extract and mix until smooth.

  5. Combine Wet and Dry Ingredients: Gradually add the dry ingredient mixture to the wet ingredients, mixing on low speed until just combined. Be careful not to overmix; a few lumps are okay.

Step 2: Fill the Cupcake Liners

  1. Fill the Liners: Use a spoon or an ice cream scoop to fill each cupcake liner about two-thirds full with the batter. This allows room for the cupcakes to rise without overflowing.

Step 3: Bake the Cupcakes

  1. Bake: Place the cupcake pan in the preheated oven and bake for 18-20 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.

  2. Cool: Once baked, remove the cupcakes from the oven and let them cool in the pan for 5 minutes before transferring them to a wire rack to cool completely.

Step 4: Prepare the Churro Filling

  1. Mix Churro Filling: In a small bowl, combine the applesauce (or dulce de leche), ground cinnamon, and granulated sugar. This sweet filling will add a delightful surprise to your cupcakes.

Step 5: Prepare the Cinnamon Sugar Topping

  1. Cinnamon Sugar Mixture: In another small bowl, mix together the granulated sugar and ground cinnamon. This will create the signature churro topping.

  2. Brush with Butter: Once the cupcakes are completely cool, brush the tops with melted butter and then dip them into the cinnamon sugar mixture, ensuring they are fully coated.

Step 6: Make the Cream Cheese Frosting

  1. Cream Cheese Frosting: In a large mixing bowl, beat the softened cream cheese and unsalted butter together until smooth and creamy. Gradually add the powdered sugar and continue to mix until well combined. Stir in the vanilla extract and ground cinnamon.

Step 7: Assemble the Cupcakes

  1. Fill the Cupcakes: Using a piping bag or a knife, create a small hole in the center of each cupcake and fill it generously with the churro filling.

  2. Frost the Cupcakes: Pipe the cream cheese frosting on top of each cupcake in a swirl pattern, creating a beautiful finishing touch.

  3. Final Touch: Sprinkle a little extra cinnamon sugar on top of the frosting for an added crunch and flavor.

Serving Suggestions

These Spanish Inspired Cinnamon Churro Cupcakes are perfect for various occasions, from birthday parties to holiday gatherings. Serve them alongside a hot cup of coffee or a glass of cold milk for a delightful dessert experience. They also make for an impressive centerpiece when arranged on a cake stand.

Storage Tips

If you have any leftovers (which is rare given how delicious they are!), store the cupcakes in an airtight container at room temperature for up to three days. For longer storage, you can refrigerate them for up to a week. Just be sure to bring them back to room temperature before serving for the best flavor and texture.

Conclusion

Indulging in these Spanish Inspired Cinnamon Churro Cupcakes is like taking a bite of heaven. With their moist cupcake base, sweet churro filling, and creamy frosting, they encapsulate the essence of churros in a new, exciting form. So gather your ingredients, unleash your inner baker, and treat yourself to these delightful creations. Your taste buds will thank you!

Embrace the fusion of cultures and flavors with this unique twist on a classic dessert. Whether you are celebrating a special occasion or simply looking to satisfy your sweet tooth, these cupcakes will leave a lasting impression on everyone who tries them. Happy baking!
