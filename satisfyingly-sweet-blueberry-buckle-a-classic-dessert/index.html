Satisfyingly Sweet Blueberry Buckle: A Classic Dessert


If you’re a fan of classic desserts that are bursting with flavor, then you’re in for a treat with this Satisfyingly Sweet Blueberry Buckle recipe. This delightful dessert is a perfect way to showcase the fresh and juicy flavors of blueberries, combined with a crumbly and buttery streusel topping. Whether you’re hosting a dinner party or simply craving a sweet treat, this Blueberry Buckle is sure to impress.

Ingredients

For the Cake:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 2 cups fresh blueberries

For the Streusel Topping:

  • 1/2 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/4 cup unsalted butter, chilled and cubed
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C) and grease a 9-inch square baking pan.

  2. In a medium-sized bowl, whisk together the flour, baking powder, and salt. Set aside.

  3. In a large mixing bowl, cream together the softened butter and granulated sugar until light and fluffy. This can be done using an electric mixer or by hand.

  4. Add the eggs to the butter and sugar mixture, one at a time, beating well after each addition.

  5. Gradually add the dry ingredients to the wet ingredients, alternating with the milk. Begin and end with the dry ingredients, stirring just until combined.

  6. Gently fold in the fresh blueberries, being careful not to overmix the batter.

  7. Pour the batter into the greased baking pan, spreading it evenly.

  8. In a separate bowl, combine the flour, granulated sugar, cubed butter, and ground cinnamon for the streusel topping. Use your fingers or a pastry cutter to mix until crumbly.

  9. Sprinkle the streusel topping over the cake batter, covering it evenly.

  10. Bake in the preheated oven for 45-50 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.

  11. Allow the Blueberry Buckle to cool for at least 15 minutes before serving. This will allow the flavors to meld together and the streusel topping to become crisp.

  12. Serve warm or at room temperature, and enjoy the delightful combination of sweet blueberries and buttery streusel in every bite.

Tips and Variations

  • For an extra burst of flavor, add a teaspoon of lemon zest to the cake batter.
  • Serve the Blueberry Buckle with a dollop of whipped cream or a scoop of vanilla ice cream for a decadent treat.
  • Feel free to substitute the blueberries with other fresh berries, such as raspberries or blackberries, for a different twist on the classic recipe.
  • If you prefer a gluten-free version, you can use a gluten-free flour blend in place of the all-purpose flour.

So there you have it, a delightful recipe for Satisfyingly Sweet Blueberry Buckle. This classic dessert is sure to become a favorite amongst your family and friends. Enjoy the burst of juicy blueberries, the tender cake, and the crunchy streusel topping. Make this dessert today and indulge in a taste of nostalgia and pure deliciousness!
