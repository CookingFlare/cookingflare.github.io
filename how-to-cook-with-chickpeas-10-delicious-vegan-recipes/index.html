How to Cook with Chickpeas: 10 Delicious Vegan Recipes


Chickpeas, also known as garbanzo beans, are a versatile and nutrient-packed ingredient that can transform any meal into a satisfying and delicious dish. Rich in protein, fiber, and essential vitamins, chickpeas are an excellent choice for vegans, vegetarians, and anyone looking to incorporate more plant-based foods into their diet. Whether you’re new to cooking with chickpeas or a seasoned pro, there are endless ways to enjoy their mild, nutty flavor.

In this post, we’ll explore ten mouthwatering vegan chickpea recipes that are perfect for any occasion. These recipes are simple, flavorful, and sure to become your new favorites. From savory stews to crispy snacks, there’s something for everyone.

1. Chickpea Salad with Lemon-Tahini Dressing

A light and refreshing chickpea salad is an ideal way to start incorporating this legume into your meals. This simple yet flavorful dish is perfect as a side salad or a satisfying lunch. The lemon-tahini dressing adds a creamy and tangy element that perfectly complements the earthiness of the chickpeas.

Ingredients:

  • 1 can (15 oz) chickpeas, drained and rinsed
  • 1 cucumber, diced
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1/2 red onion, finely chopped
  • 1/4 cup fresh parsley, chopped
  • 1/4 cup tahini
  • 2 tbsp lemon juice
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a large bowl, combine the chickpeas, cucumber, tomatoes, onion, and parsley.
  2. In a small bowl, whisk together the tahini, lemon juice, olive oil, salt, and pepper. Add water to thin it out to your desired consistency.
  3. Pour the dressing over the salad and toss gently to combine.
  4. Serve immediately or refrigerate for later.

2. Spicy Chickpea Tacos

Tacos are a fun and customizable meal, and chickpeas make an excellent base for a vegan-friendly version. These spicy chickpea tacos are bursting with flavor and can be made as mild or hot as you like. Add your favorite toppings such as avocado, salsa, or cilantro to make these tacos even more delicious.

Ingredients:

  • 1 can (15 oz) chickpeas, drained and rinsed
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp cumin
  • 1 tsp chili powder
  • 1/2 tsp smoked paprika
  • 1/4 tsp cayenne pepper (optional)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 6 small corn tortillas
  • Toppings: avocado, salsa, cilantro, lime wedges

Instructions:

  1. Heat the olive oil in a skillet over medium heat. Add the chickpeas and cook for 5-7 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  2. Sprinkle the cumin, chili powder, paprika, cayenne (if using), salt, and pepper over the chickpeas and toss to coat. Continue cooking for another 3-4 minutes until the chickpeas are crispy and well-seasoned.
  3. Warm the tortillas in a dry skillet or microwave.
  4. To assemble, place the seasoned chickpeas on each tortilla and top with your favorite toppings.
  5. Serve with a squeeze of lime for an extra burst of freshness.

3. Chickpea and Spinach Curry

This rich and creamy chickpea and spinach curry is a hearty, satisfying dish that is perfect for a cozy dinner. The spices in this curry blend beautifully with the chickpeas, creating a warm and comforting meal. Serve it with basmati rice or naan for a complete meal.

Ingredients:

  • 1 can (15 oz) chickpeas, drained and rinsed
  • 4 cups fresh spinach
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1-inch piece of ginger, grated
  • 1 can (14 oz) coconut milk
  • 1 can (14 oz) diced tomatoes
  • 2 tbsp curry powder
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric
  • 1/2 tsp ground cumin
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Olive oil for cooking

Instructions:

  1. Heat olive oil in a large pan over medium heat. Add the onion and sauté for 5-7 minutes, until softened.
  2. Add the garlic and ginger, cooking for another 1-2 minutes until fragrant.
  3. Stir in the curry powder, turmeric, and cumin, and cook for 1 minute.
  4. Add the diced tomatoes and coconut milk to the pan, then bring to a simmer.
  5. Stir in the chickpeas and spinach, cooking until the spinach wilts and the chickpeas are heated through.
  6. Season with salt and pepper to taste and serve over rice or with naan.

4. Chickpea Patties with Cucumber Mint Yogurt Sauce

If you’re looking for a tasty and healthy alternative to traditional burgers or meat patties, these chickpea patties are a great option. Paired with a refreshing cucumber mint yogurt sauce, they make for a satisfying meal that’s perfect for lunch or dinner.

Ingredients for Patties:

  • 1 can (15 oz) chickpeas, drained and mashed
  • 1/4 cup breadcrumbs
  • 1/4 cup grated carrot
  • 1/4 cup chopped cilantro
  • 2 tbsp ground flaxseed (or egg)
  • 1 tsp ground cumin
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Olive oil for frying

Ingredients for Sauce:

  • 1/2 cup plain vegan yogurt
  • 1/2 cucumber, grated
  • 1 tbsp fresh mint, chopped
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • Salt to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a large bowl, combine the mashed chickpeas, breadcrumbs, grated carrot, cilantro, ground flaxseed, cumin, salt, and pepper. Mix well and form into 4-6 patties.
  2. Heat olive oil in a skillet over medium heat. Cook the patties for 4-5 minutes on each side until golden brown and crispy.
  3. To make the sauce, combine the yogurt, grated cucumber, mint, lemon juice, and salt in a small bowl. Stir until smooth.
  4. Serve the patties with the cucumber mint yogurt sauce on the side or drizzled on top.

5. Chickpea Stew with Sweet Potatoes and Kale

This comforting chickpea stew is a perfect one-pot meal for busy days. The combination of chickpeas, sweet potatoes, and kale creates a hearty and flavorful dish that is both filling and nutritious. The warm spices and rich broth bring everything together for a deliciously satisfying meal.

Ingredients:

  • 1 can (15 oz) chickpeas, drained and rinsed
  • 2 medium sweet potatoes, peeled and cubed
  • 4 cups kale, chopped
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can (14 oz) diced tomatoes
  • 4 cups vegetable broth
  • 1 tsp ground cumin
  • 1 tsp smoked paprika
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Olive oil for cooking

Instructions:

  1. Heat olive oil in a large pot over medium heat. Add the onion and sauté for 5-7 minutes, until softened.
  2. Add the garlic, cumin, and paprika, cooking for another 1-2 minutes until fragrant.
  3. Stir in the sweet potatoes, chickpeas, diced tomatoes, and vegetable broth. Bring the stew to a boil, then reduce the heat and simmer for 20-25 minutes until the sweet potatoes are tender.
  4. Add the kale and cook for an additional 5 minutes until wilted.
  5. Season with salt and pepper to taste and serve hot.

6. Chickpea Stir-Fry with Veggies

A quick and easy stir-fry is a great way to use chickpeas as a base for a flavorful and nutritious meal. Packed with colorful vegetables and a savory sauce, this chickpea stir-fry is a perfect dish for a busy weeknight.

Ingredients:

  • 1 can (15 oz) chickpeas, drained and rinsed
  • 1 cup broccoli florets
  • 1 red bell pepper, sliced
  • 1/2 onion, sliced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp sesame oil
  • 1 tbsp rice vinegar
  • 1 tsp grated ginger
  • 1 tbsp sesame seeds (optional)
  • Green onions for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Heat sesame oil in a large skillet or wok over medium heat. Add the onion, bell pepper, and broccoli and sauté for 5-7 minutes until tender-crisp.
  2. Add the garlic and ginger and cook for another minute.
  3. Stir in the chickpeas, soy sauce, rice vinegar, and sesame seeds (if using). Cook for another 2-3 minutes until everything is heated through.
  4. Garnish with green onions and serve immediately over rice or noodles.

7. Chickpea Hummus

Hummus is one of the most popular ways to enjoy chickpeas, and for good reason. This creamy, flavorful dip is perfect for snacking or as a spread for sandwiches. Making your own hummus is easy and allows you to customize the flavor to your liking.

Ingredients:

  • 1 can (15 oz) chickpeas, drained and rinsed
  • 2 tbsp tahini
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 clove garlic
  • 2 tbsp lemon juice
  • 1/2 tsp ground cumin
  • Salt to taste
  • Water to thin, if necessary

Instructions:

  1. In a food processor, combine the chickpeas, tahini, olive oil, garlic, lemon juice, cumin, and salt.
  2. Process until smooth, adding water a little at a time to achieve your desired consistency.
  3. Serve with pita bread, veggies, or as a spread on sandwiches.

8. Chickpea Buddha Bowl

A Buddha bowl is a balanced and nourishing meal that is perfect for meal prep. This chickpea version is packed with protein and fiber, making it a filling and energizing dish.

Ingredients:

  • 1 can (15 oz) chickpeas, drained and rinsed
  • 1 cup cooked quinoa or rice
  • 1 cup roasted vegetables (such as sweet potatoes, zucchini, or carrots)
  • 1/2 avocado, sliced
  • 2 tbsp tahini
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, layer the quinoa or rice, roasted vegetables, chickpeas, and avocado.
  2. Drizzle with tahini and lemon juice, then season with salt and pepper.
  3. Serve immediately or refrigerate for later.

9. Chickpea and Avocado Toast

For a quick and satisfying breakfast or snack, try chickpea and avocado toast. This easy-to-make dish combines the creaminess of avocado with the protein-packed goodness of chickpeas.

Ingredients:

  • 1 can (15 oz) chickpeas, drained and mashed
  • 2 slices whole-grain bread, toasted
  • 1 avocado, mashed
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Red pepper flakes (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Mash the chickpeas and avocado together in a bowl. Add the lemon juice, salt, and pepper and mix well.
  2. Spread the chickpea and avocado mixture onto the toasted bread.
  3. Sprinkle with red pepper flakes for an extra kick and serve.

10. Chickpea and Tomato Pasta

This simple yet satisfying pasta dish is perfect for a quick weeknight dinner. The combination of chickpeas and tomatoes creates a hearty sauce that pairs beautifully with your favorite pasta.

Ingredients:

  • 1 can (15 oz) chickpeas, drained and rinsed
  • 2 cups cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 8 oz pasta (such as spaghetti or penne)
  • 1/4 cup fresh basil, chopped
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Cook the pasta according to the package instructions.
  2. In a large pan, heat olive oil over medium heat. Add the garlic and sauté for 1-2 minutes.
  3. Add the cherry tomatoes and chickpeas and cook for 5-7 minutes, until the tomatoes soften and release their juices.
  4. Toss the cooked pasta into the pan with the chickpeas and tomatoes, adding the fresh basil. Season with salt and pepper.
  5. Serve immediately, garnished with more basil if desired.

These ten vegan chickpea recipes are not only delicious but also packed with nutrients that will keep you feeling energized and satisfied. Whether you’re in the mood for something light like a salad or something hearty like a stew, chickpeas are the perfect ingredient to create a variety of dishes. The best part is that they’re incredibly versatile and easy to cook with, making them a must-have pantry staple for anyone looking to eat more plant-based meals. So, grab a can of chickpeas and get cooking!
