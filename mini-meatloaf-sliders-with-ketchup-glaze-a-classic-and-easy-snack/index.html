Mini Meatloaf Sliders with Ketchup Glaze: A Classic and Easy Snack


If you’re looking for a delicious and easy snack that will satisfy your cravings, look no further than these Mini Meatloaf Sliders with Ketchup Glaze. This classic recipe takes the traditional meatloaf and transforms it into bite-sized sliders that are perfect for any occasion, whether it’s a family gathering or a game day party.

Ingredients

For the meatloaf:

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1/2 cup breadcrumbs
  • 1/4 cup milk
  • 1/4 cup finely chopped onion
  • 1/4 cup finely chopped green bell pepper
  • 1/4 cup finely chopped celery
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 1 egg, beaten
  • 1 tablespoon Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper

For the ketchup glaze:

  • 1/2 cup ketchup
  • 2 tablespoons brown sugar
  • 1 tablespoon Dijon mustard
  • 1 tablespoon apple cider vinegar

For the sliders:

  • Mini slider buns
  • Sliced cheddar cheese
  • Lettuce
  • Tomato slices
  • Pickles

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).

  2. In a large mixing bowl, combine the ground beef, breadcrumbs, milk, onion, bell pepper, celery, garlic, egg, Worcestershire sauce, salt, and black pepper. Mix well until all the ingredients are evenly incorporated.

  3. Shape the meat mixture into small patties, about the size of your slider buns. Place the patties on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper or a silicone baking mat.

  4. In a small bowl, whisk together the ketchup, brown sugar, Dijon mustard, and apple cider vinegar to make the glaze. Brush the glaze over the top of each mini meatloaf patty, making sure they are well coated.

  5. Bake the mini meatloaf sliders in the preheated oven for 20-25 minutes, or until the internal temperature reaches 160°F (71°C). The glaze should be sticky and caramelized.

  6. While the sliders are baking, prepare your slider buns and toppings. Slice the buns in half and lightly toast them if desired. Wash and dry the lettuce and tomato slices, and slice the pickles.

  7. Once the sliders are cooked, remove them from the oven and let them rest for a few minutes.

  8. Assemble the sliders by placing a mini meatloaf patty on the bottom half of each slider bun. Top with a slice of cheddar cheese, lettuce, tomato, and pickles. Finish with the top half of the bun.

  9. Serve your Mini Meatloaf Sliders with Ketchup Glaze immediately and enjoy!

Tips and Variations

  • Feel free to customize your mini meatloaf sliders with your favorite toppings, such as bacon, avocado, or caramelized onions.
  • If you prefer a spicier glaze, add a pinch of cayenne pepper or a few dashes of hot sauce to the ketchup mixture.
  • These sliders are great for meal prep! Make a batch ahead of time and store them in the refrigerator for quick and easy lunches or snacks throughout the week.

So there you have it - a classic and easy snack that will satisfy your cravings. These Mini Meatloaf Sliders with Ketchup Glaze are sure to be a hit with friends and family. Give them a try and let us know what you think! Enjoy!
