Perfectly Crusty Olive Rosemary Bread: A Mediterranean Delight


There’s nothing quite like the aroma of freshly baked bread wafting through your home. Today, I want to share with you a recipe for a Mediterranean-inspired bread that will transport you to the sunny shores of the Mediterranean Sea. This Perfectly Crusty Olive Rosemary Bread is a delight for the senses, with its crunchy crust, fragrant rosemary, and bursts of briny olives.

Ingredients

To make this heavenly bread, you’ll need the following ingredients:

  • 3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 ½ teaspoons instant yeast
  • 1 ½ teaspoons salt
  • 1 ¼ cups warm water
  • 2 tablespoons extra-virgin olive oil
  • 1 cup pitted Kalamata olives, roughly chopped
  • 2 tablespoons fresh rosemary, finely chopped

Instructions

Let’s dive into the step-by-step process of making this delectable bread:

  1. In a large mixing bowl, combine the flour, instant yeast, and salt. Mix well to evenly distribute the yeast and salt throughout the flour.

  2. Make a well in the center of the flour mixture and pour in the warm water and olive oil. Using a wooden spoon or your hands, mix the ingredients until a shaggy dough forms.

  3. Turn the dough out onto a lightly floured surface and knead for about 10 minutes, or until the dough becomes smooth and elastic. If the dough feels too sticky, add a little more flour, a tablespoon at a time, until it reaches the desired consistency.

  4. Place the dough in a clean, greased bowl and cover it with a damp kitchen towel. Allow the dough to rise in a warm spot for about 1 hour, or until it has doubled in size.

  5. Preheat your oven to 450°F (230°C). If you have a baking stone, place it in the oven to heat up as well. If not, a regular baking sheet will do just fine.

  6. Once the dough has risen, gently punch it down to release any air bubbles. Turn it out onto a lightly floured surface and sprinkle the olives and rosemary evenly over the dough. Fold and knead the dough a few times to incorporate the olives and rosemary.

  7. Shape the dough into a round or oval loaf, and place it on your preheated baking stone or baking sheet. Cover it loosely with a clean kitchen towel and let it rise for another 30 minutes.

  8. Using a sharp knife or a razor blade, make a few shallow slashes on the top of the loaf. This will help the bread expand and create a beautiful crust.

  9. Carefully transfer the bread to the hot oven and bake for 25-30 minutes, or until the crust is golden brown and the bread sounds hollow when tapped on the bottom.

  10. Remove the bread from the oven and let it cool on a wire rack before slicing. As tempting as it may be, try to resist cutting into it immediately, as it needs a little time to cool and set.

Serving Suggestions

This Perfectly Crusty Olive Rosemary Bread is fantastic on its own, but it also pairs wonderfully with a variety of Mediterranean-inspired dishes. Serve it alongside a bowl of homemade hummus, or use it to dip into a tangy balsamic vinegar and olive oil dip. You can also slice it and toast it for crostini, topping it with your favorite bruschetta or tapenade. The possibilities are endless!

Final Thoughts

Baking bread is not only a culinary adventure but also a therapeutic experience. With this recipe, you’ll be able to create a loaf of Perfectly Crusty Olive Rosemary Bread that will transport you to the Mediterranean with every bite. So, grab your apron, dust off your baking skills, and embark on a journey of flavors and aromas that will delight your taste buds and leave you craving more. Enjoy!
