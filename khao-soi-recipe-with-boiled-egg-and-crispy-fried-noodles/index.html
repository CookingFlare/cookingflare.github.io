Khao Soi Recipe with Boiled Egg and Crispy Fried Noodles


If you’re a fan of Thai cuisine, then you’re in for a treat with this Khao Soi recipe. Hailing from the northern regions of Thailand, Khao Soi is a fragrant and flavorful coconut curry noodle soup that’s sure to tantalize your taste buds. This particular rendition of Khao Soi comes with the added goodness of boiled eggs and crispy fried noodles, taking the dish to a whole new level of deliciousness.

Ingredients

For the Khao Soi Paste:

  • 4 dried red chilies, seeded and soaked in water
  • 4 shallots, roughly chopped
  • 6 garlic cloves
  • 1 thumb-sized piece of ginger, peeled and chopped
  • 1 tablespoon ground turmeric
  • 1 tablespoon ground coriander
  • 1 teaspoon shrimp paste
  • 1 stalk lemongrass, tough outer leaves removed and finely sliced
  • Zest of 1 lime

For the Khao Soi Soup:

  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 3 tablespoons Khao Soi paste
  • 2 cups coconut milk
  • 3 cups chicken stock
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons fish sauce
  • 1 tablespoon palm sugar
  • 8 chicken thighs, bone-in and skin-on
  • Salt, to taste

For Serving:

  • 500g egg noodles
  • Vegetable oil, for frying
  • 4 boiled eggs, halved
  • 1 red onion, finely sliced
  • Fresh coriander leaves
  • Lime wedges

Instructions

Making the Khao Soi Paste:

  1. In a blender or food processor, combine the soaked red chilies, shallots, garlic, ginger, turmeric, coriander, shrimp paste, lemongrass, and lime zest. Blend until a smooth paste forms. Set aside.

Cooking the Khao Soi Soup:

  1. Heat the vegetable oil in a large pot over medium heat. Add the Khao Soi paste and cook for a few minutes until fragrant.
  2. Pour in the coconut milk and chicken stock, then add the soy sauce, fish sauce, and palm sugar. Stir to combine.
  3. Add the chicken thighs to the pot and bring the soup to a gentle simmer. Cook for 30-40 minutes until the chicken is tender and cooked through. Remove the chicken from the pot and shred the meat. Season the soup with salt to taste.

Preparing the Crispy Fried Noodles:

  1. Cook the egg noodles according to the package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a separate pan, heat vegetable oil over medium-high heat. Once hot, add the cooked egg noodles in batches and fry until golden and crispy. Remove from the oil and drain on paper towels.

Assembling the Khao Soi Bowls:

  1. Divide the cooked egg noodles among serving bowls. Ladle the fragrant Khao Soi soup over the noodles.
  2. Top each bowl with shredded chicken, halved boiled eggs, sliced red onion, and a sprinkle of fresh coriander leaves.
  3. Serve hot with lime wedges on the side for squeezing over the soup.

Enjoy Your Khao Soi Delight!

This Khao Soi recipe with boiled egg and crispy fried noodles is a wonderful combination of rich, aromatic flavors and contrasting textures. The creamy coconut curry base, tender chicken, and the crunch of the fried noodles come together to create a truly satisfying dish. Whether you’re a fan of Thai cuisine or looking to explore new flavors, this Khao Soi recipe is a must-try for any food enthusiast. So, roll up your sleeves, gather the ingredients, and embark on a culinary journey to the northern regions of Thailand right in your own kitchen!
