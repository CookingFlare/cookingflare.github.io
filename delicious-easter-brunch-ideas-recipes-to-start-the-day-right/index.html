Delicious Easter Brunch Ideas: Recipes to Start the Day Right


Easter is a time for celebration and what better way to kick-start the day than with a delicious brunch spread? Whether you’re hosting a gathering or simply want to treat yourself and your loved ones to a special morning meal, we’ve got you covered with some delectable Easter brunch ideas. From savory dishes to sweet treats, these recipes are sure to make your Easter morning extra memorable.

1. Cheesy Potato Casserole

Ingredients:

  • 6 cups frozen shredded hash browns
  • 2 cups shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1 cup sour cream
  • 1/2 cup melted butter
  • 1/2 cup chopped green onions
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C) and grease a 9x13-inch baking dish.
  2. In a large bowl, combine the frozen hash browns, shredded cheddar cheese, sour cream, melted butter, chopped green onions, salt, black pepper, and garlic powder. Mix well.
  3. Transfer the mixture to the prepared baking dish and spread it evenly.
  4. Bake for 45-50 minutes or until the top is golden brown and the cheese is melted and bubbly.
  5. Let it cool for a few minutes before serving.

2. Smoked Salmon and Avocado Toast

Ingredients:

  • 4 slices of whole wheat bread
  • 4 ounces smoked salmon
  • 1 ripe avocado, sliced
  • 1 tablespoon freshly squeezed lemon juice
  • 1 tablespoon chopped fresh dill
  • Salt and black pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Toast the slices of whole wheat bread until golden brown.
  2. In a small bowl, mash the avocado with lemon juice, chopped fresh dill, salt, and black pepper.
  3. Spread the avocado mixture evenly on each slice of toasted bread.
  4. Top the avocado spread with smoked salmon slices.
  5. Garnish with additional dill, if desired.
  6. Serve immediately.

3. Berry Spinach Salad

Ingredients:

  • 6 cups baby spinach
  • 1 cup mixed berries (strawberries, blueberries, raspberries)
  • 1/2 cup crumbled feta cheese
  • 1/4 cup sliced almonds
  • Balsamic vinaigrette dressing

Instructions:

  1. In a large bowl, combine the baby spinach, mixed berries, crumbled feta cheese, and sliced almonds.
  2. Drizzle with balsamic vinaigrette dressing and toss gently to coat.
  3. Serve chilled.

4. Lemon Blueberry Pancakes

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons granulated sugar
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 1/2 cups buttermilk
  • 1/4 cup fresh lemon juice
  • Zest of 1 lemon
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 tablespoons melted butter
  • 1 cup fresh blueberries

Instructions:

  1. In a large bowl, whisk together the flour, sugar, baking powder, baking soda, and salt.
  2. In a separate bowl, whisk together the buttermilk, lemon juice, lemon zest, eggs, and melted butter.
  3. Pour the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients and stir until just combined. Do not overmix.
  4. Gently fold in the fresh blueberries.
  5. Heat a non-stick skillet or griddle over medium heat and lightly grease with butter or cooking spray.
  6. Pour 1/4 cup of batter onto the skillet for each pancake. Cook until bubbles form on the surface, then flip and cook until golden brown.
  7. Serve the pancakes warm with maple syrup and additional blueberries, if desired.

5. Cinnamon Roll French Toast Casserole

Ingredients:

  • 1 package refrigerated cinnamon rolls with icing
  • 4 large eggs
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • Maple syrup for serving

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C) and grease a baking dish.
  2. Cut the cinnamon rolls into small pieces and spread them evenly in the prepared baking dish.
  3. In a mixing bowl, whisk together the eggs, milk, and vanilla extract.
  4. Pour the egg mixture over the cinnamon roll pieces, making sure they are all coated.
  5. Bake for 25-30 minutes or until the top is golden brown and the center is set.
  6. Remove from the oven and let it cool slightly.
  7. Drizzle the icing that came with the cinnamon rolls over the casserole.
  8. Serve warm with maple syrup.

Enjoy these delightful Easter brunch recipes with your loved ones and make lasting memories. Happy Easter!
