Homemade Bagels: Chewy and Delicious


Who doesn’t love a warm and chewy bagel fresh out of the oven? Making homemade bagels may seem like a daunting task, but with a little patience and some basic ingredients, you can achieve bakery-quality bagels right in your own kitchen. In this recipe, I’ll guide you through the process of making delicious homemade bagels that are both chewy and satisfying. So, let’s get started!

Ingredients

  • 4 cups of bread flour
  • 2 teaspoons of instant yeast
  • 2 tablespoons of sugar
  • 1 ½ teaspoons of salt
  • 1 ½ cups of warm water
  • Optional toppings: sesame seeds, poppy seeds, everything seasoning, etc.

Instructions

  1. In a large mixing bowl, combine the bread flour, instant yeast, sugar, and salt. Mix well to evenly distribute the ingredients.

  2. Gradually add the warm water to the dry ingredients. Use a wooden spoon or your hands to mix until a rough dough forms.

  3. Transfer the dough onto a lightly floured surface and knead for about 10 minutes, or until the dough becomes smooth and elastic. This step is crucial in developing the bagel’s chewy texture, so don’t rush it!

  4. Once the dough is well-kneaded, shape it into a ball and place it back into the mixing bowl. Cover the bowl with a clean kitchen towel or plastic wrap and let the dough rise in a warm place for about 1 hour, or until it doubles in size.

  5. Preheat your oven to 425°F (220°C). Line a baking sheet with parchment paper and set aside.

  6. After the dough has risen, gently punch it down to release any air bubbles. Transfer the dough onto a clean surface and divide it into 8 equal pieces.

  7. Take one piece of dough and roll it into a ball. Then, using your thumb, poke a hole through the center of the ball and gently stretch it to form a bagel shape. Repeat this step with the remaining pieces of dough.

  8. Place the shaped bagels onto the prepared baking sheet. Cover them with a kitchen towel and let them rest for about 20 minutes to allow them to rise slightly.

  9. In a large pot, bring water to a boil. Once the water is boiling, carefully drop the bagels, one or two at a time, into the water. Boil them for about 1 minute on each side, then remove them from the water using a slotted spoon and place them back onto the baking sheet.

  10. If desired, sprinkle your chosen toppings onto the boiled bagels. This is the perfect opportunity to get creative and experiment with different flavors!

  11. Bake the bagels in the preheated oven for about 20 minutes, or until they turn golden brown and develop a crispy crust.

  12. Once baked, remove the bagels from the oven and let them cool on a wire rack. This step is essential for achieving the perfect texture.

  13. Once the bagels are completely cooled, slice them in half and enjoy them as is or with your favorite spreads and toppings. Cream cheese, lox, or avocado are all popular choices!

Conclusion

Congratulations! You’ve successfully made your own batch of homemade bagels. The process may take a little time and effort, but the end result is well worth it. The chewy texture and delicious flavor of these bagels will surely impress your family and friends. So, why not give it a try and experience the satisfaction of homemade goodness? Happy baking!
