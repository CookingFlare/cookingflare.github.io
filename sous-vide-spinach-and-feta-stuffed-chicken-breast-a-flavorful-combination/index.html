Sous Vide Spinach and Feta Stuffed Chicken Breast: A Flavorful Combination


Are you ready to elevate your cooking game with a dish that is sure to impress? Look no further than this delectable Sous Vide Spinach and Feta Stuffed Chicken Breast recipe. Tender chicken breast, filled with a savory mixture of spinach and feta cheese, cooked to perfection using the Sous Vide method. This dish is a flavorful combination that will have your taste buds dancing with every bite.

Ingredients

  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 2 cups fresh spinach, chopped
  • 1 cup feta cheese, crumbled
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Olive oil

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Chicken: Using a sharp knife, carefully slice a pocket into each chicken breast, being careful not to cut all the way through. Season the inside and outside of the chicken breasts with salt and pepper.

  2. Make the Filling: In a bowl, combine the chopped spinach, crumbled feta cheese, minced garlic, dried oregano, and dried thyme. Mix well to ensure all the ingredients are evenly distributed.

  3. Stuff the Chicken: Gently stuff each chicken breast with the spinach and feta mixture, being careful not to overfill.

  4. Vacuum Seal the Chicken: Place each stuffed chicken breast into a separate vacuum-sealable bag. Drizzle a small amount of olive oil over each chicken breast to help with the cooking process. Vacuum seal the bags, ensuring they are airtight.

  5. Sous Vide Cooking: Preheat your water bath to 145°F (63°C). Once the water reaches the desired temperature, carefully place the vacuum-sealed chicken breasts into the water bath. Cook for 1.5 to 2 hours, ensuring that the chicken reaches an internal temperature of 165°F (74°C).

  6. Finishing Touch: Once the chicken is cooked, remove it from the water bath and carefully open the vacuum-sealed bags. Heat a skillet over medium-high heat and sear the chicken breasts for 1-2 minutes on each side to achieve a golden brown crust.

  7. Serve and Enjoy: Transfer the chicken to a cutting board and allow it to rest for a few minutes. Then, slice the chicken and serve it with your favorite side dishes. Enjoy the flavorful combination of tender chicken, savory spinach, and creamy feta cheese.

Why Sous Vide?

Sous Vide cooking is a method that involves cooking food in vacuum-sealed bags in a precisely controlled water bath. This technique ensures that the food is cooked evenly and retains its natural flavors and juices. When it comes to delicate proteins like chicken breast, Sous Vide cooking results in a tender and moist texture that is difficult to achieve through traditional cooking methods.

Flavorful and Versatile

This Sous Vide Spinach and Feta Stuffed Chicken Breast recipe is not only bursting with flavor but also incredibly versatile. You can customize the filling with different herbs, cheeses, or even add sun-dried tomatoes for a unique twist. The Sous Vide method allows for consistent and precise cooking, ensuring that each bite is as delicious as the last.
