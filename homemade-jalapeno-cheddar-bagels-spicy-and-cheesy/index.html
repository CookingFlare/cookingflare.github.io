Homemade Jalapeno Cheddar Bagels: Spicy and Cheesy


If you’re a fan of bagels and love a little kick in your food, then homemade jalapeno cheddar bagels are the perfect treat for you. These spicy and cheesy delights are a unique twist on the classic bagel, adding a burst of flavor to your breakfast or brunch. In this recipe, we’ll guide you through the process of creating these delectable bagels from scratch. So, roll up your sleeves, preheat your oven, and let’s get baking!

Ingredients

To make these homemade jalapeno cheddar bagels, you’ll need the following ingredients:

  • 4 cups of bread flour
  • 1 tablespoon of granulated sugar
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons of salt
  • 1 tablespoon of active dry yeast
  • 1 1/2 cups of warm water
  • 1 cup of shredded cheddar cheese
  • 2 jalapenos, finely chopped
  • 1 egg, beaten (for egg wash)
  • Cornmeal (for dusting)

Instructions

Step 1: Activate the Yeast

In a small bowl, combine the warm water, sugar, and yeast. Let it sit for about 5-10 minutes until the mixture becomes frothy.

Step 2: Mix the Dough

In a large mixing bowl, combine the bread flour and salt. Once the yeast mixture is frothy, pour it into the flour mixture. Stir until a dough starts to form.

Step 3: Knead the Dough

Transfer the dough to a floured surface and knead it for about 10 minutes until it becomes smooth and elastic. Place the dough in a greased bowl, cover it with a clean kitchen towel, and let it rise for about 1 hour or until it doubles in size.

Step 4: Add the Cheese and Jalapenos

Once the dough has risen, punch it down and knead in the shredded cheddar cheese and finely chopped jalapenos until they are evenly distributed.

Step 5: Shape the Bagels

Divide the dough into 8 equal portions and shape each portion into a ball. Then, using your thumb, poke a hole through the center of each ball and gently stretch it to form the bagel shape.

Step 6: Let the Bagels Rise

Place the shaped bagels on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper and dusted with cornmeal. Cover them with a kitchen towel and let them rise for another 30 minutes.

Step 7: Boil and Bake

Preheat your oven to 425°F (220°C). In a large pot, bring water to a boil and carefully drop the bagels in batches. Boil them for 1-2 minutes on each side, then transfer them back to the baking sheet. Brush the tops of the bagels with the beaten egg wash and bake for 20-25 minutes, or until they turn golden brown.

Step 8: Enjoy!

Once the homemade jalapeno cheddar bagels are baked to perfection, allow them to cool for a few minutes before serving. These bagels are best enjoyed fresh, either on their own or with a spread of cream cheese for an extra burst of flavor.

Conclusion

Homemade jalapeno cheddar bagels are a delightful combination of spicy and cheesy, perfect for those who enjoy a bit of heat in their baked goods. Whether you’re a seasoned baker or new to the world of bagel making, this recipe offers a fun and flavorful twist on a classic favorite. So, why not roll up your sleeves and give these homemade jalapeno cheddar bagels a try? They’re sure to spice up your breakfast or brunch routine in the most delicious way possible!
