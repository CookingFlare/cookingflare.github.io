Juicy Peach and Blueberry Crisp: A Perfect Summer Dessert


Summer is the season of vibrant, juicy fruits, and what better way to celebrate the abundance of nature’s goodness than with a delightful Peach and Blueberry Crisp? This dessert is the epitome of summer indulgence, combining the sweetness of ripe peaches with the burst of tangy blueberries, all nestled beneath a crispy, golden topping. Whether you’re hosting a backyard barbecue, a family gathering, or simply craving a taste of summer, this Juicy Peach and Blueberry Crisp is sure to be a hit.

Ingredients

For the Fruit Filling:

  • 6 ripe peaches, peeled, pitted, and sliced
  • 1 cup fresh blueberries
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 2 tablespoons cornstarch
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon

For the Crisp Topping:

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup rolled oats
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/2 cup brown sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, cold and cut into small pieces

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C) and lightly grease a 9x13-inch baking dish.

  2. In a large bowl, gently toss together the sliced peaches, blueberries, granulated sugar, cornstarch, lemon juice, vanilla extract, and ground cinnamon until the fruits are evenly coated.

  3. Transfer the fruit mixture to the prepared baking dish, spreading it out into an even layer.

  4. In a separate bowl, combine the flour, rolled oats, granulated sugar, brown sugar, ground cinnamon, and salt. Add the cold, diced butter, and use your fingers to work the butter into the dry ingredients until the mixture resembles coarse crumbs.

  5. Sprinkle the crisp topping evenly over the fruit filling.

  6. Place the baking dish in the preheated oven and bake for 35-40 minutes, or until the fruit is bubbling, and the topping is golden brown.

  7. Remove the Juicy Peach and Blueberry Crisp from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes before serving.

Serving Suggestions

This delightful dessert is best enjoyed warm, either on its own or topped with a scoop of vanilla ice cream or a dollop of freshly whipped cream. The contrast of the warm, fruity filling and the cold, creamy accompaniment is a match made in dessert heaven.

Tips and Variations

  • For an extra crunch, consider adding a handful of chopped nuts, such as almonds or pecans, to the crisp topping.
  • Experiment with different fruit combinations, such as peach and raspberry, or blueberry and blackberry, to create your own signature fruit crisp.
  • Leftovers can be stored in the refrigerator and reheated in the oven for a delicious treat the next day.

In Conclusion

The Juicy Peach and Blueberry Crisp is a celebration of summer’s bounty, offering a symphony of flavors and textures in every spoonful. Whether you’re a seasoned baker or a novice in the kitchen, this dessert is a simple yet impressive addition to your repertoire. So, embrace the essence of summer by savoring this luscious, fruit-filled crisp with your loved ones, and let it become a cherished part of your summer traditions.
