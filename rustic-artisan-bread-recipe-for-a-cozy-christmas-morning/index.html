Rustic Artisan Bread Recipe for a Cozy Christmas Morning


There’s something truly magical about waking up on Christmas morning to the comforting aroma of freshly baked bread. The warmth and rustic charm of artisan bread can elevate your holiday breakfast to a whole new level. In this recipe, we’ll guide you through the process of making a delicious Rustic Artisan Bread that will fill your home with joy and delight.

Ingredients

  • 4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 teaspoons instant yeast
  • 2 teaspoons salt
  • 2 cups warm water
  • 1 tablespoon honey
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil

Instructions

  1. In a large mixing bowl, combine the all-purpose flour, instant yeast, and salt. Mix well to distribute the ingredients evenly.

  2. In a separate bowl, whisk together the warm water, honey, and olive oil until the honey is dissolved.

  3. Slowly pour the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients while stirring with a wooden spoon. Continue mixing until the dough comes together and forms a sticky, shaggy mass.

  4. Cover the bowl with a clean kitchen towel and let the dough rest for 12-18 hours at room temperature. This long fermentation period allows the flavors to develop and gives the bread its characteristic texture.

  5. After the resting period, preheat your oven to 450°F (230°C). Place a Dutch oven or a large oven-safe pot with a lid inside the oven to heat up.

  6. Meanwhile, generously flour a clean work surface and turn the dough out onto it. Gently shape the dough into a round or oval shape, being careful not to deflate it too much.

  7. Carefully remove the hot pot from the oven and place the shaped dough inside. Cover the pot with the lid and return it to the oven.

  8. Bake the bread for 30 minutes with the lid on. Then, remove the lid and bake for an additional 15-20 minutes, or until the bread is golden brown and sounds hollow when tapped on the bottom.

  9. Once the bread is baked, carefully remove it from the pot and let it cool on a wire rack for at least 1 hour before slicing. This will allow the interior of the bread to set properly.

  10. Serve the Rustic Artisan Bread warm or at room temperature, sliced thickly and accompanied by butter or your favorite spreads. Pair it with a cup of hot cocoa or freshly brewed coffee for the perfect cozy Christmas morning treat.

Tips and Variations

  • For a festive twist, consider adding dried cranberries, chopped walnuts, or rosemary to the dough before the long fermentation period.
  • Experiment with different types of flour, such as whole wheat or rye, to create unique flavors and textures.
  • If you don’t have a Dutch oven or an oven-safe pot with a lid, you can use a baking sheet instead. Place a shallow pan filled with water on the bottom rack of the oven to create steam and achieve a crispy crust.

Enjoy the process of baking this Rustic Artisan Bread and savor the joy it brings to your Christmas morning. The rustic charm and delicious taste of this homemade bread will make your holiday breakfast truly memorable. So gather your loved ones around the table, spread some holiday cheer, and indulge in this cozy treat. Merry Christmas!
