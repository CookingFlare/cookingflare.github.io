Quick and Easy Dinners You Can Make in Under 20 Minutes


Life gets busy, and sometimes you find yourself staring at the clock, wondering what to make for dinner that won’t take hours to prepare. Whether you’re rushing after a long workday, taking care of family, or just craving something simple and satisfying, having a list of quick and easy dinner ideas is a game-changer. The good news? There are plenty of meals you can whip up in under 20 minutes that are both delicious and nutritious.

From savory stir-fries to hearty salads, here are some of the best quick and easy dinners that will satisfy your hunger and save you time. These recipes not only reduce your stress levels but also add a burst of flavor to your meal rotation. Let’s dive into these speedy dinner ideas!

1. One-Pan Lemon Garlic Chicken with Veggies

If you’re looking for a healthy, low-maintenance meal, a one-pan dinner is the perfect solution. This lemon garlic chicken with roasted vegetables is an ideal choice that doesn’t require much prep time.

Ingredients:

  • 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1 zucchini, sliced
  • 1 bell pepper, chopped
  • 1 small red onion, chopped
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 teaspoon lemon zest
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley for garnish

Directions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C).
  2. Place the chicken breasts on a baking sheet and drizzle with olive oil.
  3. Sprinkle minced garlic, lemon zest, salt, and pepper over the chicken.
  4. Add the chopped veggies around the chicken.
  5. Roast for about 15 minutes or until the chicken reaches an internal temperature of 165°F (74°C).
  6. Drizzle with lemon juice and garnish with fresh parsley before serving.

This meal is perfect for those nights when you’re craving something light but packed with flavor. The garlic and lemon give the chicken a zesty kick, while the vegetables provide a nice balance.

2. Quick Beef Stir-Fry

A beef stir-fry is one of the quickest meals to make, especially when you have all your ingredients prepped and ready. This dish is loaded with colorful vegetables and tender beef strips, making it a perfect dinner for those busy nights.

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb (450g) beef sirloin or flank steak, thinly sliced
  • 1 cup bell peppers, sliced
  • 1 cup broccoli florets
  • 1/2 onion, thinly sliced
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon oyster sauce
  • 1 tablespoon sesame oil
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 teaspoon ginger, minced
  • Cooked rice, for serving

Directions:

  1. Heat the sesame oil in a large skillet or wok over medium-high heat.
  2. Add the sliced beef and stir-fry for about 2-3 minutes until browned. Remove from the pan and set aside.
  3. In the same pan, add the garlic, ginger, and onion, and cook for 1 minute until fragrant.
  4. Add the bell peppers and broccoli, and cook for another 3-4 minutes until tender.
  5. Return the beef to the pan and pour in the soy sauce and oyster sauce. Stir to combine and heat for an additional 2 minutes.
  6. Serve the stir-fry over the cooked rice.

This stir-fry is perfect for those evenings when you want something hearty but don’t want to spend much time in the kitchen. The beef is juicy and tender, and the sauce adds just the right amount of savory flavor.

3. Shrimp Tacos with Cilantro Lime Slaw

Tacos are always a crowd-pleaser, and shrimp tacos are no exception. These shrimp tacos with a tangy cilantro lime slaw are fresh, vibrant, and incredibly easy to prepare. They’re perfect for a quick dinner or even a fun family meal.

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb (450g) shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 8 small corn or flour tortillas
  • 1 cup shredded cabbage
  • 1/4 cup chopped cilantro
  • 2 tablespoons lime juice
  • 1 tablespoon mayonnaise
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Olive oil for cooking

Directions:

  1. In a bowl, combine the shredded cabbage, cilantro, lime juice, mayonnaise, cumin, salt, and pepper. Set aside.
  2. Heat a little olive oil in a pan over medium-high heat. Season the shrimp with salt, pepper, and a pinch of cumin.
  3. Cook the shrimp for 2-3 minutes per side, or until they turn pink and opaque.
  4. Warm the tortillas in a dry skillet for about 30 seconds on each side.
  5. To assemble the tacos, place a few shrimp on each tortilla and top with the cilantro lime slaw.

These shrimp tacos are bursting with flavor, and the cilantro lime slaw adds the perfect balance of creaminess and tang. They’re great for any time of year and come together in less than 20 minutes!

4. Caprese Salad with Grilled Chicken

If you’re looking for a light and refreshing dinner, a Caprese salad with grilled chicken is an excellent option. The combination of juicy tomatoes, creamy mozzarella, and grilled chicken is simple but satisfying.

Ingredients:

  • 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 2 large tomatoes, sliced
  • 1 ball fresh mozzarella, sliced
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • 2 tablespoons balsamic glaze
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Directions:

  1. Season the chicken breasts with salt and pepper, and grill them on medium-high heat for about 5-6 minutes on each side, or until cooked through.
  2. Arrange the sliced tomatoes, mozzarella, and fresh basil on a plate.
  3. Slice the grilled chicken and add it on top of the salad.
  4. Drizzle with olive oil and balsamic glaze before serving.

This meal is perfect for warmer months when you’re craving something light but still full of flavor. The balsamic glaze adds a rich, tangy note to the creamy mozzarella, and the grilled chicken makes it a more substantial meal.

5. Vegetable Fried Rice

Fried rice is the epitome of a quick, one-pan meal. With the addition of mixed vegetables and a few pantry staples, you can have a delicious, hearty meal in just minutes.

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups cooked rice (preferably day-old rice)
  • 1 cup mixed vegetables (peas, carrots, corn)
  • 2 eggs, lightly beaten
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon sesame oil
  • 1/4 teaspoon garlic powder
  • Green onions for garnish

Directions:

  1. Heat the sesame oil in a large pan or wok over medium heat.
  2. Add the mixed vegetables and cook for 2-3 minutes until heated through.
  3. Push the vegetables to one side of the pan and scramble the eggs on the other side.
  4. Once the eggs are cooked, add the rice and soy sauce. Stir everything together and cook for 3-4 minutes.
  5. Garnish with chopped green onions before serving.

This vegetable fried rice is the perfect option for a meatless meal or a quick side dish. The eggs add richness to the rice, while the vegetables provide a burst of color and nutrition.

6. Chicken Caesar Wraps

If you’re craving something easy and filling, a Chicken Caesar Wrap is the way to go. With tender grilled chicken, crispy romaine lettuce, and creamy Caesar dressing, this wrap is a quick and delicious dinner option.

Ingredients:

  • 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 4 large flour tortillas
  • 2 cups chopped romaine lettuce
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/4 cup Caesar dressing
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Directions:

  1. Season the chicken breasts with salt and pepper and cook them on medium heat for 5-7 minutes per side, or until fully cooked.
  2. Slice the chicken into thin strips.
  3. In a large bowl, toss the romaine lettuce with Caesar dressing and grated Parmesan.
  4. Lay the tortillas flat and place a portion of the lettuce mixture in the center, followed by the sliced chicken.
  5. Roll up the wraps tightly and serve.

These wraps are a great option for a quick dinner that feels a bit indulgent but is still light and healthy. You can even add some cherry tomatoes or bacon bits for extra flavor.

7. Pasta Aglio e Olio

Sometimes, the simplest meals are the best. Pasta aglio e olio is a traditional Italian dish made with just a few ingredients—garlic, olive oil, and red pepper flakes. It’s easy to prepare and packed with flavor.

Ingredients:

  • 8 oz (225g) spaghetti
  • 4 garlic cloves, thinly sliced
  • 1/4 teaspoon red pepper flakes
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh parsley, chopped (optional)

Directions:

  1. Cook the pasta according to the package instructions.
  2. While the pasta is cooking, heat the olive oil in a pan over medium heat.
  3. Add the garlic and red pepper flakes, and cook for about 2-3 minutes until the garlic is golden brown.
  4. Drain the pasta and add it to the pan with the garlic oil. Toss to coat.
  5. Garnish with fresh parsley before serving.

Pasta aglio e olio is the perfect option for those nights when you need something quick but comforting. The garlic and olive oil bring out the natural flavors of the pasta, making this a delicious and satisfying dish.

Conclusion

Having a few quick and easy dinner recipes in your back pocket can save you a lot of time and stress. Whether you’re in the mood for chicken, shrimp, or a hearty stir-fry, these meals can be prepared in under 20 minutes and will leave you feeling satisfied. Don’t let a busy schedule prevent you from enjoying a delicious, home-cooked meal. With these recipes, you can create flavorful dinners in a flash!
