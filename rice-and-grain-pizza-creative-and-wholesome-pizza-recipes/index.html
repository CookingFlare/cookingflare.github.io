Rice and Grain Pizza: Creative and Wholesome Pizza Recipes


Are you tired of the same old pizza crust? Looking for a healthier and more creative alternative? Look no further than rice and grain pizza! This unique and wholesome twist on traditional pizza will not only satisfy your taste buds but also provide you with the nutritional benefits of whole grains. In this blog post, we will explore different rice and grain pizza recipes that are both delicious and easy to make.

1. Brown Rice Crust Pizza

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups cooked brown rice
  • 1 egg
  • 1/2 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1/4 teaspoon garlic powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 425°F (220°C).
  2. In a large bowl, combine the cooked brown rice, egg, shredded mozzarella cheese, dried oregano, garlic powder, salt, and pepper. Mix well until all the ingredients are evenly incorporated.
  3. Line a baking sheet with parchment paper and spread the rice mixture onto it, shaping it into a round pizza crust.
  4. Bake the crust in the preheated oven for about 15-20 minutes or until it becomes golden and crispy.
  5. Remove the crust from the oven and let it cool slightly before adding your favorite toppings.
  6. Once you have added your desired toppings, return the pizza to the oven and bake for an additional 10-15 minutes or until the cheese is melted and bubbly.
  7. Remove from the oven, let it cool for a few minutes, and enjoy your delicious homemade brown rice crust pizza!

2. Quinoa Crust Pizza

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup cooked quinoa
  • 1/4 cup almond flour
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1/4 teaspoon garlic powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 425°F (220°C).
  2. In a medium-sized bowl, combine the cooked quinoa, almond flour, grated Parmesan cheese, egg, dried basil, dried oregano, garlic powder, salt, and pepper. Mix well until all the ingredients are thoroughly combined.
  3. Line a baking sheet with parchment paper and spread the quinoa mixture onto it, shaping it into a round pizza crust.
  4. Bake the crust in the preheated oven for about 15-20 minutes or until it becomes golden and crispy.
  5. Remove the crust from the oven and let it cool slightly before adding your favorite toppings.
  6. Once you have added your desired toppings, return the pizza to the oven and bake for an additional 10-15 minutes or until the cheese is melted and bubbly.
  7. Remove from the oven, let it cool for a few minutes, and savor the unique flavors of your homemade quinoa crust pizza!

3. Cauliflower Rice Crust Pizza

Ingredients:

  • 1 medium-sized cauliflower head, grated or processed into rice-like consistency
  • 1 egg
  • 1/2 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/4 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1/4 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1/4 teaspoon garlic powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 425°F (220°C).
  2. Place the grated cauliflower in a microwave-safe bowl and microwave on high for 6-8 minutes until it becomes soft.
  3. Allow the cauliflower to cool slightly, then transfer it to a clean kitchen towel or cheesecloth. Squeeze out as much liquid as possible.
  4. In a mixing bowl, combine the cauliflower rice, egg, shredded mozzarella cheese, dried basil, dried oregano, garlic powder, salt, and pepper. Mix well until all the ingredients are thoroughly combined.
  5. Line a baking sheet with parchment paper and spread the cauliflower mixture onto it, shaping it into a round pizza crust.
  6. Bake the crust in the preheated oven for about 15-20 minutes or until it becomes golden and crispy.
  7. Remove the crust from the oven and let it cool slightly before adding your favorite toppings.
  8. Once you have added your desired toppings, return the pizza to the oven and bake for an additional 10-15 minutes or until the cheese is melted and bubbly.
  9. Remove from the oven, let it cool for a few minutes, and relish the deliciousness of your homemade cauliflower rice crust pizza!

Conclusion

Rice and grain pizza offers a delightful twist to traditional pizza crusts. With the recipes provided above, you can enjoy the goodness of whole grains while indulging in a scrumptious pizza experience. Feel free to experiment with different toppings and flavors to create your own unique rice and grain pizza masterpiece. So, why settle for ordinary when you can have extraordinary? Give these recipes a try and discover a whole new world of wholesome and creative pizza options. Bon appétit!
