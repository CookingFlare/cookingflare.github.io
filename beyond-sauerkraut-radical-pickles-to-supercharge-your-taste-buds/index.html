Beyond Sauerkraut: Radical Pickles to Supercharge Your Taste Buds


Are you tired of the same old pickles? Ready to elevate your taste buds to new heights? Dive into the world of radical pickles that will turn your foodie journey upside down. We’re going beyond the traditional sauerkraut and delving into a realm of pickles that will leave your taste buds dazzled and your kitchen shelves lined with jars of perfection.

Radical Pickle #1: Ginger Scallion Pickles

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups thinly sliced green cabbage
  • 1 cup thinly sliced green onions
  • 1 cup thinly sliced carrots
  • 1 cup thinly sliced Red Russian kale
  • 1 cup thinly sliced daikon radish
  • 1 cup sliced leeks
  • 1 cup sliced ginger
  • 2 cups rice vinegar
  • 2 cups water
  • 1 cup coarse salt
  • 3 cloves garlic, sliced
  • 1 tablespoon ground black pepper

Instructions:

  1. In a large bowl, combine cabbage, green onions, carrots, kale, daikon radish, leeks, ginger, and garlic.
  2. In a separate pot, mix rice vinegar, water, and coarse salt until dissolved.
  3. Add the vegetable mixture to the liquid and let it rest for 30 minutes.
  4. Add black pepper to the mixture and let it ferment for 5 days.
  5. Enjoy this explosion of flavor ontop of your favorite sushi rolls or alongside grilled meats and fish.

Radical Pickle #2: Pickled Turmeric Beets

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups of thinly sliced beets
  • 1 cup of thinly sliced lemons
  • 1 cup of thinly sliced red onions
  • 1 cup of thinly sliced shallots
  • 2 cups water
  • 2 cups apple cider vinegar
  • 1 cup of coconut sugar
  • 1 tablespoon ground turmeric
  • 1 tablespoon ground coriander
  • 1 tablespoon ground cumin
  • 2 cloves garlic, sliced
  • 1 teaspoon black peppercorns

Instructions:

  1. In a large glass jar, add beets, lemon slices, red onions, and shallots.
  2. In a separate pot, mix water, apple cider vinegar, coconut sugar, turmeric, coriander, cumin, garlic, and black peppercorns until sugar is fully dissolved.
  3. Pour the brine over the beets in the jar.
  4. Let the jar sit at room temperature for 2-3 days to ferment before moving it to a cooler location.
  5. Once well fermented, enjoy these earthy, tangy pickles in salads, sandwiches, or alongside roasted meats.

Radical Pickle #3: Spicy Kimchi Pickles

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups thinly sliced cabbage
  • 1 cup thinly sliced carrots
  • 1 cup thinly sliced Korean radish
  • 1 cup thinly sliced scallions
  • 1 cup thinly sliced garlic chives
  • 1 cup of apple cider vinegar
  • 1 cup of water
  • 1 cup of fish sauce
  • 1 teaspoon red pepper flakes
  • 2 tablespoons coarse salt
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced

Instructions:

  1. In a large bowl, mix cabbage, carrots, Korean radish, scallions, and garlic chives.
  2. In a separate bowl, mix apple cider vinegar, water, fish sauce, red pepper flakes, and salt.
  3. Add the vegetable mixture to the brine.
  4. Allow the kimchi to ferment for 1-2 days at room temperature before transferring to a cooler location.
  5. Enjoy this pungent, spicy pickle with rice bowls, noodles, or as a spicy condiment on your morning sandwich.

Conclusion:

Dive into the world of radical pickles and supercharge your taste buds with the flavors of these unique fermentations. From ginger scallion pickles to pickled turmeric beets, to spicy kimchi pickles, your palate will thank you for stepping out of the traditional pickle box. These vibrant, bold pickles will not only elevate your meals but also bring a new level of excitement to your cooking experience. Start experimenting with these pickle recipes and discover the endless possibilities of radical pickles in your culinary journey.
