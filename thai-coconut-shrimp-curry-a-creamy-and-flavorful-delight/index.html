Thai Coconut Shrimp Curry: A Creamy and Flavorful Delight


If you’re a fan of Thai cuisine and seafood, then you’re in for a treat with this delectable Thai Coconut Shrimp Curry recipe. Bursting with creamy coconut milk, aromatic herbs, and succulent shrimp, this dish is a delightful fusion of sweet, savory, and spicy flavors that will transport your taste buds to the bustling streets of Thailand.

Ingredients

To recreate this tantalizing dish at home, you’ll need the following ingredients:

  • 1 pound of large shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 1 can of coconut milk
  • 2 tablespoons of red curry paste
  • 1 tablespoon of fish sauce
  • 1 tablespoon of brown sugar
  • 1 red bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 yellow bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 onion, sliced
  • 3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 thumb-sized piece of ginger, grated
  • 1-2 fresh red chilies, sliced (adjust to your spice preference)
  • 1 bunch of fresh cilantro, chopped
  • 2 tablespoons of vegetable oil
  • Salt to taste
  • Cooked jasmine rice for serving

Instructions

Step 1: Prepare the Shrimp

Start by seasoning the shrimp with a pinch of salt. In a large skillet or wok, heat 1 tablespoon of vegetable oil over medium-high heat. Once hot, add the shrimp and cook for 2-3 minutes on each side until they turn pink and opaque. Remove the shrimp from the skillet and set aside.

Step 2: Make the Curry Base

Using the same skillet, add the remaining tablespoon of vegetable oil. Sauté the minced garlic, grated ginger, and sliced red chilies for a minute until fragrant. Then, add the sliced onions and bell peppers, and cook for an additional 3-4 minutes until they begin to soften.

Step 3: Simmer with Aromatics

Next, add the red curry paste to the skillet, stirring it into the vegetables. Pour in the coconut milk, fish sauce, and brown sugar, stirring to combine. Allow the curry to simmer for 5-7 minutes, allowing the flavors to meld together and the sauce to thicken slightly.

Step 4: Add the Shrimp and Finish

Once the curry base has simmered, return the cooked shrimp to the skillet, gently stirring them into the luscious coconut curry. Let the shrimp heat through for a couple of minutes, ensuring they’re coated in the flavorful sauce. Taste the curry and adjust the seasoning if necessary.

Step 5: Serve and Enjoy

Sprinkle the fresh cilantro over the curry, giving it a final burst of herbal fragrance. Serve the Thai Coconut Shrimp Curry over a bed of steamed jasmine rice, allowing the creamy sauce to mingle with the fluffy grains. Garnish with additional cilantro and sliced red chilies for an extra pop of color and flavor.

Conclusion

In conclusion, Thai Coconut Shrimp Curry is a dish that embodies the essence of Thai cuisine – vibrant, aromatic, and full of contrasting yet harmonious flavors. Whether you’re a seasoned home cook or a culinary adventurer looking to expand your repertoire, this recipe offers a delightful journey to the heart of Thai culinary tradition. So, roll up your sleeves, gather your ingredients, and embark on a flavorful voyage to the land of smiles with this creamy and flavorful delight.
