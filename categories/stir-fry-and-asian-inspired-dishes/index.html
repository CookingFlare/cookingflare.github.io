Wholesome and Flavorful Stir Fry Recipes with Quinoa and Veggies

Are you looking for a delicious and nutritious meal that can be whipped up in no time? Look no further than these wholesome and flavorful stir fry recipes with quinoa and veggies. Packed with protein, fiber, and a medley of colorful vegetables, these stir fries are not only satisfying but also incredibly good for you. So, grab your wok and let’s get cooking! Recipe 1: Quinoa Veggie Stir Fry Ingredients: 1 cup quinoa 2 cups water 2 tablespoons olive oil 1 onion, thinly sliced 2 cloves garlic, minced 1 red bell pepper, thinly sliced 1 yellow bell pepper, thinly sliced 1 zucchini, sliced 1 cup broccoli florets 1 cup snap peas 2 tablespoons soy sauce 1 tablespoon sesame oil Salt and pepper to taste Fresh cilantro for garnish Instructions: Rinse the quinoa thoroughly under cold water. In a medium-sized saucepan, bring the water to a boil. Add the quinoa and reduce the heat to low. Cover and simmer for about 15 minutes or until the quinoa is cooked and fluffy. In a large wok or skillet, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the onion and garlic and sauté until fragrant and translucent. Add the bell peppers, zucchini, broccoli, and snap peas to the wok. Stir fry for about 5-7 minutes or until the vegetables are crisp-tender. In a small bowl, whisk together the soy sauce and sesame oil. Pour the sauce over the vegetables and stir fry for another 2 minutes. Add the cooked quinoa to the wok and toss everything together until well combined. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Remove from heat and garnish with fresh cilantro. Serve hot and enjoy! Recipe 2: Teriyaki Quinoa Stir Fry Ingredients: 1 cup quinoa 2 cups water 2 tablespoons vegetable oil 1 onion, thinly sliced 2 cloves garlic, minced 1 cup sliced mushrooms 1 red bell pepper, thinly sliced 1 cup sliced carrots 1 cup snow peas 1 cup broccoli florets 2 tablespoons teriyaki sauce 1 tablespoon rice vinegar Salt and pepper to taste Sesame seeds for garnish Instructions: Cook the quinoa by rinsing it under cold water and then boiling it in water until fluffy. Set aside. Heat the vegetable oil in a large skillet or wok over medium heat. Add the onion and garlic and sauté until fragrant. Add the mushrooms, bell pepper, carrots, snow peas, and broccoli to the skillet. Stir fry for about 5-7 minutes or until the vegetables are tender-crisp. In a small bowl, whisk together the teriyaki sauce and rice vinegar. Pour the sauce over the vegetables and stir fry for another 2 minutes. Add the cooked quinoa to the skillet and toss everything together until well combined. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Remove from heat and garnish with sesame seeds. Serve hot and enjoy! Recipe 3: Thai Quinoa Stir Fry Ingredients: 1 cup quinoa 2 cups water 2 tablespoons coconut oil 1 onion, thinly sliced 2 cloves garlic, minced 1 red bell pepper, thinly sliced 1 cup chopped pineapple 1 cup sliced carrots 1 cup snap peas 1 cup chopped broccoli 2 tablespoons soy sauce 1 tablespoon lime juice 1 tablespoon Thai red curry paste Fresh cilantro for garnish Instructions: Cook the quinoa by rinsing it under cold water and then boiling it in water until fluffy. Set aside. In a large wok or skillet, heat the coconut oil over medium heat. Add the onion and garlic and sauté until fragrant. Add the bell pepper, pineapple, carrots, snap peas, and broccoli to the wok. Stir fry for about 5-7 minutes or until the vegetables are tender. In a small bowl, whisk together the soy sauce, lime juice, and Thai red curry paste. Pour the sauce over the vegetables and stir fry for another 2 minutes. Add the cooked quinoa to the wok and toss everything together until well combined. Remove from heat and garnish with fresh cilantro. Serve hot and enjoy! These wholesome and flavorful stir fry recipes with quinoa and veggies are not only easy to make but also perfect for a quick weeknight dinner. They are versatile, allowing you to mix and match vegetables and sauces according to your preference.
