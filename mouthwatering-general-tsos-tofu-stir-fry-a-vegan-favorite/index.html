Mouthwatering General Tso's Tofu Stir Fry: A Vegan Favorite


If you’re a fan of Chinese cuisine, then you’ve probably heard of the popular dish, General Tso’s Chicken. But what if you’re vegan or looking for a plant-based alternative? Look no further than this mouthwatering recipe for General Tso’s Tofu Stir Fry! Packed with flavor and texture, this vegan twist on the classic dish will leave you craving for more.

Ingredients

  • 1 block of firm tofu, drained and cubed
  • 1/4 cup cornstarch
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 3 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1-inch piece of ginger, grated
  • 1/2 cup vegetable broth
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons rice vinegar
  • 2 tablespoons maple syrup
  • 1 tablespoon tomato paste
  • 1 teaspoon sriracha sauce (optional)
  • 1/4 teaspoon red pepper flakes (optional)
  • 1/2 cup sliced bell peppers
  • 1/2 cup sliced carrots
  • 1/2 cup broccoli florets
  • 1/4 cup chopped green onions
  • Cooked rice or noodles, for serving

Instructions

  1. Start by pressing the tofu to remove excess moisture. Place the tofu on a plate lined with paper towels, top with more paper towels, and place a heavy object on top. Let it sit for about 30 minutes.

  2. Once the tofu is pressed, cut it into bite-sized cubes. In a large Ziploc bag, add the cornstarch and tofu cubes. Seal the bag and shake it gently to coat the tofu evenly.

  3. Heat 1 tablespoon of vegetable oil in a large skillet or wok over medium-high heat. Add the coated tofu cubes and cook until they are golden brown and crispy on all sides. This should take about 10 minutes. Transfer the tofu to a plate lined with paper towels to drain excess oil.

  4. In the same skillet or wok, add another tablespoon of vegetable oil. Stir in the minced garlic and grated ginger, and cook for about 1 minute until fragrant.

  5. In a small bowl, whisk together the vegetable broth, soy sauce, rice vinegar, maple syrup, tomato paste, sriracha sauce, and red pepper flakes. Pour the sauce into the skillet and bring it to a simmer.

  6. Add the sliced bell peppers, carrots, and broccoli florets to the skillet. Cook for about 5 minutes, until the vegetables are tender-crisp.

  7. Return the crispy tofu to the skillet and toss it with the vegetables and sauce until everything is well coated. Cook for an additional 2-3 minutes to heat the tofu through.

  8. Remove the skillet from heat and garnish with chopped green onions.

  9. Serve the General Tso’s Tofu Stir Fry over cooked rice or noodles, and enjoy!

Tips and Variations

  • For an extra kick of heat, add more sriracha sauce or red pepper flakes to the sauce.
  • Feel free to customize the vegetables in this stir fry. You can add mushrooms, snap peas, or even baby corn for added texture and flavor.
  • If you prefer a thicker sauce, you can mix 1 tablespoon of cornstarch with 2 tablespoons of water and add it to the skillet during step 6.

Now that you have the recipe for this mouthwatering General Tso’s Tofu Stir Fry, it’s time to roll up your sleeves and get cooking! This vegan favorite is sure to impress even the most avid meat-eaters with its bold flavors and satisfying textures. So, grab your ingredients and let’s dive into a plate of deliciousness!
