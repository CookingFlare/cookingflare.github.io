Slow Cooker Lemon Garlic Butter Chicken: Bursting with Flavor


Are you ready to tantalize your taste buds with a mouthwatering chicken dish that’s bursting with the flavors of lemon, garlic, and butter? Look no further than this sensational Slow Cooker Lemon Garlic Butter Chicken recipe. This dish is not only incredibly flavorful but also incredibly easy to prepare, making it a perfect choice for busy weeknights or lazy weekends. Let’s dive into the details and learn how to create this delectable dish that will leave everyone at the table craving for more.

Ingredients

  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 4 tablespoons unsalted butter
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/2 cup chicken broth
  • 1/4 cup freshly squeezed lemon juice
  • 1 teaspoon lemon zest
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • 1/2 teaspoon paprika
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley for garnish

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Chicken: Season the chicken breasts with salt and pepper. Place them in the slow cooker.

  2. Create the Lemon Garlic Butter Mixture: In a small saucepan, melt the butter over medium heat. Add the minced garlic and sauté for a minute until fragrant. Pour in the chicken broth, lemon juice, lemon zest, oregano, thyme, and paprika. Stir the mixture well and let it simmer for a couple of minutes.

  3. Pour the Mixture Over the Chicken: Once the lemon garlic butter mixture is ready, pour it over the chicken breasts in the slow cooker.

  4. Cook to Perfection: Cover the slow cooker and cook the chicken on low for 6-7 hours or on high for 3-4 hours, until the chicken is tender and thoroughly cooked.

  5. Serve and Garnish: Once the chicken is done, transfer it to a serving platter. Spoon the flavorful lemon garlic butter sauce over the chicken. Garnish with freshly chopped parsley for a pop of color and added freshness.

Tips for Success

  • For an extra burst of flavor, consider adding a sprinkle of parmesan cheese over the chicken just before serving.
  • If you prefer a thicker sauce, you can create a cornstarch slurry by mixing a tablespoon of cornstarch with a tablespoon of cold water, then stirring it into the sauce in the slow cooker. Let it cook for a few more minutes until the sauce thickens.

Final Thoughts

The Slow Cooker Lemon Garlic Butter Chicken is a true delight for the senses. The tender and juicy chicken infused with the zesty, aromatic flavors of lemon, garlic, and butter is a winning combination that’s sure to impress your family and friends. Whether you’re hosting a dinner party or simply craving a comforting homemade meal, this recipe is a must-try. So, grab your slow cooker and get ready to savor every delicious bite of this flavorful dish!

Remember, the beauty of slow cooker recipes lies in their simplicity and ability to infuse ingredients with rich flavors over time. This Lemon Garlic Butter Chicken is a prime example of how a few simple ingredients can come together to create a culinary masterpiece. Give it a try, and let your taste buds revel in the delightful harmony of flavors. Happy cooking!
