Savory and Tangy BBQ Pulled Pork: Perfect for Sandwiches


Welcome to our latest recipe! Today, we’re going to show you how to make a mouthwatering Savory and Tangy BBQ Pulled Pork that is perfect for sandwiches. This recipe is sure to become a favorite for any BBQ lover.

Ingredients

  • 1.5 kg (3.3 lbs) pork shoulder, boneless
  • 1 cup BBQ sauce
  • 1/2 cup apple cider vinegar
  • 1/4 cup brown sugar
  • 2 tablespoons Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 tablespoon Dijon mustard
  • 1 tablespoon smoked paprika
  • 1 tablespoon garlic powder
  • 1 tablespoon onion powder
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • Sandwich buns, for serving

Instructions

  1. Prepare the pork: Start by trimming any excess fat from the pork shoulder. Cut the pork into large chunks, around 5-6 pieces. This will help it cook more evenly and make it easier to shred later on.

  2. Season the pork: In a small bowl, combine the smoked paprika, garlic powder, onion powder, salt, and black pepper. Rub the spice mixture all over the pork, ensuring that each piece is well-coated.

  3. Slow cook the pork: Place the seasoned pork in a slow cooker. In a separate bowl, mix together the BBQ sauce, apple cider vinegar, brown sugar, Worcestershire sauce, and Dijon mustard. Pour the mixture over the pork, making sure it is evenly distributed. Cover the slow cooker and cook on low for 8-10 hours, or on high for 4-6 hours. The pork should be tender and easily shred with a fork.

  4. Shred the pork: Once the pork is cooked, carefully remove it from the slow cooker and place it on a cutting board. Using two forks, shred the pork into smaller pieces. The meat should be juicy and flavorful, with a perfect balance of savory and tangy flavors.

  5. Reduce the sauce: Pour the remaining sauce from the slow cooker into a saucepan. Bring it to a simmer over medium heat and let it cook for about 10 minutes, or until it thickens slightly. This will intensify the flavors and create a rich, luscious sauce to drizzle over the pulled pork.

  6. Assemble the sandwiches: Toast your sandwich buns lightly, if desired. Spoon a generous amount of the shredded pork onto the bottom half of each bun. Drizzle some of the reduced sauce over the pork. Top with the other half of the bun. Serve the sandwiches immediately.

  7. Optional toppings: Feel free to add your favorite toppings like coleslaw, pickles, or sliced onions to enhance the flavor and texture of the sandwiches.

Tips and Variations

  • If you don’t have a slow cooker, you can also cook the pork in a Dutch oven or a large pot in the oven at 150°C (300°F) for 4-5 hours.
  • For a smokier flavor, you can add a few drops of liquid smoke to the BBQ sauce mixture.
  • Leftover pulled pork can be refrigerated for up to 3 days or frozen for up to 3 months. Simply reheat it in a skillet or microwave before using.

So there you have it - a delicious recipe for Savory and Tangy BBQ Pulled Pork that is perfect for sandwiches. The tender and flavorful pork, combined with the tangy BBQ sauce, makes for a truly satisfying meal. Whether you’re hosting a summer BBQ or simply craving a tasty sandwich, this recipe is sure to impress. Give it a try and let us know what you think. Enjoy!
