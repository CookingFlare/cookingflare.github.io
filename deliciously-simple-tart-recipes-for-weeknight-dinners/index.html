Deliciously Simple Tart Recipes for Weeknight Dinners


When it comes to weeknight dinners, simplicity and flavor are key. Tarts are the perfect solution for busy evenings, offering a delightful combination of flaky crusts filled with a variety of savory or sweet ingredients. Whether you’re looking for a light dinner, a hearty meal, or a comforting dish, these deliciously simple tart recipes will make your weeknight dinners exciting and satisfying.

The Allure of Tarts

Tarts have a unique charm that makes them a favorite for many home cooks. They can be prepared in advance, filled with seasonal ingredients, and baked to perfection in less than an hour. The versatility of tarts allows you to experiment with different fillings, from vegetables and cheeses to meats and even fruits. This blog post will walk you through some easy-to-make tart recipes that are sure to impress your family and keep your dinner routine fresh.

Basic Tart Crust Recipe

Before diving into the delicious fillings, it’s essential to master a basic tart crust. This versatile recipe can be used for both savory and sweet tarts.

Ingredients:

  • 1 ½ cups all-purpose flour
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, chilled and diced
  • 1/4 cup ice water

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the Dough: In a large bowl, mix the flour and salt. Add the diced butter and mix until the mixture resembles coarse crumbs.
  2. Add Water: Gradually add ice water, one tablespoon at a time, mixing until the dough comes together. You may not need all the water.
  3. Chill the Dough: Form the dough into a ball, flatten it into a disk, wrap it in plastic wrap, and refrigerate for at least 30 minutes.
  4. Roll Out: On a floured surface, roll out the chilled dough to fit your tart pan. Transfer it to the pan and trim the edges.

Now that you have your tart crust ready, let’s explore some delicious fillings!

1. Spinach and Feta Tart

Ingredients:

  • 1 prepared tart crust
  • 2 cups fresh spinach, chopped
  • 1 cup feta cheese, crumbled
  • 1/2 cup ricotta cheese
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/4 cup milk
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the Oven: Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Sauté Spinach: In a skillet, heat olive oil over medium heat. Add the chopped spinach and cook until wilted. Remove from heat and let cool.
  3. Mix the Filling: In a bowl, whisk together the eggs, milk, salt, and pepper. Stir in the feta, ricotta, and cooled spinach.
  4. Assemble the Tart: Pour the filling into the prepared tart crust.
  5. Bake: Bake for 30-35 minutes or until the filling is set and the top is golden.
  6. Serve: Allow to cool for a few minutes before slicing and serving.

2. Tomato and Basil Tart

Ingredients:

  • 1 prepared tart crust
  • 3-4 medium tomatoes, sliced
  • 1 cup fresh basil, chopped
  • 1 cup mozzarella cheese, shredded
  • 1 tablespoon balsamic glaze
  • Salt and pepper, to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the Oven: Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C).
  2. Layer Ingredients: Place the sliced tomatoes in the tart crust, overlapping them slightly. Sprinkle with salt and pepper.
  3. Add Cheese and Basil: Top with mozzarella cheese and chopped basil.
  4. Bake: Bake for 25-30 minutes until the tomatoes are tender and the cheese is bubbly.
  5. Drizzle Balsamic: Once out of the oven, drizzle with balsamic glaze before serving.

3. Mushroom and Gruyère Tart

Ingredients:

  • 1 prepared tart crust
  • 2 cups mushrooms, sliced (e.g., cremini, shiitake)
  • 1 cup Gruyère cheese, grated
  • 1 onion, thinly sliced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 3 eggs
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • 1 tablespoon butter

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the Oven: Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Sauté Vegetables: In a skillet, melt butter over medium heat. Add onions and cook until translucent. Add garlic and mushrooms and sauté until mushrooms are browned. Season with salt and pepper.
  3. Mix Eggs and Cream: In a bowl, whisk together eggs and heavy cream. Stir in the sautéed mixture and Gruyère cheese.
  4. Bake: Pour the mixture into the tart crust and bake for 30-35 minutes until set and golden brown.
  5. Serve: Let it cool slightly before slicing.

4. Zucchini and Goat Cheese Tart

Ingredients:

  • 1 prepared tart crust
  • 2 medium zucchinis, thinly sliced
  • 1 cup goat cheese, crumbled
  • 1 egg
  • 1/4 cup cream
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • Salt and pepper, to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the Oven: Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Prepare Zucchini: Toss zucchini slices with olive oil, salt, and pepper. Let them drain on paper towels to remove excess moisture.
  3. Mix Filling: In a bowl, whisk together the egg and cream. Stir in goat cheese.
  4. Layer and Bake: Layer the zucchini slices in the tart crust and pour the egg mixture over the top. Bake for 30-35 minutes until set.
  5. Cool and Serve: Allow to cool slightly before slicing.

5. Caramelized Onion and Blue Cheese Tart

Ingredients:

  • 1 prepared tart crust
  • 2 large onions, thinly sliced
  • 1 cup blue cheese, crumbled
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • Salt and pepper, to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the Oven: Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Caramelize Onions: In a skillet, heat olive oil over medium heat. Add onions and cook, stirring frequently, until caramelized, about 20-25 minutes.
  3. Mix Filling: In a bowl, whisk together eggs, cream, salt, and pepper. Stir in blue cheese and caramelized onions.
  4. Bake: Pour the mixture into the tart crust and bake for 30-35 minutes until set.
  5. Serve: Let it cool slightly before slicing.

6. Sweet Potato and Bacon Tart

Ingredients:

  • 1 prepared tart crust
  • 2 medium sweet potatoes, peeled and sliced
  • 1 cup cooked bacon, crumbled
  • 1 cup cheddar cheese, shredded
  • 3 eggs
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • Salt and pepper, to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the Oven: Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Prepare Sweet Potatoes: Boil the sweet potato slices for about 5 minutes until tender. Drain and let cool.
  3. Mix Filling: In a bowl, whisk together eggs, milk, salt, and pepper. Stir in bacon and cheddar cheese.
  4. Layer and Bake: Place the sweet potato slices in the tart crust, pour the egg mixture over the top, and bake for 30-35 minutes until set.
  5. Cool and Serve: Let it cool slightly before slicing.

Tips for Perfect Tarts

  • Use Fresh Ingredients: For the best flavor, use the freshest vegetables, cheeses, and herbs available.
  • Pre-Bake for Crispiness: For a crisper crust, consider pre-baking your tart crust for 10-15 minutes before adding the filling.
  • Experiment with Flavors: Don’t hesitate to mix and match ingredients. Seasonal vegetables and herbs can elevate your tart.
  • Serve with a Side Salad: A fresh salad can complement the richness of the tart and create a well-rounded meal.

Conclusion

Tarts are not only delicious but also a fantastic way to showcase seasonal ingredients and flavors in your weeknight dinners. With a variety of fillings to choose from, you can easily customize each tart to suit your taste. Whether you’re in the mood for something savory or a little more indulgent, these simple tart recipes will become staples in your dinner rotation. Embrace the ease of preparing tarts and enjoy the delightful results on your dinner table. Happy cooking!
