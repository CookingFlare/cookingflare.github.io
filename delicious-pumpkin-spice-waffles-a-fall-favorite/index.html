Delicious Pumpkin Spice Waffles: A Fall Favorite


As the leaves start to change and the air becomes crisp, it’s the perfect time to indulge in the warm and comforting flavors of fall. One of the best ways to welcome the season is by treating yourself to a batch of delicious pumpkin spice waffles. These waffles are the epitome of autumn, with their rich pumpkin flavor and aromatic blend of spices. Whether you’re enjoying a leisurely weekend brunch or looking for a special breakfast treat, these waffles are sure to become a seasonal favorite.

Ingredients

Before diving into the recipe, let’s gather the ingredients you’ll need to make these scrumptious pumpkin spice waffles:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 cup brown sugar
  • 1 tablespoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 2 teaspoons ground cinnamon
  • 1 teaspoon ground ginger
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground nutmeg
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground cloves
  • 1 3/4 cups milk
  • 1 cup pumpkin puree
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1/4 cup unsalted butter, melted
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Instructions

Now that you have all the ingredients ready, let’s dive into the step-by-step process of making these delightful waffles:

  1. In a large bowl, whisk together the flour, brown sugar, baking powder, baking soda, salt, cinnamon, ginger, nutmeg, and cloves until well combined.

  2. In a separate bowl, whisk together the milk, pumpkin puree, eggs, melted butter, and vanilla extract until smooth.

  3. Pour the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients and gently stir until just combined. Be careful not to overmix, as this can result in tough waffles.

  4. Preheat your waffle iron according to the manufacturer’s instructions and lightly grease it with non-stick cooking spray or melted butter.

  5. Ladle the batter onto the preheated waffle iron, using just enough to cover the waffle grid. Close the lid and cook the waffles until golden and crisp.

  6. Once the waffles are done, transfer them to a wire rack to allow any steam to escape, which will help maintain their crispness.

  7. Serve the pumpkin spice waffles warm, topped with a dollop of whipped cream, a drizzle of maple syrup, and a sprinkle of cinnamon for the perfect finishing touch.

Tips for Success

To ensure that your pumpkin spice waffles turn out perfectly every time, here are a few tips to keep in mind:

  • Use pumpkin puree, not pumpkin pie filling, to achieve the best flavor and texture.
  • For extra indulgence, consider adding a handful of chocolate chips or chopped nuts to the waffle batter.
  • If you don’t have a waffle iron, you can also use this batter to make fluffy pumpkin spice pancakes.

Embrace the Flavors of Fall

These pumpkin spice waffles are a delightful way to embrace the flavors of fall and elevate your breakfast or brunch experience. The warm spices combined with the earthy sweetness of pumpkin create a symphony of flavors that will leave you craving more. So, whether you’re enjoying a cozy morning at home or hosting a gathering of loved ones, treat yourself to the irresistible allure of these delectable waffles. Happy fall, and happy cooking!
