Chocolate Olive Oil Cake: A Decadent Italian Dessert


Indulge in the heavenly combination of rich chocolate and zesty olive oil with this delectable Chocolate Olive Oil Cake. This delightful recipe pays tribute to the traditional flavors of Italian cuisine, offering a unique spin on a classic dessert. Soft, moist, and lusciously chocolatey, this cake is sure to become a beloved addition to your dessert repertoire. Follow this simple recipe to impress your family and friends with a truly decadent, Italian-inspired creation.

Why Olive Oil in a Chocolate Cake?

You may be wondering why olive oil should be used in a chocolate cake. Well, the healthy fats found in olive oil create an incredibly moist and tender texture to the cake. Moreover, the light flavor of the olive oil adds depth and complements the chocolate, preventing it from becoming too rich or overwhelming. By incorporating stunning Italian olive oil, you’re not only treating your taste buds to a luxurious dessert, but also upgrading the quality of your baking.

Ingredients

To make this irresistible Chocolate Olive Oil Cake, gather the following ingredients:

  • 1 and 1/2 cups (190g) all-purpose flour
  • 3/4 cup (65g) unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 3/4 cup (150g) granulated sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 cup (240ml) extra virgin olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon pure vanilla extract
  • 1 cup (240ml) buttermilk
  • 1/2 cup (120ml) hot water
  • 1/3 cup (35g) powdered sugar (for dusting)

Instructions

Step 1: Preheat and Prepare

Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C) and grease a 9-inch round cake pan with a neutral oil.

Step 2: Dry Ingredients

In a medium bowl, combine the flour, cocoa powder, baking soda, and salt. Stir with a whisk until well incorporated and set aside.

Step 3: Wet Ingredients

In a separate large bowl, whisk together the granulated sugar and eggs until smooth and slightly thickened. Gradually pour in the olive oil, while continuously whisking, followed by the vanilla extract.

Step 4: Combine

To the wet ingredients, gently stir in 1/3 of the dry mixture until just incorporated. Next, pour in the buttermilk and mix well. Add the remaining dry ingredients and mix until no lumps remain. Add the hot water and stir until evenly distributed.

Step 5: Bake

Pour the batter into the prepared cake pan. Bake in the preheated oven for 40-45 minutes or until a cake tester inserted into the center comes out clean.

Step 6: Cool

Allow the cake to cool in the pan for about 10 minutes before transferring it to a wire rack to cool completely.

Step 7: Dust and Serve

Once cooled, dust the cake with powdered sugar using a fine-mesh strainer. Slice and serve, enjoying the unparalleled combination of velvety chocolate and rich olive oil goodness.

The Perfect Pairing

The dense, yet tender texture and intense chocolate flavor of this cake make it the ideal dessert to be paired with a generous dollop of whipped cream, a scoop of creamy gelato, or a smooth glass of full-bodied red wine. Now, gather your loved ones and get ready to experience the decadence of this delightful Italian Chocolate Olive Oil Cake!

This Chocolate Olive Oil Cake leaves behind an unforgettable taste and aroma. The zesty flavors of Italy mesh with the velvety richness of chocolate to provide a truly special indulgence. Experience this extraordinary dessert for yourself, and you’ll discover a new go-to favorite for every special occasion!

Buon appetito!
