Easy Sheet Pan BBQ Chicken and Veggies: Family Recipe


If you’re looking for a simple and delicious meal that the whole family will love, look no further than this easy sheet pan BBQ chicken and veggies recipe. It’s a perfect dish for busy weeknights when you want something tasty and satisfying without spending hours in the kitchen. The best part? It’s all made on a single sheet pan, which means minimal cleanup - a win-win for any home cook.

Ingredients

  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 2 cups of your favorite BBQ sauce
  • 1 pound baby potatoes, halved
  • 3 bell peppers, sliced
  • 1 red onion, sliced
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley for garnish

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C) and line a large baking sheet with parchment paper or aluminum foil for easy cleanup.
  2. In a small bowl, mix the BBQ sauce with a splash of olive oil to thin it out slightly. This will help it coat the chicken and veggies evenly.
  3. Place the chicken breasts on one side of the prepared baking sheet, leaving some space between them. Brush each chicken breast generously with the thinned-out BBQ sauce, ensuring they are well coated on all sides.
  4. In a separate bowl, toss the halved baby potatoes with a tablespoon of olive oil, salt, and pepper. Arrange them on the baking sheet next to the chicken, making sure they are in a single layer to ensure even cooking.
  5. Next, toss the sliced bell peppers and red onion with the remaining olive oil, salt, and pepper. Arrange the veggies on the baking sheet around the chicken and potatoes, making sure everything is spread out evenly.
  6. Drizzle any remaining BBQ sauce over the veggies for extra flavor.
  7. Place the sheet pan in the preheated oven and bake for 25-30 minutes, or until the chicken is cooked through and the veggies are tender, with slightly caramelized edges.
  8. Once done, remove the sheet pan from the oven and let the chicken rest for a few minutes before slicing.
  9. Garnish with fresh parsley and serve the delicious sheet pan BBQ chicken and veggies family-style straight from the pan.

Serving Suggestions

This dish pairs perfectly with a simple green salad or some fluffy rice to soak up the extra BBQ sauce. It’s a versatile meal that can be customized to suit your family’s taste preferences. You can also use different veggies or swap the BBQ sauce for a tangy marinade of your choice to keep things interesting.

Why It’s a Family Favorite

Not only is this sheet pan BBQ chicken and veggies recipe incredibly easy to prepare, but it’s also a hit with the whole family. The tender, juicy chicken combined with the sweet and smoky flavors of the BBQ sauce, and the caramelized veggies create a harmonious blend of flavors and textures that will have everyone coming back for seconds.

Conclusion

With just a few simple ingredients and minimal prep work, you can have a wholesome and satisfying meal on the table in no time. This sheet pan BBQ chicken and veggies recipe is a testament to the fact that delicious family dinners don’t have to be complicated. Give it a try, and watch it become a regular feature on your weekly menu rotation. Happy cooking and bon appétit!
