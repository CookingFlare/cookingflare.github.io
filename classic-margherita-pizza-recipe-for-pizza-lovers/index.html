Classic Margherita Pizza Recipe for Pizza Lovers


If you’re a fan of classic Italian cuisine, you can’t go wrong with a delicious Margherita pizza. With its simple yet flavorful combination of fresh ingredients, this pizza has been a favorite for generations. Whether you’re hosting a pizza night with friends or simply craving a tasty homemade meal, learning how to make a classic Margherita pizza is a skill worth mastering.

Ingredients

For the Pizza Dough:

  • 2 1/4 cups (280g) all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon active dry yeast
  • 3/4 cup (180ml) warm water
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil

For the Toppings:

  • 1/2 cup (120ml) tomato sauce
  • 8 ounces (225g) fresh mozzarella, sliced
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • Extra virgin olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

Making the Pizza Dough:

  1. In a large bowl, mix the flour, sugar, and salt together.
  2. In a small bowl, dissolve the yeast in the warm water and let it sit for 5 minutes until foamy.
  3. Add the yeast mixture and olive oil to the flour mixture and stir until a dough forms.
  4. Knead the dough on a floured surface for about 5-7 minutes until smooth and elastic.
  5. Place the dough in a lightly oiled bowl, cover it with a kitchen towel, and let it rise in a warm place for about 1 hour or until doubled in size.

Preparing the Pizza:

  1. Preheat your oven to the highest temperature it can go, usually around 500°F (260°C).
  2. Once the dough has risen, punch it down and divide it into two equal portions.
  3. Roll out each portion of dough into a circle on a floured surface, about 12 inches (30cm) in diameter.
  4. Transfer the dough to a pizza peel or a parchment-lined baking sheet.
  5. Spread half of the tomato sauce over each pizza, leaving a border around the edges.
  6. Arrange the sliced mozzarella over the sauce.
  7. Tear the fresh basil leaves and scatter them over the pizza.
  8. Drizzle a little extra virgin olive oil over the top and season with salt and pepper.

Baking the Pizza:

  1. Slide the pizza onto a preheated pizza stone in the oven or place the baking sheet directly on the oven rack.
  2. Bake for about 10-12 minutes or until the crust is golden and the cheese is bubbly and slightly browned.
  3. Remove the pizza from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes before slicing.

Enjoying the Margherita Pizza

Once the pizza is ready, slice it into wedges and savor the delightful flavors of the classic Margherita pizza. The simple yet vibrant combination of fresh tomato sauce, creamy mozzarella, and aromatic basil creates a truly satisfying culinary experience. Whether you’re a pizza enthusiast or simply appreciate the art of Italian cuisine, this Margherita pizza recipe is sure to become a favorite in your repertoire.

So, the next time you’re in the mood for a homemade pizza night, consider trying your hand at crafting this timeless classic. With fresh, high-quality ingredients and a bit of culinary creativity, you can indulge in the authentic taste of Italy right in your own kitchen. Buon appetito!
