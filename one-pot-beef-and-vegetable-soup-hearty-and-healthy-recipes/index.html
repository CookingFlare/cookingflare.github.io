One-Pot Beef and Vegetable Soup: Hearty and Healthy Recipes


Looking for a comforting and nutritious meal that’s easy to make and perfect for any day of the week? One-Pot Beef and Vegetable Soup is the answer! This hearty soup is packed with tender beef, vibrant vegetables, and flavorful broth, making it a wholesome and delicious choice for lunch or dinner. Plus, with just one pot to clean up, it’s a convenient option for busy individuals or families. Let’s dive into the recipe and see how you can whip up this satisfying dish in no time!

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound of stewing beef, cubed
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 2 carrots, sliced
  • 2 celery stalks, chopped
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 can of diced tomatoes
  • 6 cups of beef broth
  • 1 teaspoon of dried thyme
  • 1 teaspoon of paprika
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 cups of your favorite vegetables (e.g., green beans, potatoes, peas)

Instructions:

  1. Sear the Beef: In a large pot or Dutch oven, heat some oil over medium-high heat. Add the cubed beef and brown it on all sides for about 5 minutes. This step helps to develop rich flavors in the soup.

  2. Add Aromatics: Stir in the diced onion, sliced carrots, chopped celery, and minced garlic. Cook until the vegetables start to soften, approximately 5 minutes. The aroma at this stage is simply irresistible!

  3. Combine Ingredients: Pour in the can of diced tomatoes (juices included) and beef broth. Season with dried thyme, paprika, salt, and pepper. Give everything a good stir to combine all the flavors.

  4. Simmer to Perfection: Bring the soup to a gentle boil, then reduce the heat to low. Let it simmer, partially covered, for about 1.5 to 2 hours or until the beef is tender. The longer it simmers, the more flavorful it becomes.

  5. Add Vegetables: Once the beef is almost tender, add your choice of additional vegetables like green beans, potatoes, or peas. Simmer for another 20-30 minutes until the vegetables are cooked to your liking.

  6. Serve and Enjoy: Ladle the hot soup into bowls and garnish with fresh herbs if desired. This One-Pot Beef and Vegetable Soup pairs perfectly with crusty bread or a side salad for a complete and satisfying meal.

Tips and Variations:

  • For a thicker broth, you can mix a tablespoon of flour with a bit of water and stir it into the soup during the simmering process.
  • Feel free to customize the vegetables based on what you have on hand or your personal preferences. This recipe is versatile and can accommodate a wide range of options.
  • To make it a complete meal, you can add cooked pasta, rice, or barley to the soup towards the end of the cooking time.

There you have it – a simple yet flavorful One-Pot Beef and Vegetable Soup recipe that is sure to become a staple in your kitchen. Whether you’re cooking for yourself, your family, or hosting a gathering, this hearty and healthy soup is a crowd-pleaser that will warm both hearts and bellies. Give it a try and savor the goodness in every spoonful!

Remember, the beauty of one-pot recipes lies in their simplicity and convenience, so don’t be afraid to experiment and make this recipe your own. Happy cooking and enjoy your delicious bowl of homemade goodness!
