Satisfying Loaded Nacho Fries Recipe


If you’re a fan of crispy, flavorful fries and the bold, zesty flavors of nachos, then you’re in for a treat. This loaded nacho fries recipe combines the best of both worlds, creating a satisfying and indulgent dish that’s perfect for sharing with friends or enjoying as a fun snack. Whether you’re hosting a game night or simply craving a fiesta of flavors, these loaded nacho fries are sure to impress.

Ingredients

For the Fries:

  • 4 large russet potatoes, scrubbed and dried
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon cumin
  • Salt and pepper to taste

For the Nacho Toppings:

  • 1 cup cooked ground beef or plant-based meat alternative
  • 1 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1/2 cup diced tomatoes
  • 1/4 cup sliced jalapenos
  • 1/4 cup sliced black olives
  • 1/4 cup diced red onion
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh cilantro
  • 1/2 cup sour cream
  • 1/4 cup salsa
  • Optional: Guacamole for serving

Instructions

Step 1: Prepare the Fries

  1. Preheat the oven to 425°F (220°C).
  2. Cut the potatoes into fries of your desired thickness. Soak them in cold water for 30 minutes, then pat them dry with paper towels.
  3. In a large bowl, toss the fries with olive oil, garlic powder, paprika, cumin, salt, and pepper until evenly coated.
  4. Arrange the fries in a single layer on a baking sheet and bake for 30-35 minutes, flipping once halfway through, until they are golden and crispy.

Step 2: Load Up the Fries

  1. Once the fries are cooked, transfer them to a serving platter or individual plates.
  2. Sprinkle the cooked ground beef or plant-based meat alternative over the fries, followed by the shredded cheddar cheese.
  3. Place the loaded fries back in the oven for 5-7 minutes, or until the cheese is melted and bubbly.

Step 3: Add the Toppings

  1. After removing the fries from the oven, top them with diced tomatoes, sliced jalapenos, black olives, diced red onion, and fresh cilantro.
  2. Drizzle with sour cream and salsa, and serve with guacamole on the side for an extra kick of flavor.

Serving Suggestions

These loaded nacho fries are best enjoyed immediately, while the cheese is still gooey and the fries are hot and crispy. They make a fantastic appetizer for a party or a satisfying main course for a casual dinner. Pair them with your favorite cold beverage, such as a refreshing beer or a zesty margarita, for the ultimate indulgence.

Final Thoughts

Loaded nacho fries are a delightful fusion of two beloved comfort foods, offering a symphony of textures and flavors in every bite. Whether you’re a fan of classic nachos or a devoted fries enthusiast, this recipe is sure to captivate your taste buds and leave you craving more. So, gather your ingredients, preheat the oven, and get ready to savor the irresistible combination of crispy fries, savory toppings, and a burst of zesty goodness. It’s time to elevate your snacking experience with these satisfying loaded nacho fries!
