10 Mouthwatering Wrap Recipes for a Low-Carb Delight


Who says low-carb meals have to be bland and boring? With these 10 mouthwatering wrap recipes, you can enjoy delicious and satisfying meals while sticking to your low-carb diet. From savory fillings to fresh and flavorful ingredients, these wraps are sure to delight your taste buds and leave you feeling satisfied. So, let’s dive right in and explore these tantalizing low-carb delights!

1. Greek Chicken Lettuce Wraps

Ingredients:

  • Grilled chicken breast, sliced
  • Romaine lettuce leaves
  • Diced tomatoes
  • Cucumber slices
  • Feta cheese crumbles
  • Kalamata olives
  • Tzatziki sauce

Instructions:

  1. Lay out a romaine lettuce leaf and top it with a few slices of grilled chicken breast.
  2. Add a sprinkle of diced tomatoes, cucumber slices, feta cheese crumbles, and kalamata olives.
  3. Drizzle tzatziki sauce over the top.
  4. Wrap it all up and enjoy!

2. Asian Beef Lettuce Wraps

Ingredients:

  • Ground beef
  • Iceberg lettuce leaves
  • Shredded carrots
  • Sliced bell peppers
  • Green onions
  • Soy sauce
  • Sesame oil
  • Ginger
  • Garlic

Instructions:

  1. In a skillet, brown the ground beef over medium heat.
  2. Add shredded carrots, sliced bell peppers, green onions, ginger, and garlic.
  3. Stir in soy sauce and sesame oil to taste.
  4. Spoon the beef mixture onto iceberg lettuce leaves.
  5. Roll up the lettuce wraps and savor the Asian flavors!

3. Turkey Avocado Wrap

Ingredients:

  • Sliced turkey breast
  • Whole wheat tortilla
  • Avocado slices
  • Spinach leaves
  • Sliced red onion
  • Dijon mustard

Instructions:

  1. Lay a whole wheat tortilla flat and spread a thin layer of Dijon mustard on it.
  2. Layer sliced turkey breast, avocado slices, spinach leaves, and sliced red onion on top.
  3. Roll up the tortilla tightly, cut it in half, and enjoy this protein-packed wrap.

4. Smoked Salmon Collard Wraps

Ingredients:

  • Collard green leaves
  • Smoked salmon slices
  • Cream cheese
  • Sliced cucumbers
  • Sliced red onion
  • Capers

Instructions:

  1. Blanch the collard green leaves in boiling water for a few seconds, then transfer them to ice water to cool.
  2. Spread cream cheese on the collard green leaves.
  3. Layer smoked salmon slices, cucumber slices, sliced red onion, and capers on top.
  4. Roll up the wraps and indulge in the delicate flavors of smoked salmon.

5. Caprese Chicken Lettuce Wraps

Ingredients:

  • Grilled chicken breast, sliced
  • Butter lettuce leaves
  • Sliced tomatoes
  • Fresh mozzarella cheese slices
  • Basil leaves
  • Balsamic glaze

Instructions:

  1. Place a butter lettuce leaf on a plate and top it with a few slices of grilled chicken breast.
  2. Add sliced tomatoes, fresh mozzarella cheese slices, and basil leaves.
  3. Drizzle balsamic glaze over the top.
  4. Roll up the lettuce wrap and experience the classic Caprese flavors in a low-carb package.

6. Veggie Hummus Wrap

Ingredients:

  • Whole wheat tortilla
  • Hummus
  • Sliced bell peppers
  • Sliced cucumbers
  • Shredded carrots
  • Mixed greens

Instructions:

  1. Spread a generous amount of hummus onto a whole wheat tortilla.
  2. Layer sliced bell peppers, cucumbers, shredded carrots, and mixed greens on top.
  3. Roll up the tortilla tightly and enjoy this refreshing and fiber-rich wrap.

7. Thai Peanut Chicken Lettuce Wraps

Ingredients:

  • Grilled chicken breast, sliced
  • Butter lettuce leaves
  • Shredded cabbage
  • Thinly sliced red bell pepper
  • Chopped peanuts
  • Fresh cilantro
  • Thai peanut sauce

Instructions:

  1. Place a butter lettuce leaf on a plate and top it with a few slices of grilled chicken breast.
  2. Add shredded cabbage, thinly sliced red bell pepper, chopped peanuts, and fresh cilantro.
  3. Drizzle Thai peanut sauce over the top.
  4. Roll up the lettuce wrap and enjoy the harmonious blend of flavors in this Thai-inspired dish.

8. Mediterranean Veggie Wrap

Ingredients:

  • Whole wheat tortilla
  • Hummus
  • Sliced cucumbers
  • Sliced tomatoes
  • Sliced red onion
  • Kalamata olives
  • Feta cheese crumbles

Instructions:

  1. Spread a layer of hummus onto a whole wheat tortilla.
  2. Layer sliced cucumbers, tomatoes, red onion, kalamata olives, and feta cheese crumbles on top.
  3. Roll up the tortilla tightly and savor the vibrant flavors of the Mediterranean.

9. Buffalo Chicken Lettuce Wraps

Ingredients:

  • Shredded cooked chicken breast
  • Iceberg lettuce leaves
  • Diced celery
  • Blue cheese crumbles
  • Buffalo sauce

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, mix the shredded cooked chicken breast, diced celery, blue cheese crumbles, and buffalo sauce.
  2. Spoon the buffalo chicken mixture onto iceberg lettuce leaves.
  3. Wrap it all up and experience the spicy and tangy flavors of buffalo chicken.

10. Tuna Avocado Wrap

Ingredients:

  • Canned tuna, drained
  • Whole wheat tortilla
  • Avocado, mashed
  • Sliced cucumber
  • Sliced red onion
  • Spinach leaves

Instructions:

  1. Lay a whole wheat tortilla flat and spread mashed avocado on it.
  2. Layer canned tuna, sliced cucumber, sliced red onion, and spinach leaves on top.
  3. Roll up the tortilla tightly, cut it in half, and enjoy this protein-packed wrap.

With these 10 mouthwatering wrap recipes, you can enjoy a variety of flavors while keeping your low-carb diet on track. These wraps are not only delicious but also packed with nutrients. So, whether you’re looking for a quick and easy lunch or a satisfying dinner option, give these low-carb delights a try. Happy wrapping!
