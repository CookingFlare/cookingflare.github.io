Family-Friendly One Pot Cheeseburger Pasta


Are you looking for a quick, delicious, and family-friendly dinner idea that won’t leave you with a sink full of dishes? Look no further than this easy one-pot cheeseburger pasta recipe! This hearty and comforting dish combines all the flavors of a classic cheeseburger with the simplicity of a one-pot meal. It’s a surefire way to please even the pickiest of eaters while making your life in the kitchen a whole lot easier.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 small onion, diced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 cups beef broth
  • 1 can (14 oz) diced tomatoes
  • 2 cups uncooked elbow macaroni
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1 teaspoon mustard powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1 1/2 cups shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 2 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley (optional, for garnish)

Instructions

  1. In a large pot or deep skillet, brown the ground beef over medium heat until fully cooked. Drain any excess fat from the pot.

  2. Add the diced onion and minced garlic to the pot with the cooked ground beef. Sauté for a few minutes until the onion is translucent and the garlic is fragrant.

  3. Stir in the beef broth, diced tomatoes (with their juices), uncooked elbow macaroni, paprika, mustard powder, salt, and black pepper.

  4. Bring the mixture to a gentle boil, then reduce the heat to a simmer. Cover the pot and cook for about 12-15 minutes, or until the macaroni is tender, stirring occasionally.

  5. Once the macaroni is cooked, stir in the shredded cheddar cheese and milk until the cheese is fully melted and the sauce is creamy.

  6. Serve the cheeseburger pasta hot, garnished with chopped fresh parsley if desired.

Tips for Success

  • Feel free to customize this recipe with your favorite cheeseburger toppings such as diced pickles, crispy bacon, or a dollop of ketchup or mustard on top of each serving.
  • If you’re short on time, you can use pre-shredded cheese to save a few extra minutes.

Why One-Pot Meals Are a Game Changer

One-pot meals are a lifesaver for busy families, and for good reason. Not only do they minimize the number of dishes you have to wash, but they also often require minimal prep work and cooking time. This means more time for you to spend with your loved ones and less time slaving away in the kitchen. Plus, the flavors in one-pot meals have ample time to meld together, resulting in a depth of flavor that’s truly satisfying.

The Allure of Cheeseburger Pasta

What’s not to love about the combination of juicy ground beef, tangy cheese, and comforting pasta? This cheeseburger pasta recipe takes all the elements of a classic cheeseburger and transforms them into a hearty, satisfying meal that’s perfect for any night of the week. The addition of diced tomatoes adds a touch of acidity and freshness, while the cheddar cheese and milk create a luscious, creamy sauce that coats every bite. It’s a dish that’s sure to bring smiles to the faces of everyone around your dinner table.

In Conclusion

Family-friendly one-pot cheeseburger pasta is a true crowd-pleaser that’s as easy to make as it is to devour. With simple ingredients and minimal cleanup, it’s a recipe that will make its way into your regular dinner rotation. Give it a try and watch as your family comes back for seconds – and maybe even thirds!
