A Simple yet Delicious Italian-Style Focaccia Pizza Recipe


There’s nothing quite like the aroma of freshly baked bread filling the air, and when that bread is transformed into a mouthwatering pizza, it’s a culinary delight that’s hard to resist. Today, I’m going to share with you a recipe for a simple yet delicious Italian-style focaccia pizza that will leave your taste buds tingling with delight.

Ingredients

For the Focaccia Dough:

  • 4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 ½ cups warm water
  • 2 ¼ teaspoons active dry yeast
  • 2 teaspoons salt
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon honey

For the Toppings:

  • 1 cup tomato sauce
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1 cup sliced cherry tomatoes
  • ½ cup sliced black olives
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • Olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Dough:

    • In a small bowl, combine the warm water, yeast, and honey. Let it sit for about 5 minutes until it becomes frothy.
    • In a large mixing bowl, combine the flour and salt. Make a well in the center and pour in the yeast mixture and olive oil.
    • Stir the mixture until a dough forms, then knead it on a floured surface for about 10 minutes until it becomes smooth and elastic.
    • Place the dough in a greased bowl, cover it with a clean towel, and let it rise in a warm place for about 1 hour or until it doubles in size.

  2. Assemble the Pizza:

    • Preheat your oven to 425°F (220°C).
    • Once the dough has risen, punch it down and transfer it to a greased baking sheet. Press and stretch the dough to fit the pan, creating a dimpled surface.
    • Spread the tomato sauce over the dough, leaving a small border around the edges.
    • Sprinkle the shredded mozzarella cheese evenly over the sauce, then arrange the sliced cherry tomatoes and black olives on top.
    • Drizzle a little olive oil over the toppings and season with salt and pepper.

  3. Bake to Perfection:

    • Place the focaccia pizza in the preheated oven and bake for 20-25 minutes or until the crust is golden and the cheese is bubbly and melted.
    • Once out of the oven, garnish with fresh basil leaves and an extra drizzle of olive oil if desired.

  4. Serve and Enjoy:

    • Slice the focaccia pizza into squares or wedges and serve it hot. It’s perfect for sharing with family and friends or enjoying as a delightful snack.

Tips for Success

  • For an extra flavor boost, you can add some minced garlic and dried herbs to the dough before kneading.
  • Experiment with different toppings such as roasted vegetables, prosciutto, or arugula to create your own unique flavor combinations.
  • Leftover focaccia pizza can be stored in an airtight container in the refrigerator and reheated in the oven for a quick and delicious meal.

Final Thoughts

This Italian-style focaccia pizza is a wonderful fusion of two beloved classics – focaccia bread and pizza. Its pillowy soft texture, combined with the savory toppings, creates a culinary experience that’s truly memorable. Whether you’re serving it at a gathering or savoring it on a cozy night in, this recipe is sure to become a favorite in your culinary repertoire. So, roll up your sleeves, preheat the oven, and get ready to indulge in the delightful flavors of this homemade Italian-style focaccia pizza. Buon appetito!
