Instant Pot Beef and Mushroom Stir-Fry: Savory and Satisfying


Are you looking for a quick and delicious meal that will satisfy your cravings for something savory? Look no further than this Instant Pot Beef and Mushroom Stir-Fry recipe. This dish is not only bursting with flavor but also incredibly easy to prepare, making it the perfect option for busy weeknights or lazy weekends. The combination of tender beef, earthy mushrooms, and a savory stir-fry sauce will have your taste buds dancing with delight. Let’s dive into the details of this mouthwatering recipe.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound beef sirloin, thinly sliced
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • 1 onion, thinly sliced
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup sliced mushrooms
  • 1 red bell pepper, sliced
  • 1/2 cup low-sodium soy sauce
  • 1/4 cup chicken broth
  • 2 tablespoons oyster sauce
  • 2 tablespoons brown sugar
  • 1 teaspoon sesame oil
  • 2 teaspoons cornstarch
  • Cooked rice or noodles, for serving
  • Green onions, for garnish

Instructions

  1. Sear the Beef: Start by setting your Instant Pot to sauté mode. Add the vegetable oil and sear the thinly sliced beef until it’s browned on all sides. Once the beef is browned, transfer it to a plate and set it aside.

  2. Sauté the Aromatics: In the same Instant Pot, add the sliced onion and minced garlic. Sauté for a few minutes until the onions are soft and translucent.

  3. Add the Vegetables: Next, add the sliced mushrooms and red bell pepper to the Instant Pot. Cook for a few minutes until the vegetables are slightly softened.

  4. Prepare the Sauce: In a small bowl, whisk together the soy sauce, chicken broth, oyster sauce, brown sugar, sesame oil, and cornstarch until well combined.

  5. Combine and Pressure Cook: Return the seared beef to the Instant Pot, and pour the prepared sauce over the beef and vegetables. Give everything a good stir to ensure the beef and vegetables are coated with the sauce. Close the Instant Pot lid and set the vent to sealing. Pressure cook on high for 10 minutes.

  6. Release Pressure and Thicken: Once the cooking time is complete, allow the pressure to naturally release for 5 minutes, then carefully do a quick pressure release. If the sauce needs further thickening, set the Instant Pot to sauté mode and cook, stirring constantly, until the sauce reaches your desired consistency.

  7. Serve and Enjoy: Serve the savory beef and mushroom stir-fry over cooked rice or noodles. Garnish with chopped green onions for a pop of color and freshness.

Tips and Variations

  • For an extra kick of heat, add a pinch of red pepper flakes or a drizzle of sriracha to the stir-fry sauce.
  • Feel free to customize the vegetable selection based on your preferences. Broccoli, snap peas, or baby corn would all be delicious additions to this stir-fry.

Final Thoughts

In just a few simple steps, you can have a delightful Instant Pot Beef and Mushroom Stir-Fry ready to be enjoyed by you and your loved ones. The tender beef, flavorful mushrooms, and savory stir-fry sauce come together to create a dish that is sure to become a staple in your recipe collection. Whether you’re a seasoned cook or just starting out in the kitchen, this recipe is a fantastic way to elevate your culinary skills and treat yourself to a satisfying meal.

So, next time you’re in the mood for something savory and satisfying, give this Instant Pot Beef and Mushroom Stir-Fry a try. You won’t be disappointed!
