Pesto and Goat Cheese Flatbread: A Burst of Freshness


If you’re in the mood for a light and flavorful meal that bursts with the freshness of summer, then look no further than this delightful Pesto and Goat Cheese Flatbread recipe. This dish combines the vibrant flavors of homemade pesto, creamy goat cheese, and a variety of fresh, colorful toppings, all nestled on a crispy flatbread crust. Whether you’re hosting a casual gathering with friends or simply craving a quick and satisfying meal, this flatbread is sure to impress. Let’s dive into the recipe and discover how to create this burst of freshness in your own kitchen.

Ingredients

For the Pesto:

  • 2 cups fresh basil leaves
  • 1/2 cup pine nuts
  • 3 cloves garlic
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/2 cup extra virgin olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

For the Flatbread:

  • 1 pre-made flatbread crust
  • 4 oz goat cheese, crumbled
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1/2 red onion, thinly sliced
  • 1/4 cup Kalamata olives, pitted and halved
  • 1/4 cup fresh arugula
  • Balsamic glaze (optional)

Instructions

Making the Pesto:

  1. In a food processor, combine the basil, pine nuts, and garlic. Pulse until coarsely chopped.
  2. Add the Parmesan cheese and pulse again.
  3. While the food processor is running, slowly drizzle in the olive oil until the mixture is smooth and well combined.
  4. Season the pesto with salt and pepper to taste. Set aside.

Assembling the Flatbread:

  1. Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C).
  2. Place the flatbread crust on a baking sheet.
  3. Spread a generous layer of the freshly made pesto evenly over the flatbread.
  4. Sprinkle the crumbled goat cheese over the pesto.
  5. Arrange the halved cherry tomatoes, sliced red onion, and Kalamata olives on top of the goat cheese.
  6. Bake the flatbread in the preheated oven for 10-12 minutes, or until the crust is golden and the toppings are heated through.
  7. Once out of the oven, scatter the fresh arugula over the hot flatbread.
  8. For a finishing touch, drizzle balsamic glaze over the flatbread if desired.

Serving and Enjoying:

  1. Carefully slice the flatbread into wedges or squares.
  2. Serve immediately and enjoy the burst of flavors and textures in every bite.

Tips and Variations

  • Consider adding grilled chicken or shrimp for a protein boost.
  • Experiment with different types of cheese, such as feta or mozzarella.
  • For a gluten-free option, use a gluten-free flatbread or cauliflower crust.
  • Play around with additional toppings like roasted red peppers, artichoke hearts, or fresh basil leaves.

This Pesto and Goat Cheese Flatbread is a celebration of summer’s bounty, bringing together the vibrant hues and refreshing flavors of the season. Whether you’re a seasoned home cook or just starting to explore the joys of cooking, this recipe is a wonderful way to showcase your culinary creativity. With its simple yet sophisticated combination of ingredients, this flatbread is a versatile canvas for your personal touch. Embrace the burst of freshness and savor every delightful bite of this delightful dish!
