One-Pot Lemon Garlic Butter Chicken and Potatoes: A Citrusy Twist


If you’re looking for a delicious and easy one-pot meal that will brighten up your dinner table, look no further than this One-Pot Lemon Garlic Butter Chicken and Potatoes recipe. This dish is bursting with the fresh flavors of lemon, garlic, and butter, and it’s a perfect choice for a cozy family dinner or a casual gathering with friends. The best part? It’s all made in just one pot, making cleanup a breeze!

Ingredients:

  • 4 bone-in, skin-on chicken thighs
  • 1 pound baby potatoes, halved
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 lemon, sliced
  • 4 tablespoons unsalted butter
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Preheat and Prep: Start by preheating your oven to 400°F (200°C). Season the chicken thighs with salt, pepper, and paprika.

  2. Brown the Chicken: In a large oven-safe skillet or Dutch oven, heat the olive oil over medium-high heat. Add the chicken thighs, skin side down, and cook until golden brown, about 4-5 minutes per side. Once browned, remove the chicken from the skillet and set aside.

  3. Cook the Potatoes: In the same skillet, add the halved baby potatoes and cook for 5-6 minutes, or until they start to develop a golden crust.

  4. Add Flavor: Reduce the heat to medium, then add the minced garlic, dried thyme, and lemon slices to the skillet. Stir everything together to combine the flavors.

  5. Combine Ingredients: Return the browned chicken thighs to the skillet, nestling them among the potatoes. Place the butter on top of the chicken and potatoes, then transfer the skillet to the preheated oven.

  6. Bake: Bake for 25-30 minutes, or until the chicken is cooked through and the potatoes are tender. The lemon slices will have caramelized, infusing the dish with a delightful citrusy aroma.

  7. Garnish and Serve: Once out of the oven, garnish the dish with freshly chopped parsley for a pop of color and extra freshness. Serve the One-Pot Lemon Garlic Butter Chicken and Potatoes directly from the skillet, allowing everyone to dig in and enjoy!

Why You’ll Love This Recipe:

  • Flavorful: The combination of lemon, garlic, and butter creates a zesty and rich flavor profile that will have everyone coming back for seconds.
  • Easy Cleanup: With just one pot to cook and serve in, cleanup is a breeze, allowing you to spend more time enjoying your meal and less time doing dishes.
  • Versatile: This dish is perfect on its own, but you can also pair it with a fresh salad or steamed vegetables for a well-rounded meal.

In Conclusion:

This One-Pot Lemon Garlic Butter Chicken and Potatoes recipe is a delightful twist on a classic comfort food, infusing it with the bright and tangy flavors of lemon and garlic. Whether you’re cooking for your family or entertaining guests, this dish is sure to impress with its simplicity and delicious taste. So, grab your ingredients, preheat that oven, and get ready to savor every citrusy bite!
