Instant Pot Lemon Garlic Butter Shrimp: Bursting with Freshness


If you’re a seafood lover like me, then you’re in for a treat with this delightful recipe for Instant Pot Lemon Garlic Butter Shrimp. Bursting with freshness, this dish is sure to tantalize your taste buds and leave you craving for more. With the convenience of the Instant Pot, you can whip up this flavorful meal in no time. So let’s dive right in!

Ingredients

  • 1 pound of large shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 4 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1/4 cup of unsalted butter
  • Juice of 1 lemon
  • 1 teaspoon of lemon zest
  • 1/4 cup of fresh parsley, chopped
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Start by turning on your Instant Pot and selecting the Saute function. Adjust the heat to medium.

  2. Melt the butter in the Instant Pot and add the minced garlic. Saute for about a minute until the garlic becomes fragrant and slightly golden.

  3. Add the shrimp to the Instant Pot and season with salt and pepper. Stir well to coat the shrimp with the garlic butter mixture.

  4. Close the lid of the Instant Pot and set the valve to the Sealing position. Select the Manual or Pressure Cook function and set the timer for 2 minutes.

  5. While the shrimp is cooking, prepare a bowl of ice water. This will be used to quickly cool down the shrimp after cooking.

  6. Once the timer goes off, perform a quick release by carefully turning the valve to the Venting position. Be cautious of the hot steam.

  7. Open the lid and use a slotted spoon to transfer the cooked shrimp to the bowl of ice water. Let them sit for a minute to cool down and stop the cooking process.

  8. While the shrimp is cooling, add the lemon juice and lemon zest to the remaining garlic butter mixture in the Instant Pot. Stir well to combine.

  9. Remove the shrimp from the ice water and pat them dry with a paper towel.

  10. Return the shrimp to the Instant Pot and toss them in the lemon garlic butter sauce. Make sure each shrimp is coated evenly.

  11. Sprinkle fresh parsley over the shrimp for a burst of vibrant color and flavor.

  12. Serve the Instant Pot Lemon Garlic Butter Shrimp hot with your favorite side dishes, such as rice or pasta. Enjoy!

Tips and Variations

  • For an extra kick of flavor, add a pinch of red pepper flakes to the garlic butter mixture.
  • Experiment with different herbs and spices to customize the dish to your liking. Basil, thyme, or even a sprinkle of paprika can add a unique twist.
  • If you prefer a creamier sauce, you can stir in a tablespoon of heavy cream or coconut cream at the end.
  • To make this recipe gluten-free, use a gluten-free butter substitute or olive oil instead of butter.

Final Thoughts

The Instant Pot Lemon Garlic Butter Shrimp is a versatile dish that can be served as an appetizer, a main course, or even as part of a seafood pasta dish. The tangy lemon, savory garlic, and rich butter create a harmonious blend of flavors that truly make this recipe burst with freshness. So grab your Instant Pot and get ready to impress your family and friends with this easy and delicious seafood delight!
