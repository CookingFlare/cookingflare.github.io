Tasty and Tangy Lemon Rice with Grilled Shrimp


If you’re looking for a flavorful and refreshing meal that will transport your taste buds to the sunny shores of the Mediterranean, then this recipe for Tasty and Tangy Lemon Rice with Grilled Shrimp is just what you need. With its zesty, citrusy flavors and perfectly grilled shrimp, this dish is sure to become a favorite in your kitchen.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup Basmati rice
  • 2 cups water
  • 1 lemon, zest and juice
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 teaspoon cumin powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon chili powder
  • Salt, to taste
  • 1 pound shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • Fresh cilantro, for garnish

Instructions

  1. Rinse the Basmati rice under cold water until the water runs clear. This will help remove any excess starch and ensure fluffy rice. Set aside.

  2. In a medium-sized saucepan, bring the water to a boil. Add the rice and cook according to package instructions until tender. Once cooked, fluff the rice with a fork and set aside.

  3. In a large skillet, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the chopped onion and minced garlic, and sauté until they turn translucent and fragrant.

  4. Now, it’s time to add the spices to infuse the rice with incredible flavors. Add the turmeric powder, cumin powder, and chili powder to the skillet, and stir well to coat the onions and garlic.

  5. Add the cooked rice to the skillet and mix thoroughly, ensuring that the spice mixture is evenly distributed throughout the rice.

  6. Squeeze the juice of one lemon over the rice, and sprinkle the lemon zest as well. The tangy citrus flavor will add a refreshing twist to the dish.

  7. Season with salt to taste, and give everything a final stir to combine all the ingredients. Reduce the heat to low and let the rice simmer for a few more minutes, allowing the flavors to meld together.

  8. While the rice is simmering, it’s time to prepare the star of this dish—the grilled shrimp. Preheat your grill or grill pan to medium-high heat.

  9. In a separate bowl, toss the shrimp with a drizzle of olive oil, salt, and pepper. Make sure each shrimp is coated with the seasonings.

  10. Grill the shrimp for about 2-3 minutes per side, or until they turn pink and slightly charred. Be careful not to overcook them, as shrimp can become tough and chewy if left on the grill for too long.

  11. Once the shrimp are cooked, remove them from the grill and set aside.

  12. To serve, spoon the tangy lemon rice onto a plate and top it with a generous portion of grilled shrimp. Garnish with fresh cilantro for an extra burst of flavor and color.

Enjoy the Deliciousness!

There you have it—a tantalizing recipe for Tasty and Tangy Lemon Rice with Grilled Shrimp. The vibrant flavors of the lemon-infused rice combined with the succulent grilled shrimp create a truly delightful culinary experience. This dish is perfect for a light lunch or dinner, and it’s sure to impress your family and friends. So go ahead, give it a try, and let your taste buds embark on a Mediterranean adventure!
