10-Minute Greek Chickpea Salad Wraps Recipe


If you’re looking for a quick, healthy, and flavorful meal, these 10-minute Greek chickpea salad wraps are the perfect solution. Packed with protein, fiber, and vibrant Mediterranean flavors, these wraps are a delightful option for a light lunch or a satisfying snack. The best part? They require minimal cooking and can be customized to suit your taste preferences. Let’s dive into the recipe and discover how to make these delicious wraps in just a few simple steps.

Ingredients

  • 1 can (15 oz) chickpeas, drained and rinsed
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1/2 English cucumber, diced
  • 1/4 red onion, finely chopped
  • 1/3 cup Kalamata olives, sliced
  • 1/4 cup crumbled feta cheese
  • 3 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons red wine vinegar
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 4 large whole wheat or spinach tortillas
  • Fresh parsley for garnish

Instructions

  1. In a large mixing bowl, combine the chickpeas, cherry tomatoes, cucumber, red onion, Kalamata olives, and feta cheese.

  2. In a small bowl, whisk together the extra virgin olive oil, red wine vinegar, dried oregano, salt, and pepper to create the dressing.

  3. Pour the dressing over the chickpea mixture and toss gently to coat all the ingredients.

  4. Warm the tortillas in a skillet for a few seconds on each side to make them pliable.

  5. Divide the chickpea salad mixture evenly among the tortillas, placing it in the center of each tortilla.

  6. Sprinkle fresh parsley over the salad mixture for an extra burst of flavor.

  7. Fold the sides of the tortillas over the filling and then roll them up tightly to create the wraps.

  8. Slice the wraps in half diagonally and serve immediately.

Customization Tips

Feel free to customize these wraps to your liking by adding or substituting ingredients. You can include chopped bell peppers, diced avocado, or even a dollop of tzatziki sauce for extra creaminess. For a spicy kick, sprinkle some crushed red pepper flakes into the salad mixture. The beauty of these wraps lies in their versatility, so don’t hesitate to get creative with your additions.

Nutritional Benefits

These Greek chickpea salad wraps offer a myriad of nutritional benefits. Chickpeas are an excellent source of plant-based protein and fiber, promoting a feeling of fullness and aiding in digestion. The addition of fresh vegetables and olives provides essential vitamins and minerals, while the healthy fats from the olive oil and feta cheese contribute to overall heart health. This recipe is a fantastic way to incorporate wholesome ingredients into your diet without sacrificing flavor.

Serving Suggestions

Pair these wraps with a refreshing Tzatziki dip, a side of hummus and pita chips, or a simple Greek salad for a complete Mediterranean-inspired meal. They also make a fantastic addition to a picnic or a lunchbox, as they are easy to transport and assemble on the go.

Conclusion

With its delightful combination of textures and flavors, these 10-minute Greek chickpea salad wraps are a testament to the beauty of simple, wholesome ingredients. Whether you’re a seasoned cook or a beginner in the kitchen, this recipe is a fantastic addition to your repertoire. Embrace the convenience and vibrancy of Mediterranean cuisine with these delightful wraps, and savor every nourishing bite. Enjoy!
