Leftover Lovin': How to Turn Yesterday's Pork into Today's Delectable Dishes


Have you ever found yourself with leftover pork from last night’s dinner? Don’t let it go to waste! With a little creativity and some simple ingredients, you can transform yesterday’s pork into today’s mouthwatering dishes. In this post, we’ll explore a variety of recipes that will help you make the most out of your leftovers. From hearty sandwiches to flavorful stir-fries, get ready to fall in love with your leftover pork all over again.

1. Pulled Pork Sliders

Ingredients:

  • Leftover pulled pork
  • Slider buns
  • BBQ sauce
  • Coleslaw

Instructions:

  1. Heat the leftover pulled pork in a skillet over medium heat until warmed through.
  2. Place the slider buns on a baking sheet and warm them in the oven at 350°F (175°C) for a few minutes.
  3. Spread a generous amount of BBQ sauce on the bottom half of each bun.
  4. Pile the warmed pulled pork on top of the sauce.
  5. Top with a spoonful of coleslaw.
  6. Place the top half of the bun on the coleslaw and press gently.
  7. Serve immediately and enjoy the delectable combination of flavors!

2. Pork Fried Rice

Ingredients:

  • Leftover pork, diced
  • Cooked rice, chilled
  • Frozen peas and carrots
  • Soy sauce
  • Sesame oil
  • Garlic, minced
  • Eggs, beaten
  • Green onions, chopped

Instructions:

  1. Heat sesame oil in a large skillet or wok over medium-high heat.
  2. Add minced garlic and cook until fragrant.
  3. Push the garlic to one side of the skillet and pour the beaten eggs into the other side. Scramble the eggs until cooked through.
  4. Add the diced leftover pork, frozen peas, and carrots to the skillet. Stir-fry for a few minutes until the vegetables are tender.
  5. Add the chilled cooked rice to the skillet and mix well with the other ingredients.
  6. Pour soy sauce over the rice mixture, adjusting the amount to taste.
  7. Continue stir-frying until everything is heated through and well combined.
  8. Remove from heat, sprinkle with chopped green onions, and serve hot.

3. Pork Tacos

Ingredients:

  • Leftover pork, shredded
  • Tortillas
  • Lime wedges
  • Fresh cilantro, chopped
  • Red onion, thinly sliced
  • Avocado, sliced
  • Hot sauce (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Warm the leftover shredded pork in a skillet over medium heat.
  2. Heat the tortillas in a separate skillet or in the oven until soft and pliable.
  3. Place a generous amount of the warmed shredded pork onto each tortilla.
  4. Squeeze fresh lime juice over the pork.
  5. Sprinkle with chopped cilantro, thinly sliced red onion, and sliced avocado.
  6. Add a drizzle of hot sauce if desired.
  7. Fold the tortillas in half and serve immediately.

4. Pork Stir-Fry

Ingredients:

  • Leftover pork, sliced
  • Assorted vegetables (bell peppers, broccoli, carrots, snap peas, etc.)
  • Garlic, minced
  • Soy sauce
  • Sesame oil
  • Cornstarch
  • Chicken or vegetable broth

Instructions:

  1. Heat sesame oil in a large skillet or wok over high heat.
  2. Add minced garlic and stir-fry for a few seconds until fragrant.
  3. Add the sliced leftover pork to the skillet and stir-fry until heated through.
  4. Add the assortment of vegetables to the skillet and stir-fry until they are crisp-tender.
  5. In a separate bowl, whisk together soy sauce, cornstarch, and broth until smooth.
  6. Pour the sauce mixture over the stir-fry and cook until the sauce thickens.
  7. Remove from heat and serve the delicious pork stir-fry over steamed rice or noodles.

With these creative recipes, you can turn your leftover pork into a variety of flavorful dishes. Whether you prefer sliders, fried rice, tacos, or stir-fry, there’s something here for everyone. So, the next time you have leftover pork, don’t let it sit in the fridge. Get creative in the kitchen and enjoy the satisfaction of transforming yesterday’s meal into today’s delectable dishes. Happy cooking!
