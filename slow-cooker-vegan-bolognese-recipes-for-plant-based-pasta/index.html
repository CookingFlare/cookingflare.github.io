Slow Cooker Vegan Bolognese Recipes for Plant-Based Pasta


If you’re on the lookout for a hearty and flavorful vegan dish to elevate your pasta game, look no further than these scrumptious slow cooker vegan bolognese recipes. Packed with wholesome ingredients and bursting with rich, savory flavors, these plant-based pasta recipes are sure to become a staple in your kitchen. Whether you’re a seasoned vegan cook or just beginning to explore the world of plant-based cuisine, these slow cooker bolognese recipes are bound to impress your taste buds and leave you craving more.

The Magic of Slow Cooking

Slow cooking is a game-changer when it comes to infusing depth and complexity into vegan dishes. By allowing the ingredients to simmer and meld together over an extended period, the flavors intensify, resulting in a truly delectable dining experience. The slow cooker not only simplifies the cooking process but also ensures that every mouthful is packed with robust flavors that will have you coming back for seconds.

Hearty Lentil and Mushroom Bolognese

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup dried green or brown lentils
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 2 carrots, diced
  • 2 celery stalks, diced
  • 8 oz cremini mushrooms, finely chopped
  • 1 can (28 oz) crushed tomatoes
  • 1 tablespoon tomato paste
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1/2 teaspoon red pepper flakes
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1/4 cup red wine (optional)
  • 1/4 cup fresh basil, chopped (for garnish)
  • Cooked pasta of your choice

Instructions:

  1. Rinse the lentils and place them in the slow cooker.
  2. Add the chopped onion, garlic, carrots, celery, and mushrooms to the slow cooker.
  3. Pour in the crushed tomatoes and add the tomato paste, dried oregano, dried basil, red pepper flakes, salt, and pepper. Stir to combine all the ingredients.
  4. If using, pour in the red wine for added depth of flavor.
  5. Cover and cook on low for 6-8 hours, or on high for 3-4 hours, until the lentils are tender and the flavors have melded together.
  6. Once cooked, taste and adjust the seasoning if necessary.
  7. Serve the lentil and mushroom bolognese over your favorite cooked pasta and garnish with fresh basil.

Nutty Eggplant and Walnut Bolognese

Ingredients:

  • 2 medium eggplants, diced
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 red bell pepper, diced
  • 1 can (28 oz) diced tomatoes
  • 1/2 cup tomato sauce
  • 1/2 cup chopped walnuts
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • 1 teaspoon dried rosemary
  • 1/2 teaspoon smoked paprika
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Cooked pasta of your choice
  • Fresh parsley, chopped (for garnish)

Instructions:

  1. Place the diced eggplants, chopped onion, garlic, and red bell pepper in the slow cooker.
  2. Add the diced tomatoes, tomato sauce, chopped walnuts, dried thyme, dried rosemary, smoked paprika, salt, and pepper. Stir to combine all the ingredients.
  3. Cover and cook on low for 6-8 hours, or on high for 3-4 hours, until the eggplant is tender and the flavors have melded together.
  4. Once cooked, taste and adjust the seasoning if necessary.
  5. Serve the nutty eggplant and walnut bolognese over your favorite cooked pasta and garnish with fresh parsley.

Elevate Your Plant-Based Pasta Game

With these tantalizing slow cooker vegan bolognese recipes, you can take your plant-based pasta dishes to the next level. Whether you opt for the hearty lentil and mushroom bolognese or the nutty eggplant and walnut variation, you’re in for a treat. The beauty of these recipes lies in their versatility – serve them over spaghetti, fettuccine, or even zucchini noodles for a low-carb twist. Embrace the magic of slow cooking and savor every mouthful of these soul-warming, plant-based bolognese creations.
