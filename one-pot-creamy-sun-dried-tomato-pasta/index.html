One Pot Creamy Sun-Dried Tomato Pasta


Craving a delicious and satisfying meal without spending hours in the kitchen? Look no further than this One Pot Creamy Sun-Dried Tomato Pasta recipe. It’s the perfect combination of rich, creamy, and tangy flavors, all cooked in a single pot for easy cleanup. Whether you’re cooking for a weeknight dinner or hosting a casual get-together, this recipe is sure to become a new favorite in your culinary repertoire.

Ingredients

  • 12 oz. (340g) pasta of your choice
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/2 cup sun-dried tomatoes, chopped
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1/2 teaspoon red pepper flakes
  • 2 1/2 cups vegetable or chicken broth
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh basil for garnish

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Ingredients: Start by gathering all the ingredients and chopping the sun-dried tomatoes if they’re not already diced.

  2. Sauté the Aromatics: In a large pot or deep skillet, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the minced garlic and sauté for about 1 minute until fragrant.

  3. Add the Sun-Dried Tomatoes and Spices: Stir in the chopped sun-dried tomatoes, dried basil, and red pepper flakes, allowing the flavors to meld together for another minute.

  4. Cook the Pasta: Add the pasta to the pot, followed by the broth. Bring the mixture to a gentle boil, then reduce the heat to a simmer. Cook the pasta uncovered, stirring occasionally, until it’s al dente and most of the liquid has been absorbed, about 10-12 minutes.

  5. Finish with Cream and Cheese: Pour in the heavy cream, stirring gently to combine. Sprinkle in the grated Parmesan cheese, continuing to stir until the cheese melts and the sauce thickens. Season with salt and pepper to taste.

  6. Serve and Enjoy: Once the pasta is perfectly creamy and coated in the luscious sauce, it’s ready to be served. Dish it out into individual bowls, garnish with fresh basil, and savor every mouthwatering bite.

Tips and Variations

  • To make this dish vegetarian, simply use vegetable broth instead of chicken broth.
  • For a protein boost, add grilled chicken or shrimp to the pasta during the final few minutes of cooking.
  • If you prefer a lighter version, you can substitute half-and-half for the heavy cream, though the sauce won’t be quite as rich.

Why One-Pot Recipes?

One-pot recipes are a game-changer for busy home cooks. They streamline the cooking process, minimizing the number of dishes to wash and maximizing flavor as the ingredients meld together. The beauty of this Creamy Sun-Dried Tomato Pasta is that the pasta cooks right in the sauce, absorbing all the delicious flavors as it simmers to perfection. It’s a testament to the fact that a satisfying meal doesn’t have to be complicated or time-consuming.

Conclusion

In conclusion, this One Pot Creamy Sun-Dried Tomato Pasta is a stellar example of how a few simple ingredients can come together to create a truly memorable dish. The creamy sauce, punctuated by the sweet-tartness of sun-dried tomatoes, is a symphony of flavors that will leave your taste buds singing. Add this recipe to your culinary arsenal for those days when you crave a comforting meal without the fuss. It’s a delightful union of convenience and culinary excellence that’s sure to win over everyone at the table. So, grab your largest pot and get ready to create a dish that’s guaranteed to become a household favorite.
