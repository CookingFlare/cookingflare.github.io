Savory Instant Pot Chicken Fajitas Recipe for a Tex-Mex Delight


If you’re craving a flavorful and satisfying Tex-Mex dish, look no further than this Savory Instant Pot Chicken Fajitas recipe. Packed with tender chicken, vibrant bell peppers, and aromatic spices, these fajitas are sure to impress your taste buds. And the best part? The Instant Pot makes the cooking process a breeze, allowing you to enjoy a delicious meal in no time. So, let’s dive right into the recipe!

Ingredients

  • 1.5 pounds boneless, skinless chicken breasts, sliced into thin strips
  • 2 bell peppers (any color), sliced
  • 1 onion, sliced
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons fajita seasoning
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • 1 teaspoon chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1/4 cup chicken broth
  • Juice of 1 lime
  • Tortillas, for serving
  • Optional toppings: sour cream, guacamole, shredded cheese, salsa, cilantro

Instructions

  1. Start by prepping your ingredients. Slice the chicken breasts into thin strips, and slice the bell peppers and onion.

  2. Set your Instant Pot to sauté mode and heat the olive oil. Add the chicken strips and cook until browned, about 3-4 minutes per side. Remove the chicken from the pot and set aside.

  3. In the same pot, add the sliced bell peppers, onion, and minced garlic. Sauté for 3-4 minutes, until the vegetables start to soften.

  4. Add the fajita seasoning, cumin, chili powder, paprika, salt, and black pepper to the pot. Stir well to coat the vegetables with the spices.

  5. Return the chicken to the pot, along with any juices that may have accumulated. Add the chicken broth and lime juice, and give everything a good stir.

  6. Close the Instant Pot lid and set the valve to sealing. Cook on high pressure for 8 minutes.

  7. Once the cooking time is up, carefully release the pressure using the quick release method.

  8. Open the Instant Pot and give the chicken fajitas a final stir. The chicken should be tender and the vegetables perfectly cooked.

  9. Serve the savory Instant Pot chicken fajitas in warm tortillas, and garnish with your favorite toppings such as sour cream, guacamole, shredded cheese, salsa, and fresh cilantro.

Tips and Variations

  • To add an extra kick of flavor, marinate the chicken in the fajita seasoning and lime juice for 30 minutes before cooking.
  • Feel free to customize your fajitas by adding or substituting different vegetables such as mushrooms, zucchini, or corn.
  • If you prefer a spicier fajita, increase the amount of chili powder or add a pinch of cayenne pepper.
  • Leftovers can be stored in an airtight container in the refrigerator for up to 3 days. Simply reheat in the microwave or on the stovetop for a quick and satisfying meal.

So there you have it – a delicious and easy-to-make Savory Instant Pot Chicken Fajitas recipe that will transport your taste buds to Tex-Mex heaven. With the help of your trusty Instant Pot, you can enjoy a flavorful and satisfying meal in no time. So gather your ingredients, fire up your Instant Pot, and get ready to savor the flavors of Mexico right in your own home.
