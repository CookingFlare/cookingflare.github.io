Classic Mint Julep Cupcakes: A Boozy Southern Treat


If you’re a fan of the classic mint julep cocktail and have a sweet tooth, then these Classic Mint Julep Cupcakes are the perfect treat for you. These delightful little cakes combine the refreshing flavors of mint and bourbon with the sweetness of a moist cupcake, creating a truly indulgent dessert that’s perfect for any occasion.

Ingredients

To make these delicious cupcakes, you will need the following ingredients:

For the Cupcakes:

  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 1/2 tsp baking powder
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1/2 cup whole milk
  • 2 tbsp bourbon
  • 1/2 tsp mint extract
  • Green food coloring (optional)

For the Mint Julep Buttercream:

  • 1 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 4 cups powdered sugar
  • 2 tbsp bourbon
  • 1/2 tsp mint extract
  • 1-2 tbsp heavy cream

Instructions

For the Cupcakes:

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C) and line a cupcake tin with paper liners.
  2. In a medium bowl, whisk together the flour, baking powder, and salt.
  3. In a separate large bowl, cream together the butter and sugar until light and fluffy.
  4. Beat in the eggs, one at a time, then stir in the vanilla extract.
  5. In a small bowl, combine the milk, bourbon, mint extract, and a few drops of green food coloring if desired.
  6. Gradually add the dry ingredients to the wet ingredients, alternating with the milk mixture, and mix until just combined.
  7. Divide the batter evenly among the prepared cupcake liners, filling each about 2/3 full.
  8. Bake for 18-20 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center of a cupcake comes out clean.
  9. Allow the cupcakes to cool in the tin for 5 minutes, then transfer them to a wire rack to cool completely before frosting.

For the Mint Julep Buttercream:

  1. In a large bowl, beat the softened butter until smooth and creamy.
  2. Gradually add the powdered sugar, one cup at a time, and beat until well combined.
  3. Stir in the bourbon and mint extract.
  4. Add the heavy cream, 1 tablespoon at a time, until the buttercream reaches your desired consistency.
  5. Once the cupcakes are completely cool, frost them with the mint julep buttercream using a piping bag or offset spatula.

Serving Suggestion

These Classic Mint Julep Cupcakes are best enjoyed with a glass of iced tea or, for an extra indulgent treat, a mint julep cocktail. The combination of flavors is sure to delight your taste buds and transport you to the southern charm of the Kentucky Derby.

Conclusion

Whether you’re hosting a Kentucky Derby party or simply craving a taste of the South, these Classic Mint Julep Cupcakes are a delightful way to indulge in the flavors of a classic cocktail in dessert form. The subtle hint of bourbon and refreshing mint flavor make these cupcakes a unique and memorable treat for any occasion.

So, roll up your sleeves, gather your ingredients, and get ready to bake up a batch of these boozy Southern delights. Your friends and family will thank you, and your taste buds will be in for a treat!
