Mediterranean-Inspired Chicken Kabobs: Grilled Perfection


The flavors of the Mediterranean come alive in these delicious and juicy chicken kabobs. Grilled to perfection, these kabobs are bursting with the vibrant and aromatic spices that define Mediterranean cuisine. Whether you’re hosting a backyard barbecue or simply looking to spice up your weeknight dinner, these Mediterranean-inspired chicken kabobs are sure to impress.

Ingredients

  • 1.5 pounds of boneless, skinless chicken breasts, cut into 1-inch cubes
  • 1 red bell pepper, cut into 1-inch pieces
  • 1 yellow bell pepper, cut into 1-inch pieces
  • 1 red onion, cut into 1-inch pieces
  • 1 zucchini, sliced into rounds
  • 1/4 cup extra-virgin olive oil
  • 3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons fresh lemon juice
  • 1 tablespoon dried oregano
  • 1 tablespoon dried thyme
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon ground coriander
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • Wooden skewers, soaked in water for 30 minutes

Instructions

  1. Preheat your grill to medium-high heat.

  2. In a large bowl, combine the olive oil, minced garlic, lemon juice, dried oregano, dried thyme, ground cumin, ground coriander, salt, and pepper. Mix well to create a marinade.

  3. Place the chicken cubes into the marinade, ensuring they are well-coated. Allow the chicken to marinate for at least 30 minutes, or overnight in the refrigerator for maximum flavor.

  4. Thread the marinated chicken onto the soaked wooden skewers, alternating with the bell peppers, red onion, and zucchini. This not only adds color but also infuses the kabobs with extra flavor.

  5. Place the kabobs on the preheated grill and cook for about 12-15 minutes, turning occasionally, until the chicken is cooked through and has a slightly charred appearance.

  6. Remove the kabobs from the grill and let them rest for a few minutes before serving. This allows the juices to redistribute, ensuring moist and tender chicken.

  7. Serve the Mediterranean-inspired chicken kabobs with a side of Tzatziki sauce, pita bread, and a fresh Greek salad for a complete and satisfying meal. The cool and tangy Tzatziki perfectly complements the smoky and flavorful kabobs.

Tips and Variations

  • If you prefer a spicier kick, add a pinch of red pepper flakes or a dash of cayenne pepper to the marinade.
  • Feel free to swap out the vegetables with your favorites. Cherry tomatoes, eggplant, or mushrooms would all work well.
  • To make the Tzatziki sauce, simply combine Greek yogurt, grated cucumber, minced garlic, lemon juice, and fresh dill. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  • For a vegetarian option, you can replace the chicken with tofu or halloumi cheese. Marinate and grill them in the same way for a delicious Mediterranean-inspired vegetarian kabob.

These Mediterranean-inspired chicken kabobs are a delightful way to bring the flavors of the Mediterranean to your table. The combination of aromatic herbs and spices, along with the vibrant colors of the vegetables, create a feast for both the eyes and the taste buds. So fire up your grill and get ready to experience grilled perfection with these delicious chicken kabobs!
