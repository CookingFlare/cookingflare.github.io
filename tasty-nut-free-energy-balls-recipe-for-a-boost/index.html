Tasty Nut-Free Energy Balls Recipe for a Boost


Are you looking for a healthy and delicious snack that will give you the energy boost you need to power through your day? Look no further! These nut-free energy balls are the perfect solution. Packed with nutritious ingredients and bursting with flavor, these energy balls are a convenient and satisfying way to fuel your body and satisfy your sweet tooth.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup rolled oats
  • 1/2 cup sunflower seeds
  • 1/2 cup pumpkin seeds
  • 1/2 cup shredded coconut
  • 1/2 cup dried cranberries
  • 1/3 cup honey or maple syrup
  • 1/4 cup sunflower seed butter
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • Pinch of salt

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Dry Ingredients: In a food processor, pulse the rolled oats, sunflower seeds, and pumpkin seeds until they are finely chopped but not powdered. Transfer the mixture to a large mixing bowl.

  2. Combine the Wet Ingredients: Add the shredded coconut, dried cranberries, honey or maple syrup, sunflower seed butter, vanilla extract, cinnamon, and a pinch of salt to the bowl with the dry ingredients.

  3. Mix and Form the Balls: Use a spoon or your hands to mix all the ingredients until well combined. The mixture should be sticky and hold together when pressed. If it’s too dry, add a little more honey or sunflower seed butter. If it’s too wet, add more oats.

  4. Shape the Energy Balls: Scoop out 1-2 tablespoons of the mixture and roll it between your palms to form a ball. Place the energy balls on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper.

  5. Chill and Enjoy: Once all the mixture has been rolled into balls, transfer the baking sheet to the refrigerator and chill the energy balls for at least 30 minutes before serving. Store any leftovers in an airtight container in the refrigerator for up to a week.

Benefits of Nut-Free Energy Balls

These nut-free energy balls are not only delicious but also offer a range of health benefits. The oats provide a good source of fiber and complex carbohydrates, which can help keep you feeling full and satisfied. Sunflower seeds and pumpkin seeds are rich in healthy fats, protein, and essential vitamins and minerals, making them an excellent addition to any diet. The dried cranberries add a sweet and tangy flavor while contributing antioxidants and additional fiber.

When to Enjoy Nut-Free Energy Balls

These tasty energy balls are perfect for a quick breakfast on the go, a mid-morning or afternoon snack, or a pre or post-workout pick-me-up. They are portable, mess-free, and easy to customize to suit your taste preferences. Whether you’re a busy parent, a student, an athlete, or simply someone who loves good food, these nut-free energy balls are a must-try.

Final Thoughts

In conclusion, these nut-free energy balls are a versatile and nutritious snack option that can help you power through your day. By making a batch of these energy balls ahead of time, you can ensure that you always have a healthy snack on hand when hunger strikes. Feel free to experiment with different add-ins such as chocolate chips, chia seeds, or dried apricots to create your own unique energy ball flavors. Give this recipe a try and enjoy the delicious benefits of these tasty nut-free energy balls!
