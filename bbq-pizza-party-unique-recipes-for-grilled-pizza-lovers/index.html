BBQ Pizza Party: Unique Recipes for Grilled Pizza Lovers


Are you a pizza lover who also enjoys the smoky flavors of a barbecue? Then you’re in for a treat! In this blog post, we’ll be sharing some unique and delicious recipes that combine the best of both worlds: grilled pizza. Get ready to fire up your grill and host the ultimate BBQ pizza party!

The Perfect Pizza Dough

Before we dive into the recipes, let’s start with the foundation of any great pizza: the dough. Making your own pizza dough is easier than you might think, and the results are well worth the effort. Here’s a simple recipe to get you started:

Ingredients:

  • 3 ½ cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 teaspoons instant yeast
  • 2 teaspoons salt
  • 1 ½ cups warm water
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil

Instructions:

  1. In a large bowl, combine the flour, yeast, and salt.
  2. Slowly add the warm water and olive oil to the dry ingredients, mixing until a dough forms.
  3. Turn the dough out onto a lightly floured surface and knead for about 5 minutes, or until the dough is smooth and elastic.
  4. Place the dough in a greased bowl, cover with a clean kitchen towel, and let it rise in a warm place for about 1 hour, or until doubled in size.
  5. Punch down the dough and divide it into individual portions, depending on the size of the pizza you want to make.
  6. Roll out each portion of dough into a thin circle, about 1/4 inch thick.

Now that you have your pizza dough ready, it’s time to move on to the exciting part – the toppings!

BBQ Chicken Pizza

Ingredients:

  • Prepared pizza dough
  • 1 cup barbecue sauce
  • 2 cups cooked and shredded chicken
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1 red onion, thinly sliced
  • Fresh cilantro, chopped

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your grill to medium-high heat.
  2. Place the rolled-out pizza dough directly on the grill grates and cook for about 2-3 minutes, or until the bottom is lightly charred.
  3. Flip the dough and spread barbecue sauce evenly over the cooked side.
  4. Sprinkle the shredded chicken, shredded mozzarella cheese, and sliced red onion on top of the sauce.
  5. Close the lid of the grill and cook for another 5-7 minutes, or until the cheese is melted and bubbly.
  6. Remove the pizza from the grill and garnish with chopped cilantro.
  7. Slice and serve hot.

Grilled Veggie Pizza

Ingredients:

  • Prepared pizza dough
  • 1/2 cup pizza sauce
  • 1 zucchini, thinly sliced
  • 1 yellow squash, thinly sliced
  • 1 red bell pepper, thinly sliced
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Fresh basil leaves, torn

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your grill to medium heat.
  2. Place the rolled-out pizza dough directly on the grill grates and cook for about 2-3 minutes, or until the bottom is lightly charred.
  3. Flip the dough and spread pizza sauce evenly over the cooked side.
  4. Arrange the sliced zucchini, yellow squash, and red bell pepper on top of the sauce.
  5. Sprinkle the shredded mozzarella cheese over the vegetables.
  6. Close the lid of the grill and cook for another 5-7 minutes, or until the cheese is melted and the vegetables are tender.
  7. Remove the pizza from the grill and sprinkle torn basil leaves on top.
  8. Slice and serve hot.

Grilled Margherita Pizza

Ingredients:

  • Prepared pizza dough
  • 1/2 cup pizza sauce
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1 cup fresh mozzarella cheese, sliced
  • Fresh basil leaves, torn
  • Balsamic glaze (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your grill to medium-high heat.
  2. Place the rolled-out pizza dough directly on the grill grates and cook for about 2-3 minutes, or until the bottom is lightly charred.
  3. Flip the dough and spread pizza sauce evenly over the cooked side.
  4. Arrange the halved cherry tomatoes and sliced fresh mozzarella cheese on top of the sauce.
  5. Close the lid of the grill and cook for another 5-7 minutes, or until the cheese is melted and the tomatoes are slightly roasted.
  6. Remove the pizza from the grill and sprinkle torn basil leaves on top.
  7. Drizzle with balsamic glaze, if desired.
  8. Slice and serve hot.

Conclusion

Hosting a BBQ pizza party is a fantastic way to gather friends and family for a fun and delicious meal. With these unique grilled pizza recipes, you’ll surely impress your guests and create memorable dining experiences. So fire up that grill, get creative with your toppings, and enjoy the mouthwatering flavors of grilled pizza!

We hope you enjoyed these recipes. Share your BBQ pizza party experiences in the comments below. Happy grilling!
