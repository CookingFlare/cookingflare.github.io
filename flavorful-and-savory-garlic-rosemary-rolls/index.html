Flavorful and Savory Garlic Rosemary Rolls


There’s nothing quite as comforting as the aroma of freshly baked bread wafting through the kitchen. Today, we’re bringing you a recipe for Flavorful and Savory Garlic Rosemary Rolls that will not only fill your home with a delightful fragrance but also tantalize your taste buds with every bite.

Ingredients

  • 4 cups of all-purpose flour
  • 1 tablespoon of instant yeast
  • 1 1/2 cups of warm water
  • 1/4 cup of olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons of honey
  • 1 teaspoon of salt
  • 4 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons of fresh rosemary, finely chopped
  • 1/4 cup of grated Parmesan cheese

Instructions

Step 1: Activate the Yeast

In a small bowl, combine the warm water and honey. Sprinkle the instant yeast over the mixture and let it sit for about 5-10 minutes until it becomes frothy.

Step 2: Prepare the Dough

In a large mixing bowl, combine the flour and salt. Make a well in the center and pour in the activated yeast mixture and olive oil. Use a wooden spoon or your hands to mix until a dough forms.

Step 3: Knead the Dough

Transfer the dough to a floured surface and knead it for about 8-10 minutes until it becomes smooth and elastic. Place the dough in a greased bowl, cover it with a clean kitchen towel, and let it rise in a warm place for about 1 hour or until it doubles in size.

Step 4: Prepare the Filling

While the dough is rising, prepare the flavorful garlic and rosemary filling. In a small bowl, mix together the minced garlic, chopped rosemary, and grated Parmesan cheese.

Step 5: Shape the Rolls

Once the dough has risen, punch it down and transfer it to a floured surface. Roll it out into a large rectangle, about 1/2 inch thick. Spread the garlic and rosemary filling evenly over the dough.

Step 6: Roll and Cut

Starting from one end, tightly roll the dough into a log. Using a sharp knife, cut the log into 12 equal pieces.

Step 7: Second Rise

Place the rolls on a greased baking dish, leaving some space between each roll. Cover them with a kitchen towel and let them rise for another 30 minutes.

Step 8: Bake

Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C). Once the rolls have risen, bake them for 20-25 minutes or until they turn golden brown.

Step 9: Enjoy!

Once baked, let the rolls cool for a few minutes before serving. These Flavorful and Savory Garlic Rosemary Rolls are perfect for accompanying a hearty soup, as a side to a pasta dish, or simply enjoyed on their own.

These rolls are an excellent addition to any meal and are sure to impress your family and friends with their aromatic flavors and soft, fluffy texture. So, roll up your sleeves, preheat your oven, and get ready to indulge in the delightful experience of baking and savoring these irresistible garlic rosemary rolls.
