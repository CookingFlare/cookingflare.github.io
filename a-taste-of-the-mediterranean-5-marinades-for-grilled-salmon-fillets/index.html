A Taste of the Mediterranean: 5 Marinades for Grilled Salmon Fillets


Salmon is a versatile and delicious fish that can be prepared in numerous ways. One of the best ways to bring out its natural flavors is by marinating it. In this blog post, we will explore five mouthwatering Mediterranean-inspired marinades that will take your grilled salmon fillets to a whole new level. These marinades are easy to make, packed with flavor, and will leave your taste buds craving for more. So, fire up your grill and get ready for a culinary journey through the Mediterranean!

1. Lemon and Herb Marinade

Ingredients:

  • 2 tablespoons fresh lemon juice
  • 1 tablespoon extra virgin olive oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a small bowl, whisk together the lemon juice, olive oil, minced garlic, dried oregano, dried thyme, salt, and pepper.
  2. Place the salmon fillets in a shallow dish and pour the marinade over them, making sure to coat each fillet evenly.
  3. Cover the dish with plastic wrap and refrigerate for at least 30 minutes to allow the flavors to meld.
  4. Preheat your grill to medium-high heat and lightly oil the grates.
  5. Remove the salmon fillets from the marinade and grill for about 4-5 minutes per side, or until the fish flakes easily with a fork.

2. Mediterranean Pesto Marinade

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup fresh basil leaves
  • 1/4 cup pine nuts
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 1/2 cup extra virgin olive oil
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a food processor, combine the basil leaves, pine nuts, garlic, olive oil, Parmesan cheese, salt, and pepper. Blend until smooth.
  2. Place the salmon fillets in a shallow dish and spread the pesto marinade over them, ensuring each fillet is coated evenly.
  3. Cover the dish with plastic wrap and refrigerate for at least 1 hour to allow the flavors to infuse.
  4. Heat your grill to medium heat and lightly oil the grates.
  5. Remove the salmon fillets from the marinade and grill for 5-6 minutes per side, or until the fish is opaque and flakes easily.

3. Greek Yogurt and Dill Marinade

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup Greek yogurt
  • 2 tablespoons fresh dill, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, combine the Greek yogurt, chopped dill, minced garlic, lemon juice, salt, and pepper. Mix well.
  2. Place the salmon fillets in a shallow dish and spread the yogurt marinade over them, ensuring every fillet is coated.
  3. Cover the dish with plastic wrap and refrigerate for at least 1 hour to let the flavors meld.
  4. Preheat your grill to medium-high heat and lightly oil the grates.
  5. Remove the salmon fillets from the marinade and grill for about 4-5 minutes per side, or until the fish is cooked through.

4. Sun-Dried Tomato and Olive Marinade

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 cup sun-dried tomatoes, packed in oil
  • 1/4 cup black olives, pitted and chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon balsamic vinegar
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a blender or food processor, combine the sun-dried tomatoes, black olives, minced garlic, olive oil, balsamic vinegar, salt, and pepper. Blend until smooth.
  2. Place the salmon fillets in a shallow dish and spread the sun-dried tomato marinade over them, ensuring each fillet is coated evenly.
  3. Cover the dish with plastic wrap and refrigerate for at least 1 hour to allow the flavors to develop.
  4. Heat your grill to medium heat and lightly oil the grates.
  5. Remove the salmon fillets from the marinade and grill for 5-6 minutes per side, or until the fish is cooked to your desired doneness.

5. Citrus and Honey Marinade

Ingredients:

  • 1/4 cup fresh orange juice
  • 1/4 cup fresh lemon juice
  • 2 tablespoons honey
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, whisk together the orange juice, lemon juice, honey, soy sauce, minced garlic, salt, and pepper.
  2. Place the salmon fillets in a shallow dish and pour the citrus marinade over them, making sure each fillet is coated.
  3. Cover the dish with plastic wrap and refrigerate for at least 30 minutes to let the flavors meld.
  4. Preheat your grill to medium-high heat and lightly oil the grates.
  5. Remove the salmon fillets from the marinade and grill for about 4-5 minutes per side, or until the fish is cooked through.

Conclusion

These five Mediterranean-inspired marinades will elevate your grilled salmon fillets to new heights of flavor. Whether you prefer the refreshing tang of lemon and herb, the aromatic punch of pesto, the creamy goodness of Greek yogurt and dill, the richness of sun-dried tomatoes and olives, or the zesty sweetness of citrus and honey, there is a marinade here to suit every palate. So, fire up the grill, gather your ingredients, and embark on a culinary journey through the Mediterranean. Your taste buds will thank you!
