Apple Cinnamon Breakfast Bars: A Grab-and-Go Option


Mornings can be hectic, and finding a nutritious breakfast that fits seamlessly into your busy schedule can often feel like a daunting task. Enter the Apple Cinnamon Breakfast Bars—a delightful, wholesome, and easy-to-make option that you can grab on your way out the door. These bars are not only packed with flavor but also loaded with nutrients, making them an ideal start to your day. In this post, we’ll dive into the ingredients, preparation steps, and variations of this delicious recipe, along with some tips for storing and serving your bars.

Why Choose Apple Cinnamon Breakfast Bars?

The combination of apples and cinnamon is a classic that evokes feelings of comfort and warmth. But beyond their delightful taste, these breakfast bars offer a plethora of health benefits.

Nutritional Benefits

  1. Energy Boost: The oats and apples provide complex carbohydrates that release energy slowly, helping you feel full and energized throughout the morning.
  2. Fiber-Rich: Apples and oats are excellent sources of dietary fiber, which aids in digestion and contributes to a feeling of fullness.
  3. Antioxidants: Apples are rich in antioxidants, which help combat oxidative stress in the body.
  4. Easy to Customize: You can tailor the recipe to your taste preferences or dietary needs, making it a versatile option for everyone.

Ingredients You’ll Need

To prepare your Apple Cinnamon Breakfast Bars, gather the following ingredients:

For the Base:

  • 2 cups rolled oats
  • 1 cup whole wheat flour (or gluten-free flour for a gluten-free option)
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/3 cup honey or maple syrup
  • 1/2 cup unsweetened applesauce
  • 1/4 cup coconut oil, melted (or any neutral oil)
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

For the Filling:

  • 2 medium-sized apples, peeled, cored, and diced
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 1 tablespoon brown sugar (optional, depending on the sweetness of your apples)
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 cup raisins or chopped nuts (optional)

Step-by-Step Instructions

Step 1: Preheat Your Oven

Begin by preheating your oven to 350°F (175°C). This ensures that your breakfast bars bake evenly and thoroughly.

Step 2: Prepare Your Baking Dish

Line a 9x13 inch baking dish with parchment paper, allowing some overhang to make it easier to lift the bars out once baked. This step is crucial for easy removal and cleanup.

Step 3: Mix the Dry Ingredients

In a large mixing bowl, combine the rolled oats, whole wheat flour, baking powder, baking soda, ground cinnamon, and salt. Mix well until all dry ingredients are evenly distributed.

Step 4: Blend the Wet Ingredients

In another bowl, whisk together the honey (or maple syrup), applesauce, melted coconut oil, eggs, and vanilla extract. Make sure the mixture is smooth and well combined.

Step 5: Combine Wet and Dry Ingredients

Pour the wet mixture into the bowl of dry ingredients. Stir until just combined; be careful not to overmix. The batter should be thick but spreadable.

Step 6: Prepare the Apple Filling

In a separate bowl, toss the diced apples with lemon juice, brown sugar (if using), and ground cinnamon. This mixture adds a sweet and tangy flavor that contrasts beautifully with the oats.

Step 7: Assemble Your Bars

Spread half of the oat mixture evenly in the prepared baking dish. Then, layer the apple filling on top, spreading it out evenly. Finally, spoon the remaining oat mixture over the apples, spreading it as evenly as possible.

Step 8: Bake

Place the baking dish in the preheated oven and bake for about 25-30 minutes, or until the top is golden brown and a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.

Step 9: Cool and Slice

Once baked, remove the dish from the oven and let it cool in the pan for about 10 minutes. Then, using the parchment overhang, lift the bars out of the pan and allow them to cool completely on a wire rack. Once cooled, slice into bars of your preferred size.

Step 10: Store and Enjoy

These Apple Cinnamon Breakfast Bars can be stored in an airtight container at room temperature for up to a week. For longer preservation, you can refrigerate them for up to two weeks or freeze for up to three months.

Serving Suggestions

These breakfast bars are perfect on their own, but you can elevate them with a few serving suggestions:

  • Nut Butter Drizzle: Drizzle almond or peanut butter on top for extra creaminess and protein.
  • Yogurt Parfait: Crumble the bars over a bowl of Greek yogurt, topped with fresh fruits for a hearty breakfast.
  • Smoothie Sidekick: Pair a bar with a smoothie for a balanced meal that’s quick and easy.

Variations to Try

  1. Add Some Crunch: Mix in sunflower seeds, chia seeds, or shredded coconut for extra texture.
  2. Change the Fruit: Substitute apples with other fruits like pears or peaches for a different flavor profile.
  3. Spice it Up: Experiment with spices such as nutmeg or ginger to enhance the flavor.
  4. Dairy-Free Option: Use a plant-based milk in place of eggs by substituting each egg with 1/4 cup of unsweetened applesauce or mashed banana.

Conclusion

Apple Cinnamon Breakfast Bars are a fantastic solution for those busy mornings when you need something nutritious and delicious on the go. With their wholesome ingredients and simple preparation, these bars not only satisfy your taste buds but also keep you energized throughout the day. Whether you enjoy them as a quick breakfast, a midday snack, or a post-workout treat, they’re sure to become a staple in your meal prep routine.

So, gather your ingredients, channel your inner chef, and get ready to enjoy the delightful taste of homemade Apple Cinnamon Breakfast Bars!
