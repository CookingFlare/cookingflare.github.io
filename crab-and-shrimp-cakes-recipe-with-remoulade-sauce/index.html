Crab and Shrimp Cakes Recipe with Remoulade Sauce


Crab and shrimp cakes are a delicious seafood dish that is perfect for any occasion. These crispy cakes are packed with the flavors of fresh crab and succulent shrimp, and they are complemented perfectly by a tangy and creamy remoulade sauce. In this recipe, we will guide you through the process of making these delectable cakes from scratch.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound lump crab meat
  • 1/2 pound peeled and deveined shrimp, finely chopped
  • 1/2 cup breadcrumbs
  • 1/4 cup mayonnaise
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley
  • 2 green onions, finely chopped
  • 1 tablespoon Dijon mustard
  • 1 teaspoon Worcestershire sauce
  • 1/2 teaspoon Old Bay seasoning
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Vegetable oil for frying

Instructions

  1. In a large bowl, combine the crab meat, chopped shrimp, breadcrumbs, mayonnaise, parsley, green onions, Dijon mustard, Worcestershire sauce, Old Bay seasoning, salt, and pepper. Mix well until all the ingredients are evenly incorporated.

  2. Shape the mixture into small patties, about 2 inches in diameter. Place the patties on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper and refrigerate for at least 30 minutes. Chilling the patties will help them hold their shape during frying.

  3. Heat vegetable oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Carefully place the crab and shrimp cakes in the hot oil and cook for about 3-4 minutes per side, or until golden brown and crispy. Be gentle when flipping the cakes to prevent them from falling apart.

  4. Once the crab and shrimp cakes are cooked, transfer them to a paper towel-lined plate to drain any excess oil.

Remoulade Sauce

While the crab and shrimp cakes are delicious on their own, they truly shine when served with a flavorful remoulade sauce. Here’s how to make it:

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup mayonnaise
  • 2 tablespoons Dijon mustard
  • 1 tablespoon capers, finely chopped
  • 1 tablespoon chopped fresh parsley
  • 1 tablespoon chopped fresh dill
  • 1 tablespoon chopped green onions
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 1 teaspoon Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 teaspoon hot sauce (optional)
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. In a small bowl, combine the mayonnaise, Dijon mustard, capers, parsley, dill, green onions, lemon juice, Worcestershire sauce, hot sauce (if desired), salt, and pepper. Mix well until all the ingredients are fully incorporated.

  2. Taste the remoulade sauce and adjust the seasoning according to your preferences. You can add more lemon juice, hot sauce, or salt and pepper to suit your taste.

  3. Once the remoulade sauce is ready, transfer it to a serving bowl and refrigerate until you’re ready to serve the crab and shrimp cakes.

Serving

To serve, arrange the crab and shrimp cakes on a platter and place a small dollop of remoulade sauce on top of each cake. You can also serve the remoulade sauce on the side as a dipping sauce. Garnish with additional chopped parsley or green onions for a pop of color.

These crab and shrimp cakes with remoulade sauce make for a delightful appetizer or main course. The combination of tender crab meat and shrimp, paired with the zesty and creamy remoulade sauce, is a match made in seafood heaven. So go ahead and give this recipe a try – your taste buds will thank you!

Enjoy!
