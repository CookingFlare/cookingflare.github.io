Savory and Smoky: BBQ Pulled Pork Sliders


Are you ready to sink your teeth into a mouthwatering, flavor-packed delight? Look no further than these Savory and Smoky BBQ Pulled Pork Sliders! Bursting with tender, juicy pork that has been slow-cooked to perfection and smothered in a tangy, homemade barbecue sauce, these sliders are the ultimate crowd-pleaser.

Ingredients

  • 2 pounds of pork shoulder, boneless and trimmed of excess fat
  • 1 tablespoon of paprika
  • 1 tablespoon of brown sugar
  • 1 teaspoon of garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon of onion powder
  • 1 teaspoon of salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon of black pepper
  • 1 cup of barbecue sauce, divided
  • 1/4 cup of apple cider vinegar
  • 1/4 cup of chicken broth
  • 12 slider buns

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 325°F (163°C).

  2. In a small bowl, combine the paprika, brown sugar, garlic powder, onion powder, salt, and black pepper. Rub the spice mixture evenly over the pork shoulder, ensuring that all sides are coated.

  3. Heat a large, oven-safe Dutch oven or skillet over medium-high heat. Add the pork shoulder and sear it on all sides until browned, about 3-4 minutes per side.

  4. In a separate bowl, whisk together 3/4 cup of barbecue sauce, apple cider vinegar, and chicken broth. Pour the mixture over the seared pork shoulder.

  5. Cover the Dutch oven or skillet with a lid or aluminum foil and transfer it to the preheated oven. Allow the pork to cook for 2 1/2 to 3 hours, or until it is fork-tender and easily shreds apart.

  6. Once the pork is done, remove it from the oven and transfer it to a cutting board. Using two forks, shred the pork into bite-sized pieces.

  7. In a separate saucepan, heat the remaining 1/4 cup of barbecue sauce over low heat. Add the shredded pork to the saucepan and stir until the pork is evenly coated in the sauce.

  8. To assemble the sliders, slice the slider buns in half horizontally. Place a generous portion of the BBQ pulled pork on the bottom half of each bun. Top with the other half of the bun.

Serve these delicious BBQ pulled pork sliders at your next gathering or game day party and watch as your guests devour them within seconds.
