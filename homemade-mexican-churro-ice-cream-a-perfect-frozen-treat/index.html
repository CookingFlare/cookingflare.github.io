Homemade Mexican Churro Ice Cream: A Perfect Frozen Treat


Are you ready to embark on a culinary adventure that combines the warmth of Mexican churros with the coolness of ice cream? Look no further, because today I have a delightful recipe for Homemade Mexican Churro Ice Cream that will transport your taste buds to a whole new level of deliciousness.

Ingredients

Here’s what you’ll need for this mouthwatering frozen treat:

  • 2 cups of heavy cream
  • 1 cup of whole milk
  • 1/2 cup of granulated sugar
  • 1 teaspoon of pure vanilla extract
  • 1/2 teaspoon of ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 teaspoon of salt
  • 4 large egg yolks
  • 1/2 cup of churro pieces (store-bought or homemade)
  • Caramel sauce (optional, for serving)

Instructions

  1. In a medium saucepan, combine the heavy cream, whole milk, sugar, vanilla extract, ground cinnamon, and salt. Heat the mixture over medium heat, stirring occasionally, until it reaches a gentle simmer. Remove from heat.

  2. In a separate bowl, whisk the egg yolks until smooth. Slowly pour the heated cream mixture into the egg yolks while whisking continuously. This will temper the eggs, ensuring a smooth and creamy ice cream base.

  3. Pour the mixture back into the saucepan and cook over medium-low heat, stirring constantly, until the mixture thickens and coats the back of a spoon. This should take about 8-10 minutes. Be careful not to let it boil.

  4. Once the mixture has thickened, remove it from heat and strain it through a fine-mesh sieve to remove any lumps. Allow the mixture to cool to room temperature, then cover and refrigerate for at least 4 hours or overnight.

  5. Once the ice cream base is thoroughly chilled, pour it into an ice cream maker and churn according to the manufacturer’s instructions. This usually takes about 20-25 minutes, or until the ice cream reaches a soft-serve consistency.

  6. While the ice cream is churning, prepare the churro pieces. You can either buy store-bought churros and cut them into bite-sized pieces or make your own churros using your favorite recipe. If making your own, be sure to allow them to cool completely before adding them to the ice cream.

  7. Once the ice cream has reached the desired consistency, transfer it to a lidded container, layering it with the churro pieces as you go. This will ensure that every spoonful of ice cream is packed with churro goodness.

  8. Cover the container and freeze the ice cream for at least 4 hours or until firm. This will allow the flavors to meld together and the ice cream to set.

  9. When you’re ready to enjoy your Homemade Mexican Churro Ice Cream, scoop it into bowls or cones and drizzle with caramel sauce, if desired. The combination of the creamy ice cream, crunchy churro pieces, and sweet caramel sauce is simply irresistible.

Conclusion

There you have it – a sensational recipe for Homemade Mexican Churro Ice Cream that will make your taste buds sing. This frozen treat brings together the classic flavors of cinnamon and sugar found in traditional churros, combining them with the smooth and creamy goodness of ice cream. Whether you’re hosting a summer party or simply craving a unique dessert, this recipe is sure to impress. So go ahead, indulge in a scoop (or two) of this perfect frozen treat and let your taste buds take a trip to Mexico. Enjoy!
