One-Pot Creamy Tomato Basil Pasta: Quick Dinner Recipe


If you’re in need of a quick and satisfying dinner that doesn’t require a sink full of dishes, then this one-pot creamy tomato basil pasta is the answer to your prayers. This recipe is a perfect combination of rich, creamy, and tangy flavors, all cooked together in just one pot, making it a hassle-free option for busy weeknights or lazy weekends. Let’s dive into the details of this delectable dish and get ready to tantalize your taste buds.

Ingredients

  • 12 oz (340g) of pasta (spaghetti or penne work well)
  • 1 can (14 oz/400g) of diced tomatoes
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 2 cups (480ml) of vegetable or chicken broth
  • 1 cup (240ml) of heavy cream
  • 1/2 cup (50g) of grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/4 cup (15g) of fresh basil, chopped
  • 2 tablespoons of olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Red pepper flakes (optional, for a bit of heat)

Instructions

  1. In a large pot or deep skillet, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the chopped onion and minced garlic, and sauté until the onion is translucent and the garlic is fragrant.

  2. Pour in the diced tomatoes with their juices and stir to combine with the onion and garlic. Let the mixture simmer for a few minutes to allow the flavors to meld together.

  3. Add the uncooked pasta to the pot, followed by the broth. Bring the mixture to a gentle boil, then reduce the heat to a simmer. Allow the pasta to cook in the liquid, stirring occasionally, until it is al dente and most of the liquid has been absorbed (about 10-12 minutes depending on the type of pasta).

  4. Once the pasta is cooked, pour in the heavy cream and grated Parmesan cheese. Stir well to combine, allowing the cream to thicken and the cheese to melt into a luscious sauce.

  5. Toss in the chopped fresh basil and season the dish with salt and pepper to taste. If you like a little kick of spice, sprinkle in some red pepper flakes at this stage.

  6. Remove the pot from the heat and let the pasta rest for a few minutes to allow the sauce to thicken further.

  7. Serve the creamy tomato basil pasta in deep bowls, garnished with an extra sprinkle of Parmesan cheese and fresh basil leaves, if desired.

Tips and Variations

  • For a protein boost, add cooked chicken, shrimp, or Italian sausage to the pasta during the last few minutes of cooking.
  • If you prefer a vegetarian version, you can toss in some sautéed mushrooms or spinach for added flavor and nutrients.
  • Feel free to play around with the herbs and spices to suit your taste. Fresh oregano or thyme can be delightful additions to this dish.
  • Leftovers can be stored in an airtight container in the refrigerator for up to 3 days. Simply reheat the pasta in a pot over low heat, adding a splash of broth or cream to revive the sauce.

Conclusion

This one-pot creamy tomato basil pasta is a game-changer when it comes to easy, flavorful meals. With just a handful of simple ingredients and minimal cleanup, you can whip up a restaurant-worthy dish right in your own kitchen. Whether you’re cooking for a family dinner or a cozy date night, this recipe is sure to become a go-to favorite in your repertoire. Give it a try and savor the creamy, tomato-infused goodness in every bite!

So there you have it, a delightful and effortless recipe for a one-pot creamy tomato basil pasta that will have everyone asking for seconds. Enjoy the convenience and luxury of a delicious homemade meal without the stress of a pile of dirty dishes. Happy cooking!
