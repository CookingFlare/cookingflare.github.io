Make Your Own Italian Sausage and Peppers for a Flavorful Meal


Italian cuisine is known for its rich flavors and hearty dishes, and one classic combination that never fails to please is Italian sausage and peppers. The combination of savory sausage, sweet and tangy peppers, and aromatic herbs creates a truly mouthwatering meal. Instead of relying on store-bought sausages, why not try making your own? Not only will you have control over the ingredients, but you’ll also get the satisfaction of creating something delicious from scratch.

Ingredients

  • 2 pounds of ground pork
  • 2 teaspoons of salt
  • 1 teaspoon of black pepper
  • 1 teaspoon of fennel seeds
  • 1 teaspoon of paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon of garlic powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon of dried oregano
  • 1/4 teaspoon of red pepper flakes (optional)
  • 2 tablespoons of olive oil
  • 2 medium onions, sliced
  • 3 bell peppers (red, yellow, and green), sliced
  • 4 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 cup of tomato sauce
  • 1/2 cup of chicken broth
  • Fresh basil leaves, for garnish

Instructions

  1. In a large bowl, combine the ground pork, salt, black pepper, fennel seeds, paprika, garlic powder, dried oregano, and red pepper flakes (if using). Mix well until all the spices are evenly distributed throughout the meat.

  2. Shape the mixture into small patties or links, depending on your preference. You can use your hands or a sausage stuffer if you have one. Place the sausages on a plate and refrigerate for at least 30 minutes to allow the flavors to meld together.

  3. Heat the olive oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat. Add the sausages and cook until browned on all sides, about 5 minutes. Remove the sausages from the skillet and set aside.

  4. In the same skillet, add the sliced onions, bell peppers, and minced garlic. Cook until the vegetables are tender and slightly caramelized, about 8 minutes.

  5. Return the sausages to the skillet and add the tomato sauce and chicken broth. Stir to combine, then reduce the heat to low and simmer for an additional 10 minutes, allowing the flavors to meld together.

  6. Serve the Italian sausage and peppers hot, garnished with fresh basil leaves. This dish pairs well with crusty bread or pasta, and a side of salad or roasted vegetables.

Tips and Variations

  • If you prefer a spicier sausage, add more red pepper flakes to the meat mixture.
  • You can customize the flavors by adding other herbs and spices, such as dried thyme or rosemary.
  • For a healthier version, use lean ground pork or substitute it with ground turkey or chicken.
  • If you don’t have fresh basil, you can use dried basil or substitute it with parsley or oregano.

Conclusion

Making your own Italian sausage and peppers is a rewarding culinary experience that will impress your friends and family. The homemade sausages are bursting with flavor and pair perfectly with the sweet and tangy peppers. This dish is a crowd-pleaser and is sure to become a favorite in your household. So, roll up your sleeves, gather the ingredients, and get ready to savor the taste of Italy in your own kitchen. Buon appetito!
