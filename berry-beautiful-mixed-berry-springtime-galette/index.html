Berry Beautiful: Mixed Berry Springtime Galette


Spring is the season of rebirth and vibrant colors. It’s the perfect time to indulge in the luscious flavors of fresh berries. What better way to celebrate this season than with a delicious Mixed Berry Springtime Galette? This rustic dessert is not only visually stunning but also incredibly easy to make. So, grab your apron and let’s get started!

Ingredients

For the crust:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup unsalted butter, cold and cubed
  • 1/2 cup ice water

For the filling:

  • 3 cups mixed berries (strawberries, blueberries, raspberries, blackberries)
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 2 tablespoons cornstarch
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).

  2. In a large mixing bowl, whisk together the flour, sugar, and salt. Add the cold, cubed butter and use a pastry cutter or your fingertips to cut the butter into the flour mixture until it resembles coarse crumbs.

  3. Gradually add the ice water to the flour and butter mixture, stirring with a fork until the dough starts to come together. Be careful not to overmix. Once the dough holds together when squeezed, it’s ready.

  4. Transfer the dough onto a lightly floured surface. Divide it into two equal portions and shape each portion into a disk. Wrap the disks in plastic wrap and refrigerate for at least 30 minutes.

  5. In a separate bowl, combine the mixed berries, sugar, cornstarch, lemon juice, and vanilla extract. Gently toss until the berries are evenly coated. Set aside.

  6. Remove one disk of dough from the refrigerator and roll it out into a circle, about 12 inches in diameter. Transfer the rolled-out dough onto a parchment-lined baking sheet.

  7. Spoon the berry filling onto the center of the dough, leaving about 2 inches of dough around the edges. Gently fold the edges of the dough over the filling, pleating as you go. This rustic folding technique gives the galette its charming appearance.

  8. Repeat steps 6 and 7 with the second disk of dough and the remaining berry filling.

  9. Place the baking sheet with the galettes in the preheated oven and bake for 35-40 minutes, or until the crust is golden brown and the filling is bubbly.

  10. Remove the galettes from the oven and let them cool for a few minutes before serving. This dessert can be enjoyed warm or at room temperature.

Serving Suggestions

The Mixed Berry Springtime Galette is delicious on its own, but you can take it to the next level by serving it with a scoop of vanilla ice cream or a dollop of whipped cream. The tartness of the berries combined with the sweetness of the cream creates a delightful contrast of flavors.

Conclusion

This Berry Beautiful: Mixed Berry Springtime Galette is the epitome of springtime indulgence. With its buttery crust and bursting berry filling, it’s the perfect dessert to showcase the abundance of fresh berries available during this season. Whether you’re hosting a spring brunch or simply want to treat yourself to a homemade delight, this galette is sure to impress. So, go ahead and embrace the flavors of spring with this visually stunning and scrumptious dessert. Enjoy!
