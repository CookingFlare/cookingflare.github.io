Refreshing and Tangy: The Classic Tom Collins Recipe


If you’re looking for a classic cocktail that’s both refreshing and tangy, the Tom Collins is a perfect choice. This iconic drink has been a favorite for generations, thanks to its simple yet delightful combination of flavors. In this recipe, we’ll explore the history of the Tom Collins and guide you through creating this timeless cocktail at home.

The History of the Tom Collins

The Tom Collins has a fascinating history that dates back to the 19th century. It is believed to have originated in the United States and gained popularity in the early 20th century. The cocktail is named after the Old Tom gin used in its traditional preparation. Over the years, the recipe has evolved, but its classic appeal remains unchanged.

Ingredients

To make a classic Tom Collins, you’ll need the following ingredients:

  • 2 oz Old Tom gin
  • 1 oz freshly squeezed lemon juice
  • 1/2 oz simple syrup
  • Club soda
  • Ice
  • Lemon slice and maraschino cherry for garnish

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Glass: Start by chilling a Collins glass in the freezer or filling it with ice water to keep it cold.

  2. Shake the Ingredients: In a shaker, combine the Old Tom gin, freshly squeezed lemon juice, and simple syrup. Fill the shaker with ice and shake vigorously for about 15 seconds to chill the mixture.

  3. Strain Into the Glass: Discard the ice from the Collins glass and strain the shaken mixture into the glass.

  4. Top with Club Soda: Fill the glass with club soda, leaving a little space at the top for stirring.

  5. Stir Gently: Use a bar spoon to gently stir the drink, ensuring that the ingredients are well combined.

  6. Garnish and Serve: Garnish the cocktail with a lemon slice and a maraschino cherry for a pop of color and flavor. Serve immediately and enjoy!

Tips for a Perfect Tom Collins

  • Use fresh lemon juice for the best flavor. Avoid bottled lemon juice, as it can alter the taste of the cocktail.
  • Adjust the sweetness to your preference by adding more or less simple syrup.
  • Experiment with different types of gin to find your favorite flavor profile for the Tom Collins.

The Perfect Summer Drink

The Tom Collins is a versatile cocktail that’s perfect for summer gatherings, brunches, or simply relaxing on a warm evening. Its zesty and effervescent nature makes it a crowd-pleaser, and its timeless charm ensures that it remains a beloved classic.

In Conclusion

The Tom Collins is a cocktail with a rich history and a timeless appeal. By mastering this simple yet flavorful recipe, you can impress your guests with a refreshing and tangy drink that stands the test of time. Whether you’re a seasoned mixologist or a beginner, the Tom Collins is a must-try for anyone who appreciates a well-crafted cocktail.

So, gather your ingredients, shake up a glass of this classic concoction, and savor the refreshing and tangy flavors of the iconic Tom Collins. Cheers to good times and great drinks!
