Slow Cooker Sweet and Sour Chicken: Tangy and Flavorful


Are you looking for a delicious and hassle-free dinner option? Look no further! In this recipe, we will show you how to make a mouthwatering Slow Cooker Sweet and Sour Chicken that is tangy, flavorful, and incredibly easy to prepare. Using simple ingredients and the convenience of a slow cooker, this dish is perfect for busy weeknights or lazy weekends when you want to enjoy a homemade meal without spending hours in the kitchen.

Ingredients

  • 1.5 lbs boneless, skinless chicken breasts, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 1 cup pineapple chunks, drained
  • 1 red bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 green bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 onion, sliced
  • 3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 cup ketchup
  • 1/2 cup apple cider vinegar
  • 1/4 cup brown sugar
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon cornstarch
  • 1 tablespoon water
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • Cooked white rice, for serving

Instructions

  1. In a slow cooker, combine the chicken, pineapple chunks, red and green bell peppers, onion, and minced garlic.

  2. In a mixing bowl, whisk together the ketchup, apple cider vinegar, brown sugar, soy sauce, salt, and pepper until well combined. Pour the sauce over the chicken and vegetables in the slow cooker. Stir gently to ensure everything is coated in the sauce.

  3. Cover the slow cooker and cook on low heat for 4-6 hours or on high heat for 2-3 hours. The chicken should be tender and cooked through.

  4. In a small bowl, mix the cornstarch and water until smooth. Stir the cornstarch mixture into the slow cooker, making sure to evenly distribute it. This will help thicken the sauce.

  5. Continue cooking on low heat for an additional 30 minutes to allow the sauce to thicken. If desired, you can adjust the seasoning at this point by adding more salt, pepper, or even a touch of hot sauce for extra heat.

  6. Once the sauce has thickened to your liking, serve the Slow Cooker Sweet and Sour Chicken over a bed of cooked white rice. The combination of tangy sauce, tender chicken, and colorful vegetables will tantalize your taste buds and leave you craving for more.

Tips and Variations

  • For an extra kick of flavor, add a teaspoon of grated ginger to the sauce mixture.
  • Feel free to customize the vegetables based on your preference. You can add carrots, snap peas, or even broccoli to the dish for added nutrition and color.
  • If you prefer a sweeter sauce, you can increase the amount of brown sugar or add a splash of honey.
  • Leftovers can be stored in an airtight container in the refrigerator for up to 3 days. Simply reheat in the microwave or on the stovetop for a quick and satisfying meal.

Conclusion

With this Slow Cooker Sweet and Sour Chicken recipe, you can enjoy a restaurant-quality dish at the comfort of your own home. The convenience of the slow cooker allows you to set it and forget it, while the combination of tangy sauce, tender chicken, and colorful vegetables creates a symphony of flavors that will leave you wanting more. So, why wait? Gather your ingredients and give this recipe a try. Your taste buds will thank you!
