No-Bake Granola Bars with Chocolate Chips and Peanut Butter Recipe: A Crunchy and Delicious Vegan Snack


If you’re looking for a quick and easy snack that’s both delicious and nutritious, look no further than these no-bake granola bars with chocolate chips and peanut butter. Not only are they incredibly simple to make, but they’re also vegan, making them a guilt-free treat for anyone looking to satisfy their sweet tooth without straying from their plant-based diet.

Ingredients

  • 2 1/2 cups rolled oats
  • 1/2 cup almond butter
  • 1/2 cup maple syrup
  • 1/4 cup coconut oil
  • 1/3 cup mini dairy-free chocolate chips
  • 1/3 cup chopped nuts (such as almonds, walnuts, or pecans)
  • 1/4 cup dried cranberries or raisins
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Instructions

Step 1: Prepare the Dry Ingredients

In a large mixing bowl, combine the rolled oats, chopped nuts, mini dairy-free chocolate chips, and dried cranberries or raisins. Stir the mixture until all the ingredients are evenly distributed.

Step 2: Prepare the Wet Ingredients

In a small saucepan, combine the almond butter, maple syrup, and coconut oil over low heat. Stir the mixture continuously until it becomes smooth and well combined. Remove the saucepan from the heat and stir in the salt and vanilla extract.

Step 3: Combine the Ingredients

Pour the wet ingredients into the bowl of dry ingredients. Using a spatula or your hands, mix everything together until the oats and add-ins are evenly coated with the wet mixture. Make sure all the ingredients are well combined.

Step 4: Press the Mixture into a Pan

Line an 8x8 inch baking pan with parchment paper, leaving some overhang on the sides for easy removal. Transfer the granola bar mixture into the pan and spread it out evenly. Use a spatula or the back of a spoon to press the mixture firmly into the pan.

Step 5: Chill the Bars

Place the pan in the refrigerator for at least 2 hours, or until the bars are firm and set. This will allow the coconut oil to solidify, binding the ingredients together.

Step 6: Cut and Serve

Once the bars are firm, use the parchment paper overhang to lift them out of the pan. Place them on a cutting board and slice them into individual bars or squares. Store the bars in an airtight container in the refrigerator for up to a week, or wrap them individually and freeze for longer storage.

Why These Bars Are Perfect

These no-bake granola bars with chocolate chips and peanut butter are the ideal snack for anyone on the go. They’re packed with wholesome ingredients like oats, nuts, and dried fruit, providing a good balance of fiber, healthy fats, and natural sweetness. The combination of almond butter and coconut oil not only holds the bars together but also adds a rich and creamy texture, while the mini dairy-free chocolate chips offer a delightful pop of sweetness in every bite.

Moreover, this recipe is highly customizable. Feel free to experiment with different add-ins such as shredded coconut, chia seeds, or even a drizzle of dairy-free dark chocolate on top. You can also adjust the sweetness by adding more or less maple syrup to suit your taste preferences.

These bars are not only perfect as a grab-and-go snack but also make a great addition to lunchboxes, hiking trips, or as a post-workout refuel. They are also kid-friendly, making them a convenient and healthier alternative to store-bought granola bars.

In conclusion, these no-bake granola bars with chocolate chips and peanut butter are a wonderful treat that’s simple to prepare, full of wholesome ingredients, and completely vegan. They are sure to become a household favorite, satisfying cravings for something sweet and crunchy without compromising on nutrition.

So, why not whip up a batch of these delectable bars and have a guilt-free snack ready whenever hunger strikes? Your taste buds and body will thank you for it!
