Dairy-Free BBQ: Grilled Favorites Without the Dairy


Are you ready to fire up the grill this summer but looking for dairy-free options? Look no further! In this blog post, we will explore mouthwatering dairy-free BBQ recipes that are sure to satisfy your cravings. Whether you’re lactose intolerant, vegan, or simply looking to reduce your dairy intake, these recipes will have you enjoying a delicious BBQ without compromising on flavor.

Grilled Portobello Mushroom Burger

Who says burgers have to be made with meat? This Grilled Portobello Mushroom Burger is a game-changer! The meaty texture of the portobello mushrooms combined with the smoky flavors from the grill will leave you wanting more. Here’s how to make it:

Ingredients:

  • 4 large portobello mushroom caps
  • 2 tablespoons balsamic vinegar
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Burger buns
  • Your favorite toppings (lettuce, tomatoes, onions, etc.)

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the grill to medium-high heat.
  2. In a small bowl, whisk together the balsamic vinegar, olive oil, minced garlic, salt, and pepper.
  3. Brush the mushroom caps with the marinade, making sure to coat both sides.
  4. Place the mushrooms on the grill and cook for 4-5 minutes per side, or until tender.
  5. Toast the burger buns on the grill for a few seconds.
  6. Assemble your burgers with the grilled portobello mushrooms and your favorite toppings.

Grilled Vegetable Skewers

Vegetables are a BBQ essential, and these Grilled Vegetable Skewers will add vibrant colors and flavors to your plate. Plus, they’re incredibly easy to make! Here’s what you’ll need:

Ingredients:

  • Assorted vegetables of your choice (bell peppers, zucchini, onions, cherry tomatoes, etc.)
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Soak wooden skewers in water for at least 30 minutes to prevent burning.
  2. Preheat the grill to medium heat.
  3. Cut the vegetables into bite-sized pieces.
  4. In a small bowl, mix together the olive oil, minced garlic, salt, and pepper.
  5. Thread the vegetables onto the skewers, alternating between different types for variety.
  6. Brush the vegetable skewers with the olive oil mixture.
  7. Grill the skewers for 10-12 minutes, turning occasionally, until the vegetables are tender and slightly charred.

Grilled Pineapple with Cinnamon

No BBQ is complete without a sweet treat, and this Grilled Pineapple with Cinnamon is a delightful dairy-free dessert option. The caramelization of the pineapple on the grill enhances its natural sweetness, and the hint of cinnamon adds a warm touch. Here’s how to make it:

Ingredients:

  • 1 pineapple, peeled and cored
  • 1 tablespoon coconut oil
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • Honey or maple syrup (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the grill to medium-high heat.
  2. Slice the pineapple into rings or spears.
  3. In a small bowl, melt the coconut oil and mix in the ground cinnamon.
  4. Brush the pineapple slices with the coconut oil and cinnamon mixture.
  5. Grill the pineapple for about 2-3 minutes per side, or until grill marks appear.
  6. Drizzle with honey or maple syrup if desired.

Enjoy your dairy-free BBQ feast!

With these dairy-free BBQ recipes, you can satisfy your cravings and enjoy a flavorful meal without the dairy. From the juicy Grilled Portobello Mushroom Burgers to the vibrant Grilled Vegetable Skewers and the sweet Grilled Pineapple with Cinnamon, there’s something for everyone at your next BBQ gathering. So fire up the grill, invite your friends and family, and indulge in these delicious dairy-free delights. Happy grilling!
