Instant Pot Chicken and Mushroom Creamy Pasta Recipe: A Rich and Creamy Option


Are you craving a comforting and indulgent meal that can be prepared in a flash? Look no further than this Instant Pot Chicken and Mushroom Creamy Pasta recipe. With its rich flavors and creamy sauce, it’s the perfect dish to satisfy your taste buds and leave you feeling satisfied. Plus, the convenience of using an Instant Pot makes it even easier to prepare.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound chicken breasts, boneless and skinless, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 8 ounces mushrooms, sliced
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 2 cups chicken broth
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried rosemary
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt, or to taste
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper, or to taste
  • 12 ounces pasta of your choice (penne or fettuccine work well)
  • Fresh parsley, chopped (for garnish)

Instructions

  1. Turn on your Instant Pot and select the “Saute” function. Add the butter and let it melt.

  2. Add the chopped onion and minced garlic to the pot. Saute until the onion becomes translucent and the garlic becomes fragrant, about 2-3 minutes.

  3. Add the chicken pieces to the pot and cook until they are browned on all sides, about 5 minutes. Make sure to stir occasionally to prevent sticking.

  4. Once the chicken is browned, add the sliced mushrooms to the pot. Saute for another 2-3 minutes until the mushrooms start to soften.

  5. Pour in the chicken broth, heavy cream, dried thyme, dried rosemary, salt, and black pepper. Stir well to combine all the ingredients.

  6. Add the pasta to the pot, ensuring that it is fully submerged in the liquid. Give it a gentle stir to prevent sticking.

  7. Close the Instant Pot lid and set the valve to the sealing position. Select the “Manual” or “Pressure Cook” setting, and cook on high pressure for 4 minutes.

  8. Once the cooking time is complete, perform a quick release by carefully turning the valve to the venting position. Be cautious as the steam will release rapidly.

  9. Open the lid and give the pasta a good stir. The sauce will thicken as it cools slightly.

  10. Serve the Instant Pot Chicken and Mushroom Creamy Pasta hot, garnished with freshly chopped parsley.

Tips and Variations

  • For an extra burst of flavor, you can add a splash of white wine to the pot after sauteing the mushrooms.
  • Feel free to use different types of mushrooms, such as cremini or shiitake, to add depth to the dish.
  • To enhance the creaminess, you can sprinkle grated Parmesan cheese over the pasta before serving.
  • If you prefer a lighter sauce, you can substitute half-and-half or milk for the heavy cream.

Conclusion

This Instant Pot Chicken and Mushroom Creamy Pasta recipe is a true crowd-pleaser. The convenience of the Instant Pot combined with the rich flavors of the creamy sauce and tender chicken make it a winning choice for any meal. Whether you’re cooking for your family or hosting a dinner party, this dish is sure to impress. So grab your Instant Pot and get ready to indulge in a delicious and satisfying meal. Enjoy!
