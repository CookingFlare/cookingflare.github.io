Healthy One Pot Quinoa and Black Bean Stir Fry


Are you looking for a quick and healthy meal that’s packed with protein and nutrients? Look no further than this delicious one-pot quinoa and black bean stir fry. This recipe is not only easy to make, but it’s also a great way to incorporate wholesome ingredients into your diet. Whether you’re a busy professional, a student on the go, or a parent looking to serve up a nutritious dinner, this dish is sure to become a new favorite in your recipe collection.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup quinoa
  • 1 can black beans, drained and rinsed
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 red bell pepper, diced
  • 1 cup corn kernels
  • 1 cup diced tomatoes (canned or fresh)
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • 1 teaspoon chili powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon paprika
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 cups vegetable broth
  • Fresh cilantro for garnish
  • Lime wedges for serving

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Quinoa: Begin by rinsing the quinoa under cold water to remove any bitterness. In a large pot or skillet, toast the quinoa over medium heat for a few minutes until it becomes fragrant. Add the vegetable broth and bring to a boil. Once boiling, reduce the heat to a simmer, cover, and cook for about 15 minutes or until the quinoa is fluffy and the liquid is absorbed.

  2. Sauté the Aromatics: In the same pot, heat some olive oil over medium heat. Add the diced onion and sauté until it becomes translucent. Then, add the minced garlic and cook for another minute until fragrant.

  3. Add the Veggies and Beans: Incorporate the diced bell pepper, corn kernels, and black beans into the pot. Stir well to combine all the ingredients. Cook for a few minutes until the vegetables begin to soften.

  4. Season the Stir Fry: Sprinkle the cumin, chili powder, paprika, salt, and pepper over the mixture. Stir to evenly distribute the seasonings and allow the flavors to meld together.

  5. Combine with Quinoa: Once the vegetables are tender, add the cooked quinoa to the pot. Mix everything together until the quinoa is well incorporated with the vegetables and beans.

  6. Finish and Serve: Turn off the heat and garnish the stir fry with fresh cilantro. Serve the dish with lime wedges on the side for an extra burst of citrus flavor.

Nutritional Benefits

  • Quinoa: This ancient grain is a complete protein, rich in fiber, and packed with essential vitamins and minerals such as iron and magnesium.
  • Black Beans: High in fiber and protein, black beans are a great source of plant-based nutrition. They also contain antioxidants and can help regulate blood sugar levels.
  • Vegetables: Bell peppers, onions, and corn provide a colorful array of vitamins, minerals, and antioxidants to support overall health.
  • Spices: Cumin, chili powder, and paprika not only add flavor but also contribute to the dish’s antioxidant and anti-inflammatory properties.

Conclusion

This healthy one-pot quinoa and black bean stir fry is a versatile dish that can be enjoyed on its own or as a side to your favorite protein. It’s perfect for meal prep, as the flavors develop even more over time. Whether you’re a vegetarian, vegan, or simply looking to incorporate more plant-based meals into your diet, this recipe is a satisfying and nutritious option. Plus, the convenience of cooking everything in one pot means less cleanup and more time to enjoy your meal. Try this recipe today and savor the wholesome goodness of this vibrant and flavorful stir fry!
