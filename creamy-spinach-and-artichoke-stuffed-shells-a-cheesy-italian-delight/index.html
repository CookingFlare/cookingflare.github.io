Creamy Spinach and Artichoke Stuffed Shells: A Cheesy Italian Delight


If you’re a fan of Italian cuisine and enjoy the comfort of a creamy, cheesy dish, then you’re in for a treat! Today, we’re going to dive into the delicious world of Creamy Spinach and Artichoke Stuffed Shells. This recipe combines the flavors of tender pasta shells, creamy spinach, tangy artichokes, and ooey-gooey cheese, resulting in a dish that will have your taste buds dancing with joy.

Ingredients

To create this cheesy Italian delight, you’ll need the following ingredients:

  • 1 box of jumbo pasta shells
  • 2 cups of fresh spinach, chopped
  • 1 can of artichoke hearts, drained and chopped
  • 1 cup of ricotta cheese
  • 1 cup of shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup of grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/2 cup of sour cream
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon of dried basil
  • 1/2 teaspoon of dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Olive oil for greasing the baking dish

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil and cook the jumbo pasta shells according to the package instructions. Be sure to cook them al dente, as they will continue to cook in the oven.
  3. In a large mixing bowl, combine the chopped spinach, chopped artichoke hearts, ricotta cheese, shredded mozzarella cheese, grated Parmesan cheese, sour cream, minced garlic, dried basil, dried oregano, salt, and pepper. Mix until well combined.
  4. Drain the cooked pasta shells and rinse them under cold water to stop the cooking process. Pat them dry with a paper towel.
  5. Grease a 9x13-inch baking dish with olive oil.
  6. Take each cooked pasta shell and spoon a generous amount of the creamy spinach and artichoke mixture into it. Place the filled shell into the greased baking dish. Repeat this process until all the shells are filled and arranged in the dish.
  7. Once all the shells are filled and arranged, cover the baking dish with aluminum foil and bake in the preheated oven for 20 minutes.
  8. After 20 minutes, remove the foil and continue baking for an additional 10 minutes, or until the cheese is melted and bubbly, and the tops of the shells are lightly golden.
  9. Remove from the oven and let the stuffed shells cool for a few minutes before serving.
  10. Serve hot and enjoy the creamy, cheesy goodness of these Spinach and Artichoke Stuffed Shells!

Tips and Variations

  • To add a little extra kick to your stuffed shells, sprinkle some red pepper flakes on top before baking.
  • If you’re a meat lover, you can add some cooked and crumbled Italian sausage to the filling mixture for an added layer of flavor.
  • For a vegetarian option, substitute the Parmesan cheese with a vegetarian-friendly alternative or omit it altogether.
  • These stuffed shells make for great leftovers. Simply store them in an airtight container in the refrigerator for up to three days and reheat in the oven or microwave when ready to enjoy.

Conclusion

Creamy Spinach and Artichoke Stuffed Shells are a fantastic option for a comforting and satisfying meal. The combination of flavors and textures will leave you craving more. Whether you’re cooking for yourself, your family, or hosting a dinner party, this cheesy Italian delight is sure to impress. So, gather your ingredients, roll up your sleeves, and get ready to indulge in this delicious recipe. Buon appetito!
