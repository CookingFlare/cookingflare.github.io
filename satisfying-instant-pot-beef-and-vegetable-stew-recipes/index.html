Satisfying Instant Pot Beef and Vegetable Stew Recipes


Whether it’s a chilly winter evening or a rainy day, a comforting bowl of beef and vegetable stew can warm both the body and the soul. The Instant Pot has revolutionized the way we cook, making it easier and faster to prepare hearty stews without sacrificing flavor. In this blog post, we’ll explore two satisfying Instant Pot beef and vegetable stew recipes that are sure to become staples in your kitchen.

Classic Beef and Vegetable Stew

Ingredients:

  • 2 pounds beef chuck, cut into 1-inch cubes
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 4 carrots, peeled and sliced
  • 4 celery stalks, sliced
  • 2 cups beef broth
  • 1 cup red wine
  • 1 can (14.5 ounces) diced tomatoes
  • 2 bay leaves
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 4 potatoes, peeled and diced
  • 1 cup frozen peas

Instructions:

  1. Set the Instant Pot to “Saute” mode and heat the olive oil. Add the beef cubes and brown them on all sides. Remove the beef and set it aside.

  2. Add the diced onion and minced garlic to the Instant Pot and sauté until fragrant.

  3. Pour in the red wine and scrape the bottom of the pot to deglaze it, releasing all the flavorful browned bits.

  4. Return the browned beef to the pot, along with the carrots, celery, beef broth, diced tomatoes, bay leaves, dried thyme, salt, and pepper.

  5. Close the Instant Pot and set it to “Manual” mode for 35 minutes.

  6. Once the cooking cycle is complete, allow the pressure to release naturally for 10 minutes, then carefully do a quick pressure release.

  7. Open the Instant Pot and add the diced potatoes and frozen peas.

  8. Close the lid and cook on high pressure for an additional 5 minutes.

  9. Once the cooking cycle is complete, perform a quick pressure release and carefully open the Instant Pot.

  10. Stir the stew gently and adjust the seasoning if necessary.

  11. Serve the classic beef and vegetable stew hot, garnished with fresh parsley if desired.

Spicy Beef and Vegetable Stew

Ingredients:

  • 2 pounds beef stew meat, cut into chunks
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 bell peppers, diced
  • 1 can (14.5 ounces) fire-roasted tomatoes
  • 1 cup beef broth
  • 1 cup tomato sauce
  • 2 teaspoons chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • 1/2 teaspoon paprika
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 cups butternut squash, diced
  • 1 cup corn kernels
  • Fresh cilantro for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Turn the Instant Pot to “Saute” mode and heat the vegetable oil. Add the beef chunks and brown them on all sides. Remove the beef and set it aside.

  2. Add the chopped onion and minced garlic to the Instant Pot and sauté until softened.

  3. Pour in the beef broth and deglaze the pot, scraping up any browned bits from the bottom.

  4. Return the browned beef to the pot, along with the diced bell peppers, fire-roasted tomatoes, tomato sauce, chili powder, cumin, paprika, salt, and pepper.

  5. Close the Instant Pot and set it to “Manual” mode for 30 minutes.

  6. Once the cooking cycle is complete, allow the pressure to release naturally for 10 minutes, then carefully do a quick pressure release.

  7. Open the Instant Pot and add the diced butternut squash and corn kernels.

  8. Close the lid and cook on high pressure for an additional 3 minutes.

  9. Once the cooking cycle is complete, perform a quick pressure release and carefully open the Instant Pot.

  10. Stir the stew gently and adjust the seasoning if necessary.

  11. Serve the spicy beef and vegetable stew hot, garnished with fresh cilantro for an extra kick.

These two Instant Pot beef and vegetable stew recipes are not only satisfying and delicious but also incredibly easy to prepare. The next time you’re in the mood for a comforting meal, give one of these recipes a try and let the Instant Pot work its magic. Enjoy the rich flavors and hearty goodness of these hearty stews!
