Tangy Lemon Pepper Beef Stir Fry: A Burst of Citrusy Goodness


If you’re looking for a quick and flavorful meal that will tantalize your taste buds, look no further than this Tangy Lemon Pepper Beef Stir Fry. Packed with the zesty goodness of lemon and the bold kick of pepper, this dish is sure to be a crowd-pleaser. With its bright and refreshing flavors, it’s perfect for any occasion, whether it’s a weeknight dinner or a weekend gathering with friends. So, let’s dive into the recipe and discover the burst of citrusy goodness that awaits!

Ingredients

  • 1 pound of beef, thinly sliced
  • 2 tablespoons of soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons of lemon juice
  • 1 tablespoon of lemon zest
  • 1 tablespoon of black pepper
  • 2 tablespoons of vegetable oil
  • 1 onion, thinly sliced
  • 2 bell peppers, thinly sliced
  • 3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon of cornstarch
  • 1/4 cup of water
  • Salt, to taste
  • Fresh cilantro, for garnish (optional)

Instructions

  1. In a bowl, combine the soy sauce, lemon juice, lemon zest, and black pepper. Add the beef slices to the marinade and let it sit for at least 15 minutes to allow the flavors to infuse.

  2. Heat the vegetable oil in a large skillet or wok over medium-high heat. Add the marinated beef slices and stir-fry until they are browned and cooked to your desired level of doneness. Remove the beef from the skillet and set it aside.

  3. In the same skillet, add the sliced onion and bell peppers. Stir-fry for a few minutes until they are slightly softened.

  4. Push the vegetables to one side of the skillet and add the minced garlic to the empty space. Stir-fry the garlic for about 30 seconds until fragrant, then mix it with the vegetables.

  5. In a small bowl, whisk together the cornstarch and water until smooth. Pour this mixture into the skillet and stir well to combine with the vegetables. Cook for another minute until the sauce thickens slightly.

  6. Return the cooked beef to the skillet and toss it with the vegetables and sauce. Season with salt to taste. Continue to cook for an additional minute to ensure everything is well coated in the tangy sauce.

  7. Remove the skillet from heat and garnish with fresh cilantro, if desired.

Serving Suggestions

This Tangy Lemon Pepper Beef Stir Fry is delicious on its own, but you can elevate it further by serving it with steamed rice or noodles. The tender beef and vibrant vegetables pair perfectly with the citrusy sauce, creating a harmonious blend of flavors in every bite. You can also add a squeeze of lemon juice on top for an extra burst of freshness.

Tips and Variations

  • For a spicier version, add some red pepper flakes or sliced chili peppers to the stir fry.
  • Feel free to swap out the beef with chicken, shrimp, or tofu if you prefer. Adjust the cooking time accordingly to ensure your protein is cooked through.
  • Experiment with different vegetables such as broccoli, carrots, or snap peas to add more color and texture to the dish.
  • If you prefer a thicker sauce, increase the amount of cornstarch and water mixture to achieve your desired consistency.

Conclusion

With its tangy and zesty flavors, this Tangy Lemon Pepper Beef Stir Fry is a delightful dish that will awaken your taste buds. The combination of tender beef, vibrant vegetables, and the citrusy kick of lemon and pepper creates a truly satisfying and refreshing experience. Whether you’re a seasoned cook or a beginner in the kitchen, this recipe is simple enough to follow yet impressive enough to impress your guests. So, go ahead and give it a try, and let the burst of citrusy goodness transport you to a world of deliciousness!
