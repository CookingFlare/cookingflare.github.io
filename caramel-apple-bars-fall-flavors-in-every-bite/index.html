Caramel Apple Bars: Fall Flavors in Every Bite


As the leaves turn vibrant shades of red, orange, and yellow, and there’s a crispness in the air, it can only mean one thing – fall has arrived! And with the arrival of fall comes an abundance of delicious seasonal treats, including the beloved caramel apple bars. These delightful bars are the perfect way to capture the essence of autumn in every bite, combining the sweet and tangy flavors of caramel and apples with a buttery, crumbly crust. Whether you’re baking for a cozy night in or a festive gathering, these caramel apple bars are sure to be a hit. So, let’s dive into the recipe and bring the warmth and comfort of fall flavors into your kitchen!

Ingredients

For the Crust and Crumble:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup old-fashioned oats
  • 1 cup packed brown sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup unsalted butter, cold and cubed

For the Apple Filling:

  • 4 large apples, peeled, cored, and thinly sliced
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground nutmeg

For the Caramel Drizzle:

  • 1/2 cup caramel sauce

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Crust and Crumble:

    • Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C) and grease a 9x13 inch baking pan.
    • In a large bowl, combine the flour, oats, brown sugar, baking powder, and salt. Add the cold, cubed butter and use a pastry cutter or your hands to mix until the mixture resembles coarse crumbs.
    • Take about 2 cups of the mixture and press it firmly into the bottom of the prepared baking pan. Set the remaining mixture aside for the crumble topping.

  2. Prepare the Apple Filling:

    • In another bowl, toss the sliced apples with granulated sugar, flour, cinnamon, and nutmeg until the apples are evenly coated.
    • Spread the apple mixture over the crust in the baking pan.

  3. Assemble and Bake:

    • Sprinkle the reserved crumble mixture evenly over the apple layer.
    • Bake in the preheated oven for 45-55 minutes, or until the top is golden brown.
    • Allow the bars to cool completely in the pan on a wire rack.

  4. Add the Caramel Drizzle:

    • Once the bars have cooled, drizzle the caramel sauce over the top.
    • For a decorative touch, you can also drizzle additional caramel sauce over individual bars when serving.

Serving and Storage

These delectable caramel apple bars are best enjoyed at room temperature. They can be stored in an airtight container at room temperature for up to 3 days, or in the refrigerator for up to 5 days. If you have any leftovers (which is rare!), simply reheat them in the microwave for a few seconds to restore their gooey goodness.

Fall in Every Bite

With their buttery crust, tender apple filling, and luscious caramel drizzle, these caramel apple bars encapsulate the essence of fall in every irresistible bite. Whether you’re savoring them with a cup of hot cider on a chilly evening or sharing them with loved ones at a fall gathering, these bars are guaranteed to bring joy and warmth to any occasion. So, embrace the flavors of the season and indulge in the delicious harmony of caramel and apples with this delightful recipe. Happy baking, and may your kitchen be filled with the sweet aroma of fall!
