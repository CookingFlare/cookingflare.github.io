Tender and Juicy: Lemon Herb Grilled Pork Chops


Grilling season is here, and what better way to enjoy the warm weather than with a mouthwatering plate of tender and juicy lemon herb grilled pork chops? This recipe is a surefire crowd-pleaser, combining the tangy freshness of lemon with the aromatic flavors of herbs. Whether you’re hosting a backyard barbecue or simply craving a delicious meal, these grilled pork chops are a must-try!

Ingredients

  • 4 bone-in pork chops (about 1 inch thick)
  • 2 lemons, juiced and zested
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon fresh rosemary, chopped
  • 1 tablespoon fresh thyme, chopped
  • Salt and pepper, to taste

Instructions

  1. Preheat your grill to medium-high heat. While the grill is heating up, prepare the marinade for the pork chops.

  2. In a small bowl, combine the lemon juice, lemon zest, olive oil, minced garlic, rosemary, thyme, salt, and pepper. Mix well to create a flavorful marinade.

  3. Place the pork chops in a shallow dish or a resealable plastic bag. Pour the marinade over the pork chops, ensuring that they are fully coated. Allow the pork chops to marinate for at least 30 minutes, but for best results, marinate them for 2-4 hours in the refrigerator. This will allow the flavors to penetrate the meat, resulting in tender and flavorful pork chops.

  4. Once the pork chops have finished marinating, remove them from the dish or bag and discard any excess marinade. Pat the pork chops dry with paper towels to remove any excess moisture.

  5. Season the pork chops with additional salt and pepper, according to your taste preferences.

  6. Place the pork chops on the preheated grill and cook for approximately 4-5 minutes per side, or until they reach an internal temperature of 145°F (63°C). Use a meat thermometer to ensure the pork chops are cooked to perfection.

  7. Once cooked, remove the pork chops from the grill and let them rest for a few minutes before serving. This resting period allows the juices to redistribute, resulting in tender and juicy pork chops.

  8. Serve the lemon herb grilled pork chops with a side of grilled vegetables or a refreshing salad. The zesty flavors of lemon and the aromatic herbs will complement the smoky flavors of the grilled meat, creating a harmonious and satisfying meal.

Tips and Variations

  • For an extra burst of flavor, you can brush the pork chops with the marinade while grilling. This will enhance the taste and provide a beautiful glaze on the surface of the meat.

  • If you prefer boneless pork chops, you can easily adapt this recipe by using boneless pork loin chops instead. The cooking time may vary slightly, so make sure to adjust accordingly.

  • Feel free to experiment with different herbs and spices to customize the flavor of your grilled pork chops. Basil, oregano, or even a hint of chili powder can add an exciting twist to this classic recipe.

  • If you don’t have a grill, you can also cook these pork chops on a stovetop grill pan or even in the oven. Simply adjust the cooking time and temperature accordingly.

So, fire up your grill and get ready to indulge in the tantalizing flavors of lemon herb grilled pork chops. This recipe is a true delight for your taste buds, offering a perfect balance of tangy, aromatic, and smoky flavors. Whether you’re a seasoned griller or a novice in the kitchen, this dish is guaranteed to impress. Enjoy the company of friends and family as you savor every juicy bite of these tender and flavorful pork chops. Happy grilling!
