Balsamic Glazed Chicken Pizza: A Tangy Twist

3 min · 506 words

Are you tired of the same old pizza flavors? Looking for something new and exciting to tantalize your taste buds? Well, look no further! Today, I’m going to share with you a recipe that will take your pizza game to a whole new level: Balsamic Glazed Chicken Pizza.

Ingredients

For the pizza dough:

  • 2 ½ cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • 1 tablespoon active dry yeast
  • 1 cup warm water
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil

For the balsamic glaze:

  • ½ cup balsamic vinegar
  • 2 tablespoons honey
  • 1 garlic clove, minced

For the chicken:

  • 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil

For the toppings:

  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • ½ cup crumbled feta cheese
  • ½ red onion, thinly sliced
  • ½ cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • Fresh basil leaves, for garnish

Instructions

  1. Start by preparing the pizza dough. In a large mixing bowl, combine the flour, salt, and sugar. In a separate small bowl, dissolve the yeast in warm water and let it sit for about 5 minutes until it becomes frothy. Pour the yeast mixture and olive oil into the flour mixture and stir until a dough forms.

  2. Transfer the dough onto a floured surface and knead it for about 5 minutes until it becomes smooth and elastic. Place the dough in a greased bowl, cover it with a clean kitchen towel, and let it rise in a warm place for about 1 hour or until it doubles in size.

  3. While the dough is rising, preheat your oven to 450°F (230°C). In a small saucepan, combine the balsamic vinegar, honey, and minced garlic. Bring the mixture to a boil over medium-high heat, then reduce the heat to low and let it simmer for about 10 minutes until it thickens and reduces by half. Set aside.

  4. Season the chicken breasts with salt and pepper on both sides. Heat the olive oil in a skillet over medium heat and cook the chicken for about 6-8 minutes per side until it’s cooked through and no longer pink in the center. Remove the chicken from the skillet and let it rest for a few minutes before slicing it into thin strips.

  5. Once the dough has risen, punch it down and divide it into two equal portions. Roll out each portion into a round shape and transfer them onto two greased baking sheets or pizza stones.

  6. Brush the balsamic glaze over the pizza dough, leaving a small border around the edges. Sprinkle the shredded mozzarella cheese over the glaze, followed by the crumbled feta cheese. Arrange the sliced chicken, red onion, and cherry tomatoes on top.

  7. Bake the pizzas in the preheated oven for about 12-15 minutes or until the crust is golden brown and the cheese is bubbly and slightly browned.

  8. Remove the pizzas from the oven and garnish them with fresh basil leaves. Let them cool for a few minutes before slicing and serving.

Conclusion

And there you have it, a unique and delicious twist on the classic pizza! The combination of the tangy balsamic glaze, savory chicken, and creamy cheeses will undoubtedly make your taste buds dance with delight. So, the next time you’re craving pizza, why not give this Balsamic Glazed Chicken Pizza a try? Trust me, your taste buds will thank you!
