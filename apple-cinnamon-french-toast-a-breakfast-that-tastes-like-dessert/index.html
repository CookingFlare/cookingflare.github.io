Apple Cinnamon French Toast: A Breakfast That Tastes Like Dessert


Are you ready to elevate your breakfast game? Look no further than this delightful Apple Cinnamon French Toast recipe that will have you savoring every bite. This dish is the perfect blend of comforting flavors with a touch of sweetness, making it a breakfast treat that feels like a dessert. Let’s dive into the recipe and discover how to create this delightful morning indulgence.

Ingredients

For the French toast:

  • 6 thick slices of brioche bread
  • 4 large eggs
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground nutmeg
  • 2 tablespoons unsalted butter

For the apple topping:

  • 2 large apples, peeled, cored, and thinly sliced
  • 3 tablespoons unsalted butter
  • 3 tablespoons brown sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • Pinch of salt

For garnish:

  • Powdered sugar
  • Maple syrup

Instructions

Step 1: Prepare the Apple Topping

Start by preparing the apple topping. In a skillet over medium heat, melt 3 tablespoons of unsalted butter. Add the thinly sliced apples, brown sugar, ground cinnamon, and a pinch of salt. Cook the apples until they are tender and caramelized, stirring occasionally. This process should take about 8-10 minutes. Once done, set the apple topping aside and keep it warm.

Step 2: Make the French Toast Batter

In a shallow dish, whisk together the eggs, milk, vanilla extract, ground cinnamon, and ground nutmeg until well combined. This aromatic batter will infuse the brioche bread with a sweet and fragrant flavor.

Step 3: Prepare the French Toast

Dip each slice of brioche bread into the egg mixture, ensuring that both sides are well-coated. In a large non-stick skillet or griddle, melt 2 tablespoons of unsalted butter over medium heat. Place the coated brioche slices onto the skillet and cook until golden brown on both sides, approximately 3-4 minutes per side.

Step 4: Assemble and Serve

Once the French toast slices are cooked to perfection, transfer them to serving plates. Top each slice generously with the warm apple topping, allowing the caramelized apples to cascade over the golden French toast. For the finishing touch, dust the Apple Cinnamon French Toast with powdered sugar and drizzle with maple syrup.

Indulge in a Breakfast Dessert

Now, it’s time to savor the delightful fusion of flavors in this Apple Cinnamon French Toast. The warm and tender apples complement the rich, custardy brioche, while the hint of cinnamon adds a comforting aroma. With each bite, you’ll experience a symphony of textures and tastes that blur the line between breakfast and dessert.

Whether you’re hosting a brunch gathering or simply treating yourself to a special morning meal, this Apple Cinnamon French Toast is guaranteed to impress and satisfy. Its irresistible appeal lies in its ability to transform an ordinary breakfast into a luxurious experience that tantalizes the taste buds.

So, the next time you want to indulge in a breakfast that feels like dessert, whip up a batch of Apple Cinnamon French Toast and relish in the decadent flavors that will leave you craving more.

What are you waiting for? It’s time to elevate your breakfast with a touch of sweetness and a whole lot of flavor. Enjoy!
