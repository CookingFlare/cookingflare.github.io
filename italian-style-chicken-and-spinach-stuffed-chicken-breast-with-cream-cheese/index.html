Italian-Style Chicken and Spinach Stuffed Chicken Breast with Cream Cheese

3 min · 588 words

If you’re looking for a delicious and impressive dish to serve at your next dinner party or family gathering, this Italian-style chicken and spinach stuffed chicken breast with cream cheese recipe is sure to impress. It’s a flavorful and elegant dish that combines the richness of cream cheese with the earthy taste of spinach, all wrapped in tender, juicy chicken breast. The Italian-inspired seasoning adds a burst of savory flavor that will tantalize your taste buds. Let’s dive into the recipe and learn how to create this mouthwatering dish.

Ingredients:

  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1 cup of fresh spinach, chopped
  • 1/2 cup of cream cheese, softened
  • 1/4 cup of grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon of dried Italian seasoning
  • 1/2 teaspoon of salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon of black pepper
  • 2 tablespoons of olive oil

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the Chicken Breasts:

    • Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
    • Place each chicken breast between two sheets of plastic wrap and gently pound them to an even thickness of about 1/2 inch. This will help the chicken cook evenly and make it easier to stuff.

  2. Make the Filling:

    • In a mixing bowl, combine the chopped spinach, softened cream cheese, grated Parmesan cheese, minced garlic, Italian seasoning, salt, and black pepper. Mix until all the ingredients are well combined.

  3. Stuff the Chicken Breasts:

    • Divide the filling mixture evenly among the chicken breasts, spreading it over each breast. Roll up the chicken breasts, tucking in the sides to enclose the filling. Secure the rolls with toothpicks to hold their shape.

  4. Sear the Chicken:

    • Heat the olive oil in an oven-safe skillet over medium-high heat. Once hot, add the stuffed chicken breasts and sear them for 2-3 minutes on each side, or until golden brown.

  5. Bake in the Oven:

    • Transfer the skillet to the preheated oven and bake the chicken for 20-25 minutes, or until the internal temperature reaches 165°F (74°C) and the juices run clear.

  6. Serve and Enjoy:

    • Once the chicken is cooked through, remove it from the oven. Let it rest for a few minutes before removing the toothpicks and slicing it into rounds. Serve the Italian-style chicken and spinach stuffed chicken breast with cream cheese alongside your favorite side dishes and enjoy!

This Italian-style chicken and spinach stuffed chicken breast with cream cheese is a delightful blend of flavors and textures, making it a perfect dish for a special occasion or a comforting family meal. The creamy filling, combined with the tender chicken, creates a dish that is both elegant and satisfying. Whether you’re a seasoned chef or a novice cook, this recipe is sure to impress and delight your guests.

From preparing the filling to searing and baking the chicken, each step contributes to the creation of a flavorful and visually stunning dish.
