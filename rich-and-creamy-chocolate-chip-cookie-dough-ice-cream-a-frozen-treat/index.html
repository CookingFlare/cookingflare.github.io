Rich and Creamy Chocolate Chip Cookie Dough Ice Cream: A Frozen Treat


Are you ready to indulge in a frozen treat that combines the rich flavors of chocolate chip cookies and the creaminess of ice cream? Look no further! In this recipe, I am going to show you how to make the most delectable Rich and Creamy Chocolate Chip Cookie Dough Ice Cream. Get ready to satisfy your sweet tooth with every spoonful of this mouthwatering frozen dessert.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups heavy cream
  • 1 cup whole milk
  • 3/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 1/2 cup chocolate chips

For the cookie dough:

  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/2 cup brown sugar
  • 2 tablespoons milk
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 1/4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup chocolate chips

Instructions

  1. In a medium saucepan, heat the heavy cream, whole milk, granulated sugar, and salt over medium heat. Stir occasionally until the sugar has dissolved, and the mixture is hot but not boiling. Remove from heat and let it cool for 10 minutes.

  2. In the meantime, prepare the cookie dough. In a large bowl, cream together the softened butter, granulated sugar, and brown sugar until light and fluffy. Add the milk and vanilla extract and mix until well combined. Gradually add the flour and salt, mixing until just combined. Finally, fold in the chocolate chips.

  3. Once the cream mixture has cooled, stir in the vanilla extract. Pour the mixture into an ice cream maker and churn according to the manufacturer’s instructions. During the last few minutes of churning, add the chocolate chips.

  4. While the ice cream is churning, take small pieces of the prepared cookie dough and gently fold them into the ice cream mixture. Be sure not to overmix, as you want to have chunks of cookie dough throughout the ice cream.

  5. Transfer the churned ice cream into a lidded container, layering it with additional pieces of cookie dough. Place the container in the freezer and let it harden for at least 4 hours or until firm.

  6. When you’re ready to serve, scoop the Rich and Creamy Chocolate Chip Cookie Dough Ice Cream into bowls or cones. Garnish with extra chocolate chips if desired.

Enjoy Your Frozen Delight!

There you have it - a recipe that combines the classic flavors of chocolate chip cookies with the smooth and creamy texture of ice cream. Each bite of this Rich and Creamy Chocolate Chip Cookie Dough Ice Cream will transport you to dessert heaven. The combination of the cold ice cream and the chewy cookie dough is simply irresistible.

Whether you’re enjoying a bowl of this frozen treat on a hot summer day or indulging in a late-night snack, this recipe is sure to satisfy your cravings. So gather your ingredients, fire up your ice cream maker, and get ready to experience the ultimate frozen delight.

Remember to share this recipe with your friends and family, and let them in on the secret to making the most irresistible Rich and Creamy Chocolate Chip Cookie Dough Ice Cream. Happy scooping!
