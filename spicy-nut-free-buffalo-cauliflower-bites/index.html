Spicy Nut-Free Buffalo Cauliflower Bites


If you’re looking for a delicious and healthy alternative to traditional buffalo wings, look no further than these Spicy Nut-Free Buffalo Cauliflower Bites! Packed with flavor and a satisfying crunch, these bite-sized treats are perfect for game nights, parties, or even just a tasty snack. And the best part? They’re completely nut-free, making them suitable for those with nut allergies or dietary restrictions. So let’s dive right into the recipe!

Ingredients

  • 1 large head of cauliflower, cut into florets
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup unsweetened almond milk
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon onion powder
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1/2 cup hot sauce (adjust according to your spice preference)
  • 2 tablespoons melted butter (can substitute with vegan butter for a dairy-free option)
  • 1 tablespoon maple syrup
  • Fresh cilantro or parsley, for garnish (optional)
  • Ranch or blue cheese dressing, for dipping (optional)

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 450°F (230°C). Line a baking sheet with parchment paper or lightly grease it with cooking spray.

  2. In a large mixing bowl, whisk together the flour, almond milk, garlic powder, onion powder, paprika, salt, and black pepper until you have a smooth batter.

  3. Dip each cauliflower floret into the batter, allowing any excess to drip off, and place them onto the prepared baking sheet. Make sure to spread them out evenly to ensure they cook evenly.

  4. Bake the cauliflower florets in the preheated oven for 20-25 minutes, or until they are golden brown and crispy. Flip them halfway through the cooking time to ensure both sides are evenly cooked.

  5. While the cauliflower is baking, prepare the spicy buffalo sauce. In a small bowl, combine the hot sauce, melted butter, and maple syrup. Adjust the amount of hot sauce according to your desired level of spiciness.

  6. Once the cauliflower florets are done baking, remove them from the oven and transfer them to a large mixing bowl. Pour the spicy buffalo sauce over the cauliflower and gently toss until each floret is evenly coated.

  7. Return the cauliflower to the baking sheet and bake for an additional 10 minutes, or until the sauce has slightly caramelized and the cauliflower has reached your desired level of crispiness.

  8. Remove the cauliflower from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes. Garnish with fresh cilantro or parsley if desired.

  9. Serve the Spicy Nut-Free Buffalo Cauliflower Bites with ranch or blue cheese dressing on the side for dipping, if desired.

Conclusion

These Spicy Nut-Free Buffalo Cauliflower Bites are a crowd-pleasing appetizer that will leave your taste buds wanting more. They are a healthier alternative to traditional buffalo wings, and the nut-free aspect makes them suitable for everyone to enjoy. So the next time you’re craving some spicy and savory bites, give this recipe a try. Your friends and family will be impressed, and your taste buds will thank you!

Enjoy!
