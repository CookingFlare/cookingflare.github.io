Delicious Strawberry Lemonade Cupcakes: A Summertime Refreshment


The sun is shining, the birds are singing, and the warm breeze is gently blowing through the trees. It’s the perfect time to indulge in the delightful flavors of summer with these delicious Strawberry Lemonade Cupcakes. Bursting with the tangy sweetness of fresh strawberries and the zesty kick of lemon, these cupcakes are a refreshing treat that will brighten any summer day.

Ingredients

For the Cupcakes:

  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 1/2 tsp baking powder
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1/2 cup buttermilk
  • 1/4 cup fresh lemon juice
  • Zest of 1 lemon
  • 1 cup diced fresh strawberries

For the Strawberry Lemonade Frosting:

  • 1 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 4 cups confectioners’ sugar
  • 1/4 cup fresh strawberry puree
  • 2 tbsp fresh lemon juice
  • Zest of 1 lemon
  • Pinch of salt

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C) and line a muffin tin with cupcake liners.

  2. In a medium bowl, whisk together the flour, baking powder, and salt.

  3. In a separate large bowl, cream the butter and sugar together until light and fluffy. Add the eggs one at a time, beating well after each addition. Stir in the vanilla extract.

  4. Gradually add the dry ingredients to the wet ingredients, alternating with the buttermilk. Mix until just combined. Fold in the lemon juice, lemon zest, and diced strawberries.

  5. Divide the batter evenly among the prepared cupcake liners, filling each about 2/3 full. Bake for 18-20 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean. Allow the cupcakes to cool in the pan for a few minutes before transferring them to a wire rack to cool completely.

  6. While the cupcakes are cooling, prepare the frosting. In a large bowl, beat the softened butter until smooth. Gradually add the confectioners’ sugar, strawberry puree, lemon juice, lemon zest, and salt. Beat until light and fluffy.

  7. Once the cupcakes are completely cooled, frost them with the strawberry lemonade frosting using a piping bag or offset spatula. Garnish with a slice of fresh strawberry or a twist of lemon zest for a beautiful finishing touch.

Enjoy the Fruity Bliss

These Strawberry Lemonade Cupcakes are a delightful blend of sweet and tangy flavors, making them a perfect addition to any summer gathering, picnic, or barbecue. The vibrant colors and refreshing taste will captivate your taste buds and leave you craving more. Whether you’re hosting a party or simply looking for a special treat to enjoy with your loved ones, these cupcakes are sure to impress.

So, whip up a batch of these delectable Strawberry Lemonade Cupcakes and savor the essence of summer in every bite. With their irresistible aroma and luscious taste, they are guaranteed to become a seasonal favorite in your recipe collection. Let the flavors of summer come alive with every delightful bite of these scrumptious cupcakes.
