Thai Pineapple Curry with Shrimp: A Sweet and Savory Combination


If you’re looking to add a burst of flavor to your dinner table, look no further than this delightful Thai Pineapple Curry with Shrimp recipe. This dish brings together the sweetness of pineapple with the savory goodness of shrimp and a blend of aromatic Thai spices, creating a truly mouthwatering experience. Let’s dive into the details of this exotic dish and learn how to recreate it in your own kitchen.

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb of shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 2 cups of fresh pineapple chunks
  • 1 can of coconut milk
  • 1 red bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 yellow bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 onion, sliced
  • 3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons of red curry paste
  • 1 tablespoon of fish sauce
  • 1 tablespoon of brown sugar
  • 1 tablespoon of vegetable oil
  • Fresh basil leaves for garnish
  • Cooked jasmine rice for serving

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the Ingredients: Start by gathering and preparing all the ingredients. Peel and devein the shrimp, chop the vegetables, and cut the pineapple into bite-sized chunks. Having everything ready will make the cooking process smooth and enjoyable.

  2. Sear the Shrimp: Heat the vegetable oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat. Add the shrimp and cook for 2-3 minutes on each side until they turn pink and opaque. Once cooked, transfer the shrimp to a plate and set them aside.

  3. Saute the Aromatics: In the same skillet, add the sliced onion and bell peppers. Cook for a few minutes until they begin to soften. Then, add the minced garlic and stir for another minute until fragrant.

  4. Add the Red Curry Paste: Push the sauteed vegetables to one side of the skillet and add the red curry paste to the empty space. Stir and cook the paste for about a minute to release its flavors.

  5. Incorporate the Coconut Milk: Pour the coconut milk into the skillet, stirring to combine it with the curry paste and vegetables. Bring the mixture to a gentle simmer.

  6. Introduce the Pineapple and Shrimp: Once the coconut milk is simmering, add the pineapple chunks and seared shrimp into the skillet. Stir gently to coat them with the curry sauce.

  7. Season and Simmer: Add the fish sauce and brown sugar, adjusting the quantities to suit your taste preferences. Allow the curry to simmer for 5-7 minutes, letting all the flavors meld together beautifully.

  8. Serve and Enjoy: Once the curry reaches your desired consistency, remove it from the heat. Serve the Thai Pineapple Curry with Shrimp over a bed of jasmine rice, garnished with fresh basil leaves.

Tips for Success:

  • For an extra kick of heat, consider adding a few slices of red chili or a sprinkle of chili flakes to the curry.
  • Feel free to customize the vegetables in this dish to include your favorites, such as snow peas, bamboo shoots, or carrots.
  • Ensure not to overcook the shrimp to maintain their tender texture in the curry.

Final Thoughts:

Thai Pineapple Curry with Shrimp is a delightful fusion of flavors that will transport your taste buds to the vibrant streets of Thailand. The combination of succulent shrimp, juicy pineapple, and fragrant Thai spices creates a dish that is both comforting and exciting. Whether you’re hosting a dinner party or simply craving a special meal at home, this recipe is sure to impress and satisfy. Embrace the sweet and savory magic of Thai cuisine with this remarkable dish.

So, roll up your sleeves, gather your ingredients, and embark on a culinary adventure that will tantalize your senses and leave you craving for more. Cheers to creating unforgettable moments around the dinner table with this Thai Pineapple Curry with Shrimp recipe!
