Wholesome Low Carb Lemon Garlic Butter Salmon


Are you looking for a delicious and nutritious low-carb meal that’s bursting with flavor? Look no further than this Wholesome Low Carb Lemon Garlic Butter Salmon recipe! Not only is this dish easy to prepare, but it’s also packed with healthy fats, protein, and a zesty kick of lemon and garlic. Let’s dive into the details of this delectable dish.

Ingredients

  • 4 salmon fillets
  • 4 tablespoons unsalted butter
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon lemon zest
  • 2 tablespoons fresh lemon juice
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley for garnish

Instructions

  1. Preheat the Oven: Begin by preheating your oven to 400°F (200°C). While the oven is heating up, line a baking sheet with parchment paper.

  2. Prepare the Salmon: Rinse the salmon fillets under cold water and pat them dry with paper towels. Place the fillets on the prepared baking sheet and season them with salt and black pepper.

  3. Make the Lemon Garlic Butter: In a small saucepan, melt the unsalted butter over medium heat. Once the butter has melted, add the minced garlic, lemon zest, and fresh lemon juice. Stir the mixture gently and let it simmer for 1-2 minutes until the garlic becomes fragrant.

  4. Coat the Salmon: Pour the lemon garlic butter mixture over the seasoned salmon fillets, ensuring that each fillet is generously coated with the flavorful buttery sauce.

  5. Bake the Salmon: Place the baking sheet in the preheated oven and bake the salmon for 12-15 minutes, or until it easily flakes with a fork. Keep an eye on the salmon to avoid overcooking, as it can become dry.

  6. Garnish and Serve: Once the salmon is cooked to perfection, remove it from the oven. Garnish the fillets with fresh parsley for a pop of color and added freshness. Serve the Wholesome Low Carb Lemon Garlic Butter Salmon alongside your favorite low-carb side dishes or a crisp green salad.

Nutritional Benefits

This Wholesome Low Carb Lemon Garlic Butter Salmon not only tantalizes the taste buds but also offers a range of nutritional benefits. Salmon is rich in omega-3 fatty acids, which are essential for heart health and overall well-being. The addition of garlic provides immune-boosting properties, while lemon adds a burst of vitamin C. This dish is also low in carbohydrates, making it a perfect choice for those following a low-carb or keto lifestyle.

Conclusion

In conclusion, this Wholesome Low Carb Lemon Garlic Butter Salmon is a must-try for anyone seeking a healthy, flavorful, and satisfying meal. Whether you’re cooking for a special occasion or a simple weeknight dinner, this recipe is sure to impress. With its blend of wholesome ingredients and vibrant flavors, it’s a true winner in the world of low-carb cooking.

So, gather your ingredients, preheat your oven, and get ready to indulge in a dish that’s as nutritious as it is delicious! Enjoy every flavorful bite of this Wholesome Low Carb Lemon Garlic Butter Salmon, and savor the satisfaction of nourishing your body with each mouthwatering morsel. Cheers to good health and great taste!
