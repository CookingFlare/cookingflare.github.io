Grilled Stuffed Mushrooms: Tasty and Elegant BBQ Appetizers


When it comes to hosting a barbecue, having a selection of delectable appetizers is a must. Grilled stuffed mushrooms are an elegant and flavorful choice that will impress your guests and leave them craving for more. The smoky aroma and rich, savory taste of these grilled treats make them a perfect addition to any outdoor gathering. Whether you’re a seasoned grill master or just starting out, this recipe is sure to elevate your BBQ game.

Ingredients:

  • 24 large white mushrooms
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/2 cup bread crumbs
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1/4 cup balsamic glaze (optional, for drizzling)

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the Grill: Preheat your grill to medium heat, around 350-400°F (175-200°C).

  2. Clean the Mushrooms: Gently brush the mushrooms to remove any dirt. Carefully remove the stems and set aside.

  3. Prepare the Filling: In a bowl, combine the olive oil, minced garlic, bread crumbs, Parmesan cheese, and chopped parsley. Mix well to form a cohesive filling. Season with salt and pepper to taste.

  4. Stuff the Mushrooms: Using a spoon, generously fill each mushroom cap with the prepared filling, pressing down gently to ensure it sticks together.

  5. Grill the Mushrooms: Place the stuffed mushrooms on the preheated grill, filling side up. Close the grill and cook for 8-10 minutes, or until the mushrooms are tender and the filling is golden brown.

  6. Serve: Carefully transfer the grilled stuffed mushrooms to a serving platter. If desired, drizzle with balsamic glaze for an extra burst of flavor.

  7. Enjoy: Serve the grilled stuffed mushrooms hot and watch as they disappear within minutes, leaving your guests raving about the delicious appetizer.

Tips and Variations:

  • For a smokier flavor, consider using wood chips on your grill.
  • Experiment with different cheese options such as Gouda, blue cheese, or feta for unique flavor profiles.
  • Add a dash of red pepper flakes to the filling for a hint of heat.
  • If you prefer a vegetarian option, omit the Parmesan cheese or substitute with a plant-based alternative.

Grilled stuffed mushrooms are a versatile appetizer that can be customized to suit different flavor preferences. Whether you’re aiming for a gourmet twist or a simple, crowd-pleasing option, this recipe is a fantastic starting point for your culinary creativity. The combination of earthy mushrooms, savory filling, and the hint of smokiness from the grill creates a truly delightful appetizer that will elevate any barbecue gathering.

So, the next time you fire up the grill, consider adding these elegant and tasty grilled stuffed mushrooms to the menu. Your guests will thank you, and you’ll establish yourself as a BBQ aficionado with a knack for creating unforgettable appetizers. Cheers to good food, great company, and the joy of outdoor cooking!
