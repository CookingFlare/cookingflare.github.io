The Classic Manhattan Ice Cream: A Timeless Whiskey Delight


Indulge in the sophisticated flavors of The Classic Manhattan Ice Cream, a delightful frozen treat that combines the elegance of a classic cocktail with the creamy goodness of ice cream. This recipe brings together the rich flavors of whiskey, sweet vermouth, and bitters, resulting in a dessert that is both luxurious and nostalgic.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups heavy cream
  • 1 cup whole milk
  • 3/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/4 cup whiskey
  • 1/4 cup sweet vermouth
  • 1 tablespoon bitters
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • Maraschino cherries for garnish (optional)

Instructions

  1. In a medium saucepan, combine the heavy cream, whole milk, and granulated sugar. Place the saucepan over medium heat and stir until the sugar has dissolved completely. Do not let the mixture come to a boil.

  2. Remove the saucepan from the heat and let it cool for a few minutes. Add the whiskey, sweet vermouth, bitters, and salt to the saucepan. Stir well to combine all the ingredients.

  3. Transfer the mixture to a mixing bowl and cover it with plastic wrap. Place the bowl in the refrigerator and let the mixture chill for at least 4 hours, or overnight if possible. This step is crucial for the flavors to develop and meld together.

  4. Once the mixture has chilled, remove it from the refrigerator and give it a good stir. Pour the mixture into an ice cream maker and churn according to the manufacturer’s instructions until it reaches a soft-serve consistency.

  5. Transfer the churned ice cream to an airtight container and freeze it for at least 4 hours, or until it firms up to the desired consistency.

  6. When you’re ready to serve, scoop the Classic Manhattan Ice Cream into bowls or cones. For an extra touch of elegance, garnish each serving with a maraschino cherry.

Enjoy this timeless whiskey delight and savor the harmonious blend of flavors that will transport you to the golden age of classic cocktails. Whether you’re hosting a dinner party or simply treating yourself to a special dessert, The Classic Manhattan Ice Cream is sure to impress.

Tips and Variations

  • If you prefer a stronger whiskey flavor, you can increase the amount of whiskey to suit your taste.
  • For a non-alcoholic version, you can substitute the whiskey and sweet vermouth with 1/4 cup of cherry juice and 1/4 cup of orange juice.
  • Experiment with different types of bitters to add a unique twist to the ice cream. Orange bitters or chocolate bitters can complement the flavors beautifully.
  • If you don’t have an ice cream maker, you can still make this recipe by pouring the mixture into a shallow dish and freezing it. Every 30 minutes, remove the dish from the freezer and stir the mixture vigorously with a fork to prevent ice crystals from forming. Repeat this process until the ice cream reaches the desired consistency.

So, embrace the timeless charm of The Classic Manhattan Ice Cream and delight your taste buds with this sophisticated whiskey-infused treat. It’s a dessert that embodies the spirit of an iconic cocktail and is sure to become a favorite among ice cream enthusiasts. Enjoy responsibly and savor every creamy, boozy spoonful!
