Mind-Blowing Flavor Combos: 10 Unconventional Juice Smoothie Recipes


Are you tired of the same old banana, berry, or green juice smoothies? It’s time to step out of your comfort zone and explore some mind-blowing flavor combinations! These unconventional juice smoothie recipes will tantalize your tastebuds and leave you wanting more. So, grab your Vitamix, and let’s get blending!

  1. Cacao, Avocado, and Matcha Smoothie

Start your day with an energizing blend of matcha green tea, antioxidant-rich cacao, and creamy avocado.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup unsweetened almond milk
  • 1/2 avocado
  • 1 tsp matcha green tea powder
  • 1 tbsp cocoa powder
  • 1 tbsp honey

Instructions:

  1. Blend all ingredients together in a blender until smooth.

  2. Pour into a glass and enjoy immediately.

  3. Peanut Butter Banana, Kale, and Chocolate Chip Oatmeal Smoothie

An ideal breakfast to satisfy your sweet tooth while being chock-full of nutrients!

Ingredients:

  • 1 ripe banana
  • 1/2 cup rolled oats
  • 1 handful of kale leaves
  • 1 tbsp peanut butter
  • 1 tbsp dark chocolate chips
  • 1/2 cup unsweetened almond milk
  • 1/2 cup water

Instructions:

  1. Begin by blending the kale and almond milk together until fully incorporated.

  2. Add the oats and continue blending until smooth.

  3. Add the banana, peanut butter, and chocolate chips, and blend again until smooth.

  4. Taste and adjust sweetness by adding more peanut butter or honey, if needed.

  5. Pour into a glass and enjoy!

  6. Carrot, Ginger, and Turmeric Smoothie

This smoothie boasts a vibrant orange color, and it offers a burst of beta-carotene, anti-inflammatory properties, and a delicious kick of ginger.

Ingredients:

  • 1 large carrot
  • 1-inch piece of ginger
  • 1/2-inch piece of turmeric root
  • 1/2 cup unsweetened coconut water
  • 1 cup ice

Instructions:

  1. Cut the carrot and turmeric into chunks, then peel the ginger and cut into smaller pieces.

  2. Place all ingredients in a blender and blend until smooth.

  3. Adjust sweetness, if desired, by adding a pitted date or two.

  4. Pineapple, Basil, and Coconut Milk Smoothie

This tropical combination, featuring pineapple and basil, provides a refreshing and zesty twist.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup fresh pineapple chunks
  • A handful of fresh basil leaves
  • 1 cup coconut milk
  • 1/2 cup ice

Instructions:

  1. Add the pineapple, basil, and coconut milk to a blender.

  2. Blend until smooth, then add the ice.

  3. Blend until thick and smooth.

  4. Corn, Jalapeño, and Basil Smoothie

Give your tastebuds a kick with this unique, savory smoothie.

Ingredients:

  • 1 ear of fresh corn
  • 1 small jalapeño
  • A handful of fresh basil leaves
  • 1/2 cup unsweetened almond milk
  • 1/2 cup ice

Instructions:

  1. Cut the corn off the cob, and remove the seeds from the jalapeño (for less heat).

  2. Add the corn, jalapeño, basil, and almond milk to a blender.

  3. Blend until smooth, then add ice and blend again.

  4. Taste and adjust based on your spice preference by adding more jalapeño or a sprinkle of salt.

  5. Vanilla, Parsnip, and Maple Smoothie

This unexpected combination of parsnip, vanilla, and maple syrup offers a sweet and creamy smoothie.

Ingredients:

  • 1 large parsnip
  • 1/2 vanilla bean or 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 tbsp maple syrup
  • 1 1/2 cups unsweetened almond milk
  • 1/2 cup ice

Instructions:

  1. Cut the parsnip into chunks.

  2. Scrape the seeds from the vanilla bean into a blender, if using. If using extract, add it later.

  3. Add the parsnip, maple syrup, and almond milk to the blender.

  4. Blend until smooth, then add ice and blend again.

  5. Butternut Squash, Cardamom, and Almond Smoothie

Take advantage of autumn produce with this warm-spiced, creamy smoothie.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup cooked butternut squash (roasted or steamed and skin removed)
  • 1 tsp cardamom
  • 1 tbsp almond butter
  • 1/2 cup unsweetened almond milk
  • 1/2 cup ice

Instructions:

  1. Add the butternut squash, cardamom, almond butter, and almond milk to a blender.

  2. Blend until smooth, then add ice and blend again.

  3. Beet, Orange, and Rosemary Smoothie

Experience a earthy, herby smoothie when you blend beets with orange juice and fresh rosemary.

Ingredients:

  • 1 medium beet (cooked and peeled)
  • 1 orange, juiced
  • A few springs of rosemary
  • 1 cup ice

Instructions:

  1. Add the cooked beet, orange juice, and rosemary to a blender.

  2. Blend until smooth, then add ice and blend again.

  3. Blue Cheese, Pear, and Walnut Smoothie

Oh yes, you read that right! This savory smoothie boasts a surprising and delectable flavor combo.

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 Bosc pear
  • 1 oz crumbled blue cheese
  • 1/4 cup walnuts
  • 1/2 cup unsweetened almond milk
  • 1/2 cup ice

Instructions:

  1. Core and chop the pear into chunks.

  2. Add the pear, blue cheese, walnuts, and almond milk to a blender.

  3. Blend until smooth, then add ice and blend again.

  4. Spinach, Tomas & Cinnamon Smoothie

Find a delightful fusion of sweet and savory in this spinach, tomato, and cinnamon dream.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup fresh spinach
  • 1/2 cup cherry tomatoes
  • 1 tsp cinnamon
  • 1/2 cup coconut milk
  • 1/2 cup ice

Instructions:

  1. Add the spinach, cherry tomatoes, cinnamon, and coconut milk to a blender.
  2. Blend until smooth, then add ice and blend again.

Delight in these unconventional juice smoothie recipes to tantalize your taste buds with tantalizing flavor combinations. The best part – each packed with nutrients to nourish your mind and body! Let your culinary creativity flow with these unique juices that are anything but ordinary.
