Greek Chicken Flatbread: A Mediterranean Delight


If you’re looking for a delicious and healthy meal that brings the flavors of the Mediterranean to your table, look no further than Greek Chicken Flatbread. This dish combines tender grilled chicken, tangy feta cheese, and a medley of Mediterranean vegetables, all layered on top of a crispy and flavorful flatbread. It’s a perfect option for a quick weeknight dinner or a crowd-pleasing appetizer at your next gathering.

Ingredients

  • 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 4 whole wheat flatbreads
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1 cucumber, sliced
  • 1 red onion, thinly sliced
  • 1/2 cup Kalamata olives, pitted and chopped
  • 1/2 cup crumbled feta cheese
  • 1/4 cup fresh parsley, chopped
  • 1/4 cup fresh lemon juice
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • Salt and black pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Preheat your grill or stovetop grill pan to medium-high heat.

  2. Season the chicken breasts with salt, black pepper, and dried oregano. Drizzle with olive oil and rub the seasonings into the meat.

  3. Grill the chicken breasts for about 6-8 minutes per side, or until they reach an internal temperature of 165°F (74°C). Remove from the grill and let them rest for a few minutes before slicing.

  4. While the chicken is resting, prepare the flatbreads. Place them on a baking sheet and heat in the oven at 350°F (175°C) for 5 minutes, or until they become slightly crisp.

  5. In a small bowl, whisk together the lemon juice, olive oil, minced garlic, and a pinch of salt and black pepper.

  6. Once the flatbreads are ready, remove them from the oven and set aside.

  7. Slice the grilled chicken breasts into thin strips.

  8. To assemble, spread a thin layer of the lemon dressing onto each flatbread.

  9. Top with sliced chicken, cherry tomatoes, cucumber, red onion, Kalamata olives, crumbled feta cheese, and fresh parsley.

  10. Drizzle a little more of the lemon dressing over the toppings.

  11. Serve the Greek Chicken Flatbread immediately and enjoy!

Tips and Variations

  • Feel free to add other Mediterranean ingredients to your flatbread, such as roasted red peppers, artichoke hearts, or tzatziki sauce.

  • If you prefer a vegetarian version, you can omit the chicken and add extra vegetables like roasted eggplant or zucchini.

  • For an extra kick of flavor, sprinkle some crushed red pepper flakes or drizzle some balsamic glaze over the finished flatbread.

  • Leftovers can be stored in an airtight container in the refrigerator for up to 2 days. To enjoy them again, simply reheat in the oven or microwave until warmed through.

So there you have it, a mouthwatering Greek Chicken Flatbread recipe that will transport you to the sunny shores of the Mediterranean. With its fresh and vibrant flavors, this dish is sure to become a favorite in your household. Whether you’re cooking for yourself or entertaining guests, this Mediterranean delight is a guaranteed crowd-pleaser. Enjoy!
