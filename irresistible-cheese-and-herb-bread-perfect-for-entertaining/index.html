Irresistible Cheese and Herb Bread: Perfect for Entertaining


Are you tired of serving the same old bread at your gatherings and parties? Do you want to impress your guests with a delicious and aromatic bread that will complement your dishes perfectly? Look no further! Today, I’ll share with you a recipe for irresistible cheese and herb bread that’s sure to be a hit at your next event.

The Perfect Combination

The beauty of this bread lies in its simplicity. By combining the richness of cheese with the freshness of herbs, you’ll create a flavor profile that’s both savory and aromatic. The cheese adds a creamy texture, while the herbs provide a bright and refreshing note. It’s the perfect combination for a delicious and satisfying bread that’s sure to please even the pickiest of eaters.

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups warm water
  • 2 teaspoons active dry yeast
  • 3 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup grated cheddar cheese
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh chives
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 egg, beaten (for egg wash)

Instructions

  1. In a large mixing bowl, combine the warm water and yeast. Let it sit for 5-7 minutes until the yeast is activated and foamy.
  2. Add the olive oil, salt, and 2 cups of flour to the bowl. Mix until a shaggy dough forms.
  3. Gradually add the remaining 2 cups of flour, one cup at a time, until the dough becomes smooth and elastic. You may not need all of the remaining flour, so add it gradually and be prepared to stop adding flour when the dough feels right.
  4. Turn the dough out onto a floured surface and knead for 10-15 minutes until the dough is smooth and elastic. You can also use a stand mixer with a dough hook attachment to knead the dough.
  5. Place the dough in a greased bowl, cover it with plastic wrap, and let it rise in a warm place for 1-2 hours until it has doubled in size.
  6. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C). Line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
  7. Punch down the dough and roll it out into a rectangle that’s about 1/4-inch thick.
  8. Sprinkle the grated cheddar cheese, chopped parsley, and chopped chives evenly over the dough, leaving a 1-inch border around the edges.
  9. Fold the 1-inch border over the cheese and herbs to create a crust. Brush the crust with the beaten egg for a golden glaze.
  10. Place the dough on the prepared baking sheet and bake for 25-30 minutes until the bread is golden brown and sounds hollow when tapped on the bottom.
  11. Remove the bread from the oven and let it cool on a wire rack for 10-15 minutes before slicing and serving.

Tips and Variations

  • To add an extra layer of flavor, sprinkle some dried herbs like thyme or oregano over the cheese and herbs before baking.
  • If you prefer a stronger cheese flavor, you can use more cheddar or add some grated Parmesan or Romano cheese to the dough.
  • To make the bread more visually appealing, you can create a decorative edge by folding the crust over the cheese and herbs in a decorative pattern.
  • This bread is perfect for serving with soups, salads, or as a side dish for your favorite entrees. You can also slice it and serve it with your favorite spreads or dips.

Conclusion

This irresistible cheese and herb bread is a game-changer for any gathering or party. Its rich and savory flavor profile is sure to impress your guests, and its fresh and aromatic aroma will leave them wanting more. Whether you’re serving it as a side dish or using it to make sandwiches, this bread is sure to be a hit. So go ahead, give it a try, and get ready to impress your friends and family with your delicious homemade bread!
