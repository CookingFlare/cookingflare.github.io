Scrumptious Scone Recipes to Pair with Your Morning Tea


Are you a tea lover? Do you enjoy a warm, comforting cup of tea in the morning? If so, then you’re in for a treat! We have gathered a collection of delightful scone recipes that are perfect for pairing with your morning tea. These scrumptious scones will not only satisfy your taste buds but also add a touch of elegance to your tea-time ritual.

Classic British Scone

Let’s start with the classic British scone, a timeless favorite that never fails to impress. This buttery, flaky pastry is a perfect companion to a steaming cup of tea. Here’s how you can make it:

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 tablespoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup cold unsalted butter, cubed
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 425°F (220°C) and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
  2. In a large mixing bowl, whisk together the flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt.
  3. Add the cold butter to the dry ingredients and use a pastry cutter or your fingertips to cut it into the flour mixture until it resembles coarse crumbs.
  4. Make a well in the center of the mixture and pour in the milk and vanilla extract. Stir until just combined.
  5. Turn the dough out onto a lightly floured surface and gently knead it a few times until it comes together.
  6. Roll the dough into a 1-inch thick circle and use a round cookie cutter to cut out scones.
  7. Place the scones onto the prepared baking sheet and bake for 12-15 minutes, or until they are golden brown.
  8. Remove from the oven and allow them to cool on a wire rack before serving.

Lemon Blueberry Scones

If you’re looking for a refreshing twist on the classic scone, try these delightful lemon blueberry scones. The zesty flavor of lemon combined with the burst of juicy blueberries creates a perfect harmony. Here’s how you can make them:

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 tablespoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • Zest of 1 lemon
  • 1/2 cup cold unsalted butter, cubed
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 cup fresh blueberries

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 425°F (220°C) and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
  2. In a large mixing bowl, whisk together the flour, sugar, baking powder, salt, and lemon zest.
  3. Add the cold butter to the dry ingredients and use a pastry cutter or your fingertips to cut it into the flour mixture until it resembles coarse crumbs.
  4. Make a well in the center of the mixture and pour in the milk and vanilla extract. Stir until just combined.
  5. Gently fold in the blueberries, being careful not to crush them.
  6. Turn the dough out onto a lightly floured surface and gently knead it a few times until it comes together.
  7. Roll the dough into a 1-inch thick circle and use a round cookie cutter to cut out scones.
  8. Place the scones onto the prepared baking sheet and bake for 12-15 minutes, or until they are golden brown.
  9. Remove from the oven and allow them to cool on a wire rack before serving.

Conclusion

Scones are the perfect accompaniment to your morning tea, and these scrumptious recipes will surely elevate your tea-time experience. Whether you prefer the classic British scone, the refreshing lemon blueberry scone, or the indulgent chocolate chip scone, each bite will transport you to a world of flavor and comfort. So go ahead, try these recipes, and savor the magic of a perfectly paired morning tea. Cheers!
