Time-Saving Instant Pot Breakfast Casserole Recipes


Are you a breakfast lover but often find yourself short on time in the mornings? The Instant Pot is here to save the day! With its convenience and speed, the Instant Pot is the perfect tool for whipping up delicious and time-saving breakfast casseroles. Whether you’re hosting brunch or simply want to meal prep for the week, these Instant Pot breakfast casserole recipes are sure to become your new favorites. Let’s dive into the world of flavorful, hearty, and hassle-free morning meals.

1. Classic Bacon and Egg Breakfast Casserole

Ingredients:

  • 6 slices of bacon, chopped
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 1 red bell pepper, diced
  • 1 cup baby spinach
  • 8 eggs
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Set the Instant Pot to sauté mode and cook the bacon until crispy. Remove the bacon and set it aside.
  2. In the remaining bacon fat, sauté the onion and bell pepper until softened. Add the baby spinach and cook until wilted.
  3. In a bowl, whisk together the eggs, milk, salt, and pepper.
  4. Grease the Instant Pot insert and add the sautéed vegetables. Pour the egg mixture over the vegetables and sprinkle the cooked bacon and cheddar cheese on top.
  5. Cover the Instant Pot and set it to manual high pressure for 20 minutes.
  6. Once done, quick-release the pressure and carefully remove the casserole from the Instant Pot.

2. Veggie and Cheese Breakfast Casserole

Ingredients:

  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 cup sliced mushrooms
  • 1 cup diced zucchini
  • 1 cup diced red potatoes
  • 1 cup diced bell peppers
  • 1 cup grated mozzarella cheese
  • 8 eggs
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Set the Instant Pot to sauté mode and heat the olive oil. Sauté the mushrooms, zucchini, red potatoes, and bell peppers until slightly softened.
  2. In a bowl, whisk together the eggs, milk, salt, and pepper.
  3. Grease the Instant Pot insert and add the sautéed vegetables. Pour the egg mixture over the vegetables and sprinkle the mozzarella cheese on top.
  4. Cover the Instant Pot and set it to manual high pressure for 25 minutes.
  5. Once done, quick-release the pressure and let the casserole sit for a few minutes before slicing and serving.

3. Ham and Potato Breakfast Casserole

Ingredients:

  • 1 tablespoon butter
  • 1 cup diced ham
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 2 cups frozen hash browns
  • 1 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • 8 eggs
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Set the Instant Pot to sauté mode and melt the butter. Sauté the diced ham and onion until the onion is translucent.
  2. Add the frozen hash browns to the Instant Pot and stir to combine with the ham and onion.
  3. In a bowl, whisk together the eggs, milk, salt, and pepper.
  4. Grease the Instant Pot insert and add the ham, onion, and hash brown mixture. Pour the egg mixture over the ingredients and sprinkle the cheddar cheese on top.
  5. Cover the Instant Pot and set it to manual high pressure for 22 minutes.
  6. Once done, quick-release the pressure and let the casserole cool slightly before serving.

Final Thoughts

With these time-saving Instant Pot breakfast casserole recipes, you can say goodbye to the morning rush and hello to a satisfying and stress-free breakfast. The versatility of the Instant Pot allows you to customize these recipes with your favorite ingredients, making them perfect for any palate. Whether you’re cooking for a crowd or simply planning ahead for busy mornings, these breakfast casseroles will become your go-to dishes for delicious and efficient breakfasts. So, grab your Instant Pot and get ready to revolutionize your breakfast game!
