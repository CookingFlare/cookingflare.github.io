Elevate Your Mezze Platter with Homemade Middle Eastern Labneh


If you’re a fan of Middle Eastern cuisine, you’ve likely encountered the delightful flavors and textures of a mezze platter. A mezze platter is a collection of small dishes that are perfect for sharing, and it often includes a variety of dips, spreads, and finger foods. One of the stars of a mezze platter is undoubtedly labneh, a creamy, tangy, and rich yogurt cheese that adds a luxurious touch to any spread. While store-bought labneh is readily available, there’s something truly special about making your own homemade Middle Eastern labneh. Not only is it surprisingly simple to prepare, but the flavor and texture of homemade labneh far surpasses anything you can find in a store. In this blog post, I’ll guide you through the process of making your very own labneh at home, and then I’ll share some creative ways to incorporate it into a show-stopping mezze platter.

Making Labneh at Home

Ingredients:

  • 32 oz (about 1 kg) full-fat Greek yogurt
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • Cheesecloth
  • Kitchen twine

Instructions:

  1. In a large mixing bowl, combine the Greek yogurt with the salt. The salt not only enhances the flavor but also helps to draw out excess moisture from the yogurt.

  2. Lay a piece of cheesecloth over a large bowl, ensuring that there is enough overhang to fully cover the yogurt mixture.

  3. Transfer the yogurt mixture onto the center of the cheesecloth. Gather the corners of the cheesecloth and twist them together, forming a pouch around the yogurt.

  4. Tie the pouch with kitchen twine, creating a secure bundle.

  5. Suspend the yogurt pouch over the bowl, allowing the excess liquid to drain. This can be achieved by tying the twine to a wooden spoon placed across the opening of the bowl, or by using a fine-mesh strainer to support the pouch.

  6. Place the bowl in the refrigerator and let the yogurt drain for 24 to 48 hours, depending on the desired thickness of the labneh. The longer it drains, the thicker the labneh will become.

  7. Once the labneh has reached your desired consistency, transfer it to a clean container and refrigerate until ready to use.

Serving Labneh in a Mezze Platter

Now that you have your velvety, homemade labneh ready, it’s time to put together a mezze platter that will impress your guests and tantalize your taste buds.

Mezze Platter Ideas:

  1. Classic Pairings: Serve the labneh alongside traditional mezze platter items such as hummus, baba ganoush, olives, and pita bread. The creamy, tangy flavor of the labneh complements the earthy richness of the baba ganoush and the nuttiness of the hummus.

  2. Fresh Vegetables: Include an array of fresh, crunchy vegetables such as cucumber slices, cherry tomatoes, and bell pepper strips. These vibrant vegetables provide a refreshing contrast to the richness of the labneh.

  3. Herbaceous Touch: Sprinkle the labneh with a drizzle of high-quality olive oil and a generous handful of fresh herbs such as mint, parsley, or dill. The aromatic herbs add a pop of color and a burst of freshness to the creamy labneh.

  4. Savory Additions: Elevate your platter with additional savory elements like marinated feta cheese, stuffed grape leaves, and falafel. These savory bites play off the creamy texture of the labneh, creating a harmonious balance of flavors and textures.

  5. Sweet and Spicy: For a unique twist, consider serving the labneh with a drizzle of honey and a sprinkle of za’atar spice blend. The sweet, floral notes of the honey and the warm, aromatic flavors of the za’atar create a tantalizing combination with the creamy labneh.

By incorporating your homemade Middle Eastern labneh into a thoughtfully curated mezze platter, you’ll be treating your guests to a culinary experience that is both sophisticated and utterly delicious.

Whether you’re hosting a gathering or simply indulging in a solo feast, the addition of homemade labneh will undoubtedly elevate your mezze platter to a whole new level of gastronomic delight. So, roll up your sleeves, gather your favorite mezze ingredients, and let the journey to mezze perfection begin!

Enjoy the creamy, tangy bliss of homemade labneh, and savor the delightful moments shared over a beautifully adorned mezze platter. Cheers to the art of savoring good food and good company!
