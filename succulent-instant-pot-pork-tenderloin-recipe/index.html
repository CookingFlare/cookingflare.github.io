Succulent Instant Pot Pork Tenderloin Recipe


Are you in the mood for a mouth-watering and succulent pork dish? Look no further! This Instant Pot Pork Tenderloin recipe is guaranteed to satisfy your cravings. With the magic of the Instant Pot, you can have tender and juicy pork in no time. So, let’s dive right into the recipe!

Ingredients

  • 1.5 pounds of pork tenderloin
  • 1 tablespoon of olive oil
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon of dried rosemary
  • 1 teaspoon of dried thyme
  • 1 teaspoon of paprika
  • 1 teaspoon of salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon of black pepper
  • 1 cup of chicken broth

Instructions

  1. Start by selecting the “Saute” function on your Instant Pot. Add the olive oil and allow it to heat up.

  2. While the oil is heating, season the pork tenderloin with garlic, rosemary, thyme, paprika, salt, and black pepper. Rub the seasonings all over the pork, ensuring it is well coated.

  3. Once the oil is hot, carefully place the seasoned pork tenderloin into the Instant Pot. Sear the pork on all sides until it develops a golden-brown crust. This step will help seal in the flavors and keep the pork moist.

  4. Once the pork is nicely seared, remove it from the Instant Pot and set it aside.

  5. Pour the chicken broth into the Instant Pot, scraping the bottom to release any browned bits. This will add an extra depth of flavor to the dish.

  6. Return the pork tenderloin to the Instant Pot, placing it on top of the chicken broth. Secure the lid and set the Instant Pot to “Manual” or “Pressure Cook” mode, depending on your model. Cook on high pressure for 8 minutes.

  7. Once the cooking time is complete, allow the pressure to naturally release for 10 minutes before carefully performing a quick release to release any remaining pressure.

  8. Carefully remove the pork tenderloin from the Instant Pot and let it rest for a few minutes before slicing. This will help the juices redistribute throughout the meat, ensuring a tender and juicy result.

  9. Slice the pork tenderloin into desired thickness and serve it alongside your favorite sides. Whether it’s roasted vegetables, mashed potatoes, or a fresh salad, this succulent pork tenderloin will be the star of the show.

Tips and Variations

  • For an extra burst of flavor, you can marinate the pork tenderloin in the seasoning mixture for a few hours or overnight before cooking. This will infuse the meat with even more deliciousness.
  • Feel free to customize the seasoning blend to your liking. Add a pinch of chili powder for some heat or a squeeze of lemon juice for a citrusy twist.
  • If you prefer a thicker sauce, you can mix 1 tablespoon of cornstarch with 2 tablespoons of water and whisk it into the cooked broth. Allow it to simmer on the “Saute” function until thickened.
  • Leftovers? No problem! This Instant Pot Pork Tenderloin is perfect for meal prep. Slice the leftovers and store them in an airtight container in the refrigerator for up to 3 days.

Now that you have the recipe for this succulent Instant Pot Pork Tenderloin, it’s time to gather your ingredients and get cooking. The Instant Pot will do all the hard work for you, ensuring a tender and flavorful result. Enjoy the juicy perfection of this dish, and don’t forget to share it with your family and friends. Bon appétit!
