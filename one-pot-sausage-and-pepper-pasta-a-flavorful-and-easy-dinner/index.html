One-Pot Sausage and Pepper Pasta: A Flavorful and Easy Dinner


Are you looking for a delicious and hassle-free dinner option that will satisfy the whole family? Look no further than this one-pot sausage and pepper pasta recipe! This dish is not only bursting with flavor but also incredibly easy to prepare, making it a perfect choice for busy weeknights when you want a hearty meal without spending hours in the kitchen.

Ingredients

Before we dive into the cooking process, let’s take a look at the ingredients you’ll need to whip up this delightful dish:

  • 1 pound of Italian sausage, casings removed
  • 1 onion, thinly sliced
  • 1 red bell pepper, thinly sliced
  • 1 yellow bell pepper, thinly sliced
  • 4 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon of dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon of dried basil
  • 1/2 teaspoon of red pepper flakes (adjust to your spice preference)
  • 1 can (14.5 ounces) of diced tomatoes
  • 3 cups of chicken broth
  • 1/2 cup of heavy cream
  • 12 ounces of penne pasta
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Freshly grated Parmesan cheese for serving

Instructions

Now, let’s get cooking! Follow these simple steps to create a mouthwatering one-pot sausage and pepper pasta:

  1. Brown the Sausage: In a large pot or Dutch oven, cook the Italian sausage over medium heat, breaking it into crumbles as it browns. Once the sausage is fully cooked, transfer it to a plate and set it aside.

  2. Sauté the Vegetables: In the same pot, add the sliced onion, red bell pepper, and yellow bell pepper. Sauté the vegetables until they are tender and slightly caramelized, which should take about 5-7 minutes. Stir in the minced garlic, dried oregano, dried basil, and red pepper flakes, and cook for an additional minute to allow the flavors to meld.

  3. Combine the Remaining Ingredients: Add the diced tomatoes (with their juices), chicken broth, heavy cream, and the browned Italian sausage back into the pot. Stir everything together, then add the uncooked penne pasta. Bring the mixture to a gentle boil, then reduce the heat to a simmer and cover the pot. Cook for about 15-20 minutes, stirring occasionally, until the pasta is al dente and the sauce has thickened.

  4. Serve and Enjoy: Once the pasta is cooked to perfection, season the dish with salt and pepper to taste. Ladle the sausage and pepper pasta into individual bowls, and garnish each serving with a generous sprinkle of freshly grated Parmesan cheese.

Why One-Pot Recipes Are a Game Changer

One-pot meals, like this sausage and pepper pasta, are a game changer for anyone who loves good food but doesn’t love spending hours in the kitchen. Not only do they minimize the number of dishes to wash (a win for everyone!), but they also allow the flavors of the ingredients to meld together beautifully, resulting in a dish that tastes like it’s been simmering for hours.

Final Thoughts

With its savory Italian sausage, vibrant bell peppers, and perfectly cooked penne pasta, this one-pot sausage and pepper pasta is sure to become a staple in your dinner rotation. Whether you’re cooking for a crowd or just whipping up a cozy meal for two, this recipe is versatile, flavorful, and oh-so satisfying. Give it a try, and get ready to fall in love with the simplicity and deliciousness of one-pot cooking!
