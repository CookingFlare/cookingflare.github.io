Comforting Soup Recipes for Cooking Newbies


Are you a cooking newbie looking to master the art of making comforting soups? Look no further! In this blog post, we will share some easy and delicious soup recipes that are perfect for beginners. These recipes require simple ingredients and minimal cooking skills, making them ideal for those who are just starting their culinary journey. So grab your apron and let’s get cooking!

1. Classic Chicken Noodle Soup

Preparation time: 15 minutes

Serves: 4

Ingredients:

  • 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 4 cups chicken broth
  • 1 medium onion, diced
  • 2 carrots, sliced
  • 2 celery stalks, sliced
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • 1 bay leaf
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 cups cooked egg noodles

Instructions:

  1. In a large pot, bring the chicken broth to a boil.
  2. Add the chicken breasts, onion, carrots, celery, garlic, thyme, bay leaf, salt, and pepper.
  3. Reduce the heat to low and let the soup simmer for about 20 minutes or until the chicken is cooked through.
  4. Remove the chicken breasts from the pot and shred them using two forks.
  5. Return the shredded chicken back to the pot and add the cooked egg noodles.
  6. Let the soup simmer for an additional 5 minutes.
  7. Serve hot and enjoy!

2. Creamy Tomato Basil Soup

Preparation time: 20 minutes

Serves: 4

Ingredients:

  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 medium onion, diced
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 2 cans (14 ounces each) diced tomatoes
  • 1 cup vegetable broth
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1/4 cup fresh basil leaves, chopped
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Heat olive oil in a large pot over medium heat.
  2. Add the onion and garlic, and sauté until soft and fragrant.
  3. Add the diced tomatoes and vegetable broth to the pot and bring to a boil.
  4. Reduce the heat and let the soup simmer for about 10 minutes.
  5. Using an immersion blender or regular blender, puree the soup until smooth.
  6. Return the soup to the pot and stir in the heavy cream and chopped basil leaves.
  7. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  8. Simmer the soup for an additional 5 minutes.
  9. Serve hot with a sprinkle of fresh basil on top.

3. Hearty Vegetable Minestrone

Preparation time: 30 minutes

Serves: 6

Ingredients:

  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 medium onion, diced
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 2 carrots, diced
  • 2 celery stalks, diced
  • 1 zucchini, diced
  • 1 can (14 ounces) diced tomatoes
  • 4 cups vegetable broth
  • 1 cup small pasta shells
  • 1 can (15 ounces) kidney beans, drained and rinsed
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Heat olive oil in a large pot over medium heat.
  2. Add the onion and garlic, and sauté until soft and fragrant.
  3. Add the carrots, celery, and zucchini to the pot and cook for about 5 minutes.
  4. Stir in the diced tomatoes, vegetable broth, pasta shells, kidney beans, basil, oregano, salt, and pepper.
  5. Bring the soup to a boil, then reduce the heat and let it simmer for about 15 minutes or until the vegetables are tender.
  6. Serve hot and enjoy a bowl of hearty vegetable minestrone.

Conclusion

These comforting soup recipes are perfect for cooking newbies who want to enjoy a warm and delicious meal. Whether you’re in the mood for a classic chicken noodle soup, a creamy tomato basil soup, or a hearty vegetable minestrone, these recipes are easy to follow and will surely satisfy your cravings. So why wait? Put on your apron, gather the ingredients, and start cooking your way to soup perfection. Happy cooking!
