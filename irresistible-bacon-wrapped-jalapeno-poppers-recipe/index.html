Irresistible Bacon-Wrapped Jalapeno Poppers Recipe


If you’re a fan of spicy and savory flavors, then this recipe is a must-try! Irresistible Bacon-Wrapped Jalapeno Poppers are a crowd-pleasing appetizer that combines the heat of jalapeno peppers with the smoky goodness of bacon. These poppers are crispy, cheesy, and utterly delicious, making them the perfect party snack or game day treat. So, let’s dive right into the recipe!

Ingredients

  • 12 fresh jalapeno peppers
  • 6 slices of bacon, cut in half
  • 8 ounces cream cheese, softened
  • 1 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon onion powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon smoked paprika
  • Salt and pepper, to taste

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C) and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.

  2. Start by preparing the jalapeno peppers. Slice each pepper in half lengthwise and remove the seeds and membranes. If you prefer a milder heat, you can also remove the veins of the peppers. Remember to wear gloves while handling the jalapenos to protect your hands from the heat.

  3. In a mixing bowl, combine the softened cream cheese, shredded cheddar cheese, garlic powder, onion powder, smoked paprika, salt, and pepper. Mix well until all the ingredients are evenly incorporated.

  4. Take each jalapeno half and fill it with the cream cheese mixture. Make sure to fill them generously, as the cheese will melt and shrink slightly during cooking.

  5. Wrap each stuffed jalapeno half with a half slice of bacon, securing it with a toothpick if necessary. Place the bacon-wrapped poppers on the prepared baking sheet, making sure they are evenly spaced.

  6. Bake the poppers in the preheated oven for about 20-25 minutes, or until the bacon is crispy and the cheese is melted and bubbly.

  7. Once cooked, remove the poppers from the oven and let them cool for a few minutes. Be cautious when handling them, as the cheese filling will be hot.

  8. Serve the Bacon-Wrapped Jalapeno Poppers warm and enjoy the explosion of flavors! You can serve them as is, or with your favorite dipping sauce, such as ranch dressing or spicy mayo.

Tips and Variations

  • If you prefer a spicier kick, you can leave some or all of the jalapeno veins intact.
  • For an extra flavor boost, you can add cooked and crumbled sausage or chopped cooked shrimp to the cream cheese mixture.
  • If you don’t have access to fresh jalapenos, you can also use pickled jalapenos for a tangier twist.
  • If you’re watching your calorie intake, you can use turkey bacon instead of regular bacon. It will still give the poppers a delicious smoky flavor.
  • These poppers can also be grilled. Simply preheat your grill to medium heat and cook them for about 10-15 minutes, turning them occasionally for even cooking.

Conclusion

Irresistible Bacon-Wrapped Jalapeno Poppers are a fantastic appetizer that combines the perfect balance of heat, smokiness, and cheesiness. Whether you’re hosting a party or simply looking for a flavorful snack, these poppers will surely satisfy your taste buds. So, gather your ingredients, follow the easy steps, and get ready to indulge in this irresistible treat. Enjoy!
