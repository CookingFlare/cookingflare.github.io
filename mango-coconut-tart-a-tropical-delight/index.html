Mango Coconut Tart: A Tropical Delight


If you’re a fan of tropical flavors and delightful desserts, then this Mango Coconut Tart recipe is a must-try for you. This luscious and refreshing tart combines the sweetness of ripe mangoes with the creamy richness of coconut, creating a harmonious blend of flavors that will transport your taste buds to a sunny, exotic paradise. Whether you’re hosting a summer gathering or simply craving a taste of the tropics, this Mango Coconut Tart is sure to impress and satisfy. Let’s dive into the recipe and bring a taste of the tropics to your kitchen.

Ingredients

For the Crust:

  • 1 1/2 cups of graham cracker crumbs
  • 1/3 cup of unsweetened shredded coconut
  • 1/4 cup of granulated sugar
  • 1/2 cup of unsalted butter, melted

For the Filling:

  • 2 large ripe mangoes, peeled and diced
  • 1/2 cup of coconut milk
  • 1/2 cup of sweetened condensed milk
  • 1/4 cup of fresh lime juice
  • 1 teaspoon of vanilla extract
  • 1 tablespoon of gelatin
  • 2 tablespoons of water

For the Topping:

  • Additional diced mangoes for garnish
  • Toasted coconut flakes

Instructions

Step 1: Prepare the Crust

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. In a bowl, combine the graham cracker crumbs, shredded coconut, and granulated sugar.
  3. Add the melted butter to the mixture and stir until well combined.
  4. Press the mixture into the bottom and up the sides of a tart pan.
  5. Bake the crust for 10-12 minutes, then set aside to cool.

Step 2: Make the Filling

  1. In a small bowl, sprinkle the gelatin over the water and let it bloom for 5 minutes.
  2. In a blender, combine the diced mangoes, coconut milk, sweetened condensed milk, lime juice, and vanilla extract.
  3. Blend the mixture until smooth.
  4. Transfer the mango mixture to a saucepan and warm it over low heat.
  5. Add the bloomed gelatin to the warm mango mixture and stir until the gelatin is fully dissolved.
  6. Pour the filling into the cooled crust and refrigerate for at least 4 hours to set.

Step 3: Garnish and Serve

  1. Once the tart is set, garnish the top with additional diced mangoes and toasted coconut flakes.
  2. Slice and serve the Mango Coconut Tart chilled, and enjoy the tropical bliss with every bite.

Tips and Variations

  • For a vegan version, you can use a plant-based butter substitute and swap the sweetened condensed milk with coconut cream and agave nectar.
  • Experiment with different crust options such as a coconut cookie crust for an extra coconutty flavor.
  • If fresh mangoes are not in season, you can use frozen mango chunks for the filling.

Final Thoughts

This Mango Coconut Tart is a celebration of the vibrant and refreshing flavors of the tropics. With its buttery crust, creamy coconut-mango filling, and a hint of zesty lime, this tart is a true tropical delight. Whether you’re indulging in a slice by the pool or serving it at a summer soirée, this dessert is sure to transport your senses to a sun-kissed paradise. Embrace the flavors of the tropics with this delightful Mango Coconut Tart and let your taste buds embark on a culinary vacation.
