Caramelized Onion and Pear Pizza


Have you ever dreamed of creating a unique yet delectable pizza that’s sure to impress your friends and family? Look no further! Today, we’ll guide you through preparing a heavenly Caramelized Onion and Pear Pizza. This lip-smacking dish combines the sweet flavor of pears with the nutty and mildly caramelized taste of onions to create a flavor explosion.

Before delving into the recipe, it’s vital to gather the ingredients and supplies needed. For this scrumptious pizza, you’ll require:

  • Dough for a store-bought or homemade pizza base
  • 2 tablespoons of Olive Oil
  • 1 medium Onion, thinly sliced
  • 2 pears, thinly sliced
  • 2-3 tablespoons of Brown Sugar
  • 1 cup shredded Mozzarella Cheese
  • 1 cup of ricotta cheese
  • Salt and pepper
  • Fresh Thyme or Rosemary for garnishing

With your ingredients at hand, let’s embark on our culinary adventure!

Step 1 - Preparing the Toppings

Initially, preheat your oven to approximately 425°F (219°C) while you prepare the toppings.

In a heavy-bottomed skillet, heat the olive oil over medium-low heat. Add the thinly sliced onions, stirring often, and cook until they’re translucent and tender but not browned. This will take roughly 20-25 minutes.

Next, add the brown sugar and a pinch of salt and pepper. Continue cooking and stirring until the onions have caramelized to a deep golden brown, approximately 10-15 minutes.

Finally, set aside the cooked onions and allow them to cool slightly. Simultaneously, remove the core and slices from the pears, ensuring your hands are well-protected from their inherent sharpness.

Step 2 - Assembling the Pizza

On a floured surface, roll or stretch the pizza dough to your desired size and thickness. Transfer it to a lightly greased or parchment-lined pizza sheet.

Once your dough is on the sheet, spread the ricotta cheese generously over the base, leaving a slight border around the edges. This will serve as a creamy counterpoint to the flavorful toppings.

Now, create an even layer of caramelized onions overlapping the pear slices. And do remember, layering the onions before the pears ensures the onions maintain their captivating flavor without being lost amidst the fruit.

Add shredded mozzarella cheese on top, ensuring coverage over both the caramelized onions and pears.

Step 3 - Baking

Place the pizza sheet in your preheated oven and bake for approximately 15-20 minutes. The key here is to watch carefully for the delectable interplay between the golden-brown crust and bubbly cheese.

Once your pizza attains a lovely golden crust and your cheeses have melted thoroughly, remove it from the oven.

Step 4 - Finishing Touches

Garnish your freshly-baked pizza with fresh thyme or rosemary for a touch of aromatic freshness before allowing it to cool slightly.

Cut the pizza into slices - either in squares or traditional wedges, based on your preference.

Serve your Caramelized Onion and Pear Pizza with a crisp, refreshing salad or a medium-bodied white wine on the side to enhance your dining experience even more.

The harmony of flavors in this pizza will leave your friends and family delightfully surprised and satiated, adding a unique twist to your next home-cooked feast. Enjoy!
