5 Easy and Affordable Vegan Recipes


Being vegan doesn’t mean sacrificing taste or breaking the bank. In fact, there are plenty of delicious and affordable vegan recipes that you can whip up in no time. Whether you’re a seasoned vegan or just starting out on your plant-based journey, these easy and budget-friendly recipes are sure to satisfy your taste buds. So, grab your apron and let’s get cooking!

1. Chickpea Curry

Ingredients:

  • 1 can of chickpeas
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 can of coconut milk
  • 2 tablespoons of curry powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh cilantro for garnish

Instructions:

  1. In a large saucepan, sauté the diced onion and minced garlic until fragrant and translucent.
  2. Add the curry powder and stir for a minute to release its flavors.
  3. Drain and rinse the chickpeas, then add them to the saucepan.
  4. Pour in the coconut milk and season with salt and pepper.
  5. Simmer the curry on medium heat for 15-20 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  6. Serve the chickpea curry over steamed rice or with naan bread.
  7. Garnish with fresh cilantro for an extra burst of flavor.

2. Lentil Bolognese

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup of dried lentils
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 carrot, diced
  • 1 can of crushed tomatoes
  • 2 tablespoons of tomato paste
  • 1 teaspoon of dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Whole wheat spaghetti or your favorite pasta

Instructions:

  1. Cook the lentils according to the package instructions until tender.
  2. In a separate pan, sauté the diced onion, minced garlic, and diced carrot until softened.
  3. Add the cooked lentils, crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, dried oregano, salt, and pepper to the pan.
  4. Simmer the mixture on low heat for 15-20 minutes, allowing the flavors to meld together.
  5. Meanwhile, cook the pasta according to the package instructions.
  6. Serve the lentil bolognese over the cooked pasta and enjoy!

3. Sweet Potato and Black Bean Tacos

Ingredients:

  • 2 sweet potatoes, peeled and cubed
  • 1 can of black beans, drained and rinsed
  • 1 tablespoon of olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon of chili powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon of cumin
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Taco shells or tortillas
  • Toppings: diced avocado, salsa, shredded lettuce

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C).
  2. Toss the cubed sweet potatoes with olive oil, chili powder, cumin, salt, and pepper.
  3. Spread the seasoned sweet potatoes on a baking sheet and roast for 20-25 minutes, or until tender and slightly crispy.
  4. In a separate pan, heat the black beans until warmed through.
  5. Warm the taco shells or tortillas according to the package instructions.
  6. Assemble the tacos by filling each shell with the roasted sweet potatoes, black beans, and your favorite toppings.
  7. Serve immediately and enjoy the delicious flavors!

4. Quinoa Salad

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup of cooked quinoa
  • 1 cucumber, diced
  • 1 bell pepper, diced
  • 1/4 red onion, thinly sliced
  • 1/4 cup of fresh parsley, chopped
  • Juice of 1 lemon
  • 2 tablespoons of olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a large bowl, combine the cooked quinoa, diced cucumber, diced bell pepper, sliced red onion, and chopped parsley.
  2. In a separate small bowl, whisk together the lemon juice, olive oil, salt, and pepper to create the dressing.
  3. Pour the dressing over the quinoa mixture and toss until well coated.
  4. Allow the salad to sit for 15-20 minutes to allow the flavors to meld together.
  5. Serve the quinoa salad as a refreshing side dish or a light lunch option.

5. Banana Oat Pancakes

Ingredients:

  • 1 ripe banana
  • 1 cup of rolled oats
  • 1/2 cup of plant-based milk (such as almond or soy milk)
  • 1 teaspoon of vanilla extract
  • 1/2 teaspoon of cinnamon
  • Maple syrup and fresh fruit for topping

Instructions:

  1. In a blender or food processor, combine the ripe banana, rolled oats, plant-based milk, vanilla extract, and cinnamon.
  2. Blend until the mixture forms a smooth batter.
  3. Heat a non-stick pan or griddle over medium heat.
  4. Pour 1/4 cup of the pancake batter onto the heated pan and cook until bubbles form on the surface.
  5. Flip the pancake and cook for another minute or until golden brown.
  6. Repeat with the remaining batter.
  7. Serve the banana oat pancakes with a drizzle of maple syrup and your favorite fresh fruit.

That’s it! Five easy and affordable vegan recipes to add to your repertoire. Whether you’re looking for a hearty dinner, a light lunch, or a delicious breakfast, these recipes have got you covered. So, put on your apron, gather your ingredients, and let the culinary adventures begin! Enjoy the flavors and the satisfaction of cooking delicious vegan meals without breaking the bank. Happy cooking!
