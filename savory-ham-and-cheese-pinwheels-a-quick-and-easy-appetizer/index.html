Savory Ham and Cheese Pinwheels: A Quick and Easy Appetizer


Looking for a delicious and effortless appetizer that will impress your guests? Savory Ham and Cheese Pinwheels are the perfect solution! These delectable pinwheels are not only easy to make but also incredibly versatile, making them a go-to choice for any gathering or snack time. Whether you’re hosting a party, a casual get-together, or simply craving a savory treat, these pinwheels are sure to be a hit.

Ingredients:

  • 1 sheet of puff pastry, thawed
  • 8 slices of deli ham
  • 8 slices of Swiss cheese
  • 1/4 cup Dijon mustard
  • 1/4 cup honey
  • 1 tablespoon poppy seeds
  • 1 tablespoon melted butter
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C) and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
  2. On a lightly floured surface, unfold the thawed puff pastry sheet and gently roll it out to smooth any creases.
  3. In a small bowl, mix the Dijon mustard and honey until well combined.
  4. Spread the honey mustard mixture evenly over the entire surface of the puff pastry.
  5. Layer the ham slices on top of the honey mustard, covering the entire surface of the pastry.
  6. Place the Swiss cheese slices on top of the ham, ensuring even coverage.
  7. Season with a pinch of salt and pepper for added flavor.
  8. Starting from one end, tightly roll the pastry into a log, ensuring that the filling is securely enclosed.
  9. Using a sharp knife, slice the log into 1-inch thick pinwheels.
  10. Arrange the pinwheels on the prepared baking sheet, leaving some space between each piece.
  11. In a small bowl, mix the melted butter and poppy seeds, then brush the mixture over the pinwheels.
  12. Bake in the preheated oven for 15-18 minutes, or until the pinwheels are golden brown and puffed up.
  13. Once baked, remove the pinwheels from the oven and let them cool for a few minutes before serving.

Serving Suggestions:

These Savory Ham and Cheese Pinwheels are delightful on their own, but they can also be paired with a variety of dips or sauces. Consider serving them with a creamy garlic aioli, tangy barbecue sauce, or a zesty marinara for an extra burst of flavor. Additionally, you can accompany them with a fresh garden salad or a bowl of soup for a more substantial meal. The options are endless, allowing you to tailor these pinwheels to suit any occasion or preference.

Variations:

Feel free to put your own spin on this recipe by experimenting with different types of cheese, such as cheddar, gouda, or mozzarella. You can also customize the filling by adding sliced turkey, crispy bacon, or even a layer of sautéed spinach for a vegetarian twist. For a touch of heat, consider incorporating a sprinkle of red pepper flakes or a dash of hot sauce into the honey mustard mixture. The versatility of these pinwheels makes them an ideal canvas for culinary creativity.

Conclusion:

Savory Ham and Cheese Pinwheels are a true crowd-pleaser, offering a delightful combination of flavors and a satisfying crunch. Whether you’re a seasoned home cook or just starting your culinary journey, this recipe is a must-try. With minimal prep time and maximum flavor, these pinwheels are bound to become a staple in your recipe repertoire. So, the next time you’re in need of a quick and easy appetizer that doesn’t compromise on taste, turn to these irresistible Savory Ham and Cheese Pinwheels. Your taste buds will thank you!
