8 Mouthwatering Italian Pizza Recipes to Make at Home


Pizza is more than just a meal; it’s a celebration of flavors, a canvas for culinary creativity, and a beloved dish that has its roots deeply embedded in Italian culture. Whether you’re a pizza aficionado or a novice in the kitchen, these eight mouthwatering Italian pizza recipes will inspire you to roll up your sleeves and create your own delicious masterpieces at home. From classic Margherita to inventive toppings that push boundaries, each recipe brings a slice of Italy to your table. Let’s dive into the world of homemade pizza!

1. Classic Margherita Pizza

Ingredients:

  • For the dough:

    • 2 cups all-purpose flour
    • 1 teaspoon salt
    • 1 teaspoon sugar
    • 1 packet (2 ¼ teaspoons) active dry yeast
    • ¾ cup warm water
    • 1 tablespoon olive oil

  • For the topping:

    • 1 cup crushed San Marzano tomatoes
    • 200g fresh mozzarella cheese, sliced
    • Fresh basil leaves
    • Extra virgin olive oil
    • Salt to taste

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the dough: In a bowl, combine warm water, sugar, and yeast. Let it sit for about 5 minutes until frothy. In a separate bowl, mix flour and salt. Add the yeast mixture and olive oil, combining until a dough forms. Knead on a floured surface for about 10 minutes.

  2. Let it rise: Place the dough in a greased bowl, cover with a damp cloth, and let it rise for 1-2 hours until it doubles in size.

  3. Preheat your oven: Preheat the oven to 475°F (245°C).

  4. Shape your pizza: Roll out the dough on a floured surface to your desired thickness, then transfer it to a pizza stone or baking sheet.

  5. Add the toppings: Spread crushed tomatoes over the base, season with salt, and arrange mozzarella slices. Drizzle with olive oil.

  6. Bake: Bake for 10-12 minutes or until the crust is golden and the cheese is bubbling. Once out of the oven, top with fresh basil leaves.

Enjoy:

This classic Margherita pizza perfectly balances simplicity and flavor, making it a timeless favorite.

2. Pepperoni and Mushroom Pizza

Ingredients:

  • For the dough: (same as Margherita)

  • For the topping:

    • 1 cup pizza sauce
    • 200g sliced pepperoni
    • 1 cup sliced mushrooms
    • 200g shredded mozzarella cheese
    • 1 teaspoon dried oregano

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the dough: Follow the same steps as the Margherita pizza.

  2. Preheat your oven: Preheat the oven to 475°F (245°C).

  3. Shape your pizza: Roll out the dough and place it on a baking sheet or pizza stone.

  4. Add the sauce and toppings: Spread pizza sauce over the dough, layer with mozzarella, arrange pepperoni slices, and top with mushrooms. Sprinkle oregano on top.

  5. Bake: Bake for 12-15 minutes until the edges are crispy and the cheese is melted.

Enjoy:

The combination of savory pepperoni with earthy mushrooms creates a delightful flavor explosion in every bite.

3. Quattro Stagioni (Four Seasons) Pizza

Ingredients:

  • For the dough: (same as Margherita)

  • For the topping:

    • 1 cup pizza sauce
    • 200g mozzarella cheese, shredded
    • 100g artichoke hearts, quartered
    • 100g cooked ham, sliced
    • 100g black olives, pitted and halved
    • 100g mushrooms, sliced
    • 1 teaspoon dried oregano

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the dough: Follow the same steps as the Margherita pizza.

  2. Preheat your oven: Preheat the oven to 475°F (245°C).

  3. Shape your pizza: Roll out the dough and place it on a baking sheet or pizza stone.

  4. Add the sauce and toppings: Spread pizza sauce over the dough and layer with mozzarella. Divide the pizza into four sections and top each with one of the following: artichokes, ham, black olives, and mushrooms. Sprinkle oregano over the top.

  5. Bake: Bake for 12-15 minutes until the cheese is melted and bubbly.

Enjoy:

This pizza allows you to experience four distinct flavors in one bite, embodying the essence of Italian diversity.

4. Pesto Chicken Pizza

Ingredients:

  • For the dough: (same as Margherita)

  • For the topping:

    • ½ cup pesto sauce
    • 200g cooked chicken breast, shredded
    • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
    • 200g mozzarella cheese, shredded
    • Fresh arugula for garnish
    • Balsamic glaze for drizzling

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the dough: Follow the same steps as the Margherita pizza.

  2. Preheat your oven: Preheat the oven to 475°F (245°C).

  3. Shape your pizza: Roll out the dough and place it on a baking sheet or pizza stone.

  4. Add the sauce and toppings: Spread pesto sauce over the base, followed by shredded chicken and cherry tomatoes. Top with mozzarella cheese.

  5. Bake: Bake for about 12-15 minutes until the crust is golden brown.

  6. Garnish: Once out of the oven, add fresh arugula and drizzle balsamic glaze over the top.

Enjoy:

The vibrant flavors of pesto combined with tender chicken and fresh arugula make this pizza a refreshing choice.

5. BBQ Chicken Pizza

Ingredients:

  • For the dough: (same as Margherita)

  • For the topping:

    • ½ cup barbecue sauce
    • 200g cooked chicken, shredded
    • 1 red onion, thinly sliced
    • 200g mozzarella cheese, shredded
    • Fresh cilantro for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the dough: Follow the same steps as the Margherita pizza.

  2. Preheat your oven: Preheat the oven to 475°F (245°C).

  3. Shape your pizza: Roll out the dough and place it on a baking sheet or pizza stone.

  4. Add the sauce and toppings: Spread barbecue sauce over the dough. Top with shredded chicken, red onion slices, and mozzarella cheese.

  5. Bake: Bake for 12-15 minutes until golden and bubbly.

  6. Garnish: Sprinkle fresh cilantro over the top after baking.

Enjoy:

The smoky sweetness of barbecue sauce paired with juicy chicken creates a fun twist on traditional pizza.

6. White Pizza (Pizza Bianca)

Ingredients:

  • For the dough: (same as Margherita)

  • For the topping:

    • 1 cup ricotta cheese
    • 200g mozzarella cheese, shredded
    • 100g grated Parmesan cheese
    • 2 cloves garlic, minced
    • 1 tablespoon olive oil
    • Fresh spinach or arugula
    • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the dough: Follow the same steps as the Margherita pizza.

  2. Preheat your oven: Preheat the oven to 475°F (245°C).

  3. Shape your pizza: Roll out the dough and place it on a baking sheet or pizza stone.

  4. Prepare the cheese mix: In a bowl, combine ricotta, garlic, olive oil, salt, and pepper. Spread the ricotta mixture over the dough, then add mozzarella and Parmesan on top.

  5. Bake: Bake for 12-15 minutes until the crust is golden and the cheese is melted.

  6. Garnish: Add fresh spinach or arugula after baking.

Enjoy:

This pizza is rich and creamy, showcasing the delightful flavors of cheese without the distraction of sauce.

Ingredients:

  • For the dough: (same as Margherita)

  • For the topping:

    • 1 cup crushed tomatoes
    • 200g fresh mozzarella cheese, sliced
    • Fresh basil leaves
    • Extra virgin olive oil
    • Balsamic reduction for drizzling
    • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the dough: Follow the same steps as the Margherita pizza.

  2. Preheat your oven: Preheat the oven to 475°F (245°C).

  3. Shape your pizza: Roll out the dough and place it on a baking sheet or pizza stone.

The combination of fresh ingredients captures the essence of a Caprese salad in pizza form, making it light and refreshing.

8. Sicilian Pizza

Ingredients:

  • For the dough:

    • 3 cups all-purpose flour
    • 1 teaspoon salt
    • 1 packet (2 ¼ teaspoons) active dry yeast
    • 1 teaspoon sugar
    • 1 ¼ cups warm water
    • 3 tablespoons olive oil

  • For the topping:

    • 2 cups crushed tomatoes
    • 200g mozzarella cheese, shredded
    • 100g salami, sliced
    • 1 cup bell peppers, sliced
    • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
    • 1 teaspoon red pepper flakes

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the dough: In a bowl, combine warm water, yeast, and sugar. After 5 minutes, mix flour and salt in a separate bowl. Combine all ingredients and knead for about 10 minutes.

  2. Let it rise: Place dough in a greased bowl, cover, and let it rise for 1-2 hours until doubled in size.

  3. Preheat your oven: Preheat the oven to 475°F (245°C).

  4. Shape your pizza: Spread the dough into a greased rectangular baking dish.

  5. Add the toppings: Spread crushed tomatoes over the dough, sprinkle with oregano, then layer with mozzarella, salami, and bell peppers. Add red pepper flakes for a kick.

  6. Bake: Bake for 20-25 minutes until golden and bubbling.

Enjoy:

This thick-crust pizza is hearty and filled with robust flavors, perfect for sharing with family or friends.

Now that you have eight delectable Italian pizza recipes, it’s time to gather your ingredients and bring a taste of Italy into your kitchen. Each recipe offers a unique twist on traditional flavors, allowing you to customize and explore your pizza-making skills. So roll out your dough, unleash your creativity, and enjoy the delightful experience of homemade pizza. Buon Appetito!
