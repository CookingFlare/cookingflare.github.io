Chocolate Pumpkin Bread: A Seasonal Twist on a Classic


Fall is here, and that means it’s time to indulge in all things pumpkin! From lattes to pies, pumpkin flavor is everywhere. But have you ever thought about combining pumpkin with chocolate? If not, you’re in for a treat! In this recipe, we’ll show you how to create a delicious and moist Chocolate Pumpkin Bread that will have your taste buds dancing with joy.

Ingredients

  • 1 ¾ cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • ½ teaspoon baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon cinnamon
  • ½ teaspoon nutmeg
  • ¼ teaspoon cloves
  • ¼ teaspoon salt
  • ½ cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • ½ cup granulated sugar
  • ½ cup brown sugar
  • ½ cup vegetable oil
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup canned pumpkin puree
  • ½ cup buttermilk
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • ½ cup chocolate chips (optional)

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease a 9x5-inch loaf pan and set it aside.

  2. In a medium-sized bowl, whisk together the flour, baking soda, baking powder, cinnamon, nutmeg, cloves, salt, and cocoa powder. Set aside.

  3. In a large mixing bowl, combine the granulated sugar, brown sugar, vegetable oil, eggs, pumpkin puree, buttermilk, and vanilla extract. Mix until well combined.

  4. Gradually add the dry ingredients to the wet ingredients, mixing until just combined. Be careful not to overmix, as this can result in a dense bread.

  5. If desired, fold in the chocolate chips to add an extra burst of chocolatey goodness.

  6. Pour the batter into the prepared loaf pan and smooth the top with a spatula.

  7. Bake for 60-70 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean. If the bread starts to brown too quickly, loosely cover it with aluminum foil.

  8. Once baked, remove the bread from the oven and let it cool in the pan for 10 minutes. Then, transfer it to a wire rack to cool completely.

  9. Slice and serve this delectable Chocolate Pumpkin Bread on its own or with a dollop of whipped cream for an extra special treat!

Why You’ll Love Chocolate Pumpkin Bread

  • Moist and flavorful: The combination of pumpkin and chocolate creates a moist and tender bread that is bursting with flavor. Each bite is a delightful balance of rich chocolate and subtle pumpkin spice.

  • Perfect for fall: As the leaves change color and the air becomes crisp, there’s nothing better than enjoying a slice of Chocolate Pumpkin Bread. It’s the perfect treat to enjoy on a cozy autumn afternoon.

  • Easy to make: With simple ingredients and straightforward instructions, this recipe is perfect for both novice and experienced bakers. You’ll have a delicious bread ready to enjoy in no time!

  • Versatile: This Chocolate Pumpkin Bread is versatile and can be enjoyed in various ways. Serve it as a breakfast treat, an afternoon snack, or even as a dessert. It’s a crowd-pleaser no matter the occasion.

  • Freezes well: If you can resist devouring the entire loaf in one sitting, you’ll be happy to know that this bread freezes well. Simply wrap it tightly in plastic wrap and store it in the freezer for up to three months. When you’re ready to enjoy it, let it thaw at room temperature or reheat it in the oven for a warm, comforting treat.

So, what are you waiting for? Grab your apron, preheat your oven, and get ready to bake this delightful Chocolate Pumpkin Bread. It’s a seasonal twist on a classic that will leave you craving more. Happy baking!
