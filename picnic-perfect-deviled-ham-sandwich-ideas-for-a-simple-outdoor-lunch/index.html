Picnic Perfect Deviled Ham Sandwich Ideas for a Simple Outdoor Lunch


The sun is shining, the birds are singing, and it’s the perfect day for a picnic. What better way to enjoy the great outdoors than with a delicious and satisfying deviled ham sandwich? Packed with flavor and easy to prepare, these picnic perfect sandwiches are sure to be a hit at your next outdoor lunch. Whether you’re planning a family outing, a romantic date, or a casual get-together with friends, these deviled ham sandwich ideas will take your picnic to the next level.

Classic Deviled Ham Sandwich

Let’s start with the classic deviled ham sandwich. This timeless favorite is simple yet satisfying, making it a perfect choice for any picnic. To make this classic version, you’ll need:

  • Sliced bread of your choice
  • Deviled ham spread
  • Lettuce leaves
  • Sliced tomatoes
  • Optional: mustard or mayonnaise

Spread a generous layer of deviled ham on one slice of bread. Add a few lettuce leaves and a couple of slices of tomato. If you like a little extra kick, add a dollop of mustard or mayonnaise. Top it off with another slice of bread, and your classic deviled ham sandwich is ready to be packed for your picnic.

Spicy Deviled Ham and Cheese Sandwich

If you’re a fan of spice, this variation of the deviled ham sandwich is for you. To make this zesty sandwich, gather these ingredients:

  • Sliced bread
  • Deviled ham spread
  • Sliced pepper jack or cheddar cheese
  • Sliced jalapenos
  • Optional: hot sauce

Spread the deviled ham on a slice of bread and layer on the cheese and jalapenos. For an extra kick, drizzle a little hot sauce over the filling. Top it with another slice of bread, and voila – a spicy deviled ham and cheese sandwich that’s perfect for a picnic with a kick.

Mediterranean Style Deviled Ham Wrap

For a refreshing and light option, consider a Mediterranean-style deviled ham wrap. You’ll need the following ingredients:

  • Flour tortillas
  • Deviled ham spread
  • Sliced cucumber
  • Sliced red onion
  • Crumbled feta cheese
  • Fresh mint leaves

Spread the deviled ham on a flour tortilla and layer on the sliced cucumber, red onion, feta cheese, and a few fresh mint leaves. Roll up the tortilla, and you have a delightful Mediterranean-style deviled ham wrap that’s perfect for a picnic on a warm day.

Vegan Deviled Ham and Avocado Sandwich

For those who prefer a plant-based option, a vegan deviled ham and avocado sandwich is a great choice. Gather these ingredients:

  • Sliced bread
  • Vegan deviled ham alternative
  • Sliced avocado
  • Sprouts
  • Sliced red bell pepper

Spread the vegan deviled ham alternative on a slice of bread and layer on the sliced avocado, sprouts, and red bell pepper. This colorful and flavorful sandwich is a wonderful addition to any picnic, whether you’re vegan or simply looking to try something new.

Conclusion

These deviled ham sandwich ideas are versatile, delicious, and perfect for a simple outdoor lunch. Whether you’re a fan of classic flavors, enjoy a little heat, prefer a Mediterranean twist, or opt for a plant-based option, there’s a deviled ham sandwich for everyone. So, pack your picnic basket, grab a blanket, and head outdoors to enjoy these picnic perfect sandwiches with your loved ones. Cheers to good food and great company!
