Vegan Lemon Bars: Tangy and Sweet Citrus Treats


If you’re a fan of tangy and refreshing desserts, then these Vegan Lemon Bars are a must-try! Bursting with zesty lemon flavor and a buttery crust, these citrus treats are the perfect balance of sweet and sour. What’s even better is that they are completely vegan, making them suitable for those with dietary restrictions or those who simply prefer plant-based desserts. So, let’s dive right into the recipe and learn how to make these delectable Vegan Lemon Bars!

Ingredients

For the crust:

  • 1 ½ cups of all-purpose flour
  • ½ cup of powdered sugar
  • ½ cup of vegan butter, melted
  • A pinch of salt

For the lemon filling:

  • 1 ½ cups of granulated sugar
  • ½ cup of freshly squeezed lemon juice
  • ½ cup of unsweetened almond milk
  • ¼ cup of cornstarch
  • 1 tablespoon of lemon zest
  • ½ teaspoon of vanilla extract

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C) and line an 8x8-inch baking dish with parchment paper.

  2. In a mixing bowl, combine the all-purpose flour, powdered sugar, melted vegan butter, and a pinch of salt. Stir until the mixture resembles coarse crumbs.

  3. Transfer the crust mixture into the prepared baking dish and press it firmly onto the bottom. Ensure the crust is evenly spread throughout the dish.

  4. Bake the crust in the preheated oven for about 15-18 minutes or until it turns golden brown. Once done, remove it from the oven and set it aside to cool.

  5. While the crust is cooling, let’s prepare the lemon filling. In a saucepan, whisk together the granulated sugar, lemon juice, almond milk, cornstarch, lemon zest, and vanilla extract. Cook the mixture over medium heat, stirring constantly until it thickens and becomes smooth. This usually takes around 5-7 minutes.

  6. Once the lemon filling has thickened, pour it over the cooled crust, spreading it evenly with a spatula.

  7. Return the baking dish to the oven and bake for an additional 15-20 minutes, or until the filling is set.

  8. Remove the Vegan Lemon Bars from the oven and let them cool completely in the baking dish.

  9. Once cooled, refrigerate the bars for at least 2 hours to allow them to firm up.

  10. When you’re ready to serve, carefully lift the bars out of the baking dish using the parchment paper and place them on a cutting board. Use a sharp knife to cut them into squares or rectangles.

  11. Dust the Vegan Lemon Bars with powdered sugar for an extra touch of sweetness and a beautiful presentation.

  12. Serve your Vegan Lemon Bars chilled, and enjoy the burst of tangy and sweet flavors with every bite!

Tips and Variations

  • Feel free to experiment with different citrus flavors by substituting the lemon juice for lime or orange juice. This will give your bars a unique twist.

  • If you prefer a more intense lemon flavor, increase the amount of lemon zest in the filling.

  • For a gluten-free version, you can use gluten-free all-purpose flour or almond flour as a substitute for regular flour.

  • Serve the bars with a dollop of vegan whipped cream or a scoop of dairy-free vanilla ice cream for an indulgent treat.

  • These Vegan Lemon Bars can be stored in an airtight container in the refrigerator for up to 5 days. Just make sure to separate the layers with parchment paper to prevent sticking.

Now that you have the recipe for these delightful Vegan Lemon Bars, it’s time to put on your apron and get baking! Whether you’re vegan or not, these tangy and sweet citrus treats are sure to impress everyone who tries them. So, go ahead and spread some lemony goodness with these delicious bars. Happy baking!
