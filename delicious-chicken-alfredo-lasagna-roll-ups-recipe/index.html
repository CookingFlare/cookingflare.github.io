Delicious Chicken Alfredo Lasagna Roll-Ups Recipe


Chicken Alfredo Lasagna Roll-Ups are a delicious twist on the traditional lasagna, offering the creamy, savory goodness of Alfredo sauce wrapped up in individual servings for a more convenient and fun way to enjoy this classic dish. Whether you’re looking for a comforting meal to serve at family dinner, a special dish for a gathering, or a weeknight meal that stands out, this recipe is sure to be a hit. Packed with tender chicken, creamy Alfredo sauce, and layers of melted cheese, it combines the richness of lasagna with the ease and personal touch of roll-ups.

In this post, we will walk you through the step-by-step process of making these flavorful Chicken Alfredo Lasagna Roll-Ups, from preparing the chicken and Alfredo sauce to assembling and baking the roll-ups to perfection. You’ll also find helpful tips to customize the recipe to your liking. Let’s dive into making this creamy, cheesy, and comforting dish!

Ingredients

To make these Chicken Alfredo Lasagna Roll-Ups, gather the following ingredients:

For the Roll-Ups:

  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 3 cups cooked, shredded chicken (rotisserie chicken works well)
  • 2 cups ricotta cheese
  • 1 ½ cups shredded mozzarella cheese (plus extra for topping)
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley (optional, for garnish)
  • Salt and pepper to taste

For the Alfredo Sauce:

  • 1 tablespoon unsalted butter
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 cups heavy cream
  • 1 ½ cups grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 teaspoon Italian seasoning (optional)
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

Step 1: Prepare the Lasagna Noodles

Start by cooking the lasagna noodles. Boil a large pot of salted water, and cook the noodles according to the package instructions. Typically, this takes about 8-10 minutes. Be sure to stir the noodles occasionally to prevent them from sticking together. Once they’re done, drain the noodles and rinse them with cold water to stop the cooking process. Lay the noodles out flat on a clean kitchen towel or parchment paper to keep them from sticking to each other.

Step 2: Make the Chicken Alfredo Sauce

While the noodles are cooking, it’s time to prepare the rich and creamy Alfredo sauce.

  1. In a large saucepan, melt the butter over medium heat. Add the minced garlic and sauté for about 1 minute, or until the garlic is fragrant.
  2. Pour in the heavy cream, and bring the mixture to a simmer, stirring occasionally.
  3. Gradually add in the Parmesan cheese and continue to stir until the sauce becomes thick and creamy, about 5-7 minutes.
  4. Season the sauce with salt, pepper, and Italian seasoning (if using), and continue to stir until everything is well combined. Set the sauce aside.

Step 3: Prepare the Chicken Filling

Next, prepare the chicken filling that will go inside each lasagna roll-up.

  1. In a large mixing bowl, combine the shredded chicken, ricotta cheese, 1 cup of the shredded mozzarella, and ½ cup of Parmesan cheese.
  2. Season the mixture with salt and pepper to taste. Stir everything together until well combined.

Step 4: Assemble the Chicken Alfredo Lasagna Roll-Ups

Now comes the fun part—assembling your Chicken Alfredo Lasagna Roll-Ups.

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Spread a thin layer of the Alfredo sauce on the bottom of a 9x13-inch baking dish to prevent the noodles from sticking to the pan.
  3. Take one lasagna noodle and place it flat on a clean surface. Spoon about 2 tablespoons of the chicken mixture onto the center of the noodle.
  4. Roll up the noodle carefully, making sure the filling stays inside. Place the roll-up seam side down in the prepared baking dish.
  5. Repeat this process with the remaining noodles and chicken mixture, arranging the roll-ups neatly in the baking dish.

Step 5: Bake the Roll-Ups

Once all the roll-ups are in the dish, pour the remaining Alfredo sauce over the top. Make sure each roll-up is generously coated with the creamy sauce.

  1. Sprinkle the remaining mozzarella and Parmesan cheese over the top of the roll-ups.
  2. Cover the baking dish with aluminum foil and bake for 25 minutes.
  3. After 25 minutes, remove the foil and bake for an additional 10-15 minutes, or until the cheese is melted and bubbly and the top is lightly golden brown.
  4. Let the roll-ups rest for 5 minutes before serving. This allows the sauce to thicken and the flavors to settle.

Step 6: Garnish and Serve

Before serving, sprinkle some freshly chopped parsley over the roll-ups for a pop of color and added flavor. Serve the Chicken Alfredo Lasagna Roll-Ups with a side of garlic bread or a fresh green salad for a complete meal.

Tips and Variations

Customize Your Roll-Ups

  • Vegetarian Option: For a vegetarian twist, replace the chicken with spinach, mushrooms, or a combination of both. You can also add ricotta cheese or a mixture of sautéed vegetables for a flavorful filling.
  • Add Some Spice: If you like a little heat, try adding red pepper flakes to the Alfredo sauce or mixing in a pinch of cayenne pepper for some extra spice.
  • Use Different Cheeses: While mozzarella and Parmesan are traditional, feel free to experiment with other cheeses. A blend of fontina, provolone, or even cheddar can add different textures and flavors to your roll-ups.
  • Make Ahead: These roll-ups are perfect for prepping ahead of time. You can assemble them up to a day before and store them in the refrigerator. When ready to bake, just add a few extra minutes to the baking time.

For the Sauce:

  • Make the Sauce from Scratch: If you prefer a homemade touch, you can create your own Alfredo sauce using butter, cream, garlic, and fresh Parmesan. This is a great way to control the flavor and consistency.
  • Lighten Up the Sauce: For a lighter version of this dish, substitute the heavy cream with half-and-half or whole milk. You can also try a lower-fat cream cheese or ricotta in place of the ricotta cheese to reduce the calories without sacrificing too much flavor.

Storage and Reheating:

Leftover Chicken Alfredo Lasagna Roll-Ups store well in the refrigerator for up to 3-4 days. To reheat, place the roll-ups in a baking dish, cover with foil, and bake at 350°F (175°C) for 15-20 minutes, or until warmed through. You can also microwave individual roll-ups for a quicker meal.

Why You’ll Love Chicken Alfredo Lasagna Roll-Ups

Chicken Alfredo Lasagna Roll-Ups are the perfect combination of convenience and comfort food. Here’s why this dish will quickly become a favorite in your household:

  • Individual Portions: Each roll-up is an individual serving, making them ideal for portion control or serving guests at a dinner party. It also makes for easy leftovers!
  • Creamy, Cheesy Goodness: The creamy Alfredo sauce combined with ricotta, mozzarella, and Parmesan cheeses makes every bite of these roll-ups a cheesy delight.
  • Versatile: You can adjust the filling to suit your taste preferences or dietary restrictions. Whether you go for classic chicken, a vegetarian version, or mix in some extra seasonings, these roll-ups are endlessly customizable.
  • Comfort Food at Its Best: Who doesn’t love lasagna? Now, you can enjoy all the comforting flavors of lasagna in a more convenient and fun-to-eat form.

This easy-to-follow recipe delivers on flavor and satisfaction while offering a unique twist on traditional lasagna. From the rich Alfredo sauce to the tender chicken and gooey cheese, every bite is sure to please.
