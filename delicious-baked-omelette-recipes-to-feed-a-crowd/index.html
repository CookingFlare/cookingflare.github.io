Delicious Baked Omelette Recipes to Feed a Crowd


If you’re looking for a delicious and easy way to feed a crowd for breakfast or brunch, baked omelettes are the perfect solution. Not only are they incredibly versatile, but they also allow you to prepare a large quantity of omelette in one go, making them ideal for hosting gatherings or feeding a hungry family. In this blog post, we’ll explore some mouthwatering baked omelette recipes that are sure to impress your guests or family members.

Classic Baked Omelette

Ingredients:

  • 12 large eggs
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1 cup diced ham
  • 1/2 cup chopped onions
  • 1/2 cup diced bell peppers
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C) and grease a 9x13-inch baking dish.
  2. In a large bowl, whisk together the eggs and milk until well combined.
  3. Stir in the shredded cheese, diced ham, chopped onions, and diced bell peppers.
  4. Season the mixture with salt and pepper, then pour it into the prepared baking dish.
  5. Bake for 25-30 minutes, or until the omelette is set and the top is lightly golden.
  6. Allow the omelette to cool for a few minutes before slicing and serving.

This classic baked omelette is a crowd-pleaser and can be customized with your favorite fillings such as mushrooms, spinach, or tomatoes.

Veggie Lover’s Baked Omelette

Ingredients:

  • 10 large eggs
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • 1 cup diced mushrooms
  • 1 cup chopped spinach
  • 1/2 cup diced tomatoes
  • 1/2 cup sliced black olives
  • 1 cup crumbled feta cheese
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C) and grease a 9x13-inch baking dish.
  2. In a large bowl, whisk together the eggs and heavy cream until smooth.
  3. Add the diced mushrooms, chopped spinach, diced tomatoes, sliced black olives, and crumbled feta cheese to the egg mixture.
  4. Season with salt and pepper, then pour the mixture into the prepared baking dish.
  5. Bake for 30-35 minutes, or until the omelette is puffed and golden.
  6. Let the omelette cool for a few minutes before cutting it into squares and serving.

This veggie lover’s baked omelette is a nutritious and flavorful option that’s perfect for feeding a crowd with different dietary preferences.

Southwest Baked Omelette

Ingredients:

  • 8 large eggs
  • 1/4 cup milk
  • 1 cup cooked and crumbled chorizo
  • 1/2 cup diced green chilies
  • 1/2 cup shredded pepper jack cheese
  • 1/2 cup diced red onions
  • 1/2 cup chopped cilantro
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C) and grease a 9x13-inch baking dish.
  2. In a large bowl, whisk together the eggs and milk until frothy.
  3. Stir in the cooked chorizo, diced green chilies, shredded pepper jack cheese, diced red onions, and chopped cilantro.
  4. Season the mixture with salt and pepper, then pour it into the prepared baking dish.
  5. Bake for 25-30 minutes, or until the omelette is set and the edges are golden.
  6. Allow the omelette to cool slightly before slicing and serving with salsa and avocado.

This Southwest-inspired baked omelette brings a spicy kick to the table and is a great option for those who love bold flavors.

Conclusion

Baked omelettes are a fantastic way to simplify breakfast or brunch when you have a crowd to feed. With these recipes, you can easily customize the fillings to suit your taste preferences and dietary needs. Whether you opt for a classic omelette, a veggie-packed version, or a spicy Southwest twist, these baked omelettes are guaranteed to satisfy everyone’s cravings. So, the next time you’re hosting a brunch or feeding a hungry family, consider whipping up one of these delicious baked omelette recipes for a hassle-free and impressive meal.
