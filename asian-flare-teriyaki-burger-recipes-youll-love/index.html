Asian Flare: Teriyaki Burger Recipes You'll Love


If you’re a fan of burgers and crave a taste of Asian flavors, then these Teriyaki Burger recipes are right up your alley. Combining the juiciness of a classic burger with the sweet and tangy flavors of teriyaki sauce, these recipes will take your taste buds on an exciting journey.

Recipe 1: Teriyaki Glazed Beef Burger

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1/4 cup teriyaki sauce
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon ginger powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 4 burger buns
  • Lettuce, tomato, and onion for garnish

Instructions:

  1. In a mixing bowl, combine the ground beef, teriyaki sauce, garlic powder, ginger powder, salt, and pepper. Mix well until all the ingredients are evenly incorporated.
  2. Divide the mixture into four equal portions and shape them into patties.
  3. Preheat your grill or skillet over medium-high heat.
  4. Cook the burger patties for about 4-5 minutes on each side, or until they reach your desired level of doneness.
  5. While the patties are cooking, lightly toast the burger buns.
  6. Once the patties are cooked, remove them from the heat and let them rest for a few minutes.
  7. Assemble your burgers by placing a patty on the bottom half of each bun. Top with lettuce, tomato, and onion.
  8. Drizzle some additional teriyaki sauce on top of the patty, and cover with the top half of the bun.
  9. Serve your Teriyaki Glazed Beef Burgers hot and enjoy the explosion of flavors!

Recipe 2: Teriyaki Chicken Burger with Pineapple

Ingredients:

  • 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1/4 cup teriyaki sauce
  • 1 tablespoon honey
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon ginger powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 4 burger buns
  • Sliced pineapple for garnish

Instructions:

  1. In a small bowl, mix together the teriyaki sauce, honey, soy sauce, garlic powder, ginger powder, salt, and pepper.
  2. Place the chicken breasts in a shallow dish and pour the teriyaki marinade over them. Make sure the chicken is fully coated. Let it marinate for at least 30 minutes, or overnight for maximum flavor.
  3. Preheat your grill or skillet over medium-high heat.
  4. Cook the chicken breasts for about 6-8 minutes on each side, or until they reach an internal temperature of 165°F (74°C).
  5. While the chicken is cooking, lightly toast the burger buns.
  6. Once the chicken is cooked, remove it from the heat and let it rest for a few minutes.
  7. Slice the chicken breasts into thin strips.
  8. Assemble your burgers by placing the chicken strips on the bottom half of each bun. Top with a slice of pineapple.
  9. Cover with the top half of the bun and secure with a toothpick if needed.
  10. Serve your Teriyaki Chicken Burgers with Pineapple and savor the delightful combination of flavors.

Recipe 3: Teriyaki Portobello Mushroom Burger

Ingredients:

  • 4 large portobello mushrooms
  • 1/4 cup teriyaki sauce
  • 2 tablespoons balsamic vinegar
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 4 burger buns
  • Sliced avocado and sprouts for garnish

Instructions:

  1. In a small bowl, whisk together the teriyaki sauce, balsamic vinegar, olive oil, salt, and pepper.
  2. Place the portobello mushrooms in a shallow dish and pour the teriyaki marinade over them. Make sure the mushrooms are fully coated. Let them marinate for about 15-20 minutes.
  3. Preheat your grill or skillet over medium-high heat.
  4. Cook the mushrooms for about 4-5 minutes on each side, or until they are tender and juicy.
  5. While the mushrooms are cooking, lightly toast the burger buns.
  6. Once the mushrooms are cooked, remove them from the heat and let them rest for a few minutes.
  7. Assemble your burgers by placing a mushroom on the bottom half of each bun. Top with sliced avocado and sprouts.
  8. Cover with the top half of the bun and press gently to secure the toppings.
  9. Serve your Teriyaki Portobello Mushroom Burgers and enjoy the meaty texture and rich flavors.

These Teriyaki Burger recipes are a delicious fusion of Asian and Western cuisines. They offer a unique twist to the classic burger, adding a burst of flavor that will leave you craving for more. Whether you prefer beef, chicken, or a vegetarian option, these recipes have something for everyone. So, fire up your grill or heat up your skillet, and get ready to indulge in these mouthwatering Asian-inspired burgers. Bon appétit!
