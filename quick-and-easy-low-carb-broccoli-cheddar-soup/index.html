Quick and Easy Low Carb Broccoli Cheddar Soup


If you’re looking for a comforting and healthy soup that’s both quick to make and low in carbs, then you’re in the right place. This recipe for Low Carb Broccoli Cheddar Soup is not only delicious but also incredibly easy to prepare. Packed with the goodness of fresh broccoli and the rich, creamy flavor of cheddar cheese, this soup is perfect for a cozy night in or as a starter for a dinner party. Plus, it’s low in carbs, making it suitable for those following a keto or low-carb diet. Let’s dive into the recipe and get cooking!

Ingredients

  • 4 cups fresh broccoli florets
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 4 cups chicken or vegetable broth
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 2 cups shredded cheddar cheese
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Red pepper flakes (optional, for a bit of heat)
  • Crispy bacon or croutons for garnish (optional)

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Broccoli: Start by washing the broccoli florets thoroughly and chopping them into small, bite-sized pieces. Set them aside for later.

  2. Sauté the Aromatics: In a large pot or Dutch oven, melt the butter over medium heat. Add the chopped onion and minced garlic, and sauté until the onion becomes translucent and the garlic is fragrant.

  3. Add the Broccoli: Once the aromatics are ready, add the chopped broccoli to the pot and sauté for a few minutes, allowing the flavors to meld together.

  4. Simmer with Broth: Pour in the chicken or vegetable broth, ensuring that the broccoli is fully submerged. Bring the mixture to a gentle boil, then reduce the heat and let it simmer for about 15-20 minutes, or until the broccoli is tender.

  5. Blend the Soup: Using an immersion blender or a regular blender, carefully blend the soup until it reaches your desired consistency. For a chunkier soup, blend it less, and for a smoother texture, blend it more.

  6. Add the Cream and Cheese: Return the soup to the stovetop over low heat. Stir in the heavy cream and shredded cheddar cheese, allowing the cheese to melt into the soup and create a creamy, cheesy base. Season with salt, pepper, and a pinch of red pepper flakes if you like a bit of heat.

  7. Serve and Enjoy: Once the cheese has melted and the soup is heated through, it’s ready to serve. Ladle the Low Carb Broccoli Cheddar Soup into bowls and garnish with crispy bacon or croutons if desired.

Tips and Variations

  • For an extra flavor boost, consider adding a dash of nutmeg or a sprinkle of smoked paprika to the soup.
  • If you prefer a vegetarian version, simply use vegetable broth and omit the bacon garnish.
  • This soup can be stored in the refrigerator for up to 3 days, making it a convenient make-ahead meal.

Now that you have this simple yet delectable Low Carb Broccoli Cheddar Soup recipe in your repertoire, you can enjoy a warm, satisfying bowl of soup without the guilt of excess carbs. Whether you’re cooking for yourself or entertaining guests, this soup is sure to be a hit. So, gather your ingredients and get ready to savor the creamy goodness of this delightful low-carb creation. Cheers to good food and good health!
