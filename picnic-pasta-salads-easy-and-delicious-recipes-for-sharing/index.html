Picnic Pasta Salads: Easy and Delicious Recipes for Sharing


When it comes to planning a picnic, a versatile and delicious pasta salad is a must. Not only is it easy to make, but it’s also perfect for sharing with friends and family. In this post, we’ll share three different picnic pasta salads that you can easily whip up and pack in a jar for your next outing.

Recipe 1: Classic Italian Pasta Salad

Combine the unmistakable flavors of Italy with this delicious pasta salad. Perfect for itchy feet who want to travel without leaving their backyard, this dish is vibrant in both color and taste.

Ingredients:

  • 8 ounces of your choice of pasta
  • 1 can of black olives, drained and halved
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 8 ounces mozzarella cheese, cut into cubes
  • 1/2 cup basil leaves, torn
  • 1/4 cup chopped red onion
  • 1/4 cup balsamic vinaigrette

Instructions:

  1. Cook pasta al dente, according to package instructions. Drain and rinse in cold water.
  2. In a large bowl, combine pasta, black olives, cherry tomatoes, mozzarella, basil, and red onion.
  3. Pour in the balsamic vinaigrette and toss to coat evenly.
  4. Chill in the fridge for at least an hour before serving.

Recipe 2: Greek Feta Pasta Salad

Bring the flavors of the Mediterranean to your picnic with this refreshing Greek-inspired pasta salad.

Ingredients:

  • 8 ounces of your choice of pasta
  • 1 cup cucumber, sliced
  • 1 cup Kalamata olives, pitted
  • 8 ounces feta cheese, crumbled
  • 1/4 cup red bell pepper, chopped
  • 1/4 cup red onion, chopped
  • 2 tablespoons freshly squeezed lemon juice
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt

Instructions:

  1. Cook pasta al dente, according to package instructions. Drain and rinse in cold water.
  2. In a large bowl, combine pasta, cucumber, Kalamata olives, feta cheese, red bell pepper, and red onion.
  3. In a small bowl, whisk together lemon juice, olive oil, and salt.
  4. Pour the dressing over the pasta salad and mix thoroughly.
  5. Chill in the fridge for at least an hour before serving.

Recipe 3: Asian Sesame Pasta Salad

Introduce a touch of the East to your picnic with this delightful Asian-inspired pasta salad.

Ingredients:

  • 8 ounces of your choice of pasta
  • 1 cup shredded carrots
  • 1 cup edamame, shelled
  • 1/2 cup sliced green onions
  • 8 ounces tofu, drained and cubed
  • 2 tablespoons Asian sesame oil
  • 2 tablespoons rice vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon honey
  • 1 teaspoon soy sauce
  • 1/2 teaspoon grated ginger
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt

Instructions:

  1. Cook pasta al dente, according to package instructions. Drain and rinse in cold water.
  2. In a large bowl, combine pasta, carrots, edamame, green onions, and tofu.
  3. In a small bowl, whisk together sesame oil, rice vinegar, honey, soy sauce, ginger, and salt.
  4. Pour the dressing over the pasta salad and mix thoroughly.
  5. Chill in the fridge for at least an hour before serving.

So, the next time you plan a picnic, ditch the store-bought salads and try your hand at one of these three pasta salad recipes. They’re sure to be a crowd-pleaser and provide a tasty foundation for any picnic. Enjoy!
