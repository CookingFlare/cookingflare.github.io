Summer BBQ: Recipes with Seasonal Fruits and Grilled Chicken


Summer is here, and it’s time to fire up the grill and enjoy the warm weather with a delicious BBQ. What’s better than grilled chicken and fresh seasonal fruits to make your summer BBQ a hit? In this post, we’ll share some mouth-watering recipes that combine the juiciness of grilled chicken with the sweetness of seasonal fruits.

Grilled Chicken with Peach BBQ Sauce

Peaches are in season during the summer months, and they pair perfectly with grilled chicken. This recipe is a twist on traditional BBQ sauce, using peaches to add a sweet and tangy flavor.

Ingredients:

  • 2 lbs boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1 ripe peach, diced
  • 1/4 cup ketchup
  • 1/4 cup brown sugar
  • 2 tbsp apple cider vinegar
  • 1 tsp smoked paprika
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the grill to medium-high heat.
  2. In a blender or food processor, combine peach, ketchup, brown sugar, apple cider vinegar, smoked paprika, salt, and pepper. Blend until smooth.
  3. Brush the chicken breasts with the peach BBQ sauce and grill for 6-8 minutes per side, or until cooked through.
  4. Let the chicken rest for 5 minutes before slicing and serving.

Grilled Chicken with Pineapple Salsa

Pineapple is another summer fruit that pairs well with grilled chicken. This recipe adds a fresh and tangy twist to traditional salsa.

Ingredients:

  • 2 lbs boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1 cup pineapple chunks
  • 1/2 cup red onion, diced
  • 1 jalapeño pepper, seeded and finely chopped
  • 1 lime, juiced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the grill to medium-high heat.
  2. In a bowl, combine pineapple, red onion, jalapeño pepper, lime juice, and garlic.
  3. Grill the chicken breasts for 6-8 minutes per side, or until cooked through.
  4. Serve the grilled chicken with a spoonful of pineapple salsa.

Grilled Chicken with Watermelon Salad

Watermelon is a refreshing summer fruit that pairs well with grilled chicken. This recipe adds a light and refreshing touch to your summer BBQ.

Ingredients:

  • 2 lbs boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 2 cups watermelon chunks
  • 1/4 cup feta cheese, crumbled
  • 1/4 cup mint leaves, chopped
  • 2 tbsp lemon juice
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the grill to medium-high heat.
  2. Grill the chicken breasts for 6-8 minutes per side, or until cooked through.
  3. In a bowl, combine watermelon, feta cheese, mint leaves, and lemon juice.
  4. Slice the grilled chicken and serve with the watermelon salad.

Grilled Chicken with Stone Fruit Skewers

Stone fruits like peaches and nectarines are perfect for grilling and adding to skewers. This recipe is a fun and easy way to serve grilled chicken with a sweet and tangy twist.

Ingredients:

  • 2 lbs boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1 cup stone fruit chunks (peaches, nectarines, plums)
  • 1/4 cup honey
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tsp grated ginger
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the grill to medium-high heat.
  2. In a bowl, whisk together honey, soy sauce, and grated ginger.
  3. Thread the chicken and stone fruit chunks onto skewers.
  4. Brush the skewers with the honey-soy sauce mixture and grill for 8-10 minutes, or until cooked through.

These recipes are perfect for your summer BBQ and will surely impress your guests with their unique flavor combinations. Whether you’re looking for a sweet and tangy BBQ sauce or a refreshing watermelon salad, these recipes have got you covered. So fire up the grill and get ready to enjoy the flavors of summer!
