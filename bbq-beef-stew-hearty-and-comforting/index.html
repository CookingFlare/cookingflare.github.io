BBQ Beef Stew: Hearty and Comforting


Are you craving a home-cooked meal that will warm your soul and leave you with a satisfied grin? Look no further! This BBQ Beef Stew recipe is the ultimate comfort food, perfect for chilling out on a cool evening with your family. It’s an interesting combination of beefy goodness, rich barbecue sauce, and a variety of chunky vegetables. All these ingredients combine to produce a scrumptious stew that screams ‘cozy and comforting’.

INGREDIENTS

  • 2 lbs. chuck roast
  • 3 tbsp. cooking oil
  • 1 large onion, diced
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 4 cups beef broth
  • 2 cups chopped potatoes
  • 1 cup diced carrots
  • 1 cup green beans, trimmed
  • 2 tbsp. barbecue sauce
  • 1 tbsp. tomato paste
  • 1 tsp. smoked paprika
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • Flour, for dredging

DIRECTIONS

  1. Begin by preheating your oven to 325°F (160°C). This will be our slow cooking method.

  2. Dredge the chuck roast in some flour to make it ready for a comforting crust later on. In a large ovenproof pot over medium-high heat, add your cooking oil. When the oil starts to shimmer, add the beef chuck, browning it on all sides.

  3. You may uncover a beautiful piece of meat, but don’t skip on the vegetables. Add the onions and garlic, cooking for about 5 minutes, stirring occasionally until they are translucent.

  4. It’s time to get our beef some fluids! Incorporate the beef broth, barbecue sauce, tomato paste, and smoked paprika into the pot. Stir everything well.

  5. Season with salt and pepper - remember, you can always add more later but hard to remove too much!

  6. The low and slow cooking method is ideal for breaking down the fibers of the chuck roast and creating a tender and smoked hunk of meat. Add the potatoes, carrots, and green beans to the mix and bring to a gentle simmer.

  7. Your stew needs a cozy and warm oven. Pop the pot in the preheated oven for at least 2-3 hours.

  8. When the veggies are cooked and the meat is VERY tender, bring your stew out of the oven. The stew should be very thick at this stage. Taste and season further, if necessary.

  9. Garnish your hearty Beef BBQ Stew with chopped parsley or cilantro if desired and serve with crusty bread to soak up the flavorsful stew.

TIPS AND TRICKS

  • Ensure your stew is adequately seasoned by tasting it periodically while slow cooking, then adding extra as per your taste preferences.

  • Choose quality ingredients. While it might be tempting to buy cheap, low-quality vegetables, the results will be evident in the final product.

  • Fear not the leftovers! This dish is even more flavorful and comforting after a day in the fridge.

FINAL THOUGHTS

In conclusion, nothing compares to a comforting bowl of slow-cooked BBQ Beef Stew. Although this recipe is straightforward, the outcome is something special - tender meat, smoky barbecue sauce, and hearty vegetables all come together in a crowd-favorite dish. You don’t have to be a professional chef to enjoy this hearty and comforting meal. Begin by trying this recipe today!
