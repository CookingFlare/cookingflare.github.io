Avocado and Egg Toast

The easiest and most versatile of all avocado and egg concoctions, avocado toast, can be transformed with the addition of our favorite green friend. It’s as simple as it is scrumptious.

Ingredients:

  • 1 slice of bread
  • 1 ripe avocado
  • 1 egg
  • Salt and pepper
  • Optional: hot sauce or red pepper flakes

Instructions:

  1. Toast the bread to your desired crispness.
  2. In a small bowl, mash the ripe avocado until it reaches a chunky consistency.
  3. Season the avocado with a pinch of salt and pepper, and a dash of hot sauce or red pepper flakes, if desired.
  4. Cook an egg to your preference ‚Äì fried, poached, or scrambled ‚Äì and set aside.
  5. Spread the seasoned avocado on the toasted bread, and top with the cooked egg.
  6. Enjoy your breakfast toast with a wink and a gentle nibble.

Avocado and Egg Overnight Oats

For a simple make-ahead breakfast, these creamy avocado and egg-infused overnight oats will have you prepping a healthy meal in minutes, no stove required.

Ingredients:

  • 1 ripe avocado
  • 1 cup rolled oats
  • ¬Ω cup unsweetened almond milk
  • 1 egg
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • Pinch of salt
  • Sweetener to taste (honey, maple syrup, or stevia)

Instructions:

  1. Blend ripe avocado, unsweetened almond milk, vanilla extract, and a pinch of salt until smooth.
  2. In a small bowl, combine the blended mixture and rolled oats.
  3. Whisk the egg in a separate bowl and use it to coat the insides of a mason jar or container.
  4. Pour the oat mixture into the jar or container.
  5. Cover and refrigerate overnight.
  6. Uncover, give it a good stir, and enjoy a soothing, filling breakfast, ready to go.

Avocado and Egg Stuffed Sweet Potatoes

For a heartier meal, indulge in a simple yet wholesome stuffed sweet potato. Avocado and egg together lend an extra boost of vitamins and protein.

Ingredients:

  • 1 medium sweet potato
  • 1 ripe avocado
  • 1 egg
  • Salt and pepper

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 400¬∞F (200¬∞C). Pierce sweet potatoes with a fork and arrange them directly on the oven rack.
  2. Bake until they are tender, about 45 minutes to an hour.
  3. While the sweet potatoes are baking, prepare the avocado and egg filling by mashing the ripe avocado and seasoning it with salt and pepper.
  4. When the sweet potatoes are cool enough to handle, cut them in half lengthwise and scoop out the center, leaving a thin layer to hold the shape.
  5. Fill the sweet potato skins with the seasoned avocado mixture.
  6. Top each sweet potato half with a cooked egg.
  7. Serve warm and appreciate the delightful interplay of textures and flavors in each bite.

Avocado and Egg Mini Quiches

For a perfect finger food or party appetizer, these mini quiches bring together the luscious avocado and the richness of egg, with a delightful crunch from included veggies.

Ingredients:

  • 1 ripe avocado
  • 4 eggs
  • 4 egg yolks
  • ¬º cup all-purpose flour
  • ¬Ω cup milk
  • ¬º cup chopped mixed vegetables (such as bell peppers, tomatoes, or spinach)
  • Salt and pepper

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 375¬∞F (190¬∞C). Grease a 12-hole mini muffin tin.
  2. In a blender or food processor, blend the ripe avocado, eggs, egg yolks, flour, and milk until smooth.
  3. Stir in the chopped mixed vegetables and season the mixture with salt and pepper to taste.
  4. Fill each muffin cup about three-quarters full with the avocado and egg mixture.
  5. Bake in the preheated oven for 25 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.
  6. Remove the mini quiches from the oven and allow them to cool slightly before serving.
