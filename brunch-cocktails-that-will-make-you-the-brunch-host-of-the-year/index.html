Brunch Cocktails That Will Make You the Brunch Host of the Year


Brunch is the perfect occasion to gather friends and family for a leisurely and indulgent meal. And what better way to elevate your brunch experience than with some delicious and creative cocktails? Whether you prefer something light and refreshing or bold and boozy, these brunch cocktails are sure to impress your guests and make you the brunch host of the year.

1. Sparkling Citrus Mimosa

Kickstart your brunch with a classic and refreshing mimosa with a citrusy twist. In a champagne flute, combine freshly squeezed orange juice and a splash of grapefruit juice. Top it off with chilled sparkling wine or champagne. Garnish with a twist of orange peel and a sprig of fresh mint. The combination of citrus flavors and bubbly effervescence will awaken your taste buds and set the tone for a delightful brunch.

2. Bloody Mary Martini

Take your brunch cocktails to the next level with a bold and savory Bloody Mary Martini. In a shaker, combine vodka, fresh tomato juice, a squeeze of lemon juice, Worcestershire sauce, hot sauce, and a pinch of celery salt. Shake vigorously and strain into a chilled martini glass. Garnish with a crisp celery stalk, a wedge of lemon, and a skewer of olives. The complex flavors of this cocktail will awaken your senses and complement savory brunch dishes perfectly.

3. Lavender Lemonade Spritzer

For a floral and refreshing option, try a lavender lemonade spritzer. In a pitcher, combine freshly squeezed lemon juice, lavender syrup, and chilled sparkling water. Stir gently and pour over ice. Garnish with a sprig of fresh lavender and a lemon wheel. This delightful cocktail combines the bright acidity of lemon with the soothing aroma of lavender, creating a truly unique and refreshing brunch experience.

4. Peach Bellini Float

For those with a sweet tooth, a Peach Bellini Float is a brunch cocktail that will satisfy your cravings. In a blender, combine frozen peaches, peach schnapps, and a splash of orange juice. Blend until smooth and pour into a tall glass. Top it off with chilled prosecco and a scoop of vanilla ice cream. Garnish with a peach slice and a drizzle of honey. This indulgent cocktail is like a dessert in a glass and will surely impress your guests.

5. Cucumber Basil Gin Fizz

For a light and herbaceous option, try a Cucumber Basil Gin Fizz. In a shaker, muddle fresh cucumber slices and basil leaves. Add gin, freshly squeezed lime juice, simple syrup, and ice. Shake well and strain into a tall glass filled with ice. Top it off with chilled club soda and garnish with a cucumber wheel and a sprig of basil. The refreshing combination of cucumber and basil, combined with the botanical flavors of gin, makes this cocktail a delightful choice for a brunch on a sunny day.

Conclusion

With these brunch cocktail recipes in your repertoire, you’re well on your way to becoming the brunch host of the year. Remember to serve these cocktails alongside delicious brunch dishes, such as avocado toast, eggs Benedict, or fluffy pancakes, to create a memorable and enjoyable brunch experience for your guests. Cheers to a successful and flavorful brunch!
