Raspberry White Chocolate Scones for a Sweet Breakfast


Scones are a delightful treat that pairs perfectly with a cup of coffee or tea, and when they’re infused with the sweet-tart flavor of raspberries and the indulgent creaminess of white chocolate, they become an irresistible morning indulgence. These Raspberry White Chocolate Scones are buttery, tender, and bursting with the flavors of summer. They are perfect for a leisurely weekend breakfast or as a special addition to brunch.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/3 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 tablespoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 6 tablespoons unsalted butter, cold and cut into small pieces
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • 1 large egg
  • 1 teaspoon pure vanilla extract
  • 1/2 cup white chocolate chips
  • 1/2 cup fresh raspberries, gently washed and patted dry
  • 1 tablespoon coarse sugar for sprinkling (optional)

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven and prepare the baking sheet:

    • Preheat the oven to 400°F (200°C).
    • Line a baking sheet with parchment paper or a silicone baking mat.

  2. Mix the dry ingredients:

    • In a large bowl, whisk together the flour, granulated sugar, baking powder, and salt.

  3. Cut in the butter:

    • Add the cold, cubed butter to the flour mixture.
    • Using a pastry cutter or your fingers, work the butter into the flour until the mixture resembles coarse crumbs with some larger pea-sized pieces of butter remaining.

  4. Add the wet ingredients:

    • In a separate bowl, whisk together the heavy cream, egg, and vanilla extract.
    • Pour the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients and gently mix until the dough starts to come together.

  5. Incorporate the mix-ins:

    • Gently fold in the white chocolate chips and raspberries until evenly distributed throughout the dough.

  6. Shape the scones:

    • Turn the dough out onto a lightly floured surface and pat it into a 7-inch circle, about 3/4 inch thick.
    • Cut the circle into 8 wedges and transfer them to the prepared baking sheet, leaving some space between each scone.

  7. Bake the scones:

    • Brush the tops of the scones with a little heavy cream and sprinkle with coarse sugar, if desired.
    • Bake for 15-18 minutes, or until the scones are golden brown and cooked through.

  8. Serve and enjoy:

    • Allow the scones to cool on the baking sheet for a few minutes before transferring them to a wire rack to cool completely.
    • Serve the scones warm or at room temperature, and enjoy the delightful combination of raspberries and white chocolate in every bite.

Tips for Success

  • Keep the butter cold: To ensure a tender, flaky texture, it’s important to use cold butter and work quickly when incorporating it into the flour mixture.
  • Don’t overmix the dough: Be gentle when mixing the wet and dry ingredients together, and avoid overworking the dough to prevent tough scones.
  • Customize the add-ins: While raspberries and white chocolate make a delicious combination, feel free to experiment with other mix-ins such as blueberries, lemon zest, or chopped nuts.

These Raspberry White Chocolate Scones are a delightful way to start the day. Whether you enjoy them with a dollop of clotted cream and jam or simply on their own, their tender crumb and burst of fruity sweetness make them a perfect addition to any breakfast or brunch spread. So, the next time you want to treat yourself to a sweet morning indulgence, give these scones a try and savor each delightful bite!
