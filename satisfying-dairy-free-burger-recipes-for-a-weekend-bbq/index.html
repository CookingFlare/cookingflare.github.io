Satisfying Dairy-Free Burger Recipes for a Weekend BBQ


Are you tired of the same old beef burgers at your BBQ parties? Maybe you have guests with dietary restrictions, or you’re simply looking to try something new and exciting. Well, look no further! In this blog post, we’ll be sharing some mouthwatering dairy-free burger recipes that are sure to satisfy your cravings and impress your guests. So fire up the grill and get ready for a feast!

1. Spicy Black Bean Burger

Ingredients:

  • 1 can of black beans, drained and rinsed
  • 1/2 cup of breadcrumbs
  • 1/2 cup of diced onion
  • 1/4 cup of chopped cilantro
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon of cumin
  • 1/2 teaspoon of chili powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Burger buns and desired toppings

Instructions:

  1. In a large bowl, mash the black beans with a fork until they are partially mashed but still chunky.
  2. Add the breadcrumbs, onion, cilantro, garlic, cumin, chili powder, salt, and pepper to the bowl. Mix well to combine.
  3. Form the mixture into patties of your desired size and thickness.
  4. Preheat your grill to medium-high heat. Cook the patties for about 4-5 minutes per side, or until they are heated through and have grill marks.
  5. Serve the black bean burgers on burger buns with your favorite toppings, such as avocado slices, lettuce, and tomato.

2. Portobello Mushroom Burger

Ingredients:

  • 4 large portobello mushrooms, stems removed
  • 1/4 cup of balsamic vinegar
  • 2 tablespoons of olive oil
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Burger buns and desired toppings

Instructions:

  1. In a shallow dish, whisk together the balsamic vinegar, olive oil, garlic, salt, and pepper.
  2. Add the portobello mushrooms to the dish, making sure they are coated in the marinade. Let them marinate for at least 30 minutes.
  3. Preheat your grill to medium heat. Grill the mushrooms for about 4-5 minutes per side, or until they are tender and have grill marks.
  4. Serve the portobello mushroom burgers on burger buns with your favorite toppings, such as caramelized onions and vegan cheese.

3. Lentil Walnut Burger

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup of cooked lentils
  • 1/2 cup of chopped walnuts
  • 1/4 cup of diced onion
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons of tomato paste
  • 2 tablespoons of ground flaxseed mixed with 3 tablespoons of water (flax egg)
  • 1 teaspoon of cumin
  • 1/2 teaspoon of smoked paprika
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Burger buns and desired toppings

Instructions:

  1. In a food processor, pulse the lentils and walnuts until they are finely chopped.
  2. Transfer the lentil and walnut mixture to a large bowl. Add the onion, garlic, tomato paste, flax egg, cumin, smoked paprika, salt, and pepper. Mix well to combine.
  3. Form the mixture into patties of your desired size and thickness.
  4. Preheat your grill to medium-high heat. Cook the patties for about 4-5 minutes per side, or until they are heated through and have grill marks.
  5. Serve the lentil walnut burgers on burger buns with your favorite toppings, such as sautéed mushrooms and vegan mayo.

Conclusion

These dairy-free burger recipes are not only delicious but also perfect for anyone following a dairy-free diet. Whether you’re a vegan, lactose intolerant, or simply looking to try something different, these burgers will satisfy your cravings and impress your friends and family at your next BBQ. So give them a try and enjoy a flavorful and satisfying meal without compromising on taste!
