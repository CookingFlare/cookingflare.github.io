Easy Microwave Meals for Students


Being a student can be tough, with busy schedules and limited cooking facilities. But fear not! The microwave is your best friend when it comes to quick and easy meals that are both delicious and nutritious. In this blog post, we’ll explore some creative microwave recipes that will satisfy your taste buds and save you time.

1. Cheesy Broccoli Rice

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup instant rice
  • 1 cup broccoli florets
  • 1/2 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1/4 cup milk
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a microwave-safe bowl, combine the instant rice and 1 1/2 cups of water. Microwave on high for 5 minutes or until the rice is cooked.
  2. Steam the broccoli florets in a microwave-safe bowl with a splash of water for 2-3 minutes on high.
  3. Drain any excess water from the cooked rice and fluff it with a fork.
  4. Add the steamed broccoli, shredded cheddar cheese, and milk to the rice. Stir well to combine.
  5. Microwave the mixture on high for 2-3 minutes, or until the cheese is melted and the mixture is heated through.
  6. Season with salt and pepper to taste, and enjoy!

2. Mug Omelette

Ingredients:

  • 2 eggs
  • 2 tablespoons milk
  • Your choice of fillings (e.g., cheese, ham, vegetables)
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a microwave-safe mug, whisk together the eggs and milk until well combined.
  2. Add your choice of fillings, such as cheese, ham, or vegetables. Mix well.
  3. Microwave the mug on high for 1 minute, then remove and stir.
  4. Microwave for another 1-2 minutes, or until the omelette is set and cooked through.
  5. Season with salt and pepper to taste, and voila! Your mug omelette is ready to enjoy.

3. Microwave Mac and Cheese

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup elbow macaroni
  • 1 1/2 cups water
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a microwave-safe bowl, combine the elbow macaroni and water. Microwave on high for 8-10 minutes, stirring every 2 minutes, until the macaroni is cooked and tender.
  2. Drain any excess water from the cooked macaroni.
  3. Stir in the milk and shredded cheddar cheese until well combined.
  4. Microwave the mixture on high for another 1-2 minutes, or until the cheese is melted and the mixture is heated through.
  5. Season with salt and pepper to taste, and indulge in the creamy goodness of microwave mac and cheese.

4. Chocolate Mug Cake

Ingredients:

  • 4 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons cocoa powder
  • 3 tablespoons granulated sugar
  • 1/8 teaspoon baking powder
  • A pinch of salt
  • 3 tablespoons milk
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1/4 teaspoon vanilla extract

Instructions:

  1. In a microwave-safe mug, whisk together the all-purpose flour, cocoa powder, granulated sugar, baking powder, and salt until well combined.
  2. Add the milk, vegetable oil, and vanilla extract to the mug. Stir well until smooth and no lumps remain.
  3. Microwave the mug on high for 1-2 minutes, or until the cake has risen and is set in the center.
  4. Allow the mug cake to cool for a minute or two before indulging in the rich, chocolatey goodness.

These easy microwave meals for students are proof that you don’t need fancy cooking equipment to enjoy delicious and satisfying meals. With just a few simple ingredients and a microwave, you can whip up a variety of tasty dishes in no time. So, go ahead and explore the world of microwave cooking – your taste buds will thank you!
