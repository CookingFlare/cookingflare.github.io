XOXO Xanthan Gum Chocolate Chip Cookies with Walnuts and Sea Salt and Cream Cheese Frosting and Sprinkles: A Valentine's Day Treat Recipe


XOXO Xanthan Gum Chocolate Chip Cookies with Walnuts and Sea Salt and Cream Cheese Frosting and Sprinkles: A Valentine’s Day Treat Recipe

Valentine’s Day is just around the corner, and what better way to celebrate love and sweetness than with a batch of XOXO Xanthan Gum Chocolate Chip Cookies with Walnuts and Sea Salt and Cream Cheese Frosting and Sprinkles? These delightful cookies are the perfect combination of rich, chocolatey goodness, nutty crunch, and a touch of creamy indulgence. Whether you’re planning a romantic evening in or a fun baking session with friends, these cookies are sure to be a hit!

Ingredients

For the Cookies:

  • 1 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 cup brown sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 2 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon Xanthan gum
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 2 cups chocolate chips
  • 1 cup chopped walnuts
  • Sea salt for sprinkling

For the Cream Cheese Frosting:

  • 4 oz cream cheese, softened
  • 1/4 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 2 cups powdered sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Additional:

  • Sprinkles for decoration

Instructions

Step 1: Preparing the Cookie Dough

  1. In a large mixing bowl, cream together the softened butter, granulated sugar, and brown sugar until light and fluffy.
  2. Add the eggs, one at a time, beating well after each addition. Stir in the vanilla extract.
  3. In a separate bowl, whisk together the all-purpose flour, Xanthan gum, baking soda, and salt.
  4. Gradually add the dry ingredients to the creamed mixture and mix until well combined.
  5. Gently fold in the chocolate chips and chopped walnuts.

Step 2: Baking the Cookies

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C) and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
  2. Using a cookie scoop, drop rounded balls of dough onto the prepared baking sheet, leaving enough space between each cookie.
  3. Sprinkle a pinch of sea salt over the top of each cookie for a delightful salty-sweet contrast.
  4. Bake for 10-12 minutes or until the edges are golden brown. Allow the cookies to cool on the baking sheet for a few minutes before transferring them to a wire rack to cool completely.

Step 3: Making the Cream Cheese Frosting

  1. In a medium bowl, beat the softened cream cheese and butter until smooth and creamy.
  2. Gradually add the powdered sugar, beating well after each addition, until the frosting is light and fluffy.
  3. Stir in the vanilla extract until fully incorporated.

Step 4: Assembling the Cookies

  1. Once the cookies are completely cooled, spread a generous dollop of cream cheese frosting on the flat side of half of the cookies.
  2. Top each frosted cookie with another cookie to create delightful cookie sandwiches.
  3. Decorate the edges of the frosting with colorful sprinkles to add a festive touch.

Serving Suggestion

These XOXO Xanthan Gum Chocolate Chip Cookies with Walnuts and Sea Salt and Cream Cheese Frosting and Sprinkles are best enjoyed with a tall glass of cold milk or a cup of hot cocoa. They make a wonderful treat for Valentine’s Day parties, school events, or simply as a sweet surprise for your loved ones.

Conclusion

With their rich chocolate chips, crunchy walnuts, creamy frosting, and cheerful sprinkles, these cookies are a delightful way to spread love and joy on Valentine’s Day. Whether you’re sharing them with your significant other, family, or friends, these XOXO Xanthan Gum Chocolate Chip Cookies with Walnuts and Sea Salt and Cream Cheese Frosting and Sprinkles are sure to bring smiles and warm hearts. Happy Valentine’s Day!
