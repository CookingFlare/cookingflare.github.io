In a medium-sized saucepan, bring the water to a boil. Add the quinoa and reduce the heat to low. Cover and simmer for 15 minutes, or until the quinoa is tender and the water has been absorbed. Once the quinoa is cooked, remove it from the heat and let it cool for a few minutes. In a large mixing bowl, combine the cooked quinoa, cherry tomatoes, cucumber, and red onion. Cover the bowl with plastic wrap and refrigerate for at least 30 minutes to allow the flavors to meld together. Basil, cilantro, or tarragon would also work well in this recipe. This salad makes a great side dish or a light lunch. You can also serve it as a filling for wraps or stuffed into pita bread for a quick and nutritious meal on the go.
