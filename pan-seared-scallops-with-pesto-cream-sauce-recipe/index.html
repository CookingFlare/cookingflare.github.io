Pan-Seared Scallops with Pesto Cream Sauce Recipe


If you’re looking to impress your dinner guests with a restaurant-quality dish that’s surprisingly easy to make at home, then pan-seared scallops with pesto cream sauce is the recipe for you. This elegant yet simple dish combines the delicate flavor of fresh scallops with the rich, aromatic taste of pesto cream sauce. The result is a dish that’s not only visually stunning but also bursting with flavor. So, roll up your sleeves and get ready to elevate your cooking game with this delightful recipe.

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound fresh sea scallops
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • 2 tablespoons pesto
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon butter
  • 1 tablespoon chopped fresh parsley

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the Scallops: Before you start cooking, make sure the scallops are dry. Pat them gently with paper towels to remove any excess moisture. Season the scallops with salt and pepper on both sides.

  2. Sear the Scallops: Heat the olive oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat. When the oil is hot, add the scallops to the pan, making sure not to overcrowd them. Sear the scallops for about 2-3 minutes on each side until they develop a golden crust. Be careful not to overcook them, as they can become tough and rubbery.

  3. Make the Pesto Cream Sauce: In a separate saucepan, melt the butter over medium heat. Add the minced garlic and sauté for a minute until fragrant. Pour in the heavy cream and let it simmer for a few minutes until it starts to thicken slightly. Stir in the pesto and continue to cook for another 2-3 minutes, allowing the flavors to meld together. Season the sauce with salt and pepper to taste.

  4. Serve: Once the scallops are perfectly seared and the pesto cream sauce is ready, it’s time to plate up. Arrange the scallops on a serving dish and drizzle the pesto cream sauce over them. Garnish with chopped fresh parsley for a pop of color and an extra burst of flavor.

Tips and Tricks:

  • Choosing Scallops: Opt for fresh, dry-packed sea scallops over wet-packed ones, as they tend to sear better and have a sweeter flavor.
  • Searing Scallops: To achieve a golden crust, ensure that the scallops are dry before searing them. Also, make sure not to overcrowd the pan, as this can cause them to steam rather than sear.
  • Pesto Cream Sauce: Feel free to adjust the consistency of the sauce by adding more or less heavy cream, depending on your preference.

Final Thoughts:

Pan-seared scallops with pesto cream sauce is a dish that’s perfect for a special occasion or a luxurious weeknight dinner. The succulent scallops paired with the aromatic pesto cream sauce create a harmony of flavors that will leave your taste buds singing. With this recipe in your repertoire, you’ll have a show-stopping dish that’s sure to impress even the most discerning of palates. So, why not treat yourself and your loved ones to this exquisite culinary delight? Happy cooking!
