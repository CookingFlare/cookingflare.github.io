Homemade Peppermint Ice Cream: A Refreshing Holiday Dessert


Winter is here, and with it comes the joy of indulging in sweet treats that evoke the festive spirit. Among the many delightful desserts that adorn our holiday tables, none is more refreshing and invigorating than peppermint ice cream. This classic flavor combination is a staple during the holiday season, and for good reason. The cooling sensation of peppermint is the perfect antidote to the warmth and coziness of the season, and when paired with rich and creamy ice cream, it’s a match made in heaven.

In this post, we’ll explore the art of making homemade peppermint ice cream from scratch, a process that requires minimal equipment and a bit of patience. With this recipe, you’ll be able to create a decadent and refreshing dessert that’s sure to impress your friends and family.

The Magic of Peppermint

Before we dive into the recipe, let’s take a moment to appreciate the magic of peppermint. This refreshing herb has been a staple in many cuisines for centuries, and its unique flavor profile has captured the hearts of many. Peppermint’s cooling sensation is due to the presence of menthol, a compound that’s responsible for its distinctive taste and aroma.

When it comes to using peppermint in cooking and baking, there are many ways to incorporate its flavor. From adding it to sauces and marinades to using it as a garnish for cocktails, peppermint is a versatile ingredient that can elevate a wide range of dishes. In the case of peppermint ice cream, the herb is used to create a rich and creamy base that’s infused with its signature flavor.

The Recipe

To make homemade peppermint ice cream, you’ll need the following ingredients:

  • 2 cups heavy cream
  • 1 cup whole milk
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/4 cup corn syrup
  • 1/2 teaspoon kosher salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon peppermint extract
  • 1/4 cup crushed peppermint candies (optional)

Step 1: Combine the Cream, Milk, and Sugar

In a medium-sized saucepan, combine the heavy cream, whole milk, granulated sugar, and corn syrup. Whisk the mixture until the sugar is fully dissolved, then bring it to a simmer over medium heat.

Step 2: Infuse the Peppermint Flavor

Once the mixture has reached a simmer, remove it from the heat and stir in the peppermint extract. Allow the mixture to steep for at least 30 minutes, or until it has cooled to room temperature. This step is crucial, as it allows the peppermint flavor to fully infuse into the mixture.

Step 3: Chill the Mixture

Once the mixture has cooled, cover it and refrigerate it for at least 2 hours, or overnight. This step is necessary to allow the mixture to chill and the flavors to meld together.

Step 4: Churn the Ice Cream

Once the mixture is chilled, it’s time to churn the ice cream. Pour the mixture into an ice cream maker and churn according to the manufacturer’s instructions. If you don’t have an ice cream maker, you can also place the mixture in a 9x13 inch baking dish and freeze it for about 2 hours, stirring every 30 minutes until the desired consistency is reached.

Step 5: Add the Crushed Peppermint Candies (Optional)

If you’re feeling extra festive, you can add crushed peppermint candies to the ice cream during the last 2 minutes of churning. This adds a fun and playful touch to the ice cream.

Step 6: Enjoy Your Homemade Peppermint Ice Cream

Once the ice cream has finished churning, scoop it into cones or bowls and enjoy! You can also store it in an airtight container in the freezer for up to 2 weeks.

Tips and Variations

  • To enhance the peppermint flavor, you can add a few drops of peppermint extract to the mixture during the churning process.
  • If you don’t have an ice cream maker, you can also place the mixture in a 9x13 inch baking dish and freeze it for about 2 hours, stirring every 30 minutes until the desired consistency is reached.
  • To add a fun and playful touch to the ice cream, you can add crushed peppermint candies to the mixture during the last 2 minutes of churning.
  • You can also use this recipe as a base and add other flavors, such as chocolate chips or chopped nuts, to create unique and delicious flavor combinations.

In conclusion, homemade peppermint ice cream is a refreshing and decadent dessert that’s perfect for the holiday season. With its rich and creamy texture and invigorating peppermint flavor, it’s a treat that’s sure to delight both kids and adults alike. By following this recipe and incorporating a few simple tips and variations, you’ll be able to create a dessert that’s both impressive and delicious. So go ahead, get creative, and indulge in the sweet and refreshing world of homemade peppermint ice cream!
