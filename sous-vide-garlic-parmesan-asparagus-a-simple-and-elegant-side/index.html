Sous Vide Garlic Parmesan Asparagus: A Simple and Elegant Side


Asparagus is one of the most popular spring vegetables, and for good reason – it’s tender, flavorful, and pairs well with a variety of dishes. When it comes to cooking asparagus, many people stick to traditional methods like steaming or roasting. However, with the help of sous vide technology, you can achieve a perfectly cooked asparagus dish with a rich, garlicky flavor and a satisfying crunch.

In this recipe, we’ll explore the world of sous vide cooking and show you how to prepare a simple yet elegant side dish that’s sure to impress your family and friends. Say goodbye to overcooked or undercooked asparagus and hello to a perfectly cooked, garlic-infused delight.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound fresh asparagus, trimmed
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1/4 teaspoon paprika
  • 1/4 cup white wine (optional)

Equipment

  • Sous vide machine
  • Immersion circulator
  • Large bowl or container
  • Tray or plate for serving

Instructions

Step 1: Prepare the Asparagus

Begin by placing the asparagus in a large bowl or container. Add the minced garlic, olive oil, salt, black pepper, and paprika. Toss the asparagus to coat it evenly with the garlic mixture, making sure each spear is well-coated.

Step 2: Seal the Asparagus

Transfer the asparagus mixture to a sous vide bag or a zip-top plastic bag. Seal the bag, making sure to remove as much air as possible before sealing.

Step 3: Cook the Asparagus

Place the sealed bag in the sous vide machine and set the temperature to 180°F (82°C). Cook the asparagus for 30-40 minutes, or until it reaches your desired level of doneness. You can check the asparagus for doneness by inserting a fork or knife into the thickest part of the spear – it should be tender but still retain some crunch.

Step 4: Finish with Parmesan Cheese

Once the asparagus is cooked, remove it from the sous vide machine and place it on a tray or plate. Sprinkle the grated Parmesan cheese over the asparagus, making sure each spear is well-coated.

Step 5: Serve

Serve the garlic Parmesan asparagus hot, garnished with a sprinkle of Parmesan cheese and a drizzle of white wine (if using). This dish pairs well with a variety of main courses, including grilled meats, roasted vegetables, and pasta dishes.

Tips and Variations

  • For an added layer of flavor, you can add 1-2 tablespoons of lemon juice or zest to the garlic mixture before cooking the asparagus.
  • If you prefer a stronger garlic flavor, you can increase the amount of minced garlic to 3-4 cloves.
  • To add some crunch to the dish, you can sprinkle some toasted breadcrumbs or chopped nuts over the asparagus before serving.
  • For a vegetarian or vegan option, you can substitute the Parmesan cheese with a dairy-free alternative or omit it altogether.

Conclusion

Sous vide garlic Parmesan asparagus is a simple yet elegant side dish that’s sure to impress your family and friends. With its tender, flavorful spears and rich, garlicky flavor, this dish is perfect for special occasions or everyday meals. By following these easy steps and tips, you can create a delicious and memorable dish that’s sure to become a staple in your kitchen.
