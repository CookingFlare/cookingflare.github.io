Refreshing Nut-Free Greek Salad


Are you craving something light, refreshing, and bursting with Mediterranean flavors? Look no further than this delicious Nut-Free Greek Salad recipe! Packed with crisp vegetables, tangy feta cheese, and a zesty dressing, this salad is a perfect addition to any meal or a satisfying standalone dish. Whether you’re hosting a summer barbecue, looking for a healthy lunch option, or simply want to elevate your salad game, this recipe is sure to impress your taste buds and your guests.

Ingredients

  • 4 large ripe tomatoes, diced
  • 1 English cucumber, diced
  • 1 red bell pepper, diced
  • 1 green bell pepper, diced
  • 1/2 red onion, thinly sliced
  • 1 cup Kalamata olives, pitted and halved
  • 8 ounces feta cheese, crumbled
  • 1/4 cup extra virgin olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons red wine vinegar
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • Freshly chopped parsley for garnish

Instructions

  1. In a large bowl, combine the diced tomatoes, cucumber, red and green bell peppers, and red onion. Gently toss the vegetables together to mix evenly.

  2. Add the Kalamata olives and crumbled feta cheese to the bowl, and gently toss to incorporate them with the vegetables.

  3. In a small bowl, whisk together the extra virgin olive oil, red wine vinegar, dried oregano, salt, and black pepper to create the dressing.

  4. Drizzle the dressing over the salad, using a large spoon or tongs to gently toss the salad until the dressing is evenly distributed.

  5. Once the salad is dressed, cover the bowl and refrigerate for at least 30 minutes to allow the flavors to meld together.

  6. Before serving, give the salad a final toss to ensure that the dressing coats all the ingredients. Garnish with freshly chopped parsley for a pop of color and an extra burst of flavor.

  7. Serve the Nut-Free Greek Salad as a side dish alongside grilled chicken, fish, or lamb, or enjoy it on its own as a light and satisfying meal.

Tips for Success

  • For the best flavor, use ripe, juicy tomatoes and crisp, fresh vegetables.
  • If you prefer a more pronounced tangy flavor, consider adding an extra splash of red wine vinegar to the dressing.
  • This salad can be prepared in advance and stored in the refrigerator for up to 24 hours. Just give it a gentle toss before serving to redistribute the dressing.

Why We Love This Recipe

This Nut-Free Greek Salad is a celebration of the vibrant colors and bold flavors of the Mediterranean. The combination of juicy tomatoes, crunchy cucumbers, and briny Kalamata olives creates a symphony of textures and tastes in every bite. The tangy feta cheese adds a creamy richness, while the zesty dressing ties everything together with a burst of freshness.

Not only is this salad a feast for the senses, but it’s also incredibly versatile. It’s perfect for picnics, potlucks, and outdoor gatherings, as it can be made ahead of time and easily transported. Additionally, it’s a fantastic option for anyone with nut allergies, as it delivers all the satisfaction of a Greek salad without the need for nuts or nut-based ingredients.

So, if you’re ready to elevate your salad game and delight your senses, give this Nut-Free Greek Salad a try. With its vibrant flavors and easy preparation, it’s sure to become a staple in your recipe collection. Enjoy!
