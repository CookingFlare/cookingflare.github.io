Love Potion No. 9 Lasagna Bolognese with Meatballs: A Valentine's Day Italian-Inspired Recipe


Valentine’s Day is a time to celebrate love, and what better way to do so than by indulging in a delicious and comforting Italian-inspired dish? This Love Potion No. 9 Lasagna Bolognese with Meatballs is a perfect way to show your loved one how much you care. The rich flavors, hearty meatballs, and layers of cheesy goodness will surely make this Valentine’s Day one to remember.

Ingredients:

For the Meatballs:

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1/2 cup breadcrumbs
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste

For the Bolognese Sauce:

  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 can (28 ounces) crushed tomatoes
  • 1/2 cup red wine
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste

For the Lasagna:

  • 12 lasagna noodles, cooked al dente
  • 3 cups ricotta cheese
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • Fresh basil leaves for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the Meatballs:

    • In a large bowl, combine the ground beef, breadcrumbs, Parmesan cheese, egg, garlic, oregano, salt, and pepper. Mix until well combined.
    • Shape the mixture into meatballs, about 1 inch in diameter.
    • In a large skillet, heat some olive oil over medium heat. Add the meatballs and cook until browned on all sides. Remove from the skillet and set aside.

  2. Make the Bolognese Sauce:

    • In the same skillet, add a tablespoon of olive oil if needed. Sauté the onion and garlic until softened.
    • Add the ground beef and cook until browned, breaking it up with a spoon.
    • Stir in the crushed tomatoes, red wine, basil, oregano, salt, and pepper. Simmer for about 20 minutes, allowing the flavors to meld together.

  3. Assemble the Lasagna:

    • Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
    • Spread a thin layer of the Bolognese sauce on the bottom of a 9x13-inch baking dish.
    • Arrange 4 lasagna noodles on top of the sauce.
    • Spread a layer of ricotta cheese over the noodles, followed by a layer of meatballs and some Bolognese sauce.
    • Sprinkle with mozzarella and Parmesan cheese. Repeat the layers, ending with a final layer of Bolognese sauce and cheese on top.

  4. Bake the Lasagna:

    • Cover the baking dish with foil and bake for 25 minutes.
    • Remove the foil and bake for an additional 25 minutes, or until the cheese is bubbly and golden brown.
    • Allow the lasagna to rest for 10 minutes before slicing.
    • Garnish with fresh basil leaves before serving.

Serving Suggestion:

This Love Potion No. 9 Lasagna Bolognese with Meatballs pairs beautifully with a glass of red wine and a side of garlic bread. The layers of flavors in this dish are sure to impress your loved one and create a memorable Valentine’s Day meal.

Conclusion:

Valentine’s Day is a time to cherish those we love, and what better way to do so than by sharing a homemade meal? This Love Potion No. 9 Lasagna Bolognese with Meatballs is a labor of love that will surely warm the hearts and bellies of those who gather around your table. So, this Valentine’s Day, embrace the Italian tradition of hearty and comforting food with this exquisite dish. Cheers to love, laughter, and good food!
