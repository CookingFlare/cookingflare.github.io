Budget-Friendly Italian Chicken Recipes: Affordable and Delicious


Italian cuisine is renowned for its rich flavors, aromas, and textures, yet it’s often associated with high prices and luxurious ingredients. However, with a little creativity and resourcefulness, you can create authentic Italian-inspired dishes that won’t break the bank. In this post, we’ll explore budget-friendly Italian chicken recipes that are both affordable and delicious.

Chicken Parmesan with Garlic Bread

This classic Italian-American dish is a staple for a reason. Breaded and fried chicken topped with melted mozzarella cheese and marinara sauce is a crowd-pleaser. To make it more budget-friendly, use boneless, skinless chicken breasts and substitute breadcrumbs with panko breadcrumbs.

Ingredients:

  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1 cup panko breadcrumbs
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 egg
  • 1 cup marinara sauce
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Italian seasoning
  • Salt and pepper

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 400°F (200°C).
  2. In a shallow dish, mix together panko breadcrumbs and Parmesan cheese.
  3. In a separate dish, beat the egg.
  4. Dip each chicken breast in the egg, then coat in the breadcrumb mixture.
  5. Place the coated chicken on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper.
  6. Drizzle with olive oil and sprinkle with garlic.
  7. Bake for 20-25 minutes or until cooked through.
  8. Top with marinara sauce and mozzarella cheese.
  9. Return to oven and bake for an additional 5-7 minutes or until cheese is melted and bubbly.

Chicken Fettuccine Alfredo

Alfredo sauce is a rich and creamy condiment that’s often associated with high-end Italian restaurants. However, with a few simple ingredients, you can create an authentic Alfredo sauce at home without breaking the bank.

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1 cup fettuccine pasta
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • Salt and pepper
  • Fresh parsley, chopped (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Cook fettuccine pasta according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a large skillet, melt butter over medium heat.
  3. Add garlic and cook for 1-2 minutes or until fragrant.
  4. Add heavy cream and bring to a simmer.
  5. Reduce heat to low and let simmer for 2-3 minutes or until sauce thickens.
  6. Stir in Parmesan cheese until melted and smooth.
  7. Add cooked fettuccine pasta to the skillet and toss with Alfredo sauce.
  8. Slice cooked chicken breasts into strips and add to pasta.
  9. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  10. Garnish with chopped parsley, if desired.

Chicken Marsala with Mushrooms

This earthy and savory dish is a staple of Italian cuisine. By using affordable ingredients like button mushrooms and Marsala wine, you can create a rich and flavorful sauce without breaking the bank.

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 2 cups button mushrooms, sliced
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/4 cup Marsala wine
  • 1 cup chicken broth
  • 1 tablespoon tomato paste
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • Salt and pepper

Instructions:

  1. Cook chicken breasts in a skillet over medium-high heat until browned on both sides. Remove from skillet and set aside.
  2. Add butter to the skillet and cook until melted.
  3. Add garlic and cook for 1-2 minutes or until fragrant.
  4. Add mushrooms and cook until tender, about 3-4 minutes.
  5. Add Marsala wine, chicken broth, tomato paste, and thyme to the skillet.
  6. Bring to a simmer and cook for 2-3 minutes or until sauce thickens.
  7. Return chicken breasts to the skillet and simmer until cooked through.
  8. Season with salt and pepper to taste.

These budget-friendly Italian chicken recipes are perfect for a weeknight dinner or a special occasion. With a little creativity and resourcefulness, you can create authentic Italian-inspired dishes that won’t break the bank.
