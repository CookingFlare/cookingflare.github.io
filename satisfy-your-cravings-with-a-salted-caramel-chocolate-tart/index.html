Satisfy Your Cravings with a Salted Caramel Chocolate Tart


Who can resist the divine combination of rich chocolate and luscious caramel? If you’re looking to satisfy your cravings with a dessert that will leave you wanting more, then look no further than this delectable Salted Caramel Chocolate Tart. With its buttery crust, smooth chocolate filling, and irresistible salted caramel drizzle, this tart is sure to become a favorite in your dessert repertoire.

Ingredients

For the crust:

  • 1 ½ cups of all-purpose flour
  • ½ cup of unsweetened cocoa powder
  • ½ cup of powdered sugar
  • ½ teaspoon of salt
  • ¾ cup of unsalted butter, cold and cubed

For the chocolate filling:

  • 1 ½ cups of heavy cream
  • 10 ounces of semisweet chocolate, finely chopped
  • 3 large eggs
  • ¼ cup of granulated sugar
  • 1 teaspoon of vanilla extract

For the salted caramel drizzle:

  • 1 cup of granulated sugar
  • ¼ cup of water
  • 6 tablespoons of unsalted butter
  • ½ cup of heavy cream
  • 1 teaspoon of sea salt

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C).

  2. In a food processor, combine the flour, cocoa powder, powdered sugar, and salt. Pulse a few times to mix everything together. Add the cold cubed butter and pulse until the mixture resembles coarse crumbs.

  3. Press the crust mixture evenly into the bottom and up the sides of a 9-inch tart pan with a removable bottom. Place the pan on a baking sheet and bake for 15-20 minutes, or until the crust is set. Remove from the oven and let it cool completely.

  4. In a medium saucepan, heat the heavy cream over medium heat until it begins to simmer. Remove from the heat and add the chopped chocolate. Let it sit for a minute or two, then whisk until smooth and well combined.

  5. In a separate bowl, whisk together the eggs, granulated sugar, and vanilla extract. Slowly pour the chocolate mixture into the egg mixture, whisking constantly until fully incorporated.

  6. Pour the chocolate filling into the cooled tart crust. Smooth the top with a spatula and bake for 20-25 minutes, or until the filling is set around the edges but slightly jiggly in the center. Remove from the oven and let it cool completely.

  7. While the tart is cooling, prepare the salted caramel drizzle. In a medium saucepan, combine the granulated sugar and water. Cook over medium heat, swirling the pan occasionally, until the sugar has dissolved and the mixture turns a deep amber color.

  8. Remove the saucepan from the heat and carefully add the butter and heavy cream. The mixture will bubble vigorously, so be cautious. Stir until smooth and well combined. Stir in the sea salt.

  9. Once the tart has cooled, drizzle the salted caramel over the top. You can use a spoon or a piping bag for more precision. Allow the caramel to set for a few minutes before serving.

Enjoy Every Decadent Bite!

Now that your Salted Caramel Chocolate Tart is ready, it’s time to indulge in its irresistible flavors. The buttery crust provides the perfect foundation for the velvety smooth chocolate filling, while the salted caramel drizzle adds a delightful contrast of flavors. Serve it with a dollop of whipped cream or a scoop of vanilla ice cream for an extra touch of indulgence.

This tart is perfect for any occasion, whether it’s a family gathering, a dinner party, or simply a treat for yourself. Its rich and decadent flavors are sure to impress your guests and leave them craving more. So go ahead, give in to your cravings and enjoy every bite of this heavenly Salted Caramel Chocolate Tart.
