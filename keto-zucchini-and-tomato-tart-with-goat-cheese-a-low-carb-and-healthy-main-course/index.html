Keto Zucchini and Tomato Tart with Goat Cheese: A Low-Carb and Healthy Main Course


Are you looking for a delicious, low-carb, and healthy main course that’s bursting with flavor? Look no further than this Keto Zucchini and Tomato Tart with Goat Cheese! This savory tart is not only a feast for the taste buds but also a visually stunning addition to any meal. Packed with nutritious ingredients and free from the guilt of excessive carbs, this recipe is perfect for those following a ketogenic lifestyle or anyone looking to enjoy a wholesome, satisfying meal.

Ingredients

For the Crust:

  • 2 cups almond flour
  • 1 egg
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder

For the Filling:

  • 2 medium zucchinis, thinly sliced
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 6 ounces goat cheese, crumbled
  • 1/4 cup fresh basil, chopped
  • 3 eggs
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Crust:

    • Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C).
    • In a large bowl, combine the almond flour, egg, olive oil, salt, and garlic powder. Mix until a dough forms.
    • Press the dough evenly into a greased tart pan, covering the bottom and sides. Use a fork to prick the bottom of the crust.
    • Bake the crust for 10-12 minutes or until lightly golden. Set aside to cool.

  2. Prepare the Filling:

    • In a skillet, lightly sauté the zucchini slices until they are just tender. Season with salt and pepper.
    • In a separate bowl, whisk together the eggs and heavy cream. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
    • Spread the sautéed zucchini slices evenly over the cooled crust. Scatter the halved cherry tomatoes on top.
    • Pour the egg and cream mixture over the vegetables, ensuring it is evenly distributed.
    • Sprinkle the crumbled goat cheese and chopped basil over the top of the tart.

  3. Bake the Tart:

    • Place the assembled tart in the preheated oven and bake for 25-30 minutes, or until the filling is set and the edges are golden brown.

  4. Serve and Enjoy:

    • Once the tart is baked to perfection, remove it from the oven and allow it to cool for a few minutes.
    • Slice the tart into wedges and serve warm, garnished with additional fresh basil if desired.

Nutritional Information

  • Serving Size: 1 slice (assuming 8 slices)
  • Calories: 250
  • Total Fat: 20g
  • Total Carbohydrates: 7g
  • Dietary Fiber: 3g
  • Protein: 10g

Conclusion

This Keto Zucchini and Tomato Tart with Goat Cheese is a delightful blend of flavors and textures that will satisfy your cravings while keeping your carb intake in check. Whether you’re hosting a dinner party or simply treating yourself to a special meal, this tart is a versatile dish that’s sure to impress. Embrace the goodness of fresh vegetables, creamy goat cheese, and the subtle nuttiness of almond flour in every bite. Elevate your low-carb cooking repertoire with this recipe and savor the joy of healthy indulgence!
