Flavorful One Pot Thai Green Curry


If you’re looking for a delicious and aromatic dish that will transport you to the vibrant streets of Thailand, look no further than this Flavorful One Pot Thai Green Curry recipe. Bursting with fragrant herbs, spices, and fresh vegetables, this curry is a harmonious blend of flavors that will tantalize your taste buds. Best of all, it’s a one-pot wonder, making it perfect for a quick and easy weeknight dinner.

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1 onion, thinly sliced
  • 3 garlic cloves, minced
  • 2 tablespoons Thai green curry paste
  • 1 can (14 ounces) coconut milk
  • 1 cup vegetable broth
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon fish sauce (optional for non-vegetarian version)
  • 1 tablespoon brown sugar
  • 1 medium-sized sweet potato, peeled and cubed
  • 1 red bell pepper, thinly sliced
  • 1 zucchini, sliced into half-moons
  • 1 cup broccoli florets
  • 1 cup snap peas
  • 1 cup baby corn, halved
  • 1 cup Thai basil leaves
  • Juice of 1 lime
  • Salt, to taste

Instructions

  1. In a large pot or Dutch oven, heat the vegetable oil over medium heat. Add the sliced onion and minced garlic, and sauté until the onion is translucent and fragrant.

  2. Add the Thai green curry paste to the pot and cook for a minute, stirring constantly to release the flavors.

  3. Pour in the coconut milk, vegetable broth, soy sauce, fish sauce (if using), and brown sugar. Stir well to combine the ingredients.

  4. Add the cubed sweet potato, sliced red bell pepper, zucchini, broccoli florets, snap peas, and baby corn to the pot. Stir gently to coat the vegetables with the curry sauce.

  5. Cover the pot and simmer the curry over low heat for 15-20 minutes, or until the vegetables are tender.

  6. Remove the pot from the heat and add the Thai basil leaves. Stir in the lime juice and season with salt to taste.

  7. Serve the Flavorful One Pot Thai Green Curry hot, garnished with additional Thai basil leaves, if desired. This curry pairs perfectly with steamed jasmine rice or fluffy naan bread.

Tips and Variations

  • For a spicier curry, add a few sliced Thai bird’s eye chilies or red chili flakes.
  • Customize the vegetables to your liking. You can add mushrooms, carrots, or even tofu for a vegetarian option.
  • Experiment with different proteins such as chicken, shrimp, or beef for a heartier curry.
  • Make a big batch of this curry and freeze the leftovers for a quick and delicious meal on busy days.

Enjoy the explosion of flavors and aromas in this Flavorful One Pot Thai Green Curry. With its easy preparation and captivating taste, this dish is sure to become a family favorite. So gather your ingredients, embrace the vibrant colors, and let the spices transport you to the enchanting world of Thai cuisine.
