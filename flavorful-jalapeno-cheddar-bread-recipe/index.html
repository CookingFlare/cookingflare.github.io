Flavorful Jalapeno Cheddar Bread Recipe


Bread baked with jalapeno peppers and cheddar cheese is a delightful appetizer or side dish that brings a spicy kick to your meal. This recipe is simple and straightforward, yielding a warm, crispy loaf full of flavor. Unsure of hitting the perfect balance of spiciness and taste? Fear not, as this comprehensive guide will lead you step by step through the process.

Ingredients

  • 3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 teaspoons active dry yeast
  • 2 teaspoons kosher salt
  • 2 tablespoons white sugar
  • 1.5 cups warm water
  • 2 large eggs
  • 6 jalapeno peppers, minced
  • 1 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1/2 cup vegetable oil
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder

Instructions

First things first, preheat your oven to 375 degrees Fahrenheit (190 degrees Celsius) while you gather your ingredients. In a large mixing bowl, combine 3 cups of all-purpose flour, 2 teaspoons of active dry yeast, 2 teaspoons of kosher salt, and 2 tablespoons of white sugar. Gradually pour in 1.5 cups of warm water, mixing until a smooth and slightly sticky dough forms.

Next, lightly coat your hands and the surface of your dough with vegetable oil. This prevents the dough from sticking when kneaded. Turn the dough out onto a lightly oiled surface and knead until it becomes smooth and elastic, roughly five minutes or so. As you knead, add more flour if necessary to prevent sticking.

Time to introduce the enticing flavors! Fold in 6 minced jalapeno peppers, a full cup of shredded cheddar cheese, and a teaspoon of garlic powder into the dough. Ensure all ingredients are evenly distributed. By incorporating these elements at this stage, you’re allowing flavors to meld together before the final bake.

Shape the dough into a ball and place it into a lightly greased loaf pan. Be sure to tap the pan lightly and gently on the counter to release any large air pockets.

Cover the loaf pan with a damp towel and keep it in a draft-free location for approximately 30 minutes, or until it has doubled in size. It’s crucial to maintain proper humidity and warmth during this resting period; if necessary, place a towel over the pan or use a microwave-steaming method.

Once your dough has risen, preheat your oven to 375 degrees Fahrenheit (190 degrees Celsius). Mix together the remaining vegetable oil with two beaten eggs, and use this mixture to brush over the dough. This step adds color and a soft crust.

Pop your loaf into the oven and bake until golden brown, approximately 30-45 minutes depending on your oven. Upon completion, remove your jalapeno cheddar bread from the pan and allow it to cool on a wire rack.

Slice your bread while still warm to reveal gooey, melted cheddar cheese inside. Enjoy your savory snacking experience, and if you’re looking for those remnants of balance exquisitely tailored into this recipe, rejoice. The natural sweetness of garlic and jalapenos perfectly complements the sharp, buttery provocation of cheddar cheese.

Happy baking, and remember to savor every spicy, cheesy bite!
