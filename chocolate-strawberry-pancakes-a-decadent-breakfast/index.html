Chocolate Strawberry Pancakes: A Decadent Breakfast


Who says breakfast has to be boring? If you’re looking to start your day with a touch of indulgence, then look no further than these mouthwatering Chocolate Strawberry Pancakes. This recipe takes the classic pancake and elevates it to a whole new level of deliciousness. So grab your apron and get ready to embark on a breakfast adventure!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons cocoa powder
  • 2 tablespoons granulated sugar
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup buttermilk
  • 1/4 cup milk
  • 1 large egg
  • 2 tablespoons unsalted butter, melted
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/2 cup chopped strawberries
  • 1/4 cup chocolate chips

Instructions

  1. In a large mixing bowl, whisk together the all-purpose flour, cocoa powder, granulated sugar, baking powder, baking soda, and salt. This dry mixture will be the base of our pancake batter.

  2. In a separate bowl, combine the buttermilk, milk, egg, melted butter, and vanilla extract. Whisk until well blended.

  3. Slowly pour the wet mixture into the dry mixture, stirring gently until just combined. Be careful not to overmix, as this can result in tough pancakes.

  4. Gently fold in the chopped strawberries and chocolate chips, distributing them evenly throughout the batter.

  5. Heat a non-stick skillet or griddle over medium heat. Lightly grease the surface with butter or cooking spray.

  6. Using a 1/4 cup measuring cup, pour the pancake batter onto the skillet, forming round pancakes. Cook for 2-3 minutes, or until bubbles start to form on the surface.

  7. Carefully flip the pancakes using a spatula, and cook for an additional 1-2 minutes, or until golden brown and cooked through.

  8. Remove the pancakes from the skillet and serve immediately. You can stack them up high or serve them individually, depending on your preference.

  9. For an extra touch of decadence, top your Chocolate Strawberry Pancakes with a dollop of whipped cream, a sprinkle of powdered sugar, and a few extra slices of fresh strawberries.

Why You’ll Love These Pancakes

These Chocolate Strawberry Pancakes are a treat for the senses. The rich cocoa flavor combined with the sweetness of the strawberries creates a delightful combination that will satisfy any chocolate lover’s cravings. The addition of chocolate chips adds an extra burst of decadence to every bite.

Not only do these pancakes taste amazing, but they also look stunning on the plate. The vibrant red of the strawberries contrasted with the dark chocolate chips is a feast for the eyes. They’re sure to impress your family and friends at brunch or any special occasion.

But the best part is that these pancakes are incredibly easy to make. With just a few simple ingredients and some basic cooking techniques, you can whip up a batch of these indulgent breakfast treats in no time.

So why settle for a plain old pancake when you can have a Chocolate Strawberry Pancake extravaganza? Treat yourself to a decadent breakfast and start your day on a sweet note.
