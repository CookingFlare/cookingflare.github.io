Get Your Sparkle On: 7 Delicious Mocktail Recipes with Sparkling Water


Are you looking for refreshing and flavorful mocktail recipes that will make you feel like you’re sipping on something special? Look no further! In this post, we’ll share with you 7 delicious mocktail recipes that are perfect for any occasion. The best part? They all feature the invigorating fizz of sparkling water, adding a touch of effervescence to your drink. So let’s get started and get your sparkle on!

1. Sparkling Watermelon Mojito Mocktail

Preparation time: 10 minutes | Serves: 2

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups fresh watermelon chunks
  • 8-10 fresh mint leaves
  • 1 lime, juiced
  • 1 tablespoon honey
  • 1 cup sparkling water
  • Ice cubes

Instructions:

  1. In a blender, puree the watermelon chunks until smooth.
  2. In a glass, muddle the mint leaves to release their flavor.
  3. Add the lime juice and honey to the glass and stir well.
  4. Divide the watermelon puree between two glasses.
  5. Fill the glasses with ice cubes and top with sparkling water.
  6. Stir gently and garnish with a sprig of mint. Enjoy!

2. Sparkling Peach Bellini Mocktail

Preparation time: 5 minutes | Serves: 2

Ingredients:

  • 1 ripe peach, peeled and pitted
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 1 tablespoon simple syrup
  • 1 cup sparkling water
  • Ice cubes

Instructions:

  1. In a blender, puree the ripe peach until smooth.
  2. Add the lemon juice and simple syrup to the blender and blend for a few more seconds.
  3. Divide the peach mixture between two glasses.
  4. Fill the glasses with ice cubes and top with sparkling water.
  5. Stir gently and garnish with a peach slice. Cheers!

7. Sparkling Pineapple Ginger Mocktail

Preparation time: 5 minutes | Serves: 2

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup fresh pineapple chunks
  • 1-inch piece of fresh ginger, peeled
  • 1 tablespoon honey
  • 1 cup sparkling water
  • Ice cubes

Instructions:

  1. In a blender, puree the fresh pineapple chunks and ginger until smooth.
  2. Add the honey to the blender and blend for a few more seconds.
  3. Divide the pineapple-ginger mixture between two glasses.
  4. Fill the glasses with ice cubes and top with sparkling water.
  5. Stir gently and garnish with a pineapple chunk. Enjoy the tropical zing!
