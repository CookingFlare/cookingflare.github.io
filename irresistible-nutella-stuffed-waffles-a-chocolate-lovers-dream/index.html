Irresistible Nutella Stuffed Waffles: A Chocolate Lover's Dream


Are you a chocolate lover? Do you dream about indulging in a rich and decadent treat that will satisfy your sweet tooth? Look no further than these irresistible Nutella stuffed waffles! This mouthwatering recipe combines the crispy goodness of waffles with the creamy, chocolatey goodness of Nutella, creating a heavenly combination that will leave you craving for more.

Ingredients

To make these delectable Nutella stuffed waffles, you will need the following ingredients:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons granulated sugar
  • 1 tablespoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 3/4 cups milk
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, melted
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • Nutella, for stuffing

Instructions

  1. In a large mixing bowl, whisk together the flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt. Make a well in the center of the dry ingredients.

  2. In a separate bowl, beat the eggs until well combined. Add the milk, melted butter, and vanilla extract to the beaten eggs. Mix well.

  3. Pour the wet ingredients into the well of the dry ingredients. Stir until just combined. Be careful not to overmix, as this can result in tough waffles.

  4. Preheat your waffle iron according to its instructions. Once preheated, lightly grease the waffle iron with non-stick cooking spray or melted butter.

  5. Spoon a generous amount of waffle batter onto the center of the preheated waffle iron. Use a spatula to spread the batter evenly, leaving a small border around the edges.

  6. Place a dollop of Nutella in the center of the batter. Be sure not to add too much, as it may ooze out during cooking. Fold the edges of the batter over the Nutella, sealing it inside.

  7. Close the waffle iron and cook according to the manufacturer’s instructions, or until the waffles are golden brown and crispy.

  8. Carefully remove the stuffed waffles from the iron and place them on a wire rack to cool slightly. Repeat the process with the remaining batter and Nutella.

  9. Serve the Nutella stuffed waffles warm, with a sprinkle of powdered sugar or a drizzle of chocolate syrup. You can also add fresh berries or whipped cream for an extra touch of indulgence.

Tips and Variations

  • If you don’t have a waffle iron, you can still enjoy this recipe by making it into pancakes. Simply spoon the batter onto a hot griddle, add the Nutella, and flip once the edges are set.
  • Experiment with different fillings to create your own unique twist on this recipe. Peanut butter, caramel, or even sliced bananas would all be delicious choices.
  • Make a batch of Nutella stuffed waffles ahead of time and freeze them. When you’re in need of a quick and satisfying breakfast or snack, simply pop them in the toaster or microwave for a few seconds.

Final Thoughts

These Nutella stuffed waffles are a chocolate lover’s dream come true. The combination of crispy waffles and oozing Nutella is simply irresistible. Whether you enjoy them for breakfast, brunch, or dessert, they are sure to satisfy your cravings and leave you wanting more. So go ahead, indulge in this decadent treat and experience pure chocolate bliss. You won’t be disappointed!
