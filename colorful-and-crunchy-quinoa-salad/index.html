Colorful and Crunchy Quinoa Salad


Are you looking for a delicious and nutritious salad recipe that’s bursting with color and flavor? Look no further than this Colorful and Crunchy Quinoa Salad! Packed with protein, fiber, and a rainbow of fresh vegetables, this salad is not only a feast for the eyes but also a treat for your taste buds. Whether you’re a quinoa enthusiast or just looking to incorporate more wholesome ingredients into your diet, this recipe is sure to become a favorite in your meal rotation.

Ingredients

For the Salad:

  • 1 cup quinoa, rinsed
  • 2 cups water or vegetable broth
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1 cup cucumber, diced
  • 1 red bell pepper, diced
  • 1 yellow bell pepper, diced
  • 1/2 cup red onion, finely chopped
  • 1/2 cup fresh parsley, chopped
  • 1/4 cup fresh mint, chopped
  • 1/2 cup crumbled feta cheese (optional)

For the Dressing:

  • 1/4 cup extra virgin olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons lemon juice
  • 1 tablespoon red wine vinegar
  • 1 teaspoon honey or maple syrup
  • 1 garlic clove, minced
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Cook the Quinoa: In a medium saucepan, bring the water or vegetable broth to a boil. Add the quinoa, reduce the heat to low, cover, and simmer for 15-20 minutes, or until the quinoa is tender and the liquid is absorbed. Once cooked, fluff the quinoa with a fork and allow it to cool to room temperature.

  2. Prepare the Vegetables: While the quinoa is cooling, prepare the vegetables by washing and chopping them as indicated in the ingredients list.

  3. Make the Dressing: In a small bowl, whisk together the olive oil, lemon juice, red wine vinegar, honey or maple syrup, minced garlic, salt, and pepper until well combined.

  4. Assemble the Salad: In a large mixing bowl, combine the cooked and cooled quinoa, cherry tomatoes, cucumber, red and yellow bell peppers, red onion, parsley, and mint. If using, add the crumbled feta cheese. Pour the dressing over the salad and gently toss until all the ingredients are evenly coated.

  5. Chill and Serve: Cover the salad and refrigerate for at least 30 minutes to allow the flavors to meld together. Before serving, give the salad a final toss and adjust the seasoning if necessary. Enjoy the Colorful and Crunchy Quinoa Salad as a light meal on its own or as a refreshing side dish with your favorite grilled protein.

Nutritional Benefits

This Colorful and Crunchy Quinoa Salad is not only a delight for the palate but also a nutritional powerhouse. Quinoa is a complete protein, providing all nine essential amino acids, and is also a good source of fiber, iron, and magnesium. The assortment of fresh vegetables adds a variety of vitamins, minerals, and antioxidants, while the olive oil in the dressing contributes heart-healthy monounsaturated fats. This salad is a wonderful way to nourish your body with wholesome, plant-based ingredients.

Conclusion

In conclusion, this Colorful and Crunchy Quinoa Salad is a celebration of vibrant colors, satisfying textures, and delightful flavors. Whether you’re preparing it for a summer picnic, a light lunch, or a potluck gathering, this salad is guaranteed to impress. With its nutrient-dense ingredients and simple preparation, it’s a recipe that aligns perfectly with a healthy and balanced lifestyle. Give this recipe a try, and let the wholesome goodness of this salad brighten up your table!
