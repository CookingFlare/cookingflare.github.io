Discover the Secret World of 10 Exotic Juice and Smoothie Recipes


As the sun rises on another day, so does our delicious Acai Berry Sunrise Smoothie! This vibrant concoction starts with a cup full of powerful acai berries and blends in healthy banana and a zesty squeeze of fresh lemon juice. Add in a handful of spinach to make this a green power house drink that’s as good for you as it tastes!

Instructions:

  • Place a cup of frozen acai berries, a ripe banana, half a cup of fresh spinach, and half a lemon’s worth of juice in a blender.
  • Blend until smooth and pour into glasses. Serve immediately and enjoy!

2. Avocado Mint Smoothie

Cool down with a unique Avocado-Mint Smoothie that combines creamy avocado with the freshness of mint. This concoction is rich in healthy fats, Vitamin E, and antioxidants, making this smoothie the perfect afternoon pick-me-up.

Instructions:

  • Toss two cups of fresh spinach, a handful of fresh mint leaves, a ripe avocado, and half a cucumber into your blender.
  • Blend until smooth and add a little water if necessary to reach desired consistency. Sip and savor!

3. Blue Majik Almond Milk Smoothie

Blue Majik, an algae that’s packed with nutrients, takes the spotlight in this refreshing almond milk smoothie. Paired with tangy pineapple and sweet berries, you’ll be transported to a world of tropical bliss.

Instructions:

  • Combine a scoop of Blue Majik concentrate, one cup of unsweetened almond milk, a frozen banana, and a handful of mixed berries in your blender.
  • Blend until smooth, pour, and raise your glass to a healthy and beachy state of mind!

4. Cauliflower and Kale Green Juice

Revamp your morning routine with a revitalizing Cauliflower and Kale Green Juice. This nutrient-dense beverage features cauliflower for a Creamsicle twist on two of nature’s most nutrient-rich greens.

Instructions:

  • Blend together a head of cauliflower, a palm-full of kale, a stalk of celery, a few slices of ginger, and a lemon in your juicer.
  • Give it a taste and adjust to your preference with more ginger or a bit of honey if it’s too tart.

5. Carrot Cake Smoothie

Who says you have to save dessert for the end of the meal when you can have a Carrot Cake Smoothie instead? You won’t even miss the fork with this delicious blend of creamy protein-packed tofu, sweet carrots, and warming spices.

Instructions:

  • Mix together a cup of cooked, mashed carrots, a cup of silken tofu, a half cup of almond milk, a tablespoon of maple syrup, a teaspoon each of cinnamon and ginger, and a handful of chopped walnuts in your blender.
  • Blend until thick and creamy, and grab a sip of this guilt-free delight.

6. Cherry Lime Basil Smoothie

Refreshing cherry and lime do a little dance with sweet basil leaves in this Cherry Lime Basil Smoothie. This surprising combination will awaken your senses and refresh your spirit.

Instructions:

  • Combine a cup of frozen cherries, fresh lime juice, and a handful of fresh basil leaves in your blender.
  • Top with sparkling water, some ice cubes, and blend until frothy. A flavorful and invigorating way to start your day!

7. Chocolate Non-Dairy Pudding Smoothie

Bring on the chocolate without any guilt! This Non-Dairy Chocolate Pudding Smoothie will satisfy your chocolate cravings for good while providing maximum nutrition.

Instructions:

  • Toss a ripe banana, a tablespoon of cocoa powder, a cup of unsweetened almond milk, and a tablespoon of non-dairy yogurt in a blender.
  • Blend until smooth and drizzle with some almond butter for an added treat.

8. Coconut Pineapple Turmeric Smoothie

Tropical paradise in a glass, the Coconut Pineapple Turmeric Smoothie transports you to a paradisiacal state with every sip. The luxurious addition of turmeric brings a golden touch that not only makes this smoothie gorgeous, but also packs a kick of anti-inflammatory spice.

Instructions:

  • Mix together three-quarters cup of unsweetened coconut flakes, half a cup of unsweetened shredded coconut, a cup of pineapple chunks, a tablespoon of turmeric, and a cup of coconut water in a blender.
  • Blend until smooth and delight in this sunshine in a glass!

9. Crimson Clover Smoothie Bowl

Remember the beauty and magic of spring with this Crimson Clover Smoothie Bowl. With the vibrant color and rich taste, it’s like summer in a bowl, just in the middle of wintertime.

Instructions:

  • Blend a cup of frozen red clover blossoms, a frozen banana, a half a cup of unsweetened almond milk, and a tablespoon of honey until smooth, then pour into a bowl.
  • Top with fresh fruits, nuts, and seeds for added crunch and fun.

10. Fennel Apple Ginger Smoothie

Don’t shy away from aniseed: embrace it in this refreshing Fennel Apple Ginger Smoothie that’s as surprising as it is delicious. With a bright, zesty twist from ginger, you’ll want to add this energizing concoction to your regular rotation.

Instructions:

  • Toss together a bulb of fennel, an apple, a thumb-sized piece of fresh ginger, and half a lemon in your blender.
  • Blend until smooth, sip, and savor every invigorating sip!

Delving into this incredible world of flavors and exciting ingredients, you’ll discover the unexpected joy these exotic juice and smoothie recipes bring. So, put your blender to the test, experiment with these recipes, and make your taste buds dance. Cheers to healthy and happy days!
