The Best Italian-Style Chicken and Mushroom Creamy Pasta Recipe


If you’re craving a delicious and comforting Italian-style dish, this Chicken and Mushroom Creamy Pasta recipe is sure to satisfy your taste buds. Packed with flavors and textures that will transport you straight to Italy, this dish is perfect for a cozy night in or for impressing your dinner guests.

Ingredients

  • 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 8 ounces of mushrooms, sliced
  • 4 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 1 cup of heavy cream
  • 1/2 cup of chicken broth
  • 1/2 cup of grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 tablespoon of butter
  • 1 tablespoon of olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon of dried Italian herbs
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 12 ounces of your favorite pasta, cooked al dente

Instructions

  1. Start by seasoning the chicken breasts with salt, pepper, and dried Italian herbs. In a large skillet, heat the olive oil over medium-high heat. Add the chicken breasts and cook for about 6-8 minutes on each side, or until they are cooked through. Remove the chicken from the skillet and set aside.

  2. In the same skillet, melt the butter over medium heat. Add the onions and garlic, and sauté until they become fragrant and translucent. Then, add the sliced mushrooms and cook until they are golden brown and tender.

  3. Pour in the chicken broth and let it simmer for a couple of minutes, allowing the flavors to meld together. Reduce the heat to low and add the heavy cream. Stir well to combine all the ingredients.

  4. Bring the sauce to a gentle simmer and let it cook for about 5 minutes, or until it thickens slightly. Stir in the grated Parmesan cheese, allowing it to melt into the sauce and create a creamy consistency.

  5. While the sauce is simmering, slice the cooked chicken breasts into thin strips. Add the chicken strips to the skillet and stir them into the sauce, ensuring they are evenly coated.

  6. Finally, add the cooked pasta to the skillet and toss it gently to combine with the creamy sauce and chicken. Make sure all the pasta is coated with the sauce.

  7. Serve the Italian-style Chicken and Mushroom Creamy Pasta hot, garnished with some fresh chopped parsley and additional grated Parmesan cheese if desired.

Tips and Variations

  • For an extra burst of flavor, you can add some sun-dried tomatoes or roasted red peppers to the sauce.
  • If you prefer a lighter version, you can substitute half-and-half or milk for the heavy cream.
  • Feel free to experiment with different types of mushrooms, such as cremini, shiitake, or oyster mushrooms, to add more depth to the dish.
  • If you’re a fan of spice, you can sprinkle some red pepper flakes over the pasta before serving.

This Italian-style Chicken and Mushroom Creamy Pasta is a delightful combination of tender chicken, earthy mushrooms, and a luscious creamy sauce. With its rich flavors and comforting appeal, it’s a dish that is sure to become a favorite in your household. Enjoy it on its own or pair it with a side salad and some crusty bread for a complete meal. Buon appetito!
