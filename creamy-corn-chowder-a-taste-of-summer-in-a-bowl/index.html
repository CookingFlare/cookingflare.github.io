Creamy Corn Chowder: A Taste of Summer in a Bowl


As the sun shines bright and the days grow longer, there’s nothing quite like a bowl of creamy corn chowder to capture the essence of summer. This classic comfort food is a celebration of fresh, sweet corn and the bountiful produce that graces our tables during the warmer months. Whether you’re hosting a backyard barbecue, planning a picnic in the park, or simply craving a taste of summer, this creamy corn chowder is sure to delight your taste buds and bring warmth to your soul.

Ingredients

  • 6 ears of fresh corn, husked and kernels removed
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 4 slices of bacon, chopped
  • 4 cups of chicken or vegetable broth
  • 2 potatoes, peeled and diced
  • 1 cup of heavy cream
  • 1 teaspoon of fresh thyme leaves
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Chopped fresh chives for garnish

Instructions

  1. Cook the Bacon: In a large pot over medium heat, cook the chopped bacon until it’s crispy and golden brown. Use a slotted spoon to transfer the bacon to a paper towel-lined plate, leaving the bacon drippings in the pot.

  2. Sauté the Aromatics: Add the chopped onion to the pot with the bacon drippings and cook until it’s translucent. Stir in the minced garlic and cook for another minute, allowing the fragrant aromas to fill the air.

  3. Simmer the Chowder Base: Add the corn kernels to the pot and cook for a few minutes, allowing them to release their natural sweetness. Pour in the chicken or vegetable broth, then add the diced potatoes and fresh thyme leaves. Bring the mixture to a gentle boil, then reduce the heat and let it simmer until the potatoes are tender.

  4. Blend the Chowder: Using an immersion blender, carefully blend the chowder until it reaches your desired consistency. For a creamier texture, you can blend a portion of the chowder while leaving some of the corn and potatoes whole. If you don’t have an immersion blender, you can transfer a portion of the chowder to a blender and carefully blend it in batches.

  5. Add the Cream: Stir in the heavy cream, allowing it to gently warm through the chowder without boiling. Season with salt and pepper to taste, adjusting the seasoning to suit your preference.

  6. Serve and Enjoy: Ladle the creamy corn chowder into bowls, garnish with the crispy bacon and chopped fresh chives, and serve it alongside crusty bread or a simple green salad for a complete meal.

Tips for Success

  • Fresh Corn: While you can use frozen or canned corn in a pinch, nothing beats the sweet, fresh flavor of corn straight from the cob. Look for locally grown corn at your farmers’ market or grocery store for the best results.

  • Customize Your Chowder: Feel free to add your own twist to this creamy corn chowder. You can incorporate diced carrots, bell peppers, or even a splash of hot sauce for a spicy kick.

  • Make It Ahead: This creamy corn chowder can be made ahead of time and reheated gently on the stovetop. It also freezes well, so you can enjoy a taste of summer even when the days grow colder.

In Conclusion

Creamy corn chowder is a versatile and satisfying dish that captures the essence of summer in a bowl. Its velvety texture, sweet corn flavor, and comforting warmth make it a perfect addition to your seasonal menu. Whether you’re cooking for a crowd or simply craving a taste of nostalgia, this creamy corn chowder is a delightful way to savor the flavors of summer. So, gather your fresh corn, sharpen your knives, and get ready to indulge in a bowl of summer’s bounty.
