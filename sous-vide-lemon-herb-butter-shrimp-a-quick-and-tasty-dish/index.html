Sous Vide Lemon Herb Butter Shrimp: A Quick and Tasty Dish


Are you craving a delicious seafood dish that is both quick and easy to make? Look no further than Sous Vide Lemon Herb Butter Shrimp! This mouthwatering recipe combines the delicate flavors of fresh shrimp, zesty lemon, aromatic herbs, and rich butter, resulting in a dish that is bursting with flavor. And the best part? It can be prepared in just a few simple steps using the sous vide cooking method.

What is Sous Vide Cooking?

Before we dive into the recipe, let’s quickly go over what sous vide cooking is all about. Sous vide is a French cooking technique that involves vacuum-sealing food in a bag and cooking it in a temperature-controlled water bath. This method ensures precise and consistent results, allowing the flavors of the ingredients to shine through.

Ingredients

To make this delectable dish, you will need the following ingredients:

  • 1 pound of fresh shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 2 tablespoons of butter, melted
  • 1 lemon, thinly sliced
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon of fresh parsley, chopped
  • 1 tablespoon of fresh dill, chopped
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Preheat your sous vide water bath to 135°F (57°C). This temperature will give the shrimp a tender and juicy texture.
  2. In a small bowl, combine the melted butter, minced garlic, chopped parsley, and chopped dill. Mix well to create a flavorful herb butter mixture.
  3. Season the shrimp with salt and pepper to taste, ensuring that each piece is evenly coated.
  4. Place the seasoned shrimp in a vacuum-sealed bag, arranging them in a single layer.
  5. Pour the herb butter mixture over the shrimp, ensuring that each piece is coated. Add the thinly sliced lemon on top.
  6. Seal the bag using a vacuum sealer or the water displacement method, making sure to remove as much air as possible.
  7. Gently place the sealed bag into the preheated water bath and cook for 30 minutes.
  8. Once the cooking time is up, carefully remove the bag from the water bath and transfer the shrimp to a serving dish.
  9. Garnish with additional fresh herbs and lemon slices if desired.
  10. Serve the Sous Vide Lemon Herb Butter Shrimp immediately and enjoy!

Serving Suggestions

This dish can be enjoyed on its own as a flavorful appetizer or paired with your favorite side dishes. Here are a few serving suggestions to inspire you:

  • Serve the shrimp over a bed of fluffy white rice or buttered noodles for a satisfying main course.
  • Toss the shrimp with cooked pasta, cherry tomatoes, and a drizzle of olive oil for a refreshing pasta salad.
  • Wrap the shrimp in warm tortillas with shredded lettuce, diced tomatoes, and a squeeze of fresh lime juice for a mouthwatering shrimp taco.

Tips and Tricks

  • If you don’t have a sous vide machine, you can still enjoy this recipe by using a stovetop water bath. Simply fill a large pot with water and attach a thermometer to monitor the temperature. Adjust the heat as needed to maintain a constant temperature.
  • Feel free to experiment with different herbs and spices to customize the flavor of the dish. Thyme, basil, or even a pinch of chili flakes can add an extra kick.
  • For an extra burst of flavor, marinate the shrimp in the herb butter mixture for a few hours before cooking. This will allow the flavors to infuse into the shrimp, resulting in a more intense taste.

In Conclusion

Sous Vide Lemon Herb Butter Shrimp is a quick and tasty dish that is sure to impress your family and friends. By using the sous vide cooking method, you can achieve perfectly cooked shrimp that is tender, juicy, and bursting with flavor. So why wait? Give this recipe a try and elevate your seafood game to a whole new level!
