Gluten-Free Apple Crisp: A Warm and Comforting Dessert


Are you looking for a delicious dessert that is both gluten-free and comforting? Look no further! This gluten-free apple crisp recipe is the perfect treat to satisfy your sweet tooth and warm your soul. Made with fresh apples, warm spices, and a crispy topping, this dessert is sure to become a favorite in your household.

Ingredients

For the filling:

  • 5 cups of peeled and sliced apples (about 4-5 medium-sized apples)
  • 1/4 cup of granulated sugar
  • 2 tablespoons of cornstarch
  • 1 teaspoon of ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 teaspoon of ground nutmeg
  • 1 tablespoon of lemon juice

For the topping:

  • 1 cup of gluten-free rolled oats
  • 1/2 cup of almond flour
  • 1/4 cup of packed brown sugar
  • 1/4 teaspoon of salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon of ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 cup of cold unsalted butter, cut into small pieces

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C). Grease a 9x9-inch baking dish and set aside.

  2. In a large bowl, combine the sliced apples, granulated sugar, cornstarch, ground cinnamon, ground nutmeg, and lemon juice. Toss until the apples are evenly coated with the mixture.

  3. Transfer the apple mixture to the prepared baking dish and spread it out evenly.

  4. In a separate bowl, combine the gluten-free rolled oats, almond flour, brown sugar, salt, and ground cinnamon. Mix well.

  5. Add the cold unsalted butter to the bowl and use your fingers to work it into the dry ingredients until the mixture resembles coarse crumbs.

  6. Sprinkle the topping evenly over the apple mixture in the baking dish.

  7. Bake in the preheated oven for about 35-40 minutes, or until the top is golden brown and the apples are tender when pierced with a fork.

  8. Remove from the oven and allow the gluten-free apple crisp to cool for a few minutes before serving.

  9. Serve warm, preferably with a scoop of vanilla ice cream or a dollop of whipped cream for an extra indulgence.

Tips and Variations

  • Feel free to experiment with different types of apples for this recipe. Granny Smith apples provide a tart flavor, while Honeycrisp or Gala apples add a touch of sweetness.
  • If you don’t have almond flour on hand, you can substitute it with an equal amount of gluten-free all-purpose flour or even regular all-purpose flour if you don’t have any dietary restrictions.
  • For added texture and flavor, consider adding a handful of chopped nuts, such as pecans or walnuts, to the topping mixture before baking.
  • To make this recipe vegan, simply substitute the butter with vegan butter or coconut oil, and use a plant-based sweetener instead of granulated sugar.

Enjoy Your Gluten-Free Apple Crisp!

There you have it – a delicious and comforting gluten-free apple crisp recipe that is sure to impress your family and friends. The combination of sweet and tart apples, warm spices, and a crispy topping creates a dessert that is both satisfying and full of flavor. Whether you’re serving it as a weeknight treat or as the grand finale to a special meal, this gluten-free apple crisp is guaranteed to be a hit. So, go ahead and give it a try – your taste buds will thank you!
