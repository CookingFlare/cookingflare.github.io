Wrap It Up: 5 Delicious and Easy Vegan Wrap Recipes


If you’re tired of the same old sandwiches for lunch or dinner, it’s time to wrap things up with these 5 delicious and easy vegan wrap recipes. Wraps are a versatile and convenient option that allows you to pack in a variety of flavors and textures. Plus, they’re perfect for on-the-go meals or a quick bite at home. So, let’s dive into these mouthwatering recipes and get wrapping!

1. Mediterranean Chickpea Wrap

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup cooked chickpeas
  • 1/2 cup diced cucumber
  • 1/2 cup diced tomatoes
  • 1/4 cup sliced black olives
  • 2 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 2 tablespoons tahini
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 4 whole wheat tortillas

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, mash the chickpeas using a fork until slightly chunky.
  2. Add cucumber, tomatoes, black olives, parsley, lemon juice, tahini, salt, and pepper. Mix well.
  3. Warm the tortillas in a pan or microwave.
  4. Divide the chickpea mixture evenly among the tortillas and wrap tightly.

2. Spicy Buffalo Cauliflower Wrap

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups cauliflower florets
  • 2 tablespoons hot sauce
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon paprika
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 4 large lettuce leaves
  • 4 whole wheat tortillas
  • Vegan ranch dressing (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 425°F (220°C).
  2. In a large bowl, combine hot sauce, olive oil, garlic powder, paprika, salt, and pepper.
  3. Toss the cauliflower florets in the sauce until well coated.
  4. Spread the coated cauliflower on a baking sheet and roast for 20-25 minutes, or until crispy.
  5. Place a lettuce leaf on each tortilla.
  6. Divide the roasted cauliflower among the tortillas and drizzle with vegan ranch dressing if desired. Roll up tightly.

3. Asian Tofu and Vegetable Wrap

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 cup sliced tofu
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon rice vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon maple syrup
  • 1 teaspoon sesame oil
  • 1 cup shredded cabbage
  • 1/2 cup shredded carrots
  • 1/4 cup sliced green onions
  • 4 whole wheat tortillas

Instructions:

  1. In a small bowl, whisk together soy sauce, rice vinegar, maple syrup, and sesame oil.
  2. Marinate the tofu in the mixture for at least 30 minutes.
  3. Heat a pan over medium heat and cook the tofu until golden brown.
  4. Warm the tortillas in a pan or microwave.
  5. Divide the tofu, shredded cabbage, shredded carrots, and sliced green onions among the tortillas. Wrap tightly.

4. BBQ Jackfruit Wrap

Ingredients:

  • 1 can young jackfruit in brine, drained and rinsed
  • 1/2 cup BBQ sauce
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1/2 teaspoon smoked paprika
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 4 whole wheat tortillas
  • Vegan coleslaw (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Shred the jackfruit using your hands or a fork.
  2. Heat olive oil in a pan over medium heat and add the shredded jackfruit.
  3. Cook for 5 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  4. Add BBQ sauce, smoked paprika, salt, and pepper. Cook for another 5 minutes.
  5. Warm the tortillas in a pan or microwave.
  6. Divide the BBQ jackfruit among the tortillas and top with vegan coleslaw if desired. Roll up tightly.

5. Greek Hummus Wrap

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 cup hummus
  • 1/2 cup sliced cucumber
  • 1/2 cup diced tomatoes
  • 1/4 cup sliced Kalamata olives
  • 2 tablespoons chopped fresh dill
  • Juice of 1/2 lemon
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 4 whole wheat tortillas

Instructions:

  1. Spread a generous amount of hummus on each tortilla.
  2. Top with cucumber, tomatoes, Kalamata olives, fresh dill, lemon juice, salt, and pepper.
  3. Fold in the sides of the tortilla and roll up tightly.

Enjoy these 5 delicious and easy vegan wrap recipes that are sure to satisfy your taste buds and keep you fueled throughout the day. Whether you’re a fan of Mediterranean flavors, spicy buffalo cauliflower, Asian-inspired tofu, BBQ jackfruit, or Greek hummus, there’s a wrap for everyone. So, get creative, experiment with different fillings, and wrap it up in style!
