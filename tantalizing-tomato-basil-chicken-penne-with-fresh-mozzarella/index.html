Tantalizing Tomato Basil Chicken Penne with Fresh Mozzarella


If you’re craving a comforting yet vibrant pasta dish that’s bursting with flavor, look no further than this tantalizing Tomato Basil Chicken Penne with Fresh Mozzarella recipe. This dish is a celebration of fresh, wholesome ingredients and classic Italian flavors that come together to create a truly unforgettable dining experience.

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb penne pasta
  • 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 4 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 can (28 oz) crushed tomatoes
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1/2 teaspoon red pepper flakes
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1/2 cup fresh basil leaves, torn
  • 8 oz fresh mozzarella, cubed
  • Grated Parmesan cheese for serving

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the Chicken: Start by seasoning the chicken breasts with salt and pepper. In a large skillet, heat 1 tablespoon of olive oil over medium-high heat. Add the chicken breasts and cook until golden brown and cooked through, about 6-7 minutes per side. Once cooked, transfer the chicken to a cutting board and let it rest for a few minutes before slicing it into bite-sized pieces.

  2. Cook the Pasta: In a large pot of salted boiling water, cook the penne pasta according to the package instructions until al dente. Once cooked, drain the pasta and set it aside.

  3. Make the Tomato Basil Sauce: In the same skillet used to cook the chicken, heat the remaining tablespoon of olive oil over medium heat. Add the minced garlic and cook for about 1 minute until fragrant. Pour in the crushed tomatoes, dried oregano, red pepper flakes, and season with salt and pepper. Simmer the sauce for 10-15 minutes to allow the flavors to meld together. Stir in the torn basil leaves during the last few minutes of cooking.

  4. Combine the Ingredients: Add the cooked penne pasta to the skillet with the tomato basil sauce, tossing gently to coat the pasta evenly. Gently fold in the sliced chicken and fresh mozzarella, allowing the residual heat to melt the mozzarella slightly.

  5. Serve and Enjoy: Once everything is well combined, transfer the Tomato Basil Chicken Penne to a large serving dish. Sprinkle with grated Parmesan cheese and additional torn basil leaves for garnish. Serve immediately and savor the harmonious blend of flavors.

Why This Recipe Shines:

The magic of this Tomato Basil Chicken Penne with Fresh Mozzarella recipe lies in its simplicity and the quality of its ingredients. The combination of plump, juicy tomatoes, fragrant basil, tender chicken, and creamy fresh mozzarella creates a symphony of flavors that will transport you straight to the Italian countryside with every bite.

This dish is not only a feast for the taste buds but also a visual delight, with vibrant reds, greens, and creamy whites coming together to create a beautiful and inviting plate of pasta. It’s a versatile recipe that can be enjoyed as a family meal, a romantic dinner for two, or a dish to impress guests at a dinner party.

The interplay of textures is also worth noting, with the al dente penne providing the perfect canvas for the rich, velvety tomato basil sauce, while the tender chicken and melty mozzarella add layers of indulgent goodness. The flavors are bright and satisfying, making this recipe a standout choice for any occasion.

In Conclusion:

The Tomato Basil Chicken Penne with Fresh Mozzarella is a celebration of the vibrant, sun-ripened flavors of Italy. It’s a dish that embodies the essence of Italian cooking – simple, honest, and utterly delicious. Whether you’re a seasoned home cook or a novice in the kitchen, this recipe is sure to become a cherished favorite in your culinary repertoire. So, gather the freshest ingredients, embrace the joy of cooking, and indulge in the irresistible allure of this tantalizing pasta dish. Buon appetito!
