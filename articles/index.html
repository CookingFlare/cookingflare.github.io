Mediterranean-inspired Quinoa Salad with Herbed Yogurt Dressing

Quinoa, with its ancient origins in the Andean region of South America, has become a staple in modern kitchens worldwide. Its versatility and nutritional profile make it a perfect canvas for creating wholesome salads, particularly when infused with Mediterranean flavors. Today, we’re diving into a vibrant and nutritious Mediterranean-inspired Quinoa Salad with Herbed Yogurt Dressing that promises to tantalize your taste buds and nourish your body. Ingredients For the Salad: Quinoa: 1 cup, rinsed thoroughly Cherry Tomatoes: 1 cup, halved Cucumber: 1 medium-sized, diced Red Onion: 1/2 small, finely chopped Kalamata Olives: 1/2 cup, sliced Feta Cheese: 1/2 cup, crumbled Fresh Parsley: 1/4 cup, chopped Fresh Mint: 1/4 cup, chopped Salt and Pepper: to taste For the Herbed Yogurt Dressing: Greek Yogurt: 1 cup Fresh Lemon Juice: from 1 lemon Garlic: 1 clove, minced Fresh Dill: 1 tablespoon, chopped Fresh Oregano: 1 tablespoon, chopped Salt and Pepper: to taste Instructions Cooking Quinoa: Rinse Quinoa: Before cooking, rinse the quinoa thoroughly under cold water to remove any bitterness. Cook Quinoa: In a medium saucepan, combine the rinsed quinoa with 2 cups of water. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat to low. Cover and simmer for about 15 minutes, or until the quinoa absorbs all the water and is tender. Remove from heat and let it cool. Making the Herbed Yogurt Dressing: Prepare Dressing: In a small bowl, whisk together the Greek yogurt, fresh lemon juice, minced garlic, chopped dill, chopped oregano, salt, and pepper until smooth and well combined. Adjust seasoning to taste. Assembling the Salad: Combine Ingredients: In a large mixing bowl, combine the cooked and cooled quinoa with cherry tomatoes, diced cucumber, finely chopped red onion, sliced Kalamata olives, crumbled feta cheese, chopped fresh parsley, and chopped fresh mint. Add Dressing: Pour the prepared herbed yogurt dressing over the salad ingredients. Mix Well: Gently toss everything together until the salad is evenly coated with the dressing. Season to Taste: Taste and adjust salt and pepper if needed. Serving: Chill: For best results, refrigerate the salad for at least 30 minutes before serving to allow the flavors to meld together. Serve: Serve chilled or at room temperature as a refreshing side dish or a light main course. Why Choose Mediterranean-Inspired Quinoa Salad? This Mediterranean-inspired Quinoa Salad offers a multitude of flavors and textures that harmonize beautifully. The nutty quinoa provides a satisfying base, while the juicy cherry tomatoes, crisp cucumber, and tangy Kalamata olives add bursts of freshness. Fresh and Light Quinoa and Tomato Salad

Quinoa is renowned for its nutritional benefits and versatility in the kitchen. When paired with vibrant, juicy tomatoes and a medley of fresh herbs, it transforms into a delightful salad that’s perfect for summer or anytime you crave a light and nourishing meal. Ingredients 1 cup quinoa, rinsed thoroughly 2 cups water or vegetable broth 1 pint cherry tomatoes, halved 1 cucumber, diced 1/2 red onion, finely chopped 1/4 cup fresh parsley, chopped 1/4 cup fresh basil, chopped 1/4 cup fresh mint, chopped 1/4 cup extra-virgin olive oil 2 tablespoons red wine vinegar or lemon juice Salt and pepper, to taste Instructions Cooking Quinoa In a medium saucepan, combine the rinsed quinoa and water or vegetable broth. Bring to a boil over medium-high heat. Reduce the heat to low, cover, and simmer for 15-20 minutes, or until the quinoa is tender and has absorbed all the liquid. Remove from heat and let it sit covered for 5 minutes. Fluff with a fork and transfer to a large mixing bowl to cool. Preparing the Salad While the quinoa is cooking, prepare the vegetables. Halve the cherry tomatoes, dice the cucumber, and finely chop the red onion. In the same mixing bowl with the cooled quinoa, add the cherry tomatoes, cucumber, red onion, chopped parsley, basil, and mint. Drizzle with extra-virgin olive oil and red wine vinegar or lemon juice. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Mixing and Serving Gently toss all the ingredients together until well combined and evenly coated with the dressing. Taste and adjust seasoning if needed. Serve immediately, or refrigerate for at least 30 minutes to allow the flavors to meld together before serving. Tips for the Perfect Quinoa Salad Rinsing Quinoa: Always rinse quinoa thoroughly before cooking to remove its natural coating, which can make it taste bitter. Vegetable Broth: Using vegetable broth instead of water adds extra flavor to the quinoa. Fresh Herbs: Fresh herbs like parsley, basil, and mint bring a burst of freshness and aroma to the salad. Make-Ahead: This salad can be made ahead of time and stored in the refrigerator for up to 3 days. Add fresh herbs just before serving to maintain their flavor. Variations Add Protein: Mix in grilled chicken, shrimp, or tofu for added protein. Cheese: Sprinkle feta or goat cheese over the salad for a creamy tangy touch. Nuts and Seeds: Toasted pine nuts, almonds, or pumpkin seeds add crunch and extra nutrients. Enjoy Your Quinoa and Tomato Salad! This fresh and light quinoa salad is not only delicious but also packed with wholesome ingredients that will leave you feeling satisfied and nourished. Whether you’re looking for a quick weekday lunch, a side dish for a barbecue, or a healthy potluck contribution, this salad fits the bill perfectly. Embrace the simplicity of fresh ingredients and vibrant flavors with every forkful. Cheers to good health and delightful eating! ...
