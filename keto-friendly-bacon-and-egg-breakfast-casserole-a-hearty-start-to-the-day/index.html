Keto-Friendly Bacon and Egg Breakfast Casserole: A Hearty Start to the Day


Are you looking for a delicious and satisfying breakfast that aligns with your keto lifestyle? Look no further than this Keto-Friendly Bacon and Egg Breakfast Casserole! Packed with protein and flavor, this casserole is the perfect way to start your day on a hearty note. Whether you’re following a ketogenic diet or simply looking for a wholesome breakfast option, this recipe is sure to become a favorite in your meal rotation.

Ingredients

To make this flavorful breakfast casserole, you’ll need the following ingredients:

  • 12 slices of bacon
  • 8 large eggs
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • 1 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1/2 cup diced bell peppers
  • 1/2 cup diced onions
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1/4 teaspoon red pepper flakes (optional)
  • Fresh chives for garnish

Instructions

Step 1: Prepare the Bacon

Start by preheating your oven to 350°F (175°C). While the oven is preheating, line a baking sheet with parchment paper and arrange the bacon slices in a single layer. Bake the bacon for 15-20 minutes, or until it reaches your desired level of crispiness. Once done, remove the bacon from the oven and transfer it to a plate lined with paper towels to drain excess grease. Allow the bacon to cool, then chop it into bite-sized pieces.

Step 2: Prepare the Egg Mixture

In a large mixing bowl, crack the eggs and whisk them together with the heavy cream until well combined. Add the shredded cheddar cheese, diced bell peppers, diced onions, garlic powder, salt, black pepper, and red pepper flakes (if using) to the bowl. Stir the mixture until all the ingredients are evenly distributed.

Step 3: Assemble the Casserole

Grease a 9x13 inch baking dish with butter or cooking spray. Spread the chopped bacon evenly across the bottom of the dish. Pour the egg mixture over the bacon, ensuring that it covers the bacon evenly. Use a spatula to gently spread the mixture if needed.

Step 4: Bake the Casserole

Place the baking dish in the preheated oven and bake the casserole for 25-30 minutes, or until the eggs are set and the top is lightly golden. You can check for doneness by inserting a knife into the center – if it comes out clean, the casserole is ready.

Step 5: Garnish and Serve

Once the casserole is done baking, remove it from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes. Sprinkle fresh chives over the top for a pop of color and added flavor. Slice the casserole into portions and serve warm.

Why This Recipe Works

This Keto-Friendly Bacon and Egg Breakfast Casserole is a winning choice for several reasons:

  • Convenience: You can prepare this casserole ahead of time and store it in the refrigerator, making it an ideal option for meal prepping or feeding a crowd.
  • Versatility: Feel free to customize this recipe by adding your favorite keto-friendly ingredients, such as spinach, mushrooms, or different types of cheese.
  • Nutrient-Rich: With eggs, bacon, and cheese as the main components, this casserole is rich in protein and healthy fats, keeping you satisfied throughout the morning.

Final Thoughts

This Keto-Friendly Bacon and Egg Breakfast Casserole is a delicious and nourishing way to kickstart your day. Whether you’re following a keto diet or simply enjoy a flavorful breakfast, this casserole is a must-try. Its simplicity, versatility, and hearty flavors make it a standout dish that will keep you coming back for more. Enjoy a slice of this casserole alongside a fresh cup of coffee or your favorite keto-friendly beverage for a satisfying and wholesome breakfast experience.

So go ahead, gather your ingredients, and treat yourself to a hearty start to the day with this delightful Keto-Friendly Bacon and Egg Breakfast Casserole. Your taste buds and your body will thank you!
