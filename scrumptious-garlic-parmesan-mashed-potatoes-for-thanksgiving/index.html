Scrumptious Garlic Parmesan Mashed Potatoes for Thanksgiving


Thanksgiving is a time when families gather together to share a delicious meal and create lasting memories. And what better way to elevate your Thanksgiving feast than with a side dish that is both comforting and bursting with flavor? Introducing Scrumptious Garlic Parmesan Mashed Potatoes – a creamy, savory, and indulgent delight that will have your guests coming back for seconds (and maybe even thirds!).

Ingredients

  • 4 pounds of russet potatoes, peeled and cut into chunks
  • 6 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 cup of whole milk
  • 1/2 cup of unsalted butter
  • 1 cup of grated Parmesan cheese
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Chopped fresh parsley for garnish (optional)

Instructions

  1. In a large pot, bring salted water to a boil. Add the peeled and chopped potatoes, along with the minced garlic cloves. Cook until the potatoes are fork-tender, usually around 15-20 minutes.

  2. While the potatoes are cooking, in a small saucepan, heat the milk and butter over low heat until the butter has melted. Keep warm until ready to use.

  3. Once the potatoes are cooked, drain them well and return them to the pot. Using a potato masher or a fork, mash the potatoes until they reach your desired consistency.

  4. Gradually pour the warm milk and butter mixture into the mashed potatoes, stirring continuously. The mixture should become creamy and smooth.

  5. Stir in the grated Parmesan cheese, ensuring it is evenly distributed throughout the mashed potatoes. Season with salt and pepper to taste.

  6. Transfer the Scrumptious Garlic Parmesan Mashed Potatoes to a serving bowl. If desired, garnish with chopped fresh parsley for a pop of color.

  7. Serve the mashed potatoes hot alongside your Thanksgiving turkey or any other main dish of your choice. Watch as your guests savor every spoonful of this heavenly creation.

Tips and Variations

  • For an extra tangy twist, add a tablespoon of sour cream to the mashed potatoes along with the Parmesan cheese.
  • If you prefer a smoother texture, use a hand mixer or a stand mixer fitted with the paddle attachment to whip the mashed potatoes until they are light and fluffy.
  • To make this recipe ahead of time, prepare the mashed potatoes as instructed but do not garnish with parsley. Store in an airtight container in the refrigerator for up to two days. Reheat in the microwave or on the stovetop, adding a splash of milk to restore creaminess.
  • Love bacon? Crumble some cooked bacon and sprinkle it on top of the mashed potatoes before serving for an irresistible flavor combination.

Conclusion

Scrumptious Garlic Parmesan Mashed Potatoes are a Thanksgiving side dish that will elevate your holiday feast to new heights. Creamy, garlicky, and bursting with the nutty flavor of Parmesan cheese, these mashed potatoes are sure to impress your family and friends. Whether you’re hosting a large gathering or enjoying an intimate meal, this recipe is a must-try. So, get ready to create a culinary masterpiece that will have everyone asking for seconds. Happy Thanksgiving!
