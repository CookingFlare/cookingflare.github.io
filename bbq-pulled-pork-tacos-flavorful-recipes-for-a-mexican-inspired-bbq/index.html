BBQ Pulled Pork Tacos: Flavorful Recipes for a Mexican-inspired BBQ


Tacos are a beloved dish around the world, and when combined with the smoky goodness of BBQ pulled pork, they become a culinary masterpiece. In this blog post, we will explore different recipes for BBQ pulled pork tacos, each with its own unique twist to satisfy your taste buds. So, let’s dive right in and discover the mouthwatering flavors of Mexican-inspired BBQ!

Classic BBQ Pulled Pork Tacos

Ingredients:

  • 2 pounds of pork shoulder
  • 1 cup of your favorite BBQ sauce
  • 1 tablespoon of brown sugar
  • 1 tablespoon of paprika
  • 1 teaspoon of garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon of onion powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Corn tortillas
  • Toppings: diced onions, cilantro, lime wedges, and sour cream

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your grill or smoker to 225째F (107째C).
  2. In a small bowl, combine the brown sugar, paprika, garlic powder, onion powder, salt, and pepper. Rub this mixture all over the pork shoulder, ensuring it is evenly coated.
  3. Place the pork shoulder on the grill or smoker and cook it low and slow for about 6-8 hours, or until the internal temperature reaches 195째F (90째C) and the meat is tender and easily shredable.
  4. Once the pork shoulder is cooked, remove it from the grill or smoker and let it rest for about 15 minutes. Then, using two forks, shred the meat into bite-sized pieces.
  5. In a large bowl, combine the shredded pork and BBQ sauce, mixing well to coat every piece.
  6. Warm the corn tortillas on a skillet or grill for a few seconds on each side.
  7. Fill each tortilla with a generous amount of the BBQ pulled pork and top with diced onions, cilantro, a squeeze of lime juice, and a dollop of sour cream.
  8. Serve the BBQ pulled pork tacos immediately and enjoy the explosion of flavors!

Spicy Chipotle BBQ Pulled Pork Tacos

Ingredients:

  • 2 pounds of pork shoulder
  • 1 cup of chipotle BBQ sauce
  • 2 tablespoons of adobo sauce
  • 1 tablespoon of honey
  • 1 tablespoon of cumin powder
  • 1 tablespoon of smoked paprika
  • 1 teaspoon of chili powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Flour tortillas
  • Toppings: sliced jalapenos, shredded lettuce, diced tomatoes, and avocado slices

Instructions:

  1. Follow the same steps as the Classic BBQ Pulled Pork Tacos recipe until the pork shoulder is cooked and shredded.
  2. In a small bowl, mix together the chipotle BBQ sauce, adobo sauce, honey, cumin powder, smoked paprika, chili powder, salt, and pepper.
  3. Add the spicy sauce mixture to the shredded pork and stir until well combined.
  4. Warm the flour tortillas on a skillet or grill for a few seconds on each side.
  5. Fill each tortilla with a generous amount of the spicy chipotle BBQ pulled pork and top with sliced jalapenos, shredded lettuce, diced tomatoes, and avocado slices.
  6. Serve the tacos immediately and experience the perfect balance of heat and smokiness!

Pineapple Teriyaki BBQ Pulled Pork Tacos

Ingredients:

  • 2 pounds of pork shoulder
  • 1 cup of teriyaki sauce
  • 1 cup of pineapple juice
  • 1/4 cup of soy sauce
  • 1/4 cup of brown sugar
  • 1 tablespoon of grated fresh ginger
  • 1 teaspoon of garlic powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Flour tortillas
  • Toppings: sliced pineapple, chopped green onions, shredded cabbage, and sesame seeds

Instructions:

  1. Follow the same steps as the Classic BBQ Pulled Pork Tacos recipe until the pork shoulder is cooked and shredded.
  2. In a saucepan, combine the teriyaki sauce, pineapple juice, soy sauce, brown sugar, grated ginger, garlic powder, salt, and pepper. Bring the mixture to a boil, then reduce the heat and simmer for 10 minutes.
  3. Pour the teriyaki sauce mixture over the shredded pork and mix until well coated.
  4. Warm the flour tortillas on a skillet or grill for a few seconds on each side.
  5. Fill each tortilla with a generous amount of the pineapple teriyaki BBQ pulled pork and top with sliced pineapple, chopped green onions, shredded cabbage, and a sprinkle of sesame seeds.
  6. Serve the tacos immediately and savor the sweet and tangy flavors!

Conclusion

BBQ pulled pork tacos offer a delightful fusion of Mexican and BBQ flavors. Whether you prefer the classic version, a spicy kick, or a tropical twist, these recipes will surely satisfy your cravings. So gather your ingredients, fire up the grill, and prepare to indulge in the mouthwatering goodness of BBQ pulled pork tacos. Your taste buds will thank you!

Remember, the key to achieving the perfect BBQ pulled pork tacos lies in slow cooking the pork, using quality ingredients, and adding your own personal touch. Feel free to experiment with different spices, sauces, and toppings to create your signature BBQ pulled pork tacos. Enjoy the journey and happy cooking!
