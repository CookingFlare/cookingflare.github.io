Pasta with Creamy Sun-Dried Tomato Sauce: A Burst of Mediterranean Flavors


If you’re looking for a dish that embodies the rich and vibrant flavors of the Mediterranean, look no further than this Pasta with Creamy Sun-Dried Tomato Sauce recipe. This indulgent yet easy-to-make dish is a celebration of the sun-kissed ingredients that define Mediterranean cuisine. With the luscious combination of cream, sun-dried tomatoes, and aromatic herbs, this pasta dish is sure to transport your taste buds to the shores of the Mediterranean.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound (450g) of your favorite pasta (penne, fusilli, or spaghetti work well)
  • 1 cup (240ml) heavy cream
  • 1/2 cup (75g) sun-dried tomatoes, drained and chopped
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1/4 cup (25g) grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 tablespoons (30ml) olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon (5ml) dried basil
  • 1 teaspoon (5ml) dried oregano
  • Salt and freshly ground black pepper to taste
  • Fresh basil leaves for garnish

Instructions

  1. Cook the Pasta: Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Add the pasta and cook according to the package instructions until al dente. Drain the pasta, reserving a cup of the cooking water, and set aside.

  2. Prepare the Sun-Dried Tomato Sauce: In a large skillet, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the minced garlic and cook for 1-2 minutes until fragrant. Stir in the chopped sun-dried tomatoes, dried basil, and dried oregano. Cook for another 2 minutes, allowing the flavors to meld together.

  3. Add the Cream: Pour the heavy cream into the skillet, stirring gently to combine with the sun-dried tomato mixture. Bring the sauce to a gentle simmer, then reduce the heat and let it thicken for about 5-7 minutes. Stir in the grated Parmesan cheese until it melts and the sauce becomes creamy and luxurious.

  4. Combine the Pasta and Sauce: Add the cooked pasta to the skillet with the creamy sun-dried tomato sauce. Toss gently to coat the pasta evenly, adding a splash of the reserved pasta cooking water if needed to loosen the sauce.

  5. Season and Serve: Season the pasta with salt and freshly ground black pepper to taste. Garnish with fresh basil leaves for a burst of color and an extra layer of flavor.

Tips and Variations

  • For a touch of freshness, add a handful of baby spinach leaves to the sauce just before combining it with the pasta.
  • If you prefer a hint of heat, sprinkle some red pepper flakes over the finished dish.
  • Experiment with different types of pasta to find your favorite pairing for this luscious sun-dried tomato sauce.
