10 Refreshing Salad Recipes Perfect for Summer Picnics


There’s no better time than summer to enjoy a refreshing salad. From light and zesty to hearty and satisfying, these 10 salad recipes will be the star of your next picnic. Pack up these scrumptious dishes and head to the park, beach, or backyard for a delightful meal in the sun.

  1. Strawberry Spinach Salad

Satisfy your sweet tooth with this delightful strawberry spinach salad. Something about the combination of spinach, strawberries, and nuts creates a perfect balance of flavors. The sweetness of the berries is complemented by the savory crunch of almonds and Manitoba Manitoba.Finish it off with your favorite balsamic vinaigrette, and you have a refreshing salad that you’ll want to make again and again.

  1. Greek Cucumber Salad

Here’s a refreshing side dish that will transport you straight to the sunny Mediterranean. Slice some cucumbers, sprinkle with fresh herbs, and top with a homemade vinaigrette. In just a few simple steps, you’ll have a delicious Greek-inspired salad that pairs perfectly with grilled meats.

  1. Watermelon Feta Salad

When the temperatures rise, keep your meals light and delicious. This watermelon feta salad is the perfect way to make use of the seasonal fruit. It’s embellished with a punchy mint and feta cheese combination that adds a refreshing twist to your picnic menu.

  1. Summer Peach Salad

Time to switch things up from the traditional fruit salad. This summer peach salad incorporates juicy, ripe peaches with mixed greens, avocado, and feta cheese. For a little x-factor, sprinkle pink peppercorns on top for an added burst of flavor.

  1. Asian Noodle Salad

Take your taste buds on an adventure with this Asian noodle salad. Screaming with flavor, it includes components like rice noodles, edamame, carrots, and cucumber, topped with zesty peanut dressing. It’s nutty, crunchy, and altogether refreshing, making it ideal for a warm summer’s day.

  1. Citrus Kale Salad

It may seem daring, but this citrus kale salad is bound to be a hit at your picnic. Kale really shines when paired with citrus, and this salad delivers with an ensemble of lemon, orange, and avocado. Through this combination, you won’t just enjoy a delicious meal – you’ll also enjoy much-needed vitamins and minerals.

  1. Harvard Beats Salad

Warning: this salad is addictive! Inspired by a recipe from Harvard University, it features a smart mix of beets, oranges, and walnuts with spinach leaves. For extra punch, the salad is dressed with honey ginger vinaigrette. Don’t be surprised if this salad steals the show at your picnic.

  1. Sweet Potato Salad

Not just for Thanksgiving, this sweet potato salad is a must for your summer picnics. The sweetness of the potatoes pairs wonderfully with red onions, lentils, and arugula. Toss it in a homemade lemon vinaigrette, and enjoy this hearty yet refreshing salad.

  1. Roasted Vegetable Salad

Roasted vegetables are the star in this simple yet delectable salad. It features a rainbow of colorful produce, including red peppers, zucchini, and eggplant, that brings a mouth-watering balance of taste and texture. It’s sure to leave everyone reaching for seconds.

  1. Farmers Market Garden Salad

Close out your picnic with a feast straight from your local farmers market. Use a bounty of garden-fresh ingredients like tomatoes, cucumbers, and zucchini, and serve it with a homemade balsamic vinaigrette. For an added twist, toss in some sunflower seeds for a delightful crunch.

As temperatures soar and summer days continue, embrace these creative salad recipes. Together with the warm sunlight and good company, they make your picnic a delightful and healthy experience. So, grab your ingredients and your favorite picnic blanket – new favorite salads await!
