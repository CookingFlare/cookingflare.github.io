Fluffy Banana Pancake Recipes for a Tropical Twist


Who doesn’t love pancakes? They are a classic breakfast staple that never fails to bring joy to our mornings. But why settle for plain pancakes when you can add a tropical twist to your morning routine? In this post, we will explore some delicious and fluffy banana pancake recipes that will transport you to a tropical paradise with every bite.

Recipe 1: Classic Banana Pancakes

Ingredients:

  • 2 ripe bananas, mashed
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 tablespoon sugar
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup buttermilk
  • 1 large egg
  • 2 tablespoons unsalted butter, melted
  • Optional toppings: sliced bananas, maple syrup, shredded coconut

Instructions:

  1. In a large bowl, whisk together the mashed bananas, flour, sugar, baking powder, baking soda, and salt.

  2. In a separate bowl, whisk together the buttermilk, egg, and melted butter.

  3. Pour the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients and stir until just combined. Do not overmix; some lumps are okay.

  4. Heat a non-stick skillet or griddle over medium heat. Spoon 1/4 cup of batter onto the skillet for each pancake.

  5. Cook until bubbles form on the surface of the pancake, then flip and cook for an additional 1-2 minutes.

  6. Serve the pancakes with your favorite toppings, such as sliced bananas, maple syrup, or shredded coconut.

Recipe 2: Pineapple Coconut Banana Pancakes

Ingredients:

  • 2 ripe bananas, mashed
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 tablespoon sugar
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup coconut milk
  • 1 large egg
  • 2 tablespoons unsalted butter, melted
  • 1/2 cup crushed pineapple, drained
  • Optional toppings: pineapple chunks, shredded coconut, honey

Instructions:

  1. In a large bowl, whisk together the mashed bananas, flour, sugar, baking powder, baking soda, and salt.

  2. In a separate bowl, whisk together the coconut milk, egg, and melted butter.

  3. Pour the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients and stir until just combined. Fold in the crushed pineapple.

  4. Heat a non-stick skillet or griddle over medium heat. Spoon 1/4 cup of batter onto the skillet for each pancake.

  5. Cook until bubbles form on the surface of the pancake, then flip and cook for an additional 1-2 minutes.

  6. Serve the pancakes with your favorite toppings, such as pineapple chunks, shredded coconut, or a drizzle of honey.

Recipe 3: Mango Banana Pancakes

Ingredients:

  • 2 ripe bananas, mashed
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 tablespoon sugar
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 large egg
  • 2 tablespoons unsalted butter, melted
  • 1/2 cup diced mango
  • Optional toppings: diced mango, honey, lime zest

Instructions:

  1. In a large bowl, whisk together the mashed bananas, flour, sugar, baking powder, baking soda, and salt.

  2. In a separate bowl, whisk together the milk, egg, and melted butter.

  3. Pour the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients and stir until just combined. Fold in the diced mango.

  4. Heat a non-stick skillet or griddle over medium heat. Spoon 1/4 cup of batter onto the skillet for each pancake.

  5. Cook until bubbles form on the surface of the pancake, then flip and cook for an additional 1-2 minutes.

  6. Serve the pancakes with your favorite toppings, such as diced mango, a drizzle of honey, or a sprinkle of lime zest.

Enjoy these fluffy banana pancake recipes with a tropical twist and start your day off on a delicious note. Whether you choose the classic banana pancakes, the pineapple coconut variation, or the mango-infused delight, each bite will transport you to a sunny beachside getaway. So grab your ingredients and get ready for a taste of paradise in your own kitchen!
