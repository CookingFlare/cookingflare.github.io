Delicious Apple Cinnamon Pancakes: A Warm and Spiced Treat


Are you craving a cozy and comforting breakfast that will fill your kitchen with the aroma of freshly baked apples and warm cinnamon? Look no further than these delicious apple cinnamon pancakes! This recipe is the perfect blend of sweet and spiced flavors, guaranteed to make your taste buds dance with joy.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons granulated sugar
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/2 teaspoon nutmeg
  • 1 cup buttermilk
  • 1/2 cup unsweetened applesauce
  • 1 large egg
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 medium apple, peeled, cored, and diced

Instructions

  1. In a large mixing bowl, whisk together the flour, sugar, baking powder, baking soda, salt, cinnamon, and nutmeg. This combination of spices will infuse your pancakes with that irresistible apple pie flavor.

  2. In a separate bowl, whisk together the buttermilk, applesauce, egg, and vanilla extract until well combined. The applesauce adds moisture and natural sweetness to the batter, while the buttermilk gives the pancakes a lovely tanginess.

  3. Pour the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients and gently stir until just combined. Be careful not to overmix, as this can result in tough and dense pancakes. The batter should be slightly lumpy.

  4. Gently fold in the diced apple, ensuring that it is evenly distributed throughout the batter. The apple pieces will provide delightful bursts of flavor and a pleasant texture to every bite.

  5. Heat a non-stick skillet or griddle over medium heat. Lightly grease the surface with cooking spray or a small amount of butter.

  6. Using a 1/4 cup measuring cup, pour the batter onto the skillet, spreading it slightly with the back of the measuring cup to form a round pancake shape. Cook until bubbles form on the surface, then carefully flip the pancake and cook for an additional 1-2 minutes, or until golden brown.

  7. Repeat the process with the remaining batter, adding more cooking spray or butter as needed. This recipe should yield approximately 10-12 pancakes, depending on the size.

  8. Serve the apple cinnamon pancakes warm, topped with a drizzle of maple syrup and a sprinkle of powdered sugar. The combination of the sweet syrup, spiced apples, and fluffy pancakes is simply heavenly!

Variations

  • For an extra indulgence, top your pancakes with a dollop of whipped cream and a sprinkle of cinnamon-sugar.
  • If you prefer a more rustic texture, substitute half of the all-purpose flour with whole wheat flour.
  • Add a handful of chopped walnuts or pecans to the batter for a delightful crunch.

Conclusion

These delicious apple cinnamon pancakes are the perfect breakfast treat to start your day off on a sweet and cozy note. With their warm spices and tender apple pieces, they are sure to become a favorite in your household. So grab your apron, whip up a batch of these heavenly pancakes, and savor the comforting flavors of fall all year round. Enjoy!
