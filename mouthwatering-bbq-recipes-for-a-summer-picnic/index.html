Mouthwatering BBQ Recipes for a Summer Picnic


Summer is the perfect time for outdoor gatherings and picnics, and what better way to enjoy the warm weather than with some mouthwatering BBQ recipes? Whether you’re planning a picnic in the park or a backyard cookout, these delicious BBQ recipes will surely impress your friends and family. From juicy grilled meats to flavorful side dishes, we’ve got you covered. So fire up the grill and get ready to tantalize your taste buds!

Grilled Honey-Glazed Chicken Skewers

Ingredients:

  • 1.5 lbs chicken breast, cut into cubes
  • 1/4 cup honey
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tbsp fresh lemon juice
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, whisk together the honey, soy sauce, minced garlic, lemon juice, salt, and pepper.
  2. Add the chicken cubes to the marinade and toss until well coated. Let it marinate in the refrigerator for at least 30 minutes.
  3. Preheat the grill to medium-high heat.
  4. Thread the chicken cubes onto skewers and brush with the remaining marinade.
  5. Grill the skewers for about 10-12 minutes, turning occasionally, until the chicken is cooked through and slightly charred.
  6. Serve hot and enjoy!

Tangy BBQ Pulled Pork Sandwiches

Ingredients:

  • 2.5 lbs pork shoulder
  • 1 cup BBQ sauce
  • 1/4 cup apple cider vinegar
  • 2 tbsp brown sugar
  • 1 tsp smoked paprika
  • 1/2 tsp garlic powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Hamburger buns, for serving

Instructions:

  1. In a small bowl, mix together the BBQ sauce, apple cider vinegar, brown sugar, smoked paprika, garlic powder, salt, and pepper.
  2. Rub the mixture all over the pork shoulder, ensuring it is well coated.
  3. Place the pork shoulder in a slow cooker and cook on low heat for 8-10 hours until the meat is tender and easily shreds.
  4. Remove the pork from the slow cooker and shred it using two forks.
  5. Pour the remaining sauce from the slow cooker over the shredded pork and mix well.
  6. Serve the pulled pork on hamburger buns and enjoy the tangy goodness!

Grilled Corn on the Cob with Lime and Chili

Ingredients:

  • 4 ears of corn, husked
  • 2 tbsp butter, melted
  • 1 lime, juiced
  • Chili powder, to taste
  • Salt to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the grill to medium-high heat.
  2. Brush each ear of corn with melted butter, lime juice, and sprinkle with chili powder and salt.
  3. Place the corn directly on the grill and cook for about 10-12 minutes, turning occasionally, until the corn is tender and slightly charred.
  4. Remove from the grill and serve hot.

Zesty Pasta Salad

Ingredients:

  • 8 oz pasta (penne, fusilli, or your choice)
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1/2 cup black olives, sliced
  • 1/4 cup red onion, finely chopped
  • 1/4 cup fresh basil, chopped
  • 1/4 cup feta cheese, crumbled
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 tbsp red wine vinegar
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Cook the pasta according to package instructions. Drain and rinse with cold water.
  2. In a large bowl, combine the cooked pasta, cherry tomatoes, black olives, red onion, basil, and feta cheese.
  3. In a small bowl, whisk together the olive oil, red wine vinegar, salt, and pepper.
  4. Pour the dressing over the pasta mixture and toss until well coated.
  5. Chill in the refrigerator for at least 30 minutes before serving.

Conclusion

With these mouthwatering BBQ recipes, your summer picnic is sure to be a hit! From the juicy grilled chicken skewers to the tangy pulled pork sandwiches, each dish is bursting with flavor. Don’t forget to complement your main dishes with delicious grilled corn on the cob and zesty pasta salad. So gather your loved ones, head outdoors, and enjoy the delightful flavors of a summer BBQ picnic. Happy grilling!
