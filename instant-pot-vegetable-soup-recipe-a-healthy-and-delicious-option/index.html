Instant Pot Vegetable Soup Recipe: A Healthy and Delicious Option


Are you looking for a quick, nutritious, and flavorful meal that will warm your soul? Look no further than this Instant Pot Vegetable Soup recipe. Packed with an abundance of fresh vegetables, aromatic herbs, and savory broth, this soup is not only a healthy option but also a delight to your taste buds. And the best part? It can be ready in just a matter of minutes, thanks to the magic of the Instant Pot.

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 medium onion, diced
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 carrots, sliced
  • 2 celery stalks, chopped
  • 1 red bell pepper, diced
  • 1 zucchini, diced
  • 1 cup green beans, trimmed and cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 1 can (14 ounces) diced tomatoes
  • 4 cups vegetable broth
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley for garnish

Instructions

  1. Set your Instant Pot to the sauté function and heat the olive oil. Add the diced onion and minced garlic, sautéing until they become fragrant and translucent.

  2. Add the carrots, celery, red bell pepper, zucchini, and green beans to the Instant Pot. Sauté for a few minutes until the vegetables start to soften.

  3. Pour in the diced tomatoes and vegetable broth, then add the dried thyme, dried oregano, and paprika. Give everything a good stir to combine the flavors.

  4. Secure the lid on the Instant Pot and set the valve to the sealing position. Select the “Soup” function and adjust the cooking time to 5 minutes on high pressure.

  5. Once the cooking time is complete, carefully release the pressure using the quick release method. Be cautious of the hot steam.

  6. Remove the lid and give the soup a stir. Taste and adjust the seasoning with salt and pepper according to your preference.

  7. Ladle the soup into bowls and garnish with fresh parsley. Serve hot and enjoy the comforting flavors of this Instant Pot Vegetable Soup.

Why Choose Instant Pot Vegetable Soup?

  1. Time-Saving: With the Instant Pot, you can have a hearty and nutritious soup on the table in no time. The pressure cooking function reduces the cooking time significantly, making it perfect for those busy weeknights.

  2. Nutrient-Rich: This vegetable soup is loaded with a wide variety of vegetables, providing you with a wealth of vitamins, minerals, and antioxidants. It’s an excellent way to meet your daily vegetable intake and support a healthy lifestyle.

  3. Customizable: Feel free to experiment with different vegetables or add your favorite herbs and spices to elevate the flavors. You can also add protein sources like beans or tofu to make it a more substantial meal.

  4. Budget-Friendly: Utilizing simple and affordable ingredients, this Instant Pot Vegetable Soup is a budget-friendly option that doesn’t compromise on taste or nutrition.

So, grab your Instant Pot and get ready to indulge in a bowl of warm and comforting Vegetable Soup. It’s an easy, healthy, and delicious option that will surely become a staple in your kitchen. Enjoy!
