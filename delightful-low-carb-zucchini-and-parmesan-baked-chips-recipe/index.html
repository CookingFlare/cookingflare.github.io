Delightful Low Carb Zucchini and Parmesan Baked Chips Recipe


Are you looking for a healthy and delicious snack that won’t derail your low carb diet? Look no further than these delightful low carb zucchini and Parmesan baked chips! Packed with flavor and crunch, these chips are the perfect guilt-free treat. Plus, they are incredibly easy to make. So grab your zucchinis and let’s get started!

Ingredients

  • 2 medium zucchinis
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/4 cup almond flour
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • Cooking spray

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 425°F (220°C). Line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
  2. Wash the zucchinis and cut them into thin slices, about 1/8 inch thick. A mandoline slicer works great for this, but a sharp knife will do the job too.
  3. In a shallow bowl, combine the Parmesan cheese, almond flour, garlic powder, paprika, salt, and black pepper. Mix well to ensure all the ingredients are evenly distributed.
  4. Take each zucchini slice and dip it into the Parmesan mixture, pressing down gently to make sure it sticks to both sides. Place the coated slice on the prepared baking sheet. Repeat this process until all the zucchini slices are coated.
  5. Lightly spray the coated zucchini slices with cooking spray. This will help them crisp up nicely in the oven.
  6. Bake the zucchini chips for 15-20 minutes, or until they are golden brown and crispy. Keep an eye on them as they can burn quickly.
  7. Once the chips are done, remove them from the oven and let them cool for a few minutes. They will continue to crisp up as they cool.
  8. Serve your delightful low carb zucchini and Parmesan baked chips as a snack or as a side dish. They pair perfectly with your favorite dip or salsa.
  9. Store any leftovers in an airtight container at room temperature for up to 3 days. Although, let’s be honest, they probably won’t last that long!

Why Choose Low Carb Zucchini and Parmesan Baked Chips?

These zucchini and Parmesan chips are not only delicious but also low in carbs, making them a great option for those following a low carb or keto diet. Zucchinis are a fantastic vegetable to incorporate into your diet as they are low in calories and high in fiber, vitamins, and minerals. By swapping traditional potato chips for these zucchini chips, you can satisfy your craving for a crunchy snack without the guilt.

The combination of Parmesan cheese, almond flour, and spices adds a burst of flavor to these chips. The Parmesan cheese provides a rich and savory taste, while the almond flour adds a subtle nuttiness and helps create a crispy texture. The garlic powder, paprika, salt, and black pepper elevate the flavor profile even further, making these chips downright addictive.

Not only are these chips delicious and low in carbs, but they are also incredibly easy to make. With just a few simple ingredients and minimal prep time, you can have a batch of these delightful chips ready to enjoy in no time. They are perfect for snacking on while watching your favorite TV show, for serving at parties, or even as a side dish alongside your favorite grilled meats.

So why settle for store-bought chips that are loaded with unhealthy ingredients when you can make your own wholesome and delicious low carb zucchini and Parmesan baked chips? Give this recipe a try and discover the joy of guilt-free snacking. Your taste buds and your waistline will thank you!

Note: The nutritional information for this recipe may vary depending on the size of the zucchinis and the amount of coating used. It is always a good idea to double-check the nutritional values based on the specific ingredients you use.
