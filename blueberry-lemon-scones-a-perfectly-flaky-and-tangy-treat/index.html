Blueberry Lemon Scones: A Perfectly Flaky and Tangy Treat


Are you craving a delectable pastry treat that combines the sweet and tangy flavors of blueberries and lemon? Look no further! In this recipe, we will guide you through the steps of creating mouthwatering Blueberry Lemon Scones that are perfectly flaky and bursting with flavor. The best part? These scones are incredibly easy to make, even for beginners!

Ingredients

To make these delightful Blueberry Lemon Scones, you will need the following ingredients:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 tablespoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, cold and cubed
  • 1/2 cup fresh blueberries
  • Zest of 1 lemon
  • 1/2 cup buttermilk
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C) and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.

  2. In a large mixing bowl, whisk together the flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt. Add the cold cubed butter to the dry ingredients and use a pastry cutter or your fingertips to cut the butter into the flour mixture. Continue until the mixture resembles coarse crumbs.

  3. Gently fold in the fresh blueberries and lemon zest, ensuring they are evenly distributed throughout the dough.

  4. In a separate bowl, combine the buttermilk and vanilla extract. Gradually pour the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients, stirring with a fork until a shaggy dough forms.

  5. Transfer the dough onto a lightly floured surface and knead it a few times until it comes together. Be careful not to overwork the dough, as this can result in tough scones.

  6. Shape the dough into a circle with a thickness of about 1 inch. Using a sharp knife or a pastry cutter, cut the dough into 8 equal wedges.

  7. Place the scones onto the prepared baking sheet, leaving a few inches of space between each one. Bake in the preheated oven for 15-18 minutes or until the scones turn golden brown on the edges.

  8. Once baked, remove the scones from the oven and let them cool on a wire rack for a few minutes before serving. These scones are best enjoyed warm, so grab a cup of tea or coffee and indulge in their flaky goodness!

Tips and Variations

  • For an extra touch of sweetness, you can drizzle a simple glaze made with powdered sugar and lemon juice over the cooled scones.
  • If you prefer a more buttery flavor, you can use salted butter instead of unsalted butter. Just remember to adjust the amount of salt in the recipe accordingly.
  • Feel free to experiment with different types of berries or citrus fruits to create your own unique flavor combinations. Raspberry orange scones or blackberry lime scones are just a few examples of the endless possibilities!

Conclusion

With their buttery flakiness and the burst of blueberry and lemon flavors, these Blueberry Lemon Scones are sure to become a favorite in your household. Whether you enjoy them for breakfast, as an afternoon snack, or as a delightful treat to share with loved ones, these scones are bound to impress. So, roll up your sleeves, gather your ingredients, and embark on a baking adventure that will leave you craving more. Happy baking!
