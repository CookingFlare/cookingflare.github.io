Maple-Glazed Salmon with Roasted Brussels Sprouts: A Healthy Fall Dinner


Looking for a delicious and nutritious dinner option that captures the flavors of fall? Look no further than this Maple-Glazed Salmon with Roasted Brussels Sprouts recipe. Not only does it combine the richness of maple syrup with the savory goodness of roasted Brussels sprouts, but it also provides a healthy dose of omega-3 fatty acids from the salmon. Let’s dive into the details and get cooking!

Ingredients

For the Maple-Glazed Salmon:

  • 4 salmon fillets
  • 1/4 cup maple syrup
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon Dijon mustard
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • Salt and pepper to taste

For the Roasted Brussels Sprouts:

  • 1 pound Brussels sprouts, trimmed and halved
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C).

  2. In a small bowl, whisk together the maple syrup, soy sauce, Dijon mustard, minced garlic, salt, and pepper. This will be the glaze for the salmon.

  3. Place the salmon fillets on a foil-lined baking sheet and brush the glaze generously over each fillet, making sure to coat both sides. Set aside any remaining glaze for later use.

  4. In a separate bowl, toss the halved Brussels sprouts with olive oil, minced garlic, salt, and pepper until well coated.

  5. Spread the Brussels sprouts evenly on another foil-lined baking sheet.

  6. Place both the salmon and Brussels sprouts in the preheated oven. Roast for about 15-20 minutes or until the salmon is cooked to your desired level of doneness and the Brussels sprouts are tender and slightly caramelized.

  7. While the salmon and Brussels sprouts are roasting, you can occasionally brush the remaining glaze onto the salmon to enhance the flavor.

  8. Once cooked, remove the salmon and Brussels sprouts from the oven and let them rest for a few minutes.

  9. Serve the Maple-Glazed Salmon alongside the Roasted Brussels Sprouts for a healthy and satisfying fall dinner.

Health Benefits

Not only is this Maple-Glazed Salmon with Roasted Brussels Sprouts recipe bursting with flavor, but it also offers several health benefits. Salmon is an excellent source of omega-3 fatty acids, which are known for their heart-healthy properties. It also provides a good amount of protein and essential nutrients like vitamin D and selenium.

Brussels sprouts, on the other hand, are low in calories and high in fiber, making them a great addition to a balanced diet. They are also rich in vitamins C and K, as well as antioxidants that support overall health and well-being.

Conclusion

Maple-Glazed Salmon with Roasted Brussels Sprouts is a delightful fall dinner that combines the natural sweetness of maple syrup with the earthy flavors of Brussels sprouts. This dish not only satisfies your taste buds but also nourishes your body with essential nutrients. Give it a try and experience the perfect balance of flavors and textures in every bite. Happy cooking!
