Grilled Watermelon: Unique and Refreshing Recipes


Welcome to our latest cooking adventure where we’re exploring the unique and refreshing world of grilled watermelon! If you think watermelon is best enjoyed fresh and cold, get ready to have your taste buds tantalized by the smoky, caramelized goodness of grilled watermelon. This unexpected twist on a summer favorite will elevate your grilling game and impress your friends and family at your next barbecue. Let’s dive into some exciting grilled watermelon recipes that will surely make a splash at your next gathering.

Grilled Watermelon Steaks with Balsamic Glaze

Ingredients:

  • 1 small seedless watermelon
  • 1/4 cup balsamic vinegar
  • 2 tablespoons honey
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh mint leaves for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your grill to medium-high heat.
  2. Cut the watermelon into 1-inch thick slices, forming “watermelon steaks.”
  3. In a small bowl, whisk together the balsamic vinegar, honey, and olive oil to create the glaze.
  4. Brush the watermelon steaks with the glaze on both sides and season with salt and pepper.
  5. Place the watermelon steaks on the grill and cook for 2-3 minutes on each side, or until grill marks appear.
  6. Remove the grilled watermelon steaks from the grill and drizzle with any remaining glaze.
  7. Garnish with fresh mint leaves and serve immediately.

The combination of the sweet, juicy watermelon and the tangy balsamic glaze creates a flavor explosion that will leave your guests craving more.

Grilled Watermelon Salad with Feta and Mint

Ingredients:

  • 1 small seedless watermelon, cut into cubes
  • 1/2 cup crumbled feta cheese
  • 1/4 cup fresh mint leaves, chopped
  • 2 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon fresh lime juice
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your grill to medium heat.
  2. Place the watermelon cubes on skewers for easy grilling.
  3. Grill the watermelon cubes for 1-2 minutes on each side, until lightly caramelized.
  4. Remove the watermelon from the skewers and place in a large bowl.
  5. Add the crumbled feta cheese and chopped mint to the bowl.
  6. In a small bowl, whisk together the olive oil, lime juice, salt, and pepper.
  7. Drizzle the dressing over the watermelon salad and gently toss to combine.
  8. Chill the salad for 15-20 minutes before serving.

The grilled watermelon, creamy feta, and fresh mint create a delightful harmony of flavors and textures in this vibrant and refreshing salad.

Grilled Watermelon and Prosciutto Skewers

Ingredients:

  • 1 small seedless watermelon, cut into cubes
  • 8 slices prosciutto, halved lengthwise
  • 1/4 cup balsamic glaze
  • Wooden skewers, soaked in water for 30 minutes

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your grill to medium-high heat.
  2. Thread the watermelon cubes onto the skewers, alternating with the halved prosciutto slices.
  3. Grill the skewers for 1-2 minutes on each side, until the prosciutto is crispy and the watermelon is lightly charred.
  4. Remove the skewers from the grill and drizzle with balsamic glaze before serving.

These elegant and savory-sweet skewers are a delightful appetizer that will leave your guests impressed with your culinary creativity.

Conclusion

Grilled watermelon may seem unconventional, but once you experience the delightful transformation of flavors and textures that grilling brings to this juicy fruit, you’ll be hooked. The smoky char and caramelization elevate the natural sweetness of watermelon, creating a symphony of flavors that will wow your taste buds. Whether you’re hosting a summer cookout or simply craving a unique culinary adventure, these grilled watermelon recipes are sure to delight and refresh. So fire up the grill, grab a juicy watermelon, and get ready to savor the unexpected deliciousness of grilled watermelon!
