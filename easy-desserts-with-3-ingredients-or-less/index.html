Easy Desserts with 3 Ingredients or Less


If you’re someone who loves sweets but doesn’t want to spend hours in the kitchen, you’re in the right place! Dessert doesn’t need to be complicated, and it certainly doesn’t require a long list of ingredients to be absolutely delicious. Some of the best treats can be made with just three ingredients or less. Whether you’re craving something sweet, fruity, or indulgent, these simple recipes will satisfy your dessert cravings in no time.

1. 3-Ingredient Peanut Butter Cookies

Peanut butter cookies are a classic, and the best part is that you only need three ingredients to make them! This recipe is perfect for those moments when you’re short on time but still want something delicious to satisfy your sweet tooth.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup of peanut butter
  • 1 cup of sugar (white or brown sugar works)
  • 1 egg

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C) and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
  2. In a bowl, combine the peanut butter, sugar, and egg. Mix well until smooth.
  3. Scoop spoonfuls of the dough onto the baking sheet and gently flatten them with a fork to create a crisscross pattern.
  4. Bake for 10-12 minutes, or until the edges are golden brown.
  5. Let them cool on the baking sheet for a few minutes before transferring to a wire rack to cool completely.

These cookies are chewy, slightly crunchy, and full of rich peanut butter flavor. They’re an excellent choice for a quick, homemade treat.

2. 2-Ingredient Chocolate Fudge

If you’re a chocolate lover, you’ll fall head over heels for this simple chocolate fudge recipe. With only two ingredients, it’s incredibly easy to make and perfect for satisfying those chocolate cravings.

Ingredients:

  • 1 can (14 oz) of sweetened condensed milk
  • 12 oz of semisweet chocolate chips

Instructions:

  1. In a saucepan over low heat, melt the chocolate chips with the sweetened condensed milk, stirring constantly.
  2. Once the chocolate has fully melted and the mixture is smooth, pour it into a greased or lined 8x8-inch baking dish.
  3. Refrigerate the fudge for at least 2 hours, or until it’s firm enough to cut into squares.
  4. Once chilled, cut into small squares and enjoy!

This fudge has a creamy texture that’s rich and velvety. You can even add a pinch of sea salt or a handful of crushed nuts for added flavor, but it’s just as incredible with just the two ingredients.

3. 3-Ingredient Banana Ice Cream

Looking for a healthy and dairy-free dessert? This easy banana ice cream recipe only requires three ingredients and is a fantastic way to enjoy a frozen treat without the guilt.

Ingredients:

  • 3 ripe bananas
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1-2 tbsp milk (any type will work)

Instructions:

  1. Peel and slice the bananas into small chunks, then freeze them for at least 2 hours, or overnight for the best texture.
  2. Once frozen, place the banana pieces in a food processor and blend until smooth and creamy. You may need to scrape down the sides a few times.
  3. Add the vanilla extract and milk, blending until fully combined. You can add more milk for a softer consistency if desired.
  4. Serve immediately as soft-serve, or transfer to a container and freeze for 30 minutes to harden it further.

This banana ice cream has a naturally sweet and creamy texture that feels indulgent but is packed with nutrients. Plus, it’s customizable—you can add cocoa powder, peanut butter, or even berries to create different flavor variations.

4. 2-Ingredient Nutella Brownies

Nutella is one of those ingredients that everyone loves, and when you combine it with just one other ingredient, you get a gooey, fudgy brownie that’s nothing short of amazing.

Ingredients:

  • 1 jar (13 oz) of Nutella
  • 2 large eggs

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C) and grease or line an 8x8-inch baking pan with parchment paper.
  2. In a bowl, whisk the Nutella and eggs together until smooth and well-combined.
  3. Pour the mixture into the prepared baking pan and spread it evenly.
  4. Bake for 15-20 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out with a few moist crumbs.
  5. Let the brownies cool before slicing and serving.

These Nutella brownies are incredibly easy to make and have a rich, chocolatey flavor. Their texture is wonderfully dense and fudgy, making them the perfect quick dessert.

5. 3-Ingredient Rice Krispies Treats

Rice Krispies treats are a nostalgic favorite that’s loved by both kids and adults. They’re quick, easy, and only require three ingredients to make!

Ingredients:

  • 6 cups of Rice Krispies cereal
  • 4 cups of mini marshmallows
  • 3 tbsp butter

Instructions:

  1. In a large saucepan, melt the butter over low heat.
  2. Add the marshmallows to the pan and stir until they are fully melted and smooth.
  3. Remove the pan from the heat and quickly stir in the Rice Krispies cereal until evenly coated.
  4. Press the mixture into a greased 9x9-inch baking dish and let it cool for about 30 minutes before cutting into squares.

These crispy, gooey treats are always a crowd-pleaser. They’re perfect for parties, picnics, or just a quick snack when you’re in need of something sweet.

6. 3-Ingredient Chocolate-Dipped Strawberries

Chocolate-dipped strawberries are an elegant and simple treat that feels like a special indulgence. With just three ingredients, you can enjoy the perfect combination of fruity and chocolaty goodness.

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb of fresh strawberries
  • 8 oz of dark or milk chocolate (your choice)
  • 1 tbsp coconut oil (optional, for a smooth finish)

Instructions:

  1. Wash and dry the strawberries thoroughly, making sure they’re completely dry before dipping them in chocolate.
  2. In a heatproof bowl, melt the chocolate and coconut oil (if using) over a double boiler or in the microwave in 30-second intervals.
  3. Once the chocolate is fully melted and smooth, dip each strawberry into the chocolate, covering it about two-thirds of the way up.
  4. Place the dipped strawberries on a parchment-lined tray and allow the chocolate to set at room temperature or in the fridge for faster results.

These chocolate-dipped strawberries are decadent and easy to prepare. They’re perfect for a romantic evening, a special celebration, or just when you want something sweet to brighten your day.

7. 3-Ingredient Apple Nachos

If you’re craving something fruity with a little bit of crunch, these apple nachos are a great way to indulge without going overboard. They’re perfect for a quick snack or a light dessert.

Ingredients:

  • 2 apples (any variety)
  • 1/4 cup peanut butter (or any nut butter)
  • 1/4 cup chocolate chips

Instructions:

  1. Slice the apples into thin rounds or wedges.
  2. Drizzle the peanut butter over the apple slices.
  3. Sprinkle the chocolate chips on top and enjoy immediately!

These apple nachos are a simple yet satisfying dessert. The sweet apples paired with creamy peanut butter and rich chocolate create a delightful combination that’s perfect for a quick snack.

8. 3-Ingredient Coconut Macaroons

These coconut macaroons are sweet, chewy, and a little crunchy on the outside. They’re so easy to make and are perfect for coconut lovers.

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups shredded unsweetened coconut
  • 1/2 cup sweetened condensed milk
  • 1/4 tsp vanilla extract

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C) and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
  2. In a bowl, mix together the shredded coconut, sweetened condensed milk, and vanilla extract until well combined.
  3. Scoop spoonfuls of the mixture onto the baking sheet, shaping them into small mounds.
  4. Bake for 10-12 minutes, or until the edges are golden brown.
  5. Let the macaroons cool on the baking sheet before transferring to a wire rack.

These macaroons are incredibly simple to make and offer a delicious coconut flavor. They’re perfect for an easy dessert or snack when you’re in the mood for something light and sweet.

Conclusion

Making delicious desserts doesn’t have to be a time-consuming or complicated process. With just a few basic ingredients, you can create mouthwatering treats that will satisfy your cravings in no time. Whether you’re a fan of chocolate, peanut butter, fruit, or coconut, there’s something on this list for everyone.

Next time you find yourself in need of a quick and easy dessert, turn to these recipes. With minimal ingredients and maximum flavor, they prove that sometimes the simplest desserts are the best!
