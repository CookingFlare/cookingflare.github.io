Vegan Key Lime Pie: Tangy and Refreshing


If you’re a fan of tangy desserts that burst with flavor, then this Vegan Key Lime Pie recipe is a must-try. Made with simple, plant-based ingredients, this refreshing dessert is perfect for those hot summer days or any occasion when you’re craving something sweet and zesty.

Ingredients

For the crust:

  • 1 ½ cups of graham cracker crumbs
  • ¼ cup of melted coconut oil
  • 2 tablespoons of maple syrup

For the filling:

  • 1 ½ cups of raw cashews (soaked in water for 4-6 hours, then drained)
  • 1 cup of canned coconut milk
  • ½ cup of fresh key lime juice
  • ½ cup of maple syrup
  • Zest of 2 key limes
  • 1 teaspoon of vanilla extract

For the topping:

  • ½ cup of coconut cream (refrigerated overnight)
  • 1 tablespoon of powdered sugar
  • Zest of 1 key lime

Instructions

Step 1: Prepare the crust

  1. In a medium-sized mixing bowl, combine the graham cracker crumbs, melted coconut oil, and maple syrup. Mix well until the mixture resembles wet sand.
  2. Press the crust mixture into the bottom of a 9-inch pie dish, making sure to evenly distribute it. Use the back of a spoon or your fingers to firmly press it down.
  3. Place the crust in the refrigerator to set while you prepare the filling.

Step 2: Make the filling

  1. In a high-speed blender or food processor, combine the soaked cashews, coconut milk, key lime juice, maple syrup, lime zest, and vanilla extract. Blend until smooth and creamy.
  2. Pour the filling over the prepared crust and spread it out evenly using a spatula.
  3. Place the pie in the freezer for at least 4 hours, or until firm.

Step 3: Prepare the topping

  1. In a mixing bowl, scoop out the solid part of the refrigerated coconut cream, leaving behind any liquid. Add the powdered sugar and whisk until light and fluffy.
  2. Remove the pie from the freezer and spread the coconut cream topping over the filling.
  3. Sprinkle the zest of 1 key lime over the top for an extra burst of flavor.

Step 4: Serve and enjoy

  1. Allow the pie to thaw for a few minutes at room temperature before slicing and serving.
  2. Garnish with additional lime zest if desired.
  3. Serve chilled and enjoy the tangy and refreshing flavors of this Vegan Key Lime Pie.

Tips and Variations

  • For an extra tangy flavor, you can add more key lime juice to the filling.
  • If you prefer a sweeter pie, you can increase the amount of maple syrup in the filling.
  • Feel free to get creative with the crust by using different types of vegan cookies or nuts.
  • To save time, you can use store-bought vegan graham cracker crumbs instead of crushing them yourself.
  • This pie can be stored in the freezer for up to 2 weeks. Just make sure to cover it tightly with plastic wrap or foil.

Now that you have the recipe for this delicious Vegan Key Lime Pie, it’s time to gather your ingredients and get started. Whether you’re a vegan or simply looking to try something new, this tangy and refreshing dessert is sure to satisfy your cravings. Enjoy!
