Mouthwatering Keto Broccoli Cheddar Soup Recipe


Are you looking for a delicious and satisfying keto-friendly soup recipe? Look no further! This mouthwatering Keto Broccoli Cheddar Soup is the perfect low-carb option that will warm you up on a chilly day. Packed with flavor and creamy goodness, this soup is sure to become a family favorite.

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons unsalted butter
  • 1 small onion, diced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 4 cups fresh broccoli florets
  • 4 cups chicken or vegetable broth
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 2 cups shredded cheddar cheese
  • Salt and pepper, to taste

Instructions

  1. In a large pot, melt the butter over medium heat. Add the diced onion and minced garlic, and sauté until the onion becomes translucent and fragrant.

  2. Add the broccoli florets to the pot and stir to combine with the onion and garlic. Cook for about 5 minutes, until the broccoli begins to soften slightly.

  3. Pour the chicken or vegetable broth into the pot, ensuring that the broccoli is fully submerged. Bring the mixture to a boil, then reduce the heat and let it simmer for about 15 minutes, until the broccoli is tender.

  4. Using an immersion blender or a regular blender, carefully puree the soup until smooth. Be cautious when blending hot liquids to prevent any accidents.

  5. Return the pureed soup to the pot and stir in the heavy cream. Heat the soup over low heat until warmed through, but be careful not to let it boil.

  6. Gradually add the shredded cheddar cheese to the soup, stirring constantly until it melts completely. This will give the soup its rich and cheesy flavor.

  7. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Remember to go easy on the salt if your broth is already salted.

  8. Serve the Keto Broccoli Cheddar Soup hot and garnish with additional shredded cheese if desired. You can also add crispy bacon bits or chopped fresh herbs for extra flavor.

Why Choose Keto Broccoli Cheddar Soup?

This keto-friendly version of the classic broccoli cheddar soup is not only delicious but also fits perfectly into a low-carb lifestyle. By using fresh broccoli, rich cream, and flavorful cheddar cheese, you can enjoy a creamy and satisfying soup without the guilt.

Broccoli is a nutrient-dense vegetable that is low in carbs and high in fiber, making it an excellent choice for those following a ketogenic diet. It is also packed with vitamins and minerals, including vitamin C, vitamin K, and potassium.

The combination of broccoli and cheddar cheese creates a harmonious blend of flavors in this soup. The creaminess of the cheese perfectly complements the delicate taste of the broccoli, resulting in a velvety smooth texture that will leave you craving for more.

This soup can be enjoyed as a comforting meal on its own or paired with a side salad or some crusty keto-friendly bread. It is also a great option for meal prep, as it can be stored in the refrigerator for a few days or frozen for future enjoyment.

Whether you’re following a keto diet or simply looking for a delicious and nutritious soup recipe, this Mouthwatering Keto Broccoli Cheddar Soup is a must-try. It is a versatile dish that will satisfy your cravings while keeping you on track with your health goals.

So, why wait? Gather your ingredients, follow the simple steps, and indulge in a bowl of warm and comforting keto goodness. Your taste buds will thank you!
