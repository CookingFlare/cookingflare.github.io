Healthy Instant Pot Lentil and Vegetable Soup Recipe


Looking for a hearty and nutritious meal that’s easy to make? This Healthy Instant Pot Lentil and Vegetable Soup is the answer. Packed with protein, fiber, and vitamins, this soup is not only delicious but also incredibly good for you. The best part? It’s made in the instant pot, so you can have a wholesome meal on the table in no time. Let’s dive into the recipe!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup dried green lentils, rinsed
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 3 carrots, diced
  • 3 celery stalks, chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can (14 oz) diced tomatoes
  • 6 cups vegetable broth
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley for garnish

Instructions

  1. Start by turning on the instant pot and selecting the sauté function. Add a splash of olive oil, then add the chopped onion, carrots, and celery. Sauté for 5 minutes until the vegetables start to soften.

  2. Add the minced garlic, ground cumin, paprika, and dried thyme to the pot. Sauté for another minute until the spices become fragrant.

  3. Pour in the rinsed green lentils, diced tomatoes, and vegetable broth. Give everything a good stir and season with salt and pepper.

  4. Secure the lid on the instant pot and set the valve to the sealing position. Cook on high pressure for 15 minutes.

  5. Once the cooking time is up, allow for a natural pressure release for 10 minutes, then carefully do a quick pressure release.

  6. Give the soup a good stir, taste and adjust the seasoning if needed.

  7. Ladle the soup into bowls and garnish with fresh parsley.

Why This Recipe?

This Healthy Instant Pot Lentil and Vegetable Soup is not only a breeze to make, but it’s also incredibly nutritious. Lentils are a fantastic source of plant-based protein and fiber, making this soup a filling and satisfying meal. Additionally, the combination of vegetables adds a variety of vitamins and minerals to the dish, promoting overall health and well-being.

Nutritional Benefits

  • Lentils: High in fiber and protein, which can aid in digestion and promote satiety.
  • Vegetables: Packed with essential vitamins and minerals, offering a wide range of health benefits.
  • Tomatoes: Rich in antioxidants and vitamin C, supporting immune function and overall health.
  • Spices: Cumin and paprika not only add flavor but also provide anti-inflammatory properties.

Serving Suggestions

This soup is a complete meal on its own, but if you’re looking to add something extra, consider serving it with a slice of crusty whole grain bread or a side salad for a well-rounded dining experience.

Final Thoughts

This Healthy Instant Pot Lentil and Vegetable Soup is a versatile, nutritious, and satisfying dish that’s perfect for any occasion. Whether you’re meal prepping for the week ahead or simply craving a comforting bowl of soup, this recipe is sure to become a household favorite. So, grab your instant pot and get ready to enjoy a nourishing meal that’s as easy to make as it is delicious!

Now it’s your turn to try out this fantastic recipe and experience the goodness of a wholesome, homemade soup. Enjoy!
