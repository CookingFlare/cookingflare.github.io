Keto Smoothies: 20 Deviously Delicious Low-Carb Blends


Embracing the Keto lifestyle doesn’t mean you have to say goodbye to the world of delicious, indulgent smoothies. In fact, blending your favorite fruits and veggies with Keto-friendly ingredients has never been easier. Get ready to tantalize your taste buds with these 20 deviously delicious low-carb blends.

1. Avocado Chocolate Heaven

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup unsweetened almond milk
  • 1 medium avocado
  • 2 tablespoons cocoa powder
  • 1 tablespoon almond butter
  • 1-2 tablespoons of stevia or preferred sweetener

Start by blending the almond milk and avocado together until smooth. Add your cocoa powder, almond butter, and stevia, and blend again until well combined.

2. Berry Blast

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup unsweetened almond milk
  • 1 cup mixed berries (fresh or frozen)
  • 1 tablespoon chia seeds
  • 1 tablespoon almond butter
  • 1 scoop vanilla protein powder

Combine almond milk, berries, chia seeds, almond butter, and protein powder in a blender. Blend until smooth and enjoy this protein-packed, berrylicious treat!

3. Matcha madness

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup unsweetened coconut milk
  • 1 teaspoon matcha green tea powder
  • 1 small ripe avocado
  • 1 tablespoon honey
  • 1 scoop vanilla protein powder

Blitz the coconut milk and matcha green tea powder together. Next, add in the avocado, honey, and protein powder and blend until it reaches your desired consistency.

4. Almond Butter Bliss

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup unsweetened almond milk
  • 1 scoop vanilla protein powder
  • 1-2 tablespoons almond butter
  • 1 small banana (optional)
  • 1 tablespoon chia seeds

Combine almond milk, protein powder, almond butter, banana (if using), and chia seeds. Blend until creamy and smooth.

5. Spicy Peanut Butter Blast

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup unsweetened coconut milk
  • 1 scoop chocolate protein powder
  • 2 tablespoons natural peanut butter
  • 1/4 teaspoon cayenne pepper
  • 1 tablespoon unsweetened cocoa
  • 1 tablespoons hemp seeds

Blend your coconut milk, chocolate protein powder, and peanut butter on high until smooth. Stir in the cayenne pepper and cocoa after blending, then top with hemp seeds. The cayenne pepper adds just the right amount of heat for a spicy kick.

6. Vanilla Almond Kale Booster

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup unsweetened almond milk
  • 1 cup chopped kale
  • 1 scoop vanilla protein powder
  • 1 tablespoon almond butter
  • 1 teaspoon honey

Combine almond milk, chopped kale, protein powder, almond butter, and honey. Blend until smooth for an unexpected and delicious kale-based smoothie.

7. Zesty Pineapple & Ginger Dream

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup unsweetened coconut milk
  • 1 cup pineapple chunks
  • 1-inch piece of ginger, peeled
  • 1 tablespoon flaxseed meal
  • 1 scoop unflavored protein powder

Put the coconut milk, pineapple, ginger, flaxseed meal, and protein powder into the blender and blitz until mixed.

8. Blue Cheese Spinach Temptation

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup unsweetened almond milk
  • 1 cup baby spinach
  • 1 scoop unflavored protein powder
  • 1/2 ounce blue cheese, crumbled
  • 1 tablespoon pumpkin seeds

Combine almond milk, spinach, protein powder, blue cheese, and pumpkin seeds in a blender, and you’ll have an unexpectedly delightful low-carb treat.

9. Pina Colada Protein Delight

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup unsweetened coconut milk
  • 1/2 cup unsweetened shredded coconut
  • 1 scoop vanilla protein powder
  • 1/2 cup frozen pineapple chunks
  • 1 tablespoon MCT oil

Blend the coconut milk, shredded coconut, protein powder, frozen pineapple, and MCT oil until smooth, and revive a tropical memory with this guilt-free pina colada smoothie.

10. Cucumber, Mint, & Avocado

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup unsweetened almond milk
  • 1/2 English cucumber
  • 1/2 small ripe avocado
  • Handful of fresh mint leaves
  • 1 tablespoon hemp seeds
  • 1/2 scoop unflavored protein powder

Combine almond milk, cucumber, avocado, mint leaves, hemp seeds, and protein powder in a blender. Blend until it reaches a silky, refreshing texture.

11. Espresso Chocolate Chip

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup unsweetened almond milk
  • 1-2 tablespoons instant coffee
  • 1 tablespoon coconut oil
  • 1/2 scoop protein powder
  • 1 tablespoon unsweetened cocoa
  • 5-6 ice cubes
  • Optional: 1 tablespoon dairy-free chocolate chips

Blend almond milk, instant coffee, coconut oil, protein powder, and cocoa together, then add ice cubes and blend again. For extra indulgence, add in chocolate chips before blending.

12. Mango, Turmeric & Coconut

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup unsweetened coconut milk
  • 1 frozen mango chunk
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric
  • 1 tablespoon flaxseed meal
  • 1/2 teaspoon honey
  • 1 scoop vanilla protein powder

Combine coconut milk, frozen mango, turmeric, flaxseed meal, honey, and vanilla protein powder. Then blend until you’ve got a creamy, orange-hued smoothie.

13. Chai Latte Dream

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup unsweetened almond milk
  • 1 chai tea bag (steeped & cooled)
  • 1 scoop unflavored protein powder
  • 1 tablespoon almond butter
  • 1 tablespoon unsweetened cocoa
  • 1 teaspoon honey

Combine almond milk, cooled steeped chai tea bag, protein powder, almond butter, cocoa, and honey. Blend until smooth and creamy.

14. Strawberry & Cream Silk

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup unsweetened almond milk
  • 1/2 cup frozen strawberries
  • 1 scoop vanilla protein powder
  • 1/2 cup spinach (trust us!)
  • 1 tablespoon chia seeds

Blend almond milk, frozen strawberries, vanilla protein powder, spinach, and chia seeds for a deliciously deceiving green smoothie.

15. Watermelon, Mint & Lime

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup unsweetened coconut milk
  • 1 cup seedless watermelon chunks
  • 1/4 lime, juiced
  • 1 tablespoon hemp seeds
  • Handful of fresh mint leaves

Combine coconut milk, watermelon chunks, lime juice, hemp seeds, and mint leaves. Blend until you have a divine, hydrating summer treat.

16. ‘Oreo’ Dream

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup unsweetened almond milk
  • 1 scoop chocolate protein powder
  • 1 scoop vanilla protein powder
  • 2 tablespoons unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1 tablespoon almond butter
  • 5-6 ice cubes

Blend almond milk, chocolate and vanilla protein powders, cocoa, and almond butter on high until smooth. Toss in the ice and blend again until you’ve got an ‘Oreo’ cookie lover’s dream come true.

17. Peach Pie Surprise

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup unsweetened almond milk
  • 1 frozen peach half
  • 1 sprinkle of ground cinnamon
  • 1 tablespoon pumpkin seeds
  • 1 scoop vanilla protein powder
  • 1 tablespoon unsweetened almond butter

Blend almond milk, frozen peach, cinnamon, pumpkin seeds, vanilla protein powder, and almond butter until creamy and well mixed.

18. Cinnamon Roll Delight

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup unsweetened almond milk
  • 1 scoop vanilla protein powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1 tablespoon unsweetened coconut butter
  • 1 tablespoon unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 5-6 ice cubes

Combine almond milk, vanilla protein powder, vanilla extract, cinnamon, coconut butter, and cocoa powder, then blend in ice and blend until smooth.

19. Lemon Berry Burst

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup unsweetened coconut milk
  • 1 cup mixed berries
  • 1 tablespoon chia seeds
  • 1/2 lemon, juiced
  • 1/2 inch fresh ginger, peeled
  • 1 scoop vanilla protein powder

Blend coconut milk, mixed berries, chia seeds, lemon juice, and ginger together. Finally, throw in the protein powder and blend until it reaches the desired smoothness.

20. Nutty Chia Pudding Smoothie

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup unsweetened almond milk
  • 2 tablespoons chia seeds
  • 1 scoop vanilla protein powder
  • 1/4 cup chopped walnuts
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • Warm water (small amount)

Soak the chia seeds in a small amount of almond milk and warm water for 5 minutes. Blend the soaked chia seeds, remaining almond milk, vanilla protein powder, chopped walnuts, and vanilla extract. This will give you a texture closely resembling a chia pudding.

These delightful Keto-friendly smoothie recipes make sticking to your low-carb lifestyle easy and enjoyable. With variety at your fingertips, there are infinite possibilities for blending up a devilishly good drink that won’t derail your Keto progress. So, show off that fancy blender and mix up some healthy goodness to kick off your day!
