Deliciously Tangy Lemon Bars: A Refreshing Citrus Dessert


Lemon bars are a classic dessert that never fails to delight the taste buds. The combination of tangy lemon curd and buttery shortbread crust creates a refreshing and indulgent treat that is perfect for any occasion. Whether you’re hosting a summer barbecue, a brunch with friends, or simply craving a zesty dessert, these deliciously tangy lemon bars are sure to impress. In this recipe, we’ll explore how to create the perfect balance of sweet and tangy flavors in every bite.

Ingredients

For the Shortbread Crust:

  • 1 cup (226g) unsalted butter, softened
  • 1/2 cup (100g) granulated sugar
  • 2 cups (240g) all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt

For the Lemon Filling:

  • 1 1/2 cups (300g) granulated sugar
  • 1/4 cup (30g) all-purpose flour
  • 4 large eggs
  • 2/3 cup (160ml) freshly squeezed lemon juice
  • Zest of 2 lemons
  • Powdered sugar, for dusting

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven: Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C) and line a 9x13-inch baking pan with parchment paper, leaving an overhang on the sides for easy removal.

  2. Prepare the Shortbread Crust: In a large mixing bowl, cream together the softened butter and granulated sugar until light and fluffy. Add the flour and salt, and mix until the dough comes together. Press the dough evenly into the bottom of the prepared baking pan. Bake for 20-25 minutes, or until the crust is lightly golden.

  3. Make the Lemon Filling: While the crust is baking, prepare the lemon filling. In a separate bowl, whisk together the granulated sugar and flour. Add the eggs, lemon juice, and lemon zest, and whisk until smooth and well combined.

  4. Bake the Lemon Bars: Pour the lemon filling over the hot crust, and return the pan to the oven. Bake for an additional 25-30 minutes, or until the filling is set and the edges are lightly golden.

  5. Cool and Serve: Allow the lemon bars to cool completely in the pan on a wire rack. Once cooled, refrigerate for at least 2 hours to chill and set the filling. Dust with powdered sugar before slicing into bars.

Tips for Success

  • For the best flavor, use freshly squeezed lemon juice and zest.
  • Be careful not to overbake the bars, as the filling should be just set with a slight jiggle in the center when gently shaken.
  • To achieve clean and neat slices, use a sharp knife and wipe it clean between each cut.

Serving Suggestions

These deliciously tangy lemon bars are perfect for serving at picnics, potlucks, or as a sweet ending to a dinner party. They pair beautifully with a dollop of whipped cream, a scoop of vanilla ice cream, or a sprinkle of fresh berries for an extra burst of color and flavor.

Conclusion

In conclusion, these deliciously tangy lemon bars are a delightful balance of sweet and tangy flavors that are sure to brighten any occasion. With a buttery shortbread crust and a luscious lemon filling, these bars are a refreshing citrus dessert that will leave your guests coming back for more. Whether you’re a seasoned baker or new to the world of desserts, this recipe is a must-try for anyone who appreciates the vibrant taste of lemons. So, roll up your sleeves, preheat your oven, and get ready to indulge in the irresistible allure of these lemony delights!
