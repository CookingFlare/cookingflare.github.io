Paleo Chicken Fajita Casserole Recipes: 5 Delicious and Grain-Free Options


If you’re following a paleo diet or simply looking for a healthy and delicious meal option, look no further than these Paleo Chicken Fajita Casserole recipes. Packed with flavor and nutrients, these grain-free options are sure to be a hit at your next dinner party or family gathering. So grab your apron and let’s get cooking!

Option 1: Classic Paleo Chicken Fajita Casserole

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound boneless, skinless chicken breasts, sliced into strips
  • 2 bell peppers, sliced
  • 1 onion, sliced
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh cilantro, for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C) and grease a casserole dish.
  2. In a large skillet, heat the olive oil over medium-high heat. Add the chicken strips and cook until browned, about 5 minutes per side. Remove the chicken from the skillet and set aside.
  3. In the same skillet, add the bell peppers, onion, garlic, chili powder, cumin, paprika, salt, and pepper. Sauté until the vegetables are tender, about 5 minutes.
  4. Return the chicken to the skillet and stir to combine with the vegetables. Transfer the mixture to the greased casserole dish.
  5. Bake in the preheated oven for 20-25 minutes, or until the chicken is cooked through and the vegetables are caramelized.
  6. Garnish with fresh cilantro before serving. Enjoy!

Option 2: Spicy Lime Paleo Chicken Fajita Casserole

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound boneless, skinless chicken thighs, sliced into strips
  • 2 bell peppers, sliced
  • 1 onion, sliced
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 jalapeño, seeded and diced
  • Zest and juice of 1 lime
  • 1 teaspoon chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh cilantro, for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Follow steps 1-4 from Option 1.
  2. In a small bowl, whisk together the olive oil, garlic, jalapeño, lime zest, lime juice, chili powder, cumin, salt, and pepper.
  3. Pour the marinade over the chicken and vegetable mixture in the casserole dish. Stir to coat evenly.
  4. Bake in the preheated oven for 20-25 minutes, or until the chicken is cooked through and the vegetables are tender.
  5. Garnish with fresh cilantro before serving. Enjoy!

Option 3: Mediterranean Paleo Chicken Fajita Casserole

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound boneless, skinless chicken breasts, sliced into strips
  • 2 bell peppers, sliced
  • 1 onion, sliced
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Kalamata olives, for garnish
  • Fresh parsley, for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Follow steps 1-4 from Option 1.
  2. In a small bowl, mix together the olive oil, garlic, dried oregano, dried basil, dried thyme, salt, and pepper.
  3. Pour the marinade over the chicken and vegetable mixture in the casserole dish. Stir to coat evenly.
  4. Bake in the preheated oven for 20-25 minutes, or until the chicken is cooked through and the vegetables are caramelized.
  5. Garnish with Kalamata olives and fresh parsley before serving. Enjoy!

Option 4: Coconut Curry Paleo Chicken Fajita Casserole

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound boneless, skinless chicken thighs, sliced into strips
  • 2 bell peppers, sliced
  • 1 onion, sliced
  • 2 tablespoons coconut oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon curry powder
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric
  • 1 teaspoon ginger powder
  • 1 can (13.5 ounces) full-fat coconut milk
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh cilantro, for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C) and grease a casserole dish.
  2. In a large skillet, heat the coconut oil over medium-high heat. Add the chicken strips and cook until browned, about 5 minutes per side. Remove the chicken from the skillet and set aside.
  3. In the same skillet, add the bell peppers, onion, garlic, curry powder, turmeric, ginger powder, salt, and pepper. Sauté until the vegetables are tender, about 5 minutes.
  4. Return the chicken to the skillet and stir to combine with the vegetables. Pour in the coconut milk and mix well.
  5. Transfer the mixture to the greased casserole dish and bake in the preheated oven for 20-25 minutes, or until the chicken is cooked through and the flavors have melded together.
  6. Garnish with fresh cilantro before serving. Enjoy!

Option 5: Tex-Mex Paleo Chicken Fajita Casserole

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound boneless, skinless chicken breasts, sliced into strips
  • 2 bell peppers, sliced
  • 1 onion, sliced
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh cilantro, for garnish
  • Sliced avocado, for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Follow steps 1-4 from Option 1.
  2. In a small bowl, mix together the chili powder, cumin, paprika, garlic powder, salt, and pepper.
  3. Sprinkle the spice mixture over the chicken and vegetable mixture in the casserole dish. Stir to coat evenly.
  4. Bake in the preheated oven for 20-25 minutes, or until the chicken is cooked through and the vegetables are caramelized.
  5. Garnish with fresh cilantro and sliced avocado before serving. Enjoy!

There you have it! Five delicious and grain-free options for Paleo Chicken Fajita Casserole. Whether you prefer classic flavors or want to spice things up, these recipes are sure to satisfy your cravings while keeping you on track with your paleo lifestyle. Give them a try and let your taste buds be the judge. Enjoy your meal and happy cooking!
