Irresistible Christmas Cookie Recipes for Santa and Friends


Christmas is right around the corner, and what better way to spread some holiday cheer than by baking delicious cookies for Santa and his friends? Whether you’re looking to leave a sweet treat for Santa Claus or share some delicious goodies with your loved ones, these irresistible Christmas cookie recipes are sure to bring joy and delight to everyone. So put on your apron, preheat your oven, and let’s get baking!

1. Classic Sugar Cookies

Ingredients:

  • 2 3/4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1 1/2 cups white sugar
  • 1 egg
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Instructions:

  1. In a medium-sized bowl, whisk together the flour, baking soda, and baking powder. Set aside.
  2. In a separate large bowl, cream together the softened butter and white sugar until light and fluffy.
  3. Beat in the egg and vanilla extract until well combined.
  4. Gradually add the dry ingredients to the wet ingredients, mixing until the dough comes together.
  5. Divide the dough into two equal portions, wrap each in plastic wrap, and refrigerate for at least 1 hour.
  6. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C) and line baking sheets with parchment paper.
  7. Roll out the chilled dough on a lightly floured surface to about 1/4 inch thickness.
  8. Use your favorite festive cookie cutters to cut out shapes from the dough.
  9. Place the cut-out cookies onto the prepared baking sheets, leaving some space between them.
  10. Bake for 8-10 minutes or until the edges are lightly golden.
  11. Allow the cookies to cool on the baking sheets for a few minutes before transferring them to wire racks to cool completely.
  12. Once cooled, decorate the cookies with icing, sprinkles, or any other decorations of your choice.

2. Gingerbread Cookies

Ingredients:

  • 3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 3/4 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 2/3 cup packed brown sugar
  • 1/2 cup molasses
  • 1 large egg
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground ginger
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cloves
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt

Instructions:

  1. In a medium-sized bowl, whisk together the flour, baking soda, cinnamon, ginger, cloves, and salt. Set aside.
  2. In a separate large bowl, cream together the softened butter and brown sugar until light and fluffy.
  3. Beat in the molasses and egg until well combined.
  4. Gradually add the dry ingredients to the wet ingredients, mixing until the dough comes together.
  5. Divide the dough into two equal portions, wrap each in plastic wrap, and refrigerate for at least 2 hours.
  6. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C) and line baking sheets with parchment paper.
  7. Roll out the chilled dough on a lightly floured surface to about 1/4 inch thickness.
  8. Use your favorite gingerbread cookie cutters to cut out shapes from the dough.
  9. Place the cut-out cookies onto the prepared baking sheets, leaving some space between them.
  10. Bake for 10-12 minutes or until the edges are set.
  11. Allow the cookies to cool on the baking sheets for a few minutes before transferring them to wire racks to cool completely.
  12. Once cooled, you can leave them plain or decorate them with icing, candy, or any other decorations you desire.

3. Peppermint Chocolate Cookies

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 cup packed brown sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 2 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 3/4 cup cocoa powder
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 1/2 cups chopped peppermint chocolate candies

Instructions:

  1. In a large bowl, cream together the softened butter, granulated sugar, and brown sugar until light and fluffy.
  2. Beat in the eggs, one at a time, followed by the vanilla extract.
  3. In a separate medium-sized bowl, whisk together the flour, cocoa powder, baking soda, and salt.
  4. Gradually add the dry ingredients to the wet ingredients, mixing until just combined.
  5. Stir in the chopped peppermint chocolate candies.
  6. Cover the dough and refrigerate for at least 1 hour.
  7. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C) and line baking sheets with parchment paper.
  8. Roll the chilled dough into 1-inch balls and place them onto the prepared baking sheets, leaving some space between them.
  9. Bake for 10-12 minutes or until the edges are set.
  10. Allow the cookies to cool on the baking sheets for a few minutes before transferring them to wire racks to cool completely.

Conclusion

These irresistible Christmas cookie recipes are perfect for spreading holiday cheer and indulging in some delicious treats. From classic sugar cookies to gingerbread and peppermint chocolate cookies, there’s something for everyone to enjoy. So gather your ingredients, get into the holiday spirit, and start baking these delightful cookies that are sure to make Santa and his friends very happy. Happy baking and Merry Christmas!
