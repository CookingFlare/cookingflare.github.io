10 Delicious and Beautifully Plated Brunch Sandwich Recipes


Brunch, the perfect meal that combines the best of both worlds - the comfort of breakfast and the sophistication of lunch. And what’s a better way to enjoy this delightful meal than with a beautifully plated brunch sandwich? In this post, we’ll explore 10 mouth-watering and visually stunning brunch sandwich recipes that will elevate your brunch game and impress your guests.

1. Avocado and Bacon Brunch Sandwich

Start your day with a creamy and savory avocado and bacon brunch sandwich. Toasted baguette slices are filled with crispy bacon, ripe avocado, and a fried egg, all held together with a tangy aioli spread.

Ingredients:

  • 4 slices of baguette
  • 6 slices of bacon
  • 2 ripe avocados, sliced
  • 1 large egg
  • 1 tablespoon of aioli
  • Salt and pepper to taste

2. Cinnamon French Toast Brunch Sandwich

Indulge in the sweet and savory flavors of cinnamon French toast brunch sandwich. Thick slices of cinnamon-infused French toast are filled with creamy peanut butter, sliced bananas, and a drizzle of honey, all nestled between crispy baguette slices.

Ingredients:

  • 4 slices of baguette
  • 2 tablespoons of cinnamon
  • 2 tablespoons of peanut butter
  • 2 ripe bananas, sliced
  • 1 tablespoon of honey
  • 2 tablespoons of milk
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1/4 teaspoon of salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon of ground cinnamon

3. Smoked Salmon and Cream Cheese Brunch Sandwich

Experience the luxurious flavors of smoked salmon and cream cheese brunch sandwich. Toasted baguette slices are filled with smoked salmon, cream cheese, capers, and thinly sliced red onion, all held together with a tangy lemon spread.

Ingredients:

  • 4 slices of baguette
  • 1/2 cup of smoked salmon
  • 1/4 cup of cream cheese
  • 1 tablespoon of capers
  • 1 tablespoon of thinly sliced red onion
  • 1 tablespoon of lemon spread
  • Salt and pepper to taste

4. Grilled Chicken and Avocado Brunch Sandwich

Enjoy the bold and refreshing flavors of grilled chicken and avocado brunch sandwich. Toasted baguette slices are filled with grilled chicken breast, ripe avocado, and a tangy chipotle mayo, all held together with a sprinkle of red pepper flakes.

Ingredients:

  • 4 slices of baguette
  • 1 pound of boneless, skinless chicken breast
  • 2 ripe avocados, sliced
  • 1/4 cup of chipotle mayo
  • 1 teaspoon of red pepper flakes
  • Salt and pepper to taste

5. Prosciutto and Fig Brunch Sandwich

Treat yourself to the sweet and savory flavors of prosciutto and fig brunch sandwich. Toasted baguette slices are filled with crispy prosciutto, sweet caramelized figs, and a tangy balsamic glaze, all held together with a sprinkle of arugula.

Ingredients:

  • 4 slices of baguette
  • 6 slices of prosciutto
  • 1/4 cup of caramelized figs
  • 1 tablespoon of balsamic glaze
  • 1/4 cup of arugula
  • Salt and pepper to taste

6. Spinach and Feta Brunch Sandwich

Savor the classic Greek flavors of spinach and feta brunch sandwich. Toasted baguette slices are filled with wilted spinach, crumbled feta cheese, and a tangy lemon spread, all held together with a sprinkle of red pepper flakes.

Ingredients:

  • 4 slices of baguette
  • 1/4 cup of wilted spinach
  • 1/4 cup of crumbled feta cheese
  • 1 tablespoon of lemon spread
  • 1 teaspoon of red pepper flakes
  • Salt and pepper to taste

7. Turkey and Brie Brunch Sandwich

Indulge in the rich and creamy flavors of turkey and brie brunch sandwich. Toasted baguette slices are filled with sliced turkey breast, melted brie cheese, and a tangy cranberry spread, all held together with a sprinkle of chopped fresh thyme.

Ingredients:

  • 4 slices of baguette
  • 1 pound of sliced turkey breast
  • 1/4 cup of melted brie cheese
  • 1 tablespoon of cranberry spread
  • 1 tablespoon of chopped fresh thyme
  • Salt and pepper to taste

8. Roasted Veggie Brunch Sandwich

Experience the vibrant and healthy flavors of roasted veggie brunch sandwich. Toasted baguette slices are filled with roasted eggplant, zucchini, and red bell pepper, all held together with a tangy hummus spread.

Ingredients:

  • 4 slices of baguette
  • 1 medium eggplant, sliced
  • 1 medium zucchini, sliced
  • 1 medium red bell pepper, sliced
  • 1/4 cup of hummus spread
  • Salt and pepper to taste

9. Breakfast Burrito Brunch Sandwich

Start your day with a flavorful breakfast burrito brunch sandwich. Scrambled eggs, crispy bacon, and shredded cheddar cheese are wrapped in a flour tortilla and filled with a tangy salsa spread.

Ingredients:

  • 4 flour tortillas
  • 4 large eggs
  • 6 slices of bacon
  • 1 cup of shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1/4 cup of salsa spread
  • Salt and pepper to taste

10. Croque Monsieur Brunch Sandwich

Indulge in the rich and creamy flavors of croque Monsieur brunch sandwich. Toasted baguette slices are filled with ham, melted Swiss cheese, and a tangy béchamel sauce, all held together with a sprinkle of chopped fresh parsley.

Ingredients:

  • 4 slices of baguette
  • 4 slices of ham
  • 1/4 cup of melted Swiss cheese
  • 1 tablespoon of béchamel sauce
  • 1 tablespoon of chopped fresh parsley
  • Salt and pepper to taste

These 10 delicious and beautifully plated brunch sandwich recipes are sure to impress your guests and elevate your brunch game. Whether you’re looking for sweet and savory flavors or bold and refreshing flavors, there’s something for everyone in this collection of brunch sandwich recipes. So go ahead, get creative, and start building your own brunch sandwich masterpieces!
