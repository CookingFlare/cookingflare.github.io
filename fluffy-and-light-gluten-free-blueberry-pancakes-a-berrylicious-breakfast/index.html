Fluffy and Light Gluten-Free Blueberry Pancakes: A Berrylicious Breakfast


Looking for a delicious and wholesome breakfast option? These fluffy and light gluten-free blueberry pancakes are the perfect way to start your day. Not only are they incredibly delicious, but they are also easy to make and suitable for those with gluten sensitivities. Packed with juicy blueberries and a hint of vanilla, these pancakes are a burst of berrylicious flavor in every bite. So, let’s dive into the recipe and get ready to whip up a delightful breakfast treat!

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups gluten-free all-purpose flour
  • 3 tablespoons sugar
  • 1 tablespoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 1/4 cups almond milk (or any milk of your choice)
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 egg
  • 3 tablespoons melted butter (or coconut oil for a dairy-free option)
  • 1 cup fresh blueberries

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Dry Ingredients: In a large mixing bowl, combine the gluten-free all-purpose flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt. Whisk the dry ingredients together until well combined.

  2. Mix the Wet Ingredients: In a separate bowl, whisk together the almond milk, vanilla extract, and egg until smooth. Slowly pour in the melted butter while whisking continuously.

  3. Combine the Batter: Pour the wet ingredients into the bowl of dry ingredients. Gently fold the mixture together using a spatula until just combined. Be careful not to overmix; a few lumps are okay. The key to fluffy pancakes is not overmixing the batter.

  4. Add the Blueberries: Gently fold in the fresh blueberries, being careful not to break them apart.

  5. Heat the Griddle or Pan: Place a non-stick griddle or skillet over medium heat and lightly grease it with cooking spray or a small amount of butter.

  6. Cook the Pancakes: Once the griddle is hot, pour 1/4 cup of batter for each pancake onto the surface. Cook until bubbles form on the surface of the pancake, then flip and cook the other side until golden brown.

  7. Serve and Enjoy: Transfer the cooked pancakes to a plate and serve them warm. Top with additional fresh blueberries, a drizzle of maple syrup, and a dollop of whipped cream if desired.

Tips for Perfect Pancakes

  • Ensure that your griddle or skillet is preheated before pouring the batter to achieve golden-brown pancakes.
  • If using frozen blueberries, toss them in a little bit of gluten-free flour before adding them to the batter to prevent them from sinking to the bottom of the pancakes.
  • For extra fluffiness, let the batter rest for a few minutes before cooking the pancakes.

Conclusion

These fluffy and light gluten-free blueberry pancakes are a delightful breakfast option that will surely impress your family and friends. Whether you’re following a gluten-free diet or simply enjoy the goodness of blueberries, this recipe is a must-try. The burst of juicy blueberries combined with the light, fluffy texture of the pancakes makes for a truly berrylicious breakfast experience. So, gather your ingredients and get ready to savor the deliciousness of these delightful blueberry pancakes. Enjoy!
