Instant Pot Beef and Broccoli Stir-Fry: Quick and Healthy


Are you looking for a delicious and nutritious meal that can be prepared in no time? Look no further than this Instant Pot Beef and Broccoli Stir-Fry recipe! Packed with flavor and loaded with nutrients, this dish is perfect for those busy weeknights when you want something quick, easy, and satisfying.

Ingredients

To make this mouthwatering stir-fry, you’ll need the following ingredients:

  • 1 pound of beef (sirloin or flank steak, thinly sliced)
  • 2 cups of broccoli florets
  • 1 bell pepper (sliced)
  • 1 onion (sliced)
  • 3 cloves of garlic (minced)
  • 2 tablespoons of soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon of oyster sauce
  • 1 tablespoon of sesame oil
  • 1 teaspoon of cornstarch
  • 1/2 cup of beef broth

Instructions

Here’s how you can whip up this delightful dish in your Instant Pot:

  1. Start by turning on your Instant Pot and selecting the “Saute” function. Add the sesame oil and let it heat up.

  2. Once the oil is hot, add the beef slices and saute until they are browned on all sides. This should take about 3-4 minutes.

  3. Next, add the garlic, bell pepper, and onion to the pot. Stir-fry for another 2 minutes, until the vegetables start to soften.

  4. In a small bowl, whisk together the soy sauce, oyster sauce, cornstarch, and beef broth. Pour this mixture into the Instant Pot and stir well to combine.

  5. Add the broccoli florets to the pot, making sure they are evenly distributed.

  6. Place the lid on the Instant Pot, seal the vent, and select the “Pressure Cook” function. Set the timer for 5 minutes.

  7. Once the timer goes off, carefully release the pressure manually. Remove the lid and give the stir-fry a good stir.

  8. Serve the Instant Pot Beef and Broccoli Stir-Fry over a bed of steamed rice or noodles. Garnish with some sesame seeds or green onions for an extra pop of flavor.

Benefits of Instant Pot Cooking

Using an Instant Pot to prepare this Beef and Broccoli Stir-Fry offers numerous advantages. First and foremost, it saves you a significant amount of time. The pressure cooking function ensures that your meal is ready in a fraction of the time compared to traditional stovetop cooking methods.

Additionally, the sealed environment of the Instant Pot helps to lock in the flavors and nutrients of the ingredients. This means that your stir-fry will be bursting with flavor and packed with essential vitamins and minerals.

Tips and Variations

  • If you prefer a spicier stir-fry, feel free to add some red pepper flakes or a drizzle of Sriracha sauce to the mix.

  • For a vegetarian option, you can substitute the beef with tofu or tempeh. Simply marinate the tofu or tempeh in the sauce mixture for about 15 minutes before adding it to the Instant Pot.

  • If you’re not a fan of broccoli, you can substitute it with other vegetables like snap peas, carrots, or mushrooms.

  • Don’t forget to slice your beef as thinly as possible. This will ensure that it cooks quickly and evenly.

Final Thoughts

This Instant Pot Beef and Broccoli Stir-Fry is a fantastic option for a quick and healthy meal. With the convenience of the Instant Pot and the vibrant flavors of the sauce, this dish is sure to become a family favorite. Whether you’re a busy professional or a parent on the go, this recipe is bound to save you time in the kitchen without compromising on taste or nutrition. Give it a try and enjoy a delicious meal in no time!
