Delicious Vegan Breakfast Cookies for a Grab-and-Go Morning


As we all know, mornings can be chaotic. Rushing to get ready for work or school, making sure everyone has what they need, and trying to squeeze in a quick breakfast before running out the door. It’s easy to get caught up in the hustle and bustle and forget about the most important meal of the day. But what if you could have a delicious, nutritious, and convenient breakfast option that’s easy to grab and go? Enter the vegan breakfast cookie.

These chewy, sweet, and satisfying treats are the perfect solution for busy mornings. Made with wholesome ingredients like oats, nuts, and dried fruit, they’re a great way to fuel up for the day ahead. And the best part? They’re vegan, so you can feel good about what you’re putting in your body.

In this post, we’ll explore the recipe for delicious vegan breakfast cookies that are perfect for a grab-and-go morning. We’ll cover the ingredients, instructions, and tips for making the most of these tasty treats.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups rolled oats
  • 1 cup chopped nuts (such as almonds or walnuts)
  • 1/2 cup dried fruit (such as cranberries or raisins)
  • 1/4 cup coconut sugar
  • 1/4 cup maple syrup
  • 1/2 cup vegan butter (such as Earth Balance), softened
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • Pinch of salt
  • Optional: 1/2 cup chocolate chips or 1/4 cup shredded coconut

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C). Line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
  2. In a large bowl, combine the oats, nuts, and dried fruit.
  3. In a separate bowl, cream together the coconut sugar and maple syrup until well combined. Add the vegan butter and vanilla extract, and mix until smooth.
  4. Add the wet ingredients to the dry ingredients and mix until a dough forms. If using, add chocolate chips or shredded coconut and fold them into the dough.
  5. Scoop tablespoon-sized balls of dough onto the prepared baking sheet, leaving about 2 inches of space between each cookie.
  6. Bake for 12-15 minutes, or until the edges are lightly golden.
  7. Remove the cookies from the oven and let them cool on the baking sheet for 5 minutes before transferring them to a wire rack to cool completely.

Tips and Variations

  • To make these cookies more substantial, try adding some protein powder or nut butter to the dough.
  • If you prefer a crisper cookie, bake for an additional 2-3 minutes.
  • Experiment with different types of nuts, seeds, or dried fruit to change up the flavor and texture.
  • Consider using a silicone mat instead of parchment paper for easier cleanup.

Conclusion

These delicious vegan breakfast cookies are the perfect solution for busy mornings. With their wholesome ingredients and convenient grab-and-go design, they’re a great way to fuel up for the day ahead. Whether you’re heading to work, school, or a workout, these cookies are sure to satisfy your cravings and keep you going until lunchtime. So go ahead, give them a try, and start your day off right!
