Penne with Vodka Sauce: A Creamy and Boozy Pasta Recipe


If you’re a fan of rich, creamy pasta dishes with a touch of decadence, then penne with vodka sauce is a recipe that should definitely be on your radar. This classic Italian-American dish combines the smooth flavors of a creamy tomato sauce with the subtle kick of vodka, resulting in a luxurious and indulgent pasta experience that’s perfect for a special dinner or a cozy night in.

Ingredients

To make this delectable penne with vodka sauce, you’ll need the following ingredients:

  • 1 pound penne pasta
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/2 cup vodka
  • 1 can (28 ounces) crushed tomatoes
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh basil, for garnish
  • Grated Parmesan cheese, for serving

Instructions

  1. Cook the Penne: Start by cooking the penne pasta according to the package instructions. Be sure to cook the pasta until it’s al dente, as it will continue to cook slightly in the sauce later on.

  2. Prepare the Sauce: In a large skillet, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the chopped onion and sauté until it becomes soft and translucent. Then, add the minced garlic and cook for another minute until fragrant.

  3. Add the Vodka: Pour in the vodka and allow it to simmer for a few minutes, giving it time to reduce slightly and infuse the sauce with its distinctive flavor.

  4. Simmer with Tomatoes: Once the vodka has reduced, add the crushed tomatoes to the skillet, stirring to combine everything well. Let the sauce simmer for about 15-20 minutes, allowing the flavors to meld together and the sauce to thicken slightly.

  5. Incorporate the Cream: After the sauce has simmered, pour in the heavy cream, stirring gently until it’s fully incorporated. Allow the sauce to heat through, but be careful not to let it come to a boil.

  6. Season and Serve: Season the sauce with salt and pepper to taste. Once the penne pasta is cooked, drain it and add it to the skillet with the vodka sauce. Toss the pasta gently in the sauce, ensuring that each piece of penne is coated evenly.

  7. Garnish and Enjoy: To finish, sprinkle some fresh basil over the pasta for a burst of freshness and color. Serve the penne with vodka sauce in individual bowls, topped with a generous sprinkling of grated Parmesan cheese.

Tips for Success

  • Be sure to use a good quality vodka for this recipe, as the flavor of the vodka will shine through in the finished dish.
  • If you prefer a smoother sauce, you can use an immersion blender to puree the sauce before adding the heavy cream.
  • Leftovers can be refrigerated and reheated the next day, making this dish a wonderful option for meal prep.

Final Thoughts

Penne with vodka sauce is a dish that effortlessly combines the warmth of a classic Italian pasta with the richness of a creamy, boozy sauce. Whether you’re cooking for a gathering of friends or simply treating yourself to a special meal, this recipe is sure to impress. So, next time you’re craving a comforting and indulgent pasta dish, give this penne with vodka sauce a try. You won’t be disappointed!
