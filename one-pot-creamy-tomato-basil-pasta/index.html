One Pot Creamy Tomato Basil Pasta


If you’re a fan of quick and easy pasta dishes that are bursting with flavor, then you’re in for a treat! This One Pot Creamy Tomato Basil Pasta is the perfect dish for busy weeknights when you want a delicious meal without spending hours in the kitchen. The best part? It’s all made in one pot, which means minimal cleanup and maximum satisfaction.

Ingredients:

  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 small onion, finely diced
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can (14 oz) diced tomatoes
  • 2 1/2 cups vegetable broth
  • 1/2 teaspoon red pepper flakes (adjust to taste)
  • 8 oz dried pasta (such as penne or rigatoni)
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh basil leaves, torn, for garnish
  • Grated Parmesan cheese, for serving

Instructions:

  1. Sauté the Aromatics: Heat the olive oil in a large pot over medium heat. Add the diced onion and sauté until translucent, then add the minced garlic and cook for another minute until fragrant.

  2. Add the Tomatoes and Broth: Pour in the diced tomatoes and vegetable broth, then season with red pepper flakes, salt, and pepper. Give everything a good stir, and bring the mixture to a gentle boil.

  3. Cook the Pasta: Once the mixture is boiling, add the dried pasta and stir to combine. Reduce the heat to a simmer and let the pasta cook, stirring occasionally, for about 10-12 minutes or until al dente.

  4. Add the Cream: Pour in the heavy cream and stir it into the pasta until well combined. Let the pasta simmer for another 2-3 minutes to allow the sauce to thicken slightly.

  5. Finish and Serve: Once the pasta is cooked to your liking and the sauce has thickened, remove the pot from the heat. Taste and adjust the seasoning if needed. Serve the creamy tomato basil pasta in bowls, garnished with torn fresh basil leaves and a generous sprinkle of grated Parmesan cheese.

Why You’ll Love This Recipe:

Quick and Easy:

This One Pot Creamy Tomato Basil Pasta is a lifesaver on busy days. With minimal prep and cooking time, you can have a satisfying meal on the table in no time.

Minimal Cleanup:

The beauty of a one-pot dish is that you only have one pot to clean at the end. No need to juggle multiple pots and pans – it’s a win-win situation!

Family-Friendly:

This dish is loved by both kids and adults alike. The creamy tomato basil sauce is comforting and familiar, making it a great option for family dinners.

Versatile:

Feel free to customize this recipe to suit your taste. Add in some cooked chicken or shrimp for a protein boost, or throw in some extra veggies for added nutrition.

Crowd-Pleaser:

Whether you’re hosting a casual dinner with friends or a cozy night in, this creamy tomato basil pasta is sure to impress. It’s a crowd-pleaser that never disappoints.

So there you have it – a simple yet delectable One Pot Creamy Tomato Basil Pasta that’s bound to become a regular on your dinner rotation. Give it a try and savor the creamy, flavorful goodness with every bite!
