Inexpensive and Delicious Homemade Pizza Sauce Recipes


Pizza is a beloved dish enjoyed by people all around the world. While ordering pizza from your favorite pizzeria is convenient, making your own homemade pizza can be a fun and rewarding experience. One of the key components of a delicious pizza is the sauce. Today, I’m going to share with you some inexpensive and mouthwatering homemade pizza sauce recipes that will take your homemade pizza to the next level.

Classic Tomato Pizza Sauce

Ingredients:

  • 1 can (14 ounces) crushed tomatoes
  • 2 tablespoons tomato paste
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1/2 teaspoon sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil

Instructions:

  1. In a medium saucepan, heat the olive oil over medium heat.
  2. Add the minced garlic and sauté for about a minute until fragrant.
  3. Stir in the crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, oregano, basil, sugar, salt, and black pepper.
  4. Reduce the heat to low and simmer the sauce for 20-30 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  5. Allow the sauce to cool before using it on your pizza.

Roasted Garlic Pizza Sauce

Ingredients:

  • 1 bulb of garlic
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 can (14 ounces) crushed tomatoes
  • 2 tablespoons tomato paste
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1/2 teaspoon sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 400°F (200°C).
  2. Cut the top off the garlic bulb to expose the cloves.
  3. Drizzle the garlic bulb with olive oil and wrap it in aluminum foil.
  4. Roast the garlic in the oven for 30-40 minutes until soft and golden.
  5. Squeeze the roasted garlic cloves out of the skin and mash them into a paste.
  6. In a saucepan, combine the crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, oregano, basil, sugar, salt, black pepper, and roasted garlic paste.
  7. Simmer the sauce over low heat for 20-30 minutes, stirring occasionally.

Spicy Arrabbiata Pizza Sauce

Ingredients:

  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can (14 ounces) crushed tomatoes
  • 2 tablespoons tomato paste
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1/2 teaspoon sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1/4 teaspoon red pepper flakes (adjust to taste)

Instructions:

  1. Heat the olive oil in a saucepan over medium heat.
  2. Add the minced garlic and red pepper flakes, and sauté for about a minute until fragrant.
  3. Stir in the crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, oregano, basil, sugar, salt, and black pepper.
  4. Reduce the heat to low and simmer the sauce for 20-30 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  5. Adjust the amount of red pepper flakes according to your desired level of spiciness.

White Pizza Sauce

Ingredients:

  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper

Instructions:

  1. In a saucepan, melt the butter over medium heat.
  2. Add the minced garlic and sauté for about a minute until fragrant.
  3. Stir in the flour and cook for another minute, stirring constantly.
  4. Gradually whisk in the milk, oregano, basil, salt, and black pepper.
  5. Continue to cook the sauce, whisking constantly, until it thickens.
  6. Remove the sauce from the heat and let it cool before using it on your pizza.

Now that you have these delicious homemade pizza sauce recipes, you can unleash your creativity and experiment with different toppings and cheeses to create your perfect homemade pizza. Enjoy the process and savor every bite of your homemade masterpiece!
