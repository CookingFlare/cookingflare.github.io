Delicious Picnic Sausage Recipes for Meat Lovers


If you’re a meat lover and enjoy the great outdoors, then a picnic is the perfect opportunity to combine your two passions. And what better way to elevate your picnic experience than with some delicious sausage recipes? Whether you prefer classic flavors or crave something a bit more adventurous, these picnic sausage recipes are sure to satisfy your taste buds. So grab your picnic blanket, pack your favorite sausages, and let’s get cooking!

1. Grilled Sausage Skewers

Ingredients:

  • 4 sausages of your choice
  • 1 red bell pepper, cut into chunks
  • 1 green bell pepper, cut into chunks
  • 1 red onion, cut into chunks
  • Wooden skewers
  • Olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your grill to medium-high heat.
  2. Soak the wooden skewers in water for about 15 minutes to prevent them from burning.
  3. Thread the sausages, bell peppers, and red onion alternately onto the skewers.
  4. Drizzle the skewers with olive oil and season with salt and pepper.
  5. Grill the skewers for about 10-12 minutes, turning occasionally, until the sausages are cooked through and the vegetables are charred.
  6. Remove from the grill and let them cool slightly before serving.

These grilled sausage skewers are not only easy to make but also incredibly flavorful. The smoky grilled sausages paired with the sweet bell peppers and onions create a mouthwatering combination that will impress everyone at your picnic.

2. Sausage and Cheese Stuffed Bread Rolls

Ingredients:

  • 4 sausages of your choice
  • 1 tube of refrigerated crescent roll dough
  • 1 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1 tablespoon chopped fresh parsley (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to the temperature indicated on the crescent roll dough package.
  2. Unroll the crescent roll dough and separate it into triangles.
  3. Place one sausage on the wider end of each triangle and sprinkle with shredded cheddar cheese.
  4. Roll up the dough, enclosing the sausage and cheese completely.
  5. Place the stuffed rolls on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper.
  6. Bake for the recommended time on the dough package, or until the rolls are golden brown and cooked through.
  7. Sprinkle with chopped fresh parsley, if desired, and let them cool slightly before serving.

These sausage and cheese stuffed bread rolls are a portable and delicious picnic treat. The flaky crescent roll dough beautifully encases the savory sausage and gooey cheese, resulting in a satisfying bite that will leave you craving more.

3. Sausage and Potato Foil Packets

Ingredients:

  • 4 sausages of your choice
  • 4 medium potatoes, thinly sliced
  • 1 small onion, thinly sliced
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your grill or oven to medium heat.
  2. In a large bowl, combine the sliced potatoes, onion, minced garlic, olive oil, dried thyme, salt, and pepper. Toss until well coated.
  3. Divide the potato mixture into four equal portions.
  4. On a large piece of aluminum foil, place one portion of the potato mixture and top it with a sausage.
  5. Fold the sides of the foil over the sausage and potato mixture to create a sealed packet.
  6. Repeat the process with the remaining sausages and potato mixture.
  7. Place the foil packets on the grill or in the oven and cook for about 20-25 minutes, or until the potatoes are tender and the sausages are cooked through.
  8. Carefully open the foil packets and let them cool slightly before serving.

These sausage and potato foil packets are not only a breeze to make but also incredibly flavorful. The combination of tender potatoes, caramelized onions, and juicy sausages creates a hearty and satisfying meal that is perfect for your picnic adventure.

Conclusion

These delicious picnic sausage recipes are guaranteed to impress any meat lover. From the smoky grilled sausage skewers to the savory sausage and cheese stuffed bread rolls, and the hearty sausage and potato foil packets, these recipes offer a variety of flavors and textures that will elevate your outdoor dining experience. So next time you plan a picnic, don’t forget to pack these mouthwatering sausage creations. Happy picnicking!
