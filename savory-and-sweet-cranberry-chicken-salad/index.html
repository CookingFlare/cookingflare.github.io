Savory and Sweet Cranberry Chicken Salad


Are you looking for a refreshing and satisfying dish that perfectly balances savory and sweet flavors? Look no further than this delightful Savory and Sweet Cranberry Chicken Salad recipe. Packed with protein, vitamins, and a burst of cranberry sweetness, this salad is a perfect choice for a light lunch, a picnic, or a potluck gathering. Let’s dive into the recipe and discover how to create this tantalizing dish.

Ingredients

For the Salad:

  • 2 cups cooked and shredded chicken breast
  • 1/2 cup dried cranberries
  • 1/2 cup chopped celery
  • 1/4 cup chopped red onion
  • 1/4 cup sliced almonds
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley

For the Dressing:

  • 1/2 cup mayonnaise
  • 1/4 cup Greek yogurt
  • 1 tablespoon Dijon mustard
  • 1 tablespoon honey
  • 1 tablespoon apple cider vinegar
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. In a large mixing bowl, combine the shredded chicken, dried cranberries, chopped celery, red onion, sliced almonds, and fresh parsley.

  2. In a separate bowl, whisk together the mayonnaise, Greek yogurt, Dijon mustard, honey, apple cider vinegar, salt, and pepper until the dressing is smooth and well combined.

  3. Pour the dressing over the chicken mixture and gently toss until all the ingredients are evenly coated.

  4. Cover the bowl with plastic wrap and refrigerate for at least 30 minutes to allow the flavors to meld together.

  5. Once chilled, give the salad a final toss and adjust the seasoning if necessary.

  6. Serve the Savory and Sweet Cranberry Chicken Salad on a bed of crisp lettuce, in a sandwich, or as a topping for whole grain crackers.

Tips and Variations

  • For a lighter version of this salad, you can use grilled or poached chicken instead of the shredded chicken.
  • Add a handful of halved grapes for an extra burst of sweetness and a pop of color.
  • Substitute the mayonnaise with a vegan alternative for a dairy-free version of the dressing.
  • Experiment with different nuts such as pecans or walnuts for added texture and flavor.

Health Benefits

This Savory and Sweet Cranberry Chicken Salad is not only a treat for your taste buds but also a nutritional powerhouse. Chicken is an excellent source of lean protein, while cranberries are packed with antioxidants and vitamins. The addition of celery and parsley provides a refreshing crunch and a dose of essential nutrients. The dressing, made with Greek yogurt and a touch of honey, offers a creamy texture with a hint of tanginess, without being overly heavy.

Conclusion

Whether you’re preparing a quick weekday meal or planning a special gathering, this Savory and Sweet Cranberry Chicken Salad is sure to impress. With its harmonious blend of flavors and textures, it’s a versatile dish that can be enjoyed in various ways. So, gather your ingredients and give this recipe a try – you won’t be disappointed!
