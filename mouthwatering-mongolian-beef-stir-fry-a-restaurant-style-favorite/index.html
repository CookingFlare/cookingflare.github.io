Mouthwatering Mongolian Beef Stir Fry: A Restaurant-Style Favorite


Are you craving the bold flavors and tender textures of Mongolian Beef Stir Fry? Look no further! In this recipe, we’ll show you how to recreate this restaurant-style favorite in the comfort of your own kitchen. Get ready to impress your taste buds and wow your family and friends with this mouthwatering dish!

Ingredients

To make this Mongolian Beef Stir Fry, you’ll need the following ingredients:

  • 1 pound of flank steak, thinly sliced
  • 1/4 cup of cornstarch
  • 1/4 cup of low-sodium soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons of hoisin sauce
  • 2 tablespoons of brown sugar
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon of ginger, grated
  • 1/4 cup of water
  • 2 tablespoons of vegetable oil
  • 1/2 cup of green onions, chopped
  • Sesame seeds, for garnish (optional)

Instructions

  1. In a large bowl, toss the sliced flank steak with cornstarch until evenly coated. Set aside for 10 minutes to allow the cornstarch to adhere to the beef.

  2. In a separate bowl, whisk together the soy sauce, hoisin sauce, brown sugar, minced garlic, grated ginger, and water. This flavorful marinade will be the key to achieving that authentic Mongolian Beef taste.

  3. Heat the vegetable oil in a large skillet or wok over medium-high heat. Once the oil is hot, add the coated flank steak in a single layer and cook for 2-3 minutes on each side until browned and crispy. Be careful not to overcrowd the pan; you may need to cook the beef in batches.

  4. Pour the marinade over the cooked beef in the skillet. Stir well to coat the beef evenly. Reduce the heat to medium and let the sauce simmer for 5-7 minutes, or until it thickens and becomes glossy.

  5. Sprinkle the chopped green onions over the stir fry and give it a final toss. The vibrant green color and fresh flavor of the onions will complement the rich and savory beef.

  6. Remove the skillet from the heat and let the Mongolian Beef Stir Fry rest for a few minutes. This will allow the flavors to meld together and the beef to become even more tender.

  7. Serve the stir fry over a bed of fluffy white rice or noodles, and garnish with a sprinkle of sesame seeds for an added crunch and visual appeal.

Tips and Variations

  • For an extra kick of heat, add a pinch of red pepper flakes or a drizzle of Sriracha sauce to the marinade.
  • Experiment with different vegetables to customize your stir fry. Bell peppers, broccoli, and snap peas all work well in this dish.
  • If you prefer a gluten-free version, substitute the soy sauce with tamari or coconut aminos.
  • For a healthier twist, use leaner cuts of beef such as sirloin or tenderloin, and sauté the meat in olive oil instead of vegetable oil.

Conclusion

There you have it – a foolproof recipe for Mouthwatering Mongolian Beef Stir Fry that will transport your taste buds straight to a restaurant in Mongolia. With its tender beef, rich sauce, and vibrant green onions, this dish is sure to become a family favorite. So why wait? Gather your ingredients, fire up your skillet, and get ready to indulge in this irresistible stir fry. Enjoy!

Note: This recipe serves approximately 4 people.
