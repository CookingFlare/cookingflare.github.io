Lemon Blueberry Overnight French Toast: A Citrusy and Indulgent Dessert


Are you looking for a delightful and indulgent breakfast or brunch dish that will impress your family and friends? Look no further than this Lemon Blueberry Overnight French Toast recipe! This dish is bursting with citrusy and sweet flavors, making it the perfect treat for any occasion. The best part? You can prepare it the night before, allowing you to spend more time enjoying the company of your loved ones in the morning. Let’s dive into the details of this delectable recipe!

Ingredients

  • 1 loaf of French bread, cut into 1-inch slices
  • 1 cup fresh blueberries
  • 6 large eggs
  • 1 1/2 cups whole milk
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • Zest of 2 lemons
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • Pinch of salt
  • 1/2 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup packed brown sugar
  • 1/2 cup cold unsalted butter, cut into small pieces
  • Maple syrup, for serving

Instructions

  1. Grease a 9x13 inch baking dish and arrange the bread slices in two rows, slightly overlapping each other. Sprinkle the fresh blueberries over the bread.

  2. In a large bowl, whisk together the eggs, whole milk, heavy cream, granulated sugar, lemon zest, vanilla extract, cinnamon, and a pinch of salt until well combined. Pour this mixture evenly over the bread and blueberries. Cover the baking dish with plastic wrap and refrigerate overnight.

  3. The next morning, preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C). In a separate bowl, combine the flour, brown sugar, and cold butter pieces. Use a fork or pastry cutter to mix the ingredients until crumbly. Sprinkle this streusel mixture over the soaked bread.

  4. Bake the French toast uncovered for 45-55 minutes, or until the top is golden and the bread is set.

  5. Allow the French toast to cool for a few minutes before slicing it into generous portions. Serve with a drizzle of maple syrup and extra fresh blueberries on top.
