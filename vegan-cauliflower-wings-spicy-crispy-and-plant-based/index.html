Vegan Cauliflower Wings: Spicy, Crispy, and Plant-Based


Are you craving a flavorful and satisfying snack? Look no further! These vegan cauliflower wings are the perfect combination of spicy, crispy, and plant-based goodness. With just a few simple ingredients and a little bit of creativity, you can whip up a batch of these delectable wings that will surely impress your friends and family. So, let’s dive right into the recipe!

Ingredients

  • 1 head of cauliflower
  • 1 cup of all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup of non-dairy milk (such as almond or soy milk)
  • 1 teaspoon of garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon of paprika
  • 1 teaspoon of cayenne pepper (adjust according to your spice preference)
  • 1 teaspoon of salt
  • 1 cup of your favorite hot sauce (make sure it’s vegan)

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 450°F (230°C) and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.

  2. Wash the cauliflower and cut it into bite-sized florets. Make sure to remove any tough stems.

  3. In a medium-sized bowl, whisk together the flour, non-dairy milk, garlic powder, paprika, cayenne pepper, and salt. The consistency should be thick, but still pourable.

  4. Dip each cauliflower floret into the batter, making sure to coat it evenly. Shake off any excess batter and place the coated floret onto the prepared baking sheet. Repeat this process until all the florets are coated.

  5. Bake the cauliflower wings for 20-25 minutes, or until they are golden brown and crispy.

  6. While the wings are baking, heat the hot sauce in a small saucepan over medium heat. Bring it to a simmer and let it cook for a few minutes to allow the flavors to meld together.

  7. Once the wings are done baking, remove them from the oven and transfer them to a large bowl. Pour the hot sauce over the wings and toss them gently to coat them evenly.

  8. Serve the cauliflower wings immediately with your favorite vegan dipping sauce, such as ranch or vegan blue cheese. Enjoy!

Tips and Variations

  • For an extra crispy texture, you can use panko breadcrumbs instead of flour in the batter. Simply substitute the flour with panko breadcrumbs and follow the same instructions.

  • If you prefer a milder spice level, you can reduce the amount of cayenne pepper in the batter or choose a mild hot sauce.

  • Feel free to experiment with different spices and seasonings to customize the flavor of your cauliflower wings. Add a pinch of smoked paprika for a smoky twist or sprinkle some nutritional yeast for a cheesy flavor.

  • These cauliflower wings are best enjoyed fresh out of the oven. However, if you have leftovers, you can store them in an airtight container in the refrigerator for up to 2 days. To reheat, simply place them in the oven at 350°F (180°C) for about 10 minutes, or until they are heated through.

So, there you have it! A simple yet mouthwatering recipe for vegan cauliflower wings that will surely satisfy your cravings. Whether you’re a vegan or just looking to incorporate more plant-based options into your diet, these wings are a must-try. So gather your ingredients, preheat your oven, and get ready to indulge in a plate full of spicy, crispy, and plant-based goodness. Enjoy!
