Stir-Fry Sensation: Easy Teriyaki Chicken for Beginners


Are you a beginner in the kitchen looking to impress your family and friends with a delicious and flavorful dish? Look no further! In this recipe, we will guide you through the steps of creating a mouthwatering Stir-Fry Sensation: Easy Teriyaki Chicken. With simple ingredients and easy-to-follow instructions, this recipe is perfect for beginners who want to explore the world of stir-fry cooking.

Ingredients

To create this delectable dish, you will need the following ingredients:

  • 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons honey
  • 2 tablespoons rice vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon sesame oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon grated ginger
  • 1 tablespoon cornstarch
  • 1/4 cup water
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1 red bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 green bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 medium onion, sliced
  • Steamed rice, for serving

Instructions

Now, let’s dive into the step-by-step process of creating this Stir-Fry Sensation:

  1. Begin by preparing the chicken. Cut the chicken breasts into thin, bite-sized pieces. This will ensure quick and even cooking.

  2. In a small bowl, whisk together the soy sauce, honey, rice vinegar, sesame oil, minced garlic, and grated ginger. This flavorful marinade will infuse the chicken with a burst of deliciousness.

  3. Place the chicken pieces in a shallow dish or a resealable plastic bag. Pour the marinade over the chicken, making sure each piece is coated evenly. Allow the chicken to marinate for at least 15 minutes, or for even more flavor, refrigerate it for up to an hour.

  4. In a separate bowl, mix the cornstarch with water until it forms a smooth slurry. This will act as a thickening agent for the teriyaki sauce.

  5. Heat a large skillet or wok over medium-high heat. Add the vegetable oil and swirl it around to coat the pan evenly.

  6. Once the oil is hot, carefully add the marinated chicken to the skillet. Stir-fry the chicken for about 5-7 minutes, or until it is cooked through and slightly browned.

  7. Remove the chicken from the skillet and set it aside on a plate.

  8. In the same skillet, add the sliced bell peppers and onion. Stir-fry the vegetables for 3-4 minutes, or until they are tender-crisp.

  9. Return the cooked chicken to the skillet and pour the cornstarch slurry over the chicken and vegetables. Stir everything together until the sauce has thickened and coated the ingredients evenly.

  10. Remove the skillet from the heat and let the flavors meld for a minute or two.

  11. Serve the Stir-Fry Sensation: Easy Teriyaki Chicken over a bed of steamed rice, and garnish with a sprinkle of sesame seeds or chopped green onions for an added touch of freshness and flavor.

Enjoy the Fusion of Flavors!

There you have it - a simple yet sensational dish that is sure to impress your taste buds and those around you. This Stir-Fry Sensation: Easy Teriyaki Chicken is the perfect recipe for beginners who want to delve into the world of stir-fry cooking. With its sweet and savory teriyaki sauce and tender chicken, this dish is bound to become a favorite in your kitchen.

So, what are you waiting for? Gather your ingredients, put on your apron, and embark on a culinary adventure with this Easy Teriyaki Chicken recipe. Happy cooking and bon appétit!
