Irresistible Thanksgiving Cranberry Desserts: Sweet and Tart Treats


Thanksgiving is a time for indulgence, and what better way to satisfy your sweet tooth than with irresistible cranberry desserts? Cranberries, with their vibrant color and tangy flavor, add a delightful twist to traditional Thanksgiving desserts. From pies to bars, these sweet and tart treats are sure to impress your guests. So, let’s dive into some mouthwatering recipes that will make your Thanksgiving feast even more memorable!

1. Cranberry Apple Pie

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups fresh cranberries
  • 2 cups peeled and sliced apples
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon cinnamon
  • 1/4 teaspoon nutmeg
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 1 double-crust pie pastry

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. In a large bowl, combine the cranberries, apples, sugar, flour, cinnamon, nutmeg, and lemon juice. Mix well.
  3. Roll out half of the pie pastry and line a 9-inch pie dish with it.
  4. Pour the cranberry and apple mixture into the pie dish.
  5. Roll out the remaining pastry and place it over the filling. Trim and crimp the edges.
  6. Cut a few slits on the top crust to allow steam to escape.
  7. Bake for 45-50 minutes, or until the crust is golden brown and the filling is bubbly.
  8. Allow the pie to cool before serving. Enjoy!

2. Cranberry Pecan Bars

Ingredients:

  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 3/4 cup unsalted butter, melted
  • 1/2 cup packed brown sugar
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1 1/2 cups fresh cranberries
  • 1/2 cup chopped pecans
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C) and line a 9x9-inch baking dish with parchment paper.
  2. In a medium bowl, combine the flour, melted butter, brown sugar, and salt. Mix until crumbly.
  3. Press the mixture into the bottom of the prepared baking dish to form the crust.
  4. In a separate bowl, combine the cranberries, pecans, granulated sugar, eggs, and vanilla extract. Mix well.
  5. Pour the cranberry mixture over the crust and spread it evenly.
  6. Bake for 30-35 minutes, or until the edges are golden brown and the center is set.
  7. Allow the bars to cool completely before cutting into squares. Enjoy!

3. Cranberry Cheesecake

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups graham cracker crumbs
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, melted
  • 3 (8-ounce) packages cream cheese, softened
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 3 large eggs
  • 1 cup fresh cranberries
  • 1/4 cup water
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 325°F (165°C) and grease a 9-inch springform pan.
  2. In a bowl, combine the graham cracker crumbs and melted butter. Press the mixture into the bottom of the prepared pan to form the crust.
  3. In a large mixing bowl, beat the cream cheese, sugar, and vanilla extract until smooth.
  4. Add the eggs, one at a time, beating well after each addition.
  5. Pour the cream cheese mixture over the crust in the pan.
  6. In a small saucepan, combine the cranberries, water, and sugar. Cook over medium heat until the cranberries burst and the mixture thickens slightly.
  7. Spoon the cranberry mixture over the cream cheese layer, swirling it gently with a knife.
  8. Bake for 50-60 minutes, or until the cheesecake is set and slightly golden on top.
  9. Allow the cheesecake to cool completely before refrigerating for at least 4 hours or overnight.
  10. Serve chilled and enjoy every creamy and tangy bite!

These irresistible cranberry desserts will add a burst of flavor and festive flair to your Thanksgiving table. Whether you choose the classic Cranberry Apple Pie, the nutty Cranberry Pecan Bars, or the creamy Cranberry Cheesecake, these sweet and tart treats are guaranteed to be a hit with your family and friends. So, get ready to satisfy your dessert cravings and create lasting memories with these delightful Thanksgiving creations. Happy baking and happy Thanksgiving!
