Instant Pot Beef and Mushroom Stew: Rich and Meaty


There’s something incredibly comforting about a rich and meaty stew, especially when it’s made in an Instant Pot. The pressure cooker locks in all the flavors, creating a tender and flavorful dish that’s perfect for a cozy night in or a family gathering. This Instant Pot beef and mushroom stew is a classic recipe with a twist - the addition of earthy mushrooms takes it to a whole new level of deliciousness. Let’s dive into the details of this mouthwatering dish and learn how to create it in your own kitchen.

Ingredients

  • 2 pounds of beef chuck, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 2 tablespoons of olive oil
  • 1 large onion, diced
  • 3 garlic cloves, minced
  • 2 cups of beef broth
  • 1 cup of red wine
  • 1 pound of mushrooms, sliced
  • 3 carrots, peeled and chopped
  • 2 stalks of celery, chopped
  • 2 bay leaves
  • 1 teaspoon of dried thyme
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 tablespoons of tomato paste
  • 2 tablespoons of all-purpose flour
  • Fresh parsley for garnish

Instructions

  1. Sear the Beef: Start by seasoning the beef with salt and pepper. Select the “Saute” function on the Instant Pot and heat the olive oil. Sear the beef in batches until browned on all sides. Set the beef aside.

  2. Saute the Aromatics: Add the diced onion and minced garlic to the Instant Pot and saute until the onion is soft and translucent.

  3. Deglaze the Pot: Pour in the red wine and scrape the bottom of the pot to release any browned bits. Let the wine simmer for a few minutes to reduce slightly.

  4. Add the Remaining Ingredients: Return the beef to the Instant Pot and add the beef broth, mushrooms, carrots, celery, bay leaves, and thyme. Stir in the tomato paste and sprinkle the flour over the mixture. Give everything a good stir to combine.

  5. Pressure Cook: Secure the lid on the Instant Pot and set the valve to the sealing position. Cook on high pressure for 35 minutes, then allow for a natural pressure release for 10 minutes before carefully switching the valve to the venting position.

  6. Thicken the Stew: If the stew needs further thickening, select the “Saute” function and let it simmer until it reaches your desired consistency.

  7. Serve and Enjoy: Discard the bay leaves and garnish the stew with fresh parsley before serving. This rich and meaty Instant Pot beef and mushroom stew pairs beautifully with crusty bread or creamy mashed potatoes.

Why This Recipe Works

The Instant Pot’s pressure cooking function tenderizes the beef, turning it into melt-in-your-mouth goodness. The combination of beef broth and red wine creates a robust and flavorful base for the stew, while the addition of mushrooms adds an earthy depth to the dish. The carrots and celery provide a subtle sweetness and crunch, balancing out the richness of the beef and mushrooms. The tomato paste and flour help thicken the stew, creating a luscious and velvety texture that coats every forkful.

Final Thoughts

This Instant Pot beef and mushroom stew is a soul-warming dish that’s perfect for any occasion. Whether you’re hosting a dinner party or simply craving a hearty meal on a chilly evening, this recipe is sure to impress. The convenience of the Instant Pot makes it an ideal choice for busy weeknights, allowing you to enjoy a homemade stew without spending hours in the kitchen. So, gather your ingredients, fire up your Instant Pot, and get ready to savor the rich and meaty flavors of this comforting beef and mushroom stew.
