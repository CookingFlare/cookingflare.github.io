Sinfully Rich Chocolate Chip Cookie Dough Brownies


Indulge in the ultimate treat with these Sinfully Rich Chocolate Chip Cookie Dough Brownies. This decadent dessert combines the best of both worlds – a fudgy brownie base topped with a layer of creamy cookie dough. Each bite is a heavenly combination of rich chocolate and irresistible cookie dough. Get ready to satisfy your sweet tooth with this sinfully delicious recipe!

Ingredients

For the brownie base:

  • 1 cup unsalted butter, melted
  • 2 cups granulated sugar
  • 4 large eggs
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup cocoa powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt

For the cookie dough:

  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1/2 cup brown sugar
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 2 tablespoons milk
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 1/4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup chocolate chips

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease a 9x13-inch baking dish and set aside.

  2. In a large mixing bowl, combine the melted butter and granulated sugar. Mix until well combined.

  3. Add the eggs, one at a time, mixing well after each addition. Stir in the vanilla extract.

  4. In a separate bowl, whisk together the flour, cocoa powder, and salt. Gradually add the dry ingredients to the wet ingredients, mixing until just combined. Be careful not to overmix.

  5. Pour the brownie batter into the prepared baking dish and spread it evenly.

  6. In another mixing bowl, cream together the softened butter, brown sugar, and granulated sugar until light and fluffy.

  7. Add the milk and vanilla extract, mixing until well incorporated.

  8. Gradually add the flour and salt to the butter mixture, mixing until just combined. Fold in the chocolate chips.

  9. Drop spoonfuls of the cookie dough mixture onto the brownie batter in the baking dish. Use a spatula or the back of a spoon to spread the cookie dough evenly over the brownie batter.

  10. Bake in the preheated oven for 35-40 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out with a few moist crumbs. Be careful not to overbake.

  11. Remove from the oven and allow to cool completely in the baking dish before cutting into squares.

  12. Serve and enjoy these sinfully rich Chocolate Chip Cookie Dough Brownies with a glass of cold milk or a scoop of vanilla ice cream.

Tips and Variations

  • For an extra decadent twist, drizzle melted chocolate over the cooled brownies before serving.
  • Feel free to add chopped nuts, such as walnuts or pecans, to the cookie dough for added crunch and flavor.
  • If you prefer a chewier texture, reduce the baking time by a few minutes.
  • These brownies can be stored in an airtight container at room temperature for up to 3 days. They also freeze well for longer storage.

Conclusion

These Sinfully Rich Chocolate Chip Cookie Dough Brownies are the perfect indulgence for any chocolate lover. With their fudgy brownie base and creamy cookie dough topping, they are a truly irresistible treat. Whether you’re baking for a special occasion or simply satisfying a sweet craving, these brownies are sure to impress. So go ahead, grab your apron, and get ready to dive into a sinful delight!
