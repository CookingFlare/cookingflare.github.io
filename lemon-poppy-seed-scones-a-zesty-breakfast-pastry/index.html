Lemon Poppy Seed Scones: A Zesty Breakfast Pastry


Are you looking for a delightful and refreshing breakfast treat? Look no further than these Lemon Poppy Seed Scones! Bursting with tangy lemon flavor and speckled with tiny poppy seeds, these scones are the perfect way to start your day on a zesty note. Whether you enjoy them with a cup of hot tea or a dollop of clotted cream, these scones are sure to please your taste buds.

Ingredients

To make these Lemon Poppy Seed Scones, you will need the following ingredients:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 tablespoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • Zest of 2 lemons
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, cold and cubed
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1/4 cup freshly squeezed lemon juice
  • 2 tablespoons poppy seeds

Instructions

Follow these simple steps to create your own batch of Lemon Poppy Seed Scones:

  1. Preheat your oven to 425°F (220°C) and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.

  2. In a large mixing bowl, whisk together the flour, sugar, baking powder, salt, and lemon zest. Make sure the ingredients are well combined.

  3. Add the cold, cubed butter to the dry ingredients. Using a pastry cutter or your fingertips, cut the butter into the flour mixture until it resembles coarse crumbs.

  4. In a separate bowl, combine the milk, lemon juice, and poppy seeds. Pour the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients and gently mix until just combined. Be careful not to overmix, as this can result in tough scones.

  5. Turn the dough out onto a lightly floured surface and knead it a few times to bring it together. Pat the dough into a circle about 1 inch thick.

  6. Using a sharp knife or a round biscuit cutter, cut the dough into scones. Place the scones onto the prepared baking sheet, leaving a little space between each one.

  7. Bake the scones in the preheated oven for 12-15 minutes, or until they are golden brown and cooked through. Remove from the oven and let them cool slightly before serving.

  8. Once cooled, you can enjoy these Lemon Poppy Seed Scones as is or with your favorite accompaniments. They pair wonderfully with a cup of hot tea, a drizzle of honey, or even a dollop of clotted cream.

Tips and Variations

  • For an extra burst of lemon flavor, you can add a lemon glaze to the scones. Simply mix together powdered sugar and lemon juice until you reach your desired consistency, then drizzle it over the cooled scones.

  • If you prefer a sweeter scone, you can increase the amount of sugar in the recipe. Just keep in mind that the scones will already have a lovely tang from the lemon zest and juice.

  • Feel free to experiment with different citrus flavors. You can substitute the lemon zest and juice with orange or lime for a unique twist on these scones.

  • These scones are best enjoyed fresh on the day they are baked. However, you can store any leftovers in an airtight container at room temperature for up to 2 days. To refresh them, simply warm them in the oven for a few minutes before serving.

So there you have it - a delicious recipe for Lemon Poppy Seed Scones that will brighten up your mornings. With their zesty lemon flavor and delicate poppy seed crunch, these scones are a delightful treat to share with family and friends. Give them a try and let your taste buds dance with joy!
