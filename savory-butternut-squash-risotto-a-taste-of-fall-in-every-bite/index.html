Savory Butternut Squash Risotto: A Taste of Fall in Every Bite


If there’s one dish that captures the essence of fall, it’s the rich and comforting savory butternut squash risotto. This creamy, flavorful dish is a celebration of the season’s harvest, combining the earthy sweetness of butternut squash with the creamy texture of Arborio rice. Whether you’re looking for a hearty weeknight meal or an impressive dish to serve at a dinner party, this butternut squash risotto is sure to delight your taste buds and warm your soul.

The Magic of Butternut Squash

Butternut squash is the star of this dish, bringing a delightful sweetness and velvety texture to the risotto. Packed with vitamins A and C, as well as fiber and antioxidants, butternut squash not only adds a delicious flavor but also offers a nutritional boost to this comforting dish. Its natural sweetness pairs beautifully with the creamy rice, creating a symphony of flavors that are perfect for the autumn season.

Ingredients

To create this delectable butternut squash risotto, you’ll need the following ingredients:

  • 1 small butternut squash, peeled, seeded, and diced
  • 5 cups of chicken or vegetable broth
  • 1 ½ cups Arborio rice
  • 1 cup dry white wine
  • 1 medium onion, finely chopped
  • 3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon fresh thyme leaves
  • ½ cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 4 tablespoons unsalted butter
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Olive oil for cooking

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Butternut Squash: Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C). Toss the diced butternut squash with olive oil, salt, and pepper, then spread it out on a baking sheet. Roast in the oven for 25-30 minutes, or until the squash is tender and caramelized around the edges.

  2. Simmer the Broth: In a saucepan, bring the chicken or vegetable broth to a gentle simmer. Keeping the broth warm ensures that it will be readily absorbed by the rice as it cooks.

  3. Sauté the Aromatics: In a large, heavy-bottomed pot, heat a drizzle of olive oil over medium heat. Add the chopped onion and garlic, cooking until they become translucent and fragrant. This forms the flavorful base of the risotto.

  4. Toast the Rice: Add the Arborio rice to the pot with the onions and garlic, stirring to coat the rice with the oil. Toast the rice for a few minutes until it becomes translucent around the edges.

  5. Deglaze with Wine: Pour in the white wine, stirring constantly until it has been absorbed by the rice. The wine adds a bright acidity to the dish and enhances the flavor of the squash.

  6. Add the Butternut Squash: Stir the roasted butternut squash into the pot, mashing it slightly to release its creamy texture and sweet flavor into the risotto.

  7. Start Adding Broth: Begin adding the warm broth to the pot, one ladleful at a time, stirring constantly and allowing each addition to be absorbed before adding the next. This slow cooking process allows the starch in the rice to create the creamy consistency that is characteristic of risotto.

  8. Finish and Serve: Once the rice is creamy and tender, stir in the fresh thyme, grated Parmesan cheese, and butter. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Serve the butternut squash risotto in warm bowls, garnishing with an extra sprinkle of Parmesan and a few thyme leaves.

Enjoy the Flavors of Fall

With its velvety texture, rich flavors, and comforting warmth, savory butternut squash risotto is the perfect dish to savor on a crisp autumn evening. Whether enjoyed as a main course or served alongside a roast chicken or grilled vegetables, this dish brings the taste of fall to your table in every creamy, delicious bite. So, embrace the season and indulge in the comforting flavors of butternut squash risotto – a true taste of fall.
