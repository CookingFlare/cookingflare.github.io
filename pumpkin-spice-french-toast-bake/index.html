Pumpkin Spice French Toast Bake


Fall is the perfect season for indulging in cozy and comforting dishes that warm both the body and the soul. If you’re looking for a delightful breakfast or brunch option that embodies the flavors of autumn, look no further than this Pumpkin Spice French Toast Bake recipe. This dish combines the classic appeal of French toast with the seasonal charm of pumpkin spice, creating a mouthwatering treat that will delight your taste buds.

Ingredients:

  • 1 loaf of thickly sliced bread (such as brioche or challah)
  • 4 eggs
  • 1 cup of milk
  • 1/2 cup of pumpkin puree
  • 1/4 cup of brown sugar
  • 1 teaspoon of vanilla extract
  • 1 teaspoon of pumpkin pie spice
  • 1/2 teaspoon of cinnamon
  • A pinch of salt
  • Maple syrup and powdered sugar for serving

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the Bread:

    • Start by cutting the bread into cubes and arranging them in a greased baking dish. Ensure the bread cubes are tightly packed to create a solid base for the French toast bake.

  2. Whisk the Wet Ingredients:

    • In a mixing bowl, whisk together the eggs, milk, pumpkin puree, brown sugar, vanilla extract, pumpkin pie spice, cinnamon, and salt until well combined. The pumpkin puree will add a rich and velvety texture to the custard mixture.

  3. Pour Over the Bread:

    • Carefully pour the pumpkin spice egg mixture over the bread cubes, making sure to coat each piece evenly. Press down lightly on the bread to help it absorb the liquid.

  4. Chill Overnight:

    • Cover the baking dish with plastic wrap and refrigerate it overnight. Allowing the bread to soak in the pumpkin-spiced custard overnight ensures a flavorful and decadent French toast bake.

  5. Bake to Perfection:

    • The next morning, preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C). Uncover the baking dish and bake the French toast for about 45-50 minutes, or until the top is golden brown and the custard is set.

  6. Serve and Enjoy:

    • Once the Pumpkin Spice French Toast Bake is out of the oven, let it cool slightly before slicing it into generous portions. Drizzle with maple syrup, dust with powdered sugar, and serve warm to your eager guests.

Tips and Variations:

  • Add Nuts: For added crunch and texture, sprinkle chopped pecans or walnuts over the top of the French toast bake before baking.
  • Cream Cheese Swirl: For a decadent twist, dot the top of the bake with small pieces of cream cheese before baking for a creamy surprise in every bite.
  • Whipped Cream: Serve the Pumpkin Spice French Toast Bake with a dollop of whipped cream and a sprinkle of extra pumpkin pie spice for an extra festive touch.

Indulge in the warm and comforting flavors of fall with this Pumpkin Spice French Toast Bake. Whether you’re hosting a brunch gathering or simply treating yourself to a special breakfast, this recipe is sure to impress with its delicious blend of pumpkin, spices, and sweet custardy bread. Get ready to savor the essence of autumn in every bite!

Enjoy making this delightful dish and savor the flavors of the season!
