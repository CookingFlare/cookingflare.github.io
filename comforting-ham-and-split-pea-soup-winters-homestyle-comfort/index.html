Comforting Ham and Split Pea Soup: Winter's Homestyle Comfort


Winter is a time for cozy blankets, crackling fires, and delicious comfort food that warms you from the inside out. And what better way to embrace the chilly weather than with a steaming bowl of comforting ham and split pea soup? This hearty and nutritious soup is a winter classic that will satisfy your cravings and leave you feeling warm and satisfied.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound of dried split peas
  • 1 ham hock or leftover ham bone
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 carrots, diced
  • 2 celery stalks, diced
  • 4 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 4 cups of chicken or vegetable broth
  • 4 cups of water
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 1 teaspoon of dried thyme
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Rinse the split peas under cold water and remove any debris. Set them aside.
  2. In a large pot or Dutch oven, heat some olive oil over medium heat. Add the chopped onion, carrots, celery, and minced garlic. Sauté until the vegetables are tender and fragrant.
  3. Add the ham hock or leftover ham bone to the pot. This will infuse the soup with a rich and smoky flavor. If using a ham hock, you may need to remove any excess fat.
  4. Pour in the chicken or vegetable broth, followed by the water. Add the rinsed split peas, bay leaf, dried thyme, and season with salt and pepper to taste. Stir everything together.
  5. Bring the soup to a boil, then reduce the heat to low and cover. Let it simmer for about 1 to 2 hours, or until the split peas are soft and the flavors have melded together. Stir occasionally to prevent sticking.
  6. Once the soup has finished simmering, remove the ham hock or leftover ham bone from the pot. Discard any excess fat and bones. Shred the meat and return it to the pot. Stir well to incorporate the ham into the soup.
  7. Taste the soup and adjust the seasoning if needed. You can add more salt, pepper, or herbs to suit your taste.
  8. Serve the comforting ham and split pea soup hot in bowls, garnished with a sprinkle of fresh parsley if desired. Pair it with crusty bread or a side salad for a complete and satisfying meal.

The Perfect Winter Comfort

This comforting ham and split pea soup is the epitome of winter comfort. The creamy texture of the split peas, combined with the smoky and savory flavors of the ham, creates a soup that is both hearty and satisfying. It’s a bowl of warmth that will transport you back to cozy evenings by the fireplace.

Not only is this soup delicious, but it’s also packed with nutritional benefits. Split peas are a great source of protein and fiber, making this soup a filling and nourishing option. Additionally, the vegetables add a boost of vitamins and minerals, making it a well-rounded meal.

Make a big batch of this comforting ham and split pea soup and freeze the leftovers for those days when you need a quick and easy meal. It reheats beautifully and is just as delicious the second time around.

So, when winter arrives and you’re in need of some homestyle comfort, look no further than this delightful ham and split pea soup. It’s a classic recipe that never fails to warm the soul and bring a smile to your face.

Stay warm, stay cozy, and enjoy the comforting flavors of winter!
