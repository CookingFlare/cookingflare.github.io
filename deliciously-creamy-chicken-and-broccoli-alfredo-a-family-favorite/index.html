Deliciously Creamy Chicken and Broccoli Alfredo: A Family Favorite


If there’s one dish that never fails to bring smiles to the dinner table, it’s a steaming plate of creamy chicken and broccoli alfredo. This classic Italian-American dish has been a beloved comfort food for generations, and for good reason. The rich, velvety Alfredo sauce paired with tender chicken and vibrant broccoli creates a symphony of flavors that is simply irresistible. Whether you’re cooking for a special occasion or just craving a satisfying meal, this recipe is guaranteed to be a hit with the whole family.

Ingredients

For the Chicken and Broccoli Alfredo:

  • 1 pound of boneless, skinless chicken breast, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 1 head of fresh broccoli, cut into florets
  • 1 pound of fettuccine pasta
  • 4 tablespoons of unsalted butter
  • 4 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 cup of heavy cream
  • 1 cup of whole milk
  • 1 and 1/2 cups of freshly grated Parmesan cheese
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Freshly chopped parsley for garnish

Instructions

Step 1: Prepare the Ingredients

Start by preparing the ingredients. Cut the chicken into bite-sized pieces and season with salt and pepper. Cut the broccoli into florets and set aside. Mince the garlic and grate the Parmesan cheese.

Step 2: Cook the Chicken and Broccoli

In a large skillet, melt 2 tablespoons of butter over medium-high heat. Add the seasoned chicken pieces and cook until golden brown and cooked through. Remove the chicken from the skillet and set aside. In the same skillet, add the remaining 2 tablespoons of butter and sauté the minced garlic until fragrant. Add the broccoli florets and cook until they are bright green and slightly tender.

Step 3: Prepare the Alfredo Sauce

In a separate pot, cook the fettuccine pasta according to the package instructions. While the pasta is cooking, prepare the Alfredo sauce. In the same skillet used for the chicken and broccoli, pour in the heavy cream and whole milk. Bring the mixture to a gentle simmer, then gradually whisk in the grated Parmesan cheese until the sauce is smooth and creamy. Season with salt and pepper to taste.

Step 4: Combine and Serve

Once the pasta is al dente, drain it and add it to the skillet with the Alfredo sauce. Toss the pasta in the creamy sauce until it is well coated. Add the cooked chicken and broccoli to the skillet and gently toss to combine. Allow the flavors to meld together for a few minutes over low heat.

Step 5: Garnish and Enjoy

Transfer the creamy chicken and broccoli Alfredo to a serving dish and garnish with freshly chopped parsley. Serve hot and enjoy this delightful dish with your loved ones.

The Perfect Family Meal

Creamy chicken and broccoli Alfredo is a versatile dish that can be enjoyed on its own or paired with a side of garlic bread and a crisp, green salad. The combination of tender chicken, vibrant broccoli, and luscious Alfredo sauce makes this a satisfying and comforting meal that is perfect for family gatherings or cozy weeknight dinners.

Conclusion

The next time you’re looking for a wholesome and comforting meal to share with your family, consider whipping up a batch of deliciously creamy chicken and broccoli Alfredo. Its timeless appeal and rich, satisfying flavors are sure to make it a new family favorite. So, roll up your sleeves, gather your ingredients, and get ready to create a memorable culinary experience for your loved ones. Happy cooking!
