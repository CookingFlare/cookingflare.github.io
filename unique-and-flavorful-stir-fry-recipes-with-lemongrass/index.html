Unique and Flavorful Stir Fry Recipes with Lemongrass


If you’re a fan of Asian cuisine, you probably appreciate the aromatic and citrusy flavor of lemongrass. This versatile herb adds a unique twist to stir fry dishes, elevating them to a whole new level of deliciousness. In this blog post, we’ll explore some unique and flavorful stir fry recipes that incorporate lemongrass, offering a fresh take on this classic cooking technique.

Lemongrass Chicken Stir Fry

Ingredients:

  • 2 chicken breasts, thinly sliced
  • 2 stalks of lemongrass, finely chopped
  • 1 red bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 yellow bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 onion, sliced
  • 3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon fish sauce
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • Cooking oil

Instructions:

  1. In a wok or large skillet, heat some cooking oil over high heat.

  2. Add the minced garlic and chopped lemongrass, and stir-fry for a minute until fragrant.

  3. Add the sliced chicken breasts and cook until they are no longer pink.

  4. Toss in the sliced bell peppers and onion, and continue stir-frying until the vegetables are slightly tender but still crisp.

  5. In a small bowl, mix together the soy sauce, fish sauce, sugar, and black pepper. Pour this sauce over the stir fry and toss to coat the ingredients evenly.

  6. Add the fresh basil leaves and stir-fry for another minute.

  7. Remove from heat and serve the lemongrass chicken stir fry over steamed jasmine rice.

Lemongrass Tofu and Vegetable Stir Fry

Ingredients:

  • 1 block of firm tofu, cubed
  • 2 stalks of lemongrass, finely chopped
  • 1 cup broccoli florets
  • 1 cup snow peas
  • 1 carrot, julienned
  • 3 tablespoons oyster sauce
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon honey
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • Cooking oil

Instructions:

  1. Heat some cooking oil in a wok or large skillet over medium-high heat.

  2. Add the cubed tofu and stir-fry until golden brown and crispy. Remove from the wok and set aside.

  3. In the same wok, add the minced garlic and chopped lemongrass. Stir-fry for a minute until fragrant.

  4. Toss in the broccoli, snow peas, and julienned carrot. Continue stir-frying until the vegetables are tender-crisp.

  5. In a small bowl, mix together the oyster sauce, soy sauce, and honey. Pour this sauce over the vegetables and stir to combine.

  6. Add the crispy tofu back into the wok and toss to coat everything in the sauce.

  7. Remove from heat and serve the lemongrass tofu and vegetable stir fry over steamed rice or noodles.

Lemongrass Beef Stir Fry

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound flank steak, thinly sliced
  • 2 stalks of lemongrass, finely chopped
  • 1 cup snap peas
  • 1 red onion, sliced
  • 1 red chili, thinly sliced
  • 3 tablespoons hoisin sauce
  • 2 tablespoons rice vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon sesame oil
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • Cooking oil

Instructions:

  1. Heat some cooking oil in a wok or large skillet over high heat.

  2. Add the sliced flank steak and stir-fry until browned and cooked to your liking. Remove from the wok and set aside.

  3. In the same wok, add the minced garlic and chopped lemongrass. Stir-fry for a minute until fragrant.

  4. Toss in the snap peas, sliced red onion, and red chili. Continue stir-frying until the vegetables are tender.

  5. In a small bowl, whisk together the hoisin sauce, rice vinegar, and sesame oil. Pour this sauce over the stir fry and return the cooked beef to the wok.

  6. Toss everything together until the beef and vegetables are evenly coated in the sauce.

  7. Remove from heat and serve the lemongrass beef stir fry over steamed rice or noodles.

These unique and flavorful stir fry recipes with lemongrass are sure to tantalize your taste buds and impress your family and friends. Feel free to experiment with other ingredients and customize the recipes to suit your preferences. Enjoy the vibrant flavors and aromas of lemongrass in these delightful stir fry dishes!
