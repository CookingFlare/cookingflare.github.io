The Perfect Blend: 4 Herb and Spice Rubs for Grilled Chicken


Are you tired of the same old grilled chicken? Do you want to take your backyard barbecue to the next level? Look no further! In this post, we will explore four delicious herb and spice rubs that will elevate your grilled chicken game. These rubs are easy to make, packed with flavor, and guaranteed to impress your friends and family.

1. Smoky Paprika Rub

Ingredients:

  • 2 tablespoons smoked paprika
  • 1 tablespoon garlic powder
  • 1 tablespoon onion powder
  • 1 tablespoon brown sugar
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1/2 teaspoon cayenne pepper (adjust according to your spice preference)

Instructions:

  1. In a small bowl, combine all the ingredients and mix well.
  2. Generously coat your chicken with the rub, making sure to cover all sides.
  3. Let the chicken marinate in the rub for at least 30 minutes, or overnight for a more intense flavor.
  4. Grill the chicken over medium-high heat until it reaches an internal temperature of 165°F (74°C).
  5. Serve with your favorite sides and enjoy the smoky goodness!

2. Lemon Herb Rub

Ingredients:

  • Zest of 2 lemons
  • 2 tablespoons dried thyme
  • 2 tablespoons dried rosemary
  • 1 tablespoon dried oregano
  • 1 tablespoon garlic powder
  • 1 tablespoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon black pepper

Instructions:

  1. In a small bowl, combine the lemon zest, dried herbs, garlic powder, salt, and black pepper.
  2. Rub the mixture all over the chicken, ensuring an even coating.
  3. Allow the chicken to marinate in the rub for at least 30 minutes.
  4. Grill the chicken over medium heat until it’s cooked through and juices run clear.
  5. Squeeze fresh lemon juice over the grilled chicken for an extra burst of flavor before serving.

3. Spicy Cajun Rub

Ingredients:

  • 2 tablespoons paprika
  • 1 tablespoon dried thyme
  • 1 tablespoon dried oregano
  • 1 tablespoon garlic powder
  • 1 tablespoon onion powder
  • 1 tablespoon cayenne pepper
  • 1 tablespoon black pepper
  • 1 tablespoon salt

Instructions:

  1. In a small bowl, combine all the ingredients and mix well.
  2. Rub the mixture onto the chicken, making sure to coat it thoroughly.
  3. Allow the chicken to marinate in the rub for at least 30 minutes.
  4. Grill the chicken over medium-high heat until it’s cooked through and nicely charred.
  5. Serve with a side of creamy coleslaw to balance out the spice.

4. Mediterranean Herb Rub

Ingredients:

  • 2 tablespoons dried oregano
  • 2 tablespoons dried basil
  • 1 tablespoon dried thyme
  • 1 tablespoon dried rosemary
  • 1 tablespoon garlic powder
  • 1 tablespoon onion powder
  • 1 tablespoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1/4 cup olive oil

Instructions:

  1. In a small bowl, combine the dried herbs, garlic powder, onion powder, salt, black pepper, and olive oil.
  2. Stir well to create a paste-like consistency.
  3. Rub the mixture onto the chicken, evenly coating all sides.
  4. Let the chicken marinate in the rub for at least 30 minutes.
  5. Grill the chicken over medium heat until it’s cooked through and has a golden crust.
  6. Serve with a fresh Greek salad and some warm pita bread for a complete Mediterranean feast.

There you have it – four incredible herb and spice rubs that will transform your grilled chicken into a culinary masterpiece. Whether you prefer smoky, zesty, spicy, or Mediterranean flavors, these rubs have got you covered. So fire up that grill, get creative, and enjoy the perfect blend of flavors in every juicy bite. Happy grilling!
