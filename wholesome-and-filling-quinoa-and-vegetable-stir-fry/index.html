Wholesome and Filling Quinoa and Vegetable Stir-Fry


Are you looking for a delicious and nutritious meal that is quick and easy to make? Look no further than this Wholesome and Filling Quinoa and Vegetable Stir-Fry recipe. Packed with protein, fiber, and a rainbow of vegetables, this stir-fry will leave you feeling satisfied and nourished.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup quinoa
  • 2 cups water
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 onion, thinly sliced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 red bell pepper, thinly sliced
  • 1 yellow bell pepper, thinly sliced
  • 1 zucchini, thinly sliced
  • 1 cup broccoli florets
  • 1 cup snap peas
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon sesame oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Optional toppings: chopped cilantro, sliced green onions, sesame seeds

Instructions

  1. Rinse the quinoa under cold water to remove any bitterness. In a medium saucepan, bring the water to a boil. Add the quinoa, reduce the heat to low, cover, and simmer for 15-20 minutes, or until the quinoa is tender and the water is absorbed. Fluff the quinoa with a fork and set aside.

  2. In a large skillet or wok, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the onion and garlic and sauté until fragrant, about 2 minutes.

  3. Add the bell peppers, zucchini, broccoli, and snap peas to the skillet. Stir-fry for 5-7 minutes, or until the vegetables are crisp-tender.

  4. In a small bowl, whisk together the soy sauce and sesame oil. Pour the mixture over the vegetables and stir to coat evenly. Season with salt and pepper to taste.

  5. Add the cooked quinoa to the skillet and toss to combine. Cook for an additional 2-3 minutes, or until everything is heated through.

  6. Remove the skillet from the heat and garnish with your choice of optional toppings, such as chopped cilantro, sliced green onions, or sesame seeds.

Serving Suggestions

This Wholesome and Filling Quinoa and Vegetable Stir-Fry can be enjoyed on its own as a satisfying vegetarian meal or served as a side dish alongside grilled chicken, tofu, or fish. It’s also great for meal prep, as it can be stored in the refrigerator for up to 4 days. Simply reheat in the microwave or on the stovetop for a quick and nutritious meal.

Health Benefits

The combination of quinoa and vegetables in this stir-fry provides a wide range of health benefits. Quinoa is a complete protein, meaning it contains all nine essential amino acids. It’s also high in fiber, which aids in digestion and helps to keep you feeling full. The colorful assortment of vegetables adds vitamins, minerals, and antioxidants to the dish, promoting overall health and well-being.

Conclusion

Wholesome and Filling Quinoa and Vegetable Stir-Fry is a versatile and nutritious recipe that can be enjoyed by anyone, whether you’re a vegetarian, vegan, or simply looking to incorporate more plant-based meals into your diet. With its simple preparation and vibrant flavors, this stir-fry is sure to become a household favorite. Give it a try and experience the goodness of this wholesome and filling dish!
