BBQ Chicken Wings: Finger-Licking Recipes for Game Day


When it comes to game day, finger foods are a must-have for any gathering. But what’s better than a platter of juicy, crispy, and flavorful BBQ chicken wings? In this post, we’ll dive into the world of BBQ chicken wings, sharing our favorite recipes to make your game day party a touchdown.

The Perfect BBQ Chicken Wings

Before we dive into the recipes, let’s talk about the perfect BBQ chicken wings. What makes them so special? It’s all about the combination of tender meat, crispy skin, and a tangy, sweet, and smoky BBQ sauce. To achieve this, you’ll need to start with high-quality chicken wings, preferably fresh or frozen, and not processed.

Recipe 1: Classic BBQ Chicken Wings

For a classic BBQ chicken wing recipe, you’ll need:

  • 2 pounds chicken wings
  • 1/4 cup BBQ sauce
  • 2 tablespoons brown sugar
  • 1 tablespoon apple cider vinegar
  • 1 teaspoon smoked paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
  • Salt and pepper, to taste

Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C). Rinse the chicken wings and pat them dry with paper towels. In a large bowl, whisk together the BBQ sauce, brown sugar, apple cider vinegar, smoked paprika, and garlic powder. Add the chicken wings and toss to coat. Line a baking sheet with aluminum foil and spray with cooking spray. Arrange the wings on the baking sheet in a single layer and bake for 25-30 minutes or until cooked through. Remove from the oven and toss with additional BBQ sauce, if desired.

Recipe 2: Spicy BBQ Chicken Wings

For a spicy twist on the classic BBQ chicken wing recipe, you’ll need:

  • 2 pounds chicken wings
  • 1/4 cup BBQ sauce
  • 2 tablespoons hot sauce (such as Frank’s RedHot)
  • 1 tablespoon brown sugar
  • 1 teaspoon smoked paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon cayenne pepper
  • Salt and pepper, to taste

Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C). Rinse the chicken wings and pat them dry with paper towels. In a large bowl, whisk together the BBQ sauce, hot sauce, brown sugar, smoked paprika, garlic powder, and cayenne pepper. Add the chicken wings and toss to coat. Line a baking sheet with aluminum foil and spray with cooking spray. Arrange the wings on the baking sheet in a single layer and bake for 25-30 minutes or until cooked through. Remove from the oven and toss with additional BBQ sauce, if desired.

Recipe 3: Honey BBQ Chicken Wings

For a sweet and tangy twist on the classic BBQ chicken wing recipe, you’ll need:

  • 2 pounds chicken wings
  • 1/4 cup BBQ sauce
  • 2 tablespoons honey
  • 1 tablespoon apple cider vinegar
  • 1 teaspoon smoked paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
  • Salt and pepper, to taste

Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C). Rinse the chicken wings and pat them dry with paper towels. In a large bowl, whisk together the BBQ sauce, honey, apple cider vinegar, smoked paprika, and garlic powder. Add the chicken wings and toss to coat. Line a baking sheet with aluminum foil and spray with cooking spray. Arrange the wings on the baking sheet in a single layer and bake for 25-30 minutes or until cooked through. Remove from the oven and toss with additional BBQ sauce, if desired.

Tips and Variations

  • To make your BBQ chicken wings more crispy, increase the oven temperature to 425°F (220°C) and bake for an additional 5-10 minutes.
  • To make your BBQ chicken wings more spicy, add more hot sauce or use different types of hot sauce, such as sriracha or hot sauce with a spicy kick.
  • To make your BBQ chicken wings more sweet, add more honey or use different types of sweetener, such as maple syrup or brown sugar.
  • To make your BBQ chicken wings more smoky, add more smoked paprika or use different types of smoked paprika, such as chipotle or smoked paprika with a smoky flavor.

Conclusion

BBQ chicken wings are the perfect finger food for any game day gathering. With these three recipes, you’ll have a variety of options to please even the pickiest of eaters. From classic to spicy to sweet, there’s a BBQ chicken wing recipe for everyone. So, grab your ingredients and get ready to score a touchdown with your friends and family. Happy cooking!
