Simple Pesto Shrimp Pasta Recipe


If you’re looking for a quick and delicious meal that’s perfect for both weeknight dinners and special occasions, look no further than this simple pesto shrimp pasta recipe. This dish is the epitome of comfort food, combining the fresh flavors of basil pesto with succulent shrimp and perfectly cooked pasta. The best part? It comes together in just a few easy steps, making it an ideal choice for busy cooks who still want to enjoy a homemade meal.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound of large shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 12 ounces of your favorite pasta (linguine or fettuccine work well)
  • 1/2 cup of homemade or store-bought basil pesto
  • 2 tablespoons of olive oil
  • 3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Freshly grated Parmesan cheese for garnish
  • Fresh basil leaves for garnish

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Shrimp: Start by patting the shrimp dry with a paper towel and seasoning them with a pinch of salt and pepper. This will help to enhance their natural flavors.

  2. Cook the Pasta: Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil and cook the pasta according to the package instructions until al dente. Remember to reserve about 1/2 cup of the pasta cooking water before draining the pasta.

  3. Sauté the Shrimp: In a large skillet, heat the olive oil over medium-high heat. Add the minced garlic and sauté for about 30 seconds until fragrant. Then, add the seasoned shrimp to the skillet and cook for 2-3 minutes on each side until they turn pink and opaque. Remove the cooked shrimp from the skillet and set them aside.

  4. Combine the Pasta and Pesto: In the same skillet, add the cooked pasta, basil pesto, and a splash of the reserved pasta cooking water. Toss everything together until the pasta is well coated with the pesto sauce and heated through.

  5. Add the Shrimp: Gently fold the cooked shrimp into the pesto pasta, ensuring that they are evenly distributed.

  6. Serve and Garnish: Divide the pesto shrimp pasta among serving plates and garnish with freshly grated Parmesan cheese and fresh basil leaves. The extra sprinkle of Parmesan adds a delightful nuttiness to the dish, while the basil leaves provide a pop of color and a hint of freshness.

Tips for Success

  • Fresh, high-quality ingredients make all the difference in this recipe. If possible, use homemade basil pesto for the best flavor, but store-bought pesto works perfectly well too.
  • Be mindful not to overcook the shrimp, as they can become tough and rubbery. Keep a close eye on them as they cook, and remove them from the skillet as soon as they turn pink and opaque.
  • Reserve some of the starchy pasta cooking water before draining the pasta. This water helps to loosen the pesto sauce and create a silky, creamy texture without the need for heavy cream.

Final Thoughts

This simple pesto shrimp pasta recipe is a celebration of fresh, vibrant flavors that come together effortlessly to create a memorable meal. Whether you’re cooking for a crowd or simply treating yourself to a satisfying dinner, this dish is sure to impress with its elegant simplicity and mouthwatering taste. So, next time you’re craving a comforting yet sophisticated pasta dish, give this recipe a try and savor every delightful bite. Cheers to good food, good company, and the joy of cooking!
