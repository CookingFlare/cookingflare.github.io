Easy Slow Cooker Honey Garlic Sesame Chicken Recipe


If you’re looking for a delicious and hassle-free meal, then this Easy Slow Cooker Honey Garlic Sesame Chicken recipe is just what you need. With minimal preparation and maximum flavor, this dish is perfect for busy weeknights or lazy weekends. The combination of sweet honey, savory garlic, and nutty sesame makes every bite a burst of deliciousness. Plus, the slow cooker does all the work, so you can sit back and relax while your kitchen is filled with the enticing aroma of this mouthwatering chicken dish.

Ingredients

  • 2 pounds boneless, skinless chicken thighs
  • 1/3 cup low-sodium soy sauce
  • 1/3 cup honey
  • 1/4 cup ketchup
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons sesame oil
  • 1 tablespoon rice vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon cornstarch
  • 2 tablespoons water
  • 1 tablespoon sesame seeds
  • Green onions, chopped (for garnish)

Instructions

  1. In a medium-sized bowl, whisk together the soy sauce, honey, ketchup, minced garlic, sesame oil, and rice vinegar until well combined.

  2. Place the chicken thighs in the slow cooker and pour the sauce mixture over the top, making sure to coat each piece of chicken thoroughly.

  3. Cover the slow cooker with the lid and cook on low heat for 4 hours, or until the chicken is tender and cooked through.

  4. Once the chicken is cooked, remove it from the slow cooker and set it aside. In a small bowl, whisk together the cornstarch and water until smooth. Pour the cornstarch mixture into the slow cooker and stir well to combine with the remaining sauce.

  5. Return the chicken to the slow cooker and stir gently to coat it with the thickened sauce. Cover the slow cooker again and cook on high heat for an additional 15-20 minutes, or until the sauce has thickened.

  6. Remove the chicken from the slow cooker and transfer it to a serving dish. Sprinkle with sesame seeds and chopped green onions for added flavor and garnish.

  7. Serve the Easy Slow Cooker Honey Garlic Sesame Chicken hot with steamed rice or noodles. Enjoy!

Tips and Variations

  • For an extra kick of heat, add a pinch of red pepper flakes or a drizzle of sriracha sauce to the sauce mixture.
  • Feel free to substitute chicken breasts for chicken thighs if you prefer white meat.
  • If you like your sesame chicken to have a bit of crunch, you can lightly coat the chicken with flour and pan-fry it before placing it in the slow cooker.
  • This recipe can easily be doubled or halved to suit your needs.
  • Leftovers can be stored in an airtight container in the refrigerator for up to 3 days.

Conclusion

With its irresistible blend of flavors, this Easy Slow Cooker Honey Garlic Sesame Chicken recipe is sure to become a family favorite. Whether you’re a busy parent, a working professional, or simply someone who loves good food, this dish will satisfy your cravings without the fuss. So, dust off your slow cooker and give this recipe a try. Your taste buds will thank you!
