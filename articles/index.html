Soul-Satisfying Chicken Pot Pie: A Recipe to Warm Your Heart

There’s nothing quite like a homemade chicken pot pie to bring comfort and warmth to the dinner table. The combination of tender chicken, savory vegetables, and a buttery crust is a timeless classic that never fails to satisfy. Whether you’re cooking for a family dinner, a gathering of friends, or simply craving some old-fashioned comfort food, this soul-satisfying chicken pot pie recipe is sure to hit the spot. Ingredients For the Crust: 2 1/2 cups all-purpose flour 1 cup (2 sticks) unsalted butter, cold and cubed 1 teaspoon salt 6-8 tablespoons ice water For the Filling: 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts, cooked and shredded 1 cup carrots, diced 1 cup frozen peas 1/2 cup celery, diced 1/3 cup unsalted butter 1/3 cup all-purpose flour 1/2 teaspoon salt 1/4 teaspoon black pepper 1/4 teaspoon celery seed 1 3/4 cups chicken broth 2/3 cup milk Instructions Step 1: Prepare the Crust In a large mixing bowl, combine the flour and salt. Add the cold, cubed butter to the flour mixture and use a pastry cutter or your hands to work the butter into the flour until it resembles coarse crumbs. Gradually add the ice water, one tablespoon at a time, and mix the dough until it comes together. Divide the dough in half, shape each half into a disk, wrap in plastic wrap, and refrigerate for at least 1 hour. Step 2: Make the Filling In a large saucepan, melt the butter over medium heat. Stir in the flour, salt, pepper, and celery seed until well combined to form a roux. Gradually whisk in the chicken broth and milk, stirring constantly until the mixture is thick and bubbly. Add the cooked, shredded chicken, carrots, peas, and celery to the saucepan, stirring until the filling is well combined. Remove from heat. Step 3: Assemble the Pie Preheat the oven to 425°F (220°C). Roll out one disk of the chilled dough on a floured surface to fit a 9-inch pie dish. Place the rolled-out dough into the pie dish, pressing it gently to fit the bottom and sides. Pour the chicken and vegetable filling into the prepared pie crust. Roll out the second disk of dough and place it over the filling. Trim and crimp the edges, and cut a few slits in the top crust to vent. Step 4: Bake and Enjoy Place the pie on a baking sheet to catch any drips and bake in the preheated oven for 30-35 minutes, or until the crust is golden brown and the filling is bubbling. Allow the chicken pot pie to cool for a few minutes before slicing and serving. The Perfect Comfort Food This soul-satisfying chicken pot pie is a labor of love that embodies the essence of home-cooked comfort. The flaky, buttery crust envelops a rich and creamy filling brimming with tender chicken and wholesome vegetables, creating a dish that’s as heartwarming as it is delicious.
