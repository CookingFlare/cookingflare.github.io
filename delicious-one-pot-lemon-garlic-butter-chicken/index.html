Delicious One Pot Lemon Garlic Butter Chicken


There’s nothing quite like the aroma of lemon and garlic filling your kitchen as you cook a delicious meal. If you’re a fan of one-pot wonders, then this recipe is perfect for you. This One Pot Lemon Garlic Butter Chicken is not only flavorful but also incredibly easy to make. With just a few simple ingredients and one pot, you can have a mouthwatering chicken dish on the table in no time.

Ingredients

  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 4 tablespoons unsalted butter
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 lemon, juiced and zested
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • 1 teaspoon dried rosemary
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1 cup chicken broth
  • Fresh parsley, chopped (for garnish)

Instructions

  1. In a large skillet or Dutch oven, melt the butter over medium-high heat. Ensure that the skillet is large enough to fit all the chicken breasts comfortably.

  2. Season both sides of the chicken breasts with salt and black pepper. Place the chicken breasts in the skillet and cook until browned, about 4-5 minutes per side. Remove the chicken from the skillet and set aside.

  3. In the same skillet, add the minced garlic and cook for about 1 minute, until fragrant. Be careful not to burn the garlic.

  4. Add the lemon juice, lemon zest, dried thyme, and dried rosemary to the skillet. Stir to combine with the garlic and cook for another minute.

  5. Pour in the chicken broth and bring it to a simmer. Return the chicken breasts to the skillet, nestling them in the sauce. Reduce the heat to low, cover the skillet, and let it simmer for about 15-20 minutes, or until the chicken is cooked through and no longer pink in the center.

  6. Once the chicken is cooked, remove it from the skillet and set aside. Increase the heat to medium-high and let the sauce simmer for another 2-3 minutes to thicken slightly.

  7. Serve the chicken with the lemon garlic butter sauce spooned over the top. Garnish with freshly chopped parsley for added freshness and color.

Tips and Variations

  • For added flavor, you can marinate the chicken breasts in the lemon juice, zest, garlic, and herbs for at least 30 minutes before cooking. This will infuse the chicken with even more delicious lemony goodness.
  • If you prefer a creamier sauce, you can add a splash of heavy cream or half-and-half to the skillet after simmering the chicken. Stir it in gently and let it heat through before serving.
  • Feel free to customize this recipe by adding your favorite vegetables to the skillet. Bell peppers, zucchini, or mushrooms would all be delicious additions.

Conclusion

This One Pot Lemon Garlic Butter Chicken is a winner in all aspects - delicious, easy to make, and requires minimal cleanup. The combination of tangy lemon, aromatic garlic, and savory herbs creates a flavor profile that is simply irresistible. Whether you’re cooking for a weeknight dinner or hosting a dinner party, this dish is sure to impress. So go ahead, give it a try, and let your taste buds be amazed!
