Mouthwatering Italian Meatballs Recipe - Perfect for Pasta


Italian cuisine is known for its rich and flavorful dishes, and one classic dish that never fails to impress is mouthwatering Italian meatballs. These tender and juicy meatballs, paired with a delicious tomato sauce, are the perfect accompaniment to a plate of pasta. In this recipe, I’ll share with you my secret tips and techniques to create the most flavorful Italian meatballs that will leave your taste buds craving for more.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1/2 pound ground pork
  • 1/2 cup breadcrumbs
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh basil
  • 1/4 cup milk
  • 1 egg
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1/4 teaspoon red pepper flakes (optional)

Instructions

  1. In a large mixing bowl, combine the ground beef and ground pork. Mix them together until well combined.

  2. Add the breadcrumbs, Parmesan cheese, minced garlic, fresh parsley, fresh basil, milk, egg, salt, black pepper, and red pepper flakes (if using). Mix everything together until all the ingredients are evenly distributed.

  3. Once the mixture is well combined, it’s time to shape the meatballs. Take a small handful of the mixture and roll it between your palms to form a ball. Repeat this process until all the mixture is used up, making sure to keep the meatballs uniform in size.

  4. In a large skillet, heat a drizzle of olive oil over medium heat. Once the oil is hot, add the meatballs to the skillet, making sure not to overcrowd them. Cook the meatballs in batches if necessary.

  5. Cook the meatballs for about 6-8 minutes, or until they are browned on all sides. This will give them a delicious crust and help seal in the flavors.

  6. Once the meatballs are browned, carefully remove them from the skillet and set them aside. They will finish cooking in the sauce.

  7. In the same skillet, add your favorite tomato sauce. You can use store-bought sauce or make your own from scratch. If using store-bought sauce, choose a high-quality one for the best results.

  8. Gently place the cooked meatballs into the tomato sauce, making sure they are fully submerged. Cover the skillet with a lid and let the meatballs simmer in the sauce for about 20-30 minutes, or until they are cooked through.

  9. While the meatballs are simmering in the sauce, prepare your favorite pasta according to the package instructions. Once cooked, drain the pasta and set it aside.

  10. Serve the mouthwatering Italian meatballs over a bed of cooked pasta. Garnish with some freshly grated Parmesan cheese and chopped parsley for an extra burst of flavor.

Conclusion

There you have it – a mouthwatering Italian meatballs recipe that is perfect for pasta. The combination of tender meatballs and flavorful tomato sauce creates a harmony of flavors that will satisfy even the pickiest of eaters. Whether you’re cooking for your family or hosting a dinner party, this recipe is sure to impress. So why not give it a try and experience the deliciousness of authentic Italian cuisine in the comfort of your own home?

Remember, cooking is all about experimentation and making the recipe your own. Feel free to add your own twist to this classic Italian dish by incorporating different herbs, spices, or even cheeses. The possibilities are endless! Enjoy your homemade Italian meatballs and savor the flavors of Italy. Buon appetito!
