Greek-style Lemon and Herb Couscous Salad: A Fresh and Zesty Dish


When it comes to refreshing and vibrant salads, Greek-style Lemon and Herb Couscous Salad is a delightful choice. This dish is a celebration of Mediterranean flavors, combining the zing of lemon, the fragrance of fresh herbs, and the wholesome goodness of couscous. Whether you’re looking for a light lunch, a side dish for a barbecue, or a potluck contribution, this salad is sure to impress with its burst of flavors and textures.

Ingredients

For the Salad:

  • 1 ½ cups couscous
  • 1 ¾ cups vegetable broth
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1 cucumber, diced
  • ½ red onion, finely chopped
  • ½ cup Kalamata olives, pitted and halved
  • ¼ cup crumbled feta cheese
  • 1/3 cup chopped fresh parsley
  • 1/3 cup chopped fresh mint

For the Dressing:

  • 1/3 cup extra-virgin olive oil
  • Zest of 2 lemons
  • 1/3 cup fresh lemon juice
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon Dijon mustard
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Couscous: In a medium saucepan, bring the vegetable broth to a boil. Stir in the couscous, cover the saucepan, and remove it from the heat. Let it stand for 5 minutes, then fluff the couscous with a fork and allow it to cool to room temperature.

  2. Make the Dressing: In a small bowl, whisk together the olive oil, lemon zest, lemon juice, minced garlic, Dijon mustard, salt, and pepper until well combined.

  3. Assemble the Salad: In a large mixing bowl, combine the cooked and cooled couscous, cherry tomatoes, cucumber, red onion, Kalamata olives, feta cheese, parsley, and mint. Gently toss the ingredients to distribute them evenly.

  4. Dress the Salad: Pour the prepared dressing over the salad and toss gently to coat all the ingredients with the flavorful dressing.

  5. Chill and Serve: Cover the salad and refrigerate for at least 1 hour to allow the flavors to meld together. Before serving, give the salad a final toss and adjust the seasoning if needed.

Serving Suggestions

Greek-style Lemon and Herb Couscous Salad is a versatile dish that pairs beautifully with grilled chicken, lamb, or fish. It can also be served alongside falafel, hummus, and pita bread for a complete Mediterranean feast. This salad is perfect for picnics, potlucks, and outdoor gatherings, making it an excellent option for summer entertaining.

Health Benefits

This salad is not only a treat for your taste buds but also a nutritional powerhouse. Couscous is a good source of complex carbohydrates and provides a healthy dose of fiber. The fresh vegetables and herbs contribute essential vitamins, minerals, and antioxidants, while the olive oil offers heart-healthy monounsaturated fats. With the addition of protein-rich feta cheese, this salad is a well-rounded and satisfying dish.

Conclusion

Greek-style Lemon and Herb Couscous Salad is a celebration of Mediterranean flavors and a testament to the simplicity of fresh, wholesome ingredients. Whether you’re new to Mediterranean cuisine or a seasoned enthusiast, this dish is bound to become a favorite in your recipe repertoire. Its vibrant colors, refreshing flavors, and versatility make it a go-to option for any occasion. So, next time you’re craving a zesty and invigorating salad, give this Greek-inspired creation a try and transport your taste buds to the sunny shores of the Mediterranean.
