Satisfying Keto Cauliflower Crust BBQ Chicken Pizza


If you’re looking for a delicious and satisfying keto-friendly pizza recipe, then you’re in for a treat! This Satisfying Keto Cauliflower Crust BBQ Chicken Pizza is a game-changer for pizza lovers who are following a low-carb or keto lifestyle. With a flavorful cauliflower crust, savory BBQ sauce, tender chicken, and gooey cheese, this pizza is sure to satisfy your cravings without derailing your keto goals. Let’s dive into the recipe and discover how to create this mouthwatering dish in the comfort of your own kitchen.

Ingredients

For the Cauliflower Crust:

  • 1 head of cauliflower, grated
  • 1 egg, beaten
  • 1/2 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 teaspoon Italian seasoning
  • Salt and pepper to taste

For the Toppings:

  • 1/2 cup sugar-free BBQ sauce
  • 1 cup cooked and shredded chicken breast
  • 1/2 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/4 cup red onion, thinly sliced
  • Fresh cilantro for garnish

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Cauliflower Crust:

    • Preheat your oven to 425°F (220°C) and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
    • Grate the cauliflower using a box grater or a food processor. Place the grated cauliflower in a microwave-safe bowl and microwave on high for 4-5 minutes, or until tender.
    • Allow the cauliflower to cool, then place it in a clean kitchen towel and squeeze out as much moisture as possible.
    • In a mixing bowl, combine the cauliflower, beaten egg, shredded mozzarella, Parmesan cheese, Italian seasoning, salt, and pepper. Mix until well combined.

  2. Bake the Crust:

    • Transfer the cauliflower mixture onto the prepared baking sheet and shape it into a 10-12 inch round crust, about 1/4 inch thick.
    • Bake the crust in the preheated oven for 15-20 minutes, or until golden brown and set.

  3. Assemble the Pizza:

    • Once the crust is ready, spread the sugar-free BBQ sauce evenly over the crust, leaving a small border around the edges.
    • Sprinkle the shredded chicken over the BBQ sauce, followed by the mozzarella cheese and thinly sliced red onion.

  4. Bake to Perfection:

    • Return the pizza to the oven and bake for an additional 10-12 minutes, or until the cheese is melted and bubbly.

  5. Garnish and Serve:

    • Remove the pizza from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes. Sprinkle fresh cilantro over the top for a burst of freshness.
    • Slice the pizza into wedges and serve hot.

Tips and Tricks

  • For extra flavor, you can grill the chicken with a sprinkle of BBQ seasoning before shredding it for the pizza.
  • If you prefer a crispier crust, you can place the cauliflower crust directly on a preheated pizza stone.
  • Feel free to customize the toppings with your favorite keto-friendly ingredients such as bell peppers, mushrooms, or even jalapenos for a spicy kick.

Final Thoughts

This Satisfying Keto Cauliflower Crust BBQ Chicken Pizza is a testament to the fact that you can enjoy all the flavors of a classic pizza while staying true to your keto lifestyle. The cauliflower crust provides a sturdy base for all the delicious toppings, and the combination of tangy BBQ sauce, tender chicken, and melty cheese creates a symphony of flavors in every bite. Whether you’re hosting a keto-friendly gathering or simply craving a guilt-free pizza night, this recipe is sure to become a staple in your meal rotation. Give it a try and indulge in the ultimate keto pizza experience!
