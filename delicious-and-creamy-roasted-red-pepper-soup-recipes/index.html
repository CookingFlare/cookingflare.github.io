Delicious and Creamy Roasted Red Pepper Soup Recipes


There’s something truly magical about the rich, smoky flavors of roasted red peppers. When transformed into a velvety, creamy soup, they become a comforting and satisfying dish that’s perfect for any occasion. Whether you’re looking for a quick weeknight meal or an impressive starter for a dinner party, this roasted red pepper soup recipe is sure to delight your taste buds.

Ingredients

  • 6 large red bell peppers
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 medium potato, peeled and diced
  • 4 cups vegetable or chicken broth
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon cayenne pepper (adjust to taste)
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • Fresh basil leaves for garnish

Instructions

Step 1: Roasting the Red Peppers

  1. Preheat your broiler to high. Place the whole red bell peppers on a baking sheet and drizzle them with 1 tablespoon of olive oil.
  2. Place the baking sheet under the broiler and roast the peppers, turning occasionally, until the skins are charred and blistered. This should take about 15-20 minutes.
  3. Remove the peppers from the broiler and transfer them to a bowl. Cover the bowl with plastic wrap and let the peppers steam for about 10 minutes. This will make it easier to peel off the skins.
  4. Once the peppers have cooled, peel off the charred skins, remove the stems and seeds, and roughly chop the flesh.

Step 2: Cooking the Soup

  1. In a large pot, heat the remaining 1 tablespoon of olive oil over medium heat. Add the diced onion and cook until it becomes translucent, about 5 minutes.
  2. Add the minced garlic and diced potato to the pot and cook for an additional 3 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  3. Stir in the roasted red peppers, paprika, and cayenne pepper. Cook for another 5 minutes to allow the flavors to meld.
  4. Pour in the vegetable or chicken broth and bring the mixture to a boil. Reduce the heat and let it simmer for 20-25 minutes, or until the potatoes are tender.

Step 3: Blending and Finishing

  1. Using an immersion blender or a regular blender, carefully puree the soup until smooth. Be cautious with hot liquids in a regular blender to avoid splattering.
  2. Once the soup is smooth, return it to the pot if necessary and stir in the heavy cream. Season with salt and black pepper to taste.
  3. Simmer the soup for an additional 5 minutes to allow the flavors to come together.

Step 4: Serving

  1. Ladle the creamy roasted red pepper soup into bowls and garnish with fresh basil leaves.
  2. Serve the soup hot, accompanied by crusty bread or a simple green salad for a delightful meal.

Final Thoughts

This creamy roasted red pepper soup is a delightful blend of smoky sweetness and creamy richness. It’s a versatile dish that can be enjoyed on its own or as a flavorful complement to a variety of main courses. The vibrant color and enticing aroma of this soup make it a feast for the senses, and its velvety texture will leave you craving for more.

So, the next time you’re craving a comforting bowl of soup, give this roasted red pepper soup recipe a try. It’s a surefire way to warm your heart and soul with every spoonful!
