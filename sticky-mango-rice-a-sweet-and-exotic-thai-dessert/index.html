Sticky Mango Rice: A Sweet and Exotic Thai Dessert


If you’re looking to transport your taste buds to the vibrant streets of Thailand, then sticky mango rice is the perfect indulgence. This luscious dessert combines the creaminess of coconut-infused sticky rice with the sweetness of ripe mango, creating a tantalizing treat that’s both visually stunning and incredibly delicious. Let’s dive into the enchanting world of sticky mango rice and learn how to recreate this Thai delicacy in your own kitchen.

The Ingredients You’ll Need

To embark on this culinary adventure, gather the following ingredients:

  • 1 cup of glutinous rice (also known as sticky rice)
  • 1 can of coconut milk
  • 1/2 cup of granulated sugar
  • A pinch of salt
  • 2 ripe mangoes
  • Toasted sesame seeds (optional, for garnish)

The Preparation Process

Step 1: Preparing the Rice

Start by rinsing the glutinous rice under cold water until the water runs clear. Then, soak the rice in water for at least 4 hours or ideally overnight. Once soaked, drain the rice thoroughly.

Step 2: Cooking the Rice

In a steamer, line the basket with cheesecloth and place the soaked rice on top. Steam the rice over boiling water for 30-40 minutes, or until it’s tender and translucent.

Step 3: Making the Coconut Sauce

While the rice is steaming, pour the coconut milk into a saucepan, add the sugar and a pinch of salt, and stir over low heat until the sugar has dissolved. Set aside half a cup of the coconut sauce for serving.

Step 4: Assembling the Dish

Once the rice is cooked, transfer it to a large bowl and pour most of the coconut sauce over it. Gently fold the sauce into the rice until it’s well coated, allowing it to cool slightly.

The Final Touch

Peel and slice the ripe mangoes into thin strips or bite-sized pieces. Arrange a mound of the sweet, sticky rice on a serving plate, and top it with the fresh mango slices. Drizzle the reserved coconut sauce over the mango and rice, and sprinkle with toasted sesame seeds for a delightful crunch and nuttiness.

Enjoying Sticky Mango Rice

The beauty of sticky mango rice lies not only in its taste but also in its visual appeal. The vibrant colors of the mango against the creamy white rice create a feast for the eyes, making it a dessert that’s as delightful to behold as it is to eat.

A Taste of Thailand in Every Bite

Sticky mango rice is a beloved dessert in Thailand, often savored during special occasions and festivals. Its unique combination of flavors and textures captures the essence of Thai cuisine, showcasing the harmonious blend of sweet and savory that the country is renowned for.

In Conclusion

If you’re seeking to elevate your dessert game and infuse your kitchen with a touch of Thai elegance, sticky mango rice is a must-try. With its simple yet exquisite flavors, this dessert is sure to become a cherished favorite, transporting you to the sun-kissed paradise of Thailand with every delectable spoonful.

So, gather your ingredients, embrace the art of Thai cooking, and treat yourself to the sumptuous delight of sticky mango rice. Your taste buds will thank you for the unforgettable journey through the exotic flavors of Thailand.
