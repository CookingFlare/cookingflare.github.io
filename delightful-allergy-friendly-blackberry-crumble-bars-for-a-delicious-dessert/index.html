Delightful Allergy-Friendly Blackberry Crumble Bars for a Delicious Dessert


Are you looking for a delectable dessert that’s not only delicious but also allergy-friendly? Look no further than these delightful Blackberry Crumble Bars! Packed with the goodness of juicy blackberries and the perfect balance of sweet and tart flavors, these bars are sure to satisfy your cravings. Best of all, they are free from common allergens such as gluten, dairy, and nuts, making them a safe and tasty treat for everyone to enjoy.

Ingredients

For the crust and crumble topping:

  • 2 cups gluten-free flour blend
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/2 cup dairy-free butter, melted
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

For the blackberry filling:

  • 3 cups fresh blackberries
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 2 tablespoons cornstarch

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C) and line a square baking pan with parchment paper.

  2. In a mixing bowl, combine the gluten-free flour blend, granulated sugar, melted dairy-free butter, and vanilla extract. Mix until the ingredients are well combined and a crumbly dough forms.

  3. Reserve about a cup of the crumble mixture for the topping and press the remaining mixture onto the bottom of the prepared baking pan. Use your hands or the back of a spoon to ensure an even layer.

  4. In a separate bowl, gently toss the blackberries with granulated sugar and cornstarch until the berries are coated evenly.

  5. Spread the blackberry mixture over the crust in the baking pan, ensuring it is spread out evenly.

  6. Take the reserved crumble mixture and sprinkle it over the top of the blackberry filling, creating a deliciously crunchy topping.

  7. Bake the blackberry crumble bars in the preheated oven for 35-40 minutes, or until the top is golden brown and the blackberry filling is bubbling.

  8. Once baked, remove the pan from the oven and let it cool completely before cutting into bars. This will allow the bars to set properly and make them easier to handle.

  9. Serve these delightful allergy-friendly blackberry crumble bars as a dessert or enjoy them as a sweet treat any time of the day. They pair perfectly with a scoop of dairy-free vanilla ice cream or a dollop of coconut whipped cream.

Tips and Variations

  • If you don’t have fresh blackberries, you can use frozen blackberries instead. Just make sure to thaw them before using and drain any excess liquid.

  • Feel free to customize the crumble bars by adding a sprinkle of cinnamon or a dash of lemon zest to the blackberry filling. These additions will enhance the flavors and give the bars an extra zing.

  • For an extra touch of sweetness, you can drizzle a simple glaze made with powdered sugar and dairy-free milk over the cooled bars.

  • These bars can be stored in an airtight container at room temperature for up to 3 days. They also freeze well, so you can make a batch in advance and enjoy them whenever you desire a delightful dessert.

So there you have it - a mouthwatering recipe for allergy-friendly blackberry crumble bars that will leave you craving for more. Whether you have dietary restrictions or simply want to indulge in a delicious treat, these bars are the perfect choice. Give them a try and savor the delightful flavors of juicy blackberries and buttery crumble. Enjoy!
