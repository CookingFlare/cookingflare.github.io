One Pot Lemon Garlic Butter Salmon and Asparagus


If you’re looking for a delicious, healthy, and easy-to-make meal, then you’re in the right place! This one pot lemon garlic butter salmon and asparagus recipe is a perfect choice for a quick weeknight dinner or a cozy weekend meal. Not only is it packed with flavor, but it’s also a great way to get your omega-3 fatty acids and a dose of green goodness from the asparagus. Let’s dive into the recipe and make your taste buds dance with joy!

Ingredients

  • 4 salmon fillets
  • 1 bunch of asparagus, ends trimmed
  • 4 tablespoons of butter
  • 4 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 lemon (juice and zest)
  • 1 teaspoon of dried oregano
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley for garnish

Instructions

  1. Prep the Ingredients: Start by gathering all your ingredients and prepping them. Trim the ends of the asparagus, mince the garlic, and zest the lemon. It’s always good to have everything ready before you start cooking.

  2. Season the Salmon: Season the salmon fillets with salt, black pepper, and dried oregano. This will ensure that each bite is packed with flavor.

  3. Melt the Butter: In a large skillet or pan, melt the butter over medium heat. Once the butter is melted, add the minced garlic and sauté for a minute until it becomes fragrant.

  4. Cook the Salmon: Carefully place the seasoned salmon fillets in the skillet with the melted butter and garlic. Let them cook for about 4-5 minutes on each side, or until they are golden and cooked to your liking. Once done, remove the salmon from the skillet and set it aside.

  5. Add the Asparagus: In the same skillet, add the trimmed asparagus. Cook them for 5-7 minutes, or until they are tender but still crisp. Make sure to coat them with the flavorful butter and garlic in the skillet.

  6. Combine the Flavors: Once the asparagus is cooked, return the salmon fillets to the skillet. Squeeze the juice of the lemon over the salmon and asparagus, and sprinkle the lemon zest on top for an extra zing of flavor.

  7. Serve and Enjoy: Garnish the dish with fresh parsley for a pop of color and freshness. Then, it’s time to serve up this delightful one pot lemon garlic butter salmon and asparagus. It’s best enjoyed hot, straight from the skillet to your plate.

The Perfect Meal

This one pot lemon garlic butter salmon and asparagus dish is a true celebration of fresh, vibrant flavors. The succulent salmon, infused with the tangy lemon and aromatic garlic butter, pairs perfectly with the tender-crisp asparagus. It’s a wholesome meal that’s not only a treat for your taste buds but also a nourishing choice for your body.

Conclusion

With this recipe, you have a fabulous meal that’s not only delicious but also incredibly easy to make. The best part is that you only need one pot, which means minimal cleanup – a win-win situation! Whether you’re cooking for yourself, your family, or guests, this dish is sure to impress. So, roll up your sleeves, gather your ingredients, and let’s bring this delightful one pot lemon garlic butter salmon and asparagus to life in your kitchen. Cheers to good food and happy cooking!
