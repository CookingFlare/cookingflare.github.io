Keto in Love: Romantic Low-Carb Recipes to Heat Up Your Night


Are you looking for something special to cook for your loved one this Valentine’s Day? Do you fancy indulging in some delectable gourmet cuisine while keeping it low-carb and Keto-friendly? Well, look no further because we’ve got a collection of scrumptious and romantic Keto recipes that will make your dinner date an unforgettable one.

1. Seared Scallops with Cauliflower Puree

Start your romantic evening with a dish that’s as elegant as it is delicious. Seared Scallops with a side of cauliflower puree will impress not only your date but also your taste buds.

Ingredients:

  • For the Scallops:

    • 12 large sea scallops
    • 2 tbsp avocado oil
    • Salt and pepper, to taste

  • For the Cauliflower Puree:

    • 1 medium head cauliflower, roughly chopped
    • 1 cup heavy cream
    • 2 tbsp unsalted butter
    • 2 cloves garlic, minced
    • Salt and pepper, to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your skillet or grill pan over medium-high heat. When hot, add 1 tablespoon of avocado oil. Season the scallops generously with salt and pepper on both sides.
  2. Place the scallops in the skillet and sear for 1-2 minutes on each side until golden brown. Remove from the skillet and set aside.
  3. Meanwhile, in a pot of boiling salted water, add the chopped cauliflower and cook for 15-20 minutes or until tender.
  4. Drain the cauliflower and add it to a food processor with the heavy cream, butter, and garlic. Blend until smooth and creamy. Season with salt and pepper, to taste.
  5. Serve the cauliflower puree with seared scallops, and enjoy a delightful start to your special night.

2. Lobster Bisque

No Valentine’s Day feast can be deemed complete without a luxurious and creamy bisque. Our Keto-friendly lobster bisque will make you crave more with every spoonful.

Ingredients:

  • 2 tablespoons avocado oil
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup dry white wine
  • 2 (14-ounce) cans chicken broth
  • 1 teaspoon old Bay seasoning
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • 2 tablespoons dry sherry
  • Salt and freshly ground black pepper, to taste
  • 2 cups cooked lobster meat, chopped

Instructions:

  1. In a large pot, heat the avocado oil over medium heat. Add the onion and garlic, sautéing until the onion is soft and translucent.
  2. Pour in the white wine and cook until it has reduced by half.
  3. Add the chicken broth, bay seasoning, and bring to a boil. Reduce to a simmer for around 20 minutes to allow the flavors to meld.
  4. Stir in the heavy cream and sherry, heating through but not boiling.
  5. Season with salt and pepper, to taste. Serve in bowls, topped with the cooked and chopped lobster meat.

3. Chocolate Avocado Mousse

End your romantic evening on a sweet note with this Keto-friendly dessert. Give a daring twist to the classic chocolate mousse by using avocado as its base.

Ingredients:

  • 2 ripe avocados
  • 3/4 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 3/4 cup powdered erythritol (or preferred keto-friendly sweetener)
  • 3/4 cup heavy cream
  • 1/4 teaspoon pure vanilla extract

Instructions:

  1. Scoop out the avocado flesh and place it in a food processor. Add the cocoa powder and erythritol, blending until smooth.
  2. In a separate bowl, whip the heavy cream and vanilla extract together until it forms soft peaks.
  3. Gently fold the whipped cream into the chocolate avocado mixture until well combined.
  4. Chill for at least 2 hours, or overnight, before serving for the best consistency.

Simple yet elegant, these recipes make Keto dining a heart-warming and mouth-watering experience. Comfort, love, and delectable dishes all-suite well with these romantic low-carb recipes. It’s time to heat up the night with Keto in Love. Happy Valentine’s Day!
