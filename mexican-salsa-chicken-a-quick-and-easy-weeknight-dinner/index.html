Mexican Salsa Chicken: A Quick and Easy Weeknight Dinner


Are you looking for a delicious and hassle-free dinner idea for those busy weeknights? Look no further than this mouthwatering Mexican Salsa Chicken recipe! Packed with flavor and easy to prepare, this dish is sure to become a family favorite in no time.

Ingredients

  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1 cup of your favorite salsa
  • 1 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon chili powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon onion powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh cilantro for garnish
  • Cooked rice or tortillas for serving

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. In a bowl, mix the salsa, ground cumin, chili powder, garlic powder, and onion powder.
  3. Place the chicken breasts in a baking dish and season them with salt and pepper.
  4. Pour the salsa mixture over the chicken, ensuring that each breast is evenly coated.
  5. Cover the baking dish with foil and bake for 25-30 minutes, or until the chicken is cooked through.
  6. Remove the foil and sprinkle the shredded cheddar cheese over the chicken.
  7. Return the baking dish to the oven and bake for an additional 3-5 minutes, or until the cheese is melted and bubbly.
  8. Once the chicken is done, garnish with fresh cilantro and serve with your choice of cooked rice or warm tortillas.

Why You’ll Love This Recipe

Quick and Easy Preparation

This Mexican Salsa Chicken recipe is perfect for those hectic weeknights when you need a meal that can be prepared with minimal effort. With just a few simple steps, you can have a flavorful and satisfying dinner on the table in no time.

Bursting with Flavor

The combination of salsa, cumin, and chili powder gives this dish a bold and zesty flavor that will tantalize your taste buds. The melted cheddar cheese adds a creamy and indulgent touch, making every bite a delight.

Versatile Serving Options

Whether you prefer to enjoy your Mexican Salsa Chicken over a bed of fluffy rice or wrapped in warm tortillas, this recipe offers versatility in serving options. You can even customize it with your favorite toppings such as avocado, sour cream, or lime wedges.

Family-Friendly Appeal

This dish is a hit with both kids and adults, making it an ideal choice for family dinners. The familiar flavors of salsa and cheese make it a comforting and crowd-pleasing meal that everyone will love.

Healthier Alternative

While being incredibly flavorful, this recipe is also a healthier alternative to heavy, calorie-laden meals. By using lean chicken breast and fresh salsa, you can indulge in a satisfying dinner without sacrificing your health goals.

In Conclusion

Mexican Salsa Chicken is a fantastic choice for those looking to infuse some excitement into their weeknight dinner routine. With its simple preparation, vibrant flavors, and adaptability, it’s a recipe that checks all the boxes for a convenient and satisfying meal. Add this recipe to your repertoire, and get ready to impress your loved ones with a dinner that’s as effortless as it is delicious!
