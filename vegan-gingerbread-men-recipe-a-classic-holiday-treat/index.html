Vegan Gingerbread Men Recipe: A Classic Holiday Treat


The holiday season is here, and what better way to celebrate than with a batch of delicious vegan gingerbread men? These classic holiday treats are loved by both kids and adults alike, and this vegan version is sure to impress everyone at your next holiday gathering. Made with wholesome ingredients and a delightful blend of spices, these gingerbread men are not only tasty but also cruelty-free. So let’s get baking!

Ingredients

  • 2 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup brown sugar
  • 1/4 cup molasses
  • 1/4 cup maple syrup
  • 1/2 cup coconut oil, melted
  • 1 teaspoon ground ginger
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground nutmeg
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cloves
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C) and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.

  2. In a large mixing bowl, combine the flour, brown sugar, ginger, cinnamon, nutmeg, cloves, baking soda, and salt. Mix well until all the dry ingredients are evenly incorporated.

  3. In a separate bowl, whisk together the molasses, maple syrup, and melted coconut oil. Make sure the coconut oil is not too hot, as it may affect the consistency of the dough.

  4. Pour the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients and stir until a soft dough forms. If the dough seems too dry, add a tablespoon of water at a time until it comes together.

  5. Transfer the dough onto a lightly floured surface and knead it a few times to ensure it’s well combined. Flatten the dough into a disc and wrap it in plastic wrap. Place it in the refrigerator for at least 30 minutes to chill.

  6. Once the dough has chilled, remove it from the refrigerator and roll it out to about 1/4-inch thickness. Use gingerbread man-shaped cookie cutters to cut out shapes from the dough. Carefully transfer the cutouts onto the prepared baking sheet, placing them about 1 inch apart.

  7. Bake the gingerbread men in the preheated oven for 10-12 minutes or until they are lightly golden around the edges. Keep a close eye on them, as they can quickly go from golden to burnt.

  8. Once baked, remove the gingerbread men from the oven and let them cool on the baking sheet for a few minutes. Then transfer them to a wire rack to cool completely.

  9. Once the gingerbread men are completely cooled, you can get creative and decorate them with icing, sprinkles, or any other toppings of your choice. Let your imagination run wild and have fun making each gingerbread man unique.

Serving Suggestions

Serve these vegan gingerbread men as a delightful treat at your holiday parties, or wrap them up in festive packaging to give as homemade gifts. They also make a great activity for kids to decorate and enjoy with a warm cup of cocoa.

Final Thoughts

With their warm spices and adorable shapes, vegan gingerbread men are a must-have during the holiday season. This recipe allows you to enjoy this classic treat while staying true to your vegan lifestyle. So gather your ingredients, preheat that oven, and let the delicious aroma of gingerbread fill your home. Happy baking and happy holidays!
