Chocolate Cherry Cake: Bursting with Flavor


Indulge in the rich and decadent flavors of this Chocolate Cherry Cake that will leave you craving for more! This delightful dessert is a perfect combination of moist chocolate cake and juicy cherries, creating a burst of flavor in every bite. Whether you’re celebrating a special occasion or simply satisfying your sweet tooth, this recipe is sure to impress.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 ¾ cups granulated sugar
  • ¾ cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1 ½ teaspoons baking powder
  • 1 ½ teaspoons baking soda
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 cup buttermilk
  • ½ cup vegetable oil
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 1 cup boiling water
  • 1 can (21 ounces) cherry pie filling

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease and flour two 9-inch round cake pans.

  2. In a large mixing bowl, combine the flour, sugar, cocoa powder, baking powder, baking soda, and salt. Mix well until all the ingredients are evenly distributed.

  3. Add the eggs, buttermilk, vegetable oil, and vanilla extract to the dry ingredients. Using an electric mixer, beat the mixture on medium speed for about 2 minutes. The batter should be smooth and well combined.

  4. Gradually pour in the boiling water while continuing to mix the batter. The batter will become thin, but don’t worry, it’s supposed to be that way. The hot water helps to bring out the rich chocolate flavor and creates a moist cake.

  5. Divide the batter equally between the prepared cake pans. Smooth the tops with a spatula to ensure an even surface.

  6. Bake in the preheated oven for approximately 30-35 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean. Rotate the pans halfway through baking to ensure even cooking.

  7. Remove the cakes from the oven and let them cool in the pans for about 10 minutes. Then, transfer them to a wire rack to cool completely.

  8. Once the cakes have cooled, carefully slice each cake horizontally into two layers. This will give you a total of four cake layers.

  9. Spread a generous amount of cherry pie filling on top of one cake layer. Place another cake layer on top and repeat the process until all the layers are stacked.

  10. Finally, frost the entire cake with your favorite chocolate frosting, making sure to cover the sides and top completely.

  11. For a finishing touch, you can decorate the cake with some chocolate shavings or fresh cherries.

  12. Serve and enjoy this luscious Chocolate Cherry Cake with your loved ones!

Tips and Variations

  • If you don’t have buttermilk, you can easily make a substitute by adding 1 tablespoon of lemon juice or vinegar to 1 cup of milk. Let it sit for 5 minutes before using.

  • For an extra burst of flavor, you can add a splash of cherry liqueur to the cherry pie filling before spreading it on the cake layers.

  • If you prefer a lighter frosting, consider using a whipped cream or cream cheese frosting instead of a traditional chocolate frosting.

  • This cake can be stored in an airtight container at room temperature for up to 3 days. Refrigerate if you need it to last longer.

  • Feel free to experiment with different fruit fillings. Raspberry or strawberry would make delicious alternatives to the cherry filling.

Final Thoughts

Indulge in the irresistible combination of chocolate and cherries with this Chocolate Cherry Cake recipe. The moist and tender cake layers, paired with the sweet and tangy cherry filling, create a burst of flavor that will leave your taste buds dancing. Whether you’re serving it at a party or enjoying it as a special treat, this cake is sure to impress. So, gather your ingredients and get ready to experience pure chocolate cherry bliss!
