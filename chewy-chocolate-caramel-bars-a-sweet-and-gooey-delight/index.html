Chewy Chocolate Caramel Bars: A Sweet and Gooey Delight


If you’re a fan of the delightful combination of chocolate and caramel, then you’re in for a treat with this chewy chocolate caramel bars recipe. These decadent bars strike the perfect balance between rich, gooey caramel and indulgent chocolate, making them an irresistible dessert or snack for any occasion. Whether you’re preparing them for a special celebration or simply craving a sweet indulgence, these chewy chocolate caramel bars are sure to satisfy your sweet tooth.

Ingredients

For the Crust:

  • 1 ½ cups all-purpose flour
  • ½ cup granulated sugar
  • ¾ cup unsalted butter, softened
  • A pinch of salt

For the Caramel Layer:

  • 1 can (14 ounces) sweetened condensed milk
  • 2 tablespoons unsalted butter
  • ½ cup light corn syrup
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

For the Chocolate Topping:

  • 1 ½ cups semi-sweet chocolate chips
  • 2 tablespoons unsalted butter
  • ½ teaspoon salt

Instructions

Step 1: Preparing the Crust

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C) and lightly grease a 9x13-inch baking pan.
  2. In a mixing bowl, combine the flour, sugar, softened butter, and a pinch of salt. Mix until the ingredients come together to form a crumbly dough.
  3. Press the dough evenly into the bottom of the prepared baking pan. Bake for 15-18 minutes or until the crust is lightly golden.

Step 2: Making the Caramel Layer

  1. In a medium saucepan, combine the sweetened condensed milk, butter, and corn syrup over medium heat. Stir continuously until the mixture is smooth and well combined.
  2. Bring the mixture to a gentle boil, stirring constantly. Reduce the heat to low and continue to cook, stirring constantly, for about 5-7 minutes until the caramel thickens and turns a light golden brown.
  3. Remove the saucepan from the heat and stir in the vanilla extract. Pour the caramel evenly over the baked crust. Allow it to cool and set while you prepare the chocolate topping.

Step 3: Adding the Chocolate Topping

  1. In a microwave-safe bowl, combine the chocolate chips and butter. Microwave in 30-second intervals, stirring between each, until the chocolate is melted and smooth.
  2. Pour the melted chocolate over the cooled caramel layer, spreading it evenly with a spatula.
  3. Sprinkle a pinch of salt over the chocolate layer for a delightful contrast in flavor.
  4. Allow the bars to cool at room temperature for about 1 hour, then transfer the pan to the refrigerator to chill for an additional 2 hours or until the bars are set.

Serving and Storing

Once the chewy chocolate caramel bars are fully set, use a sharp knife to cut them into squares or bars of your desired size. These delectable treats can be stored in an airtight container at room temperature for up to 5 days, or in the refrigerator for a longer shelf life.

Whether you’re enjoying them with a hot cup of coffee, sharing them at a gathering, or simply treating yourself to a little sweetness, these chewy chocolate caramel bars are guaranteed to make any moment a little more delightful. Indulge in the rich flavors and irresistible texture of these gooey bars, and savor the sweet magic of chocolate and caramel in every bite.

So, go ahead and whip up a batch of these chewy chocolate caramel bars to share with your loved ones or keep all to yourself. With their perfect blend of chewy, gooey, and chocolatey goodness, they’re bound to become a favorite in your dessert repertoire.
