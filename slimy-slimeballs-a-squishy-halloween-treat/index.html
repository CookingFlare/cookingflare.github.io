Slimy Slimeballs: A Squishy Halloween Treat


Halloween is the perfect time to unleash your creativity and surprise your friends and family with some delightfully spooky treats. This year, why not give yourself a slimy challenge and make some Slimy Slimeballs? These little gummy creatures are easy to make, and they’ll have your guests squirming with excitement! Just follow the simple recipe below.

Ingredients:

  • ½ cup of sugar
  • ⅓ cup of water
  • 1 teaspoon of liquid nitrogen or dry ice
  • 4 oz of high-quality gelatin
  • 2 tablespoons of light corn syrup
  • 1 tablespoon of unflavored gelatin
  • Food color of your choice
  • White sprinkles or sugar pearls (for decorating)

Instructions:

  1. Grease a baking tray with a non-stick cooking spray and prepare a flat workspace with parchment paper. Set it aside for now.

  2. In a saucepan, combine sugar and water over medium heat. Stir gently until the sugar dissolves completely.

  3. Now, it’s time to add the liquid nitrogen or dry ice into the mix. This will give your slimeballs a spooky fizz and a fun presentation. Be cautious while handling the dry ice, as it can cause freezing burns if touched directly.

  4. Once the dry ice has fully dissolved, add in the 4 oz of high-quality gelatin and stir until it’s fully combined. Allow the mixture to come to a near boil and then reduce the heat to low, stirring constantly.

  5. Prepare your food color by mixing it well with a small amount of the corn syrup until it’s well combined. Now, pour this colored mixture into the saucepan, stirring continuously until it reaches a uniform color.

  6. While the mixture is still warm, add in the additional tablespoon of unflavored gelatin and mix well.

  7. At this point, pour the gelatin mixture onto your parchment paper-lined workspace and allow it to cool without stirring. As it cools, you’ll notice it begins to morph into a goopy texture, resembling slimy alien goo!

  8. Once the gelatin has reached a malleable consistency, grab a handful and start shaping it into small, smooth balls—about the size of a gummy bears. Remember to grease your hands with non-stick cooking spray to prevent sticking.

  9. Gently roll each slimeball in white sprinkles or sugar pearls, making sure to cover every inch, so that your creepy creatures appear as if they’re covered in frost.

  10. Allow the Slimy Slimeballs to set in the refrigerator for at least an hour or until fully hardened.

  11. When they are firm, it’s time to make them as spooky as possible. Poke two toothpicks or skewers through each slimeball, making them appear to have tiny tentacles. Alternatively, you can use small black or green licorice strings to create even more haunting tentacles.

  12. Store your Slimy Slimeballs in an airtight container in the fridge, and enjoy them within a week for the best taste and texture.

With these Slimy Slimeballs, you’ll be providing a treat that will both surprise and delight your guests. The eerie fizz, paired with their creepy appearance, will make them the perfect addition to any Halloween party. Enjoy getting creative and having fun in the kitchen as you craft your very own slimy zombies, sea creatures, or alien slime monsters!

Do not forget the endless possibilities that these Slimy Slimeballs offer. Will you go for an underwater scene with different marine life critters? Or perhaps an alien invasion theme with little green slime creatures and glowing eyeball candies? The choice is yours! Happy Haunting!
