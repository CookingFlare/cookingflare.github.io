Yummy Keto Creamy Garlic Parmesan Chicken Recipe


Are you looking for a delectable keto-friendly dish that will tantalize your taste buds while keeping you on track with your low-carb lifestyle? Look no further than this mouthwatering Yummy Keto Creamy Garlic Parmesan Chicken recipe. Packed with flavor and easy to make, this dish is sure to become a staple in your weekly meal rotation.

Ingredients

  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup chicken broth
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1/2 cup grated parmesan cheese
  • 1 teaspoon Italian seasoning
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley for garnish

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Chicken: Start by seasoning the chicken breasts with salt and pepper on both sides. In a large skillet over medium-high heat, heat the olive oil. Add the chicken breasts and cook for 6-7 minutes on each side, or until they are golden brown and cooked through. Remove the chicken from the skillet and set aside.

  2. Make the Sauce: In the same skillet, add the minced garlic and sauté for about 1 minute, or until fragrant. Pour in the chicken broth and let it simmer for 5 minutes, allowing the flavors to meld together. Reduce the heat to medium-low and add the heavy cream, parmesan cheese, and Italian seasoning. Stir the sauce constantly until the cheese is melted and the sauce has thickened to your desired consistency.

  3. Combine and Serve: Return the chicken to the skillet, spooning the creamy garlic parmesan sauce over the top. Let the chicken simmer in the sauce for a few minutes to soak up all the flavors. Garnish with fresh parsley and serve hot.

Nutritional Information

  • Calories: 380
  • Fat: 25g
  • Protein: 32g
  • Carbohydrates: 3g
  • Fiber: 0g
  • Net Carbs: 3g

Why You’ll Love This Dish

This Yummy Keto Creamy Garlic Parmesan Chicken recipe is not only low in carbs, but it’s also incredibly rich and satisfying. The creamy garlic parmesan sauce is velvety smooth and bursting with flavor, elevating simple chicken breasts to a whole new level. Plus, with only 3g of net carbs per serving, you can indulge in this decadent dish without any guilt.

Tips and Variations

  • For a twist, try adding some chopped spinach or sun-dried tomatoes to the sauce for an extra burst of color and flavor.
  • If you’re short on time, you can use pre-cooked rotisserie chicken and simply prepare the sauce to pour over the chicken.
  • Serve this dish with a side of steamed veggies or a crisp green salad for a well-rounded keto meal.

In Conclusion

This Yummy Keto Creamy Garlic Parmesan Chicken recipe is a true winner for anyone following a keto lifestyle or simply looking for a delicious, low-carb meal option. It’s quick, easy, and sure to impress even the pickiest of eaters. So, go ahead and whip up this delightful dish for your next dinner – your taste buds will thank you!

With its creamy garlic parmesan sauce and tender chicken, this recipe is a keto dream come true. Whether you’re new to the ketogenic diet or a seasoned pro, this dish is a must-try. Enjoy!
