Nutritious Low Carb Garlic Parmesan Crispy Chicken


If you’re looking for a delicious and nutritious low-carb meal that’s easy to prepare and bursting with flavor, then you’re in for a treat! This Nutritious Low Carb Garlic Parmesan Crispy Chicken recipe is a perfect choice for those seeking a healthier alternative to traditional fried chicken. Packed with protein, low in carbohydrates, and loaded with the savory goodness of garlic and parmesan, this dish is sure to become a family favorite. Let’s dive right into the recipe!

Ingredients

  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1 cup almond flour
  • 1/2 cup grated parmesan cheese
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon onion powder
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • Fresh parsley for garnish

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C) and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.

  2. In a shallow bowl, whisk together the almond flour, grated parmesan cheese, garlic powder, onion powder, paprika, salt, and black pepper. This flavorful mixture will be the coating for your crispy chicken.

  3. In a separate bowl, beat the eggs until well combined.

  4. Dip each chicken breast into the beaten eggs, allowing any excess to drip off, then coat the chicken in the almond flour and parmesan mixture, pressing gently to adhere the coating to the chicken.

  5. Place the coated chicken breasts onto the prepared baking sheet, and drizzle with olive oil to help achieve a golden, crispy exterior.

  6. Bake the chicken in the preheated oven for 25-30 minutes, or until the chicken is cooked through and the coating is golden brown and crispy.

  7. Once cooked, remove the chicken from the oven and allow it to rest for a few minutes before serving.

  8. Garnish with fresh parsley for a pop of color and added freshness.

Serving Suggestions

This Nutritious Low Carb Garlic Parmesan Crispy Chicken pairs perfectly with a side of steamed vegetables, a crisp green salad, or cauliflower rice for a complete and satisfying meal. For an extra burst of flavor, drizzle the chicken with a squeeze of fresh lemon juice before serving.

Nutritional Information

Each serving of this delicious Garlic Parmesan Crispy Chicken is packed with protein and flavor while being low in carbohydrates. This makes it an ideal choice for those following a low-carb or keto lifestyle. The almond flour and parmesan cheese coating provides a crunchy texture without the need for traditional breadcrumbs, making this dish gluten-free as well.

Final Thoughts

With its irresistible combination of garlic, parmesan, and crispy chicken, this recipe is a fantastic way to enjoy a comforting and satisfying meal without compromising on nutrition. Whether you’re cooking for a family dinner or preparing a special meal for yourself, this Nutritious Low Carb Garlic Parmesan Crispy Chicken is a must-try. So, roll up your sleeves, gather your ingredients, and get ready to savor every bite of this delightful dish!

This recipe is a testament to the fact that healthy eating can be both delicious and enjoyable. So, why not treat yourself to this flavorful and nutritious low-carb meal today? Your taste buds will thank you!
