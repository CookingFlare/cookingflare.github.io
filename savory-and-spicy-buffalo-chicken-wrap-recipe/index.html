Savory and Spicy: Buffalo Chicken Wrap Recipe


If you’re a fan of chicken and crave something with a kick, then this Savory and Spicy Buffalo Chicken Wrap recipe is just what you need! Packed with bold flavors and a perfect balance of heat, this wrap is sure to satisfy your taste buds. Whether you’re hosting a game night, throwing a party, or simply looking for a delicious lunch option, this recipe will not disappoint. So let’s dive right in and get cooking!

Ingredients

For the Buffalo Chicken:

  • 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1/4 cup hot sauce
  • 2 tablespoons melted butter
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon onion powder
  • Salt and pepper, to taste

For the Wrap:

  • 4 large flour tortillas
  • 1 cup shredded lettuce
  • 1 cup diced tomatoes
  • 1/2 cup diced celery
  • 1/4 cup blue cheese dressing
  • 1/4 cup ranch dressing
  • Fresh cilantro, for garnish (optional)

Instructions

  1. Preheat your grill or stovetop grill pan over medium heat. Season the chicken breasts with salt, pepper, garlic powder, and onion powder.
  2. In a small bowl, mix together the hot sauce and melted butter. Brush the chicken breasts with the buffalo sauce mixture, making sure to coat them evenly.
  3. Grill the chicken breasts for about 6-8 minutes per side, or until cooked through and no longer pink in the center. Remove from heat and let them rest for a few minutes.
  4. While the chicken is resting, prepare the wrap ingredients. In a medium bowl, combine the shredded lettuce, diced tomatoes, and diced celery. Toss to mix well.
  5. Once the chicken has rested, slice it into thin strips.
  6. To assemble the wraps, place a tortilla flat on a clean surface. Spread a tablespoon each of blue cheese dressing and ranch dressing onto the tortilla.
  7. Add a handful of the lettuce, tomato, and celery mixture onto the tortilla, followed by a generous amount of the sliced buffalo chicken.
  8. If desired, top with fresh cilantro for an extra burst of flavor.
  9. Roll the tortilla tightly, tucking in the sides as you go, to form a wrap. Repeat with the remaining ingredients.
  10. Serve the Buffalo Chicken Wraps immediately, or wrap them tightly in foil and refrigerate until ready to serve.

Tips and Variations

  • For an extra spicy kick, add a few dashes of hot sauce to the wrap before rolling it up.
  • If you prefer a milder flavor, reduce the amount of hot sauce in the buffalo chicken marinade.
  • Feel free to customize your wraps by adding your favorite ingredients such as avocado slices, diced red onions, or shredded cheese.
  • If you’re in a rush, you can use pre-cooked rotisserie chicken and simply toss it in the buffalo sauce before assembling the wrap.
  • Serve these wraps with a side of crispy french fries or fresh carrot sticks for a complete meal.

Now that you have this delicious recipe in your arsenal, it’s time to gather your ingredients and get cooking! These Savory and Spicy Buffalo Chicken Wraps are perfect for any occasion and are sure to impress your family and friends. So go ahead, indulge in the bold flavors and enjoy the satisfying crunch of this mouthwatering wrap. Happy cooking!
