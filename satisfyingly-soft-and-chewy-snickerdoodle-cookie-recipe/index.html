Satisfyingly Soft and Chewy Snickerdoodle Cookie Recipe

3 min · 496 words

Are you ready to indulge in a delightful treat that will satisfy your sweet tooth? Look no further than this incredibly delicious Snickerdoodle Cookie recipe. These cookies are known for their soft and chewy texture, and they are perfect for any occasion. Whether you’re hosting a party or simply craving something sweet, these Snickerdoodle Cookies will never disappoint.

Ingredients

To create these mouthwatering cookies, you will need the following ingredients:

  • 1 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1 1/2 cups granulated sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 3/4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 teaspoons cream of tartar
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 3 tablespoons granulated sugar (for rolling)
  • 1 tablespoon ground cinnamon (for rolling)

Instructions

Now let’s dive into the step-by-step process of making these incredible Snickerdoodle Cookies:

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C) and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.

  2. In a large mixing bowl, cream together the softened butter and granulated sugar until light and fluffy. This can be done using an electric mixer or by hand.

  3. Add the eggs, one at a time, to the butter-sugar mixture, beating well after each addition. Make sure the eggs are fully incorporated before moving on to the next step.

  4. In a separate bowl, whisk together the flour, cream of tartar, baking soda, and salt. Gradually add this dry mixture to the wet mixture, mixing well after each addition. The dough should be soft and slightly sticky.

  5. In a small bowl, combine the 3 tablespoons of granulated sugar and the ground cinnamon for rolling the cookies.

  6. Using a cookie scoop or a tablespoon, scoop out portions of dough and roll them into balls. Roll each ball of dough in the cinnamon-sugar mixture until fully coated.

  7. Place the coated dough balls onto the prepared baking sheet, leaving enough space between them for spreading. Flatten each ball slightly with the bottom of a glass.

  8. Bake the cookies for 10-12 minutes, or until the edges are golden brown. The centers may still appear slightly undercooked, but they will set as the cookies cool.

  9. Remove the baking sheet from the oven and let the cookies cool for a few minutes before transferring them to a wire rack to cool completely.

  10. Once the cookies are completely cooled, store them in an airtight container to maintain their soft and chewy texture.

Enjoy Every Bite!

Congratulations! You have successfully created a batch of irresistibly soft and chewy Snickerdoodle Cookies. Now it’s time to sit back, relax, and enjoy these delightful treats. Whether you’re enjoying them with a glass of milk or sharing them with friends and family, these cookies are guaranteed to bring a smile to everyone’s face.

Remember, the key to achieving that perfect soft and chewy texture is in the cream of tartar, so don’t skip this essential ingredient. With their cinnamon-sugar coating and melt-in-your-mouth consistency, Snickerdoodle Cookies are a classic favorite that never goes out of style.

So, what are you waiting for? Gather your ingredients, preheat your oven, and let the baking adventure begin. Indulge in the sweetness and enjoy every bite of these satisfyingly soft and chewy Snickerdoodle Cookies!

Happy baking!
