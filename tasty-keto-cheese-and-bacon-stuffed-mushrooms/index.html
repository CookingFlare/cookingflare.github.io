Tasty Keto Cheese and Bacon Stuffed Mushrooms


If you’re looking for a delicious and satisfying keto-friendly appetizer or snack, these tasty cheese and bacon stuffed mushrooms are the perfect choice. Packed with flavor and low in carbs, they’re sure to be a hit at your next gathering or as a savory treat for yourself. Let’s dive into the recipe and explore how to make these mouthwatering stuffed mushrooms.

Ingredients

  • 12 large mushrooms, stems removed
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1/2 small onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 4 slices bacon, cooked and crumbled
  • 4 ounces cream cheese, softened
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/4 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/4 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1/4 teaspoon dried thyme
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley for garnish

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C). Line a baking sheet with parchment paper and set aside.

  2. Clean the mushrooms with a damp paper towel to remove any dirt. Gently remove the stems and set aside. Place the mushroom caps on the prepared baking sheet.

  3. In a skillet, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the chopped onion and sauté until translucent, about 3-4 minutes. Add the minced garlic and cook for an additional minute.

  4. In a mixing bowl, combine the sautéed onions and garlic, crumbled bacon, softened cream cheese, Parmesan cheese, mozzarella cheese, dried oregano, dried thyme, salt, and pepper. Mix until well combined.

  5. Spoon the cheese and bacon mixture into the mushroom caps, filling each one generously.

  6. Bake the stuffed mushrooms in the preheated oven for 20-25 minutes, or until the cheese is melted and bubbly, and the mushrooms are tender.

  7. Once done, remove the stuffed mushrooms from the oven and let them cool for a few minutes. Garnish with fresh parsley before serving.

Serving Suggestions

These keto cheese and bacon stuffed mushrooms are incredibly versatile and can be served in a variety of ways. They make a fantastic appetizer for parties and gatherings, or even as a flavorful side dish for a special dinner. Pair them with a crisp green salad for a light and satisfying meal. Additionally, they can be enjoyed as a delicious snack on their own, perfect for satisfying those savory cravings.

Nutritional Information

Per serving (2 stuffed mushrooms):

  • Calories: 180
  • Total Fat: 14g
  • Saturated Fat: 7g
  • Cholesterol: 35mg
  • Sodium: 280mg
  • Total Carbohydrates: 4g
  • Dietary Fiber: 1g
  • Sugars: 2g
  • Protein: 9g

Conclusion

These tasty keto cheese and bacon stuffed mushrooms are a wonderful addition to any keto-friendly recipe collection. They are easy to make, bursting with flavor, and offer a satisfying combination of creamy cheese and savory bacon. Whether you’re following a keto diet or simply looking for a delicious appetizer or snack, these stuffed mushrooms are sure to impress. So, give this recipe a try and indulge in the delightful flavors of these delectable stuffed mushrooms. Enjoy!
