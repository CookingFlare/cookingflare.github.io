Thai Basil Beef Stir Fry: A Fragrant and Spicy Thai Delight


When it comes to Thai cuisine, one dish that never fails to impress is the Thai Basil Beef Stir Fry. Bursting with fragrant herbs, bold spices, and tender strips of beef, this dish is a delightful symphony of flavors that will transport your taste buds to the bustling streets of Thailand. If you’re a fan of Thai food or simply looking to spice up your weeknight dinner routine, this recipe is a must-try.

Ingredients

To recreate the magic of this Thai Basil Beef Stir Fry in your own kitchen, you will need the following ingredients:

  • 500 grams of beef (such as sirloin or flank steak), thinly sliced
  • 2 tablespoons of vegetable oil
  • 4 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 2 Thai bird’s eye chilies, finely chopped (adjust according to your spice preference)
  • 1 bell pepper, thinly sliced
  • 1 onion, thinly sliced
  • 1 cup of fresh Thai basil leaves
  • 2 tablespoons of oyster sauce
  • 1 tablespoon of soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon of fish sauce
  • 1 teaspoon of sugar
  • Freshly ground black pepper, to taste

Instructions

Now that you have all the ingredients ready, let’s dive into the cooking process:

  1. Heat the vegetable oil in a large wok or skillet over medium-high heat. Once the oil is hot, add the minced garlic and chopped Thai bird’s eye chilies. Stir-fry for about 30 seconds until fragrant.

  2. Add the sliced beef to the wok and stir-fry for 2-3 minutes until the beef is browned and cooked to your desired doneness. Remove the beef from the wok and set it aside on a plate.

  3. In the same wok, add the sliced bell pepper and onion. Stir-fry for 2-3 minutes until the vegetables are slightly softened.

  4. Return the cooked beef to the wok and toss it with the vegetables.

  5. In a small bowl, mix together the oyster sauce, soy sauce, fish sauce, and sugar. Pour this sauce mixture over the beef and vegetables in the wok. Stir-fry everything together for another minute or two, ensuring that the sauce coats the beef and vegetables evenly.

  6. Finally, add the fresh Thai basil leaves to the wok and toss everything together for another 30 seconds until the basil leaves wilt slightly.

  7. Season the stir fry with freshly ground black pepper to taste.

  8. Remove the wok from the heat and serve the Thai Basil Beef Stir Fry immediately over steamed jasmine rice or noodles. Garnish with extra Thai basil leaves for an added touch of freshness.

Tips and Variations

  • For an extra kick of heat, you can add more Thai bird’s eye chilies or even include some sliced red chili peppers.
  • If you’re not a fan of beef, you can easily substitute it with chicken, pork, or even tofu for a vegetarian option.
  • Feel free to add other vegetables of your choice, such as carrots, broccoli, or baby corn, to make the stir fry more colorful and nutritious.

Enjoy the Thai Flavors

There you have it – a simple yet mouthwatering recipe for Thai Basil Beef Stir Fry. The combination of aromatic basil, savory beef, and spicy chilies is sure to tantalize your taste buds and leave you craving more. This dish is perfect for a quick weeknight dinner or for impressing guests at a dinner party. So, gather your ingredients, fire up your wok, and embark on a culinary journey to Thailand. Happy cooking!
