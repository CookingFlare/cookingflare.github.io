A Greek Twist on Brunch: How to Make Greek Breakfast Pie


If you’re looking to add a touch of Mediterranean flavor to your brunch menu, then Greek breakfast pie is the perfect dish to try. This savory pie is packed with the vibrant flavors of Greece and makes for a delightful addition to any brunch spread. With a flaky crust, a rich filling, and the classic combination of feta cheese, spinach, and herbs, this Greek breakfast pie is sure to be a hit with your family and friends. So, let’s dive into the recipe and learn how to bring a Greek twist to your brunch table!

Ingredients

For the Crust:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup olive oil
  • 1/2 cup Greek yogurt
  • 1 teaspoon salt

For the Filling:

  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 4 cups fresh spinach, chopped
  • 1/2 cup fresh dill, chopped
  • 1/2 cup fresh parsley, chopped
  • 1/2 cup crumbled feta cheese
  • 4 eggs
  • 1 cup milk
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Crust:

    • In a large bowl, combine the flour and salt. Add the olive oil and Greek yogurt, and mix until a dough forms. If the dough is too sticky, add a little more flour.
    • Divide the dough into two equal portions. Roll out one portion to fit the bottom of a greased pie dish.

  2. Make the Filling:

    • Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
    • In a skillet, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the chopped onion and garlic, and sauté until softened.
    • Add the chopped spinach to the skillet and cook until wilted. Remove from heat and let it cool slightly.
    • In a separate bowl, whisk together the eggs and milk. Add the chopped dill, parsley, and crumbled feta cheese. Season with salt and pepper.
    • Spread the spinach mixture evenly over the prepared pie crust in the pie dish. Pour the egg mixture over the spinach.

  3. Assemble and Bake:

    • Roll out the second portion of the dough to fit the top of the pie. Place it over the filling and crimp the edges to seal the pie.
    • Cut a few slits in the top crust to allow steam to escape.
    • Bake the pie in the preheated oven for 40-45 minutes, or until the crust is golden brown and the filling is set.
    • Allow the pie to cool for a few minutes before slicing and serving.

Serving Suggestions

This Greek breakfast pie is best served warm or at room temperature. It pairs beautifully with a refreshing Greek salad, some olives, and a dollop of tzatziki on the side. You can also serve it alongside some fresh fruit and a basket of warm, crusty bread. This pie is a versatile dish that can be enjoyed for breakfast, brunch, or even a light lunch.

Conclusion

With its flaky crust, flavorful filling, and a hint of Mediterranean charm, Greek breakfast pie is a delightful dish that will elevate your brunch experience. Whether you’re hosting a gathering or simply treating yourself to a special weekend meal, this recipe is a wonderful way to infuse your brunch table with the vibrant flavors of Greece. So, gather your ingredients, roll up your sleeves, and get ready to savor the deliciousness of Greek breakfast pie!

Now that you have the recipe in hand, it’s time to bring a bit of Greece to your brunch table. Enjoy the process of creating this delightful dish and sharing it with your loved ones. Happy cooking!
