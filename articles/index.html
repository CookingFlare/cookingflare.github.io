The key to avoiding this problem is proper storage. After purchasing your greens, make sure to store them in the refrigerator, ideally in a perforated plastic bag to maintain moisture without suffocating the leaves. Additionally, consider investing in a salad spinner to ensure that your greens are thoroughly dry before assembling your salad.
