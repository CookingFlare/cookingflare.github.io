Artichoke and Olive Pizza: A Mediterranean Twist


If you’re a pizza lover looking for a unique and flavorful twist, then this Artichoke and Olive Pizza is the perfect choice for you. Inspired by the vibrant flavors of the Mediterranean, this pizza combines the tanginess of artichokes, the brininess of olives, and the freshness of herbs to create a truly delightful culinary experience.

Ingredients

To make this mouthwatering Artichoke and Olive Pizza, you will need the following ingredients:

  • 1 pre-made pizza dough (homemade or store-bought)
  • 1 cup marinara sauce
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1 cup artichoke hearts, drained and chopped
  • 1/2 cup Kalamata olives, pitted and halved
  • 1/4 cup red onion, thinly sliced
  • 2 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • Fresh basil leaves, for garnish
  • Salt and pepper, to taste

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 450°F (230°C). If you have a pizza stone, place it in the oven while preheating to ensure a crispy crust.

  2. Roll out the pizza dough on a lightly floured surface to your desired thickness. If you prefer a thin crust, roll it out thinner. Transfer the dough to a parchment-lined baking sheet or pizza peel if using a pizza stone.

  3. In a small bowl, mix together the minced garlic and olive oil. Brush the garlic-infused olive oil evenly over the surface of the pizza dough, leaving a small border around the edges for the crust.

  4. Spread the marinara sauce over the oiled dough, ensuring an even distribution. Sprinkle the shredded mozzarella cheese over the sauce.

  5. Now it’s time to add the star ingredients - artichoke hearts and Kalamata olives. Scatter the chopped artichoke hearts and olives evenly across the pizza.

  6. Next, add the thinly sliced red onion for an extra touch of flavor and crunch.

  7. Sprinkle dried oregano over the entire pizza, and season with salt and pepper to taste.

  8. Carefully transfer the pizza to the preheated oven and bake for approximately 12-15 minutes, or until the crust is golden brown and the cheese is melted and bubbly.

  9. Remove the pizza from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes. Garnish with fresh basil leaves for a fragrant and vibrant finish.

  10. Slice and serve your Artichoke and Olive Pizza while it’s still warm and enjoy the burst of Mediterranean flavors with each bite.

A Flavorful Journey to the Mediterranean

The Artichoke and Olive Pizza brings together the best flavors of the Mediterranean in one delicious bite. The tangy artichoke hearts, briny Kalamata olives, and aromatic herbs create a symphony of flavors that will transport your taste buds to the sun-drenched shores of the Mediterranean.

The combination of artichokes and olives not only adds depth and complexity to this pizza but also provides an array of health benefits. Artichokes are rich in antioxidants, fiber, and vitamins, while olives are packed with heart-healthy monounsaturated fats.

Whether you’re hosting a pizza night with friends or simply craving a creative dinner option, this Artichoke and Olive Pizza is sure to impress. Its unique blend of flavors and the simplicity of the recipe make it an easy yet impressive choice for any occasion.

So, why not take a culinary trip to the Mediterranean tonight and indulge in the delightful flavors of this Artichoke and Olive Pizza? Your taste buds will thank you!
