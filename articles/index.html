Balsamic Berry Salad with a Sweet Surprise

Are you ready to elevate your salad game with a burst of fruity flavors and a sweet surprise? Look no further than this delightful Balsamic Berry Salad with a Sweet Surprise. This refreshing and vibrant salad is the perfect combination of tangy balsamic dressing, fresh berries, and a surprising hint of sweetness that will leave your taste buds tingling with delight. Ingredients For the Salad: 6 cups mixed greens (such as spinach, arugula, and baby kale) 1 cup fresh strawberries, sliced 1 cup fresh blueberries 1/2 cup raspberries 1/4 cup chopped walnuts 1/4 cup crumbled feta cheese For the Sweet Surprise: 1/2 cup granola 1/4 cup honey 1 teaspoon cinnamon For the Balsamic Dressing: 1/4 cup balsamic vinegar 1/2 cup extra virgin olive oil 1 tablespoon honey 1 teaspoon Dijon mustard Salt and pepper to taste Instructions Step 1: Prepare the Sweet Surprise In a small bowl, mix the granola, honey, and cinnamon until well combined. Spread the mixture onto a parchment-lined baking sheet and bake at 350°F for 10-12 minutes, or until golden and crisp. Allow it to cool. Step 2: Make the Balsamic Dressing In a mason jar or a small bowl, combine the balsamic vinegar, olive oil, honey, Dijon mustard, salt, and pepper. Shake or whisk the dressing until emulsified. Set aside. Step 3: Assemble the Salad In a large salad bowl, toss the mixed greens, strawberries, blueberries, and raspberries together. Drizzle the balsamic dressing over the salad and gently toss to coat the ingredients. Sprinkle the chopped walnuts and crumbled feta cheese over the top of the salad. Step 4: Add the Sweet Surprise Once the granola mixture has cooled, break it into small clusters. Sprinkle the sweet surprise over the assembled salad just before serving. Serving Suggestion This Balsamic Berry Salad with a Sweet Surprise is best enjoyed fresh, allowing the flavors to mingle and the sweet surprise to add a delightful crunch to each bite. Serve it as a light and refreshing lunch or as a stunning side dish alongside grilled chicken or fish. ...
