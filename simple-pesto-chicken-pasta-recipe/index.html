Simple Pesto Chicken Pasta Recipe


Pesto chicken pasta is a classic Italian dish that never fails to impress. The combination of tender chicken, flavorful pesto sauce, and al dente pasta is a match made in heaven. This simple recipe is perfect for a quick weeknight dinner or a special occasion. With just a few ingredients and simple steps, you can create a dish that’s sure to become a family favorite.

Ingredients

  • 8 oz pasta of your choice (linguine or fettuccine work well)
  • 1 lb boneless, skinless chicken breast, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 1/2 cup freshly made pesto sauce (see below for recipe)
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh basil leaves for garnish

Pesto Sauce Recipe

  • 1 cup fresh basil leaves
  • 1/3 cup pine nuts
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/2 cup extra virgin olive oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • Salt to taste

Instructions

  1. Cook the pasta: Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Cook the pasta according to the package instructions until al dente. Reserve 1 cup of pasta water before draining.
  2. Prepare the pesto sauce: In a food processor, combine the basil leaves, pine nuts, and garlic. Process until the mixture is well combined and slightly smooth. Add the Parmesan cheese and process until well combined. With the processor running, slowly pour in the olive oil through the top. Season with salt to taste.
  3. Cook the chicken: In a large skillet, heat 1 tablespoon of olive oil over medium-high heat. Add the chicken and cook until browned and cooked through, about 5-7 minutes. Remove from heat and set aside.
  4. Combine the pasta and pesto sauce: In a large serving bowl, combine the cooked pasta, pesto sauce, and reserved pasta water. Toss until the pasta is well coated with the sauce.
  5. Add the chicken and cherry tomatoes: Add the cooked chicken and cherry tomatoes to the pasta and pesto sauce. Toss until everything is well combined.
  6. Season and serve: Season with salt and pepper to taste. Garnish with fresh basil leaves and grated Parmesan cheese. Serve immediately.

Tips and Variations

  • Use leftover cooked chicken or turkey to make this recipe even quicker.
  • Add some heat to the dish by incorporating red pepper flakes into the pesto sauce.
  • Substitute the cherry tomatoes with other vegetables, such as bell peppers or zucchini, for added flavor and texture.
  • Make the pesto sauce ahead of time and store it in the refrigerator for up to 1 week.

Conclusion

This simple pesto chicken pasta recipe is a delightful combination of flavors and textures that’s sure to become a family favorite. With its rich and creamy pesto sauce, tender chicken, and al dente pasta, this dish is perfect for any occasion. Whether you’re looking for a quick weeknight dinner or a special occasion meal, this recipe is sure to impress. So go ahead, give it a try, and enjoy the delicious flavors of Italy in the comfort of your own home.
