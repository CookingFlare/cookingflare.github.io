Delightful One Pot Lemon Garlic Butter Salmon


Salmon is a versatile and delicious fish that can be prepared in a multitude of ways. If you’re looking for a quick and easy recipe that will impress your family and friends, then look no further. This one pot lemon garlic butter salmon recipe is not only incredibly tasty, but it’s also incredibly easy to make. With just a handful of ingredients, you can have a restaurant-quality meal on your table in no time.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound salmon fillets
  • 4 tablespoons unsalted butter
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 lemon, sliced
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • Fresh parsley, for garnish

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C).

  2. In a large oven-safe skillet, melt the butter over medium heat. Add the minced garlic and sauté for about a minute, until fragrant.

  3. Season the salmon fillets with salt and pepper on both sides. Place the fillets skin-side down in the skillet, on top of the melted butter and garlic.

  4. Lay the lemon slices on top of the salmon fillets, covering them completely.

  5. Transfer the skillet to the preheated oven and bake for 12-15 minutes, or until the salmon is cooked through and flakes easily with a fork.

  6. Once the salmon is done, remove it from the oven and let it rest for a few minutes.

  7. Garnish with fresh parsley and serve hot.

Why One Pot Lemon Garlic Butter Salmon?

This one pot lemon garlic butter salmon recipe is a winner for several reasons. Firstly, it’s incredibly easy to make. With just a few simple steps, you can have a delicious and nutritious meal ready in under 30 minutes. This makes it perfect for busy weeknights when you don’t have a lot of time to spend in the kitchen.

Secondly, the combination of lemon, garlic, and butter adds a burst of flavor to the salmon. The tanginess of the lemon, the richness of the butter, and the savory goodness of garlic create a harmonious blend of flavors that will leave your taste buds wanting more.

Lastly, cooking the salmon in one pot means less cleanup. By using an oven-safe skillet, you can sear the salmon on the stovetop and then transfer it directly to the oven to finish cooking. This not only saves you time but also reduces the number of dishes you have to wash afterward.

Serving Suggestions

This one pot lemon garlic butter salmon pairs well with a variety of side dishes. Here are a few suggestions to complete your meal:

  • Roasted Vegetables: Serve the salmon alongside a medley of roasted vegetables such as asparagus, Brussels sprouts, and cherry tomatoes.

  • Quinoa Salad: Whip up a refreshing quinoa salad with cucumber, cherry tomatoes, feta cheese, and a lemon vinaigrette.

  • Garlic Mashed Potatoes: For a heartier meal, serve the salmon with a side of creamy garlic mashed potatoes.

  • Steamed Broccoli: Keep it simple and healthy by serving the salmon with steamed broccoli and a squeeze of lemon juice.

Final Thoughts

One pot lemon garlic butter salmon is a simple yet elegant dish that is sure to impress. Whether you’re cooking for a special occasion or just want to treat yourself to a delicious meal, this recipe is a winner. So go ahead, give it a try, and let the flavors of lemon, garlic, and butter take your taste buds on a delightful journey. Enjoy!
