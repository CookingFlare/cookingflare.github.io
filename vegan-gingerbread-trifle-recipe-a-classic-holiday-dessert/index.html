Vegan Gingerbread Trifle Recipe: A Classic Holiday Dessert


The holiday season calls for indulging in delightful treats, and what better way to celebrate than with a classic Vegan Gingerbread Trifle? This decadent dessert is a perfect combination of rich flavors, warm spices, and creamy textures, making it a show-stopping centerpiece for any festive gathering. Whether you follow a vegan lifestyle or simply enjoy exploring new culinary horizons, this recipe is sure to become a cherished addition to your holiday traditions.

Ingredients

For the Gingerbread:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons ground ginger
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground cloves
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 3/4 cup unsweetened applesauce
  • 1/2 cup molasses
  • 1/2 cup brown sugar
  • 1/3 cup vegetable oil
  • 1/2 cup hot water

For the Vegan Custard:

  • 2 1/2 cups unsweetened almond milk
  • 1/4 cup cornstarch
  • 1/3 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground turmeric (for color)

For the Whipped Coconut Cream:

  • 2 cans full-fat coconut milk, chilled overnight
  • 2-3 tablespoons powdered sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

For Assembly:

  • 1/2 cup crystallized ginger, finely chopped
  • Vegan gingerbread cookies, crumbled (optional)
  • Ground cinnamon for dusting

Instructions

Making the Gingerbread:

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C) and lightly grease a 9x13-inch baking dish.
  2. In a large bowl, whisk together the flour, baking soda, ginger, cinnamon, cloves, and salt.
  3. In another bowl, mix the applesauce, molasses, brown sugar, and vegetable oil until well combined. Gradually add the dry ingredients to the wet ingredients, alternating with the hot water, and mix until smooth.
  4. Pour the batter into the prepared baking dish and bake for 30-35 minutes or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean. Allow the gingerbread to cool completely before cutting it into cubes.

Preparing the Vegan Custard:

  1. In a saucepan, whisk together the almond milk, cornstarch, and sugar over medium heat. Cook, stirring constantly, until the mixture thickens and coats the back of a spoon.
  2. Remove the saucepan from the heat and stir in the vanilla extract and ground turmeric. The turmeric will provide a lovely golden color to the custard. Allow the custard to cool to room temperature.

Whipping the Coconut Cream:

  1. Carefully open the chilled cans of coconut milk and scoop the thick, creamy part into a mixing bowl, leaving the watery part behind.
  2. Add the powdered sugar and vanilla extract to the coconut cream. Using a hand mixer, whip the cream until smooth and fluffy.

Assembling the Trifle:

  1. In a trifle dish or a large glass bowl, layer the gingerbread cubes, vegan custard, whipped coconut cream, and chopped crystallized ginger. Repeat the layers until the bowl is filled, ending with a layer of whipped coconut cream on top.
  2. If desired, sprinkle crumbled vegan gingerbread cookies between the layers for added texture and flavor.
  3. Cover the trifle with plastic wrap and refrigerate for at least 4 hours, or preferably overnight, to allow the flavors to meld together.
  4. Before serving, dust the top of the trifle with ground cinnamon for a festive touch.

Serving Suggestions

This Vegan Gingerbread Trifle is a stunning dessert that deserves to be showcased at your holiday table. To add an extra touch of elegance, consider garnishing each serving with a sprig of fresh mint or a sprinkle of edible gold dust. The combination of spicy gingerbread, luscious custard, and airy coconut cream is sure to captivate the taste buds of your guests and leave them yearning for more.

Conclusion

Embrace the spirit of the season with this Vegan Gingerbread Trifle, and let its charm elevate your holiday gatherings to new heights. This recipe not only embodies the essence of traditional holiday flavors but also offers a cruelty-free and delightful option for those with dietary preferences. So, gather your loved ones, revel in the joy of togetherness, and savor every spoonful of this enchanting dessert. Happy holidays!

This Vegan Gingerbread Trifle recipe combines the warmth of holiday spices with the creamy goodness of vegan custard and whipped coconut cream, making it a delightful treat for festive celebrations.
