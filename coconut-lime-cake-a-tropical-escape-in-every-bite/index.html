Coconut Lime Cake: A Tropical Escape in Every Bite


Indulge in a slice of paradise with this delightful Coconut Lime Cake recipe. Packed with the refreshing flavors of coconut and lime, this tropical dessert will transport you to a sun-soaked beach with every bite. Whether you’re hosting a summer party or simply craving a taste of the tropics, this cake is sure to impress your taste buds and leave you yearning for more.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 ½ cups granulated sugar
  • 1 ½ teaspoons baking powder
  • ½ teaspoon baking soda
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 3 large eggs
  • 1 cup coconut milk
  • ¼ cup fresh lime juice
  • 2 teaspoons lime zest
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease and flour a 9-inch round cake pan.

  2. In a mixing bowl, whisk together the flour, sugar, baking powder, baking soda, and salt. Set aside.

  3. In a separate bowl, cream together the softened butter and granulated sugar until light and fluffy. This should take about 2 minutes.

  4. Add the eggs, one at a time, beating well after each addition. This will ensure a smooth and airy texture in your cake.

  5. Gradually add the dry ingredients to the butter mixture, alternating with the coconut milk. Start and end with the dry ingredients, mixing well after each addition.

  6. Stir in the lime juice, lime zest, and vanilla extract. The lime juice will add a tangy kick, while the zest will infuse the cake with a burst of citrusy aroma.

  7. Pour the batter into the prepared cake pan and smooth the top with a spatula.

  8. Bake in the preheated oven for 30 to 35 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean. The golden-brown crust will add a lovely crunch to each slice.

  9. Once baked, remove the cake from the oven and let it cool in the pan for 10 minutes. Then, transfer it to a wire rack to cool completely.

Frosting and Decoration

Now comes the fun part – decorating your Coconut Lime Cake to resemble a tropical paradise!

Coconut Cream Frosting

  • 1 ½ cups heavy cream
  • ½ cup powdered sugar
  • 1 teaspoon coconut extract

  1. In a chilled mixing bowl, whip the heavy cream until soft peaks form.

  2. Gradually add the powdered sugar and coconut extract, continuing to whip until stiff peaks form. This will yield a creamy and luscious frosting.

  3. Spread the coconut cream frosting evenly over the cooled cake using a spatula or a piping bag for a more decorative touch.

Toasted Coconut and Lime Zest

To add an extra layer of flavor and texture to your Coconut Lime Cake, sprinkle some toasted coconut and lime zest on top.

  1. In a dry skillet over medium heat, toast shredded coconut until golden brown. Keep an eye on it as it can quickly burn.

  2. Once toasted, let the coconut cool completely before sprinkling it over the frosted cake.

  3. Using a fine grater or zester, grate fresh lime zest over the cake. The bright green specks of zest will not only enhance the visual appeal but also intensify the lime flavor.

Serving and Storage

Serve the Coconut Lime Cake chilled or at room temperature for the best experience. This tropical delight pairs well with a scoop of coconut ice cream or a refreshing glass of limeade. It’s the perfect treat to enjoy on a hot summer day or to bring a taste of the tropics to any occasion.

To store any leftovers, cover the cake with plastic wrap and refrigerate. It will stay fresh for up to 3 days, but we doubt it will last that long!

Final Thoughts

Coconut Lime Cake is a slice of paradise that will transport your taste buds to an exotic destination. The combination of luscious coconut and zesty lime creates a harmonious flavor profile that will leave you craving more. So why not embark on a tropical escape with every bite? Try this recipe today and let the flavors whisk you away to a sun-soaked beach, even if just for a moment.
