Sweet Dream Time: 7 Juice & Smoothie Recipes to Get Your Zzz's


Are you seeking a peaceful slumber filled with sweet dreams? Look no further than these seven delicious and nutritious juice and smoothie recipes, designed to provide you with the best possible sleep. Let’s explore these natural sleep-inducing concoctions that not only taste fantastic but will also help you drift off into a restful night’s sleep.

1. The Lavender Kush

Lavender has long been used to promote relaxation, making it the perfect ingredient in this herbal smoothie.

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups almond milk
  • 1 tablespoon honey
  • ½ cup fresh lavender flowers
  • 2 cups spinach
  • 1 banana
  • 1 cup unsweetened yogurt

Instructions:

  1. Place all ingredients into a blender.
  2. Blend until smooth and creamy.
  3. Pour into a glass and sip your way to dreamland.

2. The Raspberry Moonlit Coupe

This heavenly blend of raspberries, blueberries, and magnesium-loaded spinach promises a tranquil sleep.

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups of mixed berries (raspberries and blueberries)
  • 2 cups of baby spinach
  • 1 banana
  • 1 cup of unsweetened almond milk
  • ½ avocado

Instructions:

  1. Combine all ingredients in a blender.
  2. Blend until the mixture is smooth and velvety.
  3. Pour into a glass and savor the taste as you embrace the night’s slumber.

3. The Yummy Band-Aid

Cherries contain melatonin, the hormone responsible for regulating your sleep-wake cycle. This juice is an exquisite combination of tart cherries and citrus, providing energy-balancing properties essential for a good night’s rest.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup tart cherry juice
  • ½ cup orange juice
  • ½ cup lemonade
  • Splash of honey (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Stir together cherry, orange, and lemonade juices.
  2. Add a touch of honey if you prefer a sweeter taste.
  3. Cheers to a restful night’s sleep!

4. The Kiwi Calm Storm

This smoothie storm is a tranquil mix of sweet kiwi, soothing almond milk, and nourishing hemp seeds to help you keep nightmares at bay.

Ingredients:

  • 3 cups of fresh spinach
  • 1 cup of unsweetened almond milk
  • 2 kiwis
  • 2 tablespoons of hemp seeds
  • 1 cup of unsweetened yogurt

Instructions:

  1. Put all the ingredients in a blender.
  2. Blend until smooth and creamy.
  3. Sip your way to serenity as you enjoy this kiwi calm storm.

5. The Sleepytime Smoothie Bowl

Indulge in this irresistible, antioxidant-rich smoothie bowl that features the amazing properties of goji berries and spirulina to enhance your night’s rest.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup unsweetened almond milk
  • 1 banana
  • ½ cup frozen goji berries
  • ½ cu. spirulina powder
  • Optional toppings: pumpkin seeds, hemp hearts, and coconut flakes

Instructions:

  1. Place all the smoothie ingredients in a blender.
  2. Blend until perfectly smooth.
  3. Scoop the mixture into a bowl and top with your favorite crunchy toppings.
  4. Savor this dream-inducing goji berry delight.

6. The Honeydew Rhapsody

Pure and gentle, this refreshing juice made with honeydew, lemon, and mint leaves, is designed to soothe and lull you into a peaceful sleep.

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups of honeydewmelon
  • Juice of 1 lemon
  • A handful of fresh mint leaves
  • 1 cup of water
  • Optional: a teaspoon of honey

Instructions:

  1. Blend honeydew, lemon juice, mint leaves, and water together.
  2. Strain the mixture using a fine mesh strainer.
  3. Stir in honey if you wish to add sweetness.
  4. Raise a toast to a restful night ahead.

7. The Nutty Serenity Smoothie

Walnuts and almond butter come together in this soothing smoothie to create the perfect bedtime treat.

Ingredients:

  • 1.5 cups unsweetened almond milk
  • 2 tablespoons almond butter
  • ½ cup walnuts
  • 1 banana
  • 1 cup unsweetened yogurt

Instructions:

  1. Put all ingredients into a blender.
  2. Blend until smooth and creamy.
  3. Embrace the tranquility of your Nutty Serenity Smoothie as you prepare to drift off to sleep.

Get ready to indulge in these heavenly concoctions and let them guide you to the sweet dream time you deserve. Thoughtfully combined ingredients with natural sleep-inducing properties make these juice and smoothie options an excellent addition to your nightly routine. Sweet dreams!
