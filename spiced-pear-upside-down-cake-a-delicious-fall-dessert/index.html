Spiced Pear Upside-Down Cake: A Delicious Fall Dessert


Are you ready to indulge in a mouthwatering dessert that perfectly captures the flavors of fall? Look no further than this delectable Spiced Pear Upside-Down Cake. With its warm spices and tender pears, this cake is sure to become a favorite in your autumn dessert repertoire.

Ingredients

For the cake:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground ginger
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground nutmeg
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground cloves
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/2 cup milk

For the pear topping:

  • 3 ripe pears, peeled, cored, and thinly sliced
  • 1/4 cup unsalted butter
  • 1/2 cup packed brown sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground nutmeg

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease and flour a 9-inch round cake pan.

  2. In a medium mixing bowl, whisk together the flour, baking powder, salt, cinnamon, ginger, nutmeg, and cloves. Set aside.

  3. In a large mixing bowl, cream together the softened butter and granulated sugar until light and fluffy. Add the eggs, one at a time, beating well after each addition. Stir in the vanilla extract.

  4. Gradually add the dry ingredients to the butter mixture, alternating with the milk. Begin and end with the dry ingredients, mixing just until combined after each addition.

  5. In a small saucepan, melt the butter for the pear topping over medium heat. Stir in the brown sugar, cinnamon, and nutmeg until well combined. Cook for 2-3 minutes, until the mixture is bubbly and slightly thickened.

  6. Pour the pear topping into the prepared cake pan, spreading it evenly across the bottom. Arrange the sliced pears on top of the topping in a decorative pattern.

  7. Gently spoon the cake batter over the pears, spreading it evenly to cover the fruit.

  8. Bake the cake in the preheated oven for 45-50 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.

  9. Remove the cake from the oven and let it cool in the pan for 10 minutes. Carefully invert the cake onto a serving plate, allowing the pear topping to become the cake’s beautiful, caramelized top.

  10. Serve the Spiced Pear Upside-Down Cake warm or at room temperature, and prepare to be amazed by the harmonious blend of flavors and textures.

Tips and Variations

  • For an extra indulgent twist, serve the cake with a dollop of freshly whipped cream or a scoop of vanilla ice cream.
  • Experiment with different spices, such as cardamom or allspice, to add your own unique touch to the cake.
  • If you don’t have pears on hand, you can also use other fruits like apples or peaches to create your own delicious variation of this upside-down cake.

Now that you have the recipe for this Spiced Pear Upside-Down Cake, it’s time to head to the kitchen and start baking. With its warm spices, tender pears, and caramelized topping, this cake will be the perfect sweet treat to enjoy on a crisp fall day. So gather your ingredients and get ready to impress your family and friends with this delightful dessert. Happy baking!
