Cranberry Orange Tart: A Festive Holiday Dessert


The holiday season is a time for indulging in delicious treats, and what better way to celebrate than with a delightful Cranberry Orange Tart? This festive dessert is the perfect combination of sweet and tangy flavors, making it a show-stopping addition to any holiday feast. Whether you’re hosting a gathering or simply want to treat yourself to something special, this Cranberry Orange Tart is sure to impress. Let’s dive into the recipe and discover how to create this mouthwatering dessert from scratch.

Ingredients

For the Crust:

  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup confectioners’ sugar
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, cold and cut into cubes
  • 1 large egg yolk
  • 1 tablespoon ice water

For the Filling:

  • 2 cups fresh or frozen cranberries
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • Zest of 1 orange
  • 1/4 cup orange juice
  • 2 tablespoons cornstarch
  • 1/4 cup water
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract

Instructions

Making the Crust:

  1. In a food processor, combine the flour, confectioners’ sugar, and salt. Add the cold butter and pulse until the mixture resembles coarse crumbs.
  2. Add the egg yolk and ice water, and pulse until the dough comes together.
  3. Turn the dough out onto a lightly floured surface and shape it into a disk. Wrap it in plastic wrap and refrigerate for at least 30 minutes.
  4. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  5. Roll out the dough on a floured surface to fit a 9-inch tart pan. Press the dough into the pan and trim the excess. Prick the bottom of the crust with a fork, then line with parchment paper and fill with pie weights or dried beans.
  6. Bake for 15 minutes, then remove the parchment paper and weights and bake for an additional 10 minutes, or until the crust is golden brown. Allow it to cool completely.

Making the Filling:

  1. In a saucepan, combine the cranberries, granulated sugar, orange zest, and orange juice. Cook over medium heat until the cranberries burst and the mixture thickens, about 10 minutes.
  2. In a small bowl, whisk together the cornstarch and water. Stir the cornstarch mixture into the cranberry mixture and cook for an additional 2 minutes, until thickened.
  3. Remove the saucepan from the heat and stir in the vanilla extract. Allow the filling to cool slightly.

Assembling the Tart:

  1. Pour the cranberry filling into the cooled tart crust, spreading it out evenly.
  2. Refrigerate the tart for at least 2 hours to set the filling.

Serving and Storage

Once the Cranberry Orange Tart is set, it’s ready to be served. The tart can be garnished with a sprinkle of confectioners’ sugar or a dollop of whipped cream for an extra touch of indulgence. This festive dessert pairs beautifully with a cup of hot tea or coffee, making it the perfect conclusion to a holiday meal.

If you have any leftovers, store the tart in the refrigerator, where it will keep well for up to 3 days. The flavors may even intensify, making it a delightful treat to enjoy in the days following your holiday celebration.

Conclusion

The Cranberry Orange Tart is a delightful dessert that captures the essence of the holiday season. Its vibrant colors and wonderful blend of flavors make it a standout addition to any festive occasion. Whether you’re a seasoned baker or new to the world of pastry, this recipe offers a rewarding and delicious experience. So, gather your ingredients, embrace the holiday spirit, and treat yourself and your loved ones to the joy of creating and savoring this delightful Cranberry Orange Tart!
