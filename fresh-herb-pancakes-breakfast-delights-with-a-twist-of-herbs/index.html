Fresh Herb Pancakes: Breakfast Delights with a Twist of Herbs


Are you tired of the same old breakfast routine? Do you crave something unique and flavorful to start your day? Look no further than these Fresh Herb Pancakes! Packed with aromatic herbs and a hint of freshness, these pancakes will take your breakfast game to a whole new level.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup buttermilk
  • 1 large egg
  • 2 tablespoons melted butter
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh herbs (such as parsley, basil, or chives)

Instructions

  1. In a large mixing bowl, whisk together the flour, sugar, baking powder, baking soda, and salt. Set aside.

  2. In a separate bowl, whisk together the buttermilk, egg, and melted butter until well combined.

  3. Pour the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients and gently stir until just combined. Be careful not to overmix; a few lumps are okay.

  4. Add the chopped fresh herbs to the batter and gently fold them in. The herbs will infuse the pancakes with their delightful flavors.

  5. Heat a non-stick skillet or griddle over medium heat. Lightly grease with cooking spray or a small amount of butter.

  6. Using a 1/4 cup measuring cup, pour the pancake batter onto the skillet. Cook until bubbles form on the surface, then flip and cook for an additional 1-2 minutes, or until golden brown.

  7. Repeat with the remaining batter, adjusting the heat as necessary to prevent burning.

  8. Serve the Fresh Herb Pancakes warm with your favorite toppings. Syrup, fresh fruit, or a dollop of whipped cream all complement the herbaceous flavors beautifully.

Why Herb Pancakes?

You might be wondering, why add herbs to pancakes? Well, these Fresh Herb Pancakes offer a delightful twist to your traditional breakfast fare. The herbs not only add a burst of flavor but also provide additional health benefits.

Herbs, such as parsley, basil, or chives, are rich in vitamins, minerals, and antioxidants. They can help boost your immune system, aid digestion, and even reduce inflammation. By incorporating them into your pancakes, you’re not only making your breakfast more exciting but also adding a nutritional boost.

Tips and Variations

  • Experiment with different herb combinations to find your favorite flavor profile. Try adding dill, cilantro, or mint for a unique twist.
  • If you don’t have buttermilk on hand, you can easily make a substitute by adding 1 tablespoon of lemon juice or vinegar to 1 cup of milk. Let it sit for 5 minutes before using.
  • Feel free to customize your pancakes with additional mix-ins like grated cheese, cooked bacon, or sautéed mushrooms. The possibilities are endless!
  • To keep your pancakes warm while cooking batches, preheat your oven to 200°F (93°C) and place cooked pancakes on a baking sheet until ready to serve.

In Conclusion

Fresh Herb Pancakes are a delightful and nutritious way to start your day. With their aromatic flavors and fluffy texture, they are sure to become a breakfast favorite. So, why settle for ordinary pancakes when you can elevate your mornings with a twist of herbs? Give these pancakes a try and experience breakfast delights like never before!
