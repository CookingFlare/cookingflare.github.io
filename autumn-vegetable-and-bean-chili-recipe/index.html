Autumn Vegetable and Bean Chili Recipe


As the leaves start to change colors and the air turns crisper, it’s the perfect time to cozy up with a warm bowl of hearty Autumn Vegetable and Bean Chili. This comforting dish is not only delicious but also packed with nutritious ingredients that will leave you feeling satisfied and nourished. Whether you’re a seasoned chef or a beginner in the kitchen, this recipe is sure to impress your taste buds and warm your soul.

Ingredients:

  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 2 carrots, chopped
  • 2 celery stalks, diced
  • 1 red bell pepper, chopped
  • 1 yellow bell pepper, chopped
  • 1 zucchini, diced
  • 1 sweet potato, peeled and cubed
  • 1 can (15 oz) of diced tomatoes
  • 1 can (15 oz) of kidney beans, drained and rinsed
  • 1 can (15 oz) of black beans, drained and rinsed
  • 4 cups vegetable broth
  • 2 tablespoons chili powder
  • 1 tablespoon cumin
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Optional toppings: avocado, cilantro, lime wedges, sour cream

Instructions:

  1. Heat the olive oil in a large pot over medium heat. Add the diced onion and garlic, and sauté until fragrant.
  2. Add the carrots, celery, and bell peppers to the pot. Cook for a few minutes until the vegetables start to soften.
  3. Stir in the zucchini, sweet potato, diced tomatoes, kidney beans, black beans, vegetable broth, chili powder, cumin, salt, and pepper.
  4. Bring the chili to a boil, then reduce the heat and let it simmer for about 30-40 minutes, or until the vegetables are tender and the flavors have melded together.
  5. Adjust the seasoning according to your taste preferences.
  6. Serve the Autumn Vegetable and Bean Chili hot, topped with your favorite toppings like avocado, cilantro, a squeeze of lime juice, or a dollop of sour cream.

Why You’ll Love This Chili:

  • Nutritious: Packed with a variety of vegetables and beans, this chili is a great way to get your daily dose of vitamins and fiber.
  • Flavorful: The combination of spices like chili powder and cumin gives this dish a rich and warming flavor that’s perfect for the fall season.
  • Versatile: Feel free to customize this recipe by adding your favorite vegetables or adjusting the level of spiciness to suit your taste.
  • Easy to Make: With simple instructions and easy-to-find ingredients, this chili is a great option for a cozy weeknight dinner or a weekend gathering.

Whether you’re looking for a meatless meal option or simply craving a comforting bowl of chili, this Autumn Vegetable and Bean Chili recipe is a must-try. Enjoy the flavors of the season with this hearty and delicious dish that will surely become a favorite in your recipe collection. Happy cooking!

Remember, cooking is a creative process, so don’t be afraid to experiment with different vegetables or spices to make this chili your own masterpiece. Embrace the flavors of autumn and savor every spoonful of this warming dish. Cheers to delicious meals and cozy moments around the table!
