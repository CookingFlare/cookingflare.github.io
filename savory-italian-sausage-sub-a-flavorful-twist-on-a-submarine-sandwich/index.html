Savory Italian Sausage Sub: A Flavorful Twist on a Submarine Sandwich


Are you tired of the same old sandwich routine? It’s time to switch things up with a savory Italian sausage sub that will tantalize your taste buds and leave you craving more. This flavorful twist on a classic submarine sandwich is easy to make, packed with delicious ingredients, and perfect for a quick lunch or a satisfying dinner. Let’s dive into the details and learn how to create this mouthwatering masterpiece.

Ingredients:

For the Italian Sausage:

  • 1 pound of ground Italian sausage
  • 1 teaspoon of fennel seeds
  • 1 teaspoon of paprika
  • 1 teaspoon of garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon of onion powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste

For the Sub:

  • 4 Italian sub rolls
  • 1 cup of marinara sauce
  • 1 cup of shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup of sliced bell peppers
  • 1/2 cup of sliced onions
  • Fresh basil leaves

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the Italian Sausage: In a bowl, combine the ground Italian sausage with fennel seeds, paprika, garlic powder, onion powder, salt, and pepper. Mix well to ensure the spices are evenly distributed. Form the seasoned sausage into four equal-sized patties.

  2. Cook the Italian Sausage: Heat a skillet over medium heat and add the sausage patties. Cook for 5-6 minutes on each side, or until the sausage is fully cooked and browned.

  3. Assemble the Sub: Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C). Slice the Italian sub rolls lengthwise, but not all the way through. Place them on a baking sheet. Spread marinara sauce on each roll, then add a cooked sausage patty to each roll. Top with sliced bell peppers, onions, and a generous amount of shredded mozzarella cheese.

  4. Bake the Subs: Place the assembled subs in the preheated oven and bake for 10-12 minutes, or until the cheese is melted and bubbly.

  5. Garnish and Serve: Remove the subs from the oven and garnish with fresh basil leaves. Close the rolls and slice each sub in half. Serve immediately and enjoy the savory goodness!

Tips and Variations:

  • For a spicy kick, you can use hot Italian sausage instead of mild.
  • Customize the toppings to your liking by adding mushrooms, olives, or any other favorite vegetables.
  • If you prefer a toasted sub, you can toast the assembled sandwiches in a panini press or a hot skillet.

Why You’ll Love This Italian Sausage Sub:

This Italian sausage sub is a flavor explosion in every bite. The combination of seasoned Italian sausage, tangy marinara sauce, melted mozzarella cheese, and fresh vegetables creates a symphony of flavors that will leave you craving more. It’s a versatile recipe that can be enjoyed for lunch, dinner, or even as a crowd-pleasing party snack. Plus, it’s a great way to elevate your sandwich game and impress your family and friends with a delicious homemade meal.

So, next time you’re in the mood for a satisfying and indulgent sub, give this savory Italian sausage sub a try. It’s a delightful twist on a classic submarine sandwich that’s sure to become a favorite in your recipe repertoire. Buon appetito!
