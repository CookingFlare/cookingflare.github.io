Instant Pot Beef and Potato Stew: Hearty and Filling


There’s nothing quite like a warm, hearty stew to comfort the soul and fill the belly. And when it comes to convenience and speed, the Instant Pot is a game-changer. Today, we’re diving into the world of comforting stews with a recipe for Instant Pot Beef and Potato Stew that’s sure to become a staple in your kitchen. This recipe is not only delicious and satisfying but also incredibly easy to make, thanks to the magic of the Instant Pot.

Ingredients

Let’s start with the star players of this hearty dish:

  • 2 pounds of beef stew meat, cut into chunks
  • 4 large potatoes, peeled and cubed
  • 1 large onion, diced
  • 3 carrots, sliced
  • 3 celery stalks, chopped
  • 4 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 4 cups beef broth
  • 1 cup red wine (optional, but highly recommended for depth of flavor)
  • 2 tablespoons tomato paste
  • 2 teaspoons dried thyme
  • 2 teaspoons dried rosemary
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour

Instructions

Now, let’s get cooking:

  1. Prepare the Beef: Season the beef stew meat with salt and pepper. In the Instant Pot on the sauté setting, heat the olive oil. Once hot, add the beef in batches and brown on all sides. Remove the beef and set it aside.

  2. Saute the Aromatics: In the same pot, add the diced onion, carrots, celery, and garlic. Cook for a few minutes until the vegetables start to soften.

  3. Deglaze and Add Flavor: Pour in the red wine to deglaze the pot, scraping up any browned bits from the bottom. Let it simmer for a couple of minutes to reduce slightly. If you prefer not to use wine, you can use a bit of beef broth instead.

  4. Add the Beef and Potatoes: Return the browned beef to the pot. Add the cubed potatoes, dried thyme, dried rosemary, tomato paste, and beef broth. Give everything a good stir to combine.

  5. Pressure Cook: Secure the lid on the Instant Pot and set the pressure release valve to the sealing position. Cook on high pressure for 30 minutes, then allow for a natural pressure release for 10 minutes before carefully releasing any remaining pressure.

  6. Thicken the Stew: In a small bowl, mix the flour with a bit of water to create a slurry. Set the Instant Pot to the sauté setting and stir in the slurry. Let the stew simmer for a few minutes until it thickens to your liking.

  7. Serve and Enjoy: Ladle the rich, hearty stew into bowls and garnish with a sprinkle of fresh parsley if desired. Pair it with a slice of crusty bread for the ultimate comfort meal.

Why Instant Pot?

The Instant Pot is a modern-day kitchen marvel, combining the functions of a pressure cooker, slow cooker, rice cooker, steamer, sauté pan, and more in one appliance. Its ability to significantly reduce cooking time while intensifying flavors makes it a perfect tool for creating soul-warming stews like this beef and potato masterpiece.

Final Thoughts

This Instant Pot Beef and Potato Stew is a testament to the fact that comfort food doesn’t have to be complicated. With just a handful of ingredients and a trusty Instant Pot, you can whip up a meal that’s sure to impress. Whether you’re cooking for a crowd or simply craving a cozy night in, this recipe is a winner.

So, the next time you’re in the mood for something hearty and filling, give this Instant Pot Beef and Potato Stew a try. Your taste buds and your hungry belly will thank you!
