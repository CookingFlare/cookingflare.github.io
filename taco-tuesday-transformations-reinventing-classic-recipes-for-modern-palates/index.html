  1. Smoked Salmon & Avocado Tacos:

When people think ’taco,’ salmon isn’t generally the first choice. Sure, it may feel unconventional. But, trust me when I say it’s a delightful journey! Here’s a flavorful debut: Start by brining a couple of salmon fillets and then hot smoking until tender. Serve your fish atop warm corn tortillas, layering slices of lustrous avocados and a blend of lime-infused Greek yogurt and the tang of pomegranate seeds.

  1. Spicy Kimchi Fried Rice Tacos:

Consider trading in the familiar refried beans for a groundbreaking base made of quick Korean-style kimchi fried rice. Boil some rice, mix in vegetables sliced thin, bean sprouts, and kimchi, and even top it off with savory fried egg. Serves 2-3 adventurous souls.
