Savory Nut-Free Meatball Recipe


If you’re in search of a delicious and allergy-friendly meatball recipe, look no further! These savory nut-free meatballs are packed with flavor and are perfect for those with nut allergies or dietary restrictions. Made with simple ingredients and easy to prepare, they’re sure to become a family favorite. So, let’s get started!

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1/2 cup breadcrumbs
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/4 cup finely chopped onion
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1/4 teaspoon paprika
  • 1/4 teaspoon dried thyme
  • 1/4 teaspoon dried rosemary
  • 1/4 cup milk
  • 1 egg, lightly beaten
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C) and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.

  2. In a large mixing bowl, combine the ground beef, breadcrumbs, Parmesan cheese, onion, garlic, parsley, oregano, salt, black pepper, paprika, dried thyme, and dried rosemary. Mix well to evenly distribute the ingredients.

  3. In a separate small bowl, whisk together the milk and egg. Pour the milk and egg mixture into the large mixing bowl with the meat mixture. Use your hands or a spoon to thoroughly combine everything together.

  4. Shape the meat mixture into small meatballs, about 1 inch in diameter. Place the meatballs on the prepared baking sheet, leaving a little space between each one.

  5. Heat the olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Once the oil is hot, add the meatballs in batches, being careful not to overcrowd the skillet. Cook the meatballs for about 2-3 minutes on each side, or until they are browned and cooked through.

  6. Transfer the browned meatballs back onto the baking sheet and bake them in the preheated oven for an additional 10 minutes, or until they are cooked through.

  7. Once the meatballs are cooked, remove them from the oven and let them cool for a few minutes before serving. Serve them with your favorite sauce, pasta, or enjoy them on their own as appetizers.

  8. Enjoy your savory nut-free meatballs!

Tips and Variations

  • If you prefer a different type of ground meat, feel free to substitute it for the ground beef. Ground turkey or chicken would work well in this recipe.

  • For a gluten-free version, use gluten-free breadcrumbs or crushed gluten-free crackers instead of regular breadcrumbs.

  • Experiment with different herbs and spices to customize the flavor of your meatballs. Adding a pinch of cayenne pepper or chili flakes can give them a spicy kick, while adding a teaspoon of Italian seasoning can enhance the Italian flavors.

  • Make a large batch of meatballs and freeze them for later use. Simply place the cooked and cooled meatballs in an airtight container or freezer bag and store them in the freezer for up to 3 months. Thaw them in the refrigerator before reheating.

These savory nut-free meatballs are versatile and can be enjoyed in various ways. Whether you serve them as an appetizer, with pasta, or as a main dish, they’re sure to be a hit. So, gather your ingredients and give this recipe a try. Your taste buds will thank you!
