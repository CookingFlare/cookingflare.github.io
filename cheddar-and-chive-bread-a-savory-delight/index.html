Cheddar and Chive Bread: A Savory Delight


Are you ready to elevate your baking game with a delicious twist? Look no further than Cheddar and Chive Bread! This savory delight is perfect for any occasion, whether you want a tasty side to accompany your meals or a flavorful snack on its own. With the perfect balance of sharp cheddar cheese and fresh chives, this bread will have your taste buds dancing with joy. Let’s dive into the recipe and bring the aroma of freshly baked bread into your kitchen.

Ingredients:

  • 3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tablespoon baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 cup cold unsalted butter, cubed
  • 1 1/2 cups shredded sharp cheddar cheese
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh chives
  • 1 1/4 cups buttermilk

Instructions:

  1. Preheat and Prepare: Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C). Grease a loaf pan and set it aside.

  2. Mix Dry Ingredients: In a large mixing bowl, combine the flour, baking powder, salt, and baking soda. Mix well to ensure even distribution of the leavening agents.

  3. Incorporate Butter: Add the cubed cold butter to the dry ingredients. Using a pastry cutter or your fingers, work the butter into the flour mixture until it resembles coarse crumbs.

  4. Add Cheese and Chives: Gently fold in the shredded cheddar cheese and chopped chives. These ingredients will add a burst of flavor and color to your bread.

  5. Combine Wet Ingredients: Create a well in the center of the dry ingredients mixture. Pour in the buttermilk and stir until just combined. Be careful not to overmix; a few lumps are okay.

  6. Form the Dough: Transfer the dough to the prepared loaf pan, spreading it out evenly. You can sprinkle some extra cheese and chives on top for a beautiful finish.

  7. Bake to Perfection: Place the pan in the preheated oven and bake for about 45-50 minutes, or until the bread is golden brown on top and a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.

  8. Cool and Enjoy: Once baked, remove the bread from the oven and let it cool in the pan for a few minutes. Then transfer it to a wire rack to cool completely before slicing. Serve warm and enjoy the cheesy goodness!

Whether you’re a seasoned baker or just starting in the kitchen, this Cheddar and Chive Bread recipe is sure to impress. The combination of sharp cheddar and aromatic chives creates a flavorful bread that pairs perfectly with soups, salads, or simply on its own. So, gather your ingredients, preheat the oven, and let the baking begin. Your home will soon be filled with the irresistible smell of freshly baked bread. Happy baking!

Remember, the key to a successful Cheddar and Chive Bread lies in using quality ingredients and following the steps with care. Experiment with different variations, such as adding a dash of garlic powder or red pepper flakes for an extra kick. Get creative in the kitchen and savor the joy of homemade bread at its finest. Enjoy every bite and share this delightful recipe with your loved ones. Happy baking!
