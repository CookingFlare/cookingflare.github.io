Satisfying Keto Lemon Garlic Butter Shrimp


Are you looking for a delicious and satisfying keto recipe? Look no further! This Satisfying Keto Lemon Garlic Butter Shrimp recipe is packed with flavor and is perfect for those following a low-carb lifestyle. With the combination of tangy lemon, aromatic garlic, and rich butter, this dish is sure to become a favorite. Plus, it’s quick and easy to make, so you can enjoy a flavorful meal in no time.

Ingredients

To make this Satisfying Keto Lemon Garlic Butter Shrimp, you will need the following ingredients:

  • Shrimp: Use fresh or thawed shrimp for the best flavor and texture. The recipe suggests using 21-25 count shrimp, which works perfectly.
  • Garlic: Freshly minced garlic adds a wonderful aroma and flavor to the dish.
  • Lemon: Both the zest and juice of a fresh lemon are used to give the shrimp a tangy and refreshing taste.
  • Olive oil: This is used to cook the shrimp and enhance the flavors.
  • Butter: Adding butter to the dish gives it a rich and creamy texture.
  • Salt and pepper: Season the shrimp with salt and pepper to taste.

Instructions

Follow these simple steps to make the Satisfying Keto Lemon Garlic Butter Shrimp:

  1. Thaw the shrimp: If using frozen shrimp, thaw them overnight in the refrigerator. Peel the shrimp if necessary.
  2. Prepare the ingredients: Zest and juice the lemon, and mince the garlic.
  3. Cook the shrimp: Heat olive oil in a pan over medium heat. Add the minced garlic and cook for a minute until fragrant. Add the shrimp and cook for a couple of minutes on each side until they turn pink.
  4. Add the lemon: Squeeze the lemon juice over the shrimp and cook for another minute until most of the juice has evaporated.
  5. Finish with butter: Add the butter to the pan and stir until melted and well combined with the shrimp and lemon sauce.
  6. Season and serve: Season the shrimp with salt and pepper to taste. Serve the Satisfying Keto Lemon Garlic Butter Shrimp hot and enjoy!

Note: For the best flavor, turn off the heat as soon as the shrimp start to turn pink and let the residual heat from the pan finish cooking them [1].

Serving Suggestions

This Satisfying Keto Lemon Garlic Butter Shrimp can be enjoyed on its own as a main dish or paired with your favorite low-carb side dishes. Here are a few serving suggestions to consider:

  • Cauliflower rice: Serve the shrimp over a bed of cauliflower rice for a complete and satisfying meal.
  • Zucchini noodles: Toss the shrimp with zucchini noodles for a light and refreshing option.
  • Salad: Serve the shrimp on top of a fresh green salad for a nutritious and flavorful combination.

Conclusion

This Satisfying Keto Lemon Garlic Butter Shrimp recipe is a must-try for anyone following a low-carb or keto lifestyle. With its tangy lemon, aromatic garlic, and rich buttery flavors, it’s a dish that will satisfy your taste buds and keep you feeling satisfied. Plus, it’s quick and easy to make, making it perfect for busy weeknights. Give it a try and enjoy a delicious and healthy meal!

Remember, cooking is all about experimenting and making the recipe your own. Feel free to adjust the ingredients and seasonings to suit your taste preferences. Enjoy your culinary journey and have fun in the kitchen!
