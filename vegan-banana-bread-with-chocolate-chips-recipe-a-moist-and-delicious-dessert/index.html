Vegan Banana Bread with Chocolate Chips Recipe: A Moist and Delicious Dessert


Welcome to our vegan kitchen! Today, we’re going to dive into the wonderful world of vegan baking with a recipe for Vegan Banana Bread with Chocolate Chips. This moist and delicious dessert is a perfect way to satisfy your sweet tooth while indulging in a healthier treat. Whether you’re a seasoned vegan or just looking to incorporate more plant-based options into your diet, this recipe is sure to become a staple in your collection.

Ingredients:

  • 3 ripe bananas, mashed
  • 1/3 cup melted coconut oil
  • 3/4 cup organic cane sugar
  • 1 teaspoon pure vanilla extract
  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/2 cup vegan chocolate chips

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease a 9x5 inch loaf pan and set it aside.

  2. In a large mixing bowl, combine the mashed bananas, melted coconut oil, organic cane sugar, and pure vanilla extract. Stir until well combined.

  3. In a separate bowl, whisk together the all-purpose flour, baking soda, salt, and ground cinnamon.

  4. Gradually add the dry ingredients to the wet ingredients, mixing until just combined. Be careful not to overmix, as this can result in a dense banana bread.

  5. Gently fold in the vegan chocolate chips, reserving a few to sprinkle on top of the batter.

  6. Pour the batter into the prepared loaf pan, spreading it out evenly. Sprinkle the remaining chocolate chips on top.

  7. Bake for 50-60 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean. Keep an eye on the bread towards the end of the baking time to prevent over-browning.

  8. Once done, remove the banana bread from the oven and allow it to cool in the pan for 10 minutes before transferring it to a wire rack to cool completely.

Serving Suggestion:

This Vegan Banana Bread with Chocolate Chips is best enjoyed warm, with a generous spread of vegan butter or a dollop of dairy-free whipped cream. It also pairs beautifully with a cup of hot coffee or a soothing herbal tea.

Health Benefits:

Apart from being absolutely delicious, this vegan banana bread offers a range of health benefits. Bananas are a great source of potassium, fiber, and various vitamins. Additionally, coconut oil provides healthy fats, while vegan chocolate chips add a touch of antioxidants. By choosing this vegan dessert, you’re treating your taste buds and your body to a dose of wholesome goodness.

Conclusion:

Vegan baking doesn’t have to be complicated, and this Vegan Banana Bread with Chocolate Chips recipe is a shining example of that. With simple, readily available ingredients and straightforward instructions, you can whip up a delectable treat that will have everyone asking for seconds. Whether you’re hosting a gathering, treating your family, or simply craving a cozy dessert, this recipe is a winner.

So, roll up your sleeves, preheat that oven, and let the aroma of freshly baked banana bread fill your kitchen. Embrace the joy of plant-based baking and savor every bite of this moist and delicious vegan dessert. Happy baking!
