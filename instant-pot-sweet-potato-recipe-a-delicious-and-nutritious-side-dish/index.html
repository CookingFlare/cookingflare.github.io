Instant Pot Sweet Potato Recipe: A Delicious and Nutritious Side Dish


Sweet potatoes are a staple ingredient in many cuisines around the world, and for good reason. They’re packed with nutrients, delicious when cooked just right, and incredibly versatile. In this recipe, we’re going to explore the world of Instant Pot sweet potatoes, and show you how to create a mouth-watering side dish that’s perfect for any meal.

Ingredients

  • 2-3 large sweet potatoes, peeled and cubed
  • 1/4 cup water
  • 2 tablespoons brown sugar
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground nutmeg
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1 tablespoon unsalted butter
  • Optional: marshmallows, chopped nuts, or a drizzle of honey for topping

Instructions

Step 1: Prepare the Sweet Potatoes

Begin by peeling and cubing the sweet potatoes into your desired size. For this recipe, we recommend using large sweet potatoes, as they tend to hold their shape better during cooking. Place the cubed sweet potatoes in the Instant Pot.

Step 2: Add Liquid and Spices

Add the water, brown sugar, cinnamon, nutmeg, salt, and black pepper to the Instant Pot. Stir to combine, making sure that all of the sweet potatoes are coated with the liquid and spices.

Step 3: Cook the Sweet Potatoes

Close the lid of the Instant Pot and make sure the valve is set to “sealing”. Press the “manual” or “pressure cook” button and set the cooking time to 10 minutes at high pressure. When the cooking time is up, allow the pressure to release naturally for 10 minutes, then quick-release any remaining pressure.

Step 4: Mash and Add Butter

Once the pressure has been released, open the lid of the Instant Pot and use a fork or potato masher to mash the sweet potatoes to your desired consistency. Add the unsalted butter and stir until it’s fully incorporated.

Step 5: Add Toppings (Optional)

If you want to add a little extra flavor or texture to your sweet potatoes, now’s the time to do it. Try adding some marshmallows, chopped nuts, or a drizzle of honey to give your sweet potatoes a boost.

Step 6: Serve and Enjoy

Your Instant Pot sweet potatoes are now ready to be served. You can serve them as a side dish, add them to salads, or use them as a topping for burgers or sandwiches. Get creative and enjoy!

Tips and Variations

  • For a creamier sweet potato dish, try adding some heavy cream or Greek yogurt to the mashed sweet potatoes.
  • To give your sweet potatoes a spicy kick, add some diced jalapenos or red pepper flakes to the liquid and spices.
  • If you prefer a firmer sweet potato texture, cook them for an additional 5-10 minutes.
  • Sweet potatoes can be cooked in advance and refrigerated for up to 3 days. Reheat them in the Instant Pot or microwave before serving.

In conclusion, this Instant Pot sweet potato recipe is a delicious and nutritious side dish that’s perfect for any meal. With its rich, buttery flavor and tender texture, it’s sure to become a family favorite. So why not give it a try? Your taste buds will thank you!
