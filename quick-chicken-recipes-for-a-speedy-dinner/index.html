Quick Chicken Recipes for a Speedy Dinner


When it comes to preparing a quick and delicious dinner, chicken is a versatile ingredient that never fails to impress. Whether you’re cooking for a busy weeknight or hosting a last-minute gathering, these quick chicken recipes are sure to become your go-to options. From zesty lemon chicken to savory stir-fry, these recipes are not only easy to make but also packed with flavor. Let’s dive into these delightful dishes that will save you time without compromising on taste.

Lemon Garlic Chicken

Ingredients:

  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon lemon zest
  • 1/4 cup fresh lemon juice
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley for garnish

Instructions:

  1. In a small bowl, whisk together the minced garlic, lemon zest, lemon juice, olive oil, oregano, salt, and pepper.
  2. Place the chicken breasts in a resealable plastic bag and pour the marinade over the chicken. Seal the bag and refrigerate for at least 30 minutes.
  3. Preheat the grill or skillet over medium-high heat. Remove the chicken from the marinade and discard the excess liquid.
  4. Grill or pan-fry the chicken for 6-7 minutes on each side, or until the internal temperature reaches 165°F.
  5. Garnish with fresh parsley and serve with your favorite side dishes.

This lemon garlic chicken is a refreshing and vibrant dish that pairs well with a variety of sides, such as steamed vegetables, rice, or a crisp salad.

Honey Mustard Chicken Stir-Fry

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound boneless, skinless chicken thighs, cut into strips
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 red bell pepper, thinly sliced
  • 1 yellow bell pepper, thinly sliced
  • 1 cup snow peas
  • 1/4 cup honey
  • 2 tablespoons Dijon mustard
  • 2 tablespoons low-sodium soy sauce
  • 1 teaspoon minced ginger
  • Cooked rice or noodles for serving

Instructions:

  1. In a small bowl, whisk together the honey, Dijon mustard, and soy sauce. Set aside.
  2. Heat the olive oil in a large skillet or wok over medium-high heat. Add the chicken strips and cook until browned and cooked through. Remove the chicken from the skillet and set aside.
  3. In the same skillet, add the sliced bell peppers, snow peas, and minced ginger. Stir-fry for 3-4 minutes, or until the vegetables are tender-crisp.
  4. Return the cooked chicken to the skillet and pour the honey mustard sauce over the chicken and vegetables. Stir to coat everything evenly.
  5. Serve the honey mustard chicken stir-fry over cooked rice or noodles for a complete and satisfying meal.

This honey mustard chicken stir-fry is a delightful combination of sweet and savory flavors, making it a perfect choice for a quick and flavorful dinner.

Caprese Stuffed Chicken Breast

Ingredients:

  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 4 slices mozzarella cheese
  • 1/4 cup fresh basil leaves
  • 2 tablespoons balsamic glaze
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Olive oil for cooking

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 400°F (200°C).
  2. Using a sharp knife, cut a pocket into the side of each chicken breast.
  3. Season the inside of each chicken breast with salt and pepper. Stuff each breast with cherry tomatoes, mozzarella cheese, and fresh basil leaves.
  4. Heat a skillet over medium-high heat and drizzle with olive oil. Sear the stuffed chicken breasts for 2-3 minutes on each side, or until golden brown.
  5. Transfer the seared chicken breasts to a baking dish and drizzle with balsamic glaze.
  6. Bake in the preheated oven for 20-25 minutes, or until the chicken is cooked through and the cheese is melted and bubbly.

This caprese stuffed chicken breast is a visually stunning dish that’s bursting with the flavors of summer. It’s a quick and elegant option for any dinner occasion.

These quick chicken recipes are designed to simplify your meal preparation without compromising on taste. With their vibrant flavors and straightforward cooking techniques, they are bound to become staples in your recipe repertoire. Enjoy the ease and deliciousness of these speedy chicken dinners, and impress your family and friends with your culinary prowess!
