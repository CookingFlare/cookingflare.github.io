Summer Vegetable and Cheese Frittata Recipe: A Farm-to-Table Favorite


There’s something truly delightful about creating dishes that celebrate the fresh flavors of the season. And when it comes to summer, what better way to do so than with a Summer Vegetable and Cheese Frittata? This versatile and satisfying dish is not only a feast for the taste buds but also a celebration of the vibrant produce available during the warmer months. Let’s dive into this farm-to-table favorite recipe that’s perfect for any meal of the day!

Ingredients:

  • 8 large eggs
  • 1/4 cup milk
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 small red onion, thinly sliced
  • 1 red bell pepper, diced
  • 1 zucchini, sliced into half moons
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 2 cups fresh spinach
  • 1/2 cup feta cheese, crumbled
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh basil leaves for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the Oven: Start by preheating your oven to 350°F (180°C) to ensure it’s ready when your frittata is prepared.

  2. Prepare the Vegetables: In a large oven-safe skillet, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the sliced red onion and cook until softened. Then, add the diced red bell pepper, zucchini, and cherry tomatoes. Cook until the vegetables are tender.

  3. Add the Spinach: Once the vegetables are cooked, add the fresh spinach to the skillet. Allow it to wilt slightly before moving on to the next step.

  4. Whisk the Eggs: In a mixing bowl, whisk together the eggs, milk, salt, and pepper until well combined.

  5. Combine Everything: Pour the egg mixture over the cooked vegetables in the skillet. Gently stir to distribute the vegetables evenly.

  6. Add the Cheese: Sprinkle the crumbled feta cheese on top of the frittata mixture. Let it cook on the stovetop for a few minutes until the edges start to set.

  7. Bake in the Oven: Transfer the skillet to the preheated oven and bake for about 15-20 minutes or until the frittata is set and slightly golden on top.

  8. Serve and Enjoy: Once the frittata is cooked to perfection, remove it from the oven. Garnish with fresh basil leaves and an extra sprinkle of feta cheese if desired. Slice and serve hot.

Why You’ll Love This Recipe:

This Summer Vegetable and Cheese Frittata recipe is a true celebration of seasonal ingredients. The colorful medley of vegetables paired with creamy feta cheese creates a harmony of flavors that will leave your taste buds dancing. Here are a few reasons why this frittata should be on your summer menu:

  • Versatility: This frittata is incredibly versatile and can be enjoyed for breakfast, brunch, lunch, or dinner. It’s perfect for any time of the day.
  • Nutrient-Packed: Packed with fresh vegetables and protein-rich eggs, this dish not only tastes delicious but also provides a healthy dose of nutrients.
  • Easy to Make: With simple ingredients and easy-to-follow steps, this recipe is perfect for both beginner cooks and seasoned chefs.
  • Farm-to-Table Delight: By using seasonal produce, you’re not only enjoying the best flavors but also supporting local farmers and embracing sustainable eating practices.

So, the next time you’re looking for a delicious way to enjoy the flavors of summer, whip up this Summer Vegetable and Cheese Frittata. It’s a farm-to-table favorite that will brighten up your plate and your day!

Remember, cooking is all about creativity, so feel free to customize this recipe with your favorite summer vegetables or cheese. Enjoy every bite and savor the taste of the season in every mouthful! Happy cooking!
