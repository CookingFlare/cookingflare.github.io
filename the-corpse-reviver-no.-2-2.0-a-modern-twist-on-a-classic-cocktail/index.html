The Corpse Reviver No. 2 2.0: A Modern Twist on a Classic Cocktail


If you’re a fan of classic cocktails with a modern twist, then you’re in for a treat. The Corpse Reviver No. 2 2.0 is a refreshing and sophisticated update on the timeless Corpse Reviver No. 2. This revamped version brings a delightful blend of flavors that will tantalize your taste buds and elevate your cocktail game to a whole new level.

Ingredients

To create this modern twist on a classic cocktail, you’ll need the following ingredients:

  • 1 1/2 oz Gin
  • 3/4 oz Cointreau
  • 3/4 oz Lillet Blanc
  • 3/4 oz Fresh lemon juice
  • 1 dash Absinthe
  • Orange twist, for garnish

Instructions

Now, let’s dive into the preparation of The Corpse Reviver No. 2 2.0:

  1. Chill Your Glass: Begin by chilling your cocktail glass in the freezer for a few minutes. This will ensure that your drink stays wonderfully refreshing from the first sip to the last.

  2. Prepare the Cocktail Shaker: Take a cocktail shaker and fill it with ice. The use of ice will help to properly chill and dilute the cocktail, creating a harmonious balance of flavors.

  3. Add the Ingredients: Pour the gin, Cointreau, Lillet Blanc, and fresh lemon juice into the cocktail shaker. These carefully selected ingredients come together to form the heart and soul of this modernized classic cocktail.

  4. Shake It Up: Secure the lid on the cocktail shaker and shake vigorously for about 10-15 seconds. This process is essential for blending the ingredients and achieving the perfect temperature and texture for your drink.

  5. Prepare the Glass: Remove the chilled cocktail glass from the freezer and add a dash of absinthe to coat the inside of the glass. Swirl the absinthe around, then discard any excess. This step introduces a subtle hint of anise flavor, adding depth to the cocktail’s profile.

  6. Strain and Serve: Carefully strain the contents of the cocktail shaker into the prepared glass. The vibrant colors and enticing aromas of this modern twist on the classic Corpse Reviver No. 2 are now ready to be enjoyed.

  7. Garnish: To add a final touch of elegance, garnish the cocktail with a twist of orange. This not only enhances the visual appeal but also imparts a hint of citrus aroma to the drink.

The Modern Twist

What sets The Corpse Reviver No. 2 2.0 apart from its predecessor is the inclusion of Lillet Blanc, a French aperitif wine. This addition brings a subtle floral and fruity complexity to the cocktail, complementing the zesty notes of the gin and lemon juice. The result is a refined and well-balanced libation that is perfect for any occasion.

Why It’s a Must-Try

Whether you’re hosting a gathering or simply unwinding after a long day, this cocktail is a delightful companion that never fails to impress.
