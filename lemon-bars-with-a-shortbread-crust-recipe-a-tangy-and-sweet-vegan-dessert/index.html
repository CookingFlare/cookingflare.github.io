Lemon Bars with a Shortbread Crust Recipe: A Tangy and Sweet Vegan Dessert


Are you craving a tangy and sweet dessert that is also vegan-friendly? Look no further! These Lemon Bars with a Shortbread Crust are the perfect treat to satisfy your sweet tooth. With a buttery shortbread crust and a luscious lemon filling, these bars are a delightful combination of flavors and textures. Plus, they are incredibly easy to make!

Ingredients

For the Shortbread Crust:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup powdered sugar
  • 1 cup vegan butter, softened

For the Lemon Filling:

  • 1 1/2 cups granulated sugar
  • 1/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup lemon juice
  • Zest of 2 lemons
  • 1/2 cup almond milk
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease a 9x13-inch baking dish and set aside.

  2. In a mixing bowl, combine the all-purpose flour and powdered sugar for the shortbread crust. Add the softened vegan butter and mix until the mixture resembles coarse crumbs.

  3. Press the shortbread crust mixture into the bottom of the prepared baking dish, creating an even layer. Bake in the preheated oven for 15 minutes, or until the edges are lightly golden.

  4. While the crust is baking, prepare the lemon filling. In a separate bowl, whisk together the granulated sugar and all-purpose flour. Add the lemon juice, lemon zest, almond milk, and vanilla extract. Stir until well combined and the sugar has dissolved.

  5. Pour the lemon filling over the baked shortbread crust, spreading it evenly. Return the dish to the oven and bake for an additional 20-25 minutes, or until the filling is set.

  6. Remove the lemon bars from the oven and let them cool completely in the baking dish. Once cooled, refrigerate for at least 2 hours to allow the bars to firm up.

  7. Once chilled, remove the lemon bars from the baking dish and cut into squares or rectangles. Dust with powdered sugar for an extra touch of sweetness, if desired.

  8. Serve these tangy and sweet Lemon Bars with a Shortbread Crust as a refreshing dessert or a delightful afternoon treat. They are perfect for any occasion and are sure to impress both vegans and non-vegans alike!

Tips and Variations

  • For an extra burst of flavor, add a teaspoon of lemon extract to the lemon filling.
  • Feel free to experiment with different citrus flavors! Try using lime or orange juice instead of lemon juice for a unique twist on this classic recipe.
  • If you prefer a thicker crust, simply double the amount of the shortbread crust ingredients and adjust the baking time accordingly.
  • Serve these Lemon Bars with a dollop of vegan whipped cream or a scoop of dairy-free vanilla ice cream for an indulgent dessert experience.

So there you have it – a simple and delicious recipe for Lemon Bars with a Shortbread Crust. Whether you’re a vegan or not, these bars are sure to become a favorite in your household. They are tangy, sweet, and oh-so-satisfying. Give them a try and let your taste buds dance with delight!
