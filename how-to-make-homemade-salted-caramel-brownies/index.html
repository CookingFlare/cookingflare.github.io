How to Make Homemade Salted Caramel Brownies


There’s something incredibly irresistible about brownies. Their fudgy, rich texture combined with the deep flavors of chocolate makes them an all-time favorite dessert. But imagine taking that beloved treat and elevating it with the silky smoothness of homemade salted caramel. The combination of sweet, salty, and rich chocolate in salted caramel brownies will undoubtedly delight your taste buds and leave everyone asking for the recipe. Here’s how to make homemade salted caramel brownies from scratch—perfect for special occasions or when you simply need a delicious chocolatey indulgence.

Ingredients for the Brownies

Before diving into the recipe, it’s essential to gather all the ingredients. This recipe requires a few basic ingredients for the brownies and a homemade salted caramel sauce that adds an extra layer of flavor to the treat. Here’s a breakdown of the ingredients you’ll need:

For the Brownie Batter:

  • 1 cup (2 sticks) unsalted butter – Melted and cooled.
  • 1 ¼ cups granulated sugar – This provides sweetness and texture to the brownies.
  • 3 large eggs – Eggs add structure and moisture.
  • 1 teaspoon pure vanilla extract – For flavor.
  • ¾ cup all-purpose flour – Provides structure and helps the brownies hold together.
  • ¾ cup unsweetened cocoa powder – The key ingredient for a rich chocolate flavor.
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder – To help the brownies rise.
  • ½ teaspoon salt – Balances the sweetness and enhances the flavors.
  • ½ cup semi-sweet or bittersweet chocolate chips – Optional, but adds extra chocolatey goodness.

For the Salted Caramel Sauce:

  • 1 cup granulated sugar – For making the caramel base.
  • 6 tablespoons unsalted butter – Adds richness to the caramel.
  • ½ cup heavy cream – Creates a smooth texture for the caramel sauce.
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract – Enhances the caramel flavor.
  • 1 teaspoon sea salt – Essential for that perfect salty-sweet balance.

For the Final Touch:

  • Additional sea salt flakes – For sprinkling on top of the brownies for that extra salted touch.

Step-by-Step Instructions for Making Homemade Salted Caramel Brownies

Let’s break down the process into simple steps to create the perfect salted caramel brownies. The key here is to make sure each component is prepared with care—both the brownies and the salted caramel sauce. Once they’re combined, you’ll get a dessert that’s beautifully balanced in sweetness and saltiness.

Step 1: Preheat the Oven and Prepare the Baking Pan

Start by preheating your oven to 350°F (175°C). This is the perfect temperature for baking the brownies, ensuring they cook evenly and develop that fudgy texture we all crave.

Next, prepare your baking pan. Grease a 9x9-inch square baking pan or line it with parchment paper for easy removal once the brownies are baked. If you prefer a slightly thinner brownie, you can opt for an 8x8-inch pan.

Step 2: Prepare the Brownie Batter

In a large mixing bowl, whisk together the melted butter and granulated sugar until smooth and well combined. This forms the base of your brownie batter. Add the eggs, one at a time, beating well after each addition. Stir in the vanilla extract, making sure everything is fully incorporated.

Now, sift the all-purpose flour, unsweetened cocoa powder, baking powder, and salt into the wet ingredients. Use a spatula or wooden spoon to gently fold the dry ingredients into the wet mixture. Be careful not to overmix; you want to keep the batter dense and fudgy, not airy.

Once everything is well combined, fold in the chocolate chips if you’re using them. These little bits of chocolate will melt during baking, creating pockets of gooey goodness within the brownies.

Step 3: Bake the Brownies

Pour the brownie batter into your prepared baking pan and spread it out evenly. Place the pan in the preheated oven and bake for about 25 to 30 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out with just a few moist crumbs attached. The brownies should be slightly firm to the touch but still soft in the center.

Once the brownies are done baking, remove them from the oven and let them cool in the pan for about 10 minutes. This step is crucial because it helps the brownies set without falling apart when you cut them.

Step 4: Make the Homemade Salted Caramel Sauce

While the brownies are cooling, it’s time to prepare the salted caramel sauce. This step can seem intimidating, but with a little patience, you’ll create a rich, smooth caramel sauce that will take these brownies to the next level.

In a medium saucepan, melt the granulated sugar over medium heat. Stir constantly with a wooden spoon or heatproof spatula to ensure the sugar melts evenly and doesn’t burn. The sugar will first clump together before eventually turning into a golden amber liquid. This process takes about 5 to 7 minutes, so keep an eye on it.

Once the sugar has completely melted, add the butter. The mixture will bubble up when the butter is added, so be cautious. Stir until the butter is fully incorporated into the caramel.

Next, slowly pour in the heavy cream, continuing to stir. The mixture will bubble up again, so be careful not to burn yourself. Keep stirring until the caramel sauce is smooth and well combined. Remove the saucepan from the heat and stir in the vanilla extract and sea salt. Let the caramel sauce cool slightly before using it on the brownies.

Step 5: Assemble the Salted Caramel Brownies

Now it’s time for the fun part—assembling the salted caramel brownies! Once the brownies have cooled for about 10 minutes, use a spoon or small ladle to drizzle the salted caramel sauce generously over the top of the brownies. You can add as much or as little as you like, depending on how caramel-heavy you want the final result.

After drizzling the caramel, use a butter knife to swirl the caramel into the brownie batter slightly. This creates beautiful marbled swirls that will look stunning once the brownies are cut.

Step 6: Add the Finishing Touch

To give the brownies that signature salted caramel flavor, sprinkle a pinch of sea salt flakes over the top. This helps balance the sweetness of the caramel and enhances the flavor of the brownies. You can either go for a light sprinkle or be more generous, depending on your preference for saltiness.

Let the brownies cool completely in the pan. If you try to cut them too soon, the caramel will be too gooey, and they might fall apart. Once they’ve cooled to room temperature (about an hour), use a sharp knife to cut them into squares or rectangles.

Step 7: Enjoy!

Now, it’s time to enjoy your homemade salted caramel brownies! These decadent treats are perfect for any occasion—whether you’re serving them at a dinner party, bringing them to a potluck, or simply indulging yourself. The combination of rich, fudgy brownies with gooey salted caramel will have everyone coming back for more.

Tips for Perfect Salted Caramel Brownies

  • Use good-quality chocolate for the brownies. The flavor of the chocolate will shine through, so it’s worth investing in semi-sweet or bittersweet chocolate chips.
  • Don’t rush the caramel process. Stir constantly to avoid burning the sugar. Be patient, as making the perfect caramel requires attention to detail.
  • Make extra caramel sauce! If you have leftover caramel, you can drizzle it over ice cream, pancakes, or even store it in the refrigerator for future use.
  • Chill the brownies before cutting. After they’ve cooled completely, refrigerating them for 30 minutes can help set the caramel and make them easier to cut.

How to Store Salted Caramel Brownies

These salted caramel brownies can be stored at room temperature in an airtight container for up to 4 days. If you prefer them to stay extra fresh, you can refrigerate them for up to 1 week. To freeze them, wrap individual brownies in plastic wrap and place them in a freezer bag for up to 3 months.

Salted caramel brownies are the perfect dessert for anyone who loves the combination of rich chocolate and sweet, salty flavors. With a homemade salted caramel sauce that’s easy to make, this recipe takes your traditional brownie to new heights. Whether you’re baking for a special occasion or treating yourself, these brownies will never disappoint. Happy baking!
