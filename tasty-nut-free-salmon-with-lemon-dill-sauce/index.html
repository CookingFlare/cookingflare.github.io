Tasty Nut-Free Salmon with Lemon Dill Sauce


If you’re looking for a delicious and nutritious meal that’s both easy to prepare and full of flavor, then look no further than this Tasty Nut-Free Salmon with Lemon Dill Sauce recipe. This dish is perfect for those with nut allergies or anyone looking to enjoy a light and refreshing seafood meal. The combination of tender salmon and zesty lemon dill sauce is sure to tantalize your taste buds and leave you feeling satisfied. Let’s dive into the details of this delightful recipe.

Ingredients

  • 4 salmon fillets
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 tablespoons fresh dill, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/4 cup chicken or vegetable broth
  • 1/4 cup freshly squeezed lemon juice
  • 1 tablespoon Dijon mustard
  • 1 tablespoon honey
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • Fresh lemon slices for garnish

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Salmon: Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C). Place the salmon fillets on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper. Drizzle the fillets with olive oil and sprinkle with salt and pepper to taste. Bake the salmon for 12-15 minutes, or until it easily flakes with a fork.

  2. Make the Lemon Dill Sauce: While the salmon is baking, heat a skillet over medium heat. Add the chopped dill and minced garlic to the skillet and sauté for 1-2 minutes, until fragrant. Pour in the chicken or vegetable broth, lemon juice, Dijon mustard, and honey. Stir the mixture and let it simmer for 3-4 minutes. Slowly pour in the heavy cream, stirring constantly, and continue to simmer for an additional 2-3 minutes until the sauce thickens slightly.

  3. Serve and Enjoy: Once the salmon is cooked through, transfer it to serving plates. Spoon the lemon dill sauce generously over the salmon fillets. Garnish with fresh lemon slices and additional dill, if desired.

Nutritional Benefits

Salmon is a rich source of high-quality protein and omega-3 fatty acids, which are essential for heart health and brain function. This recipe provides a healthy dose of these nutrients, along with the bright flavors of lemon and dill. The absence of nuts in this dish makes it a safe and satisfying option for individuals with nut allergies, allowing them to enjoy the goodness of seafood without any concerns.

Serving Suggestions

This Tasty Nut-Free Salmon with Lemon Dill Sauce pairs beautifully with a side of steamed vegetables, such as asparagus or green beans. You can also serve it alongside a fresh garden salad for a light and balanced meal. For a heartier option, consider serving the salmon over a bed of fluffy quinoa or wild rice. The versatile nature of this dish allows you to customize it to your liking and dietary preferences.

Final Thoughts

In conclusion, this Tasty Nut-Free Salmon with Lemon Dill Sauce recipe is a delightful choice for anyone seeking a flavorful and wholesome meal that’s free from nuts and packed with nutritional benefits. Whether you’re cooking for a special occasion or simply treating yourself to a delicious dinner, this recipe is sure to impress. The simplicity of the preparation combined with the vibrant flavors of the lemon dill sauce make this dish a standout option for seafood lovers. Give it a try and savor the delightful taste of this impressive salmon dish!
