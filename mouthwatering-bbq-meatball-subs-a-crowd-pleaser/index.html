Mouthwatering BBQ Meatball Subs: A Crowd Pleaser


BBQ meatball subs are a scrumptious and satisfying meal that’s perfect for gatherings, game days, or a cozy family dinner. This crowd-pleasing dish brings together tender meatballs smothered in a homemade BBQ sauce and loaded onto warm subs. Stick around as we guide you through the process of making these irresistible subs from scratch!

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground beef or ground turkey
  • 1 cup breadcrumbs
  • 1 large egg
  • 1/4 cup finely chopped onion
  • 1/4 cup milk
  • 1 teaspoon Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 teaspoon minced garlic
  • Salt and pepper

  • 1/2 cup ketchup
  • 1/4 cup apple cider vinegar
  • 1/4 cup brown sugar
  • 1 teaspoon smoked paprika
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1/4 teaspoon cayenne pepper
  • Salt and pepper

Instructions

To make mouthwatering BBQ meatball subs that’ll leave everyone begging for more, follow these simple steps:

Cooking the Meatballs

  1. In a large mixing bowl, combine the ground beef (or turkey), breadcrumbs, egg, chopped onion, milk, Worcestershire sauce, minced garlic, salt, and pepper. Mix well, ensuring all ingredients are evenly distributed.
  2. Shape the mixture into 1.5-inch meatballs, roughly 24 in total.
  3. Heat a large skillet over medium heat. Add 1-2 tablespoons of oil and let it heat up.
  4. Add the meatballs gradually to the skillet, being careful not to overcrowd. Cook each batch for about 7-8 minutes, turning occasionally, or until cooked through and golden brown.
  5. Repeat the process with remaining meatballs and set them aside on a paper towel-lined plate to absorb excess oil.

Preparation of the BBQ Sauce

  1. In a saucepan over medium heat, combine ketchup, apple cider vinegar, brown sugar, smoked paprika, garlic powder, ground cumin, cayenne pepper, salt, and pepper.
  2. Mix all ingredients well, ensuring the BBQ sauce has a smooth consistency.
  3. Cook the sauce for about 15-20 minutes, stirring occasionally, until it has thickened and the flavors meld together.

Preparing the Subs

  1. Preheat the oven to 375Â°F (190Â°C).
  2. Split the sub rolls and place them on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper.
  3. Bake the sub rolls for about 5-7 minutes, until slightly toasted but still soft.
  4. Remove the rolls from the oven and fill with the cooked meatballs.
  5. Slowly pour the homemade BBQ sauce over each sub, ensuring all meatballs are evenly covered.

Optional: Top with your favorite toppings, such as shredded cheese, pickles, lettuce, or diced tomatoes.

Serving

  1. Slice the meatball subs into halves or quarters, depending on your preference.
  2. Serve the mouthwatering BBQ meatball subs to your friends and family while discussing the game, sharing stories, or just enjoying a delightful meal together.

These BBQ meatball subs are guaranteed to be a hit at your next gathering or any time you’re craving a delectable and comforting meal. With the ease and satisfaction they bring to the table, you’ll surely be asked to bring them to every event from now on. Happy cooking!
