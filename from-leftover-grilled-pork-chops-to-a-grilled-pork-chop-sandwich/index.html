From Leftover Grilled Pork Chops to a Grilled Pork Chop Sandwich


Are you looking for a delicious way to use up those leftover grilled pork chops? Look no further! In this recipe, we’ll show you how to transform those tasty leftovers into a mouthwatering Grilled Pork Chop Sandwich. This sandwich is the perfect combination of smoky grilled flavors, tender pork, and a medley of fresh toppings. Get ready to take your leftover game to the next level!

Ingredients

  • Leftover grilled pork chops (2-4 pieces)
  • 4 hamburger buns
  • 1 red onion, thinly sliced
  • 4 leaves of lettuce
  • 4 slices of tomato
  • 4 slices of cheese (cheddar or Swiss)
  • Pickles (optional)
  • BBQ sauce (optional)
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Start by reheating your leftover grilled pork chops. You can do this by placing them on a preheated grill or in a skillet over medium heat. Cook for a few minutes on each side until warmed through. Alternatively, you can use a microwave if you’re in a hurry.

  2. While the pork chops are reheating, prepare your hamburger buns. You can lightly toast them on the grill or in a toaster if desired. This step adds a nice crunch to the sandwich.

  3. Once the pork chops are hot, remove them from the heat and set aside. Now it’s time to assemble your sandwich!

  4. Take a hamburger bun and place a pork chop on the bottom half. If your pork chops are large, you can cut them in half to fit better. Season with salt and pepper to taste.

  5. Add a slice of cheese on top of the pork chop. The heat from the meat will melt the cheese slightly, creating a gooey and delicious layer.

  6. Next, layer on some thinly sliced red onion, a leaf of lettuce, and a slice of tomato. These fresh toppings add a nice crunch and balance out the rich flavors of the pork.

  7. If you’re a fan of pickles, now is the time to add a few slices on top. The tangy and briny taste of pickles complements the smoky flavors of the pork perfectly.

  8. Finally, spread some BBQ sauce on the top half of the bun if desired. BBQ sauce adds a sweet and tangy kick to the sandwich, enhancing the overall flavor profile.

  9. Repeat the process for the remaining pork chops and buns.

  10. Serve your Grilled Pork Chop Sandwiches immediately and enjoy!

Tips and Variations

  • If you don’t have leftover grilled pork chops, you can grill some fresh pork chops specifically for this recipe. Simply season the pork chops with your favorite spices and grill them until cooked through.

  • Feel free to customize your sandwich with additional toppings such as avocado, bacon, or jalapenos. Get creative and make it your own!

  • For an extra indulgent twist, butter the inside of the buns before toasting them. This will give your sandwich a rich and buttery flavor.

  • If you prefer a vegetarian option, you can substitute the pork chops with grilled portobello mushrooms or tofu. The sandwich will still be packed with flavor and deliciousness.

So, the next time you find yourself with leftover grilled pork chops, don’t let them go to waste. Turn them into a delectable Grilled Pork Chop Sandwich that will satisfy your craving for a flavorful and satisfying meal. Enjoy the combination of tender pork, melty cheese, and fresh toppings all nestled between a toasted bun. Bon appétit!
