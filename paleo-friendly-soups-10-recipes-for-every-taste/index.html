Paleo-Friendly Soups: 10 Recipes for Every Taste


If you’re someone who loves the warmth and comfort of a delicious soup but is also looking to stick to a Paleo lifestyle, you’re in for a treat. Paleo-friendly soups focus on fresh, whole ingredients without the use of dairy, grains, or processed foods. Packed with nutrients and flavor, these soups offer the perfect balance of taste and health benefits. Whether you’re craving a rich, creamy bowl or a light and refreshing soup, there’s something for every palate on this list.

Here are 10 delicious Paleo-friendly soup recipes that will satisfy your cravings and keep you on track with your dietary goals.

1. Classic Chicken and Vegetable Soup

Nothing beats the comforting flavor of a classic chicken soup, and this Paleo-friendly version is a great go-to when you need a warm, hearty meal. Packed with chicken, carrots, celery, and onions, this soup is rich in protein and vitamins.

Ingredients:

  • 2 chicken breasts (boneless and skinless)
  • 4 cups chicken broth (preferably homemade or no-sugar-added)
  • 1 medium onion, chopped
  • 2 carrots, sliced
  • 2 celery stalks, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley for garnish

Instructions:

  1. In a large pot, heat a tablespoon of olive oil over medium heat.
  2. Add the onions, garlic, carrots, and celery. Sauté for about 5 minutes, until softened.
  3. Pour in the chicken broth and add the chicken breasts, thyme, salt, and pepper.
  4. Bring the soup to a boil, then reduce the heat and simmer for 25-30 minutes, until the chicken is fully cooked.
  5. Remove the chicken from the pot, shred it, and return it to the soup.
  6. Garnish with fresh parsley and serve warm.

2. Roasted Butternut Squash Soup

For a creamy, slightly sweet soup that’s both comforting and Paleo-friendly, try this roasted butternut squash soup. Roasting the squash first deepens its natural sweetness and creates a rich, velvety texture.

Ingredients:

  • 1 medium butternut squash, peeled and cubed
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 medium onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 4 cups chicken or vegetable broth
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground nutmeg
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C). Place the cubed butternut squash on a baking sheet and drizzle with olive oil. Roast for 25-30 minutes, or until tender and slightly caramelized.
  2. In a large pot, heat a tablespoon of olive oil over medium heat. Add the onion and garlic, sautéing until softened.
  3. Add the roasted squash and broth to the pot, along with the cinnamon, nutmeg, salt, and pepper. Bring to a boil, then reduce the heat and simmer for 10 minutes.
  4. Using an immersion blender or regular blender, blend the soup until smooth and creamy.
  5. Adjust the seasoning as needed and serve warm.

3. Paleo Beef and Mushroom Soup

A rich, savory soup, this beef and mushroom recipe is perfect for those who crave a more hearty option. The earthy mushrooms and tender beef create a balanced dish, while the herbs bring a fragrant depth of flavor.

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb ground beef (grass-fed if possible)
  • 8 oz mushrooms, sliced
  • 1 medium onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 4 cups beef broth (preferably homemade)
  • 1 teaspoon dried rosemary
  • 1 teaspoon thyme
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley for garnish

Instructions:

  1. In a large pot, brown the ground beef over medium heat until fully cooked. Remove and set aside.
  2. In the same pot, sauté the onions, garlic, and mushrooms for about 5 minutes, until tender.
  3. Return the beef to the pot and pour in the beef broth. Add the rosemary, thyme, salt, and pepper.
  4. Bring to a boil, then reduce the heat and simmer for 20-25 minutes.
  5. Garnish with fresh parsley and serve hot.

4. Spicy Sweet Potato and Carrot Soup

This bright and flavorful soup combines the sweetness of sweet potatoes and carrots with a kick of spice. It’s perfect for those who like their soups with a bit of heat but still crave a smooth, creamy texture.

Ingredients:

  • 2 medium sweet potatoes, peeled and cubed
  • 3 large carrots, peeled and chopped
  • 1 medium onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 4 cups chicken broth
  • 1 teaspoon ground ginger
  • 1/2 teaspoon cayenne pepper (adjust to taste)
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a large pot, sauté the onions and garlic over medium heat for about 5 minutes.
  2. Add the sweet potatoes, carrots, chicken broth, ginger, cayenne, salt, and pepper to the pot. Bring to a boil.
  3. Reduce the heat and simmer for 20-25 minutes, until the vegetables are tender.
  4. Use an immersion blender or regular blender to purée the soup until smooth and creamy.
  5. Adjust the seasoning and serve hot.

5. Paleo Tomato Basil Soup

Tomato basil soup is a classic comfort food, and with a few simple tweaks, it can easily fit into a Paleo lifestyle. This version is made without any dairy or sugar, making it a healthy and satisfying choice.

Ingredients:

  • 6 large tomatoes, diced
  • 1 medium onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 4 cups chicken or vegetable broth
  • 1/4 cup fresh basil, chopped
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil

Instructions:

  1. In a large pot, heat olive oil over medium heat. Add the onions and garlic and sauté until fragrant, about 5 minutes.
  2. Add the tomatoes and broth to the pot, bring to a boil, and then simmer for 20-25 minutes.
  3. Use an immersion blender or regular blender to purée the soup until smooth.
  4. Stir in the fresh basil, season with salt and pepper, and serve warm.

6. Paleo Coconut Curry Chicken Soup

This coconut curry chicken soup brings a rich, flavorful twist to traditional chicken soup. The coconut milk adds creaminess, while the curry spices bring a warmth and depth that’s perfect for cold days.

Ingredients:

  • 2 chicken breasts, diced
  • 1 tablespoon coconut oil
  • 1 medium onion, chopped
  • 1 bell pepper, chopped
  • 1 can (14 oz) full-fat coconut milk
  • 4 cups chicken broth
  • 1 tablespoon curry powder
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a large pot, heat the coconut oil over medium heat. Add the onion and bell pepper, sautéing for about 5 minutes.
  2. Add the chicken and cook until browned on all sides.
  3. Stir in the coconut milk, chicken broth, curry powder, turmeric, cumin, salt, and pepper.
  4. Bring the soup to a boil, then reduce the heat and simmer for 20-25 minutes, until the chicken is fully cooked and tender.
  5. Serve hot, garnished with fresh cilantro or extra herbs if desired.

7. Paleo Zucchini and Spinach Soup

A light, nutrient-packed soup perfect for a refreshing meal, this zucchini and spinach soup is quick to prepare and full of fresh vegetables. It’s a great option for those looking to enjoy a lower-calorie soup.

Ingredients:

  • 2 zucchinis, chopped
  • 3 cups fresh spinach
  • 1 medium onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 4 cups vegetable broth
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil

Instructions:

  1. In a large pot, heat olive oil over medium heat. Add the onion and garlic and sauté for 5 minutes.
  2. Add the zucchinis and cook for another 5 minutes, until they begin to soften.
  3. Pour in the vegetable broth, season with salt and pepper, and bring to a boil.
  4. Reduce the heat and simmer for 15 minutes, until the zucchinis are tender.
  5. Add the spinach and cook for another 2-3 minutes.
  6. Use an immersion blender or regular blender to purée the soup until smooth.
  7. Serve warm and enjoy!

8. Paleo Egg Drop Soup

This simple yet delicious egg drop soup is a great option when you want something light but satisfying. The eggs create silky ribbons throughout the broth, giving it a creamy texture without any dairy.

Ingredients:

  • 4 large eggs, beaten
  • 4 cups chicken broth
  • 1 tablespoon coconut aminos (or tamari)
  • 1 teaspoon ginger, freshly grated
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 green onions, sliced
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a large pot, heat the chicken broth over medium heat. Add the coconut aminos, ginger, garlic, and green onions.
  2. Bring the broth to a gentle simmer.
  3. Slowly drizzle the beaten eggs into the broth while stirring gently to create ribbons.
  4. Cook for 2-3 minutes, until the eggs are fully cooked through.
  5. Season with salt and pepper and serve hot.

9. Paleo Pumpkin Soup

Perfect for fall or any time you’re craving something a little sweet and savory, this pumpkin soup is simple, nutritious, and delicious. The pumpkin provides a creamy base that’s enhanced with warming spices.

Ingredients:

  • 1 can (15 oz) pumpkin puree
  • 1 medium onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 4 cups chicken or vegetable broth
  • 1/2 teaspoon cinnamon
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground ginger
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a large pot, sauté the onion and garlic in olive oil over medium heat until softened.
  2. Add the pumpkin puree, broth, cinnamon, ginger, salt, and pepper. Bring to a boil.
  3. Reduce the heat and simmer for 15-20 minutes.
  4. Use an immersion blender to purée the soup until smooth.
  5. Adjust the seasoning and serve hot.

10. Paleo Cauliflower Soup

This creamy cauliflower soup is a great low-carb alternative to traditional potato-based soups. The cauliflower adds a smooth, velvety texture, while the garlic and herbs infuse the broth with delicious flavor.

Ingredients:

  • 1 head of cauliflower, chopped
  • 1 medium onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 4 cups vegetable broth
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a large pot, heat olive oil over medium heat. Add the onion and garlic, sautéing until softened.
  2. Add the cauliflower and vegetable broth. Bring to a boil, then reduce the heat and simmer for 20-25 minutes, until the cauliflower is tender.
  3. Use an immersion blender or regular blender to purée the soup until smooth.
  4. Season with salt and pepper, and serve hot.

Conclusion

Paleo soups are a perfect way to enjoy comforting meals that are nourishing and full of flavor. Whether you’re craving something light, spicy, or creamy, these 10 recipes offer a wide variety of options to suit every taste. By using fresh, wholesome ingredients, you can create flavorful soups that are both satisfying and aligned with your Paleo lifestyle. Enjoy experimenting with these recipes and make them your go-to meals for any time of year.
