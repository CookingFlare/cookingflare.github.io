Thanksgiving Recipes for the Busy Cook


Welcome to our ultimate guide to Thanksgiving recipes for the busy cook! Thanksgiving is a time for gratitude, family gatherings, and of course, delicious food. If you’re a busy cook looking to impress your guests without spending hours in the kitchen, we’ve got you covered. From appetizers to desserts, these quick and easy recipes will help you create a memorable Thanksgiving feast without the stress.

Appetizers

1. Cranberry Brie Bites

  • Ingredients:
    • 1 sheet of puff pastry
    • 1 wheel of Brie cheese
    • Cranberry sauce
  • Instructions:
    1. Cut the puff pastry into small squares and place them in a mini muffin tin.
    2. Add a small piece of Brie cheese and a spoonful of cranberry sauce on top.
    3. Bake in the oven until the pastry is golden and the cheese is melted. Serve warm.

2. Sweet Potato Crostini

  • Ingredients:
    • Baguette slices
    • Roasted sweet potato slices
    • Goat cheese
    • Honey
  • Instructions:
    1. Toast the baguette slices until crispy.
    2. Top each slice with a roasted sweet potato slice, a dollop of goat cheese, and a drizzle of honey.
    3. Garnish with fresh herbs and serve as a delightful appetizer.

Main Course

3. Herb-Roasted Turkey Breast

  • Ingredients:
    • Turkey breast
    • Fresh herbs (rosemary, thyme, sage)
    • Olive oil
  • Instructions:
    1. Preheat the oven and rub the turkey breast with olive oil and chopped herbs.
    2. Roast in the oven until cooked through and golden brown. Let it rest before slicing and serving.

4. Maple Glazed Ham

  • Ingredients:
    • Pre-cooked ham
    • Maple syrup
    • Dijon mustard
  • Instructions:
    1. Score the ham and brush with a mixture of maple syrup and Dijon mustard.
    2. Bake in the oven until the glaze is caramelized. Slice and serve with your favorite sides.

Sides

5. Garlic Mashed Potatoes

  • Ingredients:
    • Potatoes
    • Butter
    • Garlic
    • Milk
  • Instructions:
    1. Boil and mash the potatoes with butter, minced garlic, and milk until creamy.
    2. Season with salt and pepper, and garnish with chopped chives before serving.

6. Cranberry Pecan Salad

  • Ingredients:
    • Mixed greens
    • Dried cranberries
    • Toasted pecans
    • Feta cheese
  • Instructions:
    1. Toss the greens with cranberries, pecans, and crumbled feta cheese.
    2. Drizzle with balsamic vinaigrette and serve as a refreshing side salad.

Desserts

7. Pumpkin Cheesecake Bars

  • Ingredients:
    • Graham cracker crumbs
    • Cream cheese
    • Pumpkin puree
    • Spices (cinnamon, nutmeg)
  • Instructions:
    1. Prepare a graham cracker crust and top with a creamy pumpkin cheesecake filling.
    2. Bake until set, then chill before cutting into bars and serving.

8. Apple Crisp

  • Ingredients:
    • Sliced apples
    • Brown sugar
    • Oats
    • Butter
  • Instructions:
    1. Mix sliced apples with brown sugar and cinnamon, then top with a buttery oat crumble.
    2. Bake until golden and bubbly. Serve warm with a scoop of vanilla ice cream.

Conclusion

With these quick and easy Thanksgiving recipes, you can spend less time in the kitchen and more time enjoying the holiday with your loved ones. Whether you’re hosting a small gathering or a big feast, these dishes are sure to impress without the stress. Happy Thanksgiving!
