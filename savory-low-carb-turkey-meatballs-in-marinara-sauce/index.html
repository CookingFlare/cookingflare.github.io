Savory Low Carb Turkey Meatballs in Marinara Sauce


If you’re looking for a delicious and healthy meal that’s easy to make, then you’re in for a treat! These savory low carb turkey meatballs in marinara sauce are a perfect combination of lean protein and rich flavors. Whether you’re following a low-carb diet or simply want to enjoy a lighter alternative to traditional meatballs, this recipe is sure to become a favorite in your kitchen.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground turkey
  • 1/2 cup almond flour
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/4 cup finely chopped fresh parsley
  • 1/4 cup finely chopped onion
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 24 ounces marinara sauce

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C) and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.

  2. In a large bowl, combine the ground turkey, almond flour, Parmesan cheese, parsley, onion, garlic, oregano, basil, salt, and black pepper. Use your hands to mix the ingredients until well combined.

  3. Shape the mixture into meatballs, about 1 1/2 inches in diameter, and place them on the prepared baking sheet.

  4. Drizzle the meatballs with olive oil and bake for 20-25 minutes, or until they are cooked through and lightly browned.

  5. While the meatballs are baking, heat the marinara sauce in a large skillet over medium heat.

  6. Once the meatballs are done, transfer them to the skillet with the marinara sauce. Gently toss the meatballs in the sauce until they are well coated.

  7. Let the meatballs simmer in the sauce for an additional 5-10 minutes, allowing the flavors to meld together.

  8. Serve the savory low carb turkey meatballs in marinara sauce hot, garnished with additional Parmesan cheese and fresh parsley if desired.

Why You’ll Love This Recipe

These turkey meatballs are not only low in carbs, but they’re also packed with flavor and nutrients. The almond flour and Parmesan cheese help bind the meatballs together without the need for breadcrumbs, making this recipe gluten-free as well. The lean ground turkey provides a healthy dose of protein, while the marinara sauce adds a burst of tangy sweetness to every bite.

This recipe is versatile and can be served in various ways. Enjoy the meatballs over zucchini noodles for a complete low-carb meal, or pair them with a side of steamed vegetables for a balanced plate. You can also turn these meatballs into a delicious meatball sub by placing them in a whole grain roll and topping them with melted mozzarella cheese.

The best part about these savory low carb turkey meatballs in marinara sauce is that they can be made ahead of time and frozen for later use. Simply prepare the meatballs as directed, allow them to cool, and then store them in an airtight container in the freezer. When you’re ready to enjoy them, simply reheat the meatballs in the marinara sauce for a quick and satisfying meal.

So, whether you’re looking for a new twist on a classic favorite or seeking a healthier alternative to traditional meatballs, give this recipe a try. You’ll be amazed at how easy it is to create a dish that’s both nutritious and delicious, perfect for any occasion.

Enjoy!
