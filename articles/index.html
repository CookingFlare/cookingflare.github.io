Slow Cooker Thai Green Curry Recipes for a Spicy Delight

If you’re a fan of Thai cuisine and love the bold flavors of green curry, then you’re in for a treat! In this blog post, we’ll be sharing not just one, but three delicious and easy-to-make slow cooker Thai green curry recipes that are sure to satisfy your cravings for a spicy delight. So, let’s dive right in! Recipe 1: Slow Cooker Thai Green Curry with Chicken Ingredients: 1 pound boneless, skinless chicken breasts, cut into bite-sized pieces 2 cans (13.5 ounces each) coconut milk 1 cup chicken broth 2 tablespoons green curry paste 1 tablespoon fish sauce 1 tablespoon brown sugar 1 bell pepper, thinly sliced 1 zucchini, sliced 1 cup green beans, trimmed and halved 1 cup bamboo shoots Fresh basil leaves, for garnish Cooked jasmine rice, for serving Instructions: In a slow cooker, combine the coconut milk, chicken broth, green curry paste, fish sauce, and brown sugar. Stir until well combined. Add the chicken, bell pepper, zucchini, green beans, and bamboo shoots to the slow cooker. Stir to coat the ingredients with the curry mixture. Cover and cook on low heat for 4-6 hours or high heat for 2-3 hours, until the chicken is cooked through and the vegetables are tender. Serve the slow cooker Thai green curry over cooked jasmine rice. Garnish with fresh basil leaves. Recipe 2: Slow Cooker Thai Green Curry with Tofu Ingredients: 1 block (14 ounces) firm tofu, drained and cut into cubes 2 cans (13.5 ounces each) coconut milk 1 cup vegetable broth 2 tablespoons green curry paste 1 tablespoon soy sauce 1 tablespoon lime juice 1 tablespoon agave nectar 1 red bell pepper, thinly sliced 1 carrot, thinly sliced 1 cup snow peas Fresh cilantro leaves, for garnish Cooked brown rice, for serving Instructions: In a slow cooker, combine the coconut milk, vegetable broth, green curry paste, soy sauce, lime juice, and agave nectar. Mix well. Add the tofu, bell pepper, carrot, and snow peas to the slow cooker. Stir to combine. Cover and cook on low heat for 4-6 hours or high heat for 2-3 hours, until the tofu is heated through and the vegetables are tender. Serve the slow cooker Thai green curry over cooked brown rice. Garnish with fresh cilantro leaves. Recipe 3: Slow Cooker Thai Green Curry with Shrimp Ingredients: 1 pound shrimp, peeled and deveined 2 cans (13.5 ounces each) coconut milk 1 cup chicken broth 2 tablespoons green curry paste 1 tablespoon fish sauce 1 tablespoon lime juice 1 tablespoon honey 1 onion, thinly sliced 1 red bell pepper, thinly sliced 1 cup sliced mushrooms Fresh mint leaves, for garnish Cooked rice noodles, for serving Instructions: In a slow cooker, combine the coconut milk, chicken broth, green curry paste, fish sauce, lime juice, and honey. Stir until well combined. Add the shrimp, onion, bell pepper, and mushrooms to the slow cooker. Stir to coat the ingredients with the curry mixture. Cover and cook on low heat for 2-3 hours or high heat for 1-2 hours, until the shrimp is pink and cooked through. Serve the slow cooker Thai green curry over cooked rice noodles. Garnish with fresh mint leaves. Now that you have three amazing slow cooker Thai green curry recipes, it’s time to get cooking! Whether you prefer chicken, tofu, or shrimp, these recipes are versatile, flavorful, and perfect for a spicy delight. So, gather your ingredients, set up your slow cooker, and let the magic happen. Enjoy the aromatic flavors and the rich taste of Thai green curry, all with the convenience of your slow cooker. Happy cooking! ...
