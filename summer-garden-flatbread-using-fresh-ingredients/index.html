Summer Garden Flatbread: Using Fresh Ingredients


Summer is in full bloom, and what better way to celebrate the season than with a fresh and flavorful flatbread recipe? In this post, we’ll explore the art of creating a delectable Summer Garden Flatbread, infused with the sweetness of fresh herbs, the tanginess of citrus, and the crunch of toasted vegetables.

Ingredients

For this recipe, you’ll need the following ingredients:

  • 1 1/2 cups warm water
  • 2 teaspoons active dry yeast
  • 3 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup chopped fresh parsley
  • 1 cup chopped fresh basil
  • 1/2 cup chopped fresh mint
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/2 cup sliced red bell pepper
  • 1/2 cup sliced zucchini
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh cilantro
  • 2 tablespoons freshly squeezed lemon juice
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • Salt and pepper, to taste

Instructions

To begin, combine the warm water and yeast in a large mixing bowl. Let the mixture sit for 5-7 minutes, or until the yeast is activated and frothy. Add the olive oil, salt, and 2 cups of the flour to the bowl. Mix until a shaggy dough forms.

Knead the dough on a floured surface for 5-7 minutes, until it becomes smooth and elastic. Place the dough in a greased bowl, cover it with plastic wrap, and let it rise in a warm, draft-free place for 1-2 hours, or until it has doubled in size.

Preheat your oven to 425°F (220°C). Punch down the dough and divide it into 4-6 equal portions, depending on the size of flatbread you prefer. Roll out each portion into a thin circle, about 1/8 inch thick.

Transfer the flatbreads to a baking sheet lined with parchment paper. Brush the tops with the remaining 1 tablespoon of olive oil and sprinkle with salt.

Toppings

To add the toppings, you’ll need to toast the red bell pepper and zucchini slices in a skillet over medium-high heat for 2-3 minutes, or until they’re tender and lightly charred. Chop the toasted vegetables into small pieces and set aside.

In a small bowl, mix together the chopped parsley, basil, mint, Parmesan cheese, garlic, and a pinch of salt and pepper. Spread a spoonful of this mixture onto each flatbread, leaving a 1/2 inch border around the edges.

Drizzle the lemon juice over the herb mixture, followed by the chopped cilantro. Top with the toasted vegetable pieces and a sprinkle of salt and pepper to taste.

Baking and Serving

Bake the flatbreads in the preheated oven for 12-15 minutes, or until they’re golden brown and crispy. Remove from the oven and let cool for a few minutes before slicing and serving.

This Summer Garden Flatbread is perfect for a light and refreshing summer meal or as a snack for a garden party. The combination of fresh herbs, citrus, and toasted vegetables creates a flavorful and textured dish that’s sure to impress. So go ahead, get creative, and experiment with different toppings and ingredients to make this recipe your own!
