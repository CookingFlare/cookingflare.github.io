The Best Sandwiches for a Potluck: Easy Recipes


Potlucks are a wonderful way to gather with friends, family, and coworkers, sharing delicious dishes that everyone will enjoy. When it comes to contributing a dish, sandwiches are always a hit. They’re easy to make in large quantities, customizable to fit dietary needs, and most importantly, they’re incredibly tasty. Whether you’re hosting or attending, here are some of the best sandwich recipes that will be a crowd-pleaser at your next potluck.

Why Sandwiches Are Perfect for Potlucks

Sandwiches offer a variety of benefits that make them perfect for potluck events:

  • Portability: Sandwiches are easy to transport without creating a mess, making them great for gatherings that may not have a lot of serving space.
  • Customizability: You can create sandwiches to fit everyone’s preferences and dietary restrictions, whether vegetarian, gluten-free, or classic meat lovers.
  • No Need for Heating: Most sandwiches can be enjoyed at room temperature, eliminating the need for ovens or microwaves at the event.
  • Simple to Make: With just a few ingredients, you can create something hearty, flavorful, and impressive.

Now, let’s dive into some of the best sandwich recipes you can make for your next potluck.

1. Classic Turkey and Cheese Sandwich

A classic turkey and cheese sandwich is a crowd favorite that never goes out of style. It’s a simple yet delicious option that can be prepared in bulk and served without much fuss.

Ingredients:

  • 2 loaves of whole wheat bread
  • 1 lb sliced turkey breast
  • 1 lb sliced cheddar cheese
  • Lettuce leaves
  • Tomato slices
  • Mayonnaise
  • Dijon mustard
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Directions:

  1. Begin by laying out slices of bread on a clean surface. Spread a thin layer of mayonnaise and Dijon mustard on one slice of bread.
  2. Layer the turkey slices on top of the spread, followed by a slice of cheddar cheese.
  3. Add a few lettuce leaves and a couple of tomato slices to each sandwich.
  4. Season with salt and pepper, then top with the second slice of bread.
  5. Cut each sandwich into halves or quarters for easy serving at the potluck.

Why This Sandwich is a Winner:

This sandwich is a classic for a reason. The combination of lean turkey, creamy cheese, and crisp vegetables makes it satisfying and refreshing. The mayo and mustard give the sandwich a bit of tang and richness, perfect for a potluck spread.

2. Vegetarian Hummus and Veggie Sandwich

For those who prefer vegetarian options, a hummus and veggie sandwich is a great choice. Packed with fresh vegetables and creamy hummus, this sandwich is as nutritious as it is delicious.

Ingredients:

  • 2 loaves of multigrain bread
  • 1 cup hummus (store-bought or homemade)
  • Cucumber slices
  • Red bell pepper slices
  • Avocado slices
  • Spinach leaves
  • Shredded carrots
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Directions:

  1. Spread a generous layer of hummus on each slice of bread.
  2. Arrange the cucumber, bell pepper, avocado, spinach, and shredded carrots evenly across the bread.
  3. Sprinkle a little salt and pepper for added flavor.
  4. Close the sandwich and slice it into halves or smaller sections for easy serving.

Why This Sandwich Works for Potlucks:

Hummus and veggie sandwiches are vibrant and packed with flavor. The crunchy vegetables and creamy hummus create a great balance, and with its plant-based ingredients, this sandwich offers something for vegetarians and health-conscious guests.

3. Chicken Salad Sandwich

Chicken salad sandwiches are a great way to use up leftover chicken and turn it into a creamy, flavorful filling that’s perfect for a potluck.

Ingredients:

  • 2 loaves of white or whole wheat bread
  • 3 cups cooked chicken, shredded
  • ½ cup mayonnaise
  • ¼ cup plain Greek yogurt
  • 1 tablespoon Dijon mustard
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 1 cup celery, diced
  • 1 cup grapes, halved
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Directions:

  1. In a large bowl, combine the shredded chicken, mayonnaise, Greek yogurt, Dijon mustard, and lemon juice.
  2. Add the diced celery and halved grapes for a touch of crunch and sweetness.
  3. Mix everything together and season with salt and pepper to taste.
  4. Spread the chicken salad onto slices of bread and close the sandwich.
  5. Slice into manageable portions for easy sharing.

Why It’s Perfect for a Potluck:

The creamy texture of the chicken salad, paired with the freshness of celery and sweetness of grapes, makes this sandwich a satisfying option. It’s a wonderful dish for those who enjoy rich, savory flavors with a touch of sweetness.

4. BBQ Pulled Pork Sandwich

If you’re looking to make a sandwich that really packs a punch, a BBQ pulled pork sandwich is a surefire winner. Smoky, tangy, and tender, this sandwich is sure to be the star of the potluck.

Ingredients:

  • 2 loaves of hamburger buns
  • 3 cups cooked pulled pork (preferably slow-cooked)
  • 1 cup BBQ sauce
  • Coleslaw (optional)
  • Pickles (optional)

Directions:

  1. If you haven’t done so already, slow cook or heat the pulled pork and mix it with your favorite BBQ sauce.
  2. Toast the hamburger buns slightly to keep them from becoming soggy.
  3. Pile the pulled pork onto the bottom bun, then top it with coleslaw and pickles if desired.
  4. Place the top bun on and serve.

Why This Sandwich Stands Out:

BBQ pulled pork sandwiches bring bold flavors and a hearty filling that’s guaranteed to satisfy a crowd. The smoky BBQ sauce combined with the creamy coleslaw offers an irresistible combination of textures and flavors. This sandwich is best served with a side of chips or fries.

5. Caprese Sandwich

If you’re looking for a lighter, fresher option, the Caprese sandwich is a delicious choice. Inspired by the classic Italian Caprese salad, this sandwich combines juicy tomatoes, creamy mozzarella, and fragrant basil for a fresh, summery treat.

Ingredients:

  • 2 loaves of ciabatta bread
  • 2 large tomatoes, sliced
  • 8 oz fresh mozzarella, sliced
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • Balsamic glaze
  • Olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Directions:

  1. Cut the ciabatta bread into sandwich-sized pieces and lightly toast if desired.
  2. Layer the tomato slices, fresh mozzarella, and basil leaves on the bread.
  3. Drizzle with olive oil and balsamic glaze for an extra burst of flavor.
  4. Sprinkle with salt and pepper to taste and close the sandwich.

Why It’s Ideal for Potlucks:

This sandwich is light, refreshing, and bursting with flavor. The combination of juicy tomatoes, creamy mozzarella, and aromatic basil makes it a perfect option for warmer months, or whenever you need a sandwich that’s as fresh as it is tasty.

6. Roast Beef and Horseradish Sandwich

For those who enjoy a bit of spice, a roast beef and horseradish sandwich is an exciting addition to any potluck spread. The sharpness of the horseradish pairs perfectly with the savory roast beef, making this sandwich a flavorful choice.

Ingredients:

  • 2 loaves of rye bread
  • 1 lb roast beef, thinly sliced
  • 2 tablespoons horseradish sauce
  • 1 tablespoon Dijon mustard
  • 1 tablespoon mayonnaise
  • Lettuce leaves

Directions:

  1. Spread the horseradish sauce, Dijon mustard, and mayonnaise onto the rye bread.
  2. Layer the roast beef on top of the sauce.
  3. Add a few lettuce leaves for a fresh crunch.
  4. Top with the second slice of bread and cut into smaller portions for serving.

Why It’s a Great Potluck Choice:

The spiciness of the horseradish combined with the tender roast beef offers a delicious contrast of flavors. This sandwich is perfect for guests who appreciate bold, zesty flavors and hearty fillings.

7. Tuna Salad Sandwich

Tuna salad is another classic sandwich filling that is easy to make and can feed a crowd. It’s creamy, satisfying, and can be made with just a few ingredients.

Ingredients:

  • 2 loaves of whole grain or white bread
  • 2 cans of tuna in water or oil, drained
  • ¼ cup mayonnaise
  • 2 tablespoons Dijon mustard
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 1 tablespoon pickle relish
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Directions:

  1. In a large mixing bowl, combine the tuna, mayonnaise, Dijon mustard, lemon juice, and pickle relish.
  2. Stir everything together until well mixed.
  3. Spread the tuna salad mixture onto slices of bread and close the sandwich.
  4. Cut into halves or smaller pieces for easy serving.

Why It’s a Perfect Option:

Tuna salad sandwiches are always a crowd favorite at potlucks. They’re easy to prepare in large batches and offer a creamy, flavorful option for guests who prefer seafood. The addition of relish and lemon juice provides a tangy twist that elevates the classic tuna salad.

Conclusion

Sandwiches are an ideal choice for any potluck, offering a variety of flavors, textures, and ingredients to suit everyone’s tastes. Whether you go for a classic turkey and cheese sandwich, a vegetarian hummus and veggie sandwich, or a bold BBQ pulled pork option, these recipes are sure to impress. The best part? They’re easy to prepare in advance and can be served at room temperature, making them perfect for your next gathering. So, gather your ingredients, get creative, and enjoy the endless possibilities for delicious sandwiches at your next potluck.
