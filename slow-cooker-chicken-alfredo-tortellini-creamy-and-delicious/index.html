Slow Cooker Chicken Alfredo Tortellini: Creamy and Delicious


If you’re a fan of creamy and indulgent pasta dishes, then this Slow Cooker Chicken Alfredo Tortellini recipe is a must-try! It’s the perfect combination of tender chicken, cheesy tortellini, and a rich, creamy Alfredo sauce, all cooked to perfection in a slow cooker. This recipe is not only delicious but also incredibly convenient, making it a great option for a comforting weeknight dinner or a cozy weekend meal with family and friends.

Ingredients:

  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 20 oz refrigerated cheese tortellini
  • 2 cups chicken broth
  • 2 cups heavy cream
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon Italian seasoning
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley, chopped (for garnish)

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the Slow Cooker: Begin by spraying the inside of your slow cooker with non-stick cooking spray to prevent the pasta from sticking.

  2. Add the Chicken: Place the chicken breasts in the bottom of the slow cooker.

  3. Create the Alfredo Sauce: In a saucepan, melt the butter over medium heat. Add the minced garlic and sauté for a minute until fragrant. Pour in the heavy cream, chicken broth, and grated Parmesan cheese, stirring until the cheese has melted and the sauce is smooth. Season with salt, pepper, and Italian seasoning.

  4. Combine Ingredients in the Slow Cooker: Pour the Alfredo sauce over the chicken in the slow cooker.

  5. Cook Low and Slow: Cover the slow cooker and cook on low for 4-5 hours or until the chicken is cooked through and tender.

  6. Add the Tortellini: About 30 minutes before serving, stir in the refrigerated cheese tortellini. Cover and continue cooking until the tortellini is tender.

  7. Shred the Chicken: Once the chicken is cooked through, use two forks to shred it into bite-sized pieces, allowing it to soak up the creamy Alfredo sauce.

  8. Serve and Enjoy: Serve the Slow Cooker Chicken Alfredo Tortellini in bowls, garnished with freshly chopped parsley for a pop of color and freshness.

Tips and Tricks:

  • For an extra flavor boost, consider adding a sprinkle of red pepper flakes or a dash of nutmeg to the Alfredo sauce.
  • If you prefer, you can use boneless, skinless chicken thighs instead of chicken breasts for a slightly different flavor and texture.
  • Leftovers can be stored in an airtight container in the refrigerator for up to 3 days. Simply reheat in the microwave or on the stovetop, adding a splash of milk or cream to loosen up the sauce.

Conclusion:

This Slow Cooker Chicken Alfredo Tortellini recipe is a fantastic way to enjoy a comforting and satisfying meal with minimal effort. The slow cooker does all the work, infusing the chicken, tortellini, and creamy Alfredo sauce with rich, savory flavors. Whether you’re cooking for a crowd or simply craving a cozy night in, this dish is sure to become a new favorite in your recipe repertoire. So, grab your slow cooker and get ready to savor every creamy, delicious bite of this delightful pasta creation!
