Broccoli and Cheddar Stuffed Crust Pizza: A Cheesy Delight


If you’re a fan of pizza and cheese, then get ready for a mouthwatering treat. This recipe combines the goodness of broccoli, the richness of cheddar, and the crispiness of a stuffed crust pizza. It’s a cheesy delight that will leave you craving for more.

Ingredients

For the dough:

  • 2 ½ cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 2 ¼ teaspoons active dry yeast
  • 1 cup warm water
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil

For the filling:

  • 2 cups broccoli florets, blanched and chopped
  • 2 cups shredded cheddar cheese

For the sauce:

  • 1 cup tomato sauce
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 475°F (245°C).

  2. In a large mixing bowl, combine the flour, sugar, salt, and yeast. Mix well.

  3. Gradually add the warm water and olive oil to the dry ingredients. Stir until the dough comes together.

  4. Transfer the dough onto a lightly floured surface and knead for about 5 minutes until it becomes smooth and elastic.

  5. Place the dough in a greased bowl, cover it with a clean kitchen towel, and let it rise in a warm spot for about 1 hour or until it doubles in size.

  6. While the dough is rising, prepare the sauce by combining the tomato sauce, minced garlic, dried oregano, dried basil, salt, and pepper in a small bowl. Mix well.

  7. Once the dough has risen, punch it down and divide it into two equal portions.

  8. Roll out one portion of the dough into a circle, making sure it’s slightly larger than your pizza pan.

  9. Place the rolled-out dough onto the pan, making sure it covers the bottom and sides.

  10. Sprinkle half of the shredded cheddar cheese on the bottom of the crust, leaving a small border around the edges.

  11. Spread half of the blanched and chopped broccoli on top of the cheese.

  12. Fold the edges of the dough over the cheese and broccoli, pressing them together to seal the filling inside.

  13. Roll out the second portion of the dough into a circle and place it on top of the stuffed crust, pressing the edges to seal.

  14. Brush the top of the crust with olive oil for a golden finish.

  15. Spread the tomato sauce evenly over the top crust, leaving a small border around the edges.

  16. Sprinkle the remaining shredded cheddar cheese on top of the sauce.

  17. Add the rest of the blanched and chopped broccoli on top of the cheese.

  18. Bake the pizza in the preheated oven for about 15-20 minutes, or until the crust is golden brown and the cheese is bubbly and melted.

  19. Remove the pizza from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes before slicing and serving.

Conclusion

Broccoli and Cheddar Stuffed Crust Pizza is a delightful twist on a classic favorite. The combination of the cheesy stuffed crust, the vibrant broccoli, and the flavorful tomato sauce creates a taste sensation that will satisfy even the most discerning pizza lover. Whether you’re entertaining guests or simply indulging in a homemade treat, this pizza is sure to impress. So, gather your ingredients and get ready to enjoy a cheesy delight that will leave you craving for more.
