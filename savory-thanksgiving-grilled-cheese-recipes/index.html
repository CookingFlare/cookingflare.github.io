Savory Thanksgiving Grilled Cheese Recipes


Thanksgiving is just around the corner, and it’s time to start planning your holiday menu. While everyone loves the traditional turkey and stuffing, why not add a twist to your Thanksgiving feast this year with some savory grilled cheese recipes? These cheesy delights will not only satisfy your taste buds but also add a touch of comfort to your holiday spread. So, let’s dive into these mouthwatering recipes that are sure to impress your guests!

1. Cranberry Brie Grilled Cheese

Ingredients:

  • 8 slices of sourdough bread
  • 1 wheel of Brie cheese, sliced
  • 1 cup of cranberry sauce
  • 4 tablespoons of butter

Instructions:

  1. Take two slices of bread and spread cranberry sauce on one side of each slice.
  2. Layer the Brie cheese slices on top of the cranberry sauce.
  3. Place the second slice of bread on top to form a sandwich.
  4. Heat a skillet over medium heat and melt 1 tablespoon of butter.
  5. Place the sandwich in the skillet and cook until the bread is golden brown and the cheese has melted, about 2-3 minutes per side.
  6. Repeat the process with the remaining ingredients.
  7. Serve hot and enjoy the gooey goodness of cranberry and Brie in every bite!

2. Turkey and Stuffing Grilled Cheese

Ingredients:

  • 8 slices of whole wheat bread
  • 2 cups of leftover turkey, shredded
  • 1 cup of leftover stuffing
  • 1 cup of shredded cheddar cheese
  • 4 tablespoons of butter

Instructions:

  1. Take two slices of bread and spread a layer of leftover stuffing on one side of each slice.
  2. Layer the shredded turkey on top of the stuffing.
  3. Sprinkle shredded cheddar cheese over the turkey.
  4. Place the second slice of bread on top to form a sandwich.
  5. Heat a skillet over medium heat and melt 1 tablespoon of butter.
  6. Place the sandwich in the skillet and cook until the bread is golden brown and the cheese has melted, about 2-3 minutes per side.
  7. Repeat the process with the remaining ingredients.
  8. Serve hot and savor the flavors of Thanksgiving in a classic grilled cheese!

3. Sweet Potato and Sage Grilled Cheese

Ingredients:

  • 8 slices of multigrain bread
  • 2 cups of mashed sweet potatoes
  • 1 cup of shredded Gruyere cheese
  • 1 tablespoon of fresh sage leaves, chopped
  • 4 tablespoons of butter

Instructions:

  1. Take two slices of bread and spread a layer of mashed sweet potatoes on one side of each slice.
  2. Sprinkle shredded Gruyere cheese over the sweet potatoes.
  3. Add a sprinkle of chopped fresh sage leaves on top.
  4. Place the second slice of bread on top to form a sandwich.
  5. Heat a skillet over medium heat and melt 1 tablespoon of butter.
  6. Place the sandwich in the skillet and cook until the bread is golden brown and the cheese has melted, about 2-3 minutes per side.
  7. Repeat the process with the remaining ingredients.
  8. Serve hot and relish the combination of sweet potatoes, sage, and melted cheese!

Conclusion

These savory Thanksgiving grilled cheese recipes are a delightful way to incorporate some cheesy goodness into your holiday celebrations. Whether you go for the tangy cranberry and Brie, the comforting turkey and stuffing, or the earthy sweet potato and sage, these grilled cheese sandwiches are sure to be a hit with your family and friends. So, get creative in the kitchen this Thanksgiving and indulge in the ultimate comfort food experience. Happy grilling!
