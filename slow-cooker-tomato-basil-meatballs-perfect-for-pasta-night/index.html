Slow Cooker Tomato Basil Meatballs: Perfect for Pasta Night


There’s something truly comforting about a big bowl of pasta topped with delicious, homemade meatballs. The savory aroma of simmering tomato sauce and the tender texture of perfectly cooked meatballs can turn any ordinary evening into a special occasion. This slow cooker tomato basil meatball recipe is designed to make your pasta night effortless and utterly satisfying. Let’s dive into the details of creating this classic dish that’s sure to become a family favorite.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1/2 cup breadcrumbs
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon onion powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 2 (24-ounce) jars of your favorite marinara sauce
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh basil
  • 1 pound spaghetti or pasta of your choice

Instructions

  1. In a large bowl, combine the ground beef, breadcrumbs, Parmesan cheese, egg, garlic powder, onion powder, salt, and black pepper. Mix the ingredients thoroughly but avoid overworking the meat, as this can result in tough meatballs.

  2. Shape the mixture into meatballs, about 1 to 1.5 inches in diameter. This recipe should yield approximately 20 meatballs.

  3. Pour one jar of marinara sauce into the slow cooker, spreading it evenly across the bottom. Gently place the meatballs into the slow cooker, ensuring they are submerged in the sauce.

  4. Pour the second jar of marinara sauce over the meatballs, covering them completely. Sprinkle the chopped fresh basil on top.

  5. Cover the slow cooker and cook on low for 6-8 hours, allowing the flavors to meld together and the meatballs to become tender and flavorful.

  6. About 30 minutes before serving, cook the pasta according to the package instructions.

  7. Once the pasta is cooked al dente, drain it and toss it gently with a small amount of olive oil to prevent sticking.

  8. Serve the slow cooker tomato basil meatballs over the pasta, garnishing with additional grated Parmesan cheese and basil if desired.

Tips and Variations

  • For a lighter option, you can use ground turkey or chicken in place of beef.
  • Experiment with different herbs and spices to customize the flavor profile of the meatballs to your liking.
  • If you prefer a thicker sauce, you can reduce the amount of marinara sauce used in the slow cooker.

Why Choose Slow Cooker Meatballs?

The slow cooker method offers several advantages when preparing meatballs. Firstly, the low and slow cooking process allows the flavors of the meat and sauce to meld together, resulting in incredibly tender and flavorful meatballs. Additionally, using a slow cooker means you can prepare this dish in the morning and return to a delicious, ready-to-eat meal in the evening, making it perfect for busy days.

In Conclusion

This slow cooker tomato basil meatball recipe is a wonderful way to elevate your pasta night without spending hours in the kitchen. The simplicity of the ingredients and the hands-off cooking process make it an ideal choice for a satisfying and comforting meal. Whether you’re cooking for family, friends, or simply treating yourself, these meatballs are sure to be a hit. Enjoy the process of creating this classic dish and savor the heartwarming flavors it brings to your table.
