Raspberry Tiramisu with Ladyfingers and a Sprinkle of Cocoa Powder Recipe: A Creamy and Decadent Vegan Dessert

3 min · 492 words

If you’re craving a luscious, indulgent dessert that’s both vegan and bursting with flavor, then this Raspberry Tiramisu with Ladyfingers and a Sprinkle of Cocoa Powder is the perfect recipe for you. Combining the classic elements of tiramisu with the vibrant, tangy flavor of raspberries, this dessert is a delightful twist on the traditional Italian favorite. Whether you’re hosting a dinner party, celebrating a special occasion, or simply treating yourself, this creamy and decadent dessert is sure to impress your taste buds and those of your guests.

Ingredients

For the Raspberry Puree:

  • 2 cups fresh raspberries
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice

For the Tiramisu Cream:

  • 1 1/2 cups raw cashews, soaked overnight
  • 1/2 cup coconut cream
  • 1/4 cup maple syrup
  • 1/4 cup almond milk
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt

Additional Ingredients:

  • 1 cup brewed espresso, cooled
  • 1 tablespoon coffee liqueur (optional)
  • 1 package vegan ladyfingers
  • Cocoa powder for dusting

Instructions

Step 1: Prepare the Raspberry Puree

  1. In a saucepan, combine the fresh raspberries, granulated sugar, and lemon juice.
  2. Cook over medium heat, stirring occasionally, until the raspberries break down and the mixture thickens, about 10-15 minutes.
  3. Remove from heat and let the raspberry puree cool completely.

Step 2: Make the Tiramisu Cream

  1. In a high-speed blender, combine the soaked cashews, coconut cream, maple syrup, almond milk, vanilla extract, and salt.
  2. Blend until smooth and creamy, scraping down the sides of the blender as needed.

Step 3: Assemble the Raspberry Tiramisu

  1. In a shallow dish, mix the cooled brewed espresso with the coffee liqueur, if using.
  2. Dip each ladyfinger into the espresso mixture, ensuring they are soaked but not soggy.
  3. Arrange a layer of soaked ladyfingers in the bottom of a serving dish.
  4. Spread half of the tiramisu cream over the ladyfingers, then drizzle half of the raspberry puree on top.
  5. Repeat the layers with the remaining ladyfingers, tiramisu cream, and raspberry puree.
  6. Cover the dish with plastic wrap and refrigerate for at least 4 hours, or preferably overnight, to allow the flavors to meld together.

Step 4: Serve

  1. Before serving, dust the top of the tiramisu with a generous sprinkle of cocoa powder.
  2. Slice and serve the Raspberry Tiramisu, savoring every creamy, fruity bite.

Conclusion

This Raspberry Tiramisu with Ladyfingers and a Sprinkle of Cocoa Powder is a delightful vegan dessert that combines the richness of traditional tiramisu with the bright, fruity flavor of raspberries. The creamy cashew-based tiramisu cream, layered with espresso-soaked ladyfingers and tangy raspberry puree, creates a symphony of flavors and textures that will leave you wanting more. Whether you follow a vegan lifestyle or simply enjoy exploring new culinary creations, this decadent dessert is a must-try. Impress your friends and family with this elegant and delicious Raspberry Tiramisu, and savor the satisfaction of creating a show-stopping dessert with a vegan twist.
