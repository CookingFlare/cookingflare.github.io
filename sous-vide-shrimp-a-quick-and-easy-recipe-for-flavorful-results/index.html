Sous Vide Shrimp: A Quick and Easy Recipe for Flavorful Results


If you’re a seafood lover looking for a simple yet delicious recipe, then look no further than Sous Vide Shrimp. This cooking technique ensures perfectly cooked shrimp every time, with a tender and succulent texture that will leave your taste buds dancing. In this blog post, we’ll guide you through the steps to create this flavorful dish that will impress your family and friends.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound of fresh shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 2 tablespoons of olive oil
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon of paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon of salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon of black pepper
  • 1 tablespoon of fresh parsley, chopped

Instructions

  1. Preheat your sous vide water bath to 135°F (57°C). This temperature ensures that the shrimp cooks evenly and remains tender.

  2. In a bowl, combine the olive oil, minced garlic, paprika, salt, and black pepper. Mix well to create a flavorful marinade.

  3. Place the peeled and deveined shrimp into a resealable plastic bag. Pour the marinade over the shrimp, ensuring that they are evenly coated. Seal the bag, removing as much air as possible.

  4. Once the water bath has reached the desired temperature, carefully lower the bag of shrimp into the water. It’s important to fully submerge the bag to ensure even cooking.

  5. Cook the shrimp in the sous vide water bath for 30 minutes. This gentle cooking method allows the shrimp to reach the perfect doneness without sacrificing flavor or texture.

  6. After 30 minutes, remove the bag of shrimp from the water bath. Open the bag and carefully transfer the shrimp to a serving platter, discarding any excess marinade.

  7. Garnish the shrimp with freshly chopped parsley for a vibrant touch of freshness.

  8. Serve your sous vide shrimp immediately, and enjoy the succulent and flavorful results. This dish pairs well with a side of steamed vegetables, rice, or a light salad.

Tips for Success

  • Use fresh shrimp for the best results. The freshness of the shrimp will greatly impact the overall flavor and texture of the dish.

  • Experiment with different seasonings to customize the flavor profile of your sous vide shrimp. Consider adding lemon zest, chili flakes, or even a dash of your favorite hot sauce for an extra kick.

  • If you prefer a more intense garlic flavor, you can increase the amount of minced garlic in the marinade.

  • For added convenience, you can prepare the shrimp marinade in advance and store it in the refrigerator until you’re ready to cook.

  • Sous vide shrimp can be enjoyed as an appetizer, main course, or even added to salads or pasta dishes for a burst of flavor.

Conclusion

Sous Vide Shrimp is a quick and easy recipe that delivers exceptional flavor and texture. By using the sous vide cooking technique, you can achieve perfectly cooked shrimp without any guesswork. The tender and succulent results will leave you wanting more. So, gather your ingredients, fire up your sous vide water bath, and get ready to enjoy a mouthwatering seafood dish that will impress even the most discerning palate.
