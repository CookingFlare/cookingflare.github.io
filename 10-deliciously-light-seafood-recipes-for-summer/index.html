10 Deliciously Light Seafood Recipes for Summer


As summer approaches, it’s time to embrace the light and refreshing flavors of the season. Seafood, with its natural lightness, is the perfect choice for summer meals. Not only is it packed with protein and omega-3 fatty acids, but it also cooks quickly and pairs wonderfully with seasonal fruits and vegetables. In this post, we’ll explore ten deliciously light seafood recipes that will keep you satisfied without weighing you down. Each recipe is designed to be easy to prepare, making them ideal for summer entertaining or a casual dinner at home.

1. Grilled Lemon Herb Shrimp Skewers

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound large shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons fresh lemon juice
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley, for garnish

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, combine olive oil, lemon juice, minced garlic, oregano, salt, and pepper.
  2. Add the shrimp and toss to coat. Allow marinating for 15-20 minutes.
  3. Preheat your grill to medium-high heat.
  4. Thread the shrimp onto skewers, about 5-6 shrimp per skewer.
  5. Grill the skewers for 2-3 minutes on each side until the shrimp are pink and opaque.
  6. Garnish with fresh parsley before serving. Enjoy these skewers with a side of grilled vegetables or a light salad.

2. Ceviche with Mango and Avocado

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound fresh white fish (like tilapia or snapper), diced
  • 1 cup fresh lime juice
  • 1 mango, diced
  • 1 avocado, diced
  • 1 small red onion, finely chopped
  • 1 jalapeño, seeded and minced
  • Fresh cilantro, chopped
  • Salt to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a glass bowl, combine the diced fish and lime juice. Make sure the fish is fully submerged. Cover and refrigerate for 2-3 hours, until the fish is opaque.
  2. Once the fish is ready, drain excess lime juice and add the mango, avocado, red onion, jalapeño, and cilantro.
  3. Season with salt to taste and gently toss to combine.
  4. Serve chilled with tortilla chips or on its own as a refreshing appetizer.

3. Zucchini Noodles with Lemon Garlic Scallops

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound sea scallops
  • 4 medium zucchinis, spiralized
  • 3 tablespoons olive oil, divided
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 2 tablespoons fresh lemon juice
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Red pepper flakes (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Heat 2 tablespoons of olive oil in a skillet over medium heat. Add minced garlic and sauté until fragrant.
  2. Pat scallops dry with a paper towel, season with salt and pepper, and add to the skillet. Cook for 2-3 minutes on each side until golden brown. Remove scallops and set aside.
  3. In the same skillet, add the remaining olive oil and spiralized zucchini. Sauté for 2-3 minutes until just tender.
  4. Add lemon juice, salt, and red pepper flakes, and toss to combine. Return the scallops to the skillet and mix everything gently.
  5. Serve immediately for a light, satisfying meal.

4. Mediterranean Tuna Salad

Ingredients:

  • 1 can (15 ounces) tuna in olive oil, drained
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1 cucumber, diced
  • 1/4 red onion, finely chopped
  • 1/4 cup kalamata olives, pitted and sliced
  • 2 tablespoons capers, drained
  • 1/4 cup fresh parsley, chopped
  • 3 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon red wine vinegar
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a large bowl, combine the tuna, cherry tomatoes, cucumber, red onion, olives, capers, and parsley.
  2. In a separate small bowl, whisk together olive oil, red wine vinegar, salt, and pepper.
  3. Pour the dressing over the tuna mixture and gently toss to combine.
  4. Serve chilled or at room temperature, perfect for a light lunch or picnic.

5. Baked Cod with Tomato Basil Relish

Ingredients:

  • 4 cod fillets
  • 2 cups cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1/4 cup fresh basil, chopped
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon balsamic vinegar
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 400°F (200°C). Line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
  2. Place cod fillets on the baking sheet and season with salt and pepper.
  3. In a bowl, combine cherry tomatoes, basil, olive oil, and balsamic vinegar. Spoon the mixture over the cod fillets.
  4. Bake for 15-20 minutes, or until the fish flakes easily with a fork.
  5. Serve warm, accompanied by a side of steamed vegetables or a light grain salad.

6. Shrimp and Avocado Salad

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 2 avocados, diced
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1/4 red onion, finely chopped
  • 1 lime, juiced
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a skillet, heat olive oil over medium heat. Add the shrimp and season with salt and pepper. Cook until pink and opaque, about 3-4 minutes. Remove from heat and let cool.
  2. In a large bowl, combine diced avocados, cherry tomatoes, red onion, lime juice, and cooked shrimp.
  3. Gently toss to combine and serve immediately for a fresh and vibrant meal.

7. Spicy Fish Tacos with Slaw

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound white fish (like cod or tilapia)
  • 1 tablespoon chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 8 small corn tortillas
  • 2 cups cabbage slaw
  • 1/4 cup fresh cilantro, chopped
  • Lime wedges, for serving

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your grill or skillet on medium-high heat. Season the fish with chili powder, cumin, salt, and pepper.
  2. Cook the fish for about 4-5 minutes on each side until cooked through and flaky.
  3. Warm the corn tortillas on the grill or in a pan.
  4. Assemble the tacos by placing pieces of fish in each tortilla, topping with cabbage slaw and cilantro.
  5. Serve with lime wedges for squeezing over the top.

8. Sesame Crusted Ahi Tuna

Ingredients:

  • 2 ahi tuna steaks
  • 1/4 cup sesame seeds
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Green onions, for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Season the tuna steaks with salt and pepper. Brush lightly with soy sauce.
  2. Spread sesame seeds on a plate and press each steak into the seeds to coat both sides.
  3. Heat olive oil in a skillet over medium-high heat. Sear the tuna steaks for 1-2 minutes on each side, depending on your preferred doneness.
  4. Remove from heat and let rest for a minute. Slice and serve garnished with green onions.

9. Coconut Curry Mussels

Ingredients:

  • 2 pounds mussels, cleaned and debearded
  • 1 can (14 ounces) coconut milk
  • 2 tablespoons red curry paste
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 tablespoon ginger, grated
  • Fresh cilantro, for garnish
  • Lime wedges, for serving

Instructions:

  1. In a large pot, heat a splash of oil over medium heat. Add garlic and ginger and sauté until fragrant.
  2. Stir in the red curry paste and coconut milk, bringing to a simmer.
  3. Add the mussels, cover the pot, and cook for 5-7 minutes until the mussels open.
  4. Serve the mussels in bowls, topped with fresh cilantro and lime wedges for squeezing.

10. Grilled Salmon with Avocado Salsa

Ingredients:

  • 4 salmon fillets
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 avocado, diced
  • 1/2 red onion, diced
  • 1/2 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1 lime, juiced
  • Fresh cilantro, chopped

Instructions:

  1. Preheat grill to medium-high heat. Brush salmon fillets with olive oil and season with salt and pepper.
  2. Grill the salmon for about 4-5 minutes on each side, or until cooked through.
  3. In a bowl, combine avocado, red onion, cherry tomatoes, lime juice, and cilantro. Gently mix to combine.
  4. Serve the grilled salmon topped with the avocado salsa for a delightful and light summer dish.

Conclusion

Summer is the ideal time to enjoy the fresh, vibrant flavors of seafood. With these ten deliciously light seafood recipes, you can create healthy and satisfying meals that are perfect for warm weather. Whether you’re hosting a summer barbecue or enjoying a quiet evening at home, these recipes are sure to impress. So, gather your ingredients, fire up the grill, and savor the taste of summer with these delightful seafood dishes. Enjoy!
