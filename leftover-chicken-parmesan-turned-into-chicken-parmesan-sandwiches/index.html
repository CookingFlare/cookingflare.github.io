Leftover Chicken Parmesan Turned into Chicken Parmesan Sandwiches


When it comes to cooking, one of the greatest joys is creating delicious meals from leftovers. Not only does this practice minimize food waste, but it also allows you to indulge in new flavors and textures. Today, we’re diving into a delightful twist on a classic: transforming leftover Chicken Parmesan into scrumptious Chicken Parmesan Sandwiches. Perfect for lunch, dinner, or a quick snack, these sandwiches are packed with flavor and sure to impress.

Why Use Leftover Chicken Parmesan?

Chicken Parmesan is a beloved dish characterized by its crispy breaded chicken, rich marinara sauce, and gooey melted cheese. However, sometimes we make too much, or we simply want to switch things up a bit. Using leftover Chicken Parmesan for sandwiches not only saves time but also enhances the flavor profile. The chicken absorbs the marinara and cheese, making it even more delicious the next day!

Ingredients for Chicken Parmesan Sandwiches

To make these sandwiches, you’ll need the following ingredients:

For the Sandwiches:

  • 2-4 pieces of leftover Chicken Parmesan (chicken breasts coated with marinara and cheese)
  • 4-6 slices of your favorite bread (ciabatta, hoagie rolls, or sourdough work well)
  • 1 cup of shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Fresh basil leaves (optional, for garnish)
  • Olive oil or butter (for grilling)

For the Marinara Sauce (if you want to enhance the flavor):

  • 1 can (15 oz) crushed tomatoes
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • A pinch of sugar (optional, to balance acidity)

Instructions

Step 1: Preparing the Marinara Sauce (Optional)

If you’d like to elevate your Chicken Parmesan Sandwiches even further, consider making a quick marinara sauce to drizzle on top or use as a dip.

  1. Sauté Garlic: In a medium saucepan, heat a tablespoon of olive oil over medium heat. Add the minced garlic and sauté for about 30 seconds until fragrant.

  2. Add Tomatoes and Seasoning: Pour in the crushed tomatoes, oregano, salt, pepper, and sugar if desired. Stir to combine.

  3. Simmer: Allow the sauce to simmer for about 15-20 minutes, stirring occasionally, until it thickens slightly. Adjust seasoning to taste.

Step 2: Preparing the Chicken Parmesan

  1. Reheat the Chicken: Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C). Place the leftover Chicken Parmesan on a baking sheet and warm in the oven for 10-15 minutes until heated through. If you want the cheese to melt a bit more, place it under the broiler for a minute or two—just keep an eye on it!

  2. Slice the Chicken: Once heated, carefully slice the chicken into manageable pieces that will fit nicely on your bread.

Step 3: Assembling the Sandwiches

  1. Choose Your Bread: Select your preferred bread and slice it in half. If you’re using thick slices, you may want to toast them lightly in a pan or toaster to enhance their texture.

  2. Layer the Chicken: Place a piece or two of the reheated Chicken Parmesan on the bottom half of the bread.

  3. Add More Cheese: Sprinkle shredded mozzarella cheese generously over the chicken. If you want extra cheesiness, feel free to add more!

  4. Top with Marinara Sauce: Drizzle some of your homemade marinara sauce on top of the cheese or keep it on the side as a dipping sauce.

  5. Finish with Fresh Basil: If you’re using fresh basil, place a few leaves on top of the sauce for a burst of freshness.

  6. Cover and Grill: Close the sandwich with the top half of the bread. Heat a skillet over medium heat and add a little olive oil or butter. Place the sandwich in the skillet, pressing down gently.

Step 4: Grilling the Sandwich

  1. Toast to Perfection: Grill each side for about 3-4 minutes or until the bread is golden brown and crispy, and the cheese has melted. If you prefer a more melty sandwich, cover the skillet with a lid to retain heat.

  2. Monitor Carefully: Keep an eye on the sandwich to prevent burning. If it browns too quickly, lower the heat.

Step 5: Serving Your Chicken Parmesan Sandwiches

  1. Slice and Serve: Once the sandwiches are grilled to perfection, remove them from the skillet and let them cool for a minute. Slice them in half for easy eating and serve with extra marinara sauce on the side for dipping.

  2. Pair with Sides: These sandwiches are incredibly versatile and can be paired with a variety of sides. Consider serving them with a light salad, crispy French fries, or a side of roasted vegetables for a complete meal.

Tips for the Best Chicken Parmesan Sandwiches

  1. Experiment with Bread: Don’t be afraid to switch up the bread! Garlic bread, focaccia, or even a baguette can add an exciting twist to the classic sandwich.

  2. Add Veggies: For a bit of crunch and freshness, consider adding sliced tomatoes, arugula, or roasted peppers to your sandwich.

  3. Make It Spicy: If you enjoy a little heat, add some red pepper flakes to your marinara sauce or include jalapeños for a spicy kick.

  4. Use Different Cheeses: While mozzarella is traditional, feel free to experiment with provolone, fontina, or even a sprinkle of parmesan for added flavor.

  5. Meal Prep: These sandwiches are great for meal prep! Assemble the components ahead of time and store them separately in the fridge. When you’re ready to eat, simply reheat and grill.

Conclusion

Leftover Chicken Parmesan turned into Chicken Parmesan Sandwiches is a creative and delicious way to enjoy a classic dish in a new form. Whether you’re rushing for lunch or looking for a quick dinner option, these sandwiches are packed with flavor and comfort. Plus, they make great use of leftovers, reducing waste and giving you more bang for your buck in the kitchen.

So next time you find yourself with extra Chicken Parmesan, remember this easy recipe. Your taste buds will thank you, and your family and friends will be impressed by your culinary creativity. Enjoy your cooking, and happy eating!
