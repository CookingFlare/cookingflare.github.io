Tasty Nut-Free Cucumber Salad Recipe


If you’re searching for a refreshing, nut-free salad recipe to add to your repertoire, look no further! This Tasty Nut-Free Cucumber Salad is the perfect blend of crisp, cool cucumbers, tangy vinegar, and zesty herbs. It’s a delightful dish that can be served as a side or enjoyed on its own.

Ingredients

  • 3 medium cucumbers, thinly sliced
  • 1/2 red onion, thinly sliced
  • 1/4 cup white vinegar
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon honey
  • 1 teaspoon Dijon mustard
  • 2 tablespoons fresh dill, chopped
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Cucumbers and Onions: Start by slicing the cucumbers and red onion into thin, uniform slices. You can use a mandoline for even slices, but a sharp knife works just as well.

  2. Make the Dressing: In a small bowl, whisk together the white vinegar, olive oil, honey, Dijon mustard, and chopped dill. This tangy and slightly sweet dressing will complement the freshness of the cucumbers.

  3. Combine the Ingredients: In a large mixing bowl, combine the sliced cucumbers and red onions. Pour the dressing over the vegetables and gently toss to coat them evenly. The longer you let the salad marinate, the more the flavors will meld together.

  4. Season and Chill: Season the salad with salt and pepper to taste. Transfer the salad to the refrigerator and let it chill for at least 30 minutes before serving. This allows the flavors to fully develop and the salad to become refreshingly cool.

  5. Serve and Enjoy: Once chilled, give the salad a final toss and transfer it to a serving dish. Garnish with an extra sprig of dill for a pop of color. This nut-free cucumber salad is ready to be enjoyed alongside your favorite grilled dishes or as a light, healthy snack.

Why You’ll Love This Recipe

  • Allergy-Friendly: This recipe is nut-free, making it suitable for individuals with nut allergies or those seeking nut-free options.

  • Refreshing and Crisp: The combination of cucumbers, vinegar, and dill creates a refreshing and crisp flavor profile that’s perfect for hot summer days.

  • Versatile: This salad can be paired with a variety of main dishes, from grilled chicken to seafood, or enjoyed on its own as a light and satisfying snack.

  • Make-Ahead: You can prepare this salad in advance, allowing the flavors to intensify as it chills in the refrigerator.

  • Healthy and Nutritious: Cucumbers are low in calories and high in water content, making this salad a hydrating and nutritious choice.

Whether you’re hosting a summer barbecue, packing a picnic, or simply looking for a vibrant and healthy side dish, this Tasty Nut-Free Cucumber Salad is sure to impress. Its simplicity and fresh flavors make it a standout addition to any meal.

So, next time you’re craving a light and delicious salad, give this recipe a try and savor the delightful combination of flavors. Cheers to enjoying the bounty of fresh produce in a simple and delightful way!
