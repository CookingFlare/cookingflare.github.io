Summer Soiree: 15 Refreshing Mocktail Recipes for Your Next Party


Summer is finally here, and with it comes the perfect excuse to host a soiree that your friends and family will never forget. As the temperature rises, we often find ourselves craving refreshing drinks that can quench our thirst and provide a welcome respite from the heat. In this blog post, we’ll be sharing 15 refreshing mocktail recipes that are sure to be a hit at your next summer gathering.

Spicy Mango Mule

Combine 2 oz mango puree, 1 oz lime juice, 1 oz ginger beer, and a dash of cayenne pepper in a copper mug filled with ice. Stir well and garnish with a lime wheel and a sprig of fresh mint.

Cucumber Lime Refresher

Muddle 1/4 cup of cucumber slices with 1 oz fresh lime juice and 1 oz simple syrup in a cocktail shaker. Add 2 oz sparkling water and a splash of lemon juice. Strain into a chilled glass filled with ice and garnish with a cucumber slice.

Strawberry Basil Bliss

Muddle 1/4 cup of fresh strawberries with 1 oz fresh lime juice and 1 oz simple syrup in a cocktail shaker. Add 2 oz sparkling water and a few leaves of fresh basil. Strain into a chilled glass filled with ice and garnish with a strawberry slice and a sprig of basil.

Grapefruit Rosemary Fizz

Combine 2 oz grapefruit juice, 1 oz rosemary syrup (see note), and 1 oz sparkling water in a glass filled with ice. Stir well and garnish with a rosemary sprig.

Note: To make rosemary syrup, combine 1 cup water with 1 cup sugar and 1/4 cup fresh rosemary leaves in a saucepan. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat and simmer for 10-15 minutes. Strain and let cool.

Cranberry Orange Spritzer

Combine 2 oz cranberry juice, 1 oz orange juice, and 1 oz sparkling water in a glass filled with ice. Stir well and garnish with a slice of orange.

Pineapple Ginger Fizz

Combine 2 oz pineapple juice, 1 oz ginger beer, and 1 oz sparkling water in a glass filled with ice. Stir well and garnish with a pineapple wedge and a sprig of fresh ginger.

Lavender Lemonade

Combine 2 oz lemonade, 1 oz lavender syrup (see note), and 1 oz sparkling water in a glass filled with ice. Stir well and garnish with a lavender sprig.

Note: To make lavender syrup, combine 1 cup water with 1 cup sugar and 1/4 cup dried lavender buds in a saucepan. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat and simmer for 10-15 minutes. Strain and let cool.

Blackberry Basil Smash

Muddle 1/4 cup of fresh blackberries with 1 oz fresh lime juice and 1 oz simple syrup in a cocktail shaker. Add 2 oz sparkling water and a few leaves of fresh basil. Strain into a chilled glass filled with ice and garnish with a blackberry and a sprig of basil.

Peach Iced Tea

Combine 2 oz peach puree, 1 oz brewed tea, and 1 oz sparkling water in a glass filled with ice. Stir well and garnish with a slice of peach.

Spicy Pineapple Ginger

Combine 2 oz pineapple juice, 1 oz ginger beer, and a dash of cayenne pepper in a glass filled with ice. Stir well and garnish with a pineapple wedge and a sprig of fresh ginger.

Cranberry Lime Sparkler

Combine 2 oz cranberry juice, 1 oz fresh lime juice, and 1 oz sparkling water in a glass filled with ice. Stir well and garnish with a lime wheel.

Orange Creamsicle Fizz

Combine 2 oz orange juice, 1 oz vanilla syrup (see note), and 1 oz sparkling water in a glass filled with ice. Stir well and garnish with a slice of orange.

Note: To make vanilla syrup, combine 1 cup water with 1 cup sugar and 1/2 cup vanilla extract in a saucepan. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat and simmer for 5-7 minutes. Strain and let cool.

Grapefruit Rosemary Spritzer

Combine 2 oz grapefruit juice, 1 oz rosemary syrup, and 1 oz sparkling water in a glass filled with ice. Stir well and garnish with a rosemary sprig.

Cucumber Mint Refresher

Muddle 1/4 cup of cucumber slices with 1 oz fresh lime juice and 1 oz simple syrup in a cocktail shaker. Add 2 oz sparkling water and a few leaves of fresh mint. Strain into a chilled glass filled with ice and garnish with a cucumber slice and a sprig of mint.

Blackberry Ginger Fizz

Combine 2 oz blackberry puree, 1 oz ginger beer, and 1 oz sparkling water in a glass filled with ice. Stir well and garnish with a blackberry and a sprig of fresh ginger.

These refreshing mocktail recipes are sure to be a hit at your next summer soiree. With their unique flavor combinations and refreshing twists, they’re perfect for hot summer days and nights. Whether you’re hosting a backyard BBQ or a garden party, these drinks are sure to quench your guests’ thirst and leave them feeling refreshed and revitalized. So go ahead, get creative, and start experimenting with these recipes to create the perfect drink for your next summer gathering!
