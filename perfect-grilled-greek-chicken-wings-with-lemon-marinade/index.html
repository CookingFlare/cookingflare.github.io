Perfect Grilled Greek Chicken Wings with Lemon Marinade


When it comes to grilling, few dishes can compare to the mouthwatering flavors of chicken wings. Grilled chicken wings are a perfect balance of crispy skin, juicy meat, and bold flavors, especially when paired with a Mediterranean twist. The unique and refreshing zest of lemon, the earthy aroma of oregano, and the subtle heat of garlic come together to create an unforgettable marinade for these Perfect Grilled Greek Chicken Wings.

In this recipe, we’ll walk through the steps to create the most tender and flavorful chicken wings that are perfect for summer barbecues, family gatherings, or any occasion when you crave a savory, zesty snack. The tangy lemon marinade enhances the natural flavors of the chicken, while the grilling process locks in all the juiciness.

Ingredients for Greek Chicken Wings

To make your Perfect Grilled Greek Chicken Wings, you’ll need a few essential ingredients. Many of these ingredients are staples in Greek cuisine and are responsible for the bright, herbaceous flavors that make this dish so special.

  • Chicken Wings: 12-15 whole chicken wings (or around 2 pounds)
  • Olive Oil: 3 tablespoons of high-quality extra virgin olive oil
  • Lemon Juice: 1/4 cup fresh lemon juice (about 2 medium-sized lemons)
  • Lemon Zest: 1 teaspoon fresh lemon zest
  • Garlic: 4-5 cloves of garlic, finely minced
  • Dried Oregano: 2 tablespoons of Greek dried oregano (this herb gives the dish its signature taste)
  • Red Wine Vinegar: 1 tablespoon
  • Honey: 1 tablespoon (adds a touch of sweetness to balance the acidity)
  • Salt and Pepper: To taste (for seasoning)
  • Fresh Parsley: A handful of finely chopped parsley for garnish
  • Optional: Feta cheese crumbles for serving (this adds an extra layer of Greek flavor)

Step-by-Step Guide to Making Grilled Greek Chicken Wings

Step 1: Prepare the Chicken Wings

Start by preparing your chicken wings. If you have whole wings, you will need to separate them into three parts: the drumette, the flat, and the tip. If you’re unsure how to do this, you can simply ask your butcher to separate them for you.

Next, rinse the wings under cold water and pat them dry with a paper towel. It’s essential to remove excess moisture from the wings to ensure a crispy texture when grilling. Afterward, season the wings lightly with a pinch of salt and pepper. This ensures that the flavors are absorbed right from the start.

Step 2: Make the Lemon Marinade

Now comes the exciting part—the marinade. The marinade for these wings is the key to infusing them with a delicious Greek-inspired flavor. Here’s how to make it:

In a medium-sized bowl, whisk together the following ingredients:

  • 1/4 cup fresh lemon juice
  • 1 teaspoon fresh lemon zest
  • 3 tablespoons of extra virgin olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon of red wine vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon of honey
  • 2 tablespoons of dried oregano
  • 4-5 cloves of minced garlic
  • Salt and pepper to taste

The honey adds a subtle sweetness that balances the acidity of the lemon, while the red wine vinegar adds an extra layer of tartness, making the wings flavorful and juicy. The combination of garlic and oregano is a classic Greek flavor profile that infuses every bite.

Once all the ingredients are whisked together, taste the marinade. It should be tangy, savory, and slightly sweet with an earthy herbal aroma. If it’s too acidic, add a touch more honey. If you like your marinade a bit more savory, adjust the oregano or garlic accordingly.

Step 3: Marinate the Chicken Wings

Now, it’s time to let the chicken wings soak up all those vibrant flavors. Place the chicken wings in a large resealable plastic bag or a shallow dish. Pour the marinade over the chicken, making sure that each wing is well coated. Seal the bag or cover the dish with plastic wrap.

Let the wings marinate in the refrigerator for at least 2 hours, though overnight is ideal. The longer the wings sit in the marinade, the more flavorful they will be. If you’re in a rush, even 30 minutes will give them a decent infusion of flavor, but a longer marinating time will yield the best results.

Step 4: Preheat the Grill

While the chicken wings are marinating, it’s time to prep your grill. Preheat your grill to medium-high heat (around 375°F to 400°F). If you’re using a charcoal grill, make sure to create two zones: one with direct heat for searing and another with indirect heat for cooking through. If you’re using a gas grill, adjust the burners so that you have a hot zone and a cooler zone.

Oil the grill grates lightly with a paper towel dipped in oil or use a non-stick grill spray. This will help prevent the wings from sticking to the grates, ensuring a clean release when it’s time to flip them.

Step 5: Grill the Chicken Wings

Once the grill is preheated and ready, it’s time to cook the chicken wings. Take the marinated wings out of the refrigerator and allow them to come to room temperature for about 10-15 minutes before placing them on the grill. This helps them cook more evenly.

Place the chicken wings on the grill over the direct heat side. Grill the wings for about 5-7 minutes per side, or until the skin turns golden brown and crispy. Be sure to watch them closely to prevent burning, and flip the wings carefully using tongs. If you notice the wings are starting to char too much, move them to the cooler side of the grill to finish cooking through.

To ensure that the wings are cooked to perfection, use a meat thermometer. The internal temperature of the chicken should reach 165°F. If you don’t have a thermometer, you can also check the doneness by cutting into one of the wings at the thickest part. The meat should be fully cooked and the juices should run clear.

Step 6: Garnish and Serve

Once the wings are grilled to perfection, remove them from the grill and let them rest for a few minutes. This allows the juices to redistribute and keeps the wings tender and juicy.

While the wings are resting, finely chop some fresh parsley to sprinkle over the wings as a garnish. The fresh parsley adds a vibrant green color and a refreshing taste that complements the bold lemon flavors. If you’re feeling extra indulgent, you can sprinkle some crumbled feta cheese on top for an authentic Greek touch.

Step 7: Enjoy Your Grilled Greek Chicken Wings

These grilled Greek chicken wings are best served hot off the grill. Pair them with a simple Greek salad, a dollop of tzatziki sauce, or a side of warm pita bread to complete the meal. They are perfect for casual gatherings, BBQs, or as a flavorful appetizer to any Mediterranean-themed feast.

The crispy, flavorful skin combined with the tender, juicy chicken inside is sure to be a crowd-pleaser. Every bite is a burst of Mediterranean flavors, from the tangy lemon and oregano to the smoky char from the grill. These wings are sure to become a new favorite in your grilling repertoire.

Tips for the Perfect Grilled Chicken Wings

  • Use fresh lemon: While bottled lemon juice can be convenient, fresh lemons will give your wings a much more vibrant and tangy flavor.
  • Don’t skip the marination: The longer the wings marinate, the more flavorful they will become. Try marinating them overnight for the best results.
  • Grill with care: Keep a close eye on your wings while grilling, as they can go from perfectly crispy to burnt in just a few minutes. Always have a cooler zone on your grill for indirect heat if needed.
  • Make it spicy: If you like a bit of heat, add red pepper flakes or a touch of cayenne pepper to the marinade. This will give your wings a spicy kick without overpowering the other flavors.
  • Experiment with herbs: If you want to change things up, try adding a bit of thyme or rosemary to the marinade. Both herbs pair wonderfully with chicken and give a unique twist to the dish.

Conclusion

Grilled Greek Chicken Wings with Lemon Marinade are the perfect balance of savory, tangy, and slightly sweet flavors, making them an irresistible choice for any occasion. With their crispy skin, tender meat, and Mediterranean-inspired marinade, these wings are sure to become a grilling favorite.

By following this simple yet effective recipe, you can elevate your chicken wings game and impress your guests with a dish that’s bursting with flavor. Whether you’re grilling for a crowd or enjoying a quiet meal at home, these wings are a guaranteed hit. So fire up the grill, gather your ingredients, and get ready to enjoy the best grilled chicken wings you’ve ever made.
