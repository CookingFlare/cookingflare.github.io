The Corpse Reviver No. 2: A Classic Cocktail to Cure All Ills


Are you in need of a pick-me-up after a long day? Look no further than The Corpse Reviver No. 2, a classic cocktail that has been known to cure all ills. With its unique blend of flavors, this drink is sure to awaken your senses and lift your spirits.

Ingredients

  • 1 ounce gin
  • 1 ounce Lillet Blanc
  • 1 ounce Cointreau
  • 1 ounce freshly squeezed lemon juice
  • 1 dash absinthe

Instructions

  1. Fill a cocktail shaker with ice cubes.
  2. Add the gin, Lillet Blanc, Cointreau, lemon juice, and a dash of absinthe.
  3. Shake the ingredients vigorously for about 10 seconds to ensure they are well mixed and chilled.
  4. Strain the mixture into a chilled cocktail glass.
  5. Garnish with a lemon twist, if desired.

The History of The Corpse Reviver No. 2

The Corpse Reviver No. 2 has a fascinating history that dates back to the early 20th century. Originally created as a “hair of the dog” hangover cure, this cocktail quickly gained popularity as a favorite among cocktail enthusiasts.

The name “Corpse Reviver” refers to a family of cocktails that were believed to have the power to revive the dead, or at least those suffering from a severe hangover. While the exact origin of the Corpse Reviver No. 2 is uncertain, it is widely believed to have been first mentioned in Harry Craddock’s 1930 book, “The Savoy Cocktail Book.”

The Perfect Blend of Flavors

What sets The Corpse Reviver No. 2 apart from other cocktails is its unique blend of flavors. The gin provides a strong botanical base, while the Lillet Blanc adds a touch of sweetness and complexity. The Cointreau brings a citrusy note, and the freshly squeezed lemon juice adds a refreshing tartness. Finally, a dash of absinthe adds an herbal and aromatic element that ties everything together.

When these ingredients come together, they create a harmonious symphony of flavors that is both invigorating and satisfying. Each sip of The Corpse Reviver No. 2 is like a breath of fresh air, awakening your taste buds and revitalizing your senses.

The Art of Mixing

To truly appreciate The Corpse Reviver No. 2, it is important to master the art of mixing cocktails. The key to a well-made Corpse Reviver No. 2 lies in the proper balance of ingredients and the technique used to mix them.

When preparing this cocktail, it is crucial to use high-quality ingredients and fresh, seasonal produce whenever possible. This will ensure that the flavors are vibrant and the cocktail is at its best.

Conclusion

In conclusion, The Corpse Reviver No. 2 is a classic cocktail that offers a delightful blend of flavors and a refreshing escape from the ordinary. Whether you’re looking to cure a hangover or simply enjoy a unique and delicious drink, The Corpse Reviver No. 2 is sure to impress.

So, the next time you find yourself in need of a pick-me-up, reach for The Corpse Reviver No. 2 and let its magical powers transport you to a world of flavor and rejuvenation. Cheers!
