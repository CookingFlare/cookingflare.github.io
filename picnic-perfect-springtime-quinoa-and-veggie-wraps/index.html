Picnic Perfect: Springtime Quinoa and Veggie Wraps

3 min · 524 words

Spring is the perfect time to embrace the great outdoors, and what better way to enjoy the season than with a delightful picnic? Whether you’re heading to the park, the beach, or simply your backyard, a picnic calls for delicious and portable food. That’s where these Springtime Quinoa and Veggie Wraps come in. Packed with fresh, vibrant flavors and wholesome ingredients, these wraps are not only a treat for your taste buds but also a feast for the eyes.

Ingredients

For the Quinoa:

  • 1 cup quinoa
  • 2 cups water
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt

For the Veggie Filling:

  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 red bell pepper, thinly sliced
  • 1 yellow bell pepper, thinly sliced
  • 1 small red onion, thinly sliced
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 2 cups baby spinach
  • Salt and pepper to taste

For the Lemon-Herb Dressing:

  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons fresh lemon juice
  • 1 tablespoon chopped fresh basil
  • 1 tablespoon chopped fresh parsley
  • Salt and pepper to taste

For the Wraps:

  • 4 large whole grain tortillas
  • 1 cup hummus

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Quinoa:

    • Rinse the quinoa under cold water using a fine-mesh strainer.
    • In a medium saucepan, bring the quinoa, water, and salt to a boil.
    • Reduce the heat to low, cover, and simmer for 15-20 minutes, or until the quinoa is fluffy and the liquid is absorbed. Remove from the heat and let it cool.

  2. Make the Veggie Filling:

    • In a large skillet, heat the olive oil over medium-high heat.
    • Add the red and yellow bell peppers and red onion, and sauté for 5-7 minutes, or until the veggies are tender-crisp.
    • Add the cherry tomatoes and baby spinach, and cook for an additional 2-3 minutes until the spinach wilts.
    • Season with salt and pepper, and set aside.

  3. Prepare the Lemon-Herb Dressing:

    • In a small bowl, whisk together the olive oil, lemon juice, basil, parsley, salt, and pepper. Set aside.

  4. Assemble the Wraps:

    • Lay out the tortillas on a clean surface.
    • Spread each tortilla with a generous spoonful of hummus, leaving a border around the edges.
    • Divide the cooked quinoa among the tortillas, spreading it over the hummus.
    • Spoon the veggie filling over the quinoa on each tortilla.
    • Drizzle the lemon-herb dressing evenly over the veggie filling.

  5. Roll the Wraps:

    • Fold in the sides of each tortilla, then roll it up tightly from the bottom.
    • Place the wraps seam-side down on a cutting board and slice them in half at a diagonal.

  6. Pack and Enjoy:

    • Wrap each half in parchment paper or foil for easy transportation.
    • Pack the wraps in your picnic basket along with your favorite fruits, beverages, and a cozy blanket.

Now, you’re all set to head out and enjoy a delightful picnic with these Springtime Quinoa and Veggie Wraps. Their refreshing flavors and satisfying goodness make them a perfect companion for any outdoor adventure. So, grab a friend or loved one, find a picturesque spot, and savor the joys of spring with this delightful picnic treat.

In conclusion, these Springtime Quinoa and Veggie Wraps are a testament to the beauty of seasonal ingredients and the simplicity of a well-prepared meal. Whether you’re a seasoned picnic enthusiast or a newcomer to the outdoor dining scene, these wraps are sure to elevate your experience with their wholesome goodness and vibrant flavors. So, pack your picnic basket, gather your loved ones, and indulge in the magic of spring with these delightful wraps.
