The Best Party Dips: 8 Recipes to Get the Party Started


When it comes to throwing a party, there are a few key elements that can make or break the event. Music, decorations, and a lively atmosphere are all important, but let’s not forget about the food. Specifically, the dips. A great dip can make all the difference in getting the party started and keeping it going. In this post, we’ll explore eight of the best party dips that are sure to be a hit with your guests.

Spinach and Artichoke Dip

This classic dip is a staple for a reason. It’s easy to make, delicious, and can be served with a variety of dippers, from tortilla chips to pita bread. To make it, you’ll need:

  • 1 (14 oz) can of artichoke hearts, drained and chopped
  • 1 package of frozen chopped spinach, thawed and drained
  • 1 cup of mayonnaise
  • 1 cup of sour cream
  • 1/2 cup of grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/2 cup of chopped fresh parsley
  • 1 clove of garlic, minced
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Combine all the ingredients in a bowl and mix until smooth. Serve warm with dippers of your choice.

Roasted Red Pepper Hummus

Hummus is a great dip for those who are looking for something a little healthier. This roasted red pepper version adds a sweet and smoky flavor that’s sure to be a hit. To make it, you’ll need:

  • 2 cups of cooked chickpeas
  • 1/4 cup of lemon juice
  • 1/4 cup of tahini
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1/2 teaspoon of salt
  • 3 tablespoons of olive oil
  • 2 roasted red peppers, peeled and chopped

Combine all the ingredients in a blender or food processor and blend until smooth. Serve with pita bread or vegetables.

French Onion Dip

This savory dip is perfect for those who love the flavor of caramelized onions. To make it, you’ll need:

  • 1 large onion, caramelized
  • 1 cup of sour cream
  • 1/2 cup of mayonnaise
  • 1 tablespoon of Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 teaspoon of salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon of black pepper
  • 1/4 cup of grated cheddar cheese

Combine all the ingredients in a bowl and mix until smooth. Serve with potato chips or crackers.

Bacon Ranch Dip

This creamy dip is perfect for those who love the flavor of bacon. To make it, you’ll need:

  • 1 cup of sour cream
  • 1/2 cup of mayonnaise
  • 1 tablespoon of ranch seasoning
  • 4 slices of cooked bacon, crumbled
  • 1/2 cup of shredded cheddar cheese
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Combine all the ingredients in a bowl and mix until smooth. Serve with tortilla chips or crackers.

Sun-Dried Tomato Dip

This flavorful dip is perfect for those who love the taste of sun-dried tomatoes. To make it, you’ll need:

  • 1 cup of cream cheese, softened
  • 1/2 cup of mayonnaise
  • 1/4 cup of chopped sun-dried tomatoes
  • 1 clove of garlic, minced
  • 1/2 teaspoon of salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon of black pepper
  • 1/4 cup of grated Parmesan cheese

Combine all the ingredients in a bowl and mix until smooth. Serve with pita bread or vegetables.

Queso Dip

This cheesy dip is perfect for those who love the flavor of melted cheese. To make it, you’ll need:

  • 1 cup of shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1/2 cup of half-and-half
  • 1 tablespoon of paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon of cumin
  • 1/4 teaspoon of cayenne pepper
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Combine all the ingredients in a saucepan and heat over medium heat, stirring constantly, until the cheese is melted and the dip is smooth. Serve with tortilla chips or crackers.

Guacamole

This creamy dip is perfect for those who love the flavor of avocados. To make it, you’ll need:

  • 3 ripe avocados, peeled and pitted
  • 1/2 red onion, finely chopped
  • 1 jalapeño pepper, seeded and finely chopped
  • 1/2 lime, juiced
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Combine all the ingredients in a bowl and mix until smooth. Serve with tortilla chips or vegetables.

Spicy Pesto Dip

This flavorful dip is perfect for those who love the taste of pesto. To make it, you’ll need:

  • 1 cup of pesto
  • 1 cup of sour cream
  • 1/2 cup of mayonnaise
  • 1/4 cup of chopped fresh parsley
  • 1/4 cup of chopped fresh basil
  • 1/2 teaspoon of garlic powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Combine all the ingredients in a bowl and mix until smooth. Serve with pita bread or vegetables.

These eight dips are sure to be a hit at your next party. They’re easy to make, delicious, and offer a variety of flavors to suit any taste. So go ahead, get creative, and start dipping!
