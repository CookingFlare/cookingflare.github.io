Thai Lemongrass Chicken: A Fragrant Delight


If you’re looking for a dish that’s bursting with flavor and aroma, Thai Lemongrass Chicken is the perfect choice. This fragrant and vibrant dish is a delightful fusion of lemongrass, chicken, and an array of aromatic Thai spices. The combination of lemongrass’s citrusy notes and the rich, savory flavors of the chicken creates a mouthwatering experience that is sure to impress.

Ingredients

To create this Thai Lemongrass Chicken masterpiece, you’ll need the following ingredients:

  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 3 stalks of lemongrass, finely chopped
  • 4 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 shallot, finely chopped
  • 1 red chili, finely chopped
  • 1 tablespoon of fish sauce
  • 2 tablespoons of soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon of oyster sauce
  • 1 tablespoon of brown sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon of ground black pepper
  • 1/4 cup of chicken broth
  • 2 tablespoons of vegetable oil
  • Fresh cilantro for garnish
  • Cooked jasmine rice for serving

Instructions

Step 1: Prepare the Chicken

Begin by slicing the chicken breasts into thin, even strips. This will ensure that the chicken cooks evenly and absorbs the flavors of the lemongrass and spices.

Step 2: Marinate the Chicken

In a bowl, combine the chopped lemongrass, minced garlic, chopped shallot, and red chili. Add in the fish sauce, soy sauce, oyster sauce, brown sugar, and ground black pepper. Mix the marinade thoroughly, then add the sliced chicken to the bowl. Ensure that each piece of chicken is coated with the marinade, cover the bowl, and refrigerate for at least 30 minutes to allow the flavors to infuse.

Step 3: Sear the Chicken

Heat the vegetable oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat. Once the oil is hot, add the marinated chicken to the skillet, allowing it to sear for 3-4 minutes on each side, or until golden brown and cooked through. Remove the chicken from the skillet and set it aside.

Step 4: Create the Sauce

In the same skillet, add the chicken broth and any remaining marinade. Bring the mixture to a simmer, stirring occasionally to incorporate the flavors. Allow the sauce to thicken slightly, then return the cooked chicken to the skillet, tossing it in the sauce until it’s fully coated.

Step 5: Serve and Enjoy

Once the chicken is coated in the fragrant lemongrass sauce, remove the skillet from the heat. Serve the Thai Lemongrass Chicken over a bed of fluffy jasmine rice, garnishing with fresh cilantro for a burst of herbal freshness.

Conclusion

Thai Lemongrass Chicken is a dish that embodies the vibrant and aromatic flavors of Thai cuisine. The zesty, citrusy notes of lemongrass combined with the savory, tender chicken create a harmonious balance that is truly a delight for the senses. Whether you’re a fan of Thai cuisine or simply looking to explore new flavors, this recipe is a must-try. Enjoy the fragrant delight of Thai Lemongrass Chicken in the comfort of your own kitchen!
