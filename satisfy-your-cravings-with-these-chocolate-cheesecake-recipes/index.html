Satisfy Your Cravings with These Chocolate Cheesecake Recipes


There’s something undeniably satisfying about chocolate cheesecake. The combination of rich, velvety chocolate and smooth, creamy cheesecake creates a dessert that’s hard to resist. Whether you’re baking for a special occasion, a family gathering, or simply to treat yourself, these chocolate cheesecake recipes will surely satisfy your cravings.

In this post, we’ll explore a variety of chocolate cheesecake recipes that you can make from scratch. From classic recipes to innovative twists, there’s something for every chocolate and cheesecake lover. Let’s dive into these indulgent chocolate cheesecake creations that will take your dessert game to the next level.

1. Classic Chocolate Cheesecake

Ingredients:

For the crust:

  • 1 1/2 cups chocolate cookie crumbs
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/2 cup melted butter

For the cheesecake filling:

  • 4 (8 oz) packages cream cheese, softened
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 cup sour cream
  • 4 large eggs
  • 8 oz semisweet chocolate, melted
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the crust:

    • Preheat your oven to 325°F (163°C).
    • In a medium bowl, combine the chocolate cookie crumbs and sugar. Add the melted butter and mix until everything is well coated.
    • Press the mixture into the bottom of a springform pan, creating a uniform layer.
    • Bake the crust for 10 minutes, then remove from the oven and set aside to cool.

  2. Make the cheesecake filling:

    • In a large mixing bowl, beat the cream cheese and sugar together until smooth and creamy.
    • Add the sour cream, eggs, and vanilla extract, beating until the mixture is well combined.
    • Pour in the melted chocolate and beat again until fully incorporated.
    • Add the heavy cream and mix until the batter is smooth and silky.

  3. Assemble and bake:

    • Pour the cheesecake batter into the prepared crust and smooth the top with a spatula.
    • Bake for 55-60 minutes or until the center is slightly jiggly but set.
    • Turn off the oven and leave the cheesecake in for an additional 1 hour. After that, allow the cheesecake to cool at room temperature before transferring it to the refrigerator to chill for at least 4 hours or overnight.

  4. Serve:

    • Once chilled, carefully remove the cheesecake from the springform pan. Slice and serve as is, or with a dollop of whipped cream and chocolate shavings on top.

This classic chocolate cheesecake is a crowd-pleaser that will never go out of style. The rich, chocolatey filling pairs perfectly with the buttery cookie crust, creating the perfect balance of textures and flavors.

2. No-Bake Chocolate Cheesecake

Ingredients:

For the crust:

  • 1 1/2 cups chocolate graham cracker crumbs
  • 1/4 cup melted butter

For the filling:

  • 16 oz cream cheese, softened
  • 1 cup powdered sugar
  • 1/2 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 8 oz semi-sweet chocolate, melted
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the crust:

    • In a medium bowl, combine the chocolate graham cracker crumbs and melted butter. Stir until the crumbs are evenly coated.
    • Press the mixture into the bottom of a 9-inch springform pan to form a smooth crust. Refrigerate while you prepare the filling.

  2. Make the filling:

    • In a large bowl, beat the softened cream cheese and powdered sugar together until smooth and fluffy.
    • Add the cocoa powder and melted chocolate, mixing until the mixture is smooth and well combined.
    • In a separate bowl, whip the heavy cream and vanilla extract until stiff peaks form.
    • Gently fold the whipped cream into the chocolate mixture, being careful not to deflate the whipped cream.

  3. Assemble and chill:

    • Pour the chocolate filling over the prepared crust in the springform pan. Smooth the top with a spatula.
    • Refrigerate the cheesecake for at least 4 hours or overnight to allow it to set.

  4. Serve:

    • Once the cheesecake has fully set, remove it from the springform pan and transfer it to a serving platter.
    • Slice and serve with optional toppings like chocolate shavings, fresh berries, or whipped cream.

This no-bake chocolate cheesecake is perfect when you need a quick yet indulgent dessert. With minimal effort and no oven required, this recipe delivers creamy, chocolatey goodness in every bite.

3. Flourless Chocolate Cheesecake

Ingredients:

For the crust:

  • 1 1/2 cups almond flour
  • 2 tbsp cocoa powder
  • 2 tbsp maple syrup
  • 1/4 cup melted butter

For the filling:

  • 12 oz dark chocolate, chopped
  • 3 packages cream cheese, softened
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/4 cup heavy cream
  • 3 large eggs
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1/4 tsp salt

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the crust:

    • Preheat the oven to 350°F (177°C).
    • In a medium bowl, mix the almond flour, cocoa powder, and maple syrup. Stir in the melted butter until the mixture holds together.
    • Press the mixture into the bottom of a 9-inch springform pan, creating an even layer.
    • Bake the crust for 8-10 minutes, then remove it from the oven and set it aside to cool.

  2. Make the filling:

    • In a microwave-safe bowl, melt the dark chocolate in 30-second intervals, stirring between each, until fully melted.
    • In a separate bowl, beat the cream cheese and sugar until smooth and creamy.
    • Add the melted chocolate, heavy cream, eggs, vanilla extract, and salt. Beat until the mixture is smooth and well combined.

  3. Assemble and bake:

    • Pour the cheesecake filling over the cooled crust.
    • Bake for 35-40 minutes, or until the center is set but slightly jiggly.
    • Turn off the oven and leave the cheesecake inside for 1 hour. Afterward, allow it to cool to room temperature before refrigerating it for at least 4 hours.

  4. Serve:

    • Once chilled, remove the cheesecake from the springform pan and slice it carefully.
    • Serve as is or with a drizzle of chocolate ganache and a sprinkle of sea salt for a refined touch.

This flourless chocolate cheesecake is perfect for those who prefer a denser, richer texture without any flour in the mix. It’s a decadent treat that will satisfy your cravings with every bite.

4. Chocolate Peanut Butter Cheesecake

Ingredients:

For the crust:

  • 1 1/2 cups chocolate sandwich cookie crumbs
  • 1/4 cup melted butter

For the peanut butter layer:

  • 1 cup peanut butter
  • 1 cup powdered sugar
  • 8 oz cream cheese, softened
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract

For the chocolate layer:

  • 8 oz semisweet chocolate, melted
  • 8 oz cream cheese, softened
  • 1/2 cup powdered sugar
  • 1/2 tsp vanilla extract

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the crust:

    • Preheat your oven to 325°F (163°C).
    • In a bowl, mix the chocolate sandwich cookie crumbs with the melted butter. Press the mixture into the bottom of a springform pan.
    • Bake for 10 minutes, then let the crust cool.

  2. Make the peanut butter layer:

    • In a medium bowl, combine the peanut butter, powdered sugar, softened cream cheese, and vanilla extract. Beat until smooth and creamy.
    • Spread the peanut butter mixture over the cooled crust.

  3. Make the chocolate layer:

    • In a separate bowl, beat the melted semisweet chocolate, cream cheese, powdered sugar, and vanilla extract together until smooth.
    • Carefully spread the chocolate mixture over the peanut butter layer.

  4. Assemble and chill:

    • Refrigerate the cheesecake for at least 4 hours, or preferably overnight, to allow the layers to set.

  5. Serve:

    • Once set, remove the cheesecake from the springform pan and slice.
    • Garnish with chopped peanuts or a drizzle of melted chocolate for added flair.

This chocolate peanut butter cheesecake combines two beloved flavors into one show-stopping dessert. With layers of creamy peanut butter and smooth chocolate, it’s a dessert that will leave your taste buds dancing with delight.

5. Triple Chocolate Cheesecake

Ingredients:

For the crust:

  • 1 1/2 cups chocolate cookie crumbs
  • 1/4 cup melted butter

For the cheesecake filling:

  • 8 oz semisweet chocolate, chopped
  • 8 oz milk chocolate, chopped
  • 3 packages cream cheese, softened
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 3 large eggs
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream

For the topping:

  • 4 oz white chocolate, melted
  • Chocolate shavings

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the crust:

    • Preheat your oven to 325°F (163°C).
    • Combine chocolate cookie crumbs and melted butter in a bowl. Press the mixture into the bottom of a springform pan.
    • Bake for 10 minutes, then remove from the oven and set aside to cool.

  2. Make the cheesecake filling:

    • Melt the semisweet and milk chocolate together in a microwave-safe bowl or over a double boiler.
    • In a large bowl, beat the softened cream cheese and sugar until smooth. Add the melted chocolate, vanilla extract, and eggs. Mix until smooth.
    • Add the heavy cream and mix again until combined.

  3. Assemble and bake:

    • Pour the chocolate cheesecake batter into the prepared crust.
    • Bake for 55-60 minutes, or until the center is set but slightly jiggly.
    • Turn off the oven and let the cheesecake cool in the oven with the door slightly ajar for 1 hour. Afterward, transfer it to the fridge to chill for at least 4 hours.

  4. Serve:

    • Once chilled, carefully remove the cheesecake from the springform pan.
    • Drizzle with melted white chocolate and garnish with chocolate shavings for an extra touch of decadence.

This triple chocolate cheesecake is the ultimate indulgence for chocolate lovers. With three different types of chocolate incorporated into the filling and topped with a white chocolate drizzle, it’s the perfect treat for any occasion.

Conclusion

These chocolate cheesecake recipes offer something for everyone, whether you prefer classic, no-bake, flourless, or inventive layered variations. With each recipe, you’ll find the perfect balance of creamy texture and rich chocolate flavor, making them a go-to for any dessert lover. So go ahead, indulge in these chocolate cheesecake recipes, and satisfy your cravings in the best way possible. Happy baking!
