Hearty Italian Vegetable Soup Recipe - Comfort in a Bowl

3 min · 539 words

Nothing warms the soul quite like a comforting bowl of soup. And when it comes to hearty and satisfying soups, you can’t go wrong with an Italian vegetable soup. Packed with nutritious vegetables, aromatic herbs, and a flavorful broth, this soup is the perfect way to nourish your body and lift your spirits. So, grab your apron and let’s dive into this delicious recipe that will transport you straight to the heart of Italy.

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 medium onion, diced
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 carrots, peeled and sliced
  • 2 celery stalks, sliced
  • 1 zucchini, diced
  • 1 cup green beans, trimmed and cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 1 red bell pepper, diced
  • 1 can (14 ounces) diced tomatoes, undrained
  • 4 cups vegetable broth
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried thyme
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 cup small pasta (such as ditalini or macaroni)
  • Fresh basil leaves, for garnish

Instructions

  1. In a large pot, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the diced onion and minced garlic, and sauté until fragrant and translucent.

  2. Add the sliced carrots, celery, zucchini, green beans, and red bell pepper to the pot. Stir well to combine and cook for 5 minutes, allowing the vegetables to soften slightly.

  3. Pour in the diced tomatoes with their juice and the vegetable broth. Stir in the dried oregano, dried basil, dried thyme, salt, and pepper. Bring the soup to a boil, then reduce the heat to low and let it simmer for 15 minutes to allow the flavors to meld together.

  4. While the soup is simmering, cook the small pasta according to the package instructions. Drain and set aside.

  5. After the soup has simmered, add the cooked pasta to the pot and stir to combine. Let the soup simmer for an additional 5 minutes to allow the pasta to absorb some of the flavors.

  6. Taste the soup and adjust the seasonings if needed. If the soup is too thick, you can add a little more vegetable broth to thin it out.

  7. Ladle the hearty Italian vegetable soup into bowls and garnish with fresh basil leaves. Serve hot and enjoy the comforting flavors of Italy!

Notes

  • This soup can easily be customized to suit your taste preferences. Feel free to add or substitute any vegetables you like, such as diced potatoes, spinach, or cabbage.
  • For a heartier version, you can add cooked beans or shredded chicken to the soup.
  • Leftovers can be stored in an airtight container in the refrigerator for up to 3 days. The flavors will continue to develop and intensify over time.

Conclusion

There you have it - a delightful Hearty Italian Vegetable Soup that brings comfort and warmth to your table. With its vibrant colors and rich flavors, this soup is not only a treat for your taste buds but also a nourishing meal for your body. So, whether you’re looking for a quick weeknight dinner or a cozy meal on a chilly day, this recipe is a must-try. Give it a go and let the comforting flavors of Italy embrace you in a bowl.
