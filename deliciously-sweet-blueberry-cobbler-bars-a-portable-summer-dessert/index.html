Deliciously Sweet Blueberry Cobbler Bars: A Portable Summer Dessert


Are you craving a deliciously sweet dessert that embodies the flavors of summer? Look no further than these mouthwatering Blueberry Cobbler Bars! Bursting with juicy blueberries and topped with a buttery, crumbly crust, these portable treats are perfect for picnics, potlucks, or simply indulging in a sweet treat at home.

Ingredients

For the crust and topping:

  • 1 and 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup cold unsalted butter, cubed
  • 1 large egg

For the blueberry filling:

  • 2 cups fresh blueberries
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 2 tablespoons cornstarch
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C) and line a 9x9-inch baking pan with parchment paper.

  2. In a large mixing bowl, whisk together the flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt for the crust and topping. Add the cold cubed butter and use a pastry cutter or your fingers to cut the butter into the mixture until it resembles coarse crumbs.

  3. Add the egg to the mixture and stir until the dough comes together. Set aside about 1 cup of the mixture for the topping.

  4. Press the remaining dough into the bottom of the prepared baking pan, creating an even layer. Bake the crust in the preheated oven for 12-15 minutes, or until lightly golden brown.

  5. While the crust is baking, prepare the blueberry filling. In a medium saucepan, combine the blueberries, sugar, cornstarch, lemon juice, and vanilla extract. Cook over medium heat, stirring frequently, until the blueberries burst and the mixture thickens, about 8-10 minutes.

  6. Once the crust is baked, remove it from the oven and pour the blueberry filling over the top, spreading it evenly. Crumble the reserved dough mixture over the blueberry filling.

  7. Return the baking pan to the oven and bake for an additional 25-30 minutes, or until the topping is golden brown.

  8. Remove the pan from the oven and allow it to cool completely on a wire rack before cutting into bars.

Serving Suggestions

These Deliciously Sweet Blueberry Cobbler Bars are delightful on their own, but feel free to take them to the next level with a scoop of vanilla ice cream or a dollop of whipped cream. They also pair well with a refreshing glass of iced tea or lemonade, making them a perfect treat for a hot summer day.

Storage

If you have any leftovers (which is highly unlikely!), store the Blueberry Cobbler Bars in an airtight container at room temperature for up to 3 days. They can also be refrigerated for extended freshness.

Conclusion

These portable Blueberry Cobbler Bars are the epitome of summer indulgence. The combination of sweet blueberries and buttery crust creates a taste sensation that will have your taste buds dancing with joy. Whether you’re hosting a summer gathering or simply want to treat yourself to a delicious dessert, these bars are sure to impress. So go ahead, whip up a batch, and savor the flavors of summer in every bite!
