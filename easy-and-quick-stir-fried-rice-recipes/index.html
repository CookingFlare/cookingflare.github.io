Easy and Quick Stir-Fried Rice Recipes


Stir-fried rice is a versatile and satisfying dish that can be whipped up in a matter of minutes. Whether you’re looking for a simple side dish or a hearty main course, stir-fried rice has got you covered. Plus, it’s a great way to use up leftover rice and incorporate a variety of vegetables and proteins into your meal. Here, we’ve curated three easy and quick stir-fried rice recipes that are sure to become staples in your cooking repertoire.

Classic Vegetable Stir-Fried Rice

Ingredients:

  • 3 cups cooked white rice, cooled
  • 1 cup mixed vegetables (such as carrots, peas, and corn)
  • 2 eggs, lightly beaten
  • 3 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Optional: sliced green onions for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Heat 1 tablespoon of vegetable oil in a large wok or skillet over medium-high heat.
  2. Add the minced garlic to the pan and sauté for 30 seconds until fragrant.
  3. Add the mixed vegetables to the pan and stir-fry for 2-3 minutes until they are tender-crisp.
  4. Push the vegetables to one side of the pan and pour the beaten eggs into the other side. Scramble the eggs until they are just set.
  5. Add the cooked rice to the pan and drizzle the soy sauce over the rice. Stir-fry everything together for 3-4 minutes until the rice is heated through and well-coated with the soy sauce.
  6. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  7. Garnish with sliced green onions if desired and serve hot.

Shrimp and Broccoli Stir-Fried Rice

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups cooked jasmine rice, cooled
  • 1/2 pound medium shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 2 cups broccoli florets
  • 3 tablespoons oyster sauce
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1 teaspoon sesame oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a large wok or skillet, heat 1 tablespoon of vegetable oil over medium-high heat.
  2. Add the minced garlic to the pan and sauté for 30 seconds.
  3. Add the shrimp to the pan and stir-fry for 2-3 minutes until they turn pink and opaque.
  4. Add the broccoli florets to the pan and stir-fry for an additional 2-3 minutes until they are tender-crisp.
  5. Push the shrimp and broccoli to the side of the pan and pour the cooked rice into the center.
  6. Drizzle the oyster sauce and sesame oil over the rice, then toss everything together until well combined.
  7. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  8. Serve hot and enjoy!

Spicy Chicken Stir-Fried Rice

Ingredients:

  • 3 cups cooked brown rice, cooled
  • 1 pound boneless, skinless chicken breast, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 1 red bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 small onion, thinly sliced
  • 3 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons sriracha sauce
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Heat 1 tablespoon of vegetable oil in a large wok or skillet over medium-high heat.
  2. Add the chicken pieces to the pan and stir-fry for 5-6 minutes until they are cooked through and golden brown.
  3. Push the chicken to one side of the pan and add the sliced onion and bell pepper. Stir-fry for 2-3 minutes until they are tender-crisp.
  4. Add the minced garlic to the pan and sauté for 30 seconds.
  5. Pour the cooked rice into the pan and drizzle the soy sauce and sriracha sauce over the rice.
  6. Stir-fry everything together for 3-4 minutes until the rice is heated through and the flavors are well combined.
  7. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  8. Serve hot for a spicy kick!

Stir-fried rice is a wonderful canvas for your culinary creativity. Feel free to customize these recipes with your favorite vegetables, proteins, and sauces to suit your taste. With these quick and easy stir-fried rice recipes in your cooking arsenal, you’ll never be more than a few minutes away from a delicious and satisfying meal. Enjoy!
