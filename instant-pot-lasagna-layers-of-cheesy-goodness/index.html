Instant Pot Lasagna: Layers of Cheesy Goodness


If you’re craving a delicious and hearty Italian meal but don’t have hours to spend in the kitchen, then Instant Pot Lasagna is the perfect solution. With the help of your trusty Instant Pot, you can have layers of cheesy goodness ready to devour in no time. This recipe takes all the traditional flavors of lasagna and streamlines the cooking process, without sacrificing taste or quality.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1 can (28 ounces) crushed tomatoes
  • 1 can (6 ounces) tomato paste
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 1 box (9 ounces) no-boil lasagna noodles
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1 cup ricotta cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • Fresh basil, for garnish

Instructions

  1. Set your Instant Pot to the sauté function and heat a tablespoon of olive oil. Add the ground beef, onion, and garlic, and cook until the beef is browned and the onions are translucent.

  2. Stir in the dried oregano, dried basil, salt, and black pepper. Cook for an additional minute to release the flavors.

  3. Add the crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, and water to the Instant Pot. Stir well to combine all the ingredients.

  4. Break the no-boil lasagna noodles into smaller pieces to fit into the Instant Pot. Layer half of the broken noodles on top of the sauce.

  5. Spread half of the ricotta cheese over the noodles, followed by half of the shredded mozzarella cheese and grated Parmesan cheese.

  6. Repeat the layers with the remaining half of the broken noodles, ricotta cheese, mozzarella cheese, and Parmesan cheese.

  7. Close the lid of the Instant Pot and set the valve to the sealing position. Cook on high pressure for 20 minutes.

  8. Once the cooking time is complete, allow the Instant Pot to naturally release pressure for 10 minutes. After that, carefully release any remaining pressure by turning the valve to the venting position.

  9. Remove the lid and let the lasagna sit for a few minutes to set before serving.

  10. Garnish with fresh basil and serve hot.

Tips for Success

  • Use a lean ground beef to avoid excessive grease in your lasagna.
  • Feel free to customize the recipe by adding vegetables like spinach or mushrooms to the meat sauce.
  • For a vegetarian version, substitute the ground beef with soy-based crumbles or additional vegetables.
  • Be sure to break the no-boil lasagna noodles into smaller pieces to ensure even cooking.
  • Letting the lasagna sit for a few minutes after cooking allows the flavors to meld together and makes it easier to slice and serve.

Now you can enjoy the comforting taste of lasagna without spending hours in the kitchen. The Instant Pot does all the hard work for you, and the result is a dish that’s packed with layers of cheesy goodness. Whether you’re cooking for a family dinner or hosting a dinner party, Instant Pot Lasagna is sure to be a crowd-pleaser. So grab your Instant Pot and get ready to indulge in this delicious Italian classic!
