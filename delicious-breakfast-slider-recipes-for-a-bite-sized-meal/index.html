Delicious Breakfast Slider Recipes for a Bite-Sized Meal


Are you tired of the same old breakfast options? Looking for a delicious and convenient meal that you can enjoy on the go? Look no further! In this blog post, we will be sharing some mouthwatering breakfast slider recipes that are perfect for a bite-sized morning meal. These sliders are not only delicious but also easy to make, making them an ideal choice for busy mornings when time is of the essence.

1. Bacon, Egg, and Cheese Sliders

Ingredients:

  • 12 slider buns
  • 12 slices of bacon, cooked until crispy
  • 6 large eggs, scrambled
  • 6 slices of cheddar cheese
  • Salt and pepper, to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. Slice the slider buns in half and arrange the bottom halves on a baking sheet.
  3. Place a slice of cooked bacon on each bun.
  4. Top the bacon with a spoonful of scrambled eggs and a slice of cheddar cheese.
  5. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  6. Place the top halves of the buns on the sliders.
  7. Bake in the preheated oven for about 10 minutes, or until the cheese is melted and the sliders are heated through.
  8. Serve hot and enjoy!

2. Sausage and Pepper Sliders

Ingredients:

  • 12 slider buns
  • 12 sausage patties, cooked
  • 1 green bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 red bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 onion, sliced
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • Salt and pepper, to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. Slice the slider buns in half and arrange the bottom halves on a baking sheet.
  3. In a pan, heat olive oil over medium heat.
  4. Add the sliced peppers and onion to the pan, and cook until they are soft and slightly caramelized.
  5. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  6. Place a cooked sausage patty on each bun.
  7. Top the sausage with a spoonful of the sautéed peppers and onions.
  8. Place the top halves of the buns on the sliders.
  9. Bake in the preheated oven for about 10 minutes, or until the sliders are heated through.
  10. Serve hot and enjoy!

3. Ham and Egg Sliders

Ingredients:

  • 12 slider buns
  • 12 slices of ham
  • 6 large eggs, fried or scrambled
  • 6 slices of Swiss cheese
  • Salt and pepper, to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. Slice the slider buns in half and arrange the bottom halves on a baking sheet.
  3. Place a slice of ham on each bun.
  4. Top the ham with a spoonful of fried or scrambled eggs and a slice of Swiss cheese.
  5. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  6. Place the top halves of the buns on the sliders.
  7. Bake in the preheated oven for about 10 minutes, or until the cheese is melted and the sliders are heated through.
  8. Serve hot and enjoy!

Conclusion

Breakfast sliders are a fantastic way to enjoy a bite-sized meal packed with flavor and nutrition. Whether you’re looking for a savory combination of bacon, egg, and cheese, a hearty mix of sausage and peppers, or a classic ham and egg combination, these recipes have got you covered. The best part? You can customize the ingredients based on your preferences, making these sliders a versatile and delicious option for breakfast. So, next time you’re in need of a quick and satisfying morning meal, give these breakfast sliders a try. You won’t be disappointed!
