The Perfect Sunday Brunch: Croque Madame


Who doesn’t love a leisurely Sunday brunch? It’s the perfect way to relax and indulge in delicious food before starting a new week. If you’re looking for a brunch dish that’s both comforting and elegant, look no further than the classic French favorite, Croque Madame. This delightful dish combines the richness of a grilled ham and cheese sandwich with the luxury of a perfectly cooked egg on top. It’s a true crowd-pleaser that will make your Sunday brunch extra special.

What is Croque Madame?

Croque Madame is a variation of the Croque Monsieur, a beloved French sandwich made with ham and cheese. What sets the Croque Madame apart is the addition of a sunny-side-up or poached egg on top of the sandwich. The name “Croque Madame” is said to come from the egg resembling a woman’s hat, making it a whimsical and charming addition to this classic dish.

Ingredients

For the Sandwich:

  • 8 slices of thick white bread
  • 8 slices of ham
  • 2 cups of Gruyère cheese, grated
  • 4 tablespoons of unsalted butter
  • 4 tablespoons of all-purpose flour
  • 2 cups of whole milk
  • 1 teaspoon of Dijon mustard
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • A pinch of nutmeg
  • 4 large eggs

Optional Garnish:

  • Fresh parsley, chopped
  • Chives, finely chopped

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Bechamel Sauce:

    • In a medium saucepan, melt the butter over medium heat.
    • Add the flour and whisk constantly for about 2 minutes to make a roux.
    • Gradually whisk in the milk until the mixture thickens and becomes a smooth sauce.
    • Stir in the Dijon mustard, salt, pepper, and nutmeg. Set the bechamel sauce aside.

  2. Assemble the Sandwiches:

    • Preheat the oven to 400°F (200°C).
    • Place 4 slices of bread on a baking sheet and top each with a slice of ham and a generous amount of Gruyère cheese.
    • Place the remaining 4 slices of bread on top to form sandwiches.
    • Spread a layer of the prepared bechamel sauce over the top of each sandwich.

  3. Bake the Sandwiches:

    • Bake the sandwiches in the preheated oven for about 5-7 minutes, or until the cheese is melted and bubbly.

  4. Cook the Eggs:

    • While the sandwiches are baking, heat a non-stick skillet over medium heat.
    • Crack the eggs into the skillet and cook them sunny-side-up until the whites are set but the yolks are still runny.

  5. Assemble the Croque Madame:

    • Place a cooked egg on top of each baked sandwich.
    • Drizzle a little extra bechamel sauce over the eggs.

  6. Serve and Enjoy:

    • Garnish the Croque Madame with chopped parsley and chives, if desired.
    • Serve immediately and savor the delicious combination of flavors and textures.

Why Croque Madame?

The Croque Madame is a brunch classic for a reason. The combination of gooey cheese, savory ham, creamy bechamel sauce, and a perfectly cooked egg is simply irresistible. It’s a dish that elevates the humble sandwich to a whole new level of sophistication, making it perfect for a special occasion like a leisurely Sunday brunch.

Conclusion

Next time you’re planning a Sunday brunch, consider treating yourself and your loved ones to the indulgent delight of Croque Madame. With its rich flavors and elegant presentation, it’s sure to become a favorite on your brunch menu. So, fire up the oven, get those eggs cracking, and enjoy a memorable brunch experience with this classic French dish. Bon appétit!
