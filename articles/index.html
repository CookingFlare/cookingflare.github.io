Cheddar Cheese Rolls: Cheesy Goodness in Every Bite

If you’re a cheese lover, then these Cheddar Cheese Rolls are going to become your new favorite indulgence. Gooey, savory, and oh-so-cheesy, these rolls are perfect for a cozy night in, a family gathering, or even a potluck. Let’s dive into the recipe and discover the cheesy goodness in every bite! Ingredients For the Dough: 3 cups all-purpose flour 1 tablespoon granulated sugar 1 teaspoon salt 1 packet (2 1/4 teaspoons) active dry yeast 1 cup warm milk 1/4 cup unsalted butter, melted 1 large egg For the Filling: 2 cups shredded cheddar cheese 1/4 cup unsalted butter, softened 2 cloves garlic, minced 1 teaspoon dried parsley 1/2 teaspoon onion powder 1/2 teaspoon paprika Salt and pepper to taste For the Glaze: 2 tablespoons unsalted butter, melted 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder 1/2 teaspoon dried parsley Pinch of salt Instructions Step 1: Prepare the Dough In a large mixing bowl, combine the warm milk, sugar, and yeast. Let it sit for about 5 minutes until the mixture becomes frothy. Add the melted butter, egg, salt, and half of the flour to the yeast mixture. Mix until smooth. Gradually add the remaining flour and knead the dough until it becomes smooth and elastic. Place the dough in a greased bowl, cover it with a clean kitchen towel, and let it rise in a warm place for about 1 hour or until doubled in size. Step 2: Make the Filling In a small bowl, mix the softened butter, minced garlic, dried parsley, onion powder, paprika, salt, and pepper until well combined. Once the dough has risen, punch it down and roll it out into a rectangle about 1/4 inch thick. Spread the garlic butter mixture evenly over the dough, leaving a small border around the edges. Sprinkle the shredded cheddar cheese over the butter mixture. Step 3: Roll and Cut the Rolls Starting from one long edge, tightly roll up the dough into a log. Pinch the seam to seal. Cut the log into 12 equal pieces and place them in a greased 9x13 inch baking dish. Cover the rolls with a clean kitchen towel and let them rise for another 30 minutes. Step 4: Bake the Rolls Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C). Once the rolls have risen, bake them in the preheated oven for 20-25 minutes or until golden brown. Step 5: Add the Glaze In a small bowl, mix the melted butter, garlic powder, dried parsley, and salt. Brush the glaze over the warm rolls as soon as they come out of the oven. Enjoy the Cheesy Goodness! Once you pull these Cheddar Cheese Rolls out of the oven, the aroma of warm, cheesy goodness will fill your kitchen. Serve them warm and watch them disappear in no time. These rolls are perfect on their own, but they also make a great accompaniment to soups, stews, or as a side to your favorite main dish. So go ahead, indulge in the cheesy delight of these irresistible Cheddar Cheese Rolls! ...
