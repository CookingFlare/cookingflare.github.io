Spicy Paleo Grilled Shrimp with Pineapple Skewers


Who doesn’t love a good barbecue? The sizzling sounds, the mouthwatering aromas, and the joy of gathering around the grill with friends and family—it’s the perfect recipe for a memorable summer evening. And if you’re following a Paleo diet or simply looking for a healthy, flavorful dish, then you’re in luck. In this recipe, we’re going to show you how to make Spicy Paleo Grilled Shrimp with Pineapple Skewers that will take your taste buds on a tropical adventure. So fire up the grill, grab your skewers, and get ready for a tantalizing feast!

Ingredients

  • 1 pound of large, deveined shrimp
  • 1 tablespoon of smoked paprika
  • 1 teaspoon of cayenne pepper
  • 1 teaspoon of garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon of onion powder
  • 1 teaspoon of dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon of sea salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon of black pepper
  • 1/4 cup of coconut aminos (a Paleo-friendly alternative to soy sauce)
  • 2 tablespoons of olive oil
  • 1 ripe pineapple, peeled and cut into chunks

Instructions

  1. Preheat your grill to medium-high heat. If using wooden skewers, soak them in water for about 30 minutes to prevent them from burning.

  2. In a small bowl, combine the smoked paprika, cayenne pepper, garlic powder, onion powder, dried oregano, sea salt, and black pepper. Mix well to create a spicy seasoning blend.

  3. In a separate bowl, whisk together the coconut aminos and olive oil to create a marinade.

  4. Thread the shrimp and pineapple chunks onto the skewers, alternating between the two. Place the skewers in a shallow dish and pour the marinade over them. Make sure the shrimp and pineapple are evenly coated. Let them marinate for at least 15 minutes, allowing the flavors to meld together.

  5. Once the grill is hot, carefully place the skewers on the grates. Cook for about 2-3 minutes per side, or until the shrimp are pink and opaque. Be mindful not to overcook the shrimp, as they can become rubbery.

  6. Remove the skewers from the grill and let them rest for a few minutes. This will allow the juices to redistribute and ensure optimal flavor.

  7. Serve the Spicy Paleo Grilled Shrimp with Pineapple Skewers as a main dish or as a delightful addition to a salad or rice bowl. The combination of spicy shrimp and sweet pineapple will transport your taste buds to a tropical paradise.

Tips and Variations

  • If you prefer a milder spice level, reduce the amount of cayenne pepper in the seasoning blend.
  • Experiment with different vegetables or fruits on the skewers, such as bell peppers or cherry tomatoes, to add extra color and flavor.
  • To add a touch of freshness, squeeze some lime juice over the skewers before serving.
  • If you don’t have a grill, you can also cook the skewers in a grill pan or under the broiler in your oven. Just make sure to adjust the cooking time accordingly.

So there you have it—the recipe for Spicy Paleo Grilled Shrimp with Pineapple Skewers. It’s a dish that celebrates the vibrant flavors of summer while keeping things healthy and Paleo-friendly. Whether you’re hosting a backyard barbecue or simply craving a delicious meal, these skewers are sure to impress. So gather your loved ones, fire up the grill, and let the tropical flavors transport you to paradise. Enjoy!
