Delightful Nut-Free Strawberry Shortcake


If you’re looking for a delightful dessert that’s nut-free and bursting with the fresh flavors of summer, then this nut-free strawberry shortcake recipe is the perfect choice. With its light and fluffy cake layers, sweetened whipped cream, and juicy strawberries, this classic dessert is sure to be a hit at any gathering. Whether you’re hosting a summer barbecue, a potluck, or simply craving a sweet treat, this nut-free strawberry shortcake is a crowd-pleaser that will leave everyone asking for seconds.

Ingredients

For the Cake:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 tablespoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, cold and cut into cubes
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

For the Whipped Cream:

  • 2 cups heavy whipping cream
  • 1/4 cup powdered sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

For the Filling:

  • 4 cups fresh strawberries, hulled and sliced
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar

Instructions

  1. Preheat the Oven: Preheat your oven to 425°F (220°C). Line a baking sheet with parchment paper.

  2. Prepare the Cake: In a large mixing bowl, whisk together the flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt. Add the cubed butter and use a pastry cutter or your fingertips to work the butter into the dry ingredients until the mixture resembles coarse crumbs. In a separate bowl, mix together the milk and vanilla extract. Pour the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients and stir until just combined.

  3. Shape the Cake: Turn the dough out onto a lightly floured surface and pat it into a 1-inch thick round. Use a 3-inch round cookie cutter to cut out the shortcakes and place them on the prepared baking sheet. Gather any scraps of dough, pat it out again, and continue cutting out rounds until all the dough is used.

  4. Bake the Cake: Bake the shortcakes in the preheated oven for 15-18 minutes, or until they are golden brown. Remove them from the oven and allow them to cool on a wire rack.

  5. Prepare the Whipped Cream: In a large mixing bowl, beat the heavy whipping cream, powdered sugar, and vanilla extract together until stiff peaks form.

  6. Prepare the Strawberries: In another bowl, toss the sliced strawberries with the granulated sugar and set aside for 10-15 minutes to allow the strawberries to release their juices.

  7. Assemble the Shortcake: Once the shortcakes have cooled, carefully slice each one in half horizontally. Spoon a generous dollop of whipped cream onto the bottom half of each shortcake, followed by a spoonful of the macerated strawberries. Place the top half of the shortcake on top and add another dollop of whipped cream and a few more strawberries.

  8. Serve and Enjoy: Serve the nut-free strawberry shortcakes immediately and bask in the applause from your delighted guests!

Conclusion

This nut-free strawberry shortcake recipe offers a delightful twist on the classic dessert, making it safe for those with nut allergies to enjoy. The combination of tender cake, pillowy whipped cream, and sweet strawberries creates a harmony of flavors and textures that is simply irresistible. Whether you’re celebrating a special occasion or simply treating yourself to a taste of summer, this nut-free strawberry shortcake is a surefire way to satisfy your sweet tooth and impress your guests.

So, the next time you’re in the mood for a dessert that’s both elegant and easy to make, give this nut-free strawberry shortcake recipe a try, and indulge in the simple pleasures of life.
