Instant Pot Chicken and Rice: A Simple One-Pot Meal


When it comes to quick, easy, and flavorful meals, nothing beats the convenience of the Instant Pot. One of the best dishes to prepare in this versatile kitchen appliance is Instant Pot chicken and rice. This simple one-pot meal is packed with protein, carbs, and vegetables, offering a balanced, wholesome dinner that’s perfect for busy nights when you don’t want to spend hours in the kitchen. Whether you’re a beginner in the kitchen or a seasoned cook, this recipe will quickly become a family favorite.

Why Choose Instant Pot Chicken and Rice?

The Instant Pot has revolutionized cooking by cutting down cooking times while retaining the depth of flavor you’d typically achieve with slow-cooking methods. With its one-pot functionality, cleaning up becomes a breeze, making it a go-to tool for busy individuals or families. Instant Pot chicken and rice is the perfect combination of tender chicken, fluffy rice, and savory seasoning. It’s versatile, customizable, and can be easily adapted to suit different tastes.

The Benefits of One-Pot Meals

One-pot meals like Instant Pot chicken and rice offer numerous benefits. First and foremost, they are incredibly convenient. You don’t have to manage multiple pots or pans, which means less prep work and fewer dishes to clean up afterward. The Instant Pot also helps infuse flavors by cooking everything together, ensuring that each ingredient is perfectly seasoned. The result is a delicious, comforting meal that requires minimal effort.

Moreover, using an Instant Pot means less time spent cooking, allowing you to have a healthy, homemade meal on the table in just under an hour. Plus, this recipe is highly customizable. You can add vegetables, adjust the seasoning, or switch up the type of rice to suit your preferences.

Ingredients for Instant Pot Chicken and Rice

To make a delicious and nutritious Instant Pot chicken and rice, you’ll need the following ingredients:

  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts (or thighs for a richer flavor)
  • 1 ½ cups long-grain white rice (you can substitute with brown rice or jasmine rice)
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil or butter
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup chicken broth (low-sodium for a healthier option)
  • 1 ½ cups water
  • 1 teaspoon salt (adjust to taste)
  • ½ teaspoon black pepper
  • ½ teaspoon paprika (optional, for added flavor)
  • ½ teaspoon dried thyme or Italian seasoning
  • 1 cup frozen peas (or any other vegetable of your choice, such as carrots or corn)
  • Fresh parsley for garnish (optional)

Optional Add-ins:

  • 1 bell pepper, diced
  • 1 cup diced tomatoes (for added moisture and flavor)
  • ½ cup shredded cheese (for a cheesy version)

This basic recipe is incredibly versatile and can be modified to suit your family’s taste. You can include other vegetables, use different types of rice, or experiment with spices to create your perfect dish.

How to Make Instant Pot Chicken and Rice

Step 1: Sauté the Aromatics

Start by setting your Instant Pot to the “Sauté” function. Add 2 tablespoons of olive oil or butter to the pot. Once the oil is hot, add the chopped onion and cook for about 3-4 minutes until softened. Next, stir in the minced garlic and cook for an additional 30 seconds, or until fragrant. Sautéing the onions and garlic first builds a strong flavor base that will enhance the entire dish.

Step 2: Brown the Chicken

While the onions and garlic are cooking, season the chicken breasts with salt, pepper, and paprika. Once the onions and garlic are ready, move them to the sides of the pot and add the chicken to the center. Brown the chicken on both sides for about 2-3 minutes per side, just until golden brown. This step helps seal in the juices of the chicken and adds a savory flavor to the dish. You don’t need to cook the chicken all the way through, as it will finish cooking during the pressure cooking stage.

Step 3: Add Rice and Liquids

Once the chicken is browned, add the rice to the Instant Pot, followed by the chicken broth and water. Scrape the bottom of the pot with a wooden spoon to deglaze it, making sure no bits of onion or chicken are stuck to the bottom. These browned bits add a ton of flavor to the dish, so it’s important to loosen them up.

Season with salt, pepper, and dried thyme (or Italian seasoning). Stir everything together to combine the ingredients evenly. At this point, your Instant Pot should have all the necessary components for a flavorful chicken and rice dish.

Step 4: Pressure Cook

Now it’s time to pressure cook! Seal the Instant Pot lid, making sure the vent is set to “Sealing.” Select the “Pressure Cook” or “Manual” setting and set the cooking time for 10 minutes on high pressure. The Instant Pot will take a few minutes to come to pressure, and once it does, it will cook for 10 minutes. It’s important to follow this time to ensure the chicken is cooked through and the rice is perfectly tender.

Step 5: Natural Release and Add Vegetables

Once the cooking time is up, allow the Instant Pot to naturally release the pressure for 10 minutes. This means you don’t need to touch the valve; just let it sit. After 10 minutes, carefully switch the valve to “Venting” to release any remaining pressure.

When you open the Instant Pot, you’ll find the chicken and rice fully cooked, with the rice fluffy and tender, and the chicken juicy. Now, stir in the frozen peas (or any vegetables you’ve chosen) and let them warm through with the residual heat. This will only take a couple of minutes.

Step 6: Shred the Chicken (Optional)

If you prefer shredded chicken, take the chicken breasts out of the pot and use two forks to shred them into bite-sized pieces. Then, return the shredded chicken to the pot and stir it into the rice mixture. This adds an extra layer of flavor, as the shredded chicken soaks up all the delicious juices.

Step 7: Serve and Garnish

Your Instant Pot chicken and rice is now ready to serve! For an extra touch of freshness, sprinkle some chopped parsley over the top. If you prefer a cheesy version, you can sprinkle shredded cheese on top and let it melt before serving.

This dish pairs wonderfully with a simple side salad or steamed vegetables. It’s perfect for family dinners, meal prep, or even a quick weeknight dinner that can be on the table in no time.

Tips for the Perfect Instant Pot Chicken and Rice

  1. Rice Variety: While this recipe uses long-grain white rice, you can experiment with other types of rice, such as brown rice, jasmine rice, or basmati rice. Keep in mind that different types of rice may require different cooking times and liquid amounts. Brown rice, for example, typically needs more cooking time and extra liquid.

  2. Chicken Thighs vs. Breasts: While chicken breasts are a great option, chicken thighs add more flavor and tenderness to the dish. If you use bone-in chicken thighs, increase the cooking time by 5 minutes, and be sure to remove the bones before serving.

  3. Vegetable Variations: You can add a variety of vegetables to this dish. Some great options include bell peppers, diced carrots, corn, or spinach. Just make sure to add heartier vegetables early in the cooking process, and more delicate ones, like spinach, toward the end.

  4. Adjusting the Seasoning: Feel free to tweak the seasoning based on your preferences. If you love heat, consider adding some chili flakes, or if you prefer more of a Mediterranean flavor, try adding a touch of lemon zest or olives. The Instant Pot is incredibly forgiving, so don’t be afraid to get creative.

  5. Double the Recipe: If you’re feeding a larger group, simply double the ingredients, keeping the cooking time the same. The Instant Pot can handle larger quantities, and this recipe is easy to scale.

Storing and Reheating Leftovers

One of the best things about Instant Pot chicken and rice is that it stores and reheats beautifully. To store leftovers, let the dish cool completely, then transfer it to an airtight container. It will keep in the refrigerator for up to 4 days. You can also freeze it for up to 3 months. When reheating, add a splash of water or chicken broth to help maintain the moisture, as rice tends to dry out when stored.

Final Thoughts

Instant Pot chicken and rice is a meal that combines ease, flavor, and convenience all in one dish. Whether you’re cooking for yourself or your family, this one-pot recipe makes the perfect, comforting dinner. By using the Instant Pot, you can make this classic dish in a fraction of the time it would take on the stovetop, without sacrificing taste or texture. Plus, with endless customization options, you can create a version that suits your unique tastes and dietary preferences.

So, next time you’re looking for a quick and delicious meal, grab your Instant Pot and give this chicken and rice recipe a try. It’s sure to become a staple in your meal rotation, providing you with a comforting, hearty dish that’s easy to make and even easier to enjoy.
