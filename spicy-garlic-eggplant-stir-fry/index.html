Spicy Garlic Eggplant Stir Fry


Are you craving a delicious and flavorful vegetarian dish that will tantalize your taste buds? Look no further than this Spicy Garlic Eggplant Stir Fry recipe! This dish is a perfect balance of spicy, garlicky goodness combined with the unique texture of eggplant. It’s a simple yet satisfying dish that is sure to become a favorite in your recipe repertoire.

Ingredients:

  • 2 medium-sized eggplants, cubed
  • 3 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 red chili, thinly sliced
  • 1 tablespoon rice vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon hoisin sauce
  • 1 teaspoon cornstarch
  • 1/2 cup water
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Chopped green onions for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the Eggplant: Start by cubing the eggplants into bite-sized pieces. Place them in a bowl of salted water for about 15 minutes to remove any bitterness. Rinse and pat dry with paper towels.

  2. Create the Sauce: In a small bowl, mix the soy sauce, rice vinegar, hoisin sauce, and cornstarch. Ensure that the cornstarch is fully dissolved in the mixture.

  3. Stir-Fry: Heat the vegetable oil in a large pan or wok over medium-high heat. Add the minced garlic and sliced red chili, sautéing for about 30 seconds until fragrant.

  4. Add Eggplant: Carefully add the prepared eggplant to the pan. Stir-fry for 5-7 minutes until the eggplant is slightly browned and tender.

  5. Incorporate Sauce: Pour the sauce mixture over the eggplant, stirring well to coat each piece. Add water to the pan and let the sauce simmer and thicken for another 2-3 minutes.

  6. Seasoning: Season with salt and pepper according to your taste preference. Ensure the eggplant is cooked to your desired level of tenderness.

  7. Garnish and Serve: Once the eggplant is cooked to perfection, garnish with chopped green onions for a pop of color and freshness.

Serving Suggestions:

This Spicy Garlic Eggplant Stir Fry pairs beautifully with steamed white rice or noodles. For an extra kick, you can sprinkle some sesame seeds or drizzle a touch of chili oil on top. This dish is not only a feast for the taste buds but also a feast for the eyes with its vibrant colors and aromatic flavors.

So, next time you’re looking for a quick and satisfying meal that’s bursting with flavor, give this Spicy Garlic Eggplant Stir Fry a try. It’s a dish that proves vegetarian cooking can be exciting and utterly delicious. Enjoy every spicy, garlicky bite!

Remember, cooking is all about experimenting and making the recipe your own. Feel free to adjust the spice levels or add your favorite veggies to make this dish uniquely yours. Happy cooking!
