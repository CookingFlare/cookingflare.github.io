Hearty Cornbread Stuffing Recipe to Complement Your Thanksgiving Meal


Thanksgiving is just around the corner, and what better way to celebrate this cherished holiday than with a delicious and hearty cornbread stuffing? This classic side dish is the perfect complement to your Thanksgiving meal, adding a touch of warmth and comfort to your plate. In this recipe, I will guide you through the process of creating a mouthwatering cornbread stuffing that will have your guests coming back for seconds.

Ingredients

  • 1 batch of homemade cornbread (recipe below)
  • 8 slices of bacon, chopped
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 3 stalks of celery, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon dried sage
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • 1 teaspoon dried rosemary
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • 2 cups chicken or vegetable broth
  • 2 eggs, beaten

Homemade Cornbread Recipe

  • 1 cup cornmeal
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 tablespoon baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup buttermilk
  • 1/4 cup unsalted butter, melted
  • 1 egg, beaten

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease a 9x9 inch baking dish and set aside.

  2. In a large skillet, cook the chopped bacon over medium heat until crispy. Remove the bacon from the skillet and set aside, leaving the bacon grease in the skillet.

  3. In the same skillet, sauté the onion, celery, and garlic in the bacon grease until they are soft and translucent. This will infuse your stuffing with a rich and savory flavor.

  4. In a large bowl, crumble the cornbread into small pieces. Add the cooked bacon, sautéed vegetables, dried sage, dried thyme, dried rosemary, salt, and black pepper. Mix well to combine.

  5. In a separate bowl, whisk together the chicken or vegetable broth and beaten eggs. Pour the mixture over the cornbread mixture and stir until everything is evenly coated.

  6. Transfer the mixture to the greased baking dish and spread it out evenly. Bake in the preheated oven for 30-35 minutes, or until the top is golden brown and the stuffing is heated through.

  7. Once cooked, remove the cornbread stuffing from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes before serving. This will allow the flavors to meld together and ensure a perfect texture.

  8. Serve your hearty cornbread stuffing alongside your Thanksgiving turkey, mashed potatoes, and other favorite sides. Your guests will be delighted by the comforting taste and unique flavors of this classic dish.

Homemade Cornbread Recipe Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C). Grease a 9-inch square baking pan and set aside.

  2. In a large bowl, whisk together the cornmeal, flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt.

  3. In a separate bowl, mix together the buttermilk, melted butter, and beaten egg.

  4. Pour the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients and stir until just combined. Be careful not to overmix, as this can lead to a dense cornbread.

  5. Pour the batter into the greased baking pan and spread it out evenly.

  6. Bake in the preheated oven for 20-25 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.

  7. Once baked, remove the cornbread from the oven and let it cool before crumbling it for the stuffing recipe.

Conclusion

With this hearty cornbread stuffing recipe, you can elevate your Thanksgiving feast to new heights. The combination of crispy bacon, sautéed vegetables, and fragrant herbs creates a stuffing that is both comforting and flavorful. Serve it alongside your favorite Thanksgiving dishes, and watch as your guests indulge in this delectable side dish. From your first bite to your last, this cornbread stuffing will leave you feeling satisfied and grateful for the joys of the holiday season. Happy Thanksgiving!
