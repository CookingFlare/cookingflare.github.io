Dracula's Favorite Cake: Chocolate Black Hole Cupcakes


Introducing the dark and delectable Chocolate Black Hole Cupcakes, a hauntingly delicious dessert inspired by the vampire king himself, Count Dracula. Embark on a culinary adventure that will transport you straight to the gothic world of Transylvania, tantalizing your taste buds with each bite. Let’s get our whisking aprons ready, as we’ve stocked up your ingredients and gathered step-by-step instructions, all while enticing your senses with the eerily delightful smell and taste of these irresistible cupcakes.

Anyyypoe! Unleash the dark side in your kitchen this Halloween with these rich, moist and fudgy cupcakes, leaving your guests craving more. Here’s the secret ingredient: a dash of sinister charm that no ordinary cake possesses. Behold, the Chocolate Black Hole Cupcakes!

Ingredients:

  • 1 ½ cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • ¾ cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1 ½ tsp baking powder -¼ tsp baking soda
  • ½ tsp salt
  • 1 cup whole milk
  • 1/2 cup vegetable oil
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 tbsp distilled white vinegar
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • Green food coloring (optional)
  • 1 cup semi-sweet chocolate chips
  • 1 cup dark chocolate shavings

For the Black Hole Filling:

  • 6 oz dark chocolate
  • ¾ cup heavy cream
  • 1 tbsp powdered sugar

For the Blackberry Ghost Buttercream Frosting:

  • 1 ½ cups unsalted butter, softened
  • 5 cups powdered sugar
  • ¼ cup blackberry jam
  • 1 tbsp heavy cream

For the Vampire’s Kiss Chocolate Ganache Glaze:

  • 8 oz semi-sweet chocolate, chopped
  • 6 tbsp heavy cream
  • 1 tbsp dark cocoa powder

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (180°C). Line a 12-cup muffin tin with cupcake liners.
  2. In a large bowl, whisk together the flour, sugar, cocoa powder, baking powder, baking soda, and salt.
  3. In a separate bowl, whisk together the milk, vegetable oil, eggs, vinegar, and vanilla extract. Add the green food coloring, a few drops at a time, if desired for an eerie green hue.
  4. Gradually pour the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients, mixing until well combined.
  5. Gently fold in the chocolate chips and dark chocolate shavings using a rubber spatula.
  6. Divide the batter evenly among the prepared cupcake liners, filling each about ⅔ full.
  7. Bake for 20-25 minutes or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean. Allow the cupcakes to cool in the pan for 5 minutes, then transfer to a wire rack to cool completely.

Now that the base has set, it’s time to create the eerie fillings that will transform these cupcakes into a black hole of dark delight!

  1. For the Black Hole filling, combine the dark chocolate and heavy cream in a heatproof bowl set over a saucepan of simmering water. Stir until the chocolate melts and the mixture is smooth. Whisk in the powdered sugar until well combined, then remove from heat and allow the filling to cool slightly.
  2. Using a small paring knife, cut a small hole into the top of each cooled cupcake. Fill a piping bag fitted with a small round tip with the black hole filling, and carefully pipe it into each cupcake.
  3. Next, the Blackberry Ghost Buttercream Frosting is set to spook your taste buds! Cream together the unsalted butter and powdered sugar until light and fluffy. Stir in the blackberry jam and heavy cream, and beat until well combined. Pipe the frosting evenly onto the filled cupcakes, creating ghost shapes on the frosted surface with a toothpick or skewer.

Behold, the final act of creating Dracula’s infamously bewitching chocolate cupcake!

  1. For the Vampire’s Kiss Chocolate Ganache Glaze, combine the chopped semi-sweet chocolate, heavy cream, and dark cocoa powder in a heatproof bowl set over a saucepan of simmering water. Stir until the chocolate melts and becomes smooth. Remove from heat and let it cool for 5-7 minutes, until slightly thickened.
  2. Carefully drizzle the Chocolate Ganache Glaze over each frosted cupcake, resembling the iconic romance between Dracula and his eternal love interests.

Serve these tantalizing morsels at your Halloween gathering, and watch them disappear right before your eyes like a black hole! The enchanted combination of flavors will make your guests question the existence of ‘regular’ chocolate cakes.
