Easy Keto Pepperoni Pizza Casserole: A Low-Carb Twist on a Favorite


Are you a fan of pizza but trying to stick to a low-carb or keto diet? Look no further than this delicious and easy Keto Pepperoni Pizza Casserole! It’s a fantastic way to satisfy your pizza cravings while staying on track with your dietary goals. This recipe is loaded with all the classic pizza flavors you love, minus the carb-heavy crust. Let’s dive into how to make this mouthwatering dish that’s sure to become a regular in your meal rotation.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 teaspoon Italian seasoning
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon onion powder
  • 1/2 cup sugar-free marinara sauce
  • 1 1/2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup sliced pepperoni
  • 1/4 cup sliced black olives
  • Fresh basil, for garnish
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C) and grease a 9x13 inch baking dish.

  2. In a skillet over medium heat, cook the ground beef until browned. Drain any excess fat.

  3. Season the ground beef with Italian seasoning, garlic powder, and onion powder. Add salt and pepper to taste.

  4. Spread the ground beef in an even layer in the prepared baking dish.

  5. Pour the sugar-free marinara sauce over the ground beef and spread it out evenly.

  6. Sprinkle the shredded mozzarella cheese over the sauce layer.

  7. Arrange the sliced pepperoni over the cheese, followed by the sliced black olives.

  8. Place the casserole in the preheated oven and bake for 20-25 minutes, or until the cheese is bubbly and starting to brown.

  9. Once done, remove the casserole from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes.

  10. Garnish with fresh basil before serving.

Why You’ll Love This Recipe

This Easy Keto Pepperoni Pizza Casserole is a game-changer for anyone following a low-carb or keto lifestyle. It captures all the flavors of a classic pepperoni pizza without the guilt of a carb-loaded crust. The combination of savory ground beef, gooey melted cheese, zesty marinara sauce, and zesty pepperoni creates a symphony of flavors that will have your taste buds singing.

Not only is this casserole incredibly delicious, but it’s also a breeze to make. With just a handful of simple ingredients and minimal prep time, you can have a wholesome and satisfying meal on the table in no time. It’s perfect for busy weeknights when you want something hearty and comforting without spending hours in the kitchen.

Another reason to love this recipe is its versatility. Feel free to customize it to your liking by adding your favorite pizza toppings, such as bell peppers, mushrooms, or even more cheese. You can make it your own while still keeping it keto-friendly.

Final Thoughts

In conclusion, this Easy Keto Pepperoni Pizza Casserole is a fantastic way to enjoy the flavors of pizza while sticking to your low-carb or keto lifestyle. Whether you’re cooking for yourself, your family, or hosting a gathering, this casserole is sure to be a hit. It’s a simple, flavorful, and satisfying dish that proves you don’t have to sacrifice taste to stay committed to your dietary goals. Give it a try and get ready to fall in love with pizza night all over again!
