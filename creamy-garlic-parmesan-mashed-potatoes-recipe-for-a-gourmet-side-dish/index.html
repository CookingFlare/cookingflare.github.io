Creamy Garlic Parmesan Mashed Potatoes Recipe for a Gourmet Side Dish


When it comes to creating a gourmet side dish that will elevate any meal, look no further than these Creamy Garlic Parmesan Mashed Potatoes. This indulgent recipe combines the rich and creamy texture of mashed potatoes with the savory flavors of garlic and Parmesan cheese, resulting in a dish that is sure to impress even the most discerning palates.

Ingredients

  • 4 large Russet potatoes, peeled and cubed
  • 4 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • ½ cup unsalted butter
  • ½ cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh chives, chopped (for garnish)

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Potatoes: Begin by peeling the Russet potatoes and cutting them into evenly sized cubes. This will ensure that the potatoes cook evenly and result in a smooth texture. Place the cubed potatoes in a large pot and cover them with cold water.

  2. Boil the Potatoes: Place the pot of potatoes on the stove over medium-high heat. Bring the water to a boil and cook the potatoes until they are fork-tender, which should take approximately 15-20 minutes. Once the potatoes are cooked, drain them thoroughly and return them to the pot.

  3. Infuse with Garlic: While the potatoes are still hot, add the minced garlic to the pot. The heat from the potatoes will help to infuse the garlic flavor throughout the dish. Use a potato masher to begin mashing the potatoes and garlic together.

  4. Add the Cream and Butter: Pour the heavy cream and unsalted butter into the pot with the potatoes and garlic. Continue mashing the mixture until the cream and butter are fully incorporated, and the potatoes are smooth and creamy.

  5. Stir in the Parmesan: Once the potatoes are creamy, stir in the grated Parmesan cheese. The cheese will add a rich and savory flavor to the mashed potatoes, elevating them to gourmet status. Season the potatoes with salt and pepper to taste, adjusting the seasoning as needed.

  6. Serve and Garnish: Transfer the Creamy Garlic Parmesan Mashed Potatoes to a serving dish and garnish with freshly chopped chives for a pop of color and a hint of freshness. These mashed potatoes are best served hot, allowing the creamy texture and bold flavors to shine.

Tips for Success

  • Use Russet potatoes for the best texture and flavor in mashed potatoes.
  • Be sure to thoroughly drain the cooked potatoes to prevent them from becoming soggy.
  • Adjust the amount of garlic and Parmesan to suit your personal taste preferences.

Final Thoughts

These Creamy Garlic Parmesan Mashed Potatoes are the perfect gourmet side dish to accompany a wide range of main courses, from roasted meats to seafood and beyond. The creamy texture, combined with the robust flavors of garlic and Parmesan, will undoubtedly make this dish a standout at any gathering or special occasion. Whether you’re hosting a dinner party or simply craving a luxurious side dish, these mashed potatoes are sure to delight and impress. So, roll up your sleeves, gather your ingredients, and get ready to create a gourmet masterpiece in the form of Creamy Garlic Parmesan Mashed Potatoes. Cheers to indulgence and culinary delight!
