Homemade Salted Caramel Ice Cream: A Step-by-Step Guide


There’s something irresistible about the smooth, creamy texture of homemade ice cream, especially when it’s infused with the rich, indulgent flavor of salted caramel. In this step-by-step guide, I’ll walk you through how to create your own batch of heavenly Homemade Salted Caramel Ice Cream that will have your taste buds singing with delight.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups heavy cream
  • 1 cup whole milk
  • 3/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/4 cup water
  • 4 large egg yolks
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/2 teaspoon sea salt

Instructions

Step 1: Prepare the Salted Caramel

  1. In a heavy-bottomed saucepan, combine the granulated sugar and water over medium heat. Stir gently until the sugar has dissolved.

  2. Once the sugar has dissolved, stop stirring and allow the mixture to come to a boil. Swirl the pan occasionally to ensure the sugar caramelizes evenly. This process may take 10-12 minutes. Keep a close eye on the caramel to prevent burning.

  3. Once the caramel reaches a deep amber color, remove the pan from the heat and slowly pour in the heavy cream while stirring constantly. Be cautious as the mixture will bubble vigorously.

  4. Return the pan to the heat and stir until the caramel has fully dissolved into the cream. Then, stir in the sea salt. Set the caramel aside to cool slightly.

Step 2: Prepare the Ice Cream Base

  1. In a separate saucepan, heat the whole milk over medium heat until it begins to steam, but not boil.

  2. In a bowl, whisk the egg yolks until they are slightly thickened. Gradually pour the warm milk into the egg yolks, whisking constantly.

  3. Pour the egg and milk mixture back into the saucepan and cook over low heat, stirring constantly, until the mixture thickens enough to coat the back of a spoon. This should take about 5-7 minutes.

  4. Strain the custard through a fine-mesh sieve into a clean bowl. Then, stir in the vanilla extract.

Step 3: Combine the Caramel and Base

  1. Gradually pour the slightly cooled salted caramel into the custard base, whisking constantly until fully incorporated.

  2. Allow the mixture to cool to room temperature, then cover and refrigerate for at least 4 hours or preferably overnight.

Step 4: Churn the Ice Cream

  1. Once the custard is thoroughly chilled, pour it into your ice cream maker and churn according to the manufacturer’s instructions until it reaches a soft-serve consistency.

  2. Transfer the churned ice cream to a freezer-safe container, drizzling in additional salted caramel as you layer the ice cream.

  3. Cover the container with a tight-fitting lid and freeze for at least 4 hours, or until the ice cream is firm.

Step 5: Serve and Enjoy!

Scoop the luscious Homemade Salted Caramel Ice Cream into bowls, and if desired, drizzle with extra salted caramel for an extra indulgent touch. This creamy, dreamy treat is perfect for any occasion and will leave your guests clamoring for more.

Conclusion

Homemade Salted Caramel Ice Cream is a decadent delight that’s surprisingly simple to make. With its velvety texture and irresistible balance of sweet and salty flavors, this ice cream is sure to become a favorite in your dessert repertoire. So, roll up your sleeves, gather your ingredients, and get ready to indulge in the luxurious world of homemade ice cream.
