Sizzling BBQ Pulled Pork Tacos: Perfect for Taco Tuesday


Taco Tuesday is a beloved tradition that brings people together to enjoy delicious and flavorful tacos. If you’re looking to elevate your Taco Tuesday experience, look no further than these mouthwatering Sizzling BBQ Pulled Pork Tacos. Packed with smoky, tender pulled pork and a medley of vibrant toppings, these tacos are sure to impress both friends and family. So fire up your grill, gather your ingredients, and get ready to savor the sizzle!

Ingredients

For the pulled pork:

  • 2.5 pounds (1.1 kg) pork shoulder
  • 1 cup (240 ml) BBQ sauce
  • 1/4 cup (60 ml) apple cider vinegar
  • 2 tablespoons (30 ml) Worcestershire sauce
  • 2 tablespoons (30 ml) brown sugar
  • 1 tablespoon (15 ml) smoked paprika
  • 1 tablespoon (15 ml) garlic powder
  • 1 tablespoon (15 ml) onion powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste

For the tacos:

  • 12 small tortillas (corn or flour)
  • 1 cup (240 ml) shredded lettuce
  • 1 cup (240 ml) diced tomatoes
  • 1/2 cup (120 ml) diced red onion
  • 1/2 cup (120 ml) chopped cilantro
  • Lime wedges for serving

Instructions

  1. Preheat your grill to medium heat.

  2. In a small bowl, combine the BBQ sauce, apple cider vinegar, Worcestershire sauce, brown sugar, smoked paprika, garlic powder, onion powder, salt, and pepper. Mix well to create a flavorful marinade.

  3. Place the pork shoulder on a large piece of aluminum foil and generously coat it with the marinade, making sure to cover all sides. Wrap the pork tightly with the foil to create a packet.

  4. Place the foil packet on the preheated grill and close the lid. Allow the pork to cook for approximately 2.5 to 3 hours, or until it reaches an internal temperature of 195°F (90°C). The low and slow cooking process will ensure that the pork becomes tender and flavorful.

  5. Once the pork is cooked, carefully remove it from the grill and let it rest for 10 to 15 minutes. Then, using two forks, shred the pork into small, bite-sized pieces.

  6. Warm the tortillas on the grill for a minute or two on each side until they become soft and pliable.

  7. To assemble the tacos, place a generous amount of pulled pork onto each tortilla. Top with shredded lettuce, diced tomatoes, diced red onion, and chopped cilantro. Squeeze some fresh lime juice over the top for an extra burst of flavor.

  8. Serve the Sizzling BBQ Pulled Pork Tacos immediately and enjoy the sizzle and taste explosion with every bite!

Tips and Variations

  • If you don’t have a grill, you can also cook the pork shoulder in a slow cooker on low heat for 8 to 10 hours. The result will still be tender and delicious.
  • Feel free to customize your toppings based on your preferences. Add sliced jalapeños for a spicy kick or sprinkle some crumbled queso fresco for a creamy finish.
  • For a vegetarian twist, substitute the pulled pork with grilled portobello mushrooms or jackfruit. Marinate them in the BBQ sauce mixture and grill until tender.

Now that you have the recipe for these delectable Sizzling BBQ Pulled Pork Tacos, it’s time to gather your loved ones and make Taco Tuesday a memorable and flavorful affair. So fire up the grill, indulge in the smoky aroma, and savor each bite of these scrumptious tacos. Happy cooking!
