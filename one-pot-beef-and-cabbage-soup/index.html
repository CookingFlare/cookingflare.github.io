One Pot Beef and Cabbage Soup


If you’re looking for a comforting and hearty meal that’s easy to make and packed with flavor, then this One Pot Beef and Cabbage Soup is the perfect recipe for you. It’s a wholesome dish that combines tender beef, aromatic vegetables, and savory broth, all cooked together in one pot for easy cleanup. Whether you’re cooking for a crowd or simply want to enjoy a cozy meal at home, this soup is sure to satisfy your cravings.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound of stew beef, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 4 cups of beef broth
  • 1 can (14.5 ounces) diced tomatoes
  • 4 cups of chopped cabbage
  • 2 carrots, sliced
  • 2 stalks of celery, chopped
  • 1 teaspoon of dried thyme
  • 1 teaspoon of paprika
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley for garnish

Instructions

  1. Sear the Beef: In a large pot or Dutch oven, heat some oil over medium-high heat. Add the beef pieces and sear them until they’re browned on all sides. This step helps to develop rich flavor in the soup.

  2. Saute the Aromatics: Add the diced onion and minced garlic to the pot with the beef. Saute them until they’re softened and aromatic, about 3-4 minutes.

  3. Add the Broth and Tomatoes: Pour in the beef broth and diced tomatoes, including the juices from the can. Stir to combine all the ingredients.

  4. Simmer the Soup: Bring the mixture to a gentle boil, then reduce the heat to low and let it simmer for about 1 hour, or until the beef is tender.

  5. Add the Vegetables: Once the beef is tender, add the chopped cabbage, sliced carrots, and chopped celery to the pot. Season with dried thyme, paprika, salt, and pepper. Stir well to incorporate all the flavors.

  6. Continue Cooking: Let the soup cook for an additional 15-20 minutes, or until the vegetables are tender but still slightly crisp.

  7. Serve and Enjoy: Ladle the hot soup into bowls, garnish with fresh parsley, and serve with your favorite crusty bread or rolls.

Tips and Variations

  • For an extra kick of flavor, you can add a pinch of red pepper flakes or a splash of Worcestershire sauce to the soup.
  • If you prefer a thicker broth, you can stir in a tablespoon of tomato paste during the simmering process.
  • This soup can be easily adapted to include other vegetables such as potatoes, green beans, or bell peppers, depending on your preferences.

Final Thoughts

This One Pot Beef and Cabbage Soup is a versatile and nourishing dish that’s perfect for any occasion. It’s a great way to incorporate more vegetables into your diet while enjoying the rich and savory flavors of a comforting soup. Plus, with the convenience of only needing one pot for cooking and minimal cleanup, it’s a practical choice for busy weeknights or lazy weekends.

So, the next time you’re in the mood for a wholesome and satisfying meal, give this recipe a try and savor the deliciousness of a warm bowl of beef and cabbage soup. It’s a true comfort food that’s sure to become a staple in your kitchen repertoire.
