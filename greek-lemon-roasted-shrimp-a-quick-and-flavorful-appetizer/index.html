Greek Lemon Roasted Shrimp: A Quick and Flavorful Appetizer


When it comes to hosting a gathering or simply enjoying a delightful evening at home, having a collection of quick and flavorful appetizer recipes is essential. One dish that never fails to impress is Greek Lemon Roasted Shrimp. This dish is not only easy to prepare but also bursts with the vibrant flavors of the Mediterranean. Let’s dive into the recipe and learn how to create this mouthwatering appetizer that will leave your guests craving for more.

Ingredients

To start off your culinary adventure, gather the following ingredients:

  • 2 pounds of large shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 1/4 cup of olive oil
  • 1/4 cup of fresh lemon juice
  • Zest of 1 lemon
  • 4 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 teaspoon of dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon of dried thyme
  • 1 teaspoon of paprika
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley for garnish

Instructions

  1. Preheat the Oven: Begin by preheating your oven to 400°F (200°C). This ensures that the shrimp will cook evenly and develop a delicious roasted flavor.

  2. Prepare the Marinade: In a mixing bowl, combine the olive oil, fresh lemon juice, lemon zest, minced garlic, dried oregano, dried thyme, paprika, salt, and pepper. Whisk the ingredients together until they form a cohesive marinade.

  3. Marinate the Shrimp: Place the peeled and deveined shrimp in a large resealable plastic bag or a shallow dish. Pour the prepared marinade over the shrimp, ensuring that each piece is well coated. Allow the shrimp to marinate for 15-20 minutes to absorb the delightful flavors.

  4. Roast the Shrimp: Line a baking sheet with parchment paper or aluminum foil for easy cleanup. Arrange the marinated shrimp in a single layer on the prepared baking sheet. Roast the shrimp in the preheated oven for 8-10 minutes, or until they turn pink and opaque.

  5. Garnish and Serve: Once the shrimp are cooked to perfection, remove them from the oven. Garnish with freshly chopped parsley for a pop of color and an extra burst of freshness. Serve the Greek Lemon Roasted Shrimp hot, accompanied by lemon wedges for a final touch of citrus.

Tips and Variations

  • For a touch of heat, add a pinch of red pepper flakes to the marinade.
  • Experiment with different herbs and spices to personalize the flavor profile of the dish.
  • Serve the shrimp with a side of tzatziki sauce or a refreshing Greek salad for a complete Mediterranean experience.

Conclusion

In just a few simple steps, you can create a stunning appetizer that embodies the vibrant and robust flavors of Greek cuisine. The combination of zesty lemon, aromatic herbs, and succulent shrimp makes this dish a showstopper at any gathering. Whether you’re hosting a dinner party or simply craving a taste of the Mediterranean, Greek Lemon Roasted Shrimp is sure to delight your taste buds and those of your guests. Enjoy the simplicity and elegance of this quick and flavorful appetizer, and savor every bite of this culinary masterpiece.

So, there you have it – a delightful recipe that is both quick and bursting with flavor. Impress your guests with this Mediterranean-inspired appetizer and elevate your entertaining game with the vibrant taste of Greek Lemon Roasted Shrimp. Cheers to good food, good company, and unforgettable moments shared around the table!
