Easter Breakfast Casserole Recipes: Easy and Filling Dishes


Are you looking for delicious and filling dishes to serve for breakfast on Easter morning? Look no further! In this blog post, we will share with you some easy and mouthwatering Easter breakfast casserole recipes that will surely impress your family and guests. These recipes are not only flavorful but also perfect for feeding a crowd. So, let’s get started!

1. Cheesy Hash Brown Casserole

Ingredients:

  • 1 package (32 ounces) frozen shredded hash browns
  • 2 cups shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1 can (10.75 ounces) condensed cream of mushroom soup
  • 1 cup sour cream
  • 1/2 cup melted butter
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1/4 cup chopped green onions (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease a 9x13-inch baking dish.
  2. In a large bowl, combine the frozen hash browns, shredded cheese, condensed cream of mushroom soup, sour cream, melted butter, salt, and black pepper. Mix well.
  3. Spread the mixture evenly into the prepared baking dish. Top with chopped green onions, if desired.
  4. Bake for 45-50 minutes or until the casserole is golden brown and bubbly.
  5. Let it cool for a few minutes before serving. Enjoy!

2. Ham and Cheese Breakfast Casserole

Ingredients:

  • 8 cups cubed day-old bread
  • 2 cups diced cooked ham
  • 2 cups shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1 cup diced bell peppers
  • 1/2 cup diced onions
  • 8 large eggs
  • 2 cups milk
  • 1 teaspoon Dijon mustard
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease a 9x13-inch baking dish.
  2. Place the cubed bread, diced ham, shredded cheddar cheese, bell peppers, and onions in the prepared baking dish. Mix well.
  3. In a separate bowl, whisk together the eggs, milk, Dijon mustard, salt, and black pepper.
  4. Pour the egg mixture over the bread mixture in the baking dish. Press down gently to ensure all the bread is soaked.
  5. Bake for 45-50 minutes or until the casserole is set and the top is golden brown.
  6. Allow it to cool for a few minutes before serving. Serve warm and enjoy!

3. Spinach and Feta Breakfast Casserole

Ingredients:

  • 10 large eggs
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 2 cups fresh spinach, chopped
  • 1 cup crumbled feta cheese
  • 1/2 cup diced red bell pepper
  • 1/2 cup diced red onion
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease a 9x13-inch baking dish.
  2. In a large bowl, whisk together the eggs and milk until well combined.
  3. Add the chopped spinach, crumbled feta cheese, diced red bell pepper, diced red onion, minced garlic, dried oregano, salt, and black pepper to the egg mixture. Stir until all the ingredients are evenly distributed.
  4. Pour the mixture into the prepared baking dish.
  5. Bake for 35-40 minutes or until the casserole is set and the top is lightly browned.
  6. Let it cool for a few minutes before slicing and serving. Enjoy!

Conclusion

These Easter breakfast casserole recipes are not only easy to make but also incredibly delicious and filling. Whether you’re hosting a big family gathering or simply want to enjoy a special breakfast with your loved ones, these dishes are sure to impress. So, give them a try and let us know your favorite! Happy Easter and happy cooking!
