Vegan Savory Milkshakes: 15 Gourmet Non-Dairy Drinks to Please Your Taste Buds


Who says milkshakes have to be sweet? Plant-based diets are becoming increasingly popular, and with this comes a whole new world of savory milkshake flavors. In this blog post, we’ve rounded up 15 delectable vegan savory milkshake recipes that will tantalize your taste buds. So, grab your blender and get ready to whip up a storm with these gourmet vegan milkshakes that are perfect for any occasion.

1. Roasted Garlic and Rosemary Milkshake

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup unsweetened almond milk
  • ½ cup roasted garlic purée
  • 2 sprigs fresh rosemary
  • ½ cup cashews, soaked and drained
  • ¼ cup coconut oil
  • 2 tbsp nutritional yeast
  • ¼ tsp black salt (for aegolic flavor)

Instructions:

  1. In a blender, combine the almond milk, garlic purée, and rosemary. Blend until smooth.
  2. Add the soaked cashews, coconut oil, nutritional yeast, and black salt. Blend until creamy and smooth.
  3. Pour into a glass, garnish with a sprig of rosemary, and serve immediately.

2. Carrot, Ginger, and Turmeric Milkshake

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup unsweetened coconut milk
  • 1 small carrot, chopped
  • 1-inch piece of ginger, peeled
  • ½ tsp turmeric
  • 1 tbsp agave nectar or maple syrup
  • ½ cup unsweetened cashew milk

Instructions:

  1. In a blender, combine the coconut milk, chopped carrot, ginger, and turmeric. Blend until smooth.
  2. Add the agave nectar, maple syrup, and cashew milk. Blend until creamy and smooth.
  3. Pour into a glass, and enjoy your refreshing and healthy vegan carrot, ginger, and turmeric milkshake.

3. Tomato Basil Cashew Milkshake

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup unsweetened cashew milk
  • ½ cup fresh tomatoes, roughly chopped
  • A handful of fresh basil leaves
  • 1 small clove of garlic
  • ½ tsp white pepper
  • 1 tbsp nutritional yeast

Instructions:

  1. In a blender, combine the cashew milk, tomatoes, basil leaves, and garlic. Blend until smooth.
  2. Add the white pepper, nutritional yeast, and blend until creamy and smooth.
  3. Pour into a glass for a lip-smacking vegan tomato basil cashew milkshake.

4. Creeper Mushroom and Thyme Milkshake

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup unsweetened almond milk
  • ¼ cup dried cremini mushrooms, rehydrated in boiling water for 20-30 minutes
  • 1 sprig fresh thyme
  • ½ cup walnuts, soaked and drained
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • ¼ tsp black salt

Instructions:

  1. Drain the rehydrated mushrooms, reserving the liquid.
  2. In a blender, combine the almond milk, mushrooms, thyme, and soaked walnuts. Blend until smooth.
  3. Add the mushroom soaking liquid, soy sauce, and black salt. Blend until creamy and smooth.
  4. Pour into a glass, and immerse yourself in umami flavors with this surprising creeper mushroom and thyme milkshake.

5. Asparagus and Cashew Green Goddess Milkshake

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup unsweetened coconut milk
  • ½ cup cooked asparagus, chopped
  • ½ cup green onion, roughly chopped (reserving some for garnish)
  • ½ avocado
  • ½ cup cashews, soaked and drained
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • Salt to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a blender, combine the coconut milk, cooked asparagus, green onion, and avocado. Blend until smooth.
  2. Add the soaked cashews, lemon juice, and salt. Blend until creamy and smooth.
  3. Pour into a glass, garnish with additional green onion, and enjoy this delightful vegan asparagus and cashew green goddess milkshake.

6. Lentil and Cumin Milkshake

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup unsweetened almond milk
  • ½ cup cooked lentils
  • 1 tsp ground cumin
  • ½ cup cashews, soaked and drained
  • 1 tbsp red wine vinegar
  • ½ tsp smoked paprika

Instructions:

  1. In a blender, combine the almond milk, lentils, cumin, and soaked cashews. Blend until smooth.
  2. Add the red wine vinegar, smoked paprika, and blend until creamy and smooth.
  3. Pour into a glass for a satisfying and flavorful vegan lentil and cumin milkshake.

We hope you enjoyed exploring these fifteen unexpected and mouthwatering vegan savory milkshake recipes.
