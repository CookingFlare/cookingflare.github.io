Hearty Keto Creamy Tuscan Garlic Chicken Zoodle Recipe


If you’re on a keto diet and craving a hearty, creamy dish that’s bursting with flavor, then this Creamy Tuscan Garlic Chicken Zoodle recipe is a must-try. It’s a delightful combination of tender chicken, vibrant zucchini noodles, and a rich, garlicky cream sauce that will tantalize your taste buds. Plus, it’s low-carb and keto-friendly, making it a perfect addition to your meal rotation. Let’s dive into the details of this delectable dish.

Ingredients

  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup chicken broth
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 teaspoon Italian seasoning
  • 1/2 teaspoon red pepper flakes
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 3 medium zucchinis, spiralized into zoodles
  • Fresh parsley, chopped, for garnish

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Chicken: Season the chicken breasts with salt and pepper. In a large skillet over medium-high heat, add 1 tablespoon of olive oil. Once hot, add the chicken breasts and cook for 6-7 minutes on each side, or until golden brown and cooked through. Remove the chicken from the skillet and set aside.

  2. Make the Sauce: In the same skillet, add the remaining tablespoon of olive oil and the minced garlic. Sauté for about 1 minute, or until fragrant. Pour in the chicken broth and let it simmer for 5 minutes, allowing it to reduce slightly. Stir in the heavy cream, Parmesan cheese, Italian seasoning, and red pepper flakes. Bring the sauce to a gentle simmer and let it cook for 2-3 minutes until it thickens slightly.

  3. Add the Zoodles: Once the sauce has thickened, add the zucchini noodles to the skillet. Toss them gently in the sauce and let them cook for 2-3 minutes, until they are just tender but still have a slight crunch.

  4. Combine and Serve: Return the cooked chicken to the skillet, nestling it into the creamy zoodles. Let the chicken and zoodles simmer in the sauce for another 2-3 minutes. Ensure the chicken is heated through and the zoodles are well coated in the creamy sauce.

  5. Garnish and Enjoy: Sprinkle the dish with fresh chopped parsley for a pop of color and freshness. Serve the Creamy Tuscan Garlic Chicken Zoodles hot, and savor the creamy, garlicky goodness with each flavorful bite.

Conclusion

This Hearty Keto Creamy Tuscan Garlic Chicken Zoodle Recipe is a delightful way to indulge in a creamy, satisfying meal while staying true to your keto lifestyle. The tender chicken, flavorful zucchini noodles, and rich, garlicky cream sauce come together to create a dish that’s as comforting as it is delicious. Plus, with the nutritional information provided, you can easily incorporate this recipe into your keto meal plan. So, gather your ingredients, whip up this delightful dish, and relish every creamy, garlic-infused bite.
