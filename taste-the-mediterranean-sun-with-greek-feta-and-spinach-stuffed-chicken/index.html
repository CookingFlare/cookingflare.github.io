Taste the Mediterranean Sun with Greek Feta and Spinach Stuffed Chicken


When it comes to culinary pleasures, few cuisines can transport your taste buds quite like Mediterranean food. The vibrant flavors, fresh ingredients, and delightful aromas create a sensory experience that’s hard to forget. One dish that truly encapsulates the essence of this cuisine is the Greek Feta and Spinach Stuffed Chicken. This recipe not only showcases the rich flavors of Greece but also brings a healthy twist to your dinner table.

Why You’ll Love This Recipe

Imagine succulent chicken breasts filled to the brim with a creamy mixture of feta cheese, fresh spinach, and aromatic herbs. This dish is not only visually appealing but also bursting with flavor. It’s perfect for a weeknight dinner, a romantic date night, or even a gathering with friends. Plus, it’s an excellent way to incorporate more greens into your diet while enjoying a hearty protein source.

Ingredients You’ll Need

To embark on this culinary journey, gather the following ingredients:

Chicken

  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil

Filling

  • 1 cup fresh spinach, chopped
  • 1 cup feta cheese, crumbled
  • 1/4 cup cream cheese, softened
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/4 cup sun-dried tomatoes, chopped (optional)
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1/2 teaspoon red pepper flakes (optional for a spicy kick)
  • Zest of 1 lemon

For Serving

  • Fresh lemon wedges
  • A sprinkle of fresh herbs (like parsley or dill) for garnish

Step-by-Step Instructions

Step 1: Preparing the Chicken

  1. Preheat the Oven: Begin by preheating your oven to 375°F (190°C). This ensures that your chicken will cook evenly and develop a beautiful golden crust.

  2. Prepare the Chicken Breasts: Place each chicken breast between two sheets of plastic wrap or parchment paper. Using a meat mallet or rolling pin, gently pound the chicken to an even thickness of about 1/2 inch. This step helps the chicken cook evenly and makes it easier to stuff.

  3. Season the Chicken: Season both sides of the chicken breasts with salt and pepper. Don’t skimp on this step; well-seasoned chicken is the key to a flavorful dish.

Step 2: Making the Feta and Spinach Filling

  1. Sauté the Spinach: In a skillet over medium heat, add a tablespoon of olive oil. Once hot, add the chopped spinach and sauté until wilted, about 2-3 minutes. Remove from heat and allow it to cool slightly.

  2. Mix the Filling: In a mixing bowl, combine the sautéed spinach, crumbled feta cheese, cream cheese, minced garlic, sun-dried tomatoes (if using), dried oregano, dried basil, red pepper flakes, and lemon zest. Stir until well combined. The mixture should be creamy and slightly chunky, with the flavors melding beautifully.

Step 3: Stuffing the Chicken

  1. Fill the Chicken: Take the prepared chicken breasts and spoon a generous amount of the feta and spinach filling onto one side of each breast. Be careful not to overfill, as the filling may spill out during cooking.

  2. Secure the Filling: Fold the chicken over the filling and use toothpicks or kitchen twine to secure the edges, ensuring the filling stays inside during cooking.

Step 4: Cooking the Chicken

  1. Sear the Chicken: In the same skillet you used for the spinach, heat the remaining tablespoon of olive oil over medium-high heat. Once hot, carefully add the stuffed chicken breasts. Sear for about 3-4 minutes on each side until golden brown.

  2. Bake in the Oven: Transfer the seared chicken breasts to a baking dish and place them in the preheated oven. Bake for 25-30 minutes or until the internal temperature reaches 165°F (75°C). The chicken will be juicy and flavorful, with the filling perfectly melted inside.

Step 5: Serving the Dish

  1. Rest the Chicken: Once cooked, remove the chicken from the oven and let it rest for 5 minutes. This allows the juices to redistribute, ensuring every bite is tender and juicy.

  2. Slice and Serve: Carefully remove the toothpicks or twine and slice the chicken in half to reveal the delicious filling inside. Serve with fresh lemon wedges and a sprinkle of fresh herbs for a burst of color and flavor.

Pairing Suggestions

To elevate your Mediterranean meal, consider pairing the stuffed chicken with:

  • Greek Salad: A refreshing salad made with cucumbers, tomatoes, red onions, olives, and a drizzle of olive oil and lemon juice.
  • Quinoa or Rice Pilaf: Fluffy quinoa or rice cooked with herbs and spices makes for a perfect side.
  • Roasted Vegetables: A medley of seasonal vegetables tossed in olive oil, salt, and pepper, then roasted until golden.

Nutritional Benefits

This Greek Feta and Spinach Stuffed Chicken is not only delicious but also packed with nutrients.

  • Feta Cheese: Rich in calcium and protein, feta adds a creamy texture while packing a flavorful punch.
  • Spinach: Loaded with vitamins A, C, and K, as well as iron and antioxidants, spinach is a superfood that boosts your immune system.
  • Chicken: A great source of lean protein, chicken helps in muscle building and repair.

Conclusion

Bringing a taste of the Mediterranean into your home has never been easier or more satisfying. The Greek Feta and Spinach Stuffed Chicken is a dish that will impress your family and guests alike. With its vibrant flavors and healthy ingredients, it’s a meal that you’ll want to make again and again. So, gather your ingredients, channel your inner chef, and get ready to indulge in a culinary delight that truly captures the spirit of the Mediterranean sun. Enjoy your cooking adventure!
