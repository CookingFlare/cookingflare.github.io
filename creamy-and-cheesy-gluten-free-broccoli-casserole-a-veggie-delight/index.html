Creamy and Cheesy Gluten-Free Broccoli Casserole: A Veggie Delight


Are you looking for a delicious and comforting dish that’s both gluten-free and packed with veggies? Look no further than this Creamy and Cheesy Gluten-Free Broccoli Casserole! This mouthwatering casserole is the perfect way to elevate your dinner table with a wholesome and flavorful dish that will have everyone coming back for seconds.

Ingredients

  • 4 cups fresh broccoli florets
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 medium onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1/4 cup gluten-free all-purpose flour
  • 2 cups milk (dairy or plant-based)
  • 1 cup vegetable or chicken broth
  • 1 1/2 cups shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 cup gluten-free breadcrumbs
  • 2 tablespoons melted butter or olive oil

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C) and lightly grease a casserole dish.
  2. Bring a pot of water to a boil and blanch the broccoli florets for 2-3 minutes until they are bright green and slightly tender. Drain and set aside.
  3. In a large skillet, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the chopped onion and sauté until it becomes translucent, then add the minced garlic, dried thyme, and dried oregano. Cook for another minute until the garlic becomes fragrant.
  4. Sprinkle the gluten-free all-purpose flour over the onion and garlic mixture, stirring constantly to create a roux. Cook for 2-3 minutes to remove the raw flour taste.
  5. Gradually pour in the milk and vegetable or chicken broth, whisking constantly to prevent lumps from forming. Allow the mixture to simmer and thicken for about 5-7 minutes.
  6. Remove the skillet from the heat and stir in 1 cup of shredded cheddar cheese and 1/4 cup of grated Parmesan cheese until the sauce is smooth and creamy.
  7. Gently fold in the blanched broccoli florets until they are evenly coated with the creamy cheese sauce.
  8. Transfer the broccoli mixture to the prepared casserole dish and spread it out into an even layer.
  9. In a small bowl, combine the gluten-free breadcrumbs with the remaining 1/2 cup of shredded cheddar cheese and 1/4 cup of grated Parmesan cheese. Drizzle the melted butter or olive oil over the breadcrumb mixture and toss to combine.
  10. Sprinkle the breadcrumb and cheese mixture evenly over the broccoli in the casserole dish.
  11. Cover the casserole dish with aluminum foil and bake for 20 minutes. Then, remove the foil and continue baking for an additional 10-15 minutes, or until the top is golden brown and the casserole is bubbly around the edges.
  12. Allow the casserole to cool for a few minutes before serving.

Serving Suggestions

This Creamy and Cheesy Gluten-Free Broccoli Casserole is a versatile dish that can be served as a delightful side or as a satisfying main course. Pair it with a crisp green salad or some roasted vegetables for a complete meal. It’s also a great option for potlucks, family gatherings, or cozy weeknight dinners.

Final Thoughts

With its creamy texture, cheesy goodness, and the nutritious addition of broccoli, this casserole is a true veggie delight that will have everyone at the table asking for more. Whether you’re serving a crowd or simply treating your family to a comforting meal, this gluten-free broccoli casserole is sure to become a favorite in your recipe collection. Try it out and savor the wholesome flavors of this delightful dish!
