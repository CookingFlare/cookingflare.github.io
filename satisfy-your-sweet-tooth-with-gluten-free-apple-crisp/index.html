Satisfy Your Sweet Tooth with Gluten-Free Apple Crisp


Are you looking for a delicious dessert that is both gluten-free and satisfyingly sweet? Look no further than this mouthwatering recipe for Gluten-Free Apple Crisp! Packed with the flavors of fresh apples, warm spices, and a crunchy oat topping, this dessert is sure to become a favorite in your household. Plus, it’s incredibly easy to make, so you can enjoy a homemade treat without spending hours in the kitchen.

Ingredients

To make this delectable Gluten-Free Apple Crisp, you will need the following ingredients:

  • 4-5 medium-sized apples (use a variety such as Granny Smith or Honeycrisp)
  • 1/2 cup gluten-free all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup rolled oats (make sure they are certified gluten-free)
  • 1/2 cup brown sugar
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground nutmeg
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, melted

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C) and lightly grease a baking dish.

  2. Start by peeling, coring, and slicing the apples into thin wedges. Place the apple slices in the greased baking dish, making sure they are evenly distributed.

  3. In a separate bowl, combine the gluten-free all-purpose flour, rolled oats, brown sugar, granulated sugar, cinnamon, nutmeg, and salt. Mix well until all the ingredients are evenly incorporated.

  4. Gradually pour the melted butter into the dry mixture, stirring constantly. The mixture should resemble coarse crumbs.

  5. Sprinkle the crumb mixture over the apples in the baking dish, ensuring an even distribution.

  6. Place the baking dish in the preheated oven and bake for approximately 35-40 minutes, or until the top is golden brown and the apples are tender.

  7. Once baked, remove the Gluten-Free Apple Crisp from the oven and allow it to cool slightly before serving. Serve warm with a scoop of vanilla ice cream or a dollop of whipped cream for an extra indulgent treat.

  8. Enjoy your Gluten-Free Apple Crisp and savor every bite of this delightful dessert!

Tips and Variations

  • For added texture and flavor, consider incorporating chopped nuts such as pecans or walnuts into the crumb mixture.

  • If you prefer a sweeter dessert, increase the amount of brown sugar or add a drizzle of maple syrup over the top before baking.

  • For a touch of tartness, you can add a teaspoon of lemon juice to the sliced apples before assembling the crisp.

  • Experiment with different apple varieties to find your favorite combination. Each type of apple brings its unique flavor and texture to the dish.

  • Leftovers can be stored in an airtight container in the refrigerator for up to three days. Simply reheat in the oven or microwave before serving.

Conclusion

Gluten-Free Apple Crisp is the perfect dessert to satisfy your sweet tooth while adhering to a gluten-free lifestyle. This recipe is not only easy to make but also incredibly delicious and versatile. With its combination of fresh apples, warm spices, and a crunchy oat topping, it’s a dessert that everyone can enjoy. So go ahead and treat yourself to a slice of this delightful Gluten-Free Apple Crisp today!
