Irresistible Caramel Pecan Sticky Buns: A Decadent Indulgence


There are few things in life more satisfying than sinking your teeth into a warm, gooey caramel pecan sticky bun. The combination of rich caramel, crunchy pecans, and soft, fluffy dough is simply irresistible. If you’ve ever wanted to recreate this indulgent treat at home, you’re in luck. In this post, I’ll guide you through a step-by-step recipe for making your own batch of irresistible caramel pecan sticky buns that will have your friends and family begging for more.

Ingredients

To start off your sticky bun adventure, gather the following ingredients:

  • 1 package (2 1/4 teaspoons) active dry yeast
  • 1/2 cup warm water
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, melted
  • 1 cup warm milk
  • 2 eggs
  • 4 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup brown sugar, packed
  • 2 teaspoons ground cinnamon
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1 cup chopped pecans
  • 1 cup caramel sauce

Instructions

Step 1: Prepare the Dough

In a small bowl, dissolve the yeast in warm water and let it sit for about 5 minutes until it becomes frothy. In a large mixing bowl, combine the yeast mixture, sugar, salt, melted butter, warm milk, and eggs. Gradually mix in the flour to form a soft dough. Turn the dough out onto a lightly floured surface and knead for about 6-8 minutes, or until it is smooth and elastic.

Step 2: Let the Dough Rise

Place the dough in a greased bowl, turning once to coat the surface. Cover with a clean kitchen towel and let it rise in a warm place for about 1 hour, or until it has doubled in size.

Step 3: Prepare the Sticky Bun Filling

In a small bowl, mix together the brown sugar and cinnamon. Once the dough has risen, punch it down and roll it out into a large rectangle. Spread the softened butter over the dough, then sprinkle the brown sugar and cinnamon mixture evenly. Sprinkle the chopped pecans over the top.

Step 4: Roll and Cut the Buns

Starting with the long edge, tightly roll up the dough into a log. Cut the rolled dough into 12 equal pieces and place them in a greased 9x13 inch baking pan. Cover the pan with a kitchen towel and let the buns rise for an additional 30 minutes.

Step 5: Bake and Glaze

Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C). Once the buns have risen, bake them in the preheated oven for 25-30 minutes, or until they are golden brown. Remove the buns from the oven and immediately drizzle the caramel sauce over the top while they are still warm.

Enjoy Your Decadent Creation

Once the caramel pecan sticky buns have cooled slightly, grab a bun, pull it apart, and savor the sticky, sweet, and nutty goodness. These irresistible treats are perfect for breakfast, brunch, or as a delightful dessert. Share them with your loved ones and watch their faces light up with delight as they bite into the decadent indulgence that is a homemade caramel pecan sticky bun.

So, there you have it! With this easy-to-follow recipe, you can bring the heavenly aroma and flavor of caramel pecan sticky buns right into your kitchen. Put on your apron, gather your ingredients, and get ready to create a batch of these delectable treats that will have everyone coming back for more. Happy baking!
