Vegan Chocolate Chip Muffins with a Caramel Drizzle Recipe: A Soft and Chewy Treat


There’s something truly magical about sinking your teeth into a soft, moist muffin that’s bursting with melty chocolate chips. And when it’s vegan, you can indulge guilt-free while still savoring all the deliciousness. Today, I’m sharing with you a recipe for Vegan Chocolate Chip Muffins with a Caramel Drizzle that will take your taste buds on a heavenly journey.

Ingredients

For the muffins:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/2 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 and 1/2 cups almond milk (or any non-dairy milk of your choice)
  • 1/2 cup vegetable oil
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 cup vegan chocolate chips

For the caramel drizzle:

  • 1/2 cup coconut cream
  • 1/2 cup packed brown sugar
  • 2 tablespoons vegan butter
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • Pinch of salt

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C) and line a muffin tin with paper liners.

  2. In a large mixing bowl, whisk together the flour, sugar, cocoa powder, baking powder, baking soda, and salt. Ensure that all the dry ingredients are well combined.

  3. In a separate bowl, whisk together the almond milk, vegetable oil, and vanilla extract until fully incorporated.

  4. Pour the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients and gently mix until just combined. Be careful not to overmix, as this can result in dense muffins.

  5. Fold in the vegan chocolate chips, ensuring they are evenly distributed throughout the batter.

  6. Spoon the batter into the prepared muffin tin, filling each liner about 3/4 of the way full.

  7. Bake for 18-20 minutes or until a toothpick inserted into the center of a muffin comes out clean.

  8. While the muffins are baking, prepare the caramel drizzle. In a small saucepan, combine the coconut cream, brown sugar, vegan butter, vanilla extract, and salt. Cook over medium heat, stirring constantly, until the mixture thickens and reaches a caramel-like consistency. This should take about 5-7 minutes.

  9. Once the muffins are done baking, remove them from the oven and allow them to cool for a few minutes in the tin. Then transfer them to a wire rack to cool completely.

  10. Once the muffins have cooled, generously drizzle the caramel sauce over the top of each muffin, allowing it to drip down the sides.

  11. Serve these delectable vegan chocolate chip muffins with a caramel drizzle as a delightful treat for breakfast, brunch, or any time you’re craving something sweet.

  12. Store any leftovers in an airtight container at room temperature for up to 3 days. However, I doubt they’ll last that long!

Final Thoughts

These Vegan Chocolate Chip Muffins with a Caramel Drizzle are the epitome of indulgence. They boast a soft and chewy texture, with pockets of gooey chocolate and the rich sweetness of the caramel drizzle. Plus, they’re completely vegan, making them a guilt-free pleasure for all to enjoy.

So go ahead and give this recipe a try. Your taste buds will thank you, and you’ll have a new favorite muffin recipe in your repertoire. Happy baking!
