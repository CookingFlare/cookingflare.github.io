Picnic Pastries: Handheld Baked Goods for Outdoor Feasting


Nothing beats a delightful assortment of baked goods to elevate your picnic experience. Whether you’re enjoying a warm summer day or a cozy fall outing, handheld treats add an extra layer of charm. From savory bites to sweet delights, here’s a selection of irresistible picnic pastries that are sure to impress.

1. Savory Pretzel Bites

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • 2 1/4 tsp active dry yeast
  • 2 tsp salt
  • 1 cup warm water
  • 2 tbsp unsalted butter, melted
  • Sesame seeds or coarse salt for topping

Instructions:

  1. In a large mixing bowl, combine warm water, melted butter, and a pinch of sugar. Stir until the sugar dissolves.
  2. Add the yeast and mix well.
  3. Gradually add the flour, salt, and yeast mixture. Stir until well combined and a soft dough forms.
  4. Knead the dough on a lightly floured surface for about 5 minutes until smooth and elastic.
  5. Divide the dough into bite-sized pieces and roll each into a rope shape.
  6. Dip each pretzel bite into boiling water and arrange them on a parchment-lined baking sheet.
  7. Top with sesame seeds or coarse salt and bake in a preheated 425°F (220°C) oven for about 12-15 minutes, until golden brown.
  8. Allow to cool slightly and enjoy with your favorite dipping sauce.

2. Fruit and Nut Shortbread

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 6 oz powdered sugar
  • 1 cup mixed fruit and nuts (dried cranberries, apricots, almonds, and pistachios)

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 325°F (165°C) and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
  2. Cream the butter in a mixing bowl until it becomes light and fluffy.
  3. Gradually add the powdered sugar and flour, continuously beating until the mixture forms a smooth dough.
  4. Fold in the mixed fruit and nuts.
  5. On a lightly floured surface, roll the dough into a log shape, about 2 inches in diameter.
  6. Cut into slices and place them onto the prepared baking sheet, leaving enough space to spread during baking.
  7. Bake for about 20-25 minutes, until lightly golden brown.
  8. Allow the shortbread to cool for a few minutes before serving.

3. Caprese-Stuffed Bread Bites

Ingredients:

  • 1 pkg refrigerated polenta bites or prepared bread dough
  • 1 pint cherry tomatoes
  • Fresh basil
  • Balsamic glaze

Instructions:

  1. Prepare your choices of polenta bites or bread dough as per package instructions.
  2. On a lightly floured surface, shape the polenta or dough into bite-sized pieces.
  3. Arrange on a parchment-lined baking sheet, allowing enough space for expansion.
  4. Bake in a preheated 375°F (190°C) oven for approximately 20-25 minutes, or until the polenta bites or bread are golden brown.
  5. As the baking sheet-cooled, slice the cherry tomatoes in half and wash fresh basil leaves.
  6. Lightly brush a small amount of balsamic glaze on the cooled bites, then top each with a cherry tomato half and a leaf of fresh basil.

4. Homemade Soft Pretzel Dogs

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups warm water
  • 1 tbsp brown sugar
  • 2 1/4 tsp active dry yeast
  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 4 hot dogs, split lengthwise

Instructions:

  1. In a large bowl, combine warm water, brown sugar, and yeast. Allow to sit for a few minutes until the yeast becomes activated.
  2. Gradually add the flour and baking soda. Mix until the dough comes together.
  3. Knead the dough on a lightly floured surface for about 5 minutes, until smooth and elastic.
  4. Roll out the dough to about 1/2-inch thickness and cut into 8 equal pieces.
  5. Wrap each hot dog with the dough and arrange on a parchment-lined baking sheet.
  6. Cover and let the pretzel dogs rise in a warm, draft-free environment for about 20-30 minutes.
  7. Preheat oven to 400°F (200°C) and bake for approximately 15-20 minutes, or until golden brown.
  8. Allow the pretel dogs to cool slightly before serving.

As the summer picnics turn into cozy fall gatherings, your taste buds will be grateful for these delightful picnic pastries. Treat your loved ones to a unique and memorable outdoor dining experience with these handheld treats.
