Beef and Broccoli Stir-Fry: A Quick and Easy Comfort Meal


Are you looking for a delicious and satisfying meal that can be prepared in no time? Look no further than this Beef and Broccoli Stir-Fry recipe! Packed with tender beef, vibrant broccoli, and a savory sauce, this dish is the perfect combination of flavors and textures. Whether you’re a busy professional, a parent juggling multiple responsibilities, or simply someone who loves a good home-cooked meal, this recipe is sure to become a staple in your kitchen.

Ingredients

For the Stir-Fry:

  • 1 pound flank steak, thinly sliced
  • 4 cups broccoli florets
  • 1 medium onion, thinly sliced
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • Salt and pepper, to taste

For the Sauce:

  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons oyster sauce
  • 1 tablespoon hoisin sauce
  • 1 tablespoon honey
  • 1 teaspoon sesame oil
  • 1/2 teaspoon cornstarch

Instructions

  1. In a small bowl, whisk together the soy sauce, oyster sauce, hoisin sauce, honey, sesame oil, and cornstarch. Set aside.

  2. Heat one tablespoon of vegetable oil in a large skillet or wok over medium-high heat. Add the beef slices and cook until browned, about 2-3 minutes per side. Remove the beef from the skillet and set aside.

  3. In the same skillet, add the remaining tablespoon of vegetable oil. Stir in the minced garlic and sliced onion, and cook until the onion becomes translucent, about 2 minutes.

  4. Add the broccoli florets to the skillet and stir-fry for 4-5 minutes, or until they become bright green and slightly tender.

  5. Return the beef to the skillet and pour in the prepared sauce. Stir everything together to coat the beef and vegetables evenly. Continue to cook for an additional 2-3 minutes, or until the sauce has thickened and the beef is cooked to your desired level of doneness.

  6. Season with salt and pepper to taste, and give the stir-fry a final toss to combine all the flavors.

  7. Remove the skillet from the heat and transfer the Beef and Broccoli Stir-Fry to a serving dish.

Serving Suggestions

This Beef and Broccoli Stir-Fry pairs perfectly with steamed white rice or noodles. You can also serve it over a bed of quinoa or cauliflower rice for a healthier twist. For an extra kick of flavor, garnish with chopped green onions or sesame seeds.

Tips and Variations

  • To make the beef slices more tender, you can marinate them in a mixture of soy sauce, garlic, and ginger for 15-30 minutes before cooking.
  • Feel free to add other vegetables to the stir-fry, such as bell peppers, carrots, or snap peas, for added color and nutrition.
  • If you prefer a spicier stir-fry, add a pinch of red pepper flakes or a drizzle of sriracha sauce to the sauce mixture.
  • This recipe is great for meal prep! Double the ingredients and divide the stir-fry into individual portions for easy lunches or dinners throughout the week.

Conclusion

Beef and Broccoli Stir-Fry is a quick and easy comfort meal that is bursting with flavor. With tender beef, crisp broccoli, and a delicious sauce, it’s a dish that will satisfy your taste buds and leave you feeling satisfied. Whether you’re cooking for yourself or a crowd, this recipe is a winner. So why not give it a try and add it to your regular meal rotation? You won’t be disappointed!
