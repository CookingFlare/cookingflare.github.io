Candy Corn Fudge with Pecans and Sea Salt: A Quick and Easy Halloween Treat


As the leaves change color and the air turns crisp, the excitement of Halloween begins to fill the atmosphere. One of the most delightful ways to celebrate this spooky season is by indulging in sweet treats. This year, why not whip up a batch of Candy Corn Fudge with Pecans and Sea Salt? This quick and easy recipe combines the nostalgic flavors of candy corn with the rich creaminess of fudge, topped off with a sprinkle of pecans and a hint of sea salt for that perfect sweet-and-salty balance.

Why Candy Corn Fudge?

Candy corn is a quintessential Halloween candy, instantly recognizable and adored by many. By transforming this classic treat into a creamy fudge, you not only elevate its flavor profile but also create an eye-catching dessert that will impress friends and family alike. The addition of pecans adds a satisfying crunch, while the sea salt enhances the sweetness of the fudge, making each bite a delightful experience.

Ingredients You’ll Need

To make this delectable Candy Corn Fudge with Pecans and Sea Salt, you will need the following ingredients:

  • 2 cups of candy corn
  • 1 cup of sweetened condensed milk
  • 3 cups of white chocolate chips
  • 1 cup of chopped pecans
  • 1 teaspoon of vanilla extract
  • Â½ teaspoon of sea salt (plus more for garnish)
  • Optional: Food coloring (to enhance the fudge’s visual appeal)

Equipment Required

Before diving into the recipe, gather the necessary kitchen equipment:

  • A medium-sized saucepan
  • A large mixing bowl
  • A spatula
  • A baking dish (8x8 inches or similar)
  • Parchment paper
  • A microwave-safe bowl (if you prefer melting the chocolate in the microwave)

Step-by-Step Instructions

Step 1: Prepare Your Baking Dish

Start by lining your baking dish with parchment paper, allowing some overhang on the sides. This will make it easier to lift the fudge out once it has set. Set the dish aside while you prepare the fudge mixture.

Step 2: Melt the Candy Corn

In a medium-sized saucepan over low heat, combine the candy corn and sweetened condensed milk. Stir constantly until the candy corn is fully melted and the mixture becomes smooth. This should take about 5 to 7 minutes. Be patient, as melting candy corn can take a little time.

Step 3: Add the White Chocolate Chips

Once the candy corn is melted, remove the saucepan from the heat and add the white chocolate chips. Stir the mixture until the chocolate is fully melted and incorporated. If you want to add food coloring, this is the stage to do so. A few drops of orange or yellow can enhance the Halloween theme.

Step 4: Incorporate Vanilla and Pecans

Next, stir in the vanilla extract and chopped pecans. Make sure the pecans are evenly distributed throughout the fudge mixture. The nuts not only add texture but also a nutty flavor that complements the sweetness of the candy corn.

Step 5: Pour the Mixture into the Baking Dish

Carefully pour the fudge mixture into the prepared baking dish, using a spatula to spread it evenly. Smooth the top of the fudge for a polished finish.

Step 6: Sprinkle with Sea Salt

Before the fudge sets, sprinkle the top with sea salt. This will enhance the sweet flavor of the fudge and provide a contrast that makes each bite more enjoyable. Feel free to adjust the amount of sea salt based on your preference.

Step 7: Chill the Fudge

Place the baking dish in the refrigerator for at least 2 hours, or until the fudge is firm and completely set. This step is crucial as it allows the flavors to meld together and creates the perfect texture.

Step 8: Cut and Serve

Once the fudge has set, use the parchment paper overhang to lift it out of the baking dish. Place it on a cutting board and cut it into squares or rectangles, depending on your preference. Serve the fudge as a delightful Halloween treat at parties, gatherings, or as a sweet gift for friends and family.

Tips for the Perfect Candy Corn Fudge

  • Variations: Feel free to customize your fudge by adding other mix-ins, such as mini marshmallows, chocolate chips, or even dried cranberries for a festive touch.
  • Storage: Store any leftover fudge in an airtight container at room temperature for up to a week, or refrigerate for longer freshness.
  • Serving Suggestions: Pair your candy corn fudge with a warm cup of apple cider or hot chocolate for the ultimate cozy fall treat.

Why This Treat is Perfect for Halloween

The vibrant yellow and orange hues are reminiscent of fall and the holiday spirit, making it a visually appealing addition to any dessert table.
