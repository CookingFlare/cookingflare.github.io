Mouthwatering Low Carb Creamy Tuscan Garlic Chicken Recipe


If you’re on the lookout for a dish that perfectly balances indulgence and health, look no further than this creamy Tuscan garlic chicken recipe. Bursting with flavor and low in carbs, this delectable meal is ideal for anyone wanting to enjoy a rich culinary experience without straying from their dietary goals.

Ingredients

For the Chicken

  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • 1 teaspoon Italian seasoning

For the Creamy Sauce

  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1 cup chicken broth
  • 1 cup fresh spinach
  • 1 cup sun-dried tomatoes, chopped
  • ½ cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • ½ teaspoon red pepper flakes (optional, for a kick)

For Garnish

  • Fresh basil, chopped
  • Extra Parmesan cheese, grated

Instructions

Step 1: Prepare the Chicken

  1. Season the Chicken: Begin by seasoning the chicken breasts with salt, pepper, and Italian seasoning on both sides. This will ensure that the chicken is flavorful.

  2. Sear the Chicken: Heat the olive oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat. Once hot, add the chicken breasts and cook for about 6-7 minutes on each side, or until they are golden brown and cooked through. An internal temperature of 165°F (75°C) is ideal. Once cooked, remove the chicken from the skillet and set aside.

Step 2: Create the Creamy Sauce

  1. Sauté the Garlic: In the same skillet, reduce the heat to medium and add the minced garlic. Sauté for about 1 minute, or until fragrant. Be careful not to burn the garlic, as it can turn bitter.

  2. Add the Liquids: Pour in the chicken broth and heavy cream, stirring to combine. Allow the mixture to simmer for about 3-4 minutes, letting it reduce slightly.

  3. Incorporate Spinach and Tomatoes: Add the fresh spinach and sun-dried tomatoes to the sauce. Stir well and let it simmer for another 2-3 minutes until the spinach has wilted.

  4. Add the Cheese and Seasonings: Stir in the grated Parmesan cheese until melted and combined. Then, add the dried basil, oregano, and red pepper flakes (if using). Taste the sauce and adjust the seasoning as necessary.

Step 3: Combine and Serve

  1. Return the Chicken: Place the seared chicken breasts back into the skillet, spooning the creamy sauce over them. Let everything simmer together for an additional 2-3 minutes, allowing the flavors to meld.

  2. Garnish: Once done, remove from heat and garnish with fresh basil and additional Parmesan cheese.

  3. Serve: Serve immediately, perhaps alongside some steamed vegetables or a fresh salad for a complete low-carb meal.

Tips for the Best Creamy Tuscan Garlic Chicken

  • Chicken Variations: While this recipe uses chicken breasts, you can also opt for thighs for a juicier result. Just be mindful of the cooking time, as thighs may take a bit longer to cook through.

  • Dairy-Free Option: If you’re looking for a dairy-free version, consider substituting the heavy cream with coconut cream and using nutritional yeast instead of Parmesan for a cheesy flavor.

  • Storage: This dish can be stored in an airtight container in the refrigerator for up to 3 days. Reheat gently on the stovetop or in the microwave, adding a splash of chicken broth to loosen the sauce if needed.

Nutritional Information

This low-carb creamy Tuscan garlic chicken recipe not only satisfies your taste buds but also aligns with many dietary preferences. Here’s a quick breakdown of the nutritional benefits:

  • Low in Carbohydrates: Thanks to the absence of pasta or rice, this dish is perfect for those following a ketogenic or low-carb lifestyle.

  • High in Protein: With chicken as the main protein source, you’ll be fueling your body with the necessary nutrients to maintain muscle mass.

  • Rich in Healthy Fats: The olive oil and heavy cream offer healthy fats that can aid in satiety and provide energy.

Pairing Suggestions

While this creamy Tuscan garlic chicken can stand alone as a satisfying meal, pairing it with complementary sides can elevate your dining experience. Here are a few ideas:

  • Zucchini Noodles: For a complete low-carb experience, serve the chicken over spiralized zucchini noodles. The lightness of the zucchini complements the rich sauce beautifully.

  • Cauliflower Mash: Creamy, buttery cauliflower mash is a fantastic alternative to traditional mashed potatoes and pairs wonderfully with the Tuscan chicken.

  • Roasted Broccoli or Asparagus: A side of roasted vegetables not only adds color to your plate but also provides additional nutrients without the carbs.

Final Thoughts

This mouthwatering low-carb creamy Tuscan garlic chicken recipe is a game-changer for weeknight dinners. It captures the essence of Italian cuisine while remaining mindful of dietary choices. Whether you’re entertaining guests or enjoying a quiet evening at home, this dish is sure to impress with its rich flavors and creamy texture.

So gather your ingredients, roll up your sleeves, and get ready to create a meal that will leave everyone asking for seconds. Enjoy the culinary journey and savor every bite of this delicious, healthy dish!
