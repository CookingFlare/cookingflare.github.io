10 Trending Mocktails Recipes to Try This Year


Mocktails have taken the beverage world by storm, offering a sophisticated, alcohol-free alternative for anyone looking to enjoy the taste and artistry of crafted drinks while staying sober. This year, the rise of wellness-focused lifestyles has inspired mixologists and home bartenders alike to get creative with mocktail recipes, blending bold flavors, unique ingredients, and beautiful presentations. Whether you’re hosting a party or enjoying a quiet evening at home, these 10 mocktail recipes are sure to impress and refresh.

1. Cucumber Mint Cooler

Refreshing, hydrating, and bursting with flavor, the Cucumber Mint Cooler is the perfect mocktail for warm weather. The combination of cucumber and mint creates a crisp, rejuvenating drink that’s both herbal and slightly sweet.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cucumber, sliced
  • 10 mint leaves
  • 2 tablespoons honey or agave syrup
  • Sparkling water
  • Juice of 1 lime
  • Ice cubes

Instructions:

  1. Muddle cucumber slices and mint leaves in a glass or shaker.
  2. Add the honey or agave syrup and lime juice, and mix thoroughly.
  3. Fill a glass with ice, pour the mixture, and top with sparkling water.
  4. Stir gently and garnish with a mint sprig and a thin cucumber slice.

2. Passionfruit Sunrise

Fruity and vibrant, Passionfruit Sunrise merges tangy flavors with a visually stunning presentation. The layered appearance resembles a classic tequila sunrise but is packed with natural fruit goodness.

Ingredients:

  • 1 passionfruit
  • ½ cup orange juice
  • ½ cup pineapple juice
  • 1 tablespoon grenadine
  • Ice cubes

Instructions:

  1. Scoop out the pulp of the passionfruit and mix it with orange and pineapple juice.
  2. Fill a tall glass with ice and pour the juice mixture half full.
  3. Carefully drizzle grenadine into the glass so it sinks to the bottom to create a gradient effect.
  4. Stir lightly before drinking and garnish with a wedge of orange or a pineapple slice.

3. Lavender Lemonade Bliss

For those who enjoy floral notes in their drinks, Lavender Lemonade Bliss is the ultimate mocktail. Calming lavender pairs beautifully with the tangy, citrusy burst of fresh lemonade.

Ingredients:

  • 2 lemons, juiced
  • 1 cup water
  • 1 tablespoon dried lavender or lavender syrup
  • 2 tablespoons sugar or simple syrup
  • Ice cubes

Instructions:

  1. In a saucepan, gently simmer water, lavender, and sugar for 5 minutes. Let it cool and strain to remove lavender bits.
  2. Mix freshly squeezed lemon juice with the lavender syrup.
  3. Pour over ice in a glass and stir well.
  4. Garnish with lavender buds or lemon wedges for a delicate touch.

4. Pineapple Mojito Mocktail

Reinvent the classic mojito by swapping rum for pineapple, bringing a tropical twist to this minty favorite.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup pineapple juice
  • Juice of 1 lime
  • Handful of mint leaves
  • Crushable ice
  • Club soda or sparkling water

Instructions:

  1. Muddle mint leaves in a shaker or glass.
  2. Add pineapple juice, lime juice, and mix gently.
  3. Fill a glass with crushed ice and pour the mixture on top.
  4. Top with club soda or sparkling water and garnish with a pineapple wedge or mint sprig.

5. Spiced Apple Fizz

Perfect for cooler evenings, Spiced Apple Fizz combines warm, comforting spices with a fizzy kick. Ideal for holiday gatherings or casual nights by the fire.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup apple cider
  • ¼ teaspoon cinnamon
  • Dash of nutmeg
  • Sparkling water
  • Apple slices for garnish
  • Ice cubes

Instructions:

  1. Mix apple cider with cinnamon and nutmeg, stirring until fully combined.
  2. Fill a glass with ice and pour the spiced cider halfway.
  3. Top with sparkling water for the fizz effect.
  4. Garnish with thin apple slices and a sprinkle of cinnamon.

6. Strawberry Basil Spritz

The irresistible pairing of ripe strawberries and fragrant basil makes this mocktail a crowd-pleaser. Its vibrant red hue and aromatic profile ensure it stands out.

Ingredients:

  • 5 strawberries, hulled
  • 5 fresh basil leaves
  • 1 teaspoon honey or simple syrup
  • Juice of 1 lemon
  • Sparkling water

Instructions:

  1. Muddle strawberries and basil leaves together in a pitcher or glass.
  2. Add honey and lemon juice, and stir well.
  3. Pour the mixture into a glass filled with ice and top with sparkling water.
  4. Garnish with a basil leaf and strawberry slice.

7. Citrus & Ginger Cooler

If you’re craving something zingy and invigorating, the Citrus & Ginger Cooler is just what you need. With the bold kick of ginger paired with the tartness of citrus, this mocktail is both exciting and rejuvenating.

Ingredients:

  • Juice of 1 orange
  • Juice of 1 lemon
  • Juice of 1 lime
  • ½ inch ginger, grated
  • 1 teaspoon honey or agave syrup
  • Sparkling water or ginger ale

Instructions:

  1. Mix the citrus juices (orange, lemon, lime) in a shaker.
  2. Add grated ginger and honey, and shake thoroughly.
  3. Strain the mixture into a glass filled with ice.
  4. Top with sparkling water or ginger ale, and garnish with a citrus slice.

8. Blueberry Vanilla Refresher

Sweet and slightly creamy, this Blueberry Vanilla Refresher expertly balances fruity and soft flavors. It’s a unique choice for mocktail-lovers looking for something new.

Ingredients:

  • ½ cup blueberries
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 2 tablespoons simple syrup
  • ½ cup almond or oat milk
  • Ice cubes

Instructions:

  1. Blend blueberries until they form a smooth puree.
  2. Mix the puree with vanilla extract, simple syrup, and almond milk.
  3. Pour the mixture into a glass of ice.
  4. Garnish with a few whole blueberries on top.

9. Watermelon Basil Refresher

Watermelon’s natural sweetness pairs beautifully with basil’s slightly peppery flavor in this summer-ready mocktail.

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups watermelon chunks
  • Juice of ½ lime
  • 4 fresh basil leaves
  • Ice cubes
  • Sparkling water

Instructions:

  1. Blend watermelon chunks into a juice or puree.
  2. Muddle basil leaves with lime juice, and add them to the watermelon puree.
  3. Pour into a glass filled with ice and top with sparkling water.
  4. Garnish with a watermelon wedge or basil sprig.

10. Rosemary Grapefruit Smash

Elegant and bold, the Rosemary Grapefruit Smash features earthy rosemary combined with the bright tartness of grapefruit.

Ingredients:

  • Juice of 1 grapefruit
  • 1 tablespoon rosemary syrup (or rosemary boiled with sugar water)
  • Club soda
  • Ice cubes

Instructions:

  1. Mix grapefruit juice and rosemary syrup in a shaker.
  2. Pour into a glass filled with ice and top with club soda.
  3. Garnish with a fresh rosemary sprig or a grapefruit wedge.

These mocktails prove that going alcohol-free doesn’t mean missing out on creative, delicious drinks. Whether you’re embracing sober curiosity, hosting a mixed group of drinkers and non-drinkers, or simply exploring new flavors, these recipes are sure to delight your palate. Cheers to discovering your new favorite mocktail!
