Leftover Summer: 10 Cool Recipes for Extra BBQ and Picnic Fare


Summer is in full swing, and that means endless days of outdoor barbecues and picnics. But what happens when the party’s over, and you’re left with a fridge full of leftover BBQ and picnic fare? Don’t let those delicious dishes go to waste! With a little creativity and some innovative recipes, you can transform those leftovers into exciting new meals that will keep the summer spirit alive. Here are 10 cool recipes for repurposing your extra BBQ and picnic fare.

1. BBQ Pulled Pork Nachos

Got leftover pulled pork from your weekend BBQ? Turn it into a crowd-pleasing snack by layering it over crispy tortilla chips, smothering it with cheese, and adding your favorite toppings like jalapenos, black beans, and sour cream. Pop it in the oven until the cheese is melted, and you’ve got yourself a mouthwatering plate of BBQ pulled pork nachos.

2. Picnic Pasta Salad Remix

Leftover pasta salad from your picnic can be reinvented into a refreshing pasta salad remix. Simply add some grilled chicken, cherry tomatoes, fresh basil, and a drizzle of balsamic vinaigrette to give it a new lease on life. This revamped pasta salad is perfect for a quick and satisfying lunch or light dinner.

3. Grilled Veggie Wrap

If you have leftover grilled vegetables from your BBQ, use them to create a flavorful grilled veggie wrap. Spread a tortilla with hummus, add the grilled veggies, some crumbled feta cheese, and a handful of mixed greens. Roll it up and enjoy a healthy and delicious lunch on the go.

4. BBQ Chicken Pizza

Leftover BBQ chicken is the star ingredient for a mouthwatering BBQ chicken pizza. Use store-bought pizza dough or a pre-made crust, slather it with BBQ sauce, top it with the shredded BBQ chicken, red onions, and mozzarella cheese. Bake until the crust is golden and the cheese is bubbly for a delightful twist on traditional pizza.

5. Picnic Charcuterie Board

Repurpose your picnic charcuterie into a vibrant and colorful charcuterie board. Arrange the leftover cured meats, cheeses, olives, pickles, and crackers on a large serving board for a relaxed and fuss-free dinner. Pair it with a glass of wine, and you’ve got a sophisticated yet effortless meal.

6. Watermelon & Feta Salad

Leftover watermelon from your picnic can be transformed into a refreshing watermelon and feta salad. Toss the cubed watermelon with crumbled feta, fresh mint, and a drizzle of balsamic glaze for a sweet and savory summer salad that’s bursting with flavor.

7. BBQ Brisket Tacos

Give your leftover BBQ brisket a Mexican twist by turning it into BBQ brisket tacos. Warm up some corn tortillas, fill them with the shredded brisket, top with diced onions, cilantro, and a squeeze of lime for a zesty and satisfying meal.

8. Picnic Deviled Egg Salad Sandwich

Leftover deviled eggs from your picnic can be repurposed into a creamy deviled egg salad. Mash the leftover egg yolks with mayonnaise, mustard, and a dash of hot sauce, then fold in chopped egg whites and some diced celery. Spread the egg salad between two slices of bread for a classic and comforting sandwich.

9. Grilled Corn Salsa

If you have leftover grilled corn on the cob, use it to create a vibrant grilled corn salsa. Cut the kernels off the cob, mix them with diced tomatoes, red onion, cilantro, and a squeeze of lime juice for a tangy and irresistible salsa that pairs perfectly with tortilla chips or as a topping for grilled fish or chicken.

10. BBQ Pulled Chicken Stuffed Sweet Potatoes

Transform your leftover BBQ pulled chicken into hearty BBQ pulled chicken stuffed sweet potatoes. Bake the sweet potatoes until tender, then stuff them with the warmed pulled chicken, a dollop of Greek yogurt, and a sprinkle of green onions for a wholesome and satisfying meal.

With these 10 creative recipes, you can make the most of your leftover summer BBQ and picnic fare, ensuring that nothing goes to waste. Embrace the spirit of innovation and enjoy the flavors of summer in exciting new ways. Cheers to a season filled with delicious food and endless possibilities!
