Simple Lemon Butter Shrimp Pasta Recipe


If you’re craving a refreshing and satisfying meal that comes together in no time, this simple lemon butter shrimp pasta recipe is the answer. With its delightful combination of succulent shrimp, zesty lemon, and rich butter, this dish is a perfect balance of flavors that will leave your taste buds singing. Whether you’re cooking for a special occasion or just want to treat yourself to a restaurant-quality meal at home, this recipe is sure to impress.

Ingredients

  • 8 ounces of linguine or your favorite pasta
  • 1 pound of large shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 4 tablespoons of unsalted butter
  • 4 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1/4 teaspoon of red pepper flakes
  • 1/2 cup of chicken broth
  • 1/4 cup of freshly squeezed lemon juice
  • 1 teaspoon of lemon zest
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley for garnish

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Pasta: Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil and cook the pasta according to the package instructions until al dente. Once cooked, drain the pasta and set it aside.

  2. Cook the Shrimp: In a large skillet, melt 2 tablespoons of butter over medium-high heat. Add the shrimp to the skillet and season with salt and pepper. Cook the shrimp for 2-3 minutes on each side until they are pink and opaque. Once cooked, transfer the shrimp to a plate and set them aside.

  3. Make the Lemon Butter Sauce: In the same skillet, melt the remaining 2 tablespoons of butter. Add the minced garlic and red pepper flakes, and sauté for about a minute until the garlic becomes fragrant.

  4. Combine the Ingredients: Pour the chicken broth, lemon juice, and lemon zest into the skillet with the garlic and red pepper flakes. Bring the mixture to a simmer and let it cook for 2-3 minutes until it slightly reduces.

  5. Bring It All Together: Add the cooked shrimp back into the skillet and toss to coat them in the lemon butter sauce. Then, add the cooked pasta to the skillet and gently toss everything together until the pasta is well coated in the sauce.

  6. Serve and Garnish: Once the pasta and shrimp are evenly coated, taste for seasoning and adjust with salt and pepper if needed. Sprinkle the dish with freshly chopped parsley for a pop of color and an extra burst of freshness.

Enjoy Your Culinary Creation

Now that your simple lemon butter shrimp pasta is ready, it’s time to indulge in this delectable dish. The bright, citrusy notes from the lemon perfectly complement the tender shrimp and buttery pasta, creating a symphony of flavors that will leave you craving more. Whether you’re enjoying this meal solo or impressing your loved ones with your culinary skills, this recipe is a surefire way to elevate your dining experience.

So, the next time you’re in the mood for a quick and flavorful meal, consider whipping up this simple lemon butter shrimp pasta. It’s a dish that effortlessly combines simplicity and sophistication, making it a go-to recipe for any occasion. Happy cooking and even happier eating!
