Exquisite Dairy-Free Cherry Pie Recipe


There’s nothing quite like the delightful combination of sweet cherries and a flaky pie crust. But what if you’re dairy-free and still want to enjoy this classic dessert? Well, you’re in luck! I’ve got a delicious dairy-free cherry pie recipe that will satisfy your cravings and leave you wanting more.

Ingredients

For the crust:

  • 2 ½ cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup dairy-free margarine, chilled and cubed
  • 6-8 tablespoons ice water

For the filling:

  • 4 cups fresh cherries, pitted
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • ¼ cup cornstarch
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Instructions

Step 1: Prepare the Crust

  1. In a large mixing bowl, whisk together the flour and salt. Add the chilled dairy-free margarine cubes and use a pastry cutter or your fingertips to cut the margarine into the flour until the mixture resembles coarse crumbs.

  2. Gradually add the ice water, 1 tablespoon at a time, and mix until the dough comes together. Be careful not to overmix. Divide the dough into two equal portions, flatten each into a disk, wrap in plastic wrap, and refrigerate for at least 30 minutes.

Step 2: Make the Filling

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).

  2. In a large bowl, combine the pitted cherries, sugar, cornstarch, lemon juice, and vanilla extract. Gently toss until the cherries are evenly coated.

Step 3: Assemble and Bake the Pie

  1. On a lightly floured surface, roll out one of the chilled dough disks into a circle large enough to fit your pie dish. Carefully transfer the rolled-out dough to the pie dish and gently press it into the bottom and sides.

  2. Pour the cherry filling into the prepared crust, spreading it out evenly.

  3. Roll out the second chilled dough disk and place it on top of the cherry filling. Use your fingers or a fork to crimp the edges of the crust to seal the pie.

  4. Cut several slits in the top crust to allow steam to escape during baking.

  5. Place the pie on a baking sheet (to catch any drips) and bake in the preheated oven for 45-50 minutes, or until the crust is golden brown and the filling is bubbly.

  6. Remove the pie from the oven and let it cool completely before serving.

Step 4: Serve and Enjoy

Once the pie has cooled, slice it into beautiful wedges and serve it with a dollop of dairy-free whipped cream or a scoop of dairy-free vanilla ice cream. The sweet and tart flavors of the cherries combined with the flaky crust will surely delight your taste buds.

This exquisite dairy-free cherry pie is perfect for any occasion, whether it’s a family gathering, a summer picnic, or simply a sweet treat to enjoy on a cozy evening. Plus, it’s so delicious that even those who aren’t dairy-free won’t be able to resist!

So, whip up this dairy-free cherry pie and savor every bite. It’s a dessert that proves you don’t need dairy to create something truly exquisite. Happy baking!

Note: Remember to always check the ingredients of any pre-made products you use to ensure they are dairy-free.
