Classic and Comforting: Homemade Meatball Subs


There’s something undeniably satisfying about sinking your teeth into a warm and hearty sandwich, especially when it’s packed with classic flavors and comforting ingredients. And when it comes to classic and comforting sandwiches, few can rival the beloved Homemade Meatball Subs. With juicy meatballs, tangy marinara sauce, and melty cheese, these subs are a true crowd-pleaser. So, roll up your sleeves and get ready to embark on a culinary journey that will leave your taste buds begging for more.

Ingredients

  • For the meatballs:

    • 1 pound ground beef
    • 1/2 pound ground pork
    • 1/2 cup breadcrumbs
    • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
    • 1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley
    • 2 cloves garlic, minced
    • 1 egg, lightly beaten
    • 1 teaspoon salt
    • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper

  • For the marinara sauce:

    • 2 tablespoons olive oil
    • 1 onion, finely chopped
    • 2 cloves garlic, minced
    • 1 can (28 ounces) crushed tomatoes
    • 1 can (6 ounces) tomato paste
    • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
    • 1 teaspoon dried basil
    • 1 teaspoon sugar
    • Salt and pepper to taste

  • For the subs:

    • 4 to 6 sub rolls
    • Sliced mozzarella cheese
    • Fresh basil leaves

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).

  2. In a large mixing bowl, combine the ground beef, ground pork, breadcrumbs, Parmesan cheese, parsley, garlic, egg, salt, and black pepper. Mix well until all the ingredients are evenly incorporated.

  3. Shape the mixture into meatballs, about 1 to 2 inches in diameter. Place the meatballs on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper, making sure to leave a little space between each one.

  4. Bake the meatballs in the preheated oven for about 20 minutes or until they are cooked through and nicely browned.

  5. While the meatballs are baking, prepare the marinara sauce. In a large saucepan, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the chopped onion and minced garlic, and sauté until they are softened and fragrant.

  6. Stir in the crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, dried oregano, dried basil, sugar, salt, and pepper. Reduce the heat to low and let the sauce simmer for about 15 to 20 minutes, allowing the flavors to meld together.

  7. Once the meatballs are ready, transfer them to the marinara sauce and let them simmer for an additional 10 minutes, allowing the flavors to meld together even further.

  8. To assemble the subs, slice the sub rolls lengthwise, without cutting all the way through. Place a few meatballs on each roll, spoon some marinara sauce over the meatballs, and top with sliced mozzarella cheese.

  9. Place the subs on a baking sheet and broil in the oven until the cheese is melted and bubbly, about 2 to 3 minutes.

  10. Remove the subs from the oven, garnish with fresh basil leaves, and serve hot.

Enjoy the Classic and Comforting Deliciousness!

There you have it, a classic and comforting recipe for Homemade Meatball Subs that will transport you to sandwich heaven. With juicy meatballs, tangy marinara sauce, and melty cheese, these subs are the epitome of comfort food. So, gather your ingredients, roll up your sleeves, and get ready to enjoy a delicious meal that will warm your heart and satisfy your cravings. Whether you’re hosting a game night, planning a family dinner, or simply craving a satisfying sandwich, these Homemade Meatball Subs are sure to hit the spot. Bon appétit!
