Crispy Fried Green Beans with Garlic and Lemon: A Delicious and Addictive Snack


Are you looking for a mouthwatering snack that will satisfy your cravings while still being healthy? Look no further than Crispy Fried Green Beans with Garlic and Lemon! This addictive snack is not only delicious but also easy to make. With just a few simple ingredients, you can have a plateful of crispy, flavorful green beans that will leave you wanting more.

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound fresh green beans
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 lemon, zested and juiced
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • Vegetable oil, for frying

Instructions:

  1. Start by preparing the green beans. Wash them thoroughly and trim off the ends.

  2. In a large mixing bowl, combine the flour, paprika, salt, and black pepper. Mix well to ensure all the spices are evenly distributed.

  3. Heat vegetable oil in a deep skillet or a deep fryer to around 375°F (190°C). Make sure you have enough oil to fully submerge the green beans.

  4. While the oil is heating, take a handful of green beans and toss them in the flour mixture until they are coated evenly. Shake off any excess flour.

  5. Carefully add the coated green beans to the hot oil, ensuring they are not overcrowded. Fry them for about 3-4 minutes or until they turn golden brown and crispy. You may need to do this in batches depending on the size of your skillet or fryer.

  6. Once the green beans are crispy and golden, remove them from the oil using a slotted spoon or tongs and transfer them to a paper towel-lined plate to drain any excess oil.

  7. In a separate pan, heat a tablespoon of vegetable oil over medium heat. Add the minced garlic and sauté for about 1 minute or until fragrant. Be careful not to burn the garlic.

  8. Add the lemon zest and juice to the pan and stir well. Cook for another minute to allow the flavors to meld together.

  9. Remove the pan from the heat and toss the crispy green beans in the garlic and lemon mixture. Make sure all the green beans are coated evenly.

  10. Transfer the crispy fried green beans to a serving dish and garnish with a sprinkle of lemon zest for an extra burst of citrus flavor.

  11. Serve immediately and enjoy this delightful snack!

Why You’ll Love Crispy Fried Green Beans with Garlic and Lemon:

  • Crunchy and Flavorful: The crispy coating on the green beans adds a satisfying crunch to each bite, while the garlic and lemon zest bring out a burst of flavor that will leave you wanting more.

  • Quick and Easy: With just a few simple steps, you can have a plateful of delicious fried green beans ready to be devoured in no time. It’s the perfect snack for those busy days when you need something quick and satisfying.

  • Healthy Alternative: Instead of reaching for a bag of chips or other unhealthy snacks, opt for these crispy fried green beans. They are loaded with nutrients and are a great way to incorporate more vegetables into your diet.

  • Versatile: Crispy Fried Green Beans with Garlic and Lemon can be enjoyed on their own as a snack, served as a side dish with your favorite main course, or even added to salads for an extra crunch. The possibilities are endless!

So, the next time you’re in need of a tasty and addictive snack, give Crispy Fried Green Beans with Garlic and Lemon a try. With their crispy texture, zesty flavor, and simple preparation, these green beans are sure to become a favorite in your household. Enjoy!
