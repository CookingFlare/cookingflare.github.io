Flavorful Keto Lemon Butter Salmon with Broccoli


Looking for a delicious and healthy keto-friendly dinner option? Look no further than this flavorful keto lemon butter salmon with broccoli recipe. Packed with nutrients and bursting with flavor, this dish is a perfect choice for anyone looking to indulge in a satisfying meal without compromising their low-carb lifestyle. Let’s dive into the details and learn how to create this mouthwatering dish right in your own kitchen.

Ingredients

  • 4 salmon fillets
  • 1 head of broccoli, cut into florets
  • 4 tablespoons of butter
  • 4 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 lemon, juiced and zested
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley, chopped, for garnish

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Salmon: Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C). Place the salmon fillets on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper. Season the salmon with salt and pepper to taste.

  2. Roast the Broccoli: In a bowl, toss the broccoli florets with olive oil, salt, and pepper. Spread the broccoli on a separate baking sheet and roast in the preheated oven for 15-20 minutes, or until tender and slightly charred.

  3. Make the Lemon Butter Sauce: In a small saucepan, melt the butter over medium heat. Add the minced garlic and cook for 1-2 minutes, or until fragrant. Stir in the lemon juice and zest. Let the sauce simmer for a few minutes until it thickens slightly. Season with salt and pepper to taste.

  4. Cook the Salmon: Pour the lemon butter sauce over the seasoned salmon fillets, ensuring they are well coated. Bake the salmon in the preheated oven for 12-15 minutes, or until the fish is cooked through and flakes easily with a fork.

  5. Serve: Once the salmon is cooked, remove it from the oven and let it rest for a few minutes. Plate the salmon alongside the roasted broccoli. Drizzle any remaining lemon butter sauce over the salmon and broccoli. Garnish with fresh chopped parsley for a pop of color and flavor.

Nutritional Information

This keto lemon butter salmon with broccoli recipe not only tantalizes the taste buds but also provides a healthy dose of essential nutrients. Salmon is rich in omega-3 fatty acids, protein, and B vitamins, while broccoli is a powerhouse of vitamins, minerals, and fiber. This dish is low in carbs and high in good fats, making it a perfect fit for a ketogenic diet.

  • Calories: 350 per serving
  • Total Fat: 22g
  • Saturated Fat: 9g
  • Cholesterol: 90mg
  • Sodium: 260mg
  • Total Carbohydrate: 8g
  • Dietary Fiber: 3g
  • Sugars: 2g
  • Protein: 30g

Final Thoughts

This flavorful keto lemon butter salmon with broccoli recipe is a delightful combination of zesty, buttery flavors and nutritious, wholesome ingredients. Whether you’re following a keto diet or simply looking for a delectable meal to enjoy, this dish is sure to impress. So, roll up your sleeves, gather your ingredients, and treat yourself to a culinary experience that’s as nourishing as it is delicious. Cheers to good food and good health!
