Savory Gnocchi with Creamy Pesto Sauce


Welcome to our kitchen, where we’re about to embark on a culinary journey that will tantalize your taste buds and leave you craving for more. Today, we’re diving into the world of Italian comfort food with a twist – savory gnocchi with creamy pesto sauce. This dish is a delightful combination of pillowy soft gnocchi, vibrant green pesto, and a touch of creamy indulgence. Let’s get started on this mouthwatering adventure!

Ingredients

For the Gnocchi:

  • 2 pounds of russet potatoes
  • 1 ½ cups of all-purpose flour
  • 1 large egg, lightly beaten
  • Salt to taste

For the Creamy Pesto Sauce:

  • 2 cups of fresh basil leaves
  • 1/3 cup of pine nuts
  • 2 cloves of garlic
  • 1/2 cup of grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/2 cup of olive oil
  • 1/2 cup of heavy cream
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

Making the Gnocchi:

  1. Start by boiling the unpeeled potatoes in a large pot of water until they are fork-tender, usually around 45 minutes to an hour.
  2. Once the potatoes are cooked, peel and mash them in a large bowl or pass them through a potato ricer for a smoother texture.
  3. Spread the mashed potatoes on a clean, flat surface and sprinkle with the flour, a pinch of salt, and the beaten egg.
  4. Gently knead the mixture until a soft, pliable dough forms. Be careful not to overwork the dough, as this can result in tough gnocchi.
  5. Divide the dough into smaller portions and roll each portion into a long, rope-like shape.
  6. Cut the ropes into bite-sized pieces and use a fork or gnocchi board to create ridges on each piece. This step not only adds a decorative touch but also helps the sauce cling to the gnocchi.

Making the Creamy Pesto Sauce:

  1. In a food processor, combine the basil leaves, pine nuts, garlic, and Parmesan cheese. Pulse the mixture while gradually adding the olive oil until a smooth, vibrant green pesto forms.
  2. In a separate saucepan, gently warm the heavy cream over low heat. Once warmed, stir in the prepared pesto until well combined.
  3. Season the sauce with salt and pepper to taste, and allow it to simmer gently for a few minutes to let the flavors meld together.

Bringing It All Together:

  1. In a large pot of salted boiling water, cook the prepared gnocchi in batches until they float to the surface. This usually takes around 2-3 minutes.
  2. Using a slotted spoon, transfer the cooked gnocchi directly into the creamy pesto sauce. Gently toss to coat the gnocchi evenly.
  3. Once the gnocchi is well coated with the creamy pesto sauce, remove the pan from the heat and serve the dish immediately.

Serving Suggestions

This savory gnocchi with creamy pesto sauce is a star on its own, but you can elevate the experience by serving it with a sprinkle of extra Parmesan cheese, a drizzle of olive oil, and a few fresh basil leaves for a burst of color. Pair it with a crisp white wine or a refreshing lemonade to complement the flavors.

Final Thoughts

Savor each bite of this delightful dish, allowing the creamy pesto sauce to envelop the tender gnocchi, creating a symphony of flavors in your mouth. Whether you’re cooking for family, friends, or simply treating yourself to a comforting meal, this savory gnocchi with creamy pesto sauce is sure to leave a lasting impression.

So, gather your ingredients, roll up your sleeves, and let’s create a culinary masterpiece together. Buon appetito!
