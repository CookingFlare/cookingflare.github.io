Mouthwatering One-Pot Lemon Garlic Shrimp Pasta


If you’re on the hunt for a quick and delicious dinner that won’t leave you with a mountain of dishes, look no further than this mouthwatering One-Pot Lemon Garlic Shrimp Pasta recipe. Perfectly balanced with bright flavors of lemon and garlic, combined with succulent shrimp and tender pasta, this dish is sure to become a favorite in your weekly rotation.

Ingredients You’ll Need

Here’s what you’ll need to whip up this delightful meal:

  • Shrimp: Opt for large, deveined shrimp for the best texture.
  • Pasta: Use spaghetti or linguine for classic pasta texture.
  • Garlic: Fresh garlic cloves minced finely.
  • Lemon: Fresh lemon juice and zest for a citrusy punch.
  • Chicken Broth: Provides a savory base for cooking the pasta.
  • Heavy Cream: Adds richness and creaminess to the sauce.
  • Parmesan Cheese: Grated finely for a cheesy finish.
  • Butter: Enhances the richness and flavor of the sauce.
  • Fresh Herbs: Such as parsley or basil for garnish.
  • Salt and Pepper: To season, to taste.

Step-by-Step Instructions

Step 1: Sauté the Shrimp

Start by heating a large, deep skillet over medium-high heat. Add a knob of butter to melt and swirl it around the pan. Once the butter is bubbling, add the shrimp in a single layer. Season lightly with salt and pepper. Cook for about 2-3 minutes per side until the shrimp are pink and opaque. Remove them from the skillet and set aside.

Step 2: Sauté the Garlic

In the same skillet, add a bit more butter if needed, then toss in the minced garlic. Sauté for about 30 seconds until fragrant, being careful not to burn it. Garlic adds a robust aroma and flavor base to the dish.

Step 3: Cook the Pasta

Next, add the pasta directly into the skillet with the garlic. Pour in the chicken broth and bring it to a simmer. Cook the pasta according to the package instructions, stirring occasionally to ensure it cooks evenly and absorbs the liquid.

Step 4: Create the Sauce

Once the pasta is almost al dente and most of the broth has been absorbed, reduce the heat to low. Stir in heavy cream, lemon zest, and half of the lemon juice. Let it simmer gently for another 2-3 minutes until the sauce thickens slightly.

Step 5: Finish and Serve

Return the cooked shrimp to the skillet, along with any juices that may have accumulated. Toss everything together gently to coat the shrimp and pasta with the creamy lemon garlic sauce. Sprinkle grated Parmesan cheese over the top and let it melt into the dish.

Step 6: Garnish and Enjoy!

Remove the skillet from heat. Taste and adjust seasoning with salt, pepper, and more lemon juice if desired. Garnish with freshly chopped parsley or basil for a burst of freshness and color.

Tips for Success

  • Don’t Overcook the Shrimp: Shrimp cook quickly, so keep an eye on them to avoid toughness.
  • Adjust Lemon to Taste: If you love tangy flavors, add more lemon juice. For a milder taste, stick to the suggested amount.
  • Use Fresh Ingredients: Fresh garlic and lemon zest make a world of difference in flavor.
  • Customize with Vegetables: Add spinach, cherry tomatoes, or asparagus for extra color and nutrients.

Final Thoughts

This One-Pot Lemon Garlic Shrimp Pasta is not just a meal; it’s an experience. With its creamy, tangy sauce and perfectly cooked shrimp nestled among tender pasta strands, every bite is a delight. Whether you’re cooking for a family dinner or impressing guests, this recipe checks all the boxes for flavor, simplicity, and elegance. Plus, with only one pot to clean up afterward, you can spend more time enjoying your culinary masterpiece and less time in the kitchen. So, next time you’re craving something special, give this recipe a try and savor the magic of flavors coming together effortlessly in one pot. Cheers to good food and great company!
