Easy and Flavorful Chicken Stir Fry Recipe for Busy Weeknights


Are you looking for a quick and delicious dinner option for those busy weeknights? Look no further! This easy and flavorful chicken stir fry recipe is the answer to all your dinner dilemmas. Packed with vibrant flavors and colorful vegetables, this dish is not only a treat for your taste buds but also a healthy and nutritious choice for your family.

Ingredients

  • 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts, sliced into thin strips
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon oyster sauce
  • 1 tablespoon sesame oil
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1-inch piece of ginger, grated
  • 1 red bell pepper, thinly sliced
  • 1 green bell pepper, thinly sliced
  • 1 medium-sized carrot, julienned
  • 1 medium-sized onion, thinly sliced
  • 1 cup broccoli florets
  • 1 cup snap peas
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. In a bowl, combine the soy sauce, oyster sauce, sesame oil, minced garlic, and grated ginger. Mix well to create a flavorful marinade.

  2. Add the sliced chicken to the marinade and toss to coat evenly. Set it aside for about 15 minutes to marinate and absorb the flavors.

  3. Heat a large skillet or wok over medium-high heat and add the vegetable oil. Once the oil is hot, add the marinated chicken and stir-fry for about 5-6 minutes or until cooked through. Remove the chicken from the skillet and set it aside.

  4. In the same skillet, add the sliced onions and cook for 2-3 minutes or until they start to soften. Next, add the bell peppers, carrots, broccoli florets, and snap peas. Stir-fry for another 4-5 minutes or until the vegetables are tender yet still crisp.

  5. Return the cooked chicken to the skillet and toss it with the vegetables. Cook for an additional 2 minutes to heat everything through.

  6. Season the stir fry with salt and pepper to taste. Remember that the soy sauce and oyster sauce already add some saltiness, so be cautious while adding extra salt.

  7. Serve the chicken stir fry hot over steamed rice or noodles. You can garnish it with some chopped green onions or sesame seeds for an extra touch of flavor and presentation.

Tips and Variations

  • Feel free to customize this recipe by adding your favorite vegetables. You can include mushrooms, zucchini, or even baby corn to make it more interesting.
  • For a spicier version, add some red pepper flakes or a dash of sriracha sauce to the marinade.
  • If you prefer a vegetarian option, you can substitute the chicken with tofu or tempeh.
  • Don’t limit yourself to just rice or noodles as a base. This chicken stir fry also goes well with quinoa or even wrapped in lettuce leaves for a low-carb option.
  • To save time, you can chop the vegetables in advance and store them in an airtight container in the refrigerator until you’re ready to cook.

Conclusion

This easy and flavorful chicken stir fry recipe is a game-changer for busy weeknights. With minimal prep and cook time, you can have a delicious and healthy dinner on the table in no time. The combination of tender chicken, vibrant vegetables, and the savory sauce will satisfy your cravings and leave you wanting more. So, why resort to takeout when you can whip up this tasty stir fry at home? Give it a try and see for yourself how easy and satisfying homemade meals can be!
