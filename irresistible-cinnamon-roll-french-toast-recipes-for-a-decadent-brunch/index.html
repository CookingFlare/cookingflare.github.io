Irresistible Cinnamon Roll French Toast Recipes for a Decadent Brunch


Who can resist the heavenly aroma of freshly baked cinnamon rolls? Imagine taking that indulgence to the next level by transforming them into a decadent french toast dish. In this blog post, we will explore three irresistible cinnamon roll french toast recipes that are perfect for a luxurious brunch. So, grab your apron and let’s get cooking!

Recipe 1: Classic Cinnamon Roll French Toast

Ingredients:

  • 4 large cinnamon rolls
  • 4 eggs
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • Maple syrup, for serving

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your griddle or frying pan over medium heat.
  2. In a shallow bowl, whisk together the eggs, milk, vanilla extract, and ground cinnamon.
  3. Slice each cinnamon roll horizontally into two even pieces.
  4. Dip each slice of cinnamon roll into the egg mixture, making sure to coat it evenly.
  5. Place the coated slices onto the preheated griddle or frying pan and cook until golden brown on both sides, around 2-3 minutes per side.
  6. Remove from the heat and serve immediately with a drizzle of maple syrup.

Recipe 2: Stuffed Cinnamon Roll French Toast

Ingredients:

  • 4 large cinnamon rolls
  • 4 ounces cream cheese, softened
  • 1/4 cup powdered sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 4 eggs
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • Maple syrup, for serving

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your griddle or frying pan over medium heat.
  2. In a medium bowl, mix together the softened cream cheese, powdered sugar, and vanilla extract until smooth.
  3. Slice each cinnamon roll horizontally, but this time, leave one side attached to create a pocket.
  4. Spread the cream cheese mixture onto the bottom half of each cinnamon roll.
  5. Carefully close the cinnamon rolls, creating a sandwich with the cream cheese filling in the middle.
  6. In a shallow bowl, whisk together the eggs, milk, ground cinnamon, and a pinch of salt.
  7. Dip each stuffed cinnamon roll into the egg mixture, ensuring that both sides are coated.
  8. Place the coated rolls onto the preheated griddle or frying pan and cook until golden brown on both sides, approximately 2-3 minutes per side.
  9. Remove from the heat and serve warm with a drizzle of maple syrup.

Recipe 3: Cinnamon Roll French Toast Casserole

Ingredients:

  • 8 large cinnamon rolls, cut into quarters
  • 4 eggs
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/2 cup chopped pecans (optional)
  • 1/4 cup melted butter
  • Cream cheese glaze (included with the cinnamon rolls)

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. Grease a 9x9-inch baking dish.
  3. In a large bowl, whisk together the eggs, milk, granulated sugar, vanilla extract, and ground cinnamon.
  4. Add the cinnamon roll quarters to the bowl and gently toss until all the pieces are coated in the egg mixture.
  5. Transfer the coated cinnamon roll pieces to the greased baking dish.
  6. Sprinkle chopped pecans (if using) evenly over the cinnamon roll pieces.
  7. Drizzle the melted butter over the top.
  8. Bake in the preheated oven for 25-30 minutes or until the casserole is golden brown and set in the center.
  9. Remove from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes.
  10. Drizzle the cream cheese glaze that came with the cinnamon rolls over the top of the casserole.
  11. Serve warm and enjoy!

These three cinnamon roll french toast recipes are guaranteed to impress your friends and family at your next brunch gathering. Whether you prefer the classic version, the stuffed variation, or the indulgent casserole, each recipe is a delightful twist on a brunch favorite. So, go ahead and treat yourself to a decadent morning feast that will leave everyone craving for more. Bon appétit!
