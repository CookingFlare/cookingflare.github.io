Savory Low Carb Cilantro Lime Shrimp and Avocado Salad Recipe


If you’re on the lookout for a refreshing, low-carb dish that’s rich in flavor and packed with nutrients, look no further than this Savory Low Carb Cilantro Lime Shrimp and Avocado Salad. This vibrant salad combines succulent shrimp with creamy avocado, fresh cilantro, and a zesty lime dressing, making it a perfect choice for lunch, dinner, or even as a light appetizer.

Why You’ll Love This Recipe

This recipe checks all the boxes for a satisfying meal that won’t derail your dietary goals. It’s:

  • Low in Carbs: Ideal for those following a keto or low-carb lifestyle.
  • Rich in Healthy Fats: Thanks to the avocado, this dish is full of monounsaturated fats that are great for heart health.
  • Quick to Prepare: In just about 30 minutes, you can whip up this delicious dish.
  • Flavorful: With a delightful blend of spices and fresh ingredients, this salad is a taste explosion in every bite.

Ingredients You’ll Need

To create this delightful salad, you will need the following ingredients:

For the Salad

  • 1 pound large shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 2 ripe avocados, diced
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1/2 red onion, thinly sliced
  • 1/4 cup fresh cilantro, chopped
  • 1 lime, juiced
  • Salt and pepper to taste

For the Lime Dressing

  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons fresh lime juice
  • 1 teaspoon lime zest
  • 1 garlic clove, minced
  • 1 teaspoon honey (optional, can be omitted for strict low-carb)
  • 1/2 teaspoon chili powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Step-by-Step Instructions

Step 1: Prepare the Shrimp

  1. Season the Shrimp: Start by seasoning the shrimp with salt, pepper, and chili powder. This will enhance the flavor and give it a nice kick.

  2. Cook the Shrimp: Heat a skillet over medium-high heat, add a drizzle of olive oil, and cook the shrimp for 2-3 minutes on each side, or until they are pink and opaque. Remove them from the heat and let them cool slightly.

Step 2: Make the Lime Dressing

  1. Combine Ingredients: In a small bowl, whisk together the olive oil, lime juice, lime zest, minced garlic, honey (if using), and a pinch of salt and pepper. This dressing is what ties the salad together, so make sure to taste and adjust the seasoning as needed.

Step 3: Assemble the Salad

  1. Combine the Ingredients: In a large mixing bowl, combine the cooked shrimp, diced avocado, cherry tomatoes, red onion, and chopped cilantro.

  2. Dress the Salad: Drizzle the lime dressing over the salad and gently toss everything together until well combined. Be careful not to mash the avocado!

  3. Adjust Seasoning: Taste the salad and adjust the salt and pepper as necessary.

Step 4: Serve

  1. Plate and Enjoy: Serve the salad immediately on a bed of greens or on its own. This dish is best enjoyed fresh, but you can store leftovers in an airtight container in the fridge for up to 24 hours.

Nutritional Benefits

This Savory Low Carb Cilantro Lime Shrimp and Avocado Salad is not just delicious but also packed with nutritional benefits:

Shrimp

  • High in Protein: Shrimp are an excellent source of lean protein, essential for muscle repair and growth.
  • Low in Calories: Low in calories, shrimp allow you to enjoy a hearty meal without the guilt.

Avocado

  • Heart-Healthy Fats: Avocados are rich in monounsaturated fats, which can help lower bad cholesterol levels.
  • High in Fiber: Avocados are also a great source of dietary fiber, promoting digestive health.

Cilantro

  • Antioxidants: Cilantro is packed with antioxidants and has anti-inflammatory properties.
  • Rich in Vitamins: It’s a great source of vitamin K, vitamin C, and potassium.

Tips for the Perfect Salad

  1. Use Fresh Ingredients: Whenever possible, opt for fresh, high-quality ingredients. Fresh shrimp and ripe avocados make a significant difference in taste.

  2. Customize Your Salad: Feel free to add other low-carb vegetables like bell peppers, cucumber, or radishes for added crunch and flavor.

  3. Meal Prep Friendly: If you want to prepare this salad ahead of time, cook the shrimp and make the dressing in advance. Assemble the salad just before serving to keep the avocado fresh.

  4. Spice it Up: If you enjoy a bit of heat, consider adding diced jalapeños or a dash of hot sauce to the salad.

Conclusion

This Savory Low Carb Cilantro Lime Shrimp and Avocado Salad is a perfect dish for anyone looking to enjoy a healthy, flavorful meal without the carbs. With its vibrant ingredients and zesty dressing, it’s bound to become a favorite in your household. Whether you’re following a specific diet or simply want a delicious salad, this recipe is sure to impress. So grab your ingredients, get cooking, and enjoy a bowl full of freshness and flavor!

Happy cooking!
