Greek Lemon Chicken Skewers: A Tangy and Succulent Delight


Greek cuisine is renowned for its bold flavors, vibrant colors, and dedication to fresh ingredients. One dish that exemplifies these qualities is the classic Greek lemon chicken skewer. Marinated in a zesty mixture of lemon juice, olive oil, garlic, and oregano, these skewers are a symphony of flavors that will leave you craving for more. In this recipe, we’ll guide you through the process of creating these succulent and tangy delights.

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 pounds boneless, skinless chicken breast or thighs, cut into 1 1/2-inch cubes
  • 1/4 cup freshly squeezed lemon juice
  • 1/4 cup extra virgin olive oil
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons chopped fresh oregano
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1/4 teaspoon paprika
  • 10-12 bamboo skewers, soaked in water for at least 30 minutes
  • Fresh parsley, chopped (optional)

Instructions

  1. Prepare the marinade: In a large bowl, whisk together lemon juice, olive oil, garlic, oregano, salt, black pepper, and paprika.
  2. Marinate the chicken: Add the chicken cubes to the marinade and mix until they are evenly coated. Cover the bowl with plastic wrap and refrigerate for at least 2 hours or overnight.
  3. Preheat the grill: Preheat the grill to medium-high heat.
  4. Thread the skewers: Thread 4-5 chicken cubes onto each skewer, leaving a small space between each piece.
  5. Grill the skewers: Place the skewers on the grill and cook for 8-10 minutes, turning occasionally, or until the chicken is cooked through and slightly charred.
  6. Serve: Serve the skewers hot, garnished with chopped parsley if desired.

Tips and Variations

  • For an extra burst of flavor, brush the skewers with a mixture of lemon juice and honey during the last 2 minutes of grilling.
  • Substitute chicken breast with thighs for a juicier and more tender texture.
  • Add some color to your skewers by threading cherry tomatoes, bell peppers, or onions alongside the chicken.
  • Serve the skewers with a side of creamy tzatziki sauce or a simple Greek salad for a well-rounded meal.

Why This Recipe Works

The combination of lemon juice and olive oil in the marinade creates a tangy and rich flavor profile that complements the chicken perfectly. The garlic and oregano add a pungent and earthy note, while the paprika provides a subtle smokiness. The result is a dish that is both bold and subtle, making it a perfect addition to any summer barbecue or dinner party.

Conclusion

Greek lemon chicken skewers are a classic dish that is sure to impress your family and friends. With its bright and zesty flavors, this recipe is perfect for warm weather gatherings and outdoor cooking. By following these simple steps and tips, you’ll be able to create a dish that is both succulent and tangy, with a flavor profile that is quintessentially Greek. So go ahead, fire up the grill, and get ready to delight your taste buds with this incredible recipe!
