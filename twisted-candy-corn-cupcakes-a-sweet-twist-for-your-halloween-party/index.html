Twisted Candy Corn Cupcakes: A Sweet Twist for your Halloween Party


Are you looking for a delectable treat to serve at your Halloween party this year? Look no further. Today we’re giving you a scrumptious recipe for Twisted Candy Corn Cupcakes that will tantalize your taste buds and impress your guests. These wickedly sweet and visually stunning cupcakes are sure to be the star of your spooky soirée. Grab your ingredients and let’s get twisted!

Ingredients:

Cupcakes:

  • 1 ½ cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 ½ teaspoons baking powder
  • ½ teaspoon baking soda
  • ¼ teaspoon salt
  • ½ cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1 large egg
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • ½ cup buttermilk
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 2 drops yellow food coloring

Frosting:

  • ½ cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 2 ½ cups powdered sugar
  • 1 large egg white
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • 2-3 drops yellow food coloring
  • 1-2 cups green candy melts
  • 1-2 cups yellow candy melts

Instructions:

Before you start making the cupcakes, preheat your oven to 350°F (180°C) and line two cupcake tins with paper liners.

Cupcakes:

  1. In a medium bowl, whisk together the flour, sugar, baking powder, baking soda, and salt. Set aside for now.
  2. In a large bowl, cream together the softened butter and egg until light and fluffy.
  3. Add in the vanilla extract and food coloring, then mix well.
  4. Gradually add the flour mixture into the butter mixture, alternating with the buttermilk and vegetable oil. Mix until the batter is smooth and well-combined.
  5. Evenly divide the batter among the lined cupcake tin, filling each liner about 2/3 full.
  6. Bake the cupcakes for 18-20 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.
  7. Allow the cupcakes to cool in the tin for a few minutes before transferring them to a wire rack to cool completely.

Frosting:

  1. Beat the softened butter in a large bowl until it’s smooth and creamy.
  2. Gradually mix in powdered sugar until fully incorporated.
  3. Add in the egg white, vanilla extract, and vegetable oil. Beat until light and fluffy.
  4. Separate the frosting into two bowls.
  5. In one bowl, add the yellow food coloring and mix until you achieve the desired hue. Set aside.
  6. In the other bowl, add the green food coloring and mix until you achieve the desired hue. Set aside.

Assembly:

  1. Using a cupcake cutter or a sharp knife, cut off a thin slice of the cupcake’s top (the domed part), then remove the center, leaving a hole.
  2. Pipe a small dollap of the yellow frosting into each cupcake hole to make the bottom layer.
  3. Pipe a thick ring of the green frosting around the hole’s edges, forming the first candy corn layer.
  4. Fill in the center with the yellow frosting, forming the second candy corn layer.
  5. Once the cupcakes are assembled, store them in an airtight container in the refrigerator until you’re ready to serve.

Tips to ensure your Twisted Candy Corn Cupcakes are a success:

  • Make the frosting a day before, then let it sit in the fridge overnight to harden up. This will make it easier to handle when you’re assembling the cupcakes.
  • If the candy melts turn out too thick or too thin, you can adjust by adding more melts or a small amount of vegetable oil until you get the right consistency.
  • Be creative with your garnish. You can add some finely crushed candy corns, a small piece of candy corn or even sprinkle some gold or silver edible glitter on top.

There you have it! A sweet twist to elevate your Halloween party – Twisted Candy Corn Cupcakes. These delectable treats combine the flavor of classic candy corn with a fun twist, creating an irresistible dessert. Your guests will be begging for the recipe, so go ahead and impress them with these Halloween wow-worthy cupcakes. Happy baking and have a creepily delicious Halloween!
