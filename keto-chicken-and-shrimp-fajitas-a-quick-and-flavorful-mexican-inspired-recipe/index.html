Keto Chicken and Shrimp Fajitas: A Quick and Flavorful Mexican-Inspired Recipe


If you’re looking for a delicious and satisfying keto-friendly meal that’s bursting with flavor, look no further than these Keto Chicken and Shrimp Fajitas. This Mexican-inspired dish is not only quick and easy to make but also low in carbs, making it a perfect choice for anyone following a ketogenic diet.

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound of chicken breast, sliced into strips
  • 1 pound of shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 3 bell peppers (red, yellow, and green), sliced
  • 1 large onion, sliced
  • 3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons of olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons of fajita seasoning
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh cilantro, chopped, for garnish
  • Lime wedges, for serving
  • Low-carb tortillas or lettuce leaves, for serving

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the Chicken and Shrimp: In a large bowl, season the chicken strips and shrimp with fajita seasoning, salt, and pepper. Toss to coat evenly and set aside.

  2. Sauté the Vegetables: Heat 1 tablespoon of olive oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat. Add the sliced bell peppers and onion to the skillet and sauté until they are tender-crisp, about 5-7 minutes. Remove the vegetables from the skillet and set aside.

  3. Cook the Chicken and Shrimp: In the same skillet, add another tablespoon of olive oil. Add the seasoned chicken strips and cook until they are no longer pink and have a nice char, about 5-7 minutes. Then, add the seasoned shrimp to the skillet and cook until they turn pink and opaque, about 2-3 minutes.

  4. Combine and Serve: Once the chicken and shrimp are cooked through, return the sautéed vegetables to the skillet. Add minced garlic and toss everything together for a minute to combine the flavors.

  5. Serve Hot: Remove the skillet from the heat and garnish the fajitas with chopped cilantro. Serve the Keto Chicken and Shrimp Fajitas with lime wedges and your choice of low-carb tortillas or lettuce leaves.

Nutritional Information:

  • Serving Size: 1/4 of the recipe
  • Calories: 320
  • Total Fat: 15g
  • Saturated Fat: 2g
  • Cholesterol: 200mg
  • Sodium: 680mg
  • Total Carbohydrate: 10g
  • Dietary Fiber: 3g
  • Sugars: 5g
  • Protein: 35g

Tips and Variations:

  • For an extra kick of flavor, add a sprinkle of cayenne pepper or a dash of hot sauce to the fajitas.
  • You can customize the fajitas by adding sliced avocado, sour cream, or shredded cheese as toppings.
  • If you prefer a different protein, such as beef or tofu, feel free to substitute it for the chicken and shrimp.

These Keto Chicken and Shrimp Fajitas are not only a feast for the taste buds but also a wholesome and satisfying meal that fits perfectly into a keto lifestyle. Whether you’re cooking for yourself or entertaining guests, this quick and flavorful recipe is sure to impress. Enjoy the bold flavors of Mexico while staying on track with your low-carb goals!
