Creamy One Pot Garlic Parmesan Risotto


Risotto is a classic Italian dish known for its creamy texture and rich flavors. However, traditional risotto recipes often require constant stirring and multiple pots. But fear not! I have a delicious and convenient solution for you – Creamy One Pot Garlic Parmesan Risotto.

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 medium onion, finely chopped
  • 4 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 1/2 cups Arborio rice
  • 1/2 cup dry white wine
  • 4 cups vegetable or chicken broth
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley, chopped (for garnish)

Instructions

  1. Heat the olive oil in a large, deep skillet or pot over medium heat. Add the chopped onion and minced garlic, and sauté until they become translucent and fragrant.

  2. Add the Arborio rice to the pot and stir well, coating each grain with the oil. Toast the rice for about 2 minutes, until it turns slightly golden.

  3. Pour in the white wine and stir until it is absorbed by the rice. This step adds a delightful depth of flavor to the risotto.

  4. Begin adding the broth, one cup at a time, stirring constantly. Allow each cup of broth to be absorbed before adding the next. This slow and steady process allows the rice to release its starches and create that signature creaminess.

  5. Continue adding the broth and stirring until the rice is tender but still slightly firm to the bite. This should take around 20-25 minutes. If you find that you need more liquid, you can use hot water or additional broth.

  6. Once the rice is cooked to perfection, remove the pot from the heat. Stir in the grated Parmesan cheese until it melts and becomes smooth. The cheese adds a rich and nutty flavor to the dish.

  7. Finally, pour in the heavy cream and stir well. The cream adds a luscious, velvety texture that will make your taste buds sing with joy.

  8. Season the risotto with salt and pepper to taste. Remember to be conservative with the salt since the Parmesan cheese already adds a salty element.

  9. Serve the Creamy One Pot Garlic Parmesan Risotto immediately, garnished with freshly chopped parsley. The vibrant green color and fresh taste of parsley will beautifully complement the rich flavors of the risotto.

Conclusion

Creamy One Pot Garlic Parmesan Risotto is a game-changer when it comes to making risotto. With just one pot and a few simple ingredients, you can enjoy a luxurious and satisfying dish that will impress your friends and family.

The combination of garlic, Parmesan cheese, and creamy rice creates a harmony of flavors that will have you coming back for seconds. So next time you’re in the mood for a comforting and indulgent meal, give this recipe a try. You won’t be disappointed!

Remember, cooking is all about experimentation and enjoyment. Feel free to add your own twist to this recipe by incorporating ingredients like mushrooms, peas, or even a splash of lemon juice. The possibilities are endless!

Happy cooking and bon appétit!
