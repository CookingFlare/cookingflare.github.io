Beyond Beef: Unconventional Burger Patties You'll Love


When it comes to burger patties, the classic beef patty has always been a staple. However, with the rise of plant-based alternatives, there’s a whole new world of unconventional burger patties waiting to be explored. One such option that has been gaining popularity is the Beyond Beef patty. Made from plant-based ingredients, Beyond Beef offers a delicious and sustainable alternative to traditional meat patties. In this blog post, we’ll explore some unconventional and mouthwatering ways to prepare Beyond Beef burger patties that you’ll absolutely love.

Beyond Beef Taco Burger Patties

Who says burger patties have to be round? With Beyond Beef, you can get creative and shape your patties into a different form. For a fun twist, try making Beyond Beef taco burger patties. Simply season the Beyond Beef mixture with your favorite taco spices, such as cumin, chili powder, and paprika. Form the mixture into slightly flattened rounds and cook them on a skillet or grill. Serve these taco burger patties in a toasted bun with all the classic taco toppings like shredded lettuce, diced tomatoes, guacamole, and a dollop of sour cream. The fusion of burger and taco flavors will tantalize your taste buds in a whole new way.

Beyond Beef Stuffed Veggie Burger Patties

Take your burger game to the next level by creating Beyond Beef stuffed veggie burger patties. Start by shaping two thin Beyond Beef patties. Then, place a generous amount of your favorite veggies and cheese between the two patties, sealing the edges carefully. Grill or pan-fry the stuffed patties until they are cooked to perfection and the cheese is melted. The result? A juicy, flavorful patty with a surprise burst of veggies and gooey cheese in every bite. This unconventional burger patty is a delightful way to incorporate more veggies into your meal while savoring the richness of Beyond Beef.

Beyond Beef Breakfast Burger Patties

Who says burgers are only for lunch or dinner? With Beyond Beef, you can create a mouthwatering breakfast burger patty that will kick-start your day with a burst of flavor. Mix the Beyond Beef with some breakfast staples like cooked crumbled bacon, shredded cheddar cheese, and a hint of maple syrup. Form the mixture into patties and cook them until golden brown. Serve these breakfast burger patties on English muffins with a fried egg, crispy hash browns, and a drizzle of hot sauce. The combination of savory, smoky, and sweet flavors will make this breakfast burger patty a new morning favorite.

Beyond Beef Mediterranean Lamb Burger Patties

Transport your taste buds to the Mediterranean with a Beyond Beef Mediterranean lamb burger patty. Infuse the Beyond Beef mixture with classic Mediterranean flavors such as garlic, oregano, and mint. Form the seasoned mixture into patties and cook them to a perfect char. Serve these flavorful patties in a pita or flatbread with tzatziki, sliced cucumbers, and cherry tomatoes. The aromatic blend of herbs and spices will make this unconventional burger patty a delightful departure from the ordinary.

Conclusion

Beyond Beef opens up a world of possibilities when it comes to creating unconventional and delicious burger patties. Whether you’re in the mood for a fusion of flavors with the taco burger patties, a surprise burst of veggies and cheese with the stuffed veggie burger patties, a savory and sweet kick with the breakfast burger patties, or a taste of the Mediterranean with the lamb burger patties, Beyond Beef offers endless opportunities for culinary creativity. Give these unconventional burger patty ideas a try and elevate your burger game to new heights.
