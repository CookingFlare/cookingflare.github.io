Savory and Spicy Cajun Chicken Alfredo Pasta Bake: A Crowd-Pleasing Delight

3 min · 510 words

If you’re looking for a dish that’s sure to impress your family and friends, look no further than this savory and spicy Cajun Chicken Alfredo Pasta Bake. This crowd-pleasing delight brings together the rich flavors of Cajun seasoning, tender chicken, creamy Alfredo sauce, and perfectly cooked pasta, all baked to golden perfection. Whether you’re hosting a dinner party or simply craving a comforting meal, this recipe is bound to become a favorite in your home.

Ingredients

For the Cajun Chicken:

  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 2 tablespoons Cajun seasoning
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil

For the Alfredo Sauce:

  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter
  • 2 cups heavy cream
  • 2 cups grated Parmesan cheese
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • Salt and pepper to taste

For the Pasta:

  • 1 pound penne pasta
  • Salt for boiling water

Additional Ingredients:

  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Fresh parsley for garnish

Instructions

Step 1: Prepare the Cajun Chicken

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Rub the Cajun seasoning all over the chicken breasts, ensuring they are evenly coated.
  3. In a large skillet, heat the olive oil over medium-high heat. Add the seasoned chicken breasts and cook for 5-6 minutes on each side, until golden brown and cooked through. Remove from the skillet and set aside to cool. Once cooled, dice the chicken into bite-sized pieces.

Step 2: Cook the Pasta

  1. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Add the penne pasta and cook according to the package instructions until al dente. Drain the pasta and set aside.

Step 3: Prepare the Alfredo Sauce

  1. In a saucepan, melt the butter over medium heat. Add the minced garlic and cook for 1-2 minutes until fragrant.
  2. Pour in the heavy cream and bring to a simmer, stirring constantly.
  3. Gradually add the grated Parmesan cheese, stirring until the sauce is smooth and creamy. Season with salt and pepper to taste.

Step 4: Assemble the Pasta Bake

  1. In a large mixing bowl, combine the cooked pasta, diced Cajun chicken, and Alfredo sauce. Gently toss until everything is well coated.
  2. Transfer the mixture to a greased 9x13 inch baking dish and spread it out evenly.
  3. Sprinkle the shredded mozzarella cheese over the top of the pasta.

Step 5: Bake and Serve

  1. Place the baking dish in the preheated oven and bake for 25-30 minutes, or until the cheese is bubbly and golden brown.
  2. Once baked, remove the dish from the oven and let it rest for a few minutes.
  3. Garnish with fresh parsley before serving.

Serving Suggestions

This Cajun Chicken Alfredo Pasta Bake pairs beautifully with a crisp green salad dressed in a light vinaigrette. For a heartier meal, serve it alongside garlic bread or a side of steamed vegetables. This dish is versatile and can be the star of any casual dinner or special occasion.

Conclusion

With its bold flavors and comforting appeal, the Savory and Spicy Cajun Chicken Alfredo Pasta Bake is a dish that is sure to leave a lasting impression. Whether you’re a fan of Cajun cuisine or simply love a good pasta bake, this recipe has something for everyone. So, gather your ingredients and get ready to delight your taste buds with this irresistible crowd-pleaser!
