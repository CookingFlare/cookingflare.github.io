Summer Grilled Cheese Recipes: 15 Mouthwatering Options for Outdoor Cooking


Grilled cheese sandwiches are a classic comfort food that everyone loves. But during the summer months, when the weather is warm and the sun is shining, why not take your grilled cheese game to the next level and bring it outdoors? With these 15 mouthwatering summer grilled cheese recipes, you can transform your ordinary sandwich into a culinary masterpiece. Get ready to fire up the grill and indulge in these delicious creations!

1. Caprese Grilled Cheese

Combine the flavors of a classic Caprese salad with a gooey grilled cheese by adding slices of fresh mozzarella, juicy tomatoes, and fragrant basil leaves to your sandwich. Grill until the cheese is melted and the bread is crispy. Serve it with a side of balsamic glaze for dipping, and you’ve got yourself a taste of summer perfection.

2. BBQ Chicken Grilled Cheese

For all the barbecue lovers out there, this one’s for you. Shred some leftover BBQ chicken and pile it onto your grilled cheese sandwich. Add some tangy barbecue sauce and a slice of cheddar cheese for an explosion of flavors. Grill until the cheese is melted and the bread is golden brown. This sandwich is a delicious way to use up any leftover chicken and satisfy your barbecue cravings.

3. Jalapeno Popper Grilled Cheese

If you’re a fan of spicy food, this grilled cheese recipe is a must-try. Spread cream cheese on the inside of your bread slices, then layer on some sliced jalapenos and shredded cheddar cheese. Grill until the cheese is melted and the bread is crispy. The creaminess of the cream cheese balances out the heat of the jalapenos, creating a flavor combination that will leave you craving more.

4. Pesto Grilled Cheese

Brighten up your grilled cheese with the vibrant flavors of pesto. Spread a generous amount of pesto on the inside of your bread slices, then top with slices of fresh mozzarella and ripe tomatoes. Grill until the cheese is melted and the bread is golden brown. The combination of the basil pesto, creamy mozzarella, and juicy tomatoes will transport you to a sunny Italian garden.

5. Bacon and Avocado Grilled Cheese

Indulge in the ultimate combination of flavors with this bacon and avocado grilled cheese sandwich. Fry up some crispy bacon and slice a ripe avocado. Layer the bacon, avocado, and cheddar cheese between your bread slices. Grill until the cheese is melted and the bread is toasted. The creamy avocado and smoky bacon take this grilled cheese to a whole new level.

6. Hawaiian Grilled Cheese

Take a trip to the tropics with this Hawaiian-inspired grilled cheese sandwich. Layer slices of ham, pineapple rings, and Swiss cheese between your bread slices. Grill until the cheese is melted and the bread is golden brown. The combination of the sweet pineapple, salty ham, and creamy Swiss cheese will transport you to a sunny beach.

7. Mediterranean Grilled Cheese

Escape to the Mediterranean with this flavorful grilled cheese sandwich. Spread hummus on the inside of your bread slices, then top with slices of feta cheese, roasted red peppers, and kalamata olives. Grill until the cheese is melted and the bread is crispy. The tangy feta, smoky roasted red peppers, and briny olives create a taste sensation that will transport you to the shores of the Mediterranean.

8. Buffalo Chicken Grilled Cheese

For all the spice lovers out there, this buffalo chicken grilled cheese is a game-changer. Shred some leftover buffalo chicken and pile it onto your sandwich. Add some blue cheese dressing and a slice of cheddar cheese. Grill until the cheese is melted and the bread is golden brown. The combination of the spicy buffalo chicken, tangy blue cheese, and melted cheddar is a flavor explosion in every bite.

9. Spinach and Artichoke Grilled Cheese

Bring the flavors of your favorite dip to your grilled cheese sandwich with this spinach and artichoke recipe. Spread a mixture of cream cheese, spinach, and artichoke hearts on the inside of your bread slices. Top with slices of mozzarella cheese and grill until the cheese is melted and the bread is crispy. The creamy spinach and artichoke filling paired with the melted mozzarella will have you coming back for seconds.

10. Brie and Fig Grilled Cheese

Indulge in a sophisticated twist on the classic grilled cheese with this brie and fig recipe. Spread fig jam on the inside of your bread slices, then layer on slices of brie cheese. Grill until the cheese is melted and the bread is golden brown. The creamy brie and sweet fig jam create a combination that is both elegant and delicious.

11. Apple and Cheddar Grilled Cheese

Experience the perfect balance of sweet and savory with this apple and cheddar grilled cheese sandwich. Thinly slice an apple and layer it on top of cheddar cheese between your bread slices. Grill until the cheese is melted and the bread is toasted. The crisp apple and sharp cheddar create a delightful contrast of flavors.

12. Mushroom and Swiss Grilled Cheese

Elevate your grilled cheese sandwich with the earthy flavors of mushrooms and nutty Swiss cheese. Sauté some sliced mushrooms until they are golden brown, then layer them on top of Swiss cheese between your bread slices. Grill until the cheese is melted and the bread is crispy. The combination of the sautéed mushrooms and melted Swiss cheese is simply irresistible.

13. Tomato and Mozzarella Grilled Cheese

Keep it simple and let the flavors of ripe tomatoes and creamy mozzarella shine in this classic combination. Layer slices of fresh mozzarella and juicy tomatoes between your bread slices. Grill until the cheese is melted and the bread is golden brown. The freshness of the tomatoes and the creaminess of the mozzarella create a grilled cheese that is light, yet satisfying.

14. BLT Grilled Cheese

Combine two classic sandwiches into one with this BLT grilled cheese recipe. Fry up some crispy bacon, then layer it on top of cheddar cheese and lettuce between your bread slices. Grill until the cheese is melted and the bread is toasted. The combination of the smoky bacon, fresh lettuce, and melted cheddar is a match made in sandwich heaven.

15. S’mores Grilled Cheese

End your summer grilled cheese feast on a sweet note with this s’mores-inspired creation. Spread Nutella on the inside of your bread slices, then layer on some mini marshmallows and crushed graham crackers. Grill until the marshmallows are melted and the bread is golden brown. The gooey marshmallows, creamy Nutella, and crunchy graham crackers will satisfy your sweet tooth in the most delicious way.

Conclusion

There you have it, 15 mouthwatering summer grilled cheese recipes to take your outdoor cooking to the next level. Whether you’re craving something savory or sweet, there’s a grilled cheese option for everyone. So fire up the grill, gather your ingredients, and get ready to indulge in these delicious creations. Your taste buds will thank you!
