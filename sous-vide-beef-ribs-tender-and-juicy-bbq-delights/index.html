Sous Vide Beef Ribs: Tender and Juicy BBQ Delights


Are you a fan of succulent, melt-in-your-mouth barbecue ribs? Look no further! In this recipe, we’ll show you how to take your BBQ game to the next level with sous vide beef ribs. The sous vide technique ensures that the ribs are cooked to perfection, resulting in tender and juicy meat that will leave you craving more. So fire up those grills and get ready to indulge in BBQ delights!

Ingredients

  • 2 racks of beef ribs
  • 4 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons of paprika
  • 1 tablespoon of brown sugar
  • 1 tablespoon of salt
  • 1 teaspoon of black pepper
  • 1 teaspoon of cayenne pepper
  • BBQ sauce of your choice

Instructions

  1. Preheat your water bath to 135°F (57°C). The precise temperature is essential for achieving the desired tenderness and doneness of the beef ribs.

  2. Prepare the beef ribs by removing the membrane from the back of the rack. This will ensure that the ribs cook evenly and allow the flavors to penetrate the meat.

  3. In a small bowl, combine the minced garlic, paprika, brown sugar, salt, black pepper, and cayenne pepper. This flavorful rub will enhance the taste of the beef ribs.

  4. Generously coat the beef ribs with the spice rub, ensuring that every inch is covered. The rub will create a beautiful crust and add depth to the flavor profile.

  5. Place each rack of ribs in a separate vacuum-sealed bag, making sure to remove as much air as possible. This step is crucial for the sous vide cooking process.

  6. Once the water bath has reached the desired temperature, carefully submerge the sealed bags into the water. Make sure that the ribs are fully immersed.

  7. Cook the beef ribs for 24 to 48 hours. The longer cooking time allows the collagen to break down, resulting in incredibly tender and juicy ribs.

  8. After the sous vide cooking is complete, remove the bags from the water bath and carefully take out the ribs. Be gentle to avoid any breakage.

  9. Preheat your grill to high heat. While the grill is heating up, brush the ribs with your favorite BBQ sauce. This step will add a smoky and tangy flavor to the meat.

  10. Place the beef ribs on the grill and cook for 2 to 3 minutes per side, or until you achieve a beautiful caramelized crust. Keep a close eye on them to prevent burning.

  11. Once the ribs are nicely charred, remove them from the grill and let them rest for a few minutes. This resting period allows the juices to redistribute, resulting in even more flavorful meat.

  12. Finally, slice the ribs into individual portions and serve them with extra BBQ sauce on the side. These sous vide beef ribs are so tender and juicy that they practically fall off the bone!

Conclusion

Congratulations! You’ve mastered the art of sous vide beef ribs. By utilizing this cooking technique, you can achieve unparalleled tenderness and juiciness in your BBQ creations. The combination of the sous vide method and the flavorful spice rub elevates these beef ribs to a whole new level of barbecue delight. So gather your friends and family, fire up the grill, and indulge in these succulent sous vide beef ribs that will have everyone asking for seconds. Enjoy!
