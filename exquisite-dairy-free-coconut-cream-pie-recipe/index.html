Exquisite Dairy-Free Coconut Cream Pie Recipe


If you’re searching for a dessert that embodies the tropical vibes of summer while being completely dairy-free, look no further than this exquisite coconut cream pie. This recipe blends the rich creaminess of coconut with a flaky crust and a hint of sweetness, creating a delightful treat that everyone will love, whether they have dietary restrictions or not. This coconut cream pie is not just a dessert; it’s an experience that transports you to a sun-kissed beach with every bite.

Ingredients

For the Pie Crust

  • 1 ½ cups almond flour
  • ¼ cup coconut oil (melted)
  • 3 tablespoons maple syrup or agave nectar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • ¼ teaspoon sea salt

For the Coconut Cream Filling

  • 1 can (13.5 oz) full-fat coconut milk
  • ½ cup coconut cream (scooped from the top of the can)
  • 1/3 cup maple syrup (adjust based on desired sweetness)
  • 2 tablespoons cornstarch
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • ¼ teaspoon sea salt
  • ½ cup unsweetened shredded coconut (for extra flavor)

For the Topping

  • 1 can (13.5 oz) coconut whipped cream (store-bought or homemade)
  • ¼ cup unsweetened shredded coconut (toasted for garnish)
  • Fresh mint leaves (optional, for garnish)

Instructions

Step 1: Prepare the Pie Crust

  1. Preheat the Oven: Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C).

  2. Mix the Ingredients: In a mixing bowl, combine almond flour, melted coconut oil, maple syrup, vanilla extract, and sea salt. Stir until the mixture is crumbly but holds together when pressed.

  3. Form the Crust: Press the mixture evenly into the bottom and up the sides of a 9-inch pie pan. It’s important to ensure it is compact so that it holds its shape while baking.

  4. Bake the Crust: Place the crust in the preheated oven and bake for about 10-12 minutes or until it turns golden brown. Remove from the oven and allow it to cool completely before adding the filling.

Step 2: Make the Coconut Cream Filling

  1. Combine Ingredients: In a saucepan over medium heat, combine the full-fat coconut milk, coconut cream, maple syrup, cornstarch, vanilla extract, and sea salt. Whisk until smooth and well combined.

  2. Heat the Mixture: Continue to cook, stirring constantly until the mixture begins to thicken and bubble. This should take about 5-7 minutes.

  3. Add Shredded Coconut: Once thickened, remove from heat and stir in the shredded coconut. This gives the filling an extra coconut kick and delightful texture.

  4. Cool the Filling: Allow the filling to cool for a few minutes before pouring it into the cooled pie crust. Spread it evenly and smooth the top with a spatula.

Step 3: Chill the Pie

  1. Refrigerate: Cover the pie with plastic wrap and refrigerate for at least 4 hours, or until the filling is set. This step is crucial for achieving that perfect slice.

Step 4: Prepare the Topping

  1. Whip Coconut Cream: If you’re making homemade coconut whipped cream, chill a can of coconut milk overnight and scoop out the solidified cream into a mixing bowl. Whip it until fluffy using a hand mixer.

  2. Top the Pie: Once the filling is set, spread the coconut whipped cream generously over the top of the pie.

  3. Garnish: Sprinkle the toasted shredded coconut over the whipped cream for an added crunch and visual appeal. Add fresh mint leaves for a pop of color.

Step 5: Serve and Enjoy

  1. Slice the Pie: Using a sharp knife, cut the pie into slices. Serve chilled for the best flavor and texture.

  2. Storage: Leftovers can be stored in the refrigerator for up to 3 days. Keep covered to maintain freshness.

Tips for Success

  • Choose Quality Ingredients: Use high-quality coconut milk and coconut cream for the best flavor. Look for brands that contain minimal additives.
  • Toasting Coconut: To toast shredded coconut, place it in a dry skillet over medium heat. Stir frequently until it turns golden brown, then remove from heat immediately to prevent burning.
  • Serving Suggestions: This coconut cream pie pairs beautifully with fresh fruit like sliced mango or pineapple, enhancing the tropical experience.

Conclusion

This exquisite dairy-free coconut cream pie is more than just a dessert; it’s a celebration of flavors and textures that everyone can enjoy. Whether you’re hosting a summer barbecue, celebrating a special occasion, or simply indulging your sweet tooth, this pie is sure to impress. With its creamy coconut filling, flaky crust, and luscious topping, it’s a delightful treat that’s as pleasing to the eye as it is to the palate.

So gather your ingredients, roll up your sleeves, and embark on this delicious culinary adventure. You’ll be rewarded with a pie that not only satisfies your cravings but also delights your guests. Enjoy every creamy, dreamy bite!
