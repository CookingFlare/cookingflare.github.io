Pasta e Fagioli: A Hearty Italian Pasta and Bean Soup


Welcome to our blog, where we explore delicious and comforting recipes from around the world. Today, we’re diving into the heart of Italian cuisine with a classic dish that will warm your soul on even the coldest of days - Pasta e Fagioli. This hearty pasta and bean soup is a staple in Italian households, known for its simplicity, rich flavors, and satisfying textures. Join us on this culinary journey as we guide you through the steps to create your own bowl of comforting goodness.

Ingredients

To make Pasta e Fagioli, you’ll need the following ingredients:

  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 carrots, diced
  • 2 celery stalks, diced
  • 3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 2 cups cooked cannellini beans
  • 4 cups vegetable or chicken broth
  • 1 can diced tomatoes
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1 bay leaf
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 cup ditalini pasta (or any small pasta shape)
  • Fresh parsley, chopped (for garnish)

Instructions

  1. Heat the olive oil in a large pot over medium heat. Add the chopped onion, carrots, and celery. Sauté until the vegetables are softened and the onions are translucent, about 5-7 minutes.

  2. Add the minced garlic to the pot and cook for an additional minute, until fragrant.

  3. Add the cooked cannellini beans, vegetable or chicken broth, diced tomatoes (with their juice), dried oregano, dried basil, bay leaf, salt, and pepper. Stir well to combine.

  4. Bring the soup to a boil, then reduce the heat to low. Cover the pot and let it simmer for about 20-30 minutes, allowing the flavors to meld together.

  5. While the soup is simmering, bring a separate pot of salted water to a boil. Cook the ditalini pasta according to the package instructions until al dente. Drain and set aside.

  6. After the soup has simmered, remove the bay leaf and use an immersion blender or a potato masher to partially puree the soup. This will help thicken it and create a creamy texture while still leaving some whole beans intact.

  7. Add the cooked pasta to the pot of soup and stir well. Allow the soup to simmer for an additional 5-10 minutes, until the pasta is heated through and the flavors have fully combined.

  8. Taste and adjust the seasoning with salt and pepper if needed.

  9. Ladle the Pasta e Fagioli into bowls and garnish with fresh chopped parsley. Serve hot, and enjoy the comforting flavors of Italy in every spoonful.

Conclusion

Pasta e Fagioli is the epitome of Italian comfort food. This hearty soup brings together the natural flavors of beans, vegetables, and pasta, creating a dish that is both satisfying and nourishing. Its simplicity allows the ingredients to shine, while the combination of textures and aromas is sure to delight your taste buds.

Whether you’re looking for a quick weeknight meal or a cozy dish to share with friends and family, Pasta e Fagioli is a perfect choice. So gather your ingredients, follow our simple instructions, and immerse yourself in the flavors of Italy. Buon appetito!
