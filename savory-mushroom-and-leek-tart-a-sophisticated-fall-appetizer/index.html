Savory Mushroom and Leek Tart: A Sophisticated Fall Appetizer


Are you looking for an elegant and flavorful appetizer to impress your guests this fall? Look no further than this delectable Savory Mushroom and Leek Tart. With its buttery crust, rich mushroom and leek filling, and a hint of fresh herbs, this tart is sure to be a hit at any gathering. Whether you’re hosting a dinner party or simply craving a sophisticated snack, this recipe is a must-try.

Ingredients

For the crust:

  • 1 ½ cups all-purpose flour
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • 1 stick unsalted butter, cold and cubed
  • 3-4 tablespoons ice water

For the filling:

  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 leeks, white and light green parts only, thinly sliced
  • 8 ounces cremini mushrooms, sliced
  • 8 ounces shiitake mushrooms, sliced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon fresh thyme leaves
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • ½ cup grated Gruyere cheese
  • ¼ cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • ½ cup heavy cream
  • 2 large eggs

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).

  2. In a large mixing bowl, combine the flour and salt for the crust. Add the cold cubed butter and using a pastry cutter or your fingertips, mix until the mixture resembles coarse crumbs.

  3. Gradually add the ice water, one tablespoon at a time, mixing until the dough comes together. Shape the dough into a disk, wrap it in plastic wrap, and refrigerate for at least 30 minutes.

  4. While the dough is chilling, heat the olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Add the leeks and cook until softened, about 5 minutes. Add the mushrooms, garlic, thyme leaves, salt, and pepper. Sauté until the mushrooms are golden brown and any liquid has evaporated, about 10 minutes. Remove from heat and let it cool slightly.

  5. On a lightly floured surface, roll out the chilled dough to fit a 9-inch tart pan. Transfer the dough to the pan, press it into the bottom and sides, and trim any excess. Prick the bottom of the crust with a fork.

  6. In a small bowl, whisk together the heavy cream and eggs. Season with salt and pepper.

  7. Sprinkle half of the Gruyere cheese and Parmesan cheese over the bottom of the crust. Spoon the mushroom and leek mixture over the cheese. Pour the cream and egg mixture evenly over the filling. Top with the remaining cheese.

  8. Place the tart pan on a baking sheet and bake for 30-35 minutes, or until the crust is golden brown and the filling is set.

  9. Remove the tart from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes before slicing and serving.

Serving Suggestions

This Savory Mushroom and Leek Tart is best served warm or at room temperature. It makes a delightful appetizer for fall dinner parties, holiday gatherings, or even as a light lunch paired with a side salad. The earthy flavors of the mushrooms and leeks, combined with the creamy and cheesy filling, create a harmonious balance that will leave your taste buds craving more.

Conclusion

The Savory Mushroom and Leek Tart is a sophisticated and flavorful appetizer that will elevate any occasion. With its buttery crust, rich mushroom and leek filling, and a touch of fresh herbs, this tart is a true culinary delight. Impress your guests with this easy-to-make yet elegant dish that captures the essence of fall. So why wait? Try this recipe today and savor the flavors of the season!
