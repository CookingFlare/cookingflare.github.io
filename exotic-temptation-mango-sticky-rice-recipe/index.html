Exotic Temptation: Mango Sticky Rice Recipe


Are you ready to embark on a culinary journey that combines the exotic flavors of mango and the comforting sweetness of sticky rice? Look no further than this tantalizing recipe for Mango Sticky Rice! Originating from Thailand, this dessert is the perfect balance of sweetness and creaminess, making it a true delight for your taste buds.

Ingredients

  • 2 ripe mangoes
  • 1 1/2 cups of glutinous rice
  • 1 cup of coconut milk
  • 1/2 cup of sugar
  • 1/4 teaspoon of salt
  • Toasted sesame seeds (optional, for garnish)

Instructions

  1. Start by soaking the glutinous rice in water for at least 4 hours, or overnight. This will ensure that the rice becomes soft and sticky when cooked.

  2. After soaking, drain the rice and transfer it to a steamer lined with cheesecloth or a muslin cloth. Steam the rice over medium heat for approximately 30 minutes, or until it becomes tender and translucent.

  3. While the rice is steaming, prepare the coconut sauce. In a small saucepan, combine the coconut milk, sugar, and salt. Heat the mixture over low heat, stirring constantly until the sugar dissolves. Set aside.

  4. Once the rice is cooked, transfer it to a large mixing bowl. Pour half of the coconut sauce over the rice and gently fold it in until the rice is evenly coated. Allow the rice to cool for a few minutes.

  5. While the rice is cooling, peel and slice the mangoes. Set aside.

  6. To serve, place a generous portion of the sticky rice on a plate or in a bowl. Top it with a few slices of mango and drizzle the remaining coconut sauce over the mango and rice. If desired, sprinkle some toasted sesame seeds on top for added texture and flavor.

  7. Enjoy your Mango Sticky Rice while it’s still warm for the ultimate indulgence. The combination of the creamy coconut sauce, fragrant sticky rice, and juicy mangoes will transport you to a tropical paradise with every bite.

Tips and Variations

  • For an extra touch of authenticity, try using Thai sticky rice (also known as glutinous or sweet rice) instead of regular sticky rice. It has a slightly different texture and flavor that elevates the dish to a whole new level.

  • If you prefer a vegan version, you can substitute the regular coconut milk with coconut milk alternative, such as almond or soy milk. Just make sure to choose the unsweetened variety to maintain the balance of flavors.

  • Feel free to get creative with the presentation. You can use a ring mold to shape the sticky rice into a neat round shape, or serve it in individual bowls for a more elegant display.

  • If you’re feeling adventurous, you can also experiment with different fruit toppings. Try adding sliced strawberries, passion fruit, or even durian for a unique twist on this classic dessert.

So there you have it, a truly exotic temptation that will satisfy your sweet tooth and transport your taste buds to the tropics. With its creamy coconut sauce, sticky rice, and luscious mangoes, this Mango Sticky Rice recipe is a must-try for any dessert lover. Enjoy the flavors of Thailand in the comfort of your own home and let your taste buds take a vacation they will never forget!
