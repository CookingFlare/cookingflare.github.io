Delicious Family-Friendly Grilled Cheese Variations


Grilled cheese sandwiches are a classic favorite, cherished for their comforting simplicity and the delightful combination of melted cheese and crispy bread. While the traditional grilled cheese is always a hit, why not take your family’s taste buds on a delicious adventure? In this blog post, we’ll explore a variety of inventive grilled cheese variations that are sure to please even the pickiest eaters. From gourmet ingredients to fun twists, these recipes will elevate your grilled cheese game and bring everyone to the table.

The Classic Grilled Cheese with a Twist

Ingredients:

  • 2 slices of sourdough bread
  • 2 slices of sharp cheddar cheese
  • 1 tablespoon of unsalted butter
  • 1 tablespoon of Dijon mustard (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Prep the Bread: Spread butter on one side of each slice of bread. If you’re feeling adventurous, smear a thin layer of Dijon mustard on the unbuttered side of one slice.
  2. Assemble: Place one slice of bread butter-side down on a hot skillet. Lay the cheese slices on top, then cover with the second slice of bread, butter-side up.
  3. Grill: Cook over medium heat for about 3-4 minutes on each side, or until the bread is golden brown and the cheese is melted.
  4. Serve: Cut in half and enjoy with a side of tomato soup for a classic pairing.

Pesto and Tomato Grilled Cheese

Ingredients:

  • 2 slices of whole-grain bread
  • 2 slices of mozzarella cheese
  • 2 tablespoons of basil pesto
  • 1 medium tomato, sliced
  • 1 tablespoon of olive oil

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the Bread: Spread pesto on one side of each slice of bread.
  2. Layer Ingredients: On the non-pesto side, layer the tomato slices and mozzarella cheese.
  3. Grill: Heat olive oil in a skillet over medium heat. Place the sandwich in the skillet, cooking until golden brown, about 3-4 minutes per side.
  4. Serve: Slice and serve warm, garnished with fresh basil if desired.

Spinach and Feta Grilled Cheese

Ingredients:

  • 2 slices of rye bread
  • 2 slices of provolone cheese
  • 1 cup fresh spinach
  • 1/4 cup crumbled feta cheese
  • 1 tablespoon of butter

Instructions:

  1. Sauté Spinach: In a small pan, sauté spinach until wilted. Remove from heat and mix in feta cheese.
  2. Assemble the Sandwich: Butter one side of each slice of rye bread. On the unbuttered side, layer provolone cheese, the sautéed spinach and feta mixture, and then top with another slice of provolone.
  3. Grill: Cook in a skillet over medium heat until the bread is golden and the cheese is melty, about 3-4 minutes per side.
  4. Serve: Slice and serve with a tangy side salad.

Avocado and Cheddar Grilled Cheese

Ingredients:

  • 2 slices of multigrain bread
  • 2 slices of sharp cheddar cheese
  • 1/2 ripe avocado, sliced
  • 1 tablespoon of mayonnaise
  • 1 teaspoon of lime juice
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Prepare Avocado: In a small bowl, mash avocado with lime juice, salt, and pepper. Spread mayonnaise on the outside of each slice of bread.
  2. Layer: On the non-mayo side, layer the cheddar cheese and avocado slices.
  3. Grill: Cook in a skillet over medium heat until the bread is crispy and golden, about 3-4 minutes per side.
  4. Serve: Cut in half and enjoy your creamy, cheesy creation!

BBQ Chicken Grilled Cheese

Ingredients:

  • 2 slices of Texas toast
  • 1 cup shredded rotisserie chicken
  • 1/4 cup barbecue sauce
  • 2 slices of pepper jack cheese
  • 1 tablespoon of butter

Instructions:

  1. Mix Chicken: In a bowl, combine shredded chicken and barbecue sauce.
  2. Assemble: Butter one side of each slice of Texas toast. On the unbuttered side, layer pepper jack cheese, the BBQ chicken mixture, and top with another slice of cheese.
  3. Grill: Cook in a skillet over medium heat until the bread is golden brown and the cheese is melted, about 4-5 minutes per side.
  4. Serve: Slice and serve with extra barbecue sauce for dipping.

Mediterranean Grilled Cheese

Ingredients:

  • 2 slices of focaccia bread
  • 2 slices of mozzarella cheese
  • 1/4 cup roasted red peppers, sliced
  • 1 tablespoon of olive tapenade
  • 1 tablespoon of olive oil

Instructions:

  1. Assemble: Spread olive tapenade on one side of each slice of focaccia. Layer with roasted red peppers and mozzarella cheese.
  2. Grill: Heat olive oil in a skillet over medium heat. Place the sandwich in the skillet and cook until golden brown, about 3-4 minutes per side.
  3. Serve: Slice and serve with a side of olives for an extra Mediterranean touch.

Sweet and Savory Apple Grilled Cheese

Ingredients:

  • 2 slices of cinnamon raisin bread
  • 2 slices of gouda cheese
  • 1 small apple, thinly sliced (Granny Smith or Honeycrisp works well)
  • 2 tablespoons of honey
  • 1 tablespoon of butter

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the Sandwich: Butter one side of each slice of bread. On the unbuttered side, layer gouda cheese, apple slices, and drizzle honey on top.
  2. Grill: Cook in a skillet over medium heat until golden brown and the cheese is melted, about 3-4 minutes per side.
  3. Serve: Slice and enjoy the delightful mix of sweet and savory flavors.

Breakfast Grilled Cheese

Ingredients:

  • 2 slices of whole wheat bread
  • 2 slices of cheddar cheese
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 strips of cooked bacon or turkey bacon
  • 1 tablespoon of butter

Instructions:

  1. Cook Eggs: In a small skillet, fry the eggs to your liking (sunny-side up or over-easy).
  2. Assemble: Butter one side of each slice of bread. Place one slice butter-side down in a skillet, layer with cheddar cheese, bacon, and the fried egg, then top with the second slice of bread, butter-side up.
  3. Grill: Cook until the bread is golden brown and the cheese is melted, about 3-4 minutes per side.
  4. Serve: Slice and serve warm for a hearty breakfast treat.

Dessert Grilled Cheese

Ingredients:

  • 2 slices of brioche bread
  • 2 slices of cream cheese
  • 1 tablespoon of Nutella or your favorite chocolate spread
  • 1 banana, sliced
  • 1 tablespoon of butter

Instructions:

  1. Assemble: Butter one side of each slice of brioche. On the unbuttered side, spread Nutella, layer cream cheese, and add banana slices.
  2. Grill: Place the sandwich in a skillet, cooking until the bread is golden and the fillings are warm, about 3-4 minutes per side.
  3. Serve: Dust with powdered sugar and serve for a sweet finish to any meal.

Tips for Perfect Grilled Cheese

  1. Use Quality Ingredients: The flavor of your grilled cheese largely depends on the quality of the cheese and bread you choose. Opt for artisanal breads and high-quality cheeses for the best results.
  2. Low and Slow: Cooking your sandwich over medium to low heat ensures that the cheese melts perfectly without burning the bread.
  3. Experiment with Cheese: Don’t limit yourself to just cheddar! Try a blend of cheeses like fontina, gouda, or brie for a gourmet touch.
  4. Add Fresh Herbs: Incorporating fresh herbs like basil, thyme, or dill can elevate the flavor profile of your grilled cheese.
  5. Pair with Dips: While tomato soup is a classic, consider other dips like pesto, salsa, or even a spicy aioli for a fun twist.

Conclusion

Grilled cheese sandwiches are a versatile and beloved meal that can easily be adapted to suit your family’s tastes and dietary preferences. Whether you’re enjoying a classic version or trying one of these innovative variations, grilled cheese is sure to bring comfort and joy to your table. Grab your ingredients, gather the family, and embark on a delicious grilled cheese adventure today!
