Scrumptious Gluten-Free Apple Crisp Recipe for a Fall Dessert


Fall is the perfect time to indulge in warm and comforting desserts that make the most of the season’s bountiful harvest. With its delightful combination of sweet and tart flavors, the Scrumptious Gluten-Free Apple Crisp is a must-try recipe for apple lovers and dessert enthusiasts alike. This easy-to-make dessert will fill your kitchen with the irresistible aroma of cinnamon while providing a satisfying treat for your taste buds.

Ingredients

  • 6 medium-sized apples (preferably Granny Smith or Honeycrisp)
  • 1 cup gluten-free rolled oats
  • 1/2 cup almond flour
  • 1/2 cup brown sugar
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/4 cup melted coconut oil
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground nutmeg
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1 tablespoon fresh lemon juice

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease a 9x9-inch baking dish with coconut oil or non-stick cooking spray.

  2. Peel, core, and slice the apples into thin, even slices. Place the apple slices in a large mixing bowl and toss them with lemon juice to prevent browning.

  3. In a separate mixing bowl, combine the rolled oats, almond flour, brown sugar, granulated sugar, cinnamon, nutmeg, and salt. Mix well to ensure all the ingredients are evenly distributed.

  4. Pour the melted coconut oil over the dry mixture and stir until the mixture becomes crumbly and resembles coarse sand.

  5. Spread half of the apple slices evenly in the greased baking dish. Sprinkle half of the oat topping over the apples, making sure to cover them completely.

  6. Repeat the layering process with the remaining apple slices and oat topping.

  7. Bake the apple crisp in the preheated oven for 35-40 minutes, or until the apples are tender and the topping is golden brown.

  8. Once the apple crisp is done baking, remove it from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes before serving.

  9. Serve the Scrumptious Gluten-Free Apple Crisp warm, either on its own or with a scoop of vanilla ice cream for an extra indulgence.

Tips and Variations

  • To add a touch of tartness to your apple crisp, consider incorporating a small amount of cranberries or sliced rhubarb into the apple mixture.
  • Feel free to experiment with different types of apples to achieve the flavor and texture you prefer.
  • If you don’t follow a gluten-free diet, you can substitute the almond flour with all-purpose flour.
  • For a nutty twist, sprinkle some chopped walnuts or pecans over the oat topping before baking.
  • Leftovers can be stored in an airtight container in the refrigerator for up to three days. Simply reheat in the oven or microwave before serving.

Enjoy the delightful flavors of autumn with this Scrumptious Gluten-Free Apple Crisp recipe. Whether you’re hosting a fall gathering or simply craving a cozy dessert, this gluten-free option is sure to satisfy your sweet tooth. Give it a try and let the comforting aroma and delicious taste transport you to the heart of the fall season.
