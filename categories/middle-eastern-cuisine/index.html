The Perfect Pairing: Middle Eastern Tabouleh and Grilled Chicken

Welcome to our kitchen! Today, we have an exquisite Middle Eastern culinary delight for you. We present the perfect pairing of fresh and vibrant Middle Eastern Tabouleh with succulent Grilled Chicken. This combination of flavors and textures will transport your taste buds to the sun-soaked lands of the Middle East. So, let’s dive right into this delightful recipe! Ingredients For the Tabouleh: 1 cup bulgur wheat 1 1/2 cups boiling water 2 cups fresh parsley, finely chopped 1/2 cup fresh mint leaves, finely chopped 1/2 cup cherry tomatoes, halved 1/4 cup red onion, finely chopped 1/4 cup cucumber, finely chopped 2 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil 2 tablespoons lemon juice Salt and pepper to taste For the Grilled Chicken: 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts 2 tablespoons olive oil 2 cloves garlic, minced 1 teaspoon ground cumin 1 teaspoon ground coriander 1 teaspoon paprika Salt and pepper to taste Instructions 1. Prepare the Tabouleh: In a heatproof bowl, pour the boiling water over the bulgur wheat. Cover and let it sit for about 15 minutes or until the water is absorbed. Fluff the bulgur wheat with a fork and transfer it to a large mixing bowl. Add the parsley, mint, cherry tomatoes, red onion, and cucumber to the bowl with the bulgur wheat. In a small bowl, whisk together the olive oil, lemon juice, salt, and pepper. Pour the dressing over the tabouleh ingredients and toss until everything is well coated. Set the tabouleh aside to allow the flavors to meld while you prepare the grilled chicken. 2. Grill the Chicken: Preheat your grill to medium-high heat. In a small bowl, combine the olive oil, minced garlic, cumin, coriander, paprika, salt, and pepper to create a marinade. Rub the marinade evenly over both sides of the chicken breasts. Place the chicken breasts on the preheated grill and cook for approximately 6-7 minutes per side, or until the internal temperature reaches 165°F (74°C). Remove the chicken from the grill and let it rest for a few minutes before slicing. 3. Serve and Enjoy! Spoon a generous portion of the tabouleh onto each plate. Slice the grilled chicken breasts and arrange them on top of the tabouleh. Garnish with a sprig of fresh mint or parsley, if desired. Serve immediately and savor the delicious flavors of the Middle East! Conclusion Congratulations! You have successfully prepared a mouthwatering Middle Eastern feast with our perfect pairing of Tabouleh and Grilled Chicken. The fresh and zesty tabouleh perfectly complements the smoky and tender grilled chicken, creating a harmonious blend of flavors. This dish is not only a delight for the taste buds, but it also provides a wholesome and nutritious meal. So, gather your loved ones, indulge in this Middle Eastern culinary journey, and let the flavors transport you to distant lands. Enjoy! ...
