Sweet and Salty Salted Caramel Tart: A Flavorful Combination


If you’re a fan of the delightful contrast between sweet and salty flavors, then this Sweet and Salty Salted Caramel Tart is the perfect dessert for you. This indulgent treat combines the rich, buttery sweetness of caramel with a hint of salt, all encased in a crisp, buttery crust. This recipe is a true celebration of flavor and texture, and it’s perfect for impressing guests at dinner parties or simply treating yourself to something special.

Ingredients

For the Crust:

  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, cold and cut into cubes
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1 large egg yolk
  • 2-3 tablespoons ice water

For the Caramel Filling:

  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 6 tablespoons unsalted butter, room temperature
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • 1 teaspoon sea salt

For the Garnish (Optional):

  • Flaky sea salt, for sprinkling
  • Whipped cream or vanilla ice cream, for serving

Instructions

Step 1: Prepare the Crust

  1. In a food processor, combine the flour, sugar, and salt. Add the cold butter cubes and pulse until the mixture resembles coarse crumbs.
  2. Add the egg yolk and 2 tablespoons of ice water. Pulse until the dough comes together, adding an additional tablespoon of ice water if needed.
  3. Turn the dough out onto a lightly floured surface and shape it into a disk. Wrap it in plastic wrap and refrigerate for at least 30 minutes.

Step 2: Roll Out the Dough

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. On a lightly floured surface, roll out the chilled dough into a circle large enough to fit into a 9-inch tart pan. Press the dough into the pan, trimming any excess from the edges.
  3. Prick the bottom of the crust with a fork, then place a piece of parchment paper on top and fill with pie weights or dry beans.
  4. Bake for 15 minutes, then remove the parchment paper and weights and bake for an additional 10-15 minutes, or until the crust is golden brown. Allow it to cool completely.

Step 3: Make the Caramel Filling

  1. In a heavy-bottomed saucepan, heat the granulated sugar over medium heat. Stir constantly with a heatproof spatula until the sugar has melted and turned a deep amber color.
  2. Add the butter and stir until melted and combined with the caramelized sugar.
  3. Slowly pour in the heavy cream while stirring constantly. Be careful, as the mixture will bubble up.
  4. Stir in the sea salt, then remove the caramel from the heat and let it cool for a few minutes.

Step 4: Assemble the Tart

  1. Pour the slightly cooled caramel into the cooled tart crust, spreading it into an even layer.
  2. If desired, sprinkle a pinch of flaky sea salt over the top for an extra pop of flavor.
  3. Refrigerate the tart for at least 2 hours, or until the caramel is set.

Step 5: Serve and Enjoy

  1. Once the tart is chilled and the caramel is set, slice it into wedges and serve with a dollop of whipped cream or a scoop of vanilla ice cream, if desired.
  2. Indulge in the delightful combination of sweet and salty flavors, and savor each buttery, caramel-filled bite.

Conclusion

This Sweet and Salty Salted Caramel Tart is a true masterpiece of flavors and textures. The buttery, crumbly crust provides the perfect base for the rich, velvety caramel filling, while the hint of sea salt adds a delightful contrast that will keep you coming back for more. Whether it’s for a special occasion or just a regular day that calls for something extraordinary, this tart is sure to impress and satisfy. So, roll up your sleeves, gather your ingredients, and treat yourself to the delightful experience of creating and savoring this exceptional dessert.
