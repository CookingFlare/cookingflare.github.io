Sweet and Tangy BBQ Sliders: A Crowd-Pleasing Recipe


If you’re looking for a delicious and crowd-pleasing dish for your next gathering, look no further than these Sweet and Tangy BBQ Sliders. These sliders are a perfect combination of sweet and tangy flavors, packed into a small, handheld package that will have your guests coming back for more. Whether you’re hosting a game night, a family barbecue, or a casual get-together, these sliders are sure to be a hit.

Ingredients

For the sliders:

  • 2 pounds ground beef
  • 1/2 cup breadcrumbs
  • 1/4 cup milk
  • 1/4 cup finely chopped onion
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 12 slider buns

For the sweet and tangy BBQ sauce:

  • 1 cup ketchup
  • 1/2 cup brown sugar
  • 1/4 cup apple cider vinegar
  • 2 tablespoons Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 tablespoon Dijon mustard
  • 1 teaspoon smoked paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon onion powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder

For the coleslaw topping:

  • 2 cups shredded green cabbage
  • 1/2 cup shredded carrots
  • 1/4 cup mayonnaise
  • 1 tablespoon apple cider vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon honey
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Prepare the BBQ Sauce: In a small saucepan, combine the ketchup, brown sugar, apple cider vinegar, Worcestershire sauce, Dijon mustard, smoked paprika, onion powder, and garlic powder. Bring the mixture to a simmer over medium heat, then reduce the heat and let it simmer for about 15 minutes, stirring occasionally, until the sauce thickens slightly. Set aside to cool.

  2. Make the Sliders: In a large bowl, mix together the ground beef, breadcrumbs, milk, chopped onion, garlic powder, salt, and pepper. Form the mixture into small patties, slightly larger than the slider buns. Cook the patties on a grill or in a skillet over medium-high heat for about 3-4 minutes per side, or until they reach your desired level of doneness.

  3. Prepare the Coleslaw Topping: In a separate bowl, combine the shredded cabbage, shredded carrots, mayonnaise, apple cider vinegar, honey, salt, and pepper. Toss until the cabbage and carrots are well coated with the dressing.

  4. Assemble the Sliders: Split the slider buns and lightly toast them. Place a cooked beef patty on the bottom half of each bun. Spoon a generous amount of the sweet and tangy BBQ sauce over each patty. Top with a scoop of the coleslaw mixture, then cover with the top half of the slider bun.

  5. Serve and Enjoy: Arrange the sliders on a platter and serve them with your favorite side dishes. Watch as your guests dig into these irresistible Sweet and Tangy BBQ Sliders.

Tips for Success

  • Feel free to customize the coleslaw topping by adding shredded red cabbage, thinly sliced red onion, or a sprinkle of celery seeds for extra crunch and flavor.
  • If you prefer a smokier flavor, you can add a few drops of liquid smoke to the BBQ sauce.
  • For a vegetarian option, substitute the ground beef with plant-based ground meat and adjust the cooking time accordingly.

Final Thoughts

These Sweet and Tangy BBQ Sliders are the ultimate crowd-pleaser. With the perfect balance of sweet, tangy, and savory flavors, they are sure to be a hit at any gathering. The combination of juicy beef, flavorful BBQ sauce, and refreshing coleslaw topping makes these sliders a standout dish that will have everyone asking for the recipe. So, fire up the grill and get ready to impress your guests with this irresistible recipe!
