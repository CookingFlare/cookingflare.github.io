Hearty Winter Calzone Recipes for a Cheesy Delight


Winter is the perfect time to indulge in warm and comforting dishes. And what could be more comforting than a delicious, cheesy calzone? These Italian turnovers are a treat for the taste buds, with their flaky crust and gooey filling. In this post, we will explore three hearty winter calzone recipes that are sure to satisfy your cravings and keep you warm on those chilly nights.

1. Classic Margherita Calzone

Ingredients:

  • Pizza dough
  • Tomato sauce
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • Olive oil
  • Salt and pepper

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 425°F (220°C).
  2. Roll out the pizza dough into a circle or rectangle, depending on your preference.
  3. Spread a thin layer of tomato sauce on one half of the dough, leaving a small border around the edges.
  4. Top the sauce with a generous amount of mozzarella cheese.
  5. Tear the fresh basil leaves into small pieces and sprinkle them over the cheese.
  6. Fold the empty half of the dough over the filling and press the edges to seal.
  7. Brush the top of the calzone with olive oil and season with salt and pepper.
  8. Using a sharp knife, make a few small slits on top to allow steam to escape during baking.
  9. Transfer the calzone to a baking sheet and bake for 15-20 minutes, or until the crust is golden brown.
  10. Allow the calzone to cool for a few minutes before slicing and serving.

2. Spinach and Ricotta Calzone

Ingredients:

  • Pizza dough
  • Olive oil
  • Garlic, minced
  • Spinach, fresh or frozen
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Parmesan cheese, grated
  • Salt and pepper

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 425°F (220°C).
  2. Heat a tablespoon of olive oil in a pan over medium heat.
  3. Add the minced garlic and sauté for a minute until fragrant.
  4. If using fresh spinach, add it to the pan and cook until wilted. If using frozen spinach, thaw and squeeze out any excess liquid before adding it to the pan.
  5. In a bowl, combine the cooked spinach, ricotta cheese, and grated Parmesan cheese. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  6. Roll out the pizza dough into a circle or rectangle.
  7. Spread the spinach and ricotta mixture on one half of the dough, leaving a small border around the edges.
  8. Fold the empty half of the dough over the filling and press the edges to seal.
  9. Brush the top of the calzone with olive oil.
  10. Transfer the calzone to a baking sheet and bake for 15-20 minutes, or until the crust is golden brown.
  11. Allow the calzone to cool for a few minutes before slicing and serving.

3. BBQ Chicken Calzone

Ingredients:

  • Pizza dough
  • Cooked chicken, shredded
  • BBQ sauce
  • Red onion, thinly sliced
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Cilantro, chopped
  • Salt and pepper

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 425°F (220°C).
  2. Roll out the pizza dough into a circle or rectangle.
  3. Spread a thin layer of BBQ sauce on one half of the dough, leaving a small border around the edges.
  4. Top the sauce with shredded chicken, red onion slices, and mozzarella cheese.
  5. Sprinkle chopped cilantro over the filling and season with salt and pepper to taste.
  6. Fold the empty half of the dough over the filling and press the edges to seal.
  7. Transfer the calzone to a baking sheet and bake for 15-20 minutes, or until the crust is golden brown.
  8. Allow the calzone to cool for a few minutes before slicing and serving.

Whether you prefer a classic Margherita, a spinach and ricotta combo, or a BBQ chicken filling, these hearty winter calzone recipes will surely warm your heart and satisfy your cravings. So, gather your ingredients, preheat your oven, and get ready to enjoy a cheesy delight that will make your taste buds sing.
