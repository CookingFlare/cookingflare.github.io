Warm and Tasty Chicken Pot Pie Soup: A Cozy Meal


There’s something incredibly comforting about a warm and tasty bowl of chicken pot pie soup. It’s like a hug in a bowl, with its rich, creamy broth, tender chicken, and hearty vegetables. This dish embodies the essence of comfort food, perfect for chilly evenings or when you just need a little pick-me-up. In this recipe, we’ll walk through how to create this soul-soothing soup that’s bound to become a favorite in your home.

Ingredients

Before we dive into the cooking process, let’s take a look at the ingredients you’ll need to prepare this delicious chicken pot pie soup:

  • 1 pound of boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 4 tablespoons of butter
  • 1 cup of diced onion
  • 1 cup of diced celery
  • 1 cup of diced carrots
  • 3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1/2 cup of all-purpose flour
  • 4 cups of chicken broth
  • 2 cups of whole milk
  • 1 teaspoon of dried thyme
  • 1 teaspoon of dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 cup of frozen peas
  • 1/4 cup of chopped fresh parsley
  • Store-bought or homemade pie crust, for serving

Instructions

Step 1: Prepare the Chicken

Start by cooking the chicken. You can do this by poaching the chicken breasts in a pot of simmering water until they are cooked through. Once done, remove the chicken from the pot, let it cool, and then shred it into bite-sized pieces.

Step 2: Saute the Vegetables

In a large pot or Dutch oven, melt the butter over medium heat. Add the diced onion, celery, and carrots, and saute until they begin to soften, about 5 minutes. Stir in the minced garlic and cook for an additional minute.

Step 3: Create the Soup Base

Sprinkle the flour over the sauteed vegetables and stir to combine. Cook the flour for a couple of minutes to get rid of the raw taste. Then, slowly pour in the chicken broth and whole milk, stirring constantly to prevent lumps from forming. Add the dried thyme, dried oregano, salt, and pepper. Bring the mixture to a gentle simmer and let it cook for about 10 minutes, or until it thickens slightly.

Step 4: Add the Chicken and Peas

Once the soup base has thickened, add the shredded chicken and frozen peas. Let the soup simmer for an additional 5-10 minutes, allowing the flavors to meld together. Taste and adjust the seasoning if needed.

Step 5: Serve

Ladle the warm and tasty chicken pot pie soup into bowls, garnish with chopped fresh parsley, and serve with a flaky piece of store-bought or homemade pie crust on top. The buttery, golden crust adds a delightful crunch to this comforting soup.

Conclusion

With its creamy texture, savory flavors, and comforting aroma, warm and tasty chicken pot pie soup is the perfect dish for cozy nights or when you simply need a soothing meal. This recipe is versatile, allowing you to customize it with your favorite vegetables and herbs to suit your taste. Whether you’re cooking for yourself, your family, or friends, this soup is sure to warm both hearts and tummies. Enjoy the process of creating this comforting meal, and savor every spoonful of this cozy chicken pot pie soup.
