Spicy Dairy-Free BBQ Chicken Skewers for Grilling Season


Summer is here, and that means it’s time to fire up the grill and enjoy some delicious barbecue. If you’re looking for a tasty and dairy-free option, these Spicy BBQ Chicken Skewers are the perfect choice. Packed with flavor and easy to make, they are sure to be a hit at your next cookout.

Ingredients

  • 1.5 pounds of boneless, skinless chicken breasts, cut into cubes
  • 1/4 cup of olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons of soy sauce (make sure it’s gluten-free if needed)
  • 2 tablespoons of honey
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon of paprika
  • 1 teaspoon of cayenne pepper (adjust according to your spice preference)
  • 1/2 teaspoon of black pepper
  • 1/2 teaspoon of salt
  • Skewers (if using wooden skewers, soak them in water for 30 minutes before grilling)

Instructions

  1. In a bowl, whisk together the olive oil, soy sauce, honey, minced garlic, paprika, cayenne pepper, black pepper, and salt. This marinade will be the key to infusing the chicken with delicious flavors.

  2. Add the chicken cubes to the marinade and toss until they are well coated. Allow the chicken to marinate for at least 30 minutes, or up to overnight in the refrigerator. The longer the chicken marinates, the more flavorful it will be.

  3. Preheat your grill to medium-high heat. If using a charcoal grill, make sure the coals are evenly distributed.

  4. Thread the marinated chicken cubes onto the skewers, leaving a little space between each piece for even cooking.

  5. Place the skewers on the preheated grill and cook for about 10-12 minutes, turning occasionally, until the chicken is cooked through and has a nice charred exterior. Make sure the internal temperature of the chicken reaches 165°F (74°C) for safe consumption.

  6. Once cooked, remove the skewers from the grill and let them rest for a few minutes before serving. This will allow the juices to redistribute, ensuring a moist and flavorful bite.

  7. Serve the Spicy BBQ Chicken Skewers with your favorite side dishes, such as grilled vegetables, corn on the cob, or a fresh salad. The spicy kick of the marinade pairs perfectly with the smoky flavors from the grill.

Enjoy!

Tips and Variations

  • If you prefer a milder flavor, reduce the amount of cayenne pepper or omit it entirely.
  • Feel free to add some additional spices or herbs to the marinade to customize the flavor. Smoked paprika, chili powder, or dried herbs like thyme or rosemary can be great additions.
  • For an extra burst of flavor, brush the skewers with the remaining marinade during the last few minutes of grilling.
  • If you don’t have a grill, you can also cook these skewers on a stovetop grill pan or in the oven under the broiler. Adjust the cooking time accordingly.

So, the next time you’re in the mood for some delicious barbecue, give these Spicy Dairy-Free BBQ Chicken Skewers a try. They are easy to make, packed with flavor, and perfect for grilling season. Happy cooking and enjoy your summer grilling adventures!
