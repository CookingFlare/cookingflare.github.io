Pat them dry using a clean kitchen towel or paper towels. In a blender or food processor, combine the mint leaves, coriander leaves, green chilies, onion, garlic, and ginger. Blend until you achieve a coarse paste-like consistency. Add the lemon juice, roasted cumin powder, sugar, and salt to the mixture. Blend again until all the ingredients are well incorporated. If the chutney appears too thick, gradually add water in small increments until you reach your desired consistency. Remember, the chutney should have a smooth texture but not be too runny. Once blended, transfer the chutney to a serving bowl or airtight container. It can be served immediately or refrigerated for later use. The chutney can be stored for up to a week when refrigerated.
