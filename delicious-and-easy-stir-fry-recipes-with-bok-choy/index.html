Delicious and Easy Stir Fry Recipes with Bok Choy


If you’re a fan of quick and flavorful meals, then stir fry recipes are a must-have in your cooking repertoire. And when it comes to stir fry, one vegetable that adds a delightful crunch and a touch of sweetness is bok choy. This leafy green, also known as Chinese cabbage, is not only delicious but also incredibly versatile. In this blog post, we’ll explore three mouthwatering stir fry recipes featuring bok choy that are sure to impress your taste buds.

1. Garlic Bok Choy Stir Fry

Ingredients:

  • 2 heads of bok choy
  • 3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon of vegetable oil
  • 1 tablespoon of soy sauce
  • 1 teaspoon of sesame oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Start by washing the bok choy thoroughly and trimming the ends.
  2. Heat the vegetable oil in a large skillet or wok over medium-high heat.
  3. Add the minced garlic to the pan and sauté for about 1 minute until fragrant.
  4. Add the bok choy to the skillet and stir fry for 2-3 minutes until it begins to wilt.
  5. Drizzle the soy sauce and sesame oil over the bok choy and season with salt and pepper.
  6. Continue to stir fry for an additional 2 minutes until the bok choy is tender-crisp.
  7. Remove from heat and serve hot as a side dish or as a main course with steamed rice.

2. Bok Choy and Mushroom Stir Fry

Ingredients:

  • 2 heads of bok choy
  • 8 ounces of mushrooms, sliced
  • 2 tablespoons of vegetable oil
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon of soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon of oyster sauce
  • 1 teaspoon of cornstarch
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Begin by washing the bok choy thoroughly and cutting it into bite-sized pieces.
  2. Heat the vegetable oil in a large skillet or wok over medium-high heat.
  3. Add the minced garlic to the pan and sauté for about 1 minute until fragrant.
  4. Add the sliced mushrooms and bok choy to the skillet and stir fry for 3-4 minutes until the vegetables are tender.
  5. In a small bowl, whisk together the soy sauce, oyster sauce, and cornstarch until well combined.
  6. Pour the sauce over the vegetables and stir fry for an additional 2 minutes until the sauce thickens.
  7. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  8. Remove from heat and serve hot with steamed rice or noodles.

3. Spicy Bok Choy and Shrimp Stir Fry

Ingredients:

  • 2 heads of bok choy
  • 1/2 pound of shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 2 tablespoons of vegetable oil
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon of soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon of hoisin sauce
  • 1 tablespoon of sriracha sauce (adjust to taste)
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Wash the bok choy thoroughly and cut it into bite-sized pieces.
  2. In a large skillet or wok, heat the vegetable oil over medium-high heat.
  3. Add the minced garlic to the pan and sauté for about 1 minute until fragrant.
  4. Add the shrimp to the skillet and stir fry for 2-3 minutes until they turn pink and opaque.
  5. Push the shrimp to one side of the skillet and add the bok choy to the other side.
  6. Stir fry the bok choy for 2-3 minutes until it begins to wilt.
  7. In a small bowl, whisk together the soy sauce, hoisin sauce, and sriracha sauce.
  8. Pour the sauce over the shrimp and bok choy, stir fry for an additional 2 minutes until everything is well coated.
  9. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  10. Remove from heat and serve hot with steamed rice or noodles.

Conclusion

Bok choy is a fantastic vegetable to incorporate into your stir fry dishes. Its crisp texture and mild flavor make it a versatile ingredient that pairs well with various protein options and sauces. Whether you prefer a simple garlic stir fry, a hearty mushroom and bok choy combination, or a spicy shrimp stir fry, these recipes are guaranteed to satisfy your cravings. So, grab your wok, gather your ingredients, and get ready to experience the deliciousness of bok choy in these easy stir fry recipes. Enjoy!
