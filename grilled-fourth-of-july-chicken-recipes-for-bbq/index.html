Grilled Fourth of July Chicken Recipes for BBQ


The Fourth of July is just around the corner, and what better way to celebrate than with a delicious BBQ? Grilled chicken is a classic choice that never fails to impress. Whether you’re hosting a backyard party or simply enjoying the holiday with your loved ones, these grilled Fourth of July chicken recipes will surely satisfy your taste buds. Fire up the grill and get ready to indulge in some mouthwatering dishes!

1. Tangy BBQ Chicken Skewers

Ingredients:

  • 2 pounds of chicken breast, cut into cubes
  • 1/2 cup of BBQ sauce
  • 1/4 cup of honey
  • 2 tablespoons of soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon of Dijon mustard
  • 1 teaspoon of garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon of paprika
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a large bowl, combine BBQ sauce, honey, soy sauce, Dijon mustard, garlic powder, paprika, salt, and pepper. Mix well to create the marinade.
  2. Add the chicken cubes to the marinade and toss until they are well-coated. Allow the chicken to marinate for at least 30 minutes, or overnight for maximum flavor.
  3. Preheat the grill to medium-high heat.
  4. Thread the chicken cubes onto skewers, leaving a small space between each piece.
  5. Grill the skewers for about 10-12 minutes, turning occasionally, until the chicken is cooked through and slightly charred.
  6. Serve hot with additional BBQ sauce for dipping.

2. Spicy Sriracha Chicken Drumsticks

Ingredients:

  • 8 chicken drumsticks
  • 1/4 cup of Sriracha sauce
  • 2 tablespoons of soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons of honey
  • 1 tablespoon of lime juice
  • 1 teaspoon of garlic powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon of smoked paprika
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, combine Sriracha sauce, soy sauce, honey, lime juice, garlic powder, smoked paprika, salt, and pepper. Mix well to create the marinade.
  2. Place the chicken drumsticks in a large resealable bag and pour the marinade over them. Seal the bag and massage the marinade into the chicken to ensure even coating. Let it marinate in the refrigerator for at least 1 hour, or overnight.
  3. Preheat the grill to medium heat.
  4. Remove the drumsticks from the marinade, allowing any excess to drip off. Reserve the marinade for basting.
  5. Grill the drumsticks for about 20-25 minutes, turning occasionally, until the chicken is cooked through and the skin is crispy.
  6. Baste the drumsticks with the reserved marinade during the last 5 minutes of grilling.
  7. Remove from the grill and let them rest for a few minutes before serving.

3. Honey Mustard Grilled Chicken Breast

Ingredients:

  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1/4 cup of Dijon mustard
  • 2 tablespoons of honey
  • 1 tablespoon of apple cider vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon of olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon of garlic powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a small bowl, whisk together Dijon mustard, honey, apple cider vinegar, olive oil, garlic powder, salt, and pepper to create the marinade.
  2. Place the chicken breasts in a shallow dish and pour the marinade over them. Coat the chicken evenly and let it marinate for at least 15 minutes, or up to 4 hours in the refrigerator.
  3. Preheat the grill to medium-high heat.
  4. Grill the chicken breasts for about 6-8 minutes per side, or until they reach an internal temperature of 165°F (74°C).
  5. Remove from the grill and let them rest for a few minutes before slicing.
  6. Serve with a side of grilled vegetables or a fresh salad for a complete meal.

Conclusion

This Fourth of July, elevate your BBQ game with these irresistible grilled chicken recipes. The tangy BBQ chicken skewers, spicy Sriracha chicken drumsticks, and honey mustard grilled chicken breast are sure to please everyone’s taste buds. Fire up the grill, gather your friends and family, and enjoy a memorable Independence Day celebration with these crowd-pleasing dishes. Happy grilling and happy Fourth of July!
