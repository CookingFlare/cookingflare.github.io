Cooking for Two: 7 Delicious and Easy Soups


When it comes to cozy, heartwarming meals, soup is a timeless choice. Whether you’re preparing a quick dinner for two or planning an intimate lunch with your partner, soups are versatile, nutritious, and easy to make. Here’s a collection of seven mouthwatering soups tailored for smaller portions. Each recipe is simple to follow and requires ingredients that are likely already in your pantry or refrigerator.

These soups are perfect for chilly nights, busy weekdays, or moments when you just want something comforting and satisfying. Let’s dive into these delicious, soul-soothing recipes!

1. Classic Tomato Basil Soup

Few things beat the simplicity of a creamy tomato basil soup. This version retains all the nostalgic flavors you love while being delightfully easy to prepare.

Ingredients:

  • 2 large ripe tomatoes (or 1 cup canned diced tomatoes)
  • 1 small onion, chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 cup vegetable or chicken broth
  • 1 tsp olive oil
  • 2 tbsp fresh basil leaves (or 1 tsp dried basil)
  • ¼ cup heavy cream (optional for a creamier texture)
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Heat olive oil in a medium pot over medium heat. Sauté onions and garlic until they become soft and fragrant.
  2. Add the chopped tomatoes and cook for 5 minutes. If using canned tomatoes, just stir them in gently.
  3. Pour in the broth and bring the mixture to a gentle simmer. Let it cook for 10 minutes.
  4. Add basil, salt, and pepper. Use an immersion blender (or transfer to a countertop blender) to puree until smooth.
  5. Stir in the heavy cream if you prefer a creamy finish.
  6. Serve hot with crusty bread or a grilled cheese sandwich on the side.

2. Loaded Potato Soup

This rich and decadent soup is perfect for anyone who craves comfort food. It’s creamy, hearty, and packed with flavor.

Ingredients:

  • 2 large russet potatoes, peeled and diced
  • ½ cup chopped onions
  • 2 strips of bacon, cooked and crumbled
  • 2½ cups chicken broth
  • ½ cup milk or cream
  • ½ cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1 tbsp butter
  • Green onions for garnish
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a pot, melt butter over medium heat. Add the onions and sauté until soft.
  2. Add the diced potatoes and chicken broth. Bring the mixture to a gentle boil and cook until the potatoes are tender (about 15 minutes).
  3. Mash some of the potatoes directly in the pot to thicken the soup, leaving a few chunks for texture.
  4. Reduce heat to low and stir in the milk, cheese, and crumbled bacon. Continue stirring until the cheese fully melts.
  5. Taste and adjust seasoning with salt and pepper.
  6. Garnish with green onions and an extra sprinkle of cheese for a comforting touch.

3. Chicken Tortilla Soup

Bursting with bold flavors, this Mexican-inspired soup delivers a zesty punch while remaining light and refreshing.

Ingredients:

  • 1 chicken breast, cooked and shredded
  • 1 small onion, diced
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 2 cups chicken broth
  • 1 cup diced tomatoes
  • 1 small jalapeño, seeded and minced (optional for heat)
  • 1 tsp cumin
  • 1 tsp chili powder
  • ½ cup black beans or corn kernels
  • Tortilla chips and fresh cilantro for garnish
  • Lime wedges

Instructions:

  1. In a pot, sauté the onion and garlic over medium heat until soft. Add the jalapeño for an extra kick if desired.
  2. Stir in the diced tomatoes, chicken broth, cumin, and chili powder. Bring to a simmer.
  3. Add the shredded chicken and black beans (or corn) and cook for another 10 minutes.
  4. Taste and adjust seasoning to your preference.
  5. Serve with crushed tortilla chips, fresh cilantro, and lime wedges for a vibrant finish.

4. Creamy Mushroom Soup

This luxurious mushroom soup is earthy, creamy, and brimming with deep umami flavors.

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups sliced mushrooms (button or cremini work great)
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 1 small shallot, minced
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1½ cups chicken or vegetable broth
  • ½ cup heavy cream
  • 1 tsp thyme (fresh or dried)
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Melt butter in a pot over medium heat. Add the shallots and garlic, cooking until soft.
  2. Stir in the mushrooms and thyme. Cook until the mushrooms release their juices, about 5-7 minutes.
  3. Add the broth and bring to a gentle boil. Reduce heat and simmer for 10 minutes.
  4. Blend the soup until smooth using an immersion blender. Alternatively, leave it chunky for texture.
  5. Stir in the heavy cream and season with salt and pepper. Serve hot.

5. Lentil Vegetable Soup

Packed with nutrients, this hearty soup is both healthy and satisfying. It’s also completely vegan!

Ingredients:

  • ½ cup dried lentils, rinsed
  • 1 small carrot, diced
  • 1 celery stalk, diced
  • 1 small onion, chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 2 cups vegetable broth
  • 1 cup water
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • 1 tsp cumin
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Lemon wedges for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Heat some olive oil in a pot. Add the onion, carrot, celery, and garlic, sautéing until softened.
  2. Stir in the lentils, broth, water, paprika, and cumin. Bring the mixture to a boil, then reduce heat and let it simmer for 20-25 minutes or until the lentils are tender.
  3. Adjust seasoning with salt and pepper.
  4. Serve with a squeeze of lemon to brighten the flavors.

6. Butternut Squash Soup

Silky, sweet, and nourishing, this soup is a fall favorite but can be enjoyed year-round.

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups diced butternut squash
  • 1 small onion, chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 tsp olive oil
  • 2 cups vegetable or chicken broth
  • ½ cup coconut milk or cream
  • 1 tsp cinnamon or nutmeg (optional)
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Heat olive oil in a pot. Sauté the onion and garlic until fragrant.
  2. Add the diced butternut squash and cook for 5 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  3. Pour in the broth and bring to a simmer. Cover and cook until the squash is soft (about 15 minutes).
  4. Blend the soup until smooth. Stir in the coconut milk or cream, adjusting seasoning with salt, pepper, and cinnamon/nutmeg if using.
  5. Serve hot with crusty bread or a sprinkle of toasted pumpkin seeds.

7. Broccoli Cheddar Soup

This classic soup is creamy, cheesy, and loaded with tender broccoli. It’s the ultimate comfort food.

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups fresh broccoli florets
  • ½ cup chopped carrots (optional)
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 1 small onion, chopped
  • 1 garlic clove, minced
  • 2 cups chicken or vegetable broth
  • ½ cup heavy cream
  • 1 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • Salt, pepper, and nutmeg to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a pot, melt butter over medium heat. Add the onions and garlic, sautéing until soft.
  2. Stir in the broccoli and carrots. Add the broth and bring to a simmer. Cook for 10-12 minutes until the vegetables are tender.
  3. Blend the soup to your desired consistency (smooth or chunky).
  4. Stir in the cream and cheddar cheese. Cook until the cheese melts completely. Season with salt, pepper, and a pinch of nutmeg.
  5. Serve warm and enjoy every cheesy spoonful.

Wrap-Up

Cooking for two doesn’t have to feel daunting or redundant! These soups are designed to strike the perfect balance of convenience and flavor, ensuring every meal is special and satisfying. Whether you’re craving something rich and indulgent like broccoli cheddar or something light and fresh like chicken tortilla soup, there’s a recipe here for every mood and occasion. Grab your favorite ladle, fire up the stove, and get ready to create something warm, delicious, and memorable!
