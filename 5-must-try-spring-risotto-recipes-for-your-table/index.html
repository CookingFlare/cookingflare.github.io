5 Must-Try Spring Risotto Recipes for Your Table


As the chill of winter fades and spring bursts forth with vibrant colors and fresh flavors, it’s the perfect time to embrace the season’s bounty in our cooking. One of the most delightful ways to do this is through the creamy, comforting dish of risotto. With its versatility and ability to showcase seasonal ingredients, risotto is an excellent choice for any spring gathering. Here are five must-try spring risotto recipes that will elevate your dining experience and impress your guests.

1. Lemon Asparagus Risotto

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup Arborio rice
  • 1 bunch fresh asparagus, trimmed and cut into 2-inch pieces
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 4 cups vegetable broth
  • 1 cup dry white wine
  • 1 lemon (zested and juiced)
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh herbs for garnish (such as parsley or basil)

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the Broth: In a saucepan, bring the vegetable broth to a gentle simmer. Keep it warm over low heat.
  2. Sauté the Aromatics: In a large skillet, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the chopped onion and garlic, sautéing until the onion becomes translucent.
  3. Cook the Asparagus: Add the asparagus pieces to the skillet and cook for about 3-4 minutes until they are bright green and tender-crisp. Remove half of the asparagus and set aside for garnishing.
  4. Toast the Rice: Stir in the Arborio rice and toast it for 1-2 minutes, allowing it to absorb the flavors.
  5. Deglaze with Wine: Pour in the white wine, stirring until it’s mostly absorbed by the rice.
  6. Add the Broth Gradually: Begin adding the warm broth, one ladle at a time, stirring frequently. Allow the rice to absorb the liquid before adding more. This process should take about 20 minutes until the rice is creamy and al dente.
  7. Finish with Lemon and Cheese: Once the risotto is cooked, stir in the lemon zest, lemon juice, and grated Parmesan cheese. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  8. Serve: Top with the reserved asparagus and garnish with fresh herbs. Enjoy this bright and zesty dish that captures the essence of spring!

2. Pea and Mint Risotto

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup Arborio rice
  • 2 cups fresh or frozen peas
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 4 cups vegetable broth
  • 1 cup dry white wine
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1/4 cup fresh mint leaves, chopped
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Lemon wedges for serving

Instructions:

  1. Heat the Broth: Warm the vegetable broth in a saucepan and keep it at a low simmer.
  2. Sauté the Base: In a large pot, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the onion and garlic, cooking until softened.
  3. Add the Rice: Stir in the Arborio rice, allowing it to toast for 1-2 minutes.
  4. Deglaze with Wine: Add the white wine, stirring until it’s mostly absorbed.
  5. Incorporate the Broth: Gradually add the warm vegetable broth, one ladle at a time, stirring frequently until the rice is creamy and al dente.
  6. Add Peas: When there are about 5 minutes left in the cooking time, stir in the peas, allowing them to heat through.
  7. Finish with Mint and Cheese: Once the risotto is creamy, stir in the chopped mint leaves and grated Parmesan cheese. Season with salt and pepper.
  8. Serve: Dish out the risotto and serve with lemon wedges for an extra burst of freshness. This risotto is a celebration of spring with its vibrant green color and refreshing flavors!

3. Mushroom and Spinach Risotto

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup Arborio rice
  • 8 oz mushrooms (such as cremini or shiitake), sliced
  • 2 cups fresh spinach
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 4 cups vegetable broth
  • 1 cup dry white wine
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh thyme for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Warm the Broth: Heat the vegetable broth in a saucepan, keeping it warm on low.
  2. Sauté the Mushrooms: In a large skillet, heat olive oil over medium heat. Add the sliced mushrooms and cook until they are browned and tender.
  3. Add Aromatics: Incorporate the chopped onion and garlic, cooking until the onion is translucent.
  4. Toast the Rice: Stir in the Arborio rice and toast for 1-2 minutes.
  5. Deglaze with Wine: Pour in the white wine, stirring until absorbed.
  6. Add Broth Gradually: Begin adding the warm broth, one ladle at a time, stirring until absorbed before adding more.
  7. Stir in Spinach: When the risotto is nearly done, add the fresh spinach and stir until wilted.
  8. Finish with Cheese: Remove from heat, stir in the grated Parmesan cheese, and season with salt and pepper.
  9. Serve: Garnish with fresh thyme and enjoy this earthy, hearty risotto that brings the flavors of spring to your table!

4. Radish and Fennel Risotto

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup Arborio rice
  • 1 bunch radishes, trimmed and sliced
  • 1 bulb fennel, diced
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 4 cups vegetable broth
  • 1 cup dry white wine
  • 1/2 cup grated Pecorino cheese
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh dill for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the Broth: In a saucepan, bring the vegetable broth to a gentle simmer.
  2. Sauté the Fennel: Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Add the chopped onion and fennel, cooking until softened.
  3. Add Garlic and Radishes: Stir in the garlic and sliced radishes, cooking for an additional 3-4 minutes.
  4. Toast the Rice: Add the Arborio rice, stirring for 1-2 minutes until it’s lightly toasted.
  5. Deglaze with Wine: Pour in the white wine, stirring until mostly absorbed.
  6. Add Broth Gradually: Incorporate the warm broth, one ladle at a time, stirring frequently.
  7. Finish with Cheese: Once the risotto is creamy and the rice is al dente, remove from heat and stir in the grated Pecorino cheese. Season with salt and pepper.
  8. Serve: Garnish with fresh dill and enjoy this unique risotto that showcases the peppery notes of radishes and the subtle sweetness of fennel.

5. Sweet Pea and Lemon Risotto with Goat Cheese

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup Arborio rice
  • 2 cups sweet peas (fresh or frozen)
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 4 cups vegetable broth
  • 1 cup dry white wine
  • 4 oz goat cheese, crumbled
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 lemon (zested and juiced)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh basil for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Warm the Broth: Heat the vegetable broth in a saucepan, keeping it at a low simmer.
  2. Sauté the Base: In a large skillet, heat olive oil over medium heat. Add the onion and garlic, cooking until softened.
  3. Add the Rice: Stir in the Arborio rice, toasting for 1-2 minutes.
  4. Deglaze with Wine: Pour in the white wine, stirring until mostly absorbed.
  5. Add Broth Gradually: Gradually add the warm broth, one ladle at a time, stirring frequently until the rice is creamy.
  6. Incorporate Peas and Cheese: When there are about 5 minutes remaining, stir in the sweet peas and crumbled goat cheese, allowing them to heat through.
  7. Finish with Lemon: Remove from heat and stir in the lemon zest and juice. Season with salt and pepper.
  8. Serve: Garnish with fresh basil leaves for a beautiful presentation. This risotto is a delightful combination of sweet and tangy flavors, perfect for celebrating spring!

Conclusion

Spring is a time of renewal, and what better way to celebrate than with these five delicious risotto recipes? Each dish showcases seasonal ingredients, providing a burst of flavor in every bite. Whether you’re hosting a dinner party or simply enjoying a family meal, these risottos will surely impress. So gather your ingredients, roll up your sleeves, and enjoy the art of risotto-making this spring!
