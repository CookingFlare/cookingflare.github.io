Instant Pot Chicken Enchiladas: Spicy and Cheesy


Are you craving a delicious and flavorful Mexican dish that can be prepared quickly and easily? Look no further! In this recipe, we will show you how to make mouthwatering Instant Pot Chicken Enchiladas that are both spicy and cheesy. With the help of your trusty Instant Pot, you’ll have a satisfying meal on the table in no time. So, let’s get started!

Ingredients

  • 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 small onion, diced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 jalapeno pepper, seeded and minced
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon chili powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1 cup chicken broth
  • 1 can (10 ounces) red enchilada sauce
  • 1 cup shredded Mexican cheese blend
  • 8 flour tortillas
  • Optional toppings: sour cream, chopped cilantro, sliced jalapenos

Instructions

  1. Turn on your Instant Pot and select the “Saute” function. Heat the olive oil and saute the diced onion, minced garlic, and jalapeno pepper until they are soft and fragrant.

  2. In a small bowl, combine the ground cumin, chili powder, paprika, dried oregano, salt, and black pepper. Mix well to create a spice blend.

  3. Season the chicken breasts with half of the spice blend, rubbing it evenly on both sides.

  4. Add the seasoned chicken breasts to the Instant Pot and sear them on each side until browned, about 2-3 minutes per side.

  5. Once the chicken is browned, pour in the chicken broth and the red enchilada sauce. Stir well to combine all the flavors.

  6. Secure the lid on the Instant Pot and set the valve to the sealing position. Cook on “Manual” or “Pressure Cook” mode for 10 minutes.

  7. While the chicken is cooking, preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C) and prepare a baking dish by greasing it lightly.

  8. Once the cooking time is complete, carefully release the pressure using the quick release method.

  9. Remove the chicken breasts from the Instant Pot and shred them using two forks.

  10. Return the shredded chicken to the Instant Pot and mix it with the sauce to coat evenly.

  11. Take a flour tortilla and spoon a generous amount of the chicken mixture onto one end. Roll it tightly and place it seam-side down in the prepared baking dish. Repeat this step with the remaining tortillas and chicken mixture.

  12. Sprinkle the shredded Mexican cheese blend evenly over the rolled tortillas.

  13. Place the baking dish in the preheated oven and bake for 15-20 minutes, or until the cheese is melted and bubbly.

  14. Once cooked, remove the dish from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes.

  15. Serve the Instant Pot Chicken Enchiladas hot, garnished with optional toppings such as sour cream, chopped cilantro, and sliced jalapenos.

Conclusion

These Instant Pot Chicken Enchiladas are the perfect combination of spicy and cheesy, making them an irresistible Mexican dish that the whole family will love. With the help of your Instant Pot, you can have a flavorful meal on the table in no time. So, whip out your Instant Pot and give this recipe a try. Your taste buds will thank you!

Note: This recipe yields approximately 4 servings.
