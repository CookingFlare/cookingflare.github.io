Sesame Soy Beef Stir Fry: A Classic Asian Dish Bursting with Flavor


If you’re craving a delicious and satisfying meal that’s packed with flavor, look no further than Sesame Soy Beef Stir Fry. This classic Asian dish is a favorite among many, and for good reason. With its tender strips of beef, vibrant vegetables, and mouthwatering sauce, it’s a dish that never fails to impress.

Ingredients

To create this delectable stir fry, you’ll need the following ingredients:

  • 1 pound of beef, thinly sliced
  • 2 tablespoons of soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons of sesame oil
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon of ginger, grated
  • 1 red bell pepper, thinly sliced
  • 1 green bell pepper, thinly sliced
  • 1 medium-sized onion, thinly sliced
  • 1 cup of broccoli florets
  • 1 tablespoon of cornstarch
  • 1 tablespoon of water
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Cooked rice, for serving

Instructions

  1. In a bowl, combine the sliced beef with soy sauce, sesame oil, minced garlic, and grated ginger. Mix well to ensure that the beef is evenly coated. Let it marinate for at least 30 minutes to allow the flavors to meld together.

  2. Heat a large skillet or wok over high heat. Add a drizzle of sesame oil and swirl it around to coat the pan. Once the oil is hot, add the marinated beef and stir-fry for about 3-4 minutes until it’s browned and cooked through. Remove the beef from the pan and set it aside.

  3. In the same pan, add a bit more sesame oil if needed. Toss in the sliced bell peppers, onion, and broccoli florets. Stir-fry for about 2-3 minutes until the vegetables are slightly tender but still crisp.

  4. Meanwhile, in a small bowl, whisk together the cornstarch and water until smooth. This will act as a thickening agent for the sauce.

  5. Push the vegetables to one side of the pan and pour the cornstarch mixture into the cleared space. Stir it quickly to prevent any lumps from forming. Gradually add the beef back into the pan and toss everything together.

  6. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Continue cooking for an additional 1-2 minutes until the sauce has thickened and coats the beef and vegetables nicely.

  7. Serve the Sesame Soy Beef Stir Fry over a bed of fluffy, cooked rice. Garnish with a sprinkle of sesame seeds for an extra touch of flavor and visual appeal.

Tips and Variations

  • Feel free to customize this recipe by adding or substituting different vegetables. Snow peas, mushrooms, or carrots would make great additions.
  • If you prefer a spicier stir fry, you can add some red pepper flakes or a drizzle of sriracha sauce to the dish.
  • For an even richer flavor, you can marinate the beef for a longer period of time. Overnight marination will infuse the meat with even more deliciousness.

Now that you have the recipe for Sesame Soy Beef Stir Fry, it’s time to gather your ingredients and get cooking. This dish is perfect for a quick weeknight meal or for impressing guests with your culinary skills. Enjoy the explosion of flavors and savor every bite of this classic Asian favorite!
