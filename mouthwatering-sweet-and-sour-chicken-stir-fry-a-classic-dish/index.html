Mouthwatering Sweet and Sour Chicken Stir Fry: A Classic Dish


Are you tired of the same old chicken recipes? Looking for a dish that will tantalize your taste buds and leave you craving for more? Look no further, because we have the perfect recipe for you - Mouthwatering Sweet and Sour Chicken Stir Fry!

This classic dish is a crowd-pleaser, combining the perfect balance of sweet and tangy flavors with tender chicken and crisp vegetables. It’s easy to make, and the best part is, you can customize it to suit your taste preferences. So let’s dive right in and get cooking!

Ingredients

For the chicken marinade:

  • 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon cornstarch
  • 1 teaspoon sesame oil
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper

For the stir fry sauce:

  • 1/4 cup ketchup
  • 1/4 cup rice vinegar
  • 2 tablespoons honey
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon cornstarch
  • 1/2 cup chicken broth

For the stir fry:

  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1 red bell pepper, seeded and sliced
  • 1 green bell pepper, seeded and sliced
  • 1 onion, sliced
  • 1 cup pineapple chunks
  • 1/2 cup chopped scallions (optional)

Instructions

  1. In a medium bowl, combine the soy sauce, cornstarch, sesame oil, and black pepper for the chicken marinade. Add the chicken pieces and toss to coat. Set aside for 15 minutes to marinate.

  2. In another bowl, whisk together the ketchup, rice vinegar, honey, soy sauce, cornstarch, and chicken broth to make the stir fry sauce. Set aside.

  3. Heat one tablespoon of vegetable oil in a large skillet or wok over medium-high heat. Add the marinated chicken and stir fry until cooked through and browned, about 5-6 minutes. Remove the chicken from the skillet and set aside.

  4. In the same skillet, heat another tablespoon of vegetable oil. Add the sliced bell peppers and onion and stir fry for 2-3 minutes, until they begin to soften.

  5. Add the pineapple chunks and stir fry for another 2 minutes.

  6. Return the cooked chicken to the skillet and pour in the stir fry sauce. Stir everything together and let the sauce thicken for 2-3 minutes.

  7. If desired, sprinkle chopped scallions over the stir fry for added freshness and flavor.

  8. Serve the mouthwatering sweet and sour chicken stir fry over steamed rice or noodles, and enjoy!

Tips and Variations

  • For a vegetarian version, substitute the chicken with tofu or your favorite vegetables like broccoli, carrots, and snap peas.
  • Add some heat to your stir fry by including sliced chili peppers or a dash of hot sauce.
  • For a healthier option, you can bake the chicken instead of stir frying it. Simply coat the marinated chicken with a little oil and bake at 400°F (200°C) for 15-20 minutes or until cooked through.
  • Experiment with different vegetables and fruits to add your own twist to the dish. Mushrooms, baby corn, and mandarin oranges are great options.

So there you have it - a classic sweet and sour chicken stir fry recipe that will have your taste buds dancing with joy! Whether you’re cooking for a family dinner or hosting a dinner party, this dish is sure to impress. Give it a try and let us know how it turns out. Happy cooking!
