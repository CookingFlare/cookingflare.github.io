Baked Chicken and Vegetables: A Healthy Comfort Meal


If you’re looking for a delicious and wholesome meal that’s both comforting and nutritious, look no further than baked chicken and vegetables. This classic dish is not only easy to prepare but also packs a punch of flavor and nutrients. In this recipe, we’ll explore how to create a delightful baked chicken and vegetable dish that will satisfy your cravings for comfort food while keeping your health in check.

Ingredients

For the Chicken:

  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste

For the Vegetables:

  • 2 cups broccoli florets
  • 2 cups sliced carrots
  • 1 red bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 yellow bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 red onion, cut into wedges
  • 2 tablespoons balsamic vinegar
  • 2 tablespoons honey
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

Step 1: Preparing the Chicken

  1. Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C) and lightly grease a baking dish.
  2. In a small bowl, mix together the olive oil, minced garlic, paprika, dried oregano, salt, and pepper.
  3. Place the chicken breasts in the baking dish and brush the olive oil mixture over them, ensuring they are evenly coated.
  4. Bake the chicken in the preheated oven for 25-30 minutes or until the internal temperature reaches 165°F (74°C).

Step 2: Roasting the Vegetables

  1. While the chicken is baking, prepare the vegetables. In a large bowl, combine the broccoli, carrots, bell peppers, and red onion.
  2. In a separate bowl, whisk together the balsamic vinegar, honey, dried thyme, salt, and pepper.
  3. Pour the balsamic mixture over the vegetables and toss until they are well coated.
  4. Spread the vegetables onto a baking sheet and roast in the oven for 20-25 minutes or until they are tender and slightly caramelized.

Step 3: Serving

  1. Once the chicken and vegetables are cooked, remove them from the oven.
  2. Serve the baked chicken alongside the roasted vegetables for a delightful and wholesome meal.

Why Baked Chicken and Vegetables?

Baked chicken and vegetables make for the perfect healthy comfort meal for several reasons. First and foremost, this dish is incredibly versatile. You can customize it with your favorite herbs, spices, and vegetables to suit your taste preferences. Additionally, it’s a great way to incorporate a variety of colorful and nutrient-rich vegetables into your diet, providing you with essential vitamins and minerals.

Moreover, this dish is a fantastic option for meal prepping. You can easily prepare a large batch of baked chicken and vegetables to enjoy throughout the week, making it a convenient choice for those with busy schedules. It’s also a budget-friendly meal that doesn’t compromise on flavor or nutrition.

Lastly, baked chicken and vegetables are a wholesome and satisfying choice for anyone looking to maintain a balanced and healthy diet. The lean protein from the chicken and the fiber-rich vegetables make this meal a satiating option that won’t leave you feeling weighed down.

In conclusion, baked chicken and vegetables are a winning combination when it comes to creating a healthy comfort meal. Whether you’re cooking for yourself, your family, or guests, this recipe is sure to impress with its simplicity and deliciousness. So, next time you’re craving a comforting yet nutritious meal, give this recipe a try and savor the delightful flavors of baked chicken and vegetables.
