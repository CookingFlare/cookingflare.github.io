Kung Pao Tofu Stir Fry: A Vegan Version of a Chinese Takeout Favorite


If you’re a fan of Chinese takeout but follow a vegan or plant-based diet, you don’t have to miss out on the delicious flavors of Kung Pao Stir Fry. This vegan version swaps out the traditional chicken for tofu, creating a dish that is equally satisfying and packed with flavor. With a combination of savory, spicy, and tangy flavors, this Kung Pao Tofu Stir Fry is sure to become a new favorite in your recipe collection.

Ingredients

To make this vegan Kung Pao Tofu Stir Fry, you will need:

  • 14 ounces of firm tofu, drained and cubed
  • 2 tablespoons of soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons of cornstarch
  • 2 tablespoons of vegetable oil
  • 1 red bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 green bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 cup of roasted peanuts
  • 4 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1-inch piece of fresh ginger, grated
  • 4 green onions, sliced
  • 1/2 cup of vegetable broth
  • 2 tablespoons of rice vinegar
  • 2 tablespoons of hoisin sauce
  • 1 tablespoon of maple syrup
  • 1 tablespoon of sriracha sauce (adjust to taste)
  • 1 teaspoon of sesame oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. In a large bowl, combine the tofu, soy sauce, and cornstarch. Toss until the tofu is evenly coated. Set aside for 10 minutes to allow the tofu to marinate.

  2. Heat 1 tablespoon of vegetable oil in a large skillet or wok over medium-high heat. Add the marinated tofu and cook until golden brown and crispy on all sides. Remove the tofu from the skillet and set aside.

  3. In the same skillet, add the remaining tablespoon of vegetable oil. Add the sliced bell peppers and cook for 3-4 minutes, until slightly softened.

  4. Add the minced garlic, grated ginger, and sliced green onions to the skillet. Cook for an additional 1-2 minutes, until fragrant.

  5. In a small bowl, whisk together the vegetable broth, rice vinegar, hoisin sauce, maple syrup, sriracha sauce, and sesame oil. Pour the sauce mixture into the skillet and stir to combine with the vegetables.

  6. Return the crispy tofu to the skillet and toss to coat it with the sauce. Cook for an additional 2-3 minutes, until the tofu is heated through.

  7. Finally, add the roasted peanuts to the skillet and give everything a good stir. Season with salt and pepper to taste.

  8. Serve the Kung Pao Tofu Stir Fry hot over steamed rice or noodles. Garnish with additional sliced green onions, if desired.

Enjoy the Vegan Twist on a Classic Dish

This vegan version of Kung Pao Stir Fry proves that you don’t need meat to enjoy a flavorful and satisfying Chinese takeout favorite. The crispy tofu, combined with the vibrant vegetables and the bold sauce, creates a dish that is both delicious and nutritious. The roasted peanuts add a satisfying crunch, making every bite a delight.

Make this Kung Pao Tofu Stir Fry for your next weeknight dinner or when you’re craving a taste of Chinese takeout. It’s easy to prepare, packed with plant-based protein, and bursting with flavors that will leave you wanting more. Enjoy the vegan twist on this classic dish and savor every bite!

Remember to experiment with the recipe and make it your own. Feel free to add extra vegetables or adjust the spice level to suit your taste. Happy cooking!
