A Delicious Italian-Style Eggplant Parmesan Recipe


If you’re looking for a mouthwatering Italian dish that will leave you craving for more, then you’ve come to the right place. Today, I’m going to share with you a recipe for a delicious Italian-style Eggplant Parmesan. This classic dish is a favorite among many, and once you taste it, you’ll understand why.

Ingredients

To make this delectable Eggplant Parmesan, you’ll need the following ingredients:

  • 2 large eggplants
  • 2 cups of breadcrumbs
  • 1 cup of grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 cups of marinara sauce
  • 2 cups of shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/4 cup of fresh basil leaves
  • Olive oil for frying
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Start by preheating your oven to 375°F (190°C). This will ensure that your Eggplant Parmesan bakes to perfection.
  2. Next, slice the eggplants into 1/2-inch thick rounds. Sprinkle them with salt and let them sit for about 15 minutes. This will help draw out any excess moisture from the eggplant slices.
  3. While the eggplant is sitting, prepare your breadcrumb mixture. In a shallow dish, combine the breadcrumbs and grated Parmesan cheese. Mix well to ensure even distribution.
  4. In a separate bowl, whisk the eggs until well beaten. This will act as the binding agent for the breadcrumb mixture.
  5. Rinse the eggplant slices under cold water to remove the excess salt. Pat them dry with a paper towel.
  6. Dip each eggplant slice into the beaten eggs, making sure to coat both sides evenly. Then, press the eggplant slice into the breadcrumb mixture, ensuring that it is fully coated. Repeat this process for all the eggplant slices.
  7. Heat a generous amount of olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Fry the breaded eggplant slices until they are golden brown on both sides, about 2-3 minutes per side. Be sure not to overcrowd the pan; fry them in batches if necessary.
  8. Once the eggplant slices are fried, transfer them to a paper towel-lined plate to remove any excess oil.
  9. Now it’s time to assemble the Eggplant Parmesan. In a baking dish, spread a thin layer of marinara sauce on the bottom. Place a single layer of fried eggplant slices on top. Sprinkle some shredded mozzarella cheese and torn basil leaves over the eggplant. Repeat this process until all the ingredients are used, finishing with a layer of sauce and cheese on top.
  10. Cover the baking dish with foil and bake in the preheated oven for 25 minutes. After this time, remove the foil and continue baking for an additional 10 minutes, or until the cheese is melted and bubbly.
  11. Once the Eggplant Parmesan is done baking, remove it from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes before serving. This will allow the flavors to meld together beautifully.
  12. Serve the Eggplant Parmesan hot, garnished with fresh basil leaves. It pairs wonderfully with a side of pasta or a crisp green salad.

Conclusion

There you have it – a delicious Italian-style Eggplant Parmesan recipe that is sure to impress your family and friends. The crispy breaded eggplant, tangy marinara sauce, and gooey cheese come together to create a dish that is both comforting and satisfying. Whether you’re a vegetarian or simply looking to add more variety to your dinner rotation, this Eggplant Parmesan is a must-try. So, head to your kitchen, gather the ingredients, and get ready to indulge in this mouthwatering Italian delight. Buon appetito!
