BBQ Pork Sliders: Mini Sandwiches for Meat Lovers


If you’re a meat lover and a fan of BBQ, then you’re in for a treat! BBQ pork sliders are the perfect combination of smoky, tender pork and delicious, tangy BBQ sauce, all packed into a mini sandwich. These sliders are great for parties, game day snacks, or simply for a satisfying meal. In this recipe blog post, we’ll take you through the process of creating mouthwatering BBQ pork sliders that will leave your taste buds begging for more.

Ingredients

For the Pork:

  • 3 lbs pork shoulder
  • 1 tablespoon paprika
  • 1 tablespoon garlic powder
  • 1 tablespoon onion powder
  • 1 tablespoon brown sugar
  • Salt and pepper to taste

For the BBQ Sauce:

  • 1 cup ketchup
  • 1/2 cup apple cider vinegar
  • 1/4 cup brown sugar
  • 2 tablespoons molasses
  • 1 tablespoon Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 teaspoon smoked paprika
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste

For the Sliders:

  • 12 slider buns
  • Sliced pickles
  • Coleslaw (optional)

Instructions

  1. Prep the Pork:

    • In a small bowl, mix together the paprika, garlic powder, onion powder, brown sugar, salt, and pepper. Rub this mixture all over the pork shoulder, ensuring it’s evenly coated. Let it marinate for at least 30 minutes, or preferably overnight for maximum flavor.

  2. Slow Cook the Pork:

    • Place the seasoned pork shoulder in a slow cooker and cook on low for 8-10 hours, or until the pork is tender and easily shreddable. Once done, remove the pork from the slow cooker and shred it using two forks.

  3. Make the BBQ Sauce:

    • In a saucepan, combine the ketchup, apple cider vinegar, brown sugar, molasses, Worcestershire sauce, smoked paprika, garlic powder, salt, and pepper. Bring the mixture to a simmer over medium heat, then reduce the heat and let it simmer for 15-20 minutes, stirring occasionally, until the sauce thickens.

  4. Combine the Pork and BBQ Sauce:

    • Pour the BBQ sauce over the shredded pork and mix until the pork is evenly coated with the sauce.

  5. Assemble the Sliders:

    • Slice the slider buns in half and lightly toast them. Place a generous portion of the BBQ pork on the bottom half of each bun. Top with sliced pickles and coleslaw if desired. Finally, place the top half of the bun over the filling.

  6. Serve and Enjoy:

    • Arrange the BBQ pork sliders on a platter and serve them hot. Watch as your guests indulge in the irresistible combination of smoky, saucy pork nestled in a soft, mini bun.

Tips and Variations

  • For an extra kick of heat, add a dash of hot sauce to the BBQ sauce mixture.
  • If you prefer a crispy texture, you can broil the assembled sliders in the oven for a few minutes until the buns are golden brown.
  • Feel free to customize the sliders with your favorite toppings, such as sliced jalapeños or a drizzle of ranch dressing.

Conclusion

BBQ pork sliders are a crowd-pleaser that never fails to impress. With the tender, flavorful pork and the zesty BBQ sauce, these mini sandwiches are a true delight for meat lovers. Whether you’re hosting a gathering or simply craving a hearty snack, these sliders are sure to hit the spot. So, fire up the grill, gather your ingredients, and get ready to savor the irresistible goodness of BBQ pork sliders!
