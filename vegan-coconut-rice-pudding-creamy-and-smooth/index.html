Vegan Coconut Rice Pudding: Creamy and Smooth


Few desserts are as universally comforting as a warm bowl of rice pudding. Whether enjoyed as the perfect ending to a hearty dinner or as an indulgent snack, rice pudding occupies a special place in the hearts of dessert lovers worldwide. Today, we’re elevating this traditional dish with an exciting vegan twist—introducing the rich, luscious, and dairy-free Vegan Coconut Rice Pudding. The creamy, silky texture of coconut milk combined with the delicate sweetness of maple syrup and the gentle nuttiness of rice transforms this simple dessert into an unforgettable treat.

Why Choose Vegan Coconut Rice Pudding?

A vegan rice pudding made with coconut milk offers many benefits. Not only does it cater to a growing number of individuals seeking dairy-free options, but its ingredients are also accessible, versatile, and healthy. Coconut milk provides healthy fats and a delightful flavor, while the natural sweetness of maple syrup ensures this dessert is free from refined sugar. Furthermore, this recipe is naturally gluten-free, making it suitable for a variety of dietary preferences.

Whether you’re hosting a dinner party or seeking a comforting dish to enjoy solo, this recipe checks all the boxes. It’s creamy, smooth, and packed with flavor, yet simple enough to whip up in under an hour.

The Ingredients You’ll Need

Creating the perfect vegan coconut rice pudding requires just a handful of staple ingredients, the majority of which you likely already have in your pantry. For this recipe, you’ll need:

  • Short-grain white rice: Short-grain rice, such as Arborio rice or sushi rice, works best for rice pudding thanks to its high starch content, which lends creaminess to the dish. Long-grain rice varieties, like Basmati or Jasmine rice, are less starchy and won’t yield the same silky texture.
  • Full-fat coconut milk: This is the star ingredient that gives your rice pudding its irresistibly smooth texture and rich flavor. Opt for canned coconut milk—it’s thicker and creamier than carton varieties.
  • Unsweetened almond milk (or other plant-based milk): Adding almond milk or another plant-based milk lightens the dish by balancing the richness of the coconut milk. You can use oat, soy, or cashew milk as an alternative.
  • Maple syrup: An excellent natural sweetener. You can substitute agave syrup or brown rice syrup if preferred.
  • Vanilla extract: A few drops of vanilla extract elevate the dish, providing that signature aroma and flavor.
  • Ground cinnamon: A warm and aromatic complement to the coconut and vanilla.
  • Pinch of salt: Enhances every flavor, balancing the sweetness and richness.
  • Optional toppings: Roasted coconut flakes, fresh fruit, nuts, or seeds add texture, color, and a delightful crunch.

Step-by-Step Instructions to the Perfect Vegan Coconut Rice Pudding

1. Prepare the Rice

Start by rinsing the rice thoroughly under cold water until the water runs clear. This step removes excess starch from the surface of the grains, preventing clumping during cooking. Once rinsed, combine the rice with 1 ½ cups of unsweetened almond milk in a medium saucepan. Bring the mixture to a gentle boil over medium heat.

2. Simmering to Perfection

Once the almond milk begins to boil, reduce the heat to low and allow the rice to simmer for about 15 minutes, stirring frequently to prevent sticking. The rice should become tender but not mushy, absorbing much of the almond milk during this phase.

3. Introduce the Coconut Milk

After the rice has softened, pour in the full-fat coconut milk and add a pinch of salt. Stir well to combine. This is where the magic happens—the coconut milk creates the creamy foundation that makes this pudding so indulgent.

4. Sweeten & Add a Spice Kick

Add maple syrup, vanilla extract, and ground cinnamon. Adjust the sweetness to your taste—start with about 1/4 cup of maple syrup and increase as desired. Stir frequently until the mixture thickens and achieves a pudding-like consistency, which typically takes around 10–12 minutes.

5. Cool, Serve, and Garnish

Once the pudding reaches your preferred texture, remove it from the heat and let it cool slightly. Vegan coconut rice pudding retains heat well, so allowing it to cool just a bit creates a perfectly warm dessert that is still creamy and smooth. To serve, spoon the pudding into bowls and add your favorite toppings. Toasted coconut flakes, fresh berries like strawberries or raspberries, or chopped nuts like almonds or pistachios make excellent additions. You can even drizzle on a little more maple syrup for extra sweetness.

Tips and Tricks for the Perfect Coconut Rice Pudding

  • Consistency is key: Adjust the thickness of your pudding by carefully monitoring the cooking time. If you prefer a thinner pudding, add a bit more almond milk toward the end of the cooking process. For a thicker consistency, simmer for a few extra minutes to allow the rice to absorb more liquid.
  • Prevent sticking: Stir frequently during cooking, especially once the coconut milk is added. The natural fats in coconut milk can easily stick to the bottom of your saucepan if left unattended.
  • Toppings elevate every dish: While the pudding is delicious on its own, toppings add texture and visual appeal. Get creative with combinations to match any occasion!

Storage and Reheating

If you plan on making a bigger batch, vegan coconut rice pudding stores beautifully. Place any leftovers in an airtight container and refrigerate for up to 4–5 days. When reheating, add a splash of almond milk or coconut milk to restore the creamy texture. Warm it gently on the stovetop or microwave.

Why You’ll Love This Recipe

This vegan coconut rice pudding is a delightful fusion of tradition and modern innovation. It’s comforting yet versatile, offering a gluten-free and plant-based option for dessert lovers. Its subtle sweetness makes it an excellent companion to coffee, tea, or a glass of chilled plant-based milk. Plus, the ability to customize the toppings and flavors ensures there’s a version for every palate.

Whether you’re new to vegan cooking or simply on the lookout for a dairy-free dessert that doesn’t compromise on flavor, this coconut rice pudding delivers in every way. From its rich texture to its heavenly aroma, every bite is a celebration.
