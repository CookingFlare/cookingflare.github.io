Savory Creamy Chicken and Bacon Pasta: Winter's Irresistible Comfort


Winter is the perfect time to indulge in warm and comforting dishes that satisfy both our taste buds and our souls. And what could be more comforting than a bowl of savory creamy chicken and bacon pasta? This delectable dish combines tender chicken, crispy bacon, and a rich, creamy sauce to create a meal that will leave you wanting more. So, let’s dive into the recipe and discover the secrets behind this winter’s irresistible comfort.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound of boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 8 ounces of bacon, diced
  • 1 medium onion, finely chopped
  • 3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 cup of chicken broth
  • 1 ½ cups of heavy cream
  • ½ cup of grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 teaspoon of dried thyme
  • 1 teaspoon of dried rosemary
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • 12 ounces of your favorite pasta

Instructions

  1. Start by cooking the pasta according to the package instructions. Make sure to cook it al dente, as it will continue to cook in the sauce later. Drain and set aside.

  2. In a large skillet, cook the diced bacon over medium heat until crispy. Remove the bacon from the skillet and set it aside on a plate lined with paper towels to absorb the excess grease.

  3. In the same skillet, add the chicken breasts and cook them until they are no longer pink in the center. Remove the chicken from the skillet and set it aside to rest.

  4. In the same skillet, add the chopped onion and minced garlic. Sauté them until they are soft and fragrant, about 3-4 minutes.

  5. Pour in the chicken broth and scrape the bottom of the skillet to deglaze it, picking up all the flavorful bits. Let the broth simmer for a couple of minutes to reduce slightly.

  6. Add the heavy cream, dried thyme, dried rosemary, salt, and pepper to the skillet. Stir well to combine all the ingredients and bring the sauce to a gentle simmer.

  7. While the sauce is simmering, shred the cooked chicken into bite-sized pieces using two forks.

  8. Add the shredded chicken and cooked bacon to the skillet. Stir well to coat them with the creamy sauce. Let the sauce simmer for another 5 minutes to allow the flavors to meld together.

  9. Finally, add the grated Parmesan cheese to the skillet and stir until the cheese is melted and the sauce is smooth and creamy.

  10. Add the cooked pasta to the skillet and toss it in the sauce until it is well coated. Cook for an additional 2-3 minutes to allow the pasta to absorb some of the sauce.

  11. Serve the savory creamy chicken and bacon pasta hot, garnished with a sprinkle of grated Parmesan cheese and a few fresh herbs if desired.

Conclusion

This savory creamy chicken and bacon pasta recipe is a true winter’s delight. The combination of tender chicken, crispy bacon, and a velvety cream sauce creates a dish that is both comforting and indulgent. Whether you’re looking for a quick weeknight dinner or a cozy meal to warm you up on a cold winter’s day, this recipe has got you covered. So, why not give it a try and experience the irresistible comfort of this delectable pasta dish? Your taste buds will thank you!
