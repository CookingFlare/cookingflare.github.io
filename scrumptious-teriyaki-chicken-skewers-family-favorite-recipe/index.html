Scrumptious Teriyaki Chicken Skewers: Family Favorite Recipe


Are you looking for a delicious and easy-to-make dish that the whole family will love? Look no further than these scrumptious teriyaki chicken skewers! This recipe is a guaranteed hit, combining the savory flavors of teriyaki sauce with tender, juicy chicken, all grilled to perfection. Whether you’re hosting a backyard barbecue, a casual family dinner, or just craving some Asian-inspired flavors, these teriyaki chicken skewers are sure to impress.

Ingredients

  • 1.5 lbs boneless, skinless chicken thighs
  • 1/2 cup soy sauce
  • 1/4 cup honey
  • 2 tbsp rice vinegar
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tsp grated fresh ginger
  • 1/4 tsp black pepper
  • Wooden or metal skewers

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Marinade: In a bowl, whisk together the soy sauce, honey, rice vinegar, minced garlic, grated ginger, and black pepper. This flavorful marinade is the key to infusing the chicken with the delicious teriyaki flavor.

  2. Marinate the Chicken: Cut the chicken thighs into bite-sized pieces and place them in a large resealable plastic bag or a shallow dish. Pour the marinade over the chicken, making sure each piece is well-coated. Seal the bag or cover the dish and let the chicken marinate in the refrigerator for at least 30 minutes, or up to 2 hours for maximum flavor.

  3. Preheat the Grill: While the chicken is marinating, preheat your grill to medium-high heat. If you’re using wooden skewers, be sure to soak them in water for at least 30 minutes to prevent them from burning on the grill.

  4. Skewer the Chicken: Once the chicken has marinated, thread the pieces onto the skewers, leaving a little space between each piece to ensure even cooking.

  5. Grill the Skewers: Place the skewers on the preheated grill and cook for 6-8 minutes on each side, or until the chicken is fully cooked and has beautiful grill marks. Baste the skewers with any remaining marinade while grilling to enhance the flavor and keep the chicken juicy.

  6. Serve and Enjoy: Once the chicken is cooked through, remove the skewers from the grill and let them rest for a few minutes before serving. Garnish with sesame seeds and chopped green onions for a pop of color and added flavor. These teriyaki chicken skewers are perfect on their own or served over a bed of steamed rice with a side of stir-fried vegetables.

Tips and Tricks

  • For a more intense flavor, you can double the marinade recipe and reserve half for basting during grilling or as a dipping sauce for the cooked skewers.
  • If you’re short on time, you can use store-bought teriyaki sauce, but nothing beats the freshness and flavor of homemade marinade.

Conclusion

These scrumptious teriyaki chicken skewers are a surefire way to bring smiles to the faces of your loved ones. The combination of sweet, salty, and savory flavors will have everyone coming back for seconds. Whether you’re firing up the grill for a summer gathering or simply want to add a new family favorite to your dinner rotation, these teriyaki chicken skewers are an absolute winner. So, roll up your sleeves, fire up the grill, and get ready to delight your taste buds with this irresistible recipe!
