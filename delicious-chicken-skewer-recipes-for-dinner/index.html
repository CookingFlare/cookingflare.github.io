Delicious Chicken Skewer Recipes for Dinner


Chicken skewers are a versatile and flavorful option for dinner, perfect for both weeknight meals and special gatherings. Whether you’re grilling outdoors or cooking indoors, chicken skewers offer a quick, easy, and healthy meal that everyone will love. In this post, we’ll explore a variety of mouth-watering chicken skewer recipes that can suit any taste, from tangy to spicy and everything in between.

1. Classic Grilled Chicken Skewers

Ingredients:

  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts, cut into 1-inch cubes
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons lemon juice
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 tablespoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • Wooden or metal skewers

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the Marinade: In a bowl, combine olive oil, lemon juice, minced garlic, dried oregano, salt, and black pepper. Stir until the ingredients are well mixed.
  2. Marinate the Chicken: Place the cubed chicken in a zip-top bag or shallow dish and pour the marinade over the chicken. Seal the bag or cover the dish and refrigerate for at least 30 minutes, preferably up to 2 hours for maximum flavor.
  3. Prepare the Skewers: Preheat your grill to medium-high heat. If using wooden skewers, soak them in water for at least 30 minutes before threading the chicken to prevent them from burning.
  4. Skewer the Chicken: Thread the marinated chicken cubes onto the skewers, leaving a little space between each piece to ensure even cooking.
  5. Grill the Skewers: Place the skewers on the grill and cook for 6-8 minutes per side, or until the chicken reaches an internal temperature of 165°F (75°C) and is golden brown with a slight char.
  6. Serve: Remove the chicken skewers from the grill and let them rest for a few minutes before serving. Pair with a fresh salad, rice, or grilled vegetables for a complete meal.

These classic grilled chicken skewers are simple, flavorful, and perfect for any occasion. The marinade’s lemony and herbaceous notes give the chicken a bright, savory taste that’s sure to satisfy.

2. Spicy BBQ Chicken Skewers

Ingredients:

  • 4 boneless chicken thighs, cut into 1-inch pieces
  • 1/2 cup barbecue sauce
  • 2 tablespoons honey
  • 1 tablespoon hot sauce (adjust to taste)
  • 1 teaspoon smoked paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon cayenne pepper (optional)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Skewers

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the Marinade: In a small bowl, whisk together the barbecue sauce, honey, hot sauce, smoked paprika, cayenne pepper, salt, and pepper. Adjust the level of hot sauce based on your preferred spice level.
  2. Marinate the Chicken: Place the chicken pieces in a resealable bag or shallow bowl and pour the marinade over the chicken. Seal the bag or cover the bowl and refrigerate for 1 hour or overnight for maximum flavor.
  3. Skewer the Chicken: Preheat your grill or grill pan over medium-high heat. If using wooden skewers, soak them in water for 30 minutes.
  4. Grill the Skewers: Thread the marinated chicken onto the skewers and place them on the grill. Cook for 7-10 minutes per side, or until the chicken is fully cooked and has reached an internal temperature of 165°F (75°C).
  5. Serve: Once cooked, remove the skewers from the grill and allow them to rest for a few minutes. Serve the spicy BBQ chicken skewers with coleslaw or a side of crispy fries for a satisfying dinner.

These spicy BBQ chicken skewers offer the perfect balance of smoky, tangy, and spicy flavors that will leave everyone craving more. The honey adds a touch of sweetness, while the hot sauce brings the heat, making them ideal for those who enjoy bold flavors.

3. Mediterranean Chicken Skewers with Tzatziki Sauce

Ingredients:

For the Chicken:

  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts, cut into cubes
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons red wine vinegar
  • 2 teaspoons dried oregano
  • 2 teaspoons garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper

For the Tzatziki Sauce:

  • 1 cup Greek yogurt
  • 1 cucumber, grated and excess water squeezed out
  • 2 tablespoons fresh dill, chopped
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 garlic clove, minced
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the Marinade: In a bowl, whisk together olive oil, red wine vinegar, oregano, garlic powder, cumin, salt, and black pepper.
  2. Marinate the Chicken: Place the chicken cubes in a zip-top bag or shallow dish and pour the marinade over the chicken. Seal the bag or cover the dish and refrigerate for at least 1 hour, or up to 4 hours for deeper flavor.
  3. Prepare the Tzatziki Sauce: In a separate bowl, mix the Greek yogurt, grated cucumber, dill, olive oil, garlic, lemon juice, salt, and pepper. Stir until smooth and refrigerate until ready to serve.
  4. Skewer the Chicken: Preheat your grill or grill pan over medium-high heat. If using wooden skewers, soak them for at least 30 minutes. Thread the marinated chicken onto the skewers.
  5. Grill the Skewers: Place the skewers on the grill and cook for 6-8 minutes per side, or until the chicken reaches 165°F (75°C) and is lightly charred on the outside.
  6. Serve: Serve the grilled chicken skewers with a generous dollop of the tzatziki sauce. Pair them with pita bread or a Greek salad for a complete Mediterranean-inspired dinner.

These Mediterranean chicken skewers are fresh, light, and bursting with flavor. The tangy tzatziki sauce complements the marinated chicken perfectly, creating a delicious balance of flavors. This recipe is ideal for anyone craving a healthy and satisfying meal.

4. Teriyaki Chicken Skewers

Ingredients:

  • 4 boneless chicken breasts, cut into cubes
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons honey
  • 1 tablespoon rice vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon sesame oil
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1-inch piece of fresh ginger, grated
  • 1 tablespoon cornstarch (optional for thickening)
  • Sesame seeds for garnish (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the Marinade: In a small bowl, whisk together the soy sauce, honey, rice vinegar, sesame oil, minced garlic, and grated ginger. For a thicker sauce, mix cornstarch with a little water and add it to the marinade.
  2. Marinate the Chicken: Place the chicken cubes in a resealable bag or shallow dish and pour the marinade over the chicken. Seal the bag or cover the dish and refrigerate for 30 minutes to 2 hours.
  3. Skewer the Chicken: Preheat your grill or grill pan to medium-high heat. Thread the marinated chicken onto the skewers.
  4. Grill the Skewers: Grill the skewers for 7-10 minutes per side, or until the chicken reaches 165°F (75°C) and is cooked through.
  5. Serve: Garnish with sesame seeds and serve the teriyaki chicken skewers with steamed rice and vegetables for a flavorful and satisfying meal.

The sweet and savory flavors of the teriyaki sauce make these chicken skewers irresistible. The addition of ginger adds an aromatic kick, while the sesame seeds provide a nice crunch and nutty flavor.

5. Lemon Herb Chicken Skewers

Ingredients:

  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts, cut into 1-inch cubes
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons lemon zest
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 2 teaspoons fresh thyme leaves
  • 2 teaspoons fresh rosemary, chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the Marinade: In a bowl, combine the olive oil, lemon zest, lemon juice, thyme, rosemary, garlic, salt, and pepper. Stir to combine.
  2. Marinate the Chicken: Place the chicken cubes in a resealable bag or shallow dish and pour the marinade over the chicken. Seal the bag or cover the dish and refrigerate for at least 30 minutes, or up to 2 hours for more intense flavor.
  3. Skewer the Chicken: Preheat the grill to medium-high heat. Thread the marinated chicken onto the skewers.
  4. Grill the Skewers: Grill the skewers for 6-8 minutes per side, or until the chicken reaches an internal temperature of 165°F (75°C).
  5. Serve: Serve the lemon herb chicken skewers with a side of roasted potatoes or a fresh vegetable medley for a light and refreshing dinner.

The lemon herb chicken skewers are perfect for anyone craving a bright and zesty meal. The fresh herbs and citrus flavors elevate the chicken, making it an ideal dish for a spring or summer evening.

Conclusion

Chicken skewers are an excellent choice for dinner due to their versatility, ease of preparation, and the variety of flavors they can offer. From classic grilled options to spicy BBQ, Mediterranean-inspired flavors, and sweet teriyaki, there’s a recipe to suit every palate. These skewers can be customized to fit your taste preferences and paired with a range of sides, making them a perfect choice for family dinners, gatherings, or a simple weeknight meal.

Give these chicken skewer recipes a try, and enjoy a satisfying and flavorful meal that’s sure to become a regular feature in your dinner rotation!
