Crispy Bacon-Wrapped Dates: A Sweet and Savory Appetizer for Entertaining


If you’re looking for an appetizer that will impress your guests and tantalize their taste buds, look no further than crispy bacon-wrapped dates. This delightful dish combines the smoky, savory flavor of bacon with the natural sweetness of dates, creating a perfect balance of flavors and textures. Whether you’re hosting a dinner party, a casual gathering, or simply want to treat yourself to a delicious snack, these bacon-wrapped dates are sure to be a hit.

Ingredients

  • Medjool dates (pitted)
  • Bacon slices (cut in half)
  • Goat cheese
  • Almonds (whole, unsalted)
  • Balsamic glaze
  • Toothpicks

Instructions

  1. Preheat the Oven: Start by preheating your oven to 375°F (190°C). This will ensure that your bacon-wrapped dates cook evenly and become delightfully crispy.

  2. Prepare the Dates: Take each pitted date and stuff it with a small amount of goat cheese, then press an almond into the cheese-filled cavity of the date. The combination of sweet dates, creamy goat cheese, and crunchy almonds creates a delightful medley of flavors and textures.

  3. Wrap with Bacon: Once the dates are stuffed, carefully wrap each one with a half slice of bacon, securing it with a toothpick. This step not only infuses the dates with the rich, smoky flavor of bacon but also adds a satisfying crunch to each bite.

  4. Bake to Perfection: Arrange the bacon-wrapped dates on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper to prevent sticking. Place the sheet in the preheated oven and bake for 15-18 minutes, or until the bacon is crispy and golden brown. Keep an eye on them to ensure they don’t overcook.

  5. Drizzle with Balsamic Glaze: Once the bacon-wrapped dates are done baking, remove them from the oven and let them cool for a few minutes. Before serving, drizzle them with a touch of balsamic glaze to add a hint of tangy sweetness that perfectly complements the richness of the bacon and the sweetness of the dates.

  6. Serve and Enjoy: Arrange the crispy bacon-wrapped dates on a serving platter and watch as your guests eagerly reach for this irresistible appetizer. The combination of sweet, savory, and crispy elements will make these a crowd favorite at any gathering.

Tips and Variations

  • For a vegetarian twist, substitute the bacon with thin strips of zucchini and bake until the zucchini is golden and crisp.
  • Experiment with different types of cheese, such as blue cheese or Gouda, to add a unique flavor profile to the dates.
  • If you prefer a spicier kick, consider adding a sprinkle of cayenne pepper or a dash of hot sauce to the goat cheese stuffing.

Crispy bacon-wrapped dates are a versatile appetizer that can be enjoyed at any time of year and for any occasion. Their irresistible combination of sweet and savory flavors, along with their satisfying crunch, makes them a delightful addition to any menu. Whether you’re entertaining guests or simply treating yourself to a special snack, these bacon-wrapped dates are sure to leave a lasting impression. So go ahead, give this recipe a try, and savor the delightful contrast of flavors that make these appetizers a true culinary delight.
