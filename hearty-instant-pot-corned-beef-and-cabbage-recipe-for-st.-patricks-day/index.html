Hearty Instant Pot Corned Beef and Cabbage Recipe for St. Patrick's Day


As St. Patrick’s Day approaches, it’s time to start thinking about the traditional Irish fare that we all look forward to. One dish that stands out among the rest is the classic Corned Beef and Cabbage. This hearty meal is a staple on St. Patrick’s Day, and it’s no wonder why. The tender beef, flavorful vegetables, and savory broth combine to create a dish that is both comforting and satisfying. And the best part? You can easily make it in your Instant Pot!

Ingredients

  • 3 pounds corned beef brisket, trimmed of excess fat
  • 1 onion, sliced
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 4 cups beef broth
  • 1 head cabbage, cut into wedges
  • 6 carrots, peeled and cut into chunks
  • 6 small potatoes, quartered
  • 1 teaspoon whole black peppercorns
  • 2 bay leaves
  • Salt and pepper, to taste

Instructions

  1. Place the corned beef brisket in the Instant Pot, fat side up. Add the sliced onion and minced garlic.

  2. Pour the beef broth into the Instant Pot, making sure the brisket is mostly covered.

  3. Add the whole black peppercorns and bay leaves to the pot. Season with salt and pepper to taste.

  4. Close the Instant Pot and set the pressure to high. Cook for 90 minutes, allowing for a natural release of pressure.

  5. Once the pressure has been released, carefully remove the corned beef from the Instant Pot and set it aside. Cover it with foil to keep it warm.

  6. Add the cabbage wedges, carrots, and potatoes to the remaining liquid in the Instant Pot. Close the lid and set the pressure to high. Cook for an additional 5 minutes.

  7. After the 5 minutes, quick-release the pressure and carefully remove the cooked vegetables from the Instant Pot.

  8. Slice the corned beef against the grain and serve it alongside the cabbage, carrots, and potatoes. Spoon some of the cooking liquid over the meat and vegetables for added flavor.

  9. Enjoy this delicious and hearty Instant Pot Corned Beef and Cabbage with your loved ones this St. Patrick’s Day!

Tips and Variations

  • For a twist on the classic recipe, try adding a tablespoon of mustard or horseradish to the cooking liquid before pressure cooking.

  • If you prefer a more tender cabbage, cook it for an additional 2-3 minutes in the Instant Pot.

  • Leftover corned beef can be used to make sandwiches or added to salads for a tasty meal the next day.

  • Don’t forget to serve this dish with some freshly baked Irish soda bread to complete your St. Patrick’s Day feast!

Conclusion

This Hearty Instant Pot Corned Beef and Cabbage recipe is the perfect dish to celebrate St. Patrick’s Day. With the convenience of the Instant Pot, you can enjoy a flavorful and tender meal without spending hours in the kitchen. The combination of the corned beef, cabbage, and vegetables creates a well-rounded and satisfying plate of food that will have your friends and family asking for seconds. So, grab your Instant Pot, gather your ingredients, and get ready to celebrate St. Patrick’s Day with this delicious and traditional Irish recipe!
