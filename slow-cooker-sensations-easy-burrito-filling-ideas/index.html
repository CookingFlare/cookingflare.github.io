Slow Cooker Sensations: Easy Burrito Filling Ideas


Are you tired of the same old burrito fillings? Look no further! In this post, we’ll explore a variety of delicious and easy-to-make burrito filling ideas that you can prepare in your slow cooker. These slow cooker sensations are not only flavorful but also incredibly convenient. Let’s dive in!

1. Slow Cooker Shredded Chicken

Ingredients:

  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1 cup chicken broth
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Place the chicken breasts in the slow cooker.
  2. In a small bowl, mix together the chicken broth, cumin, paprika, garlic powder, salt, and pepper. Pour the mixture over the chicken.
  3. Cover the slow cooker and cook on low for 6-8 hours or on high for 3-4 hours, until the chicken is tender and easily shreds.
  4. Shred the chicken using two forks. Mix it well with the cooking juices.
  5. Use the slow cooker shredded chicken as a filling for your burritos. Add some salsa, cheese, and your favorite toppings for a burst of flavor.

2. Slow Cooker Beef Barbacoa

Ingredients:

  • 2 pounds beef chuck roast
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup beef broth
  • 2 tablespoons lime juice
  • 2 tablespoons apple cider vinegar
  • 2 tablespoons tomato paste
  • 2 teaspoons ground cumin
  • 2 teaspoons dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon chili powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Place the beef chuck roast in the slow cooker.
  2. In a bowl, mix together the diced onion, minced garlic, beef broth, lime juice, apple cider vinegar, tomato paste, cumin, oregano, chili powder, salt, and pepper. Pour the mixture over the beef.
  3. Cover the slow cooker and cook on low for 8-10 hours or on high for 4-6 hours, until the beef is tender and easily shreds.
  4. Shred the beef using two forks. Mix it well with the cooking juices.
  5. Serve the slow cooker beef barbacoa in your burritos with some fresh cilantro, diced onions, and a squeeze of lime juice for a burst of tangy flavors.

3. Slow Cooker Vegetarian Black Bean Filling

Ingredients:

  • 2 cans black beans, drained and rinsed
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 1 bell pepper, diced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup vegetable broth
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • 1 teaspoon chili powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a skillet, sauté the diced onion, bell pepper, and minced garlic until they become soft and fragrant.
  2. Transfer the sautéed vegetables to the slow cooker.
  3. Add the black beans, vegetable broth, cumin, chili powder, salt, and pepper to the slow cooker.
  4. Cover the slow cooker and cook on low for 4-6 hours or on high for 2-3 hours, until the flavors meld together.
  5. Mash the black beans slightly with a fork to create a creamy consistency.
  6. Use the slow cooker vegetarian black bean filling in your burritos. Top it with some avocado slices, sour cream, and fresh cilantro for a burst of freshness.

Conclusion

With these easy burrito filling ideas, your slow cooker will become your new favorite kitchen tool. From the tender and flavorful slow cooker shredded chicken to the tangy and juicy slow cooker beef barbacoa, or the creamy and comforting slow cooker vegetarian black bean filling, there’s a burrito filling for every palate. Get creative with your toppings and enjoy a delicious homemade burrito that will satisfy your cravings. So, grab your slow cooker and let the flavors simmer away!
