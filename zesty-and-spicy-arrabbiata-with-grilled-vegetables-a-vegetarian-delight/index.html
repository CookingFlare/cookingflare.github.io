Zesty and Spicy Arrabbiata with Grilled Vegetables: A Vegetarian Delight


If you’re a fan of bold flavors and love to experiment with vegetarian dishes, then this recipe is for you. Today, we’re going to prepare a zesty and spicy Arrabbiata sauce with grilled vegetables that will leave your taste buds tingling with delight. This dish is not only packed with flavor but also loaded with nutrients, making it a perfect choice for a healthy and satisfying meal.

Ingredients

To create this vegetarian masterpiece, here’s what you’ll need:

  • 2 large red bell peppers
  • 2 medium zucchinis
  • 1 eggplant
  • 1 red onion
  • 4 cloves of garlic
  • 1 can (400g) of diced tomatoes
  • 2 tablespoons of tomato paste
  • 1 teaspoon of dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon of dried basil
  • 1/2 teaspoon of red pepper flakes (adjust according to your spice preference)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Olive oil for grilling

Grilling the Vegetables

Before we dive into preparing the Arrabbiata sauce, we need to grill our vegetables to enhance their flavors. Start by preheating your grill to medium-high heat.

  1. Cut the red bell peppers, zucchinis, eggplant, and red onion into thick slices. Ensure they are evenly sized to ensure even grilling.

  2. In a large bowl, toss the vegetables with olive oil, salt, and pepper. Make sure each piece is coated with a thin layer of oil.

  3. Place the vegetables on the preheated grill and cook for approximately 5-7 minutes on each side or until they develop grill marks and become tender.

  4. Once grilled, remove the vegetables from the grill and set them aside to cool.

Zesty and Spicy Arrabbiata Sauce

Now that our vegetables are grilled to perfection, it’s time to prepare the Arrabbiata sauce. This sauce originates from the Italian region of Lazio and is known for its fiery flavor. Let’s get started!

  1. Heat a drizzle of olive oil in a large pan over medium heat. Add the minced garlic and sauté until fragrant, approximately 1 minute.

  2. Add the diced tomatoes, tomato paste, dried oregano, dried basil, and red pepper flakes to the pan. Stir well to combine all the ingredients.

  3. Reduce the heat to low and let the sauce simmer for about 20 minutes, allowing the flavors to meld together.

  4. While the sauce is simmering, take the cooled grilled vegetables and chop them into bite-sized pieces.

  5. Add the grilled vegetables to the simmering Arrabbiata sauce and stir gently to coat them evenly.

  6. Allow the sauce to cook for an additional 5 minutes, ensuring the vegetables are heated through.

Serving Suggestions

Now that our zesty and spicy Arrabbiata with grilled vegetables is ready, it’s time to plate up and enjoy this vegetarian delight. Here are a few serving suggestions to take this dish to the next level:

  • Serve the Arrabbiata sauce with your favorite pasta, such as penne or spaghetti, for a hearty and filling meal.
  • Top the dish with a sprinkle of freshly grated Parmesan cheese for an added touch of richness.
  • Garnish with fresh basil leaves or a drizzle of extra virgin olive oil for an extra burst of flavor.

Final Thoughts

This zesty and spicy Arrabbiata with grilled vegetables is a fantastic way to incorporate more plant-based meals into your diet without compromising on taste. The combination of smoky grilled vegetables and the fiery Arrabbiata sauce creates a harmonious balance of flavors that will leave you craving more.

So, why not give this vegetarian delight a try? It’s a dish that will not only satisfy your taste buds but also nourish your body with wholesome ingredients. Get creative in the kitchen and enjoy the bold and vibrant flavors of this zesty and spicy Arrabbiata with grilled vegetables!
