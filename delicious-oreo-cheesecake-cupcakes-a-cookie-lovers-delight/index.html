Delicious Oreo Cheesecake Cupcakes: A Cookie Lover's Delight


If you’re a fan of cookies and cheesecake, then this recipe is for you! These delicious Oreo Cheesecake Cupcakes combine the best of both worlds, creating a sweet and creamy treat that will satisfy any cookie lover’s cravings. With a rich Oreo crust, a creamy cheesecake filling, and a decadent Oreo cookie topping, these cupcakes are sure to be a hit at any gathering or dessert table.

Ingredients

For the Oreo crust:

  • 18 Oreo cookies
  • 3 tablespoons melted butter

For the cheesecake filling:

  • 16 ounces cream cheese, softened
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 2 large eggs

For the Oreo cookie topping:

  • 12 Oreo cookies, crushed

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 325°F (163°C) and line a muffin tin with cupcake liners.

  2. In a food processor or by hand, crush the 18 Oreo cookies until they resemble fine crumbs. Combine the crushed cookies with melted butter in a bowl and mix well.

  3. Spoon about 1 tablespoon of the Oreo crust mixture into each cupcake liner. Press down firmly to create an even crust at the bottom.

  4. In a separate bowl, beat the cream cheese, sugar, and vanilla extract until smooth and creamy. Add the eggs, one at a time, and continue beating until well combined.

  5. Pour the cheesecake filling over the Oreo crust, filling each cupcake liner about 2/3 full.

  6. Sprinkle the crushed Oreo cookies evenly over the top of each cupcake, pressing them gently into the filling.

  7. Place the muffin tin in the preheated oven and bake for 20-25 minutes, or until the cheesecake filling is set.

  8. Remove the cupcakes from the oven and let them cool in the pan for about 10 minutes. Then, transfer them to a wire rack to cool completely.

  9. Once the cupcakes have cooled, refrigerate them for at least 2 hours to allow the cheesecake to set and the flavors to meld together.

  10. Serve chilled and enjoy these delectable Oreo Cheesecake Cupcakes!

Tips and Variations

  • For an extra indulgent twist, you can drizzle some melted chocolate over the top of each cupcake before serving.

  • If you prefer a stronger Oreo flavor, you can add some crushed Oreo cookies to the cheesecake filling as well.

  • Feel free to experiment with different types of Oreo cookies, such as the Double Stuf or flavored varieties, to add a unique twist to your cupcakes.

  • These cupcakes can be stored in an airtight container in the refrigerator for up to 5 days.

  • Serve these cupcakes as a standalone dessert or pair them with a scoop of vanilla ice cream for an extra treat.

Conclusion

Delicious Oreo Cheesecake Cupcakes are a cookie lover’s dream come true. With their irresistible Oreo crust, creamy cheesecake filling, and crunchy Oreo cookie topping, these cupcakes are sure to impress both kids and adults alike. Whether you’re hosting a party or simply want to indulge in a sweet treat, give this recipe a try and let your taste buds experience the magic of Oreo and cheesecake in one delightful bite!
