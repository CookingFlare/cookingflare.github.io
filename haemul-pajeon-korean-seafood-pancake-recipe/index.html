Haemul Pajeon: Korean Seafood Pancake Recipe


If you’re a fan of Korean cuisine or just looking to try something new and delicious, you’re in for a treat. Haemul Pajeon, also known as Korean seafood pancake, is a popular and savory dish that’s perfect for any occasion. This crispy and flavorful pancake is filled with a delightful combination of seafood, vegetables, and a savory batter that will leave your taste buds wanting more. In this blog post, I’ll guide you through the process of creating your very own Haemul Pajeon, so get ready to embark on a culinary adventure!

Ingredients

Before we dive into the cooking process, let’s take a look at the ingredients you’ll need to prepare this mouthwatering dish:

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup water
  • 1 egg
  • 1 teaspoon soy sauce
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon onion powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon sesame oil
  • 1 cup assorted seafood (shrimp, squid, and/or clams)
  • 1 cup scallions, chopped
  • 1/2 cup onion, thinly sliced
  • Vegetable oil for frying

Instructions

Step 1: Prepare the Batter

In a mixing bowl, combine the all-purpose flour, water, egg, soy sauce, salt, sugar, garlic powder, onion powder, and sesame oil. Whisk the ingredients together until you achieve a smooth and slightly thick batter. This flavorful batter will serve as the perfect base for your Haemul Pajeon.

Step 2: Add the Seafood and Vegetables

Once the batter is ready, it’s time to incorporate the star ingredients – seafood and vegetables. Gently fold in the assorted seafood, chopped scallions, and thinly sliced onion into the batter. Ensure that the seafood and vegetables are evenly distributed throughout the batter for a balanced and delightful pancake.

Step 3: Heat the Pan

Place a non-stick skillet or frying pan over medium heat and add a generous amount of vegetable oil. It’s important to ensure that the pan is well-oiled to achieve a crispy exterior for your Haemul Pajeon.

Step 4: Cook the Pancake

Once the pan is heated, pour a ladleful of the batter mixture onto the pan, spreading it out to form a circular pancake shape. Allow the pancake to cook for a few minutes until the bottom turns golden brown and crispy. Carefully flip the pancake using a spatula and cook the other side until it achieves the same golden brown perfection.

Step 5: Serve and Enjoy

Once both sides of the pancake are beautifully crispy and golden, transfer it to a plate and cut it into wedges. Haemul Pajeon is traditionally served with a dipping sauce made of soy sauce, rice vinegar, and a sprinkle of sesame seeds. This delightful pancake is best enjoyed hot and fresh, so gather your friends and family and savor the flavors together!

Conclusion

Congratulations, you’ve just mastered the art of creating Haemul Pajeon – a delectable Korean seafood pancake that’s bursting with flavor and texture. Whether you’re hosting a gathering or simply craving a savory snack, this recipe is sure to impress and satisfy. Feel free to customize the seafood and vegetable fillings to your liking, and don’t be afraid to experiment with different dipping sauces to elevate the experience even further. Embrace the rich flavors of Korean cuisine with this delightful Haemul Pajeon recipe, and let your taste buds revel in the culinary delights of the East!

I hope you enjoy creating and savoring this delightful dish as much as I do. Happy cooking!
