Grill Master: BBQ Cooking for Two Recipes


Are you ready to fire up the grill and enjoy a delicious BBQ feast for two? Whether you’re planning a romantic dinner or just a cozy meal with your favorite person, these Grill Master BBQ recipes are perfect for creating a memorable dining experience. From juicy steaks to flavorful skewers, we’ve got you covered with these mouthwatering recipes that are sure to make your BBQ night a sizzling success.

Grilled Garlic Butter Shrimp Skewers

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 pound large shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 2 tablespoons melted butter
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Wooden skewers, soaked in water

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the grill to medium-high heat.
  2. In a small bowl, mix together the melted butter, minced garlic, lemon juice, salt, and pepper.
  3. Thread the shrimp onto the soaked skewers, then brush them generously with the garlic butter mixture.
  4. Grill the skewers for 2-3 minutes on each side, until the shrimp are pink and opaque.
  5. Serve the skewers hot, garnished with fresh parsley and a squeeze of lemon juice.

Smoky BBQ Chicken Thighs

Ingredients:

  • 4 bone-in, skin-on chicken thighs
  • 1/2 cup BBQ sauce
  • 1 teaspoon smoked paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Season the chicken thighs with salt, pepper, smoked paprika, and garlic powder.
  2. Preheat the grill to medium heat and oil the grates to prevent sticking.
  3. Place the chicken thighs on the grill, skin side down, and cook for 6-8 minutes.
  4. Flip the thighs, brush them with BBQ sauce, and continue grilling for another 6-8 minutes, or until the internal temperature reaches 165°F (74°C).
  5. Let the chicken rest for a few minutes before serving.

Grilled Ribeye Steak with Herb Butter

Ingredients:

  • 2 ribeye steaks
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Herb Butter:

  • 4 tablespoons unsalted butter, softened
  • 2 tablespoons fresh parsley, chopped
  • 1 tablespoon fresh thyme, chopped
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Rub the steaks with olive oil, salt, and pepper.
  2. Preheat the grill to high heat and oil the grates.
  3. Grill the steaks for 4-5 minutes on each side for medium-rare, or to your desired doneness.
  4. While the steaks are grilling, mix together the softened butter, chopped parsley, thyme, garlic, salt, and pepper to make the herb butter.
  5. Remove the steaks from the grill and let them rest for a few minutes. Top each steak with a generous dollop of herb butter before serving.

Grilled Veggie Skewers

Ingredients:

  • 1 zucchini, sliced into rounds
  • 1 bell pepper, cut into chunks
  • 1 red onion, cut into wedges
  • 8 cherry tomatoes
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon Italian seasoning
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Wooden skewers, soaked in water

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the grill to medium-high heat.
  2. Thread the zucchini, bell pepper, red onion, and cherry tomatoes onto the soaked skewers.
  3. In a small bowl, whisk together the olive oil, Italian seasoning, salt, and pepper.
  4. Brush the veggie skewers with the seasoned oil and place them on the grill.
  5. Grill for 10-12 minutes, turning occasionally, until the veggies are tender and slightly charred.
  6. Serve the grilled veggie skewers as a colorful and flavorful side dish.

With these delectable recipes, you can transform your backyard into a culinary paradise for two. Whether you prefer seafood, poultry, or steak, these BBQ dishes are designed to elevate your outdoor dining experience. So, fire up the grill, savor the aroma of sizzling goodness, and indulge in the pleasure of Grill Master: BBQ Cooking for Two. Cheers to good food and great company!
