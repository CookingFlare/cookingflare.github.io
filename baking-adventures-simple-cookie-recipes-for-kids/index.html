Baking Adventures: Simple Cookie Recipes for Kids


Welcome to the world of baking adventures! Today, we’re going to explore some simple and delightful cookie recipes that are perfect for kids. Baking with kids can be a fun and educational experience, and what better way to start than with some delicious cookies? Whether you’re a parent, guardian, or a teacher looking for engaging activities for kids, these cookie recipes are sure to be a hit.

Chocolate Chip Cookies

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/2 cup packed brown sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 egg
  • 1 cup semisweet chocolate chips

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. In a small bowl, whisk together the flour and baking soda.
  3. In a large bowl, cream together the butter, granulated sugar, and brown sugar until smooth. Beat in the vanilla and egg until well blended.
  4. Gradually stir in the dry ingredients until just blended. Fold in the chocolate chips.
  5. Drop by rounded spoonfuls onto ungreased baking sheets.
  6. Bake for 8 to 10 minutes in the preheated oven, or until golden brown. Allow cookies to cool on baking sheet for 5 minutes before transferring to a wire rack to cool completely.

Peanut Butter Cookies

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1/2 cup peanut butter
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/2 cup packed brown sugar
  • 1 egg
  • 1 1/4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking powder
  • 3/4 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. In a large bowl, cream together the butter, peanut butter, granulated sugar, and brown sugar until well blended.
  3. Beat in the egg.
  4. In a separate bowl, combine the flour, baking powder, baking soda, and salt. Gradually mix into the peanut butter mixture.
  5. Shape dough into 1 inch balls and place them 2 inches apart on ungreased cookie sheets.
  6. Flatten each ball with a fork, making a crisscross pattern.
  7. Bake for 10 to 12 minutes in the preheated oven. Allow cookies to cool on baking sheet for 5 minutes before transferring to a wire rack to cool completely.

Oatmeal Raisin Cookies

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup butter, softened
  • 1 cup packed brown sugar
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 1/4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 3 cups quick-cooking oats
  • 1 cup raisins

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. In a large bowl, cream together the butter, brown sugar, and granulated sugar until smooth.
  3. Beat in the eggs one at a time, then stir in the vanilla.
  4. Combine the flour, baking soda, and cinnamon; stir into the creamed mixture.
  5. Mix in the oats and raisins until evenly distributed.
  6. Drop by rounded spoonfuls onto ungreased cookie sheets.
  7. Bake for 8 to 10 minutes in the preheated oven. Allow cookies to cool on baking sheet for 5 minutes before transferring to a wire rack to cool completely.

Baking with kids is not only a creative and enjoyable activity, but it also teaches them important skills such as following instructions, measuring ingredients, and teamwork. These simple cookie recipes are a great way to introduce children to the joy of baking. So, gather your little bakers, put on your aprons, and let the baking adventures begin!
