Tasty Breakfast Pizza Recipes for a Fun and Filling Meal


Who says pizza is only for lunch or dinner? With these delicious breakfast pizza recipes, you can start your day off right with a fun and filling meal that will leave you satisfied until lunchtime. Whether you prefer savory or sweet flavors, there’s a breakfast pizza recipe here for everyone. So, let’s get cooking!

1. Classic Bacon and Egg Breakfast Pizza

Prep Time: 15 minutes
Cook Time: 20 minutes
Total Time: 35 minutes
Serves: 4

Ingredients:

  • 1 pre-made pizza dough
  • 6 slices of bacon, cooked and crumbled
  • 4 large eggs
  • 1 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh chives
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 425°F (220°C).
  2. Roll out the pizza dough on a lightly floured surface to your desired thickness.
  3. Transfer the dough to a parchment-lined baking sheet.
  4. Sprinkle half of the shredded cheddar cheese evenly over the dough.
  5. Scatter the crumbled bacon over the cheese.
  6. Create four wells on the pizza and crack an egg into each well.
  7. Season the eggs with salt and pepper.
  8. Sprinkle the remaining cheese over the eggs.
  9. Bake in the preheated oven for 15-20 minutes or until the crust is golden and the eggs are cooked to your liking.
  10. Remove from the oven and sprinkle with chopped chives.
  11. Let it cool for a few minutes before slicing and serving.

2. Veggie Delight Breakfast Pizza

Prep Time: 20 minutes
Cook Time: 25 minutes
Total Time: 45 minutes
Serves: 4

Ingredients:

  • 1 pre-made pizza dough
  • 1/2 cup marinara sauce
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1 cup sliced mushrooms
  • 1/2 cup sliced bell peppers
  • 1/4 cup sliced black olives
  • 2 green onions, sliced
  • 4 large eggs
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 425°F (220°C).
  2. Roll out the pizza dough on a lightly floured surface to your desired thickness.
  3. Transfer the dough to a parchment-lined baking sheet.
  4. Spread the marinara sauce evenly over the dough, leaving a small border around the edges.
  5. Sprinkle the shredded mozzarella cheese over the sauce.
  6. Arrange the sliced mushrooms, bell peppers, black olives, and green onions on top.
  7. Create four wells on the pizza and crack an egg into each well.
  8. Season the eggs with salt and pepper.
  9. Bake in the preheated oven for 20-25 minutes or until the crust is golden and the eggs are cooked to your liking.
  10. Remove from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes before slicing and serving.

3. Nutella and Banana Breakfast Pizza

Prep Time: 10 minutes
Cook Time: 15 minutes
Total Time: 25 minutes
Serves: 4

Ingredients:

  • 1 pre-made pizza dough
  • 1/2 cup Nutella
  • 2 ripe bananas, sliced
  • 1/4 cup chopped hazelnuts
  • Powdered sugar for dusting

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 425°F (220°C).
  2. Roll out the pizza dough on a lightly floured surface to your desired thickness.
  3. Transfer the dough to a parchment-lined baking sheet.
  4. Spread the Nutella evenly over the dough, leaving a small border around the edges.
  5. Arrange the sliced bananas on top of the Nutella.
  6. Sprinkle the chopped hazelnuts over the bananas.
  7. Bake in the preheated oven for 12-15 minutes or until the crust is golden.
  8. Remove from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes.
  9. Dust with powdered sugar before slicing and serving.

Conclusion

Breakfast pizza is a delicious and versatile meal that you can enjoy any day of the week. From the classic bacon and egg combination to vegetarian-friendly options and even sweet treats, there’s a breakfast pizza recipe here to suit every taste. So, why not shake up your breakfast routine and give these tasty recipes a try? You won’t be disappointed!

Remember, breakfast is the most important meal of the day, so why not make it a fun and filling one with these mouthwatering breakfast pizza recipes? Enjoy!
