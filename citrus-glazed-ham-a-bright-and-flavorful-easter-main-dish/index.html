Citrus Glazed Ham: A Bright and Flavorful Easter Main Dish


When it comes to Easter dinner, nothing quite steals the show like a succulent, flavorful ham at the center of the table. This year, why not elevate your Easter feast with a Citrus Glazed Ham that’s bursting with bright, tangy flavors and a hint of sweetness? This impressive main dish is surprisingly simple to prepare and is sure to leave your guests coming back for seconds.

Ingredients:

  • 1 fully cooked bone-in ham (8-10 pounds)
  • 1 cup orange juice
  • 1/2 cup honey
  • 1/4 cup brown sugar
  • 1/4 cup Dijon mustard
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon orange zest
  • 1 teaspoon ground cloves
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • Fresh thyme sprigs for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Preparation: Preheat your oven to 325°F (165°C) and place the ham on a roasting pan. Score the surface of the ham in a diamond pattern, and insert whole cloves into the center of each diamond for an extra burst of flavor.

  2. Glaze Preparation: In a saucepan over medium heat, combine the orange juice, honey, brown sugar, Dijon mustard, soy sauce, orange zest, ground cloves, and ground cinnamon. Bring the mixture to a gentle simmer, stirring occasionally, until it thickens slightly, about 10-12 minutes.

  3. Glazing the Ham: Brush a generous amount of the citrus glaze over the entire surface of the ham, ensuring it seeps into the scored cuts. Reserve some of the glaze for basting during the cooking process.

  4. Baking: Place the ham in the preheated oven and bake for 1 1/2 to 2 hours, or until the internal temperature reaches 140°F (60°C), basting with the remaining glaze every 30 minutes to keep the ham moist and flavorful.

  5. Resting and Serving: Once the ham is cooked to perfection, remove it from the oven and let it rest for 15-20 minutes before slicing. Garnish with fresh thyme sprigs for a pop of color and aromatic freshness. Serve the Citrus Glazed Ham as the star of your Easter feast, accompanied by your favorite sides and perhaps a glass of chilled white wine.

Why Choose Citrus Glazed Ham?

The combination of citrus flavors, honey, and a hint of warmth from the spices creates a glaze that not only caramelizes beautifully on the ham but also infuses it with a delightful balance of sweet and tangy notes. The brightness of the citrus perfectly complements the rich, savory flavor of the ham, making it an ideal choice for a festive Easter meal.

Tips for Success:

  • Selecting the Ham: When choosing a ham, look for one that is fully cooked and bone-in for maximum flavor and juiciness.
  • Scoring the Ham: Scoring the surface of the ham allows the glaze to penetrate and flavor the meat throughout.
  • Basting: Regularly basting the ham with the citrus glaze ensures that it stays moist and develops a beautiful, caramelized exterior.

Conclusion

This Citrus Glazed Ham is a surefire way to add a burst of bright, zesty flavors to your Easter celebration. With its vibrant appearance and irresistible aroma, it’s not only a feast for the taste buds but also a stunning centerpiece for your holiday table. So, this Easter, treat your loved ones to a memorable dining experience with this show-stopping, Citrus Glazed Ham that’s as beautiful as it is delicious.

Enjoy the process of creating this mouthwatering masterpiece and savor the joy of sharing it with those you hold dear. Happy cooking and happy Easter!
