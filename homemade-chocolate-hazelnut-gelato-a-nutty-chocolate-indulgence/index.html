Homemade Chocolate Hazelnut Gelato: A Nutty Chocolate Indulgence


If you’re a fan of creamy, nutty, and decadent desserts, then homemade chocolate hazelnut gelato is a must-try for you. This rich and luscious frozen treat combines the intense flavor of dark chocolate with the irresistible crunch of roasted hazelnuts, creating a delightful indulgence that will satisfy your sweet cravings. Whether you’re planning a summer gathering, a cozy night in, or simply looking to treat yourself to something special, this homemade chocolate hazelnut gelato is the perfect dessert to impress and delight your taste buds.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups whole milk
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 4 large egg yolks
  • 3/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon pure vanilla extract
  • 7 ounces high-quality dark chocolate, finely chopped
  • 1 cup roasted hazelnuts, chopped

Instructions

Step 1: Prepare the Base

In a medium saucepan, combine the whole milk and heavy cream. Place the saucepan over medium heat and heat the mixture until it reaches a simmer. While the milk and cream are heating, whisk together the egg yolks, sugar, and salt in a separate bowl until the mixture becomes smooth and slightly thick.

Step 2: Temper the Eggs

Once the milk and cream mixture reaches a simmer, take about 1/2 cup of the hot liquid and slowly pour it into the egg mixture, whisking constantly. This process, known as tempering, prevents the eggs from curdling when added to the hot liquid. Gradually add the tempered egg mixture back into the saucepan with the remaining hot milk and cream, stirring constantly.

Step 3: Thicken the Base

Return the saucepan to the stove over medium heat and cook the mixture, stirring constantly, until it thickens enough to coat the back of a spoon. This step is crucial for achieving the desired creamy texture of the gelato base. Be patient and continue to stir to prevent the mixture from sticking to the bottom of the saucepan.

Step 4: Add the Flavors

Once the base has thickened, remove the saucepan from the heat and stir in the vanilla extract. Then, add the finely chopped dark chocolate to the mixture and stir until it is completely melted and well incorporated. The addition of the chocolate will infuse the base with a rich, deep cocoa flavor that forms the foundation of our chocolate hazelnut gelato.

Step 5: Chill and Churn

Transfer the chocolate base to a bowl and cover it with plastic wrap, ensuring that the wrap is in direct contact with the surface of the mixture to prevent a skin from forming. Refrigerate the base for at least 4 hours, or preferably overnight, to allow the flavors to develop and the mixture to chill thoroughly.

Once the base is well chilled, pour it into an ice cream maker and churn according to the manufacturer’s instructions. As the gelato begins to thicken, add the chopped roasted hazelnuts to the churned mixture, allowing them to distribute evenly and infuse the gelato with their delightful nuttiness.

Step 6: Freeze to Perfection

Transfer the churned gelato to a freezer-safe container, smoothing the top with a spatula. Cover the container with a lid or additional plastic wrap and freeze the gelato for at least 4 hours, or until it reaches the desired firmness. This final freezing stage allows the gelato to set and develop its signature creamy texture.

Serving Suggestions

When ready to serve, remove the chocolate hazelnut gelato from the freezer and let it sit at room temperature for a few minutes to soften slightly. Scoop the gelato into bowls or cones, and for an extra touch of indulgence, garnish with a sprinkle of chopped roasted hazelnuts or a drizzle of melted dark chocolate. This luxurious treat is best enjoyed with loved ones, as a delightful ending to a special meal, or simply as a moment of self-pampering.

Conclusion

Homemade chocolate hazelnut gelato is a labor of love that rewards you with a divine dessert experience. Its velvety texture, deep chocolate flavor, and crunchy hazelnut surprise make it an irresistible treat for any occasion. By following this recipe and infusing it with your passion for creating delightful sweets, you can indulge in the luxurious world of homemade gelato and treat yourself and your loved ones to a nutty chocolate delight that will leave everyone craving for more. So, roll up your sleeves, gather your ingredients, and embark on a journey to savor the creamy, nutty, and utterly delicious world of homemade chocolate hazelnut gelato.
