Quick and Easy Low Carb Cabbage Slaw


Looking for a refreshing and healthy side dish that’s quick and easy to prepare? This low carb cabbage slaw is the perfect choice! Whether you’re following a low-carb diet or simply looking for a light and flavorful addition to your meal, this recipe is sure to become a favorite. Packed with crunchy vegetables and a zesty dressing, it’s a great option for picnics, barbecues, or weekday meals. Let’s dive into the recipe and discover how to make this delicious low carb cabbage slaw in just a few simple steps.

Ingredients

  • 1 small head of green cabbage, finely shredded
  • 1 medium carrot, grated
  • 1/2 red onion, thinly sliced
  • 1/4 cup fresh parsley, chopped

For the Dressing

  • 1/3 cup mayonnaise
  • 2 tablespoons apple cider vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon Dijon mustard
  • 1 teaspoon honey (optional)
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Vegetables: Start by thinly shredding the green cabbage using a sharp knife or a mandoline. Grate the carrot using a box grater or a food processor. Thinly slice the red onion and chop the fresh parsley. Combine all the vegetables in a large mixing bowl.

  2. Make the Dressing: In a separate bowl, whisk together the mayonnaise, apple cider vinegar, Dijon mustard, and honey (if using). Season the dressing with salt and pepper to taste. The tangy flavor of the dressing adds a delightful zing to the slaw.

  3. Combine and Toss: Pour the dressing over the shredded vegetables in the mixing bowl. Use a pair of tongs or a large spoon to toss the ingredients together, ensuring that the dressing evenly coats the cabbage, carrot, and onion. The vibrant colors of the vegetables and the creamy dressing create an inviting visual appeal.

  4. Chill and Serve: Once the slaw is thoroughly mixed, cover the bowl with plastic wrap and refrigerate for at least 30 minutes to allow the flavors to meld together. Chilling the slaw also enhances its crisp texture, making it even more enjoyable to eat. When ready to serve, give the slaw a final toss and garnish with additional parsley if desired.

Tips and Variations

  • For a spicy kick, add a pinch of red pepper flakes or a dash of hot sauce to the dressing.
  • Experiment with different types of cabbage, such as red cabbage or Napa cabbage, for a unique twist on the classic slaw.
  • Increase the protein content by tossing in some grilled chicken strips or adding a sprinkle of toasted nuts, such as almonds or pecans.

Final Thoughts

This quick and easy low carb cabbage slaw is a versatile dish that complements a wide range of main courses. Its vibrant colors and refreshing flavor make it a standout addition to any meal. Additionally, the low carb content makes it suitable for those following a keto or low-carb lifestyle. Whether you’re preparing a family dinner or hosting a gathering, this simple yet flavorful slaw is sure to impress. Give this recipe a try and enjoy a delicious, nutritious side dish that’s perfect for any occasion!
