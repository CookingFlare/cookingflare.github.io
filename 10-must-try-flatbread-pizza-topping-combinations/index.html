10 Must-Try Flatbread Pizza Topping Combinations


When it comes to pizza, the possibilities are endless. From classic margherita to indulgent meat lovers, there’s a pizza for every taste. But have you ever tried making pizza on a flatbread? Flatbread pizza is a delicious alternative to traditional pizza, with a thin and crispy crust that’s perfect for loading up with your favorite toppings. In this blog post, we’ll explore 10 must-try flatbread pizza topping combinations that will take your pizza game to the next level. So, let’s get started!

1. Mediterranean Delight

For a taste of the Mediterranean, top your flatbread pizza with olive oil, mozzarella cheese, cherry tomatoes, kalamata olives, red onion, and crumbled feta cheese. Finish it off with a sprinkle of dried oregano and a drizzle of balsamic glaze for a burst of flavor.

2. BBQ Chicken

If you’re a fan of barbecue, you’ll love this flatbread pizza topping combination. Start with a base of barbecue sauce, then add shredded chicken, red onion, cilantro, and a blend of cheddar and mozzarella cheese. The result is a sweet and tangy pizza with a hint of smokiness.

3. Caprese

For a light and refreshing option, try a caprese flatbread pizza. Spread pesto sauce on the flatbread, then layer on fresh mozzarella cheese, sliced tomatoes, and fresh basil leaves. Drizzle with balsamic glaze and sprinkle with salt and pepper for a simple yet delicious pizza.

4. Buffalo Chicken

If you like a little heat, give this buffalo chicken flatbread pizza a try. Start with a base of buffalo sauce, then top with shredded chicken, sliced red onion, and crumbled blue cheese. Finish it off with a drizzle of ranch dressing for a cool and creamy contrast.

5. Margherita

For a classic and timeless option, you can’t go wrong with a margherita flatbread pizza. Spread tomato sauce on the flatbread, then top with slices of fresh mozzarella cheese and fresh basil leaves. Drizzle with olive oil and sprinkle with salt and pepper for a pizza that’s simple yet full of flavor.

6. Greek-inspired

Take your taste buds on a trip to Greece with this Greek-inspired flatbread pizza. Start with a base of tzatziki sauce, then add sliced cucumber, cherry tomatoes, red onion, and crumbled feta cheese. Sprinkle with dried oregano and a squeeze of lemon juice for a taste of the Mediterranean.

7. Fig and Prosciutto

For a gourmet twist, try this fig and prosciutto flatbread pizza. Spread fig jam on the flatbread, then top with sliced prosciutto, crumbled goat cheese, and arugula. Drizzle with honey for a sweet and savory combination that’s sure to impress.

8. Veggie Lover’s

If you’re looking for a vegetarian option, this veggie lover’s flatbread pizza is a great choice. Start with a base of tomato sauce, then add your favorite vegetables like bell peppers, mushrooms, red onion, spinach, and artichoke hearts. Top with a blend of mozzarella and Parmesan cheese for a cheesy and satisfying pizza.

9. Hawaiian

For those who love the combination of sweet and savory, this Hawaiian flatbread pizza is a must-try. Start with a base of tomato sauce, then top with sliced ham, pineapple chunks, and mozzarella cheese. Sprinkle with dried oregano for a taste of the tropics.

10. Taco-inspired

For a fun and flavorful twist, try this taco-inspired flatbread pizza. Start with a base of refried beans, then add seasoned ground beef, diced tomatoes, sliced black olives, and shredded cheddar cheese. Top with a dollop of sour cream and a sprinkle of chopped cilantro for a pizza that’s packed with Mexican-inspired flavors.

There you have it - 10 must-try flatbread pizza topping combinations that will satisfy any pizza lover. Whether you prefer classic flavors or want to experiment with new and exciting combinations, flatbread pizza is a versatile and delicious option. So, grab your favorite toppings and get creative in the kitchen. Happy pizza-making!
