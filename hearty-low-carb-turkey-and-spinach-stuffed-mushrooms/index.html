Hearty Low Carb Turkey and Spinach Stuffed Mushrooms


If you’re looking for a delicious and satisfying low-carb appetizer or main dish, these hearty turkey and spinach stuffed mushrooms are just what you need. Packed with flavor and nutrients, these mushrooms are the perfect option for those following a low-carb or keto lifestyle. Plus, they are incredibly easy to make and are sure to impress your guests or family members.

Ingredients

  • 12 large mushrooms (cremini or white button mushrooms work well)
  • 1 pound ground turkey
  • 2 cups fresh spinach, chopped
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/4 cup almond flour
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • Olive oil for brushing

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C). Line a baking sheet with parchment paper or lightly grease with cooking spray.

  2. Remove the stems from the mushrooms and set them aside. Place the mushroom caps on the prepared baking sheet, gill-side up.

  3. In a large skillet, heat some olive oil over medium heat. Add the ground turkey and cook until browned, breaking it up with a wooden spoon as it cooks.

  4. Add the chopped mushroom stems, minced garlic, dried oregano, and dried basil to the skillet. Cook for an additional 2 minutes until the mushrooms and garlic are fragrant.

  5. Stir in the chopped spinach and cook until wilted, about 2-3 minutes. Remove the skillet from the heat and let the mixture cool slightly.

  6. In a bowl, combine the cooked turkey mixture with the grated Parmesan cheese and almond flour. Season with salt and pepper to taste and mix well until all the ingredients are evenly incorporated.

  7. Spoon the turkey and spinach mixture into each mushroom cap, filling them generously. Place the stuffed mushrooms on the prepared baking sheet.

  8. Lightly brush the tops of the stuffed mushrooms with olive oil to help them brown in the oven.

  9. Bake the mushrooms for 20-25 minutes, or until the filling is golden and the mushrooms are tender.

  10. Remove from the oven and let them cool slightly before serving.

Serving Suggestions

These hearty low carb turkey and spinach stuffed mushrooms make a fantastic appetizer or main dish. They can be served on their own as a delicious low-carb snack, or paired with a fresh salad for a satisfying meal. They are also a great option for entertaining, as they can be prepared in advance and baked just before serving.

Nutritional Information

  • Serving Size: 1 stuffed mushroom
  • Calories: 98
  • Total Fat: 5g
  • Saturated Fat: 1g
  • Cholesterol: 31mg
  • Sodium: 162mg
  • Total Carbohydrate: 3g
  • Dietary Fiber: 1g
  • Sugars: 1g
  • Protein: 10g

Conclusion

These hearty low carb turkey and spinach stuffed mushrooms are a fantastic option for those looking to enjoy a delicious and satisfying meal while keeping their carb intake in check. The combination of ground turkey, spinach, and flavorful herbs creates a filling and nutritious stuffing that perfectly complements the earthy mushrooms. Whether you serve them as an appetizer or a main dish, these stuffed mushrooms are sure to become a favorite in your low-carb recipe repertoire. So, give them a try and enjoy a guilt-free and flavorful meal!
