Satisfying One-Pot Beef Stew Recipes for Meat Lovers


Are you a meat lover looking for a hearty and comforting meal that’s easy to make? Look no further than these satisfying one-pot beef stew recipes! Nothing beats the aroma of a simmering pot of beef stew on a chilly evening, and the tender chunks of beef paired with flavorful vegetables make for a truly delicious meal. In this blog post, we’ll explore three mouthwatering variations of one-pot beef stew that are sure to please your taste buds.

Classic Beef Stew

Ingredients:

  • 2 pounds of stewing beef, cubed
  • 2 tablespoons of oil
  • 1 large onion, chopped
  • 3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 4 carrots, sliced
  • 4 potatoes, cubed
  • 1 cup of beef broth
  • 1 can of diced tomatoes
  • 1 teaspoon of thyme
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a large pot, heat the oil over medium-high heat.
  2. Brown the beef cubes in batches until they are seared on all sides. Set aside.
  3. In the same pot, sauté the onions and garlic until fragrant.
  4. Add the beef back to the pot and pour in the beef broth.
  5. Stir in the diced tomatoes, carrots, potatoes, thyme, salt, and pepper.
  6. Cover and simmer for about 2 hours until the beef is tender.
  7. Adjust seasoning if needed and serve hot.

Spicy Chili Beef Stew

Ingredients:

  • 2 pounds of beef stew meat, cubed
  • 2 tablespoons of oil
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 bell pepper, chopped
  • 1 can of kidney beans, drained
  • 1 can of corn, drained
  • 2 tablespoons of chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon of cumin
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a large pot, heat the oil over medium heat.
  2. Brown the beef cubes in batches and set aside.
  3. Sauté the onion, garlic, and bell pepper until softened.
  4. Add the beef back to the pot and sprinkle with chili powder and cumin.
  5. Pour in enough water to cover the ingredients.
  6. Simmer for 1.5 hours, then add kidney beans and corn.
  7. Continue simmering for another 30 minutes.
  8. Season with salt and pepper before serving.

Rosemary and Red Wine Beef Stew

Ingredients:

  • 2 pounds of beef chuck, cubed
  • 2 tablespoons of oil
  • 1 onion, sliced
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 cup of red wine
  • 2 cups of beef stock
  • 2 tablespoons of tomato paste
  • 2 sprigs of fresh rosemary
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a Dutch oven, heat the oil over medium-high heat.
  2. Brown the beef cubes in batches and set aside.
  3. Sauté the onion and garlic until golden.
  4. Deglaze the pot with red wine, scraping up any browned bits.
  5. Stir in the beef stock, tomato paste, and rosemary.
  6. Return the beef to the pot and bring to a simmer.
  7. Cover and cook in the oven at 325°F for 2 hours.
  8. Season with salt and pepper before serving.

These flavorful one-pot beef stew recipes are perfect for a cozy night in or a gathering with friends and family. Experiment with different herbs and spices to customize the taste to your liking. Enjoy the rich flavors and tender meat in every spoonful of these hearty stews. Happy cooking!
