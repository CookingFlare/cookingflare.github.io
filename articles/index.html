Classic Chocolate Crinkle Cookie Recipe

If you’re a fan of chocolate, then this Classic Chocolate Crinkle Cookie recipe is a must-try! These cookies are not only deliciously chocolatey, but they also have a beautiful crinkle effect that makes them look absolutely irresistible. Whether you’re baking for a special occasion or just satisfying your sweet tooth, these cookies are sure to be a hit. Ingredients 1 cup all-purpose flour 1/2 cup unsweetened cocoa powder 1 teaspoon baking powder 1/4 teaspoon salt 1/2 cup granulated sugar 1/2 cup brown sugar 1/4 cup vegetable oil 2 large eggs 1 teaspoon vanilla extract 1/2 cup powdered sugar (for rolling) Instructions Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C) and line a baking sheet with parchment paper. In a medium-sized bowl, whisk together the flour, cocoa powder, baking powder, and salt. Set aside. In a separate large bowl, beat together the granulated sugar, brown sugar, and vegetable oil until well combined. Add in the eggs one at a time, beating well after each addition. Stir in the vanilla extract. Gradually add the dry ingredients to the wet ingredients, mixing until just combined. Be careful not to overmix. Cover the bowl with plastic wrap and refrigerate the dough for at least 2 hours, or overnight if possible. Chilling the dough will make it easier to handle and will result in a better texture. Once the dough is chilled, use a cookie scoop or a tablespoon to portion the dough into small balls. Roll each ball in powdered sugar until fully coated. Place the coated dough balls onto the prepared baking sheet, spacing them about 2 inches apart. Bake for 10-12 minutes, or until the edges are set and the centers are slightly soft. Remove the cookies from the oven and allow them to cool on the baking sheet for 5 minutes before transferring them to a wire rack to cool completely. Repeat the process with the remaining dough, making sure to cool the baking sheet before placing the next batch of cookies on it. Once the cookies are completely cooled, store them in an airtight container at room temperature for up to 1 week. Enjoy! Tips and Variations For an extra chocolatey twist, you can add some chocolate chips to the dough before chilling. If you prefer a softer cookie, reduce the baking time to 8-10 minutes. You can also experiment with different flavors by adding a teaspoon of peppermint extract or a tablespoon of instant coffee powder to the dough. These cookies make a great gift! Package them in a decorative tin or wrap them in cellophane for a delicious homemade treat. So there you have it, a simple yet delicious Classic Chocolate Crinkle Cookie recipe that is sure to satisfy your chocolate cravings. Whether you’re a beginner baker or an experienced pro, these cookies are a crowd-pleaser that everyone will love.
