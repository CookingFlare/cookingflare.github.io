Decadent Oreo Ice Cream Cake Recipe


Indulge in the ultimate dessert experience with this decadent Oreo Ice Cream Cake. This rich and creamy treat combines the irresistible flavors of Oreos and ice cream into one heavenly creation. Perfect for birthdays, parties, or any special occasion, this recipe will leave your guests begging for more.

Ingredients

  • 24 Oreo cookies
  • 1/2 cup melted butter
  • 1.5 liters of your favorite ice cream flavor (we recommend vanilla or cookies and cream)
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1/4 cup powdered sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Instructions

  1. Crush 16 Oreo cookies into fine crumbs using a food processor or by placing them in a sealed plastic bag and crushing them with a rolling pin. Set aside.

  2. In a mixing bowl, combine the melted butter with the crushed Oreo crumbs. Mix until the crumbs are evenly coated with butter.

  3. Press the Oreo crumb mixture into the bottom of a 9-inch springform pan, creating an even layer. Place the pan in the freezer for 15 minutes to allow the crust to firm up.

  4. Meanwhile, remove the ice cream from the freezer and let it soften slightly for about 5 minutes.

  5. Once the crust has firmed up, spread half of the softened ice cream over the Oreo crust, creating an even layer. Use a spatula to smooth the surface.

  6. Take 8 Oreo cookies and break them into small pieces. Sprinkle the cookie pieces over the layer of ice cream, ensuring they are evenly distributed.

  7. Carefully spread the remaining half of the softened ice cream over the Oreo cookie layer, creating another even layer. Again, use a spatula to smooth the surface.

  8. Place the cake back in the freezer and let it firm up for at least 4 hours, or overnight if possible.

  9. When ready to serve, prepare the whipped cream topping. In a mixing bowl, combine the heavy cream, powdered sugar, and vanilla extract. Whip the mixture using an electric mixer until stiff peaks form.

  10. Remove the cake from the freezer and release it from the springform pan. Transfer it to a serving plate.

  11. Using a piping bag or a spoon, decorate the top of the cake with the whipped cream topping. Feel free to get creative with your design!

  12. Crush the remaining 8 Oreo cookies into coarse crumbs and sprinkle them over the whipped cream topping, adding a final touch of Oreo goodness.

  13. Slice and serve this indulgent Oreo Ice Cream Cake, and watch as your guests’ eyes light up with delight.

Tips and Variations

  • For a twist on the classic Oreo flavor, try using different flavored Oreos, such as mint or peanut butter.
  • Experiment with different ice cream flavors to create your own unique combinations.
  • Add a drizzle of chocolate sauce or caramel sauce for an extra touch of decadence.
  • To make the cake easier to slice, run a sharp knife under hot water before cutting.

Enjoy every heavenly bite of this Decadent Oreo Ice Cream Cake. With its rich Oreo crust, creamy ice cream layers, and delectable whipped cream topping, this dessert is sure to become a crowd favorite. So go ahead, treat yourself and your loved ones to this ultimate indulgence!
