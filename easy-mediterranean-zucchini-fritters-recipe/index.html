Easy Mediterranean Zucchini Fritters Recipe


Are you looking for a delicious and healthy side dish or snack that's perfect for any time of the year? Look no further than these easy Mediterranean zucchini fritters. Made with shredded zucchini, creamy feta cheese, and a blend of aromatic herbs, these crispy fritters are a taste of the Mediterranean in every bite. In this recipe, we'll show you how to make these amazing zucchini fritters using just a few simple ingredients and minimal preparation time. Whether you're a busy home cook or a seasoned chef, this recipe is perfect for anyone looking to add some excitement to their meals. Ingredients:

2 medium zucchinis, grated 1/2 cup all-purpose flour 1/2 cup grated feta cheese 1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley 1/4 cup chopped fresh mint 1/2 teaspoon salt 1/4 teaspoon black pepper 1/4 teaspoon paprika 2 cloves garlic, minced 2 tablespoons olive oil 1 egg, lightly beaten Optional: lemon wedges and tzatziki sauce for serving

Instructions:

Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C). Line a baking sheet with parchment paper or a silicone mat. In a large bowl, combine the grated zucchini, flour, feta cheese, parsley, mint, salt, black pepper, and paprika. Mix well until all the ingredients are fully incorporated. Add the minced garlic, olive oil, and beaten egg to the bowl. Mix everything together until you get a uniform batter. Using a 1/4 cup measuring cup or a small ice cream scoop, portion out the batter onto the prepared baking sheet. You should end up with around 12-15 fritters. Drizzle the tops of the fritters with a little bit of olive oil. Bake the fritters in the preheated oven for 15-20 minutes, or until they're golden brown on the bottom and crispy on the edges. Remove the fritters from the oven and let them cool on a wire rack for a few minutes.

Tips and Variations:

To make these fritters extra crispy, you can try pan-frying them in a little bit of olive oil on the stovetop. Simply heat a large skillet over medium-high heat, add a small amount of oil, and cook the fritters for 2-3 minutes on each side, until they're golden brown and crispy. If you want to add some extra flavor to your fritters, you can try adding some chopped onions, bell peppers, or jalapeños to the batter. These fritters are perfect as a side dish or snack, but you can also use them as a topping for salads or soups.

Nutritional Information: Per serving (2-3 fritters): Calorie - 120, Fat - 6g, Saturated Fat - 2g, Carbohydrates - 15g, Fiber - 2g, Protein - 3g, Sodium - 200mg, Cholesterol - 50mg. Safety and Precautions:

Make sure to grate the zucchini using a box grater or a food processor with a shredding attachment, as this will help to remove excess water from the zucchini and prevent the fritters from becoming too soggy. Use a light touch when mixing the batter, as you don't want to overmix the ingredients and end up with tough fritters.

Conclusion: These easy Mediterranean zucchini fritters are a delicious and healthy addition to any meal. With their crispy exterior and flavorful interior, they're sure to become a favorite in your household. So next time you're looking for a tasty and healthy side dish or snack, give these fritters a try!
