Easy Vegan Breakfast Burritos: Start Your Day Right


As the morning sun rises, our stomachs start to rumble, signaling the start of another busy day. What better way to kick-start your day than with a filling, flavorful, and nutritious breakfast? For those who follow a vegan lifestyle, finding a breakfast option that meets both dietary requirements and taste buds can be a challenge. Fear not, dear readers, as we have a game-changing solution for you - Easy Vegan Breakfast Burritos!

The Magic of Breakfast Burritos

Breakfast burritos have become a staple in many cultures, and for good reason. They’re easy to make, portable, and can be customized to suit individual tastes. By incorporating vegan ingredients, we can create a dish that’s not only delicious but also cruelty-free and environmentally friendly. In this recipe, we’ll show you how to create a mouth-watering, plant-based breakfast burrito that will become your new morning obsession.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup cooked brown rice
  • 1 cup cooked black beans, warmed
  • 1 cup frozen corn kernels, thawed
  • 1 small onion, diced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 red bell pepper, diced
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • 1 teaspoon smoked paprika
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • 6 whole wheat tortillas
  • 1 cup vegan scrambled eggs (see below for recipe)
  • Shredded vegan cheese (optional)
  • Chopped fresh cilantro (optional)

Vegan Scrambled Eggs Recipe

  • 1 cup cashews
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 1/2 teaspoon apple cider vinegar
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric
  • Salt and pepper, to taste

Instructions

  1. In a blender or food processor, combine cashews, water, lemon juice, apple cider vinegar, turmeric, salt, and pepper. Blend until smooth and creamy, stopping to scrape down the sides as needed.
  2. Heat a non-stick skillet over medium heat. Pour in the blended mixture and cook, stirring constantly, until the mixture thickens and resembles scrambled eggs, about 5-7 minutes.

Preparing the Filling

  1. In a large skillet, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the diced onion and cook until translucent, about 3-4 minutes.
  2. Add the minced garlic and cook for an additional minute, until fragrant.
  3. Add the diced red bell pepper and cook until tender, about 3-4 minutes.
  4. Stir in the cooked black beans, corn kernels, cumin, smoked paprika, salt, and pepper. Cook for an additional 2-3 minutes, until the filling is well combined and heated through.

Assembling the Burritos

  1. Warm the tortillas by wrapping them in a damp paper towel and microwaving for 20-30 seconds.
  2. Spoon about 1/2 cup of the filling onto the center of a tortilla, leaving a 1-inch border around the edges.
  3. Add 1/4 cup of the vegan scrambled eggs on top of the filling.
  4. Sprinkle with shredded vegan cheese and chopped cilantro, if desired.
  5. Fold the bottom edge of the tortilla up over the filling, then fold in the sides and roll the burrito into a neat cylinder. Repeat with the remaining ingredients.

Tips and Variations

  • For an extra boost of protein, add some diced tofu or tempeh to the filling.
  • Experiment with different spices, such as chili powder or cayenne pepper, to give the burrito an extra kick.
  • Substitute the corn kernels with diced mushrooms or bell peppers for added texture and flavor.
  • Make the filling and scrambled eggs ahead of time and store them in the refrigerator for up to 3 days. Assemble the burritos just before serving.

Conclusion

Easy Vegan Breakfast Burritos are the perfect solution for a quick, delicious, and nutritious breakfast on-the-go. By combining the simplicity of a breakfast burrito with the convenience of vegan ingredients, you’ll be starting your day off right. Whether you’re a busy professional, a student, or simply a breakfast enthusiast, this recipe is sure to become a staple in your morning routine. So go ahead, get creative, and start wrapping up your own Easy Vegan Breakfast Burritos today!
