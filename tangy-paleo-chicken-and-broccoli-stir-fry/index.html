Tangy Paleo Chicken and Broccoli Stir-Fry


If you’re looking for a delicious and healthy dinner option, this tangy Paleo chicken and broccoli stir-fry is the perfect choice. Packed with protein, fiber, and flavor, this recipe is easy to prepare and will satisfy your cravings without compromising your commitment to a healthy lifestyle.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound of chicken breast, cut into strips
  • 2 cups of broccoli florets
  • 1 red bell pepper, sliced
  • 1/2 cup of coconut aminos
  • 1/4 cup of chicken broth
  • 2 tablespoons of apple cider vinegar
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon of fresh ginger, grated
  • 1 tablespoon of arrowroot powder
  • 2 tablespoons of coconut oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Sesame seeds for garnish

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Sauce: In a small bowl, whisk together the coconut aminos, chicken broth, apple cider vinegar, minced garlic, grated ginger, and arrowroot powder. Set the sauce aside.

  2. Cook the Chicken: In a large skillet or wok, heat 1 tablespoon of coconut oil over medium-high heat. Add the chicken strips and season with salt and pepper. Cook the chicken until it’s no longer pink in the center, then remove it from the skillet and set it aside.

  3. Stir-Fry the Vegetables: In the same skillet, add the remaining tablespoon of coconut oil. Toss in the broccoli florets and sliced red bell pepper. Stir-fry the vegetables for 3-4 minutes, or until they are tender-crisp.

  4. Combine and Simmer: Return the cooked chicken to the skillet with the vegetables. Pour the prepared sauce over the chicken and vegetables, stirring to coat everything evenly. Allow the mixture to simmer for 2-3 minutes, or until the sauce has thickened slightly.

  5. Serve and Enjoy: Once the chicken and vegetables are coated with the tangy sauce, remove the skillet from the heat. Garnish the stir-fry with sesame seeds for an added crunch and visual appeal. Serve the tangy Paleo chicken and broccoli stir-fry hot, and enjoy the burst of flavors with every bite.

Nutritional Information

  • Calories: 280
  • Carbohydrates: 14g
  • Protein: 30g
  • Fat: 12g
  • Sodium: 840mg
  • Fiber: 4g

Why Choose This Recipe?

This tangy Paleo chicken and broccoli stir-fry is not only a delicious and satisfying meal, but it’s also a great choice for those following a Paleo diet. The use of coconut aminos as a soy sauce alternative and the absence of refined sugars make this recipe compliant with Paleo guidelines. Additionally, the combination of lean protein from the chicken and fiber-rich vegetables makes this dish a wholesome and nutritious option for anyone looking to maintain a healthy eating lifestyle.

Moreover, the tangy and savory flavors in this stir-fry come from natural ingredients like garlic, ginger, and apple cider vinegar, providing a delightful sensory experience without relying on artificial flavor enhancers.

This recipe can also be easily customized to suit individual preferences. Feel free to add more vegetables such as snap peas, mushrooms, or water chestnuts for extra crunch and variety. You can also adjust the level of tanginess by tweaking the amount of apple cider vinegar to suit your taste.

In conclusion, this tangy Paleo chicken and broccoli stir-fry is a versatile, flavorful, and healthy dish that can be enjoyed by anyone who appreciates a good stir-fry. Whether you’re following a Paleo diet or simply looking for a nutritious and delicious meal, this recipe is sure to become a favorite in your culinary repertoire.

So, why not give this recipe a try and treat yourself to a delightful dining experience that’s both wholesome and satisfying? Your taste buds and your body will thank you!
