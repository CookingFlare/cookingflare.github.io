Apple Caramel Crisp: A Warm and Comforting Fall Dessert


As the leaves start to change and the air becomes crisper, it’s the perfect time to indulge in warm and comforting fall desserts. One such treat that captures the essence of autumn is the delightful Apple Caramel Crisp. This dessert is a delightful combination of sweet, tart apples, rich caramel, and a buttery, crunchy topping that will leave your taste buds craving for more. In this blog post, we’ll explore the art of creating the perfect Apple Caramel Crisp and how to elevate it to a whole new level of deliciousness.

Ingredients

For the Apple Filling:

  • 6-8 medium-sized apples, peeled, cored, and thinly sliced
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground nutmeg
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice

For the Caramel Sauce:

  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 6 tablespoons unsalted butter, cut into pieces
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

For the Crisp Topping:

  • 1 cup old-fashioned rolled oats
  • 1/2 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup brown sugar
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, melted

Instructions

Step 1: Preparing the Apple Filling

  1. In a large bowl, toss the sliced apples with granulated sugar, flour, cinnamon, nutmeg, salt, and lemon juice until the apples are evenly coated. Set aside while you prepare the caramel sauce and crisp topping.

Step 2: Making the Caramel Sauce

  1. In a heavy-bottomed saucepan, heat the granulated sugar over medium heat, stirring constantly with a high-heat resistant rubber spatula or wooden spoon.
  2. As the sugar begins to melt, it will clump together, but keep stirring. Be careful as the sugar will become very hot.
  3. Once the sugar has completely melted, add the butter and stir until it is completely melted and combined with the sugar.
  4. Slowly pour in the heavy cream while stirring continuously. The mixture will bubble up, so be cautious.
  5. Allow the caramel to boil for 1 minute without stirring.
  6. Remove the caramel from the heat and stir in the vanilla extract. Set aside to cool slightly.

Step 3: Preparing the Crisp Topping

  1. In a medium bowl, combine the rolled oats, flour, brown sugar, cinnamon, and salt.
  2. Pour the melted butter over the oat mixture and stir until it is evenly combined and forms a crumbly texture.

Step 4: Assembling and Baking the Apple Caramel Crisp

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. Spread the prepared apple filling in an even layer in a 9x13-inch baking dish.
  3. Drizzle the warm caramel sauce over the apple filling.
  4. Sprinkle the crisp topping evenly over the caramel-covered apples.
  5. Bake the Apple Caramel Crisp in the preheated oven for 40-45 minutes, or until the topping is golden brown and the filling is bubbly.
  6. Remove the crisp from the oven and allow it to cool for 10-15 minutes before serving.

Serving Suggestions

The Apple Caramel Crisp is best enjoyed warm, preferably with a scoop of vanilla ice cream or a dollop of freshly whipped cream. The contrast of the warm, gooey apple filling with the cold, creamy ice cream is a match made in dessert heaven. You can also drizzle extra caramel sauce on top for an extra indulgent touch.

Conclusion

Apple Caramel Crisp is the epitome of fall comfort food. The aroma of cinnamon and caramel wafting through the kitchen is enough to make anyone’s mouth water. This dessert is perfect for family gatherings, cozy nights in, or any occasion that calls for a delightful sweet treat. With its simple yet irresistible combination of flavors and textures, the Apple Caramel Crisp is sure to become a fall favorite in your recipe collection. So, roll up your sleeves, gather the freshest apples, and treat yourself to this delightful indulgence that will warm your heart with every spoonful.
