Grilled Desserts: Sweet Endings to Your BBQ Feast


Who said the grill was just for savory dishes? When it comes to summer barbecues, the grill can also be your best friend for creating mouthwatering desserts that will leave your guests in awe. Grilled desserts bring a unique smoky flavor and caramelized goodness to classic sweet treats, taking your BBQ feast to the next level. In this blog post, we’ll explore some delightful grilled dessert recipes that are sure to sweeten up your summer gatherings.

Grilled Fruit Skewers

Ingredients:

  • Assorted fruits (pineapple, mango, strawberries, and kiwi)
  • Honey
  • Cinnamon
  • Wooden skewers

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the grill to medium-high heat.
  2. Cut the fruits into bite-sized pieces and thread them onto the wooden skewers.
  3. Drizzle the skewers with honey and sprinkle with a touch of cinnamon.
  4. Place the skewers on the grill and cook for 2-3 minutes per side, until the fruits are caramelized and grill marks appear.
  5. Serve the grilled fruit skewers as is or with a scoop of vanilla ice cream for an extra indulgent treat.

Grilled Banana S’mores

Ingredients:

  • Ripe bananas
  • Chocolate chips
  • Mini marshmallows
  • Aluminum foil

Instructions:

  1. Slice the bananas lengthwise, leaving the peel on.
  2. Fill the banana with chocolate chips and mini marshmallows.
  3. Wrap the filled bananas in aluminum foil, creating a sealed packet.
  4. Place the foil packets on the grill over medium heat and cook for 5-7 minutes, until the chocolate and marshmallows are melted.
  5. Carefully unwrap the foil packets and enjoy the gooey, chocolaty goodness of grilled banana s’mores.

Grilled Pound Cake with Berries

Ingredients:

  • Store-bought pound cake
  • Fresh berries (strawberries, blueberries, raspberries)
  • Whipped cream
  • Mint leaves for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Cut the pound cake into thick slices.
  2. Place the pound cake slices directly on the grill over medium heat and cook for 1-2 minutes per side, until grill marks form.
  3. Serve the grilled pound cake with a generous portion of fresh berries and a dollop of whipped cream.
  4. Garnish with mint leaves for a refreshing touch.

Grilled Cinnamon Peaches

Ingredients:

  • Ripe peaches
  • Brown sugar
  • Cinnamon
  • Vanilla ice cream

Instructions:

  1. Cut the peaches in half and remove the pits.
  2. In a small bowl, mix brown sugar and cinnamon.
  3. Sprinkle the cinnamon sugar mixture over the cut side of the peaches.
  4. Place the peaches cut side down on the grill over medium-high heat and cook for 4-5 minutes, until caramelized.
  5. Serve the grilled peaches with a scoop of vanilla ice cream for a delightful contrast of warm and cold flavors.

Grilled desserts are a fantastic way to add a sweet ending to your BBQ feast. They are simple to prepare, showcase the natural flavors of the fruits, and impress your guests with their unique grilled twist. So, the next time you fire up the grill for a summer gathering, don’t forget to save some space for these delectable grilled desserts that will leave everyone craving for more. Happy grilling and sweet indulgence!
