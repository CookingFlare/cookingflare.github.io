Roasted Butternut Squash Salad Delight


Foreword: This delectable roasted butternut squash salad is a perfect blend of flavors and textures, making it a delight to your palate! It’s packed with vibrant and earthy flavors that will satiate your senses. This dish is versatile and can be served as a side, a main course, or in a potluck, impressing your friends and family as well as your breakfast or dinner guests.

Ingredients:

  • 1 butternut squash
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • Salt and black pepper
  • 1/4 cup of chopped fresh arugula
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley
  • 1/4 cup chopped dried cranberries
  • 1/2 cup chopped walnuts
  • 1/2 cup crumbled gorgonzola cheese

Procedure:

First, preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C). Peel and cube the butternut squash into equal-sized pieces for even roasting.

In a bowl, drizzle olive oil onto the butternut squash cubes and season them with a pinch of salt and black pepper. Mix them around so they’re evenly coated.

Place the seasoned butternut squash cubes on a parchment-lined baking sheet, spacing them out for even baking. Roast them in the preheated oven for approximately 20 to 25 minutes or until golden brown. Remember to occasionally check on and flip the roasted cubes to avoid burning.

Once the roasted butternut squash cubes are ready, set them aside to cool down.

Meanwhile, in a large mixing bowl, combine the chopped fresh arugula, parsley, dried cranberries, and walnuts. Mixing these ingredients together ensures a harmonious blend of flavors.

Add the cooled down roasted butternut squash cubes to the bowl with the mix of arugula, parsley, dried cranberries, and walnuts.

Lastly, sprinkle the gorgonzola cheese over the salad mixture. Toss them gently until they’re evenly distributed.

Your roasted butternut squash salad, full of depth and textures, is now ready to serve!
