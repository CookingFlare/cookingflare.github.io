Grilled Halloumi Salad: A Light and Flavorful Fourth of July Dish


As the Fourth of July approaches, it’s time to start planning your menu for the ultimate celebration. And what better way to embrace the summer vibes than with a light and flavorful salad? Introducing the Grilled Halloumi Salad – a dish that will make your taste buds dance with joy!

The Star of the Show: Halloumi Cheese

Let’s start with the star ingredient – Halloumi cheese. Known for its unique texture and ability to hold its shape when grilled, Halloumi is a versatile cheese that adds a deliciously salty and slightly tangy flavor to any dish. It’s the perfect cheese for grilling, as it doesn’t melt but rather develops a beautiful golden crust.

Grilling the Halloumi

To begin, preheat your grill to medium-high heat. While the grill is heating up, slice the Halloumi cheese into ½-inch thick slabs. Brush each slice with a light coating of olive oil to prevent sticking and enhance the grilling process.

Once the grill is hot, place the Halloumi slices directly on the grates. Grill for about 2-3 minutes on each side, or until they develop a nice char and become slightly softened. Be mindful not to overcook the cheese, as it can become rubbery.

Building the Salad

Now that you have perfectly grilled Halloumi, it’s time to assemble the salad. Here’s what you’ll need:

Ingredients:

  • Mixed salad greens
  • Cucumber, thinly sliced
  • Cherry tomatoes, halved
  • Red onion, thinly sliced
  • Fresh mint leaves, torn
  • Fresh basil leaves, torn
  • Lemon wedges, for serving

Dressing:

  • Extra virgin olive oil
  • Freshly squeezed lemon juice
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Method:

  1. In a large bowl, combine the mixed salad greens, cucumber slices, cherry tomatoes, red onion slices, torn mint leaves, and torn basil leaves. Toss gently to mix everything together.

  2. In a small bowl, whisk together the olive oil, lemon juice, salt, and pepper to make the dressing. Adjust the seasonings according to your taste preferences.

  3. Pour the dressing over the salad and toss gently to coat all the ingredients evenly.

  4. Transfer the dressed salad onto a serving platter or individual plates.

  5. Carefully place the grilled Halloumi slices on top of the salad, arranging them in an appealing manner.

  6. Garnish with additional torn herbs and lemon wedges for a burst of freshness.

Enjoying Your Grilled Halloumi Salad

The Grilled Halloumi Salad is not only a delicious and refreshing dish, but it also offers a beautiful presentation that will impress your guests. The combination of the smoky grilled cheese, crisp salad greens, and the burst of flavors from the fresh herbs and tangy dressing creates a harmonious symphony on your palate.

Serve this salad as a main course for a light summer lunch or as a side dish alongside grilled meats or seafood. It’s a versatile dish that complements any Fourth of July celebration, adding a touch of elegance and sophistication to your outdoor gathering.

So, fire up the grill, gather your ingredients, and get ready to indulge in the sensational flavors of this Grilled Halloumi Salad. It’s the perfect dish to celebrate the Fourth of July in style. Cheers to good food, good company, and a memorable summer!
