Korean Scallion Pancake: A Savory and Crispy Treat


If you’re a fan of Korean cuisine or just looking to try something new and delicious, you’re in for a treat with this recipe for Korean Scallion Pancake, also known as Pajeon. This savory and crispy pancake is a popular Korean dish that’s perfect as a snack, appetizer, or even a light meal. With its enticing aroma and delightful crunch, it’s no wonder that Korean Scallion Pancake has gained popularity worldwide. So, let’s dive into the details of this delectable dish and learn how to make it at home.

Ingredients

Before we get started, let’s gather the ingredients we’ll need to make this mouthwatering Korean Scallion Pancake:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 cups water
  • 1 egg
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon soy sauce
  • 4 cups chopped scallions
  • 1/2 cup thinly sliced onion
  • Vegetable oil for frying
  • Dipping sauce (soy sauce, vinegar, sesame oil, and a sprinkle of sesame seeds)

Now that we have all the ingredients ready, let’s move on to the cooking process.

Cooking Instructions

Step 1: Make the Batter

In a large mixing bowl, combine the all-purpose flour, water, egg, salt, and soy sauce. Whisk the ingredients together until you have a smooth batter with a slightly runny consistency. The batter should be thin enough to easily coat the scallions and onions.

Step 2: Prepare the Scallions and Onions

Chop the scallions into about 2-inch lengths, making sure to include both the green and white parts. Thinly slice the onion and set it aside.

Step 3: Mix the Batter and Vegetables

Add the chopped scallions and sliced onion to the batter. Gently fold the vegetables into the batter, ensuring that they are evenly coated.

Step 4: Cook the Pancake

Heat a non-stick skillet or frying pan over medium-high heat and add a generous amount of vegetable oil. Once the oil is hot, pour a ladleful of the batter mixture into the skillet, spreading it out into a round shape. Cook the pancake for 3-4 minutes on each side, or until it turns golden brown and crispy.

Step 5: Serve and Enjoy

Once the Korean Scallion Pancake is golden and crispy on both sides, transfer it to a plate lined with paper towels to drain any excess oil. Cut the pancake into wedges and serve it with the dipping sauce on the side. The combination of the savory pancake and the tangy dipping sauce is sure to tantalize your taste buds.

Tips and Variations

  • For a seafood twist, you can add small, cooked shrimp or squid to the batter before cooking the pancake.
  • If you prefer a spicier flavor, you can incorporate some chopped red chili peppers into the batter for a kick of heat.
  • Feel free to customize the dipping sauce to your liking by adding garlic, ginger, or a touch of honey for a different flavor profile.

Korean Scallion Pancake is a versatile dish that can be enjoyed on its own or paired with other Korean favorites. Whether you’re hosting a gathering or simply craving a savory snack, this pancake is a delightful choice that’s easy to make and incredibly satisfying.

So, there you have it – a simple yet incredibly delicious recipe for Korean Scallion Pancake. Give it a try and experience the wonderful flavors of Korean cuisine right in your own kitchen. Happy cooking!
