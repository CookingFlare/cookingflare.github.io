Vegan Stuffed Cabbage Rolls: A Savory and Filling Dish


When it comes to comfort food, cabbage rolls are a classic choice for many. The combination of tender cabbage leaves wrapped around a flavorful filling is a match made in heaven. However, traditional cabbage rolls often rely on meat and dairy products, making them inaccessible to vegans. Fear not, dear readers, for today we’ll be exploring a vegan version of this beloved dish that’s just as satisfying and filling.

Ingredients

For this recipe, you’ll need:

  • 1 large head of cabbage
  • 1 cup cooked brown rice
  • 1 cup black beans, cooked and mashed
  • 1/2 cup chopped mushrooms
  • 1/2 cup chopped bell peppers
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 tablespoon tomato paste
  • 2 cups vegetable broth
  • 2 tablespoons nutritional yeast
  • 2 tablespoons lemon juice

Preparation

Begin by cooking the brown rice according to package instructions. Set aside.

Cut the cabbage into wedges and remove the tough outer leaves. Blanch the cabbage in boiling water for 5-7 minutes, or until the leaves are soft and pliable. Remove from the water and let cool.

In a large mixing bowl, combine the cooked rice, mashed black beans, chopped mushrooms, bell peppers, parsley, garlic, olive oil, paprika, salt, and pepper. Mix well.

To assemble the rolls, lay a cabbage leaf flat on a work surface. Place about 1/4 cup of the rice mixture in the center of the leaf. Fold the stem end of the leaf over the filling, then fold in the sides and roll the leaf into a neat package. Repeat with the remaining cabbage leaves and filling.

Cooking

In a large Dutch oven or saucepan, combine the tomato paste, vegetable broth, nutritional yeast, and lemon juice. Bring the mixture to a simmer over medium heat.

Add the cabbage rolls to the pot, seam-side down, and cook for 20-25 minutes, or until the cabbage is tender and the filling is heated through.

Serving

Serve the vegan stuffed cabbage rolls hot, garnished with chopped fresh parsley and a side of your favorite grains or vegetables.

Tips and Variations

  • To make the dish more substantial, add some sautéed vegetables like carrots, zucchini, or eggplant to the filling.
  • For an extra boost of protein, add some cooked lentils or chickpeas to the filling.
  • Experiment with different spices and herbs to give the dish a unique flavor. A pinch of cumin or smoked paprika can add a nice depth to the filling.
  • If you can’t find fresh parsley, you can substitute with dried parsley or even chopped fresh cilantro.

Conclusion

Vegan stuffed cabbage rolls are a game-changer for anyone looking for a comforting, satisfying, and filling meal. With its combination of tender cabbage, flavorful filling, and savory sauce, this dish is sure to become a staple in your vegan repertoire. So go ahead, give it a try, and enjoy the warmth and comfort that only a delicious meal can bring.
