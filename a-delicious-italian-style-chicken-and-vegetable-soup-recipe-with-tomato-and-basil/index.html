A Delicious Italian-Style Chicken and Vegetable Soup Recipe with Tomato and Basil


When it comes to comforting and flavorful dishes, Italian cuisine never fails to deliver. This Italian-style chicken and vegetable soup with tomato and basil is a perfect example of the heartwarming and satisfying meals that Italian cooking has to offer. Whether you’re looking for a cozy meal for a chilly evening or a nourishing dish to share with friends and family, this recipe is sure to become a staple in your kitchen.

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 pound boneless, skinless chicken breast, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 2 carrots, diced
  • 2 celery stalks, diced
  • 3 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 can (14 oz) diced tomatoes
  • 6 cups chicken broth
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 zucchini, diced
  • 1 cup green beans, trimmed and cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 1/2 cup small pasta (such as ditalini or small shells)
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh basil
  • Grated Parmesan cheese for serving

Instructions

  1. In a large pot, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the diced chicken and cook until browned on all sides. Remove the chicken from the pot and set aside.

  2. In the same pot, add the diced onion, carrots, and celery. Cook until the vegetables are softened, about 5 minutes. Add the minced garlic and cook for an additional minute.

  3. Return the cooked chicken to the pot. Add the diced tomatoes (with their juices), chicken broth, dried oregano, dried thyme, salt, and pepper. Bring the soup to a boil, then reduce the heat and let it simmer for 15 minutes.

  4. Add the diced zucchini and green beans to the pot. Continue to simmer the soup for an additional 10 minutes.

  5. In a separate pot, cook the small pasta according to the package instructions. Once cooked, drain the pasta and add it to the soup.

  6. Stir in the chopped fresh basil and let the soup simmer for a few more minutes to allow the flavors to meld together.

  7. Taste the soup and adjust the seasoning with salt and pepper if needed.

  8. Ladle the Italian-style chicken and vegetable soup into bowls and serve with a sprinkle of grated Parmesan cheese on top.

Why This Recipe Works

This Italian-style chicken and vegetable soup is a delightful blend of tender chicken, vibrant vegetables, and aromatic herbs. The combination of diced tomatoes and chicken broth creates a rich and flavorful base for the soup, while the addition of fresh basil adds a pop of freshness that elevates the dish to a whole new level.

By sautéing the chicken first, we build a depth of flavor that permeates the entire soup. The vegetables add a wonderful crunch and sweetness, balancing out the savory notes from the chicken and broth. The small pasta not only adds a comforting element but also helps to make the soup more substantial and satisfying.

This recipe is not only delicious but also incredibly versatile. You can customize it by adding your favorite vegetables or adjusting the seasoning to suit your taste. It’s a fantastic way to use up any vegetables you have on hand and turn them into a hearty and wholesome meal.

Final Thoughts

This Italian-style chicken and vegetable soup with tomato and basil is a celebration of simple, fresh ingredients that come together to create a bowl of comfort and joy. Whether you’re cooking for a crowd or simply craving a nourishing meal, this soup is bound to impress with its robust flavors and vibrant colors.

So, the next time you find yourself yearning for a taste of Italy, whip up a batch of this delightful soup and transport yourself to the sun-drenched hills of Tuscany or the bustling streets of Rome. Buon appetito!
