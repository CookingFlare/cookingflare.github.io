Grilling Corn Salad: Fresh and Vibrant BBQ Side Dish


When it comes to summer barbecues, having the perfect side dish can take your outdoor gathering to the next level. Grilling corn salad is a fantastic way to bring a burst of fresh and vibrant flavors to your BBQ spread. This delightful dish combines the smoky sweetness of grilled corn with the crunch of fresh vegetables and the zing of a tangy vinaigrette. Whether you’re hosting a backyard cookout or simply looking to elevate your weeknight dinner, this grilling corn salad recipe is sure to impress.

Ingredients:

  • 6 ears of corn, husks removed
  • 1 red bell pepper, diced
  • 1 green bell pepper, diced
  • 1 red onion, finely chopped
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1/4 cup fresh cilantro, chopped
  • 1/4 cup fresh parsley, chopped
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 3 tablespoons red wine vinegar
  • 1 teaspoon honey
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

Step 1: Preparing the Corn

  • Preheat your grill to medium-high heat.
  • Place the ears of corn directly on the grill grates and cook for about 10-12 minutes, turning occasionally, until the kernels are charred and tender. Remove from the grill and let them cool.

Step 2: Cutting the Corn

  • Once the corn has cooled, stand each ear upright on a cutting board and carefully slice off the kernels using a sharp knife. Transfer the kernels to a large mixing bowl.

Step 3: Mixing the Salad

  • Add the diced red and green bell peppers, finely chopped red onion, halved cherry tomatoes, fresh cilantro, and parsley to the bowl with the grilled corn.

Step 4: Making the Vinaigrette

  • In a small bowl, whisk together the olive oil, red wine vinegar, honey, salt, and pepper until well combined.

Step 5: Combining the Salad

  • Pour the vinaigrette over the salad ingredients in the bowl and gently toss everything together until the vegetables and corn are evenly coated.

Step 6: Chilling and Serving

  • Cover the bowl with plastic wrap and refrigerate the salad for at least 30 minutes to allow the flavors to meld together.
  • Once chilled, give the salad a final toss and transfer it to a serving dish.

Serving Suggestions:

This grilling corn salad is a versatile dish that pairs wonderfully with various grilled proteins such as chicken, steak, or seafood. It can also be served as a standalone side dish or even as a topping for tacos or nachos. The vibrant colors and fresh flavors make it an eye-catching and delicious addition to any BBQ spread.

Final Thoughts:

Grilling corn salad is a celebration of summer produce and the smoky essence of outdoor grilling. The combination of charred corn, crisp vegetables, and zesty vinaigrette creates a dish that is as visually appealing as it is delicious. Whether you’re a seasoned grill master or a novice cook, this recipe is an excellent way to infuse your outdoor gatherings with the flavors of the season.

So, the next time you fire up the grill, consider adding this fresh and vibrant grilling corn salad to your menu. It’s a simple yet impressive dish that embodies the essence of summer and the joy of al fresco dining. Enjoy!
