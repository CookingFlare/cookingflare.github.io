Hearty Keto Creamy Chicken and Mushroom Soup Recipe


If you’re looking for a comforting dish that fits perfectly into a ketogenic lifestyle, look no further! This Hearty Keto Creamy Chicken and Mushroom Soup is not only delicious but also packed with flavor and nutrients. It’s an ideal choice for those chilly evenings when you crave something warm and satisfying.

Ingredients

To create this rich and creamy soup, you will need the following ingredients:

  • 2 tablespoons olive oil (or avocado oil)
  • 1 medium onion, diced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 pound boneless, skinless chicken thighs, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 8 ounces mushrooms, sliced (button or cremini mushrooms work well)
  • 4 cups chicken broth (preferably low-sodium)
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • 1 cup spinach (fresh or frozen)
  • 1 tablespoon fresh parsley, chopped (for garnish)

Instructions

Step 1: Sauté the Aromatics

Begin by heating the olive oil in a large pot over medium heat. Once the oil is hot, add the diced onion and sauté for about 3-4 minutes until it becomes translucent. Add the minced garlic and cook for an additional 1 minute, stirring frequently to prevent burning. The fragrant aroma of sautéed onions and garlic will start to fill your kitchen, setting the stage for a delicious meal.

Step 2: Cook the Chicken

Next, add the chicken thighs to the pot. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Cook the chicken for about 5-7 minutes, stirring occasionally until it is browned on all sides. The slight caramelization of the chicken will add depth to the flavor of the soup.

Step 3: Add the Mushrooms

Stir in the sliced mushrooms and cook for another 4-5 minutes until they are softened and have released their juices. Mushrooms bring a wonderful umami flavor to the dish and contribute to the heartiness of the soup.

Step 4: Pour in the Broth

Once the mushrooms are cooked, pour in the chicken broth, scraping the bottom of the pot to release any browned bits. These bits are packed with flavor! Add the dried thyme and oregano, stirring to combine. Allow the soup to come to a gentle simmer, then reduce the heat and let it cook for about 15 minutes. This allows the flavors to meld beautifully.

Step 5: Cream it Up

After simmering, it’s time to add the heavy cream. Slowly pour in the cream while stirring continuously to create a luscious, creamy texture. Let the soup simmer for an additional 5 minutes, allowing it to thicken slightly.

Step 6: Add the Greens

Finally, stir in the spinach and cook for another minute until it is wilted. This adds a lovely pop of color and additional nutrients to your soup.

Step 7: Serve and Enjoy

Taste the soup and adjust the seasoning if necessary. Ladle the hearty keto creamy chicken and mushroom soup into bowls and garnish with freshly chopped parsley for a touch of brightness.

Tips for Success

  • Choosing the Right Mushrooms: While button mushrooms are commonly used, feel free to experiment with other varieties like shiitake or portobello for a different flavor profile.
  • Make it Ahead: This soup can be prepared in advance and stored in the refrigerator for up to three days. The flavors deepen as it sits, making it even more delicious!
  • Freezing: If you want to make a larger batch, this soup freezes well. Just be sure to leave out the spinach if you plan to freeze it, as it does not reheat well. Add it fresh when you reheat the soup.
  • Customize Your Soup: You can easily add other low-carb vegetables like zucchini or cauliflower to enhance the nutritional value and texture.

Nutritional Benefits

This Hearty Keto Creamy Chicken and Mushroom Soup is not only filling but also offers numerous health benefits:

  • Low-Carb: Perfect for those following a ketogenic diet, this soup is low in carbohydrates while high in healthy fats, making it an ideal meal for maintaining energy levels.
  • Rich in Protein: Chicken thighs provide a great source of protein, essential for muscle repair and overall health.
  • Nutrient-Dense: Spinach and mushrooms are packed with vitamins and minerals, including vitamin A, vitamin C, iron, and potassium.

Conclusion

This Hearty Keto Creamy Chicken and Mushroom Soup is a delightful way to warm up during those cool evenings. With its rich flavors and creamy texture, it’s sure to become a staple in your household. Not only is it easy to prepare, but it also caters to those following a ketogenic diet, making it a perfect choice for anyone looking to enjoy comfort food without compromising their health goals. So grab your ingredients and get cooking—you won’t regret it!

Feel free to share this recipe with family and friends, and let them experience the warmth and satisfaction that this hearty soup brings. Enjoy!
