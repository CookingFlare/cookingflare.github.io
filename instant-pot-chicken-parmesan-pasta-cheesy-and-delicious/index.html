Instant Pot Chicken Parmesan Pasta: Cheesy and Delicious


Are you craving a comforting and flavorful meal that is easy to prepare? Look no further than this Instant Pot Chicken Parmesan Pasta recipe! With tender chicken, al dente pasta, and a gooey cheese topping, this dish is the perfect combination of cheesy and delicious. Plus, it’s made in the Instant Pot, which means it’s ready in no time. Let’s dive right into the recipe!

Ingredients:

  • 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 medium onion, diced
  • 3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1/2 teaspoon red pepper flakes (optional, for a spicy kick)
  • 1 can (14 ounces) crushed tomatoes
  • 2 cups chicken broth
  • 8 ounces penne pasta
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • Fresh basil, for garnish (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Start by setting your Instant Pot to the sauté function. Heat the olive oil and add the diced onion, minced garlic, dried basil, dried oregano, and red pepper flakes (if using). Sauté for a few minutes until the onions are translucent and the garlic is fragrant.

  2. Season the chicken breasts with salt and pepper, then add them to the Instant Pot. Sear the chicken on both sides until golden brown, about 2-3 minutes per side. Remove the chicken from the Instant Pot and set aside.

  3. Pour in the crushed tomatoes and chicken broth, and give it a good stir to combine. Add the penne pasta and make sure it’s submerged in the liquid.

  4. Place the chicken breasts on top of the pasta mixture. Secure the lid on the Instant Pot and set the valve to the sealing position.

  5. Cook on high pressure for 5 minutes, then do a quick release of the pressure. Carefully remove the lid once the pressure has fully released.

  6. Using two forks, shred the chicken breasts directly in the Instant Pot. Stir the pasta and chicken together until well combined.

  7. Sprinkle the shredded mozzarella cheese and grated Parmesan cheese over the top of the pasta. Place the lid back on the Instant Pot and let it sit for a few minutes until the cheese has melted.

  8. Serve the Instant Pot Chicken Parmesan Pasta hot, garnished with fresh basil if desired. Enjoy the cheesy goodness!

Tips and Variations:

  • For an extra flavor boost, you can add a splash of red wine to the sautéed onion and garlic mixture before adding the crushed tomatoes and chicken broth.
  • If you prefer a creamier sauce, you can stir in a few tablespoons of heavy cream or cream cheese after cooking.
  • Feel free to customize this recipe with your favorite pasta shape or cheese. Try using rigatoni or fusilli pasta, or swap out the mozzarella for cheddar or provolone.
  • Serve this dish with a side of garlic bread or a simple green salad for a complete meal.

Now that you have this incredible Instant Pot Chicken Parmesan Pasta recipe, you can satisfy your cravings for a cheesy and delicious meal any time. Whether you’re cooking for a crowd or just for yourself, this recipe is sure to impress. So grab your Instant Pot and get ready to indulge in a bowl of comfort and flavor!
