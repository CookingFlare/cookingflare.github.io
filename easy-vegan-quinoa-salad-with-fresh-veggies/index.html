Easy Vegan Quinoa Salad with Fresh Veggies


Are you looking for a delicious and nutritious vegan salad recipe that’s quick and easy to make? Look no further! This Easy Vegan Quinoa Salad with Fresh Veggies is the perfect dish to satisfy your taste buds and provide you with a burst of flavors and nutrients. Packed with protein-rich quinoa and an assortment of fresh veggies, this salad is not only incredibly healthy but also incredibly delicious.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup quinoa
  • 2 cups water
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1 cucumber, diced
  • 1 bell pepper, diced
  • 1 red onion, thinly sliced
  • 1 cup fresh parsley, chopped
  • Juice of 1 lemon
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Rinse the quinoa under cold water to remove any bitterness. In a medium saucepan, bring 2 cups of water to a boil. Add the quinoa and reduce the heat to low. Cover the saucepan and let the quinoa simmer for about 15 minutes or until all the water is absorbed. Remove from heat and let it cool.

  2. In a large mixing bowl, combine the cooled quinoa, cherry tomatoes, cucumber, bell pepper, red onion, and fresh parsley. Mix well to combine all the ingredients.

  3. In a small bowl, whisk together the lemon juice, olive oil, salt, and pepper. Pour the dressing over the quinoa and veggies, and toss until everything is evenly coated.

  4. Taste and adjust the seasoning if needed. You can add more lemon juice, olive oil, salt, or pepper according to your preference.

  5. Transfer the salad to a serving dish and refrigerate for at least 30 minutes to allow the flavors to meld together.

  6. Serve chilled and enjoy!

Why You’ll Love This Salad

This Easy Vegan Quinoa Salad with Fresh Veggies is not only incredibly tasty but also incredibly healthy. Here are a few reasons why you’ll love this salad:

  1. Nutritious: Quinoa is a complete protein, meaning it contains all nine essential amino acids. It’s also rich in fiber, vitamins, and minerals. Combined with the fresh veggies, this salad provides a well-rounded and nutritious meal.

  2. Easy to Make: With just a few simple steps, this salad comes together in no time. It’s perfect for those busy weeknights when you want a healthy meal without spending hours in the kitchen.

  3. Versatile: Feel free to customize this salad with your favorite veggies or add some extras like avocado, olives, or roasted chickpeas. The possibilities are endless, making it a versatile dish that can be enjoyed year-round.

  4. Perfect for Meal Prep: This salad holds up well in the refrigerator, making it an excellent option for meal prepping. You can make a big batch and enjoy it throughout the week for a quick and satisfying lunch or dinner.

  5. Refreshing and Light: The combination of fresh veggies, lemon juice, and olive oil creates a refreshing and light salad that’s perfect for warm summer days or when you crave something light and healthy.

Final Thoughts

This Easy Vegan Quinoa Salad with Fresh Veggies is a true winner. It’s packed with flavor, nutrients, and can be enjoyed as a standalone meal or as a side dish. Whether you’re a vegan, vegetarian, or simply looking for a healthy and delicious recipe, this salad will not disappoint. Give it a try and let your taste buds be amazed!
