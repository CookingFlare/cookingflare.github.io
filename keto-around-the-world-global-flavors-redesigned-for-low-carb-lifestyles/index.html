Keto Around the World: Global Flavors Redesigned for Low-Carb Lifestyles


Living a low-carb lifestyle does not mean you have to bid farewell to your favorite global flavors. With a little creativity and adapted recipes, you can still indulge in the rich and bold tastes of different culinary traditions. Dive into this unique collection of Keto Around the World, as we combine the world’s most beloved cuisines with your low-carb preferences. From Italy to Japan, and all the way to India, join us on this mouthwatering adventure and rediscover your love for international flavors while staying true to your Keto journey.

1. Italian Delights: Zucchini Noodles with Avocado Pesto

Say goodbye to carb-heavy pasta and hello to a lighter, healthier, and Keto-friendly alternative! Zucchini noodles, also known as “zoodles,” pair wonderfully with a flavorful and creamy avocado pesto sauce. This dish is a delightful fusion of Italian tastes infused with a low-carb twist.

Ingredients:

  • 3 medium-sized zucchinis
  • 2 ripe avocados
  • 1/2 cup fresh basil leaves
  • 1/2 cup pine nuts
  • 3 cloves garlic
  • 1/2 cup extra virgin olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Recipe:

  1. Use a spiralizer or a mandoline to create zucchini noodles.
  2. In a food processor, blend avocados, basil, pine nuts, and garlic until well combined.
  3. Stream in the olive oil while continuously blending the mixture.
  4. Season the avocado pesto sauce with salt and pepper according to your taste preferences.
  5. Toss the zucchini noodles in the avocado pesto sauce until evenly coated.

Serve the Keto-friendly Italian delight with your choice of protein, such as grilled chicken or seafood, for a satisfying meal that will transport your taste buds straight to Italy!

2. Japanese Whispers: Miso-Glazed Salmon with Seaweed Salad

Indulge in the decadent flavors of Japan with our Keto-friendly Miso-Glazed Salmon with Seaweed Salad. This delicious dish offers a delightful harmony of rich miso flavors with the nuttiness of seaweed, complemented by the tender, flaky salmon.

Ingredients:

  • 4 salmon fillets, with skin on
  • 3 tablespoons white miso paste
  • 2 tablespoons mirin
  • 1 tablespoon coconut aminos
  • 1 tablespoon sesame oil
  • 1/2 teaspoon ginger, minced
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic, minced
  • Seaweed salad, store-bought or homemade
  • Crushed sesame seeds for garnish

Recipe:

  1. In a small bowl, whisk together miso paste, mirin, coconut aminos, sesame oil, ginger, and garlic to create a miso glaze.
  2. Arrange salmon fillets on a lined baking sheet and use a brush to coat each fillet with the miso glaze.
  3. Bake the glazed salmon in a preheated oven at 400°F (200°C) for 12-15 minutes or until cooked through.
  4. Serve the Miso-Glazed Salmon alongside a portion of seaweed salad and garnish with crushed sesame seeds for a captivating experience inspired by Japanese cuisine.

3. Indian Inspiration: Keto Butter Chicken

Transport your taste buds to the aromatic streets of India with this scrumptious Keto Butter Chicken. The creamy, spiced tomato gravy wrapped around tender chicken pieces offer an unforgettable culinary retreat that will make you fall in love with Indian flavors all over again.

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb boneless, skinless chicken breast, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 1 tablespoon ghee or coconut oil
  • 1 onions, finely diced
  • 1 tablespoon garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon ginger, grated
  • 1 can diced tomatoes, drained
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • 2 tablespoons ghee or coconut oil
  • 2 tablespoons curry powder
  • 2 tablespoons plain yogurt
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric
  • 1/2 teaspoon cumin
  • 1/4 teaspoon cayenne pepper
  • Salt to taste

Recipe:

  1. In a large skillet, heat ghee or coconut oil over medium heat. Add onions, garlic, and ginger and sauté until softened.
  2. Add chicken pieces to the skillet and cook until browned on all sides.
  3. Stir in the curry powder, turmeric, cumin, and cayenne pepper, and cook until aromatic.
  4. Toss in the drained diced tomatoes and continue to cook until they break down and form a thick sauce.
  5. Lower the heat and gently stir in the heavy cream and plain yogurt to create a creamy and rich butter chicken sauce.
  6. Season with salt to taste and let the flavors meld together for a few minutes.

Serve the Keto Butter Chicken with a side of cauliflower rice or your choice of Keto-friendly accompaniments for a true taste of India.

Go Global, Stay Keto

The variety of Keto Around the World dishes showcases that low-carb living does not confine you to a bland and monotonous diet. This delectable mix of global flavors provides a delicious alternative while adhering to your Keto lifestyle. So, dive into the rich, bold, and diverse flavors of Italian, Japanese, and Indian cuisines with the confidence that you are staying true to your low-carb journey. Happy cooking!
