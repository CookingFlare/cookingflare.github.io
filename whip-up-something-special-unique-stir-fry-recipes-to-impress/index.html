Tangy Pineapple Chicken Stir Fry

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb boneless, skinless chicken breast, sliced
  • 1 cup fresh pineapple chunks
  • 1 red bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 yellow bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 onion, sliced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 2 tbsp honey
  • 1 tbsp rice vinegar
  • 1 tsp ginger, grated
  • 2 tbsp vegetable oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Chopped green onions for garnish

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, mix soy sauce, honey, rice vinegar, and grated ginger. Marinate chicken in this mixture for 30 minutes.
  2. Heat vegetable oil in a pan, add minced garlic, and sauté until fragrant.
  3. Add marinated chicken and cook until browned.
  4. Toss in sliced bell peppers, onions, and pineapple. Cook until vegetables are tender.
  5. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  6. Garnish with chopped green onions before serving.

Spicy Mango Shrimp Stir Fry

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 1 ripe mango, cubed
  • 1 red chili, sliced
  • 1 yellow bell pepper, sliced
  • 1/2 cup snow peas
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp sriracha sauce
  • 1 tbsp honey
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • Fresh cilantro for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Heat olive oil in a pan, add minced garlic and sliced red chili.
  2. Add shrimp and cook until pink and opaque.
  3. Toss in bell pepper, snow peas, and mango cubes. Cook for a few minutes.
  4. In a small bowl, mix soy sauce, sriracha, and honey. Pour over the stir fry.
  5. Stir well to coat the ingredients evenly.
  6. Garnish with fresh cilantro before serving.

Exotic Tofu and Cashew Stir Fry

Ingredients:

  • 1 block firm tofu, cubed
  • 1 cup cashew nuts
  • 1 cup broccoli florets
  • 1 carrot, julienned
  • 1 red onion, sliced
  • 2 tbsp oyster sauce
  • 1 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tsp sesame oil
  • 1 tsp cornstarch
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tbsp vegetable oil
  • Sliced green chilies for garnish

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, mix oyster sauce, soy sauce, sesame oil, and cornstarch. Marinate tofu in this mixture for 15 minutes.
  2. Heat vegetable oil in a pan, add minced garlic and sliced red onion.
  3. Add marinated tofu and cook until lightly browned.
  4. Toss in broccoli, cashew nuts, and julienned carrot. Cook until vegetables are tender.
  5. Adjust seasoning if needed.
  6. Garnish with sliced green chilies before serving.
