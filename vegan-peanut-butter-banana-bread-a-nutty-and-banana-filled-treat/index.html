Vegan Peanut Butter Banana Bread: A Nutty and Banana-Filled Treat


Are you ready to indulge in a delightful twist on the classic banana bread? Look no further than this Vegan Peanut Butter Banana Bread recipe. With the perfect balance of nuttiness and the natural sweetness of ripe bananas, this moist and flavorful bread will surely become a favorite in your home. Whether you’re a seasoned vegan or simply looking to explore new flavors, this recipe is a must-try.

Ingredients:

  • 3 ripe bananas, mashed
  • 1/2 cup creamy peanut butter
  • 1/3 cup coconut oil, melted
  • 1/2 cup brown sugar
  • 1 teaspoon pure vanilla extract
  • 1 3/4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup chopped walnuts or pecans (optional, for added crunch)

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease a 9x5 inch loaf pan and set it aside.

  2. In a large mixing bowl, combine the mashed bananas, peanut butter, melted coconut oil, brown sugar, and vanilla extract. Mix until well combined.

  3. In a separate bowl, whisk together the flour, baking soda, baking powder, and salt.

  4. Gradually add the dry ingredients to the wet ingredients, stirring until just combined. Be careful not to overmix, as this can result in a dense loaf.

  5. If using, gently fold in the chopped walnuts or pecans, distributing them evenly throughout the batter.

  6. Pour the batter into the prepared loaf pan and smooth the top with a spatula.

  7. Bake for 55-65 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean. Keep an eye on the bread towards the end of the baking time to prevent over-browning.

  8. Once baked, remove the bread from the oven and allow it to cool in the pan for 10-15 minutes before transferring it to a wire rack to cool completely.

Serving and Storing:

This Vegan Peanut Butter Banana Bread is best enjoyed when slightly warm, with a spread of extra peanut butter or a drizzle of maple syrup. It also pairs wonderfully with a cup of hot coffee or your favorite plant-based milk.

To store the bread, wrap it tightly in plastic wrap or aluminum foil and keep it at room temperature for up to 3 days. For longer storage, refrigerate the bread for up to a week, or freeze it for up to 3 months. Simply thaw the bread at room temperature or in the refrigerator before serving.

Final Thoughts:

This Vegan Peanut Butter Banana Bread is a delightful combination of wholesome ingredients and irresistible flavors. Whether you savor it as a breakfast treat, an afternoon snack, or a comforting dessert, its rich nuttiness and moist texture are sure to leave a lasting impression. Embrace the simplicity and satisfaction of baking this vegan delight, and share the joy of this nutty and banana-filled treat with your loved ones.

So, why wait? Gather your ingredients and let the aroma of freshly baked banana bread fill your kitchen. It’s time to embark on a delicious journey of vegan baking with this delightful recipe. Happy baking!
