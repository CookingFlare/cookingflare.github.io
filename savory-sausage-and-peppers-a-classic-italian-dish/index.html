Savory Sausage and Peppers: A Classic Italian Dish


Sausage and peppers is a classic Italian dish that is packed with flavor and brings a taste of Italy right into your kitchen. This savory dish is perfect for a family dinner or a gathering with friends. The combination of juicy sausage, colorful bell peppers, and aromatic spices creates a mouthwatering medley of flavors that will leave everyone wanting more.

Ingredients

  • 4 Italian sausages (mild or hot, depending on your preference)
  • 2 bell peppers (red, green, or a mix of both), thinly sliced
  • 1 large onion, thinly sliced
  • 3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 can (14 oz) diced tomatoes
  • 1 tablespoon tomato paste
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried basil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil

Instructions

  1. Heat the olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Add the sausages and cook until browned on all sides. Remove the sausages from the skillet and set aside.

  2. In the same skillet, add the sliced onions and cook until they start to soften and become translucent, about 5 minutes. Add the minced garlic and cook for an additional minute.

  3. Add the sliced bell peppers to the skillet and cook until they begin to soften, about 8-10 minutes. Stir occasionally to ensure even cooking.

  4. While the peppers are cooking, slice the sausages into bite-sized pieces.

  5. Once the peppers have softened, add the sliced sausages back into the skillet. Stir in the diced tomatoes, tomato paste, dried oregano, dried basil, salt, and pepper. Mix well to combine all the ingredients.

  6. Reduce the heat to low and let the dish simmer for 15-20 minutes, allowing the flavors to meld together and the sauce to thicken slightly.

  7. Taste and adjust the seasonings if needed. Serve the sausage and peppers hot with crusty bread or over pasta.

Tips and Variations

  • For an extra kick of heat, use hot Italian sausages instead of mild.
  • Feel free to experiment with different types of bell peppers. Red and green peppers provide a beautiful contrast in color, but you can also use yellow or orange peppers for a sweeter flavor.
  • If you prefer a thicker sauce, you can add a tablespoon of flour to the skillet after cooking the onions and garlic. Stir the flour into the oil until it forms a paste, then proceed with the recipe.
  • This dish can be served on its own, but it also pairs well with creamy polenta or steamed rice.
  • Leftovers can be stored in an airtight container in the refrigerator for up to 3 days. Simply reheat in a skillet or microwave before serving.

Final Thoughts

Savory sausage and peppers is a timeless Italian dish that brings warmth and comfort to the table. The combination of tender sausages, vibrant bell peppers, and aromatic spices creates a symphony of flavors that will transport you to the heart of Italy. Whether you’re cooking for your family or hosting a dinner party, this classic dish is sure to impress. So, roll up your sleeves, gather your ingredients, and get ready to savor every bite of this delicious Italian favorite. Buon appetito!
