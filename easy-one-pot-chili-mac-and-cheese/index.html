Easy One Pot Chili Mac and Cheese


Looking for a delicious and comforting meal that’s easy to make and requires minimal cleanup? Look no further than this Easy One Pot Chili Mac and Cheese recipe! This hearty and flavorful dish combines the best of both worlds - the warmth of chili and the creamy goodness of mac and cheese - all cooked in one pot for ultimate convenience.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can (14.5 ounces) diced tomatoes
  • 1 can (8 ounces) tomato sauce
  • 2 1/2 cups beef broth
  • 2 1/2 cups uncooked elbow macaroni
  • 2 cups shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1 tablespoon chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh chopped parsley for garnish (optional)

Instructions

  1. In a large pot or Dutch oven, brown the ground beef over medium heat. Add the diced onion and minced garlic, and cook until the onion is translucent and the garlic is fragrant.

  2. Stir in the diced tomatoes, tomato sauce, beef broth, uncooked macaroni, chili powder, and cumin. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Bring the mixture to a boil.

  3. Once boiling, reduce the heat to a simmer and cover the pot. Let it cook for about 12-15 minutes, or until the macaroni is tender, stirring occasionally to prevent sticking.

  4. Once the macaroni is cooked, remove the pot from the heat and stir in the shredded cheddar cheese until it’s melted and well combined.

  5. Serve the chili mac and cheese hot, garnished with fresh chopped parsley if desired.

Tips and Variations

  • For a spicier kick, add a pinch of cayenne pepper or a diced jalapeño to the chili mac and cheese.
  • If you prefer a vegetarian version, you can substitute the ground beef with cooked lentils or black beans for a protein-packed alternative.
  • Experiment with different types of cheese such as pepper jack or Monterey Jack for a unique flavor twist.
  • Leftovers can be stored in an airtight container in the refrigerator for up to 3 days. Simply reheat on the stovetop or in the microwave for a quick and satisfying meal.
