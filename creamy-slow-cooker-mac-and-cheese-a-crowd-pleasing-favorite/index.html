Creamy Slow Cooker Mac and Cheese: A Crowd-Pleasing Favorite

4 min · 656 words

There’s something undeniably comforting about a steaming bowl of creamy mac and cheese. It’s a classic dish that brings back fond memories of childhood and cozy family gatherings. And what if I told you that you could achieve that same creamy, indulgent mac and cheese with minimal effort? Yes, you heard it right! With this slow cooker mac and cheese recipe, you can effortlessly whip up a crowd-pleasing favorite that will have everyone coming back for seconds.

Ingredients

  • 16 oz (450g) elbow macaroni
  • 4 cups (960ml) whole milk
  • 12 oz (340g) evaporated milk
  • 1/2 cup (115g) unsalted butter, melted
  • 3 cups (340g) shredded sharp cheddar cheese
  • 2 cups (225g) shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup (55g) grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp onion powder
  • 1/2 tsp paprika
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Begin by cooking the elbow macaroni according to the package instructions, then drain and set it aside.

  2. In the slow cooker, combine the cooked macaroni, whole milk, evaporated milk, and melted butter. Stir well to ensure that the macaroni is coated with the creamy mixture.

  3. Add the shredded sharp cheddar cheese, mozzarella cheese, and grated Parmesan cheese to the slow cooker. Gently stir the cheeses into the macaroni and milk mixture until everything is evenly combined.

  4. Season the mac and cheese mixture with garlic powder, onion powder, paprika, salt, and pepper. Stir once more to distribute the seasonings throughout the dish.

  5. Cover the slow cooker and set it to cook on low for 2-3 hours. Be sure to stir the mac and cheese occasionally to prevent the edges from becoming too crispy.

  6. Once the mac and cheese reaches the desired creamy consistency, it’s ready to be served. You can keep the slow cooker on the warm setting if you’re serving the dish at a gathering or event.

Tips for Success

  • For an extra flavor boost, consider adding a pinch of cayenne pepper or a splash of hot sauce to the mac and cheese mixture.
  • If you prefer a golden, crispy topping, transfer the cooked mac and cheese to a baking dish, sprinkle with breadcrumbs and a little extra cheese, then broil in the oven for a few minutes until golden brown.

Why Slow Cooker Mac and Cheese?

The beauty of using a slow cooker for mac and cheese lies in the hands-off approach. You can prepare the ingredients, set everything in the slow cooker, and go about your day while the magic happens on its own. This makes it an ideal choice for gatherings, potlucks, or even weeknight dinners when you want a fuss-free meal that still delivers on flavor and comfort.

Conclusion

Creamy slow cooker mac and cheese is a timeless crowd-pleaser that never fails to satisfy. Whether you’re feeding a hungry family or entertaining a group of friends, this recipe is sure to be a hit. The rich, velvety texture and the blend of cheeses make it a truly indulgent treat that will have everyone asking for the recipe. So, go ahead, dust off that slow cooker, gather your ingredients, and let the aroma of cheesy goodness fill your home.

This slow cooker mac and cheese recipe is perfect for those who want to enjoy a comforting and flavorful dish with minimal effort. The slow cooker allows the flavors to meld together, resulting in a creamy and indulgent mac and cheese that will please a crowd. Whether you’re preparing it for a family dinner or a potluck, this recipe is a guaranteed hit.
