Tasty Keto Bacon Wrapped Asparagus Bundles


Are you in the mood for a delicious and healthy keto-friendly dish? Look no further than these mouthwatering bacon-wrapped asparagus bundles. This recipe combines the crispy, savory goodness of bacon with the fresh, tender crunch of asparagus, creating a perfect balance of flavors and textures. Whether you’re following a keto diet or simply looking for a scrumptious appetizer or side dish, these bacon-wrapped asparagus bundles are sure to impress your taste buds and leave you craving more.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound fresh asparagus spears
  • 12 slices bacon
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

Preparing the Asparagus

  1. Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C) and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
  2. Rinse the asparagus spears under cold water and pat them dry with a paper towel.
  3. Trim the woody ends off the asparagus spears to ensure that you’re left with the tender, flavorful portion. You can do this by gently bending each spear until it naturally snaps at the point where it becomes tough.
  4. In a shallow dish, drizzle the asparagus spears with olive oil and sprinkle them with garlic powder, salt, and pepper. Toss the asparagus to ensure that they are evenly coated with the seasonings.

Wrapping the Asparagus

  1. Take a bundle of 3-4 asparagus spears and carefully wrap a slice of bacon around them, starting at the bottom and spiraling upward. Place the wrapped bundle seam-side down on the prepared baking sheet.
  2. Repeat the process with the remaining asparagus spears and bacon slices, ensuring that the bundles are evenly spaced on the baking sheet.

Baking the Bundles

  1. Place the baking sheet in the preheated oven and bake the bacon-wrapped asparagus bundles for 20-25 minutes, or until the bacon is crispy and the asparagus is tender-crisp.
  2. Once done, remove the baking sheet from the oven and allow the bundles to cool for a few minutes before serving.

Serving Suggestions

These delicious bacon-wrapped asparagus bundles can be served as an appetizer, side dish, or even as a flavorful addition to a salad. Pair them with a creamy garlic aioli or a zesty lemon dip for an extra burst of flavor. The crispy bacon and tender asparagus are a winning combination that will delight your taste buds and impress your guests.

Nutritional Information

For those following a keto or low-carb diet, these bacon-wrapped asparagus bundles are a fantastic choice. Packed with healthy fats, fiber, and essential nutrients, they offer a satisfying and flavorful addition to your meal plan. Each bundle provides a delightful balance of protein, healthy fats, and fiber, making it a guilt-free indulgence for anyone looking to maintain a healthy lifestyle.

Conclusion

With its simple preparation and irresistible flavors, these tasty keto bacon-wrapped asparagus bundles are a versatile and delightful addition to any meal. Whether you’re hosting a dinner party or simply treating yourself to a special dish, this recipe is sure to become a favorite. So, gather your ingredients, preheat the oven, and get ready to savor the deliciousness of these bacon-wrapped asparagus bundles. Enjoy!
