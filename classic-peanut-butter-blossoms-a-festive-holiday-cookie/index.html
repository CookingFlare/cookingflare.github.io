Classic Peanut Butter Blossoms: A Festive Holiday Cookie


There’s something magical about the holiday season that brings out the inner baker in all of us. The warm aroma of freshly baked cookies filling the air, the joy of sharing delicious treats with loved ones, and the excitement of indulging in festive flavors - it truly is a special time of year. And when it comes to holiday cookies, one classic recipe that never fails to delight is the Peanut Butter Blossom.

The Perfect Combination

Peanut Butter Blossoms are the perfect combination of rich, creamy peanut butter and a sweet, chocolatey kiss. These cookies are not only delicious but also visually appealing, with the signature Hershey’s Kiss sitting proudly atop a soft and chewy peanut butter cookie. They are a crowd-pleaser at any holiday gathering and make a delightful gift for friends and family.

Ingredients

To make these delectable Classic Peanut Butter Blossoms, you will need the following ingredients:

  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/2 cup packed brown sugar
  • 1/2 cup smooth peanut butter
  • 1 large egg
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 3/4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar (for rolling)
  • 48 Hershey’s Kisses, unwrapped

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C) and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.

  2. In a large mixing bowl, cream together the softened butter, granulated sugar, and packed brown sugar until light and fluffy.

  3. Add the smooth peanut butter, egg, and vanilla extract to the bowl and mix until well combined.

  4. In a separate bowl, whisk together the all-purpose flour, baking soda, and salt.

  5. Gradually add the dry ingredients to the peanut butter mixture, mixing until just combined. Be careful not to overmix, as this can result in a tough cookie.

  6. Shape the dough into 1-inch balls and roll each ball in the remaining granulated sugar. Place the coated dough balls onto the prepared baking sheet, spacing them about 2 inches apart.

  7. Bake the cookies in the preheated oven for 8-10 minutes, or until they are lightly golden around the edges.

  8. Remove the baking sheet from the oven and immediately press a Hershey’s Kiss into the center of each cookie. The edges may crack slightly, but that’s perfectly normal and adds to the cookie’s charm.

  9. Allow the cookies to cool on the baking sheet for 5 minutes, then transfer them to a wire rack to cool completely.

  10. Repeat the baking process with the remaining dough until all the cookies are baked and adorned with a Hershey’s Kiss.

Tips and Variations

  • For a fun twist, try using different flavors of Hershey’s Kisses, such as Hugs, Caramel-filled, or even Peppermint.

  • If you prefer a more intense peanut butter flavor, use chunky peanut butter instead of smooth.

  • To make the cookies gluten-free, substitute the all-purpose flour with a gluten-free flour blend.

  • Store the Classic Peanut Butter Blossoms in an airtight container at room temperature for up to 1 week.

Conclusion

Classic Peanut Butter Blossoms are the epitome of holiday baking. Their timeless combination of creamy peanut butter and sweet chocolate make them a favorite among both kids and adults. So, this holiday season, gather your loved ones, put on some festive music, and whip up a batch of these delightful cookies. They are sure to bring joy and warmth to your home, and create memories that will last a lifetime. Happy baking!
