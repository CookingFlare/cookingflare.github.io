Flavorful Instant Pot Curry Recipes That Are Simply Irresistible


Curry is a beloved dish that brings together the vibrant flavors and spices of various cuisines from around the world. Whether you’re a fan of Indian, Thai, or Jamaican cuisine, there’s a curry recipe out there that will tantalize your taste buds. And what better way to prepare these flavorful dishes than with the convenience and speed of an Instant Pot? In this blog post, we’ll explore some mouthwatering Instant Pot curry recipes that are sure to become your new favorites.

1. Thai Green Curry

Thai cuisine is known for its intricate flavors and aromatic spices, and Thai Green Curry is no exception. To make this delectable dish in your Instant Pot, start by sautéing onions, garlic, and ginger until fragrant. Then, add your choice of protein, such as chicken or tofu, along with green curry paste and coconut milk. Lock the lid of your Instant Pot, set it to high pressure, and let it cook for a few minutes. Once done, garnish with fresh basil leaves and serve with steamed jasmine rice.

2. Butter Chicken

Butter Chicken, also known as Murgh Makhani, is a classic Indian curry that is rich, creamy, and packed with flavor. To make this dish in your Instant Pot, marinate chicken pieces in a mixture of yogurt, spices, and lemon juice. Sauté onions, garlic, and ginger in the Instant Pot, then add the marinated chicken along with tomato puree, garam masala, and other spices. Pressure cook for a few minutes, and finish with a touch of cream. Serve with naan or rice for a truly satisfying meal.

3. Jamaican Curry Chicken

Jamaican cuisine is known for its bold and spicy flavors, and Jamaican Curry Chicken is a testament to that. Start by browning chicken pieces in the Instant Pot, then set them aside. Sauté onions, garlic, and scotch bonnet peppers for that distinct Jamaican heat. Add curry powder, thyme, allspice, and other spices, along with coconut milk and chicken broth. Return the chicken to the pot, lock the lid, and let it cook under pressure until tender. Serve with rice and peas for an authentic Jamaican experience.

4. Lentil and Vegetable Curry

For those looking for a hearty and nutritious vegetarian option, a Lentil and Vegetable Curry is the way to go. Sauté onions, garlic, and ginger in the Instant Pot, then add lentils, diced vegetables of your choice, curry powder, and vegetable broth. Cook under pressure until the lentils are tender and the flavors have melded together. Serve with basmati rice or naan for a satisfying and wholesome meal.

5. Coconut Chickpea Curry

Chickpeas are a versatile and protein-rich ingredient that shines in this Coconut Chickpea Curry. Sauté onions, garlic, and ginger in the Instant Pot, then add chickpeas, coconut milk, curry powder, and a touch of sweetness from honey or maple syrup. Pressure cook until the flavors have infused into the chickpeas, and serve with steamed rice or warm naan.

These flavorful Instant Pot curry recipes are just the tip of the iceberg when it comes to the variety of dishes you can create. Whether you’re a fan of spicy Thai flavors, creamy Indian curries, or bold Jamaican spices, there’s an Instant Pot curry recipe out there that will satisfy your cravings. So don’t wait any longer, dust off your Instant Pot, and embark on a culinary adventure that will transport your taste buds to new heights of delight.
