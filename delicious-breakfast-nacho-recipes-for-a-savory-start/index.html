Delicious Breakfast Nacho Recipes for a Savory Start


Are you tired of the same old breakfast options? If you’re looking to shake up your morning routine with a savory twist, then look no further than these delicious breakfast nacho recipes. Nachos for breakfast? Absolutely! These recipes are a creative and satisfying way to start your day on a savory note. Whether you’re hosting a brunch or just want to treat yourself to a special breakfast, these breakfast nacho recipes are sure to impress.

Classic Breakfast Nachos

Ingredients:

  • 1 bag of tortilla chips
  • 8 oz. of shredded cheddar cheese
  • 6 slices of bacon, cooked and crumbled
  • 4 large eggs, scrambled
  • 1/2 cup of black beans, drained and rinsed
  • 1/4 cup of diced tomatoes
  • 1/4 cup of diced red onion
  • 1/4 cup of sliced jalapenos
  • 1/4 cup of sour cream
  • Fresh cilantro for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. Spread the tortilla chips in a single layer on a baking sheet.
  3. Sprinkle the shredded cheddar cheese evenly over the chips.
  4. Evenly distribute the scrambled eggs, bacon, black beans, diced tomatoes, red onion, and jalapenos over the cheese-covered chips.
  5. Place the baking sheet in the oven and bake for 10-12 minutes, or until the cheese is melted and bubbly.
  6. Remove the nachos from the oven and drizzle with sour cream.
  7. Garnish with fresh cilantro and serve immediately.

Avocado Breakfast Nachos

Ingredients:

  • 1 bag of corn tortilla chips
  • 2 ripe avocados, mashed
  • 1/2 cup of crumbled feta cheese
  • 4 large eggs, over easy
  • 1/2 cup of salsa
  • 1/4 cup of sliced black olives
  • 1/4 cup of chopped green onions
  • 1/4 cup of chopped fresh cilantro
  • Lime wedges for serving

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the broiler on high.
  2. Spread the corn tortilla chips on a large oven-safe platter or baking sheet.
  3. Spread the mashed avocado evenly over the chips.
  4. Sprinkle the crumbled feta cheese over the avocado.
  5. Carefully place the eggs over the nachos, distributing them evenly.
  6. Sprinkle the salsa, black olives, and green onions over the top.
  7. Place the platter or baking sheet under the broiler for 2-3 minutes, or until the cheese is melted and bubbly.
  8. Remove from the broiler and sprinkle with fresh cilantro.
  9. Serve with lime wedges for squeezing over the nachos.

Southwest Breakfast Nachos

Ingredients:

  • 1 bag of multigrain tortilla chips
  • 8 oz. of pepper jack cheese, shredded
  • 1 cup of cooked chorizo sausage, crumbled
  • 4 large eggs, scrambled with diced green chilies
  • 1/2 cup of black beans, drained and rinsed
  • 1/2 cup of corn kernels
  • 1/4 cup of diced red bell pepper
  • 1/4 cup of sliced black olives
  • 1/4 cup of chopped fresh cilantro
  • 1/4 cup of sour cream

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Spread the multigrain tortilla chips on a baking sheet.
  3. Sprinkle the pepper jack cheese over the chips.
  4. Evenly distribute the cooked chorizo, scrambled eggs with green chilies, black beans, corn, red bell pepper, and black olives over the cheese-covered chips.
  5. Place the baking sheet in the oven and bake for 12-15 minutes, or until the cheese is golden and bubbling.
  6. Remove from the oven and drizzle with sour cream.
  7. Sprinkle with fresh cilantro and serve immediately.

These creative and delicious breakfast nacho recipes are perfect for those who crave a savory start to their day. Whether you’re hosting a weekend brunch or simply want to switch up your morning routine, these recipes are sure to satisfy your craving for a savory breakfast. So, the next time you’re looking for a unique and flavorful way to start your day, give these breakfast nacho recipes a try!
