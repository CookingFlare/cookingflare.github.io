One Pot Spicy Cajun Chicken and Rice


Are you in the mood for a quick and delicious meal that’s packed with flavor? Look no further than this One Pot Spicy Cajun Chicken and Rice recipe. It’s a perfect combination of tender chicken, aromatic spices, and hearty rice that will satisfy your taste buds and leave you wanting more. And the best part? It can all be cooked in just one pot, making cleanup a breeze!

Ingredients

  • 1 pound chicken thighs, boneless and skinless
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 1 red bell pepper, diced
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon Cajun seasoning
  • 1/2 teaspoon paprika
  • 1/4 teaspoon cayenne pepper (adjust to taste)
  • 1 cup long-grain rice
  • 1 1/2 cups chicken broth
  • 1 can diced tomatoes, drained
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley, chopped (for garnish)

Instructions

  1. Heat the olive oil in a large pot over medium heat. Add the chicken thighs and cook until browned on both sides. Remove the chicken from the pot and set aside.

  2. In the same pot, add the diced onion, red bell pepper, and minced garlic. Cook until the vegetables are softened and fragrant, about 5 minutes.

  3. Stir in the Cajun seasoning, paprika, and cayenne pepper. Cook for another minute to toast the spices and release their flavors.

  4. Add the rice to the pot and stir until it is coated in the spice mixture. Cook for a couple of minutes to toast the rice slightly.

  5. Pour in the chicken broth and diced tomatoes. Give everything a good stir and bring to a boil.

  6. Reduce the heat to low, cover the pot, and let it simmer for about 20 minutes, or until the rice is cooked and the liquid has been absorbed.

  7. While the rice is cooking, shred the cooked chicken into bite-sized pieces.

  8. Once the rice is done, add the shredded chicken back into the pot. Stir everything together to combine.

  9. Taste the dish and season with salt and pepper as needed. Remember that the Cajun seasoning already contains salt, so adjust accordingly.

  10. Serve the One Pot Spicy Cajun Chicken and Rice hot, garnished with fresh parsley for an added burst of freshness.

Why One Pot Spicy Cajun Chicken and Rice?

This recipe is a true winner when it comes to simplicity and flavor. By cooking everything in one pot, you save time and effort in the kitchen, while still enjoying a delicious and satisfying meal. Plus, the combination of Cajun spices adds a bold and spicy kick that elevates the dish to a whole new level.

The chicken thighs become incredibly tender and juicy as they cook, infusing the rice with their savory goodness. The Cajun seasoning, paprika, and cayenne pepper create a vibrant and aromatic blend of flavors that will wake up your taste buds. And the diced tomatoes add a touch of acidity that balances out the richness of the dish.

Whether you’re cooking for a weeknight dinner or entertaining guests, this One Pot Spicy Cajun Chicken and Rice recipe is sure to impress. It’s a crowd-pleaser that will have everyone reaching for seconds.

So why not give it a try? With just a few simple ingredients and a single pot, you can create a meal that’s bursting with flavor and easy on the cleanup. Enjoy!
