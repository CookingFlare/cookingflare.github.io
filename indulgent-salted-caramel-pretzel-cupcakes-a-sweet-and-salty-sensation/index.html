Indulgent Salted Caramel Pretzel Cupcakes: A Sweet and Salty Sensation


If you’re a fan of the delightful combination of sweet and salty flavors, then these indulgent salted caramel pretzel cupcakes are sure to become a new favorite in your recipe collection. This delectable treat brings together the rich, buttery sweetness of caramel with the satisfying crunch of pretzels, all nestled within a moist and flavorful cupcake. Whether you’re baking for a special occasion or simply indulging in some self-care, these cupcakes are guaranteed to impress and delight.

Ingredients

For the Cupcakes:

  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/2 cup whole milk

For the Salted Caramel Sauce:

  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 6 tablespoons unsalted butter, cut into pieces
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • 1 teaspoon sea salt

For the Topping:

  • 1 cup crushed pretzels
  • Additional sea salt for sprinkling
  • Caramel sauce for drizzling

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Cupcake Batter:

    • Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C) and line a cupcake tin with paper liners.
    • In a medium bowl, whisk together the flour, baking powder, and salt.
    • In a separate large bowl, cream together the butter and sugar until light and fluffy. Add the eggs one at a time, beating well after each addition. Stir in the vanilla extract.
    • Gradually add the dry ingredients to the wet ingredients, alternating with the milk, and mix until just combined.

  2. Bake the Cupcakes:

    • Divide the batter evenly among the prepared cupcake liners, filling each about two-thirds full.
    • Bake for 18-20 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.
    • Allow the cupcakes to cool in the tin for a few minutes before transferring them to a wire rack to cool completely.

  3. Make the Salted Caramel Sauce:

    • In a medium saucepan, heat the granulated sugar over medium heat. Stir constantly with a high heat resistant rubber spatula or wooden spoon.
    • The sugar will form clumps and eventually melt into a thick brown, amber-colored liquid as you continue to stir. Be careful not to burn it.
    • Once the sugar is completely melted, add the butter. The mixture will bubble rapidly. Stir the butter into the caramel until it is completely melted, about 2-3 minutes.
    • Very slowly, drizzle in the heavy cream while stirring. Allow the mixture to boil for 1 minute. It will rise in the pan as it boils.
    • Remove from heat and stir in the sea salt. Let it cool down before using.

  4. Assemble the Cupcakes:

    • Once the cupcakes are completely cooled, use a small knife to cut out a small piece from the center of each cupcake.
    • Fill the holes with a generous spoonful of salted caramel sauce.
    • Pipe a swirl of your favorite frosting on top of each cupcake.

  5. Add the Finishing Touches:

    • Sprinkle a generous amount of crushed pretzels on top of the frosting.
    • Drizzle additional salted caramel sauce over the pretzels.
    • Finish with a light sprinkle of sea salt for that perfect sweet and salty contrast.

Serving and Storage

These indulgent salted caramel pretzel cupcakes are best enjoyed fresh, allowing the combination of flavors and textures to shine. Serve them as a delightful dessert at a gathering, or savor them as an indulgent treat for yourself. Store any leftover cupcakes in an airtight container at room temperature for up to 3 days, although they are likely to disappear much quicker than that!

In Conclusion

The marriage of sweet and salty flavors in these indulgent salted caramel pretzel cupcakes creates a truly sensational taste experience. The moist and tender cupcake, filled with luscious salted caramel and crowned with a delightful crunch of pretzels, is a true masterpiece of baking. Whether you’re a seasoned baker or just starting out, these cupcakes are a must-try for anyone who appreciates the art of flavor pairing. So, roll up your sleeves, gather your ingredients, and prepare to indulge in the sweet and salty sensation of these extraordinary cupcakes.
