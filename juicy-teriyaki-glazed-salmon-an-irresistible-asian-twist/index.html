Juicy Teriyaki Glazed Salmon: An Irresistible Asian Twist


If you’re looking to elevate your dinner game with a dish that’s both healthy and bursting with flavor, look no further than juicy teriyaki glazed salmon. This recipe takes the classic Asian flavors of teriyaki sauce and combines them with tender, flaky salmon for a meal that will impress your family or guests. Whether you’re a seasoned home cook or just starting out, this dish is straightforward to prepare and promises delicious results every time.

Ingredients

To make juicy teriyaki glazed salmon, you’ll need the following ingredients:

  • 4 salmon fillets, preferably skin-on
  • 1/2 cup soy sauce
  • 1/4 cup mirin (Japanese sweet rice wine)
  • 1/4 cup sake (Japanese rice wine) or dry white wine
  • 2 tablespoons honey or brown sugar
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon fresh ginger, grated
  • 2 tablespoons water
  • 1 tablespoon cornstarch
  • Sesame seeds, for garnish (optional)
  • Sliced green onions, for garnish (optional)

Instructions

Step 1: Prepare the Teriyaki Sauce

  1. In a small saucepan, combine the soy sauce, mirin, sake or white wine, honey or brown sugar, minced garlic, and grated ginger.

  2. Bring the mixture to a simmer over medium heat, stirring occasionally until the sugar has dissolved and the sauce slightly thickens, about 5-7 minutes.

Step 2: Thicken the Sauce

  1. In a small bowl, whisk together the water and cornstarch until the cornstarch is completely dissolved.

  2. Gradually pour the cornstarch slurry into the simmering sauce, stirring constantly.

  3. Continue to cook the sauce for another 1-2 minutes, or until it has thickened to your desired consistency.

Step 3: Prepare the Salmon

  1. Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C). Line a baking sheet with parchment paper or lightly grease it to prevent sticking.

  2. Pat the salmon fillets dry with paper towels and place them skin-side down on the prepared baking sheet.

Step 4: Glaze the Salmon

  1. Spoon a generous amount of the prepared teriyaki sauce over each salmon fillet, using a brush to coat the salmon evenly.

  2. Reserve some of the sauce for later use.

Step 5: Bake the Salmon

  1. Transfer the baking sheet to the preheated oven and bake the salmon for 12-15 minutes, or until the salmon is cooked through and flakes easily with a fork.

Step 6: Glaze Again (Optional)

  1. About halfway through the cooking time, brush the salmon with another layer of the teriyaki sauce to intensify the flavor.

Step 7: Serve

  1. Once the salmon is done, remove it from the oven and allow it to rest for a few minutes.

  2. Drizzle any remaining teriyaki sauce over the salmon fillets.

  3. Sprinkle with sesame seeds and sliced green onions for garnish, if desired.

Tips for Success

  • Choosing Salmon: Opt for fresh salmon fillets with firm flesh and a bright color. Skin-on fillets are recommended as they hold together better during cooking.

  • Sauce Consistency: Adjust the thickness of the teriyaki sauce according to your preference. If it becomes too thick, add a little water to thin it out.

  • Flavor Variations: Experiment with adding ingredients like chili flakes for heat or a splash of citrus juice for brightness to customize the teriyaki sauce to your taste.

  • Serving Suggestions: Serve juicy teriyaki glazed salmon with steamed rice and stir-fried vegetables for a complete Asian-inspired meal. It also pairs well with a fresh green salad dressed lightly with sesame vinaigrette.

Final Thoughts

Juicy teriyaki glazed salmon offers a delightful blend of savory and sweet flavors that make it a standout dish for any occasion. Whether you’re cooking for a weeknight dinner or hosting a special gathering, this recipe is sure to leave a lasting impression. With simple ingredients and easy-to-follow steps, you can master this Asian-inspired dish and enjoy restaurant-quality salmon in the comfort of your own home. Treat yourself and your loved ones to the irresistible taste of teriyaki glazed salmon tonight!
