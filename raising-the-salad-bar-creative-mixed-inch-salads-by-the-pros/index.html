Raising the Salad Bar: Creative Mixed-Inch Salads by The Pros


Salads have long been known as a healthy starting point for any meal, substantial enough to be their own entrée. Yet, salads have often been relegated to a reputation of being plain and boring. But this doesn’t have to be the case! The key is to elevate the salad game by turning it into a global feast with an array of flavors, textures, and ingredients.

To help kick things off, we have enlisted some of the top chefs to share their creative mixed-inch salads for the perfect blend of veggies, proteins, and unique ingredients. So, break out the bowls, and let’s get mixing!

1. Mediterranean Dream Bowl

Imagine yourself walking through an open-air market in Greece, the tantalizing smell of herbs and spices filling your senses. This salad brings those Mediterranean vibes right into your kitchen!

Ingredients:

  • Romaine lettuce
  • Cherry tomatoes
  • Cucumber
  • Kalamata olives
  • Feta cheese
  • Red onion
  • Quinoa
  • Lemon vinaigrette

To create this vibrant salad, begin by combining torn romaine lettuce, halved cherry tomatoes, thinly sliced cucumber, and chopped Kalamata olives in a large bowl, followed by cooked quinoa. Crumble some feta cheese and thinly sliced red onion over the top, then drizzle generously with a lemon vinaigrette. The tangy and sweet dressing ties the refreshing ingredients together seamlessly, and the result is an enchanting taste of Greece with each bite.

2. Korean Rice Bowl Inspiration

Move over, Ssam, this salad knocks it out of the park! A delicious harmony of Korean flavors that combines savory, sweet, spicy, and umami all in one bowl.

Ingredients:

  • Cooked jasmine rice
  • Seasoned spinach
  • Grilled chicken
  • Pickled radish
  • Cucumber kimchi
  • Sriracha almond sauce

Start this feast by layering cooked jasmine rice at the bottom of the bowl followed by seasoned spinach. Top with thinly sliced grilled chicken breast and garnish with tangy pickled radish, crunchy cucumber kimchi, and serve with a dollop of Spicy Sriracha Almond Sauce on top. The final dish is a celebration of Korean cuisine that will transport your taste buds to a whole new world!

3. Angie’s Global Garden Surprise

This adventurous salad combines a burst of flavors from around the world. Think of a delightful mix of ingredients that represent various cultures, a true symphony for the palate.

Ingredients:

  • Mixed greens
  • Roasted beets
  • Quinoa
  • Chickpeas
  • Feta cheese
  • Toasted pistachios
  • Cilantro-mint dressing

Begin with mixed greens, topped with roasted beets and cooked quinoa. Sprinkle with chickpeas, crumbled feta, and add a generous handful of toasted pistachios for crunch. Finally, finish the masterpiece with a light and refreshing Cilantro-Mint dressing drizzled atop.

4. All-American BBQ Bowl

Get ready to fire up the grill, because this all-American BBQ bowl is as sizzling as summer itself.

Ingredients:

  • Grilled corn
  • Juicy watermelon
  • Cooked black-eyed peas
  • Grilled zucchini
  • Coleslaw
  • BBQ ranch dressing

In this vibrant bowl, begin with grilled corn, fresh watermelon cubes, and cooked black-eyed peas. Add sliced grilled zucchini for smoky grilled flavor and garnish with tangy coleslaw. To hold it all together, serve with a dollop of homemade BBQ Ranch dressing. It’s the very definition of summer in a bowl.

5. Thai Basil Bowl

Embrace the bold and aromatic flavors of Thai cuisine by combining mouthwatering herbs and spices.

Ingredients:

  • Rice noodles
  • Shrimp
  • Bean sprouts
  • Crispy tofu
  • Cabbage slaw
  • Thai basil pesto

Now, let’s assemble our Thai Basil Bowl! Start with a bed of rice noodles, followed by shrimp and diced crispy tofu. Not only does tofu add protein to the dish, but its crunchy texture balances the soft noodles and shrimp. Mix in some bean sprouts for a satisfying crunch and garnish with a colorful cabbage slaw. The crowning touch: a generous drizzle of homemade Thai Basil Pesto over everything. You’ll savor each spoonful of this zesty and aromatic delight.

These enticing mixed-inch salads from around the world are a feast for the eyes and an indulgence for your taste buds. Feel free to mix things up and make each salad your own. Remember, the sky’s the limit – let your culinary creativity run wild and discover the joy of building a bowl to call your own. So, gather your favorite ingredients and friends, and let your journey through a global feast begin!
