5 Easy Soup Recipes for Beginners to Try This Winter


Winter is here, and there’s nothing like a warm, comforting bowl of soup to lift your spirits and satisfy your appetite. As a beginner in the kitchen, it can be intimidating to try new recipes, especially when it comes to cooking soups. But don’t worry, we’ve got you covered! In this post, we’ll share 5 easy soup recipes that are perfect for beginners to try this winter.

Recipe 1: Creamy Tomato Soup

This classic soup recipe is a staple for a reason. It’s easy to make and requires only a few ingredients, including canned tomatoes, onions, garlic, and heavy cream. Simply sauté the onions and garlic in butter until softened, then add in the canned tomatoes and chicken broth. Let it simmer for 20-25 minutes, then puree the mixture with an immersion blender or regular blender. Stir in the heavy cream and season with salt and pepper to taste.

Servings: 4-6

Cooking Time: 25-30 minutes

Ingredients:

  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 1 medium onion, chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 cups canned crushed tomatoes
  • 2 cups chicken broth
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • Salt and pepper, to taste

Recipe 2: Butternut Squash Soup

This sweet and comforting soup is a great way to use up any leftover butternut squash. Simply roast the squash in the oven until tender, then scoop out the flesh and puree it with chicken broth, onions, and garlic. Add in some cream and nutmeg for extra flavor and creaminess.

Servings: 4-6

Cooking Time: 40-45 minutes

Ingredients:

  • 1 large butternut squash, peeled and cubed
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 medium onion, chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 4 cups chicken broth
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • 1 teaspoon ground nutmeg
  • Salt and pepper, to taste

Recipe 3: Black Bean Soup

This hearty and flavorful soup is perfect for a cold winter’s day. Simply sauté onions and garlic in olive oil until softened, then add in canned black beans, diced tomatoes, and vegetable broth. Let it simmer for 20-25 minutes, then season with cumin and chili powder.

Servings: 4-6

Cooking Time: 25-30 minutes

Ingredients:

  • 1 medium onion, chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can black beans, drained and rinsed
  • 1 can diced tomatoes
  • 4 cups vegetable broth
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon chili powder
  • Salt and pepper, to taste

Recipe 4: Spinach and Feta Soup

This creamy and savory soup is a great way to get your daily dose of greens. Simply sauté onions and garlic in butter until softened, then add in chopped spinach and chicken broth. Let it simmer for 10-15 minutes, then stir in crumbled feta cheese and heavy cream.

Servings: 4-6

Cooking Time: 20-25 minutes

Ingredients:

  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 1 medium onion, chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 cups fresh spinach, chopped
  • 2 cups chicken broth
  • 1/2 cup crumbled feta cheese
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • Salt and pepper, to taste

Recipe 5: Roasted Carrot Soup

This vibrant and flavorful soup is a great way to use up any leftover carrots. Simply roast the carrots in the oven until tender, then puree them with chicken broth, onions, and garlic. Add in some cream and nutmeg for extra flavor and creaminess.

Servings: 4-6

Cooking Time: 40-45 minutes

Ingredients:

  • 2 large carrots, peeled and cubed
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 medium onion, chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 4 cups chicken broth
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • 1 teaspoon ground nutmeg
  • Salt and pepper, to taste

These 5 easy soup recipes are perfect for beginners to try this winter. They’re easy to make, require minimal ingredients, and are sure to warm your belly and your heart. So go ahead, give them a try, and enjoy the cozy comfort of a warm bowl of soup on a cold winter’s day.
