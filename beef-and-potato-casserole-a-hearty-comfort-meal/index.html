Beef and Potato Casserole: A Hearty Comfort Meal


There’s something special about a hearty casserole that warms the soul and brings people together around the dinner table. Today, I’m excited to share with you a delicious and comforting recipe for Beef and Potato Casserole that will surely become a family favorite. This dish is perfect for cozy nights in, potlucks, or simply when you crave a satisfying and comforting meal.

Ingredients

To start off, let’s gather all the ingredients we’ll need for this mouthwatering Beef and Potato Casserole:

  • 1 1/2 pounds of ground beef
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon of dried thyme
  • 1 teaspoon of dried oregano
  • 1/2 teaspoon of paprika
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 4 cups of potatoes, thinly sliced
  • 2 cups of cheddar cheese, shredded
  • 1 cup of beef broth
  • 1/4 cup of all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup of milk
  • 2 tablespoons of butter

Instructions

Now that we have all the ingredients ready, let’s dive into the cooking process:

  1. Preheat the Oven: Start by preheating your oven to 375°F (190°C) and greasing a 9x13 inch baking dish.

  2. Cook the Beef: In a large skillet, cook the ground beef, onion, and garlic over medium heat until the beef is browned and the onion is tender. Drain any excess fat.

  3. Season the Beef: Add the dried thyme, oregano, paprika, salt, and pepper to the beef mixture, stirring well to combine. Let it simmer for a few minutes to allow the flavors to meld together.

  4. Layer the Casserole: Place half of the sliced potatoes in the bottom of the prepared baking dish. Sprinkle half of the cheddar cheese over the potatoes. Then, spread the seasoned beef mixture over the cheese. Layer the remaining potatoes on top of the beef.

  5. Prepare the Sauce: In a saucepan, melt the butter over medium heat. Stir in the flour and cook for a minute, then gradually whisk in the beef broth and milk. Cook and stir until the mixture thickens and becomes smooth.

  6. Pour the Sauce: Pour the sauce over the layered casserole, making sure it seeps down into the layers. Cover the dish with foil.

  7. Bake: Bake the casserole in the preheated oven for 45 minutes. Then, remove the foil and sprinkle the remaining cheddar cheese on top. Return the casserole to the oven and bake for an additional 15 minutes, or until the cheese is bubbly and golden.

  8. Serve and Enjoy: Once the casserole is done, remove it from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes. Then, slice into generous portions and serve while it’s still warm. The creamy sauce, tender potatoes, and savory beef will surely delight your taste buds.

Conclusion

Beef and Potato Casserole is a timeless classic that brings warmth and satisfaction to any gathering. This recipe is versatile, allowing you to customize it with your favorite herbs, spices, or cheese. Whether you’re cooking for your family or hosting a dinner party, this comforting casserole is sure to be a hit.

So, next time you’re in the mood for a hearty and comforting meal, give this Beef and Potato Casserole recipe a try. It’s a delightful combination of flavors and textures that will make it a beloved dish in your recipe collection. Enjoy every delightful bite, and savor the joy of sharing a comforting meal with your loved ones. Cheers to the simple pleasures of home cooking!
