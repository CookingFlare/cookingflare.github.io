Springtime Scones and Muffins: Easy Recipes for a Delicious Morning

3 min · 587 words

Spring is a season of renewal and vibrant flavors. It’s the perfect time to enjoy a leisurely morning with a cup of coffee or tea and indulge in some freshly baked goodies. In this blog post, we will share two delightful recipes for springtime scones and muffins that are easy to make and will undoubtedly brighten up your morning. So put on your apron and let’s get baking!

Lemon Blueberry Scones

Preparation time: 15 minutes
Baking time: 20 minutes
Yield: 8 scones

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 tablespoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, cold and cubed
  • 1/2 cup buttermilk
  • 1 large egg
  • Zest of 1 lemon
  • 1 cup fresh blueberries

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 425°F (220°C) and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
  2. In a large mixing bowl, whisk together the flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt.
  3. Add the cold, cubed butter to the dry ingredients. Using a pastry cutter or your fingers, cut the butter into the flour mixture until it resembles coarse crumbs.
  4. In a separate bowl, whisk together the buttermilk, egg, and lemon zest. Pour the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients and stir until just combined.
  5. Gently fold in the fresh blueberries, being careful not to overmix the dough.
  6. Transfer the dough onto a floured surface and shape it into a circle, about 1 inch thick. Cut the circle into 8 wedges.
  7. Place the scones onto the prepared baking sheet, leaving some space between each one. Bake for 18-20 minutes or until golden brown.
  8. Allow the scones to cool on a wire rack before serving. Enjoy with a dollop of clotted cream or lemon curd.

Raspberry Almond Muffins

Preparation time: 10 minutes
Baking time: 25 minutes
Yield: 12 muffins

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, melted and cooled
  • 1 cup buttermilk
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 teaspoon almond extract
  • 1 cup fresh raspberries
  • Sliced almonds for topping

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C) and line a muffin tin with paper liners.
  2. In a large mixing bowl, whisk together the flour, sugar, baking powder, baking soda, and salt.
  3. In a separate bowl, whisk together the melted butter, buttermilk, eggs, and almond extract.
  4. Pour the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients and stir until just combined.
  5. Gently fold in the fresh raspberries, being careful not to crush them.
  6. Divide the batter evenly among the prepared muffin cups, filling each one about three-quarters full. Sprinkle some sliced almonds on top for added crunch.
  7. Bake for 20-25 minutes or until a toothpick inserted into the center of a muffin comes out clean.
  8. Allow the muffins to cool in the tin for a few minutes before transferring them to a wire rack to cool completely.

Final Thoughts

These springtime scones and muffins are a delightful way to start your day. The zesty lemon and juicy blueberries in the scones provide a burst of flavor, while the sweet raspberries and almond extract in the muffins create a heavenly combination. Whether you’re enjoying a leisurely weekend breakfast or need a quick grab-and-go snack, these recipes are sure to satisfy your cravings.

So why not treat yourself to a delicious morning? Gather the ingredients, follow the simple instructions, and let the aroma of freshly baked goodness fill your kitchen. Embrace the joys of spring with these delightful scones and muffins. Happy baking!
