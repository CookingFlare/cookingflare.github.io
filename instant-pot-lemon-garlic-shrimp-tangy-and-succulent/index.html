Instant Pot Lemon Garlic Shrimp: Tangy and Succulent


If you’re a fan of seafood and looking for a quick and flavorful dish to whip up for dinner, then Instant Pot Lemon Garlic Shrimp is the perfect recipe for you. This dish is not only easy to prepare, but it’s also bursting with tangy and succulent flavors that will leave your taste buds wanting more. Let’s dive into the details of how to create this delightful dish using your Instant Pot.

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound of large shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 4 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1/4 cup of fresh lemon juice
  • 1/4 cup of chicken broth
  • 2 tablespoons of unsalted butter
  • 1 tablespoon of olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon of dried oregano
  • 1/2 teaspoon of red pepper flakes
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the Shrimp: Start by ensuring that your shrimp are peeled and deveined. Pat them dry with a paper towel and season with a pinch of salt and pepper.

  2. Saute the Garlic: Set your Instant Pot to the saute function and add the olive oil. Once the oil is hot, add the minced garlic and saute for about 30 seconds until fragrant.

  3. Deglaze the Pot: Pour in the chicken broth and use a wooden spoon to deglaze the bottom of the Instant Pot, ensuring that no garlic bits are stuck to the bottom.

  4. Pressure Cook the Shrimp: Place the seasoned shrimp into the Instant Pot on top of the garlic and broth. Add the lemon juice, butter, dried oregano, and red pepper flakes. Give it a gentle stir to combine all the ingredients.

  5. Pressure Cook: Lock the lid in place and set the Instant Pot to manual high pressure for 1 minute. Once the cooking time is complete, perform a quick pressure release.

  6. Finish and Serve: Carefully remove the lid and give the shrimp a good stir. The shrimp should be opaque and pink, indicating that they are perfectly cooked. Serve the shrimp and sauce over cooked pasta, rice, or with a side of crusty bread. Garnish with fresh parsley for a vibrant touch.

Why Choose Instant Pot Lemon Garlic Shrimp?

The Instant Pot is a game-changer when it comes to cooking shrimp. Not only does it lock in the flavors, but it also ensures that the shrimp are perfectly cooked in just a fraction of the time it would take using traditional methods. The combination of zesty lemon, pungent garlic, and a hint of heat from the red pepper flakes creates a symphony of flavors that will elevate your dinner to a whole new level.

The simplicity of this recipe makes it an ideal choice for both weeknight dinners and special occasions. Whether you’re cooking for yourself, your family, or entertaining guests, Instant Pot Lemon Garlic Shrimp is a versatile dish that is sure to impress.

So, next time you’re craving a seafood dish that’s both tangy and succulent, reach for your Instant Pot and give this recipe a try. You won’t be disappointed by the burst of flavors that await you in every bite. Cheers to delightful dining experiences with minimal effort!
