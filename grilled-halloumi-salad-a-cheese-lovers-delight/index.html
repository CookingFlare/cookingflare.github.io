Grilled Halloumi Salad: A Cheese Lover's Delight


Are you a cheese aficionado plagued by a desire to explore new and irresistible culinary creations? Look no further! Introducing Grilled Halloumi Salad, a divine delight for those who love cheeses. With its crispy, caramelized exterior and a wonderfully melty interior, this appetizer will win over the hearts of both cheese lovers and skeptics alike.

Halloumi, a traditional Cyprian cheese made from goat’s and sheep’s milk, is known for its incredible ability to withstand high temperatures without losing its shape or becoming rubber-like. This allows it to form those delightful, golden-brown grill marks, which recently found themselves à la mode in culinary enthusiasts’ kitchens.

To prepare this heavenly Grilled Halloumi Salad, follow these simple and scrumptious steps:

Ingredients

For the dressed greens:

  • 6 cups fresh arugula
  • 2 cups cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1 medium cucumber, thinly sliced
  • 1/2 red onion, sliced thinly
  • 1/2 cup Kalamata olives, pitted
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • Naturally, a squeeze of lemon

For the mouthwatering halloumi:

  • 1 pound halloumi cheese, cubed
  • 3 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil
  • Zest and juice of 1 large lemon
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • Salt and pepper, to taste

Directions

  1. Commence by preparing the dressed greens. In a large bowl, gently mix together fresh arugula, cherry tomatoes, cucumber slices, and thinly sliced red onion. Season with a dash of salt and pepper, followed by a refreshing squeeze of lemon.

  2. In an effort to elevate our star ingredient – the halloumi – create a flavorful marinade by whisking together extra virgin olive oil, lemon zest and juice, ground cumin, salt, and pepper in a small bowl.

  3. Coat the cubed halloumi generously with this zesty concoction. Optionally, let the cheese marinate for an hour in the refrigerator for deeper flavors.

  4. Preheat a grill or grill pan over medium-high heat. Ensure it’s adequately oiled to prevent the halloumi from sticking – waxy or undercooked pieces are the bane of any culinary creation!

  5. Grill the halloumi for approximately 3-4 minutes on each side, taking care to avoid overcooking. The cheese should sport a delectable, golden-brown crust with the most tantalizing grill marks.

  6. Once the halloumi’s reached its perfect, slightly smoky stage, allow it to cool for a few moments. By doing so, you ensure a delightful, slightly gooey interior that contrasts with the crispy exterior.

  7. Gently fold the perfectly grilled halloumi into the bed of greens you so meticulously prepared. Scatter some Kalamata olives over the salad for a tangy, briny touch of Mediterranean flair.

  8. Serve your Grilled Halloumi Salad immediately, accompanied by crusty bread to scoop up the vinaigrette or even more cheesy delights.

Prepare your palate for an exquisitely cheesy, scrumptious adventure! Grilled Halloumi Salad sets itself apart from your garden-variety salad. Its unique combination of flavors creates a savory, smoky, and gooey spectacle that will make your taste buds dance with delight. Enjoy cool summer evenings with this unforgettable salad, and create everlasting memories with friends and family.
