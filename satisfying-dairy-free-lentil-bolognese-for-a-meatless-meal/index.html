Satisfying Dairy-Free Lentil Bolognese for a Meatless Meal

3 min · 517 words

Looking for a hearty, flavorful, and dairy-free meal that’s perfect for a meatless night? This Satisfying Dairy-Free Lentil Bolognese is just what you need. Packed with protein-rich lentils, aromatic herbs, and a rich tomato sauce, this dish is a satisfying and nutritious alternative to traditional meat-based Bolognese. Whether you’re a committed vegan, a flexitarian, or just looking to incorporate more plant-based meals into your diet, this recipe is sure to become a staple in your kitchen.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups dried brown or green lentils
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 3 garlic cloves, minced
  • 2 carrots, finely diced
  • 2 celery stalks, finely diced
  • 1 red bell pepper, finely diced
  • 1 can (28 oz) crushed tomatoes
  • 1 can (6 oz) tomato paste
  • 1 cup vegetable broth
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried thyme
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 12 oz spaghetti or pasta of your choice
  • Fresh basil, for garnish

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Lentils: Rinse the lentils under cold water and drain. In a large pot, bring 4 cups of water to a boil. Add the lentils and simmer for 20-25 minutes, or until tender but not mushy. Drain any excess water and set aside.

  2. Sauté the Aromatics: In a large skillet, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the chopped onion and sauté for 3-4 minutes until translucent. Add the minced garlic and cook for an additional 1 minute. Add the diced carrots, celery, and red bell pepper, and sauté for 5-7 minutes until the vegetables are softened.

  3. Prepare the Sauce: Stir in the crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, and vegetable broth. Add the dried oregano, basil, thyme, salt, and pepper. Bring the sauce to a simmer, then reduce the heat and let it simmer for 15-20 minutes, stirring occasionally.

  4. Combine the Lentils and Sauce: Add the cooked lentils to the skillet with the sauce and stir to combine. Let the lentil Bolognese simmer for an additional 10 minutes to allow the flavors to meld together.

  5. Cook the Pasta: While the Bolognese simmers, cook the pasta according to the package instructions. Drain the pasta and set aside.

  6. Serve and Enjoy: To serve, spoon the lentil Bolognese over the cooked pasta. Garnish with fresh basil and a sprinkle of vegan parmesan cheese if desired.

Tips and Variations

  • For an extra kick of flavor, add a pinch of red pepper flakes or a splash of balsamic vinegar to the Bolognese sauce.
  • Feel free to customize the vegetable mix to include your favorites, such as mushrooms or zucchini.
  • This Bolognese sauce can be made in advance and stored in the refrigerator for up to 3 days or frozen for up to 3 months. Simply reheat it gently on the stove, adding a splash of water if needed.

Final Thoughts

This Satisfying Dairy-Free Lentil Bolognese is a versatile and comforting dish that’s perfect for a cozy night in or for entertaining guests. It’s a great way to showcase the deliciousness of plant-based cooking without compromising on flavor or satisfaction. Whether you’re a seasoned plant-based eater or just beginning to explore the world of meatless meals, this recipe is a must-try. So, grab your apron and get ready to savor every mouthful of this hearty and wholesome Lentil Bolognese!
