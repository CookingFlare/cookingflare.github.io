Decadent Chocolate Hazelnut Brownies: A Nutty Chocolate Indulgence


If you’re a fan of rich, indulgent desserts, then you’re in for a treat with this recipe for Decadent Chocolate Hazelnut Brownies. These brownies are a heavenly combination of velvety chocolate and crunchy hazelnuts, creating a truly delightful sensory experience with every bite. Whether you’re a seasoned baker or a novice in the kitchen, this recipe is sure to impress and satisfy your chocolate cravings.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup (2 sticks) unsalted butter
  • 2 cups granulated sugar
  • 4 large eggs
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup chopped hazelnuts
  • 1 cup semisweet chocolate chips

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven: Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease a 9x13-inch baking pan and set it aside.

  2. Melt the butter: In a microwave-safe bowl, melt the butter in the microwave or on the stovetop. Once melted, allow it to cool slightly.

  3. Mix the wet ingredients: In a large mixing bowl, combine the melted butter and granulated sugar. Mix well until the sugar is fully incorporated into the butter. Add the eggs, one at a time, mixing well after each addition. Stir in the vanilla extract.

  4. Add the dry ingredients: Sift the flour, cocoa powder, and salt into the wet ingredients. Gently fold the dry ingredients into the wet mixture until just combined. Be careful not to overmix.

  5. Incorporate the hazelnuts and chocolate chips: Gently fold in the chopped hazelnuts and semisweet chocolate chips, reserving a small amount of each for sprinkling on top of the brownie batter.

  6. Bake the brownies: Pour the brownie batter into the prepared baking pan, spreading it evenly. Sprinkle the reserved hazelnuts and chocolate chips on top of the batter. Bake in the preheated oven for 30-35 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out with a few moist crumbs.

  7. Cool and serve: Allow the brownies to cool completely in the pan before slicing into squares. These brownies are best enjoyed with a tall glass of cold milk or a scoop of vanilla ice cream.

Tips for Success

  • Use high-quality cocoa powder for an intense chocolate flavor.
  • Toast the hazelnuts before adding them to the brownie batter for an extra nutty aroma.
  • Don’t overmix the batter to ensure a fudgy texture in the finished brownies.
  • For an added touch of decadence, drizzle melted chocolate or sprinkle powdered sugar on top of the cooled brownies before serving.

The Ultimate Chocolate Indulgence

These Decadent Chocolate Hazelnut Brownies are a celebration of all things chocolate and nutty. With their fudgy texture, deep cocoa flavor, and satisfying crunch of hazelnuts, they are a true indulgence for any chocolate lover. Whether you’re baking for a special occasion or simply craving a sweet treat, these brownies are guaranteed to impress and delight. So, roll up your sleeves, preheat your oven, and get ready to experience the ultimate chocolate indulgence with these delightful brownies!
