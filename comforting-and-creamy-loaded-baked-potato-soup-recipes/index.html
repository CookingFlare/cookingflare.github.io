Comforting and Creamy Loaded Baked Potato Soup Recipes


There’s nothing quite like a warm bowl of comforting and creamy loaded baked potato soup to cozy up with on a chilly day. This classic soup is packed with the flavors of hearty baked potatoes, crispy bacon, melted cheese, and just the right amount of seasoning. Whether you’re looking for a quick weeknight dinner or a crowd-pleasing dish for a gathering, these loaded baked potato soup recipes are sure to satisfy your cravings.

Classic Loaded Baked Potato Soup

Ingredients:

  • 4 large baking potatoes
  • 6 slices bacon, cooked and crumbled
  • 1 medium onion, diced
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 4 cups chicken broth
  • 2 cups whole milk
  • 1 cup sour cream
  • 1 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • Chopped green onions, for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 400°F (200°C). Wash the potatoes and pierce them with a fork. Place them on a baking sheet and bake for about 1 hour or until tender. Let them cool slightly before handling.
  2. In a large pot, cook the bacon until crispy. Remove the bacon from the pot and set aside, leaving the bacon grease in the pot.
  3. In the same pot, sauté the onion and garlic until softened and fragrant.
  4. Peel the baked potatoes and roughly chop them into small pieces.
  5. Add the chopped potatoes, chicken broth, and milk to the pot. Bring to a simmer and let it cook for about 15 minutes to allow the flavors to meld together.
  6. Using an immersion blender or a regular blender, puree the soup until smooth. If using a regular blender, be sure to blend in batches to avoid any accidents.
  7. Return the soup to the pot and stir in the sour cream and shredded cheddar cheese until melted and creamy. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  8. Serve the soup hot, topped with crumbled bacon, shredded cheese, and chopped green onions.

Loaded Baked Potato Soup with a Twist

Looking to switch things up and add a unique twist to your loaded baked potato soup? Try this recipe that incorporates some unexpected flavors for a delightful surprise.

Ingredients:

  • 4 large baking potatoes
  • 6 slices bacon, cooked and crumbled
  • 1 medium onion, diced
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 4 cups chicken broth
  • 2 cups whole milk
  • 1 cup sour cream
  • 1 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1 cup frozen corn kernels
  • 1 teaspoon smoked paprika
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • Chopped green onions, for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Follow the same steps as the classic loaded baked potato soup recipe until step 4.
  2. Instead of just adding the chopped potatoes, also add the frozen corn kernels to the pot.
  3. Continue with the remaining steps, blending the soup until smooth and adding the sour cream and shredded cheddar cheese.
  4. Once the soup is creamy and smooth, sprinkle in the smoked paprika, salt, and pepper. Stir well to incorporate the flavors.
  5. Serve the soup hot, topped with crumbled bacon, shredded cheese, and chopped green onions.

Final Thoughts

These comforting and creamy loaded baked potato soup recipes are perfect for any occasion. Whether you stick to the classic version or try the one with a twist, you’re in for a treat. The rich flavors and velvety texture of these soups will warm your soul and leave you feeling satisfied. So grab a bowl, garnish it with your favorite toppings, and indulge in the ultimate comfort food experience.
