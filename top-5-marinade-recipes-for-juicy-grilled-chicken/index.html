Top 5 Marinade Recipes for Juicy Grilled Chicken


Grilled chicken is a versatile and delicious dish that can be enjoyed year-round. To take your grilled chicken to the next level, marinating the chicken before grilling is key. Marinades infuse the chicken with flavor, tenderize the meat, and help keep it juicy. In this blog post, we will share the top 5 marinade recipes for juicy grilled chicken. These marinades are easy to make and will elevate your grilled chicken game. Let’s get started!

1. Lemon Herb Marinade

Ingredients:

  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons lemon juice
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, whisk together the olive oil, lemon juice, minced garlic, dried oregano, dried thyme, salt, and pepper.
  2. Place the chicken in a resealable bag and pour the marinade over it.
  3. Seal the bag and massage the marinade into the chicken to ensure it is evenly coated.
  4. Refrigerate for at least 2 hours or overnight.
  5. Preheat your grill to medium-high heat.
  6. Grill the chicken for 5-6 minutes on each side or until cooked through [1].

2. Teriyaki Marinade

Ingredients:

  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons honey
  • 2 tablespoons rice vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon sesame oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon grated ginger
  • 1/4 teaspoon red pepper flakes (optional)

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, whisk together the soy sauce, honey, rice vinegar, sesame oil, minced garlic, grated ginger, and red pepper flakes (if using).
  2. Place the chicken in a resealable bag and pour the marinade over it.
  3. Seal the bag and massage the marinade into the chicken to ensure it is evenly coated.
  4. Refrigerate for at least 1 hour or overnight.
  5. Preheat your grill to medium-high heat.
  6. Grill the chicken for 5-6 minutes on each side or until cooked through [2].

3. Greek Yogurt Marinade

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup Greek yogurt
  • 2 tablespoons lemon juice
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, whisk together the Greek yogurt, lemon juice, minced garlic, dried oregano, paprika, salt, and pepper.
  2. Place the chicken in a resealable bag and pour the marinade over it.
  3. Seal the bag and massage the marinade into the chicken to ensure it is evenly coated.
  4. Refrigerate for at least 2 hours or overnight.
  5. Preheat your grill to medium-high heat.
  6. Grill the chicken for 5-6 minutes on each side or until cooked through [3].

4. Honey Mustard Marinade

Ingredients:

  • 1/4 cup Dijon mustard
  • 2 tablespoons honey
  • 2 tablespoons apple cider vinegar
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, whisk together the Dijon mustard, honey, apple cider vinegar, minced garlic, dried thyme, salt, and pepper.
  2. Place the chicken in a resealable bag and pour the marinade over it.
  3. Seal the bag and massage the marinade into the chicken to ensure it is evenly coated.
  4. Refrigerate for at least 1 hour or overnight.
  5. Preheat your grill to medium-high heat.
  6. Grill the chicken for 5-6 minutes on each side or until cooked through [4].

5. Spicy Chipotle Marinade

Ingredients:

  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons lime juice
  • 2 chipotle peppers in adobo sauce, minced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1/2 teaspoon smoked paprika
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, whisk together the olive oil, lime juice, minced chipotle peppers, minced garlic, ground cumin, smoked paprika, salt, and pepper.
  2. Place the chicken in a resealable bag and pour the marinade over it.
  3. Seal the bag and massage the marinade into the chicken to ensure it is evenly coated.
  4. Refrigerate for at least 2 hours or overnight.
  5. Preheat your grill to medium-high heat.
  6. Grill the chicken for 5-6 minutes on each side or until cooked through [5].

These marinades will take your grilled chicken to the next level, ensuring it is juicy, flavorful, and delicious. Experiment with different marinades to find your favorite. Remember to let the chicken rest for a few minutes before slicing and serving to allow the juices to redistribute. Enjoy your juicy grilled chicken with your favorite sides and salads!

Happy grilling!

Note: The marinade recipes provided are for a general guideline. Feel free to adjust the ingredients and measurements to suit your taste preferences.
