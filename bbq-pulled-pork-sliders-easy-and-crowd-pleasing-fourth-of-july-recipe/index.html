BBQ Pulled Pork Sliders: Easy and Crowd-Pleasing Fourth of July Recipe


Summer is here, and that means it’s time to fire up the grill and enjoy some delicious BBQ. If you’re planning a Fourth of July celebration or just looking for a tasty recipe to enjoy with family and friends, these BBQ pulled pork sliders are a must-try. They are easy to make, packed with flavor, and perfect for feeding a crowd. Let’s dive into this mouthwatering recipe that will have everyone coming back for seconds.

Ingredients

For the Pulled Pork:

  • 4 lbs pork shoulder
  • 1 cup BBQ sauce
  • 1/2 cup apple cider vinegar
  • 1/4 cup brown sugar
  • 1 tablespoon garlic powder
  • 1 tablespoon onion powder
  • 1 tablespoon smoked paprika
  • 1 teaspoon cayenne pepper
  • Salt and pepper to taste

For the Sliders:

  • 12 slider buns
  • Coleslaw (optional, for topping)

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Pork:

    • In a small bowl, mix the garlic powder, onion powder, smoked paprika, cayenne pepper, brown sugar, salt, and pepper. Rub the spice mixture all over the pork shoulder, ensuring it’s well coated. Place the pork shoulder in the slow cooker.

  2. Cook the Pork:

    • In a separate bowl, mix the BBQ sauce and apple cider vinegar. Pour the mixture over the pork shoulder in the slow cooker. Cover and cook on low for 8-10 hours, or on high for 4-6 hours, until the pork is tender and easy to shred.

  3. Shred the Pork:

    • Once the pork is cooked, remove it from the slow cooker and shred it using two forks. Return the shredded pork to the slow cooker and toss it in the flavorful juices.

  4. Assemble the Sliders:

    • Toast the slider buns lightly. Place a generous amount of the BBQ pulled pork on the bottom half of each bun. Top with coleslaw if desired, and cover with the top half of the bun.

  5. Serve and Enjoy:

    • Arrange the sliders on a platter and watch as they disappear within minutes. These BBQ pulled pork sliders are sure to be a hit at any Fourth of July gathering.

Tips and Variations

  • Sauce it Up: Feel free to experiment with different BBQ sauce flavors to customize the taste of the pulled pork. Whether it’s a smoky, sweet, or spicy sauce, it can take this recipe to a whole new level.
  • Coleslaw Crunch: Adding a crunchy coleslaw topping to the sliders provides a refreshing contrast to the rich, tender pork. It’s a fantastic way to add some texture and brightness to the dish.

Conclusion

This Fourth of July, impress your guests with these irresistible BBQ pulled pork sliders. The tender, flavorful pork combined with the soft slider buns is a winning combination that will have everyone reaching for seconds. Whether you’re hosting a backyard BBQ or a casual get-together, this easy recipe is guaranteed to be a crowd-pleaser. So, fire up the grill, get the pork shoulder ready, and treat your friends and family to a feast they won’t soon forget!

Remember, the key to successful BBQ pulled pork sliders lies in the slow cooking process, allowing the flavors to meld together and the pork to become incredibly tender. With a little patience and a lot of love, you’ll be rewarded with sliders that are bursting with smoky, savory goodness. Enjoy the celebration and the delicious food this Fourth of July!
