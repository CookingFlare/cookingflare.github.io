Chickpea and Brown Rice Curry: A Protein-Packed Meal


Looking for a hearty and nutritious meal that’s packed with protein and flavor? Look no further than this delicious Chickpea and Brown Rice Curry recipe. This vegetarian dish is not only satisfying and delicious but also incredibly easy to make. Whether you’re a seasoned chef or just starting out in the kitchen, this recipe is sure to become a favorite in your meal rotation.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup brown rice
  • 2 cups vegetable broth
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon fresh ginger, grated
  • 1 tablespoon curry powder
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon ground turmeric
  • 1 can (15 oz) chickpeas, drained and rinsed
  • 1 can (14 oz) diced tomatoes
  • 1 can (14 oz) coconut milk
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh cilantro for garnish

Instructions

  1. Cook the Brown Rice: In a medium saucepan, bring the vegetable broth to a boil. Add the brown rice, reduce the heat to low, cover, and simmer for 45-50 minutes, or until the rice is tender and the liquid is absorbed.

  2. Prepare the Curry: In a large skillet, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the diced onion and sauté until translucent. Add the minced garlic and grated ginger, and cook for an additional 1-2 minutes, until fragrant.

  3. Add the Spices: Stir in the curry powder, ground cumin, and ground turmeric, and cook for another minute to toast the spices.

  4. Simmer the Curry: Add the chickpeas, diced tomatoes, and coconut milk to the skillet. Stir to combine and bring the mixture to a simmer. Let the curry simmer for 15-20 minutes, stirring occasionally, until the flavors are well combined and the sauce has thickened slightly.

  5. Season and Serve: Season the curry with salt and pepper to taste. Serve the chickpea and brown rice curry over the cooked brown rice. Garnish with fresh cilantro for a pop of color and freshness.

Nutritional Benefits

This Chickpea and Brown Rice Curry is not only a delicious meal but also offers a range of nutritional benefits. Chickpeas are an excellent source of plant-based protein and fiber, making them a great option for vegetarians and vegans. Brown rice provides complex carbohydrates and additional fiber, while the array of spices in the curry offer antioxidant and anti-inflammatory properties.

Serving Suggestions

This curry is perfect on its own, but you can also serve it with a side of naan bread or pita for a complete meal. If you’re looking to add some extra greens to your plate, consider serving the curry alongside a simple green salad or steamed vegetables.

Conclusion

This Chickpea and Brown Rice Curry is a versatile and satisfying dish that’s perfect for busy weeknights or leisurely weekends. Packed with protein, fiber, and flavor, it’s a meal that the whole family will love. Whether you’re a vegetarian, vegan, or simply looking to incorporate more plant-based meals into your diet, this curry is sure to become a staple in your recipe collection. Give it a try and savor the delicious flavors of this protein-packed meal!
