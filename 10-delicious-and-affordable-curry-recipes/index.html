10 Delicious and Affordable Curry Recipes


Curry is one of the most versatile dishes you can make in the kitchen, offering endless variations of flavors, ingredients, and spices. Whether you’re a seasoned cook or just starting to explore the world of curry, these 10 delicious and affordable recipes will bring the bold, aromatic flavors of curry into your home without breaking the bank. From hearty vegetable curries to savory chicken and beef options, these recipes will satisfy your taste buds and your wallet. So, let’s dive into these flavorful creations that you can whip up on a budget.

1. Classic Chicken Curry

A classic chicken curry is a must-have in any curry lover’s repertoire. This rich and flavorful dish is made with tender chicken pieces simmered in a spiced tomato and onion base. The secret to its depth of flavor lies in the combination of garlic, ginger, turmeric, and garam masala. Serve it with fluffy rice or warm naan bread for a satisfying meal.

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb chicken (boneless, skinless thighs or breasts)
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1-inch piece ginger, grated
  • 1 tsp turmeric powder
  • 2 tsp garam masala
  • 1 tsp cumin powder
  • 1/2 tsp chili powder (optional)
  • 1/2 cup coconut milk
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh cilantro for garnish
  • 2 tbsp vegetable oil

Instructions:

  1. Heat oil in a large pan over medium heat. Add onions and sauté until golden brown.
  2. Add garlic and ginger and cook for another 2 minutes.
  3. Stir in the chopped tomatoes, turmeric, cumin, garam masala, and chili powder. Cook until the tomatoes break down and the oil begins to separate from the spices.
  4. Add the chicken and cook until browned on all sides.
  5. Pour in the coconut milk and a cup of water, then season with salt. Let it simmer on low heat for 20-25 minutes, until the chicken is cooked through.
  6. Garnish with fresh cilantro and serve with rice or naan.

2. Quick and Easy Lentil Curry

Lentils are a budget-friendly ingredient that absorbs spices beautifully, making them perfect for curry. This quick and easy lentil curry is packed with protein and fiber, making it both filling and nutritious. With a simple combination of onions, tomatoes, and coconut milk, this dish is simple but delicious.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup dried lentils (red or green)
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 1 can (400ml) coconut milk
  • 2 tsp curry powder
  • 1 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp cumin powder
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 tbsp vegetable oil
  • Fresh cilantro for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Rinse the lentils under cold water and set aside.
  2. Heat oil in a large pot over medium heat. Add onions and sauté until soft and golden.
  3. Stir in the curry powder, turmeric, and cumin, cooking for 1 minute until fragrant.
  4. Add the tomatoes and cook until they soften, about 5 minutes.
  5. Add the lentils, coconut milk, and 2 cups of water. Bring to a boil, then reduce to a simmer and cook for 25-30 minutes, or until the lentils are tender and the curry thickens.
  6. Season with salt, garnish with fresh cilantro, and serve hot.

3. Spicy Beef and Potato Curry

Beef and potatoes are a classic combination in curry, and this spicy version is sure to satisfy. With the richness of beef and the heartiness of potatoes, this curry is a filling dish that doesn’t require fancy ingredients. You can make it as spicy as you like by adjusting the amount of chili powder or fresh chilies.

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb beef stew meat, cubed
  • 2 potatoes, peeled and cubed
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1-inch piece ginger, grated
  • 1 tsp turmeric powder
  • 2 tsp coriander powder
  • 1 tsp chili powder (adjust to taste)
  • 1 tsp cumin powder
  • 1/2 cup beef broth
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh cilantro for garnish
  • 2 tbsp vegetable oil

Instructions:

  1. Heat oil in a large pot over medium heat. Add onions, garlic, and ginger, sautéing until the onions are golden brown.
  2. Add the beef cubes and brown on all sides.
  3. Stir in the tomatoes, turmeric, coriander, chili powder, and cumin. Cook for 5 minutes until the tomatoes break down.
  4. Add the potatoes, beef broth, and enough water to cover the ingredients. Bring to a boil, then reduce the heat and simmer for 40-45 minutes, or until the beef and potatoes are tender.
  5. Season with salt, garnish with fresh cilantro, and serve hot with rice.

4. Chickpea and Spinach Curry

This vegetarian chickpea and spinach curry is not only affordable but also packed with nutrients. The combination of chickpeas, spinach, and a creamy coconut sauce makes for a comforting and flavorful meal. You can adjust the spice level according to your preference, and it pairs beautifully with basmati rice.

Ingredients:

  • 1 can (400g) chickpeas, drained and rinsed
  • 4 cups fresh spinach, chopped
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 1 can (400ml) coconut milk
  • 2 tsp curry powder
  • 1 tsp cumin powder
  • 1/2 tsp chili powder
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 tbsp vegetable oil
  • Fresh cilantro for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Heat oil in a pan over medium heat. Add onions and sauté until soft and translucent.
  2. Add the tomatoes and cook until they soften, about 5 minutes.
  3. Stir in the curry powder, cumin, and chili powder, cooking for 1 minute until fragrant.
  4. Add the chickpeas and coconut milk, and bring the mixture to a simmer.
  5. Once the curry is simmering, add the chopped spinach and cook until it wilts.
  6. Season with salt and serve with rice or naan, garnished with fresh cilantro.

5. Butternut Squash and Coconut Curry

Butternut squash is a great addition to curry, adding natural sweetness and a rich texture. Combined with coconut milk and warming spices like ginger and cinnamon, this curry is an autumnal delight. It’s perfect for a comforting meal and pairs wonderfully with rice or crusty bread.

Ingredients:

  • 1 medium butternut squash, peeled and cubed
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1-inch piece ginger, grated
  • 1 can (400ml) coconut milk
  • 1 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp ground cinnamon
  • 1/2 tsp cumin powder
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh cilantro for garnish
  • 2 tbsp vegetable oil

Instructions:

  1. Heat oil in a large pot over medium heat. Add onions, garlic, and ginger, sautéing until the onions are soft and golden.
  2. Stir in the turmeric, cinnamon, and cumin, cooking for 1 minute.
  3. Add the cubed butternut squash and cook for 5 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  4. Pour in the coconut milk and a cup of water, then bring to a boil.
  5. Reduce the heat and simmer for 20-25 minutes, or until the squash is tender.
  6. Season with salt, garnish with fresh cilantro, and serve with rice.

6. Tofu and Vegetable Curry

For those looking for a vegan and protein-packed curry, this tofu and vegetable curry is a perfect choice. Tofu absorbs the flavors of the spices and coconut milk, making it a great meat alternative. With a variety of colorful vegetables, this curry is as nutritious as it is delicious.

Ingredients:

  • 1 block firm tofu, drained and cubed
  • 2 carrots, sliced
  • 1 bell pepper, chopped
  • 1 zucchini, chopped
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 1 can (400ml) coconut milk
  • 2 tsp curry powder
  • 1 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp cumin powder
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh cilantro for garnish
  • 2 tbsp vegetable oil

Instructions:

  1. Heat oil in a large pan over medium heat. Add onions and sauté until soft.
  2. Add the carrots, bell pepper, and zucchini, and cook for 5 minutes.
  3. Stir in the tomatoes, curry powder, turmeric, and cumin, cooking for another 2 minutes.
  4. Add the cubed tofu and coconut milk, and bring the curry to a simmer.
  5. Let the curry cook for 15-20 minutes, or until the vegetables are tender.
  6. Season with salt, garnish with fresh cilantro, and serve with rice or naan.

7. Sweet Potato and Pea Curry

Sweet potatoes are naturally sweet and creamy, making them a perfect ingredient for curry. Combined with peas and a simple spiced tomato sauce, this dish is both comforting and affordable. The earthy flavors of the sweet potato paired with the pop of peas create a balanced and satisfying meal.

Ingredients:

  • 2 medium sweet potatoes, peeled and cubed
  • 1 cup frozen peas
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 1 can (400ml) coconut milk
  • 1 tsp cumin powder
  • 1 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1/2 tsp chili powder (optional)
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh cilantro for garnish
  • 2 tbsp vegetable oil

Instructions:

  1. Heat oil in a large pan over medium heat. Add onions and sauté until golden brown.
  2. Add the chopped tomatoes and cook until soft.
  3. Stir in the cumin, turmeric, and chili powder, cooking for 1 minute.
  4. Add the sweet potatoes and cook for 5 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  5. Pour in the coconut milk and enough water to cover the ingredients. Bring to a boil, then reduce the heat and simmer for 25-30 minutes, until the sweet potatoes are tender.
  6. Stir in the peas and cook for an additional 5 minutes.
  7. Season with salt, garnish with fresh cilantro, and serve with rice.

8. Eggplant and Tomato Curry

Eggplants are a great addition to curry, providing a rich and hearty texture. Paired with tomatoes, garlic, and a blend of spices, this eggplant and tomato curry is both flavorful and easy to make. It’s the perfect dish for a simple weeknight dinner.

Ingredients:

  • 2 medium eggplants, cubed
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tsp cumin powder
  • 1 tsp coriander powder
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh cilantro for garnish
  • 2 tbsp vegetable oil

Instructions:

  1. Heat oil in a large pan over medium heat. Add onions and garlic, sautéing until soft.
  2. Stir in the tomatoes and cook until they break down and form a sauce.
  3. Add the cumin, coriander, turmeric, and salt, cooking for 1 minute.
  4. Add the cubed eggplant and cook for 10 minutes, stirring occasionally, until the eggplant softens.
  5. Add a little water if needed to help the curry cook down, then simmer for another 10 minutes.
  6. Garnish with fresh cilantro and serve with rice or naan.

9. Pumpkin Curry

Pumpkin curry is a perfect way to enjoy the seasonal flavors of autumn in a delicious and affordable dish. The creamy texture of pumpkin pairs perfectly with a spiced curry base, creating a comforting and filling meal.

Ingredients:

  • 1 small pumpkin, peeled and cubed
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 1 can (400ml) coconut milk
  • 1 tsp cumin powder
  • 1/2 tsp cinnamon powder
  • 1 tsp curry powder
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh cilantro for garnish
  • 2 tbsp vegetable oil

Instructions:

  1. Heat oil in a large pot over medium heat. Add onions and sauté until soft and golden.
  2. Add the tomatoes and cook until softened.
  3. Stir in the cumin, cinnamon, and curry powder, cooking for 1 minute.
  4. Add the cubed pumpkin and coconut milk, then bring to a boil.
  5. Reduce the heat and simmer for 20-25 minutes, or until the pumpkin is tender.
  6. Season with salt, garnish with fresh cilantro, and serve with rice.

10. Coconut Shrimp Curry

This coconut shrimp curry is a quick and easy dish that’s perfect for seafood lovers. The creamy coconut milk and fragrant spices pair beautifully with shrimp, creating a light yet flavorful dish.

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 1 can (400ml) coconut milk
  • 1 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp cumin powder
  • 1/2 tsp chili powder
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh cilantro for garnish
  • 2 tbsp vegetable oil

Instructions:

  1. Heat oil in a large pan over medium heat. Add onions and sauté until soft.
  2. Add tomatoes and cook until they break down, about 5 minutes.
  3. Stir in the turmeric, cumin, and chili powder, cooking for 1 minute.
  4. Pour in the coconut milk and bring to a simmer.
  5. Add the shrimp and cook for 3-5 minutes until they turn pink and are cooked through.
  6. Season with salt, garnish with fresh cilantro, and serve with rice.

With these 10 affordable and delicious curry recipes, you can enjoy a variety of comforting meals that are both flavorful and budget-friendly. Whether you prefer vegetarian dishes, hearty meats, or seafood, there’s a curry recipe for every taste and dietary preference.
