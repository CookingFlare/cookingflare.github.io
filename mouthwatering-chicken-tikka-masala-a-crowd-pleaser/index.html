Mouthwatering Chicken Tikka Masala: A Crowd Pleaser


If there’s one dish that never fails to impress, it’s Chicken Tikka Masala. This rich and flavorful Indian-inspired dish has become a favorite in households and restaurants around the world. The tender chunks of chicken marinated in a spiced yogurt mixture and then simmered in a creamy tomato sauce create a dish that is truly a crowd pleaser. Whether you’re a seasoned chef or a novice cook, this recipe is sure to become a staple in your culinary repertoire.

Ingredients

For the Chicken Marinade:

  • 2 lbs (900g) boneless, skinless chicken thighs, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 1 cup (240g) plain yogurt
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon grated fresh ginger
  • 1 tablespoon garam masala
  • 1 teaspoon ground turmeric
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil

For the Sauce:

  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1 large onion, finely chopped
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon grated fresh ginger
  • 1 tablespoon garam masala
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon ground coriander
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric
  • 1/2 teaspoon cayenne pepper (adjust to taste)
  • 1 can (14 oz/400g) crushed tomatoes
  • 1 cup (240ml) heavy cream
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh cilantro, for garnish

Instructions

Chicken Marinade:

  1. In a large bowl, combine the yogurt, minced garlic, grated ginger, garam masala, turmeric, cumin, paprika, salt, and black pepper.
  2. Add the chicken pieces to the marinade and toss until well coated. Cover and refrigerate for at least 1 hour, or ideally overnight.

Cooking the Chicken:

  1. Preheat the oven to 400°F (200°C).
  2. Thread the marinated chicken pieces onto skewers and place them on a baking sheet lined with foil.
  3. Bake for 20-25 minutes, or until the chicken is cooked through and slightly charred around the edges.

Making the Sauce:

  1. In a large skillet, heat 2 tablespoons of oil over medium heat. Add the chopped onion and cook until softened.
  2. Add the minced garlic and grated ginger, and cook for another minute.
  3. Stir in the garam masala, cumin, coriander, paprika, turmeric, and cayenne pepper. Cook for a couple of minutes until fragrant.
  4. Pour in the crushed tomatoes and simmer for 10 minutes.
  5. Stir in the heavy cream and let the sauce simmer for an additional 5 minutes. Season with salt to taste.

Bringing It All Together:

  1. Remove the chicken from the skewers and add it to the sauce.
  2. Let the chicken simmer in the sauce for a few minutes, allowing the flavors to meld together.
  3. Garnish with fresh cilantro and serve with steamed rice or warm naan bread.

Conclusion

The aroma of the spices, the tenderness of the chicken, and the richness of the creamy tomato sauce make Chicken Tikka Masala a dish that’s hard to resist. Whether you’re hosting a dinner party or simply craving a cozy night in, this recipe is guaranteed to satisfy. So, roll up your sleeves, gather your ingredients, and get ready to impress your family and friends with this mouthwatering Chicken Tikka Masala. Enjoy!
