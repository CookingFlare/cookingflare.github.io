Satisfying Instant Pot Vegetarian Chili Recipe for Meatless Mondays


Looking for a delicious and hearty dish to enjoy on Meatless Mondays? Look no further than this satisfying Instant Pot Vegetarian Chili recipe! Packed with flavor and wholesome ingredients, this chili is sure to become a favorite in your household. Plus, with the convenience of the Instant Pot, you can have a comforting meal ready in no time. Let’s get cooking!

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 red bell pepper, diced
  • 1 green bell pepper, diced
  • 2 carrots, diced
  • 1 zucchini, diced
  • 1 can (15 ounces) kidney beans, drained and rinsed
  • 1 can (15 ounces) black beans, drained and rinsed
  • 1 can (15 ounces) corn kernels, drained
  • 1 can (28 ounces) crushed tomatoes
  • 1 cup vegetable broth
  • 2 tablespoons chili powder
  • 1 tablespoon cumin
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1 teaspoon oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Optional toppings: shredded cheese, sour cream, chopped cilantro

Instructions

  1. Set your Instant Pot to sauté mode and heat the olive oil. Add the diced onion and minced garlic, and sauté until fragrant and translucent, about 2-3 minutes.

  2. Add the diced bell peppers, carrots, and zucchini to the Instant Pot. Sauté for another 3-4 minutes, until the vegetables are slightly softened.

  3. Next, add the kidney beans, black beans, corn kernels, crushed tomatoes, vegetable broth, chili powder, cumin, paprika, oregano, salt, and pepper to the Instant Pot. Stir well to combine all the ingredients.

  4. Close the lid of the Instant Pot and set the pressure release valve to the sealing position. Cook on high pressure for 10 minutes.

  5. Once the cooking time is up, allow the pressure to release naturally for 10 minutes, then carefully quick-release any remaining pressure.

  6. Give the chili a good stir and taste for seasoning. Adjust with more salt and pepper if needed.

  7. Serve the Instant Pot Vegetarian Chili hot, garnished with your favorite toppings such as shredded cheese, sour cream, and chopped cilantro. Enjoy with a side of warm crusty bread or cornbread for a complete meal.

Why Choose Instant Pot Vegetarian Chili?

This Instant Pot Vegetarian Chili recipe is the perfect meal for Meatless Mondays. It’s not only delicious and satisfying but also packed with nutrients from the colorful assortment of vegetables and protein-rich beans. The Instant Pot makes the cooking process a breeze, saving you time and effort in the kitchen.

By using the Instant Pot, the flavors of the chili are intensified and the vegetables are perfectly cooked, creating a rich and hearty dish that will satisfy even the most dedicated meat lovers. Plus, the pressure cooking method helps to retain the nutrients in the ingredients, making this chili a healthy choice.

Final Thoughts

If you’re looking for a tasty and filling vegetarian meal that the whole family will love, this Instant Pot Vegetarian Chili is a must-try. It’s easy to make, packed with flavors, and perfect for Meatless Mondays. So grab your Instant Pot and get ready to enjoy a bowlful of comforting goodness!

Remember, you can customize this recipe by adding your favorite vegetables or adjusting the spices to suit your taste. Feel free to experiment and make it your own. Happy cooking!
