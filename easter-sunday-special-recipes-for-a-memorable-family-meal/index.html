Easter Sunday Special: Recipes for a Memorable Family Meal


As winter fades and spring blooms, we joyfully welcome the Easter season, a time for reflection, renewal, and celebration. What better way to embrace the spirit of the day than sharing a delightful meal with loved ones? Here are three scrumptious Easter recipes perfect for creating unforgettable memories with your family. Let the holiday cooking commence!

  1. Honey-Glazed Ham

A popular choice for holiday gatherings, ham offers a delightful and tender solution that effortlessly impresses diners. Allow this honey-glazed ham to be the centerpiece of your Easter feast.

Ingredients:

  • 5-6 pounds fully-cooked spiral-cut ham
  • 1/2 cup honey
  • 1/4 cup Dijon mustard
  • 1/4 cup brown sugar
  • 3 tablespoons minced fresh ginger
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • Optional: pineapple slices and maraschino cherries

Instructions:

  • Preheat oven to 325°F (165°C).
  • In a saucepan, heat the honey, Dijon mustard, brown sugar, ginger, and cinnamon over low heat until well combined.
  • Place the ham on a baking sheet lined with foil; scoop the glaze over the ham, ensuring even coating.
  • Add pineapple slices and cherries, if desired, to the top of the ham.
  • Cover the ham with foil and bake for 20 minutes per pound.
  • Let the ham rest for 10 minutes before serving. Enjoy!
  1. Easter Deviled Eggs

No Easter meal is complete without a pert, creamy, and zesty deviled egg! Revamp the classic deviled egg with a delightful twist.

Ingredients:

  • 6 hard-boiled eggs, cooled
  • 2 tablespoons mayonnaise
  • 1 teaspoon Dijon mustard
  • 1 teaspoon yellow or Dijon mustard (optional)
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • Chopped chives or paprika, for garnish

Instructions:

  • Carefully halve the eggs lengthwise and remove the yolks.
  • In a small bowl, mash the yolks with a fork. Combine with mayonnaise, Dijon, and optional yellow or Dijon mustard.
  • Stir in salt and pepper to taste.
  • Fill each egg half with the yolk mixture, using a piping bag or teaspoon.
  • Garnish with chopped chives or paprika, and enjoy!
  1. Easter Bread

Indulge in the heavenly scent and pillowy softness of this easter bread, studded with fluffy raisins and rimmed with a golden crust. It’s not just a delicious side; it is a feast for the senses!

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup warm water (110°F/45°C)
  • 2 tablespoons active dry yeast
  • 3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tablespoon granulated sugar
  • 1 tablespoon salt
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1/4 cup vegetable oil
  • 1/3 cup golden raisins

Instructions:

  • In a bowl, combine water and yeast, and let sit for 5 minutes.
  • In a large mixing bowl, whisk together flour, sugar, and salt.
  • Add the yeast mixture, eggs, and oil; mix well.
  • Stir in raisins.
  • Knead the dough on a lightly floured surface for 10 minutes, or until smooth.
  • Place in a greased bowl, cover, and allow to rise in a warm, draft-free area for 1-1 1/2 hours.
  • Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C). Shape the dough into loaves, and place on a greased baking sheet.
  • Allow to rise for 20 minutes.
  • Brush with egg wash and sprinkle with additional raisins or seeds, if desired.
  • Bake for 30-35 minutes, or until golden brown.
  • Cool before serving.

From platego to table, enjoy the magic of Easter with these delightful recipes that evoke warmth and cheer. Let’s create memories to cherish this Easter Sunday!
