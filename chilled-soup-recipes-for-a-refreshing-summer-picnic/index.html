Chilled Soup Recipes for a Refreshing Summer Picnic


As the summer sun beats down, there’s nothing quite like a refreshing chilled soup to cool you off and satisfy your taste buds. Whether you’re planning a picnic in the park or a backyard gathering, these chilled soup recipes are perfect for a hot summer day. They’re easy to make, bursting with flavor, and can be prepared ahead of time, making them an ideal addition to your summer picnic menu.

Gazpacho

Ingredients:

  • 6 ripe tomatoes, chopped
  • 1 cucumber, peeled and chopped
  • 1 red bell pepper, chopped
  • 1 small red onion, chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 4 cups tomato juice
  • 1/4 cup red wine vinegar
  • 1/4 cup extra virgin olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Tabasco sauce (optional)
  • Fresh basil leaves for garnish

Instructions:

  1. In a blender or food processor, combine the tomatoes, cucumber, red bell pepper, red onion, and garlic. Blend until smooth.
  2. Add the tomato juice, red wine vinegar, and olive oil. Blend until well combined.
  3. Season with salt, pepper, and Tabasco sauce, if using. Blend again to incorporate the flavors.
  4. Transfer the gazpacho to a large bowl and refrigerate for at least 2 hours to chill.
  5. Serve the chilled gazpacho in individual bowls, garnished with fresh basil leaves.

Chilled Avocado Soup

Ingredients:

  • 3 ripe avocados, peeled and pitted
  • 2 cups vegetable broth
  • 1 cup plain Greek yogurt
  • 1/4 cup fresh cilantro, chopped
  • 1 jalapeño, seeded and chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • Juice of 2 limes
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Sliced radishes for garnish
  • Lime wedges for garnish

Instructions:

  1. In a blender, combine the avocados, vegetable broth, Greek yogurt, cilantro, jalapeño, garlic, and lime juice. Blend until smooth and creamy.
  2. Season the soup with salt and pepper, adjusting to taste.
  3. Transfer the avocado soup to a large bowl and refrigerate for at least 1 hour to chill.
  4. Serve the chilled avocado soup in bowls, garnished with sliced radishes and lime wedges.

Chilled Cucumber Soup

Ingredients:

  • 3 large cucumbers, peeled and chopped
  • 1 cup plain Greek yogurt
  • 1/4 cup fresh dill, chopped
  • 2 tablespoons fresh mint, chopped
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground cumin
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Sliced cucumbers and dill sprigs for garnish

Instructions:

  1. In a blender, combine the chopped cucumbers, Greek yogurt, dill, mint, lemon juice, garlic, and ground cumin. Blend until smooth.
  2. Season the soup with salt and pepper, adjusting to taste.
  3. Transfer the cucumber soup to a large bowl and refrigerate for at least 1 hour to chill.
  4. Serve the chilled cucumber soup in bowls, garnished with sliced cucumbers and dill sprigs.

Conclusion

Chilled soups are a delightful addition to any summer picnic, offering a burst of freshness and a welcome respite from the heat. With these easy-to-make recipes, you can impress your guests with vibrant flavors and keep everyone cool and satisfied. So, pack your picnic basket, gather your loved ones, and savor the joy of summer with these delicious chilled soups.
