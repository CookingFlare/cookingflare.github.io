Thai Cashew Tofu with Vegetables: A Nutty and Flavorful Vegan Dish


If you’re looking to add a burst of flavor to your vegan cooking repertoire, this Thai Cashew Tofu with Vegetables recipe is a must-try. Packed with protein-rich tofu, crunchy cashews, and a medley of colorful vegetables, this dish is a delightful blend of nuttiness and aromatic Thai flavors. Whether you’re a seasoned vegan cook or just starting to explore plant-based cuisine, this recipe is sure to impress your taste buds and leave you craving for more.

Ingredients:

  • 14 oz firm tofu, drained and cut into cubes
  • 1 cup unsalted cashews
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1 red bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 yellow bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 cup broccoli florets
  • 1 cup snap peas
  • 4 green onions, sliced
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon fresh ginger, grated
  • 1/2 cup vegetable broth
  • 3 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons hoisin sauce
  • 1 tablespoon rice vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon agave nectar
  • 1 teaspoon cornstarch
  • Cooked jasmine rice, for serving
  • Fresh cilantro, for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the Tofu: Start by pressing the tofu to remove excess moisture. Once drained, cut the tofu into cubes and set aside.

  2. Toast the Cashews: In a large skillet over medium heat, toast the cashews until they are golden and fragrant. Remove from the skillet and set aside.

  3. Sear the Tofu: In the same skillet, heat 1 tablespoon of vegetable oil over medium-high heat. Add the tofu cubes and cook until they are golden brown on all sides. Remove the tofu from the skillet and set aside.

  4. Stir-Fry the Vegetables: In the same skillet, heat the remaining tablespoon of vegetable oil over medium-high heat. Add the bell peppers, broccoli, and snap peas. Stir-fry for 3-4 minutes, or until the vegetables are tender-crisp.

  5. Make the Sauce: In a small bowl, whisk together the vegetable broth, soy sauce, hoisin sauce, rice vinegar, and agave nectar. In another small bowl, mix the cornstarch with a tablespoon of water to create a slurry.

  6. Combine and Simmer: Push the vegetables to one side of the skillet and add the garlic and ginger to the cleared space. Cook for 30 seconds, then stir in the green onions. Pour the sauce over the vegetables and bring to a simmer. Add the tofu and toasted cashews to the skillet, stirring gently to coat everything in the sauce. Cook for an additional 2-3 minutes, allowing the sauce to thicken.

  7. Serve and Enjoy: Serve the Thai Cashew Tofu and Vegetables over a bed of jasmine rice, garnished with fresh cilantro.

Nutritional Information:

  • Calories: 320
  • Protein: 18g
  • Carbohydrates: 24g
  • Fat: 18g
  • Fiber: 5g

Why You’ll Love This Dish:

  • Protein-Packed: Tofu and cashews provide a generous amount of plant-based protein, making this dish a satisfying and nutritious option.

  • Vibrant and Flavorful: The colorful array of vegetables combined with the rich Thai-inspired sauce creates a visually appealing and mouthwatering flavor profile.

  • Versatile and Customizable: Feel free to swap out the vegetables with your favorites or adjust the level of heat by adding more or less ginger and garlic.

Final Thoughts:

This Thai Cashew Tofu with Vegetables recipe is a delightful marriage of nuttiness, vibrant vegetables, and bold Thai flavors. Whether you’re a vegan enthusiast or simply seeking to incorporate more plant-based dishes into your diet, this recipe is a surefire way to elevate your cooking game and impress your family and friends. So, roll up your sleeves, grab your wok, and get ready to savor the irresistible aromas and tastes of this delectable vegan dish.
