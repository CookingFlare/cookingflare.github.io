Tangy Lemon Pepper Chicken Stir Fry: A Citrusy Twist


Are you looking for a quick and flavorful meal that will tantalize your taste buds? Look no further than this Tangy Lemon Pepper Chicken Stir Fry recipe! Packed with vibrant flavors and a citrusy twist, this dish is sure to become a family favorite. The combination of tangy lemon, zesty pepper, and tender chicken creates a delightful explosion of flavors that will leave you craving for more.

Ingredients

To create this mouthwatering dish, you’ll need the following ingredients:

  • 500g boneless chicken breast, sliced into thin strips
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 red bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 yellow bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 medium-sized onion, sliced
  • 4 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons lemon juice
  • 1 tablespoon lemon zest
  • 1 tablespoon black pepper
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • 1/2 teaspoon red chili flakes (optional)
  • Fresh parsley, chopped (for garnish)

Instructions

  1. Heat the olive oil in a large skillet or wok over medium-high heat.
  2. Add the sliced chicken strips to the pan and cook until lightly browned and cooked through, about 5-7 minutes. Remove the chicken from the pan and set aside.
  3. In the same pan, add the minced garlic and sauté for about 1 minute until fragrant.
  4. Add the sliced bell peppers and onion to the pan and cook until they start to soften, about 3-4 minutes.
  5. In a small bowl, combine the lemon juice, lemon zest, black pepper, salt, paprika, dried thyme, and red chili flakes (if using). Mix well to create a tangy marinade.
  6. Pour the marinade over the cooked vegetables in the pan and stir to coat evenly.
  7. Return the cooked chicken to the pan and toss to combine with the vegetables and marinade. Cook for an additional 2-3 minutes to allow the flavors to meld together.
  8. Remove the stir fry from the heat and garnish with fresh parsley.
  9. Serve the Tangy Lemon Pepper Chicken Stir Fry hot over a bed of steamed rice or noodles for a complete and satisfying meal.

Tips and Variations

  • For a vegetarian twist, you can substitute tofu for the chicken. Simply press the tofu to remove excess water, cut it into cubes, and follow the same cooking instructions.
  • Feel free to add more vegetables to your stir fry, such as snap peas, broccoli, or carrots, to make it even more nutritious and colorful.
  • Adjust the amount of lemon juice and black pepper according to your personal taste preferences. If you prefer a stronger citrus flavor, you can add more lemon zest as well.
  • If you’re a fan of spicy food, don’t hesitate to increase the amount of red chili flakes or add some diced jalapenos for an extra kick.

Conclusion

This Tangy Lemon Pepper Chicken Stir Fry is a delicious and refreshing twist on the classic stir fry recipe. The combination of tangy lemon, zesty pepper, and tender chicken creates a burst of flavors that will leave your taste buds craving for more. The vibrant colors of the bell peppers and onions add a visual appeal to the dish, making it perfect for both casual weeknight dinners and special occasions. So go ahead and give this recipe a try, and let the citrusy goodness of this stir fry brighten up your mealtime! Enjoy!
