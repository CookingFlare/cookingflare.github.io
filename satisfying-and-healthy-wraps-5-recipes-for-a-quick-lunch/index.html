Satisfying and Healthy Wraps: 5 Recipes for a Quick Lunch


Are you looking for a delicious and nutritious lunch option that is quick and easy to make? Look no further than these satisfying and healthy wraps! Packed with flavor and wholesome ingredients, these recipes are perfect for those busy days when you need a satisfying meal on the go. Whether you’re a vegetarian or a meat lover, there’s a wrap recipe here that will surely tickle your taste buds. So, let’s dive right in!

1. Mediterranean Veggie Wrap

Prep Time: 10 minutes | Cook Time: 0 minutes | Total Time: 10 minutes | Serves: 2

Ingredients:

  • 2 large whole-grain tortillas
  • 1 cup mixed salad greens
  • 1/2 cup sliced cucumbers
  • 1/2 cup diced tomatoes
  • 1/4 cup sliced red onions
  • 1/4 cup crumbled feta cheese
  • 2 tablespoons hummus
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a small bowl, combine the hummus and lemon juice. Mix well.
  2. Lay the tortillas flat on a clean surface. Spread the hummus mixture evenly over each tortilla.
  3. Layer the salad greens, cucumbers, tomatoes, red onions, and feta cheese on top of the hummus mixture.
  4. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  5. Roll the tortillas tightly, tucking in the sides as you go.
  6. Cut the wraps in half and serve immediately.

2. BBQ Chicken Wrap

Prep Time: 15 minutes | Cook Time: 10 minutes | Total Time: 25 minutes | Serves: 2

Ingredients:

  • 2 large whole-wheat tortillas
  • 1 cup cooked chicken breast, shredded
  • 1/4 cup barbecue sauce
  • 1/4 cup diced red bell pepper
  • 1/4 cup diced red onion
  • 1/4 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • 2 tablespoons ranch dressing
  • 2 tablespoons chopped fresh cilantro

Instructions:

  1. In a small bowl, toss the shredded chicken with barbecue sauce until well coated.
  2. Lay the tortillas flat on a clean surface. Spread the ranch dressing evenly over each tortilla.
  3. Place the barbecue chicken, red bell pepper, red onion, and cheddar cheese on top of the ranch dressing.
  4. Sprinkle with chopped cilantro.
  5. Roll the tortillas tightly, tucking in the sides as you go.
  6. Cut the wraps in half and serve immediately.

3. Greek Chicken Wrap

Prep Time: 15 minutes | Cook Time: 10 minutes | Total Time: 25 minutes | Serves: 2

Ingredients:

  • 2 large spinach tortillas
  • 1 cup cooked chicken breast, sliced
  • 1/4 cup diced cucumbers
  • 1/4 cup diced tomatoes
  • 1/4 cup crumbled feta cheese
  • 2 tablespoons Greek yogurt
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried dill
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a small bowl, mix together the Greek yogurt, lemon juice, dried dill, salt, and pepper.
  2. Lay the tortillas flat on a clean surface. Spread the Greek yogurt mixture evenly over each tortilla.
  3. Layer the sliced chicken, cucumbers, tomatoes, and feta cheese on top of the Greek yogurt mixture.
  4. Roll the tortillas tightly, tucking in the sides as you go.
  5. Cut the wraps in half and serve immediately.

4. Hummus and Veggie Wrap

Prep Time: 10 minutes | Cook Time: 0 minutes | Total Time: 10 minutes | Serves: 2

Ingredients:

  • 2 large whole-grain tortillas
  • 1/4 cup hummus
  • 1/4 cup sliced bell peppers
  • 1/4 cup sliced cucumbers
  • 1/4 cup shredded carrots
  • 1/4 cup sliced avocado
  • 2 tablespoons chopped fresh cilantro

Instructions:

  1. Lay the tortillas flat on a clean surface. Spread the hummus evenly over each tortilla.
  2. Layer the bell peppers, cucumbers, shredded carrots, avocado, and chopped cilantro on top of the hummus.
  3. Roll the tortillas tightly, tucking in the sides as you go.
  4. Cut the wraps in half and serve immediately.

5. Turkey Club Wrap

Prep Time: 15 minutes | Cook Time: 10 minutes | Total Time: 25 minutes | Serves: 2

Ingredients:

  • 2 large whole-wheat tortillas
  • 1/4 cup mayonnaise
  • 1/2 teaspoon Dijon mustard
  • 4 slices cooked turkey breast
  • 4 slices cooked bacon
  • 1/2 cup mixed salad greens
  • 1/4 cup sliced tomatoes
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a small bowl, mix together the mayonnaise and Dijon mustard.
  2. Lay the tortillas flat on a clean surface. Spread the mayonnaise mixture evenly over each tortilla.
  3. Layer the turkey breast, bacon, mixed salad greens, and sliced tomatoes on top of the mayonnaise mixture.
  4. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  5. Roll the tortillas tightly, tucking in the sides as you go.
  6. Cut the wraps in half and serve immediately.
