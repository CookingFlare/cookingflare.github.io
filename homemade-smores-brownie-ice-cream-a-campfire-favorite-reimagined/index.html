Homemade S'mores Brownie Ice Cream: A Campfire Favorite Reimagined


There’s nothing quite like the nostalgic flavors of a s’mores dessert enjoyed around a crackling campfire. The combination of gooey marshmallows, rich chocolate, and crunchy graham crackers is a classic treat that brings back memories of summer nights and outdoor adventures. Now, imagine taking those beloved flavors and turning them into a decadent homemade ice cream – that’s exactly what we’re about to do. This recipe for Homemade S’mores Brownie Ice Cream is a delightful twist on a campfire favorite, perfect for indulging in the comforts of your own kitchen.

Ingredients

For the Brownie Chunks:

  • 1 cup unsalted butter
  • 2 cups granulated sugar
  • 4 large eggs
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup cocoa powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt

For the Marshmallow Swirl:

  • 1 cup marshmallow fluff
  • 2 tablespoons unsalted butter, melted
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract

For the Ice Cream Base:

  • 2 cups whole milk
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 3/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • Pinch of salt

Additional Ingredients:

  • 1 cup graham crackers, roughly chopped
  • 1 cup semisweet chocolate chips

Instructions

Making the Brownie Chunks:

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C) and line a 9x13-inch baking pan with parchment paper.
  2. In a microwave-safe bowl, melt the butter in the microwave. Stir in the granulated sugar until well combined.
  3. Add the eggs and vanilla extract, mixing until smooth.
  4. In a separate bowl, whisk together the flour, cocoa powder, and salt. Gradually add the dry ingredients to the wet ingredients, stirring until fully incorporated.
  5. Pour the brownie batter into the prepared baking pan and spread it evenly.
  6. Bake for 25-30 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out with a few moist crumbs. Allow the brownies to cool completely, then cut them into small chunks.

Creating the Marshmallow Swirl:

  1. In a small bowl, whisk together the marshmallow fluff, melted butter, and vanilla extract until smooth and creamy.

Preparing the Ice Cream Base:

  1. In a saucepan, heat the whole milk, heavy cream, and granulated sugar over medium heat, stirring occasionally until the sugar has dissolved.
  2. Remove the mixture from the heat and stir in the vanilla extract and a pinch of salt. Allow the mixture to cool to room temperature.

Assembling the Ice Cream:

  1. Once the ice cream base has cooled, pour it into an ice cream maker and churn according to the manufacturer’s instructions.
  2. During the last few minutes of churning, add the graham crackers, chocolate chips, brownie chunks, and the marshmallow swirl, swirling them into the ice cream as it churns.
  3. Transfer the ice cream to a freezer-safe container, swirling in additional marshmallow swirl if desired.
  4. Freeze the ice cream for at least 4 hours, or until firm.

Serving Suggestions

When ready to serve, scoop the Homemade S’mores Brownie Ice Cream into bowls or cones. For an extra touch of indulgence, drizzle it with a warm chocolate sauce and sprinkle with additional graham cracker crumbs. This delightful dessert is perfect for summer gatherings, backyard barbecues, or simply as a sweet treat to enjoy on a warm evening.

Whether you’re reminiscing about campfire adventures or simply craving the delightful flavors of s’mores in a new form, this Homemade S’mores Brownie Ice Cream is sure to satisfy your sweet tooth and bring a smile to your face. So, gather your ingredients, embrace the joy of creating something delicious from scratch, and savor every spoonful of this delightful frozen delight.
