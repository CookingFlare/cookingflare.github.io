Flavorful Tex-Mex Chicken Fajita Pasta: A Spicy Twist on a Classic Dish


If you’re a fan of Tex-Mex cuisine and love a little kick in your meals, then this Flavorful Tex-Mex Chicken Fajita Pasta is the perfect dish for you. It combines the bold flavors of fajitas with the comforting goodness of pasta, creating a truly unique and satisfying meal.

Ingredients

  • 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1 red bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 green bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 yellow bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 onion, sliced
  • 3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 jalapeno, seeds removed and finely chopped
  • 2 tablespoons of olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon of chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon of cumin
  • 1 teaspoon of paprika
  • 1 teaspoon of dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 8 ounces of penne pasta
  • 1 cup of crushed tomatoes
  • 1/2 cup of heavy cream
  • 1/4 cup of chopped fresh cilantro
  • Lime wedges for serving

Instructions

  1. In a large skillet, heat the olive oil over medium-high heat. Add the chicken breasts and cook until browned and cooked through, about 5-6 minutes per side. Remove the chicken from the skillet and set aside.

  2. In the same skillet, add the sliced bell peppers, onion, garlic, and jalapeno. Cook until the vegetables are tender, about 5 minutes.

  3. While the vegetables are cooking, bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Cook the penne pasta according to the package instructions until al dente. Drain and set aside.

  4. Slice the cooked chicken into thin strips and return it to the skillet with the vegetables. Add the chili powder, cumin, paprika, dried oregano, salt, and pepper. Stir to coat the chicken and vegetables with the spices.

  5. Pour the crushed tomatoes and heavy cream into the skillet. Bring the mixture to a simmer and let it cook for 5 minutes, allowing the flavors to meld together.

  6. Add the cooked penne pasta to the skillet and toss to combine everything well. Cook for an additional 2-3 minutes, until the pasta is heated through.

  7. Remove the skillet from the heat and stir in the chopped fresh cilantro. Taste and adjust the seasoning if needed.

  8. Serve the Flavorful Tex-Mex Chicken Fajita Pasta hot, garnished with lime wedges for squeezing over the top. Enjoy!

Why You’ll Love It

This Tex-Mex Chicken Fajita Pasta is a delightful combination of flavors and textures. The tender chicken, colorful bell peppers, and aromatic spices create a mouthwatering dish that will leave you craving for more. The creaminess from the heavy cream and the acidity from the crushed tomatoes balance the dish perfectly, adding richness and depth to every bite.

Not only is this dish bursting with flavor, but it’s also incredibly easy to make. With just a few simple steps, you can have a restaurant-quality meal ready in no time. It’s perfect for a weeknight dinner or for impressing guests at a dinner party.

The Tex-Mex Chicken Fajita Pasta is also highly versatile. You can customize it to suit your taste by adding ingredients like black beans, corn, or even some shredded cheese for an extra indulgence. Feel free to experiment and make it your own.

So, if you’re looking to spice up your dinner routine and indulge in a flavorful Tex-Mex twist on a classic dish, give this recipe a try. Your taste buds will thank you!
