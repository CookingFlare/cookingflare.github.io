Keto Road Trip: Easy & Portable Low-Carb Recipes for Your Journey


Traveling on a keto diet doesn’t have to be a challenge! With these easy and portable low-carb recipes, you can satisfy your cravings and maintain your keto lifestyle while on the road. Discover delicious meals and snacks that are perfect for taking along on any road trip.

1. Avocado Beef Wraps

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb grass-fed ground beef
  • 4 large avocados, sliced
  • 8 large lettuce leaves, washed and dried
  • 1 tsp olive oil
  • Salt and pepper, to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a large skillet, heat the olive oil over medium heat.
  2. Add the ground beef and season with salt and pepper.
  3. Cook the beef until browned and fully cooked, breaking it up as it cooks.
  4. Once cooked, remove the beef from the skillet and let it cool for a few minutes.
  5. Assemble the wraps by spreading some avocado slices onto the lettuce leaves.
  6. Add a serving of cooled ground beef over the avocado.
  7. Fold the lettuce leaves over to form wraps and enjoy!

2. Chicken Caesar Salad in a Jar

Ingredients:

  • 4 cups mixed greens
  • 1 lb cooked chicken breast, shredded
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1 cup shredded parmesan cheese
  • Homemade Caesar dressing (see recipe below)
  • 4 medium mason jars

Homemade Caesar Dressing Ingredients:

  • 1 avocado
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 2 tbsp lemon juice
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tbsp Dijon mustard
  • 1 tsp Worcestershire sauce
  • Salt and pepper, to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a blender, combine all the ingredients for the Caesar dressing and blend until smooth. Set aside.
  2. Divide the mixed greens evenly into the bottom of the mason jars.
  3. Add the shredded chicken, cherry tomatoes, and Parmesan cheese on top of the greens.
  4. Pour the Caesar dressing into each jar to your desired amount.
  5. Tighten the lids and shake well to distribute the ingredients.
  6. Enjoy your salad straight from the jar!

3. Savory Breakfast Fat Bombs

Ingredients:

  • 6 oz cream cheese, softened
  • 1/2 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1/2 tsp garlic powder
  • 1/4 tsp paprika
  • 1/4 cup chopped bacon
  • Parchment paper or silicone baking mat
  • 1.5 tbsp butter, melted

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F.
  2. In a mixing bowl, combine the softened cream cheese, cheddar cheese, garlic powder, and paprika; mix until well combined.
  3. Fold in the chopped bacon and melted butter.
  4. Line a baking sheet with parchment paper or a silicone baking mat.
  5. Using an ice cream scoop, form balls with the mixture and place them on the prepared baking sheet, spacing them evenly.
  6. Bake for 20-25 minutes, or until the balls are slightly golden and firm to the touch.
  7. Let the fat bombs cool before storing them in an airtight container in the fridge.

4. Keto Trail Mix

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups unsalted mixed nuts (almonds, walnuts, cashews, etc.)
  • 1 cup unsweetened coconut flakes
  • 1 cup dry roasted edamame
  • 1/2 cup cocoa nibs
  • 1/2 cup dried unsweetened mangoes
  • 1/2 cup dried unsweetened berries

Instructions:

  1. In a large bowl, combine all the ingredients and mix well.
  2. Store the keto trail mix in an airtight container for up to two weeks.

Pack these yummy and easy-to-make recipes for your next road trip, and maintain your keto diet while exploring new destinations!
