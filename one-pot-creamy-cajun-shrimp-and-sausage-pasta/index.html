One Pot Creamy Cajun Shrimp and Sausage Pasta


If you’re looking for a flavorful, easy-to-make dish that will impress your family and friends, look no further than this One Pot Creamy Cajun Shrimp and Sausage Pasta recipe. This dish is a perfect combination of spicy Cajun flavors, succulent shrimp, savory sausage, and creamy pasta, all cooked together in one pot for easy cleanup. Let’s dive into the recipe and bring a taste of the bayou to your kitchen!

Ingredients

  • 1 pound large shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 12 ounces Andouille sausage, sliced
  • 1 tablespoon Cajun seasoning
  • 8 ounces fettuccine pasta
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 small onion, diced
  • 1 red bell pepper, diced
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 1/2 cups chicken broth
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley for garnish

Instructions

  1. Season the shrimp: In a bowl, toss the shrimp with Cajun seasoning, ensuring they are well coated. Set aside while you prepare the other ingredients.

  2. Sear the sausage: In a large pot or skillet, heat the olive oil over medium-high heat. Add the sliced Andouille sausage and cook until browned on both sides. Once done, remove the sausage from the pot and set aside.

  3. Saute the vegetables: In the same pot, add the diced onion and red bell pepper. Cook for a few minutes until the vegetables start to soften, then add the minced garlic and cook for an additional minute.

  4. Cook the pasta: Add the fettuccine pasta to the pot, followed by the chicken broth and heavy cream. Bring the mixture to a gentle boil, then reduce the heat to a simmer. Cook for about 10-12 minutes, stirring occasionally, until the pasta is tender and the sauce has thickened.

  5. Add the shrimp and sausage: Once the pasta is nearly cooked, add the seasoned shrimp and the cooked Andouille sausage back into the pot. Let the shrimp cook for 2-3 minutes until they turn pink and opaque.

  6. Season and garnish: Taste the dish and adjust the seasoning with salt and pepper as needed. Garnish with fresh parsley for a pop of color and flavor.

  7. Serve and enjoy: Once everything is cooked to perfection, it’s time to serve up this delightful One Pot Creamy Cajun Shrimp and Sausage Pasta. Dish it out into individual bowls and watch as your guests’ eyes light up with delight.

Tips for Success

  • Don’t overcook the shrimp: Shrimp cook quickly, so be sure not to overdo it. They should turn pink and opaque, indicating that they are ready to eat.
  • Adjust the spice level: If you prefer a milder or spicier dish, feel free to adjust the amount of Cajun seasoning to suit your taste.
  • Use fresh ingredients: Fresh shrimp, quality sausage, and vibrant vegetables will elevate the flavors of this dish.

Conclusion

This One Pot Creamy Cajun Shrimp and Sausage Pasta is a fantastic choice for a weeknight dinner or a special gathering. The rich, creamy sauce combined with the robust flavors of Cajun seasoning, shrimp, and sausage create a dish that will have everyone coming back for seconds. Plus, with only one pot to clean, you’ll have more time to savor the delicious meal and enjoy the company of your loved ones. Give this recipe a try, and let the flavors transport you to the heart of Louisiana!
