Sizzling Mediterranean Shrimp Skewers Recipe


Get ready to tantalize your taste buds with this mouth-watering Mediterranean Shrimp Skewers recipe that will transport you to the sun-kissed beaches of Greece. This flavorful and aromatic dish is perfect for warm weather gatherings, potlucks, or even a quick weeknight dinner. Ingredients Before we dive into the cooking process, let's gather our ingredients:

1 pound large shrimp, peeled and deveined 1/4 cup olive oil 2 cloves garlic, minced 1 tablespoon freshly squeezed lemon juice 1 teaspoon paprika 1 teaspoon dried oregano 1/2 teaspoon salt 1/4 teaspoon black pepper 1/4 cup Kalamata olives, pitted 1/4 cup artichoke hearts, drained and chopped 1/4 cup red onion, thinly sliced 20 bamboo skewers, soaked in water for at least 30 minutes

Marinating the Shrimp Marinating the shrimp is essential to infuse them with the signature Mediterranean flavors. In a large bowl, whisk together olive oil, garlic, lemon juice, paprika, oregano, salt, and black pepper. Add the shrimp and toss to coat. Cover the bowl with plastic wrap and refrigerate for at least 2 hours or overnight, giving the flavors time to meld. Preparing the Skewers While the shrimp is marinating, let's prepare the skewers. Thread one shrimp onto each bamboo skewer, leaving a small space between each shrimp. You should end up with about 20 skewers. Thread an olive, artichoke heart, and red onion slice onto each skewer, making sure to leave some space between each component. Grilling the Skewers Preheat your grill to medium-high heat. Thread the skewers onto the grill, ensuring they are not touching each other. Cook the skewers for 2-3 minutes per side or until the shrimp are pink and cooked through. The Mediterranean herbs and spices will infuse a rich and savory aroma into the air, making your stomach growl with anticipation. Serving Suggestions Serve the sizzling Mediterranean Shrimp Skewers immediately, garnished with a sprinkle of fresh parsley and a dollop of tzatziki sauce, if desired. These skewers are perfect for a variety of occasions:

As an appetizer or starter at your next dinner party As a quick weeknight dinner, served with a side of grilled pita bread and a simple green salad At your next outdoor gathering or picnic, where you can serve the skewers alongside a refreshing side of couscous or quinoa salad

Variations and Tips To add an extra layer of flavor, try adding some sliced bell peppers, zucchini, or cherry tomatoes to the skewers. Make sure to adjust the cooking time accordingly. To avoid overcrowding the grill, cook the skewers in batches if necessary. Also, be gentle when turning the skewers to prevent the shrimp from falling off. If you prefer to cook the skewers in the oven, preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C) and cook for 10-12 minutes, or until the shrimp are pink and cooked through. Conclusion Sizzling Mediterranean Shrimp Skewers are a perfect representation of the vibrant flavors and aromas of the Mediterranean diet. This recipe is a great way to spice up your grilling game and impress your friends and family with the bold flavors of Greece. With its straightforward preparation and impressive presentation, this dish is sure to become a staple in your culinary repertoire.
