Creamy and Indulgent Oreo Cheesecake: A Dessert Lover's Delight


If you’re a fan of rich, creamy desserts and have a soft spot for Oreo cookies, then this Oreo cheesecake recipe is about to become your new favorite. Indulge your sweet tooth with this decadent treat that combines the classic flavors of Oreo cookies with the velvety smoothness of cheesecake. Whether you’re hosting a dinner party, celebrating a special occasion, or simply craving a luxurious dessert, this Oreo cheesecake is sure to impress and satisfy.

Ingredients

For the Crust:

  • 24 Oreo cookies
  • 1/4 cup unsalted butter, melted

For the Cheesecake Filling:

  • 32 oz cream cheese, softened
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 4 large eggs
  • 1 cup sour cream
  • 20 Oreo cookies, coarsely chopped

For the Topping (Optional):

  • Whipped cream
  • Oreo cookies, halved

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Crust:

    • Preheat the oven to 325°F (163°C).
    • In a food processor, pulse the Oreo cookies until they form fine crumbs.
    • In a mixing bowl, combine the Oreo crumbs and melted butter. Press the mixture into the bottom of a 9-inch springform pan. Bake for 10 minutes, then set aside to cool.

  2. Make the Cheesecake Filling:

    • In a large bowl, beat the cream cheese until smooth. Add the granulated sugar and vanilla extract, and mix until combined.
    • Add the eggs one at a time, mixing well after each addition. Be careful not to overmix.
    • Gently fold in the sour cream and chopped Oreo cookies until evenly distributed.
    • Pour the filling over the prepared crust, smoothing the top with a spatula.

  3. Bake and Chill:

    • Place the springform pan on a baking sheet and bake for 45-50 minutes, or until the edges are set but the center still has a slight jiggle.
    • Turn off the oven and let the cheesecake cool in the oven with the door ajar for 1 hour.
    • Remove the cheesecake from the oven and run a knife around the edges to loosen. Allow it to cool completely, then refrigerate for at least 4 hours, or preferably overnight.

  4. Add the Topping (Optional):

    • Before serving, decorate the chilled cheesecake with whipped cream and halved Oreo cookies if desired.

Serving and Storage

Once chilled and set, carefully remove the cheesecake from the springform pan and slice into generous portions. This creamy and indulgent Oreo cheesecake is best enjoyed cold, straight from the refrigerator. Store any leftovers covered in the refrigerator for up to 5 days, if it lasts that long!

Conclusion

Indulge in the irresistible combination of velvety cheesecake and the timeless flavor of Oreo cookies with this delightful dessert. Whether you’re a seasoned baker or a novice in the kitchen, this Oreo cheesecake recipe is a must-try for anyone with a sweet tooth. Impress your friends and family with this luxurious treat, and savor every creamy, decadent bite. Happy baking!
