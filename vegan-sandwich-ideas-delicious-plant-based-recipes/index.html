Vegan Sandwich Ideas: Delicious Plant-Based Recipes


Sandwiches are a universal favorite, offering endless possibilities for creativity and flavor. For those following a plant-based diet, the world of vegan sandwiches is rich with vibrant ingredients, textures, and tastes. Whether you’re a seasoned vegan or just looking to incorporate more plant-based meals into your diet, these vegan sandwich ideas will inspire you to create delicious, satisfying meals that are both nutritious and easy to prepare.

1. Classic Chickpea Salad Sandwich

Ingredients:

  • 1 can of chickpeas, drained and rinsed
  • 2 tablespoons vegan mayonnaise
  • 1 tablespoon Dijon mustard
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 1 celery stalk, finely chopped
  • 1/4 red onion, finely chopped
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Whole grain bread or your choice of bread
  • Lettuce leaves for garnish

Instructions:

  1. In a mixing bowl, mash the chickpeas with a fork until they are mostly broken down but still have some texture.
  2. Add the vegan mayonnaise, Dijon mustard, lemon juice, celery, and red onion. Mix until well combined.
  3. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  4. Spread the chickpea salad onto slices of whole grain bread and top with fresh lettuce leaves.
  5. Close the sandwich, slice in half, and enjoy!

This classic chickpea salad sandwich is not only easy to make but also packed with protein and fiber, making it a perfect lunch option.

2. Grilled Vegetable and Hummus Sandwich

Ingredients:

  • 1 zucchini, sliced
  • 1 bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 eggplant, sliced
  • 1 cup mushrooms, sliced
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1/2 cup hummus
  • Ciabatta or focaccia bread
  • Fresh basil leaves

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your grill or grill pan over medium heat.
  2. Toss the sliced vegetables in olive oil, salt, and pepper.
  3. Grill the vegetables for about 5-7 minutes on each side until they are tender and have nice grill marks.
  4. Spread hummus on both sides of the ciabatta or focaccia bread.
  5. Layer the grilled vegetables on one slice of bread and top with fresh basil leaves.
  6. Close the sandwich, slice, and serve warm.

This grilled vegetable and hummus sandwich is bursting with flavor and is a great way to use seasonal vegetables.

3. Avocado and Tomato Sandwich

Ingredients:

  • 1 ripe avocado
  • 1 large tomato, sliced
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 slices of sourdough or whole grain bread
  • Fresh arugula or spinach

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, mash the avocado with lemon juice, salt, and pepper.
  2. Toast the bread slices until golden brown.
  3. Spread the mashed avocado generously on one slice of bread.
  4. Layer the tomato slices on top of the avocado and add fresh arugula or spinach.
  5. Top with the second slice of bread, slice in half, and enjoy!

This avocado and tomato sandwich is simple yet incredibly satisfying, perfect for a quick lunch or snack.

4. BBQ Jackfruit Sandwich

Ingredients:

  • 1 can young green jackfruit in water or brine, drained and rinsed
  • 1/2 cup BBQ sauce (vegan)
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1/2 onion, sliced
  • 1/2 cup coleslaw (vegan)
  • Burger buns

Instructions:

  1. In a skillet, heat olive oil over medium heat. Add the sliced onion and sauté until translucent.
  2. Add the jackfruit to the skillet and use a fork to shred it into smaller pieces.
  3. Pour in the BBQ sauce and stir to combine. Cook for about 10 minutes, allowing the jackfruit to absorb the flavors.
  4. Toast the burger buns and layer the BBQ jackfruit on the bottom half.
  5. Top with vegan coleslaw and close the sandwich.

This BBQ jackfruit sandwich is a fantastic alternative to pulled pork, offering a smoky flavor and satisfying texture.

5. Mediterranean Falafel Wrap

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup cooked chickpeas
  • 1/4 cup fresh parsley, chopped
  • 1/4 cup fresh cilantro, chopped
  • 1 garlic clove
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • 1 teaspoon coriander
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1/4 cup flour (or chickpea flour)
  • Pita bread or wraps
  • Lettuce, tomato, cucumber, and tahini sauce for serving

Instructions:

  1. In a food processor, combine chickpeas, parsley, cilantro, garlic, cumin, coriander, salt, and pepper. Pulse until a coarse mixture forms.
