Pumpkin Seed Pesto Pasta: A Wholesome Fall Meal


Welcome to the ultimate fall comfort food recipe - Pumpkin Seed Pesto Pasta! This delicious and wholesome dish embodies the flavors of autumn, combining the nuttiness of pumpkin seeds with the freshness of basil to create a mouthwatering pesto sauce that pairs perfectly with al dente pasta. Whether you’re looking for a quick weeknight dinner or a dish to impress guests at a fall gathering, this Pumpkin Seed Pesto Pasta is sure to become a new favorite in your recipe repertoire.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup raw pumpkin seeds
  • 2 cups fresh basil leaves
  • 3 cloves garlic
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/2 cup extra virgin olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 pound pasta of your choice (linguine or spaghetti work well)

Instructions

Step 1: Toast the Pumpkin Seeds

Start by toasting the raw pumpkin seeds in a dry skillet over medium heat. Stir them frequently until they become golden and fragrant, which should take about 5-7 minutes. Be sure to keep a close eye on them to prevent burning. Once toasted, remove the seeds from the heat and let them cool.

Step 2: Prepare the Pesto

In a food processor, combine the toasted pumpkin seeds, fresh basil leaves, garlic, and grated Parmesan cheese. Pulse the mixture until the ingredients are finely chopped. While the food processor is running, gradually pour in the olive oil until the pesto reaches a smooth and creamy consistency. Season with salt and pepper to taste, and give it a final pulse to incorporate the seasoning.

Step 3: Cook the Pasta

Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil and cook the pasta according to the package instructions until it is al dente. Before draining the pasta, reserve a cup of the pasta cooking water.

Step 4: Combine and Serve

Once the pasta is cooked, drain it and return it to the pot. Add the prepared pumpkin seed pesto to the pasta and toss to coat, adding a splash of the reserved pasta water if needed to loosen the sauce. The starchy pasta water will help the pesto adhere to the pasta, creating a creamy and flavorful coating.

Step 5: Garnish and Enjoy

Transfer the Pumpkin Seed Pesto Pasta to serving plates and garnish with an extra sprinkle of grated Parmesan cheese and a few whole basil leaves. Serve immediately and savor the delightful flavors of fall in every bite.

Why Pumpkin Seed Pesto Pasta?

Pumpkin Seed Pesto Pasta is not only a delicious meal, but it also offers a range of health benefits. Pumpkin seeds are an excellent source of protein, healthy fats, and essential minerals such as magnesium and zinc. Basil, a key ingredient in the pesto, provides a dose of vitamin K and antioxidants. When combined with whole grain pasta, this dish becomes a wholesome and satisfying option for a balanced diet.

Conclusion

As the crisp air and colorful leaves signal the arrival of fall, Pumpkin Seed Pesto Pasta is a delightful way to celebrate the season’s flavors. Whether enjoyed as a family dinner or a cozy meal for one, this recipe is a versatile and nutritious addition to your fall culinary lineup. So, gather your ingredients, embrace the spirit of autumn, and indulge in the heartwarming goodness of Pumpkin Seed Pesto Pasta!
