Olive Oil and Orange Blossom Cake: Mediterranean Flavors


Ah, the Mediterranean. With its sun-kissed landscapes, crystal-clear waters, and tantalizing cuisine, what’s not to love? Today, we are diving into the heart of the Mediterranean by crafting an Olive Oil and Orange Blossom Cake. A delightful, savory cake that captures the essence of this enchanting region. Indulge in the freshness and fragrant appeal of this simple, yet sophisticated dessert.

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 3/4 cup extra virgin olive oil
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 cup orange blossom water
  • 1/2 cup freshly squeezed orange juice
  • 2 teaspoons grated orange zest
  • Powdered sugar for dusting
  • Fresh mint leaves for garnish (optional)

Instructions:

Before we start, preheat your oven to 350°F (180°C) and grease a 9-inch springform pan with butter. Then, in a medium bowl, mix together the flour, baking powder, and salt. Set this aside.

Next, in a large mixing bowl, cream together the softened butter and sugar until light and fluffy. This should take about 3 minutes. Then, add the vanilla extract, olive oil, and eggs, one at a time, mixing well between each addition.

Now, gently stir in the orange blossom water, orange juice, and orange zest. The mixture should be well-combined. Slowly incorporate the dry ingredients you prepared earlier, being careful not to overmix the batter.

Pour the batter into the prepared springform pan and smooth down the surface. Bake for approximately 45-50 minutes or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean. Allow the cake to cool in the pan for about 10 minutes before transferring it to a wire rack to cool completely.

Once the cake has cooled, it’s time to decorate. Sift powdered sugar generously over the top of the cake, and if you’d like, garnish it with fresh mint leaves.

As though freshly harvested by the warm Mediterranean sun, this Olive Oil and Orange Blossom Cake will without a doubt transport your taste buds to a land of aromatic groves, sunflowers, and pristine beaches. Pure, rich, and delectable—each bite offers up a slice of heaven, infused with the exquisite combination of orange blossom water, zesty orange, and extra virgin olive oil—an outstanding trio that always goes hand in hand with the classic flavors of the Mediterranean.

Not to mention, versatility is at your fingertips. You could drizzle it with a sweet orange syrup, or perhaps serve it alongside a scoop of vanilla ice cream—it’s all up to you. And hey, why not sprinkle some crushed pistachios or spices like cinnamon on there while you’re at it? Let your culinary creativity run wild!

This Olive Oil and Orange Blossom Cake is the epitome of the simplicity and richness of Mediterranean flavors, unfailingly and consistently a scrumptious treat to be enjoyed with family and friends. Almond, a resounding sense of refreshment combined with sophistication, why not make a dessert worthy of the enchanting beauty of the Mediterranean sea. Enjoy!
