Thai Green Curry with Chicken: A Classic Thai Dish


If you’re looking to explore the vibrant and aromatic world of Thai cuisine, then Thai Green Curry with Chicken is an absolute must-try. This classic dish is a perfect blend of flavors, combining the richness of coconut milk with the fresh, herbal notes of green curry paste. The tender chunks of chicken, paired with a medley of vegetables, create a dish that’s not only visually appealing but also a delight for the taste buds. So, let’s dive into the art of crafting this exquisite Thai delicacy in the comfort of your own kitchen.

Ingredients:

  • 2 lbs (900g) boneless, skinless chicken breasts or thighs, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 2 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 3-4 tbsp green curry paste
  • 1 can (14 oz) coconut milk
  • 1 cup chicken broth
  • 1 tbsp fish sauce
  • 2 tsp palm sugar or brown sugar
  • 1 cup bamboo shoots, sliced
  • 1 red bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 green bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 cup Thai basil leaves
  • 4-5 kaffir lime leaves
  • 1-2 red chili peppers, thinly sliced (optional for extra heat)
  • Steamed jasmine rice, for serving

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the Chicken: In a large, deep skillet or wok, heat the vegetable oil over medium heat. Add the green curry paste and cook for a minute to release its fragrance. Then, add the chicken pieces and stir-fry until they are no longer pink on the outside.

  2. Simmer with Aromatics: Pour in the coconut milk and chicken broth. Stir in the fish sauce and palm sugar. Toss in the bamboo shoots, bell peppers, and kaffir lime leaves. Bring the mixture to a gentle simmer and let it cook for about 15-20 minutes, stirring occasionally, until the chicken is tender and the flavors have melded together beautifully.

  3. Finish with Freshness: Once the chicken is cooked through and the vegetables are tender yet still vibrant, stir in the Thai basil leaves and red chili peppers (if using). Let the curry simmer for a couple more minutes to allow the basil to infuse its aroma into the dish.

  4. Serve and Enjoy: Remove the kaffir lime leaves from the curry. Serve the Thai Green Curry with Chicken hot, alongside steamed jasmine rice. The fragrant and creamy curry is a perfect complement to the fluffy rice, creating a harmonious balance of flavors and textures.

Tips and Tricks:

  • For an extra burst of flavor, consider adding a squeeze of fresh lime juice just before serving.
  • If you prefer a thicker curry, you can let it simmer for a little longer to reduce the liquid.
  • Adjust the spiciness by varying the amount of green curry paste and red chili peppers according to your preference.

Thai Green Curry with Chicken is a dish that effortlessly captures the essence of Thai cuisine with its vibrant colors, aromatic flavors, and a perfect blend of creamy and spicy notes. It’s a delightful journey for the senses and a wonderful way to introduce your palate to the wonders of Thai cooking. So, gather your ingredients, embrace the spirit of adventure, and embark on an unforgettable culinary experience right in your own kitchen.

Whether you’re a seasoned home cook or a culinary enthusiast looking to expand your repertoire, this classic Thai dish is sure to become a favorite in your cooking arsenal. The beauty of Thai Green Curry with Chicken lies in its versatility – you can customize the heat level, swap out vegetables, or even try it with tofu for a vegetarian twist. So, roll up your sleeves, unleash your creativity, and savor the magic of Thai cuisine with this timeless masterpiece.
