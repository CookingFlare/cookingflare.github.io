Citrus Shortbread Cookies: Zesty and Refreshing Treat


If you’re a fan of citrus flavors and crave a delightful, buttery treat to accompany your afternoon tea or coffee, then these Citrus Shortbread Cookies are just the thing for you. With the perfect balance of zesty citrus and rich butter, these cookies are a delightful indulgence that will brighten up your day. This recipe is simple to follow, and the result is a batch of delicious, melt-in-your-mouth cookies that are perfect for any occasion.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1/2 cup powdered sugar
  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • Zest of 1 lemon
  • Zest of 1 orange
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • Pinch of salt

Instructions

  1. In a large mixing bowl, cream together the softened butter and powdered sugar until light and fluffy.

  2. Add the lemon zest, orange zest, and vanilla extract to the butter mixture. Mix until well combined.

  3. Sift the all-purpose flour and salt into the bowl with the butter mixture. Gently fold the dry ingredients into the wet ingredients until a soft dough forms.

  4. Transfer the dough onto a piece of parchment paper and shape it into a log, about 2 inches in diameter. Wrap the log in the parchment paper and refrigerate for at least 1 hour, or until firm.

  5. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C). Line a baking sheet with parchment paper.

  6. Once the dough is firm, remove it from the refrigerator and slice it into 1/4-inch thick rounds. Place the rounds onto the prepared baking sheet, leaving some space between each cookie.

  7. Bake the cookies for 12-15 minutes, or until the edges are lightly golden. Remove from the oven and allow the cookies to cool on the baking sheet for 5 minutes before transferring them to a wire rack to cool completely.

  8. Once the cookies are completely cooled, they are ready to be enjoyed!

Serving Suggestions

These zesty Citrus Shortbread Cookies are perfect for serving alongside a cup of hot tea or coffee. Their delicate citrus flavor pairs beautifully with the warm, comforting notes of a hot beverage. They also make a lovely addition to a dessert platter or as a sweet treat for a gathering with friends and family.

Storage

To keep these cookies fresh and delicious, store them in an airtight container at room temperature for up to one week. You can also freeze the baked cookies for up to one month, allowing you to enjoy a citrusy treat whenever the craving strikes.

Final Thoughts

With their delicate citrus flavor and rich, buttery texture, these Citrus Shortbread Cookies are a delightful treat that will brighten up any day. Whether you’re baking them for yourself or to share with loved ones, these cookies are sure to be a hit. So, gather your ingredients and get ready to indulge in the delightful aroma and taste of these zesty, refreshing treats. Happy baking!

This delightful recipe for Citrus Shortbread Cookies will surely elevate your baking game and add a burst of flavor to your day. Enjoy the zesty and refreshing taste of these delightful cookies, and share the joy with friends and family. Happy baking!
