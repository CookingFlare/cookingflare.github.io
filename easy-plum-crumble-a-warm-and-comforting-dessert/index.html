Easy Plum Crumble: A Warm and Comforting Dessert


Are you looking for a simple yet delicious dessert to satisfy your sweet tooth? Look no further than this easy plum crumble recipe! With juicy plums, a buttery crumbly topping, and a warm, comforting flavor, this dessert is sure to become a family favorite.

Ingredients

For the filling:

  • 6-8 ripe plums, pitted and sliced
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 tablespoon all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon lemon juice
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon

For the crumble topping:

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup old-fashioned rolled oats
  • 1/2 cup packed brown sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, cold and cut into small cubes

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C). Grease a baking dish with butter or cooking spray and set aside.

  2. In a large mixing bowl, combine the sliced plums, granulated sugar, flour, lemon juice, and ground cinnamon. Toss until the plums are evenly coated. Transfer the mixture to the greased baking dish and set aside.

  3. In another bowl, prepare the crumble topping. Combine the all-purpose flour, rolled oats, brown sugar, ground cinnamon, and salt. Mix well to ensure all the ingredients are evenly distributed.

  4. Add the cold cubed butter to the crumble mixture. Using your fingertips or a pastry cutter, gently work the butter into the dry ingredients until the mixture resembles coarse crumbs. Be careful not to overmix; you want to keep some larger crumbs for added texture.

  5. Sprinkle the crumble topping evenly over the plum mixture in the baking dish. Make sure to cover the plums completely.

  6. Place the baking dish in the preheated oven and bake for about 35-40 minutes, or until the plum filling is bubbly, and the crumble topping is golden brown.

  7. Once baked, remove the plum crumble from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes before serving. This dessert is best enjoyed warm, but it’s equally delicious at room temperature.

  8. Serve the plum crumble on its own or with a scoop of vanilla ice cream or a dollop of whipped cream for an extra indulgence. The creamy sweetness of the ice cream or whipped cream pairs perfectly with the tartness of the plums and the buttery crumble.

Tips and Variations

  • If you prefer a sweeter filling, you can increase the amount of granulated sugar in the plum mixture.
  • Feel free to add a handful of chopped nuts, such as almonds or walnuts, to the crumble topping for some extra crunch.
  • For a twist on the classic recipe, you can substitute plums with other stone fruits like peaches or nectarines.
  • Leftovers can be stored in an airtight container in the refrigerator for up to three days. Simply reheat in the oven or microwave before serving.

So, there you have it! A simple and delightful recipe for an easy plum crumble that will warm your heart and satisfy your cravings. Whether you’re hosting a dinner party or simply treating yourself, this dessert is guaranteed to impress. Give it a try and let the sweet aroma of baked plums fill your home. Enjoy!
