Firecracker Popcorn: Spicy and Addictive Fourth of July Snack


Fireworks, barbecues, and good company - Fourth of July is a time for celebration and indulgence. And what better way to spice up your festivities than with a fiery and addictive snack like Firecracker Popcorn? This simple and flavorful treat will leave your guests reaching for more, making it a perfect addition to your Fourth of July spread. So let’s dive right into the recipe and get ready to ignite your taste buds!

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup popcorn kernels
  • 3 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1 teaspoon chili powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon cayenne pepper
  • 1/2 teaspoon smoked paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon onion powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 2 tablespoons unsalted butter, melted
  • 1 tablespoon hot sauce (adjust according to your spice preference)
  • 1 tablespoon honey

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 300Â°F (150Â°C). Line a baking sheet with parchment paper and set aside.

  2. In a large pot with a tight-fitting lid, heat the vegetable oil over medium-high heat. Add the popcorn kernels and cover the pot with the lid. Shake the pot gently while the popcorn pops to prevent burning. Once the popping slows down, remove the pot from the heat and let it sit for a minute to allow any remaining kernels to pop.

  3. In a small bowl, combine the chili powder, cayenne pepper, smoked paprika, garlic powder, onion powder, salt, and black pepper. Mix well to create the spice blend.

  4. In a separate microwave-safe bowl, melt the butter. Add the hot sauce and honey to the melted butter and stir until well combined.

  5. Pour the spice blend over the popcorn, followed by the butter mixture. Using a spatula or your hands (be sure to use gloves if you’re sensitive to spice), gently toss the popcorn until it is evenly coated with the spices and butter.

  6. Spread the coated popcorn in a single layer on the prepared baking sheet.

  7. Bake the popcorn in the preheated oven for 10-15 minutes, or until it reaches your desired level of crispiness. Keep a close eye on it to prevent burning.

  8. Once the popcorn is done baking, remove it from the oven and let it cool completely on the baking sheet. As it cools, the flavors will intensify and the popcorn will become even crunchier.

  9. Transfer the cooled Firecracker Popcorn to a serving bowl or individual snack bags. It’s now ready to be devoured!

Tips and Variations

  • Feel free to adjust the spice levels according to your preference. If you want it extra spicy, add more cayenne pepper or hot sauce. If you prefer a milder flavor, reduce the amount of chili powder and cayenne pepper.
  • For an added kick, sprinkle some grated Parmesan cheese over the popcorn before baking. The combination of spicy and cheesy flavors will take this snack to a whole new level.
  • Experiment with different spice blends to create unique variations. Try adding a touch of cinnamon for a hint of sweetness, or swap the smoked paprika for chipotle powder for a smoky twist.

Firecracker Popcorn is not only a great snack for Fourth of July, but it’s also perfect for game nights, movie marathons, or any time you’re craving a little heat and crunch. This addictive treat will surely spark joy among your family and friends, so give it a try and let the fireworks begin!
