Savory Dairy-Free Garlic Mashed Potatoes Recipe


If you’re looking for a delicious and creamy side dish that’s also dairy-free, then you’re in for a treat with this savory garlic mashed potatoes recipe. It’s a perfect accompaniment to any main course and is sure to impress your family and friends. The secret to its creaminess lies in a surprising ingredient that will leave you wanting more. Let’s dive into the recipe and discover how to make this flavorful dish.

Ingredients

  • 2 pounds of Yukon Gold potatoes, peeled and quartered
  • 4 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1/2 cup of vegetable broth
  • 1/4 cup of olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons of nutritional yeast
  • 1 teaspoon of salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon of black pepper
  • Fresh chives for garnish

Instructions

  1. Boil the Potatoes: Place the peeled and quartered potatoes in a large pot and cover them with cold water. Add a pinch of salt and bring the water to a boil. Let the potatoes cook for about 15-20 minutes or until they are fork-tender.

  2. Prepare the Garlic: While the potatoes are boiling, heat the olive oil in a small pan over medium heat. Add the minced garlic and sauté for 1-2 minutes until it becomes fragrant. Be careful not to let it brown.

  3. Mash the Potatoes: Once the potatoes are cooked, drain them and return them to the pot. Mash the potatoes using a potato masher until they reach your desired consistency.

  4. Add Flavor: Pour in the sautéed garlic with the olive oil, vegetable broth, nutritional yeast, salt, and black pepper. Mix everything together until well combined and creamy. Adjust the seasoning to taste.

  5. Serve: Transfer the mashed potatoes to a serving dish and garnish with fresh chives for a pop of color and added flavor.

Why This Recipe Works

The use of nutritional yeast, a popular cheese substitute, lends a creamy and slightly cheesy flavor to the mashed potatoes, making them rich and satisfying without the need for dairy. The addition of garlic adds a delightful savory note, elevating the overall taste of the dish. The vegetable broth contributes depth of flavor, while the olive oil ensures a velvety texture that will rival any traditional mashed potato recipe.

Tips and Variations

  • For an extra creamy texture, use a hand mixer or immersion blender to whip the mashed potatoes.
  • Experiment with different herbs such as rosemary or thyme for added aromatic complexity.
  • If you prefer a smoother consistency, you can peel the potatoes before boiling them.

Final Thoughts

This savory dairy-free garlic mashed potatoes recipe is a testament to the fact that you don’t need dairy to create a luscious and satisfying dish. Whether you’re catering to dietary restrictions or simply looking to try something new, these mashed potatoes are a versatile and flavorful addition to any meal. So, next time you’re craving a comforting side dish, give this recipe a try and savor the creamy goodness without the dairy.

Enjoy!

This recipe is sure to impress with its creamy and savory flavors, perfect for those who are dairy-free or simply looking for a new twist on a classic dish. The use of nutritional yeast and garlic creates a rich and satisfying flavor profile, making these mashed potatoes a standout side dish. Whether you’re serving them for a holiday feast or a weeknight dinner, this recipe is bound to become a favorite in your culinary repertoire.
