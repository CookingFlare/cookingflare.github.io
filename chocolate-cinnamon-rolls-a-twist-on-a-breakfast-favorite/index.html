Chocolate Cinnamon Rolls: A Twist on a Breakfast Favorite


Who doesn’t love the smell of freshly baked cinnamon rolls wafting through the kitchen in the morning? The combination of warm, gooey dough and fragrant cinnamon sugar is enough to make anyone’s mouth water. Now, imagine taking that classic breakfast treat to the next level by adding a decadent chocolate twist. That’s right – we’re talking about Chocolate Cinnamon Rolls!

The Perfect Marriage of Chocolate and Cinnamon

Chocolate and cinnamon may seem like an unconventional pairing, but trust me when I say that they complement each other beautifully in this recipe. The rich, slightly bitter notes of the chocolate perfectly balance the warm, spicy sweetness of the cinnamon, creating a flavor profile that’s nothing short of irresistible.

Ingredients

For the Dough

  • 4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/3 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 2 1/4 teaspoons instant yeast
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1/4 cup unsalted butter
  • 2 large eggs

For the Filling

  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1 cup brown sugar
  • 2 tablespoons ground cinnamon
  • 1 cup semi-sweet chocolate chips

For the Chocolate Glaze

  • 1 cup powdered sugar
  • 2 tablespoons cocoa powder
  • 3-4 tablespoons milk
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Instructions

Step 1: Prepare the Dough

  1. In a large mixing bowl, combine 2 cups of flour, sugar, salt, and yeast.
  2. In a small saucepan, heat the milk and butter until the mixture reaches 120-130°F.
  3. Add the warm milk mixture to the dry ingredients and mix well.
  4. Add the eggs and remaining flour, mixing until a soft dough forms.
  5. Turn the dough out onto a lightly floured surface and knead until smooth and elastic.

Step 2: Roll Out the Dough

  1. Roll the dough into a 16x21-inch rectangle.
  2. Spread the softened butter over the dough, leaving a 1/4-inch border around the edges.
  3. In a small bowl, combine the brown sugar and cinnamon. Sprinkle this mixture over the buttered dough, then top with the chocolate chips.

Step 3: Roll and Cut

  1. Starting with the long edge, roll the dough into a tight log.
  2. Cut the log into 12 equal pieces and place them in a greased 9x13-inch baking pan.
  3. Cover the rolls and let them rise in a warm place for 30 minutes.

Step 4: Bake

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F.
  2. Once the rolls have risen, bake them for 20-25 minutes or until golden brown.

Step 5: Make the Chocolate Glaze

  1. In a small bowl, whisk together the powdered sugar, cocoa powder, milk, and vanilla extract until smooth.
  2. Drizzle the glaze over the warm cinnamon rolls.

Enjoy Your Chocolate Cinnamon Rolls!

These Chocolate Cinnamon Rolls are best enjoyed fresh out of the oven, while the chocolate is still melty and the glaze is gooey. Pair them with a steaming cup of coffee or a tall glass of milk for the ultimate breakfast indulgence. The combination of flavors and textures in these rolls is sure to make them a new family favorite.

So, the next time you’re craving a sweet breakfast treat, consider giving these Chocolate Cinnamon Rolls a try. They’re a delightful twist on a classic favorite, and your taste buds will thank you for it!
