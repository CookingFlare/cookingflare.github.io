Paleo Chicken Salad Recipes: 5 Delicious and Grain-Free Options


Are you following a Paleo diet and looking for some delicious and healthy chicken salad recipes? Look no further! In this post, we’ve compiled five mouthwatering Paleo chicken salad recipes that are not only delicious but also grain-free. These recipes are packed with flavor and nutrients, making them the perfect addition to your Paleo meal rotation. Whether you’re looking for a light lunch or a satisfying dinner, these recipes have got you covered. Let’s dive in!

1. Classic Paleo Chicken Salad

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups cooked chicken, shredded
  • 1/2 cup Paleo mayonnaise
  • 1 celery stalk, finely chopped
  • 1/4 cup red onion, finely chopped
  • 1/4 cup fresh parsley, chopped
  • Juice of 1/2 lemon
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a large bowl, combine the shredded chicken, Paleo mayonnaise, celery, red onion, parsley, lemon juice, salt, and pepper.
  2. Stir well until all the ingredients are evenly combined.
  3. Serve the chicken salad on a bed of lettuce or enjoy it as a sandwich filling.

2. Avocado Lime Chicken Salad

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups cooked chicken, diced
  • 1 ripe avocado, mashed
  • Juice of 1 lime
  • 1/4 cup fresh cilantro, chopped
  • 1/4 cup red bell pepper, diced
  • 1/4 cup red onion, diced
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, combine the diced chicken, mashed avocado, lime juice, cilantro, red bell pepper, red onion, salt, and pepper.
  2. Mix well until all the ingredients are thoroughly combined.
  3. Chill the salad in the refrigerator for at least 30 minutes before serving.

3. Greek Paleo Chicken Salad

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups cooked chicken, cubed
  • 1/2 cup cucumber, diced
  • 1/2 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1/4 cup kalamata olives, pitted and sliced
  • 1/4 cup red onion, thinly sliced
  • 1/4 cup fresh parsley, chopped
  • 2 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a large bowl, combine the chicken, cucumber, cherry tomatoes, kalamata olives, red onion, and parsley.
  2. In a separate small bowl, whisk together the olive oil, lemon juice, dried oregano, salt, and pepper.
  3. Pour the dressing over the salad and toss gently to coat all the ingredients.
  4. Allow the salad to marinate in the refrigerator for at least 1 hour before serving.

4. Curry Chicken Salad

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups cooked chicken, shredded
  • 1/2 cup Paleo mayonnaise
  • 2 tablespoons curry powder
  • 1/4 cup raisins
  • 1/4 cup green apple, diced
  • 1/4 cup celery, diced
  • 1/4 cup red onion, finely chopped
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a large bowl, combine the shredded chicken, Paleo mayonnaise, curry powder, raisins, green apple, celery, red onion, salt, and pepper.
  2. Mix well until all the ingredients are evenly incorporated.
  3. Refrigerate the salad for at least 1 hour to allow the flavors to meld together.

5. Asian Chicken Salad

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups cooked chicken, sliced
  • 1 cup Napa cabbage, thinly sliced
  • 1/2 cup carrots, shredded
  • 1/2 cup red bell pepper, thinly sliced
  • 1/4 cup green onions, sliced
  • 1/4 cup almonds, toasted and chopped
  • 2 tablespoons coconut aminos
  • 1 tablespoon rice vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon sesame oil
  • 1 teaspoon grated ginger
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a large bowl, combine the sliced chicken, Napa cabbage, carrots, red bell pepper, green onions, and almonds.
  2. In a separate small bowl, whisk together the coconut aminos, rice vinegar, sesame oil, grated ginger, salt, and pepper.
  3. Drizzle the dressing over the salad and toss gently to coat all the ingredients.
  4. Let the salad sit for a few minutes before serving to allow the flavors to meld together.

There you have it! Five delicious and grain-free Paleo chicken salad recipes that will satisfy your taste buds and keep you on track with your Paleo lifestyle. These salads are not only easy to make but also bursting with flavors and textures. Whether you prefer a classic chicken salad or want to experiment with different flavors, these recipes have something for everyone. Enjoy a healthy and satisfying meal with these Paleo chicken salad options!

Remember to experiment with the ingredients and adjust them to your liking. Feel free to add your favorite vegetables or spices to make these recipes your own. Happy cooking and enjoy your grain-free Paleo chicken salads!
