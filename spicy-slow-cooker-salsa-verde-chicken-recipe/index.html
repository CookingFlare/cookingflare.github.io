Spicy Slow Cooker Salsa Verde Chicken Recipe


If you’re on the hunt for a flavorful, effortless meal that will impress your family and friends, look no further than this Spicy Slow Cooker Salsa Verde Chicken recipe. This dish combines tender chicken with a zesty salsa verde, creating a perfect blend of heat and flavor that is sure to satisfy your taste buds. Not only is this recipe simple to prepare, but it also allows you to enjoy a delicious homemade meal with minimal effort. Let’s dive into how to create this culinary delight!

Ingredients

To make Spicy Slow Cooker Salsa Verde Chicken, you’ll need the following ingredients:

  • 2 pounds boneless, skinless chicken thighs (or breasts, if you prefer)
  • 1 cup salsa verde (store-bought or homemade)
  • 1 can (15 oz) black beans, drained and rinsed
  • 1 cup corn kernels (fresh, frozen, or canned)
  • 1 medium onion, diced
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1-2 jalapeño peppers, diced (adjust based on your heat preference)
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon smoked paprika
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • Fresh cilantro, for garnish
  • Lime wedges, for serving

Equipment Needed

  • Slow cooker
  • Cutting board
  • Knife
  • Measuring cups and spoons
  • Serving spoon

Preparation Steps

Step 1: Prepare the Ingredients

Start by gathering all your ingredients. Chop the onion, dice the jalapeños, and mince the garlic. If you’re using fresh corn, cut the kernels off the cob. This step will help streamline the cooking process and ensure everything is ready to go.

Step 2: Layer the Ingredients in the Slow Cooker

In your slow cooker, start by placing the chicken thighs at the bottom. This allows the chicken to absorb all the wonderful flavors from the salsa and spices as it cooks. Next, sprinkle the diced onion, minced garlic, and jalapeños over the chicken.

Step 3: Add the Salsa Verde and More

Pour the salsa verde evenly over the chicken and vegetables. Then, add the black beans and corn. Sprinkle the ground cumin and smoked paprika over the top, along with a pinch of salt and pepper. The spices will enhance the flavor of the dish, giving it that unique kick!

Step 4: Slow Cook to Perfection

Cover your slow cooker and set it to low for 6-8 hours or high for 3-4 hours. The slow cooking process allows the chicken to become incredibly tender while soaking up all the flavors from the salsa and spices. If you’re in a rush, the high setting is a great option, but for the best results, opt for low and slow.

Step 5: Shred the Chicken

Once the cooking time is complete, carefully remove the chicken thighs from the slow cooker. Using two forks, shred the chicken into bite-sized pieces. This method allows the chicken to mix beautifully with the salsa verde and other ingredients.

Step 6: Combine and Serve

Return the shredded chicken to the slow cooker, mixing it well with the beans, corn, and salsa. Allow it to simmer on low for an additional 15-30 minutes to let the flavors meld together.

When ready to serve, scoop the spicy salsa verde chicken into bowls. Top with fresh cilantro and a squeeze of lime juice for an added burst of freshness.

Serving Suggestions

This Spicy Slow Cooker Salsa Verde Chicken is incredibly versatile and can be served in various ways:

  • Tacos: Serve the chicken in soft or hard taco shells with your favorite toppings like avocado, cheese, and sour cream.
  • Burrito Bowls: Layer the chicken over rice or quinoa, adding beans, corn, and fresh veggies for a hearty meal.
  • Salads: Use the chicken as a protein-packed topping for a fresh salad, drenching it in additional salsa verde for flavor.
  • Nachos: Pile the chicken on tortilla chips, add cheese, jalapeños, and bake for an irresistible snack.

Storing Leftovers

If you have any leftovers (which is unlikely because this dish is so delicious!), store them in an airtight container in the refrigerator for up to three days. You can easily reheat the chicken in the microwave or on the stovetop. This dish also freezes well, so consider portioning it out for future meals.

Tips for the Best Salsa Verde Chicken

  • Adjust the Heat: If you prefer a milder dish, you can remove the seeds from the jalapeños or use only one pepper. Conversely, if you love heat, feel free to add more jalapeños or even a dash of cayenne pepper.
  • Homemade Salsa Verde: For an extra special touch, try making your own salsa verde. Blend together tomatillos, garlic, onion, cilantro, jalapeños, lime juice, and salt for a fresh, vibrant flavor.
  • Add More Veggies: Feel free to customize the recipe by adding bell peppers, zucchini, or spinach for extra nutrition and flavor.
  • Use Different Cuts of Chicken: While thighs are recommended for their juiciness, you can also use chicken breasts or even a whole chicken cut into pieces.

Health Benefits

This Spicy Slow Cooker Salsa Verde Chicken is not only delicious, but it’s also packed with health benefits. Chicken is a great source of lean protein, while black beans provide fiber and essential nutrients. The corn adds sweetness and a pop of color, making this dish both nutritious and visually appealing.

Conclusion

In conclusion, this Spicy Slow Cooker Salsa Verde Chicken is the ultimate comfort food that’s easy to prepare and bursting with flavor. Whether you’re cooking for a family dinner or meal prepping for the week, this dish will quickly become a favorite in your household. With minimal effort, you can create a meal that’s both satisfying and healthy. So, roll up your sleeves and give this recipe a try – your taste buds will thank you! Enjoy the delicious flavors and the joy of cooking with this fantastic slow cooker recipe.
