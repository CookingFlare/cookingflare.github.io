Delightful Keto Creamy Garlic Parmesan Zucchini Noodles Recipe


Are you looking for a delicious and healthy keto-friendly recipe that will satisfy your cravings while keeping you on track with your low-carb lifestyle? Look no further than this delightful keto creamy garlic parmesan zucchini noodles recipe! Packed with flavor and nutrients, this dish is a perfect choice for anyone following a keto diet or simply looking to add more veggies to their meals.

Ingredients

  • 4 medium zucchinis, spiralized
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1/2 cup grated parmesan cheese
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley for garnish

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Zucchini Noodles

    Start by spiralizing the zucchinis to create long, thin noodles. If you don’t have a spiralizer, you can use a vegetable peeler to create zucchini ribbons.

  2. Cook the Zucchini Noodles

    Heat 1 tablespoon of olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Add the zucchini noodles and sauté for 2-3 minutes, or until they are just tender. Be careful not to overcook them, as zucchini noodles can become mushy if cooked for too long. Once done, set the noodles aside on a plate.

  3. Prepare the Creamy Garlic Parmesan Sauce

    In the same skillet, heat the remaining 1 tablespoon of olive oil over medium heat. Add the minced garlic and sauté for 1 minute, or until fragrant. Pour in the heavy cream and bring it to a gentle simmer. Let the cream reduce slightly for about 5 minutes.

  4. Add the Parmesan Cheese

    Once the cream has thickened, stir in the grated parmesan cheese. Continue to cook the sauce, stirring frequently, until the cheese has melted and the sauce has reached your desired consistency. Season with salt and pepper to taste.

  5. Combine the Zucchini Noodles and Sauce

    Return the cooked zucchini noodles to the skillet and toss them gently in the creamy garlic parmesan sauce until they are well coated.

  6. Serve and Enjoy

    Transfer the zucchini noodles to serving plates, garnish with fresh parsley, and serve immediately. You can also top the dish with grilled chicken or shrimp for added protein, if desired.

Nutritional Information

  • Calories: 280
  • Fat: 22g
  • Carbohydrates: 7g
  • Protein: 15g

Conclusion

This delightful keto creamy garlic parmesan zucchini noodles recipe is a fantastic way to enjoy a comforting and satisfying meal without the guilt. The combination of tender zucchini noodles and rich, creamy sauce will leave you feeling fully satisfied, and the best part is that it’s all keto-friendly! Whether you’re following a low-carb diet or simply looking for a delicious new way to enjoy zucchini, this recipe is sure to become a favorite in your kitchen. Give it a try and savor the delightful flavors of this creamy, garlicky, and parmesan-infused dish!

Remember, the key to a successful keto lifestyle is to find creative and delicious ways to enjoy your meals while staying true to your dietary goals. With recipes like this one, you can indulge in amazing flavors without sacrificing your health and wellness. So go ahead, whip up a batch of these delightful keto creamy garlic parmesan zucchini noodles and treat yourself to a truly satisfying dining experience!
