How to Make Perfect Pancakes Every Time


Pancakes are a classic breakfast or brunch staple that can be enjoyed by people of all ages. Whether you’re a busy professional or a stay-at-home parent, making pancakes from scratch can be a fun and rewarding experience. However, making perfect pancakes every time can be a challenge, especially for those who are new to cooking. In this article, we’ll share our secrets for making perfect pancakes every time, so you can impress your family and friends with your culinary skills.

Step 1: Choose the Right Ingredients

The first step to making perfect pancakes is to choose the right ingredients. You’ll need the following:

  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 3 1/2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 large egg
  • 2 tablespoons unsalted butter, melted
  • Flavorings such as vanilla extract or chocolate chips (optional)

It’s important to use high-quality ingredients, as they will affect the taste and texture of your pancakes. For example, using fresh and high-quality flour will result in a lighter and fluffier pancake.

Step 2: Whisk the Dry Ingredients

In a large bowl, whisk together the flour, baking powder, and salt. Whisking the dry ingredients together will help to distribute them evenly and prevent lumps from forming.

Step 3: Whisk the Wet Ingredients

In a separate bowl, whisk together the sugar, milk, egg, and melted butter. Whisking the wet ingredients together will help to emulsify them and create a smooth batter.

Step 4: Combine the Wet and Dry Ingredients

Add the wet ingredients to the dry ingredients and whisk until just combined. Do not overmix the batter, as this will result in tough and dense pancakes.

Step 5: Add Flavorings (Optional)

If you want to add flavorings such as vanilla extract or chocolate chips to your pancakes, now is the time to do it. Simply stir them in gently until they are evenly distributed throughout the batter.

Step 6: Cook the Pancakes

Heat a non-stick skillet or griddle over medium heat. Grease the skillet or griddle with a small amount of butter or cooking spray. Using a 1/4 cup measuring cup, scoop the batter onto the skillet or griddle. Cook the pancakes for 2-3 minutes, until bubbles appear on the surface and the edges start to dry. Flip the pancakes and cook for an additional 1-2 minutes, until golden brown.

Step 7: Serve and Enjoy

Serve the pancakes hot with your favorite toppings, such as maple syrup, butter, fresh fruit, or whipped cream. You can also store leftover pancakes in an airtight container in the refrigerator for up to 3 days or freeze them for up to 2 months.

Tips and Variations

Here are some tips and variations to help you make perfect pancakes every time:

  • Use high-quality ingredients: Fresh and high-quality ingredients will result in a better-tasting pancake.
  • Don’t overmix the batter: Overmixing the batter will result in tough and dense pancakes.
  • Use the right pan: A non-stick skillet or griddle is essential for making perfect pancakes.
  • Don’t overcrowd the pan: Cook the pancakes in batches if necessary, to ensure that they have enough room to cook evenly.
  • Experiment with flavors: Try adding different flavorings such as cinnamon, nutmeg, or chocolate chips to your pancakes for a unique twist.

By following these steps and tips, you’ll be well on your way to making perfect pancakes every time. Whether you’re a seasoned cook or a beginner, these pancakes are sure to impress your family and friends. So go ahead, get cooking, and enjoy the delicious taste of homemade pancakes!
