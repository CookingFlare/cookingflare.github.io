Tomato and Cucumber Salad with Feta: A Refreshing Summer Salad


Summer is the season of fresh and vibrant flavors, and there’s no better way to celebrate than with a refreshing tomato and cucumber salad. This simple yet satisfying dish is perfect for picnics, barbecues, or as a light meal on its own. With the combination of juicy tomatoes, crisp cucumbers, and tangy feta cheese, this salad is a burst of flavors that will leave you craving for more.

Ingredients

  • 3 large tomatoes, diced
  • 2 cucumbers, sliced
  • 1 small red onion, thinly sliced
  • 1/2 cup crumbled feta cheese
  • 1/4 cup fresh basil leaves, torn into small pieces
  • 2 tablespoons extra-virgin olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon red wine vinegar
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. In a large bowl, combine the diced tomatoes, sliced cucumbers, and thinly sliced red onion.
  2. In a separate small bowl, whisk together the extra-virgin olive oil and red wine vinegar. This simple dressing will add a tangy and slightly sweet flavor to the salad.
  3. Drizzle the dressing over the tomato and cucumber mixture, and gently toss to coat all the ingredients evenly.
  4. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Remember to go easy on the salt, as the feta cheese will add a salty element to the salad.
  5. Sprinkle the crumbled feta cheese over the salad, and garnish with torn basil leaves. The feta cheese will add a creamy and tangy flavor that complements the fresh vegetables beautifully.
  6. Give the salad a final gentle toss to combine all the ingredients together. The colors and textures will create a feast for the eyes.
  7. Serve the tomato and cucumber salad immediately, or refrigerate for an hour to let the flavors mingle and develop further.
  8. Enjoy this refreshing summer salad as a side dish with grilled meats, or as a light and healthy lunch option on its own.

Tips and Variations

  • For an extra burst of flavor, you can add some sliced Kalamata olives or chopped fresh mint leaves to the salad.
  • If you prefer a creamier dressing, you can mix in a dollop of Greek yogurt or sour cream to the olive oil and vinegar dressing.
  • Feel free to experiment with different types of tomatoes and cucumbers. Cherry tomatoes or heirloom tomatoes will add a pop of color, while English cucumbers or Persian cucumbers will provide a crisp texture.
  • To make the salad more substantial, you can add some cooked quinoa or chickpeas for added protein and fiber.
  • This salad is best consumed on the same day it is made, as the tomatoes and cucumbers tend to release water over time.

In Conclusion

Tomato and cucumber salad with feta is a refreshing and delicious summer dish that showcases the best of seasonal produce. The combination of juicy tomatoes, crunchy cucumbers, and tangy feta cheese creates a symphony of flavors that will transport you to sunny Mediterranean shores. Whether you’re hosting a summer gathering or simply looking for a light and healthy meal, this salad is sure to impress. So go ahead, embrace the flavors of summer and indulge in this delightful tomato and cucumber salad with feta.
