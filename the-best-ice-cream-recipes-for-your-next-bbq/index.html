The Best Ice Cream Recipes for Your Next BBQ


As summer approaches, the sound of sizzling grills and laughter fills the air, signaling the arrival of barbecue season. What better way to elevate your next outdoor gathering than with homemade ice cream? This delightful treat is not only a crowd-pleaser but also a canvas for creativity. In this post, we’ll explore a variety of ice cream recipes that will impress your guests and add a sweet touch to your BBQ. From classic flavors to unique combinations, these recipes are sure to be a hit.

Why Homemade Ice Cream?

Homemade ice cream allows you to control the ingredients, providing a fresher taste and the ability to customize flavors to suit your preferences or dietary requirements. Plus, making ice cream can be a fun activity to involve friends and family in the preparation. Whether you have an ice cream maker or you prefer a no-churn method, you can whip up delicious flavors with ease.

Classic Vanilla Ice Cream

Ingredients

  • 2 cups heavy cream
  • 1 cup whole milk
  • 3/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 tablespoon pure vanilla extract
  • Pinch of salt

Instructions

  1. Mix Ingredients: In a large bowl, whisk together the heavy cream, whole milk, sugar, vanilla extract, and salt until the sugar is dissolved.

  2. Chill Mixture: Cover the bowl and refrigerate for at least 2 hours or overnight. This step allows the flavors to meld.

  3. Churn Ice Cream: Pour the chilled mixture into your ice cream maker and churn according to the manufacturer’s instructions, usually around 20-25 minutes or until it reaches a soft-serve consistency.

  4. Freeze: Transfer the ice cream to an airtight container and freeze for at least 4 hours for a firmer texture.

  5. Serve: Scoop and serve with your favorite toppings, such as chocolate sauce, sprinkles, or fresh fruit.

Chocolate Fudge Brownie Ice Cream

Ingredients

  • 2 cups heavy cream
  • 1 cup whole milk
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/2 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1 teaspoon pure vanilla extract
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup brownie chunks (store-bought or homemade)
  • 1/2 cup chocolate fudge sauce

Instructions

  1. Prepare Base: In a mixing bowl, whisk together the heavy cream, whole milk, sugar, cocoa powder, vanilla extract, and salt until smooth.

  2. Chill: Cover and refrigerate the mixture for at least 2 hours.

  3. Churn: Pour the base into your ice cream maker and churn according to the instructions.

  4. Add Mix-ins: When the ice cream is almost done, add the brownie chunks and chocolate fudge sauce, letting them swirl into the mixture.

  5. Freeze: Transfer to a container and freeze for at least 4 hours.

  6. Enjoy: Serve in bowls or cones, drizzled with extra fudge sauce if desired.

Strawberry Basil Swirl Ice Cream

Ingredients

  • 2 cups fresh strawberries, hulled and sliced
  • 3/4 cup granulated sugar (divided)
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1 cup whole milk
  • 1 teaspoon pure vanilla extract
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 cup fresh basil leaves, chopped

Instructions

  1. Prepare Strawberry Mixture: In a bowl, combine the strawberries with 1/4 cup of sugar. Let sit for about 30 minutes until the strawberries release their juices.

  2. Blend: After 30 minutes, blend the strawberries into a puree and set aside.

  3. Mix Ice Cream Base: In another bowl, whisk together the remaining sugar, heavy cream, milk, vanilla extract, and salt until the sugar is dissolved.

  4. Chill: Refrigerate the ice cream base for at least 2 hours.

  5. Churn: Pour the base into your ice cream maker and churn until it reaches a soft-serve consistency.

  6. Layer and Freeze: In a container, layer the ice cream with the strawberry puree and chopped basil, swirling them together for a marbled effect. Freeze for at least 4 hours.

  7. Serve: Scoop into bowls and garnish with fresh basil leaves for a refreshing twist.

Cookies and Cream Ice Cream

Ingredients

  • 2 cups heavy cream
  • 1 cup whole milk
  • 3/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 teaspoon pure vanilla extract
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 1/2 cups crushed chocolate sandwich cookies

Instructions

  1. Combine Base: Whisk together the heavy cream, whole milk, sugar, vanilla extract, and salt in a large bowl until the sugar is dissolved.

  2. Chill: Cover and refrigerate the mixture for 2 hours.

  3. Churn: Pour the mixture into your ice cream maker and churn until it reaches a soft-serve consistency.

  4. Add Cookies: Fold in the crushed cookies, ensuring they are evenly distributed.

  5. Freeze: Transfer the ice cream to a container and freeze for at least 4 hours.

  6. Serve: Scoop into bowls and garnish with additional crushed cookies if desired.

Mango Coconut Sorbet

Ingredients

  • 4 ripe mangoes, peeled and diced
  • 1 cup coconut milk
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • Juice of 1 lime

Instructions

  1. Blend Ingredients: In a blender, combine the diced mango, coconut milk, sugar, and lime juice. Blend until smooth.

  2. Chill: Pour the mixture into a bowl and refrigerate for about 1 hour.

  3. Churn: If you have an ice cream maker, pour the mixture into it and churn according to the manufacturer’s instructions.

  4. Freeze: If not using an ice cream maker, pour the mixture into a shallow dish and freeze for about 4 hours, stirring every 30 minutes to prevent crystallization.

  5. Serve: Once frozen, scoop into bowls and enjoy a refreshing sorbet perfect for a summer BBQ!

Tips for Serving Ice Cream at Your BBQ

  1. Presentation: Use colorful bowls and cones to serve your ice cream. Consider creating an ice cream bar with various toppings, such as nuts, fruits, syrups, and sprinkles for a fun DIY experience.

  2. Pairing: Serve your ice creams alongside BBQ staples like grilled peaches, brownies, or even on top of a slice of warm pie.

  3. Keep it Cool: Use a cooler filled with ice to keep your ice cream at the perfect serving temperature throughout the event.

  4. Flavor Combinations: Encourage guests to mix and match flavors with toppings or try creating their own unique concoctions.

  5. Dietary Considerations: Offer a variety of options, including dairy-free and sugar-free ice creams, to cater to guests with dietary restrictions.

Conclusion

Ice cream is the perfect way to conclude any BBQ gathering. With these recipes, you can create a memorable dessert that everyone will love. From the classic vanilla to unique flavors like strawberry basil and mango coconut sorbet, the options are endless. So, fire up the grill, gather your loved ones, and indulge in sweet, cold treats that will make your summer BBQ unforgettable! Happy grilling and enjoying those delicious scoops!
