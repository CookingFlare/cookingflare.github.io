Mouthwatering Nut-Free Peach Cobbler Recipe


Are you ready to indulge in a delightful dessert that will tantalize your taste buds and leave you craving for more? Look no further than this irresistible nut-free peach cobbler recipe. Bursting with the sweet and juicy flavor of fresh peaches, this classic dessert is the perfect way to make the most of peach season. Whether you’re hosting a summer barbecue, planning a family gathering, or simply craving a comforting treat, this peach cobbler will undoubtedly steal the show.

Ingredients

For the Filling:

  • 6 cups fresh peaches, peeled and sliced
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/4 cup brown sugar
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 2 tablespoons cornstarch
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground nutmeg

For the Cobbler Topping:

  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, cold and cubed
  • 1/3 cup whole milk
  • 1 large egg
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C) and grease a 9x13 inch baking dish.

  2. In a large mixing bowl, combine the sliced peaches, granulated sugar, brown sugar, lemon juice, cornstarch, vanilla extract, cinnamon, and nutmeg. Toss the mixture gently until the peaches are well coated, then transfer it to the prepared baking dish.

  3. To make the cobbler topping, whisk together the flour, granulated sugar, baking powder, and salt in a separate bowl. Add the cold cubed butter and use a pastry cutter or your fingertips to work the butter into the dry ingredients until the mixture resembles coarse crumbs.

  4. In a small bowl, whisk together the whole milk, egg, and vanilla extract. Pour the wet mixture into the dry ingredients and stir until just combined. Avoid overmixing, as this can result in a tough cobbler topping.

  5. Spoon the cobbler topping over the peaches in the baking dish, spreading it out evenly to cover the entire surface.

  6. Place the baking dish in the preheated oven and bake for 40-45 minutes, or until the cobbler topping is golden brown and the peach filling is bubbling around the edges.

  7. Once baked, remove the peach cobbler from the oven and allow it to cool for 10-15 minutes before serving.

  8. Serve the nut-free peach cobbler warm, preferably with a scoop of vanilla ice cream or a dollop of freshly whipped cream.

Tips and Variations

  • For a hint of citrus, consider adding a teaspoon of finely grated lemon zest to the peach filling.
  • Experiment with different spices such as cardamom or ginger to add a unique twist to the cobbler topping.
  • If fresh peaches are not in season, you can use frozen peaches as a convenient alternative.

Enjoy the Delightful Flavors

This nut-free peach cobbler is a true celebration of summer’s bounty, capturing the essence of ripe, luscious peaches in every spoonful. The contrast between the tender, sweet peaches and the buttery, golden cobbler topping is simply irresistible. Whether you’re a seasoned baker or a novice in the kitchen, this recipe is accessible and rewarding, promising a delectable dessert that will be remembered long after the last crumb has been savored. Embrace the joy of baking and treat yourself, family, and friends to this delightful peach cobbler – it’s a truly heartwarming experience!
