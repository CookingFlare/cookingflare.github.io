Al Fresco Dining: Mouth-Watering Summer Picnic Recipes for All


Nothing quite compares to enjoying a delicious meal outdoors, basking in the warm glow of the sun while surrounded by nature’s beauty. Summer picnics are an excellent opportunity to engage in al fresco dining – a memorable experience cherished by people of all ages. To make your next picnic a success, we’ve curated a range of delightful recipes that cater to everyone’s taste buds.

Sweet and Tangy BBQ Chicken

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 pounds of chicken drumsticks
  • 1 cup of barbecue sauce
  • 1/4 cup apple cider vinegar
  • 1/4 cup brown sugar
  • 2 tablespoons honey
  • 2 tablespoons Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • Salt and pepper, to taste

Instructions

  1. In a large bowl, combine the barbecue sauce, apple cider vinegar, brown sugar, honey, Worcestershire sauce, garlic powder, paprika, and a pinch of salt and pepper.
  2. Add the chicken drumsticks to the bowl, ensuring they’re coated evenly with the marinade.
  3. Cover the bowl and let the chicken marinate for at least two hours.
  4. Preheat your grill to medium-high heat.
  5. Once heated, remove the chicken from the marinade and grill for approximately 25-30 minutes, or until the internal temperature reaches 165°F (75°C).
  6. Allow the chicken to rest for 10 minutes before serving, allowing the juices to redistribute.

Healthy Quinoa Salad with Basil Vinaigrette

Ingredients

  • 2 cups cooked quinoa
  • 2 cups cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1 cucumber, sliced
  • 1 red onion, thinly sliced
  • A handful of fresh basil leaves
  • A handful of fresh parsley leaves
  • 2 tablespoons lemon juice
  • 2 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • Honey, to taste (optional)

Instructions

  1. Prepare your quinoa according to package instructions.
  2. In a large mixing bowl, combine the cooked quinoa, cherry tomatoes, cucumber, red onion, basil, and parsley.
  3. In a small bowl or jar, prepare the vinaigrette by whisking together the lemon juice, extra virgin olive oil, a pinch of salt, and a pinch of pepper. If desired, you can add a drizzle of honey for sweetness.
  4. Pour the vinaigrette over the quinoa mixture and toss to coat evenly.
  5. Serve fresh and enjoy!

Strawberry Lemonade Sorbet

Ingredients

  • 2 cups fresh strawberries, hulled
  • 2 tablespoons freshly squeezed lemon juice
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 2 cups water

Instructions

  1. In a blender, puree the strawberries until smooth.
  2. In a saucepan, dissolve the granulated sugar in water over medium heat.
  3. Once the sugar is dissolved, lower the heat and add the strained strawberry puree and lemon juice.
  4. Stir the mixture well, and let it cool to room temperature.
  5. Freeze the mixture in a shallow dish or tray according to your freezer’s instructions. Typically, stirring every hour for 6 hours ensures the sorbet attains a creamy consistency.
  6. Depending on your preference, scoop and serve the sorbet immediately, or transfer it to an airtight container and store in the freezer up to 1 month.

These scrumptious recipes will elevate your summer picnics, creating cherished memories with friends and family. Embrace all the delicious flavors that al fresco dining has to offer and don’t forget to share your culinary masterpieces with loved ones. Buon appetito!
