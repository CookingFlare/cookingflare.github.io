Not only are they healthier, but they also taste incredibly delicious! In this blog post, we will share with you a simple and easy recipe for homemade chicken nuggets that your kids will absolutely love. So, let’s get started! Ingredients 1 pound boneless, skinless chicken breasts 1 cup breadcrumbs 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese 1 teaspoon garlic powder 1 teaspoon paprika 1/2 teaspoon salt 1/4 teaspoon black pepper 2 large eggs, beaten Cooking spray Instructions Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C) and line a baking sheet with parchment paper. Cut the chicken breasts into bite-sized pieces, approximately 1-inch in size. In a shallow bowl, combine the breadcrumbs, grated Parmesan cheese, garlic powder, paprika, salt, and black pepper. Mix well. Dip each piece of chicken into the beaten eggs, making sure to coat it completely. Next, roll the chicken in the breadcrumb mixture, pressing gently to ensure the breadcrumbs adhere to the chicken. Place the coated chicken nuggets onto the prepared baking sheet, making sure to leave space between each nugget. Spray the tops of the nuggets lightly with cooking spray. This will help them achieve a crispy texture. Bake the chicken nuggets in the preheated oven for 15-20 minutes, or until they are golden brown and cooked through. Remove the nuggets from the oven and let them cool for a few minutes before serving. Serving Suggestions These homemade chicken nuggets are versatile and can be enjoyed in various ways.
