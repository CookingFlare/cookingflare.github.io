Instant Pot Beef and Onion Stew Recipe: A Hearty and Flavorful Option

There’s nothing quite like a warm and comforting stew to satisfy your cravings on a chilly day. And when it comes to convenience and flavor, it’s hard to beat the Instant Pot. This versatile kitchen appliance allows you to create delicious meals in a fraction of the time, and today, we have a recipe that will surely become a favorite in your household: Instant Pot Beef and Onion Stew. Ingredients 2 pounds beef stew meat, cut into bite-sized pieces 2 large onions, thinly sliced 3 cloves garlic, minced 4 carrots, peeled and sliced 3 celery stalks, diced 1 pound potatoes, cubed 4 cups beef broth 1 cup red wine (optional) 2 tablespoons tomato paste 2 tablespoons Worcestershire sauce 1 tablespoon dried thyme 1 tablespoon dried rosemary Salt and pepper, to taste 2 tablespoons olive oil Instructions Start by setting your Instant Pot to the sauté function. Add the olive oil and allow it to heat up. Once the oil is hot, add the beef stew meat and brown it on all sides. This step will help seal in the flavor and keep the meat tender. Remove the beef from the Instant Pot and set it aside. Add the onions and garlic to the pot and sauté until they become translucent and fragrant. Next, add the carrots, celery, and potatoes to the pot, and give them a quick stir to combine everything. Return the beef to the Instant Pot, along with any accumulated juices. Pour in the beef broth and red wine (if using), and stir in the tomato paste and Worcestershire sauce. Add the dried thyme and rosemary, and season with salt and pepper to taste. Give everything a good stir to ensure the flavors are well-distributed. Secure the lid on the Instant Pot, making sure the valve is set to the sealing position. Select the “Pressure Cook” function and set the timer for 30 minutes. Once the cooking time is up, allow the pressure to release naturally for about 10 minutes, and then carefully perform a quick release to release any remaining pressure. Give the stew a gentle stir, and if desired, you can thicken the sauce by selecting the “Sauté” function and simmering the stew for a few minutes until it reaches your desired consistency. Serve the Instant Pot Beef and Onion Stew hot, garnished with fresh parsley if desired. Pair it with crusty bread or fluffy mashed potatoes for a complete and satisfying meal. Mouthwatering Instant Pot Beef Tacos with Homemade Salsa Recipes

Add the olive oil and brown the beef in batches until golden brown on all sides. Remove the beef and set it aside. Add the diced onion to the Instant Pot and cook until softened, then add the minced garlic and cook for another minute. Return the beef to the Instant Pot and add the beef broth, chili powder, cumin, paprika, oregano, salt, and pepper. Close the lid and set the Instant Pot to “Manual” for 45 minutes. Once the cooking time is complete, allow the pressure to release naturally for 10 minutes before using the quick release. Shred the beef using two forks and return it to the cooking liquid to keep it moist and flavorful. Squeeze the lime juice over the mixture and toss to combine. Season the salsa with salt and pepper to taste, and refrigerate for at least 30 minutes to allow the flavors to meld together. Assembling the Tacos Once the beef is ready and the salsa has had time to chill, it’s time to assemble the tacos. Warm up some corn or flour tortillas, spoon some of the tender beef onto each tortilla, and top it off with a generous helping of the homemade salsa. Creamy Coconut Curry Ingredients: 1 can of coconut milk 2 cups of mixed vegetables (carrots, bell peppers, peas, cauliflower) 1 onion, diced 3 garlic cloves, minced 1 tablespoon of curry powder 1 teaspoon of turmeric powder 1 teaspoon of cumin powder Salt, to taste Fresh cilantro, for garnish Instructions: Set your Instant Pot to sauté mode and add a splash of oil. Sauté the onions and garlic until they become translucent. Add the curry powder, turmeric powder, and cumin powder. Stir well to coat the onions and garlic. Spicy Chickpea Curry Ingredients: 2 cups of chickpeas, cooked or canned 1 onion, diced 3 garlic cloves, minced 1 tablespoon of curry powder 1 teaspoon of paprika 1 teaspoon of cayenne pepper (adjust to your spice preference) 1 can of diced tomatoes 1 cup of vegetable broth Salt, to taste Fresh parsley, for garnish Instructions: Sauté the onions and garlic in the Instant Pot until they become fragrant and golden. Add the curry powder, paprika, and cayenne pepper. Stir well to coat the onions and garlic with the spices. Pour in the diced tomatoes and vegetable broth. Mix everything together. Add the chickpeas and season with salt. Close the Instant Pot and set it to manual mode for 10 minutes. Once the cooking time is up, let the pressure release naturally for 5 minutes, then do a quick release. Serve the spicy chickpea curry with rice or quinoa. Garnish with fresh parsley for a pop of freshness. 3. Fragrant Thai Green Curry Ingredients: 2 cups of mixed vegetables (zucchini, broccoli, snap peas, mushrooms) 1 onion, diced 3 garlic cloves, minced 2 tablespoons of Thai green curry paste 1 can of coconut milk 1 cup of vegetable broth 1 tablespoon of soy sauce 1 tablespoon of brown sugar Lime wedges, for serving Fresh basil leaves, for garnish Instructions: Sauté the onions and garlic in the Instant Pot until they become fragrant. Add the Thai green curry paste and stir well to coat the onions and garlic. Pour in the coconut milk, vegetable broth, soy sauce, and brown sugar. Mix everything together. Add the mixed vegetables and give it a good stir. Close the Instant Pot and set it to manual mode for 5 minutes. Once the cooking time is up, do a quick release of the pressure. Serve the fragrant Thai green curry with steamed jasmine rice. Squeeze some lime juice over the curry and garnish with fresh basil leaves for a burst of flavor.
