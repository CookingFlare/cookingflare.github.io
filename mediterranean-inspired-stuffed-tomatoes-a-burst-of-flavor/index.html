Mediterranean-Inspired Stuffed Tomatoes: A Burst of Flavor


If you’re looking for a dish that’s bursting with Mediterranean flavors, then you’re in for a treat! These Mediterranean-inspired stuffed tomatoes are not only visually appealing but also packed with a mouthwatering combination of ingredients that will tantalize your taste buds. From the vibrant colors to the delightful blend of herbs and spices, this dish is a true celebration of Mediterranean cuisine.

Ingredients

To make this delectable dish, you will need the following ingredients:

  • 6 large tomatoes
  • 1 cup cooked couscous
  • 1 cup crumbled feta cheese
  • 1/2 cup chopped Kalamata olives
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley
  • 2 tablespoons chopped fresh mint
  • 2 tablespoons chopped fresh dill
  • 1/4 cup extra virgin olive oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).

  2. Carefully slice off the tops of the tomatoes and set them aside. Gently scoop out the seeds and pulp from the tomatoes using a spoon, being careful not to puncture the skins. Reserve the pulp for later use.

  3. In a large mixing bowl, combine the cooked couscous, crumbled feta cheese, chopped Kalamata olives, chopped parsley, chopped mint, and chopped dill. Mix well until all the ingredients are evenly distributed.

  4. In a separate small bowl, whisk together the extra virgin olive oil, minced garlic, salt, and pepper. Pour this dressing over the couscous mixture and toss until everything is well coated.

  5. Take the reserved tomato pulp and give it a rough chop. Add it to the couscous mixture and stir gently to incorporate.

  6. Spoon the couscous mixture into the hollowed-out tomatoes, filling them generously. Place the tomato tops back on the tomatoes.

  7. Arrange the stuffed tomatoes in a baking dish, making sure they are snugly packed together to prevent them from tipping over.

  8. Bake the stuffed tomatoes in the preheated oven for approximately 25-30 minutes, or until the tomatoes are tender and the filling is heated through.

  9. Once cooked, remove the stuffed tomatoes from the oven and let them cool slightly before serving. Garnish with a sprinkle of fresh herbs, if desired.

Serving Suggestions

These Mediterranean-inspired stuffed tomatoes can be enjoyed as a standalone meal or served as a delightful side dish. They pair perfectly with grilled chicken, fish, or even a simple green salad. You can also serve them as part of a larger Mediterranean feast, alongside dishes like hummus, tabbouleh, and tzatziki.

Final Thoughts

These Mediterranean-inspired stuffed tomatoes are bursting with flavor and make for a stunning presentation. Whether you’re hosting a dinner party or simply looking to elevate your weeknight meal, this dish is sure to impress. The combination of juicy tomatoes, savory couscous, tangy feta cheese, and fragrant herbs creates a harmonious blend that will transport your taste buds to the sun-soaked shores of the Mediterranean. So go ahead, give this recipe a try, and savor the taste of the Mediterranean in every bite!
