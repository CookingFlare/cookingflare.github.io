A Taste of the Mediterranean: Gluten-Free Greek Chicken Souvlaki


The Mediterranean cuisine is renowned for its vibrant flavors, fresh ingredients, and healthy cooking techniques. One of the most beloved dishes from this region is the Greek Chicken Souvlaki. This gluten-free rendition of the classic Greek dish is a delightful combination of succulent chicken, aromatic herbs, and zesty lemon, making it a perfect choice for those with gluten sensitivities or those simply looking for a delicious and healthy meal option.

Ingredients

  • 1.5 pounds of boneless, skinless chicken breasts, cut into cubes
  • 1/4 cup of olive oil
  • 3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon of fresh oregano, chopped
  • 1 tablespoon of fresh thyme, chopped
  • 1 teaspoon of freshly grated lemon zest
  • 2 tablespoons of fresh lemon juice
  • 1 teaspoon of salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon of black pepper
  • 1 red onion, cut into chunks
  • 1 red bell pepper, cut into chunks
  • 1 green bell pepper, cut into chunks
  • Wooden skewers, soaked in water for at least 30 minutes

Instructions

  1. In a large bowl, combine the olive oil, minced garlic, chopped oregano, thyme, lemon zest, lemon juice, salt, and black pepper. Add the cubed chicken to the marinade, ensuring it is well coated. Cover the bowl with plastic wrap and refrigerate for at least 2 hours to allow the flavors to meld.

  2. Preheat the grill to medium-high heat. Thread the marinated chicken, red onion, and bell peppers onto the soaked wooden skewers, alternating between the ingredients.

  3. Place the skewers on the preheated grill and cook for 10-12 minutes, turning occasionally, until the chicken is cooked through and has beautiful grill marks.

  4. Once the chicken is cooked, remove the skewers from the grill and let them rest for a few minutes before serving.

  5. Serve the gluten-free Greek Chicken Souvlaki with a side of tzatziki sauce, a fresh Greek salad, and warm gluten-free pita bread.

Health Benefits

This gluten-free Greek Chicken Souvlaki is not only a flavorful and satisfying dish but also offers a range of health benefits. Chicken is an excellent source of lean protein, while the olive oil provides heart-healthy monounsaturated fats. The aromatic herbs and lemon add a burst of antioxidants and vitamin C to the dish, boosting its nutritional value.

Serving Suggestions

To create a complete Mediterranean feast, consider serving the Chicken Souvlaki alongside a refreshing Greek salad made with crisp lettuce, juicy tomatoes, cucumbers, Kalamata olives, and tangy feta cheese. For a gluten-free option, skip the pita bread or opt for a gluten-free alternative. Tzatziki, a traditional Greek yogurt and cucumber sauce, is the perfect accompaniment to the Souvlaki, adding a creamy and refreshing touch to the meal.

Final Thoughts

This Gluten-Free Greek Chicken Souvlaki offers a taste of the Mediterranean right in your own home. Whether you’re hosting a summer barbecue or simply craving a flavorful and healthy meal, this dish is sure to impress. The combination of tender chicken, aromatic herbs, and zesty lemon will transport your taste buds to the sun-drenched shores of Greece, making it a delightful addition to any meal rotation.

Embrace the flavors of the Mediterranean with this gluten-free twist on a classic Greek favorite. Enjoy the vibrant and wholesome goodness of this Gluten-Free Greek Chicken Souvlaki, and savor the essence of Mediterranean cuisine with every bite.
