Greek Lemon Roasted Chicken: A Simple and Flavorful Recipe


Are you looking for a delicious and easy-to-make chicken recipe that will transport your taste buds to the sunny Mediterranean? Look no further than Greek Lemon Roasted Chicken! This dish is bursting with vibrant flavors, tender meat, and a tangy lemon twist. It’s perfect for any occasion, whether it’s a casual weeknight dinner or a festive gathering with friends and family.

Ingredients

To make this mouthwatering Greek Lemon Roasted Chicken, you’ll need the following ingredients:

  • 1 whole chicken, about 3-4 pounds
  • 4 lemons, juiced and zested
  • 4 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons of olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon of dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon of dried thyme
  • Salt and pepper, to taste

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).

  2. In a small bowl, combine the lemon juice, lemon zest, minced garlic, olive oil, dried oregano, dried thyme, salt, and pepper. Mix well to create a flavorful marinade.

  3. Place the chicken in a roasting pan or baking dish, breast side up. Pour the marinade over the chicken, making sure to coat it evenly. You can use your hands to massage the marinade into the chicken, ensuring that it’s well seasoned.

  4. Cover the roasting pan with aluminum foil and let the chicken marinate in the refrigerator for at least 2 hours, or overnight for maximum flavor.

  5. Once the chicken has marinated, remove it from the refrigerator and let it come to room temperature for about 30 minutes. This will ensure even cooking.

  6. Remove the aluminum foil from the roasting pan and place the chicken in the preheated oven. Roast for about 1 hour and 30 minutes, or until the internal temperature reaches 165°F (75°C) and the skin is golden brown and crispy.

  7. To enhance the flavor and presentation, you can baste the chicken with the pan juices every 30 minutes during cooking.

  8. Once the chicken is cooked, remove it from the oven and let it rest for about 10 minutes before carving.

  9. Serve the Greek Lemon Roasted Chicken with your favorite sides, such as roasted potatoes, steamed vegetables, or a fresh Greek salad. Don’t forget to drizzle some of the pan juices over the chicken for an extra burst of flavor!

Tips and Variations

  • For a more pronounced lemon flavor, you can add lemon slices to the roasting pan alongside the chicken.
  • If you prefer a spicier kick, you can add a pinch of red pepper flakes or a sprinkle of paprika to the marinade.
  • Experiment with different herbs and spices to customize the flavor profile of your Greek Lemon Roasted Chicken. Fresh rosemary, dill, or basil can add a delightful twist.
  • Leftovers can be used in a variety of dishes, such as chicken salad, wraps, or sandwiches. The possibilities are endless!

Final Thoughts

Greek Lemon Roasted Chicken is a classic Mediterranean dish that never fails to impress. The combination of tangy lemon, aromatic herbs, and succulent chicken creates a symphony of flavors that will transport you to the sunny shores of Greece. Whether you’re a seasoned chef or a beginner in the kitchen, this simple and flavorful recipe is sure to become a household favorite. So, gather your ingredients, preheat your oven, and get ready to savor the taste of Greece with every bite of this delightful dish!
