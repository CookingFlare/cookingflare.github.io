Spicy Thai Curries: 7 Recipes to Ignite Your Taste Buds


If you’re a fan of bold flavors and fiery spices, then Thai curries are the perfect choice for you. Bursting with aromatic herbs, rich coconut milk, and a variety of aromatic spices, Thai curries are known for their vibrant colors and intense flavors. In this article, we bring you seven mouthwatering Thai curry recipes that will take your taste buds on a thrilling journey through Thailand’s culinary landscape. So, put on your apron and get ready to indulge in a spicy adventure!

1. Red Curry with Chicken

Ingredients:

  • 2 tablespoons red curry paste
  • 1 can coconut milk
  • 1 pound chicken breast, sliced
  • 1 cup mixed vegetables (bell peppers, snow peas, carrots)
  • 1 tablespoon fish sauce
  • 1 tablespoon palm sugar
  • Fresh basil leaves, for garnish

Instructions:

  1. In a large pan, heat the red curry paste over medium heat for a minute until fragrant.
  2. Add half of the coconut milk and stir until the paste is fully dissolved.
  3. Add the chicken and cook until it’s no longer pink.
  4. Stir in the remaining coconut milk, mixed vegetables, fish sauce, and palm sugar. Simmer for 10 minutes.
  5. Garnish with fresh basil leaves and serve hot with steamed jasmine rice.

2. Green Curry with Shrimp

Ingredients:

  • 2 tablespoons green curry paste
  • 1 can coconut milk
  • 1 pound shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 1 cup bamboo shoots
  • 1 cup Thai eggplant, quartered
  • 1 tablespoon fish sauce
  • 1 tablespoon brown sugar
  • Fresh cilantro, for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Heat the green curry paste in a pan over medium heat until fragrant.
  2. Add half of the coconut milk and stir until well combined.
  3. Add the shrimp and cook until they turn pink.
  4. Stir in the remaining coconut milk, bamboo shoots, Thai eggplant, fish sauce, and brown sugar. Simmer for 10 minutes.
  5. Garnish with fresh cilantro and serve with steamed rice.

3. Yellow Curry with Tofu

Ingredients:

  • 2 tablespoons yellow curry paste
  • 1 can coconut milk
  • 1 block firm tofu, cubed
  • 1 cup potatoes, peeled and diced
  • 1 cup carrots, sliced
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon honey
  • Roasted peanuts, for garnish

Instructions:

  1. In a pan, heat the yellow curry paste over medium heat until aromatic.
  2. Add half of the coconut milk and stir until well mixed.
  3. Add the tofu, potatoes, and carrots. Cook until the vegetables are tender.
  4. Stir in the remaining coconut milk, soy sauce, and honey. Simmer for 10 minutes.
  5. Garnish with roasted peanuts and serve hot with steamed rice.

4. Massaman Curry with Beef

Ingredients:

  • 2 tablespoons massaman curry paste
  • 1 can coconut milk
  • 1 pound beef, thinly sliced
  • 1 cup potatoes, peeled and diced
  • 1 cup onions, sliced
  • 1 tablespoon tamarind paste
  • 1 tablespoon brown sugar
  • Roasted cashews, for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Heat the massaman curry paste in a pan over medium heat until fragrant.
  2. Add half of the coconut milk and stir until well combined.
  3. Add the beef, potatoes, and onions. Cook until the beef is browned.
  4. Stir in the remaining coconut milk, tamarind paste, and brown sugar. Simmer for 15 minutes.
  5. Garnish with roasted cashews and serve with steamed rice.

5. Panang Curry with Pork

Ingredients:

  • 2 tablespoons panang curry paste
  • 1 can coconut milk
  • 1 pound pork loin, thinly sliced
  • 1 cup red bell peppers, sliced
  • 1 cup green beans
  • 1 tablespoon fish sauce
  • 1 tablespoon palm sugar
  • Fresh Thai basil, for garnish

Instructions:

  1. In a pan, heat the panang curry paste over medium heat until fragrant.
  2. Add half of the coconut milk and stir until well mixed.
  3. Add the pork loin, red bell peppers, and green beans. Cook until the pork is cooked through.
  4. Stir in the remaining coconut milk, fish sauce, and palm sugar. Simmer for 10 minutes.
  5. Garnish with fresh Thai basil and serve hot with steamed rice.

6. Jungle Curry with Vegetables

Ingredients:

  • 2 tablespoons jungle curry paste
  • 1 can coconut milk
  • Assorted vegetables (broccoli, mushrooms, baby corn, zucchini)
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon lime juice
  • Fresh cilantro, for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Heat the jungle curry paste in a pan over medium heat until fragrant.
  2. Add half of the coconut milk and stir until well combined.
  3. Add the assorted vegetables and cook until tender.
  4. Stir in the remaining coconut milk, soy sauce, and lime juice. Simmer for 10 minutes.
  5. Garnish with fresh cilantro and serve with steamed rice.

7. Pineapple Curry with Seafood

Ingredients:

  • 2 tablespoons pineapple curry paste
  • 1 can coconut milk
  • Assorted seafood (shrimp, squid, mussels)
  • 1 cup pineapple chunks
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes
  • 1 tablespoon fish sauce
  • 1 tablespoon palm sugar
  • Fresh cilantro, for garnish

Instructions:

  1. In a pan, heat the pineapple curry paste over medium heat until fragrant.
  2. Add half of the coconut milk and stir until well mixed.
  3. Add the assorted seafood, pineapple chunks, and cherry tomatoes. Cook until the seafood is cooked through.
  4. Stir in the remaining coconut milk, fish sauce, and palm sugar. Simmer for 10 minutes.
  5. Garnish with fresh cilantro and serve hot with steamed rice.

Enjoy the explosion of flavors that these Spicy Thai Curries bring to your palate. These aromatic and vibrant dishes are sure to transport you to the streets of Thailand with every bite. So, gather your ingredients, follow the recipes, and get ready for a culinary adventure that will leave you craving more. Don’t be afraid to adjust the spice levels based on your preference, and remember to have fun experimenting with different combinations of vegetables, proteins, and herbs. Happy cooking!
