The Best Ice Cream Recipes for a Summer BBQ


Summer barbecues are the perfect way to spend time with family and friends, and what better way to wrap up a flavorful feast than with a sweet, creamy, and cool dessert? Ice cream is the ultimate treat for any BBQ, offering both a refreshing and indulgent finish to a summer meal. Whether you prefer classic flavors, fruity concoctions, or adventurous combinations, there’s an ice cream recipe that can take your BBQ to the next level. In this post, we’ll explore a variety of ice cream recipes that are perfect for a summer barbecue.

1. Classic Vanilla Bean Ice Cream

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups heavy cream
  • 1 cup whole milk
  • 3/4 cup sugar
  • 1 tablespoon vanilla extract
  • 1 vanilla bean (or 2 teaspoons vanilla paste)
  • A pinch of salt
  • 5 large egg yolks

Instructions:

  1. In a saucepan, combine the heavy cream, whole milk, and sugar. Heat over medium heat until the mixture is warm, but not boiling.
  2. Split the vanilla bean lengthwise, scrape out the seeds, and add them to the saucepan along with the bean pod. Stir occasionally and let the mixture simmer for 5 minutes.
  3. While the mixture is heating, whisk together the egg yolks in a separate bowl. Gradually add a bit of the warm cream mixture to the yolks, whisking constantly to temper the eggs.
  4. Slowly pour the egg mixture back into the saucepan, stirring constantly. Cook on low heat until the custard thickens enough to coat the back of a spoon, about 10 minutes.
  5. Remove from heat and strain the custard through a fine-mesh sieve to remove the vanilla pod and any egg solids.
  6. Stir in the vanilla extract, and then chill the mixture in the fridge for at least 4 hours or overnight.
  7. Once chilled, pour the custard into your ice cream maker and churn according to the manufacturer’s instructions.
  8. Transfer the ice cream to an airtight container and freeze for an additional 4 hours before serving.

This classic vanilla bean ice cream is rich and creamy, and it pairs wonderfully with any fruit, pie, or brownie at your summer BBQ.

2. Chocolate Fudge Brownie Ice Cream

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups heavy cream
  • 1 cup whole milk
  • 3/4 cup cocoa powder
  • 3/4 cup sugar
  • 1/2 cup chocolate chips (for melting)
  • 5 large egg yolks
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 2 cups brownies, cut into small cubes
  • A pinch of salt

Instructions:

  1. In a saucepan, combine the heavy cream, whole milk, cocoa powder, and sugar. Heat over medium heat, whisking until the sugar dissolves and the mixture is smooth.
  2. Remove the mixture from the heat and add the chocolate chips. Stir until the chocolate is completely melted and smooth.
  3. In a separate bowl, whisk the egg yolks and slowly add a bit of the warm chocolate mixture to temper the eggs.
  4. Slowly pour the egg mixture into the saucepan, stirring constantly. Cook on low heat until the custard thickens enough to coat the back of a spoon, about 8-10 minutes.
  5. Remove the custard from heat, stir in the vanilla extract, and let it cool to room temperature. Once cool, chill it in the fridge for at least 4 hours.
  6. Churn the mixture in an ice cream maker according to the manufacturer’s instructions.
  7. After churning, gently fold in the brownie cubes, making sure they are evenly distributed throughout the ice cream.
  8. Transfer the ice cream to a container and freeze for at least 4 hours to firm up.

This indulgent chocolate fudge brownie ice cream will satisfy the chocolate lover in your group. The rich chocolate flavor and decadent brownie pieces make it the perfect summer BBQ treat.

3. Peach and Bourbon Swirl Ice Cream

Ingredients:

  • 3 cups fresh peaches, peeled and chopped
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 1/2 cup sugar, divided
  • 1 1/2 cups heavy cream
  • 1 cup whole milk
  • 5 large egg yolks
  • 1/4 cup bourbon
  • A pinch of salt

Instructions:

  1. In a saucepan, combine the chopped peaches, lemon juice, and 1/4 cup of sugar. Cook over medium heat until the peaches break down and soften, about 10 minutes.
  2. Use an immersion blender or a regular blender to puree the peach mixture until smooth. Let it cool to room temperature, and then refrigerate to chill.
  3. In a separate saucepan, combine the remaining sugar, heavy cream, and whole milk. Heat over medium heat until warm.
  4. Whisk the egg yolks in a separate bowl, and gradually add the warm cream mixture to the yolks, whisking constantly.
  5. Pour the egg mixture back into the saucepan and cook on low heat, stirring until the custard thickens and coats the back of a spoon, about 10 minutes.
  6. Remove from heat and let the custard cool to room temperature. Once cool, add the bourbon and stir to combine.
  7. Strain the custard through a fine mesh sieve to remove any solids, then refrigerate for 4 hours.
  8. Churn the custard in an ice cream maker according to the manufacturer’s instructions.
  9. After churning, fold in the peach puree and swirl it throughout the ice cream.
  10. Freeze for at least 4 hours before serving.

The peach and bourbon swirl ice cream is a perfect balance of fruity sweetness and smooth, rich creaminess. The bourbon adds a delightful kick to this ice cream, making it an exciting dessert to serve at your BBQ.

4. Lemon Blueberry Cheesecake Ice Cream

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup fresh blueberries
  • 1 tablespoon lemon zest
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 2 cups heavy cream
  • 1 cup whole milk
  • 4 ounces cream cheese, softened
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 5 large egg yolks
  • A pinch of salt
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Instructions:

  1. In a saucepan, combine the blueberries, lemon zest, lemon juice, and 1/4 cup of sugar. Cook over medium heat until the blueberries start to break down and the mixture becomes syrupy, about 8-10 minutes.
  2. Use an immersion blender or regular blender to puree the blueberry mixture. Strain it through a fine-mesh sieve to remove the skins, and set it aside to cool.
  3. In a separate saucepan, heat the heavy cream, whole milk, and 1/2 cup of sugar until the mixture is warm, but not boiling.
  4. Whisk the egg yolks in a separate bowl and gradually add some of the warm cream mixture to temper the eggs. Pour the egg mixture back into the saucepan and cook on low heat, stirring constantly until the custard thickens and coats the back of a spoon.
  5. Remove the custard from heat and whisk in the softened cream cheese until smooth. Let the mixture cool to room temperature.
  6. Once the custard has cooled, refrigerate it for at least 4 hours or overnight.
  7. Churn the mixture in your ice cream maker according to the manufacturer’s instructions.
  8. After churning, fold in the blueberry puree and mix gently to create a marbled effect.
  9. Freeze for at least 4 hours before serving.

This lemon blueberry cheesecake ice cream is a tangy and creamy treat that is perfect for a summer BBQ. The combination of fresh blueberries and zesty lemon adds a burst of flavor, while the cream cheese gives it a rich, cheesecake-like texture.

5. Coconut Pineapple Ice Cream (Piña Colada Style)

Ingredients:

  • 1 can (14 oz) coconut milk
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 2 cups fresh pineapple, chopped
  • 1/2 cup coconut flakes, toasted (optional)
  • 1 tablespoon rum (optional)

Instructions:

  1. In a blender, combine the coconut milk, heavy cream, sugar, and vanilla extract. Blend until the mixture is smooth and well combined.
  2. In a separate bowl, mash the fresh pineapple slightly to release its juices. Blend the mashed pineapple into the coconut cream mixture until smooth.
  3. If using, add the rum to the mixture and stir well.
  4. Chill the mixture in the refrigerator for at least 2-3 hours.
  5. Churn the mixture in your ice cream maker according to the manufacturer’s instructions.
  6. Once the ice cream is fully churned, fold in the toasted coconut flakes for an extra tropical crunch.
  7. Transfer the ice cream to a container and freeze for at least 4 hours before serving.

This Piña Colada-inspired coconut pineapple ice cream will transport you straight to a tropical paradise. The coconut milk and pineapple blend together beautifully, creating a rich and refreshing treat that’s perfect for a summer BBQ.

6. Strawberry Basil Sorbet

Ingredients:

  • 4 cups fresh strawberries, hulled
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1/4 cup water
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 1 tablespoon fresh basil, finely chopped

Instructions:

  1. Combine the strawberries, sugar, and water in a blender or food processor. Blend until smooth.
  2. Strain the mixture through a fine-mesh sieve to remove the seeds and pulp.
  3. Stir in the lemon juice and finely chopped basil.
  4. Pour the mixture into a shallow dish or an ice cream maker and freeze according to the manufacturer’s instructions.
  5. After the sorbet has frozen, serve it immediately or store it in the freezer for later.

This strawberry basil sorbet is a refreshing, light option for a summer BBQ. The basil adds a unique twist to the classic strawberry flavor, making this dessert an exciting and healthy alternative to traditional ice cream.

Conclusion

These ice cream recipes are sure to impress your guests and make your summer BBQ even more memorable. From rich and creamy chocolate to refreshing fruit-infused concoctions, there’s an ice cream flavor for every taste. Serve them as a standalone treat or alongside your favorite BBQ dishes for the perfect ending to a summer feast. Enjoy the sweet, cool bliss of homemade ice cream and make your next BBQ the talk of the summer!
