BBQ Bacon-Wrapped Shrimp: A Decadent Fourth of July Appetizer


The Fourth of July is a time for celebration, and what better way to kick off the festivities than with a mouthwatering appetizer that will leave your guests craving for more? BBQ Bacon-Wrapped Shrimp is a decadent treat that combines the smoky flavors of bacon with the succulent taste of shrimp, all glazed with a tangy barbecue sauce. This recipe is perfect for grilling outdoors, adding a sizzle to your Fourth of July gathering.

Ingredients

To create this delectable appetizer, you’ll need the following:

  • Jumbo shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • Thinly sliced bacon
  • Your favorite barbecue sauce
  • Wooden or metal skewers
  • Salt and pepper
  • Olive oil
  • Fresh parsley for garnish (optional)

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Shrimp: Begin by seasoning the shrimp with a sprinkle of salt and pepper. This will enhance the natural flavors of the shrimp and add a subtle kick to each bite.

  2. Wrap with Bacon: Take a slice of bacon and wrap it around each seasoned shrimp, securing it with a skewer to hold the bacon in place.

  3. Fire up the Grill: Preheat your grill to medium-high heat, ensuring that the grates are clean and lightly oiled to prevent sticking.

  4. Cook to Perfection: Once the grill is ready, place the bacon-wrapped shrimp on the grill and cook for 2-3 minutes on each side. The bacon should become crispy, and the shrimp should turn pink and opaque.

  5. Glaze with Barbecue Sauce: Brush each shrimp with your favorite barbecue sauce, allowing it to caramelize and infuse the dish with rich, smoky flavors.

  6. Serve with Flair: Once the shrimp is cooked to perfection and glistening with the barbecue glaze, carefully remove them from the grill and arrange them on a platter. For an added touch, garnish with freshly chopped parsley to add a pop of color and freshness.

  7. Enjoy the Sizzle: Your BBQ Bacon-Wrapped Shrimp is ready to be served! Watch as your guests marvel at the irresistible aroma and dive into these delectable bites of heaven.

Tips and Variations

  • For a spicy twist, consider adding a sprinkle of cayenne pepper or a dash of hot sauce to the barbecue glaze.
  • If you prefer a smokier flavor, try using hickory-smoked bacon and a mesquite barbecue sauce.
  • Experiment with different types of seafood, such as scallops or chunks of firm fish, to create a seafood medley of bacon-wrapped delights.

Conclusion

BBQ Bacon-Wrapped Shrimp is the ultimate crowd-pleaser, combining the indulgent flavors of bacon with the irresistible appeal of grilled shrimp. This Fourth of July, treat your guests to a tantalizing appetizer that will set the stage for a memorable celebration. With its savory, smoky, and slightly sweet profile, this dish is sure to become a staple at your summer gatherings. So fire up the grill, sizzle up some shrimp, and let the festivities begin!
