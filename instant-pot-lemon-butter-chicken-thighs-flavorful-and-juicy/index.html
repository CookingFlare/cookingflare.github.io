Instant Pot Lemon Butter Chicken Thighs: Flavorful and Juicy


If you’re looking for a quick and easy yet incredibly flavorful chicken dish, look no further than Instant Pot Lemon Butter Chicken Thighs. This recipe is a game-changer for busy weeknights or when you’re craving a comforting meal without spending hours in the kitchen. The combination of zesty lemon and rich butter creates a mouthwatering sauce that perfectly coats tender chicken thighs, resulting in a dish that’s both vibrant and indulgent. Let’s dive into the details of creating this delectable dish in your Instant Pot.

Ingredients

  • 6 bone-in, skin-on chicken thighs
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup chicken broth
  • 1/4 cup fresh lemon juice
  • Zest of 1 lemon
  • 2 tablespoons unsalted butter
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • 1 teaspoon dried rosemary
  • 1/2 teaspoon red pepper flakes (optional)
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • Fresh parsley for garnish

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Chicken Thighs: Start by patting the chicken thighs dry with paper towels. Season both sides with salt and black pepper.
  2. Sear the Chicken: Select the “Sauté” function on the Instant Pot. Once hot, add olive oil and sear the chicken thighs, skin side down, until golden brown. This should take about 4-5 minutes per side. Transfer the seared chicken to a plate and set aside.
  3. Sauté the Aromatics: Add minced garlic to the pot and sauté for about 30 seconds until fragrant.
  4. Deglaze the Pot: Pour in the chicken broth and use a wooden spoon to deglaze the bottom of the pot, scraping up any browned bits for added flavor.
  5. Pressure Cook the Chicken: Return the seared chicken thighs to the pot. Pour in the lemon juice and sprinkle the lemon zest over the chicken. Add butter, dried thyme, dried rosemary, and red pepper flakes (if using). Secure the Instant Pot lid and set the vent to “Sealing.” Pressure cook on high for 8 minutes.
  6. Natural Pressure Release: Once the cooking cycle is complete, allow the pressure to release naturally for 5 minutes. Then, carefully switch the vent to “Venting” to release any remaining pressure.
  7. Add the Finishing Touch: Open the lid and transfer the chicken to a serving platter. Select the “Sauté” function again and pour in the heavy cream. Let the sauce simmer and reduce for a few minutes until slightly thickened.
  8. Serve and Enjoy: Pour the luscious lemon butter sauce over the chicken thighs, garnish with fresh parsley, and serve hot.

Tips for Success

  • For an extra burst of flavor, add a splash of white wine to the sauce before pressure cooking.
  • If you prefer a thicker sauce, you can create a slurry by mixing a tablespoon of cornstarch with a tablespoon of water and stirring it into the sauce during the sautéing step.
  • Ensure the chicken thighs reach an internal temperature of 165°F (74°C) before serving.

Final Thoughts

Instant Pot Lemon Butter Chicken Thighs are a delightful fusion of bright citrus, savory herbs, and rich cream, resulting in a dish that’s both elegant and comforting. The tender, juicy chicken thighs bathed in the luscious lemon butter sauce are sure to impress your family and guests. This recipe strikes the perfect balance between convenience and gourmet, making it a must-try for any home cook. Whether you’re new to using an Instant Pot or a seasoned pro, this dish is a testament to the magic of pressure cooking. Give it a try and savor the delightful flavors of this succulent chicken dish!
