Delicious Greek Gigantes Plaki: A Flavorful Bean Dish


If you’re looking to add a taste of Greece to your dinner table, then Greek Gigantes Plaki is a must-try dish. This flavorful bean dish is a staple in Greek cuisine, and it’s not hard to see why. The combination of tender, creamy beans, tangy tomatoes, and aromatic herbs makes this dish a true delight for the taste buds.

Ingredients

To make this delicious Greek Gigantes Plaki, you’ll need the following ingredients:

  • 500g dried gigantes beans
  • 1 large onion, finely chopped
  • 3 garlic cloves, minced
  • 2 carrots, diced
  • 1 celery stalk, diced
  • 1 can (400g) of chopped tomatoes
  • 1/2 cup extra virgin olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon tomato paste
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley for garnish

Instructions

Step 1: Prepare the Beans

Start by soaking the gigantes beans in cold water overnight. The next day, drain the beans and rinse them thoroughly.

Step 2: Cook the Beans

Place the beans in a large pot and cover them with water. Bring the water to a boil, then reduce the heat and let the beans simmer for about 45 minutes, or until they are tender but still hold their shape. Drain the beans and set them aside.

Step 3: Prepare the Sauce

In a separate pan, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the chopped onion, garlic, carrots, and celery, and sauté until the vegetables are soft and fragrant.

Step 4: Add the Tomatoes

Stir in the chopped tomatoes, tomato paste, sugar, dried oregano, and thyme. Season the sauce with salt and pepper to taste. Let the sauce simmer for about 10 minutes, allowing the flavors to meld together.

Step 5: Combine the Beans and Sauce

Preheat your oven to 180°C (350°F). In a large baking dish, spread out the cooked gigantes beans. Pour the tomato sauce over the beans, making sure to coat them evenly.

Step 6: Bake the Gigantes Plaki

Cover the baking dish with aluminum foil and bake the gigantes plaki for 45 minutes. Then, remove the foil and bake for an additional 15 minutes, or until the top is golden and bubbly.

Step 7: Serve and Enjoy

Once the gigantes plaki is ready, remove it from the oven and let it cool slightly. Sprinkle some freshly chopped parsley over the dish for a pop of color and freshness. Serve the gigantes plaki warm, accompanied by crusty bread to mop up the delicious sauce.

Final Thoughts

Greek Gigantes Plaki is a versatile dish that can be served as a main course or as a side dish to complement grilled meats or fish. The creamy texture of the beans, combined with the rich tomato sauce and aromatic herbs, makes this dish a true crowd-pleaser.

So, next time you’re in the mood for a taste of the Mediterranean, give this delicious Greek Gigantes Plaki recipe a try. It’s a simple, hearty, and flavorful dish that will transport your taste buds to the sunny shores of Greece. Enjoy!
