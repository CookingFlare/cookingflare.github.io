Indulgent Chocolate Cupcakes with Salted Caramel Buttercream


If you’re a chocolate lover, then you’re in for a treat with these indulgent chocolate cupcakes topped with a luscious salted caramel buttercream. This recipe is perfect for special occasions or when you simply want to treat yourself to a little slice of heaven. So, let’s get started!

Ingredients

For the chocolate cupcakes:

  • 1 and 3/4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 and 1/2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1 and 1/2 teaspoons baking soda
  • 3/4 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 2 cups granulated sugar
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 cup whole milk
  • 1/2 cup vegetable oil
  • 2 teaspoons pure vanilla extract
  • 1 cup boiling water

For the salted caramel buttercream:

  • 1 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 4 cups powdered sugar
  • 1/4 cup salted caramel sauce (store-bought or homemade)
  • 1 teaspoon pure vanilla extract
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C) and line two cupcake pans with paper liners.

  2. In a large mixing bowl, whisk together the flour, baking powder, baking soda, cocoa powder, granulated sugar, and salt until well combined.

  3. Add the eggs, milk, vegetable oil, and vanilla extract to the dry ingredients. Mix on medium speed until the batter is smooth and well incorporated.

  4. Reduce the speed to low and slowly pour in the boiling water. Mix until the batter is smooth. The batter will be thin, but don’t worry, it will bake up beautifully.

  5. Using a scoop or a spoon, fill each cupcake liner about two-thirds full with the batter. This will allow room for the cupcakes to rise.

  6. Bake for 18-20 minutes or until a toothpick inserted into the center of a cupcake comes out clean.

  7. Remove the cupcakes from the oven and let them cool in the pan for 5 minutes. Then transfer them to a wire rack to cool completely.

  8. While the cupcakes are cooling, prepare the salted caramel buttercream. In a large mixing bowl, beat the softened butter until creamy and smooth.

  9. Gradually add the powdered sugar, salted caramel sauce, vanilla extract, and salt. Beat on low speed until the powdered sugar is fully incorporated. Then increase the speed to medium-high and beat for an additional 2-3 minutes until the buttercream is light and fluffy.

  10. Once the cupcakes have cooled completely, pipe or spread the salted caramel buttercream onto each cupcake. You can use a piping bag fitted with a star tip for a decorative touch, or simply use a butter knife to spread the buttercream.

  11. For an extra indulgent touch, drizzle a little extra salted caramel sauce over each cupcake.

  12. Serve and enjoy these moist and decadent chocolate cupcakes with a generous helping of salted caramel buttercream.

Tips and Variations

  • If you’re feeling adventurous, you can add a teaspoon of instant espresso powder to the batter to enhance the chocolate flavor.
  • For a more intense salted caramel flavor, you can add an additional tablespoon of salted caramel sauce to the buttercream.
  • If you don’t have salted caramel sauce, you can make your own by melting 1 cup of granulated sugar in a saucepan over medium heat until it turns into a deep amber color. Remove from heat and carefully add 1/2 cup of heavy cream, followed by 1 teaspoon of sea salt. Stir until smooth and let it cool before using in the buttercream.

These indulgent chocolate cupcakes with salted caramel buttercream are a true delight for any chocolate lover. The rich cocoa flavor of the cupcakes combined with the sweet and salty caramel buttercream create a perfect balance of flavors. Whether you’re celebrating a special occasion or simply craving a decadent treat, these cupcakes are sure to satisfy your sweet tooth. Enjoy!
