Indulgent Blackberry Crumble Bars: A Sweet and Tart Treat


If you’re in the mood for a delightful dessert that perfectly balances sweetness and tartness, then look no further than these indulgent Blackberry Crumble Bars. These bars are a perfect combination of buttery crust, luscious blackberry filling, and a crumbly topping that will leave your taste buds craving for more. Whether you’re hosting a gathering, packing a picnic, or simply treating yourself, these blackberry crumble bars are sure to impress.

Ingredients

For the Crust and Crumble Topping:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • A pinch of salt
  • Zest of one lemon
  • 1 cup unsalted butter, cold and cut into cubes
  • 1 egg

For the Blackberry Filling:

  • 4 cups fresh blackberries
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 2 tablespoons cornstarch
  • Juice of one lemon

Instructions

Step 1: Preparing the Crust and Crumble Topping

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C) and line a 9x13-inch baking pan with parchment paper, leaving an overhang for easy removal.
  2. In a large bowl, combine the flour, sugar, baking powder, salt, and lemon zest.
  3. Add the cold butter cubes to the dry ingredients and use a pastry cutter or your fingers to work the butter into the mixture until it resembles coarse crumbs.
  4. Add the egg and mix until the dough comes together. Set aside about 1 1/2 cups of the mixture for the crumble topping.
  5. Press the remaining dough into the bottom of the prepared baking pan to form the crust.

Step 2: Making the Blackberry Filling

  1. In a separate bowl, gently toss the blackberries with sugar, cornstarch, and lemon juice until well coated.
  2. Spread the blackberry mixture evenly over the crust in the baking pan.

Step 3: Assembling the Bars

  1. Sprinkle the reserved crumble topping evenly over the blackberry filling.
  2. Bake in the preheated oven for 45-50 minutes, or until the top is golden brown and the blackberry filling is bubbling.

Step 4: Cooling and Serving

  1. Allow the blackberry crumble bars to cool completely in the pan on a wire rack.
  2. Once cooled, use the parchment paper overhang to lift the bars out of the pan and onto a cutting board.
  3. Cut into squares and serve.

Tips and Variations

  • For a twist, you can add a handful of chopped nuts such as almonds or pecans to the crumble topping for extra crunch.
  • These bars are best enjoyed within a few days of baking and can be stored in an airtight container at room temperature.

Conclusion

These Indulgent Blackberry Crumble Bars are a delightful way to showcase the seasonal bounty of fresh blackberries. The buttery crust, sweet-tart blackberry filling, and crumbly topping make for a truly irresistible treat that will have everyone asking for more. Whether you’re a seasoned baker or just starting out, this recipe is sure to impress and satisfy your cravings for something sweet and indulgent. So, roll up your sleeves, preheat that oven, and get ready to savor every bite of these delectable blackberry crumble bars.
