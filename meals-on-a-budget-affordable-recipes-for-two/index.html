Meals on a Budget: Affordable Recipes for Two


Are you looking for delicious and budget-friendly recipes that are perfect for two people? Look no further! In this blog post, we will share some mouthwatering recipes that won’t break the bank. These recipes are not only easy to make but also packed with flavor and nutrition. So, let’s get cooking!

1. Creamy Tomato Pasta

Ingredients:

  • 200g spaghetti
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can (400g) diced tomatoes
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh basil leaves for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Cook the spaghetti according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a large pan, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the minced garlic and sauté until fragrant.
  3. Add the diced tomatoes, including the juice, to the pan. Season with salt and pepper. Let it simmer for about 10 minutes.
  4. Stir in the heavy cream and cook for an additional 2-3 minutes, until the sauce thickens slightly.
  5. Add the cooked spaghetti to the pan and toss until well coated with the sauce.
  6. Serve hot, garnished with fresh basil leaves.

2. Stuffed Bell Peppers

Ingredients:

  • 2 bell peppers (any color)
  • 200g ground beef
  • 1/2 cup cooked rice
  • 1/4 cup diced onion
  • 1/4 cup diced tomato
  • 1/4 cup shredded cheese
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 180°C (350°F).
  2. Cut the tops off the bell peppers and remove the seeds and membranes.
  3. In a bowl, mix together the ground beef, cooked rice, diced onion, diced tomato, shredded cheese, salt, and pepper.
  4. Stuff the bell peppers with the mixture and place them in a baking dish.
  5. Bake for about 25-30 minutes, until the peppers are tender and the filling is cooked through.
  6. Serve hot and enjoy!

3. Veggie Stir-Fry

Ingredients:

  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • 1/2 onion, sliced
  • 1 bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 carrot, julienned
  • 1 zucchini, sliced
  • 1 cup broccoli florets
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon honey
  • 1/2 teaspoon grated ginger
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • Sesame seeds for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Heat the vegetable oil in a large pan or wok over medium-high heat.
  2. Add the sliced onion, bell pepper, carrot, zucchini, and broccoli to the pan. Stir-fry for about 5 minutes, until the vegetables are crisp-tender.
  3. In a small bowl, whisk together the soy sauce, honey, grated ginger, and minced garlic.
  4. Pour the sauce over the vegetables and stir-fry for an additional 2-3 minutes, until the sauce thickens slightly.
  5. Remove from heat and garnish with sesame seeds.
  6. Serve hot with steamed rice or noodles.

4. Baked Chicken and Potatoes

Ingredients:

  • 2 chicken thighs
  • 2 potatoes, cut into wedges
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 200°C (400°F).
  2. Place the chicken thighs and potato wedges in a baking dish.
  3. In a small bowl, mix together the olive oil, dried thyme, paprika, salt, and pepper.
  4. Drizzle the mixture over the chicken and potatoes, ensuring they are evenly coated.
  5. Bake for about 30-35 minutes, until the chicken is cooked through and the potatoes are golden and crispy.
  6. Serve hot and enjoy!

These affordable recipes for two are perfect for those who want to enjoy delicious meals without breaking the bank. Whether you’re a busy couple or simply looking to save money, these recipes are sure to satisfy your taste buds and your wallet. So, roll up your sleeves, grab your apron, and start cooking up some budget-friendly culinary delights!
