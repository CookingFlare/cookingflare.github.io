Vegan BBQ Bonanza: 20 Flavor-Packed, High-Protein Recipes for Dad's Special Day


Father’s Day is long overdue for a plant-based refresh, and we have you covered with a mouthwatering variety of vegan BBQ recipes to cater to Dad’s tastebuds. This BBQ bonanza is filled with 20 flavor-packed, protein-rich recipes, sure to satisfy your carnivore dad while satisfying your plant-based heart. It’s time to fire up the grill and ditch the doubt, let us serve you the ultimate vegan BBQ collection for Dad’s special day.

  1. Portobello Mushroom Burgers with Quinoa Slaw

Start Dad’s day off right with a fully loaded portobello mushroom burger, packed with savory umami flavor and fresh quinoa slaw. Grill the Portobello cap until it’s juicy and the gills are slightly crisp. Then, crown it with a pile of flavorful quinoa slaw and tomato.

Ingredients:

  • 4 large portobello mushroom caps
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 cup cooked quinoa
  • 2 tablespoons lemon juice
  • 1/2 cup vegan mayo
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 1/2 head of green cabbage, thinly sliced
  • 2 carrots, shredded
  • 1 red bell pepper, thinly sliced
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the grill to medium heat.
  2. Brush the mushroom caps with olive oil and season with salt and pepper.
  3. Grill for about 4-5 minutes on each side, until soft.
  4. In a bowl, combine quinoa, lemon juice, and vegan mayo.
  5. Fill a separate bowl with water, then whisk in the same amount of vegan mayo, and mix-in the cabbage, carrots, bell pepper, and parsley.
  6. Grill a whole wheat bun, then stack the Portobello mushroom cap, slather with quinoa mixture, top with a generous dollop of slaw, and serve.
