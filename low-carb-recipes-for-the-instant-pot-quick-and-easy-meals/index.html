Low Carb Recipes for the Instant Pot: Quick and Easy Meals


When life gets busy, meal prep often feels like a daunting task. For those following a low carb lifestyle, finding delicious recipes that are quick, easy, and satisfying can be even more challenging. Luckily, the Instant Pot offers a solution—it’s a versatile kitchen tool perfect for whipping up low carb meals in a fraction of the time. Whether you’re managing your carb intake for health reasons, pursuing a ketogenic diet, or just want to clean up your eating habits, these Instant Pot recipes will make your culinary routine easier and far more enjoyable.

Why the Instant Pot Is Perfect for Low Carb Cooking

The Instant Pot has revolutionized home cooking, especially when it comes to healthy eating. Its combination of pressure cooking, sautéing, and slow-cooking functions allows you to prepare complex meals with minimal effort. For low carb recipes, this is a game-changer. Ingredients like protein-packed meats, nutrient-rich vegetables, and hearty broths cook quickly, retaining flavor and nutrients. Plus, you’ll minimize the need for additional ingredients like flour or starch, which are commonly used in traditional recipes but avoided on a low carb diet.

Another key benefit: the Instant Pot’s one-pot approach makes cleanup fast and simple. Instead of juggling multiple pans or racking up a sink full of dishes, you can prepare entire meals in one convenient device. Now let’s dive into some delicious low carb Instant Pot recipes.

1. Instant Pot Lemon Garlic Chicken Thighs

Chicken thighs are the perfect protein for a low carb lifestyle, thanks to their higher fat content and robust flavor. This lemon garlic recipe is simple to make and delivers a bright, tangy sauce that pairs beautifully with steamed veggies or cauliflower rice.

Ingredients:

  • 6 bone-in, skin-on chicken thighs
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 4 garlic cloves, minced
  • Juice of 1 lemon
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 cup chicken broth

Instructions:

  1. Set your Instant Pot to sauté mode and add olive oil. When the oil is hot, sear the chicken thighs on each side for 2–3 minutes until golden brown. Remove and set aside.
  2. Add garlic to the pot and sauté until fragrant, about 30 seconds. Pour in chicken broth, lemon juice, oregano, and paprika. Stir to combine.
  3. Return the chicken thighs to the pot, skin-side up. Secure the lid and set the Instant Pot to pressure cook on high for 15 minutes.
  4. Once cooking is complete, allow a natural release for 5 minutes before manually releasing any remaining pressure.
  5. Serve the chicken with your favorite low carb sides and drizzle the lemon garlic sauce on top for extra flavor.

2. Low Carb Beef Stroganoff

Beef stroganoff, traditionally made with flour and heavy pasta, gets a low carb makeover in this Instant Pot recipe. Using mushrooms and zucchini noodles instead of carb-heavy ingredients gives this dish the classic creamy flavor without the guilt.

Ingredients:

  • 1-pound beef stew meat, cubed
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 1 small onion, diced
  • 2 cups sliced mushrooms
  • 1 tablespoon Dijon mustard
  • 1 cup beef broth
  • 1 cup sour cream
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Zucchini noodles (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Set the Instant Pot to sauté mode and melt the butter. Add the diced onion and mushrooms, cooking until softened.
  2. Add the beef stew meat to the pot, seasoning with salt and pepper. Sear until lightly browned.
  3. Stir in Dijon mustard and beef broth. Close the lid and pressure cook on high for 20 minutes.
  4. After the timer is up, perform a quick pressure release. Stir in sour cream and adjust seasoning as needed.
  5. Serve over zucchini noodles or alongside steamed broccoli for a perfect low carb plate.

3. Instant Pot Cauliflower Soup

For a filling and creamy soup without the carbs, cauliflower is an ideal base. It’s rich in nutrients, low in carbohydrates, and blends perfectly to achieve a comforting texture. This Instant Pot cauliflower soup is quick, delicious, and perfect for batch cooking.

Ingredients:

  • 1 head of cauliflower, chopped into florets
  • 1 small onion, diced
  • 3 garlic cloves, minced
  • 3 cups chicken or vegetable broth
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1 teaspoon thyme
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Turn your Instant Pot to sauté mode and cook the onions and garlic until softened. Add chopped cauliflower florets and thyme.
  2. Pour in the chicken or vegetable broth, ensuring the cauliflower is mostly covered. Close the lid and set the Instant Pot to pressure cook on high for 8 minutes.
  3. Once cooking is done, perform a quick release. Use an immersion blender to puree the soup directly in the pot until smooth.
  4. Stir in heavy cream and adjust seasoning. Serve warm with a sprinkle of fresh parsley or shredded cheese for garnish.

4. Instant Pot Pulled Pork Lettuce Wraps

Who says you need bread for a satisfying pulled pork sandwich? These lettuce wraps offer a low carb alternative that doesn’t skimp on flavor. The Instant Pot makes the pulled pork tender and juicy, perfect for customizing with your favorite toppings.

Ingredients:

  • 2 pounds pork shoulder, trimmed and cut into chunks
  • 1 tablespoon smoked paprika
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon onion powder
  • 1 cup chicken broth
  • Butter lettuce leaves for wrapping
  • Low carb barbecue sauce (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Rub the pork chunks with smoked paprika, garlic powder, and onion powder, ensuring they’re well coated.
  2. Pour chicken broth into the Instant Pot and place the pork inside. Close the lid and pressure cook on high for 45 minutes.
  3. Once the pork is cooked, shred it using two forks. If desired, toss with a low carb barbecue sauce for additional flavor.
  4. Serve the shredded pork in butter lettuce leaves for a delicious and carb-conscious meal.

5. Instant Pot Egg Bites

Egg bites are a versatile low carb option for breakfast, lunch, or a quick snack. The Instant Pot makes these perfectly cooked and creamy, similar to the ones you’d grab at a coffee shop—but with fewer carbs and customizable flavors.

Ingredients:

  • 6 large eggs
  • 1/3 cup heavy cream
  • 1 cup shredded cheese (cheddar, Gruyère, or mozzarella)
  • 1/2 cup cooked bacon or sausage, crumbled
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Silicone egg bite molds

Instructions:

  1. Mix eggs, heavy cream, shredded cheese, and crumbled bacon in a bowl. Season with salt and pepper.
  2. Pour the mixture into silicone egg bite molds, filling each compartment about ¾ full.
  3. Place the molds on the Instant Pot trivet with one cup of water in the bottom of the pot. Close the lid and pressure cook on high for 8 minutes.
  4. Once cooking is complete, allow a natural release for 5 minutes, then manually release any remaining pressure.
  5. Let the egg bites cool slightly before removing from the molds. Store in the fridge for grab-and-go convenience.

Final Thoughts

Low carb meals don’t have to be time-consuming or complicated, and the Instant Pot proves that with every recipe. From creamy soups and hearty meat dishes to quick breakfast bites, this appliance takes the hassle out of healthy cooking. By incorporating these recipes into your weekly rotation, you’ll enjoy the benefits of a low carb lifestyle without sacrificing flavor or convenience. Whether you’re feeding your family or prepping for the week ahead, the Instant Pot can help you stick to your goals and create meals everyone will love.
