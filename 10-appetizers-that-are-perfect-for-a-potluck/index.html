10 Appetizers That Are Perfect for a Potluck


When it comes to potlucks, it’s essential to bring a dish that’s both delicious and easy to share. Here are 10 appetizer recipes that are perfect for a potluck, ranging from classic favorites to modern twists on traditional dishes.

1. Spinach and Artichoke Dip

This creamy dip is a crowd-pleaser, and it’s easy to make in large quantities. Simply combine 1 (14 oz) can of artichoke hearts, 1 package of frozen spinach, 1 cup of mayonnaise, 1/2 cup of sour cream, 1/2 cup of grated Parmesan cheese, and 1/2 teaspoon of lemon juice in a bowl. Serve with tortilla chips or pita bread.

2. Mini Quiches

These individual quiches are perfect for a potluck, and they can be made in advance. Simply mix 1 cup of grated cheddar cheese, 1/2 cup of diced ham, and 1/2 cup of chopped fresh parsley into 1 cup of pie crust dough. Roll out the dough, cut into squares, and bake at 375°F for 15-20 minutes.

3. Caprese Skewers

These colorful skewers are easy to make and require minimal ingredients. Simply thread cherry tomatoes, mozzarella balls, and fresh basil leaves onto skewers. Drizzle with olive oil and balsamic glaze before serving.

4. Stuffed Mushrooms

These earthy mushrooms are filled with a savory mixture of sausage, breadcrumbs, and cheese. Simply fill 1 cup of cleaned and stemmed mushrooms with a mixture of 1/2 cup of cooked sausage, 1/4 cup of breadcrumbs, and 1/4 cup of grated Parmesan cheese. Bake at 375°F for 15-20 minutes.

5. Bacon-Wrapped Dates

These sweet and savory bites are a crowd-pleaser, and they’re easy to make. Simply wrap 1/4 cup of pitted dates with 6 slices of bacon, securing with a toothpick if necessary. Bake at 400°F for 15-20 minutes, or until the bacon is crispy.

6. Prosciutto-Wrapped Melon

This refreshing appetizer is perfect for a summer potluck. Simply wrap 1/4 cup of sliced prosciutto around 1/2 cup of sliced melon, securing with a toothpick if necessary. Serve chilled.

7. Mini Beef Sliders

These bite-sized burgers are perfect for a potluck, and they can be customized to suit your guests’ tastes. Simply mix 1 pound of ground beef with 1/4 cup of chopped onion, 1/4 cup of chopped fresh parsley, and 1 egg. Form into small patties and grill or pan-fry until cooked through. Serve on small buns with cheese, lettuce, and tomato.

8. Roasted Red Pepper Hummus

This creamy dip is a twist on traditional hummus, and it’s perfect for a potluck. Simply roast 2 red bell peppers in the oven until charred, then peel and puree in a blender with 1/4 cup of tahini, 1/4 cup of lemon juice, and 2 cloves of garlic. Serve with pita chips or crudités.

9. Baked Brie and Fresh Fruit

This elegant appetizer is perfect for a wine and cheese party. Simply bake 1 wheel of brie cheese at 350°F for 10-15 minutes, or until softened. Serve with 1/2 cup of sliced fresh fruit, such as grapes and berries.

10. Spicy Shrimp Ceviche

This refreshing appetizer is perfect for a summer potluck. Simply marinate 1 cup of peeled and deveined shrimp in a mixture of 1/2 cup of lime juice, 1/4 cup of chopped fresh cilantro, and 1/4 cup of diced jalapeño pepper. Serve with tortilla chips or tostadas.

These 10 appetizers are sure to impress your guests at your next potluck. Whether you’re looking for classic comfort foods or modern twists on traditional dishes, there’s something on this list for everyone. So go ahead, get cooking, and enjoy the party!
