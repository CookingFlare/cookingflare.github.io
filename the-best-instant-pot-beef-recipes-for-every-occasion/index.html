The Best Instant Pot Beef Recipes for Every Occasion


The Instant Pot has revolutionized the way we cook. It’s the perfect kitchen tool for busy individuals, families, and even cooking enthusiasts who love a great meal without the hassle of hours spent over the stove. When it comes to beef, the Instant Pot can do wonders, transforming tough cuts of meat into tender, flavorful dishes in no time. Whether you’re preparing a weeknight dinner, hosting a special occasion, or looking for a hearty meal to enjoy with family, there are a variety of beef recipes that can be made effortlessly in the Instant Pot.

In this post, we’ll explore some of the best Instant Pot beef recipes for every occasion, from quick weeknight dinners to luxurious Sunday feasts. These recipes cater to different tastes and preferences, ensuring that you have something delicious for any occasion.

1. Instant Pot Beef Stew

The Ultimate Comfort Food

Beef stew is a classic comfort food that is perfect for chilly nights or when you’re craving something hearty. With the Instant Pot, you can prepare this dish in under an hour, significantly cutting down the traditional cooking time while still achieving that rich, melt-in-your-mouth tenderness that you crave.

Ingredients:

  • 2 pounds beef stew meat, cut into cubes
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 3 carrots, sliced
  • 3 potatoes, diced
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 4 cups beef broth
  • 2 tablespoons tomato paste
  • 1 teaspoon thyme
  • 1 teaspoon rosemary
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 tablespoons flour (optional for thickening)

Instructions:

  1. Sauté the beef: Start by turning the Instant Pot on to the “Sauté” setting. Add olive oil and heat it until it shimmers. Add the beef cubes and season with salt and pepper. Brown the beef on all sides for about 5-7 minutes.
  2. Sauté the vegetables: Add the chopped onion and garlic to the pot, cooking for 2-3 minutes until softened.
  3. Add the broth and seasonings: Stir in the beef broth, tomato paste, thyme, and rosemary. Mix well to combine.
  4. Pressure cook: Close the Instant Pot lid and set the valve to sealing. Set the Instant Pot to “Manual” and cook for 30 minutes on high pressure.
  5. Add the vegetables: After the 30 minutes, quickly release the pressure and add the carrots and potatoes to the pot. Close the lid again and cook for an additional 5 minutes on high pressure.
  6. Thicken the stew (optional): If you’d like a thicker stew, remove the beef and vegetables, then set the Instant Pot to “Sauté” mode. Stir in 2 tablespoons of flour to the broth and cook until it thickens, about 5 minutes. Return the beef and vegetables to the pot and mix well.
  7. Serve and enjoy: Taste for seasoning and adjust with salt and pepper as needed. Serve the stew with warm crusty bread or over rice.

Why You’ll Love It:

This Instant Pot beef stew is a flavorful, filling meal that’s perfect for a cozy family dinner or a crowd. The beef becomes wonderfully tender, and the vegetables absorb all the rich flavors from the broth. Plus, the Instant Pot cuts down the cooking time, making it quicker than traditional stovetop methods.

2. Instant Pot Beef Short Ribs

Rich and Tender Short Ribs in No Time

Beef short ribs are a show-stopping dish that’s perfect for special occasions like family gatherings or dinner parties. Typically, cooking short ribs takes several hours, but with the Instant Pot, you can enjoy this dish in just about 1.5 hours.

Ingredients:

  • 3-4 pounds beef short ribs
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 onion, sliced
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup red wine
  • 1 cup beef broth
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons brown sugar
  • 1 teaspoon thyme
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Sauté the ribs: Set the Instant Pot to “Sauté” mode. Add the olive oil and heat. Season the short ribs with salt and pepper, and brown them in batches for about 3-4 minutes on each side.
  2. Cook the aromatics: Remove the ribs and set aside. Add the sliced onion and minced garlic to the pot, and sauté for 2-3 minutes until softened.
  3. Deglaze the pot: Pour in the red wine, scraping the bottom of the pot to release any browned bits. Let the wine simmer for 2-3 minutes, reducing slightly.
  4. Add the liquid and seasonings: Stir in the beef broth, soy sauce, brown sugar, and thyme. Return the ribs to the pot, making sure they’re submerged in the liquid.
  5. Pressure cook: Close the lid and set the valve to sealing. Cook on high pressure for 45 minutes, followed by a natural release for 10 minutes before quick releasing the remaining pressure.
  6. Serve and enjoy: Remove the ribs from the pot and let them rest for a few minutes. Serve them with mashed potatoes, polenta, or over a bed of rice for a truly indulgent meal.

Why You’ll Love It:

Instant Pot beef short ribs come out incredibly tender and flavorful, with rich gravy that can be served over a side of your choice. The pressure cooking method locks in all the flavors and ensures that the ribs are fork-tender, making them perfect for an elegant dinner.

3. Instant Pot Beef Tacos

Easy and Flavorful Weeknight Dinner

Tacos are always a crowd-pleaser, and with the Instant Pot, you can have a flavorful beef filling ready in no time. This recipe is perfect for taco night or when you want to make a delicious dinner without spending hours in the kitchen.

Ingredients:

  • 2 pounds ground beef
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 packet taco seasoning
  • 1 cup beef broth
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Taco shells or tortillas
  • Toppings: shredded lettuce, cheese, salsa, sour cream, avocado, etc.

Instructions:

  1. Sauté the beef: Turn on the Instant Pot and set it to “Sauté” mode. Add the ground beef and season with salt and pepper. Break it up with a spatula as it cooks, stirring occasionally until browned, about 5-7 minutes.
  2. Cook the aromatics: Add the chopped onion and minced garlic to the pot. Sauté for another 2-3 minutes until the onions become soft and translucent.
  3. Add seasoning and broth: Stir in the taco seasoning and beef broth. Mix well to combine.
  4. Pressure cook: Close the lid and set the valve to sealing. Cook on high pressure for 5 minutes, followed by a quick release of the pressure.
  5. Serve: Spoon the beef mixture into taco shells or tortillas. Top with your favorite toppings like shredded lettuce, cheese, salsa, sour cream, and avocado.

Why You’ll Love It:

These Instant Pot beef tacos are quick, delicious, and customizable. You can adjust the seasoning to suit your taste and load up on toppings for extra flavor. Whether you’re feeding a large family or preparing dinner for one, this recipe is a winner.

4. Instant Pot Beef and Broccoli

A Healthier Take on a Classic Chinese Dish

Beef and broccoli is a classic takeout dish, and with the Instant Pot, you can recreate it at home in under 30 minutes. This recipe combines tender beef with crisp broccoli and a flavorful sauce that’s perfect over a bowl of rice.

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound flank steak or sirloin, thinly sliced against the grain
  • 1 tablespoon sesame oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon grated ginger
  • 1 cup beef broth
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon oyster sauce
  • 2 tablespoons brown sugar
  • 2 teaspoons cornstarch (optional, for thickening)
  • 2 cups broccoli florets
  • Cooked rice, for serving

Instructions:

  1. Sauté the beef: Set the Instant Pot to “Sauté” mode and add the sesame oil. Once heated, add the beef slices and cook for 2-3 minutes until browned. Remove the beef from the pot and set aside.
  2. Cook the aromatics: Add the minced garlic and grated ginger to the pot and sauté for about 1 minute until fragrant.
  3. Make the sauce: Stir in the beef broth, soy sauce, oyster sauce, and brown sugar. If you prefer a thicker sauce, dissolve the cornstarch in 1 tablespoon of water and add it to the pot.
  4. Pressure cook: Return the beef to the pot and close the lid. Set the Instant Pot to “Manual” mode and cook on high pressure for 10 minutes.
  5. Add broccoli: Quick release the pressure and add the broccoli florets. Stir them into the beef and sauce, then set the Instant Pot to “Sauté” mode again for 2-3 minutes to cook the broccoli.
  6. Serve: Serve the beef and broccoli over cooked rice, and enjoy!

Why You’ll Love It:

Instant Pot beef and broccoli is a healthier, homemade alternative to takeout. The beef stays tender, and the broccoli is perfectly cooked, making it a quick and nutritious dinner option.

5. Instant Pot Beef Chili

A Spicy, Hearty Dish for Any Occasion

Beef chili is the epitome of a satisfying meal. Whether you’re having a cozy night in or preparing for a game-day feast, this hearty dish can be made easily in the Instant Pot. Packed with spices, ground beef, beans, and tomatoes, it’s a warming and filling dish.

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 1 bell pepper, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can (14.5 oz) diced tomatoes
  • 1 can (15 oz) kidney beans, drained and rinsed
  • 1 can (15 oz) black beans, drained and rinsed
  • 2 tablespoons chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • 1 teaspoon smoked paprika
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 cup beef broth
  • Optional toppings: sour cream, shredded cheese, green onions

Instructions:

  1. Sauté the beef: Set the Instant Pot to “Sauté” mode and add the ground beef. Cook, breaking it up with a spatula, until browned, about 5-7 minutes. Remove excess fat if necessary.
  2. Cook the vegetables: Add the chopped onion, bell pepper, and garlic to the pot. Sauté for 3-4 minutes until softened.
  3. Add the beans and seasonings: Stir in the diced tomatoes, kidney beans, black beans, chili powder, cumin, smoked paprika, salt, and pepper. Mix well.
  4. Pressure cook: Pour in the beef broth, close the lid, and set the Instant Pot to “Manual” for 15 minutes on high pressure.
  5. Serve: Once the pressure releases, stir the chili and taste for seasoning. Serve hot with optional toppings like sour cream, shredded cheese, or green onions.

Why You’ll Love It:

This Instant Pot beef chili is the perfect comfort food that’s bursting with flavors. It’s easy to customize by adding different beans, veggies, or spices, and it’s sure to satisfy a crowd. Plus, it’s ready in a fraction of the time compared to traditional methods.

These five Instant Pot beef recipes offer a wide range of options for every occasion. From quick weeknight meals to festive gatherings, the Instant Pot makes cooking beef easier and faster while still delivering delicious, tender results. Try them all, and discover how the Instant Pot can become your go-to tool for creating beef dishes that will impress your family and friends!
