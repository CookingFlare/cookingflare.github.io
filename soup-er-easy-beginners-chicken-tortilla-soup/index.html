Soup-er Easy: Beginner's Chicken Tortilla Soup


If you’re looking for a delicious and comforting meal that’s perfect for beginners, look no further than this Soup-er Easy Chicken Tortilla Soup recipe. Packed with flavor and simple to make, this soup will warm your soul and impress your taste buds. So, let’s dive right in!

Ingredients:

  • 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 medium onion, diced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 jalapeno pepper, seeded and finely chopped
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 can (14 ounces) diced tomatoes, undrained
  • 4 cups chicken broth
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 cup frozen corn kernels
  • 1 can (15 ounces) black beans, rinsed and drained
  • Juice of 1 lime
  • Fresh cilantro, chopped
  • Tortilla chips, crushed

Instructions:

  1. Start by cooking the chicken breasts. Heat the olive oil in a large pot over medium heat. Season the chicken breasts with salt and pepper, then add them to the pot. Cook for about 6-8 minutes per side, or until cooked through. Remove the chicken from the pot and set it aside to cool.

  2. In the same pot, add the diced onion, minced garlic, and jalapeno pepper. Sauté until the onion becomes translucent and the garlic becomes fragrant, about 5 minutes.

  3. Stir in the ground cumin, chili powder, and dried oregano. Cook for an additional minute to toast the spices and enhance their flavors.

  4. Add the diced tomatoes, chicken broth, salt, and pepper to the pot. Bring the mixture to a boil, then reduce the heat and let it simmer for about 15 minutes to allow the flavors to meld together.

  5. While the soup is simmering, shred the cooked chicken breasts using two forks. Once shredded, add the chicken to the pot.

  6. Stir in the frozen corn kernels and black beans. Continue to simmer the soup for an additional 10 minutes to ensure everything is heated through.

  7. Just before serving, squeeze the juice of one lime into the soup. This will add a fresh and tangy flavor that complements the other ingredients perfectly.

  8. Ladle the soup into bowls and garnish with fresh cilantro and crushed tortilla chips. The cilantro adds a burst of freshness, while the tortilla chips provide a satisfying crunch.

Serving Suggestions:

This soup is delicious on its own, but you can also customize it to suit your taste. Here are a few serving suggestions to consider:

  • Top the soup with a dollop of sour cream or Greek yogurt for a creamy and tangy twist.
  • Sprinkle some shredded cheese on top and let it melt into the hot soup for added richness.
  • Serve the soup alongside a bowl of freshly made guacamole or salsa for a complete Mexican-inspired meal.
  • If you prefer a spicier kick, add a few dashes of hot sauce or a sprinkle of crushed red pepper flakes.

Final Thoughts:

This Soup-er Easy Chicken Tortilla Soup is the perfect recipe for beginners who want to explore the world of homemade soups. With simple ingredients and straightforward instructions, you’ll have a delicious and comforting meal in no time. So, grab your apron, gather your ingredients, and get ready to enjoy a bowl of soup that will warm your heart and satisfy your cravings.
