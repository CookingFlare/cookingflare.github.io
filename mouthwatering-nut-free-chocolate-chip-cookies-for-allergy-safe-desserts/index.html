Mouthwatering Nut-Free Chocolate Chip Cookies for Allergy-Safe Desserts


If you or someone you know has a nut allergy, finding delicious desserts can sometimes be a challenge. But fear not, because I have the perfect recipe for mouthwatering nut-free chocolate chip cookies that are not only safe for those with allergies but also incredibly tasty!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 cup brown sugar, packed
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 2 cups chocolate chips (nut-free)

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C) and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.

  2. In a large mixing bowl, cream together the softened butter, granulated sugar, and brown sugar until light and fluffy. This step is crucial for achieving that perfect cookie texture, so make sure to cream the ingredients well.

  3. Add the eggs, one at a time, beating well after each addition. Then, stir in the vanilla extract.

  4. In a separate bowl, whisk together the all-purpose flour, baking soda, and salt. Gradually add the dry ingredients to the wet mixture, mixing until just combined. Be careful not to overmix, as this can result in dense cookies.

  5. Gently fold in the chocolate chips, ensuring they are evenly distributed throughout the dough. You can use nut-free chocolate chips to keep the recipe completely allergy-safe.

  6. Drop rounded tablespoons of dough onto the prepared baking sheet, spacing them about 2 inches apart. If you prefer larger cookies, you can use a larger cookie scoop or spoon.

  7. Bake the cookies in the preheated oven for 10-12 minutes, or until the edges are golden brown. The centers may still appear slightly soft, but they will firm up as the cookies cool.

  8. Allow the cookies to cool on the baking sheet for a few minutes before transferring them to a wire rack to cool completely. This will help them set and prevent them from breaking apart.

  9. Once cooled, indulge in these delectable nut-free chocolate chip cookies! They are perfect for satisfying your sweet tooth without any worry about nut allergies.

Tips and Variations

  • For an extra twist, you can add chopped dried fruits, such as cranberries or cherries, to the dough. This will add a burst of flavor and a touch of tartness to the cookies.
  • If you prefer chewy cookies, slightly underbake them by reducing the baking time by a minute or two.
  • To make these cookies vegan, you can substitute the butter with a vegan butter alternative and use flax eggs instead of regular eggs. The result will still be deliciously nut-free!
  • These cookies can be stored in an airtight container at room temperature for up to a week. However, I doubt they will last that long—they’re just too irresistible!

So there you have it—a scrumptious recipe for nut-free chocolate chip cookies that everyone can enjoy, even those with allergies. These cookies are so delectable that no one will even notice the absence of nuts. So go ahead and whip up a batch of these allergy-safe treats and indulge in their mouthwatering goodness!
