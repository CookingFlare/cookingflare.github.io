Savory Chicken Tortilla Soup: A Hearty Mexican Comfort Food


When it comes to comfort food, nothing beats a warm and flavorful bowl of chicken tortilla soup. This Mexican classic is a perfect blend of savory ingredients, spices, and textures that will leave you craving for more. Whether you’re looking for a quick weeknight dinner or a comforting meal on a chilly day, this savory chicken tortilla soup is the answer to your cravings. So, let’s dive right into the recipe!

Ingredients

  • 2 cups shredded cooked chicken
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 jalapeno pepper, seeded and finely diced
  • 1 red bell pepper, diced
  • 1 can (14.5 oz) diced tomatoes
  • 4 cups chicken broth
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1 cup corn kernels
  • 1 cup black beans, rinsed and drained
  • Fresh cilantro, chopped (for garnish)
  • Lime wedges (for serving)
  • Tortilla chips (for serving)

Instructions

  1. In a large pot, heat the vegetable oil over medium heat. Add the diced onion, minced garlic, jalapeno pepper, and red bell pepper. Sauté until the vegetables are softened and fragrant, about 5 minutes.

  2. Add the diced tomatoes (with their juice) to the pot and stir well. Cook for another 2 minutes.

  3. Pour in the chicken broth and add the shredded chicken, ground cumin, chili powder, dried oregano, salt, and pepper. Give it a good stir to combine all the flavors. Bring the soup to a boil, then reduce the heat and let it simmer for 15-20 minutes to allow the flavors to meld together.

  4. After the soup has simmered, add the corn kernels and black beans. Stir well and let it cook for an additional 5 minutes.

  5. Taste the soup and adjust the seasoning according to your preference. If you like it spicier, you can add more chili powder or jalapeno pepper.

  6. Once the soup is ready, ladle it into bowls and garnish with fresh cilantro. Serve with lime wedges and tortilla chips on the side for added crunch and flavor.

Tips and Variations

  • To save time, you can use pre-cooked rotisserie chicken or leftover roasted chicken for this recipe.
  • If you prefer a smoother soup, you can use an immersion blender or a regular blender to puree a portion of the soup before adding the corn and black beans.
  • For a creamier version of the soup, you can stir in a dollop of sour cream or add a splash of heavy cream at the end.
  • Feel free to customize your toppings according to your taste. Some popular options include diced avocado, shredded cheese, or a drizzle of hot sauce.

Conclusion

Savory chicken tortilla soup is the epitome of comfort food. With its rich flavors, hearty ingredients, and a touch of spice, this Mexican classic is sure to warm your soul and satisfy your taste buds. Whether enjoyed on a cozy night at home or shared with loved ones, this delicious soup is a treat that never disappoints. So, grab your ingredients and get ready to savor the goodness of this hearty Mexican comfort food. Buen provecho!
