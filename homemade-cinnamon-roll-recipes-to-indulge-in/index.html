Homemade Cinnamon Roll Recipes to Indulge in


If you’re a fan of sweet, gooey, and oh-so-delicious cinnamon rolls, then you’re in for a treat! In this blog post, we’re going to share with you some amazing homemade cinnamon roll recipes that will have you reaching for seconds (and maybe even thirds!). Whether you’re an experienced baker or just starting out, these recipes are sure to impress your friends and family. So grab your apron and let’s get baking!

Classic Cinnamon Rolls

Ingredients:

  • 2 ¾ cups all-purpose flour
  • ¼ cup granulated sugar
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 2 ¼ teaspoons instant yeast
  • ½ cup milk
  • ¼ cup water
  • 3 tablespoons unsalted butter, melted
  • 1 large egg

Filling:

  • ½ cup unsalted butter, softened
  • ¾ cup packed brown sugar
  • 2 tablespoons ground cinnamon

Icing:

  • 1 cup powdered sugar
  • 2 tablespoons milk
  • ½ teaspoon vanilla extract

Instructions:

  1. In a large mixing bowl, combine the flour, sugar, salt, and instant yeast. Mix well.
  2. In a separate microwave-safe bowl, heat the milk, water, and melted butter until warm (about 110°F/43°C).
  3. Pour the warm milk mixture into the flour mixture. Add the egg. Mix until a soft dough forms.
  4. Knead the dough on a lightly floured surface for about 5 minutes, until smooth and elastic.
  5. Place the dough in a greased bowl, cover it with a clean kitchen towel, and let it rise in a warm place for about 1 hour, or until doubled in size.
  6. Punch down the dough and roll it out into a 16x12-inch rectangle.
  7. Spread the softened butter evenly over the dough.
  8. In a small bowl, combine the brown sugar and cinnamon. Sprinkle the mixture over the buttered dough.
  9. Starting from the long side, tightly roll the dough into a log. Cut into 12 equal pieces.
  10. Place the cinnamon rolls in a greased baking dish, cover with a kitchen towel, and let them rise for another 30 minutes.
  11. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C). Bake the cinnamon rolls for 20-25 minutes, or until golden brown.
  12. While the cinnamon rolls are cooling, prepare the icing. In a small bowl, whisk together the powdered sugar, milk, and vanilla extract until smooth.
  13. Drizzle the icing over the warm cinnamon rolls and serve.

Chocolate Cinnamon Rolls

Ingredients:

  • 2 ¾ cups all-purpose flour
  • ¼ cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • ¼ cup granulated sugar
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 2 ¼ teaspoons instant yeast
  • ½ cup milk
  • ¼ cup water
  • 3 tablespoons unsalted butter, melted
  • 1 large egg

Filling:

  • ½ cup unsalted butter, softened
  • ¾ cup packed brown sugar
  • 2 tablespoons ground cinnamon
  • ½ cup semisweet chocolate chips

Icing:

  • 1 cup powdered sugar
  • 2 tablespoons milk
  • ½ teaspoon vanilla extract

Instructions:

  1. Follow the same instructions as the Classic Cinnamon Rolls recipe, but add the cocoa powder to the flour mixture.
  2. After spreading the softened butter over the dough, sprinkle the brown sugar, cinnamon, and chocolate chips on top.
  3. Roll, cut, and bake the cinnamon rolls following the same instructions as the Classic Cinnamon Rolls recipe.
  4. Prepare the icing by whisking together the powdered sugar, milk, and vanilla extract.
  5. Drizzle the icing over the warm chocolate cinnamon rolls and enjoy!

Gluten-Free Cinnamon Rolls

Ingredients:

  • 2 ½ cups gluten-free flour blend
  • 2 teaspoons xanthan gum (if not included in the flour blend)
  • ¼ cup granulated sugar
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 2 ¼ teaspoons instant yeast
  • ½ cup milk (or dairy-free alternative)
  • ¼ cup water
  • 3 tablespoons unsalted butter (or dairy-free alternative), melted
  • 1 large egg (or egg substitute)

Filling:

  • ½ cup unsalted butter (or dairy-free alternative), softened
  • ¾ cup packed brown sugar
  • 2 tablespoons ground cinnamon

Icing:

  • 1 cup powdered sugar
  • 2 tablespoons milk (or dairy-free alternative)
  • ½ teaspoon vanilla extract

Instructions:

  1. In a large mixing bowl, combine the gluten-free flour blend, xanthan gum (if needed), sugar, salt, and instant yeast.
  2. Heat the milk, water, and melted butter until warm (about 110°F/43°C).
  3. Pour the warm milk mixture into the flour mixture. Add the egg. Mix until a soft dough forms.
  4. Knead the dough on a lightly floured surface for about 5 minutes, until smooth and elastic.
  5. Place the dough in a greased bowl, cover it with a clean kitchen towel, and let it rise in a warm place for about 1 hour, or until doubled in size.
  6. Follow the same instructions for rolling, cutting, and baking the cinnamon rolls as in the Classic Cinnamon Rolls recipe.
  7. Prepare the icing by whisking together the powdered sugar, milk, and vanilla extract.
  8. Drizzle the icing over the warm gluten-free cinnamon rolls and savor every bite!

Conclusion

Now that you have three amazing homemade cinnamon roll recipes, it’s time to put your baking skills to the test. Whether you prefer the classic version, a chocolate twist, or need a gluten-free option, there’s a recipe here to satisfy your cravings. So gather your ingredients, preheat your oven, and get ready to indulge in the sweet aroma of freshly baked cinnamon rolls. Your taste buds will thank you!
