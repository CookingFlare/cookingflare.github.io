Bagel Brunch & Beyond: Delightful Bites for Little Food Critics

So, let’s tuck our aprons, fire up the oven, and wow those tiny taste buds once and for all with these delightful bites!

The Ultimate Bagel Bonanza

Who doesn’t love a classic bagel with cream cheese? Here’s a fully loaded twist that’ll turn it into a show-stopping sensation:

Bagel Burger Sliders

Ingredients:

  • 8 mini bagel buns
  • 8 oz ground beef
  • Salt and pepper
  • 1 tbsp barbecue sauce
  • 8 slices cheddar cheese
  • Optional toppings: caramelized onions, pickles, and tomato slices

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 400째F (200째C).
  2. Divide the ground beef into eight portions and shape them into small patties. Season with salt and pepper.
  3. Place the patties on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper. Bake for 10 minutes or until fully cooked. Top each patty with a slice of cheddar cheese and bake for another minute.
  4. Toast your mini bagel buns and spread with your favorite cream cheese mixture. Layer in barbecue sauce, a burger patty, caramelized onions, pickles, and a slice of tomato. Seal the deal with another bagel bun, and watch the little ones’ eyes widen in delight!

Beyond the Bagel Sandwiches

Brunch isn’t complete without a good sandwich, and these next dishes will have tiny jaws dropping in amazement:

Sunrise Sunrise Bagel Sandwich

Ingredients:

  • 2 slices of whole grain bagel
  • 2 scrambled eggs
  • 1 slice of Swiss cheese
  • 1/4 avocado, thinly sliced
  • 1 cherry tomato, halved
  • A handful of baby spinach leaves
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Toast your bagel slices and spread with a bit of butter. Meanwhile, scramble your eggs in a non-stick pan until cooked. Season with salt and pepper.
  2. On one slice of the bagel, layer the Swiss cheese, scrambled eggs, baby spinach, avocado, and cherry tomato halves. Top with the second bagel slice, and slice in half.

Smoked Salmon & Goat Cheese Bagel Bites

Ingredients:

  • 8 mini bagel buns
  • 8 oz smoked salmon
  • 8 oz soft goat cheese
  • 2 tbsp fresh dill, chopped
  • Lemon zest for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Slice each mini bagel bun in half horizontally. In a small bowl, mix the goat cheese with freshly chopped dill. Spread the mixture on the bottom half of each bagel bun.
  2. Place a slice of smoked salmon on top of the goat cheese mixture, add the other half of the bagel bun, firmly pressing the top on to ensure the flavors meld together. Garnish with a bit of lemon zest for a zesty twist!

Bagels for the Adventurous Soul

These recipes are perfect for the kiddos craving something out of the ordinary:

Bagel Breakfast Waffles

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 bagel (any flavor) smashed into a fine crumb
  • 1/4 cup milk
  • 1 egg
  • 2 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1/2 tsp baking powder
  • Cooking spray

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your waffle iron. In a large bowl, combine the flour, milk, smashed bagel, egg, vegetable oil, vanilla extract, and baking powder. Mix until well combined.
  2. Lightly coat your waffle iron with cooking spray. Pour your batter onto the hot waffle iron and cook according to the manufacturer’s instructions. Serve warm, topped with cream cheese and fresh berries for an extraordinary touch!

Sweet Bagel French Toast Sticks

Ingredients:

  • 4 bagels (any flavor), sliced into 8 equal sticks
  • 2 eggs
  • 3/4 cup milk
  • 2 tbsp sugar
  • 1 tsp cinnamon
  • Cooking spray

Instructions:

  1. In a shallow dish, whisk together the eggs, milk, sugar, and cinnamon. Coat each bagel stick in the mixture until fully absorbed.
  2. Grease a baking sheet with cooking spray and arrange the soaked bagel sticks in a single layer. Bake at 375째F (190째C) for 15-20 minutes, flipping halfway through, until golden brown. Serve with your favorite sweet dipping sauce!
