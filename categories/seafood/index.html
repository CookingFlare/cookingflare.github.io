Savory Garlic Butter Shrimp and Broccoli Stir-Fry: A Quick and Healthy Option

Are you looking for a delicious and nutritious meal that can be whipped up in no time? Look no further! This Savory Garlic Butter Shrimp and Broccoli Stir-Fry recipe is the perfect choice for those busy weeknights when you crave something flavorful, healthy, and hassle-free. Ingredients 1 pound of shrimp, peeled and deveined 2 cups of broccoli florets 2 tablespoons of butter 4 cloves of garlic, minced 1 tablespoon of soy sauce 1 tablespoon of honey Salt and pepper to taste Instructions In a large skillet or wok, melt the butter over medium-high heat. Add the minced garlic to the skillet and sauté for about 1 minute until fragrant. Add the shrimp to the skillet and cook for 2-3 minutes on each side until they turn pink and opaque. Remove the shrimp from the skillet and set aside. In the same skillet, add the broccoli florets and sauté for about 3-4 minutes until they become tender-crisp. While the broccoli is cooking, whisk together the soy sauce and honey in a small bowl. Once the broccoli is cooked, return the shrimp to the skillet and pour the soy sauce-honey mixture over the shrimp and broccoli. Stir well to coat everything evenly. Cook for an additional 2-3 minutes until the sauce thickens slightly and coats the shrimp and broccoli nicely. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Serve the Savory Garlic Butter Shrimp and Broccoli Stir-Fry hot, and enjoy!
