Delightful Christmas Doughnut Recipes to Brighten Your Day


The holiday season is a time for joy, celebration, and of course, delicious treats! One of the most delightful ways to spread cheer during Christmas is by baking doughnuts that not only taste amazing but also look festive. In this blog post, we’ll explore a variety of Christmas doughnut recipes that will brighten your day and impress your family and friends. From classic flavors to innovative twists, these recipes are sure to become holiday favorites.

The Magic of Christmas Doughnuts

Doughnuts are a versatile treat that can be enjoyed at any time of the day. They can be glazed, filled, or topped with a variety of ingredients, making them perfect for holiday gatherings. The best part? They can be made in advance and served fresh, allowing you to enjoy more time with your loved ones. Let’s dive into some delightful recipes that will make your Christmas celebrations even sweeter!

1. Classic Eggnog Doughnuts

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 tablespoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon nutmeg
  • 1/2 teaspoon cinnamon
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup eggnog
  • 1/4 cup milk
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1/4 cup melted butter
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • Powdered sugar for dusting

Instructions

  1. Preheat the Oven: Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C) and grease a doughnut pan.

  2. Mix Dry Ingredients: In a large bowl, whisk together the flour, sugar, baking powder, nutmeg, cinnamon, and salt.

  3. Combine Wet Ingredients: In another bowl, mix the eggnog, milk, eggs, melted butter, and vanilla extract until well combined.

  4. Combine Mixtures: Pour the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients and stir until just combined. Be careful not to overmix.

  5. Fill the Pan: Spoon the batter into the prepared doughnut pan, filling each cavity about 2/3 full.

  6. Bake: Bake for 10-12 minutes or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.

  7. Cool and Dust: Allow the doughnuts to cool for a few minutes before removing them from the pan. Dust with powdered sugar before serving.

These eggnog doughnuts are a festive twist on a classic flavor, perfect for holiday brunches or as a sweet treat after dinner.

2. Peppermint Mocha Doughnuts

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/2 cup cocoa powder
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup brewed coffee, cooled
  • 1/2 cup buttermilk
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1/4 cup vegetable oil
  • 1 teaspoon peppermint extract
  • Crushed peppermint candies for topping
  • Chocolate glaze (optional)

Instructions

  1. Preheat the Oven: Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C) and grease a doughnut pan.

  2. Mix Dry Ingredients: In a large bowl, combine the flour, sugar, cocoa powder, baking powder, baking soda, and salt.

  3. Combine Wet Ingredients: In another bowl, whisk together the coffee, buttermilk, eggs, vegetable oil, and peppermint extract.

  4. Combine Mixtures: Add the wet ingredients to the dry ingredients and mix until just combined.

  5. Fill the Pan: Spoon the batter into the doughnut pan, filling each cavity about 2/3 full.

  6. Bake: Bake for 10-12 minutes or until a toothpick comes out clean.

  7. Cool and Decorate: Allow the doughnuts to cool before glazing with chocolate and sprinkling with crushed peppermint candies.

These peppermint mocha doughnuts are a delightful combination of chocolate and mint, making them a perfect treat for coffee lovers during the holiday season.

3. Gingerbread Doughnuts with Cream Cheese Glaze

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup brown sugar
  • 1 tablespoon ground ginger
  • 1 teaspoon cinnamon
  • 1/2 teaspoon nutmeg
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup molasses
  • 1/2 cup buttermilk
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1/4 cup vegetable oil

For the Cream Cheese Glaze

  • 4 oz cream cheese, softened
  • 1 cup powdered sugar
  • 2 tablespoons milk
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Instructions

  1. Preheat the Oven: Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C) and grease a doughnut pan.

  2. Mix Dry Ingredients: In a large bowl, whisk together the flour, brown sugar, ginger, cinnamon, nutmeg, baking powder, baking soda, and salt.

  3. Combine Wet Ingredients: In another bowl, mix the molasses, buttermilk, eggs, and vegetable oil until well combined.

  4. Combine Mixtures: Pour the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients and stir until just combined.

  5. Fill the Pan: Spoon the batter into the doughnut pan, filling each cavity about 2/3 full.

  6. Bake: Bake for 10-12 minutes or until a toothpick comes out clean.

  7. Make the Glaze: In a bowl, beat the cream cheese until smooth. Gradually add the powdered sugar, milk, and vanilla extract, mixing until smooth.

  8. Cool and Glaze: Allow the doughnuts to cool before dipping them in the cream cheese glaze.

These gingerbread doughnuts are a festive favorite, bringing the warm spices of the season to your holiday table.

4. Cranberry Orange Doughnuts

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 tablespoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup orange juice
  • 1/4 cup vegetable oil
  • 2 large eggs
  • Zest of 1 orange
  • 1 cup fresh cranberries, chopped

For the Orange Glaze

  • 1 cup powdered sugar
  • 2 tablespoons orange juice
  • Zest of 1 orange

Instructions

  1. Preheat the Oven: Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C) and grease a doughnut pan.

  2. Mix Dry Ingredients: In a large bowl, whisk together the flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt.

  3. Combine Wet Ingredients: In another bowl, mix the orange juice, vegetable oil, eggs, and orange zest until well combined.

  4. Combine Mixtures: Pour the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients and stir until just combined. Gently fold in the chopped cranberries.

  5. Fill the Pan: Spoon the batter into the doughnut pan, filling each cavity about 2/3 full.

  6. Bake: Bake for 10-12 minutes or until a toothpick comes out clean.

  7. Make the Glaze: In a bowl, mix the powdered sugar, orange juice, and orange zest until smooth.

  8. Cool and Glaze: Allow the doughnuts to cool before dipping them in the orange glaze.

These cranberry orange doughnuts are a refreshing treat that captures the essence of the holiday season with their bright flavors.

5. Chocolate Peppermint Doughnuts

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/2 cup cocoa powder
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup buttermilk
  • 1/4 cup vegetable oil
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 teaspoon peppermint extract
  • 1/2 cup chocolate chips

For the Topping

  • 1 cup powdered sugar
  • 2 tablespoons milk
  • Crushed peppermint candies

Instructions

  1. Preheat the Oven: Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C) and grease a doughnut pan.

  2. Mix Dry Ingredients: In a large bowl, combine the flour, sugar, cocoa powder, baking powder, baking soda, and salt.

  3. Combine Wet Ingredients: In another bowl, whisk together the buttermilk, vegetable oil, eggs, and peppermint extract.

  4. Combine Mixtures: Add the wet ingredients to the dry ingredients and mix until just combined. Fold in the chocolate chips.

  5. Fill the Pan: Spoon the batter into the doughnut pan, filling each cavity about 2/3 full.

  6. Bake: Bake for 10-12 minutes or until a toothpick comes out clean.

  7. Make the Topping: In a bowl, mix the powdered sugar and milk until smooth.

  8. Cool and Decorate: Allow the doughnuts to cool before drizzling with the topping and sprinkling with crushed peppermint candies.

These chocolate peppermint doughnuts are a decadent treat that combines the rich flavor of chocolate with the refreshing taste of peppermint, making them a perfect addition to your holiday dessert table.

Tips for Perfect Doughnuts

  • Use Fresh Ingredients: Always use fresh ingredients for the best flavor and texture.
  • Don’t Overmix
