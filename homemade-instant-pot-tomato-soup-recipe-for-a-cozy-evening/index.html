Homemade Instant Pot Tomato Soup Recipe for a Cozy Evening


There’s nothing quite like a warm bowl of homemade tomato soup to make a cozy evening complete. This Instant Pot Tomato Soup recipe is not only delicious but also incredibly easy to make, making it the perfect go-to for a comforting meal. With the convenience of the Instant Pot, you can have this soul-soothing soup ready in no time, allowing you to spend more of your evening relaxing and enjoying the rich flavors of this classic dish.

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 medium onion, diced
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 carrots, peeled and chopped
  • 2 celery stalks, chopped
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried thyme
  • 1/4 teaspoon red pepper flakes
  • 2 tablespoons tomato paste
  • 2 cans (28 ounces each) whole tomatoes
  • 4 cups vegetable broth
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • Fresh basil, for garnish (optional)

Instructions

  1. Turn on the Instant Pot and select the “Saute” function. Heat the olive oil and add the diced onions. Saute for 3-4 minutes until the onions are soft and translucent.

  2. Add the minced garlic, chopped carrots, and celery to the Instant Pot. Stir and cook for another 3-4 minutes until the vegetables start to soften.

  3. Stir in the dried basil, oregano, thyme, and red pepper flakes, and cook for an additional minute to allow the flavors to bloom.

  4. Add the tomato paste to the pot and cook, stirring constantly, for 1-2 minutes to caramelize the tomato paste and enhance its flavor.

  5. Pour in the whole tomatoes, including their juices, and the vegetable broth. Season with salt and pepper to taste.

  6. Secure the lid on the Instant Pot and set the valve to the sealing position. Cancel the “Saute” function and select the “Soup” function, adjusting the time to 10 minutes.

  7. Once the cooking cycle is complete, allow for a natural pressure release for 10 minutes before carefully switching the valve to the venting position to release any remaining pressure.

  8. Open the lid and, using an immersion blender, blend the soup until smooth. Alternatively, carefully transfer the soup to a blender in batches and blend until smooth before returning it to the Instant Pot.

  9. Stir in the heavy cream until fully incorporated, creating a creamy and luxurious texture.

  10. Ladle the soup into bowls and garnish with fresh basil, if desired.

Why Instant Pot Tomato Soup?

The Instant Pot has revolutionized the way we cook, and it’s no different when it comes to making tomato soup. The pressure cooking function of the Instant Pot allows the flavors of the ingredients to meld together beautifully in a fraction of the time it would take on the stovetop. This means you can enjoy a rich and flavorful tomato soup in just a matter of minutes, perfect for those evenings when you crave a comforting yet convenient meal.

Tips for Serving and Storing

This Homemade Instant Pot Tomato Soup pairs wonderfully with a grilled cheese sandwich or a simple side salad for a well-rounded meal. If you have leftovers, store them in an airtight container in the refrigerator for up to 3 days. The flavors of the soup will continue to develop, making it even more delightful upon reheating.

In Conclusion

Homemade Instant Pot Tomato Soup is the ultimate comfort food for a cozy evening at home. Its rich, velvety texture and robust flavors make it a timeless classic that’s sure to warm both body and soul. With the simplicity of the Instant Pot, you can enjoy this delightful soup whenever the craving strikes, making it a staple recipe in your collection.

So, the next time you’re looking for a satisfying and heartwarming dish, give this Homemade Instant Pot Tomato Soup recipe a try. You’ll be amazed at how something so simple can bring so much joy to your evening.
