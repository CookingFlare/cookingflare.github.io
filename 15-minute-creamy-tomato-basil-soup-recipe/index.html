15-Minute Creamy Tomato Basil Soup Recipe


Are you craving a comforting bowl of soup but don’t have hours to spend in the kitchen? Look no further! This 15-Minute Creamy Tomato Basil Soup is the perfect solution for a quick, delicious, and satisfying meal. With simple ingredients and minimal prep time, you can enjoy a warm bowl of homemade soup without the hassle. Let’s dive into the recipe!

Ingredients:

  • 1 can (28 oz) whole peeled tomatoes
  • 1 cup chicken or vegetable broth
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • 1/4 cup fresh basil leaves, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Optional: grated Parmesan cheese for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the Base: In a large saucepan, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the chopped onion and minced garlic, and sauté until the onion becomes translucent and the garlic is fragrant.

  2. Simmer the Flavors: Pour the entire can of whole peeled tomatoes into the saucepan, breaking them up with a spoon. Stir in the chicken or vegetable broth, and bring the mixture to a gentle simmer. Let it cook for about 10 minutes, allowing the flavors to meld together.

  3. Blend and Cream: Using an immersion blender or a regular blender, carefully blend the soup until smooth. Return the blended mixture to the saucepan over low heat.

  4. Add the Creamy Touch: Stir in the heavy cream and chopped fresh basil. Let the soup simmer for an additional 2-3 minutes, allowing the basil to infuse its flavor into the soup. Season with salt and pepper to taste.

  5. Serve and Enjoy: Ladle the creamy tomato basil soup into bowls, and if desired, sprinkle with grated Parmesan cheese for an extra touch of richness. Serve hot with your favorite bread or a side salad.

Tips and Variations:

  • For a vegan-friendly version, simply substitute the heavy cream with coconut cream or a dairy-free alternative.
  • If you prefer a smoother texture, you can strain the soup through a fine-mesh sieve after blending to remove any remaining tomato seeds or skin.
  • Feel free to customize the soup with additional herbs and spices such as oregano, thyme, or a pinch of red pepper flakes for a hint of heat.

Final Thoughts:

This 15-Minute Creamy Tomato Basil Soup is a testament to the fact that a delicious homemade meal doesn’t have to be time-consuming. Whether you’re looking for a quick lunch, a cozy dinner, or a soul-soothing remedy for a chilly day, this recipe has got you covered. So, the next time you’re in need of a flavorful and comforting dish in a hurry, give this recipe a try and savor every spoonful of this delightful soup!

Enjoy the simplicity and richness of this creamy tomato basil soup, and let its vibrant flavors warm both your heart and your stomach. Happy cooking!
