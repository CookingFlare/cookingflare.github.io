Vegan Chocolate Peanut Butter Cupcakes Recipe with a Creamy Frosting


If you’re a fan of the rich, indulgent combination of chocolate and peanut butter, then you’re in for a treat with this vegan cupcake recipe. These moist and decadent chocolate cupcakes are infused with the irresistible flavor of peanut butter and topped with a luscious creamy frosting that will have you coming back for more. Whether you’re a seasoned vegan baker or just looking to try something new, these cupcakes are sure to impress.

Ingredients:

For the Cupcakes:

  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/3 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup unsweetened almond milk
  • 1/2 cup unsweetened applesauce
  • 1/3 cup creamy peanut butter
  • 1/4 cup vegetable oil
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 tablespoon apple cider vinegar

For the Frosting:

  • 1/2 cup creamy peanut butter
  • 1/4 cup vegan butter, softened
  • 2 cups powdered sugar
  • 2-3 tablespoons unsweetened almond milk
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Instructions:

For the Cupcakes:

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C) and line a muffin tin with paper liners.
  2. In a large mixing bowl, whisk together the flour, sugar, cocoa powder, baking soda, and salt until well combined.
  3. In a separate bowl, mix together the almond milk, applesauce, peanut butter, vegetable oil, and vanilla extract until smooth.
  4. Pour the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients and stir until just combined. Be careful not to overmix.
  5. Quickly stir in the apple cider vinegar, and then immediately divide the batter evenly among the prepared muffin cups.
  6. Bake for 18-20 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center of a cupcake comes out clean.
  7. Allow the cupcakes to cool in the tin for 5 minutes before transferring them to a wire rack to cool completely.

For the Frosting:

  1. In a mixing bowl, beat together the peanut butter and vegan butter until smooth and creamy.
  2. Gradually add the powdered sugar, alternating with the almond milk, and continue to beat until the frosting is smooth and fluffy.
  3. Mix in the vanilla extract until fully incorporated.

Assembly:

Once the cupcakes are completely cooled, generously frost each cupcake with the creamy peanut butter frosting. You can use a piping bag for a decorative swirl, or simply spread the frosting with a knife for a more rustic look. For an extra touch, sprinkle some chopped peanuts or chocolate shavings on top of the frosting.

These vegan chocolate peanut butter cupcakes are a delightful indulgence that’s perfect for any occasion. Whether you’re hosting a party, celebrating a special event, or just craving a sweet treat, these cupcakes are guaranteed to satisfy. Plus, they’re vegan-friendly, so you can feel good about enjoying every decadent bite.

So, gather your ingredients, preheat your oven, and get ready to whip up a batch of these irresistible vegan cupcakes. You’ll be amazed at how easy it is to create such a delectable dessert without any animal products. From the first bite to the last crumb, these chocolate peanut butter cupcakes are sure to become a new favorite in your recipe collection. Enjoy!
