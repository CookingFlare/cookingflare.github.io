Decadent Instant Pot Cheesecake Recipes to Indulge In


If you’re a fan of cheesecake, then you’re in for a treat! In this blog post, we’ll explore some decadent instant pot cheesecake recipes that are sure to satisfy your sweet tooth. The best part? These recipes are incredibly easy to make using your trusty instant pot. So, let’s dive right in and discover the world of indulgent cheesecake goodness!

Classic New York Style Cheesecake

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups graham cracker crumbs
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, melted
  • 32 oz cream cheese, softened
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 4 large eggs
  • 1 cup sour cream
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract

Instructions:

  1. In a medium bowl, combine the graham cracker crumbs and melted butter until well mixed. Press the mixture into the bottom of a 7-inch springform pan, creating an even crust.
  2. In a large mixing bowl, beat the cream cheese and sugar together until smooth and creamy. Add the eggs one at a time, beating well after each addition. Stir in the sour cream and vanilla extract.
  3. Pour the cream cheese mixture over the crust in the springform pan. Cover the pan with aluminum foil.
  4. Pour 1 cup of water into the instant pot and place the trivet inside. Carefully lower the springform pan onto the trivet.
  5. Close the instant pot lid and set the valve to the sealing position. Select the “Manual” or “Pressure Cook” function and set the timer for 35 minutes on high pressure.
  6. Once the cooking time is complete, allow the pressure to release naturally for 10 minutes. Then, manually release any remaining pressure.
  7. Carefully remove the cheesecake from the instant pot. Let it cool at room temperature for 1 hour, then refrigerate for at least 4 hours or overnight.
  8. Serve chilled and enjoy every creamy, indulgent bite!

Raspberry Swirl Cheesecake

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups graham cracker crumbs
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, melted
  • 32 oz cream cheese, softened
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 4 large eggs
  • 1 cup sour cream
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1/2 cup raspberry preserves

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the graham cracker crust as described in the previous recipe and press it into the bottom of a 7-inch springform pan.
  2. In a large mixing bowl, beat the cream cheese and sugar together until smooth and creamy. Add the eggs one at a time, beating well after each addition. Stir in the sour cream and vanilla extract.
  3. Pour half of the cream cheese mixture over the crust in the springform pan. Drop spoonfuls of raspberry preserves onto the cream cheese mixture, and then swirl it gently with a knife.
  4. Pour the remaining cream cheese mixture over the raspberry swirls. Again, drop spoonfuls of raspberry preserves on top and swirl gently with a knife.
  5. Follow steps 4 to 8 from the previous recipe to cook and cool the cheesecake.
  6. Get ready to experience the perfect balance of tangy creaminess and sweet raspberry goodness in every bite!

Chocolate Marble Cheesecake

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups chocolate cookie crumbs
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, melted
  • 32 oz cream cheese, softened
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 4 large eggs
  • 1 cup sour cream
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1/2 cup chocolate chips, melted

Instructions:

  1. In a medium bowl, combine the chocolate cookie crumbs and melted butter until well mixed. Press the mixture into the bottom of a 7-inch springform pan, creating an even crust.
  2. In a large mixing bowl, beat the cream cheese and sugar together until smooth and creamy. Add the eggs one at a time, beating well after each addition. Stir in the sour cream and vanilla extract.
  3. Pour half of the cream cheese mixture over the crust in the springform pan. Drizzle half of the melted chocolate over the cream cheese mixture.
  4. Repeat step 3 with the remaining cream cheese mixture and melted chocolate.
  5. Follow steps 4 to 8 from the first recipe to cook and cool the cheesecake.
  6. Get ready to indulge in a heavenly combination of rich chocolate and creamy cheesecake that will satisfy all your dessert cravings!

Conclusion

There you have it – three delectable instant pot cheesecake recipes that will take your dessert game to a whole new level. Whether you prefer the classic New York style, the fruity kick of raspberry swirls, or the decadent allure of chocolate marble, these recipes are sure to impress. So, grab your instant pot and get ready to indulge in creamy, dreamy cheesecake perfection. Happy baking!
