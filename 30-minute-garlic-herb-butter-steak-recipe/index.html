30-Minute Garlic Herb Butter Steak Recipe


Are you craving a delicious and juicy steak but don’t have a lot of time to spare? Look no further! This 30-minute garlic herb butter steak recipe is the answer to your prayers. With a perfect blend of flavors and a quick cooking time, this recipe is sure to become a favorite in your household.

Ingredients:

  • 2 New York strip steaks (or any cut of your preference)
  • 4 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons fresh parsley, finely chopped
  • 1 tablespoon fresh thyme leaves
  • 1 tablespoon fresh rosemary, finely chopped
  • 4 tablespoons unsalted butter
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Olive oil

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the Pan: Place a large skillet over medium-high heat and add a drizzle of olive oil. Allow the skillet to heat up while you prepare the steaks.

  2. Season the Steaks: Pat the steaks dry with a paper towel to remove any excess moisture. Season both sides of the steaks generously with salt and pepper.

  3. Cook the Steaks: Once the skillet is hot, carefully place the steaks in the pan. For medium-rare, cook the steaks for about 3-4 minutes on each side. Adjust the cooking time based on your desired level of doneness.

  4. Prepare the Garlic Herb Butter: While the steaks are cooking, melt the butter in a small saucepan over medium heat. Add the minced garlic and cook for 1-2 minutes until fragrant. Stir in the chopped parsley, thyme, and rosemary. Remove the saucepan from the heat.

  5. Baste the Steaks: As the steaks near your desired level of doneness, spoon the garlic herb butter over the steaks. Baste the steaks continuously for the last minute of cooking to infuse them with the rich flavors of the butter and herbs.

  6. Rest the Steaks: Once the steaks are done, transfer them to a cutting board and allow them to rest for a few minutes. This will ensure that the juices redistribute throughout the meat, resulting in a tender and juicy steak.

  7. Serve and Enjoy: After resting, slice the steaks against the grain and serve them with your favorite sides. Drizzle any remaining garlic herb butter over the steaks for an extra burst of flavor.

Why This Recipe Works:

This 30-minute garlic herb butter steak recipe is a testament to the fact that you don’t need hours to create a mouthwatering dish. The combination of searing the steaks in a hot skillet and then basting them with the fragrant garlic herb butter elevates the flavor and juiciness of the meat.

The garlic adds a robust savory note, while the fresh herbs bring a burst of freshness to every bite. The butter ties everything together, creating a luscious and indulgent experience. The quick cooking time ensures that you can whip up this steak recipe on a busy weeknight or whenever the craving strikes.

So, the next time you’re short on time but still want a gourmet meal, give this 30-minute garlic herb butter steak recipe a try. It’s a simple yet elegant dish that will leave you and your loved ones wanting more. Happy cooking!
