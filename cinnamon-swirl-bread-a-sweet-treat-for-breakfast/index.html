Cinnamon Swirl Bread: A Sweet Treat for Breakfast


Are you looking to add a delightful twist to your morning routine? Look no further than this irresistible Cinnamon Swirl Bread recipe! This sweet treat is the perfect way to start your day, with its warm, comforting aroma and mouthwatering flavor. Whether enjoyed fresh out of the oven or toasted with a generous slather of butter, this homemade bread is sure to become a cherished favorite. Let’s dive into the delicious world of Cinnamon Swirl Bread and discover how to create this delightful masterpiece in your own kitchen.

The Magic of Cinnamon Swirl Bread

Cinnamon Swirl Bread is a timeless classic that never fails to impress. The combination of tender, buttery bread and a luscious swirl of cinnamon sugar creates a symphony of flavors and textures that is simply irresistible. The aroma of cinnamon wafting through your kitchen as this bread bakes is enough to make anyone’s mouth water.

Ingredients:

  • 3 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 2 1/4 teaspoons instant yeast
  • 1 cup warm milk
  • 1/4 cup unsalted butter, melted
  • 1 large egg
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 2 tablespoons ground cinnamon
  • 2 tablespoons unsalted butter, softened

Instructions:

  1. In a large mixing bowl, combine 3 cups of the flour, 1/4 cup sugar, salt, and yeast.
  2. Add the warm milk, melted butter, and egg to the dry ingredients. Mix until a soft dough forms.
  3. Turn the dough out onto a lightly floured surface and knead for 6-8 minutes, adding more flour as needed, until the dough is smooth and elastic.
  4. Place the dough in a greased bowl, cover with a clean kitchen towel, and let it rise in a warm place for about 1 hour, or until doubled in size.
  5. In a small bowl, mix together 1/2 cup sugar and ground cinnamon for the filling.
  6. Punch down the risen dough and roll it out into a 9x18-inch rectangle.
  7. Spread the softened butter over the dough, then sprinkle the cinnamon sugar mixture evenly over the butter.
  8. Starting from one of the short ends, tightly roll up the dough into a log.
  9. Place the rolled dough into a greased 9x5-inch loaf pan, cover it, and let it rise for another 30-45 minutes.
  10. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C).
  11. Once the dough has risen, bake the bread for 30-35 minutes, or until golden brown.
  12. Remove the bread from the oven and let it cool in the pan for 10 minutes before transferring it to a wire rack to cool completely.

Serving Suggestions

This delectable Cinnamon Swirl Bread is a treat all on its own, but it can also be enjoyed in a variety of ways. Here are a few serving suggestions to enhance your breakfast experience:

  • Toasted Delight: Slice the bread and toast it until golden brown. Enjoy it with a spread of cream cheese or a dollop of honey for an extra touch of sweetness.
  • French Toast: Elevate your brunch game by using slices of Cinnamon Swirl Bread to make indulgent French toast. Top it with fresh berries and a drizzle of maple syrup for a truly decadent meal.
  • Bread Pudding: Transform leftover Cinnamon Swirl Bread into a luxurious bread pudding. Simply cube the bread, soak it in a rich custard, and bake to perfection.

The Perfect Start to Your Day

With its enchanting aroma and heavenly taste, Cinnamon Swirl Bread is a delightful addition to any breakfast or brunch table. Whether you’re enjoying a leisurely weekend morning or need a comforting treat to brighten up a weekday, this sweet bread is sure to bring joy to your kitchen. So, roll up your sleeves, gather your ingredients, and embark on a culinary adventure to create your very own batch of Cinnamon Swirl Bread. Your mornings will never be the same again!
