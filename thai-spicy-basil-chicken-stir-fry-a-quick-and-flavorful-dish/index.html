Thai Spicy Basil Chicken Stir-Fry: A Quick and Flavorful Dish


If you’re craving a taste of Thailand right in your own kitchen, look no further than this Thai Spicy Basil Chicken Stir-Fry recipe. This dish is a perfect blend of spicy, savory, and aromatic flavors that will transport your taste buds to the bustling streets of Bangkok. Best of all, it’s a quick and easy dish that you can whip up in no time, making it an ideal choice for busy weeknights or lazy weekends.

Ingredients:

  • 500g boneless, skinless chicken thighs, thinly sliced
  • 2 tablespoons oyster sauce
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon fish sauce
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 shallots, thinly sliced
  • 1-3 Thai bird’s eye chilies, finely chopped (adjust according to your spice preference)
  • 1 cup fresh basil leaves
  • Freshly ground black pepper

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the Sauce: In a small bowl, mix together the oyster sauce, soy sauce, fish sauce, and sugar until well combined. Set aside.

  2. Stir-Fry the Chicken: Heat the vegetable oil in a wok or large skillet over high heat. Add the minced garlic and shallots, and stir-fry for about 30 seconds until fragrant.

  3. Add the Chicken: Add the sliced chicken to the wok and stir-fry for 2-3 minutes or until the chicken is no longer pink.

  4. Incorporate the Sauce: Pour the prepared sauce over the chicken, and stir-fry for another 1-2 minutes, ensuring that the chicken is evenly coated with the sauce.

  5. Spice it Up: Add the chopped Thai bird’s eye chilies to the wok and stir-fry for an additional 1 minute. The aroma of the chilies will infuse the dish with a delightful heat.

  6. Finishing Touch: Toss in the fresh basil leaves and stir-fry for another 30 seconds, allowing the basil to wilt slightly. Season with freshly ground black pepper to taste.

  7. Serve and Enjoy: Transfer the Thai Spicy Basil Chicken Stir-Fry to a serving dish and serve it hot with steamed jasmine rice.

Tips and Tricks:

  • For an extra kick of heat, you can increase the amount of Thai bird’s eye chilies or add a sprinkle of dried chili flakes.
  • If you’re not a fan of chicken, you can easily substitute it with thinly sliced beef, pork, or even tofu for a vegetarian option.
  • Ensure that all the ingredients are prepped and ready to go before you start cooking, as stir-frying requires quick and continuous action.

The Flavors of Thailand in Every Bite

This Thai Spicy Basil Chicken Stir-Fry is a celebration of bold flavors and vibrant aromas that are synonymous with Thai cuisine. The combination of fragrant basil, fiery chilies, and savory sauces creates a symphony of tastes that will leave you craving more. Whether you’re a spice enthusiast or someone looking to explore the world of Thai cooking, this dish is sure to impress your taste buds and leave you wanting to recreate the magic again and again.

So, the next time you’re in the mood for a quick and flavorful meal that packs a punch, whip up this Thai Spicy Basil Chicken Stir-Fry and embark on a culinary journey to the heart of Thailand. From the sizzle of the wok to the first glorious bite, every moment of preparing and savoring this dish is an experience to be cherished.
