Satisfying Philly Cheesesteak Stuffed Mushrooms: A Low-Carb Appetizer


If you’re looking for a delicious and satisfying low-carb appetizer that’s bursting with flavor, then you’re in for a treat! These Philly Cheesesteak Stuffed Mushrooms are the perfect combination of savory, cheesy, and oh-so-satisfying. Whether you’re hosting a party or simply craving a mouthwatering snack, these stuffed mushrooms are sure to impress your taste buds and leave you wanting more.

Ingredients:

  • 24 large white mushrooms, stems removed
  • 1 pound thinly sliced steak (such as ribeye or sirloin), diced into small pieces
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 1 green bell pepper, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon Worcestershire sauce
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 cup shredded provolone cheese
  • 1/2 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Fresh parsley for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C) and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.

  2. Place the mushroom caps on the prepared baking sheet, gill side up.

  3. In a large skillet, heat the olive oil over medium-high heat. Add the diced steak and cook until browned, then remove the steak from the skillet and set it aside.

  4. In the same skillet, add the chopped onion, green bell pepper, and minced garlic. Sauté until the vegetables are tender and fragrant.

  5. Return the cooked steak to the skillet and stir in the Worcestershire sauce, salt, and pepper. Cook for an additional 2-3 minutes, allowing the flavors to meld together.

  6. Spoon the steak and vegetable mixture into each mushroom cap, filling them generously.

  7. Sprinkle the provolone and mozzarella cheese over the stuffed mushrooms, ensuring each one is generously topped with cheese.

  8. Bake the stuffed mushrooms in the preheated oven for 18-20 minutes, or until the cheese is melted and bubbly, and the mushrooms are tender.

  9. Remove the stuffed mushrooms from the oven and garnish with fresh parsley for a pop of color and flavor.

  10. Serve the Philly Cheesesteak Stuffed Mushrooms warm and watch them disappear in no time!

Why You’ll Love These Stuffed Mushrooms:

These Philly Cheesesteak Stuffed Mushrooms are a delightful twist on the classic cheesesteak sandwich. By using mushrooms as the base instead of bread, this recipe transforms a beloved comfort food into a low-carb, keto-friendly appetizer that’s perfect for anyone watching their carbohydrate intake.

The juicy steak, sautéed vegetables, and gooey melted cheese create a flavor explosion in every bite, making these stuffed mushrooms a crowd-pleaser at any gathering. Plus, they are easy to prepare and can be made ahead of time, making them a convenient option for entertaining.

Whether you’re a fan of traditional Philly cheesesteak or simply love the idea of a hearty, flavorful appetizer that won’t weigh you down, these stuffed mushrooms are sure to become a new favorite in your recipe collection.

So, the next time you’re craving a satisfying and indulgent appetizer without the guilt, give these Philly Cheesesteak Stuffed Mushrooms a try. Your taste buds will thank you!
