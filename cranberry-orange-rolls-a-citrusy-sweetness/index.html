Cranberry Orange Rolls: A Citrusy Sweetness


Cranberry Orange Rolls are a scrumptious treat that combines the tartness of cranberries and the sweet, zesty flavor of oranges. These tender, flaky rolls are filled with a delicious cranberry-orange concoction, making them the perfect addition to your breakfast, brunch, or dessert table.

Ingredients:

For the Dough:

  • 1 cup warm water (110°F)
  • 2 1/4 teaspoons active dry yeast
  • 1 tablespoon granulated sugar
  • 3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1 teaspoon salt

For the Filling:

  • 1 cup fresh cranberries
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 1/2 cup orange juice
  • 1 tablespoon grated orange zest
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon

For the Glaze:

  • 1 cup powdered sugar
  • 2-3 tablespoons orange juice

Instructions:

First, let’s prepare the dough. In a bowl, combine the warm water and yeast. Add the sugar and let it sit for about 5 minutes or until the yeast becomes frothy. In another bowl, combine the flour, salt, and vegetable oil. Slowly add the yeast mixture to the flour and mix until a soft, sticky dough forms.

Cover the dough and let it rise in a warm, draft-free place for about 1-2 hours or until it doubles in size.

Now that our dough has risen, let’s prepare the filling. In a saucepan, combine the cranberries, sugar, water, orange juice, orange zest, and cinnamon. Cook over medium heat for about 10 minutes or until the cranberries burst open and the mixture thickens into a slightly chunky, sweet-tart sauce.

Once the filling is complete, drain the excess liquid, and set it aside to cool slightly.

Now, we’ll roll out our dough. On a lightly floured surface, roll the dough into a large rectangle. Spread the cooled filling evenly over the top of the dough, leaving about 1/2-inch border on one of the longest sides.

Starting at the other long side, roll up the dough tightly, and pinch the edges to seal. With a sharp knife or a thin piece of wire, slice the log into 12 to 15 equal portions.

At this point, you can choose to either lay the rolls side by side in a greased 9x13-inch baking dish or place them in a circle around the perimeter of the dish leaving an empty center space, with a more aesthetically pleasing arrangement.

Cover the rolls and let them rise again in a warm place for about 30 minutes.

Finally, it’s time to bake our rolls. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C). In a small bowl, mix the egg with 1 tablespoon of water to create an egg wash. Brush this egg wash over the tops of the rolls. Bake them in the oven for 20-25 minutes or until the rolls are golden brown and sound hollow when tapped.

While the rolls bake, make the glaze. In a bowl, whisk together the powdered sugar and orange juice until smooth.

Once the rolls are finished baking, remove them from the oven and let them cool slightly. Drizzle the glaze over the warm rolls and enjoy!

Thank you for checking out our recipe for Cranberry Orange Rolls. These delightful treats are sure to brighten up any occasion with their citrusy sweetness. Happy baking!
