10 Easy Sandwich Recipes for Quick Weeknight Dinners


Weeknight dinners can be a challenge. After a long day at work, school, or managing your household, the thought of preparing a complicated meal is often daunting. That’s where sandwiches come in. A quick, versatile, and satisfying solution for busy evenings, sandwiches can be adapted to your tastes and dietary needs, all while being a breeze to prepare.

Whether you’re craving something classic like a hearty grilled cheese or looking for a more creative twist with international flavors, there’s a sandwich for everyone. In this post, we’ll explore 10 easy sandwich recipes that make perfect quick weeknight dinners. These sandwiches are simple to make, require minimal ingredients, and deliver delicious results. So, let’s dive into these savory, satisfying meals!

1. Classic Grilled Cheese Sandwich

Let’s start with a timeless favorite— the classic grilled cheese sandwich. This recipe is all about simplicity, and that’s what makes it so perfect for a quick weeknight dinner.

Ingredients:

  • 2 slices of your favorite bread (white, whole wheat, or sourdough)
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 2 slices of cheddar cheese (or your preferred cheese)

Instructions:

  1. Butter the Bread: Spread butter evenly on one side of each slice of bread.
  2. Assemble the Sandwich: Place one slice of cheese between the two slices of bread, buttered side out.
  3. Grill: Heat a non-stick pan over medium heat. Place the sandwich in the pan and cook for 2-3 minutes on each side, or until golden brown and the cheese has melted.
  4. Serve: Slice and serve with a side of soup or some crispy pickles.

This classic never gets old and offers a perfect balance of crunchy, buttery bread with gooey, melted cheese.

2. Turkey and Avocado Club

For a heartier sandwich with a bit more flair, the turkey and avocado club is a great choice. With lean turkey breast, creamy avocado, and crisp lettuce, it’s a satisfying meal that comes together quickly.

Ingredients:

  • 3 slices of whole wheat or white bread
  • 4 slices of turkey breast
  • 1/2 avocado, sliced
  • 2 slices of tomato
  • 2-3 leaves of lettuce
  • Mayonnaise or mustard (optional)
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Toast the Bread: Toast the bread slices lightly in a toaster or on a pan.
  2. Assemble the Sandwich: Spread mayo or mustard on one slice of toast. Layer turkey slices, avocado, tomato, and lettuce.
  3. Top with the Second Slice: Place the second slice of toast on top, then layer with more turkey, avocado, and a sprinkle of salt and pepper.
  4. Add the Third Slice: Top off with the final slice of toast to complete the club.
  5. Serve: Cut into halves or quarters and enjoy with your favorite chips or a side salad.

The combination of lean turkey, creamy avocado, and crunchy veggies makes this sandwich both nutritious and tasty.

3. Veggie and Hummus Sandwich

This vegetarian option is fresh, flavorful, and packed with nutrients. It’s perfect for those looking for a lighter, plant-based meal that’s also quick and easy to prepare.

Ingredients:

  • 2 slices of whole-grain bread
  • 2 tablespoons of hummus (store-bought or homemade)
  • 1/2 cucumber, thinly sliced
  • 1/2 bell pepper, thinly sliced
  • 1/2 avocado, mashed
  • Handful of spinach leaves
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Spread the Hummus: Spread a generous amount of hummus on one slice of bread.
  2. Assemble the Veggies: Layer cucumber, bell pepper, spinach, and mashed avocado on top.
  3. Season: Add a pinch of salt and pepper to enhance the flavors.
  4. Top with the Second Slice: Place the other slice of bread on top to finish the sandwich.
  5. Serve: Cut the sandwich in half and enjoy a fresh and healthy meal.

Packed with fiber, healthy fats, and fresh veggies, this sandwich is a great option for a quick, light dinner.

4. BBQ Chicken Sandwich

For a more indulgent option, a BBQ chicken sandwich is the way to go. With tender chicken breast, tangy BBQ sauce, and a touch of cheese, this sandwich will satisfy your cravings for something rich and flavorful.

Ingredients:

  • 2 slices of sandwich bun or soft roll
  • 1 cooked chicken breast, shredded
  • 2 tablespoons of BBQ sauce
  • 1 slice of cheddar or mozzarella cheese
  • Coleslaw (optional)
  • Pickles (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Shred the Chicken: Shred the cooked chicken breast using a fork or your hands.
  2. Mix with BBQ Sauce: Toss the shredded chicken in BBQ sauce until well-coated.
  3. Assemble the Sandwich: Place the BBQ chicken on the bottom half of the bun. Top with a slice of cheese and a dollop of coleslaw or pickles, if desired.
  4. Top with the Bun: Place the top half of the bun on the sandwich.
  5. Serve: Serve with a side of fries or roasted veggies for a complete meal.

This sandwich is full of smoky, sweet flavors that are perfect for a quick and satisfying dinner.

5. Caprese Sandwich

Inspired by the classic Caprese salad, this sandwich is light, fresh, and packed with flavor. The combination of mozzarella, tomatoes, and basil with a balsamic glaze is a simple yet delicious option for a weeknight meal.

Ingredients:

  • 2 slices of ciabatta or baguette
  • Fresh mozzarella, sliced
  • 2-3 slices of ripe tomato
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • Balsamic glaze (optional)
  • Olive oil (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Assemble the Sandwich: Place the fresh mozzarella, tomato slices, and basil leaves on one slice of bread.
  2. Add Oil and Glaze: Drizzle a little olive oil and balsamic glaze for extra flavor.
  3. Top with the Second Slice: Place the second slice of bread on top.
  4. Serve: Slice the sandwich in half and enjoy the refreshing flavors of this Mediterranean-inspired meal.

The combination of creamy mozzarella, juicy tomatoes, and aromatic basil is light yet satisfying.

6. Tuna Salad Sandwich

A tuna salad sandwich is an easy and comforting choice for dinner. With canned tuna, mayo, and crunchy veggies, it’s a filling option that you can throw together in minutes.

Ingredients:

  • 2 slices of whole-grain bread
  • 1 can of tuna, drained
  • 2 tablespoons mayonnaise
  • 1 tablespoon relish or diced pickles (optional)
  • 1/4 red onion, thinly sliced
  • Lettuce leaves

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the Tuna Salad: In a bowl, combine the tuna, mayo, relish or pickles (if using), and red onion.
  2. Assemble the Sandwich: Spread the tuna salad onto one slice of bread.
  3. Add Lettuce: Layer a few fresh lettuce leaves on top of the tuna salad.
  4. Top with the Second Slice: Place the second slice of bread on top.
  5. Serve: Cut the sandwich in half and enjoy with a pickle on the side.

This simple sandwich packs protein and healthy fats, making it a great quick dinner option.

7. Chicken Caesar Sandwich

Caesar salads are a classic, and they make for an excellent sandwich filling. The creamy Caesar dressing paired with tender chicken and crisp romaine creates a deliciously satisfying meal.

Ingredients:

  • 2 slices of sandwich bread or a soft roll
  • 1 cooked chicken breast, sliced
  • Romaine lettuce
  • 2 tablespoons Caesar dressing
  • Grated Parmesan cheese

Instructions:

  1. Assemble the Sandwich: Layer sliced chicken breast on the bottom half of the bread.
  2. Add Romaine and Dressing: Top the chicken with a few leaves of romaine lettuce and drizzle Caesar dressing over the top.
  3. Finish with Parmesan: Sprinkle grated Parmesan cheese over the lettuce.
  4. Top with the Other Slice: Place the other slice of bread on top to finish the sandwich.
  5. Serve: Cut and serve with a side of crunchy chips.

With all the flavors of a Caesar salad in sandwich form, this one is sure to be a favorite.

8. Steak and Cheese Sandwich

For a filling, hearty dinner, the steak and cheese sandwich is a fantastic option. Tender strips of steak, melted cheese, and sautéed onions create a flavorful sandwich that’s perfect for a quick meal.

Ingredients:

  • 2 slices of hoagie roll
  • 1/2 pound of steak (flank steak or sirloin works well)
  • 1/2 onion, thinly sliced
  • 2 slices of provolone or mozzarella cheese
  • Olive oil
  • Salt and pepper

Instructions:

  1. Cook the Steak: Season the steak with salt and pepper and cook in a skillet over medium-high heat for 3-4 minutes per side. Let it rest for a few minutes, then slice it thinly.
  2. Sauté the Onions: In the same skillet, sauté the onions in a bit of olive oil until softened and caramelized.
  3. Assemble the Sandwich: Place the steak slices on the bottom half of the hoagie roll, followed by the sautéed onions and cheese.
  4. Melt the Cheese: Place the sandwich under a broiler or in a pan to melt the cheese.
  5. Serve: Once the cheese has melted, serve immediately for a hearty dinner.

The steak and cheese sandwich offers a savory and indulgent meal that’s packed with flavor.

9. BLT (Bacon, Lettuce, and Tomato)

The BLT is another classic that never goes out of style. Crispy bacon, fresh lettuce, and juicy tomatoes come together in this simple yet satisfying sandwich.

Ingredients:

  • 2 slices of bread (white, whole wheat, or toasted)
  • 4 slices of bacon
  • 2-3 slices of tomato
  • Lettuce leaves
  • Mayonnaise

Instructions:

  1. Cook the Bacon: Cook the bacon in a skillet until crispy, then set aside on paper towels to drain excess fat.
  2. Assemble the Sandwich: Spread mayonnaise on one side of each slice of bread. Layer the bacon, lettuce, and tomato on one slice.
  3. Top and Serve: Place the second slice of bread on top, and slice the sandwich in half for easy eating.

This sandwich is a quick, satisfying choice with the perfect balance of flavors.

10. Breakfast Sandwich

Who says breakfast sandwiches are just for mornings? This quick and hearty option is perfect for a weeknight dinner, especially if you’re craving a little breakfast-for-dinner vibe.

Ingredients:

  • 1 English muffin or croissant
  • 1 egg
  • 1 slice of cheese
  • 2 slices of cooked bacon or sausage patty
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Cook the Egg: In a small pan, cook the egg sunny side up or scrambled, depending on your preference.
  2. Toast the Muffin or Croissant: While the egg cooks, toast your English muffin or croissant.
  3. Assemble the Sandwich: Place the egg, cheese, and bacon or sausage on the bottom half of the muffin or croissant.
  4. Top with the Other Half: Place the top half of the muffin or croissant to complete the sandwich.
  5. Serve: Cut and enjoy with a side of fruit or hash browns.

This breakfast sandwich is a filling, protein-packed option that will satisfy you all night long.

There you have it—10 easy sandwich recipes that are perfect for quick weeknight dinners. From classic favorites like grilled cheese to more inventive options like the chicken Caesar sandwich, there’s something for everyone. These sandwiches are simple, satisfying, and easy to customize based on what you have in your pantry. The next time you’re in need of a fast, delicious meal, reach for one of these sandwich recipes for a stress-free dinner!
