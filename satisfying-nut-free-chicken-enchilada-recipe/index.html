Satisfying Nut-Free Chicken Enchilada Recipe

3 min · 583 words

If you’re looking for a delicious and satisfying nut-free meal, look no further than this flavorful chicken enchilada recipe. Packed with savory shredded chicken, zesty enchilada sauce, and gooey melted cheese, these enchiladas are sure to become a family favorite. Whether you’re cooking for a weeknight dinner or entertaining guests, this nut-free chicken enchilada recipe is a surefire crowd-pleaser.

Ingredients

For the Enchiladas:

  • 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 small onion, diced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1 (14-ounce) can red enchilada sauce
  • 8-10 corn tortillas
  • 2 cups shredded Monterey Jack cheese
  • Fresh cilantro, for garnish

For the Garnish:

  • Fresh cilantro
  • Sour cream
  • Sliced jalapeños
  • Diced tomatoes

Instructions

Step 1: Cook the Chicken

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. In a large skillet, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the diced onion and cook until softened, about 5 minutes.
  3. Add the minced garlic, chili powder, cumin, oregano, salt, and pepper to the skillet. Cook for an additional 1-2 minutes, until fragrant.
  4. Add the chicken breasts to the skillet and pour in 1/2 cup of the enchilada sauce. Cover the skillet and simmer for 15-20 minutes, or until the chicken is cooked through.
  5. Once the chicken is cooked, transfer it to a plate and shred it using two forks.

Step 2: Assemble the Enchiladas

  1. Pour 1/4 cup of the enchilada sauce into the bottom of a 9x13-inch baking dish, spreading it out evenly.
  2. Warm the corn tortillas in the microwave for 30 seconds to make them more pliable.
  3. Spoon a small amount of the shredded chicken onto each tortilla, then sprinkle with a little shredded cheese. Roll up the tortillas and place them seam-side down in the baking dish.
  4. Once all the enchiladas are assembled, pour the remaining enchilada sauce over the top. Sprinkle the remaining shredded cheese over the enchiladas.

Step 3: Bake and Serve

  1. Cover the baking dish with aluminum foil and bake in the preheated oven for 20 minutes.
  2. Remove the foil and bake for an additional 5-10 minutes, or until the cheese is bubbly and lightly browned.
  3. Garnish the enchiladas with fresh cilantro and serve with sour cream, sliced jalapeños, and diced tomatoes on the side.

Conclusion

This nut-free chicken enchilada recipe is a fantastic way to enjoy the rich flavors of traditional Mexican cuisine without the use of nuts. The combination of tender shredded chicken, zesty enchilada sauce, and gooey melted cheese makes for a truly satisfying meal. Whether you’re catering to nut allergies or simply looking for a delicious dinner idea, these enchiladas are sure to hit the spot. So, gather your ingredients and get ready to savor the delightful flavors of these nut-free chicken enchiladas!
