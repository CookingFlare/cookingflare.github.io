Deliciously Creamy Coconut Rice Pudding Recipe


If you’re a fan of creamy, comforting desserts with a tropical twist, then you’re in for a treat with this deliciously creamy coconut rice pudding recipe. This dessert is a delightful fusion of silky coconut milk, aromatic vanilla, and tender rice, resulting in a lusciously creamy and indulgent treat that’s perfect for any occasion. Whether you’re hosting a dinner party or simply craving a cozy dessert at home, this recipe is sure to impress and satisfy your sweet tooth.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of jasmine rice
  • 1 can (13.5 oz) of coconut milk
  • 2 cups of whole milk
  • 1/2 cup of granulated sugar
  • 1 teaspoon of vanilla extract
  • 1/4 teaspoon of salt
  • Ground cinnamon for garnish
  • Toasted coconut flakes for garnish

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Rice: In a medium saucepan, combine the jasmine rice, coconut milk, whole milk, and salt. Bring the mixture to a gentle boil over medium heat, stirring occasionally to prevent the rice from sticking to the bottom of the pan.

  2. Simmer and Stir: Once the mixture reaches a gentle boil, reduce the heat to low and let the rice simmer for 30-35 minutes, stirring occasionally to ensure that the rice cooks evenly and absorbs the creamy liquid.

  3. Sweeten the Pudding: After the rice has cooked and the mixture has thickened to a creamy consistency, stir in the granulated sugar and vanilla extract. Continue to cook the pudding for an additional 5-10 minutes, allowing the flavors to meld together while maintaining the creamy texture.

  4. Serve and Garnish: Once the pudding has reached your desired consistency, remove it from the heat and let it cool slightly. Spoon the creamy coconut rice pudding into individual serving bowls and sprinkle with a dash of ground cinnamon and toasted coconut flakes for a delightful crunch and added visual appeal.

  5. Chill and Enjoy: For an extra indulgent experience, refrigerate the pudding for a few hours to serve it chilled. The cool, creamy texture paired with the subtle sweetness of coconut and vanilla creates a truly heavenly dessert that’s perfect for warm-weather gatherings or a refreshing treat any time of the year.

Tips and Variations

  • For a vegan-friendly version, substitute the whole milk with coconut milk or almond milk for a dairy-free alternative.
  • Experiment with different spices such as cardamom or nutmeg to infuse the pudding with aromatic flavors.
  • Add a tropical twist by incorporating diced mango or pineapple for a burst of fruity sweetness.

Conclusion

With its rich, velvety texture and delightful coconut flavor, this creamy coconut rice pudding is a must-try dessert that will transport your taste buds to a tropical paradise. Whether you enjoy it warm or chilled, this indulgent treat is guaranteed to become a favorite in your dessert repertoire. So, gather your ingredients, embrace the aromas of coconut and vanilla, and treat yourself to a spoonful of pure bliss with this delectable coconut rice pudding. Cheers to creating sweet memories and savoring every delightful bite!
