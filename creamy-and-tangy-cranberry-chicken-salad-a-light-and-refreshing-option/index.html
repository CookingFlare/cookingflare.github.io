Creamy and Tangy Cranberry Chicken Salad: A Light and Refreshing Option


Are you looking for a light and refreshing salad that is bursting with flavor? Look no further than this Creamy and Tangy Cranberry Chicken Salad! With a combination of tender chicken, tangy cranberries, and a creamy dressing, this salad is sure to become a favorite in your household.

Ingredients

To make this delicious salad, you will need the following ingredients:

  • 2 cups cooked and shredded chicken breast
  • 1 cup dried cranberries
  • 1/2 cup chopped celery
  • 1/2 cup chopped walnuts
  • 1/4 cup diced red onion
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley
  • 1/2 cup mayonnaise
  • 1/4 cup Greek yogurt
  • 2 tablespoons Dijon mustard
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. In a large bowl, combine the shredded chicken, dried cranberries, celery, walnuts, red onion, and parsley. Toss gently to mix the ingredients evenly.

  2. In a separate bowl, whisk together the mayonnaise, Greek yogurt, Dijon mustard, lemon juice, salt, and pepper. This creamy dressing will add a tangy and refreshing flavor to the salad.

  3. Pour the dressing over the chicken mixture and toss until everything is well coated. Make sure all the ingredients are evenly distributed.

  4. Cover the bowl with plastic wrap and refrigerate for at least 30 minutes to allow the flavors to meld together. This step is crucial for achieving the perfect balance of flavors in the salad.

  5. After the salad has chilled, give it a taste and adjust the seasonings if necessary. You can add more salt, pepper, or lemon juice to suit your personal preference.

  6. Serve the Creamy and Tangy Cranberry Chicken Salad on a bed of fresh lettuce or as a sandwich filling. It’s versatile and can be enjoyed in different ways.

Tips and Variations

  • For an extra burst of flavor, you can add some crumbled feta cheese to the salad. The creamy and tangy cheese pairs perfectly with the cranberries and chicken.

  • If you prefer a healthier option, you can substitute Greek yogurt for the mayonnaise or use a combination of both. This will reduce the calorie content and add a tangy twist to the dressing.

  • Feel free to experiment with different nuts in this salad. Pecans or almonds would work well in place of walnuts, adding a different texture and flavor profile.

  • To make this salad vegetarian-friendly, you can substitute the chicken with cooked chickpeas or tofu. The tangy dressing and cranberries will still provide a burst of flavor to the dish.

  • This salad is perfect for meal prep. You can make a large batch and store it in an airtight container in the refrigerator for up to 3 days. It makes for a quick and convenient lunch option during busy weekdays.

Conclusion

Creamy and Tangy Cranberry Chicken Salad is a light and refreshing option that combines the flavors of tender chicken, tangy cranberries, and a creamy dressing. It’s a versatile dish that can be enjoyed as a salad or as a sandwich filling. With its vibrant flavors and easy preparation, this salad is sure to become a favorite in your household. Give it a try and let your taste buds be delighted!
