Don't Skip Brunch! Creative Breakfast Sandwich ideas for On-the-Go


Often underestimated, breakfast is the most important meal of the day. We can easily forget its significance when we’re rushing out the door. One solution is to make our morning meal portable and convenient. Enter the scrumptious breakfast sandwich. In this guide, we’ll uncover creative, delectable flavors for your breakfast sandwich repertoire.

Meet Your New Favorite Breakfast Duo: Avocado and Grapefruit

First up, a dynamic duo that will brighten your morning: avocado and grapefruit. The creaminess of avocado contrasts wonderfully with the tangy, refreshing grapefruit. How to make it:

  1. Hash-brown potatoes or potato cakes
  2. Grilled, seasoned avocado
  3. Fresh grapefruit slices
  4. Sliced Swiss cheese
  5. Cooked, scrambled eggs or egg substitute
  6. Whole grain toast

These elements meld together for a heavenly, nutritious start to your day!

Sweetify Your Morning with PB&B: Peanut Butter, Banana, and Bacon

Next, try a cult-favorite: peanut butter, banana, and bacon (PB&B). This sweet-and-savory combination is inspired by the classic Elvis Presley sandwich. Can’t decide between sweet or savory? Indulge in both! Recipe:

  1. Crispy bacon strips
  2. Soft, sliced banana
  3. Creamy peanut butter
  4. Sliced Colby-Jack cheese
  5. Scrambled eggs or egg substitute
  6. Whole grain or sourdough toast

It’s a king’s feast in a sandwich. Enjoy!

Surf’s Up: Surf and Turf Breakfast Sandwich

Thirdly, let’s ride the waves of flavor with our “Surf and Turf” sandwich. The history of this classic meal finds its roots in hormone-injected cattle grazing on the western range. Our version opts for plant-based proteins and incorporates California inspiration with the flavors of “surfin’ surf and turf.” Directions:

  1. Grilled Portobello mushrooms
  2. Pan-seared tofu or plant-based burgers
  3. Fresh spinach
  4. Grilled onion and red bell pepper
  5. Sliced Monterey Jack cheese
  6. Cooked eggs or egg substitute
  7. Whole grain rolls

This vegan delight is sure to impress and energize your day.

Make It a Meal: Get Creative with Egg Substitutes

Finally, not everyone revels in the idea of consuming eggs. Fear not! Multiple egg substitutes can bring joy and flavor to your breakfast sandwich. Some options include:

  • Nutritional yeast: A vegan, delectable, cheesy experience!
  • Miso: Provides umami depth and highlights flavors.
  • Nut or seed butters: Almond, sunflower seed, or peanut butter lends additional textures.
  • Tofu or tempeh: Protein-rich alternatives that absorb flavors.

Remember, every cook should feel free to experiment with ingredients. Think beyond the traditional and rejoice in the joy of creating.

Conclusion

In a world constantly on the move, starting your day with a breakfast sandwich packed with flavor just makes sense. From unexpected combos like avocado and grapefruit to the beloved PB&B, there’s something to satisfy everyone’s taste buds. Harness the power of bread to transform routine mornings into nutritious, flavorful adventures. Bon appétit!
