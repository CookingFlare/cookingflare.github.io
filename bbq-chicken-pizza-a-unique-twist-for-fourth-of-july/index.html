BBQ Chicken Pizza: A Unique Twist for Fourth of July


The Fourth of July is a time for celebration, and what better way to mark the occasion than with a delicious BBQ chicken pizza? This unique twist on a classic favorite combines the smoky flavors of barbecue with the comfort of a cheesy, crispy pizza crust. Whether you’re hosting a backyard gathering or simply looking to treat yourself to a special meal, this BBQ chicken pizza recipe is sure to impress.

Ingredients

For the Pizza Dough:

  • 2 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup warm water
  • 1 tablespoon active dry yeast
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil

For the BBQ Chicken Topping:

  • 1 cup cooked, shredded chicken
  • 1/2 cup barbecue sauce
  • 1/4 cup red onion, thinly sliced
  • 1 cup mozzarella cheese, shredded
  • 1/4 cup fresh cilantro, chopped
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Pizza Dough:

    • In a large bowl, combine the warm water, sugar, and yeast. Let it sit for 5-10 minutes until foamy.
    • Add the flour, salt, and olive oil to the yeast mixture and stir until a dough forms.
    • Knead the dough on a floured surface for about 5 minutes, until smooth and elastic. Place the dough in a greased bowl, cover it with a clean kitchen towel, and let it rise for 1-2 hours, or until doubled in size.

  2. Preheat the Oven:

    • Preheat the oven to 475°F (245°C). If you have a pizza stone, place it in the oven to heat up.

  3. Prepare the BBQ Chicken Topping:

    • In a small bowl, mix the shredded chicken with the barbecue sauce until well coated.

  4. Assemble the Pizza:

    • Once the dough has risen, punch it down and roll it out on a floured surface to your desired thickness.
    • Transfer the rolled-out dough to a baking sheet or pizza peel if using a pizza stone.
    • Spread the barbecue chicken mixture evenly over the dough, leaving a small border around the edges.
    • Sprinkle the sliced red onions over the chicken, followed by the shredded mozzarella cheese.

  5. Bake the Pizza:

    • Carefully transfer the assembled pizza to the preheated oven or pizza stone.
    • Bake for 12-15 minutes, or until the crust is golden brown and the cheese is bubbly and melted.

  6. Finish and Serve:

    • Remove the pizza from the oven and sprinkle the chopped cilantro over the top.
    • Let the pizza cool for a few minutes before slicing and serving.

Tips and Variations

  • For an extra kick of flavor, consider adding sliced jalapeños or red pepper flakes to the pizza.
  • If you prefer a thinner crust, you can divide the dough in half and make two smaller pizzas.
  • This recipe can also be adapted to a grilled pizza by cooking it on a preheated grill instead of in the oven.

Conclusion

This BBQ chicken pizza is a delicious and unique way to celebrate the Fourth of July with friends and family. The combination of tangy barbecue sauce, tender chicken, and gooey cheese atop a crispy crust is a crowd-pleaser that will have everyone coming back for more. Whether you’re firing up the grill or heating up the oven, this recipe is a must-try for your holiday festivities. Enjoy and happy Fourth of July!
