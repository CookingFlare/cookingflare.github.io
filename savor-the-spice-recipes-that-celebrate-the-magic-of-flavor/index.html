The Power of Spices

From the humble cinnamon to the fiery cayenne pepper, each spice brings its own unique flavor profile and health benefits to the table.

Recipe 1: Fragrant Indian Curry

Ingredients:

  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon ginger, grated
  • 2 teaspoons garam masala
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • 1 teaspoon coriander
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1 teaspoon chili powder (adjust to taste)
  • 400g diced tomatoes
  • 400ml coconut milk
  • 500g chicken breast, cubed
  • Salt, to taste
  • Fresh cilantro, for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Heat the vegetable oil in a large pan over medium heat. Add the chopped onion and cook until golden brown.
  2. Add the minced garlic and grated ginger to the pan and cook for another minute until fragrant.
  3. In a small bowl, mix together the garam masala, turmeric, cumin, coriander, paprika, and chili powder.
  4. Add the spice mixture to the pan and cook for a minute to release the flavors.
  5. Stir in the diced tomatoes and coconut milk. Bring the mixture to a simmer.
  6. Add the cubed chicken breast to the pan and season with salt. Cook for 15-20 minutes, or until the chicken is cooked through and tender.
  7. Serve the fragrant Indian curry over steamed rice, garnished with fresh cilantro.

Recipe 2: Moroccan Tagine with Apricots and Almonds

Ingredients:

  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 teaspoons ground cumin
  • 2 teaspoons ground coriander
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric
  • 500g lamb shoulder, cubed
  • 400g canned chickpeas, rinsed and drained
  • 200g dried apricots
  • 100g blanched almonds
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • Fresh parsley, for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Heat the olive oil in a large pot over medium heat. Add the chopped onion and cook until softened and translucent.
  2. Stir in the minced garlic, ground cumin, ground coriander, ground cinnamon, paprika, and turmeric. Cook for a minute until fragrant.
  3. Add the cubed lamb shoulder to the pot and brown on all sides.
  4. Pour in enough water to cover the lamb, then bring to a simmer. Reduce the heat to low, cover, and let it cook for 1.5-2 hours, or until the lamb is tender.
  5. Add the rinsed and drained chickpeas, dried apricots, and blanched almonds to the pot. Season with salt and pepper.
  6. Cover the pot and continue cooking for another 20-30 minutes, until the flavors have melded together.
  7. Serve the Moroccan tagine with apricots and almonds over couscous or with crusty bread. Garnish with fresh parsley.

Recipe 3: Thai Green Curry with Vegetables

Ingredients:

  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 2 tablespoons green curry paste
  • 1 can (400ml) coconut milk
  • 1 cup vegetable broth
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon brown sugar
  • 1 tablespoon lime juice
  • 1 cup mixed vegetables (such as bell peppers, snow peas, and carrots)
  • 200g tofu, cubed
  • Fresh basil leaves, for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Heat the vegetable oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Add the green curry paste and cook for a minute until fragrant.
  2. Pour in the coconut milk and vegetable broth. Stir in the soy sauce, brown sugar, and lime juice. Bring the mixture to a simmer.
  3. Add the mixed vegetables and tofu to the skillet. Cook for 5-7 minutes, or until the vegetables are tender and the tofu is heated through.
  4. Serve the Thai green curry with vegetables over steamed jasmine rice. Garnish with fresh basil leaves.
