One-Pot Creamy Chicken Alfredo: A Simple and Delicious Pasta Recipe


If you’re a fan of creamy, comforting pasta dishes but don’t want to deal with multiple pots and pans, then this One-Pot Creamy Chicken Alfredo recipe is perfect for you. It’s a simple and delicious dish that brings together tender chicken, al dente fettuccine, and a rich, creamy sauce in just one pot. This recipe is a lifesaver for busy weeknights when you crave a satisfying meal without the hassle of extensive cleanup. Let’s dive into the details of creating this mouthwatering One-Pot Creamy Chicken Alfredo!

Ingredients

  • 1 pound boneless, skinless chicken breast, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 1/2 cups chicken broth
  • 2 1/2 cups whole milk
  • 8 ounces fettuccine pasta
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1 cup freshly grated Parmesan cheese
  • Fresh parsley, chopped, for garnish

Instructions

  1. Sear the Chicken: In a large pot or deep skillet, heat the olive oil over medium-high heat. Add the chicken pieces and cook until they are golden brown on all sides, about 5-6 minutes. Remove the chicken from the pot and set it aside.

  2. Prepare the Sauce: In the same pot, add the minced garlic and sauté for about 1 minute until fragrant. Pour in the chicken broth and whole milk, then add the uncooked fettuccine, salt, and pepper. Stir to combine.

  3. Simmer: Bring the mixture to a gentle boil, then reduce the heat to medium-low. Let the pasta simmer, uncovered, for about 15-20 minutes, stirring occasionally to prevent sticking, until the pasta is tender and the sauce has thickened.

  4. Add the Chicken and Cheese: Return the cooked chicken to the pot and stir it into the creamy pasta. Sprinkle the grated Parmesan cheese over the top and gently fold it into the pasta until the cheese is melted and the sauce is smooth and creamy.

  5. Serve: Once the chicken is heated through and the sauce is velvety, remove the pot from the heat. Garnish with freshly chopped parsley for a pop of color and freshness.

Tips and Variations

  • For added flavor, consider incorporating a pinch of nutmeg or a dash of red pepper flakes into the sauce.
  • If you prefer a thicker sauce, let the pasta simmer for a few extra minutes until it reaches your desired consistency.
  • To make this dish vegetarian, simply omit the chicken and use vegetable broth instead of chicken broth.

Final Thoughts

This One-Pot Creamy Chicken Alfredo is a fantastic way to indulge in a classic Italian favorite without the hassle of multiple dishes to clean up. The combination of tender chicken, creamy sauce, and perfectly cooked pasta makes this recipe a winner for both family dinners and entertaining guests. Whether you’re a novice cook or a seasoned pro, this simple and delicious pasta dish is sure to become a regular feature in your recipe repertoire. So, grab a pot, gather your ingredients, and treat yourself to the comforting goodness of One-Pot Creamy Chicken Alfredo tonight!
