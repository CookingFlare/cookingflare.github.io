Best Cheese Options for Tacos and Burritos


Tacos and burritos are two of the most beloved Mexican dishes enjoyed worldwide, and one of the secrets to making them truly exceptional is the cheese you choose. Whether you’re preparing a quick weeknight dinner or planning a festive taco night, the right cheese can elevate the flavor and texture of your tacos and burritos. In this guide, we’ll explore the best cheese options to enhance your favorite Mexican meals.

Why Cheese is Essential in Tacos and Burritos

Cheese adds richness, creaminess, and a savory depth to tacos and burritos. Its melting qualities help bind the various ingredients together, creating a delightful fusion of flavors in each bite. The best cheeses for tacos and burritos provide a balance of texture, flavor, and meltability, ensuring your dish is satisfying and delicious.

When considering which cheese to use, think about the other components of your taco or burrito, such as meats, vegetables, salsas, and sauces. Different cheeses complement different ingredients, so it’s essential to match the cheese to the overall flavor profile of your dish.

Let’s dive into some of the best cheese options that will make your tacos and burritos shine.

1. Cheddar Cheese

Cheddar cheese is a classic choice for both tacos and burritos. It’s widely available, easy to work with, and offers a sharp, tangy flavor that pairs well with beef, chicken, and even vegetarian fillings. The firm texture of cheddar cheese allows it to hold up well when melted, adding both creaminess and structure to your tacos or burritos.

Best Pairings:

  • Ground beef or steak tacos
  • Shredded chicken burritos
  • Beef and bean burritos
  • Vegetarian tacos with black beans and avocado

How to Use:

Grate or shred the cheddar cheese and sprinkle it generously on top of your taco or burrito filling. For an extra melty effect, consider heating the cheese under a broiler or in a hot skillet for a few seconds to get that gooey, golden finish.

2. Monterey Jack Cheese

Monterey Jack cheese is another fantastic cheese option for tacos and burritos. Known for its smooth, mild flavor and excellent melting properties, Monterey Jack offers a subtle creaminess that enhances the other ingredients without overwhelming them. It’s perfect for those who prefer a more neutral cheese that complements rather than competes with the bold flavors of your fillings.

Best Pairings:

  • Grilled chicken tacos
  • Pork carnitas burritos
  • Shrimp tacos
  • Veggie-filled burritos

How to Use:

Monterey Jack cheese works well when shredded or sliced. It melts effortlessly, making it a great choice for quesadillas and for melting over tacos and burritos. For added richness, you can mix it with other cheeses like cheddar or pepper jack.

3. Pepper Jack Cheese

For those who enjoy a bit of heat in their tacos or burritos, pepper jack cheese is an excellent option. This semi-soft cheese is infused with spicy peppers, such as jalapeños, giving it a zesty kick that pairs perfectly with a variety of meats, especially those with bold flavors like barbacoa or chorizo. The spiciness of the cheese adds an exciting layer of flavor that enhances the overall experience of your dish.

Best Pairings:

  • Chorizo tacos
  • Barbacoa burritos
  • Grilled steak tacos with jalapeños and salsa
  • Spicy shrimp burritos

How to Use:

Shred the pepper jack cheese and sprinkle it over your taco or burrito before serving. If you want to boost the flavor, consider lightly toasting the cheese in a skillet to bring out its spicy nuances.

4. Queso Fresco

Queso fresco is a soft, crumbly Mexican cheese that is widely used in traditional taco and burrito recipes. With its mild, slightly tangy flavor, queso fresco adds a fresh and light contrast to heavier fillings like carne asada or carnitas. It’s often used as a finishing touch, crumbled over the top of tacos and burritos, and adds a nice texture without being overly rich.

Best Pairings:

  • Carne asada tacos
  • Pork carnitas burritos
  • Fish tacos
  • Veggie tacos with cilantro and lime

How to Use:

Queso fresco is typically crumbled over the top of tacos or burritos as a garnish. Its light, crumbly texture provides a pleasant contrast to the other ingredients, adding freshness to each bite.

5. Cotija Cheese

Cotija cheese is often referred to as the “Parmesan of Mexico.” This crumbly, salty cheese is perfect for adding a savory depth of flavor to tacos and burritos. Unlike cheeses that melt, cotija retains its texture when sprinkled over a dish, making it an excellent finishing cheese. Its sharp, salty taste is ideal for pairing with rich and fatty fillings, cutting through the heaviness with its bold flavor.

Best Pairings:

  • Grilled corn tacos
  • Beef burritos
  • Tacos with spicy salsa
  • Mexican street corn (elote)

How to Use:

Sprinkle cotija cheese over the top of your tacos or burritos just before serving. A little goes a long way due to its intense flavor, so be sure to use it sparingly for the best effect.

6. Mexican Blend Cheese

A Mexican blend cheese typically combines shredded Monterey Jack, cheddar, and other varieties like asadero or queso blanco. This pre-shredded cheese mix offers the best of both worlds, combining the creamy meltiness of Monterey Jack with the sharpness of cheddar. It’s a versatile option that works well with almost any taco or burrito filling, providing the perfect amount of flavor and meltability.

Best Pairings:

  • Beef tacos
  • Chicken burritos
  • Ground turkey or veggie tacos
  • Fajita-style burritos with peppers and onions

How to Use:

Since Mexican blend cheese is pre-shredded, it’s incredibly convenient for taco or burrito preparation. Simply sprinkle it on top of your fillings and let it melt into the other ingredients for a rich, cheesy experience.

7. Asadero Cheese

Asadero cheese is a soft, semi-mild Mexican cheese that’s perfect for melting. It has a creamy texture and a slightly tangy flavor that works well with many taco and burrito fillings. Asadero is often used in quesadillas, but it’s also a great choice for tacos, where its smooth melt and mild flavor can enhance the other ingredients without overpowering them.

Best Pairings:

  • Chicken or beef tacos
  • Cheese-stuffed burritos
  • Grilled vegetables with melted cheese
  • Cheese quesadillas

How to Use:

Asadero cheese is ideal for melting. For the best results, shred it and use it to fill your burrito or top your taco before grilling or broiling to get a gooey, melted cheese effect.

8. Oaxaca Cheese

Oaxaca cheese is a stringy, semi-soft cheese that’s perfect for melting and stretching. It has a mild, buttery flavor that makes it an excellent addition to both tacos and burritos. Oaxaca is similar to mozzarella in texture, but it has a slightly richer flavor profile, making it ideal for pairing with savory, seasoned fillings like carne asada or grilled chicken.

Best Pairings:

  • Carne asada tacos
  • Grilled chicken burritos
  • Breakfast burritos with eggs and bacon
  • Veggie burritos

How to Use:

Oaxaca cheese can be torn into strips or shredded and melted over your tacos or burritos. Its stretchy, gooey texture makes it a delightful cheese to use in dishes where you want the cheese to pull apart and melt seamlessly into your fillings.

Conclusion: Choosing the Best Cheese for Your Tacos and Burritos

The best cheese for your tacos and burritos ultimately depends on your personal preferences and the other ingredients you’re working with. For a classic, sharp flavor, cheddar is always a safe bet, while milder options like Monterey Jack and Oaxaca provide a creamy, smooth experience. If you like a bit of heat, pepper jack will add the right amount of spice to your dishes.

Cheeses like queso fresco and cotija offer freshness and saltiness that work beautifully with meats like carne asada, carnitas, and barbacoa. Meanwhile, Mexican blend cheese provides a versatile mix that’s perfect for any filling. By selecting the right cheese and pairing it with complementary ingredients, you’ll create tacos and burritos that are bursting with flavor and texture.

Next time you’re assembling your tacos or burritos, consider these cheese options and experiment with different combinations to find your perfect cheesy match. Enjoy the deliciously melty, creamy, and flavorful result that will have everyone coming back for seconds!
