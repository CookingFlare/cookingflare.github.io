Instant Pot Chicken Recipes: Juicy and Flavorful Dishes


Cooking chicken in an Instant Pot is a game-changer for busy home cooks. The pressure cooking method not only saves time but also locks in moisture and flavor, resulting in juicy and tender chicken dishes that your family will love. In this blog post, we will explore a variety of Instant Pot chicken recipes that are not only easy to prepare but also bursting with flavor. Whether you’re looking for a quick weeknight dinner or a dish to impress your guests, these recipes will have you covered.

Why Choose Instant Pot for Chicken?

The Instant Pot is a versatile kitchen appliance that combines the functions of a pressure cooker, slow cooker, rice cooker, steamer, sauté pan, and even a yogurt maker. Here are a few reasons why cooking chicken in an Instant Pot is a fantastic choice:

  1. Speed: The pressure cooking feature significantly reduces cooking time. What would typically take hours can be done in a fraction of the time.
  2. Flavor: The sealed environment of the Instant Pot traps steam and flavors, resulting in moist and flavorful chicken.
  3. Versatility: You can prepare a wide range of chicken dishes, from soups and stews to curries and casseroles.
  4. Ease of Use: With just a few buttons to press, you can set your cooking time and let the Instant Pot do the work for you.

Now, let’s dive into some delicious Instant Pot chicken recipes that will elevate your dinner game.

1. Instant Pot Garlic Parmesan Chicken

Ingredients

  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup chicken broth
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 teaspoon Italian seasoning
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley for garnish

Instructions

  1. Sauté the Chicken: Set your Instant Pot to the sauté function. Add olive oil and let it heat up. Season the chicken breasts with salt and pepper, then add them to the pot. Sauté for about 3-4 minutes on each side until golden brown. Remove the chicken and set aside.

  2. Prepare the Sauce: In the same pot, add minced garlic and sauté for about 30 seconds until fragrant. Pour in the chicken broth and scrape the bottom of the pot to deglaze, ensuring no bits are stuck.

  3. Add Cream and Cheese: Stir in the heavy cream, Parmesan cheese, and Italian seasoning. Mix well until the cheese is melted and the sauce is smooth.

  4. Pressure Cook: Return the chicken to the pot, ensuring it is submerged in the sauce. Close the lid, set the valve to sealing, and cook on high pressure for 10 minutes.

  5. Release Pressure: Once the cooking time is up, allow for a natural pressure release for 5 minutes, then switch to a quick release to let out any remaining steam.

  6. Serve: Remove the chicken from the pot and slice it. Serve it drizzled with the creamy garlic Parmesan sauce and garnish with fresh parsley.

Tips

  • Serve this dish over pasta or rice to soak up the delicious sauce.
  • You can add vegetables like spinach or broccoli to the sauce for added nutrition.

2. Instant Pot Chicken Tacos

Ingredients

  • 2 pounds boneless, skinless chicken thighs
  • 1 packet taco seasoning
  • 1 cup chicken broth
  • 1 cup salsa
  • Tortillas for serving
  • Toppings: shredded cheese, lettuce, diced tomatoes, avocado, sour cream

Instructions

  1. Combine Ingredients: Place the chicken thighs in the Instant Pot. Sprinkle the taco seasoning over the chicken, then pour in the chicken broth and salsa.

  2. Pressure Cook: Close the lid, set the valve to sealing, and cook on high pressure for 15 minutes.

  3. Release Pressure: After the cooking time is complete, perform a quick release.

  4. Shred the Chicken: Remove the chicken from the pot and shred it using two forks. Return the shredded chicken to the pot and mix it with the sauce.

  5. Serve: Warm the tortillas and fill them with the shredded chicken. Top with your favorite toppings and enjoy!

Tips

  • For a spicier kick, add jalapeños or hot sauce to the chicken mixture.
  • You can also use this chicken for burrito bowls or salads.
