Butternut Squash and Kale Salad: A Fresh Fall Option


As the leaves start to turn and the air becomes crisp, it’s the perfect time to embrace the flavors of fall with a refreshing and nutritious Butternut Squash and Kale Salad. This vibrant and hearty salad is a celebration of seasonal produce, bringing together the earthy sweetness of butternut squash with the robust and slightly bitter notes of kale. With a burst of color and a medley of flavors, this salad is not only a feast for the senses but also a nourishing addition to your fall menu.

Ingredients:

For the Salad:

  • 1 medium butternut squash, peeled, seeded, and cut into cubes
  • 1 bunch of kale, stems removed and leaves torn into bite-sized pieces
  • 1/2 cup of pecans, toasted and roughly chopped
  • 1/4 cup of dried cranberries
  • 1/4 cup of crumbled feta cheese

For the Dressing:

  • 1/4 cup of extra virgin olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons of apple cider vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon of honey
  • 1 teaspoon of Dijon mustard
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven: Preheat the oven to 400°F (200°C).

  2. Roast the Butternut Squash: On a baking sheet, toss the cubed butternut squash with a drizzle of olive oil, salt, and pepper. Roast in the preheated oven for 25-30 minutes, or until the squash is tender and lightly caramelized.

  3. Prepare the Kale: While the butternut squash is roasting, place the torn kale leaves in a large bowl. Drizzle with a little olive oil and massage the leaves for a few minutes until they soften and become more vibrant in color.

  4. Make the Dressing: In a small bowl, whisk together the extra virgin olive oil, apple cider vinegar, honey, Dijon mustard, salt, and pepper until well combined.

  5. Assemble the Salad: Once the butternut squash is roasted, allow it to cool slightly. Add the roasted butternut squash, toasted pecans, dried cranberries, and crumbled feta cheese to the bowl of kale. Drizzle the dressing over the salad and toss gently to combine.

  6. Serve and Enjoy: Transfer the salad to a serving platter or individual plates. Garnish with an extra sprinkle of pecans and feta cheese if desired. Serve the salad immediately and relish the delightful combination of flavors.

Why You’ll Love This Salad:

Nutritious and Satisfying:

The butternut squash provides a rich source of vitamin A and fiber, while the kale is packed with vitamins K, A, and C. Together, they create a nutrient-dense salad that is both satisfying and wholesome.

Burst of Fall Flavors:

The warmth of the roasted butternut squash, the nuttiness of the pecans, and the sweetness of the cranberries come together to create a symphony of fall flavors in every bite.

Versatile and Adaptable:

This salad serves as a perfect standalone meal or a delightful side dish for your fall gatherings. You can also customize it by adding grilled chicken, quinoa, or even a drizzle of balsamic glaze for an extra layer of flavor.

Simple and Wholesome Ingredients:

With simple and wholesome ingredients, this salad is a testament to the beauty of seasonal produce and the joy of creating nourishing meals from scratch.

Final Thoughts:

Embrace the essence of fall with this Butternut Squash and Kale Salad. Whether you’re looking for a vibrant addition to your autumn table or a nourishing meal to savor, this salad is sure to captivate your palate and nourish your body. So, gather your ingredients, celebrate the flavors of the season, and indulge in the wholesome goodness of this delightful fall salad.
