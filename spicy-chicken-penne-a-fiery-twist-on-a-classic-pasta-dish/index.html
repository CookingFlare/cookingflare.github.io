Spicy Chicken Penne: A Fiery Twist on a Classic Pasta Dish


Are you tired of the same old pasta dishes? Looking for a way to spice up your dinner routine? Look no further than this mouthwatering recipe for Spicy Chicken Penne! With its fiery twist on a classic pasta dish, this recipe is sure to bring some excitement to your dinner table. So, grab your apron and get ready to embark on a culinary adventure!

Ingredients

  • 1 pound of penne pasta
  • 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 2 tablespoons of olive oil
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 red bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 green bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 can of diced tomatoes
  • 1 teaspoon of dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon of dried basil
  • 1 teaspoon of crushed red pepper flakes (adjust according to your spice preference)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley, chopped (for garnish)

Instructions

  1. Cook the penne pasta according to package instructions until al dente. Drain and set aside.

  2. In a large skillet, heat the olive oil over medium-high heat. Add the chicken pieces and cook until browned and cooked through, about 5-7 minutes. Remove the chicken from the skillet and set aside.

  3. In the same skillet, add the diced onion and minced garlic. Sauté until the onion becomes translucent and fragrant, about 2-3 minutes.

  4. Add the sliced red and green bell peppers to the skillet and cook until they start to soften, about 5 minutes.

  5. Pour in the can of diced tomatoes, followed by the dried oregano, dried basil, and crushed red pepper flakes. Stir well to combine the flavors. Season with salt and pepper to taste.

  6. Return the cooked chicken to the skillet and stir to coat it with the spicy tomato sauce. Reduce the heat to low and let the flavors simmer together for 10-15 minutes, allowing the sauce to thicken slightly.

  7. Add the cooked penne pasta to the skillet and toss it with the spicy chicken and sauce until everything is well combined.

  8. Serve the Spicy Chicken Penne hot, garnished with fresh parsley for an added touch of freshness.

Tips and Tricks

  • If you prefer a milder spice level, you can reduce the amount of crushed red pepper flakes or omit them altogether. Adjust the seasoning according to your taste.
  • Feel free to experiment with different types of pasta shapes. Fusilli or rigatoni would work well with this dish, too.
  • For an extra kick of flavor, you can sprinkle some grated Parmesan cheese on top of the dish before serving.
  • This recipe can easily be doubled or halved to accommodate your serving needs.

Conclusion

Spicy Chicken Penne is the perfect dish for those who crave a little heat in their meals. The combination of tender chicken, flavorful spices, and al dente pasta creates a harmonious balance of flavors and textures. Whether you’re cooking for a weeknight dinner or entertaining guests, this fiery twist on a classic pasta dish is sure to impress. So, why settle for ordinary when you can indulge in the extraordinary? Give this recipe a try and let your taste buds dance with delight!
