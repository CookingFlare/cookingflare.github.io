Savor the Flavor with Dairy-Free Beef and Broccoli Stir-Fry


Are you looking for a delicious and healthy meal that’s packed with flavor? Look no further than this dairy-free beef and broccoli stir-fry recipe! With tender beef, crisp broccoli, and a savory sauce, this dish is sure to satisfy your taste buds. Plus, it’s quick and easy to make, making it perfect for busy weeknights. Let’s dive right into the recipe!

Ingredients

  • 1 pound beef, thinly sliced
  • 2 cups broccoli florets
  • 1 red bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 onion, sliced
  • 3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce (use gluten-free soy sauce if needed)
  • 1 tablespoon sesame oil
  • 1 tablespoon cornstarch (optional, for thickening the sauce)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Cooked rice or noodles for serving

Instructions

  1. In a large skillet or wok, heat the sesame oil over medium-high heat. Add the sliced beef and cook until browned, about 2-3 minutes per side. Remove the beef from the skillet and set it aside.

  2. In the same skillet, add the minced garlic and sauté for about 1 minute until fragrant. Add the sliced onion and red bell pepper and cook for another 2-3 minutes until they begin to soften.

  3. Add the broccoli florets to the skillet and cook for an additional 2-3 minutes until they turn bright green and are tender-crisp.

  4. In a small bowl, whisk together the soy sauce and cornstarch (if using) until smooth. Pour the mixture over the vegetables in the skillet and stir well to coat.

  5. Return the cooked beef to the skillet and stir-fry for another 2-3 minutes until everything is well combined and heated through. Season with salt and pepper to taste.

  6. Serve the dairy-free beef and broccoli stir-fry over cooked rice or noodles. Enjoy the flavors and savor each bite!

Tips and Variations

  • For a spicier version, add some red pepper flakes or a dash of sriracha sauce to the stir-fry sauce.
  • Feel free to add other vegetables like carrots, snap peas, or mushrooms to the dish for added color and nutrients.
  • If you prefer a thicker sauce, dissolve an additional teaspoon of cornstarch in water and stir it into the sauce during step 4.
  • For a complete meal, serve the stir-fry with a side of steamed edamame or a fresh green salad.

Why Dairy-Free?

By opting for a dairy-free stir-fry, you can enjoy all the wonderful flavors without any of the potential digestive discomfort that dairy can cause for some individuals. The combination of beef, broccoli, and the delicious stir-fry sauce creates a satisfying and wholesome meal that is suitable for those with lactose intolerance or a dairy-free lifestyle.

Conclusion

This dairy-free beef and broccoli stir-fry recipe is a fantastic way to savor the flavors of a classic Asian dish without the need for dairy products. With its tender beef, crisp broccoli, and mouthwatering sauce, this stir-fry is a crowd-pleaser that can be enjoyed by everyone. So, next time you’re craving a quick and delicious meal, give this recipe a try and savor the flavor!
