Mediterranean-Inspired Grilled Vegetable Recipes


If you’re in the mood for a light and flavorful meal, look no further than these Mediterranean-inspired grilled vegetable recipes. Bursting with vibrant colors, bold flavors, and healthy ingredients, these recipes are perfect for those seeking a delicious and wholesome dish. Whether you’re a vegetarian, vegan, or simply looking to incorporate more vegetables into your diet, these recipes are sure to satisfy your cravings. So fire up the grill and get ready to indulge in these mouthwatering creations!

Grilled Eggplant and Zucchini Skewers

Preparation Time: 10 minutes / Cooking Time: 15 minutes / Serves: 4

Ingredients:

  • 1 large eggplant, cut into 1-inch cubes
  • 2 zucchinis, sliced into ½-inch rounds
  • 1 red bell pepper, cut into 1-inch pieces
  • 1 yellow bell pepper, cut into 1-inch pieces
  • 1 red onion, cut into 1-inch pieces
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Juice of 1 lemon
  • Fresh parsley, chopped (for garnish)

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the grill to medium-high heat.
  2. In a large bowl, combine the eggplant, zucchinis, bell peppers, red onion, olive oil, garlic, dried oregano, salt, and pepper. Toss until the vegetables are evenly coated.
  3. Thread the vegetables onto skewers, alternating between the different vegetables.
  4. Place the skewers on the grill and cook for about 10-15 minutes, turning occasionally, until the vegetables are tender and slightly charred.
  5. Remove the skewers from the grill and drizzle them with the lemon juice.
  6. Garnish with freshly chopped parsley and serve hot.

Grilled Portobello Mushroom Burger

Preparation Time: 15 minutes / Cooking Time: 10 minutes / Serves: 2

Ingredients:

  • 2 large portobello mushrooms
  • 2 tablespoons balsamic vinegar
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 whole wheat burger buns
  • Toppings of your choice (lettuce, tomato, red onion, avocado, etc.)

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the grill to medium heat.
  2. In a small bowl, whisk together the balsamic vinegar, olive oil, garlic, salt, and pepper.
  3. Place the portobello mushrooms in a shallow dish and pour the marinade over them. Let them marinate for about 10 minutes, flipping them halfway through.
  4. Place the mushrooms on the grill, gill side down, and cook for about 5 minutes.
  5. Flip the mushrooms and continue grilling for another 5 minutes, or until they are tender and juicy.
  6. Remove the mushrooms from the grill and assemble your burger with the bun and desired toppings.

Grilled Mediterranean Vegetable Salad

Preparation Time: 20 minutes / Cooking Time: 10 minutes / Serves: 4

Ingredients:

  • 1 large red bell pepper
  • 1 large yellow bell pepper
  • 1 large zucchini, sliced lengthwise into ½-inch-thick strips
  • 1 large eggplant, sliced lengthwise into ½-inch-thick strips
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • ½ cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • ½ cup kalamata olives, pitted and halved
  • ½ cup crumbled feta cheese
  • Fresh basil leaves, torn (for garnish)

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the grill to medium-high heat.
  2. Brush the bell peppers, zucchini, and eggplant slices with olive oil and sprinkle them with salt and pepper.
  3. Place the vegetables on the grill and cook for about 4-5 minutes per side, or until they are tender and have grill marks.
  4. Remove the vegetables from the grill and let them cool slightly.
  5. Once cooled, chop the grilled vegetables into bite-sized pieces and transfer them to a large bowl.
  6. Add the cherry tomatoes, kalamata olives, and crumbled feta cheese to the bowl and gently toss everything together.
  7. Garnish with fresh torn basil leaves and serve at room temperature.

Conclusion

These Mediterranean-inspired grilled vegetable recipes are not only delicious but also a great way to incorporate more veggies into your diet. From the smoky flavors of the grilled eggplant and zucchini skewers to the hearty and satisfying grilled portobello mushroom burger, and the refreshing grilled Mediterranean vegetable salad, these recipes offer a variety of options for every palate. So, fire up your grill and savor the tastes of the Mediterranean this summer!
