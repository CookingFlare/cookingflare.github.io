Vegan Nutripots: 20 Hearty, Nutritious, & Eco-Friendly Plant-Based Meals for Kids


Are you craving for nutritious meals that are not only delicious but also cause no harm to our planet? Look no further! Introducing Vegan Nutripots – easy-to-assemble plant-based meals perfect for your little ones. Embrace a wholesome lifestyle by incorporating these guilt-free recipes in your family’s meal rotation. Dive in and learn how to cook 20 fantastic Vegan Nutripots that will delight both you and your children.

1. Quinoa Pudding with Banana & Cinnamon

A creamy dessert that’s packed with protein and nutrients.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup cooked quinoa
  • 1 ripe banana, mashed
  • 2 tablespoons almond milk
  • 1 teaspoon cinnamon
  • A handful of chopped nuts (optional)

Instructions: Mix all ingredients in a bowl until evenly combined. Serve chilled, garnished with nuts if desired.

2. Sweet Potato & Chickpea Fritters

A scrumptious spin on classic fritters, promising both flavor and nutrition.

Ingredients:

  • 1 large sweet potato, cooked and mashed
  • 1 can chickpeas, drained and rinsed
  • 1/2 cup whole wheat flour
  • 1 small onion, chopped
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 1 egg
  • Spices (salt, pepper, paprika)
  • Oil for frying

Instructions: Combine all ingredients in a bowl, except oil, and mix until a dough forms. Divide the mixture into patties, dip in the flour, and fry on both sides until golden brown. Serve with a side of ketchup or tahini sauce.

3. Tofu Scramble with Bell Peppers

A protein-packed breakfast alternative to eggs.

Ingredients:

  • 1 block of firm tofu
  • 1 bell pepper, chopped
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 tablespoons nutritional yeast
  • Spices (turmeric, salt, pepper)
  • 1 tablespoon oil

Instructions: Crumble the tofu and sauté it in oil with chopped onion and bell pepper until tender. Stir in the remaining ingredients and cook until well-combined. Serve with a side of whole grain toast.

4. Vegalish Stir-fry

A swap that tastes just like the original but is completely plant-based.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup of veggies (carrots, broccoli, snap peas)
  • 1/2 cup cooked rice
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons cornstarch
  • 1 tablespoon oil
  • Spices (garlic, ginger, red pepper flakes)

Instructions: In a bowl, whisk together soy sauce and cornstarch. Set aside. Heat oil in a pan, add veggies and cook until tender. Add the sauce mixture, stir well, and cook until the sauce thickens. Mix in cooked rice and serve.

5. Chickpea Curry with Vegetable Dumplings

A hearty dish that combines robust flavors with wholesome goodness.

Ingredients: Chickpea Curry:

  • 1 can chickpeas, drained & rinsed
  • 1 can diced tomatoes
  • 2 cups vegetable broth
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • Spices (curry powder, turmeric, salt)

Dumplings:

  • 1 cup whole wheat flour
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 1 tablespoon oil
  • A handful of chopped veggies

Instructions: For the curry: sauté onion and garlic, add spices, tomatoes, and broth. Bring to boil, then mix in chickpeas. Cook for 15 minutes. For dumplings: mix flour and water, add veggies, and oil. Roll out the dough, create circles, and place a spoonful of the filling in the center. Seal, and cook in boiling water for 10 minutes.

These plant-based recipes are not only sustainable but also provide the essential nutrition needed for your children’s growth and development. Start discovering the goodness of Vegan Nutripots right in your kitchen. You and your kids will not only enjoy tasty meals but also contribute positively to our environment.
