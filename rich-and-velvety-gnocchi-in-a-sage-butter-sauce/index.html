Rich and Velvety Gnocchi in a Sage Butter Sauce


If you’re looking for a dish that combines rich flavors, velvety textures, and a touch of elegance, then this recipe for Rich and Velvety Gnocchi in a Sage Butter Sauce is perfect for you. This classic Italian dish brings together pillowy soft gnocchi, fragrant sage, and indulgent butter to create a mouthwatering experience that will leave you craving for more.

Ingredients

For the Gnocchi:

  • 2 pounds of potatoes
  • 2 cups of all-purpose flour
  • 1 large egg
  • Salt, to taste

For the Sage Butter Sauce:

  • 1/2 cup of unsalted butter
  • Handful of fresh sage leaves
  • Salt and pepper, to taste

Instructions

  1. Start by preparing the gnocchi. Peel the potatoes and cut them into chunks. Boil the potatoes in a large pot of salted water until they are fork-tender, about 15-20 minutes.

  2. Drain the cooked potatoes and let them cool slightly. Once they are cool enough to handle, pass them through a potato ricer or mash them until they are smooth and free of lumps.

  3. In a large mixing bowl, combine the mashed potatoes, flour, egg, and a pinch of salt. Mix until all the ingredients are well incorporated and a soft dough forms.

  4. Divide the dough into small portions and roll each portion into a long rope, about 1/2 inch in diameter. Cut the ropes into small bite-sized pieces and gently press each piece with a fork to create ridges.

  5. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Add the gnocchi to the boiling water and cook until they float to the surface, about 2-3 minutes. Remove the cooked gnocchi using a slotted spoon and set them aside.

  6. In a separate large skillet, melt the butter over medium heat. Add the sage leaves to the melted butter and cook until they become crispy and fragrant, about 2-3 minutes. Be careful not to burn the butter.

  7. Once the butter is infused with the sage flavor, add the cooked gnocchi to the skillet. Toss gently to coat the gnocchi with the butter sauce. Season with salt and pepper to taste.

  8. Serve the Rich and Velvety Gnocchi in a Sage Butter Sauce immediately. Garnish with additional fresh sage leaves if desired. This dish pairs well with a crisp white wine and a simple green salad.

Tips and Variations

  • For a more indulgent version, you can add grated Parmesan cheese to the gnocchi while tossing them in the sage butter sauce.
  • If you prefer a vegetarian option, you can substitute vegetable broth for the chicken broth in the sage butter sauce.
  • To add some color to the dish, you can stir in some sautéed mushrooms or roasted cherry tomatoes along with the gnocchi.
  • If you’re feeling adventurous, you can experiment with different herbs and spices to customize the flavors of the sage butter sauce.

So there you have it – a recipe for Rich and Velvety Gnocchi in a Sage Butter Sauce that will impress your family and friends. With its heavenly combination of flavors and textures, this dish is perfect for a special occasion or simply when you want to treat yourself to a luxurious meal. Enjoy!
