Irresistible Low Carb Avocado Egg Salad


If you’re looking for a delicious and healthy low-carb dish that’s packed with flavor, look no further than this irresistible avocado egg salad. This recipe is perfect for anyone following a low-carb or keto lifestyle, or for those who simply want to enjoy a light and satisfying meal. The combination of creamy avocado and perfectly boiled eggs creates a rich and satisfying texture, while the addition of fresh herbs and zesty lemon juice brings a burst of freshness to every bite.

Ingredients

  • 4 ripe avocados
  • 6 hard-boiled eggs
  • 1/4 cup diced red onion
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh cilantro
  • 2 tablespoons chopped fresh dill
  • 1/4 cup mayonnaise
  • 2 tablespoons Dijon mustard
  • 2 tablespoons fresh lemon juice
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Begin by hard-boiling the eggs. Place the eggs in a saucepan and cover them with water. Bring the water to a boil, then reduce the heat to a gentle simmer and cook for 10 minutes. Once done, transfer the eggs to a bowl of ice water to cool before peeling and chopping them.

  2. While the eggs are cooking, prepare the avocados. Slice the avocados in half and remove the pits. Scoop the flesh into a large mixing bowl and mash them with a fork until creamy but still slightly chunky.

  3. Add the diced red onion, chopped cilantro, and fresh dill to the mashed avocado. Gently fold the ingredients together until well combined.

  4. In a separate small bowl, whisk together the mayonnaise, Dijon mustard, and fresh lemon juice until smooth. Pour this dressing over the avocado mixture and gently fold until everything is evenly coated.

  5. Add the chopped hard-boiled eggs to the avocado mixture and gently fold them in, being careful not to break them up too much. Season the salad with salt and pepper to taste.

  6. Once everything is well combined, cover the bowl with plastic wrap and refrigerate for at least 30 minutes to allow the flavors to meld together.

  7. When ready to serve, give the salad a final gentle stir and taste for seasoning, adding more salt, pepper, or lemon juice if needed.

Serving Suggestions

This irresistible low carb avocado egg salad can be enjoyed in a variety of ways. Serve it on a bed of crisp lettuce leaves for a light and refreshing meal, or scoop it onto whole grain toast for a satisfying open-faced sandwich. It also makes a delightful filling for lettuce wraps or a topping for crackers. No matter how you choose to enjoy it, this salad is sure to be a hit at any gathering or as a simple weekday lunch.

Conclusion

With its creamy texture, vibrant flavors, and low-carb profile, this avocado egg salad is a must-try for anyone looking to add a healthy and delicious dish to their repertoire. It’s quick and easy to prepare, making it a perfect option for meal prepping or last-minute entertaining. Whether you’re following a specific dietary plan or simply love flavorful, wholesome food, this irresistible avocado egg salad is a winner on all fronts. Give it a try and savor the delightful combination of avocado, eggs, and zesty herbs in every bite!
