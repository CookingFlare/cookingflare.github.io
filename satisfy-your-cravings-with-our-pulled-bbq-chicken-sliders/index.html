Satisfy Your Cravings with our Pulled BBQ Chicken Sliders


Summer is here, and so are the cravings for delicious and easy-to-make meals that can be enjoyed with family and friends. In this post, we’ll explore the world of pulled BBQ chicken sliders, a mouth-watering dish that’s perfect for any occasion. Whether you’re planning a backyard BBQ, a casual get-together with friends, or just a quick lunch for yourself, our pulled BBQ chicken sliders are sure to satisfy your cravings.

What are Pulled BBQ Chicken Sliders?

Pulled BBQ chicken sliders are a twist on the classic pulled pork sandwich. Instead of using pork, we’re using juicy chicken breast, marinated in a sweet and tangy BBQ sauce, and then shredded to perfection. The result is a tender, flavorful, and juicy chicken that’s easy to bite into and enjoy.

How to Make Pulled BBQ Chicken Sliders

Making pulled BBQ chicken sliders is a breeze. Here’s a simple recipe to get you started:

Ingredients:

  • 2 lbs boneless, skinless chicken breast
  • 1 cup BBQ sauce
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 tbsp brown sugar
  • 1 tsp smoked paprika
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp onion powder
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/4 tsp black pepper
  • 4 hamburger buns
  • Coleslaw (optional)
  • Pickle slices (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. In a large bowl, whisk together the BBQ sauce, olive oil, brown sugar, smoked paprika, garlic powder, onion powder, salt, and black pepper.
  3. Add the chicken breast to the bowl and toss to coat evenly with the marinade.
  4. Cover the bowl with plastic wrap and refrigerate for at least 2 hours or overnight.
  5. Remove the chicken from the marinade and place it on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper.
  6. Bake the chicken for 25-30 minutes or until it reaches an internal temperature of 165°F (74°C).
  7. Remove the chicken from the oven and let it cool for 5-10 minutes.
  8. Shred the chicken with two forks until it’s tender and stringy.
  9. Split the hamburger buns in half and toast them in the oven for 2-3 minutes.
  10. Assemble the sliders by placing a generous amount of the shredded chicken on each bun, followed by a dollop of coleslaw and a few pickle slices (if using).

Tips and Variations

  • For an extra crispy bun, try toasting the buns in a pan with a little butter or non-stick spray.
  • Add some crunch to your sliders by topping them with chopped bacon, diced onions, or crumbled blue cheese.
  • Experiment with different BBQ sauces to find your favorite flavor profile.
  • Serve the sliders with a side of sweet potato fries or a green salad for a well-rounded meal.

Why You’ll Love Pulled BBQ Chicken Sliders

  • They’re easy to make: With just a few simple ingredients and some basic cooking skills, you can create a delicious and satisfying meal.
  • They’re customizable: Add your favorite toppings or sauces to make the sliders your own.
  • They’re perfect for any occasion: Whether you’re hosting a party or just want a quick lunch, pulled BBQ chicken sliders are a great option.

Conclusion

Pulled BBQ chicken sliders are a mouth-watering dish that’s sure to satisfy your cravings. With their tender, juicy chicken and sweet and tangy BBQ sauce, they’re a perfect addition to any meal. Whether you’re a BBQ aficionado or just looking for a new recipe to try, our pulled BBQ chicken sliders are a must-try. So go ahead, give them a try, and taste the difference for yourself!
