Delicious Instant Pot Mac and Cheese Recipe for Family Dinners


Are you looking for a quick and easy meal that will satisfy the whole family? Look no further than this delicious Instant Pot mac and cheese recipe. With the help of your trusty Instant Pot, you can have creamy, cheesy mac and cheese on the table in no time. Whether it’s a busy weeknight or a lazy weekend, this recipe is sure to become a family favorite.

Ingredients

  • 16 oz elbow macaroni
  • 4 cups chicken broth
  • 2 cups whole milk
  • 4 tbsp unsalted butter
  • 1 tsp mustard powder
  • 1/2 tsp garlic powder
  • 1/2 tsp onion powder
  • 1/2 tsp paprika
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/4 tsp black pepper
  • 3 cups shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/2 cup panko breadcrumbs (optional, for topping)

Instructions

  1. Start by adding the elbow macaroni, chicken broth, whole milk, butter, mustard powder, garlic powder, onion powder, paprika, salt, and black pepper to the Instant Pot.

  2. Close the lid and set the Instant Pot to manual high pressure for 4 minutes.

  3. Once the cooking cycle is complete, do a quick pressure release and carefully open the lid.

  4. Stir in the shredded cheddar cheese, mozzarella cheese, and Parmesan cheese until the mixture is creamy and the cheese is fully melted.

  5. If desired, you can sprinkle panko breadcrumbs on top for a crunchy topping.

  6. Serve the mac and cheese immediately, and watch it disappear in no time!

Tips and Tricks

  • For an extra flavor boost, you can add cooked and crumbled bacon, diced ham, or even some sautéed vegetables to the mac and cheese.
  • If you prefer a bit of spice, a dash of hot sauce or a sprinkle of red pepper flakes can add a kick to the dish.
  • Leftovers can be stored in an airtight container in the refrigerator for up to 3 days. Simply reheat in the microwave or on the stovetop with a splash of milk to restore the creamy texture.

Why Instant Pot Mac and Cheese?

The Instant Pot makes cooking mac and cheese a breeze. Not only does it cut down on the cooking time, but it also ensures that the pasta cooks evenly and absorbs all the delicious flavors of the broth and spices. The result is a creamy, indulgent mac and cheese that rivals any stovetop or oven-baked version.

Family-Friendly Appeal

Mac and cheese is a classic comfort food that appeals to both kids and adults. With this Instant Pot recipe, you can whip up a batch of mac and cheese that will please even the pickiest eaters. The creamy texture and cheesy goodness make it a winner for family dinners, potlucks, or any occasion that calls for a comforting dish.

Conclusion

With this Instant Pot mac and cheese recipe, you can take the stress out of mealtime and treat your family to a dish that’s both delicious and satisfying. The convenience of the Instant Pot combined with the irresistible appeal of mac and cheese makes this recipe a must-try for busy home cooks. Give it a whirl and watch as it becomes a regular feature on your family’s menu!
