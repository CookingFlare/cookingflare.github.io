Tasty Slow Cooker Moroccan Lamb Stew Recipe


Are you looking for a delicious and aromatic dish that will transport you to the exotic flavors of Morocco? Look no further! This Tasty Slow Cooker Moroccan Lamb Stew Recipe is the perfect blend of tender lamb, fragrant spices, and hearty vegetables. With the convenience of a slow cooker, you can effortlessly create a mouthwatering meal that will impress your family and friends. Let’s get started!

Ingredients

  • 2 pounds of lamb, cut into chunks
  • 2 onions, diced
  • 4 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 2 teaspoons of ground cumin
  • 2 teaspoons of ground coriander
  • 1 teaspoon of ground cinnamon
  • 1 teaspoon of ground ginger
  • 1/2 teaspoon of ground turmeric
  • 1/2 teaspoon of ground paprika
  • 1/4 teaspoon of cayenne pepper (optional, for extra heat)
  • 1 can of diced tomatoes
  • 3 cups of beef or vegetable broth
  • 2 cups of carrots, sliced
  • 2 cups of potatoes, diced
  • 1 cup of dried apricots, chopped
  • 1 cup of green olives, pitted
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh cilantro, chopped (for garnish)

Instructions

  1. In a large skillet, heat some oil over medium-high heat. Add the lamb chunks and brown them on all sides. This will help seal in the juices and add extra flavor to the stew. Once browned, transfer the lamb to the slow cooker.

  2. In the same skillet, add the diced onions and minced garlic. Sauté until the onions are translucent and fragrant. Then, add the ground cumin, coriander, cinnamon, ginger, turmeric, paprika, and cayenne pepper (if using). Stir well to coat the onions and garlic with the spices. Cook for an additional minute to release the flavors.

  3. Transfer the onion and spice mixture to the slow cooker, on top of the lamb.

  4. Add the diced tomatoes and beef or vegetable broth to the slow cooker. Stir everything together to combine the flavors.

  5. Add the sliced carrots, diced potatoes, chopped dried apricots, and pitted green olives to the slow cooker. Gently stir to distribute the ingredients evenly.

  6. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Remember to go easy on the salt, as the olives and broth already contain sodium.

  7. Cover the slow cooker and set it to cook on low for 6-8 hours, or on high for 3-4 hours. The longer cooking time will allow the flavors to meld together and the lamb to become tender and succulent.

  8. Once the cooking time is complete, give the stew a good stir. Taste and adjust the seasoning if needed.

  9. Serve the Tasty Slow Cooker Moroccan Lamb Stew in bowls, garnished with fresh chopped cilantro. This stew pairs perfectly with couscous or crusty bread to soak up the delicious sauce.

Enjoy the rich flavors and comforting aromas of this Tasty Slow Cooker Moroccan Lamb Stew. It’s a dish that will surely impress and satisfy your taste buds. Bon appétit!

Remember to experiment with the spices and adjust them to your liking. Cooking is all about personal taste and preferences, so don’t be afraid to make this recipe your own. Share your experience in the comments below and let us know how you enjoyed this flavorful Moroccan delight. Happy cooking!
