Tasty and Easy Beef Stir Fry Recipes That Will Impress Your Guests

3 min · 559 words

Are you looking for a delicious and impressive dish to serve your guests? Look no further than these tasty and easy beef stir fry recipes! Stir fry dishes are not only flavorful and satisfying, but they are also quick to make, making them the perfect choice for entertaining.

1. Teriyaki Beef Stir Fry

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound beef sirloin, thinly sliced
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons honey
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon ginger, grated
  • 1 red bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 cup broccoli florets
  • 1 carrot, julienned
  • 1/2 cup snow peas
  • 1 green onion, sliced
  • Sesame seeds, for garnish

Instructions:

  1. In a small bowl, whisk together soy sauce, honey, garlic, and ginger. Set aside.
  2. Heat vegetable oil in a large skillet or wok over high heat. Add beef slices and stir fry until browned. Remove beef from the skillet and set aside.
  3. In the same skillet, add bell pepper, broccoli, carrot, and snow peas. Stir fry for 3-4 minutes or until vegetables are crisp-tender.
  4. Return the beef to the skillet and pour the teriyaki sauce over the mixture. Stir fry for an additional 2-3 minutes, until the beef is cooked through and the sauce has thickened.
  5. Remove from heat and garnish with sliced green onions and sesame seeds. Serve hot with steamed rice or noodles.

2. Mongolian Beef Stir Fry

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound flank steak, thinly sliced
  • 2 tablespoons cornstarch
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons brown sugar
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon ginger, grated
  • 1/2 cup beef broth
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1 onion, sliced
  • 1 red bell pepper, sliced
  • 1/2 cup sliced mushrooms
  • Green onions, for garnish

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, toss the sliced beef with cornstarch until well coated. Set aside.
  2. In another bowl, whisk together soy sauce, brown sugar, garlic, ginger, and beef broth. Set aside.
  3. Heat vegetable oil in a large skillet or wok over medium-high heat. Add the beef slices and cook until browned. Remove beef from the skillet and set aside.
  4. In the same skillet, add onion, bell pepper, and mushrooms. Stir fry for 3-4 minutes or until vegetables are tender.
  5. Return the beef to the skillet and pour the soy sauce mixture over the mixture. Stir fry for an additional 2-3 minutes, until the beef is cooked through and the sauce has thickened.
  6. Remove from heat and garnish with chopped green onions. Serve hot with steamed rice.

3. Spicy Garlic Beef Stir Fry

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound beef tenderloin, thinly sliced
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 red chili pepper, sliced (adjust according to your spice preference)
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons oyster sauce
  • 1 tablespoon brown sugar
  • 1 tablespoon cornstarch
  • 1/4 cup water
  • 1 red bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 cup snap peas
  • 1/2 cup baby corn
  • Fresh cilantro, for garnish

Instructions:

  1. In a large skillet or wok, heat vegetable oil over high heat. Add minced garlic and sliced chili pepper. Stir fry for 1 minute or until fragrant.
  2. Add beef slices to the skillet and stir fry until browned. Remove beef from the skillet and set aside.
  3. In a small bowl, whisk together soy sauce, oyster sauce, brown sugar, cornstarch, and water. Set aside.
  4. In the same skillet, add bell pepper, snap peas, and baby corn. Stir fry for 3-4 minutes or until vegetables are tender.
  5. Return the beef to the skillet and pour the sauce mixture over the mixture. Stir fry for an additional 2-3 minutes, until the beef is cooked through and the sauce has thickened.
  6. Remove from heat and garnish with fresh cilantro. Serve hot with steamed rice or noodles.

These beef stir fry recipes are not only delicious but also versatile. Feel free to customize them by adding your favorite vegetables or adjusting the level of spiciness. With these easy recipes, you can impress your guests with a flavorful and satisfying meal in no time. Enjoy!
