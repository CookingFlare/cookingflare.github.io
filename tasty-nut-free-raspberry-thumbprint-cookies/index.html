Tasty Nut-Free Raspberry Thumbprint Cookies


If you’re looking for a delicious and allergy-friendly cookie recipe, you’ve come to the right place. These Tasty Nut-Free Raspberry Thumbprint Cookies are the perfect treat for those with nut allergies or anyone who wants to enjoy a delightful and fruity dessert. With a buttery cookie base and a burst of sweet raspberry jam, these cookies are sure to satisfy your cravings. Plus, they’re incredibly easy to make!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 2 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup raspberry jam

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C) and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.

  2. In a large mixing bowl, cream together the softened butter and granulated sugar until light and fluffy. This usually takes about 2-3 minutes with an electric mixer.

  3. Add the eggs, one at a time, mixing well after each addition. Then, stir in the vanilla extract.

  4. In a separate bowl, whisk together the all-purpose flour and salt. Gradually add the dry ingredients to the butter mixture, mixing until just combined. Be careful not to overmix, as this can result in tough cookies.

  5. Roll the dough into 1-inch balls and place them onto the prepared baking sheet, spacing them about 2 inches apart. Using your thumb or the back of a teaspoon, make an indentation in the center of each dough ball.

  6. Fill each indentation with about 1/2 teaspoon of raspberry jam. You can use more or less depending on your preference, but be careful not to overfill as it can cause the jam to spill over during baking.

  7. Bake the cookies in the preheated oven for 12-15 minutes, or until the edges are lightly golden. The centers may still be slightly soft, but they will firm up as they cool.

  8. Remove the cookies from the oven and allow them to cool on the baking sheet for 5 minutes before transferring them to a wire rack to cool completely.

  9. Once the cookies have cooled, you can serve them immediately or store them in an airtight container for up to one week. These cookies also freeze well, so you can enjoy them at a later time.

Tips and Variations

  • If you don’t have raspberry jam on hand, feel free to use any other fruit jam or preserve of your choice. Strawberry, blueberry, or apricot would all work well in this recipe.

  • For an extra special touch, you can dust the cooled cookies with powdered sugar before serving. This adds a touch of elegance and makes them look even more enticing.

  • If you prefer a more citrusy flavor, you can add a teaspoon of lemon zest to the cookie dough. This will give the cookies a refreshing twist and complement the raspberry jam beautifully.

  • If you’re feeling adventurous, you can experiment with different flavors of jam and even mix and match. Try using a combination of raspberry and blackberry jam for a burst of berry goodness in every bite.

Enjoy these Tasty Nut-Free Raspberry Thumbprint Cookies as a delightful treat for yourself or share them with family and friends. These cookies are perfect for any occasion, whether it’s a cozy afternoon tea or a festive holiday gathering. Their nut-free nature makes them a safe and delicious option for those with food allergies, so everyone can enjoy a taste of sweetness. So go ahead, indulge in these delightful cookies and savor the joy they bring with every bite!
