Vegan Chocolate Cream Pie Recipe with a Graham Cracker Crust and Whipped Coconut Cream


If you’re in the mood for a decadent and luscious dessert that’s not only delicious but also vegan, then you’re in for a treat with this Vegan Chocolate Cream Pie with a Graham Cracker Crust and Whipped Coconut Cream. This recipe combines the rich, velvety texture of chocolate cream with the crunch of a graham cracker crust, all topped off with a light and fluffy whipped coconut cream. It’s the perfect indulgence for any occasion, and it’s sure to impress vegans and non-vegans alike.

Ingredients

For the Crust:

  • 1 1/2 cups of vegan graham cracker crumbs
  • 1/4 cup of coconut sugar
  • 1/3 cup of melted coconut oil

For the Chocolate Cream Filling:

  • 1 can of full-fat coconut milk
  • 8 oz of dairy-free dark chocolate, chopped
  • 1/4 cup of maple syrup
  • 1 teaspoon of vanilla extract

For the Whipped Coconut Cream:

  • 1 can of full-fat coconut milk, refrigerated overnight
  • 1-2 tablespoons of powdered sugar
  • 1 teaspoon of vanilla extract

Instructions

Step 1: Prepare the Crust

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. In a bowl, mix the graham cracker crumbs, coconut sugar, and melted coconut oil until well combined.
  3. Press the mixture into a 9-inch pie dish, making sure to cover the bottom and sides evenly.
  4. Bake the crust for 10 minutes, then remove from the oven and let it cool completely.

Step 2: Make the Chocolate Cream Filling

  1. In a saucepan, heat the coconut milk over medium heat until it just starts to simmer.
  2. Remove the saucepan from the heat and add the chopped dark chocolate, maple syrup, and vanilla extract.
  3. Stir the mixture until the chocolate is completely melted and the filling is smooth.
  4. Pour the chocolate cream filling into the cooled graham cracker crust.
  5. Refrigerate the pie for at least 4 hours, or until the filling is set.

Step 3: Prepare the Whipped Coconut Cream

  1. Carefully open the refrigerated can of coconut milk without shaking it.
  2. Scoop out the solid coconut cream that has risen to the top and place it in a mixing bowl, leaving the liquid behind (save it for another use).
  3. Add powdered sugar and vanilla extract to the coconut cream.
  4. Using a hand mixer or a stand mixer, whip the coconut cream until it becomes light and fluffy.

Step 4: Assemble and Serve

  1. Once the chocolate cream filling is set, spread the whipped coconut cream over the top of the pie.
  2. For an extra touch, you can garnish with chocolate shavings or a sprinkle of cocoa powder.
  3. Slice and serve the pie, and enjoy the creamy, chocolatey goodness!

Conclusion

This Vegan Chocolate Cream Pie with a Graham Cracker Crust and Whipped Coconut Cream is a delightful dessert that’s perfect for any gathering or as a sweet indulgence for yourself. The combination of the crunchy crust, rich chocolate filling, and airy coconut cream creates a symphony of flavors and textures that will leave everyone wanting more. Whether you’re a vegan or just looking to try something new, this recipe is a must-try for anyone with a sweet tooth. Enjoy every bite of this heavenly, plant-based dessert!
