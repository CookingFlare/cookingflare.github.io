Soulful and Satisfying: Southern Smothered Pork Chops Recipe


If you’re craving a hearty and comforting meal that will transport you to the warm and welcoming kitchens of the South, then look no further than this Soulful and Satisfying Southern Smothered Pork Chops recipe. Packed with flavor and cooked to perfection, these tender pork chops will leave you feeling satisfied and content.

Ingredients

  • 4 bone-in pork chops
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon cayenne pepper (optional)
  • 1/2 cup vegetable oil
  • 1 large onion, thinly sliced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 cups chicken broth
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • Fresh parsley, chopped (for garnish)

Instructions

  1. In a shallow dish, combine the flour, salt, black pepper, garlic powder, paprika, and cayenne pepper (if using). Mix well.

  2. Dredge each pork chop in the seasoned flour mixture, making sure to coat both sides evenly. Shake off any excess flour.

  3. In a large skillet over medium-high heat, heat the vegetable oil. Add the pork chops and cook until golden brown, about 4-5 minutes per side. Remove the pork chops from the skillet and set them aside.

  4. In the same skillet, add the sliced onion and minced garlic. Cook until the onions are softened and translucent, about 5 minutes.

  5. Reduce the heat to medium and sprinkle the remaining seasoned flour mixture over the onions and garlic. Stir well to combine and cook for an additional 2 minutes.

  6. Slowly pour in the chicken broth, stirring constantly to avoid any lumps. Bring the mixture to a simmer and cook for 5 minutes, or until the sauce has thickened slightly.

  7. Return the pork chops to the skillet, nestling them into the sauce. Cover the skillet with a lid and reduce the heat to low. Let the pork chops simmer in the sauce for 20-25 minutes, or until they are cooked through and tender.

  8. Remove the pork chops from the skillet and place them on a serving platter. Cover them with foil to keep them warm.

  9. Increase the heat to medium-high and stir in the heavy cream. Cook the sauce for an additional 2-3 minutes, or until it has thickened to your desired consistency.

  10. Pour the creamy sauce over the pork chops, garnish with fresh parsley, and serve hot.

Serving Suggestions

Serve these Soulful and Satisfying Southern Smothered Pork Chops with a side of creamy mashed potatoes and steamed green beans for a complete and comforting meal. The rich and flavorful sauce pairs perfectly with the tender pork chops, creating a culinary experience that will leave your taste buds singing.

Conclusion

Indulge in the flavors of the South with this Soulful and Satisfying Southern Smothered Pork Chops recipe. From the golden brown pork chops to the creamy and flavorful sauce, every bite is a delight for the senses. Whether you’re looking for a comforting weeknight meal or a dish to impress your guests, this recipe is sure to satisfy. So grab your skillet and get ready to experience the soulful flavors of the South in your own kitchen. Enjoy!
