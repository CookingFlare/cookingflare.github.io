Raisin Cinnamon Swirl Bread: A Sweet Treat


Welcome to today’s cooking adventure! Let’s dive into the much-loved Raisin Cinnamon Swirl Bread – a delectable and versatile treat that is both warm and welcoming. It brings a sense of comfort and nostalgia, making it a perfect addition to your family’s weekly baking routine or even a special gift for a dear friend.

To begin, let’s gather the ingredients we’ll need for this delightful bread:

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons granulated sugar
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • ¼ teaspoon salt
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 cup packed brown sugar
  • 1 cup raisins
  • 2 teaspoons ground cinnamon
  • 1 cup milk
  • ½ cup unsalted butter, at room temperature

Now that we’ve got the ingredients ready, let’s move on to the main steps involved in preparing the Raisin Cinnamon Swirl Bread:

Step-by-Step Guide

Step 1: Mix the dry ingredients.

Combine the flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt in a large mixing bowl, giving it a good whisk to ensure even distribution of the ingredients.

Step 2: Prepare the wet ingredients.

In a separate large bowl, beat the eggs, and then stir in the brown sugar, milk, and melted butter until completely blended. Folding in the plumped raisins and cinnamon later will add bursts of sweetness and warmth to your bread.

Step 3: Bring the wet and dry ingredients together.

Gradually pour the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients, stirring until well mixed. Then, set aside a small portion of this dough, reserving it for the cinnamon swirl later.

Step 4: Introduce the raisins and cinnamon.

Mix in the plumped raisins and the ground cinnamon to the rest of your dough.

Step 5: Prepare the cinnamon swirl layer.

In a small bowl, mix the reserved dough portion with the additional 2 teaspoons of cinnamon. This layer will provide the swirl that adds a delightful touch to your bread.

Step 6: Prepare the bread pan.

Liberally grease a 9x4 inch loaf pan with butter to prevent sticking.

Step 7: Assemble the swirl bread.

Spread half of the raisin and cinnamon dough in the prepared bread pan. Then, layer on the cinnamon swirl dough on top. Spread the remaining raisin and cinnamon dough over the swirl layer.

Step 8: Rest the dough.

Cover the loaf pan with plastic wrap and allow it to rest for 30 minutes. Resting time allows the dough to set and helps achieve a better texture.

Step 9: Bake the bread.

Preheat your oven to 350℃ (approximately 175℉). Uncover the loaf pan and bake your Raisin Cinnamon Swirl Bread for approximately 50 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center of the bread comes out clean.

Step 10: Let it cool.

Remove your delectable creation from the oven, and let the bread cool in the loaf pan for about 15 minutes before transferring it to a wire rack to cool completely.

Step 11: Serve and enjoy!

Slice your Raisin Cinnamon Swirl Bread and indulge in the delightful fusion of flavors. It’s a tantalizing combination of sweet and warm, perfect for breakfast, snack time, or dessert.

There you have it – a step-by-step guide to making the irresistible Raisin Cinnamon Swirl Bread, just in time for cozy autumn days and gatherings with friends and family. Happy baking!
