A Simple yet Delicious Italian-Style Focaccia Pizza Recipe with Prosciutto


Welcome to my kitchen, where today we’ll be exploring the wonderful world of Italian cuisine. In this recipe, we’ll be combining two beloved dishes, focaccia bread and pizza, to create a mouthwatering Italian-style focaccia pizza topped with prosciutto. This recipe is simple yet bursting with flavors that will transport you to the streets of Italy. So let’s get started!

Ingredients

For the Focaccia Dough:

  • 2 ½ cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • 1 tablespoon instant yeast
  • 1 cup warm water
  • 3 tablespoons olive oil

For the Pizza Topping:

  • 1 cup pizza sauce
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 6 slices prosciutto
  • Fresh basil leaves, for garnish

Instructions

  1. In a large mixing bowl, combine the all-purpose flour, salt, and sugar. Mix well to evenly distribute the ingredients.

  2. In a separate small bowl, dissolve the instant yeast in warm water. Let it sit for 5 minutes until it becomes frothy.

  3. Make a well in the center of the dry ingredients and pour in the yeast mixture and olive oil. Stir everything together until a sticky dough forms.

  4. Transfer the dough onto a lightly floured surface and knead for about 5-7 minutes until the dough becomes smooth and elastic.

  5. Place the dough in a greased bowl, cover it with a clean kitchen towel, and let it rise in a warm spot for about 1 hour or until it doubles in size.

  6. Preheat your oven to 425°F (220°C) while the dough is rising.

  7. Once the dough has risen, punch it down to release any air bubbles. Transfer the dough onto a lightly greased baking sheet and gently press it down to fit the size of the sheet. If desired, you can divide the dough into smaller portions to make individual-sized pizzas.

  8. Spread the pizza sauce evenly over the dough, leaving a small border around the edges. Sprinkle the shredded mozzarella cheese on top.

  9. Tear the prosciutto into small pieces and distribute them evenly on the pizza.

  10. Bake the focaccia pizza in the preheated oven for 15-20 minutes or until the crust turns golden brown and the cheese is bubbly and melty.

  11. Remove the focaccia pizza from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes. Garnish with fresh basil leaves for an extra touch of freshness.

  12. Slice the focaccia pizza into squares or wedges and serve it warm. Enjoy the delightful combination of the soft and chewy focaccia bread with the savory prosciutto and gooey cheese.

Tips and Variations

  • Feel free to experiment with other toppings such as sliced tomatoes, olives, or roasted vegetables to add more depth of flavor to your focaccia pizza.
  • If you prefer a thinner crust, you can divide the dough in half and roll it out into two thinner rectangles before adding the toppings.
  • For a vegetarian version, omit the prosciutto and add your favorite veggies instead.
  • This Italian-style focaccia pizza pairs perfectly with a crisp green salad and a glass of Italian wine for a complete meal.

Now that you have the recipe for this simple yet delicious Italian-style focaccia pizza with prosciutto, it’s time to gather your ingredients and start cooking. Whether you’re hosting a casual dinner or simply craving a taste of Italy, this recipe is sure to impress. Buon appetito!
