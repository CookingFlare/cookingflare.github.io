Homestyle Chicken and Rice Skillet: One-Pan Comfort Food


If you’re looking for a delicious and comforting meal that requires minimal effort and cleanup, then look no further than this Homestyle Chicken and Rice Skillet recipe. Packed with flavor and made in just one pan, this dish is perfect for those busy weeknights when you want something warm and satisfying without spending hours in the kitchen.

Ingredients

  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 medium onion, diced
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 red bell pepper, diced
  • 1 cup long-grain white rice
  • 2 cups chicken broth
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1 cup frozen peas
  • Fresh parsley, for garnish

Instructions

  1. In a large skillet, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the chicken breasts and cook until browned on both sides, about 5 minutes per side. Remove the chicken from the skillet and set aside.

  2. In the same skillet, add the diced onion, minced garlic, and diced red bell pepper. Sauté until the vegetables are softened, about 5 minutes.

  3. Add the rice to the skillet and stir to coat it with the oil and vegetables. Cook for an additional 2 minutes to lightly toast the rice.

  4. Pour in the chicken broth, dried thyme, paprika, salt, and black pepper. Stir everything together and bring the mixture to a boil.

  5. Reduce the heat to low, cover the skillet, and simmer for 15 minutes, or until the rice is cooked and the liquid has been absorbed.

  6. While the rice is cooking, slice the cooked chicken breasts into thin strips.

  7. Once the rice is cooked, add the frozen peas and sliced chicken to the skillet. Stir everything together and cook for an additional 2-3 minutes, until the peas are heated through.

  8. Remove the skillet from the heat and let it sit, covered, for 5 minutes to allow the flavors to meld together.

  9. Serve the Homestyle Chicken and Rice Skillet hot, garnished with fresh parsley for a pop of color.

Why You’ll Love It

This Homestyle Chicken and Rice Skillet recipe is a true comfort food that offers a multitude of benefits. First and foremost, it’s incredibly easy to make, requiring only one pan and a handful of ingredients that you likely already have in your kitchen. This means fewer dishes to wash and less time spent on cleanup.

Additionally, this dish is versatile and can be customized to suit your taste preferences. Feel free to add in your favorite vegetables, such as carrots or corn, or experiment with different herbs and spices to elevate the flavor profile even further.

Not only is this recipe convenient and customizable, but it’s also a wholesome and nutritious meal. Chicken is a great source of lean protein, while the rice provides a satisfying and filling carbohydrate. The addition of vegetables adds essential vitamins and minerals, making this a well-rounded dish that will leave you feeling satisfied and nourished.

So the next time you’re in need of a quick and comforting meal, give this Homestyle Chicken and Rice Skillet recipe a try. With its delicious flavors, minimal cleanup, and nourishing ingredients, it’s sure to become a family favorite in no time. Enjoy!
