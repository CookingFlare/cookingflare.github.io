A Burst of Flavor: Gluten-Free Lemon Garlic Chicken Pasta


Are you craving a delicious and flavorful pasta dish that’s both gluten-free and bursting with zesty lemon and garlic flavors? Look no further! Our Gluten-Free Lemon Garlic Chicken Pasta recipe is the perfect choice for a satisfying and healthy meal. Whether you’re following a gluten-free diet or simply looking to try something new, this recipe is sure to impress.

Ingredients

To create this burst of flavor, you’ll need the following ingredients:

  • 8 ounces gluten-free pasta (your choice of shape)
  • 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 4 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 lemon, zested and juiced
  • 1/2 cup low-sodium chicken broth
  • 1/4 cup fresh parsley, chopped
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Cook the gluten-free pasta according to the package instructions. Drain and set aside.

  2. While the pasta is cooking, season the chicken breasts with salt and pepper. In a large skillet, heat 1 tablespoon of olive oil over medium-high heat. Add the chicken breasts and cook for about 6-8 minutes per side, or until cooked through. Remove the chicken from the skillet and set aside.

  3. In the same skillet, add another tablespoon of olive oil and the minced garlic. Sauté the garlic for about 1-2 minutes, until fragrant.

  4. Add the lemon zest, lemon juice, and chicken broth to the skillet with the garlic. Stir to combine and bring to a simmer.

  5. Slice the cooked chicken breasts into thin strips and add them back to the skillet. Cook for an additional 2-3 minutes, until the chicken is heated through and coated in the lemon garlic sauce.

  6. Add the cooked gluten-free pasta to the skillet and toss to coat the pasta in the sauce. Cook for another minute, allowing the flavors to meld together.

  7. Remove the skillet from the heat and sprinkle the chopped parsley over the pasta. Give it a gentle toss to incorporate the parsley.

  8. Serve the Gluten-Free Lemon Garlic Chicken Pasta hot, garnished with additional lemon zest or parsley if desired.

Enjoying the Burst of Flavor

Now that you’ve prepared this delightful gluten-free pasta dish, it’s time to sit back, relax, and savor the burst of flavor. The combination of zesty lemon, aromatic garlic, and tender chicken will leave your taste buds dancing with delight. This dish is perfect for a quick weeknight dinner or for entertaining guests.

Not only is this recipe delicious, but it’s also gluten-free, making it suitable for those with dietary restrictions or preferences. The gluten-free pasta option ensures that everyone can enjoy a satisfying and flavorful meal without compromising on taste or health.

Feel free to experiment with this recipe by adding your own twist. You can toss in some sautéed vegetables like bell peppers or zucchini for added color and nutrients. Additionally, you can sprinkle some grated Parmesan cheese on top for a cheesy finish.

So why wait? Gather the ingredients, follow the simple steps, and treat yourself to a burst of flavor with our Gluten-Free Lemon Garlic Chicken Pasta recipe. Bon appétit!
