Top 7 Slow Cooker Soups for Chilly Days


As the temperature drops and leaves begin to change, there’s nothing quite like a warm bowl of soup to soothe the soul. Slow cookers are a busy cook’s best friend, allowing you to create hearty and flavorful soups with minimal effort. Hereâ€™s a curated list of the top seven slow cooker soups that are perfect for those chilly days, providing warmth and comfort with every spoonful.

1. Classic Chicken Noodle Soup

Nothing says comfort like a bowl of chicken noodle soup. This classic recipe is simple to make in your slow cooker and bursts with flavor.

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 4 cups chicken broth
  • 2 cups carrots, chopped
  • 2 cups celery, chopped
  • 1 medium onion, diced
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • 1 teaspoon dried rosemary
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 cups egg noodles
  • Fresh parsley for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Place the chicken breasts at the bottom of the slow cooker.
  2. Add the chicken broth, carrots, celery, onion, garlic, thyme, rosemary, salt, and pepper.
  3. Cover and cook on low for 6 to 8 hours or on high for 3 to 4 hours.
  4. About 30 minutes before serving, shred the chicken with two forks and return it to the pot.
  5. Stir in the egg noodles and cook until tender.
  6. Garnish with fresh parsley before serving.

This classic chicken noodle soup is perfect for those chilly evenings when you need some homemade warmth.

2. Hearty Beef and Barley Soup

For a filling meal that warms your belly, try this beef and barley soup. It’s rich, savory, and packed with nutrients.

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound beef chuck, cut into 1-inch cubes
  • 1 cup barley
  • 4 cups beef broth
  • 2 cups diced tomatoes (canned or fresh)
  • 1 cup carrots, sliced
  • 1 cup celery, diced
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 teaspoons Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In the slow cooker, combine all ingredients except for the barley.
  2. Cover and cook on low for 8 hours or high for 4 hours.
  3. About an hour before serving, stir in the barley.
  4. Adjust salt and pepper as needed.

This soup is perfect for meal prep and can be stored in the fridge or frozen for future enjoyment.

3. Creamy Potato and Leek Soup

This creamy potato and leek soup is not only delicious but also incredibly simple to make. It’s perfect for those who love a smooth, velvety texture.

Ingredients:

  • 4 large potatoes, peeled and diced
  • 3 leeks, cleaned and sliced
  • 4 cups vegetable broth
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh chives for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Place the potatoes, leeks, garlic, and vegetable broth in the slow cooker.
  2. Cover and cook on low for 6-8 hours.
  3. Once cooked, use an immersion blender to puree until smooth.
  4. Stir in the heavy cream and adjust seasoning with salt and pepper.
  5. Garnish with fresh chives before serving.

This silky soup is a delightful choice for a cozy night in, perfect with a slice of crusty bread.

4. Spicy Lentil Soup

For those who crave a little heat, this spicy lentil soup is a fantastic option. Packed with protein and fiber, it’s as nutritious as it is delicious.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup lentils, rinsed
  • 1 can (14 oz) diced tomatoes
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 carrots, diced
  • 2 celery stalks, diced
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 4 cups vegetable broth
  • 1 tablespoon curry powder
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • 1 teaspoon chili powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Cilantro for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Add all ingredients to the slow cooker and stir well.
  2. Cover and cook on low for 8 hours or high for 4 hours.
  3. Adjust seasoning as needed before serving.
  4. Garnish with fresh cilantro.

This soup is perfect for a filling lunch or dinner and is sure to warm you from the inside out.

5. Tomato Basil Soup

Rich and flavorful, this tomato basil soup is a breeze to make and pairs beautifully with grilled cheese sandwiches.

Ingredients:

  • 6 cups fresh tomatoes, chopped (or 2 cans of whole tomatoes)
  • 1 medium onion, chopped
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 cups vegetable broth
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1 teaspoon sugar (optional)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • Fresh basil for garnish

Instructions:

  1. In the slow cooker, combine tomatoes, onion, garlic, vegetable broth, basil, sugar, salt, and pepper.
  2. Cover and cook on low for 6 hours or high for 3 hours.
  3. Blend the soup until smooth using an immersion blender.
  4. Stir in the heavy cream and adjust seasoning as needed.
  5. Garnish with fresh basil when serving.

This delightful soup is perfect for those who want a quick and comforting meal.

6. Butternut Squash Soup with Apples

This butternut squash soup with apples is a perfect balance of sweet and savory, making it a favorite for fall.

Ingredients:

  • 1 medium butternut squash, peeled and diced
  • 1 apple, peeled and diced
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 4 cups vegetable broth
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • 1 teaspoon cinnamon
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 cup coconut milk (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Combine all ingredients except for coconut milk in the slow cooker.
  2. Cover and cook on low for 6 hours or high for 3 hours.
  3. Blend until smooth with an immersion blender.
  4. Stir in coconut milk if using, and adjust seasoning as needed.

This soup is not only delicious but also makes for a beautiful presentation with its vibrant color.

7. Mexican Tortilla Soup

Spice up your soup night with this Mexican tortilla soup! It’s perfect for those chilly nights when you need a little kick.

Ingredients:

  • 1 can (14 oz) diced tomatoes
  • 1 can (15 oz) black beans, rinsed and drained
  • 1 cup corn (fresh or frozen)
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 4 cups chicken or vegetable broth
  • 1 tablespoon chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Tortilla strips, avocado, and lime for garnish

Instructions:

  1. In the slow cooker, combine all ingredients except for the toppings.
  2. Cover and cook on low for 6 hours or high for 3 hours.
  3. Adjust seasoning before serving.
  4. Serve topped with tortilla strips, avocado, and a squeeze of lime.

This vibrant soup not only warms you up but also adds a festive touch to your dinner table.
