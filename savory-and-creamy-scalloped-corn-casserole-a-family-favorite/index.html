Savory and Creamy Scalloped Corn Casserole: A Family Favorite


If you’re looking for a delicious and comforting dish that will please the whole family, look no further than this Savory and Creamy Scalloped Corn Casserole. This hearty casserole is packed with flavor and has a creamy texture that will leave you craving more. Whether you’re hosting a family gathering or simply want to add some variety to your weeknight dinners, this recipe is sure to become a favorite!

Ingredients

  • 4 cups frozen corn kernels
  • 1 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1 cup sour cream
  • 1/2 cup diced onions
  • 1/2 cup diced red bell pepper
  • 1/2 cup diced green bell pepper
  • 1/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried thyme
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease a 9x13-inch baking dish with butter or cooking spray.

  2. In a large skillet, melt the butter over medium heat. Add the onions, red bell pepper, and green bell pepper. Sauté until the vegetables are tender, about 5 minutes.

  3. Sprinkle the flour over the vegetables and stir well to combine. Cook for an additional 2 minutes to cook off the raw flour taste.

  4. Gradually whisk in the sour cream and garlic powder until the mixture is smooth and well combined.

  5. Stir in the frozen corn, cheddar cheese, dried thyme, salt, and pepper. Cook for another 5 minutes, or until the cheese is melted and the mixture is heated through.

  6. Transfer the mixture to the prepared baking dish and spread it out evenly.

  7. Bake in the preheated oven for 30-35 minutes, or until the top is golden brown and the casserole is bubbling around the edges.

  8. Remove from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes before serving.

Serving Suggestions

This Scalloped Corn Casserole is a versatile dish that can be served as a main course or as a side dish. Here are a few serving suggestions to get you started:

  • Serve it alongside grilled chicken or steak for a hearty and satisfying meal.
  • Pair it with a fresh salad or steamed vegetables for a well-rounded and nutritious dinner.
  • Add it to your holiday menu for a unique and flavorful twist on traditional casseroles.
  • Enjoy it as a standalone dish for a comforting and indulgent treat.

Make it Your Own

While this recipe is delicious as is, feel free to make it your own by adding your favorite ingredients or making some substitutions. Here are a few ideas to get you inspired:

  • Add cooked bacon or diced ham for an extra savory kick.
  • Stir in some chopped jalapeños or hot sauce for a spicy twist.
  • Swap the cheddar cheese for Monterey Jack or Swiss cheese for a different flavor profile.
  • Experiment with different herbs and spices to suit your taste preferences.

Conclusion

This Savory and Creamy Scalloped Corn Casserole is a true crowd-pleaser that will bring comfort and joy to your family dinners. With its creamy texture, flavorful ingredients, and endless customization options, it’s no wonder this dish has become a family favorite. So, gather your loved ones around the table and savor every bite of this delicious casserole.
