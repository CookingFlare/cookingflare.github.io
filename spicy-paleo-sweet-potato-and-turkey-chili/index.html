Spicy Paleo Sweet Potato and Turkey Chili


Are you craving a hearty and flavorful meal that’s also healthy and satisfying? Look no further than this Spicy Paleo Sweet Potato and Turkey Chili recipe. Packed with protein, fiber, and a medley of spices, this chili is a perfect option for a cozy night in or a gathering with friends. Plus, it’s paleo-friendly, which means it’s free from grains, dairy, and processed ingredients, making it a great choice for those following a paleo lifestyle.

Ingredients

To make this delicious Spicy Paleo Sweet Potato and Turkey Chili, you will need the following ingredients:

  • 1 pound ground turkey
  • 2 medium sweet potatoes, peeled and diced
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 red bell pepper, diced
  • 1 can (14 ounces) diced tomatoes
  • 1 can (14 ounces) tomato sauce
  • 2 cups chicken broth
  • 1 tablespoon chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon cayenne pepper (adjust to taste for spiciness)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Avocado, cilantro, and lime wedges for garnish

Instructions

  1. In a large pot or Dutch oven, brown the ground turkey over medium heat until fully cooked. Remove the turkey from the pot and set it aside.

  2. In the same pot, add the diced onion and cook until translucent. Add the minced garlic and cook for an additional minute.

  3. Add the diced sweet potatoes and red bell pepper to the pot. Stir and cook for about 5 minutes, allowing the vegetables to soften slightly.

  4. Return the cooked ground turkey to the pot. Add the diced tomatoes, tomato sauce, and chicken broth. Stir well to combine.

  5. Season the chili with chili powder, cumin, paprika, cayenne pepper, salt, and pepper. Stir to distribute the spices evenly.

  6. Bring the chili to a gentle boil, then reduce the heat and let it simmer for 20-25 minutes, or until the sweet potatoes are tender.

  7. Once the sweet potatoes are cooked through and the flavors have melded together, taste the chili and adjust the seasoning if needed.

  8. Serve the Spicy Paleo Sweet Potato and Turkey Chili hot, garnished with sliced avocado, fresh cilantro, and a squeeze of lime juice.

Why This Recipe Works

This Spicy Paleo Sweet Potato and Turkey Chili is a fantastic option for anyone looking to enjoy a comforting, flavorful meal without compromising on their health goals. Here’s why this recipe works so well:

  • Protein-Packed: With the lean ground turkey as the star ingredient, this chili is loaded with protein, which is essential for muscle repair and overall well-being.

  • Nutrient-Dense: Sweet potatoes are rich in vitamins, minerals, and fiber, making them a nutritious addition to this chili. They add a natural sweetness and heartiness to the dish.

  • Balanced Spices: The combination of chili powder, cumin, paprika, and cayenne pepper creates a well-balanced blend of spices that adds depth and a kick of heat to the chili.

  • Paleo-Friendly: By using wholesome, unprocessed ingredients, this recipe aligns with the paleo diet principles, making it suitable for those following a paleo lifestyle.

Final Thoughts

This Spicy Paleo Sweet Potato and Turkey Chili is a versatile and satisfying dish that’s perfect for meal prep and can be enjoyed as leftovers. Whether you’re following a paleo diet or simply looking for a nourishing meal to warm you up, this chili is sure to become a favorite in your recipe collection. So, gather your ingredients, get cooking, and savor the delicious flavors of this wholesome and hearty chili!
