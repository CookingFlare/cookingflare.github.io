Vegetarian Instant Pot Recipes That Are Bursting with Flavor


If you’re a vegetarian and own an Instant Pot, you’re in for a treat! This versatile kitchen gadget can whip up delicious vegetarian meals in no time, while preserving the flavors and nutrients of your favorite ingredients. In this blog post, we’ll explore some mouthwatering vegetarian Instant Pot recipes that are guaranteed to satisfy your taste buds. So, let’s dive right in!

1. Creamy Coconut Curry Lentils

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup red lentils
  • 1 can coconut milk
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon curry powder
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh cilantro for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Rinse the lentils under cold water and drain well.
  2. Set your Instant Pot to sauté mode and add the diced onion and minced garlic. Sauté for 2-3 minutes until fragrant.
  3. Add the curry powder and turmeric, and stir well to coat the onions and garlic.
  4. Add the lentils, coconut milk, salt, and pepper to the Instant Pot. Stir everything together.
  5. Close the lid of the Instant Pot and set it to manual mode for 8 minutes.
  6. Once the cooking time is up, let the pressure naturally release for 5 minutes, and then carefully do a quick release.
  7. Serve the creamy coconut curry lentils over steamed rice or with naan bread. Garnish with fresh cilantro.

2. Spicy Black Bean Soup

Ingredients:

  • 1 can black beans, drained and rinsed
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 1 bell pepper, diced
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 can diced tomatoes
  • 2 cups vegetable broth
  • 1 tablespoon chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh lime wedges for serving

Instructions:

  1. Set your Instant Pot to sauté mode and add the diced onion, bell pepper, and minced garlic. Sauté until the vegetables are tender.
  2. Add the black beans, diced tomatoes, vegetable broth, chili powder, cumin, salt, and pepper to the Instant Pot. Stir well to combine.
  3. Close the lid of the Instant Pot and set it to manual mode for 10 minutes.
  4. Once the cooking time is up, let the pressure naturally release for 5 minutes, and then carefully do a quick release.
  5. Using an immersion blender, blend the soup until smooth and creamy.
  6. Serve the spicy black bean soup with a squeeze of fresh lime juice on top.

3. Vegetable Biryani

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup basmati rice
  • 1 onion, thinly sliced
  • 1 carrot, diced
  • 1 bell pepper, diced
  • 1 zucchini, diced
  • 1 cup frozen peas
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon biryani masala
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh cilantro for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Rinse the basmati rice under cold water until the water runs clear. Drain well.
  2. Set your Instant Pot to sauté mode and add the thinly sliced onion and minced garlic. Sauté until the onions are caramelized.
  3. Add the diced carrot, bell pepper, zucchini, frozen peas, biryani masala, turmeric, and salt to the Instant Pot. Stir well to coat the vegetables with the spices.
  4. Add the rinsed basmati rice to the Instant Pot and stir gently to combine with the vegetables.
  5. Pour 2 cups of water into the Instant Pot and give everything one final stir.
  6. Close the lid of the Instant Pot and set it to manual mode for 5 minutes.
  7. Once the cooking time is up, let the pressure naturally release for 10 minutes, and then carefully do a quick release.
  8. Fluff the vegetable biryani with a fork and garnish with fresh cilantro before serving.

Conclusion

These vegetarian Instant Pot recipes are perfect for busy weeknights when you’re craving a flavorful and nutritious meal. With the help of your Instant Pot, you can now enjoy delicious vegetarian dishes without spending hours in the kitchen. Give these recipes a try and let us know which one becomes your new favorite. Happy cooking!
