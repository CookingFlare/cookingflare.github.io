Authentic Vietnamese Fresh Spring Rolls


Are you craving a light, fresh, and flavorful dish that’s perfect for warm weather? Look no further than Authentic Vietnamese Fresh Spring Rolls! These delightful rolls, also known as “Goi Cuon,” are a staple of Vietnamese cuisine, offering a harmonious blend of textures and flavors in every bite. Whether you’re a seasoned cook or a beginner in the kitchen, this recipe is sure to impress your taste buds and guests.

Ingredients:

  • Rice Paper: The foundation of the spring rolls.
  • Shrimp: Cooked, peeled, and deveined.
  • Rice Vermicelli: Pre-cooked and cooled.
  • Lettuce: Crisp and fresh.
  • Mint Leaves: Fragrant and aromatic.
  • Cilantro: Adds a burst of flavor.
  • Bean Sprouts: For a crunchy texture.
  • Peanuts: Crushed for a nutty crunch.
  • Dipping Sauce: A mix of sweet, savory, and tangy flavors.

Instructions:

  1. Prep Ingredients:

    • Soak the rice vermicelli in warm water until softened, then drain.
    • Prepare all the vegetables by washing and chopping them into thin strips.
    • Peel and devein the shrimp, then cook them in boiling water until pink. Let them cool before slicing in half.

  2. Assemble the Rolls:

    • Fill a shallow dish with warm water. Dip a rice paper wrapper into the water for a few seconds until soft.
    • Place the softened rice paper on a clean surface. Layer lettuce leaves on the bottom half of the wrapper.
    • Add a small handful of rice vermicelli on top of the lettuce.
    • Top with shrimp halves, mint leaves, cilantro, bean sprouts, and a sprinkle of crushed peanuts.

  3. Roll the Spring Rolls:

    • Fold the sides of the rice paper over the filling, then tightly roll from the bottom to the top, enclosing the ingredients.
    • Repeat with the remaining ingredients.

  4. Serve:

    • Cut the rolls in half diagonally and serve with a side of homemade dipping sauce.

Dipping Sauce Recipe:

  • Ingredients:
    • Hoisin sauce, peanut butter, water, lime juice, garlic, and chili sauce.
  • Instructions:
    • Whisk all the ingredients together until smooth and well combined.

Tips for Perfect Spring Rolls:

  • Fresh Ingredients: Use the freshest produce and herbs for the best flavors.
  • Softened Rice Paper: Ensure the rice paper is pliable but not too wet when assembling.
  • Tightly Roll: Roll the spring rolls tightly to keep the fillings secure.
  • Experiment: Feel free to customize the fillings based on your preferences.

These Authentic Vietnamese Fresh Spring Rolls are a versatile dish that can be enjoyed as a light meal, appetizer, or party snack. They are not only delicious but also healthy and visually appealing. Impress your family and friends with this classic Vietnamese recipe that celebrates the vibrant and fresh flavors of the cuisine.

Get ready to embark on a culinary journey with these delightful spring rolls. Enjoy the process of assembling them and savoring each bite filled with the goodness of fresh ingredients. Happy rolling!
