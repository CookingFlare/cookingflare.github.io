Pumpkin Spice Chex Mix: A Hauntingly Tasty Snack


Are you ready to indulge in a hauntingly delicious snack that captures the essence of fall? Look no further than this Pumpkin Spice Chex Mix recipe! This delightful treat combines the warm and comforting flavors of pumpkin spice with the satisfying crunch of Chex cereal, creating a snack that’s perfect for cozy evenings, Halloween parties, or any time you’re craving a touch of autumn in a bowl. Let’s dive into the recipe and discover how to make this irresistible Pumpkin Spice Chex Mix.

Ingredients

  • 4 cups of Corn Chex cereal
  • 2 cups of pretzels
  • 1 cup of pecans
  • 1/2 cup of unsalted butter
  • 1/2 cup of brown sugar
  • 2 tablespoons of pumpkin pie spice
  • 1 teaspoon of vanilla extract
  • 1 cup of dried cranberries
  • 1 cup of white chocolate chips

Instructions

  1. Preheat and Prep: Start by preheating your oven to 250°F (120°C). Then, line a large baking sheet with parchment paper or a silicone baking mat.

  2. Mix and Melt: In a large mixing bowl, combine the Corn Chex cereal, pretzels, and pecans. In a separate microwave-safe bowl, melt the butter. Once melted, stir in the brown sugar, pumpkin pie spice, and vanilla extract until well combined.

  3. Coat and Bake: Pour the butter and spice mixture over the Chex cereal mixture, stirring gently to ensure everything is coated evenly. Spread the coated mixture onto the prepared baking sheet in an even layer. Place the sheet in the preheated oven and bake for 1 hour, stirring every 15 minutes to prevent sticking and ensure even baking.

  4. Cool and Add: Once the mix is golden and fragrant, remove it from the oven and allow it to cool completely. After cooling, gently stir in the dried cranberries and white chocolate chips, ensuring they are distributed throughout the mix.

  5. Serve and Enjoy: Your Pumpkin Spice Chex Mix is now ready to be served! Transfer it to an airtight container for storage, or portion it out into individual servings for a delightful snack on the go.

Hauntingly Delicious

This Pumpkin Spice Chex Mix recipe is a wonderful way to celebrate the flavors of fall in a fun and delicious snack. The combination of warm spices, crunchy cereal, and sweet additions like white chocolate chips and dried cranberries make this mix a true delight for the taste buds. Whether you’re hosting a Halloween gathering, enjoying a movie night, or simply want to add a touch of autumn to your day, this snack is sure to satisfy.

So, why not embrace the spirit of the season and whip up a batch of Pumpkin Spice Chex Mix? It’s a delightful treat that’s perfect for sharing with friends and family, or simply enjoying on a cozy evening at home. With its irresistible flavor and satisfying crunch, this snack is bound to become a favorite in your fall recipe collection.

Enjoy the magic of fall with every bite of this delightful Pumpkin Spice Chex Mix, and let the cozy, comforting flavors transport you to a world of autumnal bliss. Happy snacking!
