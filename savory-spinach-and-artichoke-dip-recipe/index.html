Savory Spinach and Artichoke Dip Recipe


If you’re looking for a crowd-pleasing appetizer that’s easy to make and packed with flavor, this savory spinach and artichoke dip recipe is a must-try. Whether you’re hosting a casual get-together or a formal dinner party, this creamy and indulgent dip is sure to be a hit with your guests. The combination of tender spinach, tangy artichokes, and gooey cheese creates a mouthwatering dish that’s perfect for dipping bread, crackers, or fresh vegetables.

Ingredients

  • 1 (10-ounce) package frozen chopped spinach, thawed and drained
  • 1 (14-ounce) can artichoke hearts, drained and chopped
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 cup mayonnaise
  • 1 cup sour cream
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 teaspoon onion powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 teaspoon red pepper flakes (optional)
  • 1 loaf of crusty bread, sliced, or crackers for serving

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).

  2. In a large mixing bowl, combine the drained spinach, chopped artichoke hearts, grated Parmesan cheese, mayonnaise, sour cream, shredded mozzarella cheese, minced garlic, and onion powder. Mix well until all the ingredients are thoroughly combined.

  3. Season the mixture with salt, pepper, and red pepper flakes (if using), adjusting the seasoning to taste.

  4. Transfer the mixture to a baking dish, spreading it out evenly to ensure even cooking. You can use a 9x9-inch baking dish or a similarly sized oven-safe dish.

  5. Place the baking dish in the preheated oven and bake for 25-30 minutes, or until the dip is hot and bubbly, and the top is golden brown.

  6. Once the dip is ready, remove it from the oven and allow it to cool for a few minutes before serving.

  7. While the dip is cooling, slice the crusty bread or prepare your choice of crackers for serving.

  8. Serve the savory spinach and artichoke dip warm, alongside the sliced bread or crackers, and watch it disappear in no time!

Tips and Variations

  • For an extra kick of flavor, consider adding a dash of hot sauce or a sprinkle of smoked paprika to the dip mixture.
  • If you prefer a smoother texture, you can use a food processor to blend the ingredients to your desired consistency before baking.
  • Experiment with different types of cheese, such as Gruyère or fontina, to customize the flavor profile of the dip.
  • To make this recipe vegetarian-friendly, ensure that the Parmesan cheese you use is free of animal rennet.

Conclusion

This savory spinach and artichoke dip is a versatile and delicious addition to any gathering. Its rich and creamy texture, combined with the earthy flavors of spinach and artichoke, makes it a standout appetizer that’s sure to impress. Whether you’re hosting a game night or a holiday party, this easy-to-make dip will quickly become a favorite among your friends and family. So, go ahead and whip up a batch of this irresistible dip for your next social event, and get ready to receive compliments and recipe requests!
