Wholesome Dairy-Free Zucchini Bread for a Healthy Snack


Are you looking for a delicious and healthy snack that’s easy to make and perfect for any time of day? Look no further than this wholesome dairy-free zucchini bread recipe. Packed with the goodness of fresh zucchini and a blend of flavorful spices, this bread is sure to become a favorite in your household. Whether you’re a seasoned baker or just starting out, this recipe is simple and yields a moist, flavorful loaf that’s perfect for breakfast, snacking, or even dessert.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups shredded zucchini
  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground nutmeg
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground cloves
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1/2 cup coconut oil, melted
  • 3/4 cup brown sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/2 cup chopped walnuts or pecans (optional)

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C) and grease a 9x5-inch loaf pan.

  2. In a large bowl, combine the shredded zucchini, eggs, melted coconut oil, brown sugar, and vanilla extract. Mix well until the ingredients are thoroughly combined.

  3. In a separate bowl, whisk together the flour, baking powder, baking soda, salt, cinnamon, nutmeg, and cloves.

  4. Gradually add the dry ingredients to the zucchini mixture, stirring until just combined. Be careful not to overmix.

  5. If desired, fold in the chopped nuts for an added crunch and nutty flavor.

  6. Pour the batter into the prepared loaf pan, spreading it out evenly.

  7. Bake for 55-65 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.

  8. Allow the zucchini bread to cool in the pan for 10-15 minutes, then transfer it to a wire rack to cool completely.

Tips and Variations

  • For a gluten-free option, use a 1:1 gluten-free flour blend in place of all-purpose flour.
  • Add a handful of dairy-free chocolate chips for a sweeter, indulgent twist.
  • Experiment with different spices such as cardamom or ginger for a unique flavor profile.
  • This zucchini bread freezes well, so consider making a double batch and storing some for later.

Health Benefits

Zucchini is a versatile and nutritious vegetable that adds moisture and a subtle sweetness to baked goods. It’s an excellent source of antioxidants, vitamin C, and fiber, making it a great addition to any diet. By using coconut oil in place of butter and eggs as a binding agent, this recipe offers a dairy-free alternative that’s lower in saturated fat and cholesterol, perfect for those with dietary restrictions or anyone looking to make healthier choices.

Conclusion

This wholesome dairy-free zucchini bread is a delightful way to enjoy a nutritious snack while satisfying your sweet tooth. Whether you’re baking for yourself, your family, or a special gathering, this recipe is certain to impress. With simple ingredients and straightforward instructions, you can whip up a loaf of this delectable bread in no time. So, grab your zucchinis and get ready to bake your way to a healthier, happier snacking experience!
