Spicy Thai Basil Pork Stir Fry


Are you craving a flavor-packed and aromatic dish that will transport your taste buds to the bustling streets of Thailand? Look no further than this Spicy Thai Basil Pork Stir Fry recipe. Bursting with vibrant colors and bold flavors, this dish is a perfect combination of heat, sweetness, and savory goodness. Let’s dive into how you can recreate this classic Thai favorite in your own kitchen!

Ingredients:

  • 500g pork loin, thinly sliced
  • 2 tablespoons oyster sauce
  • 3 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon fish sauce
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 onion, sliced
  • 1 red bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 yellow bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 cup basil leaves
  • 2-3 red chilies, sliced
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Marinate the Pork: In a bowl, combine the thinly sliced pork loin with oyster sauce, soy sauce, and fish sauce. Let it marinate for at least 30 minutes to allow the flavors to infuse.

  2. Heat the Wok: Heat vegetable oil in a wok or a large pan over medium-high heat. Add the minced garlic and sauté until fragrant.

  3. Stir Fry the Pork: Add the marinated pork to the wok and stir-fry until it is cooked through and slightly caramelized.

  4. Add Vegetables: Incorporate the sliced onion, red bell pepper, and yellow bell pepper into the wok. Stir-fry for a few minutes until the vegetables are slightly tender yet still crisp.

  5. Spice it Up: Toss in the sliced red chilies to bring the heat to the dish. Adjust the amount according to your spice preference for that extra kick.

  6. Finish with Basil: Add the fresh basil leaves to the stir fry and gently toss until the leaves are wilted but still vibrant green.

  7. Season to Perfection: Season the dish with salt and pepper to taste. Remember, the sauces used in the marinade already provide a salty umami flavor, so adjust accordingly.

  8. Serve and Enjoy: Transfer the Spicy Thai Basil Pork Stir Fry to a serving plate and relish the aroma that will surely make your mouth water. This dish pairs perfectly with steamed jasmine rice or noodles.

Tips and Variations:

  • Feel free to customize the level of spiciness by adjusting the amount of red chilies.
  • You can substitute pork with chicken, beef, or tofu for a different twist.
  • For a vegetarian option, omit the meat and double up on colorful vegetables like broccoli, snap peas, and mushrooms.

There you have it, a tantalizing Spicy Thai Basil Pork Stir Fry that is simple to prepare yet bursting with complex flavors. Whether you’re a fan of spicy foods or just looking to add a new recipe to your repertoire, this dish is sure to impress your family and friends. So, roll up your sleeves, grab your wok, and embark on a culinary journey to Thailand right in your own kitchen!

Remember, the key to a perfect stir fry lies in the freshness of ingredients and the quick cooking process, so don’t be afraid to experiment and make this recipe your own. Happy cooking and happy eating!
