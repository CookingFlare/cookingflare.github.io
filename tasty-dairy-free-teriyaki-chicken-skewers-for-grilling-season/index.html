Tasty Dairy-Free Teriyaki Chicken Skewers for Grilling Season


Summer is here, and that means it’s time to fire up the grill and enjoy some delicious outdoor cooking. If you’re looking for a tasty and dairy-free option, these Teriyaki Chicken Skewers are just what you need. Packed with flavor and easy to make, these skewers are perfect for grilling season.

Ingredients

  • 1 ½ pounds boneless, skinless chicken breasts, cut into 1-inch cubes
  • ½ cup low-sodium soy sauce or tamari for a gluten-free option
  • ¼ cup honey or maple syrup for a vegan option
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon grated fresh ginger
  • 2 tablespoons rice vinegar
  • 2 tablespoons sesame oil
  • 1 tablespoon cornstarch
  • 1 tablespoon water
  • Bamboo skewers, soaked in water for 30 minutes

Instructions

  1. In a small bowl, whisk together the soy sauce, honey or maple syrup, minced garlic, grated ginger, rice vinegar, and sesame oil. This will be your teriyaki marinade.
  2. Place the chicken cubes in a large zip-top bag and pour the marinade over them. Seal the bag and massage gently to ensure all the chicken is coated. Marinate in the refrigerator for at least 1 hour, or overnight for maximum flavor.
  3. Preheat your grill to medium-high heat. If using a charcoal grill, wait until the coals are ashed over.
  4. While the grill is heating, prepare the skewers by threading the marinated chicken cubes onto the soaked bamboo skewers.
  5. In a small saucepan, whisk together the cornstarch and water to create a slurry. Place the saucepan over medium heat and cook, stirring constantly, until the mixture thickens and becomes glossy. This will be your glaze for the chicken skewers.
  6. Once the grill is hot, place the chicken skewers on the grill grates and cook for 4-5 minutes per side, or until the chicken is cooked through and nicely charred.
  7. During the last few minutes of grilling, brush the glaze onto the chicken skewers, turning occasionally to ensure even coating. This will add an extra layer of flavor and caramelization.
  8. Remove the chicken skewers from the grill and let them rest for a few minutes before serving. This will allow the juices to redistribute and ensure maximum tenderness.
  9. Serve the Tasty Dairy-Free Teriyaki Chicken Skewers hot off the grill, garnished with sesame seeds and chopped green onions for added freshness and crunch.

Tips and Variations

  • If you prefer a sweeter teriyaki sauce, you can increase the amount of honey or maple syrup in the marinade.
  • For an extra kick of heat, add a pinch of red pepper flakes or a few dashes of hot sauce to the marinade.
  • Feel free to customize your skewers by adding your favorite vegetables, such as bell peppers, onions, or zucchini, between the chicken cubes.
  • If you don’t have a grill, you can also cook these skewers on a stovetop grill pan or in the oven under the broiler.
  • Serve the skewers with steamed rice or a refreshing Asian-inspired salad for a complete and satisfying meal.

Enjoy these Tasty Dairy-Free Teriyaki Chicken Skewers for your next grilling adventure. The combination of juicy chicken, flavorful marinade, and the smoky grill will surely make your taste buds dance with joy. So gather your friends and family, fire up the grill, and savor the deliciousness of this dairy-free summer delight. Happy grilling!
