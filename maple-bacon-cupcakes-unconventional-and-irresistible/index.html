Maple Bacon Cupcakes: Unconventional and Irresistible


Ah, cupcakes. For many, these delightful morsels of sweetness are a comfort food classic - the perfect pick-me-up for an afternoon tea break or a delightfully sinful indulgence for a birthday celebration. As with any beloved culinary classic, ingenious bakers tend to experiment and create unique twists to provide their tastebuds with an unforgettable experience. And that’s where our very own maple bacon cupcakes come into the picture. Combining the smoky, salty notes of crispy bacon, with the rich, sticky sweetness of maple syrup, this is a match made in heaven for anyone looking to delight a crowd of both sweet and savory gourmet enthusiasts.

Ingredients

For the Cupcakes:

  • 1 ½ cups all-purpose flour
  • ¾ cup granulated sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • ⅓ cup unsalted butter, melted
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 ½ teaspoons baking powder
  • ¼ teaspoon baking soda
  • ¼ teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • ½ cup buttermilk
  • 2 tbsp maple syrup

For the Maple Bacon Frosting:

  • 8 oz cream cheese, softened
  • ½ cup unsalted butter, softened
  • ¼ cup maple syrup
  • 3 ½ cups powdered sugar
  • ¼ teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup crumbled bacon

Instructions

Start by cooking the bacon:

  • Cook the bacon in a large heavy skillet over medium heat until crisp. Transfer the bacon to a paper-towel-lined plate to drain excess grease.
  • Finely chop the cooked bacon and set aside.

Prepare the Cupcakes:

  1. Preheat your oven to 350 degrees Fahrenheit (175 degrees Celsius). Position the rack in the center of the oven and line 18 muffin cups with parchment liners.
  2. In a medium bowl, whisk together the flour, sugar, eggs, melted butter, olive oil, baking powder, baking soda, salt, and vanilla extract. Mix until smooth and well combined.
  3. To the dry ingredients, add the buttermilk and maple syrup. Mix gently until fully incorporated. Be careful not to over mix the batter, as this can lead to chewy cupcakes.
  4. Divide the batter evenly among the prepared muffin cups, filling each about halfway. Each cup should hold approximately ¼ cup of batter.
  5. Bake the cupcakes for 18-20 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted in the center comes out clean.
  6. Remove the cupcakes from the oven and allow them to cool in the pan for 5 minutes, then use a knife to loosen the edges of the liners and transfer them to a wire rack to cool completely.

Maple Bacon Frosting:

  1. In a medium bowl, beat the cream cheese and butter until smooth and creamy.
  2. Stir in the maple syrup and salt, then begin to gradually add the powdered sugar, 1 cup at a time, beat until fluffy and well-combined.
  3. Fold in the crumbled bacon into the frosting gently and continue storing until ready to use.

Frost & Serve:

  1. Once the cupcakes have cooled completely, pipe the maple bacon frosting onto each cupcake using a piping bag fitted with your desired tip.
  2. Top each frosted cupcake with a small sprinkle of bacon for decoration, and optionally, a tiny drizzle of maple syrup.

You now have your own irresistible and unconventional maple bacon cupcakes. Share them at your next gathering or keep them all to yourself – however you choose to indulge, these dainty desserts are sure to delight.

Remember, baking is an art, and each ingredient you add to the mix is a brushstroke on your creation’s palette. Experiment with the intensity of flavors or degrees of sweetness until you find the perfect balance for your taste buds. Enjoy the process as much as you’ll enjoy the final product. Happy Baking!
