Delicious Desserts for BBQ Parties


Fire up the grill, gather your friends and family, and get ready to indulge in mouthwatering dessert favorites! Whether you’re hosting a casual barbecue or an elaborate outdoor bash, a delectable spread of sweets is a must-have for any summer gathering. In this post, we’ll uncover some scrumptious and easy-to-make dessert ideas that will leave your guests begging for more.

  1. Grilled Pineapple with Brown Sugar and Cinnamon

Prep time: 5 minutes Cook time: 8-10 minutes Servings: 4

Ingredients:

  • 1 large pineapple, sliced into 1-inch-thick rounds
  • 1/4 cup brown sugar
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 cup melted butter

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your grill to medium heat.

  2. In a small bowl, combine brown sugar and cinnamon.

  3. Brush both sides of pineapple slices with melted butter.

  4. Coat each slice with the brown sugar mixture, pressing gently to adhere.

  5. Grill the pineapple slices for 8-10 minutes, turning once, until caramelized and tender.

  6. Remove from the grill and let cool for a few minutes.

  7. Slice into wedges and serve with a scoop of vanilla ice cream for a delightful dessert!

  8. S’mores Brownie Bars

Prep time: 15 minutes Cook time: 25-30 minutes Servings: 12

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, room temperature
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 tablespoon unsaturated room-temperature water
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 teaspoon pure vanilla extract
  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2/3 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup semisweet chocolate chips
  • Graham crackers and marshmallows for assembly

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (177°C) and line a 9x13-inch baking dish with parchment paper.

  2. In a large bowl, cream together butter and sugar until well combined.

  3. Mix in eggs and vanilla extract.

  4. In a separate bowl, whisk together flour, cocoa powder, baking powder, and salt.

  5. Gradually stir dry ingredients into wet ingredients, ensuring not to overmix.

  6. Fold in chocolate chips.

  7. Pour the brownie batter into the prepared baking dish and smooth the top.

  8. Bake for 25-30 minutes or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.

  9. Remove from the oven and let cool completely.

  10. Cut into bars and serve individually with a graham cracker and a few marshmallows on top!

  11. Homemade Ice Cream Sandwiches

Prep time: 10 minutes Freeze time: At least 4 hours Servings: 6

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 2/3 cup whole milk
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 2 teaspoons pure vanilla extract
  • 1 1/2 cups cookie crumbs (choose your preferred cookie)
  • 1/2 cup softened ice cream (choose your preferred flavor)

Instructions:

  1. In a large bowl, whip the heavy cream and milk together until soft peaks form.
  2. Gradually add sugar and vanilla extract, whisking continuously.
  3. Once all the sugar is incorporated, continue whipping until the mixture is stiff.
  4. Fold in softened ice cream until fully combined.
  5. Line a loaf pan or baking dish with wax paper and spread about 1/3 of the ice cream mixture onto the bottom.
  6. Sprinkle 1/2 cup of cookie crumbs evenly over the ice cream layer.
  7. Repeat these steps for two more layers, topping the final layer with the remaining cookie crumbs.
  8. Freeze the ice cream sandwiches for at least 4 hours or overnight.
  9. Remove from the freezer and cut into individual portions.
  10. If desired, re-whip any remaining ice cream mixture and mold into balls or other shapes for additional treats!

As the sun sets and the last grill marks fade away, your friends and family will still be talking about these delicious dessert options that took your barbecue party to new heights. So gather your ingredients, get creative, and show them that you’re not just a great grill-master, but also a Dessert Extraordinaire! Enjoy the sweetest moments of your summer BBQ parties with these incredible treats.
