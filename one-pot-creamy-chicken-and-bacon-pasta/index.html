One Pot Creamy Chicken and Bacon Pasta


Creamy, flavorful, and oh-so-satisfying, this One Pot Creamy Chicken and Bacon Pasta is the ultimate comfort food. With just a handful of ingredients and a single pot, you can whip up a delicious meal that will impress even the pickiest eaters. Say goodbye to multiple pans and endless clean-up, because this recipe is all about simplicity without compromising on taste.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound boneless, skinless chicken breasts, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 8 ounces bacon, chopped
  • 1 medium onion, diced
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 cups chicken broth
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 8 ounces penne pasta
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • Fresh parsley, for garnish

Instructions

  1. In a large pot or Dutch oven, cook the bacon over medium heat until crispy. Remove the bacon from the pot and set aside on a paper towel-lined plate to drain the excess grease.

  2. In the same pot, add the diced chicken and cook until browned and cooked through. Remove the chicken from the pot and set aside.

  3. Add the diced onion to the pot and cook until translucent, about 5 minutes. Stir in the minced garlic and cook for an additional minute.

  4. Pour in the chicken broth and heavy cream, and bring to a boil. Add the penne pasta and cook according to package instructions, until al dente.

  5. Once the pasta is cooked, stir in the cooked chicken and crispy bacon. Add the grated Parmesan cheese and stir until melted and creamy. Season with salt and pepper to taste.

  6. Remove the pot from the heat and let it sit for a few minutes to allow the flavors to meld together.

  7. Serve the One Pot Creamy Chicken and Bacon Pasta hot, garnished with fresh parsley for a pop of color.

Tips and Variations

  • To add some extra flavor, try adding a teaspoon of dried Italian seasoning or a pinch of red pepper flakes while cooking the onions and garlic.
  • If you prefer a lighter version, you can substitute half-and-half or whole milk for the heavy cream. The sauce will be slightly less creamy but still delicious.
  • Feel free to customize this recipe by adding your favorite vegetables. Sautéed mushrooms, diced bell peppers, or steamed broccoli are all fantastic additions.

Why You’ll Love This Recipe

  1. One Pot Wonder: With only one pot to clean, this recipe makes cooking and cleaning up a breeze. No more piles of dishes to tackle after a delicious meal!

  2. Creamy and Flavorful: The combination of chicken, bacon, and creamy Parmesan sauce creates a rich and indulgent dish that will leave you wanting more.

  3. Quick and Easy: This recipe comes together in under 30 minutes, making it perfect for busy weeknights when you need a satisfying meal in a hurry.

  4. Family-Friendly: With its familiar flavors and comforting creaminess, this One Pot Creamy Chicken and Bacon Pasta is sure to please even the pickiest eaters in your family.

So, gather your ingredients, grab a pot, and get ready to enjoy a mouthwatering bowl of One Pot Creamy Chicken and Bacon Pasta. It’s the perfect dish to satisfy your cravings and impress your loved ones. Enjoy!
