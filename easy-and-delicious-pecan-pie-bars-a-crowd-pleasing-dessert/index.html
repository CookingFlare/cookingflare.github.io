Easy and Delicious Pecan Pie Bars: A Crowd-Pleasing Dessert


If you’re looking for a dessert that will satisfy your sweet tooth and impress your friends and family, look no further than these Easy and Delicious Pecan Pie Bars. With a buttery crust and a gooey pecan filling, these bars are a crowd-pleaser that will have everyone coming back for seconds.

Ingredients

  • For the crust:

    • 2 cups all-purpose flour
    • 1/2 cup powdered sugar
    • 1 cup unsalted butter, cold and cubed

  • For the filling:

    • 1 cup light corn syrup
    • 1 cup packed light brown sugar
    • 1/3 cup unsalted butter, melted
    • 3 large eggs, beaten
    • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
    • 2 cups pecans, chopped

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C) and line a 9x13-inch baking dish with parchment paper.

  2. In a large bowl, combine the flour and powdered sugar for the crust. Add the cold and cubed butter and use a pastry cutter or your hands to cut the butter into the flour mixture until it resembles coarse crumbs.

  3. Press the crust mixture evenly into the bottom of the prepared baking dish. Bake for 15 minutes, or until lightly golden.

  4. While the crust is baking, prepare the filling. In a medium-sized bowl, whisk together the corn syrup, brown sugar, melted butter, beaten eggs, and vanilla extract until well combined.

  5. Stir in the chopped pecans until evenly distributed throughout the filling.

  6. Once the crust is done baking, pour the pecan filling over the hot crust. Spread it out evenly using a spatula.

  7. Return the baking dish to the oven and bake for an additional 25-30 minutes, or until the filling is set and slightly golden on top.

  8. Remove the pecan pie bars from the oven and let them cool completely in the baking dish.

  9. Once cooled, lift the bars out of the dish using the parchment paper and transfer them to a cutting board. Use a sharp knife to cut them into squares or rectangles.

  10. Serve the Easy and Delicious Pecan Pie Bars at room temperature. They can be enjoyed on their own, or you can add a dollop of whipped cream or a scoop of vanilla ice cream for an extra indulgence.

Tips and Variations

  • For a twist on the classic recipe, try adding a sprinkling of chocolate chips to the pecan filling before baking. The melted chocolate will add a delightful richness to the bars.

  • If you prefer a more caramel-like flavor, substitute dark corn syrup for the light corn syrup in the filling. The darker syrup will give the bars a deeper, more complex taste.

  • To make these bars ahead of time, simply bake and cool them as directed. Once cooled, store them in an airtight container at room temperature for up to three days.

  • If you’re making these bars for a special occasion, consider drizzling them with a homemade caramel sauce or a dusting of powdered sugar for an extra touch of elegance.

Enjoy making and sharing these Easy and Delicious Pecan Pie Bars with your loved ones. They’re sure to become a favorite dessert in your household and a go-to recipe for any occasion. Happy baking!
