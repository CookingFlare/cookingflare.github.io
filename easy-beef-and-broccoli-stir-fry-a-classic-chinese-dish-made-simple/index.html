Easy Beef and Broccoli Stir Fry: A Classic Chinese Dish Made Simple


If you’re a fan of Chinese cuisine, you’ve probably tried Beef and Broccoli Stir Fry at least once. This classic dish is known for its flavorful combination of tender beef, crisp broccoli, and a savory sauce that brings it all together. While it may seem intimidating to recreate this dish at home, I’m here to show you just how easy it can be!

Ingredients

To make this Easy Beef and Broccoli Stir Fry, you’ll need the following ingredients:

  • 1 pound of beef (sirloin or flank steak works great)
  • 2 cups of broccoli florets
  • 2 tablespoons of vegetable oil
  • 3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon of fresh ginger, grated
  • 1/4 cup of soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons of oyster sauce
  • 1 tablespoon of cornstarch
  • 1/4 cup of water
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Start by slicing the beef into thin strips. Make sure to cut against the grain for the most tender results.

  2. In a small bowl, whisk together the soy sauce, oyster sauce, cornstarch, and water. Set this sauce aside for later.

  3. Heat a large skillet or wok over high heat. Add the vegetable oil and swirl it around to coat the pan.

  4. Once the oil is hot, add the minced garlic and grated ginger. Stir-fry for about 30 seconds until fragrant.

  5. Add the beef to the pan and season with salt and pepper. Stir-fry the beef until it’s browned on all sides, about 2-3 minutes.

  6. Remove the beef from the pan and set it aside. In the same pan, add the broccoli florets and stir-fry for about 2 minutes until they’re bright green and slightly tender.

  7. Return the beef to the pan and pour in the sauce mixture. Stir everything together until the beef and broccoli are coated in the sauce.

  8. Cook for an additional 2-3 minutes until the sauce has thickened and the beef is cooked to your liking. If the sauce is too thick, you can add a little more water to thin it out.

  9. Serve the Easy Beef and Broccoli Stir Fry over steamed rice or noodles for a complete meal. Garnish with sliced green onions or sesame seeds if desired.

Tips and Variations

  • For an extra kick of flavor, you can add a teaspoon of chili garlic sauce or red pepper flakes to the sauce mixture.

  • Feel free to customize this dish by adding other vegetables such as bell peppers, carrots, or mushrooms. Just make sure to adjust the cooking time accordingly.

  • If you prefer a vegetarian version, you can substitute the beef with tofu or seitan. Simply stir-fry them until they’re golden brown before adding the sauce and broccoli.

  • To save time, you can prep the ingredients ahead of time. Slice the beef and chop the garlic and ginger in advance, so everything is ready to go when it’s time to cook.

  • Leftovers can be stored in an airtight container in the refrigerator for up to 3 days. Reheat in a skillet or microwave until heated through.

Now that you know how to make Easy Beef and Broccoli Stir Fry, there’s no need to order takeout anymore. This simple recipe allows you to enjoy the flavors of your favorite Chinese dish in the comfort of your own home. So, grab your wok and get cooking!
