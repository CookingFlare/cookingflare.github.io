Perfect Pairings: Wine and Grilled Pizza Recipes


When it comes to enjoying a delicious meal, few things compare to the perfect pairing of wine and grilled pizza. The combination of smoky flavors from the grill and the rich, complex notes of wine creates a culinary experience that is truly unforgettable. In this blog post, we will explore some mouthwatering grilled pizza recipes and suggest the ideal wine pairings to elevate your dining experience.

Grilled Margherita Pizza

Ingredients:

  • Pizza dough
  • Fresh mozzarella cheese
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • Tomato sauce
  • Olive oil
  • Salt and pepper

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your grill to medium-high heat.
  2. Roll out the pizza dough into a circle or your desired shape.
  3. Brush one side of the dough with olive oil and place it on the grill, oiled side down.
  4. Grill the dough for about 2-3 minutes, or until it starts to bubble and lightly char.
  5. Flip the dough over and spread a thin layer of tomato sauce on the grilled side.
  6. Add slices of fresh mozzarella cheese on top of the sauce.
  7. Close the grill and cook for an additional 3-4 minutes, or until the cheese has melted and the crust is golden brown.
  8. Remove the pizza from the grill and top with fresh basil leaves.
  9. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  10. Slice and serve hot.

Wine Pairing: For this classic Margherita pizza, a light-bodied red wine like a Pinot Noir or a medium-bodied white wine such as a Chardonnay would be an excellent choice. The fruity and earthy notes of the Pinot Noir complement the fresh tomato and basil flavors, while the buttery texture of the Chardonnay enhances the creaminess of the mozzarella cheese.

Grilled BBQ Chicken Pizza

Ingredients:

  • Pizza dough
  • Grilled or cooked chicken breast, shredded
  • BBQ sauce
  • Red onion, thinly sliced
  • Fresh cilantro leaves
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Olive oil
  • Salt and pepper

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your grill to medium-high heat.
  2. Roll out the pizza dough into a circle or your desired shape.
  3. Brush one side of the dough with olive oil and place it on the grill, oiled side down.
  4. Grill the dough for about 2-3 minutes, or until it starts to bubble and lightly char.
  5. Flip the dough over and spread a generous layer of BBQ sauce on the grilled side.
  6. Add the shredded chicken, sliced red onion, and mozzarella cheese on top of the sauce.
  7. Close the grill and cook for an additional 3-4 minutes, or until the cheese has melted and the crust is golden brown.
  8. Remove the pizza from the grill and garnish with fresh cilantro leaves.
  9. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  10. Slice and serve hot.

Wine Pairing: The bold and tangy flavors of the BBQ chicken pizza call for a wine with a bit more intensity. A medium-bodied red wine such as a Zinfandel or a Malbec would be a fantastic choice. The ripe fruit flavors and smoky undertones of the Zinfandel complement the BBQ sauce, while the robust character of the Malbec pairs beautifully with the grilled chicken.

Grilled Vegetable Pizza

Ingredients:

  • Pizza dough
  • Assorted vegetables (bell peppers, zucchini, eggplant, etc.)
  • Olive oil
  • Balsamic glaze
  • Goat cheese
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • Salt and pepper

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your grill to medium-high heat.
  2. Slice the vegetables into thin strips and toss them with olive oil, salt, and pepper.
  3. Grill the vegetables for about 3-4 minutes on each side, or until they are tender and lightly charred.
  4. Roll out the pizza dough into a circle or your desired shape.
  5. Brush one side of the dough with olive oil and place it on the grill, oiled side down.
  6. Grill the dough for about 2-3 minutes, or until it starts to bubble and lightly char.
  7. Flip the dough over and arrange the grilled vegetables on top.
  8. Crumble the goat cheese over the vegetables and drizzle with balsamic glaze.
  9. Close the grill and cook for an additional 3-4 minutes, or until the cheese has softened and the crust is golden brown.
  10. Remove the pizza from the grill and garnish with fresh basil leaves.
  11. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  12. Slice and serve hot.

Wine Pairing: For this vegetarian delight, a crisp and refreshing white wine like a Sauvignon Blanc or a dry Rosé would be an excellent choice. The citrusy and herbal notes of the Sauvignon Blanc complement the grilled vegetables, while the fruity and floral characteristics of the Rosé enhance the creamy goat cheese.

Conclusion

Grilled pizza is a culinary masterpiece that combines the smoky flavors of the grill with the deliciousness of pizza. By pairing your grilled pizzas with the right wine, you can elevate your dining experience to new heights. Whether you prefer a classic Margherita, a tangy BBQ chicken, or a flavorful vegetable pizza, there’s a wine out there that perfectly complements your creation. So fire up the grill, uncork a bottle of your favorite wine, and indulge in the perfect pairing of grilled pizza and wine. Cheers!
