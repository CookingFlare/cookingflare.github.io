Spicy and Tangy Chicken Mulligatawny Soup Recipes for Indian Cuisine Lovers


Are you a fan of Indian cuisine? Do you crave the bold flavors and aromatic spices that make Indian dishes so special? If so, then you’re in for a treat! Today, we’re going to dive into the world of Indian soups and explore the tantalizing flavors of Spicy and Tangy Chicken Mulligatawny Soup.

What is Mulligatawny Soup?

Mulligatawny Soup is a popular dish in Indian cuisine that originated from the Tamil word “milagu” meaning pepper and “tanni” meaning water. Traditionally, this soup is made with a combination of lentils, vegetables, and fragrant spices, creating a flavorful and satisfying bowl of goodness. The addition of chicken adds an extra layer of richness and protein to this already hearty soup.

Ingredients

To make this Spicy and Tangy Chicken Mulligatawny Soup, you’ll need the following ingredients:

  • 2 tablespoons of vegetable oil
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1-inch piece of ginger, grated
  • 1 teaspoon of ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon of ground coriander
  • 1/2 teaspoon of turmeric powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon of chili powder (adjust according to your spice preference)
  • 2 tomatoes, diced
  • 1 carrot, diced
  • 1 potato, diced
  • 1 cup of red lentils, rinsed
  • 4 cups of chicken broth
  • 2 cups of cooked chicken, shredded
  • Juice of 1 lemon
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh cilantro for garnish

Instructions

  1. Heat the vegetable oil in a large pot over medium heat. Add the chopped onion, minced garlic, and grated ginger. Sauté until the onion becomes translucent and the mixture becomes fragrant.

  2. Add the ground cumin, ground coriander, turmeric powder, and chili powder to the pot. Stir well to coat the onion mixture with the spices. Let it cook for a minute or two to enhance the flavors.

  3. Now, add the diced tomatoes, carrot, and potato to the pot. Stir everything together and let it cook for a few minutes until the vegetables start to soften.

  4. Add the rinsed red lentils and chicken broth to the pot. Stir well and bring the mixture to a boil. Reduce the heat to low and let it simmer for about 20-25 minutes, or until the lentils are cooked and the vegetables are tender.

  5. Once the soup has thickened and the lentils and vegetables are cooked, add the shredded cooked chicken to the pot. Stir well and let it simmer for another 5 minutes to allow the flavors to meld together.

  6. Finally, squeeze the juice of one lemon into the pot and season with salt and pepper to taste. Give it a final stir and remove it from the heat.

  7. Ladle the Spicy and Tangy Chicken Mulligatawny Soup into bowls and garnish with fresh cilantro.

Enjoy the Flavors of India!

There you have it! A delicious and aromatic Spicy and Tangy Chicken Mulligatawny Soup that will transport you to the vibrant streets of India. This soup is perfect for those chilly nights when you’re craving something comforting yet exciting.

The blend of spices, the creamy lentils, and the tender chicken come together to create a symphony of flavors that will leave you wanting more. So gather your ingredients, put on your apron, and get ready to embark on a culinary adventure through the heart of Indian cuisine.

Remember, you can always adjust the spice levels according to your preference. Feel free to add more chili powder if you like it hotter or reduce it if you prefer a milder flavor. Mulligatawny Soup is all about personalization, so make it your own!

So, what are you waiting for? Spice up your kitchen and indulge in the flavors of India with this Spicy and Tangy Chicken Mulligatawny Soup. Happy cooking, Indian cuisine lovers!
