Easy Cheesy Garlic Breadsticks Recipe


If you’re a fan of cheesy, garlicky goodness in the form of breadsticks, you’re in for a treat with this easy recipe! These homemade cheesy garlic breadsticks are perfect for a cozy night in, a family gathering, or as a delicious side to your favorite pasta dish. The combination of gooey cheese, aromatic garlic, and fluffy bread is sure to impress your taste buds and leave you craving more. Let’s dive into the step-by-step guide to creating these irresistible cheesy garlic breadsticks right in your own kitchen.

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound pizza dough
  • 1/4 cup unsalted butter, melted
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1/2 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • Fresh parsley, chopped (for garnish)

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the Oven: Start by preheating your oven to 400°F (200°C) and lining a baking sheet with parchment paper.

  2. Prepare the Dough: Roll out the pizza dough on a lightly floured surface to your desired thickness for the breadsticks. Transfer the dough to the prepared baking sheet.

  3. Make the Garlic Butter: In a small bowl, combine the melted butter, minced garlic, salt, pepper, oregano, and basil. Mix well to ensure the garlic and seasonings are evenly distributed.

  4. Add the Toppings: Brush the garlic butter mixture evenly over the rolled-out dough. Sprinkle the shredded mozzarella and grated Parmesan cheese over the buttered dough, ensuring they are spread out evenly.

  5. Cut the Dough: Using a sharp knife or pizza cutter, slice the dough into strips to form breadsticks. You can make them as thick or thin as you prefer.

  6. Bake to Perfection: Place the baking sheet in the preheated oven and bake the breadsticks for about 15-20 minutes, or until the cheese is melted and bubbly, and the breadsticks are golden brown.

  7. Garnish and Serve: Once out of the oven, sprinkle the freshly chopped parsley over the hot cheesy garlic breadsticks for a pop of color and freshness. Serve warm and enjoy!

These easy cheesy garlic breadsticks are best served fresh out of the oven when the cheese is gooey and the flavors are at their peak. They make a fantastic appetizer for parties, a tasty snack for movie nights, or a delightful accompaniment to your favorite meals. Feel free to customize the recipe by adding other toppings like pepperoni, olives, or even more cheese for an extra indulgent treat.

Next time you’re craving a comforting and flavorful snack, whip up a batch of these homemade cheesy garlic breadsticks. They are simple to make, require minimal ingredients, and are guaranteed to be a hit with your family and friends. Get ready to enjoy a taste of cheesy, garlicky heaven in every bite!
