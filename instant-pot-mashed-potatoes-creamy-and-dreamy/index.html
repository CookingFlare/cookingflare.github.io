Instant Pot Mashed Potatoes: Creamy and Dreamy


If you’re a fan of comfort food, then you’re in for a treat! Instant Pot mashed potatoes are a game-changer when it comes to whipping up this classic side dish. Not only are they incredibly creamy and dreamy, but they’re also a breeze to make. Whether you’re hosting a dinner party or simply craving a hearty meal, these Instant Pot mashed potatoes are sure to become a staple in your recipe collection.

Why Instant Pot Mashed Potatoes?

The Instant Pot has revolutionized the way we cook, and mashed potatoes are no exception. Traditionally, making mashed potatoes involved boiling the potatoes in a pot of water, draining them, and then mashing them by hand. With the Instant Pot, this process is streamlined and significantly faster. Plus, the potatoes retain more of their natural flavor and nutrients when cooked in a pressure cooker, resulting in a richer and more vibrant taste.

Ingredients

To make these creamy and dreamy Instant Pot mashed potatoes, you’ll need the following ingredients:

  • 3 pounds of Yukon Gold potatoes, peeled and cut into chunks
  • 1 cup of chicken or vegetable broth
  • 1/2 cup of unsalted butter, cubed
  • 1/2 cup of sour cream
  • 1/4 cup of whole milk
  • 4 cloves of garlic, minced
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Chopped chives for garnish (optional)

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Potatoes: Start by peeling the Yukon Gold potatoes and cutting them into chunks. Rinse the potato chunks under cold water to remove excess starch.

  2. Pressure Cook the Potatoes: Place the potato chunks in the Instant Pot and add the chicken or vegetable broth. Close the lid and set the Instant Pot to high pressure for 8 minutes. Once the cooking cycle is complete, perform a quick pressure release.

  3. Mash and Mix: Carefully open the Instant Pot and drain any excess liquid from the potatoes. Add the cubed butter, sour cream, minced garlic, and whole milk to the pot. Using a potato masher or a hand mixer, mash the potatoes until they reach your desired consistency. Season with salt and pepper to taste.

  4. Serve and Garnish: Transfer the creamy mashed potatoes to a serving dish and garnish with chopped chives for a pop of color and added flavor.

Tips for Perfect Mashed Potatoes

  • Use Yukon Gold potatoes for their naturally creamy texture and rich flavor.
  • Be mindful not to overmix the potatoes, as this can make them gluey. Stop mashing once they reach a smooth and creamy consistency.
  • Experiment with different add-ins such as cream cheese, Parmesan, or roasted garlic for a unique twist on classic mashed potatoes.

In Conclusion

Instant Pot mashed potatoes are a delightful addition to any meal, offering a silky-smooth texture and a buttery, garlicky flavor. With the convenience of the Instant Pot, you can whip up this beloved side dish in a fraction of the time it would traditionally take. Whether you’re serving them alongside a Sunday roast or as a comforting weeknight side, these creamy and dreamy Instant Pot mashed potatoes are sure to impress your family and friends.

So, fire up your Instant Pot and get ready to indulge in the velvety goodness of these irresistible mashed potatoes. Once you try this recipe, you may never go back to the traditional method again!
