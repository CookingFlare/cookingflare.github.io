Baked Cod with Lemon and Herbs: A Paleo-Approved Recipe for a Quick and Easy Dinner


When it comes to cooking a quick and easy dinner, few options can rival the simplicity and flavor of baked cod. This recipe combines the delicate taste of cod with the brightness of lemon and the earthiness of herbs, making it a perfect fit for a paleo diet. In this post, we’ll explore the benefits of using cod, the importance of choosing the right herbs, and the ease of preparation that makes this recipe a staple for busy weeknights.

The Benefits of Using Cod

Cod is an excellent source of protein, low in fat, and rich in omega-3 fatty acids. It’s also a mild-flavored fish that pairs well with a variety of seasonings and ingredients. For those following a paleo diet, cod is an excellent choice as it’s free from added preservatives, artificial flavors, and other unhealthy additives found in many commercial fish products.

Choosing the Right Herbs

When it comes to choosing the right herbs for this recipe, the options are endless. However, we’ll focus on a few popular herbs that complement the flavor of cod perfectly. Fresh parsley, dill, and thyme are all excellent choices, as they add a bright, citrusy flavor to the dish. You can also experiment with other herbs like basil, oregano, or rosemary to find the combination that suits your taste buds the best.

The Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 4 cod fillets (6 oz each)
  • 2 lemons, juiced
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 tbsp chopped fresh parsley
  • 2 tbsp chopped fresh dill
  • 2 tbsp chopped fresh thyme
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 tsp garlic powder (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C).
  2. Rinse the cod fillets under cold water, pat them dry with paper towels, and place them on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper.
  3. Drizzle the olive oil over the cod, making sure each fillet is coated evenly.
  4. Sprinkle the chopped parsley, dill, and thyme over the cod, dividing the herbs evenly among the four fillets.
  5. Squeeze the lemon juice over the cod, making sure each fillet gets a good amount of juice.
  6. Season with salt, pepper, and garlic powder (if using).
  7. Bake the cod for 12-15 minutes, or until it reaches an internal temperature of 145°F (63°C).
  8. Remove the cod from the oven and let it rest for 2-3 minutes before serving.

Tips and Variations

  • To add some extra flavor to the dish, you can sprinkle some chopped onions or garlic over the cod before baking.
  • If you prefer a crisper top on your cod, you can broil it for an additional 2-3 minutes after baking.
  • You can also serve the cod with a side of roasted vegetables, quinoa, or brown rice for a more filling meal.

Conclusion

Baked cod with lemon and herbs is a simple, yet flavorful recipe that’s perfect for a quick and easy dinner. With its paleo-approved ingredients and easy preparation, it’s an excellent option for busy weeknights or special occasions. Whether you’re a seasoned cook or a beginner in the kitchen, this recipe is sure to become a staple in your household. So go ahead, give it a try, and enjoy the delicious flavors of cod, lemon, and herbs!
