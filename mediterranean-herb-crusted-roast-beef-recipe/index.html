Mediterranean Herb-Crusted Roast Beef Recipe


Are you tired of the same old roast beef recipes? Look no further! This Mediterranean Herb-Crusted Roast Beef Recipe is sure to tantalize your taste buds and leave you wanting more. When it comes to cooking a roast, everyone knows it's not just about throwing some seasoning on the meat and hoping for the best. A good roast needs a solid foundation, and that foundation starts with the right herbs and spices. For this recipe, we'll be combining the classic Mediterranean flavors of rosemary, thyme, and oregano to create a crust that's both fragrant and savory. Ingredients

3-4 pound prime ribeye roast 2 tablespoons olive oil 2 tablespoons chopped fresh rosemary 2 tablespoons chopped fresh thyme 1 tablespoon chopped fresh oregano 2 cloves garlic, minced 1 onion, sliced 2 cloves 1 teaspoon salt 1/2 teaspoon black pepper 1/4 cup red wine (optional) 1 cup beef broth 2 tablespoons butter

Preparing the Herb Crust Before we even start cooking the roast, we need to prepare the herb crust. In a food processor, combine the chopped rosemary, thyme, oregano, and garlic. Process the mixture until it's well combined and slightly crushed. Season with salt and pepper to taste. You want to add enough salt to bring out the flavors, but not so much that it overpowers the dish. Preparing the Roast To ensure the roast cooks evenly, make sure it's at room temperature before cooking. Pat the roast dry with a paper towel to remove excess moisture. In a small bowl, mix together the olive oil, onion slices, and red wine (if using). Rub the mixture all over the roast, making sure to coat it evenly. Sprinkle the herb crust mixture over the roast, pressing gently to ensure the herbs adhere. Cooking the Roast Preheat your oven to 425°F (220°C). Place the roast on a rimmed baking sheet and put it in the oven. Roast for 20-25 minutes per pound, or until the internal temperature reaches 130-135°F (54-57°C) for medium-rare. Remove the roast from the oven and let it rest for 10-15 minutes before slicing. Making the Jus While the roast is resting, we're going to make the jus. Pour the beef broth into a saucepan and bring it to a simmer over medium heat. Scrape the juices from the roasting pan with a wooden spoon and add them to the saucepan. Reduce the heat to low and let it simmer for 5-10 minutes, or until the liquid has thickened slightly. Stir in the butter until it's melted and the sauce is smooth. Assembly and Serving Slice the roast against the grain and serve with the jus spooned over the top. Add a side of roasted vegetables, such as asparagus or Brussels sprouts, for a well-rounded meal. Tips and Variations

If you don't have red wine, you can omit it or substitute it with beef broth. To make the herb crust more potent, you can increase the amount of garlic and herbs. For a more visually appealing dish, you can add some fresh herbs on top of the roast before serving.

This Mediterranean Herb-Crusted Roast Beef Recipe is sure to be a hit at any dinner party or family gathering. The combination of rosemary, thyme, and oregano creates a flavor profile that's both earthy and aromatic, perfect for the Mediterranean cuisine. So go ahead and give it a try – your taste buds will thank you!
