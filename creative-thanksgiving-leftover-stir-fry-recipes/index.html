Creative Thanksgiving Leftover Stir-Fry Recipes


Are you tired of eating the same old Thanksgiving leftovers for days on end? Well, I have the perfect solution for you - creative Thanksgiving leftover stir-fry recipes! These recipes are not only delicious but also a great way to repurpose those turkey, stuffing, and cranberry sauce leftovers into something exciting and new. So grab your wok and let’s get cooking!

1. Turkey and Vegetable Stir-Fry

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups leftover turkey, shredded
  • 1 cup carrots, sliced
  • 1 cup bell peppers, sliced
  • 1 cup green beans, trimmed
  • 1 cup mushrooms, sliced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon oyster sauce
  • 1 tablespoon sesame oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Heat the sesame oil in a wok over medium-high heat.
  2. Add the garlic and stir-fry for 1 minute until fragrant.
  3. Add the carrots, bell peppers, green beans, and mushrooms. Stir-fry for 3-4 minutes until the vegetables are tender-crisp.
  4. Add the leftover turkey and stir-fry for another 2-3 minutes until heated through.
  5. In a small bowl, combine the soy sauce and oyster sauce. Pour the sauce over the stir-fry and toss to coat.
  6. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  7. Serve hot and enjoy!

2. Stuffing and Egg Stir-Fry

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups leftover stuffing
  • 4 eggs, beaten
  • 1 cup spinach
  • 1/2 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 2 green onions, chopped
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Heat the olive oil in a wok over medium heat.
  2. Add the leftover stuffing and stir-fry for 2-3 minutes until heated through.
  3. Push the stuffing to one side of the wok and pour the beaten eggs into the other side.
  4. Scramble the eggs until cooked, then mix them together with the stuffing.
  5. Add the spinach, cherry tomatoes, and green onions. Stir-fry for another 2-3 minutes until the spinach wilts.
  6. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  7. Serve hot and enjoy!

3. Cranberry Sauce and Brussels Sprouts Stir-Fry

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups leftover cranberry sauce
  • 2 cups Brussels sprouts, halved
  • 1 cup bacon, cooked and crumbled
  • 1/2 cup pecans, chopped
  • 2 tablespoons balsamic vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon honey
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Heat the olive oil in a wok over medium-high heat.
  2. Add the Brussels sprouts and stir-fry for 4-5 minutes until tender and lightly browned.
  3. Add the cranberry sauce, bacon, pecans, balsamic vinegar, and honey. Stir-fry for another 2-3 minutes until heated through.
  4. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  5. Serve hot and enjoy!

Conclusion

These creative Thanksgiving leftover stir-fry recipes are a fantastic way to transform your holiday leftovers into exciting and flavorful meals. By combining different ingredients and flavors, you can create new dishes that will make everyone forget they’re eating leftovers. So don’t let your Thanksgiving leftovers go to waste - give these stir-fry recipes a try and enjoy a whole new culinary experience!
