Brunch Around the World: International Breakfast Recipes to Try


Breakfast is the most important meal of the day, but it doesn’t have to be boring or mundane. In fact, around the world, people start their day with a wide variety of unique, flavorful, and often surprising dishes. Brunch, in particular, has become a popular weekend tradition, not just for lazy mornings but for indulging in culinary creativity. If you’re someone who enjoys experimenting with different flavors, textures, and ingredients, these international breakfast recipes are perfect for adding an exciting twist to your brunch spread. Let’s embark on a journey around the world, exploring diverse and delicious brunch ideas to try at home.

1. Shakshuka – Middle Eastern Delight

Shakshuka, a dish of poached eggs in a spicy tomato and pepper sauce, is a popular breakfast across the Middle East and North Africa. This savory dish is packed with rich flavors and is typically served with warm, crusty bread to scoop up the sauce.

Ingredients:

  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 1 bell pepper, diced
  • 4 ripe tomatoes, chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 tsp ground cumin
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • 1/2 tsp chili flakes (optional)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 4 large eggs
  • Fresh parsley for garnish
  • Crusty bread for serving

Directions:

  1. Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Add the chopped onion and bell pepper and cook until softened.
  2. Stir in the garlic, cumin, paprika, and chili flakes (if using). Cook for another minute until fragrant.
  3. Add the tomatoes, salt, and pepper, and let the mixture simmer for about 10-15 minutes, or until the tomatoes break down into a sauce.
  4. Make small wells in the sauce and gently crack the eggs into each well.
  5. Cover and cook until the eggs are done to your liking, about 5-7 minutes for runny yolks.
  6. Garnish with fresh parsley and serve with crusty bread to dip into the flavorful sauce.

Shakshuka is not only satisfying but also highly customizable. You can add ingredients like feta cheese, olives, or spinach to make it your own. It’s a wonderful brunch option that pairs perfectly with a light salad or fresh fruit on the side.

2. Croque Madame – French Elegance

Croque Madame is a French take on the classic grilled cheese sandwich, elevated with a creamy béchamel sauce and a fried egg on top. This indulgent brunch dish is both rich and comforting, making it a beloved staple in cafés throughout France.

Ingredients:

  • 4 slices of white bread
  • 2 tbsp unsalted butter
  • 4 slices of ham
  • 2 slices of Gruyère cheese
  • 1/2 cup béchamel sauce (homemade or store-bought)
  • 2 large eggs
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Directions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C). Butter both sides of the bread slices.
  2. On two slices of bread, layer a slice of ham and a slice of cheese. Top with the other slices of bread.
  3. Heat a skillet over medium heat and grill the sandwiches on both sides until golden brown, about 3-4 minutes per side.
  4. Place the grilled sandwiches on a baking sheet and spoon a generous amount of béchamel sauce over the top.
  5. Bake in the oven for 5-7 minutes until the cheese is melted and bubbly.
  6. While the sandwiches are baking, fry the eggs in a separate pan to your desired doneness.
  7. Remove the sandwiches from the oven, top each with a fried egg, and season with salt and pepper.

Croque Madame is a perfect brunch option for when you’re craving something cheesy and rich. Pair it with a light green salad dressed in a tangy vinaigrette to balance the richness of the sandwich.

3. Tamago Sushi – Japanese Breakfast Twist

In Japan, breakfast can be a delicate and refined affair, with a focus on fresh ingredients and simple flavors. Tamago sushi, or sweet Japanese omelet sushi, is a fun and unique way to enjoy eggs at breakfast. It’s often served as part of a sushi platter but can also stand alone as a delightful brunch dish.

Ingredients:

  • 4 large eggs
  • 1 tbsp sugar
  • 1 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp mirin (optional)
  • 1 tsp vegetable oil
  • 2 cups sushi rice, cooked
  • Nori (seaweed) sheets, cut into small strips for garnish

Directions:

  1. In a bowl, whisk together the eggs, sugar, soy sauce, and mirin until well combined.
  2. Heat a nonstick skillet over low heat and brush with a little vegetable oil.
  3. Pour a thin layer of the egg mixture into the pan and let it cook until set but not browned. Roll it up and push it to one side of the pan.
  4. Pour in more egg mixture, lifting the rolled omelet to let the uncooked egg run underneath. Continue cooking and rolling until all the egg mixture is used up.
  5. Let the rolled omelet cool slightly, then slice it into bite-sized pieces.
  6. To assemble the sushi, place a small mound of sushi rice on each piece of tamago. Garnish with a strip of nori for decoration.

Tamago sushi is light yet flavorful, and its subtle sweetness makes it a great option for a unique brunch. Pair it with a cup of green tea or miso soup for an authentic Japanese experience.

4. Full English Breakfast – A Hearty Start

For those who enjoy a hearty, filling brunch, the Full English Breakfast is a classic British dish that brings together eggs, sausages, bacon, beans, mushrooms, grilled tomatoes, and toast. It’s a meal that covers all the bases, perfect for those who want something substantial to kickstart their day.

Ingredients:

  • 2 sausages
  • 4 slices of bacon
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup baked beans (canned or homemade)
  • 1 tomato, halved
  • 1/2 cup mushrooms, sliced
  • 2 slices of bread for toasting
  • Butter for frying

Directions:

  1. Heat a large frying pan over medium heat. Add the sausages and bacon and cook until browned and crispy, about 5-7 minutes.
  2. In another pan, fry the eggs to your liking (sunny side up or scrambled).
  3. In the same pan as the bacon and sausages, add the mushrooms and grilled tomato halves. Cook for another 3-4 minutes until tender.
  4. Toast the bread and butter it while everything finishes cooking.
  5. To serve, arrange everything on a plate: sausages, bacon, eggs, mushrooms, tomatoes, baked beans, and toast.

This filling meal is perfect for weekends when you want something satisfying and packed with flavors. The Full English Breakfast is a great choice for brunch gatherings, as it appeals to all tastes.

5. Arepas – Colombian Comfort

Arepas are a staple in Colombia and Venezuela, and they are versatile enough to be filled with a variety of delicious fillings. A perfect blend of crispy on the outside and soft on the inside, these cornmeal cakes can be paired with cheese, eggs, or meats, making them an ideal choice for brunch.

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups cornmeal (preferably pre-cooked)
  • 1 1/2 cups warm water
  • 1 tbsp salt
  • 1 tbsp butter
  • Cheese, scrambled eggs, or meats for filling

Directions:

  1. In a bowl, combine the cornmeal, warm water, and salt. Mix until a dough forms.
  2. Divide the dough into small balls and flatten them into patties about 1/2 inch thick.
  3. Heat a nonstick skillet over medium heat and cook the patties for about 4-5 minutes per side until golden and crispy.
  4. Once cooked, slice the arepas open and fill them with your desired fillings, such as cheese, scrambled eggs, or shredded beef.

Arepas are a fun and customizable brunch dish that can be sweet or savory. They are perfect for serving guests, as everyone can personalize their own arepa according to their tastes.

6. Açaí Bowl – Brazilian Refreshment

Originating from Brazil, the açaí bowl is a delicious, nutritious, and refreshing breakfast or brunch dish. Made from frozen açaí berries, it’s often blended with fruits and topped with granola, nuts, and seeds, making it a healthy yet indulgent choice.

Ingredients:

  • 2 frozen açaí packets (or 1 cup açaí puree)
  • 1 banana
  • 1/2 cup almond milk (or any milk of choice)
  • 1 tbsp honey or agave syrup
  • Toppings: granola, coconut flakes, chia seeds, fresh berries, nuts

Directions:

  1. In a blender, combine the frozen açaí, banana, almond milk, and honey. Blend until smooth and creamy.
  2. Pour the mixture into a bowl and top with granola, fresh berries, coconut flakes, and chia seeds.
  3. Serve immediately for a refreshing and nutritious brunch.

The açaí bowl is not only delicious but also packed with antioxidants, fiber, and healthy fats. It’s the perfect dish for a light yet energizing brunch.

7. Chilaquiles – Mexican Comfort Food

Chilaquiles are a traditional Mexican breakfast dish made from crispy corn tortillas smothered in a flavorful sauce, typically red or green, and topped with a fried egg. This hearty dish is full of flavor and makes for an amazing brunch option.

Ingredients:

  • 4 corn tortillas, cut into triangles
  • 1 cup red or green salsa
  • 2 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 2 eggs
  • Crumbled cheese (such as cotija)
  • Fresh cilantro for garnish
  • Sour cream (optional)

Directions:

  1. Heat vegetable oil in a skillet over medium heat. Fry the tortilla triangles until crispy and golden brown, about 3-4 minutes per side.
  2. Once the tortillas are crispy, drain them on a paper towel.
  3. In the same skillet, pour the salsa and heat until warmed through.
  4. Add the crispy tortilla chips to the salsa and toss to coat.
  5. Fry the eggs to your liking and place them on top of the chilaquiles.
  6. Garnish with crumbled cheese, fresh cilantro, and a dollop of sour cream if desired.

Chilaquiles are a vibrant and bold brunch option that’s both comforting and flavorful. They are perfect for those who enjoy spicy dishes.

Conclusion

Brunch is a time to explore new flavors and enjoy a leisurely meal with family and friends. By trying breakfast recipes from around the world, you can take your brunch game to the next level and experience a wide variety of tastes and textures. From the rich and cheesy Croque Madame to the fresh and refreshing açaí bowl, there is a dish to suit every taste preference. Whether you’re looking for something savory, sweet, or healthy, these international breakfast recipes are sure to inspire your next brunch gathering. So, grab your ingredients and get ready to embark on a culinary adventure!
