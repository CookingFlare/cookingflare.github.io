Crispy Coconut Shrimp with Sweet Chili Sauce: A Tropical Treat


If you’re looking to bring a taste of the tropics into your kitchen, look no further than this delightful recipe for Crispy Coconut Shrimp with Sweet Chili Sauce. This dish combines the succulent flavors of shrimp with the sweet and spicy notes of coconut and chili, resulting in a truly tropical treat that will transport your taste buds to paradise. Whether you’re planning a summer cookout or simply want to add a touch of exotic flair to your dinner table, this recipe is sure to impress both family and friends.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound of large shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 1 cup of all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup of shredded coconut
  • 1 teaspoon of salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon of black pepper
  • 2 eggs, beaten
  • Vegetable oil, for frying

Sweet Chili Sauce Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup of sweet chili sauce
  • 2 tablespoons of lime juice
  • 1 tablespoon of soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon of honey

Instructions

  1. In a shallow bowl, combine the flour, shredded coconut, salt, and black pepper. Mix well.

  2. Dip each shrimp into the beaten eggs, then coat them in the flour and coconut mixture, pressing gently to ensure an even coating.

  3. Place the coated shrimp on a baking sheet and refrigerate for 15 minutes to allow the coating to set.

  4. In the meantime, prepare the sweet chili sauce by combining the sweet chili sauce, lime juice, soy sauce, and honey in a small bowl. Stir well to combine.

  5. Heat vegetable oil in a deep frying pan or pot to 350°F (175°C).

  6. Carefully place the coated shrimp into the hot oil, frying them in small batches to maintain the temperature of the oil.

  7. Fry the shrimp for 2-3 minutes on each side, or until they turn golden brown and crispy.

  8. Using a slotted spoon, transfer the fried shrimp to a paper towel-lined plate to drain excess oil.

  9. Serve the crispy coconut shrimp immediately with the sweet chili sauce on the side for dipping.

  10. Enjoy the explosion of flavors as you bite into the crispy coconut coating and succulent shrimp, enhanced by the sweet and tangy notes of the chili sauce.

Tips and Variations

  • For an extra punch of flavor, you can add a teaspoon of curry powder to the flour and coconut mixture.
  • If you prefer a spicier sauce, you can add a teaspoon of red pepper flakes or a dash of hot sauce to the sweet chili sauce.
  • This recipe works well with both fresh and frozen shrimp. Just make sure to thaw frozen shrimp before using, and pat them dry to ensure a crispy coating.
  • Serve the crispy coconut shrimp as an appetizer, or pair it with a fresh green salad and steamed rice for a complete meal.

Final Thoughts

Crispy Coconut Shrimp with Sweet Chili Sauce is a dish that brings the flavors of the tropics straight to your plate. The combination of the crispy coconut coating and the sweet and tangy chili sauce creates a tropical treat that is both satisfying and refreshing. Whether you’re hosting a summer gathering or simply want to indulge in a taste of paradise, this recipe is sure to impress. So, grab your apron, fire up the frying pan, and get ready to transport your taste buds to a tropical oasis. Enjoy!
