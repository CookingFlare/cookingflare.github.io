Savory Paleo Beef and Cabbage Stir-Fry


Are you looking for a quick and delicious meal that is both healthy and flavorful? Look no further than this savory Paleo Beef and Cabbage Stir-Fry recipe! Packed with protein and fiber, this dish is not only satisfying but also perfect for those following a Paleo lifestyle. Let’s dive right into the recipe!

Ingredients

  • 1 pound grass-fed beef, thinly sliced
  • 1 small head of cabbage, thinly sliced
  • 1 red bell pepper, thinly sliced
  • 1 yellow bell pepper, thinly sliced
  • 1 medium onion, thinly sliced
  • 3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon coconut oil
  • 2 tablespoons coconut aminos
  • 1 tablespoon fish sauce
  • 1 tablespoon apple cider vinegar
  • 1 teaspoon ground ginger
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh cilantro, for garnish

Instructions

  1. Heat the coconut oil in a large skillet or wok over medium-high heat.

  2. Add the garlic and onion to the skillet and sauté until the onion becomes translucent, about 2 minutes.

  3. Add the beef to the skillet and cook until browned, about 4-5 minutes. Season with salt and pepper to taste.

  4. Add the cabbage and bell peppers to the skillet. Stir-fry for about 3-4 minutes or until the vegetables are tender-crisp.

  5. In a small bowl, whisk together the coconut aminos, fish sauce, apple cider vinegar, and ground ginger.

  6. Pour the sauce over the beef and vegetable mixture in the skillet. Stir well to coat everything evenly.

  7. Reduce the heat to low and let the stir-fry simmer for an additional 2-3 minutes to allow the flavors to meld together.

  8. Remove the skillet from the heat and garnish with fresh cilantro.

  9. Serve the Savory Paleo Beef and Cabbage Stir-Fry hot and enjoy!

Why Choose Paleo?

The Paleo diet is based on the principle of eating like our ancestors, focusing on whole foods that were available to them during the Paleolithic era. By eliminating processed foods, grains, dairy, and legumes, the Paleo diet aims to provide a nutrient-dense, balanced approach to eating. This savory stir-fry is a perfect example of how delicious and satisfying Paleo meals can be.

The Benefits of Beef and Cabbage

Beef is an excellent source of protein, iron, and various B vitamins. Grass-fed beef, in particular, is higher in omega-3 fatty acids and conjugated linoleic acid (CLA), which have been linked to numerous health benefits.

Cabbage, on the other hand, is low in calories but rich in vitamins K, C, and B6. It is also a good source of fiber, which aids in digestion and promotes a healthy gut.

Final Thoughts

This Savory Paleo Beef and Cabbage Stir-Fry is the perfect weeknight meal that checks all the boxes - it’s healthy, flavorful, and easy to make. The combination of tender beef, crunchy cabbage, and vibrant bell peppers creates a delightful medley of flavors and textures. So why not give this recipe a try and experience the deliciousness of Paleo cooking for yourself? Enjoy!

Note:

This recipe serves 4 people and can be easily doubled or halved to suit your needs.
