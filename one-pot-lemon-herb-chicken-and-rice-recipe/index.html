One-Pot Lemon Herb Chicken and Rice Recipe


If you’re looking for a delicious and easy one-pot meal that will satisfy your taste buds and make your weeknight cooking a breeze, then this One-Pot Lemon Herb Chicken and Rice recipe is the perfect choice. Packed with fresh flavors and wholesome ingredients, this dish is sure to become a family favorite.

Ingredients

  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1 cup long-grain white rice
  • 2 cups low-sodium chicken broth
  • 1 lemon, juiced and zested
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon fresh thyme leaves
  • 1 tablespoon fresh rosemary, chopped
  • 1 tablespoon fresh parsley, chopped
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil

Instructions

  1. Start by seasoning the chicken breasts with salt and pepper on both sides. Heat the olive oil in a large skillet or Dutch oven over medium-high heat.

  2. Once the oil is hot, add the chicken breasts to the skillet and cook until golden brown on each side, about 4-5 minutes per side. Remove the chicken from the skillet and set aside.

  3. In the same skillet, add the minced garlic and cook for about 1 minute until fragrant. Add the rice and stir to coat it with the garlic-infused oil.

  4. Pour in the chicken broth, lemon juice, and lemon zest. Stir well to combine and bring the mixture to a boil. Reduce the heat to low, cover, and let it simmer for about 15 minutes or until the rice is tender and the liquid is absorbed.

  5. Meanwhile, slice the cooked chicken breasts into thin strips. Once the rice is cooked, add the sliced chicken back into the skillet along with the fresh thyme, rosemary, and parsley. Stir gently to incorporate all the flavors.

  6. Cover the skillet again and let it simmer for another 5 minutes to allow the chicken to warm through and the herbs to infuse their aroma into the dish.

  7. Remove from heat and let it sit for a few minutes before serving to allow the flavors to meld together. Give the dish a final stir to fluff up the rice and distribute the chicken evenly.

  8. Serve the One-Pot Lemon Herb Chicken and Rice hot, garnished with a sprinkle of fresh herbs and a lemon wedge for an extra burst of citrusy flavor.

Tips and Variations

  • Feel free to customize this recipe by adding your favorite vegetables such as peas, carrots, or bell peppers. Simply sauté them with the garlic before adding the rice and broth.
  • For a creamier texture, you can stir in a splash of heavy cream or coconut milk towards the end of the cooking process.
  • If you prefer a stronger lemon flavor, add an extra squeeze of lemon juice or grate more lemon zest into the dish.
  • Leftovers can be stored in an airtight container in the refrigerator for up to 3 days. Reheat in a microwave or on the stovetop with a splash of water or broth to prevent drying out.

Enjoy this One-Pot Lemon Herb Chicken and Rice recipe as a comforting and flavorful meal that requires minimal effort and cleanup. It’s a perfect option for busy weeknights or when you want to impress your guests with a delicious and satisfying dish. Give it a try and let its vibrant flavors brighten up your dining table!
