Delightful Nut-Free Lemon Garlic Shrimp Recipe


If you’re looking for a refreshing and flavorful seafood dish that’s also nut-free, then this delightful lemon garlic shrimp recipe is a must-try. Packed with zesty lemon, aromatic garlic, and succulent shrimp, this dish is perfect for a quick weeknight dinner or a special gathering with friends and family. Not only is it easy to prepare, but it’s also bursting with vibrant flavors that will leave your taste buds tingling. Let’s dive into the details of this mouthwatering recipe.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound of large shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 4 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1/4 cup of fresh lemon juice
  • 2 tablespoons of olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon of lemon zest
  • 1/2 teaspoon of red pepper flakes
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Chopped fresh parsley for garnish
  • Cooked pasta or crusty bread for serving

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Shrimp: Start by patting the shrimp dry with paper towels to remove any excess moisture. Season the shrimp with salt and pepper to taste.

  2. Sear the Shrimp: In a large skillet, heat the olive oil over medium-high heat. Add the seasoned shrimp to the skillet and cook for 1-2 minutes on each side until they turn pink and opaque. Once cooked, transfer the shrimp to a plate and set aside.

  3. Infuse the Flavors: In the same skillet, add the minced garlic and red pepper flakes. Sauté for 1 minute until the garlic becomes fragrant, being careful not to let it brown.

  4. Lemon Infusion: Pour the fresh lemon juice into the skillet, using it to deglaze the pan and infuse the garlic with the citrusy flavor. Add the lemon zest and stir the mixture to combine.

  5. Combine and Simmer: Return the cooked shrimp to the skillet, coating them evenly with the lemon garlic sauce. Allow the shrimp to simmer in the sauce for an additional 2-3 minutes, ensuring they are thoroughly heated through.

  6. Serve and Garnish: Once the shrimp are fully coated with the lemon garlic sauce, remove the skillet from the heat. Garnish the dish with chopped fresh parsley for a pop of color and freshness.

  7. Serve with Complements: Serve the delightful nut-free lemon garlic shrimp over a bed of cooked pasta or with some crusty bread to soak up the flavorful sauce.

Flavorful and Versatile

This nut-free lemon garlic shrimp recipe is incredibly versatile. You can customize it by adding your favorite vegetables, such as cherry tomatoes, spinach, or asparagus, to enhance the dish with extra nutrients and colors. Additionally, you can adjust the level of red pepper flakes to suit your heat preference, making it a flexible recipe for all taste buds.

Nutritional Benefits

In addition to being a burst of flavor, shrimp is also a great source of lean protein and essential nutrients such as vitamin B12, iodine, and selenium. The addition of fresh lemon juice not only elevates the taste but also provides a dose of vitamin C, known for its antioxidant properties.

Conclusion

This nut-free lemon garlic shrimp recipe is a delightful and easy-to-make dish that will impress your family and friends with its bright flavors and wholesome ingredients. Whether you’re a seafood enthusiast or just looking to add a new recipe to your culinary repertoire, this dish is a winner. Give it a try and savor the delightful combination of zesty lemon and savory garlic with succulent shrimp. Bon appétit!
