Top 10 Marinades for Grilled Chicken


When it comes to grilling chicken, a good marinade can elevate the entire dish, making it juicy, tender, and bursting with flavor. In this blog post, we’ll explore the top 10 marinades for grilled chicken, adapting flavors from around the world and utilizing a variety of ingredients. So, grab your favorite spices, oils, and vinegars, and get ready to level up your grilling game!

  1. Classic Lemon & Herb Marinade Ingredients:
  • Juice of 1 lemon
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh herbs (such as parsley, basil, and cilantro)
  • 1/4 cup chopped onion
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • Salt and freshly ground black pepper, to taste

Directions: Combine all ingredients in a blender or large bowl. Mix well. Submerge chicken in the marinade and refrigerate for at least 2 hours or overnight for maximum flavor infusion. Grill as desired and serve.

  1. Asian-inspired Teriyaki Marinade Ingredients:
  • 1/2 cup soy sauce
  • 1/2 cup rice vinegar
  • 1/2 cup honey
  • 1/4 cup sake or mirin
  • 6 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 tablespoon grated ginger
  • 1 tablespoon cornstarch

Directions: In a saucepan, combine the first five ingredients and bring to a boil. Stir in garlic and ginger. Reduce heat and let simmer for 5 minutes. Add cornstarch mixed with water to thicken the sauce. Cool, then pour over chicken, and marinate for at least 2 hours.

  1. Qualitative Chimichurri Marinade Ingredients:
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 1/4 cup red wine vinegar
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 teaspoon smoked paprika
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • Salt and freshly ground black pepper
  • Hot sauce, to taste

Directions: Combine olive oil and vinegar in a bowl. Mix in garlic, paprika, cumin, oregano, and black pepper. If desired, add a dash of hot sauce for an extra kick. Submerge chicken in the marinade and refrigerate for 2-4 hours. Grill as desired, and enjoy!

  1. Sweet and Tangy Barbecue Marinade Ingredients:
  • 1/2 cup ketchup
  • 1/4 cup honey
  • 1/4 cup apple cider vinegar
  • 2 tablespoons brown sugar
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 teaspoon Worcestershire sauce
  • Hot sauce, to taste

Directions: In a saucepan, combine ketchup, honey, vinegar, and brown sugar. Cook over medium heat, stirring frequently, until the sugar has dissolved and the mixture is well combined. Remove from heat and stir in garlic, Worcestershire sauce, and hot sauce. Pour the marinade over chicken and let sit for at least 2 hours.

  1. Indian-Inspired Tikka Marinade Ingredients:
  • 1 cup plain Greek yogurt
  • 2 tablespoons lemon juice
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1 teaspoon ground coriander
  • 1 teaspoon garam masala
  • Salt and freshly ground black pepper

Directions: In a bowl, whisk together yogurt, lemon juice, garlic, and spices. Submerge chicken in the marinade and refrigerate for at least 2 hours or overnight for best results. Be sure to turn the chicken occasionally for even marination. Grill and serve with your favorite Indian-inspired side dishes.

  1. Zesty Lime & Chipotle Marinade Ingredients:
  • Juice and zest of 2 limes
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 can diced tomatoes, drained
  • 1 tablespoon adobo sauce from a can of chipotles in adobo
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • Salt and freshly ground black pepper

Directions: In a blender or food processor, combine lime juice and zest, olive oil, garlic, diced tomatoes, adobo sauce, and cumin. Mix until smooth. Pour the marinade over chicken and refrigerate for at least 2 hours, or overnight for maximum flavor.

  1. Savory Mustard & Rosemary Marinade Ingredients:
  • 1/2 cup Dijon mustard
  • 1/2 cup olive oil
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 2 tablespoons chopped fresh rosemary
  • 1 teaspoon fresh thyme
  • Salt and freshly ground black pepper

Directions: In a bowl, whisk together mustard, olive oil, garlic, rosemary, thyme, and black pepper. Submerge chicken in the marinade and refrigerate for a minimum of 2 hours, turning occasionally. Grill and enjoy!

  1. Classic Italian Herb Marinade Ingredients:
  • 1/2 cup olive oil
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 2 tablespoons chopped fresh basil
  • 2 tablespoons chopped fresh oregano
  • 1 tablespoon chopped fresh parsley
  • Salt and freshly ground black pepper

Directions: In a bowl, mix together olive oil, garlic, basil, oregano, parsley, and black pepper. Submerge chicken in the marinade and refrigerate for at least 2 hours. Grilling makes a perfect Italian feast!

  1. Bold Jamaican Jerk Marinade Ingredients:
  • 1/2 cup olive oil
  • 1/4 cup lime juice
  • 3 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 tablespoon paprika
  • 1 tablespoon ground allspice
  • 1 teaspoon ground ginger
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper

Directions: In a bowl, combine olive oil, lime juice, garlic, paprika, allspice, ginger, cinnamon, and black pepper. Rub the mixture onto the chicken, being sure to cover all sides. Let the chicken marinate for at least 2 hours or up to overnight before grilling.

  1. Simple Lemon & Garlic Marinade Ingredients:
  • Juice of 1 lemon
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • Salt and freshly ground black pepper

Directions: In a small bowl, whisk together lemon juice, olive oil, garlic, salt, and black pepper. Pour the marinade over chicken and refrigerate for at least 2 hours before grilling.

Conclusion: That wraps up our top 10 list of marinades for grilled chicken! With so many flavors to choose from, you’ll never run out of options for injecting creativity and unique tastes into your grilled chicken dishes. So fire up the grill, and be sure to try out at least a few of these marinades for a chicken experience that will have your friends and family begging for more!
