Creamy Instant Pot Mashed Potatoes Recipe for Comfort Food


There’s something undeniably comforting about a creamy, velvety bowl of mashed potatoes. Whether you’re serving them alongside a roast dinner, pairing them with a juicy steak, or simply indulging in a bowlful on their own, mashed potatoes are the epitome of comfort food. And when it comes to making the perfect mashed potatoes, the Instant Pot can be a game-changer. This Creamy Instant Pot Mashed Potatoes recipe will show you how to achieve that ideal creamy texture and rich flavor with minimal effort.

Ingredients

  • 3 lbs Yukon Gold potatoes, peeled and cut into chunks
  • 1 cup chicken or vegetable broth
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, cubed
  • 1/2 cup sour cream
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Chopped fresh chives for garnish (optional)

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Potatoes: Start by peeling the Yukon Gold potatoes and cutting them into chunks. This will ensure that they cook evenly and result in a smoother texture.

  2. Pressure Cook the Potatoes: Place the potato chunks in the Instant Pot and add the chicken or vegetable broth. Close the lid and set the Instant Pot to high pressure for 8 minutes. Once the cooking cycle is complete, perform a quick pressure release.

  3. Add the Flavor: After releasing the pressure, carefully open the lid and add the minced garlic and cubed butter to the hot potatoes. The residual heat will help melt the butter and infuse the potatoes with a rich, garlicky flavor.

  4. Mash and Cream: Use a potato masher to mash the potatoes to your desired consistency. For an extra creamy texture, add the sour cream and heavy cream. Continue mashing until the ingredients are well combined and the potatoes are smooth and creamy.

  5. Season to Perfection: Season the mashed potatoes with salt and pepper to taste. This is your chance to adjust the seasoning to suit your preferences, so don’t be afraid to taste and add more salt or pepper as needed.

  6. Serve and Garnish: Transfer the creamy mashed potatoes to a serving bowl and garnish with chopped fresh chives for a pop of color and an herby freshness.

Tips for Perfect Instant Pot Mashed Potatoes

  • Don’t Over Mash: Be mindful not to overwork the potatoes when mashing, as this can result in a gluey texture. Stop mashing once the potatoes are smooth and creamy.

  • Use Yukon Gold Potatoes: Yukon Gold potatoes are naturally creamy and buttery, making them perfect for mashed potatoes. Their thin skin also means you can leave them unpeeled for a more rustic texture if desired.

  • Customize the Creaminess: Feel free to adjust the amount of sour cream and heavy cream to achieve your preferred level of creaminess. Some may prefer a richer mash, while others may opt for a lighter touch.

  • Keep Warm Function: If you’re not serving the mashed potatoes immediately, you can utilize the Keep Warm function on the Instant Pot to maintain their temperature until you’re ready to enjoy them.

Final Thoughts

With this Creamy Instant Pot Mashed Potatoes recipe, you can elevate your comfort food game with minimal effort. The Instant Pot streamlines the cooking process, resulting in luxuriously creamy mashed potatoes that are sure to become a staple at your table. Whether you’re cooking for a family dinner or a special occasion, these mashed potatoes are a crowd-pleaser that will leave everyone feeling warm, cozy, and utterly satisfied. Enjoy!

So there you have it – a mouthwatering, soul-soothing recipe for Creamy Instant Pot Mashed Potatoes. Give it a try and let the irresistible aroma of rich, creamy potatoes fill your kitchen, and your heart. Your taste buds will thank you!
