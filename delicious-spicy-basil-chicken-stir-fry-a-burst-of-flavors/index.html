Delicious Spicy Basil Chicken Stir Fry: A Burst of Flavors


If you’re craving a dish that’s packed with bold flavors and tantalizing aromas, then look no further than this delicious Spicy Basil Chicken Stir Fry recipe. This Thai-inspired dish is a perfect balance of spicy, savory, and aromatic flavors that will leave your taste buds dancing with delight. Whether you’re a fan of spicy food or simply looking to explore new culinary adventures, this recipe is sure to become a favorite in your kitchen.

Ingredients

To create this mouthwatering Spicy Basil Chicken Stir Fry, you’ll need the following ingredients:

  • 1 pound of boneless, skinless chicken breasts, thinly sliced
  • 2 tablespoons of vegetable oil
  • 3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 red bell pepper, thinly sliced
  • 1 yellow bell pepper, thinly sliced
  • 1 onion, thinly sliced
  • 1 cup of fresh basil leaves
  • 2 tablespoons of soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon of oyster sauce
  • 1 tablespoon of fish sauce
  • 1 tablespoon of sugar
  • 1 teaspoon of chili flakes
  • Cooked rice, for serving

Instructions

Step 1: Prepare the Chicken

Start by slicing the boneless, skinless chicken breasts into thin strips. This will ensure that the chicken cooks quickly and evenly in the stir fry.

Step 2: Heat the Wok

Place a wok or a large skillet over high heat and add the vegetable oil. Once the oil is hot, add the minced garlic and sauté for a few seconds until it becomes fragrant.

Step 3: Stir Fry the Chicken

Add the sliced chicken to the wok and stir fry for 3-4 minutes or until it is fully cooked and has a golden brown color.

Step 4: Add the Vegetables

Next, add the sliced red bell pepper, yellow bell pepper, and onion to the wok. Continue stir frying for an additional 2-3 minutes until the vegetables are slightly tender but still vibrant in color.

Step 5: Create the Sauce

In a small bowl, combine the soy sauce, oyster sauce, fish sauce, and sugar. Mix well until the sugar has dissolved.

Step 6: Add the Sauce and Basil

Pour the sauce over the chicken and vegetables in the wok. Stir everything together to ensure that the sauce coats all the ingredients evenly. Then, add the fresh basil leaves and chili flakes to the wok and gently toss everything together.

Step 7: Serve

Once the basil has wilted and the flavors have melded together, your Spicy Basil Chicken Stir Fry is ready to be served. Spoon the stir fry over a bed of cooked rice and garnish with additional fresh basil leaves for a burst of color and aroma.

Conclusion

This Spicy Basil Chicken Stir Fry recipe is a celebration of vibrant flavors and textures. The tender chicken, crisp vegetables, and aromatic basil come together in a symphony of taste that will leave you craving for more. Whether you’re preparing a quick weeknight meal or entertaining guests, this stir fry is a versatile and satisfying dish that is sure to impress. So, roll up your sleeves, grab your wok, and embark on a culinary adventure with this delightful Spicy Basil Chicken Stir Fry.
