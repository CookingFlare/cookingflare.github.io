Delicious Homemade Chicken and Dumplings


There’s something comforting about a steaming bowl of chicken and dumplings. The warm broth, tender chicken, and soft, pillowy dumplings create a dish that’s perfect for a cozy family dinner or a comforting meal on a chilly evening. If you’ve never made chicken and dumplings from scratch, you’re in for a treat. This recipe is a labor of love, but the end result is well worth the effort. Let’s dive into the art of creating delicious homemade chicken and dumplings.

Ingredients

For the Chicken and Broth:

  • 1 whole chicken, cut into pieces
  • 2 onions, chopped
  • 3 carrots, chopped
  • 3 celery stalks, chopped
  • 4 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 8 cups of chicken broth
  • 2 bay leaves
  • Salt and pepper to taste

For the Dumplings:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tablespoon baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup milk
  • 2 tablespoons melted butter

Instructions

Step 1: Prepare the Chicken and Broth

  1. In a large pot, combine the chicken pieces, onions, carrots, celery, garlic, chicken broth, bay leaves, salt, and pepper.
  2. Bring the mixture to a boil, then reduce the heat and let it simmer for about 1 hour, or until the chicken is tender and cooked through.
  3. Once the chicken is cooked, remove it from the pot and let it cool. Shred the chicken into bite-sized pieces and set aside.

Step 2: Make the Dumplings

  1. In a mixing bowl, combine the flour, baking powder, and salt.
  2. In a separate bowl, mix the milk and melted butter.
  3. Gradually add the milk mixture to the dry ingredients, stirring until a soft dough forms.

Step 3: Assemble the Dish

  1. Return the pot with the broth to a gentle boil.
  2. Drop spoonfuls of the dumpling dough into the simmering broth.
  3. Cover the pot and let the dumplings cook for 15-20 minutes, or until they are firm and cooked through.
  4. Return the shredded chicken to the pot and let everything simmer together for a few more minutes to allow the flavors to meld.

Serving Suggestion

Serve the homemade chicken and dumplings in bowls, ensuring that each serving has a generous amount of chicken, dumplings, and broth. Garnish with freshly chopped parsley for a pop of color and freshness.

Conclusion

Homemade chicken and dumplings are a true labor of love, but the reward is a dish that warms the soul and brings joy to the table. Whether you’re cooking for a special occasion or simply craving some comfort food, this recipe is sure to become a favorite in your culinary repertoire. So, roll up your sleeves, gather your ingredients, and embark on the journey of creating a pot of delicious homemade chicken and dumplings that will leave everyone asking for seconds. Enjoy!

This recipe for homemade chicken and dumplings is a testament to the joy of slow-cooked comfort food. It’s a dish that nourishes both the body and the spirit, making it a timeless classic in many households. I hope this recipe inspires you to embrace the art of homemade cooking and savor the simple pleasures of a well-crafted meal.
