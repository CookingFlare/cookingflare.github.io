Earl Grey Elegance: London Fog Mocktail Recipe


If you’re a fan of the classic London Fog tea latte, you’re going to love this refreshing twist—a London Fog mocktail! This delightful beverage combines the aromatic flavors of Earl Grey tea, creamy milk, vanilla, and a hint of sweetness, creating a sophisticated and elegant drink that’s perfect for any occasion.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups of water
  • 2 Earl Grey tea bags
  • 1/4 cup of granulated sugar
  • 1 teaspoon of vanilla extract
  • 1 cup of milk (dairy or non-dairy)
  • Ice cubes
  • Lavender sprigs for garnish (optional)

Instructions

  1. Begin by boiling 2 cups of water in a saucepan. Once the water reaches a rolling boil, remove the saucepan from the heat and add the 2 Earl Grey tea bags. Allow the tea to steep for about 5 minutes to infuse the water with the delicate flavors of the tea.

  2. After steeping, remove the tea bags and stir in the granulated sugar until it completely dissolves. The sugar will add a subtle sweetness to the mocktail.

  3. Next, stir in the vanilla extract, infusing the brew with a warm, fragrant note of vanilla. This step elevates the flavor profile of the mocktail, adding a touch of sophistication.

  4. Allow the brewed tea mixture to cool to room temperature before transferring it to a pitcher. Once cooled, refrigerate the tea for at least 1 hour, or until it’s thoroughly chilled.

  5. When you’re ready to serve the mocktail, fill your glasses with ice cubes and pour the chilled Earl Grey tea over the ice, filling each glass about three-quarters full.

  6. Slowly pour the milk over the tea, allowing it to cascade down the sides of the glass, creating a beautiful marbled effect as it blends with the tea.

  7. To add a final touch of elegance, garnish each mocktail with a sprig of fresh lavender, which not only enhances the visual appeal but also imparts a subtle floral aroma to the drink.

  8. Serve the London Fog mocktail immediately and enjoy the harmonious blend of flavors and aromas.

The Perfect Mocktail for Any Occasion

This London Fog mocktail is the epitome of sophistication and elegance. Its delicate balance of floral Earl Grey tea, creamy milk, and fragrant vanilla creates a drink that’s perfect for afternoon gatherings, brunches, or even as a refreshing pick-me-up during a warm summer day.

The versatility of this mocktail also makes it an excellent choice for entertaining. Whether you’re hosting a formal tea party or a casual get-together, the London Fog mocktail is sure to impress your guests with its refined flavors and eye-catching presentation.

Additionally, this mocktail can easily be customized to suit individual preferences. For those with a sweet tooth, a touch more sugar can be added to enhance the sweetness, while those who prefer a creamier texture can opt for a richer milk alternative. The possibilities are endless, allowing you to tailor the mocktail to your liking.

So, the next time you’re in the mood for a sophisticated and refreshing beverage, consider treating yourself and your guests to the Earl Grey elegance of a London Fog mocktail. Its alluring flavors and timeless appeal are bound to make it a favorite in your repertoire of mocktail recipes. Cheers to elegance in every sip!
