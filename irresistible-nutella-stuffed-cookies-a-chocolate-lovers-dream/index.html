Irresistible Nutella Stuffed Cookies: A Chocolate Lover's Dream


If you’re a chocolate lover like me, then you’re in for a treat! Today, I’m going to share with you a recipe for irresistible Nutella stuffed cookies that will satisfy your sweet tooth and leave you craving for more. These cookies are the perfect combination of chewy and gooey, with a surprise burst of Nutella in every bite. Trust me, once you try these cookies, you won’t be able to resist their chocolatey goodness!

Ingredients

To make these heavenly Nutella stuffed cookies, you’ll need the following ingredients:

  • 2 and 1/4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 3/4 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/2 cup packed brown sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 cup Nutella (for stuffing)

Instructions

Now, let’s get started on making these delectable cookies:

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C) and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.

  2. In a medium-sized bowl, whisk together the flour, baking soda, and salt. Set aside.

  3. In a separate large bowl, cream together the softened butter, granulated sugar, and brown sugar until light and fluffy. This should take about 2-3 minutes.

  4. Add the vanilla extract and eggs to the butter-sugar mixture, one at a time, beating well after each addition.

  5. Gradually add the dry ingredients to the wet ingredients, mixing until just combined. Be careful not to overmix; you want a soft and chewy texture.

  6. Take about 1 tablespoon of cookie dough and flatten it into a small disc on your palm. Place a teaspoon of Nutella in the center of the disc.

  7. Fold the edges of the dough over the Nutella, sealing it completely. Roll the dough between your palms to form a smooth ball. Repeat this process for the remaining dough and Nutella.

  8. Place the stuffed cookie dough balls on the prepared baking sheet, leaving about 2 inches of space between each cookie.

  9. Bake in the preheated oven for 10-12 minutes, or until the edges are golden brown. The centers may still look slightly undercooked, but they will firm up as they cool.

  10. Allow the cookies to cool on the baking sheet for 5 minutes, then transfer them to a wire rack to cool completely.

  11. Once cooled, these Nutella stuffed cookies are ready to be devoured! Serve them with a glass of cold milk for the ultimate chocolate experience.

Tips and Variations

  • You can personalize these Nutella stuffed cookies by adding chopped nuts, chocolate chips, or sprinkles to the dough before stuffing them with Nutella.

  • If you prefer a softer cookie, reduce the baking time by a minute or two. For a crispier cookie, increase the baking time slightly.

  • These cookies can be stored in an airtight container for up to 5 days. They also freeze well, so you can make a big batch and enjoy them whenever the craving strikes.

Final Thoughts

These Nutella stuffed cookies are a chocolate lover’s dream come true. With their irresistibly gooey centers and chewy exteriors, they’re bound to become a favorite in your household. Whether you’re baking them for a special occasion or just satisfying a late-night craving, these cookies are sure to impress. So go ahead, indulge in the chocolatey goodness of these Nutella stuffed cookies and enjoy every bite!
