Slow Cooker Vegetarian Chili Mac Recipes for a Comforting Meal

Are you in the mood for a hearty and comforting meal that’s also vegetarian-friendly? Look no further than these delicious slow cooker vegetarian chili mac recipes. Packed with flavor, protein, and wholesome ingredients, these recipes are sure to satisfy your cravings while providing a nutritious meal. Recipe 1: Classic Vegetarian Chili Mac Ingredients: 1 cup dried macaroni 1 can (15 ounces) kidney beans, rinsed and drained 1 can (15 ounces) black beans, rinsed and drained 1 can (15 ounces) diced tomatoes 1 onion, diced 1 bell pepper, diced 2 cloves garlic, minced 2 tablespoons chili powder 1 teaspoon cumin 1 teaspoon paprika Salt and pepper, to taste Optional toppings: shredded cheese, chopped cilantro, sour cream Instructions: In a slow cooker, combine the kidney beans, black beans, diced tomatoes, onion, bell pepper, garlic, chili powder, cumin, paprika, salt, and pepper. Stir well to combine. Cover and cook on low heat for 6-8 hours or on high heat for 3-4 hours. Cook the macaroni according to the package instructions until al dente. Drain and set aside. Just before serving, stir the cooked macaroni into the slow cooker. Allow it to heat through for 10-15 minutes. Serve hot with your choice of optional toppings such as shredded cheese, chopped cilantro, or sour cream. Recipe 2: Creamy Butternut Squash Chili Mac Ingredients: 1 cup dried macaroni 2 cups butternut squash, peeled and diced 1 can (15 ounces) white beans, rinsed and drained 1 can (15 ounces) diced tomatoes 1 onion, diced 2 cloves garlic, minced 1 tablespoon chili powder 1 teaspoon cumin 1/2 teaspoon smoked paprika 1/2 cup vegetable broth Salt and pepper, to taste Optional toppings: grated Parmesan cheese, chopped parsley Instructions: In a slow cooker, combine the butternut squash, white beans, diced tomatoes, onion, garlic, chili powder, cumin, smoked paprika, vegetable broth, salt, and pepper. Stir well to combine. Cover and cook on low heat for 6-8 hours or on high heat for 3-4 hours, until the butternut squash is tender. Using an immersion blender or a countertop blender, puree the mixture until smooth and creamy. Cook the macaroni according to the package instructions until al dente. Drain and set aside. Just before serving, stir the cooked macaroni into the slow cooker. Allow it to heat through for 10-15 minutes. Serve hot with optional toppings such as grated Parmesan cheese and chopped parsley. Recipe 3: Spicy Lentil Chili Mac Ingredients: 1 cup dried macaroni 1 cup dried lentils 1 can (15 ounces) crushed tomatoes 1 onion, diced 2 cloves garlic, minced 1 jalapeno pepper, seeded and diced 1 tablespoon chili powder 1 teaspoon cumin 1/2 teaspoon smoked paprika 3 cups vegetable broth Salt and pepper, to taste Optional toppings: sliced green onions, hot sauce Instructions: In a slow cooker, combine the lentils, crushed tomatoes, onion, garlic, jalapeno pepper, chili powder, cumin, smoked paprika, vegetable broth, salt, and pepper. Stir well to combine. Cover and cook on low heat for 6-8 hours or on high heat for 3-4 hours, until the lentils are tender. Cook the macaroni according to the package instructions until al dente. Drain and set aside. Just before serving, stir the cooked macaroni into the slow cooker. Allow it to heat through for 10-15 minutes. Serve hot with optional toppings such as sliced green onions and hot sauce. These slow cooker vegetarian chili mac recipes are a perfect combination of comfort and nutrition. They are easy to prepare and can be customized with your favorite toppings for added flavor. Slow Cooker Mexican Shredded Chicken: Versatile and Delicious

Are you looking for a delicious and easy-to-make recipe that can be used in a variety of dishes? Look no further than slow cooker Mexican shredded chicken! This recipe is a game-changer for anyone who loves Mexican cuisine, as it can be used to make tacos, burritos, quesadillas, and more. Ingredients 1 lb boneless, skinless chicken breasts 1 can of diced tomatoes with green chilies 1 can of black beans, drained and rinsed 1 can of diced green chilies 1 tablespoon of olive oil 1 teaspoon of cumin 1 teaspoon of chili powder Salt and pepper, to taste 1 cup of chicken broth Optional: jalapenos, sour cream, shredded cheese, and cilantro for topping Instructions Place the chicken breasts in the slow cooker. In a separate bowl, mix together the diced tomatoes with green chilies, black beans, diced green chilies, olive oil, cumin, chili powder, salt, and pepper. Pour the mixture over the chicken breasts in the slow cooker. Pour in the chicken broth and stir to combine. Cook on low for 6-8 hours or high for 3-4 hours. When the chicken is cooked through and shreds easily with a fork, it is done. Shred the chicken with two forks and return it to the slow cooker. Taste and adjust the seasoning as needed.
