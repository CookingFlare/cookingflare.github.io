Summer BBQ: Gluten-Free Grilling Recipes for Everyone


Summer is the perfect time to fire up the grill and enjoy delicious outdoor meals with family and friends. If you follow a gluten-free diet or have guests who do, you might be wondering how to create a flavorful BBQ spread that everyone can enjoy. Worry not! We’ve curated a collection of mouthwatering gluten-free grilling recipes that will please every palate at your next summer BBQ.

Grilled Lemon Herb Chicken Skewers

Ingredients:

  • 1.5 lbs boneless, skinless chicken breasts, cut into cubes
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • Juice of 1 lemon
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh herbs for garnish

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, whisk together olive oil, lemon juice, garlic, oregano, salt, and pepper.
  2. Add the chicken cubes to the marinade and let it sit for at least 30 minutes.
  3. Thread the marinated chicken onto skewers.
  4. Preheat the grill and cook the skewers for about 10-12 minutes, turning occasionally until chicken is cooked through.
  5. Garnish with fresh herbs before serving.

Grilled Veggie Platter with Balsamic Glaze

Ingredients:

  • Assorted vegetables (bell peppers, zucchini, mushrooms, cherry tomatoes)
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Balsamic glaze for drizzling

Instructions:

  1. Cut the vegetables into bite-sized pieces.
  2. Toss the veggies with olive oil, salt, and pepper.
  3. Grill the vegetables until they are tender and slightly charred.
  4. Arrange the grilled veggies on a platter and drizzle with balsamic glaze before serving.

Grilled Pineapple Shrimp Skewers

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb large shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 1 cup pineapple chunks
  • 2 tablespoons coconut aminos
  • 1 tablespoon honey
  • 1 teaspoon ginger, grated
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, mix coconut aminos, honey, ginger, salt, and pepper.
  2. Add shrimp and pineapple to the marinade and let it marinate for 15-20 minutes.
  3. Thread the shrimp and pineapple onto skewers.
  4. Grill the skewers for 2-3 minutes per side or until shrimp is pink and cooked through.

Grilled Stuffed Portobello Mushrooms

Ingredients:

  • 4 large portobello mushrooms
  • 1/2 cup goat cheese
  • 1/4 cup sun-dried tomatoes, chopped
  • 2 tablespoons fresh basil, chopped
  • 2 tablespoons balsamic glaze

Instructions:

  1. Remove the stems from the mushrooms and scrape out the gills.
  2. In a bowl, mix goat cheese, sun-dried tomatoes, and basil.
  3. Stuff the mushrooms with the cheese mixture.
  4. Grill the mushrooms for 5-7 minutes until the cheese is melted and mushrooms are tender.
  5. Drizzle with balsamic glaze before serving.

Grilled Peach Salad with Arugula and Feta

Ingredients:

  • 2 ripe peaches, halved and pitted
  • 4 cups arugula
  • 1/2 cup crumbled feta cheese
  • 2 tablespoons balsamic vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon honey
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Brush peach halves with olive oil and grill for 2-3 minutes per side.
  2. In a bowl, whisk together balsamic vinegar, honey, olive oil, salt, and pepper.
  3. Toss arugula with the dressing and divide onto plates.
  4. Top with grilled peaches and feta cheese.

Conclusion

With these delectable gluten-free grilling recipes, your summer BBQ will be a hit with everyone, regardless of dietary restrictions. So fire up the grill, gather your loved ones, and savor the flavors of summer with these delightful dishes. Happy grilling!
