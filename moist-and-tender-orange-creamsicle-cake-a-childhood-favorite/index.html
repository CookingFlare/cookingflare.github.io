Moist and Tender Orange Creamsicle Cake: A Childhood Favorite


There’s something truly nostalgic about the flavors of an orange creamsicle. The combination of citrusy orange and creamy vanilla takes us back to carefree days and simple pleasures. That’s why today, I’m sharing a delightful recipe for Moist and Tender Orange Creamsicle Cake – a childhood favorite that’s sure to bring back fond memories and create new ones.

Ingredients

For the Cake:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 1/2 cups granulated sugar
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1 cup fresh orange juice
  • Zest of 2 oranges
  • 1/2 cup plain yogurt
  • 3 large eggs
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

For the Creamsicle Frosting:

  • 1 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 4 cups confectioners’ sugar
  • 1/4 cup fresh orange juice
  • Zest of 1 orange
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt

Instructions

  1. Preheat and Prepare: Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease and flour two 9-inch round cake pans.

  2. Mix the Dry Ingredients: In a medium bowl, whisk together the flour, baking powder, baking soda, and salt. Set aside.

  3. Cream the Butter and Sugar: In a large mixing bowl, cream together the softened butter and granulated sugar until light and fluffy.

  4. Incorporate the Wet Ingredients: Add the eggs one at a time, beating well after each addition. Stir in the orange zest and vanilla extract.

  5. Alternate Mixing: Gradually add the dry ingredients to the wet ingredients, alternating with the orange juice and yogurt. Begin and end with the dry ingredients, mixing until just combined.

  6. Bake to Perfection: Divide the batter evenly between the prepared cake pans. Bake for 25-30 minutes or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean. Allow the cakes to cool in the pans for 10 minutes before transferring to wire racks to cool completely.

  7. Prepare the Creamsicle Frosting: In a large mixing bowl, beat the softened butter until smooth. Gradually add the confectioners’ sugar, alternating with the orange juice. Mix in the orange zest, vanilla extract, and salt until the frosting is smooth and creamy.

  8. Assemble the Cake: Once the cakes are completely cooled, place one layer on a serving plate. Spread a generous layer of the creamsicle frosting over the top. Carefully place the second cake layer on top and frost the top and sides with the remaining frosting.

  9. Enjoy the Delight: Slice into this moist and tender orange creamsicle cake and savor every nostalgic bite.

Conclusion

This Moist and Tender Orange Creamsicle Cake is a delightful treat that captures the essence of a beloved childhood flavor. Whether you’re reminiscing about summer days gone by or introducing a new generation to the joy of orange creamsicles, this cake is sure to be a hit. With its moist crumb and creamy, citrus-kissed frosting, it’s a dessert that embodies the sweetness of simpler times.

So, gather your ingredients, preheat your oven, and get ready to indulge in a slice of pure nostalgia with this delightful orange creamsicle cake.
