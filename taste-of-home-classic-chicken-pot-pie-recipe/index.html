Taste of Home: Classic Chicken Pot Pie Recipe


There are few dishes as comforting and satisfying as a classic chicken pot pie. This timeless recipe has been a staple in American households for generations, and for good reason. With its buttery, flaky crust and hearty, savory filling, chicken pot pie is the epitome of home-cooked comfort food. In this blog post, we’ll take a deep dive into the Taste of Home classic chicken pot pie recipe, exploring its history, ingredients, and step-by-step preparation.

History of Chicken Pot Pie

Chicken pot pie has a rich history that dates back to medieval Europe. Its origins can be traced to the Roman Empire, where meat pies were a popular dish. As European settlers brought their culinary traditions to America, the chicken pot pie evolved into the beloved recipe we know today. It has become a symbol of warmth, tradition, and the simple pleasures of home cooking.

Ingredients

For the Crust:

  • 2 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup cold butter, cubed
  • 1/2 cup ice water

For the Filling:

  • 1/3 cup butter
  • 1/3 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon pepper
  • 1 3/4 cups chicken broth
  • 2/3 cup milk
  • 2 1/2 cups shredded cooked chicken
  • 1 cup frozen mixed vegetables (carrots, peas, corn)
  • 1/3 cup chopped onion

Step-by-Step Preparation

  1. Prepare the Crust:

    • In a large bowl, combine the flour and salt. Cut in the butter until the mixture resembles coarse crumbs. Gradually add ice water, tossing with a fork until a ball forms. Divide the dough in half, shape into discs, wrap in plastic, and refrigerate for at least 30 minutes.

  2. Make the Filling:

    • In a large saucepan, melt the butter over medium heat. Stir in the flour, salt, and pepper until smooth. Gradually whisk in the chicken broth and milk. Bring to a boil, stirring constantly; cook and stir for 2 minutes or until thickened. Stir in the chicken, vegetables, and onion. Remove from the heat.

  3. Assemble the Pie:

    • Preheat the oven to 425°F. On a lightly floured surface, roll out one half of the dough to fit a 9-inch pie plate. Transfer the crust to the pie plate. Add the filling. Roll out the remaining dough to fit the top of the pie. Place over the filling. Trim, seal, and flute the edges. Cut slits in the top.

  4. Bake and Enjoy:

    • Bake for 35-40 minutes or until the crust is golden brown and the filling is bubbly. Let it stand for 15 minutes before cutting into the warm, comforting goodness of your homemade chicken pot pie.

Tips and Variations

  • For a twist on the classic recipe, consider adding diced potatoes or mushrooms to the filling.
  • You can use a store-bought pie crust if you’re short on time, but there’s something special about a homemade crust.
  • To make individual pot pies, simply divide the dough and filling into smaller portions and bake in ramekins.

Conclusion

The Taste of Home classic chicken pot pie recipe is a timeless favorite that never fails to warm the heart and soul. Whether you’re serving it for a cozy family dinner or bringing it to a potluck, this dish is sure to be a hit. The combination of flaky crust, creamy filling, and tender chicken creates a symphony of flavors that will transport you to a place of comfort and contentment. So, roll up your sleeves, preheat your oven, and get ready to savor the taste of home with this classic chicken pot pie recipe.
