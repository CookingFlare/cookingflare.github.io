Deliciously Creamy Bacon Ranch Pasta Salad: Perfect for BBQs


If you’re looking for the perfect dish to bring to your next BBQ, look no further! This Deliciously Creamy Bacon Ranch Pasta Salad is a crowd-pleaser that will have everyone coming back for seconds. Packed with savory bacon, crisp vegetables, and a creamy ranch dressing, this pasta salad is sure to be a hit at any gathering. Plus, it’s easy to make and can be prepared ahead of time, making it a convenient option for any outdoor event.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound of pasta (such as rotini or fusilli)
  • 1 cup of ranch dressing
  • 1/2 cup of mayonnaise
  • 1 teaspoon of garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon of onion powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 pound of bacon, cooked and crumbled
  • 1 cup of cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1 cup of diced cucumber
  • 1/2 cup of sliced black olives
  • 1/4 cup of chopped fresh chives
  • 1/2 cup of shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1/4 cup of grated Parmesan cheese

Instructions

  1. In a large pot of boiling salted water, cook the pasta according to the package instructions. Drain and rinse the pasta under cold water to stop the cooking process. Set aside.

  2. In a small bowl, whisk together the ranch dressing, mayonnaise, garlic powder, onion powder, salt, and pepper until well combined.

  3. In a large mixing bowl, combine the cooked and cooled pasta, crumbled bacon, cherry tomatoes, diced cucumber, black olives, and chives.

  4. Pour the ranch dressing mixture over the pasta salad and toss gently to coat everything evenly.

  5. Add the shredded cheddar cheese and grated Parmesan cheese to the salad and toss again to distribute the cheese throughout.

  6. Cover the pasta salad and refrigerate for at least 1 hour before serving to allow the flavors to meld together.

  7. Before serving, give the pasta salad a final toss and adjust the seasoning if needed. You can also garnish with extra chives and a sprinkle of grated Parmesan cheese for a beautiful presentation.

Tips for Success

  • Use a sturdy pasta shape like rotini or fusilli that can hold up to the creamy dressing and mix-ins without getting mushy.
  • For a lighter version, you can use Greek yogurt in place of mayonnaise in the dressing.
  • Feel free to customize the mix-ins based on your preferences. Bell peppers, red onions, or even diced grilled chicken can be great additions to this pasta salad.

Serving Suggestions

This Deliciously Creamy Bacon Ranch Pasta Salad pairs perfectly with grilled meats, making it an ideal side dish for BBQs, picnics, or potlucks. It’s also a great option for meal prep, as it can be stored in the refrigerator for a few days, allowing you to enjoy it as a quick and satisfying lunch.

Conclusion

With its creamy texture, smoky bacon flavor, and refreshing mix-ins, this pasta salad is a delightful addition to any summer gathering. Whether you’re hosting a BBQ or simply looking for a flavorful and satisfying dish to enjoy, this recipe is a must-try. So, gather your ingredients, whip up a batch of this Deliciously Creamy Bacon Ranch Pasta Salad, and get ready to impress your friends and family with this delectable creation!
