Homemade Chicken and Waffles: Comfort Food Breakfast Recipe


There’s something truly special about the combination of crispy fried chicken and fluffy waffles. This classic comfort food dish has been a favorite in the American South for generations, and it’s not hard to see why. The contrast of savory and sweet, crunchy and soft, makes for a truly satisfying meal that’s perfect for a leisurely weekend breakfast or brunch. In this recipe, we’ll walk you through how to create your own homemade chicken and waffles that will rival any restaurant’s version.

Ingredients

For the Fried Chicken:

  • 4 chicken thighs, bone-in and skin-on
  • 1 cup buttermilk
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • Vegetable oil, for frying

For the Waffles:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tablespoon baking powder
  • 1 tablespoon sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 3/4 cups milk
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, melted
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

For the Maple Butter:

  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1/4 cup pure maple syrup
  • Pinch of salt

Instructions

Making the Fried Chicken:

  1. Start by marinating the chicken thighs in buttermilk for at least 1 hour, or preferably overnight. This will help tenderize the meat and infuse it with flavor.
  2. In a shallow dish, mix together the flour, salt, black pepper, paprika, and garlic powder.
  3. Remove the chicken from the buttermilk, allowing any excess to drip off, then coat each piece thoroughly in the seasoned flour mixture.
  4. In a large skillet, heat about 1 inch of vegetable oil over medium-high heat until it reaches 350°F.
  5. Carefully place the chicken thighs in the hot oil, skin side down, and fry for about 6-8 minutes per side, or until golden brown and cooked through. Use a meat thermometer to ensure the internal temperature reaches 165°F.
  6. Once cooked, transfer the chicken to a wire rack set over a baking sheet to drain any excess oil.

Making the Waffles:

  1. Preheat your waffle iron according to the manufacturer’s instructions.
  2. In a large bowl, whisk together the flour, baking powder, sugar, and salt.
  3. In a separate bowl, beat the eggs, then stir in the milk, melted butter, and vanilla extract.
  4. Pour the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients and stir until just combined. It’s okay if there are a few lumps.
  5. Cook the waffles according to your waffle iron’s instructions until they are golden and crisp.

Making the Maple Butter:

  1. In a small bowl, combine the softened butter, maple syrup, and a pinch of salt. Mix until smooth and well combined.

Serving the Chicken and Waffles:

To serve, place a crispy waffle on a plate and top it with a piece of fried chicken. Drizzle the whole dish with the maple butter, and if you like, you can also add a sprinkle of chopped fresh herbs for a pop of color and flavor.

This homemade chicken and waffles recipe is a labor of love, but the end result is well worth the effort. The combination of crispy, juicy chicken and fluffy, buttery waffles drizzled with sweet maple butter is a true indulgence that’s perfect for a special weekend breakfast or brunch with family and friends. So, next time you’re in the mood for a comforting and satisfying meal, give this recipe a try and savor every delicious bite!
