Mouthwatering Chicken Cashew Stir Fry Recipe


If you’re looking for a quick and delicious meal that combines the perfect balance of flavors and textures, then this Chicken Cashew Stir Fry recipe is sure to become a favorite in your kitchen. This dish is not only easy to make but also packs a punch with its savory, sweet, and nutty notes that will leave your taste buds craving for more.

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb boneless, skinless chicken breasts, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 1 red bell pepper, sliced thinly
  • 1 yellow bell pepper, sliced thinly
  • 1 cup snow peas
  • 1 cup roasted cashew nuts
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1-inch piece of ginger, grated
  • 3 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons oyster sauce
  • 1 tablespoon hoisin sauce
  • 1 tablespoon cornstarch
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • Green onions for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Marinate the Chicken: In a bowl, combine the chicken pieces with soy sauce, cornstarch, and a pinch of salt. Let it marinate for about 15-20 minutes.

  2. Prepare the Sauce: In another bowl, mix together the oyster sauce, hoisin sauce, sugar, and a splash of water. Set the sauce aside for later use.

  3. Stir-Fry: Heat vegetable oil in a large pan or wok over medium-high heat. Add the marinated chicken and stir-fry until it is cooked through and slightly browned. Remove the chicken from the pan and set it aside.

  4. Vegetables: In the same pan, add a bit more oil if needed. Stir in the garlic and ginger until fragrant. Add the sliced bell peppers and snow peas. Stir-fry for a few minutes until the vegetables are slightly tender but still crisp.

  5. Combine: Return the cooked chicken to the pan. Pour the prepared sauce over the chicken and vegetables. Toss everything together to ensure the sauce coats all the ingredients evenly.

  6. Cashews: Add in the roasted cashew nuts and give it a final stir to incorporate them into the stir-fry.

  7. Serve: Once everything is heated through and well combined, remove the pan from the heat. Taste and adjust the seasoning with salt and pepper if needed.

  8. Garnish: Sprinkle some chopped green onions over the dish for a pop of freshness and color.

This Chicken Cashew Stir Fry is best served hot over a bed of steamed rice or noodles. The tender chicken, crunchy cashews, and vibrant vegetables come together in a harmonious medley of flavors that will surely impress your family and friends. It’s a versatile dish that you can customize by adding your favorite vegetables or adjusting the level of spice to suit your taste preferences.

Whether you’re a seasoned home cook or just starting your culinary journey, this recipe is a great way to elevate your cooking skills and enjoy a restaurant-quality meal in the comfort of your own home. So, gather your ingredients, fire up your stove, and get ready to whip up a delightful Chicken Cashew Stir Fry that will tantalize your senses and satisfy your hunger cravings. Happy cooking!
