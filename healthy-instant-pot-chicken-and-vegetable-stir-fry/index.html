Healthy Instant Pot Chicken and Vegetable Stir-Fry


Are you tired of spending hours in the kitchen preparing meals? Do you want a delicious and healthy dish that can be made in a flash? Look no further! In this recipe, I will show you how to make a mouthwatering Instant Pot Chicken and Vegetable Stir-Fry that will satisfy your cravings and keep you feeling nourished.

Ingredients

To make this delightful dish, you will need the following ingredients:

  • 1 pound of boneless, skinless chicken breasts, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 2 tablespoons of olive oil
  • 1 medium onion, thinly sliced
  • 3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 red bell pepper, thinly sliced
  • 1 yellow bell pepper, thinly sliced
  • 1 cup of broccoli florets
  • 1 cup of sugar snap peas
  • 1 cup of sliced carrots
  • 1/4 cup of low-sodium soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons of oyster sauce
  • 1 tablespoon of cornstarch
  • 1/2 cup of chicken broth
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Optional garnish: chopped green onions, sesame seeds

Instructions

  1. Turn on your Instant Pot and select the “Sauté” function. Add the olive oil and let it heat up for a minute.

  2. Add the chicken pieces to the Instant Pot and cook until they are browned on all sides. This will take about 5 minutes. Remove the chicken from the pot and set it aside.

  3. In the same pot, add the sliced onions and minced garlic. Sauté until they are fragrant and translucent, which should take about 2 minutes.

  4. Add the sliced bell peppers, broccoli florets, sugar snap peas, and carrots to the pot. Sauté for another 3-4 minutes, or until the vegetables start to soften.

  5. In a small bowl, whisk together the soy sauce, oyster sauce, cornstarch, and chicken broth. Pour this mixture into the Instant Pot and stir well to combine.

  6. Return the chicken to the pot, making sure to coat it with the sauce and vegetables. Season with salt and pepper to taste.

  7. Secure the lid on the Instant Pot and set the valve to the sealing position. Cook on high pressure for 5 minutes.

  8. Once the cooking time is complete, do a quick release of the pressure. Carefully remove the lid and give the stir-fry a good stir.

  9. Serve the Healthy Instant Pot Chicken and Vegetable Stir-Fry over steamed rice or noodles. Garnish with chopped green onions and sesame seeds if desired.

The Benefits of Instant Pot Cooking

Using an Instant Pot to make this Chicken and Vegetable Stir-Fry has many advantages. First and foremost, it saves you valuable time in the kitchen. The Instant Pot’s pressure cooking function cooks food in a fraction of the time it would take using traditional methods. This means you can enjoy a healthy and delicious meal without spending hours in the kitchen.

Additionally, the Instant Pot helps to retain the nutrients in your food. The high pressure and sealed environment prevent the loss of vitamins and minerals that can occur with other cooking methods. This means that your Healthy Instant Pot Chicken and Vegetable Stir-Fry will not only taste great but will also provide you with the essential nutrients your body needs.

Conclusion

In conclusion, this Healthy Instant Pot Chicken and Vegetable Stir-Fry is the perfect dish for those who are looking for a quick, easy, and nutritious meal. With just a few simple ingredients and the help of an Instant Pot, you can have a delicious stir-fry on the table in no time. So why wait? Give this recipe a try and enjoy a healthy and satisfying meal today!
