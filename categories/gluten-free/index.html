Wholesome and Filling: Gluten-Free Quinoa Stuffed Peppers

Are you looking for a delicious and nutritious meal that is both gluten-free and filling? Look no further than these wholesome Quinoa Stuffed Peppers! Packed with flavor and loaded with wholesome ingredients, this recipe is perfect for those following a gluten-free diet or anyone who wants to enjoy a hearty and satisfying meal. Ingredients 4 large bell peppers (any color) 1 cup quinoa 2 cups vegetable broth 1 tablespoon olive oil 1 onion, diced 2 cloves garlic, minced 1 zucchini, diced 1 cup mushrooms, sliced 1 cup spinach, chopped 1 can diced tomatoes, drained 1 teaspoon dried oregano 1 teaspoon dried basil Salt and pepper to taste Grated cheese (optional, for topping) Instructions Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C). Cut the tops off the bell peppers and remove the seeds and membranes. Place the peppers in a baking dish, standing upright. Rinse the quinoa under cold water to remove any bitterness. In a saucepan, bring the vegetable broth to a boil. Add the quinoa, reduce the heat to low, cover, and simmer for about 15 minutes or until the quinoa is cooked and the liquid is absorbed. While the quinoa is cooking, heat the olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Add the onion and garlic and sauté until fragrant and translucent. Add the zucchini, mushrooms, and spinach to the skillet. Cook until the vegetables are tender and any excess moisture has evaporated. Stir in the diced tomatoes, dried oregano, dried basil, salt, and pepper. Cook for an additional 2 minutes to allow the flavors to meld together. Remove the skillet from the heat and add the cooked quinoa. Mix well to combine all the ingredients. Spoon the quinoa mixture into the bell peppers, filling them to the top. If desired, sprinkle grated cheese on top of each pepper. Cover the baking dish with foil and bake for 30 minutes. Remove the foil and bake for an additional 10 minutes or until the peppers are tender and the cheese is golden and bubbly. Remove from the oven and let the stuffed peppers cool for a few minutes before serving. Enjoy!
