Savory and Satisfying Italian Sausage and Pepper Pasta


If you’re craving a hearty and flavorful dinner that’s both comforting and easy to make, look no further than this Italian Sausage and Pepper Pasta recipe. Bursting with savory Italian sausage, sweet bell peppers, and aromatic herbs, this dish brings together the best of Italian flavors in every bite. Whether you’re cooking for a family dinner, hosting friends, or simply treating yourself to a delicious meal, this recipe is sure to satisfy.

Ingredients

To create this delectable Italian-inspired dish, gather the following ingredients:

  • Italian Sausage: Opt for a mix of sweet and spicy Italian sausage links for depth of flavor.
  • Bell Peppers: Use a combination of red, green, and yellow bell peppers for color and sweetness.
  • Onion: A yellow or white onion adds a savory base to the dish.
  • Garlic: Fresh garlic cloves minced finely for a robust flavor.
  • Tomato Paste: Adds richness and depth to the sauce.
  • Crushed Tomatoes: Provides a smooth texture and vibrant tomato flavor.
  • Italian Seasoning: A blend of dried herbs like oregano, basil, and thyme for an authentic Italian taste.
  • Chicken Broth: Adds depth of flavor and helps to thin the sauce.
  • Pasta: Choose your favorite pasta shape such as penne, fusilli, or spaghetti.
  • Salt and Pepper: To season the dish to taste.
  • Fresh Basil: Chopped for garnish and added freshness.

Instructions

Step 1: Prepare the Ingredients

Begin by slicing the Italian sausage into bite-sized pieces. Dice the bell peppers and onion into uniform pieces. Mince the garlic cloves finely to release their flavor during cooking.

Step 2: Brown the Sausage

Heat a large skillet or Dutch oven over medium-high heat. Add a drizzle of olive oil and then add the sliced Italian sausage. Cook, stirring occasionally, until the sausage is browned and cooked through. This step helps to develop deep flavors in the dish.

Step 3: Sauté the Vegetables

Once the sausage is cooked, add the diced bell peppers and onion to the skillet. Sauté for 4-5 minutes until the vegetables begin to soften and become fragrant. Stir in the minced garlic and cook for an additional minute until aromatic.

Step 4: Create the Sauce

Push the sausage and vegetables to one side of the skillet. Add the tomato paste to the empty side and cook for 1-2 minutes, stirring constantly, until it darkens slightly in color. This caramelization process enhances the richness of the sauce. Then, stir the tomato paste into the sausage and vegetables.

Step 5: Simmer the Sauce

Pour in the crushed tomatoes and chicken broth, stirring well to combine all the ingredients. Season the sauce with Italian seasoning, salt, and pepper to taste. Bring the mixture to a simmer, then reduce the heat to low. Let the sauce simmer gently for 15-20 minutes, allowing the flavors to meld together and the sauce to thicken slightly.

Step 6: Cook the Pasta

While the sauce is simmering, cook the pasta according to the package instructions in a large pot of salted boiling water until al dente. Reserve a cup of pasta cooking water before draining.

Step 7: Combine and Serve

Once the pasta is cooked, add it directly to the skillet with the sausage and pepper sauce. Toss the pasta gently in the sauce, adding a splash of reserved pasta water if needed to loosen the sauce and help it coat the pasta evenly.

Step 8: Garnish and Enjoy

Remove the skillet from the heat and sprinkle the dish with freshly chopped basil for a burst of freshness and color. Serve the Italian Sausage and Pepper Pasta immediately, piping hot, with additional grated Parmesan cheese on the side if desired.

Tips for Success

  • Choose Quality Sausage: Opt for high-quality Italian sausage for the best flavor. You can adjust the spice level by choosing mild or hot sausage according to your preference.
  • Balance the Flavors: Taste and adjust the seasoning of the sauce before adding the pasta. You can add a pinch of sugar to balance the acidity of the tomatoes if needed.
  • Don’t Overcook the Pasta: Cook the pasta just until al dente to ensure it maintains a pleasing texture when combined with the sauce.

Conclusion

This Italian Sausage and Pepper Pasta recipe is a perfect example of how simple ingredients can come together to create a dish that’s both comforting and satisfying. With its robust flavors and colorful presentation, it’s sure to become a favorite in your repertoire of go-to meals. Whether you’re cooking for a crowd or enjoying a quiet dinner at home, this dish promises to deliver on taste and ease of preparation. So, gather your ingredients and get ready to enjoy a taste of Italy right in your own kitchen. Buon appetito!
