Mouthwatering Keto Coconut Shrimp with Dipping Sauce Recipe


Are you looking for a delicious and healthy keto-friendly dish that will impress your family and friends? Look no further than this mouthwatering Keto Coconut Shrimp with Dipping Sauce recipe! Not only is this dish incredibly flavorful and satisfying, but it’s also low in carbs and packed with protein, making it the perfect addition to your keto meal plan.

Ingredients

For the Coconut Shrimp:

  • 1 pound large shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 1 cup unsweetened shredded coconut
  • 1/2 cup almond flour
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Cooking oil for frying

For the Dipping Sauce:

  • 1/4 cup mayonnaise
  • 1 tablespoon sugar-free ketchup
  • 1 tablespoon Sriracha
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon smoked paprika
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

Coconut Shrimp:

  1. In a shallow bowl, whisk the eggs together.
  2. In another shallow bowl, mix the shredded coconut, almond flour, garlic powder, paprika, salt, and pepper.
  3. Dip each shrimp into the egg mixture, allowing any excess to drip off, then coat it in the coconut mixture, pressing gently to adhere.
  4. Heat the cooking oil in a large skillet over medium heat.
  5. Fry the coated shrimp in batches for 2-3 minutes on each side, or until golden brown and crispy.
  6. Remove the shrimp from the skillet and place them on a plate lined with paper towels to drain any excess oil.

Dipping Sauce:

  1. In a small bowl, whisk together the mayonnaise, sugar-free ketchup, Sriracha, garlic powder, smoked paprika, salt, and pepper until smooth and well combined.
  2. Adjust the seasoning to taste, adding more Sriracha for extra heat or more smoked paprika for a smokier flavor.

Serve the crispy coconut shrimp with the spicy dipping sauce on the side, and get ready to indulge in a flavor explosion that’s perfect for any occasion.

Why You’ll Love This Recipe

Not only is this Keto Coconut Shrimp with Dipping Sauce recipe incredibly delicious, but it’s also a healthier alternative to traditional fried shrimp. The combination of the crispy coconut coating and the spicy, creamy dipping sauce creates a harmony of flavors and textures that will leave your taste buds dancing.

Additionally, this dish is perfect for those following a low-carb or ketogenic diet, as it’s high in healthy fats, moderate in protein, and low in carbs. The use of almond flour and unsweetened shredded coconut keeps the dish grain-free and gluten-free, making it suitable for those with dietary restrictions.

Final Thoughts

Whether you’re hosting a dinner party, looking for a crowd-pleasing appetizer, or simply craving a flavorful and satisfying meal, this Keto Coconut Shrimp with Dipping Sauce recipe is sure to become a staple in your kitchen. With its irresistible crunch, succulent shrimp, and bold dipping sauce, it’s a dish that will have everyone coming back for more.

So, gather your ingredients, fire up the skillet, and treat yourself to this mouthwatering keto-friendly dish that’s as impressive as it is delicious!
