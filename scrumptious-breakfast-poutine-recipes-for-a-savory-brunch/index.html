Scrumptious Breakfast Poutine Recipes for a Savory Brunch


Whether you’re looking for a hearty breakfast to start your day or a satisfying brunch dish to impress your guests, breakfast poutine is the ultimate comfort food that never fails to deliver on flavor. This Canadian-inspired dish takes the classic poutine to a whole new level by adding breakfast elements such as eggs, bacon, and sausage. If you’re ready to elevate your brunch game, here are three scrumptious breakfast poutine recipes that will leave everyone craving for more.

Classic Breakfast Poutine

Ingredients:

  • 4 medium potatoes, cut into fries
  • 1 cup cheese curds
  • 4 slices of bacon, cooked and crumbled
  • 4 large eggs
  • 2 tablespoons chopped chives
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Hollandaise sauce (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 425°F (220°C) and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
  2. Spread the potato fries in a single layer on the baking sheet, drizzle with olive oil, and season with salt and pepper. Bake for 30-35 minutes or until golden and crispy.
  3. While the fries are baking, cook the eggs according to your preference (poached, fried, or scrambled).
  4. Once the fries are ready, divide them among serving plates or bowls. Sprinkle with cheese curds and crumbled bacon.
  5. Top the poutine with the cooked eggs and drizzle with hollandaise sauce if desired. Garnish with chopped chives and a sprinkle of black pepper.

Vegetarian Breakfast Poutine

Ingredients:

  • 4 medium sweet potatoes, cut into fries
  • 1 cup vegetarian cheese curds
  • 1 cup vegetarian sausage crumbles
  • 4 large eggs
  • 2 tablespoons chopped green onions
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Vegetarian gravy (store-bought or homemade)

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C) and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
  2. Spread the sweet potato fries on the baking sheet, drizzle with olive oil, and season with salt and pepper. Bake for 25-30 minutes or until tender and slightly crispy.
  3. In a skillet, cook the vegetarian sausage crumbles according to the package instructions.
  4. While the fries are baking, prepare the eggs to your liking.
  5. Once the sweet potato fries are done, divide them among serving plates or bowls. Sprinkle with vegetarian cheese curds and cooked sausage crumbles.
  6. Top the poutine with the cooked eggs and drizzle with vegetarian gravy. Garnish with chopped green onions and a sprinkle of black pepper.

Mexican-Inspired Breakfast Poutine

Ingredients:

  • 4 medium potatoes, cut into fries
  • 1 cup Mexican cheese blend
  • 1 cup chorizo, cooked and crumbled
  • 4 large eggs
  • 1 avocado, sliced
  • 2 tablespoons chopped cilantro
  • Salsa for topping

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 425°F (220°C) and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
  2. Spread the potato fries on the baking sheet, drizzle with olive oil, and season with salt and pepper. Bake for 30-35 minutes or until crispy.
  3. While the fries are baking, cook the chorizo in a skillet until fully cooked and crumbled.
  4. Prepare the eggs to your liking while the fries are in the oven.
  5. Once the fries are ready, divide them among serving plates or bowls. Sprinkle with Mexican cheese blend and cooked chorizo.
  6. Top the poutine with the cooked eggs, sliced avocado, and a spoonful of salsa. Garnish with chopped cilantro.

There you have it – three delicious and unique breakfast poutine recipes that are perfect for a savory brunch. Whether you prefer the classic version, a vegetarian twist, or a spicy Mexican-inspired rendition, these recipes are sure to satisfy your cravings and impress your friends and family. Enjoy your brunch poutine and get ready for a flavor-packed meal that will become a new favorite at your table!
