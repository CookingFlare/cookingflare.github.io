Grilled Pineapple: Sweet and Tangy Recipes


Grilled pineapple is a delightful treat that combines the sweetness of the fruit with a smoky and caramelized flavor. Whether you’re hosting a barbecue or simply looking for a unique dessert, grilled pineapple is a versatile ingredient that can be enjoyed in various ways. In this blog post, we’ll explore some delicious sweet and tangy recipes that will surely impress your taste buds.

Grilled Pineapple with Cinnamon Sugar

Preparation time: 10 minutes
Grilling time: 6-8 minutes
Serving size: 4

Ingredients:

  • 1 pineapple, peeled and cored
  • 1 tablespoon melted butter
  • 2 tablespoons brown sugar
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your grill to medium-high heat.
  2. Cut the pineapple into thick slices, about 1/2 inch thick.
  3. In a small bowl, combine the melted butter, brown sugar, and ground cinnamon.
  4. Brush both sides of each pineapple slice with the cinnamon sugar mixture.
  5. Place the pineapple slices on the preheated grill and cook for 3-4 minutes per side, or until grill marks appear and the pineapple is slightly softened.
  6. Remove the pineapple slices from the grill and let them cool for a few minutes before serving.
  7. Serve the grilled pineapple slices as a refreshing dessert or alongside grilled meats for a delightful twist.

Grilled Pineapple Salsa

Preparation time: 15 minutes
Grilling time: 6-8 minutes
Serving size: 6

Ingredients:

  • 1 pineapple, peeled and cored
  • 1 red bell pepper, seeded and diced
  • 1/2 red onion, diced
  • 1 jalapeno pepper, seeded and finely chopped
  • 1/4 cup fresh cilantro, chopped
  • Juice of 1 lime
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your grill to medium-high heat.
  2. Cut the pineapple into spears, about 1/2 inch thick.
  3. Place the pineapple spears on the preheated grill and cook for 3-4 minutes per side, or until grill marks appear and the pineapple is slightly softened.
  4. Remove the pineapple spears from the grill and let them cool for a few minutes.
  5. Dice the grilled pineapple and transfer it to a medium-sized bowl.
  6. Add the red bell pepper, red onion, jalapeno pepper, fresh cilantro, lime juice, salt, and pepper to the bowl with the grilled pineapple.
  7. Gently toss all the ingredients together until well combined.
  8. Let the salsa sit for at least 10 minutes to allow the flavors to meld together.
  9. Serve the grilled pineapple salsa as a topping for tacos, grilled fish, or as a snack with tortilla chips.

Grilled Pineapple Upside-Down Cake

Preparation time: 20 minutes
Grilling time: 25-30 minutes
Serving size: 8

Ingredients:

  • 1 pineapple, peeled and cored
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, melted
  • 1 cup brown sugar
  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • Whipped cream or vanilla ice cream for serving (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your grill to medium heat.
  2. Cut the pineapple into thick slices, about 1/2 inch thick.
  3. Brush both sides of the pineapple slices with melted butter.
  4. Sprinkle brown sugar evenly over one side of each pineapple slice.
  5. Place the pineapple slices, sugar-side down, on the preheated grill and cook for 3-4 minutes, or until the sugar has caramelized and the pineapple is slightly softened.
  6. Remove the pineapple slices from the grill and let them cool.
  7. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C).
  8. In a medium-sized bowl, whisk together the flour, baking powder, and salt.
  9. In a separate large bowl, beat together the granulated sugar, milk, eggs, and vanilla extract until well combined.
  10. Gradually add the dry ingredients to the wet ingredients, mixing until just combined.
  11. Grease a 9-inch round cake pan and arrange the grilled pineapple slices in a single layer on the bottom of the pan.
  12. Pour the cake batter over the pineapple slices, spreading it evenly.
  13. Bake the cake in the preheated oven for 25-30 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.
  14. Remove the cake from the oven and let it cool in the pan for 10 minutes.
  15. Invert the cake onto a serving plate, allowing the pineapple slices to be on top.
  16. Serve the grilled pineapple upside-down cake warm or at room temperature, optionally topped with whipped cream or vanilla ice cream.

Grilled pineapple is a versatile ingredient that can be enjoyed in various ways, from simple desserts to flavorful salsas and even baked goods. The smoky and caramelized flavors of the grilled pineapple add a unique twist to these recipes, making them a hit at any gathering. So fire up your grill and give these sweet and tangy grilled pineapple recipes a try!
