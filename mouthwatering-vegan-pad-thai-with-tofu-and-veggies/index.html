Mouthwatering Vegan Pad Thai with Tofu and Veggies

3 min · 598 words

If you’re looking for a vibrant and flavorful vegan dish that’s quick and easy to prepare, look no further than this mouthwatering vegan pad Thai with tofu and veggies. This recipe combines the bold flavors of traditional pad Thai with the goodness of plant-based ingredients, making it a perfect choice for a satisfying and nutritious meal.

Ingredients:

  • 8 oz. rice noodles
  • 1 block extra-firm tofu, drained and pressed
  • 2 cups mixed vegetables (such as bell peppers, bean sprouts, and carrots)
  • 1/4 cup chopped peanuts
  • 2 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 3 tbsp tamarind paste
  • 2 tbsp maple syrup
  • 1/4 cup water
  • 1 lime, juiced
  • Red pepper flakes (optional)
  • Fresh cilantro for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the Rice Noodles:

    • Begin by soaking the rice noodles in a bowl of hot water for about 10-15 minutes, or until they are tender but still slightly firm. Once done, drain and set aside.

  2. Prep the Tofu:

    • Cut the pressed tofu into small cubes. In a large pan, heat 1 tablespoon of vegetable oil over medium-high heat. Add the tofu cubes and cook for 5-7 minutes, or until they are golden brown and crispy. Once done, transfer the tofu to a plate and set aside.

  3. Cook the Vegetables:

    • In the same pan, add the remaining vegetable oil and minced garlic. Sauté for a minute until fragrant. Add the mixed vegetables and cook for 3-4 minutes, or until they are tender-crisp.

  4. Make the Pad Thai Sauce:

    • In a small bowl, whisk together the soy sauce, tamarind paste, maple syrup, water, lime juice, and red pepper flakes (if using). Adjust the sweetness and tanginess to your preference.

  5. Bring It All Together:

    • Add the soaked rice noodles to the pan with the vegetables. Pour the pad Thai sauce over the noodles and vegetables. Toss everything together until the noodles are well coated with the sauce.

  6. Finish and Serve:

    • Finally, add the cooked tofu to the pan and gently toss to combine. Once everything is heated through, remove from the heat. Serve the vegan pad Thai garnished with chopped peanuts and fresh cilantro.

This vegan pad Thai is a delightful harmony of textures and flavors, with the chewy rice noodles, crispy tofu, and crunchy vegetables coming together in a symphony of taste. The tangy and slightly sweet sauce perfectly complements the dish, making every bite a burst of deliciousness.

Health Benefits:

This vegan pad Thai offers a multitude of health benefits. Tofu is an excellent source of plant-based protein and provides essential amino acids, while the mixed vegetables offer an array of vitamins, minerals, and dietary fiber. The use of rice noodles makes this dish gluten-free, catering to individuals with dietary restrictions.

Final Thoughts:

Whether you’re a vegan, a lover of Thai cuisine, or simply someone looking to expand their culinary horizons, this vegan pad Thai with tofu and veggies is a must-try. It’s a dish that’s not only satisfying to the taste buds but also nourishing to the body, making it a delightful addition to your recipe repertoire. So, why not gather the ingredients and embark on a culinary adventure to create this delectable vegan masterpiece? Your taste buds will thank you!
