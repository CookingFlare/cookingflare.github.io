Paleo Pork Tenderloin Recipes: 5 Delicious and Grain-Free Options


If you’re looking for delicious and healthy pork tenderloin recipes that are grain-free and adhere to the paleo diet, you’ve come to the right place. Pork tenderloin is a versatile cut of meat that is lean, tender, and packed with flavor. In this blog post, we’ll explore five mouth-watering recipes that will satisfy your taste buds without compromising your commitment to a healthy lifestyle. So, let’s get cooking!

1. Garlic and Herb Crusted Pork Tenderloin

Ingredients:

  • 1 pork tenderloin
  • 3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons of fresh rosemary, chopped
  • 2 tablespoons of fresh thyme, chopped
  • 1 tablespoon of olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C).
  2. In a small bowl, combine the minced garlic, rosemary, thyme, olive oil, salt, and pepper.
  3. Rub the garlic and herb mixture all over the pork tenderloin, ensuring it is evenly coated.
  4. Place the tenderloin on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper.
  5. Roast in the preheated oven for 25-30 minutes or until the internal temperature reaches 145°F (63°C).
  6. Remove from the oven and let it rest for 5 minutes before slicing.
  7. Serve with your favorite side dishes and enjoy!

2. Balsamic Glazed Pork Tenderloin

Ingredients:

  • 1 pork tenderloin
  • ¼ cup of balsamic vinegar
  • 2 tablespoons of coconut aminos
  • 1 tablespoon of raw honey
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your grill to medium-high heat.
  2. In a small bowl, whisk together the balsamic vinegar, coconut aminos, honey, minced garlic, salt, and pepper.
  3. Place the pork tenderloin on the grill and brush it with the balsamic glaze.
  4. Grill for 15-20 minutes, turning occasionally and basting with the glaze.
  5. Remove from the grill and let it rest for 5 minutes before slicing.
  6. Serve with a drizzle of the remaining glaze and enjoy!

3. Citrus and Herb Marinated Pork Tenderloin

Ingredients:

  • 1 pork tenderloin
  • Juice of 2 oranges
  • Juice of 1 lemon
  • 2 tablespoons of olive oil
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon of fresh thyme, chopped
  • 1 tablespoon of fresh rosemary, chopped
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a large bowl, whisk together the orange juice, lemon juice, olive oil, minced garlic, thyme, rosemary, salt, and pepper.
  2. Place the pork tenderloin in a resealable plastic bag and pour the marinade over it.
  3. Seal the bag and marinate in the refrigerator for at least 2 hours, or overnight for maximum flavor.
  4. Preheat your grill to medium-high heat.
  5. Remove the tenderloin from the marinade and discard the excess.
  6. Grill the pork for 15-20 minutes, turning occasionally, until the internal temperature reaches 145°F (63°C).
  7. Let it rest for 5 minutes before slicing.
  8. Serve with a squeeze of fresh citrus juice and enjoy!

4. Spicy Mustard and Herb Pork Tenderloin

Ingredients:

  • 1 pork tenderloin
  • 2 tablespoons of Dijon mustard
  • 1 tablespoon of whole grain mustard
  • 1 tablespoon of olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon of paprika
  • 1 teaspoon of dried thyme
  • ½ teaspoon of cayenne pepper
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C).
  2. In a small bowl, whisk together the Dijon mustard, whole grain mustard, olive oil, paprika, dried thyme, cayenne pepper, salt, and pepper.
  3. Rub the mustard and herb mixture all over the pork tenderloin, ensuring it is evenly coated.
  4. Place the tenderloin on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper.
  5. Roast in the preheated oven for 25-30 minutes or until the internal temperature reaches 145°F (63°C).
  6. Remove from the oven and let it rest for 5 minutes before slicing.
  7. Serve with a dollop of mustard on the side for an extra kick!

5. Apple Cider Glazed Pork Tenderloin

Ingredients:

  • 1 pork tenderloin
  • ½ cup of apple cider
  • 2 tablespoons of coconut aminos
  • 1 tablespoon of apple cider vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon of raw honey
  • 1 teaspoon of ground cinnamon
  • ½ teaspoon of ground nutmeg
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your grill to medium-high heat.
  2. In a small saucepan, combine the apple cider, coconut aminos, apple cider vinegar, honey, cinnamon, nutmeg, salt, and pepper.
  3. Simmer the glaze over low heat for 10 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  4. Place the pork tenderloin on the grill and brush it with the apple cider glaze.
  5. Grill for 15-20 minutes, turning occasionally and basting with the glaze.
  6. Remove from the grill and let it rest for 5 minutes before slicing.
  7. Serve with a drizzle of the remaining glaze and enjoy!

That concludes our list of delicious and grain-free paleo pork tenderloin recipes. We hope you enjoy trying these recipes and incorporating them into your healthy lifestyle. Remember, cooking is a creative process, so feel free to add your own personal touch to these recipes. Bon appétit!
