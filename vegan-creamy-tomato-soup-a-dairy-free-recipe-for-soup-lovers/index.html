Vegan Creamy Tomato Soup: A Dairy-Free Recipe for Soup Lovers


Are you a soup lover looking for a delectable and dairy-free option? Look no further! This vegan creamy tomato soup recipe is the perfect solution for those craving a warm and comforting bowl of soup without any animal products. With its rich and velvety texture, this recipe will surely satisfy your taste buds and leave you feeling nourished and content.

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 medium-sized onion, finely chopped
  • 3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 can (400g) of crushed tomatoes
  • 2 cups vegetable broth
  • 1 can (400ml) of coconut milk
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon smoked paprika
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • Fresh basil leaves, for garnish

Instructions

  1. In a large pot, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the chopped onion and minced garlic, and sauté until they turn translucent and fragrant.

  2. Next, add the crushed tomatoes, vegetable broth, coconut milk, dried basil, dried oregano, and smoked paprika to the pot. Stir well to combine all the ingredients.

  3. Bring the mixture to a gentle boil, then reduce the heat to low and let it simmer for about 20 minutes. This will allow all the flavors to meld together and develop a rich taste.

  4. After simmering, remove the pot from the heat and let the soup cool slightly. Using an immersion blender or a regular blender, carefully blend the soup until it reaches a smooth and creamy consistency.

  5. Return the blended soup to the pot and place it back on the stove over low heat. Season with salt and pepper to taste, and stir well to incorporate the seasoning.

  6. Allow the soup to simmer for another 5 minutes, ensuring it is heated through. This will also allow the flavors to further develop and intensify.

  7. Once ready, ladle the vegan creamy tomato soup into bowls. Garnish with fresh basil leaves for an extra burst of flavor and a beautiful presentation.

  8. Serve the soup hot and enjoy its velvety texture and robust flavors. Pair it with some crusty bread or a side salad for a complete and satisfying meal.

Tips and Variations

  • For a thicker soup, you can add a tablespoon of cornstarch mixed with a tablespoon of water to the soup before blending. This will give it a creamier consistency.

  • If you prefer a spicier kick, you can add a pinch of red chili flakes or a dash of hot sauce to the soup while it simmers.

  • Feel free to get creative with the garnishes. Besides fresh basil leaves, you can also top the soup with a drizzle of coconut cream, chopped chives, or a sprinkle of nutritional yeast for a cheesy flavor.

  • This recipe is versatile, allowing you to customize it to your taste preferences. You can add additional vegetables like bell peppers or carrots for added texture and flavor.

  • Leftovers can be stored in an airtight container in the refrigerator for up to 3 days. Simply reheat the soup on the stovetop over low heat before serving.

Conclusion

Vegan creamy tomato soup is a delightful and dairy-free alternative for soup enthusiasts. It’s creamy, flavorful, and incredibly satisfying, making it a perfect choice for a cozy meal or a quick weeknight dinner. With its simple ingredients and easy-to-follow instructions, this recipe is a must-try for anyone looking to explore the world of vegan soups. So, grab your pot and get ready to indulge in a bowl of pure comfort and goodness!
