A Taste of the Tropics: Gluten-Free Pineapple Teriyaki Chicken


If you’re craving a delicious and exotic dish that will transport you to the sunny beaches of the tropics, look no further. Our Gluten-Free Pineapple Teriyaki Chicken recipe is the perfect blend of sweet and savory flavors, guaranteed to make your taste buds dance with joy. And the best part? It’s incredibly easy to make!

Ingredients

  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1 cup gluten-free soy sauce
  • 1 cup pineapple juice
  • 1/2 cup brown sugar
  • 1/4 cup rice vinegar
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon fresh ginger, grated
  • 1 cup pineapple chunks
  • 2 tablespoons cornstarch
  • 2 tablespoons water
  • Optional: chopped green onions and sesame seeds for garnish

Instructions

  1. In a mixing bowl, combine the gluten-free soy sauce, pineapple juice, brown sugar, rice vinegar, minced garlic, and grated ginger. Stir well to ensure the sugar is dissolved and all the ingredients are well combined. This will be the marinade for our chicken.

  2. Place the chicken breasts in a large resealable plastic bag and pour the marinade over them. Seal the bag and refrigerate for at least 2 hours, or overnight for maximum flavor.

  3. Preheat your grill to medium-high heat. Remove the chicken breasts from the marinade, allowing any excess marinade to drip off. Reserve the marinade for later use.

  4. Grill the chicken breasts for about 6-8 minutes per side, or until they are cooked through and reach an internal temperature of 165°F (75°C). Baste the chicken with the reserved marinade during the last few minutes of grilling for an extra burst of flavor.

  5. While the chicken is grilling, prepare the pineapple teriyaki sauce. In a small saucepan, combine the pineapple chunks with the reserved marinade. Bring the mixture to a simmer over medium heat.

  6. In a separate bowl, whisk together the cornstarch and water until smooth. Slowly pour the cornstarch mixture into the simmering sauce, stirring continuously. Cook for an additional 2-3 minutes, or until the sauce has thickened to your desired consistency.

  7. Remove the chicken from the grill and let it rest for a few minutes. Then, slice it into thin strips.

  8. Serve the grilled chicken over a bed of steamed rice or quinoa, and generously drizzle the pineapple teriyaki sauce over the top. Garnish with chopped green onions and sesame seeds, if desired.

  9. Enjoy your Taste of the Tropics! The combination of tender grilled chicken, sweet pineapple, and flavorful teriyaki sauce is sure to transport you to a tropical paradise with every bite.

Conclusion

Indulge in the flavors of the tropics with this Gluten-Free Pineapple Teriyaki Chicken recipe. Whether you’re hosting a summer barbecue or simply looking to add some excitement to your weeknight dinners, this dish is a winner. The tangy sweetness of the pineapple combined with the savory teriyaki sauce creates a harmony of flavors that will leave you craving for more. Plus, being gluten-free, it’s a perfect option for those with dietary restrictions. So, fire up that grill and get ready to experience a true taste of the tropics in the comfort of your own home.
