Classic Alfredo Sauce Ingredients: 1 cup heavy cream 1/2 cup unsalted butter 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese 2 cloves garlic, minced Salt and pepper to taste Instructions: In a saucepan over medium heat, melt the butter and add the minced garlic. Sauté for a minute until fragrant. Reduce the heat to low and pour in the heavy cream. Stir continuously to prevent the cream from scorching. Slowly add in the grated Parmesan cheese while stirring. Continue stirring until the cheese has melted and the sauce has thickened to your desired consistency. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Remember, Parmesan cheese can be salty, so be cautious with the amount of salt you add. Remove from heat and let the sauce cool for a few minutes before serving. Pour over your favorite pasta and toss until well coated. Garnish with additional Parmesan cheese if desired. This classic alfredo sauce recipe is perfect for those who love the traditional flavors of creamy Parmesan.
