Mouthwatering Keto Garlic Herb Pork Chops


If you’re on a keto diet or simply looking for a delicious low-carb meal, these mouthwatering keto garlic herb pork chops are a must-try. Packed with flavor and easy to make, this recipe is perfect for a satisfying weeknight dinner or a special weekend meal. The combination of garlic, herbs, and perfectly seared pork chops will leave your taste buds tingling and your family asking for seconds.

Ingredients:

  • 4 bone-in pork chops
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • 1 teaspoon dried rosemary
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Prep the Pork Chops:

    • Start by patting the pork chops dry with paper towels. This helps them sear beautifully and creates a delicious crust. Season both sides generously with salt and pepper.

  2. Sear the Pork Chops:

    • In a large skillet, heat the olive oil over medium-high heat. Once the oil is hot, add the pork chops and sear for 3-4 minutes on each side, or until they develop a golden-brown crust. Remove the pork chops from the skillet and set aside.

  3. Prepare the Garlic Herb Sauce:

    • In the same skillet, add the minced garlic and sauté for about 1 minute, or until fragrant. Be careful not to burn the garlic. Add the dried thyme, rosemary, and oregano to the skillet, stirring to combine with the garlic.

  4. Combine and Simmer:

    • Return the pork chops to the skillet, nestling them into the garlic herb mixture. Reduce the heat to medium-low and cover the skillet. Allow the pork chops to simmer in the flavorful sauce for 10-12 minutes, or until they reach an internal temperature of 145°F (63°C).

  5. Serve and Enjoy:

    • Once the pork chops are cooked through, remove them from the skillet and let them rest for a few minutes. This allows the juices to redistribute, ensuring a juicy and tender result. Garnish with fresh parsley and serve the pork chops with your favorite keto-friendly side dishes, such as roasted vegetables or a crisp salad.

Why You’ll Love These Pork Chops:

  • Flavorful and Keto-Friendly: The garlic herb sauce infuses the pork chops with rich, aromatic flavors, making them a standout keto-friendly dish.
  • Quick and Easy: This recipe comes together in less than 30 minutes, making it perfect for busy weeknights when you want a satisfying meal without spending hours in the kitchen.
  • Versatile: You can customize the herbs and spices to suit your taste preferences. Consider adding a pinch of red pepper flakes for a subtle kick or fresh lemon zest for a bright, citrusy twist.

Whether you’re following a keto lifestyle or simply want to enjoy a delicious, low-carb meal, these garlic herb pork chops are sure to become a staple in your recipe collection. With their tender texture and bold flavors, they’re bound to impress even the pickiest of eaters. So, fire up your skillet and get ready to savor every bite of these mouthwatering keto garlic herb pork chops!
