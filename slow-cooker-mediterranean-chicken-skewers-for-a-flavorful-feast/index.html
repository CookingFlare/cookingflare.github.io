Slow Cooker Mediterranean Chicken Skewers for a Flavorful Feast


Are you ready to take your taste buds on a journey to the Mediterranean? Look no further than these delectable slow cooker Mediterranean chicken skewers. Bursting with the flavors of the Mediterranean, this dish is a perfect addition to your recipe repertoire. Whether you’re hosting a gathering with friends or simply craving a taste of the exotic, these chicken skewers are sure to impress.

Ingredients

  • 2 lbs. boneless, skinless chicken breasts, cut into bite-sized chunks
  • 1 red onion, cut into chunks
  • 1 red bell pepper, cut into chunks
  • 1 yellow bell pepper, cut into chunks
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • 1 tsp dried thyme
  • 1/2 tsp dried rosemary
  • 1/2 tsp paprika
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Wooden skewers, soaked in water for 30 minutes

Instructions

  1. In a small bowl, whisk together the olive oil, minced garlic, dried oregano, thyme, rosemary, paprika, salt, and pepper to create a flavorful marinade.

  2. Place the chicken chunks in a large resealable plastic bag or a shallow dish, then pour the marinade over the chicken. Ensure that the chicken is evenly coated. Cover and refrigerate for at least 1 hour to allow the flavors to meld.

  3. After marinating, thread the marinated chicken, red onion, and bell pepper chunks onto the soaked wooden skewers, alternating between the chicken and vegetables for a colorful presentation.

  4. Place the skewers in a single layer in the slow cooker, then pour any remaining marinade over the skewers.

  5. Cover and cook on low for 4-5 hours or on high for 2-3 hours, or until the chicken is cooked through and the vegetables are tender.

  6. Once cooked, carefully remove the skewers from the slow cooker and transfer them to a serving platter.

  7. Serve the Mediterranean chicken skewers with a side of couscous, rice, or a fresh salad for a complete and satisfying meal.

Tips and Variations

  • For a citrusy twist, add the zest and juice of a lemon to the marinade.
  • If you prefer a spicier kick, sprinkle some red pepper flakes into the marinade.
  • Feel free to customize the vegetable selection based on your preferences. Cherry tomatoes, zucchini, and mushrooms also work well on these skewers.

Why Slow Cooker?

The slow cooker is a convenient and hands-off way to prepare these Mediterranean chicken skewers. The low and slow cooking method allows the flavors to meld and intensify, resulting in tender, juicy chicken and perfectly cooked vegetables. Plus, using the slow cooker means you can set it and forget it, allowing you to attend to other tasks while the meal cooks to perfection.

Embracing Mediterranean Flavors

The combination of olive oil, garlic, and fragrant herbs in the marinade pays homage to the classic flavors of the Mediterranean. These ingredients infuse the chicken and vegetables with a rich and aromatic essence that captures the essence of Mediterranean cuisine.

Final Thoughts

These slow cooker Mediterranean chicken skewers are a delightful way to bring the flavors of the Mediterranean into your home. With minimal effort and maximum flavor, this dish is perfect for both weeknight dinners and special occasions. So, gather your ingredients, fire up the slow cooker, and get ready to savor a truly flavorful feast. Buon appetito!
