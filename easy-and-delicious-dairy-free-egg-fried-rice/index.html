Easy and Delicious Dairy-Free Egg Fried Rice


Are you looking for a quick and nutritious meal that’s both dairy-free and packed with flavor? Look no further! In this recipe, we’ll show you how to make an easy and delicious dairy-free egg fried rice that will satisfy your taste buds and keep you feeling satisfied.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups cooked rice (preferably day-old rice)
  • 3 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 carrot, diced
  • 1 bell pepper, diced
  • 1 cup frozen peas
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce (or tamari for gluten-free option)
  • 1 tablespoon sesame oil
  • 2 green onions, sliced
  • 4 eggs, beaten
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Heat 1 tablespoon of vegetable oil in a large skillet or wok over medium heat. Add the beaten eggs and scramble them until they are fully cooked. Remove the scrambled eggs from the skillet and set them aside.

  2. In the same skillet, add the remaining 2 tablespoons of vegetable oil and heat it over medium-high heat. Add the diced onion and minced garlic, and sauté until they become fragrant and translucent.

  3. Add the diced carrot, bell pepper, and frozen peas to the skillet. Stir-fry the vegetables for about 3-4 minutes, or until they are tender-crisp.

  4. Push the vegetables to one side of the skillet and add the cooked rice to the other side. Use a spatula to break up any clumps of rice and stir-fry it for a few minutes, allowing it to heat through.

  5. Pour the soy sauce (or tamari) and sesame oil over the rice and vegetables. Mix everything together until the rice is evenly coated with the sauce.

  6. Add the scrambled eggs back into the skillet and stir-fry for another minute or two, until everything is well combined and heated through.

  7. Season with salt and pepper to taste, and garnish with sliced green onions.

Serving Suggestions

This dairy-free egg fried rice can be enjoyed as a complete meal on its own, or you can serve it as a side dish alongside your favorite protein. It pairs well with grilled chicken, tofu, or shrimp. You can also customize the recipe by adding other vegetables like broccoli, mushrooms, or corn.

Why Dairy-Free?

Dairy-free recipes are great for those who have lactose intolerance, allergies, or simply prefer to avoid dairy products. By using vegetable oil instead of butter and omitting any dairy-based ingredients, you can still enjoy a flavorful and satisfying meal without compromising your dietary needs or preferences.

Benefits of Egg Fried Rice

Egg fried rice is not only delicious but also a versatile and nutritious dish. Here are a few benefits:

  1. Protein: Eggs are an excellent source of protein, which is essential for building and repairing tissues in our bodies. Adding eggs to your fried rice boosts its protein content and makes it a more balanced meal.

  2. Fiber: Vegetables like carrots, bell peppers, and peas are high in fiber, which aids in digestion and helps maintain a healthy digestive system.

  3. Vitamins and Minerals: This dish is packed with vitamins and minerals from the variety of vegetables used. Carrots are rich in vitamin A, bell peppers provide vitamin C, and peas offer a good dose of vitamin K.

  4. Quick and Easy: This recipe is perfect for busy individuals or families as it can be whipped up in no time. It’s a great option for weeknight dinners when you need something delicious and nutritious on the table fast.

So, if you’re looking for a dairy-free meal that’s both easy to make and full of flavor, give this dairy-free egg fried rice recipe a try. It’s a crowd-pleaser and a great way to incorporate more vegetables into your diet. Enjoy!
