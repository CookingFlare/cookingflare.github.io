Pumpkin Spice Energy Balls: A Nutritious Fall Snack


Fall is here, and that means it’s time to indulge in the flavors of the season. One iconic flavor that instantly brings to mind cozy evenings and changing leaves is pumpkin spice. But who says pumpkin spice can only be enjoyed in lattes and pies? In this recipe, we’ll show you how to create Pumpkin Spice Energy Balls – a delicious and nutritious fall snack that will satisfy your cravings and boost your energy levels.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup rolled oats
  • 1/2 cup pumpkin puree
  • 1/4 cup almond butter
  • 1/4 cup honey or maple syrup
  • 1/2 cup shredded coconut
  • 1/4 cup chopped nuts (such as almonds or walnuts)
  • 2 tablespoons chia seeds
  • 1 teaspoon pumpkin spice blend (cinnamon, nutmeg, ginger, and cloves)
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • Pinch of salt

Instructions

  1. In a large mixing bowl, combine the rolled oats, pumpkin puree, almond butter, honey or maple syrup, shredded coconut, chopped nuts, chia seeds, pumpkin spice blend, vanilla extract, and a pinch of salt. Mix well until all the ingredients are evenly incorporated.

  2. Once the mixture is well combined, cover the bowl and refrigerate for at least 30 minutes. This will allow the mixture to firm up, making it easier to shape into balls.

  3. After the mixture has chilled, remove it from the refrigerator. Using your hands, take small portions of the mixture and roll them into bite-sized balls. Place the balls on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper.

  4. Once all the mixture has been rolled into balls, return them to the refrigerator and let them chill for another 30 minutes. This will help the energy balls set and hold their shape.

  5. After the chilling time, the Pumpkin Spice Energy Balls are ready to be enjoyed! You can store them in an airtight container in the refrigerator for up to a week, making them a perfect grab-and-go snack for busy weekdays or a quick boost of energy before a workout.

Nutritional Benefits

These Pumpkin Spice Energy Balls are not only delicious but also packed with nutrients. Let’s take a look at some of the benefits of the key ingredients:

  • Pumpkin Puree: Pumpkin is rich in antioxidants, vitamins A and C, and fiber. It supports healthy digestion, boosts immune function, and promotes glowing skin.

  • Almond Butter: Almonds are a great source of healthy fats, protein, and fiber. They provide sustained energy, support brain health, and help lower cholesterol levels.

  • Rolled Oats: Oats are an excellent source of complex carbohydrates, fiber, and minerals like iron and magnesium. They provide long-lasting energy, aid in digestion, and promote heart health.

  • Chia Seeds: Chia seeds are a nutritional powerhouse, packed with omega-3 fatty acids, fiber, and protein. They support digestion, promote healthy skin, and help stabilize blood sugar levels.

  • Honey or Maple Syrup: These natural sweeteners add a touch of sweetness to the energy balls while providing antioxidants and trace minerals.

Final Thoughts

Pumpkin Spice Energy Balls are a delightful way to enjoy the flavors of fall while nourishing your body with wholesome ingredients. Whether you need a quick snack between meals or an energy boost before a workout, these nutritious bites will hit the spot. So, grab your ingredients, get rolling, and savor the taste of autumn with every bite!
