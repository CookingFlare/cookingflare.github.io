Pasta with Creamy Sun-Dried Tomato and Spinach Sauce: A Burst of Flavor


If you’re looking to add a burst of flavor to your pasta dishes, then this recipe is a must-try! The combination of creamy sun-dried tomatoes and fresh spinach creates a rich and indulgent sauce that will leave your taste buds tingling. Plus, it’s incredibly easy to make, making it perfect for busy weeknights or lazy weekends.

Ingredients

  • 8 ounces of pasta (your choice)
  • 1 cup of sun-dried tomatoes, drained and chopped
  • 2 cups of fresh spinach, roughly chopped
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 cup of heavy cream
  • 1/2 cup of grated Parmesan cheese
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Olive oil for cooking

Instructions

  1. Cook the pasta according to the package instructions until al dente. Drain and set aside.

  2. In a large skillet, heat a drizzle of olive oil over medium heat. Add the minced garlic and sauté until fragrant, about 1 minute.

  3. Add the sun-dried tomatoes to the skillet and cook for another 2 minutes, allowing the flavors to meld together.

  4. Stir in the fresh spinach and cook until wilted, about 3-4 minutes. The vibrant green color of the spinach will add a pop of freshness to the dish.

  5. Reduce the heat to low and pour in the heavy cream. Stir gently to combine with the vegetables.

  6. Gradually sprinkle in the grated Parmesan cheese, stirring continuously until the sauce becomes thick and creamy. This will take about 5 minutes.

  7. Season the sauce with salt and pepper to taste. Remember, the sun-dried tomatoes can be quite salty, so be mindful of the amount of salt you add.

  8. Add the cooked pasta to the skillet and toss everything together until the pasta is evenly coated with the creamy sun-dried tomato and spinach sauce.

  9. Serve the pasta in bowls, garnished with some extra Parmesan cheese if desired. The creamy sauce will cling to the pasta, ensuring every bite is bursting with flavor.

Tips and Variations

  • You can use any type of pasta for this recipe, such as penne, fusilli, or spaghetti. Choose your favorite or experiment with different shapes to keep things interesting.

  • If you prefer a lighter sauce, you can substitute half-and-half or milk for the heavy cream. However, keep in mind that the sauce won’t be as rich and creamy.

  • For an added kick, sprinkle some red pepper flakes or drizzle some hot sauce over the pasta before serving. This will give the dish a spicy twist that complements the creaminess of the sauce.

  • To make this recipe vegetarian-friendly, use vegetable broth instead of chicken broth.

  • Feel free to add other ingredients to the sauce, such as cooked chicken, shrimp, or mushrooms. Just make sure to adjust the cooking time accordingly.

This Pasta with Creamy Sun-Dried Tomato and Spinach Sauce is a simple yet satisfying dish that will impress your family and friends. The combination of tangy sun-dried tomatoes, earthy spinach, and velvety cream creates a flavor explosion that will have everyone asking for seconds. So, grab your apron and get ready to indulge in this burst of flavor!
