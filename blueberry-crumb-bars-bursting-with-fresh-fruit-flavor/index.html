Blueberry Crumb Bars: Bursting with Fresh Fruit Flavor


If you’re a fan of blueberries and crave a dessert that’s bursting with fresh fruit flavor, then you’re in for a treat! Blueberry crumb bars are the perfect balance of sweet, tart, and buttery goodness, making them an absolute delight for your taste buds. Whether you’re hosting a summer gathering, looking for a delightful snack, or simply want to indulge in a delicious homemade treat, these blueberry crumb bars are sure to impress.

Ingredients

For the Crust and Crumb:

  • 1 cup unsalted butter, cold and cubed
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 egg
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • Zest of one lemon

For the Blueberry Filling:

  • 4 cups fresh blueberries
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 4 teaspoons cornstarch
  • Juice of one lemon

Instructions

Step 1: Preparing the Crust and Crumb

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C). Grease a 9x13-inch baking pan and line it with parchment paper, leaving an overhang on the sides for easy removal later.

  2. In a large mixing bowl, combine the granulated sugar, flour, baking powder, salt, and lemon zest. Add the cold, cubed butter and egg, then mix until the dough comes together but is still crumbly.

  3. Take about two-thirds of the dough and press it evenly into the bottom of the prepared baking pan to form the crust. Set the remaining one-third of the dough aside to use for the crumb topping.

Step 2: Making the Blueberry Filling

  1. In a separate bowl, gently toss the fresh blueberries with granulated sugar, cornstarch, and lemon juice until the berries are well coated.

  2. Evenly spread the blueberry mixture over the crust in the baking pan.

Step 3: Assembling and Baking

  1. Crumble the remaining one-third of the dough over the blueberry filling, creating a deliciously crumbly topping.

  2. Place the baking pan in the preheated oven and bake for 45 to 55 minutes, or until the top is golden brown and the blueberry filling is bubbly.

  3. Once baked, remove the blueberry crumb bars from the oven and allow them to cool completely in the pan on a wire rack.

  4. Once cooled, use the parchment paper overhang to lift the bars out of the pan. Place them on a cutting board and carefully slice them into squares.

Serving and Storing

These delightful blueberry crumb bars are best served at room temperature. They can be stored in an airtight container at room temperature for up to 3 days, or in the refrigerator for up to a week.

Final Thoughts

Blueberry crumb bars are a delightful way to enjoy the fresh, vibrant flavors of summer. Whether you serve them at a picnic, bring them to a potluck, or simply enjoy them with a cup of tea, these bars are sure to be a hit. The buttery crust, sweet blueberry filling, and crumbly topping come together to create a truly irresistible treat that celebrates the natural goodness of fresh blueberries.

So, the next time you find yourself with an abundance of blueberries, give this recipe a try and treat yourself to the delightful experience of savoring these blueberry crumb bars. Your taste buds will thank you!
