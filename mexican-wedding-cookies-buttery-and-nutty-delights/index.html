Mexican Wedding Cookies: Buttery and Nutty Delights


If you’re a fan of cookies that melt in your mouth, then Mexican Wedding Cookies are an absolute must-try! These delightful treats are known for their buttery texture and nutty flavor, making them the perfect sweet indulgence for any occasion. In this recipe, we’ll walk you through the steps to create these delicious cookies that are sure to impress your friends and family.

Ingredients

To make these Mexican Wedding Cookies, you’ll need the following ingredients:

  • 1 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1/2 cup powdered sugar
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup finely chopped pecans or walnuts
  • Additional powdered sugar for coating

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 325°F (165°C) and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.

  2. In a large mixing bowl, cream together the softened butter and powdered sugar until light and fluffy. This process helps to incorporate air into the mixture, resulting in a lighter texture for the cookies.

  3. Add the vanilla extract to the butter-sugar mixture and mix well. The vanilla extract adds a subtle but essential flavor to the cookies.

  4. Gradually add the all-purpose flour to the mixture, mixing until just combined. Be careful not to overmix, as this can result in tough cookies. The dough should come together and be slightly crumbly.

  5. Stir in the finely chopped pecans or walnuts. These nuts add a delightful crunch and nuttiness to the cookies. If you prefer a smoother texture, you can use ground nuts instead.

  6. Take about a tablespoon of dough and roll it into a small ball between the palms of your hands. Place the dough balls onto the prepared baking sheet, spacing them about an inch apart. The cookies will spread slightly as they bake, so leave some room for expansion.

  7. Bake the cookies in the preheated oven for approximately 15-18 minutes, or until they are just lightly golden brown on the bottom. Over-baking can result in dry cookies, so keep a close eye on them to ensure they don’t overcook.

  8. Remove the cookies from the oven and let them cool on the baking sheet for a few minutes. While they are still warm, carefully roll each cookie in powdered sugar until fully coated. The powdered sugar coating adds a touch of sweetness and a beautiful snowy appearance to the cookies.

  9. Transfer the cookies to a wire rack to cool completely. Once cooled, you can give them another light dusting of powdered sugar for an extra touch of elegance.

  10. Serve and enjoy! These Mexican Wedding Cookies are perfect for any occasion, from weddings to holiday parties or simply as an everyday treat. They pair wonderfully with a cup of coffee or tea, and their buttery and nutty flavor will surely leave you craving more.

Conclusion

Mexican Wedding Cookies are a classic dessert that never fails to impress. With their delicate texture, rich buttery taste, and satisfying nuttiness, they are a true delight to the senses. Whether you’re making them for a special celebration or just to satisfy your sweet tooth, these cookies are sure to be a hit. So grab your apron, gather your ingredients, and get ready to bake a batch of these irresistible treats. Once you take your first bite, you’ll understand why they’re called “delights”!
