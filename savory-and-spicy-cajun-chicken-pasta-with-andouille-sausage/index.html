Savory and Spicy Cajun Chicken Pasta with Andouille Sausage


If you’re a fan of bold flavors and comforting pasta dishes, then this Savory and Spicy Cajun Chicken Pasta with Andouille Sausage is sure to become a new favorite in your recipe collection. This dish combines tender chicken, smoky andouille sausage, and a creamy Cajun-infused sauce to create a meal that’s bursting with flavor. Whether you’re cooking for a crowd or just looking to treat yourself to a satisfying dinner, this recipe is sure to impress.

Ingredients

  • 12 oz. penne pasta
  • 1 lb. boneless, skinless chicken breasts, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 12 oz. andouille sausage, sliced
  • 1 tablespoon Cajun seasoning
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 1 red bell pepper, diced
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup chicken broth
  • 1 1/2 cups heavy cream
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried thyme
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley for garnish

Instructions

  1. Cook the Pasta: Begin by cooking the penne pasta according to the package instructions. Once cooked, drain the pasta and set it aside.

  2. Season and Sear the Chicken and Sausage: In a large skillet, heat the olive oil over medium-high heat. Season the chicken pieces with Cajun seasoning and add them to the skillet. Cook until the chicken is no longer pink, then add the sliced andouille sausage and cook until both the chicken and sausage are nicely browned. Remove the chicken and sausage from the skillet and set aside.

  3. Saute the Vegetables: In the same skillet, add the chopped onion and diced bell pepper. Cook until the vegetables are softened, then add the minced garlic and cook for an additional minute.

  4. Prepare the Sauce: Pour the chicken broth into the skillet, scraping up any browned bits from the bottom of the pan. Add the heavy cream, paprika, dried thyme, and salt and pepper to taste. Allow the sauce to simmer for a few minutes, allowing it to thicken slightly.

  5. Combine and Serve: Add the cooked chicken, sausage, and drained pasta to the skillet with the sauce. Toss everything together until the pasta is coated in the creamy Cajun sauce and the chicken and sausage are evenly distributed. Serve the Cajun chicken pasta hot, garnished with fresh parsley for a pop of color and freshness.

Tips for Success

  • For an extra kick of heat, consider adding a pinch of cayenne pepper to the Cajun seasoning or sauce.
  • If you prefer a lighter version of this dish, you can substitute half-and-half for the heavy cream.
  • Feel free to customize the vegetables in this recipe to suit your preferences. Sliced mushrooms or diced tomatoes would be excellent additions.

Enjoy the Bold Flavors of Cajun Cuisine

This Savory and Spicy Cajun Chicken Pasta with Andouille Sausage is a celebration of the vibrant and robust flavors of Cajun cuisine. The combination of tender chicken, smoky andouille sausage, and a creamy, spicy sauce makes for a truly memorable meal that’s perfect for any occasion. Whether you’re hosting a dinner party or simply craving a cozy night in, this recipe is sure to satisfy your cravings for something hearty and delicious. So, roll up your sleeves, gather your ingredients, and get ready to indulge in a dish that’s as exciting to make as it is to eat.
