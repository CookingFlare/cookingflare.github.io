Cooking with Jackfruit: Vegan Indian Recipes


Jackfruit, the largest tree-borne fruit in the world, has gained popularity in recent years as a versatile and delicious ingredient in vegan cooking. Originating from India, this tropical fruit has a meaty texture that makes it a perfect substitute for meat in many dishes. In this blog post, we will explore some mouthwatering vegan Indian recipes that feature jackfruit as the star ingredient.

1. Jackfruit Biryani

Biryani, a fragrant rice dish layered with flavorful spices, is a staple in Indian cuisine. By replacing meat with jackfruit, you can create a vegan version that is just as satisfying. Here’s how to make it:

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups basmati rice
  • 1 medium-sized jackfruit, ripe and unripe parts separated
  • 1 large onion, thinly sliced
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 4 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1-inch piece of ginger, grated
  • 2 green chilies, slit
  • 1/2 cup coconut milk
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 teaspoon garam masala
  • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 1 cinnamon stick
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh cilantro for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Cook the basmati rice according to package instructions and set aside.
  2. Heat oil in a large pan and add cumin seeds and cinnamon stick. Allow them to splutter.
  3. Add sliced onions and sauté until golden brown.
  4. Add minced garlic, grated ginger, and green chilies. Sauté for a minute.
  5. Add chopped tomatoes and cook until they soften.
  6. Add the ripe part of the jackfruit and cook for 5 minutes.
  7. Add turmeric powder, garam masala, and salt. Mix well.
  8. Add the unripe part of the jackfruit and coconut milk. Cover and cook until the jackfruit is tender.
  9. In a separate pan, layer half of the cooked rice followed by the jackfruit mixture. Repeat the layers.
  10. Cover the pan and cook on low heat for 10 minutes to allow the flavors to meld together.
  11. Garnish with fresh cilantro and serve hot.

2. Jackfruit Curry

Curries are a staple in Indian cuisine, and this jackfruit curry will surely impress your taste buds. Here’s how to make it:

Ingredients:

  • 1 medium-sized jackfruit, chopped into bite-sized pieces
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, pureed
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 1 teaspoon coriander powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon red chili powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon garam masala
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh cilantro for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Heat oil in a pan and add cumin seeds. Allow them to splutter.
  2. Add chopped onions and sauté until they turn translucent.
  3. Add pureed tomatoes and cook until the oil separates.
  4. Add coriander powder, turmeric powder, red chili powder, and salt. Mix well.
  5. Add the chopped jackfruit and stir to coat it with the spice mixture.
  6. Cover and cook on low heat for 15-20 minutes or until the jackfruit is tender.
  7. Sprinkle garam masala and garnish with fresh cilantro before serving.

3. Jackfruit Pulao

Pulao, a one-pot rice dish, is a quick and easy meal that can be enjoyed any day of the week. By incorporating jackfruit, you can elevate the flavors and make it a satisfying vegan option. Here’s how to make it:

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups basmati rice
  • 1 medium-sized jackfruit, ripe and unripe parts separated
  • 1 onion, thinly sliced
  • 2 green chilies, slit
  • 1-inch piece of ginger, grated
  • 4 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 teaspoon garam masala
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh mint leaves for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Cook the basmati rice according to package instructions and set aside.
  2. Heat oil in a pan and add cumin seeds. Allow them to splutter.
  3. Add sliced onions and sauté until golden brown.
  4. Add minced garlic, grated ginger, and green chilies. Sauté for a minute.
  5. Add the ripe part of the jackfruit and cook for 5 minutes.
  6. Add turmeric powder, garam masala, and salt. Mix well.
  7. Add the unripe part of the jackfruit and cook for another 5 minutes.
  8. Add the cooked basmati rice to the pan and gently mix until well combined.
  9. Cover and cook on low heat for 5 minutes to allow the flavors to meld together.
  10. Garnish with fresh mint leaves and serve hot.

Conclusion

Cooking with jackfruit opens up a world of possibilities for vegan Indian recipes. Whether you’re craving biryani, curry, or pulao, jackfruit can be the star ingredient that adds texture and flavor to your dishes. Give these recipes a try and discover the deliciousness of jackfruit in your vegan cooking. Enjoy!
