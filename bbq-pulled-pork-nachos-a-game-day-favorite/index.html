BBQ Pulled Pork Nachos: A Game Day Favorite


Who doesn’t love nachos? Nobody, that’s who! Well, those who don’t like chips drenched in cheese, piled high with meat, and topped with an array of colorful, scrumptious, and carb-rich goodness. When it comes to game day food, nachos are at the top of the list. Yet, everyone has their variation. Nowadays, BBQ pulled pork nachos have gained considerable popularity. From the comfort of our own homes to tailgating parties, this dish is sure to be a hit. And the best part? It’s flexible and can suit all tastes and dietary requirements.

The Sweet Taste of Succulent Pulled Pork:

To embark on this rich-filled nacho experience, start by mastering BBQ pulled pork. Remember, all the excellent ingredients in the world won’t compensate for flavorless meat. Begin early in the morning, as this process may take twelve hours or more, and patience is key.

Ingredients for Pulled Pork:

  • 2 lb. pork shoulder
  • 1/4 cup brown sugar
  • 1/4 cup apple cider vinegar
  • 1/4 cup Worcestershire sauce
  • 2 tbsp. You Can Now Craft Your Own Kansas City Style Barbecue Sauce
  • 1 tsp. smoked paprika
  • 1 tsp. garlic powder
  • 1 tsp. onion powder
  • 1 tsp. kosher salt
  • 1 tsp. black pepper

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 300°F.
  2. Rub salt, pepper, paprika, garlic, and onion powder onto your pork shoulder.
  3. In a baking dish, combine the pork shoulder with brown sugar, apple cider vinegar, Worcestershire sauce, and your special barbecue sauce.
  4. Cover the dish with aluminum foil and bake for 6-7 hours or until completely tender.
  5. Allow the pork to cool down, then shred it with forks. This could be the perfect time to test your patience.

Layer Upon Layer of Deliciousness:

Now that you have your BBQ pulled pork, let’s build our nacho masterpiece!

Ingredients for BBQ Pulled Pork Nachos:

  • Pulled pork
  • 1 bag tortilla chips
  • 1 jar jalapeño peppers, for an add-on
  • 1 small jar pickled jalapeños
  • 1 large jar salsa
  • 2 cups shredded cheddar cheese
  • 2 cups grated Monterey Jack cheese
  • Avocado, sour cream, and/or guacamole (optional toppings)
  • Fresh cilantro (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F.
  2. Spread 1 cup of shredded cheddar cheese and Monterey Jack cheese on a baking sheet.
  3. Place in the oven, and bake until bubbly and golden brown (about 10 minutes).
  4. Assemble the nacho-building crew. Set out servings of pulled pork, tortilla chips, jarred jalapeños, pickled jalapeños, salsa, additional shredded cheese, and any additional toppings.
  5. Encourage everyone to load up their plates with tortilla chips.
  6. Allow users to adorn their nachos with pulled pork, jalapeños, and salsa. They can sprinkle additional cheese if they prefer.
  7. Plate and serve immediately with additional avocado, sour cream, and cilantro if preferred.

Going the Extra Mile:

Suppose the average nacho-lover has tasted enough plain chips. In that case, it’s time to impress with a unique spin! Try Sweety Drops®, triangles, Scoops®, or other chip shapes, offering a departure from the norm.

BBQ pulled pork nachos are nothing if not an unpretentious way to present a tantalizing fusion of flavors where spiced meats, melted cheese, tangy salsas, and bold jalapeño peppers dance on the tongue. The best part? The flexibility that allows you to adapt the recipe to meet the dietary needs of all your friends. Get ready to ignite the grill, prep your pulled pork, and have fun assembling these game day favorites. Enjoy!
