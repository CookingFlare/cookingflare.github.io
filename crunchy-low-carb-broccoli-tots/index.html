Crunchy Low Carb Broccoli Tots


Are you looking for a delicious and healthy snack that’s packed with flavor? Look no further than these Crunchy Low Carb Broccoli Tots! These bite-sized delights are not only incredibly tasty, but they’re also low in carbs, making them the perfect guilt-free treat. Whether you’re following a low-carb diet or simply looking to incorporate more veggies into your meals, these broccoli tots are sure to satisfy your cravings.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups broccoli florets, finely chopped
  • 1/2 cup almond flour
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/4 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/2 teaspoon onion powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C) and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.

  2. In a large bowl, combine the finely chopped broccoli florets, almond flour, Parmesan cheese, shredded cheddar cheese, eggs, minced garlic, onion powder, salt, and black pepper. Mix well until all the ingredients are thoroughly combined.

  3. Using your hands or a spoon, scoop out about a tablespoon of the mixture and shape it into a small tot. Place the tot on the prepared baking sheet. Repeat this process until all the mixture is used, leaving a small gap between each tot to allow for even cooking.

  4. Bake the tots in the preheated oven for 15-20 minutes, or until they are golden brown and crispy on the outside.

  5. Once the tots are cooked, remove them from the oven and allow them to cool slightly before serving. Serve them as a snack or as a side dish with your favorite dipping sauce.

Tips and Variations

  • For an extra kick of flavor, try adding some diced jalapenos or red pepper flakes to the mixture before shaping the tots.
  • If you don’t have almond flour on hand, you can substitute it with coconut flour or breadcrumbs.
  • Experiment with different types of cheese to customize the flavor of your tots. Mozzarella, Gouda, or feta cheese can all work well in this recipe.
  • If you prefer a softer texture, you can steam the broccoli florets before chopping them. This will make the tots more tender and less crunchy.

Health Benefits of Broccoli

Broccoli is not only delicious, but it also boasts an impressive array of health benefits. Packed with essential vitamins and minerals, it’s a nutrient-rich vegetable that can support your overall well-being. Some of the key benefits of incorporating broccoli into your diet include:

  • High in fiber: Broccoli is an excellent source of dietary fiber, which aids in digestion and can help you feel fuller for longer.
  • Rich in antioxidants: Broccoli contains powerful antioxidants, such as vitamin C and beta-carotene, which can help protect your cells from damage caused by harmful free radicals.
  • Supports heart health: The fiber and antioxidants in broccoli have been linked to a reduced risk of heart disease by lowering cholesterol levels and improving blood pressure.
  • Boosts immune function: The vitamin C in broccoli can strengthen your immune system and help ward off common illnesses like colds and flu.
  • Promotes bone health: Broccoli is a good source of calcium, which is essential for strong bones and teeth. It also contains vitamin K, which plays a role in bone health and blood clotting.

Conclusion

These Crunchy Low Carb Broccoli Tots are a delicious and healthy snack option that will satisfy your cravings without derailing your diet. Packed with flavor and loaded with nutrients, they’re a win-win for both taste and health. Give them a try and let us know how you like them!
