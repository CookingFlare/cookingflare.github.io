Vegan Quiche: A Savory and Satisfying Brunch Option


Are you looking for a delicious and satisfying vegan brunch option? Look no further than our Vegan Quiche recipe! This savory dish is packed with flavor and perfect for those following a plant-based lifestyle. With a flaky crust and a creamy filling, this Vegan Quiche is sure to become a favorite in your household.

Ingredients

For the crust:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup vegan butter, chilled and cubed
  • 4-6 tablespoons ice-cold water

For the filling:

  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 small onion, diced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup diced vegetables (such as bell peppers, mushrooms, and spinach)
  • 1 cup firm tofu, drained and crumbled
  • 1/2 cup nutritional yeast
  • 1/4 cup unsweetened plant-based milk
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black salt (optional, for an eggy flavor)
  • Freshly ground black pepper, to taste

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).

  2. In a large mixing bowl, combine the flour and salt for the crust. Add the chilled vegan butter and use your fingers or a pastry cutter to cut it into the flour until the mixture resembles coarse crumbs.

  3. Gradually add the ice-cold water, one tablespoon at a time, until the dough comes together. Be careful not to overwork the dough. Shape it into a ball, cover with plastic wrap, and refrigerate for 30 minutes.

  4. While the dough chills, heat the olive oil in a skillet over medium heat. Add the diced onion and minced garlic, and sauté until translucent and fragrant, about 5 minutes. Add the diced vegetables and sauté until they are tender, about 7-8 minutes. Remove from heat and set aside.

  5. In a blender or food processor, combine the crumbled tofu, nutritional yeast, plant-based milk, turmeric, salt, black salt (if using), and black pepper. Blend until smooth and creamy.

  6. Remove the dough from the refrigerator and roll it out on a lightly floured surface to fit a 9-inch pie dish. Carefully transfer the rolled-out dough to the pie dish and press it into the bottom and sides. Trim any excess dough.

  7. Spread the sautéed vegetables evenly over the crust. Pour the tofu mixture over the vegetables, making sure it covers them completely.

  8. Bake the Vegan Quiche in the preheated oven for 30-35 minutes, or until the crust is golden brown and the filling is set. Allow it to cool for a few minutes before slicing and serving.

Serving Suggestions

This Vegan Quiche is versatile and can be enjoyed on its own or paired with a side salad for a complete meal. You can also customize the recipe by adding your favorite herbs and spices to the filling. The possibilities are endless!

Conclusion

With its flaky crust and creamy filling, our Vegan Quiche is a delicious and satisfying brunch option. Packed with veggies and plant-based protein, it’s a dish that will please both vegans and non-vegans alike. So why not give it a try and impress your friends and family with this savory and flavorful creation? Enjoy!
