Comforting One Pot Chicken Tortilla Soup


There’s nothing quite like a steaming bowl of chicken tortilla soup to warm you up on a chilly day. This comforting one-pot meal is a delicious blend of tender chicken, flavorful spices, hearty beans, and vibrant vegetables, all simmered together to create a rich and satisfying dish. Whether you’re hosting a gathering with friends and family or simply craving a cozy night in, this recipe is sure to become a staple in your kitchen.

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 red bell pepper, diced
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon smoked paprika
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 can (14 oz) fire-roasted diced tomatoes
  • 1 can (14 oz) black beans, drained and rinsed
  • 1 can (14 oz) corn kernels, drained
  • 6 cups chicken broth
  • 1 lb boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Juice of 1 lime
  • Fresh cilantro, chopped
  • Tortilla chips, for serving
  • Avocado, sliced (optional)
  • Shredded cheese (optional)
  • Sour cream (optional)

Instructions

  1. In a large pot or Dutch oven, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the diced onion and cook until softened, about 5 minutes. Stir in the minced garlic and diced bell pepper, and cook for an additional 2-3 minutes.

  2. Sprinkle the ground cumin, chili powder, smoked paprika, and dried oregano over the onion mixture. Stir well to coat the vegetables in the spices.

  3. Pour in the fire-roasted diced tomatoes, black beans, and corn kernels. Stir to combine everything in the pot.

  4. Add the chicken broth to the pot and increase the heat to bring the soup to a gentle boil.

  5. Once the soup is boiling, carefully add the chicken breasts to the pot. Reduce the heat to low, cover, and let the soup simmer for 20-25 minutes, or until the chicken is fully cooked through.

  6. Use a pair of tongs to transfer the cooked chicken to a cutting board. Shred the chicken using two forks, then return the shredded chicken to the pot.

  7. Season the soup with salt, pepper, and the juice of 1 lime. Taste and adjust the seasoning as needed.

  8. To serve, ladle the chicken tortilla soup into bowls. Top each serving with a handful of crushed tortilla chips, a sprinkle of fresh cilantro, and any optional toppings you desire, such as sliced avocado, shredded cheese, or a dollop of sour cream.

Tips and Variations

  • For a spicier kick, add a diced jalapeño to the soup when sautéing the onion and bell pepper.
  • Make this soup vegetarian by using vegetable broth and adding extra beans or tofu in place of the chicken.
  • If you prefer a thicker soup, blend a portion of the soup in a blender or with an immersion blender before adding the shredded chicken back in.

This heartwarming chicken tortilla soup is a versatile dish that can be customized to suit your taste preferences. Its soul-soothing aroma and vibrant flavors make it an ideal choice for a cozy night in or a casual gathering with loved ones. Serve it with a side of warm cornbread or a crisp green salad for a satisfying meal that will warm both body and soul.

Enjoy the comforting flavors of this one-pot chicken tortilla soup and savor each spoonful of this delightful creation.
