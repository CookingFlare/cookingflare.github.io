Thai Green Curry with Beef: A Fragrant and Spicy Delight


If you’re craving a dish that’s both fragrant and spicy, look no further than Thai Green Curry with Beef. This delightful Thai dish is a perfect balance of flavors, combining the aromatic blend of green curry paste with the richness of beef and the creaminess of coconut milk. Whether you’re a seasoned Thai cuisine enthusiast or new to the world of exotic flavors, this recipe is sure to tantalize your taste buds and transport you to the bustling streets of Thailand.

Ingredients:

  • 500g beef, thinly sliced
  • 2 tbsp green curry paste
  • 400ml coconut milk
  • 1 cup bamboo shoots, sliced
  • 1 red chili, sliced
  • 1 green chili, sliced
  • 1 tbsp fish sauce
  • 1 tsp palm sugar
  • 1 cup Thai basil leaves
  • 2 kaffir lime leaves
  • 1 red bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 tbsp vegetable oil
  • Salt to taste

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the Beef: In a large pan, heat the vegetable oil over medium heat. Add the green curry paste and cook for a minute until fragrant.

  2. Cook the Beef: Add the thinly sliced beef to the pan and stir-fry until it’s browned and cooked through.

  3. Add Coconut Milk: Pour in the coconut milk and stir well, ensuring the curry paste blends into the milk.

  4. Add Aromatics: Add the bamboo shoots, red and green chilies, kaffir lime leaves, and red bell pepper to the pan. Stir to combine all the ingredients.

  5. Season the Curry: Season the curry with fish sauce, palm sugar, and salt to taste. Adjust the seasoning according to your preference for saltiness and sweetness.

  6. Simmer and Serve: Let the curry simmer for 10-15 minutes, allowing the flavors to meld together. Once the beef is tender and the vegetables are cooked to your liking, remove the curry from the heat.

  7. Garnish and Enjoy: Garnish the curry with Thai basil leaves before serving. The aromatic fragrance of the basil will add a fresh note to the dish. Serve the Thai Green Curry with steamed jasmine rice for a complete and satisfying meal.

Tips and Tricks:

  • Choosing the Right Beef: For this recipe, opt for tender cuts of beef such as sirloin or tenderloin. Slicing the beef thinly ensures it cooks quickly and remains tender.

  • Green Curry Paste: If you’re feeling adventurous, consider making your own green curry paste for a truly authentic experience. However, store-bought green curry paste works perfectly well and saves time.

  • Customize the Heat: Adjust the spiciness of the curry by adding or reducing the amount of green chilies. Remove the seeds for a milder flavor or leave them in for an extra kick.

  • Vegetarian Option: If you prefer a vegetarian version, substitute the beef with tofu or your favorite assortment of vegetables such as eggplant, zucchini, and snap peas.

Final Thoughts:

Thai Green Curry with Beef is a delightful dish that captures the essence of Thai cuisine with its harmonious blend of flavors. The fragrance of the curry paste, the richness of coconut milk, and the tender beef create a truly memorable dining experience. Whether you’re cooking for a special occasion or simply craving a culinary adventure, this recipe is a must-try for anyone who appreciates the bold and vibrant flavors of Thai cuisine. So, roll up your sleeves, gather your ingredients, and embark on a journey to create this fragrant and spicy delight in the comfort of your own kitchen.
