Savory Biscuits and Gravy: A Comforting Breakfast Choice


If there’s one dish that embodies the essence of comfort food, it’s savory biscuits and gravy. This classic Southern breakfast staple is a hearty and satisfying way to start the day. The creamy, peppery gravy served over warm, flaky biscuits is a combination that never fails to delight the taste buds. Whether you’re looking for a weekend brunch treat or a cozy breakfast on a chilly morning, biscuits and gravy are sure to hit the spot.

The Perfect Biscuits

To start off this delectable dish, let’s focus on the biscuits. The key to achieving the perfect biscuits lies in the technique. You want them to be light, fluffy, and buttery. To achieve this, use cold butter and buttermilk, and be mindful not to overwork the dough. A hot oven is essential for creating that golden-brown exterior while maintaining a soft interior.

Ingredients for Biscuits

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tablespoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup cold unsalted butter, cubed
  • 3/4 cup cold buttermilk

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 450°F (230°C).
  2. In a large bowl, whisk together the flour, baking powder, baking soda, and salt.
  3. Add the cold cubed butter to the flour mixture and use a pastry cutter or your fingers to work the butter into the flour until it resembles coarse crumbs.
  4. Make a well in the center of the mixture and pour in the cold buttermilk. Gently stir until the dough just comes together.
  5. Turn the dough out onto a lightly floured surface and gently pat it into a 1-inch thick round. Avoid overworking the dough.
  6. Use a biscuit cutter to cut out the biscuits and place them on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper.
  7. Bake for 12-15 minutes or until the biscuits are golden brown.

Creamy Sausage Gravy

Now, let’s move on to the star of the show - the creamy sausage gravy. This rich and flavorful gravy is the perfect complement to the fluffy biscuits. The combination of savory sausage, aromatic herbs, and creamy gravy makes for a truly indulgent breakfast experience.

Ingredients for Gravy

  • 1/2 pound breakfast sausage
  • 1/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • 3 cups whole milk
  • Salt and black pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. In a skillet over medium heat, cook the breakfast sausage, breaking it into small pieces as it browns.
  2. Once the sausage is fully cooked, sprinkle the flour over it and stir to combine. Cook for a few minutes to get rid of the raw flour taste.
  3. Gradually pour in the milk, stirring constantly to prevent lumps from forming.
  4. Continue cooking and stirring until the gravy thickens to your desired consistency.
  5. Season the gravy with salt and plenty of black pepper.

Bringing It All Together

Once you have your biscuits fresh out of the oven and your gravy piping hot, it’s time to assemble this comforting breakfast dish. Split the warm biscuits in half and ladle the creamy sausage gravy over the top. Garnish with a sprinkle of freshly ground black pepper and chopped parsley for a touch of freshness.

The Final Word

Savory biscuits and gravy are a timeless breakfast classic that never fails to bring a sense of warmth and satisfaction. Whether you’re sharing it with loved ones or savoring it in quiet solitude, this dish has a way of making any morning feel just a little bit brighter. So, the next time you’re in the mood for a comforting breakfast choice, consider treating yourself to the simple pleasures of homemade biscuits and gravy.
