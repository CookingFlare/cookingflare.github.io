Traditional Mexican Tostadas with Refried Beans


If you’re craving a taste of authentic Mexican cuisine, look no further than traditional tostadas with refried beans. This classic dish is a staple in Mexican households and is loved for its crispy texture, flavorful beans, and vibrant toppings. Whether you’re cooking for a family dinner or hosting a casual get-together, tostadas with refried beans are sure to be a hit. In this recipe, we’ll walk you through the process of creating this delicious and satisfying dish from scratch.

Ingredients

For the Refried Beans:

  • 2 cups of pinto beans
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon of cumin
  • 1 teaspoon of chili powder
  • Salt to taste
  • 4 cups of water
  • 2 tablespoons of vegetable oil

For the Tostadas:

  • Corn tortillas
  • Vegetable oil for frying

Toppings:

  • Shredded lettuce
  • Diced tomatoes
  • Sliced avocado
  • Crumbled queso fresco
  • Salsa or hot sauce
  • Sour cream

Instructions

Step 1: Prepare the Refried Beans

  1. Rinse the pinto beans under cold water and remove any debris.
  2. In a large pot, combine the beans, chopped onion, minced garlic, cumin, chili powder, salt, and water.
  3. Bring the mixture to a boil, then reduce the heat and let it simmer for about 2 hours, or until the beans are very soft.
  4. Once the beans are cooked, remove them from the heat and let them cool slightly.
  5. In a separate pan, heat the vegetable oil over medium heat.
  6. Add the cooked beans to the pan and mash them using a potato masher or the back of a spoon.
  7. Continue to cook the beans, stirring frequently, until they reach a thick and creamy consistency.
  8. Adjust the seasoning to taste and set the refried beans aside.

Step 2: Prepare the Tostadas

  1. In a large skillet, heat about 1 inch of vegetable oil over medium-high heat.
  2. Once the oil is hot, carefully add a corn tortilla to the pan using tongs.
  3. Fry the tortilla for about 2-3 minutes on each side, until it is golden and crispy.
  4. Remove the fried tortilla from the oil and place it on a paper towel-lined plate to drain any excess oil.
  5. Repeat this process with the remaining tortillas.

Step 3: Assemble the Tostadas

  1. To assemble the tostadas, spread a generous layer of the prepared refried beans onto each crispy tortilla.
  2. Top the beans with shredded lettuce, diced tomatoes, sliced avocado, crumbled queso fresco, a drizzle of salsa or hot sauce, and a dollop of sour cream.

Step 4: Serve and Enjoy

Arrange the assembled tostadas on a platter and serve them immediately. Encourage your guests to customize their tostadas with their favorite toppings and enjoy the delightful combination of flavors and textures.

Conclusion

Tostadas with refried beans are a celebration of Mexican flavors and culinary traditions. The crispy texture of the tostada, the creamy richness of the refried beans, and the freshness of the toppings come together to create a truly satisfying dish. Whether you’re a fan of Mexican cuisine or simply looking to explore new flavors, this recipe is a must-try. So roll up your sleeves, gather your ingredients, and embark on a flavorful journey to Mexico with these traditional tostadas with refried beans. Buena provecho!
