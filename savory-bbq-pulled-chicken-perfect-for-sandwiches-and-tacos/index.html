Savory BBQ Pulled Chicken: Perfect for Sandwiches and Tacos


If you’re a fan of finger-licking good barbecue flavors and enjoy the versatility of pulled chicken, then this recipe is for you. Savory BBQ Pulled Chicken is the perfect dish for sandwiches and tacos. With its tender and juicy meat, smoky barbecue sauce, and a hint of spice, it’s a crowd-pleaser that will leave everyone wanting more.

Ingredients

  • 2 pounds boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1 cup barbecue sauce
  • 1/2 cup chicken broth
  • 1/4 cup brown sugar
  • 2 tablespoons apple cider vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 teaspoon smoked paprika
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon onion powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon chili powder
  • Salt and pepper, to taste

Instructions

  1. In a slow cooker or Instant Pot, combine the barbecue sauce, chicken broth, brown sugar, apple cider vinegar, Worcestershire sauce, smoked paprika, garlic powder, onion powder, chili powder, salt, and pepper. Mix well until the ingredients are fully combined.

  2. Add the chicken breasts to the slow cooker or Instant Pot, making sure they are fully submerged in the sauce mixture. If using a slow cooker, cook on low heat for 6-8 hours or high heat for 3-4 hours. If using an Instant Pot, select the “Pressure Cook” or “Manual” setting and cook for 15 minutes, then allow for natural pressure release for 10 minutes before manually releasing any remaining pressure.

  3. Once the chicken is cooked, remove it from the slow cooker or Instant Pot and transfer it to a cutting board. Use two forks to shred the chicken into small, bite-sized pieces.

  4. While shredding the chicken, preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C). Line a baking sheet with parchment paper or aluminum foil.

  5. Place the shredded chicken on the prepared baking sheet, spreading it out evenly. Drizzle some of the reserved barbecue sauce from the slow cooker or Instant Pot over the shredded chicken, ensuring that it is well coated.

  6. Bake the shredded chicken in the preheated oven for 10-15 minutes, or until the edges start to caramelize and become slightly crispy. This step helps to enhance the flavors and textures of the pulled chicken.

  7. Once the shredded chicken is done baking, remove it from the oven and let it cool slightly before using it in your favorite sandwiches or tacos.

Serving Suggestions

  • For a classic pulled chicken sandwich, pile the savory BBQ pulled chicken onto a soft hamburger bun and top it with coleslaw for a refreshing crunch.
  • If you prefer tacos, warm up some corn tortillas and fill them with the savory BBQ pulled chicken. Top it off with your favorite taco toppings like diced tomatoes, shredded lettuce, sliced avocado, and a squeeze of lime juice.
  • You can also use the savory BBQ pulled chicken as a topping for loaded nachos or as a filling for quesadillas or enchiladas.

Conclusion

Savory BBQ Pulled Chicken is a versatile and delicious dish that is perfect for sandwiches and tacos. The combination of tender, juicy chicken, smoky barbecue sauce, and a hint of spice creates a flavor explosion that will leave your taste buds wanting more. Whether you’re hosting a backyard barbecue or looking for a quick and easy weeknight meal, this recipe is sure to satisfy. So fire up the slow cooker or Instant Pot and get ready to enjoy some finger-licking good BBQ pulled chicken.
