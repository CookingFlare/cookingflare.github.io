10 Sweet Brunch Cocktails to Elevate Your Weekend


Brunch has become a beloved ritual, synonymous with indulgence and leisure. It’s the perfect time to gather with friends, enjoy delicious food, and, of course, sip on creative cocktails. Sweet brunch cocktails add extra flair to your mid-morning meal, balancing fresh fruit flavors, decadent sweeteners, and just the right splash of your favorite spirits. Whether you’re hosting a cozy at-home gathering or visiting your favorite brunch spot, these ten cocktail recipes will make your weekend unforgettable.

1. Peach Bellini

A classic among brunch cocktails, the Peach Bellini is both elegant and refreshing. Its sweetness comes from ripe peaches blended into puree and topped with sparkling prosecco.

Ingredients:

  • 1 ripe peach
  • 1 tbsp sugar (optional)
  • 1 bottle prosecco

Instructions:

  1. Peel and pit the peach, then puree in a blender. Add sugar for extra sweetness if desired.
  2. Add one to two tablespoons of peach puree to a champagne flute.
  3. Top with chilled prosecco, pouring slowly to avoid overflow.

Peach Bellinis are perfect for brightening up a sunny brunch with light fruit flavor and bubbly fun.

2. Raspberry Mimosa

Upgrade the traditional orange mimosa with a tangy-sweet raspberry twist. This cocktail adds natural sweetness while keeping the simplicity of preparation intact.

Ingredients:

  • 2 oz raspberry juice or puree
  • 4 oz orange juice
  • Champagne or sparkling wine

Instructions:

  1. Combine raspberry juice and orange juice in a mixing glass. Stir gently.
  2. Pour the mixture into a chilled champagne flute.
  3. Slowly top with champagne or sparkling wine for fizzy perfection.

The Raspberry Mimosa is a crowd-pleaser, offering the ideal balance of citrus and berry notes to pair with your favorite brunch staples.

3. Honey Lavender Fizz

Delightfully floral with just a hint of sweetness, the Honey Lavender Fizz is a sophisticated cocktail that turns any brunch into an elegant affair.

Ingredients:

  • 1 tbsp honey
  • 2 oz vodka
  • 1 oz lavender simple syrup
  • Soda water
  • Ice

Instructions:

  1. Mix honey, vodka, and lavender simple syrup in a cocktail shaker. Add ice and shake well.
  2. Strain the mixture into a highball glass filled with fresh ice.
  3. Top with soda water and garnish with a sprig of lavender or lemon wheel.

This aromatic cocktail pairs beautifully with light brunch dishes like fruit salads and pastries.

4. Coconut Cream Sangria

If you’re feeling tropical, the Coconut Cream Sangria is your brunch cocktail match! Sweet coconut cream blends seamlessly with fruity white wine, delivering a lush and creamy treat.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup coconut cream
  • 2 cups diced tropical fruits (pineapple, mango, kiwi)
  • 1 bottle white wine
  • 1/4 cup sugar

Instructions:

  1. In a pitcher, combine diced fruits and sugar. Let sit for 10 minutes, allowing the juices to extract.
  2. Add coconut cream and white wine. Stir gently and refrigerate for 15-30 minutes to chill.
  3. Serve over ice and garnish with a fruit skewer.

Coconut Cream Sangria transports you to an island paradise, one sip at a time.

5. Espresso Martini Delight

Ditch the typical morning coffee for this irresistible dessert-inspired cocktail. The Espresso Martini Delight serves as both a caffeinated pick-me-up and a flavorful indulgence.

Ingredients:

  • 2 oz vodka
  • 1 oz coffee liqueur
  • 1 oz freshly brewed espresso
  • 1 tbsp simple syrup

Instructions:

  1. Add vodka, coffee liqueur, espresso, and simple syrup to a cocktail shaker with ice.
  2. Shake vigorously for about 20 seconds.
  3. Strain into a chilled martini glass and garnish with three coffee beans for luck.

Pair the Espresso Martini Delight with buttery croissants, pancakes, or chocolate desserts for the ultimate brunch pairing.

6. Mango Sunrise Cocktail

Bright and colorful, the Mango Sunrise Cocktail is as visually stunning as it is delicious. This citrusy-sweet drink captures the essence of a relaxing weekend morning.

Ingredients:

  • 2 oz mango nectar
  • 1 oz tequila
  • 1 oz orange juice
  • Grenadine

Instructions:

  1. Combine mango nectar, tequila, and orange juice in a shaker with ice. Shake well.
  2. Strain the mixture into a glass filled with ice. Slowly drizzle grenadine into the glass to create a sunrise effect.
  3. Garnish with an orange slice or cherry for a tropical presentation.

This tropical cocktail pairs well with savory brunch dishes like omelets or avocado toast.

7. Strawberry Mojito Bliss

Refreshing mint combines with sweet strawberries and just the right hint of rum for a reinvented mojito that’s perfect for brunch.

Ingredients:

  • 2 oz white rum
  • 1 oz strawberry syrup or puree
  • 1 oz lime juice
  • Fresh mint leaves
  • Soda water

Instructions:

  1. Muddle mint leaves in a glass with lime juice and strawberry syrup.
  2. Add crushed ice and pour in the rum.
  3. Top with soda water, stirring gently. Garnish with a strawberry and mint sprig.

This cocktail is light, bright, and balances sweetness with refreshing herbal notes.

8. Maple Bourbon Smash

Maple syrup lends a seasonal touch to this rich and robust bourbon cocktail, making it ideal for brunches with heartier dishes.

Ingredients:

  • 2 oz bourbon
  • 1 tbsp maple syrup
  • 1 oz lemon juice
  • Ice

Instructions:

  1. In a shaker, combine bourbon, maple syrup, and lemon juice. Add ice and shake well.
  2. Strain into a glass with fresh ice and garnish with a lemon wedge or cinnamon stick.

The Maple Bourbon Smash makes a cozy addition to brunch fare, especially during cooler months.

9. Pineapple Gin Spritz

This light and fruity cocktail combines tropical pineapple with botanical gin for a brunch drink that’s as refreshing as it is sweet.

Ingredients:

  • 2 oz gin
  • 2 oz pineapple juice
  • 1 oz elderflower liqueur
  • Soda water
  • Ice

Instructions:

  1. Mix gin, pineapple juice, and elderflower liqueur in a shaker with ice. Shake thoroughly.
  2. Strain into a tall glass filled with fresh ice. Top with soda water and garnish with a pineapple slice.

The Pineapple Gin Spritz is a delicate option for brunches, complementing lighter bites like smoked salmon or fresh veggies.

10. Creme Brulee White Russian

For the ultimate dessert-style brunch cocktail, look no further than the Creme Brulee White Russian. It’s creamy, indulgent, and guaranteed to impress your guests.

Ingredients:

  • 1 oz vodka
  • 1 oz coffee liqueur
  • 2 oz heavy cream
  • 1 tbsp caramel syrup

Instructions:

  1. Mix vodka, coffee liqueur, and caramel syrup in a shaker.
  2. Pour over a glass of ice. Add heavy cream and stir lightly for a layered effect.
  3. Garnish with a caramel drizzle or whipped cream for extra decadence.

Pair this cocktail with French toast or waffles for a brunch experience straight out of a dream.

Sweet brunch cocktails are a fun, flavorful way to elevate your weekend. Whether you’re drawn to fruity, tropical flavors or creamy dessert-inspired drinks, these ten recipes are sure to impress at your next gathering. Experiment with fresh ingredients, unique garnishes, and the perfect food pairings to make your brunch truly memorable. Cheers to soaking up the weekend in style!
