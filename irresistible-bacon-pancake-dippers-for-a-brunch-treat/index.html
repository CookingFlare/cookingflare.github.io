Irresistible Bacon Pancake Dippers for a Brunch Treat


Brunch, the most wonderful meal of the day, when the world stops and everyone comes together to share a delicious meal. And what’s better than a stack of fluffy pancakes, crispy bacon, and a drizzle of sweet syrup? Today, we’re going to take this classic combination to the next level by turning it into a delightful dipper. Say hello to Irresistible Bacon Pancake Dippers, a brunch treat that will leave your taste buds singing.

The Recipe

To make these irresistible dippers, you’ll need the following ingredients:

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 large egg
  • 2 tablespoons melted butter
  • 6 slices of bacon
  • 1 cup pancake mix
  • Maple syrup (for serving)

Step 1: Make the Pancake Batter

In a large bowl, whisk together the flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt. In a separate bowl, whisk together the milk, egg, and melted butter. Pour the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients and stir until just combined. The batter should still be slightly lumpy.

Step 2: Cook the Bacon

Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C). Line a baking sheet with aluminum foil and lay the bacon slices on it. Bake for 15-20 minutes, or until crispy and golden brown. Remove the bacon from the oven and set aside to cool.

Step 3: Cook the Pancakes

Heat a non-stick skillet or griddle over medium heat. Pour the pancake batter onto the skillet, using about 1/4 cup of batter for each pancake. Cook the pancakes for 2-3 minutes, until bubbles appear on the surface and the edges start to dry. Flip the pancakes and cook for an additional 1-2 minutes, until golden brown. Repeat with the remaining batter.

Step 4: Assemble the Dippers

Once the pancakes are cooked, cut them into quarters or sixths, depending on your desired dipper size. Cut the cooled bacon into strips, about 1 inch (2.5 cm) long. Dip each pancake piece into the melted butter, then coat with a piece of bacon. Place the dippers on a serving plate or tray and drizzle with maple syrup. Serve immediately and enjoy!

Tips and Variations

  • For an extra crispy bacon, bake it in the oven for an additional 5-10 minutes.
  • Use different types of syrup, such as honey or agave, for a unique flavor.
  • Add some chopped nuts or chocolate chips to the pancake batter for added texture and flavor.
  • Serve the dippers with a side of fresh fruit or whipped cream for a sweet and satisfying treat.

Conclusion

And there you have it, folks! Irresistible Bacon Pancake Dippers, a brunch treat that’s sure to impress your friends and family. With its crispy bacon, fluffy pancakes, and sweet syrup, this dish is a match made in heaven. So go ahead, give it a try, and indulge in the sweet and savory goodness of these irresistible dippers. Happy cooking!
