Moist and Flavorful Carrot Cake with Cream Cheese Frosting: A Classic Favorite


Carrot cake is a timeless classic that never fails to delight. With its moist texture, rich flavor, and creamy cream cheese frosting, it’s no wonder why this cake has become a favorite dessert for many. Whether you’re a seasoned baker or just starting out, this recipe will guide you through the steps to create a carrot cake that will impress even the most discerning taste buds.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons baking soda
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground nutmeg
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 1/2 cups granulated sugar
  • 1 cup vegetable oil
  • 4 large eggs
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 3 cups grated carrots
  • 1 cup crushed pineapple, drained
  • 1/2 cup shredded coconut (optional)
  • 1/2 cup chopped walnuts (optional)

Cream Cheese Frosting

  • 8 oz cream cheese, softened
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 4 cups powdered sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease and flour two 9-inch round cake pans, or line them with parchment paper for easy removal.

  2. In a large mixing bowl, whisk together the flour, baking powder, baking soda, cinnamon, nutmeg, and salt. Set aside.

  3. In another bowl, beat the sugar, vegetable oil, eggs, and vanilla extract until well combined and creamy.

  4. Gradually add the dry ingredients to the wet ingredients, mixing until just combined. Be careful not to overmix, as this can result in a dense cake.

  5. Fold in the grated carrots, crushed pineapple, shredded coconut (if using), and chopped walnuts (if using). These ingredients add extra moisture and texture to the cake.

  6. Divide the batter equally between the prepared cake pans. Smooth the tops with a spatula to ensure even baking.

  7. Bake for approximately 30-35 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center of the cakes comes out clean.

  8. Remove the cakes from the oven and let them cool in the pans for about 10 minutes. Then, transfer them to a wire rack to cool completely.

  9. While the cakes are cooling, prepare the cream cheese frosting. In a mixing bowl, beat the softened cream cheese and butter until smooth and creamy. Gradually add the powdered sugar, one cup at a time, mixing well after each addition. Stir in the vanilla extract.

  10. Once the cakes are completely cooled, spread a generous layer of cream cheese frosting on top of one cake layer. Place the second cake layer on top and frost the entire cake with the remaining cream cheese frosting. Optionally, you can garnish the cake with additional chopped walnuts or grated carrots.

  11. Slice, serve, and enjoy this moist and flavorful carrot cake with its creamy cream cheese frosting. It’s a classic favorite that never disappoints!

Conclusion

Carrot cake with cream cheese frosting is a true classic that has stood the test of time. Its combination of moist cake, sweet carrots, and tangy cream cheese frosting creates a delightful harmony of flavors. Whether you’re celebrating a special occasion or simply craving a delicious treat, this recipe is sure to satisfy. So, gather your ingredients, preheat your oven, and get ready to indulge in a slice of this beloved dessert. Your taste buds will thank you!
