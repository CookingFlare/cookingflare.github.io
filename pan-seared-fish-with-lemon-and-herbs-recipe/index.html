Pan-Seared Fish with Lemon and Herbs Recipe


Are you looking for a quick and delicious seafood dish that will impress your family and friends? Look no further! This Pan-Seared Fish with Lemon and Herbs recipe is the perfect choice for a flavorful and healthy meal. With just a few simple ingredients and minimal prep time, you can have a restaurant-quality dish right in the comfort of your own home.

Ingredients

  • Fresh fish fillets (such as snapper, halibut, or cod)
  • Olive oil
  • Salt and pepper
  • Fresh lemon juice
  • Fresh herbs (such as parsley, thyme, or dill)

Instructions

  1. Preheat your skillet over medium-high heat. While the skillet is heating up, pat the fish fillets dry with a paper towel to remove any excess moisture. This will ensure a nice sear on the fish.

  2. Season the fish fillets with salt and pepper on both sides. Be generous with the seasoning to enhance the flavors of the fish.

  3. Add a drizzle of olive oil to the hot skillet and carefully place the fish fillets in the pan, skin side down if applicable. Allow the fish to cook undisturbed for about 3-4 minutes, or until the skin is crispy and golden brown.

  4. Flip the fish fillets gently using a spatula or tongs. If the fish has skin, be extra careful not to break it. Cook for an additional 2-3 minutes, or until the fish is opaque and flakes easily with a fork.

  5. Squeeze fresh lemon juice over the fish fillets to add a burst of citrusy flavor. The acidity of the lemon will complement the delicate taste of the fish.

  6. Sprinkle a generous amount of fresh herbs over the fish. The fragrant herbs will elevate the dish and add a touch of freshness.

  7. Remove the fish from the skillet and transfer it to a serving plate. Garnish with additional herbs, if desired.

  8. Serve the Pan-Seared Fish with Lemon and Herbs immediately while it’s still hot. This dish pairs perfectly with a side of steamed vegetables or a light salad.

Tips for Success

  • Choose fresh fish fillets for the best results. Look for fillets that are firm, shiny, and have a mild oceanic scent.

  • Experiment with different herbs to find your favorite combination. Each herb offers a unique flavor profile that can enhance the overall taste of the dish.

  • Don’t overcrowd the skillet when cooking the fish. This will ensure that each fillet cooks evenly and achieves a beautiful sear.

  • If you prefer a crispy skin, make sure to pat the fish fillets dry before seasoning and cooking. Moisture on the skin can prevent it from crisping up.

  • Adjust the cooking time based on the thickness of the fish fillets. Thicker fillets may require a few more minutes of cooking to ensure they are fully cooked through.

Now that you have the recipe for Pan-Seared Fish with Lemon and Herbs, it’s time to gather your ingredients and get cooking! Impress your loved ones with this simple yet elegant dish that bursts with flavors. Enjoy the delicious combination of tender fish, tangy lemon, and aromatic herbs. Bon appétit!
