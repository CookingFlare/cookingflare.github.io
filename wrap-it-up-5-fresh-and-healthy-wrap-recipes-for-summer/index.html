Wrap It Up: 5 Fresh and Healthy Wrap Recipes for Summer


Summer is the perfect time to indulge in light, fresh, and healthy meals that won’t weigh you down. Wraps are a fantastic option for a quick, satisfying, and portable meal that can be enjoyed on the go or as a leisurely al fresco dining experience. In this blog post, we’ll explore five delicious wrap recipes that are bursting with seasonal flavors and are sure to become your new summer favorites.

1. Mediterranean Chickpea Wrap

Ingredients:

  • Whole grain wrap
  • 1 cup cooked chickpeas
  • 1/2 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1/4 cup diced cucumber
  • 2 tablespoons diced red onion
  • 2 tablespoons crumbled feta cheese
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley
  • 1 tablespoon extra virgin olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, combine the chickpeas, cherry tomatoes, cucumber, red onion, feta cheese, and parsley.
  2. Drizzle the olive oil and lemon juice over the mixture and toss to coat.
  3. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  4. Place the chickpea mixture onto the whole grain wrap and fold it into a burrito-style wrap.

2. Thai Peanut Tofu Wrap

Ingredients:

  • Large tortilla wrap
  • 1 cup firm tofu, sliced into strips
  • 1/4 cup shredded carrots
  • 1/4 cup shredded purple cabbage
  • 2 tablespoons chopped peanuts
  • Fresh cilantro leaves
  • Thai peanut sauce

Instructions:

  1. Sauté the tofu strips in a pan until golden brown.
  2. Place the tofu, shredded carrots, purple cabbage, chopped peanuts, and cilantro leaves on the tortilla wrap.
  3. Drizzle with Thai peanut sauce.
  4. Roll the wrap tightly, tucking in the sides as you go.

3. Grilled Veggie Hummus Wrap

Ingredients:

  • Spinach wrap
  • 1 zucchini, sliced
  • 1 yellow bell pepper, sliced
  • 1/2 red onion, sliced
  • 1/4 cup hummus
  • Fresh baby spinach leaves
  • Balsamic glaze

Instructions:

  1. Grill the zucchini, yellow bell pepper, and red onion until tender and slightly charred.
  2. Spread the hummus evenly on the spinach wrap.
  3. Layer the grilled veggies and baby spinach leaves on top of the hummus.
  4. Drizzle with balsamic glaze before wrapping it up.

4. California Turkey Club Wrap

Ingredients:

  • Large whole wheat wrap
  • 4 slices roasted turkey breast
  • 2 slices cooked bacon
  • 1/2 avocado, sliced
  • 1/2 cup mixed greens
  • 2 tablespoons ranch dressing

Instructions:

  1. Lay the whole wheat wrap on a flat surface.
  2. Arrange the turkey breast, bacon, avocado, and mixed greens on the wrap.
  3. Drizzle with ranch dressing.
  4. Roll up the wrap tightly.

5. Greek Yogurt Chicken Caesar Wrap

Ingredients:

  • Whole grain wrap
  • 1 cup cooked chicken breast, sliced
  • 1/4 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1/4 cup sliced cucumber
  • 2 tablespoons grated Parmesan cheese
  • Fresh romaine lettuce
  • Greek yogurt Caesar dressing

Instructions:

  1. Place the sliced chicken breast, cherry tomatoes, cucumber, Parmesan cheese, and romaine lettuce on the whole grain wrap.
  2. Drizzle with Greek yogurt Caesar dressing.
  3. Roll up the wrap, ensuring all the ingredients are tightly enclosed.

Enjoy these fresh and healthy wrap recipes that are perfect for summer picnics, beach days, or quick and easy meals at home. Experiment with different fillings, sauces, and wraps to create your own signature summer wraps that will keep you cool, satisfied, and energized throughout the season. Happy wrapping!
