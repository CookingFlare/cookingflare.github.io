Thai Basil Pork Noodle Soup: A Hearty and Aromatic Bowl


If you’re a fan of Thai cuisine, you’re in for a treat with this Thai Basil Pork Noodle Soup recipe. This dish is a delightful combination of bold flavors, tender pork, and aromatic herbs, all coming together in a comforting and satisfying bowl of soup. Whether you’re looking for a quick and easy weeknight meal or wanting to impress your friends and family with your culinary skills, this recipe is sure to become a favorite in your kitchen.

Ingredients

For the Soup:

  • 1 pound pork tenderloin, thinly sliced
  • 8 cups chicken broth
  • 1 can (14 ounces) coconut milk
  • 4-5 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon ginger, grated
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons fish sauce
  • 1 tablespoon brown sugar
  • 1 teaspoon red curry paste
  • 4 ounces rice noodles
  • 1 red bell pepper, thinly sliced
  • 1 cup snap peas
  • 1 cup fresh basil leaves
  • Salt and pepper to taste

For Garnish:

  • Fresh cilantro, chopped
  • Green onions, thinly sliced
  • Lime wedges
  • Red pepper flakes (optional)

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Pork: In a large pot, heat some oil over medium-high heat. Add the sliced pork tenderloin and cook until browned and cooked through. Once done, remove the pork from the pot and set it aside.

  2. Make the Soup Base: In the same pot, add the minced garlic and grated ginger. Sauté for a minute until fragrant. Pour in the chicken broth and coconut milk, stirring to combine. Add the soy sauce, fish sauce, brown sugar, and red curry paste. Bring the mixture to a gentle simmer.

  3. Cook the Noodles and Vegetables: While the soup base simmers, cook the rice noodles according to the package instructions. In the last 2 minutes of cooking, add the red bell pepper and snap peas to the pot. Drain the noodles and vegetables, then set them aside.

  4. Assemble the Soup: Return the cooked pork to the pot with the soup base. Stir in the fresh basil leaves and let the soup simmer for another 5 minutes to allow the flavors to meld together. Season with salt and pepper to taste.

  5. Serve: Divide the cooked noodles and vegetables into serving bowls. Ladle the fragrant Thai basil pork soup over the noodles. Garnish with chopped cilantro, sliced green onions, and a wedge of lime. For an extra kick of heat, sprinkle some red pepper flakes on top.

Enjoy the Flavors of Thailand

This Thai Basil Pork Noodle Soup is a wonderful representation of the vibrant and aromatic flavors that Thai cuisine is known for. The tender pork, fragrant basil, and the rich coconut milk all come together to create a truly comforting and satisfying meal that will transport your taste buds to the bustling streets of Thailand.

Whether you’re a seasoned home cook or just starting your culinary journey, this recipe is a fantastic way to explore the diverse and delightful world of Thai cooking. So, gather your ingredients, put on your apron, and embark on a culinary adventure with this delicious Thai Basil Pork Noodle Soup. Your taste buds will thank you!
