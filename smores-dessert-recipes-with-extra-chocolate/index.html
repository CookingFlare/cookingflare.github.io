S'mores Dessert Recipes with Extra Chocolate


There’s nothing quite like the magic of gooey marshmallows, crispy graham crackers, and the unmistakable richness of chocolate to transport you back to moments around the campfire. But what if you could elevate this classic treat to new heights? Enter the world of S’mores dessert recipes infused with extra chocolate for an indulgence you won’t be able to resist. Whether you’re preparing for a cozy family gathering, hosting a sweet dessert night, or just satisfying your own cravings, these recipes are bound to ignite your love for this iconic pairing. Let’s dive into creative, decadent spins on S’mores desserts that’ll make chocolate lovers swoon.

1. Decadent Double Chocolate S’mores Brownies

For fans of rich, fudgy desserts, S’mores brownies combine the best of both worlds—chewy brownies and gooey marshmallow layers. To take it up a notch, we’ll pack this recipe with extra chocolate.

Ingredients:

  • 3/4 cup unsalted butter (melted)
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/2 cup packed brown sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1/2 cup cocoa powder
  • 3/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1 cup chocolate chips (dark or semi-sweet)
  • 5 graham crackers (crushed into coarse crumbs)
  • 2 cups mini marshmallows

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C) and grease an 8x8-inch baking dish.
  2. In a mixing bowl, whisk melted butter, granulated sugar, and brown sugar until smooth. Add eggs and vanilla extract, continuing to whisk until creamy.
  3. Sift in cocoa powder, flour, and salt, and gently fold the mixture until fully incorporated. Stir in the chocolate chips for that extra chocolatey bite.
  4. Spread half of the brownie batter into the prepared dish. Layer crushed graham crackers evenly over the batter, followed by mini marshmallows. Top with the remaining brownie batter.
  5. Bake for 30-35 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out with moist crumbs.
  6. Allow the brownies to cool before slicing into servings. These indulgent treats are perfect for melting chocolate lovers.

2. Creamy S’mores Cheesecake with Extra Chocolate Ganache

Cheesecake meets S’mores in this ultra-creamy, decadent dessert. With a graham cracker crust, velvety marshmallow filling, and a thick layer of chocolate ganache, this recipe is destined to become your next showstopper.

Ingredients for the Graham Cracker Crust:

  • 2 cups graham cracker crumbs
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter (melted)
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar

Ingredients for Marshmallow Cheesecake Filling:

  • 16 oz cream cheese (softened)
  • 1 cup marshmallow fluff
  • 1/2 cup powdered sugar
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 2 large eggs

Ingredients for Chocolate Ganache Topping:

  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1 1/2 cups dark chocolate chips

Instructions:

  1. Make the Crust: Preheat your oven to 325°F (163°C). Mix graham cracker crumbs, melted butter, and sugar in a bowl. Press the mixture evenly into the bottom of a springform pan. Bake for 10 minutes and set aside to cool.
  2. Prepare the Filling: Beat the cream cheese, marshmallow fluff, powdered sugar, and vanilla extract until smooth. Add eggs one at a time, mixing thoroughly after each addition. Pour the filling over the cooled crust.
  3. Bake: Bake the cheesecake for 40-45 minutes, or until it is set in the center. Cool completely and refrigerate for at least 4 hours (or overnight).
  4. Make the Ganache: Heat heavy cream in a saucepan until it just begins to simmer. Pour over the chocolate chips in a heatproof bowl and let sit for 2 minutes. Whisk until smooth, then spread over the cooled cheesecake.
  5. Serve: Slice and garnish with mini marshmallows, chocolate drizzle, or graham cracker crumbs for a polished finish.

3. Gooey S’mores Chocolate Lava Cakes

The indulgence factor skyrockets with this molten S’mores chocolate lava cake recipe. Perfect for individual servings, these cakes create a warm, chocolatey explosion with every cut.

Ingredients:

  • 8 oz semi-sweet chocolate (chopped)
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter
  • 1 cup powdered sugar
  • 2 eggs + 2 egg yolks
  • 1/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup graham cracker crumbs
  • Mini marshmallows for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 425°F (220°C). Grease four ramekins with butter and dust with graham cracker crumbs.
  2. Melt chocolate and butter together in a heatproof bowl over simmering water or in the microwave in 20-second bursts. Stir until smooth.
  3. Whisk together powdered sugar, eggs, and egg yolks in a mixing bowl. Slowly pour in the melted chocolate mixture, followed by the flour, and mix gently until combined.
  4. Divide the batter evenly among the ramekins. Bake for 12-14 minutes, or until the edges appear set but the center jiggles slightly.
  5. Serve immediately with marshmallows, graham crackers, and extra melted chocolate on top for the full S’mores effect.

4. S’mores Chocolate Bark

For an easy yet crowd-pleasing dessert, S’mores chocolate bark delivers maximum flavor in minimal prep time. This recipe is customizable to your favorite toppings and is entirely beginner-friendly.

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups milk chocolate chips
  • 1 cup dark chocolate chips
  • 1 cup mini marshmallows
  • 5 graham crackers (broken into small pieces)
  • Optional toppings: white chocolate drizzle, crushed pretzels, toasted coconut

Instructions:

  1. Line a baking sheet with parchment paper. Melt the milk and dark chocolate chips together using a double boiler or microwave in 20-second increments. Stir until smooth.
  2. Spread the melted chocolate evenly over the parchment paper, creating a thin layer.
  3. Immediately sprinkle mini marshmallows, graham cracker pieces, and any optional toppings over the chocolate while it’s still warm. Press gently to ensure everything sticks.
  4. Refrigerate the sheet for 1-2 hours or until the chocolate sets completely. Break into irregular pieces and serve.

5. No-Bake S’mores Icebox Cake

Ideal for hot days or when you don’t want to turn on the oven, this no-bake S’mores icebox cake satisfies every sweet tooth. With layers of graham crackers, whipped marshmallow cream, and a rich chocolate filling, it’s a dessert as easy as it is delicious.

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups heavy whipping cream
  • 1 cup marshmallow fluff
  • 2 cups semi-sweet chocolate chips
  • 1/2 cup whole milk
  • 1 box graham crackers

Instructions:

  1. Whip heavy cream to stiff peaks, then fold in marshmallow fluff until combined. Set aside.
  2. In a microwave-safe bowl, melt chocolate chips and milk together in 20-second intervals, stirring until smooth.
  3. In a rectangular baking dish, layer graham crackers to cover the bottom. Top with a layer of whipped marshmallow cream, followed by a drizzle of the melted chocolate mixture. Repeat the layers until all ingredients are used, ending with whipped cream on top.
  4. Cover and refrigerate the cake for at least 4 hours (or overnight) to let the flavors meld and the graham crackers soften into a cake-like texture.

These extra-chocolate-filled S’mores desserts prove that creativity knows no bounds when it comes to turning a nostalgic treat into something unforgettable. Whether you prefer brownies, cheesecakes, lava cakes, or simpler options like bark or icebox cakes, there’s a recipe here to suit every mood and occasion. Ready to indulge? It’s time to gather the ingredients and unlock the door to chocolate bliss!
