Instant Pot Chili with Ground Turkey: A Leaner Option


If you’re craving a hearty, flavorful chili but want to keep it light and healthy, this Instant Pot chili with ground turkey is the perfect recipe. It’s packed with bold flavors, loaded with healthy ingredients, and incredibly easy to make. The Instant Pot takes all the guesswork out of preparing a satisfying meal, reducing cooking time while preserving the rich, complex taste of a slow-simmered chili. Whether you’re preparing for a family dinner, meal prep for the week, or feeding a crowd, this recipe is a winner!

Why Choose Ground Turkey for Chili?

Chili is traditionally made with beef, but if you’re looking to reduce fat and calories without sacrificing flavor, ground turkey is a great substitute. Ground turkey is lean, high in protein, and has a mild taste that allows the spices and other ingredients to shine. It’s a heart-healthy option that makes this chili lighter but still satisfying. Plus, using lean ground turkey helps balance the richness of the chili, making it a perfect choice for those who want to enjoy their favorite comfort foods without the guilt.

Ingredients You’ll Need

For this recipe, we’ll be using a variety of fresh ingredients, pantry staples, and aromatic spices that make chili so delicious. Here’s everything you’ll need to create the perfect Instant Pot chili:

  • 1 pound lean ground turkey: This provides a lean source of protein and a mild base for the chili.
  • 1 medium onion: Adds sweetness and depth to the chili’s base.
  • 3 cloves garlic: Minced for that irresistible aromatic punch.
  • 1 red bell pepper: Adds sweetness and color.
  • 1 green bell pepper: For additional flavor and crunch.
  • 2 cans (14.5 oz each) diced tomatoes: These form the base of the chili’s sauce.
  • 1 can (15 oz) kidney beans: Rich in fiber and protein, this adds heartiness to the chili.
  • 1 can (15 oz) black beans: For texture and added protein.
  • 1 can (4 oz) diced green chilies: Adds a slight heat and extra flavor.
  • 1 tablespoon chili powder: A key seasoning that gives the chili its signature flavor.
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin: For a warm, earthy undertone.
  • 1 teaspoon smoked paprika: Adds a subtle smokiness to the chili.
  • 1/2 teaspoon oregano: For an herby, slightly bitter flavor.
  • 1/2 teaspoon crushed red pepper flakes: For an extra kick of heat (optional).
  • 1/2 cup low-sodium chicken broth: Helps to create a savory, liquid base for the chili.
  • Salt and pepper to taste: For seasoning.
  • Olive oil: For sautéing the vegetables and turkey.

Instructions: Step-by-Step

Step 1: Sauté the Vegetables and Ground Turkey

The first step in making this Instant Pot chili is to sauté the vegetables and turkey, which helps develop the rich flavors right from the start.

  • Turn on the Instant Pot and select the “Sauté” function. Add a tablespoon of olive oil to the pot.
  • Once the oil is hot, add the diced onion, red bell pepper, and green bell pepper to the pot. Sauté for about 3-4 minutes, or until the vegetables begin to soften.
  • Add the minced garlic and sauté for another 30 seconds, or until fragrant.
  • Add the ground turkey to the pot, breaking it apart with a spoon or spatula. Cook the turkey until it is browned and no longer pink, about 5-7 minutes. Stir frequently to ensure the turkey is evenly cooked.

Step 2: Add the Spices and Stir

Once the turkey is browned and the vegetables are softened, it’s time to add the spices that will give the chili its deep, rich flavor.

  • Stir in the chili powder, cumin, smoked paprika, oregano, and crushed red pepper flakes (if using). Stir well to coat the meat and vegetables in the spices.
  • Allow the spices to toast for about 1 minute to release their flavors. This step deepens the flavor of your chili and brings out the best in each spice.

Step 3: Add the Canned Ingredients

Now that your meat and veggies are perfectly seasoned, it’s time to add the canned ingredients that will form the base of your chili.

  • Add the diced tomatoes (with their juices), kidney beans, black beans, and diced green chilies to the Instant Pot.
  • Pour in the chicken broth, which will help loosen everything and create a nice, stew-like consistency.
  • Stir everything together to combine, making sure the ingredients are evenly distributed.

Step 4: Pressure Cook the Chili

Now it’s time to let the Instant Pot work its magic.

  • Close the Instant Pot lid and ensure the valve is set to the “Sealing” position.
  • Select the “Manual” or “Pressure Cook” function and set the timer for 15 minutes at high pressure.
  • Once the cooking time is up, allow the Instant Pot to naturally release pressure for 10 minutes, then carefully turn the valve to “Venting” to release the remaining pressure.

Step 5: Final Touches and Adjust Seasoning

Once the pressure has been released, open the Instant Pot and give the chili a good stir. Taste and adjust the seasoning if necessary. You might want to add a pinch of salt or a dash of pepper depending on your taste preferences. If you like your chili thicker, you can select the “Sauté” function again and cook the chili for an additional 5-10 minutes to reduce the liquid to your desired consistency.

Serving Suggestions

This Instant Pot chili with ground turkey is versatile, and there are plenty of ways to serve it. Here are a few ideas:

  • Serve with toppings: Add shredded cheese, sour cream, sliced green onions, or fresh cilantro for extra flavor. If you’re looking for a dairy-free option, try avocado slices or a squeeze of lime juice.
  • Pair with cornbread: The slightly sweet flavor of cornbread pairs wonderfully with the spiciness of chili. You can either bake it separately or use a cornbread mix for a quick and easy side dish.
  • Over rice or quinoa: For an extra filling meal, serve the chili over a bed of rice or quinoa. The grains absorb the chili’s flavors and provide a heartier base.
  • Enjoy as a stand-alone meal: If you prefer to keep things simple, this chili is fantastic on its own, served in a bowl with a side of crusty bread for dipping.

Storing and Freezing Leftovers

This Instant Pot chili makes great leftovers and can be stored in the fridge for up to 3-4 days. Let the chili cool to room temperature before transferring it to an airtight container. When you’re ready to eat, simply reheat on the stovetop or in the microwave.

If you want to make this chili in advance or have extra servings, it freezes beautifully. To freeze, let the chili cool completely, then transfer it to a freezer-safe container or zip-top bag. It will keep in the freezer for up to 3 months. When ready to eat, thaw overnight in the fridge and reheat on the stovetop.

Why Use an Instant Pot for Chili?

Making chili in an Instant Pot is a game changer for busy cooks. The Instant Pot’s pressure-cooking function allows you to make chili in a fraction of the time it would take on the stovetop, without sacrificing flavor. Traditional chili recipes require hours of simmering to develop deep, rich flavors, but the Instant Pot accelerates the process while retaining all the savory goodness. Plus, the convenience of using a single pot means cleanup is a breeze!

Variations to Customize Your Chili

This recipe is very customizable, allowing you to make the chili your own. Here are a few ways you can modify it to suit your preferences:

  • Spicy options: Add more heat by using jalapeños, serrano peppers, or adding extra crushed red pepper flakes.
  • Add veggies: Sneak in more veggies like zucchini, carrots, or sweet potatoes for added nutrition.
  • Beans or no beans: If you’re not a fan of beans, you can omit them entirely or use just one type of bean for a more streamlined chili.
  • Use ground beef or chicken: If you prefer a different protein, feel free to substitute the ground turkey with lean ground beef, ground chicken, or even plant-based ground meat for a vegetarian version.
  • Use different spices: Swap in your favorite chili seasonings, such as coriander, cinnamon, or even a splash of hot sauce, to add a unique flavor profile.

Conclusion

This Instant Pot chili with ground turkey is the perfect dish for a cozy, nutritious, and delicious meal. By using lean ground turkey and wholesome ingredients, you’re able to create a chili that’s both satisfying and healthier than traditional versions made with beef. The Instant Pot makes this recipe incredibly easy and quick, allowing you to enjoy a homemade, comforting chili without spending hours in the kitchen. Whether you’re feeding a family, meal prepping for the week, or serving a crowd, this chili is sure to become a staple in your recipe rotation.

So, fire up your Instant Pot, gather your ingredients, and enjoy a bowl of this hearty and lean Instant Pot chili with ground turkey today!
