Homemade Strawberry Mango Ice Cream: A Tropical Fusion


Are you ready to embark on a delightful journey to the tropics right from the comfort of your own kitchen? Look no further than this homemade Strawberry Mango Ice Cream recipe! This delightful tropical fusion of flavors will transport your taste buds to a sun-kissed paradise with every spoonful.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups ripe mango, diced
  • 2 cups fresh strawberries, hulled
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 2 cups heavy cream
  • 1 cup whole milk
  • Pinch of salt

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Fruits: Begin by preparing the mango and strawberries. Peel and dice the ripe mango, ensuring that it’s free from any fibrous bits. Hull the fresh strawberries and give them a quick rinse. Once done, pat them dry with a paper towel.

  2. Blend the Fruits: In a blender, combine the diced mango, fresh strawberries, and granulated sugar. Blend until the fruits are pureed and the sugar has completely dissolved.

  3. Add Vanilla and Salt: Once the fruits are blended, add the vanilla extract and a pinch of salt to the fruit puree. Give it a quick blend to incorporate the flavors.

  4. Prepare the Ice Cream Base: In a separate bowl, whisk together the heavy cream and whole milk until well combined.

  5. Combine the Mixtures: Gently fold the fruit puree into the ice cream base, ensuring that the mixture is well incorporated but not over-mixed.

  6. Chill the Mixture: Cover the mixture with plastic wrap and chill it in the refrigerator for at least 2 hours, allowing the flavors to meld together.

  7. Churn the Ice Cream: After the mixture has chilled, pour it into an ice cream maker and churn according to the manufacturer’s instructions until it reaches a creamy, frozen consistency.

  8. Freeze the Ice Cream: Transfer the churned ice cream into a freezer-safe container, cover it with a lid, and freeze for an additional 4-6 hours, or until the ice cream is firm.

  9. Serve and Enjoy: Once the ice cream has set, scoop it into bowls or cones, and indulge in the luscious, tropical flavors of your homemade Strawberry Mango Ice Cream!

Tips and Variations

  • For an extra burst of tropical flavor, consider adding a splash of fresh lime juice to the fruit puree.
  • To make this recipe dairy-free, substitute the heavy cream and whole milk with coconut cream and coconut milk for a refreshing vegan alternative.
  • Experiment with different fruit combinations such as pineapple and kiwi for a unique twist on this tropical ice cream.

Conclusion

With its vibrant hues and refreshing flavors, this Homemade Strawberry Mango Ice Cream is the perfect dessert for hot summer days or anytime you crave a taste of the tropics. Whether enjoyed on its own or as a delightful accompaniment to your favorite desserts, this tropical fusion ice cream is sure to become a beloved treat for you and your loved ones.

So, roll up your sleeves, gather your ingredients, and get ready to savor a scoop of paradise with every bite of this delightful homemade ice cream!
