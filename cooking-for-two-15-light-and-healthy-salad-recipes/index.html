Cooking for Two: 15 Light and Healthy Salad Recipes


Salads are one of the most versatile meals that you can prepare, especially when you’re cooking for two. Whether you’re in the mood for something light and refreshing or a hearty meal to satisfy your hunger, salads can do it all. The best part? They’re packed with nutrients, customizable to your preferences, and quick to prepare. In this post, we’ll explore 15 light and healthy salad recipes that will leave you feeling full, nourished, and happy. These dishes are perfect for any season and are designed to be simple yet bursting with flavor.

1. Grilled Chicken and Avocado Salad

Grilled chicken and avocado create a perfect balance in this healthy salad. The smokiness from the grilled chicken complements the creamy texture of the avocado, while the crunchy veggies add a refreshing crunch.

Ingredients:

  • 2 chicken breasts
  • 1 avocado, sliced
  • 1 cucumber, thinly sliced
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 2 cups mixed greens (arugula, spinach, etc.)
  • Olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Lemon juice or balsamic vinegar for dressing

Instructions:

  1. Season the chicken breasts with olive oil, salt, and pepper. Grill them over medium heat until cooked through (about 6-7 minutes per side).
  2. Slice the chicken into thin strips.
  3. In a large bowl, combine the mixed greens, cucumber, cherry tomatoes, and avocado.
  4. Top with grilled chicken slices and drizzle with your choice of dressing.
  5. Toss gently and serve immediately.

2. Mediterranean Quinoa Salad

This quinoa salad is not only healthy but also packed with Mediterranean flavors like olives, feta cheese, and a tangy lemon dressing. It’s the perfect side dish or a light main meal for two.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup cooked quinoa
  • 1/4 cup Kalamata olives, sliced
  • 1/2 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1/4 cup cucumber, diced
  • 1/4 cup red onion, thinly sliced
  • 1/4 cup crumbled feta cheese
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • 1 tbsp red wine vinegar
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Cook the quinoa according to the package instructions and let it cool.
  2. In a large bowl, combine the cooked quinoa, olives, cherry tomatoes, cucumber, red onion, and feta cheese.
  3. In a small bowl, whisk together olive oil, lemon juice, red wine vinegar, salt, and pepper.
  4. Drizzle the dressing over the salad and toss gently to combine.
  5. Serve immediately or refrigerate for an hour before serving to allow the flavors to meld.

3. Asian-Inspired Shrimp Salad

This Asian-inspired shrimp salad is a refreshing and light meal with a delicious combination of shrimp, crunchy vegetables, and a zesty dressing.

Ingredients:

  • 8 oz shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 2 cups shredded cabbage (green and purple)
  • 1/2 carrot, julienned
  • 1/4 cup cucumber, julienned
  • 1/4 cup green onions, chopped
  • 1/4 cup cilantro, chopped
  • 2 tbsp sesame oil
  • 1 tbsp rice vinegar
  • 1 tbsp soy sauce (or tamari for gluten-free)
  • 1 tsp honey
  • Sesame seeds for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Cook the shrimp in a pan over medium heat for 2-3 minutes on each side until pink and cooked through. Let them cool slightly.
  2. In a large bowl, combine the shredded cabbage, carrot, cucumber, green onions, and cilantro.
  3. In a small bowl, whisk together sesame oil, rice vinegar, soy sauce, and honey.
  4. Add the shrimp to the salad and drizzle with the dressing.
  5. Toss the salad gently and sprinkle with sesame seeds before serving.

4. Spinach and Strawberry Salad with Goat Cheese

Sweet and tangy strawberries paired with creamy goat cheese and a light balsamic dressing make this spinach and strawberry salad a delightful option for two.

Ingredients:

  • 4 cups fresh spinach
  • 1 cup strawberries, sliced
  • 1/4 cup goat cheese, crumbled
  • 1/4 cup almonds, slivered or chopped
  • 2 tbsp balsamic vinegar
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a large bowl, toss together the spinach, strawberries, goat cheese, and almonds.
  2. In a small bowl, whisk together balsamic vinegar, olive oil, salt, and pepper.
  3. Drizzle the dressing over the salad and toss gently.
  4. Serve immediately for a light and refreshing meal.

5. Roasted Beet and Arugula Salad

The earthy flavor of roasted beets pairs perfectly with peppery arugula in this healthy, nutrient-packed salad.

Ingredients:

  • 2 medium beets, peeled and roasted
  • 2 cups arugula
  • 1/4 cup walnuts, toasted
  • 1/4 cup goat cheese, crumbled
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tbsp balsamic vinegar
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Roast the beets by wrapping them in foil and placing them in a 400°F (200°C) oven for about 45-60 minutes. Let them cool, then peel and slice them.
  2. In a large bowl, combine the arugula, roasted beets, toasted walnuts, and goat cheese.
  3. Drizzle with olive oil and balsamic vinegar, then season with salt and pepper.
  4. Toss gently and serve.

6. Cucumber and Chickpea Salad

This simple and refreshing cucumber and chickpea salad is filled with fiber, making it a filling but light meal for two.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cucumber, diced
  • 1 can chickpeas, drained and rinsed
  • 1/4 red onion, thinly sliced
  • 1/4 cup fresh parsley, chopped
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, combine cucumber, chickpeas, red onion, and parsley.
  2. In a separate small bowl, whisk together olive oil, lemon juice, salt, and pepper.
  3. Pour the dressing over the salad and toss to combine.
  4. Serve chilled or at room temperature.

7. Lentil and Kale Salad with Lemon Dressing

Packed with protein and fiber, this lentil and kale salad is a hearty yet light option that makes for a great main dish.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup cooked lentils
  • 2 cups kale, chopped and massaged
  • 1/4 cup red onion, thinly sliced
  • 1/4 cup feta cheese, crumbled
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • 1 tsp Dijon mustard
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a large bowl, combine the cooked lentils, kale, red onion, and feta cheese.
  2. In a small bowl, whisk together olive oil, lemon juice, Dijon mustard, salt, and pepper.
  3. Drizzle the dressing over the salad and toss gently to combine.
  4. Serve immediately or let it sit for a few minutes to allow the flavors to develop.

8. Caprese Salad with Basil Pesto

A classic Caprese salad is elevated with the addition of homemade basil pesto, turning it into a refreshing and flavorful meal.

Ingredients:

  • 2 large tomatoes, sliced
  • 1/2 cup fresh mozzarella, sliced
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • 2 tbsp basil pesto
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Arrange the tomato and mozzarella slices on a plate.
  2. Tuck basil leaves in between the slices.
  3. Drizzle with basil pesto and season with salt and pepper.
  4. Serve immediately, perfect for a light and summery meal.

9. Carrot and Avocado Salad with Tahini Dressing

This vibrant and nutrient-packed carrot and avocado salad is an explosion of flavors, topped with a creamy tahini dressing.

Ingredients:

  • 2 large carrots, peeled and shredded
  • 1 avocado, sliced
  • 1/4 cup fresh cilantro, chopped
  • 2 tbsp tahini
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a large bowl, combine shredded carrots, avocado slices, and cilantro.
  2. In a small bowl, whisk together tahini, lemon juice, olive oil, salt, and pepper.
  3. Drizzle the dressing over the salad and toss gently to combine.
  4. Serve immediately or refrigerate for a few minutes to chill.

10. Sweet Potato and Black Bean Salad

This hearty and nutritious salad brings together sweet potatoes, black beans, and a flavorful lime dressing.

Ingredients:

  • 1 medium sweet potato, roasted and diced
  • 1 cup black beans, drained and rinsed
  • 1/4 red onion, diced
  • 1/4 cup cilantro, chopped
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tbsp lime juice
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Roast the sweet potato cubes in a 400°F (200°C) oven for 25-30 minutes, until tender.
  2. In a large bowl, combine the roasted sweet potato, black beans, red onion, and cilantro.
  3. In a small bowl, whisk together olive oil, lime juice, salt, and pepper.
  4. Drizzle the dressing over the salad and toss to combine.

11. Zucchini Noodle Salad with Pesto

A light and fresh alternative to pasta, zucchini noodles are the star of this healthy salad, complemented by a fragrant pesto dressing.

Ingredients:

  • 2 zucchinis, spiralized into noodles
  • 1/4 cup homemade or store-bought pesto
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1/4 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1/4 cup pine nuts, toasted

Instructions:

  1. Spiralize the zucchinis into noodles and place them in a bowl.
  2. Add the pesto, olive oil, cherry tomatoes, and pine nuts.
  3. Toss gently to combine.
  4. Serve chilled or at room temperature.

12. Apple and Pecan Salad

This fall-inspired apple and pecan salad is sweet, crunchy, and topped with a tangy vinaigrette that ties all the ingredients together.

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups mixed greens (spinach, arugula, etc.)
  • 1 apple, thinly sliced
  • 1/4 cup pecans, toasted
  • 1/4 cup goat cheese, crumbled
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tbsp apple cider vinegar
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a large bowl, toss together mixed greens, apple slices, pecans, and goat cheese.
  2. In a small bowl, whisk together olive oil, apple cider vinegar, salt, and pepper.
  3. Drizzle the dressing over the salad and toss gently before serving.

13. Cabbage and Carrot Slaw

This crunchy and tangy cabbage and carrot slaw is perfect as a side dish or light lunch, especially during warm weather.

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups shredded cabbage
  • 1 cup shredded carrots
  • 2 tbsp apple cider vinegar
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, combine the shredded cabbage and carrots.
  2. Drizzle with apple cider vinegar and olive oil.
  3. Season with salt and pepper and toss to combine.
  4. Let it sit for 10-15 minutes for the flavors to meld before serving.

14. Chickpea and Spinach Salad

This protein-packed salad features hearty chickpeas paired with fresh spinach, making it a perfect meal for two.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup cooked chickpeas
  • 2 cups fresh spinach
  • 1/4 cup red onion, sliced
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, combine chickpeas, spinach, and red onion.
  2. Drizzle with olive oil and lemon juice.
  3. Season with salt and pepper and toss gently to combine.
  4. Serve immediately or refrigerate for a chilled option.

15. Roasted Vegetable Salad with Tahini Dressing

This warm roasted vegetable salad is perfect for any season and comes with a creamy tahini dressing that adds richness to the dish.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup mixed roasted vegetables (carrots, bell peppers, zucchini, etc.)
  • 2 cups arugula
  • 1/4 cup tahini
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Roast the mixed vegetables in a 400°F (200°C) oven for 20-25 minutes.
  2. In a bowl, combine the roasted vegetables with arugula.
  3. In a separate bowl, whisk together tahini, lemon juice, salt, and pepper.
  4. Drizzle the dressing over the salad and toss gently before serving.

These 15 light and healthy salad recipes are perfect for cooking for two, offering a variety of flavors, textures, and nutrients. Whether you’re craving something fresh and light or a more hearty meal, these salads are sure to satisfy. Experiment with these ingredients and dressings to suit your taste preferences.
