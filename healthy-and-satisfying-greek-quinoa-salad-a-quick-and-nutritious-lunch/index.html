Healthy and Satisfying Greek Quinoa Salad: A Quick and Nutritious Lunch


Are you looking for a delicious, healthy, and satisfying lunch option? Look no further than this Greek Quinoa Salad! Packed with protein, fiber, and an array of vibrant flavors, this salad is not only nutritious but also easy to prepare. Whether you’re a busy professional, a student on the go, or a parent looking for a wholesome meal for the family, this recipe is sure to become a staple in your weekly menu.

Ingredients

For the Salad:

  • 1 cup quinoa, rinsed
  • 2 cups water or vegetable broth
  • 1 cucumber, diced
  • 1 pint cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1/2 red onion, finely chopped
  • 1/2 cup Kalamata olives, pitted and sliced
  • 1/2 cup crumbled feta cheese
  • 1/4 cup fresh parsley, chopped

For the Dressing:

  • 1/4 cup extra virgin olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons red wine vinegar
  • 1 garlic clove, minced
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. In a medium saucepan, combine the quinoa and water or vegetable broth. Bring to a boil, then reduce the heat to low, cover, and simmer for 15-20 minutes, or until the quinoa is tender and the liquid is absorbed. Once cooked, fluff the quinoa with a fork and allow it to cool to room temperature.

  2. In a large mixing bowl, combine the cooled quinoa, cucumber, cherry tomatoes, red onion, Kalamata olives, feta cheese, and fresh parsley.

  3. In a small bowl, whisk together the extra virgin olive oil, red wine vinegar, minced garlic, dried oregano, salt, and pepper to create the dressing.

  4. Pour the dressing over the quinoa salad and gently toss to ensure everything is evenly coated.

  5. Once the salad is dressed, cover the bowl and refrigerate for at least 30 minutes to allow the flavors to meld together.

  6. Before serving, give the salad a final toss and adjust the seasoning if necessary. You can garnish with an extra sprinkle of fresh parsley and feta cheese for an added touch.

Nutritional Benefits

This Greek Quinoa Salad is not only a feast for the taste buds but also a nutritional powerhouse. Quinoa is a complete protein, providing all nine essential amino acids, making it an excellent choice for vegetarians and vegans. Additionally, it is high in fiber, iron, and magnesium, contributing to overall heart health and digestion. The assortment of fresh vegetables in this salad adds vitamins, minerals, and antioxidants, while the healthy fats from the olive oil and feta cheese provide satiety and support brain function.

Serving Suggestions

This salad is a versatile and satisfying dish that can be enjoyed on its own as a light lunch or paired with grilled chicken, shrimp, or tofu for a heartier meal. It also makes for a delightful side dish at barbecues, picnics, or potlucks. The vibrant colors and robust flavors of the Greek Quinoa Salad are sure to impress your guests and leave them asking for the recipe.

Conclusion

In conclusion, this Healthy and Satisfying Greek Quinoa Salad is the epitome of a quick and nutritious lunch option. With its simple preparation, vibrant flavors, and abundant health benefits, it’s a recipe that checks all the boxes. Whether you’re looking to eat more plant-based meals, incorporate whole grains into your diet, or simply enjoy a delicious and satisfying lunch, this salad has something for everyone. So, why not give it a try and elevate your lunch game with this delightful Greek Quinoa Salad? Your taste buds and your body will thank you!
