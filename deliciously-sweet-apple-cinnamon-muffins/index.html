Deliciously Sweet Apple Cinnamon Muffins


Apple cinnamon muffins are a classic combination that never goes out of style. The sweetness of the apples pairs perfectly with the warmth of the cinnamon, creating a deliciously sweet and savory treat that’s perfect for any time of day. In this recipe, we’ll show you how to make the most of this incredible combination, resulting in a batch of muffins that are sure to become a family favorite.

Ingredients

  • 2 1/4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, melted
  • 1 cup milk
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 2 cups diced apples (about 2-3 apples)
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • Confectioners’ sugar, for dusting (optional)

Instructions

To start, preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C). Line a 12-cup muffin tin with paper liners to make cleanup a breeze.

In a medium-sized bowl, whisk together the flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt. Set aside.

In a large bowl, whisk together the melted butter, milk, eggs, and vanilla extract. Add the dry ingredients to the wet ingredients and stir until just combined. Do not overmix.

Add the diced apples and ground cinnamon to the batter and stir until they’re evenly distributed.

Divide the batter evenly among the muffin cups. You should have about 12 muffins.

Bake the muffins for 20-25 minutes, or until they’re golden brown and a toothpick inserted into the center of a muffin comes out clean.

Remove the muffins from the oven and let them cool in the tin for 5 minutes. Then, transfer them to a wire rack to cool completely.

Tips and Variations

If you want to take your apple cinnamon muffins to the next level, try adding a streusel topping. Simply mix together 1/4 cup all-purpose flour, 1/4 cup granulated sugar, and 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon. Cut in 1/4 cup cold unsalted butter until the mixture resembles coarse crumbs. Sprinkle the topping over the muffins before baking.

You can also customize the recipe by using different types of apples. Granny Smith apples will give your muffins a tart flavor, while Gala apples will add a touch of sweetness. Experiment with different combinations to find your favorite.

Storage and Serving

Store your apple cinnamon muffins in an airtight container at room temperature for up to 3 days. You can also freeze them for up to 2 months. Simply thaw the muffins at room temperature or reheat them in the microwave for a few seconds.

These muffins are perfect for breakfast on-the-go, a snack at work, or even as a dessert. You can also dress them up with a dollop of whipped cream or a sprinkle of confectioners’ sugar for a special occasion.

Conclusion

Apple cinnamon muffins are a delicious and easy-to-make treat that’s perfect for any time of day. With their sweet and savory flavors, they’re sure to become a family favorite. Whether you’re looking for a quick breakfast option or a tasty snack, these muffins are sure to hit the spot. So go ahead, give them a try, and enjoy the delicious combination of apples and cinnamon!
