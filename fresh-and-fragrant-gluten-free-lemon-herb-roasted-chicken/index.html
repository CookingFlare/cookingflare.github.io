Fresh and Fragrant: Gluten-Free Lemon Herb Roasted Chicken


There’s nothing quite like the aroma of lemon and herbs wafting through your kitchen as a delicious chicken roasts in the oven. Today, I want to share with you a recipe for a gluten-free lemon herb roasted chicken that is bursting with fresh flavors and is sure to impress your family and friends.

Ingredients

  • 1 whole chicken (approximately 4 pounds)
  • 2 lemons
  • 4 garlic cloves
  • 1 tablespoon fresh rosemary leaves
  • 1 tablespoon fresh thyme leaves
  • 1 tablespoon fresh parsley leaves
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 425°F (220°C) and line a roasting pan with parchment paper.

  2. Start by preparing the lemon herb rub. Zest both lemons and set the zest aside. Cut one lemon into slices and set them aside as well. Juice the second lemon and set the juice aside.

  3. In a mortar and pestle, crush the garlic cloves along with the rosemary, thyme, and parsley leaves until you have a fragrant and well-combined mixture. If you don’t have a mortar and pestle, you can use a food processor or finely chop the herbs and garlic.

  4. In a small bowl, combine the lemon zest, herb mixture, olive oil, and lemon juice. Mix well until you have a thick paste.

  5. Now it’s time to prepare the chicken. Remove any giblets from the cavity and pat it dry with paper towels. Season the chicken generously with salt and pepper, both inside and out.

  6. Carefully loosen the skin of the chicken by gently sliding your fingers beneath it, being careful not to tear it. Take half of the lemon herb paste and spread it evenly under the skin, covering as much of the chicken as possible. This will help infuse the meat with flavor.

  7. Rub the remaining paste all over the outside of the chicken, making sure to coat it evenly.

  8. Place the chicken in the prepared roasting pan and tuck the lemon slices inside the cavity.

  9. Roast the chicken in the preheated oven for about 1 hour and 30 minutes, or until the internal temperature reaches 165°F (74°C). To ensure accuracy, use a meat thermometer inserted into the thickest part of the thigh without touching the bone.

  10. Once the chicken is cooked, remove it from the oven and let it rest for about 10 minutes before carving. This allows the juices to redistribute, resulting in a moist and tender chicken.

  11. Serve the gluten-free lemon herb roasted chicken with your favorite side dishes, such as roasted vegetables or a fresh salad.

Enjoy the Fresh and Fragrant Delight!

This gluten-free lemon herb roasted chicken is a true delight for the senses. The combination of zesty lemon, aromatic herbs, and tender chicken creates a dish that is both light and flavorful. The herbs infuse the meat with their fragrant aromas, while the lemon adds a refreshing tang.

The best part is that this recipe is gluten-free, making it suitable for those with dietary restrictions. It’s a versatile dish that can be enjoyed by everyone, whether they follow a gluten-free lifestyle or not.

So why not give this recipe a try? The next time you’re in the mood for a delicious and aromatic roast chicken, reach for this gluten-free lemon herb roasted chicken recipe. Your taste buds will thank you!

Note: Remember to always double-check the ingredients you use to ensure they are gluten-free, as some brands may vary.
