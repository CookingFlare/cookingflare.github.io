Vegan Brunch Waffles for a Delicious Weekend Morning


There’s something special about a leisurely weekend morning, and nothing quite sets the tone like a delicious brunch. If you’re in the mood for a delightful vegan option, look no further than these scrumptious Vegan Brunch Waffles. They’re fluffy, flavorful, and perfect for a relaxed weekend morning at home. Let’s dive into the recipe and get ready to elevate your next brunch experience!

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 cup cornstarch
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • 1 tablespoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 3/4 cups almond milk (or any plant-based milk of your choice)
  • 1/2 cup unsweetened applesauce
  • 1/4 cup coconut oil, melted
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • Vegan butter or coconut oil for greasing the waffle iron

Instructions

  1. Start by preheating your waffle iron according to the manufacturer’s instructions.
  2. In a large mixing bowl, whisk together the all-purpose flour, cornstarch, sugar, baking powder, and salt.
  3. In a separate bowl, combine the almond milk, applesauce, melted coconut oil, and vanilla extract. Whisk until well combined.
  4. Pour the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients and stir until just combined. Be careful not to overmix; a few lumps are okay.
  5. Once the waffle iron is heated, lightly grease it with vegan butter or coconut oil.
  6. Pour the appropriate amount of batter onto the waffle iron, close the lid, and cook according to the manufacturer’s instructions, or until the waffles are golden brown and crisp.
  7. Carefully remove the waffles from the iron and place them on a wire rack to maintain crispness while you finish cooking the remaining batter.
  8. Serve the waffles warm with your favorite toppings, such as fresh fruit, maple syrup, coconut whipped cream, or a sprinkle of powdered sugar.

Tips for Perfect Vegan Brunch Waffles

  • Fluffy Texture: The combination of all-purpose flour and cornstarch helps achieve a light and fluffy texture in these waffles.
  • Customization: Feel free to add a dash of cinnamon, a sprinkle of nutmeg, or a handful of dairy-free chocolate chips to the batter for a flavor twist.
  • Make-Ahead: If you’re planning to serve a crowd, you can keep the cooked waffles warm in a 200°F oven while you finish cooking the rest.
  • Freezing: These waffles freeze beautifully! Once cooled, store them in a freezer-safe bag or container, and reheat in a toaster for a quick weekday breakfast.

The Perfect Brunch Atmosphere

Imagine the inviting aroma of freshly cooked waffles filling your kitchen, the sound of sizzling batter, and the anticipation of a delightful brunch spread. Whether you’re hosting friends or simply treating yourself, these Vegan Brunch Waffles are sure to elevate your weekend morning.

So, go ahead and plan your next leisurely brunch at home, and let these Vegan Brunch Waffles take center stage. With their delightful flavor and fluffy texture, they’re bound to become a beloved addition to your weekend routine. Enjoy and savor every bite of this plant-based brunch delight!
