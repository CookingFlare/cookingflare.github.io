10 Easy Marinade Recipes for Grilled Shrimp


Grilled shrimp is a delicious and versatile dish that can be enjoyed on its own, in salads, or as part of a main course. One of the best ways to elevate the flavor of grilled shrimp is by marinating it with a flavorful blend of ingredients. Whether you prefer a zesty citrus flavor, a spicy kick, or a savory herb-infused taste, there’s a marinade for everyone. Here are 10 easy and mouthwatering marinade recipes to take your grilled shrimp to the next level.

1. Lemon Garlic Marinade

  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons fresh lemon juice
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon lemon zest
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • Salt and black pepper to taste

Combine all the ingredients in a bowl and mix well. Marinate the shrimp for 30 minutes before grilling.

2. Honey Sriracha Marinade

  • 1/4 cup honey
  • 2 tablespoons Sriracha sauce
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon rice vinegar
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon grated ginger

Whisk together all the ingredients until well combined. Marinate the shrimp for 20 minutes, then grill to perfection.

3. Coconut Lime Marinade

  • 1/2 cup coconut milk
  • 2 tablespoons fresh lime juice
  • 1 teaspoon lime zest
  • 1 tablespoon brown sugar
  • 1 teaspoon curry powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric

Mix all the ingredients in a bowl until the sugar is dissolved. Marinate the shrimp for 15 minutes and grill for a taste of the tropics.

4. Cajun Spice Marinade

  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon Cajun seasoning
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon onion powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon cayenne pepper

Rub the mixture onto the shrimp and let it sit for 10 minutes before grilling for a spicy and flavorful kick.

5. Asian Ginger Soy Marinade

  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons rice vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon honey
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon grated ginger
  • 1 teaspoon sesame oil

Whisk together the ingredients and marinate the shrimp for 30 minutes, then grill for a savory and aromatic flavor.

6. Orange Chipotle Marinade

  • 1/4 cup orange juice
  • 2 chipotle peppers in adobo sauce, minced
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1/2 teaspoon smoked paprika
  • Salt to taste

Combine all the ingredients in a blender and blend until smooth. Marinate the shrimp for 20 minutes before grilling for a smoky and tangy taste.

7. Teriyaki Pineapple Marinade

  • 1/4 cup teriyaki sauce
  • 2 tablespoons pineapple juice
  • 1 tablespoon brown sugar
  • 1 teaspoon grated ginger
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced

Mix all the ingredients in a bowl and marinate the shrimp for 15 minutes before grilling for a sweet and savory flavor.

8. Herb and Garlic Marinade

  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons fresh lemon juice
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon chopped fresh parsley
  • 1 tablespoon chopped fresh basil
  • Salt and black pepper to taste

Combine the ingredients in a bowl and marinate the shrimp for 30 minutes before grilling for a classic and aromatic taste.

9. Spicy Mango Marinade

  • 1/2 cup pureed mango
  • 2 tablespoons lime juice
  • 1 tablespoon honey
  • 1 teaspoon chili powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon cumin
  • 1/4 teaspoon cayenne pepper

Whisk together the ingredients until smooth. Marinate the shrimp for 20 minutes and grill for a tropical and spicy flavor.

10. Herb Butter Marinade

  • 1/2 cup melted butter
  • 2 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley
  • 2 tablespoons chopped fresh chives
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon lemon juice
  • Salt and black pepper to taste

Mix all the ingredients in a bowl and brush the shrimp with the herb butter while grilling for a rich and indulgent taste.

No matter which marinade you choose, these 10 recipes are sure to add incredible flavor to your grilled shrimp. Experiment with different combinations and find your favorite, or try a new one each time you fire up the grill. Enjoy the tantalizing tastes of these easy marinade recipes and impress your family and friends with your culinary skills!
