Greek-style Baked Stuffed Tomatoes: A Burst of Mediterranean Flavors


If you’re a fan of Mediterranean cuisine, then you’re in for a treat with this Greek-style Baked Stuffed Tomatoes recipe. Bursting with vibrant flavors and wholesome ingredients, this dish is a delightful fusion of fresh produce, aromatic herbs, and savory goodness. Whether you’re a seasoned home cook or just starting your culinary journey, this recipe is sure to impress your taste buds and leave you craving for more.

Ingredients

For the Stuffed Tomatoes:

  • 6 large ripe tomatoes
  • 1 cup cooked rice
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1/2 cup crumbled feta cheese
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh mint
  • 1/4 cup pine nuts
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

For the Tomato Sauce:

  • 1 can (14 oz) diced tomatoes
  • 2 tablespoons tomato paste
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh basil
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Preheat the Oven: Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).

  2. Prepare the Tomatoes: Cut off the tops of the tomatoes and carefully scoop out the seeds and pulp, leaving a sturdy shell. Sprinkle the insides of the tomatoes with a pinch of salt and place them upside down on a paper towel to drain excess moisture.

  3. Prepare the Filling: In a large bowl, combine the cooked rice, chopped onion, minced garlic, crumbled feta cheese, chopped parsley, chopped mint, pine nuts, and olive oil. Season with salt and pepper, and mix until well combined.

  4. Stuff the Tomatoes: Gently fill the hollowed-out tomatoes with the rice and feta mixture, pressing it down lightly to ensure they are well-packed.

  5. Prepare the Tomato Sauce: In a separate bowl, mix together the diced tomatoes, tomato paste, chopped onion, minced garlic, chopped basil, chopped oregano, salt, and pepper.

  6. Bake the Stuffed Tomatoes: Place the stuffed tomatoes in a baking dish and spoon the tomato sauce over and around the tomatoes. Cover the dish with aluminum foil and bake in the preheated oven for 30 minutes. Then, remove the foil and bake for an additional 15 minutes, or until the tomatoes are tender and the tops are slightly golden.

  7. Serve and Enjoy: Once out of the oven, let the stuffed tomatoes cool for a few minutes before serving. Garnish with additional fresh herbs if desired, and savor the Mediterranean flavors that await you.

Why You’ll Love This Recipe

1. Fresh and Wholesome Ingredients

This Greek-style Baked Stuffed Tomatoes recipe celebrates the goodness of fresh produce, from ripe tomatoes to fragrant herbs and flavorful feta cheese. Each bite is a celebration of Mediterranean flavors that will transport you to the sunny shores of Greece.

2. Customizable and Versatile

Feel free to customize the filling to your liking. Add a handful of olives for a briny kick, or incorporate cooked ground lamb for a heartier option. The possibilities are endless, allowing you to tailor this recipe to your preferences.

3. Perfect for Gatherings

Whether you’re hosting a family dinner or a casual get-together with friends, these stuffed tomatoes make for an impressive and satisfying dish. The vibrant colors and savory aroma will captivate your guests, setting the stage for a memorable dining experience.

4. Nutrient-Rich and Flavorful

Not only is this recipe a feast for the senses, but it also packs a nutritional punch. With a harmonious blend of vegetables, whole grains, and herbs, it’s a wholesome dish that nourishes both body and soul.

5. A Taste of the Mediterranean

Indulge in the flavors of the Mediterranean with this Greek-inspired recipe. From the tang of feta cheese to the earthy richness of pine nuts, each component harmonizes to create a culinary masterpiece that embodies the essence of Greek cooking.

In Conclusion

Greek-style Baked Stuffed Tomatoes are a testament to the art of Mediterranean cuisine, offering a symphony of flavors that captivate the palate. Whether enjoyed as a standalone dish or paired with other Greek delicacies, this recipe is a culinary journey worth savoring. Embrace the spirit of Greece in your kitchen and treat yourself to a taste of the Mediterranean with every delectable bite.
