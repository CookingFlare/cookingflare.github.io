Allergy-Friendly Slow Cooker Recipes: Set It and Forget It


Are you tired of spending hours in the kitchen, trying to prepare meals that cater to everyone’s dietary restrictions? Look no further! In this blog post, we will share some delicious allergy-friendly slow cooker recipes that are not only easy to make but also packed with flavor. So, dust off your slow cooker and get ready to set it and forget it!

1. Slow Cooker Chicken Noodle Soup

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 4 cups low-sodium chicken broth
  • 2 carrots, peeled and sliced
  • 2 celery stalks, sliced
  • 1/2 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • 1 bay leaf
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • 8 ounces gluten-free noodles

Instructions:

  1. Place the chicken breasts in the slow cooker.
  2. Add the chicken broth, carrots, celery, onion, garlic, thyme, bay leaf, salt, and pepper.
  3. Cook on low for 6-8 hours or on high for 3-4 hours, until the chicken is cooked through and tender.
  4. Remove the chicken from the slow cooker and shred it using two forks. Return the shredded chicken to the slow cooker.
  5. Add the gluten-free noodles and cook for an additional 30 minutes, or until the noodles are tender.
  6. Serve hot and enjoy!

2. Slow Cooker Beef Stew

Ingredients:

  • 1.5 pounds beef stew meat, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 2 potatoes, peeled and diced
  • 2 carrots, peeled and sliced
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup frozen peas
  • 4 cups low-sodium beef broth
  • 2 tablespoons gluten-free Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • Salt and pepper, to taste

Instructions:

  1. Place the beef stew meat, potatoes, carrots, onion, garlic, peas, beef broth, Worcestershire sauce, thyme, salt, and pepper in the slow cooker.
  2. Cook on low for 8-10 hours or on high for 4-6 hours, until the beef is tender.
  3. Serve hot and enjoy!

3. Slow Cooker Vegan Chili

Ingredients:

  • 1 can kidney beans, drained and rinsed
  • 1 can black beans, drained and rinsed
  • 1 can diced tomatoes
  • 1 cup corn kernels
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 1 red bell pepper, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • Salt and pepper, to taste

Instructions:

  1. Place the kidney beans, black beans, diced tomatoes, corn kernels, onion, red bell pepper, garlic, chili powder, cumin, salt, and pepper in the slow cooker.
  2. Cook on low for 6-8 hours or on high for 3-4 hours.
  3. Serve hot and enjoy!

4. Slow Cooker Allergy-Friendly Apple Crisp

Ingredients:

  • 4 cups peeled, cored, and sliced apples
  • 1 cup gluten-free oats
  • 1/2 cup almond flour
  • 1/4 cup maple syrup
  • 2 tablespoons coconut oil, melted
  • 1 teaspoon cinnamon
  • Pinch of salt

Instructions:

  1. Place the sliced apples in the slow cooker.
  2. In a separate bowl, combine the oats, almond flour, maple syrup, melted coconut oil, cinnamon, and salt.
  3. Sprinkle the oat mixture over the apples in the slow cooker.
  4. Cook on low for 2-3 hours or on high for 1-2 hours, until the apples are tender and the topping is golden brown.
  5. Serve warm with a scoop of dairy-free ice cream, if desired.

Conclusion

With these allergy-friendly slow cooker recipes, you can effortlessly prepare delicious meals that everyone can enjoy, regardless of their dietary restrictions. The best part? You can simply set it and forget it, allowing your slow cooker to work its magic while you go about your day. So, give these recipes a try and savor the mouthwatering flavors that will leave you wanting more. Happy cooking!
