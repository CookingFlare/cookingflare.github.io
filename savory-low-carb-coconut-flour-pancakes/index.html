Savory Low Carb Coconut Flour Pancakes


Are you looking for a delicious and healthy breakfast option? Look no further than these savory low carb coconut flour pancakes! Packed with flavor and low in carbohydrates, these pancakes are the perfect way to start your day on a tasty note. Plus, they are gluten-free and keto-friendly, so they can easily fit into your dietary preferences.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup coconut flour
  • 4 large eggs
  • 1/2 cup unsweetened almond milk
  • 2 tablespoons melted butter
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1/4 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon onion powder
  • Optional toppings: avocado, sliced cherry tomatoes, fresh herbs

Instructions

  1. In a large bowl, whisk together the coconut flour, baking powder, salt, black pepper, garlic powder, and onion powder. Make sure all the dry ingredients are well combined.

  2. In a separate bowl, beat the eggs until well mixed. Add in the melted butter and almond milk, and whisk until everything is thoroughly combined.

  3. Pour the wet ingredients into the bowl with the dry ingredients. Stir until the batter is smooth and no lumps remain. Let the batter sit for a few minutes to allow the coconut flour to absorb the liquid.

  4. Heat a non-stick skillet or griddle over medium heat. Lightly grease the surface with cooking spray or a small amount of butter.

  5. Spoon about 1/4 cup of batter onto the skillet for each pancake. Use the back of the spoon to spread the batter into a round shape. Cook for 2-3 minutes, or until bubbles start to form on the surface of the pancake.

  6. Flip the pancake and cook for an additional 1-2 minutes, or until golden brown. Repeat with the remaining batter.

  7. Serve the pancakes warm with your favorite toppings. Sliced avocado, cherry tomatoes, and fresh herbs make great additions to these savory pancakes. Get creative and experiment with different toppings to suit your taste.

Why Choose Savory Low Carb Coconut Flour Pancakes?

These savory low carb coconut flour pancakes offer a fantastic alternative to traditional carb-heavy pancakes. Coconut flour is a great gluten-free option that adds a subtle coconut flavor to your pancakes. It’s also high in fiber, which helps keep you feeling full and satisfied.

By using coconut flour instead of regular flour, you can significantly reduce the carbohydrate content of your pancakes. This makes them a suitable choice for those following a low carb or keto diet. Additionally, these pancakes are packed with protein from the eggs, making them a nutritious and filling breakfast option.

The savory flavor profile of these pancakes sets them apart from the traditional sweet pancakes. The addition of garlic powder, onion powder, and black pepper adds a delicious savory twist that pairs perfectly with a variety of toppings. You can get creative with your toppings and explore different combinations to make these pancakes truly your own.

So, the next time you’re craving pancakes for breakfast, give these savory low carb coconut flour pancakes a try. They are easy to make, packed with flavor, and a healthier alternative to traditional pancakes. Enjoy a delicious and guilt-free breakfast that will keep you energized and satisfied throughout the day.
