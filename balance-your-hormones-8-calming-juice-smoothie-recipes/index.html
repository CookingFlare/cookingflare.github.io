Balance Your Hormones: 8 Calming Juice & Smoothie Recipes


Are you struggling with hormonal imbalances? Stress, lack of sleep, and a poor diet can often contribute to this issue. Lucky for you, there’s an easier, tastier solution that can help you achieve hormonal harmony and calmness: juicing and blending. In this blog post, we’ve gathered eight reviving juice and smoothie recipes to balance your hormones, improve overall health, and reclaim your energy levels.

1. Blushing Ginger Beet Smoothie

Beets are rich in folate and betaine, which help regulate the production of hormones while ginger promotes digestive health and reduces inflammation.

  • 1 beetroot, peeled and chopped
  • 1 carrot, peeled and chopped
  • 1-inch piece of fresh ginger, peeled
  • 2 cups of unsweetened almond milk
  • A handful of spinach leaves
  • A drizzle of honey (optional)

Place all ingredients in the blender and mix until smooth. Serve, and enjoy!

2. Citrus Cooling Calm Juice

A powerfully refreshing drink combining antioxidant-rich oranges, tangy grapefruit, and anti-inflammatory lemon to help reduce stress and keep hormones in check.

  • 1 orange, peeled
  • 1/2 grapefruit, peeled
  • 1 lemon, peeled
  • 1 cup of water

Toss all ingredients into a juicer, mix well, and sip away the stress!

3. Tropical Green Smoothie

This smoothie is the perfect blend of tropical fruit and leafy greens to balance hormones and rejuvenate your spirit.

  • 1 cup of chopped pineapple
  • 1/2 avocado
  • 1 banana
  • 2 cups of spinach
  • 1 cup of coconut water

Combine all ingredients in a blender and blend until smooth. Savor the flavors of the tropics!

4. Turmeric Tonic Smoothie

Turmeric is well-known for its anti-inflammatory properties and ability to regulate insulin production. Combine it with nuts for a healthy dose of fats that keep blood sugar levels steady.

  • 1/2 cup of unsweetened almond milk
  • 1 tablespoon of almond butter
  • 1 teaspoon of turmeric
  • 1 banana
  • 1 cup of raw kale
  • A splash of vanilla extract

Incorporate all ingredients in your blender and mix until creamy. Bon appétit!

5. Berry-Protein Blast Smoothie

Berries are loaded with antioxidants to combat inflammation and balance hormones. Along with a protein powder of your choice, this smoothie is perhaps the best goodness for a hormonal detox.

  • 1 cup of mixed berries (strawberries, raspberries, and blueberries)
  • 1 scoop of your favorite protein powder
  • 1 cup of unsweetened almond milk
  • A handful of spinach
  • 2 tablespoons of chia seeds

Toss everything in your blender and blend until smooth. Sip your way to a balanced hormonal state!

6. Lime, Lemon, and Mint Juice

The ingredients in this invigorating drink help to cleanse the liver and balance estrogen levels, providing support for hormonal health.

  • Juice of 1 lime
  • Juice of 1 lemon
  • A bunch of fresh mint leaves
  • 1 cup of water

Mix ingredients together in a juicer, and sip your way to hormonal harmony!

7. Sweet Greens Power Smoothie

Cravings can worsen hormonal imbalances, so this smoothie will help to regain control over them by incorporating nutrient-dense leafy greens and protein-packed hemp seeds.

  • 2 cups of spinach
  • 1/2 cup of chopped kale
  • 1 banana
  • 1/2 cup of hemp seeds
  • 1 cup of unsweetened almond milk
  • 1 tablespoon of almond butter

Blitz everything together in your blender, and enjoy your guilt-free smoothie creation!

8. Cinnamon-Chia Seed Pudding Smoothie

Support your adrenal glands with this pudding-bowl like smoothie, made extra comforting with the warming aroma of cinnamon and magnesium-laden chia seeds.

  • 1 tablespoon of chia seeds
  • 1 cup of unsweetened almond milk
  • 1 teaspoon of cinnamon
  • 1 banana
  • A splash of vanilla extract

Let the chia seeds soak in the almond milk for at least 15 minutes, allowing them to swell. Once they are ready, blend it all up and fill your senses with delightful flavors!

Embracing a diet rich in juicing and smoothies can be a powerful way to promote hormonal balance and overall well-being. Experiment with these recipes and find your perfect blend to support hormone harmony, relieve stress, and feel fabulous!
