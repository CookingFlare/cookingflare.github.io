Snickers Cheesecake Cupcakes: Nutty and Rich


If you’re a fan of sweet and savory treats that tantalize your taste buds, then you’ll absolutely love these Snickers Cheesecake Cupcakes! Enveloped in a delectable chocolate cupcake, this dessert boasts a luscious cream cheese filling, studded with chunks of the irresistible Snickers bar, and topped off with a generous dollop of whipped cream and a crunchy nuts garnish.

What I love about this recipe is the delightful interplay of flavors- saltiness from the Snickers, creaminess from the cheesecake, and a satisfying crunch from the nuts. Plus, this dessert is a breeze to make, perfect for those who enjoy spending less time in the kitchen but want a showstopper of a dessert.

So don’t throw away those leftover Snickers bars, and instead, use them to create these irresistible Snickers Cheesecake Cupcakes. Whip up a batch for your next gathering, and watch your guests swoon over this scrumptious combination of flavors.

Ingredients

For the Chocolate Cupcakes:

  • 1 3/4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 3/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 3/4 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 2 tsp baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, at room temperature
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1/4 cup hot water

For the Cream Cheese Filling:

  • 1 cup cream cheese, at room temperature
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 large egg
  • 1/4 cup vegetable oil
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1/4 cup Snickers bar, chopped

For the Whipped Cream Topping:

  • 1 cup heavy whipping cream
  • 2 Tbsp powdered sugar
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • A pinch of salt
  • Additional chopped Snickers for garnish

Instructions

  1. Make the Chocolate Cupcakes: Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C). Line a 12-cup muffin tin with cupcake liners and set them aside.

  2. In a large mixing bowl, whisk together the flour, granulated sugar, cocoa powder, baking powder, and salt. Add the butter, eggs, milk, vanilla, and hot water. Beat until smooth, making sure there are no lumps.

  3. Divide the batter evenly among the prepared cupcake liners. Fill each about 2/3 full. Bake for 15-18 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.

  4. Remove the cupcakes from the oven and place them on a wire rack to cool completely.

  5. Make the Cream Cheese Filling: While the cupcakes are cooling, beat the cream cheese, granulated sugar, and egg in a mixing bowl until smooth. Mix in the vegetable oil and vanilla until fully incorporated. Gently fold in the chopped Snickers pieces to create a marbled effect.

  6. Assemble the Cupcakes: Once the cupcakes have cooled completely, scoop the cream cheese mixture on top of each cupcake. Use a knife or an offset spatula to spread the filling evenly, making a slight well in the center for the whipped cream.

  7. Make the Whipped Cream Topping: In a separate mixing bowl, whip the heavy cream, powdered sugar, vanilla, and salt until stiff peaks form. Pipe a swirl of whipped cream on top of each cupcake filling, and sprinkle with additional chopped Snickers. If desired, garnish with a dash of chopped nuts.

  8. Chill the cupcakes in the refrigerator for at least 30 minutes, or until ready to serve.

Conclusion
