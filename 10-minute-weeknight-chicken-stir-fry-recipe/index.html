10-Minute Weeknight Chicken Stir-Fry Recipe


Are you in need of a quick and delicious dinner option for those busy weeknights? Look no further! This 10-minute chicken stir-fry recipe is the perfect solution. Packed with flavor, nutrients, and ready in no time, it’s a go-to meal that will satisfy your hunger without keeping you tied to the kitchen for hours.

Ingredients

  • 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts, thinly sliced
  • 2 cups mixed vegetables (bell peppers, broccoli, snap peas, carrots)
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon fresh ginger, grated
  • 3 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons oyster sauce
  • 1 tablespoon sesame oil
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Cooked rice or noodles for serving

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Ingredients: Start by slicing the chicken breasts thinly and chopping the mixed vegetables into bite-sized pieces. Mince the garlic and grate the fresh ginger. Having all the ingredients prepped and ready will make the cooking process smooth and efficient.

  2. Heat the Pan: Place a large skillet or wok over medium-high heat. Add the vegetable oil and allow it to heat for a minute.

  3. Cook the Chicken: Once the oil is hot, add the sliced chicken to the pan. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Cook for 3-4 minutes, stirring occasionally, until the chicken is no longer pink and has started to brown.

  4. Add the Aromatics: Push the cooked chicken to one side of the pan, and add the minced garlic and grated ginger to the other side. Cook for 30 seconds, stirring constantly, until fragrant.

  5. Incorporate the Vegetables: Toss in the mixed vegetables and stir to combine with the chicken, garlic, and ginger. Cook for 2-3 minutes, or until the vegetables are tender-crisp.

  6. Sauce it Up: Drizzle the soy sauce, oyster sauce, and sesame oil over the chicken and vegetables. Stir everything together, ensuring the sauce coats all the ingredients evenly. Cook for an additional 1-2 minutes, allowing the flavors to meld.

  7. Serve and Enjoy: Remove the pan from the heat and serve the chicken stir-fry immediately over a bed of cooked rice or noodles. Garnish with sliced green onions or sesame seeds if desired.

Tips for Success

  • Prep Ahead: To make this recipe even quicker, consider prepping the ingredients the night before. This way, when it’s time to cook, everything is ready to go.
  • Customize Your Veggies: Feel free to use any combination of vegetables you prefer or have on hand. This recipe is versatile and can accommodate your personal favorites.
  • Double the Batch: If you’re cooking for a larger group or want leftovers for the next day, simply double the ingredients. The cooking process remains the same, and you’ll have a meal ready in minutes.

Final Thoughts

This 10-minute weeknight chicken stir-fry is a game-changer for those hectic days when time is of the essence. With minimal prep and cook time, you can enjoy a wholesome and flavorful meal that will please everyone at the table. The best part? The possibilities for customization are endless, allowing you to tailor this recipe to your taste and dietary preferences. So, the next time you find yourself short on time but craving a satisfying dinner, remember this quick and easy stir-fry recipe – your taste buds will thank you!
