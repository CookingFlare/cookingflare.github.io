Sweet Potato and Black Bean Chili: A Hearty Fall Meal


Fall is the perfect season for cozy meals that warm both the body and the soul. And what better way to celebrate the changing leaves and crisp air than with a bowl of hearty chili? This sweet potato and black bean chili recipe is packed with flavor, nutrients, and a touch of spice, making it the ultimate comfort food for those chilly autumn evenings.

Ingredients

  • 2 medium sweet potatoes, peeled and diced
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 red bell pepper, diced
  • 1 can (15 ounces) black beans, rinsed and drained
  • 1 can (14.5 ounces) diced tomatoes
  • 1 cup vegetable broth
  • 2 tablespoons tomato paste
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • 1/2 teaspoon smoked paprika
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Heat the olive oil in a large pot over medium heat. Add the onion and garlic, and sauté until fragrant and translucent.

  2. Add the sweet potatoes, red bell pepper, chili powder, cumin, smoked paprika, salt, and pepper to the pot. Stir well to coat the vegetables with the spices. Cook for about 5 minutes, or until the sweet potatoes start to soften.

  3. Pour in the diced tomatoes, vegetable broth, black beans, and tomato paste. Stir everything together, then bring the chili to a simmer.

  4. Reduce the heat to low, cover the pot, and let the chili simmer for 30 minutes, or until the sweet potatoes are tender.

  5. Once the sweet potatoes are cooked through, taste the chili and adjust the seasonings if needed. If you prefer a spicier chili, add some cayenne pepper or hot sauce.

  6. Serve the sweet potato and black bean chili hot, garnished with your favorite toppings. Some delicious options include avocado slices, chopped cilantro, sour cream, or shredded cheese.

Why You’ll Love This Recipe

  • A Nutrient Powerhouse: Sweet potatoes are packed with essential vitamins and minerals, making them a nutritious addition to any meal. They are an excellent source of fiber, vitamin A, and potassium. Combined with black beans, this chili becomes a powerhouse of plant-based protein and fiber.

  • Fall Flavors: The combination of sweet potatoes, warming spices, and smoky paprika creates a rich and comforting flavor profile that’s perfect for fall. Each spoonful is like a cozy hug for your taste buds.

  • Easy and Versatile: This recipe requires simple ingredients and minimal prep work. Plus, you can easily customize it to suit your taste preferences. Add extra veggies, experiment with different spices, or even top it with some crunchy tortilla chips for added texture.

  • Meal Prep Friendly: This chili reheats beautifully, making it an ideal choice for meal prepping or batch cooking. Prepare a big pot of this sweet potato and black bean chili on the weekend, and enjoy comforting meals throughout the week.

  • Vegan and Gluten-Free: This recipe is plant-based and naturally gluten-free, making it suitable for a variety of dietary preferences and restrictions.

So why not embrace the flavors of fall and whip up a batch of this sweet potato and black bean chili? It’s a hearty and satisfying meal that will warm you from the inside out. Whether you’re enjoying it on a cozy night at home or serving it to friends and family, this chili is sure to impress. Give it a try and let the flavors of the season shine in every spoonful.
