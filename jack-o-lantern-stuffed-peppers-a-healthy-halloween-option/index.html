Jack-o'-Lantern Stuffed Peppers: A Healthy Halloween Option


Halloween is just around the corner, and it’s the perfect time to get creative in the kitchen. If you’re looking for a healthy and fun Halloween dish, look no further than Jack-o’-Lantern Stuffed Peppers. These adorable pepper pumpkins are not only delicious but also a great way to incorporate more veggies into your Halloween festivities.

Ingredients:

  • 4 large orange bell peppers
  • 1 pound ground turkey or lean ground beef
  • 1 cup cooked quinoa
  • 1 cup black beans, drained and rinsed
  • 1 cup corn kernels, fresh or frozen
  • 1 small onion, diced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • 1/2 teaspoon paprika
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Olive oil for cooking

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).

  2. Cut the tops off the bell peppers and remove the seeds and membranes. Use a small knife to carve a jack-o’-lantern face on each pepper, making sure to leave enough room for the filling. Set aside.

  3. Heat a tablespoon of olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Add the diced onion and minced garlic and sauté until they become fragrant and translucent.

  4. Add the ground turkey or beef to the skillet and cook until browned. Make sure to break up any clumps as it cooks.

  5. Once the meat is cooked, add the cooked quinoa, black beans, corn kernels, chili powder, cumin, paprika, salt, and pepper. Stir everything together until well combined. Cook for an additional 2-3 minutes to allow the flavors to meld.

  6. Stuff each bell pepper with the filling mixture, making sure to pack it tightly. Place the stuffed peppers in a baking dish and cover with aluminum foil.

  7. Bake in the preheated oven for 30-35 minutes or until the peppers are tender and the filling is heated through.

  8. Remove the foil and bake for an additional 5 minutes to let the tops of the peppers brown slightly.

  9. Once done, carefully remove the stuffed peppers from the oven and let them cool for a few minutes before serving.

Serving Suggestions:

These Jack-o’-Lantern Stuffed Peppers make for a festive and healthy Halloween dinner option. Serve them alongside a side salad or some steamed vegetables for a well-rounded meal. You can also garnish them with a dollop of sour cream or some shredded cheese for added flavor.

Tips and Variations:

  • Feel free to get creative with the filling by adding other vegetables like diced zucchini or grated carrots.
  • If you’re not a fan of quinoa, you can substitute it with cooked rice or couscous.
  • For a vegetarian option, skip the meat and double up on the beans or add some chopped mushrooms for added texture.
  • To make this dish spicier, add some diced jalapeños or a sprinkle of cayenne pepper to the filling mixture.
  • If you have leftovers, these stuffed peppers can be stored in an airtight container in the refrigerator for up to 3 days. Simply reheat them in the microwave or oven before enjoying.

So there you have it! Jack-o’-Lantern Stuffed Peppers, a healthy and fun Halloween option that will impress both kids and adults alike. Get creative with your carving and enjoy a delicious meal that’s as enjoyable to make as it is to eat. Happy Halloween!
