Creamy Tomato and Basil Tortellini Soup: A Comforting Bowl of Goodness


There’s something truly special about a steaming bowl of soup—especially when it’s a comforting blend of creamy tomato, fragrant basil, and delicate tortellini. This Creamy Tomato and Basil Tortellini Soup is the epitome of comfort food, perfect for a cozy night in or as a heartwarming addition to any gathering. Let’s dive into the recipe and discover how to create this soul-warming dish that’s bound to become a new favorite in your culinary repertoire.

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 medium onion, finely chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1/2 teaspoon red pepper flakes
  • 1 can (28 ounces) crushed tomatoes
  • 4 cups vegetable or chicken broth
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 9 ounces cheese tortellini
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh basil leaves for garnish

Instructions

  1. Sauté the Aromatics: In a large pot, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the chopped onion and sauté until translucent, then add the minced garlic, dried oregano, dried basil, and red pepper flakes. Sauté for another minute until the garlic becomes fragrant.

  2. Add the Tomatoes and Broth: Pour in the crushed tomatoes and vegetable or chicken broth. Bring the mixture to a gentle boil, then reduce the heat and let it simmer for about 15 minutes, allowing the flavors to meld together.

  3. Blend the Soup: Using an immersion blender or working in batches with a regular blender, carefully blend the soup until smooth. Be cautious with the hot liquid.

  4. Add the Cream and Tortellini: Return the blended soup to the pot and stir in the heavy cream. Bring the soup back to a simmer and add the cheese tortellini. Cook according to the package instructions for the tortellini, usually about 7-9 minutes.

  5. Season and Serve: Once the tortellini are tender, season the soup with salt and pepper to taste. Ladle it into bowls and garnish with fresh basil leaves for a burst of aromatic freshness.

Tips for Success

  • For a vegan version of this soup, simply substitute the heavy cream with a non-dairy alternative such as coconut cream or cashew cream.
  • If you prefer a thinner consistency, you can adjust the soup by adding a bit more broth to reach your desired thickness.
  • Feel free to customize the level of spiciness by adjusting the amount of red pepper flakes to suit your taste.

Its vibrant colors and inviting aroma make it a feast for the senses, while its creamy texture and indulgent flavors elevate it to a truly luxurious dining experience.

From its rich and creamy base to the delightful bursts of cheese-filled tortellini, this soup is a celebration of comfort and flavor. It’s a dish that delights the senses and warms the soul, making it a must-try for anyone who appreciates the simple joy of a delicious bowl of soup.
