Mouthwatering Keto Cheesy Cauliflower Breadsticks Recipe


If you’re on a Keto diet and craving some cheesy goodness, look no further! These mouthwatering Keto Cheesy Cauliflower Breadsticks are the perfect low-carb alternative to traditional breadsticks. Packed with cheesy flavor and a satisfyingly chewy texture, these breadsticks will have you coming back for more.

Ingredients

  • 1 medium head of cauliflower, grated
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 425°F (220°C) and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.

  2. Place the grated cauliflower in a microwave-safe bowl and microwave for 5-6 minutes, until soft. Let it cool for a few minutes.

  3. Once the cauliflower has cooled, transfer it to a clean kitchen towel or cheesecloth. Squeeze out as much moisture as possible. This step is crucial to achieving the right texture for your breadsticks.

  4. In a mixing bowl, combine the cauliflower, eggs, shredded mozzarella cheese, dried oregano, garlic powder, salt, and black pepper. Mix well until all the ingredients are evenly combined.

  5. Transfer the cauliflower mixture onto the prepared baking sheet. Use your hands to shape it into a rectangle, about 1/4 inch thick.

  6. Bake in the preheated oven for 25-30 minutes, or until the edges are golden brown and the center is set.

  7. Remove from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes. Carefully cut the cauliflower crust into breadstick shapes. You can make them as thick or thin as you prefer.

  8. Once you’ve cut the breadsticks, sprinkle some additional shredded mozzarella cheese on top. Return them to the oven for another 5 minutes, or until the cheese is melted and bubbly.

  9. Serve the Keto Cheesy Cauliflower Breadsticks warm with your favorite dipping sauce, such as marinara or ranch dressing.

Tips and Variations

  • For an extra kick of flavor, add some crushed red pepper flakes or dried basil to the cauliflower mixture before baking.
  • Feel free to experiment with different types of cheese, such as cheddar or Parmesan, to create your own unique flavor profile.
  • If you want a crispier texture, flip the breadsticks halfway through baking to ensure even browning.
  • These breadsticks are best enjoyed fresh out of the oven, but you can store any leftovers in an airtight container in the refrigerator for up to 3 days. Simply reheat them in the oven or microwave before serving.

Now you can satisfy your breadstick cravings without derailing your Keto diet. These Keto Cheesy Cauliflower Breadsticks are a delicious and nutritious alternative that will leave you feeling satisfied. Give them a try and let us know how they turn out!
