Keto Comfort Foods: Home Cooking with Low-Carb Indulgence

Who says you have to sacrifice taste and comfort to stick to a ketogenic diet? Welcome to the world of delicious, low-carb indulgence, where you can satisfy your cravings while staying true to your keto lifestyle. Discover our favorite keto comfort food recipes that will soon become a staple in your home-cooking repertoire. 1. Creamy Garlic Chicken Casserole Ingredients: 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts 1 tablespoon olive oil 1 cup heavy cream 4 garlic cloves, minced 1/2 teaspoon black pepper 1/8 teaspoon cayenne pepper 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese 1 cup shredded cheddar cheese Salt, to taste 2 cups cauliflower rice Instructions: Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C). Season chicken breasts with salt and pepper, and cook in a skillet with olive oil over medium heat for 5-7 minutes per side, or until fully cooked. Remove from skillet and slice. In the same skillet, add minced garlic and stir for about 1 minute. Add heavy cream, cayenne pepper, and bring to a simmer. In a separate bowl, mix cauliflower rice with Parmesan and cheddar cheese. Add sliced chicken to the cream sauce and mix well. Place the cauliflower rice mixture in a 9x13 baking dish and top with the chicken and cream sauce. Bake uncovered for 30 minutes, or until cheese is golden and bubbly. 2. deceitfully Easy Almond Flour Chocolate Chip Cookies Ingredients: 2 cups almond flour 1/2 cup butter, softened 1/2 cup sugar substitute (like erythritol) 1 teaspoon baking soda 1/2 teaspoon salt 2 large eggs 1 teaspoon vanilla extract 1 cup dark chocolate chips Instructions: Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C) and line a baking sheet with parchment paper. In a medium bowl, mix almond flour, baking soda, and salt. In a large bowl, beat softened butter and sugar substitute using an electric mixer until smooth and creamy. Add eggs one at a time, mixing well after each addition. Stir in the vanilla extract. Gradually add the almond flour mixture to the wet ingredients, mixing until well-combined. Fold in dark chocolate chips and mix until evenly distributed. Using a cookie scoop or spoon, form balls of dough and place them on your lined baking sheet, leaving about 2 inches between each cookie. Bake for 12-15 minutes or until the edges are lightly golden. Allow cookies to cool on the baking sheet for about 10 minutes before transferring to a wire rack to cool completely. 3. Classic Broccoli Cheddar Soup with Keto Secret Ingredients: 1 tablespoon butter 1/2 small onion, diced 2 cups broccoli florets 4 cups chicken broth 1/2 cup heavy cream 1/2 cup shredded cheddar cheese 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese Salt, to taste Ground black pepper, to taste 1 cup cauliflower florets Instructions: In a large saucepan, melt the butter over medium heat. Add diced onion and cook until softened, about 5 minutes. In a food processor or blender, combine cooked onion, broccoli, cauliflower, chicken broth, and blend until smooth. Pour the blended mixture back into the saucepan and bring to a boil. Reduce heat and let simmer for 5 minutes. Stir in heavy cream, cheddar cheese, and Parmesan cheese. Continue stirring until the cheese melts and the soup thickens. Season with salt and black pepper to taste. Serve hot, garnished with a sprinkle of cheddar cheese and extra black pepper if desired.
