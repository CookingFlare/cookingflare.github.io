Keto Coffee & Tea: Exquisite Low-Carb Beverages for Every Occasion


Ah, coffee and tea – comforting, delicious beverages that can be part of a low-carb lifestyle. For those of us on the ketogenic diet, swapping sugary coffee shop drinks for healthier options can keep our carb intake in check without sacrificing taste. In this post, we’ll explore some mouth-watering recipes for keto coffee and tea drinks perfect for any time of day or occasion.

Keto Mocha Bliss

Looking for an indulgent treat to satisfy a chocolate craving? The Keto Mocha Bliss is a delightful twist on the classic mocha, giving you that sought-after taste without any of the sugar crash.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup of brewed coffee
  • 1 scoop of unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 2 tablespoons of creamer (use a low-carb version like MCT oil or heavy whipped cream)
  • 1-2 packets of sweetener (choose a keto-friendly option like Stevia or Erythritol)
  • 1/8 tsp of vanilla extract
  • Garnish with a sprinkle of cinnamon

Instructions:

  1. Brew your coffee and pour it into a cup.
  2. Mix the cocoa powder in with the coffee until fully dissolved.
  3. Add your low-carb creamer and sweetener to taste. For an extra touch of richness, you can also add a dollop of heavy whipped cream.
  4. Stir well and give it a little spritz of vanilla extract.
  5. Serve immediately and garnish with a dash of cinnamon.

Iced Raspberry Dandelion Tea

A delightful low-carb alternative to traditional iced tea, the Iced Raspberry Dandelion Tea harnesses the earthy tones of dandelion root and adds a burst of fruity sweetness from fresh raspberries.

Ingredients:

  • 1 Dandelion root tea bag
  • 1 cup of boiling water
  • 1/4 cup of fresh raspberries
  • 1 tablespoon of lemon juice
  • A handful of ice cubes
  • 1-2 packets of sweetener (use a keto-friendly option like Stevia or Erythritol)

Instructions:

  1. Boil water and pour it over the Dandelion root tea bag. Steep for at least 5 minutes or to your desired strength.
  2. Remove the tea bag and let the tea cool until it reaches room temperature.
  3. Add fresh raspberries, lemon juice, and sweetener to taste. Stir well.
  4. Pour the mixture over ice cubes to serve and give it a gentle stir.

Keto Matcha Latte

Calling all matcha lovers! Try this creamy, invigorating Keto Matcha Latte, the perfect blend of earthy and nutty flavors with a luxurious twist from coconut cream.

Ingredients:

  • 1 teaspoon of matcha green tea powder
  • 1 cup of hot water
  • 1/4 cup of coconut cream (from a can)
  • 1/2 teaspoon of vanilla extract
  • A pinch of cinnamon or nutmeg (optional)
  • 1-2 packets of sweetener (use a keto-friendly option like Stevia or Erythritol)

Instructions:

  1. Sift the matcha green tea powder into a small bowl to avoid lumps.
  2. Pour hot water into a cup.
  3. Whisk the matcha powder with a small amount of hot water in the bowl, forming a smooth paste.
  4. Pour the rest of the hot water into the cup, and then add the coconut cream and sweetener to taste.
  5. Whisk everything together until it’s frothy and well combined.
  6. Lastly, stir in the vanilla extract and a dash of cinnamon or nutmeg if desired.

Cinnamon Vanilla Bulletproof Coffee

Indulge in a creamy, satisfying late-morning or afternoon pick-me-up with our Cinnamon Vanilla Bulletproof Coffee. Rich in healthy fats and free from sugar, this coffee is what ketogenic dreams are made of.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup of brewed coffee
  • 1 tablespoon of grass-fed butter
  • 1 tablespoon of MCT oil
  • 1/2 teaspoon of ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 teaspoon of vanilla extract
  • 1-2 packets of sweetener (use a keto-friendly option like Stevia or Erythritol)

Instructions:

  1. Brew your coffee and pour it into a blender.
  2. Add in the grass-fed butter, MCT oil, cinnamon, vanilla extract, and your chosen sweetener.
  3. Blend the mixture on high speed for about 30 seconds, or until it’s fully and evenly mixed.
  4. Pour the mixture into a mug and enjoy the caffeine and MCT oil boost.

In conclusion, enjoying keto-friendly coffee and tea options is a delightful way to maintain the ketogenic diet without feeling deprived of flavorful beverages. With these scrumptious recipes, you’ll embrace every occasion with luxurious and low-carb drinks, concocted to keep your taste buds enchanted and your carb count intact.
