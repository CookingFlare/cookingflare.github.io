Loaded Sweet Potato Fries: A Healthy Comfort Snack


Are you looking for a delicious and nutritious snack that satisfies your cravings for comfort food? Look no further than loaded sweet potato fries! These tasty treats are not only a healthier alternative to regular fries, but they are also loaded with flavor and toppings that will leave you wanting more. Whether you’re hosting a game night or simply craving a snack, loaded sweet potato fries are sure to be a hit.

Ingredients

To make loaded sweet potato fries, you will need the following ingredients:

  • 2 medium sweet potatoes
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon onion powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • Toppings of your choice (see suggestions below)

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 425°F (220°C). Line a baking sheet with parchment paper or aluminum foil for easy cleanup.

  2. Wash and dry the sweet potatoes. Cut them into thin strips, about 1/4 inch thick. Try to make them as uniform as possible to ensure even baking.

  3. In a large bowl, combine the olive oil, paprika, garlic powder, onion powder, salt, and black pepper. Add the sweet potato fries to the bowl and toss until they are evenly coated with the seasoning mixture.

  4. Spread the seasoned sweet potato fries in a single layer on the prepared baking sheet. Make sure they are not overcrowded to ensure crispy fries.

  5. Bake the sweet potato fries in the preheated oven for about 25-30 minutes or until they are golden brown and crispy. Flip the fries halfway through the baking time to ensure even cooking.

  6. While the fries are baking, prepare your desired toppings. The possibilities are endless! Here are a few suggestions to get you started:

    • Guacamole: Mash a ripe avocado with lime juice, salt, and pepper.
    • Black Bean Salsa: Combine black beans, diced tomatoes, red onion, jalapeno, cilantro, lime juice, and salt.
    • Greek Yogurt Sauce: Mix Greek yogurt, lemon juice, garlic powder, and fresh dill.
    • Cheddar Cheese: Grate some cheddar cheese for a classic topping.

  7. Once the sweet potato fries are done baking, remove them from the oven and let them cool for a few minutes. This will help them become even crispier.

  8. To assemble your loaded sweet potato fries, place them on a serving platter or individual plates. Top them with your desired toppings, or let your guests customize their own.

  9. Serve the loaded sweet potato fries immediately and enjoy the delicious combination of crispy fries and flavorful toppings. These make a great snack for movie nights, parties, or even as a side dish for your favorite burger.

Conclusion

Loaded sweet potato fries are a fantastic way to enjoy a healthy and satisfying snack. With their crispy texture, sweet flavor, and endless topping options, they are sure to be a crowd-pleaser. Plus, since they are baked instead of fried, you can indulge without feeling guilty. So, the next time you’re in the mood for a comforting snack, give loaded sweet potato fries a try. Your taste buds will thank you!
