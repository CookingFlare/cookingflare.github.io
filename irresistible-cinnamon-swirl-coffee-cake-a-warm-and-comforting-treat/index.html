Irresistible Cinnamon Swirl Coffee Cake: A Warm and Comforting Treat


If you’re a fan of cozy mornings and delicious baked treats, then this recipe is for you. This Irresistible Cinnamon Swirl Coffee Cake is the perfect combination of warmth and comfort, with a sweet and spicy cinnamon swirl that will leave you craving for more. Whether you’re enjoying it with a steaming cup of coffee or sharing it with loved ones, this cake is sure to bring joy to your taste buds.

Ingredients

For the cake:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1 cup milk
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

For the cinnamon swirl:

  • 1/2 cup brown sugar
  • 1 tablespoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 cup unsalted butter, melted

For the streusel topping:

  • 1/2 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/4 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease and flour a 9-inch round cake pan.

  2. In a large mixing bowl, combine the flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt. Add the softened butter and mix until the mixture resembles coarse crumbs.

  3. In a separate bowl, whisk together the milk, eggs, and vanilla extract. Pour the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients and mix until just combined. Be careful not to overmix, as this can result in a dense cake.

  4. In a small bowl, mix together the brown sugar and ground cinnamon for the cinnamon swirl. Set aside.

  5. Pour half of the cake batter into the prepared cake pan. Sprinkle the cinnamon swirl mixture evenly over the batter. Drizzle the melted butter over the cinnamon swirl.

  6. Carefully spread the remaining cake batter over the cinnamon swirl layer, covering it completely.

  7. In a bowl, combine the flour, granulated sugar, softened butter, and ground cinnamon for the streusel topping. Use your fingers to crumble the mixture over the top of the cake batter.

  8. Bake in the preheated oven for 35-40 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean. The streusel topping should be golden brown and the cake should be firm to the touch.

  9. Remove the cake from the oven and let it cool in the pan for 10 minutes. Then, transfer it to a wire rack to cool completely before serving.

Serving Suggestions

This Irresistible Cinnamon Swirl Coffee Cake is delicious on its own, but you can take it to the next level with a few serving suggestions:

  • Serve it warm with a dollop of whipped cream or a scoop of vanilla ice cream for a decadent dessert.
  • Enjoy it alongside a cup of hot coffee or tea for a delightful afternoon treat.
  • Top it with a dusting of powdered sugar for an extra touch of sweetness.

Conclusion

This Irresistible Cinnamon Swirl Coffee Cake is a true delight for your taste buds. Its warm and comforting flavors, combined with the sweet and spicy cinnamon swirl, make it a perfect treat for any occasion. Whether you’re enjoying it alone or sharing it with loved ones, this cake is sure to bring smiles and satisfaction. So, don’t wait any longer – gather your ingredients, preheat your oven, and indulge in the irresistible goodness of this Cinnamon Swirl Coffee Cake!
