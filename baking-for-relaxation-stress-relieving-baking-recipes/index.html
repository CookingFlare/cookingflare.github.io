Baking for Relaxation: Stress-Relieving Baking Recipes


In today’s fast-paced world, finding moments of calm and relaxation is more important than ever. One wonderful way to unwind and de-stress is through the therapeutic art of baking. The process of measuring ingredients, mixing batter, and watching your creations rise in the oven can be incredibly soothing for the mind and soul. If you’re looking to destress and indulge in some self-care, here are a few stress-relieving baking recipes to try out:

1. Lavender Lemon Loaf

Prep Time: 15 minutes | Cook Time: 50 minutes | Total Time: 1 hour 5 minutes

Ingredients:

  • 1 ½ cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 2 tablespoons dried culinary lavender
  • 3 large eggs
  • 1 cup plain yogurt
  • ½ cup vegetable oil
  • Zest of 1 lemon
  • Juice of 1 lemon
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C) and grease a loaf pan.
  2. In a bowl, whisk together the flour, baking powder, and salt.
  3. In a separate bowl, mix the sugar and dried lavender.
  4. Add eggs, yogurt, oil, lemon zest, lemon juice, and vanilla extract to the sugar mixture. Mix well.
  5. Gradually incorporate the dry ingredients into the wet ingredients.
  6. Pour the batter into the loaf pan and bake for 50 minutes or until a toothpick comes out clean.
  7. Once baked, let the loaf cool before slicing and enjoying a slice with a cup of tea.

2. Cinnamon Roll Cookies

Prep Time: 30 minutes | Cook Time: 12 minutes | Total Time: 42 minutes

Ingredients:

  • 2 ¾ cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1 large egg
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 2 tablespoons ground cinnamon

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C) and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
  2. In a bowl, whisk together the flour and baking powder.
  3. In a separate bowl, cream the butter and sugar until light and fluffy.
  4. Add the egg and vanilla extract to the butter mixture and mix well.
  5. Gradually add the dry ingredients to form a dough.
  6. Roll out the dough on a floured surface and sprinkle cinnamon evenly.
  7. Roll the dough into a log and slice into cookies.
  8. Place the cookies on the baking sheet and bake for 12 minutes.
  9. Allow the cookies to cool before serving and enjoy the warm, comforting flavors.

3. Chocolate Avocado Brownies

Prep Time: 20 minutes | Cook Time: 25 minutes | Total Time: 45 minutes

Ingredients:

  • 2 ripe avocados
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 2 large eggs
  • ½ cup cocoa powder
  • ½ cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • ¼ teaspoon salt
  • ½ cup chocolate chips

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C) and grease a brownie pan.
  2. In a blender, puree the avocados, sugar, and vanilla extract until smooth.
  3. Add the eggs one at a time, blending well after each addition.
  4. In a bowl, whisk together the cocoa powder, flour, baking powder, and salt.
  5. Gradually add the dry ingredients to the avocado mixture and mix until combined.
  6. Fold in the chocolate chips.
  7. Pour the batter into the brownie pan and bake for 25 minutes.
  8. Allow the brownies to cool before cutting into squares and savoring the fudgy goodness.

Baking can be a form of mindfulness, allowing you to focus on the present moment and create something delicious in the process. So, the next time you feel overwhelmed, step into the kitchen, put on some calming music, and bake your stress away with these delightful recipes. Remember, taking care of yourself is essential, and a little baking therapy can go a long way in nurturing your well-being. Happy baking and happy relaxing!
