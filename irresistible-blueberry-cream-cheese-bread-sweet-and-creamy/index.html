Irresistible Blueberry Cream Cheese Bread: Sweet and Creamy


There’s something undeniably comforting about the aroma of freshly baked bread wafting through the kitchen. Add in the sweet, juicy burst of blueberries and the creamy richness of cream cheese, and you have a recipe for pure delight. This irresistible blueberry cream cheese bread is a delightful treat that’s perfect for breakfast, brunch, or as a sweet snack any time of the day.

Ingredients

For the Bread:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 cup plain Greek yogurt
  • 1 1/2 cups fresh blueberries (tossed in 1 tablespoon of flour)

For the Cream Cheese Filling:

  • 8 oz cream cheese, softened
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 egg
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

For the Streusel Topping:

  • 1/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 3 tablespoons unsalted butter, cold and cubed

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease and flour a 9x5-inch loaf pan.

  2. In a medium bowl, whisk together the flour, baking powder, baking soda, and salt.

  3. In a large bowl, cream together the softened butter and sugar until light and fluffy. Beat in the eggs, one at a time, then stir in the vanilla extract.

  4. Gradually mix in the dry ingredients, alternating with the Greek yogurt, until just combined. Be careful not to overmix. Gently fold in the blueberries.

  5. In a separate bowl, beat together the softened cream cheese, sugar, egg, and vanilla extract until smooth and creamy.

  6. In another small bowl, combine the flour, sugar, and cold cubed butter for the streusel topping. Use a fork or pastry cutter to mix until crumbly.

  7. Pour half of the bread batter into the prepared loaf pan. Spread the cream cheese mixture over the batter, then top with the remaining batter.

  8. Sprinkle the streusel topping evenly over the top of the batter.

  9. Bake in the preheated oven for 50-60 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.

  10. Allow the bread to cool in the pan for 10 minutes, then transfer to a wire rack to cool completely before slicing.

Serving Suggestions

This blueberry cream cheese bread is delicious on its own, but you can take it to the next level by serving it with a dollop of whipped cream or a drizzle of warm blueberry compote. It pairs perfectly with a cup of freshly brewed coffee or a glass of cold milk.

Storage

Store any leftovers (if there are any!) in an airtight container at room temperature for up to 3 days, or in the refrigerator for up to a week. You can also freeze slices of the bread for up to 3 months. Just be sure to wrap them tightly in plastic wrap and aluminum foil before freezing.

Final Thoughts

The combination of sweet blueberries, creamy cream cheese, and tender bread in this irresistible blueberry cream cheese bread is simply divine. Whether you’re baking it for a special occasion or simply to treat yourself and your loved ones, this recipe is sure to become a favorite. Enjoy the process of baking and savoring every delicious bite of this sweet and creamy delight!
