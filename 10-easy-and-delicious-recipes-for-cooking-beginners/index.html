10 Easy and Delicious Recipes for Cooking Beginners


Are you new to cooking? Looking for some simple yet tasty recipes to get started in the kitchen? Look no further! In this blog post, we have curated a list of 10 easy and delicious recipes that are perfect for cooking beginners. These recipes are designed to help you build your confidence in the kitchen and explore your culinary skills. So put on your apron, grab your ingredients, and let’s get cooking!

1. Creamy Garlic Parmesan Pasta

This creamy pasta dish is a classic and perfect for beginners. Cook some pasta according to the package instructions. In a separate pan, melt butter and sauté minced garlic until fragrant. Add heavy cream, grated Parmesan cheese, and salt. Let it simmer until the sauce thickens. Toss in the cooked pasta and mix well. Serve with a sprinkle of parsley on top.

2. Baked Chicken Tenders

For a quick and easy dinner, try these crispy baked chicken tenders. Preheat your oven to 425°F. In a bowl, mix together breadcrumbs, grated Parmesan cheese, garlic powder, salt, and pepper. Dip chicken tenders into beaten eggs, then coat them in the breadcrumb mixture. Place the coated chicken tenders on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper and bake for 15-20 minutes until golden brown and cooked through.

3. One-Pan Roasted Veggies

This recipe is perfect for those who want a healthy and hassle-free side dish. Preheat your oven to 400°F. Chop your favorite vegetables like bell peppers, zucchini, and carrots into bite-sized pieces. Toss them in olive oil, salt, pepper, and any herbs or spices you like. Spread the seasoned vegetables on a baking sheet and roast for 20-25 minutes, or until they are tender and slightly charred.

4. Caprese Salad

For a refreshing and simple salad, try this Caprese salad. Slice fresh tomatoes and mozzarella cheese into rounds. Arrange them on a plate, alternating between tomato and cheese slices. Drizzle with olive oil, sprinkle with salt and pepper, and garnish with fresh basil leaves. Serve as an appetizer or a light lunch.

5. Homemade Pizza

Making your own pizza is not only fun but also delicious. Start by preheating your oven to 475°F. Roll out pizza dough on a floured surface and transfer it onto a baking sheet. Spread tomato sauce on the dough and top with your favorite toppings such as cheese, pepperoni, and vegetables. Bake for 12-15 minutes or until the crust is golden and the cheese is bubbly.

6. Classic French Toast

Breakfast couldn’t be easier with this classic French toast recipe. In a shallow bowl, whisk together eggs, milk, vanilla extract, and a pinch of cinnamon. Dip slices of bread into the egg mixture, making sure both sides are coated. Cook the bread on a greased skillet over medium heat until golden brown on both sides. Serve with maple syrup and a sprinkle of powdered sugar.

7. Veggie Stir-Fry

Stir-fries are quick, versatile, and a great way to use up leftover vegetables. Heat oil in a wok or skillet over high heat. Add sliced vegetables like bell peppers, broccoli, and carrots. Toss them around for a few minutes until they are slightly tender. In a separate bowl, mix together soy sauce, garlic, ginger, and a pinch of sugar. Pour the sauce over the vegetables and cook for another minute or two. Serve over steamed rice.

8. Tomato Basil Soup

Nothing beats a comforting bowl of tomato basil soup. In a large pot, sauté chopped onions and minced garlic in olive oil until fragrant. Add canned tomatoes, vegetable broth, and fresh basil leaves. Let it simmer for 20-30 minutes. Use an immersion blender or a regular blender to puree the soup until smooth. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Serve with a dollop of sour cream and a sprinkle of fresh basil.

9. Easy Banana Bread

Banana bread is a classic treat that is perfect for baking beginners. Preheat your oven to 350°F. In a bowl, mash ripe bananas with a fork. Stir in melted butter, sugar, eggs, and vanilla extract. Add flour, baking soda, and a pinch of salt. Mix until just combined. Pour the batter into a greased loaf pan and bake for 55-60 minutes or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean. Let it cool before slicing.

10. Chocolate Chip Cookies

No list of easy recipes is complete without chocolate chip cookies. Preheat your oven to 375°F. In a bowl, cream together softened butter, granulated sugar, and brown sugar until light and fluffy. Beat in eggs and vanilla extract. In a separate bowl, whisk together flour, baking soda, and salt. Gradually add the dry ingredients to the wet ingredients and mix until combined. Stir in chocolate chips. Drop spoonfuls of dough onto a baking sheet and bake for 9-11 minutes or until golden brown. Let them cool on a wire rack before enjoying.

There you have it, 10 easy and delicious recipes for cooking beginners. We hope these recipes inspire you to get creative in the kitchen and discover the joy of cooking. Remember, practice makes perfect, so don’t be afraid to experiment and have fun with your culinary adventures. Happy cooking!
