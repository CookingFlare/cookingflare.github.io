Mouthwatering Keto Chicken Alfredo Casserole Recipe


Are you looking for a delicious and satisfying keto recipe that will leave you wanting more? Look no further than this mouthwatering Keto Chicken Alfredo Casserole! Packed with flavor and low in carbs, this casserole is the perfect option for a hearty and indulgent meal. Plus, it’s easy to make and can be enjoyed by the whole family.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups cooked chicken breast, shredded
  • 2 cups cauliflower florets
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/4 cup unsalted butter
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon dried Italian seasoning
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • Fresh parsley, for garnish

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).

  2. In a large pot of boiling water, cook the cauliflower florets until tender. Drain and set aside.

  3. In a separate saucepan, melt the butter over medium heat. Add the minced garlic and sauté until fragrant.

  4. Add the heavy cream, Parmesan cheese, Italian seasoning, salt, and black pepper to the saucepan. Stir well to combine and bring to a simmer.

  5. Once the sauce is simmering, remove it from the heat and set aside.

  6. In a large mixing bowl, combine the shredded chicken and cooked cauliflower. Pour the Alfredo sauce over the top and stir gently to coat everything evenly.

  7. Transfer the mixture to a greased casserole dish and spread it out evenly.

  8. Bake in the preheated oven for 20-25 minutes, or until the top is golden and bubbly.

  9. Remove from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes before serving.

  10. Garnish with fresh parsley and serve hot.

Nutritional Information

  • Calories: 350
  • Total Fat: 25g
  • Carbohydrates: 6g
  • Fiber: 2g
  • Protein: 25g

Why Choose Keto Chicken Alfredo Casserole?

Not only is this Keto Chicken Alfredo Casserole absolutely delicious, but it’s also a great option for those following a ketogenic diet. The combination of chicken, cauliflower, and creamy Alfredo sauce creates a satisfying and nourishing meal that is low in carbs and high in healthy fats. It’s a perfect choice for anyone looking to maintain ketosis while still enjoying a comforting and indulgent dish.

Tips for Making the Perfect Casserole

  • Make sure to cook the cauliflower until it is tender but not mushy. This will ensure that it holds its shape and adds a pleasant texture to the casserole.
  • Feel free to add some extra flavor to the casserole by including your favorite herbs or spices. You can experiment with different combinations to suit your taste preferences.
  • If you’re short on time, you can use pre-cooked chicken or a rotisserie chicken to save time on preparation.
  • Leftovers can be stored in an airtight container in the refrigerator for up to 3 days. Simply reheat in the microwave or oven before serving.

Final Thoughts

This mouthwatering Keto Chicken Alfredo Casserole is a true crowd-pleaser. With its creamy sauce, tender chicken, and flavorful cauliflower, it’s sure to become a family favorite. It’s a great option for those following a keto diet or anyone looking for a delicious and satisfying meal. So why wait? Give this recipe a try today and enjoy the indulgent flavors without the guilt!
