Spicy Keto Buffalo Chicken Cauliflower Casserole Recipe


Are you looking for a mouth-watering, spicy, and satisfying casserole recipe that’s perfect for a keto diet? Look no further! Today, we’re going to share a unique and delicious Spicy Keto Buffalo Chicken Cauliflower Casserole recipe that’s sure to become a favorite in your household.

Ingredients

  • 1 head of cauliflower, broken into florets
  • 2 lbs boneless, skinless chicken breasts, cut into 1-inch pieces
  • 1/2 cup buffalo wing sauce
  • 1/4 cup ranch dressing
  • 1/2 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh cilantro
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon onion powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon paprika
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. In a large bowl, toss the cauliflower florets with 1 tablespoon of olive oil, salt, and pepper until they’re evenly coated. Spread them out in a single layer on a baking sheet and roast for 20-25 minutes, or until they’re tender and lightly browned.
  3. While the cauliflower is roasting, cook the chicken in a large skillet over medium-high heat, adding the remaining 1 tablespoon of olive oil. Cook until the chicken is browned and cooked through, about 5-7 minutes. Remove the chicken from the skillet and set it aside.
  4. In the same skillet, add the buffalo wing sauce and bring it to a simmer. Let it cook for about 2-3 minutes, or until the sauce has thickened slightly.
  5. In a large mixing bowl, combine the cooked chicken, buffalo wing sauce, and ranch dressing. Stir until the chicken is well coated with the sauce.
  6. In a 9x13-inch baking dish, create a layer of the roasted cauliflower. Top it with a layer of the chicken mixture, followed by a sprinkle of shredded cheddar cheese and chopped cilantro.
  7. Repeat the layering process one more time, finishing with a layer of cheese on top.
  8. Sprinkle the paprika, garlic powder, and onion powder over the top of the cheese.
  9. Cover the dish with aluminum foil and bake for 20-25 minutes, or until the cheese is melted and bubbly.
  10. Remove the foil and continue baking for an additional 5-10 minutes, or until the top is lightly browned.

Tips and Variations

  • To make this recipe more spicy, add more buffalo wing sauce or use hot sauce like Frank’s RedHot.
  • If you prefer a milder flavor, use less buffalo wing sauce or substitute it with a milder sauce like teriyaki.
  • To make this recipe more substantial, add some chopped bacon or diced onions to the chicken mixture.
  • Experiment with different types of cheese, such as mozzarella or Monterey Jack, for a unique flavor profile.

Nutritional Information

Per serving (serves 6-8):

  • Calories: 320
  • Protein: 37g
  • Fat: 24g
  • Carbohydrates: 5g
  • Fiber: 2g
  • Net Carbs: 3g

Conclusion

This Spicy Keto Buffalo Chicken Cauliflower Casserole recipe is a game-changer for anyone following a keto diet or just looking for a delicious and spicy casserole. With its unique combination of buffalo wing sauce, ranch dressing, and cauliflower, it’s a flavor profile unlike any other. So go ahead, give it a try, and enjoy the perfect blend of spicy and satisfying!
