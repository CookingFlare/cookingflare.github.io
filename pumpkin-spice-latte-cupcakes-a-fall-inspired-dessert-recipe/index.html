Pumpkin Spice Latte Cupcakes: A Fall-Inspired Dessert Recipe


Fall is in the air, and what better way to celebrate the season than with a delightful and flavorful dessert? These Pumpkin Spice Latte Cupcakes are the perfect treat to satisfy your autumn cravings. Packed with warm spices, rich pumpkin flavor, and a hint of coffee, these cupcakes will transport your taste buds to a cozy coffee shop.

Ingredients

  • 1 3/4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 2 teaspoons ground cinnamon
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground ginger
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground nutmeg
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground cloves
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/2 cup brown sugar, packed
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 cup canned pumpkin puree
  • 1/4 cup strong brewed coffee, cooled
  • 1/4 cup milk
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C) and line a muffin tin with cupcake liners.
  2. In a medium bowl, whisk together the flour, baking powder, baking soda, salt, cinnamon, ginger, nutmeg, and cloves. Set aside.
  3. In a large mixing bowl, cream together the softened butter, granulated sugar, and brown sugar until light and fluffy.
  4. Add the eggs one at a time, beating well after each addition. Then, mix in the pumpkin puree, brewed coffee, milk, and vanilla extract until well combined.
  5. Gradually add the dry ingredients to the wet ingredients, mixing until just combined. Be careful not to overmix.
  6. Divide the batter evenly among the prepared cupcake liners, filling each about 2/3 full.
  7. Bake in the preheated oven for 18-20 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.
  8. Remove the cupcakes from the oven and let them cool in the pan for a few minutes before transferring them to a wire rack to cool completely.

Frosting

To complete the pumpkin spice latte experience, top these cupcakes with a delicious and creamy coffee-flavored frosting.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 4 cups powdered sugar
  • 1/4 cup strong brewed coffee, cooled
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Instructions

  1. In a large mixing bowl, beat the softened butter until creamy.
  2. Gradually add the powdered sugar, about 1 cup at a time, mixing well after each addition.
  3. Add the brewed coffee and vanilla extract, and continue to beat until the frosting is smooth and fluffy.
  4. Once the cupcakes have cooled, frost them generously with the coffee frosting.

Serving Suggestions

These Pumpkin Spice Latte Cupcakes are delicious on their own, but you can also add a little extra flair by garnishing them with a sprinkle of cinnamon or a drizzle of caramel sauce. Serve them with a hot cup of coffee or a steaming mug of pumpkin spice latte for the ultimate fall treat.

Storage

Store any leftover cupcakes in an airtight container at room temperature for up to 3 days. You can also freeze the cupcakes (without frosting) for up to 3 months. Just make sure to thaw them before adding the frosting.

Conclusion

Indulge in the flavors of fall with these Pumpkin Spice Latte Cupcakes. The combination of pumpkin, warm spices, and a hint of coffee will make your taste buds dance with joy. Whether you’re hosting a fall gathering or simply craving a cozy dessert, this recipe is sure to impress. So go ahead, bake a batch of these delightful cupcakes and savor the taste of autumn!
