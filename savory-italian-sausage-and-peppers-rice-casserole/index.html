Savory Italian Sausage and Peppers Rice Casserole


Are you looking for a hearty and flavorful dish that will satisfy your family’s cravings? Look no further than this delicious Savory Italian Sausage and Peppers Rice Casserole! Packed with the vibrant flavors of Italian sausage, bell peppers, and fragrant spices, this casserole is a crowd-pleaser that will leave everyone asking for seconds.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound Italian sausage, casings removed
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 2 bell peppers, thinly sliced
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon dried Italian seasoning
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon red pepper flakes (optional, for heat)
  • 1 cup long-grain rice
  • 1 can (14.5 ounces) diced tomatoes, undrained
  • 1 cup chicken broth
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • Fresh parsley, for garnish

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).

  2. In a large oven-safe skillet or casserole dish, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the Italian sausage and cook until browned, breaking it up into crumbles with a spoon or spatula.

  3. Once the sausage is cooked, remove it from the skillet and set it aside.

  4. In the same skillet, add the diced onion and bell peppers. Sauté until they are slightly softened, about 5 minutes.

  5. Add the minced garlic, dried Italian seasoning, paprika, and red pepper flakes (if using). Stir to combine and cook for an additional minute until fragrant.

  6. Return the cooked Italian sausage to the skillet and mix well with the vegetables.

  7. Next, add the long-grain rice, diced tomatoes (undrained), and chicken broth. Stir everything together until well combined.

  8. Season with salt and pepper to taste.

  9. Bring the mixture to a boil, then cover the skillet or casserole dish with a lid or aluminum foil. Transfer it to the preheated oven and bake for 25-30 minutes, or until the rice is cooked and the flavors have melded together.

  10. Remove from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes. Garnish with fresh parsley before serving.

  11. Serve hot and enjoy the delightful flavors of this Savory Italian Sausage and Peppers Rice Casserole!

Additional Tips

  • Feel free to add your favorite vegetables to the casserole, such as zucchini, mushrooms, or spinach, for an extra boost of nutrition and flavor.
  • If you prefer a spicier casserole, you can increase the amount of red pepper flakes or add a dash of hot sauce to the mixture.
  • Leftovers can be stored in an airtight container in the refrigerator for up to three days. Simply reheat in the microwave or oven before serving.

This Savory Italian Sausage and Peppers Rice Casserole is a versatile dish that can be enjoyed for lunch or dinner. Its robust flavors and comforting textures make it a perfect choice for any occasion. Whether you’re cooking for family, friends, or simply craving a satisfying meal, this recipe will surely impress. So, gather your ingredients and get ready to indulge in a delicious and hearty Italian-inspired casserole that will leave everyone asking for more.
