Brunch Cocktails That Will Transport You to a Beachside Resort


When it comes to brunch, there’s nothing quite like sipping on a refreshing cocktail that takes you to a beachside resort, even if you’re miles away from the ocean. These tropical-inspired drinks are the perfect way to start your day and set the tone for a relaxing and enjoyable brunch experience. So gather your friends, put on your favorite beach playlist, and get ready to transport your taste buds to paradise with these delightful brunch cocktails.

1. Pineapple Mojito

Ingredients:

  • 2 ounces of white rum
  • 1 ounce of fresh lime juice
  • 1/2 ounce of simple syrup
  • 4-6 fresh mint leaves
  • 1/4 cup of fresh pineapple chunks
  • Club soda
  • Ice cubes

Instructions:

  1. In a cocktail shaker, muddle the mint leaves and pineapple chunks until well combined.
  2. Add the white rum, lime juice, and simple syrup to the shaker.
  3. Fill the shaker with ice cubes and shake vigorously for about 15 seconds.
  4. Strain the mixture into a glass filled with ice cubes.
  5. Top with club soda and garnish with a pineapple wedge and mint sprig.
  6. Stir gently and enjoy the tropical flavors of this refreshing pineapple mojito.

2. Coconut Margarita

Ingredients:

  • 2 ounces of silver tequila
  • 1 ounce of coconut cream
  • 1/2 ounce of triple sec
  • 1 ounce of fresh lime juice
  • Shredded coconut (for rimming the glass)
  • Lime wedges (for garnish)
  • Ice cubes

Instructions:

  1. Rim a margarita glass with shredded coconut by dipping the rim in lime juice and then in the coconut.
  2. Fill the glass with ice cubes.
  3. In a cocktail shaker, combine the tequila, coconut cream, triple sec, and lime juice.
  4. Fill the shaker with ice cubes and shake vigorously for about 15 seconds.
  5. Strain the mixture into the prepared glass.
  6. Garnish with a lime wedge and enjoy the creamy and tropical flavors of this coconut margarita.

3. Passionfruit Bellini

Ingredients:

  • 2 ounces of passionfruit puree
  • Prosecco or champagne
  • Fresh mint leaves (for garnish)

Instructions:

  1. Pour the passionfruit puree into a champagne flute.
  2. Slowly fill the glass with chilled prosecco or champagne, allowing the puree to mix with the bubbles.
  3. Garnish with fresh mint leaves and enjoy the sweet and bubbly flavors of this passionfruit bellini.

4. Watermelon Basil Smash

Ingredients:

  • 2 ounces of vodka
  • 1/2 cup of fresh watermelon chunks
  • 4-6 fresh basil leaves
  • 1/2 ounce of simple syrup
  • 1 ounce of fresh lime juice
  • Ice cubes

Instructions:

  1. In a cocktail shaker, muddle the watermelon chunks and basil leaves until well combined.
  2. Add the vodka, simple syrup, and lime juice to the shaker.
  3. Fill the shaker with ice cubes and shake vigorously for about 15 seconds.
  4. Strain the mixture into a glass filled with ice cubes.
  5. Garnish with a watermelon slice and a basil leaf, and enjoy the refreshing and herbaceous flavors of this watermelon basil smash.

5. Mango Mimosa

Ingredients:

  • 2 ounces of mango puree
  • Orange juice
  • Champagne or sparkling wine
  • Fresh mint leaves (for garnish)

Instructions:

  1. Pour the mango puree into a champagne flute.
  2. Fill the glass halfway with orange juice.
  3. Slowly fill the rest of the glass with chilled champagne or sparkling wine.
  4. Garnish with fresh mint leaves and enjoy the tropical and bubbly flavors of this mango mimosa.

Whether you’re hosting a brunch gathering or simply treating yourself to a special morning cocktail, these beachside-inspired drinks will transport you to a state of pure relaxation. So sit back, sip on one of these delightful concoctions, and let your imagination wander to the sunny shores of a beachside resort. Cheers to a brunch experience that feels like a vacation!
