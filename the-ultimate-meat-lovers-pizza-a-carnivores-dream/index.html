The Ultimate Meat Lover's Pizza: A Carnivore's Dream


If you’re a meat lover and a pizza enthusiast, then you’re in for a treat with the ultimate meat lover’s pizza recipe. This carnivore’s dream combines a variety of delicious meats on a bed of perfectly baked pizza crust, creating a flavor explosion that will leave you craving for more. Whether you’re hosting a game night with friends or simply indulging in a night of comfort food, this recipe is sure to satisfy your carnivorous cravings. So, roll up your sleeves and get ready to embark on a culinary adventure that will elevate your pizza game to a whole new level.

Ingredients

Pizza Dough

  • 2 1/4 teaspoons active dry yeast
  • 1 1/2 cups warm water
  • 3 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon salt

Pizza Sauce

  • 1 can (28 ounces) whole peeled tomatoes
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Toppings

  • 1/2 cup pepperoni, sliced
  • 1/2 cup Italian sausage, cooked and crumbled
  • 1/2 cup bacon, cooked and chopped
  • 1/2 cup ham, diced
  • 1/4 cup ground beef, cooked
  • 1 1/2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Pizza Dough

    • In a small bowl, dissolve the yeast in warm water and let it sit for about 5 minutes until it becomes frothy.
    • In a large mixing bowl, combine the flour and salt. Make a well in the center and add the yeast mixture and olive oil. Stir until a soft dough forms.
    • Turn the dough onto a floured surface and knead for about 5-7 minutes until it becomes smooth and elastic. Place the dough in a greased bowl, cover with a clean kitchen towel, and let it rise in a warm place for about 1 hour.

  2. Make the Pizza Sauce

    • In a food processor, blend the whole peeled tomatoes until smooth.
    • In a saucepan, heat some olive oil over medium heat and sauté the minced garlic until fragrant. Add the blended tomatoes, oregano, basil, salt, and pepper. Simmer the sauce for about 20-30 minutes, stirring occasionally, until it thickens.

  3. Assemble the Pizza

    • Preheat your oven to 475°F (245°C) and place a pizza stone or upside-down baking sheet inside to heat.
    • Roll out the pizza dough on a floured surface into a 12-inch circle. Transfer the dough to a piece of parchment paper.
    • Spread the pizza sauce evenly over the dough, leaving a small border for the crust. Sprinkle half of the mozzarella and Parmesan cheese over the sauce.
    • Arrange the pepperoni, Italian sausage, bacon, ham, and ground beef evenly over the cheese. Top with the remaining cheese.

  4. Bake the Pizza

    • Transfer the pizza on the parchment paper to the preheated pizza stone or baking sheet. Bake for 12-15 minutes, or until the crust is golden and the cheese is bubbly and slightly browned.

  5. Serve and Enjoy

    • Remove the pizza from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes before slicing. Serve hot and indulge in the carnivorous delight of the ultimate meat lover’s pizza.

Conclusion

With its hearty combination of savory meats and gooey cheese, the ultimate meat lover’s pizza is a carnivore’s dream come true. Whether you’re a fan of pepperoni, Italian sausage, bacon, ham, or ground beef, this pizza has it all. The crispy crust, flavorful sauce, and generous toppings make this pizza a standout dish that will impress even the most discerning meat lovers. So, gather your ingredients, preheat your oven, and get ready to savor every carnivorous bite of this indulgent masterpiece. It’s time to elevate your pizza game and treat yourself to the ultimate meat lover’s pizza experience.
