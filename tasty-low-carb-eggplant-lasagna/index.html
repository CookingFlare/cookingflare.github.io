Tasty Low Carb Eggplant Lasagna


Are you looking for a delicious and healthy twist on traditional lasagna? Look no further! This tasty low carb eggplant lasagna is a fantastic alternative to the classic pasta dish. Packed full of flavor and nutrients, it’s the perfect meal for anyone watching their carb intake or simply looking to incorporate more vegetables into their diet.

Ingredients:

  • 2 medium-sized eggplants
  • 1 pound ground turkey or beef
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can (14 oz) crushed tomatoes
  • 1 can (6 oz) tomato paste
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • Fresh basil for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the Oven: Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).

  2. Prepare the Eggplant: Slice the eggplants lengthwise into 1/4-inch thick slices. Sprinkle the slices with salt and let them sit for 10-15 minutes to draw out the moisture. Rinse the salt off the eggplant slices and pat them dry with a paper towel.

  3. Cook the Meat Sauce: In a large skillet, brown the ground turkey or beef over medium heat. Add the chopped onion and garlic, cooking until the onion is soft and translucent. Stir in the crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, oregano, basil, salt, and pepper. Simmer for 10-15 minutes.

  4. Assemble the Lasagna: In a greased 9x13 inch baking dish, spread a thin layer of the meat sauce. Arrange a layer of eggplant slices on top of the sauce. Sprinkle with a layer of mozzarella and Parmesan cheese. Repeat the layers, finishing with a final layer of meat sauce and cheese on top.

  5. Bake: Cover the baking dish with foil and bake in the preheated oven for 25 minutes. Remove the foil and bake for an additional 25 minutes, or until the cheese is golden and bubbly.

  6. Let it Rest: Allow the lasagna to rest for 10-15 minutes before slicing. Garnish with fresh basil before serving.

Why Choose Low Carb Eggplant Lasagna?

This low carb eggplant lasagna is not only delicious but also offers a range of health benefits. By replacing traditional lasagna noodles with eggplant, you significantly reduce the carb content while increasing the fiber and nutrient content. Eggplant is a great source of antioxidants, vitamins, and minerals, making it a nutritious addition to any meal.

Additionally, by using lean ground turkey or beef, you cut down on saturated fat, making this dish heart-healthy and suitable for those looking to manage their weight or cholesterol levels.

Tips for Success:

  • Ensure the eggplant slices are evenly sliced for consistent layering.
  • Don’t skip the salting step for the eggplant, as it helps to draw out excess moisture and bitterness.
  • Let the lasagna rest before serving to allow the flavors to meld together.

In Conclusion

This tasty low carb eggplant lasagna is a crowd-pleaser that’s perfect for family dinners, entertaining guests, or meal prepping for the week ahead. It’s a versatile dish that can be adapted to suit various dietary preferences, making it a go-to recipe for many occasions. Give it a try and enjoy the wonderful flavors of this healthy and satisfying low carb alternative to traditional lasagna.
