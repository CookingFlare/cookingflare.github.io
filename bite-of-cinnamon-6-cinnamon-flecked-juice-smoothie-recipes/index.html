Bite of Cinnamon: 6 Cinnamon-flecked Juice & Smoothie Recipes


Ah, cinnamon - that warm, delightful spice that adds a delightful twist to both sweet and savory dishes. Today, we have some exciting news for you: a collection of juice and smoothie recipes that put this wonderful spice to great use! Say hello to a world of flavors with our amazing cinnamon-flecked concoctions. Grab your juicer or blender, and let’s get some ingredients together.

1. Cinnamon Apple Booster

This refreshing boost of nutrients is sure to be a favorite for the morning rush. The fusion of sweet apples and invigorating cinnamon will bring a new zeal to your mornings. Don’t forget to add a pinch of ginger for that extra warmth.

Ingredients:

  • 2 apples (preferably high-quality, crisp Regina or Braeburn)
  • ½ medium-sized beetroot
  • 1 inch ginger
  • ¼ teaspoon of cinnamon powder
  • ¼ cup almond milk (optional, for more creaminess)
  • Water, if needed

Instructions:

  1. Rinse your apples and beetroot thoroughly. Peel and chop them into smaller pieces.
  2. Do the same with ginger.
  3. Get your juicer or blender ready and combine apple, beetroot, and ginger in it.
  4. Add cinnamon powder and almond milk.
  5. Blend or juice everything together, adjusting the consistency with water if needed.
  6. Sip, relish, and enjoy your invigorating Cinnamon Apple Booster!

2. Cinnamon Twist Green Smoothie

An eccentric twist on the classic green smoothie, this recipe is one-of-a-kind! With spinach, kale, ripe bananas, and a dash of cinnamon, we challenge you to resist this unique concoction.

Ingredients:

  • 1 banana
  • A handful of spinach leaves
  • A handful of kale leaves
  • ½ cup of unsweetened soy milk
  • 1 teaspoon of cinnamon
  • A few ice cubes (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Wash and dry your spinach leaves and kale. Roughly chop them.
  2. Transfer kale and spinach to your blender.
  3. Peel and slice banana and add it to the blender as well.
  4. Pour in unsweetened soy milk and sprinkle cinnamon over the mix.
  5. Pulse everything together till you reach your desired consistency; add ice cubes if you prefer a thicker, frostier smoothie.
  6. Blend till smooth and let the flavors blend.

3. Winter Warmer – Immunity Booster Juice

Keep the chills away this winter with our immune-boosting juice! Loaded with citrusy goodness and heaps of cinnamon, this concoction will leave you feeling cozy and refreshed.

Ingredients:

  • 1 inch piece of ginger
  • 1 large carrot
  • 1 medium-sized lemon
  • 2 medium-sized oranges
  • ¼ teaspoon of cinnamon powder
  • ½ cup of freshly brewed, cooled chamomile tea

Instructions:

  1. Gather all your ingredients and, using a sharp knife, peel the ginger, lemon, and oranges.
  2. Grate the ginger and cut the lemon and oranges into smaller pieces.
  3. Add carrot, ginger, lemon, and oranges into your juicer.
  4. Collect the juice in a large glass and add cinnamon powder.
  5. Pour in chamomile tea and mix everything well.
  6. Relish the warmth and goodness of nature’s potions.

4. Sweet Cinnamon Berry Spartans

Get your spartans ready with the Sweet Cinnamon Berry Spartans - a lush mix of berries, banana, and cinnamon, garnished with a hint of honey. Unleash your inner warrior!

Ingredients:

  • 1 banana
  • ¼ cup of mixed berries (like strawberries, blueberries, and raspberries)
  • ½ cup of unsweetened almond milk
  • ½ teaspoon of cinnamon powder
  • A drizzle of honey
  • A handful of ice cubes

Instructions:

  1. Peel and slice the banana.
  2. Toss the banana with mixed berries into your blender.
  3. Pour in almond milk and sprinkle cinnamon.
  4. Blend everything together until smooth. For a slushier feel, add some ice cubes.
  5. Drizzle honey as per your taste and blend again.
  6. Server your Sweet Cinnamon Berry Spartans in a tall glass.

5. Cinnamon Grape Smoothie

Unwind with a glass of our Cinnamon Grape Smoothie, a blend of sweet grapes, zingy lime juice, and an Indian quintessential- cinnamon. The result is a delightful concoction perfect for winding down.

Ingredients:

  • A cup of red or green grapes
  • ½ cup of unsweetened coconut milk
  • 1 teaspoon of lime juice
  • 1 teaspoon of honey
  • A pinch of cinnamon

Instructions:

  1. Carefully wash and dry the grapes. Remove the stems.
  2. Put grapes in the blender along with lime juice, honey, and cinnamon.
  3. Pour in unsweetened coconut milk.
  4. Puree everything together, keeping the consistency as thick as possible for a smoother taste.
  5. Give it a taste. Adjust honey or lime juice if needed.
  6. Pour your refreshing Cinnamon Grape Smoothie into a glass and enjoy!

6. Cinnamartic Mango Sunrise

A symphony of mango and pineapple sweetness merrily dances with cinnamon spice in the Cinnamartic Mango Sunrise. The perfect amalgamation of flavors to brighten up your day.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup frozen mango chunks
  • 1 cup frozen pineapple chunks
  • ½ cup unsweetened almond milk
  • 1 teaspoon of lime juice
  • 1 teaspoon of cinnamon

Instructions:

  1. Put frozen mango chunks, pineapple chunks, almond milk, lime juice, and cinnamon into your blender.
  2. Blend everything till smooth; if you prefer it a little more runny, you may add more almond milk.
  3. Taste the smoothie and adjust the flavors according to your preference.
  4. Serve in a tall glass and indulge in the beautiful Cinnamartic Mango Sunrise!

There you have it! Six delicious cinnamon-flecked juice and smoothie recipes to indulge your taste buds and tantalize your senses. Don’t be afraid to experiment and tweak these recipes to suit your taste, after all, cooking is an art that speaks directly to the heart! Enjoy your gastronomic adventure and find your new favorite blend with these cinnamon-inspired concoctions. Happy blending!
