10 Delicious Vegan and Vegetarian Pasta Salad Recipes


Pasta salads are the perfect blend of flavors, textures, and colors that fit every occasion—from picnics to weeknight dinners or festive gatherings. Whether you’re seeking a fresh dish bursting with summer produce or a protein-packed meal to satisfy your hunger, these 10 vegan and vegetarian pasta salad recipes are sure to inspire your culinary adventures.

1. Mediterranean Orzo Pasta Salad

This vibrant pasta salad combines the best Mediterranean flavors into one dish. Made with orzo pasta, cherry tomatoes, Kalamata olives, cucumbers, and fresh parsley, it’s light yet satisfying. Toss everything with a zesty lemon-olive oil dressing and sprinkle lightly with vegan feta cheese. The tangy dressing pairs beautifully with the crunch of fresh vegetables and the soft texture of the orzo.

Ingredients:

  • Orzo pasta, cooked and cooled
  • Cherry tomatoes, halved
  • Cucumbers, diced
  • Kalamata olives, pitted and sliced
  • Fresh parsley, chopped
  • Lemon-olive oil dressing
  • Optional: vegan feta cheese for garnish

Pro Tip:

Make extra dressing for the salad to absorb overnight for an even more robust flavor.

2. Creamy Avocado-Pesto Penne Salad

If you’re a fan of bold and creamy flavors, this avocado-pesto pasta salad will win your heart. Penne is coated in a homemade avocado pesto sauce and topped with roasted cherry tomatoes and toasted pine nuts for added texture. Its creamy consistency comes naturally, thanks to ripe avocados blended with basil, garlic, lemon juice, and a touch of nutritional yeast.

Ingredients:

  • Penne pasta, cooked and drained
  • Avocado, ripe
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • Garlic clove, minced
  • Lemon juice
  • Nutritional yeast
  • Roasted cherry tomatoes
  • Pine nuts, toasted

Pro Tip:

Serve chilled, or let it sit at room temperature for 15 minutes after refrigerating—it enhances the creaminess.

3. Southwestern Pasta Salad with Black Beans

This pasta salad features bold and smoky Southwestern flavors. Rotini pasta is joined by black beans, corn kernels, diced red bell peppers, and fresh cilantro. The dressing is a spicy lime-cumin vinaigrette that ties all the ingredients together for a refreshing yet hearty salad.

Ingredients:

  • Rotini pasta, cooked and cooled
  • Black beans, rinsed and drained
  • Corn kernels
  • Red bell peppers, diced
  • Fresh cilantro, chopped
  • Lime-cumin vinaigrette

Pro Tip:

Add sliced avocado just before serving for an extra creamy finish.

4. Roasted Veggie Rainbow Pasta Salad

Loaded with colorful roasted veggies, this pasta salad is as visually stunning as it is delicious. Toss bowtie pasta with caramelized zucchini, bell peppers, cherry tomatoes, and red onions, and finish with a balsamic glaze drizzle. It’s a great way to use seasonal vegetables.

Ingredients:

  • Bowtie pasta, cooked and cooled
  • Zucchini, roasted
  • Bell peppers, roasted
  • Cherry tomatoes, roasted
  • Red onions, roasted
  • Balsamic glaze or dressing

Pro Tip:

Roast vegetables in olive oil, garlic powder, and a pinch of smoked paprika for extra depth of flavor.

5. Thai Peanut Noodle Salad

For those who love a fusion of flavors, this Thai-inspired pasta salad delivers a punch of sweet, savory, and tangy notes. Spaghetti or linguine is tossed with julienned carrots, red cabbage, green onions, and a rich peanut sauce. Topped with chopped peanuts and sesame seeds, this dish is a crowd-pleaser.

Ingredients:

  • Spaghetti or linguine
  • Carrots, julienned
  • Red cabbage, shredded
  • Green onions, thinly sliced
  • Peanut sauce
  • Chopped peanuts and sesame seeds for garnish

Pro Tip:

Include a splash of soy sauce if you prefer a saltier kick in your peanut sauce.

6. Caprese-Inspired Pasta Salad

Capture the essence of Italy with this Caprese-inspired pasta salad. Cherry tomatoes, fresh basil leaves, and vegan mozzarella alternative are paired with rotini pasta and tossed in a garlic-infused olive oil dressing. It’s simple yet packed with flavor.

Ingredients:

  • Rotini pasta, cooked and cooled
  • Cherry tomatoes, halved
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • Vegan mozzarella alternative
  • Garlic-infused olive oil dressing

Pro Tip:

For added zest, incorporate a drizzle of balsamic reduction.

7. Harvest Pumpkin Pasta Salad

Perfect for autumn, this pasta salad celebrates pumpkin season with roasted butternut squash, spinach, and dried cranberries. The creamy tahini dressing complements the sweetness of the squash and the tanginess of the cranberries.

Ingredients:

  • Shell or elbow pasta
  • Roasted butternut squash
  • Fresh spinach leaves
  • Dried cranberries
  • Tahini dressing with maple syrup

Pro Tip:

Top with toasted pumpkin seeds for a crunchy garnish.

8. Lemon-Tahini Zoodles Pasta Salad

For a lighter alternative to traditional pasta, zucchini noodles (zoodles) shine in this citrusy lemon-tahini pasta salad. Add cherry tomatoes, cucumbers, and red onions for crunch, then toss with a creamy lemon-tahini dressing.

Ingredients:

  • Zucchini noodles
  • Cherry tomatoes, halved
  • Cucumbers, diced
  • Red onions, thinly sliced
  • Lemon-tahini dressing

Pro Tip:

Blanch the zoodles briefly in boiling water to soften slightly without compromising their crunch.

9. Spinach and Chickpea Pasta Salad

High in protein and nutrients, this spinach and chickpea pasta salad is ideal for a satisfying lunch or dinner. Combine classic rotini pasta with fresh spinach, chickpeas, sun-dried tomatoes, and a tangy vinaigrette.

Ingredients:

  • Rotini pasta
  • Fresh spinach leaves
  • Chickpeas, rinsed and drained
  • Sun-dried tomatoes, sliced
  • Vinaigrette dressing

Pro Tip:

Sprinkle with nutritional yeast for a cheesy flavor that complements the salad’s natural nuttiness.

10. Tropical Pineapple-Coconut Pasta Salad

Bring a bit of sunshine to your table with this tropical-inspired fruity pasta salad. Bowtie pasta is mixed with fresh pineapple chunks, shredded coconut, and bright green peas, all tossed in a creamy coconut-lime dressing.

Ingredients:

  • Bowtie pasta, cooked and cooled
  • Fresh pineapple chunks
  • Shredded coconut
  • Green peas, boiled
  • Coconut-lime dressing

Pro Tip:

Chill the pasta salad for at least an hour before serving to let tropical flavors meld together.

Whether you’re seeking a crowd-pleaser for your next barbecue, a quick lunch that packs in nutrients, or a jazzy dinner option, these vegan and vegetarian pasta salad recipes are guaranteed to elevate your menu. Each dish invites you to play with flavors, experiment with fresh ingredients, and whip up meals that are not only delicious but also satisfying for everyone at the table. So roll up your sleeves, and let the pasta creations begin!
