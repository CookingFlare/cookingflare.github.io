Budget-Friendly Tofu Recipes for Plant-Based Meals


If you’re looking for budget-friendly and nutritious plant-based meals, tofu is a great ingredient to incorporate into your cooking. Tofu, also known as bean curd, is a versatile and affordable source of protein that can be used in a variety of dishes. Whether you’re a long-time tofu enthusiast or just starting to explore plant-based cooking, these budget-friendly tofu recipes are sure to tantalize your taste buds and keep your wallet happy.

Crispy Baked Tofu Nuggets

Ingredients:

  • 1 block of extra-firm tofu
  • 1/4 cup cornstarch
  • 1/4 cup nutritional yeast
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon smoked paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • Cooking spray

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 400°F (200°C) and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
  2. Drain the tofu and press it between paper towels to remove excess moisture. Cut the tofu into bite-sized nuggets.
  3. In a shallow bowl, combine the cornstarch, nutritional yeast, garlic powder, smoked paprika, and salt.
  4. Coat each tofu nugget in the cornstarch mixture, then place them on the prepared baking sheet.
  5. Lightly spray the nuggets with cooking spray.
  6. Bake for 25-30 minutes, flipping the nuggets halfway through, until they are golden and crispy.

These crispy baked tofu nuggets are perfect for dipping in your favorite sauce or adding to salads and bowls.

Tofu Stir-Fry with Mixed Vegetables

Ingredients:

  • 1 block of firm tofu
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon hoisin sauce
  • 1 tablespoon rice vinegar
  • 1 teaspoon sesame oil
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • 1 bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 cup broccoli florets
  • 1 cup snap peas
  • 3-4 green onions, chopped
  • Cooked rice or noodles for serving

Instructions:

  1. Press the tofu to remove excess water, then cut it into cubes.
  2. In a small bowl, whisk together the soy sauce, hoisin sauce, rice vinegar, and sesame oil.
  3. Heat the vegetable oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat. Add the tofu cubes and cook until they are golden and crispy.
  4. Add the bell pepper, broccoli, snap peas, and green onions to the skillet. Stir-fry for 5-7 minutes, or until the vegetables are tender-crisp.
  5. Pour the sauce over the tofu and vegetables, tossing to coat everything evenly.
  6. Serve the tofu stir-fry over cooked rice or noodles.

This tofu stir-fry is a colorful and flavorful way to enjoy a variety of vegetables with protein-packed tofu.

Tofu Scramble Breakfast Burritos

Ingredients:

  • 1 block of medium-firm tofu
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1/2 onion, diced
  • 1 bell pepper, diced
  • 2 cups spinach
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric
  • 1/2 teaspoon cumin
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 4 large flour tortillas
  • Salsa, avocado, and cilantro for serving

Instructions:

  1. Crumble the tofu into a skillet and sauté it in olive oil over medium heat.
  2. Add the diced onion and bell pepper to the skillet and cook until they are soft.
  3. Stir in the spinach, turmeric, cumin, salt, and pepper, cooking until the spinach is wilted.
  4. Warm the tortillas in a dry skillet or microwave.
  5. Divide the tofu scramble among the tortillas, and add salsa, avocado, and cilantro if desired.
  6. Roll up the tortillas into burritos and serve.

These tofu scramble breakfast burritos are a satisfying and protein-rich way to start your day.

Conclusion

Tofu is a fantastic ingredient for creating budget-friendly and nutritious plant-based meals. These versatile tofu recipes are not only affordable but also delicious, making it easy to incorporate more plant-based options into your weekly meal plan without breaking the bank. Whether you’re a dedicated vegan, curious flexitarian, or simply looking to try something new, these recipes are sure to impress. Enjoy experimenting with tofu in your kitchen and discovering the endless possibilities it offers for creating satisfying plant-based meals.
