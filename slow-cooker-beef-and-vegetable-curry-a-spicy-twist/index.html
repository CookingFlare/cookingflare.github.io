Slow Cooker Beef and Vegetable Curry: A Spicy Twist


If you’re a fan of hearty, flavorful dishes that practically cook themselves, then you’re in for a treat with this Slow Cooker Beef and Vegetable Curry recipe. This dish is a delightful fusion of tender beef, vibrant vegetables, and aromatic spices, all slowly simmered to perfection in a rich, fragrant curry sauce. The best part? You can set it and forget it, allowing the slow cooker to work its magic while you go about your day. Let’s dive into the details of this tantalizing recipe.

Ingredients

  • 2 lbs beef stew meat, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 1 large onion, finely chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1-inch piece of ginger, grated
  • 2 cups diced potatoes
  • 2 cups carrots, sliced
  • 1 red bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 cup frozen peas
  • 1 can (14 oz) coconut milk
  • 1 can (14 oz) diced tomatoes
  • 2 tbsp curry powder
  • 1 tsp cumin
  • 1 tsp turmeric
  • 1/2 tsp cinnamon
  • 1/4 tsp cayenne pepper (adjust to taste)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh cilantro for garnish

Instructions

  1. In a large bowl, combine the beef, onion, garlic, ginger, curry powder, cumin, turmeric, cinnamon, cayenne pepper, salt, and pepper. Toss until the beef is well coated with the spices.

  2. Transfer the beef mixture to the slow cooker. Add the diced potatoes, carrots, red bell pepper, frozen peas, coconut milk, and diced tomatoes. Gently stir to combine all the ingredients.

  3. Cover and cook on low for 7-8 hours, or on high for 3-4 hours, until the beef is tender and the vegetables are cooked through.

  4. Once the cooking time is complete, taste and adjust the seasoning if needed. If you prefer a thicker sauce, you can mix 2 tbsp of cornstarch with 1/4 cup of cold water and stir the mixture into the slow cooker. Cook on high for an additional 15-20 minutes, or until the sauce thickens.

  5. Serve the Slow Cooker Beef and Vegetable Curry over steamed rice or with warm naan bread. Garnish with fresh cilantro for a burst of color and added flavor.

A Spicy Twist

What sets this Slow Cooker Beef and Vegetable Curry apart is the bold and aromatic blend of spices. The curry powder, cumin, turmeric, and cinnamon create a warm, earthy base, while the cayenne pepper adds a fiery kick. The slow cooking process allows these spices to infuse the dish, resulting in a deep, complex flavor that will tantalize your taste buds.

The combination of tender beef and an assortment of colorful vegetables makes this curry not only delicious but also nutrient-rich. The addition of coconut milk lends a creamy texture and a hint of sweetness, balancing out the heat from the spices.

This recipe is perfect for busy weeknights when you want a comforting meal without the fuss. It’s also a great option for entertaining, as the slow cooker does all the work, allowing you to focus on other preparations.

So, whether you’re a curry aficionado or new to the world of slow cooking, this Slow Cooker Beef and Vegetable Curry is sure to become a favorite in your recipe repertoire. The enticing aroma that fills your kitchen as it cooks is just a preview of the incredible flavors that await you.

Give this recipe a try and savor the spicy twist it brings to your dinner table!

Conclusion

With its rich flavors, vibrant colors, and effortless preparation, this Slow Cooker Beef and Vegetable Curry is a standout dish that will impress your family and friends. Embrace the convenience of slow cooking and the irresistible allure of aromatic spices with this recipe. Let the slow cooker work its magic, and indulge in a satisfying and spicy meal that will leave everyone asking for seconds.
