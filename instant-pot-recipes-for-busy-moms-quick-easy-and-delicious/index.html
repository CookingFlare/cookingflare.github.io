Instant Pot Recipes for Busy Moms: Quick, Easy, and Delicious


Being a mom means juggling countless responsibilities – from managing school schedules and preparing meals to keeping the house organized and making sure everyone is happy. With so much to do in a single day, finding the time to cook wholesome meals can be overwhelming. Fortunately, the Instant Pot has become a lifesaver in many kitchens. This magical kitchen appliance allows you to prepare delicious, nutritious meals in a fraction of the time it would take with traditional methods.

Whether you’re a seasoned Instant Pot user or a beginner, this post is filled with simple, tasty, and quick Instant Pot recipes that will help busy moms save time and create meals that their families will love. These recipes cover a variety of meal types, from breakfast to dinner, and even desserts, ensuring that you can use your Instant Pot for every part of your day.

Why Choose Instant Pot Recipes?

The Instant Pot is more than just a pressure cooker. It’s a multi-functional device that combines multiple kitchen tools into one. With the ability to pressure cook, slow cook, sauté, steam, and even make yogurt, the Instant Pot helps streamline meal prep, making it an indispensable tool for busy families. Here are some reasons why it’s perfect for moms:

  1. Time-Saving: Instant Pot cuts down cooking times drastically. What would normally take hours can now be done in minutes, which is a huge advantage when you’re trying to prepare meals between work, school runs, and everything else on your to-do list.
  2. One-Pot Cooking: Clean-up becomes a breeze because you’re using one pot for the entire meal. No more washing multiple pots and pans after dinner.
  3. Versatility: From soups to stews, roasts, rice, and even desserts, the Instant Pot can cook nearly anything you can imagine.
  4. Hands-Off Cooking: Once you set up your Instant Pot, it does all the work. You can focus on other tasks or spend quality time with your family without worrying about monitoring the stove.
  5. Healthy Meals: The Instant Pot preserves nutrients and flavors, making it easier to create healthy, flavorful meals without losing the essential vitamins and minerals.

With these advantages in mind, let’s dive into some incredible Instant Pot recipes that will make meal prep a breeze for busy moms.

1. Instant Pot Chicken and Rice

This chicken and rice recipe is a family favorite. It’s easy, flavorful, and perfectly cooked in the Instant Pot.

Ingredients:

  • 2 chicken breasts (or thighs)
  • 1 cup long-grain white rice
  • 2 cups chicken broth
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1 teaspoon thyme
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil

Instructions:

  1. Set your Instant Pot to sauté mode. Once hot, add olive oil, chopped onion, and minced garlic. Sauté for 2-3 minutes until softened.
  2. Season the chicken breasts with paprika, thyme, salt, and pepper.
  3. Add the chicken breasts to the pot and sauté for another 2-3 minutes on each side to brown them.
  4. Add the rice and chicken broth. Stir to combine and ensure the rice is submerged.
  5. Close the lid and set the Instant Pot to pressure cook for 10 minutes.
  6. Once the cooking time is up, allow for a 10-minute natural release. Then, quick release the remaining pressure.
  7. Remove the chicken and fluff the rice with a fork. Serve the chicken over the rice, and enjoy!

2. Instant Pot Beef Stew

This hearty beef stew is a perfect dinner for chilly nights, and the Instant Pot ensures the beef becomes tender and juicy in no time.

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb beef stew meat, cubed
  • 4 potatoes, peeled and diced
  • 2 carrots, sliced
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 4 cups beef broth
  • 2 tablespoons tomato paste
  • 1 teaspoon dried rosemary
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 tablespoons flour (optional, for thickening)

Instructions:

  1. Set the Instant Pot to sauté mode. Add the beef stew meat and sear on all sides until browned, about 5 minutes. Remove the beef and set it aside.
  2. Add the chopped onion and garlic to the pot and sauté for 2-3 minutes until fragrant.
  3. Stir in the tomato paste and cook for 1 more minute.
  4. Return the beef to the pot and add the potatoes, carrots, beef broth, rosemary, thyme, salt, and pepper. Stir to combine.
  5. Close the lid and set the Instant Pot to pressure cook for 35 minutes.
  6. Once the cooking time is up, allow for a natural pressure release for 10 minutes, then quick release the remaining pressure.
  7. If you want a thicker stew, mix the flour with a little water to form a slurry, and stir it into the stew. Set the Instant Pot to sauté mode again and cook for another 5 minutes until thickened.
  8. Serve hot and enjoy a comforting, rich meal!

3. Instant Pot Mac and Cheese

Creamy mac and cheese is a crowd-pleaser, and with the Instant Pot, you can have it ready in less than 30 minutes.

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups elbow macaroni
  • 2 cups water
  • 1 cup milk
  • 2 cups shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Add the macaroni and water to the Instant Pot. Close the lid and set it to pressure cook for 4 minutes.
  2. Once the cooking time is up, quick release the pressure and carefully open the lid.
  3. Stir in the milk, butter, cheddar cheese, Parmesan cheese, salt, pepper, and garlic powder. Stir until everything is melted and creamy.
  4. Serve immediately for a rich, cheesy treat that everyone will love!

4. Instant Pot Chicken Fajitas

These chicken fajitas are perfect for busy nights when you want something quick and flavorful. The Instant Pot does all the work, and you can have a Mexican-inspired dinner ready in just 25 minutes.

Ingredients:

  • 2 chicken breasts, sliced
  • 1 bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 onion, sliced
  • 1 packet fajita seasoning (or homemade)
  • 1 cup chicken broth
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil

Instructions:

  1. Set the Instant Pot to sauté mode and add olive oil.
  2. Add the chicken slices and sauté for 3-4 minutes until the chicken starts to brown.
  3. Add the bell pepper, onion, fajita seasoning, and chicken broth. Stir to combine.
  4. Close the lid and set the Instant Pot to pressure cook for 8 minutes.
  5. Once the cooking time is up, quick release the pressure.
  6. Serve the chicken fajitas on tortillas, and top with your favorite toppings, such as sour cream, guacamole, or salsa.

5. Instant Pot Breakfast Casserole

Mornings can be rushed, but this Instant Pot breakfast casserole gives you a delicious and filling breakfast with minimal effort.

Ingredients:

  • 6 large eggs
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 cup shredded cheese
  • 1/2 cup cooked bacon or sausage, crumbled
  • 1/2 onion, chopped
  • 1 cup diced potatoes (fresh or frozen)
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper

Instructions:

  1. Grease the Instant Pot insert with cooking spray or butter.
  2. In a mixing bowl, whisk together the eggs, milk, salt, and pepper.
  3. Add the diced potatoes to the Instant Pot. Pour the egg mixture over the potatoes.
  4. Sprinkle the cheese, bacon (or sausage), and chopped onion over the top.
  5. Cover the Instant Pot with foil or a silicone lid.
  6. Set the Instant Pot to pressure cook for 20 minutes.
  7. Once done, allow a natural release for 5 minutes before quick releasing the remaining pressure.
  8. Remove the casserole from the Instant Pot and serve hot for a satisfying breakfast!

6. Instant Pot Chocolate Lava Cake

Craving something sweet? This rich and decadent chocolate lava cake can be made in your Instant Pot in less than 30 minutes.

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 4 ounces semisweet chocolate
  • 1/2 cup powdered sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • Pinch of salt

Instructions:

  1. Set the Instant Pot to sauté mode and melt the butter and chocolate together until smooth.
  2. Remove from heat and whisk in the powdered sugar, eggs, vanilla, flour, and salt until the batter is smooth.
  3. Grease ramekins and pour the batter into them, filling about halfway.
  4. Add 1/2 teaspoon of chocolate chips or a small piece of chocolate into the center of each ramekin.
  5. Add 1 cup of water to the Instant Pot and place the trivet inside.
  6. Set the ramekins on the trivet and close the lid.
  7. Set the Instant Pot to pressure cook for 8 minutes.
  8. Once done, allow for a 5-minute natural release before quick releasing the remaining pressure.
  9. Carefully remove the ramekins and let the lava cakes cool for a few minutes. Serve warm and enjoy the gooey chocolate center!

Final Thoughts

The Instant Pot is an invaluable tool for any busy mom who needs to prepare quick, easy, and delicious meals. These recipes are just a starting point—there are endless possibilities for what you can create in this multi-functional appliance. With just a little planning and the push of a button, you can have dinner on the table in no time. Whether you’re cooking for picky eaters or trying to get everyone to sit down for a meal, the Instant Pot makes it easier than ever to prepare healthy and tasty dishes that everyone will enjoy.

The best part? You can use your Instant Pot for a wide range of meals throughout the day—breakfast, lunch, dinner, and even dessert. So go ahead, make your life a little easier with these Instant Pot recipes and watch how they transform your cooking routine.
