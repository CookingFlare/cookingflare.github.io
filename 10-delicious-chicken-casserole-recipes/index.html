10 Delicious Chicken Casserole Recipes


Chicken casseroles are a comforting and versatile dish that can easily be tailored to suit any flavor profile. Whether you are preparing a meal for the whole family or looking to bring something delicious to a potluck, a chicken casserole is sure to please. These dishes combine tender chicken with creamy sauces, savory vegetables, and a delicious topping for a meal that’s both hearty and satisfying.

Here are ten chicken casserole recipes that are perfect for any occasion. From creamy and cheesy to light and flavorful, these casseroles will bring warmth and joy to your kitchen.

1. Classic Chicken and Rice Casserole

This comforting classic is the perfect weeknight dinner. The combination of tender chicken, fluffy rice, and a creamy sauce is simple yet satisfying.

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups cooked chicken, shredded
  • 1 cup rice, uncooked
  • 1 can cream of chicken soup
  • 1 ½ cups chicken broth
  • 1 small onion, diced
  • 1 cup frozen peas
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp salt
  • ½ tsp pepper
  • 1 cup shredded cheddar cheese

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. In a large bowl, mix together the cream of chicken soup, chicken broth, garlic powder, salt, and pepper.
  3. Add the uncooked rice, shredded chicken, peas, and diced onion. Stir until everything is well combined.
  4. Pour the mixture into a greased 9x13-inch baking dish.
  5. Cover the dish with foil and bake for 45 minutes.
  6. Remove the foil, sprinkle the shredded cheddar cheese on top, and bake for an additional 10-15 minutes, until the cheese is melted and bubbly.
  7. Serve warm and enjoy!

2. Chicken Alfredo Casserole

If you love the rich and creamy taste of Alfredo sauce, this casserole is for you. With tender chicken and pasta baked in a decadent Alfredo sauce, it’s a dish that’s sure to impress.

Ingredients:

  • 3 cups cooked chicken, shredded or cubed
  • 8 oz pasta (penne or rotini work well)
  • 1 jar (16 oz) Alfredo sauce
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • ½ cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 cup frozen broccoli florets
  • 1 tsp Italian seasoning
  • 1 tbsp olive oil

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook the pasta according to the package instructions, then drain and set aside.
  3. In a large bowl, combine the cooked chicken, Alfredo sauce, mozzarella cheese, Parmesan cheese, broccoli, and Italian seasoning. Stir in the cooked pasta until everything is evenly coated.
  4. Pour the mixture into a greased 9x13-inch baking dish.
  5. Drizzle with olive oil and bake for 20-25 minutes, or until the casserole is heated through and the cheese is melted.
  6. Let cool for a few minutes before serving.

3. Chicken and Broccoli Casserole

This is a healthy and hearty casserole that combines chicken, broccoli, and a creamy sauce for a well-balanced meal. The addition of breadcrumbs on top adds a nice crunch.

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups cooked chicken, shredded
  • 2 cups steamed broccoli florets
  • 1 can cream of mushroom soup
  • ½ cup sour cream
  • 1 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1 cup crushed crackers or breadcrumbs
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tbsp butter

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. In a large bowl, combine the cream of mushroom soup, sour cream, garlic powder, and ½ cup of shredded cheddar cheese.
  3. Add the shredded chicken and steamed broccoli to the mixture, and stir to combine.
  4. Pour the mixture into a greased 9x13-inch baking dish.
  5. In a small bowl, mix the crushed crackers or breadcrumbs with the remaining ½ cup of cheddar cheese. Sprinkle this mixture evenly over the casserole.
  6. Dot the top with small pieces of butter.
  7. Bake for 30-35 minutes or until bubbly and golden brown on top.
  8. Serve hot and enjoy!

4. Chicken and Spinach Lasagna Casserole

This twist on traditional lasagna is a deliciously healthy option. The layers of chicken, spinach, and ricotta cheese create a flavorful and satisfying casserole.

Ingredients:

  • 3 cups cooked chicken, shredded
  • 12 oz no-boil lasagna noodles
  • 1 cup ricotta cheese
  • 1 ½ cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1 jar marinara sauce
  • 1 cup frozen spinach, thawed and drained
  • 1 tsp dried basil
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • ½ cup grated Parmesan cheese

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. In a medium bowl, combine the ricotta cheese, 1 cup of mozzarella cheese, spinach, basil, and oregano.
  3. Spread a thin layer of marinara sauce on the bottom of a greased 9x13-inch baking dish.
  4. Layer the no-boil lasagna noodles on top of the sauce, followed by half of the chicken, and then half of the ricotta mixture.
  5. Repeat the layers, ending with a layer of marinara sauce on top.
  6. Sprinkle the remaining mozzarella cheese and Parmesan cheese over the top.
  7. Cover the dish with foil and bake for 25 minutes.
  8. Remove the foil and bake for an additional 10 minutes until the cheese is melted and bubbly.
  9. Let it cool for a few minutes before serving.

5. Buffalo Chicken Casserole

For those who love bold, spicy flavors, this buffalo chicken casserole is a game changer. It combines the classic heat of buffalo sauce with creamy cheese and tender chicken.

Ingredients:

  • 3 cups cooked chicken, shredded
  • 1 cup buffalo sauce
  • 1 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1 cup cream cheese, softened
  • ½ cup ranch dressing
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp onion powder
  • 2 cups cooked pasta (penne or rotini work well)

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. In a large bowl, combine the buffalo sauce, cream cheese, ranch dressing, garlic powder, and onion powder.
  3. Stir in the shredded chicken and cooked pasta until everything is well coated.
  4. Pour the mixture into a greased 9x13-inch baking dish.
  5. Sprinkle the shredded cheddar cheese on top.
  6. Bake for 25-30 minutes or until the casserole is heated through and the cheese is melted and bubbly.
  7. Let it cool for a few minutes before serving.

6. Cheesy Chicken and Potato Casserole

This casserole is loaded with creamy cheese and tender potatoes, making it a hearty meal perfect for a cozy dinner. The chicken adds protein and flavor to the dish.

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups cooked chicken, cubed
  • 4 large potatoes, peeled and diced
  • 1 can cream of chicken soup
  • 1 cup sour cream
  • 1 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp black pepper

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. In a large bowl, mix together the cream of chicken soup, sour cream, garlic powder, salt, and pepper.
  3. Add the diced potatoes, shredded chicken, and ½ cup of shredded cheddar cheese. Stir to combine.
  4. Pour the mixture into a greased 9x13-inch baking dish.
  5. Drizzle with olive oil and sprinkle the remaining ½ cup of cheddar cheese on top.
  6. Cover with foil and bake for 40 minutes.
  7. Remove the foil and bake for an additional 15-20 minutes until the potatoes are tender and the cheese is golden brown.
  8. Serve warm and enjoy!

7. Chicken Enchilada Casserole

If you love Mexican flavors, this chicken enchilada casserole is the perfect dinner. It’s loaded with layers of chicken, cheese, and enchilada sauce, all baked to perfection.

Ingredients:

  • 3 cups cooked chicken, shredded
  • 10 corn tortillas, cut into strips
  • 1 can red enchilada sauce
  • 1 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1 small onion, diced
  • 1 tsp cumin
  • 1 tsp chili powder
  • 1 cup sour cream (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. In a large bowl, combine the shredded chicken, diced onion, cumin, chili powder, and half of the enchilada sauce.
  3. Layer half of the tortilla strips in a greased 9x13-inch baking dish.
  4. Spread half of the chicken mixture on top, then sprinkle with half of the cheddar cheese and mozzarella cheese.
  5. Repeat the layers with the remaining tortillas, chicken mixture, and cheese.
  6. Pour the remaining enchilada sauce over the top and bake for 30 minutes.
  7. Remove from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes before serving.
  8. Serve with sour cream if desired.

8. Creamy Chicken and Mushroom Casserole

If you’re a fan of creamy and earthy flavors, this chicken and mushroom casserole is for you. The combination of tender chicken, sautéed mushrooms, and a creamy sauce is irresistible.

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups cooked chicken, shredded
  • 1 cup sliced mushrooms
  • 1 can cream of mushroom soup
  • ½ cup heavy cream
  • 1 cup shredded Swiss cheese
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp thyme
  • 1 tbsp parsley

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. In a skillet, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Sauté the mushrooms until soft, about 5 minutes.
  3. In a large bowl, combine the sautéed mushrooms, cream of mushroom soup, heavy cream, thyme, and parsley.
  4. Add the shredded chicken and mix until well combined.
  5. Pour the mixture into a greased 9x13-inch baking dish.
  6. Sprinkle the shredded Swiss cheese on top.
  7. Bake for 25-30 minutes, or until the casserole is heated through and the cheese is melted and bubbly.
  8. Let it cool for a few minutes before serving.

9. Chicken and Zucchini Casserole

This light and healthy casserole uses zucchini to add a fresh, crunchy texture. The chicken is complemented by a creamy sauce and topped with melted cheese.

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups cooked chicken, shredded
  • 2 zucchinis, sliced thinly
  • 1 cup cream of chicken soup
  • ½ cup sour cream
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp dried basil

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. In a large bowl, combine the cream of chicken soup, sour cream, garlic powder, and basil.
  3. Add the shredded chicken and zucchini, and stir to combine.
  4. Pour the mixture into a greased 9x13-inch baking dish.
  5. Sprinkle the shredded mozzarella cheese on top.
  6. Bake for 30-35 minutes, until the casserole is bubbly and the cheese is golden brown.
  7. Let it cool for a few minutes before serving.

10. Chicken Caesar Casserole

For fans of Caesar salad, this casserole brings all the flavors you love into a warm, comforting dish. The creamy Caesar dressing, chicken, and crunchy croutons make this dish irresistible.

Ingredients:

  • 3 cups cooked chicken, shredded
  • 3 cups cooked pasta (rotini or penne)
  • 1 cup Caesar dressing
  • 1 cup shredded Parmesan cheese
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1 ½ cups croutons

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. In a large bowl, combine the shredded chicken, cooked pasta, and Caesar dressing.
  3. Stir in half of the Parmesan cheese and half of the mozzarella cheese.
  4. Pour the mixture into a greased 9x13-inch baking dish.
  5. Sprinkle the remaining cheeses over the top and add the croutons for crunch.
  6. Bake for 20-25 minutes, or until the casserole is golden and bubbly.
  7. Let cool for a few minutes before serving.

These 10 delicious chicken casserole recipes offer something for everyone. Whether you prefer a creamy, cheesy dish or something light and healthy, there’s a casserole on this list for every taste. Try one (or all) of these recipes to enjoy comforting, satisfying meals with minimal effort!
