Light and Fresh Low Carb Mediterranean Chicken Salad


Are you in the mood for a light and refreshing meal that’s bursting with Mediterranean flavors? Look no further than this delightful Low Carb Mediterranean Chicken Salad. Packed with protein, healthy fats, and vibrant vegetables, this salad is not only delicious but also perfect for those following a low-carb lifestyle. Let’s dive into the recipe and discover how to create this culinary masterpiece in your own kitchen.

Ingredients

For the Chicken:

  • 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste

For the Salad:

  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1 cucumber, diced
  • 1/2 red onion, thinly sliced
  • 1/2 cup Kalamata olives, pitted and halved
  • 1/4 cup crumbled feta cheese
  • 1/4 cup fresh parsley, chopped

For the Dressing:

  • 1/4 cup extra virgin olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons red wine vinegar
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

Step 1: Prepare the Chicken

  1. Preheat your grill or grill pan over medium-high heat.
  2. In a small bowl, combine the olive oil, dried oregano, salt, and pepper. Rub this mixture evenly over the chicken breasts.
  3. Grill the chicken for 6-7 minutes per side, or until cooked through. Once done, set the chicken aside to cool.

Step 2: Assemble the Salad

  1. In a large bowl, combine the cherry tomatoes, cucumber, red onion, Kalamata olives, feta cheese, and fresh parsley.
  2. Once the chicken has cooled, slice it into bite-sized pieces and add it to the bowl with the salad ingredients.

Step 3: Prepare the Dressing

  1. In a small jar, combine the extra virgin olive oil, red wine vinegar, minced garlic, dried oregano, salt, and pepper. Shake the jar vigorously until the dressing is well combined.

Step 4: Toss and Serve

  1. Drizzle the dressing over the salad and gently toss everything together until the salad is evenly coated with the dressing.
  2. Divide the salad into individual serving bowls and garnish with an extra sprinkle of feta cheese and a few fresh parsley leaves.

Why You’ll Love This Salad

This Low Carb Mediterranean Chicken Salad is a celebration of fresh, wholesome ingredients that come together to create a symphony of flavors and textures. The tender grilled chicken pairs beautifully with the crisp vegetables, briny olives, and tangy feta cheese, all brought together by the vibrant and zesty dressing. Not only is this salad a feast for the taste buds, but it’s also a nutritional powerhouse, providing a generous dose of protein, healthy fats, and essential vitamins and minerals.

Final Thoughts

Whether you’re looking for a quick and satisfying weeknight dinner or a flavorful dish to bring to a summer potluck, this Low Carb Mediterranean Chicken Salad is sure to impress. With its colorful presentation and irresistible taste, it’s a versatile recipe that can easily become a staple in your culinary repertoire. So, gather your ingredients, embrace the Mediterranean spirit, and indulge in the delightful flavors of this sensational salad. Cheers to good food and good health!
