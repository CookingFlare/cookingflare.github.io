Instant Pot Chicken and Mushroom Stroganoff Recipe: A Creamy and Rich Option


If you’re looking for a delicious and comforting meal that you can whip up in no time, then this Instant Pot Chicken and Mushroom Stroganoff recipe is perfect for you. With its creamy and rich flavors, it’s guaranteed to satisfy your taste buds and leave you wanting more. Plus, with the convenience of the Instant Pot, you’ll have dinner on the table in a flash.

Ingredients

  • 1.5 pounds boneless, skinless chicken breasts, cubed
  • 8 ounces cremini mushrooms, sliced
  • 1 medium onion, diced
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 cups chicken broth
  • 1 cup sour cream
  • 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley, chopped (for garnish)

Instructions

  1. Set your Instant Pot to the sauté function and heat the olive oil. Add the diced onion and minced garlic, and sauté until fragrant and translucent.

  2. Add the cubed chicken breasts to the pot, along with the dried thyme, paprika, salt, and pepper. Sauté until the chicken is browned on all sides.

  3. Push the chicken and onions to one side of the pot and add the sliced mushrooms. Sauté the mushrooms until they release their moisture and start to brown.

  4. In a separate bowl, whisk together the chicken broth, sour cream, and flour until well combined. Pour the mixture into the Instant Pot, making sure to scrape up any browned bits from the bottom of the pot.

  5. Secure the lid on the Instant Pot and set the pressure release valve to the sealing position. Cook on high pressure for 8 minutes.

  6. Once the cooking time is complete, allow for a natural pressure release for 5 minutes, then carefully switch the pressure release valve to the venting position to release any remaining pressure.

  7. Open the Instant Pot and give the stroganoff a good stir. The sauce should thicken up nicely. If it’s not thick enough, you can set the Instant Pot to the sauté function and cook for a few more minutes, stirring occasionally, until desired thickness is reached.

  8. Serve the Instant Pot Chicken and Mushroom Stroganoff over cooked egg noodles, rice, or mashed potatoes. Garnish with fresh parsley.

Tips and Variations

  • For a twist on the classic stroganoff flavor, you can add a splash of white wine to the Instant Pot after sautéing the mushrooms.

  • If you prefer a creamier consistency, you can increase the amount of sour cream in the recipe.

  • Feel free to customize this recipe by adding your favorite herbs and spices. Some popular additions include rosemary, parsley, or even a touch of smoked paprika.

  • This recipe can easily be doubled or halved depending on the number of servings you need.

  • Leftovers can be stored in an airtight container in the refrigerator for up to 3 days. Simply reheat in the microwave or on the stovetop.

Enjoy this Instant Pot Chicken and Mushroom Stroganoff as a hearty weeknight dinner or impress your guests with its rich and creamy flavors at your next dinner party. Either way, this recipe is sure to become a favorite in your kitchen. Happy cooking!
