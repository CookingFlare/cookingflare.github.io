Baked Cod with Lemon and Garlic Recipe


When it comes to easy and flavorful seafood dishes, baked cod with lemon and garlic is a go-to recipe that never disappoints. This dish is not only quick and simple to prepare, but it also delivers a burst of fresh and zesty flavors that will leave your taste buds dancing. Whether you’re a seafood enthusiast or looking to incorporate more fish into your diet, this recipe is a winner. Let’s dive into the details of creating this delectable dish.

Ingredients:

  • 4 fresh cod fillets
  • 4 tablespoons of unsalted butter
  • 4 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 lemon, juiced and zested
  • 2 tablespoons of fresh parsley, chopped
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the Oven: Begin by preheating your oven to 400°F (200°C). This ensures that the oven is ready to perfectly bake the cod to tender perfection.

  2. Prepare the Cod: Pat the cod fillets dry with a paper towel and place them in a baking dish that has been lightly greased to prevent sticking.

  3. Create the Lemon Garlic Butter: In a small saucepan, melt the unsalted butter over low heat. Add the minced garlic and sauté for a minute until fragrant. Stir in the lemon juice and zest, then remove the saucepan from the heat. This aromatic lemon garlic butter will be the star of the dish.

  4. Season the Cod: Drizzle the lemon garlic butter over the cod fillets, ensuring each fillet is generously coated. Season the fillets with salt and pepper to taste, allowing the flavors to meld together.

  5. Bake to Perfection: Place the baking dish in the preheated oven and bake the cod for about 15-20 minutes, or until it easily flakes with a fork. The cooking time may vary depending on the thickness of the fillets, so keep an eye on them to prevent overcooking.

  6. Garnish and Serve: Once the cod is beautifully baked, sprinkle the fresh chopped parsley over the fillets. The vibrant green color of the parsley adds a lovely visual appeal and an extra layer of flavor. Serve the baked cod with your choice of sides, such as steamed vegetables or a light salad.

The result? Tender, flaky cod infused with the bright flavors of lemon and garlic, all enveloped in a luscious buttery sauce. This dish is a celebration of simplicity and quality ingredients, making it a delightful addition to any meal rotation.

Health Benefits:

Aside from its delightful taste, baked cod with lemon and garlic also offers a range of health benefits. Cod is a lean, mild-flavored fish that is a good source of high-quality protein and essential nutrients such as vitamin B12 and omega-3 fatty acids. The addition of fresh lemon and garlic not only enhances the flavor but also contributes to the dish’s nutritional value. Garlic is known for its potential health benefits, including immune-boosting properties, while lemon adds a dose of vitamin C and antioxidants.

Serving Suggestions:

This baked cod dish pairs beautifully with a variety of sides. Consider serving it with a vibrant quinoa salad, roasted asparagus, or a medley of sautéed summer vegetables. The versatility of this recipe makes it an ideal choice for both casual weeknight dinners and special occasions.

In conclusion, the baked cod with lemon and garlic recipe is a testament to the magic that can be achieved with a handful of simple ingredients. Its ease of preparation, vibrant flavors, and healthful qualities make it a standout dish that is sure to become a staple in your culinary repertoire. Give it a try and savor the delightful marriage of flavors in every bite.
