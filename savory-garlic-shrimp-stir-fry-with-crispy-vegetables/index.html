Savory Garlic Shrimp Stir Fry with Crispy Vegetables


If you’re looking for a quick and delicious meal that’s bursting with flavor, then this savory garlic shrimp stir fry with crispy vegetables is just what you need. Packed with the goodness of fresh shrimp, a medley of colorful vegetables, and a mouthwatering garlic sauce, this stir fry is a surefire way to satisfy your cravings for a wholesome yet indulgent dish. Let’s dive into the recipe and discover how to create this delightful culinary masterpiece in the comfort of your own kitchen.

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound of large shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 2 tablespoons of olive oil
  • 4 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon of grated ginger
  • 1 red bell pepper, thinly sliced
  • 1 yellow bell pepper, thinly sliced
  • 1 cup of snap peas
  • 1 cup of broccoli florets
  • 1 carrot, julienned
  • 3 green onions, chopped
  • 3 tablespoons of low-sodium soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon of oyster sauce
  • 1 teaspoon of sesame oil
  • 1 teaspoon of cornstarch
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Cooked rice or noodles for serving

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the Shrimp:

    • In a large bowl, season the shrimp with salt and pepper. Set aside while you prepare the vegetables.

  2. Make the Garlic Sauce:

    • In a small bowl, whisk together the soy sauce, oyster sauce, sesame oil, and cornstarch. Set the sauce aside for later use.

  3. Stir Fry the Vegetables:

    • Heat 1 tablespoon of olive oil in a wok or large skillet over medium-high heat.
    • Add the minced garlic and grated ginger, and sauté for about 30 seconds until fragrant.
    • Add the bell peppers, snap peas, broccoli, and carrot to the wok. Stir-fry the vegetables for 3-4 minutes until they are tender-crisp. Transfer the vegetables to a plate.

  4. Cook the Shrimp:

    • In the same wok, add the remaining tablespoon of olive oil.
    • Add the seasoned shrimp to the wok and cook for 2-3 minutes until they turn pink and opaque.

  5. Combine and Finish the Dish:

    • Return the cooked vegetables to the wok with the shrimp.
    • Pour the prepared garlic sauce over the shrimp and vegetables.
    • Toss everything together until the shrimp and vegetables are evenly coated with the sauce.
    • Stir in the chopped green onions and cook for an additional 1-2 minutes.

  6. Serve and Enjoy:

    • Serve the savory garlic shrimp stir fry over a bed of steamed rice or noodles.
    • Garnish with additional green onions or a sprinkle of sesame seeds, if desired.

Tips and Variations:

  • For an extra kick of heat, add a pinch of red pepper flakes or a drizzle of sriracha to the garlic sauce.
  • Experiment with different vegetables such as snow peas, baby corn, or water chestnuts to customize the stir fry to your taste.
  • You can substitute the shrimp with chicken, beef, or tofu for a delightful variation of this dish.

This savory garlic shrimp stir fry with crispy vegetables is a delightful combination of succulent seafood, vibrant vegetables, and a delectable garlic-infused sauce. It’s a versatile recipe that can be adapted to suit your preferences, making it an ideal choice for a satisfying weeknight dinner or a special occasion meal. Embrace the flavors of this irresistible stir fry and elevate your culinary repertoire with this simple yet impressive dish.
