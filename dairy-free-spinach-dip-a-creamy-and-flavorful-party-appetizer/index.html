Dairy-Free Spinach Dip: A Creamy and Flavorful Party Appetizer


If you’re looking for a delicious and crowd-pleasing appetizer that’s dairy-free, look no further than this creamy spinach dip recipe. Perfect for parties, gatherings, or just a cozy night in, this dairy-free spinach dip is packed with flavor and texture that will have your guests coming back for more.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup raw cashews, soaked in water for 4-6 hours or overnight
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 (10-ounce) package frozen chopped spinach, thawed and excess water squeezed out
  • 1 can (14 ounces) artichoke hearts, drained and chopped
  • 1/4 cup nutritional yeast
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground nutmeg
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 1/2 cup unsweetened almond milk
  • 1/4 cup dairy-free mayonnaise
  • Fresh parsley, for garnish
  • Sliced vegetables, crackers, or tortilla chips, for serving

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. In a food processor or high-powered blender, combine the soaked cashews and almond milk. Blend until smooth and creamy. Set aside.
  3. In a large skillet, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the chopped onion and sauté until translucent, about 5 minutes. Add the minced garlic and cook for an additional 2 minutes.
  4. Stir in the chopped spinach, artichoke hearts, nutritional yeast, nutmeg, salt, and black pepper. Cook for 3-4 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  5. Add the cashew cream, lemon juice, and dairy-free mayonnaise to the skillet. Stir well to combine all the ingredients.
  6. Transfer the mixture to a baking dish and smooth the top with a spatula.
  7. Bake for 20-25 minutes, or until the edges are golden and the dip is heated through.
  8. Remove from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes before serving.
  9. Garnish with fresh parsley and serve with sliced vegetables, crackers, or tortilla chips.

Tips and Variations

  • For a spicy kick, add a pinch of cayenne pepper or a few dashes of hot sauce to the dip.
  • If you prefer a smoother texture, you can blend the spinach dip in a food processor or blender before transferring it to the baking dish.
  • Feel free to customize the dip with your favorite herbs and spices, such as dill, basil, or smoked paprika.

The Perfect Party Appetizer

This dairy-free spinach dip is a versatile and flavorful appetizer that will please vegans, dairy-free individuals, and omnivores alike. Its creamy texture and savory taste make it a hit at any gathering, from casual get-togethers to elegant dinner parties. Plus, it’s easy to make ahead of time and can be reheated when you’re ready to serve, making it a stress-free option for entertaining.

Whether you’re hosting a game night, a holiday celebration, or just craving a savory snack, this dairy-free spinach dip is sure to be a crowd favorite. So, gather your ingredients, whip up a batch of this creamy delight, and get ready to impress your guests with a dairy-free appetizer that’s as delicious as it is wholesome.
