Thai Pandan Coconut Rice Recipe with Chicken and Vegetables


Are you ready to embark on a culinary adventure to the vibrant streets of Thailand? Indulge in the exquisite flavors of Thai cuisine with this delectable Pandan Coconut Rice Recipe with Chicken and Vegetables. This dish is a perfect balance of fragrant pandan, creamy coconut, succulent chicken, and an array of colorful vegetables. Let’s dive right into the recipe!

Ingredients

  • 2 cups jasmine rice
  • 4 pandan leaves
  • 1 can coconut milk (400ml)
  • 1 cup chicken breast, diced
  • 1 cup mixed vegetables (carrots, bell peppers, peas)
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon fish sauce
  • Salt, to taste
  • Fresh cilantro, for garnish

Instructions

  1. Start by rinsing the jasmine rice under cold water until the water runs clear. Drain and set aside.

  2. In a large pot, combine the rinsed rice, pandan leaves, and coconut milk. Add water according to the package instructions for cooking rice (typically a 1:1 ratio of rice to water). Bring the mixture to a boil over medium heat, then reduce the heat to low, cover, and let it simmer for about 15-20 minutes, or until the rice is tender and the liquid is absorbed.

  3. While the rice is cooking, heat the vegetable oil in a separate pan over medium-high heat. Add the minced garlic and sauté until fragrant, about 1 minute.

  4. Add the diced chicken to the pan and cook until it turns golden brown and is cooked through, about 5-7 minutes.

  5. Next, add the mixed vegetables to the pan and sauté for an additional 3-4 minutes, or until they are tender-crisp.

  6. In a small bowl, whisk together the soy sauce and fish sauce. Pour the sauce over the chicken and vegetables, stirring well to evenly coat everything. Cook for another minute or two to allow the flavors to meld together. Season with salt to taste.

  7. Once the rice is cooked, remove the pandan leaves and fluff the rice gently with a fork.

  8. Serve the fragrant Pandan Coconut Rice on a plate, topped with the flavorful chicken and vegetables. Garnish with fresh cilantro for an added burst of freshness.

Tips and Variations

  • For a vegetarian version, you can omit the chicken and increase the quantity of mixed vegetables.
  • If you can’t find fresh pandan leaves, you can use pandan extract or essence to infuse the rice with its unique aroma.
  • Feel free to customize the vegetable mix according to your preferences. You can add broccoli, mushrooms, or any other vegetables you enjoy.
  • To make the dish spicier, you can add a dash of chili flakes or finely chopped fresh chili peppers.

Final Thoughts

The Thai Pandan Coconut Rice Recipe with Chicken and Vegetables is a culinary masterpiece that will transport your taste buds to the bustling streets of Thailand. The fragrant pandan-infused rice, combined with the creamy coconut milk, succulent chicken, and colorful vegetables, creates a harmonious symphony of flavors. Whether you’re hosting a dinner party or simply craving a taste of Thai cuisine, this recipe is sure to impress. So, put on your apron, gather the ingredients, and let the magic of Thai cooking unfold in your kitchen. Enjoy!
