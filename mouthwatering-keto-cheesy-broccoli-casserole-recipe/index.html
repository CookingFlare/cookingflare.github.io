Mouthwatering Keto Cheesy Broccoli Casserole Recipe


Are you looking for a delicious and satisfying keto-friendly dish to add to your recipe collection? Look no further than this mouthwatering Keto Cheesy Broccoli Casserole! Packed with flavor, low in carbs, and easy to make, this casserole is a perfect choice for a comforting family dinner or a crowd-pleasing potluck dish. Let’s dive into the details of this delectable recipe and learn how to create a cheesy, flavorful masterpiece that will have everyone coming back for seconds.

Ingredients

  • 4 cups fresh broccoli florets
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated parmesan cheese
  • 1/4 cup cream cheese
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon Dijon mustard
  • 1/2 teaspoon onion powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon paprika
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • Cooking spray

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C) and lightly grease a baking dish with cooking spray.

  2. In a large pot of salted boiling water, blanch the broccoli florets for 2-3 minutes until they are bright green and slightly tender. Drain the broccoli and transfer it to the prepared baking dish.

  3. In a saucepan over medium heat, melt the butter and add the minced garlic. Sauté for a minute until fragrant.

  4. Pour in the heavy cream and add the cream cheese, stirring until the cream cheese is fully melted and the mixture is smooth.

  5. Add the Dijon mustard, onion powder, paprika, and season with salt and pepper. Stir well to combine all the flavors.

  6. Sprinkle in the shredded cheddar and grated parmesan cheese, reserving a small amount of each for later. Stir until the cheeses are fully melted and the sauce is thick and creamy.

  7. Pour the cheese sauce over the broccoli in the baking dish, ensuring that the broccoli is evenly coated.

  8. Sprinkle the remaining cheddar and parmesan cheese on top of the casserole for an extra cheesy crust.

  9. Bake in the preheated oven for 20-25 minutes, or until the cheese is bubbly and golden brown.

  10. Once done, remove the casserole from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes before serving.

Serving Suggestions

This Keto Cheesy Broccoli Casserole is a versatile dish that can be served as a satisfying side to your favorite protein, such as grilled chicken, steak, or fish. It can also stand alone as a flavorful vegetarian main course. For added texture and flavor, consider topping the casserole with crispy bacon bits or toasted almond slices before serving.

Nutritional Information

  • Serving Size: 1 cup
  • Calories: 280
  • Total Fat: 24g
  • Carbohydrates: 6g
  • Dietary Fiber: 2g
  • Net Carbs: 4g
  • Protein: 10g

Final Thoughts

With its creamy, cheesy goodness and tender broccoli, this Keto Cheesy Broccoli Casserole is a winning recipe that will please even the pickiest eaters. Whether you’re following a keto lifestyle or simply want to enjoy a delicious, low-carb dish, this casserole is a must-try. The combination of wholesome ingredients and bold flavors makes it a standout choice for any occasion. Give it a try and savor every delectable bite!
