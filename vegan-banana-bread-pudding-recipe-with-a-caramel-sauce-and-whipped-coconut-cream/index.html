Vegan Banana Bread Pudding Recipe with a Caramel Sauce and Whipped Coconut Cream


If you’re a fan of classic comfort desserts with a vegan twist, then you’re in for a treat with this mouthwatering Vegan Banana Bread Pudding recipe. This delightful dessert combines the rich flavors of ripe bananas, warm spices, and creamy coconut, all topped off with a decadent caramel sauce and fluffy whipped coconut cream. Whether you’re vegan, dairy-free, or simply love a good bread pudding, this recipe is sure to impress your taste buds and satisfy your sweet cravings.

Ingredients:

For the Bread Pudding:

  • 6 ripe bananas, mashed
  • 1 loaf of day-old vegan bread, cut into cubes
  • 1 1/2 cups of coconut milk
  • 1/2 cup of brown sugar
  • 1 teaspoon of vanilla extract
  • 1 teaspoon of ground cinnamon
  • 1/2 teaspoon of ground nutmeg
  • Pinch of salt

For the Caramel Sauce:

  • 1 cup of coconut sugar
  • 1/2 cup of coconut cream
  • 2 tablespoons of vegan butter
  • 1 teaspoon of vanilla extract
  • Pinch of salt

For the Whipped Coconut Cream:

  • 2 cans of full-fat coconut milk, chilled overnight
  • 1/4 cup of powdered sugar
  • 1 teaspoon of vanilla extract

Instructions:

1. Preparing the Bread Pudding:

  • Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C).
  • In a large bowl, combine the mashed bananas, coconut milk, brown sugar, vanilla extract, cinnamon, nutmeg, and salt.
  • Gently fold in the cubed bread until it is well coated with the banana mixture.
  • Transfer the mixture to a greased baking dish and bake for 35-40 minutes, or until the top is golden brown and the pudding is set.

2. Making the Caramel Sauce:

  • In a saucepan over medium heat, combine the coconut sugar, coconut cream, and vegan butter.
  • Stir constantly until the mixture comes to a gentle boil.
  • Reduce the heat and let it simmer for 5-7 minutes, stirring occasionally, until the sauce thickens.
  • Remove from heat and stir in the vanilla extract and a pinch of salt.

3. Whipping the Coconut Cream:

  • Carefully open the chilled cans of coconut milk and scoop out the thick cream that has risen to the top, leaving the watery liquid behind.
  • Place the coconut cream in a mixing bowl and add the powdered sugar and vanilla extract.
  • Whip the mixture with a hand mixer until it becomes light and fluffy.

4. Serving the Banana Bread Pudding:

  • Once the bread pudding is baked and slightly cooled, drizzle the caramel sauce over the top.
  • Serve the pudding warm with a dollop of whipped coconut cream on each serving.

Tips and Variations:

  • For an extra indulgent twist, sprinkle some toasted coconut flakes or chopped nuts on top of the whipped cream.
  • You can also add a hint of rum or rum extract to the caramel sauce for a boozy flavor.

Final Thoughts:

This Vegan Banana Bread Pudding with Caramel Sauce and Whipped Coconut Cream is a delightful dessert that’s perfect for any occasion. The combination of sweet bananas, warm spices, and luscious coconut flavors creates a truly indulgent treat that’s both satisfying and guilt-free. Whether you’re hosting a dinner party or simply craving a cozy dessert, this recipe is a must-try for any vegan or non-vegan dessert lover. Enjoy the creamy, dreamy goodness of this vegan delight!

So there you have it – a vegan twist on a classic bread pudding that’s sure to please even the most discerning dessert connoisseur. Give this recipe a try and indulge in the delightful flavors of ripe bananas, rich caramel, and fluffy coconut cream. Happy cooking and even happier eating!
