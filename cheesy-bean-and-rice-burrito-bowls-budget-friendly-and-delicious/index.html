Cheesy Bean and Rice Burrito Bowls: Budget-Friendly and Delicious


Are you looking for a delicious and budget-friendly meal that the whole family will love? Look no further than these Cheesy Bean and Rice Burrito Bowls! Packed with flavor and loaded with nutritious ingredients, this recipe is sure to become a new favorite in your household. Plus, it’s easy to make and can be customized to suit your taste preferences. Let’s dive right in!

Ingredients

To make these Cheesy Bean and Rice Burrito Bowls, you’ll need the following ingredients:

  • 1 cup of cooked rice (white or brown)
  • 1 can of black beans, drained and rinsed
  • 1 cup of corn kernels (fresh or frozen)
  • 1 bell pepper, diced
  • 1 small red onion, diced
  • 1 teaspoon of cumin
  • 1 teaspoon of chili powder
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • 1 cup of shredded cheese (cheddar or Mexican blend)
  • Optional toppings: salsa, avocado, sour cream, cilantro

Instructions

  1. In a large skillet, heat some oil over medium heat. Add the diced onion and bell pepper, and cook until they are softened.

  2. Add the cooked rice, black beans, corn kernels, cumin, chili powder, salt, and pepper to the skillet. Stir everything together to combine the flavors.

  3. Sprinkle the shredded cheese over the top of the mixture, covering it evenly. Reduce the heat to low, cover the skillet, and let it cook for a few minutes until the cheese is melted and bubbly.

  4. Remove the skillet from the heat and let it cool slightly before serving. This will allow the flavors to meld together even more.

  5. Divide the mixture into individual bowls and top with your favorite toppings, such as salsa, avocado, sour cream, or cilantro. Get creative and add any other toppings you love!

Now you have a delicious and budget-friendly Cheesy Bean and Rice Burrito Bowl ready to enjoy! This meal is perfect for busy weeknights, as it comes together quickly and can be easily customized to suit your family’s preferences. Plus, it’s packed with protein and fiber from the black beans and rice, making it a nutritious option as well.

Customization Ideas

Here are a few ideas to help you customize your Cheesy Bean and Rice Burrito Bowls:

  • Protein: Add some cooked chicken, ground beef, or grilled shrimp for an extra protein boost.
  • Vegetables: Feel free to add any other vegetables you enjoy, such as diced tomatoes, sliced jalapeños, or shredded lettuce.
  • Spiciness: Adjust the amount of chili powder and cumin to suit your spice tolerance. If you like it hot, add some hot sauce or crushed red pepper flakes.
  • Gluten-Free: Use gluten-free ingredients, such as gluten-free rice or corn tortillas, if you have dietary restrictions.

Final Thoughts

These Cheesy Bean and Rice Burrito Bowls are a fantastic way to enjoy a flavorful and satisfying meal without breaking the bank. The combination of beans, rice, and melted cheese creates a delicious and comforting dish that will have everyone coming back for seconds. So why not give this recipe a try and see how it becomes a staple in your meal rotation? Happy cooking!
