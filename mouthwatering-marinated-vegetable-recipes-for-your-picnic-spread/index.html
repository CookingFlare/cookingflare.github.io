Mouthwatering Marinated Vegetable Recipes for Your Picnic Spread


Who doesn’t love a good picnic? The sun shining, the birds chirping, and the fresh air all make for a delightful outdoor experience. And what better way to enhance your picnic than with some mouthwatering marinated vegetable recipes? These flavorful and vibrant dishes are sure to be a hit at your next outdoor gathering. So grab your picnic blanket and get ready to tantalize your taste buds!

1. Tangy Lemon and Herb Marinated Zucchini

Preparation time: 15 minutes | Marinating time: 1 hour | Serves: 4

Ingredients:

  • 2 medium zucchinis, sliced
  • Juice of 1 lemon
  • 3 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon chopped fresh parsley
  • 1 tablespoon chopped fresh basil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a small bowl, whisk together the lemon juice, olive oil, garlic, parsley, basil, salt, and pepper.
  2. Place the sliced zucchinis in a shallow dish and pour the marinade over them. Toss gently to coat.
  3. Cover the dish with plastic wrap and refrigerate for at least 1 hour to allow the flavors to meld.
  4. When ready to serve, remove the zucchinis from the marinade and arrange them on a platter. Drizzle any remaining marinade over the top.
  5. Enjoy these tangy and herb-infused zucchini slices as a refreshing addition to your picnic spread!

2. Spicy Asian Sesame Marinated Cucumbers

Preparation time: 10 minutes | Marinating time: 30 minutes | Serves: 4

Ingredients:

  • 2 English cucumbers, thinly sliced
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon rice vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon sesame oil
  • 1 teaspoon sriracha sauce (adjust to taste)
  • 1 teaspoon honey
  • 1 green onion, thinly sliced
  • 1 tablespoon sesame seeds

Instructions:

  1. In a large bowl, whisk together the soy sauce, rice vinegar, sesame oil, sriracha sauce, and honey.
  2. Add the sliced cucumbers and green onions to the bowl. Toss well to coat the vegetables in the marinade.
  3. Cover the bowl with plastic wrap and refrigerate for at least 30 minutes to allow the flavors to develop.
  4. Before serving, sprinkle the marinated cucumbers with sesame seeds for added crunch and visual appeal.
  5. These spicy Asian sesame marinated cucumbers will add a kick to your picnic spread!

3. Herb-Roasted Cherry Tomato Skewers

Preparation time: 10 minutes | Cooking time: 20 minutes | Serves: 4

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups cherry tomatoes
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon chopped fresh thyme
  • 1 tablespoon chopped fresh rosemary
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Wooden skewers

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C).
  2. In a bowl, combine the cherry tomatoes, olive oil, minced garlic, thyme, rosemary, salt, and pepper. Toss well to coat the tomatoes evenly.
  3. Thread the marinated cherry tomatoes onto wooden skewers, leaving a little space between each tomato.
  4. Place the skewers on a baking sheet and roast in the preheated oven for about 20 minutes, or until the tomatoes are slightly charred and bursting with flavor.
  5. Serve these herb-roasted cherry tomato skewers as a delicious finger food option at your picnic.

Conclusion

With these mouthwatering marinated vegetable recipes, your picnic spread is sure to be a hit. The tangy lemon and herb marinated zucchini, the spicy Asian sesame marinated cucumbers, and the flavorful herb-roasted cherry tomato skewers will add a burst of freshness and vibrancy to your outdoor gathering. So pack your baskets, grab your loved ones, and enjoy a delightful picnic with these delicious vegetable dishes!
