Salad Series: Seasonal, Regional Salads from Around the World


Grouped by season, discover a range of unique and flavorful salad recipes emphasizing the freshest ingredients from various global regions. Prepare to embark on a culinary journey that offers a taste of local flavors and traditions!

Spring

Greece - Horiatiki Salad

Vibrant, colorful, and perfectly balanced, horiatiki is a classic Greek village salad. It combines juicy tomatoes, earthy cucumbers, soft onions, salted green olives, and feta cheese, all dressed in a tangy olive oil and red wine vinegar mix. This humble dish delightfully showcases the bounty of Greek spring produce.

Japan - Ohitashi

A delicate yet satisfying spring salad, ohitashi features tender, steamed spinach dressed in rich, savory dashi broth with a hint of soy sauce and mirin. Topped with shredded, slightly umami-rich nori seaweed and adorned with a delicate sprinkle of bonito flakes, ohitashi captures the essence of Japanese flavors in a comforting, light dish.

Summer

Spain - Ensalada Mixta

The Spanish ensalada mixta brings together an assortment of flavors and textures in a delightful medley. Combining crisp lettuce, assertive onions, sweet bell peppers, juicy cherry tomatoes, and hard-boiled eggs, it’s completed with a generous drizzle of sherry vinegar and extra virgin olive oil. Optional additions of tuna, anchovies, or olives add depth to this versatile salad.

Mexico - Ceviche

Harnessing the freshness of summer ingredients, ceviche is a refreshing Mexican dish featuring marinated seafood. Typically made with white fish like tilapia or snapper, it’s “cooked” in citrus juices – a nod to the ancient Andean practice. Mixed with the appropriate amount of onions, cilantro, chili peppers, and lime juice, this zesty, tangy salad highlights the coastal flavors of Mexico.

Autumn

Turkey - Döner Salad

Inspired by the famous döner kebab, this Turkish salad boast a hearty mix of shaved lamb, lettuce, tomatoes, cucumbers, and onions, bathed in a cool yogurt sauce. With a trifecta of Turkish flavors – musky lamb, fresh vegetables, and tangy yogurt – this autumnal salad is an impressive fusion of cultures.

Italy - Insalata Caprese

Iconic Italian insalata caprese pays homage to its home region, the Isle of Capri. This beautiful salad consists of nothing more than perfectly ripe, juicy tomatoes, creamy buffalo mozzarella, and fragrant fresh basil, dressed with the finest extra virgin olive oil. Minimalist, yet so heartily delicious, it’s an ode to simplicity and elegance of Italian cooking.

Winter

Russia - Okroshka

Drawing on Slavic culinary traditions, okroshka is a hearty, refreshing winter soup-salad hybrid. A mix of diced boiled potatoes, eggs, sausage, pickles, radishes, and fresh herbs is bathed in a cool sour-cream and kvass broth. This refrigerated, soothing salad hits the perfect balance of warming and invigorating flavors that defy the winter chill.

Indonesia - Gado Gado

A classic Indonesian salad showcasing a harmony of flavors, gado gado includes a delightful combination of boiled sprouts, eggs, tofu, and potato – dressed in a creamy, spicy peanut sauce. Laced with a hint of lime juice, sweet soy sauce, and spiced up with sambal, this vegetarian treat elicits a lively dance of textures and bold taste that keeps you coming back for more.

In this diverse tour of the world’s regional and seasonal salads, it’s clear that the humble vegetable plate has endless potential. With a little inspiration, a dash of local flavors, and a pinch of creativity, anyone can set the table for a flavorful exploration of different cultures with salads from across the globe.

And as the seasons change, let your tastebuds travel – discover the ever-evolving, astonishingly tasteful universe of salads worldwide!
