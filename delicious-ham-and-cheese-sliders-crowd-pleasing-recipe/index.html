Delicious Ham and Cheese Sliders: Crowd-Pleasing Recipe


Are you in search of a simple yet crowd-pleasing recipe that will leave your guests asking for seconds? Look no further than these delicious ham and cheese sliders! Packed with flavors and easy to make, these sliders are the perfect choice for any gathering or even a cozy night in.

Ingredients:

  • 12 slider buns
  • 12 slices of ham
  • 12 slices of Swiss cheese
  • 1/4 cup of melted butter
  • 1 tablespoon of Dijon mustard
  • 1 tablespoon of Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 tablespoon of poppy seeds
  • 1 teaspoon of garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon of onion powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. Slice the slider buns in half horizontally, keeping them connected.
  3. Place the bottom half of the buns in a baking dish.
  4. Layer the ham slices on top of the buns, followed by the Swiss cheese slices.
  5. Place the top half of the buns over the cheese, creating sliders.
  6. In a small bowl, mix together the melted butter, Dijon mustard, Worcestershire sauce, poppy seeds, garlic powder, onion powder, salt, and pepper. Stir until well combined.
  7. Carefully pour the butter mixture over the sliders, ensuring each one is coated evenly.
  8. Cover the baking dish with aluminum foil and bake in the preheated oven for 15-20 minutes, or until the cheese is melted and the sliders are heated through.
  9. Remove the foil and let the sliders bake for an additional 5 minutes, allowing the tops to turn golden and slightly crispy.
  10. Once done, remove from the oven and let the sliders cool for a few minutes before serving.

Serve and Enjoy:

These delicious ham and cheese sliders are perfect for any occasion. Whether you’re hosting a party, having a picnic, or simply craving a satisfying snack, these sliders will not disappoint. The combination of savory ham, gooey Swiss cheese, and the flavorful butter mixture will leave your taste buds begging for more.

Serve them alongside a fresh green salad, crispy fries, or even a bowl of comforting tomato soup. The versatility of these sliders allows you to pair them with your favorite sides to create a complete and satisfying meal.

Get Creative:

While the classic ham and cheese combination is always a winner, feel free to get creative and experiment with different flavors. Add a slice of pineapple for a touch of sweetness, or spice things up with a dollop of Sriracha sauce. You can even swap out the ham for turkey or roast beef if you prefer.

These sliders are also perfect for customization. Set up a slider bar at your next gathering and let your guests choose their own toppings. Provide a variety of cheeses, condiments, and even some fresh veggies to create a personalized slider experience.

Conclusion:

In conclusion, these delicious ham and cheese sliders are a crowd-pleasing recipe that will never disappoint. Simple to make and bursting with flavor, these sliders are a guaranteed hit at any event. So go ahead, gather your ingredients, and get ready to impress your guests or simply treat yourself to a scrumptious homemade meal.
