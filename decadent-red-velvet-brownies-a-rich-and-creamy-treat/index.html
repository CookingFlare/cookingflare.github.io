Decadent Red Velvet Brownies: A Rich and Creamy Treat


If you’re a fan of red velvet cake and brownies, then you’re in for a real treat with this decadent dessert recipe. Red velvet brownies combine the best of both worlds – the rich, chocolatey flavor of brownies with the hint of cocoa and the subtle tanginess of buttermilk that is characteristic of red velvet cake. These indulgent treats are perfect for any occasion, whether you’re looking to impress your guests at a dinner party or simply satisfy your sweet tooth on a cozy night in.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup (2 sticks) unsalted butter, melted
  • 2 cups granulated sugar
  • 4 large eggs
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 1/4 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1 teaspoon white vinegar
  • 1 ounce red food coloring
  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt

Cream Cheese Swirl

  • 8 ounces cream cheese, softened
  • 1/3 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 large egg
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C) and line a 9x13-inch baking pan with parchment paper, leaving an overhang on the sides to easily lift the brownies out after baking.

  2. In a large bowl, combine the melted butter and sugar. Mix until well combined.

  3. Add the eggs, one at a time, mixing well after each addition. Then, stir in the vanilla extract.

  4. In a small bowl, mix the cocoa powder, vinegar, and red food coloring to form a smooth paste. Add this paste to the large bowl and mix until the batter is uniformly red.

  5. Gradually add the flour and salt, mixing until just combined. Be careful not to overmix the batter.

  6. In a separate bowl, prepare the cream cheese swirl by beating the cream cheese, sugar, egg, and vanilla extract until smooth and creamy.

  7. Pour the red velvet brownie batter into the prepared baking pan, spreading it out evenly. Then, drop spoonfuls of the cream cheese mixture on top of the brownie batter.

  8. Use a knife to create a swirl pattern by running it through the batter and cream cheese mixture.

  9. Bake for 30-35 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out with a few moist crumbs. Be careful not to overbake.

  10. Allow the brownies to cool completely in the pan before lifting them out using the parchment paper overhang.

  11. Once cooled, slice the red velvet brownies into squares and serve.

Serving Suggestions

These luscious red velvet brownies can be enjoyed on their own, but they also pair wonderfully with a dollop of whipped cream or a scoop of vanilla ice cream. The contrast of the creamy topping against the rich, chocolatey base is sure to delight your taste buds.

Conclusion

With their vibrant red color and irresistible cream cheese swirl, these red velvet brownies are the perfect combination of flavors and textures. Whether you’re a red velvet enthusiast or a brownie aficionado, this recipe is bound to become a favorite in your dessert repertoire. Treat yourself and your loved ones to the decadent goodness of these red velvet brownies, and savor every rich and creamy bite. Enjoy!
