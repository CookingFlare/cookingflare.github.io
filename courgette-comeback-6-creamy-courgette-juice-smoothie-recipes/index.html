Courgette Comeback: 6 Creamy Courgette Juice & Smoothie Recipes


Lately, we’ve been rediscovering some kitchen staples, and today we’re diving into the versatile world of courgette. Who knew that this humble vegetable could make such fabulous drinks? So, let’s put our juicers and blenders to good use and give a hearty courgette comeback!

1. Creamy Avocado Courgette Green Smoothie

If you’re searching for that dreamy, green smoothie to start your day, this one’s for you. It’s rich, creamy, and packed with nutrients. Best of all, the courgette adds a subtle flavor that goes well with the avocado and spinach.

Ingredients:

  • 1 ripe avocado
  • 1 cup fresh spinach
  • 1 medium courgette
  • 1 banana
  • 1 cup unsweetened almond milk
  • 1/2 cup ice cubes
  • 1 tablespoon honey (optional, adjust depending on your taste)

Directions:

  1. Add all ingredients into a blender.
  2. Blend until smooth and creamy.
  3. Add more almond milk if you prefer a thinner consistency.
  4. Taste and adjust for sweetness by adding honey if desired.
  5. Pour into a tall glass and enjoy this green elixir.

2. Spiced Orange-Courgette Juice

Pump up your morning with this zesty, warm, and comforting spiced orange-courgette juice. The spices add a unique twist, turning ordinary fruits into something extraordinary.

Ingredients:

  • 1 large carrot
  • 1 apple
  • 1 medium courgette
  • 3 oranges
  • 1-inch piece of ginger
  • 1 cinnamon stick
  • 1 cup water

Directions:

  1. Peel carrot and ginger. Coarsely chop all ingredients, except for the cinnamon stick.
  2. Combine everything in a juicer and extract the juice.
  3. Strain the juice through a fine-mesh strainer.
  4. Add the cinnamon stick to the strained juice and let it steep for about 5 minutes for a subtle cinnamon flavor.
  5. Remove the cinnamon stick and chill your juice before serving.

3. Courgette, Turmeric, and Ginger Cooler

This is the ultimate wellness drink! Packed with turmeric and ginger, this courgette cooler is bound to boost your immune system and help you wind down after a long day.

Ingredients:

  • 1 medium courgette
  • 1/2 lemon
  • 1-inch piece of ginger
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 1 tablespoon honey

Directions:

  1. In a small saucepan, simmer ginger with water until tender, about 10 minutes.
  2. In a blender, combine courgette, ginger, lemon juice, turmeric, and honey. Blend until smooth.
  3. Strain the mixture through a fine-mesh strainer into a pitcher.
  4. Chill before serving over ice.

4. Zesty Courgette and Pineapple Smoothie Bowl

This vibrant smoothie bowl is like a tropical getaway for your taste buds! The thirst-quenching blend of courgette, pineapple, and banana create a creamy and luscious base, perfect for topping with your favorite fruits and nuts.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup frozen pineapple chunks
  • 1 medium banana
  • 1 medium courgette
  • 1/2 cup unsweetened coconut milk (from a can)
  • Toppings: sliced banana, diced pineapple, shredded coconut, chia seeds or almonds

Directions:

  1. Add all ingredients to a blender and blend until completely smooth and creamy.
  2. Pour into a bowl and add your desired toppings. Enjoy immediately.

5. Courgette, Banana, and Spinach Power-Up Smoothie

This creamy and thick concoction is the ideal way to fuel your morning. It’s rich in vitamins and minerals, perfect for jump-starting your day and keeping you feeling energized and satisfied.

Ingredients:

  • 1 medium courgette
  • 2 ripe bananas
  • 2 cups fresh spinach
  • 1 cup unsweetened almond milk
  • 1 tablespoon peanut butter (or any nut butter of your choice)
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract

Directions:

  1. Add all ingredients to a blender and blend until completely smooth.
  2. Add more almond milk if you prefer a thinner consistency.
  3. Pour into a tall glass and enjoy!

6. Courgette and Greek Yogurt Breakfast Bowl

Who says smoothies are the only way to enjoy courgette drinks? This thick and creamy breakfast bowl is a fantastic option, offering all the health benefits of courgette in a yogurt-based concoction.

Ingredients:

  • 1 medium courgette
  • 1 cup Greek yogurt
  • 1 cup milk (dairy or your favorite plant-based option)
  • 1 tablespoon honey
  • Toppings: granola, sliced almonds, chia seeds, or fresh berries

Directions:

  1. Add courgette, greek yogurt, milk, and honey to a blender and blend until smooth.
  2. Pour into a bowl and, if using, microwave for about 1 minute to warm it up a bit.
  3. Add your desired toppings and enjoy a delicous breakfast or snack.

There you have it, six delightful and creamy courgette juice and smoothie recipes that’ll make your taste buds sing. Revisit this underrated veggie and reward yourself with these nutrient-packed drinks. Go ahead and happy blending!
