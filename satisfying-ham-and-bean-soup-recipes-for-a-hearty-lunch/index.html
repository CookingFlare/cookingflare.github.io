Satisfying Ham and Bean Soup Recipes for a Hearty Lunch


When it comes to comfort food, few dishes can match the heartiness and satisfaction of a good old bowl of ham and bean soup. Whether it’s a chilly winter day or you’re simply craving a filling, nutritious meal, ham and bean soup is a go-to option. The combination of tender ham, flavorful beans, and aromatic herbs and spices creates a symphony of flavors that is sure to warm both body and soul. In this blog post, we’ll explore three different ham and bean soup recipes that are not only delicious but also easy to prepare. So, grab your apron and let’s get cooking!

Classic Ham and Bean Soup

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb dried navy beans
  • 8 cups chicken broth
  • 1 ham hock
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 carrots, diced
  • 2 celery stalks, diced
  • 2 bay leaves
  • 1 tsp dried thyme
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Rinse the navy beans under cold water and remove any debris.
  2. In a large pot, combine the beans, chicken broth, ham hock, onion, garlic, carrots, celery, bay leaves, and thyme.
  3. Bring the mixture to a boil, then reduce the heat and let it simmer for 2-3 hours, or until the beans are tender.
  4. Once the beans are cooked, remove the ham hock from the pot and shred the meat. Discard the bone and fat, then return the shredded ham to the pot.
  5. Season the soup with salt and pepper to taste.
  6. Serve hot, garnished with fresh parsley if desired.

Smoky Ham and White Bean Soup

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups diced ham
  • 2 cans cannellini beans, drained and rinsed
  • 4 cups chicken broth
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tsp smoked paprika
  • 1/2 tsp cumin
  • 1/2 tsp dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Olive oil for sautéing

Instructions:

  1. In a large pot, heat a drizzle of olive oil over medium heat. Add the diced ham and sauté until lightly browned.
  2. Add the chopped onion and garlic to the pot and cook until softened.
  3. Stir in the smoked paprika, cumin, and oregano, and cook for another minute to release the flavors.
  4. Add the cannellini beans and chicken broth to the pot. Bring to a simmer and let it cook for 20-30 minutes.
  5. Using an immersion blender, blend a portion of the soup until it reaches your desired consistency.
  6. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  7. Ladle into bowls and serve with a sprinkle of fresh herbs on top.

Ham and Lentil Soup

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup dried green or brown lentils
  • 1 cup diced ham
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 carrots, diced
  • 2 celery stalks, diced
  • 4 cups vegetable broth
  • 1 can diced tomatoes
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tsp ground cumin
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric
  • 1/2 tsp coriander
  • Fresh parsley for garnish
  • Olive oil for sautéing

Instructions:

  1. Heat a drizzle of olive oil in a large pot over medium heat. Add the onion, carrots, and celery, and sauté until softened.
  2. Stir in the garlic, cumin, turmeric, and coriander, and cook for another minute.
  3. Add the diced ham, lentils, vegetable broth, and diced tomatoes to the pot. Bring to a boil, then reduce the heat and let it simmer for 25-30 minutes, or until the lentils are tender.
  4. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  5. Ladle the soup into bowls and garnish with fresh parsley before serving.

Conclusion

There you have it – three delightful ham and bean soup recipes to add to your culinary repertoire. Whether you prefer the classic navy bean version, the smoky twist with cannellini beans, or the hearty lentil variation, each recipe offers a comforting and flavorful experience. So, next time you’re in the mood for a hearty lunch or a cozy dinner, whip up a batch of one of these satisfying soups and enjoy the warmth and nourishment they bring. Happy cooking!
