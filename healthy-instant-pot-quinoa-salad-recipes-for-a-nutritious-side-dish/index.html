Healthy Instant Pot Quinoa Salad Recipes for a Nutritious Side Dish


Quinoa salad is a popular and nutritious side dish that can be easily prepared with the help of an Instant Pot. Quinoa is a complete protein and a good source of fiber, iron, and magnesium, making it an excellent addition to a healthy diet. In this article, we will share three healthy Instant Pot quinoa salad recipes that are easy to prepare and packed with nutrients.

Recipe 1: Quinoa Salad with Roasted Vegetables

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup quinoa
  • 2 cups water
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 cups mixed vegetables (such as broccoli, carrots, and bell peppers)
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 tablespoons fresh parsley, chopped

Instructions:

  1. Rinse the quinoa and add it to the Instant Pot with the water. Close the lid and set the valve to “sealing”. Cook on high pressure for 6 minutes, followed by a 10-minute natural release.
  2. Preheat the oven to 400°F (200°C). Toss the chopped onion and minced garlic with olive oil, salt, and pepper. Spread on a baking sheet and roast for 20-25 minutes, or until the onion is caramelized.
  3. Add the roasted onion and mixed vegetables to the cooked quinoa. Mix in cumin, salt, and pepper to taste. Garnish with chopped parsley and serve.

Recipe 2: Quinoa Salad with Lemon-Herb Dressing

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup quinoa
  • 2 cups water
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/4 cup freshly squeezed lemon juice
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh dill
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1/4 cup crumbled feta cheese (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Cook the quinoa in the Instant Pot with the water, following the same steps as Recipe 1.
  2. In a large bowl, whisk together olive oil, minced garlic, lemon juice, parsley, dill, salt, and pepper.
  3. Add the cooked quinoa to the bowl and toss to combine. If using feta cheese, crumble it on top and serve.

Recipe 3: Quinoa Salad with Spicy Black Beans and Corn

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup quinoa
  • 2 cups water
  • 1 can black beans, drained and rinsed
  • 1 cup frozen corn kernels
  • 1 red bell pepper, diced
  • 1 jalapeño pepper, diced
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 tablespoons chopped fresh cilantro

Instructions:

  1. Cook the quinoa in the Instant Pot with the water, following the same steps as Recipe 1.
  2. In a large bowl, combine the cooked quinoa, black beans, corn kernels, red bell pepper, and jalapeño pepper.
  3. In a small bowl, whisk together olive oil, cumin, salt, and pepper. Pour the dressing over the quinoa mixture and toss to combine. Garnish with chopped cilantro and serve.

Conclusion These three healthy Instant Pot quinoa salad recipes are perfect for a quick and nutritious side dish. Quinoa is a great source of protein and fiber, and when combined with a variety of vegetables and herbs, it becomes a delicious and healthy meal. You can customize these recipes to your taste by adding your favorite vegetables, herbs, and spices. Enjoy!
