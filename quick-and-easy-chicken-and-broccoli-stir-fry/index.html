Quick and Easy Chicken and Broccoli Stir Fry


If you’re looking for a simple and delicious meal that you can whip up in no time, then this Quick and Easy Chicken and Broccoli Stir Fry is the perfect recipe for you. Packed with flavor, protein, and healthy veggies, this stir fry is a great option for a busy weeknight dinner or a quick and satisfying lunch. Let’s dive into the recipe and get cooking!

Ingredients

For this Chicken and Broccoli Stir Fry, you’ll need:

  • 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts, sliced into thin strips
  • 2 cups of fresh broccoli florets
  • 1 red bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 small onion, sliced
  • 3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon of fresh ginger, grated
  • 1/4 cup of low-sodium soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons of oyster sauce
  • 2 tablespoons of hoisin sauce
  • 1 tablespoon of cornstarch
  • 1/2 cup of chicken broth
  • 2 tablespoons of vegetable oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Cooked rice or noodles for serving

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Sauce: In a small bowl, whisk together the soy sauce, oyster sauce, hoisin sauce, cornstarch, and chicken broth. Set the sauce aside.

  2. Cook the Chicken: Heat 1 tablespoon of vegetable oil in a large skillet or wok over medium-high heat. Add the sliced chicken and season with salt and pepper. Cook the chicken until it’s browned and cooked through, then transfer it to a plate and set it aside.

  3. Stir Fry Veggies: In the same skillet, add the remaining tablespoon of vegetable oil. Add the minced garlic and grated ginger, and stir-fry for about 30 seconds until fragrant. Add the sliced onion, bell pepper, and broccoli florets to the skillet. Stir-fry the veggies for 3-4 minutes until they are tender-crisp.

  4. Combine and Simmer: Return the cooked chicken to the skillet with the stir-fried veggies. Pour the prepared sauce over the chicken and veggies. Stir everything together and let it simmer for a few minutes until the sauce thickens and coats the chicken and vegetables.

  5. Serve: Once the sauce has thickened and the chicken and veggies are well-coated, remove the skillet from the heat. Serve the Chicken and Broccoli Stir Fry over cooked rice or noodles.

Tips and Variations

  • For an extra kick of heat, add a sprinkle of red pepper flakes or a few dashes of hot sauce to the stir fry.
  • You can customize this stir fry by adding other veggies such as snap peas, carrots, or water chestnuts.
  • If you’re vegetarian, you can easily make this recipe with tofu instead of chicken for a delicious vegetarian stir fry option.
  • Feel free to adjust the sauce to your taste by adding more or less soy sauce, oyster sauce, or hoisin sauce.

Conclusion

This Quick and Easy Chicken and Broccoli Stir Fry is not only simple to make but also incredibly flavorful and satisfying. It’s a versatile dish that you can easily customize to suit your taste preferences and dietary needs. Whether you’re cooking for yourself, your family, or a group of friends, this stir fry is sure to be a hit. Give this recipe a try and enjoy a delicious homemade meal in no time!
