Instant Pot Chicken Alfredo: Creamy and Delicious


If you’re a fan of creamy pasta dishes and love the convenience of using an Instant Pot, then you’re in for a treat. This Instant Pot Chicken Alfredo recipe is a game-changer when it comes to quick and satisfying meals. The rich and velvety sauce combined with tender chicken and perfectly cooked pasta makes for a truly indulgent dining experience. Plus, the best part is that it’s all done in one pot, making cleanup a breeze. Let’s dive into the details of this mouthwatering dish.

Ingredients

For the Chicken Alfredo:

  • 1.5 lbs boneless, skinless chicken breasts, diced
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 cups chicken broth
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 teaspoon Italian seasoning
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 lb fettuccine pasta, uncooked
  • Fresh parsley for garnish

Instructions

  1. Sauté the Chicken: Set the Instant Pot to “Sauté” mode and add the olive oil. Once hot, add the diced chicken and sauté until lightly browned.

  2. Add the Ingredients: Add the minced garlic and sauté for another 30 seconds. Pour in the chicken broth and scrape the bottom of the pot to release any browned bits. This adds incredible flavor to the dish.

  3. Pressure Cook: Stir in the heavy cream, Parmesan cheese, Italian seasoning, salt, and pepper. Break the fettuccine pasta in half and layer it on top of the chicken mixture. Avoid stirring at this point. Close the Instant Pot with the lid and set the valve to sealing. Cook on high pressure for 6 minutes.

  4. Quick Release and Garnish: Once the cooking cycle is complete, perform a quick pressure release. Give everything a good stir to combine the sauce with the pasta. Let it sit for a few minutes to thicken. Serve the Instant Pot Chicken Alfredo hot, garnished with fresh parsley for a pop of color and flavor.

Tips and Tricks

  • Creamy Sauce Consistency: If you prefer a thicker sauce, you can turn on the “Sauté” mode again after the pressure cooking is done and simmer the dish for a few minutes, stirring constantly, until the sauce reaches your desired consistency.

  • Add Vegetables: Feel free to add some vegetables such as broccoli or peas to the dish for extra color and nutrition. Simply toss them in before pressure cooking, and they’ll cook perfectly along with the pasta.

  • Leftover Magic: This Instant Pot Chicken Alfredo reheats beautifully, so don’t hesitate to make a larger batch and enjoy the leftovers for lunch the next day.

Final Thoughts

The Instant Pot has truly revolutionized the way we cook, and this Chicken Alfredo recipe is a shining example of its versatility. In just a few simple steps, you can have a restaurant-quality pasta dish on the table in no time. The creamy and luscious sauce paired with tender chicken will make this recipe a family favorite. So, dust off your Instant Pot and give this recipe a try - you won’t be disappointed!

Remember to pair this dish with some garlic bread and a crisp green salad for a complete and satisfying meal. Whether it’s a busy weeknight or a laid-back weekend, this Instant Pot Chicken Alfredo is sure to impress. Happy cooking!
