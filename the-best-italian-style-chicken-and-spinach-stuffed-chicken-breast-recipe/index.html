The Best Italian-Style Chicken and Spinach Stuffed Chicken Breast Recipe


Are you looking for an impressive and delicious recipe to wow your family and friends? Look no further than this mouthwatering Italian-style chicken and spinach stuffed chicken breast recipe. Bursting with flavor and filled with creamy spinach and cheese, this dish is sure to become a family favorite. It’s a perfect choice for a special occasion or a cozy dinner at home. Let’s dive into the recipe!

Ingredients

  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 2 cups fresh spinach, chopped
  • 1 cup ricotta cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh basil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1 cup marinara sauce
  • Fresh basil leaves for garnish

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).

  2. In a large skillet, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the minced garlic and sauté until fragrant, about 1 minute.

  3. Add the chopped spinach to the skillet and cook until wilted, about 2-3 minutes. Remove from heat and let it cool slightly.

  4. In a mixing bowl, combine the ricotta cheese, grated Parmesan cheese, chopped basil, salt, and black pepper. Mix well.

  5. Once the spinach has cooled down, add it to the cheese mixture and stir until everything is well combined.

  6. Take each chicken breast and make a horizontal slit in the center, creating a pocket. Be careful not to cut all the way through.

  7. Stuff each chicken breast with the spinach and cheese mixture, using a spoon or your hands to press it down and ensure an even distribution.

  8. Place the stuffed chicken breasts in a baking dish. Pour the marinara sauce over the top, covering the chicken breasts completely.

  9. Cover the baking dish with aluminum foil and bake for 25-30 minutes, or until the chicken is cooked through and no longer pink in the center.

  10. Remove the foil and bake for an additional 5 minutes to allow the cheese to melt and the chicken to brown slightly.

  11. Once done, remove from the oven and let it rest for a few minutes. Garnish with fresh basil leaves.

  12. Serve the Italian-style chicken and spinach stuffed chicken breasts with your favorite side dish, such as roasted vegetables, pasta, or a fresh salad.

Tips and Variations

  • Feel free to experiment with different types of cheese, such as mozzarella or fontina, for added flavor and creaminess.
  • If you prefer a spicier kick, you can add a pinch of red pepper flakes to the cheese mixture.
  • For a gluten-free option, ensure that the marinara sauce you use is gluten-free or make your own from scratch.
  • If you have any leftover stuffed chicken breasts, they can be refrigerated and enjoyed the next day. Simply reheat in the oven or microwave until warmed through.

Now that you have the recipe for the best Italian-style chicken and spinach stuffed chicken breasts, it’s time to gather your ingredients and get cooking. This dish is sure to impress with its flavors and presentation. Enjoy the aromas of Italy wafting through your kitchen and delight in every bite. Buon appetito!
