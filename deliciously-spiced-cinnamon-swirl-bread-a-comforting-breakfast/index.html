Deliciously Spiced Cinnamon Swirl Bread: A Comforting Breakfast


There’s nothing quite like the aroma of freshly baked bread wafting through the kitchen, especially when it’s infused with warm, comforting spices like cinnamon. If you’re a fan of cozy breakfast treats, then this recipe for Deliciously Spiced Cinnamon Swirl Bread is sure to become a staple in your home. The combination of soft, fluffy bread and a sweet, aromatic cinnamon swirl makes it the perfect indulgence for a leisurely weekend morning or a special holiday brunch. Plus, the process of making this bread is a labor of love that will fill your home with a sense of warmth and comfort.

Ingredients

For the Bread:

  • 4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 2 1/4 teaspoons instant yeast
  • 1 cup warm milk
  • 1/4 cup unsalted butter, melted
  • 2 large eggs

For the Cinnamon Swirl:

  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 2 tablespoons ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 cup unsalted butter, softened

Instructions

  1. In a large mixing bowl, combine the flour, sugar, salt, and instant yeast.

  2. In a separate bowl, whisk together the warm milk, melted butter, and eggs.

  3. Gradually add the wet ingredients to the dry ingredients, mixing until a soft dough forms.

  4. Turn the dough out onto a lightly floured surface and knead for 8-10 minutes, or until the dough is smooth and elastic.

  5. Place the dough in a greased bowl, cover with a clean kitchen towel, and let it rise in a warm, draft-free place for about 1 hour, or until doubled in size.

  6. While the dough is rising, prepare the cinnamon swirl by combining the granulated sugar and ground cinnamon in a small bowl.

  7. After the dough has doubled in size, punch it down and roll it out into a large rectangle.

  8. Spread the softened butter over the surface of the dough, then sprinkle the cinnamon sugar mixture evenly over the butter.

  9. Starting from one of the long sides, tightly roll the dough into a log.

  10. Place the rolled dough into a greased 9x5-inch loaf pan, cover it with a kitchen towel, and let it rise for another 30-45 minutes.

  11. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C).

  12. Once the dough has risen again, bake the bread for 30-35 minutes, or until it’s golden brown and sounds hollow when tapped on the bottom.

  13. Remove the bread from the oven and let it cool in the pan for 10 minutes before transferring it to a wire rack to cool completely.

  14. Slice, serve, and enjoy!

Tips for Success

  • For an extra touch of sweetness, consider adding a simple glaze made from powdered sugar and a splash of milk drizzled over the cooled cinnamon swirl bread.

  • This bread freezes well, so you can easily make a double batch and store one loaf for later enjoyment.

  • Experiment with different variations of the cinnamon swirl by adding chopped nuts, raisins, or even a hint of orange zest for a unique flavor profile.

Whether you’re a seasoned bread baker or just starting to explore the world of homemade bread, this Deliciously Spiced Cinnamon Swirl Bread is a delightful recipe that’s worth the time and effort. The combination of soft, pillowy bread and a fragrant, sweet cinnamon filling is a true breakfast treat that will add a touch of warmth and comfort to your mornings. So, roll up your sleeves, gather your ingredients, and get ready to fill your home with the irresistible scent of freshly baked cinnamon swirl bread.
