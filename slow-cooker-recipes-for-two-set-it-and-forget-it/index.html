Slow Cooker Recipes for Two: Set It and Forget It


If you’re a couple with a busy schedule or simply prefer cooking in smaller portions, slow cooker recipes for two can be a game-changer. With minimal effort and maximum flavor, these recipes allow you to set it and forget it, letting the slow cooker do all the work while you go about your day. In this blog post, we’ll explore some delicious and easy slow cooker recipes that are perfect for two people. So dust off your slow cooker and get ready to create some mouthwatering meals!

Creamy Chicken and Mushroom Stew

Preparation time: 15 minutes
Cooking time: 4-6 hours on low

Ingredients

  • 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1 cup sliced mushrooms
  • 1 small onion, diced
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 cup chicken broth
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Place the chicken breasts at the bottom of the slow cooker.
  2. Add the sliced mushrooms, diced onion, and minced garlic on top of the chicken.
  3. In a separate bowl, whisk together the chicken broth, heavy cream, all-purpose flour, dried thyme, salt, and pepper until well combined.
  4. Pour the mixture over the chicken and vegetables in the slow cooker.
  5. Cover and cook on low for 4-6 hours or until the chicken is cooked through and tender.
  6. Serve the creamy chicken and mushroom stew over cooked rice or with crusty bread for a hearty and satisfying meal.

BBQ Pulled Pork Sliders

Preparation time: 10 minutes
Cooking time: 8-10 hours on low

Ingredients

  • 1 pound pork shoulder roast
  • 1 cup barbecue sauce
  • 1/4 cup apple cider vinegar
  • 2 tablespoons brown sugar
  • 1 tablespoon Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 teaspoon smoked paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Slider buns, for serving

Instructions

  1. Place the pork shoulder roast in the slow cooker.
  2. In a separate bowl, whisk together the barbecue sauce, apple cider vinegar, brown sugar, Worcestershire sauce, smoked paprika, garlic powder, salt, and pepper until well combined.
  3. Pour the mixture over the pork shoulder in the slow cooker, ensuring it is evenly coated.
  4. Cover and cook on low for 8-10 hours or until the pork is tender and easily shreds with a fork.
  5. Using two forks, shred the pork in the slow cooker and stir it in the sauce.
  6. Serve the BBQ pulled pork on slider buns for a delicious and satisfying meal.

Vegetarian Chili

Preparation time: 15 minutes
Cooking time: 6-8 hours on low

Ingredients

  • 1 can (15 ounces) kidney beans, drained and rinsed
  • 1 can (15 ounces) black beans, drained and rinsed
  • 1 can (15 ounces) diced tomatoes, undrained
  • 1 small onion, diced
  • 1 bell pepper, diced
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 cup vegetable broth
  • 1 tablespoon chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • 1/2 teaspoon smoked paprika
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. In the slow cooker, combine the kidney beans, black beans, diced tomatoes, diced onion, diced bell pepper, minced garlic, vegetable broth, chili powder, cumin, smoked paprika, salt, and pepper.
  2. Stir well to combine all the ingredients.
  3. Cover and cook on low for 6-8 hours, allowing the flavors to meld together.
  4. Serve the vegetarian chili with a dollop of sour cream and a sprinkle of shredded cheese for a comforting and flavorsome meal.

Conclusion

Slow cooker recipes for two are a fantastic way to simplify your cooking routine while still enjoying delicious and flavorful meals. With these recipes, you can set it and forget it, allowing the slow cooker to work its magic while you focus on other tasks. Whether you’re a meat lover or a vegetarian, there’s a slow cooker recipe here to suit your tastes. So grab your slow cooker and start creating these mouthwatering dishes for a stress-free and satisfying dining experience.
