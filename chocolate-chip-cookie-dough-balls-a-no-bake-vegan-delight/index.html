Chocolate Chip Cookie Dough Balls: A No-Bake Vegan Delight


Who doesn’t love indulging in a delicious cookie dough treat? Well, get ready to satisfy your sweet tooth with this amazing recipe for Chocolate Chip Cookie Dough Balls! These delightful no-bake treats are not only vegan but also super easy to make. Whether you’re a dedicated vegan or just looking for a guilt-free dessert option, these cookie dough balls are sure to become a favorite in your household.

Ingredients

For the Cookie Dough Balls:

  • 2 cups of almond flour
  • 1/2 cup of maple syrup
  • 1/4 cup of coconut oil, melted
  • 1 teaspoon of vanilla extract
  • 1/2 cup of dairy-free chocolate chips

For the Chocolate Coating:

  • 1 cup of dairy-free chocolate chips
  • 1 tablespoon of coconut oil

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Cookie Dough Balls:

    • In a large mixing bowl, combine the almond flour, maple syrup, melted coconut oil, and vanilla extract. Mix until a dough forms.
    • Gently fold in the dairy-free chocolate chips until they are evenly distributed throughout the dough.
    • Using your hands, roll the dough into small, bite-sized balls and place them on a parchment-lined baking sheet. Place the sheet in the refrigerator to chill while you prepare the chocolate coating.

  2. Create the Chocolate Coating:

    • In a microwave-safe bowl, melt the dairy-free chocolate chips and coconut oil in 30-second intervals, stirring in between, until smooth and fully melted.

  3. Coat the Cookie Dough Balls:

    • Remove the chilled cookie dough balls from the refrigerator.
    • Using a fork or a toothpick, carefully dip each ball into the melted chocolate coating, ensuring that it is evenly coated.
    • Return the coated balls to the parchment-lined baking sheet.

  4. Chill and Serve:

    • Once all the cookie dough balls are coated, place the baking sheet back in the refrigerator to allow the chocolate coating to set.
    • Once the chocolate has hardened, the cookie dough balls are ready to be served and enjoyed!

Tips and Variations

  • Feel free to get creative with the ingredients! You can add chopped nuts, shredded coconut, or even a sprinkle of sea salt to the cookie dough for added flavor and texture.
  • If you prefer a softer cookie dough texture, you can reduce the amount of almond flour and increase the amount of maple syrup for a chewier consistency.
  • These cookie dough balls can be stored in an airtight container in the refrigerator for up to a week, making them the perfect make-ahead dessert for any occasion.

There you have it – a simple yet delightful recipe for Chocolate Chip Cookie Dough Balls that’s not only vegan but also incredibly delicious. Whether you’re hosting a gathering or just craving a sweet treat, these no-bake delights are sure to impress. So, gather your ingredients and get ready to whip up a batch of these irresistible cookie dough balls – your taste buds will thank you!
