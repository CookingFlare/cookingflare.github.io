Stout Brownie Bites Ingredients: 1 cup stout beer 1 cup unsalted butter 1 ½ cups sugar ¾ cup cocoa powder 2 eggs 1 tsp vanilla extract 1 cup all-purpose flour ½ tsp salt ½ cup chocolate chips Instructions: In a saucepan, bring the stout beer to a simmer and reduce it to ½ cup. Let it cool. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C) and grease a mini muffin tin. In a bowl, melt the butter and mix in sugar and cocoa powder. Add the eggs and vanilla extract, then stir in the reduced stout beer. Fold in the flour, salt, and chocolate chips. Fill the muffin tin with the batter and bake for 12-15 minutes.
