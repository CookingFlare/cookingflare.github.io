Creamy Chicken and Rice Casserole: Easy Family Recipe


As the weather starts to cool down, there’s nothing quite like a warm, comforting casserole to satisfy the family’s cravings. And what better way to do so than with a creamy chicken and rice casserole that’s easy to make and packed with flavor? This recipe is a staple in many households, and for good reason - it’s a crowd-pleaser that’s sure to become a regular in your dinner rotation.

Ingredients:

  • 1 1/2 cups uncooked white rice
  • 3 boneless, skinless chicken breasts, cut into 1-inch pieces
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup frozen peas and carrots
  • 1 cup chicken broth
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • 1/2 teaspoon paprika
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • 1 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (180°C).
  2. Cook the rice according to package instructions until it’s tender and fluffy. Set aside.
  3. In a large skillet, heat the olive oil over medium-high heat. Add the chicken and cook until browned on all sides and cooked through, about 5-7 minutes. Remove from heat and set aside.
  4. In the same skillet, add the diced onion and minced garlic. Cook until the onion is translucent, about 3-4 minutes.
  5. Add the frozen peas and carrots to the skillet and cook until they’re tender, about 2-3 minutes.
  6. In a large mixing bowl, combine the cooked rice, cooked chicken, and vegetable mixture.
  7. In a separate bowl, whisk together the chicken broth, heavy cream, dried thyme, paprika, salt, and pepper. Pour the mixture over the rice and chicken mixture, and stir until everything is well combined.
  8. Transfer the mixture to a 9x13 inch baking dish and top with the shredded cheddar cheese and grated Parmesan cheese.
  9. Bake in the preheated oven for 25-30 minutes, or until the cheese is melted and bubbly.
  10. Remove from the oven and let it rest for 5-10 minutes before serving.

Tips and Variations:

  • To make this recipe more substantial, add some diced bell peppers or mushrooms to the skillet with the onion and garlic.
  • If you want to make it a bit spicier, add some red pepper flakes to the chicken broth mixture.
  • You can also customize this recipe by using different types of cheese, such as mozzarella or provolone.
  • If you’re short on time, you can cook the chicken and vegetables in advance and refrigerate them until you’re ready to assemble the casserole.

Conclusion:

This creamy chicken and rice casserole is a classic comfort food dish that’s sure to become a staple in your household. With its rich and creamy sauce, tender chicken, and fluffy rice, it’s a meal that’s sure to satisfy even the pickiest of eaters. And the best part? It’s incredibly easy to make, making it perfect for busy weeknights or special occasions. So go ahead, give it a try, and enjoy the warm and fuzzy feeling that comes with serving a delicious homemade meal to your loved ones!
