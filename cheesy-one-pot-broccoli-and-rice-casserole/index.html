Cheesy One Pot Broccoli and Rice Casserole


If you’re looking for a comforting and flavorful dish that’s easy to make and requires minimal cleanup, then this Cheesy One Pot Broccoli and Rice Casserole is just what you need. Packed with creamy cheese, tender broccoli, and fluffy rice, this casserole is a crowd-pleaser that will surely become a family favorite. The best part? It all comes together in just one pot, making it a hassle-free option for busy weeknights.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups of broccoli florets
  • 1 ½ cups of long-grain white rice
  • 3 cups of chicken or vegetable broth
  • 1 cup of milk
  • 2 tablespoons of unsalted butter
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 ½ cups of shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1 cup of grated Parmesan cheese
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Begin by melting the butter in a large, oven-safe pot over medium heat. Add the chopped onion and minced garlic, and sauté until the onion becomes translucent, about 3-4 minutes.

  2. Add the rice to the pot and stir it around for a couple of minutes to toast it slightly. This will enhance the nutty flavor of the rice.

  3. Pour in the chicken or vegetable broth and milk, and bring the mixture to a gentle boil. Reduce the heat to low, cover the pot, and let the rice cook for 15-20 minutes, or until it’s almost tender.

  4. Once the rice is almost cooked, add the broccoli florets to the pot and stir them in gently. Continue to cook the rice and broccoli together for another 5-7 minutes, until the broccoli is tender but still vibrant green.

  5. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).

  6. Stir in 1 cup of cheddar cheese and half of the Parmesan cheese into the rice and broccoli mixture, and season with salt and pepper to taste. Ensure that the cheese is evenly distributed throughout the casserole.

  7. Sprinkle the remaining cheddar and Parmesan cheese on top of the casserole.

  8. Transfer the pot to the preheated oven and bake for 15-20 minutes, or until the cheese is melted and bubbly, and the top is golden brown.

  9. Once the casserole is done, remove it from the oven and let it sit for a few minutes before serving.

Serving Suggestions

This Cheesy One Pot Broccoli and Rice Casserole is a complete meal in itself, but you can also pair it with a fresh green salad or some crusty bread for a more substantial dinner. It’s also a great side dish for roasted or grilled meats.

Conclusion

With its creamy texture, cheesy goodness, and the wholesome addition of broccoli, this one pot casserole is a winner in every aspect. It’s a versatile dish that can be customized with your favorite cheeses or additional vegetables, making it perfect for any occasion. Plus, the fact that it all comes together in just one pot means less time spent on cleanup and more time enjoying a delicious meal with your loved ones. Give this recipe a try, and you’ll have a new go-to comfort food that’s sure to impress!

I hope you find this recipe for Cheesy One Pot Broccoli and Rice Casserole as delightful to make and indulge in as I do. It’s a wonderful dish that brings warmth and comfort to the table with every serving. Enjoy!
