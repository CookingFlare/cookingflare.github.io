Grilled Halloumi Skewers with Lemon Herb Marinade: A Flavorful Fourth of July Appetizer


The Fourth of July is a time for celebration, and what better way to kick off your festivities than with a delicious and flavorful appetizer? Grilled halloumi skewers with a zesty lemon herb marinade are the perfect addition to your Independence Day spread. These savory skewers are not only easy to make but also bursting with fresh flavors that will delight your guests. Whether you’re hosting a backyard barbecue or a cozy gathering with friends and family, these grilled halloumi skewers are sure to be a hit.

Ingredients

For the Lemon Herb Marinade:

  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • Zest and juice of 1 lemon
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon fresh parsley, chopped
  • 1 tablespoon fresh mint, chopped
  • Salt and pepper to taste

For the Halloumi Skewers:

  • 1 block of halloumi cheese, cut into cubes
  • Cherry tomatoes
  • Red onion, cut into chunks
  • Bell peppers, cut into squares
  • Wooden skewers, soaked in water for 30 minutes

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Lemon Herb Marinade:

    • In a small bowl, whisk together the olive oil, lemon zest, lemon juice, minced garlic, chopped parsley, and chopped mint.
    • Season the marinade with salt and pepper to taste. Set aside.

  2. Assemble the Halloumi Skewers:

    • Thread the halloumi cubes, cherry tomatoes, red onion chunks, and bell pepper squares onto the soaked wooden skewers, alternating the ingredients for a colorful presentation.

  3. Marinate and Grill:

    • Place the assembled skewers in a shallow dish and pour the lemon herb marinade over them, ensuring that the skewers are well coated. Allow the skewers to marinate for at least 30 minutes, turning them occasionally to distribute the flavors evenly.
    • Preheat the grill to medium-high heat. Once hot, place the marinated halloumi skewers on the grill and cook for 2-3 minutes on each side, or until the halloumi develops grill marks and the vegetables are tender-crisp.

  4. Serve and Enjoy:

    • Once grilled to perfection, remove the skewers from the grill and place them on a serving platter. Garnish with a sprinkle of fresh herbs for an extra burst of flavor.
    • These grilled halloumi skewers are best enjoyed hot off the grill, so invite your guests to indulge in this delectable appetizer right away.

Flavorful and Festive

These grilled halloumi skewers with lemon herb marinade are a celebration of summer flavors and are a delightful addition to any Fourth of July gathering. The tangy zest of the lemon, the aromatic herbs, and the smoky char from the grill come together to create a dish that is both vibrant and satisfying. Whether you’re a fan of vegetarian grilling or simply looking to add a unique twist to your holiday menu, these skewers are a must-try.

So this Independence Day, fire up the grill, gather your loved ones, and treat them to the irresistible flavors of these grilled halloumi skewers. With minimal prep and maximum taste, this appetizer will elevate your Fourth of July celebration to a whole new level. Happy grilling and happy Fourth of July!
