Decadent Strawberry Chocolate Ganache Cake: A Romantic Dessert


Indulge in the ultimate romantic dessert with this Decadent Strawberry Chocolate Ganache Cake recipe. This rich and luscious cake combines the irresistible flavors of chocolate and strawberries, making it the perfect treat for special occasions or a romantic night in. Follow this step-by-step guide to create a cake that will impress your loved ones and leave them craving for more.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 ½ cups granulated sugar
  • ¾ cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1 ½ teaspoons baking powder
  • 1 ½ teaspoons baking soda
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup milk
  • ½ cup vegetable oil
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 1 cup boiling water
  • 2 cups fresh strawberries, chopped

Strawberry Filling

  • 2 cups fresh strawberries, sliced
  • ¼ cup granulated sugar
  • 2 tablespoons cornstarch

Chocolate Ganache

  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 8 ounces dark chocolate, finely chopped

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease and flour two 9-inch round cake pans.

  2. In a large mixing bowl, combine the flour, sugar, cocoa powder, baking powder, baking soda, and salt. Whisk together until well combined.

  3. In a separate bowl, whisk together the milk, vegetable oil, eggs, and vanilla extract. Gradually add the wet ingredients to the dry ingredients, mixing until just combined.

  4. Stir in the boiling water until the batter becomes smooth. Be careful as the batter will be thin.

  5. Gently fold in the chopped strawberries, ensuring they are evenly distributed throughout the batter.

  6. Divide the batter equally between the prepared cake pans. Smooth the tops with a spatula.

  7. Bake for 30-35 minutes or until a toothpick inserted into the center of the cakes comes out clean.

  8. While the cakes cool, prepare the strawberry filling. In a saucepan, combine the sliced strawberries, sugar, and cornstarch. Cook over medium heat until the mixture thickens, stirring frequently. Remove from heat and let it cool completely.

  9. To make the chocolate ganache, heat the heavy cream in a saucepan until it simmers. Remove from heat and add the chopped dark chocolate. Stir until the chocolate is completely melted and the ganache is smooth and glossy.

  10. Once the cakes have cooled, carefully remove them from the pans. Place one cake layer on a serving plate and spread the strawberry filling evenly over the top. Place the second cake layer on top.

  11. Pour the chocolate ganache over the assembled cake, allowing it to drip down the sides. Use a spatula to smooth the ganache and create a beautiful finish.

  12. For an extra touch, garnish the cake with fresh strawberries or chocolate shavings.

  13. Refrigerate the cake for at least 1 hour to allow the ganache to set.

  14. Serve and enjoy this decadent strawberry chocolate ganache cake with your loved ones.

Conclusion

This Decadent Strawberry Chocolate Ganache Cake is a show-stopping dessert that is sure to impress. The combination of rich chocolate cake, sweet strawberry filling, and smooth chocolate ganache creates a symphony of flavors that will leave you wanting more. Whether you’re celebrating a special occasion or simply indulging in a romantic dessert, this cake is the perfect choice. So go ahead, take your taste buds on a journey and enjoy every delicious bite.
