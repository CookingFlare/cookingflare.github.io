5 Ice Cream Recipes Inspired by Your Favorite Candy


When it comes to indulgent treats, there are few things as delightful as combining the creamy sweetness of ice cream with the nostalgic flavors of your favorite candy. Whether you’re craving the chewiness of caramel, the crunch of peanut butter, or the burst of mint, these five ice cream recipes will transport you to dessert heaven. Inspired by classic candy cravings, these homemade ice cream recipes are a scrumptious celebration of your sweet tooth. Dive into this world of candy-infused frozen delights!

1. Peanut Butter Cup Swirl Ice Cream

Peanut butter lovers rejoice! This creamy dessert brings together the rich, nutty flavor of peanut butter cups in an irresistible swirl of chocolate and vanilla ice cream. Forget store-bought pints—this DIY version will leave you swooning.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups heavy cream
  • 1 cup whole milk
  • 3/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/2 cup creamy peanut butter
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 cup chopped peanut butter cups
  • 1/2 cup dark chocolate sauce (store-bought or homemade)

Instructions

  1. In a mixing bowl, whisk together heavy cream, milk, sugar, peanut butter, and vanilla extract until smooth.
  2. Transfer to an ice cream maker and churn according to manufacturer’s instructions.
  3. Once churned, layer the ice cream in a container with swirls of dark chocolate sauce and chunks of peanut butter cups.
  4. Freeze for at least 4 hours or overnight to set.
  5. Serve scoops topped with extra peanut butter cup pieces for added decadence!

The combination of velvety peanut butter and rich chocolate makes this frozen treat incredibly satisfying.

2. Mint Chocolate Candy Bar Ice Cream

There’s something magical about the refreshing taste of mint paired with decadent chocolate. Inspired by popular mint chocolate candy bars, this ice cream is a cooling treat perfect for hot summer days.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1 cup whole milk
  • 3/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 2 tsp peppermint extract
  • 1-2 drops green food coloring (optional)
  • 1 cup chopped mint chocolate candy bars or peppermint patties

Instructions

  1. In a bowl, whisk together heavy cream, milk, sugar, peppermint extract, and food coloring (if desired).
  2. Pour the mixture into your ice cream maker and churn until thickened.
  3. Fold in the chopped mint chocolate candy bars.
  4. Transfer to a container, and freeze for at least 4 hours.
  5. Serve in scoops and garnish with mint leaves or chocolate shavings.

The refreshing mint flavor and rich chocolate bits create an irresistible blend of cool and creamy goodness.

3. Salted Caramel Toffee Crunch Ice Cream

Who can resist the allure of salted caramel and crunchy toffee? Inspired by buttery toffee bars, this ice cream combines sweet and salty notes for a dessert that’s positively addictive.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups heavy cream
  • 1 cup whole milk
  • 3/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/2 cup salted caramel sauce
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 cup crushed toffee candy or toffee bits

Instructions

  1. Whisk together heavy cream, milk, sugar, vanilla, and salted caramel sauce.
  2. Pour into an ice cream maker and churn until creamy and smooth.
  3. Gently fold in crushed toffee candy or bits.
  4. Freeze in a container for at least 4 hours or overnight.
  5. Top each serving with a drizzle of salted caramel for an extra layer of indulgence.

The crunch of toffee combined with the creamy salted caramel base makes this a show-stopping dessert.

4. Sour Gummy Candy Ice Cream

For those who love a tangy twist to their desserts, this ice cream is inspired by sour gummy candies. Bursting with fruity and tart flavors, it’s a colorful treat that will leave you with a smile.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1 cup whole milk
  • 3/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/2 cup sour gummy candies, chopped
  • 1 tsp lemon juice
  • 1/2 tsp lemon zest

Instructions

  1. In a mixing bowl, combine heavy cream, milk, sugar, lemon juice, and lemon zest.
  2. Transfer the mixture to an ice cream maker and churn as per instructions.
  3. Once churned, gently fold in the chopped sour gummy candies.
  4. Freeze in a container for at least 4 hours to allow flavors to meld together.
  5. Serve in bright-colored bowls for a playful twist and garnish with more gummy candies if desired!

Every bite of this ice cream offers bursts of tangy, fruity flavor paired with creamy indulgence.

5. Chocolate Hazelnut Crunch Ice Cream

Inspired by chocolate hazelnut candy, this ice cream delivers nutty richness coupled with decadent chocolate. A gourmet treat that tastes like scooping up your favorite chocolate hazelnut spread right from the jar but better!

Ingredients

  • 2 cups heavy cream
  • 1 cup whole milk
  • 3/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/2 cup chocolate hazelnut spread
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1/2 cup chopped hazelnut candies or roasted hazelnuts

Instructions

  1. Blend heavy cream, milk, sugar, chocolate hazelnut spread, and vanilla extract until smooth and well combined.
  2. Pour the mixture into your ice cream maker and churn until thick and creamy.
  3. Fold in chopped hazelnut candies or roasted hazelnuts for added texture.
  4. Freeze the mixture for 4 hours or overnight before serving.
  5. Serve with a drizzle of chocolate syrup or sprinkle of nuts on top for a stunning finish.

The indulgent nutty flavors of this ice cream are sure to have guests coming back for more!

Final Thoughts

These candy-inspired ice cream recipes are a perfect way to bring your favorite flavors to life. Whether it’s the nostalgic crunch of toffee or the refreshing coolness of mint, there’s a recipe here for every candy lover. Try your hand at one—or all—of these frozen delights during your next dessert-making adventure.

Homemade ice cream is not only fun to make but also allows you to customize every aspect of the recipe, from the ingredients to the toppings. Treat yourself, impress your friends, and indulge in sweet creations that are as unique as your favorite candies.
