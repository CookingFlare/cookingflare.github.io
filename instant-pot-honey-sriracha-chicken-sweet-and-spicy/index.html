Instant Pot Honey Sriracha Chicken: Sweet and Spicy


If you’re looking for a delicious and effortless weeknight dinner, look no further than Instant Pot Honey Sriracha Chicken. This recipe combines the perfect balance of sweet and spicy flavors, creating a dish that will tantalize your taste buds. The best part? It’s incredibly easy to make in your Instant Pot, saving you time and effort in the kitchen.

Ingredients

  • 2 pounds of chicken thighs, boneless and skinless
  • 1/2 cup honey
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons sriracha sauce (adjust to your preferred level of spiciness)
  • 4 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon ginger, grated
  • 1/4 cup chicken broth
  • 1 tablespoon cornstarch
  • 1 tablespoon water
  • Optional garnish: sesame seeds, chopped green onions

Instructions

  1. Start by preparing the sauce. In a bowl, whisk together the honey, soy sauce, sriracha sauce, minced garlic, and grated ginger.

  2. Place the chicken thighs in the Instant Pot and pour the prepared sauce over the chicken.

  3. Add the chicken broth to the Instant Pot, then secure the lid and set the vent to the sealing position.

  4. Cook on high pressure for 10 minutes, followed by a natural pressure release for 10 minutes. After that, carefully do a quick pressure release.

  5. Once the chicken is cooked, carefully remove it from the Instant Pot and set it aside.

  6. In a small bowl, mix the cornstarch with water to create a slurry. Set the Instant Pot to the sauté function and stir in the cornstarch slurry. Allow the sauce to thicken, stirring constantly.

  7. Return the chicken to the Instant Pot and toss it in the thickened sauce, ensuring it is well coated.

  8. Serve the Instant Pot Honey Sriracha Chicken over steamed rice, garnished with sesame seeds and chopped green onions if desired.

Why Instant Pot Honey Sriracha Chicken?

The Instant Pot is a game-changer when it comes to cooking flavorful and tender chicken dishes. Its pressure cooking function allows the chicken to cook quickly and evenly, resulting in a juicy and succulent texture. The combination of honey and sriracha creates a harmonious blend of sweet and spicy, appealing to those who crave a balance of flavors in their meals.

Moreover, this recipe is highly versatile. You can adjust the level of spiciness by simply adding more or less sriracha sauce, catering to your personal preference. Additionally, the use of chicken thighs ensures that the meat remains moist and flavorful, making it an ideal choice for this dish.

Final Thoughts

Instant Pot Honey Sriracha Chicken is a fantastic addition to your recipe repertoire. Its simplicity, bold flavors, and tender chicken will make it a go-to dish for busy weeknights or casual gatherings. The Instant Pot streamlines the cooking process, allowing you to enjoy a homemade meal without spending hours in the kitchen. Give this recipe a try, and you’ll be amazed at how such simple ingredients can result in a truly spectacular dish.

So, fire up your Instant Pot, gather your ingredients, and get ready to savor the delightful combination of sweet and spicy flavors in this Instant Pot Honey Sriracha Chicken recipe. Your taste buds will thank you!
