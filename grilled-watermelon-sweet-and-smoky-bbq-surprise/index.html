Grilled Watermelon: Sweet and Smoky BBQ Surprise


When it comes to summer grilling, most of us immediately think of steaks, burgers, and veggies sizzling on the barbecue. But have you ever considered grilling fruit? Grilled watermelon is a unique and unexpected dish that will surprise your taste buds with its sweet and smoky flavors. In this recipe, we’ll explore how to transform this juicy summer fruit into a mouthwatering BBQ delight.

Ingredients:

  • 1 small seedless watermelon, cut into 1-inch thick slices
  • 1/4 cup honey
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon smoked paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon sea salt
  • Freshly ground black pepper
  • Fresh mint leaves for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the Grill: Preheat your grill to medium-high heat, around 375-400°F (190-205°C). Brush the grill grates with oil to prevent the watermelon from sticking.

  2. Prepare the Watermelon Slices: Cut the watermelon into 1-inch thick slices and remove the rind. Pat the slices dry with a paper towel to remove excess moisture.

  3. Create the Marinade: In a small bowl, whisk together the honey, olive oil, smoked paprika, sea salt, and a pinch of black pepper. This flavorful marinade will give the watermelon its sweet and smoky undertones.

  4. Grill the Watermelon: Carefully brush both sides of the watermelon slices with the marinade. Place the watermelon slices on the preheated grill and cook for 2-3 minutes on each side, or until grill marks form and the watermelon caramelizes slightly.

  5. Serve and Garnish: Once the watermelon slices are grilled to perfection, remove them from the grill and arrange them on a serving platter. Drizzle any remaining marinade over the grilled watermelon and garnish with fresh mint leaves for a pop of color and freshness.

Tips and Tricks:

  • Choosing the Right Watermelon: Look for a small seedless watermelon with a deep red color. This variety tends to be sweeter and more flavorful, making it perfect for grilling.
  • Get Creative with Seasonings: If you’re feeling adventurous, consider experimenting with different seasonings such as cinnamon, cayenne pepper, or even a sprinkle of feta cheese for a savory twist.
  • Pairing Suggestions: Grilled watermelon pairs beautifully with savory dishes like grilled halloumi, shrimp skewers, or even on top of a bed of arugula for a refreshing summer salad.

Why Grilled Watermelon?

Grilling watermelon not only enhances its natural sweetness but also adds a delightful smoky flavor that elevates this refreshing fruit to a whole new level. The heat from the grill caramelizes the sugars in the watermelon, creating a unique taste experience that is both unexpected and utterly delicious. Whether you’re hosting a backyard BBQ or simply looking to impress your dinner guests, grilled watermelon is sure to be a sweet and smoky surprise that will leave everyone craving for more.

So, the next time you fire up the grill, don’t forget to throw some watermelon slices on there and watch as this summer staple transforms into a delectable BBQ treat. Enjoy the sweet, smoky goodness of grilled watermelon and surprise your taste buds with this unconventional yet irresistible dish.

In conclusion, grilled watermelon is a delightful and unexpected addition to any summer BBQ menu. Its sweet and smoky flavors, combined with the ease of preparation, make it a perfect choice for both seasoned grill masters and beginners. So, go ahead, grab a watermelon, fire up the grill, and get ready to amaze your friends and family with this unforgettable BBQ surprise.
