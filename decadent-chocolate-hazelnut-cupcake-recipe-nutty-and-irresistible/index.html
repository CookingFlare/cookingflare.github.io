Decadent Chocolate Hazelnut Cupcake Recipe: Nutty and Irresistible


If you’re a fan of the rich, indulgent combination of chocolate and hazelnut, then this Decadent Chocolate Hazelnut Cupcake recipe is tailor-made for you. These nutty and irresistible treats are perfect for satisfying your sweet tooth and impressing guests at any gathering. Let’s dive into the delightful process of creating these delectable cupcakes.

Ingredients

For the Cupcakes:

  • 1 3/4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 cups granulated sugar
  • 3/4 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons baking soda
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 cup whole milk
  • 1/2 cup vegetable oil
  • 2 teaspoons pure vanilla extract
  • 1 cup boiling water

For the Hazelnut Frosting:

  • 1 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 3 cups confectioners’ sugar
  • 1/3 cup Nutella or any chocolate hazelnut spread
  • 2-3 tablespoons heavy cream
  • 1 teaspoon pure vanilla extract
  • A pinch of salt

Additional:

  • Whole hazelnuts for garnish (optional)

Instructions

Cupcakes:

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C) and line the cupcake tin with paper liners.

  2. In a large bowl, mix together the flour, sugar, cocoa, baking powder, baking soda, and salt.

  3. Add eggs, milk, oil, and vanilla extract to the dry ingredients and mix until well combined.

  4. Gradually stir in the boiling water until the batter is smooth. The batter will be thin, but that’s perfect!

  5. Fill each cupcake liner about 2/3 full with batter.

  6. Bake for 20-22 minutes or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.

  7. Allow the cupcakes to cool in the tin for 10 minutes, then transfer them to a wire rack to cool completely.

Hazelnut Frosting:

  1. In a large bowl, beat the butter until creamy.
  2. Gradually add the confectioners’ sugar and beat until well combined.
  3. Mix in the Nutella, heavy cream, vanilla extract, and salt until smooth and fluffy.
  4. If the frosting is too thick, add more cream, a tablespoon at a time, until the desired consistency is reached.

Assembly:

  1. Once the cupcakes are completely cooled, pipe or spread the hazelnut frosting onto each cupcake.
  2. For an extra touch of elegance, garnish with a whole hazelnut on top of each cupcake.

Serving and Storing

These Decadent Chocolate Hazelnut Cupcakes are best served fresh, but you can store them in an airtight container at room temperature for up to 3 days. If you want to keep them longer, store them in the refrigerator for up to a week.

Final Thoughts

The combination of rich chocolate and the nutty flavor of hazelnut makes these cupcakes truly irresistible. Whether you’re hosting a party or simply craving a sweet indulgence, these Decadent Chocolate Hazelnut Cupcakes are sure to impress. The moist, tender crumb of the cupcake paired with the creamy, nutty frosting creates a symphony of flavors that will leave everyone craving for more. So, roll up your sleeves, gather your ingredients, and treat yourself to the delightful experience of baking and savoring these delectable cupcakes.
