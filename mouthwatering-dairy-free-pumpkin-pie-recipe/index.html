Mouthwatering Dairy-Free Pumpkin Pie Recipe


Are you ready to indulge in a scrumptious dairy-free pumpkin pie that will leave you craving for more? Look no further! This mouthwatering recipe is perfect for those with dairy allergies or those who simply prefer a dairy-free dessert option. With its creamy texture, rich flavors, and aromatic spices, this pumpkin pie will be the star of your holiday table. So, let’s get baking!

Ingredients

For the crust:

  • 1 ½ cups of gluten-free graham cracker crumbs
  • ¼ cup of melted coconut oil
  • 2 tablespoons of maple syrup
  • ½ teaspoon of ground cinnamon

For the filling:

  • 1 can (15 ounces) of pumpkin puree
  • ¾ cup of full-fat coconut milk
  • ½ cup of pure maple syrup
  • 2 tablespoons of cornstarch
  • 1 teaspoon of vanilla extract
  • 1 teaspoon of ground cinnamon
  • ½ teaspoon of ground ginger
  • ¼ teaspoon of ground nutmeg
  • ¼ teaspoon of ground cloves
  • A pinch of salt

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C).

  2. In a medium-sized bowl, combine the gluten-free graham cracker crumbs, melted coconut oil, maple syrup, and ground cinnamon. Mix well until the crumbs are evenly coated.

  3. Press the crumb mixture into a 9-inch pie dish, ensuring an even layer on the bottom and sides. Use the back of a spoon or your fingers to firmly press the crust.

  4. Bake the crust in the preheated oven for 10 minutes, or until golden brown. Remove from the oven and let it cool completely.

  5. In a large mixing bowl, whisk together the pumpkin puree, full-fat coconut milk, maple syrup, cornstarch, vanilla extract, ground cinnamon, ground ginger, ground nutmeg, ground cloves, and salt. Whisk until all the ingredients are well combined and smooth.

  6. Pour the pumpkin filling into the cooled crust, spreading it evenly.

  7. Bake the pie in the preheated oven for 45-50 minutes, or until the filling is set and a toothpick inserted in the center comes out clean.

  8. Once baked, remove the pie from the oven and let it cool at room temperature for about 1 hour. Then, transfer it to the refrigerator and let it chill for at least 4 hours, or preferably overnight.

  9. Serve chilled and garnish with a dollop of dairy-free whipped cream or a sprinkle of ground cinnamon, if desired.

Tips and Variations

  • If you prefer a different type of crust, you can use a store-bought gluten-free pie crust or make your own using a different recipe.
  • For a nutty twist, you can add a handful of chopped pecans or walnuts to the crust mixture before pressing it into the pie dish.
  • If you like your pumpkin pie on the sweeter side, you can increase the amount of maple syrup in the filling.
  • Feel free to adjust the spices according to your taste preferences. You can also add a dash of allspice or cardamom for a unique flavor profile.

Now that you have this delectable dairy-free pumpkin pie recipe in your arsenal, it’s time to impress your friends and family with your baking skills. Whether it’s for a holiday gathering or a cozy night in, this pie is sure to be a crowd-pleaser. So, gather your ingredients, preheat that oven, and get ready to savor the irresistible flavors of this mouthwatering dairy-free pumpkin pie. Happy baking!
