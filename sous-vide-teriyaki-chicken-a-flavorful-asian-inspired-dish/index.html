Sous Vide Teriyaki Chicken: A Flavorful Asian-Inspired Dish


If you’re looking to elevate your Asian cuisine game, look no further than this mouthwatering Sous Vide Teriyaki Chicken recipe. The sous vide cooking method ensures that the chicken is perfectly tender and infused with the rich flavors of the teriyaki sauce. Whether you’re a seasoned home cook or just starting your culinary journey, this recipe is sure to impress your taste buds and those of your loved ones.

Ingredients:

  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1 cup soy sauce
  • 1/2 cup mirin
  • 1/4 cup sake
  • 1/4 cup brown sugar
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon grated fresh ginger
  • 2 green onions, thinly sliced
  • 1 tablespoon sesame seeds (optional, for garnish)

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the Teriyaki Sauce: In a small saucepan, combine the soy sauce, mirin, sake, brown sugar, garlic, and ginger. Heat the mixture over medium heat, stirring occasionally, until the sugar has dissolved. Let the sauce cool to room temperature.

  2. Marinate the Chicken: Place the chicken breasts in a large resealable plastic bag or a vacuum-sealed bag. Pour the teriyaki sauce into the bag, ensuring that the chicken is well coated. Seal the bag and refrigerate for at least 1 hour, or ideally overnight, to allow the chicken to marinate and absorb the flavors.

  3. Preheat the Sous Vide Water Bath: Fill a large container or pot with water and attach the sous vide precision cooker. Set the temperature to 145°F (63°C) for juicy and tender chicken.

  4. Cook the Chicken: Once the water reaches the desired temperature, carefully lower the sealed bag of marinated chicken into the water bath. Ensure that the chicken is fully submerged. Cook for 1 to 2 hours, maintaining the water temperature.

  5. Finish on the Grill or Skillet: After the sous vide cooking, remove the chicken from the bag and pat it dry with paper towels. Preheat a grill or skillet over medium-high heat. Sear the chicken for 1-2 minutes on each side until it develops a caramelized crust.

  6. Serve and Garnish: Transfer the chicken to a cutting board and let it rest for a few minutes. Slice the chicken and arrange it on a serving platter. Garnish with sliced green onions and sesame seeds for an extra burst of flavor.

Serving Suggestions:

This Sous Vide Teriyaki Chicken pairs perfectly with steamed white rice and a side of stir-fried vegetables. The tender and flavorful chicken combined with the savory teriyaki sauce will undoubtedly make this dish a crowd-pleaser at any gathering or family dinner.

Conclusion:

Incorporating the sous vide cooking technique into this classic teriyaki chicken recipe takes it to a whole new level. The precise temperature control ensures that the chicken is cooked to perfection, while the marination infuses it with the bold and irresistible flavors of the teriyaki sauce. Whether you’re hosting a dinner party or simply craving a delicious homemade meal, this Sous Vide Teriyaki Chicken will undoubtedly become a staple in your recipe collection. So, fire up your sous vide precision cooker and get ready to savor the incredible flavors of this Asian-inspired dish!
