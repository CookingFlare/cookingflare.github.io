Light and Lively: Spring Pasta Recipes You'll Love


Spring is a time of renewal, fresh beginnings, and vibrant flavors. As the weather warms up and nature comes alive, it’s the perfect season to lighten up your meals with fresh ingredients and colorful dishes. One of the most versatile and beloved dishes that can be adapted in countless ways to embrace the spirit of spring is pasta. In this collection of light and lively spring pasta recipes, you’ll discover a variety of delicious and easy-to-make dishes that celebrate the flavors of the season.

1. Lemon Asparagus Pasta

Ingredients:

  • 8 oz pasta of your choice
  • 1 bunch of asparagus, trimmed and cut into bite-sized pieces
  • Zest of 1 lemon
  • Juice of 1 lemon
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Olive oil

Instructions:

  1. Cook the pasta according to package instructions. Reserve 1 cup of pasta water before draining.
  2. In a large pan, heat olive oil over medium heat. Add the asparagus and garlic, sauté until the asparagus is tender-crisp.
  3. Add the cooked pasta to the pan, along with lemon zest, lemon juice, and Parmesan cheese. Toss to combine, adding pasta water as needed to create a creamy sauce.
  4. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Serve hot, garnished with extra Parmesan if desired.

2. Spring Pea and Mint Pasta

Ingredients:

  • 8 oz pasta of your choice
  • 1 cup fresh or frozen peas
  • Handful of fresh mint leaves
  • 1/4 cup pine nuts, toasted
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 clove of garlic, minced
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Grated Pecorino cheese

Instructions:

  1. Cook the pasta according to package instructions. Reserve 1 cup of pasta water before draining.
  2. In a separate pan, heat olive oil over medium heat. Add the garlic and cook until fragrant.
  3. Add the peas and cook until heated through. Add the cooked pasta to the peas.
  4. Tear mint leaves and add them to the pasta along with pine nuts. Toss to combine, adding pasta water as needed.
  5. Season with salt and pepper. Serve with a generous sprinkle of Pecorino cheese.

3. Tomato Basil Pasta Salad

Ingredients:

  • 8 oz pasta of your choice
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • Handful of fresh basil leaves, torn
  • 1/4 cup black olives, sliced
  • 2 tablespoons balsamic vinegar
  • 3 tablespoons olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Crumbled feta cheese

Instructions:

  1. Cook the pasta according to package instructions. Rinse with cold water after draining and set aside.
  2. In a bowl, whisk together balsamic vinegar, olive oil, salt, and pepper.
  3. Add the cooked pasta, cherry tomatoes, basil, and olives to the bowl. Toss to combine.
  4. Chill the pasta salad for at least 30 minutes to let the flavors meld.
  5. Before serving, top with crumbled feta cheese for an extra burst of flavor.

These light and lively spring pasta recipes are perfect for embracing the fresh produce and flavors of the season. Whether you’re looking for a quick weeknight meal or planning a spring gathering, these dishes are sure to impress with their simplicity and delicious taste. Enjoy the bounty of spring with these delightful pasta creations!
