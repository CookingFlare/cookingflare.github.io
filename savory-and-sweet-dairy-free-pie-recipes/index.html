Savory and Sweet Dairy-Free Pie Recipes


When it comes to pies, the possibilities are endless. From hearty savory bakes perfect for dinners to indulgent sweet treats that can rival the best desserts, pies are an ideal way to please a crowd or satisfy your own cravings. For those avoiding dairy, you no longer need to miss out on these delectable creations! In this post, we’re diving into a variety of imaginative dairy-free pie recipes that will leave you inspired to try something new in your kitchen.

Savory Dairy-Free Pies for Hearty Meals

Dairy-free savory pies offer boundless opportunities to incorporate bold flavors, wholesome ingredients, and satisfying textures. Whether it’s pie night with loved ones or meal prep for the week ahead, these recipes will quickly become household favorites.

1. Dairy-Free Mushroom and Leek Pot Pie

This mushroom and leek pot pie delivers a creamy, savory filling without a hint of dairy. The secret lies in the velvety coconut milk paired with vegetable broth, which creates richness without overpowering the balance of flavors.

Ingredients:

  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 cups chopped mushrooms (button, cremini, or mixed)
  • 1 large leek, sliced thinly
  • 3 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 cup unsweetened coconut milk
  • 1 cup vegetable broth
  • 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour (gluten-free option available)
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Dairy-free pie crust (store-bought or homemade)

Method:

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Heat olive oil in a pan over medium heat. Add mushrooms, leeks, and garlic, cooking until soft and fragrant.
  3. Sprinkle flour over the cooked veggies, stirring well to coat evenly. Slowly pour in the coconut milk and vegetable broth, whisking continuously to avoid lumps.
  4. Add thyme, salt, and pepper, then simmer until the filling thickens.
  5. Transfer the mixture to a pie dish with the prepared crust. Add a top crust, seal the edges, and cut small vents for steam.
  6. Bake for 35-40 minutes or until golden brown. Serve warm.

2. Dairy-Free Spinach and Tofu Quiche Pie

This eggless quiche pie is brimming with protein-packed tofu, fresh spinach, and aromatic spices. Its creamy consistency proves you don’t need dairy to create a satisfying savory option.

Ingredients:

  • 1 pie crust (vegan and dairy-free)
  • 1 block (14 oz.) firm tofu, drained
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 3 cups fresh spinach
  • 1 cup unsweetened soy milk
  • 1 tablespoon nutritional yeast
  • ½ teaspoon turmeric
  • ½ teaspoon garlic powder
  • ½ teaspoon onion powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Method:

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C). Roll out the crust and fit it into a pie dish.
  2. Heat olive oil in a pan and sauté spinach until wilted.
  3. In a blender, combine tofu, soy milk, nutritional yeast, turmeric, garlic powder, onion powder, salt, and pepper. Blend until smooth.
  4. Spread the spinach evenly into the pie crust. Pour the tofu mixture over the spinach, smoothing with a spatula.
  5. Bake for 40 minutes or until firm and golden. Allow to cool slightly before slicing.

3. Dairy-Free Mexican Taco Pie

This savory pie layers vibrant flavors with a spiced bean filling and crunchy tortilla crust. Add fresh toppings for a bold, crowd-pleasing dish!

Ingredients:

  • 1 package dairy-free tortilla wraps
  • 1 tablespoon oil
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 1 bell pepper, diced
  • 1 cup black beans, cooked and drained
  • 1 cup diced tomatoes
  • ½ teaspoon cumin
  • ½ teaspoon chili powder
  • 1 cup shredded dairy-free cheese alternative (optional)

Method:

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Heat oil in a pan, sauté onion and bell pepper until softened. Add black beans, tomatoes, cumin, and chili powder, cooking for 5 minutes.
  3. Line a pie dish with tortilla wraps to form a crust. Layer the bean filling, optional cheese, and more tortillas as desired.
  4. Bake for 15-20 minutes. Top with avocado slices, dairy-free sour cream, and fresh cilantro before serving.

Sweet Dairy-Free Pies for Every Occasion

No meal feels fully complete without dessert, and sweet pies are a timeless way to wrap up a gathering or enjoy a peaceful moment of indulgence. These dairy-free pie recipes are rich, satisfying, and celebratory.

4. Classic Dairy-Free Apple Pie

A golden crust bursting with warm, spiced apples is as comforting as dessert gets. This recipe avoids butter yet retains the buttery-elegance we all crave.

Ingredients:

  • 6 cups thinly sliced apples (Granny Smith or Honeycrisp work best)
  • ¾ cup granulated sugar or coconut sugar
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 1 teaspoon cinnamon
  • ½ teaspoon nutmeg
  • 1 tablespoon cornstarch
  • Dairy-free pie crust for bottom and top

Method:

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C). Fit one of the crusts into a pie dish.
  2. In a bowl, mix sliced apples, sugar, lemon juice, cinnamon, nutmeg, and cornstarch thoroughly.
  3. Pour the apple mixture into the crust and cover with the top crust. Seal the edges and cut small slits for ventilation.
  4. Bake for 50-60 minutes or until golden brown and bubbling. Allow to cool before serving.

5. Dairy-Free Chocolate Silk Pie

This no-bake dessert dazzles with rich, chocolatey elegance topped with fluffy coconut whipped cream.

Ingredients:
For the crust:

  • 2 cups crushed graham crackers
  • ½ cup melted coconut oil

For the filling:

  • 1 cup dairy-free chocolate chips, melted
  • 1 block (12 oz.) silken tofu
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

For topping:

  • 1 cup canned coconut cream, chilled and whipped

Method:

  1. Mix crushed graham crackers with melted coconut oil and press them into a pie dish. Chill for 10 minutes.
  2. Blend tofu, melted chocolate, and vanilla extract until smooth. Spread the mixture evenly over the chilled crust.
  3. Chill the entire pie in the fridge for at least 2 hours. Before serving, top with coconut whipped cream.

6. Dairy-Free Pumpkin Pie

Perfect for fall, this creamy pumpkin pie uses coconut milk to achieve delectable smoothness.

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups pure pumpkin puree
  • ¾ cup unsweetened coconut milk
  • ¾ cup brown sugar
  • 2 eggs or egg substitute for vegan option
  • 1 teaspoon cinnamon
  • ½ teaspoon nutmeg
  • ½ teaspoon ginger
  • A pinch of salt
  • Dairy-free pie crust

Method:

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. Whisk pumpkin, coconut milk, sugar, eggs or substitute, cinnamon, nutmeg, ginger, and salt together until blended.
  3. Pour the filling into the prepared crust. Bake for 50-60 minutes or until set. Let it cool completely before slicing.

Tips for Perfect Dairy-Free Pie Baking

  1. Choose the Right Milk Alternatives: Coconut milk, almond milk, and soy milk are excellent choices for creamy fillings. Each offers a distinct flavor profile, so experiment to find your favorite.
  2. Select Dairy-Free Alternatives for Butter: Plant-based butter, coconut oil, or olive oil can often be substituted in crust recipes without sacrificing texture or flavor.
  3. Season Creatively: Savory pies benefit from bolder seasonings like herbs, spices, and nutritional yeast, while sweet pies shine with fragrant options like vanilla, cinnamon, or nutmeg.
  4. Test for Doneness: Use visual cues such as a golden crust or firm filling to gauge readiness, and don’t be afraid to tweak baking times slightly for your oven.

Dairy-free pies are a testament to the countless ways to create delicious meals and desserts free of animal-based dairy products. Whether you’re serving up a savory mushroom pot pie or delighting in a chocolate silk masterpiece, these recipes promise to transport your taste buds to pie heaven while accommodating dietary restrictions. The next time you’re pondering a dairy-free creation, remember: pies are always a good idea! Happy baking!
