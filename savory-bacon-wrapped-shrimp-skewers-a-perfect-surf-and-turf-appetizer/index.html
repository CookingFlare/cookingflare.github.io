Savory Bacon-Wrapped Shrimp Skewers: A Perfect Surf and Turf Appetizer


If you’re looking to elevate your appetizer game and impress your guests with a mouthwatering dish, then look no further than these savory bacon-wrapped shrimp skewers. This delightful surf and turf combination brings together the smoky richness of bacon with the delicate sweetness of shrimp, creating a flavor explosion that will leave everyone wanting more. Whether you’re hosting a dinner party, a summer barbecue, or simply craving a delicious treat, these bacon-wrapped shrimp skewers are sure to be a hit.

Ingredients

To create this delectable appetizer, you will need the following ingredients:

  • Large shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • Thinly sliced bacon
  • Olive oil
  • Lemon juice
  • Minced garlic
  • Paprika
  • Salt and pepper
  • Wooden or metal skewers

Instructions

Step 1: Prepare the Shrimp

Start by patting the shrimp dry with paper towels to ensure that they are free of excess moisture. This will help the bacon adhere to the shrimp and promote even cooking.

Step 2: Marinate the Shrimp

In a bowl, combine the olive oil, lemon juice, minced garlic, paprika, salt, and pepper. Add the shrimp to the bowl and toss gently to coat them evenly with the marinade. Let the shrimp marinate for 15-20 minutes to allow the flavors to infuse.

Step 3: Wrap the Shrimp with Bacon

Take each shrimp and wrap it with a slice of bacon, securing it with a skewer. Make sure the bacon is wrapped snugly around the shrimp to prevent it from unravelling during cooking.

Step 4: Grill the Skewers

Preheat your grill to medium-high heat. Place the bacon-wrapped shrimp skewers on the grill and cook for 2-3 minutes on each side, or until the bacon is crispy and the shrimp is opaque and slightly charred. Be sure to keep a close eye on the skewers to prevent the bacon from burning.

Step 5: Serve and Enjoy

Once the bacon-wrapped shrimp skewers are cooked to perfection, carefully remove them from the grill and transfer them to a serving platter. Garnish with a sprinkle of fresh herbs or a squeeze of lemon juice for an extra burst of flavor. These savory skewers are best enjoyed hot off the grill, so be prepared to watch them disappear within minutes!

Tips and Variations

  • For a spicy kick, add a pinch of cayenne pepper or red pepper flakes to the marinade.
  • Experiment with different seasonings such as smoked paprika, cumin, or chili powder to customize the flavor profile of the shrimp.
  • If you prefer, these bacon-wrapped shrimp skewers can also be cooked in the oven at 400°F for 12-15 minutes, turning once halfway through.

Conclusion

In conclusion, these savory bacon-wrapped shrimp skewers are a show-stopping appetizer that combines the best of land and sea. The succulent shrimp wrapped in crispy bacon are a match made in culinary heaven, and the simple yet flavorful marinade takes them to the next level. Whether you’re a seafood enthusiast, a bacon lover, or simply someone who appreciates good food, these skewers are guaranteed to be a hit at any gathering. So, fire up the grill, gather your ingredients, and treat yourself and your loved ones to this irresistible surf and turf delight!
