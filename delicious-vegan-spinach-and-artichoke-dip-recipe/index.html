Delicious Vegan Spinach and Artichoke Dip Recipe


Are you looking for a scrumptious and healthy appetizer to impress your guests at your next gathering? Look no further! This Vegan Spinach and Artichoke Dip is a crowd-pleaser that will leave everyone reaching for more. Packed with flavor and creamy goodness, this dip is the perfect addition to any party spread.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup raw cashews, soaked in water for 4 hours or overnight
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 can (14 oz) artichoke hearts, drained and chopped
  • 2 cups fresh spinach, chopped
  • 1 cup unsweetened almond milk
  • 2 tablespoons nutritional yeast
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 1 teaspoon onion powder
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).

  2. In a blender, combine the soaked cashews and almond milk. Blend until smooth and creamy. Set aside.

  3. In a large skillet, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the chopped onion and garlic, and sauté until softened and fragrant.

  4. Add the chopped artichoke hearts and spinach to the skillet. Cook until the spinach is wilted and the artichokes are heated through.

  5. Pour the creamy cashew mixture from the blender into the skillet with the sautéed vegetables. Stir to combine.

  6. Add the nutritional yeast, lemon juice, onion powder, garlic powder, salt, and pepper to the skillet. Stir well to incorporate all the flavors.

  7. Transfer the mixture to a baking dish and spread it out evenly.

  8. Bake in the preheated oven for 20-25 minutes, or until the dip is hot and bubbly.

  9. Remove from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes before serving.

  10. Garnish with a sprinkle of nutritional yeast or fresh herbs, if desired.

  11. Serve the Vegan Spinach and Artichoke Dip with your favorite dippers such as tortilla chips, pita bread, or fresh vegetable sticks.

Tips and Variations

  • For a touch of heat, add a pinch of red pepper flakes to the dip.
  • You can customize this recipe by adding a handful of chopped sun-dried tomatoes or a dash of smoked paprika for extra depth of flavor.
  • Leftovers can be refrigerated in an airtight container for up to 3 days. Simply reheat in the oven or microwave before serving.

Health Benefits

This Vegan Spinach and Artichoke Dip is not only delicious but also nutritious. Spinach is rich in iron, vitamins, and antioxidants, while artichokes are a good source of fiber and essential minerals. The creamy cashew base provides healthy fats and protein, making this dip a guilt-free indulgence.

So, there you have it – a delectable Vegan Spinach and Artichoke Dip that will elevate your entertaining game and leave your guests raving. Whether you’re hosting a party or simply craving a flavorful snack, this recipe is a must-try for any lover of good food. Enjoy!
