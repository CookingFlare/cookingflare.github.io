Delicious One Pot Teriyaki Chicken and Rice


If you’re looking for a delicious and hassle-free meal that’s packed with flavor, then this One Pot Teriyaki Chicken and Rice recipe is just what you need. This dish is the perfect combination of tender chicken, fluffy rice, and a sweet and savory teriyaki sauce that will leave your taste buds wanting more. The best part? It’s all made in one pot, making cleanup a breeze. Let’s dive into the details of this mouthwatering recipe.

Ingredients:

  • 1 ½ lbs. boneless, skinless chicken thighs, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 1 cup long-grain white rice
  • 1 ½ cups chicken broth
  • 1 cup water
  • 1 cup sliced carrots
  • 1 cup broccoli florets
  • ½ cup soy sauce
  • ¼ cup honey
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon grated ginger
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Sesame seeds and sliced green onions for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the Teriyaki Sauce:

    • In a small bowl, whisk together the soy sauce, honey, minced garlic, and grated ginger. Set aside.

  2. Brown the Chicken:

    • Heat the olive oil in a large pot or deep skillet over medium-high heat. Add the chicken pieces, season with salt and pepper, and cook until browned on all sides. This should take about 5-6 minutes. Once done, remove the chicken from the pot and set aside.

  3. Cook the Vegetables:

    • In the same pot, add the sliced carrots and broccoli florets. Sauté for about 3-4 minutes until slightly tender.

  4. Add the Rice and Liquid:

    • Return the browned chicken to the pot. Add the rice, chicken broth, and water. Pour the teriyaki sauce over the chicken and rice. Stir to combine all the ingredients.

  5. Simmer and Cook:

    • Bring the mixture to a boil, then reduce the heat to low. Cover the pot and let it simmer for 20-25 minutes, or until the rice is tender and the chicken is cooked through.

  6. Serve and Enjoy:

    • Once the rice is cooked, remove the pot from the heat. Let it sit, covered, for a few minutes. This allows the flavors to meld together. Serve the One Pot Teriyaki Chicken and Rice in bowls, garnished with sesame seeds and sliced green onions.

This One Pot Teriyaki Chicken and Rice recipe is a complete meal in itself, offering a balance of protein, carbohydrates, and veggies. The teriyaki sauce adds an irresistible umami flavor, making it a hit with both kids and adults. Plus, the convenience of cooking everything in one pot means fewer dishes to wash, which is always a win in my book.

Whether you’re cooking for your family on a busy weeknight or entertaining guests, this recipe is sure to impress. The simplicity of the cooking process combined with the bold flavors of the teriyaki sauce makes it a standout dish that will have everyone asking for seconds.

So, the next time you’re craving a comforting and satisfying meal, give this One Pot Teriyaki Chicken and Rice recipe a try. It’s a culinary adventure that brings together the heartiness of chicken and rice with the irresistible allure of teriyaki. Happy cooking!
