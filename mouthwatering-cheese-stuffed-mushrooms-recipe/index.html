Mouthwatering Cheese Stuffed Mushrooms Recipe


If you’re looking for a delectable appetizer or a savory side dish to impress your guests, these cheese-stuffed mushrooms are a must-try. This recipe combines the earthy flavor of fresh mushrooms with the creamy richness of cheese, creating a mouthwatering combination that will leave everyone craving for more. Whether you’re hosting a dinner party or simply want to treat yourself to a gourmet snack, these cheese-stuffed mushrooms are sure to delight your taste buds.

Ingredients:

  • 24 large white mushrooms
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1/2 cup finely chopped onion
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 8 ounces cream cheese, softened
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/4 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/4 cup breadcrumbs
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1/4 teaspoon paprika
  • 1/4 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1/4 teaspoon dried thyme
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh parsley, chopped, for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the Oven: Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C). Line a baking sheet with parchment paper and set it aside.

  2. Prepare the Mushrooms: Gently wipe the mushrooms with a damp paper towel to remove any dirt. Carefully remove the stems from the mushrooms and finely chop them. Arrange the mushroom caps on the prepared baking sheet, stem side up.

  3. Prepare the Filling: In a skillet, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the chopped mushroom stems, onions, and garlic. Sauté until the vegetables are soft and the excess moisture has evaporated. Remove the skillet from the heat and let it cool for a few minutes.

  4. Make the Cheese Filling: In a mixing bowl, combine the cream cheese, Parmesan cheese, mozzarella cheese, breadcrumbs, black pepper, paprika, dried oregano, dried thyme, and salt. Add the sautéed mushroom mixture to the bowl and mix until well combined.

  5. Stuff the Mushrooms: Using a small spoon, generously fill each mushroom cap with the cheese mixture, mounding it slightly. Place the stuffed mushrooms on the baking sheet.

  6. Bake the Mushrooms: Bake the stuffed mushrooms in the preheated oven for 20-25 minutes, or until the filling is golden and the mushrooms are tender.

  7. Garnish and Serve: Once out of the oven, sprinkle the stuffed mushrooms with freshly chopped parsley for a pop of color and added freshness. Arrange them on a serving platter and serve immediately.

Tips and Variations:

  • For a hint of heat, add a pinch of cayenne pepper to the cheese filling.
  • Experiment with different types of cheese, such as blue cheese or Gruyère, for unique flavor profiles.
  • If you prefer a vegetarian version, omit the Parmesan and use a vegetarian-friendly alternative.

These cheese-stuffed mushrooms are a guaranteed crowd-pleaser and are perfect for any occasion. They are versatile enough to complement a variety of main courses or can stand alone as a delightful appetizer. The combination of savory cheese and tender mushrooms makes this recipe a timeless classic that will have everyone asking for seconds. Enjoy the irresistible flavors of these mouthwatering cheese-stuffed mushrooms at your next gathering!
