Instant Pot Pork Tenderloin: A Simple Yet Delicious Recipe


If you’re looking for an easy and delicious way to cook pork tenderloin, the Instant Pot is the answer. The Instant Pot, a pressure cooker that has revolutionized home cooking, can help you create tender, flavorful meals in no time. In this post, we’ll explore how to make Instant Pot pork tenderloin – a dish that’s juicy, packed with flavor, and so easy to prepare. Whether you’re a seasoned Instant Pot user or just starting, this recipe will quickly become a favorite in your rotation.

Why Instant Pot Pork Tenderloin?

Pork tenderloin is one of the most tender cuts of pork, making it ideal for quick cooking methods like pressure cooking. Unlike traditional methods that require long roasting or slow cooking times, the Instant Pot can cook pork tenderloin in a fraction of the time, without compromising on flavor or texture.

The beauty of Instant Pot pork tenderloin is that it allows you to infuse the meat with rich flavors, while retaining its natural juiciness. The high pressure inside the pot locks in moisture, ensuring that the pork turns out tender and succulent. Plus, the versatility of the Instant Pot means you can experiment with different seasonings, marinades, and sauces, making it a perfect dish for a busy weeknight dinner or a special occasion.

Ingredients You’ll Need

Before we dive into the cooking process, let’s gather the ingredients for this Instant Pot pork tenderloin recipe:

  • 1 to 1.5 pounds of pork tenderloin (trimmed of excess fat)
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 2 teaspoons garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon onion powder
  • 1 teaspoon smoked paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1/2 cup low-sodium chicken broth
  • 1/4 cup balsamic vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon honey (optional for a touch of sweetness)
  • 1 teaspoon Dijon mustard (for an extra tang)

These simple ingredients come together to create a flavorful and tender pork dish that is perfect for busy days or special dinners.

Step 1: Seasoning the Pork Tenderloin

Start by preparing your pork tenderloin. If it’s not already trimmed, remove any visible silver skin or excess fat. Silver skin is a tough connective tissue that doesn’t break down during cooking and can make the pork chewy.

Pat the pork tenderloin dry with paper towels, as this will help the seasonings stick better. Once dried, generously season the pork with garlic powder, onion powder, smoked paprika, salt, and black pepper. Massage the seasoning mix into the meat, ensuring every surface is covered.

For added flavor, you can marinate the pork tenderloin for 30 minutes to a few hours before cooking, though this step is optional. If you marinate, store the pork in the fridge to keep it fresh until you’re ready to cook.

Step 2: Searing the Pork

The Instant Pot can be used to sear the pork tenderloin, which will lock in the flavors and give the pork a nice golden crust. To do this, set the Instant Pot to the “Sauté” function, and add a tablespoon of olive oil.

Allow the oil to heat up in the pot for a minute or two. Once hot, carefully place the seasoned pork tenderloin in the pot and sear it on all sides. Turn the tenderloin every 2-3 minutes until it is browned and caramelized all over. This step should take about 5-7 minutes.

Searing not only enhances the flavor but also adds depth to the overall dish. You’re essentially building layers of flavor that will make the finished product even more delicious.

Step 3: Cooking the Pork Tenderloin

After searing, remove the pork tenderloin from the Instant Pot and set it aside on a plate. This step is essential as you’ll now prepare the flavorful cooking liquid.

In the same Instant Pot, pour in the chicken broth, balsamic vinegar, honey, and Dijon mustard. Stir the mixture to combine, scraping the bottom of the pot to release any caramelized bits left from the searing process. These bits contribute even more flavor to the sauce.

Once the liquid is ready, place the seared pork tenderloin back into the Instant Pot. Ensure that the tenderloin is nestled in the liquid, but it doesn’t need to be completely submerged. The pressure cooking will ensure that the pork absorbs all those wonderful flavors.

Seal the Instant Pot lid, making sure the vent is set to “Sealing.” Set the Instant Pot to “Pressure Cook” on high for 4-5 minutes. If your tenderloin is on the larger side (closer to 1.5 pounds), you may want to increase the cooking time by another minute. The goal is for the pork to reach an internal temperature of 145°F (63°C), which is the recommended safe temperature for pork.

Once the cooking time is up, allow the Instant Pot to naturally release pressure for about 5-10 minutes. After that, you can switch the valve to “Venting” to release any remaining pressure. Carefully remove the lid, and you’ll find your pork tenderloin perfectly cooked and juicy.

Step 4: Resting the Pork Tenderloin

After removing the pork from the Instant Pot, transfer it to a cutting board and let it rest for about 5-10 minutes. Resting the meat allows the juices to redistribute, ensuring that every slice is moist and flavorful.

While the pork is resting, you can make the sauce even more decadent. If desired, set the Instant Pot to the “Sauté” function again and reduce the cooking liquid for 2-3 minutes to thicken the sauce. This will intensify the flavor and create a rich sauce to pour over the pork when serving.

Step 5: Slicing and Serving

Once the pork has rested, slice it into medallions about 1/2 inch thick. Arrange the slices on a platter or individual plates, and drizzle the reduced sauce over the top.

This Instant Pot pork tenderloin is best served with a side of roasted vegetables, mashed potatoes, or a simple green salad. The sweet and tangy balsamic sauce complements the pork perfectly, making each bite a delightful experience.

Variations to Try

While this recipe is fantastic on its own, don’t hesitate to experiment with different flavor combinations to suit your tastes. Here are some variations you can try:

Herb-Infused Pork Tenderloin

For a more herbaceous flavor, add fresh herbs like rosemary, thyme, or sage to the seasoning mix. You can either rub the herbs directly on the pork or place them in the Instant Pot during cooking to infuse the flavors into the meat and sauce.

Spicy Pork Tenderloin

If you love a bit of heat, add some red pepper flakes or chili powder to the seasoning mix. A splash of hot sauce in the cooking liquid will also add an extra kick to the sauce.

Apple Cider Glaze

For a sweeter, fruity twist, replace the balsamic vinegar with apple cider vinegar and add a splash of apple juice or cider to the cooking liquid. This will create a slightly sweet and tangy sauce that pairs wonderfully with the pork.

Garlic and Herb Butter

Once the pork is done cooking, melt some butter with minced garlic, fresh parsley, and a squeeze of lemon juice. Pour the garlic herb butter over the sliced pork tenderloin for an extra layer of richness and flavor.

Why This Recipe Works

This Instant Pot pork tenderloin recipe is not only simple and fast, but it also delivers on flavor. By searing the pork and creating a flavorful cooking liquid, the Instant Pot locks in moisture and ensures a tender, juicy roast every time. The balance of savory seasonings, tangy balsamic vinegar, and a touch of sweetness from honey (optional) makes each bite irresistible. Whether you serve it with mashed potatoes, rice, or vegetables, this dish is a crowd-pleaser that will impress family and guests alike.

In addition, the Instant Pot makes cooking pork tenderloin so much easier than traditional methods. You get all the benefits of slow roasting in a fraction of the time, making it the perfect recipe for busy weeknights or when you want a comforting, delicious meal with minimal effort.

Final Thoughts

This Instant Pot pork tenderloin recipe is a great example of how easy it can be to make a flavorful, tender, and satisfying meal in a short amount of time. The versatility of the Instant Pot ensures that you can adjust the seasonings, sauces, and sides to fit your taste, making it a truly customizable dish. Whether you’re preparing dinner for your family or hosting friends, this pork tenderloin will be the star of the meal.

So next time you’re craving a quick yet impressive meal, reach for your Instant Pot and give this pork tenderloin recipe a try. You won’t be disappointed!
