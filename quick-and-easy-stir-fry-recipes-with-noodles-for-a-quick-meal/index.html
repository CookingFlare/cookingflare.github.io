Quick and Easy Stir Fry Recipes with Noodles for a Quick Meal


Are you in need of a quick and satisfying meal? Look no further than these delicious stir fry recipes with noodles! Packed with flavor and ready in no time, these dishes are perfect for those busy days when you want something tasty without spending hours in the kitchen. Whether you’re a meat lover or prefer a vegetarian option, there’s a stir fry recipe here for everyone. So grab your wok and let’s get cooking!

1. Classic Chicken Stir Fry with Noodles

This classic stir fry recipe is a crowd-pleaser and requires minimal ingredients. Here’s what you’ll need:

  • 2 chicken breasts, thinly sliced
  • 1 red bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 yellow bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 medium-sized onion, thinly sliced
  • 3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon oyster sauce
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 200 grams of noodles (your choice)
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Cook the noodles according to the package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  2. Heat the vegetable oil in a wok or large skillet over medium-high heat.
  3. Add the chicken slices and stir fry until they are cooked through, about 5-7 minutes. Remove from the wok and set aside.
  4. In the same wok, add the minced garlic and sliced onions. Stir fry for 2-3 minutes until fragrant.
  5. Add the sliced bell peppers and continue to stir fry for another 2-3 minutes until they are slightly softened.
  6. Return the cooked chicken to the wok and mix well with the vegetables.
  7. Add the cooked noodles to the wok, followed by soy sauce and oyster sauce. Stir fry for another 2-3 minutes until everything is well combined.
  8. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  9. Serve hot and enjoy!

2. Spicy Beef Stir Fry with Noodles

If you’re a fan of bold flavors and a little heat, this spicy beef stir fry recipe is perfect for you. Here’s what you’ll need:

  • 500 grams beef sirloin, thinly sliced
  • 1 red onion, thinly sliced
  • 1 red chili, thinly sliced
  • 3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon fish sauce
  • 1 tablespoon brown sugar
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 200 grams of noodles (your choice)
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Cook the noodles according to the package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  2. Heat the vegetable oil in a wok or large skillet over medium-high heat.
  3. Add the minced garlic, sliced red onion, and chili to the wok. Stir fry for 2-3 minutes until fragrant.
  4. Add the beef slices to the wok and stir fry until they are cooked to your liking, about 5-7 minutes.
  5. In a small bowl, mix together soy sauce, fish sauce, and brown sugar. Pour the mixture into the wok and stir fry for another 2-3 minutes until everything is well coated.
  6. Add the cooked noodles to the wok and toss until they are evenly coated with the sauce.
  7. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  8. Serve hot and enjoy the spicy goodness!

3. Veggie Stir Fry with Noodles

For all the vegetarians out there, this veggie stir fry recipe is packed with colorful and nutritious ingredients. Here’s what you’ll need:

  • 1 carrot, julienned
  • 1 zucchini, sliced
  • 1 red bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 yellow bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 cup broccoli florets
  • 3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon hoisin sauce
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 200 grams of noodles (your choice)
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Cook the noodles according to the package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  2. Heat the vegetable oil in a wok or large skillet over medium-high heat.
  3. Add the minced garlic and stir fry for 1-2 minutes until fragrant.
  4. Add the julienned carrot, sliced zucchini, bell peppers, and broccoli florets to the wok. Stir fry for 4-5 minutes until the vegetables are tender-crisp.
  5. In a small bowl, mix together soy sauce and hoisin sauce. Pour the mixture into the wok and stir fry for another 2-3 minutes until the vegetables are well coated.
  6. Add the cooked noodles to the wok and toss until they are evenly mixed with the vegetables and sauce.
  7. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  8. Serve hot and enjoy the burst of flavors!

These quick and easy stir fry recipes with noodles are perfect for when you need a delicious meal in a hurry. Experiment with different ingredients and sauces to create your own unique variations. Don’t forget to share your creations with us in the comments below. Happy cooking!
