Not Just for Breakfast: Easy and Tasty Breakfast Sandwich Recipes


Breakfast sandwiches have come a long way since their humble beginnings as a simple egg and cheese meal. With countless flavor combinations and ingredients, they’ve become versatile, delicious, and customizable to suit anyone’s taste. Here are some easy and tasty breakfast sandwich recipes that aren’t just for breakfast!

Spicy Mexican-Style Breakfast Sandwich

Spice up your morning with this flavorsome breakfast sandwich. Ingredients include:

  • 1 crispy bacon strip
  • 1 large egg
  • 1/2 avocado, sliced
  • Small handful of arugula
  • Flavor bomb hot sauce (optional)

Heap the cooked bacon on the bun bottom, and top it with a sunny-side-up or over-easy egg. Arrange the sliced avocado and arugula on top, and finish the sandwich off with a shopping of hot sauce for an extra kick.

Classic Croissant Breakfast Sandwich

Indulge in a flaky, buttery croissant filled with the goodness of a breakfast sandwich. You’ll need:

  • 1 flaky croissant
  • 1 egg, any style
  • 1/4 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Start by toasting the croissant, then layer on the cooked egg and cheese. Finish off with a sprinkle of salt and pepper for that perfect balance of savory and sweet.

Savory Veggie Breakfast Sandwich

This vegetarian breakfast sandwich packs a nutritious punch with a medley of vegetables:

  • 1 bagel or English muffin, toasted
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1/2 cup canned diced green chilies
  • 1/2 cup shredded jack cheese
  • 1/4 cup chopped tomatoes
  • 1/4 cup sliced mushrooms
  • 1/4 cup roasted red peppers
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Heat the olive oil in a small skillet over medium heat. Add the green chilies, tomatoes, mushrooms, and roasted red peppers and cook until softened. Fill the bagel or English muffin with the vegetable mixture and shredded cheese, and sprinkle with salt and pepper to complete the sandwich.

Breakfast Pizzadelight

This miniature pizza expands the traditional breakfast sandwich to include a delightful crust:

  • 1 refrigerated pizza dough
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup cooked crumbled sausage or bacon
  • 1/2 cup sliced spinach
  • 1/4 cup thinly sliced red onion
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Preheat the oven to 400°F (200°C). Roll out the pizza dough on a floured surface to about 1/4 inch thickness. Place on a baking sheet and bake for 10 minutes. Remove from the oven, and layer on the cooked sausage or bacon, spinach, red onion, and cheese. Return to the oven and bake for an additional five minutes until the cheese is melted and the crust is golden brown. Slice into four portions and let the slices cool slightly before serving.

Far from just a grab-and-go breakfast, these five easy and tasty breakfast sandwich recipes have something to offer for everyone. From spicy Mexican to classic croissant and everything in between, satisfy your cravings with endless flavor combinations that make your taste buds dance with delight. Enjoy!
