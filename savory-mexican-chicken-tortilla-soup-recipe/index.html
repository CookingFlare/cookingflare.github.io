Savory Mexican Chicken Tortilla Soup Recipe


If you’re looking for a hearty, flavorful dish that warms both the body and soul, look no further than a bowl of Savory Mexican Chicken Tortilla Soup. This iconic Mexican dish combines tender chicken, vibrant vegetables, and crispy tortilla strips in a spicy broth that’s nothing short of comforting. Perfect for chilly evenings or a casual gathering, this soup is bound to impress your family and friends. Let’s dive into the details of crafting this delicious meal.

Ingredients

For the Soup

  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 medium onion, diced
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 bell pepper, diced (red or green)
  • 2 medium carrots, diced
  • 2 stalks celery, diced
  • 1 jalapeño pepper, seeded and minced (optional for extra heat)
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon smoked paprika
  • 1 teaspoon chili powder
  • 1 can (14.5 oz) diced tomatoes with green chilies
  • 6 cups chicken broth
  • 2 cups cooked chicken, shredded (rotisserie chicken works great)
  • 1 can (15 oz) black beans, rinsed and drained
  • 1 cup corn (fresh, frozen, or canned)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Juice of 1 lime

For the Tortilla Strips

  • 4 corn tortillas
  • Vegetable oil for frying
  • Salt to taste

Garnishes

  • Sour cream or Greek yogurt
  • Fresh cilantro, chopped
  • Avocado, diced
  • Shredded cheese (cheddar or queso fresco)
  • Lime wedges

Instructions

Step 1: Prepare the Tortilla Strips

  1. Cut the Tortillas: Stack the corn tortillas and slice them into thin strips, about 1/4 inch wide.
  2. Heat the Oil: In a deep skillet or pot, heat about 1/2 inch of vegetable oil over medium-high heat.
  3. Fry the Strips: Once the oil is hot, carefully add the tortilla strips in batches. Fry until they are golden brown and crispy, about 2-3 minutes. Be sure to flip them occasionally for even cooking.
  4. Drain and Season: Remove the tortilla strips with a slotted spoon and place them on a paper towel-lined plate to drain excess oil. Sprinkle with salt while they are still hot.

Step 2: Make the Soup

  1. Sauté the Vegetables: In a large stockpot or Dutch oven, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the diced onion and sauté until translucent, about 5 minutes. Stir in the minced garlic and cook for another minute until fragrant.
  2. Add the Peppers and Carrots: Incorporate the bell pepper, carrots, celery, and jalapeño (if using) into the pot. Sauté for an additional 5-7 minutes until the vegetables begin to soften.
  3. Spice It Up: Sprinkle in the ground cumin, smoked paprika, and chili powder. Stir well to coat the vegetables with the spices, allowing the flavors to meld for about 2 minutes.
  4. Combine the Tomatoes and Broth: Pour in the canned tomatoes with their juices and the chicken broth. Bring the mixture to a simmer.
  5. Add the Chicken and Beans: Stir in the shredded chicken and black beans. Let the soup simmer for about 15-20 minutes, allowing the flavors to combine.
  6. Add Corn and Season: In the last 5 minutes of cooking, add the corn and season with salt and pepper to taste. Squeeze in the juice of one lime for brightness.

Step 3: Serve

  1. Ladle the Soup: Once the soup is ready, ladle it into bowls.
  2. Top with Garnishes: Top each bowl with crispy tortilla strips, a dollop of sour cream, diced avocado, fresh cilantro, and shredded cheese. Serve with lime wedges on the side for an extra burst of flavor.

Tips for the Perfect Tortilla Soup

  • Customize Your Ingredients: Feel free to customize your soup by adding other vegetables like zucchini or bell peppers. If you prefer a vegetarian version, simply replace the chicken with more beans or tofu.
  • Make it Ahead: This soup can be made ahead of time and stored in the refrigerator for up to 3 days. Reheat gently on the stovetop before serving.
  • Storage Tips: To keep the tortilla strips from getting soggy, store them separately from the soup until you are ready to serve.
  • Spice Levels: Adjust the heat by adding more or less jalapeño, or even a dash of hot sauce to your bowl.

Nutritional Benefits

This Savory Mexican Chicken Tortilla Soup is not only delicious but also packed with nutrients. Here are some health benefits of the main ingredients:

  • Chicken: A great source of lean protein, which is essential for muscle health and repair.
  • Beans: Packed with fiber and protein, black beans are beneficial for digestive health and can help keep you feeling full longer.
  • Vegetables: The assortment of vegetables provides essential vitamins and minerals, contributing to overall health.
  • Avocado: A source of healthy fats, avocados can help improve heart health and provide a creamy texture to your soup.

Conclusion

Savory Mexican Chicken Tortilla Soup is a delightful blend of flavors and textures that is sure to become a staple in your home. Whether enjoyed on a chilly night or served at a festive gathering, this dish captures the essence of Mexican cuisine and comfort food. With its warm broth, tender chicken, and crispy tortilla toppings, each spoonful is a celebration of taste and tradition.

So why wait? Gather your ingredients, follow this simple recipe, and enjoy a bowl of homemade goodness that will have everyone coming back for seconds. Happy cooking!
