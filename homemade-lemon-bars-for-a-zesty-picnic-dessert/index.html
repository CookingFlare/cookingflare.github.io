Homemade Lemon Bars for a Zesty Picnic Dessert


When it comes to a delightful picnic dessert, nothing beats the refreshing tang of homemade lemon bars. These zesty treats are a perfect balance of sweet and tangy, making them a crowd-pleaser for all ages. Whether you’re planning a family picnic, a romantic outdoor date, or a casual get-together with friends, these homemade lemon bars are sure to brighten up the occasion. So, grab your picnic basket and let’s dive into the delightful world of zesty homemade lemon bars!

Ingredients

For the Crust:

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1/4 cup powdered sugar
  • A pinch of salt

For the Lemon Filling:

  • 1 1/2 cups granulated sugar
  • 1/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • 4 large eggs
  • Zest of 2 lemons
  • 1/2 cup freshly squeezed lemon juice
  • Powdered sugar for dusting

Instructions

Step 1: Preparing the Crust

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C) and line an 8x8 inch baking pan with parchment paper.
  2. In a mixing bowl, combine the flour, powdered sugar, and a pinch of salt.
  3. Cut in the softened butter using a pastry cutter or fork until the mixture resembles coarse crumbs.
  4. Press the mixture into the bottom of the prepared baking pan and bake for 15-20 minutes, or until lightly golden.

Step 2: Making the Lemon Filling

  1. In a separate bowl, whisk together the granulated sugar and flour.
  2. Add the eggs, lemon zest, and lemon juice, and whisk until well combined.
  3. Pour the lemon filling over the baked crust and return the pan to the oven.
  4. Bake for an additional 20-25 minutes, or until the filling is set and the edges are lightly golden.

Step 3: Finishing Touches

  1. Allow the lemon bars to cool completely in the pan on a wire rack.
  2. Once cooled, dust the top with powdered sugar.
  3. Use the parchment paper to lift the bars out of the pan, then slice into squares.

Serving Suggestion

These homemade lemon bars are best served chilled, making them the perfect make-ahead dessert for your picnic. Pack them in an airtight container and keep them cool until it’s time to indulge in their zesty goodness.

Picnic Perfect

The bright, citrusy flavor of these homemade lemon bars is like a burst of sunshine in every bite, making them an ideal dessert for a picnic. Whether you’re spreading out a blanket in the park, lounging by the beach, or enjoying a leisurely afternoon in your backyard, these lemon bars are a delightful addition to your outdoor spread.

So, the next time you’re planning a picnic, consider whipping up a batch of these homemade lemon bars to add a zesty touch to your outdoor gathering. Your friends and family will thank you for introducing them to this irresistible citrus delight!

With their tangy zest and buttery crust, these homemade lemon bars are a true crowd-pleaser, perfect for any picnic or outdoor gathering. Their vibrant flavor and easy portability make them an ideal dessert to enjoy in the great outdoors. So, pack your picnic basket, grab a few lemon bars, and savor the sunny flavors of this delightful treat!
