Hearty Low Carb Stuffed Bell Peppers


Looking for a delicious and satisfying low carb meal? Look no further! These hearty stuffed bell peppers are not only packed with flavor but also low in carbohydrates, making them the perfect option for those following a low carb or keto diet. With a colorful array of ingredients and a burst of flavors, this recipe is sure to become a family favorite. Let’s dive right in!

Ingredients

  • 4 large bell peppers (any color)
  • 1 pound ground turkey (or your preferred protein)
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 small onion, diced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup cauliflower rice
  • 1 cup chopped spinach
  • 1 can diced tomatoes, drained
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C). Line a baking dish with parchment paper or lightly grease with olive oil.

  2. Slice off the tops of the bell peppers and remove the seeds and membranes. Place the hollowed-out bell peppers in the prepared baking dish, open side up.

  3. In a large skillet, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the diced onion and minced garlic, sautéing until they become fragrant and translucent.

  4. Add the ground turkey to the skillet, breaking it up with a spoon. Cook until the turkey is browned and cooked through.

  5. Stir in the cauliflower rice, chopped spinach, diced tomatoes, dried oregano, dried basil, salt, and black pepper. Cook for an additional 5 minutes, allowing the flavors to meld together.

  6. Spoon the turkey and vegetable mixture into the hollowed-out bell peppers, filling them to the top. Press the filling down gently to ensure they are packed tightly.

  7. Sprinkle the shredded mozzarella cheese evenly over the stuffed bell peppers.

  8. Cover the baking dish with foil and bake in the preheated oven for 25 minutes.

  9. Remove the foil and continue baking for an additional 10 minutes, or until the cheese is golden and bubbly.

  10. Carefully remove the stuffed bell peppers from the oven and let them cool for a few minutes before serving.

Serve these hearty low carb stuffed bell peppers as a main dish or alongside a fresh salad for a complete and satisfying meal. The combination of the tender bell pepper, flavorful turkey and vegetable filling, and gooey melted cheese creates a mouthwatering experience that will leave you craving more.

Feel free to customize this recipe to your liking by adding your favorite spices or substituting ingredients. You can also prepare the filling ahead of time and store it in the refrigerator, making it a convenient option for busy weeknight dinners.

Enjoy the deliciousness of these low carb stuffed bell peppers while staying on track with your low carb lifestyle. They are not only visually appealing but also packed with nutrients, making them a wholesome choice for any occasion. So, gather your ingredients and get ready to savor every bite of this delightful dish!

Happy cooking!
