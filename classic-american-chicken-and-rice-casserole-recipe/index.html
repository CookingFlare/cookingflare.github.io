Classic American Chicken and Rice Casserole Recipe


If you’re looking for a hearty and comforting dish that’s perfect for family dinners or potlucks, look no further than the classic American Chicken and Rice Casserole. This timeless recipe has been a staple in American households for generations, and for good reason. It’s creamy, flavorful, and oh-so-satisfying. Plus, it’s easy to make and can be customized to suit your taste preferences. Let’s dive into the details of this beloved dish and learn how to make it from scratch.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups of cooked chicken, shredded
  • 1 cup of uncooked white rice
  • 1 can of cream of chicken soup
  • 1 cup of chicken broth
  • 1 cup of sour cream
  • 1 cup of frozen peas
  • 1 cup of shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1/2 cup of diced onion
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon of dried thyme
  • 1 teaspoon of dried parsley
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 cup of breadcrumbs
  • 2 tablespoons of butter, melted

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven: Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease a 9x13-inch baking dish and set it aside.

  2. Cook the rice: In a medium saucepan, cook the white rice according to the package instructions. Once cooked, set it aside.

  3. Prepare the chicken mixture: In a large mixing bowl, combine the shredded chicken, cream of chicken soup, chicken broth, sour cream, frozen peas, cheddar cheese, diced onion, minced garlic, dried thyme, dried parsley, salt, and pepper. Mix until all ingredients are well combined.

  4. Assemble the casserole: Spread the cooked rice evenly in the prepared baking dish. Pour the chicken mixture over the rice and spread it out to cover the rice completely.

  5. Top with breadcrumbs: In a small bowl, mix the breadcrumbs with the melted butter. Sprinkle the breadcrumb mixture evenly over the chicken mixture in the baking dish.

  6. Bake the casserole: Place the baking dish in the preheated oven and bake for 30-35 minutes, or until the casserole is bubbly and the breadcrumbs are golden brown.

  7. Serve and enjoy: Once done, remove the casserole from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes before serving. Scoop out generous portions and savor the creamy, flavorful goodness of this classic American dish.

Tips for Customization

While the classic American Chicken and Rice Casserole is delicious as is, feel free to get creative and customize it to your liking. Here are a few ideas to inspire your culinary experimentation:

  • Add veggies: Mix in your favorite vegetables such as carrots, broccoli, or bell peppers to enhance the nutritional value and add a pop of color to the casserole.
  • Swap the protein: If you’re not a fan of chicken, try using cooked turkey, ham, or even tofu as a protein alternative.
  • Experiment with cheeses: Instead of cheddar, try using Monterey Jack, mozzarella, or Swiss cheese for a different flavor profile.
  • Seasoning variations: Feel free to experiment with different herbs and spices to tailor the flavor to your preference. You can add a dash of paprika, cayenne pepper, or even curry powder for a unique twist.

Final Thoughts

The classic American Chicken and Rice Casserole is a versatile and satisfying dish that has stood the test of time for good reason. Its creamy texture, savory flavors, and simple preparation make it a go-to recipe for busy weeknights or special gatherings. Whether you follow the traditional recipe or put your own spin on it, this comforting casserole is sure to become a favorite in your recipe repertoire. So, gather your ingredients, preheat your oven, and get ready to savor the heartwarming goodness of this beloved American classic.
