Easy Dairy-Free Tomato Basil Pasta for Quick Weeknight Dinners


Are you looking for a simple, delicious, and dairy-free recipe to whip up on a busy weeknight? Look no further! This Easy Dairy-Free Tomato Basil Pasta is a perfect solution for those nights when you need a quick and satisfying meal without the fuss. With just a handful of ingredients and minimal prep time, you can have a flavorful pasta dish on the table in no time.

Ingredients:

  • 8 oz. of pasta of your choice (spaghetti, linguine, or penne work well)
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can (14 oz.) diced tomatoes
  • 1/4 cup fresh basil, chopped
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Red pepper flakes (optional, for a kick of heat)

Instructions:

  1. Cook the Pasta: Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Add the pasta and cook according to the package instructions until al dente. Drain and set aside.

  2. Prepare the Sauce: In a large skillet, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the minced garlic and sauté for about 1 minute, or until fragrant.

  3. Add the Tomatoes and Basil: Pour the can of diced tomatoes into the skillet, including the juice. Stir in the chopped basil and season with salt and pepper to taste. If you like a little heat, now’s the time to sprinkle in some red pepper flakes.

  4. Simmer the Sauce: Allow the sauce to simmer for about 10 minutes, stirring occasionally, until it has thickened slightly and the flavors have melded together.

  5. Combine the Pasta and Sauce: Add the cooked pasta to the skillet with the tomato basil sauce. Toss everything together until the pasta is well coated with the sauce.

  6. Serve and Enjoy: Divide the pasta into serving bowls and garnish with additional fresh basil if desired. Serve hot and enjoy your delicious dairy-free tomato basil pasta!

Why This Recipe Works:

This recipe is a winner for several reasons. First and foremost, it’s incredibly easy to make, making it perfect for those evenings when you’re short on time but still want a homemade meal. The combination of garlic, tomatoes, and fresh basil creates a burst of flavor that will have your taste buds singing. Plus, this recipe is dairy-free, making it suitable for those with lactose intolerance or dairy allergies.

The beauty of this dish lies in its simplicity. By using just a few high-quality ingredients, you can create a dish that is both satisfying and full of fresh flavors. The acidity of the tomatoes, the earthiness of the basil, and the richness of the olive oil come together to create a harmonious blend that will leave you craving more.

Final Thoughts:

This Easy Dairy-Free Tomato Basil Pasta is a versatile dish that can be easily customized to suit your preferences. Feel free to add in some sautéed vegetables, grilled chicken, or a sprinkle of vegan parmesan for extra pizzazz. Whether you’re cooking for yourself, your family, or guests, this recipe is sure to be a hit.

So, next time you’re in need of a quick and delicious weeknight meal, give this dairy-free tomato basil pasta a try. It’s a no-fuss recipe that delivers on both flavor and convenience, making it a go-to option for busy evenings. Enjoy!
