Satisfying Low Carb Chicken Fajita Bowl


Are you looking for a delicious and satisfying low-carb recipe that will leave you feeling full and satisfied? Look no further than this mouthwatering Satisfying Low Carb Chicken Fajita Bowl! Packed with flavor and nutritious ingredients, this dish is perfect for those following a low-carb or keto diet.

Ingredients

For the chicken marinade:

  • 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons lime juice
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • Salt and pepper, to taste

For the fajita vegetables:

  • 1 red bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 green bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 yellow bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 onion, sliced
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • Salt and pepper, to taste

For the cauliflower rice:

  • 1 head of cauliflower, riced
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • Salt and pepper, to taste

For the toppings:

  • Avocado slices
  • Fresh cilantro, chopped
  • Lime wedges
  • Sour cream (optional)

Instructions

  1. Start by marinating the chicken. In a bowl, combine the olive oil, lime juice, minced garlic, chili powder, cumin, salt, and pepper. Mix well. Place the chicken breasts in the marinade and coat them thoroughly. Let the chicken marinate for at least 30 minutes, or overnight for maximum flavor.

  2. While the chicken is marinating, prepare the fajita vegetables. Heat a tablespoon of olive oil in a skillet over medium heat. Add the sliced bell peppers and onion to the skillet and sauté until they are tender and slightly caramelized. Sprinkle the chili powder, cumin, salt, and pepper over the vegetables and stir well. Remove the vegetables from the skillet and set aside.

  3. In the same skillet, add another tablespoon of olive oil and the riced cauliflower. Sauté the cauliflower rice until it is cooked through and slightly golden. Season with salt and pepper to taste.

  4. Now it’s time to cook the marinated chicken. Heat a grill pan or skillet over medium-high heat. Cook the chicken breasts for about 6-8 minutes per side, or until they are cooked through and no longer pink in the center. Remove the chicken from the heat and let it rest for a few minutes before slicing into strips.

  5. Assemble your low-carb chicken fajita bowls. Start with a bed of cauliflower rice, then top it with the sliced chicken, sautéed fajita vegetables, avocado slices, and a sprinkle of fresh cilantro. Squeeze some lime juice over the top for an extra burst of flavor. If desired, add a dollop of sour cream for a creamy touch.

  6. Serve your Satisfying Low Carb Chicken Fajita Bowls immediately and enjoy! This dish is perfect for a quick and healthy weeknight dinner or for meal prep. It’s packed with protein, fiber, and essential nutrients, making it a satisfying and nutritious choice.

Conclusion

This Satisfying Low Carb Chicken Fajita Bowl is a delicious and healthy option for those following a low-carb or keto lifestyle. With tender and flavorful chicken, sautéed fajita vegetables, and cauliflower rice, this dish is bursting with flavor and will leave you feeling satisfied. Give it a try and enjoy a tasty meal that won’t derail your healthy eating goals.
