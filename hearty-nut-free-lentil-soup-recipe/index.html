Hearty Nut-Free Lentil Soup Recipe


When it comes to wholesome and comforting meals, a hearty lentil soup is a timeless classic. Packed with protein, fiber, and an array of essential nutrients, this nut-free lentil soup is not only delicious but also a nourishing addition to your culinary repertoire. Whether you’re a seasoned cook or new to the kitchen, this recipe is simple, versatile, and perfect for any occasion.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup dried green or brown lentils
  • 1 large onion, finely chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 carrots, diced
  • 2 celery stalks, diced
  • 1 can (14 oz) diced tomatoes
  • 6 cups vegetable or chicken broth
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon ground coriander
  • 1 teaspoon smoked paprika
  • 1 bay leaf
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley or cilantro for garnish
  • Olive oil for sautéing

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the Lentils: Rinse the lentils under cold water and drain. Set aside.

  2. Sauté the Aromatics: In a large pot, heat a drizzle of olive oil over medium heat. Add the chopped onion, garlic, carrots, and celery. Sauté for 5-7 minutes until the vegetables are softened and the onions are translucent.

  3. Add the Spices: Stir in the ground cumin, coriander, and smoked paprika. Let the spices toast for a minute to release their flavors.

  4. Simmer the Soup: Pour in the diced tomatoes, lentils, and bay leaf. Add the vegetable or chicken broth and bring the soup to a gentle boil. Reduce the heat to low, cover the pot, and let it simmer for 25-30 minutes or until the lentils are tender.

  5. Season and Garnish: Once the lentils are cooked, season the soup with salt and pepper to taste. Remove the bay leaf. Ladle the soup into bowls and garnish with fresh parsley or cilantro.

  6. Serve and Enjoy: This nut-free lentil soup is delightful on its own or paired with a slice of crusty bread. It’s a satisfying meal that warms the soul and nourishes the body.

Tips and Variations:

  • For added richness, drizzle a little extra virgin olive oil over the soup before serving.
  • Feel free to customize the vegetables based on what you have on hand. Bell peppers, zucchini, or spinach are wonderful additions.
  • If you prefer a smoother texture, blend a portion of the soup with an immersion blender before serving.
  • This soup freezes well, making it a convenient option for meal prepping.

Nutritional Benefits:

Lentils are a nutritional powerhouse, offering a generous dose of protein, fiber, and various vitamins and minerals. They are a fantastic source of plant-based protein, making this soup an excellent option for vegetarians and vegans. Additionally, the combination of vegetables and spices in this recipe provides a spectrum of antioxidants and essential nutrients, contributing to overall well-being.

Final Thoughts:

This nut-free lentil soup is a versatile and satisfying dish that can be enjoyed year-round. Its simplicity and heartiness make it a go-to meal for busy weeknights or lazy weekends. Whether you’re seeking a nourishing bowl of comfort or looking to expand your culinary skills, this recipe is a wonderful addition to any cook’s arsenal. So, grab your apron, gather your ingredients, and savor the delightful flavors of this hearty lentil soup. Cheers to good food and good health!
