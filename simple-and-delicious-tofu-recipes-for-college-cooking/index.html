Simple and Delicious Tofu Recipes for College Cooking

3 min · 566 words

Cooking in college can be challenging, especially when you have limited time and resources. But fear not, because tofu is here to save the day! This versatile and nutritious ingredient is perfect for quick and easy meals that will satisfy your taste buds and keep you energized throughout the day. In this blog post, we will explore some simple and delicious tofu recipes that are perfect for college cooking.

1. Crispy Tofu Stir-Fry

Ingredients:

  • 1 block of firm tofu, drained and cubed
  • 2 tablespoons of soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon of cornstarch
  • 1 tablespoon of vegetable oil
  • 1 cup of mixed vegetables (such as bell peppers, broccoli, and carrots)
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, combine the soy sauce and cornstarch. Add the tofu cubes and gently toss to coat.
  2. Heat the vegetable oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat. Add the tofu and cook until crispy and golden brown on all sides.
  3. Remove the tofu from the skillet and set aside. In the same skillet, add the mixed vegetables and garlic. Cook until the vegetables are tender-crisp.
  4. Return the tofu to the skillet and stir to combine. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  5. Serve the crispy tofu stir-fry over cooked rice or noodles for a complete meal.

2. Tofu Scramble

Ingredients:

  • 1 block of firm tofu, drained and crumbled
  • 1 tablespoon of olive oil
  • 1 small onion, diced
  • 1 bell pepper, diced
  • 1 cup of spinach
  • 1 teaspoon of turmeric
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Heat the olive oil in a skillet over medium heat. Add the onion and bell pepper and sauté until softened.
  2. Add the crumbled tofu to the skillet and cook for 5-7 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  3. Stir in the turmeric, salt, and pepper. Add the spinach and cook until wilted.
  4. Serve the tofu scramble as a filling for breakfast burritos, on toast, or alongside roasted potatoes for a hearty breakfast or brunch option.

3. Baked Tofu Nuggets

Ingredients:

  • 1 block of firm tofu, drained and cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 1/2 cup of breadcrumbs
  • 1/4 cup of nutritional yeast
  • 1 teaspoon of paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon of garlic powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Cooking spray

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 400°F (200°C) and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
  2. In a bowl, combine the breadcrumbs, nutritional yeast, paprika, garlic powder, salt, and pepper.
  3. Dip each tofu piece into the breadcrumb mixture, pressing gently to adhere.
  4. Place the coated tofu pieces on the prepared baking sheet. Lightly spray the tops with cooking spray.
  5. Bake for 20-25 minutes, or until the tofu nuggets are crispy and golden brown.
  6. Serve the baked tofu nuggets with your favorite dipping sauce, such as barbecue sauce or honey mustard.

Conclusion

Tofu is an excellent ingredient for college cooking. It’s affordable, easy to prepare, and packed with protein. These simple and delicious tofu recipes will help you create satisfying meals that are both healthy and flavorful. Whether you’re a vegetarian, vegan, or simply looking to incorporate more plant-based options into your diet, tofu is a versatile ingredient that will never disappoint. So, grab a block of tofu, get creative in the kitchen, and enjoy these tasty recipes during your college years!
