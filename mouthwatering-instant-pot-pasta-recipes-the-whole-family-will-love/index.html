Mouthwatering Instant Pot Pasta Recipes the Whole Family Will Love


Cooking pasta has never been easier and more delicious than with the Instant Pot. This versatile kitchen appliance not only speeds up the cooking process but also infuses flavors in a way that traditional methods often miss. In this blog post, we’ll explore a variety of mouthwatering Instant Pot pasta recipes that are sure to delight the whole family. From classic spaghetti to creamy sauces and veggie-packed dishes, these recipes are bound to become staples in your household.

Why Choose Instant Pot for Pasta?

The Instant Pot is a game-changer for home cooks. With its pressure cooking capabilities, it can reduce cooking time significantly while retaining the flavors and nutrients in your ingredients. Here are some reasons why you should consider using the Instant Pot for your pasta dishes:

  • Speed: Instant Pot can cook pasta in a fraction of the time it takes on the stovetop.
  • Flavor: Cooking pasta in a flavorful broth or sauce allows the noodles to absorb all those delicious flavors.
  • One-Pot Wonder: Most recipes can be made in a single pot, reducing cleanup time and effort.
  • Versatile: You can cook a wide range of pasta types and pair them with various sauces, proteins, and vegetables.

Now, let’s dive into some fantastic Instant Pot pasta recipes that your family will love!

1. Creamy Instant Pot Garlic Parmesan Pasta

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound fettuccine pasta
  • 4 cups chicken broth
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley, chopped (for garnish)

Instructions:

  1. Sauté: Set the Instant Pot to sauté mode. Add butter and minced garlic, cooking until fragrant (about 1 minute).
  2. Add Ingredients: Pour in the chicken broth and add the fettuccine pasta. Make sure the pasta is submerged in the liquid.
  3. Pressure Cook: Close the lid and set the valve to sealing. Cook on high pressure for 4 minutes.
  4. Quick Release: Once the cooking time is up, perform a quick release.
  5. Stir in Cream and Cheese: Open the lid and stir in the heavy cream and grated Parmesan cheese. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  6. Serve: Garnish with fresh parsley and serve immediately.

This creamy garlic Parmesan pasta is rich, comforting, and perfect for a cozy family dinner.

2. Instant Pot Spaghetti and Meatballs

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound spaghetti
  • 1 jar (24 oz) marinara sauce
  • 2 cups water
  • 1 pound frozen meatballs (store-bought or homemade)
  • 1 teaspoon Italian seasoning
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Grated Parmesan cheese (for serving)
  • Fresh basil (for garnish)

Instructions:

  1. Layer Ingredients: Pour the marinara sauce into the Instant Pot. Place the frozen meatballs on top, followed by the spaghetti arranged in a crisscross pattern.
  2. Add Water: Pour the water over the spaghetti and sprinkle Italian seasoning, salt, and pepper.
  3. Pressure Cook: Close the lid and set the valve to sealing. Cook on high pressure for 8 minutes.
  4. Natural Release: Allow for a natural pressure release for 5 minutes, then perform a quick release.
  5. Serve: Stir gently and serve with grated Parmesan cheese and fresh basil.

This classic dish takes less than 30 minutes from start to finish and is always a hit with kids and adults alike!

3. Instant Pot Pesto Pasta with Cherry Tomatoes

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound penne pasta
  • 2 cups vegetable broth
  • 1 cup basil pesto
  • 1 pint cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1 cup spinach
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Pine nuts (for garnish)
  • Grated Parmesan cheese (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Combine Ingredients: Add penne pasta and vegetable broth to the Instant Pot. Stir to ensure the pasta is submerged.
  2. Pressure Cook: Close the lid and cook on high pressure for 4 minutes.
  3. Quick Release: Once cooking is complete, perform a quick release.
  4. Mix in Pesto and Veggies: Stir in the basil pesto, cherry tomatoes, and spinach. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  5. Serve: Top with pine nuts and grated Parmesan cheese if desired.

This vibrant pasta dish is not only easy to make but also a great way to sneak in some vegetables!

4. Instant Pot Mac and Cheese

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound elbow macaroni
  • 4 cups water
  • 4 cups shredded sharp cheddar cheese
  • 2 cups milk
  • 1 teaspoon mustard powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Add Pasta and Water: Place elbow macaroni and water in the Instant Pot.
  2. Pressure Cook: Close the lid and set to high pressure for 4 minutes.
  3. Quick Release: Perform a quick release after cooking.
  4. Mix in Cheese and Milk: Add shredded cheddar cheese, milk, mustard powder, salt, and pepper. Stir until creamy and well combined.
  5. Serve: Enjoy this classic comfort food that’s sure to please pasta lovers of all ages!

5. Instant Pot Veggie Lasagna

Ingredients:

  • 9 lasagna noodles
  • 2 cups marinara sauce
  • 1 cup ricotta cheese
  • 2 cups spinach
  • 1 zucchini, thinly sliced
  • 1 bell pepper, diced
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1 teaspoon Italian seasoning
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Layer Ingredients: Spread a thin layer of marinara sauce on the bottom of the Instant Pot. Place 3 lasagna noodles on top. Layer with ricotta cheese, spinach, zucchini, bell pepper, and a sprinkle of mozzarella. Repeat the layers twice more, finishing with marinara and mozzarella on top.
  2. Add Water: Pour 1 cup of water into the bottom of the Instant Pot.
  3. Pressure Cook: Close the lid and cook on high pressure for 20 minutes.
  4. Natural Release: Allow for a natural pressure release for 10 minutes before opening the lid.
  5. Serve: Let it rest for a few minutes before slicing. Enjoy a hearty and healthy lasagna that’s packed with vegetables!

6. Instant Pot Pasta Primavera

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound fusilli pasta
  • 3 cups vegetable broth
  • 1 cup broccoli florets
  • 1 bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 carrot, julienned
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon Italian seasoning
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Grated Parmesan cheese (for serving)

Instructions:

  1. Combine Ingredients: Add fusilli pasta and vegetable broth to the Instant Pot. Stir in broccoli, bell pepper, carrot, garlic powder, Italian seasoning, salt, and pepper.
  2. Pressure Cook: Close the lid and cook on high pressure for 4 minutes.
  3. Quick Release: Perform a quick release once cooking is complete.
  4. Serve: Toss gently and serve with grated Parmesan cheese.

Pasta Primavera is a delightful way to incorporate seasonal vegetables into your meals, making it a great option for healthy eating.

Tips for Perfect Instant Pot Pasta

  1. Water to Pasta Ratio: The general rule of thumb is to use 1.5 times the amount of liquid to pasta. For every cup of pasta, add 1.5 cups of liquid.
  2. Avoid Overcooking: Since the Instant Pot retains heat, remember to adjust cooking times from traditional stovetop methods. Most pasta cooks in 3-8 minutes under pressure.
  3. Quick Release: For most pasta dishes, a quick release is ideal to prevent overcooking.
  4. Flavor Infusion: Always consider cooking pasta in broth or adding spices and herbs to the water for extra flavor.
  5. Experiment: Don’t hesitate to try different pasta shapes, sauces, and mix-ins to create your unique family favorites.

Conclusion

The Instant Pot revolutionizes the way we cook pasta, making it not only faster but also more flavorful. These mouthwatering recipes are sure to please even the pickiest eaters in your family. Whether it’s a comforting mac and cheese, a veggie-packed lasagna, or a creamy garlic Parmesan dish, there’s something for everyone to enjoy. So grab your Instant Pot and get ready to create some delightful pasta dishes that the whole family will love!

Happy cooking!
