Creepy Crawly Pasta Salad: A Spooky Halloween Side Dish Recipe


Looking for something tantalizingly delightfully evil for your Halloween party? Say hello to the Creepy Crawly Pasta Salad! Packed with spooky surprises, this eerie side dish will be sure to send shivers up everyone’s spines.

Ingredients

  • 16 oz pasta (shaped like ghosts, witches, or pumpkins - try looking for Halloween-shaped pasta in your local grocery store!)
  • 2 cups cooked and cooled chicken (or “mock chicken” for a vegetarian/vegan option), diced
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1 ripe avocado, diced
  • 1/2 cup black olives, sliced
  • 1 bell pepper (pick a color you like - orange or yellow will match the overall theme!), chopped
  • 1/4 cup diced red onion
  • 3/4 cup cream cheese, softened
  • 1/4 cup pesto
  • Salt and pepper (to taste)

Instructions

  1. Grab your creativity and cook that pasta! Follow the package instructions. When done, drain and rinse under cold water to stop the cooking process. Set it aside to cool.

  2. While your pasta is cooling, take the chance to prepare your vegetables. Chop up those cherry tomatoes, bell pepper, and red onion. Dice the avocado and slice those black olives. If you have help around, this might be a great moment to get the kids involved in chopping fruits and veggies under an adult’s supervision.

  3. Toss your chopped vegetables in a large mixing bowl. Remember our Halloween theme? If you can find pasta shapes resembling ghosts, witches, or pumpkins, feel free to mix them in for a fun twist.

  4. In a separate small bowl, mix the softened cream cheese and pesto until it’s all smooth and well combined. If you prefer, you can substitute the pesto with a cup of mayo for a richer flavor.

  5. Season the mixture with salt and cooked chicken, mix everything together to create a spectacularly spooky salady masterpiece. Remember, that’s the fun part - give it a good toss around until everything is well combined.

  6. Chill your salad in the refrigerator for at least an hour, so all those amazing flavors can get to know each other better. This allows the colors to blend together for a spooky effect.

  7. When you’re ready to serve, take the Creepy Crawly Pasta Salad out of the fridge and give it a final stir. Get ready to be amazed by the vibrant colors and playful shapes!

Serve with a Smile

In a Halloween-themed party, presentation matters. Plate this chilling delight in a large serving dish, surrounded by spooky decorations or themed party props. You can even go as far as setting the table with witch-themed minions, to match the creepy crawly namesake.

For those still hungry, you can also prepare eerie themed desserts to finish off the meal with a bang. Try making your very own mummy hot dogs, blood-red fruit punch, or even a batch of chocolate-covered insects to serve alongside this utterly beguiling pasta salad.

As your guests dig in, you can even share the story of how you came up with this masterful creation and the twist of mystery in each delicious bite. But remember, don’t let them know about your secret weapon: black food coloring in the mayonnaise will give the final dish a fun-filled, segment-twisting feel.

Enjoy your Creepy Crawly Pasta Salad at your Halloween gathering and give your friends a deliciously fun experience they won’t forget! Happy haunting!
