Spanish Inspired Bacon-Wrapped Dates Recipe


When it comes to appetizers, few can rival the delightful combination of sweet and savory that Spanish-inspired bacon-wrapped dates offer. These little morsels are not only a culinary treat but also a testament to the beautiful flavors found in Spanish cuisine. Perfect for gatherings, parties, or simply to indulge in a delicious snack, this recipe will walk you through the steps to create these delectable bites that are sure to impress your family and friends.

The Origins of Bacon-Wrapped Dates

Bacon-wrapped dates have roots in Spanish tapas, where small plates of various dishes are shared among friends and family. The contrast between the rich, smoky flavor of the bacon and the natural sweetness of the dates creates a flavor profile that is both complex and satisfying. Traditionally, these dates are often stuffed with cheese or nuts, adding an extra layer of decadence. This recipe takes inspiration from those origins, elevating the dish with a few thoughtful twists.

Ingredients You Will Need

To make this Spanish inspired bacon-wrapped dates recipe, you will need the following ingredients:

For the Dates:

  • 20 large Medjool dates
  • 10 slices of thick-cut bacon
  • 1 cup of crumbled goat cheese (or blue cheese for a stronger flavor)
  • 1/4 cup of chopped almonds (optional)
  • 1 tablespoon of fresh rosemary, finely chopped (or 1 teaspoon of dried rosemary)

For the Glaze:

  • 1/4 cup of honey
  • 1 tablespoon of balsamic vinegar
  • 1 teaspoon of smoked paprika
  • A pinch of salt and pepper

Optional for Serving:

  • Fresh rosemary sprigs for garnish
  • Toothpicks or small skewers

Preparing the Dates

Step 1: Preheat Your Oven

Begin by preheating your oven to 400°F (200°C). This high temperature will help the bacon crisp up beautifully while keeping the dates tender and warm.

Step 2: Prepare the Dates

  1. Pit the Dates: Slice each Medjool date lengthwise on one side to create a pocket. Carefully remove the pit and set the dates aside.
  2. Stuff the Dates: Using a small spoon or your fingers, fill each date with a generous amount of crumbled goat cheese. If you’re using almonds, you can drop a piece into the cheese-filled cavity as well. The nuts add a delightful crunch that complements the softness of the dates.

Step 3: Wrap with Bacon

  1. Cut each slice of bacon in half, creating two shorter strips. This size will make it easier to wrap around the dates.
  2. Take a stuffed date and wrap a piece of bacon around it, securing it with a toothpick if necessary. Repeat this for all the dates, placing them on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper.

Making the Glaze

While the dates are being prepared, you can create a simple yet delicious glaze to enhance their flavor.

  1. In a small saucepan over medium heat, combine the honey, balsamic vinegar, smoked paprika, salt, and pepper.
  2. Stir the mixture until it begins to bubble, then reduce the heat and let it simmer for about 2-3 minutes until it thickens slightly. Remove from heat and let it cool.

Baking the Bacon-Wrapped Dates

With the oven preheated and the dates wrapped, it’s time to bake them to perfection.

  1. Place the baking sheet in the oven and bake the bacon-wrapped dates for about 15-20 minutes or until the bacon is crispy and golden brown. Keep an eye on them to prevent burning, as ovens can vary in temperature.
  2. Halfway through baking, brush some of the glaze over the dates to add an extra layer of flavor. You can also flip them halfway through to ensure even cooking.

Serving Suggestions

Once the bacon-wrapped dates are out of the oven, let them cool for a few minutes. Arrange them on a serving platter and drizzle the remaining glaze over the top. Garnish with fresh rosemary sprigs for a touch of color and aroma.

These delightful bites can be served warm or at room temperature. They pair beautifully with a variety of beverages, from a crisp white wine to a robust red. If you’re hosting a gathering, consider serving them alongside other Spanish-inspired tapas for a complete culinary experience.

Tips for Perfect Bacon-Wrapped Dates

  • Choosing the Right Dates: Medjool dates are the best choice for this recipe due to their size and sweetness. Their natural caramel flavor complements the salty bacon perfectly.
  • Bacon Selection: Opt for thick-cut bacon for a heartier bite. Regular bacon may not provide the same level of crispiness and richness.
  • Cheese Variations: While goat cheese is a favorite, feel free to experiment with other cheeses like feta, cream cheese, or even manchego for a true Spanish twist.
  • Make Ahead: You can prepare the dates in advance, wrapping them in bacon and storing them in the refrigerator until you’re ready to bake. This makes them a great option for entertaining.

Nutritional Information

While these bacon-wrapped dates are indeed a treat, it’s worth noting their nutritional profile. They are high in calories due to the bacon and cheese, but they also provide a good source of fiber from the dates and healthy fats from the nuts. Enjoy them in moderation as part of a balanced diet.

Final Thoughts

The Spanish inspired bacon-wrapped dates recipe is a perfect fusion of flavors and textures that captures the heart of Spanish cuisine. Whether you’re serving them at a dinner party, a casual gathering, or simply indulging yourself, these bites are sure to be a hit. With their sweet, savory, and slightly smoky flavor, they represent the essence of good tapas—a celebration of sharing and enjoying good food with great company.

So roll up your sleeves, gather your ingredients, and get ready to impress your guests with this simple yet sophisticated dish. Once you take that first bite, you’ll understand why bacon-wrapped dates have become a beloved classic in kitchens around the world. Enjoy!
