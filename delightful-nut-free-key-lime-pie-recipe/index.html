Delightful Nut-Free Key Lime Pie Recipe


Are you a fan of tangy and sweet desserts? If so, you’re in for a treat! This delightful nut-free key lime pie recipe is a perfect combination of zesty lime flavor and creamy texture, all nestled in a buttery graham cracker crust. Whether you’re hosting a summer barbecue or simply craving a refreshing dessert, this recipe is sure to impress your taste buds and those of your guests. Plus, it’s nut-free, making it a safe and delicious option for those with nut allergies. Let’s dive into the recipe and get ready to indulge in this delightful treat.

Ingredients

For the Crust:

  • 1 ½ cups graham cracker crumbs
  • 6 tablespoons unsalted butter, melted
  • ¼ cup granulated sugar

For the Filling:

  • 1 14-ounce can sweetened condensed milk
  • 4 large egg yolks
  • ½ cup freshly squeezed key lime juice
  • 1 tablespoon key lime zest
  • 1 teaspoon pure vanilla extract

For the Whipped Cream Topping:

  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 2 tablespoons powdered sugar
  • 1 teaspoon pure vanilla extract

Instructions

Making the Crust:

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. In a medium bowl, combine the graham cracker crumbs, melted butter, and granulated sugar. Mix until the crumbs are evenly coated.
  3. Press the mixture into the bottom and up the sides of a 9-inch pie dish, creating an even layer.
  4. Bake the crust for 10 minutes, then remove from the oven and let it cool while you prepare the filling.

Making the Filling:

  1. In a large bowl, whisk together the sweetened condensed milk and egg yolks until well combined.
  2. Add the key lime juice, key lime zest, and vanilla extract to the bowl. Continue to whisk until the mixture is smooth and slightly thickened.
  3. Pour the filling into the cooled graham cracker crust, spreading it evenly.

Baking the Pie:

  1. Put the pie back into the oven and bake for 15 minutes, or until the filling is set.
  2. Remove the pie from the oven and let it cool to room temperature. Once cooled, refrigerate the pie for at least 2 hours, or until it is completely chilled.

Making the Whipped Cream Topping:

  1. In a large bowl, whip the heavy cream, powdered sugar, and vanilla extract until stiff peaks form.
  2. Spread the whipped cream over the chilled pie, creating a decorative swirl or smooth layer, as desired.

Serving and Enjoying

Once the pie is topped with the whipped cream, it’s ready to be served and enjoyed! The combination of the tangy key lime filling and the sweet, airy whipped cream is truly a match made in dessert heaven. Whether you’re savoring a slice on a warm summer evening or treating yourself to a midday indulgence, this nut-free key lime pie is sure to delight your taste buds and leave you wanting more.

Conclusion

This nut-free key lime pie recipe is a wonderful way to enjoy the classic flavors of key lime pie without the use of nuts, making it a safe and delectable option for all dessert lovers. Its simple preparation and refreshing taste make it a perfect addition to any gathering or a delightful treat for yourself. So, gather your ingredients and get ready to create this delightful dessert that’s bound to become a favorite in your recipe collection. Happy baking and indulging!
