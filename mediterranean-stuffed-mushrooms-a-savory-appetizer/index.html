Mediterranean Stuffed Mushrooms: A Savory Appetizer


If you’re looking for a crowd-pleasing appetizer that’s bursting with Mediterranean flavors, then look no further than these delectable Mediterranean Stuffed Mushrooms. Packed with a savory filling and baked to perfection, these mushrooms are sure to be a hit at your next gathering.

Ingredients

  • 24 large cremini mushrooms
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup spinach, chopped
  • 1/2 cup roasted red peppers, chopped
  • 1/4 cup Kalamata olives, pitted and chopped
  • 1/4 cup feta cheese, crumbled
  • 1/4 cup breadcrumbs
  • 2 tablespoons fresh parsley, chopped
  • Salt and pepper, to taste

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C). Line a baking sheet with parchment paper or lightly grease it with olive oil.

  2. Clean the mushrooms by wiping them gently with a damp cloth. Remove the stems and set them aside. Place the mushroom caps on the prepared baking sheet, gill-side up.

  3. In a large skillet, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the chopped onion and minced garlic, and sauté until the onion turns translucent and fragrant, about 3-4 minutes.

  4. Add the chopped mushroom stems to the skillet and cook for an additional 2 minutes, until they release their moisture. Stir in the chopped spinach and cook until wilted, about 2-3 minutes more.

  5. Remove the skillet from the heat and transfer the mixture to a mixing bowl. Allow it to cool for a few minutes.

  6. To the mixing bowl, add the roasted red peppers, Kalamata olives, feta cheese, breadcrumbs, and chopped parsley. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Mix well until all the ingredients are evenly combined.

  7. Spoon the filling into the mushroom caps, mounding it slightly. Place the stuffed mushrooms back on the baking sheet.

  8. Bake in the preheated oven for about 20 minutes, or until the mushrooms are tender and the filling is golden brown.

  9. Remove the mushrooms from the oven and let them cool slightly before serving. These Mediterranean Stuffed Mushrooms are best enjoyed warm.

Serving Suggestions

These Mediterranean Stuffed Mushrooms can be served as a delightful appetizer or as a side dish. They pair perfectly with a refreshing glass of white wine or a light Mediterranean salad. Serve them at your next dinner party, potluck, or game night, and watch them disappear within minutes.

Variations

Feel free to get creative with the filling for these stuffed mushrooms. You can add some crumbled bacon or cooked ground sausage for a meaty twist. For a vegan option, omit the feta cheese and add some diced tofu or vegan cheese instead. Experiment with different herbs and spices to suit your taste preferences.

Conclusion

These Mediterranean Stuffed Mushrooms are a true crowd-pleaser, combining the earthy flavors of mushrooms with the vibrant tastes of the Mediterranean. With their enticing aroma and rich filling, they are the perfect appetizer to kick-start any gathering. So, gather your ingredients, roll up your sleeves, and enjoy the process of creating this savory masterpiece. Your taste buds will thank you!
