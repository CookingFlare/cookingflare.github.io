Delicious and Easy Chicken Recipes for Cooking Newbies


Are you a cooking newbie looking to impress your family and friends with delicious chicken dishes? Look no further! In this blog post, we will share some easy and mouthwatering chicken recipes that are perfect for beginners in the kitchen. Whether you’re a student living on your own or a busy professional with limited cooking experience, these recipes will make you feel like a pro chef in no time. So let’s get cooking!

1. Lemon Herb Roasted Chicken

This simple and aromatic recipe is a crowd-pleaser. Here’s what you’ll need:

  • 1 whole chicken
  • 2 lemons
  • Fresh herbs (such as rosemary, thyme, and parsley)
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Rinse the chicken thoroughly and pat it dry with paper towels.
  3. Cut one lemon into slices and stuff them inside the chicken cavity along with a handful of fresh herbs.
  4. Squeeze the juice of the remaining lemon over the chicken, and then sprinkle salt and pepper all over.
  5. Place the chicken on a roasting pan and roast it in the preheated oven for about 1 hour and 30 minutes, or until the internal temperature reaches 165°F (74°C).
  6. Let the chicken rest for a few minutes before carving. Serve with roasted vegetables or a side salad.

2. Creamy Garlic Parmesan Chicken

Indulge in the creamy goodness of this easy-to-make chicken recipe. Here’s what you’ll need:

  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 4 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Chopped parsley for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Season the chicken breasts with salt and pepper.
  2. In a large skillet, melt the butter over medium heat. Add the minced garlic and cook for a minute until fragrant.
  3. Add the chicken breasts to the skillet and cook for about 6-7 minutes on each side, or until they are golden brown and cooked through. Remove the chicken from the skillet and set aside.
  4. Reduce the heat to low and pour the heavy cream into the skillet. Stir in the grated Parmesan cheese until melted and smooth.
  5. Return the chicken to the skillet and coat it with the creamy sauce. Simmer for a few minutes until the sauce thickens.
  6. Garnish with chopped parsley and serve with your favorite side dish, such as pasta or steamed vegetables.

3. Honey Mustard Baked Chicken

This sweet and tangy recipe will have your taste buds dancing with delight. Here’s what you’ll need:

  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1/4 cup Dijon mustard
  • 2 tablespoons honey
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C).
  2. In a small bowl, whisk together the Dijon mustard, honey, olive oil, garlic powder, salt, and pepper.
  3. Place the chicken breasts in a baking dish and pour the honey mustard mixture over them, making sure they are coated evenly.
  4. Bake in the preheated oven for 20-25 minutes, or until the chicken is cooked through and the sauce has caramelized.
  5. Serve with steamed rice or a fresh green salad for a complete meal.

Conclusion

Cooking delicious chicken dishes doesn’t have to be complicated, even if you’re a cooking newbie. With these easy and flavorful recipes, you’ll be able to create mouthwatering meals that will impress everyone. So put on your apron, gather your ingredients, and let your culinary journey begin! Happy cooking!

Note: Remember to adjust cooking times and temperatures according to your specific oven and preferences.
