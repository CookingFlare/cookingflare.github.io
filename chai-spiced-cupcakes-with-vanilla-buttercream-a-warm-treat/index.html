Chai-Spiced Cupcakes with Vanilla Buttercream: A Warm Treat


Are you looking to cozy up with a simple, yet delightfully unique bake for this season? Well, look no further! These Chai-Spiced Cupcakes with Vanilla Buttercream are here to warm your heart and your kitchen with their rich, aromatic flavors. Bursting with the familiar taste of your favorite chai tea, these cupcakes are the perfect excuse to nestle down with a steaming hot drink and indulge in a sweet treat.

Ingredients

To make Chai-Spiced Cupcakes with Vanilla Buttercream, you’ll need:

For the Cupcakes:

  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, at room temperature
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 1/2 cup buttermilk
  • 2 1/2 teaspoons chai spice mixture (3 parts ground cinnamon, 1 part ground cardamom, 1/2 part ground cloves, 1/2 part grated nutmeg, 1/4 part ground ginger, 1/4 part black pepper)

For the Vanilla Buttercream:

  • 1 cup unsalted butter, at room temperature
  • 4 cups powdered sugar
  • 2 teaspoons pure vanilla extract
  • 1/4 cup whole milk

Instructions

Prepping the Oven and Pan:

  • Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C). Line a 12-cup cupcake pan with cupcake liners to ensure easy removal of the cupcakes after baking.
  • Prepare the chai spice mixture and set aside; it should be well combined and ready for use.

Making the Cupcakes:

  • In a medium bowl, whisk together the flour, baking powder, baking soda, salt, and chai spice mixture. Set aside while you prepare the wet ingredients.
  • In a large bowl, cream together the butter and sugar using a hand mixer or stand mixer until pale and fluffy. This usually takes around 3 minutes.
  • Gradually add the eggs to the butter-sugar mixture, making sure to fully incorporate each egg before adding the next. Be sure to scrape down the sides of the bowl if necessary.
  • Add the vanilla extract to the batter and mix until well combined.
  • Add half of the dry ingredients to the wet ingredients. Mix until just incorporated, then add the buttermilk. Follow this by adding the remaining dry ingredients and continue mixing until fully combined. Be sure not to overmix, as this might result in tough cupcakes.
  • Divide the batter evenly between the cupcake liners (about 3/4 of the way full). Each liner should contain approximately 2 tablespoons of batter.
  • Bake for 17-20 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center of a cupcake comes out clean.
  • Allow the cupcakes to cool in the pan for 5 minutes before transferring them to a wire rack to cool completely.

Making the Vanilla Buttercream:

  • While the cupcakes are cooling, prepare the buttercream. Beat the butter in a large bowl until creamy and smooth. This usually takes around 3 minutes.
  • Gradually add the powdered sugar to the butter, 1 cup at a time, and mix until well combined after each addition.
  • Add the vanilla extract and milk to the buttercream. Begin mixing on a low speed, gradually increasing as the ingredients come together. Continue mixing for 5 minutes, or until the buttercream is fluffy and light.
  • Cover the buttercream with a cloth or lid to prevent it from crusting over while you frost the cupcakes.

Assembling the Cupcakes:

  • Once the cupcakes and buttercream are cool, it’s time to frost the cupcakes. Spoon the buttercream onto each cupcake, using an offset spatula or butter knife if desired. Spread the buttercream smoothly and evenly over the cupcake, casually swirling it to create a light pattern.
  • Place the frosted cupcakes on a serving plate and, ready for serving, enjoy! Pair these delightful treats with your favorite warm beverage.

The Conclusion

Indulge in the delightful fusion of chai spices and classic vanilla buttercream as you sink your teeth into these scrumptious Chai-Spiced Cupcakes. Accompanied by a steaming cup of coffee, tea, or even hot chocolate, they are affectionately guaranteed to become a cozy favorite in your baking repertoire. So go ahead, embrace the season, and warm your heart with these extraordinary treats. Enjoy!
