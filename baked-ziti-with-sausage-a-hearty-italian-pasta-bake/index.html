Baked Ziti with Sausage: A Hearty Italian Pasta Bake


If there’s one dish that embodies comfort food, it’s Baked Ziti with Sausage. This hearty Italian pasta bake is a crowd-pleaser, perfect for family dinners, potlucks, or any occasion that calls for a delicious and satisfying meal. The combination of ziti pasta, savory sausage, rich tomato sauce, and melted cheese creates a symphony of flavors that will have everyone coming back for seconds. Plus, it’s a great make-ahead dish that can be prepared in advance and baked to perfection when you’re ready to serve.

Ingredients

To make this mouthwatering Baked Ziti with Sausage, you’ll need the following ingredients:

  • 1 pound ziti pasta
  • 1 pound Italian sausage, casings removed
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can (28 ounces) crushed tomatoes
  • 1 can (15 ounces) tomato sauce
  • 2 teaspoons dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 cup ricotta cheese
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • Fresh basil or parsley for garnish

Instructions

Step 1: Preparing the Pasta and Sausage

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C). Grease a 9x13-inch baking dish and set aside.
  2. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Cook the ziti pasta according to the package instructions until al dente. Drain and set aside.
  3. In a large skillet, cook the Italian sausage over medium heat, breaking it up with a spoon as it cooks, until browned and cooked through. Add the chopped onion and garlic, and sauté until the onion is translucent.

Step 2: Making the Tomato Sauce

  1. Pour in the crushed tomatoes and tomato sauce, and add the dried oregano, dried basil, salt, and pepper. Stir to combine and let the sauce simmer for 10-15 minutes to allow the flavors to meld together.

Step 3: Assembling the Baked Ziti

  1. In a large bowl, combine the cooked ziti pasta and the tomato sauce, tossing to coat the pasta evenly.
  2. In the greased baking dish, spread half of the pasta and sauce mixture evenly. Dollop half of the ricotta cheese over the pasta, then sprinkle with half of the mozzarella and Parmesan cheese. Repeat with the remaining pasta, ricotta, mozzarella, and Parmesan cheese.

Step 4: Baking the Ziti

  1. Cover the baking dish with aluminum foil and bake in the preheated oven for 25 minutes.
  2. Remove the foil and continue baking for an additional 10-15 minutes, or until the cheese is melted and bubbly and the edges are golden brown.
  3. Let the Baked Ziti with Sausage rest for a few minutes before serving. Garnish with fresh basil or parsley for a pop of color and freshness.

Conclusion

Baked Ziti with Sausage is a classic Italian-American dish that never fails to impress. Whether you’re hosting a casual gathering or simply craving a comforting meal, this hearty pasta bake is sure to satisfy. With its layers of pasta, savory sausage, and cheesy goodness, it’s a dish that brings people together and leaves everyone feeling warm and content. So, roll up your sleeves, gather your ingredients, and treat yourself to a taste of Italy with this delightful Baked Ziti with Sausage recipe. Buon appetito!
