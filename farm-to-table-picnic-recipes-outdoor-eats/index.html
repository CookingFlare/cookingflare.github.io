Farm to Table Picnic Recipes: Outdoor Eats


Are you looking for delicious and wholesome recipes to elevate your outdoor dining experience? Look no further! In this blog post, we’ll share some farm-to-table picnic recipes that will delight your taste buds while embracing the beauty of nature. So pack your picnic basket and let’s dive into these mouthwatering delights!

1. Fresh Tomato and Basil Bruschetta

Ingredients:

  • 4 ripe tomatoes, diced
  • 1/4 cup fresh basil leaves, chopped
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Baguette, sliced and toasted

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, combine the diced tomatoes, chopped basil, minced garlic, and olive oil.
  2. Season with salt and pepper, and mix well.
  3. Let the mixture sit for about 10 minutes to allow the flavors to meld.
  4. Spoon the tomato mixture onto the toasted baguette slices.
  5. Serve immediately and enjoy the burst of freshness in every bite!

2. Grilled Vegetable Skewers

Ingredients:

  • Assorted vegetables of your choice (bell peppers, zucchini, cherry tomatoes, red onions, mushrooms, etc.)
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons balsamic vinegar
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your grill to medium-high heat.
  2. Cut the vegetables into bite-sized pieces.
  3. In a bowl, whisk together the olive oil, balsamic vinegar, minced garlic, salt, and pepper.
  4. Thread the vegetables onto skewers, alternating the different vegetables.
  5. Brush the vegetable skewers with the marinade mixture.
  6. Grill the skewers for about 8-10 minutes, turning occasionally, until the vegetables are tender and slightly charred.
  7. Remove from the grill and serve hot. These skewers are a colorful and healthy addition to your picnic spread!

3. Quinoa Salad with Fresh Herbs

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup cooked quinoa
  • 1 cup mixed fresh herbs (such as parsley, mint, and cilantro), chopped
  • 1/2 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1/4 cup red onion, finely chopped
  • 1/4 cup feta cheese, crumbled
  • Juice of 1 lemon
  • 2 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a large bowl, combine the cooked quinoa, chopped herbs, cherry tomatoes, red onion, and feta cheese.
  2. In a separate small bowl, whisk together the lemon juice, olive oil, salt, and pepper.
  3. Pour the dressing over the quinoa mixture and toss gently to combine.
  4. Let the salad sit for about 10 minutes to allow the flavors to meld.
  5. Serve chilled or at room temperature. This refreshing quinoa salad is packed with nutrients and makes a perfect side dish for your outdoor feast!

4. Fresh Berry Parfait

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup Greek yogurt
  • 1 cup mixed fresh berries (such as strawberries, blueberries, and raspberries)
  • 1/4 cup granola
  • 1 tablespoon honey (optional)

Instructions:

  1. In a glass or jar, layer the Greek yogurt, fresh berries, and granola.
  2. Drizzle honey on top, if desired, for added sweetness.
  3. Repeat the layers until the glass or jar is filled.
  4. Refrigerate for at least 30 minutes before serving.
  5. Indulge in this light and fruity parfait, the perfect ending to your farm-to-table picnic!

These farm-to-table picnic recipes are not only delicious but also celebrate the freshness and flavors of locally sourced ingredients. Whether you’re planning a romantic outing or a family gathering, these recipes will impress your guests and make your picnic a memorable experience. So go ahead, embrace the beauty of nature, and savor these outdoor eats!

Remember to pack reusable utensils, plates, and napkins to minimize waste and leave no trace of your picnic behind. Enjoy your meal and the great outdoors!
