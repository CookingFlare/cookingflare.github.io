Roasted Brussels Sprouts with Bacon: A Tasty Side for Fall


When the air turns crisp and the leaves start to change color, it’s time to embrace the flavors of fall. And what better way to do that than with a delicious, hearty side dish that perfectly captures the essence of the season? Enter roasted Brussels sprouts with bacon – a savory, smoky, and satisfying dish that will have everyone coming back for seconds.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound Brussels sprouts, trimmed and halved
  • 4 slices of bacon, chopped
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C) and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.

  2. In a large bowl, toss the Brussels sprouts with olive oil, garlic powder, salt, and pepper until they are evenly coated.

  3. Spread the Brussels sprouts out in a single layer on the prepared baking sheet, making sure they are not crowded.

  4. Scatter the chopped bacon over the Brussels sprouts, distributing it evenly.

  5. Roast in the preheated oven for 25-30 minutes, or until the Brussels sprouts are crispy on the outside and tender on the inside, and the bacon is cooked to your desired level of crispiness.

  6. Once out of the oven, let the dish cool for a few minutes before serving.

Why Roasted Brussels Sprouts with Bacon is Perfect for Fall

  • Flavor Fusion: The earthy, nutty flavor of Brussels sprouts pairs perfectly with the smoky, salty goodness of bacon, creating a mouthwatering combination that’s hard to resist.

  • Comforting and Satisfying: As the days get cooler, we crave heartier, more substantial dishes, and this roasted Brussels sprouts with bacon recipe delivers just that. It’s the ultimate comfort food with a gourmet twist.

  • Seasonal and Versatile: Brussels sprouts are in season during the fall, making them a fresh and flavorful choice for your autumn menu. This dish can be served alongside a variety of main courses, from roasted chicken to grilled steak, making it a versatile addition to your recipe repertoire.

  • Easy and Nourishing: With just a handful of ingredients and minimal prep time, this dish is a breeze to make. Plus, Brussels sprouts are packed with nutrients, making this side dish a healthy and wholesome option for your fall table.

Serving Suggestions

  • Serve the roasted Brussels sprouts with bacon as a side dish for a cozy family dinner or a festive holiday feast.

  • Pair it with roasted chicken, grilled pork chops, or seared salmon for a complete and satisfying meal.

  • For a vegetarian option, omit the bacon and add a sprinkle of smoked paprika for that same hint of smokiness.

Conclusion

Roasted Brussels sprouts with bacon is a delightful and flavorful side dish that embodies the essence of fall. With its simple preparation, robust flavors, and seasonal appeal, this recipe is sure to become a staple on your autumn menu. Whether you’re hosting a gathering of loved ones or simply enjoying a quiet evening at home, this dish is a delicious celebration of the harvest season. So, embrace the flavors of fall and savor every bite of this delectable roasted Brussels sprouts with bacon recipe.
