Vegan Dessert Recipes Inspired by Classic Favorites


Desserts often evoke memories of cherished moments, family gatherings, and celebrations. The good news is that you can enjoy all those sweet delights in a plant-based version! In this blog post, we’ll explore how to create vegan dessert recipes inspired by classic favorites, so you can indulge in a guilt-free way. Whether you’re a long-time vegan or simply exploring plant-based options, these recipes will satisfy your sweet tooth while keeping your values intact.

1. Vegan Chocolate Chip Cookies

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup almond flour
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 tsp baking soda
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/2 cup coconut oil, solid
  • 1/2 cup brown sugar
  • 1/4 cup maple syrup
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1/2 cup dark chocolate chips (dairy-free)

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C) and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
  2. In a bowl, mix the almond flour, all-purpose flour, baking soda, and salt.
  3. In another bowl, cream together the solid coconut oil and brown sugar until smooth. Add the maple syrup and vanilla extract, mixing until combined.
  4. Gradually add the dry ingredients to the wet ingredients, stirring until a dough forms. Fold in the dark chocolate chips.
  5. Scoop tablespoon-sized amounts of dough onto the prepared baking sheet, spacing them apart.
  6. Bake for 10-12 minutes, or until the edges are golden. Let them cool before enjoying!

These vegan chocolate chip cookies are chewy, indulgent, and perfect for sharing—or not!

2. Vegan Cheesecake

Ingredients:

  • 1 1/2 cups raw cashews (soaked in water for at least 4 hours)
  • 1/2 cup coconut cream
  • 1/4 cup maple syrup
  • 1/4 cup lemon juice
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1/2 cup graham cracker crumbs (vegan)
  • 1/4 cup melted coconut oil

Instructions:

  1. To make the crust, combine the graham cracker crumbs and melted coconut oil in a bowl. Press this mixture into the bottom of a springform pan.
  2. Drain and rinse the soaked cashews, then blend them in a high-speed blender with coconut cream, maple syrup, lemon juice, and vanilla extract until smooth and creamy.
  3. Pour the cashew mixture over the crust and smooth it out evenly.
  4. Freeze for at least 4 hours or until firm. Before serving, let it thaw for about 30 minutes.
  5. Top with fresh berries or a berry compote for an extra burst of flavor.

This creamy vegan cheesecake is an impressive dessert that will leave your guests asking for the recipe!

3. Vegan Brownies

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup black beans (cooked and drained)
  • 1/2 cup almond butter
  • 1/4 cup cocoa powder
  • 1/4 cup maple syrup
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1/2 tsp baking powder
  • 1/2 cup dairy-free chocolate chips

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C) and grease an 8x8 inch baking dish.
  2. In a food processor, blend the black beans, almond butter, cocoa powder, maple syrup, vanilla extract, and baking powder until smooth.
  3. Stir in the chocolate chips, saving a few to sprinkle on top.
  4. Pour the batter into the prepared baking dish, smoothing it out with a spatula. Sprinkle the remaining chocolate chips on top.
  5. Bake for 20-25 minutes. Allow them to cool before cutting into squares.

These fudgy vegan brownies are rich, decadent, and surprisingly healthy!

4. Vegan Banana Bread

Ingredients:

  • 3 ripe bananas, mashed
  • 1/3 cup melted coconut oil
  • 1/2 cup maple syrup or agave nectar
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • Pinch of salt
  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup walnuts (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C) and grease a loaf pan.
  2. In a mixing bowl, combine the mashed bananas and melted coconut oil. Stir in the maple syrup and vanilla extract.
  3. Add the baking soda and salt, mixing well. Gradually fold in the flour until just combined. If using, fold in the walnuts.
  4. Pour the batter into the greased loaf pan and smooth the top.
  5. Bake for 50-60 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean. Let it cool before slicing.

This vegan banana bread is moist, flavorful, and makes for a perfect breakfast or snack.

5. Vegan Tiramisu

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup raw cashews (soaked for at least 4 hours)
  • 1 cup coconut cream
  • 1/4 cup maple syrup
  • 1/4 cup brewed coffee (cooled)
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 package of vegan ladyfingers or sponge cake
  • Unsweetened cocoa powder for dusting

Instructions:

  1. In a blender, combine the soaked cashews, coconut cream, maple syrup, brewed coffee, and vanilla extract. Blend until smooth and creamy.
  2. In a dish, layer the vegan ladyfingers or sponge cake, dipping each piece briefly in brewed coffee to soak.
  3. Spread a layer of the cashew cream mixture over the ladyfingers. Repeat the layering process, finishing with a layer of cashew cream on top.
  4. Refrigerate for at least 4 hours or overnight to allow the flavors to meld.
  5. Before serving, dust the top with cocoa powder for a classic finish.

This vegan tiramisu captures the essence of the traditional dessert, delivering a deliciously rich experience.

6. Vegan Ice Cream

Ingredients:

  • 2 ripe bananas, sliced and frozen
  • 1 cup coconut milk
  • 1/4 cup almond butter or peanut butter
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract

Instructions:

  1. In a blender or food processor, combine the frozen banana slices, coconut milk, almond butter, and vanilla extract.
  2. Blend until smooth and creamy, stopping to scrape down the sides as necessary.
  3. Transfer the mixture to a container and freeze for at least 2 hours before serving.

This vegan ice cream is a simple, no-churn dessert that’s creamy and satisfying!

7. Vegan Apple Crisp

Ingredients:

  • 4 cups sliced apples (Granny Smith or Honeycrisp work well)
  • 1/2 cup rolled oats
  • 1/2 cup brown sugar
  • 1/4 cup almond flour
  • 1/4 cup coconut oil, melted
  • 1 tsp cinnamon
  • 1/4 tsp salt

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C) and grease a baking dish.
  2. In a large bowl, toss the sliced apples with cinnamon and a pinch of salt. Spread them evenly in the greased baking dish.
  3. In another bowl, combine the rolled oats, brown sugar, almond flour, and melted coconut oil. Mix until crumbly.
  4. Sprinkle the oat mixture over the apples.
  5. Bake for 30-35 minutes, or until the apples are tender and the topping is golden brown.

This vegan apple crisp is warm, comforting, and perfect for a cozy night in!

8. Vegan Panna Cotta

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup coconut milk
  • 1/2 cup almond milk
  • 1/4 cup maple syrup
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 tbsp agar-agar powder

Instructions:

  1. In a saucepan, combine the coconut milk, almond milk, maple syrup, and vanilla extract over medium heat.
  2. Sprinkle the agar-agar powder over the mixture, whisking constantly to dissolve. Bring to a gentle boil, then reduce the heat and simmer for 2-3 minutes.
  3. Pour the mixture into molds or cups and refrigerate for at least 4 hours until set.
  4. Serve with a berry compote or fresh fruit on top.

This vegan panna cotta is a creamy, luxurious dessert that’s sure to impress guests!

Conclusion

Transitioning to a vegan lifestyle does not mean giving up your favorite desserts. With a little creativity and the right ingredients, you can recreate classic favorites that are just as delicious as their traditional counterparts. From chewy chocolate chip cookies to creamy cheesecake, these vegan dessert recipes will satisfy your cravings and delight your palate. So gather your ingredients, roll up your sleeves, and get ready to indulge in these delectable treats!
