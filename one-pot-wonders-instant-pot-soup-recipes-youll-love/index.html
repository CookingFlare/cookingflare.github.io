One-Pot Wonders: Instant Pot Soup Recipes You'll Love


It’s that time of year again when the weather starts to cool down, and there’s nothing more comforting than a warm bowl of soup. And what better way to make soup than with the convenience of an Instant Pot? With its ability to cook dishes quickly and effortlessly, the Instant Pot has become a staple in many kitchens. In this blog post, we’ll be sharing some delicious and easy one-pot wonder soup recipes that you’ll absolutely love.

1. Creamy Tomato Basil Soup

Ingredients:

  • 1 can diced tomatoes
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup vegetable broth
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 2 tablespoons tomato paste
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Set the Instant Pot to sauté mode and add the chopped onion and minced garlic. Sauté until fragrant and onions are translucent.
  2. Add the diced tomatoes, vegetable broth, tomato paste, dried basil, salt, and pepper. Stir well to combine.
  3. Close the Instant Pot lid and set the pressure release valve to sealing. Cook on high pressure for 10 minutes.
  4. Once the cooking time is complete, allow for a natural pressure release for 5 minutes, then quick release the remaining pressure.
  5. Use an immersion blender to puree the soup until smooth. Alternatively, you can transfer the soup to a blender and blend until smooth.
  6. Stir in the heavy cream and adjust the seasoning if needed. Serve hot and garnish with fresh basil leaves.

2. Chicken Noodle Soup

Ingredients:

  • 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 4 cups chicken broth
  • 2 carrots, sliced
  • 2 celery stalks, sliced
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 cups egg noodles

Instructions:

  1. Place the chicken breasts in the Instant Pot and add the chicken broth, carrots, celery, onion, garlic, dried thyme, salt, and pepper.
  2. Close the Instant Pot lid and set the pressure release valve to sealing. Cook on high pressure for 15 minutes.
  3. Once the cooking time is complete, allow for a natural pressure release for 10 minutes, then quick release the remaining pressure.
  4. Remove the chicken breasts from the Instant Pot and shred them using two forks. Set aside.
  5. Set the Instant Pot to sauté mode and add the egg noodles. Cook until tender, about 5 minutes.
  6. Return the shredded chicken to the Instant Pot and stir to combine. Adjust the seasoning if needed. Serve hot and enjoy!

3. Butternut Squash Soup

Ingredients:

  • 1 butternut squash, peeled, seeded, and cubed
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 4 cups vegetable broth
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • Chopped fresh parsley for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Place the cubed butternut squash, chopped onion, minced garlic, vegetable broth, dried thyme, salt, and pepper in the Instant Pot.
  2. Close the Instant Pot lid and set the pressure release valve to sealing. Cook on high pressure for 8 minutes.
  3. Once the cooking time is complete, allow for a natural pressure release for 5 minutes, then quick release the remaining pressure.
  4. Use an immersion blender to puree the soup until smooth. Alternatively, you can transfer the soup to a blender and blend until smooth.
  5. Stir in the heavy cream and adjust the seasoning if needed. Serve hot and garnish with chopped fresh parsley.

Conclusion

There you have it, three amazing one-pot wonder soup recipes that you can easily make using your Instant Pot. Whether you’re craving a creamy tomato basil soup, a classic chicken noodle soup, or a comforting butternut squash soup, these recipes are sure to satisfy your taste buds. So, grab your Instant Pot and get cooking!
