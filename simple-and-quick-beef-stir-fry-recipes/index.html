Simple and Quick Beef Stir-Fry Recipes


If you’re looking for a delicious and satisfying meal that can be prepared in no time, look no further than these simple and quick beef stir-fry recipes. Stir-frying is a cooking technique that originated in China and has gained popularity worldwide for its ability to retain the natural flavors and textures of the ingredients while bringing out a burst of vibrant flavors. With just a few key ingredients and some basic kitchen skills, you can whip up a mouthwatering beef stir-fry that will impress your family and friends. So, let’s get started!

Classic Beef Stir-Fry

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound of beef (sirloin, flank, or tenderloin), thinly sliced
  • 2 tablespoons of vegetable oil
  • 1 onion, thinly sliced
  • 2 bell peppers (any color), thinly sliced
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon of soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon of oyster sauce
  • 1 teaspoon of cornstarch
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a small bowl, whisk together the soy sauce, oyster sauce, and cornstarch. Set aside.
  2. Heat the vegetable oil in a large skillet or wok over high heat.
  3. Add the beef slices to the hot oil and stir-fry for 2-3 minutes until browned. Remove the beef from the skillet and set aside.
  4. In the same skillet, add the onion, bell peppers, and garlic. Stir-fry for 2-3 minutes until the vegetables are crisp-tender.
  5. Return the beef to the skillet and pour in the sauce mixture. Stir-fry for another 2-3 minutes until the sauce thickens and coats the beef and vegetables.
  6. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  7. Serve hot with steamed rice or noodles.

Spicy Szechuan Beef Stir-Fry

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound of beef (flank or sirloin), thinly sliced
  • 2 tablespoons of vegetable oil
  • 1 red bell pepper, thinly sliced
  • 1 green bell pepper, thinly sliced
  • 1 onion, thinly sliced
  • 3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon of Szechuan peppercorns, crushed
  • 2 tablespoons of soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon of hoisin sauce
  • 1 tablespoon of chili garlic sauce
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a small bowl, combine the soy sauce, hoisin sauce, and chili garlic sauce. Set aside.
  2. Heat the vegetable oil in a large skillet or wok over high heat.
  3. Add the beef slices to the hot oil and stir-fry for 2-3 minutes until browned. Remove the beef from the skillet and set aside.
  4. In the same skillet, add the bell peppers, onion, garlic, and Szechuan peppercorns. Stir-fry for 2-3 minutes until the vegetables are crisp-tender.
  5. Return the beef to the skillet and pour in the sauce mixture. Stir-fry for another 2-3 minutes until the sauce coats the beef and vegetables.
  6. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  7. Serve hot with steamed rice or noodles.

Teriyaki Beef Stir-Fry

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound of beef (sirloin or tenderloin), thinly sliced
  • 2 tablespoons of vegetable oil
  • 1 carrot, thinly sliced
  • 1 zucchini, thinly sliced
  • 1 red bell pepper, thinly sliced
  • 1 green bell pepper, thinly sliced
  • 3 tablespoons of teriyaki sauce
  • 2 tablespoons of honey
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon of sesame oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a small bowl, whisk together the teriyaki sauce, honey, garlic, and sesame oil. Set aside.
  2. Heat the vegetable oil in a large skillet or wok over high heat.
  3. Add the beef slices to the hot oil and stir-fry for 2-3 minutes until browned. Remove the beef from the skillet and set aside.
  4. In the same skillet, add the carrot, zucchini, and bell peppers. Stir-fry for 2-3 minutes until the vegetables are crisp-tender.
  5. Return the beef to the skillet and pour in the sauce mixture. Stir-fry for another 2-3 minutes until the sauce thickens and coats the beef and vegetables.
  6. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  7. Serve hot with steamed rice or noodles.

Conclusion

These simple and quick beef stir-fry recipes are perfect for busy weeknights when you want a delicious and satisfying meal without spending hours in the kitchen. With just a few ingredients and some basic cooking techniques, you can create a flavorful stir-fry that will impress your taste buds. So, why not give these recipes a try and elevate your weeknight dinner game? Happy cooking!
