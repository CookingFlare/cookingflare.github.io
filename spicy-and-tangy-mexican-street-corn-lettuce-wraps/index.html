Spicy and Tangy Mexican Street Corn Lettuce Wraps


If you’re looking for a fresh and flavorful dish to spice up your summer menu, these Spicy and Tangy Mexican Street Corn Lettuce Wraps are just what you need. Inspired by the vibrant flavors of Mexican street food, this recipe combines the smokiness of grilled corn with the zing of lime and the kick of chili, all wrapped up in crisp lettuce leaves. Whether you’re planning a casual get-together or simply craving a taste of summer, these lettuce wraps are sure to be a hit.

Ingredients

For the Corn Filling:

  • 4 ears of corn, husked
  • 1/4 cup mayonnaise
  • 1/4 cup sour cream
  • 1/2 cup crumbled cotija cheese
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh cilantro
  • 1 teaspoon chili powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon smoked paprika
  • 1/4 teaspoon cayenne pepper
  • Salt and pepper to taste

For the Lettuce Wraps:

  • 1 head of butter or iceberg lettuce, leaves separated and cleaned
  • Additional cotija cheese for garnish
  • Lime wedges for serving
  • Extra cilantro for garnish

Instructions

  1. Grill the Corn: Preheat your grill to medium-high heat. Place the husked ears of corn directly on the grill grates and cook, turning occasionally, until the kernels are charred in spots, about 10 minutes. Remove the corn from the grill and allow it to cool slightly.

  2. Prepare the Filling: In a small bowl, combine the mayonnaise, sour cream, crumbled cotija cheese, chopped cilantro, chili powder, smoked paprika, cayenne pepper, salt, and pepper. Stir until well combined.

  3. Cut the Corn: Once the corn has cooled, stand each ear upright on a cutting board and use a sharp knife to cut the kernels from the cob.

  4. Mix and Assemble: Add the grilled corn kernels to the bowl of seasoned mayonnaise and cheese. Gently toss to coat the corn evenly with the mixture.

  5. Assemble the Lettuce Wraps: Spoon the corn filling into individual lettuce leaves, creating small bundles. Sprinkle with extra cotija cheese and cilantro for garnish.

  6. Serve: Arrange the lettuce wraps on a platter and serve with lime wedges on the side for squeezing over the wraps before eating.

Tips and Variations

  • For a smokier flavor, you can char the corn on a stovetop grill pan or under the broiler.
  • If cotija cheese is unavailable, you can substitute it with crumbled feta cheese.
  • Add a dash of hot sauce or sprinkle of Tajin seasoning for an extra kick of heat.
  • You can also add diced avocado or grilled shrimp to the filling for a more substantial meal.

Conclusion

These Spicy and Tangy Mexican Street Corn Lettuce Wraps are a celebration of summer flavors and the vibrant spirit of Mexican street food. The combination of smoky grilled corn, creamy cotija cheese, and zesty lime creates a burst of freshness in every bite. Whether you’re planning a backyard barbecue or a casual weeknight dinner, these lettuce wraps are a versatile and delicious addition to your repertoire. So, gather your ingredients and get ready to savor the taste of summer with this irresistible recipe!
