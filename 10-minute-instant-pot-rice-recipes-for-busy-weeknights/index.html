10-Minute Instant Pot Rice Recipes for Busy Weeknights


Are you always on the lookout for quick and easy recipes that can be whipped up in a flash? Look no further! In this blog post, we will be exploring some delightful 10-minute Instant Pot rice recipes that are perfect for those busy weeknights when time is of the essence. With the help of your trusty Instant Pot, you can have a delicious and satisfying meal on the table in no time. So, let’s dive right in!

1. Garlic Butter Rice

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup basmati rice
  • 2 cups water
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • Salt to taste

Instructions:

  1. Rinse the basmati rice under cold water until the water runs clear.
  2. In the Instant Pot, melt the butter on the sauté mode.
  3. Add the minced garlic and sauté until fragrant.
  4. Add the rinsed rice and stir well to coat it with the butter and garlic.
  5. Pour in the water and season with salt.
  6. Close the Instant Pot lid and set the timer to 4 minutes on high pressure.
  7. Once the timer goes off, allow the pressure to release naturally for 5 minutes, then perform a quick release.
  8. Fluff the rice with a fork and serve hot.

2. Lemon Herb Rice

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup jasmine rice
  • 1 ¾ cups vegetable broth
  • Zest of 1 lemon
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 2 tablespoons fresh herbs (such as parsley, dill, or cilantro), chopped
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Rinse the jasmine rice thoroughly.
  2. In the Instant Pot, combine the rinsed rice, vegetable broth, lemon zest, lemon juice, and a pinch of salt and pepper.
  3. Close the Instant Pot lid and set the timer to 3 minutes on high pressure.
  4. Once the timer goes off, allow the pressure to release naturally for 5 minutes, then perform a quick release.
  5. Stir in the fresh herbs and adjust the seasoning if needed.
  6. Serve the lemon herb rice as a side dish or as a base for your favorite protein.

3. Mexican Rice

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup long-grain white rice
  • 1 ¼ cups chicken broth
  • ½ cup tomato sauce
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 1 jalapeño, seeded and finely chopped (optional)
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • ½ teaspoon chili powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Rinse the long-grain white rice until the water runs clear.
  2. In the Instant Pot, heat the vegetable oil on the sauté mode.
  3. Add the chopped onion and jalapeño (if using) and sauté until softened.
  4. Stir in the cumin and chili powder, and cook for an additional 30 seconds.
  5. Add the rinsed rice, chicken broth, tomato sauce, salt, and pepper. Stir well to combine.
  6. Close the Instant Pot lid and set the timer to 5 minutes on high pressure.
  7. Once the timer goes off, allow the pressure to release naturally for 5 minutes, then perform a quick release.
  8. Fluff the rice with a fork and serve alongside your favorite Mexican dishes.

Conclusion

There you have it - three mouthwatering 10-minute Instant Pot rice recipes that are perfect for those busy weeknights. With these quick and easy recipes, you can enjoy a delicious and satisfying meal without spending hours in the kitchen. So, fire up your Instant Pot and give these recipes a try. Your taste buds will thank you!
