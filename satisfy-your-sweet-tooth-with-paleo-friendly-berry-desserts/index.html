Satisfy Your Sweet Tooth with Paleo-Friendly Berry Desserts


Are you on a paleo diet and craving something sweet? Look no further! In this post, we’ll explore some delicious and healthy berry desserts that are paleo-friendly. These desserts are not only satisfying for your sweet tooth but also packed with antioxidants and nutrients from the berries. So let’s dive in and discover these delightful treats!

1. Mixed Berry Crumble

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups mixed berries (strawberries, blueberries, raspberries)
  • 1 cup almond flour
  • 1/2 cup shredded coconut
  • 1/4 cup honey
  • 1/4 cup coconut oil
  • 1 teaspoon cinnamon
  • Pinch of salt

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. In a bowl, mix the berries with 2 tablespoons of almond flour and spread them evenly in a baking dish.
  3. In another bowl, combine the remaining almond flour, shredded coconut, honey, coconut oil, cinnamon, and salt. Mix until crumbly.
  4. Sprinkle the crumble mixture over the berries.
  5. Bake for 25-30 minutes or until the topping is golden brown and the berries are bubbling.
  6. Allow it to cool for a few minutes before serving. Serve warm with a dollop of coconut cream or paleo-friendly ice cream.

2. Chilled Berry Mousse

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups mixed berries (blackberries, strawberries, blueberries)
  • 1 can coconut milk (chilled in the refrigerator overnight)
  • 2 tablespoons honey
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • Fresh mint leaves (for garnish)

Instructions:

  1. Place the berries, honey, and vanilla extract in a blender or food processor. Blend until smooth.
  2. Open the chilled can of coconut milk and scoop out only the thick cream on top, leaving the liquid behind.
  3. Add the coconut cream to the blender and blend until well combined.
  4. Pour the mixture into serving glasses or bowls and refrigerate for at least 2 hours or until set.
  5. Before serving, garnish with fresh mint leaves for an extra touch of freshness.

3. Berry Coconut Popsicles

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups mixed berries (blueberries, raspberries, strawberries)
  • 1 can coconut milk
  • 2 tablespoons honey
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Instructions:

  1. In a blender, combine the berries, coconut milk, honey, and vanilla extract. Blend until smooth.
  2. Pour the mixture into popsicle molds, leaving a little space at the top for expansion.
  3. Insert popsicle sticks into each mold.
  4. Freeze for at least 4 hours or until solid.
  5. To remove the popsicles from the molds, run warm water over the bottom of the molds for a few seconds.
  6. Enjoy these refreshing and guilt-free popsicles on a hot summer day!

Conclusion

These paleo-friendly berry desserts are not only delicious but also a great way to satisfy your sweet tooth without compromising your dietary goals. Whether you choose the mixed berry crumble, chilled berry mousse, or berry coconut popsicles, you can enjoy the natural sweetness of berries while staying true to your paleo lifestyle. So go ahead, indulge in these guilt-free treats and delight your taste buds with the vibrant flavors of nature’s bounty!
