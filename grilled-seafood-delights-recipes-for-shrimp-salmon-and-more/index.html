Grilled Seafood Delights: Recipes for Shrimp, Salmon, and More


If you’re a seafood lover like me, then you’re in for a treat! Grilling seafood not only brings out the natural flavors but also adds a smoky and charred touch that will leave your taste buds craving for more. In this blog post, we’ll explore some delicious grilled seafood recipes that are perfect for a summer cookout or a cozy dinner at home. From shrimp to salmon, we’ve got you covered with these mouthwatering delights.

Grilled Shrimp Skewers with Lemon Garlic Butter

Let’s start with a classic favorite - grilled shrimp skewers. These little bites of heaven are incredibly easy to make and bursting with flavor. To make this recipe, you’ll need:

  • 1 pound of large shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 2 tablespoons of olive oil
  • 3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • Juice of one lemon
  • 2 tablespoons of melted butter
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Wooden skewers, soaked in water for 30 minutes
  1. Preheat your grill to medium-high heat.
  2. In a small bowl, mix together the olive oil, minced garlic, lemon juice, melted butter, salt, and pepper.
  3. Thread the shrimp onto the soaked wooden skewers, about 4-5 shrimp per skewer.
  4. Brush the shrimp skewers with the garlic butter mixture, making sure to coat them evenly.
  5. Place the skewers on the preheated grill and cook for 2-3 minutes on each side, or until the shrimp turn pink and opaque.
  6. Remove from the grill and serve hot with a squeeze of fresh lemon juice.

Grilled Salmon with Honey Mustard Glaze

Next up, we have a crowd-pleaser - grilled salmon with a sweet and tangy honey mustard glaze. This recipe is perfect for those who enjoy a balance of flavors. Here’s what you’ll need:

  • 1 pound of salmon fillets
  • 2 tablespoons of Dijon mustard
  • 2 tablespoons of honey
  • 1 tablespoon of soy sauce
  • 1 clove of garlic, minced
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  1. Preheat your grill to medium heat.
  2. In a small bowl, whisk together the Dijon mustard, honey, soy sauce, minced garlic, salt, and pepper.
  3. Place the salmon fillets on a sheet of aluminum foil and brush them generously with the honey mustard glaze.
  4. Carefully transfer the foil with the salmon onto the preheated grill.
  5. Grill the salmon for 10-12 minutes, or until it flakes easily with a fork, basting with the remaining glaze halfway through.
  6. Remove from the grill and let it rest for a few minutes before serving.

Grilled Lobster Tails with Herb Butter

If you’re looking to impress your guests, grilled lobster tails with herb butter are the way to go. This elegant dish is surprisingly simple to make and will elevate any occasion. Here’s what you’ll need:

  • 4 lobster tails
  • 4 tablespoons of melted butter
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon of chopped fresh parsley
  • 1 tablespoon of chopped fresh chives
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  1. Preheat your grill to medium-high heat.
  2. Using kitchen shears, carefully cut the top shell of each lobster tail down the center, without cutting through the bottom shell.
  3. Gently pull the shell apart to expose the meat, but keep it attached at the base.
  4. In a small bowl, mix together the melted butter, minced garlic, chopped parsley, chopped chives, salt, and pepper.
  5. Brush the herb butter mixture generously over the exposed meat of each lobster tail.
  6. Place the lobster tails on the preheated grill, shell side down, and cook for 5-6 minutes.
  7. Flip the lobster tails and continue grilling for another 4-5 minutes, or until the meat is opaque and slightly charred.
  8. Remove from the grill and serve hot with extra herb butter on the side.

Grilled Seafood Delights for Everyone to Enjoy!

Whether you’re a shrimp lover, a salmon enthusiast, or a fan of lobster, these grilled seafood recipes are sure to please your palate. The smoky flavors, combined with the natural sweetness of the seafood, create a harmonious and delectable experience. So fire up your grill, gather your loved ones, and indulge in these grilled seafood delights. Happy grilling!

Note: Remember to always prioritize safety when grilling. Follow proper grilling techniques and ensure that your seafood is cooked thoroughly before serving.
