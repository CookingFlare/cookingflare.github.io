Mexican Chocolate Dipped Strawberries: A Sweet and Decadent Dessert


Who doesn’t love the combination of juicy strawberries and rich, velvety chocolate? It’s a match made in dessert heaven! But have you ever tried Mexican chocolate dipped strawberries? This delightful twist on a classic treat adds a touch of warmth and spice to elevate your taste buds to new heights. Get ready to indulge in a sweet and decadent dessert that will leave you craving for more.

Ingredients

To make these mouthwatering Mexican chocolate dipped strawberries, you will need the following ingredients:

  • Fresh strawberries
  • Mexican chocolate (preferably a brand like Abuelita or Ibarra)
  • Heavy cream
  • Ground cinnamon
  • Chili powder (optional, for those who like a little heat)
  • Chopped nuts (such as almonds or pistachios) for garnish

Instructions

  1. Start by washing and drying the strawberries thoroughly. Make sure they are completely dry to ensure the chocolate adheres well.

  2. In a microwave-safe bowl, break the Mexican chocolate into small pieces. Add a splash of heavy cream to the bowl to help melt the chocolate more smoothly.

  3. Microwave the chocolate and cream mixture in 30-second intervals, stirring well after each interval, until the chocolate is completely melted and smooth. Be careful not to overheat the chocolate, as it can burn easily.

  4. Once the chocolate is melted, add a pinch of ground cinnamon to the bowl and mix well. This will enhance the flavor and give the strawberries a delightful Mexican twist.

  5. If you like a little kick of spice, sprinkle a touch of chili powder into the chocolate mixture and stir it in. Adjust the amount according to your taste preferences.

  6. Take a strawberry by its stem and dip it into the melted Mexican chocolate, swirling it around to ensure it’s coated evenly. Allow any excess chocolate to drip off.

  7. Place the chocolate-dipped strawberry on a parchment-lined baking sheet or a plate. Repeat the process with the remaining strawberries.

  8. If desired, sprinkle some chopped nuts (such as almonds or pistachios) over the chocolate-dipped strawberries while the chocolate is still wet. This will add a delightful crunch and extra flavor.

  9. Once all the strawberries are dipped and garnished, place the baking sheet or plate in the refrigerator for about 15-20 minutes to allow the chocolate to set.

  10. After the chocolate has hardened, remove the strawberries from the refrigerator and serve them immediately. These Mexican chocolate dipped strawberries are best enjoyed fresh!

Tips and Variations

  • Experiment with different types of Mexican chocolate to find your favorite flavor profile. Some brands offer different levels of sweetness or spiciness, allowing you to customize the taste to your liking.

  • For an extra touch of indulgence, drizzle some melted white chocolate over the Mexican chocolate-dipped strawberries. The contrasting colors and flavors will make them even more visually appealing.

  • If you want to make these strawberries in advance for a party or special occasion, simply store them in an airtight container in the refrigerator. They should stay fresh for up to 24 hours.

  • Don’t limit yourself to just strawberries! This Mexican chocolate dip works beautifully with other fruits like bananas, pineapple chunks, or even orange segments. Get creative and explore different flavor combinations.

Conclusion

Mexican chocolate dipped strawberries are an irresistible dessert that combines the sweetness of ripe berries with the rich, complex flavors of Mexican chocolate. The addition of cinnamon and optional chili powder elevates this treat to a whole new level, making it a memorable and indulgent experience. Whether you’re hosting a party or simply treating yourself, these sweet and decadent delights are sure to impress. So, grab some fresh strawberries, Mexican chocolate, and get ready to savor the perfect combination of flavors in every bite!
