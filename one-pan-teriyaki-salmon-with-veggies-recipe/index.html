One-Pan Teriyaki Salmon with Veggies Recipe


If you’re looking for a delicious and hassle-free meal that’s bursting with flavor, look no further than this One-Pan Teriyaki Salmon with Veggies recipe. Not only is it a feast for the taste buds, but it’s also a convenient option for those busy weeknights when you want to put a nutritious meal on the table without spending hours in the kitchen.

Ingredients

  • 4 salmon fillets
  • 1 cup teriyaki sauce
  • 2 tablespoons honey
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon rice vinegar
  • 1 teaspoon grated ginger
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 red bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 yellow bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 cup snap peas
  • 1 cup broccoli florets
  • 1 tablespoon sesame seeds
  • 2 green onions, chopped
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C).

  2. In a small bowl, whisk together the teriyaki sauce, honey, soy sauce, rice vinegar, grated ginger, and minced garlic. This will be the flavorful marinade for your salmon.

  3. Place the salmon fillets in a shallow dish and pour half of the teriyaki marinade over them. Allow the salmon to marinate for 15-20 minutes while you prepare the vegetables.

  4. In a large bowl, toss the sliced bell peppers, snap peas, and broccoli florets with olive oil, salt, and pepper.

  5. Take a large, oven-safe skillet and arrange the marinated salmon fillets and the seasoned vegetables in it. Drizzle the remaining teriyaki marinade over the vegetables.

  6. Place the skillet in the preheated oven and bake for 12-15 minutes, or until the salmon is cooked through and the vegetables are tender-crisp.

  7. Once the salmon is cooked, sprinkle the sesame seeds and chopped green onions over the dish for a pop of color and extra flavor.

  8. Serve the One-Pan Teriyaki Salmon with Veggies hot, straight from the skillet, and savor the delightful blend of sweet and savory flavors.

Why You’ll Love It

This One-Pan Teriyaki Salmon with Veggies recipe is a game-changer for anyone who craves a wholesome, Asian-inspired dish without the fuss. The teriyaki marinade infuses the salmon with a delightful umami flavor, while the vibrant medley of vegetables adds a satisfying crunch to every bite. What’s more, the simplicity of cooking everything in a single pan means minimal clean-up, giving you more time to enjoy your meal and less time spent washing dishes.

The beauty of this recipe lies in its versatility. You can easily customize the vegetable selection to suit your preferences or what’s in season. Whether you opt for tender asparagus, earthy mushrooms, or even baby corn, the teriyaki marinade will tie everything together in a harmonious symphony of flavors.

Not only is this dish a winner in the flavor department, but it also packs a nutritional punch. Salmon is rich in omega-3 fatty acids, which are known for their heart-healthy benefits, while the colorful array of vegetables provides essential vitamins and minerals. By bringing all these wholesome ingredients together in one pan, you’re treating your body to a nourishing and satisfying meal.

Make this One-Pan Teriyaki Salmon with Veggies your go-to recipe for a stress-free yet impressive dinner that’s sure to earn rave reviews. Whether you’re cooking for family, friends, or simply treating yourself to a delightful meal, this recipe is bound to become a cherished favorite in your culinary repertoire.
