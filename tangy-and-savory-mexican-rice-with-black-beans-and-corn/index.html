Tangy and Savory Mexican Rice with Black Beans and Corn


If you’re a fan of Mexican cuisine, then you’re in for a treat with this tangy and savory Mexican rice recipe. It’s a delicious and flavorful dish that combines the richness of black beans and the sweetness of corn with the zesty flavors of tomatoes, onions, and spices. This dish is perfect as a side dish or as a main course for a vegetarian meal. So let’s dive right into the recipe!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup long-grain white rice
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 can (15 ounces) black beans, rinsed and drained
  • 1 cup frozen corn kernels
  • 1 can (14 ounces) diced tomatoes, undrained
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon chili powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon paprika
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh cilantro, chopped (for garnish)

Instructions

  1. In a large pan, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the chopped onion and minced garlic, and sauté until they become translucent and fragrant.

  2. Add the rice to the pan and stir it well, making sure each grain is coated with the oil. Cook the rice for a couple of minutes, stirring occasionally, until it starts to turn golden brown.

  3. Next, add the diced tomatoes, black beans, and frozen corn to the pan. Stir everything together to combine the ingredients evenly.

  4. Sprinkle the ground cumin, chili powder, paprika, salt, and pepper over the mixture. Stir well to ensure that the spices are evenly distributed throughout the dish.

  5. Reduce the heat to low, cover the pan, and let the mixture simmer for about 20-25 minutes, or until the rice is cooked and the flavors have melded together.

  6. Once the rice is cooked, remove the pan from the heat and let it sit, covered, for about 5 minutes to allow the flavors to further develop.

  7. Fluff the rice with a fork, garnish with fresh chopped cilantro, and serve hot.

Serving Suggestions

This tangy and savory Mexican rice with black beans and corn is a versatile dish that can be enjoyed on its own or as a side dish. Here are a few serving suggestions to enhance your culinary experience:

  • Serve it alongside grilled chicken or steak for a complete and satisfying meal.
  • Use it as a filling for burritos or tacos for a flavorful twist on traditional Mexican dishes.
  • Top it with avocado slices, shredded cheese, and a dollop of sour cream for added creaminess and richness.

Final Thoughts

This tangy and savory Mexican rice with black beans and corn is a crowd-pleasing dish that brings together the vibrant flavors of Mexican cuisine. The combination of rice, black beans, and corn creates a satisfying and nutritious meal that will leave you craving for more. So next time you’re in the mood for a taste of Mexico, give this recipe a try and let your taste buds dance with delight!

Remember to adjust the spices and seasonings according to your personal preferences. Feel free to experiment with different ingredients, such as adding diced bell peppers or jalapeños for an extra kick of heat. The beauty of cooking is that you can make it your own and tailor it to suit your taste.

Enjoy this tangy and savory Mexican rice with black beans and corn, and let it transport you to the vibrant streets of Mexico with each flavorful bite!
