How to Make Perfect Pigs in a Blanket: A Classic Recipe


Pigs in a blanket are a beloved classic, often found at parties, family gatherings, and potlucks. These delightful little treats consist of cocktail sausages or hot dogs wrapped in a flaky pastry, making them the perfect finger food. Whether you’re serving them as an appetizer, snack, or a fun addition to a meal, mastering the art of making perfect pigs in a blanket is essential for any home cook. In this guide, we will walk you through the steps to create these delicious bites, along with tips, variations, and serving suggestions.

Ingredients

To make perfect pigs in a blanket, you’ll need the following ingredients:

  • 1 package of cocktail sausages or mini hot dogs (about 12-16 sausages)
  • 1 package of refrigerated crescent roll dough (or puff pastry)
  • 1 egg (for egg wash)
  • 1 tablespoon of water
  • Optional: sesame seeds or poppy seeds (for garnish)
  • Dipping sauces (mustard, ketchup, or your favorite sauce)

Equipment

Before diving into the recipe, gather the following equipment:

  • Baking sheet
  • Parchment paper (optional, for easy cleanup)
  • Rolling pin (if using puff pastry)
  • Sharp knife or pizza cutter
  • Mixing bowl (for egg wash)
  • Brush for egg wash

Step-by-Step Instructions

Step 1: Preheat the Oven

Begin by preheating your oven to 375°F (190°C). This ensures that your pigs in a blanket bake evenly and achieve that golden-brown color we all love.

Step 2: Prepare the Baking Sheet

Line a baking sheet with parchment paper. This step is optional but highly recommended for easy cleanup and to prevent sticking.

Step 3: Roll Out the Dough

If you’re using crescent roll dough, open the package and unroll it onto a clean surface. Separate the dough into triangles along the perforated lines. If you’re using puff pastry, roll it out on a lightly floured surface until it’s about 1/8 inch thick. Cut the pastry into rectangles, approximately 3 inches by 4 inches.

Step 4: Wrap the Sausages

Take one cocktail sausage or mini hot dog and place it at the wide end of the triangle or rectangle of dough. Roll the dough around the sausage, ensuring that the dough overlaps slightly. Continue rolling until the sausage is fully wrapped. Place the wrapped sausage seam-side down on the prepared baking sheet. Repeat this process until all sausages are wrapped.

Step 5: Prepare the Egg Wash

In a small bowl, whisk together the egg and water to create an egg wash. This mixture will give your pigs in a blanket a beautiful golden color when baked.

Step 6: Apply the Egg Wash

Using a brush, gently apply the egg wash over each wrapped sausage. This step is crucial for achieving that mouthwatering golden finish. If desired, sprinkle sesame seeds or poppy seeds on top for added flavor and texture.

Step 7: Bake

Place the baking sheet in the preheated oven and bake for 12-15 minutes, or until the dough is golden brown and puffed. Keep an eye on them to prevent over-baking. The aroma of baking pigs in a blanket will fill your kitchen, making it hard to resist!

Step 8: Serve

Once baked, remove the pigs in a blanket from the oven and allow them to cool for a few minutes. Serve warm with a selection of dipping sauces, such as mustard, ketchup, or a spicy aioli for an extra kick.

Tips for Perfect Pigs in a Blanket

  • Choose Quality Ingredients: The quality of your sausages and dough can significantly affect the overall taste. Opt for high-quality cocktail sausages or mini hot dogs for the best flavor.
  • Experiment with Dough: While crescent roll dough is a classic choice, you can also use puff pastry for a flakier texture. Additionally, try using different types of dough, such as biscuit dough or even homemade pastry for a gourmet twist.
  • Add Cheese: For a cheesy variation, place a small piece of cheese (like cheddar or mozzarella) on top of the sausage before wrapping. This adds an extra layer of flavor and gooeyness.
  • Spice It Up: For those who enjoy a bit of heat, consider using spicy sausages or adding jalapeños to the mix. You can also incorporate spices into the dough for an extra kick.
  • Make Ahead: Pigs in a blanket can be assembled ahead of time and refrigerated until ready to bake. This is a great time-saver for parties or gatherings.

Variations

While the classic pigs in a blanket are delicious on their own, there are countless variations to explore:

1. Buffalo Chicken Pigs in a Blanket

Swap out the sausages for cooked, shredded buffalo chicken. Wrap the chicken in dough and bake as usual. Serve with ranch or blue cheese dressing.

2. Vegetarian Option

Use vegetarian sausages or even roasted vegetables like zucchini or mushrooms wrapped in dough for a meat-free alternative. This option is perfect for vegetarian guests.

3. Breakfast Pigs in a Blanket

For a breakfast twist, use breakfast sausage links and wrap them in crescent roll dough. Serve with maple syrup for dipping.

4. Sweet Pigs in a Blanket

For a dessert version, use mini chocolate bars or marshmallows wrapped in dough. Bake until golden and serve with a dusting of powdered sugar.

Serving Suggestions

Pigs in a blanket are incredibly versatile and can be served in various ways:

  • As an Appetizer: Serve them on a platter with an assortment of dipping sauces for a fun appetizer at parties.
  • At Game Day Gatherings: These treats are perfect for game day. Pair them with chips and a variety of dips for a complete snack spread.
  • For Kid-Friendly Meals: Kids love pigs in a blanket! Serve them with fruit and veggies on the side for a balanced meal.
  • At Holiday Parties: Dress them up with festive garnishes and serve alongside other holiday-themed appetizers for a cheerful spread.

Conclusion

Making perfect pigs in a blanket is a straightforward process that yields delicious results. With just a few ingredients and some simple steps, you can create a crowd-pleasing snack that everyone will love. Whether you stick to the classic recipe or explore the many variations, these little bites are sure to become a favorite in your household. So gather your ingredients, preheat that oven, and get ready to impress your family and friends with your culinary skills!
