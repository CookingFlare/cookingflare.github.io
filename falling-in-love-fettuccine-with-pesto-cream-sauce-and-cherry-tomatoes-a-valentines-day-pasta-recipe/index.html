Falling in Love Fettuccine with Pesto Cream Sauce and Cherry Tomatoes: A Valentine's Day Pasta Recipe


Valentine’s Day is the perfect occasion to show your loved ones just how much you care. And what better way to celebrate this day of love than with a delicious, homemade pasta dish? Our Falling in Love Fettuccine with Pesto Cream Sauce and Cherry Tomatoes is not only a treat for the taste buds but also a feast for the eyes. The vibrant colors and flavors of this dish are sure to make your Valentine’s Day dinner unforgettable.

Ingredients

  • 8 ounces of fettuccine pasta
  • 1 cup of fresh basil leaves
  • 2 cloves of garlic
  • 1/4 cup of pine nuts
  • 1/2 cup of grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/2 cup of heavy cream
  • 1 tablespoon of olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 cup of cherry tomatoes, halved
  • Fresh basil leaves for garnish

Instructions

  1. Cook the fettuccine pasta according to the package instructions. Drain and set aside.

  2. In a food processor, combine the fresh basil leaves, garlic, pine nuts, and grated Parmesan cheese. Pulse until well combined and finely chopped.

  3. With the food processor still running, slowly add the olive oil in a steady stream until the mixture forms a smooth paste. Add salt and pepper to taste.

  4. In a separate saucepan, heat the heavy cream over medium heat until it simmers. Add the pesto sauce and stir until well combined. Cook for an additional 2-3 minutes, or until the sauce thickens slightly.

  5. Add the cooked fettuccine pasta to the saucepan with the pesto cream sauce. Toss gently to coat the pasta evenly.

  6. Add the cherry tomatoes to the pan and cook for an additional 2-3 minutes, or until the tomatoes soften slightly.

  7. Remove the pan from heat and garnish with fresh basil leaves.

  8. Serve the Falling in Love Fettuccine immediately, while it’s still hot and the flavors are at their peak.

Tips for Success

  • To enhance the flavors of the dish, toast the pine nuts in a dry skillet over medium heat until they turn golden brown. This will add a nutty aroma and depth to the pesto sauce.
  • If you prefer a lighter version of the dish, you can substitute half-and-half or milk for the heavy cream. However, keep in mind that the sauce may not be as rich and creamy.
  • Feel free to customize this recipe by adding your favorite proteins or vegetables. Grilled chicken, shrimp, or sautéed mushrooms would make excellent additions.
  • Serve the Falling in Love Fettuccine with a side of garlic bread or a fresh green salad to complete the meal.

In Conclusion

This Valentine’s Day, surprise your loved ones with our Falling in Love Fettuccine with Pesto Cream Sauce and Cherry Tomatoes. This pasta dish combines the freshness of basil pesto, the richness of cream sauce, and the burst of flavor from cherry tomatoes. It’s a perfect balance of colors, textures, and tastes that will make your Valentine’s Day celebration extra special. So go ahead, create a romantic atmosphere, and enjoy this delightful dish with your loved ones. Happy Valentine’s Day!
