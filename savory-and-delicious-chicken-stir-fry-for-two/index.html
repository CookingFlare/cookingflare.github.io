Savory and Delicious Chicken Stir-Fry for Two


When it comes to quick, flavorful meals, nothing beats the simplicity and versatility of a chicken stir-fry. This dish not only packs a punch with its savory flavors but is also incredibly easy to prepare. Whether you’re cooking for a romantic dinner or a cozy night in with a friend, this savory chicken stir-fry recipe is guaranteed to impress.

Ingredients

To create a delicious chicken stir-fry for two, gather the following ingredients:

For the Stir-Fry

  • 1 pound (450g) of boneless, skinless chicken breasts - sliced into thin strips
  • 2 tablespoons of vegetable oil - for frying
  • 1 bell pepper - sliced (any color you prefer)
  • 1 medium carrot - julienned
  • 1 cup of broccoli florets - fresh or frozen
  • 1 cup of snap peas - trimmed
  • 2 cloves of garlic - minced
  • 1 tablespoon of fresh ginger - minced
  • 3 green onions - chopped (white and green parts separated)

For the Stir-Fry Sauce

  • 3 tablespoons of soy sauce - low sodium preferred
  • 1 tablespoon of oyster sauce
  • 1 tablespoon of hoisin sauce
  • 1 teaspoon of sesame oil
  • 1 teaspoon of cornstarch - mixed with 1 tablespoon of water to thicken the sauce
  • ½ teaspoon of red pepper flakes - optional, for a spicy kick

Preparation Steps

Step 1: Marinate the Chicken

Start by marinating the chicken strips to infuse them with flavor. In a bowl, combine the sliced chicken with 1 tablespoon of soy sauce, a pinch of salt, and black pepper. Allow the chicken to marinate for at least 15-20 minutes while you prepare the other ingredients. This marination step enhances the flavor and tenderness of the chicken.

Step 2: Prepare the Vegetables

While the chicken is marinating, take the time to prepare your vegetables. Wash and slice the bell pepper, julienne the carrot, and chop the broccoli into bite-sized florets. Trim the snap peas and set all the vegetables aside.

Step 3: Make the Stir-Fry Sauce

In a small bowl, whisk together the soy sauce, oyster sauce, hoisin sauce, sesame oil, cornstarch mixed with water, and red pepper flakes (if using). This combination of sauces will give your stir-fry a rich, savory flavor that complements the chicken and vegetables perfectly.

Step 4: Stir-Frying the Chicken

Heat a large skillet or wok over medium-high heat and add 1 tablespoon of vegetable oil. Once the oil is hot, add the marinated chicken strips in a single layer. Stir-fry for about 5-6 minutes, or until the chicken is cooked through and golden brown. Make sure to stir frequently to prevent sticking and ensure even cooking.

Step 5: Adding the Aromatics

When the chicken is nearly cooked, add the minced garlic, ginger, and the white parts of the chopped green onions to the skillet. Stir-fry for an additional minute until fragrant, taking care not to burn the garlic.

Step 6: Incorporating the Vegetables

Next, add all the prepared vegetables (bell pepper, carrot, broccoli, and snap peas) to the skillet. Stir-fry for about 3-4 minutes until the vegetables are tender-crisp but still vibrant in color. The goal is to maintain a slight crunch for added texture.

Step 7: Adding the Sauce

Pour the prepared stir-fry sauce over the chicken and vegetables. Stir well to coat everything evenly. Allow the sauce to bubble and thicken for about 1-2 minutes, ensuring that the chicken and veggies are well coated in the savory goodness.

Step 8: Final Touches

Once the sauce has thickened, taste and adjust the seasoning as needed. You may want to add a splash more soy sauce or a sprinkle of salt to enhance the flavors. Remove the skillet from heat and sprinkle the green parts of the chopped green onions over the stir-fry for a fresh finish.

Serving Suggestions

Serve your savory chicken stir-fry immediately over a bed of cooked rice or noodles. Jasmine rice or brown rice adds a wonderful nutty flavor, while rice noodles can give a delightful chewiness to the dish.

Consider garnishing with sesame seeds or additional sliced green onions for an extra touch. If you love a bit of heat, serve with chili oil or sriracha on the side.

Storage Tips

If you have leftovers (which is rare because this dish is so delicious), store them in an airtight container in the refrigerator for up to 2 days. Reheat in the microwave or on the stovetop until heated through.

Nutritional Information

This chicken stir-fry is not only satisfying but also packed with nutrients. Here’s a rough estimate of the nutritional information per serving:

  • Calories: 350
  • Protein: 30g
  • Carbohydrates: 30g
  • Fat: 15g
  • Fiber: 5g

The combination of lean protein from the chicken and a variety of colorful vegetables makes this dish a well-rounded meal.

Tips for a Perfect Stir-Fry

  1. High Heat: Stir-frying requires high heat to quickly cook the ingredients while retaining their texture and nutrients. Make sure your pan is hot before adding the oil.

  2. Prep Ahead: Have all your ingredients prepped and ready before you start cooking. Stir-frying goes quickly, and having everything on hand will prevent any delays.

  3. Don’t Overcrowd the Pan: If you’re making a larger batch, consider cooking in batches to avoid steaming the ingredients. Overcrowding can lead to soggy vegetables.

  4. Variety of Vegetables: Feel free to use any vegetables you have on hand. Bell peppers, zucchini, mushrooms, and bok choy are all great additions.

  5. Customize Your Sauce: Experiment with different sauces or add-ins, such as garlic chili sauce for extra heat or peanut butter for a nutty twist.

Conclusion

This savory and delicious chicken stir-fry for two is a fantastic option for a quick weeknight dinner or a special occasion. With its vibrant colors, rich flavors, and nutritional benefits, it’s a dish that delights the senses and nourishes the body.

Whether you’re a seasoned chef or a kitchen novice, this recipe is sure to become a staple in your cooking repertoire. So grab your ingredients, fire up the skillet, and enjoy a delightful meal that’s both satisfying and simple to prepare!
