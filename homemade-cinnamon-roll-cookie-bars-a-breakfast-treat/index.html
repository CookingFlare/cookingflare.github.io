Homemade Cinnamon Roll Cookie Bars: A Breakfast Treat


If you’re a fan of cinnamon rolls and cookies, then you’re in for a real treat with these homemade cinnamon roll cookie bars! Imagine the comforting aroma of cinnamon wafting through your kitchen as these delicious bars bake to golden perfection. Whether you’re looking for a delightful breakfast option or a sweet treat to enjoy with your afternoon coffee, these cookie bars are sure to hit the spot.

Ingredients

For the Cookie Dough:

  • 2 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/2 cup brown sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

For the Cinnamon Filling:

  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, melted
  • 3/4 cup brown sugar
  • 2 tablespoons ground cinnamon

For the Cream Cheese Icing:

  • 4 oz cream cheese, softened
  • 1/4 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1 cup powdered sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C) and line a 9x13 inch baking pan with parchment paper.

  2. In a medium bowl, whisk together the flour, baking powder, and salt for the cookie dough.

  3. In a separate large bowl, cream together the softened butter, granulated sugar, and brown sugar until light and fluffy. Add the eggs one at a time, beating well after each addition. Stir in the vanilla extract.

  4. Gradually add the dry ingredients to the wet ingredients, mixing until well combined.

  5. In another bowl, prepare the cinnamon filling by mixing together the melted butter, brown sugar, and ground cinnamon until a smooth paste forms.

  6. Press half of the cookie dough into the bottom of the prepared baking pan, spreading it out into an even layer.

  7. Spread the cinnamon filling over the cookie dough base, creating an even layer.

  8. Drop the remaining cookie dough by spoonfuls over the cinnamon filling, then use a spatula to carefully spread it out to cover the filling.

  9. Bake in the preheated oven for 25-30 minutes, or until the cookie bars are golden brown and a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.

  10. While the bars are baking, prepare the cream cheese icing by beating together the softened cream cheese, butter, powdered sugar, and vanilla extract until smooth and creamy.

  11. Once the cookie bars are done baking, remove them from the oven and allow them to cool in the pan for 10-15 minutes.

  12. Drizzle the cream cheese icing over the warm cookie bars, then allow them to cool completely before slicing and serving.

Enjoy Your Homemade Cinnamon Roll Cookie Bars

These homemade cinnamon roll cookie bars are a delightful twist on the classic cinnamon roll, and they’re perfect for sharing with family and friends. The combination of buttery cookie dough, sweet cinnamon filling, and creamy icing creates a truly irresistible treat that’s sure to become a breakfast favorite. So, roll up your sleeves, preheat your oven, and get ready to indulge in the warm, comforting flavors of these delightful cookie bars. Whether enjoyed with a cup of coffee or as a special weekend breakfast, these homemade cinnamon roll cookie bars are a delicious way to start your day.
