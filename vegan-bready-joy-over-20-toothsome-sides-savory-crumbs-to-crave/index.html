Vegan Bready Joy: Over 20 Toothsome Sides & Savory Crumbs to Crave


Are you looking to indulge in some heavenly vegan bites without sacrificing flavor or texture? Look no further than these fantastic sides and savory crumb recipes that will have you craving more! This collection features over 20 mouthwatering dishes that are sure to be crowd-pleasers, plant-based or not. Let’s dive into this vegan bready joy!

  1. Crispy Sesame Garlicky Tofu Cubes

Your taste buds will do a happy dance over these crunchy and chewy tofu cubes, coated in aromatic sesame seeds, and showered with garlic goodness. They are a heavenly contrast of textures that you’ll want to eat on their own or stir into salads, grains, and vegetables.

  1. Grilled Cheese Sandwiches with Mushroom & Spinach

Upgrade your classic grilled cheese with earthy mushrooms and vibrant spinach. This twist on a timeless dish brings in rich umami flavors and some greens to up the nutrition ante, without sacrificing that creamy, crispy goodness we all love.

  1. Herb & Garlic Roasted Potatoes

A side dish classic with an exotic twist, these potatoes are perfect for any meal and pack a punch with their herb and garlic seasoning. Their crispy exterior gives way to fluffy, tender interiors that tantalize every taste receptor in your mouth.

  1. The Ultimate Vegan Mac & Cheese

It ain’t a vegan celebration without this creamy, dreamy, and comforting mac & cheese. Thanks to nutritional yeast and cashew cream, you’ll never believe this tasty dish is animal-product-free. The best part? It’s made in just one pot!

  1. Healthy & Tasty Vegan Green Bean Casserole

A warm and cozy dish that embodies the joy of sharing, this festive green bean casserole is full of earthy flavors and creaminess. Swap out dairy for a vegan cashew sour cream, and sprinkle crispy textured soy protein on top for some crunch.

  1. Cheesy Tomato & Roasted Garlic Bisque

Sip on this heavenly blend of luscious tomatoes, roasted garlic, and velvety almond milk. This creamy vegan bisque is the epitome of richness and will warm you from the inside out.

  1. Sweet & Spicy Roasted Chickpeas

These crunchy, spicy, and sweet treats are the perfect easy-to-make snack that won’t disappoint. Blast themselves to stardom by tossing them with a combination of maple syrup, cumin, chili powder, and lime zest.

  1. Buttery & Flaky Vegan Shortbread Cookies

These simple yet enticing vegan shortbread cookies are an absolute must-bake for any event. Vanilla-infused and topped with a hint of orange zest, these golden, crumbly cookies epitomize pure bliss.

  1. Crispy Sweet Potato Wedges with Herbs & Spices

These tasty, baked sweet potato wedges are seasoned with a magical blend of simple flavors and baked to a perfect crisp. They are colorful, delicious, and loved by all. Serve them with a side of ketchup or vegan garlic aioli for some extra fun.

  1. Alfredo Pasta with Caramelized Leeks & Spinach

This silky, elegant pasta dish is perfect for cozy nights in or a special dinner party. The garlic and rosemary complement the caramelized leeks and spinach to create a mouthwatering sauce that you’ll want to drink straight from the bowl.

  1. Turmeric & Coconut Broccoli

With vibrant turmeric and nutty-coconut flavors, this dish will be an instant hit. Roasted in the oven and lightly caramelized, you’ll want to make this side over and over again.

  1. Savory Bread Sticks with Garlic & Herbs

Homemade breadsticks hold so much irresistible appeal - from their aromatic garlic and herb fragrance to their tender yet crispy, cheesy bites. Whip up a batch to pair with your favorite tomato dipping sauce or simply munch on them on their own!

  1. Cheesy Broccoli & Cauliflower Soup

Whip up a batch of this warming soup when you’re craving something soul-soothing and comforting. The cashew-based cheese alternative adds rich, cheesy flavor, while the broccoli and cauliflower provide a satisfying bite.

  1. Cheddar & Cauliflower Bites

Bite-sized pieces of florets, coated in a crunchy-crisp batter, and seasoned with our favorite cheddar cheese flavors. These cauliflower bites are a tasty appetizer, side dish, or snack, and an impressive crowd-pleaser too.

  1. Vegan Quiche with Spinach & Mushrooms

Say goodbye to the excuse that vegan cuisine can’t be hearty or flavorful. Introducing this crunchy, tender quiche that is overflowing with spinach, mushrooms, and a perfect tofu-based filling that makes you wonder if the eggs are even missing.

  1. French Onion Soup with Herbed Croutons

We’ve given a light but flavorful vegan twist to a classic recipe, and the result is a gorgeous and delectable French onion soup, complete with herbed croutons on top. Don’t forget the vegan cheese for an added touch!

  1. Vegan Grilled Corn on the Cob

Grilled corn on the cob never tasted so good! The sweet summer flavor is amplified with a vegan, homemade garlic aioli, making it impossible to resist. Be prepared for your taste buds to do a happy dance.

  1. Pesto Stuffed Portobello Mushrooms

There’s nothing like the bold burst of flavors in a properly seasoned stuffed mushroom. Pesto joins forces with veggie-packed goodness, melting in your mouth with every bite. You won’t miss the meat at all!

  1. Creamy Vegan Mashed Potatoes

Buttery and light, this mashed potato dish delivers on comfort food without the animal ingredients. Try them with plant-based butter and a hint of cashew cream for that touch of richness you crave.

  1. Jalapeño Popper Stuffed Baked Sweet Potatoes

Fluffy baked sweet potatoes filled with creaminess, bursting with jalapeño heat, and sprinkled with vegan cheese. These are perfect for a fun twist on potato skins or a hearty dinner side dish you’ll want more of.

  1. Honey Glazed Carrots

Bring vibrancy and sweetness to your table with this honey glazed carrot dish! Besides their vibrant orange color and hint of natural sweetness, they offer you a burst of nutrients that will help you savor the dish even more.

Wrap up and Share the Joy:

I hope these recipes bring a little joy and excitement to your meals. Get your hands dirty in the kitchen and let your loved ones taste the delightful outcome. Remember that cooking is an art form and should be appreciated fully by all those who enjoy the fruits of your labor. Bon appétit!
