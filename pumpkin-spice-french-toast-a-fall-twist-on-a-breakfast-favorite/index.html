Pumpkin Spice French Toast: A Fall Twist on a Breakfast Favorite


Fall is in the air, and what better way to start your day than with a warm and comforting breakfast? If you’re looking to add a touch of autumn to your morning routine, then look no further than Pumpkin Spice French Toast. This delightful twist on a breakfast favorite will satisfy your taste buds and fill your kitchen with the irresistible aroma of fall spices.

Ingredients

To make this Pumpkin Spice French Toast, you will need:

  • 4 slices of thick-cut bread (brioche or challah work best)
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1/2 cup of milk
  • 1/4 cup of canned pumpkin puree
  • 2 tablespoons of granulated sugar
  • 1 teaspoon of vanilla extract
  • 1 teaspoon of pumpkin pie spice
  • Butter or cooking spray for greasing the pan

Instructions

  1. In a shallow bowl, whisk together the eggs, milk, pumpkin puree, sugar, vanilla extract, and pumpkin pie spice. Make sure all the ingredients are well combined.

  2. Heat a non-stick skillet or griddle over medium heat and lightly grease it with butter or cooking spray.

  3. Dip each slice of bread into the pumpkin spice mixture, making sure to coat both sides evenly. Allow any excess mixture to drip off.

  4. Place the coated bread slices onto the preheated skillet or griddle. Cook for 2-3 minutes on each side, or until golden brown and slightly crisp.

  5. Once cooked, transfer the Pumpkin Spice French Toast to a plate and serve immediately. You can garnish it with a dusting of powdered sugar, a dollop of whipped cream, or a drizzle of maple syrup for added sweetness.

  6. Repeat the dipping and cooking process with the remaining bread slices until all the batter is used.

Tips and Variations

  • For an extra indulgent treat, you can add a sprinkle of cinnamon sugar on top of each slice of Pumpkin Spice French Toast before cooking.

  • If you prefer a lighter version, you can use low-fat milk or almond milk instead of regular milk.

  • Feel free to experiment with different types of bread. Cinnamon swirl bread or even pumpkin bread would be a delicious alternative.

  • To make the Pumpkin Spice French Toast ahead of time, you can prepare the batter the night before and refrigerate it. In the morning, simply dip the bread slices and cook as directed.

  • If you have any leftovers, you can store them in an airtight container in the refrigerator for up to two days. To reheat, simply pop them in the toaster or warm them in a skillet over low heat.

Final Thoughts

Pumpkin Spice French Toast is the perfect way to celebrate the flavors of fall and elevate your breakfast experience. With its warm spices and cozy aroma, it’s sure to become a family favorite. Whether you’re enjoying it on a lazy weekend morning or serving it up for a special occasion brunch, this delightful twist on French toast is guaranteed to impress. So grab your favorite mug of coffee, cozy up in a blanket, and savor every delicious bite of this autumn-inspired breakfast treat. Happy cooking!
