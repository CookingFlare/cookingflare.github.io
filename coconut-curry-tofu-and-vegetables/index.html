Coconut Curry Tofu and Vegetables


Looking for a flavorful and satisfying plant-based dish that will tantalize your taste buds? Look no further! This Coconut Curry Tofu and Vegetables recipe is a perfect blend of creamy coconut, aromatic spices, tender tofu, and assorted veggies that come together to create a mouthwatering meal that’s both nutritious and delicious. Let’s dive right into the creation of this culinary masterpiece!

Ingredients:

  • 1 block of firm tofu, cubed
  • 1 can of coconut milk
  • 2 tablespoons of red curry paste
  • 1 tablespoon of soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon of coconut sugar
  • 1 red bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 yellow bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 medium zucchini, chopped
  • 1 cup of broccoli florets
  • 1 small onion, diced
  • 3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1-inch piece of ginger, grated
  • Fresh cilantro for garnish
  • Cooked rice or quinoa for serving

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the Tofu: Start by pressing the tofu to remove excess water. Once pressed, cut the tofu into cubes and set aside.

  2. Make the Curry Sauce: In a large skillet or wok, heat a small amount of coconut milk over medium heat. Add the red curry paste and stir until fragrant.

  3. Cook the Aromatics: Add the diced onion, minced garlic, and grated ginger to the skillet. Sauté until the onions are translucent and the mixture is aromatic.

  4. Add the Vegetables: Toss in the sliced bell peppers, chopped zucchini, and broccoli florets. Stir-fry for a few minutes until the vegetables start to soften.

  5. Simmer with Coconut Milk: Pour in the remaining coconut milk, soy sauce, and coconut sugar. Stir well to combine all the flavors.

  6. Introduce the Tofu: Gently add the cubed tofu to the skillet, making sure to coat it with the curry sauce. Allow the tofu to soak up the flavors.

  7. Let it Simmer: Cover the skillet and let the curry simmer on low heat for about 10-15 minutes, allowing all the ingredients to meld together beautifully.

  8. Serve and Enjoy: Once the vegetables are tender and the tofu is infused with the rich curry sauce, remove from heat. Serve the Coconut Curry Tofu and Vegetables over cooked rice or quinoa.

  9. Garnish: Sprinkle fresh cilantro on top for a burst of freshness and color.

Tips:

  • Feel free to customize your curry by adding other vegetables like carrots, snow peas, or mushrooms.
  • Adjust the spiciness by increasing or decreasing the amount of red curry paste to suit your taste preferences.
  • For an extra kick, squeeze some lime juice over the curry before serving.

This Coconut Curry Tofu and Vegetables dish is a delightful medley of flavors and textures that will surely become a favorite in your recipe rotation. Whether you’re a seasoned chef or a novice in the kitchen, this recipe is simple to follow and guaranteed to impress your family and friends.
