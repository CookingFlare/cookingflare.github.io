Lavender Shortbread Cookies: Floral and Delicate Delights


Do you want to add a touch of elegance and floral delight to your baking repertoire? Look no further than these Lavender Shortbread Cookies. These delicate treats are not only visually stunning but also carry the subtle floral notes of lavender, making them a perfect addition to any afternoon tea or special occasion. In this recipe, we’ll guide you through the steps to create these delightful cookies from scratch. Let’s dive in!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1/2 cup powdered sugar
  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tablespoon culinary lavender buds
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt

Instructions

Step 1: Preparing the Lavender

Start by preheating your oven to 325°F (165°C). While the oven is warming up, take the culinary lavender buds and grind them in a mortar and pestle until they are finely ground. You can also use a spice grinder for this step. The ground lavender will infuse the cookies with a delicate floral aroma and flavor.

Step 2: Creaming the Butter and Sugar

In a mixing bowl, cream together the softened butter and powdered sugar until light and fluffy. This process can take about 3-5 minutes, and it’s essential for creating the delicate texture of the shortbread cookies. The powdered sugar helps keep the cookies tender and crumbly, while the butter provides richness.

Step 3: Adding the Lavender and Flour

Once the butter and sugar are creamed, add the ground lavender to the bowl and mix until it’s evenly distributed throughout the mixture. Then, gradually add the flour and salt, mixing until a soft dough forms. Be careful not to overmix the dough, as this can result in tough cookies.

Step 4: Shaping the Cookies

Next, turn the dough out onto a lightly floured surface and gently knead it a few times to bring it together. Then, using a rolling pin, roll the dough out to a 1/4-inch thickness. You can use cookie cutters to cut the dough into your desired shapes. Traditional shortbread shapes include rounds, squares, or even floral shapes to complement the lavender flavor.

Step 5: Baking the Cookies

Carefully transfer the cut-out cookies onto a parchment-lined baking sheet. Place the cookies in the preheated oven and bake for 12-15 minutes, or until the edges are just beginning to turn golden. It’s essential to keep a close eye on the cookies as they bake to prevent them from overbaking, as this can result in a dry texture.

Step 6: Cooling and Enjoying

Once the cookies are done, remove them from the oven and allow them to cool on the baking sheet for a few minutes before transferring them to a wire rack to cool completely. This cooling process allows the cookies to firm up and develop their characteristic shortbread texture. Once cooled, these floral delights are ready to be enjoyed with a cup of tea or as a sweet treat on their own.

Conclusion

These Lavender Shortbread Cookies are a delightful departure from traditional shortbread, offering a unique floral twist that is sure to impress. Whether you’re looking to elevate your baking skills or simply want to indulge in a delicate treat, these cookies are the perfect choice. Their buttery, crumbly texture and subtle floral notes make them a standout addition to any gathering or afternoon tea. So, roll up your sleeves, gather your ingredients, and immerse yourself in the delightful world of lavender-infused baking. Happy baking!
