Delicious Instant Pot Chocolate Lava Cake Recipes


If you’re a chocolate lover like me, then this blog post is a treat for you! Today, I’m going to share some mouthwatering recipes for delicious Instant Pot Chocolate Lava Cakes. These decadent desserts are perfect for any occasion and can be whipped up in no time using your trusty Instant Pot. So, let’s dive right in and satisfy our sweet tooth!

Classic Chocolate Lava Cake

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter
  • 4 oz semi-sweet chocolate, chopped
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 large egg yolks
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • Pinch of salt

Instructions:

  1. Start by melting the butter and chopped chocolate in a microwave-safe bowl. Heat in 30-second intervals, stirring after each interval until completely melted and smooth.
  2. In a separate bowl, whisk together the eggs, egg yolks, and sugar until well combined.
  3. Slowly pour the melted chocolate mixture into the egg mixture, whisking continuously.
  4. Add the flour, vanilla extract, and salt to the batter, and stir until just combined. Be careful not to overmix.
  5. Grease your ramekins or small oven-safe bowls with butter or cooking spray.
  6. Divide the batter evenly among the ramekins, filling them about 3/4 of the way full.
  7. Place a trivet inside your Instant Pot and add 1 cup of water.
  8. Carefully place the ramekins on the trivet, making sure they are not touching the sides of the Instant Pot.
  9. Close the lid, set the vent to the sealing position, and cook on high pressure for 6 minutes.
  10. Once the cooking time is up, do a quick pressure release and carefully remove the ramekins from the Instant Pot using oven mitts.
  11. Let the cakes cool for a few minutes before inverting them onto serving plates.
  12. Serve warm with a dusting of powdered sugar, a scoop of vanilla ice cream, or a dollop of whipped cream. Enjoy!

Peanut Butter Chocolate Lava Cake

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter
  • 4 oz semi-sweet chocolate, chopped
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 large egg yolks
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • Pinch of salt
  • 1/4 cup creamy peanut butter

Instructions:

  1. Follow the same instructions as the Classic Chocolate Lava Cake recipe, but add the creamy peanut butter to the batter along with the flour, vanilla extract, and salt.
  2. Mix well to ensure the peanut butter is evenly distributed throughout the batter.
  3. Continue with the remaining steps of the recipe, cooking the cakes in the Instant Pot as directed.
  4. Serve warm and enjoy the irresistible combination of chocolate and peanut butter!

Raspberry Chocolate Lava Cake

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter
  • 4 oz semi-sweet chocolate, chopped
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 large egg yolks
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • Pinch of salt
  • 1/4 cup raspberry jam

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the batter following the instructions for the Classic Chocolate Lava Cake recipe.
  2. After filling the ramekins with the batter, drop a teaspoon of raspberry jam into the center of each ramekin.
  3. Use a toothpick or a skewer to swirl the jam into the batter, creating a marbled effect.
  4. Cook the cakes in the Instant Pot as directed in the previous recipes.
  5. The burst of tangy raspberry flavor adds a delightful twist to the classic chocolate lava cake.

Conclusion

There you have it, three delicious variations of Instant Pot Chocolate Lava Cakes to satisfy your sweet cravings. Whether you prefer the classic version, the peanut butter twist, or the fruity raspberry variation, these recipes are guaranteed to impress your guests or simply indulge yourself. So, grab your Instant Pot and let the decadence begin!
