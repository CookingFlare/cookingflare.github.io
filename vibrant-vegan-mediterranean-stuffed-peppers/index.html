Vibrant Vegan Mediterranean Stuffed Peppers


If you’re looking for a delicious and nutritious vegan dish that’s bursting with Mediterranean flavors, then you’re in for a treat with these vibrant vegan Mediterranean stuffed peppers. This recipe combines the freshness of Mediterranean ingredients with the heartiness of peppers, creating a dish that’s not only visually stunning but also incredibly satisfying.

Ingredients

For the Stuffed Peppers:

  • 6 large bell peppers (assorted colors for a visually appealing presentation)
  • 1 cup quinoa, rinsed
  • 2 cups vegetable broth
  • 1 can (15 ounces) chickpeas, drained and rinsed
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1/2 cup Kalamata olives, pitted and chopped
  • 1/2 cup red onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons fresh parsley, chopped
  • 2 tablespoons fresh mint, chopped
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Olive oil for drizzling

For the Lemon Tahini Dressing:

  • 1/4 cup tahini
  • 3 tablespoons fresh lemon juice
  • 2 tablespoons water
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 1/2 teaspoon cumin
  • Salt to taste

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Peppers: Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C). Cut the tops off the bell peppers and remove the seeds and membranes. Place the peppers in a baking dish, cut side up, and set aside.

  2. Cook the Quinoa: In a medium saucepan, combine the quinoa and vegetable broth. Bring to a boil, then reduce the heat to low, cover, and simmer for 15-20 minutes, or until the quinoa is cooked and the liquid is absorbed.

  3. Prepare the Filling: In a large mixing bowl, combine the cooked quinoa, chickpeas, cherry tomatoes, Kalamata olives, red onion, garlic, parsley, mint, and oregano. Season with salt and pepper, and toss to combine.

  4. Stuff the Peppers: Spoon the quinoa and vegetable mixture into the prepared bell peppers, packing it in tightly. Drizzle the stuffed peppers with a bit of olive oil for extra flavor.

  5. Bake the Stuffed Peppers: Cover the baking dish with foil and bake for 25-30 minutes, or until the peppers are tender.

  6. Make the Lemon Tahini Dressing: In a small bowl, whisk together the tahini, lemon juice, water, minced garlic, cumin, and salt until smooth and creamy.

  7. Serve and Enjoy: Once the stuffed peppers are done, drizzle them with the lemon tahini dressing and serve immediately.

Nutritional Benefits

These vibrant vegan Mediterranean stuffed peppers are not only a feast for the taste buds but also a nutritional powerhouse. Bell peppers are rich in vitamin C, quinoa provides a good dose of protein and fiber, while chickpeas add plant-based protein and essential minerals. The combination of fresh herbs and olives infuses the dish with antioxidants and healthy fats, making it a well-rounded meal for anyone looking to embrace a plant-based lifestyle.

Final Thoughts

The vibrant colors and flavors of the Mediterranean come together in this delightful vegan dish that’s perfect for a family dinner or a gathering with friends. Whether you follow a vegan diet or simply enjoy exploring new culinary horizons, these stuffed peppers are sure to become a favorite in your recipe collection. So, roll up your sleeves, gather your ingredients, and get ready to savor the taste of the Mediterranean with these vibrant vegan stuffed peppers!
