Whip Up a Dairy-Free Tiramisu for a Decadent Dessert


Are you a fan of the classic Italian dessert Tiramisu but looking for a dairy-free version that doesn’t compromise on flavor? Look no further! This Dairy-Free Tiramisu recipe is here to satisfy your cravings and impress your guests. With its luscious layers of coffee-soaked sponge cake and creamy filling, this dessert is a guaranteed crowd-pleaser. Best of all, it’s entirely dairy-free, making it suitable for those with lactose intolerance or dairy allergies. So, let’s dive into the delightful world of dairy-free Tiramisu and whip up this indulgent treat in the comfort of your own kitchen.

Ingredients:

For the Sponge Cake:

  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup brewed strong coffee, cooled
  • 1/4 cup vegetable oil
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 tablespoon white vinegar

For the Creamy Filling:

  • 1 1/2 cups raw cashews, soaked in water for 4-6 hours and drained
  • 1/2 cup coconut cream
  • 1/2 cup maple syrup
  • 1/4 cup coconut oil, melted
  • 2 tablespoons lemon juice
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Additional:

  • Unsweetened cocoa powder for dusting
  • Extra brewed coffee for soaking the sponge cake

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the Sponge Cake:

    • Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease and line a 9x9 inch square baking pan with parchment paper.
    • In a large bowl, whisk together the flour, sugar, baking powder, baking soda, and salt.
    • In a separate bowl, mix the brewed coffee, vegetable oil, and vanilla extract. Pour the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients and stir until just combined.
    • Add the vinegar and stir quickly. The mixture will fizz slightly.
    • Pour the batter into the prepared pan and bake for 25-30 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean. Allow the cake to cool completely.

  2. Prepare the Creamy Filling:

    • In a high-speed blender, combine the soaked cashews, coconut cream, maple syrup, melted coconut oil, lemon juice, and vanilla extract. Blend until smooth and creamy, scraping down the sides as needed.

  3. Assemble the Tiramisu:

    • Cut the cooled sponge cake into ladyfinger-sized pieces.
    • Dip each piece of sponge cake into the extra brewed coffee and arrange a layer of them in the bottom of a 9x9 inch dish.
    • Spread half of the creamy filling over the soaked sponge cake layer.
    • Repeat with another layer of soaked sponge cake pieces and top with the remaining creamy filling.
    • Cover and refrigerate the Tiramisu for at least 4 hours, or preferably overnight, to allow the flavors to meld and the dessert to set.

  4. Serve and Enjoy:

    • Before serving, dust the top of the Tiramisu with unsweetened cocoa powder for a finishing touch.
    • Slice and serve this decadent dairy-free Tiramisu to your eagerly awaiting guests. Watch as they savor every spoonful of this luscious dessert.

Conclusion:

This dairy-free Tiramisu is a testament to the fact that indulgent desserts can be enjoyed without dairy. With its rich flavors and creamy texture, it’s a delightful treat for any occasion. Whether you’re hosting a dinner party or simply treating yourself, this dairy-free Tiramisu is sure to leave a lasting impression. So, go ahead and whip up this decadent dessert in your own kitchen, and relish in the satisfaction of creating a dairy-free masterpiece that’s bound to impress. Cheers to the sweet taste of success!
