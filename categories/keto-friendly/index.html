Packed with flavor, healthy fats, and lean protein, it’s a satisfying meal that will keep you feeling satisfied and energized. Ingredients 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts 1 tablespoon olive oil Salt and pepper, to taste 4 cups romaine lettuce, chopped 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese Keto-friendly Caesar dressing (store-bought or homemade) Optional toppings: cherry tomatoes, sliced avocado, crispy bacon Instructions Preheat your grill or stovetop grill pan over medium heat. Season the chicken breasts with salt, pepper, and olive oil. Grill the chicken for about 6-8 minutes per side, or until it reaches an internal temperature of 165°F (74°C). Remove from heat and let it rest for a few minutes before slicing. While the chicken is resting, prepare the salad. In a large bowl, combine the chopped romaine lettuce and grated Parmesan cheese. Toss to combine. Slice the grilled chicken breasts into thin strips or bite-sized pieces. Add the sliced chicken to the bowl of lettuce and Parmesan cheese. Drizzle the desired amount of Caesar dressing over the salad. Toss until the chicken and lettuce are evenly coated with the dressing. If desired, add optional toppings such as cherry tomatoes, sliced avocado, or crispy bacon for extra flavor and texture. Serve immediately and enjoy! Why Choose Keto Chicken Caesar Salad? Low in Carbs: Traditional Caesar salads often contain croutons, which are high in carbohydrates.
