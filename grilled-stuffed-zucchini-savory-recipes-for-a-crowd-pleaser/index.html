Grilled Stuffed Zucchini: Savory Recipes for a Crowd-Pleaser


There’s nothing quite like the sizzle of a grill and the mouthwatering aroma of delicious food cooking to perfection. And when it comes to crowd-pleasers, grilled stuffed zucchini is a dish that never fails to impress. Whether you’re hosting a backyard barbecue or simply looking for a flavorful side dish, this recipe is sure to be a hit. So fire up the grill and get ready to tantalize your taste buds with these savory stuffed zucchini recipes!

Classic Cheese and Herb Stuffed Zucchini

Ingredients:

  • 4 medium-sized zucchinis
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh basil
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your grill to medium heat.
  2. Cut the zucchinis in half lengthwise and scoop out the seeds to create a hollow cavity.
  3. In a bowl, combine the Parmesan cheese, mozzarella cheese, basil, parsley, garlic, salt, and pepper.
  4. Fill each zucchini half with the cheese and herb mixture, pressing it down lightly.
  5. Place the stuffed zucchinis on the grill, close the lid, and cook for about 10-15 minutes, or until the cheese is melted and bubbly.
  6. Remove from the grill and serve hot as a side dish or appetizer.

Mediterranean-inspired Stuffed Zucchini

Ingredients:

  • 4 medium-sized zucchinis
  • 1/2 cup cooked quinoa
  • 1/2 cup crumbled feta cheese
  • 1/4 cup sun-dried tomatoes, chopped
  • 1/4 cup chopped Kalamata olives
  • 2 tablespoons chopped fresh dill
  • 2 tablespoons chopped fresh mint
  • 2 tablespoons lemon juice
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your grill to medium heat.
  2. Cut the zucchinis in half lengthwise and scoop out the seeds to create a hollow cavity.
  3. In a bowl, combine the cooked quinoa, feta cheese, sun-dried tomatoes, Kalamata olives, dill, mint, lemon juice, salt, and pepper.
  4. Fill each zucchini half with the Mediterranean-inspired filling, pressing it down lightly.
  5. Place the stuffed zucchinis on the grill, close the lid, and cook for about 10-15 minutes, or until the zucchinis are tender and the filling is heated through.
  6. Remove from the grill and serve warm as a light and refreshing main dish or side.

Spicy Taco Stuffed Zucchini

Ingredients:

  • 4 medium-sized zucchinis
  • 1 lb ground beef or turkey
  • 1 packet taco seasoning
  • 1 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1/2 cup salsa
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh cilantro
  • Sour cream, for serving

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your grill to medium heat.
  2. Cut the zucchinis in half lengthwise and scoop out the seeds to create a hollow cavity.
  3. In a skillet, cook the ground beef or turkey over medium heat until browned. Drain any excess fat.
  4. Add the taco seasoning and cook according to package instructions.
  5. Fill each zucchini half with the spicy taco meat mixture, then top with shredded cheddar cheese.
  6. Place the stuffed zucchinis on the grill, close the lid, and cook for about 10-15 minutes, or until the zucchinis are tender and the cheese is melted and bubbly.
  7. Remove from the grill and garnish with salsa, chopped cilantro, and a dollop of sour cream.

These grilled stuffed zucchini recipes are versatile, delicious, and perfect for any occasion. From the classic combination of cheese and herbs to the exotic flavors of the Mediterranean, there’s a stuffed zucchini recipe to suit every taste. So why not give them a try at your next gathering? Your guests will be impressed, and your taste buds will thank you! Enjoy the smoky goodness of grilled stuffed zucchini and savor the flavors that will make your mouth water. Happy grilling!
