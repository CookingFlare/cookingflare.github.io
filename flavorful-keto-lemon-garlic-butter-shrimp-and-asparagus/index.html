Flavorful Keto Lemon Garlic Butter Shrimp and Asparagus


If you’re looking for a delicious and healthy keto-friendly dish, this Flavorful Keto Lemon Garlic Butter Shrimp and Asparagus recipe is a must-try. Not only is it packed with flavor, but it’s also quick and easy to make, making it perfect for busy weeknights or a special weekend dinner. The combination of succulent shrimp, tender asparagus, and zesty lemon garlic butter sauce creates a mouthwatering dish that will leave you wanting more.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound large shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 1 bunch asparagus, trimmed and cut into 2-inch pieces
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/4 cup unsalted butter
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 lemon, juiced and zested
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley for garnish

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Shrimp and Asparagus: Start by patting the shrimp dry with paper towels and seasoning with salt and pepper. In a large skillet, heat the olive oil over medium-high heat. Add the shrimp to the skillet and cook for 2-3 minutes on each side until they turn pink and opaque. Remove the shrimp from the skillet and set aside.

  2. Cook the Asparagus: In the same skillet, add the asparagus and sauté for 4-5 minutes or until they are tender-crisp. Season with salt and pepper and transfer the asparagus to a plate.

  3. Make the Lemon Garlic Butter Sauce: Using the same skillet, melt the butter over medium heat. Add the minced garlic and cook for 1-2 minutes until fragrant. Stir in the lemon juice and zest, and let the sauce simmer for a couple of minutes until it slightly thickens.

  4. Combine and Serve: Return the cooked shrimp and asparagus to the skillet, tossing them gently in the lemon garlic butter sauce until everything is well coated. Cook for an additional 1-2 minutes to heat everything through.

  5. Garnish and Enjoy: Once everything is heated through, sprinkle fresh parsley on top for a pop of color and added freshness. Serve the flavorful keto lemon garlic butter shrimp and asparagus immediately, and enjoy the burst of flavors in every bite.

Health Benefits of the Ingredients

Shrimp

Shrimp is a great source of protein and low in carbohydrates, making it an ideal choice for keto diets. It also contains omega-3 fatty acids, which are beneficial for heart health.

Asparagus

Asparagus is rich in fiber, folate, vitamins A, C, and K, and antioxidants. It’s also low in calories and carbohydrates, making it a perfect vegetable for keto-friendly meals.

Lemon

Lemon is not only a great flavor enhancer but also a good source of vitamin C and antioxidants that can boost the immune system and aid in digestion.

Garlic

Garlic not only adds a wonderful depth of flavor to the dish but also offers various health benefits, including boosting the immune system and reducing the risk of heart disease.

Final Thoughts

This Flavorful Keto Lemon Garlic Butter Shrimp and Asparagus recipe is a delightful combination of fresh and vibrant flavors that will satisfy your taste buds while keeping you on track with your keto lifestyle. Whether you’re a seafood lover or just looking for a new keto-friendly dish to add to your repertoire, this recipe is sure to become a favorite. Give it a try and savor the delightful flavors of this easy and healthy dish.
