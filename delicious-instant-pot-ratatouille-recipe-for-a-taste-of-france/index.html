Delicious Instant Pot Ratatouille Recipe for a Taste of France


Are you ready to take your taste buds on a journey to the south of France without leaving your kitchen? Ratatouille is a classic French dish that bursts with the flavors of summer vegetables and aromatic herbs. Traditionally, this dish is slowly simmered on the stovetop, but with the help of the Instant Pot, you can achieve the same rich flavors in a fraction of the time. Let’s dive into this delicious Instant Pot Ratatouille recipe and transport ourselves to the charming streets of Provence.

Ingredients

  • 1 large eggplant, diced
  • 2 zucchinis, sliced
  • 1 large onion, chopped
  • 3 bell peppers (red, yellow, and green), diced
  • 4 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 4 large tomatoes, diced
  • 1/4 cup tomato paste
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried basil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh basil, for garnish

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Vegetables: Start by prepping the vegetables – dice the eggplant, chop the onion, slice the zucchinis, and dice the bell peppers. This vibrant medley of vegetables forms the heart of the Ratatouille.

  2. Sauté the Aromatics: Set the Instant Pot to “Sauté” mode and heat the olive oil. Add the chopped onion and minced garlic, and sauté until they turn golden and fragrant.

  3. Add the Vegetables: Once the aromatics are ready, add the diced eggplant, sliced zucchinis, and diced bell peppers to the Instant Pot. Sauté for a few minutes until the vegetables begin to soften.

  4. Incorporate the Tomatoes: Stir in the diced tomatoes, tomato paste, dried thyme, oregano, and basil. These ingredients infuse the Ratatouille with the essence of the Mediterranean.

  5. Pressure Cook: Secure the lid on the Instant Pot and set it to “Manual” mode for 5 minutes. The high pressure will quickly meld the flavors of the vegetables, creating a luscious and savory stew.

  6. Release the Pressure: Once the cooking time is complete, perform a quick pressure release. Carefully open the lid, and behold the vibrant colors and enticing aroma of the Ratatouille.

  7. Season and Garnish: Season the Ratatouille with salt and pepper to taste. Finish by garnishing the dish with fresh basil leaves for a touch of brightness and herbaceous aroma.

Serving Suggestions

This Instant Pot Ratatouille is a versatile dish that can be enjoyed in various ways:

  • As a Main Course: Serve the Ratatouille over a bed of fluffy couscous or quinoa for a satisfying vegetarian main course.
  • Accompaniment: Pair the Ratatouille with crusty French bread or alongside a perfectly grilled chicken or fish for a wholesome meal.
  • Brunch Delight: Top a slice of toasted baguette with a generous spoonful of Ratatouille and a poached egg for a delightful brunch treat.

Conclusion

With its vibrant colors, robust flavors, and wholesome ingredients, this Instant Pot Ratatouille embodies the essence of French countryside cooking. Whether you’re a fan of Mediterranean cuisine or simply seeking a delightful way to savor the bounties of summer produce, this recipe is sure to delight your palate. So, gather your fresh vegetables and let the Instant Pot work its magic as you savor the taste of France right in your own kitchen.

Bon appétit!
