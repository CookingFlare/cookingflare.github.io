Instant Pot Beef and Vegetable Curry: Aromatic and Delicious


If you’re looking for a flavorful and comforting meal that’s easy to prepare, look no further than this Instant Pot beef and vegetable curry recipe. Packed with aromatic spices, tender beef, and a variety of colorful vegetables, this dish is a perfect balance of savory, spicy, and hearty flavors. Plus, with the convenience of the Instant Pot, you can have this mouthwatering curry ready in no time.

Ingredients

  • 1.5 pounds beef stew meat, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon grated fresh ginger
  • 2 tablespoons curry powder
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon ground coriander
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric
  • 1/2 teaspoon cayenne pepper (adjust to taste)
  • 1 can (14 oz) coconut milk
  • 1 cup beef or vegetable broth
  • 2 large carrots, sliced
  • 2 potatoes, peeled and cubed
  • 1 red bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 cup green beans, trimmed
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh cilantro for garnish

Instructions

  1. Start by setting your Instant Pot to the sauté function. Add the vegetable oil and brown the beef stew meat in batches, then transfer to a plate.

  2. In the same pot, add the chopped onion, garlic, and ginger. Sauté for a few minutes until fragrant.

  3. Stir in the curry powder, cumin, coriander, turmeric, and cayenne pepper. Cook for another minute to toast the spices.

  4. Pour in the coconut milk and beef or vegetable broth, scraping the bottom of the pot to release any browned bits.

  5. Add the browned beef back into the pot, along with the sliced carrots, cubed potatoes, red bell pepper, and green beans. Season with salt and pepper.

  6. Secure the lid on the Instant Pot and set the valve to the sealing position. Cook on high pressure for 35 minutes, then allow a natural pressure release for 10 minutes before carefully venting any remaining pressure.

  7. Once the pressure has been released, carefully open the lid. Give the curry a good stir and adjust the seasoning if needed.

  8. Serve the aromatic beef and vegetable curry over steamed rice, garnished with fresh cilantro.

Tips and Tricks

  • For a richer flavor, you can brown the beef in the Instant Pot before sautéing the aromatics. This adds an extra depth of flavor to the curry.
  • Feel free to customize the vegetables based on what you have on hand. Cauliflower, peas, or even butternut squash would be wonderful additions to this versatile dish.
  • If you prefer a thicker curry, you can mix a tablespoon of cornstarch with a little water and stir it into the finished curry while using the sauté function to thicken it up.

Final Thoughts

With the Instant Pot, creating a flavorful and aromatic beef and vegetable curry has never been easier. The combination of tender beef, vibrant vegetables, and fragrant spices makes this dish a true crowd-pleaser. Whether you’re cooking for a weeknight dinner or entertaining guests, this Instant Pot beef and vegetable curry is sure to impress with its rich and complex flavors. So, grab your Instant Pot and get ready to experience the delightful aroma and taste of this delicious curry!
