Valentine's Day Fish Feast: Grilled Fish Recipes for Two


Valentine’s Day is a time to celebrate love and what better way to do so than by creating a delicious and romantic dinner for your significant other. If you’re looking to impress your partner with a special meal that’s both healthy and indulgent, then look no further than these grilled fish recipes for two. Grilling fish not only adds a smoky flavor to the dish but also provides a light and elegant meal perfect for the occasion.

Grilled Lemon Herb Salmon

Ingredients:

  • 2 salmon fillets
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon fresh lemon juice
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon fresh thyme, chopped
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Lemon slices for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the grill to medium-high heat.
  2. In a small bowl, mix together the olive oil, lemon juice, minced garlic, and chopped thyme.
  3. Season the salmon fillets with salt and pepper, then brush them with the lemon herb mixture.
  4. Place the salmon fillets on the grill and cook for 4-5 minutes on each side, or until the fish is opaque and easily flakes with a fork.
  5. Garnish with lemon slices and serve hot.

Serving Suggestion:

Pair this Grilled Lemon Herb Salmon with a side of roasted asparagus and a glass of crisp white wine for a delightful Valentine’s Day dinner.

Grilled Garlic Butter Shrimp Skewers

Ingredients:

  • 12 large shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 2 tablespoons melted butter
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon fresh parsley, chopped
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Wooden skewers, soaked in water

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the grill to medium heat.
  2. In a small bowl, combine the melted butter, minced garlic, chopped parsley, salt, and pepper.
  3. Thread the shrimp onto the wooden skewers, then brush them with the garlic butter mixture.
  4. Grill the shrimp skewers for 2-3 minutes on each side, or until they are pink and opaque.
  5. Serve the shrimp skewers hot, garnished with additional chopped parsley.

Serving Suggestion:

Serve the Grilled Garlic Butter Shrimp Skewers over a bed of fluffy quinoa and steamed broccoli for a complete and satisfying Valentine’s Day meal.

Grilled Swordfish Steaks with Mango Salsa

Ingredients:

  • 2 swordfish steaks
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Mango Salsa:

  • 1 ripe mango, diced
  • 1/4 cup red onion, finely chopped
  • 1/4 cup fresh cilantro, chopped
  • 1 jalapeno, seeded and finely chopped
  • 2 tablespoons lime juice
  • Salt to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the grill to medium-high heat.
  2. Brush the swordfish steaks with olive oil and sprinkle them with paprika, salt, and pepper.
  3. Grill the swordfish steaks for 3-4 minutes on each side, or until they are cooked through and have grill marks.
  4. In a bowl, combine the diced mango, red onion, cilantro, jalapeno, lime juice, and salt to make the mango salsa.
  5. Serve the grilled swordfish steaks with a generous spoonful of mango salsa on top.

Serving Suggestion:

Accompany the Grilled Swordfish Steaks with Mango Salsa with a side of wild rice pilaf and a refreshing cucumber salad for a tropical-inspired Valentine’s Day dinner.

Conclusion

These grilled fish recipes are not only delicious but also easy to prepare, making them perfect for a romantic dinner for two. Whether you decide to grill salmon, shrimp, or swordfish, these recipes are sure to impress your loved one and make this Valentine’s Day a memorable one. So fire up the grill, put on your chef’s hat, and treat your special someone to a delightful fish feast that’s bound to win their heart. Happy Valentine’s Day!
