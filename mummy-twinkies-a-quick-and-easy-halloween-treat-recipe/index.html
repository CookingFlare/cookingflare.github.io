Mummy Twinkies: A Quick and Easy Halloween Treat Recipe


Halloween is just around the corner, and it’s the perfect time to get creative with some spooky treats that are sure to impress your friends and family. One of the most popular Halloween treats is the Mummy Twinkie, a simple and delicious dessert that’s easy to make and looks like a mummy straight out of a tomb. In this recipe, we’ll show you how to make Mummy Twinkies using just a few ingredients and some basic baking skills.

Ingredients:

  • 1 package of Twinkies
  • 1 package of white chocolate chips
  • 1 package of black gel icing
  • 1 package of orange gel icing
  • 1 package of yellow gel icing
  • 1 package of green gel icing
  • 1 package of orange and yellow candy melts
  • 1 package of edible markers
  • 1 package of black sprinkles
  • 1 package of white sprinkles

Instructions:

  1. Start by unwrapping the Twinkies and placing them on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper.
  2. Melt the white chocolate chips in a microwave-safe bowl in 30-second increments, stirring between each interval, until smooth.
  3. Dip each Twinkie into the melted white chocolate, coating about 2/3 of the snack cake. Place the dipped Twinkie back on the baking sheet.
  4. Melt the orange and yellow candy melts in a microwave-safe bowl in 30-second increments, stirring between each interval, until smooth.
  5. Use the melted candy melts to create the mummy’s bandages. Simply drizzle a small amount of the melted candy onto the Twinkie, starting at the top and working your way down. Use a toothpick or a small knife to spread the candy evenly and create a striped pattern.
  6. Use the black gel icing to create the mummy’s eyes. Simply dot a small amount of the icing onto the Twinkie, about 1/4 inch apart.
  7. Use the orange and yellow gel icing to create the mummy’s nose and mouth. Simply dot a small amount of the icing onto the Twinkie, about 1/4 inch apart.
  8. Use the edible markers to add some personality to your mummy. You can add some eyebrows, a smile, or even some wrinkles to give your mummy some character.
  9. Finally, sprinkle some black and white sprinkles onto the Twinkie to give it a festive touch.

Tips and Variations:

  • To make your Mummy Twinkies more festive, you can add some Halloween-themed sprinkles or edible decorations.
  • You can also use different flavors of gel icing or candy melts to create different colors and designs.
  • If you want to make your Mummy Twinkies even more realistic, you can use some black and white fondant to create the mummy’s wrap. Simply wrap the fondant around the Twinkie, starting at the top and working your way down.
  • You can also add some candy eyes or a candy nose to give your mummy some extra personality.

Conclusion:

Mummy Twinkies are a fun and easy Halloween treat that are sure to impress your friends and family. With just a few simple ingredients and some basic baking skills, you can create a spooky and delicious dessert that’s perfect for any Halloween party. So why not give it a try and see what kind of creative and delicious designs you can come up with?
