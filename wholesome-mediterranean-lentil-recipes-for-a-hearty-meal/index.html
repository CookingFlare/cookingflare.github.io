Wholesome Mediterranean Lentil Recipes for a Hearty Meal


The Mediterranean diet is renowned for its heart-healthy components, and lentils are a staple ingredient in many Mediterranean dishes. Packed with plant-based protein, fiber, and essential nutrients, lentils are a versatile and nutritious addition to any meal. In this blog post, we’ll explore some delightful and wholesome Mediterranean lentil recipes that are not only delicious but also perfect for a hearty, satisfying meal.

1. Greek Lentil Soup (Fakes)

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup green or brown lentils
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 carrots, diced
  • 2 celery stalks, chopped
  • 3 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 can (14 oz) diced tomatoes
  • 4 cups vegetable broth
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley for garnish

Instructions:

  1. In a large pot, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the onion, carrots, and celery, and sauté until softened.
  2. Add the garlic and cook for another minute.
  3. Stir in the lentils, diced tomatoes, vegetable broth, and oregano. Bring to a boil, then reduce the heat and simmer for about 30-35 minutes, or until the lentils are tender.
  4. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  5. Serve the soup hot, garnished with fresh parsley.

2. Lebanese Lentil Salad (Mujadara)

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup brown or green lentils
  • 1 cup basmati rice
  • 2 large onions, thinly sliced
  • 4 cups water
  • 3 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • 1 teaspoon cinnamon
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh mint leaves for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Rinse the lentils and place them in a pot with 4 cups of water. Bring to a boil, then reduce the heat and simmer for 20-25 minutes, or until the lentils are tender but not mushy.
  2. In a separate pot, heat 2 tablespoons of olive oil over medium heat. Add the onions and cook, stirring occasionally, until they are caramelized and crispy. This may take around 20-25 minutes.
  3. In another pot, heat 1 tablespoon of olive oil over medium heat. Add the rice and sauté for a few minutes. Stir in the cumin, cinnamon, salt, and pepper.
  4. Add the cooked lentils to the rice and gently combine.
  5. Serve the mujadara topped with the caramelized onions and fresh mint leaves.

3. Italian Lentil Stew

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup brown lentils
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 2 carrots, diced
  • 2 celery stalks, chopped
  • 3 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 can (14 oz) crushed tomatoes
  • 4 cups vegetable broth
  • 2 tablespoons balsamic vinegar
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh basil for garnish

Instructions:

  1. In a large pot, sauté the onions, carrots, and celery in olive oil until softened.
  2. Add the garlic and cook for another minute.
  3. Stir in the lentils, crushed tomatoes, vegetable broth, balsamic vinegar, and thyme. Bring to a boil, then reduce the heat and simmer for about 35-40 minutes, or until the lentils are tender.
  4. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  5. Serve the Italian lentil stew hot, garnished with fresh basil.

These wholesome Mediterranean lentil recipes are not only delicious but also nutritious and satisfying. Whether you’re craving a comforting soup, a flavorful salad, or a hearty stew, these recipes have got you covered. Embrace the goodness of the Mediterranean diet with these delightful lentil creations, and enjoy a wholesome, satisfying meal that’s both good for your body and your taste buds.
