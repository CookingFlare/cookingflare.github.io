Wholesome Instant Pot Lentil and Vegetable Soup Recipes


If you’re looking for a hearty, nutritious, and easy-to-make meal, then look no further than these wholesome Instant Pot lentil and vegetable soup recipes. Packed with protein, fiber, and a variety of vitamins and minerals, these recipes are perfect for a cozy night in or a nourishing lunch. The Instant Pot makes the cooking process a breeze, allowing you to enjoy a delicious homemade soup without spending hours in the kitchen.

Classic Lentil and Vegetable Soup

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup green or brown lentils, rinsed
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 2 carrots, chopped
  • 2 celery stalks, chopped
  • 3 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 can diced tomatoes
  • 6 cups vegetable broth
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Set the Instant Pot to sauté mode and add a splash of olive oil. Sauté the onions, carrots, and celery until they begin to soften, then add the garlic and sauté for an additional minute.

  2. Add the lentils, diced tomatoes, vegetable broth, cumin, paprika, and thyme to the Instant Pot. Give everything a good stir and season with salt and pepper.

  3. Close the Instant Pot lid and set the valve to the sealing position. Cook on high pressure for 15 minutes, then allow for a natural pressure release for 10 minutes before carefully releasing any remaining pressure.

  4. Once the pressure has been fully released, open the Instant Pot and give the soup a stir. Taste and adjust the seasoning if needed.

  5. Ladle the soup into bowls and garnish with fresh parsley. Serve hot and enjoy!

Spicy Lentil and Vegetable Soup

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup red lentils, rinsed
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 1 red bell pepper, chopped
  • 1 green bell pepper, chopped
  • 3 carrots, chopped
  • 4 cups vegetable broth
  • 1 can coconut milk
  • 2 teaspoons curry powder
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric
  • 1 teaspoon cayenne pepper
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh cilantro for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Using the sauté function on the Instant Pot, sauté the onion, red and green bell peppers, and carrots until they start to soften.

  2. Add the red lentils, vegetable broth, coconut milk, curry powder, turmeric, and cayenne pepper to the Instant Pot. Stir well and season with salt and pepper.

  3. Secure the lid and set the Instant Pot to cook on high pressure for 10 minutes. Once the cooking time is complete, allow for a natural pressure release for 5 minutes before releasing any remaining pressure.

  4. Open the Instant Pot and give the soup a good stir. Taste and adjust the seasoning as needed.

  5. Serve the soup in bowls and garnish with fresh cilantro. This flavorful and spicy soup is sure to warm you up from the inside out.

Conclusion

These Instant Pot lentil and vegetable soup recipes are not only delicious and satisfying but also incredibly versatile. Feel free to customize the ingredients and seasonings to suit your taste preferences. Whether you prefer a classic, comforting soup or a spicy, exotic variation, these recipes are sure to become staples in your kitchen. Embrace the simplicity and wholesomeness of these Instant Pot soups, and treat yourself to a nourishing and flavorful meal.
