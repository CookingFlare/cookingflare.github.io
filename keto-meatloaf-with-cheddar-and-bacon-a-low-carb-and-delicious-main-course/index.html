Keto Meatloaf with Cheddar and Bacon: A Low-Carb and Delicious Main Course


Are you looking for a hearty and flavorful low-carb dish to add to your keto recipe collection? Look no further than this Keto Meatloaf with Cheddar and Bacon. Packed with savory flavors and easy to prepare, this dish is sure to become a staple in your weekly meal plan. The combination of ground meat, sharp cheddar, and crispy bacon creates a mouthwatering experience that will satisfy your cravings without derailing your keto lifestyle.

Ingredients

For the Meatloaf:

  • 2 pounds ground beef
  • 1/2 cup almond flour
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/2 cup chopped parsley
  • 1/4 cup chopped onions
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1/2 teaspoon paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried thyme

For the Filling:

  • 6 slices sharp cheddar cheese
  • 6 slices bacon, cooked until crispy

For the Glaze:

  • 1/4 cup sugar-free ketchup
  • 2 tablespoons Worcestershire sauce

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C) and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.

  2. In a large mixing bowl, combine the ground beef, almond flour, eggs, Parmesan cheese, parsley, onions, garlic, salt, pepper, paprika, oregano, and thyme. Mix until well combined.

  3. On a separate piece of parchment paper, press the meat mixture into a rectangle, about 1/2 inch thick.

  4. Layer the cheddar cheese slices and cooked bacon on top of the meat mixture, leaving a small border around the edges.

  5. Using the parchment paper to help you, carefully roll the meatloaf, tucking in the filling as you go. Seal the edges and ends to prevent the filling from oozing out while baking.

  6. Place the rolled meatloaf on the prepared baking sheet.

  7. In a small bowl, mix together the sugar-free ketchup and Worcestershire sauce. Brush the glaze over the top of the meatloaf.

  8. Bake for 45-55 minutes, or until the internal temperature reaches 160°F (71°C).

  9. Remove the meatloaf from the oven and let it rest for 10 minutes before slicing.

Serving Suggestions

This Keto Meatloaf with Cheddar and Bacon pairs beautifully with a side of steamed vegetables or a crisp green salad. The rich and savory flavors of the meatloaf are perfectly complemented by the freshness of the vegetables, creating a well-balanced meal that is both satisfying and nutritious.

Final Thoughts

With its robust flavors and low-carb profile, this Keto Meatloaf with Cheddar and Bacon is a winning choice for anyone following a keto lifestyle. Whether you’re cooking for a family dinner or meal prepping for the week, this dish is a versatile and delicious option that will leave everyone asking for seconds. Give it a try and experience the joy of indulging in a comforting meatloaf without compromising your health goals.
