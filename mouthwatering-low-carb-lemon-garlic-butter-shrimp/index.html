Mouthwatering Low Carb Lemon Garlic Butter Shrimp


If you’re a seafood lover looking for a delightful low-carb dish, then you’re in for a treat with this mouthwatering low carb lemon garlic butter shrimp recipe. Bursting with flavors of zesty lemon, savory garlic, and rich butter, this dish is not only a culinary delight but also a healthy option for those watching their carb intake. Let’s dive into the recipe and learn how to create this delectable dish in the comfort of your kitchen.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound of fresh shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 4 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1/4 cup of unsalted butter
  • 1 tablespoon of olive oil
  • 1 lemon (juice and zest)
  • 1 teaspoon of paprika
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley for garnish

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Shrimp: Start by patting the shrimp dry with paper towels to ensure they cook evenly. Season the shrimp with salt, black pepper, and paprika, ensuring that each piece is well coated.

  2. Sauté the Shrimp: In a large skillet, heat the olive oil over medium-high heat. Add the seasoned shrimp to the skillet and cook for 2-3 minutes on each side until they turn pink and opaque. Once cooked, remove the shrimp from the skillet and set them aside.

  3. Prepare the Garlic Butter Sauce: In the same skillet, melt the unsalted butter over medium heat. Add the minced garlic and sauté for a minute until fragrant. Be careful not to let the garlic brown.

  4. Add Lemon Zest and Juice: Once the garlic is fragrant, add the zest and juice of the lemon to the skillet. Stir the mixture gently to combine the flavors.

  5. Combine Shrimp and Sauce: Return the cooked shrimp to the skillet, tossing them in the flavorful lemon garlic butter sauce. Ensure that the shrimp are evenly coated with the sauce.

  6. Garnish and Serve: Sprinkle some fresh parsley over the shrimp for a pop of color and added freshness. Serve the low carb lemon garlic butter shrimp hot, accompanied by your favorite side dishes or over a bed of zucchini noodles for a complete low-carb meal.

Why You’ll Love This Dish

The combination of zesty lemon, aromatic garlic, and rich butter creates a symphony of flavors that perfectly complements the natural sweetness of the shrimp. This dish is not only a treat for the taste buds but also a great option for those following a low-carb or keto lifestyle. The simplicity of the recipe makes it perfect for a quick weeknight dinner or a special weekend meal.

Health Benefits

Shrimp is a fantastic source of protein and is low in calories, making it an excellent choice for those looking to maintain a healthy diet. The addition of lemon and garlic not only enhances the taste but also provides a dose of vitamin C and other essential nutrients. With the absence of heavy carbs, this dish is not only delicious but also fits well into a low-carb or keto diet plan.

Final Thoughts

The low carb lemon garlic butter shrimp is a versatile dish that can be served as an appetizer, a main course, or even as part of a seafood spread. Its vibrant flavors and health benefits make it a winner in any culinary repertoire. Whether you’re cooking for yourself or entertaining guests, this recipe is sure to impress. So, gather your ingredients, fire up your stove, and treat yourself to this delightful and satisfying dish. Enjoy!

In conclusion, this delectable low carb lemon garlic butter shrimp recipe is a true culinary gem that combines the freshness of seafood with the zing of lemon and the comforting richness of butter. Whether you’re looking for a healthy low-carb option or simply craving a flavorful seafood dish, this recipe is a must-try. So, grab your apron and get ready to create a memorable dining experience right in your kitchen. Happy cooking!
