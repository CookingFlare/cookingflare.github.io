The Perfect Snack: Spicy and Crispy Samosas


Craving a delicious and satisfying snack? Look no further than the classic Indian delicacy - Samosas! These triangular pockets of goodness are not only packed with flavor, but they also offer a delightful crunch that will leave you craving for more. In this recipe, we will guide you through the process of making the perfect snack: Spicy and Crispy Samosas.

Ingredients

For the dough:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup water

For the filling:

  • 2 medium-sized potatoes, boiled and mashed
  • 1/2 cup green peas, boiled
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 2 green chilies, finely chopped
  • 1 teaspoon ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 1 teaspoon coriander powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon red chili powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon garam masala
  • Salt to taste
  • Vegetable oil for frying

Instructions

  1. In a mixing bowl, combine the all-purpose flour, vegetable oil, and salt. Gradually add water and knead the dough until it becomes smooth and pliable. Cover the dough with a damp cloth and set it aside for 30 minutes.

  2. In a separate pan, heat some vegetable oil over medium heat. Add the cumin seeds and let them splutter. Then, add the chopped onions and sauté until they turn golden brown.

  3. Add the ginger-garlic paste and green chilies to the pan. Sauté for a minute until the raw smell disappears.

  4. Now, it’s time to add the spices! Sprinkle in the coriander powder, turmeric powder, red chili powder, garam masala, and salt. Mix well to combine the spices with the onion mixture.

  5. Add the mashed potatoes and boiled green peas to the pan. Stir everything together until the filling is well-coated with the spices. Cook for a few minutes, allowing the flavors to infuse.

  6. Remove the pan from heat and let the filling cool down to room temperature.

  7. Take the dough and divide it into small lemon-sized balls. Roll each ball into a thin circular shape, around 5 inches in diameter.

  8. Cut the circular dough in half, creating two semi-circles.

  9. Take one semi-circle and fold it into a cone shape, overlapping the straight edges. Seal the edge with a little water.

  10. Fill the cone with a spoonful of the potato and pea filling. Be careful not to overfill it, as it may burst while frying. Seal the open end of the cone by pressing the edges together.

  11. Repeat the process with the remaining dough and filling.

  12. Heat vegetable oil in a deep pan or kadhai over medium heat. Once the oil is hot, carefully slide in the samosas, a few at a time. Fry them until they turn golden brown and crispy on all sides.

  13. Remove the samosas from the oil and place them on a paper towel to drain excess oil.

  14. Serve the Spicy and Crispy Samosas hot with your favorite chutney or sauce. They make a perfect snack for any occasion!

Conclusion

Congratulations! You’ve mastered the art of making Spicy and Crispy Samosas. These delectable treats are guaranteed to impress your family and friends. The crispy outer layer, combined with the flavorful and spicy filling, will make your taste buds dance with joy. So, the next time you’re in the mood for a snack that packs a punch, give this recipe a try. Happy cooking!
