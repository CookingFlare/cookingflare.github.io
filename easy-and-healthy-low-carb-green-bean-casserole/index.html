Easy and Healthy Low Carb Green Bean Casserole


Are you looking for a delicious and nutritious dish that won’t derail your low carb diet? Look no further than this Easy and Healthy Low Carb Green Bean Casserole recipe! Packed with flavor and loaded with nutrients, this dish is sure to become a family favorite.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound fresh green beans, trimmed and halved
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup sliced mushrooms
  • 1 cup chicken or vegetable broth
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1 teaspoon Dijon mustard
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C). Prepare a casserole dish by greasing it lightly with olive oil or cooking spray.

  2. In a large pot of boiling water, blanch the green beans for 3 minutes. Drain and immediately transfer the beans to a bowl of ice water to stop the cooking process. Drain again and set aside.

  3. In a skillet, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the chopped onion and minced garlic, sautéing until fragrant and translucent.

  4. Add the sliced mushrooms to the skillet and cook until they release their moisture and begin to brown.

  5. Pour in the chicken or vegetable broth and bring to a simmer. Allow the mixture to simmer for about 5 minutes to allow the flavors to meld together.

  6. Stir in the heavy cream, Dijon mustard, dried thyme, salt, and pepper. Cook for an additional 2-3 minutes, until the sauce starts to thicken slightly.

  7. Add the blanched green beans to the skillet and toss gently to coat them in the creamy sauce.

  8. Transfer the green bean mixture to the prepared casserole dish, spreading it out evenly. Sprinkle the grated Parmesan cheese over the top.

  9. Bake in the preheated oven for 20-25 minutes, or until the casserole is bubbly and the cheese is golden brown.

  10. Remove from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes before serving. Garnish with fresh thyme leaves, if desired.

Why You’ll Love This Low Carb Green Bean Casserole

This Easy and Healthy Low Carb Green Bean Casserole is a wonderful dish for those following a low carb or keto lifestyle. By replacing traditional ingredients like canned soup and fried onions with fresh, whole foods, you can enjoy this classic comfort food guilt-free.

Not only is this casserole low in carbs, but it’s also packed with nutrients. Green beans are an excellent source of vitamins A, C, and K, as well as fiber and antioxidants. The addition of mushrooms provides even more nutritional value, as they are rich in B vitamins, minerals, and immune-boosting compounds.

The creamy sauce in this recipe is made with heavy cream, which adds richness and flavor without the added carbs. The Dijon mustard and dried thyme give it a little kick, while the Parmesan cheese adds a savory, cheesy finish.

Whether you’re serving this dish as a side for a holiday feast or as a main course for a weeknight dinner, it’s sure to impress. The combination of fresh ingredients and simple preparation makes this recipe a winner in terms of taste, health benefits, and ease of cooking.

So, why not give this Easy and Healthy Low Carb Green Bean Casserole a try? It’s the perfect way to enjoy a classic comfort food dish while staying on track with your low carb goals.
