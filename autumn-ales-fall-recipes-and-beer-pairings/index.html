Autumn Ales: Fall Recipes and Beer Pairings


As the leaves change color and the air turns crisp, it’s time to embrace the flavors of fall with delicious recipes and perfectly paired autumn ales. Whether you’re hosting a cozy gathering or simply looking to unwind after a long day, these fall-inspired dishes and beer recommendations are sure to elevate your autumn experience.

Pumpkin Ale Mac and Cheese

Servings: 4

Ingredients:

  • 8 oz macaroni
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 2 tbsp all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup milk
  • 2 cups shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1/2 cup pumpkin ale
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Chopped parsley for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Cook the macaroni according to package instructions, then drain and set aside.
  2. In a saucepan, melt the butter over medium heat. Stir in the flour until well combined.
  3. Gradually whisk in the milk until smooth, then add the cheddar cheese and pumpkin ale. Stir until the cheese is melted and the sauce is creamy.
  4. Season with salt and pepper, then add the cooked macaroni to the sauce. Mix well.
  5. Serve the pumpkin ale mac and cheese hot, garnished with chopped parsley.

Pairing Suggestion: Enjoy this rich and creamy dish with a spiced pumpkin ale for a perfect balance of flavors.

Beer-Braised Bratwurst with Sauerkraut

Servings: 6

Ingredients:

  • 6 bratwurst sausages
  • 1 onion, sliced
  • 2 cups sauerkraut, drained
  • 1 bottle of amber ale
  • 1 tbsp brown sugar
  • 1 tsp caraway seeds
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Mustard for serving

Instructions:

  1. In a large skillet, brown the bratwurst sausages over medium heat.
  2. Add the sliced onion and sauté until translucent.
  3. Stir in the sauerkraut, amber ale, brown sugar, and caraway seeds. Season with salt and pepper.
  4. Cover and simmer for about 20 minutes, or until the sausages are cooked through.
  5. Serve the beer-braised bratwurst with sauerkraut hot, accompanied by mustard.

Pairing Suggestion: Pair this hearty dish with a malty Oktoberfest beer to complement the savory flavors.

Apple Cider Donuts

Yield: 12 donuts

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp baking soda
  • 1 tsp cinnamon
  • 1/4 tsp nutmeg
  • 1/2 cup apple cider
  • 1/4 cup melted butter
  • 1 egg
  • 1/2 tsp vanilla extract
  • Powdered sugar for dusting

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C) and grease a donut pan.
  2. In a bowl, whisk together the flour, sugar, baking powder, baking soda, cinnamon, and nutmeg.
  3. In another bowl, mix the apple cider, melted butter, egg, and vanilla extract.
  4. Combine the wet and dry ingredients until just mixed.
  5. Spoon the batter into the donut pan and bake for 12-15 minutes.
  6. Allow the donuts to cool slightly, then dust with powdered sugar.

Pairing Suggestion: Pair these delightful apple cider donuts with a crisp hard cider for a delightful fall treat.

Conclusion

Embrace the flavors of fall with these delicious recipes and beer pairings that capture the essence of autumn. Whether you’re craving comfort food or looking to explore seasonal flavors, these dishes are perfect for enjoying cooler evenings and festive gatherings. Cheers to a flavorful fall season filled with warmth and delicious tastes!
