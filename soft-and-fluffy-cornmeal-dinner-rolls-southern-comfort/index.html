Soft and Fluffy Cornmeal Dinner Rolls: Southern Comfort


There’s nothing quite like a warm, homemade dinner roll to complement a comforting Southern meal. These Soft and Fluffy Cornmeal Dinner Rolls are the ultimate comfort food. With their delicate texture and subtle cornmeal flavor, they are sure to become a family favorite.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup warm milk (around 110°F)
  • 2 1/4 teaspoons active dry yeast
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/4 cup unsalted butter, melted
  • 1 large egg
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup finely ground cornmeal
  • 3 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 cup unsalted butter, melted (for brushing)

Instructions

  1. In a large mixing bowl, combine the warm milk, yeast, and sugar. Let it sit for about 5 minutes, or until the mixture becomes frothy.

  2. Add the melted butter, egg, salt, and cornmeal to the bowl. Stir until well combined.

  3. Gradually add the flour, one cup at a time, stirring well after each addition. The dough should come together and become slightly sticky.

  4. Turn the dough onto a lightly floured surface and knead for about 5 minutes, or until the dough becomes smooth and elastic.

  5. Place the dough in a greased bowl and cover it with a clean kitchen towel. Allow the dough to rise in a warm place for about 1 hour, or until it has doubled in size.

  6. Once the dough has risen, punch it down to release any air bubbles. Turn it out onto a lightly floured surface and divide it into 12 equal pieces.

  7. Shape each piece into a smooth ball by tucking the edges underneath. Place the rolls onto a greased baking sheet, spacing them about 2 inches apart.

  8. Cover the rolls with a clean kitchen towel and let them rise for another 30 minutes, or until they have doubled in size.

  9. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).

  10. Once the rolls have risen, brush the tops with melted butter. This will give them a beautiful golden-brown color and add a touch of richness.

  11. Bake the rolls in the preheated oven for 18-20 minutes, or until they are nicely golden on top.

  12. Remove the rolls from the oven and let them cool slightly on a wire rack before serving.

Serving Suggestions

These Soft and Fluffy Cornmeal Dinner Rolls are incredibly versatile and can be served alongside a variety of dishes. Here are a few suggestions:

  • Enjoy them with a hot bowl of chili or soup for a comforting winter meal.
  • Serve them with fried chicken or barbecue for a classic Southern feast.
  • Pair them with a fresh garden salad and some grilled vegetables for a lighter option.

However you choose to enjoy them, these rolls are sure to bring a touch of Southern comfort to your table.

There you have it! A recipe for Soft and Fluffy Cornmeal Dinner Rolls that will surely impress your family and friends. The combination of cornmeal and all-purpose flour creates a unique texture that is both soft and slightly crumbly. These rolls are perfect for any occasion, from weeknight dinners to special holiday meals. Give them a try and let the comforting aroma fill your kitchen. Happy baking!
