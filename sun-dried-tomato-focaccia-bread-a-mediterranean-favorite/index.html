Sun-Dried Tomato Focaccia Bread: A Mediterranean Favorite


Focaccia bread is a delightful staple in Mediterranean cuisine, known for its light, airy texture and versatility in flavors. One particular variation that stands out is the Sun-Dried Tomato Focaccia Bread. This savory and aromatic bread is a true crowd-pleaser, perfect for sharing with family and friends or enjoying on a cozy evening.

Ingredients:

  • 4 cups of all-purpose flour
  • 1 tablespoon of sugar
  • 1 tablespoon of salt
  • 1 packet of active dry yeast
  • 1 1/2 cups of warm water
  • 1/2 cup of extra virgin olive oil
  • 1/2 cup of sun-dried tomatoes (chopped)
  • 2 cloves of garlic (minced)
  • Fresh rosemary leaves
  • Sea salt flakes

Instructions:

  1. Activate the Yeast: In a small bowl, combine the warm water, sugar, and yeast. Let it sit for about 10 minutes until frothy.

  2. Prepare the Dough: In a large mixing bowl, mix the flour and salt. Slowly add the yeast mixture and 1/4 cup of olive oil. Knead the dough until smooth and elastic.

  3. First Rise: Place the dough in a greased bowl, cover it with a damp cloth, and let it rise in a warm place for about an hour until doubled in size.

  4. Shape the Focaccia: Preheat the oven to 400°F (200°C). Punch down the dough and transfer it to a baking sheet. Press the dough out to fit the pan. Let it rest for 15 minutes.

  5. Flavor Infusion: In a small bowl, mix the remaining olive oil, sun-dried tomatoes, garlic, and rosemary. Spread this mixture over the dough, gently pressing it in. Sprinkle sea salt flakes on top.

  6. Final Rise: Cover the dough with a clean kitchen towel and let it rise for another 30 minutes.

  7. Bake to Perfection: Bake the focaccia in the preheated oven for 20-25 minutes or until golden brown.

  8. Enjoy the Aroma: Once baked, remove the focaccia from the oven and let it cool slightly. Slice and serve warm.

Serving Suggestions:

  • Pair this Sun-Dried Tomato Focaccia Bread with a balsamic glaze and a selection of olives for a Mediterranean-inspired appetizer.
  • Use leftovers to make delicious sandwiches or toast them for a crispy snack.

Conclusion:

Sun-Dried Tomato Focaccia Bread is a flavor-packed twist on a classic favorite. The combination of tangy sun-dried tomatoes, fragrant garlic, and aromatic rosemary creates a culinary experience that transports you to the sunny shores of the Mediterranean. Whether you bake it for a special occasion or as a comforting treat, this bread is sure to impress. So, roll up your sleeves, preheat your oven, and indulge in the delightful world of homemade bread making!

Remember, the key to a perfect focaccia lies in patience and quality ingredients. Happy baking!
