Instant Pot Chicken Parmesan: Crispy and Cheesy


If you’re a fan of Italian cuisine but don’t have the time to spend hours in the kitchen, then this Instant Pot Chicken Parmesan recipe is perfect for you! With the help of your trusty Instant Pot, you can enjoy a crispy and cheesy chicken parmesan dish in no time. This recipe is not only delicious but also incredibly easy to make. So, let’s dive right in!

Ingredients

  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1 cup breadcrumbs
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 2 eggs, beaten
  • 2 cups marinara sauce
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Fresh basil leaves, for garnish

Instructions

  1. In a medium-sized bowl, combine the breadcrumbs, grated Parmesan cheese, dried basil, dried oregano, garlic powder, salt, and black pepper. Mix well to ensure all the flavors are evenly distributed.

  2. Dip each chicken breast into the beaten eggs, making sure to coat both sides. Then, dredge the chicken breasts in the breadcrumb mixture, pressing gently to make sure the breadcrumbs adhere to the chicken.

  3. Preheat your Instant Pot by selecting the “Saute” function. Add a tablespoon of olive oil to the pot and wait until it becomes hot. Carefully place the chicken breasts into the pot, ensuring they are not touching each other.

  4. Cook the chicken breasts for about 2-3 minutes on each side, or until they are golden brown and crispy. Once cooked, remove the chicken from the pot and set aside.

  5. Pour the marinara sauce into the Instant Pot, making sure to scrape the bottom to remove any browned bits. Place the chicken breasts back into the pot, on top of the sauce.

  6. Close the Instant Pot lid and set the pressure release valve to the “Sealing” position. Select the “Manual” or “Pressure Cook” function, and set the timer for 8 minutes on high pressure.

  7. Once the cooking time is complete, allow the pressure to release naturally for 5 minutes, then carefully perform a quick release by turning the pressure release valve to the “Venting” position. Be cautious of the hot steam.

  8. Open the Instant Pot lid and sprinkle the shredded mozzarella cheese over the chicken breasts. Close the lid and let it sit for a few minutes until the cheese melts.

  9. Serve the Instant Pot Chicken Parmesan hot, garnished with fresh basil leaves. You can pair it with your favorite pasta or a side salad for a complete meal.

Final Thoughts

This Instant Pot Chicken Parmesan recipe is a game-changer for busy individuals who still want to enjoy a homemade Italian classic. The Instant Pot ensures that the chicken turns out tender and juicy, while the crispy breadcrumb coating adds the perfect texture. The melted mozzarella cheese and tangy marinara sauce bring all the flavors together, making each bite a delight.

So, the next time you’re craving a comforting and satisfying meal, give this Instant Pot Chicken Parmesan recipe a try. It’s a quick and easy dish that will impress your family and friends. Happy cooking!
