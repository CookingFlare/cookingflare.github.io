Satisfying Nut-Free BBQ Turkey Meatballs


Are you looking for a delicious and satisfying appetizer or main course that’s both nut-free and packed with flavor? Look no further than these mouthwatering BBQ turkey meatballs! Made with lean ground turkey and a tangy homemade BBQ sauce, these meatballs are sure to be a hit at your next gathering or weeknight dinner.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound lean ground turkey
  • 1/2 cup breadcrumbs
  • 1/4 cup finely chopped onion
  • 1/4 cup finely chopped red bell pepper
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley
  • 1/4 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1/4 teaspoon dried thyme
  • 1/4 teaspoon paprika
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1/2 cup barbecue sauce

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C) and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.

  2. In a large mixing bowl, combine the ground turkey, breadcrumbs, chopped onion, chopped red bell pepper, minced garlic, chopped parsley, dried oregano, dried thyme, paprika, salt, and black pepper. Mix well until all the ingredients are evenly incorporated.

  3. Using your hands or a small scoop, shape the mixture into meatballs, about 1 inch in diameter. Place the meatballs onto the prepared baking sheet, leaving a little space between each one.

  4. Bake the meatballs in the preheated oven for 20-25 minutes, or until they are cooked through and golden brown.

  5. While the meatballs are baking, prepare the BBQ sauce. In a small saucepan, combine the barbecue sauce with a splash of water and warm over low heat until heated through.

  6. Once the meatballs are cooked, remove them from the oven and transfer them to a serving dish. Pour the warm BBQ sauce over the meatballs, ensuring they are coated evenly.

  7. Serve the nut-free BBQ turkey meatballs as an appetizer with toothpicks or as a main course with a side of your choice. They are perfect for parties, game nights, or even as a quick and easy dinner option.

Tips and Variations

  • For a spicier kick, add a pinch of cayenne pepper or a few dashes of hot sauce to the meatball mixture.
  • If you prefer a sweeter BBQ sauce, you can add a tablespoon of honey or brown sugar to the saucepan while warming the barbecue sauce.
  • Feel free to experiment with different herbs and spices to customize the flavor of the meatballs. Try adding a teaspoon of dried basil or a dash of smoked paprika for a unique twist.
  • These meatballs can be made ahead of time and stored in the refrigerator for up to 3 days. When ready to serve, simply reheat them in the oven or microwave and toss with BBQ sauce.
  • If you’re following a gluten-free diet, you can substitute the breadcrumbs with gluten-free breadcrumbs or crushed gluten-free crackers.

Enjoy these satisfying nut-free BBQ turkey meatballs as a delicious and healthier alternative to traditional meatballs. Their juicy and flavorful taste will have everyone reaching for seconds! Whether you’re hosting a party or simply looking for a tasty weeknight meal, these meatballs are sure to impress. So fire up the oven and get ready to indulge in a mouthwatering treat!
