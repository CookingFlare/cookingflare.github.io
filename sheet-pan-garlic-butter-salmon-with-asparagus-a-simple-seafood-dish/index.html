Sheet Pan Garlic Butter Salmon with Asparagus: A Simple Seafood Dish


If you’re looking for a delicious and effortless seafood dish, look no further than this sheet pan garlic butter salmon with asparagus recipe. This dish is a perfect combination of tender, flaky salmon and crisp, flavorful asparagus, all cooked to perfection on a single sheet pan. The best part? It’s incredibly easy to make and requires minimal cleanup, making it an ideal choice for a quick weeknight dinner or a special weekend meal.

Ingredients

To create this delectable dish, you’ll need the following ingredients:

  • 4 salmon fillets
  • 1 bunch of asparagus, trimmed
  • 4 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1/4 cup of melted butter
  • 2 tablespoons of fresh lemon juice
  • 1 teaspoon of paprika
  • 1 teaspoon of dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley for garnish

Instructions

Step 1: Preheat the Oven

Begin by preheating your oven to 400°F (200°C). This will ensure that it’s nice and hot when you’re ready to cook the salmon and asparagus.

Step 2: Prepare the Garlic Butter Sauce

In a small bowl, combine the minced garlic, melted butter, fresh lemon juice, paprika, and dried oregano. Mix the ingredients thoroughly to create a flavorful garlic butter sauce.

Step 3: Arrange the Salmon and Asparagus

Place the salmon fillets and trimmed asparagus on a large sheet pan, ensuring that they are arranged in a single layer. Season the salmon and asparagus with salt and pepper to taste.

Step 4: Drizzle with Garlic Butter Sauce

Pour the prepared garlic butter sauce over the salmon and asparagus, ensuring that they are evenly coated with the flavorful mixture.

Step 5: Bake to Perfection

Transfer the sheet pan to the preheated oven and bake for approximately 12-15 minutes, or until the salmon is cooked through and the asparagus is tender yet still slightly crisp.

Step 6: Garnish and Serve

Once the salmon and asparagus are beautifully cooked, remove the sheet pan from the oven. Garnish the dish with fresh parsley for a pop of color and added freshness. Serve the sheet pan garlic butter salmon with asparagus immediately, and enjoy the delightful flavors!

Benefits of Sheet Pan Cooking

Sheet pan meals, such as this garlic butter salmon with asparagus, offer a range of benefits that make them a popular choice for home cooks. Not only are they incredibly convenient, requiring minimal preparation and cleanup, but they also allow for the flavors of the ingredients to meld together beautifully while cooking. Additionally, sheet pan meals are versatile, allowing you to customize the ingredients and seasonings to suit your personal taste preferences.

Nutritional Value

Salmon is a rich source of omega-3 fatty acids, protein, and essential vitamins and minerals, making it an excellent choice for a nutritious meal. Asparagus, on the other hand, is packed with fiber, folate, and vitamins A, C, and K. Together, these ingredients create a dish that not only tantalizes the taste buds but also provides a nourishing boost for the body.

Final Thoughts

This sheet pan garlic butter salmon with asparagus recipe is a testament to the fact that delicious, restaurant-quality meals can be easily prepared at home with minimal effort. The combination of succulent salmon, vibrant asparagus, and flavorful garlic butter sauce is sure to impress your family and friends. Whether you’re a seafood enthusiast or simply looking to expand your culinary repertoire, this simple yet elegant dish is a must-try. So, gather your ingredients, preheat your oven, and get ready to savor every delightful bite of this fantastic seafood creation. Cheers to good food and memorable moments around the dining table!
