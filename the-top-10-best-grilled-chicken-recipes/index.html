The Top 10 Best Grilled Chicken Recipes


Grilled chicken is a classic favorite that never goes out of style. It’s healthy, versatile, and can be seasoned in a multitude of ways to suit all tastes. Whether you’re hosting a weekend BBQ or simply preparing a weekday meal, grilled chicken can be transformed into something special with just the right ingredients and techniques. In this post, we’re going to explore the top 10 best grilled chicken recipes that will satisfy any craving and impress your guests.

1. Classic Grilled Chicken with Lemon and Herb Marinade

Let’s start with a timeless favorite: classic grilled chicken. This recipe features tender, juicy chicken thighs marinated in a zesty lemon and herb mixture, giving it a fresh and tangy flavor that’s perfect for any occasion.

Ingredients:

  • 4 bone-in, skin-on chicken thighs
  • 1 lemon, juiced and zested
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon fresh rosemary, chopped
  • 1 tablespoon fresh thyme, chopped
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, combine lemon juice, lemon zest, garlic, olive oil, rosemary, thyme, salt, and pepper.
  2. Place the chicken thighs in a large resealable plastic bag or shallow dish and pour the marinade over them.
  3. Seal and refrigerate for at least 1 hour (or overnight for maximum flavor).
  4. Preheat your grill to medium-high heat.
  5. Grill the chicken for 6-8 minutes per side, or until the internal temperature reaches 165°F (75°C).
  6. Remove from the grill and let rest for 5 minutes before serving.

This grilled chicken recipe is light, flavorful, and a great introduction to the world of grilled chicken. It pairs beautifully with grilled vegetables or a fresh salad.

2. BBQ Grilled Chicken

When you think of grilling, BBQ grilled chicken probably comes to mind. This recipe gives you that smoky, tangy flavor you love, with a simple but delicious BBQ sauce that elevates the dish to the next level.

Ingredients:

  • 4 chicken breasts or thighs
  • 1 cup BBQ sauce (store-bought or homemade)
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your grill to medium-high heat.
  2. Rub the chicken with olive oil and season with salt and pepper.
  3. Grill the chicken for 6-7 minutes per side until fully cooked and the internal temperature reaches 165°F (75°C).
  4. In the last 2 minutes of grilling, brush the BBQ sauce onto the chicken and allow it to caramelize.
  5. Remove from the grill, let rest for 5 minutes, and serve.

This BBQ grilled chicken is perfect for summer cookouts and is sure to please a crowd. Pair it with coleslaw or cornbread for a true BBQ feast.

3. Spicy Grilled Chicken with Chili-Lime Marinade

If you’re looking to spice things up, this spicy grilled chicken with a chili-lime marinade is the recipe for you. The combination of chili powder, cayenne, and lime juice gives the chicken an irresistible kick, while the honey adds a subtle sweetness that balances the heat.

Ingredients:

  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 2 tablespoons chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon cayenne pepper
  • 1 tablespoon honey
  • 2 tablespoons lime juice
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, combine chili powder, cayenne pepper, honey, lime juice, garlic, olive oil, salt, and pepper.
  2. Place the chicken breasts in a resealable bag or shallow dish and pour the marinade over them. Seal and refrigerate for at least 1 hour.
  3. Preheat the grill to medium-high heat.
  4. Grill the chicken for 6-7 minutes per side until the internal temperature reaches 165°F (75°C).
  5. Remove from the grill, let rest, and serve with a side of rice or grilled corn on the cob.

This recipe is perfect for anyone who loves a little heat with their meal. The chili-lime combo brings bold flavors that will keep everyone coming back for more.

4. Grilled Chicken with Pesto Sauce

For a fresh and fragrant option, grilled chicken with pesto sauce is a fantastic choice. The basil pesto complements the smoky flavor of the grilled chicken perfectly, creating a delicious and well-rounded dish that’s great for any meal.

Ingredients:

  • 4 chicken breasts
  • 1/4 cup basil pesto (store-bought or homemade)
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your grill to medium-high heat.
  2. Brush the chicken with olive oil and season with salt and pepper.
  3. Grill the chicken for 6-7 minutes per side until fully cooked.
  4. In the last minute of grilling, brush the chicken with a generous amount of pesto sauce.
  5. Remove from the grill, let rest, and serve with a side of pasta or grilled vegetables.

The earthy, vibrant flavors of pesto bring an Italian twist to your grilled chicken. It’s light yet packed with flavor, making it a great option for a weeknight dinner or a special gathering.

5. Grilled Chicken Kebabs with Vegetables

If you’re in the mood for something fun and interactive, grilled chicken kebabs are always a hit. The beauty of kebabs lies in their versatility—you can use any vegetables and seasoning that you love.

Ingredients:

  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts, cut into cubes
  • 1 red bell pepper, cut into chunks
  • 1 yellow bell pepper, cut into chunks
  • 1 onion, cut into chunks
  • 1 zucchini, sliced
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a large bowl, combine olive oil, lemon juice, paprika, salt, and pepper.
  2. Add the chicken cubes to the marinade and let them sit for at least 30 minutes.
  3. Thread the chicken, bell peppers, onion, and zucchini onto skewers.
  4. Preheat the grill to medium-high heat.
  5. Grill the kebabs for about 8-10 minutes, turning occasionally, until the chicken is cooked through and has an internal temperature of 165°F (75°C).
  6. Serve the kebabs with a side of rice or a fresh green salad.

These chicken kebabs are perfect for outdoor gatherings. The combination of grilled chicken and veggies provides a balanced and satisfying meal that’s sure to please.

6. Grilled Chicken with Teriyaki Glaze

For a sweet and savory twist, grilled chicken with teriyaki glaze is a fantastic choice. The rich, umami flavor of teriyaki sauce brings a mouthwatering depth to your grilled chicken, making it a crowd favorite at any cookout.

Ingredients:

  • 4 chicken breasts or thighs
  • 1/2 cup teriyaki sauce
  • 2 tablespoons honey
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon sesame oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, mix together the teriyaki sauce, honey, soy sauce, sesame oil, and garlic.
  2. Marinate the chicken in the mixture for at least 30 minutes (or overnight for enhanced flavor).
  3. Preheat your grill to medium-high heat.
  4. Grill the chicken for 6-7 minutes per side, basting with more teriyaki glaze as it cooks.
  5. Once the chicken reaches 165°F (75°C), remove from the grill, let rest, and serve with steamed rice or stir-fried vegetables.

This teriyaki grilled chicken is sweet, savory, and slightly tangy. It pairs wonderfully with Asian-inspired sides like fried rice or bok choy.

7. Grilled Chicken with Avocado Salsa

For a fresh, healthy twist, try grilled chicken with avocado salsa. The creamy avocado and tangy lime combine to create a topping that’s perfect for grilled chicken. The flavors are light yet satisfying, making this a great dish for warmer months.

Ingredients:

  • 4 chicken breasts
  • 2 ripe avocados, diced
  • 1 small red onion, finely chopped
  • 1/2 cup cilantro, chopped
  • 1 lime, juiced
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your grill to medium-high heat.
  2. Season the chicken breasts with olive oil, salt, and pepper.
  3. Grill the chicken for 6-7 minutes per side, or until the internal temperature reaches 165°F (75°C).
  4. In a bowl, combine the diced avocado, red onion, cilantro, and lime juice. Season with salt and pepper.
  5. Once the chicken is grilled, top it with the fresh avocado salsa and serve with a side of quinoa or a mixed green salad.

This dish is light, refreshing, and full of healthy fats, making it the perfect meal for anyone looking for a nutritious yet flavorful option.

8. Grilled Chicken with Smoky Paprika Rub

For those who love bold, smoky flavors, this grilled chicken with smoky paprika rub is a must-try. The smoky paprika combined with garlic and cumin creates a deep, flavorful crust that perfectly complements the juicy chicken.

Ingredients:

  • 4 chicken thighs or breasts
  • 2 tablespoons smoked paprika
  • 1 tablespoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1 tablespoon brown sugar
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a small bowl, mix together smoked paprika, garlic powder, cumin, brown sugar, salt, and pepper.
  2. Rub the seasoning mixture evenly over the chicken.
  3. Preheat your grill to medium-high heat.
  4. Grill the chicken for 6-8 minutes per side, or until fully cooked with an internal temperature of 165°F (75°C).
  5. Let the chicken rest for 5 minutes before serving.

This smoky paprika rub creates a deeply flavorful crust that’s both bold and aromatic. Serve it with roasted potatoes or a crunchy slaw for a satisfying meal.

9. Grilled Lemon Garlic Chicken Skewers

For an easy and delicious grilled chicken option, try grilled lemon garlic chicken skewers. The marinade is simple but packed with flavor, and grilling the chicken on skewers gives it a nice char and crispy edges.

Ingredients:

  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts, cut into cubes
  • 2 lemons, juiced and zested
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, combine lemon juice, lemon zest, garlic, olive oil, salt, and pepper.
  2. Add the chicken cubes and marinate for at least 1 hour.
  3. Thread the chicken onto skewers.
  4. Preheat the grill to medium-high heat.
  5. Grill the skewers for 6-8 minutes, turning occasionally, until the chicken reaches 165°F (75°C).
  6. Serve with a light yogurt sauce or a Greek salad for a Mediterranean-inspired meal.

These lemon garlic chicken skewers are perfect for a quick dinner or a party appetizer. Their bright and zesty flavors are sure to be a hit.

10. Grilled Chicken with Mango Salsa

For a tropical twist, try grilled chicken with mango salsa. The sweetness of the mango, combined with the slight heat from the jalapeño and the freshness of the cilantro, makes for a refreshing and vibrant topping for grilled chicken.

Ingredients:

  • 4 chicken breasts or thighs
  • 2 ripe mangos, diced
  • 1/2 red onion, finely chopped
  • 1 jalapeño, deseeded and finely chopped
  • 1/4 cup cilantro, chopped
  • 1 lime, juiced
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your grill to medium-high heat.
  2. Grill the chicken for 6-8 minutes per side until the internal temperature reaches 165°F (75°C).
  3. While the chicken grills, combine the diced mango, red onion, jalapeño, cilantro, and lime juice in a bowl.
  4. Season with salt and pepper.
  5. Once the chicken is done, top it with the mango salsa and serve with rice or a light salad.

The combination of grilled chicken and tropical mango salsa is bright and full of flavor, perfect for a sunny day.

These top 10 best grilled chicken recipes offer a variety of flavors, from classic herb marinades to bold spicy glazes. Whether you prefer sweet, savory, or spicy, there’s a recipe here for every palate. Each of these recipes is easy to prepare and perfect for any occasion, from weeknight dinners to weekend barbecues. So fire up the grill, gather your ingredients, and enjoy a flavorful chicken feast!
