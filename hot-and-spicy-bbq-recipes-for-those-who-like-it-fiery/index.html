Hot and Spicy BBQ: Recipes for Those Who Like It Fiery


Welcome to the sizzling world of hot and spicy BBQ! If you’re a fan of bold flavors and fiery sensations, then you’re in for a treat. In this blog post, we’re going to explore some mouth-watering recipes that will ignite your taste buds and leave you craving for more. From zesty marinades to smoky rubs, get ready to take your BBQ game to the next level with these tantalizing hot and spicy creations.

Sriracha Honey Glazed Chicken Skewers

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound of chicken breast, cut into chunks
  • 1/4 cup of honey
  • 2 tablespoons of sriracha sauce
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon of soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon of lime juice
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Skewers, soaked in water

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, whisk together the honey, sriracha sauce, minced garlic, soy sauce, and lime juice.
  2. Season the chicken chunks with salt and pepper, then thread them onto the soaked skewers.
  3. Brush the chicken skewers with the sriracha honey glaze, making sure to coat them evenly.
  4. Preheat the grill to medium-high heat and grill the skewers for 8-10 minutes, turning occasionally and basting with the remaining glaze.
  5. Once the chicken is cooked through and has a beautiful glaze, remove from the grill and let it rest for a few minutes before serving.

These succulent chicken skewers are a perfect balance of sweet and spicy, making them an irresistible addition to any BBQ spread.

Fiery Jalapeno and Pineapple BBQ Pork Ribs

Ingredients:

  • 2 racks of pork ribs
  • 1 cup of BBQ sauce
  • 2 jalapenos, finely chopped
  • 1 cup of pineapple juice
  • 2 tablespoons of brown sugar
  • 1 tablespoon of paprika
  • 2 teaspoons of cayenne pepper
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 275°F (135°C).
  2. In a bowl, combine the BBQ sauce, chopped jalapenos, pineapple juice, brown sugar, paprika, cayenne pepper, salt, and pepper.
  3. Place each rack of ribs on a large piece of aluminum foil and generously brush them with the spicy BBQ sauce mixture.
  4. Wrap the ribs tightly in the foil and place them on a baking sheet.
  5. Bake the ribs in the preheated oven for 2.5 to 3 hours, or until the meat is tender and begins to pull away from the bones.
  6. Once the ribs are cooked, carefully unwrap them from the foil and finish them on the grill over medium heat for 10-15 minutes, basting with extra BBQ sauce for a caramelized finish.

These jalapeno and pineapple BBQ pork ribs are a fiery twist on a classic favorite, delivering a perfect blend of heat and sweetness in every juicy bite.

Spicy Chipotle BBQ Corn on the Cob

Ingredients:

  • 4 ears of corn, husked
  • 1/4 cup of mayonnaise
  • 2 tablespoons of chipotle peppers in adobo sauce, minced
  • 1/4 cup of grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 tablespoon of chili powder
  • 1 tablespoon of fresh cilantro, chopped
  • Lime wedges
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the grill to medium-high heat.
  2. In a small bowl, mix together the mayonnaise and minced chipotle peppers.
  3. Brush each ear of corn with the chipotle mayonnaise mixture, then sprinkle with Parmesan cheese, chili powder, and a pinch of salt and pepper.
  4. Grill the corn for 10-12 minutes, turning occasionally, until lightly charred and tender.
  5. Remove the corn from the grill and sprinkle with fresh cilantro. Serve with lime wedges for an added zing.

This spicy chipotle BBQ corn on the cob is a sensational side dish that will have your taste buds dancing with delight.

Conclusion

There you have it, a fiery collection of hot and spicy BBQ recipes that will set your taste buds ablaze. Whether you’re hosting a summer cookout or simply craving some bold flavors, these recipes are sure to impress. So, fire up the grill, gather your friends and family, and get ready to indulge in the irresistible heat of these sizzling creations. Happy grilling!
