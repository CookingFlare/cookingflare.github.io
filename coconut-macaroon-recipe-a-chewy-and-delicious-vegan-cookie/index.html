Coconut Macaroon Recipe: A Chewy and Delicious Vegan Cookie

3 min · 492 words

If you’re a fan of coconut and all things sweet, you’re in for a treat! These coconut macaroons are not only incredibly delicious but also vegan-friendly, making them a perfect option for those with dietary restrictions or anyone looking for a healthier alternative to traditional cookies. With their chewy texture, sweet coconut flavor, and simple preparation, these macaroons are sure to become a staple in your baking repertoire.

Ingredients

  • 3 cups shredded coconut
  • 1/2 cup coconut cream
  • 1/2 cup maple syrup
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 cup almond flour
  • 1/4 cup coconut oil, melted

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 325°F (165°C) and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.

  2. In a large mixing bowl, combine the shredded coconut, coconut cream, maple syrup, vanilla extract, and salt. Stir until well combined.

  3. Add the all-purpose flour and almond flour to the mixture and stir until the dry ingredients are fully incorporated.

  4. Lastly, pour in the melted coconut oil and mix until the mixture holds together.

  5. Using a cookie scoop or your hands, form the coconut mixture into small mounds and place them on the prepared baking sheet, leaving some space between each macaroon.

  6. Bake the macaroons for 20-25 minutes, or until the edges are golden brown and the tops are lightly toasted.

  7. Once baked, remove the macaroons from the oven and allow them to cool on the baking sheet for a few minutes before transferring them to a wire rack to cool completely.

  8. Store the cooled macaroons in an airtight container at room temperature for up to a week.

Tips for Success

  • Make sure to use unsweetened shredded coconut to control the sweetness of the macaroons.
  • If you prefer a darker color and richer flavor, you can toast the shredded coconut before using it in the recipe.
  • For an extra indulgent touch, you can dip the bottoms of the cooled macaroons in melted dairy-free chocolate and let them set on parchment paper before serving.

Why You’ll Love These Macaroons

What sets these coconut macaroons apart is their simplicity and the use of wholesome, plant-based ingredients. The combination of shredded coconut, coconut cream, and a touch of maple syrup creates a delightful sweetness that is perfectly complemented by the nutty flavors from the almond flour and coconut oil. The result is a chewy, golden-brown macaroon that is bursting with coconut goodness in every bite.

Final Thoughts

Whether you’re vegan, dairy-free, or simply a coconut enthusiast, these macaroons are a delightful treat that is easy to make and even easier to enjoy. Their chewy texture and sweet, coconutty flavor make them a hit with kids and adults alike. Plus, with their minimal ingredients and straightforward preparation, you can whip up a batch of these vegan macaroons whenever a craving strikes. So, go ahead and indulge in these delightful treats, and savor the satisfying simplicity of homemade coconut macaroons!
