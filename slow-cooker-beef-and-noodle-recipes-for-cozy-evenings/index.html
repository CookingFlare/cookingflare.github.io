Slow Cooker Beef and Noodle Recipes for Cozy Evenings


Are you craving a warm and comforting meal that’s perfect for those cozy evenings at home? Look no further than these delicious slow cooker beef and noodle recipes. Packed with flavor and easy to prepare, these dishes will leave you satisfied and ready to curl up on the couch with a good book or your favorite TV show. Let’s dive into these mouthwatering recipes that are sure to become your go-to comfort food options.

1. Classic Beef Stroganoff

Ingredients:

  • 2 pounds beef stew meat, cubed
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup beef broth
  • 1 cup sour cream
  • 2 tablespoons Worcestershire sauce
  • 2 tablespoons Dijon mustard
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 8 ounces egg noodles, cooked according to package instructions

Instructions:

  1. In a slow cooker, combine the beef, onion, garlic, beef broth, Worcestershire sauce, Dijon mustard, salt, and pepper.
  2. Cover and cook on low heat for 6-8 hours or until the beef is tender.
  3. Stir in the sour cream and cook for an additional 10 minutes.
  4. Serve the beef stroganoff over cooked egg noodles and garnish with parsley, if desired.

2. Asian-Inspired Beef and Broccoli Noodles

Ingredients:

  • 2 pounds beef sirloin, thinly sliced
  • 1 cup low-sodium soy sauce
  • 1/4 cup hoisin sauce
  • 1/4 cup honey
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons cornstarch
  • 2 cups broccoli florets
  • 8 ounces rice noodles, cooked according to package instructions
  • Sesame seeds for garnish (optional)

Instructions:

  1. In a slow cooker, combine the beef, soy sauce, hoisin sauce, honey, and garlic.
  2. Cover and cook on low heat for 4-6 hours or until the beef is tender.
  3. In a small bowl, whisk together the cornstarch and 1/4 cup of water until smooth. Stir the mixture into the slow cooker and add the broccoli.
  4. Cover and cook on high heat for an additional 30 minutes or until the sauce has thickened and the broccoli is tender.
  5. Serve the beef and broccoli over cooked rice noodles and garnish with sesame seeds, if desired.

3. Italian-Inspired Beef and Tomato Noodles

Ingredients:

  • 2 pounds beef chuck roast, cut into chunks
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can (28 ounces) crushed tomatoes
  • 1 can (6 ounces) tomato paste
  • 2 teaspoons dried basil
  • 2 teaspoons dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 8 ounces spaghetti, cooked according to package instructions

Instructions:

  1. In a slow cooker, combine the beef, onion, garlic, crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, basil, oregano, thyme, salt, and pepper.
  2. Cover and cook on low heat for 8-10 hours or until the beef is tender and easily shreds.
  3. Use two forks to shred the beef in the slow cooker.
  4. Serve the beef and tomato sauce over cooked spaghetti and sprinkle with Parmesan cheese, if desired.

Conclusion

There you have it! Three delicious slow cooker beef and noodle recipes that are perfect for those cozy evenings at home. Whether you’re in the mood for a classic stroganoff, an Asian-inspired dish, or an Italian-inspired creation, these recipes have got you covered. So grab your slow cooker, gather the ingredients, and get ready to enjoy a warm and comforting meal that will satisfy your taste buds and warm your soul.
