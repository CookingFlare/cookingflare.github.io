Rustic Corn Chowder: A Comforting Soup Option


When it comes to comfort food, few things can beat a warm and hearty bowl of soup. And if you’re looking for a soup that will not only satisfy your taste buds but also warm your soul, then look no further than Rustic Corn Chowder. This creamy and flavorful soup is the perfect option for those chilly evenings when you crave something comforting and satisfying.

The Ingredients You’ll Need

To make this delicious Rustic Corn Chowder, you’ll need the following ingredients:

  • 4 cups of fresh or frozen corn kernels
  • 3 slices of bacon, chopped
  • 1 medium onion, diced
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 2 medium potatoes, peeled and diced
  • 4 cups of chicken or vegetable broth
  • 1 cup of heavy cream
  • 1 teaspoon of dried thyme
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Chopped fresh chives for garnish

Let’s Get Cooking!

  1. In a large pot, cook the chopped bacon over medium heat until crispy. Remove the bacon from the pot and set it aside, leaving the bacon grease in the pot.

  2. Add the diced onion and minced garlic to the pot with the bacon grease. Saute them until they become soft and translucent, usually about 5 minutes.

  3. Add the diced potatoes and corn kernels to the pot, stirring them well to combine with the onion and garlic mixture. Cook for another 5 minutes or until the vegetables start to soften.

  4. Pour in the chicken or vegetable broth, ensuring that the vegetables are fully submerged. Bring the mixture to a boil, then reduce the heat to low and let it simmer for about 15-20 minutes or until the potatoes are tender.

  5. Using an immersion blender or a regular blender, puree about half of the soup until smooth. This step will give the chowder a creamy consistency while still maintaining some texture.

  6. Return the pureed soup back to the pot and stir in the heavy cream and dried thyme. Season with salt and pepper according to your taste. Allow the soup to simmer for an additional 5 minutes to let the flavors meld together.

  7. Serve the Rustic Corn Chowder hot, garnished with the crispy bacon and chopped fresh chives. You can also sprinkle some additional corn kernels on top for added texture if desired.

Why You’ll Love This Soup

Rustic Corn Chowder is a soup that offers both simplicity and depth of flavor. The natural sweetness of the corn combined with the smoky and savory notes from the bacon create a harmonious and comforting taste experience. The creamy texture makes each spoonful a delight to savor, while the addition of thyme adds a subtle earthiness that enhances the overall flavor profile.

Not only is this soup delicious, but it’s also incredibly versatile. You can easily customize it by adding other ingredients such as diced carrots, bell peppers, or even some diced chicken or ham for a heartier version. Feel free to experiment with different spices and herbs to suit your personal taste.

Whether you’re looking for a quick and easy weeknight meal or a dish to impress your guests, Rustic Corn Chowder is a fantastic choice. Its simplicity, heartiness, and comforting flavors make it a soup that will bring warmth and satisfaction to any occasion.

So, next time you’re in the mood for some soul-soothing soup, give this Rustic Corn Chowder a try. You won’t be disappointed!
