Grilled Fruits: Enhancing Desserts with a BBQ Twist


Who says the grill is only for savory dishes? When it comes to desserts, the grill can be your secret weapon for adding a smoky and caramelized twist to your favorite fruits. Grilled fruits not only make for a visually stunning dessert but also offer a deliciously unique flavor profile that will leave your taste buds begging for more. So, fire up the grill and get ready to take your desserts to the next level with these mouthwatering grilled fruit recipes.

Grilled Pineapple with Coconut Whipped Cream

Let’s start with a classic: grilled pineapple. The natural sweetness of pineapple intensifies when it’s grilled, creating a caramelized exterior and a juicy, tender interior. To make this dessert even more indulgent, serve it with a dollop of homemade coconut whipped cream.

Ingredients:

  • 1 ripe pineapple, peeled, cored, and cut into rings
  • 1 can of full-fat coconut milk, refrigerated overnight
  • 1 tablespoon of powdered sugar
  • Fresh mint leaves for garnish (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your grill to medium-high heat.

  2. Place the pineapple rings directly on the grill grates and cook for 3-4 minutes on each side, or until grill marks appear.

  3. While the pineapple is grilling, prepare the coconut whipped cream. Open the can of refrigerated coconut milk and scoop out the thick, creamy layer that has solidified at the top. Discard the watery portion.

  4. In a mixing bowl, whisk the coconut cream and powdered sugar together until light and fluffy.

  5. Once the pineapple rings are grilled to perfection, remove them from the grill and let them cool slightly.

  6. Serve the grilled pineapple with a generous dollop of coconut whipped cream and garnish with fresh mint leaves if desired.

Grilled Peaches with Honey and Mascarpone

Peaches are a summer favorite, and grilling them takes their natural sweetness to a whole new level. The heat of the grill caramelizes the sugars in the peaches, creating a heavenly flavor that pairs perfectly with creamy mascarpone and a drizzle of honey.

Ingredients:

  • 4 ripe peaches, halved and pitted
  • 1 tablespoon of olive oil
  • 1/4 cup of honey
  • 1/2 cup of mascarpone cheese
  • Fresh thyme leaves for garnish (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your grill to medium heat.

  2. Brush the cut side of each peach half with olive oil.

  3. Place the peaches, cut side down, on the grill grates and cook for 3-4 minutes, or until grill marks appear.

  4. Flip the peaches and cook for an additional 2-3 minutes, or until tender.

  5. Remove the grilled peaches from the grill and let them cool slightly.

  6. Serve each peach half with a dollop of mascarpone cheese, a drizzle of honey, and a sprinkle of fresh thyme leaves if desired.

Grilled Watermelon Salad with Feta and Mint

Watermelon is a summer staple, and grilling it brings out its natural sweetness while adding a hint of smokiness. This refreshing grilled watermelon salad combines the smoky watermelon with tangy feta cheese, fresh mint, and a zesty lime dressing. It’s the perfect balance of flavors and textures.

Ingredients:

  • 1 small seedless watermelon, cut into wedges
  • 1 cup of crumbled feta cheese
  • Fresh mint leaves, torn
  • Juice of 1 lime
  • 2 tablespoons of extra-virgin olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your grill to medium-high heat.

  2. Place the watermelon wedges directly on the grill grates and cook for 2-3 minutes on each side, or until grill marks appear.

  3. Remove the grilled watermelon from the grill and let it cool slightly.

  4. Cut the grilled watermelon into bite-sized pieces and transfer them to a serving platter.

  5. Sprinkle the crumbled feta cheese and torn mint leaves over the grilled watermelon.

  6. In a small bowl, whisk together the lime juice, extra-virgin olive oil, salt, and pepper. Drizzle the dressing over the salad.

  7. Toss the salad gently to combine all the flavors, and serve immediately.

Conclusion

Grilling fruits is a fantastic way to elevate your desserts and add a unique twist to classic recipes. Whether you choose to grill pineapple, peaches, watermelon, or any other fruit that catches your fancy, the smoky flavor and caramelized sweetness will transform your desserts into show-stopping creations. So, embrace the BBQ twist and let your grill work its magic on these delicious grilled fruit desserts. Your taste buds will thank you!

Note: Remember to always exercise caution when grilling fruits, as they can become soft quickly. Keep a close eye on them to prevent burning or overcooking.
