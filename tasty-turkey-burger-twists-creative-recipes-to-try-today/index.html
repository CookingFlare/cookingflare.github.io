Tasty Turkey Burger Twists: Creative Recipes to Try Today


Turkey burgers are a fantastic alternative to traditional beef patties, offering a leaner option that doesn’t skimp on flavor. Whether you’re looking to cut down on red meat or simply want to try something new, turkey burgers can be incredibly versatile and delicious. In this blog post, we’ll explore some creative twists on the classic turkey burger recipe that will have your taste buds dancing.

Classic Turkey Burger Recipe

Before we dive into the creative twists, it’s essential to nail the basics. Here’s a simple yet flavorful turkey burger recipe to get you started:

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound ground turkey
  • 1/4 cup bread crumbs
  • 1 egg
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1/2 teaspoon paprika
  • Olive oil for grilling

Instructions:

  1. In a large bowl, combine the ground turkey, bread crumbs, egg, onion, garlic, Worcestershire sauce, salt, pepper, and paprika. Mix until well combined.
  2. Shape the mixture into four equal-sized patties.
  3. Preheat your grill or a skillet over medium heat and lightly coat with olive oil.
  4. Cook the patties for about 5-6 minutes per side, or until the internal temperature reaches 165°F (74°C).
  5. Serve on a bun with your favorite toppings and condiments.

Now that we’ve got the classic recipe down, let’s move on to some exciting variations that will take your turkey burger game to the next level.

Greek-Style Turkey Burgers

Inspired by Mediterranean flavors, these Greek-style turkey burgers are a delightful change of pace. They’re packed with herbs and topped with a tangy tzatziki sauce.

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound ground turkey
  • 1/4 cup crumbled feta cheese
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh spinach
  • 1/4 cup chopped red onion
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon chopped fresh oregano (or 1 teaspoon dried)
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper

Tzatziki Sauce:

  • 1 cup plain Greek yogurt
  • 1/4 cup grated cucumber, excess water squeezed out
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon chopped fresh dill (or 1 teaspoon dried)
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a large bowl, mix together the ground turkey, feta cheese, spinach, red onion, garlic, oregano, salt, and pepper until well combined.
  2. Form the mixture into four patties.
  3. Preheat your grill or skillet over medium heat and lightly coat with olive oil.
  4. Cook the patties for 5-6 minutes per side, or until the internal temperature reaches 165°F (74°C).
  5. To make the tzatziki sauce, combine all the ingredients in a bowl and mix well.
  6. Serve the burgers on whole wheat buns or pita bread with a generous dollop of tzatziki sauce and additional toppings like lettuce, tomato, and cucumber slices.

BBQ Bacon Turkey Burgers

If you’re a fan of smoky barbecue flavors, these BBQ bacon turkey burgers are sure to hit the spot. The combination of crispy bacon, tangy BBQ sauce, and savory turkey patty is irresistible.

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound ground turkey
  • 1/4 cup breadcrumbs
  • 1 egg
  • 1/4 cup finely chopped onion
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon BBQ sauce (plus more for topping)
  • 1 teaspoon smoked paprika
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • 4 slices cooked bacon
  • Cheddar cheese slices
  • Buns

Instructions:

  1. In a large bowl, mix together the ground turkey, breadcrumbs, egg, onion, garlic, BBQ sauce, smoked paprika, salt, and pepper until well combined.
  2. Shape the mixture into four patties.
  3. Preheat your grill or skillet over medium heat and lightly coat with olive oil.
  4. Cook the patties for 5-6 minutes per side, or until the internal temperature reaches 165°F (74°C).
  5. During the last minute of cooking, top each patty with a slice of cheddar cheese and let it melt.
  6. Assemble the burgers on buns with crispy bacon slices and a generous drizzle of BBQ sauce. Add additional toppings like lettuce, tomato, and pickles if desired.

Spicy Southwestern Turkey Burgers

For those who love a bit of heat, these spicy Southwestern turkey burgers are a must-try. Packed with bold flavors and a kick of spice, they’re perfect for spicing up your dinner routine.

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound ground turkey
  • 1/4 cup corn kernels (fresh, frozen, or canned)
  • 1/4 cup black beans, rinsed and drained
  • 1 jalapeño, finely chopped
  • 1/4 cup chopped cilantro
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • Pepper Jack cheese slices
  • Avocado slices
  • Buns

Instructions:

  1. In a large bowl, combine the ground turkey, corn, black beans, jalapeño, cilantro, garlic, cumin, chili powder, salt, and pepper. Mix until well combined.
  2. Form the mixture into four patties.
  3. Preheat your grill or skillet over medium heat and lightly coat with olive oil.
  4. Cook the patties for 5-6 minutes per side, or until the internal temperature reaches 165°F (74°C).
  5. During the last minute of cooking, top each patty with a slice of Pepper Jack cheese and let it melt.
  6. Assemble the burgers on buns with avocado slices and your favorite toppings. Consider adding a dollop of spicy mayo or chipotle sauce for an extra kick.

Asian-Inspired Turkey Burgers

These Asian-inspired turkey burgers are bursting with unique flavors, thanks to the combination of ginger, soy sauce, and sesame oil. Topped with a crunchy slaw, they’re both refreshing and satisfying.

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound ground turkey
  • 1/4 cup breadcrumbs
  • 1 egg
  • 1/4 cup finely chopped green onions
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon grated fresh ginger
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon sesame oil
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper

Asian Slaw:

  • 1 cup shredded cabbage
  • 1/4 cup grated carrot
  • 1/4 cup sliced green onions
  • 2 tablespoons rice vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon sesame oil
  • 1 teaspoon honey
  • 1 teaspoon grated fresh ginger

Instructions:

  1. In a large bowl, mix together the ground turkey, breadcrumbs, egg, green onions, soy sauce, ginger, garlic, sesame oil, salt, and pepper until well combined.
  2. Form the mixture into four patties.
  3. Preheat your grill or skillet over medium heat and lightly coat with olive oil.
  4. Cook the patties for 5-6 minutes per side, or until the internal temperature reaches 165°F (74°C).
  5. To make the Asian slaw, combine all the slaw ingredients in a bowl and toss to mix.
  6. Serve the burgers on buns with a generous portion of the Asian slaw and additional toppings as desired.

Italian Caprese Turkey Burgers

Inspired by the classic Caprese salad, these Italian turkey burgers are fresh and flavorful, featuring mozzarella, tomatoes, and basil.

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound ground turkey
  • 1/4 cup breadcrumbs
  • 1 egg
  • 1/4 cup finely chopped basil leaves
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • Fresh mozzarella slices
  • Tomato slices
  • Balsamic glaze
  • Buns

Instructions:

  1. In a large bowl, combine the ground turkey, breadcrumbs, egg, basil, Parmesan cheese, garlic, salt, and pepper. Mix until well combined.
  2. Shape the mixture into four patties.
  3. Preheat your grill or skillet over medium heat and lightly coat with olive oil.
  4. Cook the patties for 5-6 minutes per side, or until the internal temperature reaches 165°F (74°C).
  5. During the last minute of cooking, top each patty with a slice of fresh mozzarella and let it melt.
  6. Assemble the burgers on buns with tomato slices and a drizzle of balsamic glaze. Add fresh basil leaves for an extra burst of flavor.

Conclusion

Turkey burgers are a versatile and healthy alternative to traditional beef burgers. With these creative recipes, you can enjoy a variety of flavors and textures that will keep your meals exciting. Whether you prefer the Mediterranean flair of Greek-style burgers or the smoky goodness of BBQ bacon, there’s a turkey burger twist for everyone. So fire up the grill or heat up your skillet, and give these tasty turkey burger recipes a try today!
