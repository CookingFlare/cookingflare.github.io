Sheet Pan Garlic Butter Steak and Potatoes: A Quick and Mouthwatering Dish


Are you looking for a delicious and hassle-free meal that will satisfy your cravings? Look no further than this sheet pan garlic butter steak and potatoes recipe! With just a few simple ingredients and minimal preparation time, you can have a mouthwatering dish on your table in no time.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound of sirloin steak, thinly sliced
  • 4 cups of baby potatoes, halved
  • 2 tablespoons of melted butter
  • 4 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon of dried thyme
  • 1 teaspoon of dried rosemary
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley, chopped (for garnish)

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 425°F (220°C) and line a large sheet pan with parchment paper.

  2. In a small bowl, combine the melted butter, minced garlic, dried thyme, dried rosemary, salt, and pepper. Mix well to create a flavorful garlic butter sauce.

  3. Place the sliced sirloin steak and halved baby potatoes on the prepared sheet pan. Drizzle the garlic butter sauce over the steak and potatoes, ensuring that they are evenly coated.

  4. Using your hands or a spatula, toss the steak and potatoes to ensure they are well coated with the garlic butter sauce. Spread them out in a single layer on the sheet pan, making sure they have enough space to cook evenly.

  5. Place the sheet pan in the preheated oven and bake for 20-25 minutes, or until the steak is cooked to your desired level of doneness and the potatoes are tender and golden brown.

  6. Once cooked, remove the sheet pan from the oven and let the steak and potatoes rest for a few minutes. This will allow the flavors to meld together and ensure that the steak remains juicy and tender.

  7. Serve the sheet pan garlic butter steak and potatoes hot, garnished with freshly chopped parsley for a pop of freshness and color.

Tips and Variations

  • For an extra burst of flavor, you can add some sliced bell peppers and onions to the sheet pan along with the steak and potatoes. They will complement the dish perfectly and add some vibrant colors to your meal.

  • If you prefer your steak cooked to medium or well-done, you can adjust the cooking time accordingly. Keep an eye on the steak and potatoes while they are in the oven to ensure they don’t overcook.

  • Feel free to customize the seasoning according to your taste preferences. You can add a pinch of chili flakes for some heat or sprinkle some grated Parmesan cheese over the potatoes for an indulgent twist.

  • This sheet pan garlic butter steak and potatoes recipe is perfect for meal prepping. Simply double or triple the ingredients and divide them into individual portions. Store them in airtight containers and refrigerate for up to 4 days. When you’re ready to eat, simply reheat in the oven or microwave for a quick and satisfying meal.

So there you have it - a quick and mouthwatering dish that will impress your family and friends without the hassle of multiple pots and pans. This sheet pan garlic butter steak and potatoes recipe is perfect for busy weeknights when you want a delicious meal without spending hours in the kitchen. Give it a try and let your taste buds thank you!
