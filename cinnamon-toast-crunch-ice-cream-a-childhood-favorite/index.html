Cinnamon Toast Crunch Ice Cream: A Childhood Favorite


Who doesn’t love the nostalgic flavors of their favorite childhood cereal? From the moment you pour a bowl of Cinnamon Toast Crunch and take that first bite, you’re transported back to carefree mornings and happy memories. Now, imagine capturing that same magic in a creamy, dreamy dessert: Cinnamon Toast Crunch Ice Cream.

The Perfect Blend of Crunch and Cream

This delightful frozen treat combines the irresistible taste of cinnamon sugar with the smoothness of homemade ice cream. With just a handful of ingredients, you can indulge in a scoop of pure nostalgia. So, let’s dive right into the recipe and create our very own batch of Cinnamon Toast Crunch Ice Cream!

Ingredients

  • 2 cups heavy cream
  • 1 cup whole milk
  • 3/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 teaspoon pure vanilla extract
  • 1 1/2 cups Cinnamon Toast Crunch cereal, crushed

Instructions

  1. In a medium saucepan, combine the heavy cream, whole milk, and granulated sugar. Heat the mixture over medium-low heat until it reaches a gentle simmer, stirring occasionally to dissolve the sugar.

  2. Once the mixture is simmering, remove it from the heat and let it cool for a few minutes. Stir in the vanilla extract.

  3. Transfer the mixture to a container with a tight-fitting lid and refrigerate it until it’s completely chilled, at least 4 hours or overnight.

  4. While the ice cream base is chilling, take your Cinnamon Toast Crunch cereal and crush it into small pieces. You can do this by placing the cereal in a zip-top bag and gently rolling over it with a rolling pin. You want a mix of finely crushed and slightly larger pieces for added texture.

  5. Once the ice cream base is chilled, pour it into your ice cream maker and churn according to the manufacturer’s instructions. This usually takes around 20-25 minutes, but make sure to check the instructions for your specific machine.

  6. As the ice cream churns, gradually add in the crushed Cinnamon Toast Crunch cereal. The swirling cinnamon and sugar will infuse into the ice cream, creating pockets of deliciousness.

  7. Once the ice cream has reached a soft-serve consistency, transfer it to a lidded container, layering it with additional crushed cereal for extra crunch. Place the container in the freezer for at least 2 hours to firm up.

  8. When you’re ready to serve, scoop generous portions of the Cinnamon Toast Crunch Ice Cream into bowls or cones. Top it off with a sprinkle of crushed cereal for an extra burst of flavor and texture.

Enjoy a Scoop of Nostalgia

There’s something truly magical about recreating our favorite childhood flavors in new and exciting ways. Cinnamon Toast Crunch Ice Cream takes us back to those cherished moments of Saturday morning cartoons and lazy summer afternoons. With its creamy base and the irresistible crunch of cinnamon-sugar goodness, this frozen treat is sure to bring a smile to your face.

So, why not whip up a batch of Cinnamon Toast Crunch Ice Cream and indulge in a little taste of nostalgia? It’s the perfect dessert to share with loved ones, or to enjoy all by yourself as a sweet reminder of childhood memories.
