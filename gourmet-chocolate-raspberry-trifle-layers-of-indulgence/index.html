Gourmet Chocolate Raspberry Trifle: Layers of Indulgence


Indulge in the delightful combination of rich chocolate, luscious raspberries, and creamy goodness with our Gourmet Chocolate Raspberry Trifle recipe. This decadent dessert is a show-stopper that will leave your guests in awe of your culinary skills. Whether you’re hosting a dinner party, celebrating a special occasion, or simply craving a sweet treat, this trifle is sure to impress and satisfy.

Ingredients

For the Chocolate Cake Layers:

  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 1/2 cups granulated sugar
  • 3/4 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons baking soda
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 2 large eggs
  • 3/4 cup whole milk
  • 1/2 cup vegetable oil
  • 2 teaspoons pure vanilla extract
  • 3/4 cup boiling water

For the Raspberry Filling:

  • 3 cups fresh raspberries
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 2 tablespoons water
  • 1 tablespoon cornstarch

For the Chocolate Ganache:

  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 8 ounces semi-sweet chocolate, chopped
  • 1 tablespoon unsalted butter

For the Whipped Cream:

  • 2 cups heavy cream
  • 1/4 cup powdered sugar
  • 1 teaspoon pure vanilla extract

Additional:

  • Fresh raspberries for garnish
  • Chocolate shavings for garnish

Instructions

Chocolate Cake Layers:

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease and flour two 9-inch round cake pans.
  2. In a large bowl, combine the flour, sugar, cocoa, baking powder, baking soda, and salt.
  3. Add the eggs, milk, oil, and vanilla extract to the dry ingredients and mix until well combined.
  4. Stir in the boiling water, the batter will be thin. Pour the batter evenly into the prepared pans.
  5. Bake for 30 to 35 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean. Allow the cakes to cool in the pans for 10 minutes, then transfer them to a wire rack to cool completely.

Raspberry Filling:

  1. In a saucepan, combine the raspberries, sugar, and water. Cook over medium heat until the raspberries break down and release their juices.
  2. In a small bowl, mix the cornstarch with a few tablespoons of water to create a slurry. Add the slurry to the raspberry mixture and cook until thickened. Remove from heat and let it cool.

Chocolate Ganache:

  1. In a heatproof bowl, place the chopped chocolate and butter.
  2. In a small saucepan, heat the heavy cream until it just begins to boil. Pour the hot cream over the chocolate and let it sit for a minute.
  3. Gently stir the mixture with a spatula until the chocolate is completely melted and the ganache is smooth.

Whipped Cream:

  1. In a large bowl, whip the heavy cream, powdered sugar, and vanilla extract until stiff peaks form.

Assembly

  1. Cut the cooled chocolate cakes into bite-sized cubes.
  2. In a trifle dish or a large glass bowl, layer the chocolate cake cubes, raspberry filling, chocolate ganache, and whipped cream, repeating the layers until the bowl is filled.
  3. Finish by topping the trifle with fresh raspberries and chocolate shavings for a beautiful presentation.

Serving

Refrigerate the trifle for at least 4 hours before serving to allow the flavors to meld together. When ready to serve, scoop generous portions into individual dessert glasses or bowls. This Gourmet Chocolate Raspberry Trifle is a delightful combination of textures and flavors that will leave your guests craving for more.
