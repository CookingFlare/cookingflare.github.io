5 Cheese Pizza Recipes to Indulge In


Pizza is a beloved dish around the world, and when it comes to cheese, the possibilities are endless. Whether you’re a fan of gooey mozzarella, tangy goat cheese, or rich blue cheese, there’s a pizza recipe here for everyone. In this post, we’ll explore five indulgent cheese pizza recipes that will satisfy your cravings and elevate your pizza night to a whole new level.

1. Classic Five Cheese Pizza

Ingredients:

  • Dough:

    • 2 cups all-purpose flour
    • 1 packet (2 ¼ tsp) active dry yeast
    • ¾ cup warm water
    • 1 tsp sugar
    • 1 tsp salt
    • 1 tbsp olive oil

  • Cheese Toppings:

    • ½ cup mozzarella cheese, shredded
    • ½ cup cheddar cheese, shredded
    • ½ cup parmesan cheese, grated
    • ½ cup ricotta cheese
    • ½ cup gorgonzola cheese, crumbled

  • Sauce:

    • 1 cup marinara sauce

  • Garnish:

    • Fresh basil leaves
    • Olive oil for drizzling

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the Dough:

    • In a bowl, combine warm water, sugar, and yeast. Let it sit for about 5 minutes until frothy.
    • In a large mixing bowl, combine flour and salt. Add the yeast mixture and olive oil. Mix until a dough forms.
    • Knead the dough on a floured surface for about 5-7 minutes until smooth. Place it in a greased bowl, cover, and let it rise for 1 hour.

  2. Preheat the Oven:

    • Preheat your oven to 475°F (245°C).

  3. Assemble the Pizza:

    • Roll out the dough on a floured surface to your desired thickness. Transfer it to a pizza stone or baking sheet.
    • Spread marinara sauce evenly over the dough.
    • Sprinkle mozzarella, cheddar, parmesan, ricotta, and gorgonzola cheeses on top.

  4. Bake:

    • Bake in the preheated oven for 12-15 minutes or until the crust is golden and the cheese is bubbly.

  5. Garnish and Serve:

    • Remove from the oven, drizzle with olive oil, and top with fresh basil leaves. Slice and enjoy!

2. White Pizza with Ricotta and Spinach

Ingredients:

  • Dough:

    • 2 cups all-purpose flour
    • 1 packet (2 ¼ tsp) active dry yeast
    • ¾ cup warm water
    • 1 tsp sugar
    • 1 tsp salt
    • 1 tbsp olive oil

  • Cheese Toppings:

    • 1 cup ricotta cheese
    • 1 cup mozzarella cheese, shredded
    • ½ cup parmesan cheese, grated

  • Vegetables:

    • 2 cups fresh spinach, sautéed
    • 2 cloves garlic, minced

  • Garnish:

    • Red pepper flakes
    • Olive oil for drizzling

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the Dough:

    • Follow the same steps as in the Classic Five Cheese Pizza to prepare the dough.

  2. Preheat the Oven:

    • Preheat your oven to 475°F (245°C).

  3. Sauté Spinach:

    • In a skillet, heat a little olive oil over medium heat. Add minced garlic and sauté for 1 minute. Add spinach and cook until wilted. Set aside.

  4. Assemble the Pizza:

    • Roll out the dough and place it on a pizza stone or baking sheet.
    • Spread ricotta cheese evenly over the dough. Top with sautéed spinach and sprinkle mozzarella and parmesan cheeses.

  5. Bake:

    • Bake for 12-15 minutes until the crust is golden and the cheese is melted.

  6. Garnish and Serve:

    • Drizzle with olive oil and sprinkle red pepper flakes before slicing. Enjoy the creamy goodness!

3. Pesto and Goat Cheese Pizza

Ingredients:

  • Dough:

    • 2 cups all-purpose flour
    • 1 packet (2 ¼ tsp) active dry yeast
    • ¾ cup warm water
    • 1 tsp sugar
    • 1 tsp salt
    • 1 tbsp olive oil

  • Cheese Toppings:

    • 1 cup goat cheese, crumbled
    • 1 cup mozzarella cheese, shredded

  • Sauce:

    • ½ cup pesto sauce

  • Vegetables:

    • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
    • ½ cup red onion, thinly sliced

  • Garnish:

    • Fresh arugula
    • Balsamic glaze for drizzling

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the Dough:

    • Follow the same steps as in the Classic Five Cheese Pizza to prepare the dough.

  2. Preheat the Oven:

    • Preheat your oven to 475°F (245°C).

  3. Assemble the Pizza:

    • Roll out the dough and place it on a pizza stone or baking sheet.
    • Spread pesto sauce evenly over the dough. Top with mozzarella and goat cheese, followed by cherry tomatoes and red onion.

  4. Bake:

    • Bake for 12-15 minutes until the crust is golden and the cheese is bubbly.

  5. Garnish and Serve:

    • Top with fresh arugula and drizzle with balsamic glaze before slicing. This pizza is a delightful combination of flavors!

4. BBQ Chicken and Cheese Pizza

Ingredients:

  • Dough:

    • 2 cups all-purpose flour
    • 1 packet (2 ¼ tsp) active dry yeast
    • ¾ cup warm water
    • 1 tsp sugar
    • 1 tsp salt
    • 1 tbsp olive oil

  • Cheese Toppings:

    • 1 cup mozzarella cheese, shredded
    • ½ cup cheddar cheese, shredded

  • Sauce:

    • ½ cup BBQ sauce

  • Toppings:

    • 1 cup cooked chicken, shredded
    • ½ cup red onion, thinly sliced
    • ½ cup cilantro, chopped

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the Dough:

    • Follow the same steps as in the Classic Five Cheese Pizza to prepare the dough.

  2. Preheat the Oven:

    • Preheat your oven to 475°F (245°C).

  3. Assemble the Pizza:

    • Roll out the dough and place it on a pizza stone or baking sheet.
    • Spread BBQ sauce evenly over the dough. Top with shredded chicken, mozzarella, and cheddar cheeses, followed by red onion.

  4. Bake:

    • Bake for 12-15 minutes until the crust is golden and the cheese is melted.

  5. Garnish and Serve:

    • Sprinkle with chopped cilantro before slicing. This pizza is perfect for BBQ lovers!

5. Truffle and Fontina Cheese Pizza

Ingredients:

  • Dough:

    • 2 cups all-purpose flour
    • 1 packet (2 ¼ tsp) active dry yeast
    • ¾ cup warm water
    • 1 tsp sugar
    • 1 tsp salt
    • 1 tbsp olive oil

  • Cheese Toppings:

    • 1 cup fontina cheese, shredded
    • ½ cup mozzarella cheese, shredded

  • Sauce:

    • ½ cup white truffle oil

  • Garnish:

    • Fresh thyme leaves
    • Shaved parmesan cheese

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the Dough:

    • Follow the same steps as in the Classic Five Cheese Pizza to prepare the dough.

  2. Preheat the Oven:

    • Preheat your oven to 475°F (245°C).

  3. Assemble the Pizza:

    • Roll out the dough and place it on a pizza stone or baking sheet.
    • Drizzle white truffle oil over the dough. Top with fontina and mozzarella cheeses.

  4. Bake:

    • Bake for 12-15 minutes until the crust is golden and the cheese is bubbly.

  5. Garnish and Serve:

    • Top with fresh thyme leaves and shaved parmesan cheese before slicing. This pizza is a luxurious treat!

Conclusion

These five cheese pizza recipes are sure to impress your family and friends. Each recipe offers a unique combination of flavors and textures, making them perfect for any occasion. Whether you prefer a classic five cheese pizza or something more adventurous like truffle and fontina, there’s a recipe here to satisfy every cheese lover’s cravings. So gather your ingredients, roll out the dough, and indulge in the cheesy goodness of homemade pizza!
