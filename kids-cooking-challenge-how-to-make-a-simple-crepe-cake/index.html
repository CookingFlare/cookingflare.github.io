Kids' Cooking Challenge: How to Make a Simple Crepe Cake


If you’re looking for a fun and delicious activity to do with your kids, why not try making a simple crepe cake? This delightful dessert is not only visually stunning but also incredibly easy to make. With just a few basic ingredients and some creativity, you and your little ones can whip up a masterpiece that will impress everyone.

Ingredients

For the crepes:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 4 large eggs
  • 2 cups milk
  • 2 tablespoons melted butter
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • Pinch of salt

For the filling:

  • 2 cups heavy cream
  • 1/4 cup powdered sugar
  • Fresh fruits of your choice (strawberries, blueberries, bananas, etc.)

Instructions

  1. In a large mixing bowl, whisk together the flour and eggs until well combined. Slowly pour in the milk while whisking continuously to avoid lumps. Add the melted butter, vanilla extract, and salt, and whisk until the batter is smooth.

  2. Heat a non-stick frying pan over medium heat and lightly grease it with butter or cooking spray. Pour a small ladleful of the crepe batter into the pan, tilting it in a circular motion to spread the batter evenly. Cook for about 1-2 minutes, or until the edges of the crepe start to brown slightly. Flip the crepe and cook for another 1-2 minutes. Repeat this process with the remaining batter until you have a stack of crepes.

  3. In a chilled mixing bowl, whip the heavy cream and powdered sugar together until stiff peaks form. This will be your filling.

  4. To assemble the crepe cake, place one crepe on a serving plate and spread a thin layer of whipped cream on top. Add a layer of sliced fruits on the whipped cream. Repeat this process, alternating between crepes, whipped cream, and fruits until you’ve used up all the crepes.

  5. Finish the cake by spreading a generous layer of whipped cream on top and garnishing it with more fresh fruits. You can get creative here and make patterns or designs with the fruits.

  6. Place the crepe cake in the refrigerator for at least 2 hours to allow it to set. This will make it easier to slice and serve.

  7. Once chilled, carefully slice the crepe cake into wedges and serve. Enjoy the light and airy layers of crepes, the creamy filling, and the burst of freshness from the fruits.

Tips and Tricks

  • Make sure your frying pan is well-heated before pouring in the batter to ensure even cooking.
  • If you don’t have a non-stick pan, you can use a regular frying pan and lightly grease it with butter or cooking spray before each crepe.
  • Experiment with different fruits and fillings to create your own unique flavor combinations. You can also add a drizzle of chocolate or caramel sauce for extra indulgence.
  • If you want to make the crepe cake ahead of time, you can store it in the refrigerator for up to 24 hours before serving. Just make sure to cover it with plastic wrap or a cake dome to prevent it from drying out.

Now that you know how to make a simple crepe cake, it’s time to gather your little kitchen helpers and embark on a fun and delicious cooking adventure. Enjoy the process of creating something beautiful and tasty together, and savor the joy of sharing it with your loved ones. Happy cooking!
