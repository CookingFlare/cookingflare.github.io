Creamy and Garlicy Mashed Cauliflower and Potatoes Recipe for a Lower-Carb Side Dish


Are you looking for a delicious and healthy alternative to traditional mashed potatoes? Look no further! This Creamy and Garlicy Mashed Cauliflower and Potatoes recipe is the perfect lower-carb side dish that will leave you satisfied and guilt-free. With just a few simple ingredients, you can create a creamy and flavorful dish that will impress your family and friends. So let’s get started!

Ingredients

  • 1 head of cauliflower, cut into florets
  • 2 medium-sized potatoes, peeled and cubed
  • 4 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1/4 cup of unsalted butter
  • 1/2 cup of milk (dairy or non-dairy)
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • Fresh chives, for garnish (optional)

Instructions

  1. In a large pot, bring water to a boil and add a generous pinch of salt. Add the cauliflower florets and cubed potatoes to the boiling water. Cook for about 10-12 minutes or until the vegetables are fork-tender.

  2. While the cauliflower and potatoes are cooking, melt the butter in a small saucepan over medium heat. Add the minced garlic and sauté for 2-3 minutes until fragrant and lightly golden. Set aside.

  3. Once the cauliflower and potatoes are cooked, drain them well and transfer them back to the pot. Use a potato masher or a fork to mash the vegetables until smooth and creamy.

  4. Gradually add the melted garlic butter and milk to the mashed cauliflower and potatoes. Stir well to combine and achieve a creamy consistency. Add more milk if needed.

  5. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Remember to taste as you go and adjust the seasoning accordingly.

  6. Transfer the mashed cauliflower and potatoes to a serving bowl. Garnish with fresh chives for an extra burst of flavor (optional).

  7. Serve this creamy and garlicy mashed cauliflower and potatoes as a side dish alongside your favorite protein or enjoy it on its own as a light and satisfying meal.

Why choose Cauliflower and Potatoes?

Cauliflower is a versatile vegetable that is low in calories and packed with nutrients. It is an excellent source of vitamin C, vitamin K, and dietary fiber. By combining cauliflower with potatoes, you can create a lower-carb alternative to traditional mashed potatoes without compromising on flavor or texture.

Health Benefits

This creamy and garlicy mashed cauliflower and potatoes recipe offers numerous health benefits. By replacing some of the starchy potatoes with cauliflower, you can reduce the overall carbohydrate content of the dish. This makes it suitable for those following a low-carb or keto diet.

Additionally, cauliflower is rich in antioxidants, which help protect your body from oxidative stress and promote overall well-being. The garlic adds a boost of flavor and has antimicrobial properties, making it beneficial for your immune system.

Conclusion

With this Creamy and Garlicy Mashed Cauliflower and Potatoes recipe, you can enjoy a tasty and nutritious side dish that is lower in carbs compared to traditional mashed potatoes. The combination of creamy cauliflower and buttery potatoes, infused with the aromatic garlic, creates a flavorful dish that will leave you wanting more. So why not give it a try today and impress your loved ones with this healthy and delicious alternative? Happy cooking!
