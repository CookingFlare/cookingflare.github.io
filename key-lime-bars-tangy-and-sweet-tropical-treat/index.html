Key Lime Bars: Tangy and Sweet Tropical Treat


If you’re a fan of citrus desserts, then these Key Lime Bars are a must-try! With their tangy and sweet flavor, they are the perfect tropical treat for any occasion. Whether you’re hosting a summer gathering or simply craving a refreshing dessert, these bars will surely delight your taste buds. So, let’s get started!

Ingredients

For the crust:

  • 1 ½ cups graham cracker crumbs
  • ⅓ cup melted butter
  • ¼ cup granulated sugar

For the filling:

  • 4 large egg yolks
  • 2 cans (14 ounces each) sweetened condensed milk
  • ½ cup fresh key lime juice
  • Zest of 2 key limes

For the topping (optional):

  • Whipped cream
  • Lime slices

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease a 9x9-inch baking pan and set it aside.

  2. In a medium-sized bowl, combine the graham cracker crumbs, melted butter, and granulated sugar. Mix until the crumbs are evenly coated with the butter and sugar. Press the mixture firmly into the bottom of the prepared baking pan to form the crust.

  3. Bake the crust in the preheated oven for about 10 minutes or until it turns golden brown. Remove it from the oven and let it cool while you prepare the filling.

  4. In a large mixing bowl, whisk together the egg yolks, sweetened condensed milk, key lime juice, and key lime zest until well combined. Pour the mixture over the cooled crust, spreading it evenly.

  5. Place the pan back into the oven and bake for approximately 15 minutes or until the filling is set. You’ll notice a slight jiggle in the center, but it should not be liquid. Once done, remove the pan from the oven and let it cool completely.

  6. Once the bars have cooled, refrigerate them for at least 2 hours or until they are chilled and firm. This step helps enhance the flavors and makes cutting and serving easier.

  7. When you’re ready to serve the Key Lime Bars, you can top them with a dollop of whipped cream for an extra touch of indulgence. You can also garnish with lime slices to add a pop of color and freshness.

  8. Slice the bars into squares, and they are ready to be enjoyed! The tangy and sweet flavors of the key lime filling combined with the buttery graham cracker crust will surely delight your taste buds and transport you to a tropical paradise.

Tips and Variations

  • For an extra tangy flavor, you can add more key lime juice or zest to the filling mixture. Adjust according to your preference.
  • If you can’t find key limes, regular limes can be used as a substitute. However, keep in mind that the flavor may be slightly different.
  • Feel free to experiment with different crust variations. You can try using crushed vanilla wafers or even shortbread cookies for a twist.
  • These Key Lime Bars can be stored in an airtight container in the refrigerator for up to 3-4 days. They make a great make-ahead dessert for parties or gatherings.

So, there you have it! A simple yet delicious recipe for Key Lime Bars. The tangy and sweet flavors combined with the buttery crust make these bars a delightful tropical treat. Give them a try and let your taste buds embark on a refreshing journey. Enjoy!
