Vegan 'Pork' Charms: 15Scrumptious Plant-Based Recipes Mimicking Grilled & Braised Pork


Are you a fan of pork but looking to jump on the vegan bandwagon? Do you crave the flavor and texture of succulent grilled and braised pork but want to go meatless? Look no further! Today, we’re unearthing 15 delectable vegan ‘pork’ charm recipes that promise to deliver the rich flavors and tantalizing textures you crave from this beloved meat.

1. Grilled Jackfruit ‘Pork’ Sandwiches

Embrace the smoky, succulent goodness of grilled pork with these protein-packed, fiber-rich jackfruit sandwiches. Shred a young, green jackfruit and marinate it in a finger-licking blend of smoked paprika, garlic, and liquid smoke. Then, load it onto a toasted bun and enjoy!

2. Braised Tempeh in Mushroom Gravy

Delight in the umami essence of braised pork by giving this braised tempeh in mushroom gravy a try. The flavors of savory tempeh and rich, earthy mushroom gravy come together to create a hearty, mouth-watering meal.

3. Teriyaki Seitan ‘Pork’ Skewers

Enhance your BBQ with these delightful vegan pork skewers made with seitan. The savory seitan, accented with teriyaki sauce, grill marks, and a sprinkle of sesame seeds make these the perfect addition to any cookout.

4. Spicy Korean BBQ ‘Pork’ Tacos

Get ready for some K-pop in your mouth with these spicy Korean BBQ pork tacos. Made with zesty Impossibleâ„¢ ground “beef,” kimchi, and gochujang mayo, these vegan tacos are sure to be a hit at your next fiesta.

5. Vegan ‘Pork’ and Sauerkraut Cabbage Rolls

These cabbage rolls bring back memories of traditional pork and sauerkraut, but with a vegan twist! Packed with flavorful vegan ‘pork’ and tangy sauerkraut, these rolls are the perfect comfort food for chilly nights.

6. Vegan ‘Pork’ Banh Mi Sandwiches

Discover the vibrant flavors of Vietnamese cuisine in these satisfying banh mi sandwiches. Stuffed with marinated vegan ‘pork,’ pickled vegetables, fresh cilantro, and chili peppers, these sandwiches are modern taste adventures.

7. Cashew ‘Ham’ Croissants

Brunch is back and more delicious than ever with these savory cashew ham croissants. With the nutty richness of cashew cheese and the flakiness of vegan croissants, you’ll forget all about traditional pork ham.

8. BBQ Pulled ‘Pork’ Pizza

Who said vegan food can’t party? This BBQ pulled ‘pork’ pizza is the ultimate party pleaser, offering up a medley of flavors from smoky BBQ sauce to tangy vegan coleslaw and ooey-gooey vegan mozzarella.

9. ‘Pork’ Belly Tacos with Pineapple-Chili Salsa

Experience the perfect blend of sweet, savory, and spicy in these ‘pork’ belly tacos. The crispy edges of vegan ‘pork’ belly, accompanied by a refreshing pineapple-chili salsa, make for the ultimate fiesta bite.

10. Vegan ‘Pork’ Chops with Apple-Raisin Stuffing

Indulge in the succulence of pork chops by whipping up these satisfying vegan pork chops. Topped with an apple-raisin stuffing and baked until golden brown, you’ll think you’re dining at a gourmet restaurant.

11. Hearty Vegan ‘Pork’ Cassoulet

Cozy up with this filling vegan ‘pork’ cassoulet. Featuring tender vegan ‘pork’ cuts, meaty beans, and savory sausage, this heartwarming dish is perfect for a chilly day spent in your comfiest pj’s.

12. ‘Pork’ Fried Rice with Cabbage and mushrooms

Your favorite Chinese take-out spot just got a plant-based makeover! Loaded with crispy fried ‘pork,’ stir-fried cabbage, and umami mushrooms, this vegan fried rice tastes just as good, if not better, than the original.

13. Vegan ‘Pork’ Gumbo

Enjoy the slow-cooked comfort of gumbo without the meat by using a Vegan Blend for a hearty gumbo filled with the savory taste of vegan ‘pork.’ Perfected with okra and spices, it gives you the authentic taste of Southern cuisine.

14. Cantonese Vegan ‘Pork’ Dumplings

Take your dumpling game to the next level with these Chinese-inspired vegan pork dumplings. The rich, savory filling, encased in homemade vegan dough, offers a perfect fusion of flavors.

15. Vegan ‘Pork’ and Vegetable Stir-Fry

End on a high note with this veggie-packed vegan pork and vegetable stir-fry. With large, succulent chunks of plant-based pork, broccoli, bell pepper, and snap peas, this dish is not only flavorful but also nutritious.

These dishes cover a variety of flavors and textures that fans of ‘pork’ are sure to enjoy. So, dive into these vegan ‘pork’ charmers and expand your vegan culinary horizons today!
