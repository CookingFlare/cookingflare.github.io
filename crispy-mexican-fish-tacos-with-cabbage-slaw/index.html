Crispy Mexican Fish Tacos with Cabbage Slaw


If you’re a fan of Mexican cuisine, you’re in for a treat with this mouthwatering recipe for Crispy Mexican Fish Tacos with Cabbage Slaw. These delicious tacos are a perfect combination of crispy, flavorful fish and refreshing, tangy slaw, all wrapped up in a warm tortilla. Whether you’re hosting a dinner party or simply craving a flavorful meal, these fish tacos are sure to impress.

Ingredients

For the Fish Tacos:

  • 1 pound of white fish fillets (such as tilapia or cod)
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon onion powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Vegetable oil for frying
  • 8-10 small corn or flour tortillas

For the Cabbage Slaw:

  • 2 cups shredded green cabbage
  • 1/4 cup mayonnaise
  • 2 tablespoons lime juice
  • 1 teaspoon honey
  • 1/2 teaspoon cumin
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh cilantro for garnish

Optional Toppings:

  • Sliced avocado
  • Salsa
  • Hot sauce
  • Lime wedges

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Cabbage Slaw: In a large bowl, combine the shredded cabbage, mayonnaise, lime juice, honey, cumin, salt, and pepper. Toss everything together until the cabbage is well coated. Cover the bowl and refrigerate the slaw while you prepare the fish.

  2. Prep the Fish: Rinse the fish fillets under cold water and pat them dry with paper towels. Cut the fillets into strips that will fit nicely in the tortillas.

  3. Season the Fish: In a shallow dish, combine the flour, chili powder, cumin, garlic powder, onion powder, salt, and pepper. Dredge each fish strip in the seasoned flour, shaking off any excess.

  4. Fry the Fish: In a large skillet, heat about 1/2 inch of vegetable oil over medium-high heat. Once the oil is hot, carefully add the fish strips in a single layer. Fry the fish for 3-4 minutes on each side, or until golden brown and crispy. Once cooked, transfer the fish to a plate lined with paper towels to drain any excess oil.

  5. Warm the Tortillas: In a separate skillet, warm the tortillas for about 30 seconds on each side until they are pliable and heated through.

  6. Assemble the Tacos: To assemble the tacos, place a few pieces of crispy fish in the center of each tortilla. Top the fish with a generous spoonful of the cabbage slaw and any optional toppings of your choice. Garnish with fresh cilantro and serve with lime wedges on the side.

Serving Suggestions

These Crispy Mexican Fish Tacos with Cabbage Slaw are best served fresh, allowing the crispy fish and tangy slaw to shine. Pair them with a side of Mexican rice, black beans, or a refreshing cucumber and tomato salad for a complete meal. They also make a fantastic addition to any taco night spread or outdoor summer gathering.

So, the next time you’re looking to add a little flair to your dinner menu, give these Crispy Mexican Fish Tacos with Cabbage Slaw a try. They’re quick, easy, and packed with vibrant flavors that will transport your taste buds south of the border. Enjoy!
