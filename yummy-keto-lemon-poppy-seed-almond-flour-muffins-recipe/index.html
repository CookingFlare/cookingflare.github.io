Yummy Keto Lemon Poppy Seed Almond Flour Muffins Recipe


If you’re on a ketogenic diet and craving something sweet yet healthy, you’re in for a treat! These Keto Lemon Poppy Seed Almond Flour Muffins are not only low in carbs but also bursting with flavor, making them the perfect guilt-free indulgence. Let’s dive into this delightful recipe that combines the tangy zest of lemons with the nutty crunch of poppy seeds, all while keeping it keto-friendly.

Why Choose Almond Flour?

Almond flour is a fantastic alternative to traditional wheat flour, especially for those following a low-carb or keto lifestyle. It’s rich in healthy fats, protein, and fiber, providing essential nutrients while keeping the carbohydrate count low. Almond flour is also gluten-free, making it suitable for those with gluten sensitivities.

The Benefits of a Keto Diet

Before we get into the recipe, let’s briefly discuss why a keto diet is beneficial. The ketogenic diet emphasizes high healthy fat intake, moderate protein, and very low carbohydrates. This approach helps the body enter a state of ketosis, where it burns fat for fuel instead of glucose. This can lead to weight loss, improved energy levels, and better mental clarity. Incorporating delicious recipes like these muffins can make sticking to your keto plan easier and more enjoyable.

Ingredients You’ll Need

To whip up these yummy Keto Lemon Poppy Seed Almond Flour Muffins, gather the following ingredients:

Dry Ingredients:

  • 2 cups almond flour
  • 1/4 cup erythritol or your preferred keto sweetener
  • 1 tablespoon poppy seeds
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt

Wet Ingredients:

  • 3 large eggs
  • 1/3 cup unsweetened almond milk (or coconut milk)
  • 1/4 cup melted coconut oil (or unsalted butter)
  • Zest of 2 lemons
  • 2 tablespoons fresh lemon juice
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Optional Topping:

  • Extra poppy seeds for sprinkling

Step-by-Step Instructions

Now, let’s get started on making these delightful muffins!

Step 1: Preheat the Oven

Begin by preheating your oven to 350°F (175°C). This ensures that your muffins bake evenly and rise perfectly.

Step 2: Prepare the Muffin Tin

Line a muffin tin with silicone or paper liners. This will prevent the muffins from sticking and make cleanup a breeze.

Step 3: Combine Dry Ingredients

In a large mixing bowl, combine the almond flour, erythritol, poppy seeds, baking powder, baking soda, and salt. Whisk the mixture together until well combined, ensuring there are no clumps.

Step 4: Mix Wet Ingredients

In another bowl, whisk together the eggs, almond milk, melted coconut oil, lemon zest, lemon juice, and vanilla extract. Mix until everything is well incorporated.

Step 5: Combine Wet and Dry Ingredients

Pour the wet ingredients into the dry ingredient bowl. Gently fold the mixture together using a spatula. Be careful not to overmix; a few lumps are okay.

Step 6: Fill the Muffin Tin

Spoon the muffin batter into the prepared muffin tin, filling each cup about 3/4 full. If desired, sprinkle a few extra poppy seeds on top for added texture and visual appeal.

Step 7: Bake the Muffins

Place the muffin tin in the preheated oven and bake for 18-22 minutes, or until the muffins are golden brown and a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.

Step 8: Cool and Enjoy

Once baked, remove the muffins from the oven and let them cool in the tin for about 5 minutes. Then transfer them to a wire rack to cool completely. Enjoy these delicious muffins warm or at room temperature!

Storing Your Muffins

To keep your Keto Lemon Poppy Seed Almond Flour Muffins fresh, store them in an airtight container at room temperature for up to 3 days. For longer storage, you can refrigerate them for up to a week or freeze them for up to three months. Just make sure to wrap them well in plastic wrap or foil before freezing.

Tips for Perfect Muffins

  1. Fresh Ingredients: Use fresh lemons for the best flavor. The zest and juice are key components that give these muffins their delicious tang.
  2. Egg Temperature: For best results, use room temperature eggs. They mix better with the other ingredients, leading to a fluffier muffin.
  3. Don’t Overmix: Mix just until the ingredients are combined. Overmixing can lead to dense muffins.
  4. Check for Doneness: Ovens can vary, so start checking your muffins a few minutes before the recommended baking time.

Serving Suggestions

These muffins are a delightful breakfast treat or a satisfying snack throughout the day. Pair them with a dollop of whipped cream cheese or a smear of almond butter for added richness. They also make a fantastic addition to a brunch spread or can be served alongside a refreshing cup of herbal tea.

  • Calories: Approximately 180
  • Fat: 15g
  • Carbohydrates: 5g

  • Protein: 6g

These muffins fit perfectly into a keto diet, allowing you to indulge without the guilt!

Conclusion

Incorporating these Keto Lemon Poppy Seed Almond Flour Muffins into your diet is an excellent way to satisfy your sweet tooth while adhering to your low-carb lifestyle. With their delightful flavor and simple preparation, they’re bound to become a staple in your kitchen. So go ahead and treat yourself to this delicious recipe, and don’t forget to share it with friends and family who are also looking for keto-friendly options!

Whether you’re a seasoned keto veteran or just beginning your low-carb journey, these muffins are sure to impress. Happy baking!
