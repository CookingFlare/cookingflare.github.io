Mouthwatering Pulled Pork Sandwiches: A Slow Cooker Recipe


There’s nothing quite like sinking your teeth into a mouthwatering pulled pork sandwich. The tender, juicy meat, combined with the perfect balance of smoky and tangy flavors, is a taste sensation that will leave you craving more. And the best part? You can achieve this culinary delight with minimal effort, thanks to this slow cooker recipe.

Ingredients

  • 1 (4-5 pound) boneless pork shoulder roast
  • 1 tablespoon brown sugar
  • 1 tablespoon paprika
  • 1 tablespoon garlic powder
  • 1 tablespoon onion powder
  • 1 tablespoon cumin
  • 1 tablespoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1 cup apple cider vinegar
  • 1 cup barbecue sauce
  • 8 hamburger buns

Instructions

  1. In a small bowl, mix together the brown sugar, paprika, garlic powder, onion powder, cumin, salt, and black pepper. This flavorful rub will infuse the pork with a delicious blend of spices.

  2. Rub the spice mixture all over the pork shoulder, ensuring that it is evenly coated. This step is crucial for developing a rich, robust flavor.

  3. Place the seasoned pork shoulder in the slow cooker and pour the apple cider vinegar over it. The acidity of the vinegar will help tenderize the meat and add a subtle tanginess.

  4. Cover the slow cooker and cook on low heat for 8-10 hours, or until the pork is fork-tender and easily pulls apart. The long, slow cooking process allows the flavors to meld together, creating a truly irresistible dish.

  5. Once the pork is cooked, remove it from the slow cooker and transfer it to a cutting board. Using two forks, shred the meat into bite-sized pieces. The pork should be so tender that it practically falls apart with minimal effort.

  6. Return the shredded pork to the slow cooker and pour in the barbecue sauce. Stir well to coat the meat evenly with the smoky, sweet flavors of the sauce.

  7. Continue to cook on low heat for an additional 30 minutes to allow the flavors to meld together and the sauce to thicken slightly. This step is essential for achieving the perfect consistency and flavor profile.

  8. Toast the hamburger buns to add a delicious crunch to your pulled pork sandwiches. You can do this by placing them under the broiler for a few minutes or toasting them in a skillet with a bit of butter.

  9. Assemble your pulled pork sandwiches by placing a generous amount of the saucy, tender pork onto each bun. Feel free to add your favorite toppings, such as coleslaw, pickles, or sliced onions, for an extra burst of flavor and texture.

  10. Serve your mouthwatering pulled pork sandwiches alongside a side of crispy fries or a refreshing salad. This crowd-pleasing dish is perfect for a casual get-together or a satisfying weeknight dinner.

Conclusion

With this slow cooker recipe for mouthwatering pulled pork sandwiches, you can enjoy the incredible flavors of a barbecue classic with minimal effort. The tender, juicy pork, combined with the smoky and tangy barbecue sauce, creates a taste sensation that will have you coming back for seconds. So fire up your slow cooker and get ready to indulge in the ultimate comfort food experience.
