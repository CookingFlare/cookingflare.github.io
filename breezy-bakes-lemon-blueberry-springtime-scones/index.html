Breezy Bakes: Lemon Blueberry Springtime Scones


Scones are a classic treat that can be enjoyed any time of the year. But when spring comes around, it’s the perfect opportunity to infuse these delightful pastries with the fresh flavors of the season. And that’s exactly what we’re going to do with our Lemon Blueberry Springtime Scones from Breezy Bakes.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 tablespoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, cold and cubed
  • 1/2 cup fresh blueberries
  • Zest of 1 lemon
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 425°F (220°C) and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.

  2. In a large mixing bowl, whisk together the flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt. Add the cold, cubed butter to the bowl and use a pastry cutter or your fingertips to cut it into the dry ingredients until the mixture resembles coarse crumbs.

  3. Gently fold in the fresh blueberries and lemon zest, being careful not to crush the berries.

  4. In a separate bowl, combine the milk, lemon juice, and vanilla extract. Pour this mixture into the dry ingredients and gently stir until a soft dough forms. Be careful not to overmix, as this can result in tough scones.

  5. Turn the dough out onto a lightly floured surface and knead it gently a few times to bring it together. Pat the dough into a circle about 1 inch thick.

  6. Using a sharp knife or a biscuit cutter, cut the dough into triangles or desired shapes. Place the scones onto the prepared baking sheet, leaving some space between each one.

  7. Bake the scones in the preheated oven for 12-15 minutes, or until they are golden brown on top. Remove them from the oven and allow them to cool slightly before serving.

  8. Serve your Lemon Blueberry Springtime Scones warm or at room temperature, with a dollop of clotted cream or your favorite fruit preserves.

Tips and Variations

  • For an extra burst of lemon flavor, drizzle a simple lemon glaze over the cooled scones. To make the glaze, whisk together 1 cup of powdered sugar and the juice of 1 lemon until smooth. Drizzle the glaze over the scones and allow it to set before serving.

  • If you prefer a sweeter scone, you can increase the amount of sugar to 1/2 cup.

  • Feel free to experiment with different fruits in this recipe. Raspberries, blackberries, or even diced strawberries would all work well alongside the lemon zest.

  • These scones are best enjoyed fresh on the day they are baked. However, you can store any leftovers in an airtight container at room temperature for up to 2 days. To reheat, simply place them in a preheated oven at 350°F (180°C) for a few minutes until warmed through.

Conclusion

Breezy Bakes: Lemon Blueberry Springtime Scones are a delightful treat that perfectly captures the essence of spring. With the tangy flavor of fresh lemon and the burst of sweetness from blueberries, these scones are sure to impress. Whether you’re hosting a brunch or simply want to indulge in a homemade pastry, give this recipe a try. You won’t be disappointed!
