Paleo Quinoa Salad with Roasted Vegetables and Lemon Vinaigrette: A Healthy and Filling Recipe


As we all know, eating a balanced diet is essential for maintaining good health and well-being. One of the most important things to consider is the quality of our food, making sure that we’re providing our bodies with the necessary nutrients and energy to function at their best. In recent years, the paleo diet has gained popularity for its focus on whole, unprocessed foods and elimination of grains, dairy, and processed ingredients. Today, we’re going to share a delicious and nutritious paleo quinoa salad recipe that incorporates roasted vegetables and a tangy lemon vinaigrette.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of quinoa, rinsed and drained
  • 2 cups of water or vegetable broth
  • 2 tablespoons of olive oil
  • 1 large sweet potato, peeled and cubed
  • 1 large red bell pepper, seeded and cubed
  • 1 large red onion, peeled and thinly sliced
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon of salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon of black pepper
  • 1/4 teaspoon of paprika
  • 2 tablespoons of freshly squeezed lemon juice
  • 1 tablespoon of apple cider vinegar
  • 1/4 cup of chopped fresh parsley

Instructions

Preheat your oven to 425°F (220°C). Rinse the quinoa and cook according to the package instructions using 2 cups of water or vegetable broth. Set aside to cool.

In a large bowl, toss the sweet potato, red bell pepper, and red onion with 1 tablespoon of olive oil, salt, black pepper, and paprika until they’re evenly coated. Spread the vegetables out in a single layer on a baking sheet and roast in the oven for 30-40 minutes, or until they’re tender and lightly caramelized.

While the vegetables are roasting, prepare the lemon vinaigrette by whisking together the lemon juice, apple cider vinegar, and 1 tablespoon of olive oil in a small bowl.

Once the quinoa has cooled, fluff it with a fork and transfer it to a large serving bowl. Add the roasted vegetables, chopped garlic, and chopped parsley to the bowl with the quinoa. Pour the lemon vinaigrette over the top and toss everything together until the salad is well combined and coated with the dressing.

Tips and Variations

  • To make this recipe more substantial, you can add some protein sources such as grilled chicken, salmon, or tofu.
  • If you prefer a creamier dressing, you can add some Greek yogurt or avocado to the lemon vinaigrette.
  • Feel free to customize the recipe by using different vegetables or adding some nuts or seeds for extra crunch and nutrition.
  • This salad is perfect for meal prep, as it can be made ahead of time and refrigerated for up to 3 days.

Conclusion

This paleo quinoa salad with roasted vegetables and lemon vinaigrette is a delicious and nutritious recipe that’s perfect for a quick and easy lunch or dinner. With its focus on whole, unprocessed foods and elimination of grains, dairy, and processed ingredients, it’s a great option for those following a paleo diet. We hope you enjoy this recipe and that it becomes a staple in your household!
