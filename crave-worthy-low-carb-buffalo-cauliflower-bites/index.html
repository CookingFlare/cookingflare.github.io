Crave-Worthy Low Carb Buffalo Cauliflower Bites


Are you on a low carb diet but still crave the delicious flavors of buffalo wings? Look no further! These Crave-Worthy Low Carb Buffalo Cauliflower Bites are the perfect guilt-free alternative that will satisfy your taste buds without derailing your healthy eating goals. Packed with flavor and easy to make, these cauliflower bites are sure to become a favorite in your household.

Ingredients

  • 1 large head of cauliflower, cut into florets
  • 1/2 cup almond flour
  • 1/2 cup unsweetened almond milk
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon onion powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1/4 cup hot sauce
  • 2 tablespoons unsalted butter, melted
  • Ranch dressing, for serving (optional)
  • Celery sticks, for serving (optional)

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 425°F (220°C) and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.

  2. In a large mixing bowl, whisk together the almond flour, almond milk, garlic powder, onion powder, paprika, salt, and black pepper until well combined. The mixture should be smooth and slightly thick.

  3. Dip each cauliflower floret into the batter, making sure it is fully coated, then place it on the prepared baking sheet. Repeat this process until all the cauliflower florets are coated.

  4. Bake the cauliflower bites in the preheated oven for 20-25 minutes, or until they are golden brown and crispy.

  5. While the cauliflower bites are baking, prepare the buffalo sauce by combining the hot sauce and melted butter in a small bowl. Stir well to combine.

  6. Once the cauliflower bites are done baking, remove them from the oven and transfer them to a large mixing bowl. Pour the buffalo sauce over the cauliflower bites and toss them gently until they are evenly coated.

  7. Return the coated cauliflower bites to the baking sheet and bake for an additional 5 minutes to allow the sauce to soak in and become sticky.

  8. Remove from the oven and let the cauliflower bites cool for a few minutes before serving.

  9. Serve the Crave-Worthy Low Carb Buffalo Cauliflower Bites with ranch dressing and celery sticks for a complete buffalo wing experience.

Tips and Variations

  • For a spicier version, add a pinch of cayenne pepper to the buffalo sauce.
  • If you prefer a milder flavor, reduce the amount of hot sauce in the buffalo sauce mixture.
  • Feel free to experiment with different spices and seasonings to customize the flavor of your cauliflower bites.
  • These bites can be enjoyed as an appetizer, snack, or even as a side dish to accompany your favorite main course.

Final Thoughts

These Crave-Worthy Low Carb Buffalo Cauliflower Bites are the ultimate guilt-free indulgence. They offer all the flavors you love from traditional buffalo wings without the excess carbs. Whether you’re following a low carb diet or simply looking for a healthier alternative, this recipe is a must-try. So grab a head of cauliflower and get ready to enjoy a delicious and satisfying snack that won’t leave you feeling guilty.
