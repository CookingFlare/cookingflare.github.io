Deliciously Tangy Lemon Blueberry Bars


Looking for a delightful and refreshing treat to satisfy your sweet tooth? Look no further! These Deliciously Tangy Lemon Blueberry Bars are the perfect combination of tart lemon and juicy blueberries, all wrapped up in a buttery crust. They are incredibly easy to make and will surely impress your family and friends. So, let’s get baking!

Ingredients

For the crust:

  • 1 ½ cups all-purpose flour
  • ½ cup granulated sugar
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • ¾ cup unsalted butter, cold and cubed

For the filling:

  • 1 ½ cups fresh blueberries
  • ¾ cup granulated sugar
  • 3 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • 3 large eggs
  • Zest and juice of 2 lemons

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C) and line a 9x9-inch baking pan with parchment paper.

  2. In a large mixing bowl, combine the all-purpose flour, granulated sugar, and salt for the crust. Add in the cold cubed butter and use a pastry cutter or your fingers to cut the butter into the dry ingredients until the mixture resembles coarse crumbs.

  3. Press the crust mixture evenly into the bottom of the prepared baking pan. Bake in the preheated oven for about 15-18 minutes, or until the edges turn golden brown. Remove from the oven and set aside.

  4. In another mixing bowl, gently toss the fresh blueberries with the all-purpose flour until they are coated. This will help prevent the blueberries from sinking to the bottom of the bars during baking.

  5. In a separate bowl, whisk together the granulated sugar, eggs, lemon zest, and lemon juice until well combined. Add in the flour-coated blueberries and gently fold them into the mixture.

  6. Pour the filling over the baked crust and spread it out evenly. Return the pan to the oven and bake for an additional 25-30 minutes, or until the filling is set and the edges are golden brown.

  7. Once baked, remove the pan from the oven and allow the bars to cool completely in the pan. This will help them set and make them easier to slice.

  8. Once cooled, carefully lift the bars out of the pan using the parchment paper and transfer them to a cutting board. Cut into squares or bars of your desired size.

  9. Serve these Deliciously Tangy Lemon Blueberry Bars as is, or for an extra touch, dust them with powdered sugar or drizzle them with a simple glaze made from powdered sugar and lemon juice.

  10. Enjoy these tangy and fruity bars with a cup of tea or coffee, or as a refreshing dessert on a hot summer day.

Conclusion

These Deliciously Tangy Lemon Blueberry Bars are a delightful treat that combines the tanginess of lemons with the sweetness of blueberries. The buttery crust adds a lovely texture and complements the fruity filling perfectly. They are simple to make and will surely be a hit with everyone who tries them. So, don’t wait any longer – gather your ingredients and get baking! Your taste buds will thank you.

Remember to let them cool completely before slicing to ensure they hold their shape. And feel free to experiment with different fruits or even add a crumble topping for some extra crunch. The possibilities are endless! Happy baking!
