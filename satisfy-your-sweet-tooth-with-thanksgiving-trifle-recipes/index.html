Satisfy Your Sweet Tooth with Thanksgiving Trifle Recipes


Thanksgiving is just around the corner, and it’s the perfect time to indulge in some decadent desserts. If you have a sweet tooth like me, you’re probably always on the lookout for new and exciting recipes to satisfy your cravings. This year, why not try something different and make a Thanksgiving trifle? Trifles are not only delicious, but they also make a stunning centerpiece for your holiday table. In this blog post, I will share with you three mouthwatering trifle recipes that will leave your guests begging for more.

1. Pumpkin Spice Trifle

Ingredients:

  • 1 package of pumpkin spice cake mix
  • 1 cup of whole milk
  • 1 can of pumpkin puree
  • 1 teaspoon of cinnamon
  • 1/2 teaspoon of nutmeg
  • 1/4 teaspoon of cloves
  • 1/4 teaspoon of ginger
  • 2 cups of whipped cream
  • 1/2 cup of caramel sauce
  • 1/2 cup of chopped pecans

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the pumpkin spice cake mix according to the package instructions.
  2. In a separate bowl, mix together the pumpkin puree, cinnamon, nutmeg, cloves, and ginger until well combined.
  3. Once the cake is baked and cooled, cut it into small cubes.
  4. In a trifle dish, start layering the ingredients. Begin with a layer of pumpkin cake cubes, followed by a layer of the pumpkin puree mixture, then a layer of whipped cream.
  5. Repeat the layers until all the ingredients are used, ending with a layer of whipped cream.
  6. Drizzle caramel sauce over the top and sprinkle with chopped pecans.
  7. Refrigerate for at least 2 hours before serving.

2. Apple Caramel Trifle

Ingredients:

  • 1 package of vanilla pound cake
  • 4 cups of chopped apples
  • 1/2 cup of brown sugar
  • 1 teaspoon of cinnamon
  • 1/4 teaspoon of nutmeg
  • 1/4 cup of apple cider
  • 2 cups of whipped cream
  • 1/2 cup of caramel sauce

Instructions:

  1. Cut the vanilla pound cake into small cubes.
  2. In a saucepan, combine the chopped apples, brown sugar, cinnamon, nutmeg, and apple cider. Cook over medium heat until the apples are tender and the mixture is thickened.
  3. In a trifle dish, start layering the ingredients. Begin with a layer of pound cake cubes, followed by a layer of the apple mixture, then a layer of whipped cream.
  4. Repeat the layers until all the ingredients are used, ending with a layer of whipped cream.
  5. Drizzle caramel sauce over the top.
  6. Refrigerate for at least 2 hours before serving.

3. Chocolate Peppermint Trifle

Ingredients:

  • 1 package of chocolate cake mix
  • 1 package of instant chocolate pudding mix
  • 2 cups of milk
  • 1/2 teaspoon of peppermint extract
  • 2 cups of whipped cream
  • 1/2 cup of crushed candy canes

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the chocolate cake mix according to the package instructions.
  2. In a separate bowl, prepare the instant chocolate pudding by whisking it with milk until it thickens. Add peppermint extract and mix well.
  3. Once the cake is baked and cooled, cut it into small cubes.
  4. In a trifle dish, start layering the ingredients. Begin with a layer of chocolate cake cubes, followed by a layer of chocolate pudding, then a layer of whipped cream.
  5. Repeat the layers until all the ingredients are used, ending with a layer of whipped cream.
  6. Sprinkle crushed candy canes over the top.
  7. Refrigerate for at least 2 hours before serving.

These Thanksgiving trifle recipes are sure to impress your family and friends. They are not only delicious but also a beautiful addition to your holiday table. So, this Thanksgiving, satisfy your sweet tooth with these delightful trifles. Happy cooking!
