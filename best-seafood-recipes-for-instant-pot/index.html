Best Seafood Recipes for Instant Pot


Seafood lovers, rejoice! The Instant Pot is a game-changer for preparing quick, flavorful dishes that celebrate the ocean’s bounty. Whether you’re craving tender shrimp, succulent mussels, flaky fish, or hearty seafood stews, the Instant Pot delivers spectacular results with minimal effort. Dive into this treasure trove of recipes that turn everyday seafood into gourmet meals anyone can master at home.

Why Choose Instant Pot for Seafood?

Seafood cooks quickly and can easily become overdone. The Instant Pot’s precise pressure control ensures perfect texture and moisture retention, locking in flavors and enhancing the freshness inherent in seafood. Plus, the appliance shortens cook times dramatically, making it ideal for busy weeknights.

1. Instant Pot Garlic Butter Shrimp

This aromatic shrimp dish is simplicity at its best. The rich garlic butter sauce pairs beautifully with the natural sweetness of shrimp.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound large shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 4 tablespoons unsalted butter
  • 5 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon smoked paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon cayenne pepper (optional for heat)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 tablespoons fresh lemon juice
  • 2 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley

Instructions

  1. Set your Instant Pot to sauté mode. Melt the butter and add minced garlic, cooking until fragrant (about one minute).
  2. Add shrimp, smoked paprika, cayenne (if using), salt, and pepper. Stir well to coat.
  3. Add the lemon juice, then close the lid and set to manual high pressure for 2 minutes.
  4. Once finished, quick-release the pressure immediately to prevent overcooking.
  5. Open, sprinkle fresh parsley, and serve with crusty bread or over rice.

This shrimp recipe is sensational for an appetizer or a main course, effortlessly combining rich, buttery flavors with a little kick from the spices.

2. Instant Pot Seafood Paella

Inspired by the classic Spanish dish, this paella combines rice, mussels, shrimp, and saffron in one pot for a hearty feast.

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 small onion, diced
  • 1 red bell pepper, sliced
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 ½ cups Arborio or short-grain rice
  • 1 teaspoon smoked paprika
  • 1 teaspoon saffron threads soaked in ¼ cup warm water
  • 3 ½ cups seafood broth
  • 12 mussels, scrubbed and debearded
  • 1/2 pound shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • ½ cup frozen peas
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh lemon wedges for garnish

Instructions

  1. Turn Instant Pot to sauté. Heat olive oil, then cook onion, bell pepper, and garlic until softened.
  2. Stir in rice and smoked paprika, coating the grains well. Pour in saffron water and seafood broth.
  3. Seal the lid and cook on high pressure for 6 minutes.
  4. Quick release pressure, then open and gently stir in the mussels, shrimp, and peas.
  5. Close the lid again, and set to sauté mode for 5 minutes or until mussels open and shrimp is pink.
  6. Season with salt and pepper, garnish with fresh lemon wedges, and serve immediately.

The result is a vibrant, comforting meal that’s perfect for impressing guests or elevating a family dinner.

3. Instant Pot Thai Coconut Fish Curry

This aromatic curry captures the rich flavors of Thailand, transforming mild white fish into a luscious, fragrant dish.

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon coconut oil
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 2 tablespoons Thai red curry paste
  • 1 can (13.5 oz) coconut milk
  • 1 tablespoon fish sauce
  • 1 tablespoon brown sugar
  • 1 pound white fish fillets (cod or tilapia), cut into chunks
  • 1 red bell pepper, sliced
  • Juice of 1 lime
  • Fresh cilantro and basil leaves for garnish
  • Cooked jasmine rice, to serve

Instructions

  1. Activate sauté mode on Instant Pot; melt coconut oil and cook onion until translucent.
  2. Stir in Thai red curry paste, mixing well for 1-2 minutes until fragrant.
  3. Pour in coconut milk, fish sauce, and brown sugar—stir to blend.
  4. Add fish chunks and bell pepper slices, gently stirring to coat.
  5. Seal the lid; set on manual high pressure for 3 minutes.
  6. Once complete, quick-release the pressure.
  7. Squeeze lime juice over the curry and garnish with fresh herbs.
  8. Serve hot alongside jasmine rice.

This curry swirls creamy, spicy, tangy, and sweet notes into a soulful dish that transports your taste buds to Southeast Asia.

4. Instant Pot Mussels in White Wine Sauce

Mussels steam quickly while soaking up an irresistible broth made with garlic, white wine, and herbs.

Ingredients

  • 2 pounds fresh mussels, scrubbed and debearded
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup dry white wine
  • 1/2 cup chicken or seafood broth
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme or 1 tablespoon fresh thyme leaves
  • 2 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley
  • Juice of 1 lemon
  • Crusty bread, for serving

Instructions

  1. Turn Instant Pot on sauté mode. Heat olive oil, then sauté garlic until golden.
  2. Pour in white wine and broth, bringing to a gentle simmer.
  3. Add mustard thyme and stir.
  4. Place mussels into the pot and lock the lid.
  5. Cook on high pressure for 3 minutes.
  6. Perform a quick release and open the lid carefully.
  7. Discard any mussels that did not open.
  8. Stir in lemon juice and fresh parsley.
  9. Ladle mussels and broth into bowls and serve with bread to soak up the savory sauce.

This recipe is elegant yet effortless, making it a perfect starter or a light main course.

5. Instant Pot Seafood Chowder

A comforting bowl of creamy seafood chowder brimming with clams, shrimp, and potatoes, perfect for colder days.

Ingredients

  • 4 slices bacon, diced
  • 1 small onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 3 medium potatoes, peeled and diced
  • 2 cups seafood stock or clam juice
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1/2 pound clams, shelled (canned clams can be substituted)
  • 1/2 pound shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 1/2 cup corn kernels (fresh or frozen)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Chopped chives for garnish

Instructions

  1. On sauté mode, cook bacon until crisp. Remove and set aside, leaving fat in the pot.
  2. Add onion and garlic; sauté until soft.
  3. Stir in potatoes and pour in seafood stock.
  4. Seal and cook on high pressure for 5 minutes.
  5. Quick-release pressure, then open lid and add cream, clams, shrimp, and corn.
  6. Set Instant Pot to sauté again and cook for 3-4 minutes until shrimp are pink and chowder is heated through.
  7. Return bacon to the pot, season with salt and pepper, and stir well.
  8. Serve garnished with chives and extra bacon bits.

This chowder brings soothing warmth with bold seafood flavors, worthy of any cozy meal.

Pro Tips for Perfect Instant Pot Seafood

  • Avoid Overcooking: Seafood cooks rapidly under pressure. Use short cook times and quick pressure release to prevent rubbery textures.
  • Use Fresh Ingredients: Fresh seafood yields the best flavor—pick the freshest fish, shellfish, and crustaceans you can find.
  • Customize Broths and Aromatics: Experiment with ingredients like lemongrass, ginger, or star anise for Asian-inspired seafood dishes.
  • Layer Flavors: Sautéing aromatics first helps develop deep flavors before pressure cooking.
  • Don’t Overfill: Seafood expands and releases juices; avoid filling your Instant Pot beyond halfway to ensure even cooking.

Wrapping Up

The Instant Pot reinvents seafood cooking, making it accessible, fast, and delicious. From vibrant curries and fragrant paellas to rich chowders and delicate shrimp dishes, this kitchen marvel allows the excellence of seafood to shine in every meal you create. Ready to dive in? Grab your favorite seafood, fire up your Instant Pot, and cook your way to delightful ocean flavors with ease!

If you’re adventurous and hungry for more seafood dishes made easy, keep experimenting with different spices, herbs, and combinations. Your Instant Pot can become your best ally in crafting memorable meals packed with nutrition, texture, and taste—one seafood recipe at a time.
