Spicy Nut-Free Sriracha Chicken Recipe


If you’re a fan of spicy food and are looking for a delicious nut-free recipe, then you’re in for a treat! This Spicy Nut-Free Sriracha Chicken recipe is the perfect combination of heat and flavor, and it’s incredibly easy to make. Whether you’re cooking for yourself, your family, or hosting a dinner party, this recipe is sure to impress.

Ingredients

  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons honey
  • 3 tablespoons sriracha sauce
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon grated ginger
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon lime juice
  • Chopped fresh cilantro for garnish

Instructions

  1. In a bowl, whisk together the soy sauce, honey, sriracha sauce, minced garlic, grated ginger, black pepper, and lime juice. This will be the marinade for the chicken.

  2. Place the chicken breasts in a resealable plastic bag or a shallow dish, and pour the marinade over the chicken. Ensure that the chicken is evenly coated in the marinade. Seal the bag or cover the dish, and refrigerate for at least 30 minutes to allow the flavors to infuse.

  3. In a large skillet, heat the olive oil over medium-high heat. Once the oil is hot, add the marinated chicken breasts to the skillet. Cook for 6-7 minutes on each side, or until the chicken is fully cooked through and has a beautiful golden-brown color.

  4. Once the chicken is cooked, transfer it to a serving plate. Drizzle any remaining sauce from the skillet over the chicken, and garnish with chopped fresh cilantro for a burst of freshness and color.

  5. Serve the Spicy Nut-Free Sriracha Chicken with your choice of sides, such as steamed vegetables, rice, or a crisp salad. The spicy, savory flavors of the chicken pair wonderfully with a variety of accompaniments.

This recipe serves 4 and is perfect for a weeknight dinner or a weekend gathering. The combination of sriracha sauce, honey, and a hint of lime juice creates a tantalizing flavor profile that will leave your taste buds tingling.

Health Benefits

In addition to being incredibly flavorful, this Spicy Nut-Free Sriracha Chicken recipe offers several health benefits. Chicken is an excellent source of lean protein, making this dish a great option for those looking to maintain a balanced diet. The combination of garlic, ginger, and lime juice not only enhances the flavor but also contributes to the dish’s nutritional value.

Conclusion

Spice up your culinary repertoire with this Spicy Nut-Free Sriracha Chicken recipe. Whether you’re a spice enthusiast or simply enjoy experimenting with new flavors, this dish is a must-try. With its simple preparation and bold, zesty taste, it’s bound to become a favorite in your home. So, gather your ingredients and get ready to savor the delightful heat and succulent goodness of this delectable chicken dish!

Enjoy your cooking adventure, and may your kitchen be filled with the enticing aroma of Spicy Nut-Free Sriracha Chicken!
