Classic Chicken Enchiladas: A Family Favorite


Are you looking for a delicious and comforting meal that the whole family will love? Look no further than Classic Chicken Enchiladas! This traditional Mexican dish is packed with flavor and is sure to become a family favorite. In this recipe, tender chicken is combined with a savory sauce, wrapped in tortillas, and baked to perfection. Let’s get started!

Ingredients

  • 2 cups cooked chicken, shredded
  • 8 flour tortillas
  • 1 cup shredded cheese (cheddar or Monterey Jack)
  • 1/2 cup diced onion
  • 1/2 cup diced bell pepper
  • 1/2 cup diced tomato
  • 1/4 cup chopped cilantro
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • 1 teaspoon chili powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1 cup enchilada sauce
  • Sour cream and salsa, for serving

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C) and lightly grease a baking dish.
  2. In a skillet, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the onion, bell pepper, and garlic, and sauté until they become tender and fragrant.
  3. Add the shredded chicken, cumin, chili powder, salt, and black pepper to the skillet. Stir well to combine the flavors and cook for an additional 2-3 minutes.
  4. Remove the skillet from the heat and set it aside.
  5. Warm the tortillas in the microwave for about 30 seconds or until they are pliable.
  6. Spoon a generous amount of the chicken mixture onto each tortilla, then sprinkle with a little shredded cheese.
  7. Roll up the tortilla tightly and place it seam-side down in the greased baking dish. Repeat this process for the remaining tortillas.
  8. Pour the enchilada sauce over the rolled tortillas, making sure they are evenly covered.
  9. Sprinkle the remaining shredded cheese on top of the enchiladas.
  10. Cover the baking dish with aluminum foil and bake in the preheated oven for 20-25 minutes or until the cheese is melted and the enchiladas are heated through.
  11. Remove the foil and bake for an additional 5-10 minutes or until the cheese is bubbly and golden.
  12. Once the enchiladas are done, remove them from the oven and let them cool for a few minutes.
  13. Serve the enchiladas with a dollop of sour cream, a spoonful of salsa, and a sprinkle of chopped cilantro.
  14. Enjoy your Classic Chicken Enchiladas!

Tips and Variations

  • You can use either store-bought or homemade enchilada sauce for this recipe. If you prefer a spicier flavor, opt for a hot enchilada sauce.
  • Feel free to add your favorite toppings to the enchiladas, such as sliced avocado, diced jalapeños, or black olives.
  • If you’re short on time, you can use rotisserie chicken instead of cooking the chicken from scratch.
  • For a vegetarian version, substitute the chicken with sautéed vegetables like zucchini, mushrooms, and corn.
  • These enchiladas can be made ahead of time and stored in the refrigerator. Simply reheat them in the oven before serving.

Classic Chicken Enchiladas are a versatile and crowd-pleasing dish that is perfect for family dinners, potlucks, or casual gatherings. The combination of tender chicken, flavorful spices, and melted cheese wrapped in a warm tortilla is simply irresistible. Give this recipe a try and watch as it becomes a new family favorite in your home!
