Grilled Brunch: Start Your Day with BBQ Flair


Who says grilling is just for lunch and dinner? Brunch is the perfect time to bring out the BBQ and add a smoky, charred flavor to your morning feast. Whether you’re hosting a casual weekend get-together or just want to spice up your own morning routine, a grilled brunch is a delightful way to start the day. From savory to sweet, here are some mouthwatering grilled brunch recipes to add a touch of BBQ flair to your mornings.

Grilled Avocado Toast

Start your grilled brunch with a twist on the classic avocado toast. Cut ripe avocados in half, remove the pits, and brush the cut sides with olive oil. Place the avocados cut side down on a preheated grill and cook for 3-4 minutes until grill marks appear. Meanwhile, grill thick slices of whole grain bread until lightly charred. Once the avocados and bread are grilled, mash the avocado onto the grilled bread, sprinkle with sea salt, and finish with a drizzle of balsamic glaze. The creamy, smoky flavors will elevate your brunch game.

BBQ Breakfast Skewers

These skewers are a fun and customizable way to serve breakfast on the grill. Assemble skewers with alternating pieces of cooked breakfast sausage, bell peppers, cherry tomatoes, and cubes of crusty bread. Brush the skewers with a mixture of olive oil, honey, and a touch of balsamic vinegar before grilling them until the ingredients are charred and fragrant. The combination of savory sausage, sweet honey, and smoky grill marks will leave everyone reaching for seconds.

Grilled Banana Pancakes

Take your pancake game to the next level by grilling them. Prepare your favorite pancake batter and pour it onto a preheated grill, cooking until bubbles form on the surface. While the pancakes are cooking, slice ripe bananas in half lengthwise and brush the cut sides with melted butter and a sprinkle of brown sugar. Place the bananas on the grill, cut side down, until caramelized and grill marks appear. Serve the grilled bananas on top of the grilled pancakes, drizzle with maple syrup, and sprinkle with chopped nuts for a sweet and smoky brunch treat.

Smoked Salmon Breakfast Pizza

Who says pizza is just for lunch or dinner? For a gourmet twist on breakfast, grill a breakfast pizza topped with smoked salmon, cream cheese, red onion, capers, and fresh dill. Roll out your favorite pizza dough, brush it with olive oil, and grill until lightly charred. Spread a layer of cream cheese on the grilled crust, then add the smoked salmon, red onion, and capers. Return the pizza to the grill until the crust is crispy and the toppings are heated through. Finish with a generous sprinkle of fresh dill, and you’ve got a brunch dish that’s both elegant and delicious.

Grilled Fruit Parfait

No brunch is complete without something sweet, and grilled fruit parfait is a delightful way to satisfy your sweet tooth. Grill a medley of your favorite fruits such as peaches, pineapple, and strawberries until caramelized and juicy. Layer the grilled fruits with Greek yogurt, granola, and a drizzle of honey in individual serving glasses for a brunch dish that’s as visually stunning as it is delicious. The combination of smoky, sweet, and creamy flavors will make this parfait a brunch favorite.

Conclusion

Grilling isn’t just for meats and veggies – it’s a versatile cooking method that can add depth and flavor to your brunch dishes. Whether you’re a fan of sweet or savory brunch options, these grilled recipes are sure to impress your family and friends. So, fire up the grill, embrace the morning sunshine, and start your day with a BBQ-flavored brunch that’s anything but ordinary. Cheers to sizzling mornings and delightful flavors!
