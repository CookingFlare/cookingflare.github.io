Savory Ham and Cheese Quiche: A Brunch Staple


When it comes to brunch, few dishes are as versatile and satisfying as a savory ham and cheese quiche. This classic French dish has found its way into kitchens around the world, and for good reason. With a buttery crust, creamy egg custard, and the rich flavors of ham and cheese, this quiche is a crowd-pleaser that’s perfect for any time of day. Whether you’re hosting a weekend brunch, planning a picnic, or just looking for a delicious make-ahead meal, this savory ham and cheese quiche is sure to impress.

Ingredients

For the Crust:

  • 1 1/4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, cold and cut into cubes
  • 1/4 cup ice water

For the Filling:

  • 1 cup diced ham
  • 1 cup shredded Gruyère cheese
  • 4 large eggs
  • 1 1/2 cups heavy cream
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1/4 teaspoon nutmeg
  • 1 tablespoon chopped fresh chives

Instructions

Step 1: Prepare the Crust

  1. In a food processor, combine the flour and salt. Add the cold, cubed butter and pulse until the mixture resembles coarse crumbs.
  2. While pulsing, gradually add the ice water until the dough comes together.
  3. Turn the dough out onto a floured surface and shape it into a disk. Wrap in plastic wrap and refrigerate for at least 1 hour.

Step 2: Pre-bake the Crust

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Roll out the chilled dough on a floured surface to fit a 9-inch pie dish. Press the dough into the dish and trim any excess.
  3. Line the crust with parchment paper and fill with pie weights or dried beans.
  4. Bake for 15 minutes, then remove the parchment paper and weights and bake for an additional 10 minutes, or until the crust is golden. Set aside to cool.

Step 3: Prepare the Filling

  1. In a bowl, whisk together the eggs, heavy cream, salt, pepper, and nutmeg until well combined.
  2. Stir in the diced ham, shredded Gruyère cheese, and chopped chives.

Step 4: Assemble and Bake

  1. Pour the filling into the pre-baked crust.
  2. Reduce the oven temperature to 350°F (175°C) and bake the quiche for 35-40 minutes, or until the filling is set and the top is golden brown.
  3. Allow the quiche to cool for 10-15 minutes before slicing and serving.

Serving Suggestions

This savory ham and cheese quiche can be served warm or at room temperature, making it an ideal dish for brunch, lunch, or a light dinner. Pair it with a simple green salad dressed with vinaigrette for a well-balanced meal. The quiche also makes a delightful addition to a picnic spread or a brunch buffet.

Make-Ahead Tips

One of the best things about quiche is that it can be made ahead of time. Prepare the crust and filling up to a day in advance, then assemble and bake the quiche when you’re ready to serve. Leftover quiche can be refrigerated for up to 3 days and gently reheated in the oven.

Conclusion

Savory ham and cheese quiche is a timeless brunch classic that never fails to impress. With its flaky crust, creamy filling, and delicious combination of ham and cheese, it’s a dish that’s as delightful to make as it is to eat. Whether you’re hosting a gathering or simply treating yourself to a special meal, this quiche is sure to become a staple in your culinary repertoire. Enjoy!
