Scrumptious Apple Cider Donuts: A Seasonal Treat


There’s nothing quite like the taste of freshly baked apple cider donuts on a crisp autumn day. The combination of warm spices, tangy apple cider, and a sweet glaze makes these treats irresistible. Whether you’re planning a cozy fall gathering or simply craving a homemade delight, this recipe will guide you through the process of making scrumptious apple cider donuts from scratch.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground nutmeg
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground cloves
  • 2 tablespoons unsalted butter, melted
  • 1/2 cup apple cider
  • 1/4 cup milk
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 large egg

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease a donut pan with non-stick cooking spray or butter.

  2. In a large mixing bowl, whisk together the flour, sugar, baking powder, cinnamon, nutmeg, salt, and cloves. Make sure the ingredients are well combined.

  3. In a separate bowl, combine the melted butter, apple cider, milk, vanilla extract, and egg. Whisk until the mixture is smooth and fully incorporated.

  4. Gradually pour the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients, stirring gently until just combined. Be careful not to overmix, as this can result in dense donuts.

  5. Spoon the batter into the prepared donut pan, filling each cavity about three-quarters full. Smooth the tops with the back of a spoon to ensure even baking.

  6. Place the donut pan in the preheated oven and bake for 12-15 minutes, or until the donuts are lightly golden brown and a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.

  7. Remove the donuts from the oven and allow them to cool in the pan for 5 minutes. Then, transfer them to a wire rack to cool completely.

  8. While the donuts are cooling, prepare the glaze by combining 1 cup of powdered sugar and 2-3 tablespoons of apple cider in a shallow bowl. Whisk until smooth and creamy.

  9. Once the donuts have cooled, dip each one into the glaze, allowing any excess glaze to drip off. Place the glazed donuts back on the wire rack to set.

  10. Serve the scrumptious apple cider donuts immediately and enjoy the delightful combination of flavors and textures. These treats pair perfectly with a hot cup of coffee or a glass of cold milk.

Tips and Variations

  • For an extra burst of flavor, add a teaspoon of grated orange zest to the batter.
  • If you don’t have a donut pan, you can use a muffin tin instead. Simply fill each muffin cavity about half full and adjust the baking time accordingly.
  • Experiment with different glazes to suit your taste. Try a maple glaze, a spiced caramel glaze, or even a simple dusting of powdered sugar.
  • These apple cider donuts are best enjoyed on the day they are made, but you can store any leftovers in an airtight container at room temperature for up to 2 days.

Conclusion

Scrumptious apple cider donuts are a seasonal treat that brings joy to any gathering or a cozy day at home. With their warm spices and tangy apple flavor, these homemade delights are the perfect addition to your fall baking repertoire. Give this recipe a try and experience the irresistible taste of freshly baked apple cider donuts. Happy baking!
