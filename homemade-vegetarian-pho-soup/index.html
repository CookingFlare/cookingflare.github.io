Homemade Vegetarian Pho Soup


Are you craving a comforting bowl of flavorful soup that’s both healthy and satisfying? Look no further than this Homemade Vegetarian Pho Soup recipe! Pho, a traditional Vietnamese dish, is a fragrant and delicious soup that’s typically made with meat-based broths. However, this vegetarian version captures all the authentic flavors of pho while being entirely plant-based.

Ingredients:

  • For the Broth:

    • 2 large onions, peeled and halved
    • 3-inch piece of fresh ginger, sliced
    • 4-5 star anise
    • 6-8 cloves
    • 2 cinnamon sticks
    • 1 teaspoon coriander seeds
    • 4 cups vegetable broth
    • 4 cups water
    • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
    • 1 tablespoon hoisin sauce
    • Salt and pepper to taste

  • For the Soup:

    • 8 oz rice noodles
    • 1 block extra-firm tofu, sliced into cubes
    • 2 cups bean sprouts
    • 1 jalapeño, sliced
    • Fresh herbs like cilantro, Thai basil, and mint
    • Lime wedges
    • Hoisin sauce and sriracha for serving

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the Broth:

    • In a large pot, char the onions and ginger over an open flame or in the oven until slightly blackened.
    • Add the star anise, cloves, cinnamon sticks, and coriander seeds to the pot.
    • Pour in the vegetable broth and water. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat and simmer for about 30 minutes.
    • Strain the broth and return it to the pot. Add soy sauce, hoisin sauce, salt, and pepper. Keep warm.

  2. Prepare the Soup Ingredients:

    • Cook the rice noodles according to the package instructions. Drain and set aside.
    • In a separate pan, pan-fry the tofu cubes until golden and crisp.
    • Arrange the bean sprouts, jalapeño slices, herbs, and lime wedges on a serving platter.

  3. Assemble the Soup:

    • Divide the cooked noodles among serving bowls.
    • Top with tofu cubes and ladle the hot broth over the noodles.
    • Serve immediately with the prepared bean sprouts, herbs, lime wedges, hoisin sauce, and sriracha on the side.

  4. Enjoy Your Homemade Vegetarian Pho Soup!

Tips for a Flavorful Pho:

  • For an extra depth of flavor, you can roast the onions and ginger in the oven before adding them to the broth.
  • Adjust the spices and seasonings in the broth to suit your taste preferences.
  • Feel free to customize your toppings with additional vegetables like bok choy, mushrooms, or broccoli.

Final Thoughts

Homemade Vegetarian Pho Soup is a nourishing and aromatic dish that’s perfect for cozy nights in or casual gatherings with friends and family. This recipe allows you to enjoy all the rich flavors of traditional pho in a meatless version that’s both easy to make and incredibly satisfying. So, next time you’re in the mood for a steaming bowl of comfort, give this Homemade Vegetarian Pho Soup a try. Your taste buds will thank you!

Remember, the key to a good pho is patience and attention to detail. Let the fragrant broth simmer, the flavors meld, and the noodles soak up all the deliciousness. Dive in, slurp up, and savor every spoonful of this homemade delight!
