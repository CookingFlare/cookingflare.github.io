The Classic Manhattan Cupcakes: A Timeless Whiskey Treat


If you’re a fan of the classic Manhattan cocktail and have a sweet tooth, then these Manhattan cupcakes are the perfect treat for you. Infused with the rich flavors of bourbon and vermouth, topped with a luxurious cherry buttercream, these cupcakes are a delightful twist on a timeless drink. Whether you’re baking for a special occasion or just craving a unique dessert, these Manhattan cupcakes are sure to impress.

Ingredients

For the Cupcakes:

  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/2 cup whole milk
  • 1/4 cup bourbon
  • 2 tablespoons sweet vermouth

For the Cherry Buttercream:

  • 1 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 3 cups powdered sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/4 cup maraschino cherry juice
  • Maraschino cherries for garnish

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C) and line a cupcake tin with paper liners.

  2. In a medium bowl, whisk together the flour, baking powder, and salt. Set aside.

  3. In a large mixing bowl, cream together the butter and sugar until light and fluffy. Add the eggs one at a time, beating well after each addition. Stir in the vanilla extract.

  4. In a separate bowl, combine the milk, bourbon, and sweet vermouth.

  5. Gradually add the dry ingredients to the butter mixture, alternating with the milk mixture, beginning and ending with the dry ingredients. Mix until just combined.

  6. Divide the batter evenly among the prepared cupcake liners, filling each about two-thirds full.

  7. Bake for 18-20 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center of a cupcake comes out clean. Allow the cupcakes to cool in the tin for a few minutes before transferring them to a wire rack to cool completely.

  8. While the cupcakes are cooling, prepare the cherry buttercream. In a large mixing bowl, beat the butter until creamy. Gradually add the powdered sugar, beating well after each addition. Stir in the vanilla extract and maraschino cherry juice, and beat until smooth and fluffy.

  9. Once the cupcakes are completely cooled, frost them with the cherry buttercream using a piping bag or offset spatula.

  10. Garnish each cupcake with a maraschino cherry for the perfect finishing touch.

Serving and Enjoying

These Manhattan cupcakes are best enjoyed alongside a glass of the classic cocktail itself. The rich, boozy flavors of bourbon and vermouth combined with the sweet cherry buttercream create a delectable treat that is perfect for any whiskey enthusiast or dessert lover. Whether you’re hosting a sophisticated gathering or simply indulging in a cozy night at home, these cupcakes are a delightful way to savor the essence of the iconic Manhattan cocktail in a whole new form.

So, roll up your sleeves, gather your ingredients, and get ready to bake up a batch of these irresistible Manhattan cupcakes. With their unique blend of flavors and elegant presentation, they are sure to become a staple in your dessert repertoire. Cheers to indulging in this delightful whiskey-infused treat!
