Mouthwatering Keto Chicken Parmesan Casserole: A Comforting Classic


If you’re a fan of comfort food and looking for a delicious keto-friendly recipe, then this Mouthwatering Keto Chicken Parmesan Casserole is just what you need. It’s a comforting classic with a low-carb twist that will leave your taste buds satisfied and your hunger completely satisfied. This recipe is not only easy to make but also packed with flavor, making it a perfect choice for a cozy family dinner or a potluck with friends.

Ingredients

  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1 cup almond flour
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 teaspoons Italian seasoning
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon onion powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 cup marinara sauce
  • 1 ½ cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Fresh basil for garnish

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C) and lightly grease a 9x13-inch baking dish.

  2. In a shallow bowl, mix together the almond flour, grated Parmesan cheese, Italian seasoning, garlic powder, onion powder, salt, and pepper. In another bowl, beat the eggs.

  3. Dip each chicken breast into the beaten eggs, allowing any excess to drip off, then coat them in the almond flour and Parmesan mixture, pressing gently to adhere.

  4. Place the coated chicken breasts in the prepared baking dish and bake for 20-25 minutes or until the chicken is cooked through and the coating is golden brown.

  5. Once the chicken is cooked, remove the baking dish from the oven and spoon marinara sauce over each chicken breast. Sprinkle the shredded mozzarella cheese on top.

  6. Return the baking dish to the oven and bake for an additional 10-15 minutes, or until the cheese is melted and bubbly.

  7. Remove the casserole from the oven and let it rest for a few minutes before serving.

  8. Garnish with fresh basil and serve hot.

Why This Recipe Works

This keto Chicken Parmesan Casserole is a delightful twist on the classic Italian dish. By using almond flour and Parmesan cheese as the coating, this recipe eliminates the need for breadcrumbs, making it low in carbs and keto-friendly. The combination of Italian seasoning, garlic, and onion powders infuses the chicken with savory flavors, while the marinara sauce and melted mozzarella cheese add a rich and comforting touch. This casserole is not only delicious but also a healthier alternative to the traditional chicken parmesan, making it an ideal choice for those following a low-carb or keto lifestyle.

Serving Suggestions

This Mouthwatering Keto Chicken Parmesan Casserole pairs wonderfully with a side of zucchini noodles or a crisp green salad dressed with a light vinaigrette. It’s also great alongside steamed broccoli or roasted asparagus for a complete and satisfying keto-friendly meal. For a heartier option, serve it with a side of cauliflower rice or mashed cauliflower for a comforting twist.

Final Thoughts

In conclusion, this Mouthwatering Keto Chicken Parmesan Casserole is a comforting classic that’s been reimagined to fit a keto lifestyle. Its crispy coating, gooey cheese, and flavorful marinara will make it a hit with both keto and non-keto eaters alike. Whether you’re hosting a dinner party or simply craving a cozy and indulgent meal, this recipe is sure to become a regular in your meal rotation. So, gather your ingredients, preheat your oven, and get ready to savor every delicious bite of this mouthwatering keto chicken parmesan casserole!
