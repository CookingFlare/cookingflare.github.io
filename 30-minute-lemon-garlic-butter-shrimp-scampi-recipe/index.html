30-Minute Lemon Garlic Butter Shrimp Scampi Recipe


Are you craving a delicious seafood dish that’s quick and easy to prepare? Look no further than this mouthwatering 30-Minute Lemon Garlic Butter Shrimp Scampi recipe. Bursting with vibrant flavors and ready in no time, this dish is perfect for a weeknight dinner or a special occasion. Let’s dive into the details and get cooking!

Ingredients

  • 1 pound large shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/4 cup fresh lemon juice
  • 1/2 cup chicken broth
  • 1/4 cup dry white wine
  • 2 tablespoons unsalted butter
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon red pepper flakes
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • 1/4 cup fresh parsley, chopped
  • 8 ounces linguine or your pasta of choice

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Shrimp: Season the shrimp with a pinch of salt and black pepper. Set aside while you prepare the sauce.

  2. Cook the Pasta: Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Cook the pasta according to the package instructions until al dente. Drain and set aside.

  3. Make the Sauce: In a large skillet, heat the olive oil and butter over medium heat. Add the minced garlic and red pepper flakes, sautéing for about a minute until fragrant.

  4. Cook the Shrimp: Add the seasoned shrimp to the skillet and cook for 2-3 minutes on each side until pink and opaque. Remove the shrimp from the skillet and set aside.

  5. Deglaze the Pan: Pour in the white wine and chicken broth, scraping up any browned bits from the bottom of the skillet. Let the mixture simmer for a few minutes until slightly reduced.

  6. Finish the Dish: Stir in the fresh lemon juice and chopped parsley. Return the cooked shrimp to the skillet, tossing everything together to coat the shrimp with the flavorful sauce.

  7. Assemble and Serve: Add the cooked pasta to the skillet, tossing it with the shrimp and sauce until everything is well combined. Season with additional salt and pepper if needed.

Tips for Success

  • For the best flavor, use fresh shrimp and squeeze the lemon juice just before cooking.
  • Adjust the level of red pepper flakes according to your spice preference.
  • Feel free to customize the dish with additional veggies such as cherry tomatoes or spinach.

Enjoy Your Culinary Creation

Now that you’ve mastered this 30-Minute Lemon Garlic Butter Shrimp Scampi recipe, it’s time to savor the fruits of your labor. The succulent shrimp, zesty lemon, and aromatic garlic come together to create a delightful symphony of flavors. Whether you’re cooking for yourself or entertaining guests, this dish is sure to impress. Pair it with a crisp white wine and enjoy a restaurant-quality meal in the comfort of your own home.
