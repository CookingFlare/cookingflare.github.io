Creamy Keto Garlic Parmesan Shrimp Scampi: A Classic Made Low-Carb


If you’re a fan of seafood and looking for a delicious, low-carb meal option, then you’re in for a treat. Our creamy keto garlic parmesan shrimp scampi recipe is a classic dish made with a twist to fit into your keto lifestyle. Bursting with flavors and easy to prepare, this dish is sure to impress both your taste buds and your dinner guests.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound of shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 4 tablespoons of butter
  • 4 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 cup of heavy cream
  • ½ cup of grated parmesan cheese
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley, chopped (for garnish)
  • Zucchini noodles or cauliflower rice (for serving)

Instructions

  1. In a large skillet, melt the butter over medium heat. Add the minced garlic and cook for about 1 minute until fragrant.

  2. Add the shrimp to the skillet and cook until they turn pink and opaque, about 2-3 minutes per side. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Remove the shrimp from the skillet and set aside.

  3. In the same skillet, pour in the heavy cream and bring it to a gentle simmer. Reduce the heat to low and stir in the grated parmesan cheese. Continue stirring until the cheese has melted and the sauce has thickened slightly, about 3-4 minutes.

  4. Return the cooked shrimp to the skillet and toss them in the creamy garlic parmesan sauce, making sure each shrimp is well-coated. Cook for an additional 2-3 minutes until the shrimp are heated through.

  5. Remove the skillet from heat and garnish with freshly chopped parsley. Serve the creamy keto garlic parmesan shrimp scampi over zucchini noodles or cauliflower rice for a complete low-carb meal.

Tips and Variations

  • For an extra kick of flavor, you can add a pinch of red pepper flakes to the garlic butter sauce while cooking the shrimp.

  • If you prefer a thicker sauce, you can stir in a teaspoon of xanthan gum or arrowroot powder after adding the heavy cream. This will help thicken the sauce without adding any additional carbs.

  • Feel free to experiment with different types of seafood, such as scallops or even chunks of white fish, in place of shrimp. The creamy garlic parmesan sauce pairs well with various seafood options.

  • To add some color and freshness to your dish, you can also toss in some sautéed spinach or cherry tomatoes along with the shrimp.

  • If you don’t follow a keto diet, you can serve this creamy shrimp scampi over regular pasta or rice for a more traditional take on the dish.

Final Thoughts

Creamy keto garlic parmesan shrimp scampi is a delightful twist on a classic seafood dish. With its rich and creamy sauce, flavorful garlic, and the added tanginess of parmesan cheese, this recipe is a winner for both keto and non-keto eaters alike. It’s a simple and quick dish to prepare, making it perfect for busy weeknight dinners or special occasions. So why not give it a try and indulge in the deliciousness of this low-carb shrimp scampi? Your taste buds will thank you!
