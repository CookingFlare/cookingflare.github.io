Savory Breakfast Frittata Cup Recipes for a Portable Meal


Are you tired of the same old breakfast routine? Do you find yourself rushing out the door every morning with no time to sit down and enjoy a proper meal? Well, fret not! In this blog post, we’ll be sharing some delicious and easy-to-make savory breakfast frittata cup recipes that are not only portable but also packed with flavor and nutrients. Whether you’re a busy professional or a parent on the go, these frittata cups will revolutionize your breakfast game. So, let’s dive right in!

Recipe 1: Spinach and Feta Frittata Cups

Ingredients:

  • 6 large eggs
  • 1 cup spinach, chopped
  • 1/2 cup feta cheese, crumbled
  • 1/4 cup milk
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • Cooking spray

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. In a medium bowl, whisk together the eggs, milk, salt, and black pepper.
  3. Spray a muffin tin with cooking spray to prevent sticking.
  4. Divide the chopped spinach evenly among the muffin cups.
  5. Sprinkle the crumbled feta cheese over the spinach.
  6. Pour the egg mixture into each muffin cup, filling them about 3/4 full.
  7. Bake for 20-25 minutes, or until the frittata cups are set and slightly golden on top.
  8. Remove from the oven and let them cool for a few minutes before removing them from the muffin tin.
  9. Serve warm or let them cool completely before storing them in an airtight container in the refrigerator.

Recipe 2: Bacon and Cheddar Frittata Cups

  8. Remove from the oven and let them cool for a few minutes before removing them from the muffin tin.
  9. Serve warm or let them cool completely before storing them in an airtight container in the refrigerator.

  • 6 large eggs

  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
