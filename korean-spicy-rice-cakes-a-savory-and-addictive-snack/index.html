Korean Spicy Rice Cakes: A Savory and Addictive Snack


If you’re a fan of Korean cuisine or looking to explore new flavors, then you’re in for a treat! Korean Spicy Rice Cakes, also known as Tteokbokki, are a popular street food snack in South Korea. These chewy and spicy rice cakes are not only incredibly delicious but also easy to make at home. So, get ready to embark on a culinary adventure with this recipe!

Ingredients

To make this mouthwatering dish, you’ll need the following ingredients:

  • 1 pound of Korean rice cakes (Tteok)
  • 2 cups of water
  • 1 tablespoon of vegetable oil
  • 1 onion, thinly sliced
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon of gochujang (Korean chili paste)
  • 1 tablespoon of soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon of sugar
  • 1 teaspoon of sesame oil
  • 1 cup of cabbage, thinly sliced
  • 2 green onions, sliced
  • Sesame seeds for garnish

Instructions

  1. Begin by soaking the rice cakes in cold water for about 20 minutes. This will help soften them and make them easier to cook.

  2. In a large pan or wok, heat the vegetable oil over medium heat. Add the sliced onion and minced garlic, and sauté until they become translucent and fragrant.

  3. Drain the rice cakes and add them to the pan. Stir-fry for a couple of minutes, allowing the rice cakes to absorb the flavors of the onion and garlic.

  4. In a small bowl, combine the gochujang, soy sauce, sugar, and sesame oil. Mix well until all the ingredients are incorporated.

  5. Pour the sauce over the rice cakes in the pan, making sure they are well-coated. Stir-fry for another 2-3 minutes, until the sauce thickens and coats the rice cakes evenly.

  6. Add the sliced cabbage and green onions to the pan, and cook for an additional 2-3 minutes. The cabbage will add a delightful crunch to the dish.

  7. Remove the pan from heat and transfer the spicy rice cakes to a serving dish. Sprinkle sesame seeds on top for a touch of nuttiness and visual appeal.

  8. Korean Spicy Rice Cakes are traditionally served hot and enjoyed as a snack or light meal. They are perfect for sharing with friends or family, as their addictive flavors will leave everyone craving for more!

Tips and Variations

  • If you prefer a milder version, you can reduce the amount of gochujang or add some chicken or vegetable broth to dilute the spice.
  • Feel free to experiment with additional vegetables such as carrots, bell peppers, or mushrooms to enhance the dish’s nutritional value and add extra texture.
  • For a protein-packed option, you can add cooked seafood like shrimp or squid, or even thinly sliced beef or chicken to make it a heartier meal.
  • If you’re not a fan of spicy food, you can omit the gochujang altogether and make a non-spicy version of the dish. Simply substitute the gochujang with a mild chili sauce or tomato paste for a different flavor profile.

In Conclusion

Korean Spicy Rice Cakes, or Tteokbokki, are a delightful and addictive snack that will take your taste buds on an adventure. With their chewy texture and spicy flavors, these rice cakes are a true crowd-pleaser. Whether you’re a fan of Korean cuisine or simply looking to try something new, this recipe is guaranteed to satisfy your cravings. So, gather your ingredients, follow the easy steps, and get ready to enjoy this savory delight in the comfort of your own home. Don’t forget to share the experience with your loved ones, as these Spicy Rice Cakes are meant to be enjoyed together!
