Christmas Cocktails: 10 Festive Drink Recipes to Toast the Season


The holiday season is here, and what better way to celebrate the joy of Christmas than with a festive drink in hand? Whether you’re hosting a cozy family gathering or a lively holiday party, a well-crafted cocktail can set the mood for the festivities. From spicy to sweet, fruity to creamy, the possibilities are endless. In this post, we’ve curated 10 delightful Christmas cocktail recipes that will make your celebrations sparkle. So, let’s raise a glass and toast to the season with these festive concoctions!

1. Classic Eggnog

Eggnog is a quintessential Christmas drink, with its creamy texture and comforting spices. Whether you enjoy it with or without alcohol, this rich beverage is perfect for warming up on a cold winter evening.

Ingredients:

  • 4 large eggs
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 2 cups whole milk
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1/2 cup bourbon or rum (optional)
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground nutmeg
  • A pinch of salt

Instructions:

  1. In a large bowl, beat the eggs and sugar until smooth and pale.
  2. In a saucepan, combine the milk and cream. Heat over medium heat until the mixture is just about to simmer.
  3. Slowly pour the hot milk mixture into the egg mixture while whisking constantly to temper the eggs.
  4. Return the mixture to the saucepan and cook on low heat, stirring constantly, until it thickens slightly and reaches 160°F.
  5. Remove from heat and stir in the vanilla extract, nutmeg, and salt. Allow the eggnog to cool.
  6. For an alcoholic twist, add bourbon or rum and stir well. Serve chilled or over ice with a sprinkle of nutmeg on top.

This creamy drink is perfect for holiday gatherings and a great way to enjoy the rich flavors of Christmas.

2. Cranberry Moscow Mule

A festive twist on the classic Moscow Mule, this drink is tart, refreshing, and vibrant in color. The combination of cranberry and ginger beer brings a touch of sparkle to your holiday celebrations.

Ingredients:

  • 1 1/2 ounces vodka
  • 1/2 ounce lime juice
  • 2 ounces cranberry juice
  • Ginger beer
  • Fresh cranberries and lime slices for garnish
  • Ice

Instructions:

  1. In a cocktail shaker, combine vodka, lime juice, and cranberry juice. Fill the shaker with ice and shake well.
  2. Strain the mixture into a copper mug or highball glass filled with ice.
  3. Top off with ginger beer and stir gently to combine.
  4. Garnish with fresh cranberries and a lime slice.

This cocktail is a perfect balance of tart and sweet, making it an ideal drink for any holiday celebration.

3. Candy Cane Martini

Sweet, minty, and utterly festive, the Candy Cane Martini is a fun and indulgent drink that captures the flavors of Christmas candy in a glass. It’s perfect for those who love a refreshing, minty cocktail.

Ingredients:

  • 1 1/2 ounces vanilla vodka
  • 1 ounce peppermint schnapps
  • 1/2 ounce crème de menthe
  • 1 ounce heavy cream
  • Crushed candy cane for rim garnish
  • Ice

Instructions:

  1. Crush a candy cane and place the crumbs on a small plate.
  2. Moisten the rim of your martini glass with a little bit of water and dip it into the crushed candy cane to coat the rim.
  3. In a shaker, combine vanilla vodka, peppermint schnapps, crème de menthe, and heavy cream with ice. Shake well.
  4. Strain the mixture into the prepared martini glass.
  5. Garnish with a small candy cane on the rim or some extra crushed candy cane on top.

This drink is sweet, creamy, and full of holiday cheer. A wonderful choice for those who love a touch of nostalgia and sweetness.

4. Mulled Wine

A classic winter drink, mulled wine is the perfect way to infuse your home with the scents of Christmas. It’s warm, spiced, and just the right drink for sipping by the fire.

Ingredients:

  • 1 bottle of red wine (preferably full-bodied)
  • 1/2 cup brandy
  • 1/4 cup honey or sugar (to taste)
  • 2 cinnamon sticks
  • 4 whole cloves
  • 2 star anise
  • 1 orange, sliced
  • 1 lemon, sliced

Instructions:

  1. Pour the wine and brandy into a large pot and heat over low to medium heat.
  2. Add the honey or sugar, cinnamon sticks, cloves, star anise, and citrus slices.
  3. Stir well and allow the mixture to simmer for 15-20 minutes, making sure it doesn’t boil.
  4. Taste the mulled wine and adjust the sweetness if needed.
  5. Serve warm, garnished with a cinnamon stick or additional orange slices.

Mulled wine is the perfect drink for cozy holiday gatherings and can be made in advance to keep the party going smoothly.

5. Santa’s Little Helper

A playful and sweet cocktail, Santa’s Little Helper combines the flavors of coffee, chocolate, and hazelnut with a splash of vodka for a drink that will help you relax and enjoy the holidays.

Ingredients:

  • 1 ounce vodka
  • 1 ounce hazelnut liqueur
  • 1 ounce coffee liqueur
  • 1 ounce chocolate liqueur
  • 1/2 ounce heavy cream
  • Chocolate shavings or cocoa powder for garnish
  • Ice

Instructions:

  1. Fill a shaker with ice and add the vodka, hazelnut liqueur, coffee liqueur, and chocolate liqueur.
  2. Shake well until chilled.
  3. Strain the mixture into a martini glass.
  4. Top with a splash of heavy cream and garnish with chocolate shavings or a dusting of cocoa powder.

This dessert-like cocktail will make you feel like a kid on Christmas morning, full of excitement and sweetness.

6. Gingerbread Martini

This gingerbread-inspired martini is everything you love about Christmas cookies in a glass. It’s rich, flavorful, and perfect for those who love the taste of spiced baked goods.

Ingredients:

  • 1 1/2 ounces vanilla vodka
  • 1 ounce ginger liqueur
  • 1 ounce cinnamon schnapps
  • 1/2 ounce heavy cream
  • Crushed gingerbread cookies for garnish
  • Ice

Instructions:

  1. In a shaker, combine the vodka, ginger liqueur, cinnamon schnapps, and heavy cream with ice. Shake well.
  2. Strain the mixture into a martini glass.
  3. Garnish with crushed gingerbread cookies on the rim of the glass.

This drink is perfect for those who love the warmth of ginger and cinnamon during the holidays.

7. Holiday Punch

If you’re throwing a holiday party and need a drink that can serve a crowd, this Holiday Punch is an excellent choice. It’s fruity, flavorful, and can be made in advance so you can focus on enjoying your celebration.

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups cranberry juice
  • 2 cups orange juice
  • 1 cup pineapple juice
  • 1/2 cup lemon juice
  • 1/2 cup rum or vodka (optional)
  • Slices of orange, lemon, and lime for garnish
  • Fresh cranberries and mint for garnish
  • Ice

Instructions:

  1. In a large pitcher or bowl, combine cranberry juice, orange juice, pineapple juice, and lemon juice.
  2. Stir in rum or vodka, if desired.
  3. Add ice and garnish with slices of citrus fruits, fresh cranberries, and mint.
  4. Serve in glasses filled with ice.

This punch is a crowd-pleaser and is perfect for holiday parties of any size.

8. Frosty the Snowman

Frosty the Snowman is a creamy and frothy cocktail that brings the coolness of winter and the warmth of holiday spices together. It’s a delicious dessert drink to enjoy after a big holiday meal.

Ingredients:

  • 1 ounce vanilla vodka
  • 1 ounce white chocolate liqueur
  • 1/2 ounce Irish cream liqueur
  • 1/2 ounce crème de menthe
  • Whipped cream for topping
  • Mini marshmallows for garnish
  • Ice

Instructions:

  1. In a shaker, combine vanilla vodka, white chocolate liqueur, Irish cream liqueur, and crème de menthe with ice.
  2. Shake well and strain into a chilled glass.
  3. Top with a generous dollop of whipped cream and garnish with mini marshmallows.

This indulgent drink is perfect for dessert and will make everyone feel festive.

9. Spiced Apple Cider Cocktail

Apple cider is a classic fall and winter drink, and when paired with spiced rum, it becomes a warm and comforting holiday cocktail. Perfect for a chilly evening by the fire.

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups apple cider
  • 1 1/2 ounces spiced rum
  • 1/2 ounce cinnamon schnapps
  • 1 tablespoon brown sugar
  • Cinnamon sticks and apple slices for garnish
  • Ice

Instructions:

  1. In a saucepan, heat the apple cider and brown sugar until warm, but not boiling.
  2. Pour the cider into a mug or glass and add the spiced rum and cinnamon schnapps.
  3. Stir well and garnish with a cinnamon stick and apple slices.

This cocktail is warm, spicy, and perfect for enjoying while watching holiday movies or caroling.

10. Pomegranate Prosecco Cocktail

This sparkling cocktail is refreshing and light, perfect for ringing in the New Year or as a pre-dinner drink. The tartness of pomegranate balances beautifully with the crispness of Prosecco.

Ingredients:

  • 2 ounces pomegranate juice
  • 4 ounces Prosecco
  • 1/2 ounce simple syrup
  • Pomegranate seeds and mint for garnish
  • Ice

Instructions:

  1. In a glass, combine pomegranate juice and simple syrup.
  2. Add ice and top with Prosecco.
  3. Stir gently to combine and garnish with pomegranate seeds and a sprig of mint.

This cocktail adds a touch of sparkle to any holiday event and is perfect for those who enjoy light and refreshing drinks.

Conclusion

These Christmas cocktails are the perfect way to get into the holiday spirit. Whether you’re in the mood for something creamy, fruity, or spiced, there’s a drink on this list for everyone. The best part? You can easily customize many of these drinks to fit your personal preferences, making your holiday celebrations even more special.

So, gather your ingredients, put on your festive attire, and toast to the season with one (or more!) of these delightful cocktails. Cheers to a merry and bright Christmas!
