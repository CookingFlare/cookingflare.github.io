Decadent Chocolate Yule Log: A Festive Dessert

The holiday season is upon us, and what better way to celebrate than with a show-stopping dessert like the Decadent Chocolate Yule Log. Also known as Bûche de Noël, this traditional French dessert is a festive and indulgent treat that will impress your family and friends. With its rich chocolate flavor and whimsical log-like appearance, the Yule Log is the perfect centerpiece for your holiday table. Ingredients For the Chocolate Sponge: 6 large eggs 150g caster sugar 120g all-purpose flour 30g cocoa powder A pinch of salt For the Chocolate Buttercream: 200g dark chocolate, finely chopped 250g unsalted butter, softened 250g icing sugar 1 tsp vanilla extract 2-3 tbsp heavy cream For the Chocolate Ganache: 150g dark chocolate, finely chopped 150ml heavy cream For Decoration: Icing sugar, for dusting Fresh berries, for garnish Sprigs of rosemary, for a festive touch Instructions Making the Chocolate Sponge: Preheat your oven to 180°C (350°F) and line a 13x9 inch baking tray with parchment paper. In a large bowl, beat the eggs and caster sugar with an electric mixer until pale and thick. Sift in the flour, cocoa powder, and salt. Gently fold the dry ingredients into the egg mixture until just combined. Pour the batter into the prepared baking tray and spread it out evenly. Bake for 10-12 minutes until the sponge is springy to the touch. Preparing the Chocolate Buttercream: Melt the dark chocolate in a heatproof bowl set over a pan of simmering water. Allow it to cool slightly. In a separate bowl, beat the softened butter until creamy. Gradually add the icing sugar and beat until light and fluffy. Stir in the melted chocolate and vanilla extract. Add the heavy cream, a tablespoon at a time, until the buttercream is smooth and spreadable. Assembling the Yule Log: Carefully turn the sponge onto a clean kitchen towel dusted with icing sugar. Peel off the parchment paper and trim the edges of the sponge. Spread a layer of chocolate buttercream evenly over the sponge, leaving a small border around the edges. Starting from one of the shorter ends, roll the sponge and towel together into a log shape. Place it seam side down and refrigerate for 30 minutes to set. Making the Chocolate Ganache: Place the finely chopped dark chocolate in a heatproof bowl. In a small saucepan, heat the heavy cream until it just begins to simmer. Pour the hot cream over the chocolate and let it sit for a minute. Stir until the ganache is smooth and shiny. Final Assembly: Remove the Yule Log from the refrigerator and unwrap it. Using a spatula, spread the chocolate ganache over the log, creating a bark-like texture. Use a fork to create lines resembling tree bark on the ganache. Decorate with fresh berries and sprigs of rosemary for a festive touch. Serving and Storage The Decadent Chocolate Yule Log is best served chilled, allowing the flavors to meld together. Simply slice with a sharp knife and enjoy with a cup of hot cocoa or a glass of mulled wine. Any leftovers can be stored in the refrigerator for up to 3 days, although it’s unlikely there will be any remaining! ...
