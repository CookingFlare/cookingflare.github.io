Heartfelt Homemade Valentine's Day Pasta Recipe


Valentine’s Day is a time to celebrate love and affection, and what better way to do that than by preparing a delicious homemade meal for your loved one? This Heartfelt Homemade Valentine’s Day Pasta Recipe is the perfect dish to show your affection and create a memorable dining experience.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/4 cup fresh basil leaves, chopped
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. In a large mixing bowl, combine the all-purpose flour and salt. Create a well in the center of the mixture.
  2. Crack the eggs into the well and add the olive oil. Use a fork to beat the eggs and incorporate the flour gradually until a dough starts to form.
  3. Transfer the dough onto a lightly floured surface and knead for about 5 minutes until smooth and elastic. If the dough feels too sticky, add a little more flour.
  4. Wrap the dough in plastic wrap and let it rest for 30 minutes at room temperature. This will allow the gluten to relax and make the dough easier to roll out.
  5. After the resting period, divide the dough into four equal parts. Take one portion and cover the remaining dough with a damp cloth to prevent it from drying out.
  6. Roll out the dough with a rolling pin until it reaches your desired thickness. If you have a pasta machine, you can use that instead for a more consistent thickness.
  7. Cut the rolled-out dough into your preferred pasta shape. You can use a knife to cut long, thin strips for tagliatelle or a pasta machine attachment for fettuccine or spaghetti.
  8. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Cook the pasta for about 2-3 minutes or until al dente.
  9. While the pasta is cooking, heat a tablespoon of olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Add the minced garlic and cook until fragrant.
  10. Add the cherry tomatoes to the skillet and cook until they start to soften and release their juices. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  11. Drain the cooked pasta and add it to the skillet with the cherry tomatoes. Toss to coat the pasta evenly with the sauce.
  12. Sprinkle the freshly chopped basil leaves and grated Parmesan cheese over the pasta. Toss again to combine all the flavors.
  13. Remove from heat and transfer the pasta to serving plates. Serve immediately and enjoy!

Conclusion

This Heartfelt Homemade Valentine’s Day Pasta Recipe is a labor of love that will impress your loved one and create a special moment to cherish. The combination of fresh pasta, sweet cherry tomatoes, and aromatic basil will tantalize your taste buds and leave you wanting more. The simplicity of this recipe allows you to focus on the quality of the ingredients and the love you put into preparing the meal. So, this Valentine’s Day, take the time to show your affection with this delicious homemade pasta dish. Your loved one will appreciate the effort, and you’ll create a lasting memory together.
