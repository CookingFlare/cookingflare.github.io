5 Chicken Recipes That are Perfect for Meal Prep


Finding versatile, easy-to-make meals that fit perfectly into a busy schedule is a game-changer. If you’re looking to simplify your weekly routine while maintaining a balance of nutrition and bold flavors, chicken meal prep recipes are truly unmatched. They’re high in protein, adaptable to various diets, and work wonderfully as part of a make-ahead strategy for lunches and dinners. Below, you’ll find five chicken recipes that are perfect for meal prep. From creamy marinades to vibrant spices, these dishes are guaranteed to add excitement to your weekly menu!

1. Lemon Herb Grilled Chicken

Grilled chicken infused with zesty lemon and fresh herbs is a classic that’s unbeatable for meal prep. It’s light, flavorful, and pairs beautifully with just about any side.

Ingredients:

  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 2 lemons (juiced)
  • 3 tablespoons olive oil
  • 3 garlic cloves (minced)
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • ½ teaspoon black pepper

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the marinade: Combine the lemon juice, olive oil, minced garlic, oregano, thyme, salt, and pepper in a bowl.
  2. Marinate the chicken: Place the chicken breasts in a resealable bag and pour the marinade over them. Seal the bag, ensuring the chicken is fully coated, and refrigerate for at least 30 minutes (or up to 4 hours for enhanced flavor).
  3. Grill to perfection: Preheat your grill to medium-high heat. Remove the chicken from the marinade and grill for 6–8 minutes on each side until fully cooked.
  4. Cool and portion: Let the grilled chicken rest for a few minutes. Slice into portions and store in airtight containers for up to 4 days.

Pair this vibrant grilled chicken with roasted vegetables, quinoa, or a green salad to create balanced meal prep containers.

2. Honey Garlic Chicken Thighs

For a dish packed with sweet and savory flavors, honey garlic chicken thighs are your answer. They’re incredibly moist, easy to prepare, and perfect for reheating without losing their quality.

Ingredients:

  • 6 chicken thighs (skinless and boneless)
  • ⅓ cup honey
  • ¼ cup soy sauce
  • 3 garlic cloves (minced)
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • ½ teaspoon ground ginger
  • ½ teaspoon chili flakes (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Whisk the sauce: In a bowl, mix honey, soy sauce, garlic, olive oil, ground ginger, and chili flakes (if using).
  2. Coat and marinate: Add the chicken thighs to the sauce mixture and coat evenly. Marinate in the refrigerator for at least 30 minutes or overnight.
  3. Cook the chicken: Heat a skillet over medium heat and cook the marinated chicken thighs for 6–7 minutes on each side until golden brown and cooked through.
  4. Cool and store: Cool the chicken thighs and store them in single-serving containers.

These honey garlic chicken thighs pair beautifully with steamed broccoli, brown rice, or roasted sweet potatoes.

3. Spicy Chicken Burrito Bowls

Craving meal prep with a Southwestern twist? Spicy chicken burrito bowls are packed with bold flavors, vibrant ingredients, and wholesome nutrition.

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound chicken breast (cubed)
  • 2 tablespoons taco seasoning
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 cup cooked white or brown rice
  • 1 cup black beans (drained and rinsed)
  • 1 cup corn kernels
  • 1 cup diced tomatoes
  • ½ cup shredded cheese
  • Chopped cilantro for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Season and cook the chicken: Toss the chicken cubes with taco seasoning. Heat olive oil in a skillet over medium heat and cook the chicken for about 5–7 minutes until fully cooked.
  2. Assemble the bowls: Divide cooked rice, black beans, corn, diced tomatoes, and chicken evenly among meal prep containers.
  3. Top it off: Sprinkle each bowl with shredded cheese and garnish with fresh cilantro before sealing the containers.

These burrito bowls are easy to customize. You can swap rice for cauliflower rice or add a dollop of plain Greek yogurt for creaminess.

4. Creamy Pesto Chicken Bake

Elevate your meal prep game with creamy pesto chicken bake. This recipe is indulgently flavorful while still being a practical choice for weekly meals.

Ingredients:

  • 4 chicken breasts
  • ½ cup basil pesto
  • ¼ cup cream cheese (softened)
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes (halved)
  • ½ cup shredded mozzarella

Instructions:

  1. Make the creamy sauce: Combine basil pesto and cream cheese in a small bowl.
  2. Prepare the chicken: Slice each chicken breast horizontally to create a pocket for stuffing. Fill each pocket with a spoonful of the pesto mixture.
  3. Bake the chicken: Transfer the stuffed chicken breasts to a baking dish. Top with halved cherry tomatoes and a sprinkle of shredded mozzarella. Bake at 375°F (190°C) for 25–30 minutes until the chicken is cooked through.
  4. Portion and store: Cool the chicken bake before slicing and portioning into containers.

This creamy pesto chicken pairs wonderfully with roasted zucchini, mashed cauliflower, or couscous for a hearty meal.

5. Teriyaki Chicken Stir Fry

Chop, toss, and sauté your way to a colorful and flavorful meal prep with teriyaki chicken stir fry. This dish is packed with veggies and glazed in an irresistible sauce.

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound chicken breast (thinly sliced)
  • 2 cups broccoli florets
  • 1 cup snap peas
  • 1 cup sliced carrots
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • ⅓ cup teriyaki sauce
  • 1 teaspoon sesame seeds (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Cook the chicken: Heat olive oil in a large skillet or wok. Add the sliced chicken and cook until lightly browned and fully cooked.
  2. Add the vegetables: Toss in the broccoli, snap peas, and carrots. Stir fry until the vegetables are tender-crisp.
  3. Glaze with teriyaki: Pour the teriyaki sauce over the chicken and vegetables, stirring to coat evenly. Cook another 2–3 minutes.
  4. Cool and portion: Sprinkle with sesame seeds, if desired. Divide the stir fry into containers.

Serve this teriyaki chicken stir fry alongside jasmine rice or steamed noodles for a balanced meal.

Meal Prep Tips for Success

To maximize efficiency, consider the following pointers:

  1. Double your batches: If you have the space to store it, make extra servings to save even more time during the week.
  2. Invest in quality containers: Airtight and microwave-safe containers are ideal for maintaining freshness and reheating with ease.
  3. Rotate your recipes weekly: Variety will keep your meal prep exciting and ensure you don’t get bored of your routine.
  4. Label and date storage: Add labels to your containers for easy identification and to track freshness.

Chicken serves as the foundation for countless meal prep possibilities. These five recipes ensure your meals are not only delicious but also protein-packed to keep you fueled throughout the week. Whether you prefer sweet and savory, spicy and bold, or creamy and comforting, these pre-made chicken dishes offer convenience and satisfaction—all while helping you stay committed to your health goals!

Adjust spices, sides, and garnishes to cater to your preferences and dietary needs, and watch how easily meal prepping transforms your weekly routine into a flavorful experience.
