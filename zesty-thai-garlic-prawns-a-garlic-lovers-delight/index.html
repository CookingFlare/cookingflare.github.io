Zesty Thai Garlic Prawns: A Garlic Lover's Delight


If you’re a fan of bold flavors and succulent seafood, then Zesty Thai Garlic Prawns are the perfect dish for you. This recipe combines the aromatic essence of garlic with the vibrant flavors of Thai cuisine to create a mouthwatering dish that will tantalize your taste buds. Whether you’re cooking for a special occasion or simply craving a flavorful meal, these Zesty Thai Garlic Prawns are sure to impress.

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound of large prawns, peeled and deveined
  • 6 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon of fresh ginger, grated
  • 2 tablespoons of soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon of fish sauce
  • 1 tablespoon of oyster sauce
  • 1 tablespoon of honey
  • 1 teaspoon of chili flakes
  • 1 tablespoon of vegetable oil
  • Fresh cilantro, chopped, for garnish
  • Cooked rice, for serving

Instructions:

  1. Marinate the Prawns: In a bowl, combine the prawns, minced garlic, grated ginger, soy sauce, fish sauce, and oyster sauce. Toss the prawns until they are well coated with the marinade. Cover the bowl and let the prawns marinate in the refrigerator for at least 30 minutes to allow the flavors to meld together.

  2. Prepare the Sauce: In a small bowl, mix the honey and chili flakes to create a sweet and spicy sauce that will elevate the flavors of the prawns. Set the sauce aside for later use.

  3. Cook the Prawns: Heat the vegetable oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat. Once the oil is hot, add the marinated prawns to the skillet, making sure they are in a single layer. Cook the prawns for 2-3 minutes on each side until they turn pink and opaque.

  4. Add the Garlic: After cooking the prawns, remove them from the skillet and set them aside. In the same skillet, add the minced garlic and sauté it for 1-2 minutes until it becomes fragrant and slightly golden.

  5. Combine the Prawns and Sauce: Return the cooked prawns to the skillet with the sautéed garlic. Pour the prepared honey and chili flakes sauce over the prawns and toss them gently to coat them evenly with the sauce.

  6. Serve and Enjoy: Once the prawns are coated with the zesty garlic sauce, remove the skillet from the heat. Garnish the prawns with freshly chopped cilantro and serve them immediately over a bed of steamed rice.

Why You’ll Love Zesty Thai Garlic Prawns:

The combination of garlic, ginger, and Thai sauces creates a symphony of flavors that will awaken your senses. The savory umami from the soy and fish sauces, combined with the subtle sweetness of honey, balances perfectly with the pungent aroma of garlic and the heat from the chili flakes. The prawns, with their tender texture, serve as the perfect canvas to absorb all these delightful flavors.

Zesty Thai Garlic Prawns are not only bursting with flavor, but they are also quick and easy to prepare, making them an ideal dish for both weeknight dinners and special occasions. The vibrant colors and aromatic fragrance of this dish will entice your guests and leave them craving for more.

So, if you’re a garlic lover looking to elevate your culinary skills and impress your loved ones, Zesty Thai Garlic Prawns are a must-try. With their bold flavors and tantalizing aroma, this dish is guaranteed to become a favorite in your recipe collection. Treat yourself and your loved ones to a truly delightful dining experience with this sensational Zesty Thai Garlic Prawns recipe.
