However, it can also be a hectic and stressful period, especially for those responsible for preparing meals and hosting gatherings. If you find yourself juggling a busy schedule but still want to serve delicious and impressive dishes, these time-saving recipes will be your holiday lifesavers. 1. Instant Pot Cranberry Orange Glazed Ham Ingredients: 1 bone-in ham 1 cup cranberry juice 1/2 cup orange marmalade 1/4 cup brown sugar 1 tablespoon Dijon mustard 1 teaspoon ground cloves Instructions: Place the ham in the Instant Pot. In a bowl, mix the cranberry juice, orange marmalade, brown sugar, Dijon mustard, and ground cloves. Pour the mixture over the ham. Close the Instant Pot and set it to high pressure for 15 minutes. Once done, allow the pressure to release naturally for 10 minutes before quick-releasing the remaining pressure. Transfer the ham to a serving platter, then pour the glaze from the Instant Pot over the ham before serving.
