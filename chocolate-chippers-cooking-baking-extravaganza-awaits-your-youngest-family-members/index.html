Chocolate Chippers: Cooking & Baking Extravaganza Awaits Your Youngest Family Members


Are you tired of the same old dessert routine? Maybe you’ve finally found the perfect recipe to add some excitement to your family’s dessert selection – Chocolate Chippers! A delightful treat that will surely tempt even the pickiest of your young family members, this recipe combines classic chocolate chip cookie dough with the irresistible crunch of chopped pistachios and the burst of dried cherries.

Say goodbye to ordinary cookies, as you embark on a delightful adventure into the world of Chocolate Chippers. These cookies are not only a pleasure to make, but also a fun activity your kids can get involved in, making memories and creating your very own baking extravaganza.

Ingredients

  • 2 1/4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 cup unsalted butter (room temperature)
  • 3/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 3/4 cup brown sugar (packed)
  • 1 tsp pure vanilla extract
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 cups semisweet chocolate chips
  • 1 cup chopped pistachios
  • 1/2 cup dried cherries
  • Extra granulated sugar (for rolling)

Instructions

Step 1: Preheat your oven and prep baking sheets

Begin by preheating your oven to 375°F (190°C). You’ll also need to line two baking sheets with parchment paper or silicone baking mats. This will ensure that your cookies won’t stick to the sheets and will make for easier cleanup.

Step 2: Combine dry ingredients

In a medium-sized bowl, whisk together the flour, baking soda, and salt. Set aside and let it rest while you work on the wet ingredients.

Step 3: Cream together butter and sugar

In a large bowl, cream together the butter, brown sugar, and granulated sugar using an electric or stand mixer. This should take about 2 to 3 minutes until the mixture is smooth and slightly fluffy. Make sure that the butter is at room temperature for the best results.

Step 4: Add the remaining wet ingredients

Next, add the vanilla extract and eggs, one at a time, to the butter-sugar mixture, mixing well after each addition. You want to ensure that each ingredient is thoroughly combined before adding the next.

Step 5: Combine wet and dry ingredients

Now, slowly add the dry ingredient mixture to the wet ingredients, mixing at a low speed until just combined. Don’t overmix – this can lead to dense cookies, and we want these to be light and packed with our delicious add-ins.

Step 6: Fold in chocolate chips, pistachios, and dried cherries

Use a wooden spoon, rubber spatula, or your hands to gently fold in the chocolate chips, chopped pistachios, and dried cherries. This is also the stage where you can get your young family members involved! Having them carefully fold in the add-ins not only helps with the mixing process but also builds their interest and excitement for these tasty cookies.

Step 7: Form the cookies

Once the ingredients are well combined, get your hands dirty (literally) and grab a small handful of dough. Roll it into a ball in the palm of your hand, then roll it once more in a bowl filled with granulated sugar. This adds an extra/surprising crunch to the cookie that your family is sure to love! Repeat this process for each cookie, spacing them about 2 inches apart on the prepared baking sheets.

Step 8: Bake and cool

Place both baking sheets into the preheated oven and bake for 10-12 minutes, or until the edges are slightly golden and the cookies are set. Make sure to switch the sheets halfway through the baking process to ensure even cooking.

Once done, remove the cookies from the oven and allow them to cool on the baking sheets for about 5 minutes before transferring them to a wire rack to cool completely. Remember, patience is a virtue — let your Chocolate Chippers cool to achieve the perfect texture.

Enjoy!

The aroma of freshly baked Chocolate Chippers is sure to drive your family wild with anticipation. As they cool, the flavors within the cookies will mingle and deepen, creating an unparalleled taste experience that is sure to leave your youngest family members smacking their lips.

Take a moment to appreciate the flavors and textures that come together to form this delightful dessert. The rich, indulgent chocolate chips, the delightfully unusual crunch of chopped pistachios, the bursts of tart dried cherries, and the subtle sweet spot in the sugar-coated exterior create an irresistible combination. Hand a fresh, warm Chocolate Chipper to your little ones and watch their hearts melt, just like your cookies!

Making Chocolate Chippers is indeed a culinary journey into a world of creativity, taste, and fun, where young and old alike can indulge their sweet tooth and create memories that’ll last a lifetime. So get ready for the ultimate cooking and baking extravaganza with this delectable dessert!
