Indulgent Gluten-Free Ice Cream Recipes for Dessert Time


When it comes to indulging in delicious desserts, ice cream is a timeless favorite. And for those with gluten sensitivities or celiac disease, enjoying a creamy scoop of ice cream can sometimes feel like a distant dream. But fear not, because we’ve got you covered with a selection of indulgent gluten-free ice cream recipes that will satisfy your sweet tooth without compromising on flavor or texture.

Classic Vanilla Bean Ice Cream

Prep Time: 10 minutes
Freezing Time: 4-6 hours
Serving: 6

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups heavy cream
  • 1 cup whole milk
  • 3/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 vanilla bean, split and scraped
  • 6 large egg yolks

Instructions:

  1. In a saucepan, combine the heavy cream, whole milk, and scraped vanilla bean (including the pod). Heat the mixture over medium heat until it begins to simmer. Remove from heat and let it steep for 30 minutes.
  2. In a separate bowl, whisk together the egg yolks and sugar until pale and thick.
  3. Gradually pour the warm cream mixture into the egg yolk mixture, whisking constantly.
  4. Return the mixture to the saucepan and cook over low heat, stirring constantly, until it thickens and coats the back of a spoon.
  5. Strain the custard through a fine-mesh sieve into a clean bowl and let it cool to room temperature.
  6. Cover the bowl with plastic wrap, ensuring it touches the surface of the custard to prevent a skin from forming. Refrigerate for at least 4 hours or overnight.
  7. Once chilled, pour the custard into an ice cream maker and churn according to the manufacturer’s instructions.
  8. Transfer the churned ice cream into a freezer-safe container, cover, and freeze for 4-6 hours until firm.

Decadent Chocolate Hazelnut Ice Cream

Prep Time: 15 minutes
Freezing Time: 6-8 hours
Serving: 8

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups whole milk
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 3/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/2 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1/2 cup chocolate hazelnut spread
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/2 cup chopped toasted hazelnuts

Instructions:

  1. In a saucepan, whisk together the whole milk, heavy cream, sugar, and cocoa powder over medium heat until the mixture is hot and the sugar has dissolved.
  2. Remove the saucepan from the heat and whisk in the chocolate hazelnut spread until smooth and well combined.
  3. Stir in the vanilla extract and chopped toasted hazelnuts.
  4. Let the mixture cool to room temperature before covering it with plastic wrap and refrigerating for 2-3 hours.
  5. Once chilled, pour the chocolate base into an ice cream maker and churn according to the manufacturer’s instructions.
  6. Transfer the churned ice cream into a freezer-safe container, cover, and freeze for 6-8 hours until set.

Refreshing Berry Coconut Sorbet

Prep Time: 5 minutes
Freezing Time: 4-6 hours
Serving: 4

Ingredients:

  • 3 cups mixed berries (such as strawberries, blueberries, raspberries)
  • 1/2 cup coconut milk
  • 1/3 cup honey or agave nectar
  • 1 tablespoon fresh lime juice
  • Pinch of salt

Instructions:

  1. In a blender, combine the mixed berries, coconut milk, honey, lime juice, and a pinch of salt. Blend until smooth.
  2. Pour the mixture through a fine-mesh sieve into a bowl, pressing on the solids to extract as much liquid as possible. Discard the solids.
  3. Transfer the mixture to an ice cream maker and churn according to the manufacturer’s instructions.
  4. Once churned, transfer the sorbet into a freezer-safe container, cover, and freeze for 4-6 hours until firm.

Conclusion

With these delightful gluten-free ice cream recipes, you can indulge in a variety of flavors without having to worry about gluten. Whether you’re a fan of classic vanilla, rich chocolate, or refreshing fruit sorbets, there’s something for everyone to enjoy. So go ahead, whip up a batch of your favorite flavor and treat yourself to a scoop of creamy, dreamy goodness. After all, dessert time should be a celebration of flavor and joy, and these indulgent gluten-free ice cream recipes are here to make it extra special.
