Sweet and Savory French Toast Recipes for Kids


Are you looking for a delicious and fun breakfast idea that your kids will love? Look no further than these sweet and savory French toast recipes! French toast is a classic breakfast dish that can be made in a variety of ways, and today we’re going to explore some creative and kid-friendly options.

Sweet French Toast

  1. Cinnamon Sugar French Toast

    • Ingredients:
      • 4 slices of bread
      • 2 eggs
      • 1/4 cup milk
      • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
      • 2 tablespoons sugar
      • 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
    • Instructions:
      1. In a shallow dish, whisk together the eggs, milk, and vanilla extract.
      2. In another shallow dish, mix together the sugar and cinnamon.
      3. Dip each slice of bread into the egg mixture, making sure it is completely coated.
      4. Place the bread onto a hot greased skillet or griddle.
      5. Cook for 2-3 minutes on each side, until golden brown.
      6. Remove from the heat and immediately sprinkle with the cinnamon sugar mixture.
      7. Serve warm with maple syrup or fresh fruit.

  2. Berry Stuffed French Toast

    • Ingredients:
      • 4 slices of bread
      • 4 ounces cream cheese, softened
      • 1/2 cup mixed berries (strawberries, blueberries, raspberries)
      • 2 eggs
      • 1/4 cup milk
      • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
    • Instructions:
      1. Spread a thin layer of cream cheese on each slice of bread.
      2. Place a handful of mixed berries on two of the slices.
      3. Top with the remaining slices to make sandwiches.
      4. In a shallow dish, whisk together the eggs, milk, and vanilla extract.
      5. Dip each sandwich into the egg mixture, making sure it is completely coated.
      6. Place the sandwiches onto a hot greased skillet or griddle.
      7. Cook for 2-3 minutes on each side, until golden brown and the cream cheese is melted.
      8. Serve warm with a dusting of powdered sugar and extra berries on top.

Savory French Toast

  1. Ham and Cheese French Toast

    • Ingredients:
      • 4 slices of bread
      • 4 slices of ham
      • 4 slices of cheese (cheddar, Swiss, or your choice)
      • 2 eggs
      • 1/4 cup milk
      • Salt and pepper to taste
    • Instructions:
      1. Layer a slice of ham and a slice of cheese on two slices of bread.
      2. Top with the remaining slices of bread to make sandwiches.
      3. In a shallow dish, whisk together the eggs, milk, salt, and pepper.
      4. Dip each sandwich into the egg mixture, making sure it is completely coated.
      5. Place the sandwiches onto a hot greased skillet or griddle.
      6. Cook for 2-3 minutes on each side, until golden brown and the cheese is melted.
      7. Serve warm with a side of ketchup or your favorite dipping sauce.

  2. Veggie and Cheese French Toast

    • Ingredients:
      • 4 slices of bread
      • 1/2 cup mixed veggies (spinach, bell peppers, onions, mushrooms)
      • 2 eggs
      • 1/4 cup milk
      • Salt and pepper to taste
    • Instructions:
      1. In a small pan, sauté the mixed veggies until they are soft and slightly caramelized.
      2. In a shallow dish, whisk together the eggs, milk, salt, and pepper.
      3. Dip each slice of bread into the egg mixture, making sure it is completely coated.
      4. Place the bread onto a hot greased skillet or griddle.
      5. Cook for 2-3 minutes on each side, until golden brown.
      6. Top each slice with a spoonful of sautéed veggies.
      7. Serve warm with a sprinkle of grated cheese on top.

These sweet and savory French toast recipes are sure to be a hit with your kids. The best part is that you can customize them to suit your family’s tastes and preferences. Whether you choose to go sweet with cinnamon sugar or fruity with mixed berries, or opt for a savory twist with ham and cheese or veggies and cheese, these recipes are versatile and delicious. So, why not surprise your little ones with a special breakfast treat this weekend? They’ll be begging for more!
