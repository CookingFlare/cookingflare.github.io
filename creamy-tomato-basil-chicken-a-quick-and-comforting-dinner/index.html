Creamy Tomato Basil Chicken: A Quick and Comforting Dinner


When it comes to weeknight dinners, we all crave something quick, comforting, and absolutely delicious. That’s where this Creamy Tomato Basil Chicken recipe comes in. It’s a perfect harmony of flavors that will leave you satisfied and wanting more. The combination of juicy chicken, rich tomato sauce, and fragrant basil creates a meal that is sure to become a family favorite. Let’s dive into the recipe and discover how to create this delightful dish in the comfort of your own kitchen.

Ingredients

  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can (14 ounces) diced tomatoes
  • 1 cup chicken broth
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/4 cup fresh basil leaves, chopped
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1/2 teaspoon red pepper flakes (optional)

Instructions

  1. Start by seasoning the chicken breasts with salt and black pepper on both sides. In a large skillet, heat the olive oil over medium-high heat. Add the chicken breasts and cook for about 6-7 minutes on each side, or until they are golden brown and fully cooked. Once done, transfer the chicken to a plate and set it aside.

  2. In the same skillet, add the minced garlic and sauté for about 1 minute, or until it becomes fragrant. Be careful not to let it burn.

  3. Next, pour in the diced tomatoes and chicken broth. Stir well, scraping up any browned bits from the bottom of the skillet. Allow the mixture to come to a gentle simmer.

  4. Once the mixture is simmering, reduce the heat to medium-low and stir in the heavy cream, Parmesan cheese, chopped basil, dried oregano, and red pepper flakes (if using). Let the sauce simmer for about 5 minutes, allowing it to thicken slightly.

  5. Return the cooked chicken breasts to the skillet, nestling them into the creamy tomato basil sauce. Spoon some of the sauce over the chicken and let it simmer for an additional 2-3 minutes to heat the chicken through.

  6. Once everything is heated through and the sauce has reached your desired consistency, remove the skillet from the heat.

  7. Serve the Creamy Tomato Basil Chicken over cooked pasta or with a side of crusty bread to soak up the delicious sauce. Garnish with additional fresh basil and a sprinkle of grated Parmesan cheese, if desired.

Why You’ll Love It

This Creamy Tomato Basil Chicken recipe is designed to be both quick and comforting. The creamy tomato basil sauce is incredibly flavorful and pairs perfectly with the tender, juicy chicken. The addition of garlic, oregano, and red pepper flakes adds a depth of flavor that will tantalize your taste buds. Plus, the ease of preparation makes it an ideal choice for busy weeknights when you want a homemade meal without spending hours in the kitchen.

Not only is this dish a delight to the senses, but it also provides a wholesome and satisfying meal for you and your loved ones. The creamy tomato basil sauce can be customized to suit your taste preferences, whether you prefer it with a hint of spice or a touch of extra Parmesan cheese. It’s a versatile recipe that allows for creativity and personalization, making it a go-to option for any occasion.

So, the next time you’re in need of a quick and comforting dinner, consider whipping up this Creamy Tomato Basil Chicken. It’s a delightful culinary experience that will have everyone at the table asking for seconds. Enjoy the process of creating this mouthwatering dish and savor every bite of its rich and creamy goodness. Happy cooking!
