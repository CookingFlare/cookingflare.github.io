Classic American Chicken Pot Pie Recipe for Cozy Nights


There’s nothing quite like the warmth and comfort of a classic American chicken pot pie on a cozy night. This timeless dish combines tender chicken, hearty vegetables, and a flaky crust to create a satisfying meal that’s perfect for any occasion. Whether you’re looking for a comforting family dinner or a dish to impress your guests, this chicken pot pie recipe is sure to be a hit. So, roll up your sleeves, preheat your oven, and let’s get cooking!

Ingredients

For the Crust:

  • 2 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup unsalted butter, cold and cut into cubes
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup ice water

For the Filling:

  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts, cooked and shredded
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 3 carrots, diced
  • 2 celery stalks, diced
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/3 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 1/2 cups chicken broth
  • 1 cup whole milk
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 cup frozen peas

Instructions

Step 1: Prepare the Crust

  1. In a large bowl, combine the flour and salt. Add the cold, cubed butter and use a pastry cutter or your hands to work the butter into the flour until the mixture resembles coarse crumbs.
  2. Gradually add the ice water, a few tablespoons at a time, and mix until the dough comes together. Be careful not to overwork the dough. Divide the dough in half, shape into two discs, wrap in plastic wrap, and refrigerate for at least 30 minutes.

Step 2: Prepare the Filling

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. In a large skillet, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the onion, carrots, and celery, and cook until the vegetables are tender, about 5-7 minutes. Add the garlic and cook for an additional minute.
  3. Sprinkle the flour over the vegetables and stir to combine. Cook for 1-2 minutes to remove the raw flour taste.
  4. Gradually whisk in the chicken broth and milk, stirring constantly to prevent lumps from forming. Bring the mixture to a simmer and cook until it thickens, about 5-7 minutes.
  5. Add the shredded chicken, dried thyme, salt, and pepper. Stir in the frozen peas and cook for an additional 2-3 minutes. Remove the skillet from the heat and set aside.

Step 3: Assemble and Bake

  1. Roll out one of the chilled dough discs on a floured surface to fit your pie dish. Carefully transfer the dough to the dish and trim any excess overhang.
  2. Pour the chicken filling into the prepared crust.
  3. Roll out the second dough disc and place it over the filling. Trim and crimp the edges to seal the pie. Cut a few slits in the top crust to allow steam to escape.
  4. Place the pie on a baking sheet and bake for 45-50 minutes, or until the crust is golden brown and the filling is bubbling.
  5. Allow the chicken pot pie to cool for a few minutes before serving.

Enjoy Your Cozy Night with Classic American Chicken Pot Pie

There you have it - a classic American chicken pot pie recipe that’s perfect for cozy nights in. The combination of tender chicken, flavorful vegetables, and a flaky crust makes this dish a true comfort food favorite. Whether you’re enjoying it with your family or serving it to guests, this timeless recipe is sure to warm hearts and bellies alike. So, next time you’re craving a comforting meal, give this chicken pot pie a try and savor the cozy goodness of homemade comfort food.
