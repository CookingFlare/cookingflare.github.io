Speedy Sweet and Sour Pork Stir-Fry Recipe


If you’re craving a delicious and satisfying meal that’s quick and easy to prepare, look no further than this Speedy Sweet and Sour Pork Stir-Fry recipe. Packed with flavor and vibrant colors, this dish will not only tantalize your taste buds but also impress your family and friends. Let’s dive into the details of this delightful recipe that will become a go-to favorite in your culinary repertoire.

Ingredients

For the Pork Marinade:

  • 1 pound (450g) pork tenderloin, thinly sliced
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon cornstarch
  • 1 teaspoon sesame oil
  • 1 teaspoon grated ginger
  • 1 clove garlic, minced

For the Stir-Fry Sauce:

  • 1/2 cup (120ml) chicken broth
  • 3 tablespoons rice vinegar
  • 2 tablespoons ketchup
  • 2 tablespoons brown sugar
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon cornstarch

For the Stir-Fry:

  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1 red bell pepper, chopped
  • 1 green bell pepper, chopped
  • 1 small onion, chopped
  • 1 cup (150g) pineapple chunks
  • Cooked white rice, for serving

Instructions

  1. In a bowl, combine the sliced pork with the soy sauce, cornstarch, sesame oil, grated ginger, and minced garlic. Toss to coat the pork evenly, then set it aside to marinate while you prepare the rest of the ingredients.

  2. In another bowl, whisk together the chicken broth, rice vinegar, ketchup, brown sugar, soy sauce, and cornstarch to create the stir-fry sauce. Set the sauce aside.

  3. Heat 1 tablespoon of vegetable oil in a large skillet or wok over high heat. Once the oil is hot, add the marinated pork and stir-fry for 2-3 minutes until it’s no longer pink. Remove the pork from the skillet and set it aside.

  4. In the same skillet, heat the remaining tablespoon of vegetable oil. Add the chopped red and green bell peppers, along with the chopped onion, and stir-fry for 2-3 minutes until they begin to soften.

  5. Return the cooked pork to the skillet, then pour the stir-fry sauce over the pork and vegetables. Add the pineapple chunks and stir everything together. Allow the sauce to thicken and the pineapple to heat through, which should take about 2-3 minutes.

  6. Once the sauce has thickened to your desired consistency and everything is heated through, remove the skillet from the heat.

  7. Serve the Speedy Sweet and Sour Pork Stir-Fry over cooked white rice and garnish with sliced green onions or sesame seeds for an extra pop of flavor and visual appeal.

Tips for Success

  • To save time, you can prepare the pork marinade and stir-fry sauce in advance and store them in the refrigerator until you’re ready to cook.
  • Feel free to customize the vegetables in this stir-fry based on your preferences. Broccoli, snap peas, or carrots would also work beautifully in this dish.
  • For a touch of heat, add a sprinkle of crushed red pepper flakes or a drizzle of sriracha to the stir-fry sauce.

Final Thoughts

The Speedy Sweet and Sour Pork Stir-Fry is a perfect example of how a few simple ingredients can come together to create a dish that’s bursting with flavor and texture. The tender pork, crisp vegetables, and sweet-sour sauce combine to deliver a symphony of tastes in every bite. Whether you’re cooking for a busy weeknight dinner or entertaining guests, this recipe is sure to be a hit.

So, next time you’re in the mood for a meal that’s both speedy and satisfying, give this Sweet and Sour Pork Stir-Fry a try. Your taste buds will thank you!
