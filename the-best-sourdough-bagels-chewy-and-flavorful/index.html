The Best Sourdough Bagels: Chewy and Flavorful


Who doesn’t love a good bagel? That chewy texture, the crispy crust, and the ability to be topped with a variety of delicious spreads and toppings. Bagels are a staple in many households, but have you ever tried making them with sourdough? If not, you’re in for a treat. In this recipe, I’ll guide you through the process of making the best sourdough bagels that are both chewy and flavorful.

Ingredients

  • 400g bread flour
  • 100g whole wheat flour
  • 350g active sourdough starter
  • 10g salt
  • 15g honey
  • 250ml warm water

Instructions

  1. In a large mixing bowl, combine the bread flour, whole wheat flour, and salt. Mix well to evenly distribute the salt.
  2. In a separate container, dissolve the honey in warm water. Add the sourdough starter to the honey-water mixture and stir until well combined.
  3. Make a well in the center of the dry ingredients and pour in the sourdough mixture. Using your hands or a wooden spoon, mix the ingredients until a shaggy dough forms.
  4. Turn the dough out onto a lightly floured surface and knead for about 10 minutes, or until the dough becomes smooth and elastic.
  5. Place the dough in a clean, oiled bowl and cover with a damp cloth. Let it rise at room temperature for about 4-6 hours, or until doubled in size.
  6. Once the dough has doubled in size, gently punch it down to release any air bubbles. Divide the dough into equal-sized portions, depending on how big you want your bagels to be.
  7. Shape each portion into a ball and then poke a hole in the center using your thumb. Stretch the hole to about 1-2 inches in diameter, making sure it’s evenly shaped.
  8. Place the shaped bagels on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper. Cover them with a damp cloth and let them rest for about 30 minutes.
  9. Preheat your oven to 425°F (220°C) and bring a large pot of water to a boil. Once the water is boiling, carefully drop the bagels into the pot, a few at a time, and boil for about 30 seconds on each side.
  10. Remove the bagels from the boiling water and place them back on the baking sheet. If desired, you can sprinkle them with toppings like sesame seeds, poppy seeds, or everything bagel seasoning.
  11. Bake the bagels in the preheated oven for about 20-25 minutes, or until they turn golden brown.
  12. Allow the bagels to cool on a wire rack before slicing and enjoying them with your favorite spreads and toppings.

Tips and Variations

  • For added flavor, you can add ingredients like dried herbs, cheese, or even cinnamon and raisins to the dough before shaping the bagels.
  • To achieve a chewier crust, you can brush the bagels with an egg wash (1 beaten egg mixed with a tablespoon of water) before baking.
  • If you prefer a sweeter bagel, you can increase the amount of honey in the dough recipe.
  • Experiment with different toppings and spreads to create your perfect bagel combination. Cream cheese, smoked salmon, avocado, and tomato are all classic choices.

Now that you have the recipe for the best sourdough bagels, it’s time to get baking! These chewy and flavorful bagels will make your breakfasts, lunches, and snacks extra special. Enjoy the process of making them from scratch and savor every bite.
