Spiced Apple Cider Cupcakes: Fall Flavors at Their Best


As the leaves turn vibrant shades of red and gold, and the air becomes crisp and fragrant with the scent of apples and spices, it’s time to indulge in the delightful flavors of fall. Capturing the essence of the season, these Spiced Apple Cider Cupcakes are the perfect treat to celebrate autumn and its bountiful harvest.

Ingredients

For the Cupcakes:

  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground nutmeg
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/2 cup spiced apple cider

For the Spiced Apple Cider Reduction:

  • 1 cup spiced apple cider
  • 1 cinnamon stick
  • 2 cloves

For the Cinnamon Cream Cheese Frosting:

  • 8 oz cream cheese, softened
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 3-4 cups powdered sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C) and line a muffin tin with cupcake liners.

  2. In a small saucepan, bring 1 cup of spiced apple cider, a cinnamon stick, and cloves to a boil. Reduce the heat to low and simmer for about 15-20 minutes, until the cider has reduced to about 1/4 cup. Remove from heat, discard the cinnamon stick and cloves, and let the reduction cool.

  3. In a medium bowl, whisk together the flour, baking powder, salt, ground cinnamon, and ground nutmeg.

  4. In a separate mixing bowl, cream the butter and sugar together until light and fluffy. Add the eggs, one at a time, beating well after each addition. Stir in the vanilla extract.

  5. Gradually add the dry ingredients to the wet mixture, alternating with 1/2 cup of spiced apple cider, beginning and ending with the dry ingredients. Mix until just combined.

  6. Divide the batter evenly among the prepared cupcake liners, filling each about 2/3 full. Bake for 18-20 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center of a cupcake comes out clean. Allow the cupcakes to cool completely.

  7. While the cupcakes are cooling, prepare the cinnamon cream cheese frosting. In a large bowl, beat the cream cheese and butter together until smooth. Add the powdered sugar, one cup at a time, until the frosting reaches your desired sweetness and consistency. Mix in the vanilla extract and ground cinnamon.

  8. Once the cupcakes are completely cooled, use a small knife to cut out a small piece from the center of each cupcake. Fill the cavities with the spiced apple cider reduction.

  9. Pipe or spread the cinnamon cream cheese frosting onto the cupcakes, covering the filled centers.

  10. Serve and enjoy the delightful flavors of fall in every bite!

Tips and Variations

  • For an extra burst of flavor, sprinkle a pinch of ground cinnamon or nutmeg over the frosting before serving.
  • You can also garnish the cupcakes with a small apple slice or a drizzle of caramel sauce for an elegant presentation.

Conclusion

These Spiced Apple Cider Cupcakes are a delightful fusion of warm spices and the sweet, tangy essence of apple cider, making them a perfect indulgence for the crisp days of autumn. Whether you’re hosting a fall gathering or simply craving a taste of the season, these cupcakes are sure to impress with their comforting flavors and charming appeal.

Embrace the spirit of fall with each delectable bite of these Spiced Apple Cider Cupcakes, and savor the magic of autumn in every mouthful.
