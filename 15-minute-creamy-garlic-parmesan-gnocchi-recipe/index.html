15-Minute Creamy Garlic Parmesan Gnocchi Recipe


Are you craving a delicious and satisfying meal but don’t have a lot of time to spare in the kitchen? Look no further! This 15-Minute Creamy Garlic Parmesan Gnocchi Recipe is the perfect solution to your time-pressed cravings. With its creamy and flavorful sauce, paired with tender gnocchi, this dish is sure to become a favorite in your household.

Ingredients

  • 1 package (16 oz) of gnocchi
  • 2 tablespoons of butter
  • 4 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 cup of heavy cream
  • 1 cup of grated Parmesan cheese
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Chopped fresh parsley for garnish (optional)

Instructions

  1. Start by bringing a large pot of salted water to a boil. Once the water is boiling, add the gnocchi and cook according to the package instructions. This usually takes around 2-3 minutes or until the gnocchi floats to the top. Once cooked, drain and set aside.

  2. While the gnocchi is cooking, melt the butter in a large skillet over medium heat. Add the minced garlic and sauté for about 1 minute, or until fragrant.

  3. Reduce the heat to low and pour in the heavy cream. Stir gently to combine with the garlic and butter. Cook for 2-3 minutes, allowing the cream to heat through.

  4. Gradually add the grated Parmesan cheese to the skillet, stirring constantly to ensure it melts evenly into the sauce. Continue stirring until the sauce becomes smooth and creamy. Season with salt and pepper to taste.

  5. Once the sauce has reached the desired consistency, add the cooked gnocchi to the skillet. Gently toss the gnocchi in the sauce, ensuring it is evenly coated.

  6. Remove the skillet from heat and let it sit for a minute to allow the sauce to thicken slightly. This will help it adhere to the gnocchi better.

  7. Serve the Creamy Garlic Parmesan Gnocchi in bowls, garnishing with chopped fresh parsley if desired. Enjoy while it’s still warm and creamy!

Tips and Variations

  • For an added burst of flavor, you can sauté some diced bacon or pancetta along with the garlic. This will give the dish a delicious smoky taste that pairs perfectly with the creamy sauce.

  • If you’re looking to incorporate more vegetables into your meal, feel free to add some sautéed spinach or mushrooms to the dish. Simply cook them separately and then mix them in with the gnocchi and sauce before serving.

  • To make this recipe vegetarian-friendly, substitute the chicken broth with vegetable broth. This will still give the sauce a rich and savory flavor.

  • If you want to make this dish a bit lighter, you can use half-and-half or whole milk instead of heavy cream. The sauce will be slightly less creamy but still just as delicious.

Now that you have this quick and easy 15-Minute Creamy Garlic Parmesan Gnocchi Recipe in your repertoire, you can satisfy your cravings for a decadent meal without spending hours in the kitchen. This dish is perfect for busy weeknights when you want something comforting and delicious on the table in no time. Give it a try and let your taste buds be delighted by the creamy, garlicky goodness that awaits you!
