Instant Pot Chicken Shawarma Bowls: A Middle Eastern Feast


When it comes to vibrant flavors and hearty meals, few cuisines capture the imagination quite like Middle Eastern dishes. One standout favorite is Chicken Shawarma, a dish known for its aromatic spices, tender chicken, and delicious versatility. Among the easiest and most efficient ways to prepare this classic meal is using an Instant Pot. Combining convenience with authentic taste, Instant Pot Chicken Shawarma Bowls offer a culinary experience that’s both satisfying and simple to make at home.

The Essence of Chicken Shawarma

Chicken Shawarma is a street food staple across the Middle East, celebrated for its bold marinade featuring spices like cumin, paprika, turmeric, and garlic. Traditionally cooked on a vertical rotisserie, the marinated chicken is shaved off in tender, juicy pieces and served with flatbreads, rice, or salads. While you might not have a rotisserie in your kitchen, the Instant Pot provides a practical way to recreate those irresistible textures and flavors.

Served in bowls, this recipe presents Chicken Shawarma on a bed of rice or pilaf, topped with fresh veggies, creamy sauces, and tangy pickles. It’s a meal-in-a-bowl that’s perfect for lunch, dinner, or even meal prepping.

Ingredients You’ll Need

Crafting the perfect Instant Pot Chicken Shawarma Bowl hinges on using fresh, quality ingredients and the right blend of spices. Here’s what you’ll need:

For the Chicken Marinade:

  • 2 pounds of boneless, skinless chicken thighs: Thighs are ideal for their rich flavor and ability to stay tender.
  • 3 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon smoked paprika
  • 1 teaspoon ground turmeric
  • 1 teaspoon ground coriander
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • ½ teaspoon ground cayenne pepper (optional for a kick)
  • Salt and pepper, to taste

For the Rice Base:

  • 2 cups basmati or jasmine rice
  • 3 cups chicken broth
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • ½ teaspoon ground turmeric (for color and flavor)
  • Pinch of salt

For the Toppings and Garnishes:

  • Chopped tomatoes
  • Sliced cucumbers
  • Pickled red onions or pickled turnips
  • Chopped parsley
  • Crumbled feta cheese
  • Tahini sauce or garlic yogurt sauce
  • Lemon wedges

Step-by-Step Instructions

Step 1: Marinate the Chicken

Start by preparing the chicken. In a bowl, combine olive oil, lemon juice, minced garlic, cumin, paprika, turmeric, coriander, cinnamon, cayenne pepper, salt, and pepper. Whisk together until the spices are evenly distributed.

Add the chicken thighs to the bowl and massage the marinade into the meat, ensuring every piece is well-coated. Cover the bowl with plastic wrap and refrigerate for at least 30 minutes to allow the flavors to penetrate the chicken. For best results, let it marinate overnight.

Step 2: Sear the Chicken

Set your Instant Pot to “Sauté” mode and allow it to heat for a minute. Add a splash of olive oil and sear the chicken thighs for 2-3 minutes on each side until they develop a golden-brown crust. Searing locks in flavor and enhances the texture of the final dish.

Once browned, remove the chicken and set it aside.

Step 3: Cook the Chicken

Add a cup of chicken broth to the Instant Pot to deglaze the pot, scraping the bottom to release any stuck-on bits. Return the seared chicken thighs to the pot. Close the lid, set the pressure valve to “Sealing,” and cook on high pressure for 10 minutes. Let the pot naturally release pressure for 5 minutes before carefully performing a quick release.

After cooking, remove the chicken and let it rest for a few minutes before slicing or shredding into bite-sized pieces.

Step 4: Prepare the Rice

While the chicken is cooking, rinse your rice under cold water until the water runs clear—this removes excess starch for fluffy rice.

Once the chicken is done and removed from the Instant Pot, clean the pot and set it back to “Sauté” mode. Add olive oil, turmeric, and a pinch of salt, then stir in the rinsed rice to coat it evenly in the oil and spices. Pour in the chicken broth, stirring gently to combine.

Switch the Instant Pot to “Rice” mode or set it to high pressure for 4-5 minutes. Allow the pressure to naturally release for 10 minutes before fluffing the rice with a fork.

Step 5: Assemble the Bowls

Now comes the fun part—assembling your Chicken Shawarma Bowls. Start with a base of fluffy rice, layering thinly sliced or shredded chicken on top. Arrange your tomatoes, cucumbers, pickled onions, and parsley around the bowl for a burst of color and freshness.

Drizzle a generous helping of tahini sauce or garlic yogurt sauce over the entire dish. Sprinkle crumbled feta cheese for a creamy finish and serve with a wedge or two of lemon for brightness.

Tips for Customization

While this recipe creates an unforgettable combination of flavors and textures, feel free to adjust the ingredients to suit your preferences. Here are a few ideas for customization:

  • If you’re craving crunch, add a handful of roasted chickpeas or crispy pita chips to your bowl.
  • Swap the rice for another grain like quinoa or couscous for added variety.
  • For a low-carb option, replace the rice base with cauliflower rice or shredded lettuce.
  • Experiment with additional garnishes like pomegranate seeds, toasted pine nuts, or a dollop of hummus.

Why the Instant Pot is a Game-Changer

The Instant Pot makes recreating traditionally slow-cooked dishes quick and simple, without compromising on flavor. For Chicken Shawarma, its pressure-cooking ability helps infuse the chicken with the bold, aromatic spices while ensuring unparalleled tenderness. At the same time, its versatility allows you to cook the rice and chicken in one device, saving time and cleanup.

Whether you’re cooking for a family dinner, entertaining guests, or prepping meals for a busy week, this appliance can effortlessly turn labor-intensive dishes into foolproof recipes with incredible results.

Serving and Storing Suggestions

Instant Pot Chicken Shawarma Bowls are best served fresh, allowing you to enjoy the warm, fragrant chicken paired with crisp, cool toppings. However, they’re also ideal for meal prep. Store leftovers in airtight containers, keeping the chicken, rice, and toppings separate for up to 4 days in the refrigerator. Reheat the chicken and rice before reassembling the bowls for a quick and delicious meal.

Additionally, this recipe lends itself well to dinner parties or gatherings. Offer toppings buffet-style, allowing guests to customize their bowls to their liking—this interactive element is always a crowd-pleaser.

A Feast for the Senses

The marriage of flavors and textures in these Instant Pot Chicken Shawarma Bowls makes them a standout choice for food enthusiasts and home cooks alike. The rich blend of spices, tender chicken, and vibrant accompaniments is a testament to the enduring appeal of Middle Eastern cuisine.

Whether you’re new to cooking with an Instant Pot or simply looking for a fresh take on a classic dish, this recipe is sure to become a staple in your kitchen. With minimal effort and maximum flavor, you’ll create a meal that’s as satisfying as it is memorable—a Middle Eastern feast that’s perfect for any occasion. Happy cooking!
