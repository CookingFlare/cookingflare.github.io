Tropical Mango Coconut Bars: A Taste of Paradise


If you’re craving a taste of the tropics, look no further than these delightful Tropical Mango Coconut Bars. Bursting with the luscious flavors of mango and coconut, these bars are like a vacation for your taste buds. Whether you’re hosting a summer gathering, planning a beach-themed party, or simply want to indulge in a sweet treat that transports you to a sun-kissed paradise, these bars are the perfect choice.

Ingredients

For the Crust:

  • 1 ½ cups graham cracker crumbs
  • ⅓ cup granulated sugar
  • ½ cup unsalted butter, melted

For the Mango Coconut Filling:

  • 2 cups fresh ripe mango, diced
  • 1 cup sweetened shredded coconut
  • 1 can (14 ounces) sweetened condensed milk
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • ¼ cup all-purpose flour
  • ½ teaspoon baking powder
  • Pinch of salt

For the Topping (Optional):

  • Additional shredded coconut for sprinkling

Instructions

  1. Preheat the Oven: Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C). Line an 8x8-inch baking pan with parchment paper, leaving an overhang on the sides for easy removal.

  2. Prepare the Crust: In a medium bowl, combine the graham cracker crumbs, sugar, and melted butter. Press the mixture evenly into the bottom of the prepared pan. Bake for 10 minutes, then remove from the oven and set aside.

  3. Make the Mango Coconut Filling: In a blender or food processor, puree the diced mango until smooth. In a large bowl, whisk together the mango puree, sweetened shredded coconut, sweetened condensed milk, eggs, vanilla extract, flour, baking powder, and salt until well combined. Pour the filling over the crust, spreading it out evenly.

  4. Bake to Perfection: Return the pan to the oven and bake for 25-30 minutes, or until the filling is set and the edges are golden brown. If desired, sprinkle additional shredded coconut over the top during the last 5 minutes of baking for an extra tropical touch.

  5. Cool and Chill: Allow the bars to cool completely in the pan on a wire rack. Once cooled, transfer the pan to the refrigerator and chill for at least 2 hours to firm up the filling.

  6. Slice and Serve: Use the parchment overhang to lift the bars out of the pan, then transfer them to a cutting board. Cut into squares or bars of your desired size. Serve chilled and enjoy the taste of the tropics!

Tips and Variations

  • For an extra burst of citrus flavor, add a tablespoon of fresh lime juice to the mango coconut filling.
  • Feel free to swap the mango for other tropical fruits such as pineapple or papaya for a different twist on these bars.
  • To make these bars even more decadent, drizzle them with melted white chocolate before serving.

Final Thoughts

These Tropical Mango Coconut Bars are a delightful fusion of tropical flavors, and they’re sure to brighten any occasion with their sunny disposition. Whether you’re a fan of mango, coconut, or simply crave a taste of paradise, these bars are a must-try. So, gather your ingredients, preheat your oven, and get ready to embark on a culinary journey to the tropics with these irresistible bars. Cheers to savoring the flavors of paradise!
