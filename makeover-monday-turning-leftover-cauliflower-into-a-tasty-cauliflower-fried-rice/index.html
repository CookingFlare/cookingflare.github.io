Makeover Monday: Turning Leftover Cauliflower into a Tasty Cauliflower Fried Rice


Welcome to another Makeover Monday! This week, we’re tackling one of the most versatile vegetables in the kitchen - cauliflower. If you’ve got leftover cauliflower from last night’s dinner or simply want to try something new, we’ve got just the recipe for you. Say goodbye to boring leftovers and hello to a delicious and healthy Cauliflower Fried Rice.

Ingredients

  • 1 head of cauliflower
  • 2 tablespoons of vegetable oil
  • 2 eggs, beaten
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 cup of mixed vegetables (carrots, peas, corn)
  • 3 tablespoons of soy sauce
  • 1 teaspoon of sesame oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Green onions for garnish

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Cauliflower: Cut the cauliflower into florets and pulse in a food processor until it resembles rice. If you don’t have a food processor, you can also grate the cauliflower using a box grater.

  2. Cook the Eggs: Heat 1 tablespoon of vegetable oil in a large pan over medium heat. Add the beaten eggs and cook, stirring occasionally, until set. Remove the eggs from the pan and set aside.

  3. Sauté Aromatics: In the same pan, add the remaining tablespoon of vegetable oil. Sauté the diced onion and minced garlic until fragrant.

  4. Add Vegetables and Cauliflower: Stir in the mixed vegetables and the cauliflower “rice”. Cook for 5-7 minutes, stirring occasionally, until the cauliflower is tender but not mushy.

  5. Season and Finish: Return the cooked eggs to the pan, then add soy sauce, sesame oil, salt, and pepper. Stir well to combine all the ingredients. Cook for an additional 2-3 minutes to let the flavors meld.

  6. Serve and Enjoy: Garnish the Cauliflower Fried Rice with chopped green onions and serve hot.

Why Cauliflower Fried Rice?

Cauliflower Fried Rice is a fantastic way to enjoy a classic dish with a healthy twist. By using cauliflower instead of rice, you’re cutting down on carbohydrates and adding an extra serving of vegetables to your meal. It’s also a great option for those following a gluten-free or low-carb diet.

This dish is incredibly versatile, allowing you to customize it with your favorite additions such as shrimp, chicken, or even tofu. The flavors are reminiscent of traditional fried rice, making it a crowd-pleaser for both vegetarians and meat-eaters alike.

Health Benefits of Cauliflower

Cauliflower is not only delicious but also packed with nutrients. It’s an excellent source of vitamins C and K, as well as fiber and antioxidants. By incorporating cauliflower into your diet, you’re supporting your overall health and wellness.

Final Thoughts

Next time you find yourself with leftover cauliflower, don’t let it go to waste. Instead, give this Cauliflower Fried Rice recipe a try and transform those leftovers into a satisfying and flavorful meal. Whether you’re cooking for yourself or feeding a family, this dish is sure to impress.

So, grab that cauliflower, fire up the stove, and get ready to savor every bite of this delightful Cauliflower Fried Rice. Cheers to a delicious Makeover Monday!
