XOXO Xanthan Gum Chocolate Truffles: A Valentine's Day Treat Recipe


Valentine’s Day is just around the corner, and what better way to celebrate than with a decadent treat that will impress your loved ones? These XOXO Xanthan Gum Chocolate Truffles are the perfect combination of rich, creamy chocolate and a hint of romance. Whether you’re treating your significant other, your friends, or simply indulging in some self-love, these truffles are sure to delight your taste buds.

Ingredients

  • 8 ounces of high-quality dark chocolate, finely chopped
  • 1/2 cup of heavy cream
  • 1/4 teaspoon of XOXO xanthan gum
  • 1/4 cup of unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1/4 cup of powdered sugar
  • 1 teaspoon of pure vanilla extract
  • Pinch of salt
  • Optional: red sprinkles or edible gold dust for decoration

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Chocolate: Place the finely chopped dark chocolate in a heatproof bowl.

  2. Heat the Cream: In a small saucepan, heat the heavy cream over medium heat until it just begins to simmer.

  3. Combine the Ingredients: Once the cream is hot, pour it over the chopped chocolate. Let the mixture sit for a minute to allow the chocolate to melt.

  4. Whisk the Mixture: After a minute, gently whisk the chocolate and cream mixture until it becomes smooth and glossy.

  5. Add XOXO Xanthan Gum: Sprinkle in the XOXO xanthan gum and continue to whisk until fully incorporated. The xanthan gum will help give the truffle mixture a smooth and creamy texture.

  6. Chill the Mixture: Cover the bowl with plastic wrap and refrigerate for at least 2 hours, or until the mixture is firm and easy to handle.

  7. Shape the Truffles: Once the mixture is firm, use a small spoon to scoop out portions and roll them into small balls. Place the shaped truffles on a parchment-lined baking sheet.

  8. Coat the Truffles: In a shallow bowl, combine the unsweetened cocoa powder, powdered sugar, and a pinch of salt. Roll each truffle in the cocoa mixture until evenly coated.

  9. Decorate (Optional): For an extra touch of Valentine’s Day charm, you can roll the truffles in red sprinkles or dust them with edible gold dust.

  10. Chill Again: Place the coated truffles back in the refrigerator for 20-30 minutes to set.

  11. Serve and Enjoy: Once the truffles have set, they are ready to be enjoyed! Arrange them on a pretty platter or package them in a decorative box for gifting.

Tips and Tricks

  • For best results, use high-quality dark chocolate with at least 70% cocoa content. The richness of the chocolate will shine through in the truffles.
  • If you prefer a sweeter truffle, you can substitute some of the dark chocolate with semi-sweet or milk chocolate.
  • XOXO xanthan gum is used in this recipe to provide a creamy texture to the truffles. It acts as a stabilizer, giving the truffle mixture a luxurious mouthfeel.
  • Get creative with the decorations! You can roll the truffles in finely chopped nuts, shredded coconut, or even drizzle them with melted white chocolate.

Share the Love

These XOXO Xanthan Gum Chocolate Truffles are a delightful way to spread love and sweetness on Valentine’s Day. Whether you’re surprising your partner with a homemade treat or hosting a Galentine’s Day celebration, these truffles are a lovely gesture that will be appreciated by all.
