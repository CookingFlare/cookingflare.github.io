Creative Sandwiches to Take on Your Next Picnic


Picnics are a delightful way to enjoy the great outdoors, and what better way to elevate your picnic experience than with a selection of creative sandwiches? Whether you’re lounging in a park, hiking in the hills, or enjoying a day at the beach, sandwiches are the perfect portable meal. They are easy to prepare, customizable, and can cater to various dietary preferences. In this blog post, we’ll explore a variety of innovative sandwich ideas that will impress your friends and family while ensuring your picnic is a delicious success.

The Classic Reimagined: Gourmet Grilled Cheese

Ingredients:

  • 2 slices of sourdough bread
  • 1 tablespoon of butter
  • 1/4 cup of shredded sharp cheddar cheese
  • 1/4 cup of crumbled blue cheese
  • 1/4 cup of caramelized onions
  • Fresh arugula

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the Bread: Spread butter on one side of each slice of sourdough bread.
  2. Layer the Cheese: On the unbuttered side of one slice, layer the sharp cheddar and blue cheese.
  3. Add Onions and Greens: Top the cheese with caramelized onions and a handful of fresh arugula.
  4. Close and Cook: Place the other slice of bread on top, buttered side up. Heat a skillet over medium heat and grill the sandwich for about 3-4 minutes on each side, or until golden brown and the cheese is melted.
  5. Slice and Serve: Cut the sandwich into quarters and pack it in your picnic basket. This gourmet twist on a classic will have everyone asking for seconds!

Mediterranean Delight: Hummus and Veggie Wrap

Ingredients:

  • 1 large whole wheat tortilla
  • 1/2 cup of hummus (store-bought or homemade)
  • 1/4 cup of sliced cucumbers
  • 1/4 cup of shredded carrots
  • 1/4 cup of roasted red peppers
  • 1/4 cup of baby spinach
  • Feta cheese (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Spread the Hummus: Lay the tortilla flat and spread a generous layer of hummus over the entire surface.
  2. Layer the Veggies: Arrange the sliced cucumbers, shredded carrots, roasted red peppers, and baby spinach in the center of the tortilla.
  3. Add Feta: If desired, sprinkle feta cheese over the veggies for an extra burst of flavor.
  4. Wrap It Up: Fold in the sides of the tortilla and roll it tightly from the bottom up. Slice in half for easy handling.
  5. Pack and Go: This wrap is not only healthy but also refreshing, making it a perfect addition to your picnic spread.

Sweet and Savory: Fig and Prosciutto Sandwich

Ingredients:

  • 2 slices of ciabatta bread
  • 2 tablespoons of fig jam
  • 4 slices of prosciutto
  • 1/4 cup of arugula
  • 1 ounce of goat cheese

Instructions:

  1. Spread the Jam: On one slice of ciabatta, spread a layer of fig jam.
  2. Layer the Ingredients: Top the jam with slices of prosciutto, arugula, and crumbled goat cheese.
  3. Top and Close: Place the second slice of ciabatta on top and press down gently.
  4. Slice and Serve: Cut the sandwich in half and wrap it in parchment paper for easy transport. The combination of sweet and savory flavors will tantalize your taste buds.

Vegan Option: Avocado and Chickpea Salad Sandwich

Ingredients:

  • 1 can of chickpeas, drained and rinsed
  • 1 ripe avocado
  • 1 tablespoon of lemon juice
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 slices of whole grain bread
  • Lettuce leaves
  • Tomato slices

Instructions:

  1. Mash the Chickpeas and Avocado: In a bowl, mash the chickpeas and avocado together until well combined. Add lemon juice, salt, and pepper to taste.
  2. Assemble the Sandwich: Spread the chickpea and avocado mixture onto one slice of whole grain bread.
  3. Add Fresh Ingredients: Layer lettuce leaves and tomato slices on top.
  4. Close and Cut: Place the second slice of bread on top, cut in half, and pack it for your picnic. This sandwich is not only filling but also packed with nutrients.
