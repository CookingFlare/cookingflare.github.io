Grandma's Famous Chicken and Dumplings Recipe


Comfort food often brings to mind nostalgic memories of family gatherings and cozy dinners. For many, nothing embodies this sentiment more than Grandma’s famous chicken and dumplings. This dish is a heartwarming blend of tender chicken simmered in a savory broth, accompanied by fluffy dumplings that soak up all the delicious flavors. In this blog post, we’ll take a journey through the history of chicken and dumplings, explore the ingredients that make this dish special, and walk you through Grandma’s treasured recipe step-by-step.

The History of Chicken and Dumplings

Chicken and dumplings has a rich history that dates back to the early days of American cuisine. This dish is thought to have originated from the culinary traditions of Europe, particularly England, where dumplings were commonly made as a way to stretch meals further. As settlers arrived in America, they brought their recipes with them, adapting them to include local ingredients and flavors.

Over the years, chicken and dumplings have become a staple in Southern cooking, where it’s often served as a comforting meal during family gatherings or Sunday dinners. Each family has its own version, with some opting for creamy sauces while others prefer a broth-based approach. Regardless of the style, chicken and dumplings remain a beloved dish that evokes feelings of warmth and nostalgia.

Ingredients for Grandma’s Chicken and Dumplings

To recreate Grandma’s famous chicken and dumplings, you’ll need to gather a few key ingredients. Each component plays a crucial role in delivering the rich flavors and comforting textures that make this dish so special.

For the Chicken and Broth

  • 1 whole chicken (about 3-4 pounds): Using a whole chicken allows for maximum flavor and tenderness. You can also use bone-in, skin-on chicken pieces.
  • 8 cups of chicken broth: Homemade broth is ideal, but store-bought can work in a pinch. The broth serves as the foundation of the dish.
  • 1 onion: Diced. Onions add depth to the broth.
  • 2 carrots: Sliced. Carrots introduce sweetness and color.
  • 2 celery stalks: Chopped. Celery provides an aromatic flavor.
  • 3 cloves of garlic: Minced. Garlic enhances the overall taste.
  • Salt and pepper: To taste. Seasoning is key for flavor enhancement.

For the Dumplings

  • 2 cups of all-purpose flour: This forms the base of the dumplings.
  • 1 tablespoon of baking powder: Helps the dumplings rise and become fluffy.
  • ½ teaspoon of salt: For flavor.
  • 1 cup of milk: Adds moisture to the dumpling batter.
  • ¼ cup of unsalted butter: Melted. Butter adds richness.

Optional Ingredients

  • Fresh herbs: Such as thyme or parsley, for garnish.
  • Additional seasoning: Like paprika or cayenne pepper, for a bit of heat.

Step-by-Step Instructions

Now that we have our ingredients ready, let’s dive into the step-by-step process of making Grandma’s famous chicken and dumplings.

Step 1: Prepare the Chicken and Broth

  1. Cook the Chicken: In a large pot, add the whole chicken and cover it with water. Bring to a boil over medium-high heat. Once boiling, reduce the heat to low and let it simmer for about 1 hour, or until the chicken is cooked through and tender.

  2. Add Aromatics: After the chicken has cooked, remove it from the pot and set it aside to cool. Add the diced onion, sliced carrots, chopped celery, and minced garlic to the pot. Let these ingredients simmer in the broth for about 15-20 minutes, allowing the flavors to meld together.

  3. Shred the Chicken: Once the chicken is cool enough to handle, remove the skin and bones, and shred the meat into bite-sized pieces. Return the shredded chicken to the pot and season with salt and pepper to taste.

Step 2: Make the Dumplings

  1. Combine Dry Ingredients: In a large mixing bowl, whisk together the flour, baking powder, and salt until well combined.

  2. Mix Wet Ingredients: In a separate bowl, combine the melted butter and milk. Gradually add the wet mixture to the dry ingredients, stirring until just combined. Be careful not to overmix; the batter should be slightly lumpy.

Step 3: Cook the Dumplings

  1. Add Dumplings to the Broth: Once the broth is simmering, drop spoonfuls of the dumpling batter into the pot, making sure they are spaced out. You can also use a cookie scoop for uniform sizes.

  2. Cover and Cook: Reduce the heat to low and cover the pot with a lid. Allow the dumplings to cook for about 15-20 minutes. This step is crucial, as the dumplings will steam and rise, becoming light and fluffy.

Step 4: Serve and Enjoy

Once the dumplings are cooked through, ladle the chicken and dumplings into bowls. If desired, sprinkle with fresh herbs for an added touch of flavor and presentation. Serve with crusty bread or a side salad for a complete meal that will surely impress family and friends.

Tips for Perfect Chicken and Dumplings

  • Use Quality Ingredients: The quality of your chicken and broth will significantly impact the final dish. Whenever possible, opt for organic chicken and homemade broth.

  • Don’t Overmix the Dumpling Batter: Overmixing can lead to tough dumplings. Mix until just combined for the best texture.

  • Adjust Consistency: If you prefer a thicker broth, you can add a cornstarch slurry (cornstarch mixed with a little water) to the simmering broth before adding the dumplings.

  • Herb Infusion: Experiment with different herbs to customize the flavor. Sage and rosemary can add delightful notes to the dish.

Conclusion

Grandma’s famous chicken and dumplings recipe is more than just a meal; it’s a loving tradition passed down through generations. The combination of tender chicken, flavorful broth, and fluffy dumplings creates a dish that warms the heart and satisfies the soul. Whether you’re cooking for family, friends, or simply treating yourself, this recipe is sure to become a staple in your kitchen.

Next time you gather around the table, take a moment to appreciate the comforting embrace of this classic dish. With each bite, you’ll not only savor the delicious flavors but also the cherished memories that come with Grandma’s famous chicken and dumplings. Happy cooking!
