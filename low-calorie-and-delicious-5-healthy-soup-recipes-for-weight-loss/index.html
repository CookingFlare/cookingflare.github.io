Low-Calorie and Delicious: 5 Healthy Soup Recipes for Weight Loss


When it comes to healthy eating and weight loss, soups are a fantastic option. They are not only low in calories but also packed with nutrients, making them a perfect choice for anyone looking to shed those extra pounds. In this blog post, I will share with you five delicious and low-calorie soup recipes that will not only tantalize your taste buds but also help you achieve your weight loss goals. So without further ado, let’s dive right into these flavorful and healthy creations!

1. Creamy Broccoli Soup

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups broccoli florets
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 3 cups vegetable broth
  • 1 cup unsweetened almond milk
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a large pot, sauté the chopped onion and minced garlic until fragrant.
  2. Add the broccoli florets and vegetable broth to the pot. Bring to a boil and then reduce the heat to a simmer. Cook until the broccoli is tender.
  3. Using an immersion blender or a regular blender, puree the soup until smooth.
  4. Return the soup to the pot and stir in the almond milk. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  5. Simmer for a few more minutes until heated through.
  6. Serve hot and enjoy the creamy goodness!

2. Spicy Lentil Soup

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup red lentils
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 carrots, diced
  • 2 celery stalks, diced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 4 cups vegetable broth
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • ½ teaspoon cayenne pepper (adjust according to your spice preference)
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Rinse the lentils under cold water until the water runs clear.
  2. In a large pot, sauté the chopped onion, diced carrots, diced celery, and minced garlic until they start to soften.
  3. Add the rinsed lentils, vegetable broth, cumin, paprika, and cayenne pepper to the pot. Bring to a boil and then reduce the heat to a simmer.
  4. Cook for about 20-25 minutes until the lentils are tender and the flavors have melded together.
  5. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  6. Serve hot and enjoy the spicy kick!

3. Tangy Tomato Soup

Ingredients:

  • 4 large tomatoes, chopped
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 3 cups vegetable broth
  • 1 tablespoon balsamic vinegar
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a large pot, sauté the chopped onion and minced garlic until fragrant.
  2. Add the chopped tomatoes, vegetable broth, balsamic vinegar, dried basil, and dried oregano to the pot. Bring to a boil and then reduce the heat to a simmer.
  3. Cook for about 15-20 minutes until the tomatoes are soft and flavors have melded together.
  4. Using an immersion blender or a regular blender, puree the soup until smooth.
  5. Return the soup to the pot and season with salt and pepper to taste.
  6. Simmer for a few more minutes until heated through.
  7. Serve hot and enjoy the tangy goodness!

4. Hearty Minestrone Soup

Ingredients:

  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 carrots, diced
  • 2 celery stalks, diced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 4 cups vegetable broth
  • 1 can diced tomatoes
  • 1 can kidney beans, drained and rinsed
  • 1 cup whole wheat pasta
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a large pot, sauté the chopped onion, diced carrots, diced celery, and minced garlic until they start to soften.
  2. Add the vegetable broth, diced tomatoes, kidney beans, whole wheat pasta, dried basil, and dried oregano to the pot. Bring to a boil and then reduce the heat to a simmer.
  3. Cook for about 10-15 minutes until the pasta is al dente and the flavors have melded together.
  4. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  5. Serve hot and enjoy the hearty goodness!

5. Coconut Curry Soup

Ingredients:

  • 1 tablespoon coconut oil
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon curry powder
  • 1 can coconut milk
  • 3 cups vegetable broth
  • 1 cup diced sweet potatoes
  • 1 cup diced bell peppers
  • 1 cup diced zucchini
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a large pot, heat the coconut oil and sauté the chopped onion and minced garlic until fragrant.
  2. Add the curry powder to the pot and cook for another minute.
  3. Stir in the coconut milk, vegetable broth, diced sweet potatoes, diced bell peppers, and diced zucchini. Bring to a boil and then reduce the heat to a simmer.
  4. Cook for about 15-20 minutes until the vegetables are tender and the flavors have melded together.
  5. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  6. Serve hot and enjoy the flavorful coconut curry!

And there you have it, five delicious and low-calorie soup recipes that are perfect for weight loss. These soups are not only nutritious but also incredibly satisfying, making them a great addition to your weight loss journey. So grab your pot, gather the ingredients, and get ready to indulge in these healthy and mouthwatering soups. Happy cooking!
