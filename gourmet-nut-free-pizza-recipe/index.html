Gourmet Nut-Free Pizza Recipe


Are you ready to elevate your pizza game to a gourmet level? Look no further because we have the perfect nut-free pizza recipe for you! This recipe combines the classic flavors of a traditional pizza with a gourmet twist, making it a delightful treat for your taste buds. Whether you have a nut allergy or simply prefer to avoid nuts, this recipe will allow you to enjoy a delicious pizza without any compromise on flavor.

Ingredients

For the Pizza Dough:

  • 2 1/4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 3/4 cup warm water
  • 1 packet active dry yeast

For the Pizza Sauce:

  • 1 can (14 oz) crushed tomatoes
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

For the Toppings:

  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/2 cup sliced pepperoni
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • Any other toppings of your choice (e.g., bell peppers, onions, mushrooms)

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Pizza Dough:

    • In a small bowl, combine the warm water, sugar, and yeast. Let it sit for 5-10 minutes until frothy.
    • In a large mixing bowl, combine the flour and salt. Make a well in the center and pour in the yeast mixture and olive oil. Stir until a dough forms.
    • Knead the dough on a floured surface for about 5-7 minutes until it becomes smooth and elastic. Place the dough in a greased bowl, cover it with a damp cloth, and let it rise for about 1 hour, or until doubled in size.

  2. Make the Pizza Sauce:

    • In a saucepan, heat a tablespoon of olive oil over medium heat. Add the minced garlic and sauté for a minute until fragrant.
    • Pour in the crushed tomatoes and add the dried oregano, dried basil, salt, and pepper. Let the sauce simmer for 15-20 minutes until it thickens slightly. Set aside to cool.

  3. Assemble the Pizza:

    • Preheat your oven to 475°F (245°C) and place a pizza stone or an inverted baking sheet on the middle rack.
    • Punch down the pizza dough and divide it in half. Roll out each half into a 12-inch circle on a lightly floured surface.
    • Transfer the rolled-out dough to a pizza peel or another inverted baking sheet dusted with cornmeal.
    • Spread the pizza sauce over the dough, leaving a small border for the crust. Sprinkle the shredded mozzarella and grated Parmesan cheese over the sauce.
    • Arrange the pepperoni slices and any other toppings of your choice on top of the cheese.

  4. Bake the Pizza:

    • Carefully slide the pizza onto the preheated pizza stone or baking sheet in the oven. Bake for 12-15 minutes until the crust is golden and the cheese is bubbly and melted.
    • Remove the pizza from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes. Garnish with fresh basil leaves before slicing and serving.

Enjoy Your Gourmet Nut-Free Pizza!

This gourmet nut-free pizza is a feast for the senses, with its flavorful sauce, gooey cheese, and perfectly baked crust. Whether you’re hosting a dinner party or simply craving a gourmet pizza night at home, this recipe is sure to impress. Feel free to customize the toppings to suit your preferences, and don’t forget to pair it with a glass of your favorite wine or a refreshing salad for a complete dining experience.

So, what are you waiting for? Gather your ingredients, roll up your sleeves, and get ready to savor the deliciousness of this gourmet nut-free pizza. Happy cooking and bon appétit!
