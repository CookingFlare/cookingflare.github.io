Greek Tzatziki Chicken Salad: A Refreshing and Protein-Packed Meal


When it comes to wholesome and flavorful meals, Greek cuisine never fails to impress. This Greek Tzatziki Chicken Salad is a perfect example of how simple ingredients can come together to create a refreshing and protein-packed dish that’s perfect for any occasion. Whether you’re looking for a light lunch, a satisfying dinner, or a crowd-pleasing potluck contribution, this recipe has got you covered.

Ingredients

For the Chicken:

  • 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste

For the Tzatziki Sauce:

  • 1 cup Greek yogurt
  • 1 cucumber, grated and drained
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons fresh dill, chopped
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • Salt and pepper to taste

For the Salad:

  • 4 cups mixed greens
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1/2 red onion, thinly sliced
  • 1/2 cup Kalamata olives
  • 1/4 cup crumbled feta cheese
  • 1/4 cup fresh mint leaves, chopped

Instructions

Step 1: Prepare the Chicken

  1. In a bowl, combine the minced garlic, olive oil, dried oregano, salt, and pepper.
  2. Add the chicken breasts to the bowl and coat them evenly with the marinade.
  3. Cover and refrigerate for at least 30 minutes to allow the flavors to infuse.

Step 2: Make the Tzatziki Sauce

  1. Grate the cucumber and place it in a fine-mesh sieve. Sprinkle with a little salt and let it sit for 10 minutes to drain excess moisture.
  2. In a bowl, combine the Greek yogurt, minced garlic, chopped dill, lemon juice, and the drained cucumber.
  3. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Refrigerate until ready to use.

Step 3: Grill the Chicken

  1. Preheat a grill or grill pan over medium-high heat.
  2. Grill the marinated chicken breasts for 6-7 minutes on each side, or until fully cooked and nicely charred.
  3. Remove from the grill and let them rest for a few minutes before slicing.

Step 4: Assemble the Salad

  1. In a large salad bowl, combine the mixed greens, cherry tomatoes, red onion, Kalamata olives, and mint leaves.
  2. Toss the salad with half of the prepared Tzatziki sauce.
  3. Top the salad with the sliced grilled chicken, crumbled feta cheese, and an extra drizzle of Tzatziki sauce.

Serving Suggestions

This Greek Tzatziki Chicken Salad is best served immediately, allowing the flavors to meld together beautifully. Serve it with warm pita bread or grilled flatbread for a complete Mediterranean experience. It also makes a great meal prep option for a week of nutritious and satisfying lunches.

Final Thoughts

With its vibrant colors, bold flavors, and ample protein, this Greek Tzatziki Chicken Salad is a true celebration of Mediterranean cuisine. Whether you’re a fan of Greek food or simply looking for a delicious and healthy meal, this recipe is sure to become a favorite in your culinary repertoire. Enjoy the simplicity and goodness of this salad that’s perfect for warm summer days or cozy winter nights.

So, what are you waiting for? Gather your ingredients, fire up the grill, and treat yourself to a taste of Greece with this delightful Tzatziki Chicken Salad. Opa!
