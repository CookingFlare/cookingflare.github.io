Instant Pot Beef and Mushroom Gravy Recipe: A Savory and Hearty Option


Are you craving a delicious and comforting meal that will warm your soul? Look no further than this Instant Pot Beef and Mushroom Gravy recipe! Packed with rich flavors and tender beef, this dish is perfect for those cozy nights at home. And the best part? It’s incredibly easy to make in your trusty Instant Pot!

Ingredients

  • 2 pounds beef chuck roast, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 8 ounces button mushrooms, sliced
  • 1 cup beef broth
  • 1 cup red wine
  • 2 tablespoons Worcestershire sauce
  • 2 tablespoons tomato paste
  • 2 sprigs fresh thyme
  • Salt and pepper, to taste

Instructions

  1. Turn on your Instant Pot and select the “Saute” function. Heat a tablespoon of oil and add the diced onion. Cook until translucent and fragrant, about 3 minutes.

  2. Add the minced garlic and sliced mushrooms to the pot. Cook for another 2 minutes, or until the mushrooms have softened and released their juices.

  3. Push the onion and mushroom mixture to one side of the pot and add the beef chuck roast. Brown the meat on all sides, about 5 minutes. This step will add depth of flavor to the final dish.

  4. In a separate bowl, whisk together the beef broth, red wine, Worcestershire sauce, and tomato paste until well combined. Pour this mixture over the beef and mushrooms in the Instant Pot.

  5. Add the fresh thyme sprigs and season with salt and pepper to taste. Give everything a good stir to ensure the flavors are evenly distributed.

  6. Secure the lid of the Instant Pot and set the vent to the sealing position. Select the “Meat/Stew” function and set the cooking time to 35 minutes.

  7. Once the cooking time is up, allow the pressure to naturally release for 10 minutes before carefully venting to release any remaining pressure.

  8. Remove the lid and give the beef and mushroom gravy a gentle stir. The meat should be tender and easily fall apart.

  9. Serve the savory and hearty beef and mushroom gravy over a bed of mashed potatoes or cooked egg noodles. Garnish with fresh parsley, if desired.

Tips and Variations

  • For a deeper flavor, you can marinate the beef in red wine and Worcestershire sauce for a few hours before cooking.

  • Feel free to add your favorite herbs and spices to customize the dish to your taste. Thyme pairs well with beef and mushrooms, but you can experiment with rosemary, oregano, or even a touch of smoked paprika.

  • If you prefer a thicker gravy, you can mix a tablespoon of cornstarch with a tablespoon of cold water and stir it into the pot after cooking. Allow the gravy to simmer for a few minutes until it reaches the desired consistency.

  • This recipe is also great for meal prepping. Simply divide the cooked beef and mushroom gravy into individual portions and store in the refrigerator or freezer for later use.

Conclusion

With the help of your trusty Instant Pot, you can create a savory and hearty beef and mushroom gravy that will satisfy your taste buds and warm your soul. The tender beef, flavorful mushrooms, and rich gravy come together to create a truly comforting meal. So go ahead, give this recipe a try, and enjoy a delicious homemade dinner that will leave you feeling satisfied and content.
