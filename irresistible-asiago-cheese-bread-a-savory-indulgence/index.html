Irresistible Asiago Cheese Bread: A Savory Indulgence


Are you ready to embark on a culinary adventure that will tantalize your taste buds and leave you craving for more? Look no further than this recipe for Irresistible Asiago Cheese Bread. This savory indulgence is a masterpiece of flavors, combining the rich and nutty taste of Asiago cheese with the soft and fluffy texture of freshly baked bread. With just a few simple ingredients and a little bit of patience, you can create a mouthwatering treat that will impress even the most discerning food connoisseurs.

Ingredients

To make this delectable Asiago Cheese Bread, you will need:

  • 3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tablespoon active dry yeast
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup warm water
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 cup shredded Asiago cheese
  • 1 teaspoon dried Italian herbs

Instructions

  1. In a large mixing bowl, combine the warm water, sugar, and active dry yeast. Stir gently and let it sit for about 5 minutes, or until the mixture becomes frothy.

  2. Add the flour, salt, and olive oil to the yeast mixture. Mix well until the dough comes together.

  3. Transfer the dough onto a lightly floured surface and knead for about 5-7 minutes, or until it becomes smooth and elastic.

  4. Place the dough in a greased bowl and cover it with a clean kitchen towel. Let it rise in a warm place for about 1 hour, or until it doubles in size.

  5. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C). Grease a loaf pan and set it aside.

  6. Punch down the risen dough and transfer it onto a lightly floured surface. Flatten the dough into a rectangular shape, approximately 10x12 inches.

  7. Sprinkle the shredded Asiago cheese evenly over the dough, leaving a small border around the edges.

  8. Starting from one of the longer sides, tightly roll the dough into a log. Pinch the edges to seal.

  9. Place the rolled dough into the greased loaf pan, seam side down. Sprinkle the top with dried Italian herbs.

  10. Bake the bread in the preheated oven for 30-35 minutes, or until it turns golden brown and sounds hollow when tapped on the bottom.

  11. Remove the bread from the oven and let it cool in the pan for 10 minutes. Then, transfer it to a wire rack to cool completely before slicing.

Serving Suggestions

This Irresistible Asiago Cheese Bread is best enjoyed fresh out of the oven, when the cheese is still warm and gooey. Serve it as a side dish to complement your favorite pasta or soup, or simply enjoy it on its own as a delightful snack. The bread pairs well with a glass of red wine or a hot cup of tea.

Final Thoughts

With its irresistible combination of flavors and textures, this Asiago Cheese Bread is sure to become a favorite among your family and friends. The nutty aroma of the Asiago cheese will fill your kitchen as the bread bakes to golden perfection. So, why wait? Gather your ingredients, follow the steps, and treat yourself to this savory indulgence. Get ready to experience the pure joy of biting into a slice of warm, cheesy goodness. Bon appétit!
