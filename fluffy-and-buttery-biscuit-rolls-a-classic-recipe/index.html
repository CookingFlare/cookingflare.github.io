Fluffy and Buttery Biscuit Rolls: A Classic Recipe


There’s nothing quite like the aroma of freshly baked biscuit rolls wafting through the kitchen. These fluffy and buttery treats are a classic favorite that never fails to delight. Whether you’re serving them for breakfast, brunch, or as a side dish for dinner, biscuit rolls are a versatile and comforting addition to any meal. In this recipe, we’ll take you through the steps to create the perfect batch of fluffy and buttery biscuit rolls that will have your friends and family coming back for more.

Ingredients

  • 4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tablespoon baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • ½ cup unsalted butter, cold and cut into small cubes
  • 1 ½ cups buttermilk
  • ¼ cup melted butter for brushing

Instructions

Step 1: Preheat the Oven

Preheat your oven to 425°F (220°C) and lightly grease a baking dish or line it with parchment paper.

Step 2: Mix the Dry Ingredients

In a large mixing bowl, combine the all-purpose flour, baking powder, baking soda, and salt. Whisk the dry ingredients together until well combined.

Step 3: Incorporate the Butter

Add the cold, cubed unsalted butter to the dry ingredients. Using a pastry cutter or your fingertips, work the butter into the flour mixture until it resembles coarse crumbs. It’s important to keep the butter cold to achieve a flaky texture in the biscuit rolls.

Step 4: Add the Buttermilk

Create a well in the center of the flour and butter mixture, then pour in the buttermilk. Gently stir the ingredients together until a soft dough forms. Be careful not to overmix, as this can result in tough biscuit rolls.

Step 5: Roll and Cut the Dough

Transfer the dough onto a lightly floured surface and gently knead it a few times until it comes together. Roll the dough out to about 1-inch thickness. Then, using a biscuit cutter or a glass, cut out the biscuit rolls and place them in the prepared baking dish, ensuring they are touching each other.

Step 6: Bake to Perfection

Brush the tops of the biscuit rolls with melted butter to help them achieve a beautiful golden brown color during baking. Place the baking dish in the preheated oven and bake for 15-20 minutes, or until the biscuit rolls are puffed up and golden on top.

Step 7: Enjoy!

Once the biscuit rolls are baked to perfection, remove them from the oven and allow them to cool for a few minutes before serving. These fluffy and buttery biscuit rolls are best enjoyed warm, with a generous slather of butter or your favorite jam.

Tips for Success

  • Cold Ingredients: Keeping the butter and buttermilk cold is crucial for achieving the perfect texture in your biscuit rolls.
  • Gentle Handling: Avoid overmixing the dough, as this can result in tough biscuit rolls. A light hand is key to fluffy and tender results.
  • Customization: Feel free to add shredded cheese, herbs, or spices to the dough for a flavorful twist on this classic recipe.

There you have it – a timeless recipe for fluffy and buttery biscuit rolls that are sure to become a staple in your kitchen. Whether you’re a seasoned baker or just starting out, these biscuit rolls are a delightful way to bring warmth and comfort to any meal. So, roll up your sleeves and get ready to savor the irresistible aroma and taste of these classic treats. Enjoy!
