Creamy Dairy-Free Spinach Artichoke Dip for Any Occasion


Are you looking for a delicious and creamy dip that’s dairy-free and perfect for any occasion? Look no further! This creamy dairy-free spinach artichoke dip is a crowd-pleaser and will leave your guests asking for the recipe. Whether you’re hosting a party, a casual get-together, or simply craving a savory snack, this dip is a must-try. The best part? It’s easy to make and can be customized to suit your taste preferences.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup raw cashews, soaked in water for 4-6 hours
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 3 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 can (14 oz) artichoke hearts, drained and chopped
  • 1 1/2 cups fresh spinach, chopped
  • 1/4 cup nutritional yeast
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon onion powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1/4 teaspoon cayenne pepper (optional)
  • 1/2 cup unsweetened almond milk
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 1 tablespoon fresh parsley, chopped (for garnish)

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).

  2. In a food processor or high-speed blender, combine the soaked cashews and 1/2 cup of water. Blend until smooth and creamy, adding more water if needed to achieve a thick, but pourable consistency. Set aside.

  3. In a skillet, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the chopped onion and sauté until translucent, about 3-4 minutes. Add the minced garlic and sauté for another 1-2 minutes.

  4. Stir in the chopped artichoke hearts and chopped spinach. Cook for 3-4 minutes, or until the spinach wilts and the artichokes are heated through.

  5. Add the nutritional yeast, garlic powder, onion powder, salt, black pepper, and cayenne pepper (if using). Stir to combine.

  6. Pour in the creamy cashew mixture, almond milk, and lemon juice. Stir well to combine all the ingredients. Cook for an additional 2-3 minutes, allowing the flavors to meld together.

  7. Transfer the mixture to an oven-safe dish and smooth the top with a spatula.

  8. Bake for 20-25 minutes, or until the dip is bubbly and the top is lightly golden.

  9. Remove from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes. Sprinkle with fresh chopped parsley for a pop of color and freshness.

  10. Serve the creamy dairy-free spinach artichoke dip with your favorite dippers such as tortilla chips, pita bread, or fresh vegetable sticks.

Customization Tips

  • For an extra kick of flavor, add a pinch of red pepper flakes or a dash of hot sauce.
  • If you prefer a thicker dip, reduce the amount of almond milk.
  • Experiment with different herbs and spices such as thyme, rosemary, or smoked paprika to create your own unique flavor profile.

Conclusion

This creamy dairy-free spinach artichoke dip is a versatile and flavorful option for any gathering. Whether you’re catering to dairy-free guests or simply looking for a healthier alternative to traditional dips, this recipe is sure to impress. The creamy texture, combined with the savory flavors of spinach and artichoke, makes it a winning choice for any occasion. So, next time you’re planning a party or a cozy night in, whip up a batch of this delicious dip and watch it disappear in no time!
