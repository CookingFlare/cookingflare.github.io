Vegan Black Forest Cake: Layers of Chocolatey Goodness


If you’re a fan of rich, decadent desserts but follow a vegan lifestyle, you’re in for a treat! This Vegan Black Forest Cake is a delightful twist on the classic German dessert, featuring layers of moist chocolate cake, luscious whipped coconut cream, and juicy cherries. The combination of deep, dark chocolate and tart cherries makes this cake an irresistible indulgence for any occasion.

Ingredients

For the Chocolate Cake:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 1/2 cups granulated sugar
  • 3/4 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 2 tsp baking powder
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1 3/4 cups almond milk
  • 1/2 cup vegetable oil
  • 2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 2 tbsp apple cider vinegar

For the Whipped Coconut Cream:

  • 2 cans full-fat coconut milk, refrigerated overnight
  • 1/4 cup powdered sugar
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract

For the Cherry Filling:

  • 2 cups pitted cherries, fresh or frozen
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 2 tbsp cornstarch
  • 1/4 cup water
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice

For Assembly:

  • 1/4 cup kirsch (cherry liqueur) or cherry juice
  • Vegan chocolate shavings for garnish

Instructions

Step 1: Prepare the Chocolate Cake

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C) and grease two 9-inch round cake pans.
  2. In a large bowl, sift together the flour, sugar, cocoa powder, baking powder, baking soda, and salt.
  3. In a separate bowl, whisk together the almond milk, vegetable oil, vanilla extract, and apple cider vinegar.
  4. Gradually add the wet ingredients to the dry ingredients, mixing until just combined.
  5. Divide the batter evenly between the prepared cake pans and smooth the tops.
  6. Bake for 30-35 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center of the cakes comes out clean.
  7. Allow the cakes to cool in the pans for 10 minutes, then transfer them to a wire rack to cool completely.

Step 2: Make the Whipped Coconut Cream

  1. Carefully open the refrigerated cans of coconut milk without shaking them. Scoop out the solid coconut cream that has risen to the top and place it in a mixing bowl.
  2. Add the powdered sugar and vanilla extract to the coconut cream.
  3. Using an electric mixer, whip the coconut cream until light and fluffy. Refrigerate until ready to use.

Step 3: Prepare the Cherry Filling

  1. In a saucepan, combine the cherries, sugar, and water. Cook over medium heat, stirring occasionally, until the cherries release their juices.
  2. In a small bowl, whisk together the cornstarch and lemon juice. Add this mixture to the cherries and stir until the filling thickens. Remove from heat and let it cool.

Step 4: Assemble the Cake

  1. Once the cakes have cooled, use a long serrated knife to level the tops if necessary.
  2. Place one cake layer on a serving plate. Drizzle it with half of the kirsch or cherry juice.
  3. Spread a layer of whipped coconut cream over the cake, then spoon half of the cherry filling on top.
  4. Carefully place the second cake layer on top and repeat the process with the remaining kirsch or cherry juice, whipped coconut cream, and cherry filling.
  5. Refrigerate the cake for at least 1 hour to allow the flavors to meld.
  6. Before serving, garnish the cake with vegan chocolate shavings.

Conclusion

Indulge in the divine flavors of this Vegan Black Forest Cake, and treat yourself to a guilt-free, plant-based dessert that’s sure to impress. Whether you’re celebrating a special occasion or simply craving a slice of chocolatey goodness, this cake is a delightful way to satisfy your sweet tooth while staying true to your vegan principles. Enjoy each luscious bite and share the joy of cruelty-free, decadent desserts with your loved ones!

This Vegan Black Forest Cake recipe offers a delectable and cruelty-free take on a beloved classic. With moist chocolate cake, luscious whipped coconut cream, and juicy cherries, it’s a treat for the senses. Whether you’re a vegan, have dairy allergies, or simply love trying new desserts, this recipe is sure to become a favorite.
