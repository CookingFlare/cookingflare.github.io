Scrumptious Breakfast Slider Recipes for a Bite-Sized Meal


Looking for a delicious and satisfying way to start your day? Look no further than these scrumptious breakfast slider recipes! These bite-sized meals are packed with flavor and are perfect for those busy mornings when you need something quick and easy. Whether you prefer savory or sweet, there’s a slider recipe here to satisfy every taste bud. So, roll up your sleeves, put on your apron, and let’s get cooking!

1. Bacon, Egg, and Cheese Sliders

Ingredients:

  • 12 slider buns
  • 12 slices of bacon
  • 6 eggs
  • 6 slices of cheddar cheese
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. Cook the bacon in a skillet until crispy. Remove from the skillet and drain on paper towels.
  3. In the same skillet, melt the butter over medium heat. Crack the eggs into the skillet and cook until the whites are set but the yolks are still slightly runny. Season with salt and pepper.
  4. Cut the slider buns in half horizontally. Place a slice of bacon, a cooked egg, and a slice of cheese on each bottom bun. Top with the other half of the bun.
  5. Place the sliders on a baking sheet and bake for 10 minutes or until the cheese is melted and the buns are slightly toasted.
  6. Serve warm and enjoy!

2. Sausage and Peppers Breakfast Sliders

Ingredients:

  • 12 slider buns
  • 12 breakfast sausage patties
  • 1 red bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 green bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 onion, sliced
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Heat the olive oil in a skillet over medium heat. Add the sliced peppers and onion and cook until they are soft and slightly caramelized. Season with salt and pepper.
  3. Cook the breakfast sausage patties according to the package instructions.
  4. Cut the slider buns in half horizontally. Place a cooked sausage patty on each bottom bun and top with a spoonful of the sautéed peppers and onions. Top with the other half of the bun.
  5. Place the sliders on a baking sheet and bake for 5-7 minutes or until the buns are slightly toasted.
  6. Serve warm and enjoy!

3. French Toast and Berry Sliders

Ingredients:

  • 12 slider buns
  • 6 slices of brioche bread
  • 4 eggs
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 cup mixed berries (strawberries, blueberries, raspberries)
  • Maple syrup for serving

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. Cut the brioche bread into small squares.
  3. In a shallow dish, whisk together the eggs, milk, and vanilla extract.
  4. Dip each piece of brioche bread into the egg mixture, making sure it’s coated on all sides.
  5. Heat a skillet over medium heat and lightly grease it with butter or cooking spray. Cook the French toast sliders until golden brown on each side.
  6. Cut the slider buns in half horizontally. Place a piece of French toast on each bottom bun and top with a spoonful of mixed berries. Top with the other half of the bun.
  7. Place the sliders on a baking sheet and bake for 5-7 minutes or until the buns are slightly toasted.
  8. Serve warm with a drizzle of maple syrup and enjoy!

These scrumptious breakfast slider recipes are a fantastic way to start your day off right. Whether you’re a fan of classic bacon and eggs, savory sausage and peppers, or sweet French toast and berries, these bite-sized meals are sure to satisfy your cravings. So, why wait? Whip up a batch of these delicious sliders and enjoy a tasty breakfast on the go!
