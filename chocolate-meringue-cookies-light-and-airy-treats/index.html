Chocolate Meringue Cookies: Light and Airy Treats


Who doesn’t love a light and airy treat that melts in your mouth? Chocolate meringue cookies are the perfect answer to satisfy your sweet tooth while still keeping things light and delicate. With their crisp exteriors and soft, pillowy centers, these cookies are a delightful indulgence for any occasion.

Ingredients

To create these heavenly cookies, you’ll need the following ingredients:

  • 4 large egg whites
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/4 teaspoon cream of tartar
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 3 tablespoons unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1/2 cup semisweet chocolate chips

Instructions

Now that you have your ingredients ready, let’s dive into the step-by-step process of making these delectable chocolate meringue cookies:

  1. Preheat your oven to 225°F (110°C) and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.

  2. In a clean, dry mixing bowl, beat the egg whites on medium-high speed until they become frothy. This should take about 1-2 minutes.

  3. Gradually add the sugar, cream of tartar, and salt to the egg whites while continuing to beat. Increase the speed to high and beat until stiff peaks form. This should take about 5 minutes.

  4. Gently fold in the vanilla extract and cocoa powder until well combined. Be careful not to overmix, as you want to maintain the light and airy texture of the meringue.

  5. Spoon the meringue mixture onto the prepared baking sheet, forming small mounds about 2 inches apart. You can use a piping bag or simply use two spoons to shape the cookies.

  6. Place the baking sheet in the preheated oven and bake for approximately 1.5 to 2 hours, or until the cookies are dry and crisp on the outside. The centers should still be slightly soft.

  7. Once the cookies are done baking, remove them from the oven and allow them to cool completely on a wire rack.

  8. In the meantime, melt the semisweet chocolate chips in a microwave-safe bowl, stirring every 30 seconds until smooth and melted.

  9. Once the cookies have cooled, drizzle or dip the bottoms of each cookie into the melted chocolate, allowing any excess chocolate to drip off. Place the cookies back on the wire rack to allow the chocolate to set.

  10. Once the chocolate has hardened, your chocolate meringue cookies are ready to be enjoyed! Store them in an airtight container to maintain their freshness.

Serving Suggestions

These light and airy chocolate meringue cookies are a delightful treat on their own, but they also pair perfectly with a cup of hot cocoa or a scoop of vanilla ice cream. You can even sandwich them together with a dollop of whipped cream for an extra indulgent dessert.

Final Thoughts

Chocolate meringue cookies are an elegant and sophisticated treat that will impress your friends and family. Their delicate texture and rich chocolate flavor make them a standout dessert for any occasion. Whether you’re hosting a dinner party or simply craving a sweet treat, these cookies are sure to be a hit! So go ahead, whip up a batch of these light and airy delights and indulge in a little chocolatey bliss. You deserve it!
