Hearty Slow Cooker Chicken and White Bean Chili Recipe


This hearty slow cooker chicken and white bean chili is the perfect meal for a cozy night in or a gathering with friends and family. Packed with protein, fiber, and a flavorful blend of spices, this comforting dish is sure to become a favorite in your recipe collection. The beauty of this recipe lies in its simplicity and the depth of flavors it offers. Let’s dive into the details of how to prepare this soul-warming chili.

Ingredients

  • 1.5 lbs boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 2 cans (15 oz each) white beans, drained and rinsed
  • 1 large onion, diced
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can (4 oz) diced green chilies
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1/2 teaspoon paprika
  • 1/4 teaspoon cayenne pepper (adjust to taste)
  • 4 cups low-sodium chicken broth
  • 1 cup frozen corn kernels
  • 1/4 cup fresh cilantro, chopped
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Optional toppings: shredded cheese, sour cream, avocado, lime wedges

Instructions

  1. Begin by placing the chicken breasts in the bottom of the slow cooker.

  2. Add the white beans, diced onion, minced garlic, diced green chilies, ground cumin, dried oregano, paprika, and cayenne pepper to the slow cooker.

  3. Pour the low-sodium chicken broth over the ingredients in the slow cooker, ensuring that the chicken is fully submerged.

  4. Cover the slow cooker and cook on low for 6-8 hours, or on high for 3-4 hours, until the chicken is tender and easily shreds with a fork.

  5. Once the chicken is cooked, remove it from the slow cooker and shred it using two forks. Return the shredded chicken to the slow cooker.

  6. Add the frozen corn kernels and chopped cilantro to the chili, and give everything a good stir. Allow the chili to cook for an additional 15-20 minutes to allow the flavors to meld together.

  7. Season the chili with salt and pepper to taste, and adjust the level of cayenne pepper if you prefer more heat.

  8. Serve the hearty slow cooker chicken and white bean chili in bowls, and offer optional toppings such as shredded cheese, sour cream, avocado slices, and lime wedges for a burst of freshness.

Tips for Serving and Storing

This chili is delicious when served with a side of warm cornbread or tortilla chips for dipping. Leftovers can be stored in an airtight container in the refrigerator for up to 3 days, and the flavors will continue to develop, making it even more delicious with each passing day.

Final Thoughts

The beauty of this slow cooker chicken and white bean chili is that it requires minimal effort to prepare, yet the result is a rich and satisfying meal that will warm both body and soul. Whether you’re hosting a casual gathering or simply craving a comforting dinner, this recipe is a winner. The tender chicken, creamy white beans, and aromatic spices come together to create a chili that is both hearty and wholesome. Give this recipe a try, and let its comforting flavors elevate your next mealtime experience.
