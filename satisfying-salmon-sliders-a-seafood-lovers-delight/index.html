Satisfying Salmon Sliders: A Seafood Lover's Delight


If you’re a seafood enthusiast looking for a delicious and satisfying meal, look no further than these mouthwatering salmon sliders. Packed with flavor and easy to make, these sliders are perfect for a quick lunch, a family dinner, or even a gathering with friends. The combination of succulent salmon, zesty seasonings, and a hint of freshness from the accompanying toppings creates a culinary experience that’s sure to impress. Let’s dive into the recipe and discover how to create these delectable salmon sliders that will leave everyone craving for more.

Ingredients

For the Salmon Patties:

  • 1 pound fresh salmon fillet, skin removed
  • 1/4 cup bread crumbs
  • 1/4 cup mayonnaise
  • 1 tablespoon Dijon mustard
  • 1 tablespoon fresh dill, chopped
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil for frying

For the Lemon-Dill Aioli:

  • 1/2 cup mayonnaise
  • 1 tablespoon fresh dill, chopped
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • Zest of 1 lemon
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • Salt and pepper to taste

For Assembling:

  • Slider buns or small dinner rolls, lightly toasted
  • Sliced tomatoes
  • Fresh arugula or lettuce leaves

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Salmon Patties:

    • Cut the salmon fillet into small pieces and place them in a food processor. Pulse until the salmon is finely chopped.
    • In a bowl, combine the chopped salmon, bread crumbs, mayonnaise, Dijon mustard, fresh dill, garlic powder, salt, and pepper. Mix until well combined.
    • Shape the mixture into small patties, about the size of your slider buns.

  2. Cook the Salmon Patties:

    • Heat the olive oil in a skillet over medium heat.
    • Carefully place the salmon patties in the skillet and cook for 3-4 minutes on each side, or until golden brown and cooked through.

  3. Prepare the Lemon-Dill Aioli:

    • In a small bowl, combine the mayonnaise, fresh dill, minced garlic, lemon zest, lemon juice, salt, and pepper. Mix until smooth and well incorporated.

  4. Assemble the Sliders:

    • Spread a generous amount of the lemon-dill aioli on the bottom half of each toasted slider bun.
    • Place a salmon patty on top of the aioli.
    • Add a slice of tomato and a few arugula or lettuce leaves.
    • Top with the other half of the slider bun and secure with a toothpick if needed.

Serving Suggestion

Serve these delightful salmon sliders with a side of crispy sweet potato fries or a light garden salad for a well-rounded and satisfying meal.

Final Thoughts

These salmon sliders are a celebration of seafood in every bite. The tender, flavorful salmon patties combined with the zesty lemon-dill aioli and fresh toppings create a symphony of flavors that will tantalize your taste buds. Whether you’re hosting a casual get-together or simply craving a scrumptious meal, these sliders are sure to be a hit. So, roll up your sleeves, gather your ingredients, and get ready to savor the goodness of these satisfying salmon sliders. Enjoy!
