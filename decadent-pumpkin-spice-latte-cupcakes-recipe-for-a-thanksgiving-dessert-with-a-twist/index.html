Decadent Pumpkin Spice Latte Cupcakes Recipe for a Thanksgiving Dessert with a Twist


When it comes to Thanksgiving desserts, the classic pumpkin pie has always been a staple on the table. However, if you’re looking to add a unique, indulgent twist to your dessert spread this year, look no further than these Decadent Pumpkin Spice Latte Cupcakes. Combining the warm, comforting flavors of pumpkin spice with the rich, aromatic essence of a latte, these cupcakes are sure to delight your taste buds and impress your guests. So, let’s dive into the recipe and get ready to elevate your Thanksgiving feast to a whole new level!

Ingredients

For the Cupcakes:

  • 1 3/4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 2 teaspoons pumpkin pie spice
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 cup canned pumpkin puree
  • 1/2 cup brewed espresso or strong coffee, cooled
  • 1/4 cup milk
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

For the Pumpkin Spice Latte Frosting:

  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 3-4 cups confectioners’ sugar
  • 2-3 tablespoons brewed espresso or strong coffee, cooled
  • 1 teaspoon pumpkin pie spice
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Instructions

Step 1: Preparing the Cupcake Batter

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C) and line a muffin tin with cupcake liners.
  2. In a medium bowl, whisk together the flour, baking powder, baking soda, salt, and pumpkin pie spice.
  3. In a separate large bowl, cream together the softened butter and granulated sugar until light and fluffy.
  4. Beat in the eggs, one at a time, then stir in the pumpkin puree.
  5. Combine the brewed espresso (or strong coffee), milk, and vanilla extract in a small bowl.
  6. Gradually add the dry ingredients and the coffee mixture to the wet ingredients, alternating between the two, and mix until just combined.

Step 2: Baking the Cupcakes

  1. Divide the batter evenly among the prepared cupcake liners, filling each about 2/3 full.
  2. Bake for 18-20 minutes or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.
  3. Remove the cupcakes from the oven and let them cool in the tin for 5 minutes, then transfer to a wire rack to cool completely.

Step 3: Making the Pumpkin Spice Latte Frosting

  1. In a large bowl, beat the softened butter until creamy.
  2. Gradually add the confectioners’ sugar, 1 cup at a time, until the frosting reaches your desired sweetness and consistency.
  3. Beat in the brewed espresso (or strong coffee), pumpkin pie spice, and vanilla extract until smooth and fluffy.

Step 4: Decorating the Cupcakes

  1. Once the cupcakes are completely cooled, frost them generously with the Pumpkin Spice Latte Frosting using a piping bag or a knife.
  2. For an extra touch of elegance, sprinkle a pinch of pumpkin pie spice on top of each frosted cupcake.

Serving and Enjoying

These Decadent Pumpkin Spice Latte Cupcakes are best enjoyed with a steaming cup of coffee or a glass of milk. Their rich, moist texture and the perfect balance of pumpkin and coffee flavors make them a delightful treat for Thanksgiving or any fall gathering. Whether you’re hosting a festive dinner or simply craving a cozy dessert, these cupcakes will undoubtedly steal the show and leave everyone craving for more.

So, this Thanksgiving, surprise your loved ones with a dessert that’s both familiar and unexpected. With the irresistible combination of pumpkin, spice, and latte, these cupcakes are a delicious ode to the season’s flavors. From the first bite to the last crumb, they’re guaranteed to be a hit and a memorable addition to your holiday traditions. Happy baking, and may your Thanksgiving be filled with warmth, joy, and, of course, delightful treats!
