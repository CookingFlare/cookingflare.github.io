The Ultimate Comfort Food: Chicken Parmesan Recipe


When it comes to comfort food, few dishes can compete with the classic Chicken Parmesan. This beloved Italian-American dish combines tender chicken breast, savory marinara sauce, and melted cheese, creating a symphony of flavors that warms the heart and soul. Whether you’re cooking for yourself, your family, or friends, Chicken Parmesan is sure to impress. In this post, we’ll guide you through the steps to create the ultimate Chicken Parmesan, complete with tips and variations to make it your own.

Ingredients

For the Chicken

  • 2 large boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 tablespoon water
  • 1 cup breadcrumbs (preferably Italian-style)
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • Olive oil, for frying

For the Sauce

  • 2 cups marinara sauce (store-bought or homemade)
  • 1 teaspoon olive oil
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 1/2 teaspoon red pepper flakes (optional)
  • Fresh basil leaves, for garnish

For the Topping

  • 1 1/2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Freshly grated Parmesan cheese, for finishing

Preparation Steps

Step 1: Prepare the Chicken

Start by preparing the chicken breasts. Place each chicken breast between two sheets of plastic wrap and pound them to an even thickness, about 1/2 inch thick. This ensures that the chicken cooks evenly and stays juicy. Season both sides of the chicken with salt and pepper.

Step 2: Set Up the Breading Station

To create the crispy coating, set up a breading station with three shallow dishes:

  1. In the first dish, place the flour.
  2. In the second dish, whisk together the eggs and water until well combined.
  3. In the third dish, combine the breadcrumbs, grated Parmesan cheese, garlic powder, oregano, and basil.

Step 3: Bread the Chicken

Dredge each chicken breast in the flour, shaking off any excess. Next, dip it into the egg mixture, allowing any excess to drip off. Finally, coat the chicken in the breadcrumb mixture, pressing gently to ensure the breadcrumbs adhere well. Repeat this process for all chicken breasts and set them aside on a plate.

Step 4: Cook the Chicken

In a large skillet, heat a generous amount of olive oil over medium heat. Once the oil is hot, carefully add the breaded chicken breasts, cooking in batches if necessary to avoid overcrowding the pan. Fry the chicken for about 4-5 minutes on each side, or until golden brown and cooked through (internal temperature should reach 165°F). Transfer the cooked chicken to a paper towel-lined plate to drain any excess oil.

Step 5: Prepare the Sauce

While the chicken is cooking, you can prepare the marinara sauce. In a saucepan, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the minced garlic and sauté for about 30 seconds, or until fragrant. If you like a bit of heat, add the red pepper flakes at this point. Pour in the marinara sauce and simmer for about 10 minutes to allow the flavors to meld.

Step 6: Assemble the Chicken Parmesan

Now it’s time to bring everything together. Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C). In a large baking dish, spread a thin layer of marinara sauce on the bottom. Place the fried chicken breasts on top of the sauce. Spoon additional marinara sauce over each piece of chicken, then sprinkle generously with shredded mozzarella and a little extra grated Parmesan.

Step 7: Bake

Place the baking dish in the preheated oven and bake for about 15-20 minutes, or until the cheese is bubbly and golden. If you prefer a slightly crispy top, you can broil it for an additional 2-3 minutes, but keep a close eye to prevent burning.

Step 8: Serve

Once baked, remove the Chicken Parmesan from the oven and let it rest for a few minutes. Garnish with fresh basil leaves for a burst of color and flavor. Serve hot over a bed of spaghetti, on a sub roll for a delicious sandwich, or alongside a fresh salad for a lighter option.

Tips for the Perfect Chicken Parmesan

  • Use Fresh Ingredients: Fresh herbs and high-quality cheese can elevate your dish significantly.
  • Pound Evenly: Ensure that chicken breasts are of uniform thickness for even cooking.
  • Don’t Skip the Rest: Allow the chicken to rest after baking; this helps to keep it juicy.
  • Experiment with Cheese: Feel free to mix different cheeses, such as provolone or fontina, for a unique twist.
  • Make it Ahead: You can prepare the chicken and sauce ahead of time. Just assemble and bake when ready to serve.

Variations

Chicken Parmesan with a Twist

  1. Eggplant Parmesan: Substitute the chicken with slices of eggplant for a vegetarian option.
  2. Stuffed Chicken Parmesan: Stuff the chicken breasts with spinach and ricotta cheese before breading for an added flavor dimension.
  3. Spicy Chicken Parmesan: Add diced jalapeños to the marinara sauce for a spicy kick.

Pairing Suggestions

Chicken Parmesan pairs beautifully with a variety of sides. Here are some classic accompaniments:

  • Spaghetti: A traditional pairing, toss it with olive oil, garlic, and parsley for a simple side.
  • Garlic Bread: Perfect for soaking up extra marinara sauce.
  • Salad: A fresh green salad with a light vinaigrette balances the richness of the dish.
  • Wine: A glass of Chianti or a crisp Pinot Grigio complements the flavors wonderfully.

Conclusion

Chicken Parmesan is more than just a meal; it’s an experience. The aroma of frying chicken, the comforting taste of marinara, and the gooey melted cheese all contribute to its status as the ultimate comfort food. Following this recipe, you’ll create a delicious dish that warms the heart and satisfies the soul. So, roll up your sleeves, gather your ingredients, and embark on this culinary journey that will leave your taste buds dancing with joy. Enjoy your homemade Chicken Parmesan, and don’t forget to share with loved ones—because comfort food is best enjoyed together!
