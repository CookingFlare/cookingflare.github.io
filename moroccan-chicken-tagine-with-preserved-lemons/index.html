Moroccan Chicken Tagine with Preserved Lemons


The tantalizing aroma of exotic spices, tender chicken, and tangy preserved lemons… Welcome to the world of Moroccan cuisine! Today, we’re diving into the art of creating a delightful Moroccan Chicken Tagine with Preserved Lemons. This dish is a true masterpiece, blending savory and citrusy flavors in a harmonious dance that will transport your taste buds to the bustling streets of Marrakech. Let’s embark on this culinary adventure together!

Ingredients:

  • 1 whole chicken, cut into pieces
  • 2 onions, finely chopped
  • 4 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 2 teaspoons ground ginger
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon ground coriander
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1 teaspoon ground turmeric
  • 1/2 teaspoon saffron threads
  • 1/2 cup green olives
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh cilantro
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley
  • 2 preserved lemons, cut into quarters
  • 2 cups chicken broth
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Olive oil

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the Chicken:

    • Season the chicken pieces with salt and pepper.
    • In a large tagine or a heavy-bottomed pot, heat a drizzle of olive oil over medium heat.
    • Brown the chicken pieces in batches until golden brown. Remove and set aside.

  2. Create the Flavor Base:

    • In the same pot, add a bit more olive oil if needed.
    • Sauté the onions and garlic until softened and fragrant.
    • Add the ground ginger, cumin, coriander, cinnamon, turmeric, and saffron. Stir well to combine and let the spices bloom for a minute.

  3. Braise the Chicken:

    • Return the chicken to the pot, nestling it among the fragrant spices and onions.
    • Pour in the chicken broth and bring the liquid to a gentle simmer.
    • Cover the pot and let the chicken braise for about 45 minutes, or until it’s tender and cooked through.

  4. Add the Finishing Touches:

    • Gently stir in the green olives, fresh cilantro, and parsley.
    • Carefully place the preserved lemon quarters on top of the chicken.
    • Cover the pot again and let the flavors meld together for an additional 10-15 minutes.

  5. Serve and Enjoy:

    • Once the chicken is tender and infused with all the wonderful flavors, it’s time to serve!
    • Spoon the chicken pieces, olives, and preserved lemons onto a serving platter, and drizzle the fragrant sauce over them.
    • Garnish with a sprinkle of fresh herbs for a burst of color and freshness.

A Culinary Journey to Remember

The Moroccan Chicken Tagine with Preserved Lemons is a celebration of bold spices, tender meat, and the zesty brightness of preserved lemons. Every bite is a symphony of flavors, transporting you to the colorful markets and sun-soaked landscapes of Morocco.

This dish is perfect for a special family dinner or a gathering with friends. Pair it with fluffy couscous or warm, crusty bread to soak up the exquisite sauce. The combination of savory, tangy, and fragrant elements is guaranteed to leave a lasting impression on your palate.

So, gather your ingredients, embrace the enchanting aromas, and embark on a culinary journey to the heart of Morocco with this remarkable Chicken Tagine. Let the flavors of the Maghreb captivate your senses and create unforgettable memories around the dining table. Bon appétit!
