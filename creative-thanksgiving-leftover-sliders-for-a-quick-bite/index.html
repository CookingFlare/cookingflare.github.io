Creative Thanksgiving Leftover Sliders for a Quick Bite


Ah, Thanksgiving – a time for family, gratitude, and of course, a delicious feast. But as much as we all love the big meal, sometimes the best part of Thanksgiving is the leftovers. And what better way to enjoy those leftovers than by creating something entirely new and utterly delicious? That’s where these creative Thanksgiving leftover sliders come in. These sliders are the perfect way to transform your Thanksgiving leftovers into a quick and satisfying bite that everyone will love.

Ingredients:

  • Leftover turkey, shredded or sliced
  • Cranberry sauce
  • Stuffing
  • Dinner rolls or slider buns
  • Gravy
  • Cheese (optional)
  • Butter

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the Rolls: Preheat your oven to 350°F. Slice the dinner rolls or slider buns in half and place them on a baking sheet. Brush the insides of the rolls with melted butter and toast them in the oven for 5-7 minutes until they are golden brown.

  2. Layer the Goodness: Once the rolls are toasted, it’s time to start layering the deliciousness. Begin by spreading a layer of cranberry sauce on the bottom half of each roll. Next, add a generous portion of leftover turkey on top of the cranberry sauce.

  3. Add the Stuffing: Now, it’s time to pile on the stuffing. Take a scoop of your leftover stuffing and place it on top of the turkey. If you’re feeling extra indulgent, sprinkle some shredded cheese over the stuffing for an extra creamy touch.

  4. Drizzle with Gravy: Drizzle a spoonful of warm gravy over the stuffing. The gravy will not only add moisture and flavor but also bring all the Thanksgiving flavors together in one bite.

  5. Top it Off: Finally, place the top half of the roll on the slider and gently press down to hold everything together.

  6. Serve and Enjoy: These sliders are best served warm, so gather your family and friends, and enjoy this creative twist on your Thanksgiving leftovers.

Tips and Variations:

  • If you want to add a bit of freshness to your sliders, consider adding a layer of crisp lettuce or a slice of juicy tomato.
  • For a little extra kick, spread a thin layer of mayonnaise or Dijon mustard on the top half of the rolls before assembling the sliders.
  • Feel free to experiment with different types of cheese to elevate the flavor of your sliders. From sharp cheddar to creamy brie, the possibilities are endless.

Conclusion:

Transforming your Thanksgiving leftovers into these creative sliders is not only a delicious way to enjoy the flavors of the holiday for days to come but also a convenient way to have a quick bite when you’re feeling peckish. These sliders are versatile, customizable, and a guaranteed crowd-pleaser. So, next time you find yourself with an abundance of Thanksgiving leftovers, give these sliders a try and savor every bite of the holiday goodness.

There you have it – a simple yet delicious way to reinvent your Thanksgiving leftovers. These sliders are perfect for a quick snack, a casual lunch, or even a fun dinner option. So, don’t let those Thanksgiving leftovers go to waste; instead, turn them into something truly extraordinary with these creative sliders.
