Slow Cooker Thai Coconut Recipes for Creamy and Spicy Goodness


Are you ready to take your taste buds on a flavor-packed journey to Thailand? Look no further! In this blog post, we’ll explore the wonderful world of Slow Cooker Thai Coconut Recipes that are sure to satisfy your cravings for creamy and spicy goodness. From aromatic curries to tender meats, these recipes are not only delicious but also incredibly easy to prepare. So, let’s dive right in!

Thai Red Curry with Chicken

Preparation time: 15 minutes Cooking time: 4 hours Serves: 4

Ingredients

  • 1.5 lbs boneless, skinless chicken thighs, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 1 can (13.5 oz) coconut milk
  • 2-3 tablespoons Thai red curry paste
  • 1 red bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 onion, sliced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon fish sauce
  • 1 tablespoon brown sugar
  • Juice of 1 lime
  • Fresh cilantro, for garnish

Instructions

  1. In your slow cooker, combine the coconut milk, Thai red curry paste, minced garlic, fish sauce, and brown sugar. Mix well until the ingredients are fully incorporated.
  2. Add the chicken thighs, red bell pepper, and onion to the slow cooker. Stir to coat the ingredients with the curry mixture.
  3. Cover and cook on low heat for 4 hours, or until the chicken is tender and cooked through.
  4. Just before serving, squeeze in the lime juice for a burst of freshness.
  5. Serve the Thai red curry over steamed rice or with a side of naan bread. Garnish with fresh cilantro for an extra burst of flavor.

Slow Cooker Massaman Beef Curry

Preparation time: 20 minutes Cooking time: 6 hours Serves: 6

Ingredients

  • 2 lbs beef chuck roast, cut into cubes
  • 1 can (13.5 oz) coconut milk
  • 3 tablespoons Massaman curry paste
  • 1 onion, sliced
  • 2 potatoes, peeled and cubed
  • 1 carrot, sliced
  • 2 tablespoons brown sugar
  • 2 tablespoons fish sauce
  • 1 tablespoon tamarind paste
  • 1 cinnamon stick
  • 2 bay leaves
  • Fresh cilantro, for garnish

Instructions

  1. In your slow cooker, combine the coconut milk, Massaman curry paste, brown sugar, fish sauce, tamarind paste, cinnamon stick, and bay leaves. Stir until the ingredients are well combined.
  2. Add the beef, onion, potatoes, and carrot to the slow cooker. Mix well to coat the ingredients with the curry mixture.
  3. Cover and cook on low heat for 6 hours, or until the beef is tender and the flavors have melded together.
  4. Remove the cinnamon stick and bay leaves before serving.
  5. Serve the Massaman beef curry over steamed jasmine rice. Garnish with fresh cilantro for a pop of color and freshness.

Slow Cooker Thai Coconut Sticky Rice with Mango

Preparation time: 10 minutes Cooking time: 2 hours Serves: 4

Ingredients

  • 1 cup glutinous rice (sticky rice), soaked for 2 hours
  • 1 can (13.5 oz) coconut milk
  • 1/4 cup sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 2 ripe mangoes, sliced
  • Toasted sesame seeds, for garnish

Instructions

  1. Drain the soaked sticky rice and place it in your slow cooker.
  2. In a separate bowl, combine the coconut milk, sugar, and salt. Stir until the sugar has dissolved.
  3. Pour the coconut milk mixture over the sticky rice in the slow cooker. Stir gently to ensure the rice is evenly coated.
  4. Cover and cook on low heat for 2 hours, or until the rice is tender and has absorbed most of the coconut milk.
  5. Serve the coconut sticky rice with slices of ripe mango on top. Sprinkle toasted sesame seeds for added crunch and flavor.

Conclusion

There you have it, three incredible Slow Cooker Thai Coconut Recipes that will transport you to the vibrant streets of Thailand. Whether you’re in the mood for a rich and fragrant curry or a sweet and satisfying dessert, these recipes have got you covered. So, grab your slow cooker and get ready to indulge in the creamy and spicy goodness of Thai cuisine. Bon appétit!
