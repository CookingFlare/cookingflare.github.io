Vegan Soups of the Season: 10 Warm & Cozy Recipes for Gathering & Sharing


As the autumn chill sets in, we find ourselves drawn to warm and cozy meals that gather family and friends to share comforting conversations and stories. Soups are an excellent way to bring us together, and in this post, we will explore ten delightful vegan recipes to nourish your soul and honor the flavors of the season. Get ready for some scrumptious and satisfying dishes that everyone will enjoy.

  1. Roasted Sweet Potato and Apple Soup

Welcome the sweetness of the season into your home with this delicious soup. Begin by preheating your oven to 400°F (200°C) and roasting cubed sweet potatoes and chopped apples with a bit of olive oil, cinnamon, and salt till tender. Saute some onion and garlic, then add the roasted sweet potatoes, apples, and vegetable broth to a large pot. Puree until smooth, adjust seasoning with salt and pepper, and enjoy!

  1. Hearty Winter Vegetable Soup

Hearty, robust, and filling, this soup will provide warmth and comfort on a chilly day. Start by sautéing sliced celery, diced onions, minced garlic, and chopped carrots in a large pot with some olive oil. Add vegetable broth, sliced mushrooms, and chopped kale. Bring to a simmer and let it cook until the vegetables are tender. For added flavor, consider throwing in some dried herbs like thyme or rosemary. Puree a portion of the soup for a creamy consistency if desired.

  1. Creamy Cauliflower and Chickpea Soup

Don’t underestimate the power of cauliflower – in this soup, it transforms into a velvety, rich substitute for heavy cream. Boil florets till tender, then blend them with cooked chickpeas, vegetable broth, sauteed onions and garlic, and a pinch of cumin. Mix well in a pot and simmer for a few minutes before seasoning with salt and pepper. Finish with some fresh lemon juice and a sprinkle of paprika for added flavor.

  1. Spiced Red Lentil and Carrot Soup

The warm spices balance the natural sweetness of this vegan soup, making it perfect for colder nights. Begin by sautéing diced onions, minced garlic, and grated ginger in a pot with olive oil. Add grated carrots and red lentils, then pour in vegetable broth and spices like cumin, turmeric, and cayenne pepper. Cook until lentils are tender, adjust seasoning with salt and pepper, and finish with a squeeze of lemon juice and a sprinkle of chopped fresh cilantro.

  1. Butternut Squash and Sage Soup

Rich in creamy squash flavor and aromatic sage, this soup is a taste of fall in every spoonful. Begin by roasting cubed butternut squash until tender, then saute sliced onions and minced garlic till golden brown. Add roasted squash, vegetable broth, and chopped fresh sage to a large pot, then blend until smooth. Season with salt and pepper, garnish with a drizzle of olive oil and additional chopped sage, and serve warm.

  1. Vegan Sweet Corn Chowder

Recreate the flavors of summer with this fresh corn chowder. Begin by sautéing sliced onions and minced garlic in a pot with olive oil. Add chopped potatoes and cook for a few more minutes before pouring in vegetable broth. Bring to a simmer and add corn kernels - either fresh or canned. Cook until potatoes are tender, then blend a potion of the soup and combine everything back in the pot. Stir in non-dairy creamer or yogurt, season with salt and pepper, and enjoy this comforting dish.

  1. Tomato Basil Soup

A classic for a reason, this vegan tomato soup is aromatic, comforting, and satisfying. Sauté minced garlic and chopped onions in a pot with olive oil, add chopped tomatoes, and let it simmer for 10-15 minutes. Stir in fresh basil leaves, vegetable broth, and spices like dried oregano and red pepper flakes. Cook until tomatoes are tender and season with salt and pepper. For a richer flavor, blend a potion of the soup and return it to the pot with some non-dairy creamer or yogurt.

  1. Vegan Spinach and Potato Soup

Rich in texture and flavor, this soup is a delightful fusion of seasonal potatoes and hearty spinach. Boil cubed potatoes till tender, then blend them with sautéed onions, minced garlic, and vegetable broth. Add spinach, non-dairy milk, and spices like turmeric and nutmeg. Cook until spinach is tender, adjust seasoning with salt and pepper, and finish with some chopped scallions.

  1. Vegan Pumpkin Soup with Coconut

Warm up with this exotic twist on a classic favorite. Sauté chopped onions, minced garlic, and grated ginger with olive oil in a pot. Add grated pumpkin, vegetable broth, and simmer for about 20 minutes. Stir in light coconut milk and fresh cilantro, blend until smooth, and season with salt, pepper, and a squeeze of lime juice.

  1. Vegan Mushroom Barley Soup

Earthy, filling, and satisfying, this mushroom soup will delight your taste buds. Sauté sliced mushrooms, chopped onions, and minced garlic in a pot with olive oil, then add barley and vegetable broth. Cook until barley and mushrooms are tender, adjusting the seasoning as needed. For an added flavor dimension, try adding some cooked chopped greens at the end like spinach or kale.

So gather your loved ones around the dinner table and share these vegan soups of the season. Cozy up with a block of cheese, some crusty bread, or a hearty sandwich to make the meal complete. Most importantly, enjoy the warmth and comfort these flavorful blends provide on a chilly evening. Whatever your preference, there’s a vegan soup to match every taste, so prepare to encounter new favorites and rediscover the joy of this humble dish!
