Creamy Instant Pot Potato Leek Soup Recipe


If you’re in the mood for a comforting and delicious soup that’s easy to make, then this Creamy Instant Pot Potato Leek Soup is the perfect recipe for you. With the magic of the Instant Pot, you can have this hearty and creamy soup ready in no time, making it an ideal option for a quick weeknight dinner or a cozy weekend meal.

Ingredients

  • 4 large leeks, white and light green parts only, sliced
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 4 large potatoes, peeled and diced
  • 4 cups vegetable or chicken broth
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 1 bay leaf
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Chopped chives or parsley for garnish

Instructions

  1. Start by preparing the leeks. Slice off the dark green tops and the root ends, then cut the leeks in half lengthwise. Rinse them under cold water to remove any dirt or grit, then slice them into half-moons.

  2. Set your Instant Pot to the sauté function and melt the butter. Add the sliced leeks and minced garlic, and sauté for a few minutes until they soften and become fragrant.

  3. Add the diced potatoes, bay leaf, and broth to the pot. Close the lid and set the Instant Pot to cook at high pressure for 8 minutes.

  4. Once the cooking time is complete, allow for a natural pressure release for 10 minutes before carefully venting any remaining pressure. Open the lid and discard the bay leaf.

  5. Using an immersion blender, blend the soup until smooth and creamy. If you don’t have an immersion blender, you can carefully transfer the soup to a traditional blender in batches to blend until smooth.

  6. Stir in the heavy cream and season the soup with salt and pepper to taste. If the soup is too thick, you can add a little more broth or cream to achieve your desired consistency.

  7. Ladle the creamy potato leek soup into bowls and garnish with chopped chives or parsley for a pop of color and freshness.

Tips and Variations

  • For a dairy-free version, you can substitute the heavy cream with coconut cream for a lusciously creamy texture.
  • Add a sprinkle of crispy bacon or croutons on top for a delightful crunch.
  • If you prefer a chunkier soup, you can reserve some of the cooked potatoes before blending and stir them back into the soup before serving.

Final Thoughts

This Creamy Instant Pot Potato Leek Soup is a versatile and comforting dish that’s perfect for any occasion. Whether you’re craving a warm bowl of soup on a chilly day or need a simple and satisfying meal, this recipe delivers on flavor and ease of preparation. The creamy texture and delicate flavors of the leeks and potatoes make this soup a standout dish that will surely become a favorite in your repertoire. So, fire up your Instant Pot and treat yourself to a bowl of this creamy goodness!

Whether you’re a seasoned home cook or just starting your culinary journey, this recipe is a must-try for anyone looking for a delicious and hassle-free meal. So, gather your ingredients, grab your Instant Pot, and get ready to enjoy a bowl of pure comfort with this Creamy Instant Pot Potato Leek Soup.
