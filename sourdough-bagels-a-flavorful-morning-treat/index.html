Sourdough Bagels: A Flavorful Morning Treat


Starting your day with a delicious, homemade breakfast treat can make all the difference. Enter: the delectable and doughy world of sourdough bagels. Sourdough bagels are an elevated morning food that combines the tangy goodness of sourdough with the chewy texture of bagels, all packed into a perfect, bite-sized bundle. Are you ready to take your breakfast game to the next level? Let’s dive in!

Ingredients:

  • 200g active sourdough starter
  • 500g bread flour
  • 1 tbsp sugar
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tbsp active dry yeast
  • 2 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 1 cup warm water (120° F / 49° C)

Instructions:

  1. In a large mixing bowl, combine the active sourdough starter, warm water, sugar, and yeast. Stir well to create a smooth and slightly thick batter. Allow this mixture to rest for about 10 minutes, or until the yeast begins to froth.

  2. In another large mixing bowl, combine the bread flour and salt. Gradually pour the batter into the flour mixture and knead. Add the vegetable oil and continue kneading. You should end up with a soft, pliable dough that easily comes together.

  3. Once your dough is formed, cover it with a damp towel and place it in a warm spot for about 45 minutes to an hour. This is called proofing, and it allows the yeast to do its magic, causing the dough to rise and become light and fluffy.

  4. Preheat your oven to 425° F / 218° C and prepare a rack for baking. Once your dough has finished proofing, divide it into six even sections.

  5. Roll each portion into a ball and then stretch it into the classic bagel shape – about 2 inches (5 cm) wide and 1 inch (2.5 cm) thick.

  6. At this point, you can opt to add any toppings you desire – herbs, seeds, or flavored salts. Simply sprinkle them onto the top of your bagels before moving on to the next step.

  7. Place the shaped bagels onto the prepared baking rack, leaving enough space between each to accommodate expansion during baking.

  8. Bake the bagels in the preheated oven for 20-25 minutes, turning halfway through to ensure even browning. When done, they should be golden brown and sound hollow when tapped on the bottom.

  9. Remove the bagels from the oven and transfer them onto a wire rack to cool for about 10 minutes. While still warm, enjoy your freshly baked sourdough bagels slathered with your favorite cream cheese.

Variations:

Feeling adventurous? Try adding different flavors to your sourdough bagels for a unique twist. Here are a few ideas:

  • Caramelized onion and balsamic reduction
  • Everything bagel seasoning
  • Roasted garlic and parmesan
  • Rosemary and sea salt
  • Savory pumpkin seed and thyme

Conclusion:

Sourdough bagels bring a breath of fresh dough to your breakfast routine. Not only are they tasty and versatile, but they’re also playful and fun to make. So go ahead and invite your friends and family to join in the bagel-making process – after all, the best ingredients are shared joy, love, and laughter. Remember, homemade sourdough bagels can be enjoyed anytime, so don’t limit them to your morning meal – they make a perfect snack as well. Happy baking!
