Easy and Delicious Christmas Breakfast Casserole Recipe


Are you looking for a mouthwatering breakfast dish to kick off your Christmas morning? Look no further! This Easy and Delicious Christmas Breakfast Casserole Recipe is the perfect way to start your day with a festive and satisfying meal. Packed with flavor and simple to prepare, this casserole will become a holiday favorite for years to come.

Ingredients

  • 8 slices of bread, crusts removed
  • 1 pound (450g) of sausage, cooked and crumbled
  • 2 cups (200g) of shredded cheddar cheese
  • 6 large eggs
  • 2 cups (480ml) of milk
  • 1 teaspoon of Dijon mustard
  • 1/2 teaspoon of salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon of black pepper
  • 2 green onions, thinly sliced

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease a 9x13-inch (23x33cm) baking dish and set aside.

  2. Cut the bread slices into small cubes and spread them evenly across the bottom of the prepared baking dish. Sprinkle the cooked sausage over the bread cubes, followed by the shredded cheddar cheese.

  3. In a large mixing bowl, whisk together the eggs, milk, Dijon mustard, salt, and black pepper until well combined. Pour the egg mixture over the bread, sausage, and cheese in the baking dish. Gently press down on the ingredients to ensure they are fully submerged in the egg mixture.

  4. Sprinkle the sliced green onions over the top of the casserole. Cover the dish with aluminum foil and refrigerate for at least 4 hours or overnight. This allows the flavors to meld and the bread to soak up the egg mixture.

  5. When you’re ready to bake the casserole, remove it from the refrigerator and let it sit at room temperature for 15-20 minutes. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C).

  6. Once the casserole has come to room temperature, place it in the preheated oven and bake for 45-50 minutes, or until the top is golden brown and the center is set. You can test the doneness by inserting a knife into the center of the casserole – if it comes out clean, it’s ready!

  7. Remove the casserole from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes before serving. Cut into squares and serve warm. This casserole pairs perfectly with a side of fresh fruit or a cup of hot coffee, making it a complete and satisfying Christmas morning breakfast.

Tips and Variations

  • Feel free to customize this casserole to your taste preferences. Add in some diced bell peppers, mushrooms, or spinach for added flavor and nutrition.
  • If you prefer a meat-free version, simply omit the sausage and add in some sautéed vegetables instead.
  • To make this casserole ahead of time, prepare it up until step 4 and then cover and refrigerate overnight. The next morning, proceed with the remaining steps.
  • Leftovers can be stored in the refrigerator for up to 3 days. Simply reheat in the microwave or oven before enjoying.

There you have it – an Easy and Delicious Christmas Breakfast Casserole Recipe that is sure to impress your loved ones and make your Christmas morning extra special. This hearty and flavorful casserole is a breeze to prepare, allowing you more time to relax and enjoy the holiday festivities. Give it a try this Christmas and be prepared for everyone to ask for seconds!
