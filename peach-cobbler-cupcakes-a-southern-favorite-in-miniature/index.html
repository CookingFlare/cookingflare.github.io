Peach Cobbler Cupcakes: A Southern Favorite in Miniature


If you’re a fan of traditional Southern desserts, you’re in for a treat with these delightful Peach Cobbler Cupcakes. This recipe takes the classic flavors of peach cobbler and transforms them into individual, bite-sized treats that are perfect for any occasion. These cupcakes are a wonderful blend of sweet, fruity, and comforting flavors, making them a hit at potlucks, picnics, or simply as a special treat for yourself.

Ingredients

For the Cupcakes:

  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/2 cup milk

For the Peach Filling:

  • 2 cups fresh or canned peaches, diced
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1 tablespoon cornstarch
  • 2 tablespoons water

For the Streusel Topping:

  • 1/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 2 tablespoons unsalted butter, melted
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C) and line a cupcake pan with paper liners.

  2. In a medium bowl, whisk together the flour, baking powder, and salt for the cupcakes.

  3. In a separate large bowl, cream together the butter and sugar until light and fluffy. Add the eggs one at a time, beating well after each addition. Stir in the vanilla extract.

  4. Gradually add the dry ingredients to the creamed mixture, alternating with the milk. Mix until just combined.

  5. Fill each cupcake liner about halfway with the batter.

  6. To make the peach filling, combine the peaches, sugar, and cinnamon in a saucepan over medium heat. In a small bowl, mix together the cornstarch and water, then add the mixture to the peaches. Cook, stirring constantly, until the mixture thickens. Remove from heat and let it cool.

  7. Spoon a small amount of the peach filling onto the center of each cupcake batter.

  8. In a small bowl, mix together the streusel topping ingredients until crumbly. Sprinkle the streusel over the peach filling on each cupcake.

  9. Bake the cupcakes for 20-25 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the cake portion comes out clean.

  10. Let the cupcakes cool in the pan for 10 minutes, then transfer them to a wire rack to cool completely.

Serving Suggestions

These Peach Cobbler Cupcakes are delicious on their own, but you can take them to the next level by serving them with a dollop of whipped cream or a scoop of vanilla ice cream. They are best enjoyed fresh, but you can store any leftovers in an airtight container at room temperature for up to 2 days.
