Slow Cooker Thai Peanut Recipes for a Nutty Twist


Are you a fan of Thai cuisine? Do you love the rich and bold flavors that come with it? If you’re nodding your head, then you’re in for a treat! In this blog post, we will be exploring some delicious Slow Cooker Thai Peanut recipes that will give your taste buds a nutty twist. From creamy curries to tender satay, get ready to embark on a culinary journey that will leave you craving for more.

Thai Peanut Curry

Let’s start with a classic Thai Peanut Curry. This slow cooker recipe is perfect for those busy days when you want a flavorful meal without spending hours in the kitchen. The combination of creamy peanut butter, coconut milk, and aromatic spices creates a harmonious flavor profile that will transport you straight to the streets of Thailand.

To make this mouthwatering curry, you will need:

  • 2 pounds of boneless chicken, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 1 onion, thinly sliced
  • 3 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 red bell pepper, thinly sliced
  • 1 can of coconut milk
  • 1/2 cup of peanut butter
  • 2 tablespoons of red curry paste
  • 2 tablespoons of soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon of brown sugar
  • 1 teaspoon of ground ginger
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh cilantro for garnish

Instructions:

  1. In the slow cooker, combine the chicken, onion, garlic, and red bell pepper.
  2. In a separate bowl, whisk together the coconut milk, peanut butter, red curry paste, soy sauce, brown sugar, ginger, salt, and pepper until well combined.
  3. Pour the peanut sauce mixture over the chicken and vegetables in the slow cooker.
  4. Stir everything together to ensure the chicken and vegetables are coated in the sauce.
  5. Cover the slow cooker and cook on low for 6-8 hours or on high for 3-4 hours, until the chicken is tender and cooked through.
  6. Serve the Thai Peanut Curry over steamed rice, garnished with fresh cilantro.

Thai Peanut Satay

If you’re a fan of grilled skewers, then you’ll love this Slow Cooker Thai Peanut Satay recipe. It’s a fantastic option for parties or gatherings, as it’s easy to prepare and sure to impress your guests. The combination of tender chicken or beef, marinated in a flavorful peanut sauce, is a match made in heaven.

To make this delectable satay, you will need:

  • 1 pound of chicken or beef, thinly sliced
  • 1/2 cup of peanut butter
  • 1/4 cup of soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons of honey
  • 2 tablespoons of lime juice
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 teaspoon of ground coriander
  • 1/2 teaspoon of ground cumin
  • Bamboo skewers, soaked in water for at least 30 minutes

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, whisk together the peanut butter, soy sauce, honey, lime juice, garlic, coriander, and cumin until well combined.
  2. Add the sliced chicken or beef to the bowl and toss to coat the meat in the marinade.
  3. Cover the bowl and let the meat marinate in the refrigerator for at least 2 hours, or overnight for best results.
  4. Thread the marinated meat onto the soaked bamboo skewers.
  5. Place the skewers in the slow cooker, making sure they are evenly spaced.
  6. Cover the slow cooker and cook on low for 4-6 hours or on high for 2-3 hours, until the meat is tender and cooked through.
  7. Serve the Thai Peanut Satay with a side of peanut sauce for dipping.

Conclusion

There you have it, two incredible Slow Cooker Thai Peanut recipes that will take your taste buds on a flavor-packed adventure. Whether you prefer the creamy indulgence of the Thai Peanut Curry or the grilled perfection of the Thai Peanut Satay, these recipes are sure to impress. So dust off your slow cooker, gather your ingredients, and get ready to enjoy a nutty twist on your favorite Thai dishes.

Remember to experiment with the recipes and make them your own. Add some extra vegetables, adjust the spice level to your liking, and don’t be afraid to get creative in the kitchen. Happy cooking!
