Indian Palak Paneer Recipe with Spinach and Cheese


Palak Paneer is a classic Indian dish that combines the vibrant flavors of spinach and the creamy richness of paneer cheese. This vegetarian recipe is not only delicious but also packed with nutrients, making it a perfect choice for a healthy and satisfying meal. In this blog post, I will guide you through the step-by-step process of preparing this delectable dish.

Ingredients

To make Palak Paneer, you will need the following ingredients:

  • 2 cups of fresh spinach leaves
  • 200 grams of paneer cheese, cubed
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, pureed
  • 2 green chilies, slit lengthwise
  • 1-inch piece of ginger, grated
  • 4-5 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 1 teaspoon garam masala
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon red chili powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon coriander powder
  • 1/4 cup of heavy cream
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 tablespoons of oil or ghee for cooking

Instructions

  1. Start by blanching the spinach leaves. Bring a pot of water to a boil and add the spinach leaves. Let them cook for about 2 minutes until they wilt. Drain the spinach and immediately transfer it to a bowl of ice water to retain its vibrant green color. Once cooled, blend the spinach into a smooth puree using a blender or food processor.

  2. Heat oil or ghee in a pan over medium heat. Add the cumin seeds and let them splutter. Then, add the chopped onions and sauté until they turn golden brown.

  3. Add the grated ginger and minced garlic to the pan and sauté for another minute until the raw smell disappears.

  4. Now, add the pureed tomatoes and green chilies to the pan. Cook the mixture for about 5 minutes until the tomatoes are well cooked and the oil starts to separate.

  5. Add the turmeric powder, red chili powder, and coriander powder to the pan. Mix well and cook for another minute to allow the spices to release their flavors.

  6. Stir in the spinach puree and cook for about 5 minutes, allowing the flavors to meld together. Add salt to taste.

  7. Gently add the cubed paneer cheese to the pan and mix it with the spinach mixture. Be careful not to break the paneer cubes.

  8. Finally, pour in the heavy cream and sprinkle the garam masala over the dish. Stir well and let the Palak Paneer simmer for a few minutes until the flavors have fully infused.

  9. Serve the Palak Paneer hot with naan or rice. Garnish it with some fresh cilantro leaves for an added burst of flavor.

Conclusion

Palak Paneer is a delightful dish that showcases the vibrant colors and flavors of Indian cuisine. The combination of spinach and paneer cheese creates a harmonious blend of textures and tastes. This recipe is not only a treat for your taste buds but also a nutritious choice, as spinach is rich in iron, vitamins, and antioxidants.

Next time you’re craving a vegetarian delight, give this Palak Paneer recipe a try. Its creamy, spinach-infused goodness will surely leave you craving for more. Enjoy this classic Indian dish with your loved ones and savor the flavors of India right in your own kitchen.
