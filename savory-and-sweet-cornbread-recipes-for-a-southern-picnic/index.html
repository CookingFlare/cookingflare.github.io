Savory and Sweet Cornbread Recipes for a Southern Picnic


Are you planning a delightful Southern picnic and in need of some crowd-pleasing recipes? Look no further! In this blog post, we’ll explore two delicious cornbread recipes—savory and sweet—that are sure to impress your guests. So, grab your apron and get ready to whip up some mouthwatering treats.

Savory Cornbread

Let’s start with a classic savory cornbread recipe that perfectly complements any picnic spread. Here’s what you’ll need:

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup cornmeal
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 tablespoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup buttermilk
  • 1/4 cup vegetable oil
  • 2 tablespoons honey
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/2 cup grated cheddar cheese
  • 1/4 cup chopped green onions

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C) and grease a 9-inch square baking pan.
  2. In a large bowl, combine the cornmeal, flour, baking powder, and salt.
  3. In a separate bowl, whisk together the buttermilk, vegetable oil, honey, and eggs.
  4. Pour the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients and stir until just combined.
  5. Fold in the grated cheddar cheese and chopped green onions.
  6. Pour the batter into the prepared baking pan and smooth the top.
  7. Bake for 20-25 minutes or until the top is golden brown and a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.
  8. Allow the cornbread to cool for a few minutes before cutting it into squares.
  9. Serve warm and enjoy the irresistible, savory flavor.

Sweet Cornbread

Now, let’s move on to a delectable sweet cornbread recipe that will satisfy your sweet tooth. Here are the ingredients you’ll need:

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup cornmeal
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 tablespoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1/4 cup vegetable oil
  • 1/4 cup honey
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 2 eggs

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C) and grease a 9-inch square baking pan.
  2. In a large bowl, combine the cornmeal, flour, baking powder, and salt.
  3. In a separate bowl, whisk together the milk, vegetable oil, honey, sugar, and eggs.
  4. Pour the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients and stir until just combined.
  5. Pour the batter into the prepared baking pan and smooth the top.
  6. Bake for 20-25 minutes or until the top is golden brown and a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.
  7. Allow the cornbread to cool for a few minutes before cutting it into squares.
  8. Serve with a drizzle of honey or a dollop of whipped cream for an extra touch of sweetness.

Final Thoughts

Whether you prefer savory or sweet, these cornbread recipes are guaranteed to be a hit at your Southern picnic. The savory cornbread’s cheesy goodness and the sweet cornbread’s delightful sweetness will leave your guests craving for more. So, gather your loved ones, head outdoors, and enjoy a delightful Southern picnic with these scrumptious cornbread treats.

Remember, cornbread is versatile, so feel free to add your own twist by incorporating ingredients like jalapeños, bacon, or even blueberries. The possibilities are endless! Happy picnicking and happy cooking!
