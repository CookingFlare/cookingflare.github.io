Instant Pot Chicken Tacos: A Flavorful and Easy Recipe


When it comes to weeknight dinners, nothing beats the ease and convenience of an Instant Pot. And if you’re craving a meal that is not only quick but also bursting with flavor, Instant Pot chicken tacos are the answer. Whether you’re looking to prepare a delicious meal for your family or want to impress guests with minimal effort, this recipe will have you covered.

Instant Pot chicken tacos are a perfect combination of juicy, tender chicken, zesty spices, and the convenience of cooking everything in one pot. The Instant Pot ensures that the chicken is cooked to perfection, allowing you to easily shred it and assemble your tacos in no time. In this post, we will guide you through the entire process step by step, from preparing the ingredients to assembling your tacos. Plus, we will share some tips and variations to make your chicken tacos even more flavorful.

Why Choose Instant Pot for Chicken Tacos?

The Instant Pot is a game-changer when it comes to cooking. It’s a versatile kitchen appliance that combines multiple cooking methods, such as pressure cooking, slow cooking, sautéing, and even yogurt making. This means you can prepare meals in a fraction of the time compared to traditional cooking methods. For chicken tacos, the Instant Pot excels at tenderizing the meat while infusing it with flavor in a short amount of time.

By cooking the chicken in the Instant Pot, it locks in the moisture, making sure the chicken is juicy and easy to shred. Moreover, using the Instant Pot means less clean-up, as everything is cooked in one pot, making this a perfect recipe for busy weeknights or meal prep.

Ingredients for Instant Pot Chicken Tacos

To make this recipe, you’ll need simple ingredients that you can easily find at your local grocery store. Here’s what you’ll need:

For the Chicken

  • Boneless, skinless chicken breasts or thighs – Chicken thighs tend to be juicier and more flavorful, but chicken breasts work just as well for a leaner option.
  • Taco seasoning – You can use store-bought taco seasoning or make your own using a combination of chili powder, cumin, paprika, garlic powder, onion powder, oregano, and salt.
  • Olive oil – To sauté the chicken and vegetables at the beginning.
  • Chicken broth – Adds moisture and flavor while the chicken cooks. You can also use water, but chicken broth will elevate the taste.
  • Lime juice – A splash of fresh lime juice enhances the flavor and adds brightness to the dish.
  • Garlic – Fresh minced garlic will add aromatic flavor to the chicken.

For the Tacos

  • Taco shells – You can use soft flour tortillas or crispy corn taco shells. The choice is yours!
  • Toppings – A variety of toppings will make your tacos even more delicious. Here are some popular options:
    • Shredded lettuce
    • Diced tomatoes
    • Chopped onions
    • Avocado or guacamole
    • Shredded cheese (cheddar, Mexican blend, or cotija)
    • Sour cream or Greek yogurt
    • Fresh cilantro
    • Salsa or pico de gallo
    • Jalapeño slices for an extra kick

Feel free to mix and match these toppings based on your preference. The more, the merrier when it comes to taco toppings!

Step-by-Step Instructions for Instant Pot Chicken Tacos

Step 1: Prepare the Chicken

Start by seasoning your chicken with taco seasoning. If you’re using store-bought seasoning, ensure the chicken is evenly coated with a generous amount. If you prefer to make your own seasoning, combine chili powder, cumin, paprika, garlic powder, onion powder, oregano, and salt in a bowl, and rub it over the chicken. The seasoning will infuse the chicken with bold flavors, creating the base for your tacos.

Step 2: Sauté the Chicken

Turn on the Instant Pot and set it to the “Sauté” mode. Add a tablespoon of olive oil to the pot. Once the oil is hot, add the seasoned chicken breasts or thighs and sear them for 2-3 minutes on each side. This step is optional, but it helps develop a nice golden crust on the chicken, enhancing the flavor. After searing, remove the chicken from the Instant Pot and set it aside.

Step 3: Sauté the Aromatics

With the chicken removed, add a little more olive oil to the Instant Pot if necessary. Add the minced garlic and sauté for about 30 seconds until fragrant. Be careful not to burn the garlic, as it can become bitter. The garlic will add a delicious savory note to the dish.

Step 4: Pressure Cook the Chicken

Return the seared chicken to the Instant Pot and pour in the chicken broth. Scrape the bottom of the pot with a wooden spoon to release any browned bits stuck to the bottom, as this adds rich flavor to the sauce. Close the lid of the Instant Pot, set the valve to “Sealing,” and cook on “Manual” or “Pressure Cook” mode for 10 minutes for chicken breasts, or 12 minutes for chicken thighs.

Once the cooking time is up, allow the Instant Pot to naturally release pressure for 5 minutes. After that, carefully switch the valve to “Venting” to release the remaining pressure.

Step 5: Shred the Chicken

Once the pressure has fully released, open the Instant Pot and remove the chicken. Using two forks, shred the chicken right in the pot, stirring it in with the broth and seasonings. The chicken will absorb all the flavors, and the result will be incredibly tender and juicy.

Step 6: Assemble Your Tacos

Now that your chicken is ready, it’s time to assemble your tacos! Warm your taco shells in the oven or on a stovetop skillet for a few seconds. Then, fill each shell with the shredded chicken, and top with your favorite taco toppings.

Some great taco combinations include:

  • A classic combo of shredded lettuce, diced tomatoes, shredded cheese, and sour cream.
  • A fresh and zesty option with avocado, cilantro, and a squeeze of lime.
  • A spicy version with jalapeño slices and salsa.

Feel free to get creative and add any other toppings you like.

Tips and Variations for Perfect Chicken Tacos

1. Use Chicken Thighs for Extra Flavor

While chicken breasts are a great option, using chicken thighs will result in more flavorful and juicy meat. The higher fat content in chicken thighs makes them perfect for slow cooking or pressure cooking, ensuring that the chicken remains tender and moist.

2. Make It Spicy

If you love spice, feel free to add some heat to your Instant Pot chicken tacos. You can sprinkle in a little cayenne pepper or chili flakes while seasoning the chicken. If you prefer fresh heat, top your tacos with diced jalapeños or a drizzle of hot sauce.

3. Make Your Own Taco Seasoning

Store-bought taco seasoning packets can be convenient, but making your own allows you to control the flavor and avoid unnecessary preservatives. A simple homemade taco seasoning blend typically includes chili powder, cumin, garlic powder, onion powder, paprika, and a pinch of salt and pepper. Store the mixture in an airtight jar for future use.

4. Experiment with Toppings

While classic toppings like shredded lettuce, cheese, and sour cream are always a hit, there are many other creative options to explore. Try adding a dollop of guacamole, a spoonful of salsa, or a sprinkle of crumbled feta cheese for a Mediterranean twist. Pickled onions or even grilled corn can also add a unique touch to your tacos.

5. Make It a Salad

If you’re in the mood for something lighter, you can turn your Instant Pot chicken tacos into a salad. Simply skip the taco shells and serve the shredded chicken on a bed of lettuce, topped with your favorite taco toppings. Add some crushed tortilla chips for crunch and drizzle with a creamy dressing, like ranch or cilantro-lime dressing.

6. Double the Recipe for Meal Prep

This Instant Pot chicken taco recipe is perfect for meal prepping. Double the recipe and store the leftover chicken in an airtight container in the fridge. You can use the shredded chicken in salads, wraps, or even as a topping for nachos or quesadillas throughout the week.

7. Add Vegetables

Want to make your chicken tacos even healthier? Add some vegetables to the mix! You can sauté bell peppers, onions, and zucchini in the Instant Pot before adding the chicken, or even toss in some corn and black beans to make your tacos more hearty and flavorful.

Conclusion

Instant Pot chicken tacos are a simple, delicious, and customizable recipe that will make your weeknight dinners a breeze. With just a few ingredients, you can create a flavorful meal that’s sure to please everyone at the table. Whether you prefer classic toppings or enjoy experimenting with new flavors, these chicken tacos are the perfect base for a variety of delicious combinations.

With the help of the Instant Pot, you can save time while still enjoying tender, juicy chicken that’s full of flavor. So next time you’re craving tacos, give this Instant Pot chicken taco recipe a try – it’s quick, easy, and, most importantly, incredibly tasty.
