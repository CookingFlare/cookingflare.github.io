The Best Pie Recipes for Your Next Family Gathering


Pies have a special place in the hearts of many, especially during family gatherings. There’s something about the aroma of a freshly baked pie wafting through the house that brings everyone together, evoking memories of cherished moments. Whether you prefer fruity fillings, creamy custards, or savory delights, pies can cater to all tastes. In this post, we will explore a variety of delectable pie recipes that are sure to impress your family and friends at your next gathering.

Classic Apple Pie

Ingredients

  • 6 to 8 cups of sliced, peeled apples (Granny Smith or Honeycrisp work best)
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground nutmeg
  • 2 tablespoons lemon juice
  • 1 tablespoon butter
  • 1 package of refrigerated pie crusts (or homemade if you prefer)
  • 1 egg (for egg wash)

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 425°F (220°C).
  2. In a large bowl, combine the sliced apples, sugar, flour, cinnamon, nutmeg, and lemon juice. Toss until the apples are evenly coated.
  3. Roll out one pie crust and fit it into a 9-inch pie pan. Pour the apple filling into the crust and dot with butter.
  4. Roll out the second crust and place it over the filling. Cut slits in the top crust to allow steam to escape. You can also create a lattice design for a beautiful presentation.
  5. Brush the top crust with a whisked egg for a golden finish.
  6. Bake for 15 minutes in the preheated oven, then reduce the temperature to 350°F (175°C) and continue baking for 35-45 minutes, or until the apples are tender and the crust is golden brown.
  7. Allow the pie to cool before serving with a scoop of vanilla ice cream.

Rich Chocolate Cream Pie

Ingredients

  • 1 pre-baked 9-inch pie crust
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/3 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1/4 cup cornstarch
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 2 3/4 cups whole milk
  • 2 tablespoons unsalted butter
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 cup heavy whipping cream
  • 2 tablespoons powdered sugar (for whipped cream topping)

Instructions

  1. In a medium saucepan, whisk together sugar, cocoa powder, cornstarch, and salt. Gradually whisk in the milk until smooth.
  2. Cook the mixture over medium heat, stirring constantly until it thickens and begins to bubble.
  3. Remove from heat and stir in butter and vanilla until fully combined.
  4. Pour the chocolate filling into the pre-baked pie crust. Cover with plastic wrap and refrigerate for at least 4 hours or until set.
  5. When ready to serve, whip the heavy cream with powdered sugar until stiff peaks form. Spread or pipe the whipped cream over the chilled chocolate filling.
  6. Slice and enjoy this decadent dessert that’s sure to delight chocolate lovers!

Savory Chicken Pot Pie

Ingredients

  • 1 pound cooked chicken, shredded
  • 1 cup frozen peas and carrots
  • 1/2 cup frozen corn
  • 1/3 cup butter
  • 1/3 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 1/2 cups chicken broth
  • 2/3 cup milk
  • 1 teaspoon onion powder
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 pre-made pie crusts

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 425°F (220°C).
  2. In a saucepan, melt butter over medium heat. Stir in flour, onion powder, garlic powder, salt, and pepper until smooth.
  3. Gradually whisk in chicken broth and milk. Cook until the mixture thickens, stirring constantly.
  4. Stir in the cooked chicken, peas, carrots, and corn until well combined.
  5. Roll out one pie crust and place it in a 9-inch pie pan. Fill with the chicken mixture.
  6. Top with the second pie crust, sealing the edges. Cut slits in the top crust for ventilation.
  7. Bake in the preheated oven for 30-35 minutes, or until the crust is golden brown. Let it cool before serving.

Decadent Pumpkin Pie

Ingredients

  • 1 (15 oz) can of pumpkin puree
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 3/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/2 cup packed brown sugar
  • 3 large eggs
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground ginger
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground nutmeg
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground cloves
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 pre-made pie crust

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 425°F (220°C).
  2. In a large bowl, whisk together pumpkin, heavy cream, sugars, eggs, and spices until smooth.
  3. Pour the filling into the pie crust.
  4. Bake for 15 minutes, then reduce the temperature to 350°F (175°C) and continue baking for 40-50 minutes, or until the filling is set.
  5. Cool completely before serving. Top with whipped cream for an extra touch of indulgence.

Blueberry Pie

Ingredients

  • 6 cups fresh blueberries (or frozen, if not in season)
  • 3/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/4 cup cornstarch
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1 tablespoon butter
  • 2 pre-made pie crusts

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 425°F (220°C).
  2. In a large bowl, combine blueberries, sugar, cornstarch, lemon juice, and cinnamon. Toss gently to combine.
  3. Roll out one pie crust and place it in a 9-inch pie pan. Fill with the blueberry mixture and dot with butter.
  4. Roll out the second pie crust on top, sealing the edges and cutting slits for ventilation.
  5. Bake for 15 minutes, then reduce the temperature to 350°F (175°C) and bake for an additional 30-40 minutes, or until the filling is bubbly and the crust is golden.
  6. Allow to cool before serving to let the juices set.

Key Lime Pie

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups graham cracker crumbs
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, melted
  • 4 large egg yolks
  • 1 (14 oz) can sweetened condensed milk
  • 1/2 cup fresh lime juice
  • Zest of 2 limes
  • Whipped cream (for topping)

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. In a bowl, combine graham cracker crumbs, sugar, and melted butter. Press the mixture firmly into the bottom of a 9-inch pie pan to form a crust.
  3. In a separate bowl, whisk together egg yolks, condensed milk, lime juice, and zest until smooth. Pour the filling into the prepared crust.
  4. Bake for 15 minutes, then remove from the oven and let cool to room temperature. Refrigerate for at least 3 hours before serving.
  5. Top with whipped cream before serving for a refreshing treat.

Tips for Perfect Pie Baking

  1. Use Fresh Ingredients: For the best flavor, use fresh fruits and high-quality dairy products.
  2. Chill Your Dough: If making your own crust, chill the dough before rolling it out. This prevents shrinking and leads to a flakier texture.
  3. Don’t Overfill: When adding your filling, be mindful not to overfill the pie crust to avoid spills during baking.
  4. Check the Temperature: Invest in an oven thermometer to ensure your oven is at the correct temperature for even baking.
  5. Let It Cool: Allow your pies to cool properly before slicing. This helps the filling set and makes for cleaner slices.

Conclusion

Pies are more than just desserts; they are a symbol of togetherness and celebration. Each recipe shared here offers a unique flavor profile and a chance to bring family and friends closer. Whether it’s the classic apple pie that reminds you of childhood or the rich chocolate cream pie that speaks to your sweet tooth, these recipes are sure to be a hit at your next family gathering. So, gather your loved ones, preheat your oven, and start creating delicious memories with these delectable pies!
