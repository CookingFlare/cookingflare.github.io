Coconut Mango Chia Pudding: A Healthy and Tropical Delight

If you’re looking for a refreshing and nutritious treat, look no further than Coconut Mango Chia Pudding. This delightful dessert combines the tropical flavors of mango and coconut with the health benefits of chia seeds. Packed with fiber, omega-3 fatty acids, and antioxidants, this dessert is not only delicious but also good for you. Ingredients To make this Coconut Mango Chia Pudding, you will need the following ingredients: 1 ripe mango, peeled and diced 1 cup coconut milk 1/4 cup chia seeds 1 tablespoon honey (optional, for added sweetness) 1/4 teaspoon vanilla extract Pinch of salt Fresh mint leaves for garnish Instructions In a blender, puree the diced mango until smooth. Set aside. In a medium-sized bowl, combine the coconut milk, chia seeds, honey (if using), vanilla extract, and a pinch of salt. Stir well to ensure the chia seeds are evenly distributed. Let the mixture sit for about 10 minutes to allow the chia seeds to absorb the liquid. After 10 minutes, give the mixture a good stir to prevent clumping. Cover the bowl with plastic wrap and refrigerate for at least 4 hours or overnight to allow the chia seeds to fully gel and thicken. Once the pudding has set, give it a good stir to break up any clumps. To serve, divide the pudding into individual bowls or glasses. Top each serving with a dollop of the mango puree and garnish with fresh mint leaves. Serve chilled and enjoy! Tips and Variations For a creamier pudding, you can use canned coconut cream instead of coconut milk. Feel free to adjust the sweetness to your liking by adding more or less honey. If you prefer a smoother texture, you can blend the chia pudding mixture after it has set to achieve a more consistent texture. Get creative with your toppings! You can add fresh berries, sliced bananas, or toasted coconut flakes for additional flavor and texture.
