Coral Cuisine: Irresistible Seafood Recipes from the Ocean's Depths


Ahoy, fellow food enthusiasts! Are you ready to embark on a culinary adventure to the depths of the ocean where a plethora of tantalizing seafood delicacies await? Prepare to tantalize your taste buds with an array of irresistible recipes, all infused with the luscious flavors of the sea. From appetizers to main courses, discover delightful dishes that will have you diving for more!

1. Ocean Swirls of Smoked Salmon

Nothing says “welcome” like tantalizing appetizers. Kick off your next soirée with our Ocean Swirls of Smoked Salmon. A showstopper sure to impress, this dish combines silky smooth smoked salmon with tangy cream cheese and tantalizing bits of capers.

Ingredients:

  • 250g cream cheese, softened
  • 150g smoked salmon, finely chopped
  • 2 tbsp finely chopped capers
  • Juice and zest of 1/2 lemon
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Mix pack of multigrain and regular bread crisps for serving

Instructions:

  1. In a medium bowl, mix together cream cheese, smoked salmon, capers, lemon juice, lemon zest, salt, and pepper until well-combined.
  2. Using a piping bag or spatula, swirl the mixture onto the bread crisps.
  3. Refrigerate for 30 minutes to let the flavors meld together. Serve cold with toothpicks or cracker spears.

2. Sunsplash Shrimp Skewers

Calling all sunshine seekers! Adding a dose of Vitamin Sea never tasted so good with our Sunsplash Shrimp Skewers. Bursting with fresh flavors and tropical zest, this delightful dish is the perfect picnic addition or light meal for your beach days.

Ingredients:

  • 500g large shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 2 limes, juiced
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 4 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 tbsp honey
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 6 skewers
  • Mixed grill spices for brushing
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh cilantro
  • Sliced red onions for garnish

Instructions:

  1. In a large bowl, combine shrimp, lime juice, olive oil, garlic, honey, paprika, salt, and pepper. Mix until evenly coated.
  2. Preheat the grill to medium-high heat. Thread shrimp onto skewers, leaving a small space between each piece.
  3. Brush skewers with a mix of olive oil and grill spices, ensuring each piece is coated for the perfect grilled exterior.
  4. Grill skewers for 2-3 minutes on each side or until the shrimp turn a lovely pink/orange hue. The protein development on the surface and the slight char from the grilled exterior pairs amazingly well with the tender juices inside the shrimp.
  5. Allow the skewers to sit for a few minutes before removing from the skewers. Mix with cilantro and sliced red onion for a burst of color and freshness.

3. Seabreeze Quinoa Stuffed Bell Peppers

Are you serious about fusion cuisine? Our Seabreeze Quinoa Stuffed Bell Peppers will make your taste buds sway with flavors from the land and sea. A delightful combination of quinoa, succulent shrimp, and zesty herbs, this dish brings coastal vibes to your next dinner party.

Ingredients:

  • 4 bell peppers, tops chopped off and seeded
  • 1/2 cup cooked quinoa
  • 250g cooked shrimp, chopped
  • 1/2 chopped red onion
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley
  • 1 egg, beaten
  • 1 1/2 cups breadcrumbs
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan
  • 2 tsp dried dill
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1/4 cup olive oil

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (180°C).
  2. In a large bowl, mix quinoa, shrimp, red onion, parsley, beaten egg, breadcrumbs, Parmesan, dill, salt, pepper, and olive oil until evenly blended.
  3. Stuff bell peppers with the shrimp mixture, pressing down gently.
  4. Place peppers in a baking dish and bake for 30-40 minutes or until the peppers are tender and the top turns a golden brown.

4. Coral Goggle Mussels

Unleash your inner pirate as we traverse through the warm, tropical waters with the ultimate vessel of seafood - mussels! These buttery gems are elevated with fresh tomatoes, tantalizing onions, and hints of white wine, exclusive to our “Coral Goggle Mussels” dish.

Ingredients:

  • 1kg mussels, clean and debearded
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 shallots, finely chopped
  • 4 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 tbsp tomato paste
  • 1 cup white wine
  • 3 cups chopped fresh tomatoes
  • 2 tsp dried thyme
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 cups chopped fresh parsley
  • Crusty bread for serving

Instructions:

  1. In a large, deep pot or Dutch oven, heat olive oil over medium heat. Add shallots and garlic, sautéing until tender and fragrant.
  2. Stir in the tomato paste, white wine, tomatoes, and thyme. Cook for 10 minutes, allowing the flavors to meld together.
  3. Add the mussels to the pot, stirring gently until they are submerged in the broth. Cover the pot and steam for 5-7 minutes or until the mussels have opened. They tantalize the taste buds with a burst of freshness and a hint of a buttery exposed muscle.
  4. Season with salt and pepper before serving with a sprinkle of fresh parsley and crusty bread for dipping.

These four incredible seafood dishes will transport your taste buds to a world of oceanic flavors. Now go ahead and immerse your senses with our Coral Cuisine and revel in the irresistible recipes from the depths of the sea. Happy cooking!
