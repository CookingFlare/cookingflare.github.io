Chocolate Chip Cookie Dough Truffles: Edible Bliss


If you’re a fan of cookie dough and truffles, then you’re in for a treat! These Chocolate Chip Cookie Dough Truffles are the perfect combination of rich chocolate and indulgent cookie dough. They’re easy to make and even easier to devour. So, let’s dive right into this heavenly recipe.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 cup packed brown sugar
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup mini chocolate chips
  • 12 ounces semi-sweet chocolate, melted

Instructions

  1. In a large mixing bowl, cream together the softened butter, granulated sugar, and brown sugar until light and fluffy. This usually takes about 3-4 minutes.

  2. Add the vanilla extract to the butter mixture and mix until well combined.

  3. In a separate bowl, whisk together the all-purpose flour and salt. Gradually add this dry mixture to the butter mixture, mixing until just combined. Be careful not to overmix.

  4. Gently fold in the mini chocolate chips until evenly distributed throughout the dough.

  5. Line a baking sheet with parchment paper. Using a small cookie scoop or a tablespoon, scoop out rounded portions of the cookie dough and roll them into balls. Place the dough balls onto the prepared baking sheet. Repeat until all the dough is used. Place the baking sheet in the freezer for about 30 minutes to firm up the dough.

  6. While the dough is chilling, melt the semi-sweet chocolate in a microwave-safe bowl or using a double boiler. Stir the chocolate until smooth and creamy.

  7. Remove the dough balls from the freezer. Using a fork or a toothpick, dip each dough ball into the melted chocolate, ensuring it is fully coated. Allow any excess chocolate to drip off before placing the coated truffle back onto the baking sheet.

  8. Once all the truffles are coated, refrigerate them for at least 30 minutes to allow the chocolate to set.

  9. Once the chocolate has set, you can enjoy these delectable Chocolate Chip Cookie Dough Truffles. They can be stored in an airtight container in the refrigerator for up to a week.

Tips and Variations

  • For an extra indulgent twist, try adding a teaspoon of instant coffee granules to the cookie dough mixture. This will add a subtle hint of mocha flavor to the truffles.

  • Feel free to experiment with different types of chocolate for coating the truffles. Milk chocolate or white chocolate would both work well and provide a unique flavor profile.

  • If you prefer a softer cookie dough center, let the truffles sit at room temperature for a few minutes before enjoying them. This will allow the dough to soften slightly.

  • You can also customize these truffles by rolling them in chopped nuts, shredded coconut, or even sprinkles before the chocolate sets. This will add an extra layer of texture and flavor.

So there you have it, a recipe that combines two irresistible treats into one mouthwatering bite. These Chocolate Chip Cookie Dough Truffles are sure to satisfy your sweet tooth and leave you craving more. Whether you’re making them for a special occasion or simply treating yourself, these truffles are guaranteed to bring edible bliss to your day. Enjoy!
