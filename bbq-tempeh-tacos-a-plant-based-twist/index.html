BBQ Tempeh Tacos: A Plant-Based Twist


Imagine sinking your teeth into a scrumptious, smoky taco filled with tender, succulent protein while avoiding the traditional meat-based choices. Indulge in the delightful flavors of BBQ tempeh tacos – a delightful plant-based alternative that’s just as mouthwatering as the original.

There’s a little genius behind making this delightful platter, and a lot of story behind what a plant-based meal can offer. Whether you are a BBQ aficionado or someone who can’t go without their daily dose of proteins, this recipe will blow your mind.

Ingredients

  • 1 block of tempeh (14 oz.)
  • 1 tsp. paprika
  • 1 tsp. garlic powder
  • 1 tsp. smoked paprika
  • 1 tsp. dried oregano
  • 1 tsp. salt
  • 1 tsp. freshly ground black pepper
  • 1/4 cup liquid smoke
  • 1/4 cup apple cider vinegar
  • 1/4 cup barbeque sauce
  • 8 small corn tortillas
  • 1/2 cup finely chopped red onion
  • 1/2 cup shredded cabbage
  • 1 avocado, sliced
  • 1 lime

Instructions

  1. In a blender, combine all of the marinade ingredients (paprika, garlic powder, smoked paprika, oregano, salt, black pepper, liquid smoke, apple cider vinegar, barbeque sauce). Blend until smooth.
  2. Cut the tempeh into cubes and place them in a shallow dish or resealable plastic bag. Pour the marinade over the tempeh, ensuring that all pieces get nicely coated. Let it rest for at least an hour, up to four hours for maximum flavor infusion.
  3. Preheat your grill or grill pan to medium-high heat. If using a grill, ensure the grate is clean.
  4. Once your grill’s heated up, carefully place the tempeh cubes on the hot grate. Cook for about 4-5 minutes each side until the tempeh is slightly charred and caramelized. Make sure to regularly flip the cubes to prevent any from burning.
  5. While the tempeh is cooking, warm up your corn tortillas on the grill for an extra smoky flavor. Stack them on a plate and gently cover them with a towel. Let them steam for a couple of minutes.
  6. To assemble your BBQ tempeh tacos, begin by adding a cube or two of grilled tempeh to each tortilla. Top with chopped red onion and shredded cabbage for extra crunch. Finish off your taco with a slice of delicious avocado.
  7. Serve immediately with a squeeze of lime juice for a refreshing contrast to the smokiness of the barbeque tempeh. Your guests will be enamored by the depth of flavors in these plant-based tacos!

Due to the lower fat content of plant-based proteins, enjoy these BBQ tempeh tacos with your favorite water-based dipping sauce or pub-style brew for maximum enjoyment. Feel free to experiment with additional toppings like vegan sour cream or vegan cheese for a more indulgent experience.

BBQ tempeh tacos are a fantastic culinary departure from the ordinary – giving you a simple and delectable way to explore the world of plant-based alternatives. Say goodbye to the boring veggies and hello to rich, smoky flavors that bring a delightful twist to an all-time favorite, the taco. Give this recipe a try and enjoy a delightful plant-based meal!
