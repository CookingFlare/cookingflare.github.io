Slow Cooker Specials: Crockpot Cooking for Two Recipes


Are you tired of spending hours in the kitchen preparing elaborate meals for just the two of you? Do you find yourself wishing for a simpler way to enjoy delicious home-cooked food without all the hassle? Look no further than your trusty slow cooker or crockpot! These kitchen appliances are a game-changer when it comes to cooking for two. In this blog post, we’ll explore some mouthwatering recipes specifically designed for slow cookers that will make your weeknight dinners a breeze.

1. Creamy Tuscan Chicken

Prep Time: 15 minutes
Cook Time: 4 hours
Serves: 2

Ingredients:

  • 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1 cup sun-dried tomatoes (drained and chopped)
  • 1 cup baby spinach
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 cloves garlic (minced)
  • 1 teaspoon dried Italian seasoning
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Place the chicken breasts at the bottom of the slow cooker.
  2. In a mixing bowl, combine the sun-dried tomatoes, baby spinach, heavy cream, Parmesan cheese, minced garlic, Italian seasoning, salt, and pepper. Mix well.
  3. Pour the mixture over the chicken breasts, ensuring they are fully coated.
  4. Cook on low heat for 4 hours or until the chicken is tender and cooked through.
  5. Serve over cooked pasta or rice, garnished with fresh basil leaves if desired.

2. Beef Stew

Prep Time: 20 minutes
Cook Time: 6-8 hours
Serves: 2

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb beef stew meat (cut into bite-sized pieces)
  • 1 onion (chopped)
  • 2 carrots (peeled and sliced)
  • 2 potatoes (peeled and cubed)
  • 2 cloves garlic (minced)
  • 2 cups beef broth
  • 1 tablespoon Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Place the beef stew meat, onion, carrots, potatoes, and minced garlic in the slow cooker.
  2. In a separate bowl, whisk together the beef broth, Worcestershire sauce, dried thyme, salt, and pepper.
  3. Pour the mixture over the ingredients in the slow cooker, ensuring everything is well-coated.
  4. Cook on low heat for 6-8 hours or until the beef is tender.
  5. Serve hot with crusty bread or mashed potatoes.

3. Vegetarian Chili

Prep Time: 15 minutes
Cook Time: 6-8 hours
Serves: 2

Ingredients:

  • 1 can kidney beans (drained and rinsed)
  • 1 can black beans (drained and rinsed)
  • 1 can diced tomatoes
  • 1 onion (chopped)
  • 1 bell pepper (chopped)
  • 2 cloves garlic (minced)
  • 1 tablespoon chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1/2 teaspoon paprika
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Place all the ingredients in the slow cooker and stir well to combine.
  2. Cook on low heat for 6-8 hours or on high heat for 3-4 hours.
  3. Serve hot with shredded cheese, sour cream, and a side of cornbread.

4. Apple Crisp

Prep Time: 15 minutes
Cook Time: 2-3 hours
Serves: 2

Ingredients:

  • 2 apples (peeled, cored, and sliced)
  • 1/2 cup rolled oats
  • 1/4 cup flour
  • 1/4 cup brown sugar
  • 1/4 cup butter (melted)
  • 1 teaspoon cinnamon
  • Vanilla ice cream (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Place the sliced apples at the bottom of the slow cooker.
  2. In a mixing bowl, combine the rolled oats, flour, brown sugar, melted butter, and cinnamon. Mix well.
  3. Sprinkle the mixture over the apples in the slow cooker, ensuring they are evenly coated.
  4. Cook on low heat for 2-3 hours or until the apples are tender and the topping is golden brown.
  5. Serve warm with a scoop of vanilla ice cream for a delightful dessert.

Conclusion

With these slow cooker recipes tailored for two, you can enjoy flavorful and satisfying meals without the stress and fuss of traditional cooking methods. Whether you’re a busy couple or simply looking to simplify your mealtime routine, these recipes will surely become staples in your kitchen. Give them a try and savor the convenience and deliciousness that slow cooker cooking brings. Happy cooking!
