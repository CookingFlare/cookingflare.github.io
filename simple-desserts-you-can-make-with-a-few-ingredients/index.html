Simple Desserts You Can Make with a Few Ingredients


Are you craving something sweet but don’t want to spend hours in the kitchen or make a trip to the grocery store? We’ve got you covered with these simple dessert recipes that require just a few basic ingredients. Whether you’re entertaining guests or simply treating yourself, these easy-to-make desserts are sure to satisfy your sweet tooth.

Chocolate Mug Cake

Ingredients:

  • 4 tbsp all-purpose flour
  • 2 tbsp granulated sugar
  • 2 tbsp unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1/8 tsp baking powder
  • 3 tbsp milk
  • 3 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 1/4 tsp vanilla extract
  • Pinch of salt

Instructions:

  1. In a microwave-safe mug, whisk together the flour, sugar, cocoa powder, baking powder, and salt.
  2. Add the milk, vegetable oil, and vanilla extract to the mug. Stir until smooth and well-combined.
  3. Microwave the mug for 1-2 minutes, or until the cake has risen and set in the middle.
  4. Let it cool for a minute before digging in. You can also top it with a dollop of whipped cream or a sprinkle of powdered sugar for an extra touch.

This indulgent chocolate mug cake is perfect for those moments when you need a quick dessert fix. It’s moist, rich, and takes just minutes to prepare.

Lemon Bars

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1/4 cup powdered sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 3/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 3 tbsp fresh lemon juice
  • Zest of 1 lemon
  • 2 tbsp all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 tsp baking powder
  • Powdered sugar for dusting

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C) and grease an 8-inch square baking pan.
  2. In a mixing bowl, cream together the softened butter, 1 cup of flour, and powdered sugar. Press the mixture into the bottom of the prepared pan.
  3. Bake the crust for 20 minutes, or until lightly golden.
  4. In another bowl, whisk together the eggs, granulated sugar, lemon juice, lemon zest, 2 tbsp of flour, and baking powder until well combined.
  5. Pour the lemon mixture over the hot crust and return the pan to the oven.
  6. Bake for an additional 20-25 minutes, or until the edges are lightly golden and the center is set.
  7. Let the lemon bars cool completely in the pan before cutting into squares. Dust with powdered sugar before serving.

These tangy and sweet lemon bars are a delightful treat that’s perfect for any occasion.

Peanut Butter Chocolate Chip Cookies

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup creamy peanut butter
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 large egg
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1/2 cup chocolate chips

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C) and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
  2. In a mixing bowl, combine the peanut butter, sugar, egg, and vanilla extract until smooth.
  3. Gently fold in the chocolate chips until evenly distributed.
  4. Scoop tablespoon-sized portions of dough onto the prepared baking sheet, leaving space between each cookie.
  5. Flatten each cookie slightly with a fork, creating a crisscross pattern on the top.
  6. Bake for 10-12 minutes, or until the cookies are golden around the edges.
  7. Let the cookies cool on the baking sheet for a few minutes before transferring them to a wire rack to cool completely.

These flourless peanut butter chocolate chip cookies are a delightful combination of nutty and sweet flavors, perfect for a quick and easy homemade treat.

Conclusion

With just a handful of ingredients, you can whip up these delicious desserts in no time. Whether you’re a beginner in the kitchen or a seasoned baker looking for simple yet satisfying recipes, these easy-to-make treats are sure to impress. So, the next time you’re craving something sweet, give these recipes a try and indulge in a little homemade goodness. Happy baking!
