Quick and Easy Instant Pot Chicken Marsala Recipes


If you’re looking for a delicious, savory dish that can be whipped up in no time, look no further than Chicken Marsala made in your Instant Pot. This classic Italian-American recipe combines tender chicken breasts with a rich, flavorful sauce featuring Marsala wine and mushrooms. The best part? You can prepare this mouthwatering meal in a fraction of the time it would take using traditional methods. Let’s dive into the world of Instant Pot Chicken Marsala with a couple of quick and easy recipes that will have your family and friends asking for seconds.

Why Choose Instant Pot for Chicken Marsala?

The Instant Pot is a game-changer for home cooks. It not only speeds up the cooking process but also infuses flavors beautifully due to its pressure cooking capabilities. Here are some reasons why the Instant Pot is perfect for making Chicken Marsala:

  1. Speed: Cooking under pressure significantly reduces the cooking time, allowing you to enjoy a gourmet meal in about 30 minutes.
  2. Flavor: The closed environment of the Instant Pot helps to retain moisture and intensify the flavors of the ingredients, making each bite succulent and rich.
  3. Convenience: With the sauté function, you can brown your chicken and cook your sauce all in one pot, minimizing cleanup time.

Essential Ingredients for Chicken Marsala

To create a delectable Chicken Marsala, you’ll need a few key ingredients. Here’s a list:

  • Chicken Breasts: Boneless, skinless chicken breasts work best for this dish, as they cook quickly and stay tender.
  • Marsala Wine: This sweet wine is essential for the authentic flavor of Chicken Marsala. Opt for dry Marsala for a more savory taste.
  • Mushrooms: Fresh mushrooms, such as cremini or button, add depth and earthiness to the dish.
  • Chicken Broth: This enhances the flavor of the sauce and keeps the chicken moist.
  • Garlic and Onions: Aromatics that elevate the dish’s overall flavor profile.
  • Butter and Olive Oil: For sautéing the chicken and mushrooms, providing richness to the sauce.
  • Seasonings: Salt, pepper, and fresh herbs like parsley for garnish.

Basic Instant Pot Chicken Marsala Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1.5 pounds boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1 cup Marsala wine
  • 1 cup chicken broth
  • 8 ounces fresh mushrooms, sliced
  • 1 medium onion, chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Chopped fresh parsley for garnish

Instructions

  1. Sauté the Chicken:

    • Set your Instant Pot to the ‘Sauté’ mode and add the olive oil and butter.
    • Once hot, season the chicken breasts with salt and pepper, and add them to the pot.
    • Brown the chicken on both sides for about 3-4 minutes until golden. Remove the chicken and set aside.

  2. Cook the Vegetables:

    • In the same pot, add the chopped onion and sliced mushrooms. Sauté until the onions are translucent and mushrooms are tender, about 5 minutes.
    • Add the minced garlic and cook for an additional minute until fragrant.

  3. Deglaze the Pot:

    • Pour in the Marsala wine, scraping the bottom of the pot with a wooden spoon to deglaze it. This step is crucial to prevent the burn notice and to incorporate all those flavorful bits.

  4. Pressure Cook:

    • Return the browned chicken to the pot and pour in the chicken broth.
    • Close the lid, set the valve to sealing, and select ‘Manual’ or ‘Pressure Cook’ on high for 8 minutes.

  5. Release Pressure:

    • Once cooking is complete, allow the pressure to release naturally for 5 minutes, then switch to quick release to let out any remaining steam.

  6. Serve:

    • Remove the chicken and slice it if desired. You can thicken the sauce by setting the Instant Pot back to ‘Sauté’ mode and simmering for a few minutes if you prefer a richer sauce.
    • Serve the Chicken Marsala over pasta, rice, or mashed potatoes, and garnish with fresh parsley.

Instant Pot Creamy Chicken Marsala Variation

For those who crave a creamier version of Chicken Marsala, this variation adds a luscious touch to the dish.

Ingredients

  • Everything from the basic recipe, plus:
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • 1 teaspoon cornstarch (optional, for thickening)

Instructions

  1. Follow Steps 1-5 from the basic recipe above.

  2. Add Cream:

    • After pressure cooking, stir in the heavy cream until well combined. If you want a thicker sauce, mix the cornstarch with a tablespoon of water to create a slurry and add it to the sauce while it simmers on ‘Sauté’ mode.

  3. Serve:

    • Similar to the basic recipe, serve over your choice of sides, and enjoy the creamy, comforting flavors of this variation.

Tips for the Perfect Chicken Marsala

  • Wine Selection: The type of Marsala you choose can affect the flavor profile. For a sweeter taste, use a sweet Marsala, but for a more balanced sauce, opt for a dry one.
  • Mushroom Variety: Experiment with different types of mushrooms like shiitake or portobello for added flavor and texture.
  • Fresh Herbs: Fresh parsley or basil can elevate the dish, adding freshness to every bite.
  • Serving Suggestions: Chicken Marsala pairs beautifully with creamy polenta, buttery noodles, or a simple side salad.

Meal Prep and Leftovers

Chicken Marsala is perfect for meal prepping. You can store leftovers in an airtight container in the refrigerator for up to 3 days. Reheat gently on the stove or in the microwave, adding a splash of chicken broth to keep it moist.

Conclusion

Quick and easy Instant Pot Chicken Marsala recipes are a fantastic way to bring restaurant-quality dining into your home without spending hours in the kitchen. Whether you stick with the classic version or opt for the creamy variation, you’re bound to impress everyone at the table with your culinary skills. So grab your Instant Pot, gather your ingredients, and get ready to enjoy a delectable meal that’s sure to become a family favorite!
