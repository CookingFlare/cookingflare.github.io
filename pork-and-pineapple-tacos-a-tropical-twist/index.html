Pork and Pineapple Tacos: A Tropical Twist


If you’re looking to add a burst of tropical flavor to your taco night, look no further than these delightful Pork and Pineapple Tacos. With the perfect blend of savory and sweet, these tacos are a true crowd-pleaser. The succulent pork combined with the juicy pineapple creates a harmony of flavors that will transport your taste buds to a sunny island paradise. Let’s dive into the recipe and bring a taste of the tropics to your kitchen!

Ingredients

  • 1 lb pork loin, thinly sliced
  • 2 cups fresh pineapple, diced
  • 1 red onion, thinly sliced
  • 1 red bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 jalapeño, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/4 cup fresh cilantro, chopped
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1 teaspoon chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 12 small flour tortillas
  • Lime wedges, for serving

Instructions

Step 1: Marinate the Pork

In a bowl, combine the sliced pork loin with the cumin, paprika, chili powder, oregano, minced garlic, and a pinch of salt and pepper. Toss the pork until it’s well coated with the seasoning. Cover the bowl and let the pork marinate in the refrigerator for at least 30 minutes to allow the flavors to meld together.

Step 2: Grill the Pork and Pineapple

Preheat your grill or grill pan over medium-high heat. Once hot, grill the marinated pork slices for 3-4 minutes on each side until they develop a beautiful char and are cooked through. At the same time, grill the diced pineapple until it’s lightly caramelized and has grill marks, about 2-3 minutes per side.

Step 3: Sauté the Peppers and Onions

In a separate pan, heat a drizzle of oil over medium-high heat. Add the sliced red onion, red bell pepper, and jalapeño to the pan. Sauté for 5-6 minutes until the vegetables are tender-crisp and slightly charred. Season with salt and pepper to taste.

Step 4: Assemble the Tacos

Warm the flour tortillas on the grill for 20-30 seconds on each side. Once warmed, fill each tortilla with a generous portion of the grilled pork, sautéed peppers and onions, and a handful of the grilled pineapple. Garnish with fresh cilantro and a squeeze of lime juice for an extra pop of flavor.

Serving

Serve the Pork and Pineapple Tacos warm, accompanied by lime wedges for a tangy finish. These tropical tacos pair perfectly with a side of Mexican street corn, black beans, or a refreshing mango salsa. Don’t forget to mix up a batch of margaritas or piña coladas to complete the tropical experience!

Final Thoughts

Pork and Pineapple Tacos are the perfect way to infuse some sunshine into your mealtime. The combination of tender, flavorful pork and sweet, juicy pineapple creates a tantalizing fusion of tastes that will transport you to a beachside paradise with every bite. Whether you’re hosting a summer barbecue or simply craving a taste of the tropics, these tacos are sure to be a hit. So, fire up the grill and get ready to savor the flavors of summer with this delightful recipe!
