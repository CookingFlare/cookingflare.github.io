蛋糕和cupcake的new year snack版本：10种new year snack版的食谱


Are you ready to ring in the New Year with some delicious snacks? If you’re tired of the same old treats, we’ve got you covered. In this blog post, we’ll explore 10 exciting versions of egg cake and cupcake snacks that are perfect for celebrating the New Year. From sweet to savory, these recipes will satisfy all your cravings. So, let’s dive right in!

1. Matcha Egg Cake

Start the year on a vibrant note with this matcha-infused egg cake. The earthy flavor of matcha beautifully complements the soft and fluffy texture of the cake. It’s a delightful twist on the classic egg cake that will surely impress your guests.

2. Red Velvet Cupcake Bites

Turn your favorite red velvet cupcakes into bite-sized treats that are perfect for snacking. These little morsels are moist, rich, and topped with a creamy frosting. They’re a crowd-pleaser and will add a touch of elegance to any New Year’s gathering.

3. Salted Caramel Egg Cake

Indulge your taste buds with the heavenly combination of sweet and salty in this salted caramel egg cake. The caramel drizzle on top adds a decadent touch, making it an irresistible treat for those with a sweet tooth.

4. Lemon Blueberry Cupcake Muffins

Start the year fresh with these zesty lemon blueberry cupcake muffins. Bursting with tangy lemon flavor and juicy blueberries, they’re a refreshing option that will brighten up any snack table. Plus, they’re packed with antioxidants, making them a healthier choice.

5. Chocolate Chip Egg Cake Bars

For the chocolate lovers out there, these chocolate chip egg cake bars are a must-try. Loaded with melty chocolate chips, they’re a gooey and indulgent treat that will satisfy your cravings. Serve them warm for an extra touch of deliciousness.

6. Peanut Butter Cup Cupcakes

Combine the classic flavors of peanut butter and chocolate in these delightful peanut butter cup cupcakes. The creamy peanut butter frosting and the hidden chocolate surprise inside the cupcake will make every bite a delightful adventure.

7. Matcha Coconut Egg Cake

Transport yourself to a tropical paradise with this matcha coconut egg cake. The combination of matcha and coconut creates a unique and refreshing flavor that will transport you to sandy beaches and ocean breezes.

8. Raspberry Cheesecake Cupcakes

For those who love the tangy sweetness of raspberries and the creamy richness of cheesecake, these raspberry cheesecake cupcakes are a dream come true. The luscious raspberry filling and the smooth cream cheese frosting make them an irresistible treat.

9. Savory Egg Cake Muffins

If you prefer savory snacks, these savory egg cake muffins are the perfect choice. Packed with cheese, herbs, and your favorite veggies, they’re a delicious and portable option for those who want something different for their New Year’s celebration.

10. Chai Spice Cupcakes

Warm up your taste buds with these chai spice cupcakes. Infused with aromatic spices like cinnamon, cardamom, and ginger, they’re a cozy and comforting treat that will make you feel like you’re sipping a cup of chai tea on a winter’s day.

So there you have it – 10 mouthwatering versions of egg cake and cupcake snacks to celebrate the New Year in style. Whether you’re a fan of sweet or savory, there’s something on this list for everyone. So go ahead, try out these recipes, and make your New Year’s celebration extra special. Cheers to a delicious and joyful year ahead!
