Slow Cooker Orange Chicken: Sweet and Tangy


If you’re a fan of Chinese takeout, then you’re going to love this slow cooker orange chicken recipe. It’s sweet, tangy, and incredibly flavorful, and the best part is that you can make it right in your own kitchen. With the help of your trusty slow cooker, you can achieve that perfect balance of citrusy goodness and tender chicken without all the hassle. Let’s dive into this mouthwatering recipe and explore how you can create a restaurant-quality dish at home.

Ingredients:

  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 1 cup orange marmalade
  • 1/2 cup low-sodium soy sauce
  • 1/4 cup hoisin sauce
  • 2 tablespoons rice vinegar
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon fresh ginger, grated
  • 1/2 teaspoon red pepper flakes (adjust to taste)
  • 2 tablespoons cornstarch
  • 2 tablespoons water
  • Optional: sesame seeds and sliced green onions for garnish

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, mix together the orange marmalade, soy sauce, hoisin sauce, rice vinegar, minced garlic, grated ginger, and red pepper flakes.

  2. Place the chicken pieces in the slow cooker and pour the sauce over the chicken, ensuring that each piece is coated evenly.

  3. Cover and cook on low for 4-5 hours or on high for 2-3 hours, until the chicken is tender and cooked through.

  4. In a small bowl, mix the cornstarch and water to create a slurry. Stir the slurry into the slow cooker, coating the chicken and sauce mixture. Cook for an additional 30 minutes on high, or until the sauce has thickened.

  5. Once the sauce has thickened, give everything a good stir to ensure the chicken is coated in the delicious orange glaze.

  6. Serve the slow cooker orange chicken over cooked rice, garnished with sesame seeds and sliced green onions if desired.

Tips and Tricks:

  • For an extra kick of flavor, add a splash of orange juice or a sprinkle of orange zest to the sauce mixture.
  • If you prefer a thicker sauce, you can increase the amount of cornstarch in the slurry or cook the sauce for a longer period after adding the slurry.
  • To make this dish a bit healthier, you can use skinless chicken thighs instead of chicken breasts, and opt for low-sodium soy sauce and sugar-free orange marmalade.

Why Choose the Slow Cooker Method?

Using a slow cooker for this orange chicken recipe has its advantages. Not only does it allow the chicken to become tender and soak up all the delicious flavors, but it also gives you the flexibility to prepare the dish in the morning and come home to a perfectly cooked meal in the evening. The slow cooking process also helps the flavors meld together, creating a depth of taste that’s hard to achieve with a quick stovetop method.

Conclusion:

This slow cooker orange chicken recipe is a game-changer for anyone who loves the flavors of Chinese takeout but prefers to cook at home. Its sweet and tangy profile, combined with the tenderness of the chicken, makes it a crowd-pleaser for family dinners or gatherings with friends. By utilizing the convenience of a slow cooker, you can enjoy the satisfaction of a homemade meal that rivals your favorite restaurant dish. So, dust off your slow cooker and get ready to savor the delightful flavors of this sweet and tangy orange chicken!
