Vegan Potluck Staples: 30 Top-Rated Dishes That Everyone Will Love


Are you planning a vegan potluck and feeling stuck for ideas? Worry not! We’ve rounded up 30 top-rated vegan dishes that are sure to satisfy any crowd. These plant-based meals are delicious, simple to prepare, and offer something for everyone. From hearty mains to delightful desserts, you’ll find everything you need to make your potluck an unforgettable experience. So break out the serving utensils and get ready to dig in!

1. Vegan Caesar Salad

Start your potluck with a twist on the classic Caesar salad. This vegan version swaps out the traditional parmesan and anchovies for nutty hemp seeds and flavorful homemade dressing.

Ingredients:

  • 2-3 hearts of romaine, chopped
  • 1 cup hemp seeds, toasted
  • Caesar dressing (see recipe below)
  • Olive oil, for drizzling

Caesar Dressing:

  • 6 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 teaspoons Dijon mustard
  • 1 tablespoon capers, chopped
  • Juice of 1 lemon
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons water
  • Salt and pepper, to taste

2. Vegan Chili

A warm and comforting chili is an excellent choice for any potluck. This vegan chili is packed with protein, fiber, and flavorful spices that will warm you up on even the coldest days.

Ingredients:

  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 medium onion, chopped
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 bell peppers (any color), chopped
  • 2 stalks celery, chopped
  • 1 red pepper chili
  • 1 can (15 oz) kidney beans, rinsed and drained
  • 1 can (15 oz) black beans, rinsed and drained
  • 1 can (15 oz) corn, drained
  • 1 can (28 oz) crushed tomatoes
  • 2 tablespoons chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper, to taste

3. Vegan Shepherd’s Pie

This vegan take on the traditional shepherd’s pie is sure to be a crowd-pleaser. It’s loaded with lentils, vegetables, and a creamy mashed potato topping.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup green lentils
  • 2 stalks celery, chopped
  • 1 carrot, chopped
  • 1 large onion, chopped
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can (14 oz) diced tomatoes
  • 1 cup vegetable broth
  • 2 tablespoons tomato paste
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • Mashed potatoes (1 kg potatoes, peeled and chopped, boiled, and mashed with plant-based milk, butter, and salt)

4. Vegan Stuffed Peppers

Stuffed peppers are a fun and visually appealing dish that’s easy to make ahead of time. These vegan stuffed peppers are filled with quinoa, black beans, and flavorful spices.

Ingredients:

  • 4 large bell peppers, halved and seeded
  • 1 cup quinoa
  • 1 can (15 oz) black beans, rinsed and drained
  • 1 cup corn (frozen and thawed works well)
  • 1 small onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup salsa
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • Salt and pepper, to taste

5. Vegan Spring Rolls

Impress your guests with these fresh and tasty vegan spring rolls. They’re the perfect handheld appetizer, packed with crunchy vegetables, and served with a tangy dipping sauce.

Ingredients:

  • 8 rice paper wrappers
  • 1 cucumber, sliced
  • 1 large carrot, julienned
  • 1 bell pepper, julienned
  • 1/4 head of cabbage, shredded
  • 1/2 cup fresh cilantro, chopped
  • 1 zucchini, julienned
  • Peanut dipping sauce (see recipe below)

Peanut Dipping Sauce:

  • 1/2 cup peanut butter
  • 1/4 cup water
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon rice vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon maple syrup
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 1 red chili pepper, sliced (optional)

6. Vegan Portobello Mushrooms

A hit at any barbecue or potluck, these roasted portobello mushrooms are packed with smoky flavor, making them a delicious and hearty main dish.

Ingredients:

  • 4 large portobello mushrooms, stems removed
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons balsamic vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • 1/4 cup sun-dried tomatoes, sliced
  • 1/4 cup roasted red peppers, sliced

7. Vegan Mac and Cheese

The kids will love this ingenious vegan twist on a classic comfort food. Made with a creamy sauce of cashews, nutritional yeast, and plant-based milk, no one will even notice it’s missing the cheese.

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb elbow macaroni
  • 1 cup raw cashews, soaked
  • 3/4 cup unsweetened plant-based milk
  • 1/2 cup nutritional yeast
  • 3 cloves garlic
  • 1 teaspoon onion powder
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric
  • Salt and pepper, to taste

8. Vegan Chocolate Lava Cake

Satisfy your sweet tooth with this incredible vegan chocolate lava cake. Rich, fudgy, and chocolatey, it will be the perfect ending to your potluck feast.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup raw cashews, soaked
  • 2 cups (480 ml) water
  • 1/3 cup cocoa powder
  • 2/3 cup maple syrup
  • 1/3 cup melted coconut oil
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1/2 tsp baking powder (make sure it’s aluminum-free)

Serve the lava cake with a scoop of non-dairy ice cream and a sprinkle of cocoa powder for an extra touch of decadence.

9. Vegan Sushi Rolls

Sushi is a crowd-pleaser, and these vegan rolls are no exception. Fill them with an assortment of vegetables to create a magnificent rainbow roll.

Ingredients:

  • 3 cups sushi rice, prepared and seasoned (2 cups sushi rice cooked in 1 cup water (440 ml))
  • Nori sheets
  • 1 cucumber, julienned
  • 1 avocado, sliced
  • Carrot, julienned
  • Cucumber, julienned
  • 1/2 cup cooked Firm tofu, sliced
  • Soy sauce, wasabi, and pickled ginger for serving

10. Vegan Falafel

This classic Middle Eastern dish features crispy falafel balls served on a bed of fresh greens with a tangy tahini dressing.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup dried chickpeas
  • 1/2 onion
  • 1/4 cup parsley
  • 1/4 cup cilantro
  • 1/4 cup mint
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 1 tablespoon cumin
  • 1 tablespoon coriander
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • Tahini dressing (see below)

Tahini Dressing:

  • 1/2 cup tahini
  • 2 tablespoons lemon juice
  • 2 tablespoons water
  • 1 minced garlic clove
  • Salt and pepper, to taste

These dishes will undoubtedly have everyone coming back for seconds and asking for the recipes. So, gather your friends, family, and favorite vegan dishes, and enjoy the perfect potluck!
