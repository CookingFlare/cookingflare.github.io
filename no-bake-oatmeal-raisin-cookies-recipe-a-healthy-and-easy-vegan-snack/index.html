No-Bake Oatmeal Raisin Cookies Recipe: A Healthy and Easy Vegan Snack


If you’re on the lookout for a guilt-free, delicious vegan snack, look no further. These no-bake oatmeal raisin cookies are not only easy to make, but they are also packed with wholesome ingredients that will satisfy your sweet cravings without compromising your health goals. Whether you’re a seasoned vegan or just looking to incorporate more plant-based options into your diet, these cookies are sure to become a staple in your snack repertoire.

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups rolled oats
  • 1 cup almond butter
  • 1/2 cup maple syrup
  • 1/2 cup raisins
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt

Instructions:

  1. In a large mixing bowl, combine the rolled oats, almond butter, maple syrup, raisins, vanilla extract, ground cinnamon, and salt. Stir the mixture until all the ingredients are well combined.

  2. Once the mixture is evenly combined, use a cookie scoop or your hands to form the dough into individual cookie shapes. Place the cookies on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper.

  3. Refrigerate the cookies for at least 30 minutes to allow them to set.

  4. After the cookies have chilled, they are ready to be enjoyed! Store any leftovers in an airtight container in the refrigerator for up to one week.

Why No-Bake Oatmeal Raisin Cookies?

Healthy Ingredients

These no-bake oatmeal raisin cookies are a nutritional powerhouse. Rolled oats are an excellent source of fiber and can help lower cholesterol levels. Almond butter provides healthy fats and protein, while raisins add natural sweetness and a dose of antioxidants. The use of maple syrup as a sweetener not only adds a delightful flavor but also ensures that the cookies are free from refined sugars.

Easy Preparation

One of the best things about this recipe is its simplicity. With just a handful of ingredients and no baking required, these cookies can be whipped up in a matter of minutes. Whether you’re a busy professional, a student, or a parent on the go, this recipe fits seamlessly into any schedule.

Vegan and Allergy-Friendly

Being vegan, these cookies are free from animal products and are suitable for those following a plant-based diet. Additionally, they are naturally free from dairy and eggs, making them an ideal treat for individuals with dairy or egg allergies.

Versatile and Customizable

Feel free to get creative with this recipe. If you prefer a different dried fruit, such as cranberries or chopped apricots, you can easily swap out the raisins. You can also add a handful of chopped nuts for extra crunch and protein.

Final Thoughts

In conclusion, these no-bake oatmeal raisin cookies are a stellar option for anyone seeking a healthy, easy, and delicious snack. Whether you enjoy them as an afternoon pick-me-up, a post-workout treat, or a lunchbox addition, these cookies are sure to hit the spot. Embrace the simplicity and wholesomeness of this recipe and indulge in a guilt-free snack that’s as nutritious as it is tasty. Happy snacking!
