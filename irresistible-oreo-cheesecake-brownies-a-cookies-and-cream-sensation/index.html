Irresistible Oreo Cheesecake Brownies: A Cookies and Cream Sensation


If you’re a fan of the classic combination of cookies and cream, then these irresistible Oreo cheesecake brownies are about to become your new favorite dessert. This decadent treat combines the fudgy goodness of brownies with the creamy richness of cheesecake, all swirled with Oreo cookies for the ultimate indulgence. Whether you’re hosting a dinner party or simply craving a sweet pick-me-up, these Oreo cheesecake brownies are guaranteed to satisfy your dessert cravings.

Ingredients

For the Brownie Layer:

  • 1 cup unsalted butter, melted
  • 2 cups granulated sugar
  • 1 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 4 large eggs
  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup semi-sweet chocolate chips
  • 1 cup crushed Oreo cookies

For the Cheesecake Layer:

  • 16 oz cream cheese, softened
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1/2 cup crushed Oreo cookies

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease a 9x13 inch baking pan and set aside.

  2. Prepare the Brownie Layer:

    • In a large mixing bowl, combine the melted butter, granulated sugar, cocoa powder, and vanilla extract. Mix until well combined.
    • Add the eggs one at a time, mixing well after each addition.
    • Stir in the flour and salt until just combined, then fold in the chocolate chips and crushed Oreo cookies.
    • Spread the brownie batter evenly into the prepared baking pan and set aside.

  3. Prepare the Cheesecake Layer:

    • In a separate mixing bowl, beat the cream cheese, granulated sugar, and vanilla extract until smooth and creamy.
    • Add the eggs one at a time, beating well after each addition.
    • Gently fold in the crushed Oreo cookies.

  4. Assemble and Bake:

    • Carefully spread the cheesecake mixture over the brownie batter in the pan, creating an even layer.
    • Use a knife to create a swirling pattern by gently running it through the batter.
    • Bake in the preheated oven for 35-40 minutes, or until the edges are set and the center is slightly jiggly.
    • Remove from the oven and allow the Oreo cheesecake brownies to cool to room temperature before refrigerating for at least 3 hours.

  5. Serve and Enjoy:

    • Once chilled, cut the brownies into squares and serve. Store any leftovers in the refrigerator for up to 5 days.

Final Thoughts

These Oreo cheesecake brownies are the perfect marriage of fudgy, creamy, and crunchy, making them a show-stopping dessert for any occasion. Whether you’re a fan of Oreo cookies, cheesecake, or brownies, this recipe brings together the best of all worlds in one delectable treat. Serve them at your next gathering or indulge in a slice to satisfy your sweet tooth – either way, they’re sure to be a hit. So, roll up your sleeves, preheat the oven, and get ready to create a cookies and cream sensation that will have everyone asking for the recipe. Enjoy!
