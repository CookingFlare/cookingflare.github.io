Decadent Chocolate Hazelnut Tart


Indulge in the rich and irresistible flavors of a Decadent Chocolate Hazelnut Tart. This heavenly dessert combines the velvety smoothness of chocolate with the nutty crunch of hazelnuts, creating a treat that is sure to satisfy even the most discerning sweet tooth.

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups crushed chocolate cookies
  • 1/2 cup melted unsalted butter
  • 1 cup roasted hazelnuts, roughly chopped
  • 1 1/2 cups heavy cream
  • 12 ounces semisweet chocolate, chopped
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • Pinch of salt
  • Whipped cream and additional chopped hazelnuts for garnish (optional)

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C).

  2. In a medium bowl, combine the crushed chocolate cookies and melted butter. Mix well until the crumbs are evenly coated. Press the mixture firmly into the bottom and up the sides of a 9-inch tart pan to form the crust. Place the tart pan in the refrigerator to chill while you prepare the filling.

  3. Spread the chopped hazelnuts evenly over a baking sheet and toast them in the preheated oven for about 8-10 minutes, or until they become fragrant and slightly golden. Remove from the oven and set aside to cool.

  4. In a small saucepan, heat the heavy cream over medium heat until it starts to simmer. Remove from the heat and add the chopped semisweet chocolate. Let it sit for a minute, then stir until the chocolate is completely melted and the mixture is smooth.

  5. Add the granulated sugar, vanilla extract, and salt to the chocolate mixture. Stir until well combined.

  6. Take the tart pan out of the refrigerator and pour the chocolate mixture into the prepared crust. Smooth the top with a spatula.

  7. Sprinkle the toasted hazelnuts over the top of the tart, gently pressing them into the chocolate.

  8. Place the tart in the refrigerator and let it chill for at least 4 hours, or until the filling is set.

  9. Once chilled, remove the tart from the refrigerator and let it sit at room temperature for a few minutes before serving.

  10. If desired, garnish with whipped cream and additional chopped hazelnuts.

Serving Suggestions

This Decadent Chocolate Hazelnut Tart is perfect on its own, but you can take it to the next level by serving it with a scoop of vanilla ice cream or a dollop of whipped cream. The combination of creamy chocolate and crunchy hazelnuts creates a symphony of flavors and textures that will leave your taste buds dancing.

Tips and Tricks

  • To crush the chocolate cookies for the crust, you can either use a food processor or place them in a plastic bag and crush them with a rolling pin.
  • Make sure to press the crust firmly into the tart pan to ensure it holds together when sliced.
  • If you’re short on time, you can use a store-bought chocolate cookie crust instead of making your own.
  • For an extra indulgent touch, drizzle some melted chocolate over the top of the tart before serving.

Conclusion

This Decadent Chocolate Hazelnut Tart is a show-stopping dessert that will impress your family and friends. With its rich chocolate filling and crunchy hazelnut topping, it’s a treat that is sure to satisfy even the most demanding sweet tooth. Whether you’re serving it at a dinner party or enjoying it as a special treat for yourself, this tart is a true delight. So go ahead, give in to your cravings and indulge in a slice of pure chocolatey bliss!
