Wholesome and Flavorful Stir Fry Recipes with Teriyaki Sauce


Looking for a delicious and healthy meal that’s quick and easy to prepare? Look no further than these wholesome and flavorful stir fry recipes with teriyaki sauce! Packed with vibrant colors, fresh ingredients, and the irresistible umami flavor of teriyaki, these stir fry dishes are perfect for any occasion. Whether you’re a seasoned chef or a beginner in the kitchen, these recipes will surely impress your taste buds and leave you craving for more.

Teriyaki Sauce: The Star of the Show

Before we dive into the recipes, let’s talk about the star of these dishes - the teriyaki sauce. Teriyaki sauce is a classic Japanese sauce made from a combination of soy sauce, mirin (a sweet rice wine), sugar, and other flavorful ingredients. It’s known for its sweet and savory taste, making it the perfect complement to stir fry dishes. You can easily find teriyaki sauce in most grocery stores, but if you’re feeling adventurous, you can try making your own from scratch.

Recipe 1: Teriyaki Chicken Stir Fry

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound boneless, skinless chicken breast, sliced into thin strips
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1 red bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 green bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 small broccoli crown, cut into florets
  • 1 carrot, sliced
  • 1/2 cup teriyaki sauce
  • 2 green onions, sliced (for garnish)

Instructions:

  1. Heat the vegetable oil in a large skillet or wok over medium-high heat.
  2. Add the chicken strips to the skillet and cook until browned and cooked through, about 5-7 minutes.
  3. Remove the chicken from the skillet and set aside.
  4. In the same skillet, add the bell peppers, broccoli, and carrot. Stir fry for 3-4 minutes, until the vegetables are crisp-tender.
  5. Return the chicken to the skillet and pour in the teriyaki sauce. Stir well to coat the chicken and vegetables.
  6. Cook for another 2-3 minutes, until everything is heated through.
  7. Garnish with sliced green onions and serve hot over steamed rice or noodles.

Recipe 2: Teriyaki Tofu and Vegetable Stir Fry

Ingredients:

  • 1 block firm tofu, drained and cubed
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1 zucchini, sliced
  • 1 yellow squash, sliced
  • 1 cup snap peas
  • 1 cup mushrooms, sliced
  • 1/2 cup teriyaki sauce
  • 2 tablespoons sesame seeds (for garnish)

Instructions:

  1. Heat the vegetable oil in a large skillet or wok over medium-high heat.
  2. Add the tofu cubes to the skillet and cook until golden brown on all sides, about 5 minutes.
  3. Remove the tofu from the skillet and set aside.
  4. In the same skillet, add the zucchini, yellow squash, snap peas, and mushrooms. Stir fry for 3-4 minutes, until the vegetables are crisp-tender.
  5. Return the tofu to the skillet and pour in the teriyaki sauce. Stir well to coat the tofu and vegetables.
  6. Cook for another 2-3 minutes, until everything is heated through.
  7. Sprinkle with sesame seeds and serve hot over steamed rice or quinoa.

Recipe 3: Teriyaki Beef and Broccoli Stir Fry

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound flank steak, thinly sliced
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 2 cups broccoli florets
  • 1 red bell pepper, sliced
  • 1/2 cup sliced water chestnuts
  • 1/4 cup teriyaki sauce
  • 1 tablespoon cornstarch (optional, for thickening)
  • 2 tablespoons water (optional, for thickening)

Instructions:

  1. Heat the vegetable oil in a large skillet or wok over medium-high heat.
  2. Add the flank steak to the skillet and cook until browned and cooked through, about 3-4 minutes.
  3. Remove the steak from the skillet and set aside.
  4. In the same skillet, add the broccoli, bell pepper, and water chestnuts. Stir fry for 3-4 minutes, until the vegetables are crisp-tender.
  5. Return the steak to the skillet and pour in the teriyaki sauce. Stir well to coat the steak and vegetables.
  6. If desired, mix the cornstarch and water in a small bowl to make a slurry. Add the slurry to the skillet and cook for another minute or until the sauce thickens.
  7. Serve hot over steamed rice or noodles.

Conclusion

These wholesome and flavorful stir fry recipes with teriyaki sauce are a perfect way to add some excitement to your weeknight dinners. With their vibrant colors, fresh ingredients, and the delicious umami flavor of teriyaki, these dishes are sure to please even the pickiest of eaters. So why wait? Grab your wok, whip up some teriyaki sauce, and get ready to take your taste buds on a flavorful adventure!
