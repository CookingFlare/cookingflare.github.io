Cinnamon Roll Delights: A Sweet Breakfast Treat


There’s nothing quite like the warm, comforting aroma of freshly baked cinnamon rolls wafting through the kitchen in the morning. These soft, fluffy pastries swirled with cinnamon and sugar, topped with a rich cream cheese glaze, are the epitome of a sweet breakfast treat. Whether you’re a seasoned baker or new to the world of homemade pastries, making cinnamon rolls from scratch is a rewarding experience that will delight your taste buds and those of your loved ones. In this blog post, we’ll explore the art of creating the perfect cinnamon roll delights right in your own kitchen.

Ingredients

For the Dough:

  • 4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/3 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 packet (2 1/4 teaspoons) active dry yeast
  • 1 cup whole milk
  • 1/4 cup unsalted butter
  • 2 large eggs

For the Filling:

  • 1 cup packed light brown sugar
  • 2 1/2 tablespoons ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 cup unsalted butter, softened

For the Cream Cheese Glaze:

  • 4 ounces cream cheese, softened
  • 1/4 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1 cup powdered sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon pure vanilla extract

Instructions

Step 1: Prepare the Dough

  1. In a large mixing bowl, combine 3 1/2 cups of flour, granulated sugar, salt, and yeast.
  2. In a small saucepan, heat the milk and butter until the mixture reaches 120-130°F. Add the warm milk mixture to the dry ingredients, and then add the eggs. Mix until well combined.
  3. Gradually add the remaining 1/2 cup of flour until the dough begins to form a ball.
  4. Turn the dough out onto a lightly floured surface and knead for 5-7 minutes until smooth and elastic. Place the dough in a greased bowl, cover, and let it rise for 1 hour, or until doubled in size.

Step 2: Make the Filling

  1. In a small bowl, mix together the brown sugar and cinnamon for the filling.
  2. Once the dough has risen, punch it down and roll it out into a 16x21 inch rectangle. Spread the softened butter over the dough, and then sprinkle the cinnamon-sugar mixture evenly.

Step 3: Assemble and Bake

  1. Starting with the long edge, roll the dough into a tight log. Cut the log into 12 equal pieces and place them in a greased 9x13 inch baking dish. Cover and let them rise for an additional 30 minutes.
  2. Preheat the oven to 375°F. Bake the rolls for 25-30 minutes, or until golden brown.

Step 4: Prepare the Cream Cheese Glaze

  1. In a medium bowl, beat the cream cheese and butter until smooth. Add the powdered sugar and vanilla extract, and beat until creamy.
  2. Spread the glaze over the warm cinnamon rolls and enjoy!

Tips and Tricks

  • For an extra flavor boost, add a handful of chopped pecans or walnuts to the filling before rolling the dough.
  • If you prefer a lighter glaze, substitute the cream cheese glaze with a simple powdered sugar and milk icing.

Final Thoughts

Cinnamon roll delights are a timeless classic that never fails to bring joy to the breakfast table. The process of making these delightful treats from scratch is a labor of love that pays off in the form of irresistibly soft, gooey rolls that are sure to impress. Whether you’re indulging in a lazy weekend brunch or surprising your family with a special morning treat, these homemade cinnamon rolls are bound to become a beloved tradition in your household.

So, roll up your sleeves, preheat your oven, and embark on a cinnamon-scented journey that will leave you craving for more. After all, there’s no better way to start the day than with a warm, freshly baked cinnamon roll in hand.
