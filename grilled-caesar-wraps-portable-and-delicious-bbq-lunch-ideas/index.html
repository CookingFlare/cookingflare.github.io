Grilled Caesar Wraps: Portable and Delicious BBQ Lunch Ideas


Summer is the perfect time for firing up the grill and enjoying delicious outdoor meals. If you’re looking for a portable and mouthwatering BBQ lunch idea, look no further than Grilled Caesar Wraps. These wraps are not only easy to make but also pack a flavorful punch that will impress your family and friends at any summer gathering. Whether you’re planning a picnic, a beach day, or just a backyard BBQ, these wraps are sure to be a hit.

Ingredients

For the Grilled Chicken:

  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • Salt and pepper
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon paprika

For the Caesar Dressing:

  • 1/2 cup mayonnaise
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 tablespoons lemon juice
  • 2 teaspoons Dijon mustard
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Additional Ingredients:

  • 4 large flour tortillas
  • 4 cups chopped romaine lettuce
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1/2 cup shredded Parmesan cheese
  • 1/2 cup croutons

Instructions

Step 1: Prepare the Grilled Chicken

  1. Preheat the grill to medium-high heat.
  2. Season the chicken breasts with salt, pepper, garlic powder, and paprika.
  3. Drizzle the olive oil over the seasoned chicken breasts.
  4. Grill the chicken for 6-7 minutes per side, or until it reaches an internal temperature of 165°F. Once cooked, set aside to cool.

Step 2: Make the Caesar Dressing

  1. In a small bowl, whisk together the mayonnaise, grated Parmesan cheese, lemon juice, Dijon mustard, minced garlic, salt, and pepper until well combined.

Step 3: Assemble the Wraps

  1. Lay out the flour tortillas on a clean surface.
  2. Spread a generous amount of the prepared Caesar dressing onto each tortilla.
  3. Place a handful of chopped romaine lettuce on top of the dressing.
  4. Slice the grilled chicken into strips and distribute evenly among the tortillas.
  5. Add the cherry tomatoes, shredded Parmesan cheese, and croutons on top of the chicken.

Step 4: Grill the Wraps

  1. Carefully fold the sides of each tortilla over the filling, then roll up tightly to create a wrap.
  2. Place the wraps seam-side down on the grill.
  3. Grill for 2-3 minutes per side, or until the tortillas are lightly charred and the filling is heated through.

Serving Suggestions

Once the Grilled Caesar Wraps are ready, serve them with a side of fresh fruit, potato salad, or corn on the cob for a complete BBQ lunch experience. These wraps also pair beautifully with a refreshing iced tea or a cold beer.

Final Thoughts

Grilled Caesar Wraps are not only delicious and satisfying but also incredibly convenient for outdoor dining. The flavorful combination of grilled chicken, tangy Caesar dressing, and crunchy vegetables wrapped in a warm tortilla is a surefire way to elevate your BBQ lunch game. So, the next time you’re planning a summer gathering, consider adding these portable and delicious wraps to your menu. They are bound to become a crowd favorite and will leave everyone asking for the recipe. Enjoy the sunny weather, good company, and, of course, the mouthwatering Grilled Caesar Wraps!
