Pear Perfection: Irresistible Pear Crumble Recipe


If you’re a fan of warm, comforting desserts, then this Pear Perfection: Irresistible Pear Crumble Recipe is a must-try! Made with juicy pears, a buttery crumble topping, and a hint of cinnamon, this dessert is a delicious way to celebrate the flavors of the season. Whether you’re hosting a dinner party or simply want to treat yourself to a sweet indulgence, this recipe is sure to impress.

Ingredients

For the pear filling:

  • 6-8 ripe pears, peeled, cored, and sliced
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 1/3 cup granulated sugar
  • 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon

For the crumble topping:

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup rolled oats
  • 1/2 cup brown sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, cold and cut into small pieces

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C). Grease a baking dish with butter or cooking spray and set aside.

  2. In a large bowl, combine the sliced pears, lemon juice, granulated sugar, all-purpose flour, and ground cinnamon. Gently toss until the pears are well coated. Transfer the pear mixture to the prepared baking dish, spreading it out evenly.

  3. In another bowl, combine the all-purpose flour, rolled oats, brown sugar, and ground cinnamon for the crumble topping. Mix well to combine.

  4. Add the cold, diced butter to the bowl and use your fingers or a pastry cutter to blend the butter into the dry ingredients. Continue mixing until the mixture resembles coarse crumbs.

  5. Sprinkle the crumble topping evenly over the pear mixture in the baking dish. Press it down lightly to ensure an even layer.

  6. Place the baking dish in the preheated oven and bake for 30-35 minutes, or until the crumble topping is golden brown and the pear filling is bubbling.

  7. Remove the pear crumble from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes before serving. This dessert is best enjoyed warm, either on its own or with a scoop of vanilla ice cream.

Serving Suggestions

For an extra touch of indulgence, drizzle some caramel sauce or serve with a dollop of whipped cream on top of the warm pear crumble. This dessert is perfect for any occasion, from casual family dinners to elegant gatherings with friends.

Tips and Variations

  • You can use any variety of pears for this recipe, but I recommend using ripe and juicy ones for the best flavor.
  • Feel free to add a handful of chopped nuts, such as walnuts or almonds, to the crumble topping for added crunch and flavor.
  • If you prefer a spicier flavor, you can add a pinch of nutmeg or ginger to the pear filling.
  • Leftovers can be stored in an airtight container in the refrigerator for up to three days. Simply reheat in the oven or microwave before serving.

Conclusion

Indulge your taste buds with this Pear Perfection: Irresistible Pear Crumble Recipe. The combination of sweet, juicy pears and a buttery crumble topping is simply irresistible. Whether you’re hosting a dinner party or craving a comforting dessert, this recipe is sure to satisfy. So, grab your apron, gather your ingredients, and get ready to enjoy a slice of pear perfection!
