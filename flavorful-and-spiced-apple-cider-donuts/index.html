Flavorful and Spiced Apple Cider Donuts


As the crisp autumn air settles in, our taste buds crave the warm, comforting flavors of the season. What better way to indulge in the sweetness of fall than with a batch of freshly baked donuts infused with the essence of apple cider and a hint of spice? In this recipe, we’ll guide you through the process of creating these delightful treats, perfect for a cozy gathering with friends and family.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/2 cup whole milk
  • 2 teaspoons active dry yeast
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 tablespoons unsalted butter, melted
  • 1 cup apple cider
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground nutmeg
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground cardamom
  • Confectioners’ sugar, for dusting

Instructions

  1. Proof the yeast: In a small bowl, combine the warm milk (around 100°F to 110°F) and yeast. Stir gently to dissolve the yeast, then let it sit for 5 to 7 minutes, or until the mixture becomes frothy and bubbly.
  2. Mix the dough: In a large mixing bowl, whisk together the flour, sugar, and salt. Add the melted butter, eggs, apple cider, cinnamon, nutmeg, and cardamom. Mix the dough until it comes together in a shaggy mass.
  3. Knead the dough: Using a stand mixer fitted with a dough hook attachment, knead the dough for 5 to 7 minutes, or until it becomes smooth and elastic. Alternatively, knead the dough by hand for 10 to 12 minutes.
  4. Let the dough rise: Place the dough in a lightly oiled bowl, cover it with plastic wrap or a damp towel, and let it rise in a warm, draft-free area for about 1 hour, or until it has doubled in size.
  5. Punch down the dough: Once the dough has risen, punch it down to release any air bubbles. Turn the dough out onto a floured surface and gently knead it a few times to redistribute the yeast.
  6. Cut out the donuts: Using a donut cutter or a glass, cut out the donuts. You should be able to get around 6 to 8 donuts.
  7. Fry the donuts: Heat about 2 to 3 inches of vegetable oil in a deep frying pan over medium-high heat until it reaches 350°F. Fry the donuts for 1 to 2 minutes on each side, or until they are golden brown and cooked through. Use a slotted spoon to remove the donuts from the oil and place them on a paper towel-lined plate to drain excess oil.
  8. Glaze the donuts: While the donuts are still warm, dust them with confectioners’ sugar. Alternatively, you can drizzle them with a simple glaze made by whisking together 1 cup powdered sugar, 2 tablespoons apple cider, and 1 teaspoon vanilla extract.
  9. Serve and enjoy: Serve the donuts warm, or let them cool completely and store them in an airtight container for up to 2 days.

Tips and Variations

  • To enhance the apple cider flavor, use a high-quality apple cider with a strong flavor profile.
  • If you prefer a stronger spice flavor, increase the amount of cinnamon, nutmeg, and cardamom to your liking.
  • Consider adding chopped nuts or dried cranberries to the dough for added texture and flavor.
  • For a boozy twist, substitute some of the apple cider with a splash of bourbon or apple brandy.

Conclusion

These Flavorful and Spiced Apple Cider Donuts are the perfect treat to warm your heart and satisfy your sweet tooth. With their delicate balance of sweet and spicy flavors, they’re sure to become a new fall favorite. So go ahead, indulge in the joys of apple cider season, and share these delightful donuts with the people you love.
