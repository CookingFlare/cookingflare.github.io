Delicious Vegan Breakfast Muffins for a Quick and Easy Morning


Are you a fan of breakfast but often find yourself short on time in the morning? Look no further! These delicious vegan breakfast muffins are not only quick and easy to make but also packed with wholesome ingredients to kick-start your day. Whether you’re a busy professional, a student rushing to class, or a parent on the go, these muffins are the perfect solution for a nutritious and satisfying breakfast on the run.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups whole wheat flour
  • 1/2 cup rolled oats
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 ripe banana, mashed
  • 1/4 cup maple syrup
  • 1/4 cup coconut oil, melted
  • 1 cup unsweetened almond milk
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 cup fresh blueberries
  • 1/2 cup chopped walnuts
  • 1/4 cup shredded coconut (optional)

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C) and line a muffin tin with paper liners or lightly grease the cups.

  2. In a large bowl, combine the whole wheat flour, rolled oats, baking powder, baking soda, and salt. Mix well to ensure the ingredients are evenly distributed.

  3. In another bowl, whisk together the mashed banana, maple syrup, melted coconut oil, almond milk, and vanilla extract until smooth.

  4. Pour the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients and gently fold together until just combined. Be careful not to overmix the batter as this can result in dense muffins.

  5. Gently fold in the blueberries, chopped walnuts, and shredded coconut if using, being careful not to crush the blueberries.

  6. Spoon the batter into the prepared muffin cups, filling each about 3/4 full.

  7. Bake in the preheated oven for 20-25 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center of a muffin comes out clean.

  8. Once baked, remove the muffins from the oven and allow them to cool in the tin for 5 minutes before transferring them to a wire rack to cool completely.

Serving Suggestions

These vegan breakfast muffins are perfect on their own, but you can take them to the next level with a few additional serving ideas:

  • Spread with a dollop of almond butter or your favorite nut butter for added protein and flavor.
  • Enjoy with a side of fresh fruit or a smoothie for a complete and balanced breakfast.
  • Pair with a cup of hot herbal tea or a refreshing glass of almond milk for a cozy morning treat.

Make-Ahead Tips

If you prefer to have breakfast prepped in advance, these muffins are perfect for meal prep. Simply store them in an airtight container at room temperature for up to 3 days, or in the refrigerator for up to a week. You can also freeze them for longer storage, just be sure to thaw them at room temperature or in the microwave before enjoying.

Final Thoughts

With these delicious vegan breakfast muffins in your recipe arsenal, you can say goodbye to morning hunger pangs and rushed breakfasts. Not only are they quick and easy to make, but they are also customizable to suit your taste preferences. Whether you’re a blueberry lover, nut enthusiast, or coconut fanatic, these muffins can easily accommodate your cravings. So, the next time you find yourself short on time in the morning, reach for one of these satisfying muffins and fuel your day the delicious and nutritious way!
