BBQ Chicken Drumsticks: Finger-Licking Recipes for Any Occasion


Are you craving a delicious and finger-licking BBQ chicken drumstick? Look no further! In this blog post, we’ll explore some mouthwatering recipes that are perfect for any occasion. Whether you’re hosting a backyard BBQ, having a picnic, or simply enjoying a family dinner, these recipes are sure to satisfy your taste buds. So, let’s fire up the grill and get cooking!

Classic BBQ Chicken Drumsticks

First up, we have the classic BBQ chicken drumsticks recipe. This timeless favorite is a crowd-pleaser and never fails to impress. To start, gather the following ingredients:

  • 8 chicken drumsticks
  • 1 cup BBQ sauce
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon paprika
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon onion powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  1. Preheat your grill to medium-high heat.
  2. In a small bowl, mix together the olive oil, paprika, garlic powder, onion powder, salt, and pepper to create a flavorful marinade.
  3. Coat the drumsticks evenly with the marinade, making sure to cover all sides.
  4. Place the drumsticks on the grill and cook for about 15-20 minutes, turning occasionally.
  5. Brush the drumsticks with BBQ sauce during the last 5 minutes of cooking, allowing them to caramelize and develop a deliciously sticky glaze.
  6. Remove from the grill and let them rest for a few minutes before serving.

Serve these classic BBQ chicken drumsticks with your favorite sides, such as coleslaw, corn on the cob, or potato salad. The smoky flavor and tender meat will have everyone coming back for seconds!

Sweet and Spicy BBQ Chicken Drumsticks

If you’re looking to add a little kick to your BBQ chicken drumsticks, this sweet and spicy recipe is a must-try. Here’s what you’ll need:

  • 8 chicken drumsticks
  • 1 cup BBQ sauce
  • 2 tablespoons honey
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 1 teaspoon chili powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon cayenne pepper
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  1. Preheat your grill to medium heat.
  2. In a mixing bowl, combine the BBQ sauce, honey, soy sauce, chili powder, cayenne pepper, salt, and pepper. Mix well to create a flavorful glaze.
  3. Generously brush the drumsticks with the glaze, coating them evenly.
  4. Place the drumsticks on the grill and cook for approximately 15-20 minutes, turning occasionally.
  5. Brush the drumsticks with more glaze during the last few minutes of cooking, allowing the flavors to intensify.
  6. Remove from the grill and let them rest for a few minutes before serving.

These sweet and spicy BBQ chicken drumsticks are the perfect balance of flavors. The sweetness from the honey pairs wonderfully with the heat from the chili powder and cayenne pepper. Be prepared for a burst of flavor with every bite!

Honey Mustard BBQ Chicken Drumsticks

Last but not least, we have a tangy and irresistible honey mustard BBQ chicken drumsticks recipe. Here’s what you’ll need:

  • 8 chicken drumsticks
  • 1/2 cup Dijon mustard
  • 1/4 cup honey
  • 2 tablespoons apple cider vinegar
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon smoked paprika
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  1. Preheat your grill to medium-high heat.
  2. In a bowl, combine the Dijon mustard, honey, apple cider vinegar, garlic powder, smoked paprika, salt, and pepper. Mix well to create a flavorful marinade.
  3. Coat the drumsticks evenly with the marinade, ensuring all sides are covered.
  4. Place the drumsticks on the grill and cook for about 15-20 minutes, turning occasionally.
  5. Brush the drumsticks with more marinade during the last few minutes of cooking, allowing the flavors to meld together.
  6. Remove from the grill and let them rest for a few minutes before serving.

These honey mustard BBQ chicken drumsticks are a tangy and savory delight. The combination of mustard, honey, and vinegar creates a unique and irresistible flavor profile.

Conclusion

There you have it, three finger-licking BBQ chicken drumstick recipes that are perfect for any occasion. Whether you prefer the classic flavors, a bit of heat, or a tangy twist, these recipes are sure to impress your guests and leave them craving more. So, fire up the grill, grab some drumsticks, and get ready for a BBQ feast like no other. Enjoy!
