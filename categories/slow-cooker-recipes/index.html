Flavorful Slow Cooker Mexican Shredded Chicken Recipe

Are you craving a delicious and flavorful Mexican dish that is easy to prepare? Look no further! This flavorful slow cooker Mexican shredded chicken recipe is the perfect choice for a tasty and hassle-free meal. With the convenience of a slow cooker, you can have a mouthwatering dish ready in no time. So, let’s get started! Ingredients 2 pounds boneless, skinless chicken breasts 1 onion, diced 3 cloves garlic, minced 1 jalapeno pepper, seeded and diced 1 can (14.5 ounces) diced tomatoes, undrained 1 can (4 ounces) diced green chilies 1 tablespoon chili powder 1 teaspoon ground cumin 1 teaspoon paprika 1/2 teaspoon dried oregano 1/2 teaspoon salt 1/4 teaspoon black pepper 1/4 cup fresh cilantro, chopped Juice of 1 lime Instructions Place the chicken breasts at the bottom of your slow cooker. Add the diced onion, minced garlic, and jalapeno pepper on top of the chicken. In a separate bowl, combine the diced tomatoes, diced green chilies, chili powder, ground cumin, paprika, dried oregano, salt, and black pepper. Mix well. Pour the tomato mixture over the chicken in the slow cooker, ensuring that the chicken is well-coated with the sauce. Cover the slow cooker and cook on low heat for 6-8 hours or on high heat for 3-4 hours, until the chicken is tender and easily shreds. Once cooked, remove the chicken from the slow cooker and shred it using two forks. Return the shredded chicken to the slow cooker and mix it with the sauce. Add the fresh cilantro and lime juice to the slow cooker. Stir well to incorporate the flavors. Cover the slow cooker again and let the chicken simmer on low heat for an additional 15-30 minutes to allow the flavors to meld together. Serve the flavorful slow cooker Mexican shredded chicken on warm tortillas, as a filling for tacos, burritos, or enchiladas. You can also serve it over rice or as a topping for salads. Optional: Garnish with additional cilantro, lime wedges, or your favorite Mexican toppings like sour cream, guacamole, or shredded cheese. Tips and Variations For an extra kick of spice, leave the seeds in the jalapeno pepper or add a pinch of cayenne pepper to the sauce. If you prefer a milder flavor, you can omit the jalapeno pepper altogether. Feel free to customize the recipe by adding your favorite vegetables such as bell peppers, corn, or black beans. Leftover shredded chicken can be stored in an airtight container in the refrigerator for up to 3 days or frozen for future use. Enjoy the delicious flavors of this slow cooker Mexican shredded chicken recipe. The tender and juicy chicken, combined with the aromatic spices and tangy tomatoes, will transport your taste buds to Mexico. This versatile dish can be used in a variety of ways, making it perfect for weeknight dinners, meal prep, or entertaining guests.
