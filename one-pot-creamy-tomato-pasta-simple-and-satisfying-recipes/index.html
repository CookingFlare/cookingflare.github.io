One-Pot Creamy Tomato Pasta: Simple and Satisfying Recipes


Are you looking for a quick and delicious dinner idea that requires minimal effort and cleanup? Look no further than this One-Pot Creamy Tomato Pasta recipe! This dish is a perfect combination of rich tomato flavor, creamy goodness, and satisfying pasta all cooked together in one pot for easy preparation and minimal dishes to wash. Let’s dive into the details of how to create this mouthwatering meal!

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound of your favorite pasta
  • 1 can (14 oz) of diced tomatoes
  • 1 cup of heavy cream
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 tablespoons of olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh basil leaves for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Sauté the Aromatics: In a large pot, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the minced garlic and chopped onion, and sauté until the onion is soft and translucent, about 3-4 minutes.

  2. Add the Pasta: Pour in the pasta and canned diced tomatoes with their juices. Stir everything together to combine.

  3. Cook the Pasta: Add water or broth to cover the pasta completely. Bring the mixture to a simmer, then reduce the heat to low. Cover the pot and let the pasta cook according to the package instructions, stirring occasionally.

  4. Creamy Goodness: Once the pasta is cooked and most of the liquid is absorbed, stir in the heavy cream. Simmer for an additional 2-3 minutes until the sauce has thickened slightly.

  5. Season to Perfection: Season the creamy tomato pasta with salt and pepper according to your taste. Feel free to add a pinch of red pepper flakes for a hint of heat if desired.

  6. Garnish and Serve: Remove the pot from the heat and tear fresh basil leaves over the top for a pop of freshness and color. Serve the creamy tomato pasta hot in bowls, garnished with additional basil leaves if desired.

Tips and Variations:

  • For a boost of protein, add cooked chicken, shrimp, or sausage to the pasta.
  • Experiment with different herbs like oregano or thyme for added flavor.
  • Replace heavy cream with coconut milk for a dairy-free alternative.
  • Sprinkle grated Parmesan cheese on top before serving for extra richness.

This One-Pot Creamy Tomato Pasta is a versatile dish that can be customized to suit your preferences and is sure to become a favorite in your recipe rotation. Whether you’re cooking for yourself, your family, or guests, this simple and satisfying recipe is bound to impress. Enjoy the convenience and deliciousness of a hearty pasta dish without the hassle of multiple pots and pans. Happy cooking!

Remember, the key to a successful one-pot meal is to layer flavors and stir occasionally to prevent sticking. Let the creamy tomato goodness simmer and meld together, creating a comforting and flavorful sauce that coats every strand of pasta. Dive in and savor the delightful combination of tomatoes, cream, and pasta in every bite. Cheers to easy, delicious meals that bring joy to your table!
