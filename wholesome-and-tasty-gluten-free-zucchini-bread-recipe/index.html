Wholesome and Tasty: Gluten-Free Zucchini Bread Recipe


Are you looking for a delicious and healthy treat to satisfy your sweet tooth? Look no further than this gluten-free zucchini bread recipe! Packed with wholesome ingredients and bursting with flavor, this zucchini bread is the perfect way to enjoy a guilt-free indulgence. Whether you follow a gluten-free diet or simply want to incorporate more nutritious options into your baking repertoire, this recipe is sure to become a staple in your kitchen.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups grated zucchini
  • 3 eggs
  • 1/2 cup honey
  • 1/2 cup coconut oil, melted
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 1/2 cups almond flour
  • 1/2 cup coconut flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1 teaspoon cinnamon
  • 1/2 teaspoon nutmeg
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup chopped walnuts (optional)

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C) and grease a 9x5 inch loaf pan.

  2. In a large bowl, whisk together the eggs, honey, melted coconut oil, and vanilla extract until well combined.

  3. Add the grated zucchini to the wet ingredients and stir until evenly distributed.

  4. In a separate bowl, combine the almond flour, coconut flour, baking powder, baking soda, cinnamon, nutmeg, and salt.

  5. Gradually add the dry ingredients to the wet ingredients, stirring until just combined. Be careful not to overmix the batter.

  6. If using, gently fold in the chopped walnuts.

  7. Pour the batter into the prepared loaf pan and smooth the top with a spatula.

  8. Bake for 50-60 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center of the bread comes out clean.

  9. Once baked, remove the zucchini bread from the oven and allow it to cool in the pan for 10-15 minutes before transferring it to a wire rack to cool completely.

Serving Suggestions

This gluten-free zucchini bread is delicious on its own, but you can take it to the next level with a few simple serving ideas. Spread a generous dollop of creamy almond butter on a slice for a satisfying breakfast or snack. For a touch of indulgence, warm a slice in the microwave and top it with a scoop of dairy-free vanilla ice cream. The possibilities are endless!

Health Benefits of Zucchini Bread

Zucchini is a nutrient-dense vegetable that adds moisture and a subtle sweetness to this bread. It’s a good source of dietary fiber, vitamins, and minerals, making it a great addition to a balanced diet. By using almond and coconut flour instead of traditional wheat flour, this recipe offers a gluten-free alternative that’s suitable for those with gluten sensitivities or celiac disease.

Conclusion

With its moist texture, delightful aroma, and irresistible taste, this gluten-free zucchini bread is a must-try for anyone looking to elevate their baking game. Whether you enjoy it for breakfast, as a snack, or as a dessert, this recipe is a testament to the fact that gluten-free and wholesome can be synonymous with delicious. Give it a try and savor the wholesome goodness of this delectable zucchini bread!
