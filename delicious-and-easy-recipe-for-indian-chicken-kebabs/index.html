Delicious and Easy Recipe for Indian Chicken Kebabs


If you’re looking to add a burst of flavor to your dinner table, Indian chicken kebabs are a fantastic choice. This delicious and easy recipe combines tender chicken with a perfect blend of Indian spices, resulting in a mouthwatering dish that’s sure to impress your family and friends. Whether you’re grilling for a summer barbecue or simply craving a taste of India, these chicken kebabs are a versatile and satisfying option.

Ingredients

To get started, gather the following ingredients:

  • 1.5 pounds of boneless, skinless chicken thighs, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 1 cup of plain yogurt
  • 2 tablespoons of fresh lemon juice
  • 2 tablespoons of olive oil
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon of grated fresh ginger
  • 1 tablespoon of garam masala
  • 1 teaspoon of ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon of ground coriander
  • 1/2 teaspoon of turmeric
  • 1/2 teaspoon of cayenne pepper
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh cilantro for garnish

Instructions

Step 1: Marinate the Chicken

In a large bowl, combine the yogurt, lemon juice, olive oil, garlic, ginger, garam masala, cumin, coriander, turmeric, cayenne pepper, and salt. Mix well to ensure all the ingredients are fully incorporated. Add the chicken pieces to the marinade and toss to coat evenly. Cover the bowl and refrigerate for at least 2 hours, allowing the flavors to meld and tenderize the chicken.

Step 2: Skewer the Chicken

Preheat your grill to medium-high heat. While the grill is heating, remove the marinated chicken from the refrigerator and thread the pieces onto metal or soaked wooden skewers. Be sure to leave a little space between each piece to ensure even cooking.

Step 3: Grill the Kebabs

Once the grill is ready, place the chicken kebabs on the grill grate. Cook for 10-12 minutes, turning occasionally, until the chicken is fully cooked and has developed a beautiful charred exterior.

Step 4: Serve and Enjoy

Remove the kebabs from the grill and transfer them to a serving platter. Garnish with freshly chopped cilantro for a burst of fresh flavor. These Indian chicken kebabs pair perfectly with fluffy basmati rice, warm naan bread, or a crisp green salad. Enjoy the tantalizing aroma and rich flavors of this delightful dish!

Tips and Variations

  • For a spicier kick, increase the amount of cayenne pepper or add a dash of hot chili powder to the marinade.
  • Experiment with different cuts of chicken or even try using boneless, skinless chicken breasts for a leaner option.
  • If you don’t have a grill, these kebabs can also be cooked in the oven under the broiler for a similar charred effect.

Whether you’re a fan of Indian cuisine or simply love trying new flavors, these delicious and easy Indian chicken kebabs are a must-try. The aromatic blend of spices, the tender texture of the chicken, and the smoky char from the grill make this recipe a standout. Impress your guests with this delightful dish at your next gathering, and savor the delightful flavors of India right in your own home. Enjoy!
