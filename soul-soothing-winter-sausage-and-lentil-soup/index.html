Soul-Soothing Winter Sausage and Lentil Soup


Winter is the perfect time to indulge in warm, comforting soups that not only fill our bellies but also warm our souls. And when it comes to soul-soothing soups, it’s hard to beat the rich, hearty flavors of a sausage and lentil soup. This recipe takes the classic combination of sausage and lentils and elevates it to new levels of deliciousness with the addition of aromatic herbs and spices. So, grab your soup pot and let’s get cooking!

Ingredients

  • 1 lb Italian sausage, casing removed
  • 1 medium onion, diced
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 medium carrots, diced
  • 2 celery stalks, diced
  • 1 cup dried lentils, rinsed and drained
  • 4 cups chicken or vegetable broth
  • 1 can (14 oz) diced tomatoes
  • 2 teaspoons dried thyme
  • 1 teaspoon dried rosemary
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • Fresh parsley, for garnish

Instructions

  1. In a large soup pot or Dutch oven, cook the Italian sausage over medium heat until browned, breaking it up into smaller pieces with a spoon. Remove the sausage from the pot and set aside.

  2. In the same pot, add the diced onion, minced garlic, diced carrots, and diced celery. Sauté for about 5 minutes, or until the vegetables have softened.

  3. Return the cooked sausage to the pot and stir well to combine with the vegetables.

  4. Add the rinsed and drained lentils, chicken or vegetable broth, diced tomatoes (with their juices), dried thyme, and dried rosemary to the pot. Season with salt and pepper, to taste.

  5. Bring the soup to a boil, then reduce the heat to low and simmer for about 30-40 minutes, or until the lentils are tender.

  6. Taste the soup and adjust the seasoning if needed. If the soup is too thick, you can add more broth or water to reach your desired consistency.

  7. Serve the soul-soothing winter sausage and lentil soup hot, garnished with fresh parsley for an added pop of color and freshness.

Why We Love This Soup

This soul-soothing winter sausage and lentil soup is the epitome of comfort food. The combination of Italian sausage, lentils, and aromatic herbs and spices creates a rich and flavorful broth that warms you from the inside out. The sausage adds a savory depth of flavor, while the lentils provide a hearty and satisfying texture.

Not only is this soup absolutely delicious, but it’s also incredibly nutritious. Lentils are an excellent source of plant-based protein and fiber, making this soup a filling and wholesome meal option. The vegetables add vitamins and minerals, and the herbs and spices not only enhance the flavor but also provide potential health benefits.

This recipe is also incredibly versatile. You can customize it to suit your taste preferences by using different types of sausage or adding additional vegetables. If you prefer a spicier kick, you can opt for hot Italian sausage or add some crushed red pepper flakes. Feel free to get creative and make this soup your own!

So, when the winter chill sets in and you’re in need of some cozy comfort, look no further than this soul-soothing winter sausage and lentil soup. It’s the perfect dish to warm you up on a cold winter’s day and nourish your body and soul. Give it a try, and you’ll see why it’s a favorite in our kitchen!

Note: This recipe can easily be doubled or halved to accommodate your needs. Leftovers can be stored in an airtight container in the refrigerator for up to 3 days or frozen for future enjoyment. Simply reheat on the stovetop or in the microwave until heated through.
