Leftover Remix: 5 Easy Ways to Give Your Leftovers a Delicious Upgrade


We’ve all been there – staring at a fridge full of leftovers, unsure of what to do with them. It’s easy to fall into the trap of simply reheating the same dish day after day until you can’t stand the sight of it anymore. But fear not! In this post, we’ll show you how to give your leftovers a delicious upgrade with just a few simple tweaks. Get ready to turn those forgotten meals into culinary masterpieces!

1. Leftover Pizza Frittata

Got a stack of leftover pizza slices? Don’t toss them out just yet! Turn them into a mouthwatering frittata that will leave your taste buds begging for more. Here’s how to do it:

  • Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
  • Break the pizza slices into bite-sized pieces and set aside.
  • In a bowl, whisk together 6 large eggs, ¼ cup of milk, salt, and pepper.
  • Heat a non-stick skillet over medium heat and add a drizzle of olive oil.
  • Add the pizza pieces to the skillet and cook for a few minutes until slightly crispy.
  • Pour the egg mixture over the pizza and cook for another 2-3 minutes, until the edges start to set.
  • Transfer the skillet to the preheated oven and bake for about 10 minutes, or until the frittata is set and golden brown.
  • Remove from the oven, let it cool slightly, and serve with a side salad for a complete meal.

2. Leftover Roast Chicken Tacos

Roast chicken is a versatile leftover that can be transformed into a variety of delicious meals. One of our favorites is roast chicken tacos. Here’s how to make them:

  • Shred the leftover roast chicken into bite-sized pieces and set aside.
  • Warm up some corn tortillas in a dry skillet over medium heat.
  • Fill each tortilla with the shredded roast chicken, and top with your favorite taco toppings such as salsa, guacamole, and shredded cheese.
  • Squeeze a wedge of lime over the tacos for an extra burst of flavor.
  • Serve with a side of Mexican rice and enjoy!

3. Leftover Pasta Bake

If you have leftover pasta from last night’s dinner, don’t let it go to waste. Transform it into a mouthwatering pasta bake that will have everyone asking for seconds. Here’s how to do it:

  • Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
  • Grease a baking dish with butter or cooking spray.
  • In a bowl, mix together the leftover pasta, your favorite pasta sauce, and any additional ingredients you like such as cooked vegetables or diced cooked meat.
  • Transfer the pasta mixture to the greased baking dish and top with shredded cheese.
  • Bake for about 20-25 minutes, or until the cheese is melted and bubbly.
  • Remove from the oven, let it cool slightly, and serve with a fresh green salad.

4. Leftover Rice Stir-fry

Leftover rice is a blank canvas for creating a quick and delicious stir-fry. Here’s how to make a flavorful rice stir-fry using your leftovers:

  • Heat a wok or a large skillet over high heat and add a drizzle of vegetable oil.
  • Add any leftover cooked vegetables or protein you have on hand, such as diced chicken, shrimp, or tofu.
  • Stir-fry for a few minutes until heated through.
  • Push the ingredients to one side of the pan and crack an egg into the empty space.
  • Scramble the egg and then mix it with the rest of the ingredients.
  • Add the leftover rice to the pan and stir-fry for another 2-3 minutes, until everything is well combined and heated through.
  • Season with soy sauce, sesame oil, and any other desired seasonings.
  • Serve hot and enjoy!

5. Leftover Vegetable Soup Smoothie

Yes, you read that right – a smoothie made from leftover vegetable soup! It may sound unconventional, but trust us, it’s delicious and a great way to use up those leftover veggies. Here’s how to make it:

  • In a blender, combine the leftover vegetable soup with a handful of ice cubes.
  • Blend until smooth and creamy.
  • Taste and adjust the seasoning if needed.
  • Pour into a glass and garnish with a sprig of fresh herbs or a drizzle of olive oil.
  • Sip and savor the unique combination of flavors in this refreshing and nutritious smoothie.

Conclusion

Leftovers don’t have to be boring or uninspiring. With a little creativity and some simple tweaks, you can transform your leftovers into delicious meals that will have everyone asking for seconds. Give these 5 easy leftover remixes a try and see for yourself how a little imagination can go a long way in the kitchen. Happy cooking!
