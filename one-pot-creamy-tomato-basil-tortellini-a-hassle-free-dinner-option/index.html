One-Pot Creamy Tomato Basil Tortellini: A Hassle-Free Dinner Option


Are you looking for a quick and delicious dinner option that won’t leave you with a pile of dishes to clean up afterward? Look no further than this One-Pot Creamy Tomato Basil Tortellini recipe! This dish is the perfect combination of creamy, savory, and comforting flavors, and the best part is, it all comes together in just one pot. Say goodbye to multiple pots and pans cluttering up your kitchen, and say hello to a hassle-free dinner option that will have your family asking for seconds.

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can (28 ounces) crushed tomatoes
  • 2 cups vegetable or chicken broth
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1/2 teaspoon red pepper flakes (adjust to taste)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 package (20 ounces) refrigerated cheese tortellini
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • Fresh basil leaves for garnish

Instructions

  1. Heat the olive oil in a large pot over medium heat. Add the chopped onion and sauté until it becomes translucent, about 3-4 minutes. Add the minced garlic and cook for an additional 1-2 minutes until fragrant.

  2. Pour in the crushed tomatoes and broth, then stir in the dried basil, oregano, red pepper flakes, salt, and pepper. Bring the mixture to a gentle boil.

  3. Once the mixture is boiling, add the tortellini to the pot and stir gently to ensure it doesn’t stick together. Reduce the heat to a simmer and let the tortellini cook according to the package instructions, usually for about 7-9 minutes.

  4. After the tortellini is cooked to al dente, stir in the heavy cream and grated Parmesan cheese. Allow the mixture to simmer for a few more minutes until the sauce thickens slightly and coats the back of a spoon.

  5. Taste and adjust the seasoning if necessary. If you prefer a thinner consistency, you can add a little more broth or cream.

  6. Once the sauce reaches your desired consistency, remove the pot from the heat. Serve the creamy tomato basil tortellini in individual bowls, garnished with fresh basil leaves and extra Parmesan cheese if desired.

Why One-Pot Meals?

One-pot meals are a lifesaver for busy weeknights when you want to put a delicious and satisfying meal on the table without spending hours in the kitchen or dealing with a mountain of dishes afterward. The beauty of one-pot recipes lies in their simplicity and ease of preparation. By cooking everything in a single pot, the flavors have a chance to meld together, resulting in a deeply satisfying dish that doesn’t compromise on taste.

Creamy Tomato Basil Tortellini: A Family Favorite

This One-Pot Creamy Tomato Basil Tortellini is a family favorite for many reasons. Not only is it incredibly delicious, but it’s also versatile and customizable. You can add your favorite protein such as cooked chicken or shrimp to make it heartier, or throw in some fresh spinach or kale for added nutrients. The creamy tomato basil sauce pairs perfectly with the cheesy tortellini, creating a dish that is both comforting and indulgent.

Make-Ahead and Freezing Instructions

If you’re looking to save even more time, you can prepare the sauce ahead of time and store it in an airtight container in the refrigerator for up to 2 days. When you’re ready to enjoy the dish, simply reheat the sauce in a pot and cook the tortellini according to the instructions. This makes it a great option for meal prep or for busy days when you want a homemade meal without the fuss.

Additionally, this creamy tomato basil tortellini freezes well. Simply let the dish cool completely, then transfer it to a freezer-safe container or resealable bags. When you’re ready to enjoy it, thaw it in the refrigerator overnight and reheat it in a pot over medium heat until warmed through.

In Conclusion

One-pot meals like this Creamy Tomato Basil Tortellini are a game-changer for anyone who loves good food but doesn’t love spending hours in the kitchen. With simple ingredients and minimal cleanup, this dish is a winner for busy weeknights, casual dinner parties, or anytime you crave a comforting and satisfying meal. Give this recipe a try and see for yourself just how delicious and hassle-free dinner can be!
