Grilled Goodness: BBQ-Style Tacos and Burritos


Grilled food lovers rejoice! We’re about to take your taste buds on a wild ride with our mouthwatering BBQ-style tacos and burritos. Get ready to fire up the grill and indulge in the smoky flavors that will transport you straight to food heaven. Whether you’re hosting a backyard barbecue or simply craving a delicious meal, these recipes are guaranteed to satisfy your cravings. So, grab your apron and let’s get grilling!

BBQ-Style Chicken Tacos

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1/2 cup BBQ sauce
  • 8 small flour tortillas
  • 1 red onion, thinly sliced
  • 1 avocado, sliced
  • Fresh cilantro, chopped

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the grill to medium-high heat.
  2. In a small bowl, combine the paprika, garlic powder, chili powder, cumin, salt, and pepper. Rub the spice mixture evenly onto the chicken breasts.
  3. Place the chicken on the grill and cook for about 6-8 minutes per side, or until the internal temperature reaches 165°F (75°C).
  4. Remove the chicken from the grill and let it rest for a few minutes. Then, thinly slice it.
  5. Warm the flour tortillas on the grill for about 1 minute per side.
  6. To assemble the tacos, spread a spoonful of BBQ sauce onto each tortilla. Top with sliced chicken, red onion, avocado, and cilantro.
  7. Serve the BBQ-style chicken tacos immediately and enjoy the explosion of flavors!

BBQ-Style Steak Burritos

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound flank steak
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons Worcestershire sauce
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon smoked paprika
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 4 large flour tortillas
  • 1 cup cooked rice
  • 1 cup black beans, drained and rinsed
  • 1 cup corn kernels
  • 1/2 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • Sour cream and guacamole for serving

Instructions:

  1. In a shallow dish, whisk together the soy sauce, Worcestershire sauce, olive oil, minced garlic, smoked paprika, salt, and pepper.
  2. Place the flank steak in the marinade and let it marinate for at least 30 minutes, or overnight for maximum flavor.
  3. Preheat the grill to medium-high heat.
  4. Remove the steak from the marinade and discard the excess marinade.
  5. Grill the steak for about 4-5 minutes per side, or until it reaches your desired level of doneness. Let the steak rest for a few minutes before slicing it thinly.
  6. Warm the flour tortillas on the grill for about 1 minute per side.
  7. To assemble the burritos, spread a spoonful of sour cream onto each tortilla. Top with cooked rice, black beans, corn kernels, sliced steak, and shredded cheddar cheese.
  8. Roll up the burritos, tucking in the sides as you go.
  9. Serve the BBQ-style steak burritos with a dollop of guacamole on top and enjoy the explosion of flavors and textures!

Grilled goodness doesn’t get any better than these BBQ-style tacos and burritos. The smoky flavors of the grilled chicken and steak, combined with the tangy BBQ sauce and fresh toppings, create a culinary experience that will leave you craving for more. So, fire up the grill, gather your loved ones, and enjoy a delicious meal that will surely impress. Happy grilling!
