Scrumptious Chicken Marsala Recipe - Italian Wine-Infused Dish


If you’re looking to impress your dinner guests or simply treat yourself to a restaurant-quality meal at home, then this Chicken Marsala recipe is a must-try. This classic Italian dish is a perfect combination of tender chicken, earthy mushrooms, and a rich Marsala wine sauce that will tantalize your taste buds. Let’s dive into the details of how to create this delectable dish in your own kitchen.

Ingredients

  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1/2 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 4 tablespoons unsalted butter
  • 4 tablespoons olive oil
  • 8 ounces mushrooms, sliced
  • 1/2 cup Marsala wine
  • 1/2 cup chicken broth
  • 1/4 cup heavy cream
  • Fresh parsley, chopped (for garnish)

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Chicken: Start by placing the chicken breasts between two sheets of plastic wrap and gently pound them to an even thickness. This will ensure that the chicken cooks evenly. In a shallow dish, mix the flour with salt, pepper, and dried oregano. Dredge each chicken breast in the seasoned flour, shaking off any excess.

  2. Sear the Chicken: In a large skillet, heat 2 tablespoons of butter and 2 tablespoons of olive oil over medium-high heat. Once the butter is melted and sizzling, add the chicken breasts to the skillet. Cook for about 3-4 minutes on each side until they are golden brown and cooked through. Remove the chicken from the skillet and set aside.

  3. Cook the Mushrooms: In the same skillet, add the remaining butter and olive oil. Toss in the sliced mushrooms and sauté them until they are golden and tender.

  4. Create the Marsala Sauce: Pour the Marsala wine into the skillet with the mushrooms, using a wooden spoon to scrape up any browned bits from the bottom of the pan. Let the wine simmer and reduce for a couple of minutes. Then, add the chicken broth and simmer for another few minutes until the sauce slightly thickens.

  5. Finish with Cream: Stir in the heavy cream and let the sauce simmer for a few more minutes until it coats the back of a spoon. Return the cooked chicken to the skillet, turning them to coat in the luscious Marsala sauce.

  6. Serve and Enjoy: Once the chicken is heated through, sprinkle the dish with chopped fresh parsley for a vibrant finish. Serve the Chicken Marsala over a bed of pasta, mashed potatoes, or with crusty bread to soak up the flavorful sauce.

Tips for Success

  • Use a good quality Marsala wine for the best flavor. Avoid using “cooking Marsala,” as it tends to be lower in quality.
  • If you prefer a thicker sauce, you can mix a teaspoon of cornstarch with a tablespoon of cold water and stir it into the sauce during the final simmering stage.
  • Feel free to customize the dish by adding a touch of garlic, shallots, or even a squeeze of lemon juice to the sauce for extra depth of flavor.

Final Thoughts

This Chicken Marsala recipe is a delightful blend of simple ingredients that come together to create a dish that’s perfect for both weeknight dinners and special occasions. The tender chicken, savory mushrooms, and luscious Marsala wine sauce make this recipe a timeless favorite. So, gather your ingredients, pour yourself a glass of wine, and let’s get cooking. Buon appetito!
