Chocolate Bread Pudding: Comforting and Delicious


Are you craving a warm and decadent dessert that will satisfy your sweet tooth? Look no further than this irresistible recipe for Chocolate Bread Pudding. Made with simple ingredients and bursting with rich chocolate flavor, this comforting dessert is sure to become a family favorite. Whether you’re hosting a dinner party or simply treating yourself to a special treat, this Chocolate Bread Pudding is a delightful choice.

Ingredients

  • 6 cups of stale bread, cubed
  • 2 cups of whole milk
  • 1 cup of heavy cream
  • 1 cup of granulated sugar
  • 4 large eggs
  • 1/4 cup of unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1 teaspoon of vanilla extract
  • 1/2 teaspoon of salt
  • 1 cup of semi-sweet chocolate chips

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease a 9x13-inch baking dish with butter or cooking spray.

  2. In a large mixing bowl, combine the milk and heavy cream. Whisk in the sugar until it is fully dissolved.

  3. Add the eggs, cocoa powder, vanilla extract, and salt to the bowl. Whisk until the mixture is well combined and smooth.

  4. Place the stale bread cubes into the prepared baking dish. Pour the chocolate mixture over the bread, making sure to evenly coat each cube.

  5. Sprinkle the chocolate chips over the top of the bread pudding, pressing them gently into the mixture.

  6. Allow the bread pudding to sit for about 15 minutes, giving the bread time to soak up the chocolate mixture.

  7. Place the baking dish in the preheated oven and bake for 45-50 minutes, or until the bread pudding is set and the top is golden brown.

  8. Remove the bread pudding from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes before serving. The pudding will continue to set as it cools.

  9. Serve your Chocolate Bread Pudding warm, either on its own or with a dollop of whipped cream or a scoop of vanilla ice cream. The creamy, chocolatey goodness will melt in your mouth, creating a truly indulgent experience.

Tips and Variations

  • Stale bread works best for this recipe as it absorbs the liquid mixture more effectively. If you don’t have stale bread on hand, you can simply cube fresh bread and leave it out overnight to dry.

  • Feel free to experiment with different types of bread. Brioche or challah bread add a rich and buttery flavor to the pudding, while French bread creates a denser texture.

  • For an extra indulgent twist, consider adding a handful of chopped nuts, such as walnuts or pecans, to the bread pudding mixture. They will provide a delightful crunch and complement the chocolate flavor perfectly.

  • If you prefer a more intense chocolate flavor, you can substitute some of the semi-sweet chocolate chips with dark chocolate chips or chunks.

  • Leftovers can be stored in an airtight container in the refrigerator for up to three days. Simply reheat individual portions in the microwave or oven before serving.

Conclusion

Chocolate Bread Pudding is the ultimate comfort dessert that combines the richness of chocolate with the softness of bread. This recipe is easy to make and will leave you and your loved ones craving for more. Treat yourself to a slice of this delicious pudding and indulge in the warm, gooey, and satisfying taste. Whether you’re looking for a dessert to impress guests or simply want to satisfy your chocolate cravings, this Chocolate Bread Pudding is the perfect choice. Enjoy!
