Cupid's Arrow Chicken Fajitas with Peppers and Onions and Sour Cream: A Valentine's Day Tex-Mex Recipe


Valentine’s Day is just around the corner, and what better way to celebrate with your loved one than with a delicious and romantic dinner? This Cupid’s Arrow Chicken Fajitas with Peppers and Onions and Sour Cream recipe is sure to hit the spot and leave you and your partner feeling loved and satisfied.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound boneless, skinless chicken breasts, cut into thin strips
  • 1/2 cup lime juice
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1/4 teaspoon paprika
  • 1/4 teaspoon cayenne pepper
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • 1 large onion, sliced
  • 2 large bell peppers (any color), sliced
  • 4 small flour tortillas
  • Sour cream, for serving
  • Optional toppings: diced tomatoes, shredded cheese, cilantro, avocado

Instructions

  1. In a large resealable plastic bag, combine lime juice, olive oil, garlic, oregano, cumin, paprika, cayenne pepper, salt, and pepper. Add the chicken and marinate for at least 30 minutes, or up to 2 hours in the refrigerator.
  2. Heat a large cast-iron skillet or griddle over medium-high heat. Remove the chicken from the marinade, letting any excess liquid drip off. Add the chicken to the skillet and cook until browned and cooked through, about 5-7 minutes. Transfer the chicken to a plate and cover with foil to keep warm.
  3. In the same skillet, add the sliced onions and bell peppers. Cook, stirring occasionally, until the vegetables are tender and lightly charred, about 10-12 minutes.
  4. Warm the tortillas by wrapping them in a damp paper towel and microwaving for 20-30 seconds.
  5. Assemble the fajitas by placing a portion of the chicken and vegetables onto a tortilla, and top with a dollop of sour cream. Add any desired toppings, such as diced tomatoes, shredded cheese, cilantro, and avocado.
  6. Serve immediately and enjoy!

Tips and Variations

  • For an added crunch, top the fajitas with crispy tortilla strips or crumbled queso fresco.
  • Substitute chicken breasts with shrimp or steak for a different protein option.
  • Add some heat to the dish by incorporating diced jalapeños or serrano peppers into the marinade or sautéing them with the onions and bell peppers.
  • Make it a vegetarian option by substituting the chicken with sautéed mushrooms or roasted sweet potatoes.

Why This Recipe is Perfect for Valentine’s Day

This Cupid’s Arrow Chicken Fajitas with Peppers and Onions and Sour Cream recipe is the perfect way to celebrate Valentine’s Day with your loved one. The dish is romantic, flavorful, and easy to make, making it a great option for a special occasion. The combination of tender chicken, crispy vegetables, and creamy sour cream is sure to impress your partner, and the Tex-Mex flavors will transport you to a warm and cozy dinner date. Plus, the recipe is easy to customize with your favorite toppings, making it a fun and interactive dining experience. So why not give it a try and make this Valentine’s Day one to remember?
