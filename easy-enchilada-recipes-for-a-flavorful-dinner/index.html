Easy Enchilada Recipes for a Flavorful Dinner


Are you craving a delicious and flavorful dinner that’s quick and easy to make? Look no further than these easy enchilada recipes! Enchiladas are a classic Mexican dish that combines tortillas, flavorful fillings, and a savory sauce. Whether you’re a seasoned chef or a beginner in the kitchen, these recipes are sure to impress your taste buds and satisfy your hunger. So, let’s dive right in!

1. Chicken Enchiladas

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups cooked and shredded chicken
  • 1 cup shredded cheese (cheddar or Monterey Jack)
  • 1/2 cup diced onion
  • 1/2 cup diced bell pepper
  • 1/4 cup diced jalapeno (optional for some extra heat)
  • 1 can (10 ounces) enchilada sauce
  • 8 flour tortillas
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. In a large bowl, combine the shredded chicken, shredded cheese, diced onion, diced bell pepper, and diced jalapeno (if using). Mix well.
  3. Warm the flour tortillas in the microwave for about 15 seconds to make them more pliable.
  4. Spoon the chicken mixture onto each tortilla and roll them up tightly. Place the enchiladas seam-side down in a lightly greased baking dish.
  5. Pour the enchilada sauce evenly over the rolled tortillas, making sure to cover them completely.
  6. Sprinkle some extra shredded cheese on top of the enchiladas.
  7. Cover the baking dish with aluminum foil and bake for 20 minutes.
  8. Remove the foil and bake for an additional 10 minutes, or until the cheese is melted and bubbly.
  9. Serve hot and enjoy!

2. Beef Enchiladas

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1/2 cup diced onion
  • 1/2 cup diced bell pepper
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can (10 ounces) red enchilada sauce
  • 1 can (4 ounces) diced green chilies
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 8 corn tortillas
  • 1 cup shredded cheese (cheddar or Mexican blend)

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. In a large skillet, brown the ground beef over medium heat. Drain any excess grease.
  3. Add the diced onion, diced bell pepper, and minced garlic to the skillet. Cook until the vegetables are tender.
  4. Stir in the red enchilada sauce, diced green chilies, ground cumin, salt, and pepper. Simmer for 5 minutes.
  5. Warm the corn tortillas in the microwave for about 15 seconds to make them more pliable.
  6. Spoon the beef mixture onto each tortilla and roll them up tightly. Place the enchiladas seam-side down in a lightly greased baking dish.
  7. Pour any remaining sauce over the rolled tortillas, making sure to cover them completely.
  8. Sprinkle the shredded cheese on top of the enchiladas.
  9. Cover the baking dish with aluminum foil and bake for 20 minutes.
  10. Remove the foil and bake for an additional 10 minutes, or until the cheese is melted and bubbly.
  11. Serve hot and enjoy!

3. Vegetarian Enchiladas

Ingredients:

  • 1 can (15 ounces) black beans, drained and rinsed
  • 1 cup corn kernels
  • 1/2 cup diced onion
  • 1/2 cup diced bell pepper
  • 1/4 cup diced jalapeno (optional for some extra heat)
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon chili powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 can (10 ounces) green enchilada sauce
  • 8 flour tortillas
  • 1 cup shredded cheese (cheddar or Monterey Jack)

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. In a large bowl, combine the black beans, corn kernels, diced onion, diced bell pepper, diced jalapeno (if using), ground cumin, chili powder, salt, and pepper. Mix well.
  3. Warm the flour tortillas in the microwave for about 15 seconds to make them more pliable.
  4. Spoon the bean mixture onto each tortilla and roll them up tightly. Place the enchiladas seam-side down in a lightly greased baking dish.
  5. Pour the green enchilada sauce evenly over the rolled tortillas, making sure to cover them completely.
  6. Sprinkle some extra shredded cheese on top of the enchiladas.
  7. Cover the baking dish with aluminum foil and bake for 20 minutes.
  8. Remove the foil and bake for an additional 10 minutes, or until the cheese is melted and bubbly.
  9. Serve hot and enjoy!

Final Thoughts

With these easy enchilada recipes, you can create a flavorful dinner that will impress your family and friends. Whether you prefer chicken, beef, or a vegetarian option, there’s an enchilada recipe for everyone. So, grab your ingredients and get cooking! These recipes are sure to become a staple in your kitchen. Enjoy the delicious flavors and the satisfaction of a homemade meal. Happy cooking!
