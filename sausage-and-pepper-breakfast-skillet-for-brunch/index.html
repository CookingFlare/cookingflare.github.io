Sausage and Pepper Breakfast Skillet for Brunch


When it comes to brunch, there’s nothing quite like a hearty, flavorful dish to start your day off right. This Sausage and Pepper Breakfast Skillet is the perfect combination of savory sausage, colorful bell peppers, and perfectly cooked eggs, all cooked together in one skillet for a convenient and delicious meal. Whether you’re hosting a weekend brunch with friends or simply want to treat yourself to a special morning meal, this recipe is sure to impress.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound of breakfast sausage, casings removed
  • 1 red bell pepper, thinly sliced
  • 1 green bell pepper, thinly sliced
  • 1 onion, thinly sliced
  • 4 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon of smoked paprika
  • 1 teaspoon of dried oregano
  • 1/2 teaspoon of red pepper flakes (optional)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 6 large eggs
  • Fresh parsley for garnish

Instructions

  1. Cook the Sausage: In a large skillet over medium heat, cook the sausage, breaking it up with a spoon, until it’s browned and cooked through. Use a slotted spoon to transfer the cooked sausage to a plate, leaving the rendered fat in the skillet.

  2. Sauté the Vegetables: In the same skillet with the rendered fat, add the sliced bell peppers and onion. Sauté for about 5 minutes, until the vegetables are softened and slightly caramelized. Add the minced garlic, smoked paprika, dried oregano, red pepper flakes (if using), salt, and pepper. Stir everything together and cook for another 2 minutes.

  3. Create Wells for the Eggs: Use a spoon to create 6 wells in the vegetable mixture. Crack an egg into each well, taking care not to break the yolks.

  4. Cook the Eggs: Cover the skillet and cook for about 5-7 minutes, until the egg whites are set but the yolks are still runny. If you prefer firmer yolks, cook for an additional 2-3 minutes.

  5. Serve: Sprinkle the cooked sausage over the skillet and garnish with fresh parsley. Serve the Sausage and Pepper Breakfast Skillet directly from the skillet, allowing everyone to dig in and enjoy!

Why This Recipe Works

This Sausage and Pepper Breakfast Skillet is a standout brunch dish for several reasons. The combination of savory sausage, sweet bell peppers, and aromatic spices creates a flavorful base for the eggs to cook in. The addition of smoked paprika and dried oregano adds depth of flavor, while the red pepper flakes provide a hint of heat for those who enjoy a little spice in their meals.

Cooking everything in one skillet not only makes for easy cleanup but also allows the flavors to meld together as the dish cooks. The eggs, cooked directly in the flavorful vegetable and sausage mixture, add a creamy, indulgent element to the dish that’s perfect for brunch.

Make It Your Own

This recipe is highly customizable to suit your preferences and dietary needs. You can use turkey sausage or plant-based sausage for a lighter option, or add in additional vegetables such as spinach, mushrooms, or cherry tomatoes for extra color and nutrition. For a cheesy twist, sprinkle some shredded cheddar or crumbled feta over the skillet during the last few minutes of cooking.

Final Thoughts

The Sausage and Pepper Breakfast Skillet is a fantastic option for brunch, whether you’re entertaining guests or simply treating yourself to a special morning meal. With its vibrant colors, robust flavors, and satisfying ingredients, it’s a recipe that’s sure to become a favorite in your brunch repertoire. So, gather your ingredients, heat up your skillet, and get ready to savor every delicious bite of this delightful brunch dish.
