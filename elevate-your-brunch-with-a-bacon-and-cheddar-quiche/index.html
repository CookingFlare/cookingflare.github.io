Elevate Your Brunch with a Bacon and Cheddar Quiche


If you’re looking to add a touch of elegance to your brunch spread, a bacon and cheddar quiche is the perfect dish to impress your guests. This classic savory pie is not only delicious but also quite versatile, allowing you to customize it with your favorite ingredients. Whether you’re hosting a brunch gathering or simply craving a special homemade meal, this bacon and cheddar quiche recipe is sure to elevate your culinary skills and delight your taste buds.

Ingredients

For the Crust

  • 1 1/4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, chilled and diced
  • 1/4 cup ice water

For the Filling

  • 6 slices of bacon, cooked and crumbled
  • 1 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1/2 cup chopped onion
  • 4 large eggs
  • 1 1/2 cups heavy cream
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground nutmeg

Optional Add-Ins

  • Sautéed mushrooms
  • Spinach
  • Sun-dried tomatoes
  • Diced ham
  • Fresh herbs such as thyme or chives

Instructions

Step 1: Prepare the Crust

  1. In a large mixing bowl, combine the flour and salt.
  2. Add the chilled, diced butter to the bowl and use a pastry cutter or your fingers to work the butter into the flour until the mixture resembles coarse crumbs.
  3. Gradually add the ice water, a tablespoon at a time, and mix the dough with a fork until it starts to come together.
  4. Gather the dough into a ball, flatten it into a disk, wrap it in plastic wrap, and refrigerate for at least 30 minutes.

Step 2: Preheat the Oven

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).

Step 3: Roll Out the Crust

  1. On a lightly floured surface, roll out the chilled dough into a circle large enough to fit into a 9-inch pie dish.
  2. Carefully transfer the dough to the pie dish, gently press it into the bottom and sides, and trim any excess overhang.

Step 4: Prepare the Filling

  1. In a mixing bowl, whisk together the eggs, heavy cream, salt, pepper, and nutmeg until well combined.
  2. Sprinkle the crumbled bacon, shredded cheddar cheese, and chopped onion evenly over the bottom of the pie crust.
  3. Pour the egg mixture over the bacon, cheese, and onion. If using any optional add-ins, distribute them evenly in the pie crust.

Step 5: Bake the Quiche

  1. Place the quiche in the preheated oven and bake for 40-45 minutes, or until the filling is set and the crust is golden brown.
  2. If the crust starts to brown too quickly, cover the edges with foil to prevent burning.

Step 6: Serve and Enjoy

  1. Once the quiche is done, remove it from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes before slicing.
  2. Serve the bacon and cheddar quiche warm, alongside a fresh green salad or a bowl of fruit for a delightful brunch experience.

Tips and Tricks

  • For a flakier crust, freeze the butter and use a box grater to grate it into the flour before mixing.
  • Experiment with different cheese varieties such as Gruyère, Swiss, or feta for a unique flavor profile.
  • To save time, you can prepare the crust and filling a day in advance and assemble the quiche just before baking.

Conclusion

Elevate your next brunch gathering with the irresistible aroma and flavor of a homemade bacon and cheddar quiche. This timeless dish offers a delightful combination of creamy, cheesy filling and crispy, buttery crust, making it a crowd-pleaser for any occasion. With the freedom to customize the fillings to your liking, this quiche recipe opens the door to endless flavor possibilities. Whether you’re a seasoned home cook or a brunch enthusiast looking to impress, this bacon and cheddar quiche is a must-try addition to your culinary repertoire. So, roll up your sleeves, gather your ingredients, and get ready to savor the indulgent goodness of a perfectly baked quiche.
