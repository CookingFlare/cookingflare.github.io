One-Pot Chicken Enchiladas: Easy Mexican-Inspired Recipes


Looking for a delicious and easy way to enjoy the flavors of Mexico in the comfort of your own kitchen? One-Pot Chicken Enchiladas are the perfect solution! This recipe is not only mouthwatering but also convenient as it requires just one pot for cooking, making cleanup a breeze. Let’s dive into how you can whip up this flavorful dish that will surely impress your friends and family.

Ingredients:

  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 pound boneless, skinless chicken breast, diced
  • 1 can black beans, drained and rinsed
  • 1 can corn kernels, drained
  • 1 can diced tomatoes
  • 1 can enchilada sauce
  • 2 cups chicken broth
  • 1 teaspoon chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 8-10 corn tortillas, cut into quarters
  • 2 cups shredded cheese
  • Fresh cilantro, for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Sauté the Aromatics: Heat olive oil in a large pot over medium heat. Add the diced onion and minced garlic, sautéing until fragrant.

  2. Cook the Chicken: Add the diced chicken breast to the pot and cook until no longer pink.

  3. Add Beans, Corn, and Tomatoes: Stir in the black beans, corn kernels, and diced tomatoes.

  4. Season the Mixture: Pour in the enchilada sauce, chicken broth, chili powder, cumin, salt, and pepper. Mix well to combine all the flavors.

  5. Simmer: Let the mixture simmer for about 10-15 minutes, allowing the flavors to meld together.

  6. Layer with Tortillas and Cheese: Place the corn tortilla quarters on top of the mixture. Sprinkle shredded cheese over the tortillas, creating layers.

  7. Finish in the Oven: Cover the pot and transfer it to a preheated oven. Bake at 375°F (190°C) for 20-25 minutes until the cheese is melted and bubbly.

  8. Garnish and Serve: Once cooked, garnish with fresh cilantro for a burst of freshness. Serve hot and enjoy the cheesy goodness!

Tips for Success:

  • Feel free to customize the recipe by adding your favorite toppings like sliced jalapeños, avocado, or sour cream.
  • Substitute the chicken with shredded rotisserie chicken for a quicker alternative.
  • For a spicier kick, mix in some chopped green chilies or a dash of hot sauce.

One-Pot Chicken Enchiladas are a fantastic way to enjoy the bold and vibrant flavors of Mexican cuisine without the hassle of multiple pots and pans. This recipe is not only a crowd-pleaser but also a time-saver, perfect for busy weeknights or casual gatherings. So, roll up your sleeves, grab your ingredients, and get ready to savor a taste of Mexico with this simple yet satisfying dish!
