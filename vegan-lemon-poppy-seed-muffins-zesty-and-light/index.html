Vegan Lemon Poppy Seed Muffins: Zesty and Light


There’s something truly delightful about the combination of zesty lemon and the subtle crunch of poppy seeds in a light, fluffy muffin. These Vegan Lemon Poppy Seed Muffins are a perfect treat for breakfast, brunch, or a snack any time of the day. Not only are they bursting with citrusy flavor, but they are also incredibly easy to make and completely vegan, making them a guilt-free indulgence for everyone to enjoy.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup almond milk (or any plant-based milk of your choice)
  • 1/2 cup maple syrup
  • 1/3 cup coconut oil, melted
  • 1/4 cup fresh lemon juice
  • Zest of 2 lemons
  • 2 tablespoons poppy seeds
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt

Instructions

Step 1: Preheat and Prepare

Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C) and line a muffin tin with paper liners or lightly grease the cups.

Step 2: Mix the Dry Ingredients

In a large mixing bowl, combine the flour, baking powder, baking soda, poppy seeds, and salt. Mix well to ensure the ingredients are evenly distributed.

Step 3: Mix the Wet Ingredients

In a separate bowl, whisk together the almond milk, maple syrup, melted coconut oil, lemon juice, lemon zest, and vanilla extract until well combined.

Step 4: Combine and Fold

Pour the wet ingredients into the bowl of dry ingredients. Gently fold the mixture together using a spatula. Be careful not to overmix; a few lumps are okay.

Step 5: Fill the Muffin Cups

Spoon the batter into the prepared muffin cups, filling each about two-thirds full.

Step 6: Bake

Place the muffin tin in the preheated oven and bake for 20-25 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center of a muffin comes out clean.

Step 7: Cool and Enjoy

Once baked, remove the muffins from the oven and allow them to cool in the tin for a few minutes before transferring them to a wire rack to cool completely. These muffins are best enjoyed when they are still slightly warm.

Serving Suggestions

These Vegan Lemon Poppy Seed Muffins are delicious on their own, but if you want to take them to the next level, consider drizzling them with a simple lemon glaze made from powdered sugar and fresh lemon juice. You can also sprinkle a few extra poppy seeds on top for a decorative touch.

Final Thoughts

With their bright, citrusy flavor and delicate texture, these Vegan Lemon Poppy Seed Muffins are sure to become a favorite in your household. Whether you’re a vegan, trying to cut back on dairy and eggs, or simply crave a delightful baked treat, these muffins are a must-try. So, gather your ingredients, preheat your oven, and let the aroma of lemon and poppy seeds fill your kitchen as you bake up a batch of these zesty and light delights. Enjoy!

Remember, these muffins are best enjoyed fresh, but they can also be stored in an airtight container at room temperature for up to three days. You can also freeze them for longer storage and simply reheat them in the microwave or oven for a warm, comforting treat anytime. Happy baking!
