One-Pot Creamy Tomato Basil Gnocchi Recipe


Are you looking for a quick and delicious meal that will satisfy your cravings without keeping you in the kitchen for hours? Look no further! This One-Pot Creamy Tomato Basil Gnocchi recipe is the answer to your prayers. With just a few simple ingredients and minimal prep time, you can whip up a flavorful and comforting dish that will impress your family and friends.

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 medium onion, finely chopped
  • 3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 can (14 ounces) diced tomatoes
  • 1 cup vegetable broth
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh basil
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • 1 package (16 ounces) potato gnocchi
  • Fresh basil leaves, for garnish

Instructions

  1. Heat the olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Add the chopped onion and minced garlic, and sauté until they become soft and fragrant, about 3-4 minutes.

  2. Add the diced tomatoes (with their juice) to the skillet, along with the vegetable broth. Bring the mixture to a simmer and let it cook for about 5 minutes, until the flavors meld together.

  3. Stir in the heavy cream, grated Parmesan cheese, and chopped fresh basil. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Allow the mixture to simmer for another 2-3 minutes, until it thickens slightly.

  4. Add the potato gnocchi to the skillet and gently stir to coat them with the creamy tomato basil sauce. Cover the skillet and let the gnocchi cook for approximately 5-6 minutes, or until they are tender and cooked through.

  5. Once the gnocchi are cooked, remove the skillet from the heat. Give it a final stir to combine all the flavors, and let it sit for a couple of minutes to allow the sauce to thicken further.

  6. Serve the One-Pot Creamy Tomato Basil Gnocchi hot, garnished with fresh basil leaves. Enjoy the creamy, tomato-infused goodness that will leave you craving for more.

Tips and Variations

  • If you want to add some protein to this dish, consider including cooked chicken, crispy bacon, or sautéed shrimp. Simply add them to the skillet at the same time as the gnocchi, and let them heat through.

  • For a vegetarian option, you can swap the vegetable broth for mushroom or vegetable stock. This will add an extra layer of flavor to the dish.

  • Feel free to experiment with different herbs and spices to customize the flavor of the creamy tomato basil sauce. Oregano, thyme, or even a pinch of red pepper flakes can add a delightful twist to the recipe.

  • If you prefer a lighter version of this dish, you can substitute the heavy cream with half-and-half or whole milk. The sauce will still be creamy and flavorful, but with fewer calories.

Conclusion

In just a single pot, you can create a delicious and comforting meal that will satisfy your taste buds. This One-Pot Creamy Tomato Basil Gnocchi recipe is perfect for busy weeknights when you want to spend less time cooking and more time enjoying your meal. With its creamy tomato sauce infused with aromatic basil, every bite will transport you to a world of culinary delight. So why wait? Gather your ingredients, fire up the stove, and treat yourself to a bowl of pure comfort and satisfaction.
