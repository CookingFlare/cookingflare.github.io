Sizzling Bacon-Wrapped BBQ Shrimp: Perfect for Summer Cookouts


Are you ready to take your summer cookouts to the next level? Look no further than this mouthwatering recipe for sizzling bacon-wrapped BBQ shrimp. With the perfect combination of smoky bacon, succulent shrimp, and a tangy barbecue sauce, this dish is sure to be a crowd-pleaser. Whether you’re hosting a backyard party or simply craving a delicious seafood treat, these bacon-wrapped BBQ shrimp are guaranteed to impress.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound of large shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 12 slices of bacon
  • 1/2 cup of your favorite barbecue sauce
  • 2 tablespoons of brown sugar
  • 1 teaspoon of garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon of paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon of cayenne pepper (optional)
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Preheat your grill to medium-high heat.

  2. In a small bowl, combine the barbecue sauce, brown sugar, garlic powder, paprika, cayenne pepper (if using), salt, and pepper. Mix well to create a flavorful marinade.

  3. Take a slice of bacon and wrap it tightly around a shrimp. Repeat this process for the remaining shrimp, ensuring that each one is securely wrapped in bacon.

  4. Place the bacon-wrapped shrimp in a shallow dish and pour the marinade over them. Make sure each shrimp is coated evenly. Allow the shrimp to marinate for at least 30 minutes, or up to 2 hours for maximum flavor.

  5. Once the shrimp have marinated, it’s time to grill them. Thread the shrimp onto skewers, making sure to leave a little space between each one.

  6. Place the skewers on the preheated grill and cook for 2-3 minutes per side, or until the bacon is crispy and the shrimp are opaque and pink.

  7. Remove the shrimp from the grill and let them rest for a few minutes before serving. This will allow the flavors to meld together and the shrimp to become even more tender.

  8. Serve the sizzling bacon-wrapped BBQ shrimp as an appetizer or as part of a main course. They pair perfectly with a side of grilled vegetables or a fresh summer salad.

Tips and Variations

  • For an extra kick of heat, add a dash of cayenne pepper to the marinade. Adjust the amount to suit your taste preferences.

  • If you don’t have access to a grill, you can also cook these bacon-wrapped BBQ shrimp in the oven. Simply place them on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper and bake at 400°F (200°C) for 12-15 minutes, or until the bacon is crispy and the shrimp are cooked through.

  • Feel free to experiment with different barbecue sauces to find your favorite flavor combination. Whether you prefer a smoky, sweet, or spicy sauce, there are endless possibilities to explore.

  • If you’re serving these shrimp as an appetizer, consider serving them with a dipping sauce on the side. A tangy remoulade or a creamy garlic aioli would be the perfect accompaniment.

  • Don’t limit yourself to shrimp! This bacon-wrapped BBQ technique works well with other seafood options such as scallops or even chunks of white fish.

Conclusion

With its irresistible combination of bacon, shrimp, and barbecue sauce, this recipe for sizzling bacon-wrapped BBQ shrimp is a must-try for any summer cookout. The smoky flavors from the grill, the crispy bacon, and the tender shrimp create a taste sensation that will leave your guests craving more. So fire up the grill, gather your friends and family, and get ready for a summer cookout that’s bursting with flavor. Enjoy!
