Tex-Mex Rice Skillet: A Flavor-Packed and Easy One-Pan Meal


Are you craving a delicious and hassle-free dinner that’s packed with flavor? Look no further than this Tex-Mex Rice Skillet recipe! This one-pan meal is not only incredibly tasty but also easy to prepare, making it perfect for busy weeknights or lazy weekends. With a combination of savory spices, tender rice, and a medley of colorful vegetables, this dish will surely become a family favorite. So, let’s get cooking!

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 red bell pepper, diced
  • 1 green bell pepper, diced
  • 1 cup corn kernels, fresh or frozen
  • 1 can (14 ounces) black beans, drained and rinsed
  • 1 can (14 ounces) diced tomatoes, undrained
  • 1 cup long-grain rice
  • 2 cups chicken broth
  • 1 tablespoon chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • 1/2 teaspoon paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • Optional toppings: shredded cheese, sliced avocado, chopped cilantro, sour cream, lime wedges

Instructions

  1. Heat the olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Add the ground beef and cook until browned, breaking it up into crumbles with a spoon. Remove any excess fat from the skillet.

  2. Add the diced onion and minced garlic to the skillet with the ground beef. Cook for 2-3 minutes, or until the onion becomes translucent and fragrant.

  3. Stir in the diced red and green bell peppers, corn kernels, black beans, and diced tomatoes with their juices. Mix well to combine all the ingredients.

  4. Add the long-grain rice, chicken broth, chili powder, cumin, paprika, salt, and black pepper to the skillet. Give everything a good stir to evenly distribute the spices.

  5. Bring the mixture to a boil, then reduce the heat to low. Cover the skillet with a lid and let it simmer for about 20 minutes, or until the rice is tender and has absorbed most of the liquid. Stir occasionally to prevent sticking.

  6. Once the rice is cooked, remove the skillet from the heat and let it sit, covered, for 5 minutes to allow the flavors to meld together.

  7. Serve the Tex-Mex Rice Skillet hot, garnished with your favorite toppings such as shredded cheese, sliced avocado, chopped cilantro, sour cream, and a squeeze of fresh lime juice. Feel free to get creative with the toppings and customize the dish to your liking!

Why You’ll Love This Recipe

This Tex-Mex Rice Skillet is not only a crowd-pleaser but also a time-saver. By cooking everything in one pan, you’re minimizing the cleanup and maximizing the convenience. The combination of spices gives this dish a vibrant and robust flavor profile, while the colorful vegetables add a delightful crunch and freshness. Whether you’re cooking for your family or hosting a casual dinner party, this recipe is sure to impress.

Moreover, this dish is versatile and customizable. You can easily swap out ingredients to suit your taste preferences or dietary restrictions. For a vegetarian version, simply omit the ground beef and double the amount of beans and vegetables. If you’re a spice enthusiast, feel free to add a pinch of cayenne pepper or some diced jalapenos for an extra kick. The possibilities are endless!

So, what are you waiting for? Grab your skillet and get ready to indulge in this flavor-packed Tex-Mex Rice Skillet. With its simplicity and deliciousness, it’s bound to become a staple in your recipe repertoire. Enjoy!
