Flavorful Lemongrass Chicken Stir Fry


Are you looking to add a burst of exotic flavors to your weeknight dinner routine? Look no further than this tantalizing Lemongrass Chicken Stir Fry recipe. Packed with zesty lemongrass, vibrant vegetables, and tender chicken, this dish is a perfect balance of freshness and savoriness. Let’s dive into the step-by-step guide to create this delectable dish in the comfort of your own kitchen.

Ingredients:

  • 500g chicken breast, thinly sliced
  • 3 stalks of lemongrass, finely chopped
  • 1 red bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 yellow bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 onion, thinly sliced
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1-inch piece of ginger, grated
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon oyster sauce
  • 1 tablespoon fish sauce
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh cilantro for garnish
  • Steamed rice to serve

Instructions:

  1. Marinate the Chicken: In a bowl, combine the sliced chicken breast with soy sauce, oyster sauce, fish sauce, sugar, minced garlic, grated ginger, and half of the chopped lemongrass. Mix well to ensure the chicken is coated evenly. Let it marinate for at least 15 minutes to allow the flavors to meld together.

  2. Sear the Chicken: Heat a wok or large skillet over high heat. Add a splash of oil and sear the marinated chicken until it is cooked through and slightly caramelized. Remove the chicken from the pan and set it aside.

  3. Stir Fry the Vegetables: In the same wok, add a bit more oil if needed. Toss in the remaining lemongrass, sliced bell peppers, and onion. Stir fry for a few minutes until the vegetables are slightly tender but still vibrant and crisp.

  4. Combine and Season: Return the cooked chicken to the wok with the vegetables. Stir everything together to combine. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Make sure the flavors are well-balanced before proceeding.

  5. Serve and Garnish: Once everything is heated through, remove the wok from the heat. Serve the Lemongrass Chicken Stir Fry over steamed rice. Garnish with fresh cilantro for an extra burst of flavor and color.

Tips for Success:

  • Prep Ahead: Consider marinating the chicken in advance to save time during the cooking process.
  • Customize Vegetables: Feel free to add your favorite vegetables like broccoli, snap peas, or mushrooms to personalize this dish.
  • Spice it Up: Add a touch of chili paste or fresh chilies if you prefer a spicier kick to your stir fry.
  • Leftover Love: This dish reheats well, making it a great option for meal prepping or enjoying as leftovers.

There you have it – a sensational Lemongrass Chicken Stir Fry that is sure to impress your taste buds. The fragrant lemongrass combined with the tender chicken and crisp vegetables create a harmony of flavors that will have you coming back for more. Whether you’re a stir fry enthusiast or new to Asian-inspired cuisine, this recipe is a must-try for anyone looking to elevate their culinary skills. Enjoy the journey of creating this delightful dish and savor every flavorful bite!
