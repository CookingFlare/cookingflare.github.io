Keto Lemon Garlic Shrimp: A Quick and Flavorful Seafood Recipe


Looking for a delicious and healthy seafood dish that’s quick and easy to prepare? Look no further than this Keto Lemon Garlic Shrimp recipe. Packed with flavor and low in carbohydrates, it’s the perfect meal for those following a keto lifestyle or anyone looking to enjoy a light and refreshing dish.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound of large shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 2 tablespoons of olive oil
  • 4 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon of paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon of red pepper flakes
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • Zest and juice of 1 lemon
  • 2 tablespoons of fresh parsley, chopped
  • 2 tablespoons of unsalted butter
  • Cauliflower rice or zucchini noodles (optional, for serving)

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Shrimp: In a large bowl, toss the shrimp with the olive oil, minced garlic, paprika, red pepper flakes, salt, and black pepper. Ensure that the shrimp are well coated with the seasonings.

  2. Sear the Shrimp: Heat a large skillet over medium-high heat. Once hot, add the shrimp in a single layer. Cook for 1-2 minutes on each side, until they turn pink and opaque. Be careful not to overcook the shrimp as they can become rubbery.

  3. Add Flavor: Once the shrimp are cooked, add the lemon zest and juice to the skillet. Stir to combine, allowing the flavors to meld for about 1 minute.

  4. Finish with Butter: Add the unsalted butter to the skillet and gently toss with the shrimp until it melts and creates a silky sauce.

  5. Garnish and Serve: Sprinkle the chopped fresh parsley over the shrimp for a burst of color and freshness. Serve the lemon garlic shrimp over a bed of cauliflower rice or zucchini noodles for a complete keto-friendly meal.

Why Keto Lemon Garlic Shrimp?

This recipe is not only a delight for your taste buds, but it’s also a fantastic option for those following a ketogenic diet. Shrimp is a great source of protein and healthy fats, making it a perfect choice for low-carb, high-fat diets. The addition of garlic, lemon, and fresh herbs provides depth of flavor without adding unnecessary carbs or sugars.

The use of olive oil and unsalted butter in this recipe ensures that you’re getting those essential healthy fats that are crucial for maintaining ketosis. Plus, the dish comes together in just under 15 minutes, making it an ideal option for a quick weeknight dinner or a stress-free entertaining option.

Final Thoughts

Whether you’re a seafood lover, a keto enthusiast, or simply someone looking for a light and flavorful meal, this Keto Lemon Garlic Shrimp recipe is sure to become a staple in your culinary repertoire. With its vibrant flavors and simple preparation, it’s a dish that proves you don’t have to compromise taste for health.

So, the next time you’re craving something zesty and satisfying, give this recipe a try and savor the delightful combination of lemon, garlic, and tender shrimp. Your taste buds and your body will thank you for it!
