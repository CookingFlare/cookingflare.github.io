Vegan Blueberry Galette: Rustic and Delicious


Are you craving a delicious and rustic dessert that is both vegan and bursting with flavor? Look no further than this Vegan Blueberry Galette recipe! Galettes are a simple and versatile dessert that are perfect for showcasing the natural sweetness of fresh fruits. This recipe takes advantage of plump and juicy blueberries, creating a mouthwatering treat that will impress your family and friends. So, let’s get started!

Ingredients

For the crust:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 tablespoon sugar
  • 1/2 cup plant-based butter, cold and cubed
  • 1/4 cup ice water

For the filling:

  • 4 cups fresh blueberries
  • 1/3 cup sugar
  • 2 tablespoons cornstarch
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).

  2. In a large mixing bowl, combine the flour, salt, and sugar for the crust. Add the cold and cubed plant-based butter and use a pastry cutter or your hands to incorporate it into the flour mixture until it resembles coarse crumbs.

  3. Slowly drizzle in the ice water while gently mixing the dough with a fork. Continue mixing until the dough comes together and forms a ball. Be careful not to overmix.

  4. Transfer the dough onto a lightly floured surface and shape it into a disk. Wrap it in plastic wrap and refrigerate for at least 30 minutes to allow it to rest.

  5. In another bowl, combine the blueberries, sugar, cornstarch, lemon juice, and vanilla extract for the filling. Gently toss the ingredients together until the blueberries are evenly coated.

  6. Remove the dough from the refrigerator and place it on a piece of parchment paper. Roll it out into a circle about 12 inches in diameter. The edges can be rustic and uneven - that’s the beauty of a galette!

  7. Transfer the parchment paper with the rolled-out dough onto a baking sheet. Spoon the blueberry filling onto the center of the dough, leaving a 2-inch border around the edges.

  8. Fold the edges of the dough over the filling, creating pleats as you go. This will help contain the filling and give the galette its signature rustic appearance.

  9. Bake the galette in the preheated oven for 35-40 minutes, or until the crust is golden brown and the filling is bubbling.

  10. Remove the galette from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes before serving. You can enjoy it warm or at room temperature.

  11. Serve slices of the Vegan Blueberry Galette on their own, or with a dollop of vegan whipped cream or a scoop of vegan ice cream for an extra treat!

Conclusion

This Vegan Blueberry Galette is the perfect combination of rustic charm and delicious flavor. The flaky crust pairs perfectly with the sweet and tangy blueberry filling, creating a dessert that will satisfy your cravings and impress your taste buds. Whether you’re a vegan or just looking to try something new, this galette is sure to become a favorite in your household. So, go ahead and give it a try - you won’t be disappointed!
