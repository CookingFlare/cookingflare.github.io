Delicious Hungarian Paprika Chicken: 6 Recipes to Try


If you’re a fan of bold flavors and hearty meals, Hungarian cuisine has a lot to offer. One dish that stands out is the Delicious Hungarian Paprika Chicken. This flavorful and comforting dish is infused with the rich flavors of paprika, garlic, and onions. In this blog post, we’ll explore six different recipes for Hungarian Paprika Chicken that you can try at home. From classic stews to modern twists, these recipes will surely tantalize your taste buds.

1. Traditional Hungarian Paprika Chicken

Ingredients:

  • 1 whole chicken, cut into pieces
  • 2 tablespoons Hungarian sweet paprika
  • 2 onions, finely chopped
  • 4 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 red bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 cup chicken broth
  • 1 cup sour cream
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil

Instructions:

  1. Heat the vegetable oil in a large pot over medium-high heat. Add the chicken pieces and cook until browned on all sides. Remove the chicken from the pot and set aside.
  2. In the same pot, add the onions and garlic. Cook until they become translucent and fragrant.
  3. Add the paprika and stir well to coat the onions and garlic.
  4. Return the chicken to the pot and add the bell pepper. Pour in the chicken broth and season with salt and pepper.
  5. Cover the pot and simmer for about 40 minutes, or until the chicken is cooked through and tender.
  6. Stir in the sour cream and simmer for an additional 5 minutes.
  7. Serve the Traditional Hungarian Paprika Chicken hot with steamed rice or crusty bread.

2. Hungarian Paprika Chicken Soup

Ingredients:

  • 4 chicken breasts, boneless and skinless, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 2 tablespoons Hungarian sweet paprika
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 2 carrots, diced
  • 2 potatoes, peeled and cubed
  • 4 cups chicken broth
  • 1 cup sour cream
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley for garnish

Instructions:

  1. In a large pot, heat some vegetable oil over medium heat. Add the chicken pieces and cook until browned. Remove the chicken from the pot and set aside.
  2. In the same pot, add the onions and garlic. Cook until they become translucent and fragrant.
  3. Sprinkle the paprika over the onions and garlic, stirring well to coat.
  4. Add the carrots, potatoes, and chicken broth. Season with salt and pepper.
  5. Bring the soup to a boil, then reduce the heat to low and simmer for about 20 minutes, or until the vegetables are tender.
  6. Return the chicken to the pot and simmer for an additional 10 minutes.
  7. Stir in the sour cream and cook for a few more minutes until heated through.
  8. Garnish with fresh parsley before serving.

3. Hungarian Paprika Chicken Casserole

Ingredients:

  • 8 chicken thighs, bone-in and skin-on
  • 2 tablespoons Hungarian sweet paprika
  • 1 onion, thinly sliced
  • 1 red bell pepper, thinly sliced
  • 1 yellow bell pepper, thinly sliced
  • 1 green bell pepper, thinly sliced
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 cup chicken broth
  • 1 cup sour cream
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Season the chicken thighs with salt, pepper, and half of the paprika.
  3. In a large skillet, heat some vegetable oil over medium-high heat. Add the chicken thighs, skin side down, and cook until browned. Remove the chicken from the skillet and set aside.
  4. In the same skillet, add the onions, bell peppers, and garlic. Cook until they become soft and slightly caramelized.
  5. Sprinkle the remaining paprika over the vegetables, stirring well to coat.
  6. Pour in the chicken broth and bring to a simmer.
  7. Return the chicken thighs to the skillet, skin side up, and transfer the skillet to the preheated oven.
  8. Bake for about 30 minutes, or until the chicken is cooked through and the skin is crispy.
  9. Remove the skillet from the oven and let it cool slightly. Stir in the sour cream.
  10. Garnish with fresh parsley before serving.

4. Hungarian Paprika Chicken Skewers

Ingredients:

  • 4 chicken breasts, boneless and skinless, cut into cubes
  • 2 tablespoons Hungarian sweet paprika
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons honey
  • 2 tablespoons apple cider vinegar
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, whisk together the paprika, olive oil, honey, apple cider vinegar, garlic, salt, and pepper.
  2. Add the chicken cubes to the bowl and toss to coat them evenly with the marinade. Let them marinate for at least 30 minutes, or overnight in the refrigerator.
  3. Preheat the grill to medium-high heat.
  4. Thread the marinated chicken cubes onto skewers.
  5. Grill the skewers for about 8-10 minutes, turning occasionally, until the chicken is cooked through and slightly charred.
  6. Serve the Hungarian Paprika Chicken Skewers hot with a side of tzatziki sauce or a fresh salad.

5. Hungarian Paprika Chicken Stir-Fry

Ingredients:

  • 2 chicken breasts, boneless and skinless, thinly sliced
  • 2 tablespoons Hungarian sweet paprika
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon honey
  • 1 tablespoon sesame oil
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • 1 onion, thinly sliced
  • 1 red bell pepper, thinly sliced
  • 1 green bell pepper, thinly sliced
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, whisk together the paprika, soy sauce, honey, sesame oil, salt, and pepper.
  2. Add the chicken slices to the bowl and toss to coat them evenly with the marinade. Let them marinate for at least 15 minutes.
  3. Heat the vegetable oil in a large skillet or wok over high heat. Add the onions, bell peppers, and garlic. Stir-fry for a few minutes until the vegetables are slightly softened.
  4. Add the marinated chicken slices to the skillet and stir-fry for about 5-7 minutes, or until the chicken is cooked through.
  5. Serve the Hungarian Paprika Chicken Stir-Fry hot with steamed rice or noodles.

6. Hungarian Paprika Chicken Salad

Ingredients:

  • 2 chicken breasts, boneless and skinless, grilled and sliced
  • 2 tablespoons Hungarian sweet paprika
  • 2 tablespoons lemon juice
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon honey
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Mixed salad greens
  • Cherry tomatoes, halved
  • Cucumber, sliced
  • Red onion, thinly sliced
  • Feta cheese, crumbled

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, whisk together the paprika, lemon juice, olive oil, honey, salt, and pepper.
  2. Add the grilled and sliced chicken breasts to the bowl and toss to coat them evenly with the dressing. Let them marinate for at least 10 minutes.
  3. In a large salad bowl, combine the mixed salad greens, cherry tomatoes, cucumber, red onion, and feta cheese.
  4. Add the marinated chicken slices to the salad bowl and toss everything together.
  5. Serve the Hungarian Paprika Chicken Salad chilled as a refreshing and flavorful meal.

Whether you prefer a classic stew, a hearty casserole, or a lighter option like a salad, these six recipes for Delicious Hungarian Paprika Chicken are sure to satisfy your cravings. Experiment with different variations and make adjustments according to your taste preferences. Enjoy the bold and comforting flavors of Hungarian cuisine right in your own kitchen!
