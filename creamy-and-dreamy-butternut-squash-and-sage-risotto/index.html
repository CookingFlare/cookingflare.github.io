Creamy and Dreamy Butternut Squash and Sage Risotto


Risotto, with its creamy texture and rich flavors, has long been a favorite comfort food for many. When combined with the earthy sweetness of butternut squash and the aromatic essence of sage, it transforms into a dish that is both comforting and sophisticated. This recipe for Butternut Squash and Sage Risotto celebrates the flavors of fall, making it perfect for cozy dinners or festive gatherings.

Ingredients

To create this creamy and dreamy Butternut Squash and Sage Risotto, you’ll need:

  • 1 small butternut squash, peeled, seeded, and diced into small cubes
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 ½ cups Arborio rice
  • 1 cup dry white wine
  • 6 cups vegetable or chicken broth, kept warm
  • 1 tablespoon fresh sage leaves, chopped (plus extra for garnish)
  • ½ cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 tablespoons unsalted butter
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Olive oil for cooking

Instructions

Preparing the Butternut Squash

  1. Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C).
  2. Place the diced butternut squash on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper.
  3. Drizzle olive oil over the squash, season with salt and pepper, and toss to coat evenly.
  4. Roast in the preheated oven for 20-25 minutes, or until the squash is tender and lightly caramelized. Set aside.

Making the Risotto

  1. In a large skillet or heavy-bottomed pot, heat 2 tablespoons of olive oil over medium heat.
  2. Add the chopped onion and cook until softened and translucent, about 5 minutes.
  3. Stir in the minced garlic and cook for another 1-2 minutes until fragrant.
  4. Add the Arborio rice to the skillet and toast it for 2-3 minutes, stirring constantly, until the rice grains are translucent around the edges.

Cooking the Risotto

  1. Pour in the white wine and stir continuously until the wine is absorbed by the rice.
  2. Begin adding the warm broth to the rice, one ladleful at a time, stirring frequently.
  3. Allow each addition of broth to be absorbed before adding the next. This process will take about 18-20 minutes. The rice should be creamy and tender, with a slight firmness (al dente) when done.

Adding the Butternut Squash and Sage

  1. Once the risotto reaches the desired consistency, stir in the roasted butternut squash cubes and chopped sage leaves.
  2. Cook for an additional 2-3 minutes to heat through and allow the flavors to meld together.
  3. Remove from heat and stir in the grated Parmesan cheese and butter until melted and creamy.
  4. Season with salt and pepper to taste.

Serving

  1. Divide the Butternut Squash and Sage Risotto among serving plates or bowls.
  2. Garnish with additional chopped sage leaves and a sprinkle of Parmesan cheese if desired.
  3. Serve hot, and enjoy the creamy, savory goodness of this autumn-inspired dish.

Tips for Success

  • Consistency: Risotto should be creamy and slightly runny, not stiff.
  • Stirring: Stir frequently during the cooking process to release the rice’s starches, creating a creamy texture.
  • Ingredients: Use fresh sage for the best flavor, and adjust the amount of Parmesan cheese according to your preference.

Variations

  • Vegetarian: Substitute vegetable broth for chicken broth to keep this dish vegetarian-friendly.
  • Vegan: Replace butter and Parmesan cheese with vegan alternatives for a dairy-free version.
  • Additions: Incorporate cooked pancetta or crispy bacon for added richness and texture.

Enjoy Your Butternut Squash and Sage Risotto!

This Butternut Squash and Sage Risotto recipe is sure to become a staple in your fall cooking repertoire. Its creamy texture, combined with the sweet and earthy flavors of butternut squash and aromatic sage, makes it a dish that delights the senses and warms the soul. Whether you’re hosting a dinner party or simply craving a comforting meal, this risotto is perfect for any occasion. So gather your ingredients, embrace the flavors of the season, and savor every spoonful of this delightful dish!
