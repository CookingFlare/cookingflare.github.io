Classic American Biscuits and Gravy Recipe for Breakfast


There’s nothing quite like waking up to the comforting aroma of freshly baked biscuits and the savory goodness of creamy sausage gravy. This classic American breakfast dish has been a staple in Southern kitchens for generations, and for good reason. The flaky, buttery biscuits smothered in rich, flavorful gravy make for a hearty and satisfying meal that’s perfect for a leisurely weekend brunch or a special treat any day of the week.

The Perfect Biscuits

To start off this classic recipe, we’ll need to prepare the perfect biscuits. Here’s what you’ll need:

  • 2 cups of all-purpose flour
  • 1 tablespoon of baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon of sugar
  • 1 teaspoon of salt
  • 6 tablespoons of unsalted butter, chilled and cut into small pieces
  • 3/4 cup of buttermilk
  1. Preheat your oven to 425°F (220°C) and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
  2. In a large mixing bowl, whisk together the flour, baking powder, sugar, and salt.
  3. Add the chilled butter to the dry ingredients and use a pastry cutter or your fingertips to work the butter into the flour mixture until it resembles coarse crumbs.
  4. Pour in the buttermilk and stir until the dough just comes together.
  5. Turn the dough out onto a lightly floured surface and gently knead it a few times until it holds together.
  6. Pat the dough into a 1-inch thick round and use a biscuit cutter to cut out the biscuits. Place the biscuits on the prepared baking sheet, leaving a little space between each one.
  7. Gather the remaining dough, pat it out again, and cut out more biscuits until all the dough is used.
  8. Bake the biscuits for 15-18 minutes, or until they are golden brown and cooked through.

Hearty Sausage Gravy

Now, let’s move on to the heart of this classic dish - the sausage gravy. Here are the ingredients you’ll need:

  • 1/2 pound of breakfast sausage
  • 1/4 cup of all-purpose flour
  • 3 cups of whole milk
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  1. In a large skillet over medium heat, cook the breakfast sausage, breaking it up with a spoon as it browns.
  2. Once the sausage is fully cooked, sprinkle the flour over the sausage and stir to combine. Cook for a minute to get rid of the raw flour taste.
  3. Gradually pour in the milk, stirring constantly to prevent lumps from forming.
  4. Continue to cook the gravy, stirring frequently, until it thickens to your desired consistency.
  5. Season the gravy with salt and pepper to taste.

Bringing It All Together

Once the biscuits are golden brown and the sausage gravy is thick and creamy, it’s time to bring it all together.

Split the warm biscuits in half and place them on serving plates. Ladle the hot sausage gravy over the biscuits, allowing it to spill over the sides. The contrast of the flaky biscuits and the creamy gravy is a match made in breakfast heaven.

Serving Suggestions

Classic American Biscuits and Gravy pairs wonderfully with a side of scrambled eggs, crispy bacon, or fresh fruit for a well-rounded breakfast. It’s a dish that’s best enjoyed hot and fresh from the kitchen, so gather your loved ones around the table and savor every comforting bite.

Conclusion

Whether you’re preparing a special breakfast for guests or simply treating yourself to a delicious morning indulgence, Classic American Biscuits and Gravy is a timeless dish that never fails to satisfy. The buttery, flaky biscuits and the rich, savory gravy come together to create a breakfast experience that’s both comforting and indulgent, making it a beloved favorite in American homes. So, roll up your sleeves, preheat that oven, and get ready to savor the classic flavors of this iconic Southern breakfast dish!
