Indulgent Nutella French Toast Recipes for a Decadent Brunch


Who doesn’t love a delicious and indulgent brunch? And when it comes to brunch, French toast is an absolute classic. But why settle for ordinary French toast when you can take it to a whole new level of decadence with the addition of Nutella? In this post, we’ll explore three mouthwatering Nutella French toast recipes that will make your brunch a truly indulgent affair.

Classic Nutella French Toast

Ingredients:

  • 4 slices of thick bread (brioche or challah work best)
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/2 cup of milk
  • 1 teaspoon of vanilla extract
  • Nutella
  • Butter, for frying
  • Powdered sugar, for dusting

Instructions:

  1. In a shallow bowl, whisk together the eggs, milk, and vanilla extract.
  2. Spread Nutella generously on two slices of bread, then sandwich them with the remaining two slices.
  3. Heat a knob of butter in a non-stick skillet over medium heat.
  4. Dip the Nutella sandwiches into the egg mixture, ensuring they are fully coated on both sides.
  5. Place the soaked sandwiches in the skillet and cook until golden brown, about 2-3 minutes per side.
  6. Repeat with the remaining sandwiches.
  7. Serve the Nutella French toast warm, dusted with powdered sugar.

Nutella-Stuffed French Toast Roll-Ups

Ingredients:

  • 8 slices of bread
  • Nutella
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/2 cup of milk
  • 1 teaspoon of vanilla extract
  • Butter, for frying
  • Maple syrup, for serving

Instructions:

  1. Trim the crusts off the bread slices and flatten them with a rolling pin.
  2. Spread Nutella on each slice of bread, leaving a small border around the edges.
  3. Roll up each slice tightly and set aside.
  4. In a shallow bowl, whisk together the eggs, milk, and vanilla extract.
  5. Heat a knob of butter in a non-stick skillet over medium heat.
  6. Dip each Nutella roll-up into the egg mixture, ensuring they are fully coated.
  7. Place the roll-ups in the skillet and cook until golden brown, about 2-3 minutes per side.
  8. Serve the Nutella French toast roll-ups warm, drizzled with maple syrup.

Nutella-Stuffed French Toast Casserole

Ingredients:

  • 1 loaf of French bread, cut into cubes
  • Nutella
  • 6 eggs
  • 2 cups of milk
  • 1/4 cup of granulated sugar
  • 1 teaspoon of vanilla extract
  • Butter, for greasing
  • Maple syrup, for serving

Instructions:

  1. Grease a baking dish with butter and set aside.
  2. Spread Nutella on half of the bread cubes and place them in the greased baking dish.
  3. Top with the remaining bread cubes.
  4. In a large bowl, whisk together the eggs, milk, sugar, and vanilla extract.
  5. Pour the egg mixture over the bread cubes, ensuring they are evenly coated.
  6. Cover the baking dish with foil and refrigerate for at least 2 hours or overnight.
  7. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C).
  8. Bake the Nutella French toast casserole, covered, for 30 minutes.
  9. Remove the foil and bake for an additional 15 minutes, or until golden brown and set.
  10. Serve the Nutella French toast casserole warm, with a drizzle of maple syrup.
