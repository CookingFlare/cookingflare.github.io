Savor the Flavor: Gluten-Free Pasta Primavera Recipe


If you’re looking for a delicious and healthy gluten-free pasta dish, look no further! This Gluten-Free Pasta Primavera recipe is a celebration of fresh, vibrant flavors and is the perfect way to savor the bountiful produce of the season. Packed with colorful vegetables and tossed with gluten-free pasta, this dish is a feast for the eyes and the palate. Whether you’re following a gluten-free diet or simply seeking a lighter alternative to traditional pasta dishes, this recipe is sure to become a favorite in your culinary repertoire.

Ingredients

  • 12 oz gluten-free penne pasta
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 small red onion, thinly sliced
  • 1 red bell pepper, julienned
  • 1 yellow bell pepper, julienned
  • 1 small zucchini, halved and sliced
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1 cup broccoli florets
  • 1/2 cup fresh or frozen peas
  • 1/4 cup vegetable or chicken broth
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese (optional)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh basil, chopped, for garnish

Instructions

  1. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Cook the gluten-free penne pasta according to the package instructions. Drain and set aside.

  2. In a large skillet, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the minced garlic and sauté for about 1 minute until fragrant.

  3. Add the sliced red onion to the skillet and cook for 2-3 minutes until softened.

  4. Toss in the julienned red and yellow bell peppers, and sauté for 3-4 minutes until they begin to soften.

  5. Add the sliced zucchini, cherry tomatoes, broccoli florets, and peas to the skillet. Cook for an additional 5-6 minutes, stirring occasionally, until the vegetables are tender-crisp.

  6. Pour in the vegetable or chicken broth and let the mixture simmer for 2-3 minutes. This will help create a light sauce that coats the vegetables and pasta.

  7. Add the cooked gluten-free penne pasta to the skillet and toss everything together until well-combined.

  8. If using, sprinkle the grated Parmesan cheese over the pasta primavera and gently mix it in.

  9. Season with salt and pepper to taste.

  10. Garnish with freshly chopped basil before serving.

Serving Suggestions

This Gluten-Free Pasta Primavera is a versatile dish that can be enjoyed on its own as a satisfying meal or paired with a protein of your choice, such as grilled chicken or shrimp. It’s also delightful served alongside a crisp green salad and a glass of your favorite white wine.

Final Thoughts

With its vibrant medley of vegetables and gluten-free pasta, this Pasta Primavera recipe is a celebration of the joys of seasonal eating. It’s a testament to the fact that gluten-free dining can be both nutritious and utterly delicious. So, next time you’re craving a taste of summer on a plate, whip up a batch of this Pasta Primavera and savor the fresh, vibrant flavors of the season.

We hope you enjoy making and indulging in this delightful Gluten-Free Pasta Primavera recipe. Happy cooking!
