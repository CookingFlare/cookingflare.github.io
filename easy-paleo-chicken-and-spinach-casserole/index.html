Easy Paleo Chicken and Spinach Casserole


If you’re on the lookout for a delicious and healthy meal that’s easy to prepare, look no further than this Easy Paleo Chicken and Spinach Casserole. Packed with protein, vitamins, and flavor, this dish is perfect for those following a paleo lifestyle or anyone looking for a nutritious, satisfying meal. Let’s dive into the recipe and discover how to make this delectable casserole that’s sure to become a family favorite.

Ingredients:

  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 2 cups fresh spinach
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1 small onion, diced
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup coconut milk
  • 1/2 cup chicken broth
  • 1 tablespoon arrowroot powder
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Cooking fat (coconut oil, ghee, or olive oil)

Instructions:

Step 1: Preheat and Prepare

Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C). Grease a casserole dish with your choice of cooking fat and set it aside.

Step 2: Cook the Chicken

In a large skillet over medium heat, add a tablespoon of cooking fat. Season the chicken breasts with salt, pepper, oregano, and thyme, then place them in the skillet. Cook for 5-6 minutes on each side, or until browned and cooked through. Once done, remove the chicken from the skillet and set it aside to cool.

Step 3: Sauté the Vegetables

Using the same skillet, add a bit more cooking fat if needed. Sauté the diced onion and minced garlic until they become fragrant and translucent. Add the cherry tomatoes and cook for an additional 2-3 minutes. Finally, add the fresh spinach and cook until wilted. Remove the skillet from heat and set it aside.

Step 4: Prepare the Sauce

In a small bowl, whisk together the coconut milk, chicken broth, arrowroot powder, paprika, salt, and pepper until well combined.

Step 5: Assemble the Casserole

Slice the cooked chicken breasts into bite-sized pieces. Place half of the sautéed vegetables in the greased casserole dish, followed by the sliced chicken. Pour half of the prepared sauce over the chicken and vegetables. Then, layer the remaining vegetables on top and pour the rest of the sauce over the casserole.

Step 6: Bake

Cover the casserole dish with foil and bake in the preheated oven for 25-30 minutes, or until the casserole is bubbling and the chicken is heated through.

Step 7: Serve and Enjoy

Once done, remove the casserole from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes before serving. This Easy Paleo Chicken and Spinach Casserole pairs well with a fresh green salad or steamed vegetables. Enjoy the flavorful and nutritious meal with your loved ones!

Conclusion:

This Easy Paleo Chicken and Spinach Casserole is a simple yet satisfying dish that’s perfect for a weeknight dinner or a weekend gathering. With wholesome ingredients and delightful flavors, it’s a go-to recipe for those following a paleo lifestyle or anyone seeking a nourishing meal. Give this recipe a try and savor the wholesome goodness of this delightful casserole!
