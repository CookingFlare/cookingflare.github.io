Hearty Instant Pot Beef Stew Recipe with Red Wine


There’s nothing quite like a warm, comforting bowl of beef stew on a chilly evening. And when you add a splash of red wine to the mix, the flavors reach a whole new level of richness and depth. In this recipe, we’ll show you how to make a hearty and flavorful Instant Pot beef stew with red wine that will have your family and friends coming back for seconds.

Ingredients

  • 2 pounds of beef chuck, cut into cubes
  • 2 tablespoons of olive oil
  • 1 large onion, diced
  • 3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 3 carrots, peeled and chopped
  • 3 celery stalks, chopped
  • 1 pound of baby potatoes, halved
  • 2 cups of beef broth
  • 1 cup of red wine
  • 2 tablespoons of tomato paste
  • 1 teaspoon of dried thyme
  • 2 bay leaves
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 tablespoons of all-purpose flour
  • Chopped fresh parsley for garnish

Instructions

  1. Start by seasoning the beef cubes with salt and pepper. Then, select the “Sauté” function on your Instant Pot and heat the olive oil. Once hot, add the beef cubes in batches and brown them on all sides. Remove the beef and set it aside.

  2. In the same pot, add the diced onion and minced garlic. Sauté for a few minutes until they become fragrant and slightly golden. Add the carrots and celery, and cook for another 3-4 minutes.

  3. Sprinkle the flour over the vegetables and stir well to coat. This will help thicken the stew later on.

  4. Pour in the red wine and scrape the bottom of the pot to deglaze, ensuring you pick up all the flavorful browned bits. Let the wine simmer for a couple of minutes to cook off the alcohol.

  5. Add the browned beef back into the pot, along with the baby potatoes, beef broth, tomato paste, dried thyme, and bay leaves. Stir everything together to combine.

  6. Secure the lid on the Instant Pot and set the valve to the sealing position. Cook on high pressure for 35 minutes, then allow for a natural pressure release for 10 minutes before manually releasing any remaining pressure.

  7. Once the pressure has been released, carefully open the lid and give the stew a good stir. Taste and adjust the seasoning, if necessary.

  8. Ladle the beef stew into bowls, garnish with chopped fresh parsley, and serve with crusty bread or over a bed of mashed potatoes. Enjoy the rich flavors and tender beef in every spoonful.

Tips and Tricks

  • For an extra depth of flavor, you can add a tablespoon of balsamic vinegar along with the red wine.
  • If you have the time, you can marinate the beef cubes in the red wine for a few hours or overnight to further enhance the flavor.
  • This beef stew freezes well, so consider making a double batch and storing some for a quick and delicious meal on a busy day.

There you have it – a soul-warming Instant Pot beef stew with red wine that’s perfect for cozy dinners or entertaining guests. The combination of tender beef, hearty vegetables, and the rich taste of red wine creates a dish that’s truly irresistible. Whether it’s a special occasion or just a weeknight dinner, this recipe is sure to become a favorite in your household. So, grab your Instant Pot and a bottle of red wine, and get ready to savor every spoonful of this comforting stew. Cheers to good food and great company!
