Roasted Sweet Potato and Kale Salad: A Healthy Fall Option

Fall is the perfect time to enjoy hearty and nutritious salads that are both satisfying and packed with flavor. One such salad that embodies the essence of autumn is the Roasted Sweet Potato and Kale Salad. This combination of vibrant colors, nutritious ingredients, and delicious flavors makes it a standout dish for any fall gathering or weeknight dinner. Ingredients To create this tantalizing salad, you will need: 2 medium sweet potatoes, peeled and cubed 1 bunch of kale, stems removed and leaves torn into bite-sized pieces 1 red onion, thinly sliced 1/2 cup of dried cranberries 1/2 cup of crumbled goat cheese 1/4 cup of chopped pecans 2 tablespoons of olive oil 1 tablespoon of maple syrup Salt and pepper, to taste Instructions Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C). Line a baking sheet with parchment paper. In a bowl, toss the cubed sweet potatoes with 1 tablespoon of olive oil, maple syrup, salt, and pepper until well coated. Spread them evenly on the prepared baking sheet and roast for 20-25 minutes, or until the sweet potatoes are tender and slightly caramelized. While the sweet potatoes are roasting, prepare the kale by massaging it with 1 tablespoon of olive oil for a few minutes. This will help to soften the kale and reduce any bitterness. In a large salad bowl, combine the massaged kale, sliced red onion, dried cranberries, crumbled goat cheese, and chopped pecans. Once the sweet potatoes are ready, remove them from the oven and let them cool for a few minutes. Add the roasted sweet potatoes to the salad bowl and gently toss all the ingredients together until well combined. Season with additional salt and pepper, if desired. Serve the Roasted Sweet Potato and Kale Salad immediately, or refrigerate it for later use.
