Creamy and Comforting Butternut Squash and Apple Soup Recipes


Whether you’re looking for a hearty meal during the chilly autumn months or a warm and comforting dish to enjoy on a cozy winter evening, butternut squash and apple soup is the perfect choice. This creamy and flavorful soup combines the natural sweetness of butternut squash with the tartness of apples, resulting in a delightful blend of flavors that will leave you craving for more. In this blog post, we’ll explore two unique and delicious recipes for butternut squash and apple soup that are sure to become staples in your kitchen.

Classic Butternut Squash and Apple Soup

Ingredients:

  • 1 medium butternut squash, peeled, seeded, and cubed
  • 2 apples, peeled, cored, and chopped
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 4 cups vegetable or chicken broth
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground nutmeg
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Olive oil

Instructions:

  1. In a large pot, heat a drizzle of olive oil over medium heat. Add the chopped onion and cook until softened.
  2. Add the minced garlic to the pot and cook for an additional minute, stirring constantly.
  3. Add the cubed butternut squash and chopped apples to the pot, along with the ground cinnamon and nutmeg. Stir to combine the ingredients and cook for 5 minutes, allowing the flavors to meld.
  4. Pour in the vegetable or chicken broth, ensuring that the squash and apples are fully covered. Bring the mixture to a boil, then reduce the heat and let it simmer for 20-25 minutes, or until the squash and apples are tender.
  5. Using an immersion blender, puree the soup until smooth. Alternatively, transfer the soup in batches to a blender and blend until smooth, then return it to the pot.
  6. Stir in the heavy cream and season the soup with salt and pepper to taste. Allow the soup to simmer for an additional 5 minutes to incorporate the cream and seasonings.
  7. Serve the soup hot, garnished with a drizzle of cream, a sprinkle of cinnamon, and a few apple slices for a touch of elegance.

Rustic Roasted Butternut Squash and Apple Soup

Ingredients:

  • 1 large butternut squash, halved and seeded
  • 2 apples, halved and cored
  • 1 onion, quartered
  • 4 cloves garlic
  • 4 cups vegetable or chicken broth
  • 1/4 cup maple syrup
  • 1/4 teaspoon cayenne pepper
  • 1/2 cup sour cream or Greek yogurt
  • Fresh thyme leaves
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Olive oil

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 400°F (200°C).
  2. Drizzle the halved butternut squash, apples, onion, and garlic cloves with olive oil and place them on a baking sheet. Roast in the preheated oven for 45-50 minutes, or until the vegetables are tender and caramelized.
  3. Once roasted, scoop the flesh of the butternut squash and apples into a large pot. Add the roasted onion and garlic as well.
  4. Pour in the vegetable or chicken broth, maple syrup, and cayenne pepper. Bring the mixture to a simmer and cook for 10-15 minutes, allowing the flavors to meld.
  5. Using an immersion blender, puree the soup until smooth. Alternatively, transfer the soup in batches to a blender and blend until smooth, then return it to the pot.
  6. Stir in the sour cream or Greek yogurt, and season the soup with salt and pepper to taste.
  7. Serve the soup hot, garnished with a dollop of sour cream or Greek yogurt and a sprinkle of fresh thyme leaves for a burst of flavor.

Whether you opt for the classic version of butternut squash and apple soup or the rustic roasted variation, both recipes promise a bowlful of comfort and warmth. The natural sweetness of the butternut squash and apples, combined with the aromatic spices and creamy texture, make this soup a truly satisfying dish for any occasion. Pair it with a crusty loaf of bread or a simple salad for a complete and wholesome meal that will surely leave your taste buds singing.

So, the next time you’re in the mood for a soul-soothing soup, consider whipping up a batch of butternut squash and apple soup. It’s a delightful celebration of seasonal flavors that will have everyone at the table asking for seconds. Enjoy the process of creating this heartwarming dish, and savor every spoonful of this creamy and comforting delight!
