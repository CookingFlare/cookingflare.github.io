5 Quick and Easy Curry Recipes for New Cooks


Curry is a beloved dish across the globe, known for its vibrant flavors and comforting aromas. If you’re a new cook eager to delve into the world of curry, you’re in for a treat! This post will guide you through five quick and easy curry recipes that are perfect for beginners. With simple ingredients and straightforward instructions, you’ll be whipping up delicious curries in no time. Let’s get started!

1. Simple Chickpea Curry

Chickpeas are a fantastic source of protein and fiber, making this chickpea curry not only quick to prepare but also healthy and filling.

Ingredients:

  • 1 can (15 oz) chickpeas, drained and rinsed
  • 1 can (14 oz) coconut milk
  • 1 medium onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon curry powder
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh cilantro for garnish
  • Cooked rice or naan for serving

Instructions:

  1. Sauté the Aromatics: In a large pan, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the chopped onion and garlic, sautéing until the onion is translucent, about 5 minutes.

  2. Add Spices: Stir in the curry powder and cumin, cooking for an additional minute to release their flavors.

  3. Combine Chickpeas and Coconut Milk: Mix in the chickpeas and coconut milk, bringing the mixture to a gentle simmer.

  4. Season and Simmer: Season with salt and pepper. Let it simmer for about 10 minutes, allowing the flavors to meld together.

  5. Garnish and Serve: Serve hot over cooked rice or with naan, garnished with fresh cilantro.

2. Quick Vegetable Curry

This vegetable curry is a vibrant medley of seasonal vegetables, making it a versatile dish that you can customize based on what you have at home.

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups mixed vegetables (carrots, bell peppers, peas, etc.)
  • 1 can (14 oz) coconut milk
  • 1 medium onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon curry powder
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh lime juice for serving
  • Cooked rice for serving

Instructions:

  1. Sauté the Base: Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Add the chopped onion and minced garlic, cooking until soft.

  2. Add Vegetables and Spices: Add the mixed vegetables, stirring in the curry powder. Cook for a few minutes until the vegetables begin to soften.

  3. Pour in Coconut Milk: Add the coconut milk, stirring well to combine. Bring to a gentle simmer.

  4. Season and Cook: Season with salt and pepper, then cover and cook for 10-15 minutes until the vegetables are tender.

  5. Finish and Serve: Squeeze fresh lime juice over the curry before serving. Enjoy it over rice.

3. Easy Chicken Curry

Chicken curry is a classic dish that is both hearty and satisfying. This quick recipe ensures you don’t spend too much time in the kitchen while still enjoying a delicious meal.

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb chicken breast, cubed
  • 1 medium onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon curry powder
  • 1 can (14 oz) diced tomatoes
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley for garnish
  • Cooked rice or quinoa for serving

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the Chicken: In a large skillet, heat olive oil over medium heat. Add the chicken cubes and cook until browned on all sides.

  2. Add Aromatics: Stir in the chopped onion and minced garlic, cooking until the onion is soft and translucent.

  3. Incorporate Spices: Add the curry powder, stirring to coat the chicken evenly.

  4. Add Tomatoes: Pour in the diced tomatoes with their juices. Stir well, then season with salt and pepper.

  5. Simmer: Reduce the heat to low, cover, and let it simmer for 15-20 minutes until the chicken is cooked through and the flavors are well combined.

  6. Serve: Garnish with fresh parsley and serve over rice or quinoa.

4. Lentil Curry

Lentils are not only nutritious but also cook relatively quickly, making this lentil curry a perfect choice for a weeknight dinner.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup red lentils, rinsed
  • 1 medium onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon curry powder
  • 1 can (14 oz) coconut milk
  • 2 cups vegetable broth
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh spinach (optional)
  • Cooked rice for serving

Instructions:

  1. Sauté the Base: In a pot, heat olive oil over medium heat. Add the chopped onion and garlic, sautéing until the onion is soft.

  2. Add Lentils and Spices: Stir in the curry powder and rinsed lentils, cooking for a minute before adding the liquids.

  3. Pour in Liquids: Add the coconut milk and vegetable broth. Stir well to combine.

  4. Simmer: Bring to a boil, then reduce the heat to low. Cover and let it simmer for about 20-25 minutes, or until the lentils are tender.

  5. Finish with Spinach: If using, stir in fresh spinach just before serving, allowing it to wilt.

  6. Serve: Enjoy your lentil curry over cooked rice.

5. Quick Shrimp Curry

Seafood lovers will adore this quick shrimp curry, which is not only tasty but also comes together in a flash.

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 1 medium onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon curry powder
  • 1 can (14 oz) coconut milk
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh cilantro for garnish
  • Cooked rice or bread for serving

Instructions:

  1. Sauté the Onion and Garlic: In a skillet, heat olive oil over medium heat. Add the onion and garlic, cooking until soft.

  2. Season the Shrimp: Add the curry powder and shrimp to the skillet, stirring to coat the shrimp in the spices.

  3. Add Coconut Milk: Pour in the coconut milk, stirring gently. Bring to a simmer.

  4. Cook the Shrimp: Cook for about 5 minutes, or until the shrimp are pink and cooked through. Season with salt and pepper.

  5. Garnish and Serve: Serve hot, garnished with fresh cilantro, over rice or with bread.

Conclusion

Curry is a versatile and forgiving dish, making it perfect for new cooks. With these five quick and easy curry recipes, you can experiment with flavors and techniques, gaining confidence in the kitchen. Whether you prefer chickpeas, vegetables, chicken, lentils, or shrimp, there’s a curry here for everyone. So gather your ingredients, fire up the stove, and enjoy the delightful world of homemade curry! Happy cooking!
