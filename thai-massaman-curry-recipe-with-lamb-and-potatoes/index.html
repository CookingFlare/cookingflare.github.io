Thai Massaman Curry Recipe with Lamb and Potatoes


Massaman curry is a popular Thai dish that originates from the southern region of Thailand. It is a rich and flavorful curry made with a blend of spices, herbs, and ingredients such as lamb, potatoes, onions, and peanuts. In this recipe, we will use lamb and potatoes as the main ingredients, and add a variety of spices and herbs to create a delicious and aromatic dish.

Ingredients

  • 500g lamb shoulder or lamb shanks, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 2 large potatoes, peeled and cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 2 medium onions, thinly sliced
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 2 tablespoons Massaman curry paste
  • 2 tablespoons grated fresh ginger
  • 1 tablespoon grated fresh lemongrass
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground coriander
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground cardamom
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground cayenne pepper
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 2 cups coconut milk
  • 2 cups water
  • 2 tablespoons fish sauce
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons brown sugar
  • 2 tablespoons chopped fresh cilantro
  • 2 tablespoons chopped roasted peanuts
  • 2 tablespoons chopped scallions

Instructions

  1. Heat the oil in a large saucepan over medium heat. Add the onions and cook until they are softened and lightly browned, about 5 minutes.
  2. Add the garlic, ginger, lemongrass, cumin, coriander, cinnamon, cardamom, and cayenne pepper to the saucepan. Cook for 1 minute, stirring constantly, until the spices are fragrant.
  3. Add the lamb to the saucepan and cook until it is browned on all sides, about 5 minutes.
  4. Add the potatoes, curry paste, salt, and black pepper to the saucepan. Stir to combine.
  5. Pour in the coconut milk, water, fish sauce, soy sauce, and brown sugar. Stir to combine.
  6. Bring the mixture to a simmer, then reduce the heat to low and let it cook for 1 1/2 hours, or until the lamb is tender and the sauce has thickened.
  7. Stir in the chopped cilantro, peanuts, and scallions.
  8. Serve the Massaman curry hot over steamed rice or noodles.

Tips and Variations

  • To make the dish more flavorful, you can add 2-3 tablespoons of fish sauce and 1-2 tablespoons of soy sauce to the saucepan with the lamb and potatoes.
  • You can also add 1-2 cups of chopped bell peppers, carrots, or zucchini to the saucepan with the lamb and potatoes for added color and nutrients.
  • If you prefer a thicker sauce, you can add 1-2 tablespoons of cornstarch or flour to the saucepan and stir to combine.
  • You can serve the Massaman curry with steamed rice, noodles, or roti (Thai flatbread).
  • You can also serve the curry with a side of pickled vegetables, such as cucumbers, carrots, or daikon radish.

Conclusion

Massaman curry is a delicious and aromatic Thai dish that is made with a blend of spices, herbs, and ingredients such as lamb, potatoes, onions, and peanuts. This recipe uses lamb and potatoes as the main ingredients, and adds a variety of spices and herbs to create a flavorful and aromatic dish. You can serve the curry with steamed rice, noodles, or roti, and add a variety of toppings such as chopped cilantro, peanuts, and scallions. With its rich and flavorful sauce, this Massaman curry is a great dish for special occasions or everyday meals.
