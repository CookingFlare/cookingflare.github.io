How to Host a French Themed Dinner Party


Hosting a dinner party can be a daunting task, but with a clear vision and planning, it can be a truly unforgettable experience. A French themed dinner party, in particular, offers endless possibilities to transport your guests to the romantic streets of Paris. In this post, we will guide you through the planning process, highlighting essential elements that will turn your dinner party into a heavenly experience. Choosing the Theme and Color Scheme The French theme is rich and indulgent, with a focus on elegance and refinement. To create the perfect ambiance, choose a soft and muted color palette that exudes Old World charm. Consider a palette of lavenders, creams, and deep berry tones that evoke the delicate beauty of French rococo art. Your table linens, glassware, and china should complement this palette, adding a warm and inviting touch to the atmosphere. Menu and Wine Pairing A French themed dinner party isn't complete without a classic French menu. Plan a multi-course meal that showcases the country's culinary artistry, including dishes such as Coq au Vin, Bouillabaisse, and Ratatouille. To add an extra layer of sophistication, pair each course with an expertly selected wine from France's renowned wine regions, such as Bordeaux or Burgundy. Don't forget to include a delicious cheese course featuring an array of artisanal French cheeses.
