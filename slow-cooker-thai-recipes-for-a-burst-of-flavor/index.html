Slow Cooker Thai Recipes for a Burst of Flavor


If you’re a fan of Thai cuisine and love the convenience of slow cooker recipes, then you’re in for a treat! In this blog post, we’ll explore some delicious and flavorful slow cooker Thai recipes that are sure to satisfy your taste buds. From spicy curries to aromatic soups, these recipes will transport you to the streets of Thailand without ever leaving your kitchen.

1. Slow Cooker Thai Red Curry

Ingredients:

  • 1 ½ lbs boneless chicken, sliced into bite-sized pieces
  • 1 can (14 oz) coconut milk
  • 2 tablespoons red curry paste
  • 1 tablespoon fish sauce
  • 1 tablespoon brown sugar
  • 1 red bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 onion, sliced
  • 1 cup bamboo shoots
  • Fresh basil leaves for garnish

Instructions:

  1. In a slow cooker, combine the coconut milk, red curry paste, fish sauce, and brown sugar. Mix well to combine.
  2. Add the chicken, bell pepper, onion, and bamboo shoots to the slow cooker. Stir to coat the ingredients with the sauce.
  3. Cover and cook on low for 6-8 hours or on high for 3-4 hours, until the chicken is cooked through and tender.
  4. Serve the Thai red curry over steamed rice and garnish with fresh basil leaves. Enjoy!

2. Slow Cooker Thai Peanut Chicken

Ingredients:

  • 1 ½ lbs boneless chicken thighs, cut into chunks
  • ½ cup creamy peanut butter
  • ¼ cup soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons honey
  • 2 tablespoons lime juice
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon ground ginger
  • ½ teaspoon red pepper flakes
  • Chopped peanuts and sliced green onions for garnish

Instructions:

  1. In a slow cooker, combine the peanut butter, soy sauce, honey, lime juice, garlic, ginger, and red pepper flakes. Stir until well combined.
  2. Add the chicken to the slow cooker and toss to coat it evenly with the sauce.
  3. Cover and cook on low for 4-6 hours or on high for 2-3 hours, until the chicken is cooked through and tender.
  4. Serve the Thai peanut chicken over cooked rice or noodles. Garnish with chopped peanuts and sliced green onions for extra flavor and crunch.

3. Slow Cooker Thai Coconut Soup

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 1 can (14 oz) coconut milk
  • 3 cups chicken broth
  • 2 tablespoons red curry paste
  • 2 tablespoons fish sauce
  • 2 tablespoons lime juice
  • 1 tablespoon brown sugar
  • 1 red bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 cup sliced mushrooms
  • 1 cup snap peas
  • Fresh cilantro for garnish

Instructions:

  1. In a slow cooker, combine the coconut milk, chicken broth, red curry paste, fish sauce, lime juice, and brown sugar. Stir until well mixed.
  2. Add the shrimp, bell pepper, mushrooms, and snap peas to the slow cooker. Stir to combine.
  3. Cover and cook on low for 2-3 hours or on high for 1-2 hours, until the shrimp is cooked through and the vegetables are tender.
  4. Serve the Thai coconut soup hot, garnished with fresh cilantro. Enjoy the creamy and flavorful soup!

Conclusion

Slow cooker Thai recipes are a fantastic way to enjoy the bold and vibrant flavors of Thailand with minimal effort. Whether you choose the aromatic Thai red curry, the nutty and savory Thai peanut chicken, or the creamy and comforting Thai coconut soup, each dish is sure to tantalize your taste buds and transport you to the bustling streets of Bangkok. So dust off your slow cooker and get ready to embark on a culinary adventure that will leave you craving more. Happy cooking!
