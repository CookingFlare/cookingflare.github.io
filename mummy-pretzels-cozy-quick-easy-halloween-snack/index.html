Mummy Pretzels: Cozy, Quick & Easy Halloween Snack


Ah, Halloween! The one time of year when our home becomes a haunted house, and our snack table turns into a spooky morsels buffet. But you know what’s even better? Sneaking in some deliciously fun treats without spending hours in the kitchen. Behold, Mummy Pretzels – the perfect blend of simplicity and spookiness! Let’s get our aprons ready and dive right in.

Ingredients

  • Soft pretzels (approximately 1 per mummy)
  • Almond bark or white chocolate chips
  • Coconut oil (if using almond bark)
  • Black decorating gel or icing
  • Parchment paper

Equipment

  • Baking sheet
  • Microwave-safe bowl
  • Pastry brush

Step 1: Prepare the Pretzels

First, gather your soft pretzels. To make these Mummy Pretzels even more no-fuss, consider purchasing them from a local bakery or supermarket. It’ll save you time and effort! Lay them out on a cutting board or a plate.

Step 2: Melt the Chocolate

Next, prepare your chocolate. If using almond bark, melt it in a microwave-safe bowl with a small amount of coconut oil. This will help the chocolate flow smoothly over your pretzels. If you’re using white chocolate chips, simply follow the package instructions or microwave them in 30-second intervals, stirring in between, until fully melted.

Step 3: Set Up for Action

To avoid any chocolate disasters, line your baking sheet with parchment paper. Now’s the time to recruit a little helper and spread a bit of Halloween spirit!

Step 4: Drape the Mummy Wrappers

Using a pastry brush or spoon, spread a generous layer of melted chocolate onto each pretzel, making sure to cover it well. For added authenticity, leave a little choc-nostril at the top. Then, using the black decorating gel or icing, create a zigzag pattern to resemble gauzy bandages. The more zigzags, the more authentic the mummy look!

Step 5: Indulge & Chill

Now, it’s time to feast! But not before giving your Mummy Pretzels a quick chill in the refrigerator for about 20 minutes, or until the chocolate hardens. This will guarantee they hold their shape and won’t be gooey.

Step 6: Add Those Gory Details

Once the chocolate has set, it’s time to give your mummies a little personality. Carefully place miniature candy eyes or colorful sprinkles onto the exposed chocolate “wraps.” With each mummy emerging from the fridge, it’ll be like giving life to the undead – in a tasty way, of course!

Step 7: Serve with Spooky Style

For the ultimate Halloween flair, serve your Mummy Pretzels on a tray covered with crypticallywrapped parchment paper, or on a platter surrounded by spiders, bats, and other spooky elements. Remember, presentation is key.

Did It Spook Ya?

If you’re in need of a spooky snack that won’t take you into a stressful cooking session, Mummy Pretzels are just what you need. An easy, no-fuss method and a delightfully eerie appearance – life literally tastes sweeter with these undead treats. May your Halloween be filled with ghouls, ghosts, and of course, these mouthwatering Mummy Pretzels. Enjoy!
