Satisfying Breakfast Scone Recipes for a Classic Treat


Scones are a classic treat that can make any breakfast feel special. With their flaky texture and delicious flavors, they are perfect for a leisurely weekend morning or a quick grab-and-go breakfast on a busy weekday. In this post, we will explore some satisfying breakfast scone recipes that are sure to delight your taste buds and leave you craving for more.

1. Blueberry Lemon Scones

Start your day off right with these delightful Blueberry Lemon Scones. The burst of tangy lemon combined with the sweetness of blueberries creates a perfect balance of flavors. To make these scones, you’ll need:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 tablespoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, cold and cubed
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1 tablespoon fresh lemon juice
  • 1 tablespoon lemon zest
  • 1 cup fresh blueberries

To prepare the scones, simply mix the dry ingredients together, then cut in the cold butter until the mixture resembles coarse crumbs. In a separate bowl, whisk together the milk, lemon juice, and lemon zest. Pour the wet ingredients into the dry mixture and gently fold in the blueberries. Form the dough into a round shape and cut it into wedges. Bake at 425°F for 15-18 minutes until golden brown.

2. Chocolate Chip Espresso Scones

For those who need a little extra kick in the morning, these Chocolate Chip Espresso Scones are the perfect choice. The combination of rich chocolate and bold espresso flavors will surely satisfy your cravings. Here’s what you’ll need:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 tablespoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, cold and cubed
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 2 tablespoons instant espresso powder
  • 1 cup chocolate chips

To make these scones, start by mixing the dry ingredients together. Cut in the cold butter until the mixture resembles coarse crumbs. In a separate bowl, dissolve the instant espresso powder in the milk. Pour the wet ingredients into the dry mixture and add the chocolate chips. Mix until just combined. Shape the dough into a round and cut into wedges. Bake at 425°F for 15-18 minutes until golden brown.

3. Maple Pecan Scones

Indulge in the warm and comforting flavors of these Maple Pecan Scones. The sweetness of maple syrup combined with the crunch of pecans creates a truly satisfying breakfast treat. Here’s what you’ll need:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 tablespoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, cold and cubed
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1/4 cup maple syrup
  • 1 cup chopped pecans

To make these scones, start by mixing the dry ingredients together. Cut in the cold butter until the mixture resembles coarse crumbs. In a separate bowl, whisk together the milk and maple syrup. Pour the wet ingredients into the dry mixture and fold in the chopped pecans. Shape the dough into a round and cut into wedges. Bake at 425°F for 15-18 minutes until golden brown.

Conclusion

Scones are a delightful breakfast treat that can be enjoyed in various flavors and combinations. Whether you prefer the tangy sweetness of blueberries and lemon, the rich indulgence of chocolate and espresso, or the comforting combination of maple and pecans, there’s a scone recipe for everyone. So, why not treat yourself to a satisfying breakfast with these delicious scones? Grab your apron and get baking!
