Vegan Gingerbread Men with a Royal Icing Recipe: A Classic Holiday Treat


Gingerbread men are a beloved holiday treat that brings back memories of childhood and the joy of baking during the festive season. While traditional gingerbread recipes often include eggs and butter, this vegan version allows everyone to enjoy the classic flavors of ginger, cinnamon, and molasses. Paired with a simple and vegan-friendly royal icing recipe, these gingerbread men will delight both vegans and non-vegans alike.

Ingredients

  • 3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 2 teaspoons ground ginger
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground cloves
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup coconut oil, melted
  • 1/2 cup molasses
  • 1/2 cup brown sugar
  • 1/4 cup almond milk
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C) and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.

  2. In a large mixing bowl, whisk together the flour, baking soda, ginger, cinnamon, cloves, and salt. Set aside.

  3. In a separate bowl, combine the melted coconut oil, molasses, brown sugar, almond milk, and vanilla extract. Mix until well combined.

  4. Gradually add the wet ingredients to the dry ingredients, stirring until a soft dough forms. If the dough seems too dry, add a tablespoon of almond milk at a time until it reaches the desired consistency.

  5. Divide the dough in half and roll out one portion on a lightly floured surface to a thickness of about 1/4 inch. Use gingerbread man-shaped cookie cutters to cut out shapes from the dough. Repeat with the remaining dough.

  6. Place the cut-out gingerbread men on the prepared baking sheet, leaving a small space between each cookie.

  7. Bake for 8-10 minutes or until the edges are lightly golden. Remove from the oven and allow the cookies to cool completely on a wire rack.

Vegan Royal Icing

While the gingerbread men are cooling, prepare the vegan royal icing to add the finishing touch to these delightful holiday treats.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups powdered sugar
  • 2 tablespoons aquafaba (the liquid from a can of chickpeas)
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • Food coloring (optional)

Instructions

  1. In a medium-sized mixing bowl, whisk together the powdered sugar, aquafaba, and vanilla extract until smooth and well combined. If the icing is too thick, add a teaspoon of water at a time until desired consistency is reached.

  2. If using food coloring, divide the icing into separate bowls and add a few drops of food coloring to each bowl. Mix well until the desired color is achieved.

  3. Transfer the icing to piping bags or small ziplock bags with the corner snipped off. Decorate the cooled gingerbread men with the royal icing, creating faces, buttons, and any other designs you desire.

  4. Allow the icing to set completely before storing the gingerbread men in an airtight container. They will stay fresh for up to one week.

Enjoy the Festive Delights

These vegan gingerbread men with a royal icing recipe are not only a classic holiday treat but also a celebration of inclusivity and the joy of sharing delicious food with loved ones. From the warm spices of ginger and cinnamon to the sweet and delicate royal icing, these gingerbread men will surely become a favorite in your holiday baking repertoire. So gather your loved ones, put on some festive music, and enjoy the process of creating these delightful treats together. Happy baking and happy holidays!

Note: While this recipe is vegan, it can also be enjoyed by those with dietary restrictions or preferences. Always check the labels of your ingredients to ensure they meet your specific dietary needs.
