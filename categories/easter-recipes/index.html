Chocolate-Covered Marshmallow Eggs Ingredients: 1 cup of marshmallows 2 cups of milk chocolate chips 1 tablespoon of vegetable shortening Directions: Start by creating a double boiler. Fill a large pot halfway with water and place a heatproof bowl on top. Ensure that the bowl rests on the pot without touching the water. In the heatproof bowl, combine the marshmallows and milk chocolate chips. Heat the water in the pot over medium heat until it simmers, then slowly stir the mixture until the chocolate and marshmallows are completely melted. Remove the bowl from the heat and stir in the vegetable shortening to help thicken the chocolate. Let the mixture cool slightly, then use a melon baller or spoon to scoop mixture and form eggs. Chill for about 30 minutes, then dip in melted chocolate and add decorations like sprinkles, coconut, or crushed nuts. Allow the chocolate to cool and harden before serving.
