Irresistible Bacon-Wrapped Chicken Bites: A Perfect Party Appetizer


Are you looking for the perfect party appetizer that will have your guests coming back for more? Look no further than these irresistible bacon-wrapped chicken bites! With their mouthwatering combination of crispy bacon and tender chicken, these bites are sure to be a hit at your next gathering.

Ingredients

To make these delicious bacon-wrapped chicken bites, you will need the following ingredients:

  • Chicken breast fillets
  • Bacon slices
  • Toothpicks
  • Barbecue sauce
  • Brown sugar
  • Garlic powder
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).

  2. Cut the chicken breast fillets into bite-sized pieces. Make sure they are evenly sized to ensure even cooking.

  3. Season the chicken pieces with salt, pepper, and garlic powder. This will add flavor to the chicken and complement the smoky bacon.

  4. Take a slice of bacon and wrap it around each chicken piece. Secure the bacon in place by inserting a toothpick through the chicken and bacon.

  5. In a small bowl, mix together the barbecue sauce and brown sugar. This sweet and tangy glaze will take your bacon-wrapped chicken bites to the next level.

  6. Place the bacon-wrapped chicken bites on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper. Brush the glaze generously over each bite, ensuring they are well coated.

  7. Bake the chicken bites in the preheated oven for about 20-25 minutes, or until the bacon is crispy and the chicken is cooked through. Keep an eye on them to prevent burning.

  8. Once cooked, remove the toothpicks from the chicken bites. Be careful as they will be hot!

  9. Serve these irresistible bacon-wrapped chicken bites on a platter with toothpicks for easy serving. Your guests won’t be able to resist these tasty treats!

Tips and Variations

  • For an extra kick of flavor, try adding some chopped jalapeños or a sprinkle of cayenne pepper to the barbecue sauce glaze.

  • If you prefer a healthier option, you can use turkey bacon instead of regular bacon. It will still provide that delicious smoky flavor without the added fat.

  • These bacon-wrapped chicken bites can also be cooked on a grill. Simply preheat your grill to medium heat and cook the bites for approximately 10-15 minutes, turning them occasionally to ensure even cooking.

  • Serve these bites with a dipping sauce of your choice, such as ranch, honey mustard, or even a spicy sriracha mayo.

  • Don’t limit yourself to just chicken! You can use the same technique with other meats like beef or shrimp for a variety of flavors.

Conclusion

These irresistible bacon-wrapped chicken bites are the perfect party appetizer that will leave your guests wanting more. The combination of crispy bacon, tender chicken, and sweet barbecue glaze is simply irresistible. With just a few simple ingredients and easy steps, you can whip up a batch of these delicious bites in no time. So, the next time you’re hosting a party or gathering, make sure to include these mouthwatering bacon-wrapped chicken bites on your menu. Your guests will thank you!
