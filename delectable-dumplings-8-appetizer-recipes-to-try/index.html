Delectable Dumplings: 8 Appetizer Recipes to Try


Dumplings have been a staple in many cultures around the world, and for good reason - they’re delicious, versatile, and can be filled with a wide variety of ingredients. Whether you’re a seasoned chef or a beginner in the kitchen, there’s a dumpling recipe out there for you. In this post, we’ll be exploring 8 appetizer dumpling recipes that are sure to please even the pickiest of eaters.

1. Spinach and Feta Dumplings

These savory dumplings are perfect for a quick and easy appetizer. Simply mix together 1 package of frozen spinach, 1/2 cup of crumbled feta cheese, 1/2 cup of chopped fresh parsley, 1/4 cup of grated Parmesan cheese, 1 egg, and 1 tablespoon of olive oil. Place the mixture in the center of a wonton wrapper, and fold the wrapper into a triangle. Seal the edges with a fork, and repeat with the remaining wrappers and filling. Serve with a side of marinara sauce for dipping.

2. Shrimp and Chive Dumplings

These dumplings are a great way to add some seafood flair to your appetizer spread. Simply mix together 1 cup of cooked and peeled shrimp, 1/4 cup of chopped fresh chives, 1/4 cup of grated Parmesan cheese, 1 egg, and 1 tablespoon of olive oil. Place the mixture in the center of a wonton wrapper, and fold the wrapper into a triangle. Seal the edges with a fork, and repeat with the remaining wrappers and filling. Serve with a side of cocktail sauce for dipping.

3. Bacon and Cheddar Dumplings

These savory dumplings are perfect for a football game or a casual gathering with friends. Simply mix together 6 slices of cooked and crumbled bacon, 1/2 cup of grated cheddar cheese, 1/4 cup of chopped scallions, 1 egg, and 1 tablespoon of olive oil. Place the mixture in the center of a wonton wrapper, and fold the wrapper into a triangle. Seal the edges with a fork, and repeat with the remaining wrappers and filling. Serve with a side of ranch dressing for dipping.

4. Mushroom and Leek Dumplings

These earthy dumplings are perfect for a fall or winter gathering. Simply mix together 1 cup of sautéed mushrooms and leeks, 1/4 cup of grated Parmesan cheese, 1 egg, and 1 tablespoon of olive oil. Place the mixture in the center of a wonton wrapper, and fold the wrapper into a triangle. Seal the edges with a fork, and repeat with the remaining wrappers and filling. Serve with a side of cream sauce for dipping.

5. Chicken and Ginger Dumplings

These dumplings are a great way to add some Asian flair to your appetizer spread. Simply mix together 1 cup of cooked and shredded chicken, 1/4 cup of chopped fresh ginger, 1/4 cup of grated scallions, 1 egg, and 1 tablespoon of soy sauce. Place the mixture in the center of a wonton wrapper, and fold the wrapper into a triangle. Seal the edges with a fork, and repeat with the remaining wrappers and filling. Serve with a side of soy sauce and ginger for dipping.

6. Broccoli and Cheddar Dumplings

These vegetarian dumplings are perfect for a healthy and delicious appetizer option. Simply mix together 1 cup of steamed broccoli florets, 1/2 cup of grated cheddar cheese, 1/4 cup of chopped scallions, 1 egg, and 1 tablespoon of olive oil. Place the mixture in the center of a wonton wrapper, and fold the wrapper into a triangle. Seal the edges with a fork, and repeat with the remaining wrappers and filling. Serve with a side of ranch dressing for dipping.

7. Pumpkin and Sage Dumplings

These seasonal dumplings are perfect for a fall or winter gathering. Simply mix together 1/2 cup of cooked and mashed pumpkin, 1/4 cup of chopped fresh sage, 1/4 cup of grated Parmesan cheese, 1 egg, and 1 tablespoon of olive oil. Place the mixture in the center of a wonton wrapper, and fold the wrapper into a triangle. Seal the edges with a fork, and repeat with the remaining wrappers and filling. Serve with a side of cream sauce for dipping.

8. Sweet Potato and Cilantro Dumplings

These sweet dumplings are perfect for a unique and delicious appetizer option. Simply mix together 1/2 cup of cooked and mashed sweet potatoes, 1/4 cup of chopped fresh cilantro, 1/4 cup of grated Parmesan cheese, 1 egg, and 1 tablespoon of olive oil. Place the mixture in the center of a wonton wrapper, and fold the wrapper into a triangle. Seal the edges with a fork, and repeat with the remaining wrappers and filling. Serve with a side of sour cream and chives for dipping.

Conclusion

Dumplings are a versatile and delicious appetizer option that can be filled with a wide variety of ingredients. Whether you’re looking for something savory or sweet, there’s a dumpling recipe out there for you. With these 8 recipes, you’ll be well on your way to creating a delicious and memorable appetizer spread for your next gathering.
