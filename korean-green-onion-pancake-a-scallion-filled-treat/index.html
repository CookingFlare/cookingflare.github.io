Korean Green Onion Pancake: A Scallion-Filled Treat


If you’re a fan of Korean cuisine or just love trying out new and exciting recipes, then you’re in for a treat with this delightful dish – Korean Green Onion Pancake, also known as Pajeon. This savory pancake is a popular Korean street food and a beloved appetizer that’s perfect for sharing with family and friends. The crispy texture, combined with the flavorful green onions, makes this dish an irresistible choice for any occasion.

Ingredients

Before we dive into the cooking process, let’s take a look at the ingredients you’ll need to recreate this mouthwatering Korean delicacy:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 cups water
  • 4 cups chopped green onions
  • 1 cup chopped kimchi (optional)
  • 1 cup seafood such as squid or shrimp (optional)
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon sugar
  • Vegetable oil for frying

Instructions

Step 1: Prepare the Batter

In a large mixing bowl, combine the all-purpose flour and water. Mix the ingredients thoroughly until you achieve a smooth, lump-free batter. The consistency should be similar to that of pancake batter. Once the batter is ready, set it aside for a moment.

Step 2: Chop the Green Onions

Take the fresh green onions and chop them into 2-inch long pieces, ensuring that they are clean and thoroughly dried. You can also include kimchi for an extra kick of flavor and seafood for a delightful twist. The addition of these ingredients will elevate the taste of the pancake and add a unique dimension to the dish.

Step 3: Mix the Ingredients

Add the chopped green onions (and optional kimchi and seafood) to the batter. Season the mixture with salt and sugar, and then gently fold the ingredients together until they are evenly distributed.

Step 4: Heat the Pan

Place a non-stick skillet or frying pan over medium-high heat and add a generous amount of vegetable oil. It’s essential to ensure that the entire surface of the pan is evenly coated with oil to achieve a crispy texture for the pancake.

Step 5: Cook the Pancake

Pour a ladleful of the batter mixture onto the heated pan, spreading it out into a thin, even layer. Allow the pancake to cook for 3-4 minutes on each side, or until it turns golden brown and crispy. Repeat this process for the remaining batter, adding more oil to the pan as needed.

Step 6: Serve and Enjoy!

Once the Korean Green Onion Pancake is golden and crispy on both sides, remove it from the pan and place it on a cutting board. Allow it to cool for a minute before cutting it into bite-sized pieces. You can serve the pancake with a dipping sauce made from soy sauce, rice vinegar, and a sprinkle of sesame seeds for an extra burst of flavor.

Conclusion

The Korean Green Onion Pancake, or Pajeon, is a delightful dish that embodies the essence of Korean street food culture. Its simple yet flavorful ingredients make it a versatile and satisfying choice for any occasion. Whether you’re hosting a casual gathering or simply craving a savory snack, this scallion-filled treat is sure to impress your taste buds and leave you craving for more. So, roll up your sleeves, gather the ingredients, and embark on a culinary adventure to savor the delectable flavors of Korean cuisine. Happy cooking!
