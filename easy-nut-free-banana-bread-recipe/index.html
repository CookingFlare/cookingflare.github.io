Easy Nut-Free Banana Bread Recipe


Banana bread is a classic treat that is loved by many for its moist texture and sweet, comforting flavor. However, for those with nut allergies, finding a nut-free banana bread recipe that still delivers on taste and texture can be a bit of a challenge. Fear not, because I’ve got the perfect nut-free banana bread recipe that will satisfy your cravings and make your kitchen smell absolutely divine.

Ingredients:

  • 3 ripe bananas
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, melted
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/2 cup brown sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground nutmeg
  • 1/4 cup plain yogurt or sour cream

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease a 9x5-inch loaf pan and set it aside.

  2. In a large mixing bowl, mash the ripe bananas with a fork or potato masher until smooth.

  3. Add the melted butter to the mashed bananas and mix well.

  4. Incorporate the granulated sugar and brown sugar into the banana-butter mixture. Stir until the sugars are fully dissolved.

  5. Beat the eggs into the mixture one at a time, ensuring each egg is fully incorporated before adding the next.

  6. Stir in the vanilla extract and plain yogurt (or sour cream) until the mixture is smooth and well combined.

  7. In a separate bowl, whisk together the all-purpose flour, baking soda, salt, ground cinnamon, and ground nutmeg.

  8. Gradually add the dry ingredients to the wet ingredients, stirring until just combined. Be careful not to overmix the batter, as this can result in a tough banana bread.

  9. Pour the batter into the prepared loaf pan, spreading it out evenly.

  10. Bake in the preheated oven for 60 to 65 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.

  11. Once baked, remove the banana bread from the oven and allow it to cool in the pan for 10 minutes before transferring it to a wire rack to cool completely.

  12. Slice, serve, and enjoy your delicious nut-free banana bread!

Tips and Variations:

  • For an extra burst of flavor, consider adding 1/2 cup of chocolate chips or chopped dried fruits to the batter.
  • If you prefer a bit of crunch, sprinkle the top of the batter with a tablespoon of coarse sugar before baking.
  • To make this recipe dairy-free, simply substitute the butter with a neutral-flavored oil and the yogurt (or sour cream) with a dairy-free alternative.

Nutritional Information:

  • Serving Size: 1 slice
  • Calories: 220
  • Total Fat: 8g
  • Saturated Fat: 5g
  • Cholesterol: 45mg
  • Sodium: 220mg
  • Total Carbohydrate: 34g
  • Dietary Fiber: 2g
  • Sugars: 19g
  • Protein: 3g

There you have it! A simple and delightful nut-free banana bread recipe that’s perfect for any occasion. Whether you’re indulging in a slice for breakfast, as a snack, or as a comforting dessert, this recipe is sure to become a favorite in your household. So, grab those ripe bananas and get baking! Your taste buds will thank you.
