Korean Spicy Pork Belly Stir-Fry: A Fiery and Addictive Dish


If you’re a fan of bold flavors and spicy dishes, then Korean Spicy Pork Belly Stir-Fry is a must-try for you. This fiery and addictive dish is a staple in Korean cuisine, known for its tantalizing blend of spicy, sweet, and savory flavors. Made with succulent pork belly, vibrant vegetables, and a mouthwatering spicy sauce, this stir-fry is a perfect balance of heat and umami. Whether you’re a seasoned home cook or a novice in the kitchen, this recipe is sure to impress your taste buds and leave you craving for more.

Ingredients

To create this tantalizing dish, you’ll need the following ingredients:

  • 500g pork belly, thinly sliced
  • 1 onion, thinly sliced
  • 2 green onions, cut into 1-inch pieces
  • 1 red bell pepper, thinly sliced
  • 1 green bell pepper, thinly sliced
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons gochujang (Korean red chili paste)
  • 1 tablespoon gochugaru (Korean red chili flakes)
  • 1 tablespoon honey
  • 1 tablespoon sesame oil
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • Toasted sesame seeds for garnish
  • Cooked white rice for serving

Instructions

Step 1: Marinate the Pork Belly

Start by marinating the thinly sliced pork belly with soy sauce, minced garlic, and a dash of black pepper. Let it sit for about 15 minutes to allow the flavors to infuse into the meat.

Step 2: Prepare the Spicy Sauce

In a small bowl, combine the gochujang, gochugaru, honey, and sesame oil to create the spicy sauce. Adjust the amount of gochugaru according to your spice preference. Stir the mixture until well combined and set it aside.

Step 3: Sear the Pork Belly

Heat a wok or a large skillet over medium-high heat and add the vegetable oil. Once the oil is hot, add the marinated pork belly and stir-fry until it’s golden brown and crispy on the edges. Remove the pork belly from the wok and set it aside.

Step 4: Stir-Fry the Vegetables

In the same wok, add the onion, green onions, and bell peppers. Stir-fry until the vegetables are tender-crisp and slightly caramelized.

Step 5: Bring It All Together

Return the seared pork belly to the wok with the stir-fried vegetables. Pour the spicy sauce over the pork belly and vegetables, tossing everything together until well coated. Cook for an additional 2-3 minutes to allow the flavors to meld together.

Step 6: Serve and Enjoy

Transfer the Korean Spicy Pork Belly Stir-Fry to a serving platter and garnish with toasted sesame seeds. Serve the stir-fry hot with steamed white rice and enjoy the explosion of flavors in every bite.

Tips for Success

  • For an extra kick of heat, add extra gochugaru or a sprinkle of red pepper flakes.
  • Ensure that the pork belly is thinly sliced to ensure quick and even cooking.
  • Adjust the sweetness of the dish by adding more or less honey to the spicy sauce according to your taste preference.

Final Thoughts

Korean Spicy Pork Belly Stir-Fry is a dish that embodies the vibrant and dynamic flavors of Korean cuisine. Its fiery nature and addictive taste make it a favorite among spice enthusiasts. Whether you’re preparing it for a family dinner or a special gathering, this dish is sure to leave a lasting impression. So, roll up your sleeves, gather your ingredients, and embark on a culinary adventure that will delight your senses and leave you craving for more.
