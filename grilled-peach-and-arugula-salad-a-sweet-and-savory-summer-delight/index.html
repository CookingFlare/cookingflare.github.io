Grilled Peach and Arugula Salad: A Sweet and Savory Summer Delight


Are you looking for a refreshing salad recipe that perfectly captures the flavors of summer? Look no further than this delectable Grilled Peach and Arugula Salad. With its combination of sweet grilled peaches, peppery arugula, tangy goat cheese, and crunchy almonds, this salad is a true delight for the senses. Whether you’re hosting a backyard barbecue or simply want to enjoy a light and flavorful meal, this recipe is sure to impress.

Ingredients

  • 4 peaches, ripe but firm
  • 6 cups arugula
  • 1/2 cup crumbled goat cheese
  • 1/2 cup sliced almonds
  • 1/4 cup extra virgin olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons balsamic vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon honey
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Preheat your grill to medium-high heat. While the grill is heating up, wash and slice the peaches in half, removing the pits.

  2. In a small bowl, whisk together the olive oil, balsamic vinegar, honey, salt, and pepper to create a flavorful dressing.

  3. Brush the cut side of each peach half with a generous amount of the dressing, ensuring they are well-coated.

  4. Place the peaches cut side down on the grill grates and cook for about 3-4 minutes, or until grill marks appear and the peaches are slightly softened.

  5. Remove the grilled peaches from the grill and set aside to cool slightly. Once cooled, slice the peaches into thin wedges.

  6. In a large salad bowl, combine the arugula, goat cheese, sliced almonds, and grilled peach wedges.

  7. Drizzle the remaining dressing over the salad and gently toss to ensure all the ingredients are well coated.

  8. Serve immediately and enjoy the delightful combination of sweet and savory flavors in every bite.

Tips and Variations

  • If you don’t have access to a grill, you can achieve a similar effect by using a grill pan on your stovetop. Simply heat the grill pan over medium-high heat and follow the same instructions for grilling the peaches.

  • For an extra burst of flavor, consider adding a handful of fresh mint leaves or a sprinkle of lemon zest to the salad.

  • This salad pairs well with grilled chicken or shrimp, making it a versatile option for a complete and satisfying meal.

  • If you prefer a vegetarian option, you can omit the goat cheese or replace it with a dairy-free alternative.

  • Feel free to experiment with other fruits such as nectarines or plums in place of peaches, depending on what is available and in season.

Final Thoughts

Grilled Peach and Arugula Salad is a simple yet elegant dish that showcases the best flavors of summer. The combination of sweet, juicy peaches with the peppery bite of arugula creates a harmonious balance of flavors that is both refreshing and satisfying. Whether you’re enjoying this salad as a light lunch or a side dish at your next gathering, it is sure to become a favorite in your recipe repertoire. So fire up the grill, gather your ingredients, and get ready to savor the taste of summer with this delightful salad.
