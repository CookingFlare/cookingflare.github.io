Fresh and Vibrant: Mango Salsa Chicken Wrap


Are you looking for a delicious and refreshing meal that’s perfect for summer? Look no further than the Mango Salsa Chicken Wrap! This vibrant dish combines the juicy sweetness of mango salsa with the savory goodness of grilled chicken, all wrapped up in a soft tortilla. It’s a burst of fresh flavors in every bite, and it’s easy to make at home. Let’s dive into the recipe and get ready to tantalize your taste buds!

Ingredients

For the Mango Salsa:

  • 2 ripe mangoes, diced
  • 1/2 red onion, finely chopped
  • 1 red bell pepper, diced
  • 1/4 cup fresh cilantro, chopped
  • Juice of 1 lime
  • Salt and pepper to taste

For the Grilled Chicken:

  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • Salt and pepper to taste

For the Wrap:

  • 4 large flour tortillas
  • 1 cup mixed greens
  • 1/2 cup plain Greek yogurt
  • 1 lime, cut into wedges for serving

Instructions

Step 1: Make the Mango Salsa

  1. In a mixing bowl, combine the diced mangoes, red onion, red bell pepper, and cilantro.
  2. Squeeze the lime juice over the mixture and gently toss to combine.
  3. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Set aside to let the flavors meld while you prepare the chicken.

Step 2: Grill the Chicken

  1. Preheat your grill or grill pan over medium-high heat.
  2. In a small bowl, whisk together the olive oil, minced garlic, paprika, salt, and pepper.
  3. Brush the seasoned oil mixture over the chicken breasts.
  4. Grill the chicken for 6-7 minutes on each side, or until cooked through and nicely charred. Remove from the grill and let it rest for a few minutes before slicing.

Step 3: Assemble the Wraps

  1. Warm the flour tortillas in a dry skillet for a few seconds on each side to make them pliable.
  2. Spread a couple of spoonfuls of Greek yogurt down the center of each tortilla.
  3. Arrange a handful of mixed greens on top of the yogurt.
  4. Place the grilled chicken slices on the greens, and then spoon the mango salsa over the chicken.
  5. Squeeze a lime wedge over the filling for an extra pop of citrus flavor.

Step 4: Wrap and Serve

  1. To wrap the chicken wrap, fold the bottom edge of the tortilla up over the filling.
  2. Fold in the sides, then roll it up tightly from the bottom, keeping the filling secure.
  3. Cut each wrap in half diagonally and serve immediately.

Why You’ll Love This Recipe

The Mango Salsa Chicken Wrap is a delightful combination of flavors and textures. The sweetness of the mango salsa pairs beautifully with the smoky, grilled chicken, while the Greek yogurt adds a creamy, tangy element. The addition of mixed greens provides a fresh crunch, and the warm, soft tortilla brings everything together in a comforting embrace.

This recipe is perfect for a quick and satisfying lunch or dinner, and it’s also great for meal prepping. You can make the mango salsa and grill the chicken ahead of time, then assemble the wraps as needed. It’s a versatile dish that’s equally enjoyable at home or on the go.

So, if you’re craving a taste of summer, whip up these Mango Salsa Chicken Wraps and transport yourself to a tropical paradise with every bite. Enjoy the explosion of flavors and the satisfaction of creating a dish that’s as visually appealing as it is delicious!

With the Mango Salsa Chicken Wrap, you can elevate your mealtime with a burst of fresh, vibrant flavors. This recipe is a celebration of summer and a testament to the beauty of simple, wholesome ingredients coming together in perfect harmony. Whether you’re hosting a gathering or simply treating yourself to a delightful meal, this dish is sure to impress. So, roll up your sleeves and get ready to experience a taste sensation like no other!
