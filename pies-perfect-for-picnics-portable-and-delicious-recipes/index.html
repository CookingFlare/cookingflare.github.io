Pies Perfect for Picnics: Portable and Delicious Recipes


when planning a picnic, one of the most important considerations is what to bring for food. you want something that’s easy to transport, doesn’t require a lot of preparation, and is still delicious and satisfying. pies are a great option for a picnic, as they’re portable, easy to eat, and can be made in a variety of flavors. in this article, we’ll explore some of the best pie recipes perfect for a picnic.

savory pie options

savory pies are a great option for a picnic, as they’re often easy to eat and can be made with a variety of ingredients. here are a few savory pie recipes you might consider:

chicken and mushroom pie

this pie is a classic combination of flavors, with chicken, mushrooms, and a creamy sauce all wrapped up in a flaky crust. to make it, you’ll need:

  • 1 1/2 cups chicken breast or thighs, cut into small pieces
  • 1 cup mixed mushrooms, sliced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/2 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup chicken broth
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1 pie crust

preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C). in a large skillet, sauté the chicken, mushrooms, and garlic until the chicken is cooked through and the mushrooms are tender. stir in the flour, chicken broth, and heavy cream, and bring to a boil. reduce the heat and simmer until the filling has thickened. season with thyme, salt, and pepper. roll out the pie crust and place it in a 9-inch pie dish. fill the pie crust with the filling and cover with another pie crust or a piece of aluminum foil. bake for 25-30 minutes, or until the crust is golden brown.

spinach and feta pie

this pie is a great option for a picnic, as it’s easy to eat and can be made with a variety of ingredients. to make it, you’ll need:

  • 1 package frozen chopped spinach, thawed and drained
  • 1/2 cup crumbled feta cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated cheddar cheese
  • 1/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1 egg, beaten
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1 pie crust

preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C). in a large bowl, combine the spinach, feta cheese, cheddar cheese, flour, milk, egg, salt, and pepper. mix well until all the ingredients are fully incorporated. roll out the pie crust and place it in a 9-inch pie dish. fill the pie crust with the filling and cover with another pie crust or a piece of aluminum foil. bake for 25-30 minutes, or until the crust is golden brown.

this pie is a great option for a picnic, as it’s easy to eat and can be made with a variety of ingredients. to make it, you’ll need:

  • 3 cups broccoli florets
  • 1 cup grated cheddar cheese
  • 1/2 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1 egg, beaten
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt

preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C). in a large skillet, sauté the broccoli until it’s tender. in a large bowl, combine the broccoli, cheddar cheese, flour, milk, egg, salt, and pepper. mix well until all the ingredients are fully incorporated. roll out the pie crust and place it in a 9-inch pie dish. fill the pie crust with the filling and cover with another pie crust or a piece of aluminum foil. bake for 25-30 minutes, or until the crust is golden brown.

strawberry pie

  • 2 cups sliced strawberries
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 2 tablespoons cornstarch
  • 1/4 cup lemon juice

  • 1 pie crust

tips for transporting pies

when transporting pies, it’s important to keep them cool and protected from the elements. here are a few tips to help you do just that:

keep pies cool

pies are best served at room temperature, so it’s important to keep them cool during transport. you can do this by placing them in a cooler with ice packs or by wrapping them in a towel and placing them in a insulated bag.

protect pies from the elements

pies are sensitive to moisture and extreme temperatures, so it’s important to protect them from the elements. you can do this by wrapping them in plastic wrap or aluminum foil and placing them in a container or bag.

use a pie carrier

if you’re transporting multiple pies, consider using a pie carrier. a pie carrier is a container with multiple compartments that can hold multiple pies. it’s a great way to keep your pies organized and protected during transport.

keep pies upright

when transporting pies, it’s important to keep them upright to prevent them from getting damaged. you can do this by placing them in a container or bag with a handle or by using a pie carrier with upright compartments.

by following these tips, you can ensure that your pies arrive at your picnic destination in perfect condition. with these portable and delicious pie recipes, you’ll be sure to impress your friends and family at your next picnic.
