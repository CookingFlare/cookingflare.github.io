Sinfully Delicious Chocolate Banana Cream Pie: A Creamy Delight


If you’re a fan of decadent desserts and the irresistible combination of chocolate and bananas, then this sinfully delicious chocolate banana cream pie is the perfect treat for you. Indulge in the creamy, rich flavors and the delightful contrast of textures in every bite. Whether you’re hosting a dinner party or simply craving a sweet delight, this recipe is bound to impress and satisfy your taste buds.

Ingredients

For the Crust:

  • 1 1/2 cups of chocolate cookie crumbs
  • 6 tablespoons of unsalted butter, melted
  • 1/4 cup of granulated sugar

For the Filling:

  • 2 large ripe bananas, sliced
  • 1/2 cup of granulated sugar
  • 1/4 cup of cornstarch
  • 1/4 teaspoon of salt
  • 2 cups of whole milk
  • 4 large egg yolks
  • 6 ounces of semisweet chocolate, chopped
  • 2 tablespoons of unsalted butter
  • 1 teaspoon of pure vanilla extract

For the Topping:

  • 1 1/2 cups of heavy cream
  • 2 tablespoons of confectioners’ sugar
  • 1 teaspoon of pure vanilla extract
  • Chocolate shavings for garnish (optional)

Instructions

Step 1: Prepare the Crust

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. In a medium bowl, combine the chocolate cookie crumbs, melted butter, and granulated sugar.
  3. Press the mixture into the bottom and up the sides of a 9-inch pie dish.
  4. Bake the crust for 10 minutes, then let it cool completely.

Step 2: Make the Filling

  1. Place the sliced bananas in a single layer on the cooled pie crust.
  2. In a medium saucepan, whisk together the granulated sugar, cornstarch, and salt.
  3. Gradually whisk in the milk until smooth.
  4. In a separate bowl, whisk the egg yolks. Gradually whisk in the milk mixture.
  5. Cook the mixture over medium heat, stirring constantly, until it thickens and boils.
  6. Remove the pan from the heat and add the chopped chocolate, butter, and vanilla extract. Stir until smooth.
  7. Pour the filling over the bananas in the pie crust. Place plastic wrap directly on the surface of the filling and chill for at least 2 hours.

Step 3: Prepare the Topping

  1. In a large mixing bowl, whip the heavy cream, confectioners’ sugar, and vanilla extract until stiff peaks form.
  2. Spread the whipped cream over the chilled pie filling.
  3. If desired, garnish with chocolate shavings.
