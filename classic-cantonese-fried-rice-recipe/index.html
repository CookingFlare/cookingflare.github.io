Classic Cantonese Fried Rice Recipe


Are you craving a delicious and satisfying meal that’s quick and easy to prepare? Look no further than the classic Cantonese Fried Rice. This popular dish is a staple in Chinese cuisine, known for its vibrant flavors and simple yet hearty ingredients. Whether you’re a seasoned home cook or just starting your culinary journey, this recipe is sure to impress your taste buds and leave you wanting more.

Ingredients:

  • 3 cups cooked jasmine rice (preferably day-old rice)
  • 2 eggs, beaten
  • 1 cup mixed vegetables (such as peas, carrots, and corn)
  • 1/2 cup diced chicken, pork, or shrimp (optional)
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon oyster sauce
  • 1 tablespoon sesame oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Green onions, chopped (for garnish)
  • Vegetable oil for cooking

Instructions:

  1. Preparation: Start by ensuring all your ingredients are prepped and ready to go. Having everything measured and chopped beforehand will make the cooking process smoother.

  2. Heat the Wok: In a large wok or skillet, heat a drizzle of vegetable oil over medium-high heat. Make sure the pan is hot before adding any ingredients.

  3. Scramble the Eggs: Pour the beaten eggs into the wok and scramble them until they are cooked through. Once done, remove the eggs from the wok and set them aside.

  4. Stir-fry the Protein: If using chicken, pork, or shrimp, add them to the wok and cook until they are fully cooked. Remove the protein from the wok and set it aside with the eggs.

  5. Cook the Vegetables: Add the mixed vegetables to the wok and stir-fry until they are tender. You can also blanch the vegetables beforehand for a brighter color and quicker cooking time.

  6. Add Aromatics: Push the vegetables to the side of the wok and add the minced garlic. Stir-fry for a few seconds until fragrant, being careful not to burn the garlic.

  7. Combine Ingredients: Return the cooked eggs and protein to the wok. Add the cooked rice, soy sauce, oyster sauce, and sesame oil. Stir everything together until well combined.

  8. Season to Taste: Season the fried rice with salt and pepper according to your preference. Adjust the soy sauce or oyster sauce if needed for more flavor.

  9. Finish and Garnish: Once everything is heated through and well mixed, remove the wok from the heat. Garnish the fried rice with chopped green onions for a pop of freshness and color.

  10. Serve and Enjoy: Transfer the classic Cantonese Fried Rice to a serving dish and enjoy it hot. This dish pairs well with a side of crispy egg rolls or a simple vegetable stir-fry.

Now that you have mastered the art of making Classic Cantonese Fried Rice, you can impress your family and friends with this flavorful and comforting dish. The beauty of this recipe lies in its versatility, allowing you to customize it with your favorite ingredients and make it your own. So, roll up your sleeves, grab your wok, and start cooking up a storm in your kitchen. Happy cooking!

Remember, practice makes perfect, so don’t be afraid to experiment with different flavors and textures to create your signature fried rice masterpiece. Let your creativity shine through, and most importantly, enjoy the process of cooking and sharing delicious meals with your loved ones. Bon appétit!
