Tasty Teriyaki Glazed Salmon - Sweet and Savory


If you’re looking for a delicious and healthy meal that’s bursting with flavor, then you’ve come to the right place. This Tasty Teriyaki Glazed Salmon recipe is the perfect combination of sweet and savory, and it’s sure to impress your family and friends. Not only is this dish incredibly delicious, but it’s also quick and easy to prepare, making it an ideal choice for busy weeknights or special occasions. So, let’s dive into the details of this mouthwatering recipe!

Ingredients

For this Tasty Teriyaki Glazed Salmon recipe, you’ll need the following ingredients:

  • 4 salmon fillets
  • 1/2 cup soy sauce
  • 1/4 cup brown sugar
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon grated fresh ginger
  • 2 tablespoons honey
  • 2 tablespoons rice vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon cornstarch
  • 2 tablespoons water
  • Sesame seeds and chopped green onions for garnish

Instructions

Step 1: Preparing the Teriyaki Glaze

To start, let’s prepare the delicious teriyaki glaze. In a small saucepan, combine the soy sauce, brown sugar, minced garlic, grated ginger, honey, and rice vinegar. Stir the mixture over medium heat until the sugar has completely dissolved and the sauce begins to simmer.

Step 2: Thickening the Glaze

In a separate bowl, mix the cornstarch and water until the cornstarch is fully dissolved. Then, gradually add this mixture to the simmering teriyaki sauce, stirring constantly. Continue to cook the sauce for an additional 2-3 minutes, or until it thickens to the desired consistency. Once the glaze has thickened, remove it from the heat and set it aside.

Step 3: Preparing the Salmon

Now, it’s time to prepare the star of the show – the salmon! Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C). Place the salmon fillets on a lined baking sheet, and generously brush each fillet with the prepared teriyaki glaze.

Step 4: Baking the Salmon

Bake the salmon in the preheated oven for 12-15 minutes, or until the fish is cooked through and flakes easily with a fork. While the salmon is baking, you can brush it with additional teriyaki glaze halfway through the cooking time to enhance the flavor.

Step 5: Garnishing and Serving

Once the salmon is perfectly cooked, remove it from the oven and garnish with a sprinkle of sesame seeds and chopped green onions. This will add a beautiful touch and an extra burst of flavor to the dish. Serve the Tasty Teriyaki Glazed Salmon alongside steamed rice and your favorite vegetables for a complete and satisfying meal.

Enjoy Your Culinary Creation!

There you have it – a delectable Tasty Teriyaki Glazed Salmon recipe that’s both sweet and savory, and sure to be a crowd-pleaser. Whether you’re cooking for a special occasion or simply treating yourself to a flavorful and nutritious meal, this recipe is a winner. So, roll up your sleeves, gather your ingredients, and embark on a culinary adventure with this delightful dish. Enjoy every bite of this mouthwatering creation, and don’t hesitate to share the recipe with your loved ones. Happy cooking!
