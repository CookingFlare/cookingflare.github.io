Vibrant Veggie Frittata for a Healthy Brunch


Are you looking for a delicious and healthy brunch option that will impress your guests? Look no further than this vibrant veggie frittata! Packed with colorful vegetables and flavorful herbs, this dish is not only a feast for the eyes but also a nutritious treat for your body. Whether you’re hosting a brunch gathering or simply want to elevate your weekend breakfast routine, this vibrant veggie frittata is the perfect choice.

Ingredients

  • 8 large eggs
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 small red onion, diced
  • 1 red bell pepper, diced
  • 1 yellow bell pepper, diced
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 2 cups fresh baby spinach
  • 1/2 cup crumbled feta cheese
  • 2 tablespoons chopped fresh basil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Preheat the Oven: Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C).

  2. Prepare the Vegetables: In a large oven-safe skillet, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the diced red onion and sauté until translucent. Then, add the diced red and yellow bell peppers, and cook until they start to soften. Add the cherry tomatoes and cook for another 2-3 minutes. Finally, add the fresh baby spinach and cook until wilted.

  3. Whisk the Eggs: In a large bowl, whisk together the eggs, milk, chopped fresh basil, salt, and pepper until well combined.

  4. Combine the Ingredients: Pour the egg mixture over the sautéed vegetables in the skillet. Gently stir to distribute the vegetables evenly. Sprinkle the crumbled feta cheese on top.

  5. Bake the Frittata: Transfer the skillet to the preheated oven and bake for 20-25 minutes, or until the frittata is set in the middle and the edges are golden brown.

  6. Serve and Enjoy: Once the frittata is cooked, remove it from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes. Slice it into wedges and serve with a side of fresh fruit or a green salad.

Tips for Success

  • Use a non-stick oven-safe skillet to ensure that the frittata doesn’t stick to the pan.
  • Feel free to customize the vegetable selection based on your preferences or seasonal availability.
  • For a dairy-free option, you can omit the feta cheese or substitute it with dairy-free cheese.

Nutritional Benefits

This vibrant veggie frittata is not only a treat for your taste buds but also a nutritional powerhouse. Packed with protein from the eggs and milk, as well as an array of vitamins and minerals from the colorful vegetables, this frittata provides a balanced and wholesome meal option. The addition of spinach offers a dose of iron and fiber, while the bell peppers contribute vitamin C and antioxidants.

Conclusion

Next time you’re planning a brunch or simply want to indulge in a flavorful and healthy meal, consider making this vibrant veggie frittata. It’s a versatile dish that can be enjoyed warm or at room temperature, making it perfect for entertaining or for a leisurely weekend brunch at home. With its vibrant colors and fresh flavors, this frittata is sure to become a staple in your recipe repertoire. So, gather your favorite vegetables and get ready to create a delightful and nutritious frittata that will brighten up any brunch table!
