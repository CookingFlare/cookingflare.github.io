BBQ Potato Salad: A Classic Side Dish Reinvented


Potato salad, a timeless classic that’s ever so comforting, is the secret ingredient you need to elevate your barbecue experience. This BBQ potato salad is a reinvented take on the traditional side dish that spices up your gatherings with its tangy, smoky flavors. Let’s dive into the deets of this scrumptious barbecue potato salad that will steal the show!

Ingredients

First, let us look into what you would need to whip up this mouthwatering creation:

  • 2 lbs. of baby potatoes
  • 1/2 red onion, finely chopped
  • 2 stalks of celery, sliced
  • 1/2 cup of real bacon bits
  • 2 green bell peppers, diced
  • 1 cup of grape tomatoes, halved
  • 1/2 cup of pickles, sliced
  • 1/2 cup of real mayonnaise
  • 1/4 cup of apple cider vinegar
  • 1/4 cup of sweet BBQ sauce
  • 1/2 teaspoon of smoked paprika
  • Salt and black pepper, to taste
  • Fresh parsley, chopped
  • Optional: 8 oz. of cream cheese, softened

Directions

With the ingredients well-prepared, let’s proceed to the tantalizing creation of the BBQ potato salad!

  1. Start by filling a large pot with cold water and bringing it to a gentle boil. Carefully add in the baby potatoes, and allow them to simmer for about 15-20 minutes or until fork-tender. Drain and let them cool enough to handle.
  2. In a large bowl, combine the diced bell peppers, chopped onions, and celery, and set them aside.
  3. On low heat, mix together the mayonnaise, apple cider vinegar, BBQ sauce, smoked paprika, salt, and black pepper in a saucepan. Stir it up, allowing the flavors to meld.
  4. Once the potatoes are cool enough, slice them in half and gently fold them into the vegetable mixture.
  5. Pour the sauce over the potato salad and mix thoroughly. If cream cheese is preferred, blend it in with the mayo mixture.
  6. Add in bacon bits, grape tomatoes, and sliced pickles. Give it a gentle mix to blend everything—the potato salad will be unctuous, tangy, and smoky all in one!
  7. Garnish with freshly chopped parsley and let it stand for about 30 minutes to set the flavors further.
  8. Finally, serve this tantalizing barbecue potato salad on the side of your favorite barbecue feast.

Variations

Just like a well-written story, this BBQ potato salad offers different interpretations that can spruce up the menu!

  • Add in some crumbled feta cheese for a Greek flair.
  • Make it a Southern-style potato salad by incorporating finely chopped onions and roasted corn.
  • For a kick of spice, stir in some jalapeno peppers or dust with cayenne pepper.
  • Make it a complete meal by adding in cooked and peeled hard-boiled eggs.

Conclusion

Give your barbecues a much-needed lift with a zesty spin on the classic side dish. This BBQ potato salad promises to wet your appetite and is sure to be a hit at your next get-together! With its scrumptious balance of flavors and textures, this potato salad will surely become your signature dish. So, what’s holding you back from sizzling up your barbecue nights?
