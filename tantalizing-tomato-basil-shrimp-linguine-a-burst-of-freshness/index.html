Tantalizing Tomato Basil Shrimp Linguine: A Burst of Freshness

3 min · 510 words

There’s something truly special about the combination of juicy shrimp, fragrant basil, and ripe tomatoes. When these ingredients come together in a dish, they create a burst of freshness that is simply irresistible. That’s why I’m excited to share with you my recipe for Tantalizing Tomato Basil Shrimp Linguine. This dish is not only a celebration of summer flavors but also a delightful culinary experience that will leave your taste buds dancing.

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound of fresh linguine
  • 1 pound of large shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 4 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 4 cups of ripe tomatoes, chopped
  • 1 cup of fresh basil leaves, chopped
  • 1/2 teaspoon of red pepper flakes
  • 1/4 cup of extra virgin olive oil
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • Grated Parmesan cheese for serving
  • Fresh basil leaves for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the Linguine: Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Add the linguine and cook according to the package instructions until al dente. Drain the linguine and set it aside.

  2. Cook the Shrimp: In a large skillet, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the minced garlic and red pepper flakes, and sauté for about a minute until fragrant. Add the shrimp to the skillet and cook for 2-3 minutes on each side until they turn pink and opaque. Remove the shrimp from the skillet and set them aside.

  3. Make the Tomato Basil Sauce: In the same skillet, add the chopped tomatoes and season with salt and black pepper. Cook the tomatoes for 5-7 minutes until they start to break down and release their juices. Add the chopped basil and stir to combine. Let the sauce simmer for a few minutes until it thickens slightly.

  4. Combine the Ingredients: Add the cooked linguine to the skillet with the tomato basil sauce. Toss everything together until the linguine is well coated with the sauce.

  5. Serve: Divide the linguine among serving plates. Arrange the cooked shrimp on top of the linguine. Garnish with fresh basil leaves and a generous sprinkle of grated Parmesan cheese.

  6. Enjoy: Serve the Tantalizing Tomato Basil Shrimp Linguine immediately and savor the burst of flavors with every bite.
