Satisfy Your Cravings with our Teriyaki Chicken Sliders


Are you craving a delicious and satisfying meal that’s bursting with flavor? Look no further than our mouthwatering Teriyaki Chicken Sliders! These sliders are the perfect combination of tender chicken, tangy teriyaki sauce, and fresh ingredients, all nestled between soft buns. Whether you’re cooking for a crowd or just treating yourself, these sliders are sure to satisfy your cravings.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound boneless, skinless chicken thighs
  • 1/2 cup teriyaki sauce
  • 1/4 cup mayonnaise
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon honey
  • 1 teaspoon sesame oil
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon ginger powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 12 slider buns
  • Fresh lettuce leaves
  • Sliced tomatoes
  • Sliced red onions

Instructions

  1. In a bowl, combine the teriyaki sauce, soy sauce, honey, sesame oil, garlic powder, ginger powder, and black pepper. Mix well to create the marinade.

  2. Place the chicken thighs in a shallow dish and pour the marinade over them. Make sure the chicken is fully coated. Cover the dish and refrigerate for at least 30 minutes, allowing the flavors to infuse.

  3. Preheat your grill or stovetop grill pan over medium-high heat. Remove the chicken from the marinade and discard any excess marinade.

  4. Grill the chicken for about 4-5 minutes on each side, or until it reaches an internal temperature of 165°F (75°C). The chicken should be nicely charred and cooked through.

  5. While the chicken is cooking, lightly toast the slider buns on the grill or in a toaster oven. This will give them a nice crunch and enhance their flavor.

  6. Once the chicken is cooked, remove it from the grill and let it rest for a few minutes. Then, slice the chicken into thin strips.

  7. Assemble the sliders by spreading a thin layer of mayonnaise on the bottom half of each bun. Top with a few slices of chicken, followed by lettuce, tomatoes, and red onions. Place the top half of the bun over the fillings.

  8. Serve your Teriyaki Chicken Sliders immediately and enjoy the explosion of flavors with every bite!

Tips and Variations

  • For a spicy twist, add a few dashes of hot sauce to the marinade.
  • Feel free to customize your sliders with additional toppings like avocado, pickles, or cheese.
  • If you prefer a lighter option, you can use boneless, skinless chicken breasts instead of thighs.
  • Make a larger batch of the marinade and save it for future use. It’s great for marinating other meats or as a flavorful sauce for stir-fries.
  • If you don’t have a grill, you can cook the chicken in a skillet over medium heat until it’s cooked through.

Now that you have the recipe for our delicious Teriyaki Chicken Sliders, it’s time to get cooking! Whether you’re hosting a party, preparing a quick weeknight meal, or simply treating yourself, these sliders are guaranteed to satisfy your cravings. So gather your ingredients, fire up the grill, and get ready to indulge in a flavor-packed culinary adventure. Enjoy!
