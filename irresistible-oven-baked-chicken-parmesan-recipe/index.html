Irresistible Oven-Baked Chicken Parmesan Recipe


If you’re craving a comforting and flavorful Italian dish, then this irresistible oven-baked chicken parmesan recipe is just what you need. This classic favorite is a perfect choice for a cozy family dinner or a special gathering with friends. With a crispy coating, tender chicken, and savory marinara sauce, this dish is sure to become a staple in your recipe collection.

Ingredients

For the Chicken:

  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 3 large eggs
  • 2 cups Italian seasoned breadcrumbs
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Olive oil cooking spray

For the Marinara Sauce:

  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can (28 oz) crushed tomatoes
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste

For the Assembly:

  • 1 ½ cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Fresh basil leaves for garnish
  • Cooked spaghetti or your choice of pasta (optional)

Instructions

  1. Preheat the Oven: Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C). Line a baking sheet with parchment paper.

  2. Prepare the Chicken: Place each chicken breast between two sheets of plastic wrap and gently pound them to an even thickness. Season both sides of the chicken breasts with salt and pepper.

  3. Coating the Chicken: Set up three shallow dishes. In the first dish, place the flour. In the second dish, beat the eggs. In the third dish, combine the Italian seasoned breadcrumbs and grated Parmesan cheese. Dredge each chicken breast in the flour, then dip into the beaten eggs, and finally coat with the breadcrumb mixture, pressing gently to adhere.

  4. Bake the Chicken: Place the coated chicken breasts on the prepared baking sheet. Lightly spray the tops of the chicken with olive oil cooking spray. Bake in the preheated oven for 25-30 minutes, or until the chicken is golden and cooked through.

  5. Prepare the Marinara Sauce: While the chicken is baking, heat the olive oil in a saucepan over medium heat. Add the chopped onion and garlic, and cook until softened. Stir in the crushed tomatoes, sugar, basil, oregano, salt, and pepper. Simmer the sauce for 15-20 minutes, stirring occasionally.

  6. Assemble the Chicken Parmesan: Once the chicken is cooked, remove it from the oven. Spoon a generous amount of marinara sauce over each chicken breast and sprinkle with shredded mozzarella cheese.

  7. Final Bake: Return the chicken to the oven and bake for an additional 5-10 minutes, or until the cheese is melted and bubbly.

  8. Serve: Garnish the chicken parmesan with fresh basil leaves and serve with cooked spaghetti or your choice of pasta if desired.

Tips and Variations

  • For a lighter version, you can use skinless chicken thighs instead of breasts.
  • Add a kick of heat by sprinkling red pepper flakes into the marinara sauce.
  • Substitute the mozzarella cheese with provolone or a blend of Italian cheeses for a different flavor profile.

Conclusion

This irresistible oven-baked chicken parmesan recipe is a crowd-pleaser that will have everyone coming back for seconds. The crispy coating, tender chicken, and rich marinara sauce all come together to create a truly comforting and satisfying dish. Whether you’re cooking for a weeknight dinner or entertaining guests, this recipe is sure to impress. Serve it alongside a fresh green salad and a glass of your favorite wine for a complete Italian dining experience. Buon appetito!
