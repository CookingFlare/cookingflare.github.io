Sicilian Arancini Recipe - Crispy Italian Rice Balls


If you’re looking to add a taste of Sicily to your culinary repertoire, then look no further than the classic Sicilian arancini. These delightful crispy Italian rice balls are a true delight for the senses, offering a perfect blend of flavors and textures that will leave you craving for more. In this recipe, I’ll guide you through the process of creating these delectable treats from scratch, allowing you to impress your friends and family with your newfound Sicilian culinary prowess.

Ingredients

For the Rice

  • 2 cups Arborio rice
  • 4 cups chicken or vegetable broth
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 eggs
  • Salt and pepper to taste

For the Filling

  • 1 cup ground beef or sausage
  • 1/2 cup chopped onion
  • 1/4 cup chopped bell pepper
  • 1/4 cup chopped parsley
  • 1/2 cup tomato sauce
  • 1/2 cup frozen peas
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 cup mozzarella cheese, cut into small cubes

For Breading and Frying

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 eggs, beaten
  • 2 cups breadcrumbs
  • Vegetable oil for frying

Instructions

Step 1: Prepare the Rice

  1. In a large saucepan, bring the chicken or vegetable broth to a boil.
  2. Add the Arborio rice and cook according to the package instructions until the rice is tender and the liquid is absorbed.
  3. Once the rice is cooked, remove it from the heat and let it cool for a few minutes.
  4. Stir in the grated Parmesan cheese, eggs, salt, and pepper until well combined. Set aside to cool completely.

Step 2: Prepare the Filling

  1. In a skillet, cook the ground beef or sausage over medium heat until browned.
  2. Add the chopped onion, bell pepper, and parsley, and cook until the vegetables are softened.
  3. Stir in the tomato sauce, frozen peas, salt, and pepper, and cook for a few minutes until the mixture is well combined and heated through.
  4. Remove the skillet from the heat and let the filling cool slightly.

Step 3: Assemble the Arancini

  1. Take a small handful of the cooled rice mixture and flatten it in the palm of your hand.
  2. Place a cube of mozzarella cheese in the center of the rice and top it with a spoonful of the filling.
  3. Carefully shape the rice around the filling to form a ball, ensuring that the filling is completely enclosed.
  4. Repeat this process with the remaining rice, cheese, and filling to make the arancini.

Step 4: Bread and Fry the Arancini

  1. Roll each arancini in the all-purpose flour, shaking off any excess.
  2. Dip the arancini into the beaten eggs, allowing any excess to drip off.
  3. Finally, roll the arancini in the breadcrumbs, ensuring that they are evenly coated.
  4. In a large, deep skillet, heat the vegetable oil to 350°F (175°C).
  5. Carefully lower the arancini into the hot oil and fry until golden brown and crispy, turning occasionally to ensure even cooking.
  6. Once cooked, remove the arancini from the oil and place them on a paper towel-lined plate to drain any excess oil.

Step 5: Serve and Enjoy

  1. Allow the arancini to cool slightly before serving to avoid burning your mouth.
  2. These delightful crispy Italian rice balls are best enjoyed warm, either on their own or paired with a side of marinara sauce for dipping.
  3. Serve them as an appetizer, snack, or even as a main course alongside a crisp salad for a truly satisfying meal.

Conclusion

With their crispy exterior, creamy interior, and savory filling, Sicilian arancini are a true culinary delight that will transport your taste buds to the sun-drenched shores of Sicily. Whether you’re looking to impress guests at a dinner party or simply craving a taste of Italy, this recipe is sure to become a favorite in your cooking repertoire. So roll up your sleeves, gather your ingredients, and embark on a culinary journey to create these irresistible Sicilian arancini. Buon appetito!
