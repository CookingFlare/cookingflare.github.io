Grilled Pasta Creations: Unconventional Italian BBQ Dishes


If you’re a fan of both Italian cuisine and BBQ, then you’re in for a treat! In this blog post, we’ll be exploring the world of grilled pasta creations - unconventional Italian BBQ dishes that will satisfy your cravings and impress your guests. Get ready to fire up the grill and take your pasta game to a whole new level!

Grilled Caprese Ravioli Skewers

Let’s start with a twist on a classic Italian dish - the Caprese salad. Instead of serving it in the traditional way, we’re going to grill some ravioli and turn it into a delicious skewer. Here’s how you can make it:

  1. Cook some cheese ravioli according to the package instructions.
  2. Thread the cooked ravioli onto skewers, alternating with cherry tomatoes and fresh basil leaves.
  3. Brush the skewers with olive oil and season with salt and pepper.
  4. Grill the skewers over medium heat for about 2-3 minutes on each side, until they’re nicely charred.
  5. Serve the grilled Caprese ravioli skewers with a drizzle of balsamic glaze for an extra burst of flavor.

Grilled Shrimp Scampi Linguine

Next up, we have a mouthwatering combination of grilled shrimp and linguine - a dish that will transport you straight to the Italian coast. Here’s how to make it:

  1. Toss some peeled and deveined shrimp with olive oil, minced garlic, lemon juice, salt, and pepper.
  2. Grill the shrimp over medium-high heat for about 2-3 minutes on each side, until they’re pink and slightly charred.
  3. Cook linguine according to package instructions until al dente.
  4. In a large skillet, melt some butter and sauté minced garlic until fragrant.
  5. Add the grilled shrimp to the skillet and toss to combine.
  6. Add the cooked linguine to the skillet and toss until well coated with the shrimp and garlic mixture.
  7. Serve the grilled shrimp scampi linguine with a sprinkle of freshly chopped parsley and grated Parmesan cheese.

Grilled Zucchini Carbonara

If you’re looking for a lighter option, this grilled zucchini carbonara is the perfect choice. It’s a healthier twist on the traditional carbonara pasta, with a smoky flavor from the grilled zucchini. Here’s how you can make it:

  1. Slice zucchini into long, thin strips and brush them with olive oil.
  2. Grill the zucchini over medium heat for about 2-3 minutes on each side, until they’re tender and have grill marks.
  3. Cook spaghetti according to package instructions until al dente.
  4. In a bowl, whisk together eggs, grated Parmesan cheese, minced garlic, salt, and pepper.
  5. Drain the cooked spaghetti and immediately toss it with the egg and cheese mixture.
  6. Add the grilled zucchini to the spaghetti and toss until well combined.
  7. Serve the grilled zucchini carbonara with a sprinkle of freshly ground black pepper and additional grated Parmesan cheese.

Grilled Lobster Fettuccine Alfredo

For a truly indulgent BBQ pasta dish, look no further than grilled lobster fettuccine Alfredo. The smoky grilled lobster pairs perfectly with the creamy Alfredo sauce, creating a decadent and unforgettable meal. Here’s how to make it:

  1. Split lobsters in half lengthwise and brush them with melted butter.
  2. Grill the lobsters over medium-high heat, shell side down, for about 5-6 minutes, until the meat is opaque and cooked through.
  3. Remove the lobster meat from the shells and chop it into bite-sized pieces.
  4. Cook fettuccine according to package instructions until al dente.
  5. In a saucepan, heat heavy cream over medium heat until it starts to simmer.
  6. Add grated Parmesan cheese, minced garlic, salt, and pepper to the saucepan and stir until the cheese is melted and the sauce is smooth.
  7. Add the grilled lobster meat to the Alfredo sauce and toss to coat.
  8. Serve the grilled lobster fettuccine Alfredo with a sprinkle of freshly chopped parsley.

Conclusion

These grilled pasta creations are a delightful fusion of Italian flavors and BBQ goodness. Whether you’re hosting a summer cookout or just looking to try something new, these dishes are sure to impress. So, fire up the grill, grab your favorite pasta, and get ready to embark on a delicious journey through unconventional Italian BBQ dishes. Buon appetito!
