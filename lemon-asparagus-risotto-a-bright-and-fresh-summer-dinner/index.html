Lemon Asparagus Risotto: A Bright and Fresh Summer Dinner


Risotto is a classic Italian dish that is loved by many for its creamy texture and rich flavors. Today, we’re putting a fresh and vibrant twist on this traditional recipe with the addition of lemon and asparagus. This Lemon Asparagus Risotto is the perfect dish to enjoy on a warm summer evening, as it combines the brightness of lemon with the earthiness of asparagus.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup Arborio rice
  • 4 cups vegetable broth
  • 1 bunch asparagus, trimmed and cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/2 cup dry white wine
  • 1 lemon, zest and juice
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley, chopped (for garnish)

Instructions

  1. In a large saucepan, heat the vegetable broth over medium heat until it simmers. Reduce the heat to low and keep the broth warm.

  2. In a separate large saucepan or Dutch oven, melt the butter over medium heat. Add the chopped onion and minced garlic, and sauté until they become translucent and fragrant.

  3. Add the Arborio rice to the pan and stir to coat it with the butter, onion, and garlic mixture. Cook for about 2 minutes, stirring constantly, until the rice becomes slightly toasted.

  4. Pour in the white wine and continue stirring until it has been absorbed by the rice.

  5. Begin adding the warm vegetable broth to the rice mixture, one ladleful at a time, stirring constantly. Allow each ladleful of broth to be absorbed by the rice before adding the next. This slow and steady process of adding broth and stirring is what gives risotto its creamy texture.

  6. After about 15 minutes of adding broth and stirring, taste the rice to check for doneness. It should be tender with a slight bite (al dente). If necessary, continue adding broth and stirring until the desired texture is achieved.

  7. In a separate pan, blanch the asparagus in boiling salted water for 2-3 minutes, until it becomes bright green and slightly tender. Drain the asparagus and set it aside.

  8. Once the risotto is cooked to perfection, remove the pan from the heat. Stir in the lemon zest, lemon juice, grated Parmesan cheese, and blanched asparagus. Season with salt and pepper to taste and give everything a final stir to combine.

  9. Cover the pan and let the risotto rest for a few minutes to allow the flavors to meld together.

  10. Serve the Lemon Asparagus Risotto in bowls, garnished with freshly chopped parsley. This dish pairs well with a crisp white wine and is best enjoyed immediately.

Conclusion

Lemon Asparagus Risotto is a delightful and refreshing summer dinner option that will impress your family and friends. The combination of bright lemon and earthy asparagus creates a harmonious balance of flavors, while the creamy texture of the risotto adds a luxurious touch. Whether you’re hosting a dinner party or simply looking for a delicious meal to enjoy on a warm summer night, this Lemon Asparagus Risotto is sure to be a crowd-pleaser. Give it a try and let the flavors of summer shine through!
