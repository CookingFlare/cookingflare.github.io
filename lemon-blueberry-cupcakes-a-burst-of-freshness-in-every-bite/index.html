Lemon Blueberry Cupcakes: A Burst of Freshness in Every Bite


Cupcakes have always been a crowd-pleaser, but when you combine the zesty tang of lemon with the burst of juicy blueberries, you get a flavor combination that will leave your taste buds dancing. Lemon blueberry cupcakes are the perfect treat for any occasion, whether it’s a birthday party, a bridal shower, or just a weekend indulgence. In this recipe, we’ll walk you through the steps to create these delightful cupcakes from scratch.

Ingredients

Here’s what you’ll need:

  • 1 ½ cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 ½ teaspoons baking powder
  • ¼ teaspoon salt
  • ½ cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • ½ cup milk
  • Zest of 1 lemon
  • 1 cup fresh blueberries

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C) and line a cupcake pan with paper liners.

  2. In a medium-sized bowl, whisk together the flour, baking powder, and salt. Set aside.

  3. In a separate large bowl, cream together the softened butter and granulated sugar until light and fluffy. This will take about 3-4 minutes with an electric mixer on medium speed.

  4. Add the eggs, one at a time, beating well after each addition. Mix in the vanilla extract.

  5. Slowly add the dry ingredients to the butter mixture, alternating with the milk. Begin and end with the dry ingredients, mixing until just combined.

  6. Gently fold in the lemon zest and fresh blueberries, being careful not to overmix.

  7. Divide the batter evenly among the prepared cupcake liners, filling each about 2/3 full.

  8. Bake for 18-20 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center of a cupcake comes out clean.

  9. Remove the cupcakes from the oven and let them cool in the pan for 5 minutes. Then transfer them to a wire rack to cool completely before frosting.

Frosting

To complement the refreshing flavors of the lemon blueberry cupcakes, we recommend a luscious cream cheese frosting. Here’s how to make it:

Ingredients

  • 8 oz (225g) cream cheese, softened
  • ½ cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 4 cups powdered sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Instructions

  1. In a large bowl, beat the cream cheese and butter until smooth and creamy.

  2. Gradually add the powdered sugar, beating well after each addition.

  3. Mix in the vanilla extract until well combined.

  4. Once the cupcakes have cooled completely, frost them generously with the cream cheese frosting.

Serving and Storage

Lemon blueberry cupcakes are best served fresh, allowing you to fully enjoy their moist texture and vibrant flavors. However, if you have leftovers, store them in an airtight container in the refrigerator for up to 3 days. Just make sure to bring them to room temperature before serving to maximize their taste.

Final Thoughts

There’s something undeniably delightful about the combination of tangy lemon and sweet blueberries. These lemon blueberry cupcakes are a testament to the magic that can happen when you bring together contrasting flavors. The soft, moist cake bursting with juicy blueberries, topped with a creamy cream cheese frosting, creates a symphony of flavors that will leave you craving more.

Whether you’re a seasoned baker or a novice in the kitchen, this recipe is sure to impress. So go ahead, gather your ingredients, and embark on a baking adventure. Treat yourself and your loved ones to these Lemon Blueberry Cupcakes, and savor the burst of freshness in every bite.
