Savory and Smoky: BBQ Pulled Chicken Sliders


There’s something about a perfectly cooked BBQ pulled chicken slider that just hits the spot. The tender, smoky chicken combined with the tangy barbecue sauce and soft slider buns is a match made in culinary heaven. Whether you’re hosting a summer cookout or simply looking for a delicious meal, these Savory and Smoky BBQ Pulled Chicken Sliders are sure to satisfy your taste buds.

Ingredients

  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1 cup barbecue sauce (homemade or store-bought)
  • 1/4 cup apple cider vinegar
  • 2 tablespoons brown sugar
  • 1 tablespoon smoked paprika
  • 1 tablespoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon black pepper
  • 8 slider buns
  • Coleslaw, for serving (optional)
  • Pickles, for serving (optional)

Instructions

  1. In a slow cooker, combine the barbecue sauce, apple cider vinegar, brown sugar, smoked paprika, garlic powder, salt, and black pepper. Stir until well combined.

  2. Add the chicken breasts to the slow cooker and coat them evenly with the sauce mixture. Cover and cook on low for 6-8 hours or on high for 3-4 hours, until the chicken is tender and easily shreds.

  3. Once the chicken is cooked, remove it from the slow cooker and transfer it to a cutting board. Use two forks to shred the chicken into bite-sized pieces.

  4. While you’re shredding the chicken, place the slider buns on a baking sheet and toast them in the oven for a few minutes until they are lightly golden.

  5. To assemble the sliders, spoon a generous amount of the shredded BBQ chicken onto the bottom half of each slider bun. Top with a dollop of coleslaw, if desired, and a pickle slice. Place the top half of the bun on top and secure with a toothpick.

  6. Serve the Savory and Smoky BBQ Pulled Chicken Sliders as a main dish or as a party appetizer. They pair perfectly with a side of fries or a crisp green salad.

Tips and Variations

  • If you don’t have a slow cooker, you can also cook the chicken in the oven. Simply place the chicken breasts in a baking dish, pour the sauce mixture over them, cover with foil, and bake at 350°F (175°C) for about 45 minutes to 1 hour, or until the chicken is tender and shreds easily.

  • Feel free to customize the BBQ sauce to suit your taste. If you prefer a sweeter sauce, add more brown sugar. If you like it spicier, add a dash of hot sauce or cayenne pepper.

  • For an extra smoky flavor, you can also add a few drops of liquid smoke to the sauce mixture. Just be careful not to overpower the other flavors.

  • If you’re serving these sliders at a party, consider setting up a DIY slider bar with various toppings like sliced onions, jalapenos, or different types of cheese. Let your guests create their own ultimate BBQ pulled chicken sliders.

These Savory and Smoky BBQ Pulled Chicken Sliders are a crowd-pleasing dish that will leave your guests begging for more. The combination of tender chicken, tangy barbecue sauce, and soft slider buns is simply irresistible. So fire up your slow cooker or preheat your oven, and get ready to indulge in these mouthwatering sliders. Enjoy!
