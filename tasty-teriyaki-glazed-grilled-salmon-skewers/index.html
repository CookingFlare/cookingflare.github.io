Tasty Teriyaki Glazed Grilled Salmon Skewers


There’s something undeniably satisfying about a perfectly grilled piece of salmon, especially when it’s coated in a mouthwatering teriyaki glaze. And what’s even better than that? Turning that delicious salmon into skewers that are perfect for sharing at a summer barbecue or a cozy dinner at home. These Tasty Teriyaki Glazed Grilled Salmon Skewers are a delightful blend of sweet, savory, and slightly smoky flavors that will have your taste buds singing.

Ingredients

For the Teriyaki Glaze:

  • 1/2 cup soy sauce
  • 1/4 cup mirin
  • 2 tablespoons brown sugar
  • 1 tablespoon honey
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon grated fresh ginger
  • 1 tablespoon cornstarch
  • 2 tablespoons water

For the Salmon Skewers:

  • 1 1/2 pounds fresh salmon, cut into 1-inch cubes
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • Wooden skewers, soaked in water for at least 30 minutes

For Garnish:

  • Thinly sliced green onions
  • Sesame seeds

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Teriyaki Glaze:

    • In a small saucepan, combine the soy sauce, mirin, brown sugar, honey, garlic, and ginger. Bring the mixture to a gentle simmer over medium heat, stirring occasionally.
    • In a small bowl, dissolve the cornstarch in water to create a slurry. Slowly pour the slurry into the saucepan while whisking constantly.
    • Continue to cook the glaze for 2-3 minutes, or until it thickens to a syrupy consistency. Remove from heat and set aside.

  2. Prepare the Salmon Skewers:

    • Preheat your grill to medium-high heat.
    • Season the cubed salmon with salt and pepper. Thread the salmon onto the soaked wooden skewers, leaving a little space between each piece to ensure even cooking.

  3. Grill the Salmon Skewers:

    • Place the salmon skewers on the preheated grill and cook for 3-4 minutes on each side, or until the salmon is just cooked through and has a nice char from the grill.
    • During the last minute of grilling, brush the teriyaki glaze generously over the salmon skewers, allowing it to caramelize slightly.

  4. Serve and Enjoy:

    • Carefully transfer the grilled salmon skewers to a serving platter, and sprinkle with thinly sliced green onions and sesame seeds for a burst of freshness and crunch.
    • Serve the Tasty Teriyaki Glazed Grilled Salmon Skewers with steamed white rice, a crisp salad, or grilled vegetables for a complete and satisfying meal.

Tips and Tricks

  • Choosing the Right Salmon: Look for fresh, high-quality salmon with vibrant color and a clean, oceanic scent. If possible, opt for sustainably sourced salmon for a more eco-friendly choice.

  • Soaking the Skewers: Soaking the wooden skewers in water before grilling helps prevent them from burning on the hot grill, ensuring that your salmon stays securely in place.

  • Grilling Time: Keep a close eye on the salmon while grilling to prevent overcooking. The key is to achieve a slightly charred exterior while keeping the interior tender and moist.

Final Thoughts

These Tasty Teriyaki Glazed Grilled Salmon Skewers are a delightful way to elevate your grilling game and impress your friends and family with a burst of Asian-inspired flavors. The tender, succulent salmon, kissed by the smoky grill and adorned with a glossy teriyaki glaze, is a true celebration of summer dining. Whether you’re hosting a barbecue or simply craving a special dinner, these skewers are sure to steal the spotlight and become a new favorite in your recipe collection. So fire up the grill, gather your ingredients, and get ready to savor every delectable bite of these irresistible salmon skewers.
