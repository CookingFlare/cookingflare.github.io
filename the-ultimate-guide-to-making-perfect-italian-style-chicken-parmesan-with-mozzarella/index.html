The Ultimate Guide to Making Perfect Italian-Style Chicken Parmesan with Mozzarella


If you’re a fan of Italian cuisine, then you’ve probably come across the classic and comforting dish that is Chicken Parmesan. Also known as Chicken Parmigiana, this dish features tender chicken breasts coated in breadcrumbs, topped with tomato sauce, and smothered in melted mozzarella cheese. It’s a dish that never fails to satisfy and is a favorite among both kids and adults.

In this ultimate guide, we’ll take you through the step-by-step process of making the perfect Italian-style Chicken Parmesan with Mozzarella. From selecting the right ingredients to achieving that crispy, golden crust, we’ve got you covered. So, let’s dive in!

Ingredients

To make Chicken Parmesan, you’ll need the following ingredients:

  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 large eggs, beaten
  • 2 cups breadcrumbs
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 2 cups marinara sauce
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Fresh basil leaves, for garnish

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C). Line a baking sheet with parchment paper and set aside.

  2. Place the flour in a shallow dish and season it with salt and pepper. In another shallow dish, combine the breadcrumbs, dried basil, dried oregano, garlic powder, salt, and pepper.

  3. Dip each chicken breast into the flour, coating it evenly on both sides. Shake off any excess flour.

  4. Next, dip the chicken into the beaten eggs, allowing any excess to drip off.

  5. Coat the chicken in the breadcrumb mixture, pressing gently to ensure the breadcrumbs adhere to the chicken. Make sure both sides are well coated.

  6. Place the breaded chicken breasts onto the prepared baking sheet and bake for 20-25 minutes, or until the chicken is cooked through and the breadcrumbs are golden brown.

  7. While the chicken is baking, heat the marinara sauce in a saucepan over medium heat until warmed through.

  8. Once the chicken is cooked, remove it from the oven and spoon a generous amount of marinara sauce over each breast.

  9. Sprinkle the shredded mozzarella cheese over the top of each chicken breast, covering them completely.

  10. Return the chicken to the oven and bake for an additional 10 minutes, or until the cheese is melted and bubbly.

  11. Remove the chicken from the oven and let it rest for a few minutes before serving. Garnish with fresh basil leaves for an extra touch of flavor.

Serving Suggestions

Italian-style Chicken Parmesan pairs perfectly with a side of spaghetti or pasta of your choice. You can also serve it with a fresh green salad or steamed vegetables to balance out the richness of the dish. Don’t forget to sprinkle some grated Parmesan cheese on top for an extra burst of flavor!

Conclusion

There you have it—the ultimate guide to making perfect Italian-style Chicken Parmesan with Mozzarella. With its crispy and flavorful coating, juicy chicken, and gooey melted cheese, this dish is a crowd-pleaser that will transport you straight to Italy. Whether you’re cooking for a special occasion or simply craving a taste of comfort, this recipe is sure to impress. So gather your ingredients, follow the steps, and get ready to enjoy a truly delicious meal. Buon appetito!
