BBQ Meatballs: Savory and Tangy Recipes for Appetizers


If you’re looking for a crowd-pleasing appetizer that’s both savory and tangy, look no further than BBQ meatballs. These delectable bites are perfect for any gathering, from game day parties to family barbecues. In this post, we’ll explore two mouthwatering recipes for BBQ meatballs that are sure to impress your guests. Whether you prefer classic flavors or want to experiment with a unique twist, we’ve got you covered.

Classic BBQ Meatballs

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1/2 cup breadcrumbs
  • 1/4 cup milk
  • 1 egg
  • 1/4 cup finely chopped onion
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1 cup BBQ sauce

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C) and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
  2. In a large bowl, combine the ground beef, breadcrumbs, milk, egg, onion, garlic powder, salt, and black pepper. Mix until well combined.
  3. Shape the mixture into meatballs, approximately 1 inch in diameter, and place them on the prepared baking sheet.
  4. Bake the meatballs for 20 minutes or until they are cooked through.
  5. In a separate bowl, mix the BBQ sauce with a splash of water to thin it out slightly.
  6. Once the meatballs are cooked, transfer them to a large skillet or saucepan. Pour the thinned BBQ sauce over the meatballs and gently toss to coat.
  7. Simmer the meatballs in the BBQ sauce for an additional 5-10 minutes, stirring occasionally, until the sauce thickens and coats the meatballs evenly.

Serve the classic BBQ meatballs on a platter with toothpicks for easy snacking. These timeless treats are sure to disappear quickly, so be prepared to make a second batch!

Tangy Pineapple BBQ Meatballs

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound ground pork
  • 1/2 cup panko breadcrumbs
  • 1/4 cup pineapple juice
  • 1/4 cup finely chopped red bell pepper
  • 1 teaspoon soy sauce
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground ginger
  • 1/4 teaspoon paprika
  • 1 cup BBQ sauce
  • 1/2 cup crushed pineapple, drained

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C) and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
  2. In a large bowl, combine the ground pork, panko breadcrumbs, pineapple juice, red bell pepper, soy sauce, ground ginger, and paprika. Mix until well combined.
  3. Shape the mixture into meatballs, approximately 1 inch in diameter, and place them on the prepared baking sheet.
  4. Bake the meatballs for 20 minutes or until they are cooked through.
  5. In a separate bowl, mix the BBQ sauce with the crushed pineapple.
  6. Once the meatballs are cooked, transfer them to a large skillet or saucepan. Pour the pineapple BBQ sauce over the meatballs and gently toss to coat.
  7. Simmer the meatballs in the sauce for an additional 5-10 minutes, stirring occasionally, until the sauce thickens and coats the meatballs evenly.

The tangy pineapple BBQ meatballs offer a delightful twist on the classic recipe, combining the sweetness of pineapple with the smoky flavor of BBQ sauce. Serve these irresistible morsels at your next gathering and watch them disappear within minutes.

Whether you opt for the classic BBQ meatballs or the tangy pineapple variation, these appetizers are guaranteed to be a hit. With their savory and tangy flavors, they’re the perfect addition to any party spread. Experiment with different BBQ sauces and meatball sizes to tailor these recipes to your taste. Enjoy creating and savoring these delicious BBQ meatballs at your next event!
