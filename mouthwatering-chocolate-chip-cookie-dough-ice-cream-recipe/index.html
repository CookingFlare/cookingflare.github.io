Mouthwatering Chocolate Chip Cookie Dough Ice Cream Recipe


If you’re a fan of the classic combination of chocolate chip cookies and creamy ice cream, then get ready to indulge in a homemade treat that will leave your taste buds singing. This mouthwatering chocolate chip cookie dough ice cream recipe is the perfect blend of rich, velvety ice cream and chunks of irresistible cookie dough. Whether you’re hosting a summer gathering, craving a sweet treat, or simply looking to impress your friends and family with your culinary skills, this recipe is sure to be a hit. So, roll up your sleeves, gather your ingredients, and let’s dive into the delicious world of homemade chocolate chip cookie dough ice cream.

Ingredients:

For the Cookie Dough:

  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1/2 cup packed light brown sugar
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 2 tablespoons milk
  • 1 teaspoon pure vanilla extract
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup mini chocolate chips

For the Ice Cream Base:

  • 2 cups heavy cream
  • 1 cup whole milk
  • 3/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 teaspoon pure vanilla extract
  • 1/2 cup mini chocolate chips

Instructions:

Making the Cookie Dough:

  1. In a mixing bowl, cream together the softened butter, light brown sugar, and granulated sugar until light and fluffy.
  2. Add the milk and vanilla extract to the mixture and beat until well combined.
  3. Gradually add the flour and salt, mixing until a smooth dough forms.
  4. Gently fold in the mini chocolate chips until evenly distributed.
  5. Roll the cookie dough into small, bite-sized balls and place them on a parchment-lined baking sheet. Freeze the dough balls for at least 1 hour.

Making the Ice Cream Base:

  1. In a separate bowl, whisk together the heavy cream, whole milk, granulated sugar, and vanilla extract until the sugar is completely dissolved.
  2. Pour the mixture into an ice cream maker and churn according to the manufacturer’s instructions.
  3. During the last few minutes of churning, add the mini chocolate chips to the ice cream base and continue churning until evenly distributed.

Assembling the Ice Cream:

  1. Once the ice cream reaches a soft-serve consistency, transfer half of it to a freezer-safe container.
  2. Take the frozen cookie dough balls and gently fold them into the ice cream in the container.
  3. Add the remaining half of the ice cream on top of the cookie dough chunks and gently swirl the mixture to distribute the cookie dough evenly.
  4. Cover the container and freeze the ice cream for at least 4 hours, or until it reaches your desired firmness.

Serving Suggestions:

Scoop generous servings of the chocolate chip cookie dough ice cream into bowls or cones, and top with additional mini chocolate chips for a delightful crunch. This delectable treat pairs perfectly with freshly baked chocolate chip cookies, creating the ultimate indulgence for any dessert lover.

Conclusion:

This homemade chocolate chip cookie dough ice cream is a delightful blend of creamy, luscious ice cream and chunks of irresistible cookie dough. By following this simple recipe, you can create a frozen dessert that will impress and delight your family and friends. So, grab your mixing bowls and get ready to embark on a delicious journey that culminates in a scoop of pure bliss. Enjoy every spoonful of this homemade delight and revel in the satisfaction of creating a treat that is sure to become a household favorite.
