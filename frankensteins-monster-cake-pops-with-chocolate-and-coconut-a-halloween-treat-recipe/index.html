Frankenstein's Monster Cake Pops with Chocolate and Coconut: A Halloween Treat Recipe


Halloween is the perfect time for spooky treats, and what better way to celebrate than with Frankenstein’s Monster Cake Pops? These delightful little desserts are not only fun to make but also a hit at any Halloween gathering. Made with moist chocolate cake, coated in creamy chocolate, and rolled in coconut, they are as delicious as they are adorable. In this blog post, we’ll guide you through creating these bewitching bites step-by-step, ensuring your Halloween is filled with sweet treats that are sure to impress!

Ingredients

For the Cake Pops

  • 1 box of chocolate cake mix (plus ingredients required on the box)
  • 1 can of chocolate frosting (approximately 16 oz)
  • 1 cup of shredded coconut (unsweetened or sweetened, based on your preference)
  • 1 package of lollipop sticks (approximately 50 count)

For the Coating

  • 12 oz of dark chocolate (you can use candy melts or chocolate chips)
  • 1 cup of shredded coconut (for rolling)
  • Green food coloring (for decorating)
  • Candy eyes (for the finishing touches)
  • Black icing gel (for facial details)

Equipment Needed

  • Mixing bowls
  • Whisk or electric mixer
  • Baking sheet
  • Parchment paper
  • Microwave-safe bowl or double boiler
  • Styrofoam block or cake pop stand (to hold the pops while drying)

Instructions

Step 1: Bake the Cake

  1. Preheat your oven according to the instructions on the cake mix box.
  2. Prepare the cake batter by mixing the chocolate cake mix with the required ingredients (usually water, oil, and eggs). Follow the instructions on the box for the best results.
  3. Pour the batter into a greased 9x13 inch baking pan and bake according to the package instructions. Once baked, allow the cake to cool completely.

Step 2: Crumble the Cake

  1. Once the cake has cooled, remove it from the pan and transfer it to a large mixing bowl.
  2. Crumble the cake into fine pieces using your hands or a fork. This will create the base for the cake pops.

Step 3: Mix the Frosting

  1. Add the chocolate frosting to the crumbled cake. Start with half the can and mix it in thoroughly. You want the mixture to be moist but not overly sticky.
  2. Add more frosting as needed, until the mixture holds together when you squeeze it in your hand.

Step 4: Form the Cake Balls

  1. Using your hands, roll the mixture into balls about 1 inch in diameter. Place them on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper.
  2. Once all the mixture is rolled into balls, place them in the refrigerator for about 30 minutes to firm up.

Step 5: Prepare the Coating

  1. While the cake balls are chilling, melt the dark chocolate in a microwave-safe bowl. Heat in 30-second intervals, stirring between each, until completely melted and smooth.
  2. If you’re using candy melts, follow the package instructions for melting.

Step 6: Coat the Cake Pops

  1. Remove the cake balls from the refrigerator. Take a lollipop stick and dip one end into the melted chocolate before inserting it into the center of a cake ball. This helps to secure the stick.
  2. Dip the cake pop into the melted chocolate, making sure it is fully coated. Allow any excess chocolate to drip off.
  3. Roll the coated cake pop in shredded coconut while it’s still wet. This will give it a spooky, fuzzy appearance reminiscent of Frankenstein’s monster.

Step 7: Decorating the Pops

  1. Place the pops upright in a styrofoam block or cake pop stand to dry completely.
  2. Once the chocolate has set, it’s time to add some personality to your cake pops!
  3. Use green food coloring to create a green “skin” effect by carefully painting it on with a small brush or toothpick.
  4. Attach candy eyes to each pop, and use black icing gel to draw on facial features like scars or hair to give your Frankenstein pops their character.

Step 8: Serve and Enjoy!

  1. Once everything has set, your Frankenstein’s Monster Cake Pops are ready to be served!
  2. Arrange them on a festive platter or display them in a Halloween-themed container for your guests to enjoy.
  3. These cake pops are perfect for parties, trick-or-treating gatherings, or just a fun activity to do with kids during the Halloween season.

Tips for Success

  • Experiment with flavors: While chocolate cake is a classic, you can use any flavor of cake mix you like (such as vanilla or red velvet) for a twist.
  • Make ahead: These cake pops can be made a day in advance. Just store them in an airtight container in the refrigerator until you’re ready to serve.
  • Get creative: Other decorations such as sprinkles or edible glitter can be added for extra flair. You can also use different colored chocolate for a more colorful display.

Conclusion

Frankenstein’s Monster Cake Pops are a delightful addition to your Halloween festivities. With their fun designs and delicious flavors, they are sure to be a crowd-pleaser. Making cake pops may seem daunting at first, but with this step-by-step guide, you’ll see just how easy and rewarding it can be. So gather your ingredients, invite some friends over, and get ready for a spooky and sweet Halloween celebration! Happy baking!
