Korean Braised Chicken Wings: A Sticky and Finger-Licking Treat


Korean braised chicken wings, also known as “dakgangjeong” in Korean, is a popular Korean dish that has gained worldwide recognition for its unique flavor and texture. This dish is a staple in Korean cuisine, and its popularity can be attributed to its ease of preparation, versatility, and the fact that it is a crowd-pleaser.

What makes Korean braised chicken wings special?

The secret to Korean braised chicken wings lies in its sauce, which is made with a combination of soy sauce, Korean chili flakes, garlic, ginger, and sugar. The sauce is simmered for hours to develop a rich, thick consistency that coats the chicken wings evenly. The result is a dish that is both sweet and spicy, with a sticky texture that is irresistible to the taste buds.

How to make Korean braised chicken wings

Making Korean braised chicken wings is relatively easy and requires minimal ingredients. Here’s a simple recipe to get you started:

Ingredients:

  • 2 pounds chicken wings
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon Korean chili flakes
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1-inch piece of ginger, grated
  • 1 tablespoon sugar
  • 1 tablespoon rice vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • 1/4 cup water
  • Salt and black pepper, to taste
  • Scallions, chopped (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 400°F (200°C).
  2. In a large bowl, whisk together soy sauce, Korean chili flakes, garlic, ginger, sugar, rice vinegar, and vegetable oil.
  3. Add the chicken wings to the bowl and toss to coat evenly with the sauce.
  4. Line a baking sheet with aluminum foil and spray with cooking spray. Arrange the chicken wings on the baking sheet in a single layer.
  5. Bake the chicken wings for 30 minutes, or until they are cooked through and lightly browned.
  6. Remove the chicken wings from the oven and increase the oven temperature to broil.
  7. Broil the chicken wings for an additional 2-3 minutes, or until they are crispy and caramelized.
  8. Remove the chicken wings from the oven and toss with salt and black pepper to taste.
  9. Garnish with chopped scallions, if desired.
  10. Serve the Korean braised chicken wings hot with a side of steamed rice or noodles.

Tips and variations

  • To make the dish more spicy, add more Korean chili flakes or use hot sauce like gochujang.
  • To make the dish more sweet, add more sugar or use honey.
  • To make the dish more savory, add more soy sauce or use fish sauce.
  • To make the dish more crispy, increase the baking time or broil the chicken wings for a longer period.
  • To make the dish more tender, cook the chicken wings in a slow cooker or Instant Pot for a longer period.

Conclusion

Korean braised chicken wings is a dish that is both easy to make and delicious to eat. With its unique flavor and sticky texture, it is a crowd-pleaser that is sure to impress. Whether you are a fan of spicy food or sweet and savory flavors, this dish has something for everyone. So why not give it a try and experience the flavors of Korea for yourself?
