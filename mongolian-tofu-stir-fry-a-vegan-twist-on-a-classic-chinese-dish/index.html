Mongolian Tofu Stir Fry: A Vegan Twist on a Classic Chinese Dish


If you’re looking for a flavorful and satisfying vegan dish that’s quick and easy to make, then you’re in for a treat! This Mongolian Tofu Stir Fry recipe offers a delightful twist on the classic Chinese dish, incorporating the rich flavors of Mongolian cuisine with the wholesome goodness of tofu. Whether you’re a dedicated vegan, a tofu enthusiast, or simply someone looking to explore new culinary horizons, this recipe is sure to become a favorite in your kitchen.

Ingredients

For the Tofu Marinade:

  • 1 block of firm tofu, drained and cubed
  • 3 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons rice vinegar
  • 2 tablespoons maple syrup
  • 1 tablespoon cornstarch
  • 1 teaspoon minced garlic
  • 1 teaspoon grated ginger
  • 1/2 teaspoon red pepper flakes

For the Stir Fry:

  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1 small yellow onion, thinly sliced
  • 1 red bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 cup snow peas
  • 3 green onions, chopped
  • Cooked rice or noodles, for serving

For the Sauce:

  • 1/3 cup soy sauce
  • 1/4 cup water
  • 2 tablespoons hoisin sauce
  • 2 tablespoons brown sugar
  • 1 teaspoon sesame oil
  • 1 teaspoon cornstarch

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Tofu: In a shallow dish, whisk together the soy sauce, rice vinegar, maple syrup, cornstarch, garlic, ginger, and red pepper flakes. Add the cubed tofu and gently toss to coat. Allow the tofu to marinate for at least 15 minutes, or up to 1 hour for maximum flavor infusion.

  2. Make the Sauce: In a small bowl, whisk together the soy sauce, water, hoisin sauce, brown sugar, sesame oil, and cornstarch until smooth. Set aside.

  3. Cook the Tofu: In a large skillet or wok, heat 1 tablespoon of vegetable oil over medium-high heat. Add the marinated tofu (reserving any excess marinade) and cook until golden and crispy on all sides, about 5-7 minutes. Remove the tofu from the skillet and set aside.

  4. Prepare the Stir Fry: In the same skillet, heat the remaining tablespoon of vegetable oil. Add the sliced onion, bell pepper, and snow peas, and sauté for 3-4 minutes, or until the vegetables are tender-crisp.

  5. Combine and Simmer: Return the cooked tofu to the skillet with the vegetables. Pour the prepared sauce over the tofu and vegetables, along with the reserved marinade. Stir gently to coat everything in the sauce, then simmer for 2-3 minutes, or until the sauce has thickened.

  6. Serve and Enjoy: Sprinkle the chopped green onions over the stir fry, and serve hot over cooked rice or noodles.

Conclusion

This Mongolian Tofu Stir Fry recipe offers a delightful fusion of classic Chinese flavors with a vegan twist, making it a perfect choice for anyone seeking a delicious and wholesome meal. With its combination of savory tofu, crisp vegetables, and rich Mongolian-inspired sauce, this dish is a true celebration of plant-based cooking. Whether you’re a seasoned chef or a novice in the kitchen, this recipe is a fantastic way to expand your culinary repertoire while enjoying a truly satisfying dining experience. So, gather your ingredients, fire up your stove, and indulge in the irresistible flavors of this delectable Mongolian Tofu Stir Fry!
