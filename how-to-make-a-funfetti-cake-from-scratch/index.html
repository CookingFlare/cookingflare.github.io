How to Make a Funfetti Cake from Scratch


There’s something magical about a Funfetti cake – the cheerful, colorful sprinkles embedded in the moist cake, the sweet frosting, and the excitement it brings to any celebration. Whether you’re preparing for a birthday, an anniversary, or just because you want to treat yourself, making a Funfetti cake from scratch is a fun and rewarding baking project.

In this guide, we’ll walk you through the process of creating this delightful cake, from the essential ingredients to the step-by-step instructions. By the end of this post, you’ll know how to make a Funfetti cake that rivals anything you could find at a bakery. So let’s get started!

Ingredients You’ll Need

Before you begin, it’s important to gather all the necessary ingredients to make your Funfetti cake. Since we’re making the cake from scratch, the ingredients are simple but important for ensuring the cake turns out perfectly. Here’s what you’ll need:

For the Cake:

  • 2 ½ cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 ½ cups granulated sugar
  • 1 tablespoon baking powder
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup unsalted butter (room temperature)
  • 4 large eggs
  • 1 tablespoon vanilla extract
  • 1 cup whole milk
  • ½ cup rainbow sprinkles (plus extra for topping)

For the Frosting:

  • 1 cup unsalted butter (room temperature)
  • 4 cups powdered sugar
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 2 tablespoons heavy cream (adjust for consistency)
  • Pinch of salt
  • ½ cup rainbow sprinkles (for decorating)

Equipment You’ll Need

  • 2 9-inch round cake pans
  • Electric mixer or stand mixer
  • Mixing bowls
  • Measuring cups and spoons
  • Rubber spatula
  • Cooling rack
  • Piping bags (optional, for decorating)

Step-by-Step Instructions

Step 1: Preheat the Oven and Prepare the Pans

Start by preheating your oven to 350°F (175°C). This ensures that the oven is at the correct temperature when you’re ready to bake. While the oven is heating up, prepare your cake pans. Grease two 9-inch round cake pans with butter or cooking spray and line the bottom with parchment paper for easy removal. You can also lightly dust the pans with flour to prevent sticking.

Step 2: Mix the Dry Ingredients

In a medium-sized mixing bowl, whisk together the flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt. Whisking these dry ingredients ensures that everything is evenly distributed and there are no lumps of flour or baking powder. Set this bowl aside as you move on to the next step.

Step 3: Cream the Butter and Sugar

Using an electric mixer, cream the softened butter and granulated sugar together in a large bowl until light and fluffy. This process usually takes about 3-5 minutes. The texture should be pale and smooth, and you should be able to see tiny air pockets in the mixture. This helps to create a light and fluffy cake texture.

Step 4: Add the Eggs and Vanilla

Next, add the eggs to the creamed butter and sugar mixture, one at a time. Be sure to beat well after each addition. This ensures that the eggs are fully incorporated and the batter is smooth. Once all the eggs are added, mix in the vanilla extract. The vanilla will add a warm, aromatic flavor to the cake.

Step 5: Alternate Adding Dry Ingredients and Milk

Now, it’s time to combine the dry ingredients and the milk into the batter. Start by adding about a third of the dry ingredients to the butter, sugar, and egg mixture. Mix until just incorporated. Then, add about half of the milk, and mix again. Repeat this process, alternating between adding the dry ingredients and the milk until both are fully incorporated into the batter.

Make sure not to overmix the batter. Overmixing can lead to a dense cake, and we want a light, airy texture. Once the batter is smooth and well combined, gently fold in the rainbow sprinkles using a rubber spatula.

Step 6: Bake the Cake

Divide the batter evenly between the two prepared cake pans. Smooth the tops with a spatula, making sure the batter is spread out evenly. Place the pans in the preheated oven and bake for about 25-30 minutes. You’ll know the cakes are done when they’re golden brown on top and a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.

Once the cakes are baked, remove them from the oven and allow them to cool in the pans for about 10 minutes. After that, carefully turn the cakes out onto a cooling rack to cool completely. It’s essential to let the cakes cool completely before frosting them to avoid the frosting melting.

Step 7: Prepare the Frosting

While the cakes are cooling, you can prepare the frosting. In a large bowl, beat the softened butter with an electric mixer until it’s smooth and creamy. Gradually add the powdered sugar, 1 cup at a time, mixing well after each addition. Once all the powdered sugar is incorporated, add the vanilla extract, heavy cream, and a pinch of salt.

Continue mixing until the frosting is smooth and fluffy. If the frosting is too thick, add a little more heavy cream, one tablespoon at a time, until you reach your desired consistency. If it’s too thin, add a little more powdered sugar to thicken it up.

Step 8: Frost the Cake

Once the cakes have completely cooled, it’s time to frost them. Place one cake layer on a serving plate or cake stand. Use a spatula to spread a generous amount of frosting over the top, smoothing it evenly. Then, place the second cake layer on top and press down gently.

Frost the top and sides of the entire cake with the remaining frosting. For a fun touch, use extra sprinkles to decorate the top of the cake. You can also pipe some frosting onto the sides of the cake for a more decorative look. If you want to get even more creative, try adding some extra frosting swirls or patterns.

Step 9: Serve and Enjoy

Once the cake is frosted and decorated, it’s time to serve it! Slice it up and enjoy the moist, flavorful layers with the colorful pop of sprinkles in every bite. Whether you’re serving it for a special occasion or simply enjoying a treat with family and friends, this homemade Funfetti cake is sure to be a hit!

Tips for Success

  • Room temperature ingredients: For the best results, make sure your butter, eggs, and milk are at room temperature. This helps the ingredients blend more easily and ensures a smooth batter.
  • Don’t overmix the batter: Overmixing can result in a dense cake, so mix the batter just until the ingredients are combined.
  • Use quality sprinkles: Some sprinkles tend to bleed their color into the cake batter, creating a marbled effect. To avoid this, choose high-quality, colorfast sprinkles designed specifically for baking.
  • Chill the frosting: If you find that the frosting is too soft or difficult to work with, place it in the fridge for 10-15 minutes to firm it up slightly.
  • Decorating tips: If you’re not sure how to decorate the cake, consider using a piping bag to create rosettes, swirls, or other patterns on the top. A simple sprinkle of extra rainbow sprinkles is an easy and fun way to finish the cake.

Variations to Try

While the classic Funfetti cake is a crowd-pleaser, you can easily customize it to suit your preferences or dietary needs. Here are a few variations to try:

  • Chocolate Funfetti Cake: For a chocolate twist, substitute half of the all-purpose flour with unsweetened cocoa powder. This will give the cake a rich, chocolaty flavor that pairs wonderfully with the rainbow sprinkles.
  • Gluten-Free Funfetti Cake: If you’re looking to make a gluten-free version, replace the all-purpose flour with a gluten-free flour blend. Be sure to check that the sprinkles you use are also gluten-free.
  • Vegan Funfetti Cake: To make a vegan Funfetti cake, substitute the eggs with flaxseed eggs (1 tablespoon ground flaxseed + 3 tablespoons water per egg) and use plant-based butter and milk. Ensure the sprinkles are vegan-friendly as well.

Conclusion

Making a Funfetti cake from scratch is an enjoyable and rewarding experience, and the result is a moist, colorful cake that’s perfect for any celebration. With just a few basic ingredients and a little bit of patience, you can create a homemade Funfetti cake that’s not only delicious but also a visual treat.

Whether you’re baking for a birthday, a special occasion, or simply indulging your sweet tooth, this Funfetti cake recipe is sure to become a favorite. So, gather your ingredients, put on your apron, and get ready to bake a cake that will bring joy to everyone who tastes it! Happy baking!
