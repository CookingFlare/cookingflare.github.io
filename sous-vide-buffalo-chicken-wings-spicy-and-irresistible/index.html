Sous Vide Buffalo Chicken Wings: Spicy and Irresistible


When it comes to chicken wings, few recipes are as beloved as the classic buffalo wings. The perfect combination of spicy, tangy, and savory flavors makes them a favorite at parties, game nights, and casual gatherings. While the traditional method of deep-frying chicken wings is undeniably delicious, there’s a game-changing technique that takes these wings to a whole new level of succulence and flavor – sous vide. In this recipe, we’ll explore how to create the most mouthwatering sous vide buffalo chicken wings that are sure to be a hit at any event.

Ingredients:

  • 2 pounds of chicken wings
  • 1/4 cup hot sauce
  • 3 tablespoons unsalted butter
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1 teaspoon cayenne pepper
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 tablespoon honey
  • 1 tablespoon apple cider vinegar
  • Ranch or blue cheese dressing for serving
  • Celery sticks for serving

Instructions:

Step 1: Preparing the Sous Vide Bath

  • Preheat the water bath to 150°F (65.5°C).

Step 2: Preparing the Chicken Wings

  1. Pat the chicken wings dry with paper towels and season them with salt and black pepper.
  2. Place the seasoned chicken wings in a single layer in a large resealable bag.

Step 3: Sous Vide Cooking

  1. Once the water bath reaches the desired temperature, carefully lower the bag of chicken wings into the water, ensuring they are fully submerged.
  2. Cook the chicken wings in the sous vide bath for 2 hours to achieve tender, juicy results.

Step 4: Making the Buffalo Sauce

  1. In a saucepan over medium heat, melt the butter and add the minced garlic, cooking until fragrant.
  2. Stir in the hot sauce, paprika, cayenne pepper, honey, and apple cider vinegar, allowing the flavors to meld together for a few minutes.

Step 5: Finishing the Chicken Wings

  1. After the sous vide cooking is complete, remove the chicken wings from the bag and pat them dry with paper towels.
  2. Preheat the broiler in your oven.
  3. Toss the chicken wings in the prepared buffalo sauce until they are evenly coated.
  4. Place the wings on a broiler pan or wire rack set over a baking sheet and broil for 3-5 minutes, until they are crispy and caramelized.

Step 6: Serving

  • Serve the sous vide buffalo chicken wings hot, alongside ranch or blue cheese dressing and celery sticks for a classic accompaniment.

Conclusion

The sous vide method elevates the humble buffalo chicken wings to new heights, producing tender, succulent meat that’s infused with the flavors of the buffalo sauce. Whether you’re hosting a party or simply craving a delicious snack, these sous vide buffalo chicken wings are sure to be a crowd-pleaser. The convenience of sous vide cooking, combined with the irresistible taste of buffalo wings, makes this recipe a must-try for any culinary enthusiast. So, fire up your sous vide machine, gather your ingredients, and get ready to savor the spicy, irresistible goodness of these sous vide buffalo chicken wings!
