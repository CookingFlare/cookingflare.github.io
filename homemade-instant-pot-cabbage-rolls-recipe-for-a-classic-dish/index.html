Homemade Instant Pot Cabbage Rolls Recipe for a Classic Dish


Cabbage rolls, a traditional Eastern European dish, are a staple in many households. The combination of tender cabbage leaves wrapped around a flavorful filling, slow-cooked in a rich tomato-based sauce, is a true delight. In this recipe, we’ll show you how to make homemade cabbage rolls using an Instant Pot, making it easier and faster than ever before.

Ingredients:

  • 1 medium-sized head of cabbage
  • 1 pound ground pork
  • 1 medium onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 cup cooked rice
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1 teaspoon caraway seeds
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1 can (28 oz) crushed tomatoes
  • 1 cup chicken broth
  • 1 teaspoon tomato paste
  • Fresh parsley, chopped (optional)

Instructions:

Step 1: Prepare the Cabbage

Begin by removing the tough outer leaves from the cabbage head. Blanch the cabbage in boiling water for 5-7 minutes, or until the leaves are soft and pliable. Remove the cabbage from the water and let it cool. Once cooled, carefully remove the leaves from the head, taking care not to tear them.

Step 2: Prepare the Filling

In a large mixing bowl, combine the ground pork, chopped onion, minced garlic, cooked rice, paprika, caraway seeds, salt, and pepper. Mix well with your hands or a wooden spoon until the ingredients are evenly distributed.

Step 3: Assemble the Cabbage Rolls

To assemble the cabbage rolls, lay a cabbage leaf flat on a work surface. Place about 1/4 cup of the filling in the center of the leaf. Fold the stem end of the leaf over the filling, then fold in the sides and roll the leaf into a neat package. Repeat with the remaining cabbage leaves and filling.

Step 4: Cook the Cabbage Rolls in the Instant Pot

Add the vegetable oil to the Instant Pot and press the “Saute” button. Once the oil is hot, add the cabbage rolls seam-side down in a single layer. Cook for 2-3 minutes on each side, or until the cabbage is lightly browned. Remove the cabbage rolls from the pot and set them aside.

Step 5: Cook the Tomato-Based Sauce

Add the crushed tomatoes, chicken broth, and tomato paste to the Instant Pot. Stir to combine and bring the mixture to a simmer. Close the lid of the Instant Pot and set the valve to “Sealing”. Press the “Manual” or “Pressure Cook” button and set the cooking time to 10 minutes at high pressure.

Step 6: Finish the Dish

After the cooking time has elapsed, allow the pressure to release naturally for 10 minutes, then quick-release any remaining pressure. Open the lid and add the cooked cabbage rolls to the tomato-based sauce. Simmer the dish for an additional 10-15 minutes, or until the cabbage rolls are fully coated in the sauce and the flavors have melded together.

Step 7: Serve and Enjoy

Serve the homemade cabbage rolls hot, garnished with chopped fresh parsley if desired. Traditionally, cabbage rolls are served with a side of boiled potatoes, rye bread, or egg noodles.

Tips and Variations:

  • To make the dish more substantial, add some cooked sausage or bacon to the filling.
  • For a vegetarian option, substitute the ground pork with cooked mushrooms or lentils.
  • Experiment with different spices and herbs to give the dish your own unique flavor.
  • Cabbage rolls can be made ahead of time and refrigerated or frozen for later use.

With this recipe, you’ll be able to enjoy the classic flavors of homemade cabbage rolls without the hassle of traditional slow-cooking methods. The Instant Pot makes it easy to cook the dish quickly and efficiently, ensuring that the cabbage rolls are tender and flavorful. So go ahead, give this recipe a try, and experience the delight of homemade cabbage rolls in your own kitchen!
