Delicious Instant Pot Chicken Enchiladas Recipe for Southwestern Flavor


Looking for a delicious and easy dinner recipe that will transport you to the flavors of the Southwest? Look no further than this mouthwatering Instant Pot Chicken Enchiladas recipe. Packed with bold flavors and cooked to perfection in your trusty Instant Pot, these enchiladas will have your taste buds dancing with joy. So, put on your apron and let’s get cooking!

Ingredients

  • 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 small onion, diced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon chili powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1 cup chicken broth
  • 1 can (10 ounces) red enchilada sauce
  • 1 can (4 ounces) diced green chilies
  • 8 flour tortillas
  • 2 cups shredded Mexican blend cheese
  • Optional toppings: diced tomatoes, sliced avocado, chopped cilantro, sour cream

Instructions

  1. Start by setting your Instant Pot to the “Saute” function. Add the olive oil and heat it up.
  2. Add the diced onion and minced garlic to the pot and saute until the onion is translucent and fragrant.
  3. In a small bowl, combine the ground cumin, chili powder, dried oregano, salt, and black pepper. Mix well.
  4. Season the chicken breasts with half of the spice mixture, making sure to coat both sides.
  5. Add the chicken breasts to the Instant Pot and cook for 2-3 minutes on each side, or until they are browned.
  6. Pour the chicken broth, red enchilada sauce, and diced green chilies into the pot, along with the remaining spice mixture. Stir well to combine.
  7. Secure the lid on the Instant Pot and set the valve to the sealing position. Select the “Manual” or “Pressure Cook” function and set the timer for 10 minutes.
  8. Once the cooking time is complete, allow for a natural pressure release for 5 minutes, then carefully perform a quick release to release any remaining pressure.
  9. Remove the chicken breasts from the pot and shred them using two forks.
  10. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C).
  11. Take a tortilla and spoon some of the enchilada sauce mixture onto it. Add a portion of the shredded chicken and a sprinkle of shredded cheese. Roll the tortilla tightly and place it seam-side down in a baking dish. Repeat with the remaining tortillas.
  12. Pour any remaining enchilada sauce over the rolled tortillas and sprinkle with the remaining shredded cheese.
  13. Cover the baking dish with aluminum foil and bake in the preheated oven for 20 minutes.
  14. After 20 minutes, remove the foil and bake for an additional 5-10 minutes, or until the cheese is melted and bubbly.
  15. Remove the enchiladas from the oven and let them cool for a few minutes before serving.
  16. Serve the Instant Pot Chicken Enchiladas with your favorite toppings, such as diced tomatoes, sliced avocado, chopped cilantro, and a dollop of sour cream.

Enjoy the Flavors of the Southwest

There you have it, a delicious Instant Pot Chicken Enchiladas recipe that is sure to satisfy your cravings for Southwestern flavors. With its tender shredded chicken, flavorful sauce, and gooey melted cheese, these enchiladas are a crowd-pleaser that will have everyone coming back for seconds. So, fire up your Instant Pot and get ready to indulge in this irresistible dish. Happy cooking!
