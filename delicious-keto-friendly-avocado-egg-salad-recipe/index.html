Delicious Keto-Friendly Avocado Egg Salad Recipe


Are you looking for a delicious yet healthy dish to add to your keto recipe collection? Look no further! This avocado egg salad is a perfect blend of creamy avocado, protein-packed eggs, and zesty flavors that will tantalize your taste buds while keeping you on track with your keto lifestyle.

Ingredients

  • 4 hard-boiled eggs, diced
  • 2 ripe avocados, peeled and pitted
  • 1/4 cup mayonnaise
  • 1 tablespoon Dijon mustard
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 1/4 cup red onion, finely chopped
  • 2 tablespoons fresh cilantro, chopped
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. In a large bowl, mash the ripe avocados until smooth.
  2. Add the mayonnaise, Dijon mustard, and lemon juice to the bowl with the mashed avocados. Mix well until all the ingredients are combined.
  3. Gently fold in the diced hard-boiled eggs, red onion, and fresh cilantro into the avocado mixture.
  4. Season the salad with salt and pepper according to your taste preferences. Mix gently to ensure the seasoning is evenly distributed.
  5. Once mixed, cover the bowl with plastic wrap and refrigerate for at least 30 minutes to allow the flavors to meld together.

Serving Suggestions

This keto-friendly avocado egg salad is versatile and can be served in various ways:

  • Enjoy it on its own as a light and satisfying lunch.
  • Serve it on a bed of fresh lettuce for a refreshing salad.
  • Spread it on low-carb bread for a delicious avocado egg salad sandwich.
  • Use it as a flavorful topping for cucumber slices or keto-friendly crackers.

Nutritional Information

This avocado egg salad not only tastes amazing but also offers a nutritional punch. Here’s a breakdown of the key nutritional components per serving:

  • Calories: 235
  • Total Fat: 20g
  • Saturated Fat: 4g
  • Cholesterol: 186mg
  • Sodium: 207mg
  • Total Carbohydrates: 7g
  • Dietary Fiber: 5g
  • Sugars: 1g
  • Protein: 8g

Benefits of Avocado Egg Salad on a Keto Diet

Avocado and eggs are both superstar ingredients for those following a keto diet:

  • Healthy Fats: Avocados are rich in heart-healthy monounsaturated fats, which are an essential component of a keto diet. These fats help keep you feeling full and satisfied, making it easier to stick to your dietary goals.

  • Protein Powerhouse: Eggs are a fantastic source of high-quality protein, making them a perfect addition to a keto meal. Protein is essential for muscle repair and growth, and it also helps keep you feeling full for longer periods.

  • Low in Carbs: With just 7g of total carbohydrates and 5g of dietary fiber per serving, this avocado egg salad is a low-carb option that won’t derail your keto efforts.

  • Nutrient-Rich: Avocados are packed with vitamins and minerals, including potassium, vitamin K, and folate. Eggs are also rich in essential nutrients like vitamin D and B vitamins, making this salad a nutrient-dense choice for your keto meal plan.

Final Thoughts

This delicious keto-friendly avocado egg salad is not only a feast for the taste buds but also a nutritious addition to your keto lifestyle. Whether you’re following a keto diet or simply looking for a flavorful and healthy dish, this recipe is sure to become a favorite in your meal rotation. Try it out and savor the creamy, zesty goodness of this delightful avocado egg salad!
