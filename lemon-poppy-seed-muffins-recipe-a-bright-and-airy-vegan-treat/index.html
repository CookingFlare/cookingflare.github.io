Lemon Poppy Seed Muffins Recipe: A Bright and Airy Vegan Treat


Are you on the lookout for a delightful and zesty treat that’s not only vegan but also bursting with flavor? Look no further than these Lemon Poppy Seed Muffins! This recipe is a perfect combination of brightness and airiness, making it a delightful addition to any brunch, snack, or dessert spread. The tangy lemon flavor paired with the subtle crunch of poppy seeds creates a symphony of tastes and textures that will leave you craving for more. Best of all, these muffins are completely vegan, so you can indulge guilt-free. Let’s dive into the recipe and discover how to create this delightful treat in your own kitchen.

Ingredients

Dry Ingredients:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 2 tbsp poppy seeds
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp baking soda
  • 1/4 tsp salt

Wet Ingredients:

  • 1 cup non-dairy milk (such as almond milk or oat milk)
  • 1/2 cup vegetable oil
  • 1/4 cup fresh lemon juice
  • Zest of 2 lemons
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract

Instructions

  1. Preparation: Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C) and line a muffin tin with paper liners or lightly grease the tin.

  2. Mixing the Dry Ingredients: In a large bowl, whisk together the all-purpose flour, granulated sugar, poppy seeds, baking powder, baking soda, and salt until well combined.

  3. Mixing the Wet Ingredients: In a separate bowl, combine the non-dairy milk, vegetable oil, fresh lemon juice, lemon zest, and vanilla extract. Whisk the wet ingredients together until they are thoroughly combined.

  4. Combining the Ingredients: Make a well in the center of the dry ingredients and pour the wet ingredients into the well. Gently fold the dry and wet ingredients together until just combined. Be careful not to overmix, as this can result in dense muffins.

  5. Filling the Muffin Tin: Divide the batter evenly among the prepared muffin cups, filling each cup about two-thirds full.

  6. Baking: Place the muffin tin in the preheated oven and bake for 18-20 minutes, or until the muffins are golden brown and a toothpick inserted into the center of a muffin comes out clean.

  7. Cooling: Once baked, remove the muffin tin from the oven and allow the muffins to cool in the tin for 5 minutes. Then transfer the muffins to a wire rack to cool completely.

  8. Enjoying: These Lemon Poppy Seed Muffins are best enjoyed fresh, but they can also be stored in an airtight container at room temperature for up to 3 days.

Serving Suggestions

These delightful Lemon Poppy Seed Muffins are perfect for sharing with friends and family over a cup of tea or coffee. Their bright and tangy flavor makes them an ideal addition to any brunch or as a light and refreshing dessert. You can also elevate these muffins by serving them with a dollop of dairy-free yogurt and a sprinkle of additional lemon zest for an extra burst of citrus flavor.

Final Thoughts

With their vibrant lemon flavor and delicate texture, these Lemon Poppy Seed Muffins are a true delight for the taste buds. Whether you’re a seasoned vegan baker or just starting to explore plant-based baking, this recipe is sure to become a favorite in your collection. So, gather your ingredients, preheat your oven, and get ready to savor the bright and airy goodness of these vegan treats. Happy baking!
