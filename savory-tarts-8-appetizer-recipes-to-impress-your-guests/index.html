Savory Tarts: 8 Appetizer Recipes to Impress Your Guests


Savory tarts are a versatile and delicious way to add flavor and elegance to any gathering. Whether you’re hosting a dinner party, a wine tasting, or a casual get-together with friends, these 8 appetizer recipes are sure to impress. From classic combinations to innovative twists, we’ll explore the world of savory tarts and provide you with the perfect inspiration for your next culinary endeavor.

Recipe 1: Spinach, Feta, and Sun-dried Tomato Tart

  • 1 sheet puff pastry, thawed
  • 1/2 cup fresh spinach leaves
  • 1/2 cup crumbled feta cheese
  • 1/4 cup sun-dried tomatoes, chopped
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 egg, beaten (for egg wash)

Preheat oven to 400°F (200°C). Roll out puff pastry to a thickness of about 1/8 inch. In a small bowl, mix together spinach, feta, and sun-dried tomatoes. Spread the mixture evenly over the center of the pastry, leaving a 1-inch border around the edges. Fold the edges up over the filling, pressing gently to seal. Brush the egg wash over the pastry and bake for 25-30 minutes, or until golden brown.

Recipe 2: Caramelized Onion and Goat Cheese Tart

  • 1 sheet puff pastry, thawed
  • 1 large onion, thinly sliced
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1/4 cup goat cheese, crumbled
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh thyme
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 egg, beaten (for egg wash)

Preheat oven to 400°F (200°C). Roll out puff pastry to a thickness of about 1/8 inch. In a large skillet, cook the onions over medium heat for 20-25 minutes, stirring occasionally, until caramelized. Spread the onions evenly over the center of the pastry, leaving a 1-inch border around the edges. Top with goat cheese and thyme. Fold the edges up over the filling, pressing gently to seal. Brush the egg wash over the pastry and bake for 25-30 minutes, or until golden brown.

Recipe 3: Mushroom and Leek Tart

  • 1 sheet puff pastry, thawed
  • 1 cup mixed mushrooms (such as cremini, shiitake, and button), sliced
  • 2 medium leeks, thinly sliced
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 1/4 cup grated cheddar cheese
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 egg, beaten (for egg wash)

Preheat oven to 400°F (200°C). Roll out puff pastry to a thickness of about 1/8 inch. In a large skillet, cook the mushrooms and leeks over medium heat for 10-12 minutes, stirring occasionally, until tender. Spread the mixture evenly over the center of the pastry, leaving a 1-inch border around the edges. Top with cheddar cheese. Fold the edges up over the filling, pressing gently to seal. Brush the egg wash over the pastry and bake for 25-30 minutes, or until golden brown.

Recipe 4: Roasted Garlic and Asparagus Tart

  • 1 sheet puff pastry, thawed
  • 2 heads roasted garlic, mashed
  • 1 pound fresh asparagus, trimmed
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 egg, beaten (for egg wash)

Preheat oven to 400°F (200°C). Roll out puff pastry to a thickness of about 1/8 inch. Spread the roasted garlic evenly over the center of the pastry, leaving a 1-inch border around the edges. Top with asparagus. Fold the edges up over the filling, pressing gently to seal. Brush the egg wash over the pastry and bake for 25-30 minutes, or until golden brown.

Recipe 5: Prosciutto and Arugula Tart

  • 1 sheet puff pastry, thawed
  • 4 slices prosciutto, thinly sliced
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh arugula
  • 1/4 cup shaved Parmesan cheese
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 egg, beaten (for egg wash)

Preheat oven to 400°F (200°C). Roll out puff pastry to a thickness of about 1/8 inch. Arrange the prosciutto slices evenly over the center of the pastry, leaving a 1-inch border around the edges. Top with arugula and Parmesan cheese. Fold the edges up over the filling, pressing gently to seal. Brush the egg wash over the pastry and bake for 25-30 minutes, or until golden brown.

Recipe 6: Sweet Potato and Sage Tart

  • 1 sheet puff pastry, thawed
  • 2 large sweet potatoes, cooked and mashed
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons chopped fresh sage
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 egg, beaten (for egg wash)

Preheat oven to 400°F (200°C). Roll out puff pastry to a thickness of about 1/8 inch. Spread the mashed sweet potatoes evenly over the center of the pastry, leaving a 1-inch border around the edges. Top with sage. Fold the edges up over the filling, pressing gently to seal. Brush the egg wash over the pastry and bake for 25-30 minutes, or until golden brown.

Recipe 7: Bacon and Chive Tart

  • 1 sheet puff pastry, thawed
  • 4 slices bacon, cooked and crumbled
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh chives
  • 1/4 cup grated cheddar cheese
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 egg, beaten (for egg wash)

Preheat oven to 400°F (200°C). Roll out puff pastry to a thickness of about 1/8 inch. Spread the crumbled bacon evenly over the center of the pastry, leaving a 1-inch border around the edges. Top with chives and cheddar cheese. Fold the edges up over the filling, pressing gently to seal. Brush the egg wash over the pastry and bake for 25-30 minutes, or until golden brown.

Recipe 8: Artichoke and Spinach Tart

  • 1 sheet puff pastry, thawed
  • 1 (14 oz) can artichoke hearts, drained and chopped
  • 1/2 cup fresh spinach leaves
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 egg, beaten (for egg wash)

Preheat oven to 400°F (200°C). Roll out puff pastry to a thickness of about 1/8 inch. Spread the chopped artichoke hearts evenly over the center of the pastry, leaving a 1-inch border around the edges. Top with spinach and Parmesan cheese. Fold the edges up over the filling, pressing gently to seal. Brush the egg wash over the pastry and bake for 25-30 minutes, or until golden brown.
