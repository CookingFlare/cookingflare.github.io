The Ultimate Guide to Cooking with Peas: 7 Spring Recipes


Spring is the perfect time to celebrate the fresh flavors of peas. Whether you prefer them in salads, soups, or as a standalone side, peas are versatile and nutritious. In this ultimate guide, we’ll explore seven delightful recipes that showcase the beauty of peas in springtime cooking.

1. Pea and Mint Soup

Prep Time: 10 minutes | Cook Time: 30 minutes | Total Time: 40 minutes

This vibrant green soup is a celebration of spring. Start by sautéing onions and garlic until soft, then add fresh or frozen peas, vegetable broth, and a handful of mint leaves. Simmer until the peas are tender, then blend until smooth. Finish with a swirl of cream and a sprinkle of freshly ground black pepper.

2. Spring Pea Risotto

Prep Time: 15 minutes | Cook Time: 25 minutes | Total Time: 40 minutes

Risotto is a comforting dish that welcomes the addition of sweet peas. Begin by sautéing arborio rice in butter until translucent, then gradually add warm chicken or vegetable broth, stirring constantly. When the rice is almost tender, fold in a generous amount of fresh or thawed peas and a handful of grated Parmesan cheese. The result is a creamy, luxurious dish that captures the essence of spring.

3. Pea and Asparagus Salad

Prep Time: 15 minutes | Cook Time: 5 minutes | Total Time: 20 minutes

This light and refreshing salad is a celebration of seasonal produce. Blanch fresh asparagus briefly in boiling water, then plunge into an ice bath to preserve its vibrant color. Toss the asparagus with blanched peas, a zesty vinaigrette, and a sprinkle of crumbled feta cheese. The combination of tender asparagus and sweet peas is a match made in springtime heaven.

4. Pea and Pancetta Pasta

Prep Time: 10 minutes | Cook Time: 20 minutes | Total Time: 30 minutes

This quick and flavorful pasta dish is perfect for a busy weeknight. Sauté diced pancetta until crispy, then add a generous amount of fresh or frozen peas and a splash of chicken broth. Toss with cooked pasta, a squeeze of lemon juice, and a handful of freshly grated Parmesan cheese. The result is a satisfying and comforting meal that sings with the flavors of spring.

5. Minted Pea Puree

Prep Time: 5 minutes | Cook Time: 10 minutes | Total Time: 15 minutes

This simple yet elegant puree is the perfect accompaniment to grilled lamb or roasted chicken. Start by blanching fresh peas until tender, then blend with a handful of fresh mint leaves, a drizzle of olive oil, and a squeeze of lemon juice. Season to taste with salt and pepper, and serve alongside your favorite protein for a taste of spring on a plate.

6. Pea and Potato Curry

Prep Time: 15 minutes | Cook Time: 30 minutes | Total Time: 45 minutes

This fragrant and comforting curry is a wonderful way to showcase the humble pea. Sauté onions, garlic, and ginger until fragrant, then add diced potatoes, peas, and a blend of aromatic spices. Simmer until the potatoes are tender and the flavors have melded together. Serve with fluffy basmati rice for a satisfying and wholesome meal.

7. Pea and Ricotta Tart

Prep Time: 20 minutes | Cook Time: 40 minutes | Total Time: 1 hour

This show-stopping tart is a beautiful centerpiece for any spring gathering. Start by making a buttery tart crust, then fill with a creamy mixture of ricotta, eggs, and blanched peas. Bake until the filling is set and the crust is golden, then finish with a sprinkle of fresh herbs and a drizzle of extra virgin olive oil. This tart is a delightful way to showcase the delicate sweetness of peas in a stunning savory creation.

In conclusion, peas are a versatile and delightful ingredient that shines in springtime cooking. Whether you prefer them in soups, salads, or savory tarts, these recipes are a celebration of the fresh and vibrant flavors of peas. Embrace the bounty of spring and savor the deliciousness of peas in these seven delightful recipes.
