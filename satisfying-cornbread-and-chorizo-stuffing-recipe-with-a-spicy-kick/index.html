Satisfying Cornbread and Chorizo Stuffing Recipe with a Spicy Kick


Are you ready to take your stuffing game to the next level? Look no further than this mouthwatering Cornbread and Chorizo Stuffing recipe. This dish is packed with flavor, featuring the perfect balance of savory cornbread, spicy chorizo, and a blend of aromatic herbs and spices. Whether you’re preparing for a festive gathering or simply craving a comforting meal, this recipe is sure to impress your taste buds and leave you craving more.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb chorizo, casing removed
  • 1 cup unsalted butter
  • 1 large onion, finely chopped
  • 4 stalks celery, diced
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 6 cups crumbled cornbread
  • 1 tablespoon fresh sage, chopped
  • 1 tablespoon fresh thyme leaves
  • 1 teaspoon smoked paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon cayenne pepper
  • 1/2 cup chicken or vegetable broth
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C) and grease a 9x13-inch baking dish.

  2. In a large skillet over medium heat, cook the chorizo, breaking it up with a spoon, until it’s browned and cooked through. Transfer the chorizo to a plate lined with paper towels to drain any excess fat.

  3. In the same skillet, melt the butter over medium heat. Add the chopped onion, celery, and garlic, and sauté until the vegetables are soft and fragrant, about 5-7 minutes.

  4. In a large mixing bowl, combine the crumbled cornbread, cooked chorizo, sautéed vegetables, chopped sage, thyme, smoked paprika, and cayenne pepper. Toss everything together until well combined.

  5. Gradually pour the chicken or vegetable broth over the mixture, stirring gently to moisten the ingredients. Add more broth if you prefer a softer, moister stuffing.

  6. Season the mixture with salt and pepper to taste, keeping in mind that the chorizo already adds some saltiness to the dish.

  7. Transfer the stuffing mixture to the prepared baking dish, spreading it out evenly.

  8. Cover the dish with foil and bake for 25 minutes. Then, remove the foil and bake for an additional 10-15 minutes, or until the top is golden brown and slightly crispy.

  9. Once done, remove the stuffing from the oven and let it rest for a few minutes before serving.

Serving Suggestions

This Cornbread and Chorizo Stuffing pairs beautifully with a variety of main dishes. Serve it alongside roasted turkey, grilled chicken, or even as a standalone star of your meal. For an extra kick, consider drizzling it with a zesty chipotle aioli or topping it with pickled jalapeños for a burst of tangy heat.

Conclusion

With its robust flavors and satisfying texture, this Cornbread and Chorizo Stuffing is a delightful departure from the traditional stuffing recipes. The combination of hearty cornbread, spicy chorizo, and aromatic herbs creates a dish that will leave your guests coming back for seconds. Whether you’re hosting a holiday feast or simply want to elevate your weeknight dinner, this recipe is guaranteed to add a spicy kick to your table. So, roll up your sleeves, gather your ingredients, and get ready to savor every delicious bite. Happy cooking!
