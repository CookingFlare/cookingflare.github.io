How to Make Classic Chicken and Waffles Like a Pro


Chicken and waffles is a classic dish that combines the savory goodness of fried chicken with the sweet indulgence of waffles. It’s a perfect balance of flavors and textures that will leave you craving for more. In this recipe, we will show you how to make classic chicken and waffles like a pro right in your own kitchen.

Ingredients

For the Chicken:

  • 2 pounds of chicken wings or drumsticks
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 tablespoon paprika
  • 1 tablespoon garlic powder
  • 1 tablespoon onion powder
  • Vegetable oil for frying

For the Waffles:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 2 cups milk
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1/4 cup unsalted butter, melted
  • Non-stick cooking spray

Instructions

Step 1: Prepare the Chicken

  1. Start by seasoning the chicken pieces with salt and pepper. This will help to enhance the flavor of the meat.

  2. In a shallow dish, combine the flour, paprika, garlic powder, and onion powder. Mix well.

  3. Heat vegetable oil in a large skillet or deep fryer to a temperature of 350°F (175°C).

  4. Dredge each piece of chicken in the flour mixture, making sure to coat it evenly. Shake off any excess flour.

  5. Carefully place the chicken in the hot oil, making sure not to overcrowd the pan. Fry the chicken for about 10-12 minutes, or until golden brown and cooked through. Flip the chicken halfway through to ensure even cooking.

  6. Once cooked, transfer the chicken to a paper towel-lined plate to drain any excess oil.

Step 2: Make the Waffles

  1. In a large mixing bowl, whisk together the flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt.

  2. In a separate bowl, whisk together the milk, eggs, and melted butter.

  3. Pour the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients and stir until just combined. Be careful not to overmix, as this can result in dense waffles.

  4. Preheat your waffle iron according to the manufacturer’s instructions.

  5. Lightly spray the waffle iron with non-stick cooking spray.

  6. Pour the batter onto the preheated waffle iron, using the recommended amount for your specific waffle iron. Close the lid and cook until the waffles are golden brown and crispy.

  7. Remove the waffles from the iron and repeat the process until all the batter is used.

Step 3: Serve and Enjoy

  1. To serve, place a waffle on a plate and top it with a piece of fried chicken.

  2. Drizzle your favorite syrup over the chicken and waffle stack. Maple syrup is a classic choice, but you can also get creative with flavored syrups or even hot sauce for a spicy twist.

  3. Garnish with fresh herbs, such as parsley or chives, to add a pop of color and freshness.

  4. Serve immediately and enjoy the perfect combination of savory and sweet flavors in every bite.

Conclusion

Now that you know how to make classic chicken and waffles like a pro, it’s time to put your skills to the test. This dish is perfect for brunch, lunch, or dinner and is sure to impress your family and friends. So grab your apron, fire up the stove, and get ready to indulge in this delicious comfort food. Happy cooking!
