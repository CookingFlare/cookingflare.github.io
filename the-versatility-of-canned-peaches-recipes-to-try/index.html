The Versatility of Canned Peaches: Recipes to Try


Canned peaches are a versatile ingredient that can be used in a variety of dishes, from sweet to savory. Whether you’re looking to whip up a quick dessert or add a touch of sweetness to your main course, canned peaches are a pantry staple that shouldn’t be overlooked. In this blog post, we’ll explore some delicious recipes that showcase the versatility of canned peaches. So, grab a can of peaches and let’s get cooking!

1. Peach Cobbler Delight

Peach cobbler is a classic dessert that never fails to impress. With canned peaches, you can enjoy this delicious treat any time of the year. Here’s a simple recipe to try:

Ingredients:

  • 2 cans of sliced peaches in syrup
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, melted
  • 1 tablespoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • Ground cinnamon (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. In a baking dish, combine the flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt.
  3. Add the milk, melted butter, and vanilla extract to the dry ingredients. Stir until well combined.
  4. Drain the canned peaches and arrange them evenly over the batter in the baking dish.
  5. Sprinkle some ground cinnamon over the peaches, if desired.
  6. Bake for about 40-45 minutes, or until the cobbler is golden brown and bubbling.
  7. Serve warm with a scoop of vanilla ice cream or a dollop of whipped cream.

2. Grilled Peach and Chicken Salad

Peaches can also add a delightful sweetness to savory dishes. This grilled peach and chicken salad is a perfect example:

Ingredients:

  • 2 cans of peach halves in juice
  • 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 4 cups mixed salad greens
  • 1/2 cup crumbled feta cheese
  • 1/4 cup sliced red onions
  • 1/4 cup chopped pecans
  • Balsamic vinaigrette dressing

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your grill to medium-high heat.
  2. Drain the canned peach halves and pat them dry with a paper towel.
  3. Grill the peach halves for about 2-3 minutes on each side, or until grill marks appear. Set aside.
  4. Season the chicken breasts with salt and pepper, then grill for about 6-8 minutes per side, or until cooked through. Let them rest for a few minutes, then slice.
  5. In a large salad bowl, combine the mixed greens, feta cheese, sliced red onions, and chopped pecans.
  6. Add the grilled peach halves and sliced chicken to the salad.
  7. Drizzle with balsamic vinaigrette dressing and toss gently to combine.

3. Peach Glazed Pork Chops

For a mouthwatering main course, try these succulent peach glazed pork chops:

Ingredients:

  • 4 bone-in pork chops
  • 2 cans of peach slices in juice
  • 1/2 cup brown sugar
  • 1/4 cup Dijon mustard
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 teaspoon minced garlic
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Season the pork chops with salt and pepper, then place them in a greased baking dish.
  3. In a saucepan, combine the peach slices (with the juice), brown sugar, Dijon mustard, soy sauce, and minced garlic.
  4. Cook over medium heat until the mixture thickens slightly, about 10 minutes.
  5. Pour the peach glaze over the pork chops, ensuring they are evenly coated.
  6. Bake for about 25-30 minutes, or until the pork chops are cooked through and the glaze is caramelized.
  7. Serve with your favorite side dishes, such as roasted vegetables or mashed potatoes.

Conclusion

Canned peaches are a versatile ingredient that can elevate both sweet and savory dishes. Whether you’re craving a comforting dessert or a flavorful main course, these recipes showcase the endless possibilities of canned peaches. So, the next time you’re in the mood for something delicious, reach for a can of peaches and let your creativity shine in the kitchen. Enjoy!
