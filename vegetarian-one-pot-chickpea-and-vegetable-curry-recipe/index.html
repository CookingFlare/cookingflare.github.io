Vegetarian One Pot Chickpea and Vegetable Curry Recipe


Are you in the mood for a delicious and nutritious vegetarian dish that’s easy to make and packed with flavors? Look no further than this Vegetarian One Pot Chickpea and Vegetable Curry Recipe! This hearty curry is not only satisfying but also a great option for those looking to incorporate more plant-based meals into their diet.

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon of vegetable oil
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon of ginger, grated
  • 1 teaspoon of cumin powder
  • 1 teaspoon of coriander powder
  • 1 teaspoon of turmeric powder
  • 1 teaspoon of paprika
  • 1 teaspoon of garam masala
  • 1 cup of diced tomatoes
  • 1 cup of vegetable broth
  • 1 can of chickpeas, drained and rinsed
  • 2 cups of mixed vegetables (such as carrots, bell peppers, and peas)
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • Fresh cilantro, for garnish

Instructions

  1. Heat the vegetable oil in a large pot over medium heat. Add the diced onion and sauté until translucent.

  2. Add the minced garlic and grated ginger to the pot and cook for another minute, until fragrant.

  3. In a small bowl, combine the cumin powder, coriander powder, turmeric powder, paprika, and garam masala. Stir the spice mixture into the pot, coating the onion, garlic, and ginger.

  4. Add the diced tomatoes and vegetable broth to the pot, stirring well to combine. Bring the mixture to a simmer.

  5. Add the drained and rinsed chickpeas to the pot, followed by the mixed vegetables. Stir everything together, ensuring the vegetables are well-coated with the flavorful sauce.

  6. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Cover the pot and let the curry simmer for about 20-25 minutes, or until the vegetables are tender.

  7. Remove the pot from the heat and let it sit for a few minutes to allow the flavors to meld together. Serve the Vegetarian One Pot Chickpea and Vegetable Curry hot, garnished with fresh cilantro.

Health Benefits

This Vegetarian One Pot Chickpea and Vegetable Curry Recipe is not only delicious but also packed with health benefits. Chickpeas, the star ingredient of this dish, are an excellent source of plant-based protein and fiber, making them a great choice for vegetarians and vegans. They also contain important vitamins and minerals, such as iron, folate, and magnesium.

The mixed vegetables in this curry provide a variety of nutrients, including vitamins A, C, and K, as well as antioxidants. Plus, the combination of spices like cumin, coriander, turmeric, and garam masala adds not only incredible flavor but also potential health benefits. Turmeric, in particular, is known for its anti-inflammatory properties.

Final Thoughts

This Vegetarian One Pot Chickpea and Vegetable Curry Recipe is a must-try for anyone looking to enjoy a flavorful and nutritious meal. It’s a simple and convenient one-pot dish that can be customized with your favorite vegetables and spices. Whether you’re a vegetarian or simply want to incorporate more plant-based meals into your diet, this curry is sure to satisfy your taste buds and leave you feeling nourished. Give it a try and discover the joy of wholesome vegetarian cooking!
