Sous Vide Beef Stew: Hearty Comfort Food Perfected


There’s something undeniably comforting about a warm, hearty bowl of beef stew. The rich flavors, tender meat, and aromatic vegetables all come together to create a dish that warms the soul. Today, I want to share with you a recipe for Sous Vide Beef Stew, a technique that takes this classic comfort food to a whole new level.

The Magic of Sous Vide

Sous vide, which means “under vacuum” in French, is a cooking technique that involves sealing food in a vacuum-sealed bag and cooking it in a water bath at a precise temperature. This method ensures even and precise cooking, resulting in meat that is perfectly tender and flavorful.

Ingredients

  • 2 pounds of beef stew meat, cubed
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 2 carrots, sliced
  • 2 celery stalks, sliced
  • 3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 cup of red wine
  • 2 cups of beef broth
  • 2 tablespoons of tomato paste
  • 1 tablespoon of Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 teaspoon of dried thyme
  • 1 bay leaf
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Preheat your sous vide water bath to 135°F (57°C).

  2. Season the beef stew meat with salt and pepper. Place the seasoned meat in a vacuum-sealed bag, ensuring that it is in a single layer. Seal the bag using a vacuum sealer or the water displacement method.

  3. Once the water bath has reached the desired temperature, carefully place the sealed bag of beef into the water. Cook for 24 hours to achieve the perfect tenderness.

  4. While the beef is cooking, prepare the vegetables. In a large skillet, heat some olive oil over medium heat. Add the diced onion, sliced carrots, and celery. Sauté until the vegetables are softened and lightly browned.

  5. Add the minced garlic to the skillet and cook for another minute, until fragrant. Pour in the red wine, scraping the bottom of the skillet to release any browned bits.

  6. Stir in the tomato paste, Worcestershire sauce, dried thyme, and bay leaf. Cook for a few minutes to allow the flavors to meld together.

  7. Pour in the beef broth and bring the mixture to a simmer. Let it cook for about 10 minutes, allowing the flavors to develop.

  8. Once the beef has finished cooking in the sous vide bath, carefully remove the bag from the water. Open the bag and transfer the beef and any accumulated juices to the skillet with the vegetables and broth. Stir to combine.

  9. Simmer the stew over low heat for another 30 minutes to allow the flavors to meld together and the stew to thicken slightly.

  10. Season with additional salt and pepper to taste. Serve hot and enjoy the comforting goodness of your Sous Vide Beef Stew.

Conclusion

Sous Vide Beef Stew takes the classic comfort food to new heights, providing a tender and flavorful experience that will leave you craving more. With the precise cooking method of sous vide, you’ll achieve perfectly cooked beef that melts in your mouth. The combination of aromatic vegetables, red wine, and rich beef broth creates a depth of flavor that will warm your soul. So, gather your ingredients, fire up your sous vide machine, and get ready to savor the deliciousness of this hearty comfort food perfected.
