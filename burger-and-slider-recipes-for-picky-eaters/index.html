Burger and Slider Recipes for Picky Eaters


Are you looking for delicious burger and slider recipes that will satisfy even the pickiest eaters? Look no further! In this blog post, we will share some mouthwatering recipes that are sure to please even the most discerning palates. From classic beef burgers to unique vegetarian options, we’ve got you covered. So, let’s dive right in!

1. Classic Beef Burger

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1/4 cup breadcrumbs
  • 1/4 cup finely chopped onion
  • 1/4 cup ketchup
  • 1 tablespoon Worcestershire sauce
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Hamburger buns
  • Your favorite toppings (lettuce, tomato, cheese, etc.)

Instructions:

  1. In a large bowl, combine the ground beef, breadcrumbs, onion, ketchup, Worcestershire sauce, salt, and pepper.
  2. Mix until well combined, but be careful not to overmix as it can make the burgers tough.
  3. Divide the mixture into equal portions and shape them into patties.
  4. Preheat your grill or stovetop pan over medium-high heat.
  5. Cook the patties for about 4-5 minutes per side, or until they reach your desired level of doneness.
  6. Toast the burger buns, then assemble the burgers with your favorite toppings.
  7. Serve hot and enjoy!

2. Veggie Sliders

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup cooked quinoa
  • 1 cup black beans, rinsed and drained
  • 1/2 cup grated zucchini
  • 1/4 cup finely chopped onion
  • 1/4 cup breadcrumbs
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Slider buns
  • Toppings of your choice (avocado, salsa, etc.)

Instructions:

  1. In a large bowl, mash the black beans with a fork or potato masher until they are mostly mashed but still have some texture.
  2. Add the cooked quinoa, grated zucchini, onion, breadcrumbs, cumin, paprika, salt, and pepper to the bowl.
  3. Mix until well combined.
  4. Divide the mixture into small portions and shape them into slider-sized patties.
  5. Heat a non-stick pan over medium heat and lightly grease it with cooking spray.
  6. Cook the patties for about 3-4 minutes per side, or until they are golden brown and heated through.
  7. Toast the slider buns, then assemble the sliders with your favorite toppings.
  8. Serve hot and enjoy the delicious and healthy veggie sliders!

3. Chicken Parmesan Burger

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound ground chicken
  • 1/4 cup breadcrumbs
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/4 cup marinara sauce
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh basil
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Hamburger buns
  • Mozzarella cheese slices
  • Additional marinara sauce

Instructions:

  1. In a large bowl, combine the ground chicken, breadcrumbs, Parmesan cheese, marinara sauce, basil, garlic, salt, and pepper.
  2. Mix until well combined.
  3. Divide the mixture into equal portions and shape them into patties.
  4. Preheat your grill or stovetop pan over medium-high heat.
  5. Cook the patties for about 4-5 minutes per side, or until they are cooked through.
  6. During the last minute of cooking, place a slice of mozzarella cheese on top of each patty and allow it to melt.
  7. Toast the burger buns, then assemble the burgers with additional marinara sauce.
  8. Serve hot and enjoy the flavorful chicken Parmesan burgers!

Conclusion

Whether you’re a meat lover or prefer vegetarian options, these burger and slider recipes are sure to please even the pickiest eaters. From the classic beef burger to the healthy veggie sliders, and the flavorful chicken Parmesan burger, there’s something for everyone. So, get creative in the kitchen and indulge in these delicious recipes that are guaranteed to satisfy your taste buds. Happy cooking!
