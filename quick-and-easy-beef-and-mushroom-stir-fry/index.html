Quick and Easy Beef and Mushroom Stir Fry


If you’re in the mood for a delicious and satisfying meal that’s ready in a flash, then this Quick and Easy Beef and Mushroom Stir Fry is the perfect choice. Packed with savory flavors and wholesome ingredients, this recipe is a go-to option for busy weeknights or whenever you crave a fuss-free, homemade dinner.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound of flank steak, thinly sliced
  • 2 cups of sliced mushrooms (such as cremini or shiitake)
  • 1 bell pepper, thinly sliced
  • 3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon of grated fresh ginger
  • 1/4 cup of low-sodium soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons of oyster sauce
  • 1 tablespoon of brown sugar
  • 2 tablespoons of vegetable oil
  • 1 teaspoon of cornstarch
  • Cooked rice or noodles for serving
  • Optional garnish: sliced green onions, sesame seeds

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Ingredients: Start by slicing the flank steak thinly against the grain for a tender result. Slice the mushrooms, bell pepper, and mince the garlic. Grate the fresh ginger and set aside.

  2. Make the Stir Fry Sauce: In a small bowl, whisk together the low-sodium soy sauce, oyster sauce, brown sugar, and cornstarch. Ensure that the cornstarch is fully dissolved to avoid lumps in the sauce.

  3. Cook the Beef: Heat a large skillet or wok over medium-high heat and add 1 tablespoon of vegetable oil. Once the oil is hot, add the sliced flank steak and stir-fry for 2-3 minutes until it’s browned and cooked through. Remove the beef from the skillet and set it aside.

  4. Sauté the Vegetables: In the same skillet, add the remaining tablespoon of vegetable oil. Add the sliced mushrooms and bell pepper, and sauté for 3-4 minutes until they are tender-crisp. Add the minced garlic and grated ginger, and cook for an additional minute until fragrant.

  5. Combine and Simmer: Return the cooked beef to the skillet with the sautéed vegetables. Pour the stir-fry sauce over the beef and vegetables, and gently toss everything together to ensure the sauce coats all the ingredients. Allow the mixture to simmer for a couple of minutes until the sauce thickens slightly and everything is heated through.

  6. Serve: Once the beef and mushroom stir fry is ready, serve it immediately over cooked rice or noodles. Garnish with sliced green onions and sesame seeds for an extra pop of flavor and visual appeal.

Tips and Variations

  • For a spicy kick, add a pinch of red pepper flakes or a drizzle of sriracha to the stir fry sauce.
  • Feel free to customize the vegetable selection based on your preferences. Broccoli, snap peas, or baby corn can be delightful additions to this stir fry.
  • To make it a complete meal, consider adding additional protein such as tofu or shrimp.

Enjoy the delightful combination of tender beef, earthy mushrooms, and vibrant bell peppers in this Quick and Easy Beef and Mushroom Stir Fry. It’s a versatile dish that can be tailored to suit your taste preferences and is sure to become a regular favorite in your recipe repertoire. Happy cooking!
