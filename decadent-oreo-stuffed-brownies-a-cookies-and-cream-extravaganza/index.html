Decadent Oreo Stuffed Brownies: A Cookies and Cream Extravaganza


Are you ready to take your brownie game to the next level? Look no further than these decadent Oreo stuffed brownies – a cookies and cream extravaganza that will delight your taste buds and impress your friends and family. This indulgent treat combines the rich, fudgy goodness of brownies with the irresistible appeal of Oreo cookies, creating a dessert that’s sure to become a new favorite. Whether you’re a baking enthusiast or a novice in the kitchen, this recipe is guaranteed to be a hit.

Ingredients

To embark on this delightful baking adventure, you’ll need the following ingredients:

  • 1 cup unsalted butter
  • 2 cups granulated sugar
  • 4 large eggs
  • 1 tablespoon vanilla extract
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 20 Oreo cookies
  • 1 cup semi-sweet chocolate chips

Instructions

Step 1: Preparing the Brownie Batter

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C) and line a 9x13-inch baking dish with parchment paper.
  2. In a microwave-safe bowl, melt the butter in 30-second intervals until it’s completely melted.
  3. In a large mixing bowl, combine the melted butter and granulated sugar, and mix until well combined.
  4. Add the eggs and vanilla extract to the butter-sugar mixture, and whisk until smooth.
  5. Sift in the flour, cocoa powder, and salt, and gently fold the dry ingredients into the wet ingredients until just combined. Be careful not to overmix the batter.

Step 2: Assembling the Oreo Stuffed Brownies

  1. Pour half of the brownie batter into the prepared baking dish and spread it evenly.
  2. Place the Oreo cookies in an even layer on top of the brownie batter.
  3. Pour the remaining brownie batter over the Oreo cookies, ensuring that they are completely covered.
  4. Gently press the Oreo cookies down into the batter to ensure they’re nestled in nicely.

Step 3: Baking the Brownies

  1. Sprinkle the chocolate chips over the top of the brownie batter.
  2. Place the baking dish in the preheated oven and bake for 35-40 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out with moist crumbs, but not wet batter.
  3. Once baked, remove the brownies from the oven and let them cool completely in the baking dish before slicing.

Serving Suggestions

These indulgent Oreo stuffed brownies are best enjoyed with a glass of cold milk or a scoop of vanilla ice cream. Serve them at your next gathering, or simply treat yourself to a delightful cookies and cream experience.

Conclusion

This recipe for decadent Oreo stuffed brownies is a celebration of all things indulgent and delicious. With its fudgy brownie base and surprise Oreo filling, it’s a treat that will leave everyone craving for more. Whether you’re baking for a special occasion or simply looking to satisfy your sweet tooth, these brownies are the perfect choice. So, roll up your sleeves, preheat your oven, and get ready to embark on a cookies and cream extravaganza that will have everyone asking for seconds!
