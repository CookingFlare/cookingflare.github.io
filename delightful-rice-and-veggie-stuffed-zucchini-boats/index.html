Delightful Rice and Veggie Stuffed Zucchini Boats


If you’re looking for a delicious and healthy meal that’s both satisfying and visually appealing, then you’re in for a treat with these delightful rice and veggie stuffed zucchini boats. This recipe combines the freshness of zucchini with the heartiness of rice and a medley of flavorful vegetables, resulting in a dish that’s as beautiful as it is delicious. Whether you’re a seasoned chef or just starting out in the kitchen, these zucchini boats are a fantastic addition to your culinary repertoire.

Ingredients

For the Zucchini Boats:

  • 4 medium zucchinis
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

For the Rice and Veggie Filling:

  • 1 cup cooked rice (white or brown, your choice)
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 medium red bell pepper, diced
  • 1 medium yellow bell pepper, diced
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1 cup baby spinach
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Grated Parmesan cheese for garnish (optional)

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Zucchini:

    • Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
    • Cut the zucchinis in half lengthwise and use a spoon to scoop out the seeds, creating a hollow “boat” shape.
    • Brush the zucchini boats with olive oil and sprinkle with salt and pepper.

  2. Roast the Zucchini:

    • Place the prepared zucchini boats on a baking sheet and roast in the preheated oven for 15-20 minutes, or until they are slightly tender. Remove from the oven and set aside.

  3. Make the Rice and Veggie Filling:

    • In a large skillet, heat olive oil over medium heat.
    • Add the chopped onion and sauté until translucent, then add the minced garlic and cook for another minute.
    • Stir in the diced bell peppers and cherry tomatoes, and cook until the vegetables are tender.
    • Add the cooked rice to the skillet, along with the dried oregano, dried basil, salt, and pepper. Stir to combine.
    • Finally, add the baby spinach to the skillet and cook until it wilts.

  4. Assemble the Zucchini Boats:

    • Spoon the rice and veggie filling into the roasted zucchini boats, mounding the filling on the top.
    • If desired, sprinkle the filled zucchini boats with grated Parmesan cheese for an extra layer of flavor.

  5. Bake the Stuffed Zucchini:

    • Return the stuffed zucchini boats to the oven and bake for an additional 15-20 minutes, or until the zucchini is tender and the filling is heated through.

  6. Serve and Enjoy:

    • Once the zucchini boats are done, remove them from the oven and let them cool for a few minutes.
    • Serve the stuffed zucchini boats warm, garnished with fresh herbs if desired.

Final Thoughts

These delightful rice and veggie stuffed zucchini boats are a wonderful way to enjoy the abundance of summer produce and create a meal that’s as nutritious as it is flavorful. The combination of tender zucchini, savory rice, and vibrant vegetables makes for a dish that’s sure to impress both your family and any guests you may have. Whether you’re looking for a light lunch, a tasty side dish, or even a vegetarian main course, these zucchini boats have you covered. So, roll up your sleeves, head to the kitchen, and get ready to enjoy a delightful culinary adventure with these stuffed zucchini boats!
