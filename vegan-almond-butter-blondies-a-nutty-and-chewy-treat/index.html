Vegan Almond Butter Blondies: A Nutty and Chewy Treat


Are you craving a delicious and indulgent treat that is not only vegan but also packed with nutty flavors and chewy goodness? Look no further than these Vegan Almond Butter Blondies! Made with simple and wholesome ingredients, these blondies are the perfect balance of sweet, nutty, and chewy. Whether you’re a vegan or simply looking to expand your dessert repertoire, these blondies are sure to satisfy your sweet tooth.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup almond butter
  • 1/2 cup maple syrup
  • 1/4 cup melted coconut oil
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 cup oat flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup chopped almonds

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C) and line a baking dish with parchment paper.

  2. In a large mixing bowl, combine the almond butter, maple syrup, melted coconut oil, and vanilla extract. Stir until well combined and smooth.

  3. In a separate bowl, whisk together the oat flour, baking soda, and salt. Gradually add the dry ingredients to the wet ingredients, stirring until a thick batter forms.

  4. Fold in the chopped almonds, ensuring they are evenly distributed throughout the batter.

  5. Transfer the batter to the prepared baking dish and spread it out evenly using a spatula.

  6. Bake for 20-25 minutes, or until the edges are golden brown and a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.

  7. Remove from the oven and let the blondies cool completely in the baking dish before cutting into squares.

  8. Serve and enjoy!

Why Choose Vegan Almond Butter Blondies?

These Vegan Almond Butter Blondies are not only incredibly delicious but also offer a range of health benefits. Almond butter, a key ingredient in this recipe, is a great source of healthy fats, protein, and fiber. It may help lower cholesterol levels, reduce the risk of heart disease, and promote weight management. Additionally, almond butter is rich in vitamin E, magnesium, and potassium, which are essential for overall health and wellbeing.

By using oat flour instead of traditional all-purpose flour, these blondies become gluten-free and offer a higher fiber content. Oats are not only a great source of fiber but also provide important nutrients like iron, zinc, and B vitamins.

Maple syrup, another ingredient in this recipe, is a natural sweetener that adds a distinct flavor while providing essential minerals like manganese and zinc. It is a healthier alternative to refined sugar and does not cause the same spikes in blood sugar levels.

Lastly, these Vegan Almond Butter Blondies are not only vegan-friendly but also suitable for those with dairy or egg allergies. They are a great option for people with dietary restrictions or anyone looking to reduce their consumption of animal products.

Get Creative with Add-ins

While this recipe calls for chopped almonds, feel free to get creative with your add-ins. You can switch it up by adding dried fruits like cranberries or raisins, or even incorporate chocolate chips for an extra indulgent treat. The possibilities are endless, so don’t be afraid to experiment and make these Vegan Almond Butter Blondies your own.

Final Thoughts

These Vegan Almond Butter Blondies are an irresistible combination of nutty flavors, chewy texture, and wholesome ingredients. They are perfect for satisfying your dessert cravings without compromising on taste or your dietary preferences. Whether you’re a vegan, have food allergies, or simply enjoy exploring new recipes, these blondies are a must-try. So go ahead, whip up a batch, and indulge in this delicious and guilt-free treat!
