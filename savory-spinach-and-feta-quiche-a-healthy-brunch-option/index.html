Savory Spinach and Feta Quiche: A Healthy Brunch Option


If you’re looking for a delicious and healthy brunch option that’s perfect for entertaining or simply enjoying with your family, look no further than this savory spinach and feta quiche. This classic dish is not only packed with flavor, but it’s also a great way to incorporate some nutritious greens into your meal. Whether you’re hosting a brunch gathering or simply want to elevate your weekend breakfast, this quiche is sure to impress.

Ingredients

For the Crust:

  • 1 1/4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, chilled and cubed
  • 1/4 cup ice water

For the Filling:

  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 3 cups fresh spinach, chopped
  • 4 large eggs
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1/2 cup crumbled feta cheese
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Pinch of nutmeg

Instructions

Step 1: Prepare the Crust

  1. In a large bowl, combine the flour and salt. Add the chilled, cubed butter and use a pastry cutter or fork to mix until the mixture resembles coarse crumbs.
  2. Gradually add the ice water and mix until the dough comes together. Shape the dough into a disk, wrap it in plastic wrap, and refrigerate for at least 30 minutes.

Step 2: Preheat the Oven and Prepare the Filling

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. In a skillet, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the chopped onion and cook until softened.
  3. Add the chopped spinach to the skillet and cook until wilted. Remove from heat and set aside.

Step 3: Roll Out the Dough and Assemble the Quiche

  1. On a floured surface, roll out the chilled dough into a circle large enough to fit into a 9-inch pie dish.
  2. Carefully transfer the rolled-out dough to the pie dish and press it gently into the bottom and sides. Trim any excess dough and crimp the edges.

Step 4: Prepare the Filling and Bake

  1. In a mixing bowl, whisk together the eggs, milk, crumbled feta cheese, salt, pepper, and nutmeg.
  2. Spread the spinach and onion mixture over the bottom of the crust. Pour the egg mixture over the top.
  3. Place the quiche in the preheated oven and bake for 35-40 minutes, or until the filling is set and the crust is golden brown.

Serving Suggestions

Once the quiche is baked to perfection, allow it to cool for a few minutes before slicing into wedges. Serve it alongside a fresh green salad for a complete and satisfying brunch. This quiche is also great for meal prep – simply store any leftovers in the refrigerator and reheat for a quick and delicious breakfast throughout the week.

Conclusion

Savory spinach and feta quiche is a versatile dish that can be enjoyed for brunch, lunch, or even dinner. Its flavorful combination of spinach, feta, and eggs makes it a go-to option for those looking for a healthy and satisfying meal. Whether you’re a seasoned home cook or just starting out, this quiche is a great opportunity to explore your culinary skills and treat yourself to a delightful, homemade dish.

Try out this recipe for a delightful culinary experience that’s sure to become a favorite in your household. Enjoy!
