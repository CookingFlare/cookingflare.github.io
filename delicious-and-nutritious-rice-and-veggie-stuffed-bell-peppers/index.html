Delicious and Nutritious Rice and Veggie Stuffed Bell Peppers


Are you looking for a flavorful and healthy recipe that will satisfy your taste buds? Look no further than these delicious and nutritious rice and veggie stuffed bell peppers! Packed with vibrant colors and a medley of flavors, this dish is sure to impress both your family and friends. Plus, it’s a great way to sneak in some extra veggies into your diet. Let’s dive right into the recipe!

Ingredients

  • 4 large bell peppers (any color)
  • 1 cup cooked rice (white or brown)
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 medium onion, diced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 medium zucchini, diced
  • 1 medium carrot, grated
  • 1 cup mushrooms, sliced
  • 1 cup spinach, chopped
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C). Prepare a baking dish by greasing it lightly with olive oil or non-stick cooking spray.

  2. Cut the tops off the bell peppers and remove the seeds and membranes. Rinse the peppers under cold water and set them aside.

  3. In a large skillet, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the diced onion and minced garlic, and sauté until they become fragrant and translucent.

  4. Add the diced zucchini, grated carrot, sliced mushrooms, and chopped spinach to the skillet. Cook for about 5 minutes or until the vegetables are tender.

  5. Stir in the cooked rice, dried oregano, dried basil, salt, and pepper. Mix everything together until well combined.

  6. Spoon the rice and veggie mixture into each bell pepper, filling them to the top. Place the stuffed bell peppers in the prepared baking dish.

  7. Sprinkle the shredded mozzarella cheese on top of each stuffed bell pepper, covering them evenly.

  8. Bake in the preheated oven for 25-30 minutes or until the bell peppers are tender and the cheese is melted and lightly golden.

  9. Once cooked, remove the stuffed bell peppers from the oven and let them cool for a few minutes before serving.

Serving Suggestions

These delicious rice and veggie stuffed bell peppers can be served as a main course or as a side dish. They pair well with a fresh green salad or a side of steamed vegetables. For an extra kick of flavor, you can drizzle some balsamic glaze or sprinkle some chopped fresh herbs on top before serving.

Nutritional Information

This recipe is not only delicious but also packed with essential nutrients. Bell peppers are a great source of vitamins A and C, while the combination of vegetables provides a range of vitamins, minerals, and fiber. The addition of rice adds carbohydrates for energy, making this dish a well-rounded meal option.

Final Thoughts

These delicious and nutritious rice and veggie stuffed bell peppers are a perfect way to add some excitement to your dinner table. The vibrant colors, flavorful filling, and cheesy topping make this dish a crowd-pleaser. Whether you’re a vegetarian or simply looking for a healthy meal option, this recipe is sure to satisfy your cravings. Give it a try and let your taste buds thank you!

Remember, cooking is all about experimenting and having fun in the kitchen. Feel free to customize this recipe by adding your favorite vegetables or spices. Enjoy the process and enjoy the delicious results!
