30-Minute Beef and Broccoli Ramen Stir-Fry Recipe


If you’re looking for a quick and delicious meal that’s packed with flavor, then you’re in the right place. This 30-minute beef and broccoli ramen stir-fry is the perfect dish for those busy weeknights when you want something hearty and satisfying without spending hours in the kitchen. Plus, it’s a great way to put a new spin on the classic beef and broccoli stir-fry by adding a touch of Asian-inspired flair with ramen noodles.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound flank steak, thinly sliced
  • 3 packs of ramen noodles (discard seasoning packets)
  • 3 cups broccoli florets
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon fresh ginger, grated
  • 1/2 cup low-sodium soy sauce
  • 1/4 cup hoisin sauce
  • 2 tablespoons brown sugar
  • 2 tablespoons cornstarch
  • 1/4 teaspoon red pepper flakes
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 2 green onions, thinly sliced (for garnish)
  • Sesame seeds (for garnish)

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Noodles: Cook the ramen noodles according to the package instructions. Once cooked, drain and set aside.

  2. Make the Sauce: In a small bowl, whisk together the soy sauce, hoisin sauce, brown sugar, cornstarch, and red pepper flakes. Set the sauce aside.

  3. Cook the Beef: Heat 1 tablespoon of vegetable oil in a large skillet or wok over medium-high heat. Add the sliced flank steak and cook until browned, about 3-4 minutes. Remove the beef from the skillet and set aside.

  4. Stir-Fry the Broccoli: In the same skillet, add another tablespoon of vegetable oil. Add the broccoli florets and stir-fry for 2-3 minutes until they are crisp-tender. Add the minced garlic and grated ginger, and cook for an additional 1-2 minutes.

  5. Combine and Simmer: Return the cooked beef to the skillet with the broccoli. Pour the prepared sauce over the beef and broccoli. Stir to coat everything evenly and let the sauce come to a simmer. Cook for 2-3 minutes, stirring occasionally, until the sauce has thickened.

  6. Add the Noodles: Add the cooked ramen noodles to the skillet and toss to combine with the beef, broccoli, and sauce. Continue to cook for another 2-3 minutes until everything is heated through.

  7. Serve: Once everything is well combined and heated, remove the skillet from the heat. Garnish the dish with sliced green onions and sesame seeds.

Tips and Variations

  • For an extra kick of heat, add a sprinkle of chili flakes or a drizzle of sriracha sauce.
  • Feel free to customize the vegetables in this stir-fry. You can add bell peppers, snap peas, or mushrooms for a different twist.
  • If you prefer a different protein, such as chicken or tofu, you can easily swap out the beef for your protein of choice.

Final Thoughts

This 30-minute beef and broccoli ramen stir-fry is a fantastic dish to have in your recipe arsenal. It’s quick, flavorful, and versatile, making it a go-to option for busy nights when you want a homemade meal without a lot of fuss. With the perfect balance of savory beef, crisp broccoli, and comforting ramen noodles, this stir-fry is sure to become a family favorite. So, next time you’re craving a satisfying and easy-to-make dinner, give this recipe a try and enjoy a delicious meal in no time!
