Flavorful Meat Skewers: 8 Appetizer Recipes to Try


Meat skewers are a staple of many cuisines around the world, and for good reason. They’re easy to make, fun to eat, and can be customized to suit a wide range of tastes and dietary preferences. In this post, we’ll explore eight different meat skewer recipes that are perfect for appetizers, parties, or just a quick and easy dinner.

Recipe 1: Classic Beef and Onion Skewers

  • 500g beef sirloin, cut into 1-inch cubes
  • 1 large onion, cut into 1-inch pieces
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1/4 teaspoon paprika

Thread the beef and onion cubes onto skewers, leaving a small space between each piece. Brush with olive oil and season with salt, pepper, and paprika. Grill or broil for 10-12 minutes, or until the beef is cooked to your liking.

Recipe 2: Spicy Shrimp Skewers

  • 500g large shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon honey
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1/2 teaspoon cayenne pepper
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Thread the shrimp onto skewers, leaving a small space between each piece. In a small bowl, whisk together the olive oil, garlic, soy sauce, honey, cumin, and cayenne pepper. Brush the mixture evenly over the shrimp. Grill or broil for 8-10 minutes, or until the shrimp are pink and cooked through.

Recipe 3: Chicken Souvlaki Skewers

  • 500g boneless, skinless chicken breast, cut into 1-inch cubes
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 1 tablespoon chopped fresh oregano
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper

Thread the chicken cubes onto skewers, leaving a small space between each piece. In a small bowl, whisk together the olive oil, garlic, lemon juice, oregano, salt, and pepper. Brush the mixture evenly over the chicken. Grill or broil for 10-12 minutes, or until the chicken is cooked through.

Recipe 4: Lamb Koftas Skewers

  • 500g ground lamb
  • 1/4 cup breadcrumbs
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1/4 teaspoon cumin

Mix the ground lamb, breadcrumbs, parsley, garlic, salt, pepper, and cumin in a large bowl until just combined. Divide the mixture into 8 equal portions and shape each portion into a ball. Thread the lamb balls onto skewers, leaving a small space between each piece. Brush with olive oil and grill or broil for 10-12 minutes, or until the lamb is cooked through.

Recipe 5: Turkey and Apple Skewers

  • 500g ground turkey
  • 1/2 cup chopped fresh apple
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh sage
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper

Mix the ground turkey, apple, sage, garlic, salt, and pepper in a large bowl until just combined. Divide the mixture into 8 equal portions and shape each portion into a ball. Thread the turkey balls onto skewers, leaving a small space between each piece. Brush with olive oil and grill or broil for 10-12 minutes, or until the turkey is cooked through.

Recipe 6: Pork and Pineapple Skewers

  • 500g pork tenderloin, cut into 1-inch cubes
  • 1 cup pineapple chunks
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh cilantro
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper

Thread the pork and pineapple chunks onto skewers, leaving a small space between each piece. In a small bowl, whisk together the olive oil, garlic, salt, and pepper. Brush the mixture evenly over the pork and pineapple. Grill or broil for 10-12 minutes, or until the pork is cooked through.

Recipe 7: Beef and Mushroom Skewers

  • 500g beef sirloin, cut into 1-inch cubes
  • 1 cup sliced mushrooms
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh thyme
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper

Thread the beef and mushroom slices onto skewers, leaving a small space between each piece. In a small bowl, whisk together the olive oil, garlic, thyme, salt, and pepper. Brush the mixture evenly over the beef and mushrooms. Grill or broil for 10-12 minutes, or until the beef is cooked to your liking.

Recipe 8: Chicken and Bacon Skewers

  • 500g boneless, skinless chicken breast, cut into 1-inch cubes
  • 6 slices of bacon, cut into 1-inch pieces
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper

Thread the chicken and bacon pieces onto skewers, leaving a small space between each piece. In a small bowl, whisk together the olive oil, garlic, parsley, salt, and pepper. Brush the mixture evenly over the chicken and bacon. Grill or broil for 10-12 minutes, or until the chicken is cooked through.

Conclusion

Meat skewers are a versatile and delicious appetizer option that can be customized to suit a wide range of tastes and dietary preferences. Whether you’re looking for a classic beef and onion skewer or something more adventurous like lamb koftas or chicken and bacon skewers, we’ve got you covered. Try out these eight different recipes and discover the perfect meat skewer for your next gathering or dinner party.
