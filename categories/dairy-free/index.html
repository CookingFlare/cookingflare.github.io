Satisfying Dairy-Free Vegetable Lasagna for a Comforting Meal

Are you looking for a delicious and comforting meal that’s dairy-free and packed with wholesome goodness? Look no further than this satisfying dairy-free vegetable lasagna recipe! Whether you’re following a dairy-free diet or simply want to add more plant-based goodness to your meals, this lasagna is sure to impress your taste buds and satisfy your cravings. Ingredients For the Vegetable Filling: 2 tbsp olive oil 1 large onion, finely chopped 3 garlic cloves, minced 2 medium zucchinis, diced 2 large carrots, diced 1 red bell pepper, diced 1 cup sliced mushrooms 1 tsp dried oregano 1 tsp dried basil Salt and pepper to taste For the Dairy-Free Bechamel Sauce: 3 tbsp dairy-free butter 3 tbsp all-purpose flour 2 ½ cups unsweetened almond milk 1/2 tsp nutmeg Salt and pepper to taste For the Assembly: 12 lasagna noodles, cooked according to package instructions 1 ½ cups dairy-free shredded mozzarella cheese Fresh basil leaves for garnish Instructions Step 1: Prepare the Vegetable Filling In a large skillet, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the chopped onion and garlic, and sauté until fragrant. Add the diced zucchinis, carrots, red bell pepper, and mushrooms to the skillet. Cook until the vegetables are tender. Season the vegetable mixture with dried oregano, dried basil, salt, and pepper. Stir well to combine, then remove from heat and set aside. Step 2: Make the Dairy-Free Bechamel Sauce In a separate saucepan, melt the dairy-free butter over medium heat. Once melted, add the all-purpose flour and whisk continuously to form a smooth paste. Gradually pour in the unsweetened almond milk, whisking constantly to prevent lumps from forming. Add the nutmeg, salt, and pepper to the sauce, and continue to cook, stirring frequently, until the sauce thickens to a creamy consistency. Remove from heat and set aside. Step 3: Assemble the Lasagna Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C). In a 9x13 inch baking dish, spread a thin layer of the dairy-free bechamel sauce to coat the bottom. Place a layer of cooked lasagna noodles over the sauce, followed by a portion of the prepared vegetable filling. Drizzle a generous amount of the dairy-free bechamel sauce over the vegetable layer, then sprinkle a layer of dairy-free shredded mozzarella cheese on top. Repeat the layers until all the ingredients are used, finishing with a layer of dairy-free bechamel sauce and a sprinkle of dairy-free shredded mozzarella cheese on top. Step 4: Bake and Serve Cover the baking dish with aluminum foil and bake in the preheated oven for 35-40 minutes, or until the lasagna is hot and bubbly. Remove the foil and bake for an additional 10 minutes, or until the cheese is golden and slightly crispy. Once done, remove the lasagna from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes before slicing. Garnish with fresh basil leaves before serving. Conclusion There you have it – a scrumptious dairy-free vegetable lasagna that’s perfect for a comforting meal any day of the week. Packed with vibrant vegetables, flavorful bechamel sauce, and gooey dairy-free mozzarella, this lasagna is a true crowd-pleaser. Whether you’re cooking for a special occasion or simply craving a cozy homemade meal, this recipe is bound to hit the spot.
