The Mediterranean Influence: Fresh Herb Recipes from the Region


The Mediterranean region is renowned for its vibrant and flavorful cuisine, which is heavily influenced by the abundant use of fresh herbs. From the sun-drenched coasts of Greece to the rustic villages of Italy, the use of herbs such as basil, oregano, rosemary, and thyme infuses the dishes with a distinctive and aromatic character. In this blog post, we’ll explore some delightful recipes that showcase the rich and diverse flavors of Mediterranean cuisine, focusing on the creative use of fresh herbs.

Greek Lemon and Herb Roast Chicken

Ingredients:

  • 1 whole chicken
  • 4 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1/4 cup of fresh oregano, chopped
  • 1/4 cup of fresh rosemary, chopped
  • 1/4 cup of fresh thyme, chopped
  • Zest and juice of 2 lemons
  • 1/4 cup of olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. In a small bowl, combine the minced garlic, chopped herbs, lemon zest, lemon juice, and olive oil to make a marinade.
  3. Place the whole chicken in a roasting pan and generously season the inside and outside with salt and pepper.
  4. Gently lift the skin of the chicken and rub half of the prepared marinade underneath the skin, ensuring it covers the breast and thigh meat.
  5. Pour the remaining marinade over the chicken, making sure it is evenly coated.
  6. Roast the chicken in the preheated oven for 1.5 to 2 hours, or until the internal temperature reaches 165°F (74°C) and the skin is golden brown and crispy.
  7. Let the chicken rest for 10 minutes before carving. Serve with roasted vegetables and a sprinkle of fresh herbs for a burst of Mediterranean flavor.

Italian Caprese Salad with Basil Infused Olive Oil

Ingredients:

  • 4 ripe tomatoes, sliced
  • 1 pound of fresh mozzarella, sliced
  • 1/2 cup of fresh basil leaves
  • 1/4 cup of extra virgin olive oil
  • Balsamic glaze
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Arrange the slices of tomato and mozzarella on a serving platter, alternating them for an attractive presentation.
  2. In a blender, combine the fresh basil leaves and olive oil. Blend until the basil is finely chopped and the oil is infused with the aromatic herb.
  3. Drizzle the basil-infused olive oil over the tomato and mozzarella slices.
  4. Season the salad with salt and pepper, and then drizzle with balsamic glaze for a touch of sweetness.
  5. Garnish with additional fresh basil leaves before serving. This simple and elegant salad celebrates the essence of Mediterranean flavors.

Spanish Garlic and Herb Shrimp

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound of large shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 4 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1/4 cup of fresh parsley, chopped
  • 1/4 cup of fresh cilantro, chopped
  • 1/4 cup of extra virgin olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon of smoked paprika
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a large skillet, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the minced garlic and cook until fragrant, about 1 minute.
  2. Add the shrimp to the skillet and sauté for 2-3 minutes, or until they turn pink and opaque.
  3. Sprinkle the chopped parsley, cilantro, and smoked paprika over the shrimp, and toss to coat evenly.
  4. Season with salt and pepper to taste, and continue to cook for another minute.
  5. Remove the skillet from the heat and serve the garlic and herb shrimp immediately, accompanied by crusty bread to soak up the flavorful herb-infused oil.

The use of fresh herbs in Mediterranean cooking not only enhances the taste of the dishes but also contributes to their healthful qualities. The recipes presented here offer a glimpse into the culinary heritage of the Mediterranean region, where the aromatic symphony of fresh herbs takes center stage. Whether it’s the earthy notes of rosemary, the peppery sweetness of basil, or the citrusy brightness of oregano, these recipes capture the essence of Mediterranean cuisine, inviting you to savor the flavors of this sun-soaked region in your own kitchen.
