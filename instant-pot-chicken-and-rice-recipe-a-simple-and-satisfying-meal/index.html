Instant Pot Chicken and Rice Recipe: A Simple and Satisfying Meal


If you’re looking for a quick and easy meal that will satisfy your cravings, look no further than this Instant Pot Chicken and Rice recipe. With just a few simple ingredients and the convenience of the Instant Pot, you can have a delicious and hearty meal on the table in no time.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups of white rice
  • 1 pound of chicken breasts, boneless and skinless
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon of dried thyme
  • 1 teaspoon of dried rosemary
  • 1 teaspoon of paprika
  • 1 teaspoon of salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon of black pepper
  • 3 cups of chicken broth

Instructions

  1. Start by rinsing the rice thoroughly under cold water. This helps remove any excess starch and ensures that the rice cooks evenly.

  2. Set your Instant Pot to the sauté function and add a drizzle of oil. Once the pot is hot, add the diced onion and minced garlic. Sauté for a few minutes until the onions are soft and translucent.

  3. While the onions and garlic are cooking, season the chicken breasts with the dried thyme, dried rosemary, paprika, salt, and black pepper. Make sure to coat the chicken evenly with the spices.

  4. Push the onions and garlic to one side of the pot and add the seasoned chicken breasts. Sear the chicken on both sides until they are browned, about 2 minutes per side.

  5. Once the chicken is browned, add the rinsed rice and chicken broth to the Instant Pot. Give everything a good stir to combine the ingredients.

  6. Secure the lid on the Instant Pot and set the venting knob to the sealing position. Cook on high pressure for 8 minutes.

  7. After the cooking time is complete, allow the Instant Pot to naturally release the pressure for 10 minutes. Then, carefully release any remaining pressure by turning the venting knob to the venting position.

  8. Once the pressure has been released, remove the lid from the Instant Pot. Give the chicken and rice a gentle stir to fluff the rice and ensure that everything is well combined.

  9. Serve the Instant Pot Chicken and Rice hot and enjoy!

Tips and Variations

  • For added flavor, you can sauté some diced carrots and celery along with the onions and garlic.
  • If you prefer a spicier dish, you can add a pinch of red pepper flakes or a dash of hot sauce to the chicken and rice before cooking.
  • Feel free to customize this recipe by adding your favorite vegetables, such as peas, corn, or bell peppers.
  • To make this recipe vegetarian-friendly, you can substitute the chicken broth with vegetable broth and omit the chicken breasts. Instead, add some diced tofu or your favorite meat alternative.
  • Leftovers can be stored in an airtight container in the refrigerator for up to 3 days. Simply reheat in the microwave or on the stovetop before serving.

Enjoy the simplicity and deliciousness of this Instant Pot Chicken and Rice recipe. It’s the perfect dish for busy weeknights or lazy weekends when you want a satisfying meal without spending hours in the kitchen. Give it a try and let your taste buds thank you!
