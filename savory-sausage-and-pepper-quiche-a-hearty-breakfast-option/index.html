Savory Sausage and Pepper Quiche: A Hearty Breakfast Option


Are you tired of the same old breakfast options? Looking for a hearty and flavorful dish to start your day off right? Look no further than this delectable savory sausage and pepper quiche! Packed with savory sausage, colorful peppers, and creamy cheese, this quiche is a perfect option for a leisurely weekend brunch or a make-ahead breakfast for a busy weekday morning.

Ingredients

For the Crust:

  • 1 1/4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup cold unsalted butter, cut into cubes
  • 3-4 tablespoons ice water

For the Filling:

  • 8 ounces ground sausage
  • 1/2 cup chopped bell peppers (red, green, or yellow)
  • 1/2 cup chopped onion
  • 1 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • 4 large eggs
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1/4 teaspoon paprika

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Crust:

    • In a large bowl, mix the flour and salt. Add the cold butter cubes and use a pastry cutter or fork to blend the mixture until it resembles coarse crumbs. Gradually add the ice water, 1 tablespoon at a time, and mix until the dough comes together. Form the dough into a disk, wrap it in plastic wrap, and refrigerate for at least 30 minutes.

  2. Preheat the Oven:

    • Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).

  3. Prepare the Filling:

    • In a skillet over medium heat, cook the ground sausage until browned and cooked through. Add the chopped peppers and onions, and sauté until the vegetables are tender. Remove from heat and allow the mixture to cool slightly.

  4. Assemble the Quiche:

    • Roll out the chilled dough on a floured surface and carefully transfer it to a 9-inch pie dish. Crimp the edges as desired. Spread the sausage and pepper mixture evenly over the crust, then sprinkle the shredded cheddar cheese on top.

  5. Prepare the Egg Mixture:

    • In a separate bowl, whisk together the eggs, milk, salt, pepper, and paprika until well combined.

  6. Bake the Quiche:

    • Pour the egg mixture over the sausage and pepper filling in the pie dish. Place the quiche in the preheated oven and bake for 35-40 minutes, or until the center is set and the crust is golden brown.

  7. Serve and Enjoy:

    • Remove the quiche from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes before slicing. Serve the savory sausage and pepper quiche warm, and savor every delicious bite!

Tips and Variations

  • Make It Ahead: You can prepare the quiche up to a day in advance and refrigerate it before baking. This makes it a convenient option for busy mornings.
  • Vegetarian Option: If you prefer a vegetarian version, simply omit the sausage and add more vegetables like spinach, mushrooms, or zucchini.
  • Customize the Cheese: Feel free to experiment with different types of cheese such as Swiss, mozzarella, or pepper jack for a unique flavor profile.

Final Thoughts

This savory sausage and pepper quiche is a versatile dish that can be enjoyed for breakfast, brunch, or even a light dinner. With its flaky crust, savory filling, and customizable options, it’s sure to become a favorite in your recipe collection. Whether you’re hosting a gathering or simply treating yourself to a special meal, this quiche is a delicious way to start the day. So, gather your ingredients and get ready to savor the irresistible flavors of this hearty breakfast option!
