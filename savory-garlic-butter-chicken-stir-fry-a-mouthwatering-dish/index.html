Savory Garlic Butter Chicken Stir Fry: A Mouthwatering Dish


Are you looking for a delicious and effortless meal that will leave your taste buds tingling with delight? Look no further than this savory garlic butter chicken stir fry. Packed with flavor and easy to make, this dish is the perfect choice for a quick weeknight dinner or a special weekend treat. Let’s dive into the recipe and discover how to create this mouthwatering delight in your own kitchen.

Ingredients:

  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts, thinly sliced
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 red bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 green bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 cup snap peas
  • 1 cup broccoli florets
  • 1/2 cup chicken broth
  • 4 tablespoons unsalted butter
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Cooked rice or noodles for serving

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the Chicken: Start by seasoning the chicken slices with salt and pepper. In a large skillet or wok, heat the olive oil over medium-high heat. Add the seasoned chicken slices to the skillet and cook until they are golden brown and cooked through, about 5-7 minutes. Once cooked, transfer the chicken to a plate and set it aside.

  2. Prepare the Vegetables: In the same skillet, add the minced garlic and cook for about 1 minute until fragrant. Then, add the sliced red and green bell peppers, snap peas, and broccoli florets. Stir-fry the vegetables for 3-4 minutes until they are tender-crisp.

  3. Combine and Flavor: Return the cooked chicken to the skillet with the vegetables. Pour in the chicken broth and add the unsalted butter. Stir everything together and allow the butter to melt, creating a delicious sauce that coats the chicken and vegetables.

  4. Finish and Serve: Drizzle the soy sauce over the stir fry and toss everything together to ensure that the flavors are evenly distributed. Cook for an additional 2-3 minutes to allow the sauce to thicken slightly. Once ready, remove the skillet from the heat.

  5. Serve: Serve the savory garlic butter chicken stir fry over a bed of cooked rice or noodles, and enjoy the delightful medley of flavors and textures with every bite.

Tips and Variations:

  • For a spicy kick, add a pinch of red pepper flakes or a splash of hot sauce to the stir fry.
  • Customize the vegetables to your liking by adding mushrooms, carrots, or any other favorites you have on hand.
  • Consider marinating the chicken in a mix of soy sauce, garlic, and ginger for an extra boost of flavor.

Conclusion:

This savory garlic butter chicken stir fry is a versatile and crowd-pleasing dish that is sure to become a favorite in your recipe collection. Its simple preparation and rich, savory flavors make it a go-to option for both busy weeknights and leisurely weekends. Whether you’re cooking for yourself, your family, or guests, this dish is a winner every time. So, roll up your sleeves, gather your ingredients, and get ready to savor the delightful aromas and tastes of this mouthwatering stir fry. Bon appétit!
