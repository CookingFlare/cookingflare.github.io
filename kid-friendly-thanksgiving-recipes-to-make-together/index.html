Kid-Friendly Thanksgiving Recipes to Make Together


Thanksgiving is a fantastic time to gather with family and friends, creating cherished memories and enjoying delicious cuisine. To ensure that everyone, including kids, has fun in the kitchen, here are some simple and nutritious Thanksgiving recipes that children can help prepare. Not only will these meals surprise you with their flavor profiles, but they also offer an opportunity for quality time and learning experiences. So, grab a chef’s apron, and let’s get cooking!

  1. Mini Pecan Pie Bites

One of the culinary highlights of Thanksgiving is dessert – specifically, pecan pie. These mini bites are a delightful twist on the classic pie and a great way to involve kids.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1/4 cup brown sugar
  • 1/4 cup chopped pecans
  • 1/4 cup unsweetened applesauce
  • 1/4 cup dark corn syrup
  • 1 egg yolk
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (176°C).

  2. In a mixing bowl, combine flour and butter until it forms a crumbly texture.

  3. Add brown sugar, pecans, applesauce, corn syrup, egg yolk, and vanilla extract. Mix well.

  4. Form small balls from the dough and place them on a parchment-lined baking sheet.

  5. Bake for 15 to 20 minutes, or until golden and crispy.

  6. Cranberry Orange Relish

This festive relish adds a tangy touch to your Thanksgiving feast. Kids will enjoy helping measure and chop the ingredients, which will give them a sense of accomplishment.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup fresh orange juice
  • 1 cup water
  • 1 cup cranberries
  • 1/2 cup orange, chopped
  • 1/4 cup red onion, chopped
  • 1/4 cup sugar
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • Salt and pepper, to taste

Instructions:

  1. Bring orange juice and water to a boil in a saucepan.

  2. Add cranberries and cook for about 10 minutes, stirring occasionally.

  3. Remove from heat and let cool.

  4. In a mixing bowl, combine cranberries, orange, onion, sugar, and cinnamon. Mix well and season with salt and pepper to taste.

  5. Gravy-Inspired Mashed Potatoes

Mashed potatoes are a staple side dish at any Thanksgiving table. Jazz up the traditional recipe by adding a hint of gravy. This easy and delicious mash is perfect for kids to help prepare.

Ingredients:

  • 2 pounds russet potatoes
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1/4 cup chicken broth
  • 2 tablespoons unsalted butter
  • 1/4 cup gravy mix
  • Salt and pepper, to taste

Instructions:

  1. Peel and chop potatoes, and boil until tender.
  2. Drain water from the potatoes and return them to the pot.
  3. Heat milk, chicken broth, and butter in a saucepan until warm.
  4. Mash the potatoes with the milk mixture and add gravy mix. Mix well, and season with salt and pepper to taste.

Conclusion:

Creating wonderful memories and engaging kids in cooking is as simple as adding a few twists to familiar dishes. With these fun and simple recipes, you’ll have the whole family happily involved in preparing the Thanksgiving meal. So, gather your loved ones in the kitchen, and let the cooking magic begin!
