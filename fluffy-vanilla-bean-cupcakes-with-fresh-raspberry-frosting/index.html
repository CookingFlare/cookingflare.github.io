Fluffy Vanilla Bean Cupcakes with Fresh Raspberry Frosting


Indulge in the delightful combination of fluffy vanilla bean cupcakes and fresh raspberry frosting. These cupcakes are a treat for the senses and will leave you craving for more. The light and airy vanilla cake pairs perfectly with the tangy and sweet raspberry frosting, creating a harmonious balance of flavors. Whether you’re celebrating a special occasion or simply want to satisfy your sweet tooth, these cupcakes are sure to impress.

Ingredients

  • 1 ½ cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 ½ teaspoons baking powder
  • ¼ teaspoon salt
  • ½ cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 vanilla bean, scraped
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • ¾ cup whole milk

For the Fresh Raspberry Frosting:

  • 1 cup fresh raspberries
  • 1 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 4 cups powdered sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C). Line a muffin tin with cupcake liners and set aside.

  2. In a medium bowl, whisk together the flour, baking powder, and salt. Set aside.

  3. In a large mixing bowl, cream together the softened butter and granulated sugar until light and fluffy. This should take about 2-3 minutes.

  4. Add the eggs one at a time, beating well after each addition. Scrape the vanilla bean and add the vanilla extract, mixing until well combined.

  5. Gradually add the dry ingredients to the butter mixture, alternating with the milk. Begin and end with the dry ingredients, mixing just until combined after each addition. Be careful not to overmix.

  6. Fill each cupcake liner about two-thirds full with the batter. Smooth the tops with the back of a spoon or an offset spatula.

  7. Bake for 18-20 minutes or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean. Remove from the oven and let the cupcakes cool in the pan for 5 minutes before transferring them to a wire rack to cool completely.

  8. While the cupcakes are cooling, prepare the fresh raspberry frosting. In a blender or food processor, puree the fresh raspberries until smooth. Strain the puree through a fine-mesh sieve to remove the seeds.

  9. In a large mixing bowl, beat the softened butter until creamy. Gradually add the powdered sugar, one cup at a time, beating well after each addition. Add the vanilla extract and the raspberry puree, and continue beating until the frosting is smooth and fluffy.

  10. Once the cupcakes are completely cooled, frost them generously with the fresh raspberry frosting. You can use a piping bag fitted with a star tip for a decorative touch, or simply spread the frosting with a knife.

  11. Serve and enjoy! These fluffy vanilla bean cupcakes with fresh raspberry frosting are best enjoyed the day they are made, but they can be stored in an airtight container at room temperature for up to 3 days.

Conclusion

These Fluffy Vanilla Bean Cupcakes with Fresh Raspberry Frosting are a delightful treat that will impress your family and friends. The combination of the light and airy vanilla cake with the tangy and sweet raspberry frosting creates a perfect balance of flavors. Whether you’re celebrating a special occasion or simply indulging in a sweet treat, these cupcakes are sure to satisfy your cravings. So, put on your apron and get baking! Your taste buds will thank you.
