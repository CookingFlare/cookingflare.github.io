Irresistible Mango Coconut Rice Pudding: A Tropical Comfort Dessert


If you’re craving a taste of the tropics, look no further than this delightful and creamy Mango Coconut Rice Pudding. This exotic dessert is a perfect blend of rich coconut, sweet mango, and comforting rice, creating a treat that’s both indulgent and satisfying. Whether you’re hosting a summer gathering or simply want to transport your taste buds to a sunny paradise, this recipe will surely become a favorite in your dessert repertoire.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup Arborio rice
  • 2 cups coconut milk
  • 1 cup whole milk
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1 ripe mango, diced
  • 1/2 cup sweetened shredded coconut
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cardamom
  • Fresh mint leaves for garnish

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Rice: In a saucepan, combine the coconut milk, whole milk, sugar, and salt. Bring the mixture to a gentle simmer over medium heat, stirring occasionally to dissolve the sugar. Add the Arborio rice and reduce the heat to low. Simmer the rice, stirring frequently, for about 25-30 minutes or until the mixture thickens and the rice is tender.

  2. Add the Flavors: Once the rice is cooked, stir in the diced mango, sweetened shredded coconut, vanilla extract, and ground cardamom. Continue to cook the mixture for an additional 5 minutes, allowing the flavors to meld together and the mango to soften slightly.

  3. Chill and Serve: Remove the saucepan from the heat and let the rice pudding cool to room temperature. Then, transfer it to individual serving bowls or a large serving dish. Cover the pudding with plastic wrap and refrigerate for at least 2 hours, or until it’s thoroughly chilled and set.

  4. Garnish and Enjoy: Before serving, garnish the mango coconut rice pudding with fresh mint leaves to add a pop of color and freshness. The cooling mint complements the tropical flavors of the pudding beautifully.

Tips for Success

  • Use ripe, fragrant mangoes for the best flavor and sweetness.
  • Stir the rice pudding frequently while it simmers to prevent it from sticking to the bottom of the pan.
  • For an extra touch of indulgence, consider serving the pudding with a dollop of whipped coconut cream on top.

Embracing Tropical Flavors

This Mango Coconut Rice Pudding is a celebration of the vibrant and luscious flavors found in tropical cuisine. The creamy coconut milk adds a luxurious richness to the pudding, while the sweet mango brings a burst of sunshine to every spoonful. The aromatic hint of cardamom and the toasty sweetness of the shredded coconut elevate this dessert to a true culinary escape.

Final Thoughts

Whether you’re savoring a bowl of Mango Coconut Rice Pudding on a warm summer evening or treating yourself to a taste of the tropics in the midst of winter, this dessert is sure to transport you to a place of relaxation and pure indulgence. The combination of creamy, comforting rice pudding with the exotic allure of mango and coconut makes this recipe a standout choice for those seeking a truly irresistible and comforting dessert experience. Enjoy every spoonful and savor the tropical paradise in each bite.
