Top 5 Chocolate Desserts for the Holidays


The holiday season is a time for indulgence, and what better way to treat yourself and your loved ones than with some rich, decadent chocolate desserts? Whether you’re hosting a holiday gathering or simply enjoying a cozy night in, chocolate desserts are a surefire way to add sweetness to your celebrations. Here are the top five chocolate desserts to make your holidays even more special.

1. Classic Chocolate Yule Log

Nothing says “holiday season” like a chocolate Yule log. This traditional dessert is not only a feast for the eyes but also an absolute delight for your taste buds. A Yule log, or “bûche de Noël,” is a rolled sponge cake filled with chocolate ganache and often decorated to look like a log. It’s a great centerpiece for any holiday dinner.

Ingredients:

  • For the sponge cake:

    • 4 large eggs
    • 1 cup all-purpose flour
    • 1/2 cup cocoa powder
    • 1/4 teaspoon baking powder
    • 1/4 teaspoon salt
    • 1 cup granulated sugar
    • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

  • For the chocolate ganache:

    • 8 oz dark chocolate (70% cocoa)
    • 1 cup heavy cream
    • 1/2 cup unsalted butter

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C) and line a 10x15-inch baking sheet with parchment paper.
  2. In a large bowl, whisk the eggs and sugar until they are light and fluffy. Add the vanilla extract.
  3. In a separate bowl, sift together the flour, cocoa powder, baking powder, and salt. Gently fold the dry ingredients into the egg mixture until fully incorporated.
  4. Pour the batter onto the prepared baking sheet and spread it out evenly. Bake for 12-15 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.
  5. While the cake is baking, prepare the ganache. Heat the cream in a small saucepan over medium heat until it just begins to simmer. Pour it over the chopped dark chocolate and let it sit for a minute. Stir until smooth and glossy. Let the ganache cool to room temperature.
  6. Once the cake is baked, remove it from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes. Then, turn it out onto a clean kitchen towel dusted with powdered sugar. Carefully peel off the parchment paper and gently roll the cake up with the towel.
  7. After the cake has cooled completely, unroll it and spread a generous amount of ganache over the surface. Roll the cake back up without the towel, ensuring the seam is on the bottom.
  8. Frost the outside of the log with the remaining ganache. Use a fork to create texture, making it look like tree bark. Chill in the fridge for at least an hour before serving.

Why This Dessert is Perfect for the Holidays:

The rich chocolate flavor and beautiful presentation make the Yule log a standout dessert at any holiday table. Plus, the rolled sponge cake is light enough to complement heavier holiday meals, providing the perfect balance of sweetness and texture.

2. Chocolate Peppermint Bark

If you’re looking for something quick, easy, and absolutely delicious, chocolate peppermint bark is a perfect choice. This dessert combines the flavors of rich chocolate with the cool, refreshing taste of peppermint, making it a festive treat that everyone will love. It’s also incredibly easy to make, and you can store it in airtight containers for a few days, making it ideal for holiday gifting.

Ingredients:

  • 12 oz dark chocolate (70% cocoa)
  • 12 oz white chocolate
  • 1/2 teaspoon peppermint extract
  • 1/2 cup crushed peppermint candies or candy canes

Instructions:

  1. Line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
  2. Melt the dark chocolate in a heatproof bowl over simmering water (double boiler method) or in the microwave in short bursts, stirring in between.
  3. Once the dark chocolate is melted and smooth, pour it onto the prepared baking sheet and spread it into an even layer. Place it in the fridge to set for 15 minutes.
  4. While the dark chocolate is setting, melt the white chocolate using the same method.
  5. Once the white chocolate is smooth, stir in the peppermint extract. Pour the white chocolate over the dark chocolate layer, spreading it out evenly.
  6. Sprinkle the crushed peppermint candies on top of the white chocolate while it’s still soft.
  7. Refrigerate for about 30 minutes or until fully set. Once hardened, break it into pieces and serve.

Why This Dessert is Perfect for the Holidays:

Chocolate peppermint bark is a festive and delightful treat that combines two beloved holiday flavors: chocolate and peppermint. It’s perfect for holiday parties, gift exchanges, or simply enjoying with family. Plus, it’s easy to make in large batches, so you can share the holiday cheer with everyone!

3. Chocolate Hazelnut Truffles

For those who want to create an indulgent and luxurious treat, chocolate hazelnut truffles are the way to go. These little bites of heaven are rich, creamy, and bursting with nutty flavor. The combination of smooth chocolate and crunchy hazelnuts makes them the perfect holiday treat for anyone with a refined sweet tooth.

Ingredients:

  • 8 oz dark chocolate (70% cocoa)
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • 1/4 cup hazelnut spread (such as Nutella)
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/2 cup crushed hazelnuts (for coating)

Instructions:

  1. Chop the dark chocolate into small pieces and place it in a heatproof bowl.
  2. In a small saucepan, heat the cream over medium heat until it begins to simmer. Pour the hot cream over the chopped chocolate and let it sit for a minute. Stir until the chocolate is completely melted and smooth.
  3. Stir in the hazelnut spread and vanilla extract until fully combined.
  4. Allow the mixture to cool to room temperature, then cover and refrigerate for at least 2 hours, or until firm.
  5. Once the mixture has chilled, scoop out small portions and roll them into balls using your hands.
  6. Roll each truffle in the crushed hazelnuts to coat. Place the truffles on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper.
  7. Chill the truffles in the fridge for 30 minutes before serving.

Why This Dessert is Perfect for the Holidays:

These truffles are luxurious yet simple to make, making them an ideal choice for a holiday gift or party treat. The rich chocolate and hazelnut combination is a crowd-pleaser that works well for any occasion during the holiday season.

4. Flourless Chocolate Cake

A flourless chocolate cake is one of the most decadent and luxurious desserts you can make. It’s dense, rich, and incredibly chocolatey, and it’s perfect for those who are gluten-free or just love a chocolate dessert that’s all about the cocoa flavor. A flourless chocolate cake is also surprisingly easy to make and requires minimal ingredients.

Ingredients:

  • 8 oz dark chocolate (70% cocoa)
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter
  • 3/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 4 large eggs, beaten
  • 1/4 cup cocoa powder

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease a 9-inch round cake pan and line the bottom with parchment paper.
  2. Melt the dark chocolate and butter in a heatproof bowl over simmering water or in the microwave in short intervals, stirring until smooth.
  3. Stir in the sugar, salt, and vanilla extract into the melted chocolate mixture.
  4. Gradually mix in the beaten eggs, one at a time, followed by the cocoa powder. The batter should be thick and glossy.
  5. Pour the batter into the prepared cake pan and smooth the top.
  6. Bake for 20-25 minutes, or until the cake has set but is still soft in the center. It should have a slightly cracked top.
  7. Let the cake cool in the pan for 10 minutes, then remove from the pan and transfer to a wire rack to cool completely.
  8. Serve with a dusting of powdered sugar, fresh berries, or a dollop of whipped cream.

Why This Dessert is Perfect for the Holidays:

The richness of this flourless chocolate cake makes it the perfect ending to a holiday feast. It’s a dessert that can stand on its own, but it also pairs wonderfully with a variety of toppings like whipped cream, fresh berries, or even a drizzle of chocolate sauce.

5. Hot Chocolate Lava Cakes

If you’re looking for a dessert that’s both fun and delicious, hot chocolate lava cakes are an exciting option. These individual-sized cakes feature a molten chocolate center that oozes out when you cut into them, creating a heavenly chocolate experience. They’re perfect for impressing guests or enjoying a cozy treat by the fire.

Ingredients:

  • 6 oz dark chocolate (70% cocoa)
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter
  • 1 cup powdered sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 large egg yolks
  • 1/2 cup all-purpose flour
  • Pinch of salt
  • Butter and cocoa powder (for greasing the ramekins)

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 425°F (220°C). Grease four 6-ounce ramekins with butter and dust them with cocoa powder.
  2. In a heatproof bowl, melt the dark chocolate and butter together over simmering water or in the microwave in short intervals, stirring until smooth.
  3. Stir in the powdered sugar, followed by the eggs and egg yolks. Mix until combined.
  4. Add the flour and a pinch of salt, stirring until smooth.
  5. Divide the batter evenly between the prepared ramekins.
  6. Bake for 12-14 minutes, or until the edges are firm but the center is still soft.
  7. Let the cakes cool for a minute before carefully inverting them onto plates.
  8. Serve immediately with a scoop of vanilla ice cream or fresh berries.

Why This Dessert is Perfect for the Holidays:

The molten center of these lava cakes makes them an incredibly indulgent dessert, perfect for impressing your guests or treating yourself. They’re easy to make but look impressive, making them a perfect addition to any holiday gathering.

Whether you’re a fan of classic chocolate desserts or are looking for something a little different, these top five chocolate desserts will elevate your holiday season. From the rich and decadent Yule log to the gooey and indulgent lava cakes, there’s a chocolate treat here for everyone. Get baking, and enjoy the sweet moments with friends and family!
