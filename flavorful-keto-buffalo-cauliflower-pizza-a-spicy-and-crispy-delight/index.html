Flavorful Keto Buffalo Cauliflower Pizza: A Spicy and Crispy Delight


If you’re following a keto diet but still crave the deliciousness of pizza, look no further. Our Flavorful Keto Buffalo Cauliflower Pizza is here to satisfy your taste buds and keep you on track with your low-carb lifestyle. This spicy and crispy delight is a game-changer, delivering all the flavors you love without the guilt. So, let’s dive right into the recipe!

Ingredients

  • 1 head of cauliflower
  • 1/2 cup almond flour
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1/4 cup buffalo sauce
  • 1/4 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/4 cup crumbled blue cheese
  • Fresh cilantro, for garnish

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 425°F (220°C). Line a baking sheet with parchment paper and set aside.

  2. Trim the cauliflower head and remove the leaves. Cut the cauliflower into florets and transfer them to a food processor. Pulse until the cauliflower resembles rice-like grains.

  3. Place the cauliflower rice in a microwave-safe bowl, cover with a damp paper towel, and microwave for 5 minutes. Let it cool for a few minutes, then transfer the cauliflower rice to a clean kitchen towel. Squeeze out as much moisture as possible.

  4. In a large bowl, combine the cauliflower rice, almond flour, Parmesan cheese, garlic powder, dried oregano, salt, and black pepper. Mix well until all the ingredients are evenly incorporated.

  5. In a separate bowl, whisk the eggs together. Pour the whisked eggs into the cauliflower mixture and mix until a dough forms.

  6. Transfer the cauliflower dough onto the prepared baking sheet. Using your hands, shape the dough into a round pizza crust, about 1/4-inch thick. Make sure to press the dough evenly to ensure even baking.

  7. Bake the cauliflower pizza crust in the preheated oven for 25-30 minutes, or until golden brown and crispy around the edges.

  8. Remove the crust from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes. Then, spread the buffalo sauce evenly over the crust.

  9. Sprinkle the shredded mozzarella cheese and crumbled blue cheese on top of the buffalo sauce.

  10. Return the pizza to the oven and bake for an additional 5-7 minutes, or until the cheese is melted and bubbly.

  11. Once the pizza is done, remove it from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes. Garnish with fresh cilantro.

  12. Slice the cauliflower pizza into wedges and serve hot.

Serving Suggestions

This Flavorful Keto Buffalo Cauliflower Pizza is perfect for a cozy night in or a gathering with friends. Serve it as an appetizer or main dish alongside a fresh green salad to balance out the flavors. You can also customize your pizza by adding your favorite toppings such as diced chicken, red onions, or even extra hot sauce for an extra kick.

Final Thoughts

Our Flavorful Keto Buffalo Cauliflower Pizza is the ultimate keto-friendly alternative to traditional pizza. With its spicy buffalo sauce, crispy cauliflower crust, and gooey cheese, it’s a true delight for your taste buds. Plus, it’s low in carbs and packed with flavor, making it a winning choice for anyone following a keto lifestyle.

So, go ahead and give this recipe a try. You won’t be disappointed! Enjoy the spicy and crispy goodness of this keto buffalo cauliflower pizza while staying true to your low-carb goals. Happy cooking!
