Crunchy Low Carb Cauliflower Tots


Are you looking for a delicious and healthy alternative to traditional tater tots? Look no further! These Crunchy Low Carb Cauliflower Tots are the perfect guilt-free snack or side dish. Packed with flavor and nutrients, they are sure to satisfy your cravings while keeping you on track with your low carb lifestyle.

Ingredients

  • 1 medium head of cauliflower, grated
  • 2 eggs, beaten
  • 1/4 cup almond flour
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon onion powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried parsley
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C) and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
  2. Grate the cauliflower using a box grater or pulse it in a food processor until it resembles rice.
  3. Place the grated cauliflower in a microwave-safe bowl and microwave on high for 5 minutes. This will help soften the cauliflower and remove excess moisture.
  4. Allow the cauliflower to cool slightly, then transfer it to a clean kitchen towel or cheesecloth. Squeeze out as much liquid as possible. This step is crucial to achieving a crispy texture.
  5. In a large mixing bowl, combine the grated cauliflower, beaten eggs, almond flour, Parmesan cheese, garlic powder, onion powder, dried parsley, salt, and pepper. Mix well until all the ingredients are evenly incorporated.
  6. Take about 1 tablespoon of the cauliflower mixture and shape it into a tot-like shape. Place it on the prepared baking sheet. Repeat until all the mixture is used, leaving some space between each tot.
  7. Bake in the preheated oven for 20-25 minutes, or until the tots are golden brown and crispy. Flip them halfway through baking to ensure even browning.
  8. Remove the tots from the oven and let them cool slightly before serving. They are best enjoyed fresh out of the oven, but you can also store them in an airtight container in the refrigerator for up to 3 days. To reheat, simply pop them in the oven or air fryer until heated through and crispy again.

Serving Suggestions

  • Serve these Crunchy Low Carb Cauliflower Tots as a tasty appetizer with your favorite dipping sauce, such as marinara, ranch, or guacamole.
  • Enjoy them as a side dish alongside grilled chicken or fish for a complete and satisfying meal.
  • Add them to a salad for an extra crunch and burst of flavor.

Nutritional Information

These Crunchy Low Carb Cauliflower Tots are not only delicious but also packed with nutrients. Here’s a breakdown of the nutritional information per serving:

  • Calories: 60
  • Fat: 3g
  • Carbohydrates: 4g
  • Fiber: 2g
  • Protein: 4g

Conclusion

Crunchy Low Carb Cauliflower Tots are a fantastic alternative to traditional tater tots, offering a healthier twist without sacrificing flavor. They are easy to make, low in carbs, and packed with nutrients. Whether you’re following a low carb diet or simply looking for a delicious way to incorporate more vegetables into your meals, these tots are a must-try. So, go ahead and give this recipe a whirl – your taste buds will thank you!
