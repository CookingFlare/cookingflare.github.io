Egg-Free Sweet Cornbread: A Moist and Delicious Side Dish


Cornbread is a classic American side dish that pairs perfectly with various meals, from barbecue to chili. However, for those with dietary restrictions or allergies, traditional cornbread recipes can be off-limits due to the inclusion of eggs. But fear not! This egg-free sweet cornbread recipe is here to save the day. Not only is it moist and delicious, but it’s also completely free of eggs. So, let’s get started!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup yellow cornmeal
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 tablespoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup almond milk (or any non-dairy milk of your choice)
  • 1/4 cup vegetable oil
  • 1/4 cup unsweetened applesauce
  • 1/4 cup honey (or maple syrup for a vegan option)
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C). Grease a 9-inch square baking dish or line it with parchment paper for easy removal.

  2. In a large mixing bowl, combine the all-purpose flour, yellow cornmeal, granulated sugar, baking powder, and salt. Mix well to ensure all the dry ingredients are evenly distributed.

  3. In a separate bowl, whisk together the almond milk, vegetable oil, unsweetened applesauce, honey (or maple syrup), and vanilla extract until well combined.

  4. Gradually pour the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients, stirring gently until just combined. Be careful not to overmix, as this can result in a tougher texture.

  5. Pour the batter into the prepared baking dish, spreading it evenly with a spatula.

  6. Place the dish in the preheated oven and bake for 20-25 minutes, or until the top is golden brown and a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.

  7. Once baked, remove the cornbread from the oven and let it cool in the dish for a few minutes. Then, transfer it to a wire rack to cool completely before serving.

Serving Suggestions

This egg-free sweet cornbread is incredibly versatile and pairs well with a variety of dishes. Here are a few serving suggestions to get you started:

  • Serve it alongside a warm bowl of chili for a comforting and filling meal.
  • Enjoy it as a side dish with barbecue, grilled meats, or roasted vegetables.
  • Toast leftover cornbread and spread it with butter or your favorite jam for a delicious breakfast or snack.

Tips and Variations

  • For added texture and flavor, consider adding a handful of frozen corn kernels or diced jalapeños to the batter before baking.
  • If you prefer a sweeter cornbread, you can increase the amount of sugar or honey in the recipe.
  • Experiment with different types of non-dairy milk, such as coconut milk or oat milk, to customize the flavor to your liking.
  • To make this recipe gluten-free, simply substitute the all-purpose flour with a gluten-free flour blend.

Enjoy Egg-Free Sweet Cornbread!

Now you can enjoy delicious, moist, and egg-free sweet cornbread without any worries. This recipe is not only suitable for those with dietary restrictions but also for anyone looking to add a unique twist to their cornbread repertoire. So go ahead, give it a try, and savor the delightful flavors of this classic side dish. Happy baking!
