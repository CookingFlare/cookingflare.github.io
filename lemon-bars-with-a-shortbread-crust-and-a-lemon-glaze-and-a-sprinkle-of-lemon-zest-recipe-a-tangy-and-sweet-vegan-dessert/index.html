Lemon Bars with a Shortbread Crust and a Lemon Glaze and a Sprinkle of Lemon Zest Recipe: A Tangy and Sweet Vegan Dessert


If you’re craving a tangy and sweet dessert that’s bursting with lemon flavor, then you’re in for a treat! These Lemon Bars with a Shortbread Crust and a Lemon Glaze are not only delicious but also completely vegan, making them a perfect option for those with dietary restrictions or anyone looking to indulge in a guilt-free treat.

The Shortbread Crust

To start off, let’s focus on the foundation of these delectable lemon bars – the shortbread crust. What makes this crust so special is its buttery, crumbly texture that perfectly complements the zesty lemon filling. To achieve this, you’ll need a few simple ingredients, including all-purpose flour, powdered sugar, and vegan butter. The key to a successful shortbread crust is to ensure that the dough is well chilled before baking, as this will help it retain its shape and prevent it from becoming too crumbly.

Once the crust is prepared and baked to golden perfection, it’s time to move on to the star of the show – the luscious lemon filling.

The Luscious Lemon Filling

The lemon filling is where the magic truly happens. It’s the perfect balance of tangy and sweet, with a burst of citrus flavor that will tantalize your taste buds. The filling is made using fresh lemon juice, lemon zest, sugar, and a touch of cornstarch to achieve that smooth, velvety texture. The key here is to use freshly squeezed lemon juice for the best flavor – it makes all the difference!

Once the filling is poured over the pre-baked shortbread crust, it’s time to bake the bars until the filling is set. The aroma of fresh lemon will fill your kitchen, building anticipation for the final touch – the lemon glaze.

The Zesty Lemon Glaze

To elevate the lemon bars to the next level, a simple yet delightful lemon glaze is drizzled over the top. This glaze is made with a combination of powdered sugar and fresh lemon juice, creating a sweet and tangy coating that perfectly complements the bars’ flavors. Once the bars have cooled slightly, the glaze is poured over the top, creating a beautiful, glossy finish that adds a burst of extra lemony goodness.

Serving and Storage

Once the lemon bars have been glazed and cooled, it’s time to cut them into delectable squares and sprinkle a touch of lemon zest over the top for a pop of color and an extra burst of citrus aroma. These bars are best enjoyed chilled, making them a refreshing treat on a warm day. They can be stored in the refrigerator for several days, but chances are they won’t last that long once you’ve had your first taste!

Final Thoughts

These Lemon Bars with a Shortbread Crust and a Lemon Glaze are the perfect combination of tangy, sweet, and refreshing flavors, making them an ideal dessert for any occasion. Whether you’re hosting a summer gathering, craving a zesty treat, or simply looking to indulge in a delightful dessert, these vegan lemon bars are sure to impress. So, roll up your sleeves, zest those lemons, and get ready to enjoy a slice of sunshine in every bite!
