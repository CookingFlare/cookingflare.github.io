Grilled Watermelon Salad: Refreshing and Tangy BBQ Side Dish


Are you looking for a unique and refreshing side dish to complement your summer BBQ? Look no further than this Grilled Watermelon Salad recipe! This vibrant and flavorful dish combines the smokiness of grilled watermelon with the tanginess of feta cheese and the freshness of mint to create a delightful explosion of flavors in every bite. Whether you’re hosting a backyard cookout or simply craving a light and refreshing salad, this recipe is sure to impress your taste buds and your guests.

Ingredients

  • 1 small seedless watermelon, sliced into 1-inch thick wedges
  • 1/4 cup extra virgin olive oil
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • 1 cup crumbled feta cheese
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh mint leaves
  • 1/4 cup balsamic glaze
  • 1/2 cup arugula

Instructions

  1. Preheat the Grill: Preheat your grill to medium-high heat. While the grill is heating up, brush the watermelon wedges with olive oil and season them with salt and black pepper.

  2. Grill the Watermelon: Place the seasoned watermelon wedges on the preheated grill and cook for about 2-3 minutes on each side, or until grill marks appear. Grilling the watermelon helps to enhance its natural sweetness and adds a subtle smoky flavor to the salad.

  3. Assemble the Salad: Once the watermelon wedges are grilled to perfection, transfer them to a cutting board and let them cool for a few minutes. Then, cut the watermelon into bite-sized pieces and place them in a large mixing bowl. Add the crumbled feta cheese, chopped mint leaves, and arugula to the bowl with the grilled watermelon.

  4. Drizzle with Balsamic Glaze: Drizzle the balsamic glaze over the salad ingredients. The sweet and tangy flavor of the balsamic glaze adds a delightful depth to the salad, perfectly complementing the grilled watermelon and feta cheese.

  5. Toss Gently: Gently toss all the ingredients together until the watermelon, feta, and mint are evenly distributed and coated with the balsamic glaze.

  6. Serve and Enjoy: Transfer the Grilled Watermelon Salad to a serving platter or individual plates. Garnish with a few fresh mint leaves for a pop of color and an extra burst of freshness. This salad is best enjoyed immediately to savor the contrast of warm grilled watermelon and cool, tangy feta.

Tips for Success

  • Choose a ripe, seedless watermelon for the best flavor and texture in this salad.
  • Don’t overcook the watermelon on the grill; a light charring and grill marks are all you need to enhance its natural sweetness.
  • Adjust the amount of feta cheese and mint to suit your taste preferences. Feel free to experiment with different types of cheese or herbs for a personalized twist on this recipe.

Conclusion

This Grilled Watermelon Salad is a celebration of summer flavors, combining the smokiness of grilled watermelon with the zing of feta cheese and the herbaceous freshness of mint. It’s a versatile side dish that pairs beautifully with grilled meats, seafood, or as a standalone light meal. Surprise your family and friends with this unexpected and delightful salad at your next BBQ gathering, and watch as it becomes a new summertime favorite. Cheers to enjoying the best of the season’s produce in a simple and delicious way!
