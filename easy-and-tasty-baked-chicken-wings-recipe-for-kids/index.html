Easy and Tasty Baked Chicken Wings Recipe for Kids


If you’re on the lookout for a delicious and hassle-free meal that your kids will love, then look no further than this easy and tasty baked chicken wings recipe. Not only is it a healthier alternative to fried chicken wings, but it’s also a versatile dish that can be enjoyed as a snack, lunch, or dinner. Let’s dive into the details of this mouthwatering recipe that will have your little ones asking for seconds.

Ingredients

  • 2 pounds of chicken wings
  • 2 tablespoons of olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon of garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon of paprika
  • 1 teaspoon of onion powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon of salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon of black pepper
  • 1/2 cup of barbecue sauce (optional for dipping)

Instructions

  1. Preheat the Oven: Start by preheating your oven to 400°F (200°C). This will ensure that your chicken wings cook evenly and develop a delicious crispy coating.

  2. Prepare the Chicken Wings: Rinse the chicken wings under cold water and pat them dry with paper towels. This step helps to remove any excess moisture, allowing the wings to crisp up nicely in the oven.

  3. Season the Wings: In a large bowl, drizzle the olive oil over the chicken wings and toss them to coat evenly. Then, sprinkle the garlic powder, paprika, onion powder, salt, and black pepper over the wings. Toss them again to ensure that each wing is thoroughly seasoned.

  4. Arrange on Baking Sheet: Place a wire rack on a baking sheet and arrange the seasoned chicken wings in a single layer. The wire rack allows the hot air to circulate around the wings, resulting in a crispy texture all over.

  5. Bake to Perfection: Pop the baking sheet into the preheated oven and bake the wings for 45-50 minutes, or until they are golden brown and crispy. Remember to flip the wings halfway through the cooking time to ensure even browning.

  6. Serve and Enjoy: Once the chicken wings are cooked to perfection, remove them from the oven and let them cool for a few minutes. You can serve them as is or with a side of barbecue sauce for dipping. Get ready to watch your kids devour these delectable baked chicken wings!

Tips for Success

  • For extra flavor, you can marinate the chicken wings in the seasoning mix for an hour before baking.
  • If your kids prefer a saucier finish, toss the baked wings in barbecue sauce before serving.
  • Feel free to customize the seasoning to suit your kids’ taste buds. You can experiment with different herbs and spices to create unique flavor profiles.

Final Thoughts

This easy and tasty baked chicken wings recipe is a surefire way to win over your kids’ hearts and taste buds. Not only is it a healthier alternative to fried wings, but it’s also a fun and versatile dish that can be enjoyed on various occasions. Whether it’s a quick after-school snack or a fun family dinner, these baked chicken wings are bound to be a hit. So, roll up your sleeves, preheat that oven, and get ready to delight your kids with this scrumptious treat!
