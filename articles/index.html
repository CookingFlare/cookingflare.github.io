Healthy and Hearty Lentil Soup: Nourishing Recipe

Looking for a delicious and nutritious soup to warm your soul? Look no further than this Healthy and Hearty Lentil Soup recipe! Packed with wholesome ingredients and bursting with flavors, this soup is not only satisfying but also incredibly nourishing. Whether you’re a seasoned cook or just starting out in the kitchen, this recipe is sure to become a favorite in your household. Ingredients 1 cup dried green lentils 1 onion, finely chopped 2 carrots, diced 2 celery stalks, diced 3 cloves of garlic, minced 1 can diced tomatoes 4 cups vegetable broth 1 teaspoon cumin 1 teaspoon paprika 1/2 teaspoon turmeric Salt and pepper to taste Fresh parsley, chopped (for garnish) Instructions Rinse the lentils under cold water and set them aside. In a large pot, heat a drizzle of olive oil over medium heat. Add the chopped onion, carrots, and celery. Sauté until the vegetables are tender, about 5 minutes. Add the minced garlic to the pot and cook for an additional minute, stirring constantly. Stir in the diced tomatoes, vegetable broth, cumin, paprika, turmeric, salt, and pepper. Bring the mixture to a boil. Once the soup is boiling, reduce the heat to low and add the rinsed lentils. Cover the pot and let the soup simmer for about 30-40 minutes, or until the lentils are tender. Remove the pot from the heat and let it cool slightly. Using an immersion blender or a regular blender, puree about half of the soup until smooth. This will give the soup a creamy texture while still leaving some chunky bits for added texture. Return the pot to the heat and simmer for an additional 5 minutes to allow the flavors to meld together. Serve the Healthy and Hearty Lentil Soup hot, garnished with a sprinkle of fresh parsley. Enjoy! Health Benefits of Lentil Soup Not only is this soup incredibly delicious, but it’s also packed with nutritional benefits. Lentils are an excellent source of plant-based protein, fiber, and essential vitamins and minerals. They are also low in fat and cholesterol, making them a great addition to a healthy diet. The vegetables in this soup add even more vitamins, minerals, and antioxidants, making it a truly nourishing meal. ...
