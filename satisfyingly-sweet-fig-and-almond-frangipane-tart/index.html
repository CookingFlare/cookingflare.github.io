Satisfyingly Sweet Fig and Almond Frangipane Tart


If you’re looking for a dessert that combines the delightful sweetness of figs with the rich nuttiness of almonds, then you’re in for a treat! Our Satisfyingly Sweet Fig and Almond Frangipane Tart is the perfect balance of flavors and textures, making it an absolute delight for your taste buds. With a buttery tart shell, a luscious almond frangipane filling, and beautiful fresh figs on top, this tart is sure to impress your family and friends.

Ingredients

For the tart shell:

  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, cold and cubed
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1 large egg yolk
  • 2 tablespoons ice water

For the almond frangipane filling:

  • 1 1/2 cups almond flour
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/4 teaspoon almond extract
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt

For the fig topping:

  • 10-12 fresh figs, sliced

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C).

  2. In a large mixing bowl, combine the all-purpose flour, granulated sugar, and salt for the tart shell. Add the cold cubed butter and use your fingertips or a pastry cutter to cut the butter into the flour mixture until it resembles coarse crumbs.

  3. In a small bowl, whisk together the egg yolk and ice water. Add this mixture to the flour mixture and gently knead until the dough comes together. Form the dough into a disk, wrap it in plastic wrap, and refrigerate for at least 30 minutes.

  4. While the dough is chilling, prepare the almond frangipane filling. In a separate mixing bowl, cream together the softened butter and granulated sugar until light and fluffy. Add the eggs one at a time, beating well after each addition. Stir in the vanilla extract, almond extract, almond flour, and salt until well combined.

  5. Remove the tart shell dough from the refrigerator and roll it out on a lightly floured surface to fit a 9-inch tart pan. Press the dough into the pan, making sure to evenly distribute it along the bottom and up the sides. Trim any excess dough.

  6. Spread the almond frangipane filling evenly over the tart shell.

  7. Arrange the sliced figs on top of the filling in a decorative pattern.

  8. Place the tart in the preheated oven and bake for 35-40 minutes, or until the crust is golden brown and the filling is set.

  9. Remove the tart from the oven and let it cool completely before serving.

  10. Slice and serve the Satisfyingly Sweet Fig and Almond Frangipane Tart as is, or add a dollop of whipped cream or a scoop of vanilla ice cream for an extra indulgence.

This tart is not only visually stunning but also a delightful combination of flavors and textures. The buttery tart shell provides a crisp and flaky base, while the almond frangipane filling adds a rich and creamy texture. The fresh figs on top bring a natural sweetness and a beautiful pop of color.

Whether you’re hosting a dinner party or simply want to treat yourself to a delicious dessert, this Satisfyingly Sweet Fig and Almond Frangipane Tart is sure to be a crowd-pleaser. So go ahead, give it a try, and savor every bite of this delectable creation. Happy baking!
