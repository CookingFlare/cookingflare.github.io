1. Zucchini Fritters

Indulge in delicate, golden zucchini fritters, kissed by a zesty hint of lemon, sprinkled with fragrant herbs, and enclosed in a crispy nest of cornmeal. The perfect finger food, they’re served with a vibrant, tangy tzatziki for a delightful twist!

Ingredients

  • 1 medium zucchini, grated
  • 2 corn tortillas, crumbled
  • 1/4 cup whole wheat pastry flour
  • 1/4 cup cornmeal
  • 2 tbsp. olive oil
  • 1/4 cup Greek yogurt
  • 1 small cucumber, diced
  • Juice of 1/2 lemon
  • 1/2 clove garlic, minced
  • 1/4 cup fresh dill, chopped

Instructions

  1. Squeeze grated zucchini to remove excess moisture.
  2. Mix zucchini, tortillas, flour, cornmeal, salt, and pepper in a bowl.
  3. Heat olive oil in a pan and cook fritters until golden brown.
  4. For tzatziki, blend Greek yogurt, cucumber, lemon juice, garlic, and dill.
  5. Serve warm fritters with tzatziki on the side.

2. Quinoa and Black Bean Stuffed Bell Peppers

A stellar source of protein and fiber, this satisfying dish showcases vibrant bell peppers, brimming with juicy chunks of tomatoes, protein-packed quinoa, and black beans, all spiced with smoky Cajun seasoning, and finished with fresh cilantro.

Ingredients

  • 4 bell peppers, halved and seeds removed
  • 2 cups cooked quinoa
  • 1 can black beans, rinsed and drained
  • 1 can diced tomatoes, drained
  • 3 tbsp. Cajun seasoning
  • 1 avocado, diced
  • 1/4 cup fresh cilantro, chopped

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 375째F (190째C).
  2. Mix quinoa, black beans, tomatoes, and Cajun seasoning in a bowl.
  3. Stuff bell peppers with the quinoa mixture, and place them in a baking dish.
  4. Bake for 30 minutes, until peppers are tender.
  5. Serve topped with diced avocado and fresh cilantro.

3. Eggplant Rollatini

A delightful fusion of tender sliced eggplant, creamy ricotta, aromatic basil, and a blanket of melted mozzarella, this comforting nut-free recipe is perfect to share with family and friends amidst quality time, laughter, and delectable conversations.

Ingredients

  • 2 large eggplants, sliced into 1/4 inch rounds
  • Salt
  • 2 cups ricotta cheese
  • 1/2 cup fresh basil, finely chopped
  • 1 egg, beaten
  • 3 cups marinara sauce
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese

Instructions

  1. Sprinkle sliced eggplants with salt, let them sit, and rinse them to remove bitterness.
  2. Preheat oven to 375째F (190째C).
  3. Combine ricotta, basil, and 1/2 cup of mozzarella.
  4. Spread marinara sauce in the bottom of a baking dish.
  5. Assemble rollatini by placing eggplant rounds on a flat surface, spreading ricotta mixture, and rolling them up.
  6. Dip each rollatini in beaten egg and place in the baking dish, seam-side down.
  7. Top with remaining marinara sauce and mozzarella.
  8. Bake for 30 minutes, until golden and bubbly.

4. Vegan Chocolate Avocado Mousse

This scrumptious, silky-smooth vegan chocolate mousse - packed with heart-healthy monounsaturated fats, antioxidants, and fiber-rich cocoa - is proof that healthy indulgence exists! Whip this delight up to please vegans and non-vegans alike, leaving behind a lingering blissful taste.

Ingredients

  • 2 ripe avocados, pitted
  • 1/2 cup unsweetened almond milk
  • 1/2 cup cocoa powder
  • 1/2 cup maple syrup
  • 1 tsp. vanilla extract

Instructions

  1. Scoop avocado flesh into a food processor, followed by almond milk, cocoa powder, maple syrup, and vanilla extract.
  2. Blend until smooth, and the desired consistency is reached.
  3. Chill the mousse in the refrigerator for 2 hours before serving, garnishing with a dusting of cocoa powder or fresh berries.
