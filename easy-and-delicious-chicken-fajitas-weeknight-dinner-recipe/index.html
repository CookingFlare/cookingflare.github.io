Easy and Delicious Chicken Fajitas: Weeknight Dinner Recipe


Are you tired of spending hours in the kitchen after a long day at work? Look no further! This Easy and Delicious Chicken Fajitas recipe is here to save the day. With a few simple ingredients and minimal prep time, you can have a scrumptious dinner on the table in no time. Let’s get cooking!

Ingredients

  • 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1 red bell pepper
  • 1 green bell pepper
  • 1 yellow bell pepper
  • 1 onion
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 teaspoons chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Tortillas, for serving
  • Optional toppings: shredded cheese, sour cream, guacamole, salsa

Instructions

  1. Start by thinly slicing the chicken breasts, bell peppers, and onion. The key to tender and flavorful fajitas is uniform slicing, so take your time and try to keep everything evenly sized.

  2. In a large skillet, heat the olive oil over medium-high heat. Add the sliced chicken and season with chili powder, cumin, paprika, garlic powder, salt, and pepper. Cook until the chicken is browned and cooked through, about 5-7 minutes. Remove the chicken from the skillet and set aside.

  3. In the same skillet, add the sliced bell peppers and onion. Season with salt and pepper. Sauté for 5-7 minutes, or until the vegetables are tender-crisp. You want them to have a bit of crunch, so be careful not to overcook.

  4. Once the vegetables are cooked, return the chicken to the skillet and toss everything together. This will allow the flavors to mingle and coat the chicken and vegetables evenly.

  5. Warm up your tortillas according to the package instructions. I prefer to heat them up in a dry skillet for a few seconds on each side, but you can also use a microwave or oven.

  6. Serve the chicken fajita mixture on warm tortillas and top with your favorite toppings. Shredded cheese, sour cream, guacamole, and salsa are all delicious options that add extra flavor and creaminess to your fajitas.

  7. Roll up the tortillas, tucking in the sides as you go, and enjoy your Easy and Delicious Chicken Fajitas!

Tips and Variations

  • Feel free to customize your fajitas by adding additional vegetables such as mushrooms, zucchini, or corn.
  • If you prefer a spicier kick, add some chopped jalapeños or a dash of hot sauce to the chicken while cooking.
  • To save time on busy weeknights, you can prepare the chicken and vegetables in advance and store them separately in airtight containers in the refrigerator. When ready to serve, simply reheat everything in a skillet until warmed through.
  • Leftover fajita mixture can be used in various ways. Try stuffing it into quesadillas, topping it on a salad, or even adding it to a breakfast omelet for a flavorful twist.

So there you have it, an Easy and Delicious Chicken Fajitas recipe that will become your go-to weeknight dinner. With its simple ingredients, quick preparation, and mouthwatering flavors, this dish is sure to satisfy your cravings without causing any stress in the kitchen. Give it a try tonight and enjoy a tasty meal that will leave everyone asking for seconds!
