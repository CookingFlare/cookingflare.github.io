Butternut Squash and Apple Pizza: A Creative Fall Twist


As the leaves begin to change and the air turns crisp, there’s no better way to embrace the flavors of fall than with a Butternut Squash and Apple Pizza. This unique twist on a classic favorite combines the seasonal sweetness of butternut squash and apple with savory, melty cheese and a hint of aromatic herbs. Whether you’re hosting a cozy gathering or simply craving a taste of autumn, this recipe is sure to delight your taste buds and impress your guests.

Ingredients

For the Pizza Dough:

  • 2 1/4 tsp active dry yeast
  • 1 1/2 cups warm water
  • 3 1/2 - 4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp honey

For the Toppings:

  • 1 small butternut squash, peeled and thinly sliced
  • 2 apples, cored and thinly sliced
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup crumbled goat cheese
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh sage
  • 1/4 cup chopped walnuts
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Pizza Dough:

    • In a small bowl, dissolve the yeast in warm water and let it sit for about 5 minutes until it becomes frothy.
    • In a large mixing bowl, combine 3 1/2 cups of flour, salt, and honey. Make a well in the center and add the yeast mixture and olive oil. Stir until a soft dough forms.
    • Turn the dough out onto a floured surface and knead for about 5-7 minutes, adding more flour as needed, until the dough is smooth and elastic. Place the dough in a greased bowl, cover with a clean kitchen towel, and let it rise in a warm place for about 1 hour, or until doubled in size.

  2. Prepare the Toppings:

    • While the dough is rising, preheat the oven to 400°F (200°C). Place the butternut squash and apple slices on a baking sheet, drizzle with olive oil, and sprinkle with salt and pepper. Roast for about 15-20 minutes, or until the edges start to caramelize. Remove from the oven and set aside.

  3. Assemble the Pizza:

    • Once the dough has risen, punch it down and divide it in half. Roll each half into a 12-inch circle and transfer to a lightly greased baking sheet.
    • Sprinkle the mozzarella cheese over the dough, leaving a small border around the edges. Arrange the roasted butternut squash and apple slices on top of the cheese. Crumble the goat cheese over the pizza and sprinkle with chopped sage and walnuts.

  4. Bake and Enjoy:

    • Bake the pizzas in the preheated oven for 15-20 minutes, or until the crust is golden and the cheese is bubbly.
    • Remove from the oven, let it cool for a few minutes, then slice and serve.

A Taste of Autumn

The Butternut Squash and Apple Pizza is a delightful blend of sweet and savory, capturing the essence of fall in every bite. The creamy mozzarella, tangy goat cheese, and earthy sage complement the natural sweetness of the roasted squash and apple, creating a symphony of flavors on a golden, crispy crust. This recipe is a testament to the versatility of seasonal produce and the endless possibilities of creative cooking.

Whether you’re a seasoned home chef or just starting your culinary journey, this Butternut Squash and Apple Pizza is a must-try for anyone looking to savor the flavors of fall in a unique and delicious way. So, gather your loved ones, turn on the oven, and let the aroma of autumn fill your kitchen as you embark on a culinary adventure that celebrates the bounty of the season. Cheers to good food, good company, and the simple joy of sharing a meal made with love.
