Healthy Nut-Free Zucchini Bread Recipe


Welcome to our kitchen! Today, we have a delicious and healthy recipe for you: Nut-Free Zucchini Bread. This recipe is perfect for those with nut allergies or anyone looking for a nutritious and tasty treat. Packed with fresh zucchini and other wholesome ingredients, this bread is a great way to sneak some veggies into your diet. So, let’s get baking!

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground nutmeg
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, melted
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/2 cup packed brown sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 1/2 cups grated zucchini
  • 1/2 cup plain Greek yogurt

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease and flour a 9x5-inch loaf pan.

  2. In a medium bowl, whisk together the flour, baking powder, baking soda, salt, cinnamon, and nutmeg. Set aside.

  3. In a large bowl, beat the eggs until light and fluffy. Add the melted butter, granulated sugar, brown sugar, and vanilla extract. Mix well to combine.

  4. Add the grated zucchini and Greek yogurt to the wet ingredients. Stir until well incorporated.

  5. Gradually add the dry ingredients to the wet ingredients, mixing just until combined. Be careful not to overmix, as this can result in a dense loaf.

  6. Pour the batter into the prepared loaf pan, smoothing the top with a spatula.

  7. Bake in the preheated oven for 55-60 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.

  8. Remove the bread from the oven and allow it to cool in the pan for 10 minutes. Then, transfer it to a wire rack to cool completely before slicing.

Tips and Variations

  • For added texture and flavor, you can mix in 1/2 cup of chocolate chips or chopped walnuts into the batter before baking.
  • If you prefer a sweeter bread, you can increase the amount of sugar to 3/4 cup or add a drizzle of honey or maple syrup on top.
  • To make this recipe vegan, you can substitute the eggs with flax eggs (1 tablespoon ground flaxseed mixed with 3 tablespoons water for each egg) and use vegan butter or coconut oil instead of regular butter.
  • This bread can be stored in an airtight container at room temperature for up to 5 days, or you can freeze it for longer storage.

Conclusion

Congratulations! You have successfully baked a delicious and healthy Nut-Free Zucchini Bread. It’s a versatile recipe that you can enjoy for breakfast, as a snack, or even as a dessert. With its moist texture and subtle sweetness, this bread is sure to become a favorite in your household. So, go ahead, slice a piece, and savor the goodness of this nutritious treat.
