Delicious Instant Pot Corn on the Cob Recipe


Corn on the cob is a classic summer dish that brings back memories of backyard barbecues and family gatherings. While traditionally cooked on the grill or boiled on the stovetop, using an Instant Pot can save you time and make the corn even more flavorful. In this recipe, we’ll show you how to make delicious Instant Pot corn on the cob that will have your taste buds dancing with joy.

Ingredients

  • Fresh corn on the cob
  • Water
  • Butter
  • Salt

Instructions

  1. Start by shucking the corn. Remove the outer husks and silks, ensuring that the corn is clean and ready for cooking.

  2. Place the trivet in the Instant Pot and pour in one cup of water. The trivet will keep the corn elevated from the water, allowing it to steam perfectly.

  3. Arrange the shucked corn on the trivet, making sure they fit comfortably without overcrowding the Instant Pot.

  4. Close the lid of the Instant Pot and set the valve to the sealing position. Select the “Manual” or “Pressure Cook” button and set the cooking time to 2 minutes for crisp-tender corn or 4 minutes for more tender corn.

  5. Once the cooking time is complete, allow for a natural release of pressure for 5 minutes. Then, carefully perform a quick release by turning the valve to the venting position. Be cautious of the hot steam.

  6. Open the Instant Pot and remove the corn using tongs or a slotted spoon. Place the corn on a platter or individual plates.

  7. Add a generous pat of butter to each ear of corn, allowing it to melt and coat the kernels. Sprinkle with salt to taste.

  8. Serve the delicious Instant Pot corn on the cob immediately while it’s still warm. Enjoy the sweet and succulent flavors of perfectly cooked corn.

Tips and Variations

  • For added flavor, you can experiment with different seasonings such as garlic powder, paprika, or chili powder. Sprinkle these spices on the corn after adding the butter and salt.

  • If you prefer your corn on the cob to have a slight char, you can place the cooked corn on a preheated grill for a few minutes. This will give it a smoky flavor and a lovely caramelized finish.

  • If you’re cooking for a crowd, you can easily double or triple the recipe. Just make sure not to overcrowd the Instant Pot and adjust the cooking time accordingly.

  • Don’t be afraid to get creative with toppings. Consider adding grated cheese, a squeeze of lime or lemon juice, or even a sprinkle of fresh herbs like parsley or cilantro.

  • Leftover corn can be stored in an airtight container in the refrigerator for up to 3 days. To reheat, simply microwave or steam until warm.

Now that you have this delicious Instant Pot corn on the cob recipe, you can enjoy this summer favorite anytime you want. Whether you’re hosting a backyard barbecue or simply craving a taste of nostalgia, this recipe is sure to satisfy. So grab some fresh corn, fire up your Instant Pot, and get ready to indulge in the sweet and juicy flavors of perfectly cooked corn on the cob.
