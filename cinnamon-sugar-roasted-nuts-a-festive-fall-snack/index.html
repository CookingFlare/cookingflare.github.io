Cinnamon Sugar Roasted Nuts: A Festive Fall Snack


As the air turns crisp and the leaves begin to change, it’s the perfect time to indulge in the warm and comforting flavors of fall. One delightful treat that embodies the essence of the season is cinnamon sugar roasted nuts. Whether you’re hosting a cozy gathering or simply craving a wholesome snack, these roasted nuts are a must-try for autumn.

Ingredients

  • 4 cups of mixed nuts (such as almonds, pecans, and walnuts)
  • 1 egg white
  • 1 tablespoon of water
  • 1 cup of granulated sugar
  • 1 teaspoon of ground cinnamon
  • 1/2 teaspoon of salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon of ground nutmeg
  • 1/4 teaspoon of ground cloves

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Nuts: Preheat your oven to 300°F (150°C). Line a baking sheet with parchment paper and set it aside. In a large bowl, whisk the egg white and water until frothy. Add the mixed nuts to the bowl and toss until they are evenly coated with the egg white mixture.

  2. Create the Cinnamon Sugar Coating: In a separate bowl, combine the granulated sugar, ground cinnamon, salt, nutmeg, and cloves. Mix well until all the spices are evenly distributed throughout the sugar.

  3. Coat the Nuts: Pour the cinnamon sugar mixture over the coated nuts and stir until all the nuts are thoroughly coated.

  4. Bake to Perfection: Spread the coated nuts in a single layer on the prepared baking sheet. Bake for 30-35 minutes, stirring every 10 minutes to ensure even roasting. The nuts are ready when they are golden brown and fragrant.

  5. Cool and Enjoy: Once the nuts are done, remove them from the oven and let them cool completely. As they cool, the cinnamon sugar coating will harden, creating a delightful crunch. Serve the nuts in a decorative bowl and watch them disappear in no time.

The Perfect Fall Snack

These cinnamon sugar roasted nuts are a delightful combination of sweet, spicy, and nutty flavors. They are not only a fantastic snack for casual munching but also a thoughtful homemade gift for friends and family. The warmth of cinnamon and the richness of nuts make this snack an ideal accompaniment to a cup of hot cider or a cozy evening by the fireplace.

Health Benefits of Nuts

Apart from being incredibly delicious, nuts are packed with essential nutrients. Almonds, for instance, are a great source of healthy fats, protein, and fiber. Pecans are rich in antioxidants and walnuts are known for their omega-3 fatty acids. When combined with the metabolism-boosting properties of cinnamon, these roasted nuts offer a guilt-free indulgence that supports overall well-being.

Serving Suggestions

These cinnamon sugar roasted nuts can be enjoyed in various ways. They make a charming addition to a cheese board, providing a delightful contrast to creamy cheeses. You can also incorporate them into your favorite salads for a crunchy texture and hint of sweetness. Furthermore, they can be used as a topping for yogurt or oatmeal, adding a burst of autumnal flavor to your breakfast routine.

Conclusion

In conclusion, cinnamon sugar roasted nuts are a quintessential fall snack that captures the warmth and coziness of the season. Whether you’re entertaining guests or simply pampering yourself, these nuts are sure to delight your taste buds and elevate your autumn experience. Embrace the spirit of fall with this simple yet irresistible recipe, and savor the magic of the season with every crunchy bite.
