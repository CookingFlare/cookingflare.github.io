Peach Raspberry Crisp: A Sweet and Juicy Dessert with a Crunchy Topping


If you’re on the lookout for a delightful dessert that celebrates the flavors of summer, look no further than this Peach Raspberry Crisp recipe. This delightful dish brings together the natural sweetness of ripe peaches and the tartness of fresh raspberries, all topped with a crunchy, buttery oat topping. Whether you’re hosting a summer gathering, craving a comforting treat, or simply want to make the most of seasonal fruits, this Peach Raspberry Crisp is the perfect choice.

Ingredients:

Filling:

  • 6 cups sliced fresh peaches
  • 2 cups fresh raspberries
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt

Topping:

  • 1 cup old-fashioned oats
  • 1/2 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup packed brown sugar
  • 1/4 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, melted

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the Oven: Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C) and grease a 9x13-inch baking dish.

  2. Prepare the Filling: In a large bowl, gently toss together the sliced peaches, raspberries, granulated sugar, flour, vanilla extract, cinnamon, and salt. Ensure that the fruit is evenly coated with the sugar and flour mixture.

  3. Make the Topping: In another bowl, combine the oats, flour, brown sugar, baking powder, and baking soda. Pour the melted butter over the oat mixture and stir until it resembles coarse crumbs.

  4. Assemble the Crisp: Transfer the fruit filling into the prepared baking dish and spread it out evenly. Sprinkle the oat topping over the fruit, covering it completely.

  5. Bake to Perfection: Place the baking dish in the preheated oven and bake for 35-40 minutes, or until the topping is golden brown and the fruit filling is bubbling around the edges.

  6. Serve and Enjoy: Once out of the oven, allow the Peach Raspberry Crisp to cool slightly before serving. This dessert is best enjoyed warm, perhaps with a scoop of vanilla ice cream or a dollop of whipped cream.

Tips and Variations:

  • To add an extra crunch to the topping, consider incorporating chopped nuts such as almonds or pecans.
  • For a hint of citrusy flavor, grate some lemon zest into the fruit filling before baking.
  • This recipe can be easily adapted to accommodate other seasonal fruits such as blueberries, blackberries, or even a combination of fruits for a mixed fruit crisp.

Final Thoughts:

Peach Raspberry Crisp is a versatile dessert that captures the essence of summer in every bite. The marriage of sweet peaches, tangy raspberries, and a buttery oat topping creates a symphony of flavors and textures that will leave your taste buds dancing. Whether you’re a seasoned baker or a novice in the kitchen, this recipe is a delightful way to showcase the bounty of the season and treat yourself to a truly satisfying dessert.

So, the next time you find yourself with an abundance of fresh peaches and raspberries, consider whipping up this Peach Raspberry Crisp and savoring the taste of summer in every spoonful. Cheers to indulging in the simple pleasures of life and enjoying the fruits of the season!
