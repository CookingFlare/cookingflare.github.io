Hearty Paleo Beef and Broccoli Stir-Fry


When it comes to quick and satisfying Paleo meals, a hearty beef and broccoli stir-fry is a staple that never disappoints. Packed with protein, vibrant vegetables, and wholesome flavors, this dish ticks all the boxes for a delicious and nutritious dinner. Best of all, it’s easy to prepare and perfect for busy weeknights when you crave something both wholesome and flavorful.

Ingredients

To create this savory Paleo beef and broccoli stir-fry, you’ll need:

  • Beef: Opt for a tender cut like sirloin or flank steak, thinly sliced against the grain.
  • Broccoli: Fresh broccoli florets that add crunch and vibrant color.
  • Coconut Aminos: A Paleo-friendly alternative to soy sauce, adding depth of flavor.
  • Garlic and Ginger: Freshly minced garlic and ginger for aromatic seasoning.
  • Arrowroot Starch: To thicken the stir-fry sauce without using cornstarch.
  • Beef Broth: Provides a savory base for the sauce.
  • Sesame Oil: Adds a nutty aroma and enhances the overall flavor profile.
  • Salt and Pepper: For seasoning to taste.
  • Optional: Red pepper flakes for a hint of heat, and green onions for garnish.

Instructions

Step 1: Prep Your Ingredients

Start by slicing your beef thinly against the grain. This ensures tenderness in every bite. Cut the broccoli into florets of similar size for even cooking. Mince the garlic and ginger finely to release their flavors during cooking.

Step 2: Prepare the Sauce

In a small bowl, mix coconut aminos, minced garlic, ginger, beef broth, and arrowroot starch. Whisk until the starch is dissolved and everything is well combined. This sauce will serve as the flavorful base for your stir-fry.

Step 3: Stir-Fry the Beef

Heat a large skillet or wok over medium-high heat. Add a drizzle of sesame oil and swirl to coat the pan. Add the sliced beef in a single layer and let it sear for about 1-2 minutes without stirring, allowing it to develop a nice caramelized crust. Then, stir-fry the beef for another 1-2 minutes until it’s cooked to your desired doneness. Remove the beef from the skillet and set it aside.

Step 4: Cook the Broccoli

In the same skillet, add a bit more sesame oil if needed. Toss in the broccoli florets and stir-fry for 3-4 minutes until they are crisp-tender but still bright green. Season lightly with salt and pepper to taste.

Step 5: Combine and Simmer

Return the cooked beef to the skillet with the broccoli. Give the prepared sauce a quick stir to recombine, then pour it over the beef and broccoli. Stir everything together gently to coat evenly in the sauce. Bring the mixture to a simmer and cook for another 2-3 minutes until the sauce has thickened slightly and everything is heated through.

Step 6: Serve and Enjoy

Once the beef and broccoli stir-fry is ready, remove it from the heat. Taste and adjust seasoning if needed. If you like a bit of heat, sprinkle with red pepper flakes. Serve the stir-fry hot, garnished with sliced green onions for a pop of color and freshness.

Tips for Success

  • Slice the Beef Thinly: This ensures that the beef cooks quickly and remains tender.
  • Don’t Overcook the Broccoli: Stir-fry just until it turns bright green and retains a slight crunch.
  • Customize to Your Taste: Feel free to add other vegetables like bell peppers or mushrooms for extra variety.
  • Serve Immediately: Enjoy the stir-fry right away while it’s hot and flavorful.

Why Choose Paleo Beef and Broccoli Stir-Fry?

This Paleo-friendly recipe not only satisfies cravings for Chinese takeout but also aligns with a Paleo lifestyle by avoiding grains, dairy, and processed ingredients. It’s a wholesome meal that provides a balance of protein, fiber, and essential nutrients from fresh vegetables and lean beef. Plus, making it at home allows you to control the quality of ingredients and tailor the flavors to your liking.

Whether you’re following a Paleo diet or simply looking to add more nutritious meals to your repertoire, this beef and broccoli stir-fry is a winner. It’s quick enough for a weeknight dinner yet impressive enough for entertaining guests. Give it a try and discover how delicious and satisfying Paleo cooking can be!

Enjoy this hearty Paleo beef and broccoli stir-fry as a wholesome dinner option that’s as delicious as it is nutritious. With simple ingredients and easy steps, it’s a recipe that will become a favorite in your kitchen.
