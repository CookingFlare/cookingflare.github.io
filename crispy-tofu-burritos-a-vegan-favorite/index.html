Crispy Tofu Burritos: A Vegan Favorite


When it comes to vegan cuisine, one dish that has gained immense popularity in recent years is the crispy tofu burrito. This flavorful and filling meal has captured the hearts of many, and for good reason. The combination of crispy tofu, savory spices, and soft tortilla wrapped around a flavorful filling is a match made in heaven. In this recipe, we’ll explore the world of crispy tofu burritos and provide you with a simple and delicious guide to making this vegan favorite at home.

Ingredients

  • 1 block of extra-firm tofu, drained and crumbled
  • 1/2 cup of cornstarch
  • 1/2 cup of vegetable oil
  • 1 small onion, diced
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 cup of mixed bell peppers, diced
  • 1 can of black beans, drained and rinsed
  • 1 teaspoon of cumin
  • 1 teaspoon of smoked paprika
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • 4 large flour tortillas
  • Shredded lettuce, diced tomatoes, and avocado, for garnish

Instructions

The first step in making crispy tofu burritos is to prepare the tofu. In a shallow dish, mix together the cornstarch and a pinch of salt. Cut the tofu into small cubes and toss them in the cornstarch mixture until they are evenly coated. This step is crucial in achieving the crispy exterior and tender interior of the tofu.

Heat the vegetable oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat. Once the oil is hot, add the tofu cubes and cook for about 3-4 minutes on each side, or until they are golden brown and crispy. Remove the tofu from the oil and set it aside on a paper towel-lined plate to drain any excess oil.

In the same skillet, add the diced onion and minced garlic and cook until the onion is translucent. Add the mixed bell peppers and cook until they are tender. Add the cumin, smoked paprika, salt, and pepper, and stir to combine.

Add the black beans to the skillet and stir to combine with the vegetables and spices. Cook for an additional 2-3 minutes, or until the filling is heated through.

Warm the flour tortillas by wrapping them in a damp paper towel and microwaving for 20-30 seconds. This will make them more pliable and easier to roll.

To assemble the burritos, lay a tortilla flat on a work surface and add a few tablespoons of the filling in the center of the tortilla. Add a few pieces of the crispy tofu on top of the filling, followed by any desired garnishes such as shredded lettuce, diced tomatoes, and avocado. Fold the bottom edge of the tortilla up over the filling, then fold in the sides and roll the burrito up tightly.

Tips and Variations

  • To add an extra layer of flavor to your burritos, try adding some diced jalapeños or serrano peppers to the filling.
  • For a spicy kick, add some sriracha or hot sauce to the filling.
  • Experiment with different types of tortillas, such as whole wheat or corn tortillas, for a different texture and flavor.
  • Add some chopped cilantro or scallions to the filling for an extra burst of flavor.

Conclusion

Crispy tofu burritos are a delicious and satisfying vegan meal that is easy to make and customize to your taste. With its crispy exterior and tender interior, this dish is sure to please even the most discerning palates. Whether you’re a seasoned vegan or just looking for a new recipe to try, this crispy tofu burrito recipe is a must-try. So go ahead, give it a try, and enjoy the delicious flavors and textures of this vegan favorite!
