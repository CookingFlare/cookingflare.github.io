Instant Pot Recipes for Winter: Hearty Meals to Keep You Warm


As the cold winter months settle in, there’s nothing more comforting than a warm, hearty meal to keep you cozy and satisfied. The Instant Pot has become a kitchen essential for busy families, home cooks, and those looking to simplify their meals while still delivering delicious results. This versatile appliance offers a quick and easy way to create comforting, flavorful dishes that are perfect for winter. Whether you’re craving a rich stew, a creamy soup, or a flavorful roast, the Instant Pot makes it all possible with minimal effort.

In this blog post, we’ll explore a variety of Instant Pot recipes that are perfect for winter, with hearty ingredients that provide both warmth and nourishment. From savory dishes to indulgent desserts, these recipes will help you make the most of your Instant Pot and enjoy satisfying meals throughout the colder months.

1. Instant Pot Beef Stew: A Classic Winter Comfort

Ingredients:

  • 2 lbs beef stew meat, cut into cubes
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 medium onion, diced
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 4 medium carrots, sliced
  • 4 medium potatoes, diced
  • 2 cups beef broth
  • 1 cup red wine (optional, for richer flavor)
  • 2 tablespoons tomato paste
  • 2 teaspoons dried thyme
  • 1 teaspoon rosemary
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • 1 bay leaf

Instructions:

  1. Sauté the Beef: Begin by setting your Instant Pot to the “Sauté” mode. Add olive oil and allow it to heat. Season the beef stew meat with salt and pepper, and brown the meat in batches for about 5 minutes until all sides are browned. Remove the beef from the pot and set it aside.

  2. Sauté Vegetables: In the same pot, add the diced onion and garlic. Sauté for 2-3 minutes until fragrant and translucent. Add the tomato paste, stirring to combine with the onions and garlic, then let it cook for another minute.

  3. Deglaze the Pot: Pour in the red wine (if using) or a bit of broth to deglaze the pot, scraping any brown bits from the bottom with a wooden spoon. This will add extra flavor to your stew.

  4. Pressure Cook: Return the beef to the pot and add the carrots, potatoes, beef broth, thyme, rosemary, and bay leaf. Stir to combine, then close the lid of the Instant Pot and set it to “Pressure Cook” for 35 minutes on high pressure.

  5. Release Pressure and Serve: Once the cooking time is up, allow the pressure to release naturally for 10 minutes before manually releasing any remaining pressure. Remove the bay leaf, taste for seasoning, and adjust with additional salt and pepper if needed. Serve hot with crusty bread.

Why This Recipe Works:

This beef stew is rich, flavorful, and hearty—perfect for warming up after a day in the cold. The Instant Pot makes it possible to achieve the same deep flavors of a slow-cooked stew in a fraction of the time, making it ideal for busy weeknights or a lazy weekend meal.

2. Instant Pot Chicken and Rice: A Cozy One-Pot Meal

Ingredients:

  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1 cup long-grain white rice
  • 2 cups chicken broth
  • 1 small onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • Fresh cilantro, chopped (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Sauté the Chicken: Set the Instant Pot to “Sauté” mode and add a drizzle of olive oil. Season the chicken breasts with salt and pepper, then brown them on both sides for about 3-4 minutes per side. Remove the chicken and set it aside.

  2. Cook the Aromatics: In the same pot, add the onion and garlic, sautéing for 2 minutes until fragrant. Add the turmeric, cumin, paprika, and cinnamon, stirring to toast the spices for 1 minute.

  3. Add Rice and Broth: Add the rice to the pot and stir it into the spices, allowing it to lightly toast for 2 minutes. Pour in the chicken broth and give everything a good mix.

  4. Pressure Cook: Place the browned chicken breasts back into the pot on top of the rice. Close the lid and set the Instant Pot to “Pressure Cook” on high for 10 minutes.

  5. Release Pressure and Serve: After the cooking time is complete, let the pressure release naturally for 5 minutes before manually releasing the rest of the pressure. Fluff the rice with a fork, shred the chicken with two forks, and stir it all together. Garnish with fresh cilantro, if desired, and serve immediately.

Why This Recipe Works:

Chicken and rice is a timeless comfort food, and this Instant Pot version makes it quick and easy to prepare. The combination of warm spices like turmeric and cinnamon infuses the rice with vibrant flavors, while the chicken remains juicy and tender. This dish is not only delicious but also filling, making it an ideal meal for winter nights.

3. Instant Pot Creamy Tomato Soup: A Classic with a Twist

Ingredients:

  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 1 medium onion, chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 cans (14.5 oz each) diced tomatoes
  • 1 cup vegetable broth
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • Salt and pepper, to taste

Instructions:

  1. Sauté the Aromatics: Set the Instant Pot to “Sauté” mode and add butter. Once melted, add the chopped onion and garlic, sautéing for about 3 minutes until the onions are softened and fragrant.

  2. Add Tomatoes and Broth: Pour in the diced tomatoes (with their juices) and vegetable broth. Add the sugar, basil, oregano, salt, and pepper, stirring to combine.

  3. Pressure Cook: Close the lid of the Instant Pot and set it to “Pressure Cook” on high for 10 minutes.

  4. Blend the Soup: After the cooking time is complete, use a quick release to release the pressure. Carefully open the lid and use an immersion blender to blend the soup until smooth and creamy. Alternatively, you can transfer the soup to a blender in batches.

  5. Add Cream: Stir in the heavy cream to create a velvety texture. Taste the soup and adjust the seasoning with more salt and pepper if needed.

  6. Serve: Ladle the soup into bowls and serve with grilled cheese sandwiches or a drizzle of olive oil for an extra touch of richness.

Why This Recipe Works:

Creamy tomato soup is a classic winter dish, and the Instant Pot helps you make it faster than traditional methods. The immersion blender gives it a smooth texture, while the addition of sugar balances the acidity of the tomatoes. Paired with a grilled cheese sandwich, this meal is the epitome of comfort.

4. Instant Pot Lentil Soup: A Hearty Vegetarian Option

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups dried lentils, rinsed and drained
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 medium onion, chopped
  • 2 carrots, diced
  • 2 celery stalks, chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon smoked paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric
  • 1/4 teaspoon cayenne pepper (optional)
  • 6 cups vegetable broth
  • 1 bay leaf
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • Fresh parsley, chopped (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Sauté the Vegetables: Set the Instant Pot to “Sauté” mode and add olive oil. Add the onion, carrots, celery, and garlic, sautéing for 5 minutes until the vegetables are softened.

  2. Add Spices: Sprinkle in the cumin, smoked paprika, turmeric, and cayenne (if using). Stir to coat the vegetables in the spices, allowing them to toast for 1-2 minutes.

  3. Add Lentils and Broth: Pour in the lentils, vegetable broth, and bay leaf. Stir to combine, then close the lid and set the Instant Pot to “Pressure Cook” on high for 15 minutes.

  4. Release Pressure and Serve: Once the cooking time is up, allow the pressure to release naturally for 10 minutes, then manually release any remaining pressure. Discard the bay leaf, season with salt and pepper, and garnish with fresh parsley if desired.

Why This Recipe Works:

Lentil soup is an excellent choice for a filling, nutritious winter meal. Packed with protein and fiber, lentils are an ideal base for a hearty, comforting soup. This recipe is not only vegan and vegetarian-friendly but also incredibly flavorful thanks to the combination of spices.

5. Instant Pot Apple Crisp: A Warm Winter Dessert

Ingredients:

  • 6 medium apples, peeled and sliced
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/4 cup brown sugar
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground nutmeg
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 1 cup rolled oats
  • 1/2 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 cup unsalted butter, cubed
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the Apples: In a bowl, toss the sliced apples with granulated sugar, brown sugar, cinnamon, nutmeg, and lemon juice. Set aside.

  2. Make the Crumble Topping: In a separate bowl, combine the oats, flour, butter, and salt. Use a pastry cutter or your hands to mix until the mixture resembles coarse crumbs.

  3. Layer the Apples: Place the seasoned apples into the Instant Pot and top with the crumble mixture.

  4. Cook the Crisp: Close the lid and set the Instant Pot to “Pressure Cook” on high for 8 minutes. Once the cooking time is up, quickly release the pressure.

  5. Serve: Serve the apple crisp warm with a scoop of vanilla ice cream or a dollop of whipped cream.

Why This Recipe Works:

Apple crisp is a comforting dessert that’s perfect for chilly winter evenings. The Instant Pot helps cook the apples until tender while keeping the topping crisp and crumbly. It’s a simple yet indulgent way to end a cozy winter meal.

Final Thoughts

The Instant Pot is a game-changer for anyone looking to prepare hearty winter meals quickly and easily. Whether you’re craving a warming stew, a comforting soup, or even a sweet dessert, the Instant Pot has you covered. These recipes not only warm you up but also provide the nutrients and flavors you need to thrive during the winter months. Try these dishes, and you’ll find yourself reaching for your Instant Pot all season long.
