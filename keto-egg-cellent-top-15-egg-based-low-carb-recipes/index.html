Keto Egg-cellent: Top 15 Egg-Based Low-Carb Recipes


Welcome, dear food aficionados and keto enthusiasts! Eggs are incredibly versatile and widely-used ingredients that boast a striking number of health benefits, making them the perfect staple for a ketogenic lifestyle. This guide brings you a magnificent list of egg-based mouthwatering dishes that will add variety to your low-carb culinary experience, helping you savor scrumptious meals without jeopardizing your carb intake.

1. Creamy Avocado Deviled Eggs

Prepare yourself for an exceptional deconstruction of traditional deviled eggs with a luscious twist - avocado! This healthy indulgence is bound to seduce your most refined taste buds.

Ingredients:

  • 6 hard-boiled eggs, halved and yolk further mashed
  • 1/2 ripe avocado
  • 2 Tablespoons mayo
  • 1 pinch of cayenne pepper
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Chives for garnish

Mash the egg yolks and soften the mashed avocado. Add the mayo, pepper, salt, and stir until you attain a smooth consistency. Garnish the egg halves with the mixture, and sprinkle some chives for an additional pop of flavor.

2. Spinach and Feta Stuffed Chicken

Feeling a bit adventurous? This dish will fulfill your wanderlust with its subtle Mediterranean flavors. Enjoy the harmonious symphony of spinach, feta cheese, and eggs wrapped in a protein-packed chicken breast.

Ingredients:

  • 4 boneless skinless chicken breasts
  • 1 cup spinach, chopped
  • 1/4 cup crumbled feta cheese
  • 2 egg whites
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C). Using a sharp knife, make a slit in each chicken breast to create a pocket for the stuffing without cutting through. In a mixing bowl, combine spinach, feta, and egg whites, stirring gently to mix well. Stuff this mixture into the chicken pockets, and then season with salt and pepper. Bake for 30-35 minutes until fully cooked, and serve with a side of sautéed vegetables for a well-rounded meal.

3. Low-Carb Egg Roll in a Bowl

Recreate the nostalgic flavors of your favorite Chinese takeout without exceeding your keto macros. This dish is comfort food at its best, packed with umami and bursting with colorful vegetables.

Ingredients:

  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon sesame oil
  • 1 small head of napa cabbage, shredded
  • 2 medium carrots, grated
  • 1 medium red bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 small onion, sliced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce or liquid aminos
  • 1 tablespoon oyster sauce
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 6 large eggs

Heat the olive oil and sesame oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Sauté onion, garlic, and cabbage until they soften, and add carrots, bell peppers, and seasoning. Simmer for about 10 minutes until vegetables are cooked to your desired level. Vigorously whisk the eggs in a bowl and pour them over the vegetables. Stir everything together gently until the eggs are cooked through. Serve hot, and enjoy the essence of an egg roll while keeping it low-carb.

4. Bacon and Cheddar Quiche

A cresendo to breakfasts or a light dinner, this warm quiche filled with smoky, savory bacon and sharp cheddar cheese offers the perfect balance for even the most discerning palates.

Ingredients:

  • 1 unbaked pie crust
  • 6 large eggs
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1 cup cheddar cheese, shredded
  • 6 slices of bacon, cooked and crumbled
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C). In a mixing bowl, whisk together the eggs, cream, salt, and pepper until well combined. Mix in the shredded cheddar cheese and crumbled bacon. Pour the mixture into the unbaked pie crust and smooth out the surface. Bake for 30-40 minutes or until the center is set. Allow it to cool slightly before slicing and savoring this tasty and satisfying dish.

5. Zucchini Noodles with Pesto and Poached Egg

Combine the goodness of garden-fresh zucchini noodles with the famous basil pesto and expertly poached egg for an exquisite, light, and satisfyingly low-carb meal.

Ingredients:

  • 4 medium zucchini, spiralized
  • 1/2 cup prepared basil pesto
  • 4 large eggs

Cook the zucchini noodles in a pot of boiling water for about 2 minutes, drain, and toss with pesto. Poach the eggs in simmering water (about 2-4 minutes) and serve them over the zucchini noodles. Season with salt and pepper, and, voilà! A tantalizing meal fit for a king.

6. Egg Fried Rice

Bored of the same ol’ keto meals? This unique spin on the infamous Chinese fried rice caters to your cravings for a warm, customizable, low-carb meal that is sure to satisfy any mm-m-m moments.

Ingredients:

  • 2 tablespoons coconut oil
  • 1/2 cup diced cauliflower
  • 2 green onions, sliced
  • 2 eggs, beaten
  • 1/2 cup frozen peas
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce (or liquid aminos)
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Heat the coconut oil in a skillet or wok over medium-high heat. Add the cauliflower, green onions, peas, and some salt to the pan, and cook until the cauliflower and peas are tender. Pour in the beaten eggs, stirring until the eggs scramble and integrate with the vegetables. Stir in the soy sauce and give everything a taste test, adjusting the seasoning as needed. Serve hot and enjoy this scrumptious, comforting low-carb meal.

7. Caprese Stuffed Tomatoes

Transport yourself to an Italian garden with this sumptuous, refreshing Caprese-stuffed tomato recipe. Light, savory, and filled with dollops of creamy mozzarella, it is a must-try for any keto adventurer.

Ingredients:

  • 4 medium-sized tomatoes, halved and seeded
  • 8 large egg whites
  • 1/2 cup mozzarella cheese, shredded
  • 1/4 cup fresh basil leaves, chopped
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C). In a large mixing bowl, * whisk* the egg whites until stiff peaks form. Gently fold in the shredded mozzarella, chopped basil, and a pinch of salt and pepper. Spoon the egg mixture gently into the tomato halves, filling up nearly to the top, and bake for 20-25 minutes until the eggs are cooked. Offer this delightfully tempting dish as an appetizer, side, or even a light meal.

8. Burger Bites with Guacamole

Transform your ordinary burger into an unforgettable masterpiece by substituting ground beef with juicy bites of hard-boiled eggs and tantalizing guacamole for an orthodox experience of indulgence.

Ingredients:

  • 6 medium-sized hard-boiled eggs, halved
  • 1 ripe avocado
  • 1/4 cup diced tomato
  • Juice from 1/2 lime
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1/2 jalapeno, seeded and minced (optional)
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh cilantro

For the guacamole: Mash the avocado in a bowl and mix in the tomato, lime juice, salt, pepper, jalapeno, and cilantro. Evenly spread the guacamole onto the halved hard-boiled egg whites. Top with a yolks, serve, and delight in these heavenly morsels.

9. Egg Drop Soup

Cozy up with this traditional Chinese comfort food that is reimagined and rendered keto-friendly. Its velvety broth, studded with flavorful eggs, will warm your heart and bring you joy in every spoonful.

Ingredients:

  • 6 cups chicken broth
  • 2 tablespoons cornstarch
  • 2 cups heavy cream or coconut milk
  • 6 large eggs, beaten
  • 2 green onions, sliced
  • Salt and pepper to taste

In a large pot, whisk together the cornstarch and chicken broth until thoroughly combined and smooth. Add the heavy cream or coconut milk next, stirring gently to incorporate. Slowly pour in the beaten eggs while gently stirring the broth. This will create a lovely and delicate custardy texture. Season with salt, pepper, and garnish with green onions. Allow the bowl to coo for a few minutes before serving to better enjoy the flavors melding together.

10. Breakfast Casserole

Indulge your inner carb-craver with this lavish and hearty breakfast casserole, replete with succulent sausage, tender zucchini, and scrumptious eggs to lull you into a sunny day’s start.

Ingredients:

  • 8 slices of bacon, cooked and crumbled
  • 1 cup cooked and crumbled sausage
  • 2 cups shredded zucchini
  • 1 cup heavy cream or half-and-half
  • 8 large eggs
  • 2 cups shredded cheddar cheese
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C). In a large bowl, combine the zucchini, sausage, bacon, heavy cream, eggs, and cheddar cheese. Pour the mixture into a greased casserole dish and give it a gentle stir. Bake for 45-50 minutes, or until the center is set, and the top is golden-brown. Let it sit for a few minutes before cutting and serving.

11. Keto Shakshuka

Rediscover the charm of the classic North African one-skillet marvel with a perfect low-carb twist! Egg-topped tomatoes and spices make for a mouthwatering main dish brimming with flavor and warmth.

Ingredients:

  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 small onion, diced
  • 1 Bell pepper, diced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 cups crushed tomatoes
  • 1/2 teaspoon paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon cumin
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh cilantro
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 6 large eggs

Heat the olive oil in a large oven-safe skillet over medium heat. Add the onion, bell pepper, and garlic, stirring occasionally until softened. Pour in the crushed tomatoes, paprika, cumin, salt, and pepper. Let the mixture simmer for 10-15 minutes until it thickens to your liking. Crack the eggs gently into the sauce, and either cover the skillet with its lid or transfer it to a preheated oven at 350°F (175°C) for about 8-10 minutes, until the egg whites are set. Garnish with chopped cilantro and serve with a side of veggie-laden sautéed greens for a nourishing, comprehensive meal.

12. Egg and Chorizo Tacos

Have a fiesta on your plate with these delightfully different Egg and Chorizo Tacos - a fiendish blend of smoky flavors, perfectly crunchy on the outside and tender within, all in delightful handheld form.

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 pound chorizo
  • 8 large eggs
  • 1 cup shredded lettuce
  • 1 avocado, diced
  • 1/2 white onion, sliced
  • 1 lime, juiced
  • Shredded cheese to taste
  • Taco shells, hard or soft, or lettuce wraps

cook the chorizo according to the package instructions. In a separate pan, scramble the eggs and season with salt and pepper to pass muster. In this order, layer the lettuce, scrambled eggs, and chorizo in each taco shell, topped with avocado, onion, cheese, and a squeeze of fresh lime juice. Tuck in, and taste Mexico in every bite.

13. Creamy Brussels Sprouts Gratin

Let indulgence know no bounds as you savor this scrumptious, oven-roasted Brussels sprouts gratin, smothered in a velvety cheese sauce spiked with eggs for a distinctly memorable low-carb meal.

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound Brussels sprouts, halved
  • 1 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream or coconut milk
  • 4 large eggs
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C). Toss the Brussels sprouts in a baking dish with a bit of olive oil, salt, and pepper. Bake for 20-25 minutes until the sprouts are tender and slightly browned. While they roast, blend together the heavy cream (or coconut milk), cheddar cheese, eggs, and Parmesan cheese. Once your Brussels sprouts are ready, pour this mixture over them and mix well. Bake for another 20-25 minutes or until the top is golden and set. Let it cool for a few minutes before digging into this cheerful and joyous gratin.

14. Mozzarella Stuffed Chicken Breast

Prepare to be wowed as you sink your teeth into these succulent chicken breasts stuffed to the brim with gooey mozzarella and marinated in a robust blend of garlic and herbs, clocking in at just 2g of net carbs per serving.

Ingredients:

  • 4 boneless skinless chicken breasts
  • 8 large egg whites
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1/4 cup olive oil

Whisk the egg whites in a shallow dish till frothy. In another dish, mix the mozzarella, minced garlic, dried oregano, dried basil, and olive oil. Slice a pocket in each chicken breast and stuff with cheese mixture. Dip the stuffed chicken in the egg whites and season both sides with salt and pepper. Heat a large pan over medium-high heat with some olive oil, and cook each chicken breast for about 4-5 minutes on each side. Poof - a mouthwatering masterpiece, fluffy and tender on the inside with a crispy, browned exterior.

15. Double Chocolate Avocado Mousse

Banish thoughts of deprivation as you embark on a chocolate journey like no other with this sinfully delicious mousse, proudly low-carb, and free of guilt.

Ingredients:

  • 2 ripe avocados
  • 1/4 cup cocoa powder
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 4 large egg yolks
  • 2 tablespoons unsweetened vanilla almond milk
  • 1/2 cup erythritol or Monk fruit sweetener (to taste)
  • Jackfruit coconut whipped cream (optional, for garnish)

Take the pit out of each ripe avocado and scoop out the flesh. In a food processor, blend the avocado, cocoa powder, and heavy cream into a smooth, velvety puree. In a separate bowl, beat the egg yolks, vanilla almond milk, and sweetener until thick and lemon-colored. Gradually stir the yolk mixture into the avocado mixture and blend until fully incorporated. Refrigerate for at least two hours before serving, and for that crowning touch of opulence, top each serving with a dollop of jackfruit coconut whipped cream.

Dive into these scrumptious creations, imaginative and stirring memories on taste buds that still yearn for delicious food without compromising your low-carb lifestyle. These recipes showcase the versatility of eggs while honoring the principles of a keto diet, ensuring that your stomach is always content and your commitment unwavering. Happy eating, and may every meal spark joy!
