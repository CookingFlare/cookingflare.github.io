Grilling Halloumi Skewers: Cheese Recipes for a Delicious BBQ


When it comes to grilling, most people think of juicy steaks, succulent burgers, and marinated chicken. But what about cheese? Yes, you read that right – cheese! Grilling halloumi skewers is a game-changer for your BBQ gatherings. This firm, salty cheese from Cyprus holds its shape when grilled, giving it a deliciously crispy exterior and a gooey, indulgent center. If you’re looking to add a vegetarian option or simply want to elevate your grilling game, these halloumi skewers are the way to go.

Choosing the Right Halloumi

Before we dive into the recipe, let’s talk about the star of the show – the halloumi cheese. When selecting halloumi for grilling, opt for a block that is firm and not too crumbly. This will ensure that it holds its shape on the grill and develops a beautiful golden crust while maintaining a creamy interior. Look for halloumi that is labeled as grilling or cooking halloumi for the best results.

Ingredients

Now, let’s gather the ingredients for these mouthwatering halloumi skewers. You will need:

  • 1 block of halloumi cheese
  • 1 red bell pepper
  • 1 yellow bell pepper
  • 1 zucchini
  • 1 red onion
  • Cherry tomatoes
  • Olive oil
  • Freshly ground black pepper
  • Fresh oregano (optional, for garnish)
  • Wooden skewers, soaked in water

Preparation

  1. Start by cutting the halloumi, bell peppers, zucchini, and red onion into evenly sized chunks. This will ensure even cooking and a harmonious blend of flavors.
  2. Thread the halloumi, bell peppers, zucchini, red onion, and cherry tomatoes onto the soaked wooden skewers, alternating the ingredients for a vibrant presentation.
  3. Brush the skewers with olive oil and sprinkle with freshly ground black pepper to add a hint of smokiness to the dish.

Grilling the Halloumi Skewers

Preheat the grill to medium-high heat and lightly oil the grates to prevent sticking. Once the grill is ready, place the halloumi skewers on the grates and let them cook for about 2-3 minutes on each side, or until they develop a golden-brown crust.

As the halloumi sizzles on the grill, its salty flavor intensifies, while the heat softens the cheese, resulting in a luxurious texture that contrasts beautifully with the charred exterior. The vegetables become tender and caramelized, offering a burst of sweetness with every bite.

Serving Suggestions

Once the halloumi skewers are perfectly grilled, remove them from the heat and let them rest for a minute. Sprinkle with fresh oregano for a pop of herbal fragrance and serve them alongside a refreshing Greek salad, warm pita bread, and a dollop of tzatziki for a complete Mediterranean-inspired feast.

Final Thoughts

Grilling halloumi skewers is a delightful departure from traditional BBQ fare. Whether you’re a cheese enthusiast or simply looking to diversify your grilling repertoire, these skewers are sure to impress. The combination of smoky halloumi and charred vegetables creates a symphony of flavors that will tantalize the taste buds of vegetarians and meat lovers alike. So, fire up the grill, gather your ingredients, and get ready to savor the irresistible charm of these grilled halloumi skewers at your next BBQ gathering. Cheers to cheese on the grill!
