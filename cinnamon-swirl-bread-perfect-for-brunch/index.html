Cinnamon Swirl Bread: Perfect for Brunch


There’s something truly comforting about the aroma of freshly baked bread wafting through the kitchen, especially when it’s infused with warm, fragrant cinnamon. Cinnamon swirl bread is a delightful treat that combines the richness of a homemade bread with the sweetness of cinnamon, making it a perfect addition to your brunch table. In this recipe, we will guide you through the steps to create your very own cinnamon swirl bread from scratch.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup warm milk
  • 2 1/4 tsp active dry yeast
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/3 cup unsalted butter, melted
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 2 eggs
  • 4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 tbsp ground cinnamon
  • 1/3 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/4 cup unsalted butter, softened

Instructions:

  1. Activate the Yeast: In a bowl, combine warm milk, yeast, and a pinch of sugar. Let it sit for about 5-10 minutes until foamy.

  2. Prepare the Dough: In a large mixing bowl, combine the activated yeast mixture, sugar, melted butter, salt, eggs, and flour. Knead the dough until it’s smooth and elastic. Let it rise in a greased bowl, covered, for about 1-2 hours until doubled in size.

  3. Make the Cinnamon Filling: Mix ground cinnamon, sugar, and softened butter in a small bowl until well combined.

  4. Shape the Bread: Roll out the risen dough into a rectangle. Spread the cinnamon filling evenly over the dough. Roll it up tightly from one end to the other to form a log.

  5. Bake the Bread: Place the rolled dough into a greased loaf pan. Cover and let it rise for another 30-45 minutes. Preheat your oven to 350°F (180°C). Bake the bread for 30-35 minutes until golden brown.

  6. Enjoy Your Cinnamon Swirl Bread: Once baked, let the bread cool before slicing. Serve slices of this delightful cinnamon swirl bread with a dollop of butter or a drizzle of honey, and enjoy its warm, sweet flavors with a cup of coffee or tea.

Why Cinnamon Swirl Bread is Perfect for Brunch:

Cinnamon swirl bread is the ideal addition to any brunch spread for several reasons. Its sweet and aromatic flavors complement a variety of brunch dishes, from savory egg bakes to fresh fruit salads. The soft, pillowy texture of the bread makes it a versatile option that can be enjoyed on its own or used to create delicious French toast or bread pudding.

Whether you’re hosting a brunch gathering or simply treating yourself to a leisurely weekend breakfast, cinnamon swirl bread adds a touch of homemade warmth and comfort to the table. The process of baking this bread from scratch is not only rewarding but also allows you to customize the sweetness and cinnamon intensity to suit your preferences.

So, the next time you’re planning a brunch affair or looking to elevate your breakfast routine, consider baking a loaf of cinnamon swirl bread. With its irresistible smell, soft texture, and delightful taste, this homemade bread is sure to become a brunch favorite in your household.

Embrace the joy of baking and savor the simple pleasures of a freshly baked slice of cinnamon swirl bread. Happy brunching!
