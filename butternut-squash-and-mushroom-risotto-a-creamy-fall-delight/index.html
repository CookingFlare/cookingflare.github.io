Butternut Squash and Mushroom Risotto: A Creamy Fall Delight


There’s something magical about fall - the crisp air, the vibrant colors, and the cozy feeling that comes with the changing season. And what better way to embrace the flavors of fall than with a warm and comforting bowl of Butternut Squash and Mushroom Risotto? This creamy dish is the epitome of comfort food, with a rich and velvety texture that will leave you craving more.

Ingredients

  • 1 butternut squash, peeled, seeded, and cut into small cubes
  • 1 cup mushrooms, sliced
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 1/2 cups Arborio rice
  • 4 cups vegetable broth
  • 1/2 cup dry white wine
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley, chopped (for garnish)

Instructions

  1. Heat the vegetable broth in a saucepan and keep it warm over low heat.
  2. In a large skillet, heat the olive oil and butter over medium heat. Add the chopped onion and minced garlic, and sauté until the onion becomes translucent.
  3. Add the Arborio rice to the skillet and stir it around for a minute or two, allowing the grains to become coated with the oil and butter mixture.
  4. Pour in the white wine and stir until it is absorbed by the rice.
  5. Begin adding the warm vegetable broth, one ladleful at a time, stirring constantly. Allow each ladleful to be absorbed by the rice before adding the next. This process will take approximately 20 minutes.
  6. While the risotto is cooking, preheat the oven to 400°F (200°C). Place the cubed butternut squash on a baking sheet, drizzle with olive oil, and season with salt and pepper. Roast in the oven for about 20 minutes or until the squash is tender and slightly caramelized.
  7. In a separate pan, sauté the sliced mushrooms until they are golden brown and slightly crispy.
  8. Once the risotto is creamy and the rice is al dente, remove it from the heat and stir in the grated Parmesan cheese. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  9. Gently fold in the roasted butternut squash and sautéed mushrooms, reserving a few for garnish.
  10. Serve the Butternut Squash and Mushroom Risotto in bowls, garnished with a sprinkle of fresh parsley and the reserved roasted squash and mushrooms.

Enjoy the Flavors of Fall

This Butternut Squash and Mushroom Risotto is a delightful combination of flavors, textures, and colors. The creamy rice, tender butternut squash, and earthy mushrooms come together to create a dish that is both comforting and sophisticated. The process of making risotto may be time-consuming, but the end result is well worth the effort.

Whether you’re hosting a fall dinner party or simply looking for a cozy meal on a chilly evening, this Butternut Squash and Mushroom Risotto will not disappoint. It’s a dish that celebrates the flavors of the season and brings warmth to the table.

So, grab a glass of wine, put on your favorite sweater, and savor every spoonful of this creamy fall delight. It’s time to embrace the flavors of autumn and indulge in the comfort of Butternut Squash and Mushroom Risotto.
