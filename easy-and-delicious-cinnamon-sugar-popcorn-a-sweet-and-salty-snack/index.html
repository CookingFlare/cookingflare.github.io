Easy and Delicious Cinnamon Sugar Popcorn: A Sweet and Salty Snack


Popcorn is a classic snack that can be enjoyed in many different ways. Whether you’re watching a movie, hosting a party, or simply craving a tasty treat, popcorn always hits the spot. And if you’re looking to take your popcorn game to the next level, look no further than this easy and delicious recipe for Cinnamon Sugar Popcorn!

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup popcorn kernels
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1/4 cup unsalted butter
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt

Instructions

  1. In a large pot with a tight-fitting lid, heat the vegetable oil over medium-high heat. Add the popcorn kernels and cover the pot with the lid. Shake the pot gently to coat the kernels with oil.

  2. Once the popcorn starts popping, continue shaking the pot occasionally to prevent burning. When the popping slows down to about 2 seconds between pops, remove the pot from heat and let it sit for a minute to allow any remaining kernels to pop.

  3. While the popcorn is popping, melt the butter in a small saucepan over medium heat. Once melted, remove the saucepan from heat and set it aside.

  4. In a small bowl, combine the granulated sugar, ground cinnamon, and salt. Mix well to ensure that the cinnamon and salt are evenly distributed throughout the sugar.

  5. Pour the melted butter over the popped popcorn, using a spatula or wooden spoon to evenly coat the kernels. Sprinkle the cinnamon sugar mixture over the popcorn, again using the spatula or spoon to distribute it evenly.

  6. Toss the popcorn gently to ensure that every kernel is coated with the cinnamon sugar mixture. You can use your hands or two spoons to carefully mix the popcorn, being mindful not to crush the delicate kernels.

  7. Allow the popcorn to cool for a few minutes before serving. This will give the cinnamon sugar coating a chance to set and adhere to the popcorn.

  8. Serve the Easy and Delicious Cinnamon Sugar Popcorn in a large bowl or individual serving bags. Enjoy the perfect combination of sweet and salty flavors in every bite!

Tips and Variations

  • For an extra kick of flavor, add a pinch of cayenne pepper to the cinnamon sugar mixture. This will give your popcorn a subtle spicy twist.

  • If you prefer a more caramelized coating, you can bake the popcorn in the oven at 250°F (120°C) for about 10 minutes after tossing it with the cinnamon sugar mixture. Just make sure to spread the popcorn in a single layer on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper before baking.

  • Experiment with different spices to create your own unique flavor combinations. Try adding a dash of nutmeg, ginger, or even cardamom to the cinnamon sugar mixture for a delightful twist.

  • If you’re hosting a party or movie night, consider serving this Easy and Delicious Cinnamon Sugar Popcorn alongside other popcorn flavors like buttered, cheesy, or even spicy. It will make for a fun and tasty popcorn bar experience!

So there you have it – a simple and irresistible recipe for Easy and Delicious Cinnamon Sugar Popcorn. It’s the perfect snack for any occasion, combining the comforting flavors of cinnamon and sugar with the satisfying crunch of popcorn. Give it a try and watch it disappear before your eyes!
