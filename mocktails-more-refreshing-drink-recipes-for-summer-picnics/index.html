Mocktails & More: Refreshing Drink Recipes for Summer Picnics


Summer picnics are all about enjoying the warm weather, good company, and refreshing beverages. While alcoholic drinks may be common during these gatherings, they aren’t for everyone. For those who prefer a non-alcoholic option or those entertaining young children, mocktails can be a delightful choice. In this post, we present a variety of delicious and unique mocktail recipes that will be the star of your summer picnic.

Sunset Spritzer

Serves 4

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup cranberry juice
  • 1/2 cup fresh orange juice
  • 1/4 cup simple syrup
  • 1/4 cup sparkling water
  • 1 lemon, thinly sliced
  • 4 sprigs fresh mint leaves

Instructions:

  1. In a large pitcher, combine cranberry juice, fresh orange juice, and simple syrup.
  2. Stir well to incorporate all the ingredients.
  3. Add ice and sparkling water to the pitcher.
  4. To serve, fill glasses with ice, and pour in Sunset Spritzer mixture.
  5. Garnish each glass with a slice of lemon and mint leaves.

Berry Bliss

Serves 6

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups fresh strawberries, hulled and sliced
  • 1 cup blueberries
  • 1 cup freshly squeezed pineapple juice
  • 1 cup orange juice
  • 1 cup club soda
  • 2 tablespoons honey
  • 1 lemon, lemon zest, and juice
  • 4 sprigs fresh mint leaves

Instructions:

  1. In a blender, combine strawberries, blueberries, pineapples, orange juice, honey, lemon zest, and juice.
  2. Blend until smooth.
  3. Strain the mixture through a fine-mesh sieve, pressing on the solids to extract as much liquid as possible.
  4. In a large pitcher, combine the strained juice with club soda.
  5. Stir gently to combine.
  6. To serve, pour over ice in glasses and garnish each glass with fresh mint leaves and a slice of lemon.

Sparkling Peach Tea

Serves 4

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 cup fresh peach nectar
  • 1/2 cup freshly brewed tea ( prefers peach or jasmine)
  • 1/4 cup lemon juice
  • 1/4 cup simple syrup
  • 3 cups club soda
  • 1 peach, sliced for garnish
  • 4 sprigs fresh mint leaves

Instructions:

  1. In a pitcher, combine peach nectar, brewed tea, lemon juice, and simple syrup.
  2. Stir to combine.
  3. Add ice and club soda to the pitcher.
  4. To serve, fill glasses with ice, and pour in sparkling peach tea mixture.
  5. Garnish each glass with a slice of peach and mint leaves.

Fruit Fusion Smoothie

Serves 2

Ingredients:

  • 2 ripe bananas, sliced
  • 2 cups plain Greek yogurt
  • 1 cup diced strawberries
  • 1 cup pineapple chunks
  • 1 cup fresh mango chunks
  • 1/2 cup fresh orange juice
  • 1/4 cup honey
  • 1 cup milk
  • 8-10 ice cubes

Instructions:

  1. In a blender, combine bananas, Greek yogurt, strawberries, pineapple, mango, orange juice, honey, and milk.
  2. Blend until smooth.
  3. Add ice cubes to the blender and blend again.
  4. Divide the smoothie among 2 glasses.
  5. Serve chilled and enjoy!

These mocktail recipes will be the perfect addition to any summer picnic. They’re refreshing, tasty, and a healthy alternative to traditional alcoholic beverages. So, gather your friends and family, and enjoy a fun and tipsy-free summer getaway. Cheers!
