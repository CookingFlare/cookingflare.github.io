Vegan Peanut Butter and Jelly Bars with a Graham Cracker Crust and a Sprinkle of Peanut Butter Chips Recipe: A No-Bake Twist on the Classic Sandwich

If you’re a fan of the classic peanut butter and jelly sandwich, then you’re in for a treat with this no-bake twist on the timeless favorite. These Vegan Peanut Butter and Jelly Bars with a Graham Cracker Crust and a Sprinkle of Peanut Butter Chips are a delightful combination of sweet, nutty, and fruity flavors, all wrapped up in a convenient and easy-to-make bar. Ingredients: For the Crust: 2 cups vegan graham cracker crumbs 1/2 cup coconut oil, melted 1/4 cup maple syrup For the Filling: 1 1/2 cups creamy peanut butter 1/2 cup coconut oil, melted 1/2 cup maple syrup 1 teaspoon vanilla extract 1/2 cup your favorite vegan jelly or jam For the Topping: 1/2 cup vegan peanut butter chips Instructions: Step 1: Prepare the Crust In a mixing bowl, combine the graham cracker crumbs, melted coconut oil, and maple syrup. Press the mixture firmly into the bottom of a parchment-lined 9x9 inch baking dish. Place the dish in the refrigerator to chill while preparing the filling. Step 2: Make the Filling In a mixing bowl, whisk together the creamy peanut butter, melted coconut oil, maple syrup, and vanilla extract until smooth and well combined. Pour the peanut butter filling over the chilled crust and spread it evenly with a spatula. Gently warm the vegan jelly or jam in a small saucepan over low heat until it becomes easier to drizzle. Drizzle the jelly or jam over the peanut butter filling in a decorative pattern. Step 3: Add the Topping Sprinkle the vegan peanut butter chips over the top of the bars, gently pressing them into the filling. Place the bars in the refrigerator and chill for at least 2 hours, or until set. Step 4: Serve and Enjoy Once the bars are firm, remove them from the baking dish using the parchment paper overhang. Cut the bars into squares or rectangles and serve. Store any leftovers in the refrigerator for up to one week. The creamy peanut butter filling pairs perfectly with the fruity sweetness of the jelly, all nestled on a buttery graham cracker crust.
