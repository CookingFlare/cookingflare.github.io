Homemade Chicken and Waffle Sliders: Perfect for Brunch


Who doesn’t love a delicious brunch? It’s the perfect opportunity to indulge in a mouthwatering meal that combines the best of breakfast and lunch. And if you’re looking to add a unique twist to your brunch menu, look no further than these Homemade Chicken and Waffle Sliders. This delightful combination of savory chicken and fluffy waffles is sure to impress your friends and family. So, let’s get cooking!

Ingredients

For the Chicken:

  • 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1 cup buttermilk
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • Vegetable oil for frying

For the Waffles:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • 1 tablespoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 3/4 cups milk
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, melted

For the Sliders:

  • Maple syrup
  • Coleslaw
  • Pickles

Instructions

Step 1: Prepare the Chicken

  1. In a bowl, combine the buttermilk, paprika, garlic powder, salt, and pepper. Place the chicken breasts in the mixture and let them marinate for at least 30 minutes.
  2. In a separate bowl, mix the flour with salt and pepper.
  3. Heat vegetable oil in a frying pan over medium-high heat.
  4. Remove the chicken from the buttermilk mixture, allowing any excess liquid to drip off.
  5. Dredge the chicken in the flour mixture, making sure to coat it evenly.
  6. Carefully place the chicken in the hot oil and cook until golden brown and cooked through, about 5-6 minutes per side.
  7. Once cooked, transfer the chicken to a paper towel-lined plate to drain excess oil.

Step 2: Make the Waffles

  1. Preheat your waffle iron according to the manufacturer’s instructions.
  2. In a large bowl, whisk together the flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt.
  3. In a separate bowl, beat the eggs and then add the milk and melted butter. Whisk until well combined.
  4. Pour the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients and stir until just combined. Be careful not to overmix; a few lumps are okay.
  5. Grease the waffle iron with cooking spray or melted butter.
  6. Pour the batter onto the hot waffle iron, close the lid, and cook until golden brown and crispy.
  7. Repeat until all the batter is used, keeping the cooked waffles warm in a low oven.

Step 3: Assemble the Sliders

  1. Cut the chicken breasts into smaller pieces, approximately the size of the waffles.
  2. Take one waffle and place a piece of chicken on top.
  3. Drizzle some maple syrup over the chicken.
  4. Add a spoonful of coleslaw on top of the chicken.
  5. Place a pickle slice on top of the coleslaw.
  6. Top with another waffle to create a sandwich-like slider.
  7. Repeat with the remaining chicken and waffles.

Step 4: Serve and Enjoy!

Homemade Chicken and Waffle Sliders are best served warm, so make sure to enjoy them right away. You can serve them as a main dish for brunch or cut them into smaller pieces for a delightful appetizer. The combination of crispy chicken, fluffy waffles, tangy coleslaw, and sweet maple syrup is a flavor explosion that will leave your taste buds wanting more. So, gather your loved ones, whip up a batch of these sliders, and indulge in a brunch experience like no other.

That’s it! You’ve just learned how to make Homemade Chicken and Waffle Sliders, a perfect dish for your next brunch gathering. Impress your guests with this unique and delicious combination that is guaranteed to satisfy their cravings. So, what are you waiting for? Get cooking and enjoy a brunch to remember!
