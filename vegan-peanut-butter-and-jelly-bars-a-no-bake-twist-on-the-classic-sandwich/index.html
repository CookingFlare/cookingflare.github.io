Vegan Peanut Butter and Jelly Bars: A No-Bake Twist on the Classic Sandwich


Are you a fan of the timeless combination of peanut butter and jelly? If so, you’re in for a treat! In this recipe, we’re giving the classic PB&J sandwich a delicious twist by transforming it into Vegan Peanut Butter and Jelly Bars. The best part? These bars are completely no-bake, making them a quick and easy treat to satisfy your cravings.

Ingredients

For the base:

  • 2 cups of rolled oats
  • 1 cup of pitted dates
  • 1 cup of roasted peanuts
  • 1/4 cup of maple syrup
  • 1/4 cup of peanut butter
  • 1/4 teaspoon of salt

For the peanut butter layer:

  • 1 cup of peanut butter
  • 1/4 cup of maple syrup
  • 1/4 cup of coconut oil, melted
  • 1/2 teaspoon of vanilla extract

For the jelly layer:

  • 1 cup of your favorite fruit preserves or jam

Instructions

Step 1: Prepare the Base

  1. In a food processor, combine the rolled oats, pitted dates, roasted peanuts, maple syrup, peanut butter, and salt. Pulse until the mixture is well combined and starts to stick together.

  2. Line a square baking pan with parchment paper. Transfer the mixture into the pan and press it down firmly to create an even layer. Place the pan in the refrigerator to chill while you prepare the remaining layers.

Step 2: Make the Peanut Butter Layer

  1. In a medium bowl, whisk together the peanut butter, maple syrup, melted coconut oil, and vanilla extract until smooth and well combined.

  2. Remove the pan from the refrigerator and pour the peanut butter mixture over the chilled base. Use a spatula to spread it evenly.

Step 3: Add the Jelly Layer

  1. Spoon the fruit preserves or jam over the peanut butter layer. Use a spatula to spread it out evenly, covering the entire surface.

Step 4: Chill and Serve

  1. Place the pan back in the refrigerator and let it chill for at least 2 hours, or until the bars are firm and set.

  2. Once the bars are chilled, remove them from the pan using the parchment paper as handles. Cut them into squares or rectangles, and they’re ready to be enjoyed!

Tips and Variations

  • If you prefer a smoother texture, you can process the oats and peanuts separately before adding them to the mixture.

  • Feel free to get creative with the jelly layer by using different flavors of fruit preserves or jam. Strawberry, raspberry, or even mixed berry would all be delicious choices.

  • For an extra touch of sweetness, sprinkle some powdered sugar on top of the bars before serving.

  • These bars can be stored in an airtight container in the refrigerator for up to a week, making them a perfect make-ahead snack or dessert.

Conclusion

With their irresistible combination of rich peanut butter, sweet jelly, and chewy oat base, these Vegan Peanut Butter and Jelly Bars are sure to become a favorite treat for vegans and non-vegans alike. The best part? They require no baking, making them a quick and easy option for satisfying your sweet tooth. Give this recipe a try and indulge in the nostalgic flavors of a classic PB&J sandwich, in a whole new way!
