BBQ Chickpea Stuffed Bell Peppers: A Protein-Packed Dish


Barbeque Chickpea Stuffed Bell Peppers are not only a delightful twist on a classic, but they’re also a fantastic source of protein. This succulent meal provides an enticing combination of hearty flavors and a multitude of nutrients. Plus, it’s a plant-based dish that won’t compromise on taste! By experimenting with ingredients and spices, you’ll achieve a delicious and satisfying outcome. So, let’s embark on this culinary journey and create protein-packed bell peppers.

INGREDIENTS

For the filling:

  • 2 cans of chickpeas, drained and rinsed
  • 1 red onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 tbsp of olive oil
  • 1 tbsp of smoked paprika
  • 1 tsp of ground cumin
  • 1 tsp of salt
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper
  • 1/2 tsp of hot sauce
  • 1/4 tsp of seasoned salt (e.g., garlic powder, onion powder)
  • 1/4 cup of barbecue sauce
  • Chopped fresh cilantro, for garnish

For the bell peppers:

  • 4 large bell peppers, any color
  • Salt for the water
  • 1 lb of your favorite barbeque sauce

DIRECTIONS

Prepare the filling:

  1. Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Add the chopped onion and sauté until softened and translucent, about 5-7 minutes.
  2. Incorporate the minced garlic and cook for an additional 1-2 minutes.
  3. Combine the chickpeas, smoked paprika, ground cumin, salt, black pepper, hot sauce, seasoned salt, and barbecue sauce. Mix well, ensuring all components are equally distributed.
  4. Cook the mixture in the skillet for approximately 10-15 minutes, stirring occasionally, until the chickpeas are heated through and the flavors meld.
  5. Turn off the heat and mix in chopped cilantro.

Prepare the bell peppers:

  1. Rinse the bell peppers and remove the tops, seeds, and membranes. Make sure they are well-drained.
  2. Fill a large pot with water and add a generous pinch of salt. Boil the bell peppers for about 5-8 minutes until they’re slightly tender but retaining their structure. Drain the peppers and set aside.
  3. Preheat your barbeque grill, griddle, or oven to medium-high heat.

Assembly and BBQ:

  1. Place each drained bell pepper into a foil packet with a few inches of space. Place the packets on the grill or griddle to avoid direct flame.
  2. Spoon the chickpea mixture into each bell pepper, pressing gently to create a compact and flush fit.
  3. Drizzle barbecue sauce over each pepper and fold the packets, securely sealing them with a little extra foil or adhesive metal bands.
  4. Close the grill lid or cover the peppers with aluminum foil and allow them to cook undisturbed for about 20-25 minutes.
  5. Open the packets carefully, allowing steam to escape. Garnish your barbeque chickpea stuffed bell peppers with chopped cilantro. Optionally, you can serve with additional barbecue sauce or plain yogurt.

TIPS & VARIATIONS:

When stuffing the bell peppers, pat the chickpea mixture into the pepper to reduce air pockets and ensure an even, compact fit. This will prevent leaks and help retain flavors. Feel free to experiment and modify the filling with any other protein sources like quinoa or lentils. Additionally, utilize various barbeque sauces and spice combinations to suit personal preferences.

A NUTRITIOUS AND DELICIOUS PROTEIN-PACKED DISH

Barbeque Chickpea Stuffed Bell Peppers offer a delightful balance of textures and flavors for a wholesome meal. The combination of hearty bell peppers, flavorful chickpeas, and succulent barbeque sauce creates a protein-packed culinary experience. Incorporating a variety of ingredients and spices not only stimulates taste buds but provides numerous health benefits. So, create this chili-laden dish, and delight in its quintessential goodness!
