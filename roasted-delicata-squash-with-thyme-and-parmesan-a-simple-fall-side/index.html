Roasted Delicata Squash with Thyme and Parmesan: A Simple Fall Side


When fall arrives, there’s nothing quite like the comforting aroma of roasted vegetables filling the kitchen. One of the season’s most delightful offerings is delicata squash. Its creamy texture and sweet, nutty flavor make it a versatile ingredient that pairs beautifully with a variety of herbs and seasonings. Today, we’re sharing a simple and delicious recipe for Roasted Delicata Squash with Thyme and Parmesan that will elevate any fall meal.

Ingredients:

  • 2 medium delicata squash, halved lengthwise, seeds removed, and sliced into half-moons
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2-3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon fresh thyme leaves
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • 1/2 cup freshly grated Parmesan cheese

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the Oven: Begin by preheating your oven to 400°F (200°C). Line a baking sheet with parchment paper for easy cleanup.

  2. Prepare the Squash: Once the delicata squash has been halved, seeded, and sliced into half-moons, place the slices in a large mixing bowl. Drizzle the olive oil over the squash, then add the minced garlic, fresh thyme leaves, salt, and black pepper. Gently toss the squash until it is evenly coated with the seasonings.

  3. Roast the Squash: Arrange the seasoned delicata squash slices in a single layer on the prepared baking sheet. Roast in the preheated oven for 20-25 minutes, or until the squash is tender and caramelized around the edges.

  4. Add the Parmesan: Once the squash is roasted to perfection, remove it from the oven and sprinkle the freshly grated Parmesan cheese over the top. Return the baking sheet to the oven for an additional 5 minutes, allowing the cheese to melt and develop a golden crust.

  5. Serve and Enjoy: Remove the Roasted Delicata Squash with Thyme and Parmesan from the oven and transfer it to a serving platter. Garnish with additional fresh thyme leaves if desired. This delightful fall side dish is ready to be enjoyed alongside your favorite main course.

Why We Love This Recipe:

  • Simplicity: With just a handful of ingredients and minimal prep time, this recipe is incredibly easy to make, making it perfect for busy weeknights or holiday gatherings.
  • Versatility: Roasted Delicata Squash with Thyme and Parmesan pairs well with a wide range of proteins, from roasted chicken to grilled steak, making it a versatile addition to any meal.
  • Flavorful: The natural sweetness of delicata squash is enhanced by the earthy aroma of fresh thyme and the savory richness of Parmesan cheese, creating a flavor combination that is sure to impress.

Nutritional Benefits:

Delicata squash is not only delicious but also packed with essential nutrients. It’s a great source of vitamins A and C, as well as fiber and antioxidants. Additionally, the use of olive oil provides healthy monounsaturated fats, while Parmesan cheese offers a dose of calcium and protein.

Conclusion:

As the air turns crisp and the leaves begin to change, embrace the flavors of the season with this delightful Roasted Delicata Squash with Thyme and Parmesan recipe. Whether you’re hosting a fall feast or simply craving a comforting side dish, this simple yet elegant recipe is sure to become a seasonal favorite. Enjoy the warm, inviting flavors of autumn with every bite of this roasted delicacy!

So go ahead, pick up some delicata squash from your local market, and let the magic of fall unfold in your kitchen with this irresistible recipe.
