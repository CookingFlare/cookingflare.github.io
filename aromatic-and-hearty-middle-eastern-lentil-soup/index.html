Aromatic and Hearty: Middle Eastern Lentil Soup


One of the most comforting and delicious dishes in Middle Eastern cuisine is the aromatic and hearty lentil soup. This soup not only warms your body but also fills your senses with its rich flavors and fragrant spices. Whether you’re a fan of Middle Eastern cuisine or looking to explore new flavors, this recipe is sure to become a favorite in your kitchen.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup red lentils
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 3 garlic cloves, minced
  • 2 carrots, diced
  • 2 celery stalks, diced
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon ground coriander
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground turmeric
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 4 cups vegetable broth
  • 2 cups water
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • Fresh cilantro, for garnish

Instructions

  1. In a large pot, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the chopped onion and sauté until it becomes translucent and slightly caramelized.

  2. Add the minced garlic, diced carrots, and celery stalks to the pot. Sauté for a few more minutes until the vegetables soften.

  3. Rinse the red lentils under cold water to remove any impurities. Add the lentils to the pot along with the cumin, coriander, turmeric, and cinnamon. Stir well to coat the lentils and vegetables with the spices.

  4. Pour the vegetable broth and water into the pot. Bring the soup to a boil, then reduce the heat and let it simmer for about 20-25 minutes, or until the lentils are cooked through and tender.

  5. Once the lentils are cooked, use an immersion blender or a regular blender to puree the soup until smooth and creamy. If using a regular blender, be careful not to fill it too full and blend in batches if necessary.

  6. Season the soup with salt and pepper to taste. Adjust the seasoning according to your preference.

  7. Serve the aromatic and hearty Middle Eastern lentil soup hot, garnished with fresh cilantro. You can also drizzle a bit of olive oil on top for extra flavor.

Why You’ll Love It

This Middle Eastern lentil soup is a true culinary masterpiece. The combination of spices like cumin, coriander, turmeric, and cinnamon creates a symphony of flavors that will transport you to the bustling streets of the Middle East. The red lentils add a creamy texture to the soup, making every spoonful a delight.

Not only is this soup delicious, but it is also incredibly nutritious. Lentils are packed with protein, fiber, and essential vitamins and minerals. They are a great source of plant-based protein, making this soup a perfect option for vegetarians and vegans. Additionally, the aromatic spices used in this recipe have numerous health benefits, from reducing inflammation to boosting the immune system.

This aromatic and hearty Middle Eastern lentil soup is a comforting and satisfying dish that will warm your body and soul. It’s perfect for cold winter nights or whenever you’re in need of a nourishing meal. So, gather your ingredients and get ready to embark on a culinary journey to the Middle East from the comfort of your own kitchen. Enjoy!
