Classic American Sausage and Peppers Recipe for Flavorful Dinner


If you’re craving a hearty and flavorful dinner that’s easy to make, look no further than the classic American sausage and peppers recipe. This dish is a staple in many households, and for good reason – it’s delicious, satisfying, and perfect for any occasion. Whether you’re cooking for a crowd or enjoying a cozy meal at home, this recipe is sure to impress.

Ingredients

To make this classic American dish, you’ll need the following ingredients:

  • 4 Italian sausages (mild or hot, depending on your preference)
  • 2 bell peppers (red, green, or a mix of both), thinly sliced
  • 1 large onion, thinly sliced
  • 3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 can (14.5 oz) diced tomatoes
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Olive oil for cooking

Instructions

  1. In a large skillet, heat a drizzle of olive oil over medium heat. Add the sausages and cook until browned on all sides. This will take about 8-10 minutes. Once cooked, remove the sausages from the skillet and set them aside.

  2. In the same skillet, add a little more olive oil if needed, and then add the sliced onions and bell peppers. Cook them until they become soft and slightly caramelized, stirring occasionally. This will take about 10-12 minutes.

  3. Once the onions and peppers are cooked, add the minced garlic and cook for an additional minute until fragrant.

  4. Next, add the diced tomatoes, dried oregano, dried basil, salt, and pepper to the skillet. Stir everything together and let the mixture simmer for about 5 minutes, allowing the flavors to meld together.

  5. While the sauce is simmering, slice the sausages into bite-sized pieces. Then, add the sliced sausages back into the skillet with the peppers and tomatoes. Stir everything together to combine.

  6. Cover the skillet and let the sausage and peppers mixture simmer for another 10-15 minutes, allowing the flavors to fully develop. Stir occasionally to prevent sticking.

  7. Once the sausages are fully cooked and the flavors have melded together, remove the skillet from the heat.

  8. Serve the classic American sausage and peppers over a bed of cooked rice, pasta, or with a side of crusty bread. This dish is also delicious on its own as a low-carb option.

Tips and Variations

  • If you prefer a spicier dish, use hot Italian sausages instead of mild.
  • Feel free to add other vegetables to the skillet, such as zucchini or mushrooms, for added flavor and texture.
  • For a smoky twist, grill the sausages instead of cooking them in the skillet. This will give them a delicious charred flavor.
  • Leftovers can be stored in an airtight container in the refrigerator for up to three days. Simply reheat in a skillet or microwave before enjoying.

Conclusion

The classic American sausage and peppers recipe is a timeless dish that never fails to satisfy. With its bold flavors and hearty ingredients, it’s the perfect choice for a flavorful dinner that’s quick and easy to make. Whether you’re cooking for a family gathering or simply craving a comforting meal, this recipe is sure to become a favorite in your household. So gather your ingredients, follow the simple steps, and get ready to enjoy this classic American dish. Bon appétit!
