Simple and Delicious Chicken Quesadilla Recipe for Kids


Are you looking for a quick, easy, and kid-friendly meal that the whole family will love? Look no further! This simple and delicious chicken quesadilla recipe is the perfect solution. Packed with flavor, protein, and cheesy goodness, these quesadillas are sure to be a hit with even the pickiest of eaters. Plus, they are so easy to make that your kids can even help out in the kitchen!

Ingredients

  • 2 cups shredded cooked chicken
  • 1 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup diced bell peppers
  • 1/4 cup diced onions
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh cilantro
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon chili powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon paprika
  • 8 small flour tortillas
  • Cooking spray
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. In a large mixing bowl, combine the shredded chicken, cheddar cheese, mozzarella cheese, bell peppers, onions, cilantro, ground cumin, chili powder, garlic powder, and paprika. Mix everything together until well combined.

  2. Lay out 4 tortillas on a clean work surface. Divide the chicken and cheese mixture evenly among the tortillas, spreading it out in an even layer.

  3. Place the remaining 4 tortillas on top of the chicken and cheese mixture to form quesadillas.

  4. Heat a large skillet over medium heat and lightly coat it with cooking spray.

  5. Carefully place one quesadilla in the skillet and cook for 2-3 minutes on each side, or until the tortilla is golden brown and the cheese is melted.

  6. Repeat with the remaining quesadillas.

  7. Once cooked, remove the quesadillas from the skillet and let them cool for a minute or two.

  8. Using a sharp knife, slice the quesadillas into wedges and serve with your favorite toppings such as salsa, guacamole, or sour cream.

Tips for Making the Perfect Chicken Quesadillas

  • Use a non-stick skillet to ensure that the quesadillas don’t stick to the pan and become crispy and golden brown.
  • Feel free to customize the filling with your kids’ favorite ingredients such as black beans, corn, or even some diced tomatoes.
  • Get your kids involved in the kitchen by allowing them to help with the assembly of the quesadillas. It’s a great way to teach them about cooking and make mealtime more enjoyable.

Nutritional Benefits

These chicken quesadillas are not only delicious but also nutritious. Packed with protein from the chicken and calcium from the cheese, they make for a well-rounded meal for growing kids. The bell peppers and onions add a dose of vitamins and minerals, making this recipe a wholesome option for a family meal.

Conclusion

This simple and delicious chicken quesadilla recipe is a surefire way to please even the pickiest of eaters. With its easy preparation and mouthwatering flavors, it’s a perfect meal for busy weeknights or a fun weekend lunch. So, gather your little chefs and whip up a batch of these delightful quesadillas for a meal that’s guaranteed to put smiles on everyone’s faces!
