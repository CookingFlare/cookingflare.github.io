No-Bake Energy Balls with Peanut Butter and Banana and a Sprinkle of Cinnamon Recipe: A Healthy and Easy Vegan Snack


No-Bake Energy Balls with Peanut Butter and Banana and a Sprinkle of Cinnamon Recipe: A Healthy and Easy Vegan Snack

Are you looking for a quick and healthy snack that will keep you energized throughout the day? Look no further! These No-Bake Energy Balls with Peanut Butter, Banana, and a Sprinkle of Cinnamon are just what you need. Packed with nutritious ingredients and bursting with flavor, these vegan energy balls are not only delicious but also incredibly easy to make.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup rolled oats
  • 1/2 cup creamy peanut butter
  • 1 ripe banana, mashed
  • 1/4 cup ground flaxseed
  • 1/4 cup chia seeds
  • 1/4 cup honey or maple syrup
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • A pinch of salt
  • Optional: shredded coconut or chopped nuts for coating

Instructions

  1. In a large mixing bowl, combine the rolled oats, peanut butter, mashed banana, ground flaxseed, chia seeds, honey or maple syrup, vanilla extract, ground cinnamon, and a pinch of salt. Mix well until all the ingredients are fully incorporated.

  2. Once the mixture is well combined, cover the bowl and refrigerate for about 30 minutes. Chilling the mixture will make it easier to form into balls.

  3. After chilling, remove the mixture from the refrigerator and start shaping it into bite-sized balls. Take about a tablespoon of the mixture and roll it between your palms until it forms a compact ball. Repeat this process with the remaining mixture.

  4. If desired, roll the energy balls in shredded coconut or chopped nuts to add an extra layer of texture and flavor. Alternatively, you can leave them plain.

  5. Once all the energy balls are formed, place them on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper and refrigerate for at least one hour to allow them to firm up.

  6. After chilling, the energy balls are ready to be enjoyed! Store them in an airtight container in the refrigerator for up to one week.

Why Choose No-Bake Energy Balls?

These No-Bake Energy Balls with Peanut Butter, Banana, and a Sprinkle of Cinnamon are the perfect snack for anyone on the go. Here are a few reasons why you should give them a try:

  1. Quick and Easy: With just a few simple ingredients and no baking required, these energy balls can be whipped up in no time. They are perfect for those busy days when you need a healthy snack on the fly.

  2. Nutritious and Filling: Packed with rolled oats, peanut butter, banana, flaxseed, and chia seeds, these energy balls are loaded with essential nutrients, fiber, and healthy fats. They will keep you feeling satisfied and energized throughout the day.

  3. Vegan-Friendly: These energy balls are completely plant-based, making them suitable for vegans and those following a vegan lifestyle. They are also a great option for anyone looking to incorporate more plant-based foods into their diet.

  4. Versatile: This recipe serves as a base, allowing you to customize the flavor to your liking. Feel free to add in extras such as dried fruits, chocolate chips, or even protein powder to make them even more delicious and nutritious.

So, the next time you’re in need of a quick and healthy snack, give these No-Bake Energy Balls with Peanut Butter, Banana, and a Sprinkle of Cinnamon a try. Your taste buds and your body will thank you!

Remember, you can store these energy balls in the refrigerator for up to a week, making them perfect for meal prepping or grabbing on the go. Enjoy!
