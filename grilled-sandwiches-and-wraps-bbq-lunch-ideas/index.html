Grilled Sandwiches and Wraps: BBQ Lunch Ideas


When the weather is perfect, and the hunger hits, nothing satisfies quite like a grilled sandwich or wrap filled with flavorful ingredients. A BBQ-themed lunch takes this simple meal to the next level, bringing together smokiness, tanginess, and richness in every bite. Whether you’re looking to feed a crowd, treat yourself, or just explore new lunch ideas, grilled sandwiches and wraps make for the perfect go-to meal.

In this post, we’ll dive into delicious BBQ-inspired recipes that will elevate your lunch game, offering variety for every taste preference. We’ll cover grilling techniques, tips for achieving the perfect balance of flavors, and ways to add unique twists to your sandwich and wrap creations. Let’s fire up the grill and get ready for an unforgettable BBQ lunch!

Grilled BBQ Chicken Sandwich

Ingredients:

  • 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon smoked paprika
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 cup BBQ sauce
  • 4 sandwich buns (preferably brioche)
  • 1 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • Pickles and onions for garnish (optional)
  • Fresh lettuce or coleslaw (optional)

Directions:

  1. Prepare the Chicken: Start by marinating the chicken breasts. Rub them with olive oil and season them with smoked paprika, garlic powder, salt, and pepper. Let the chicken sit for about 10 minutes to absorb the flavors.

  2. Grill the Chicken: Preheat your grill to medium-high heat. Grill the chicken for about 6-8 minutes on each side, or until the internal temperature reaches 165°F (75°C). Brush the chicken with BBQ sauce in the last few minutes of grilling for a sticky, caramelized glaze.

  3. Toast the Buns: While the chicken is cooking, slice your sandwich buns in half and toast them lightly on the grill until golden brown.

  4. Assemble the Sandwiches: Once the chicken is grilled, place it on the bottom half of the toasted buns. Top with shredded cheddar cheese, pickles, onions, and any other toppings of your choice.

  5. Serve: Place the other half of the bun on top and serve with a side of crispy fries or a fresh salad.

This grilled BBQ chicken sandwich is a classic for a reason. The smokiness from the BBQ sauce combined with the richness of the cheddar cheese and the crunch of fresh toppings makes every bite irresistible.

BBQ Pulled Pork Wrap

Ingredients:

  • 2 pounds pork shoulder
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon paprika
  • 1 tablespoon brown sugar
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • 1 teaspoon onion powder
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon cayenne pepper (optional)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 cups BBQ sauce
  • 4 large flour tortillas
  • 1 cup coleslaw
  • 1/2 cup pickled red onions
  • Fresh cilantro for garnish

Directions:

  1. Cook the Pork: Rub the pork shoulder with olive oil, paprika, brown sugar, cumin, onion powder, garlic powder, cayenne pepper, salt, and pepper. Place the pork in a slow cooker or an oven set to 300°F (150°C) and cook for about 6-8 hours until the pork is tender and easy to shred.

  2. Shred the Pork: Once the pork is fully cooked, remove it from the cooker and shred it using two forks. Return the shredded pork to the slow cooker and mix with BBQ sauce. Let it simmer for 20 minutes to absorb the flavors.

  3. Prepare the Wrap: Warm your flour tortillas on a grill or in a skillet for a few seconds on each side.

  4. Assemble the Wraps: Lay out the warm tortillas and add a generous portion of the pulled pork in the center. Top with coleslaw, pickled red onions, and fresh cilantro for an added burst of flavor.

  5. Serve: Roll up the tortillas, folding in the sides, and slice them in half for easy serving. Pair with chips or a side of grilled vegetables for a complete meal.

This BBQ pulled pork wrap is a great option for those who enjoy a satisfying, savory lunch with a touch of tanginess. The combination of smoky pork, creamy coleslaw, and zesty pickled onions creates a mouthwatering wrap that’s perfect for BBQ lovers.

Grilled Veggie and Hummus Wrap

Ingredients:

  • 1 zucchini, sliced
  • 1 red bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 yellow bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 red onion, sliced
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 4 large whole wheat tortillas
  • 1/2 cup hummus (store-bought or homemade)
  • 1/4 cup crumbled feta cheese
  • 1 tablespoon fresh basil, chopped
  • Lemon juice (optional)

Directions:

  1. Prepare the Vegetables: Toss the zucchini, bell peppers, and red onion with olive oil, salt, and pepper. Grill them over medium heat for about 4-5 minutes per side, until they are tender and slightly charred.

  2. Prepare the Wrap: While the vegetables are grilling, warm the tortillas on the grill or in a skillet.

  3. Assemble the Wraps: Spread a generous layer of hummus on each tortilla. Once the veggies are grilled, arrange them in the center of the wrap. Top with crumbled feta cheese and fresh basil. Drizzle with a little lemon juice for an extra zing if desired.

  4. Serve: Roll up the tortilla, slice it in half, and enjoy! Serve with a side of fruit or a light salad for a fresh, healthy meal.

This grilled veggie and hummus wrap is a perfect choice for those seeking a lighter, plant-based option without sacrificing flavor. The grilled vegetables provide a smoky depth, while the hummus adds a creamy, savory contrast. It’s a filling yet wholesome lunch that can be customized with your favorite veggies.

BBQ Steak Sandwich

Ingredients:

  • 2 ribeye steaks (about 6-8 ounces each)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1/4 cup BBQ sauce
  • 4 sandwich rolls or baguettes
  • 1/2 cup arugula
  • 1/2 cup caramelized onions
  • 1/2 cup blue cheese crumbles (optional)

Directions:

  1. Prepare the Steaks: Preheat your grill to high heat. Season the ribeye steaks generously with salt and pepper. Drizzle with olive oil before placing them on the grill.

  2. Grill the Steaks: Grill the steaks for about 4-5 minutes on each side for medium-rare, or longer for your desired doneness. During the last minute of grilling, brush the steaks with BBQ sauce for extra flavor.

  3. Prepare the Sandwich Rolls: Toast the sandwich rolls or baguettes on the grill for a minute or two until they are crispy and golden.

  4. Assemble the Sandwiches: Once the steaks are grilled, slice them thinly against the grain. Place the steak slices on the bottom half of each toasted roll. Top with arugula, caramelized onions, and blue cheese crumbles if desired.

  5. Serve: Place the other half of the roll on top and serve with a side of crispy fries or a tangy pickle.

This BBQ steak sandwich is a meaty masterpiece. The combination of tender, juicy steak, smoky BBQ sauce, and the sharpness of blue cheese makes it a favorite for anyone craving a hearty, satisfying lunch.

BBQ Shrimp and Avocado Wrap

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon smoked paprika
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 4 large tortillas
  • 1 avocado, sliced
  • 1/2 cup lettuce, shredded
  • 1/4 cup ranch dressing or BBQ sauce
  • Fresh cilantro for garnish

Directions:

  1. Prepare the Shrimp: In a bowl, toss the shrimp with olive oil, smoked paprika, garlic powder, salt, and pepper. Let them marinate for 10 minutes.

  2. Grill the Shrimp: Heat a grill or grill pan over medium-high heat. Grill the shrimp for about 2-3 minutes on each side, until they are pink and opaque.

  3. Prepare the Wrap: While the shrimp is cooking, warm your tortillas on the grill or in a skillet for a few seconds on each side.

  4. Assemble the Wraps: Lay the warm tortillas flat and add the grilled shrimp in the center. Top with sliced avocado, shredded lettuce, and a drizzle of ranch dressing or BBQ sauce.

  5. Serve: Roll up the tortillas and serve with a side of roasted vegetables or tortilla chips.

This BBQ shrimp and avocado wrap is a light yet flavorful option for those who love seafood. The smoky grilled shrimp combined with creamy avocado and a tangy sauce makes it a refreshing twist on the traditional BBQ meal.

Tips for Perfect Grilled Sandwiches and Wraps

  1. Choose Quality Bread: For sandwiches, the bread is key. Opt for fresh, hearty buns or rolls, such as brioche or ciabatta, for a great texture. For wraps, choose large, soft tortillas that won’t tear easily when rolling.

  2. Don’t Overstuff: While it’s tempting to pile on the ingredients, overstuffing your sandwich or wrap can lead to soggy or difficult-to-handle meals. Aim for a balanced amount of filling that’s easy to eat and full of flavor.

  3. Grill with Care: When grilling, keep an eye on your sandwich or wrap to prevent burning. A little char is great for flavor, but too much can ruin your meal.

  4. Add Fresh Toppings: To balance the richness of BBQ flavors, add fresh toppings like lettuce, tomatoes, pickles, or onions. These add crunch and freshness that complement the smoky, savory flavors.

  5. Experiment with Sauces: BBQ sauce is a classic, but don’t be afraid to try other sauces like ranch, aioli, or hot sauce for different flavor profiles. The sauce can make or break your wrap or sandwich, so choose wisely!

Conclusion

Grilled sandwiches and wraps are the ultimate way to enjoy a BBQ-inspired lunch, whether you’re using chicken, pork, steak, veggies, or shrimp. Each recipe offers a unique take on traditional BBQ flavors, ensuring there’s something for everyone. The possibilities are endless when it comes to experimenting with different fillings, sauces, and toppings.

So, fire up the grill, gather your ingredients, and get creative with these delicious BBQ lunch ideas. Whether you’re hosting a family get-together or just making a quick lunch for yourself, these grilled sandwiches and wraps are sure to be a hit every time. Enjoy the smoky, savory goodness in every bite!
