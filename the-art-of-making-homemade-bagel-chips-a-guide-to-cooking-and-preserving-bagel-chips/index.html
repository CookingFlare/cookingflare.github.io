The Art of Making Homemade Bagel Chips: A Guide to Cooking and Preserving Bagel Chips


Bagel chips are a delicious and versatile snack that can be enjoyed on their own or paired with your favorite dips and spreads. Made from sliced bagels, these crispy treats are perfect for satisfying your cravings. While store-bought bagel chips are widely available, there’s something special about making your own at home. In this guide, we’ll take you through the art of making homemade bagel chips and share some tips on how to preserve them for future enjoyment.

Ingredients:

  • Bagels (any flavor of your choice)
  • Olive oil
  • Salt
  • Optional: additional seasonings such as garlic powder, onion powder, or dried herbs

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C).

  2. Start by selecting your favorite bagel flavor. Plain, everything, or sesame are popular choices, but feel free to experiment with different flavors to suit your taste preferences.

  3. Using a sharp knife, carefully slice the bagels into thin rounds. Aim for slices that are about 1/8 to 1/4 inch thick. Thinner slices will result in crispier chips, while thicker slices will be more chewy.

  4. Lay the bagel slices in a single layer on a baking sheet. Make sure they are not overlapping to ensure even baking.

  5. Drizzle a small amount of olive oil over the bagel slices. Using your hands or a pastry brush, gently spread the oil to coat both sides of the slices. This will help the chips crisp up in the oven.

  6. Sprinkle a pinch of salt over the oiled bagel slices. If desired, you can also add other seasonings such as garlic powder, onion powder, or dried herbs to enhance the flavor.

  7. Place the baking sheet in the preheated oven and bake for about 10-15 minutes, or until the bagel chips turn golden brown and crispy. Keep a close eye on them, as they can quickly go from golden to burnt.

  8. Once the bagel chips are cooked to your desired level of crispiness, remove them from the oven and let them cool completely on a wire rack. As they cool, they will become even more crispy.

Preserving Bagel Chips:

If you want to enjoy your homemade bagel chips beyond the day they are made, proper storage is essential. Follow these simple steps to preserve their freshness and crunchiness:

  1. Make sure the bagel chips are completely cooled before storing them. Any residual heat can create condensation and make them soggy.

  2. Transfer the cooled bagel chips to an airtight container or resealable plastic bag. This will protect them from moisture and keep them crispy.

  3. Store the bagel chips in a cool, dry place, such as a pantry or cupboard. Avoid storing them in the refrigerator, as the moisture can affect their texture.

  4. When stored properly, homemade bagel chips can stay fresh for up to a week. However, they are likely to be devoured long before then!

Serving Suggestions:

Homemade bagel chips are incredibly versatile and can be enjoyed in various ways. Here are some serving suggestions to inspire your culinary creativity:

  • Serve alongside your favorite dips such as hummus, spinach artichoke dip, or homemade salsa.
  • Use them as a crunchy topping for soups or salads.
  • Pair them with cheese and cured meats for an easy and delicious charcuterie board.
  • Crush them up and use them as a breadcrumb substitute in recipes that call for breadcrumbs.

Conclusion:

Making homemade bagel chips is a simple yet rewarding culinary adventure. With just a few ingredients and some oven time, you can create a batch of crispy, flavorful chips that will impress your taste buds and your guests. Experiment with different bagel flavors and seasonings to find your favorite combination. Whether you enjoy them on their own or as a companion to dips and spreads, homemade bagel chips are sure to become a tasty staple in your snack repertoire. So, roll up your sleeves, grab some bagels, and let the bagel chip-making begin!
