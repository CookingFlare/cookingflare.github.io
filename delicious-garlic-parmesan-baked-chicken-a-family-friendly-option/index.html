Delicious Garlic Parmesan Baked Chicken: A Family-Friendly Option


Are you tired of cooking the same old chicken recipes for your family? Look no further! In this blog post, I will share with you a delicious and family-friendly recipe for Garlic Parmesan Baked Chicken that will leave everyone asking for seconds.

Ingredients

To make this mouthwatering dish, you will need the following ingredients:

  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/2 cup breadcrumbs
  • 1/4 cup melted butter
  • 3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper

Instructions

Let’s get started on preparing this flavorful chicken dish:

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C). Line a baking sheet with parchment paper to prevent sticking.

  2. In a shallow bowl, combine the grated Parmesan cheese, breadcrumbs, minced garlic, dried oregano, dried basil, salt, and black pepper. Mix well to ensure all the ingredients are evenly distributed.

  3. Dip each chicken breast into the melted butter, making sure to coat both sides. Then, dredge the chicken in the Parmesan cheese mixture, pressing gently to adhere the coating to the chicken.

  4. Place the coated chicken breasts onto the prepared baking sheet. Make sure to leave space between each piece to ensure even cooking.

  5. Bake the chicken in the preheated oven for approximately 25-30 minutes, or until the internal temperature reaches 165°F (74°C). The chicken should be golden brown and crispy on the outside.

  6. Once the chicken is cooked, remove it from the oven and let it rest for a few minutes before serving. This will allow the juices to redistribute and ensure a tender and juicy chicken.

  7. Serve the Garlic Parmesan Baked Chicken with your favorite side dishes, such as roasted vegetables or a fresh salad. The flavorful combination of garlic and Parmesan cheese will have your family begging for more!

Tips and Variations

  • For an extra kick of flavor, try adding some crushed red pepper flakes to the Parmesan cheese mixture.
  • If you prefer a gluten-free version, you can substitute the breadcrumbs with almond flour or gluten-free breadcrumbs.
  • Feel free to experiment with different herbs and spices to customize the flavor of the chicken. Italian seasoning or paprika can add a unique twist to this dish.

Final Thoughts

Garlic Parmesan Baked Chicken is a versatile and delicious dish that the whole family will love. The combination of garlic, Parmesan cheese, and crispy coating creates a flavorful and satisfying meal. Whether you’re looking for a quick weeknight dinner or a crowd-pleasing dish for a special occasion, this recipe is sure to impress.

Don’t hesitate to try this recipe and make it a regular part of your family’s meal rotation. With its simple ingredients and easy preparation, this Garlic Parmesan Baked Chicken recipe is a fantastic option for busy households. Enjoy the amazing flavors and happy cooking!
