Sous Vide Lemon Garlic Chicken: A Fresh and Zesty Recipe


If you’re looking to elevate your chicken game, then look no further than this sous vide lemon garlic chicken recipe. With its fresh and zesty flavors, this dish is sure to become a favorite in your household. The sous vide cooking method ensures that the chicken is perfectly cooked, tender, and juicy, while the combination of lemon and garlic adds a burst of tangy and aromatic goodness. Let’s get started!

Ingredients

  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 4 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 2 lemons, juiced and zested
  • 2 tablespoons of olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon of salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon of black pepper
  • Fresh parsley, for garnish

Instructions

  1. Preheat your sous vide water bath to 145°F (63°C). This temperature will give you tender and moist chicken.
  2. In a bowl, combine the minced garlic, lemon juice, lemon zest, olive oil, salt, and black pepper. Mix well to create a marinade.
  3. Place the chicken breasts in a resealable plastic bag and pour the marinade over them. Make sure the chicken is evenly coated. Seal the bag, removing as much air as possible.
  4. Gently lower the sealed bag into the preheated water bath, making sure the chicken is fully submerged. Cook for 1 to 2 hours, depending on the thickness of the chicken breasts. The longer cooking time will result in even more tender chicken.
  5. Once the chicken is done cooking, remove it from the water bath and carefully take it out of the bag. Pat the chicken dry with paper towels.
  6. Heat a skillet over medium-high heat and add a tablespoon of olive oil. When the oil is hot, add the chicken breasts and sear them for about 1 minute on each side, until golden brown.
  7. Transfer the seared chicken to a cutting board and let it rest for a few minutes. This allows the juices to redistribute and ensures maximum flavor.
  8. Slice the chicken breasts into thin, even slices. Garnish with fresh parsley for a pop of color and added freshness.
  9. Serve your sous vide lemon garlic chicken with your favorite sides, such as roasted vegetables, a crisp salad, or creamy mashed potatoes. Enjoy!

Tips and Variations

  • For an extra flavor boost, you can marinate the chicken breasts for a few hours or overnight in the refrigerator before sous vide cooking.
  • Experiment with different herbs and spices to customize the marinade to your liking. Fresh rosemary or thyme would work beautifully in this recipe.
  • If you don’t have a sous vide machine, you can still achieve similar results by poaching the chicken in a pot of simmering water. Just make sure to monitor the internal temperature of the chicken to ensure it reaches 165°F (74°C) for food safety.
  • Leftover sous vide lemon garlic chicken makes a great addition to salads, sandwiches, or wraps. The flavors will continue to develop and meld together, creating an even tastier meal the next day.

In Conclusion

Sous vide lemon garlic chicken is a simple yet elegant dish that will impress both your family and guests. The sous vide cooking method guarantees perfectly cooked chicken every time, while the lemon and garlic bring a fresh and zesty flavor profile to the table. Whether you’re hosting a dinner party or simply want to elevate your weeknight dinner, this recipe is a winner. Give it a try and prepare to be blown away by the tender, juicy, and flavorful chicken that awaits you. Enjoy!
