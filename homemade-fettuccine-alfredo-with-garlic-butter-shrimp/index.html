Homemade Fettuccine Alfredo with Garlic Butter Shrimp


Are you in the mood for a decadent and delicious pasta dish? Look no further than this Homemade Fettuccine Alfredo with Garlic Butter Shrimp recipe! It’s a classic Italian dish that combines creamy alfredo sauce with perfectly cooked shrimp, all served over a bed of homemade fettuccine noodles. Trust me, once you try this dish, you’ll never want to go back to store-bought pasta again!

Ingredients

For the pasta:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • Water, as needed

For the alfredo sauce:

  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 4 tablespoons unsalted butter
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • Salt and pepper, to taste

For the shrimp:

  • 1 pound shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 2 tablespoons unsalted butter
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • Fresh parsley, chopped (for garnish)

Instructions

  1. Start by making the homemade pasta. In a large mixing bowl, combine the flour and salt. Create a well in the center and crack the eggs into the well. Gradually mix the eggs into the flour until a dough forms. If the dough seems dry, add water, a tablespoon at a time, until it comes together. Knead the dough on a floured surface for about 5 minutes, or until it becomes smooth and elastic. Wrap the dough in plastic wrap and let it rest for 30 minutes.

  2. While the dough is resting, prepare the alfredo sauce. In a saucepan over medium heat, melt the butter. Add the minced garlic and cook for 1-2 minutes until fragrant. Pour in the heavy cream and bring to a simmer. Reduce the heat to low and stir in the grated Parmesan cheese until it melts and the sauce becomes smooth. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Keep the sauce warm over low heat.

  3. Once the dough has rested, divide it into four equal portions. Taking one portion at a time, flatten it with your hands and run it through a pasta machine on the widest setting. Fold the dough in half and run it through the machine again. Repeat this process several times, gradually reducing the width of the machine until you reach the desired thickness for your fettuccine noodles. Pass the dough through the fettuccine cutter attachment on the pasta machine, or cut it into thin strips using a sharp knife. Repeat with the remaining portions of dough.

  4. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Cook the homemade fettuccine noodles for 2-3 minutes, or until they are al dente. Drain the pasta, reserving a small amount of the cooking water.

  5. While the pasta is cooking, prepare the shrimp. In a large skillet over medium-high heat, melt the butter. Add the minced garlic and cook for 1-2 minutes until fragrant. Add the shrimp to the skillet and season with salt and pepper. Cook the shrimp for 2-3 minutes per side, or until they are pink and opaque.

  6. To serve, divide the cooked fettuccine noodles among plates. Spoon the alfredo sauce over the noodles and top with the garlic butter shrimp. Garnish with chopped parsley for a fresh burst of flavor. Serve immediately and enjoy!

Final Thoughts

There you have it - a homemade fettuccine alfredo with garlic butter shrimp recipe that will impress your family and friends. The combination of creamy alfredo sauce, tender shrimp, and fresh homemade pasta creates a truly indulgent dish that is perfect for special occasions or even just a cozy night in. So why wait? Get cooking and treat yourself to this restaurant-worthy meal in the comfort of your own home. Bon appétit!
