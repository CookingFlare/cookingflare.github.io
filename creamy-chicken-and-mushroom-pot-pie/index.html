Creamy Chicken and Mushroom Pot Pie


There’s nothing quite like a warm, comforting pot pie on a chilly evening. This Creamy Chicken and Mushroom Pot Pie is the perfect dish to cozy up with, combining tender chicken, earthy mushrooms, and a rich creamy sauce, all tucked under a flaky golden crust. Let’s dive into the details of this delectable recipe that will surely become a family favorite.

Ingredients:

  • 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 8 oz mushrooms, sliced
  • 1/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup chicken broth
  • 1 cup whole milk
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 sheet of puff pastry, thawed
  • 1 egg, beaten (for egg wash)

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven: Start by preheating your oven to 375°F (190°C) to ensure it’s ready when your pot pie is assembled.

  2. Cook the Chicken: In a large skillet, heat 1 tablespoon of olive oil over medium heat. Season the chicken breasts with salt and pepper, then cook until golden brown and no longer pink in the middle. Remove the chicken from the pan and set it aside to cool before shredding it into bite-sized pieces.

  3. Prepare the Filling: In the same skillet, add the remaining olive oil and sauté the onions until they turn translucent. Add the garlic and mushrooms, cooking until the mushrooms are tender. Sprinkle the flour over the mixture and stir well to coat everything.

  4. Add the Liquid: Slowly pour in the chicken broth and milk, stirring constantly to avoid lumps. Allow the mixture to simmer and thicken. Season with thyme, salt, and pepper for added flavor.

  5. Assemble the Pot Pie: Transfer the creamy chicken and mushroom mixture into a baking dish. Place the shredded chicken on top and gently mix it in. Lay the puff pastry over the filling, trimming any excess dough. Use a fork to crimp the edges of the pastry to the dish.

  6. Bake to Perfection: Brush the puff pastry with the beaten egg to give it a beautiful golden sheen when baked. Make a few slits on the pastry to allow steam to escape while baking. Place the pot pie in the preheated oven and bake for 30-35 minutes or until the crust is flaky and golden.

  7. Serve and Enjoy: Once the pot pie is done, let it cool slightly before serving. Scoop generous portions onto plates and savor the creamy goodness of the chicken and mushroom filling enveloped in a buttery pastry crust.

This Creamy Chicken and Mushroom Pot Pie is a delightful twist on a classic comfort food favorite. The creamy sauce, tender chicken, and flavorful mushrooms come together harmoniously under a crispy pastry crust. Whether for a family dinner or a gathering with friends, this dish is sure to impress. So, roll up your sleeves, gather your ingredients, and embark on a culinary journey that will warm both your heart and your belly. Happy cooking!
