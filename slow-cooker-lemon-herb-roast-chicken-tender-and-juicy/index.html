Slow Cooker Lemon Herb Roast Chicken: Tender and Juicy


There’s something wonderfully comforting about a perfectly cooked roast chicken. The tender meat, infused with savory herbs and tangy citrus, practically falls off the bone with each delicious bite. If you’re looking to achieve that ideal balance of juicy tenderness and flavorful seasoning without spending hours in the kitchen, then look no further than this Slow Cooker Lemon Herb Roast Chicken recipe. It’s a game-changer for anyone craving a home-cooked meal that requires minimal effort and delivers maximum satisfaction.

Ingredients:

  • 1 whole chicken (approximately 3-4 lbs)
  • 2 lemons, divided
  • 4 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons fresh rosemary, chopped
  • 2 tablespoons fresh thyme leaves
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 cup chicken broth

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the Chicken:

    • Start by rinsing the whole chicken under cold water and patting it dry with paper towels. Place the chicken on a clean cutting board.

  2. Seasoning the Chicken:

    • In a small bowl, combine the minced garlic, chopped rosemary, thyme leaves, olive oil, salt, and pepper. Mix well to create a fragrant herb mixture.
    • Carefully loosen the skin of the chicken by gently running your fingers between the skin and the meat, being careful not to tear the skin. Rub half of the herb mixture underneath the skin, spreading it evenly over the breast and thigh meat.

  3. Stuffing the Chicken:

    • Cut one lemon into quarters and stuff it into the cavity of the chicken along with a few sprigs of fresh herbs (rosemary and thyme). This will infuse the chicken with citrusy flavor as it cooks.

  4. Slow Cooking:

    • Place the seasoned chicken into the slow cooker insert. Squeeze the juice of the remaining lemon over the top of the chicken and pour the chicken broth around it.

  5. Cooking Time:

    • Cover the slow cooker with the lid and cook on low for 6-7 hours, or until the internal temperature of the chicken reaches 165°F (75°C) when measured with a meat thermometer inserted into the thickest part of the thigh without touching bone.

  6. Resting and Serving:

    • Once the chicken is fully cooked, carefully remove it from the slow cooker and transfer it to a serving platter. Allow the chicken to rest for 10-15 minutes before carving to ensure the juices redistribute evenly throughout the meat.

  7. Optional Gravy:

    • If desired, strain the cooking juices from the slow cooker into a saucepan and bring to a simmer over medium heat. In a separate bowl, whisk together 1 tablespoon of cornstarch with 2 tablespoons of cold water until smooth. Gradually whisk the cornstarch mixture into the simmering juices until the gravy thickens to your desired consistency. Season with salt and pepper to taste.

  8. Carving and Enjoying:

    • Carve the lemon herb roast chicken into slices and serve with the optional gravy alongside your favorite sides such as roasted vegetables, mashed potatoes, or a fresh green salad. Enjoy the tender, juicy goodness with family and friends!

Tips for Success:

  • Choosing the Chicken: Opt for a high-quality, fresh whole chicken for the best flavor and texture.
  • Herb Freshness: Use fresh herbs rather than dried for a more vibrant and aromatic dish.
  • Slow Cooker Settings: Adjust cooking times accordingly based on your slow cooker model to ensure the chicken is cooked through but remains moist.
  • Resting Period: Allowing the chicken to rest after cooking ensures that the juices settle back into the meat, keeping it juicy and flavorful.

This Slow Cooker Lemon Herb Roast Chicken recipe is not only simple to prepare but also yields impressive results that will have your family asking for seconds. With its tender meat infused with zesty lemon and aromatic herbs, it’s a dish that transforms any ordinary day into a special occasion. Whether you’re planning a cozy family dinner or hosting a gathering with friends, this recipe is sure to become a favorite in your kitchen. Enjoy the ease and deliciousness of slow-cooked perfection with every savory bite!
