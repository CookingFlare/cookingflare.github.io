Pasta with Creamy Lemon Garlic Shrimp: A Bright and Flavorful Seafood Dish


Are you ready to elevate your dinner game with a dish that’s as easy to make as it is delicious? Look no further than Pasta with Creamy Lemon Garlic Shrimp. This vibrant seafood dish combines succulent shrimp with a creamy, zesty sauce that clings perfectly to your favorite pasta. Whether you’re cooking for a special occasion or a casual weeknight dinner, this dish is sure to impress. Let’s dive into this culinary delight and explore every aspect of creating this unforgettable meal.

Ingredients You Will Need

To prepare this scrumptious pasta dish, gather the following ingredients:

For the Pasta:

  • 12 ounces of fettuccine or linguine (or pasta of your choice)
  • Salt (for the pasta water)

For the Creamy Lemon Garlic Shrimp:

  • 1 pound of large shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 2 tablespoons of olive oil
  • 4 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon of red pepper flakes (optional, for a bit of heat)
  • 1 cup of heavy cream
  • Zest of 1 lemon
  • 1/4 cup of freshly squeezed lemon juice (about 1-2 lemons)
  • 1/2 cup of grated Parmesan cheese
  • Fresh parsley, chopped (for garnish)
  • Salt and black pepper, to taste

Step-by-Step Instructions

Step 1: Cook the Pasta

  1. Boil the Water: In a large pot, bring salted water to a boil over high heat. The salt is essential as it enhances the pasta’s flavor.

  2. Cook the Pasta: Add the pasta to the boiling water and cook according to package instructions until al dente. Stir occasionally to prevent sticking.

  3. Reserve Pasta Water: Before draining, reserve about a cup of pasta cooking water. This starchy water can help adjust the sauce’s consistency later.

  4. Drain the Pasta: Once cooked, drain the pasta and set it aside. Do not rinse, as the starch helps the sauce cling to the noodles.

Step 2: Prepare the Lemon Garlic Shrimp

  1. Sauté the Shrimp: In a large skillet, heat the olive oil over medium-high heat. Add the shrimp in a single layer and season with salt and black pepper. Cook for about 2-3 minutes on each side until they turn pink and opaque. Remove the shrimp from the skillet and set aside.

  2. Make the Sauce: In the same skillet, add the minced garlic and red pepper flakes (if using). Sauté for about 30 seconds until fragrant, being careful not to burn the garlic.

  3. Add the Cream: Pour in the heavy cream and bring it to a gentle simmer. Stir in the lemon zest and lemon juice. Allow the sauce to simmer for about 2-3 minutes, letting it thicken slightly.

  4. Incorporate the Cheese: Gradually whisk in the grated Parmesan cheese until melted and creamy. If the sauce is too thick, add a splash of reserved pasta water to reach your desired consistency.

  5. Return the Shrimp: Add the cooked shrimp back to the skillet, tossing to coat them in the creamy lemon sauce. Taste and adjust seasoning with additional salt and black pepper if needed.

Step 3: Combine Pasta and Sauce

  1. Mix Together: Add the cooked pasta directly into the skillet with the shrimp and sauce. Toss everything together, ensuring the pasta is well-coated in the creamy lemon garlic sauce. If the dish seems dry, add more reserved pasta water until you achieve the perfect consistency.

  2. Garnish: Sprinkle chopped fresh parsley over the top for a pop of color and freshness.

Step 4: Serve and Enjoy

  1. Plate Your Dish: Serve the pasta with creamy lemon garlic shrimp hot, garnished with extra Parmesan cheese and a wedge of lemon on the side for an additional burst of flavor.

  2. Pairing Suggestions: This dish pairs beautifully with a crisp white wine, such as Sauvignon Blanc or Chardonnay. A side salad with a light vinaigrette can also complement the richness of the pasta.

Tips for the Perfect Creamy Lemon Garlic Shrimp Pasta

  • Quality Ingredients: Use fresh shrimp and high-quality Parmesan cheese for the best flavor. If possible, buy shrimp that has been wild-caught.

  • Don’t Overcook the Shrimp: Shrimp cooks quickly; be attentive to avoid rubbery shrimp. They should be just cooked through, turning pink and opaque.

  • Adjust the Creaminess: If you prefer a lighter sauce, you can substitute half and half or a mix of cream and chicken broth.

  • Add Vegetables: For added nutrition, consider incorporating vegetables such as spinach, cherry tomatoes, or asparagus into the dish.

  • Make it Ahead: While this dish is best enjoyed fresh, you can prep the shrimp and sauce ahead of time and then cook the pasta when you’re ready to serve.

Nutritional Information (Per Serving)

While the exact nutritional content can vary based on specific brands and quantities used, here’s a rough estimate to give you an idea:

  • Calories: 550
  • Protein: 30g
  • Carbohydrates: 60g
  • Fat: 20g

Conclusion

Pasta with Creamy Lemon Garlic Shrimp is more than just a meal; it’s an experience. The bright and zesty flavors of lemon combined with the savory, rich cream create a beautiful harmony that tantalizes the taste buds. This dish is not only impressive but also incredibly satisfying, making it a perfect choice for both casual dinners and special gatherings.

So, the next time you’re looking for a dish that will wow your family or guests, remember this delightful pasta recipe. With its simple preparation and delicious results, it’s sure to become a favorite in your culinary repertoire. Enjoy your cooking journey, and don’t forget to share this delightful dish with your loved ones!
