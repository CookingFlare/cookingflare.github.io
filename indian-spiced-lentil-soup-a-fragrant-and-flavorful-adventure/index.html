Indian Spiced Lentil Soup: A Fragrant and Flavorful Adventure


Are you ready to embark on a culinary journey to the vibrant and aromatic land of India? Look no further than this Indian Spiced Lentil Soup recipe! Bursting with a medley of spices and flavors, this soup will transport your taste buds to the bustling streets of Mumbai and the tranquil backwaters of Kerala. Get ready to indulge in a fragrant and flavorful adventure that will leave you craving for more!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup red lentils
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1-inch piece of ginger, grated
  • 2 tomatoes, finely chopped
  • 1 carrot, finely chopped
  • 1 potato, diced
  • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 1 teaspoon mustard seeds
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 teaspoon coriander powder
  • 1 teaspoon garam masala
  • 1 teaspoon chili powder (adjust according to your spice preference)
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh cilantro, chopped for garnish
  • Lemon wedges for serving

Instructions

  1. Rinse the red lentils thoroughly under running water until the water runs clear. Set aside.

  2. Heat a large pot over medium heat and add a splash of oil. Once the oil is hot, add the cumin seeds and mustard seeds. Allow them to sizzle and release their aroma, which will take about a minute.

  3. Add the finely chopped onion to the pot and sauté until it turns translucent and slightly golden. This will take around 5 minutes.

  4. Now, add the minced garlic and grated ginger to the pot. Sauté for another minute until the raw smell disappears and the spices coat the onion.

  5. Toss in the chopped tomatoes and cook until they soften and release their juices. This should take about 5 minutes.

  6. Add the finely chopped carrot and diced potato to the pot. Stir everything well and let them cook for a couple of minutes.

  7. Now, it’s time to add the spices! Sprinkle in the turmeric powder, coriander powder, garam masala, and chili powder. Mix well to coat the vegetables with the aromatic spices.

  8. Pour in 4 cups of water and bring the mixture to a boil. Once it starts boiling, reduce the heat to low and let it simmer for 15-20 minutes, or until the lentils and vegetables are cooked through and tender.

  9. Season with salt to taste and give everything a good stir. If the soup seems too thick, you can add more water to achieve your desired consistency.

  10. Once the soup is cooked and seasoned to perfection, remove it from the heat. Allow it to cool slightly before serving.

  11. Ladle the fragrant Indian Spiced Lentil Soup into bowls and garnish with freshly chopped cilantro.

  12. Serve the soup with a squeeze of fresh lemon juice for an added burst of tanginess.

A Flavorful Adventure Awaits!

Congratulations! You have successfully created a bowl of Indian Spiced Lentil Soup that is bursting with flavors and aromas. The combination of spices, lentils, and vegetables will take your taste buds on an unforgettable adventure through the diverse and vibrant culinary landscape of India.

This soup is not only a treat for your senses but also a nutritious and wholesome meal. Packed with protein from the lentils and an array of vitamins and minerals from the vegetables, it’s a perfect choice for those looking for a healthy and satisfying meal.

So, go ahead and savor every spoonful of this fragrant and flavorful Indian Spiced Lentil Soup. Let its rich and complex flavors transport you to the streets of Delhi, where the aroma of spices fills the air. Enjoy this culinary adventure, and don’t forget to share it with your loved ones!
