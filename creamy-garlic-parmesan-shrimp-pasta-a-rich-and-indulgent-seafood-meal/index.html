Creamy Garlic Parmesan Shrimp Pasta: A Rich and Indulgent Seafood Meal


If you’re in the mood for a luxurious and flavorful seafood dish, look no further than this Creamy Garlic Parmesan Shrimp Pasta recipe. This dish combines succulent shrimp, al dente pasta, and a velvety, garlicky Parmesan sauce to create a meal that’s perfect for a special occasion or a cozy night in. Let’s dive into the details of how to bring this indulgent dish to life in your own kitchen.

Ingredients

To prepare this delectable Creamy Garlic Parmesan Shrimp Pasta, you’ll need the following ingredients:

  • 1 pound of large shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 12 ounces of fettuccine pasta
  • 4 tablespoons of unsalted butter
  • 4 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 cup of heavy cream
  • 1 cup of grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 teaspoon of Italian seasoning
  • Salt and freshly ground black pepper, to taste
  • Fresh parsley, chopped, for garnish

Instructions

Step 1: Cook the Pasta

Begin by cooking the fettuccine pasta according to the package instructions. Remember to salt the boiling water generously before adding the pasta to infuse it with flavor. Once the pasta is cooked to al dente perfection, drain it and set it aside.

Step 2: Sauté the Shrimp

In a large skillet, melt 2 tablespoons of butter over medium heat. Add the minced garlic to the skillet and sauté for about a minute until it becomes fragrant. Then, add the shrimp to the skillet and season with salt, pepper, and Italian seasoning. Cook the shrimp for 2-3 minutes on each side until they turn pink and opaque. Once the shrimp are cooked through, transfer them to a plate and cover to keep warm.

Step 3: Prepare the Creamy Parmesan Sauce

Using the same skillet, add the remaining 2 tablespoons of butter and pour in the heavy cream. Let the cream simmer gently over medium heat, stirring occasionally, until it starts to thicken slightly. Gradually sprinkle in the grated Parmesan cheese, stirring continuously to ensure it melts and incorporates smoothly into the cream. Continue to cook the sauce until it reaches a velvety consistency that coats the back of a spoon.

Step 4: Combine and Serve

Return the cooked shrimp to the skillet with the creamy Parmesan sauce. Toss the shrimp in the sauce until they are well coated. Then, add the cooked fettuccine to the skillet and gently toss everything together until the pasta is evenly coated with the luscious sauce. If the sauce seems too thick, you can add a splash of pasta cooking water to adjust the consistency.

Step 5: Garnish and Enjoy

Once everything is well combined, sprinkle the dish with freshly chopped parsley for a pop of color and freshness. Serve the Creamy Garlic Parmesan Shrimp Pasta immediately, accompanied by a crisp salad or some crusty bread to soak up any remaining sauce.

Conclusion

Creamy Garlic Parmesan Shrimp Pasta is a dish that’s sure to impress with its rich, indulgent flavors and luxurious texture. Whether you’re hosting a dinner party or simply craving a special meal at home, this recipe is a fantastic choice for anyone who appreciates the decadence of seafood and pasta combined. Give it a try and treat yourself to a truly sumptuous dining experience!
