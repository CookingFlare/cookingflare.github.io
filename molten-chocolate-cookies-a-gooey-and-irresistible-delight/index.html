Molten Chocolate Cookies: A Gooey and Irresistible Delight


Who can resist a warm, gooey, and chocolatey cookie? If you’re a fan of indulgent desserts, then Molten Chocolate Cookies are an absolute must-try! These delectable treats are a heavenly combination of a soft and chewy cookie exterior with a luscious, molten chocolate center. Whether you’re baking them for a special occasion or just treating yourself, these cookies are guaranteed to satisfy your sweet tooth cravings.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup (226g) unsalted butter, melted
  • 2 cups (400g) granulated sugar
  • 4 large eggs
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 1 cup (120g) all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup (60g) cocoa powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 8 ounces (225g) semi-sweet chocolate, chopped into small pieces

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C) and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
  2. In a large mixing bowl, combine the melted butter and granulated sugar. Mix until well combined.
  3. Add the eggs, one at a time, mixing well after each addition. Stir in the vanilla extract.
  4. In a separate bowl, whisk together the all-purpose flour, cocoa powder, and salt. Gradually add the dry ingredients to the wet ingredients, mixing until just combined.
  5. Gently fold in the chopped semi-sweet chocolate, ensuring it is evenly distributed throughout the dough.
  6. Using a cookie scoop or tablespoon, drop rounded portions of dough onto the prepared baking sheet, spacing them about 2 inches apart.
  7. Bake the cookies for 10-12 minutes, or until the edges are set but the centers still appear slightly undercooked.
  8. Remove the baking sheet from the oven and allow the cookies to cool on the sheet for 5 minutes. This will help the centers to set and create that irresistible molten chocolate interior.
  9. Carefully transfer the cookies to a wire rack to cool completely or enjoy them warm for an extra gooey experience.
  10. Repeat the baking process with the remaining dough until all the cookies are baked.

Serving Suggestions

Molten Chocolate Cookies are perfect on their own, but you can take them to the next level by serving them with a scoop of vanilla ice cream or a dollop of freshly whipped cream. The contrast of the warm cookie and the cold creamy topping is simply divine. You can also sprinkle some powdered sugar or drizzle melted chocolate over the cookies for an extra touch of elegance.

Tips and Variations

  • For an even richer chocolate flavor, you can use dark chocolate instead of semi-sweet chocolate.
  • If you prefer a softer and more gooey center, reduce the baking time by a minute or two.
  • Feel free to add in your favorite mix-ins such as chopped nuts, white chocolate chips, or even a sprinkle of sea salt on top.
  • These cookies are best enjoyed fresh out of the oven but can be stored in an airtight container at room temperature for up to 3 days.

Now that you have the recipe for these irresistible Molten Chocolate Cookies, it’s time to get baking! Gather your ingredients, preheat your oven, and get ready to indulge in a gooey and chocolatey delight. Whether you’re sharing them with friends and family or savoring them all to yourself, these cookies are sure to bring a smile to your face and satisfy your sweet cravings. Enjoy!
