Easy Recipes for Homemade Holiday Gifts in a Jar


Holiday season is just around the corner, and it's time to start thinking about gifts for loved ones. Instead of spending money on commercial gifts that might not bring as much joy, why not try making something special by hand? Here are some delicious and creative recipes for homemade holiday gifts in a jar that are perfect for the holidays. Spiced Cranberry Jam Gift Jar This beautiful gift is a lovely combination of flavors and perfect for a winter wonderland-themed gift. Ingredients

1 package of fresh or frozen cranberries 1 cup of granulated sugar 1/4 cup of honey 1 tablespoon of grated orange zest 2 cloves 1 cinnamon stick

Instructions

Rinse the cranberries and pick out any stems. Combine the cranberries, sugar, honey, orange zest, and spices in a medium saucepan. Bring to a boil over medium heat, then reduce the heat to low and simmer for about 10-15 minutes. Remove the saucepan from the heat and let the mixture cool slightly. Use a sterilized canning funnel to fill the jar, leaving about 1/2 inch of space at the top. Seal the jar and label it.

Sugar Cookie Mix in a Jar Homemade cookies are the best, but they can be a pain to make. This simple mix is a thoughtful gift for the baker on your list. Ingredients

2 cups of all-purpose flour 1 teaspoon of baking powder 1 teaspoon of salt 1 teaspoon of baking soda 1 cup of sugar 1/2 cup of brown sugar 1/2 cup of butter 1 egg 1 teaspoon of vanilla

Instructions

Layer the dry ingredients in the jar. In a separate bowl, mix together the wet ingredients and store them in an airtight container. Attach the instructions to the jar, telling the recipient to combine the wet and dry ingredients and press "send" the mixture into balls for baking.

Cinnamon Swirl Sugar Scrub A homemade sugar scrub is a luxurious gift that anyone can enjoy. This sweet treat exfoliates and moisturizes the skin. Ingredients

1 cup of granulated sugar 1/2 cup of coconut oil 2 tablespoons of shea butter 2 tablespoons of honey 1 teaspoon of ground cinnamon

Instructions

Mix together the sugar, coconut oil, shea butter, and honey in a bowl until well combined. Stir in the cinnamon. Spoon the mixture into the jar and attach the instructions, including the steps to use and gift responsibly.

Visiting Hours Spiced Tea Mix A gift that keeps on giving all year round is this warm and comforting spiced tea mix. Perfect for friends and family to enjoy on a cold winter's night. Ingredients

1 cup of loose-leaf tea 1 cinnamon stick 6-8 whole cloves 1/2 teaspoon of ground ginger 1/2 teaspoon of ground cinnamon

Instructions

Layer the tea leaves in the jar. Add the cinnamon stick, cloves, and spices on top of the tea leaves. Provide the simple instructions for preparation, allowing your loved one to infuse and experience the bliss of your homemade gift.

Reduced-Sugar Peppermint Hot Chocolate Mix in a Jar This sweet treat is perfect for chilly winter weather and remarkably easy to make. Ingredients

2 cups of milk powder 1/2 cup of sugar-free cocoa powder 1 tablespoon of sugar-free sweetener 4-5 drops of peppermint extract 1/2 teaspoon of unsweetened vanilla

Instructions

Combine the milk powder, cocoa powder, sugar-free sweetener, and peppermint extract in the jar. Add the vanilla and spoon the mixture into the jar, allowing the recipient to complete the hot chocolate with milk and personal touches.

You don't have to spend hours in the kitchen or save up for months to give someone a truly special holiday gift. With these creative recipes for homemade gifts in a jar, the creative hands of love and care will find a more logical place to express themselves.
