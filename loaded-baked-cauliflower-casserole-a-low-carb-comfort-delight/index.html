Loaded Baked Cauliflower Casserole: A Low-Carb Comfort Delight


If you’re on the lookout for a delicious, low-carb comfort food that won’t leave you feeling guilty, then this Loaded Baked Cauliflower Casserole is the perfect recipe for you. Packed with flavor and wholesome ingredients, this dish will satisfy your cravings while keeping your health goals in check. Whether you’re following a low-carb diet or just looking to add more veggies to your meals, this cauliflower casserole is a winner.

Ingredients

  • 1 head of cauliflower, cut into florets
  • 6 slices of bacon, chopped
  • 1/2 cup of sour cream
  • 1/4 cup of cream cheese, softened
  • 1 cup of shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1/2 cup of chopped green onions
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon of onion powder
  • 1 teaspoon of paprika
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C). Grease a baking dish with butter or cooking spray.
  2. In a large pot of boiling water, cook the cauliflower florets for 5-6 minutes or until they are just tender. Drain and set aside.
  3. In a skillet over medium heat, cook the chopped bacon until crispy. Remove the bacon from the skillet and set it aside on a paper towel to drain.
  4. In a large bowl, combine the sour cream, softened cream cheese, 3/4 cup of shredded cheddar cheese, chopped green onions, minced garlic, onion powder, paprika, salt, and pepper. Mix until well combined.
  5. Gently fold in the cooked cauliflower and half of the cooked bacon into the cheese mixture until the cauliflower is evenly coated.
  6. Transfer the cauliflower mixture to the greased baking dish, spreading it out evenly.
  7. Top the cauliflower with the remaining 1/4 cup of shredded cheddar cheese and the remaining half of the cooked bacon.
  8. Bake the casserole in the preheated oven for 20-25 minutes or until the cheese is bubbly and golden brown.
  9. Once done, remove the casserole from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes before serving.

Serving Suggestions

This Loaded Baked Cauliflower Casserole is a versatile dish that can be served as a main course or as a side dish. Pair it with a fresh green salad for a light and satisfying meal, or serve it alongside grilled chicken or steak for a hearty dinner. The creamy texture and savory flavors of this casserole make it a crowd-pleaser, perfect for family gatherings, potlucks, or casual weeknight dinners.

Nutritional Information

This cauliflower casserole not only tastes amazing but also offers a nutritional boost. Cauliflower is rich in vitamins, minerals, and antioxidants, making it a great addition to any diet. With the added protein from the bacon and cheese, this dish provides a balanced mix of nutrients while keeping the carb count low. It’s a win-win for both your taste buds and your well-being.

Conclusion

Loaded Baked Cauliflower Casserole is a fantastic way to enjoy the comforting flavors of a loaded baked potato without the guilt. Whether you’re a cauliflower enthusiast or someone looking to try new low-carb recipes, this casserole is sure to become a favorite in your collection. Give it a try and savor the deliciousness of this low-carb comfort delight.
