Grilled Caesar Wrap: A Twist on a Classic Fourth of July Dish


The Fourth of July is a time for celebration, and what better way to mark the occasion than with a delicious twist on a classic dish? This Grilled Caesar Wrap takes the traditional flavors of a Caesar salad and elevates them with the addition of a smoky, grilled touch. Whether you’re hosting a backyard barbecue or simply looking for a new recipe to try, this Grilled Caesar Wrap is sure to be a hit with your friends and family.

Ingredients

For the Grilled Chicken:

  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon onion powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste

For the Caesar Dressing:

  • 1/2 cup mayonnaise
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 tablespoons lemon juice
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon Dijon mustard
  • Salt and pepper to taste

For the Wrap:

  • 4 large flour tortillas
  • Romaine lettuce, chopped
  • 1/2 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • Croutons

Instructions

Step 1: Grill the Chicken

  1. Preheat your grill to medium-high heat.
  2. In a small bowl, mix together the olive oil, garlic powder, onion powder, salt, and pepper.
  3. Brush the chicken breasts with the olive oil mixture.
  4. Grill the chicken for 6-7 minutes on each side, or until the internal temperature reaches 165°F.

Step 2: Prepare the Caesar Dressing

  1. In a bowl, whisk together the mayonnaise, grated Parmesan cheese, lemon juice, minced garlic, Dijon mustard, salt, and pepper.
  2. Adjust the seasoning to taste and set the dressing aside.

Step 3: Assemble the Wraps

  1. Lay out the tortillas and divide the chopped Romaine lettuce among them.
  2. Slice the grilled chicken breasts and place them on top of the lettuce.
  3. Add the cherry tomatoes, grated Parmesan cheese, and croutons.
  4. Drizzle the Caesar dressing over the fillings.
  5. Tightly roll up the wraps, tucking in the sides as you go.

Step 4: Grill the Wraps

  1. Preheat a grill pan over medium heat.
  2. Place the wraps seam side down on the grill pan and cook for 2-3 minutes on each side, or until golden brown and crispy.

Serving Suggestions

Once the wraps are grilled to perfection, slice them in half diagonally and serve with extra Caesar dressing on the side for dipping. Pair them with a refreshing iced tea or a classic lemonade for a delightful Fourth of July meal.

Final Thoughts

This Grilled Caesar Wrap is a wonderful way to put a new spin on a timeless favorite. The combination of smoky grilled chicken, crisp lettuce, tangy dressing, and crunchy croutons creates a medley of flavors and textures that will delight your taste buds. Whether you’re enjoying a picnic in the park or a gathering in your own backyard, this recipe is a surefire way to impress your guests and add a touch of flair to your Fourth of July celebration.

So, why not add a dash of creativity to your Fourth of July menu this year and try your hand at these Grilled Caesar Wraps? You’ll be glad you did! Happy grilling, and happy Fourth of July!
