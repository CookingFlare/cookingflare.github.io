The Classic Old Fashioned Pie: A Timeless Whiskey Delight


Are you a fan of classic desserts with a twist? Look no further than the Classic Old Fashioned Pie! This delightful dessert takes inspiration from the iconic Old Fashioned cocktail, infusing the flavors of whiskey, orange, and aromatic bitters into a rich and creamy pie filling. Prepare to indulge in a dessert that is both nostalgic and undeniably delicious.

Ingredients

For the crust:

  • 2 ½ cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup unsalted butter, cold and cubed
  • ¼ cup ice-cold water

For the filling:

  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • ½ cup unsalted butter, melted
  • ¼ cup all-purpose flour
  • 3 large eggs
  • ½ cup whiskey
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • Zest of 1 orange
  • ¼ teaspoon aromatic bitters
  • Pinch of salt

For the topping:

  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 2 tablespoons powdered sugar
  • Orange zest, for garnish

Instructions

  1. Prepare the crust: In a large mixing bowl, combine the all-purpose flour and salt. Add the cold, cubed butter and use a pastry cutter or your fingertips to cut the butter into the flour until the mixture resembles coarse crumbs.

  2. Gradually add the ice-cold water, one tablespoon at a time, mixing gently with your hands until the dough comes together. Be careful not to overmix. Shape the dough into a disc, wrap it in plastic wrap, and refrigerate for at least 30 minutes.

  3. Preheat the oven: While the dough chills, preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).

  4. Roll out the crust: On a lightly floured surface, roll out the chilled dough to fit a 9-inch pie dish. Carefully transfer the rolled-out dough to the pie dish, gently pressing it into the bottom and sides. Trim any excess dough and crimp the edges.

  5. Prepare the filling: In a medium-sized mixing bowl, whisk together the granulated sugar, melted butter, all-purpose flour, eggs, whiskey, vanilla extract, orange zest, aromatic bitters, and a pinch of salt. Mix until well combined and smooth.

  6. Bake the pie: Pour the filling into the prepared crust and smooth the top with a spatula. Place the pie on a baking sheet and bake in the preheated oven for 40-45 minutes, or until the filling is set and golden brown.

  7. Cool and serve: Remove the pie from the oven and let it cool completely on a wire rack. In the meantime, whip the heavy cream and powdered sugar together until stiff peaks form. Spread the whipped cream over the cooled pie and garnish with orange zest.

  8. Enjoy: Slice into this Classic Old Fashioned Pie and savor each bite. The combination of the whiskey-infused filling, zesty orange, and the creamy whipped topping will transport you to a world of sophisticated flavors.

Conclusion

The Classic Old Fashioned Pie is a dessert that pays homage to the timeless Old Fashioned cocktail. With its rich and creamy filling infused with the flavors of whiskey, orange, and aromatic bitters, this pie is sure to impress your guests and leave them asking for seconds. Whether you’re hosting a dinner party or simply indulging in a sweet treat, this pie is a delightful way to elevate your dessert game. Give it a try and experience the magic of a timeless whiskey delight!
