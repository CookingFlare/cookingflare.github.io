Greek Lemon Chicken Soup: A Comforting and Zesty Dish


There’s something truly magical about a bowl of steaming hot soup, especially when it’s as comforting and flavorful as Greek Lemon Chicken Soup. This traditional Greek dish, also known as Avgolemono, is a delightful combination of tender chicken, orzo pasta, and a bright, zesty lemon-infused broth. Whether you’re looking for a soul-warming meal on a chilly day or simply want to explore the rich flavors of Mediterranean cuisine, this recipe is sure to become a favorite in your kitchen.

Ingredients

Before we dive into the cooking process, let’s take a look at the key ingredients that make this Greek Lemon Chicken Soup so special:

  • Chicken: You’ll need boneless, skinless chicken breasts for this recipe. The tender, juicy meat adds a hearty element to the soup.

  • Orzo Pasta: This small, rice-shaped pasta is a staple in Greek cooking and provides a delightful texture to the soup.

  • Eggs: The eggs are a crucial component of the Avgolemono sauce, which gives the soup its signature creamy texture and a hint of richness.

  • Lemons: Freshly squeezed lemon juice is the star of the show here, infusing the soup with a refreshing citrus flavor.

  • Chicken Broth: A good-quality chicken broth forms the base of the soup, imparting depth and savory notes to the dish.

  • Herbs: A touch of dried dill and fresh parsley adds an aromatic, herbaceous quality to the soup.

Now that we’ve gathered our ingredients, let’s move on to the cooking process.

Cooking Instructions

Step 1: Poach the Chicken

Begin by poaching the chicken breasts in a pot of simmering water. This gentle cooking method ensures that the meat stays tender and moist. Once the chicken is cooked through, remove it from the pot and set it aside to cool before shredding it into bite-sized pieces.

Step 2: Cook the Orzo

In a separate pot, cook the orzo pasta according to the package instructions. Be sure to stir occasionally to prevent the pasta from sticking together. Once the orzo is al dente, drain it and set it aside.

Step 3: Prepare the Avgolemono Sauce

The Avgolemono sauce is what gives this soup its distinctive creamy texture and bright flavor. In a mixing bowl, whisk together the eggs and freshly squeezed lemon juice until well combined.

Step 4: Bring It All Together

Now, it’s time to bring everything together. In a large pot, combine the shredded chicken, cooked orzo, and chicken broth. Bring the mixture to a gentle simmer over medium heat. Once the broth is hot, carefully ladle a small amount of the hot broth into the bowl with the Avgolemono sauce, whisking constantly. This step helps temper the eggs, preventing them from curdling when added to the hot soup.

Step 5: Add the Lemon Infusion

Gradually pour the tempered Avgolemono sauce into the pot of soup, stirring continuously. The addition of the lemon-infused sauce will transform the broth into a velvety, lemony elixir.

Step 6: Season and Serve

Finally, season the soup with a sprinkle of dried dill and freshly chopped parsley. Taste and adjust the seasoning with salt and pepper as needed. Ladle the Greek Lemon Chicken Soup into bowls and serve it piping hot, perhaps with a crusty loaf of bread on the side for dipping.

Final Thoughts

Whether you’re seeking solace on a cold evening or yearning to savor the bright, sunny flavors of Greece, this soup is a must-try. Enjoy the journey of flavors and aromas as you prepare this heartwarming Greek Lemon Chicken Soup.
