Tasty Keto Garlic Parmesan Roasted Brussels Sprouts: A Flavorful Side


Are you looking for a delicious and healthy side dish that will leave your taste buds begging for more? Look no further than these Tasty Keto Garlic Parmesan Roasted Brussels Sprouts! Packed with flavor and low in carbs, this dish is perfect for those following a keto diet or simply looking to add more veggies to their meals.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound Brussels sprouts, trimmed and halved
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C). Line a baking sheet with parchment paper or foil for easy cleanup.

  2. In a large bowl, combine the Brussels sprouts, olive oil, minced garlic, salt, and black pepper. Toss well to coat the sprouts evenly with the seasonings.

  3. Spread the Brussels sprouts in a single layer on the prepared baking sheet. Make sure they are not overcrowded to allow for even roasting.

  4. Place the baking sheet in the preheated oven and roast for 25-30 minutes, or until the Brussels sprouts are tender and lightly browned. Give them a gentle stir halfway through the cooking time to ensure even browning.

  5. Once the Brussels sprouts are roasted to perfection, remove them from the oven and sprinkle the grated Parmesan cheese over the top. Return the baking sheet to the oven for an additional 3-5 minutes, or until the cheese is melted and slightly golden.

  6. Remove from the oven and let the Brussels sprouts cool for a few minutes before serving. The crispy exterior and tender interior make for the perfect texture combination.

  7. Transfer the roasted Brussels sprouts to a serving dish and garnish with some extra Parmesan cheese, if desired. Serve hot and enjoy!

Why Brussels Sprouts?

Not only are Brussels sprouts delicious, but they are also incredibly nutritious. These little green gems are packed with vitamins, minerals, and fiber, making them an excellent addition to any meal. Brussels sprouts are also low in calories and carbohydrates, making them a great choice for those following a keto or low-carb diet.

Garlic and Parmesan: A Match Made in Flavor Heaven

The combination of garlic and Parmesan in this recipe takes the flavor of these Brussels sprouts to a whole new level. The garlic adds a savory kick, while the Parmesan cheese adds a rich and nutty taste. The result is a side dish that is bursting with flavor and will leave you craving more.

Versatility at Its Finest

These Tasty Keto Garlic Parmesan Roasted Brussels Sprouts can be enjoyed on their own as a side dish, or you can get creative and use them in other ways. Add them to salads, pasta dishes, or even omelets for an extra burst of flavor and texture. The possibilities are endless!

Final Thoughts

If you’re looking for a flavorful, low-carb side dish that will please even the pickiest eaters, these Tasty Keto Garlic Parmesan Roasted Brussels Sprouts are a must-try. With their crispy exterior, tender interior, and irresistible garlic and Parmesan flavor, they are sure to become a family favorite. Give this recipe a try and let your taste buds thank you!
