Delicious Low Carb Spinach and Feta Stuffed Chicken


Are you looking for a mouthwatering low-carb dish that’s bursting with flavor? Look no further than this delicious spinach and feta stuffed chicken recipe! It’s a perfect combination of juicy chicken breasts, savory spinach, and creamy feta cheese, all wrapped up in a tender and flavorful package. This recipe is not only satisfying and delicious but also low in carbohydrates, making it an ideal choice for those looking to enjoy a healthy and balanced meal. Let’s dive into the details of this delectable dish and get cooking!

Ingredients

  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 2 cups fresh spinach, chopped
  • 1/2 cup crumbled feta cheese
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • Toothpicks

Instructions

  1. Preheat the Oven: Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C) and prepare a baking dish by lightly greasing it with olive oil or non-stick cooking spray.

  2. Prepare the Chicken: Place the chicken breasts on a clean, flat surface. Using a sharp knife, carefully slice each chicken breast horizontally to create a pocket without cutting all the way through. Be sure not to slice all the way to the edges.

  3. Prepare the Filling: In a mixing bowl, combine the chopped spinach, crumbled feta cheese, minced garlic, dried oregano, salt, and black pepper. Mix everything together until well combined.

  4. Stuff the Chicken: Spoon the spinach and feta filling into the pockets of the chicken breasts, dividing it evenly among them. Use toothpicks to secure the openings and keep the filling in place.

  5. Season the Chicken: Brush the stuffed chicken breasts with olive oil and season them with a pinch of salt and black pepper for an extra burst of flavor.

  6. Bake the Chicken: Place the stuffed chicken breasts in the prepared baking dish and transfer them to the preheated oven. Bake for 25-30 minutes or until the chicken is cooked through and the juices run clear.

  7. Serve and Enjoy: Once the chicken is cooked, remove it from the oven and let it rest for a few minutes. Then, carefully remove the toothpicks and serve the spinach and feta stuffed chicken hot, accompanied by your favorite low-carb side dishes.

Tips and Variations

  • Feel free to customize the filling by adding sun-dried tomatoes, chopped olives, or even a sprinkle of grated Parmesan cheese for an extra flavor boost.
  • For a golden and crispy exterior, you can sear the stuffed chicken breasts in a hot skillet before transferring them to the oven.
  • If you’re short on time, you can use a meat mallet to pound the chicken breasts to an even thickness, making them easier to stuff and cook more quickly.

Final Thoughts

This delightful spinach and feta stuffed chicken recipe is a true crowd-pleaser, perfect for both casual weeknight dinners and special occasions. The combination of tender chicken, flavorful spinach, and rich feta cheese creates a dish that’s as impressive as it is delicious. Plus, with its low-carb profile, it’s a fantastic option for anyone looking to indulge in a satisfying meal without the guilt. So, gather your ingredients, get creative with your fillings, and enjoy the delightful flavors of this delectable low-carb dish!
