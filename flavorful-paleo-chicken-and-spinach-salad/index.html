Flavorful Paleo Chicken and Spinach Salad


If you’re looking for a delicious and healthy meal that’s easy to prepare, this flavorful Paleo Chicken and Spinach Salad is the perfect choice. Packed with protein, nutrients, and vibrant flavors, this salad is a great option for anyone following a paleo lifestyle or simply looking for a nutritious meal. Let’s dive into the recipe and explore the wonderful combination of ingredients that make this salad a standout dish.

Ingredients

For the Chicken:

  • 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste

For the Salad:

  • 6 cups fresh spinach leaves
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1 cucumber, sliced
  • 1/4 red onion, thinly sliced
  • 1/4 cup sliced almonds

For the Dressing:

  • 3 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons balsamic vinegar
  • 1 teaspoon Dijon mustard
  • 1 teaspoon honey
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

Step 1: Prepare the Chicken

  1. Begin by seasoning the chicken breasts with paprika, garlic powder, salt, and pepper.
  2. In a skillet over medium heat, add the olive oil and place the seasoned chicken breasts. Cook for 6-7 minutes on each side or until fully cooked.
  3. Once cooked, remove the chicken from the skillet and let it rest for a few minutes. Then, slice the chicken into thin strips.

Step 2: Prepare the Salad

  1. In a large bowl, combine the fresh spinach, cherry tomatoes, sliced cucumber, red onion, and sliced almonds.
  2. Toss the salad gently to mix the ingredients evenly.

Step 3: Prepare the Dressing

  1. In a small bowl, whisk together the extra virgin olive oil, balsamic vinegar, Dijon mustard, honey, salt, and pepper until well combined.

Step 4: Assemble the Salad

  1. Place the sliced chicken on top of the prepared salad.
  2. Drizzle the dressing over the salad and chicken.

Serving Suggestion

Once the salad is assembled, it’s ready to be served. This Flavorful Paleo Chicken and Spinach Salad makes for a satisfying and wholesome meal on its own. However, if you’d like to add a bit more substance to the dish, consider pairing it with a side of sweet potato fries or a warm Paleo-friendly bread.

Nutritional Benefits

This salad is not only delicious but also offers a wealth of nutritional benefits. The chicken provides a lean source of protein, while the spinach offers an abundance of vitamins and minerals. The colorful array of vegetables in the salad adds fiber, antioxidants, and essential nutrients to the meal. With the addition of healthy fats from the olive oil and almonds, this salad truly encompasses a well-rounded and nourishing dining experience.

Final Thoughts

The Flavorful Paleo Chicken and Spinach Salad is a testament to the fact that healthy eating can be both satisfying and delicious. Whether you’re following a paleo lifestyle or simply seeking a nutrient-packed meal, this salad is sure to become a favorite in your recipe collection. It’s versatile, easy to customize, and perfect for meal prepping. So, go ahead and give this recipe a try, and enjoy the vibrant flavors and wholesome goodness it has to offer!
