Paleo Breakfast Skillet with Sausage and Sweet Potatoes: A Filling and Healthy Option


If you’re looking for a breakfast recipe that’s both filling and healthy, look no further than this Paleo Breakfast Skillet with Sausage and Sweet Potatoes. This recipe is a game-changer for anyone who wants to start their day off right with a nutrient-dense meal that’s easy to make and packed with flavor.

Why This Recipe is a Winner

This recipe checks all the boxes for a successful breakfast. It’s made with wholesome ingredients like sweet potatoes, sausage, and eggs, which provide a boost of protein, fiber, and vitamins. The sweet potatoes add a natural sweetness and creamy texture, while the sausage provides a savory flavor. The eggs, of course, add a richness and creaminess to the dish.

The Best Part? It’s Gluten-Free and Paleo-Friendly

This recipe is perfect for those who follow a gluten-free or Paleo diet. The sausage is made with natural ingredients and no added preservatives, and the sweet potatoes are a great source of fiber and vitamins. The eggs are, of course, a staple in any Paleo diet, and they provide a great source of protein.

The Recipe

Here’s what you’ll need:

  • 1 large sweet potato, peeled and diced
  • 1 pound sweet Italian sausage, casings removed
  • 4 large eggs
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Optional: chopped fresh herbs like parsley or cilantro for garnish

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C).
  2. In a large skillet, heat the olive oil over medium-high heat. Add the diced sweet potatoes and cook for about 5 minutes, or until they start to soften.
  3. Add the sweet Italian sausage to the skillet, breaking it up with a spoon as it cooks. Cook for about 5-7 minutes, or until the sausage is no longer pink and the sweet potatoes are tender.
  4. Crack the eggs into a bowl and whisk them together with a fork. Pour the eggs over the sweet potatoes and sausage in the skillet.
  5. Transfer the skillet to the preheated oven and bake for about 15-20 minutes, or until the eggs are set and the sweet potatoes are tender.
  6. Remove the skillet from the oven and season with salt and pepper to taste. Garnish with chopped fresh herbs, if desired.
  7. Serve the Paleo Breakfast Skillet hot, straight from the skillet.

Tips and Variations

  • If you prefer a spicier sausage, use hot Italian sausage instead of sweet.
  • Add some diced bell peppers or onions to the skillet with the sweet potatoes for added flavor.
  • Use different seasonings like paprika or garlic powder to add extra flavor to the dish.
  • Serve the Paleo Breakfast Skillet with a side of fresh fruit or a green salad for a well-rounded breakfast.

Conclusion

This Paleo Breakfast Skillet with Sausage and Sweet Potatoes is a filling and healthy breakfast option that’s perfect for anyone who wants to start their day off right. With its wholesome ingredients and delicious flavor, it’s a recipe that’s sure to become a staple in your breakfast rotation. So go ahead, give it a try, and start your day off with a delicious and nutritious meal that will keep you going all morning long.
