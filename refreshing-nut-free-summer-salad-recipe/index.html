Refreshing Nut-Free Summer Salad Recipe


Summer is the perfect time to indulge in light and refreshing salads that are not only delicious but also healthy. If you’re looking for a nut-free option that will satisfy your taste buds, then you’ve come to the right place! This refreshing nut-free summer salad recipe is packed with vibrant flavors and nutritious ingredients that will leave you feeling energized and satisfied. Let’s dive right in!

Ingredients

  • 2 cups mixed greens
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1 small cucumber, diced
  • 1 red bell pepper, thinly sliced
  • 1/2 red onion, thinly sliced
  • 1/4 cup Kalamata olives, pitted and halved
  • 1/4 cup feta cheese, crumbled
  • 2 tablespoons fresh basil, chopped

Dressing

  • 3 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons lemon juice
  • 1 tablespoon honey
  • 1 teaspoon Dijon mustard
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. In a large salad bowl, combine the mixed greens, cherry tomatoes, cucumber, red bell pepper, red onion, Kalamata olives, feta cheese, and fresh basil.

  2. In a separate small bowl, whisk together the olive oil, lemon juice, honey, Dijon mustard, salt, and pepper. This dressing will add a tangy and slightly sweet flavor to the salad.

  3. Pour the dressing over the salad ingredients and gently toss until everything is well coated. Make sure to mix it thoroughly, so every bite is bursting with flavor.

  4. Once the salad is evenly dressed, let it sit for a few minutes to allow the flavors to meld together. This step is crucial for achieving the perfect balance of taste.

  5. Serve the salad chilled and enjoy! It’s a great dish to bring to summer gatherings or to enjoy as a light and healthy meal on its own.

Health Benefits

Not only is this salad incredibly delicious, but it also provides a wide range of health benefits. Let’s take a closer look at some of the ingredients and their nutritional value:

  • Mixed greens: Packed with vitamins A, C, and K, as well as folate, iron, and calcium.
  • Cherry tomatoes: Rich in antioxidants and vitamins A and C, which promote healthy skin and boost the immune system.
  • Cucumber: Hydrating and low in calories, cucumbers are an excellent source of hydration during the hot summer months.
  • Red bell pepper: High in vitamin C and antioxidants that support healthy skin, improve digestion, and boost the immune system.
  • Kalamata olives: Provide healthy monounsaturated fats and contain beneficial antioxidants.
  • Feta cheese: Adds a tangy flavor while providing a good source of protein and calcium.
  • Basil: Contains essential oils that have antibacterial properties and are rich in antioxidants.

Final Thoughts

This refreshing nut-free summer salad recipe is not only a feast for the eyes but also a treat for your taste buds. It’s the perfect combination of fresh and vibrant ingredients, topped with a tangy dressing that brings all the flavors together. Whether you’re looking for a light lunch, a side dish, or a crowd-pleasing salad to bring to a summer party, this recipe has got you covered. So, grab your salad bowl and get ready to enjoy a nutritious and delicious meal that will leave you feeling refreshed and satisfied. Cheers to a healthy and flavorful summer!
