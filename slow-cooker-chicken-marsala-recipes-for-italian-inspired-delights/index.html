Slow Cooker Chicken Marsala Recipes for Italian-Inspired Delights


Are you craving a taste of Italy but don’t have the time to spend hours in the kitchen? Look no further! In this post, we’ll explore some mouthwatering Slow Cooker Chicken Marsala recipes that will transport you to the heart of Italy with every bite. These recipes are not only delicious but also incredibly easy to make, thanks to the convenience of a slow cooker. So, let’s dive right into the world of Italian-inspired delights!

Slow Cooker Chicken Marsala

Ingredients:

  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1 cup Marsala wine
  • 1 cup chicken broth
  • 1 cup sliced mushrooms
  • 1 medium onion, thinly sliced
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • 1 teaspoon dried rosemary
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Season the chicken breasts with salt, pepper, thyme, and rosemary. Set aside.
  2. In a skillet, heat the olive oil and butter over medium-high heat. Add the chicken breasts and brown them on both sides until golden. Remove the chicken and set aside.
  3. In the same skillet, add the sliced onions and mushrooms. Sauté until they are softened and slightly browned.
  4. Add the minced garlic to the skillet and cook for an additional minute, until fragrant.
  5. Sprinkle the flour over the onions, mushrooms, and garlic. Stir well to coat.
  6. Slowly pour in the Marsala wine and chicken broth. Stir continuously until the mixture thickens slightly.
  7. Transfer the chicken breasts and the Marsala sauce to the slow cooker. Cover and cook on low heat for 4-6 hours or until the chicken is tender and cooked through.
  8. Serve the Slow Cooker Chicken Marsala over pasta or mashed potatoes. Garnish with fresh parsley for an added pop of color and flavor.

Slow Cooker Chicken Marsala with a Twist

Looking to spice things up a bit? Try this variation of the classic Slow Cooker Chicken Marsala recipe that adds a touch of heat and depth of flavor to this Italian favorite.

Ingredients:

  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1 cup Marsala wine
  • 1 cup chicken broth
  • 1 cup sliced mushrooms
  • 1 medium onion, thinly sliced
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • 1 teaspoon dried rosemary
  • 1 teaspoon red pepper flakes (adjust to taste)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Follow the same steps as the classic Slow Cooker Chicken Marsala recipe above until step 6.
  2. Add the red pepper flakes to the skillet along with the flour, onions, mushrooms, and garlic. Stir well to coat.
  3. Slowly pour in the Marsala wine and chicken broth. Stir continuously until the mixture thickens slightly.
  4. Transfer the chicken breasts and the Marsala sauce to the slow cooker. Cover and cook on low heat for 4-6 hours or until the chicken is tender and cooked through.
  5. Serve the Slow Cooker Chicken Marsala with a Twist over rice or roasted potatoes for a flavorful and satisfying meal.

Conclusion

There you have it, two tantalizing Slow Cooker Chicken Marsala recipes that are sure to impress your taste buds and transport you to the charming streets of Italy. The convenience of the slow cooker makes these dishes perfect for busy weeknights or lazy weekends when you want to savor the flavors of a traditional Italian meal without the fuss. So, gather your ingredients, set up your slow cooker, and let the magic of these recipes unfold. Buon appetito!
