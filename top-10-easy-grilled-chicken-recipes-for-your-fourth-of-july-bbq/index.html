Top 10 Easy Grilled Chicken Recipes for Your Fourth of July BBQ


Grilling is a time-honored tradition for celebrating America’s Independence Day. Juicy, tender, and bursting with flavor, grilled chicken takes center stage at many Fourth of July BBQs. Whether you’re a seasoned grill master or firing up the coals for the first time, these top 10 easy grilled chicken recipes will make your celebration a delicious success. These recipes are simple, packed with flavor, and guaranteed to delight your family and friends. Let’s dive into the list of mouthwatering ideas to elevate your star-spangled holiday.

1. Classic Barbecue Grilled Chicken

When it comes to Fourth of July feasts, classic barbecue never goes out of style. This recipe features bone-in chicken pieces slathered generously with your favorite barbecue sauce.

Ingredients:

  • Chicken drumsticks or thighs
  • Barbecue sauce
  • Olive oil
  • Salt and black pepper

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your grill to medium heat.
  2. Brush the chicken pieces with olive oil, then season with salt and pepper.
  3. Place the chicken on the grill and cook for about 25-30 minutes, flipping occasionally to ensure even cooking.
  4. During the last 10 minutes of grilling, brush barbecue sauce onto the chicken pieces, letting the sauce caramelize slightly for extra flavor.

This classic recipe provides that smoky, sticky sweetness we all crave on a summer holiday.

2. Lemon Herb Grilled Chicken Breasts

Lighter and fresher, this lemon herb option is as simple as it is flavorful. Perfect for those who enjoy a zesty twist.

Ingredients:

  • Boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • Lemon juice and zest
  • Fresh rosemary and thyme
  • Olive oil
  • Garlic (minced)
  • Salt and black pepper

Instructions:

  1. Mix lemon juice, zest, olive oil, minced garlic, rosemary, thyme, salt, and pepper in a bowl to create a marinade.
  2. Marinate the chicken for at least 2 hours (overnight for best results).
  3. Heat your grill and cook the chicken for 6-8 minutes per side, or until fully cooked.

The citrusy brightness complements the smoky char from the grill, making this recipe a crowd-pleaser.

3. Spicy Cajun Grilled Chicken

For those who love a little kick in their meals, this spicy Cajun recipe is the answer. Featuring bold seasonings and vibrant heat, it’s guaranteed to spice up your barbecue menu.

Ingredients:

  • Chicken thigh fillets
  • Cajun seasoning
  • Olive oil
  • Garlic powder
  • Paprika
  • Cayenne pepper (optional for extra heat)

Instructions:

  1. Combine olive oil and Cajun seasoning in a bowl, adding garlic powder and paprika for extra flavor.
  2. Rub the mixture generously over the chicken thighs, ensuring they are fully coated.
  3. Grill the chicken thighs for 5-7 minutes per side over medium heat, or until cooked through.

This bold recipe adds an energetic kick to any Fourth of July gathering.

4. Honey Garlic Grilled Chicken Wings

Who doesn’t love chicken wings at a party? These honey-garlic grilled wings combine sweet and savory flavors for a sticky, irresistible treat.

Ingredients:

  • Chicken wings
  • Honey
  • Soy sauce
  • Garlic (minced)
  • Olive oil

Instructions:

  1. Whisk together honey, soy sauce, minced garlic, and olive oil. Marinate wings in the mixture for at least 1 hour.
  2. Preheat your grill to medium-high heat and grill the wings for 10-12 minutes, flipping halfway.
  3. Baste the wings with additional honey during the final minutes of grilling for a glazed finish.

These wings are finger-licking good and perfect as an appetizer or main dish.

5. Teriyaki Grilled Chicken Skewers

Upgrade your barbecue spread with these flavorful teriyaki skewers. With their mix of sweet and savory flavors, they’re guaranteed to impress.

Ingredients:

  • Chicken breast (cubed)
  • Teriyaki sauce
  • Pineapple chunks
  • Bell peppers (cubed)
  • Wooden skewers (soaked in water)

Instructions:

  1. Marinate the chicken cubes in teriyaki sauce for at least 1 hour.
  2. Assemble skewers by alternating chicken, pineapple, and bell peppers.
  3. Grill the skewers for 8-10 minutes, turning frequently, until the chicken is fully cooked.

The combination of chicken and juicy pineapple results in a harmonious blend of flavors.

6. Greek-Style Grilled Chicken

Bring Mediterranean vibes to your Fourth of July with Greek-style grilled chicken that’s bursting with oregano and garlic goodness.

Ingredients:

  • Chicken thighs (bone-in)
  • Olive oil
  • Lemon juice
  • Garlic (minced)
  • Dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, mix olive oil, lemon juice, minced garlic, dried oregano, salt, and pepper.
  2. Marinate chicken thighs in this mixture for at least 2 hours.
  3. Grill the chicken for 15-20 minutes over medium heat, or until cooked through.

Serve this chicken with warm pita bread and tzatziki for an unforgettable summer meal.

7. Sweet & Smoky Chipotle Chicken

This recipe combines smoky chipotle flavor with a touch of sweetness, creating a balance that’s heavenly for your taste buds.

Ingredients:

  • Chicken drumsticks
  • Chipotle chili powder
  • Brown sugar
  • Olive oil
  • Garlic powder
  • Salt and pepper

Instructions:

  1. Rub the drumsticks with a mixture of chipotle powder, brown sugar, garlic powder, and olive oil.
  2. Grill the drumsticks for 25-30 minutes, turning occasionally to prevent charring.

This sweet and smoky combination is ideal for those looking to mix things up on their grill.

8. Grilled Chicken Caesar Salad

Add a lighter touch to your holiday with this grilled chicken Caesar recipe.

Ingredients:

  • Chicken breasts
  • Caesar salad dressing
  • Romaine lettuce
  • Freshly grated Parmesan cheese

Instructions:

  1. Marinate chicken breasts in Caesar dressing for 1-2 hours.
  2. Grill the chicken for 6-8 minutes on each side, then slice into strips.
  3. Grill halved heads of romaine lettuce for 1-2 minutes for a smoky touch and assemble the salad with chicken and Parmesan.

This refreshing dish is perfect for a July day.

9. Thai Peanut Grilled Chicken

Break out exotic flavors at your barbecue with Thai-inspired grilled chicken served alongside a flavorful peanut sauce.

Ingredients:

  • Chicken thighs
  • Peanut butter
  • Soy sauce
  • Lime juice
  • Brown sugar
  • Garlic (minced)

Instructions:

  1. Prepare a marinade with peanut butter, soy sauce, lime juice, brown sugar, and garlic, and coat the chicken.
  2. Grill the chicken for 6-8 minutes per side, brushing peanut sauce over the meat while cooking.

The end result is a bold, nutty flavor that’ll have everyone begging for the recipe.

10. Herb-Marinated Chicken Drumsticks

For a simple yet sophisticated option, try this herb-marinated recipe that’s versatile and full of fresh garden flavors.

Ingredients:

  • Chicken drumsticks
  • Fresh parsley, basil, and thyme (chopped)
  • Olive oil
  • Garlic (minced)
  • Lemon zest
  • Salt and pepper

Instructions:

  1. Marinate drumsticks in olive oil, chopped herbs, minced garlic, lemon zest, salt, and pepper for at least 2 hours.
  2. Grill the drumsticks for 25-30 minutes, turning frequently.

This recipe brings out the natural flavor of the chicken while offering a delightful herbal kick.

Any of these top 10 grilled chicken recipes will make your Fourth of July BBQ a star-spangled hit. Each dish is simple to prepare, bursting with flavor, and perfect for outdoor grilling fun. Choose your favorite, fire up the grill, and enjoy the rich aroma of sizzling chicken as you celebrate Independence Day with your loved ones.

Whether you prefer sweet, spicy, zesty, or smoky flavors, there’s something here for everyone to enjoy. Happy grilling!
