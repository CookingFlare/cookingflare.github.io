Earl Grey Lavender Cake: A Fragrant and Elegant Dessert


There’s something truly magical about the combination of floral and citrus flavors in a dessert. It transports you to a serene garden, where the scent of lavender dances in the air and the delicate notes of Earl Grey tea tantalize your taste buds. If you’re looking to create a dessert that is both fragrant and elegant, then look no further than this Earl Grey Lavender Cake.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 1/2 cups granulated sugar
  • 1 tablespoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1 cup milk
  • 4 Earl Grey tea bags
  • 4 tablespoons culinary lavender
  • 4 large eggs
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C) and prepare two 8-inch round cake pans by greasing them and lining the bottoms with parchment paper.

  2. In a small saucepan, heat the milk until it begins to steam. Remove from heat and add the Earl Grey tea bags and culinary lavender. Allow them to steep for about 10 minutes, then remove the tea bags and lavender. Set the infused milk aside to cool.

  3. In a large mixing bowl, whisk together the flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt. Add the softened butter and mix until the mixture resembles coarse crumbs.

  4. In a separate bowl, beat the eggs and vanilla extract together. Gradually add the egg mixture to the dry ingredients, mixing well after each addition.

  5. Slowly pour in the infused milk, mixing until the batter is smooth and well-combined.

  6. Divide the batter evenly between the prepared cake pans and smooth the tops with a spatula.

  7. Bake for 25-30 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center of the cakes comes out clean.

  8. Remove the cakes from the oven and allow them to cool in the pans for 10 minutes. Then, transfer them to a wire rack to cool completely.

Frosting

To complement the fragrant flavors of the cake, we recommend a light and airy frosting. Here’s a simple recipe for a lavender-infused buttercream frosting:

Ingredients

  • 1 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 4 cups powdered sugar
  • 2 tablespoons milk
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 tablespoon culinary lavender, finely ground

Instructions

  1. In a mixing bowl, cream the softened butter until smooth.

  2. Gradually add the powdered sugar, mixing well after each addition.

  3. Add the milk, vanilla extract, and ground culinary lavender. Beat until the frosting is light and fluffy.

  4. Once the cakes have cooled completely, spread a generous layer of the lavender buttercream frosting on top of one cake layer. Place the second cake layer on top and frost the entire cake with the remaining frosting.

Serving Suggestions

This Earl Grey Lavender Cake is a show-stopper on its own, but you can take it to the next level with a few simple additions. Consider garnishing the cake with fresh lavender sprigs or a dusting of powdered sugar. For an extra touch of elegance, serve each slice with a dollop of whipped cream and a side of Earl Grey tea.

Conclusion

Indulge in the delicate flavors and aromas of this Earl Grey Lavender Cake. It’s a dessert that not only satisfies your sweet tooth but also transports you to a world of tranquility and refinement. With its fragrant floral notes and elegant presentation, this cake is sure to impress your family and friends on any occasion. So, go ahead and treat yourself to a slice of this exquisite creation – you deserve it.
