Delicious Vegan Breakfast Bars for On-the-Go Mornings


Are you tired of skipping breakfast because you’re always on the move? Do you find yourself reaching for unhealthy snacks during your busy mornings? Well, say goodbye to those problems because I’ve got the perfect solution for you – delicious vegan breakfast bars that are not only convenient but also nutritious and satisfying. These homemade breakfast bars are packed with wholesome ingredients and are incredibly easy to make. Whether you’re rushing out the door or need a quick energy boost at work, these bars will become your new go-to morning companion.

Why Vegan Breakfast Bars?

Vegan breakfast bars are an excellent choice for anyone looking for a nutritious and convenient breakfast option. They are free from animal products, making them suitable for vegans and those with dairy or egg allergies. Additionally, these bars are typically high in fiber, healthy fats, and protein, which can help keep you feeling full and energized throughout the morning. By making your own vegan breakfast bars, you have complete control over the ingredients, allowing you to avoid artificial additives and excessive sugars commonly found in store-bought options.

Ingredients

Dry Ingredients:

  • 2 cups rolled oats
  • 1 cup almond flour
  • 1/2 cup chopped nuts (e.g., almonds, walnuts)
  • 1/4 cup chia seeds
  • 1/4 cup unsweetened shredded coconut
  • 1/4 cup dried fruit (e.g., cranberries, raisins)

Wet Ingredients:

  • 1/2 cup natural almond butter
  • 1/2 cup pure maple syrup
  • 1/4 cup coconut oil, melted
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C) and line a baking pan with parchment paper.

  2. In a large mixing bowl, combine the rolled oats, almond flour, chopped nuts, chia seeds, shredded coconut, and dried fruit.

  3. In a separate bowl, whisk together the almond butter, maple syrup, melted coconut oil, and vanilla extract until smooth.

  4. Pour the wet mixture over the dry ingredients and mix until everything is well combined and forms a sticky dough.

  5. Transfer the mixture into the prepared baking pan and press it down firmly and evenly.

  6. Bake for 20-25 minutes or until the edges turn golden brown.

  7. Once done, remove from the oven and let it cool in the pan for about 10 minutes before transferring to a wire rack to cool completely.

  8. Once cooled, use a sharp knife to cut the mixture into bars of your desired size.

Storage and Serving

These vegan breakfast bars can be stored in an airtight container at room temperature for up to a week. For longer storage, you can refrigerate them for up to two weeks or freeze them for up to three months. They are perfect for grabbing on busy mornings or as a mid-morning snack at work. Pair them with a piece of fruit and a cup of coffee or tea for a well-rounded and satisfying breakfast.

Customization

Feel free to customize these breakfast bars to suit your taste preferences. You can swap out the nuts and dried fruits for your favorites, add a sprinkle of cinnamon or nutmeg for extra flavor, or even drizzle melted dairy-free chocolate over the top for a touch of indulgence.

Final Thoughts

These homemade vegan breakfast bars are a game-changer for anyone with a hectic morning routine. With just a little prep time, you can have a supply of wholesome and delicious breakfast bars ready to grab and go whenever you need them. Plus, knowing exactly what goes into them gives you peace of mind that you’re fueling your body with real, nourishing ingredients. Give these breakfast bars a try and say hello to stress-free, on-the-go mornings!
