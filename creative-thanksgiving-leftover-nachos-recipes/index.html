Creative Thanksgiving Leftover Nachos Recipes

3 min · 589 words

Thanksgiving is a time for family, gratitude, and of course, delicious food. But what do you do with all those Thanksgiving leftovers once the big feast is over? If you’re tired of the same old turkey sandwiches, we’ve got a solution for you – Creative Thanksgiving Leftover Nachos! These nachos are a fun and tasty way to reinvent your Thanksgiving leftovers, and they’re perfect for a cozy night in with loved ones. So, let’s dive into some mouthwatering Thanksgiving leftover nachos recipes that will have you savoring the holiday flavors all over again.

Turkey Cranberry Nachos

Ingredients:

  • Leftover turkey, shredded
  • Tortilla chips
  • Shredded cheddar cheese
  • Cranberry sauce
  • Sliced jalapenos
  • Chopped green onions
  • Sour cream

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. Arrange the tortilla chips on a baking sheet.
  3. Sprinkle the shredded turkey over the chips.
  4. Add a generous amount of shredded cheddar cheese on top of the turkey.
  5. Dollop cranberry sauce over the cheese.
  6. Place the baking sheet in the oven and bake for 10-15 minutes, or until the cheese is melted and bubbly.
  7. Remove from the oven and sprinkle sliced jalapenos and chopped green onions over the nachos.
  8. Serve with a side of sour cream for dipping.

Stuffing and Gravy Nachos

Ingredients:

  • Leftover stuffing
  • Leftover gravy
  • Tortilla chips
  • Shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Diced leftover vegetables (such as carrots, peas, or corn)
  • Chopped fresh parsley
  • Pickled hot peppers

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. Spread the tortilla chips on a baking sheet.
  3. Crumble the leftover stuffing over the chips.
  4. Drizzle the leftover gravy evenly over the stuffing.
  5. Sprinkle shredded mozzarella cheese on top of the nachos.
  6. Add the diced leftover vegetables on top of the cheese.
  7. Place the baking sheet in the oven and bake for 10-15 minutes, or until the cheese is gooey and golden.
  8. Remove from the oven and garnish with chopped fresh parsley and pickled hot peppers.

Sweet Potato and Black Bean Nachos

Ingredients:

  • Mashed sweet potatoes
  • Black beans, drained and rinsed
  • Tortilla chips
  • Crumbled feta cheese
  • Sliced avocado
  • Lime wedges
  • Chopped cilantro
  • Hot sauce (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. Lay out the tortilla chips on a baking sheet.
  3. Spread a layer of mashed sweet potatoes over the chips.
  4. Scatter the black beans evenly over the sweet potatoes.
  5. Sprinkle crumbled feta cheese on top of the nachos.
  6. Place the baking sheet in the oven and bake for 10-15 minutes, or until the cheese is melted and the nachos are heated through.
  7. Remove from the oven and top with sliced avocado, a squeeze of lime juice, chopped cilantro, and a drizzle of hot sauce if desired.

Conclusion

With these Creative Thanksgiving Leftover Nachos recipes, you can transform your holiday leftovers into something truly extraordinary. Whether you’re craving the classic flavors of turkey and cranberry, the comforting indulgence of stuffing and gravy, or a vegetarian twist with sweet potatoes and black beans, these nachos are a delicious way to savor the essence of Thanksgiving in a whole new light. So, the next time you find yourself with an abundance of Thanksgiving leftovers, grab some tortilla chips and get ready to indulge in the ultimate post-holiday treat!
