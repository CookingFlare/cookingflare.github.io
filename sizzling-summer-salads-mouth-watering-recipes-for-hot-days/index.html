Sizzling Summer Salads: Mouth-Watering Recipes for Hot Days


Are you tired of the same old boring salads every summer? Why not break free from the routine and explore new, exciting flavors this season? This post will help you tantalize your taste buds with a collection of sizzling summer salad recipes. These vibrant, bold dishes will significantly brighten up your meal plans and make your hot days truly remarkable.

  1. Middle Eastern Couscous Salad

The Middle Eastern Couscous Salad is a fusion of aromatic spices, fresh veggies, and perfectly cooked couscous. Its refreshing flavors make it the perfect choice for a sizzling summer day. The secret lies in the plethora of spices, such as cumin, paprika, and coriander, allowing the salad to burst with exotic tastes.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup Couscous
  • 2 cups Water
  • ½ cup Cherry tomatoes, halved
  • ½ cup Cucumber, diced
  • ¼ cup Red onion, finely chopped
  • ¼ cup Mint leaves, chopped
  • ¼ cup Parsley, chopped
  • 2 tbsp Extra virgin olive oil
  • 1 tbsp Lemon juice
  • ½ tsp Cumin
  • ½ tsp Paprika
  • ½ tsp Coriander
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a saucepan, bring 2 cups of water to a boil and add a pinch of salt.

  2. Add the couscous to the boiling water, stir, and then cover with a tight lid.

  3. After 5 minutes, lower the heat and let it simmer for 10-15 minutes or until the water is completely absorbed.

  4. Fluff the couscous with a fork and leave it to cool down.

  5. In a large bowl, combine the couscous with the cherry tomatoes, cucumber, red onion, mint, and parsley.

  6. In a separate small bowl, whisk together the olive oil, lemon juice, cumin, paprika, coriander, salt, and pepper.

  7. Pour the dressing over the couscous and combine well.

  8. Let the salad sit for 15 minutes to allow the flavors to meld together before serving.

  9. Thai Mango, Avocado, and Prawns Salad

This Thai Mango, Avocado, and Prawns Salad is not just visually stunning, but it is also a cornucopia of delectable tastes and textures. The sweet, succulent mangoes, creamy avocados, and succulent prawns make for an exquisite combination that you’ll want to enjoy again and again.

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups Mixed salad greens
  • 2 Mangoes, peeled and diced
  • 2 Avocados, diced
  • 1 cup Prawns, cooked and halved (or shrimps)
  • ¼ cup Red onion, thinly sliced
  • ¼ cup Glossy white sesame seeds
  • ½ cup Dry roasted peanuts, crushed

For the dressing:

  • ¼ cup Lime juice
  • 2 tbsp Fish sauce
  • 1 tbsp Brown sugar
  • 2 tbsp Crushed dried red chili
  • 1 tbsp Fresh cilantro, chopped

Instructions:

  1. In a large bowl, combine the mixed salad greens, diced mangoes, avocado, cooked prawns, red onion, white sesame seeds, and crushed peanuts.

  2. In a separate small bowl, whisk together the lime juice, fish sauce, brown sugar, crushed dried red chili, and fresh cilantro to create the dressing.

  3. Drizzle the dressing over the salad and toss well.

  4. To enhance the flavor, allow the salad to marinate for 10-15 minutes before serving. You may add more crushed dried red chili if you prefer a spicier dish.

  5. Greek Quinoa Salad

The Greek Quinoa Salad is a heartily satisfying meal that will delight your palate on hot summer days. This scrumptious salad is packed with herbs, veggies, and a zesty lemon dressing. It’s a dish that’s not only good for you but also stylish enough to serve at your next summer gathering.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup Quinoa
  • 2 cups Water
  • 1 cup Cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1 cucumber, diced
  • ½ red onion, finely chopped
  • ¼ cup Kalamata olives, pitted and halved
  • ½ cup Feta cheese, crumbled
  • ⅓ cup Fresh dill, chopped
  • ⅓ cup Fresh parsley, chopped

For the dressing:

  • ½ cup Fresh lemon juice
  • 1 tbsp Dijon mustard
  • 1 tbsp Honey
  • ½ cup Extra virgin olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. To cook the quinoa, bring 2 cups of water to a boil in a saucepan and add a pinch of salt.
  2. Add the quinoa, stir, and then cover with a tight lid.
  3. After 5 minutes, lower the heat and let it simmer for 10-15 minutes or until the water is completely absorbed.
  4. Fluff the quinoa with a fork and let it cool down.
  5. In a large bowl, combine the cooled quinoa, cherry tomatoes, cucumber, red onion, Kalamata olives, feta cheese, dill, and parsley.
  6. In a separate small bowl, whisk together the lemon juice, Dijon mustard, honey, olive oil, salt, and pepper to create the dressing.
  7. Pour the dressing over the salad and gently mix until well combined.
  8. Allow the flavors to meld for 15 minutes or serve immediately.

Embrace the summer heat with these uniquely refreshing and vibrant salads. Say goodbye to boring meals, and say hello to a feast of unparalleled tastes that will leave your family and friends craving for more. Happy cooking indeed!
