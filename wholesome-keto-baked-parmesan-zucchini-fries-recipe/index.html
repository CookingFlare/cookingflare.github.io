Wholesome Keto Baked Parmesan Zucchini Fries Recipe


Are you craving a delicious and wholesome keto snack that’s both crispy and satisfying? Look no further than these mouthwatering Baked Parmesan Zucchini Fries! Packed with flavor and perfect for dipping, these zucchini fries are a delightful alternative to traditional potato fries. Plus, they’re low-carb, gluten-free, and keto-friendly, making them an ideal choice for anyone looking to indulge without the guilt. Let’s dive into the recipe and discover the magic of these wholesome zucchini fries.

Ingredients

  • 2 medium zucchinis
  • 1 cup almond flour
  • 1 cup grated parmesan cheese
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1/2 teaspoon paprika
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Cooking spray or olive oil

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 425°F (220°C) and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.

  2. Wash the zucchinis and cut them into fry-shaped sticks, ensuring they are of similar thickness for even baking.

  3. In a shallow bowl, whisk the eggs until well beaten. In another shallow bowl, combine the almond flour, grated parmesan cheese, garlic powder, dried oregano, paprika, salt, and pepper.

  4. Dip each zucchini stick into the egg mixture, allowing any excess to drip off, then coat it in the almond flour and parmesan mixture, pressing gently to adhere the coating to the zucchini.

  5. Place the coated zucchini sticks onto the prepared baking sheet, making sure they are not touching each other. Lightly spray the fries with cooking spray or drizzle with olive oil.

  6. Bake for 18-20 minutes, or until the zucchini fries are golden and crispy, turning them over halfway through the baking time for even browning.

  7. Once done, remove the zucchini fries from the oven and let them cool for a few minutes before serving.

Serving Suggestions

These zucchini fries are incredibly versatile and can be enjoyed on their own or with your favorite dipping sauces. Consider pairing them with a creamy garlic aioli, tangy marinara sauce, or spicy sriracha mayo for an extra kick. For a complete meal, serve them alongside a fresh green salad or grilled protein of your choice.

Nutritional Information

For those following a keto lifestyle, you’ll be pleased to know that these Baked Parmesan Zucchini Fries are not only delicious but also low in carbs. With the perfect blend of healthy fats and protein from the almond flour and parmesan, you can indulge in these guilt-free treats while staying on track with your dietary goals.

Each serving of zucchini fries (approximately 10 pieces) contains approximately:

  • Calories: 180
  • Total Fat: 12g
  • Saturated Fat: 4g
  • Cholesterol: 70mg
  • Sodium: 280mg
  • Total Carbohydrates: 8g
  • Dietary Fiber: 3g
  • Sugars: 2g
  • Protein: 12g

Final Thoughts

Wholesome, flavorful, and satisfying, these Baked Parmesan Zucchini Fries are a must-try for anyone seeking a healthier alternative to traditional fries. Whether you’re following a keto lifestyle, looking to incorporate more veggies into your diet, or simply craving a delicious snack, these zucchini fries are sure to become a favorite in your recipe collection. So, preheat that oven, gather your ingredients, and get ready to savor the delightful crunch of these guilt-free zucchini fries!
