Irresistible Garlic Butter Steak Recipe for Steak Lovers


There’s nothing quite like sinking your teeth into a perfectly cooked steak. The combination of tender, juicy meat with a burst of flavor is an experience that many steak lovers cherish. If you’re looking to take your steak game to the next level, then this irresistible garlic butter steak recipe is just what you need. This recipe is a symphony of flavors that will tantalize your taste buds and leave you craving for more. So, let’s dive right into creating this mouthwatering dish.

Ingredients:

  • 2 boneless ribeye steaks (1 1/2 inches thick)
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 4 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1/4 cup unsalted butter
  • 1 teaspoon fresh thyme leaves
  • Salt and freshly ground black pepper, to taste

Instructions:

Preparing the Steaks

  1. Bring the Steaks to Room Temperature: Remove the steaks from the refrigerator and let them sit at room temperature for about 30 minutes. This will ensure even cooking.

  2. Season the Steaks: Season the steaks generously with salt and freshly ground black pepper on both sides. The seasoning will enhance the natural flavors of the meat.

  3. Preheat the Grill or Pan: If using a grill, preheat it to high heat. If using a cast-iron skillet, heat it over high heat until it’s smoking hot.

Cooking the Steaks

  1. Grilling or Pan-Searing: If grilling, place the steaks on the grill and cook for about 4-5 minutes on each side for medium-rare, or until the desired level of doneness is reached. If pan-searing, add the steaks to the hot skillet and cook for the same amount of time.

  2. Rest the Steaks: Once the steaks are cooked to your liking, remove them from the grill or skillet and let them rest for 5-10 minutes. This allows the juices to redistribute, ensuring a juicy steak.

Making the Garlic Butter Sauce

  1. Saute the Garlic: In a small saucepan, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the minced garlic and sauté for about 1 minute until fragrant.

  2. Add the Butter and Thyme: Reduce the heat to low and add the unsalted butter and fresh thyme leaves to the saucepan. Let the butter melt slowly, infusing with the garlic and thyme.

  3. Drizzle Over the Steaks: Once the butter is melted, pour the garlic butter sauce over the rested steaks, allowing the flavors to meld with the meat.

Serving

  1. Slice and Enjoy: Slice the steaks against the grain and serve them with your favorite sides. The tender, flavorful meat combined with the rich garlic butter sauce is a match made in steak heaven.

Conclusion

This irresistible garlic butter steak recipe is a celebration of simple ingredients coming together to create a sensational dish. The juicy, succulent steaks paired with the aromatic garlic butter sauce will undoubtedly elevate your steak experience. Whether you’re cooking for a special occasion or simply treating yourself, this recipe is a surefire way to impress any steak lover. So, fire up the grill or heat up the skillet, and get ready to savor every delightful bite of this garlic butter steak. Happy cooking!
