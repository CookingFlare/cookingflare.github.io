Quick and Easy Shrimp Recipes


If you’re a seafood lover, then you’re in for a treat! Shrimp is one of the most versatile and delicious ingredients that can elevate any dish to a whole new level. Whether you’re hosting a dinner party or simply looking for a quick and easy meal, these shrimp recipes are sure to impress. From zesty shrimp tacos to creamy shrimp pasta, there’s a recipe here for every taste bud. So let’s dive right in!

1. Garlic Butter Shrimp Scampi

Prep Time: 10 minutes
Cook Time: 10 minutes
Total Time: 20 minutes
Servings: 4

Ingredients

  • 1 pound of shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 4 tablespoons of butter
  • 4 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1/4 cup of chicken broth
  • Juice of 1 lemon
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley, chopped (for garnish)

Instructions

  1. In a large skillet, melt the butter over medium heat.
  2. Add the minced garlic and cook until fragrant, about 1 minute.
  3. Increase the heat to medium-high and add the shrimp to the skillet. Cook for 2-3 minutes on each side, until pink and opaque.
  4. Remove the shrimp from the skillet and set aside.
  5. Add the chicken broth and lemon juice to the skillet, scraping the bottom to deglaze.
  6. Cook for 2-3 minutes, until the sauce has reduced slightly.
  7. Return the shrimp to the skillet and toss to coat in the sauce.
  8. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  9. Serve hot, garnished with fresh parsley.

2. Spicy Shrimp Tacos

Prep Time: 15 minutes
Cook Time: 10 minutes
Total Time: 25 minutes
Servings: 4

Ingredients

  • 1 pound of shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 2 tablespoons of olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon of chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon of cumin
  • 1/2 teaspoon of paprika
  • 1/4 teaspoon of cayenne pepper (optional)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 8 small flour tortillas
  • Shredded lettuce, diced tomatoes, and sliced avocado (for toppings)

Instructions

  1. In a medium bowl, combine the olive oil, chili powder, cumin, paprika, cayenne pepper (if using), salt, and pepper.
  2. Add the shrimp to the bowl and toss to coat evenly.
  3. Heat a large skillet over medium-high heat and add the shrimp.
  4. Cook for 2-3 minutes on each side, until pink and opaque.
  5. Remove the shrimp from the skillet and set aside.
  6. Warm the flour tortillas in a dry skillet or microwave.
  7. Assemble the tacos by placing a few shrimp on each tortilla, and topping with shredded lettuce, diced tomatoes, and sliced avocado.
  8. Serve hot and enjoy!

3. Creamy Shrimp Pasta

Prep Time: 10 minutes
Cook Time: 20 minutes
Total Time: 30 minutes
Servings: 4

Ingredients

  • 8 ounces of linguine or your favorite pasta
  • 1 pound of shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 2 tablespoons of butter
  • 4 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 cup of heavy cream
  • 1/2 cup of grated Parmesan cheese
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley, chopped (for garnish)

Instructions

  1. Cook the pasta according to the package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a large skillet, melt the butter over medium heat.
  3. Add the minced garlic and cook until fragrant, about 1 minute.
  4. Increase the heat to medium-high and add the shrimp to the skillet. Cook for 2-3 minutes on each side, until pink and opaque.
  5. Remove the shrimp from the skillet and set aside.
  6. Reduce the heat to medium and add the heavy cream to the skillet. Bring to a simmer.
  7. Gradually whisk in the grated Parmesan cheese until melted and smooth.
  8. Season the sauce with salt and pepper to taste.
  9. Add the cooked pasta and shrimp to the skillet and toss to coat in the sauce.
  10. Serve hot, garnished with fresh parsley.

These quick and easy shrimp recipes are perfect for any occasion. Whether you’re cooking for your family or entertaining guests, these dishes will surely impress. So grab some shrimp and get cooking!
