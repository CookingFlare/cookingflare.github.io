Satisfying One Pot Creamy Garlic Parmesan Pasta


Are you looking for a quick and delicious recipe that will satisfy your cravings for a creamy and flavorful pasta dish? Look no further, because this One Pot Creamy Garlic Parmesan Pasta is here to save the day! With a rich and indulgent sauce, tender pasta, and the perfect hint of garlic, this dish is a crowd-pleaser and a go-to for busy weeknights or lazy weekends. Plus, the best part is that it all comes together in just one pot, making cleanup a breeze. Let’s dive into the details of this mouthwatering recipe.

Ingredients

  • 12 oz fettuccine pasta
  • 2 tbsp unsalted butter
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 1/2 cups chicken broth
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1/2 cup freshly grated Parmesan cheese
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley, chopped, for garnish

Instructions

  1. Sauté the Garlic: In a large pot or deep skillet, melt the butter over medium heat. Add the minced garlic and sauté for about a minute, or until fragrant. Be careful not to let the garlic brown.

  2. Add the Liquid: Pour in the chicken broth and heavy cream, then add the fettuccine pasta. Bring the mixture to a boil, then reduce the heat to a simmer. Let the pasta cook in the creamy liquid, stirring occasionally, until the pasta is al dente and the sauce has thickened, about 15-20 minutes.

  3. Finish with Parmesan: Once the pasta is cooked to your liking and the sauce has thickened, stir in the freshly grated Parmesan cheese. Continue to stir until the cheese has melted and the sauce is smooth and creamy.

  4. Season and Serve: Season the dish with salt and black pepper to taste. Garnish with chopped fresh parsley for a pop of color and added freshness.

  5. Enjoy: Serve the One Pot Creamy Garlic Parmesan Pasta in individual bowls and savor every bite of this delightful dish. Pair it with a crisp green salad or some garlic bread for a complete and satisfying meal.

Tips and Variations

  • Add Protein: If you’re looking to add some protein to this dish, consider tossing in some grilled chicken, shrimp, or even crispy bacon for an extra layer of flavor and texture.
  • Vegetarian Option: To make this recipe vegetarian-friendly, simply substitute the chicken broth with vegetable broth, and you’ll have a creamy and delicious vegetarian pasta dish.
  • Customize the Cheese: While Parmesan cheese is the star of this recipe, feel free to experiment with different cheeses like Asiago, Romano, or even a blend of your favorites to create your own unique twist on this classic dish.

Final Thoughts

The One Pot Creamy Garlic Parmesan Pasta is the epitome of comfort food – rich, creamy, and oh-so-satisfying. Whether you’re cooking for a family dinner, a cozy date night in, or simply treating yourself to a delicious meal, this recipe is sure to impress. The convenience of preparing everything in just one pot, coupled with the luxurious flavors of garlic and Parmesan, makes this dish a winner in every aspect. So, roll up your sleeves, grab your favorite pot, and get ready to indulge in a bowl of this irresistible pasta creation. Happy cooking!
