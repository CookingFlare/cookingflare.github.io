Mouthwatering and Juicy: Grilled Teriyaki Pork Chops


If you’re looking for a delightful and flavorful dish to add to your grilling repertoire, look no further than these mouthwatering and juicy grilled teriyaki pork chops. This recipe combines the succulent goodness of pork chops with the sweet and savory flavors of teriyaki sauce, resulting in a dish that will have your taste buds dancing with joy. Whether you’re hosting a summer barbecue or simply craving a delicious homemade meal, these grilled teriyaki pork chops are sure to impress.

Ingredients

  • 4 bone-in pork chops
  • 1 cup teriyaki sauce
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon brown sugar
  • 1 teaspoon ginger, grated
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1/4 teaspoon red pepper flakes (optional)
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • Green onions, thinly sliced (for garnish)

Instructions

  1. Marinate the Pork Chops: In a bowl, whisk together the teriyaki sauce, soy sauce, minced garlic, brown sugar, grated ginger, black pepper, and red pepper flakes (if using). Place the pork chops in a shallow dish or resealable plastic bag and pour the marinade over them, ensuring that each chop is well coated. Cover the dish or seal the bag, then refrigerate for at least 2 hours, or preferably overnight, to allow the flavors to meld and the pork chops to absorb the delicious marinade.

  2. Prepare the Grill: Preheat your grill to medium-high heat, about 375-400°F (190-200°C). Brush the grill grates with vegetable oil to prevent the pork chops from sticking.

  3. Grill the Pork Chops: Once the grill is ready, remove the pork chops from the marinade and discard the excess marinade. Place the pork chops on the grill and cook for 4-5 minutes on each side, or until they reach an internal temperature of 145°F (63°C), followed by a 3-minute rest.

  4. Glaze with Teriyaki Sauce: During the last 2 minutes of grilling, brush the pork chops with additional teriyaki sauce to create a caramelized glaze. Be sure to turn the chops and brush both sides to achieve a beautiful, glossy finish.

  5. Serve and Enjoy: Once the pork chops are cooked to perfection, remove them from the grill and let them rest for a few minutes. Sprinkle the grilled teriyaki pork chops with thinly sliced green onions for a fresh and vibrant garnish. Serve the chops alongside steamed rice, grilled vegetables, or a refreshing salad for a complete and satisfying meal.

Tips for Grilling Success

  • Temperature Control: It’s important to maintain a consistent grill temperature to ensure even cooking and to prevent the pork chops from drying out.

  • Resting Time: Allowing the pork chops to rest after grilling helps redistribute the juices, resulting in tender and juicy meat.

  • Safety First: Always use a meat thermometer to check the internal temperature of the pork chops to ensure they are safe to eat.

Final Thoughts

These grilled teriyaki pork chops are a fantastic way to elevate your outdoor cooking experience. The combination of tender pork chops and the irresistible flavors of teriyaki sauce make this dish a standout crowd-pleaser. Whether you’re grilling for a gathering of friends and family or simply treating yourself to a special meal, these mouthwatering and juicy grilled teriyaki pork chops are a must-try. Enjoy the delightful flavors and the satisfaction of creating a delicious meal right from your own grill. Happy grilling!
