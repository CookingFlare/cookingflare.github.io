Paleo Pork Chops Recipes: 5 Delicious and Grain-Free Options


When it comes to delicious and healthy meals, pork chops are a versatile and flavorful choice. And if you’re following a paleo diet, you’ll be happy to know that there are plenty of ways to enjoy pork chops while staying grain-free. In this post, we’ll explore five mouthwatering paleo pork chop recipes that are sure to satisfy your cravings. So let’s dive in!

1. Garlic and Herb Pork Chops

Ingredients:

  • 4 bone-in pork chops
  • 3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon of fresh rosemary, chopped
  • 1 tablespoon of fresh thyme, chopped
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • 2 tablespoons of olive oil

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C).
  2. In a small bowl, combine the minced garlic, chopped rosemary, chopped thyme, salt, and pepper.
  3. Rub the mixture evenly over both sides of the pork chops.
  4. Heat olive oil in a skillet over medium-high heat.
  5. Sear the pork chops for 2-3 minutes on each side until browned.
  6. Transfer the skillet to the preheated oven and bake for 15-20 minutes or until the pork chops reach an internal temperature of 145°F (63°C).
  7. Allow the pork chops to rest for a few minutes before serving. Enjoy!

2. Apple and Cinnamon Pork Chops

Ingredients:

  • 4 boneless pork chops
  • 2 apples, peeled, cored, and thinly sliced
  • 1 teaspoon of ground cinnamon
  • 1 tablespoon of ghee or coconut oil
  • Salt and pepper, to taste

Instructions:

  1. Season the pork chops with salt, pepper, and ground cinnamon on both sides.
  2. Heat ghee or coconut oil in a skillet over medium heat.
  3. Add the pork chops and cook for 4-5 minutes on each side until browned.
  4. Remove the pork chops from the skillet and set aside.
  5. In the same skillet, add the sliced apples and cook for 3-4 minutes until softened.
  6. Return the pork chops to the skillet and cook for an additional 2-3 minutes, or until the pork chops are cooked through.
  7. Serve the pork chops with the sautéed apples. Enjoy!

3. Balsamic Glazed Pork Chops

Ingredients:

  • 4 bone-in pork chops
  • 1/4 cup of balsamic vinegar
  • 2 tablespoons of honey
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon of olive oil
  • Salt and pepper, to taste

Instructions:

  1. Season the pork chops with salt and pepper on both sides.
  2. In a small bowl, whisk together the balsamic vinegar, honey, minced garlic, and olive oil.
  3. Heat olive oil in a skillet over medium-high heat.
  4. Sear the pork chops for 2-3 minutes on each side until browned.
  5. Reduce the heat to medium and pour the balsamic glaze over the pork chops.
  6. Cook for an additional 3-4 minutes, or until the pork chops are cooked through and the glaze has thickened.
  7. Serve the pork chops with the remaining glaze. Enjoy!

4. Spicy Mustard Pork Chops

Ingredients:

  • 4 boneless pork chops
  • 2 tablespoons of Dijon mustard
  • 1 tablespoon of coconut aminos (or soy sauce if not strict paleo)
  • 1 teaspoon of paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon of cayenne pepper
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • 1 tablespoon of ghee or coconut oil

Instructions:

  1. In a small bowl, combine the Dijon mustard, coconut aminos, paprika, cayenne pepper, salt, and pepper.
  2. Rub the mustard mixture evenly over both sides of the pork chops.
  3. Heat ghee or coconut oil in a skillet over medium heat.
  4. Add the pork chops and cook for 4-5 minutes on each side until browned and cooked through.
  5. Serve the pork chops with your favorite side dish. Enjoy!

5. Lemon and Herb Pork Chops

Ingredients:

  • 4 bone-in pork chops
  • Juice and zest of 1 lemon
  • 2 tablespoons of fresh parsley, chopped
  • 1 tablespoon of fresh thyme, chopped
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • 2 tablespoons of olive oil

Instructions:

  1. In a small bowl, combine the lemon juice, lemon zest, chopped parsley, chopped thyme, salt, and pepper.
  2. Rub the mixture evenly over both sides of the pork chops.
  3. Heat olive oil in a skillet over medium-high heat.
  4. Sear the pork chops for 2-3 minutes on each side until browned.
  5. Reduce the heat to medium and cook for an additional 6-8 minutes, or until the pork chops are cooked through.
  6. Serve the pork chops with a squeeze of fresh lemon juice. Enjoy!

These five paleo pork chop recipes offer a variety of flavors to suit your taste buds while keeping your meals grain-free. Whether you prefer a tangy balsamic glaze or a spicy mustard kick, these recipes are sure to satisfy your cravings. So go ahead and try them out – your taste buds will thank you!
