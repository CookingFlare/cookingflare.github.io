Decadent Chocolate Peppermint Whoopie Pies: A Fun Dessert


If you’re a fan of chocolate and peppermint, then you’re in for a treat with this recipe! Decadent Chocolate Peppermint Whoopie Pies are the perfect combination of rich, chocolatey goodness and refreshing peppermint flavor. These delightful treats are not only delicious but also fun to make, making them an ideal dessert for any occasion. Whether you’re hosting a holiday gathering, a birthday party, or simply craving something sweet, these whoopie pies are sure to impress your taste buds and those of your guests.

Ingredients

For the Chocolate Peppermint Cookies:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 large egg
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/2 teaspoon peppermint extract
  • 1/2 cup buttermilk

For the Peppermint Filling:

  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 2 cups powdered sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon peppermint extract
  • 1-2 tablespoons heavy cream

Instructions

Chocolate Peppermint Cookies:

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C) and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
  2. In a medium bowl, whisk together the flour, cocoa powder, baking powder, baking soda, and salt.
  3. In a separate large bowl, cream together the butter and sugar until light and fluffy. Add the egg, vanilla extract, and peppermint extract, and mix until well combined.
  4. Gradually add the dry ingredients to the wet ingredients, alternating with the buttermilk, until the batter is smooth.
  5. Drop rounded tablespoons of batter onto the prepared baking sheet, leaving space between each mound. Bake for 10-12 minutes, or until the cookies spring back when lightly touched. Allow the cookies to cool completely.

Peppermint Filling:

  1. In a medium bowl, beat the softened butter until creamy. Gradually add the powdered sugar and peppermint extract, and mix until smooth.
  2. Add heavy cream, 1 tablespoon at a time, until the filling reaches a smooth and spreadable consistency.

Assembling the Whoopie Pies:

  1. Match up the cooled cookies in pairs of similar size.
  2. Spread a generous amount of peppermint filling on the flat side of one cookie, then top it with another cookie to create a sandwich. Repeat with the remaining cookies and filling.

Serving and Storage

These Decadent Chocolate Peppermint Whoopie Pies are best enjoyed fresh, but they can be stored in an airtight container at room temperature for up to 3 days. For longer storage, place them in the refrigerator for up to a week. Serve these delightful whoopie pies at your next gathering and watch them disappear in no time!

Conclusion

Decadent Chocolate Peppermint Whoopie Pies are a delightful and fun dessert that will satisfy any chocolate and peppermint lover. The rich, chocolate cookies combined with the refreshing peppermint filling create a flavor explosion that is sure to impress. Whether you’re a seasoned baker or just starting out, these whoopie pies are a joy to make and share with family and friends. So go ahead, whip up a batch of these delightful treats and indulge in the irresistible combination of chocolate and peppermint! Enjoy!
