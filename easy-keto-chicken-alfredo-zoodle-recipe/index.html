Easy Keto Chicken Alfredo Zoodle Recipe


If you’re looking for a delicious and easy low-carb meal, this Easy Keto Chicken Alfredo Zoodle recipe is a must-try! Packed with flavor and healthy ingredients, this dish is perfect for anyone following a keto lifestyle or simply looking to add more nutritious meals to their menu. The combination of tender chicken, creamy alfredo sauce, and fresh zucchini noodles creates a satisfying and guilt-free meal that you’ll want to make again and again.

Ingredients

  • 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 3 medium zucchinis, spiralized into zoodles
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/4 teaspoon garlic powder
  • Fresh parsley for garnish

Instructions

  1. Cook the Chicken: Season the chicken breasts with salt and pepper. In a large skillet, heat 1 tablespoon of olive oil over medium-high heat. Add the chicken breasts and cook for 6-7 minutes on each side, or until they are no longer pink in the middle. Once cooked, remove the chicken from the skillet and set aside.

  2. Prepare the Zoodles: Using a spiralizer, create zoodles from the zucchinis. If you don’t have a spiralizer, you can use a vegetable peeler to create long, thin strips. Set the zoodles aside.

  3. Make the Alfredo Sauce: In the same skillet, add the remaining tablespoon of olive oil and minced garlic. Sauté for 1-2 minutes until fragrant. Pour in the heavy cream and bring to a simmer. Stir in the grated Parmesan cheese and garlic powder. Continue to simmer for 3-4 minutes, or until the sauce thickens slightly.

  4. Combine and Serve: Slice the cooked chicken into strips and add it back to the skillet with the alfredo sauce. Add the zucchini noodles and toss everything together until the zoodles are coated in the sauce and the chicken is heated through. This should take about 2-3 minutes.

  5. Garnish and Enjoy: Once everything is well combined, remove the skillet from the heat. Garnish with fresh parsley and an extra sprinkle of Parmesan cheese if desired. Serve the Chicken Alfredo Zoodles immediately and savor every flavorful bite!

Why Choose Keto Chicken Alfredo Zoodles?

This recipe offers a delightful alternative to traditional pasta dishes, allowing you to enjoy the creamy goodness of alfredo sauce without the heavy carbs. Zucchini noodles, or zoodles, provide a light and refreshing base for the dish while adding a generous dose of fiber and essential nutrients. The tender, juicy chicken complements the creamy alfredo sauce, creating a satisfying and protein-packed meal that will leave you feeling nourished and content.

By opting for this Easy Keto Chicken Alfredo Zoodle recipe, you’re not only treating your taste buds to a delectable meal but also supporting your health and wellness goals. Whether you’re new to the keto lifestyle or a seasoned enthusiast, this dish is a fantastic addition to your recipe repertoire. It’s quick to prepare, bursting with flavor, and showcases the versatility of zucchini as a nutritious pasta substitute.

So, the next time you’re craving a comforting and wholesome meal, consider whipping up this Easy Keto Chicken Alfredo Zoodle recipe. It’s a delightful way to indulge in a classic favorite while staying true to your keto journey.
