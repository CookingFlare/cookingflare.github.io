Savory Snack Recipes: 10 Appetizers You Can Make in Under 30 Minutes

2. Baked Camembert with Fresh Fruit

将1 wheel of Camembert cheese place on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper。 Sprinkle 1 tablespoon of honey and 1 tablespoon of chopped fresh thyme on top of the cheese。 Bake in a preheated oven at 375°F (190°C) for 10-12 minutes，直到 cheese becomes soft and runny。 Serve with sliced fresh fruit, such as apples or grapes.

3. Roasted Edamame with Miso Glaze

将1 cup of edamame place on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper。 Drizzle with 2 tablespoons of olive oil and sprinkle with 1 teaspoon of salt。 Roast in a preheated oven at 400°F (200°C) for 10-12 minutes，直到 edamame becomes tender。 Meanwhile, mix 2 tablespoons of miso paste with 1 tablespoon of soy sauce and 1 tablespoon of honey。 Brush the glaze on the edamame during the last 2 minutes of roasting. Serve warm.

4. Grilled Peach and Prosciutto Skewers

将1 ripe peach and 4 slices of prosciutto cut into 1-inch pieces。 Alternate the peach and prosciutto on skewers。 Brush with 1 tablespoon of olive oil and season with salt and pepper。 Grill over medium heat for 2-3 minutes per side，直到 peach becomes caramelized and prosciutto becomes crispy. Serve warm.

5. Spinach and Artichoke Dip

将1 (14.5 oz) can of artichoke hearts drained and chopped、1 package of frozen chopped spinach thawed and drained、1 cup of mayonnaise、1/2 cup of sour cream、1/2 cup of grated Parmesan cheese、and 1/2 teaspoon of lemon juice mix together in a bowl。 Add 1/2 teaspoon of salt and 1/4 teaspoon of black pepper to taste。 Serve with tortilla chips or pita bread.

6. Mini Caprese Salads

将1 pint of cherry tomatoes halved、1 ball of fresh mozzarella cheese sliced into 1/4-inch thick rounds、and 1/4 cup of chopped fresh basil leaves mix together in a bowl。 Drizzle with 1 tablespoon of olive oil and sprinkle with salt and pepper to taste。 Serve on toothpicks or small plates.

7. Crispy Fried Cauliflower Bites

将1 head of cauliflower broken into florets。 Dip each floret in 1 cup of beaten eggs and then coat with 1 cup of panko breadcrumbs。 Fry in 1/2-inch of hot oil until golden brown and crispy。 Drain on paper towels and serve warm.

8. Smoked Salmon Canapés

将1 package of smoked salmon sliced into 1/4-inch thick pieces。 Mix 1 tablespoon of cream cheese with 1 tablespoon of chopped fresh dill and 1 tablespoon of lemon juice。 Spread a small amount of the cream cheese mixture on a crackers or toasted bagel。 Top with a piece of smoked salmon and a sprinkle of capers. Serve chilled.

9. Prosciutto-Wrapped Melon

将1 ripe melon (such as cantaloupe or honeydew) cut into 1-inch thick slices。 Wrap each slice with 1 slice of prosciutto。 Drizzle with 1 tablespoon of honey and sprinkle with 1/4 cup of chopped fresh mint leaves。 Serve chilled.

10. Bacon-Wrapped Dates

将1 package of dates pitted。 Wrap each date with 1 slice of bacon。 Secure with a toothpick if needed。 Bake in a preheated oven at 400°F (200°C) for 10-12 minutes，直到 bacon becomes crispy。 Serve warm.
