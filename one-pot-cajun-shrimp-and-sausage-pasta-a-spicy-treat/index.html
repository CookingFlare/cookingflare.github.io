One-Pot Cajun Shrimp and Sausage Pasta: A Spicy Treat

3 min · 542 words

If you’re a fan of spicy flavors and are looking for a quick and easy one-pot meal, then this recipe is for you. Our One-Pot Cajun Shrimp and Sausage Pasta is not only packed with bold and vibrant flavors but also incredibly convenient to make. With just a few simple ingredients and a single pot, you can have a delicious and satisfying meal on the table in no time. So, let’s dive in and discover the magic of this spicy treat!

Ingredients

  • 1 pound of large shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 1 pound of smoked sausage, sliced
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 1 red bell pepper, diced
  • 3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 2 cups of chicken broth
  • 1 can (14.5 ounces) of diced tomatoes
  • 1 tablespoon of Cajun seasoning
  • 1 teaspoon of paprika
  • 1 teaspoon of dried thyme
  • 1/2 teaspoon of cayenne pepper (adjust according to your spice preference)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 8 ounces of penne pasta
  • Fresh parsley, chopped (for garnish)

Instructions

  1. In a large pot or Dutch oven, heat some oil over medium heat. Add the smoked sausage and cook until browned. Remove the sausage from the pot and set aside.

  2. In the same pot, add the diced onion, red bell pepper, and minced garlic. Sauté until the vegetables are softened and fragrant.

  3. Add the chicken broth, diced tomatoes (with their juice), Cajun seasoning, paprika, dried thyme, cayenne pepper, salt, and pepper to the pot. Stir well to combine all the flavors.

  4. Bring the mixture to a boil, then reduce the heat to low and let it simmer for about 10 minutes. This will allow the flavors to meld together.

  5. Add the penne pasta to the pot, stirring to ensure it is well coated with the sauce. Cover the pot and cook for about 10-12 minutes, or until the pasta is tender.

  6. Once the pasta is cooked, add the shrimp and cooked sausage back to the pot. Stir gently to mix everything together. Cook for an additional 2-3 minutes, or until the shrimp are pink and cooked through.

  7. Remove the pot from the heat and let it sit for a few minutes to allow the flavors to develop even further. Give it a final taste and adjust the seasoning if needed.

  8. Serve the One-Pot Cajun Shrimp and Sausage Pasta hot, garnished with freshly chopped parsley for a pop of freshness and color.

Tips and Variations

  • If you prefer a milder version, reduce the amount of Cajun seasoning and cayenne pepper according to your taste.

  • Feel free to add some vegetables of your choice, such as sliced mushrooms or chopped spinach, for an extra nutritional boost.

  • For a creamier pasta dish, stir in a splash of heavy cream or coconut milk at the end.

  • This recipe can easily be doubled or halved depending on your needs. Just adjust the ingredients accordingly.

Final Thoughts

One-Pot Cajun Shrimp and Sausage Pasta is a delightful dish that brings together the bold flavors of Cajun cuisine with the convenience of a one-pot meal. The combination of succulent shrimp, smoky sausage, and aromatic spices creates a symphony of flavors that will leave your taste buds dancing. Whether you’re cooking for yourself, your family, or entertaining guests, this recipe is sure to impress. So, why wait? Grab your pot, gather the ingredients, and get ready to indulge in this spicy treat that will transport you straight to the heart of Louisiana. Enjoy!
