Sizzling Steak Tacos: A Mouthwatering Recipe


When it comes to tacos, the debate is always centered around the type of meat to use. While some swear by traditional ground beef, others opt for the bold flavor of chicken or the tender texture of fish. But for those who crave something more decadent, there’s only one option: steak. Specifically, sizzling steak tacos that will leave you wanting more.

In this recipe, we’ll show you how to create the perfect sizzling steak tacos, complete with a crispy shell, flavorful steak, and a tangy slaw. It’s a game-changer for taco Tuesday, and you’ll be the envy of all your friends.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound flank steak
  • 1/4 cup lime juice
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1/4 teaspoon paprika
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • 8-10 corn tortillas
  • Vegetable oil, for brushing
  • Sizzling steak slaw (recipe below)
  • Optional toppings: diced tomatoes, shredded lettuce, diced avocado, sour cream, salsa

Sizzling Steak Slaw

  • 1 cup shredded red cabbage
  • 1 cup shredded carrot
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh cilantro
  • 2 tablespoons lime juice
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon Dijon mustard
  • Salt and pepper, to taste

Instructions

  1. Marinate the Steak: In a large ziplock bag, combine lime juice, garlic, oregano, cumin, paprika, salt, and pepper. Add the flank steak and massage the marinade into the meat, making sure it’s evenly coated. Seal the bag and refrigerate for at least 2 hours or overnight.
  2. Grill the Steak: Preheat a grill or grill pan to medium-high heat. Remove the steak from the marinade, letting any excess liquid drip off. Brush the grill or grill pan with vegetable oil to prevent sticking. Grill the steak for 3-4 minutes per side, or until it reaches your desired level of doneness. Transfer the steak to a cutting board and let it rest for 5 minutes before slicing it thinly against the grain.
  3. Prepare the Slaw: In a large bowl, combine the shredded cabbage, carrot, and cilantro. In a small bowl, whisk together lime juice, olive oil, Dijon mustard, salt, and pepper. Pour the dressing over the slaw and toss to combine.
  4. Assemble the Tacos: Warm the tortillas by wrapping them in a damp paper towel and microwaving for 20-30 seconds. Slice the grilled steak into thin strips. Assemble the tacos by placing a few slices of steak onto a warmed tortilla, followed by a spoonful of sizzling steak slaw. Add any desired toppings and serve immediately.

Tips and Variations

  • For an added crunch, top the tacos with toasted pepitas or crispy tortilla strips.
  • Swap out the flank steak for skirt steak or ribeye for a different flavor profile.
  • Add some heat to the slaw by incorporating diced jalapeños or serrano peppers.
  • Experiment with different types of cheese, such as queso fresco or crumbled feta, for added flavor.

With this recipe, you’ll be on your way to creating the perfect sizzling steak tacos that will impress even the most discerning palates. So go ahead, fire up the grill, and get ready to indulge in a culinary experience that will leave you wanting more.
