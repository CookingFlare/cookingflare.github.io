Vegan Butternut Squash Soup: Creamy and Nutty Comfort in a Bowl


Autumn is here, and with it comes the cozy feeling of sipping on a warm, comforting bowl of soup. And what better way to do so than with a creamy and nutty vegan butternut squash soup? This recipe is a game-changer for anyone looking for a delicious and satisfying plant-based meal that’s perfect for the chilly fall and winter months.

The Magic of Butternut Squash

Before we dive into the recipe, let’s talk about the star of the show: butternut squash. This versatile gourd is a staple in many fall recipes, and for good reason. Its sweet, nutty flavor and velvety texture make it the perfect base for a comforting soup. And when you combine it with some aromatic spices and creamy plant-based milk, you get a dish that’s truly greater than the sum of its parts.

The Recipe

** Servings: 6-8**

Cooking Time: 45-50 minutes

Ingredients:

  • 1 large butternut squash (about 2 lbs)
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon smoked paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 4 cups vegetable broth
  • 1 cup plant-based milk (such as soy or almond milk)
  • 1/4 cup chopped walnuts
  • Fresh cilantro leaves for garnish (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C).
  2. Cut the butternut squash in half lengthwise and scoop out the seeds. Place the squash on a baking sheet, cut side up, and drizzle with the olive oil.
  3. Roast the squash in the oven for 30-40 minutes, or until it’s tender and caramelized.
  4. While the squash is roasting, heat the olive oil in a large pot over medium heat. Add the diced onion and cook until it’s translucent, about 5-7 minutes.
  5. Add the minced garlic, ground cumin, smoked paprika, salt, and black pepper to the pot. Cook for an additional 1-2 minutes, stirring constantly, until the spices are fragrant.
  6. Remove the squash from the oven and let it cool slightly. Scoop the flesh into the pot with the onion and spices.
  7. Pour in the vegetable broth and bring the mixture to a boil. Reduce the heat to low and simmer for 15-20 minutes, or until the soup has thickened slightly.
  8. Use an immersion blender to puree the soup until it’s smooth. Alternatively, you can transfer the soup to a blender and blend until smooth, then return it to the pot.
  9. Stir in the plant-based milk and chopped walnuts. Bring the soup to a simmer and cook for an additional 5-10 minutes, or until the flavors have melded together.
  10. Taste and adjust the seasoning as needed. Serve the soup hot, garnished with fresh cilantro leaves if desired.

The Verdict

This vegan butternut squash soup is the perfect comfort food for a chilly fall or winter evening. The roasted butternut squash adds a deep, caramelized flavor to the soup, while the plant-based milk and walnuts provide a creamy and nutty texture. And with its aromatic spices and warm, comforting bowl, this soup is sure to become a favorite in your household.

So go ahead, grab a spoon, and indulge in a bowl of creamy and nutty vegan butternut squash soup. Your taste buds – and your belly – will thank you.
