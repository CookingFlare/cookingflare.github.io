Salted Caramel Sky High Cake: Extraordinary, Incredible, Indulgent


Welcome, dear readers, to the tantalizing world of an extraordinary cake experience. Today, we are diving into the depths of culinary passion to bring you a recipe straight from the pages of a sweet dream - the Salted Caramel Sky High Cake. Download your apron, gather your ingredients, and prepare to embark on an indulgent journey with us. As light hits the cascading layers of this magnificent dessert, it will feel as if clouds have parted to reveal pure cake magic.

Ingredients:

For the Vanilla Cake:

  • 3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 ½ cups granulated sugar
  • 1 ½ cups unsalted butter, softened
  • 8 large eggs, room temperature
  • 2 tsp baking powder
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 2 tbsp vanilla extract
  • 1 cup whole milk

For the Salted Caramel:

  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • ½ cup water
  • ½ cup heavy cream
  • 2 tbsp unsalted butter
  • Pinch of salt

For the Vanilla Buttercream:

  • 2 ½ cups unsalted butter, softened
  • 1 cup pure vanilla extract
  • 1 ½ tsp salt
  • 10 ½ cups powdered sugar

For the Assembly:

  • 2/3 cup salted caramel sauce
  • 2 ½ cups vanilla buttercream
  • An extra pinch of salt
  • Large piping or decorating bag
  • Round pastry tip
  • Cake stand

Instructions:

First, let’s dive into the vanilla cake recipe. Begin by preheating your oven to 350°F (175°C) and preparing three 9-inch cake pans. Then, in a large bowl, cream together the softened butter and sugar until light and fluffy. Next, one at a time, incorporate the eggs into the mix, whisking well after each addition. Make sure to add the vanilla extract and mix thoroughly.

Slowly, incorporate the dry ingredients – flour, baking powder, and salt – and mix until combined. Now, whisk in the milk, scraping the sides of the bowl as needed to ensure everything is well mixed. Distribute the batter evenly between the prepared cake pans and bake for 20-25 minutes or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.

While the cakes are baking, let’s shift our focus to the salted caramel sauce. With a heavy-bottomed saucepan over medium heat, dissolve the sugar in water. Allow the mixture to come to a simmer without stirring and cook until it turns amber. Keep an eye out for those wriggling tones of burnt sugar hues, carefully avoid a granulated sugar sea trip and temptations of stirring. Once the desired amber color is reached, gently whisk in the heavy cream, butter, and salt. Allow the sauce to cool to room temperature.

In a large bowl, prepare the vanilla buttercream by creaming together softened butter, powdered sugar, vanilla extract, and salt until smooth, rich, and ready for assembly.

Now, let our cakes cool down and reveal their beauty by turning them upside down onto a wire rack. Level them with a serrated knife. Time for a culinary magic gathering: generously spread a layer of salted caramel sauce over the first cake layer. On top of this, spread a layer of vanilla buttercream and a pinch of salt. Repeat the process with the second and third layers, making sure to cover the entire cake in a succulent, glorious cloud of buttercream.

For the pièce de résistance, create a majestic swirl pattern on top by using a piping or decorating bag fitted with a round pastry tip, then finish it off with a final dusting or drizzle of salted caramel sauce, brought down from the cloud realm.

Now that you’ve brought this extraordinary, incredible, and indulgent concoction to life, decadently slice and savor the salted caramel sky high cake you’ve created. The decadent blend of sweetness and salt in each bite will take your senses on an unforgettable journey. Happy baking!
