Tropical Twist: 5 Marinades for Grilled Pineapple


Summer is here, and with it comes the perfect excuse to fire up the grill and indulge in the sweet, juicy flavors of grilled pineapple. But why settle for the same old boring recipes when you can give your pineapple a tropical twist? In this blog post, we’ll explore five mouth-watering marinades that will take your grilled pineapple game to the next level.

Marinade 1: Spicy Mango Magic

Combine 1/2 cup of mango puree, 1/4 cup of olive oil, 2 cloves of minced garlic, 1 tablespoon of grated fresh ginger, 1 teaspoon of ground cumin, 1/2 teaspoon of smoked paprika, salt, and pepper in a blender. Blend until smooth. Pour the marinade over 1 cup of pineapple chunks and refrigerate for at least 2 hours or overnight. Grill the pineapple until caramelized and serve with a sprinkle of chopped cilantro and a dollop of sour cream.

Marinade 2: Coconut Lime Oasis

Mix 1/2 cup of coconut milk, 1/4 cup of freshly squeezed lime juice, 2 tablespoons of olive oil, 1 tablespoon of grated fresh ginger, 1 teaspoon of honey, salt, and pepper in a bowl. Stir until well combined. Pour the marinade over 1 cup of pineapple chunks and refrigerate for at least 2 hours or overnight. Grill the pineapple until slightly charred and serve with a sprinkle of toasted coconut flakes and a squeeze of fresh lime juice.

Marinade 3: Korean BBQ Fusion

Combine 1/2 cup of soy sauce, 1/4 cup of brown sugar, 2 tablespoons of rice vinegar, 2 tablespoons of olive oil, 2 cloves of minced garlic, 1 tablespoon of grated fresh ginger, 1 teaspoon of crushed red pepper flakes, salt, and pepper in a blender. Blend until smooth. Pour the marinade over 1 cup of pineapple chunks and refrigerate for at least 2 hours or overnight. Grill the pineapple until caramelized and serve with a sprinkle of toasted sesame seeds and a side of steamed bok choy.

Marinade 4: Indian Spice Route

Mix 1/2 cup of plain yogurt, 1/4 cup of freshly squeezed lemon juice, 2 tablespoons of olive oil, 1 tablespoon of grated fresh ginger, 1 teaspoon of ground cumin, 1/2 teaspoon of ground coriander, 1/4 teaspoon of cayenne pepper, salt, and pepper in a bowl. Stir until well combined. Pour the marinade over 1 cup of pineapple chunks and refrigerate for at least 2 hours or overnight. Grill the pineapple until slightly charred and serve with a sprinkle of chopped fresh cilantro and a side of basmati rice.

Marinade 5: Brazilian Citrus Zing

Combine 1/2 cup of freshly squeezed orange juice, 1/4 cup of olive oil, 2 tablespoons of honey, 2 cloves of minced garlic, 1 tablespoon of grated fresh ginger, salt, and pepper in a blender. Blend until smooth. Pour the marinade over 1 cup of pineapple chunks and refrigerate for at least 2 hours or overnight. Grill the pineapple until caramelized and serve with a sprinkle of toasted almonds and a side of grilled shrimp or chicken.

There you have it – five mouth-watering marinades to give your grilled pineapple a tropical twist. Whether you’re looking for a spicy kick, a creamy sauce, or a burst of citrus flavor, there’s something on this list for everyone. So go ahead, fire up the grill, and get ready to indulge in the sweet, juicy flavors of grilled pineapple like never before.
