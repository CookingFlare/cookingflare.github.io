Flavorful Low Carb Asian Stir Fry Recipe


If you’re looking for a delicious and healthy way to enjoy Asian cuisine without the guilt, then this flavorful low carb Asian stir fry recipe is just what you need. Packed with vibrant flavors, fresh ingredients, and a satisfying crunch, this dish is a perfect balance of taste and nutrition. Whether you’re following a low carb diet or simply want to incorporate more veggies into your meals, this stir fry is a fantastic option that’s quick and easy to make.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb (450g) boneless, skinless chicken breasts, thinly sliced
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon oyster sauce
  • 1 tablespoon sesame oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon fresh ginger, grated
  • 1 red bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 yellow bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 cup broccoli florets
  • 1 cup snap peas
  • 1 cup sliced mushrooms
  • 1/2 cup water chestnuts, sliced
  • 2 green onions, chopped
  • 1/4 cup chicken broth
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. In a small bowl, combine the soy sauce, oyster sauce, and sesame oil. Add the sliced chicken and toss to coat. Set aside to marinate for 10-15 minutes.

  2. Heat 1 tablespoon of vegetable oil in a large wok or skillet over medium-high heat. Add the marinated chicken and stir-fry for 3-4 minutes, or until fully cooked. Remove the chicken from the wok and set aside.

  3. In the same wok, heat the remaining tablespoon of vegetable oil. Add the minced garlic and grated ginger, and stir-fry for 1 minute until fragrant.

  4. Add the sliced bell peppers, broccoli florets, snap peas, mushrooms, and water chestnuts to the wok. Stir-fry for 3-4 minutes, or until the vegetables are tender-crisp.

  5. Return the cooked chicken to the wok, along with the chopped green onions. Stir in the chicken broth and continue to cook for an additional 2-3 minutes, allowing the flavors to meld together.

  6. Season the stir fry with salt and pepper to taste. Serve hot and garnish with additional chopped green onions, if desired.

Tips for Success

  • To save time, you can prep the vegetables and slice the chicken in advance.
  • Feel free to customize the recipe by adding other low carb vegetables such as zucchini, cauliflower, or bok choy.
  • For a vegetarian option, substitute the chicken with tofu or tempeh.

Health Benefits

This low carb Asian stir fry is not only bursting with flavor but also provides a wealth of health benefits. Packed with lean protein from the chicken and an assortment of nutrient-dense vegetables, this dish is a great way to fuel your body with essential vitamins and minerals. The use of minimal oil and the absence of heavy sauces make it a light and satisfying option for those looking to maintain a balanced diet.

Conclusion

In conclusion, this flavorful low carb Asian stir fry recipe is a versatile and wholesome dish that’s perfect for any occasion. Whether you’re cooking for a weeknight dinner or entertaining guests, this stir fry is sure to impress with its vibrant colors and bold flavors. With a harmonious blend of protein and vegetables, it’s a delightful way to savor the tastes of Asia while staying true to your low carb lifestyle. Give it a try and let your taste buds take a flavorful journey to the heart of Asia!
