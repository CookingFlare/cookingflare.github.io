Creamy Corn Chowder: Summer in a Bowl


There’s something incredibly satisfying about indulging in a warm, creamy bowl of soup, especially during the summer months when fresh produce is abundant. One soup that perfectly captures the essence of summer is Creamy Corn Chowder. Bursting with the flavors of sweet corn, velvety potatoes, and a hint of smoky bacon, this chowder is like a taste of sunshine in every spoonful.

Ingredients

  • 4 cups fresh sweet corn kernels (approximately 6-8 ears of corn)
  • 4 slices bacon, diced
  • 1 medium onion, finely chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 medium potatoes, peeled and diced
  • 4 cups chicken or vegetable broth
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh chives, chopped (for garnish)

Instructions

  1. In a large pot or Dutch oven, cook the diced bacon over medium heat until crispy. Remove the bacon from the pot, leaving the drippings behind.

  2. Add the chopped onion to the pot and sauté until translucent, about 5 minutes. Stir in the minced garlic and cook for an additional minute.

  3. Add the diced potatoes to the pot and stir to coat them with the bacon drippings. Cook for 3-4 minutes, allowing the potatoes to slightly brown.

  4. Pour in the chicken or vegetable broth, scraping the bottom of the pot to release any flavorful bits. Bring the mixture to a boil, then reduce the heat to low and simmer for 15-20 minutes, until the potatoes are tender.

  5. While the potatoes are cooking, reserve 1 cup of the corn kernels and set aside. Blend the remaining corn kernels in a blender or food processor until smooth.

  6. Once the potatoes are tender, stir in the blended corn and reserved corn kernels. Simmer for an additional 5 minutes, allowing the flavors to meld together.

  7. Stir in the heavy cream and dried thyme, and season with salt and pepper to taste. Continue to simmer for another 5 minutes until the chowder reaches a creamy consistency.

  8. Ladle the Creamy Corn Chowder into bowls and garnish with the crispy bacon and chopped fresh chives. Serve hot and enjoy!

Tips and Variations

  • For a vegetarian or vegan version, simply omit the bacon and use vegetable broth instead of chicken broth. You can also replace the heavy cream with coconut milk or a non-dairy alternative for a creamy and dairy-free option.

  • If you prefer a chunkier chowder, you can reserve more corn kernels to add at the end, giving a delightful texture to each spoonful.

  • Feel free to customize your chowder by adding other vegetables such as diced carrots, bell peppers, or even jalapeños for a spicy kick. Experiment with different herbs and spices to suit your taste.

  • To make this recipe even more convenient, you can prepare it in advance and freeze it in individual portions. Simply thaw and reheat whenever you’re craving a taste of summer.

Creamy Corn Chowder: Summer in a Bowl is a delightful way to celebrate the flavors of the season. With its creamy texture, sweet corn, and savory bacon, this chowder is sure to become a staple in your summer recipe repertoire. So grab a spoon and savor the taste of summer in every delicious bite.
