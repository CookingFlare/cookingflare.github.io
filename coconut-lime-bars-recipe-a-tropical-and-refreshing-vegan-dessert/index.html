Coconut Lime Bars Recipe: A Tropical and Refreshing Vegan Dessert


If you’re looking for a delightful and refreshing dessert to satisfy your sweet tooth, these Coconut Lime Bars are the perfect choice. Not only are they delicious, but they’re also vegan, making them suitable for a wide range of dietary preferences. Bursting with tropical flavors and a hint of zesty lime, these bars are a delightful treat for any occasion. Whether you’re hosting a summer gathering or simply craving a taste of the tropics, these Coconut Lime Bars are sure to impress.

Ingredients

For the Crust:

  • 1 ½ cups almond flour
  • 1/4 cup coconut oil, melted
  • 1/4 cup maple syrup
  • A pinch of salt

For the Filling:

  • 1 can (14 oz) coconut milk
  • 1/2 cup fresh lime juice
  • Zest of 2 limes
  • 1/3 cup cornstarch
  • 1/2 cup maple syrup
  • A pinch of salt

For the Topping:

  • Shredded coconut for garnish

Instructions

  1. Preheat the Oven: Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C) and line an 8x8 inch baking pan with parchment paper, allowing some overhang for easy removal.

  2. Prepare the Crust: In a bowl, combine the almond flour, melted coconut oil, maple syrup, and a pinch of salt. Mix until the ingredients are well combined. Press the mixture into the bottom of the prepared baking pan, creating an even layer. Bake for 10-12 minutes, or until the crust is lightly golden. Remove from the oven and set aside to cool.

  3. Make the Filling: In a saucepan, whisk together the coconut milk, lime juice, lime zest, cornstarch, maple syrup, and a pinch of salt. Place the saucepan over medium heat and cook, stirring constantly, until the mixture thickens. This should take about 5-7 minutes. Once thickened, remove the mixture from the heat and pour it over the cooled crust, spreading it evenly.

  4. Chill and Set: Place the baking pan in the refrigerator and chill the bars for at least 4 hours, or until the filling is set.

  5. Serve: Once the bars are set, remove them from the pan using the parchment paper overhang. Sprinkle the top with shredded coconut for a decorative touch. Cut the bars into squares and serve chilled.

Tips and Variations

  • For an extra tropical twist, consider adding a splash of coconut rum to the filling mixture for an adult version of these delightful bars.
  • You can experiment with different citrus flavors such as lemon or orange for a unique twist on this recipe.
  • If you prefer a sweeter filling, you can adjust the amount of maple syrup to suit your taste.

Final Thoughts

These Coconut Lime Bars are a delightful fusion of tropical flavors and creamy texture, making them a perfect dessert for warm weather or any time you’re craving a taste of the tropics. The zesty lime and creamy coconut come together beautifully in every bite, creating a truly refreshing and indulgent treat. Whether you’re hosting a summer soirée or simply want to treat yourself to a taste of paradise, these vegan Coconut Lime Bars are sure to impress your taste buds and leave you craving more. Enjoy this delightful dessert and share the tropical goodness with friends and family!
