Perfectly Fluffy Vanilla Bean Cupcakes: A Classic


There’s something undeniably charming about a perfectly baked vanilla cupcake. The subtle sweetness, the comforting aroma, and the delicate crumb all come together to create a classic treat that never goes out of style. In this recipe, I’ll share with you the secrets to creating the most perfectly fluffy vanilla bean cupcakes that will have your friends and family coming back for more.

Ingredients

  • 1 3/4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 1/4 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 vanilla bean
  • 2 teaspoons pure vanilla extract
  • 1/2 cup sour cream
  • 1/2 cup whole milk

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C) and line a cupcake tin with paper liners.

  2. In a medium bowl, whisk together the flour, baking powder, baking soda, and salt.

  3. In a separate bowl, beat the butter and sugar together until light and fluffy.

  4. Split the vanilla bean lengthwise and scrape out the seeds. Add the seeds and the vanilla extract to the butter and sugar mixture, and beat until well combined.

  5. Add the eggs one at a time, beating well after each addition.

  6. In a small bowl, whisk together the sour cream and milk.

  7. Gradually add the flour mixture and the sour cream mixture to the butter and sugar mixture, alternating between the two, beginning and ending with the flour mixture. Mix until just combined, being careful not to overmix.

  8. Divide the batter evenly among the prepared cupcake liners, filling each about two-thirds full.

  9. Bake for 18-20 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center of a cupcake comes out clean.

  10. Allow the cupcakes to cool in the tin for a few minutes before transferring them to a wire rack to cool completely.

Frosting

No vanilla cupcake is complete without a luscious frosting to top it off. Here’s a simple vanilla bean frosting that pairs perfectly with these fluffy cupcakes.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 2 cups confectioners’ sugar
  • 1 vanilla bean
  • 1-2 tablespoons heavy cream
  • Pinch of salt

Instructions

  1. In a large bowl, beat the butter until smooth and creamy.

  2. Split the vanilla bean lengthwise and scrape out the seeds. Add the seeds to the butter and beat until well combined.

  3. Gradually add the confectioners’ sugar, beating well after each addition.

  4. Add the heavy cream, a tablespoon at a time, until the frosting reaches your desired consistency.

  5. Add a pinch of salt and beat the frosting for an additional minute until light and fluffy.

  6. Once the cupcakes are completely cooled, generously frost each cupcake with the vanilla bean frosting.

Conclusion

These perfectly fluffy vanilla bean cupcakes are a timeless classic that’s perfect for any occasion. Whether you’re hosting a birthday party, a bridal shower, or simply craving a sweet treat, these cupcakes are sure to delight. The aromatic vanilla bean specks in the cupcakes and the luscious vanilla bean frosting will elevate your baking game and leave everyone asking for the recipe. So, go ahead and treat yourself to these delightful cupcakes – you won’t be disappointed!
