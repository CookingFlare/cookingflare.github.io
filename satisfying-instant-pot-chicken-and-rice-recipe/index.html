Satisfying Instant Pot Chicken and Rice Recipe


If you’re looking for a delicious and comforting meal that’s easy to make, look no further than this satisfying Instant Pot chicken and rice recipe. The Instant Pot is a versatile kitchen appliance that can help you create flavorful dishes in a fraction of the time it would take with traditional cooking methods. This recipe is perfect for busy weeknights when you want a hearty meal without spending hours in the kitchen.

Ingredients

  • 1 ½ pounds of boneless, skinless chicken thighs
  • 1 tablespoon of olive oil
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 ½ cups of long-grain white rice
  • 2 ½ cups of chicken broth
  • 1 teaspoon of dried thyme
  • 1 teaspoon of paprika
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley for garnish

Instructions

  1. Start by seasoning the chicken thighs with salt and pepper. Set your Instant Pot to the sauté function and add the olive oil. Once the pot is hot, add the chicken thighs and brown them on both sides. This should take about 3-4 minutes per side. Once browned, remove the chicken from the pot and set it aside.

  2. In the same pot, add the chopped onion and garlic. Sauté for a few minutes until the onion is soft and translucent. Add the rice to the pot and stir to coat it with the onion and garlic mixture.

  3. Pour in the chicken broth and add the dried thyme and paprika. Give everything a good stir to ensure the rice is evenly distributed in the pot.

  4. Place the browned chicken thighs on top of the rice mixture. Secure the lid on the Instant Pot and set the valve to the sealing position. Cook on high pressure for 10 minutes.

  5. Once the cooking time is up, allow the pressure to release naturally for 10 minutes, then carefully switch the valve to the venting position to release any remaining pressure.

  6. Carefully remove the lid and use a fork to fluff up the rice. The chicken should be tender and juicy, and the rice should be perfectly cooked.

  7. Serve the chicken and rice hot, garnished with fresh parsley for a pop of color and flavor.

Tips and Tricks

  • Feel free to customize this recipe with your favorite herbs and spices. Add a squeeze of lemon juice for a bright, citrusy flavor, or toss in some chopped fresh vegetables for added nutrition and texture.
  • If you prefer to use bone-in chicken thighs, increase the cooking time by a few minutes to ensure they are fully cooked through.
  • Leftovers can be stored in an airtight container in the refrigerator for up to 3 days. Simply reheat in the microwave for a quick and satisfying meal.

Final Thoughts

This Instant Pot chicken and rice recipe is a fantastic way to enjoy a comforting and satisfying meal without spending hours in the kitchen. The Instant Pot makes the cooking process a breeze, and the result is tender, flavorful chicken paired with perfectly cooked rice. Whether you’re cooking for a crowd or just for yourself, this recipe is sure to become a go-to favorite in your meal rotation. So, grab your Instant Pot and get ready to enjoy a delicious and satisfying meal with minimal effort!
