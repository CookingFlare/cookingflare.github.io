One Pot Creamy Pesto Chicken and Rice


If you’re looking for a simple yet delicious meal that requires minimal cleanup, then this one pot creamy pesto chicken and rice recipe is perfect for you. This dish is packed with flavor, combining tender chicken, fragrant pesto, and creamy rice in one pot, making it a convenient option for busy weeknights or lazy weekends. Let’s dive into the details of this mouthwatering recipe.

Ingredients

  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 1/2 cups Arborio rice
  • 4 cups chicken broth
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • 1/2 cup basil pesto
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Freshly grated Parmesan cheese for garnish
  • Fresh basil leaves for garnish

Instructions

  1. Start by seasoning the chicken breasts with salt and pepper. In a large skillet or pot, heat the olive oil over medium-high heat. Add the chicken breasts and cook until they are golden brown on both sides. Once cooked, remove the chicken from the skillet and set it aside.

  2. In the same skillet, add the chopped onion and minced garlic. Sauté them until they are soft and fragrant. Add the Arborio rice to the skillet and stir it for a couple of minutes until it’s well coated with the oil and has a slightly toasted aroma.

  3. Pour in the chicken broth and bring the mixture to a gentle boil. Reduce the heat to low, cover the skillet, and let the rice simmer for about 15-20 minutes, or until it’s tender and most of the liquid is absorbed.

  4. While the rice is cooking, slice the cooked chicken into bite-sized pieces. Once the rice is tender, add the sliced chicken back to the skillet.

  5. Stir in the heavy cream and basil pesto, ensuring that the ingredients are well combined. Let the mixture simmer for a few more minutes, allowing the flavors to meld together and the sauce to thicken slightly.

  6. Once the creamy pesto chicken and rice is ready, season it with additional salt and pepper to taste. Serve the dish hot, garnished with freshly grated Parmesan cheese and fresh basil leaves for an extra burst of flavor.

Tips and Variations

  • To add some extra texture and color, consider tossing in some halved cherry tomatoes or steamed broccoli florets when you add the chicken back to the skillet.
  • If you prefer a bit of heat, sprinkle some red pepper flakes over the finished dish for a subtle kick.
  • This recipe can also be made with leftover cooked chicken. Simply skip the initial step of cooking the chicken breasts and proceed with sautéing the onions and garlic.

In Conclusion

This one pot creamy pesto chicken and rice dish is a fantastic option for anyone seeking a comforting and satisfying meal without the hassle of using multiple pots and pans. The creamy texture of the rice, paired with the vibrant flavors of basil pesto and tender chicken, makes this recipe a standout. Whether you’re cooking for your family or hosting a small gathering, this dish is sure to impress. Give it a try and savor the delightful combination of flavors in every bite!
