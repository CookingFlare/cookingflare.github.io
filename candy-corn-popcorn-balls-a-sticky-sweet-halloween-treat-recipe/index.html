Candy Corn Popcorn Balls: A Sticky-Sweet Halloween Treat Recipe


Halloween is just around the corner, and what better way to get into the spooky spirit than with a sweet and sticky treat? Candy corn popcorn balls are a classic Halloween snack that combines the crunch of popcorn with the sweetness of candy corn. In this recipe, we’ll show you how to make these adorable and delicious treats at home.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup light corn syrup
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/4 cup water
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon baking soda
  • 2 tablespoons unsalted butter
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 6 cups popped popcorn (unsalted)
  • 1 cup candy corn
  • 1 cup white chocolate chips
  • 1 tablespoon shortening (such as Crisco)
  • Confectioners’ sugar (for dusting)

Instructions

To start, combine the corn syrup, sugar, water, and salt in a medium saucepan. Place the saucepan over medium heat and stir until the sugar has dissolved. Bring the mixture to a boil, then reduce the heat to medium-low and simmer for 10 minutes.

Remove the saucepan from the heat and stir in the baking soda. The mixture will start to foam and bubble. Let it cool slightly, then stir in the butter and vanilla extract until they are fully incorporated.

Next, add the popped popcorn to the saucepan and stir until it is evenly coated with the syrup mixture. The popcorn should be moist and sticky, but not soggy.

Line a large baking sheet with parchment paper. Using a greased spoon or spatula, drop small balls of the popcorn mixture onto the baking sheet. You should end up with around 20-25 balls.

Melt the white chocolate chips in a double boiler or in the microwave in 30-second increments, stirring between each interval until smooth. Stir in the shortening until fully incorporated.

Dip each popcorn ball into the melted white chocolate, using a fork or dipping tool to coat the ball evenly. Tap off any excess chocolate by gently tapping the fork or dipping tool against the side of the bowl.

Place the dipped popcorn balls on a piece of parchment paper or a silicone mat. Refrigerate for at least 30 minutes to allow the chocolate to set.

Once the chocolate is set, dust the popcorn balls with confectioners’ sugar. You can also add additional decorations, such as candy eyes or edible markers, to make the popcorn balls look like little monsters.

Tips and Variations

  • To make the popcorn balls more festive, you can add a few drops of orange or yellow food coloring to the syrup mixture to give it a candy corn color.
  • If you find that your popcorn balls are too sticky, you can try adding a little more baking soda to the syrup mixture. If they are too dry, you can try adding a little more corn syrup.
  • You can also add other mix-ins, such as chopped nuts or dried cranberries, to the popcorn mixture for added texture and flavor.

Conclusion

Candy corn popcorn balls are a fun and easy Halloween treat that are perfect for parties or trick-or-treat bags. With their sticky-sweet coating and crunchy popcorn center, they’re sure to be a hit with both kids and adults. So why not give them a try this Halloween season?
