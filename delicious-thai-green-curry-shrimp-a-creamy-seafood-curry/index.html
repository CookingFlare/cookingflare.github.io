Delicious Thai Green Curry Shrimp: A Creamy Seafood Curry


If you’re looking to elevate your culinary repertoire, there’s nothing quite like crafting a bowl of Thai Green Curry Shrimp. This dish is a delightful fusion of flavors, featuring succulent shrimp simmered in a fragrant, creamy green curry sauce. With a burst of freshness from herbs and a touch of spice, this recipe is sure to impress family and friends alike. Let’s dive into the ingredients, preparation, and tips for creating the perfect Thai green curry shrimp that will leave your taste buds singing.

What is Thai Green Curry?

Thai green curry, or “Kaeng Khiao Wan,” is a popular dish in Thai cuisine, renowned for its vibrant green color and aromatic flavor profile. The green hue comes from fresh green chilies and herbs, particularly basil and cilantro. This curry offers a unique balance of heat and creaminess, often made with coconut milk that provides a rich base for the dish. While traditionally prepared with chicken or beef, shrimp is an excellent seafood option that absorbs the flavors beautifully.

Ingredients You’ll Need

To create a delicious Thai Green Curry Shrimp, gather the following ingredients:

For the Curry Paste:

  • 1 tablespoon of vegetable oil
  • 2 tablespoons of green curry paste (store-bought or homemade)
  • 1 can (400 ml) of coconut milk
  • 1 cup of vegetable or chicken broth
  • 1 tablespoon of fish sauce
  • 1 tablespoon of brown sugar
  • 1 tablespoon of lime juice

For the Shrimp and Vegetables:

  • 1 pound of large shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 1 cup of bell peppers, sliced (red, yellow, and green for color)
  • 1 cup of zucchini, sliced
  • 1 cup of snap peas or green beans
  • 1 cup of Thai basil leaves
  • 1-2 kaffir lime leaves (optional for extra aroma)
  • 1 tablespoon of fresh cilantro, chopped (for garnish)
  • 1 tablespoon of lime wedges (for serving)

For Serving:

  • Jasmine rice or sticky rice

Making Your Thai Green Curry Shrimp

Step 1: Preparing the Ingredients

Begin by preparing your shrimp and vegetables. Ensure the shrimp is cleaned and deveined, and cut the vegetables into bite-sized pieces. Having all your ingredients ready will make the cooking process smoother and more enjoyable.

Step 2: Cooking the Curry Paste

In a large pan or wok, heat the vegetable oil over medium heat. Add the green curry paste and sauté for about 1-2 minutes until it becomes fragrant. This step is crucial, as it helps to release the essential oils in the curry paste, enhancing the overall flavor of the dish.

Step 3: Adding Coconut Milk

Once the curry paste is aromatic, slowly pour in the coconut milk while stirring continuously. This will help to blend the curry paste evenly into the coconut milk, creating a luscious sauce. After the coconut milk is fully incorporated, add the vegetable or chicken broth, fish sauce, brown sugar, and lime juice. Stir well and let the mixture come to a gentle simmer.

Step 4: Cooking the Shrimp and Vegetables

When the curry sauce is simmering, it’s time to add the shrimp. Gently place the shrimp into the pan, followed by the bell peppers, zucchini, and snap peas. If you’re using kaffir lime leaves, add them now for an extra layer of flavor. Allow everything to simmer together for about 5-7 minutes or until the shrimp turn pink and the vegetables are tender but still crisp.

Step 5: Finishing Touches

Once the shrimp are cooked through, stir in the fresh Thai basil leaves. The residual heat will wilt the basil, releasing its aromatic essence into the curry. Taste your dish and adjust the seasoning, adding more fish sauce or lime juice if necessary.

Step 6: Serving

Serve your Thai Green Curry Shrimp hot over a bed of fluffy jasmine or sticky rice. Garnish with fresh cilantro and lime wedges for an added zing. Enjoy the creamy, spicy, and savory flavors that make this dish a standout.

Tips for the Perfect Thai Green Curry Shrimp

  1. Quality Ingredients: The key to a great Thai Green Curry is using high-quality ingredients, especially the curry paste and coconut milk. If possible, opt for brands that use natural ingredients.

  2. Fresh Herbs: Fresh basil and cilantro make a world of difference in this dish. For an authentic taste, use Thai basil, which has a slightly different flavor profile than regular basil.

  3. Balance the Flavors: Taste your curry as you go. Adjust the fish sauce, lime juice, and sugar to find the perfect balance of salty, sour, and sweet.

  4. Customize Your Vegetables: Feel free to mix and match vegetables based on what’s in season or what you have on hand. Eggplant, mushrooms, or broccoli also work wonderfully in green curry.

  5. Heat Level: If you’re sensitive to spice, start with a smaller amount of green curry paste and gradually increase it to your taste preferences.

Nutritional Benefits of Thai Green Curry Shrimp

This dish not only tantalizes your taste buds but also offers several health benefits. Shrimp is a fantastic source of lean protein, low in calories, and packed with essential nutrients like selenium, vitamin B12, and iodine. The inclusion of fresh vegetables adds fiber, vitamins, and antioxidants, making this curry a well-rounded meal.

Coconut milk, while rich and creamy, provides healthy fats, particularly medium-chain triglycerides (MCTs) that may aid in metabolism. The herbs and spices used, such as basil and lime, are also known for their anti-inflammatory and digestive benefits.

Conclusion

Delicious Thai Green Curry Shrimp is a dish that embodies the heart of Thai cuisine—bold flavors, vibrant colors, and a delightful balance of ingredients. Whether you’re hosting a dinner party or simply treating yourself to a comforting meal, this creamy seafood curry is sure to impress. With its rich aroma and delectable taste, it’s a dish that will transport you straight to the bustling streets of Thailand.

So next time you’re craving something exotic and fulfilling, whip up this Thai Green Curry Shrimp. Your palate will thank you! Enjoy the journey of cooking and the delightful experience of sharing this dish with loved ones. Happy cooking!
