Creamy Nut-Free Hummus Recipe


If you’re a fan of creamy, flavorful dips, then you’re in for a treat with this nut-free hummus recipe. Hummus is a versatile and nutritious spread that originates from the Middle East and is loved by people all over the world. While traditional hummus recipes often contain tahini, which is made from sesame seeds, this nut-free version is just as delicious and suitable for those with nut allergies or sensitivities.

Ingredients

  • 2 cans of chickpeas, drained and rinsed
  • 3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1/4 cup of extra virgin olive oil
  • 3 tablespoons of freshly squeezed lemon juice
  • 1 teaspoon of ground cumin
  • 1/2 teaspoon of paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon of salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon of black pepper
  • 2-3 tablespoons of water
  • Fresh parsley for garnish

Instructions

  1. In a food processor, combine the chickpeas, minced garlic, extra virgin olive oil, lemon juice, cumin, paprika, salt, and black pepper.

  2. Blend the ingredients until smooth, scraping down the sides of the food processor as needed to ensure everything is well incorporated.

  3. With the food processor running, gradually add the water, one tablespoon at a time, until the hummus reaches your desired creamy consistency.

  4. Taste the hummus and adjust the seasonings if needed. You can add more lemon juice, salt, or cumin according to your preference.

  5. Once the hummus is smooth and creamy, transfer it to a serving bowl. Drizzle a bit of extra virgin olive oil on top and sprinkle with paprika for a pop of color.

  6. Garnish the hummus with fresh parsley for a touch of freshness and a burst of flavor.

  7. Serve the nut-free hummus with your favorite dippers such as pita bread, vegetable sticks, or crackers. It also makes a delightful spread for sandwiches or wraps.

Nutritional Benefits

This nut-free hummus is not only delicious but also packed with nutritional benefits. Chickpeas are a great source of plant-based protein, fiber, and various vitamins and minerals. They can aid in digestion, help manage blood sugar levels, and support heart health. Combined with the healthy fats from the olive oil and the immune-boosting properties of garlic and lemon, this hummus is a powerhouse of goodness.

Customization

One of the best things about hummus is its versatility. You can customize this nut-free recipe to suit your taste preferences. For a spicy kick, add a pinch of cayenne pepper or a drizzle of hot sauce. If you love the earthy flavor of roasted vegetables, consider blending in some roasted red peppers or carrots for a colorful twist. You can even experiment with fresh herbs like cilantro or basil for a unique flavor profile.

Final Thoughts

Making your own nut-free hummus at home is not only simple but also allows you to control the quality of ingredients and adjust the flavors to your liking. Whether you’re hosting a gathering, packing a picnic, or simply craving a wholesome snack, this creamy hummus is sure to be a hit. Enjoy the process of creating this delightful dip, and savor every creamy, flavorful bite!

I hope you find this nut-free hummus recipe enjoyable and easy to follow. Whether you’re a seasoned cook or a beginner in the kitchen, making your own hummus is a rewarding experience. The versatility of this dip makes it a perfect addition to any meal or gathering. So, roll up your sleeves, gather your ingredients, and prepare to delight your taste buds with this creamy creation!
