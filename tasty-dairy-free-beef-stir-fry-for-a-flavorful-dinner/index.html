Tasty Dairy-Free Beef Stir-Fry for a Flavorful Dinner


Are you craving a delicious and satisfying dinner that’s dairy-free and packed with flavor? Look no further than this mouthwatering dairy-free beef stir-fry recipe. This dish is a perfect blend of tender beef, crisp vegetables, and savory sauce that will leave your taste buds tingling. Plus, it’s easy to make and customizable to suit your preferences. Let’s dive into the details of this delectable dish!

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound of beef (flank steak or sirloin, thinly sliced)
  • 2 tablespoons of soy sauce (or tamari for a gluten-free option)
  • 2 tablespoons of oyster sauce (look for a dairy-free version)
  • 1 tablespoon of rice vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon of sesame oil
  • 3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon of fresh ginger, grated
  • 1 teaspoon of cornstarch
  • 2 tablespoons of vegetable oil
  • 1 onion, sliced
  • 1 red bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 green bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 cup of snap peas
  • Cooked rice or noodles for serving

Instructions:

  1. Marinate the Beef:

    • In a bowl, combine the thinly sliced beef, soy sauce, oyster sauce, rice vinegar, sesame oil, minced garlic, grated ginger, and cornstarch. Toss to coat the beef evenly and let it marinate for at least 15 minutes.

  2. Prepare the Vegetables:

    • While the beef is marinating, prepare the vegetables by slicing the onion, red bell pepper, and green bell pepper, and set them aside with the snap peas.

  3. Stir-Fry the Beef:

    • Heat a large skillet or wok over high heat and add the vegetable oil. Once the oil is hot, add the marinated beef to the skillet in a single layer. Let it sear for about 1-2 minutes without stirring to get a nice, caramelized crust. Then, stir-fry the beef for another 2-3 minutes until it’s cooked to your desired doneness. Remove the beef from the skillet and set it aside.

  4. Cook the Vegetables:

    • In the same skillet, add a little more oil if needed, then add the sliced onion and bell peppers. Stir-fry for 2-3 minutes until the vegetables are slightly softened but still crisp. Add the snap peas and continue stir-frying for another minute.

  5. Combine and Serve:

    • Return the cooked beef to the skillet with the vegetables. Pour any remaining marinade over the beef and vegetables. Toss everything together until the sauce thickens slightly and coats the beef and vegetables. Serve the dairy-free beef stir-fry over cooked rice or noodles and enjoy!

Tips for Customization:

  • Feel free to add other vegetables such as broccoli, mushrooms, or baby corn to the stir-fry for extra color and nutrients.
  • Adjust the level of heat by adding red pepper flakes or a drizzle of sriracha for a spicy kick.
  • For a low-carb option, serve the stir-fry over cauliflower rice or zucchini noodles.
  • Experiment with different cuts of beef or even try it with chicken or tofu for a versatile twist.

This dairy-free beef stir-fry is a fantastic way to enjoy a flavorful and wholesome dinner without the need for any dairy products. It’s a great option for those with lactose intolerance or anyone looking to reduce their dairy consumption. Plus, the combination of tender beef, vibrant vegetables, and a savory sauce makes it an irresistible choice for a quick and satisfying meal. Try this recipe for your next dinner, and savor the delightful flavors of this dairy-free delight!
