Zesty Lemon Garlic Grilled Scallops: A Seafood Delight


If you’re a seafood lover, you’re in for a treat with this zesty lemon garlic grilled scallops recipe. This dish is a true delight for the senses, combining the succulent flavor of scallops with the bright, tangy zest of lemon and the aromatic allure of garlic. Grilling the scallops adds a beautiful charred flavor that elevates this dish to a whole new level. Whether you’re hosting a summer barbecue or simply craving a taste of the sea, these grilled scallops are sure to impress your family and friends.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound fresh scallops
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • Zest of 1 lemon
  • Juice of 1 lemon
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • Fresh parsley for garnish

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Scallops: Start by patting the scallops dry with a paper towel to remove excess moisture. This will help the scallops achieve a beautiful sear on the grill.

  2. Make the Marinade: In a small bowl, combine the minced garlic, lemon zest, lemon juice, olive oil, salt, and black pepper. Whisk the ingredients together to create a flavorful marinade for the scallops.

  3. Marinate the Scallops: Place the scallops in a shallow dish and pour the marinade over them, ensuring that each scallop is coated evenly. Cover the dish and let the scallops marinate in the refrigerator for 30 minutes to 1 hour, allowing the flavors to infuse.

  4. Preheat the Grill: Prepare your grill for direct grilling over medium-high heat. If using a gas grill, preheat it to around 400°F. If using a charcoal grill, ensure that the coals are hot and ready for grilling.

  5. Grill the Scallops: Remove the scallops from the marinade and discard any excess marinade. Place the scallops directly on the grill grates and cook for 2-3 minutes on each side, or until they develop a beautiful golden-brown crust. Be careful not to overcook the scallops, as they can become tough and rubbery.

  6. Serve and Garnish: Once the scallops are grilled to perfection, transfer them to a serving platter. Sprinkle fresh parsley over the scallops for a pop of color and added freshness. Serve the grilled scallops immediately, and watch as your guests savor each juicy, flavorful bite.

Tips for Success

  • Selecting Scallops: When purchasing scallops, opt for fresh, dry-packed scallops if possible. These scallops have a sweet, delicate flavor and will grill beautifully.

  • Grilling Time: Keep a close eye on the scallops while grilling, as they cook quickly. Overcooking can result in a loss of their tender texture, so aim for a golden sear on the outside while maintaining a juicy interior.

  • Marinating: Allow the scallops to marinate for at least 30 minutes to let the flavors penetrate the flesh. This step is crucial for infusing the scallops with the zesty lemon garlic goodness.

  • Presentation: For an elegant presentation, consider serving the grilled scallops on a bed of mixed greens or alongside a fresh citrus salad.

There you have it - a tantalizing recipe for zesty lemon garlic grilled scallops that’s perfect for any occasion. Whether you’re a seasoned seafood enthusiast or new to the world of scallops, this dish is bound to become a favorite in your culinary repertoire. So fire up the grill, gather your ingredients, and get ready to savor the irresistible flavors of these delectable grilled scallops!
