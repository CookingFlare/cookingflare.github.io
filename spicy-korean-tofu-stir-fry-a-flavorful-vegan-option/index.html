Spicy Korean Tofu Stir Fry: A Flavorful Vegan Option


If you’re looking for a delicious and satisfying vegan dish that’s packed with flavor, this Spicy Korean Tofu Stir Fry is the perfect choice. This dish combines the bold and spicy flavors of Korean cuisine with the wholesome goodness of tofu, making it a delightful and nutritious option for any mealtime. Whether you’re a seasoned vegan cook or just looking to incorporate more plant-based meals into your diet, this recipe is sure to become a favorite in your kitchen.

Ingredients

For the Tofu Marinade:

  • 14 oz (400g) firm tofu, drained and cubed
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp sesame oil
  • 1 tbsp rice vinegar
  • 1 tbsp maple syrup
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tsp grated ginger
  • 1/2 tsp red pepper flakes

For the Stir Fry:

  • 1 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 1 red bell pepper, thinly sliced
  • 1 yellow bell pepper, thinly sliced
  • 1 cup sliced mushrooms
  • 1 cup snap peas
  • 4 green onions, sliced
  • Cooked rice or noodles, for serving

For the Spicy Sauce:

  • 3 tbsp Korean chili paste (Gochujang)
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp maple syrup
  • 1 tbsp rice vinegar
  • 1 tsp sesame oil

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Tofu:

    • In a bowl, whisk together the soy sauce, sesame oil, rice vinegar, maple syrup, garlic, ginger, and red pepper flakes to create the marinade.
    • Add the cubed tofu to the marinade, gently toss to coat, and let it marinate for at least 30 minutes.

  2. Make the Spicy Sauce:

    • In a small bowl, combine the Korean chili paste, soy sauce, maple syrup, rice vinegar, and sesame oil to create the spicy sauce. Set aside.

  3. Cook the Tofu:

    • In a large skillet or wok, heat the vegetable oil over medium-high heat.
    • Add the marinated tofu to the skillet and cook until golden brown on all sides. Remove the tofu from the skillet and set aside.

  4. Stir Fry the Vegetables:

    • In the same skillet, add the sliced bell peppers, mushrooms, and snap peas, and stir fry for 3-4 minutes until the vegetables are tender-crisp.

  5. Combine and Serve:

    • Return the cooked tofu to the skillet with the vegetables.
    • Pour the spicy sauce over the tofu and vegetables, and toss to coat evenly.
    • Cook for an additional 2-3 minutes, allowing the flavors to meld together.
    • Remove from heat and stir in the sliced green onions.
    • Serve the Spicy Korean Tofu Stir Fry over cooked rice or noodles.

Why You’ll Love This Dish

  • Flavorful and Spicy: The combination of Gochujang and aromatic spices creates a bold and spicy flavor profile that will tantalize your taste buds.
  • Versatile: This dish can be served over rice, noodles, or even as a filling for lettuce wraps, making it a versatile option for any occasion.
  • Nutritious: Tofu is a great source of plant-based protein, and the colorful array of vegetables provides essential vitamins and minerals.
  • Easy to Make: With simple ingredients and easy-to-follow instructions, this Spicy Korean Tofu Stir Fry is a breeze to prepare, making it perfect for busy weeknights.

Whether you’re a vegan connoisseur or simply looking to expand your culinary horizons, this Spicy Korean Tofu Stir Fry is a must-try recipe. It’s a delightful fusion of flavors and textures that will leave you craving more. So, roll up your sleeves and get ready to create a sensational vegan dish that’s sure to impress even the most discerning palates. Enjoy!
