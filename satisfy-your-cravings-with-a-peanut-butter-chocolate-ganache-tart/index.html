Satisfy Your Cravings with a Peanut Butter Chocolate Ganache Tart


If you’re a fan of the classic combination of peanut butter and chocolate, then this indulgent Peanut Butter Chocolate Ganache Tart is the perfect treat for you. This delectable dessert brings together the rich, nutty flavor of peanut butter with the smooth, luxurious taste of chocolate ganache, all nestled in a crisp, buttery crust. Whether you’re hosting a special gathering or simply craving a luxurious dessert, this recipe is sure to impress.

Ingredients

For the Crust:

  • 1 ½ cups of chocolate wafer cookies, finely crushed
  • 6 tablespoons of unsalted butter, melted
  • 1 tablespoon of granulated sugar
  • Pinch of salt

For the Peanut Butter Filling:

  • 1 cup of creamy peanut butter
  • 8 oz of cream cheese, softened
  • 1 cup of confectioners’ sugar
  • 1 teaspoon of pure vanilla extract
  • 1 cup of heavy cream

For the Chocolate Ganache:

  • 8 oz of semi-sweet chocolate, finely chopped
  • 1 cup of heavy cream
  • 2 tablespoons of unsalted butter, softened
  • 1 teaspoon of pure vanilla extract

Instructions

Step 1: Prepare the Crust

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. In a medium bowl, combine the finely crushed chocolate wafer cookies, melted butter, granulated sugar, and a pinch of salt. Mix until the crumbs are evenly moistened.
  3. Press the mixture into the bottom and up the sides of a 9-inch tart pan with a removable bottom.
  4. Bake the crust for 10 minutes, then remove it from the oven and let it cool completely.

Step 2: Make the Peanut Butter Filling

  1. In a large bowl, beat the creamy peanut butter and softened cream cheese together until smooth and creamy.
  2. Add the confectioners’ sugar and vanilla extract, and beat until well combined.
  3. In a separate bowl, whip the heavy cream until stiff peaks form.
  4. Gently fold the whipped cream into the peanut butter mixture until no streaks remain.
  5. Spread the peanut butter filling evenly over the cooled crust.

Step 3: Prepare the Chocolate Ganache

  1. Place the finely chopped semi-sweet chocolate in a heatproof bowl.
  2. In a small saucepan, heat the heavy cream until it just begins to simmer.
  3. Pour the hot cream over the chopped chocolate and let it sit for 2-3 minutes.
  4. Gently whisk the chocolate and cream together until smooth and glossy.
  5. Add the softened butter and vanilla extract, and whisk until the ganache is silky and well combined.

Step 4: Assemble the Tart

  1. Pour the chocolate ganache over the peanut butter filling, spreading it evenly with a spatula.
  2. Refrigerate the tart for at least 4 hours, or until the ganache is set.

Serving

Slice the Peanut Butter Chocolate Ganache Tart into elegant wedges and serve chilled. For an extra touch of indulgence, sprinkle some chopped peanuts or chocolate shavings over each slice before serving.

This Peanut Butter Chocolate Ganache Tart is a true celebration of the timeless pairing of peanut butter and chocolate. With its buttery crust, creamy peanut butter filling, and luscious chocolate ganache, this dessert is a symphony of flavors and textures that will leave your taste buds singing. Whether you share it with friends and family or savor it all to yourself, this tart is guaranteed to satisfy even the most discerning sweet tooth. Enjoy every rich, decadent bite!
