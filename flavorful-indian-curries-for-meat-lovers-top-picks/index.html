Flavorful Indian Curries for Meat Lovers: Top Picks


Indian cuisine is renowned for its rich and diverse range of curries, with each region boasting its unique flavor profiles and cooking techniques. For meat lovers, the options can be overwhelming. From the spicy to the mild and creamy, Indian curries have a rich flavor that's sure to tantalize your taste buds. If you're a meat enthusiast, you'll love these top picks for flavorful Indian curries that are sure to elevate your cooking to the next level.

  1. Butter Chicken (Murgh Makhani) A classic dish from the heartland of India, Butter Chicken or sometimes called Murgh Makhani, is a staple for meat enthusiasts. This rich and creamy curry made with marinated chicken breast or thighs is cooked in a mixture of tomato puree, butter, and spices, which results in a smooth and velvety texture. The secret to this dish lies in the use of besan (gram flour) to thicken the sauce, creating a unique flavor and aroma that's a must-try for any meat lover. To make Butter Chicken, you'll need the following ingredients:

500g boneless chicken breast or thighs 250g tomato puree 120g unsalted butter 2 tablespoons gram flour 2 teaspoons garam masala 1 teaspoon cumin powder 1 teaspoon cinnamon powder Salt and pepper, to taste 2 tablespoons chopped onion and green chilies for garnish

Cook the chicken, onion, and green chilies in a mixture of oil and spices until well-cooked. Then add the tomato puree and gram flour to thicken the sauce before adding the crumbled butter. Let it simmer until the sauce thickens, then let it rest for a while before serving. 2.
