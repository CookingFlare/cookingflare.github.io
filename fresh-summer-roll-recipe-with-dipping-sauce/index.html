Fresh Summer Roll Recipe with Dipping Sauce


Summer rolls are a delightful and refreshing treat, perfect for warm days and light meals. These Fresh Summer Rolls filled with crisp vegetables, herbs, and protein of your choice, wrapped in delicate rice paper, are not only delicious but also a feast for the eyes. Paired with a flavorful dipping sauce, they make a fantastic appetizer or a light meal on their own. Let’s dive into the recipe to create these vibrant and tasty Fresh Summer Rolls with Dipping Sauce.

Ingredients:

  • Rice paper wrappers
  • Rice vermicelli noodles
  • Carrots, julienned
  • Cucumber, julienned
  • Red bell pepper, thinly sliced
  • Lettuce leaves
  • Fresh mint leaves
  • Fresh cilantro leaves
  • Cooked shrimp or tofu (for protein)
  • Warm water

Dipping Sauce Ingredients:

  • Soy sauce
  • Rice vinegar
  • Lime juice
  • Brown sugar
  • Garlic, minced
  • Red chili flakes (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the Ingredients:

    • Cook the rice vermicelli noodles according to the package instructions, then drain and set aside.
    • Julienne the carrots and cucumber, slice the red bell pepper thinly, and prepare the lettuce, mint, and cilantro leaves.
    • If using shrimp, make sure they are cooked and peeled. If using tofu, cut it into thin strips.

  2. Prepare the Dipping Sauce:

    • In a small bowl, mix together soy sauce, rice vinegar, lime juice, brown sugar, minced garlic, and red chili flakes. Adjust the taste according to your preference and set the sauce aside.

  3. Assemble the Fresh Summer Rolls:

    • Fill a shallow dish with warm water.
    • Dip a rice paper wrapper into the water for a few seconds until it softens.
    • Place the softened rice paper on a clean surface.
    • Start by layering lettuce leaves on the bottom half of the rice paper, leaving space at the edges.
    • Add a small bunch of rice vermicelli noodles on top of the lettuce.
    • Arrange the julienned carrots, cucumber, and red bell pepper on top of the noodles.
    • Place a few mint and cilantro leaves over the vegetables.
    • Add the cooked shrimp or tofu strips on the vegetables.

  4. Roll the Summer Rolls:

    • Fold the sides of the rice paper over the filling.
    • Tuck and roll the rice paper tightly, making sure the filling is secure.
    • Repeat with the remaining ingredients.

  5. Serve the Fresh Summer Rolls:

    • Cut the rolls in half or leave them whole.
    • Serve the rolls with the prepared dipping sauce on the side.
    • Enjoy the Fresh Summer Rolls with the flavorful dipping sauce.

These Fresh Summer Rolls with Dipping Sauce are not only a delicious and healthy snack but also a fun dish to prepare. The combination of crunchy vegetables, fragrant herbs, and a tasty dipping sauce makes them a crowd-pleaser at any gathering. Customize the filling with your favorite ingredients and enjoy the burst of flavors in every bite.

So, next time you’re looking for a light and refreshing dish, give these Fresh Summer Rolls a try. They are perfect for a quick meal, a party appetizer, or even a colorful addition to your lunchbox. Dive into the world of fresh flavors and enjoy the simplicity and vibrancy of these delightful summer rolls!
