How to Make Vegan Chocolate Cream Pie: Decadent and Rich


If you’re searching for a dessert that combines indulgence with plant-based goodness, look no further than this Vegan Chocolate Cream Pie. With a rich, velvety filling and a crunchy crust, this pie will satisfy even the most discerning chocolate lovers. Whether you’re hosting a dinner party, celebrating a special occasion, or simply treating yourself, this recipe will impress everyone at the table.

Ingredients

For the Crust

  • 1 ½ cups almond flour
  • ½ cup cocoa powder
  • ¼ cup maple syrup
  • ½ cup coconut oil, melted
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt

For the Chocolate Cream Filling

  • 1 ½ cups full-fat coconut milk (chilled)
  • 1 cup dark chocolate chips (dairy-free)
  • ½ cup maple syrup
  • 2 tablespoons cornstarch
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • Pinch of salt

For the Topping

  • 1 cup coconut cream (chilled)
  • 2 tablespoons powdered sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • Dark chocolate shavings (for garnish)

Equipment Needed

  • Mixing bowls
  • 9-inch pie dish
  • Saucepan
  • Whisk
  • Electric mixer or whisk
  • Rubber spatula
  • Refrigerator

Step-by-Step Instructions

Step 1: Prepare the Crust

  1. Preheat the Oven: Start by preheating your oven to 350°F (175°C). This ensures that the crust bakes evenly.

  2. Mix the Ingredients: In a mixing bowl, combine the almond flour, cocoa powder, and salt. Pour in the melted coconut oil and maple syrup. Use a spatula to mix until the ingredients are well combined and form a dough-like consistency.

  3. Press into Pie Dish: Transfer the dough into a 9-inch pie dish. Press it evenly across the bottom and up the sides, ensuring there are no cracks or holes.

  4. Bake the Crust: Place the crust in the preheated oven and bake for about 10-12 minutes. The crust should be firm to the touch. Once baked, remove it from the oven and let it cool completely.

Step 2: Make the Chocolate Cream Filling

  1. Melt the Chocolate: In a saucepan over low heat, combine the dark chocolate chips and half of the chilled coconut milk. Stir continuously until the chocolate is fully melted and the mixture is smooth. Remove from heat.

  2. Whisk Remaining Ingredients: In a separate bowl, whisk together the remaining coconut milk, maple syrup, cornstarch, vanilla extract, and a pinch of salt.

  3. Combine Mixtures: Slowly add the coconut milk mixture to the melted chocolate, whisking continuously until fully incorporated. Make sure there are no lumps.

  4. Heat the Filling: Return the mixture to medium heat and bring it to a gentle simmer. Cook for about 5-7 minutes, stirring often, until the mixture thickens and starts to bubble. Remove from heat.

Step 3: Assemble the Pie

  1. Pour Filling into Crust: Once the chocolate filling has cooled slightly, pour it into the cooled pie crust. Use a spatula to smooth the top.

  2. Chill the Pie: Cover the pie with plastic wrap and refrigerate for at least 4 hours, or overnight if possible. This allows the filling to set properly and enhances the flavors.

Step 4: Make the Coconut Whipped Topping

  1. Prepare Coconut Cream: When you’re ready to serve the pie, take the chilled cans of coconut cream and scoop out the solid cream into a mixing bowl, leaving the liquid behind.

  2. Whip the Cream: Using an electric mixer or a whisk, beat the coconut cream until it becomes light and fluffy. Add powdered sugar and vanilla extract, then continue to whip until fully combined.

Step 5: Serve

  1. Top the Pie: Once the chocolate cream pie has set, remove it from the refrigerator. Spread the coconut whipped cream over the top, creating an even layer.

  2. Garnish: Finish with dark chocolate shavings for a beautiful presentation.

  3. Slice and Enjoy: Cut into slices and serve chilled. Watch as your guests fall in love with this decadent vegan dessert!

Tips for Success

  • Quality Chocolate: Choose high-quality dark chocolate chips for the best flavor. Look for brands that are labeled dairy-free to ensure they fit your vegan diet.
  • Chill Everything: Make sure to chill your coconut milk and cream before using them. This helps achieve a fluffier whipped topping.
  • Experiment with Flavors: Feel free to add a splash of almond extract or a pinch of espresso powder to the filling for a unique twist.

Storage

Store any leftover pie in an airtight container in the refrigerator for up to 4 days. The chocolate filling may become a bit firmer after being refrigerated, but it will still taste delicious.

Nutritional Information

While this Vegan Chocolate Cream Pie is a treat, it also provides some nutritional benefits:

  • Coconut Milk: Rich in healthy fats and may support heart health.
  • Dark Chocolate: Packed with antioxidants and may improve heart health.
  • Almond Flour: A good source of protein and fiber, making it a healthier alternative to traditional flour.

Conclusion

This Vegan Chocolate Cream Pie is not only decadent and rich but also incredibly simple to make. By following this recipe, you can create a dessert that is sure to impress your friends and family, regardless of their dietary preferences. Celebrate special occasions or just indulge yourself with a slice of this heavenly pie. The combination of the crunchy chocolate crust, silky chocolate filling, and fluffy coconut topping is a match made in dessert heaven. Enjoy your culinary adventure and the joy of sharing this delightful treat!
