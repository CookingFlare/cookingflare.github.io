Italian-Style Baked Ziti with Meatballs and Ricotta Recipe


If you’re looking for a hearty and comforting Italian dish that will impress your family and friends, this Italian-style baked ziti with meatballs and ricotta recipe is just what you need. This classic dish combines tender meatballs, zesty marinara sauce, al dente ziti pasta, and creamy ricotta cheese, all baked to perfection. It’s a crowd-pleaser that’s perfect for any occasion, from casual weeknight dinners to special gatherings.

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound ziti pasta
  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1/2 cup breadcrumbs
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 4 cups marinara sauce
  • 1 1/2 cups ricotta cheese
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Fresh basil for garnish

Instructions:

Step 1: Prepare the Meatballs

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. In a large bowl, combine the ground beef, breadcrumbs, Parmesan cheese, egg, garlic powder, dried oregano, dried basil, salt, and pepper. Mix until well combined.
  3. Shape the mixture into meatballs, about 1 inch in diameter, and place them on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper.
  4. Bake the meatballs in the preheated oven for 15-20 minutes or until they are cooked through.

Step 2: Cook the Ziti

  1. While the meatballs are baking, bring a large pot of salted water to a boil.
  2. Add the ziti pasta and cook according to the package instructions until al dente. Drain the pasta and set it aside.

Step 3: Assemble the Baked Ziti

  1. In a large bowl, mix the cooked ziti with the marinara sauce until the pasta is well coated.
  2. In a greased 9x13 inch baking dish, spread half of the ziti and marinara mixture evenly.
  3. Place the cooked meatballs on top of the ziti mixture.
  4. Spoon dollops of ricotta cheese over the meatballs.
  5. Sprinkle half of the mozzarella cheese over the ricotta.
  6. Add the remaining ziti and marinara mixture on top of the cheese layer.
  7. Sprinkle the rest of the mozzarella cheese over the ziti.

Step 4: Bake the Dish

  1. Cover the baking dish with aluminum foil and bake in the preheated oven for 25 minutes.
  2. Remove the foil and bake for an additional 10-15 minutes, or until the cheese is bubbly and golden brown.

Step 5: Serve and Enjoy

  1. Once the baked ziti is done, remove it from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes.
  2. Garnish with fresh basil before serving.
  3. Serve the Italian-style baked ziti with meatballs and ricotta hot, and watch it disappear in no time!

This Italian-style baked ziti with meatballs and ricotta recipe is a delightful combination of flavors and textures that will surely become a favorite in your recipe collection. Whether you’re hosting a casual dinner or a festive gathering, this dish is sure to be a hit. So gather your ingredients, roll up your sleeves, and get ready to create a delicious masterpiece that will have everyone asking for seconds. Buon appetito!
