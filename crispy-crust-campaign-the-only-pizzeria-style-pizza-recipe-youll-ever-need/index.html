Crispy Crust Campaign: The Only Pizzeria-Style Pizza Recipe You'll Ever Need


Are you tired of bland, soggy pizzas that leave you feeling disappointed every time? Make room for a game-changer in the world of homemade pizza – the Crispy Crust Campaign recipe! This foolproof guide will help you recreate that pizzeria-style pizza, complete with a golden-brown and crispy crust, exemplary cheese pull, and a balanced blend of savory toppings in the comfort of your own kitchen. What’s more? Our recipe does away with all those complicated techniques, leaving you with delightful results every time. So, put on your apron, grab your favorite dough mixer and prepare to embark on this culinary adventure!

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup (240ml) lukewarm water
  • 2 ¼ tsp (1 packet) active dry yeast
  • 1 tbsp (15g) sugar
  • 2 ½ tsp (12 ½ g) salt
  • 3 ½ cups (420g) bread flour or all-purpose flour
  • 2 tbsp (30ml) olive oil
  • 1 cup (236ml) tomato sauce (store-bought or homemade)
  • 2 cups (230g) shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Optional toppings: pepperoni, olives, bell peppers, onions, mushrooms, etc.

Equipment:

  • Mixing bowl
  • Dough rising bucket or container
  • Pizza stone or baking sheet
  • Silpat mat or parchment paper
  • Pastry brush
  • Instant-read thermometer
  • Medium-sized bowl
  • Large cutting board
  • Chef’s knife
  • Measuring cups and spoons

Instructions:

Part 1: Prepare the Crispy Pizza Dough

1

In a mixing bowl, combine the lukewarm water, active dry yeast, and sugar. Allow the yeast mixture to sit, undisturbed, for 5-10 minutes. By this time, the yeast should become frothy, indicating it’s ready for the next phase.

2

In the same bowl, add salt and gradually incorporate the flour, one cup at a time. After incorporating the first cup, mix the dough until it starts to form a ball. This may prove challenging initially, but be patient – it will come together easily.

3

Turn the dough onto a lightly floured surface and knead for around 5-7 minutes, or until it’s smooth and elastic. Remember not to skimp on the kneading process; this will ensure the development of a crispy crust.

4

Drizzle the 2 tbsp of olive oil over the dough and gently knead to coat it evenly. Place it back into the mixing bowl, cover it with a clean cloth, and allow it to rise in a warm, draft-free place for about 1-1.5 hours or until it has doubled in size.

Part 2: Create the Flavorful Pizza Toppings

5

While your dough rises, prepare the toppings. In a medium-sized bowl, mix tomato sauce, salt, and any Italian herbs (basil, oregano, thyme, etc.) to taste. Adding some finely chopped garlic enhances the flavor profile to perfection.

6

Preheat your oven to 475°F (245°C) and prepare your pizza stone or baking sheet by placing a Silpat mat or parchment paper on it.

Part 3: Assemble the Pizzeria-Style Pizza

7

Once your dough has risen, gently punch down the dough and transfer it to a lightly floured surface. Roll or stretch the dough into a 12”-14” round circle. Remember, the thinner the dough, the crispier the pizza crust will be!

8

Transfer your prepared dough onto the Silpat or parchment-lined pizza stone or baking sheet.

9

Apply a generous layer of the prepared tomato sauce, leaving some space at the edges for the crust. Sprinkle the shredded mozzarella cheese evenly over the sauce.

10

Now is the time to get creative with your favorite toppings. Artfully arrange pepperoni slices, olives, bell peppers, onions, mushrooms, or any topping of your choice.

Part 4: Achieving the Perfect Pizzeria-Style Pizza

11

Place the pizza in the preheated oven and bake it for 12-14 minutes, keeping a keen eye on the progress. The pizza is done when the crust turns a lovely golden-brown color and the cheese turns bubbly and slightly charred.

12

Remove the cooked pizza from the oven and let it cool for about 5 minutes. This waiting period allows for easier slicing and sharing without losing the heat and ooey-gooey cheese.

13

Slice your pizzeria-style pizza using a sharp chef’s knife into the desired number of pieces and serve hot. Don’t forget to pair it with garlic knots, a crisp green salad, or your favorite dip to complete the experience!

Final Thoughts

Congratulations! You have successfully completed the Crispy Crust Campaign recipe and created an authentic pizzeria-style pizza. Homemade goodness coupled with irresistible flavors make this recipe impossible to beat. Gather your friends and family around the dinner table to enjoy your delicious creations, and treasure the memories you make along the way. Happy pizza-making!
