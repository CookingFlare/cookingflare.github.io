Delicious Picnic Chicken Drumstick Recipes


Looking for some mouthwatering chicken drumstick recipes to elevate your next picnic? Look no further! In this blog post, we’ll share three delicious and easy-to-make recipes that are perfect for outdoor gatherings. From tangy barbecue to zesty lemon herb, these recipes will surely impress your friends and family. So grab your picnic basket, blanket, and let’s get cooking!

1. Barbecue Glazed Chicken Drumsticks

Ingredients:

  • 8 chicken drumsticks
  • 1 cup barbecue sauce
  • 2 tablespoons honey
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon Worcestershire sauce
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon smoked paprika
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your grill to medium-high heat.
  2. In a bowl, combine the barbecue sauce, honey, soy sauce, Worcestershire sauce, minced garlic, smoked paprika, salt, and pepper.
  3. Place the chicken drumsticks in a large resealable plastic bag and pour the marinade over them. Seal the bag and massage the marinade into the drumsticks, ensuring they are evenly coated. Let them marinate in the fridge for at least 1 hour or overnight for maximum flavor.
  4. Remove the drumsticks from the marinade and grill them for about 15-20 minutes, turning occasionally, until they are cooked through and nicely charred.
  5. Brush the drumsticks with the remaining marinade during the last few minutes of grilling.
  6. Serve hot and enjoy!

2. Lemon Herb Chicken Drumsticks

Ingredients:

  • 8 chicken drumsticks
  • Zest and juice of 1 lemon
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon fresh rosemary, chopped
  • 1 tablespoon fresh thyme leaves
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C).
  2. In a bowl, combine the lemon zest, lemon juice, olive oil, minced garlic, chopped rosemary, thyme leaves, salt, and pepper.
  3. Place the chicken drumsticks in a large baking dish and pour the marinade over them. Toss the drumsticks to coat them evenly.
  4. Bake the drumsticks for about 35-40 minutes, or until they are golden brown and cooked through. Flip them halfway through the cooking time to ensure even browning.
  5. Remove from the oven and let them rest for a few minutes before serving.
  6. Serve warm and enjoy the burst of citrusy flavors!

3. Spicy Buffalo Chicken Drumsticks

Ingredients:

  • 8 chicken drumsticks
  • 1/2 cup hot sauce
  • 1/4 cup melted butter
  • 2 tablespoons honey
  • 1 tablespoon Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon onion powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 425°F (220°C).
  2. In a bowl, whisk together the hot sauce, melted butter, honey, Worcestershire sauce, garlic powder, onion powder, salt, and pepper.
  3. Place the chicken drumsticks in a large baking dish and pour the buffalo sauce over them. Toss the drumsticks to coat them evenly.
  4. Bake the drumsticks for about 40-45 minutes, or until they are crispy and cooked through. Flip them halfway through the cooking time to ensure even browning.
  5. Remove from the oven and let them cool for a few minutes before serving.
  6. Serve with your favorite dipping sauce and enjoy the fiery kick!

Conclusion

These delicious picnic chicken drumstick recipes are simple to make and packed with flavor. Whether you prefer the tangy barbecue glaze, the zesty lemon herb marinade, or the spicy buffalo sauce, these recipes are sure to satisfy your taste buds. So, next time you plan a picnic, don’t forget to add these mouthwatering drumsticks to your menu. Happy cooking and happy picnicking!
