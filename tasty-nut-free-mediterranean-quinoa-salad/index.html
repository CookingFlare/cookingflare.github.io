Tasty Nut-Free Mediterranean Quinoa Salad


Looking for a delicious and healthy salad recipe that is not only packed with flavor but also nut-free? Look no further than this Tasty Nut-Free Mediterranean Quinoa Salad! This vibrant and nutritious dish is perfect for lunch, dinner, or as a side dish. It’s easy to make and bursting with Mediterranean flavors that will leave you craving for more.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of quinoa
  • 2 cups of vegetable broth
  • 1 cucumber, diced
  • 1 red bell pepper, diced
  • 1 yellow bell pepper, diced
  • 1/2 red onion, finely chopped
  • 1 cup of cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1/2 cup of Kalamata olives, pitted and halved
  • 1/2 cup of crumbled feta cheese
  • 1/4 cup of fresh parsley, chopped

Dressing

  • 1/4 cup of extra virgin olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons of lemon juice
  • 1 clove of garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon of dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Rinse the quinoa under cold water to remove any bitterness. In a medium-sized saucepan, bring the vegetable broth to a boil. Add the quinoa, reduce heat to low, cover, and let it simmer for about 15-20 minutes or until all the liquid is absorbed. Remove from heat and let it cool.

  2. In a large mixing bowl, combine the diced cucumber, red bell pepper, yellow bell pepper, red onion, cherry tomatoes, Kalamata olives, and crumbled feta cheese.

  3. In a small bowl, whisk together the extra virgin olive oil, lemon juice, minced garlic, dried oregano, salt, and pepper.

  4. Once the quinoa has cooled, add it to the bowl with the vegetables and gently toss to combine.

  5. Pour the dressing over the salad and toss again to ensure everything is well-coated.

  6. Sprinkle fresh parsley over the top for an added burst of flavor and color.

  7. Serve chilled and enjoy!

Nutritional Information

  • Calories: 310
  • Fat: 18g
  • Carbohydrates: 29g
  • Fiber: 5g
  • Protein: 8g

Why You’ll Love This Salad

Not only is this Tasty Nut-Free Mediterranean Quinoa Salad incredibly delicious, but it’s also loaded with nutritional benefits. Quinoa is a complete protein, meaning it contains all nine essential amino acids, making it an excellent choice for vegetarians and vegans. It’s also high in fiber, which aids in digestion and promotes a healthy gut.

The vibrant colors of the fresh vegetables add a visual appeal to the dish, making it perfect for summer gatherings or potlucks. The combination of crisp cucumbers, sweet bell peppers, tangy Kalamata olives, and creamy feta cheese creates a delightful medley of flavors in every bite.

The homemade dressing, made with extra virgin olive oil, lemon juice, and aromatic herbs, complements the salad perfectly, adding a zesty and refreshing touch. Plus, it’s a healthier alternative to store-bought dressings that are often loaded with preservatives and unhealthy fats.

Not only is this salad nut-free, making it suitable for those with allergies or dietary restrictions, but it’s also gluten-free and vegetarian. It’s a versatile dish that can be enjoyed on its own as a light meal or paired with grilled chicken or fish for a heartier option.

In conclusion, this Tasty Nut-Free Mediterranean Quinoa Salad is not only a feast for the taste buds but also a nutritional powerhouse. It’s easy to make, bursting with Mediterranean flavors, and perfect for any occasion. Give it a try and discover a new favorite salad that will leave you feeling satisfied and nourished.
