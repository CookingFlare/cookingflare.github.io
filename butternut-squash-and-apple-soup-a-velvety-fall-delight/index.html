Butternut Squash and Apple Soup: A Velvety Fall Delight


As the leaves start to turn golden and the air becomes crisp, it’s the perfect time to cozy up with a bowl of warm and comforting soup. And what better way to celebrate the flavors of fall than with a delicious butternut squash and apple soup? This velvety smooth soup is a true celebration of the season, combining the natural sweetness of butternut squash with the tartness of apples for a delightful taste sensation. Whether you’re looking for a quick weeknight meal or an impressive starter for a dinner party, this soup is sure to delight your taste buds and warm your soul.

Ingredients

  • 1 medium-sized butternut squash
  • 2 apples, peeled, cored, and chopped
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 4 cups of vegetable or chicken broth
  • 1 teaspoon of ground cinnamon
  • 1/2 teaspoon of ground nutmeg
  • 1/2 teaspoon of ground ginger
  • 1/2 cup of heavy cream (optional)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Olive oil for roasting

Instructions

Step 1: Prepare the Butternut Squash

Start by preheating your oven to 400°F (200°C). Carefully cut the butternut squash in half lengthwise and scoop out the seeds. Place the squash halves on a baking sheet, drizzle with olive oil, and season with salt and pepper. Roast in the oven for about 45 minutes, or until the squash is tender and caramelized.

Step 2: Sauté the Aromatics

While the butternut squash is roasting, heat a large pot over medium heat. Add a splash of olive oil, then sauté the chopped onion and minced garlic until soft and fragrant. This will create a flavorful base for your soup.

Step 3: Simmer the Soup

Once the butternut squash is roasted and tender, scoop out the flesh and add it to the pot with the sautéed onions and garlic. Add the chopped apples, ground cinnamon, nutmeg, and ginger, then pour in the vegetable or chicken broth. Bring the mixture to a simmer and let it cook for about 20-25 minutes, or until the apples are soft and the flavors have melded together beautifully.

Step 4: Blend to Perfection

Using a blender or an immersion blender, carefully puree the soup until it is smooth and velvety. If you prefer a creamier texture, you can add half a cup of heavy cream at this stage and blend it in for extra richness.

Step 5: Season and Serve

Taste the soup and adjust the seasoning with salt and pepper as needed. Ladle the soup into bowls and garnish with a drizzle of cream, a sprinkle of freshly ground black pepper, and a few toasted pumpkin seeds for a delightful crunch.

Tips for Serving

This butternut squash and apple soup pairs beautifully with a crusty loaf of bread or a simple green salad for a light and satisfying meal. You can also elevate it with a dollop of crème fraîche or a sprinkle of freshly chopped herbs for an extra touch of elegance.

Final Thoughts

With its warm, inviting flavors and luxurious texture, this butternut squash and apple soup is the perfect addition to your fall recipe repertoire. Whether you’re hosting a gathering or simply craving a comforting meal on a chilly evening, this velvety delight is sure to impress. So, embrace the flavors of the season and savor every spoonful of this soul-warming soup. Cheers to fall and the delicious dishes it brings!
