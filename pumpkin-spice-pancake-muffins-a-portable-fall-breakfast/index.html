Pumpkin Spice Pancake Muffins: A Portable Fall Breakfast


There’s something magical about the arrival of fall – the crisp air, the vibrant leaves, and of course, the delicious flavors that come with the season. One of the most beloved fall flavors is pumpkin spice, and what better way to enjoy it than in a portable and delectable breakfast treat? Introducing Pumpkin Spice Pancake Muffins – a delightful twist on traditional pancakes that you can enjoy on the go. These muffins are not only convenient but also bursting with warm, comforting flavors that capture the essence of autumn.

Ingredients

For the Pancake Muffins:

  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground nutmeg
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground ginger
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground cloves
  • 1 cup pumpkin puree
  • 1/2 cup buttermilk
  • 1/4 cup unsalted butter, melted
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

For the Cinnamon Sugar Topping:

  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 2 tablespoons unsalted butter, melted

Instructions

  1. Preheat and Prep: Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease a 12-cup muffin tin or line with paper liners.

  2. Combine Dry Ingredients: In a large bowl, whisk together the flour, sugar, baking powder, baking soda, salt, cinnamon, nutmeg, ginger, and cloves.

  3. Mix Wet Ingredients: In another bowl, whisk together the pumpkin puree, buttermilk, melted butter, eggs, and vanilla extract until well combined.

  4. Combine and Fold: Pour the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients and gently fold together until just combined. Be careful not to overmix, as this can result in dense muffins.

  5. Fill Muffin Cups: Divide the batter evenly among the prepared muffin cups, filling each about 3/4 full.

  6. Bake: Bake for 18-20 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center of a muffin comes out clean.

  7. Prepare Cinnamon Sugar Topping: In a small bowl, mix together the granulated sugar and ground cinnamon for the topping.

  8. Coat with Cinnamon Sugar: While the muffins are still warm, brush the tops with melted butter and sprinkle the cinnamon sugar mixture over each muffin.

  9. Serve and Enjoy: Allow the muffins to cool for a few minutes before serving. These Pumpkin Spice Pancake Muffins are best enjoyed warm and paired with a hot cup of coffee or tea.

Why Pumpkin Spice Pancake Muffins?

These delightful muffins offer the comforting flavors of pumpkin spice in a convenient, portable form. Whether you’re heading to work, going for a fall hike, or simply enjoying a lazy weekend morning, these muffins are the perfect grab-and-go breakfast option. The combination of pumpkin, warm spices, and a hint of sweetness makes them a delightful treat for the entire family.

Embracing the Fall Spirit

As the leaves change and the air becomes crisper, there’s no better way to embrace the spirit of fall than by indulging in its signature flavors. Pumpkin Spice Pancake Muffins capture the essence of the season and bring a sense of warmth and comfort to your breakfast routine. The aroma of cinnamon, nutmeg, and cloves will fill your kitchen, creating an inviting atmosphere that welcomes the joys of autumn.

Conclusion

Pumpkin Spice Pancake Muffins are a delightful departure from traditional pancakes, offering the same beloved flavors in a convenient and portable form. Whether you’re a pumpkin spice enthusiast or simply looking for a delicious fall-inspired breakfast, these muffins are sure to become a seasonal favorite. Embrace the flavors of fall and savor the joys of the season with these delightful Pumpkin Spice Pancake Muffins.
