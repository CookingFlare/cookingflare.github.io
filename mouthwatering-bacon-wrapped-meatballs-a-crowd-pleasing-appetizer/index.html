Mouthwatering Bacon-Wrapped Meatballs: A Crowd-Pleasing Appetizer


When it comes to appetizers, there’s nothing quite like the combination of crispy bacon and juicy meatballs. The irresistible aroma, the savory flavors, and the satisfying crunch make bacon-wrapped meatballs a crowd-pleasing favorite at any gathering. Whether you’re hosting a party, attending a potluck, or simply looking for a delicious and easy-to-make appetizer, this recipe is sure to impress your guests.

Ingredients

To make these mouthwatering bacon-wrapped meatballs, you’ll need the following ingredients:

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1/2 cup breadcrumbs
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/4 cup chopped parsley
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1 egg, beaten
  • 16 bacon slices

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C) and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.

  2. In a large mixing bowl, combine the ground beef, breadcrumbs, Parmesan cheese, parsley, minced garlic, dried oregano, salt, black pepper, and beaten egg. Mix well until all the ingredients are evenly incorporated.

  3. Take a small portion of the meat mixture and roll it into a bite-sized meatball, about 1 inch in diameter. Repeat this step until all the meat mixture is used, making approximately 16 meatballs.

  4. Take a slice of bacon and wrap it around one meatball, securing it with a toothpick. Place the bacon-wrapped meatball onto the prepared baking sheet. Repeat this step for the remaining meatballs.

  5. Bake the bacon-wrapped meatballs in the preheated oven for 20-25 minutes, or until the bacon is crispy and the meatballs are cooked through.

  6. Once cooked, remove the toothpicks from the meatballs and transfer them to a serving platter.

  7. Serve the mouthwatering bacon-wrapped meatballs hot, alongside your favorite dipping sauce like marinara, barbecue, or sweet chili sauce.

Tips and Variations

  • For a smoky twist, try using smoked bacon instead of regular bacon. This will add an extra layer of flavor to your meatballs.

  • If you prefer a spicier kick, you can add some finely chopped jalapenos or a dash of cayenne pepper to the meat mixture.

  • Experiment with different types of ground meat, such as turkey, chicken, or a combination of beef and pork, to create your own unique flavor profile.

  • To save time, you can prepare the meatballs ahead of time and refrigerate them until you’re ready to wrap them with bacon and bake.

  • If you’re serving these bacon-wrapped meatballs at a party, consider making a double batch because they’ll disappear quickly!

Conclusion

Bacon-wrapped meatballs are a winning appetizer that will leave your guests craving for more. The combination of juicy meatballs and crispy bacon is simply irresistible. This recipe is not only easy to make but also versatile, allowing you to customize it to your taste preferences. Whether you’re hosting a gathering or simply looking for a delicious snack, these mouthwatering bacon-wrapped meatballs will surely be a crowd-pleasing hit!
