Thai Green Curry with Vegetables: A Healthy and Delicious Choice


Thai cuisine is known for its vibrant flavors and aromatic spices, and one dish that captures the essence of Thai cooking is the Green Curry. This delicious and healthy dish is a perfect combination of fresh vegetables, fragrant herbs, and creamy coconut milk. In this recipe, we will explore how to make a flavorful Thai Green Curry with Vegetables that is sure to impress your taste buds.

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • 2 tablespoons Thai green curry paste
  • 1 can (400 ml) coconut milk
  • 1 cup vegetable broth
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon brown sugar
  • 1 cup broccoli florets
  • 1 cup sliced bell peppers
  • 1 cup sliced carrots
  • 1 cup sliced mushrooms
  • 1 cup snap peas
  • 1 cup baby corn
  • Fresh basil leaves, for garnish

Instructions

  1. Heat the vegetable oil in a large pan over medium heat. Add the green curry paste and stir-fry for a minute, until fragrant.

  2. Pour in the coconut milk and vegetable broth, and stir well to combine. Bring the mixture to a simmer.

  3. Add the soy sauce and brown sugar to the pan, and stir until the sugar has dissolved.

  4. Add the broccoli, bell peppers, carrots, mushrooms, snap peas, and baby corn to the pan. Stir well to coat the vegetables in the curry sauce.

  5. Cover the pan and let the vegetables cook for about 10 minutes, or until they are tender-crisp.

  6. Remove the lid and let the curry simmer for another 5 minutes, or until the sauce has thickened slightly.

  7. Taste the curry and adjust the seasoning if needed. You can add more soy sauce or sugar to balance the flavors according to your preference.

  8. Serve the Thai Green Curry with Vegetables hot, garnished with fresh basil leaves. It pairs perfectly with steamed jasmine rice or noodles.

Health Benefits

Not only is this Thai Green Curry delicious, but it is also packed with nutrients from the variety of vegetables used. Broccoli is rich in vitamins C and K, bell peppers are a great source of vitamin C and antioxidants, carrots are loaded with vitamin A, and mushrooms provide essential minerals like selenium and potassium. The coconut milk used in the curry adds a creamy texture and is a good source of healthy fats.

Moreover, the Thai green curry paste contains herbs and spices like lemongrass, galangal, and kaffir lime leaves, which not only add incredible flavor but also offer various health benefits. Lemongrass is known for its anti-inflammatory properties, galangal aids digestion, and kaffir lime leaves are rich in antioxidants.

By using a variety of vegetables and incorporating these beneficial ingredients, Thai Green Curry with Vegetables becomes a wholesome and nutritious choice for a satisfying meal.

Conclusion

Thai Green Curry with Vegetables is a fantastic dish to add to your cooking repertoire. It’s not only easy to make but also bursting with flavors that will transport you to the vibrant streets of Thailand. This recipe allows you to customize the vegetables according to your preference, making it versatile and adaptable to what you have on hand.

So, the next time you’re craving a healthy and delicious meal, give this Thai Green Curry with Vegetables recipe a try. Your taste buds will thank you, and your body will benefit from the array of nutrients packed into this delightful dish. Enjoy!
