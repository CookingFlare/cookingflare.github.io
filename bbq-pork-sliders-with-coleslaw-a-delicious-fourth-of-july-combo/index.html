BBQ Pork Sliders with Coleslaw: A Delicious Fourth of July Combo


Summer is here, and so is the perfect excuse to fire up the grill and get ready for a star-spangled celebration! As the Fourth of July approaches, you’re probably thinking about what delicious dishes to serve to your family and friends. Look no further, because today we’re going to share a mouth-watering recipe that’s sure to become a new favorite: BBQ Pork Sliders with Coleslaw.

The Secret to Tender and Juicy Pork Sliders

To make the best BBQ Pork Sliders, you’ll need to start with the right cut of meat. We recommend using pork shoulder or butt, which is rich in flavor and tender when cooked low and slow. Once you’ve got your pork, you’ll need to season it with a blend of spices and herbs that will give it a deep, smoky flavor. We like to use a combination of paprika, garlic powder, salt, and black pepper to get the job done.

Next, you’ll need to cook the pork. You can do this by slow-cooking it in a crockpot or by grilling it over low heat for several hours. Either way, the key is to cook it until it’s tender and falls apart easily. Once it’s cooked, you can shred it with two forks and set it aside for later.

The Magic of BBQ Sauce

Now it’s time to talk about the sauce. We’re not talking about any ordinary BBQ sauce here – we’re talking about a tangy, sweet, and smoky sauce that will take your pork sliders to the next level. To make it, you’ll need to combine ketchup, brown sugar, apple cider vinegar, Worcestershire sauce, and a little bit of liquid smoke. Yes, you read that right – liquid smoke is the secret ingredient that will give your sauce a deep, smoky flavor.

Assembling the Sliders

Now that you’ve got your pork and sauce, it’s time to assemble the sliders. Simply place a small amount of pork onto a bun, followed by a spoonful of BBQ sauce, a few slices of red onion, and a sprinkle of cilantro. Top it all off with a dollop of coleslaw, and you’ve got a slider that’s sure to impress.

The Perfect Coleslaw Recipe

Speaking of coleslaw, we’ve got a recipe that’s sure to become a new favorite. To make it, you’ll need to combine shredded cabbage, mayonnaise, apple cider vinegar, sugar, salt, and a pinch of black pepper. Yes, it’s that simple – but trust us, the results are well worth the effort. The key is to use a combination of green and red cabbage, which will give your slaw a beautiful color and a tangy flavor.

Tips and Variations

Before we wrap things up, we wanted to give you a few tips and variations to try. First, if you want to add a little extra flavor to your sliders, try using different types of cheese, such as cheddar or American. You could also add some diced bell peppers or jalapeños for an extra kick of flavor. And if you want to make your coleslaw a little more interesting, try adding some diced apples or carrots.

Conclusion

And there you have it – a delicious Fourth of July combo that’s sure to become a new favorite. With its tender and juicy pork, tangy BBQ sauce, and creamy coleslaw, this recipe is the perfect way to celebrate the summer season. So fire up the grill, gather your friends and family, and get ready to enjoy a star-spangled celebration that you’ll never forget. Happy cooking!
