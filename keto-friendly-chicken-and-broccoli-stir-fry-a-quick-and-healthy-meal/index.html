Keto-Friendly Chicken and Broccoli Stir-Fry: A Quick and Healthy Meal


Are you looking for a delicious and healthy meal that fits perfectly into your keto diet? Look no further than this mouthwatering Keto-Friendly Chicken and Broccoli Stir-Fry recipe! Packed with flavor and essential nutrients, this dish is not only easy to make but also incredibly satisfying. So, let’s dive right into the recipe!

Ingredients

  • 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts, thinly sliced
  • 2 cups broccoli florets
  • 1 red bell pepper, thinly sliced
  • 1 small onion, thinly sliced
  • 3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce (or tamari for gluten-free option)
  • 1 tablespoon sesame oil
  • 1 tablespoon rice vinegar
  • 1 teaspoon ginger, grated
  • 1/2 teaspoon red pepper flakes (optional for some heat)
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. In a large skillet or wok, heat the olive oil over medium-high heat. Add the chicken slices and cook until they are no longer pink in the center. Remove the chicken from the skillet and set it aside.

  2. In the same skillet, add the minced garlic, grated ginger, and red pepper flakes (if using). Sauté for about a minute until fragrant.

  3. Add the broccoli florets, sliced bell pepper, and onion to the skillet. Stir-fry for about 3-4 minutes, or until the vegetables are tender-crisp.

  4. Meanwhile, in a small bowl, whisk together the soy sauce, sesame oil, and rice vinegar. Pour the sauce over the cooked vegetables and stir well to combine.

  5. Return the cooked chicken to the skillet and toss everything together until the chicken and vegetables are evenly coated with the sauce. Cook for an additional 1-2 minutes to heat everything through.

  6. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Be mindful of the amount of salt you add, as the soy sauce already contains sodium.

  7. Serve the Keto-Friendly Chicken and Broccoli Stir-Fry hot and enjoy!

Nutritional Information

This Keto-Friendly Chicken and Broccoli Stir-Fry recipe serves 2 people. Here’s the approximate nutritional information per serving:

  • Calories: 350
  • Fat: 18g
  • Carbohydrates: 10g
  • Fiber: 4g
  • Protein: 35g

Please note that these values may vary depending on the specific ingredients and brands you use.

Final Thoughts

This Keto-Friendly Chicken and Broccoli Stir-Fry recipe is a perfect option for those following a keto diet or anyone looking for a quick and healthy meal. Not only is it packed with essential nutrients, but it’s also bursting with flavor. The combination of tender chicken, crisp broccoli, and vibrant bell pepper will leave your taste buds satisfied.

Feel free to customize this recipe to your liking by adding other low-carb vegetables like mushrooms or zucchini. You can also experiment with different seasonings or spices to give it your own unique twist. The possibilities are endless!

Remember, eating healthy doesn’t mean sacrificing taste. With this Keto-Friendly Chicken and Broccoli Stir-Fry recipe, you can enjoy a delicious meal that supports your keto lifestyle. So, why wait? Head to your kitchen and whip up this delightful dish tonight!
