Hearty Bacon and Lentil Soup: Winter Comfort with a Twist


As the winter chill settles in, there’s nothing quite as comforting as a steaming bowl of hearty soup. This recipe for Hearty Bacon and Lentil Soup is a delightful twist on the classic lentil soup, infusing it with the smoky richness of bacon and the earthy flavors of lentils. It’s a perfect balance of wholesome ingredients and bold flavors, making it a winter favorite for many.

Ingredients

  • 6 slices of bacon, chopped
  • 1 large onion, finely chopped
  • 3 carrots, diced
  • 3 celery stalks, diced
  • 3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon of dried thyme
  • 1 teaspoon of smoked paprika
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 1 cup of green or brown lentils, rinsed
  • 6 cups of chicken or vegetable broth
  • 1 can (14 oz) of diced tomatoes
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley, for garnish

Instructions

  1. In a large pot or Dutch oven, cook the chopped bacon over medium heat until it’s golden and crispy. Use a slotted spoon to transfer the bacon to a plate lined with paper towels, leaving the bacon fat in the pot.

  2. Add the chopped onion, carrots, and celery to the pot, and cook them in the bacon fat until they start to soften, about 5 minutes. Stir in the minced garlic, dried thyme, and smoked paprika, and cook for another minute, allowing the flavors to meld together.

  3. Add the rinsed lentils, bay leaf, chicken or vegetable broth, and diced tomatoes to the pot. Bring the mixture to a boil, then reduce the heat and let it simmer for about 25-30 minutes, or until the lentils are tender.

  4. Once the lentils are cooked through, remove the bay leaf from the pot and discard it. Using an immersion blender, partially blend the soup to create a creamy texture while still leaving some whole lentils and vegetables for texture. Alternatively, you can transfer a portion of the soup to a blender and blend until smooth, then return it to the pot.

  5. Season the soup with salt and pepper to taste. If the soup is too thick, you can add more broth to reach your desired consistency.

  6. Ladle the Hearty Bacon and Lentil Soup into bowls, and garnish with the crispy bacon pieces and fresh parsley.

The Twist

What sets this Hearty Bacon and Lentil Soup apart is the smoky, savory flavor that the bacon infuses into every spoonful. The bacon adds a rich depth to the soup, complementing the earthy lentils and sweet vegetables. This hearty and wholesome soup is a satisfying meal on its own, perfect for warming up on a cold winter day.

Nutritional Benefits

Lentils are a fantastic source of plant-based protein and fiber, making this soup not only delicious but also nutritious. The addition of vegetables like carrots and celery provides essential vitamins and minerals, while the bacon contributes a touch of indulgent flavor.

Serving Suggestions

Serve this Hearty Bacon and Lentil Soup with a slice of crusty bread or a simple green salad for a complete and satisfying meal. The leftovers can be stored in the refrigerator for a few days, allowing the flavors to meld even further.

Conclusion

This Hearty Bacon and Lentil Soup is a comforting and flavorful twist on a classic winter soup. It’s a versatile dish that can be enjoyed as a nourishing lunch or a cozy dinner, bringing warmth and satisfaction with every spoonful. Give this recipe a try and experience the delightful combination of smoky bacon and wholesome lentils in every comforting bowl.
