Savory and Spicy Cajun Shrimp and Sausage Pasta Jambalaya


If you’re a fan of bold flavors and hearty meals, then you’re in for a treat with this savory and spicy Cajun Shrimp and Sausage Pasta Jambalaya recipe. This dish is a fusion of Cajun and Creole cuisines, offering a delightful blend of spices, protein, and pasta that will tantalize your taste buds.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound large shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 1/2 pound andouille sausage, sliced
  • 1 red bell pepper, diced
  • 1 green bell pepper, diced
  • 1 yellow onion, diced
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can (14.5 oz) diced tomatoes
  • 3 cups chicken broth
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 12 oz fettuccine pasta
  • 2 tablespoons Cajun seasoning
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley for garnish

Instructions

  1. In a large pot or Dutch oven, heat some oil over medium-high heat. Add the sliced andouille sausage and cook until browned. Remove the sausage from the pot and set it aside.

  2. In the same pot, add the diced onions and bell peppers. Sauté until they begin to soften, then add the minced garlic and continue cooking for another minute.

  3. Add the diced tomatoes (with their juices) to the pot and stir to combine with the vegetables. Allow the mixture to simmer for a few minutes, allowing the flavors to meld together.

  4. Stir in the Cajun seasoning, paprika, and dried thyme, ensuring that the spices coat the vegetables evenly.

  5. Pour in the chicken broth and heavy cream, then bring the mixture to a gentle boil. Add the fettuccine pasta, making sure it’s submerged in the liquid, and cook according to the package instructions until al dente.

  6. Once the pasta is almost cooked, return the andouille sausage to the pot, along with the peeled and deveined shrimp. Allow the shrimp to cook in the liquid for a few minutes until they turn pink and opaque.

  7. Season the jambalaya with salt and black pepper to taste. If the sauce is too thin, allow it to simmer for a few more minutes to thicken slightly.

  8. Garnish the Cajun Shrimp and Sausage Pasta Jambalaya with freshly chopped parsley before serving.

Serving Suggestions

This delectable dish is best enjoyed hot, straight from the pot. Serve the Cajun Shrimp and Sausage Pasta Jambalaya in deep plates or bowls, allowing your guests to dig into the flavorful combination of shrimp, sausage, and pasta. Pair it with a side of crusty French bread or a simple green salad to complete the meal.

Conclusion

With its rich and robust flavors, this Savory and Spicy Cajun Shrimp and Sausage Pasta Jambalaya is sure to become a favorite in your recipe collection. The combination of tender shrimp, smoky andouille sausage, and perfectly cooked pasta, all infused with the warmth of Cajun spices, makes for a truly satisfying dining experience. Whether you’re hosting a dinner party or simply craving a comforting meal, this jambalaya is a fantastic choice that will impress your guests and leave everyone wanting more.

So, gather your ingredients and get ready to embark on a culinary adventure with this delightful Cajun-inspired recipe. Enjoy the bold and spicy flavors of the bayou right in your own home!
