Flatbread Desserts: Sweet Treats Beyond the Savory


Have you ever considered using flatbread as a base for your desserts? While commonly associated with savory dishes, flatbread can be transformed into delicious and unique sweet treats that will leave your taste buds craving for more. In this blog post, we will explore the world of flatbread desserts and share some mouthwatering recipes that will surely impress your family and friends.

Apple Cinnamon Flatbread Roll-Ups

Ingredients:

  • 4 pieces of flatbread
  • 2 apples, thinly sliced
  • 2 tablespoons of brown sugar
  • 1 teaspoon of cinnamon
  • 2 tablespoons of melted butter
  • Powdered sugar (for dusting)

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. Lay the flatbread pieces on a clean surface.
  3. In a small bowl, mix together the brown sugar and cinnamon.
  4. Brush each flatbread with melted butter, then sprinkle the cinnamon sugar mixture evenly over the surface.
  5. Place the thinly sliced apples on top of the flatbread, making sure to cover the entire surface.
  6. Roll up each flatbread tightly and place them on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper.
  7. Bake for about 15-20 minutes, or until the flatbread is golden brown and the apples are tender.
  8. Remove from the oven and let them cool for a few minutes.
  9. Sprinkle powdered sugar on top for an extra touch of sweetness.
  10. Serve warm and enjoy!

Strawberry Nutella Flatbread Pizza

Ingredients:

  • 1 flatbread
  • 2 tablespoons of Nutella
  • 1 cup of fresh strawberries, sliced
  • 2 tablespoons of sliced almonds
  • Honey (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Place the flatbread on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper.
  3. Spread the Nutella evenly over the flatbread, leaving a small border around the edges.
  4. Arrange the sliced strawberries on top of the Nutella.
  5. Sprinkle the sliced almonds over the strawberries.
  6. Drizzle honey on top if desired, for an extra touch of sweetness.
  7. Bake for about 10-12 minutes, or until the flatbread is crispy and the strawberries are slightly softened.
  8. Remove from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes.
  9. Slice into wedges and serve.

Banana and Chocolate Flatbread Wrap

Ingredients:

  • 1 flatbread
  • 1 ripe banana, sliced
  • 2 tablespoons of chocolate hazelnut spread
  • 2 tablespoons of chopped walnuts
  • Whipped cream (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Lay the flatbread on a clean surface.
  2. Spread the chocolate hazelnut spread evenly over the flatbread.
  3. Arrange the sliced bananas on top of the spread.
  4. Sprinkle the chopped walnuts over the bananas.
  5. If desired, add a dollop of whipped cream for extra indulgence.
  6. Roll up the flatbread tightly and secure with toothpicks if needed.
  7. Slice into smaller portions for easier serving.
  8. Enjoy this delightful treat!

Conclusion

Who would have thought that flatbread could be transformed into such delectable desserts? From Apple Cinnamon Flatbread Roll-Ups to Strawberry Nutella Flatbread Pizza and Banana and Chocolate Flatbread Wrap, there are endless possibilities to explore. These recipes are just the beginning, so don’t be afraid to get creative and experiment with your own combinations. So, the next time you’re craving something sweet, think outside the box and give flatbread desserts a try. Your taste buds will thank you!
