Instant Pot Beef Stew: Hearty and Delicious


Is there anything more comforting than a warm bowl of beef stew on a chilly evening? The tender chunks of beef, the rich and flavorful broth, and the hearty vegetables all come together to create a dish that is not only delicious but also incredibly satisfying. And with the help of an Instant Pot, you can have this hearty meal ready in no time!

Ingredients

  • 2 pounds beef stew meat, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 4 carrots, peeled and sliced
  • 4 potatoes, peeled and diced
  • 2 stalks celery, sliced
  • 4 cups beef broth
  • 1 cup red wine (optional)
  • 2 tablespoons tomato paste
  • 1 tablespoon Worcestershire sauce
  • 2 bay leaves
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • Salt and pepper, to taste

Instructions

  1. Set your Instant Pot to sauté mode and heat the olive oil. Add the beef stew meat and brown it on all sides. This step will help seal in the juices and add extra flavor to the stew.

  2. Once the beef is browned, add the diced onion and minced garlic. Sauté for a few minutes until the onion becomes translucent and the garlic becomes fragrant.

  3. Add the carrots, potatoes, and celery to the Instant Pot. Stir everything together to combine the flavors.

  4. In a separate bowl, whisk together the beef broth, red wine (if using), tomato paste, Worcestershire sauce, bay leaves, dried thyme, salt, and pepper. Pour this mixture into the Instant Pot, making sure to scrape up any browned bits from the bottom of the pot.

  5. Secure the lid on the Instant Pot and set it to high pressure for 35 minutes. Allow the pressure to release naturally for 10 minutes, then manually release any remaining pressure.

  6. Carefully remove the lid and give the stew a good stir. The beef should be tender and the vegetables should be cooked through.

  7. Taste the stew and adjust the seasoning if needed. You can add more salt, pepper, or herbs to suit your taste.

  8. Serve the Instant Pot beef stew hot, garnished with fresh parsley if desired. It pairs perfectly with crusty bread or fluffy mashed potatoes.

Tips and Variations

  • For an extra kick of flavor, try adding a splash of balsamic vinegar or a dash of hot sauce to the stew.
  • If you prefer a thicker stew, you can mix a tablespoon of cornstarch with a little water and stir it into the stew. Allow the stew to simmer for a few minutes until it thickens.
  • Feel free to customize this recipe by adding your favorite vegetables. Mushrooms, peas, or green beans would all be great additions.
  • Leftovers can be stored in an airtight container in the refrigerator for up to 3 days. The flavors will continue to develop, making the stew even more delicious the next day.

Enjoy this Instant Pot beef stew on a cozy evening, and let its rich flavors warm you from the inside out. With just a few simple ingredients and the convenience of the Instant Pot, you can create a hearty and delicious meal that will satisfy even the hungriest of appetites.
