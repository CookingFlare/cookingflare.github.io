Salad as the Star: Creative Recipes with Wholesome Ingredients


Salads are more than just a side dish, they can be the star of any meal when you get creative with flavors and textures. In today’s modern and health-conscious world, incorporating more greens and botanicals into our meals is not only beneficial but exciting as well. We’ve scoured the culinary landscape and developed a collection of cutting-edge recipes that will illuminate the true potential of a simple salad. Every recipe in this blog post highlights bold flavors and nutritious ingredients, making wholesome eating enjoyable and fun. So, let your creativity run wild while embracing these vibrant, scrumptious recipes.

  1. Spicy Peanut Tempeh Salad

The crisp crunch of fresh veggies marries exquisitely with succulent tempeh and the rich flavors of a zesty peanut dressing. Tempeh is a great source of plant-based protein, making this dish a perfect option for vegetarians and meat-eaters alike. You’ll be surprised at the delightful harmony of zingy lime, creamy peanut butter, and a cayenne kick.

Ingredients:

  • 200g tempeh, sliced
  • 1 tbsp tamari (or soy sauce)
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp maple syrup
  • 1 cucumber, sliced
  • 1 small red bell pepper, julienned
  • 1 small carrot, julienned
  • 1/2 cup shredded purple cabbage
  • 1/2 cup fresh cilantro, chopped
  • 1/2 cup fresh mint, chopped

Peanut Dressing:

  • 1/4 cup smooth peanut butter
  • 2 tbsp lime juice
  • 2 tbsp tamari (or soy sauce)
  • 1 tsp maple syrup
  • 1/2 tsp cayenne pepper
  • 3 tbsp warm water

Instructions:

  1. In a large bowl, mix tamari and maple syrup. Add the tempeh slices to marinate for at least 15 minutes.

  2. Heat a large non-stick skillet over medium heat with a tablespoon of olive oil. Cook the tempeh slices for 6-8 minutes, ensuring they gain a slight golden-brown color. Set aside.

  3. While the tempeh cooks, place the peanut dressing ingredients in a bowl and whisk to blend. Adjust seasoning to your taste. Set aside.

  4. Combine cucumber, red bell pepper, carrot, purple cabbage, cilantro, and mint in a large salad bowl.

  5. Toss the salad gently with most of the peanut dressing, reserving some to drizzle over the tempeh.

  6. Crumble the cooked tempeh over the salad, then drizzle the remaining dressing over the tempeh to achieve a delectable coating.

  7. Toss the combined salad gently, and enjoy the spicy peanut tempeh salad!

  8. Vibrant Quinoa and Avocado Salad with a Citrus Vinaigrette

This salad is an exquisite fusion of color, texture, and freshness. Each bite will transport your taste buds to a citrus-inspired garden party, with a medley of vibrant ingredients like quinoa, avocado, and blood oranges, all enveloped by a tangy citrus vinaigrette.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup quinoa
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 3/4 cup water
  • 1 avocado, diced
  • 1 blood orange, peeled and segmented
  • 1/2 cup corn kernels (fresh or frozen)
  • 1/2 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1/4 cup fresh basil, chopped
  • 2 tbsp pumpkin seeds

Citrus Vinaigrette:

  • 2 tbsp fresh orange juice
  • 1 tbsp fresh lemon juice
  • 1 tsp maple syrup
  • 1/2 tsp Dijon mustard
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1/2 cup olive oil

Instructions:

  1. In a medium saucepan, bring water and quinoa to a boil. Lower heat to simmer and cover the saucepan. Cook until all water is absorbed, approximately 18-20 minutes. Set aside to cool.

  2. While quinoa is cooling, prepare the citrus vinaigrette by combining orange juice, lemon juice, maple syrup, Dijon mustard, salt, and pepper in a small bowl. Slowly whisk in olive oil to blend, adjusting seasoning as desired.

  3. In a large mixing bowl combine cooked quinoa, avocado, blood orange segments, corn kernels, cherry tomatoes, basil, and pumpkin seeds.

  4. Lightly toss the salad with the citrus vinaigrette and let the magic of this vibrant, flavorful salad unfold!

  5. Crunchy Kale and Edamame Salad with Sesame Ginger Dressing

Kale and edamame come together in this power-packed salad to create an irresistible harmony of flavors and textures. The sesame ginger dressing adds a fragrant and zesty note, while the cashews provide an added crunch and heartiness.

Ingredients:

  • 5 cups kale, stemmed and chopped
  • 1 cup cooked edamame
  • 1 avocado, diced
  • 1/2 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1/4 cup cashews, chopped
  • 1/4 cup fresh cilantro, chopped

Sesame Ginger Dressing:

  • 2 tbsp sesame oil
  • 1 tbsp ginger, minced
  • 2 tbsp lime juice
  • 2 tsp tamari (or soy sauce)
  • 1 tsp maple syrup
  • 1/4 cup warm water

Instructions:

  1. In a large saucepan, cook edamame according to package instructions. Once cooked, remove from heat and let cool.
  2. Meanwhile, prepare the kale by chopping it into bite-sized pieces. Toss the kale with a dash of olive oil and some salt. Place the kale on a baking sheet and roast in a preheated oven at 350°F (175°C) for about 10 minutes or until slightly crispy and fragrant.
  3. While kale and edamame cool, prepare the sesame ginger dressing by whisking together sesame oil, ginger, lime juice, tamari, and maple syrup. Add warm water to thin the dressing and blend.
  4. In a large bowl, combine cooled kale, edamame, avocado, cherry tomatoes, cashews, and cilantro.
  5. Drizzle the sesame ginger dressing over the salad and gently toss to coat evenly, creating a delightful dance of flavors. Enjoy your crunchy kale and edamame salad!

So, gather your ingredients, grab your apron, and let your creative side shine in the kitchen!
