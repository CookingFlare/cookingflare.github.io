Sous Vide Chicken Wings: Finger-Licking Goodness


Who doesn’t love a plate of crispy, tender, and juicy chicken wings? They are the ultimate game-day snack or a delicious appetizer to share with friends. Today, I’m going to share with you a foolproof method to make the most flavorful and succulent chicken wings using the sous vide technique. Get ready to indulge in finger-licking goodness!

Ingredients

  • 2 pounds of chicken wings
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons honey
  • 2 tablespoons sriracha sauce
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon ginger, grated
  • 1 tablespoon sesame oil
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • Optional garnish: chopped green onions and sesame seeds

Instructions

  1. Set your sous vide water bath to 165°F (74°C). This temperature will ensure perfectly cooked chicken wings with a tender texture.

  2. In a bowl, whisk together the soy sauce, honey, sriracha sauce, minced garlic, grated ginger, sesame oil, salt, and pepper. This flavorful marinade will infuse the chicken wings with a mouthwatering taste.

  3. Place the chicken wings in a resealable bag or vacuum-sealed pouch. Pour the marinade over the wings, making sure they’re well-coated. Massage the marinade into the wings to ensure every piece is covered.

  4. Seal the bag using the water displacement method or a vacuum sealer. This will prevent any water from entering the bag during the sous vide cooking process.

  5. Submerge the bag of chicken wings into the preheated water bath and cook for 1.5 to 2 hours. The extended cooking time allows the wings to become tender while maintaining their juiciness.

  6. Once the cooking time is up, remove the bag from the water bath. Carefully open the bag and transfer the wings to a baking sheet lined with parchment paper.

  7. Preheat your broiler on high heat. Place the baking sheet with the wings under the broiler for 3-4 minutes per side, or until they develop a crispy and golden-brown exterior.

  8. Keep a close eye on the wings while broiling to avoid burning. The high heat will help achieve that irresistible crispy texture while retaining the juiciness from the sous vide cooking.

  9. Once the wings are nicely browned, remove them from the broiler and let them rest for a few minutes. This allows the juices to redistribute and ensures maximum flavor in every bite.

  10. Serve the sous vide chicken wings hot and garnish with chopped green onions and sesame seeds, if desired. These wings are perfect on their own, but you can also serve them with your favorite dipping sauce or alongside a refreshing salad.

Conclusion

Using the sous vide technique for chicken wings takes your game-day snack to a whole new level. The low and slow cooking method ensures that the wings are cooked to perfection, with a juicy interior and a crispy exterior. The marinade adds a delightful blend of sweet, spicy, and savory flavors that will have you coming back for more.

So, the next time you’re craving some finger-licking goodness, give this sous vide chicken wings recipe a try. Your taste buds will thank you, and your guests will be impressed by your culinary skills. Enjoy!
