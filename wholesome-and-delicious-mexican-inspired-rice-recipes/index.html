Wholesome and Delicious Mexican-Inspired Rice Recipes


If you’re a fan of Mexican cuisine, you’ll know that rice is a staple ingredient in many traditional dishes. From flavorful Arroz Rojo to creamy Arroz Verde, Mexican-inspired rice recipes are not only delicious but also versatile and wholesome. In this post, we’ll explore some mouthwatering Mexican-inspired rice recipes that are easy to prepare and perfect for sharing with family and friends.

Arroz Rojo (Mexican Red Rice)

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups long-grain white rice
  • 1 can (14 oz) diced tomatoes
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 cups chicken or vegetable broth
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh cilantro for garnish

Instructions:

  1. In a large skillet, heat a small amount of oil over medium heat. Add the rice and cook, stirring frequently, until it turns golden brown.
  2. Add the diced tomatoes, onion, and garlic to the skillet, stirring to combine with the rice.
  3. Pour in the chicken or vegetable broth, then season with cumin, paprika, salt, and pepper. Stir well.
  4. Bring the mixture to a boil, then reduce the heat to low, cover, and simmer for 20-25 minutes, or until the rice is cooked through and the liquid is absorbed.
  5. Fluff the rice with a fork and garnish with fresh cilantro before serving.

Arroz Verde (Mexican Green Rice)

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups long-grain white rice
  • 1 cup packed fresh spinach leaves
  • 1/2 cup fresh cilantro leaves
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 3 cups chicken or vegetable broth
  • 1 small onion, chopped
  • Salt to taste
  • Lime wedges for serving

Instructions:

  1. In a blender, combine the spinach, cilantro, garlic, and 1 cup of the chicken or vegetable broth. Blend until smooth.
  2. In a large saucepan, heat a small amount of oil over medium heat. Add the rice and cook, stirring frequently, until it turns golden brown.
  3. Pour the green mixture from the blender into the saucepan, along with the chopped onion, and stir to combine with the rice.
  4. Add the remaining 2 cups of chicken or vegetable broth and season with salt. Stir well.
  5. Bring the mixture to a boil, then reduce the heat to low, cover, and simmer for 20-25 minutes, or until the rice is cooked through and the liquid is absorbed.
  6. Serve the rice with lime wedges for squeezing over the top.

Mexican Rice Pudding (Arroz con Leche)

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup long-grain white rice
  • 4 cups whole milk
  • 1 cinnamon stick
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • Ground cinnamon for garnish

Instructions:

  1. In a medium saucepan, combine the rice, milk, and cinnamon stick. Bring to a boil over medium heat, then reduce the heat to low and simmer, stirring occasionally, for 20-25 minutes, or until the rice is tender and the mixture has thickened.
  2. Stir in the sugar and vanilla extract, then continue to simmer for an additional 10 minutes.
  3. Remove the cinnamon stick and discard.
  4. Transfer the rice pudding to serving dishes and sprinkle with ground cinnamon before serving.

These Mexican-inspired rice recipes are sure to add a burst of flavor to your dining table. Whether you’re preparing a festive feast or a cozy family dinner, these wholesome and delicious dishes are bound to be a hit. Enjoy the vibrant and aromatic flavors of Mexico with these delightful rice recipes!
