Classic Vanilla Marshmallow Cupcakes: Shockingly Fluffy


Are you craving something light, airy, and incredibly delicious? Look no further! Presenting the classic vanilla marshmallow cupcakes – a scrumptious treat that will delight your taste buds while taking you down memory lane. These cupcakes are the epitome of comfort food, stuffed with fluffy marshmallow cream and topped with a luscious vanilla buttercream frosting.

The Secret Ingredient: Marshmallow Fluff

To craft these shockingly fluffy delights, you’ll need a jar of everyone’s beloved Marshmallow Fluff. If you can’t find it in your local grocery store, fear not! Homemade marshmallow cream is equally delicious and simpler to make than you’d think. This addition creates a heavenly texture, marrying the moist, soft cake with the pillowy cream, taking your cupcakes to new heights.

Ingredients

To create these Classic Vanilla Marshmallow Cupcakes, gather the following ingredients:

For the Marshmallow Fluff (homemade alternative):

  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 3 tablespoons light corn syrup
  • 3/4 cup water
  • 1/4 teaspoon cream of tartar

For the Cupcakes:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 and 1/2 cups granulated sugar
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 4 large eggs
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup buttermilk
  • 1 tablespoon vanilla extract

For the Vanilla Buttercream Frosting:

  • 2 sticks unsalted butter, softened
  • 4 cups powdered sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon pure vanilla extract
  • Pinch of salt

Instructions

Homemade Marshmallow Fluff:

  1. In a heavy-bottomed saucepan over medium heat, combine granulated sugar, light corn syrup, and water. Stir occasionally to dissolve sugar.
  2. Once the sugar has dissolved, bring the mixture to a full rolling boil. Add cream of tartar, reduce heat to medium-low, and let it simmer for 8 minutes.
  3. Monitor the consistency of the mixture to ensure it thickens to a marshmallow-like consistency. Once it reaches the desired thickness, remove from heat and allow to cool. Your homemade marshmallow fluff is ready!

Classic Vanilla Cupcakes:

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C). Line cupcake tins with your favorite cupcake liners.
  2. In a medium bowl, whisk together all-purpose flour, granulated sugar, baking powder, baking soda, and salt.
  3. In another bowl, beat softened unsalted butter until creamy. Gradually add the eggs to the butter while continuously beating on low speed. Mix in the vanilla extract.
  4. With the mixer on low speed, alternate adding the dry flour mixture and the buttermilk to the butter mixture. Ensure you begin and end with the dry ingredients.
  5. Fill each cupcake liner about 2/3 full with the batter and bake for 18-20 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted in the center comes out clean.
  6. Allow the cupcakes to cool completely before proceeding to the frosting.

Vanilla Buttercream Frosting:

  1. Beat softened butter in a stand mixer until creamy. Gradually add powdered sugar, alternating with the vanilla extract and salt. Mix on medium-high speed until a light and fluffy frosting is formed.
  2. Fill a piping bag fitted with your preferred tip (preferably a large round or star tip) with the buttercream frosting.
  3. Pipe generous swirls of frosting on the cooled cupcakes, ensuring each is smothered in a healthy dollop of vanilla bliss.
  4. To take your cupcakes to the next level of marshmallow delight, create a well in the top of each cupcake and fill it with a dollop of homemade marshmallow fluff.

The Finishing Touches

Decorate your cupcakes with sprinkles, tiny marshmallows, or edible violet flowers and serve. You’ll be stunned by how these fluffy cupcakes dance on your tongue with sheer delight.

And there you have it – a recipe for Classic Vanilla Marshmallow Cupcakes that’ll create an enchanting delight never to be forgotten. Grab your oven mitts, pull up a chair, and let the fluffy magic begin!
