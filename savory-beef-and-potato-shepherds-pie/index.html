Savory Beef and Potato Shepherd's Pie


Welcome to our kitchen! Today, we have an absolute classic for you: Savory Beef and Potato Shepherd’s Pie. This hearty dish is perfect for those cold winter nights when you crave something comforting and satisfying. It’s a crowd-pleaser that will leave everyone at your table asking for seconds. So, without further ado, let’s dive into the recipe!

Ingredients

For the beef filling:

  • 1.5 pounds ground beef
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 2 carrots, peeled and diced
  • 1 cup frozen peas
  • 2 tablespoons tomato paste
  • 1 cup beef broth
  • 1 teaspoon Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • Salt and pepper to taste

For the mashed potato topping:

  • 2 pounds potatoes, peeled and cut into chunks
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 4 tablespoons butter
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).

  2. In a large skillet, brown the ground beef over medium heat. Once cooked, remove any excess fat and set the beef aside.

  3. In the same skillet, sauté the diced onion until translucent. Add the minced garlic and cook for an additional minute.

  4. Stir in the diced carrots and frozen peas, and cook for about 5 minutes until the vegetables start to soften.

  5. Add the tomato paste, beef broth, Worcestershire sauce, dried thyme, salt, and pepper to the skillet. Mix well and let it simmer for 10 minutes, allowing the flavors to meld together.

  6. While the beef filling is simmering, prepare the mashed potato topping. Place the peeled and chopped potatoes in a large pot of salted water. Bring it to a boil and cook until the potatoes are tender when pierced with a fork, about 15-20 minutes.

  7. Drain the cooked potatoes and return them to the pot. Add the milk, butter, salt, and pepper. Mash everything together until smooth and creamy.

  8. Pour the beef filling into a 9x13-inch baking dish, spreading it out evenly. Then, carefully spoon the mashed potatoes on top, smoothing it out with a spatula.

  9. Place the baking dish in the preheated oven and bake for 25-30 minutes, or until the top is golden brown and the filling is bubbly.

  10. Remove the Shepherd’s Pie from the oven and let it rest for a few minutes before serving. This allows the flavors to settle and makes it easier to dish out.

  11. Serve hot and enjoy the deliciousness of this classic Shepherd’s Pie!

Tips and Variations

  • Feel free to add other vegetables to the filling, such as corn or green beans, to customize the recipe to your liking.
  • For an extra flavor boost, you can sprinkle some grated cheese on top of the mashed potato layer before baking.
  • Leftovers can be stored in an airtight container in the refrigerator for up to 3 days. Simply reheat in the oven or microwave before serving.

That’s it! You’ve just learned how to make a delectable Savory Beef and Potato Shepherd’s Pie. We hope you enjoy making and devouring this comforting dish as much as we do. Remember, cooking is all about exploring flavors and making it your own. So, feel free to experiment and make this recipe uniquely yours. Happy cooking!
