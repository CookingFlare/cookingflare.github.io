Grilled Watermelon: Sweet and Smoky Recipes for a Refreshing BBQ


Summer is here, and what better way to celebrate than firing up the grill and enjoying some delicious BBQ? While burgers and hotdogs may be the go-to options, why not add a twist to your grilling game with a surprising and refreshing ingredient: watermelon!

Grilled watermelon may sound unusual, but trust me, it’s a game-changer. The heat of the grill caramelizes the sugars in the watermelon, giving it a delightful smoky flavor that perfectly balances its natural sweetness. Plus, the vibrant red color and juicy texture make it a visually stunning addition to any BBQ spread.

In this blog post, we’ll explore three mouthwatering recipes that showcase the versatility of grilled watermelon. From appetizers to desserts, these dishes will impress your guests and leave them craving for more.

Grilled Watermelon Salad with Feta and Mint

Ingredients:

  • 1 small watermelon, cut into thick slices
  • 1 cup crumbled feta cheese
  • Fresh mint leaves, torn
  • Balsamic glaze
  • Olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your grill to medium-high heat.
  2. Brush the watermelon slices with olive oil and season with salt and pepper.
  3. Place the watermelon slices on the grill and cook for about 2-3 minutes on each side, or until grill marks appear.
  4. Remove the watermelon from the grill and let it cool slightly.
  5. Cut the grilled watermelon into bite-sized cubes and transfer to a serving platter.
  6. Sprinkle the crumbled feta cheese and torn mint leaves over the watermelon.
  7. Drizzle with balsamic glaze and a drizzle of olive oil.
  8. Season with additional salt and pepper if desired.
  9. Toss gently to combine and serve immediately.

This grilled watermelon salad is a delightful combination of sweet and savory flavors. The smoky watermelon pairs perfectly with the tangy feta cheese and refreshing mint. The balsamic glaze adds a touch of acidity and sweetness, tying all the flavors together. Serve it as a side dish or a light summer lunch.

Grilled Watermelon Skewers with Prosciutto and Basil

Ingredients:

  • 1 small watermelon, cut into cubes
  • Thinly sliced prosciutto
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • Balsamic glaze
  • Wooden skewers, soaked in water for 30 minutes

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your grill to medium-high heat.
  2. Thread a cube of watermelon, a folded slice of prosciutto, and a basil leaf onto each skewer.
  3. Repeat the process until all the ingredients are used.
  4. Place the skewers on the grill and cook for about 2 minutes on each side, or until the prosciutto is crispy and the watermelon is slightly caramelized.
  5. Remove the skewers from the grill and let them cool slightly.
  6. Arrange the grilled watermelon skewers on a serving platter.
  7. Drizzle with balsamic glaze.
  8. Serve immediately and enjoy!

These grilled watermelon skewers are the perfect appetizer for any BBQ gathering. The combination of the smoky watermelon, salty prosciutto, and fragrant basil creates a burst of flavors in every bite. The balsamic glaze adds a touch of sweetness and acidity, elevating the dish to new heights.

Grilled Watermelon with Honey and Lime

Ingredients:

  • 1 small watermelon, cut into thick slices
  • Honey
  • Fresh lime juice
  • Fresh mint leaves, for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your grill to medium-high heat.
  2. Brush the watermelon slices with honey and sprinkle with fresh lime juice.
  3. Place the watermelon slices on the grill and cook for about 2-3 minutes on each side, or until grill marks appear.
  4. Remove the watermelon from the grill and let it cool slightly.
  5. Transfer the grilled watermelon slices to a serving platter.
  6. Drizzle with additional honey and lime juice.
  7. Garnish with fresh mint leaves.
  8. Serve immediately for a refreshing and satisfying dessert.

Grilling watermelon brings out its natural sweetness, and when paired with the tangy honey and zesty lime, it creates a delightful and refreshing dessert. The smoky flavor adds depth to the overall taste, making it a unique treat that will impress your guests.

So, the next time you fire up the grill, don’t forget to include some watermelon in your BBQ menu. These grilled watermelon recipes are sure to be a hit, offering a refreshing twist to your summer gatherings. Give them a try and let the flavors of sweet and smoky watermelon take your BBQ experience to a whole new level. Enjoy!
