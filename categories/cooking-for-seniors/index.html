Combine: Return the cooked tofu to the skillet, add the soy sauce, and mix everything together. Cook for an additional 2 minutes to heat through. Serve: Serve the stir-fry over cooked rice or noodles. Enjoy this colorful and nutritious meal! Benefits: This dish is packed with protein from the tofu and a variety of vitamins from the vegetables.
