10-Minute Greek Quinoa Salad Recipe


Looking for a quick and delicious salad recipe that’s packed with flavor and nutrients? Look no further than this 10-Minute Greek Quinoa Salad! This refreshing and satisfying dish is not only incredibly easy to make, but it also offers a delightful combination of Mediterranean flavors that will leave your taste buds craving for more. Whether you’re looking for a light lunch, a side dish, or a healthy snack, this recipe has got you covered.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup cooked quinoa
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1 cucumber, diced
  • 1/2 red onion, thinly sliced
  • 1/2 cup Kalamata olives, pitted and halved
  • 1/2 cup crumbled feta cheese
  • 2 tablespoons fresh lemon juice
  • 2 tablespoons extra-virgin olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • Fresh parsley, chopped (for garnish)

Instructions

  1. In a large bowl, combine the cooked quinoa, cherry tomatoes, cucumber, red onion, Kalamata olives, and crumbled feta cheese.

  2. In a small bowl, whisk together the fresh lemon juice, extra-virgin olive oil, dried oregano, salt, and pepper.

  3. Pour the dressing over the quinoa mixture and toss until everything is well coated.

  4. Garnish with fresh parsley.

  5. Serve immediately and enjoy!

Flavorful and Nutritious

This Greek Quinoa Salad is not only a feast for the eyes but also a nutritional powerhouse. Quinoa, known as the “superfood of the Incas,” is a complete protein, meaning it contains all nine essential amino acids. It’s also high in fiber, rich in vitamins and minerals, and gluten-free, making it an excellent option for those with dietary restrictions.

The vibrant colors of the cherry tomatoes, cucumber, and red onion not only make this salad visually appealing but also provide a variety of vitamins and antioxidants. The Kalamata olives add a tangy and salty flavor, while the crumbled feta cheese brings a creamy and savory element to the dish.

Time-Saving Tips

One of the best things about this recipe is how quick and easy it is to prepare. Here are a few time-saving tips to make the process even faster:

  • Cook the quinoa in advance and store it in the refrigerator. This way, when you’re ready to make the salad, you’ll just need to combine the ingredients and toss them together.

  • Use pre-sliced cherry tomatoes and pre-diced cucumber to save time on chopping.

  • If you’re short on time, skip the fresh lemon juice and use a store-bought Greek salad dressing instead.

Customization Options

Feel free to customize this recipe to suit your taste preferences and dietary needs. Here are a few suggestions:

  • Add some grilled chicken or shrimp for an extra boost of protein.

  • Swap the feta cheese for a vegan alternative or omit it altogether for a dairy-free option.

  • Experiment with different herbs and spices to add more depth of flavor. Fresh basil, mint, or dill would all work well in this salad.

Conclusion

With its vibrant colors, refreshing flavors, and quick preparation time, this 10-Minute Greek Quinoa Salad is a winner in every aspect. Whether you’re a busy professional looking for a nutritious lunch, a health-conscious individual seeking a satisfying snack, or just someone who appreciates delicious food, this recipe is sure to become a staple in your kitchen. So go ahead, give it a try, and let your taste buds embark on a Mediterranean adventure!
