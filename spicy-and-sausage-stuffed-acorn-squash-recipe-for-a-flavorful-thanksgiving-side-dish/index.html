Spicy and Sausage-Stuffed Acorn Squash Recipe for a Flavorful Thanksgiving Side Dish


As the leaves turn vibrant shades of red and gold and the air becomes crisp, it’s time to start planning for the most anticipated meal of the year – Thanksgiving. While the turkey may take center stage, the side dishes are what truly elevate the feast. If you’re looking to add a burst of flavor and warmth to your Thanksgiving table, look no further than this Spicy and Sausage-Stuffed Acorn Squash recipe.

Ingredients

  • 4 acorn squashes, halved and seeds removed
  • 1 lb spicy Italian sausage, casings removed
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 red bell pepper, diced
  • 1 teaspoon smoked paprika
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1/2 teaspoon red pepper flakes
  • 1 cup cooked quinoa
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley for garnish

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Place the acorn squash halves on a baking sheet, cut side down, and roast for 30 minutes, or until tender.
  3. In a large skillet over medium heat, cook the spicy Italian sausage, breaking it up with a spoon as it browns.
  4. Add the chopped onion, minced garlic, and diced red bell pepper to the skillet with the sausage. Cook until the vegetables are soft and the sausage is cooked through.
  5. Stir in the smoked paprika, dried thyme, dried oregano, and red pepper flakes, and cook for an additional 2 minutes to release the flavors.
  6. Remove the skillet from the heat and stir in the cooked quinoa and grated Parmesan cheese. Season the mixture with salt and pepper to taste.
  7. Once the acorn squash halves are tender, flip them over and fill each cavity with the sausage and quinoa mixture.
  8. Return the stuffed squash to the oven and bake for an additional 15-20 minutes, or until the filling is heated through and the tops are golden brown.
  9. Garnish with fresh parsley before serving.

Why this recipe is perfect for Thanksgiving

The Spicy and Sausage-Stuffed Acorn Squash is an ideal Thanksgiving side dish for several reasons. First, the combination of spicy Italian sausage and aromatic spices infuses the squash with a rich, savory flavor that perfectly complements the traditional Thanksgiving spread. The addition of quinoa adds a nutty texture and boosts the dish’s nutritional value, making it a versatile and modern addition to any holiday menu.

Furthermore, this dish is a feast for the eyes as well as the taste buds. The vibrant colors of the acorn squash and red bell pepper, combined with the golden-brown topping, create a visually stunning addition to the Thanksgiving table.

Finally, this recipe is incredibly versatile and can easily be adapted to suit various dietary preferences. For a vegetarian option, simply swap the spicy Italian sausage for a plant-based alternative and omit the Parmesan cheese for a dairy-free version.

Conclusion

Thanksgiving is a time for gathering, gratitude, and, of course, indulging in delicious food. This Spicy and Sausage-Stuffed Acorn Squash recipe is a guaranteed crowd-pleaser, bringing a touch of warmth and spice to your holiday feast. Whether you’re hosting a traditional Thanksgiving dinner or looking to add a unique dish to a potluck celebration, this recipe is sure to impress. So, this year, elevate your Thanksgiving table with this flavorful, comforting, and oh-so-satisfying side dish.
