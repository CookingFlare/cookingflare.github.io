Hearty Mediterranean Soups for Cozy Evenings


As the weather starts to cool and the evenings become longer, there’s nothing quite like a hearty Mediterranean soup to warm the soul and satisfy the senses. Mediterranean cuisine is known for its vibrant flavors, fresh ingredients, and heart-healthy benefits, making it the perfect choice for a cozy evening meal. Whether you’re looking for a comforting bowl of soup after a long day or planning a casual dinner with loved ones, these hearty Mediterranean soup recipes are sure to delight your taste buds and bring warmth to your home.

1. Tuscan White Bean Soup

Preparation time: 30 minutes Cooking time: 1 hour Serves: 6

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups dried white beans, soaked overnight
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 carrots, diced
  • 2 celery stalks, diced
  • 1 can diced tomatoes
  • 6 cups vegetable broth
  • 2 sprigs fresh rosemary
  • 1 bay leaf
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Extra virgin olive oil

Instructions:

  1. In a large pot, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the onion, garlic, carrots, and celery. Sauté until the vegetables are soft.
  2. Add the soaked white beans, diced tomatoes, rosemary, bay leaf, and vegetable broth to the pot. Bring to a boil, then reduce the heat and simmer for 1 hour, or until the beans are tender.
  3. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Remove the rosemary sprigs and bay leaf before serving.
  4. Ladle the soup into bowls and drizzle with a bit of extra virgin olive oil. Serve with crusty bread for a complete meal.

2. Greek Lemon Chicken Soup

Preparation time: 15 minutes Cooking time: 30 minutes Serves: 4

Ingredients:

  • 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 6 cups chicken broth
  • 1/2 cup orzo pasta
  • 3 eggs
  • Juice of 2 lemons
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh dill for garnish

Instructions:

  1. In a large pot, bring the chicken broth to a boil. Add the chicken breasts and cook until they are no longer pink in the center. Remove the chicken from the pot and shred it with two forks.
  2. Return the shredded chicken to the pot and add the orzo pasta. Cook until the pasta is al dente.
  3. In a bowl, whisk together the eggs and lemon juice until frothy. Slowly pour a ladleful of the hot broth into the egg mixture, whisking constantly.
  4. Pour the egg and lemon mixture back into the pot, stirring constantly. The soup will thicken slightly.
  5. Season with oregano, salt, and pepper. Ladle the soup into bowls and garnish with fresh dill.

3. Moroccan Spiced Lentil Soup

Preparation time: 20 minutes Cooking time: 45 minutes Serves: 8

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups red lentils
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 carrots, diced
  • 2 teaspoons ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon ground coriander
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 teaspoon cayenne pepper
  • 6 cups vegetable broth
  • 1 can diced tomatoes
  • Juice of 1 lemon
  • Fresh cilantro for garnish

Instructions:

  1. In a large pot, heat a bit of olive oil over medium heat. Add the onion, garlic, and carrots. Sauté until the vegetables are soft.
  2. Add the ground cumin, coriander, cinnamon, and cayenne pepper to the pot. Cook for 1-2 minutes, until fragrant.
  3. Add the red lentils, vegetable broth, and diced tomatoes to the pot. Bring to a boil, then reduce the heat and simmer for 30-40 minutes, or until the lentils are tender.
  4. Stir in the lemon juice and season with salt to taste. Ladle the soup into bowls and garnish with fresh cilantro.

These hearty Mediterranean soups are not only delicious but also nourishing, making them the perfect choice for cozy evenings with family and friends. Whether you prefer the earthy flavors of Tuscan white bean soup, the zesty tang of Greek lemon chicken soup, or the aromatic spices of Moroccan spiced lentil soup, there’s a Mediterranean soup recipe to suit every palate. So, grab a ladle, simmer up a pot of soup, and savor the warmth and comfort of Mediterranean cuisine right in your own kitchen.
