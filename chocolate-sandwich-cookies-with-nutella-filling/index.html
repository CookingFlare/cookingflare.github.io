Chocolate Sandwich Cookies with Nutella Filling


Indulging in rich, chocolatey treats often feels like a decadent experience, and chocolate sandwich cookies deliver exactly that. These delightful bites combine crisp cocoa cookies with luxurious Nutella filling, offering both textural contrast and irresistible flavor. Whether you’re preparing for a party, surprising loved ones, or just craving a rewarding experience in the kitchen, this recipe is sure to satisfy.

Why Make Chocolate Sandwich Cookies from Scratch?

Homemade treats always carry a unique charm—they’re fresher, customizable, and allow you to control ingredients to suit your preferences. With store-bought cookies, you often miss out on the nuanced flavors and textures. By making chocolate sandwich cookies at home, you can achieve a balanced sweetness, a satisfying crunch, and a creamy, gooey filling, while ensuring every bite is crafted with care.

Pairing the crisp cocoa cookies with Nutella is pure genius. The hazelnut notes in the filling amplify the deep flavor of the chocolate, creating a harmonious experience with every bite. Plus, they freeze well, making them perfect for meal prepping or saving as a sweet surprise for another day.

Let’s dive into the steps for creating these crowd-pleasing cookies!

Ingredients You’ll Need

For the Cookies:

  • 1 cup (125g) all-purpose flour
  • ½ cup (50g) unsweetened cocoa powder
  • ½ teaspoon baking soda
  • ¼ teaspoon salt
  • ½ cup (115g) unsalted butter, softened
  • ⅔ cup (130g) granulated sugar
  • 1 large egg
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

For the Nutella Filling:

  • ½ cup (150g) Nutella
  • ¾ cup (85g) powdered sugar
  • 2 tablespoons unsalted butter, room temperature
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Step-by-Step Instructions

Step 1: Prepare the Cookie Dough

Start by whisking together the dry ingredients. In a medium bowl, combine the all-purpose flour, cocoa powder, baking soda, and salt. Be sure to sift the cocoa powder for a lump-free mixture—it makes a big difference in achieving smooth cookies.

In a separate large bowl, cream the softened butter and granulated sugar until pale and fluffy. This process takes about 2-3 minutes using an electric mixer. Add the egg and vanilla extract to the mixture, blending thoroughly.

Gradually add the dry mixture to the wet ingredients. Mix on a low speed until a cohesive dough forms. If the dough feels sticky, refrigerate it for about 30 minutes to firm up—it will be easier to handle later.

Step 2: Shape and Bake the Cookies

Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C) and line two baking sheets with parchment paper. To shape the cookies, roll the dough into balls, approximately 1 tablespoon each. Place the dough balls on the prepared baking sheets, spacing them about 2 inches apart. Once all the balls are lined up, gently press them flat with the back of a glass or your palm. The thickness should be consistent to ensure even baking.

Bake the cookies for 8-10 minutes. They may still look slightly soft in the center when done, but they will firm up as they cool. Allow them to rest on the pan for 5 minutes before transferring to a wire rack to cool completely.

Step 3: Whip Up the Nutella Filling

While the cookies cool, focus on the star ingredient—the Nutella filling. In a mixing bowl, combine Nutella, powdered sugar, room-temperature butter, and vanilla extract. Blend using an electric mixer on medium speed until smooth and creamy.

For a firmer filling, chill it in the fridge for 10-15 minutes before assembly. This step is optional but helps if you prefer a thicker, structured filling.

Step 4: Assemble the Sandwich Cookies

Take one cooled cookie and spread about 1-2 teaspoons of Nutella filling on the flat side. Use a small offset spatula for precision, or simply pipe the filling onto the cookie for a polished look. Top with a second cookie, flat side down, and gently press together until the filling distributes evenly. Repeat with the remaining cookies.

For added flair, roll the edges of the assembled cookies in crushed hazelnuts or mini chocolate chips. This optional step adds both texture and visual appeal.

Tips for Success

What sets homemade cookies apart is the attention to detail. Here are a few tips to ensure your chocolate sandwich cookies turn out perfectly:

  1. Choose High-Quality Cocoa Powder: The cocoa is the backbone of the cookie flavor, so opt for a rich, unsweetened cocoa for deeper chocolate notes.
  2. Room Temperature Ingredients: Butter and eggs mix more seamlessly when at room temperature. This step improves texture and consistency.
  3. Don’t Overbake: Removing the cookies while they are still slightly soft in the center is key to achieving tender results.
  4. Chilling Time: If the dough or filling feels sticky, don’t skip the chilling step—it makes assembly much smoother.

Storage and Serving Suggestions

Chocolate sandwich cookies with Nutella filling are best enjoyed within two days of preparation. However, you can store them in an airtight container at room temperature for up to four days or refrigerate for up to a week. If you make a large batch, consider freezing the cookies without assembly. Then, you can add fresh Nutella filling when ready to serve.

Serve these cookies with a steaming cup of coffee, milk, or even as an ice cream accompaniment for a dessert that wows. Their versatility makes them a hit at casual gatherings, festive celebrations, or even as gifts.

Variations to Try

The beauty of this recipe is its adaptability! Here are some creative ways to customize your chocolate sandwich cookies:

  1. Add Espresso Powder: Enhance the cocoa flavor by adding 1 teaspoon of instant espresso powder to the cookie dough.
  2. Switch the Filling: While Nutella is a clear favorite, you could use peanut butter, salted caramel, or even cream cheese frosting for a completely different flavor profile.
  3. Dip in Chocolate: After assembling, dip half of each cookie sandwich into melted dark or white chocolate and let it set for a more elegant presentation.
  4. Seasonal Add-Ons: Top the cookies with festive sprinkles, crushed peppermint, or edible glitter to match the holidays or special occasions.

Why You’ll Love This Recipe

These chocolate sandwich cookies offer everything you want in a homemade treat—bold flavor, satisfying texture, and the creamy joy of Nutella filling. They look stunning when arranged on a serving platter and are sure to impress anyone who tastes them.

The recipe is approachable for beginner bakers while still holding plenty of opportunities for creative touches for seasoned pros. Whether you stick to the classic version or experiment with new additions, these cookies will undoubtedly become a household favorite.

Dive into this recipe, and unlock the magic of homemade cookies paired with Nutella’s rich, nutty allure.
