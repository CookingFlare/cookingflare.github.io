Decadent Hazelnut Chocolate Cake Recipe: Nutty Delight in Every Bite


If you’re a fan of rich, indulgent desserts that combine the luxurious flavors of chocolate and hazelnut, then this Decadent Hazelnut Chocolate Cake is the perfect treat for you. This recipe brings together the irresistible combination of moist chocolate cake and the nutty goodness of hazelnuts, creating a dessert that’s sure to impress even the most discerning of palates. Whether you’re hosting a dinner party, celebrating a special occasion, or simply craving a slice of something truly decadent, this cake is guaranteed to be a showstopper.

Ingredients

For the Cake:

  • 1 3/4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 3/4 cups granulated sugar
  • 3/4 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons baking soda
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 cup whole milk
  • 1/2 cup vegetable oil
  • 2 teaspoons pure vanilla extract
  • 1 cup boiling water

For the Hazelnut Frosting:

  • 1 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 3 cups confectioners’ sugar
  • 1/3 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1/4 cup whole milk
  • 1 teaspoon pure vanilla extract
  • 1/2 cup finely chopped hazelnuts

For the Chocolate Ganache:

  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • 1 cup semisweet chocolate chips
  • 1/2 teaspoon pure vanilla extract
  • 1/4 cup finely chopped hazelnuts, for garnish

Instructions

For the Cake:

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease and flour two 9-inch round cake pans.
  2. In a large bowl, combine the flour, sugar, cocoa powder, baking powder, baking soda, and salt.
  3. Add the eggs, milk, oil, and vanilla extract to the dry ingredients and mix until well combined.
  4. Stir in the boiling water. The batter will be thin, but that’s the way it should be.
  5. Pour the batter evenly into the prepared pans.
  6. Bake for 30 to 35 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center of the cakes comes out clean.
  7. Allow the cakes to cool in the pans for 10 minutes before transferring them to a wire rack to cool completely.

For the Hazelnut Frosting:

  1. In a large bowl, beat the butter until creamy.
  2. Gradually add the confectioners’ sugar and cocoa powder, mixing well after each addition.
  3. Pour in the milk and vanilla extract, and beat until light and fluffy.
  4. Stir in the finely chopped hazelnuts.

For the Chocolate Ganache:

  1. In a small saucepan, heat the heavy cream until it just begins to boil.
  2. Place the chocolate chips in a heatproof bowl and pour the hot cream over the chocolate.
  3. Let it sit for a minute, then stir until the chocolate is completely melted and the mixture is smooth.
  4. Stir in the vanilla extract.

Assembly:

  1. Place one of the cooled cake layers on a serving plate. Spread a generous layer of hazelnut frosting over the top.
  2. Carefully place the second cake layer on top and frost the top and sides of the cake with the remaining hazelnut frosting.
  3. Once the frosting is set, pour the chocolate ganache over the top of the cake, allowing it to drip down the sides.
  4. Sprinkle the finely chopped hazelnuts over the ganache for an elegant finish.

Serving and Storage:

This Decadent Hazelnut Chocolate Cake is best enjoyed at room temperature. The flavors meld beautifully after a day, so it can be made ahead of time. Store any leftovers in an airtight container at room temperature for up to 3 days or in the refrigerator for up to 5 days.

Final Thoughts

Indulge in the richness of chocolate and the nuttiness of hazelnuts with every forkful of this exquisite Decadent Hazelnut Chocolate Cake. Whether you’re sharing it with loved ones or savoring it all to yourself, this cake is a true delight that’s bound to leave a lasting impression. So, roll up your sleeves, gather your ingredients, and treat yourself to the ultimate nutty chocolate experience. Happy baking!
