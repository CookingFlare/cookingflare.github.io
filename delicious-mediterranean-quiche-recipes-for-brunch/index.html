Delicious Mediterranean Quiche Recipes for Brunch


Whether you’re hosting a weekend brunch or simply looking for a delicious and satisfying meal, Mediterranean quiches are a perfect choice. Packed with vibrant flavors and wholesome ingredients, these quiches offer a delightful fusion of Mediterranean herbs and fresh vegetables. In this blog post, we will explore three mouthwatering Mediterranean quiche recipes that are guaranteed to impress your guests.

1. Spinach and Feta Quiche

Ingredients:

  • 1 pre-made pie crust
  • 1 cup fresh spinach, chopped
  • 1/2 cup feta cheese, crumbled
  • 1/4 cup sun-dried tomatoes, chopped
  • 1/4 cup red onion, finely chopped
  • 4 large eggs
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Place the pre-made pie crust in a greased pie dish and set aside.
  3. In a mixing bowl, whisk together the eggs, milk, dried oregano, salt, and pepper.
  4. Spread the chopped spinach evenly over the pie crust, followed by the feta cheese, sun-dried tomatoes, and red onion.
  5. Pour the egg mixture over the vegetables, ensuring everything is evenly coated.
  6. Bake for 35-40 minutes, or until the quiche is set and lightly golden on top.
  7. Allow the quiche to cool for a few minutes before slicing and serving.

2. Roasted Red Pepper and Goat Cheese Quiche

Ingredients:

  • 1 pre-made pie crust
  • 2 roasted red peppers, sliced
  • 4 ounces goat cheese, crumbled
  • 1/4 cup fresh basil, chopped
  • 4 large eggs
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried thyme
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Grease a pie dish and place the pre-made pie crust in it.
  3. In a mixing bowl, whisk together the eggs, milk, dried thyme, salt, and pepper.
  4. Spread the roasted red pepper slices evenly over the pie crust, followed by the crumbled goat cheese and fresh basil.
  5. Pour the egg mixture over the filling ingredients, making sure everything is well-coated.
  6. Bake for 35-40 minutes, or until the quiche is set and lightly browned.
  7. Allow the quiche to cool slightly before cutting into slices.

3. Tomato and Mozzarella Quiche

Ingredients:

  • 1 pre-made pie crust
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 4 ounces mozzarella cheese, sliced
  • 2 tablespoons fresh basil, chopped
  • 4 large eggs
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried rosemary
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Grease a pie dish and place the pre-made pie crust in it.
  3. In a mixing bowl, whisk together the eggs, milk, dried rosemary, salt, and pepper.
  4. Arrange the cherry tomato halves on the pie crust, followed by the mozzarella slices and fresh basil.
  5. Pour the egg mixture over the filling ingredients, ensuring they are evenly coated.
  6. Bake for 35-40 minutes, or until the quiche is set and the cheese is golden and bubbly.
  7. Allow the quiche to cool slightly before serving.

Conclusion

These delicious Mediterranean quiches are perfect for brunch or any meal of the day. Their enticing flavors and vibrant colors make them a feast for both the eyes and the taste buds. Whether you choose the spinach and feta, roasted red pepper and goat cheese, or tomato and mozzarella quiche, you’re sure to delight your guests with these Mediterranean-inspired creations. So go ahead, gather your ingredients, and let the aroma of these quiches fill your kitchen as you prepare for a memorable brunch experience. Enjoy!
