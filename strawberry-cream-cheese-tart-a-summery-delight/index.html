Strawberry Cream Cheese Tart: A Summery Delight


Summer is the perfect time to indulge in delicious desserts that capture the essence of the season. One such delightful treat is the Strawberry Cream Cheese Tart. This mouthwatering dessert combines the tanginess of cream cheese with the sweetness of fresh strawberries, resulting in a heavenly combination that will leave your taste buds begging for more.

Ingredients

To create this summery delight, you will need the following ingredients:

  • 1 ½ cups of all-purpose flour
  • ½ cup of unsalted butter, cold and cubed
  • ¼ cup of granulated sugar
  • 1 teaspoon of vanilla extract
  • 8 ounces of cream cheese, softened
  • ⅓ cup of powdered sugar
  • 1 cup of heavy cream
  • 1 teaspoon of lemon zest
  • 2 cups of fresh strawberries, sliced
  • ¼ cup of strawberry jam
  • Fresh mint leaves for garnish

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C). In a mixing bowl, combine the flour, granulated sugar, and cold cubed butter. Using your fingers or a pastry cutter, work the butter into the flour until the mixture resembles coarse crumbs.

  2. Add the vanilla extract to the mixture and continue to mix until the dough comes together. Press the dough evenly into the bottom and sides of a 9-inch tart pan with a removable bottom. Prick the bottom of the crust with a fork to prevent it from puffing up during baking.

  3. Bake the crust in the preheated oven for 12-15 minutes, or until it turns golden brown. Remove from the oven and let it cool completely.

  4. In a separate mixing bowl, beat the softened cream cheese and powdered sugar until smooth and creamy. In another bowl, whip the heavy cream until stiff peaks form. Gently fold the whipped cream into the cream cheese mixture until well combined.

  5. Stir in the lemon zest to add a refreshing citrusy kick to the cream cheese filling. Spread the cream cheese mixture evenly onto the cooled tart crust.

  6. Arrange the fresh strawberry slices on top of the cream cheese filling, pressing them gently into the mixture. This will create a beautiful pattern and ensure every bite is filled with luscious strawberries.

  7. In a small saucepan, heat the strawberry jam over low heat until it becomes smooth and pourable. Drizzle the warm jam over the strawberry slices, creating a glossy and irresistible glaze.

  8. Place the tart in the refrigerator for at least 2 hours to allow the flavors to meld together and the filling to set.

  9. When you’re ready to serve, garnish the tart with fresh mint leaves for an added touch of freshness. Cut into slices and enjoy this summery delight with your loved ones.

Conclusion

The Strawberry Cream Cheese Tart is the epitome of summer in a dessert. With its buttery crust, creamy filling, and juicy strawberries, it is a treat that will transport you to sunny days and picnics in the park. Whether you’re hosting a barbecue or simply craving a sweet treat, this tart is sure to impress. So go ahead and indulge in this summery delight, and let the flavors of the season dance on your palate.
