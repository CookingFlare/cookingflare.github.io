Flavorful Vegan Thai Green Curry with Tofu


If you’re looking for a delicious and satisfying plant-based dish that’s bursting with flavor, then this Vegan Thai Green Curry with Tofu is just what you need. This aromatic and creamy curry is a perfect blend of fresh vegetables, fragrant herbs, and creamy coconut milk, creating a symphony of flavors that will tantalize your taste buds. Whether you’re a seasoned vegan or just looking to incorporate more plant-based meals into your diet, this recipe is sure to become a staple in your culinary repertoire.

Ingredients

For the Green Curry Paste:

  • 2 stalks of lemongrass, sliced
  • 4 green chilies, chopped
  • 4 shallots, diced
  • 6 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 thumb-sized piece of galangal, chopped
  • 1 bunch of fresh cilantro, including the stems
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon ground coriander
  • 1 teaspoon black peppercorns
  • Zest of 1 lime
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon coconut sugar

For the Curry:

  • 14 oz (400g) firm tofu, drained and cubed
  • 1 can (14 oz/400ml) coconut milk
  • 2 cups vegetable broth
  • 1 red bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 cup snap peas
  • 1 cup bamboo shoots
  • 1 cup baby corn
  • 1 tablespoon coconut oil
  • Fresh basil leaves for garnish

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Green Curry Paste:

    • In a food processor, combine lemongrass, green chilies, shallots, garlic, galangal, cilantro, cumin, coriander, black peppercorns, lime zest, soy sauce, and coconut sugar. Blend until a smooth paste forms.

  2. Sauté the Tofu:

    • In a large skillet, heat coconut oil over medium heat. Add the cubed tofu and sauté until golden brown. Set aside.

  3. Cook the Curry:

    • In a large pot, heat a small amount of coconut oil over medium heat. Add the green curry paste and cook for 2-3 minutes, stirring constantly to release the aromas.

  4. Add the Vegetables:

    • Pour in the coconut milk and vegetable broth. Stir well to combine. Add the red bell pepper, snap peas, bamboo shoots, and baby corn. Simmer for 10-15 minutes until the vegetables are tender.

  5. Combine the Tofu:

    • Gently stir in the sautéed tofu, ensuring it’s well coated with the flavorful curry sauce.

  6. Serve and Enjoy:

    • Ladle the Vegan Thai Green Curry into bowls and garnish with fresh basil leaves. Serve over steamed jasmine rice or rice noodles for a complete and satisfying meal.

Tips for a Perfect Vegan Thai Green Curry:

  • For a spicier kick, increase the number of green chilies in the curry paste.
  • Feel free to customize the vegetables based on your preferences and seasonal availability.
  • Leftover curry can be stored in an airtight container in the refrigerator for up to 3 days, allowing the flavors to further develop.

This Vegan Thai Green Curry with Tofu is a celebration of vibrant flavors and wholesome ingredients that come together to create a truly memorable dining experience. Whether you’re cooking for yourself or entertaining guests, this curry is a surefire way to impress and delight. So, roll up your sleeves, gather your ingredients, and embark on a culinary journey that will transport you to the heart of Thailand with every delicious bite.
