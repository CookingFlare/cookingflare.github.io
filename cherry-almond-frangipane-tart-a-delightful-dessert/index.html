Cherry Almond Frangipane Tart: A Delightful Dessert


If you’re looking for a show-stopping dessert that’s both elegant and delicious, then look no further than the Cherry Almond Frangipane Tart. This delightful tart combines the sweet and tangy flavor of cherries with the rich, nutty taste of almond frangipane, all encased in a buttery, flaky pastry shell. Whether you’re hosting a dinner party, celebrating a special occasion, or simply craving a decadent treat, this Cherry Almond Frangipane Tart is sure to impress and satisfy.

Ingredients

For the Pastry:

  • 1 1/4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, cold and cut into cubes
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 egg yolk
  • 2 tablespoons ice water

For the Almond Frangipane Filling:

  • 1 cup almond flour
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/4 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 teaspoon almond extract
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt

For the Cherry Topping:

  • 2 cups fresh cherries, pitted and halved
  • 2 tablespoons granulated sugar
  • 1 tablespoon cornstarch
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice

Instructions

Making the Pastry:

  1. In a food processor, pulse the flour and sugar together. Add the cold butter and pulse until the mixture resembles coarse crumbs.

  2. Add the egg yolk and ice water, and pulse until the dough comes together. Be careful not to overmix.

  3. Turn the dough out onto a lightly floured surface and shape it into a disc. Wrap in plastic wrap and refrigerate for at least 30 minutes.

  4. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).

  5. Roll out the chilled dough on a floured surface and carefully transfer it to a 9-inch tart pan. Press the dough into the pan and trim any excess from the edges.

  6. Prick the bottom of the pastry with a fork, line with parchment paper, and fill with pie weights or dried beans. Blind bake for 15 minutes, then remove the weights and parchment and bake for an additional 10 minutes, or until golden brown. Set aside to cool.

Making the Almond Frangipane Filling:

  1. In a bowl, cream together the almond flour, sugar, and softened butter until light and fluffy.

  2. Add the eggs, almond extract, and salt, and mix until well combined.

Assembling the Tart:

  1. Spread the almond frangipane filling evenly into the cooled tart shell.

  2. In a separate bowl, toss the cherries with sugar, cornstarch, and lemon juice until well coated.

  3. Arrange the cherry halves on top of the frangipane in a decorative pattern.

  4. Bake the tart in the preheated oven for 30-35 minutes, or until the filling is set and the cherries are bubbling and slightly caramelized.

  5. Allow the tart to cool before slicing and serving.

Serving Suggestions

This Cherry Almond Frangipane Tart pairs beautifully with a dollop of lightly sweetened whipped cream or a scoop of vanilla bean ice cream. For an extra touch of elegance, garnish with a few slivered almonds and a dusting of powdered sugar.

Whether you’re a seasoned baker or just starting out, this Cherry Almond Frangipane Tart is a wonderful way to showcase your skills and treat yourself and your loved ones to a truly delightful dessert experience. So, roll up your sleeves, preheat your oven, and get ready to create a masterpiece that’s as beautiful as it is delicious. Enjoy!
