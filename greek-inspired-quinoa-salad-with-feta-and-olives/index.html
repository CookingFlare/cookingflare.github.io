Greek-inspired Quinoa Salad with Feta and Olives


Are you looking for a refreshing and flavorful salad that’s packed with protein and Mediterranean-inspired goodness? Look no further than this Greek-inspired quinoa salad with feta and olives. This dish is a perfect balance of fresh ingredients, bold flavors, and healthy grains, making it an ideal choice for a light lunch, a side dish, or a vibrant addition to any summer gathering. Let’s dive into the recipe and bring a taste of Greece to your table!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup quinoa
  • 1 3/4 cups water or vegetable broth
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1/2 English cucumber, diced
  • 1/2 red onion, finely chopped
  • 1/2 cup Kalamata olives, pitted and halved
  • 4 ounces feta cheese, crumbled
  • 1/4 cup fresh parsley, chopped
  • 1/4 cup extra virgin olive oil
  • 3 tablespoons red wine vinegar
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Cook the Quinoa: Rinse the quinoa under cold water using a fine mesh strainer. In a saucepan, bring the water or vegetable broth to a boil. Add the quinoa, reduce the heat to low, cover, and simmer for 15-20 minutes, or until the liquid is absorbed. Remove from heat and let it sit, covered, for 5 minutes. Fluff the quinoa with a fork and transfer it to a large mixing bowl to cool.

  2. Prepare the Vinaigrette: In a small bowl, whisk together the olive oil, red wine vinegar, dried oregano, salt, and pepper. Set the vinaigrette aside.

  3. Assemble the Salad: Once the quinoa has cooled, add the cherry tomatoes, cucumber, red onion, Kalamata olives, and feta cheese to the bowl. Pour the vinaigrette over the salad and gently toss to combine. Ensure that the ingredients are evenly coated with the vinaigrette.

  4. Add the Finishing Touch: Sprinkle the chopped parsley over the salad and give it a final toss to incorporate the vibrant green color and fresh flavor.

  5. Chill and Serve: Cover the salad and refrigerate for at least 1 hour to allow the flavors to meld together. When ready to serve, give the salad a quick toss and adjust the seasoning if needed. Serve in a large bowl or as individual portions, and enjoy the taste of Greece in every bite!

Flavorful and Nutritious

This Greek-inspired quinoa salad with feta and olives is a celebration of Mediterranean flavors and wholesome ingredients. The nutty and fluffy quinoa provides a protein-packed base, while the juicy cherry tomatoes, crisp cucumber, and tangy Kalamata olives add a burst of freshness to every bite. The creamy and salty feta cheese brings a luxurious texture and rich flavor, perfectly complemented by the herbaceous notes of fresh parsley and the zesty vinaigrette. With a perfect balance of textures and flavors, this salad is a true delight for the senses.

Versatile and Vibrant

One of the best things about this salad is its versatility. Whether you’re hosting a summer barbecue, packing a picnic, or simply craving a nourishing meal, this Greek-inspired quinoa salad is a delightful choice. It’s a fantastic side dish for grilled meats or seafood, a wholesome addition to a mezze platter, or a satisfying standalone lunch option. The vibrant colors and robust flavors of this salad make it a feast for the eyes as well as the palate, adding a touch of Mediterranean elegance to any table.

Embracing Mediterranean Wellness

Beyond its delectable taste, this salad encapsulates the essence of the Mediterranean diet, known for its health benefits and emphasis on fresh, wholesome ingredients. The combination of quinoa, vegetables, olives, and olive oil creates a nutrient-dense dish that’s rich in fiber, antioxidants, and heart-healthy fats. This salad is a wonderful example of how simple, quality ingredients can come together to create a dish that’s as nourishing as it is delicious.

Conclusion

Incorporate the flavors of the Mediterranean into your culinary repertoire with this Greek-inspired quinoa salad with feta and olives. Whether you’re a fan of Greek cuisine or simply seeking a vibrant and nutritious dish, this salad is sure to become a favorite. Elevate your dining experience with this versatile, flavorful, and wholesome recipe that celebrates the best of Mediterranean-inspired cooking. Enjoy the fusion of vibrant flavors and nourishing ingredients in every delightful forkful!
