Mouthwatering Instant Pot Beef Tacos Recipes


If you’re a fan of Mexican cuisine and love the convenience of using a pressure cooker, then you’re in for a treat! Instant Pot beef tacos are a delicious and satisfying dish that can be enjoyed by the whole family. The Instant Pot makes the beef incredibly tender and flavorful, allowing you to achieve that slow-cooked taste in a fraction of the time. Whether you’re hosting a casual get-together or simply craving a tasty weeknight meal, these mouthwatering Instant Pot beef tacos are sure to become a favorite in your recipe collection.

Ingredients

For the Beef:

  • 2 pounds beef chuck roast, cut into 2-inch cubes
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup beef broth
  • 1 tablespoon tomato paste
  • 1 tablespoon chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1 teaspoon oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste

For Serving:

  • Corn or flour tortillas
  • Your choice of taco toppings (e.g., shredded lettuce, diced tomatoes, sliced avocado, sour cream, shredded cheese, salsa, lime wedges)

Instructions

  1. Sear the Beef: Select the “Saute” function on the Instant Pot and add the olive oil. Once the pot is hot, add the beef cubes in a single layer and sear for 2-3 minutes per side until browned. Work in batches if necessary. Transfer the seared beef to a plate and set aside.

  2. Saute the Aromatics: Add the diced onion to the Instant Pot and saute for 2-3 minutes until softened. Stir in the minced garlic and saute for an additional 30 seconds.

  3. Deglaze the Pot: Pour the beef broth into the pot and use a wooden spoon to scrape up any browned bits from the bottom. This adds flavor to the dish!

  4. Pressure Cook the Beef: Return the seared beef to the pot. Stir in the tomato paste, chili powder, cumin, paprika, oregano, salt, and pepper. Secure the lid and set the Instant Pot to cook on high pressure for 45 minutes. Once the cooking time is complete, allow for a natural pressure release for 15 minutes before manually releasing any remaining pressure.

  5. Shred the Beef: Use two forks to shred the tender beef directly in the pot. The meat should easily fall apart.

  6. Assemble the Tacos: Warm the tortillas and fill each with a generous portion of the flavorful beef. Top with your favorite taco toppings and a squeeze of fresh lime juice.

Tips for Success

  • For an extra depth of flavor, consider adding a chipotle pepper in adobo sauce to the cooking liquid.
  • If you prefer a spicier kick, increase the amount of chili powder or add a pinch of cayenne pepper to the seasoning mix.
  • To save time, you can prepare the beef filling in advance and reheat it when you’re ready to assemble the tacos.

Final Thoughts

These Instant Pot beef tacos are a fantastic way to enjoy the bold flavors of Mexican cuisine with minimal effort. The tender, savory beef combined with your favorite toppings makes for a delightful and customizable meal that’s perfect for any occasion. Whether you’re a busy parent, a working professional, or simply someone who appreciates good food, this recipe is a game-changer. So, fire up your Instant Pot and get ready to savor the irresistible taste of homemade beef tacos!
