Apple Cider Braised Pork Tenderloin


Fall is the perfect season for cozy, comforting dishes that warm both the body and the soul. One such dish that embodies the essence of autumn flavors is Apple Cider Braised Pork Tenderloin. This recipe is a delightful blend of sweet and savory, with tender pork infused with the rich, tangy taste of apple cider. Let’s dive into how to create this mouthwatering dish that will surely impress your family and guests.

Ingredients:

  • 2 lbs pork tenderloin
  • 2 cups apple cider
  • 1/2 cup chicken broth
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 onion, sliced
  • 2 apples, cored and sliced
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat and Sear: Start by preheating your oven to 375°F (190°C). Season the pork tenderloin with salt and pepper. In a large ovenproof skillet, heat olive oil over medium-high heat. Sear the pork on all sides until golden brown, about 3-4 minutes per side. Remove the pork from the skillet and set it aside.

  2. Saute Aromatics: In the same skillet, melt butter over medium heat. Add the minced garlic, sliced onion, and apples. Saute until the onions are translucent and the apples are slightly caramelized, about 5 minutes.

  3. Deglaze and Braise: Pour in the apple cider and chicken broth, stirring to scrape up any browned bits from the bottom of the skillet. Bring the liquid to a simmer. Add the dried thyme and return the pork tenderloin to the skillet. Cover the skillet with a lid or foil and transfer it to the preheated oven.

  4. Braise in Oven: Let the pork braise in the oven for about 25-30 minutes or until it reaches an internal temperature of 145°F (63°C). Baste the pork occasionally with the cider mixture to keep it moist and flavorful.

  5. Rest and Serve: Once the pork is cooked through, remove it from the oven and let it rest for a few minutes before slicing. Serve the apple cider braised pork tenderloin sliced, drizzled with the delicious pan sauce, and accompanied by your favorite sides like roasted vegetables or mashed potatoes.

Tips:

  • For an extra depth of flavor, consider adding a splash of apple cider vinegar or a sprinkle of cinnamon to the braising liquid.
  • Ensure not to overcook the pork tenderloin to keep it juicy and tender.
  • Feel free to customize the recipe by incorporating herbs like rosemary or sage for a herbal twist.

This Apple Cider Braised Pork Tenderloin recipe is a wonderful way to celebrate the flavors of the season and elevate your dinner table with a dish that is both elegant and comforting. The tender pork, infused with the sweetness of apple cider, is sure to become a favorite among your family and friends. Enjoy the process of creating this delightful meal and savor the delicious results!

Remember, cooking is an art where you can unleash your creativity and share love through the food you prepare. So, gather your ingredients, set the stage, and let the aroma of Apple Cider Braised Pork Tenderloin fill your kitchen with warmth and joy. Happy cooking!
