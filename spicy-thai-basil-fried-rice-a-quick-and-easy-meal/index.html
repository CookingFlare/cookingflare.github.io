Spicy Thai Basil Fried Rice: A Quick and Easy Meal


If you’re looking for a delicious and satisfying meal that’s quick and easy to make, look no further than Spicy Thai Basil Fried Rice. This flavorful dish is a staple of Thai cuisine, known for its aromatic blend of spices, fresh herbs, and savory flavors. Whether you’re a fan of spicy food or simply enjoy the bold and vibrant tastes of Thai cooking, this recipe is sure to become a favorite in your culinary repertoire.

Ingredients

To create this delectable Spicy Thai Basil Fried Rice, you’ll need the following ingredients:

  • 3 cups cooked jasmine rice
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1-2 Thai bird’s eye chilies, finely chopped (adjust to taste)
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 1 red bell pepper, diced
  • 1 cup protein of your choice (chicken, shrimp, tofu, or a combination)
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon oyster sauce
  • 1 teaspoon fish sauce
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • 1 cup fresh Thai basil leaves
  • Sliced cucumber and lime wedges for serving

Instructions

Step 1: Prepare the Ingredients

Begin by cooking the jasmine rice according to the package instructions and set it aside. Prepare the garlic, chilies, onion, red bell pepper, and your choice of protein by chopping and dicing them as specified in the ingredients list.

Step 2: Heat the Wok

In a wok or large skillet, heat the vegetable oil over medium-high heat. Once the oil is hot, add the minced garlic and chopped Thai bird’s eye chilies. Sauté them for a minute until they become fragrant.

Step 3: Add the Aromatics

Next, add the chopped onion and diced red bell pepper to the wok. Stir-fry the vegetables for a few minutes until they start to soften and develop a slightly caramelized edge.

Step 4: Stir-Fry the Protein

Push the vegetables to one side of the wok and add the protein of your choice to the empty space. Cook the protein until it’s fully cooked through, whether it’s chicken, shrimp, tofu, or a combination of these ingredients.

Step 5: Toss in the Rice

Once the protein is cooked, add the cooked jasmine rice to the wok. Use a spatula to break up any clumps and stir-fry the rice with the vegetables and protein, ensuring that the rice is evenly coated with the aromatic flavors from the wok.

Step 6: Season the Fried Rice

Pour the soy sauce, oyster sauce, fish sauce, and sugar over the rice. Continue to stir-fry the rice, ensuring that the sauces are evenly distributed and the rice takes on a beautiful caramelized color.

Step 7: Add the Thai Basil

Finally, toss the fresh Thai basil leaves into the wok. The fragrance of the basil will infuse into the rice, adding a final layer of aromatic freshness to the dish. Stir the basil into the rice until it wilts slightly.

Step 8: Serve and Enjoy

Once the Thai basil has wilted, your Spicy Thai Basil Fried Rice is ready to be served. Plate the fried rice and garnish it with sliced cucumber and lime wedges for a refreshing touch. The citrusy lime can be squeezed over the rice to add a zesty kick.

Conclusion

Spicy Thai Basil Fried Rice is a delightful dish that captures the essence of Thai cuisine with its bold flavors and aromatic ingredients. By following this simple recipe, you can create a restaurant-quality meal right in your own kitchen. Whether you’re cooking for yourself or entertaining guests, this quick and easy recipe is sure to impress with its vibrant flavors and satisfying appeal. So, go ahead, gather your ingredients, and embark on a culinary adventure with this Spicy Thai Basil Fried Rice!
