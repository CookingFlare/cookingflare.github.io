Orange & Almond Flourless Cake Ingredients: 2 large oranges 6 eggs 250g almond meal 200g honey 1 tsp baking powder A pinch of salt Method: Wash the oranges, then boil them whole for 2 hours. Once cooled, blend them into a smooth puree. Preheat the oven to 180°C (350°F) and grease a cake tin. In a bowl, beat the eggs and honey until light and fluffy. Fold in the almond meal, baking powder, and a pinch of salt. Gently mix in the orange puree. Pour the batter into the cake tin and bake for 40-45 minutes. Allow the cake to cool before serving.
