Spiced Nut-Free Carrot Ginger Soup


If you’re looking for a comforting and nutritious soup that’s bursting with flavor, this spiced nut-free carrot ginger soup is the perfect choice. Packed with the goodness of carrots and the warmth of ginger and spices, this soup is not only delicious but also suitable for those with nut allergies. Whether you’re craving a cozy meal on a chilly evening or seeking a healthy option for lunch, this soup is sure to satisfy your taste buds and nourish your body.

Ingredients:

  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon fresh ginger, grated
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon ground coriander
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric
  • 1/4 teaspoon cinnamon
  • 1/4 teaspoon cayenne pepper (adjust to taste)
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt (adjust to taste)
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1 pound carrots, peeled and chopped
  • 4 cups vegetable broth
  • 1 cup coconut milk
  • Fresh cilantro or parsley for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Sauté the Aromatics: Heat the olive oil in a large pot over medium heat. Add the chopped onion and sauté until it becomes translucent, then add the minced garlic and grated ginger. Sauté for another minute until fragrant.

  2. Add the Spices: Stir in the ground cumin, ground coriander, turmeric, cinnamon, cayenne pepper, salt, and black pepper. Cook for a minute to toast the spices and release their flavors.

  3. Cook the Carrots: Add the chopped carrots to the pot and stir to coat them with the aromatic spices. Cook for a few minutes, allowing the carrots to soften slightly.

  4. Simmer with Broth: Pour in the vegetable broth and bring the mixture to a boil. Reduce the heat to a simmer and let the soup cook for about 15-20 minutes, or until the carrots are tender.

  5. Blend the Soup: Once the carrots are soft, carefully blend the soup using an immersion blender or transfer it in batches to a regular blender. Blend until smooth and creamy.

  6. Add Coconut Milk: Return the blended soup to the pot and stir in the coconut milk. Allow the soup to simmer for a few more minutes, allowing the flavors to meld together.

  7. Adjust Seasoning: Taste the soup and adjust the seasoning as needed. Add more salt, pepper, or cayenne pepper to suit your preferences.

  8. Serve and Garnish: Ladle the soup into bowls and garnish with fresh cilantro or parsley for a burst of freshness and color.

This spiced nut-free carrot ginger soup is a versatile dish that can be enjoyed as a starter, a light meal, or even as a nourishing snack. The combination of carrots and ginger not only provides a vibrant hue but also a wealth of health benefits. Carrots are rich in vitamin A and antioxidants, while ginger is known for its anti-inflammatory and immune-boosting properties. The aromatic spices add depth and warmth to the soup, making it a delightful treat for the senses.

Whether you’re cooking for yourself, your family, or entertaining guests, this soup is a fantastic addition to your recipe repertoire. It’s simple to make, allergy-friendly, and can be easily customized to suit various dietary preferences. Serve it with a slice of crusty bread or a simple green salad for a complete and satisfying meal.

So, the next time you find yourself with an abundance of carrots, whip up a batch of this spiced nut-free carrot ginger soup and bask in the comforting flavors and nourishing goodness it has to offer. Enjoy every spoonful of this delightful soup and revel in the joy of creating a wholesome and flavorful dish from scratch. Cheers to good food, good health, and good times around the table!
