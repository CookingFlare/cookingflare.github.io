Irresistible Mango Cheesecake Bars: A Tropical Delight


If you’re a fan of both cheesecake and mangoes, then you’re in for a treat! These Irresistible Mango Cheesecake Bars are the perfect combination of creamy cheesecake and tropical sweetness. With a buttery graham cracker crust, a luscious mango-flavored cheesecake filling, and a tangy mango glaze, these bars are sure to transport your taste buds to a tropical paradise.

Ingredients

For the crust:

  • 2 cups graham cracker crumbs
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, melted
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar

For the filling:

  • 16 ounces cream cheese, softened
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1/2 cup mango puree
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

For the glaze:

  • 1 cup mango puree
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 2 tablespoons cornstarch
  • 1/4 cup water

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 325°F (165°C). Line a 9x13-inch baking pan with parchment paper, leaving an overhang on the sides for easy removal.

  2. In a medium bowl, combine the graham cracker crumbs, melted butter, and sugar for the crust. Mix until the crumbs are evenly coated. Press the mixture into the bottom of the prepared baking pan, creating an even layer. Bake the crust for 10 minutes, then remove it from the oven and set it aside to cool.

  3. In a large mixing bowl, beat the cream cheese and sugar together until smooth and creamy. Add the eggs, one at a time, beating well after each addition. Stir in the mango puree and vanilla extract until fully incorporated.

  4. Pour the mango cheesecake filling over the cooled crust, spreading it out evenly. Smooth the top with a spatula.

  5. Bake the cheesecake bars for 30-35 minutes or until the edges are set and the center is slightly jiggly. Remove the pan from the oven and set it on a wire rack to cool completely.

  6. While the bars are cooling, prepare the mango glaze. In a small saucepan, whisk together the mango puree, sugar, cornstarch, and water. Cook the mixture over medium heat, stirring constantly, until it thickens and comes to a boil. Continue to cook for an additional 1-2 minutes until the glaze is smooth and glossy. Remove the glaze from the heat and let it cool for a few minutes.

  7. Once the bars have cooled to room temperature, pour the mango glaze over the top, spreading it out evenly. Place the bars in the refrigerator to chill for at least 4 hours, or overnight, until the glaze is set.

  8. When ready to serve, lift the bars out of the pan using the parchment paper overhang. Cut into squares and garnish with fresh mango slices if desired. Enjoy the tropical flavors of these irresistible mango cheesecake bars!

Conclusion

These Irresistible Mango Cheesecake Bars are the perfect dessert for any occasion. With their creamy texture, tropical flavor, and beautiful presentation, they are sure to impress your family and friends. Whether you’re hosting a summer barbecue or simply craving a taste of the tropics, these bars are a must-try. So go ahead, indulge in this tropical delight and let the flavors of mango and cheesecake transport you to paradise.
