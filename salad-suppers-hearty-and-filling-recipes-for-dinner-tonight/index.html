Salad Suppers: Hearty and Filling Recipes for Dinner Tonight


When it comes to a satisfying and nutritious dinner, salads are often overlooked as a main course option. However, with the right ingredients and a bit of creativity, salads can be transformed into hearty and filling meals that are perfect for dinner. Whether you’re looking for a light and refreshing option or a more substantial and protein-packed dish, these salad supper recipes are sure to delight your taste buds and leave you feeling fully satisfied.

Mediterranean Quinoa Salad

Prep Time: 20 minutes | Cook Time: 15 minutes | Total Time: 35 minutes

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup quinoa
  • 2 cups water
  • 1 can chickpeas, rinsed and drained
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1 cucumber, diced
  • 1/2 cup kalamata olives, pitted and halved
  • 1/4 cup red onion, finely chopped
  • 1/2 cup crumbled feta cheese
  • 1/4 cup fresh parsley, chopped
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons red wine vinegar
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Rinse the quinoa under cold water and drain.
  2. In a medium saucepan, bring the water to a boil. Add the quinoa, reduce heat to low, cover, and simmer for 15 minutes, or until the water is absorbed. Remove from heat and let it sit for 5 minutes, then fluff with a fork and let it cool.
  3. In a large bowl, combine the cooked quinoa, chickpeas, cherry tomatoes, cucumber, olives, red onion, feta cheese, and parsley.
  4. In a small bowl, whisk together the olive oil, red wine vinegar, dried oregano, salt, and pepper. Pour the dressing over the salad and toss to combine.
  5. Serve immediately or refrigerate until ready to serve.

This Mediterranean Quinoa Salad is a perfect example of a salad supper that is both hearty and satisfying. The combination of protein-packed quinoa and chickpeas, along with the fresh and zesty flavors of the vegetables and feta cheese, makes this salad a complete meal in itself.

Asian-Inspired Chicken Salad

Prep Time: 15 minutes | Cook Time: 15 minutes | Total Time: 30 minutes

Ingredients:

  • 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 6 cups mixed salad greens
  • 1 red bell pepper, thinly sliced
  • 1 cup shredded carrots
  • 1/4 cup sliced almonds
  • 1/4 cup chopped cilantro
  • 1/4 cup chopped green onions
  • 1/4 cup sesame seeds
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons rice vinegar
  • 2 tablespoons honey
  • 1 tablespoon sesame oil
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon fresh ginger, grated

Instructions:

  1. Season the chicken breasts with salt and pepper. In a skillet over medium heat, cook the chicken for 6-7 minutes per side, or until no longer pink in the center. Let it rest for a few minutes, then slice into thin strips.
  2. In a large bowl, combine the salad greens, red bell pepper, carrots, almonds, cilantro, and green onions.
  3. In a small bowl, whisk together the soy sauce, rice vinegar, honey, sesame oil, garlic, and ginger to make the dressing.
  4. Add the sliced chicken to the salad, drizzle with the dressing, and toss to combine. Sprinkle with sesame seeds before serving.

This Asian-Inspired Chicken Salad is a delicious and filling option for a salad supper. The tender chicken, crunchy vegetables, and flavorful dressing come together to create a meal that is both satisfying and full of vibrant flavors.

Southwest Black Bean Salad

Prep Time: 15 minutes | Total Time: 15 minutes

Ingredients:

  • 2 cans black beans, rinsed and drained
  • 1 cup corn kernels
  • 1 red bell pepper, diced
  • 1 avocado, diced
  • 1/4 cup red onion, finely chopped
  • 1/4 cup fresh cilantro, chopped
  • 1/4 cup lime juice
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1/2 teaspoon chili powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a large bowl, combine the black beans, corn, red bell pepper, avocado, red onion, and cilantro.
  2. In a small bowl, whisk together the lime juice, olive oil, ground cumin, chili powder, salt, and pepper to make the dressing.
