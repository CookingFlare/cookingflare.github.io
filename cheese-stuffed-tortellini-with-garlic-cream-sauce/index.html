Cheese-Stuffed Tortellini with Garlic Cream Sauce


There’s something undeniably comforting about a bowl of warm, creamy pasta, and when it’s topped with a rich garlic-infused cream sauce, the experience is taken to a whole new level of satisfaction. Cheese-stuffed tortellini with garlic cream sauce is a dish that combines the best of both worlds—delicate pasta filled with creamy cheese, enveloped in a smooth and flavorful sauce. It’s perfect for a cozy dinner, whether you’re cooking for one or preparing a meal to share with loved ones.

In this recipe, we’ll guide you through making cheese-stuffed tortellini from scratch, and of course, we’ll teach you how to prepare a delicious garlic cream sauce that will transform the dish into a truly mouthwatering experience. Let’s dive into this easy-to-follow, flavorful recipe that’s bound to become a favorite in your home.

Ingredients for Cheese-Stuffed Tortellini

Making cheese-stuffed tortellini from scratch might seem daunting at first, but it’s actually easier than you think. With just a few ingredients, you can create pasta that’s soft, pillowy, and perfect for holding the rich cheese filling.

For the Pasta Dough:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • A pinch of salt
  • Water (as needed)

For the Cheese Filling:

  • 1 cup ricotta cheese
  • 1 cup freshly grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/2 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1/4 teaspoon freshly grated nutmeg
  • 1 egg (to bind the mixture)

This combination of cheeses results in a creamy and savory filling that pairs perfectly with the garlic cream sauce. The ricotta brings a mild and smooth texture, the Parmesan adds sharpness and depth, and the mozzarella melts beautifully to create that perfect gooey texture.

How to Make Cheese-Stuffed Tortellini

Step 1: Prepare the Pasta Dough

Start by making the dough for the tortellini. On a clean surface, mound the flour and create a well in the center. Crack the eggs into the well, add the olive oil and salt. Gently whisk the eggs with a fork, gradually incorporating the flour from the edges. As the mixture starts to come together, use your hands to knead the dough. If the dough feels too dry, add a small amount of water, a teaspoon at a time, until it’s smooth and elastic. Knead for about 10 minutes, then cover the dough with a damp towel and let it rest for 30 minutes. This resting period helps the dough relax, making it easier to roll out.

Step 2: Prepare the Cheese Filling

While the dough rests, combine all the ingredients for the cheese filling in a mixing bowl. Stir well until the cheeses are fully blended, and the mixture is smooth. Taste and adjust the seasoning if necessary. The filling should have a savory balance of flavors, with a slight hint of nutmeg and pepper.

Step 3: Roll Out the Dough

After the dough has rested, divide it into two portions. Roll out the dough on a lightly floured surface, working with one portion at a time. Roll the dough as thin as possible, aiming for about 1/16-inch thickness. You can also use a pasta machine for even consistency.

Step 4: Cut the Dough into Squares

Once the dough is rolled out, cut it into small squares, about 2 inches in size. Place a small teaspoon of the cheese filling in the center of each square. Be careful not to overfill the tortellini, as it may cause them to burst open while cooking.

Step 5: Shape the Tortellini

Now comes the fun part—shaping the tortellini! Take each square of dough, fold it into a triangle, and press the edges together to seal. Then, bring the two corners of the triangle together and pinch them to form the classic tortellini shape. Repeat this process with all the squares of dough until the filling is used up.

Step 6: Cook the Tortellini

Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Gently drop the tortellini into the boiling water, making sure not to overcrowd the pot. Cook the tortellini for about 3-5 minutes or until they float to the surface. Using a slotted spoon, remove the tortellini and set them aside.

Garlic Cream Sauce: The Perfect Accompaniment

While the tortellini is cooking, it’s time to prepare the garlic cream sauce. This sauce is the perfect complement to the cheesy tortellini, adding a luscious and flavorful richness to the dish.

Ingredients for Garlic Cream Sauce:

  • 2 tablespoons unsalted butter
  • 4 cloves garlic, finely minced
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1/2 cup chicken or vegetable broth (optional, for thinning the sauce)
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt (to taste)
  • 1/2 cup freshly grated Parmesan cheese
  • Fresh parsley, chopped (for garnish)

Step 1: Sauté the Garlic

Start by melting the butter in a large skillet over medium heat. Once the butter is melted, add the minced garlic and sauté for about 1-2 minutes, or until the garlic becomes fragrant and slightly golden. Be careful not to burn the garlic, as it can turn bitter.

Step 2: Add the Cream

Next, pour in the heavy cream and stir to combine with the garlic butter. Allow the cream to come to a simmer, then reduce the heat to low. The sauce should start to thicken slightly. If you prefer a thinner sauce, you can add a splash of chicken or vegetable broth to achieve your desired consistency.

Step 3: Add Cheese and Season

Stir in the Parmesan cheese, allowing it to melt into the sauce, creating a creamy, velvety texture. Season with salt and pepper to taste. If you like a little extra flavor, a pinch of freshly grated nutmeg can be added, which pairs beautifully with the garlic cream.

Step 4: Combine the Tortellini with the Sauce

Once the sauce is ready and the tortellini is cooked, gently toss the tortellini in the garlic cream sauce. Use a spoon or tongs to carefully coat each piece with the sauce, making sure that every bite is covered in the creamy goodness.

Step 5: Garnish and Serve

To finish the dish, garnish with freshly chopped parsley for a burst of color and freshness. Serve the cheese-stuffed tortellini with garlic cream sauce immediately, while it’s still warm and comforting.

Tips and Variations

While this dish is delicious as is, there are many ways to customize it to your liking. Here are some tips and variations to try:

  1. Add Protein: For a heartier meal, consider adding grilled chicken, shrimp, or bacon bits to the tortellini. These proteins complement the creamy sauce and add extra flavor.

  2. Vegetable Add-ins: If you want to add some veggies to the dish, sauté spinach, mushrooms, or sun-dried tomatoes in the garlic butter before adding the cream. This will add texture and flavor to the sauce.

  3. Make It Lighter: For a lighter version of the sauce, substitute the heavy cream with half-and-half or a plant-based alternative like coconut cream or almond milk.

  4. Spicy Kick: Add a pinch of red pepper flakes to the garlic cream sauce for a touch of heat. The spice pairs wonderfully with the richness of the cheese.

  5. Frozen Tortellini: If you’re short on time or don’t feel like making homemade tortellini, you can always use store-bought cheese tortellini. Just cook it according to the package instructions and proceed with the garlic cream sauce.

Pairing Suggestions

This dish is rich and indulgent, so pairing it with a light, refreshing side can help balance out the flavors. Consider serving it with a simple green salad with a lemon vinaigrette or roasted vegetables for a complete meal. A crisp white wine, like a Chardonnay or Sauvignon Blanc, pairs beautifully with the creamy tortellini.

Why You’ll Love This Recipe

Cheese-stuffed tortellini with garlic cream sauce is a comforting and indulgent dish that brings warmth and satisfaction to any meal. Whether you’re making it for a special occasion or simply treating yourself to a delicious homemade dinner, this recipe is sure to impress. The delicate, handmade tortellini filled with creamy cheese provides a rich base, while the garlicky cream sauce elevates the dish with its smooth, velvety texture.

This recipe is also versatile, allowing you to make changes based on what ingredients you have on hand. You can easily customize the filling, the sauce, or even the type of pasta, all while maintaining the core flavors that make this dish so irresistible. Whether you’re a beginner cook or an experienced chef, this recipe is a great way to explore the joy of making fresh pasta at home.

So why not give it a try? Your taste buds will thank you for the indulgent, creamy, cheesy goodness of homemade cheese-stuffed tortellini with garlic cream sauce!
