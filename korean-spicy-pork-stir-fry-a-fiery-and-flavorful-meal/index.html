Korean Spicy Pork Stir-Fry: A Fiery and Flavorful Meal


Are you ready to take your taste buds on a fiery and flavorful journey? Look no further than this delicious Korean Spicy Pork Stir-Fry recipe! Bursting with the bold and vibrant flavors of Korean cuisine, this dish is sure to be a hit at your next meal.

Ingredients:

  • 500 grams of pork belly, thinly sliced
  • 2 tablespoons of gochujang (Korean red chili paste)
  • 2 tablespoons of soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon of honey
  • 1 tablespoon of sesame oil
  • 4 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 onion, thinly sliced
  • 1 carrot, julienned
  • 1 red bell pepper, thinly sliced
  • 4 green onions, cut into 2-inch pieces
  • 1 tablespoon of vegetable oil
  • Sesame seeds, for garnish

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, combine the gochujang, soy sauce, honey, sesame oil, and minced garlic. Mix well to create a flavorful marinade.

  2. Add the thinly sliced pork belly to the marinade, ensuring each piece is coated evenly. Let it marinate for at least 30 minutes to allow the flavors to infuse into the meat.

  3. Heat a tablespoon of vegetable oil in a large skillet or wok over medium-high heat. Add the marinated pork slices, reserving the marinade for later use. Stir-fry the pork until it is cooked through and slightly caramelized, approximately 5-7 minutes.

  4. Once the pork is cooked, remove it from the skillet and set it aside. In the same skillet, add the sliced onion, julienned carrot, and thinly sliced red bell pepper. Stir-fry the vegetables for 2-3 minutes until they are tender-crisp.

  5. Return the cooked pork to the skillet with the vegetables, along with the reserved marinade. Stir-fry everything together for an additional 2-3 minutes, allowing the flavors to meld.

  6. Add the green onions to the skillet and stir-fry for another minute, just until they are slightly wilted.

  7. Remove the skillet from the heat and transfer the spicy pork stir-fry to a serving dish. Garnish with sesame seeds for an extra touch of flavor and presentation.

  8. Serve the Korean Spicy Pork Stir-Fry hot with steamed rice or noodles, and enjoy the explosion of flavors with every bite!

Tips and Variations:

  • If you prefer a milder version of this dish, you can adjust the amount of gochujang according to your taste preferences.

  • Feel free to add other vegetables of your choice, such as mushrooms or zucchini, to enhance the nutritional value and texture of the stir-fry.

  • For an extra kick of heat, you can sprinkle some crushed red pepper flakes or add a few slices of fresh red chili peppers to the stir-fry.

  • This dish can also be made with chicken or beef instead of pork, depending on your dietary preferences.

  • Leftovers can be stored in an airtight container in the refrigerator for up to three days. Simply reheat in a skillet or microwave before serving.

So there you have it – a sizzling Korean Spicy Pork Stir-Fry recipe that will tantalize your taste buds and leave you craving more. Whether you’re a fan of spicy food or simply looking to explore the bold flavors of Korean cuisine, this dish is a must-try. Gather your ingredients, fire up your stove, and get ready to embark on a culinary adventure like no other. Enjoy!
