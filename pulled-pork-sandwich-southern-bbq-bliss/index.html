Pulled Pork Sandwich: Southern BBQ Bliss


There’s nothing quite like sinking your teeth into a delicious, tender pulled pork sandwich. The succulent meat, the tangy barbecue sauce, and the soft, pillowy bun all come together to create a symphony of flavors that epitomize Southern comfort food. Whether you’re hosting a summer barbecue, planning a casual dinner, or simply craving a hearty meal, the pulled pork sandwich is a timeless classic that never fails to satisfy.

The Art of Perfecting Pulled Pork

Before we dive into the recipe, let’s talk about the star of the show: the pulled pork itself. Achieving that melt-in-your-mouth tenderness and rich, smoky flavor is an art form, and it all starts with the right cut of meat. For this recipe, we recommend using a bone-in pork shoulder or pork butt. These cuts are marbled with just the right amount of fat to keep the meat moist and flavorful during the slow cooking process.

Ingredients:

  • 4-5 lbs pork shoulder or pork butt
  • 1 large onion, coarsely chopped
  • 4 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 2 cups of chicken or beef broth
  • 1 cup of your favorite BBQ sauce
  • 2 tbsp brown sugar
  • 1 tbsp smoked paprika
  • 1 tbsp cumin
  • 1 tbsp chili powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Slow-Cooked Perfection

  1. Begin by seasoning the pork shoulder generously with salt, pepper, smoked paprika, cumin, and chili powder. Rub the spices into the meat, ensuring an even coating.

  2. In a large skillet over medium-high heat, sear the seasoned pork shoulder on all sides until it develops a rich, caramelized crust. This step is crucial for locking in the meat’s natural juices and intensifying the flavor.

  3. Transfer the seared pork shoulder to a slow cooker and add the chopped onion, minced garlic, and chicken or beef broth. Cover and cook on low heat for 8-10 hours, or until the meat is tender and easily pulls apart with a fork.

  4. Once the pork is done cooking, carefully remove it from the slow cooker and transfer it to a cutting board. Using two forks, shred the meat, discarding any excess fat or bone.

  5. In a separate saucepan, combine the BBQ sauce and brown sugar, heating the mixture over medium heat until it begins to simmer. Pour the warm BBQ sauce over the shredded pork, tossing gently to coat the meat evenly.

Building the Perfect Sandwich

With the pulled pork ready to go, it’s time to assemble the ultimate Southern BBQ bliss: the pulled pork sandwich.

Ingredients:

  • Fresh burger buns or sandwich rolls
  • Creamy coleslaw
  • Pickles (optional)
  • Additional BBQ sauce for drizzling

  1. Begin by lightly toasting the burger buns or sandwich rolls to create a sturdy base for the hearty filling.

  2. Spoon a generous portion of the saucy pulled pork onto the bottom half of each bun, allowing the meat to spill over enticingly.

  3. Top the pulled pork with a generous heap of creamy coleslaw, adding a refreshing crunch and creamy texture to complement the rich, smoky flavors.

  4. If desired, add a few pickle slices for a tangy kick that cuts through the richness of the pork.

  5. Complete the sandwich with the top half of the bun and give it a gentle press to meld all the flavors together.

The Final Word

The pulled pork sandwich is a masterpiece of Southern cuisine, capturing the essence of slow-cooked, smoky goodness in every bite. Whether served at a casual backyard gathering or enjoyed as a comforting weeknight meal, this recipe promises to deliver a taste of Southern BBQ bliss that will have everyone coming back for seconds. So fire up that slow cooker, stock up on your favorite BBQ sauce, and get ready to savor a truly timeless classic.
