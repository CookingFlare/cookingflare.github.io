Vegan Coconut Lime Bars: A Refreshing and Coconutty Dessert


There’s nothing quite like the refreshing combination of coconut and lime to transport your taste buds to a tropical paradise. And when it comes in the form of vegan coconut lime bars, it’s pure dessert bliss. These bars are not only delicious but also vegan-friendly, making them a guilt-free treat for everyone to enjoy. So let’s dive right into this recipe and prepare to indulge in a delightful dessert experience!

Ingredients

For the crust:

  • 1 ½ cups of almond flour
  • ½ cup of shredded coconut
  • ¼ cup of coconut oil, melted
  • 2 tablespoons of maple syrup
  • A pinch of salt

For the filling:

  • 1 can of full-fat coconut milk
  • ½ cup of lime juice
  • Zest of 2 limes
  • ½ cup of agave syrup
  • 2 tablespoons of cornstarch
  • A pinch of salt

Instructions

Step 1: Preparing the Crust

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C) and line an 8x8-inch baking dish with parchment paper.
  2. In a mixing bowl, combine almond flour, shredded coconut, melted coconut oil, maple syrup, and a pinch of salt.
  3. Stir the mixture until well combined and press it evenly into the bottom of the prepared baking dish.
  4. Bake the crust for about 10 minutes, or until it turns golden brown. Remove it from the oven and set it aside to cool.

Step 2: Making the Filling

  1. In a saucepan, whisk together the coconut milk, lime juice, lime zest, agave syrup, cornstarch, and a pinch of salt.
  2. Place the saucepan over medium heat and bring the mixture to a gentle boil, stirring continuously.
  3. Once the mixture thickens, remove it from the heat and pour it over the cooled crust.
  4. Use a spatula to spread the filling evenly, ensuring it reaches all corners of the baking dish.

Step 3: Chilling and Serving

  1. Place the baking dish in the refrigerator and allow the bars to chill for at least 4 hours, or until the filling is firm.
  2. Once chilled, remove the bars from the baking dish using the parchment paper and transfer them to a cutting board.
  3. Slice the bars into squares or rectangles, depending on your preference.
  4. Serve the vegan coconut lime bars chilled and enjoy the burst of tropical flavors with every bite.

Tips and Variations

  • For an extra burst of coconut flavor, sprinkle some toasted coconut flakes on top of the bars before chilling.
  • If you prefer a sweeter dessert, you can increase the amount of agave syrup or add a dusting of powdered sugar before serving.
  • Feel free to experiment with other citrus flavors, such as lemon or orange, for a different twist on these bars.
  • These bars can be stored in an airtight container in the refrigerator for up to 5 days, though they’re so delicious they might not last that long!

Vegan coconut lime bars are the perfect dessert to satisfy your sweet tooth while transporting you to a tropical oasis. Their creamy coconut filling and tangy lime flavor make them a refreshing treat for any occasion. Plus, knowing that they’re vegan-friendly makes them even more enjoyable. So go ahead and treat yourself to these delightful bars – you won’t be disappointed!
