From Oven to Table: Quick and Easy Pizza Recipes


Pizza, the ultimate comfort food. Who doesn’t love the combination of melted cheese, savory sauce, and fresh toppings on a crispy crust? But making pizza from scratch can be a daunting task, especially when you’re short on time. That’s why we’ve put together this collection of quick and easy pizza recipes that go from oven to table in no time.

The Classic Margherita

There’s a reason why the Margherita is a timeless favorite. This simple yet elegant pizza is a masterclass in balance and restraint. With just a few ingredients, you can create a pizza that’s both familiar and exciting.

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb pizza dough
  • 1 cup tomato sauce
  • 8 oz mozzarella cheese, sliced
  • 1/4 cup fresh basil leaves
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 425°F (220°C).
  2. Roll out the pizza dough to a thickness of about 1/4 inch.
  3. Spread the tomato sauce over the dough, leaving a small border around the edges.
  4. Arrange the mozzarella slices on top of the sauce.
  5. Sprinkle the basil leaves over the cheese.
  6. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  7. Bake for 12-15 minutes, or until the crust is golden brown and the cheese is melted and bubbly.

The Meat Lover’s Delight

For all you meat lovers out there, this pizza is a dream come true. With pepperoni, sausage, and bacon, this pizza is packed with flavor and texture.

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb pizza dough
  • 1 cup tomato sauce
  • 8 oz mozzarella cheese, sliced
  • 12 slices pepperoni
  • 6 slices sausage
  • 6 slices bacon
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 425°F (220°C).
  2. Roll out the pizza dough to a thickness of about 1/4 inch.
  3. Spread the tomato sauce over the dough, leaving a small border around the edges.
  4. Arrange the mozzarella slices on top of the sauce.
  5. Add the pepperoni, sausage, and bacon on top of the cheese.
  6. Sprinkle the parsley over the top.
  7. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  8. Bake for 15-20 minutes, or until the crust is golden brown and the cheese is melted and bubbly.

The Veggie Extravaganza

Sometimes, you just need a break from the meat. This veggie-packed pizza is the perfect solution. With roasted vegetables and gooey cheese, this pizza is a game-changer.

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb pizza dough
  • 1 cup tomato sauce
  • 8 oz mozzarella cheese, sliced
  • 1 cup roasted vegetables (such as zucchini, bell peppers, and onions)
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh cilantro
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 425°F (220°C).
  2. Roll out the pizza dough to a thickness of about 1/4 inch.
  3. Spread the tomato sauce over the dough, leaving a small border around the edges.
  4. Arrange the mozzarella slices on top of the sauce.
  5. Add the roasted vegetables on top of the cheese.
  6. Sprinkle the cilantro over the top.
  7. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  8. Bake for 12-15 minutes, or until the crust is golden brown and the cheese is melted and bubbly.

The White Pizza

If you’re looking for a change of pace from the traditional tomato sauce, this white pizza is the way to go. With creamy ricotta cheese and fresh herbs, this pizza is a breath of fresh air.

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb pizza dough
  • 8 oz ricotta cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh basil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 425°F (220°C).
  2. Roll out the pizza dough to a thickness of about 1/4 inch.
  3. Spread the ricotta cheese over the dough, leaving a small border around the edges.
  4. Sprinkle the Parmesan cheese over the ricotta.
  5. Add the parsley and basil on top of the cheese.
  6. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  7. Bake for 12-15 minutes, or until the crust is golden brown and the cheese is melted and bubbly.

There you have it, four quick and easy pizza recipes that are sure to become your new favorites. Whether you’re a meat lover, a veggie enthusiast, or just looking for a change of pace, these recipes have got you covered. So go ahead, get creative, and start cooking up a storm in your kitchen!
