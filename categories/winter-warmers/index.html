minutes. add the minced garlic and cook for an additional minute. in a large mixing bowl, combine the cooked turkey, chicken broth, white wine (if using), and seasonings. stir until the turkey is well edges. dot the top of the stuffing layer with the remaining 1 tablespoon of butter. cover the dish with aluminum foil and bake for 30 minutes. remove the foil and continue baking for an additional 20-25 minutes, or until the top is golden brown and the casserole is heated through. remove the dish from the oven and let it rest for 10-15 minutes before serving. tips and variations to make the casserole more substantial, add some sautéed mushrooms or diced bell peppers to the turkey mixture. for an extra boost of flavor, add some chopped fresh sage or thyme to the stuffing mixture. if you
