Easy Christmas Cookie Recipes to Impress Your Guests


The holiday season is upon us, and what better way to spread joy than with the sweet aroma of freshly baked Christmas cookies wafting through your home? This blog post will guide you through some easy and delightful Christmas cookie recipes that are sure to impress your guests. Whether you’re hosting a festive gathering or gifting cookies to loved ones, these recipes will add a special touch to your holiday celebrations.

Classic Sugar Cookies

Ingredients:

  • 2 ¾ cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • ½ teaspoon baking powder
  • 1 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1 ½ cups white sugar
  • 1 egg
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • ½ teaspoon almond extract
  • 3 to 4 tablespoons sugar for decoration

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the Oven: Begin by preheating your oven to 375°F (190°C). Line a baking sheet with parchment paper.

  2. Mix Dry Ingredients: In a bowl, combine the flour, baking soda, and baking powder. Set aside.

  3. Cream Butter and Sugar: In a large bowl, cream together the softened butter and sugar until smooth. Beat in the egg, vanilla extract, and almond extract.

  4. Combine Ingredients: Gradually blend in the dry ingredients. Roll rounded teaspoonfuls of dough into balls and place them onto the prepared baking sheet. Flatten each ball with the bottom of a glass, dipped in sugar for a sparkling finish.

  5. Bake: Bake for 8 to 10 minutes in the preheated oven. Cookies should be golden around the edges. Let them cool on the baking sheet for a few minutes before transferring to wire racks.

Tips for Customization:

  • Decorate with Royal Icing: Once cooled, use royal icing to create festive designs on your cookies.
  • Add Sprinkles: Before baking, roll the dough balls in colorful sprinkles for a cheerful touch.

Peppermint Bark Cookies

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 egg
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • ½ cup cocoa powder
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup crushed peppermint candies
  • 1 cup semi-sweet chocolate chips

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the Oven: Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C) and prepare your baking sheets.

  2. Cream Ingredients: In a mixing bowl, cream together the butter and sugar until light and fluffy. Beat in the egg and vanilla extract.

  3. Combine Flour Mixture: In another bowl, whisk together the flour, cocoa powder, baking soda, and salt. Gradually add the dry ingredients to the wet mixture.

  4. Fold in Peppermint: Stir in the crushed peppermint candies and chocolate chips.

  5. Scoop and Bake: Drop spoonfuls of dough onto the prepared baking sheets, spacing them apart. Bake for 10-12 minutes or until set. Allow to cool completely.

Unique Serving Suggestion:

  • Serve with Hot Cocoa: Place a few cookies in a festive tin and pair them with a homemade hot cocoa mix for a great gift.

Gingerbread Men Cookies

Ingredients:

  • 3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tablespoon ground ginger
  • 1 tablespoon ground cinnamon
  • 1 teaspoon ground nutmeg
  • ½ teaspoon ground cloves
  • ½ teaspoon baking soda
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1 cup brown sugar, packed
  • 1 egg
  • 1 cup molasses

Instructions:

  1. Mix Dry Ingredients: In a bowl, mix together the flour, spices, baking soda, and salt.

  2. Prepare Wet Ingredients: In a separate bowl, cream the butter and brown sugar. Add the egg and molasses.

  3. Combine: Gradually add the dry mixture to the wet mixture until combined. Chill the dough for at least 2 hours.

  4. Roll and Cut: Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C). Roll out the dough on a floured surface to about ¼ inch thick. Use cookie cutters to create gingerbread men shapes.

  5. Bake: Place on a lined baking sheet and bake for 8-10 minutes. Let them cool completely before decorating.

Decorating Ideas:

  • Royal Icing: Use royal icing to create fun designs. Add edible glitter for a festive sparkle.
  • Candy Decorations: Attach small candies for buttons and eyes for a playful touch.

Chocolate Chip Peppermint Cookies

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • ¾ cup granulated sugar
  • ¾ cup brown sugar, packed
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 ¼ cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup chocolate chips
  • ½ cup crushed peppermint candies

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the Oven: Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C) and prepare baking sheets.

  2. Cream Together: In a bowl, cream together the butter, granulated sugar, brown sugar, and vanilla extract until fluffy. Beat in the eggs one at a time.

  3. Combine Dry Ingredients: In another bowl, mix the flour, baking soda, and salt. Gradually blend this into the wet mixture. Fold in the chocolate chips and crushed peppermint.

  4. Scoop and Bake: Drop rounded tablespoons of dough onto baking sheets. Bake for 10-12 minutes until the edges are golden.

Serving Suggestion:

  • Holiday Gift Boxes: Pack these cookies in decorative boxes with a festive ribbon for a charming homemade gift.

Snowball Cookies

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • ½ cup powdered sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup chopped nuts (walnuts or pecans)
  • ½ cup powdered sugar for rolling

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the Oven: Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C) and prepare a baking sheet.

  2. Cream Butter and Sugar: In a mixing bowl, cream the butter and ½ cup of powdered sugar together until smooth. Stir in vanilla.

  3. Combine Ingredients: Gradually mix in flour and nuts until combined.

  4. Shape and Bake: Form the dough into small balls and place them on the baking sheet. Bake for 10-12 minutes, until lightly golden.

  5. Dust with Sugar: While still warm, roll the cookies in powdered sugar. Allow to cool completely before serving.

Festive Variation:

  • Add Flavorings: Mix in almond extract or a bit of cocoa powder for different flavor profiles.

Conclusion

These easy Christmas cookie recipes are perfect for holiday gatherings, family traditions, or simply enjoying with a cup of tea. Each cookie brings its own flavor and charm, making your holiday season deliciously memorable. Whether you choose to stick with classic sugar cookies or experiment with peppermint bark, your guests will be impressed with your festive baking skills. So, gather your ingredients, put on your favorite holiday playlist, and let the baking begin! Happy Holidays and happy baking!
