Slow Cooker Chicken Marsala: A Gourmet Italian Dish


If you’re a fan of Italian cuisine, you’ve probably heard of Chicken Marsala. This classic dish is known for its rich flavors and tender chicken, all cooked in a savory Marsala wine sauce. While traditional recipes for Chicken Marsala involve stovetop cooking, we’re going to take a unique approach by preparing this gourmet Italian dish in a slow cooker. Yes, you read that right – Slow Cooker Chicken Marsala!

Ingredients

Let’s start with the ingredients you’ll need to recreate this delightful dish:

  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1 cup of all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon of salt
  • 1 teaspoon of black pepper
  • 2 tablespoons of olive oil
  • 1 cup of sliced mushrooms
  • 1 cup of Marsala wine
  • 1 cup of chicken broth
  • 1/2 cup of heavy cream
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon of dried thyme
  • 1 teaspoon of dried oregano
  • Fresh parsley for garnish

Instructions

Step 1: Prepare the Chicken

Start by seasoning the chicken breasts with salt and pepper. In a shallow dish, combine the all-purpose flour with an additional pinch of salt and pepper. Dredge each chicken breast in the seasoned flour until evenly coated.

Step 2: Brown the Chicken

Heat the olive oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat. Once the oil is hot, add the chicken breasts and brown them on both sides for about 3-4 minutes per side. The goal here is not to fully cook the chicken, but to develop a golden-brown crust on the outside. Once browned, transfer the chicken to the slow cooker.

Step 3: Prepare the Sauce

Using the same skillet, add the sliced mushrooms and garlic. Sauté them for a few minutes until the mushrooms are tender and the garlic is fragrant. Next, pour in the Marsala wine and chicken broth, scraping any browned bits from the bottom of the skillet. Let the mixture simmer for a couple of minutes before pouring it over the chicken in the slow cooker.

Step 4: Slow Cook

Add the dried thyme and oregano to the slow cooker, gently stirring to combine the ingredients. Cover the slow cooker and cook the Chicken Marsala on low for 4-5 hours, or until the chicken is tender and cooked through.

Step 5: Finish the Dish

Once the chicken is fully cooked, carefully remove it from the slow cooker and transfer it to a serving platter. Stir the heavy cream into the sauce remaining in the slow cooker, allowing it to thicken slightly. Pour the creamy Marsala sauce over the chicken and garnish with fresh parsley.

Serving Suggestions

Slow Cooker Chicken Marsala pairs beautifully with a side of al dente pasta, such as fettuccine or linguine. The pasta can be tossed in the luscious Marsala sauce for an indulgent dining experience. Additionally, a simple green salad or roasted vegetables can complement the richness of the dish.

Conclusion

With its tender chicken and luxurious Marsala wine sauce, Slow Cooker Chicken Marsala is a gourmet Italian dish that’s sure to impress. By utilizing the convenience of a slow cooker, you can achieve the same depth of flavors and succulent texture that you’d expect from a restaurant-quality Chicken Marsala. Whether you’re hosting a dinner party or simply craving a taste of Italy, this recipe is a must-try for any cooking enthusiast.

So, gather your ingredients, dust off that slow cooker, and get ready to savor the aromas of this delectable Slow Cooker Chicken Marsala. Buon appetito!
