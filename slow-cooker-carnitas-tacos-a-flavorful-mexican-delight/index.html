Slow Cooker Carnitas Tacos: A Flavorful Mexican Delight


Are you craving the bold and vibrant flavors of Mexican cuisine? Look no further than these Slow Cooker Carnitas Tacos! This delicious and easy-to-make dish will transport your taste buds straight to the streets of Mexico. With tender, juicy pork that practically melts in your mouth, combined with a medley of aromatic spices, these tacos are a true delight for any food lover.

Ingredients

To make this mouthwatering dish, you will need the following ingredients:

  • 2-3 pounds of pork shoulder, boneless and trimmed of excess fat
  • 1 large onion, diced
  • 4 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon of chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon of cumin
  • 1 teaspoon of oregano
  • 1 teaspoon of paprika
  • 1 teaspoon of salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon of black pepper
  • 1/2 cup of orange juice
  • 1/4 cup of lime juice
  • 1/4 cup of chicken broth
  • Corn tortillas
  • For serving: fresh cilantro, diced onions, lime wedges, and your favorite salsa or hot sauce

Instructions

  1. In a small bowl, combine the chili powder, cumin, oregano, paprika, salt, and black pepper. Mix well to create a flavorful spice rub.

  2. Rub the spice mixture all over the pork shoulder, ensuring that it is evenly coated. This will infuse the meat with a delicious blend of flavors.

  3. Place the diced onions and minced garlic at the bottom of your slow cooker. They will help to enhance the overall taste of the carnitas.

  4. Carefully place the seasoned pork shoulder on top of the onions and garlic. The slow cooker will work its magic, transforming the meat into tender perfection.

  5. Pour the orange juice, lime juice, and chicken broth over the pork shoulder. These citrusy liquids will add a tangy brightness to the dish.

  6. Cover the slow cooker and cook on low heat for 8-10 hours, or on high heat for 4-6 hours. The long and slow cooking process will ensure that the pork becomes incredibly tender and flavorful.

  7. Once the cooking time is up, remove the pork from the slow cooker and shred it using two forks. The meat should easily fall apart, showcasing its succulent texture.

  8. If desired, you can crisp up the shredded pork by placing it on a baking sheet and broiling it for a few minutes. This will add a delightful crunch to your tacos.

  9. Warm the corn tortillas in a dry skillet or on a griddle until they are pliable and slightly toasted.

  10. Assemble your tacos by placing a generous amount of the slow-cooked carnitas on each tortilla. Top with fresh cilantro, diced onions, and a squeeze of lime juice. Feel free to add your favorite salsa or hot sauce for an extra kick of flavor.

  11. Serve the Slow Cooker Carnitas Tacos immediately and enjoy the explosion of flavors with every bite. These tacos are perfect for sharing with family and friends, or for a cozy night in.

Conclusion

With its tender and juicy pork, infused with a blend of aromatic spices, the Slow Cooker Carnitas Tacos are a true taste of Mexico. This flavorful dish will transport you to the streets of Mexico City, where the air is filled with the enticing scent of sizzling meats and warm tortillas. So gather your ingredients, fire up your slow cooker, and get ready to experience the vibrant flavors of these mouthwatering carnitas tacos. Buen provecho!
