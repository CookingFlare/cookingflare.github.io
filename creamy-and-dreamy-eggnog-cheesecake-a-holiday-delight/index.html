Creamy and Dreamy Eggnog Cheesecake: A Holiday Delight


‘Tis the season to indulge in all things sweet and festive! And what better way to celebrate the holiday season than with a creamy and dreamy eggnog cheesecake? This delightful dessert is the perfect blend of rich, velvety cheesecake and the warm flavors of everyone’s favorite holiday drink, eggnog. Get ready to impress your friends and family with this show-stopping treat that will leave them begging for more.

Ingredients

For the crust:

  • 1 ½ cups graham cracker crumbs
  • ¼ cup granulated sugar
  • ½ cup unsalted butter, melted

For the filling:

  • 3 (8 oz) packages cream cheese, softened
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 cup eggnog
  • 3 large eggs
  • 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • ½ teaspoon ground nutmeg
  • ¼ teaspoon salt

For the eggnog glaze:

  • 1 cup powdered sugar
  • 2 tablespoons eggnog
  • ½ teaspoon vanilla extract
  • Ground nutmeg, for garnish

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 325°F (163°C). Grease a 9-inch springform pan and set aside.

  2. In a medium bowl, combine the graham cracker crumbs, sugar, and melted butter. Mix well until the mixture resembles wet sand. Press the crumb mixture into the bottom of the prepared springform pan, creating an even layer. Set aside.

  3. In a large mixing bowl, beat the cream cheese and sugar together until smooth and creamy. Add the eggnog, eggs, flour, vanilla extract, nutmeg, and salt. Beat on low speed until well combined, scraping down the sides of the bowl as needed.

  4. Pour the filling over the crust in the springform pan. Smooth the top with a spatula to ensure an even layer.

  5. Place the springform pan on a baking sheet and transfer it to the preheated oven. Bake for 60-65 minutes or until the edges are set and the center is slightly jiggly. Turn off the oven and leave the cheesecake inside for another hour to cool gradually.

  6. After an hour, remove the cheesecake from the oven and let it cool completely on a wire rack. Once cooled, cover it with plastic wrap and refrigerate for at least 4 hours, or overnight, to allow it to set.

  7. In the meantime, prepare the eggnog glaze by whisking together powdered sugar, eggnog, and vanilla extract in a small bowl until smooth and pourable.

  8. Once the cheesecake is fully set, remove it from the springform pan and place it on a serving plate. Drizzle the eggnog glaze over the top, allowing it to cascade down the sides. Sprinkle with ground nutmeg for a festive touch.

  9. Slice and serve this creamy and dreamy eggnog cheesecake for a holiday delight that will have everyone reaching for seconds.

Tips and Variations

  • For an extra festive touch, garnish the cheesecake with crushed candy canes or a sprinkle of cinnamon.
  • You can make this cheesecake ahead of time and store it in the refrigerator for up to three days before serving.
  • Feel free to experiment with different flavors of eggnog, such as cinnamon, vanilla, or even pumpkin spice, to add a unique twist to your cheesecake.
  • Serve the cheesecake chilled for the best texture and flavor.

Get ready to wow your taste buds with this creamy and dreamy eggnog cheesecake. It’s the perfect dessert to elevate your holiday celebrations and leave your guests in awe. So go ahead, put on your apron, and indulge in this holiday delight. Happy baking!
