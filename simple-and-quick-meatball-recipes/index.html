Simple and Quick Meatball Recipes


Are you looking for a delicious and easy-to-make dish that will satisfy even the pickiest eaters? Look no further than these simple and quick meatball recipes. Whether you’re hosting a dinner party or simply want to treat yourself to a tasty meal, these recipes are sure to impress. So, roll up your sleeves and let’s get cooking!

Classic Italian Meatballs

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1/2 cup bread crumbs
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley
  • 1/4 cup milk
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1/4 teaspoon red pepper flakes (optional)
  • 1 egg

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C).
  2. In a large bowl, combine the ground beef, bread crumbs, Parmesan cheese, parsley, milk, salt, black pepper, garlic powder, dried oregano, red pepper flakes (if using), and egg. Mix well until all the ingredients are evenly combined.
  3. Shape the mixture into meatballs, about 1 inch in diameter, and place them on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper.
  4. Bake the meatballs for 20 minutes or until they are cooked through and browned on the outside.
  5. Serve the meatballs with your favorite pasta and marinara sauce for a classic Italian meal. Enjoy!

Sweet and Tangy BBQ Meatballs

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1/2 cup bread crumbs
  • 1/4 cup chopped onion
  • 1/4 cup milk
  • 1/4 cup barbecue sauce
  • 1 tablespoon brown sugar
  • 1 tablespoon Worcestershire sauce
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1 egg

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. In a bowl, combine the ground beef, bread crumbs, chopped onion, milk, barbecue sauce, brown sugar, Worcestershire sauce, garlic powder, salt, black pepper, and egg. Mix well until all the ingredients are thoroughly combined.
  3. Shape the mixture into meatballs, about 1 inch in diameter, and place them on a baking sheet.
  4. Bake the meatballs for 20 minutes or until they are cooked through.
  5. In a small saucepan, heat some barbecue sauce until warmed. Once the meatballs are cooked, toss them in the warmed barbecue sauce until coated.
  6. Serve the sweet and tangy BBQ meatballs as an appetizer or as a main dish with mashed potatoes or rice.

Asian-inspired Teriyaki Meatballs

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound ground chicken or turkey
  • 1/2 cup bread crumbs
  • 1/4 cup chopped green onions
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon minced garlic
  • 1 tablespoon grated ginger
  • 1 tablespoon honey
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1 egg

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. In a mixing bowl, combine the ground chicken or turkey, bread crumbs, chopped green onions, soy sauce, minced garlic, grated ginger, honey, salt, black pepper, and egg. Mix until well combined.
  3. Shape the mixture into meatballs, about 1 inch in diameter, and place them on a baking sheet.
  4. Bake the meatballs for 20 minutes or until they are cooked through and slightly browned.
  5. In a small saucepan, combine some soy sauce, honey, minced garlic, and grated ginger. Heat the mixture over medium heat until it thickens slightly.
  6. Once the meatballs are cooked, toss them in the teriyaki sauce until coated.
  7. Serve the Asian-inspired teriyaki meatballs as an appetizer with toothpicks or as a main dish with steamed rice and vegetables.

Conclusion

With these simple and quick meatball recipes, you can create delicious meals in no time. Whether you prefer a classic Italian flavor, a sweet and tangy BBQ twist, or an Asian-inspired teriyaki taste, there’s a meatball recipe for everyone. So, gather your ingredients, get cooking, and enjoy these delectable meatballs with your loved ones. Bon appétit!
