Easy and Flavorful Mexican Rice Casserole


Are you craving a delicious and satisfying Mexican dish that is easy to make? Look no further! This Easy and Flavorful Mexican Rice Casserole is the perfect recipe to satisfy your taste buds. Packed with bold flavors and wholesome ingredients, this dish will become a family favorite in no time.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups long-grain white rice
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 red bell pepper, diced
  • 1 jalapeno pepper, seeded and minced
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • 1 teaspoon chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 can (15 ounces) black beans, rinsed and drained
  • 1 can (15 ounces) corn kernels, drained
  • 1 can (14 ounces) diced tomatoes, undrained
  • 1 cup vegetable broth
  • 1 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • Fresh cilantro, chopped (for garnish)

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).

  2. In a large skillet, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the diced onion, minced garlic, red bell pepper, and jalapeno pepper. Sauté until the vegetables are softened, about 5 minutes.

  3. Add the cumin, chili powder, paprika, and salt to the skillet. Stir well to coat the vegetables with the spices.

  4. Add the long-grain white rice to the skillet and stir to combine with the vegetables and spices. Cook for 2-3 minutes, until the rice is slightly toasted.

  5. Transfer the rice mixture to a 9x13-inch baking dish. Spread it out evenly.

  6. Add the black beans, corn kernels, diced tomatoes (with their juice), and vegetable broth to the baking dish. Stir everything together until well combined.

  7. Cover the baking dish with aluminum foil and place it in the preheated oven. Bake for 30 minutes.

  8. After 30 minutes, remove the foil and sprinkle the shredded cheddar cheese evenly over the top of the casserole. Return the dish to the oven and bake for an additional 10 minutes, or until the cheese is melted and bubbly.

  9. Once the cheese is melted, remove the casserole from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes. Garnish with fresh cilantro before serving.

Serving Suggestions

This Easy and Flavorful Mexican Rice Casserole can be served as a main course or as a side dish. It pairs well with a dollop of sour cream, a squeeze of lime juice, and some freshly chopped avocado. You can also serve it with warm tortillas or a side salad for a complete and satisfying meal.

Tips and Variations

  • For added protein, you can include cooked chicken or ground beef to the casserole before baking.
  • If you prefer a spicier kick, leave the jalapeno seeds in or add a pinch of cayenne pepper.
  • Feel free to customize the recipe by adding your favorite vegetables, such as diced zucchini or sliced bell peppers.

Conclusion

This Easy and Flavorful Mexican Rice Casserole is a crowd-pleasing dish that combines the vibrant flavors of Mexican cuisine with the simplicity of a casserole. It’s perfect for busy weeknights when you want a delicious meal on the table without spending hours in the kitchen. So gather your ingredients and give this recipe a try. Your taste buds will thank you!
