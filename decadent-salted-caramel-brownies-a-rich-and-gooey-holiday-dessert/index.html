Decadent Salted Caramel Brownies: A Rich and Gooey Holiday Dessert


Indulge your taste buds this holiday season with these decadent salted caramel brownies. With their rich and gooey texture, these brownies are sure to be a hit at any gathering. The combination of fudgy chocolate, creamy caramel, and a touch of sea salt creates a flavor explosion that will leave you wanting more.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup unsalted butter, melted
  • 2 cups granulated sugar
  • 4 large eggs
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 3/4 cup cocoa powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup caramel sauce
  • 1 teaspoon sea salt flakes

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C) and line a 9x13-inch baking dish with parchment paper.
  2. In a large mixing bowl, whisk together the melted butter and granulated sugar until well combined.
  3. Add the eggs, one at a time, mixing well after each addition. Stir in the vanilla extract.
  4. In a separate bowl, sift together the flour, cocoa powder, and salt. Gradually add the dry ingredients to the wet ingredients, mixing until just combined. Be careful not to overmix.
  5. Pour half of the brownie batter into the prepared baking dish and spread it evenly.
  6. Drizzle the caramel sauce over the brownie batter, reserving a small amount for later. Sprinkle half of the sea salt flakes over the caramel.
  7. Carefully pour the remaining brownie batter over the caramel layer and spread it evenly.
  8. Drizzle the remaining caramel sauce over the top of the brownie batter and sprinkle the remaining sea salt flakes.
  9. Use a toothpick or a knife to create a marbled effect by swirling the batter and caramel together.
  10. Bake in the preheated oven for 30-35 minutes or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out with a few moist crumbs.
  11. Remove from the oven and let the brownies cool completely in the baking dish before cutting into squares.

Serving Suggestions

Serve these decadent salted caramel brownies with a scoop of vanilla ice cream or a dollop of whipped cream for an extra indulgent treat. They are perfect for holiday parties, potlucks, or simply as a delicious dessert to enjoy with family and friends.

Tips and Variations

  • For an extra gooey texture, slightly underbake the brownies by reducing the baking time by a few minutes.
  • Add chopped nuts, such as pecans or walnuts, to the brownie batter for added crunch and flavor.
  • Experiment with different types of caramel sauce, such as salted caramel or butterscotch, to create unique variations of this recipe.
  • If you prefer a less sweet version, reduce the amount of sugar in the brownie batter and caramel sauce.
  • Store any leftover brownies in an airtight container at room temperature for up to 3 days, or in the refrigerator for up to 5 days.

Conclusion

These decadent salted caramel brownies are the ultimate holiday dessert. Their rich and gooey texture, combined with the perfect balance of sweet and salty flavors, is sure to satisfy your sweet tooth. Whether you’re hosting a holiday gathering or simply looking for a special treat to enjoy at home, these brownies are a must-try. So go ahead, bake a batch, and indulge in the irresistible deliciousness of these salted caramel brownies.
