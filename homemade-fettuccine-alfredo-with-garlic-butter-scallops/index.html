Homemade Fettuccine Alfredo with Garlic Butter Scallops


There’s nothing quite like a homemade pasta dish to impress your family and friends, and this recipe for Homemade Fettuccine Alfredo with Garlic Butter Scallops is sure to do just that. The creamy, indulgent Alfredo sauce perfectly complements the delicate flavors of the seared scallops, creating a dish that is both comforting and elegant. Whether you’re cooking for a special occasion or just craving a restaurant-quality meal at home, this recipe is a must-try.

Ingredients

For the Fettuccine:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 3 large eggs
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt

For the Alfredo Sauce:

  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1 cup freshly grated Parmesan cheese
  • Salt and pepper to taste

For the Garlic Butter Scallops:

  • 1 pound fresh sea scallops
  • 2 tablespoons unsalted butter
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley, chopped for garnish

Instructions

Making the Fettuccine:

  1. Create a mound with the flour on a clean surface, then make a well in the center. Crack the eggs into the well, add the olive oil and salt, and gradually incorporate the flour into the eggs until a dough forms.
  2. Knead the dough for about 10 minutes until it becomes smooth and elastic. Wrap it in plastic wrap and let it rest at room temperature for 30 minutes.
  3. Roll out the pasta dough using a pasta machine or a rolling pin and cut it into fettuccine noodles. Dust the noodles with flour to prevent sticking.

Preparing the Alfredo Sauce:

  1. In a medium saucepan, melt the butter over medium heat. Once melted, stir in the heavy cream and bring the mixture to a simmer.
  2. Reduce the heat to low and gradually whisk in the grated Parmesan cheese until the sauce is smooth and creamy. Season with salt and pepper to taste.

Cooking the Garlic Butter Scallops:

  1. Pat the scallops dry with paper towels and season them with salt and pepper.
  2. In a large skillet, melt the butter over medium-high heat. Add the minced garlic and cook for about 1 minute until fragrant.
  3. Add the seasoned scallops to the skillet and cook for 2-3 minutes on each side until they develop a golden crust.

Bringing It All Together:

  1. Cook the fettuccine in a large pot of salted boiling water until al dente, then drain and return it to the pot.
  2. Pour the Alfredo sauce over the cooked fettuccine and toss to coat the noodles evenly.
  3. To serve, divide the fettuccine Alfredo among serving plates and top with the garlic butter scallops. Garnish with chopped parsley and additional grated Parmesan cheese if desired.

Tips for Success

  • For the best texture and flavor, try to make the pasta from scratch. However, store-bought fettuccine can be used as a time-saving alternative.
  • Be sure to use fresh, high-quality Parmesan cheese for the Alfredo sauce for the most authentic taste.
  • Pat the scallops dry before cooking to ensure a good sear and avoid excess moisture in the skillet.

Conclusion

Homemade Fettuccine Alfredo with Garlic Butter Scallops is a delightful combination of rich, creamy pasta and tender, flavorful scallops that will elevate any mealtime. The process of making the pasta and sauce from scratch may take a little extra time and effort, but the results are well worth it. Whether you’re a pasta enthusiast or a seafood lover, this dish is a true crowd-pleaser that will leave everyone at the table wanting more. So, roll up your sleeves, gather your ingredients, and get ready to savor the irresistible flavors of this indulgent homemade dish.
