Vegan Ice Cream Sandwiches: A Cool and Sweet Summer Treat


As the sun shines brightly and temperatures rise, nothing hits the spot quite like a cool and creamy dessert. If you’re looking for the perfect treat to beat the heat, vegan ice cream sandwiches offer a refreshing and indulgent way to satisfy your sweet tooth. These sandwiches are not only delicious but also dairy-free, making them a great option for vegans, those with lactose intolerance, or anyone simply looking to enjoy a lighter yet equally satisfying dessert.

In this blog post, we’ll guide you through making your own homemade vegan ice cream sandwiches. We’ll explore the best plant-based ice cream flavors, the perfect cookie options, and how to assemble these treats for maximum indulgence. Whether you’re hosting a summer barbecue, planning a picnic, or just treating yourself to a sweet snack, these vegan ice cream sandwiches are sure to become a crowd favorite.

Why Choose Vegan Ice Cream Sandwiches?

Traditional ice cream sandwiches, while tasty, often contain dairy, eggs, and other animal products. For those following a plant-based diet or looking to reduce their environmental impact, choosing vegan alternatives can make a huge difference. Vegan ice cream sandwiches are made using plant-based ingredients, ensuring they’re cruelty-free and suitable for a variety of dietary needs. Plus, they are just as delicious and satisfying as the traditional versions, if not more so!

Vegan ice cream can be made with coconut milk, almond milk, soy milk, or cashew milk, offering a rich, creamy texture without the need for dairy. The cookies used in these sandwiches are typically vegan-friendly, made with plant-based substitutes for eggs and butter. This makes vegan ice cream sandwiches a wonderful option for everyone to enjoy!

The Perfect Vegan Ice Cream for Your Sandwich

When it comes to creating the ideal vegan ice cream sandwich, the choice of ice cream is crucial. The ice cream filling should be creamy, flavorful, and easy to work with, ensuring it holds up well between the cookies. Fortunately, there are many fantastic plant-based ice cream options available today.

Here are a few popular plant-based ice cream flavors that are perfect for vegan ice cream sandwiches:

1. Coconut Milk Ice Cream

Coconut milk ice cream has a rich, creamy texture and a subtle tropical flavor that pairs wonderfully with various cookie types. Its natural sweetness enhances the overall dessert, making it a popular choice for vegan ice cream sandwiches.

2. Almond Milk Ice Cream

Almond milk ice cream tends to be lighter than coconut milk ice cream, but still creamy and delicious. With flavors like chocolate, vanilla, and even coffee, almond milk ice cream offers a smooth base for a satisfying treat.

3. Soy Milk Ice Cream

Soy milk ice cream is another fantastic option for those looking for a vegan alternative. It has a thick and creamy consistency, with a flavor that pairs well with almost any type of cookie. Soy-based ice cream is available in an array of fun and creative flavors, making it a versatile choice.

4. Cashew Milk Ice Cream

If you want something ultra-creamy with a rich texture, cashew milk ice cream is the way to go. Cashews give the ice cream a luxurious mouthfeel and can hold up perfectly when used as the filling in ice cream sandwiches.

5. Homemade Ice Cream

If you prefer a more personalized touch, making your own vegan ice cream is a fantastic option. By using coconut milk, cashew milk, or another plant-based milk as a base, you can create flavors like chocolate peanut butter, matcha, or even vanilla bean, all made with simple and wholesome ingredients.

Choosing the Right Cookies for Your Sandwich

The cookies are just as important as the ice cream when it comes to vegan ice cream sandwiches. The perfect cookie should be sturdy enough to hold the ice cream without falling apart, but soft and chewy enough to create a satisfying bite. When choosing cookies, it’s essential to pick vegan-friendly varieties—luckily, there are plenty of vegan cookie recipes to choose from!

1. Chocolate Chip Cookies

A classic chocolate chip cookie pairs perfectly with any type of ice cream. The sweet, slightly salty flavor of the cookie combined with the rich, creamy ice cream filling is a timeless combination that never fails to delight.

2. Oatmeal Cookies

For a heartier, chewier cookie, oatmeal cookies are an excellent choice. Their texture provides a nice contrast to the smoothness of the ice cream, and they can be enhanced with raisins, dried cranberries, or even chocolate chips for extra flavor.

3. Sugar Cookies

Sugar cookies are simple yet delicious, offering a sweet and light base for your ice cream. Their mild flavor complements the richness of the ice cream, making them a great option for a more subtle sandwich.

4. Peanut Butter Cookies

For those who love a nutty twist, peanut butter cookies are a fantastic choice. The saltiness of the peanut butter combined with the sweetness of the ice cream creates a unique and mouthwatering flavor profile.

5. Ginger Molasses Cookies

For a spiced option, ginger molasses cookies are a flavorful and aromatic choice. The warming spices from the ginger and molasses add a wonderful contrast to the cool, creamy ice cream and make for a more complex, interesting flavor experience.

How to Make Vegan Ice Cream Sandwiches at Home

Making vegan ice cream sandwiches at home is a fun and easy process that requires just a few key ingredients and a little bit of patience. Here’s a step-by-step guide to creating your own homemade vegan ice cream sandwiches:

Step 1: Gather Your Ingredients

For the cookies, you’ll need:

  • 1 ½ cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • ¼ teaspoon salt
  • ½ cup coconut oil (or vegan butter), softened
  • ¾ cup coconut sugar or brown sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 tablespoon non-dairy milk (such as almond milk)
  • ½ cup dairy-free chocolate chips (optional)

For the vegan ice cream, you’ll need:

  • 1 quart of your preferred plant-based ice cream (store-bought or homemade)

Step 2: Make the Cookies

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C) and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
  2. In a medium bowl, whisk together the flour, baking soda, and salt.
  3. In a separate large bowl, cream together the coconut oil (or vegan butter) and sugar until smooth and fluffy. Add in the vanilla extract and non-dairy milk, mixing until combined.
  4. Gradually add the dry ingredients to the wet ingredients and mix until a dough forms.
  5. Fold in the dairy-free chocolate chips, if using.
  6. Roll the dough into small balls (about 1 tablespoon each) and place them on the prepared baking sheet. Flatten each ball slightly with your hands or a fork.
  7. Bake for 8–10 minutes, or until the edges of the cookies are golden brown. Allow them to cool on a wire rack.

Step 3: Assemble the Ice Cream Sandwiches

  1. Once the cookies have cooled completely, take one cookie and scoop a generous amount of vegan ice cream onto the center.
  2. Place another cookie on top of the ice cream and gently press down to form a sandwich.
  3. If desired, roll the edges of the ice cream sandwich in sprinkles, crushed nuts, or chocolate chips for added texture and flavor.
  4. Repeat the process for the remaining cookies and ice cream.
  5. For best results, place the assembled sandwiches in the freezer for at least 30 minutes before serving to ensure they hold their shape.

Step 4: Enjoy Your Vegan Ice Cream Sandwiches!

Once your ice cream sandwiches are ready, it’s time to enjoy the ultimate summer treat. The cookies should be slightly crisp on the outside, while the ice cream inside remains cool and creamy. The combination of textures and flavors is sure to delight your taste buds with every bite.

Tips for Perfect Vegan Ice Cream Sandwiches

  • Use a sturdy ice cream: When selecting plant-based ice cream, look for a brand with a dense, creamy texture. This will prevent the ice cream from melting too quickly when assembled in the sandwich.

  • Customize the flavors: Feel free to experiment with different ice cream and cookie combinations. Try pairing fruit-flavored ice creams, like strawberry or mango, with almond or sugar cookies for a light, refreshing sandwich.

  • Make ahead and store: Vegan ice cream sandwiches can be stored in the freezer for up to a week. Simply wrap each sandwich in parchment paper or plastic wrap to prevent freezer burn.

  • Double up on cookies: If you like a higher ratio of cookies to ice cream, simply use larger cookies or more than two per sandwich.

Conclusion

Vegan ice cream sandwiches are the perfect way to cool off and enjoy a sweet treat during the summer months. With endless flavor combinations, these ice cream sandwiches offer the same delicious satisfaction as traditional versions, without any animal products. Whether you’re making them from scratch or using your favorite store-bought vegan ice cream, this treat is sure to impress.

So, gather your ingredients, get creative with your flavors, and enjoy these cool and refreshing vegan ice cream sandwiches with friends and family. They’re the ideal summer dessert, and they’re guaranteed to bring smiles to everyone’s faces. Happy snacking!
