Roasted Brussels Sprouts with Bacon: A Tasty Fall Side Dish


Autumn is the perfect time to enjoy the bountiful flavors of seasonal vegetables, and one dish that never fails to impress is Roasted Brussels Sprouts with Bacon. This delightful side dish combines the earthy sweetness of Brussels sprouts with the rich, smoky goodness of bacon. The result is a mouthwatering combination that will have your taste buds dancing with delight.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound Brussels sprouts, trimmed and halved
  • 4 slices of bacon, cut into small pieces
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C) and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
  2. In a large bowl, toss the Brussels sprouts with olive oil, salt, and pepper until they are evenly coated.
  3. Spread the Brussels sprouts in a single layer on the prepared baking sheet.
  4. Scatter the bacon pieces over the Brussels sprouts, making sure they are evenly distributed.
  5. Place the baking sheet in the preheated oven and roast for about 20-25 minutes, or until the Brussels sprouts are tender and lightly browned, and the bacon is crispy.
  6. Remove the baking sheet from the oven and let the Brussels sprouts cool for a few minutes before serving.

Serving Suggestions

Roasted Brussels Sprouts with Bacon can be served as a side dish to complement a variety of main courses. Here are a few serving suggestions to get you started:

  • Serve alongside roasted chicken or turkey for a hearty and satisfying meal.
  • Pair with grilled steak or pork chops for a delicious and flavorful combination.
  • Use as a topping for a fall-inspired salad, adding a burst of flavor and texture.

Tips and Tricks

  • Make sure to trim and halve the Brussels sprouts before roasting to ensure even cooking.
  • For extra crispy bacon, you can cook it separately and sprinkle it over the roasted Brussels sprouts just before serving.
  • Experiment with different seasonings to suit your taste. Add a pinch of chili flakes for a spicy kick or sprinkle with grated Parmesan cheese for a cheesy twist.
  • If you’re a vegetarian, you can omit the bacon and still enjoy delicious roasted Brussels sprouts. Simply increase the amount of olive oil and seasonings.

Why You’ll Love Roasted Brussels Sprouts with Bacon

There are plenty of reasons to fall in love with this tasty fall side dish. Here are just a few:

  1. Flavorful Combination: The combination of roasted Brussels sprouts and crispy bacon creates a perfect balance of flavors. The sweetness of the Brussels sprouts pairs beautifully with the savory, smoky bacon.
  2. Easy to Make: With just a few simple ingredients and minimal preparation, this dish is a breeze to make. It’s perfect for those busy weeknight dinners or when you want to impress your guests with minimal effort.
  3. Versatile: This dish can be served as a side to a variety of main courses or used as a topping for salads. It’s a versatile dish that can be enjoyed in many different ways.
  4. Nutritious: Brussels sprouts are packed with vitamins, minerals, and fiber, making them a nutritious addition to any meal. The addition of bacon adds protein and a burst of flavor.
  5. Perfect for Fall: As the weather cools down and the leaves change color, it’s the perfect time to enjoy the flavors of fall. Roasted Brussels Sprouts with Bacon captures the essence of the season and brings warmth and comfort to your table.

So, why wait? Grab some Brussels sprouts and bacon, and get ready to indulge in this delicious fall side dish. Your taste buds will thank you!
