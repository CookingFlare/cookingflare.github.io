Keto Zucchini Boats with Ricotta and Spinach: A Low-Carb and Healthy Appetizer


If you’re looking for a delicious and healthy appetizer that’s low in carbs and big on flavor, then these Keto Zucchini Boats with Ricotta and Spinach are just what you need. Packed with nutrients and bursting with savory goodness, this recipe is sure to become a staple in your low-carb cooking repertoire. Not only is it a great way to incorporate more vegetables into your diet, but it’s also a crowd-pleaser that will impress your guests at any gathering.

Ingredients

  • 4 medium zucchinis
  • 1 cup ricotta cheese
  • 1 cup chopped spinach
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon onion powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Olive oil for drizzling

Instructions

  1. Preheat the Oven: Start by preheating your oven to 400°F (200°C). This will ensure that your zucchini boats cook evenly and develop a delicious golden brown crust.

  2. Prepare the Zucchinis: Wash the zucchinis and slice them in half lengthwise. Using a spoon, gently scoop out the seeds and flesh from the center of each zucchini half, creating a hollow “boat” to hold the filling. Place the zucchini boats on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper.

  3. Make the Filling: In a mixing bowl, combine the ricotta cheese, chopped spinach, grated Parmesan cheese, garlic powder, onion powder, dried oregano, salt, and pepper. Mix everything together until well combined.

  4. Fill the Zucchini Boats: Spoon the ricotta and spinach filling into each zucchini boat, distributing it evenly among them. Don’t be afraid to fill them generously – the more filling, the better!

  5. Bake to Perfection: Drizzle the filled zucchini boats with a bit of olive oil for that extra touch of flavor and moisture. Place the baking sheet in the preheated oven and bake for 20-25 minutes, or until the zucchinis are tender and the filling is golden and bubbly.

  6. Serve and Enjoy: Once the zucchini boats are done, remove them from the oven and let them cool for a few minutes. Then, carefully transfer them to a serving platter and get ready to impress your taste buds and those of your guests.

Why This Recipe Rocks

  • Low-Carb Goodness: Zucchinis are naturally low in carbs, making them the perfect vehicle for a keto-friendly, guilt-free appetizer.
  • Nutrient-Packed: Spinach is a rich source of vitamins and minerals, while ricotta and Parmesan cheese provide a satisfying dose of protein and calcium.
  • Versatile and Customizable: Feel free to customize the filling with your favorite herbs, spices, or additional ingredients like diced bell peppers, mushrooms, or cooked ground meat for a heartier version.

In Conclusion

These Keto Zucchini Boats with Ricotta and Spinach are a delightful way to enjoy a low-carb, veggie-packed appetizer that doesn’t skimp on flavor. Whether you’re hosting a dinner party, looking for a light lunch, or simply craving a savory snack, this recipe is a winner. The combination of creamy ricotta, earthy spinach, and tender zucchini creates a symphony of flavors that will leave you wanting more. So, go ahead and give this recipe a try – your taste buds will thank you!

So, there you have it – a simple yet impressive dish that’s perfect for any occasion. We hope you enjoy making and savoring these delightful zucchini boats as much as we do!
