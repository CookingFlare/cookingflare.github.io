Flavorful Instant Pot Thai Curry Recipes for Exotic Flavors


If you’re looking to add a burst of exotic flavors to your dinner table, Thai curry is the perfect choice. The aromatic blend of herbs and spices, combined with creamy coconut milk, creates a symphony of tastes that will transport your taste buds to the streets of Thailand. And with the convenience of an Instant Pot, you can achieve these mouthwatering flavors in a fraction of the time. In this blog post, we’ll explore three delightful Instant Pot Thai curry recipes that are sure to tantalize your senses and impress your family and friends.

Instant Pot Red Curry with Chicken

Ingredients:

  • 1.5 lbs boneless, skinless chicken thighs, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 1 can (14 oz) coconut milk
  • 2-3 tbsp red curry paste
  • 1 red bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 onion, sliced
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1-inch piece of ginger, grated
  • 2 tbsp fish sauce
  • 1 tbsp brown sugar
  • 1 cup chicken broth
  • Fresh basil leaves for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Turn the Instant Pot to sauté mode and add the red curry paste. Cook for 1-2 minutes until fragrant.
  2. Add the chicken pieces and cook until lightly browned.
  3. Stir in the coconut milk, fish sauce, brown sugar, garlic, ginger, bell pepper, and onion.
  4. Pour in the chicken broth and give everything a good mix.
  5. Secure the lid and set the Instant Pot to high pressure for 8 minutes.
  6. Once the cooking cycle is complete, allow the pressure to release naturally for 10 minutes before doing a quick release.
  7. Serve the red curry over steamed jasmine rice and garnish with fresh basil leaves.

Instant Pot Green Curry with Tofu

Ingredients:

  • 14 oz firm tofu, cubed
  • 1 can (14 oz) coconut milk
  • 3-4 tbsp green curry paste
  • 1 cup bamboo shoots, drained
  • 1 cup snap peas
  • 1 red chili, sliced
  • 2-3 kaffir lime leaves
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp palm sugar
  • Fresh cilantro for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Press the sauté button on the Instant Pot and add the green curry paste. Sauté for 1-2 minutes.
  2. Gently stir in the cubed tofu, bamboo shoots, snap peas, red chili, kaffir lime leaves, coconut milk, soy sauce, and palm sugar.
  3. Close the lid and cook on high pressure for 5 minutes.
  4. Once done, perform a quick pressure release and carefully open the lid.
  5. Serve the green curry alongside fragrant jasmine rice and garnish with fresh cilantro.

Instant Pot Massaman Curry with Beef

Ingredients:

  • 1.5 lbs beef stew meat, cut into chunks
  • 1 can (14 oz) coconut milk
  • 3-4 tbsp massaman curry paste
  • 1 large potato, peeled and cubed
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 1 cup unsalted peanuts
  • 2 sticks cinnamon
  • 2 star anise
  • 2 tbsp tamarind paste
  • 1 tbsp brown sugar
  • Fresh basil for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Select the sauté setting on the Instant Pot and add the massaman curry paste. Sauté for a minute.
  2. Add the beef chunks and brown them on all sides.
  3. Pour in the coconut milk, tamarind paste, brown sugar, potato, onion, peanuts, cinnamon, and star anise.
  4. Close the lid and pressure cook for 35 minutes.
  5. Once the cooking is complete, let the pressure release naturally for 10 minutes before doing a quick release.
  6. Discard the cinnamon sticks and star anise, and serve the massaman curry with steamed rice. Garnish with fresh basil.

Conclusion

With these Instant Pot Thai curry recipes, you can bring the vibrant and tantalizing flavors of Thailand right to your dinner table. Whether you prefer the fiery kick of red curry, the fresh and herbaceous taste of green curry, or the rich and nutty notes of massaman curry, there’s a recipe here to satisfy every craving. Embrace the convenience of the Instant Pot and treat yourself to a culinary journey that will delight your senses and leave you craving more. So, fire up your Instant Pot and get ready to savor the exotic flavors of Thai curry!
