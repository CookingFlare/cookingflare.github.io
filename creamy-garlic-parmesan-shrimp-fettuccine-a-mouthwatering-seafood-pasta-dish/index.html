Creamy Garlic Parmesan Shrimp Fettuccine: A Mouthwatering Seafood Pasta Dish


If you’re a seafood lover and a fan of creamy pasta dishes, then this Creamy Garlic Parmesan Shrimp Fettuccine recipe is sure to tantalize your taste buds. This delectable dish combines succulent shrimp, al dente fettuccine, and a rich and creamy garlic parmesan sauce that is simply irresistible. Whether you’re looking to impress guests at a dinner party or simply craving a luxurious meal at home, this recipe is a must-try.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound (450g) fettuccine pasta
  • 1 pound (450g) large shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 4 tablespoons unsalted butter
  • 5 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 1/2 cups heavy cream
  • 1 cup freshly grated parmesan cheese
  • Salt and freshly ground black pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley, chopped, for garnish

Instructions

  1. Begin by bringing a large pot of salted water to a boil. Add the fettuccine pasta and cook according to the package instructions until al dente. Once cooked, drain the pasta and set it aside.

  2. In a large skillet, melt 2 tablespoons of butter over medium heat. Add the shrimp to the skillet and season with salt and black pepper. Cook the shrimp for 2-3 minutes on each side until they are pink and opaque. Remove the shrimp from the skillet and set them aside.

  3. In the same skillet, melt the remaining 2 tablespoons of butter. Add the minced garlic to the skillet and sauté for 1-2 minutes until fragrant.

  4. Pour the heavy cream into the skillet with the garlic and butter. Allow the cream to simmer gently, stirring occasionally, for about 5 minutes until it begins to thicken slightly.

  5. Gradually add the grated parmesan cheese to the cream sauce, stirring constantly until the cheese is fully melted and the sauce is smooth and creamy.

  6. Return the cooked shrimp to the skillet, tossing them in the creamy garlic parmesan sauce to coat evenly.

  7. Add the cooked fettuccine to the skillet, tossing it with the shrimp and sauce until the pasta is well coated.

  8. Season the dish with additional salt and black pepper to taste, and garnish with chopped fresh parsley for a pop of color and freshness.

  9. Serve the Creamy Garlic Parmesan Shrimp Fettuccine immediately, and enjoy the luxurious combination of flavors and textures.

Conclusion

Creamy Garlic Parmesan Shrimp Fettuccine is a sophisticated yet approachable dish that is perfect for a special occasion or a memorable family dinner. The succulent shrimp, velvety parmesan sauce, and tender fettuccine come together to create a culinary experience that is truly unforgettable. Whether you’re a seasoned home chef or a novice in the kitchen, this recipe is a great way to elevate your cooking skills and treat yourself to a restaurant-quality meal at home.

So, the next time you’re craving a mouthwatering seafood pasta dish, give this Creamy Garlic Parmesan Shrimp Fettuccine recipe a try and prepare to be amazed by the delightful flavors and aromas that will fill your kitchen. Cheers to indulging in the simple pleasures of good food and good company!
