Savory Onion and Herb Rolls Recipe


Are you looking to impress your guests at your next dinner party or simply craving a delightful homemade treat? Look no further than these delectable Savory Onion and Herb Rolls. These flavorful rolls are a perfect combination of savory onions, aromatic herbs, and soft, pillowy dough that will leave your taste buds singing. Let’s dive into the recipe and learn how to create these mouthwatering delights in your own kitchen.

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 packet instant yeast
  • 1/2 cup warm water
  • 1/4 cup milk
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 1 large onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon fresh thyme, chopped
  • 1 tablespoon fresh rosemary, chopped
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the Dough:

    • In a mixing bowl, combine the flour and instant yeast.
    • In a separate bowl, mix the warm water, milk, and butter until the butter melts.
    • Gradually add the wet ingredients to the dry ingredients and knead until a smooth dough forms.
    • Cover the dough and let it rise for about 1 hour until it doubles in size.

  2. Prepare the Filling:

    • In a pan, sauté the chopped onions and garlic until they are soft and translucent.
    • Add the fresh thyme, rosemary, salt, and pepper to the onion mixture and cook for another minute.
    • Remove the filling from heat and let it cool.

  3. Assemble the Rolls:

    • Roll out the dough into a rectangle on a floured surface.
    • Spread the cooled onion and herb filling evenly over the dough.
    • Roll the dough tightly from one end to the other to form a log.
    • Cut the log into equal-sized rolls and place them on a baking tray lined with parchment paper.

  4. Bake the Rolls:

    • Cover the rolls and let them rise for another 30 minutes.
    • Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
    • Bake the rolls for 20-25 minutes or until they are golden brown and cooked through.

  5. Serve and Enjoy:

    • Once baked, remove the rolls from the oven and let them cool slightly.
    • Serve these savory onion and herb rolls warm with a pat of butter on the side.

These Savory Onion and Herb Rolls are a perfect accompaniment to soups, salads, or enjoyed on their own as a delicious snack. The combination of caramelized onions, fragrant herbs, and soft bread is sure to elevate any meal. So, roll up your sleeves and get baking to treat yourself and your loved ones to these irresistible savory delights.

With just a few simple ingredients and easy steps, you can create a batch of these flavorful rolls that will have everyone asking for more. Whether you’re a seasoned baker or just starting in the kitchen, this recipe is bound to be a hit. So, why wait? Start baking and savor the deliciousness of these Savory Onion and Herb Rolls today!
