How to Make Instant Pot Chicken Parmesan in Just 30 Minutes


Looking for a quick and delicious dinner recipe that satisfies everyone at the table? Chicken Parmesan is a classic dish that combines tender chicken, tangy marinara sauce, gooey melted cheese, and a light crunch that makes it irresistible to both kids and adults alike. And when prepared using an Instant Pot, you can cut down on cook time without sacrificing flavor or texture. So, let’s dive into the step-by-step process for making Instant Pot Chicken Parmesan in just 30 minutes.

Why Instant Pot Chicken Parmesan is a Game Changer

Using the Instant Pot transforms this traditional recipe into a time-saving yet flavorful dish. The pressure cooker locks in moisture, ensuring the chicken remains juicy and tender while the marinara sauce infuses it with rich, savory flavor. Best of all, this method drastically reduces cleanup, making it an ideal choice for busy weeknights when you crave a comforting homemade meal without spending hours in the kitchen. Plus, since everything cooks in one pot, it’s an efficient way to streamline cooking without compromising the food quality.

Essential Ingredients for Instant Pot Chicken Parmesan

Before diving into the cooking process, gather all the ingredients. Here’s what you’ll need for this recipe:

Chicken

  • 2 large boneless, skinless chicken breasts (pounded to an even thickness or halved for quicker cooking)

Seasonings

  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon onion powder
  • 1 teaspoon Italian seasoning
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon black pepper

Coating

  • ⅔ cup breadcrumbs (regular or panko for added crunch)
  • ⅓ cup grated Parmesan cheese

Liquid Base

  • 1 cup chicken broth or water (essential for pressure cooking)
  • 1 jar (about 2 cups) marinara sauce

Optional Oil

  • 2 tablespoons olive oil (for sautéing, if desired for crispier edges)

Toppings

  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese (or slices for easier melting)
  • Extra Parmesan cheese for garnish
  • Chopped fresh parsley or basil (optional, for garnish)

Step-By-Step Instructions to Make Instant Pot Chicken Parmesan

Here’s how you can whip up this flavorful dish in just 30 minutes.

Step 1: Prep the Ingredients

Start by pounding the chicken breasts to an even thickness. This ensures they cook evenly without drying out. If you prefer smaller portions or want faster cooking, slice the chicken breast horizontally to create thinner pieces.

Mix the breadcrumbs and grated Parmesan cheese in a shallow bowl for the coating. In a separate small bowl, combine garlic powder, onion powder, Italian seasoning, salt, and pepper.

Rub the dry seasoning mix over both sides of the chicken breasts for an extra flavor boost. Then, dredge them in the breadcrumb-Parmesan mixture to coat them evenly.

Step 2: Sauté for Crispness (Optional)

If you love a crispy exterior for your chicken Parmesan, use the Instant Pot’s sauté function before pressure cooking. Add 2 tablespoons of olive oil to the pot and let it heat up.

Sauté the coated chicken for about 2 minutes on each side, just until golden brown. You don’t need to fully cook the chicken as the pressure cooker will handle that later. Remove the chicken and set it aside.

Step 3: Add the Liquid and Sauce

Next, pour 1 cup of chicken broth (or water) into the Instant Pot. This is an essential step as pressure cooking requires liquid to create steam and prevent burning.

Place a layer of marinara sauce (about half the jar) in the pot to enhance the flavor at the base. Arrange the chicken breasts evenly over the sauce layer, avoiding overlapping for better cooking. Pour the remaining marinara sauce over the chicken, coating it thoroughly.

Step 4: Pressure Cook

Secure the lid on your Instant Pot, ensuring the sealing ring is properly in place. Set the valve to “Sealing” and select the “Pressure Cook” or “Manual” setting. Adjust the timer to 7 minutes for halved/thin chicken breasts or 10 minutes for full-sized chicken breasts.

Once the cook time is complete, allow the pressure to naturally release for 5 minutes. After that, carefully switch the valve to “Venting” for quick release of any remaining pressure. Lift the lid when the pot is fully depressurized.

Step 5: Add Cheese and Broil

Now comes the magic. Sprinkle shredded mozzarella cheese generously over the cooked chicken. If your Instant Pot includes an air fryer lid, use it to broil the cheese for 3-5 minutes until bubbly and golden.

For those without an air fryer lid, simply transfer the cooked chicken to an oven-safe dish, sprinkle the topping cheese, and broil in the oven for 3-4 minutes. This step adds a delicious layer of gooeyness that makes Chicken Parmesan irresistible.

Step 6: Serve and Garnish

Carefully transfer the Instant Pot Chicken Parmesan to serving plates. Spoon some marinara sauce from the pot over each piece for an added burst of flavor.

Garnish with fresh parsley or basil for color and finish with an extra sprinkling of Parmesan cheese. Pair this dish with spaghetti, garlic bread, a side salad, or steamed veggies to complete your meal.

Tips for Making the Best Instant Pot Chicken Parmesan

  1. Even Cooking: If using thicker chicken breasts, slice or pound them to the same thickness to ensure they cook at the right pace.
  2. Extra Crunch: Add finely chopped fresh herbs (like parsley or thyme) to the breadcrumb mixture for added flavor depth.
  3. Storage: Leftovers can be refrigerated in an airtight container for up to 3 days. Reheat in the oven or microwave until warmed through.
  4. Customizations: Feel free to swap marinara sauce for a homemade tomato sauce or even spicy arrabbiata sauce for a kick. Alternatively, switch mozzarella for provolone if you prefer a heartier cheese topping.

Why You’ll Love This Recipe

This Instant Pot Chicken Parmesan recipe delivers all the rich flavors of the traditional dish with none of the hassle. Pressure cooking ensures the chicken is juicy and perfectly infused with marinara sauce, while broiling adds a restaurant-quality finish to the cheese topping. It’s a lifesaver for busy home cooks who want to prepare a wholesome, comforting meal in less than half an hour.

Final Thoughts

With its quick prep and delicious results, Instant Pot Chicken Parmesan is bound to become one of your favorite weeknight staples. It combines the efficiency of modern cooking methods with the timeless appeal of a classic Italian dish. Perfectly balanced, this dish packs protein, gooey cheese, and vibrant tomato flavors in each bite. Try this recipe tonight, and enjoy a meal that’s both easy to make and guaranteed to impress!
