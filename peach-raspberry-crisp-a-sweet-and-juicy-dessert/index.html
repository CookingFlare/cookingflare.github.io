Peach Raspberry Crisp: A Sweet and Juicy Dessert


If you’re looking for a delightful dessert that captures the essence of summer, look no further than Peach Raspberry Crisp. This mouthwatering treat combines the natural sweetness of ripe peaches with the tartness of fresh raspberries, all topped with a crispy, buttery crumble. Whether you’re hosting a dinner party or simply craving a cozy night in, this dessert is sure to impress and satisfy.

Ingredients

For the Filling:

  • 4 cups sliced fresh peaches
  • 2 cups fresh raspberries
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 2 tablespoons cornstarch
  • 1 tablespoon freshly squeezed lemon juice
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

For the Crisp Topping:

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup old-fashioned rolled oats
  • 1 cup brown sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, cold and cut into small pieces

Instructions

  1. Preheat the Oven: Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease a 9x13 inch baking dish or a similar-sized oven-safe dish.

  2. Prepare the Filling: In a large bowl, gently toss together the sliced peaches, raspberries, granulated sugar, cornstarch, lemon juice, and vanilla extract until the fruit is well coated. Transfer the fruit mixture into the prepared baking dish, spreading it out evenly.

  3. Make the Crisp Topping: In another bowl, combine the flour, oats, brown sugar, cinnamon, and salt. Add the cold butter pieces and use your fingers or a pastry cutter to blend the mixture until it resembles coarse crumbs. Sprinkle the crisp topping evenly over the fruit filling.

  4. Bake to Perfection: Place the dish in the preheated oven and bake for 45-50 minutes, or until the fruit is bubbly and the topping is golden brown.

  5. Serve and Enjoy: Allow the Peach Raspberry Crisp to cool for a few minutes before serving. This dessert pairs beautifully with a scoop of vanilla ice cream or a dollop of freshly whipped cream.

Tips for Success

  • Choose ripe, juicy peaches and fresh, plump raspberries for the best flavor and texture.
  • Adjust the sweetness of the dessert by adding more or less sugar to the fruit filling, depending on your preference and the natural sweetness of the fruit.
  • Experiment with different spices in the crisp topping, such as nutmeg or ginger, to add a unique twist to the flavor profile.

The Perfect Summer Treat

Peach Raspberry Crisp is a versatile dessert that can be enjoyed warm or at room temperature, making it an ideal choice for any occasion. The combination of sweet peaches and tangy raspberries, topped with a crunchy oat crumble, creates a symphony of flavors and textures that will leave your taste buds singing. Whether you’re a seasoned baker or just starting out, this recipe is simple to follow and yields impressive results.

Indulge in the goodness of summer fruits with this delightful Peach Raspberry Crisp recipe. It’s a dessert that celebrates the vibrant flavors of the season and brings a touch of sunshine to your table. So, roll up your sleeves, preheat your oven, and get ready to savor every spoonful of this sweet and juicy delight!
