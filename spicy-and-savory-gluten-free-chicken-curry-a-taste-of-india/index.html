Spicy and Savory Gluten-Free Chicken Curry: A Taste of India


Are you craving a delicious and flavorful dish that will transport your taste buds straight to the vibrant streets of India? Look no further! In this recipe, we will guide you through the steps of creating a Spicy and Savory Gluten-Free Chicken Curry that is sure to impress your family and friends.

Ingredients

  • 500g boneless chicken, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1 medium-sized onion, finely chopped
  • 3 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1-inch piece of ginger, grated
  • 2 green chilies, slit lengthwise
  • 2 tomatoes, pureed
  • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 2 teaspoons coriander powder
  • 1 teaspoon red chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon garam masala
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh cilantro leaves, chopped for garnish

Instructions

  1. Heat the vegetable oil in a large pan over medium heat. Add the cumin seeds and allow them to sizzle for a few seconds until fragrant.

  2. Add the chopped onions to the pan and sauté until they turn golden brown. This will take around 5-6 minutes.

  3. Next, add the minced garlic, grated ginger, and slit green chilies to the pan. Sauté for another 2-3 minutes until the raw smell of the ginger and garlic disappears.

  4. Now, it’s time to add the pureed tomatoes. Cook them for about 5 minutes until the oil starts to separate from the mixture.

  5. Reduce the heat to low and add the turmeric powder, coriander powder, red chili powder, and salt. Mix well and cook for another 2-3 minutes, allowing the spices to release their flavors.

  6. Add the chicken pieces to the pan and coat them well with the spice mixture. Cook for 5 minutes, stirring occasionally, until the chicken is sealed and slightly browned.

  7. Pour in enough water to cover the chicken and bring the mixture to a boil. Once it starts boiling, reduce the heat to low, cover the pan, and let it simmer for about 20 minutes, or until the chicken is cooked through and tender.

  8. Sprinkle the garam masala over the curry and give it a good stir. Allow it to simmer for another 2-3 minutes to let the flavors meld together.

  9. Remove the pan from heat and garnish the curry with freshly chopped cilantro leaves.

  10. Serve the Spicy and Savory Gluten-Free Chicken Curry hot with steamed rice, naan bread, or roti. The choice is yours!

Conclusion

In just a few simple steps, you have created a Spicy and Savory Gluten-Free Chicken Curry that is bursting with the authentic flavors of India. The combination of spices, tender chicken, and aromatic herbs will leave your taste buds craving for more. This dish is not only delicious but also gluten-free, making it a perfect option for those with dietary restrictions.

So, the next time you’re in the mood for a taste of India, whip up this flavorful curry and transport yourself to the bustling streets of this vibrant country. Enjoy the rich and aromatic flavors of this gluten-free chicken curry, and let it take you on a culinary journey you won’t forget. Happy cooking!
