Simple Chinese Steamed Pork Patty Recipe


Chinese cuisine is rich with a variety of flavors, textures, and techniques. Among the many beloved dishes, steamed pork patties stand out for their simplicity, tenderness, and flavorful combination of ingredients. This dish, known as 蒸猪肉饼 (zhēng zhūròu bǐng), is a classic comfort food that is both delicious and easy to prepare. The tender pork, infused with aromatic seasonings, melts in your mouth while remaining juicy and full of flavor.

In this post, we will guide you step by step through making a traditional Chinese steamed pork patty. Whether you’re a beginner or a seasoned cook, this recipe is approachable and yields impressive results. Let’s dive in!

Ingredients

To prepare this simple Chinese steamed pork patty, you will need the following ingredients:

  • Ground Pork (500g): Fresh ground pork is the base of this dish, and it’s essential to use meat with a bit of fat to keep the patties tender and juicy.
  • Water Chestnuts (50g): These crunchy vegetables add texture and a refreshing sweetness to the dish.
  • Ginger (1-inch piece, minced): Adds a fragrant, warm flavor that complements the pork.
  • Garlic (2 cloves, minced): Garlic infuses the pork with a savory, aromatic depth.
  • Shaoxing Wine (1 tablespoon): A traditional Chinese cooking wine that adds complexity and enhances the flavors of the dish.
  • Soy Sauce (2 tablespoons): This will provide a salty, umami-rich flavor.
  • Oyster Sauce (1 tablespoon): Adds a layer of savory, slightly sweet flavor.
  • Sesame Oil (1 teaspoon): A small amount of sesame oil will lend a toasted, nutty flavor to the pork.
  • Sugar (1 teaspoon): A pinch of sugar balances the savory components and rounds out the dish.
  • Cornstarch (1 tablespoon): This helps to bind the ingredients together and ensures the patty stays moist during steaming.
  • Green Onions (2 stalks, chopped): A garnish for both color and freshness.
  • Salt (to taste): Essential to enhance the flavors of the pork and other ingredients.
  • Black Pepper (to taste): A subtle heat to contrast the richness of the pork.

Optional Ingredients:

  • Shiitake Mushrooms (a few, finely chopped): If you want to elevate the flavor and texture of the patties, shiitake mushrooms work wonderfully with pork.
  • Chinese Five-Spice Powder (1/2 teaspoon): A pinch of this aromatic spice blend will enhance the fragrance and depth of the dish.

Preparation

Making steamed pork patties might seem daunting, but it’s actually quite straightforward. The key to success lies in mixing the ingredients properly and steaming them until they’re perfectly cooked. Here’s a step-by-step guide to help you make simple Chinese steamed pork patties.

1. Prepare the Ingredients

Start by gathering all your ingredients. Ensure your ground pork is fresh and has a bit of fat content. The fat in the pork will give the patties the tenderness they need to remain juicy and soft after steaming.

Next, finely chop the water chestnuts, ginger, and garlic. The water chestnuts will add a crunch, while the ginger and garlic will infuse the pork with a beautiful fragrance. Chop the green onions as well, saving a small portion for garnishing later.

If you are using shiitake mushrooms or Chinese five-spice powder, prepare them now too.

2. Mix the Pork Patty Filling

In a large bowl, combine the ground pork with all the prepared ingredients: water chestnuts, ginger, garlic, soy sauce, oyster sauce, Shaoxing wine, sesame oil, sugar, cornstarch, salt, and pepper. Mix well with your hands or a spoon until the ingredients are evenly distributed. You want to ensure that the pork mixture becomes smooth and slightly sticky, as this will help the patties hold together during steaming.

If you’re using shiitake mushrooms or Chinese five-spice powder, add them to the mix at this stage.

3. Shape the Pork Patties

Once the mixture is thoroughly combined, it’s time to shape the patties. You can either form them into small, round patties or make them into flat, round cakes, depending on your preference.

To shape the patties, wet your hands with a bit of water to prevent the pork from sticking to your skin. Scoop a generous amount of the mixture and mold it into a smooth, round patty about 1 inch thick. If you want, you can make multiple smaller patties or one large one—this will depend on the size of your steaming dish.

4. Prepare the Steaming Setup

To steam the pork patties, you will need a steaming setup. This can be done using a steamer basket, steamer rack, or a bamboo steamer. If you don’t have a bamboo steamer, you can also use a heatproof dish or a ceramic plate that fits comfortably inside a large pot.

Fill the pot with about 1-2 inches of water, making sure that the water doesn’t touch the patties when they’re placed inside the steamer. Bring the water to a gentle simmer over medium heat.

If you are using a bamboo steamer, line it with a piece of parchment paper or lettuce leaves to prevent the patties from sticking.

5. Steam the Pork Patties

Place the shaped pork patties onto the steaming rack or plate. Arrange them in a single layer with a little space between each patty to allow the steam to circulate evenly.

Cover the pot with a lid and steam the patties for about 20-25 minutes. You’ll know they are done when they turn a pale, golden brown color, and the juices run clear when pierced with a fork. The pork should be fully cooked, tender, and juicy.

6. Garnish and Serve

Once the patties are steamed to perfection, carefully remove them from the steamer. Garnish with chopped green onions for a fresh and vibrant finish. If you like, you can drizzle a bit more sesame oil or soy sauce over the top for added flavor.

Serve your simple Chinese steamed pork patties with steamed rice, sautéed vegetables, or a side of pickled vegetables. This dish pairs wonderfully with a bowl of hot jasmine rice or noodles, allowing you to soak up the savory juices from the pork.

Tips for the Best Steamed Pork Patties

While this recipe is quite simple, there are a few tips that will help you make the best Chinese steamed pork patties possible.

1. Choose the Right Pork

The key to juicy, tender patties is using ground pork that has enough fat content. Pork with about 20% fat will give you the right balance between tenderness and flavor. Lean pork can result in dry, tough patties, so don’t skimp on the fat!

2. Don’t Overmix the Pork

Mixing the pork too vigorously can make the texture of the patties tough. Stir the mixture just enough to combine the ingredients, ensuring that it’s smooth but not overly compacted.

3. Adjust the Seasonings to Taste

While the recipe provides a great foundation of flavors, feel free to adjust the seasonings according to your personal taste. If you prefer a sweeter version, add a little extra sugar or hoisin sauce. If you like more heat, you can include a small amount of chopped fresh chili peppers.

4. Experiment with Additional Ingredients

The beauty of Chinese steamed pork patties is that you can customize the flavors. If you want to make them more flavorful, consider adding finely chopped mushrooms, bamboo shoots, or even a touch of Chinese five-spice powder. Each ingredient will bring something unique to the table.

Variations of Chinese Steamed Pork Patties

While the basic recipe for steamed pork patties is already delicious, here are some variations you can try for a bit of culinary creativity:

1. Pork and Shrimp Patties

For a surf-and-turf twist, you can mix finely chopped shrimp with the ground pork. This adds a sweet, delicate flavor to the patties, and the shrimp provides an interesting texture.

2. Spicy Pork Patties

If you like your food with a bit of spice, try adding some chopped chili peppers or chili paste to the pork mixture. A few dashes of hot sauce can also be added to bring some heat to the dish.

3. Mushroom and Pork Patties

For a rich, earthy flavor, consider incorporating finely chopped mushrooms, such as shiitake, into the pork mixture. The mushrooms add moisture and depth to the patties, making them extra flavorful.

Final Thoughts

Chinese steamed pork patties are an easy yet flavorful dish that brings comfort and satisfaction with every bite. Their tender texture, aromatic seasonings, and savory flavor make them a great addition to any meal. Whether you’re a beginner or an experienced cook, this simple recipe will allow you to enjoy a delicious, traditional Chinese dish in no time.

This dish is incredibly versatile and can be served with a variety of side dishes, making it an excellent choice for both weeknight dinners and special occasions. The gentle steaming process locks in the flavors, resulting in tender, juicy pork patties that are a true delight.

Give this simple Chinese steamed pork patty recipe a try, and enjoy a taste of authentic Chinese home cooking right in your own kitchen!
