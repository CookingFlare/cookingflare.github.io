Creamy Chicken and Dumplings Stew: A Comforting Classic


There’s nothing quite like a warm, comforting bowl of creamy chicken and dumplings stew to soothe the soul and satisfy the appetite. This classic dish is a favorite in many households, and for good reason – it’s hearty, flavorful, and perfect for those chilly evenings when you need a little extra warmth. In this recipe, we’ll walk you through the steps to create your own creamy chicken and dumplings stew that will have your family and friends coming back for seconds.

Ingredients

For the Stew:

  • 2 pounds of boneless, skinless chicken breasts, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 1 large onion, finely chopped
  • 3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 4 carrots, peeled and sliced
  • 4 celery stalks, sliced
  • 1 teaspoon of dried thyme
  • 1 teaspoon of dried rosemary
  • 6 cups of chicken broth
  • 1 cup of heavy cream
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 tablespoons of olive oil

For the Dumplings:

  • 2 cups of all-purpose flour
  • 1 tablespoon of baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon of salt
  • 1/4 cup of unsalted butter, cold and cut into small pieces
  • 3/4 cup of milk

Instructions

  1. In a large pot or Dutch oven, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the chopped onion and cook until softened, about 5 minutes. Add the minced garlic and cook for an additional 1-2 minutes.

  2. Season the bite-sized chicken pieces with salt and pepper, then add them to the pot. Cook until the chicken is no longer pink on the outside, about 5-7 minutes.

  3. Add the sliced carrots and celery to the pot, stirring to combine with the chicken and onions. Cook for a few minutes until the vegetables begin to soften.

  4. Sprinkle in the dried thyme and rosemary, then pour in the chicken broth. Bring the mixture to a simmer and let it cook for about 20-25 minutes, or until the chicken is fully cooked and the vegetables are tender.

  5. In the meantime, prepare the dumplings. In a mixing bowl, combine the flour, baking powder, and salt. Add the cold, cubed butter and use a pastry cutter or fork to cut the butter into the flour mixture until it resembles coarse crumbs. Pour in the milk and stir until just combined.

  6. Once the stew is ready, pour in the heavy cream and stir to combine. Season with additional salt and pepper to taste.

  7. Using a spoon, drop spoonfuls of the dumpling batter into the simmering stew. Cover the pot with a lid and let the dumplings cook for about 15 minutes, or until they are cooked through and fluffy.

  8. Once the dumplings are cooked, ladle the creamy chicken and dumplings stew into bowls and serve hot. Garnish with fresh parsley if desired.

Conclusion

Creamy chicken and dumplings stew is a timeless classic that brings warmth and comfort to any table. With tender pieces of chicken, hearty vegetables, and fluffy dumplings in a creamy broth, it’s a dish that’s sure to please everyone. Whether it’s a weeknight dinner or a gathering with loved ones, this recipe is a go-to for those seeking a satisfying and delicious meal. So, grab your ingredients and get ready to enjoy a bowl of this comforting classic!
