Vegan Chocolate Dipped Almond Biscotti: Crunchy and Delicious


If you’re on the hunt for a delectable treat that pairs perfectly with your morning coffee or afternoon tea, look no further than vegan chocolate-dipped almond biscotti. These twice-baked, Italian-inspired cookies offer a balance of crunch, nuttiness, and rich chocolate indulgence—all without using any animal-derived ingredients. Whether you’re planning to serve them as festive gifts, enjoy them as a daily snack, or prepare them for a dinner party, this recipe is sure to impress.

The Appeal of Biscotti

Biscotti have been savored for generations as a distinctive dessert due to their firm and crunchy texture, designed specifically for dunking. Traditionally crafted with eggs and butter, this vegan alternative swaps out animal-based ingredients for plant-based substitutes while maintaining the iconic bite that biscotti are known for. Adding almond slices provides a nutty depth of flavor, while the decadent dark chocolate coating takes these treats to a whole new level.

Creating vegan biscotti may sound like a challenge, but it’s surprisingly straightforward, even for novice bakers. By using simple substitutions and wholesome ingredients, you can prepare a batch of these chocolaty almond delights that will satisfy cravings and leave guests asking for the recipe.

Ingredients Needed for Vegan Biscotti

The beauty of this recipe lies in its simplicity and versatility. You’ll likely have many of the ingredients in your pantry already. Here’s what you’ll need:

For the Biscotti:

  • 2 ½ cups (310 grams) all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • ¼ teaspoon baking soda
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • ¾ cup (150 grams) granulated sugar
  • ½ cup (120 ml) neutral vegetable oil (such as sunflower or canola)
  • ¼ cup (60 ml) plant-based milk (almond or soy works best)
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • ½ teaspoon almond extract
  • ¾ cup sliced or slivered almonds

For the Chocolate Coating:

  • 1 cup (175 grams) vegan dark chocolate chips or chopped chocolate
  • 1 tablespoon coconut oil (optional, for smoother melting)

Step-by-Step Preparation

Step 1: Preheat Your Oven and Prepare Your Baking Sheet

Start by preheating your oven to 350°F (175°C). Line a large baking sheet with parchment paper or a silicone baking mat to prevent the biscotti from sticking. The parchment also makes cleanup effortless, which is always a bonus.

Step 2: Prepare the Dough

In a large mixing bowl, whisk together the dry ingredients: flour, baking powder, baking soda, and salt. This lays down the foundation for your biscotti’s structure.

In a separate bowl, combine the sugar, vegetable oil, plant-based milk, vanilla extract, and almond extract. Whisk vigorously until the mixture is smooth and well-integrated. Slowly add the wet ingredients into the bowl with the dry ingredients, stirring until it forms a cohesive but slightly sticky dough. As an important step, fold in the sliced or slivered almonds for that distinct crunch and nutty essence.

Step 3: Shape and Bake the First Time

Transfer the dough onto a lightly floured surface and shape it into a rectangular log about 10 inches long and 3 inches wide. Flatten the top slightly to ensure even baking. Place the log onto your prepared baking sheet and bake for 25–30 minutes, or until it’s firm to the touch and lightly golden.

Once baked, remove the log from the oven and allow it to cool for 15 minutes. Cooling is crucial to ensure the biscotti retain their structure when you slice them.

Step 4: Slice and Bake Again

Using a sharp serrated knife, slice the log diagonally into ¾-inch thick biscotti pieces. The diagonal cut not only gives the traditional biscotti shape but also makes them perfect for dunking. Place the slices cut-side down back onto the baking sheet.

Bake the biscotti again at 325°F (160°C) for 10–12 minutes, then flip them over and bake for another 8–10 minutes. This double-baking process is what gives biscotti their distinctive crunch. Remove the biscotti from the oven and let them cool completely before proceeding to the next step.

Step 5: Melt the Chocolate

While the biscotti cool, prepare the chocolate for dipping. In a heatproof bowl, melt the vegan dark chocolate and coconut oil (if using) using a double boiler or microwave. Stir frequently to ensure the chocolate melts evenly and reaches a smooth consistency.

Step 6: Dip and Decorate

Once the biscotti have fully cooled, dip one end of each piece into the melted chocolate. You can dip halfway for a striking visual effect or cover one complete side for a richer bite. Lay the dipped biscotti on a wire rack or parchment paper until the chocolate solidifies. If you’d like to add flair, sprinkle finely chopped almonds or flaky sea salt onto the chocolate before it sets.

Step 7: Storage

Once the chocolate is firm, transfer the biscotti to an airtight container. They’ll stay fresh for up to two weeks at room temperature, making them the perfect treat to prepare in advance.

Expert Tips for Perfect Vegan Biscotti

  • Choose High-Quality Chocolate: Opt for vegan dark chocolate with at least 70% cocoa for a deep, rich flavor.
  • Monitor Baking Times: Precise baking is critical for achieving the right texture. Overbaking can make them overly hard, while underbaking can lead to a softer, less authentic biscotti.
  • Experiment with Add-Ins: This recipe is highly adaptable. Mix in dried fruit, such as cranberries or apricots, or add spices like cinnamon or cardamom to customize the flavor profile.
  • Serve Creatively: Pair biscotti with coffee, tea, or even a glass of almond milk for a delightful snack or dessert spread.

Why You’ll Love This Recipe

Vegan chocolate-dipped almond biscotti strike the perfect balance between indulgence and comfort. They elevate the experience of everyday snacks with a crunch and sweetness that’s hard to beat. Each bite delivers an irresistible blend of nutty almonds and rich dark chocolate, topped off with the satisfaction of knowing they’re completely plant-based.

Plus, these biscotti are fun to make! From shaping the dough to dipping them in chocolate, this recipe offers both creativity and simplicity. It’s a sure way to bring a little joy to your day, whether you’re baking for yourself or loved ones.

Make a batch today and savor the luxury of homemade biscotti. One bite, and you’ll see why this timeless treat deserves a place in your kitchen—and your heart!
