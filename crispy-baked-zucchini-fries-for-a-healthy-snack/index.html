Crispy Baked Zucchini Fries for a Healthy Snack


Are you craving a tasty and satisfying snack that won’t derail your healthy eating goals? Look no further than these Crispy Baked Zucchini Fries! Packed with flavor and crunch, this recipe offers a guilt-free alternative to traditional fries. Plus, it’s a great way to sneak in some extra veggies into your diet. So, let’s dive right in and learn how to make these delicious zucchini fries.

Ingredients

To make these Crispy Baked Zucchini Fries, you will need the following ingredients:

  • 2 medium-sized zucchinis
  • 1 cup of breadcrumbs (you can use whole wheat for added nutrition)
  • 1/2 cup of grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 teaspoon of dried Italian seasoning
  • 1/2 teaspoon of garlic powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon of paprika
  • 2 eggs
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 425°F (220°C) and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
  2. Wash the zucchinis thoroughly and cut them into thin, fry-like strips. It’s up to you whether you want to leave the skin on or peel it off.
  3. In a shallow bowl, whisk the eggs until well beaten.
  4. In a separate bowl, combine the breadcrumbs, grated Parmesan cheese, dried Italian seasoning, garlic powder, paprika, salt, and pepper. Mix well.
  5. Dip each zucchini strip into the beaten eggs, allowing any excess to drip off.
  6. Next, coat the zucchini strip with the breadcrumb mixture, making sure to evenly cover all sides.
  7. Place the coated zucchini strips onto the prepared baking sheet, leaving some space between each strip.
  8. Bake the zucchini fries in the preheated oven for about 20-25 minutes, or until they turn golden brown and crispy. Remember to flip them halfway through the cooking time to ensure even browning.
  9. Once done, remove the zucchini fries from the oven and let them cool slightly before serving.
  10. Serve your Crispy Baked Zucchini Fries with your favorite dipping sauce, such as marinara, ranch, or tzatziki. Enjoy!

Benefits of Zucchini Fries

These Crispy Baked Zucchini Fries are not only delicious but also offer several health benefits. Zucchinis are low in calories and high in fiber, making them a great choice for weight management. They are also rich in vitamins A and C, potassium, and antioxidants, which support a healthy immune system and aid in digestion.

By baking the zucchini fries instead of frying them, you significantly reduce the amount of unhealthy fats and calories. This makes them a healthier alternative to traditional french fries or other deep-fried snacks. Plus, the crispy coating provides a satisfying crunch without the greasy aftermath.

Conclusion

If you’re looking for a healthy and flavorful snack, Crispy Baked Zucchini Fries are a must-try. With their crunchy exterior and tender interior, they are sure to satisfy your craving for something savory. Whether you enjoy them on their own or paired with a dipping sauce, these zucchini fries are a guilt-free treat that won’t disappoint.

So, why not give this recipe a try? Your taste buds and your body will thank you. Get creative with your seasonings or experiment with different dipping sauces to customize this snack to your liking. Enjoy the deliciousness of zucchini fries without any guilt!
