Creamy Garlic Parmesan Bacon Pasta: A Decadent and Flavorful Dish


Are you ready to indulge in a dish that will leave your taste buds begging for more? Look no further than this creamy garlic parmesan bacon pasta recipe. With its velvety sauce, savory bacon, and rich flavors, this dish is sure to satisfy even the most discerning palates. So, roll up your sleeves, grab your apron, and let’s get cooking!

Ingredients

  • 8 ounces of penne pasta
  • 6 slices of bacon, diced
  • 4 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 cup of heavy cream
  • 1/2 cup of grated parmesan cheese
  • 1/4 cup of chopped fresh parsley
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Cook the penne pasta according to the package instructions until al dente. Drain and set aside.

  2. In a large skillet, cook the diced bacon over medium heat until crispy. Using a slotted spoon, remove the bacon from the skillet and set it aside on a paper towel-lined plate to drain excess grease.

  3. In the same skillet, discard all but 2 tablespoons of the bacon grease. Add the minced garlic to the skillet and sauté for about 1 minute, until fragrant.

  4. Reduce the heat to low and add the heavy cream to the skillet. Stir well to combine with the garlic, allowing the flavors to meld together.

  5. Slowly sprinkle the grated parmesan cheese into the skillet, stirring constantly to ensure it melts evenly into the sauce.

  6. Once the sauce has thickened slightly, add the cooked penne pasta to the skillet, tossing gently to coat each noodle with the creamy sauce.

  7. Stir in the crispy bacon and chopped fresh parsley, reserving some parsley for garnish.

  8. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Remember, the bacon and parmesan cheese already add a certain level of saltiness, so adjust accordingly.

  9. Remove the skillet from the heat and let it sit for a few minutes to allow the flavors to fully develop.

  10. Serve the creamy garlic parmesan bacon pasta in bowls, garnished with a sprinkle of fresh parsley.

Tips and Tricks

  • For a twist on the classic recipe, try adding sautéed mushrooms or sun-dried tomatoes to the dish. They will add an extra layer of flavor and texture to the pasta.
  • If you prefer a lighter version of this dish, you can substitute half-and-half or whole milk for the heavy cream. However, keep in mind that the sauce may not be as rich and creamy.
  • To make this recipe vegetarian-friendly, simply omit the bacon and use vegetable broth or water to deglaze the skillet after sautéing the garlic.
  • This dish pairs perfectly with a crisp green salad and a glass of your favorite white wine. The freshness of the salad and the acidity of the wine will help balance out the richness of the pasta.

Now that you have the recipe for this decadent and flavorful dish, it’s time to gather your ingredients and get cooking. Whether you’re preparing a romantic dinner for two or hosting a dinner party, this creamy garlic parmesan bacon pasta is guaranteed to impress. So, go ahead and take a culinary adventure with this indulgent recipe that will leave you and your guests craving seconds. Enjoy!
