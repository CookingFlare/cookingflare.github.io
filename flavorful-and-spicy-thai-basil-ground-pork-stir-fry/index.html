Flavorful and Spicy: Thai Basil Ground Pork Stir-Fry


Craving a quick and delicious meal that’s packed with bold flavors? Look no further than this Flavorful and Spicy Thai Basil Ground Pork Stir-Fry! Bursting with aromatic Thai basil, tender ground pork, and a medley of vibrant vegetables, this dish is sure to satisfy your taste buds. Plus, it’s easy to make and can be ready in just under 30 minutes. So, roll up your sleeves and let’s dive into this tantalizing recipe!

Ingredients

  • 500g ground pork
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 Thai chili peppers, sliced (adjust to your spice preference)
  • 1 red bell pepper, thinly sliced
  • 1 yellow bell pepper, thinly sliced
  • 1 medium onion, thinly sliced
  • 1 cup fresh Thai basil leaves
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon oyster sauce
  • 1 tablespoon fish sauce
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • Freshly ground black pepper, to taste

Instructions

  1. Heat a large skillet or wok over medium-high heat. Add the vegetable oil and swirl to coat the pan evenly.

  2. Add the minced garlic and sliced Thai chili peppers to the hot oil. Stir-fry for about 30 seconds until fragrant, being careful not to burn the garlic.

  3. Add the ground pork to the skillet and break it up into small crumbles using a spatula or wooden spoon. Cook until the pork is browned and cooked through, approximately 5-7 minutes.

  4. Push the cooked ground pork to one side of the skillet, creating a space in the center. Add the sliced bell peppers and onion to the center of the skillet and stir-fry for 2-3 minutes until they start to soften.

  5. In a small bowl, combine the soy sauce, oyster sauce, fish sauce, and sugar. Stir well to dissolve the sugar.

  6. Pour the sauce mixture over the pork, bell peppers, and onion. Stir everything together until well coated.

  7. Add the fresh Thai basil leaves to the skillet and continue stir-frying for an additional 1-2 minutes until the basil leaves wilt.

  8. Season with freshly ground black pepper to taste. Remember that the sauces already contain saltiness, so adjust the seasoning accordingly.

  9. Remove the skillet from the heat and transfer the stir-fry to a serving dish.

  10. Serve the Thai Basil Ground Pork Stir-Fry over steamed jasmine rice or with a side of noodles for a complete meal. Enjoy the explosion of flavors!

Tips and Variations

  • For a vegetarian option, replace the ground pork with crumbled tofu or seitan.
  • Feel free to add additional vegetables like snap peas, broccoli, or mushrooms to the stir-fry for extra color and nutrients.
  • Adjust the spiciness of the dish by increasing or decreasing the number of Thai chili peppers.
  • If you can’t find Thai basil, regular basil can be used as a substitute, although it will have a slightly different flavor profile.
  • Leftovers can be stored in an airtight container in the refrigerator for up to 3 days. The flavors tend to meld together even more, making it even more delicious the next day.

Conclusion

There you have it, a Flavorful and Spicy Thai Basil Ground Pork Stir-Fry recipe that is sure to become a family favorite. With its aromatic blend of Thai basil and tender ground pork, this dish will transport you to the vibrant streets of Thailand. Don’t be afraid to spice it up and make it your own by adding your favorite vegetables or adjusting the heat level. So, grab your apron and get cooking - a world of bold flavors awaits!
