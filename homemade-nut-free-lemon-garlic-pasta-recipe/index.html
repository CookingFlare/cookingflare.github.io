Homemade Nut-Free Lemon Garlic Pasta Recipe


Are you tired of the same old pasta dishes? Looking for a fresh twist to add some zest to your meals? Look no further! Today, I’m sharing with you a delicious and easy-to-make Homemade Nut-Free Lemon Garlic Pasta recipe that is sure to become a family favorite.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound of your favorite pasta (such as spaghetti or linguine)
  • 4 tablespoons of unsalted butter
  • 4 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 lemon, zest and juice
  • 1 cup of heavy cream
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley, chopped (for garnish)

Instructions

  1. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil and cook the pasta according to the package instructions until al dente. Drain and set aside.

  2. In a large skillet, melt the butter over medium heat. Add the minced garlic and cook until fragrant, about 1-2 minutes.

  3. Add the lemon zest and juice to the skillet, stirring to combine. Allow the flavors to meld together for about a minute.

  4. Pour in the heavy cream and season with salt and pepper. Stir well to combine and bring the mixture to a simmer. Let it cook for about 2-3 minutes, until the sauce thickens slightly.

  5. Add the cooked pasta to the skillet and toss until each strand is coated with the creamy lemon garlic sauce. Cook for an additional 2-3 minutes, allowing the pasta to absorb the flavors.

  6. Remove from heat and garnish with freshly chopped parsley for an added burst of freshness.

  7. Serve immediately and enjoy your homemade nut-free lemon garlic pasta!

Why This Recipe is a Winner

  1. Nut-Free: This recipe is perfect for individuals with nut allergies or dietary restrictions, as it does not contain any nuts or nut products. You can confidently serve this dish to friends and family without worrying about allergic reactions.

  2. Burst of Citrus Flavor: The combination of fresh lemon zest and juice adds a vibrant and refreshing twist to the traditional garlic pasta. The citrus flavor adds brightness and balance to the creamy sauce, making each bite a delightful experience.

  3. Quick and Easy: With just a handful of ingredients, this recipe comes together in no time. It’s perfect for those busy weeknights when you need a satisfying meal without spending hours in the kitchen.

  4. Versatile: Feel free to customize this recipe to suit your taste preferences. You can add grilled chicken or shrimp for an extra protein boost, or toss in some sautéed vegetables like spinach or cherry tomatoes for added color and nutrients.

  5. Family-Friendly: This nut-free lemon garlic pasta is a crowd-pleaser, loved by both kids and adults alike. The creamy sauce and tangy lemon flavor appeal to a wide range of palates, making it a go-to recipe for family dinners or potlucks.

So, there you have it – a homemade nut-free lemon garlic pasta recipe that is sure to impress. Give it a try and let your taste buds dance with delight. Happy cooking!

Note: Remember to always double-check ingredient labels to ensure they are nut-free, as some products may have been processed in facilities that handle nuts.
