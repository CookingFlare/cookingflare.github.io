Tasty Bacon-Wrapped BBQ Chicken Bites


If you’re looking for a crowd-pleasing appetizer that combines the smoky goodness of bacon with the tangy flavors of barbecue sauce, then you’re in luck! These Tasty Bacon-Wrapped BBQ Chicken Bites are sure to impress your guests and leave them craving for more. With the perfect balance of sweet and savory, these bite-sized treats are a must-try for any BBQ lover.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 12 slices of bacon
  • 1/2 cup barbecue sauce
  • 2 tablespoons honey
  • 1 tablespoon Dijon mustard
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon smoked paprika
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Toothpicks for securing the bacon

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C) and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.

  2. Cut the chicken breasts into bite-sized cubes, ensuring they are all roughly the same size. Season the chicken with salt, pepper, garlic powder, and smoked paprika. Toss the chicken to evenly coat it in the seasonings.

  3. In a small bowl, whisk together the barbecue sauce, honey, and Dijon mustard until well combined. Set aside.

  4. Take a slice of bacon and wrap it tightly around a cube of seasoned chicken. Secure the bacon with a toothpick, making sure it goes through both ends of the bacon to hold it in place. Repeat this process with the remaining chicken and bacon.

  5. Place the bacon-wrapped chicken bites onto the prepared baking sheet, leaving some space between each bite to ensure even cooking.

  6. Brush each bacon-wrapped chicken bite generously with the barbecue sauce mixture, making sure to coat all sides.

  7. Place the baking sheet in the preheated oven and bake for 20-25 minutes, or until the bacon is crispy and the chicken is cooked through, with an internal temperature of 165°F (74°C).

  8. Once cooked, remove the bacon-wrapped chicken bites from the oven and let them cool slightly. Serve them warm with extra barbecue sauce for dipping, if desired.

Tips and Variations

  • For an extra kick of flavor, try adding a sprinkle of cayenne pepper or chili powder to the seasoning mix.
  • If you prefer a smokier taste, substitute the regular paprika with smoked paprika.
  • These bacon-wrapped chicken bites can be prepared in advance and refrigerated until ready to bake. Simply follow the recipe until step 6, then cover and refrigerate for up to 24 hours. When ready to serve, remove from the refrigerator, brush with the barbecue sauce, and bake as directed.
  • If you don’t have toothpicks on hand, you can secure the bacon with a piece of kitchen twine or simply place the seam side down on the baking sheet.

Conclusion

These Tasty Bacon-Wrapped BBQ Chicken Bites are the perfect combination of crispy bacon, juicy chicken, and tangy barbecue sauce. They make for a delicious appetizer or party snack that is sure to be a hit at any gathering. With their irresistible flavors and easy preparation, these bites are a must-try for any bacon and BBQ lover. So fire up your oven, grab some toothpicks, and get ready to indulge in a mouthwatering treat that will leave your taste buds craving for more!
