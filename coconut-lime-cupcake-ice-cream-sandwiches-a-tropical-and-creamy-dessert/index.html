Coconut Lime Cupcake Ice Cream Sandwiches: A Tropical and Creamy Dessert


Indulge in a taste of the tropics with our delicious Coconut Lime Cupcake Ice Cream Sandwiches. These cool and creamy treats combine the refreshing flavors of coconut and lime with the delightful sweetness of cupcakes and ice cream. Perfect for a hot summer day or any time you’re craving a tropical dessert, this recipe is sure to be a hit with friends and family.

Ingredients

For the Cupcakes:

  • 1 ½ cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 ½ teaspoons baking powder
  • ¼ teaspoon salt
  • ½ cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • ¾ cup coconut milk
  • Zest of 1 lime

For the Coconut Lime Ice Cream:

  • 2 cups heavy cream
  • 1 cup coconut milk
  • ¾ cup granulated sugar
  • Zest of 2 limes
  • ¼ cup lime juice

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C) and line a cupcake tin with paper liners.

  2. In a medium bowl, whisk together the flour, baking powder, and salt. Set aside.

  3. In a large mixing bowl, cream together the softened butter and granulated sugar until light and fluffy. Add the eggs one at a time, beating well after each addition. Stir in the vanilla extract.

  4. Gradually add the dry ingredients to the butter mixture, alternating with the coconut milk. Mix until just combined. Stir in the lime zest.

  5. Divide the batter evenly among the prepared cupcake liners, filling each about 2/3 full. Bake for 18-20 minutes or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean. Remove from the oven and let cool completely.

  6. While the cupcakes are cooling, prepare the coconut lime ice cream. In a saucepan, heat the heavy cream, coconut milk, and granulated sugar over medium heat until the sugar has dissolved. Stir in the lime zest and lime juice. Remove from heat and let cool to room temperature.

  7. Once cooled, pour the ice cream mixture into an ice cream maker and churn according to the manufacturer’s instructions. Transfer the churned ice cream to a lidded container and freeze for at least 4 hours or until firm.

  8. To assemble the ice cream sandwiches, take one cupcake and slice it in half horizontally. Place a scoop of the coconut lime ice cream on the bottom half of the cupcake and top with the other half. Gently press down to secure.

  9. Repeat the process with the remaining cupcakes and ice cream. Serve immediately or wrap each ice cream sandwich in plastic wrap and freeze for later enjoyment.

Tips and Variations

  • For an extra tropical twist, sprinkle toasted coconut flakes on top of the ice cream before assembling the sandwiches.
  • Experiment with different cupcake flavors like lemon or pineapple to create unique combinations.
  • If you don’t have an ice cream maker, you can pour the ice cream mixture into a shallow container and freeze, stirring every hour until it reaches the desired consistency.
  • Customize your ice cream sandwiches by rolling the edges in chopped nuts, sprinkles, or mini chocolate chips.

These Coconut Lime Cupcake Ice Cream Sandwiches are the perfect blend of tropical flavors and creamy indulgence. Whether you’re hosting a summer party or simply treating yourself to a delicious dessert, this recipe is sure to impress. So grab your spatula and get ready to embark on a tropical taste adventure!
