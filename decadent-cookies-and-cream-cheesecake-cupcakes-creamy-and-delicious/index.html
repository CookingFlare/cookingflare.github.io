Decadent Cookies and Cream Cheesecake Cupcakes: Creamy and Delicious


If you’re a fan of creamy cheesecake and irresistible cookies and cream, then you’re in for a treat with these decadent Cookies and Cream Cheesecake Cupcakes. These little delights are the perfect combination of rich, creamy cheesecake and crunchy chocolate cookies. With each bite, you’ll experience a burst of flavors that will leave you craving for more.

Ingredients

For the crust:

  • 1 and 1/2 cups of crushed chocolate sandwich cookies
  • 1/4 cup of unsalted butter, melted

For the cheesecake filling:

  • 16 ounces of cream cheese, softened
  • 1/2 cup of granulated sugar
  • 2 teaspoons of vanilla extract
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1/2 cup of sour cream
  • 1 cup of crushed chocolate sandwich cookies

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 325°F (160°C) and line a muffin tin with cupcake liners.

  2. In a medium bowl, combine the crushed chocolate sandwich cookies and melted butter for the crust. Mix until well combined. Press a spoonful of the mixture into the bottom of each cupcake liner, creating a firm crust.

  3. In a large mixing bowl, beat the cream cheese until smooth and creamy. Add the sugar and vanilla extract, and continue beating until well combined.

  4. Add the eggs, one at a time, mixing well after each addition. Be sure to scrape down the sides of the bowl to ensure everything is fully incorporated.

  5. Mix in the sour cream until the batter is smooth and creamy. Gently fold in the crushed chocolate sandwich cookies, making sure they are evenly distributed throughout the batter.

  6. Pour the cheesecake batter over the crust in each cupcake liner, filling them about three-quarters full. Smooth the tops with a spoon or spatula.

  7. Bake the cupcakes in the preheated oven for about 20-25 minutes, or until the edges are set and the centers are slightly jiggly.

  8. Remove the cupcakes from the oven and let them cool in the muffin tin for about 10 minutes. Then, transfer them to a wire rack to cool completely.

  9. Once cooled, refrigerate the cupcakes for at least 2 hours, or overnight if possible. This will allow the cheesecake to set and become firm.

  10. Serve the decadent Cookies and Cream Cheesecake Cupcakes chilled and enjoy the creamy, dreamy goodness!

Tips and Variations

  • For a twist on the classic cookies and cream flavor, try using different flavored sandwich cookies, such as mint or peanut butter, in the crust and the cheesecake filling.
  • Drizzle some melted chocolate or caramel sauce over the cupcakes for an extra touch of decadence.
  • Top each cupcake with a dollop of whipped cream and a sprinkle of crushed cookies for added texture and presentation.
  • These cupcakes can be stored in the refrigerator for up to 3 days, making them a perfect make-ahead dessert option for parties or gatherings.

So, if you’re looking to indulge in a creamy and delicious dessert, these Decadent Cookies and Cream Cheesecake Cupcakes are a must-try. With their irresistible combination of flavors and textures, they’re sure to delight your taste buds and leave you craving for more. Enjoy!
