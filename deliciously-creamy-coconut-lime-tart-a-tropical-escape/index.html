Deliciously Creamy Coconut Lime Tart: A Tropical Escape


If you’re craving a taste of the tropics, look no further than this delightful Coconut Lime Tart. With its creamy coconut filling and zesty lime flavor, this tart is a perfect dessert for hot summer days or any time you want to transport your taste buds to a sunny beach paradise.

Ingredients

For the Crust:

  • 1 ½ cups of graham cracker crumbs
  • ½ cup of sweetened shredded coconut
  • ⅓ cup of granulated sugar
  • ⅓ cup of melted butter

For the Filling:

  • 1 can (14 ounces) of coconut milk
  • 1 cup of heavy cream
  • 1 cup of granulated sugar
  • ½ cup of fresh lime juice
  • Zest of 2 limes
  • 5 large egg yolks
  • ¼ cup of cornstarch
  • ¼ teaspoon of salt
  • 2 tablespoons of unsalted butter
  • 1 teaspoon of pure vanilla extract

Instructions

Step 1: Prepare the Crust

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. In a bowl, mix the graham cracker crumbs, shredded coconut, sugar, and melted butter until well combined.
  3. Press the mixture into the bottom and up the sides of a 9-inch tart pan with a removable bottom.
  4. Bake the crust for 10-12 minutes, or until it’s golden brown. Let it cool completely.

Step 2: Make the Filling

  1. In a medium saucepan, combine the coconut milk, heavy cream, and granulated sugar. Heat the mixture over medium heat, stirring occasionally, until it’s hot but not boiling.
  2. In a separate bowl, whisk together the lime juice, lime zest, egg yolks, cornstarch, and salt until smooth.
  3. Gradually pour the hot coconut milk mixture into the lime mixture, whisking constantly.
  4. Return the combined mixture to the saucepan and cook over medium heat, whisking constantly, until it thickens and boils.
  5. Remove the pan from the heat and whisk in the butter and vanilla extract until the filling is smooth.
  6. Pour the filling into the cooled crust and smooth the top with a spatula.
  7. Place a piece of plastic wrap directly on the surface of the filling to prevent a skin from forming. Chill the tart in the refrigerator for at least 4 hours, or until it’s set.

Step 3: Serve and Enjoy

  1. Once the tart is fully chilled and set, carefully remove it from the tart pan and place it on a serving platter.
  2. For an extra flourish, garnish the tart with additional lime zest and shredded coconut.
  3. Slice and serve the Coconut Lime Tart and watch as your guests delight in the tropical flavors and creamy texture.

Conclusion

This Deliciously Creamy Coconut Lime Tart is a true taste of the tropics, combining the richness of coconut with the tangy brightness of lime. Whether you’re hosting a summer gathering or simply want to treat yourself to a tropical escape, this tart is sure to impress. The buttery graham cracker and coconut crust provides the perfect base for the luscious lime-infused filling, creating a dessert that’s both refreshing and indulgent. So, go ahead, take a bite, and let this Coconut Lime Tart transport you to a sun-soaked paradise.

So, what are you waiting for? It’s time to whip up this delightful dessert and savor a little taste of the tropics right in your own home. Enjoy!
