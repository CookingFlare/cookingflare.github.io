Thai Basil Beef Stir-Fry with Noodles: A Quick and Aromatic Meal


If you’re craving a flavorful and aromatic dish that comes together in a flash, look no further than this Thai Basil Beef Stir-Fry with Noodles. This recipe combines tender strips of beef, vibrant vegetables, and fragrant Thai basil, all tossed in a delectable sauce and served over a bed of noodles. It’s a perfect balance of savory, spicy, and aromatic flavors that will transport your taste buds to the bustling streets of Thailand.

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound flank steak, thinly sliced
  • 8 oz rice noodles
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 red bell pepper, thinly sliced
  • 1 cup snap peas
  • 1/2 cup sliced carrots
  • 1/2 cup sliced onions
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons oyster sauce
  • 2 tablespoons fish sauce
  • 1 tablespoon sugar
  • 1 cup fresh Thai basil leaves
  • Lime wedges, for serving
  • Crushed peanuts (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the Noodles: Cook the rice noodles according to the package instructions. Drain and set aside.

  2. Marinate the Beef: In a bowl, combine the thinly sliced flank steak with 1 tablespoon of soy sauce and let it marinate while you prepare the other ingredients.

  3. Make the Stir-Fry Sauce: In a small bowl, whisk together the remaining soy sauce, oyster sauce, fish sauce, and sugar. Set aside.

  4. Stir-Fry the Beef: Heat 1 tablespoon of vegetable oil in a wok or large skillet over high heat. Add the marinated beef to the pan and stir-fry for 2-3 minutes until it’s browned and cooked through. Remove the beef from the pan and set it aside.

  5. Stir-Fry the Vegetables: In the same pan, add another tablespoon of oil if needed. Add the minced garlic and stir-fry for 30 seconds until fragrant. Then, add the sliced red bell pepper, snap peas, carrots, and onions. Stir-fry for 3-4 minutes until the vegetables are crisp-tender.

  6. Combine and Finish: Return the cooked beef to the pan with the vegetables. Pour the stir-fry sauce over the beef and vegetables. Toss everything together until the sauce evenly coats the ingredients. Add the cooked rice noodles to the pan and gently toss to combine everything.

  7. Add the Thai Basil: Once the noodles are coated in the sauce and heated through, add the fresh Thai basil leaves to the pan. Toss everything together for another minute until the basil wilts and becomes aromatic.

  8. Serve: Remove the pan from the heat. Serve the Thai Basil Beef Stir-Fry over noodles, garnished with lime wedges. Optionally, sprinkle with crushed peanuts for added texture and flavor.

Tips for Success:

  • Prep Ahead: Cut the vegetables and slice the beef ahead of time to streamline the cooking process.
  • High Heat: Ensure your wok or skillet is very hot to achieve that perfect stir-fry texture and flavor.
  • Customization: Feel free to adjust the level of spiciness by adding chili flakes or fresh chilies to suit your preference.

This Thai Basil Beef Stir-Fry with Noodles is a wonderful way to introduce the vibrant and aromatic flavors of Thai cuisine into your home kitchen. With its quick preparation and explosive flavors, it’s sure to become a regular in your meal rotation. Enjoy the delightful combination of tender beef, crisp vegetables, and fragrant basil, all coming together in a harmonious stir-fry that’s perfect for any occasion.
