Flavorful Keto Garlic Butter Steak Bites Recipe


Are you looking for a delicious and satisfying keto-friendly meal that will tantalize your taste buds? Look no further than this Flavorful Keto Garlic Butter Steak Bites recipe. Packed with savory flavors and succulent bites of steak, this dish is perfect for anyone following a low-carb or keto lifestyle. Whether you’re cooking for a special occasion or simply craving a hearty and flavorful meal, these steak bites are sure to impress.

Ingredients

Before we dive into the cooking process, let’s gather the ingredients we’ll need to bring this dish to life:

  • 1 1/2 pounds of sirloin steak, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 4 tablespoons of unsalted butter
  • 4 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon of fresh parsley, finely chopped
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 tablespoon of olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon of paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon of red pepper flakes (optional)
  • 1/2 cup of beef broth
  • 1 tablespoon of Worcestershire sauce

Instructions

Step 1: Prepare the Steak

To begin, pat the steak pieces dry with a paper towel and season them generously with salt, pepper, and paprika. This will ensure that the steak is well-seasoned and ready to absorb the flavors of the garlic butter sauce.

Step 2: Sear the Steak

In a large skillet over medium-high heat, add the olive oil and sear the steak pieces for 2-3 minutes on each side, or until they develop a golden-brown crust. Once seared, remove the steak from the skillet and set it aside.

Step 3: Make the Garlic Butter Sauce

In the same skillet, melt the butter over medium heat. Add the minced garlic and sauté for 1-2 minutes, allowing the garlic to become fragrant and infuse the butter with its flavor. Be careful not to let the garlic brown.

Step 4: Combine the Ingredients

Once the garlic is fragrant, return the seared steak to the skillet. Add the beef broth and Worcestershire sauce, allowing the liquid to come to a gentle simmer. Let the steak cook in the sauce for an additional 2-3 minutes, allowing the flavors to meld together.

Step 5: Garnish and Serve

To finish, sprinkle the chopped parsley over the steak bites for a fresh burst of color and flavor. Serve the steak bites hot, and enjoy the rich, buttery, and garlicky goodness with each bite.
