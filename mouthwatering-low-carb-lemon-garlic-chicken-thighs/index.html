Mouthwatering Low Carb Lemon Garlic Chicken Thighs


Are you tired of the same old chicken recipes? Do you crave something that’s not only delicious but also healthy and low in carbs? Look no further than these mouthwatering low carb lemon garlic chicken thighs! This recipe is a game-changer for anyone looking to spice up their meal routine while keeping their carb count in check.

The Secret to Success

The key to making these chicken thighs truly mouthwatering lies in the combination of flavors and techniques used in this recipe. First, we’ll start with the chicken itself. Boneless, skinless chicken thighs are the perfect choice for this recipe, as they’re tender, juicy, and easy to cook. Next, we’ll add a generous amount of garlic, which not only adds flavor but also has numerous health benefits. Finally, we’ll top it all off with a tangy and refreshing lemon sauce that will leave you wanting more.

The Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken thighs
  • 3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons of olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon of freshly squeezed lemon juice
  • 1 tablespoon of chopped fresh parsley
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1/4 cup of grated Parmesan cheese (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C).
  2. In a small bowl, mix together the minced garlic and olive oil.
  3. Place the chicken thighs in a large baking dish and brush the garlic and olive oil mixture evenly over both sides of the chicken.
  4. Season the chicken with salt and pepper to taste.
  5. Bake the chicken in the preheated oven for 25-30 minutes, or until cooked through.
  6. While the chicken is baking, mix together the lemon juice and chopped parsley in a small bowl.
  7. Remove the chicken from the oven and brush the lemon and parsley mixture evenly over both sides of the chicken.
  8. If using Parmesan cheese, sprinkle it over the top of the chicken.
  9. Return the chicken to the oven and bake for an additional 5-7 minutes, or until the cheese is melted and bubbly.
  10. Remove the chicken from the oven and let it rest for a few minutes before serving.

Tips and Variations

  • To make this recipe even healthier, try using bone-in, skin-on chicken thighs. This will not only add more flavor but also provide a good source of protein and omega-3 fatty acids.
  • If you’re looking for a lower-calorie alternative to Parmesan cheese, try using feta or goat cheese instead.
  • To add some extra flavor to this recipe, try adding some chopped fresh herbs like thyme or rosemary to the garlic and olive oil mixture.
  • If you prefer a creamier sauce, try adding some Greek yogurt or sour cream to the lemon and parsley mixture.

Conclusion

These mouthwatering low carb lemon garlic chicken thighs are the perfect recipe for anyone looking to spice up their meal routine while keeping their carb count in check. With its combination of flavors and techniques, this recipe is sure to become a new favorite in your household. So go ahead, give it a try, and enjoy the delicious and healthy benefits that come with it!
