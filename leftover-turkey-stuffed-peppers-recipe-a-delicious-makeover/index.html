Leftover Turkey Stuffed Peppers Recipe: A Delicious Makeover


So, you’ve got some leftover turkey from that big holiday feast, and you’re wondering what to do with it. Well, look no further because I’ve got the perfect solution for you – Leftover Turkey Stuffed Peppers! This recipe is not only delicious but also a great way to give your leftover turkey a fantastic makeover. Let’s dive into the details and get cooking!

Ingredients:

  • 4 large bell peppers (any color will do)
  • 2 cups leftover turkey, shredded
  • 1 cup cooked rice
  • 1 can (14 oz) diced tomatoes
  • 1 small onion, diced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1/2 teaspoon paprika
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Fresh parsley for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the Oven: Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).

  2. Prepare the Peppers: Cut the tops off the bell peppers and remove the seeds and membranes. Rinse the peppers and set them aside.

  3. Prepare the Filling: In a large bowl, combine the leftover turkey, cooked rice, diced tomatoes, onion, garlic, oregano, basil, paprika, salt, and pepper. Mix everything together until well combined.

  4. Stuff the Peppers: Stuff each pepper with the turkey and rice mixture, pressing down gently to pack the filling.

  5. Bake the Stuffed Peppers: Place the stuffed peppers in a baking dish. Cover the dish with foil and bake for 25 minutes. Then, remove the foil, sprinkle the shredded mozzarella cheese over the peppers, and bake for an additional 10 minutes or until the cheese is melted and bubbly.

  6. Garnish and Serve: Once the stuffed peppers are out of the oven, garnish them with fresh parsley and serve hot.

Tips and Variations:

  • If you want to add some extra heat to these stuffed peppers, feel free to sprinkle some red pepper flakes over the filling mixture before baking.
  • For a low-carb version, you can substitute the rice with cauliflower rice.
  • If you’re not a fan of mozzarella, you can use cheddar or any other cheese of your choice.

Why You’ll Love This Recipe:

Leftover Turkey Stuffed Peppers are a fantastic way to transform your holiday leftovers into a whole new meal. The combination of tender turkey, savory rice, and flavorful spices, all nestled inside sweet bell peppers, creates a dish that’s hearty, satisfying, and full of comforting flavors. Plus, it’s a great way to sneak in some extra veggies with the added bonus of being a complete meal in one colorful package. This recipe is not only easy to make but also a crowd-pleaser that will have everyone asking for seconds.

Final Thoughts:

So, the next time you find yourself with a surplus of turkey after a big family gathering, give this Leftover Turkey Stuffed Peppers recipe a try. It’s a delicious makeover for your leftovers that will have you looking forward to enjoying the last remnants of that holiday bird. Whether it’s for a cozy weeknight dinner or a casual get-together with friends, these stuffed peppers are sure to be a hit. Enjoy!

That’s it for today’s recipe! I hope you enjoy making and savoring these delicious Leftover Turkey Stuffed Peppers. Until next time, happy cooking!
