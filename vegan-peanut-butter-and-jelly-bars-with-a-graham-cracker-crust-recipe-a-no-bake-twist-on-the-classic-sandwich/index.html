Vegan Peanut Butter and Jelly Bars with a Graham Cracker Crust Recipe: A No-Bake Twist on the Classic Sandwich


Vegan Peanut Butter and Jelly Bars with a Graham Cracker Crust Recipe: A No-Bake Twist on the Classic Sandwich

If you’re a fan of the timeless combination of peanut butter and jelly, then you’re in for a treat with this vegan twist on a classic favorite. These Vegan Peanut Butter and Jelly Bars with a Graham Cracker Crust are not only delicious but also incredibly easy to make. The best part? No baking required! Whether you’re hosting a party, need a quick snack, or just want to indulge in a sweet treat, these bars are the perfect solution.

Ingredients:

For the Graham Cracker Crust:

  • 2 cups vegan graham cracker crumbs
  • 1/3 cup coconut sugar
  • 1/2 cup melted vegan butter

For the Peanut Butter Filling:

  • 1 1/2 cups creamy peanut butter
  • 3/4 cup powdered sugar
  • 1/2 cup coconut oil, melted
  • 1 teaspoon pure vanilla extract
  • Pinch of salt

For the Jelly Layer:

  • 1 1/2 cups mixed berry preserves (or your favorite jelly or jam)

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the Graham Cracker Crust:

    • In a bowl, combine the graham cracker crumbs and coconut sugar. Pour in the melted vegan butter and mix until the crumbs are evenly coated.
    • Press the mixture firmly and evenly into the bottom of a parchment-lined 9x9-inch baking dish. Place the dish in the refrigerator to chill while you prepare the filling.

  2. Make the Peanut Butter Filling:

    • In a separate bowl, beat the creamy peanut butter, powdered sugar, melted coconut oil, vanilla extract, and salt together until smooth and well combined.
    • Remove the baking dish from the refrigerator and spread the peanut butter filling over the chilled graham cracker crust. Use a spatula to ensure an even layer.

  3. Add the Jelly Layer:

    • Gently warm the mixed berry preserves in a small saucepan over low heat until it becomes slightly runny.
    • Pour the warmed preserves over the peanut butter filling, spreading it out evenly to cover the entire surface.

  4. Chill and Serve:

    • Place the baking dish back in the refrigerator and chill for at least 4 hours, or until the bars are set.
    • Once set, use a sharp knife to cut the bars into squares or rectangles.
    • Serve and enjoy these no-bake Vegan Peanut Butter and Jelly Bars with a Graham Cracker Crust!

Tips and Variations:

  • For a nut-free version, you can substitute the peanut butter with almond or sunflower seed butter.
  • Feel free to experiment with different flavors of jelly or jam to create your own unique twist on this recipe.
  • If you prefer a thicker jelly layer, simply increase the amount of preserves used.

Final Thoughts

These Vegan Peanut Butter and Jelly Bars with a Graham Cracker Crust are a delightful take on the beloved peanut butter and jelly sandwich. The no-bake aspect makes them a convenient and quick dessert or snack option for any occasion. Whether you’re following a vegan diet or simply looking for a delicious treat, these bars are sure to impress with their rich, creamy peanut butter filling and fruity jelly layer. So, roll up your sleeves and whip up a batch of these delightful bars to indulge in a childhood favorite with a vegan twist!
