Flavorful Venison Steak Recipes for the Wild at Heart


Are you a nature enthusiast with a taste for adventure? Do you love the thrill of the hunt and the satisfaction of preparing a delicious meal from your own wild game? If so, you’re in for a treat! In this blog post, we’ll explore some mouthwatering venison steak recipes that are sure to satisfy your wild heart and tantalize your taste buds.

1. Grilled Garlic and Herb Venison Steak

Ingredients:

  • 2 venison steaks
  • 4 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons of fresh rosemary, chopped
  • 2 tablespoons of fresh thyme, chopped
  • 2 tablespoons of olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your grill to medium-high heat.
  2. In a small bowl, combine the minced garlic, chopped rosemary, chopped thyme, olive oil, salt, and pepper.
  3. Rub the garlic and herb mixture all over the venison steaks, ensuring they are well coated.
  4. Place the steaks on the preheated grill and cook for about 4-5 minutes per side for medium-rare, or adjust the cooking time according to your desired level of doneness.
  5. Remove the steaks from the grill and let them rest for a few minutes before serving. This allows the juices to redistribute, resulting in a tender and flavorful steak.

2. Pan-Seared Venison Steak with Mushroom Sauce

Ingredients:

  • 2 venison steaks
  • 2 tablespoons of butter
  • 1 tablespoon of olive oil
  • 8 ounces of mushrooms, sliced
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 cup of beef broth
  • 1/2 cup of heavy cream
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Season the venison steaks with salt and pepper on both sides.
  2. In a large skillet, heat the butter and olive oil over medium-high heat.
  3. Add the venison steaks to the skillet and cook for about 3-4 minutes per side for medium-rare, or adjust the cooking time to your preference.
  4. Remove the steaks from the skillet and set them aside.
  5. In the same skillet, add the sliced mushrooms and minced garlic. Cook until the mushrooms are golden brown and tender.
  6. Pour in the beef broth and bring it to a simmer. Cook for a few minutes until the broth has reduced slightly.
  7. Stir in the heavy cream and continue to simmer until the sauce has thickened.
  8. Return the venison steaks to the skillet and cook for an additional 2 minutes to heat them through.
  9. Serve the venison steaks with the mushroom sauce spooned over the top.

3. Citrus-Marinated Venison Steak

Ingredients:

  • 2 venison steaks
  • 1 orange, juiced
  • 1 lemon, juiced
  • 2 tablespoons of soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons of honey
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a shallow dish, combine the orange juice, lemon juice, soy sauce, honey, minced garlic, salt, and pepper.
  2. Place the venison steaks in the marinade and let them sit for at least 30 minutes, or refrigerate overnight for maximum flavor.
  3. Preheat your grill to medium-high heat.
  4. Remove the venison steaks from the marinade and discard the marinade.
  5. Grill the steaks for about 4-5 minutes per side for medium-rare, or adjust the cooking time to your liking.
  6. Let the steaks rest for a few minutes before slicing and serving.

Conclusion

These flavorful venison steak recipes are perfect for those with a wild heart and a love for the outdoors. Whether you prefer the smoky flavors of grilled garlic and herb, the rich and savory mushroom sauce, or the tangy and refreshing citrus marinade, there’s a recipe here to satisfy your cravings. So, grab your hunting gear, fire up the grill, and indulge in these delicious creations that celebrate the wild at heart. Happy cooking!
