15-Minute Creamy Tuscan Chicken Recipe


If you’re looking for a delicious and quick dinner option, this 15-minute creamy Tuscan chicken recipe is the perfect choice. This dish is a wonderful blend of tender chicken, flavorful sun-dried tomatoes, and a creamy, garlicky sauce that will leave your taste buds wanting more. Best of all, it’s easy to make and requires minimal ingredients, making it a great option for busy weeknights or when you want to impress guests with minimal effort.

Ingredients:

  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup chicken broth
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/2 cup sun-dried tomatoes, chopped
  • 1 teaspoon Italian seasoning
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh basil leaves for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Start by seasoning the chicken breasts with salt and pepper on both sides. In a large skillet, heat the olive oil over medium-high heat.

  2. Add the chicken breasts to the skillet and cook for 6-7 minutes on each side, or until they are golden brown and cooked through. Once done, remove the chicken from the skillet and set it aside on a plate.

  3. In the same skillet, add the minced garlic and sauté for about 1 minute, until it becomes fragrant.

  4. Pour in the chicken broth and allow it to simmer for a few minutes, scraping the bottom of the skillet to incorporate any browned bits from the chicken.

  5. Reduce the heat to low and stir in the heavy cream, grated Parmesan cheese, chopped sun-dried tomatoes, and Italian seasoning. Allow the sauce to simmer for 2-3 minutes until it thickens slightly.

  6. Return the chicken breasts to the skillet and spoon the creamy Tuscan sauce over them. Let the chicken simmer in the sauce for an additional 2-3 minutes to allow the flavors to meld together.

  7. Once the chicken is heated through and the sauce has reached the desired consistency, remove the skillet from the heat.

  8. Garnish the creamy Tuscan chicken with fresh basil leaves for a pop of color and an extra burst of flavor.

Serving Suggestions:

This creamy Tuscan chicken pairs beautifully with a side of pasta, steamed vegetables, or a fresh green salad. The rich, creamy sauce is perfect for drizzling over pasta or rice, allowing you to savor every last drop of flavor.

Conclusion:

With its rich and creamy sauce, vibrant sun-dried tomatoes, and tender chicken, this 15-minute creamy Tuscan chicken recipe is sure to become a favorite in your household. Whether you’re cooking for your family or hosting a dinner party, this dish is a winner in terms of both taste and convenience. Try it out for your next meal and savor the delightful flavors of Tuscany in the comfort of your own home. Enjoy!
