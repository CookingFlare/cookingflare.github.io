Instant Pot Chicken Fricassee Recipe: A French-Inspired Dish

French cuisine is renowned for its rich flavors, intricate preparations, and exquisite presentation. Today, we’re going to bring a taste of France to your table with a classic dish that’s both elegant and effortless: Instant Pot Chicken Fricassee. This heavenly recipe is a perfect blend of tender chicken, creamy sauce, and aromatic spices, all cooked to perfection in the Instant Pot. Ingredients 1 1/2 pounds boneless, skinless chicken thighs, cut into 1-inch pieces 2 tablespoons butter 1 medium onion, diced 2 cloves garlic, minced 1 cup all-purpose flour 1 cup chicken broth 1 cup heavy cream 1 teaspoon dried thyme 1/2 teaspoon paprika Salt and pepper, to taste Fresh parsley, chopped (optional) Instructions Press the “Saute” button on the Instant Pot and melt the butter. Add the diced onion and cook until it’s translucent, about 5 minutes. Add the minced garlic and cook for an additional minute, stirring constantly to prevent burning. Add the chicken pieces and cook until they’re browned on all sides, about 5-7 minutes. Remove the chicken from the pot and set it aside. Add the flour to the pot and cook for 1 minute, stirring constantly. The mixture should form a smooth paste. Gradually add the chicken broth and heavy cream, whisking continuously to prevent lumps. Bring the mixture to a simmer and cook for 2-3 minutes, or until it thickens slightly. Add the thyme, paprika, salt, and pepper to the pot. Stir to combine. Return the chicken to the pot and stir to coat with the sauce. Close the lid of the Instant Pot and set the valve to “Sealing”. Press the “Manual” or “Pressure Cook” button and set the cooking time to 10 minutes at high pressure. When the cooking time is up, allow the pressure to release naturally for 10 minutes, then quick-release any remaining pressure. Open the lid and check the chicken for tenderness. If it’s not cooked to your liking, close the lid and cook for an additional 5 minutes. Taste and adjust the seasoning as needed. Serve the Chicken Fricassee hot, garnished with chopped parsley if desired. Tips and Variations For a richer sauce, use 1 1/2 cups of heavy cream or add 1/2 cup of grated Parmesan cheese. To add some color and texture, stir in 1 cup of frozen peas during the last 2 minutes of cooking. For a spicy kick, add 1/4 teaspoon of cayenne pepper or red pepper flakes to the sauce. This recipe freezes well, so feel free to make a batch and store it in the freezer for up to 3 months. Simply thaw and reheat when needed. Conclusion Instant Pot Chicken Fricassee is a show-stopping dish that’s sure to impress your family and friends. With its tender chicken, creamy sauce, and aromatic spices, it’s a perfect representation of French cuisine. The best part? It’s incredibly easy to make and requires minimal effort, thanks to the Instant Pot. So go ahead, give this recipe a try, and experience the magic of French cooking in the comfort of your own home. Mouthwatering Instant Pot Beef and Broccoli Stir-Fry Recipes

Are you craving a delicious and satisfying meal that is quick and easy to make? Look no further than these mouthwatering Instant Pot Beef and Broccoli Stir-Fry recipes. With the convenience of the Instant Pot, you can have a flavorful and nutritious meal on the table in no time. So, grab your apron and let’s get cooking! Recipe 1: Classic Beef and Broccoli Stir-Fry Ingredients: 1 pound beef sirloin, thinly sliced 2 cups broccoli florets 1/2 cup low-sodium soy sauce 1/4 cup brown sugar 2 cloves garlic, minced 1 teaspoon fresh ginger, grated 1 tablespoon cornstarch 1/4 cup water 2 tablespoons vegetable oil Salt and pepper, to taste Steamed rice, for serving Instructions: In a small bowl, whisk together the soy sauce, brown sugar, garlic, ginger, cornstarch, and water. Set aside. Season the beef slices with salt and pepper. Set your Instant Pot to the sauté function and heat the vegetable oil. Add the beef slices to the Instant Pot and cook until browned on all sides. Remove the beef and set aside. Add the broccoli florets to the Instant Pot and sauté for 2-3 minutes, until slightly tender. Return the beef to the Instant Pot and pour in the soy sauce mixture. Secure the lid on the Instant Pot and set the vent to the sealing position. Cook on high pressure for 5 minutes, then do a quick release of the pressure. Serve the beef and broccoli stir-fry over steamed rice and enjoy! Recipe 2: Spicy Sesame Beef and Broccoli Stir-Fry Ingredients: 1 pound beef flank steak, thinly sliced 2 cups broccoli florets 1/4 cup low-sodium soy sauce 2 tablespoons hoisin sauce 1 tablespoon sesame oil 1 tablespoon Sriracha sauce 2 cloves garlic, minced 1 tablespoon cornstarch 1/4 cup water 2 tablespoons vegetable oil Salt and pepper, to taste Sesame seeds, for garnish Steamed rice, for serving Instructions: In a small bowl, whisk together the soy sauce, hoisin sauce, sesame oil, Sriracha sauce, garlic, cornstarch, and water. Set aside. Season the beef slices with salt and pepper. Set your Instant Pot to the sauté function and heat the vegetable oil. Add the beef slices to the Instant Pot and cook until browned on all sides. Remove the beef and set aside. Add the broccoli florets to the Instant Pot and sauté for 2-3 minutes, until slightly tender. Return the beef to the Instant Pot and pour in the sauce mixture. Secure the lid on the Instant Pot and set the vent to the sealing position. Cook on high pressure for 5 minutes, then do a quick release of the pressure. Serve the spicy sesame beef and broccoli stir-fry over steamed rice, garnish with sesame seeds, and enjoy! Recipe 3: Teriyaki Beef and Broccoli Stir-Fry Ingredients: 1 pound beef tenderloin, thinly sliced 2 cups broccoli florets 1/2 cup teriyaki sauce 2 tablespoons honey 2 cloves garlic, minced 1 tablespoon cornstarch 1/4 cup water 2 tablespoons vegetable oil Salt and pepper, to taste Green onions, sliced, for garnish Steamed rice, for serving Instructions: In a small bowl, whisk together the teriyaki sauce, honey, garlic, cornstarch, and water. Set aside. Season the beef slices with salt and pepper. Set your Instant Pot to the sauté function and heat the vegetable oil. Add the beef slices to the Instant Pot and cook until browned on all sides. Remove the beef and set aside. Add the broccoli florets to the Instant Pot and sauté for 2-3 minutes, until slightly tender. Return the beef to the Instant Pot and pour in the teriyaki sauce mixture. Secure the lid on the Instant Pot and set the vent to the sealing position. Cook on high pressure for 5 minutes, then do a quick release of the pressure. Serve the teriyaki beef and broccoli stir-fry over steamed rice, garnish with sliced green onions, and enjoy! These Instant Pot Beef and Broccoli Stir-Fry recipes are perfect for busy weeknights when you want a flavorful and nutritious meal without spending hours in the kitchen. The Instant Pot makes the cooking process quick and easy, while still delivering mouthwatering results. So, why wait?
