Keto Kneads: Delicious Keto-friendly Pizza and Flatbread Recipes for Diet-Conscious Foodies


Hey foodies! Welcome to a tantalizing world filled with scrumptious low-carb fare and Gluten-free pizza and flatbreads that will make your taste buds do a happy dance. In this blog post, we’ll dive into mouth-watering recipes that infuse the goodness of the ketogenic diet with the love of fluffy, crispy flatbreads and pizzas. Tired of feeling restricted on a diet? We’ve got you covered with these delicious and filling recipes that will make you fall head over heels in love with keto!

Keto Pizza Paradise

A pizza night without guilt? Sign us up! We’re thrilled to present to you a low-carb, high-flavor pizza that will leave you dreaming of cheesy, gooey goodness. Introducing the Keto Pizza Crust.

Ingredients:

  • Chestnut Flour (1 cup)
  • Psyllium Husk (1 tbsp)
  • Baking Powder (1 tsp)
  • Egg (1 large)
  • Olive Oil (2 tbsp)
  • Salt (to taste)
  • Mozzarella Cheese (1 cup, shredded)
  • Toppings of your choice

Instructions:

  1. Start by heating your oven to 425°F (218°C) and lining a baking sheet with parchment paper.
  2. In a bowl, mix together the chestnut flour, psyllium husk, baking powder, and salt.
  3. Whisk in the egg and olive oil, stirring until you form a dough.
  4. Press the dough onto the prepared baking sheet, spreading it out into a flat circle (about 1/4-inch thick).
  5. Bake for 10-12 minutes or until the crust is lightly golden.
  6. Remove from the oven and top with shredded mozzarella and your preferred toppings.
  7. Place the pizza back into the oven and bake for another 5-7 minutes or until the cheese is bubbly and beginning to brown.
  8. Slice and serve hot!

Yum, a low-carb pizza dreams are made of!

Breath-taking Basics: Simple Keto Flatbreads

Who says simplicity isn’t delightful? Let’s level up your flatbread game with this deceptively easy and scrumptious flatbread recipe that anyone can master.

Ingredients:

  • Almond Flour (2 cups)
  • Psyllium Husk (2 tbsp)
  • Baking Powder (1 tsp)
  • Salt (1/2 tsp)
  • Water (1/2 cup)
  • Olive Oil (1 tbsp)
  • Rosemary (optional, for topping)

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 425°F (218°C) and prepare a baking sheet with parchment paper.
  2. In a large bowl, mix the almond flour, psyllium husk, baking powder, and salt.
  3. Add water and olive oil, stirring until the dough forms. Be careful not to over-mix.
  4. With your hands or a rolling pin, shape the dough into small, flat discs or rectangles – about ¼ inch in thickness.
  5. Place the flatbreads onto the prepared baking sheet and use a fork to prick a few holes in each one. This prevents bloating.
  6. Bake the flatbreads in the oven for 12-15 minutes until they start to crisp up.
  7. Remove from the oven and give them a light brush with olive oil. If using, sprinkle some freshly chopped rosemary on the hot flatbreads.
  8. Let them cool down a little, then enjoy!

Keto Veggie-Lovers Dream

Have you ever found yourself having to choose between a pizza or a salad? Worry not! This veggie-packed flatbread will completely submerge you in a sea of flavors.

Ingredients:

  • Keto Flatbread Dough (see recipe above)
  • Avocado (1, thinly sliced)
  • Cherry Tomatoes (1 cup, halved)
  • Spinach (handful)
  • Feta Cheese (1/4 cup, crumbled)
  • Olive Oil (for drizzling)

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 425°F (218°C) if not already on.
  2. Take your flatbread dough and evenly spread it out on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper.
  3. Bake the flatbread for 12-15 minutes until golden and crisp.
  4. Remove from the oven, and add a layer of spread avocado on the flatbread.
  5. Pile on the halved cherry tomatoes and fresh spinach leaves. Sprinkle over crumbled feta cheese.
  6. Return the flatbread to the oven for another 3-5 minutes, just to lightly wilt the spinach and warm everything through.
  7. Drizzle with a bit of olive oil, slice and serve!
