Tasty Low Carb Lemon Garlic Butter Shrimp and Broccoli Recipe


Looking for a quick, delicious, and healthy dinner option? Look no further! This Tasty Low Carb Lemon Garlic Butter Shrimp and Broccoli recipe combines the succulent flavors of shrimp with the vibrant taste of fresh broccoli, all enveloped in a rich, buttery garlic sauce. Perfect for those following a low-carb diet or anyone seeking a nutritious meal that doesn’t skimp on flavor, this dish is both easy to prepare and incredibly satisfying.

Ingredients

For the Shrimp and Broccoli:

  • 1 pound large shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 2 cups broccoli florets (fresh or frozen)
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • Lemon wedges, for serving

For the Lemon Garlic Butter Sauce:

  • 4 tablespoons unsalted butter
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon lemon zest
  • 2 tablespoons lemon juice (freshly squeezed is best)
  • 1 teaspoon red pepper flakes (optional, for a bit of heat)
  • Fresh parsley, chopped (for garnish)

Equipment Needed

  • Skillet or frying pan
  • Spatula
  • Measuring cups and spoons
  • Knife and cutting board

Preparation Steps

Step 1: Prepare the Broccoli

If using fresh broccoli, start by washing and cutting it into bite-sized florets. For frozen broccoli, simply measure out the desired amount. In a skillet, heat 1 tablespoon of olive oil over medium heat. Add the broccoli florets and sauté for about 4-5 minutes, or until they are tender but still bright green. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Once done, remove the broccoli from the skillet and set it aside.

Step 2: Cook the Shrimp

In the same skillet, add the shrimp. Cook them over medium-high heat for about 2-3 minutes on each side, or until they turn pink and opaque. Be careful not to overcook the shrimp, as they can become rubbery. Once cooked, remove the shrimp from the skillet and set them aside with the broccoli.

Step 3: Make the Lemon Garlic Butter Sauce

In the same skillet, reduce the heat to medium and add the unsalted butter. Allow it to melt completely, then add the minced garlic. Sauté for about 1 minute until the garlic becomes fragrant but not browned. Next, stir in the lemon zest and lemon juice, mixing well to combine. If you enjoy a bit of heat, sprinkle in the red pepper flakes at this point.

Step 4: Combine Everything

Return the cooked shrimp and broccoli to the skillet, tossing to coat them in the luscious lemon garlic butter sauce. Allow everything to simmer together for an additional 2-3 minutes to heat through and meld the flavors. Taste and adjust seasoning with more salt and pepper if needed.

Step 5: Serve and Enjoy

Remove from heat and transfer to a serving dish. Garnish with chopped fresh parsley for a pop of color and freshness. Serve with lemon wedges on the side for those who prefer an extra zing of citrus.

Nutritional Benefits

One of the standout features of this Tasty Low Carb Lemon Garlic Butter Shrimp and Broccoli recipe is its impressive nutritional profile. Both shrimp and broccoli provide a wealth of health benefits that can enhance your overall wellbeing.

Shrimp

  • High in Protein: Shrimp is an excellent source of lean protein, providing essential amino acids for muscle growth and repair.
  • Low in Calories: With only about 84 calories per 3-ounce serving, shrimp is a wonderful option for those looking to maintain or lose weight.
  • Rich in Nutrients: Shrimp contains important vitamins and minerals, including selenium, vitamin B12, and iodine, which play vital roles in metabolism and overall health.

Broccoli

  • Packed with Vitamins: Broccoli is a powerhouse of vitamins K and C, as well as fiber, which supports digestive health.
  • Antioxidant Properties: This cruciferous vegetable is rich in antioxidants, which help protect your body from cellular damage and inflammation.
  • Low in Carbs: Broccoli is an ideal choice for low-carb diets, providing essential nutrients without the extra carbs.

Meal Prep and Storage Tips

This recipe is perfect for meal prep enthusiasts. You can easily double or triple the ingredients to create multiple servings for the week. Here are some tips for meal prepping and storing your delicious shrimp and broccoli dish:

  • Storage: Allow the dish to cool completely before transferring it to an airtight container. It can be stored in the refrigerator for up to 3 days.
  • Reheating: To reheat, simply microwave for about 1-2 minutes until heated through, or warm it up in a skillet over low heat, adding a splash of water to keep the shrimp moist.
  • Freezing: If you want to store the dish for a longer period, consider freezing it. However, keep in mind that the texture of shrimp may change slightly after freezing and reheating.

Variations and Additions

One of the best aspects of this Tasty Low Carb Lemon Garlic Butter Shrimp and Broccoli recipe is its versatility. Feel free to experiment with different ingredients and flavors to suit your taste preferences:

  • Add More Veggies: Incorporate other low-carb vegetables like zucchini, asparagus, or bell peppers for added nutrients and flavor.
  • Spice it Up: If you enjoy a bit more heat, try adding cayenne pepper or a dash of hot sauce to the garlic butter sauce.
  • Herb Infusion: Experiment with different herbs like thyme, dill, or basil for a unique twist on the flavor profile.

Conclusion

This Tasty Low Carb Lemon Garlic Butter Shrimp and Broccoli recipe is a delightful addition to any dinner table. It’s simple to make, packed with flavor, and loaded with nutritional benefits. Whether you’re on a low-carb diet or just looking for a wholesome meal option, this dish is sure to impress.

So gather your ingredients, heat up that skillet, and enjoy a mouthwatering dinner that will leave you feeling satisfied and nourished. Don’t forget to share this recipe with friends and family who are also looking for healthy, delicious meal ideas. Happy cooking!
