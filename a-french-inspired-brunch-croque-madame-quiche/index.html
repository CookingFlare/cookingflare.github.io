A French-Inspired Brunch: Croque Madame Quiche

3 min · 574 words

If you’re looking to add a touch of French elegance to your brunch menu, look no further than the delightful Croque Madame Quiche. This dish takes the classic flavors of a Croque Madame sandwich and transforms them into a savory, custardy quiche that’s perfect for a leisurely weekend brunch. With its buttery crust, creamy filling, and indulgent toppings, this quiche is sure to impress your guests and elevate your culinary repertoire.

Ingredients

For the Crust:

  • 1 1/4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, cold and cut into cubes
  • 3-4 tablespoons ice water

For the Filling:

  • 6 large eggs
  • 1 1/2 cups heavy cream
  • 1 cup grated Gruyère cheese
  • 1/2 cup diced ham
  • 1/4 cup finely chopped fresh parsley
  • Salt and pepper to taste

For the Toppings:

  • 4 slices of thick-cut ham
  • 4 large eggs
  • 1/4 cup grated Gruyère cheese
  • Freshly ground black pepper

Instructions

Step 1: Prepare the Crust

  1. In a large bowl, combine the flour and salt. Add the cold butter cubes and use a pastry cutter or your fingertips to mix the butter into the flour until the mixture resembles coarse crumbs.
  2. Gradually add the ice water, one tablespoon at a time, mixing gently until the dough comes together. Be careful not to overwork the dough. Form the dough into a disk, wrap it in plastic wrap, and refrigerate for at least 1 hour.

Step 2: Pre-bake the Crust

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. On a lightly floured surface, roll out the chilled dough into a circle large enough to line a 9-inch quiche pan. Carefully transfer the dough to the pan, pressing it gently into the bottom and up the sides. Trim any excess dough.
  3. Line the crust with parchment paper and fill it with pie weights or dried beans. Bake for 15 minutes, then remove the parchment and weights and bake for an additional 10 minutes, or until the crust is golden brown. Set aside to cool.

Step 3: Prepare the Filling

  1. In a large bowl, whisk together the eggs and heavy cream. Add the grated Gruyère, diced ham, chopped parsley, salt, and pepper, and stir to combine.
  2. Pour the filling into the pre-baked crust and smooth the top with a spatula.

Step 4: Bake the Quiche

  1. Reduce the oven temperature to 350°F (175°C).
  2. Place the quiche in the oven and bake for 30-35 minutes, or until the filling is set and the top is golden brown. Remove from the oven and let it cool slightly.

Step 5: Prepare the Toppings

  1. In a skillet, fry the thick-cut ham slices until lightly browned and set aside.
  2. In the same skillet, fry the eggs to your desired level of doneness, keeping the yolks runny if preferred.

Step 6: Assemble the Croque Madame Quiche

  1. Place the fried ham slices on top of the baked quiche, overlapping them slightly.
  2. Sprinkle the grated Gruyère cheese over the ham, then carefully place the fried eggs on top.
  3. Finish with a generous grind of black pepper.

Step 7: Serve and Enjoy

Slice the Croque Madame Quiche into wedges and serve warm, accompanied by a simple green salad or fresh fruit. The combination of creamy, cheesy filling, savory ham, and luscious eggs makes this quiche a show-stopping addition to any brunch spread.

Bon Appétit!

The Croque Madame Quiche is a delightful fusion of French bistro charm and classic brunch comfort. Whether you’re hosting a special occasion or simply treating yourself to a leisurely morning meal, this quiche is a versatile and satisfying option. Its rich flavors and elegant presentation are sure to transport your taste buds to a cozy café in the heart of Paris. So, channel your inner chef and indulge in the art of French-inspired cooking with this exquisite Croque Madame Quiche.
