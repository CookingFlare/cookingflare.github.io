Satisfying Instant Pot Chicken Tortilla Soup Recipe


Are you craving a warm and comforting meal that’s bursting with flavor? Look no further than this satisfying Instant Pot Chicken Tortilla Soup recipe. Packed with tender chicken, hearty beans, and a medley of spices, this comforting soup is the perfect dish for a cozy night in or to impress your family and friends at your next gathering. Plus, with the convenience of the Instant Pot, you can have this delicious meal on the table in no time. Let’s dive into the recipe and discover how to make this delightful chicken tortilla soup.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 medium onion, diced
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 red bell pepper, chopped
  • 1 can (15 ounces) diced tomatoes
  • 1 can (15 ounces) black beans, drained and rinsed
  • 1 can (15 ounces) corn kernels, drained
  • 4 cups chicken broth
  • 1 teaspoon chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 bay leaf
  • Fresh lime juice, for serving
  • Fresh cilantro, for serving
  • Tortilla strips, for serving

Instructions

  1. Start by seasoning the chicken breasts with salt and pepper.
  2. Set the Instant Pot to “Sauté” mode and add the olive oil. Once hot, add the seasoned chicken breasts and cook until browned on both sides. This should take about 3-4 minutes per side. Once browned, remove the chicken from the Instant Pot and set it aside.
  3. In the same pot, add the diced onion, garlic, and red bell pepper. Sauté for a few minutes until the vegetables are soft and fragrant.
  4. Add the diced tomatoes, black beans, corn, chicken broth, chili powder, cumin, paprika, oregano, and the bay leaf to the pot. Give everything a good stir.
  5. Return the browned chicken breasts to the pot, nestling them into the liquid and vegetable mixture.
  6. Secure the lid on the Instant Pot and set the vent to the sealing position. Cook on high pressure for 10 minutes.
  7. Once the cooking time is complete, allow for a natural pressure release for 10 minutes, then carefully release any remaining pressure.
  8. Remove the chicken breasts from the pot and shred them using two forks. Return the shredded chicken to the pot and give everything a good stir.
  9. Squeeze in some fresh lime juice to brighten up the flavors of the soup.
  10. Serve the Instant Pot Chicken Tortilla Soup hot, garnished with fresh cilantro and crispy tortilla strips.

Tips and Variations

  • For an extra kick of heat, add a diced jalapeño pepper to the soup.
  • If you prefer a thicker soup, blend a portion of the soup in a blender or with an immersion blender before adding the shredded chicken back in.
  • This soup is incredibly versatile and can be customized with your favorite toppings such as avocado, sour cream, or shredded cheese.

Conclusion

This Instant Pot Chicken Tortilla Soup recipe is a fantastic way to enjoy a hearty and satisfying meal with minimal effort. The flavors blend together beautifully, creating a comforting bowl of soup that will warm you from the inside out. Whether you’re cooking for your family or hosting a gathering, this recipe is sure to impress. So, grab your Instant Pot and get ready to savor every spoonful of this delightful chicken tortilla soup!
