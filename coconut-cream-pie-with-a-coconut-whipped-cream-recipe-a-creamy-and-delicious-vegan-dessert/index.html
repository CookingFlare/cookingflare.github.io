Coconut Cream Pie with a Coconut Whipped Cream Recipe: A Creamy and Delicious Vegan Dessert


Coconut cream pie is a classic dessert that never fails to impress. With its creamy filling and flaky crust, it’s a treat that everyone can enjoy. And for those who follow a vegan lifestyle, we have a special twist on this beloved dessert - a vegan coconut cream pie with a homemade coconut whipped cream recipe!

Ingredients

For the crust:

  • 1 ½ cups of graham cracker crumbs
  • ¼ cup of melted coconut oil
  • 2 tablespoons of maple syrup

For the filling:

  • 1 can (14 ounces) of full-fat coconut milk
  • ½ cup of coconut cream
  • ½ cup of coconut sugar
  • ¼ cup of cornstarch
  • 1 teaspoon of vanilla extract
  • ½ teaspoon of salt

For the coconut whipped cream:

  • 1 can (14 ounces) of full-fat coconut milk, refrigerated overnight
  • 2 tablespoons of powdered sugar
  • 1 teaspoon of vanilla extract

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C).

  2. In a medium bowl, combine the graham cracker crumbs, melted coconut oil, and maple syrup. Mix until the crumbs are evenly coated. Press the mixture into the bottom of a 9-inch pie dish, forming an even crust. Bake for 10 minutes, then remove from the oven and let it cool completely.

  3. In a medium saucepan, combine the coconut milk, coconut cream, coconut sugar, cornstarch, vanilla extract, and salt. Whisk the mixture together until it’s smooth and well combined.

  4. Place the saucepan over medium heat and cook the mixture, stirring constantly, until it thickens and comes to a simmer. This should take about 8-10 minutes. Once it reaches the desired consistency, remove it from the heat and let it cool for a few minutes.

  5. Pour the coconut filling into the cooled crust, spreading it evenly. Smooth the top with a spatula. Place the pie in the refrigerator and let it chill for at least 4 hours, or until the filling has set.

  6. While the pie is chilling, it’s time to make the coconut whipped cream. Open the can of refrigerated coconut milk and scoop out the solid coconut cream that has risen to the top. Place it in a chilled mixing bowl.

  7. Using an electric mixer, beat the coconut cream on medium-high speed until it becomes light and fluffy. Add the powdered sugar and vanilla extract, and continue to beat until well combined.

  8. Once the pie has chilled and the coconut whipped cream is ready, remove the pie from the refrigerator. Spread a generous amount of the coconut whipped cream over the top of the pie, creating beautiful peaks and swirls.

  9. Serve the coconut cream pie immediately, or refrigerate until ready to serve. The pie can be enjoyed chilled or at room temperature, depending on your preference.

Conclusion

There you have it - a heavenly coconut cream pie with a luscious coconut whipped cream topping that just happens to be vegan! The creamy filling, paired with the flaky crust and the light, airy coconut whipped cream, creates a dessert that is sure to please everyone, regardless of their dietary preferences.

So the next time you’re craving a sweet treat, give this vegan coconut cream pie a try. It’s a delicious and indulgent dessert that will satisfy your cravings while also being kind to the planet and your body. Enjoy!
