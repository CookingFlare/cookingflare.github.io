15 Light and Healthy Pasta Recipes to Savor


Pasta is often considered a comfort food staple, but it can also be a part of a light and healthy diet. With the right ingredients, you can enjoy delicious pasta dishes that are not just satisfying but also nutritious. In this blog post, we will explore 15 light and healthy pasta recipes that are perfect for any meal. Each recipe is designed to be simple, flavorful, and packed with wholesome ingredients. Let’s dive into these culinary delights!

1. Zucchini Noodles with Pesto

Ingredients:

  • 4 medium zucchinis
  • 1 cup fresh basil leaves
  • 1/4 cup pine nuts
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Cherry tomatoes for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Use a spiralizer to create zucchini noodles. Set aside.
  2. In a food processor, combine basil, pine nuts, garlic, and olive oil. Blend until smooth. Season with salt and pepper.
  3. Toss the zucchini noodles with the pesto until well coated.
  4. Serve with halved cherry tomatoes on top for added flavor.

This dish is fresh, vibrant, and an excellent low-carb alternative to traditional pasta.

2. Whole Wheat Spaghetti with Spinach and Chickpeas

Ingredients:

  • 8 oz whole wheat spaghetti
  • 2 cups fresh spinach
  • 1 can (15 oz) chickpeas, drained and rinsed
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • Juice of 1 lemon
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Cook spaghetti according to package instructions; drain and set aside.
  2. In a pan, heat olive oil over medium heat. Add garlic and sauté until fragrant.
  3. Add spinach and chickpeas; cook until spinach wilts.
  4. Combine the spaghetti with the spinach and chickpeas. Drizzle with lemon juice, and season with salt and pepper.

This recipe provides a satisfying blend of protein and fiber, making it a perfect post-workout meal.

3. Lemon Garlic Shrimp Pasta

Ingredients:

  • 8 oz whole grain linguine
  • 1 lb shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • Juice and zest of 1 lemon
  • 1/4 teaspoon red pepper flakes
  • Fresh parsley for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Cook the linguine according to packaging instructions; reserve some pasta water.
  2. In a large skillet, heat olive oil over medium heat. Add garlic and red pepper flakes, cooking until fragrant.
  3. Add shrimp and cook until pink. Stir in lemon juice and zest.
  4. Toss the linguine with the shrimp mixture, adding reserved pasta water to create a light sauce. Garnish with parsley.

The combination of shrimp and lemon offers a refreshing twist that is both light and satisfying.

4. Mediterranean Orzo Salad

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup orzo pasta
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1 cucumber, diced
  • 1/2 cup feta cheese, crumbled
  • 1/4 cup olives, sliced
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon red wine vinegar
  • Fresh dill for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Cook orzo according to package instructions; cool under cold water.
  2. In a large bowl, combine orzo, tomatoes, cucumber, feta, and olives.
  3. In a separate bowl, whisk together olive oil and red wine vinegar. Pour over the salad and toss to combine.
  4. Garnish with fresh dill before serving.

This colorful dish is packed with nutrients and is perfect for a light lunch or picnic.

5. Spaghetti Squash with Marinara Sauce

Ingredients:

  • 1 medium spaghetti squash
  • 2 cups marinara sauce (homemade or store-bought)
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • Fresh basil for garnish
  • Parmesan cheese (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 400°F (200°C). Cut spaghetti squash in half and scoop out seeds.
  2. Drizzle with olive oil and place cut-side down on a baking sheet. Roast for 30-40 minutes or until tender.
  3. Once cooked, scrape the flesh with a fork to create spaghetti-like strands.
  4. Top with marinara sauce and garnish with fresh basil and Parmesan cheese if desired.

This recipe is a fantastic low-carb option that offers all the satisfaction of traditional pasta.

6. Caprese Pasta Salad

Ingredients:

  • 8 oz penne pasta
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1 cup mozzarella balls
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • 2 tablespoons balsamic glaze
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Cook penne according to package instructions; drain and cool.
  2. In a large bowl, combine penne, tomatoes, mozzarella, and basil.
  3. Drizzle with balsamic glaze and olive oil. Season with salt and pepper.
  4. Toss gently to combine and serve chilled.

This dish is light, refreshing, and perfect for summer days.

7. Garlic Lemon Broccoli Pasta

Ingredients:

  • 8 oz whole wheat spaghetti
  • 2 cups broccoli florets
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • Juice of 1 lemon
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Grated Parmesan (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Cook spaghetti according to package instructions. In the last 3 minutes, add broccoli florets.
  2. Drain and return to pot.
  3. In a pan, heat olive oil and garlic until fragrant. Add lemon juice and stir.
  4. Combine with spaghetti and broccoli; season with salt and pepper. Top with Parmesan if desired.

This dish is rich in vitamins and minerals while being light on calories.

8. Roasted Vegetable Pasta

Ingredients:

  • 8 oz pasta of choice
  • 2 cups mixed vegetables (bell peppers, zucchini, carrots)
  • 3 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon Italian seasoning
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 425°F (220°C). Toss vegetables with olive oil, garlic, Italian seasoning, salt, and pepper. Roast for 20-25 minutes.
  2. Cook pasta according to package instructions. Drain and toss with roasted vegetables.

This colorful dish is not only visually appealing but also packed with flavor and nutrients.

9. Creamy Avocado Pasta

Ingredients:

  • 8 oz fettuccine
  • 2 ripe avocados
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • Juice of 1 lime
  • 1/4 cup fresh basil leaves
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Cherry tomatoes for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Cook fettuccine according to package instructions; drain.
  2. In a blender, combine avocados, garlic, lime juice, basil, salt, and pepper. Blend until creamy.
  3. Toss pasta with avocado sauce and garnish with cherry tomatoes.

This creamy pasta is a delicious and healthy alternative to traditional creamy sauces.

10. Shrimp and Asparagus Pasta

Ingredients:

  • 8 oz linguine
  • 1 lb shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 1 bunch asparagus, trimmed and cut into pieces
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • Juice of 1 lemon
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Cook linguine according to package instructions; drain.
  2. In a skillet, heat olive oil over medium heat. Add garlic and asparagus; sauté until tender.
  3. Add shrimp and cook until pink. Stir in lemon juice, then combine with linguine.

This dish is a perfect balance of protein, veggies, and carbs, making it a wholesome meal.

11. Spinach and Ricotta Stuffed Shells

Ingredients:

  • 12 jumbo pasta shells
  • 1 cup ricotta cheese
  • 2 cups fresh spinach, chopped
  • 1/2 cup marinara sauce
  • 1/2 cup mozzarella cheese, shredded
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C). Cook pasta shells according to package instructions; drain.
  2. In a bowl, mix ricotta, spinach, salt, and pepper. Stuff each shell with the mixture.
  3. Spread marinara sauce in a baking dish and place stuffed shells on top. Sprinkle with mozzarella cheese.
  4. Bake for 20-25 minutes until cheese is bubbly.

These stuffed shells are a delightful way to enjoy pasta with a healthy twist.

12. Tomato Basil Pasta

Ingredients:

  • 8 oz spaghetti
  • 2 cups cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Cook spaghetti according to package instructions; drain.
  2. In a pan, heat olive oil and garlic until fragrant. Add cherry tomatoes and cook until they begin to burst.
  3. Toss spaghetti with the tomato mixture and add fresh basil. Season with salt and pepper.

This simple yet delicious dish highlights fresh ingredients and is perfect for weeknight dinners.

13. Cauliflower Pasta

Ingredients:

  • 8 oz pasta of choice
  • 1 head cauliflower, cut into florets
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese (optional)
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Cook pasta according to package instructions; drain.
  2. Steam cauliflower until tender.
