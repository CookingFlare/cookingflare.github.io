Roasted Sweet Potato Fries with Spicy Mayo: A Delicious and Addictive Snack


Are you in the mood for a scrumptious and addictive snack that will satisfy your cravings? Look no further than these Roasted Sweet Potato Fries with Spicy Mayo! These crispy fries paired with a tangy and spicy mayo dipping sauce are a match made in snack heaven. Whether you’re hosting a game night or simply treating yourself, this recipe is sure to be a crowd-pleaser.

Ingredients

  • 2 large sweet potatoes
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper

For Spicy Mayo:

  • 1/4 cup mayonnaise
  • 2 tablespoons sriracha sauce
  • 1 tablespoon lime juice

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 425°F (220°C). Line a baking sheet with parchment paper and set aside.

  2. Start by preparing the sweet potatoes. Peel them and cut them into long, thin strips resembling fries. Make sure they’re all similar in size so they cook evenly.

  3. In a large bowl, combine the olive oil, paprika, garlic powder, salt, and black pepper. Toss the sweet potato fries in the mixture until they’re well coated.

  4. Arrange the seasoned fries in a single layer on the prepared baking sheet. Make sure they’re not overcrowded to ensure they become crispy.

  5. Place the baking sheet in the preheated oven and roast for about 25-30 minutes, or until the fries are golden brown and crispy. Remember to flip them halfway through the cooking time to ensure even browning.

  6. While the fries are roasting, prepare the spicy mayo dipping sauce. In a small bowl, mix together the mayonnaise, sriracha sauce, and lime juice until well combined. Adjust the amount of sriracha sauce according to your spice preference.

  7. Once the sweet potato fries are done, remove them from the oven and let them cool slightly. Serve them hot with the spicy mayo dipping sauce on the side.

  8. Enjoy your delicious and addictive Roasted Sweet Potato Fries with Spicy Mayo! These fries are perfect as a party snack, a side dish, or even as a tasty appetizer. The crispy exterior combined with the creamy and spicy mayo will have you coming back for more.

Tips and Variations

  • For an extra kick of flavor, sprinkle some grated Parmesan cheese over the sweet potato fries before baking.
  • If you prefer a milder sauce, reduce the amount of sriracha sauce in the spicy mayo recipe.
  • Experiment with different spices and seasonings to customize the flavor of your sweet potato fries. Cumin, chili powder, or even a pinch of cinnamon can add an interesting twist.
  • Don’t be afraid to get creative with your dipping sauces! Try a garlic aioli, chipotle mayo, or a tangy BBQ sauce to elevate your snacking experience.

So there you have it – a simple yet irresistible recipe for Roasted Sweet Potato Fries with Spicy Mayo. These fries are guaranteed to be a hit with friends and family alike. Indulge in this addictive snack and let your taste buds rejoice!

Remember to enjoy responsibly, as it’s hard to resist going back for seconds (or thirds!). Happy snacking!
