Quick and Easy Margherita Flatbread Pizza Recipe


If you’re craving the classic flavors of Italy in a hurry, this Quick and Easy Margherita Flatbread Pizza recipe is the perfect solution. With a few simple ingredients and minimal prep time, you can enjoy a delicious homemade pizza that’s bursting with fresh flavors. Whether you’re cooking for a busy weeknight dinner or hosting a casual get-together with friends, this recipe is sure to impress without keeping you tied up in the kitchen.

Ingredients

  • 2 whole wheat or regular flatbreads
  • 1 cup tomato sauce
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 2 large tomatoes, thinly sliced
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • Olive oil
  • Salt and pepper

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C) and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
  2. Place the flatbreads on the prepared baking sheet.
  3. Spread a generous layer of tomato sauce over each flatbread, leaving a small border around the edges.
  4. Sprinkle the shredded mozzarella cheese evenly over the tomato sauce.
  5. Arrange the sliced tomatoes on top of the cheese.
  6. Drizzle a little olive oil over the pizzas and season with salt and pepper to taste.
  7. Bake in the preheated oven for 10-12 minutes, or until the cheese is bubbly and the edges of the flatbread are golden brown.
  8. Remove the pizzas from the oven and let them cool for a few minutes.
  9. Garnish with fresh basil leaves before slicing and serving.

Tips and Variations

  • For an extra burst of flavor, you can add thinly sliced fresh garlic or a sprinkle of dried oregano before baking.
  • If you prefer a crispier crust, you can pre-bake the flatbreads for a few minutes before adding the toppings.
  • Feel free to customize your Margherita flatbread pizza with additional ingredients such as olives, artichoke hearts, or thinly sliced bell peppers.
