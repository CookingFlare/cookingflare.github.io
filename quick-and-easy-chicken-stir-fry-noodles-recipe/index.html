Quick and Easy Chicken Stir-Fry Noodles Recipe


If you’re in need of a quick, satisfying, and flavorful meal, look no further than this delightful Chicken Stir-Fry Noodles recipe. Packed with tender chicken, vibrant vegetables, and savory noodles, this dish is a surefire way to please your taste buds and impress your loved ones without spending hours in the kitchen.

Ingredients

  • 8 oz (225g) chicken breast, thinly sliced
  • 8 oz (225g) egg noodles
  • 2 cups (300g) mixed vegetables (bell peppers, snap peas, carrots, and broccoli florets work well)
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 small onion, thinly sliced
  • 3 tbsp soy sauce
  • 2 tbsp oyster sauce
  • 1 tbsp sesame oil
  • 1 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 1/2 tsp red pepper flakes (optional)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh cilantro or green onions for garnish

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Noodles: Cook the egg noodles according to the package instructions. Drain and set aside.

  2. Cook the Chicken: In a large skillet or wok, heat the vegetable oil over medium-high heat. Add the sliced chicken and season with salt and pepper. Cook until the chicken is golden brown and no longer pink in the center, then transfer to a plate and set aside.

  3. Stir-Fry the Veggies: In the same skillet, add a bit more oil if needed and sauté the minced garlic and sliced onion until fragrant. Add the mixed vegetables and stir-fry for a few minutes until they are slightly tender but still crisp.

  4. Combine the Ingredients: Return the cooked chicken to the skillet. Add the soy sauce, oyster sauce, sesame oil, and red pepper flakes (if using). Toss everything together until well combined.

  5. Add the Noodles: Gently fold in the cooked egg noodles, ensuring that they are well coated with the sauce and mixed with the chicken and vegetables.

  6. Serve and Enjoy: Once everything is heated through, taste and adjust the seasoning if necessary. Transfer the chicken stir-fry noodles to a serving dish and garnish with fresh cilantro or green onions.

Tips for Success

  • Prep Ahead: You can slice the chicken and vegetables in advance to make the cooking process even quicker.
  • Customize the Veggies: Feel free to use your favorite vegetables or whatever you have on hand.
  • Spice it Up: Add extra red pepper flakes or a drizzle of chili oil for a spicier kick.
  • Leftovers: This dish reheats beautifully, making it a perfect option for meal prep.

Final Thoughts

This Chicken Stir-Fry Noodles recipe is a fantastic go-to for busy weeknights or lazy weekends when you want something delicious but don’t want to spend hours in the kitchen. It’s versatile, customizable, and guaranteed to please even the pickiest eaters. Plus, the leftovers are just as tasty, making it a great option for a flavorful lunch the next day.

So, the next time you’re craving a satisfying, Asian-inspired dish that won’t have you slaving away in the kitchen, give this quick and easy chicken stir-fry noodles recipe a try. You’ll be amazed at how something so simple can be so incredibly delicious!

Now, it’s your turn to grab those ingredients and whip up this delightful dish. Enjoy your culinary adventure and savor every flavorful bite!
