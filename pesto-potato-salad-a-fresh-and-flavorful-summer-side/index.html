Pesto Potato Salad: A Fresh and Flavorful Summer Side


As the sun starts to shine brighter and the days grow warmer, it’s the perfect time to embrace the flavors of summer with a refreshing and vibrant potato salad. This Pesto Potato Salad is a delightful twist on the classic dish, infusing it with the zesty, aromatic flavors of homemade pesto. Whether you’re hosting a backyard barbecue, planning a picnic in the park, or simply looking for a delicious side dish to accompany your weeknight dinner, this recipe is sure to become a staple in your summer menu.

Ingredients

  • 2 pounds baby potatoes, halved
  • 1/2 cup fresh basil leaves
  • 1/4 cup pine nuts
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/2 cup extra virgin olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons lemon juice
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1/4 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1/4 cup diced red onion
  • 1/4 cup sliced black olives
  • 1/4 cup crumbled feta cheese
  • Fresh basil leaves for garnish

Instructions

Step 1: Boil the Potatoes

Start by boiling the baby potatoes in a large pot of salted water until they are tender but still firm, for about 10-15 minutes. Once cooked, drain the potatoes and set them aside to cool to room temperature.

Step 2: Prepare the Pesto

While the potatoes are cooling, it’s time to make the star of the show – the pesto. In a food processor, combine the fresh basil leaves, pine nuts, garlic, and grated Parmesan cheese. Pulse the mixture until it becomes a coarse paste. Then, with the food processor running, slowly pour in the extra virgin olive oil and lemon juice until the pesto reaches a smooth and creamy consistency. Season with salt and pepper to taste.

Step 3: Assemble the Salad

In a large mixing bowl, gently toss the cooled potatoes with the freshly prepared pesto, ensuring that each potato is coated evenly. Add the cherry tomatoes, red onion, black olives, and crumbled feta cheese to the bowl, and gently fold the ingredients together to distribute the flavors.

Step 4: Garnish and Serve

To finish, garnish the Pesto Potato Salad with a few fresh basil leaves for a pop of color and an extra burst of herbal fragrance. Refrigerate the salad for at least an hour to allow the flavors to meld together before serving.

Enjoy the Freshness of Summer

This Pesto Potato Salad is a celebration of the vibrant flavors of summer, combining the earthy richness of potatoes with the aromatic essence of homemade pesto. With its bright colors and lively flavors, this salad is not only a feast for the taste buds but also a visual delight on any summer table.

Whether served alongside grilled chicken, as a complement to a charcuterie board, or as a standalone dish, this Pesto Potato Salad is a versatile and delightful addition to any summer gathering. Embrace the season’s bounty with this fresh and flavorful side dish that is sure to become a beloved favorite among family and friends.

So, gather your ingredients, embrace the simplicity of summer cooking, and savor the joy of creating memorable dishes with this Pesto Potato Salad recipe. Happy cooking and happy summer!
