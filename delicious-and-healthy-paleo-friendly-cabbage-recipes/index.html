Delicious and Healthy Paleo-Friendly Cabbage Recipes


Are you looking for delicious and healthy recipes that are also paleo-friendly? Look no further than these mouthwatering cabbage recipes! Cabbage is a versatile and nutritious vegetable that can be used in a variety of dishes, making it a perfect ingredient for anyone following a paleo diet. From crunchy coleslaw to savory stir-fries, these recipes will show you how to make the most of this humble yet powerful vegetable.

1. Cabbage and Sausage Skillet

Ingredients:

  • 1 head of cabbage, thinly sliced
  • 1 pound of paleo-friendly sausage, sliced
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon of paprika
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley for garnish

Instructions:

  1. In a large skillet, cook the sausage over medium heat until browned.
  2. Add the chopped onion and minced garlic, and sauté until the onion is soft and translucent.
  3. Add the sliced cabbage to the skillet and sprinkle with paprika, salt, and pepper.
  4. Cover the skillet and cook for 10-15 minutes, stirring occasionally, until the cabbage is tender.
  5. Serve the skillet dish hot, garnished with fresh parsley.

2. Cabbage and Bacon Stir-Fry

Ingredients:

  • 1 head of cabbage, shredded
  • 6 slices of paleo-friendly bacon, chopped
  • 1 bell pepper, sliced
  • 2 tablespoons of coconut aminos
  • 1 tablespoon of apple cider vinegar
  • 1 teaspoon of grated ginger
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • Red pepper flakes for heat (optional)

Instructions:

  1. In a large wok or skillet, cook the chopped bacon over medium heat until crispy.
  2. Add the sliced bell pepper to the bacon and cook until slightly softened.
  3. Stir in the shredded cabbage, coconut aminos, apple cider vinegar, grated ginger, and minced garlic.
  4. Cook for 5-7 minutes, stirring frequently, until the cabbage is tender yet still slightly crisp.
  5. If desired, sprinkle with red pepper flakes for a spicy kick before serving.

3. Cabbage and Carrot Slaw

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 head of cabbage, thinly sliced
  • 2 large carrots, grated
  • 1/4 cup of paleo-friendly mayonnaise
  • 2 tablespoons of apple cider vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon of raw honey
  • 1 teaspoon of celery seeds
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a large bowl, combine the thinly sliced cabbage and grated carrots.
  2. In a separate bowl, whisk together the paleo-friendly mayonnaise, apple cider vinegar, raw honey, celery seeds, salt, and pepper.
  3. Pour the dressing over the cabbage and carrots, and toss until well coated.
  4. Refrigerate the slaw for at least 30 minutes before serving to allow the flavors to meld together.

4. Cabbage and Beef Soup

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound of grass-fed ground beef
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 head of cabbage, chopped
  • 4 cups of beef broth
  • 1 can of diced tomatoes
  • 1 teaspoon of dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley for garnish

Instructions:

  1. In a large pot, brown the ground beef over medium heat, breaking it apart with a spoon as it cooks.
  2. Add the chopped onion and minced garlic, and sauté until fragrant.
  3. Stir in the chopped cabbage, beef broth, diced tomatoes, dried oregano, salt, and pepper.
  4. Bring the soup to a boil, then reduce the heat and simmer for 20-25 minutes until the cabbage is tender.
  5. Serve the hearty cabbage and beef soup hot, garnished with fresh parsley.

Conclusion

Cabbage is truly a powerhouse vegetable that can elevate any paleo-friendly meal. These delicious and healthy cabbage recipes are not only satisfying and flavorful but also easy to prepare. Whether you’re craving a comforting soup, a zesty slaw, or a hearty skillet dish, these recipes are sure to become staples in your paleo cooking repertoire. Embrace the versatility of cabbage and enjoy the nourishing benefits of these delightful dishes!
