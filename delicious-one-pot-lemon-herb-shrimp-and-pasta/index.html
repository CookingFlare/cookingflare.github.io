Delicious One Pot Lemon Herb Shrimp and Pasta


If you’re looking for a quick and flavorful meal that’s perfect for a busy weeknight or a relaxed weekend dinner, then this One Pot Lemon Herb Shrimp and Pasta is just what you need. This dish combines succulent shrimp, al dente pasta, and a burst of fresh herbs and lemon, all cooked together in one pot for easy preparation and minimal cleanup.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound of large shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 8 ounces of linguine or spaghetti
  • 3 tablespoons of olive oil
  • 4 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 2 cups of chicken broth
  • 1 cup of cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1/4 cup of fresh parsley, chopped
  • 1/4 cup of fresh basil, chopped
  • Zest and juice of 1 lemon
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Grated Parmesan cheese for garnish

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Ingredients: Start by preparing all the ingredients. Peel and devein the shrimp, mince the garlic, halve the cherry tomatoes, and chop the fresh herbs.

  2. Cook the Shrimp: In a large pot or deep skillet, heat 2 tablespoons of olive oil over medium-high heat. Add the shrimp to the pot and season with salt and pepper. Cook the shrimp for 2-3 minutes on each side until they are pink and opaque. Remove the shrimp from the pot and set aside.

  3. Cook the Aromatics: In the same pot, add the remaining tablespoon of olive oil and the minced garlic. Sauté the garlic for about 1 minute until it becomes fragrant.

  4. Add the Pasta and Broth: Break the linguine or spaghetti in half and add it to the pot along with the chicken broth. Bring the broth to a boil, then reduce the heat to a simmer. Cook the pasta for about 9-10 minutes, stirring occasionally, until it is almost al dente.

  5. Finish the Dish: Once the pasta is nearly cooked, add the halved cherry tomatoes, chopped parsley, basil, lemon zest, and lemon juice to the pot. Stir everything together and let it cook for an additional 2-3 minutes until the tomatoes soften and release their juices.

  6. Add the Shrimp and Serve: Return the cooked shrimp to the pot and toss everything together. Taste and adjust the seasoning with salt and pepper if needed. To serve, portion the pasta and shrimp into bowls, garnish with grated Parmesan cheese, and a sprinkle of fresh herbs.

Enjoy Your One Pot Lemon Herb Shrimp and Pasta!

This delightful and vibrant dish is perfect for any occasion. The combination of succulent shrimp, al dente pasta, and the bright flavors of lemon and fresh herbs will surely impress your friends and family. Plus, with only one pot to clean, this recipe makes for an easy and fuss-free meal.

Whether you’re a seasoned home cook or just starting to explore the joys of cooking, this One Pot Lemon Herb Shrimp and Pasta is a must-try. It’s a versatile dish that can be easily customized with your favorite vegetables or additional seasonings, making it a delightful addition to your recipe collection.

So, next time you’re craving a flavorful and satisfying meal without the hassle of multiple pots and pans, give this recipe a try. Your taste buds will thank you!
