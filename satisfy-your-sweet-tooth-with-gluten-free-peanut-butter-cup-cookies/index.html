Satisfy Your Sweet Tooth with Gluten-Free Peanut Butter Cup Cookies


If you’re a fan of the classic combination of peanut butter and chocolate, you’re in for a treat with these gluten-free peanut butter cup cookies. Not only are they a delightful indulgence for those with a sweet tooth, but they also cater to individuals with gluten sensitivities or dietary restrictions. These cookies are a perfect blend of chewy, nutty, and rich chocolatey flavors, making them an irresistible addition to any dessert spread.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup creamy peanut butter
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 large egg
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 12-14 gluten-free mini peanut butter cups, unwrapped and halved
  • 1/2 cup semisweet chocolate chips
  • Flaky sea salt for sprinkling (optional)

Instructions

  1. Preheat the Oven: Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C) and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.

  2. Prepare the Dough: In a mixing bowl, combine the creamy peanut butter, granulated sugar, egg, and vanilla extract. Mix until the ingredients are well combined and form a smooth dough.

  3. Form the Cookies: Using a cookie scoop or your hands, form the dough into small balls and place them on the prepared baking sheet. Gently flatten each ball with the back of a fork, creating a crisscross pattern on top of each cookie.

  4. Add the Peanut Butter Cups: Press a halved peanut butter cup into the center of each cookie, ensuring it is nestled into the dough.

  5. Bake the Cookies: Place the baking sheet in the preheated oven and bake for 10-12 minutes, or until the edges of the cookies are lightly golden. Remove from the oven and allow the cookies to cool on the baking sheet for a few minutes before transferring them to a wire rack to cool completely.

  6. Melt the Chocolate: In a microwave-safe bowl, melt the semisweet chocolate chips in 30-second intervals, stirring between each interval, until smooth and fully melted.

  7. Dip the Cookies: Once the cookies have cooled, dip the bottom of each cookie into the melted chocolate, allowing any excess to drip off. Place the dipped cookies back on the parchment paper.

  8. Add a Finishing Touch: If desired, sprinkle a pinch of flaky sea salt over the melted chocolate for a delicious sweet and salty contrast.

  9. Let the Chocolate Set: Allow the chocolate to set at room temperature or expedite the process by placing the cookies in the refrigerator for a few minutes.

  10. Enjoy!: Once the chocolate has set, your gluten-free peanut butter cup cookies are ready to be enjoyed. Store any leftovers in an airtight container at room temperature for up to 3-4 days.

Conclusion

These gluten-free peanut butter cup cookies are a delightful twist on the classic peanut butter and chocolate combination. Whether you’re catering to a gluten-free diet or simply looking for a scrumptious treat, these cookies are sure to satisfy your sweet cravings. With their chewy texture, nutty flavor, and decadent chocolatey finish, they’re a perfect addition to any cookie jar or dessert platter. So, roll up your sleeves, gather your ingredients, and treat yourself to a batch of these irresistible gluten-free cookies. Your taste buds will thank you!
