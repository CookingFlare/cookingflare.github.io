Quick and Easy Low Carb Zucchini Chips


Are you searching for a guilt-free snack that’s both delicious and healthy? Look no further than these Crispy Baked Low Carb Zucchini Chips! Packed with flavor and crunch, these zucchini chips are the perfect alternative to traditional potato chips. Not only are they easy to make, but they are also low in carbs, making them an ideal choice for anyone following a low-carb or keto lifestyle.

Ingredients

  • 2 medium zucchinis, thinly sliced
  • 1/2 cup almond flour
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 2 large eggs

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 425°F (220°C) and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.

  2. In a shallow bowl, whisk together the almond flour, Parmesan cheese, garlic powder, paprika, salt, and black pepper. This flavorful coating will give the zucchini chips a satisfying crunch and a burst of savory taste.

  3. In another bowl, beat the eggs to create an egg wash for the zucchini slices.

  4. Dip each zucchini slice into the egg wash, allowing any excess to drip off, then dredge it in the almond flour mixture, ensuring it is evenly coated. Place the coated zucchini slice on the prepared baking sheet, and repeat this process with the remaining zucchini slices.

  5. Once all the zucchini slices are coated and arranged on the baking sheet, lightly spray them with cooking spray. This will help them achieve a crispy texture as they bake.

  6. Bake the zucchini chips in the preheated oven for 25-30 minutes, or until they are golden and crispy. Be sure to flip the chips halfway through the baking time to ensure even browning.

  7. Once the zucchini chips are done, remove them from the oven and allow them to cool for a few minutes. Serve them with your favorite dipping sauce or enjoy them on their own!

Tips and Variations

  • For an extra kick of flavor, try adding a pinch of cayenne pepper to the almond flour mixture.
  • Experiment with different seasonings such as Italian herbs, onion powder, or chili powder to customize the flavor of your zucchini chips.
  • If you prefer a dairy-free option, you can substitute the Parmesan cheese with nutritional yeast for a cheesy, umami flavor.

Health Benefits of Zucchini Chips

Zucchinis are not only low in carbs, but they are also a good source of fiber, vitamins, and minerals. By swapping out starchy potatoes for nutrient-rich zucchinis, you can indulge in a satisfying snack without the guilt. These zucchini chips are a great way to sneak in some extra veggies into your diet while satisfying your cravings for something crispy and savory.

Final Thoughts

Whether you’re looking for a wholesome snack to enjoy on movie night or a crowd-pleasing appetizer for your next gathering, these Crispy Baked Low Carb Zucchini Chips are sure to be a hit. With their irresistible crunch and mouthwatering flavor, these zucchini chips are a healthy alternative to store-bought snacks. Give this easy recipe a try and discover a new favorite snack that’s both nutritious and delicious!
