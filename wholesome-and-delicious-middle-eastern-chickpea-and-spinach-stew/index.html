Wholesome and Delicious: Middle Eastern Chickpea and Spinach Stew


If you’re looking for a hearty, flavorful, and nutritious dish to warm your soul, this Middle Eastern Chickpea and Spinach Stew is the perfect choice. Packed with aromatic spices, tender chickpeas, and vibrant spinach, this stew is a celebration of Middle Eastern flavors that will leave you craving more. Whether you’re a seasoned cook or a beginner in the kitchen, this recipe is sure to impress with its simplicity and depth of flavor.

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 large onion, finely chopped
  • 4 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon ground coriander
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground turmeric
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 teaspoon cayenne pepper
  • 1 can (15 ounces) chickpeas, drained and rinsed
  • 1 can (14 ounces) diced tomatoes
  • 3 cups vegetable broth
  • 1 bunch fresh spinach, tough stems removed, coarsely chopped
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh lemon juice for serving
  • Cooked couscous or rice for serving

Instructions

  1. In a large pot, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the chopped onion and sauté until softened, about 5 minutes.

  2. Add the minced garlic, ground cumin, ground coriander, ground turmeric, ground cinnamon, and cayenne pepper to the pot. Stir the spices into the onion and garlic mixture, allowing them to toast and release their aromas for about 1 minute.

  3. Add the drained and rinsed chickpeas to the pot, stirring to coat them with the aromatic spice mixture.

  4. Pour in the diced tomatoes and vegetable broth, stirring to combine. Bring the stew to a gentle simmer and let it cook for about 15 minutes, allowing the flavors to meld together.

  5. Add the chopped spinach to the pot, stirring it into the stew. Let the spinach wilt and the flavors continue to develop for an additional 5 minutes.

  6. Season the stew with salt and pepper to taste, adjusting the seasoning as needed.

  7. Serve the Middle Eastern Chickpea and Spinach Stew over cooked couscous or rice, and drizzle with a squeeze of fresh lemon juice for a bright finish.

Tips and Variations

  • For an extra kick of flavor, add a pinch of ground cumin and coriander to the finished stew just before serving.
  • If you prefer a thicker stew, you can mash some of the chickpeas with a fork or potato masher before adding the spinach.
  • This stew is even more flavorful the next day, so feel free to make it ahead of time and reheat it for a quick and satisfying meal.

Conclusion

With its rich blend of spices, hearty chickpeas, and vibrant spinach, this Middle Eastern Chickpea and Spinach Stew is a comforting and nourishing dish that’s perfect for any occasion. Whether you’re cooking for a crowd or simply craving a wholesome meal, this recipe is a true crowd-pleaser that will transport your taste buds to the heart of the Middle East. Enjoy the warm, aromatic flavors of this stew and savor every spoonful of this delightful culinary adventure.
