Mexican Sopes with Fried Tortillas and Beans Recipe


Are you ready to embark on a culinary journey to Mexico? Get your taste buds ready because today, we’ll be diving into the delicious world of Mexican cuisine with a mouthwatering recipe for Sopes with Fried Tortillas and Beans. This traditional Mexican dish is a true delight, packed with flavors and textures that will leave you craving for more. So, let’s roll up our sleeves and get cooking!

Ingredients

For the Sopes:

  • 2 cups masa harina (corn flour)
  • 1 ¼ cups warm water
  • ¼ teaspoon salt
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil

For the Fried Tortillas:

  • 4 corn tortillas
  • Vegetable oil for frying

For the Beans:

  • 1 cup pinto beans, cooked and mashed
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • ½ teaspoon cumin
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

Step 1: Prepare the Sopes

  1. In a large mixing bowl, combine the masa harina, warm water, salt, and vegetable oil. Mix well until a soft dough forms.
  2. Divide the dough into small balls, about the size of golf balls.
  3. Place a ball of dough between two sheets of plastic wrap or parchment paper and flatten it with a tortilla press or the bottom of a heavy pan until it forms a thin disc, about 1/4 inch thick.
  4. Remove the plastic wrap and transfer the flattened dough to a hot, dry skillet or comal over medium heat. Cook for about 1-2 minutes on each side until lightly browned and cooked through. Repeat with the remaining dough.

Step 2: Fry the Tortillas

  1. Heat vegetable oil in a deep skillet or frying pan over medium-high heat.
  2. Carefully place the corn tortillas, one at a time, into the hot oil. Fry until golden brown and crispy, about 1-2 minutes per side. Remove from the oil and drain on a paper towel-lined plate. Repeat with the remaining tortillas.

Step 3: Prepare the Beans

  1. In a separate skillet, heat the vegetable oil over medium heat.
  2. Add the chopped onion and minced garlic to the skillet and sauté until the onion is translucent and fragrant.
  3. Add the mashed pinto beans to the skillet and stir well to combine.
  4. Season with cumin, salt, and pepper to taste. Cook for an additional 5 minutes, stirring occasionally, until the beans are heated through.

Step 4: Assemble the Sopes

  1. Take a fried tortilla and spread a generous amount of the prepared beans on top.
  2. Garnish with your favorite toppings such as shredded lettuce, diced tomatoes, crumbled queso fresco, sliced avocado, and a drizzle of hot sauce.
  3. Repeat with the remaining tortillas and serve immediately.

Enjoy the Authentic Flavors of Mexico!

Congratulations, you’ve successfully prepared authentic Mexican Sopes with Fried Tortillas and Beans! The combination of crispy fried tortillas, savory beans, and vibrant toppings creates a symphony of flavors that will transport you straight to the streets of Mexico.

Whether you’re hosting a Mexican-themed dinner party or simply craving a taste of Mexico, this recipe is sure to impress. So gather your ingredients, follow the steps, and indulge in the rich and satisfying flavors of Mexican cuisine.

Remember, the beauty of cooking lies in experimentation, so feel free to add your own twist to this classic recipe. From adding a squeeze of lime to sprinkling some cilantro on top, the possibilities are endless. So get creative and enjoy the culinary adventure that Mexican cuisine has to offer!

¡Buen provecho!
