Greek-style Roasted Brussels Sprouts: A Flavorful and Healthy Side


Whether you’re a Brussels sprouts lover or skeptic, this Greek-style roasted Brussels sprouts recipe is sure to win you over. Packed with flavor and boasting numerous health benefits, this side dish is a must-try for anyone looking to add some excitement to their meal. The combination of Greek-inspired ingredients and the crispy texture of roasted Brussels sprouts creates a culinary experience that will leave your taste buds dancing.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound Brussels sprouts, trimmed and halved
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried thyme
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • 1/4 cup crumbled feta cheese

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 425°F (220°C). Line a baking sheet with parchment paper and set it aside.

  2. In a large bowl, combine the olive oil, lemon juice, minced garlic, dried oregano, dried thyme, salt, and pepper. Whisk the ingredients together until well combined.

  3. Add the halved Brussels sprouts to the bowl and toss them in the marinade, ensuring that they are evenly coated.

  4. Transfer the Brussels sprouts to the prepared baking sheet, spreading them out in a single layer. This allows them to roast evenly and develop that irresistible crispiness.

  5. Place the baking sheet in the preheated oven and roast the Brussels sprouts for 20-25 minutes, or until they are golden brown and tender. Be sure to give them a gentle shake or stir halfway through the cooking time to ensure even browning.

  6. Once roasted to perfection, remove the Brussels sprouts from the oven and let them cool slightly.

  7. Sprinkle the crumbled feta cheese over the roasted Brussels sprouts, allowing the creamy tanginess to complement the flavors of the Greek-inspired marinade.

  8. Serve the Greek-style roasted Brussels sprouts as a flavorful and healthy side dish alongside your favorite main course. They pair exceptionally well with grilled chicken, lamb, or even as part of a vegetarian feast.

Health Benefits of Brussels Sprouts

Apart from being a delicious addition to your meal, Brussels sprouts offer numerous health benefits. They are rich in vitamins C and K, as well as folate, fiber, and antioxidants. These nutrients contribute to a strong immune system, improved digestion, and reduced inflammation. Brussels sprouts also contain compounds that may have cancer-fighting properties, making them an excellent choice for those interested in maintaining a healthy lifestyle.

Final Thoughts

Greek-style roasted Brussels sprouts are a simple yet elegant way to elevate your culinary repertoire. Their crispy texture, combined with the tanginess of feta cheese and Greek-inspired flavors, is a winning combination that will leave your taste buds satisfied. Plus, with their abundance of health benefits, you can enjoy this flavorful side dish guilt-free. So, why not give this recipe a try and discover a new favorite way to enjoy Brussels sprouts? Your taste buds and your body will thank you. Enjoy!
