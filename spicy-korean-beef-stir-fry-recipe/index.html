Spicy Korean Beef Stir Fry Recipe


Are you craving a flavorful and spicy dish that will tantalize your taste buds? Look no further than this mouthwatering Spicy Korean Beef Stir Fry recipe! Packed with bold Korean flavors and a kick of heat, this dish is perfect for those who love a bit of spiciness in their meals.

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound of thinly sliced beef (such as ribeye or flank steak)
  • 2 tablespoons of soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon of sesame oil
  • 3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon of gochujang (Korean chili paste)
  • 1 tablespoon of brown sugar
  • 1 onion, sliced
  • 1 red bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 green bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 carrot, julienned
  • 4 green onions, chopped
  • Sesame seeds for garnish
  • Cooked rice for serving

Instructions:

  1. Marinate the Beef: In a bowl, combine the thinly sliced beef with soy sauce, sesame oil, minced garlic, gochujang, and brown sugar. Mix well to ensure the beef is coated evenly. Let it marinate for at least 30 minutes to allow the flavors to meld together.

  2. Prepare the Vegetables: While the beef is marinating, prepare the vegetables by slicing the onion, red bell pepper, green bell pepper, and julienned carrot. Chop the green onions and set them aside for later.

  3. Stir Fry: Heat a wok or large skillet over high heat. Add a bit of oil and stir fry the marinated beef until it is cooked through. Once the beef is cooked, remove it from the pan and set it aside.

  4. Cook the Vegetables: In the same pan, add a bit more oil if needed and stir fry the sliced onion until it becomes translucent. Add the sliced bell peppers and julienned carrot, and continue to stir fry until the vegetables are slightly tender.

  5. Combine Beef and Vegetables: Return the cooked beef to the pan with the vegetables. Toss everything together to combine and let it cook for an additional minute or two to heat everything through.

  6. Finish and Serve: Sprinkle chopped green onions and sesame seeds over the dish for added freshness and nuttiness. Serve the Spicy Korean Beef Stir Fry hot over steamed rice and enjoy the explosion of flavors with every bite!

Tips:

  • Adjust the level of spiciness by adding more or less gochujang according to your preference.
  • You can customize this dish by adding other vegetables like mushrooms, zucchini, or broccoli.
  • For an extra kick of flavor, consider adding a sprinkle of Korean red pepper flakes (gochugaru) during cooking.
