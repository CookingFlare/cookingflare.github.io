Italian-Style Chicken and Spinach Stuffed Chicken Breast with Ricotta


If you’re looking for a show-stopping dish that’s bursting with Italian flavors, then this Italian-style chicken and spinach stuffed chicken breast with ricotta is the perfect recipe for you. This dish is not only delicious but also a great way to impress your family and friends with your culinary skills. The combination of tender chicken breast, savory spinach, and creamy ricotta, all seasoned with classic Italian herbs and spices, creates a flavorful and satisfying meal that will leave everyone asking for seconds.

Ingredients

  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 2 cups fresh spinach, chopped
  • 1 cup ricotta cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/4 cup sun-dried tomatoes, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Olive oil for cooking

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).

  2. In a mixing bowl, combine the chopped spinach, ricotta cheese, Parmesan cheese, sun-dried tomatoes, minced garlic, dried oregano, dried basil, salt, and pepper. Mix until well combined.

  3. Lay the chicken breasts flat on a cutting board. Using a sharp knife, carefully slice each chicken breast horizontally to create a pocket without cutting all the way through.

  4. Stuff each chicken breast with the spinach and ricotta mixture, dividing it evenly among the four breasts.

  5. In a large skillet, heat a drizzle of olive oil over medium-high heat. Once the oil is hot, add the stuffed chicken breasts to the skillet and cook for 3-4 minutes on each side, or until golden brown.

  6. Transfer the browned chicken breasts to a baking dish and bake in the preheated oven for 20-25 minutes, or until the chicken is cooked through and reaches an internal temperature of 165°F (74°C).

  7. Once cooked, remove the chicken from the oven and let it rest for a few minutes before serving.

  8. Serve the Italian-style chicken and spinach stuffed chicken breast with ricotta with a side of your choice, such as a fresh salad, roasted vegetables, or garlic bread.

Tips and Variations

  • For an extra burst of flavor, consider adding some chopped fresh basil or a sprinkle of red pepper flakes to the spinach and ricotta mixture.
  • If you’re a fan of melted cheese, top each stuffed chicken breast with a slice of mozzarella or provolone cheese before baking for an extra indulgent touch.
  • Feel free to customize the filling by adding other ingredients such as sautéed mushrooms, diced bell peppers, or even a dollop of pesto for a unique twist.

This Italian-style chicken and spinach stuffed chicken breast with ricotta recipe is a versatile dish that can be served for a special dinner party, a family gathering, or a cozy night in. The flavors and textures of this dish come together beautifully, making it a memorable and satisfying meal that will have everyone reaching for seconds. Enjoy the delightful taste of Italy right in your own kitchen with this irresistible stuffed chicken breast recipe!
