Homemade Thanksgiving Pie Recipes Worth Trying


Thanksgiving is a time for family, gratitude, and of course, delicious food. One iconic dessert that graces tables during this festive occasion is the humble pie. Whether you’re a fan of traditional flavors or looking to add a modern twist to your Thanksgiving spread, we have curated a selection of homemade pie recipes that are sure to impress your loved ones. From classic pumpkin pie to unique variations like pecan and apple crumble pie, there’s something for every palate. Get ready to elevate your Thanksgiving feast with these delectable pie recipes.

Classic Pumpkin Pie

Ingredients:

  • 1 9-inch unbaked pie crust
  • 1 can (15 oz) pumpkin puree
  • 1 cup brown sugar
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground ginger
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground cloves
  • 3 large eggs
  • 1 cup evaporated milk

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 425°F (220°C).
  2. In a mixing bowl, combine the pumpkin puree, brown sugar, spices, eggs, and evaporated milk. Mix until smooth.
  3. Pour the filling into the unbaked pie crust.
  4. Bake for 15 minutes, then reduce the oven temperature to 350°F (175°C) and bake for an additional 45-50 minutes.
  5. Let the pie cool before serving. Enjoy with a dollop of whipped cream!

Pecan Pie

Ingredients:

  • 1 9-inch unbaked pie crust
  • 1 cup corn syrup
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 3 eggs
  • 1/3 cup melted butter
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 1/2 cups pecan halves

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. In a bowl, mix the corn syrup, sugar, eggs, butter, and vanilla extract until well combined.
  3. Stir in the pecans and pour the mixture into the pie crust.
  4. Bake for 50-60 minutes until the filling is set.
  5. Allow the pie to cool before slicing. Serve with a scoop of vanilla ice cream for a decadent treat.

Apple Crumble Pie

Ingredients:

  • 1 9-inch unbaked pie crust
  • 5 cups thinly sliced apples
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1 tablespoon flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt

Crumble Topping:

  • 1/2 cup flour
  • 1/2 cup rolled oats
  • 1/2 cup brown sugar
  • 1/4 cup softened butter

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. In a bowl, combine the sliced apples, sugar, flour, cinnamon, and salt. Mix well and pour into the pie crust.
  3. In a separate bowl, mix the flour, oats, brown sugar, and butter until crumbly.
  4. Sprinkle the crumble mixture over the apples.
  5. Bake for 40-45 minutes or until the topping is golden brown.
  6. Let the pie cool slightly before serving. Pair it with a scoop of cinnamon ice cream for a delightful combination.

Conclusion

This Thanksgiving, treat your family and friends to the comforting aroma and indulgent taste of homemade pies. Whether you opt for the timeless appeal of pumpkin pie, the richness of pecan pie, or the comforting flavors of apple crumble pie, these recipes are bound to be a hit at your holiday gathering. Get creative in the kitchen, experiment with flavors, and savor the joy of sharing delicious food with your loved ones. Happy baking and happy Thanksgiving!
