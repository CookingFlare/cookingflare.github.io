Gooey and Delicious S'mores Brownies


If you’re a fan of the classic campfire treat, s’mores, and you also happen to love brownies, then you’re in for a real treat with this gooey and delicious S’mores Brownies recipe. These decadent brownies combine the rich, fudgy goodness of chocolate brownies with the irresistible flavors of toasted marshmallows and graham crackers. Whether you’re hosting a summer BBQ, a cozy winter gathering, or just craving a sweet indulgence, these S’mores Brownies are sure to impress and satisfy your sweet tooth.

Ingredients

For the Brownie Base:

  • 1 cup unsalted butter, melted
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 cup brown sugar
  • 4 large eggs
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt

For the S’mores Topping:

  • 1 1/2 cups mini marshmallows
  • 4 full-size graham crackers, broken into pieces
  • 1 cup milk chocolate chips

Instructions

  1. Preheat the Oven: Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease a 9x13 inch baking pan and set it aside.

  2. Prepare the Brownie Batter: In a large mixing bowl, combine the melted butter, granulated sugar, and brown sugar. Beat in the eggs and vanilla extract until well combined. In a separate bowl, sift together the flour, cocoa powder, and salt. Gradually add the dry ingredients to the wet ingredients, mixing until just combined.

  3. Bake the Brownies: Pour the brownie batter into the prepared baking pan, spreading it out evenly. Bake in the preheated oven for 25-30 minutes, or until the brownies are just set in the middle.

  4. Add the S’mores Topping: Remove the brownies from the oven and immediately sprinkle the mini marshmallows, graham cracker pieces, and chocolate chips over the top. Return the pan to the oven and bake for an additional 5-7 minutes, or until the marshmallows are golden brown and gooey.

  5. Cool and Serve: Allow the S’mores Brownies to cool in the pan for at least 10 minutes before cutting into squares and serving. These brownies are best enjoyed warm, with a glass of cold milk or a scoop of vanilla ice cream.

Tips and Variations

  • For an extra indulgent twist, drizzle the cooled brownies with melted chocolate or caramel sauce before serving.
  • If you don’t have mini marshmallows on hand, you can use regular-sized marshmallows, just be sure to cut them in half for even distribution.
  • Feel free to get creative with the toppings by adding chopped nuts, shredded coconut, or even a sprinkle of sea salt for a gourmet touch.

Conclusion

These Gooey and Delicious S’mores Brownies are a delightful marriage of two beloved treats – brownies and s’mores. The fudgy chocolate base, gooey marshmallows, and crunchy graham crackers come together to create a truly indulgent dessert that’s perfect for any occasion. Whether you’re a seasoned baker or just starting out, this recipe is a crowd-pleaser that will have everyone asking for seconds. So, go ahead, gather your ingredients, preheat your oven, and get ready to enjoy a slice of pure bliss with these S’mores Brownies.
