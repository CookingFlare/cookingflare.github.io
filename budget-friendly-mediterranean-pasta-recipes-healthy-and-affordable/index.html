Budget-Friendly Mediterranean Pasta Recipes: Healthy and Affordable


Are you looking for delicious and healthy pasta recipes that won’t break the bank? Look no further than these budget-friendly Mediterranean pasta recipes. Not only are they easy on the wallet, but they are also packed with the vibrant flavors and wholesome ingredients that the Mediterranean diet is known for. Let’s dive into these mouthwatering dishes that will satisfy your cravings without compromising your budget or your health.

Lemon Garlic Pasta with Spinach and Feta

Ingredients:

  • 8 oz. spaghetti
  • 3 tbsp olive oil
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • Zest of 1 lemon
  • Juice of 1 lemon
  • 4 cups fresh spinach
  • 1/2 cup crumbled feta cheese
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Cook the spaghetti according to the package instructions. Reserve 1/2 cup of pasta water before draining.
  2. In a large skillet, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the minced garlic and sauté until fragrant.
  3. Add the lemon zest and spinach to the skillet. Cook until the spinach wilts.
  4. Toss the cooked pasta in the skillet with the spinach and garlic. Add the lemon juice and some of the reserved pasta water to create a light sauce.
  5. Season with salt and pepper, then sprinkle the crumbled feta cheese on top before serving.

Mediterranean Veggie Pasta Salad

Ingredients:

  • 12 oz. rotini pasta
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1 cucumber, diced
  • 1/2 red onion, thinly sliced
  • 1/2 cup Kalamata olives, sliced
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley
  • 1/3 cup olive oil
  • 2 tbsp red wine vinegar
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Cook the rotini pasta according to the package instructions. Drain and let it cool.
  2. In a large bowl, combine the pasta, cherry tomatoes, cucumber, red onion, Kalamata olives, and parsley.
  3. In a small bowl, whisk together the olive oil, red wine vinegar, dried oregano, salt, and pepper to make the dressing.
  4. Pour the dressing over the pasta and vegetables, then toss to coat evenly.
  5. Refrigerate the pasta salad for at least 30 minutes before serving to allow the flavors to meld.

Greek-Style Stuffed Peppers with Orzo

Ingredients:

  • 4 bell peppers
  • 1 cup orzo pasta
  • 1/2 lb ground lamb or beef
  • 1/2 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • 1/2 cup crumbled feta cheese
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh mint
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F.
  2. Cut the tops off the bell peppers and remove the seeds and membranes.
  3. Cook the orzo according to the package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  4. In a skillet, brown the ground lamb or beef with the chopped onion and garlic. Season with dried oregano, salt, and pepper.
  5. Remove the skillet from the heat and stir in the cooked orzo, crumbled feta cheese, and chopped fresh mint.
  6. Spoon the orzo and meat mixture into the hollowed-out bell peppers.
  7. Place the stuffed peppers in a baking dish, cover with foil, and bake for 25-30 minutes, or until the peppers are tender.

Conclusion

These budget-friendly Mediterranean pasta recipes are not only easy on the wallet but also rich in flavor and nutrients. Whether you’re cooking for a quick weeknight dinner or preparing a meal for a gathering, these dishes are sure to impress without costing a fortune. Embrace the vibrant flavors of the Mediterranean while enjoying the affordability and health benefits of these delightful pasta recipes. Bon appétit!
