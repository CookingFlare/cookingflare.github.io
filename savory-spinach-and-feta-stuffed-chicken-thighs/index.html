Savory Spinach and Feta Stuffed Chicken Thighs


If you’re looking for a delicious and impressive dish to serve at your next dinner party, look no further than these savory spinach and feta stuffed chicken thighs. Tender and juicy chicken thighs are stuffed with a flavorful mixture of spinach, feta cheese, and aromatic herbs, creating a dish that is both visually appealing and bursting with flavor.

Ingredients

  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken thighs
  • 2 cups fresh spinach, chopped
  • 1/2 cup crumbled feta cheese
  • 1/4 cup finely chopped red onion
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon chopped fresh parsley
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).

  2. In a large mixing bowl, combine the chopped spinach, crumbled feta cheese, red onion, minced garlic, chopped fresh parsley, dried oregano, salt, and black pepper. Mix well until all the ingredients are evenly distributed.

  3. Carefully make a pocket in each chicken thigh by slicing along the side, being careful not to cut all the way through. Spoon the spinach and feta filling into each pocket, dividing it evenly among the chicken thighs.

  4. Heat the olive oil in a large oven-safe skillet over medium-high heat. Once the oil is hot, add the stuffed chicken thighs to the skillet, seam side down. Sear the chicken for 2-3 minutes on each side, until golden brown.

  5. Transfer the skillet to the preheated oven and bake for 20-25 minutes, or until the chicken reaches an internal temperature of 165°F (74°C) and is cooked through.

  6. Remove the skillet from the oven and let the chicken thighs rest for a few minutes before serving. This will allow the juices to redistribute and the flavors to meld together.

  7. Serve the savory spinach and feta stuffed chicken thighs hot, garnished with a sprinkle of fresh parsley. Pair it with a side of roasted vegetables or a crisp green salad for a complete and satisfying meal.

Tips and Variations

  • If you prefer a different type of cheese, feel free to substitute the feta with mozzarella, goat cheese, or even blue cheese for a bolder flavor.
  • Experiment with different herbs and spices to customize the flavor profile of the dish. Add a pinch of red pepper flakes for a spicy kick or sprinkle some lemon zest over the chicken for a refreshing citrus twist.
  • To make this recipe even healthier, you can use skinless chicken breasts instead of thighs. However, keep in mind that the cooking time may vary, so be sure to check the internal temperature to ensure the chicken is thoroughly cooked.
  • Leftovers can be stored in an airtight container in the refrigerator for up to three days. Enjoy them cold or reheat them in the oven for a few minutes until warmed through.

With its vibrant colors, rich flavors, and impressive presentation, this recipe is sure to become a favorite among your family and friends. The combination of tender chicken, savory spinach, and tangy feta cheese creates a harmonious blend of flavors that will tantalize your taste buds. So, why not give this delicious dish a try and elevate your next dinner party to a whole new level? Enjoy!
