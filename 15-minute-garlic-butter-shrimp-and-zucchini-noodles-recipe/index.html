15-Minute Garlic Butter Shrimp and Zucchini Noodles Recipe


Are you craving a delicious and healthy meal but don’t have much time to spare? Look no further! This 15-Minute Garlic Butter Shrimp and Zucchini Noodles recipe is not only quick and easy to make, but it’s also packed with flavor and nutrients. With just a few simple ingredients, you can whip up a satisfying dish that will leave you feeling satisfied and energized.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound of shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 3 medium-sized zucchini
  • 4 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons of butter
  • 1 tablespoon of olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon of lemon zest
  • 1 tablespoon of lemon juice
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley for garnish

Instructions

  1. Start by preparing the zucchini noodles. Using a spiralizer or a julienne peeler, create long, thin noodles from the zucchini. Set aside.

  2. In a large skillet, melt the butter and olive oil over medium heat. Add the minced garlic and sauté for about a minute until fragrant.

  3. Increase the heat to medium-high and add the shrimp to the skillet. Cook for about 2-3 minutes on each side until they turn pink and opaque. Be careful not to overcook the shrimp as they can become rubbery.

  4. Once the shrimp are cooked, remove them from the skillet and set aside. In the same skillet, add the zucchini noodles and sauté for about 2-3 minutes until they are tender but still crisp. Season with salt and pepper to taste.

  5. Return the shrimp to the skillet with the zucchini noodles. Add the lemon zest and lemon juice, and toss everything together until well combined.

  6. Cook for another minute to allow the flavors to blend together. Taste and adjust the seasoning if necessary.

  7. Remove from heat and garnish with fresh parsley.

Serving Suggestions

This 15-Minute Garlic Butter Shrimp and Zucchini Noodles recipe is a complete meal on its own, but you can also serve it with a side salad or some crusty bread for a heartier meal. It’s perfect for those following a low-carb or gluten-free diet.

Nutritional Benefits

Not only is this dish incredibly tasty, but it’s also packed with nutritional benefits. Shrimp is a great source of lean protein, while zucchini is low in calories and high in vitamins and minerals. By substituting traditional pasta with zucchini noodles, you’re reducing your carbohydrate intake while still enjoying a satisfying meal.

Final Thoughts

This 15-Minute Garlic Butter Shrimp and Zucchini Noodles recipe is a game-changer for busy individuals who want to eat healthily without sacrificing taste. With just a handful of ingredients and minimal cooking time, you can enjoy a delicious and nutritious meal that will leave you feeling satisfied and fueled for the rest of your day.

So why wait? Give this recipe a try and let your taste buds rejoice in the flavors of garlic, butter, and succulent shrimp. Your body will thank you for nourishing it with wholesome ingredients that are as good for your health as they are for your taste buds!
