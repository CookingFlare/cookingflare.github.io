Sous Vide Green Beans: Crispy and Vibrant


If you’re a fan of fresh, vibrant vegetables, then you’re in for a treat today. We’re diving into the world of sous vide cooking to create the most delicious, crispy, and vibrant green beans you’ve ever tasted. Sous vide cooking is a game-changer when it comes to preserving the natural flavors and textures of vegetables, and green beans are no exception. By cooking them at a precise temperature in a water bath, we can achieve that perfect balance of tenderness and crunch that makes green beans so irresistible.

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound fresh green beans, trimmed
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon lemon zest
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Directions:

  1. Prepare the Sous Vide Water Bath: Fill a large container or pot with water and attach your sous vide precision cooker. Preheat the water to 183°F (84°C).

  2. Season the Green Beans: In a bowl, toss the trimmed green beans with olive oil, minced garlic, lemon zest, salt, and pepper. Ensure the green beans are evenly coated with the seasonings.

  3. Vacuum Seal the Green Beans: Place the seasoned green beans in a single layer in a vacuum-sealable bag. Seal the bag, ensuring the green beans are in a flat, single layer to ensure even cooking.

  4. Cook the Green Beans Sous Vide: Once the water bath has reached the desired temperature, submerge the sealed bag of green beans in the water. Cook for 45 minutes to 1 hour, ensuring the green beans are fully tender but still vibrant and crisp.

  5. Finish in Ice Water Bath: After the green beans have finished cooking, remove them from the sous vide water bath and immediately transfer them to an ice water bath to halt the cooking process. This step will help the green beans retain their vibrant color and crisp texture.

  6. Serve and Enjoy: Once the green beans have cooled in the ice water bath, transfer them to a serving dish and drizzle with a little extra olive oil and a sprinkle of sea salt, if desired. These sous vide green beans are perfect as a side dish for any meal.

Why Sous Vide?

Sous vide cooking is a precise and reliable method for cooking vegetables. By cooking green beans at a lower temperature for a longer time, we can achieve a perfect texture without overcooking or losing their vibrant color. The gentle cooking process preserves the nutrients and flavors, resulting in green beans that are crispy, vibrant, and bursting with freshness.

Final Thoughts

Sous vide green beans are a delightful addition to any meal, bringing a burst of color and flavor to your plate. By harnessing the power of sous vide cooking, we can elevate this humble vegetable to new heights. Whether you’re a dedicated sous vide enthusiast or a newcomer to this cooking technique, these crispy and vibrant green beans are sure to impress. So, grab your sous vide precision cooker and a pound of fresh green beans, and get ready to experience green beans like never before. Cheers to vibrant, crispy, and utterly delicious green beans!
