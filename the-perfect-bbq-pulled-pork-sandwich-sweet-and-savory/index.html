The Perfect BBQ Pulled Pork Sandwich: Sweet and Savory


There’s something undeniably satisfying about sinking your teeth into a delicious BBQ pulled pork sandwich. The tender, juicy pork, smothered in a sweet and savory barbecue sauce, nestled between two soft buns – it’s a culinary experience that can transport you to a place of pure comfort and indulgence. In this recipe, we’ll explore how to create the perfect BBQ pulled pork sandwich that strikes the ideal balance between sweet and savory flavors, leaving your taste buds craving more.

Ingredients

For the Pulled Pork:

  • 4-5 pounds pork shoulder
  • 1 tablespoon brown sugar
  • 1 tablespoon paprika
  • 1 tablespoon garlic powder
  • 1 tablespoon onion powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste

For the BBQ Sauce:

  • 1 cup ketchup
  • 1/2 cup apple cider vinegar
  • 1/4 cup brown sugar
  • 2 tablespoons honey
  • 1 tablespoon Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 teaspoon smoked paprika
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste

For the Sandwich:

  • Soft hamburger buns
  • Coleslaw (optional, for topping)

Instructions

Step 1: Preparing the Pork

  1. In a small bowl, mix together the brown sugar, paprika, garlic powder, onion powder, salt, and pepper to create a dry rub.
  2. Rub the dry rub all over the pork shoulder, ensuring it’s evenly coated.
  3. Place the pork shoulder in a slow cooker and cook on low for 8-10 hours, or until the meat is tender and easily pulls apart with a fork.
  4. Once cooked, remove the pork from the slow cooker and transfer it to a cutting board. Use two forks to shred the pork into bite-sized pieces.

Step 2: Making the BBQ Sauce

  1. In a saucepan, combine the ketchup, apple cider vinegar, brown sugar, honey, Worcestershire sauce, smoked paprika, garlic powder, salt, and pepper.
  2. Bring the mixture to a simmer over medium heat, then reduce the heat and let it simmer for 15-20 minutes, stirring occasionally, until the sauce thickens slightly.
  3. Taste the sauce and adjust the seasoning to your preference.

Step 3: Assembling the Sandwich

  1. Place the shredded pork in a large bowl and pour the BBQ sauce over it. Toss the pork until it’s evenly coated with the sauce.
  2. Toast the hamburger buns lightly to add a bit of crispness.
  3. Spoon the saucy pulled pork onto the bottom half of each bun.
  4. Top the pork with a generous helping of coleslaw if desired, then place the top half of the bun on top.
  5. Serve the sandwiches with extra BBQ sauce on the side for dipping.

Tips and Serving Suggestions

  • For an extra layer of flavor, consider adding a slice of sharp cheddar cheese to the sandwich before topping it with coleslaw.
  • If you prefer a spicier kick, add a dash of hot sauce or a sprinkle of cayenne pepper to the BBQ sauce.
  • Serve the pulled pork sandwiches with a side of crispy sweet potato fries or a refreshing cucumber salad for a well-rounded meal.

This sweet and savory BBQ pulled pork sandwich recipe is a crowd-pleaser that’s perfect for summer cookouts, game day gatherings, or simply indulging in a comforting meal at home. The tender, flavorful pork combined with the rich, tangy BBQ sauce and the soft, pillowy buns create a symphony of taste and texture that will leave everyone craving seconds. So fire up the grill or dust off your slow cooker, and treat yourself to the ultimate BBQ experience with this mouthwatering recipe.
