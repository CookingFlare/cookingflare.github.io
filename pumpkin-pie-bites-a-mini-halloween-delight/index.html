Pumpkin Pie Bites: A Mini Halloween Delight


Who doesn’t love a bite-sized treat that perfectly captures the essence of fall? These pumpkin pie bites are a delightful twist on the classic pumpkin pie, and they’re perfect for Halloween gatherings, cozy autumn evenings, or anytime you’re craving a little taste of the season. With a buttery crust, creamy pumpkin filling, and a hint of warm spices, these mini pumpkin pie bites are sure to become a favorite in your recipe collection.

Ingredients

For the Crust:

  • 1 1/4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, chilled and cubed
  • 1/4 cup ice water

For the Filling:

  • 1 cup pumpkin puree
  • 1/2 cup brown sugar
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground ginger
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground nutmeg
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground cloves
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2/3 cup evaporated milk

For the Whipped Cream Topping:

  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 2 tablespoons powdered sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Crust:

    • In a large bowl, whisk together the flour and salt. Add the chilled, cubed butter and use a pastry cutter or your fingers to work the butter into the flour until it resembles coarse crumbs.
    • Drizzle the ice water over the mixture and stir until the dough begins to come together. Turn the dough out onto a clean surface and knead it gently until it forms a cohesive ball. Flatten the dough into a disc, wrap it in plastic wrap, and refrigerate for at least 30 minutes.

  2. Make the Filling:

    • In a medium bowl, whisk together the pumpkin puree, brown sugar, cinnamon, ginger, nutmeg, cloves, and salt. Add the eggs and whisk until smooth. Gradually stir in the evaporated milk until fully combined.

  3. Assemble the Pumpkin Pie Bites:

    • Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C). Grease a mini muffin tin.
    • Roll out the chilled dough on a lightly floured surface to about 1/8-inch thickness. Use a round cookie cutter or a small glass to cut out circles of dough. Press each circle into the prepared mini muffin tin, forming a small pie crust in each cup.
    • Spoon the pumpkin filling into each mini pie crust, filling just below the top.

  4. Bake and Serve:

    • Bake the pumpkin pie bites for 12-15 minutes, or until the crust is golden and the filling is set. Remove from the oven and let the bites cool in the pan for 10 minutes before carefully transferring them to a wire rack to cool completely.
    • In the meantime, make the whipped cream topping by whipping the heavy cream, powdered sugar, and vanilla extract together until soft peaks form.
    • Once the pumpkin pie bites are completely cooled, top each one with a dollop of whipped cream.

Tips and Tricks

  • For a festive touch, sprinkle a little extra ground cinnamon or pumpkin pie spice on top of the whipped cream.
  • These pumpkin pie bites can be made a day in advance and stored in the refrigerator. Just add the whipped cream topping right before serving.
  • If you don’t have a mini muffin tin, you can make these as regular-sized muffin tin pies. Adjust the baking time as needed.

There you have it – a delightful and delicious recipe for pumpkin pie bites that are perfect for Halloween or any autumn occasion. These mini treats are bursting with all the flavors of fall, and they’re sure to be a hit with anyone who tries them. So, roll up your sleeves, gather your ingredients, and get ready to enjoy the sweet taste of the season in every bite!
