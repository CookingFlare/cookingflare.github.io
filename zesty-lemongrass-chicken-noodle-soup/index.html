Zesty Lemongrass Chicken Noodle Soup


There’s something incredibly satisfying about a steaming bowl of flavorful soup, especially when it’s packed with aromatic spices and fresh ingredients. Today, we’re diving into the world of Asian-inspired cuisine with a tantalizing recipe for Zesty Lemongrass Chicken Noodle Soup. This dish strikes the perfect balance between tangy lemongrass, tender chicken, and slurp-worthy noodles. Let’s get cooking!

Ingredients:

  • 2 lemongrass stalks
  • 4 cloves of garlic
  • 1 onion
  • 1-inch piece of ginger
  • 1 red chili
  • 6 cups of chicken broth
  • 2 chicken breasts
  • 200g rice noodles
  • 1 carrot
  • 1 red bell pepper
  • 1 can of coconut milk
  • Soy sauce, fish sauce, and lime juice to taste
  • Fresh cilantro and sliced green onions for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the Ingredients: Start by finely chopping the lemongrass, garlic, onion, ginger, and red chili. Cut the chicken breasts into bite-sized pieces. Julienne the carrot and red bell pepper.

  2. Sauté Aromatics: In a large pot, heat some oil over medium heat. Sauté the lemongrass, garlic, onion, ginger, and red chili until fragrant.

  3. Add Chicken and Broth: Add the chicken pieces to the pot and cook until they start to brown. Pour in the chicken broth and bring the soup to a simmer.

  4. Infuse Flavors: Let the soup simmer for about 20 minutes to allow the flavors to meld together. This is where the magic happens!

  5. Cook Noodles: In a separate pot, cook the rice noodles according to the package instructions. Once cooked, drain and set aside.

  6. Incorporate Veggies and Coconut Milk: Add the julienned carrot and red bell pepper to the soup. Pour in the coconut milk for a creamy texture.

  7. Season to Perfection: Season the soup with soy sauce, fish sauce, and lime juice according to your taste preferences. These ingredients will elevate the flavors and add depth to the dish.

  8. Serve and Garnish: Divide the cooked noodles into serving bowls and ladle the fragrant soup over them. Garnish with fresh cilantro and sliced green onions for a burst of color and freshness.

Enjoy Your Zesty Lemongrass Chicken Noodle Soup!
