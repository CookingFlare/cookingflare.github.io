Slow Cooker Italian Sausage and Peppers: A Flavorful Combination


Welcome to our blog, where we bring you delicious and easy-to-make recipes. Today, we’re going to share a recipe that will transport you straight to the streets of Italy. Get ready to tantalize your taste buds with the incredible flavors of Slow Cooker Italian Sausage and Peppers.

Ingredients

To make this mouthwatering dish, you will need the following ingredients:

  • 1.5 pounds of Italian sausage links
  • 2 bell peppers (red, green, or a mix), sliced
  • 1 large onion, sliced
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 can (14 ounces) of diced tomatoes, undrained
  • 1 can (6 ounces) of tomato paste
  • 1 teaspoon of dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon of dried basil
  • 1/2 teaspoon of salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon of black pepper
  • Fresh basil, for garnish (optional)
Instructions

  1. Begin by browning the sausage links in a skillet over medium heat. This step will add depth and richness to the final dish. Once the sausages are browned, transfer them to your slow cooker.

  2. In the same skillet, sauté the sliced bell peppers, onion, and minced garlic until they become tender and fragrant. This will take about 5 minutes. Once done, transfer them to the slow cooker, on top of the sausage links.

  3. Now, it’s time to add the tomatoes, tomato paste, dried oregano, dried basil, salt, and black pepper to the slow cooker. Give everything a good stir, ensuring that the sausage and peppers are well-coated with the flavorful tomato sauce.

  4. Cover the slow cooker and set it to low heat. Let the ingredients simmer away for 6-8 hours, allowing all the flavors to meld together and create a delicious harmony. Alternatively, you can set it to high heat and reduce the cooking time to 3-4 hours.

  5. Once the cooking time is up, you can serve the Slow Cooker Italian Sausage and Peppers on a bed of fluffy rice, or with a side of crusty bread. The choice is yours! If desired, garnish with fresh basil for an extra burst of freshness.

Tips and Variations

  • For an extra kick, you can add some crushed red pepper flakes to the slow cooker along with the other ingredients. This will give the dish a spicy twist that will leave you wanting more.

  • If you prefer a thicker sauce, you can mix a tablespoon of cornstarch with a tablespoon of water and stir it into the slow cooker during the last 30 minutes of cooking.

  • This recipe is incredibly versatile. You can tweak it to suit your taste preferences by adding other vegetables like mushrooms, zucchini, or even some diced eggplant.

  • Leftovers can be stored in an airtight container in the refrigerator for up to 3 days. They make a delicious filling for sandwiches or can be reheated and enjoyed on their own.

Conclusion

There you have it, a recipe that brings the flavors of Italy right to your dinner table. With just a few simple ingredients and minimal effort, you can create a dish that will impress your family and friends. The Slow Cooker Italian Sausage and Peppers is a flavorful combination that is sure to become a favorite in your household. So, grab your slow cooker and get ready to indulge in a taste of Italy. Buon appetito!
