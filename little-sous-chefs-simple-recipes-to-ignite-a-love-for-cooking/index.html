Little Sous Chefs: Simple Recipes to Ignite a Love for Cooking


Are you looking for ways to nurture a love for cooking in your little ones? Want to inspire their creativity and give them the confidence to experiment in the kitchen? This collection of simple recipes is perfect for little sous chefs! These easy-to-follow recipes will engage the young cooks’ enthusiasm while delighting their taste buds. By empowering them to cook with you, they’ll discover the joy of bringing food to life while creating memories that will last a lifetime.

Whether they’re hunting for ingredients in the pantry, cracking eggs, or estimating measurements, cooking is an incredible way to teach life skills and foster independence. So, put on aprons and let’s take a culinary adventure together!

  1. Chocolate Chip Pancakes

Warm up on a chilly morning with these delicious pancakes! Your little sous chefs can easily help with this recipe:

Ingredients:

  • 1 and 3/4 cups all-purpose flour (200g)
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder (10g)
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda (3g)
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt (3g)
  • 1 tablespoon granulated sugar (15g)
  • 1 and 1/4 cups milk (150ml)
  • 1 large egg (50g)
  • 1/4 cup unsalted butter, melted (60ml)
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract (5ml)
  • 1/4 cup chocolate chips (50g)

Instructions:

  1. In a large mixing bowl, whisk together the flour, baking powder, baking soda, salt, and sugar.

  2. In another bowl, mix the milk, egg, melted butter, and vanilla extract. Gradually pour this wet mixture into the dry ingredients until fully incorporated but don’t overmix - a few small lumps are okay.

  3. Fold in the chocolate chips.

  4. Heat a non-stick pan or griddle over medium heat. If you’re using a regular pan, lightly grease it with a little butter or cooking spray.

  5. Use an ice cream scoop or a ladle to pour pancake batter onto the pan. Wait until bubbles appear on the surface before flipping.

  6. Cook for another 2-3 minutes until golden brown on the other side. Repeat with the remaining batter.

  7. Serve the pancakes with your favorite toppings: fresh fruit, maple syrup, whipped cream, or perhaps a dollop of yogurt.

  8. Mini Cheeseburgers

Who doesn’t love a good cheeseburger? This kid-friendly adaptation of a timeless classic is a sure-fire winner:

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound ground beef (227g)
  • 10 English muffins, split (180g)
  • 1 cup tomato sauce (245g)
  • 2 teaspoons Worcestershire sauce (10ml)
  • 1/2 cup shredded cheddar cheese (45g)
  • Lettuce, tomato slices, and condiments of your choice

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 375째F (190째C). Divide the ground beef into 10 equal-sized portions and shape them into patties.

  2. Place the patties on a lined baking sheet and bake for about 10 minutes.

  3. While the patties are cooking, mix the tomato sauce and Worcestershire sauce in a small bowl.

  4. Split and lightly toast the English Muffins.

  5. Once the patties are done, place a cheese slice on top of each patty and return them to the oven for an additional 2 minutes, or until the cheese has melted.

  6. Slather half of the muffin with the tomato sauce mixture (you can easier do this with a butter knife) and load it up with your lettuce, tomato slices, condiments, and the mini cheeseburger patty.

  7. The kids can assemble their own mini cheeseburgers by adding toppings as they please. There’s no wrong way to build a burger that little chefs can love!

  8. Homemade Pizza

Make pizza night even more exciting by letting the kids make their own pizzas! Don’t forget to let them pick their favorite toppings.

Ingredients:

  • 1 and 1/2 cups all-purpose flour (180g)
  • 1 and 1/4 teaspoons instant yeast (6g)
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt (3g)
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil (30ml)
  • 1 cup warm water (120ml)
  • Your choice of toppings: pizza sauce, mozzarella cheese, pepperoni, bell peppers, olives, mushrooms, onions, and more!

Instructions:

  1. Prepare a baking sheet by lining it with parchment paper.

  2. In a large mixing bowl, combine the flour, instant yeast, and salt. Stir to mix.

  3. Add the olive oil and warm water to the dry ingredients, stirring until a soft dough forms. Use your hands to bring the dough together; it should be slightly sticky.

  4. On a lightly floured surface, knead the dough for about 5 minutes until smooth and elastic. Place the dough in a lightly oiled bowl, cover it with a damp cloth, and let it rise for about 1 hour, or until doubled in size.

  5. Preheat your oven to 425째F (220째C).

  6. Once the dough has risen, divide it into eight smaller portions. Shape each portion into a thin circle, about 6 inches in diameter. Place the rounds on the baking sheet and let the kids take over to add their toppings.

  7. Bake the pizzas for 12-15 minutes, or until they’re golden and crisp. Slice them and enjoy!

  8. Rainbow Veggie Pasta

Encourage your kids to eat their colors with this incredibly fun and tasty rainbow veggie pasta!

Ingredients:

  • 8 ounces pasta (227g)
  • 1/2 cup cherry tomatoes, halved (75g)
  • 1/2 cup shredded carrot (50g)
  • 1/2 cup corn (85g)
  • 1/4 cup grated zucchini (45g)
  • 1/4 cup finely chopped bell peppers (30g)
  • 1/4 cup finely chopped cucumber (30g)
  • 1/4 cup peas (45g)
  • 1/4 cup chopped olives (45g)
  • Olive oil
  • Salt and pepper

Instructions:

  1. Cook the pasta according to the package instructions until al dente. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a large bowl, mix together all the veggies.
  3. Add the cooked pasta to the bowl and gently toss with olive oil, salt, and pepper. Encourage the kids to explore the different colors and textures of the rainbow veggies!
  4. Serve with a sprinkle of grated Parmesan or a dollop of ricotta cheese, if desired.

Conclusion

Cooking with your children helps them to develop their creativity, acquire valuable life skills, and experience the joy of nourishing themselves and their loved ones. These simple recipes will be the perfect stepping stones to ignite their love for cooking and explore the colorful world of food. Gather your little sous chefs in the kitchen, and let the culinary adventure begin!
