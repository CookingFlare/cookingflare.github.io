Satisfyingly Sweet Orange Cardamom Madeleines


If you’re looking for a delightful treat to accompany your afternoon tea or coffee, look no further than these Satisfyingly Sweet Orange Cardamom Madeleines. These delicate French sponge cakes are infused with the bright, citrusy flavor of orange and the warm, aromatic notes of cardamom, creating a truly indulgent experience for your taste buds.

Ingredients

  • 2/3 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1/3 cup granulated sugar
  • Zest of 1 orange
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cardamom
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, melted and cooled

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Madeleine Pans: Begin by preheating your oven to 375°F (190°C) and generously butter and flour your madeleine pans, tapping out any excess flour.

  2. Sift the Dry Ingredients: In a small bowl, sift together the all-purpose flour, baking powder, and salt. Set this aside for later use.

  3. Whisk the Eggs and Sugar: In a large mixing bowl, whisk together the eggs and granulated sugar until the mixture becomes pale and thick. This should take about 5 minutes.

  4. Add Flavorings: Once the egg and sugar mixture reaches the desired consistency, gently fold in the orange zest, ground cardamom, and vanilla extract.

  5. Incorporate the Dry Ingredients: Gradually add the sifted dry ingredients to the egg mixture, folding gently until just combined.

  6. Add the Melted Butter: Pour the cooled melted butter into the batter, folding gently until the butter is fully incorporated and the batter is smooth.

  7. Chill the Batter: Cover the bowl with plastic wrap and refrigerate the batter for at least 30 minutes. This chilling period allows the batter to rest and helps the madeleines achieve their classic humped shape when baked.

  8. Fill the Madeleine Pans: After the batter has chilled, spoon a small amount of batter into each madeleine mold, filling them about three-quarters full.

  9. Bake to Perfection: Place the filled madeleine pans in the preheated oven and bake for 10-12 minutes, or until the edges of the madeleines are golden brown and the centers spring back when lightly pressed.

  10. Cool and Enjoy: Once baked, remove the madeleines from the oven and allow them to cool in the pans for a few minutes before transferring them to a wire rack to cool completely.

Serving Suggestions

These delightful Orange Cardamom Madeleines are perfect for serving alongside a steaming cup of tea or coffee. Their delicate texture and aromatic flavor make them a sophisticated addition to any afternoon tea gathering or brunch spread. For an extra special touch, dust the madeleines with a light sprinkle of powdered sugar before serving.

Conclusion

With their elegant shell-like shape and captivating flavor, these Satisfyingly Sweet Orange Cardamom Madeleines are sure to become a favorite in your baking repertoire. Whether you’re an experienced baker or just beginning your culinary adventures, these madeleines are a delightful way to infuse a touch of French elegance into your home baking. So, gather your ingredients, preheat your oven, and embark on a culinary journey that will leave you and your loved ones craving more.
