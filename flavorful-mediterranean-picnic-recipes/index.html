Flavorful Mediterranean Picnic Recipes


Are you planning a picnic and looking to add a touch of Mediterranean flair to your menu? Look no further! In this blog post, we’ll explore some delicious and flavorful Mediterranean picnic recipes that are sure to impress your friends and family. From refreshing salads to savory wraps, these recipes will transport you straight to the sunny shores of the Mediterranean. So pack your picnic basket and let’s get cooking!

Mediterranean Pasta Salad

Ingredients:

  • 8 ounces of cooked pasta (penne or fusilli work well)
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1 cup cucumber, diced
  • 1/2 cup Kalamata olives, pitted and halved
  • 1/4 cup red onion, thinly sliced
  • 1/4 cup feta cheese, crumbled
  • 2 tablespoons fresh parsley, chopped

For the dressing:

  • 3 tablespoons extra-virgin olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons red wine vinegar
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a large bowl, combine the cooked pasta, cherry tomatoes, cucumber, Kalamata olives, red onion, feta cheese, and parsley.
  2. In a small bowl, whisk together the olive oil, red wine vinegar, minced garlic, dried oregano, salt, and pepper.
  3. Pour the dressing over the pasta salad and toss until everything is well coated.
  4. Refrigerate for at least 30 minutes before serving to allow the flavors to meld together. Enjoy!

Greek Chicken Skewers

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound boneless, skinless chicken breasts, cut into cubes
  • 1/4 cup extra-virgin olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons lemon juice
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried thyme
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, combine the olive oil, lemon juice, minced garlic, dried oregano, dried thyme, salt, and pepper.
  2. Add the chicken cubes to the marinade, making sure each piece is coated. Let it marinate in the refrigerator for at least 1 hour.
  3. Preheat the grill to medium-high heat.
  4. Thread the chicken cubes onto skewers and grill for about 8-10 minutes, turning occasionally, until cooked through and slightly charred.
  5. Serve the Greek chicken skewers with pita bread, tzatziki sauce, and a side of Greek salad. Enjoy the delicious flavors!

Mediterranean Hummus Wrap

Ingredients:

  • 4 large whole wheat or spinach tortillas
  • 1 cup hummus
  • 1 cup baby spinach leaves
  • 1/2 cup roasted red peppers, sliced
  • 1/2 cup cucumber, thinly sliced
  • 1/4 cup red onion, thinly sliced
  • 1/4 cup Kalamata olives, pitted and sliced
  • 1/4 cup crumbled feta cheese

Instructions:

  1. Lay out the tortillas and spread a generous amount of hummus on each one.
  2. Layer the baby spinach leaves, roasted red peppers, cucumber, red onion, Kalamata olives, and crumbled feta cheese on top of the hummus.
  3. Roll up the tortillas tightly, tucking in the sides as you go.
  4. Slice the wraps in half and secure with toothpicks if needed.
  5. Pack them in your picnic basket and enjoy the Mediterranean flavors on the go!

Watermelon Feta Salad

Ingredients:

  • 4 cups watermelon, cubed
  • 1 cup feta cheese, crumbled
  • 1/2 cup fresh mint leaves, chopped
  • 1/4 cup red onion, thinly sliced
  • 2 tablespoons extra-virgin olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon lime juice
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a large bowl, combine the watermelon cubes, crumbled feta cheese, chopped mint leaves, and thinly sliced red onion.
  2. In a small bowl, whisk together the olive oil, lime juice, salt, and pepper.
  3. Drizzle the dressing over the watermelon salad and gently toss to combine.
  4. Refrigerate for at least 15 minutes before serving to allow the flavors to meld together. Enjoy the refreshing and sweet-savory combination!

Conclusion

With these flavorful Mediterranean picnic recipes, your outdoor dining experience will be elevated to a whole new level. From the tangy Greek flavors in the chicken skewers to the refreshing watermelon feta salad, these dishes are sure to please your taste buds and impress your fellow picnic-goers. So go ahead, embrace the Mediterranean spirit, and enjoy a picnic filled with deliciousness!
