Spicy Chorizo and Red Pepper Pasta


If you love bold, flavorful dishes that combine rich spices with savory meats, look no further than this Spicy Chorizo and Red Pepper Pasta. This dish brings together the robust taste of chorizo, the sweetness of roasted red peppers, and a kick of heat, all mingled with the perfect amount of pasta to make a satisfying meal. Whether you’re cooking for a weeknight dinner or impressing guests at a dinner party, this recipe is guaranteed to be a hit.

In this post, we’ll walk you through every step of the recipe, including ingredient recommendations, cooking tips, and variations to suit different preferences. So, grab your apron and let’s dive into creating a delicious, spicy, and satisfying pasta dish!

Ingredients You’ll Need

Before you begin, let’s gather everything you’ll need to make this mouthwatering pasta dish. The ingredients are simple but full of flavor.

For the Pasta:

  • 400g (14 oz) spaghetti (or any pasta of your choice)
  • Salt (for boiling the pasta)

For the Sauce:

  • 250g (9 oz) chorizo sausage, casings removed and crumbled
  • 2 red bell peppers, sliced thinly
  • 1 medium onion, finely chopped
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil (for sautéing)
  • 1 teaspoon smoked paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon red chili flakes (adjust according to heat preference)
  • 1/2 cup chicken broth (or vegetable broth for a vegetarian version)
  • 1 can (14 oz) crushed tomatoes
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream (or a dairy-free substitute like coconut cream)
  • Salt and black pepper to taste

Garnish:

  • Fresh parsley, chopped
  • Freshly grated Parmesan (optional)

The Key to a Great Spicy Chorizo and Red Pepper Pasta

The combination of spicy chorizo and the sweetness of red peppers creates a unique depth of flavor in this dish. Here are some key tips to ensure you make the best version possible:

  • Choose the Right Chorizo: There are two types of chorizo – Spanish and Mexican. For this recipe, we recommend using Spanish chorizo, which is typically cured and crumbles well when cooked. However, if you prefer the smoky flavor of Mexican chorizo, that works too.

  • Roast the Red Peppers: Roasting red peppers brings out their natural sweetness and enhances their flavor. You can roast the peppers on a grill or in the oven. If you’re short on time, you can use jarred roasted peppers, but fresh roasted peppers add a depth of flavor you can’t replicate.

  • Adjust the Spice Level: The beauty of this dish is that you can adjust the level of heat to suit your taste. If you like things extra spicy, feel free to increase the amount of chili flakes, or even add a chopped jalapeño or two.

Now that you’ve got all your ingredients and tips in mind, let’s get started with the cooking process!

Step-by-Step Guide to Making Spicy Chorizo and Red Pepper Pasta

Step 1: Boil the Pasta

Start by bringing a large pot of salted water to a boil. Add your pasta and cook it according to the package directions, usually around 8-10 minutes, until it reaches al dente. Drain the pasta, reserving about a cup of the pasta water to help loosen the sauce later.

Step 2: Cook the Chorizo

While the pasta is cooking, heat 1 tablespoon of olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Once the oil is hot, add the crumbled chorizo. Cook for about 5-7 minutes, stirring occasionally, until the chorizo is nicely browned and cooked through. The chorizo will release its flavorful oils, which will form the base of your sauce.

Step 3: Sauté the Vegetables

Next, add the chopped onion and sliced red bell peppers to the skillet with the chorizo. Cook for 5-6 minutes until the vegetables begin to soften. Add the minced garlic and sauté for another 2 minutes until fragrant. The combination of garlic, onions, and peppers will fill your kitchen with an irresistible aroma!

Step 4: Spice It Up

Now, it’s time to add the spices! Sprinkle in the smoked paprika and red chili flakes. If you’re using a fresh chili, add it now as well. Stir everything together to ensure the spices coat the chorizo and vegetables evenly. This will infuse the entire dish with a warm, smoky heat.

Step 5: Add the Liquids

Pour in the chicken broth and let it simmer for 2-3 minutes, allowing the flavors to meld together. Next, add the crushed tomatoes and bring the sauce to a simmer. Allow it to cook for about 10 minutes, letting the sauce reduce slightly and thicken. Taste and season with salt and pepper as needed.

Step 6: Make the Sauce Creamy

After the sauce has reduced, add the heavy cream to give it a rich, silky texture. Stir to combine and let it simmer for another 3-4 minutes. If the sauce seems too thick, add a little bit of the reserved pasta water until it reaches your desired consistency.

Step 7: Combine Pasta and Sauce

Now, it’s time to bring everything together. Add the drained pasta directly into the skillet with the sauce. Toss the pasta in the sauce, making sure each strand is coated in the creamy, spicy sauce. If needed, add more pasta water to help the sauce coat the pasta better.

Step 8: Garnish and Serve

Once your pasta is perfectly coated in the sauce, it’s time to plate up! Garnish with freshly chopped parsley and a generous sprinkle of freshly grated Parmesan cheese (optional, but highly recommended for extra flavor). Serve immediately and enjoy!

Tips for Perfecting Your Spicy Chorizo and Red Pepper Pasta

1. Make It Vegetarian

To make this recipe vegetarian, simply replace the chorizo with plant-based chorizo or sautéed mushrooms. The smoky, savory flavors from the chorizo can easily be mimicked with a meaty mushroom substitute, and you’ll still get that hearty texture.

2. Add Some Veggies

Feel free to add more vegetables to your pasta. Zucchini, spinach, or cherry tomatoes would all complement the flavors of the chorizo and red peppers nicely. Just sauté them alongside the onions and peppers for a burst of color and nutrients.

3. Make It Extra Creamy

If you want a richer, creamier pasta, you can add more cream or even a dollop of mascarpone cheese at the end. This will give the sauce a velvety finish and a more indulgent feel.

4. Use a Different Type of Pasta

While spaghetti is the go-to choice for this dish, feel free to experiment with other types of pasta. Penne, rigatoni, or even farfalle (bow-tie pasta) all work beautifully. Choose a shape that will hold onto the sauce for maximum flavor with every bite.

5. Store Leftovers

If you have leftovers, store them in an airtight container in the fridge for up to 3 days. The pasta may absorb some of the sauce as it sits, so you might want to add a little splash of water or broth when reheating to bring the sauce back to life.

Why You’ll Love This Recipe

There’s so much to love about this Spicy Chorizo and Red Pepper Pasta. It’s the perfect balance of heat and sweetness, with the chorizo providing a rich, savory flavor and the red peppers adding a natural sweetness. The creamy sauce ties everything together beautifully, while the pasta offers a satisfying base for the bold flavors.

Not only is this dish bursting with flavor, but it’s also relatively easy to make. With just a few simple ingredients and a bit of cooking time, you can create a restaurant-worthy meal at home. Whether you’re feeding a family or enjoying a solo dinner, this pasta is sure to become a regular in your weeknight meal rotation.

Variations to Try

1. Add a Protein

Want to make your pasta even more filling? Add grilled chicken, shrimp, or even some crispy bacon to the dish. These additions will add an extra layer of texture and flavor to the meal.

2. Spicy Sausage

If you enjoy a bit of extra heat, opt for a spicy Italian sausage instead of the chorizo. The fennel and garlic flavors from the sausage will pair beautifully with the red peppers and create an equally satisfying dish.

3. Vegan Version

To make this dish fully vegan, use plant-based chorizo, and substitute the heavy cream with coconut milk or a plant-based cream alternative. Nutritional yeast can be used in place of Parmesan for a cheesy flavor without any dairy.

4. Roast the Red Peppers

If you prefer a more intense pepper flavor, consider roasting the red peppers before adding them to the dish. Roasting intensifies their natural sweetness and gives them a smoky depth, making them even more delicious when combined with the chorizo.

Final Thoughts

This Spicy Chorizo and Red Pepper Pasta is a flavor-packed meal that will satisfy your cravings for something hearty and delicious. With its smoky, spicy chorizo, creamy sauce, and sweet roasted peppers, it’s the perfect combination of savory and spicy. Plus, it’s versatile enough to accommodate dietary preferences, making it a great choice for a wide range of palates. Give it a try tonight, and prepare to be amazed by how well these ingredients come together to create a comforting, indulgent dish.

Bon appétit!
