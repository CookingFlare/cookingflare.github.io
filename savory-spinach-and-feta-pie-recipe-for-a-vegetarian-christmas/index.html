Savory Spinach and Feta Pie Recipe for a Vegetarian Christmas


Are you looking for a show-stopping dish to impress your vegetarian guests this Christmas? Look no further than this delectable Savory Spinach and Feta Pie. Bursting with flavors and textures, this dish is sure to be the star of your holiday feast. With a golden, flaky crust and a creamy, savory filling, it’s a perfect option for those who prefer a plant-based meal. Let’s dive into the recipe and discover how to create this mouthwatering masterpiece.

Ingredients

For the crust:

  • 2 ½ cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup unsalted butter, chilled and diced
  • ½ cup ice water

For the filling:

  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 large onion, diced
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 12 ounces fresh spinach, roughly chopped
  • 1 cup crumbled feta cheese
  • ½ cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • ½ cup chopped fresh dill
  • 4 large eggs, beaten
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).

  2. In a large mixing bowl, combine the flour and salt for the crust. Add the diced butter and use your fingertips to rub it into the flour until the mixture resembles coarse crumbs.

  3. Gradually add the ice water, a tablespoon at a time, mixing the dough with a fork until it starts to come together. Once the dough holds its shape when squeezed, transfer it onto a lightly floured surface.

  4. Roll the dough into a circle large enough to fit your pie dish. Carefully transfer the dough to the dish, pressing it gently into the bottom and sides. Trim any excess dough hanging over the edges.

  5. In a large skillet, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the diced onion and minced garlic, cooking until softened and fragrant, about 5 minutes.

  6. Add the chopped spinach to the skillet and cook until wilted, about 3-4 minutes. Remove from heat and let it cool slightly.

  7. In a separate bowl, combine the feta cheese, Parmesan cheese, chopped dill, beaten eggs, salt, and pepper. Mix well.

  8. Add the cooled spinach mixture to the bowl and stir until everything is evenly incorporated.

  9. Pour the filling into the prepared pie crust, spreading it evenly.

  10. Place the pie in the preheated oven and bake for 40-45 minutes, or until the crust is golden and the filling is set.

  11. Remove the pie from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes before serving. This will allow the filling to set and make it easier to slice.

  12. Serve the Savory Spinach and Feta Pie warm or at room temperature. It pairs perfectly with a fresh green salad or roasted vegetables for a complete vegetarian Christmas feast.

Conclusion

This Savory Spinach and Feta Pie is a delightful addition to any vegetarian Christmas spread. The combination of earthy spinach, tangy feta cheese, and aromatic dill creates a harmonious blend of flavors that will leave your taste buds dancing. With its golden crust and creamy filling, this pie is a true show-stopper that will impress even the most discerning palates. So, gather your ingredients, roll up your sleeves, and get ready to create a memorable vegetarian Christmas dish that will have everyone coming back for seconds. Happy cooking and happy holidays!
