Creamy One Pot Chicken Parmesan Pasta


If you’re looking for a delicious and comforting meal that’s easy to make and requires minimal cleanup, then you’re in for a treat! This creamy one pot chicken parmesan pasta is the perfect combination of tender chicken, al dente pasta, and a rich, creamy sauce that will have you coming back for seconds. Not to mention, it’s all made in just one pot, which means less time spent on dishes and more time enjoying your meal.

Ingredients:

  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon Italian seasoning
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 1 24-ounce jar of marinara sauce
  • 3 cups chicken broth
  • 8 ounces penne pasta
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated parmesan cheese
  • Fresh basil, for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Begin by seasoning the chicken breasts with Italian seasoning, salt, and pepper.
  2. In a large pot or deep skillet, heat the olive oil over medium-high heat. Add the seasoned chicken breasts and cook them for 6-7 minutes on each side, or until they are golden brown and fully cooked. Once cooked, remove the chicken from the pot and set it aside.
  3. In the same pot, add the minced garlic and chopped onion. Sauté them for 2-3 minutes, or until they are fragrant and the onion is translucent.
  4. Pour in the marinara sauce and chicken broth, and stir to combine. Bring the mixture to a simmer.
  5. Add the penne pasta to the pot and stir to ensure it’s fully submerged in the liquid. Cover the pot and allow the pasta to cook for 10-12 minutes, or until it’s al dente.
  6. While the pasta is cooking, slice the cooked chicken into bite-sized pieces.
  7. Once the pasta is cooked, reduce the heat to low and stir in the heavy cream, shredded mozzarella, and grated parmesan cheese. Continue stirring until the cheese is fully melted and the sauce is smooth and creamy.
  8. Add the sliced chicken back into the pot and stir to combine everything. Allow the chicken to heat through in the sauce.
  9. Finally, garnish the creamy one pot chicken parmesan pasta with fresh basil before serving.

Tips and Variations:

  • You can customize this recipe by adding your favorite vegetables, such as spinach, mushrooms, or bell peppers, during the cooking process.
  • For an extra kick of flavor, try adding a pinch of red pepper flakes to the sauce.
  • If you prefer a lighter version, you can use half-and-half instead of heavy cream for a slightly less indulgent sauce.

This creamy one pot chicken parmesan pasta is a crowd-pleaser and a perfect dish for busy weeknights or casual gatherings. The combination of tender chicken, perfectly cooked pasta, and a luscious, creamy sauce will have everyone asking for the recipe. Plus, with just one pot to clean, it’s a hassle-free meal that satisfies both your cravings and your desire for simplicity. So, roll up your sleeves, grab your ingredients, and get ready to create a meal that will become a new family favorite!
