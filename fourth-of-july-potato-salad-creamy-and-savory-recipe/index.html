Fourth of July Potato Salad: Creamy and Savory Recipe


Summer is in full swing, and that means it’s time to fire up the grill and gather with loved ones for some delicious food. And what better way to celebrate the Fourth of July than with a classic side dish that never fails to impress? Today, I’m sharing with you my recipe for a creamy and savory Fourth of July Potato Salad that will be the star of any backyard barbecue.

Ingredients

  • 2 pounds red potatoes
  • 1 cup mayonnaise
  • 2 tablespoons Dijon mustard
  • 1 tablespoon apple cider vinegar
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1/4 cup chopped red onion
  • 1/4 cup chopped celery
  • 2 tablespoons chopped fresh dill
  • 2 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley

Instructions

  1. Start by boiling the red potatoes. Place them in a large pot and cover with water. Bring to a boil over high heat, then reduce the heat to medium-low and simmer for about 15-20 minutes, or until the potatoes are fork-tender. Drain and let them cool slightly.

  2. While the potatoes are cooling, let’s prepare the dressing. In a small bowl, whisk together the mayonnaise, Dijon mustard, apple cider vinegar, sugar, salt, and black pepper until well combined. Set aside.

  3. Once the potatoes have cooled enough to handle, cut them into bite-sized pieces and transfer them to a large mixing bowl.

  4. Add the chopped red onion, celery, dill, and parsley to the bowl with the potatoes.

  5. Now, pour the prepared dressing over the potato mixture and gently toss until all the ingredients are well coated.

  6. Cover the bowl with plastic wrap and refrigerate for at least 2 hours to allow the flavors to meld together.

  7. When ready to serve, give the potato salad a quick stir and taste for seasoning. Adjust with salt and pepper if needed.

  8. Transfer the potato salad to a serving bowl and garnish with some additional fresh dill and parsley.

Serving Suggestions

This Fourth of July Potato Salad pairs perfectly with grilled meats like burgers, hot dogs, or even barbecued chicken. It also makes a great addition to a summer potluck or picnic spread. Serve it alongside other classic dishes like coleslaw, baked beans, and cornbread for a complete and satisfying meal.

Tips and Variations

  • For a tangier flavor, you can add a squeeze of fresh lemon juice to the dressing.
  • Feel free to customize the potato salad by adding your favorite ingredients like chopped pickles, hard-boiled eggs, or crispy bacon.
  • If you prefer a smoother texture, you can mash some of the potatoes with a fork before adding the dressing.

Final Thoughts

This creamy and savory Fourth of July Potato Salad is a crowd-pleaser that will have everyone coming back for seconds. The combination of tender red potatoes, crunchy vegetables, and a tangy dressing creates a flavor explosion that is sure to impress. So, fire up the grill, gather your friends and family, and celebrate Independence Day with this delicious and patriotic side dish. Enjoy!
