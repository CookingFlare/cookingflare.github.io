Mongolian Tofu Stir Fry: A Vegetarian Delight


If you’re a fan of Asian cuisine and looking for a delicious vegetarian meal, look no further than Mongolian Tofu Stir Fry. This dish combines the bold flavors of Mongolian cuisine with the meaty texture of tofu, creating a satisfying and flavorful meal that is sure to impress. In this recipe, we’ll show you how to make this vegetarian delight step by step, so let’s get started!

Ingredients

For the stir fry sauce, you’ll need:

  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons hoisin sauce
  • 2 tablespoons rice vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon brown sugar
  • 1 tablespoon sesame oil
  • 1 tablespoon cornstarch
  • 1/2 cup vegetable broth

For the stir fry, gather the following ingredients:

  • 1 block of firm tofu, drained and cubed
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1 onion, thinly sliced
  • 3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 red bell pepper, thinly sliced
  • 1 cup sliced mushrooms
  • 1 cup broccoli florets
  • 1/2 cup snap peas
  • 2 green onions, sliced
  • Sesame seeds for garnish (optional)

Instructions

  1. Start by preparing the stir fry sauce. In a small bowl, whisk together the soy sauce, hoisin sauce, rice vinegar, brown sugar, sesame oil, cornstarch, and vegetable broth. Set the sauce aside.

  2. Heat one tablespoon of vegetable oil in a large skillet or wok over medium-high heat. Add the tofu cubes and cook until golden brown on all sides, about 5-7 minutes. Remove the tofu from the skillet and set it aside.

  3. In the same skillet, add another tablespoon of vegetable oil and sauté the onion and garlic until fragrant, about 2 minutes. Add the red bell pepper, mushrooms, broccoli, and snap peas. Stir fry the vegetables for 5-7 minutes, or until they are tender-crisp.

  4. Return the tofu to the skillet and pour the stir fry sauce over the tofu and vegetables. Stir everything together to coat evenly. Cook for an additional 2-3 minutes, or until the sauce has thickened.

  5. Remove the skillet from the heat and sprinkle the sliced green onions and sesame seeds over the stir fry for added freshness and crunch. Serve the Mongolian Tofu Stir Fry hot over steamed rice or noodles.

Tips and Variations

  • For a spicier kick, add a teaspoon of sriracha or red pepper flakes to the stir fry sauce.
  • Feel free to customize the vegetables according to your preference. You can add carrots, baby corn, or bok choy for extra color and flavor.
  • If you prefer a gluten-free option, substitute tamari or coconut aminos for the soy sauce.

Conclusion

Mongolian Tofu Stir Fry is a delightful vegetarian dish that combines the richness of Mongolian flavors with the versatility of tofu. This recipe provides a balanced mix of protein, vegetables, and aromatic spices, making it a wholesome and satisfying meal for vegetarians and non-vegetarians alike. With its quick and easy preparation, you can enjoy this flavorful stir fry any day of the week. So gather your ingredients, fire up your skillet, and treat yourself to this vegetarian delight that will transport your taste buds to the heart of Mongolia. Enjoy!
