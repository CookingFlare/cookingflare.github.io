Creamy Chocolate Hazelnut Swirl Ice Cream: A Nutella Lover's Dream


If you’re a fan of Nutella, then get ready to take your love for this delicious hazelnut chocolate spread to the next level with this dreamy Creamy Chocolate Hazelnut Swirl Ice Cream recipe. This decadent frozen treat combines the rich and creamy goodness of ice cream with the irresistible flavor of Nutella, creating a dessert that will have you coming back for more. Whether you’re hosting a summer gathering, looking for a special treat for your family, or simply craving something indulgent, this recipe is sure to satisfy your sweet tooth.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups heavy cream
  • 1 cup whole milk
  • 3/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 4 large egg yolks
  • 1 teaspoon pure vanilla extract
  • 1 cup Nutella
  • 1/2 cup chopped hazelnuts

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Ice Cream Base: In a saucepan, combine the heavy cream, whole milk, and half of the sugar. Heat the mixture over medium heat, stirring occasionally, until it reaches a simmer. Remove the saucepan from the heat.

  2. Whisk the Egg Yolks: In a separate bowl, whisk the egg yolks with the remaining sugar until the mixture becomes pale and slightly thick. Slowly pour the warm cream mixture into the bowl with the egg yolks, whisking constantly to combine.

  3. Cook the Custard: Pour the combined mixture back into the saucepan and return it to the stovetop over low heat. Stir the custard constantly until it thickens enough to coat the back of a spoon. Be careful not to let it boil.

  4. Chill the Custard: Once the custard has thickened, remove it from the heat and stir in the vanilla extract. Pour the custard into a bowl and cover it with plastic wrap, ensuring that the wrap touches the surface of the custard to prevent a skin from forming. Chill the custard in the refrigerator for at least 4 hours or until completely cold.

  5. Churn the Ice Cream: Once the custard is thoroughly chilled, pour it into an ice cream maker and churn according to the manufacturer’s instructions until it reaches a soft-serve consistency.

  6. Layer with Nutella and Hazelnuts: In a separate microwave-safe bowl, warm the Nutella for a few seconds until it becomes slightly runny. Layer the churned ice cream into a freezer-safe container, drizzling the warmed Nutella and sprinkling chopped hazelnuts between each layer. Use a knife to gently swirl the Nutella into the ice cream, creating a marbled effect.

  7. Freeze the Ice Cream: Once the Nutella and hazelnuts are swirled into the ice cream, cover the container with a lid or additional plastic wrap and freeze the ice cream for at least 4 hours, or until it is firm.

  8. Serve and Enjoy: When you’re ready to indulge, scoop the Creamy Chocolate Hazelnut Swirl Ice Cream into bowls or cones, and savor every creamy, Nutella-infused bite.

Tips and Tricks

  • For an extra indulgent treat, serve the ice cream with a drizzle of warm Nutella and a sprinkle of chopped hazelnuts on top.
  • To enhance the hazelnut flavor, toast the chopped hazelnuts in a dry skillet over medium heat until they become fragrant before adding them to the ice cream layers.
  • Experiment with different swirl patterns to create a visually stunning presentation for your homemade ice cream.

Final Thoughts

Creamy Chocolate Hazelnut Swirl Ice Cream is a delightful fusion of creamy, luscious ice cream and the beloved flavor of Nutella. Whether you’re enjoying a scoop on a hot summer day or treating yourself to a special dessert, this recipe is a surefire way to satisfy your sweet cravings. With its velvety texture and irresistible swirls of Nutella, this ice cream is a Nutella lover’s dream come true. So, grab your ingredients, churn up a batch, and indulge in the magic of homemade Creamy Chocolate Hazelnut Swirl Ice Cream.
