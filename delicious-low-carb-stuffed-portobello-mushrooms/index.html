Delicious Low Carb Stuffed Portobello Mushrooms


If you’re looking for a delicious and low-carb meal that’s easy to make and full of flavor, then look no further than these mouthwatering stuffed portobello mushrooms. Packed with savory ingredients and bursting with umami, these stuffed mushrooms are a perfect choice for a satisfying and healthy meal. Whether you’re a vegetarian, following a low-carb diet, or simply looking for a new and exciting dish to try, these stuffed portobello mushrooms are sure to impress your taste buds.

Ingredients

  • 4 large portobello mushrooms
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup baby spinach, chopped
  • 1/2 cup sun-dried tomatoes, chopped
  • 1/2 cup feta cheese, crumbled
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/4 cup almond flour
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley for garnish

Instructions

  1. Preheat the Oven: Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C) and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.

  2. Prepare the Mushrooms: Gently clean the portobello mushrooms with a damp paper towel to remove any dirt. Carefully remove the stems and scoop out the gills using a spoon to create space for the stuffing. Place the mushrooms on the prepared baking sheet.

  3. Prepare the Filling: In a skillet over medium heat, add the olive oil and sauté the chopped onion and garlic until fragrant and translucent. Add the chopped baby spinach and sun-dried tomatoes, and cook for a few minutes until the spinach wilts. Remove the skillet from the heat and allow the mixture to cool slightly.

  4. Assemble the Stuffed Mushrooms: In a mixing bowl, combine the sautéed vegetable mixture with the crumbled feta cheese, grated Parmesan cheese, and almond flour. Season the filling with salt and pepper to taste. Mix well to ensure all the ingredients are evenly combined.

  5. Stuff the Mushrooms: Divide the filling equally among the prepared portobello mushrooms, pressing it gently to fill the cavities and create a mound on top of each mushroom.

  6. Bake to Perfection: Place the stuffed mushrooms in the preheated oven and bake for 20-25 minutes, or until the mushrooms are tender and the filling is golden brown and bubbly.

  7. Serve and Enjoy: Once cooked, remove the stuffed portobello mushrooms from the oven and allow them to cool for a few minutes. Garnish with fresh parsley and serve them warm as a delightful appetizer or a satisfying main dish.

Nutritional Information

  • Serving Size: 1 stuffed mushroom
  • Calories: 120
  • Total Fat: 8g
  • Saturated Fat: 3g
  • Cholesterol: 15mg
  • Sodium: 250mg
  • Total Carbohydrates: 8g
  • Dietary Fiber: 2g
  • Sugars: 4g
  • Protein: 6g

Why You’ll Love These Stuffed Portobello Mushrooms

These low-carb stuffed portobello mushrooms are a fantastic addition to your recipe collection for several reasons:

  • Flavorful and Satisfying: The combination of savory ingredients such as sun-dried tomatoes, feta cheese, and spinach creates a burst of flavors in every bite, making these stuffed mushrooms incredibly satisfying.

  • Low-Carb and Keto-Friendly: With only 8 grams of carbohydrates per serving, these stuffed portobello mushrooms are perfect for those following a low-carb or keto lifestyle.

  • Versatile and Customizable: You can easily customize the filling to suit your taste preferences. Add a variety of cheeses, herbs, or even cooked ground meat for a personalized twist.

  • Nutrient-Rich: Portobello mushrooms are an excellent source of fiber, vitamins, and minerals, making them a nutritious choice for a well-rounded meal.

  • Easy to Make: This recipe is straightforward and requires minimal preparation, making it an ideal option for busy weeknights or relaxed weekend cooking.

So, whether you’re hosting a dinner party, preparing a family meal, or simply treating yourself to a delicious and wholesome dish, these low-carb stuffed portobello mushrooms are a must-try. They are sure to become a favorite in your repertoire of go-to recipes for any occasion. Enjoy!
