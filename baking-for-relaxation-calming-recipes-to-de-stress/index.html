Baking for Relaxation: Calming Recipes to De-Stress


In today’s fast-paced world, finding moments of calm and relaxation is essential for our mental well-being. One way to unwind and de-stress is through the therapeutic art of baking. Baking not only allows you to create delicious treats but also provides a sense of accomplishment and mindfulness. In this blog post, we will explore some soothing baking recipes that can help you relax and rejuvenate.

1. Lavender Earl Grey Tea Cookies

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1/2 cup powdered sugar
  • 2 tablespoons dried lavender flowers
  • 1 tablespoon Earl Grey tea leaves
  • 2 cups all-purpose flour

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, cream together butter and powdered sugar until light and fluffy.
  2. Grind the lavender flowers and Earl Grey tea leaves into a fine powder.
  3. Add the lavender tea mixture to the butter mixture and mix well.
  4. Gradually add flour and mix until the dough comes together.
  5. Roll the dough into a log, wrap in plastic wrap, and chill for 30 minutes.
  6. Preheat the oven to 350°F (180°C).
  7. Slice the dough into rounds and place on a baking sheet.
  8. Bake for 12-15 minutes until the edges are golden brown.
  9. Let the cookies cool before serving.

These delicate cookies infused with the floral notes of lavender and the citrusy aroma of Earl Grey tea are perfect for enjoying with a cup of tea as you unwind.

2. Cinnamon Swirl Bread

Ingredients:

  • 3 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 package active dry yeast
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1/4 cup unsalted butter
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 tablespoons ground cinnamon
  • 1/2 cup brown sugar

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, combine 2 cups flour, sugar, salt, and yeast.
  2. Heat milk and butter until warm (120°F-130°F).
  3. Add the milk mixture and eggs to the flour mixture and beat until smooth.
  4. Stir in the remaining flour to form a soft dough.
  5. Knead the dough on a floured surface until smooth and elastic.
  6. Let the dough rise in a greased bowl until doubled in size.
  7. Punch down the dough and roll it out into a rectangle.
  8. Mix cinnamon and brown sugar, then sprinkle over the dough.
  9. Roll up the dough and place in a greased loaf pan.
  10. Let it rise again until doubled.
  11. Bake at 375°F (190°C) for 30-35 minutes until golden brown.

The aroma of freshly baked cinnamon swirl bread filling your kitchen is a sure way to ease stress and indulge in simple pleasures.

3. Lemon Ricotta Cake

Ingredients:

  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 3/4 cup ricotta cheese
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, melted
  • 3 eggs
  • Zest of 1 lemon
  • Juice of 1 lemon
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (180°C) and grease a cake pan.
  2. In a bowl, combine flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt.
  3. In another bowl, whisk together ricotta, melted butter, eggs, lemon zest, lemon juice, and vanilla.
  4. Fold the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients until just combined.
  5. Pour the batter into the cake pan and smooth the top.
  6. Bake for 30-35 minutes until a toothpick inserted comes out clean.
  7. Let the cake cool before slicing and serving.

This moist and zesty lemon ricotta cake is a delightful treat that brings a burst of sunshine to your day, making it perfect for unwinding after a long day.

Baking can be a therapeutic and rewarding activity that allows you to slow down, focus on the present moment, and savor the joys of creating something delicious. Whether you’re a seasoned baker or a novice in the kitchen, these calming recipes are sure to help you de-stress and find tranquility in the simple pleasure of baking. Take some time for yourself, put on some soothing music, and let the creativity flow as you bake your way to relaxation. Happy baking!
