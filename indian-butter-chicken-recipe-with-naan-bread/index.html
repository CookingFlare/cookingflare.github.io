Indian Butter Chicken Recipe with Naan Bread


If you’re a fan of Indian cuisine, then you’ve probably heard of the mouthwatering dish called Butter Chicken. This rich and flavorful curry is a staple in Indian restaurants around the world, and for good reason. The creamy tomato-based sauce, tender chicken, and aromatic spices make it a truly delectable experience. And what better way to enjoy it than with some freshly baked naan bread?

Ingredients

For the Butter Chicken:

  • 500 grams boneless chicken, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon ginger, grated
  • 1 teaspoon cumin powder
  • 1 teaspoon coriander powder
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1 cup tomato puree
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • Salt, to taste
  • Fresh cilantro leaves, for garnish

For the Naan Bread:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup plain yogurt
  • 1/4 cup milk
  • 2 tablespoons melted butter

Instructions

Butter Chicken:

  1. Heat the butter in a large pan over medium heat. Add the chopped onions and cook until they turn golden brown.
  2. Add the minced garlic and grated ginger to the pan. Cook for another minute until fragrant.
  3. In a small bowl, mix together the cumin powder, coriander powder, turmeric powder, and paprika. Add this spice mixture to the pan and cook for 1-2 minutes, stirring continuously.
  4. Add the chicken pieces to the pan and cook until they are browned on all sides.
  5. Pour in the tomato puree and stir well to combine. Reduce the heat to low and let the curry simmer for about 15-20 minutes, or until the chicken is cooked through and tender.
  6. Stir in the heavy cream and season with salt to taste. Cook for an additional 5 minutes to allow the flavors to meld together.
  7. Garnish with fresh cilantro leaves before serving.

Naan Bread:

  1. In a large mixing bowl, combine the all-purpose flour, baking powder, baking soda, and salt.
  2. In a separate bowl, whisk together the plain yogurt, milk, and melted butter.
  3. Pour the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients and mix until a soft dough forms. Knead the dough for a few minutes until it becomes smooth and elastic.
  4. Cover the dough with a damp cloth and let it rest for at least 30 minutes.
  5. Preheat a non-stick skillet or griddle over medium-high heat.
  6. Divide the dough into small portions and roll each portion into a tear-shaped naan bread.
  7. Place the naan bread on the hot skillet and cook for about 1-2 minutes on each side, or until it puffs up and develops golden brown spots.
  8. Brush the cooked naan bread with melted butter before serving.

Enjoy the Delicious Combo!

Now that you have mastered the art of making Indian Butter Chicken and Naan Bread, it’s time to enjoy this delightful combo. The creamy and aromatic Butter Chicken paired with the soft and fluffy Naan Bread is a match made in culinary heaven. Serve it with some basmati rice and a side of cucumber raita for a complete Indian feast.

Pro tip: To make the Butter Chicken even more indulgent, add a dollop of butter on top just before serving. Trust me, it’s worth the extra calories!

So, gather your ingredients, put on your apron, and get ready to embark on a flavor-packed journey through the vibrant world of Indian cuisine. Your taste buds will thank you!
