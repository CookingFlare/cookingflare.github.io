Wholesome Instant Pot Lentil and Vegetable Curry Recipes


If you’re looking for a hearty and nutritious meal that’s bursting with flavors, look no further than these wholesome Instant Pot lentil and vegetable curry recipes. Whether you’re a seasoned chef or a beginner in the kitchen, these recipes are easy to follow and guaranteed to impress your taste buds.

Instant Pot Lentil Curry

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup dried lentils, rinsed
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1-inch piece of ginger, grated
  • 2 carrots, diced
  • 2 potatoes, cubed
  • 1 can (14 oz) diced tomatoes
  • 1 can (14 oz) coconut milk
  • 2 cups vegetable broth
  • 2 tablespoons curry powder
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon ground turmeric
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh cilantro for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Press the “Sauté” function on your Instant Pot and heat some oil. Add the onions, garlic, and ginger, and sauté until fragrant.
  2. Stir in the curry powder, cumin, turmeric, and paprika, and cook for another minute.
  3. Add the lentils, carrots, potatoes, diced tomatoes, coconut milk, and vegetable broth. Stir well to combine.
  4. Close the lid and set the Instant Pot to high pressure for 15 minutes.
  5. Once the cooking time is up, allow the pressure to release naturally for 10 minutes, then carefully do a quick pressure release.
  6. Season with salt and pepper, and garnish with fresh cilantro before serving.

This flavorful lentil curry is perfect for a cozy night in or for entertaining guests. The combination of tender lentils, hearty vegetables, and aromatic spices will leave you craving for more.

Instant Pot Vegetable Curry

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup cauliflower florets
  • 1 cup broccoli florets
  • 1 cup green beans, trimmed
  • 1 red bell pepper, diced
  • 1 yellow bell pepper, diced
  • 1 can (14 oz) chickpeas, drained and rinsed
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1-inch piece of ginger, grated
  • 1 can (14 oz) coconut milk
  • 2 tablespoons curry powder
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon ground turmeric
  • 1 teaspoon garam masala
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh cilantro for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Press the “Sauté” function on your Instant Pot and heat some oil. Add the onions, garlic, and ginger, and sauté until fragrant.
  2. Stir in the curry powder, cumin, turmeric, and garam masala, and cook for another minute.
  3. Add the cauliflower, broccoli, green beans, bell peppers, chickpeas, and coconut milk. Stir well to combine.
  4. Close the lid and set the Instant Pot to high pressure for 5 minutes.
  5. Once the cooking time is up, do a quick pressure release.
  6. Season with salt and pepper, and garnish with fresh cilantro before serving.

This vibrant and aromatic vegetable curry is a celebration of colorful and nutritious ingredients. The medley of vegetables, chickpeas, and fragrant spices makes this dish a delightful addition to any meal.

Conclusion

These Instant Pot lentil and vegetable curry recipes are not only delicious but also packed with wholesome ingredients that will nourish your body and soul. Embrace the convenience of the Instant Pot and let the flavors of these curries transport you to a culinary journey like no other. Whether you’re a vegetarian, vegan, or simply looking to incorporate more plant-based meals into your diet, these recipes are versatile and sure to become a staple in your kitchen. Enjoy the warmth and comfort of these wholesome curries, and savor the joy of creating unforgettable meals for yourself and your loved ones.
