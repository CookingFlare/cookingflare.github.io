Cheese Lover's Dream: 4 Marinades for Grilled Halloumi


If you’re a cheese lover like me, then you’re in for a treat! Halloumi, a firm and salty cheese from Cyprus, is the star of today’s recipe. Grilled halloumi is a delicious and versatile dish that can be enjoyed as an appetizer, a main course, or even as a topping for salads and sandwiches. In this blog post, I’m going to share with you four mouthwatering marinades that will take your grilled halloumi to the next level. So grab your apron and let’s get cooking!

1. Lemon Herb Marinade

Ingredients:

  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 1 teaspoon lemon zest
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon fresh parsley, chopped
  • 1 tablespoon fresh basil, chopped
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a small bowl, whisk together the olive oil, lemon juice, lemon zest, minced garlic, chopped parsley, and basil.
  2. Season the marinade with salt and pepper to taste.
  3. Place the halloumi slices in a shallow dish and pour the marinade over them.
  4. Gently toss the halloumi slices to ensure they are evenly coated with the marinade.
  5. Cover the dish and let the halloumi marinate in the refrigerator for at least 30 minutes, or overnight for maximum flavor.
  6. Preheat your grill to medium-high heat.
  7. Remove the halloumi slices from the marinade and grill them for 2-3 minutes on each side, or until they develop grill marks and become slightly golden brown.
  8. Serve the grilled halloumi with a squeeze of fresh lemon juice and enjoy!

2. Spicy BBQ Marinade

Ingredients:

  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons BBQ sauce
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon honey
  • 1 teaspoon smoked paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon cayenne pepper (adjust to your desired level of spiciness)
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a small bowl, whisk together the olive oil, BBQ sauce, soy sauce, honey, smoked paprika, cayenne pepper, salt, and pepper.
  2. Place the halloumi slices in a shallow dish and pour the marinade over them.
  3. Gently toss the halloumi slices to ensure they are evenly coated with the marinade.
  4. Cover the dish and let the halloumi marinate in the refrigerator for at least 30 minutes.
  5. Preheat your grill to medium-high heat.
  6. Remove the halloumi slices from the marinade and grill them for 2-3 minutes on each side, or until they develop grill marks and become slightly caramelized.
  7. Serve the grilled halloumi with extra BBQ sauce for dipping and enjoy the smoky and spicy flavors!

3. Mediterranean Marinade

Ingredients:

  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon balsamic vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon fresh lemon juice
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a small bowl, whisk together the olive oil, balsamic vinegar, lemon juice, minced garlic, dried oregano, dried thyme, salt, and pepper.
  2. Place the halloumi slices in a shallow dish and pour the marinade over them.
  3. Gently toss the halloumi slices to ensure they are evenly coated with the marinade.
  4. Cover the dish and let the halloumi marinate in the refrigerator for at least 30 minutes.
  5. Preheat your grill to medium-high heat.
  6. Remove the halloumi slices from the marinade and grill them for 2-3 minutes on each side, or until they develop grill marks and become slightly crispy.
  7. Serve the grilled halloumi with a drizzle of extra virgin olive oil and a sprinkle of fresh herbs for a taste of the Mediterranean!

4. Sweet and Savory Maple Glaze

Ingredients:

  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons maple syrup
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon Dijon mustard
  • 1 teaspoon smoked paprika
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a small bowl, whisk together the olive oil, maple syrup, soy sauce, Dijon mustard, smoked paprika, salt, and pepper.
  2. Place the halloumi slices in a shallow dish and pour the marinade over them.
  3. Gently toss the halloumi slices to ensure they are evenly coated with the marinade.
  4. Cover the dish and let the halloumi marinate in the refrigerator for at least 30 minutes.
  5. Preheat your grill to medium-high heat.
  6. Remove the halloumi slices from the marinade and grill them for 2-3 minutes on each side, or until they develop grill marks and the maple glaze caramelizes.
  7. Serve the grilled halloumi with a drizzle of extra maple syrup for a perfect balance of sweet and savory flavors.

There you have it, four tantalizing marinades that will transform your grilled halloumi into a cheese lover’s dream! Each marinade offers a unique combination of flavors that will take your taste buds on a journey. Whether you prefer tangy and herby, spicy and smoky, Mediterranean-inspired, or sweet and savory, there’s a marinade for every palate. So fire up the grill and indulge in this cheesy delight. Happy grilling!
