Irresistible Cinnamon Rolls: A Comforting Sweet Treat


Welcome to our kitchen, where we’ll be sharing the recipe for the most irresistible cinnamon rolls you’ve ever tasted. These soft, fluffy, and sweet treats are perfect for breakfast or as an indulgent dessert. So, roll up your sleeves and let’s get baking!

Ingredients

For the dough:

  • 2 ¾ cups all-purpose flour
  • ¼ cup granulated sugar
  • 2 ¼ teaspoons instant yeast
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • ½ cup milk, warmed
  • ¼ cup unsalted butter, melted
  • 2 large eggs

For the filling:

  • ½ cup unsalted butter, softened
  • ¾ cup brown sugar
  • 2 tablespoons ground cinnamon

For the glaze:

  • 1 cup powdered sugar
  • 2 tablespoons milk
  • ½ teaspoon vanilla extract

Instructions

  1. In a large mixing bowl, combine the flour, sugar, yeast, and salt. Mix well.

  2. In a separate bowl, whisk together the warmed milk, melted butter, and eggs. Pour the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients and stir until a soft dough forms.

  3. Knead the dough on a lightly floured surface for about 5 minutes, or until it becomes smooth and elastic. Place the dough in a greased bowl, cover with a clean kitchen towel, and let it rise in a warm place for about 1 hour, or until it doubles in size.

  4. While the dough is rising, prepare the filling by combining the softened butter, brown sugar, and ground cinnamon in a small bowl. Mix until well combined.

  5. Once the dough has doubled in size, punch it down to release any air pockets. Roll the dough out into a 16x12-inch rectangle on a lightly floured surface.

  6. Spread the filling evenly over the dough, leaving a small border around the edges. Starting from the long side, tightly roll the dough into a log.

  7. Cut the log into 12 equal slices and place them in a greased baking dish, leaving a little space between each roll. Cover the dish with a clean kitchen towel and let the rolls rise for another 30 minutes.

  8. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C). Bake the cinnamon rolls for 25-30 minutes, or until they turn golden brown.

  9. While the rolls are baking, prepare the glaze by whisking together the powdered sugar, milk, and vanilla extract until smooth.

  10. Once the rolls are done baking, remove them from the oven and let them cool slightly. Drizzle the glaze over the warm rolls and serve immediately.

Tips and Variations

  • For an extra flavor boost, try adding chopped nuts or raisins to the filling before rolling the dough.
  • If you prefer a more gooey texture, pour a small amount of caramel sauce into the bottom of the baking dish before arranging the cinnamon roll slices.
  • These cinnamon rolls are best enjoyed fresh out of the oven, but you can store any leftovers in an airtight container for up to 2 days. Just reheat them in the microwave for a few seconds before enjoying.

There you have it, the recipe for irresistible cinnamon rolls that will fill your home with a warm and comforting aroma. Whether you’re making them for a special occasion or simply to treat yourself, these sweet delights are sure to bring joy to your taste buds. So, gather your ingredients, follow the steps, and indulge in the magic of homemade cinnamon rolls. Happy baking!
