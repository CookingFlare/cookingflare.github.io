Creamy Coconut Key Lime Pie: A Tropical Twist on a Classic


If you’re in the mood for a taste of the tropics, look no further than this delightful recipe for Creamy Coconut Key Lime Pie. This dessert is a refreshing and creamy twist on the classic key lime pie, infusing the tangy flavor of key limes with the luscious creaminess of coconut. It’s the perfect treat for warm summer days or any time you want to transport your taste buds to a sunny beach.

Ingredients

For the Crust:

  • 1 1/2 cups graham cracker crumbs
  • 1/3 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, melted

For the Filling:

  • 1 can (14 ounces) sweetened condensed milk
  • 4 large egg yolks
  • 1/2 cup key lime juice
  • 1 tablespoon key lime zest
  • 1 cup coconut milk
  • 1/2 cup shredded coconut (optional, for garnish)

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Crust:

    • Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C).
    • In a medium bowl, combine the graham cracker crumbs, sugar, and melted butter. Mix until the crumbs are evenly moistened.
    • Press the mixture into the bottom and up the sides of a 9-inch pie dish. Use the back of a spoon or a flat-bottomed glass to press the crumbs firmly into place.
    • Bake the crust for 10 minutes, then remove from the oven and let it cool while you prepare the filling.

  2. Make the Filling:

    • In a large bowl, whisk together the sweetened condensed milk, egg yolks, key lime juice, and key lime zest until smooth and well combined.
    • Gradually add the coconut milk to the mixture, whisking continuously until fully incorporated.
    • Pour the filling into the prepared crust and spread it evenly.

  3. Bake and Chill:

    • Place the pie in the preheated oven and bake for 15 minutes, or until the edges are set but the center still jiggles slightly.
    • Remove the pie from the oven and let it cool to room temperature, then refrigerate for at least 4 hours, or until the filling is fully set.

  4. Garnish and Serve:

    • If desired, sprinkle the top of the pie with shredded coconut before serving.
    • Slice the pie into wedges and serve chilled, garnished with a dollop of whipped cream or a slice of key lime for an extra tropical touch.

The Tropical Indulgence

This Creamy Coconut Key Lime Pie is a tropical indulgence that combines the zesty brightness of key lime with the rich, velvety texture of coconut. The buttery graham cracker crust provides the perfect base for the creamy filling, adding a hint of sweetness to complement the tangy lime and coconut flavors. Whether you’re hosting a summer gathering or simply craving a taste of the tropics, this pie is sure to impress and delight.

Conclusion

In conclusion, this Creamy Coconut Key Lime Pie offers a delightful departure from the traditional key lime pie, infusing it with the exotic allure of coconut. The combination of tangy key lime and creamy coconut creates a harmonious blend of flavors that is both refreshing and indulgent. Whether you’re a fan of tropical desserts or simply looking to add a touch of sunshine to your table, this recipe is a must-try. So, grab your apron and get ready to whisk your taste buds away to a sun-soaked paradise with this luscious and tropical treat!
