Pumpkin Spice Energy Balls: A Healthy Fall Snack Recipe


Fall is here, and that means it’s time to indulge in all things pumpkin spice! But who says pumpkin spice has to be limited to lattes and baked goods? These Pumpkin Spice Energy Balls are the perfect healthy snack to satisfy your autumn cravings. Packed with nutritious ingredients, these energy balls are not only delicious but also provide a boost of energy to keep you going throughout the day.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup rolled oats
  • 1/2 cup pumpkin puree
  • 1/4 cup almond butter
  • 1/4 cup honey or maple syrup
  • 1/4 cup chopped pecans
  • 1/4 cup dried cranberries
  • 2 tablespoons ground flaxseed
  • 1 teaspoon pumpkin spice
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • Pinch of salt

Instructions

  1. In a large mixing bowl, combine the rolled oats, pumpkin puree, almond butter, honey or maple syrup, chopped pecans, dried cranberries, ground flaxseed, pumpkin spice, vanilla extract, and a pinch of salt. Mix well until all the ingredients are evenly combined.

  2. Once the mixture is well mixed, cover the bowl and place it in the refrigerator for about 30 minutes. Chilling the mixture will make it easier to shape into balls.

  3. After the mixture has chilled, remove it from the refrigerator. Using your hands, roll the mixture into bite-sized balls, about 1-inch in diameter. Place the energy balls on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper.

  4. Once you have rolled all the mixture into balls, return them to the refrigerator for another 30 minutes to firm up.

  5. After the energy balls have firmed up, transfer them to an airtight container and store them in the refrigerator for up to a week. These energy balls also freeze well, so you can make a big batch and enjoy them throughout the season.

Health Benefits

These Pumpkin Spice Energy Balls are not only incredibly tasty, but they are also packed with nutrients. Let’s take a look at some of the health benefits of the key ingredients:

  • Pumpkin Puree: Pumpkin is rich in beta-carotene, which is converted into vitamin A in the body. Vitamin A is essential for healthy eyesight and a strong immune system.

  • Almond Butter: Almonds are a great source of healthy fats, protein, and fiber. They also contain vitamin E, which is important for skin health.

  • Honey or Maple Syrup: These natural sweeteners add a touch of sweetness to the energy balls. While they do contain calories, they also provide some essential nutrients and antioxidants.

  • Pecans: Pecans are a good source of monounsaturated fats, which are heart-healthy fats. They also contain antioxidants and fiber.

  • Dried Cranberries: Cranberries are known for their high antioxidant content and potential health benefits, including supporting urinary tract health.

  • Ground Flaxseed: Flaxseed is rich in omega-3 fatty acids, fiber, and lignans, which have been shown to have potential cancer-fighting properties.

Enjoy Your Pumpkin Spice Energy Balls!

These Pumpkin Spice Energy Balls are not only a delicious fall treat but also a nutritious snack option. Whether you enjoy them as a pre-workout boost, an afternoon pick-me-up, or a sweet treat after dinner, these energy balls are sure to satisfy your pumpkin spice cravings. So go ahead, whip up a batch, and enjoy the flavors of fall in a healthy and convenient way!

Remember, you can always customize this recipe to suit your preferences. Feel free to add extras like chocolate chips, shredded coconut, or even a sprinkle of cinnamon. Get creative and make it your own! Happy snacking!
