Roasted Delicata Squash with Rosemary: A Quick and Easy Fall Side


Are you looking for a delightful and easy fall side dish? Look no further than this delicious Roasted Delicata Squash with Rosemary recipe! Delicata squash, also known as sweet potato squash, is a versatile and flavorful vegetable that is perfect for roasting. Paired with the earthy aroma of fresh rosemary, this dish is sure to become a staple in your fall cooking repertoire.

Ingredients

  • 2 medium delicata squash
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon fresh rosemary, chopped
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C) and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.

  2. Wash the delicata squash and pat them dry. Cut off the ends, then slice them in half lengthwise. Use a spoon to scoop out the seeds and discard them.

  3. Cut the squash into half-inch thick slices and place them in a large mixing bowl.

  4. Drizzle the olive oil over the squash slices, then add the chopped rosemary, salt, and pepper. Toss the squash gently to coat them evenly with the oil and seasonings.

  5. Arrange the seasoned squash slices in a single layer on the prepared baking sheet.

  6. Roast the squash in the preheated oven for 25-30 minutes, or until they are tender and golden brown, flipping them halfway through the cooking time for even browning.

  7. Once the squash is roasted to perfection, remove them from the oven and let them cool for a few minutes.

  8. Transfer the roasted delicata squash to a serving dish and garnish with a sprig of fresh rosemary for an extra touch of elegance.

Serving Suggestions

This Roasted Delicata Squash with Rosemary makes a wonderful side dish for a variety of fall meals. Serve it alongside roasted chicken, grilled pork chops, or even as part of a vegetarian feast. The natural sweetness of the delicata squash pairs beautifully with savory main courses, making it a versatile addition to your dinner table.

Nutritional Benefits

Delicata squash is not only delicious but also packed with nutrients. It is a great source of dietary fiber, vitamin C, and vitamin A, making it a healthy choice for your fall menu. The addition of heart-healthy olive oil and aromatic rosemary further enhances the nutritional profile of this dish, making it a wholesome and satisfying option for you and your family.

Final Thoughts

With its simplicity and unbeatable flavor, this Roasted Delicata Squash with Rosemary recipe is a must-try for anyone who loves easy, seasonal cooking. Whether you’re hosting a fall gathering or simply craving a comforting side dish, this recipe is sure to impress. Embrace the flavors of autumn with this quick and easy dish, and elevate your culinary skills with the natural beauty of delicata squash and the aromatic essence of rosemary.

Enjoy the warmth and comfort of fall with this delightful side dish, and savor the simple pleasures of seasonal cooking. Happy roasting!
