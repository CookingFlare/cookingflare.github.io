Classic Chicken Noodle Soup

Prep Time: 15 minutes | Cook Time: 45 minutes | Total Time: 1 hour

Ingredients:

  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 carrots, sliced
  • 2 celery stalks, chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 6 cups chicken broth
  • 2 cups shredded cooked chicken
  • 2 cups egg noodles
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley for garnish

Instructions:

  1. In a large pot, heat olive oil over medium heat. Add onion, carrots, and celery. Cook until vegetables are tender.
  2. Add garlic and cook for another minute.
  3. Pour in chicken broth and bring to a simmer.
  4. Add shredded chicken, egg noodles, and dried thyme. Cook until noodles are tender.
  5. Season with salt and pepper.
  6. Serve hot, garnished with fresh parsley.

Spicy Lentil Soup

Prep Time: 10 minutes | Cook Time: 30 minutes | Total Time: 40 minutes

Ingredients:

  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 2 carrots, diced
  • 2 celery stalks, diced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup dried lentils
  • 4 cups vegetable broth
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon cayenne pepper
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh cilantro for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Heat vegetable oil in a pot over medium heat. Add onion, carrots, and celery. Cook until softened.
  2. Stir in garlic and cook for another minute.
  3. Add lentils, vegetable broth, cumin, paprika, and cayenne pepper. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat and simmer until lentils are tender.
  4. Season with salt and pepper.
  5. Serve hot, garnished with fresh cilantro.

Creamy Broccoli Cheddar Soup

Prep Time: 20 minutes | Cook Time: 30 minutes | Total Time: 50 minutes

Ingredients:

  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 4 cups broccoli florets
  • 4 cups chicken broth
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 2 cups shredded cheddar cheese
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Croutons for garnish

Instructions:

  1. In a pot, melt butter over medium heat. Add onion and garlic, cooking until softened.
  2. Add broccoli and chicken broth. Simmer until broccoli is tender.
  3. Use an immersion blender to blend the soup until smooth.
  4. Stir in heavy cream and cheddar cheese until melted.
  5. Season with salt and pepper.
  6. Serve hot, topped with croutons.
