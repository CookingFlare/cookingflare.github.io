Creamy Keto Broccoli and Bacon Salad: A Refreshing Side Dish


Are you tired of the same old side dishes that lack excitement and flavor? Look no further! This Creamy Keto Broccoli and Bacon Salad will take your taste buds on a delightful journey while keeping you on track with your low-carb, high-fat ketogenic diet. Packed with nutrients and bursting with flavor, this salad is sure to become a new favorite in your recipe repertoire.

Ingredients

  • 4 cups fresh broccoli florets
  • 8 slices of bacon, cooked and crumbled
  • 1/2 cup mayonnaise
  • 1/4 cup sour cream
  • 2 tablespoons apple cider vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon Dijon mustard
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
  • Salt and pepper, to taste

Instructions

  1. Start by blanching the broccoli florets in boiling water for about 2 minutes. This will help to soften them slightly while still maintaining their crispiness. Once blanched, immediately transfer the broccoli to an ice bath to stop the cooking process and preserve their vibrant green color. Drain well and set aside.

  2. In a large mixing bowl, combine the mayonnaise, sour cream, apple cider vinegar, Dijon mustard, garlic powder, salt, and pepper. Whisk together until well combined and creamy.

  3. Add the blanched broccoli florets and crumbled bacon to the creamy dressing. Gently toss until all the ingredients are evenly coated.

  4. Cover the bowl with plastic wrap and refrigerate for at least 30 minutes to allow the flavors to meld together and the salad to chill.

  5. Once chilled, give the salad a final toss to ensure all the flavors are well distributed. Serve immediately and enjoy the refreshing crunch of the broccoli, the smoky saltiness of the bacon, and the creamy tang of the dressing.

Tips and Variations

  • Feel free to add additional toppings to customize your salad. Chopped red onion, sunflower seeds, or grated cheddar cheese would all be delicious additions.
  • If you prefer a vegetarian option, simply omit the bacon and add some toasted nuts or seeds for an extra crunch.
  • This salad can be prepared in advance and stored in the refrigerator for up to two days. Just make sure to give it a good stir before serving to redistribute the dressing.

Nutritional Information

Each serving of this Creamy Keto Broccoli and Bacon Salad contains approximately:

  • Calories: 230
  • Fat: 20g
  • Carbohydrates: 5g
  • Fiber: 2g
  • Protein: 7g

Final Thoughts

The Creamy Keto Broccoli and Bacon Salad is a refreshing side dish that will elevate any meal. With its creamy dressing, crisp broccoli, and smoky bacon, it’s a perfect blend of flavors and textures. Whether you’re following a ketogenic diet or simply looking for a delicious and nutritious side dish, this salad is a must-try. So go ahead, indulge in this guilt-free pleasure and satisfy your taste buds while staying on track with your healthy lifestyle.
