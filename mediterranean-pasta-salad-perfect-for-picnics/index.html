Mediterranean Pasta Salad: Perfect for Picnics


As the sun starts to shine brighter and the temperatures begin to rise, there's nothing like a cool and refreshing Mediterranean pasta salad to quench your thirst for a delightful summer meal. This Mediterranean pasta salad is a vibrant and flavorful dish perfect for your next picnic or outdoor gathering. Packed with a medley of colorful vegetables, savory cheese, and a zesty Mediterranean dressing, this salad is sure to be a crowd-pleaser. The Perfect Pasta for a Mediterranean Pasta Salad For this salad, you'll want to choose a pasta that pairs well with the bold flavors of the Mediterranean. A good rule of thumb is to select a pasta that's slightly firm in texture and has a subtle texture that won't get soggy when dressed with the savory sauce. Penne or bucatini are great options, but feel free to experiment with other shapes like orzo or rotini to add a unique twist to the dish. Harvesting the Flavors of the Mediterranean The Mediterranean region is renowned for its rich and aromatic produce, and this salad takes full advantage of its bounty. Start by chopping up a cucumber, a tomato, and a Bell pepper into bite-sized pieces. For added crunch, toss in some crunchy kalamata olives and artichoke hearts. A handful of fresh parsley and basil add a fragrant and herbaceous note to the salad, while a sprinkle of feta cheese provides a salty and creamy contrast. The Magic of Mediterranean Dressing While store-bought dressings can be convenient, there's something special about creating your own Mediterranean Panina-style vinaigrette from scratch. Combine olive oil, lemon juice, and a pinch of oregano to create a zesty and refreshing dressing that complements the flavors of the Mediterranean beautifully. Make It Your Own One of the best things about this Mediterranean pasta salad is its flexibility. Feel free to add or subtract ingredients based on your personal taste and dietary preferences. Looking for a vegetarian option? Swap out the prosciutto for some roasted red peppers or grilled portobello mushrooms. Want to add some heat to the salad? Mix in some spicy olives or diced jalapeños. A Seasonal Salad that Serves a Crowd This Mediterranean pasta salad is perfect for your next picnic or outdoor gathering, and can be easily scaled up or down to suit your needs. The salad is light and easy to eat, making it an ideal choice for a summer picnic or BBQ. To make it even more substantial, toss in some grilled chicken or salmon for added protein. Cooking Time: 20-30 minutes Servings: 8-10 people **Ingredients:

1 pound of pasta (penne, bucatini, or orzo work well) 2 medium-sized cucumbers, chopped 2 medium-sized tomatoes, chopped 1 medium-sized Bell pepper, chopped 1/4 cup of artichoke hearts, canned or fresh 1/4 cup of crumbled feta cheese 1/4 cup of crunchy kalamata olives, pitted 1/4 cup of chopped fresh parsley and basil 1/4 cup of Mediterranean Panina-style vinaigrette (see below) Salt and pepper to taste Grilled chicken or salmon, optional

Mediterranean Panina-Style Vinaigrette

1/4 cup of olive oil 1/4 cup of lemon juice 1 teaspoon of dried oregano Salt and pepper to taste

Step-by-Step Instructions

Cook the pasta according to package instructions until al dente. Drain and set aside to cool. In a large bowl, combine the cooled pasta, chopped cucumbers, tomatoes, and Bell pepper. Add the artichoke hearts, feta cheese, and olives, and toss to combine. Drizzle the Mediterranean Panina-style vinaigrette over the salad and toss to coat. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Garnish with fresh parsley and basil. Serve immediately, or refrigerate for up to 24 hours before serving.
