Peachy Keen Cobbler Bars: A Portable Twist on a Classic Dessert


Craving a deliciously sweet treat that you can take on the go? Look no further than these Peachy Keen Cobbler Bars! With a portable twist on a classic dessert, these bars are sure to satisfy your cravings and leave you wanting more. Whether you’re heading to a picnic, family gathering, or simply looking for a tasty way to end your day, these bars are the perfect choice.

The Perfect Combination of Sweet and Tangy

Peaches are the star of the show in this recipe, providing a burst of natural sweetness and a tangy flavor that complements the buttery crust perfectly. The combination of juicy peaches and a crumbly, golden-brown topping creates a delightful texture that will have everyone coming back for seconds.

A Simple and Versatile Recipe

One of the best things about these Peachy Keen Cobbler Bars is how easy they are to make. With just a few simple ingredients, you can whip up a batch in no time. Plus, this recipe is incredibly versatile, allowing you to customize it to your liking. Add a sprinkle of cinnamon for some extra warmth or top it off with a scoop of vanilla ice cream for a truly indulgent experience.

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup unsalted butter, cold and cubed
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 4 cups fresh peaches, peeled and diced
  • 1/2 cup brown sugar
  • 1 tablespoon cornstarch

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C) and line a 9x13-inch baking dish with parchment paper.

  2. In a large mixing bowl, combine the flour, granulated sugar, baking powder, and salt. Cut in the cold butter using a pastry cutter or your fingertips until the mixture resembles coarse crumbs.

  3. In a separate bowl, whisk together the eggs and vanilla extract. Pour the egg mixture into the flour mixture and stir until a dough forms. Press two-thirds of the dough into the prepared baking dish, creating an even layer.

  4. In another bowl, toss the diced peaches with the brown sugar and cornstarch until well-coated. Spread the peach mixture evenly over the dough in the baking dish.

  5. Crumble the remaining dough over the peaches, creating a crumbly topping.

  6. Bake in the preheated oven for 40-45 minutes, or until the crust is golden brown and the peaches are bubbly.

  7. Allow the bars to cool completely before cutting into squares. Serve as is or with a dollop of whipped cream or a scoop of vanilla ice cream for an extra special touch.

Take Your Dessert on the Go!

The beauty of these Peachy Keen Cobbler Bars is their portability. Simply cut them into squares, pack them up, and you’re ready to hit the road. Whether you’re planning a picnic in the park or a day at the beach, these bars are the perfect sweet treat to bring along. Just be prepared to share, because once word gets out about these delicious bars, everyone will want a taste!

Final Thoughts

Peachy Keen Cobbler Bars are a delightful twist on a classic dessert. With their portable nature, easy preparation, and mouthwatering flavor, they are sure to become a staple in your dessert repertoire. So the next time you’re in the mood for something sweet, give these bars a try. They’re guaranteed to leave you feeling peachy keen!
