Soft and Fluffy Dinner Rolls: A Family Favorite Recipe


There’s nothing quite like the aroma of freshly baked dinner rolls wafting through the kitchen. Soft, fluffy, and oh-so-delicious, these dinner rolls are a family favorite that will have everyone coming back for seconds. Whether you’re hosting a special occasion or simply want to elevate your weeknight dinner, these rolls are the perfect addition to any meal.

Ingredients

  • 4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 2 1/4 teaspoons instant yeast
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, melted
  • 1 cup warm milk (around 110°F)
  • 2 large eggs

Instructions

  1. In a large mixing bowl, combine the flour, sugar, yeast, and salt. Mix well to ensure the ingredients are evenly distributed.

  2. In a separate bowl, whisk together the melted butter, warm milk, and eggs until well combined.

  3. Make a well in the center of the dry ingredients and pour in the wet mixture. Stir with a wooden spoon or a dough hook attachment until the dough comes together.

  4. Transfer the dough onto a lightly floured surface and knead for about 5-7 minutes, or until the dough is smooth and elastic. Add more flour if the dough is too sticky.

  5. Place the dough in a greased bowl, cover it with a clean kitchen towel, and let it rise in a warm place for about 1-2 hours, or until it doubles in size.

  6. Once the dough has risen, punch it down to release any air bubbles. Turn it out onto a floured surface and divide it into 12 equal pieces.

  7. Shape each piece into a ball by rolling it between your hands. Place the rolls in a greased baking dish, leaving some space between them for rising.

  8. Cover the baking dish with a clean kitchen towel and let the rolls rise for another 30-45 minutes, or until they have doubled in size.

  9. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).

  10. Bake the rolls in the preheated oven for 15-18 minutes, or until they turn golden brown on top.

  11. Remove the rolls from the oven and let them cool slightly before serving.

Serving Suggestions

These soft and fluffy dinner rolls are incredibly versatile and can be enjoyed in a variety of ways. Serve them as a side with your favorite soup or stew, use them to make sandwiches, or simply slather them with butter and enjoy them on their own. No matter how you choose to enjoy them, these dinner rolls are sure to be a hit with your family and friends.

Tips and Tricks

  • For an extra touch of flavor, you can brush the rolls with melted butter as soon as they come out of the oven.
  • If you prefer a sweeter roll, you can increase the amount of sugar in the recipe.
  • To save time, you can make the dough ahead of time and refrigerate it overnight. Just make sure to let it come to room temperature before shaping and baking.

So there you have it! A simple yet incredibly delicious recipe for soft and fluffy dinner rolls that will have your loved ones asking for more. Give it a try and let the comforting aroma of freshly baked bread fill your home. Happy baking!
