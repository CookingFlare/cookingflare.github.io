Picnic Perfect Chicken Drumstick Recipes for Easy Outdoor Dining


Are you planning a picnic and wondering what delicious dishes to pack? Look no further! In this blog post, we’ll explore some mouthwatering recipes featuring chicken drumsticks that are perfect for outdoor dining. These recipes are not only easy to make but also incredibly flavorful, making them ideal for a picnic spread. So grab your picnic basket and let’s get cooking!

1. Tangy BBQ Drumsticks

The first recipe on our list is a classic BBQ drumstick that will surely impress your friends and family. To prepare this tangy delight, follow these simple steps:

Ingredients:

  • 8 chicken drumsticks
  • 1 cup BBQ sauce
  • 2 tablespoons honey
  • 1 tablespoon Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your grill to medium-high heat.
  2. In a small bowl, mix the BBQ sauce, honey, Worcestershire sauce, garlic powder, salt, and pepper.
  3. Brush the chicken drumsticks generously with the sauce mixture.
  4. Place the drumsticks on the grill and cook for about 15-20 minutes, turning occasionally, until the chicken is cooked through and the skin is crispy.
  5. Serve hot with extra BBQ sauce on the side.

2. Lemon Herb Drumsticks

Add a burst of freshness to your picnic with these zesty and aromatic lemon herb drumsticks. Here’s how to make them:

Ingredients:

  • 8 chicken drumsticks
  • 2 lemons (juice and zest)
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon fresh thyme leaves
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a large bowl, combine the lemon juice, lemon zest, olive oil, minced garlic, thyme leaves, salt, and pepper.
  2. Add the chicken drumsticks to the bowl and toss them in the marinade, ensuring they are well coated.
  3. Cover the bowl and refrigerate for at least 30 minutes to allow the flavors to infuse.
  4. Preheat your grill to medium heat.
  5. Grill the drumsticks for about 20-25 minutes, turning occasionally, until the chicken is cooked through and the skin is golden brown.
  6. Remove from the grill and let them rest for a few minutes before serving.

3. Spicy Cajun Drumsticks

If you’re a fan of bold flavors and a little heat, these spicy Cajun drumsticks are a must-try. Here’s what you’ll need:

Ingredients:

  • 8 chicken drumsticks
  • 2 tablespoons Cajun seasoning
  • 1 tablespoon paprika
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon onion powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon cayenne pepper (adjust to your preferred spice level)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil

Instructions:

  1. In a small bowl, combine the Cajun seasoning, paprika, garlic powder, onion powder, cayenne pepper, salt, and pepper.
  2. Rub the spice mixture evenly over the chicken drumsticks, ensuring they are well coated.
  3. Heat the olive oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat.
  4. Add the drumsticks to the skillet and cook for about 15-20 minutes, turning occasionally, until the chicken is cooked through and the skin is crispy.
  5. Remove from heat and let them cool slightly before packing for your picnic.

Conclusion

There you have it! Three fantastic chicken drumstick recipes that are perfect for your next outdoor picnic. Whether you prefer tangy BBQ, zesty lemon herb, or spicy Cajun flavors, these recipes are sure to impress. So pack up your picnic basket, grab some refreshing drinks, and enjoy a delightful meal in the great outdoors. Happy picnicking!

Note: Remember to always follow food safety guidelines when preparing and storing picnic meals. Keep perishable items chilled and pack them in insulated containers to maintain freshness.
