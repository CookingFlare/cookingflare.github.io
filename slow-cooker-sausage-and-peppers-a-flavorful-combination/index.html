Slow Cooker Sausage and Peppers: A Flavorful Combination


Are you looking for a delicious and hassle-free meal that will leave your taste buds dancing with joy? Look no further! In this recipe, we will show you how to make Slow Cooker Sausage and Peppers, a flavorful combination that will satisfy your cravings and impress your family and friends.

Ingredients

To make this mouthwatering dish, you will need the following ingredients:

  • 4 Italian sausages (hot or mild, depending on your preference)
  • 2 bell peppers (red, green, or a combination of both), thinly sliced
  • 1 onion, thinly sliced
  • 3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 can (14.5 ounces) diced tomatoes
  • 1 can (6 ounces) tomato paste
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1/4 teaspoon red pepper flakes (optional, for a spicy kick)
  • Fresh basil or parsley, chopped (for garnish)

Instructions

  1. Begin by browning the sausages in a skillet over medium heat. This step adds a depth of flavor to the dish. Once browned, transfer the sausages to your slow cooker.

  2. In the same skillet, sauté the sliced peppers, onion, and minced garlic until they become tender and slightly caramelized. This process will release their natural sweetness and enhance the overall taste. Once done, transfer the sautéed vegetables to the slow cooker, on top of the sausages.

  3. In a separate bowl, combine the diced tomatoes, tomato paste, dried oregano, dried basil, salt, black pepper, and red pepper flakes (if desired). Mix well to ensure all the flavors are evenly distributed. Pour this tomato mixture over the sausages and peppers in the slow cooker.

  4. Cover the slow cooker and cook on low heat for 6-8 hours or on high heat for 3-4 hours. The longer cooking time allows the flavors to meld together, resulting in a rich and delicious sauce.

  5. Once the cooking time is up, give the dish a gentle stir to combine all the ingredients. The sausages should be tender and juicy, and the peppers should be soft and flavorful.

  6. Serve the Slow Cooker Sausage and Peppers over cooked pasta, rice, or with a crusty bread to soak up the delicious sauce. Garnish with fresh chopped basil or parsley for a pop of color and added freshness.

Tips and Variations

  • If you prefer a thicker sauce, you can remove the lid of the slow cooker during the last 30 minutes of cooking to allow some of the liquid to evaporate.
  • Feel free to add other vegetables such as mushrooms or zucchini for added texture and flavor.
  • For a complete meal, serve the Slow Cooker Sausage and Peppers with a side salad or steamed vegetables.

Conclusion

Slow Cooker Sausage and Peppers is a delightful dish that brings together the savory flavors of Italian sausages, sweet and tangy peppers, and aromatic herbs. The slow cooking process allows all the ingredients to meld together, resulting in a mouthwatering meal that is perfect for any occasion.

So why wait? Gather your ingredients, dust off your slow cooker, and get ready to impress your family and friends with this easy and delicious recipe. Enjoy the rich flavors and savor every bite of this flavorful combination!
