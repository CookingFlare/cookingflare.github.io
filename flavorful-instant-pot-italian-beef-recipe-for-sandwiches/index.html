Flavorful Instant Pot Italian Beef Recipe for Sandwiches


If you’re a fan of hearty sandwiches packed with robust flavors, then this Flavorful Instant Pot Italian Beef Recipe is for you. With tender beef cooked to perfection in a rich and savory Italian-inspired sauce, this dish is guaranteed to satisfy your cravings. Whether you’re hosting a casual gathering or simply looking for a delicious weeknight meal, this recipe is sure to impress.

Ingredients

  • 3 pounds beef chuck roast
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 onion, thinly sliced
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup beef broth
  • 1 cup red wine
  • 1 can (14 ounces) crushed tomatoes
  • 2 tablespoons tomato paste
  • 1 tablespoon Italian seasoning
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1/4 teaspoon red pepper flakes (optional, for an extra kick)

Instructions

  1. Start by seasoning the beef chuck roast with salt and pepper. This will help enhance the flavor of the meat.

  2. Set your Instant Pot to the sauté function and heat the olive oil. Once hot, add the seasoned beef roast and sear it on all sides until browned. This step will help seal in the juices and create a delicious crust on the meat.

  3. Remove the beef from the Instant Pot and set it aside. In the same pot, add the sliced onion and minced garlic. Sauté until the onion becomes translucent and fragrant.

  4. Deglaze the pot by adding the beef broth and red wine. Scrape the bottom of the pot with a wooden spoon to release any browned bits that may have stuck to the surface. These bits are packed with flavor and will enhance the taste of the sauce.

  5. Add the crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, Italian seasoning, dried basil, dried oregano, salt, black pepper, and red pepper flakes (if using). Stir well to combine all the ingredients.

  6. Return the seared beef chuck roast to the pot, making sure it’s fully submerged in the sauce.

  7. Close the Instant Pot lid and set the valve to the sealing position. Select the “Meat/Stew” function and set the cooking time to 60 minutes on high pressure.

  8. Once the cooking cycle is complete, allow the pressure to release naturally for 15 minutes. Then, carefully perform a quick pressure release to release any remaining pressure.

  9. Remove the beef from the Instant Pot and shred it using two forks. The meat should be tender and easily fall apart.

  10. If desired, you can thicken the sauce by selecting the sauté function on the Instant Pot and allowing it to simmer for a few minutes. This will help concentrate the flavors and create a richer sauce.

  11. Serve the flavorful Italian beef on your favorite sandwich roll, topped with melted provolone cheese and giardiniera for a touch of tanginess. Alternatively, you can serve it over a bed of polenta or pasta for a heartier meal.

  12. Enjoy the robust flavors of this Flavorful Instant Pot Italian Beef Recipe for Sandwiches!

Conclusion

This Flavorful Instant Pot Italian Beef Recipe is a delicious and easy way to enjoy a hearty sandwich filled with the flavors of Italy. The tender and flavorful beef, combined with the rich and savory sauce, creates a mouthwatering combination that will leave you wanting more. Whether you’re hosting a gathering or simply looking for a satisfying weeknight meal, this recipe is sure to impress. So fire up your Instant Pot and get ready to indulge in a taste of Italy.
