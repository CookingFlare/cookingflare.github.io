Butternut Squash and Lentil Curry: A Vegan Fall Dish


Welcome to our vegan fall recipe series! In today’s post, we’re excited to share a delicious and nutritious recipe for Butternut Squash and Lentil Curry. This hearty curry is packed with flavors and perfect for chilly autumn evenings. Whether you’re a vegan, vegetarian, or simply looking to incorporate more plant-based meals into your diet, this dish is sure to impress. So, let’s dive right in!

Ingredients

  • 1 medium-sized butternut squash, peeled, seeded, and cubed
  • 1 cup red lentils, rinsed and drained
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 thumb-sized piece of ginger, grated
  • 1 tablespoon curry powder
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon ground turmeric
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1 can (400ml) coconut milk
  • 2 cups vegetable broth
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh cilantro, for garnish
  • Cooked rice or naan bread, for serving

Instructions

  1. Heat the olive oil in a large pot over medium heat. Add the chopped onion, minced garlic, and grated ginger. Sauté until the onion becomes translucent and fragrant.

  2. Add the cubed butternut squash to the pot and cook for about 5 minutes, stirring occasionally.

  3. In a small bowl, combine the curry powder, cumin, turmeric, and paprika. Mix well to create a spice blend.

  4. Add the spice blend to the pot and stir to coat the squash evenly. Cook for another minute to toast the spices and enhance their flavors.

  5. Pour in the coconut milk and vegetable broth. Stir well to combine all the ingredients.

  6. Add the rinsed lentils to the pot and season with salt and pepper to taste. Give it a good stir.

  7. Bring the mixture to a boil, then reduce the heat to low. Cover the pot and let it simmer for about 25-30 minutes or until the lentils and butternut squash are tender.

  8. Once cooked, remove the pot from the heat and let it sit for a few minutes to allow the flavors to meld together.

  9. Serve the Butternut Squash and Lentil Curry over cooked rice or with warm naan bread. Garnish with fresh cilantro for added freshness and a pop of color.

Tips and Variations

  • For an extra kick of heat, add a chopped chili pepper or a sprinkle of chili flakes to the curry.
  • Feel free to add other vegetables like spinach, bell peppers, or cauliflower to the curry for added texture and nutrition.
  • If you prefer a creamier curry, you can blend a small portion of the cooked curry and stir it back into the pot before serving.
  • Leftovers can be stored in an airtight container in the refrigerator for up to 3 days. The flavors tend to develop even more over time, making it even more delicious.

Conclusion

There you have it – a scrumptious Butternut Squash and Lentil Curry that will warm your soul and satisfy your taste buds. This vegan fall dish is not only packed with plant-based goodness but also incredibly easy to prepare. So, gather your ingredients, follow our simple steps, and enjoy a comforting meal that celebrates the flavors of the season. Happy cooking!
