Gluten-Free Vegetable Lasagna: A Hearty and Nourishing Dinner


If you’re looking for a comforting and wholesome dinner option that’s also gluten-free, then this Vegetable Lasagna recipe is just what you need. Packed with layers of vibrant vegetables, rich tomato sauce, and creamy cheese, this dish is a true crowd-pleaser. Whether you’re following a gluten-free diet or simply love the idea of incorporating more veggies into your meals, this lasagna is sure to become a staple in your recipe repertoire.

Ingredients

For the Vegetable Filling:

  • 2 medium zucchinis, thinly sliced
  • 1 large eggplant, thinly sliced
  • 1 red bell pepper, thinly sliced
  • 1 yellow bell pepper, thinly sliced
  • 1 cup sliced mushrooms
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

For the Tomato Sauce:

  • 2 cans (14 oz each) crushed tomatoes
  • 3 tablespoons tomato paste
  • 2 teaspoons dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste

For the Cheese Filling:

  • 2 cups ricotta cheese
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh basil

Other:

  • 12 gluten-free lasagna noodles
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Vegetable Filling:

    • In a large skillet, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the chopped onion and garlic, and sauté until fragrant.
    • Add the sliced zucchinis, eggplant, bell peppers, and mushrooms to the skillet. Season with salt and pepper, and cook until the vegetables are tender. Set aside.

  2. Make the Tomato Sauce:

    • In a saucepan, combine the crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, oregano, basil, garlic powder, salt, and pepper. Simmer over low heat for 15-20 minutes, stirring occasionally.

  3. Prepare the Cheese Filling:

    • In a bowl, mix the ricotta cheese, grated Parmesan cheese, egg, and chopped basil until well combined.

  4. Assemble the Lasagna:

    • Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
    • Spread a layer of tomato sauce on the bottom of a 9x13 inch baking dish.
    • Place 4 lasagna noodles on top of the sauce.
    • Spread half of the vegetable filling over the noodles, followed by a layer of the cheese filling, and a sprinkle of mozzarella cheese.
    • Repeat the layers, finishing with a final layer of tomato sauce and mozzarella cheese on top.

  5. Bake and Serve:

    • Cover the baking dish with foil and bake for 40 minutes.
    • Remove the foil and bake for an additional 15 minutes, or until the cheese is golden and bubbly.
    • Allow the lasagna to cool for 10-15 minutes before slicing and serving.

Nutritional Information

  • Calories: 380kcal
  • Fat: 18g
  • Carbohydrates: 32g
  • Fiber: 5g
  • Protein: 23g

Final Thoughts

This Gluten-Free Vegetable Lasagna is not only a delicious and satisfying meal but also a great way to incorporate more vegetables into your diet. Whether you’re cooking for a family dinner or simply meal-prepping for the week, this recipe is versatile and sure to please everyone at the table. Plus, the leftovers taste even better the next day!

So, the next time you’re craving a comforting and nourishing dinner, give this recipe a try. You won’t be disappointed!

Enjoy your hearty and nourishing Gluten-Free Vegetable Lasagna!
