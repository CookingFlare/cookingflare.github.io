Spicy Korean BBQ Chicken Skewers for a Tasty Snack


Are you craving a delicious and spicy snack that will leave your taste buds begging for more? Look no further than these mouthwatering Spicy Korean BBQ Chicken Skewers! Packed with bold flavors and a delightful kick, these skewers are the perfect combination of sweet, savory, and spicy. Whether you’re hosting a party or simply looking to elevate your snacking game, these skewers will not disappoint.

Ingredients

To make these Spicy Korean BBQ Chicken Skewers, you’ll need the following ingredients:

  • 1 pound of boneless, skinless chicken thighs, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons gochujang (Korean chili paste)
  • 2 tablespoons honey
  • 1 tablespoon sesame oil
  • 1 tablespoon rice vinegar
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon grated ginger
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1/4 teaspoon red pepper flakes (adjust to your preferred level of spiciness)
  • Wooden skewers, soaked in water for 30 minutes

Instructions

  1. In a bowl, combine the soy sauce, gochujang, honey, sesame oil, rice vinegar, minced garlic, grated ginger, black pepper, and red pepper flakes. Mix well to create a marinade.

  2. Add the chicken thigh pieces to the marinade and toss until they are well coated. Allow the chicken to marinate for at least 30 minutes, or for even more flavor, refrigerate it overnight.

  3. Preheat your grill to medium-high heat.

  4. Thread the marinated chicken pieces onto the soaked wooden skewers, ensuring an even distribution.

  5. Place the skewers on the preheated grill and cook for about 4-5 minutes per side, or until the chicken is cooked through and slightly charred. Make sure to rotate the skewers to prevent burning.

  6. Once the chicken is cooked, remove the skewers from the grill and allow them to rest for a few minutes.

  7. Serve the Spicy Korean BBQ Chicken Skewers as a delicious snack or as part of a larger meal. Garnish with sesame seeds and chopped green onions for added flavor and visual appeal.

Tips and Variations

  • If you don’t have a grill, you can also cook these skewers in a grill pan or even in the oven. Simply adjust the cooking time accordingly.

  • Feel free to adjust the level of spiciness by adding more or less gochujang and red pepper flakes according to your taste preferences.

  • For an extra burst of flavor, consider brushing some of the marinade onto the skewers while grilling.

  • If you’re a fan of vegetables, you can also add bell peppers, onions, or zucchini to the skewers for added color and texture.

  • Serve these Spicy Korean BBQ Chicken Skewers with a side of steamed rice or a refreshing Asian-inspired salad for a complete and satisfying meal.

So there you have it - a tantalizing recipe for Spicy Korean BBQ Chicken Skewers that will surely spice up your snacking routine. Whether you’re a fan of Korean cuisine or simply enjoy bold flavors, these skewers are bound to become a favorite in your household. So fire up the grill, get those skewers ready, and indulge in this mouthwatering snack that perfectly balances heat and sweetness. Enjoy!
