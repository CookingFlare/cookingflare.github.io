Summer BBQ Essentials: Side Dishes


Warm weather calls for outdoor cooking, and there’s no better way to celebrate than with a delicious barbecue. However, a fantastic barbecue goes beyond grilled meats – it’s the side dishes that truly elevate the experience. To help you make the most of your summer barbecues, let’s dive into a mix of classic and creative side dishes perfect for bringing your grilling game to the next level.

1. Sweet Corn Salads

Summer produce is abundant and sweet corn is a must-have on any barbecue spread. Combined with a zesty dressing and your favorite veggies – like tomatoes, peppers, and cucumbers – it becomes a refreshing crowd-pleaser. Adding a bit of feta cheese or crunch from nuts, like almonds or pecans, will bring a delightful contrasting texture, making this dish a swoon-worthy addition to your next barbecue.

2. Heirloom Tomato Caprese Skewers

Who can resist the classic combination of ripe tomatoes, fresh mozzarella, and fragrant basil? Heirloom tomatoes provide a lively pop of color, as well as varying flavors, in this simple yet gorgeous side dish. Thread them onto skewers, drizzle with balsamic glaze, and let your guests enjoy these bite-sized treasures.

3. Grilled Zucchini Rolls

Want to wow your guests? Try this unique yet effortless zucchini roll, an Italian-inspired masterpiece. Slice and stuff the grilled zucchini with a ricotta cheese mixture flavored with garlic, lemon, and herbs, then roll up and slice for a delicious ‘cap.’ Garnished with a drizzle of balsamic glaze and a sprinkle of parmesan cheese, this side will undoubtedly steal the show.

4. Fruited Salsa

Create a summer sensation with this fruity and vibrant salsa, perfect for pairing with grilled meats. Featuring summer favorites, like watermelon, pineapple, and mango, this dish offers a flavorful burst of tropical goodness. Combined with tomatoes, red onions, and cilantro, your taste buds will thank you.

5. Smoky Grilled Potatoes

Fire up the grill and throw on some potatoes to create an irresistible side that’s as straightforward as it is delectable. A blend of smoked paprika, garlic, and cumin infuses your potatoes with robust flavors, and a squeeze of fresh lemon juice adds a zesty finishing touch.

6. Summer Gazpacho

Capture the essence of summer with a chilled garden-fresh gazpacho. This Spanish cold soup seamlessly combines ripe tomatoes, cucumber, red pepper, and onion to create a velvety, tangy concoction with a hint of garlic and sherry vinegar. Complete the dish with crumbled feta cheese or toasted crusty bread to mop up the delightful soup.

7. Grilled Artichokes with Lemon Aioli

Grilled artichokes have a striking appearance and delicious flavor that never fails to impress. To enhance this savory side, create a tangy lemon aioli by blending mayonnaise, lemon juice, garlic, and Dijon mustard. Drizzle it over freshly grilled artichokes for an unforgettable barbecued feast.

As the sun sets on your barbecue, these dishes will linger comfortably in your guests’ memories. Dive into these timeless and innovative side recipes and help your barbecue soar to new heights of delectability. So, gather your ingredients, ready the grill, and let the summer cooking magic unfold.
