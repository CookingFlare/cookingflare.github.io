Delicious Homemade Whoopie Pies Recipe


Whoopie pies are a beloved treat, offering the best of both worlds: soft, cake-like cookies sandwiched with a rich, creamy filling. Whether you are a baking enthusiast or a beginner, this homemade whoopie pies recipe is perfect for anyone craving a sweet, nostalgic dessert. In this post, we’ll walk you through how to make these delicious pies from scratch, including helpful tips to ensure your whoopie pies turn out perfectly every time.

What Are Whoopie Pies?

Whoopie pies are a unique dessert that consists of two soft, round cookies filled with a creamy filling, often made from marshmallow, buttercream, or even cream cheese. The outer layers are usually made with chocolate or pumpkin, creating a variety of flavor combinations to suit different tastes. While their origins are debated, whoopie pies have become an iconic treat across the United States, particularly in the Northeast and parts of Pennsylvania. Whether served at a family gathering or as a quick snack, these little pies are always a crowd-pleaser.

Why You’ll Love This Homemade Whoopie Pies Recipe

  • Customizable: The flavor of the cookies and the filling can be easily adapted to suit your preferences. From chocolate and vanilla to seasonal flavors like pumpkin, whoopie pies are endlessly versatile.

  • Perfect for All Occasions: These sweet treats make a great addition to birthday parties, holiday gatherings, or as an afternoon pick-me-up.

  • Made From Scratch: Making whoopie pies from scratch not only enhances the flavor but also allows you to control the ingredients, ensuring a more wholesome treat than store-bought alternatives.

Ingredients You’ll Need

To make these homemade whoopie pies, you’ll need the following ingredients for both the cookies and the filling:

For the Cookies:

  • 2 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 cup unsweetened cocoa powder (use high-quality cocoa for the best flavor)
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter (softened)
  • 1 1/2 cups granulated sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 cup buttermilk (if you don’t have buttermilk, substitute with regular milk and a tablespoon of vinegar)

For the Filling:

  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter (softened)
  • 2 cups powdered sugar
  • 1 tablespoon vanilla extract
  • 1/2 cup marshmallow fluff (or marshmallow creme)
  • Pinch of salt

How to Make Whoopie Pies: A Step-by-Step Guide

Making whoopie pies from scratch may sound like a daunting task, but it’s actually quite simple and incredibly rewarding. Follow this step-by-step guide, and you’ll have delicious homemade whoopie pies in no time!

Step 1: Prepare the Cookie Batter

Start by preheating your oven to 350°F (175°C) and lining two baking sheets with parchment paper. This ensures the cookies will not stick and helps them bake evenly.

In a medium-sized bowl, whisk together the flour, baking powder, baking soda, cocoa powder, and salt. Set this dry mixture aside.

Next, in a separate large bowl, cream together the butter and granulated sugar until the mixture becomes light and fluffy. This should take about 3-4 minutes using an electric mixer or a stand mixer. Once combined, add the eggs, one at a time, beating well after each addition. Then, stir in the vanilla extract.

Gradually add the dry ingredients to the butter mixture in batches, alternating with the buttermilk. Begin and end with the dry ingredients. Mix everything just until combined, being careful not to overmix. The batter should be thick but spreadable.

Step 2: Shape the Cookie Dough

Using a spoon or a cookie scoop, drop tablespoon-sized portions of dough onto the prepared baking sheets, spacing each one about 2 inches apart. If you prefer uniform-sized cookies, you can use a pastry bag fitted with a large round tip to pipe the dough onto the baking sheets.

Bake the cookies in the preheated oven for 8-10 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean. Be careful not to overbake them, as the cookies should remain soft and moist. Allow the cookies to cool on the baking sheets for a few minutes before transferring them to a wire rack to cool completely.

Step 3: Make the Filling

While the cookies are cooling, it’s time to prepare the creamy filling.

In a large bowl, beat the butter with an electric mixer until smooth and creamy. Gradually add the powdered sugar and continue beating until the mixture is light and fluffy, which should take about 3-4 minutes. Stir in the vanilla extract and a pinch of salt.

Finally, add the marshmallow fluff to the mixture and beat until fully incorporated. The filling should be thick, smooth, and spreadable. If it’s too thick, you can add a little milk, one teaspoon at a time, until you reach the desired consistency.

Step 4: Assemble the Whoopie Pies

Once the cookies are completely cool, it’s time to assemble your whoopie pies. Flip half of the cookies upside down, and on the flat side of each, spread a generous amount of the marshmallow filling. Place the remaining cookies on top, pressing gently to form a sandwich.

For a decorative touch, you can roll the edges of the filling in colored sprinkles, mini chocolate chips, or any topping of your choice.

Step 5: Enjoy Your Homemade Whoopie Pies

Allow the whoopie pies to rest for a few minutes to let the filling set. Then, they’re ready to enjoy! These pies are best eaten fresh but can also be stored in an airtight container at room temperature for up to 3 days. For longer storage, keep them in the fridge for up to a week.

Tips for Perfect Whoopie Pies

  • Use room temperature ingredients: When making the cookie dough and filling, make sure your butter and eggs are at room temperature. This ensures better incorporation and a smoother texture.

  • Don’t overmix the dough: Overmixing can lead to dense cookies. Mix just until the dry ingredients are incorporated into the wet ingredients.

  • Chill the cookie dough (optional): If you prefer a thicker, firmer texture for your whoopie pies, you can chill the cookie dough in the refrigerator for about 30 minutes before baking. This helps the cookies retain their shape.

  • Adjust the sweetness: The marshmallow filling can be adjusted to suit your preferences. If you want a less sweet filling, reduce the powdered sugar by 1/4 cup.

  • Add flavor variations: Get creative with your whoopie pies by changing up the flavor of the filling. You can add peanut butter, chocolate ganache, or even flavored extracts (like almond or peppermint) for a unique twist.

  • Make mini whoopie pies: For bite-sized treats, use a tablespoon to scoop smaller portions of dough, and reduce the baking time by a few minutes. Mini whoopie pies are perfect for parties or gift-giving!

Variations of Whoopie Pies

1. Pumpkin Whoopie Pies

For a seasonal twist, swap out the cocoa powder for pumpkin puree and a teaspoon of pumpkin pie spice. This variation is perfect for fall and pairs wonderfully with a cream cheese filling instead of marshmallow fluff.

2. Red Velvet Whoopie Pies

For a festive, vibrant version, use red velvet cake mix or red food coloring along with cocoa powder to create a rich, red-colored cookie. A tangy cream cheese filling complements the flavors perfectly.

3. Chocolate Chip Whoopie Pies

Incorporate mini chocolate chips into the cookie dough for a fun, chocolate-filled version of this classic dessert. You can also add some chocolate chips to the filling for an extra indulgent treat.

4. S’mores Whoopie Pies

For a summer-inspired version, add graham cracker crumbs to the cookie dough and use a filling made of chocolate ganache and marshmallow fluff. These whoopie pies will remind you of a classic campfire treat!

Conclusion

Homemade whoopie pies are a delicious and fun dessert that’s perfect for any occasion. By following this simple recipe and adding your personal touch, you can create a sweet treat that’s sure to impress. Whether you stick to the classic chocolate filling or experiment with different variations, whoopie pies are always a crowd favorite. So grab your mixing bowls, preheat your oven, and get ready to enjoy these irresistible homemade treats!
