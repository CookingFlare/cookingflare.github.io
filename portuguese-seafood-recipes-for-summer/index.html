Portuguese Seafood Recipes for Summer


When it comes to fresh and flavorful seafood, Portuguese cuisine truly shines. With its long coastline, Portugal offers an abundance of delicious seafood dishes that are perfect for the summer season. From grilled sardines to seafood rice, here are some mouthwatering Portuguese seafood recipes that will transport your taste buds to the sunny shores of Portugal.

Grilled Sardines with Piri Piri Sauce

Ingredients:

  • Fresh sardines
  • Olive oil
  • Garlic
  • Red chili peppers
  • Lemon juice
  • Sea salt

Instructions:

  1. Clean the sardines by removing the scales and gutting them. Rinse and pat dry.
  2. Preheat the grill to medium-high heat.
  3. In a small bowl, mix olive oil, minced garlic, chopped red chili peppers, lemon juice, and a pinch of sea salt to create the piri piri sauce.
  4. Brush the sardines with the piri piri sauce, ensuring they are well coated.
  5. Place the sardines on the grill and cook for 3-4 minutes on each side, or until they are golden and crispy.
  6. Serve the grilled sardines with an extra drizzle of piri piri sauce and a wedge of lemon.

Seafood Rice (Arroz de Marisco)

Ingredients:

  • Prawns
  • Clams
  • Mussels
  • Rice
  • Olive oil
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Tomato
  • White wine
  • Parsley
  • Salt and pepper

Instructions:

  1. In a large pot, heat olive oil over medium heat. Add chopped onion and minced garlic, and sauté until they are soft and fragrant.
  2. Stir in diced tomatoes and cook until they start to break down.
  3. Add the rice to the pot and stir to coat it with the mixture.
  4. Pour in the white wine and let it simmer until it reduces slightly.
  5. Add the seafood (prawns, clams, and mussels) to the pot, and gently stir to combine with the rice.
  6. Pour in enough water to cover the rice and seafood, then bring it to a boil.
  7. Reduce the heat, cover the pot, and let it simmer for about 15-20 minutes, or until the rice is cooked and the seafood is tender.
  8. Season with salt and pepper, and garnish with freshly chopped parsley before serving.

Cataplana de Marisco (Seafood Cataplana)

Ingredients:

  • Mixed seafood (such as shrimp, squid, and fish)
  • Chorizo
  • Onion
  • Bell pepper
  • Tomato
  • Garlic
  • Bay leaves
  • White wine
  • Paprika
  • Olive oil
  • Cilantro

Instructions:

  1. Heat olive oil in a cataplana or large pot over medium heat. Add sliced chorizo and cook until it releases its oils.
  2. Add chopped onion, bell pepper, and minced garlic to the pot, and sauté until they soften.
  3. Stir in diced tomatoes, bay leaves, and a pinch of paprika, and cook for a few minutes.
  4. Add the mixed seafood to the pot and gently combine with the other ingredients.
  5. Pour in the white wine, cover the cataplana, and let it simmer for about 10-15 minutes, or until the seafood is cooked through.
  6. Garnish with fresh cilantro before serving.

Conclusion

These Portuguese seafood recipes are not only delicious but also perfect for the summer months. Whether you’re hosting a backyard barbecue or enjoying a cozy dinner at home, these dishes will impress your guests and satisfy your cravings for fresh and flavorful seafood. Embrace the vibrant flavors of Portugal this summer with these delightful seafood recipes!
