Wholesome Nut-Free Greek Salad Recipe


Are you looking for a refreshing and nutritious salad that’s perfect for any occasion? Look no further than this Wholesome Nut-Free Greek Salad! Packed with vibrant colors, crisp textures, and a burst of Mediterranean flavors, this salad is a delightful addition to any meal. Whether you’re hosting a summer barbecue, planning a picnic, or simply craving a light and satisfying dish, this recipe is sure to become a favorite in your kitchen.

Ingredients

  • 4 large ripe tomatoes, diced
  • 1 English cucumber, diced
  • 1 red onion, thinly sliced
  • 1 green bell pepper, chopped
  • 1/2 cup Kalamata olives, pitted and halved
  • 200g feta cheese, crumbled
  • 1/4 cup extra virgin olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons red wine vinegar
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley for garnish

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Vegetables: In a large mixing bowl, combine the diced tomatoes, cucumber, red onion, and green bell pepper. Toss gently to mix the ingredients evenly.

  2. Add the Olives and Feta: Gently fold in the Kalamata olives and crumbled feta cheese, ensuring that they are distributed throughout the salad.

  3. Dress the Salad: In a small bowl, whisk together the extra virgin olive oil, red wine vinegar, dried oregano, salt, and pepper. Drizzle the dressing over the salad and toss gently to coat the ingredients.

  4. Chill and Serve: Cover the salad and let it chill in the refrigerator for at least 30 minutes to allow the flavors to meld together. Before serving, garnish the salad with fresh parsley for a pop of color and freshness.

The Flavors of Greece

This Wholesome Nut-Free Greek Salad is a celebration of the vibrant and robust flavors of Greek cuisine. The combination of fresh tomatoes, crisp cucumbers, tangy feta cheese, and briny Kalamata olives creates a harmonious medley of tastes and textures. The red wine vinegar and extra virgin olive oil dressing infuse the salad with a zesty and aromatic essence, elevating it to a truly delightful culinary experience.

Nutritious and Versatile

Not only is this Greek salad a feast for the senses, but it also boasts a myriad of health benefits. Tomatoes are rich in antioxidants, cucumbers provide hydration and a refreshing crunch, and olive oil offers heart-healthy fats. The protein from the feta cheese adds a satisfying element to the salad, making it a well-rounded and nutritious dish. Additionally, this recipe is completely nut-free, making it suitable for individuals with nut allergies or dietary restrictions.

Perfect for Any Occasion

Whether you’re preparing a quick and easy lunch, a side dish for a dinner party, or a contribution to a potluck, this Wholesome Nut-Free Greek Salad is a versatile and crowd-pleasing option. Its colorful presentation and vibrant flavors make it an eye-catching addition to any spread, and its simplicity ensures that it can be enjoyed year-round.

In Conclusion

With its fresh ingredients, bold flavors, and healthful qualities, this Wholesome Nut-Free Greek Salad is a true culinary gem. As you savor each bite of this wholesome and satisfying dish, you’ll be transported to the sun-drenched coasts of Greece, where the essence of Mediterranean cuisine comes to life. Embrace the joy of cooking and sharing this delightful salad with your loved ones, and let its timeless appeal bring warmth and flavor to your table. Enjoy!
