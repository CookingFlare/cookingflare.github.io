Mushroom and Spinach Stuffed Shells: A Vegetarian Dream


If you’re in the mood for a comforting and satisfying vegetarian meal, look no further than these delectable Mushroom and Spinach Stuffed Shells. This dish is a delightful combination of earthy mushrooms, tender spinach, and creamy cheese, all enveloped in perfectly cooked pasta shells and baked to perfection. Whether you’re a vegetarian or simply seeking a meatless meal option, these stuffed shells are sure to become a favorite in your recipe collection.

Ingredients

  • 1 box of jumbo pasta shells
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 8 ounces of cremini mushrooms, finely chopped
  • 6 cups fresh spinach
  • 15 ounces ricotta cheese
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 24 ounces of marinara sauce
  • Fresh basil for garnish

Instructions

Step 1: Prepare the Pasta Shells

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. Cook the jumbo pasta shells according to the package instructions until they are al dente. Drain and set aside.

Step 2: Prepare the Filling

  1. In a large skillet, heat the olive oil over medium heat.
  2. Add the finely chopped onion and sauté until it becomes translucent.
  3. Add the minced garlic and cook for another minute.
  4. Stir in the finely chopped cremini mushrooms and cook until they release their moisture and become golden brown.
  5. Add the fresh spinach to the skillet and cook until it wilts.
  6. Remove the skillet from the heat and let the mixture cool slightly.

Step 3: Prepare the Cheese Mixture

  1. In a large bowl, combine the ricotta cheese, shredded mozzarella cheese, grated Parmesan cheese, dried oregano, dried basil, salt, and pepper.
  2. Add the slightly cooled mushroom and spinach mixture to the cheese mixture and stir until well combined.

Step 4: Assemble the Stuffed Shells

  1. Spread a thin layer of marinara sauce on the bottom of a 9x13 inch baking dish.
  2. Using a spoon, carefully fill each cooked pasta shell with the mushroom, spinach, and cheese mixture.
  3. Place the filled shells in the baking dish, nestling them closely together.
  4. Pour the remaining marinara sauce over the top of the stuffed shells.
  5. Cover the baking dish with aluminum foil.

Step 5: Bake and Serve

  1. Bake the stuffed shells in the preheated oven for 25 minutes.
  2. Remove the foil and bake for an additional 10 minutes, or until the sauce is bubbly and the cheese is golden and melted.
  3. Garnish with fresh basil before serving.

Enjoy Your Vegetarian Delight

These Mushroom and Spinach Stuffed Shells are a vegetarian dream come true. With their rich, savory flavors and satisfying texture, they are perfect for a cozy dinner at home or for impressing guests at a gathering. The combination of earthy mushrooms, vibrant spinach, and creamy cheese is a true delight for the senses. Serve these stuffed shells alongside a crisp green salad and a glass of your favorite wine for a complete and memorable meal.

So, the next time you’re craving a comforting vegetarian dish, give these Mushroom and Spinach Stuffed Shells a try. They’re sure to become a beloved addition to your meatless meal repertoire. Happy cooking!
