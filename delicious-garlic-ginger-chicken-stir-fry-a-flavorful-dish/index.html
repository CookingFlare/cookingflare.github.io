Delicious Garlic Ginger Chicken Stir Fry: A Flavorful Dish


Are you a fan of quick and easy weeknight dinners that are bursting with flavor? Look no further than this delicious Garlic Ginger Chicken Stir Fry recipe. Packed with aromatic garlic, zingy ginger, and tender chicken, this dish is a surefire way to satisfy your taste buds. Best of all, it can be ready in under 30 minutes, making it the perfect option for those busy evenings when you need a satisfying meal on the table in a flash.

Ingredients

To create this mouthwatering Garlic Ginger Chicken Stir Fry, you’ll need the following ingredients:

  • 500 grams of boneless, skinless chicken breasts, cut into thin strips
  • 3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon of fresh ginger, grated
  • 2 tablespoons of soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon of oyster sauce
  • 1 tablespoon of hoisin sauce
  • 1 tablespoon of cornstarch
  • 2 tablespoons of vegetable oil
  • 1 red bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 yellow bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 medium onion, sliced
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Steamed rice, for serving

Instructions

  1. In a small bowl, combine the soy sauce, oyster sauce, hoisin sauce, and cornstarch. Stir well to ensure the cornstarch is fully dissolved. Set aside.

  2. Heat one tablespoon of vegetable oil in a large skillet or wok over medium-high heat. Add the chicken strips and cook until they are browned and cooked through, about 5-7 minutes. Remove the chicken from the skillet and set it aside.

  3. In the same skillet, add the remaining tablespoon of vegetable oil. Add the minced garlic and grated ginger, and sauté for about 1 minute until fragrant.

  4. Add the sliced bell peppers and onion to the skillet and cook for another 3-4 minutes, or until the vegetables are slightly softened but still crisp.

  5. Return the cooked chicken to the skillet and pour the sauce mixture over the chicken and vegetables. Stir well to coat everything evenly. Cook for an additional 2-3 minutes, or until the sauce has thickened slightly.

  6. Season with salt and pepper to taste, and give the stir fry a final toss to make sure all the flavors are well combined.

  7. Serve the Garlic Ginger Chicken Stir Fry over steamed rice and garnish with chopped green onions or sesame seeds for an extra touch of flavor and presentation.

Tips and Variations

  • If you prefer a spicier stir fry, you can add a pinch of red pepper flakes or a drizzle of Sriracha sauce to the dish.
  • Feel free to customize the vegetables in this stir fry based on your preferences. Broccoli florets, snap peas, or mushrooms would all be delicious additions.
  • For a vegetarian version, you can substitute tofu for the chicken and use vegetarian oyster sauce and hoisin sauce.

Final Thoughts

This Garlic Ginger Chicken Stir Fry is a versatile and satisfying dish that is perfect for busy weeknight dinners. The combination of garlic and ginger adds a delightful aroma and depth of flavor to the tender chicken and crisp vegetables. With its quick preparation time and simple ingredients, this recipe is a go-to option when you’re in need of a delicious meal that can be made in a flash. So why wait? Whip up this flavor-packed stir fry tonight and treat yourself to a memorable dining experience. Enjoy!
