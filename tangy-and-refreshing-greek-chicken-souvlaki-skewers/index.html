Tangy and Refreshing: Greek Chicken Souvlaki Skewers


Looking for a delicious and tangy dish that will transport your taste buds to the sunny shores of Greece? Look no further than Greek Chicken Souvlaki Skewers! This classic Greek dish is bursting with flavors that will leave you craving for more. With its succulent chicken, zesty marinade, and refreshing tzatziki sauce, these skewers are perfect for a summer barbecue or a weeknight dinner. So, let’s dive in and learn how to make this mouthwatering dish!

Ingredients

  • 1.5 pounds of boneless, skinless chicken breasts, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 3 tablespoons lemon juice
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • Wooden skewers, soaked in water for at least 30 minutes

Instructions

  1. In a large mixing bowl, combine the olive oil, lemon juice, minced garlic, dried oregano, dried thyme, salt, and black pepper. Whisk the ingredients together until well combined.

  2. Add the bite-sized chicken pieces to the marinade and toss until every piece is coated. Cover the bowl with plastic wrap and refrigerate for at least 1 hour, allowing the flavors to meld together.

  3. While the chicken is marinating, prepare the tzatziki sauce. You can either make your own tzatziki sauce by combining Greek yogurt, cucumber, garlic, lemon juice, and dill, or you can purchase a pre-made one from your local grocery store. Set the sauce aside until ready to serve.

  4. After the marinating time has elapsed, preheat your grill or grill pan to medium-high heat.

  5. Thread the marinated chicken onto the soaked wooden skewers, making sure to leave a little space between each piece for even cooking.

  6. Place the skewers on the preheated grill and cook for about 4-5 minutes on each side, or until the chicken is cooked through and nicely charred.

  7. Once the chicken is cooked, remove the skewers from the grill and let them rest for a few minutes.

  8. Serve the Greek Chicken Souvlaki Skewers with a side of tzatziki sauce and enjoy! You can also serve them with warm pita bread, a Greek salad, or grilled vegetables for a complete and satisfying meal.

Tips and Variations

  • If you prefer, you can use chicken thighs instead of chicken breasts for a juicier and more flavorful result.
  • Feel free to add some vegetables to the skewers, such as bell peppers, onions, or cherry tomatoes, to amp up the flavors and add some color to your dish.
  • If you don’t have a grill or grill pan, you can also cook the skewers in the oven at 400°F (200°C) for about 15-20 minutes, or until the chicken is cooked through.

Now that you have the recipe for these tantalizing Greek Chicken Souvlaki Skewers, it’s time to gather your ingredients and give it a try! Whether you’re hosting a backyard barbecue or simply craving a taste of Greece, this dish is sure to impress. The tangy marinade, the tender chicken, and the refreshing tzatziki sauce will transport you to the sunny Mediterranean with every bite. Enjoy!
