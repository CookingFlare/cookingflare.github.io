Tasty and Healthy Chicken Wrap Ideas for a Light Lunch


When it comes to light and satisfying lunches, chicken wraps are a fantastic option. They are versatile, easy to prepare, and can be customized to suit various tastes. Whether you’re looking for a quick meal on the go or a flavorful lunch at home, these tasty and healthy chicken wrap ideas are sure to hit the spot.

Grilled Chicken and Avocado Wrap

Prep Time: 15 minutes
Cook Time: 10 minutes
Total Time: 25 minutes
Servings: 2

Ingredients:

  • 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1 ripe avocado, sliced
  • 1/2 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1/4 cup red onion, thinly sliced
  • 2 large whole wheat tortillas
  • 1/4 cup Greek yogurt
  • 2 tablespoons fresh cilantro, chopped
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Season the chicken breasts with salt and pepper, then grill them until fully cooked.
  2. Once the chicken is ready, thinly slice it.
  3. Lay out the tortillas and spread Greek yogurt evenly over each one.
  4. Place the sliced avocado, cherry tomatoes, and red onion down the center of each tortilla.
  5. Add the grilled chicken slices on top of the vegetables.
  6. Sprinkle fresh cilantro over the chicken.
  7. Roll up the tortillas tightly, tucking in the sides as you go.
  8. Slice the wraps in half and serve immediately.

Buffalo Chicken Lettuce Wraps

Prep Time: 20 minutes
Cook Time: 10 minutes
Total Time: 30 minutes
Servings: 4

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound boneless, skinless chicken breasts, cut into small pieces
  • 1/2 cup hot sauce
  • 2 tablespoons unsalted butter
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon onion powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon paprika
  • 1 head of iceberg lettuce, leaves separated
  • 1/2 cup shredded carrots
  • 1/4 cup blue cheese crumbles (optional)
  • Ranch or blue cheese dressing for dipping

Instructions:

  1. In a skillet, cook the chicken pieces until no longer pink.
  2. In a small saucepan, melt the butter and stir in the hot sauce, garlic powder, onion powder, and paprika.
  3. Pour the hot sauce mixture over the cooked chicken and stir to coat evenly.
  4. Spoon the buffalo chicken onto the lettuce leaves.
  5. Top with shredded carrots and blue cheese crumbles, if desired.
  6. Roll up the lettuce leaves and secure them with toothpicks.
  7. Serve with ranch or blue cheese dressing for dipping.

Greek Chicken Wrap with Tzatziki Sauce

Prep Time: 20 minutes
Cook Time: 10 minutes
Total Time: 30 minutes
Servings: 2

Ingredients:

  • 2 boneless, skinless chicken thighs
  • 1/2 cup plain Greek yogurt
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon fresh lemon juice
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried dill
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 large whole wheat wraps
  • 1 cup shredded lettuce
  • 1/2 cup diced cucumber
  • 1/4 cup sliced red onion
  • 1/4 cup crumbled feta cheese

Instructions:

  1. Season the chicken thighs with salt and pepper, then grill or pan-fry until fully cooked.
  2. In a small bowl, combine the Greek yogurt, minced garlic, lemon juice, and dried dill to make the tzatziki sauce.
  3. Lay out the wraps and spread the tzatziki sauce over each one.
  4. Place the shredded lettuce, diced cucumber, and sliced red onion down the center of each wrap.
  5. Add the grilled chicken thighs on top of the vegetables.
  6. Sprinkle crumbled feta cheese over the chicken.
  7. Roll up the wraps, tucking in the sides as you go.
  8. Slice the wraps in half and serve with extra tzatziki sauce for dipping.

These three chicken wrap recipes are not only delicious and satisfying but also packed with nutrients. Whether you prefer a classic grilled chicken wrap, a spicy buffalo chicken option, or a refreshing Greek-inspired wrap, there’s something for everyone. Feel free to customize these recipes with your favorite vegetables, sauces, or additional toppings to make them your own. Enjoy a light and flavorful lunch with these tasty and healthy chicken wrap ideas!
