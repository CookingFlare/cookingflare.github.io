Cayenne Chocolate Cupcakes: A Spicy Halloween Dessert Recipe


Looking to add a spicy twist to your Halloween dessert creations? These Cayenne Chocolate Cupcakes will be the perfect treat for your ghoulish gatherings served up with a dollop of whipped cream or a drizzle of blood-colored icing! Prepare to tempt your taste buds and satisfy your sweet tooth with the rich combination of dark chocolate and a hint of spicy cayenne pepper. Let imagination and exquisite flavors reign supreme!

Ingredients

For the Cupcakes:

  • 1 1/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1/3 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 3/4 cup whole milk
  • 1/3 cup vegetable oil
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1/2 cup brewed coffee, cooled
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 1/8-1/4 teaspoon cayenne pepper (adjust according to your spice preference)

For the Cayenne Chocolate Ganache:

  • 1 cup dark chocolate chips
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons cayenne pepper
  • 1 tablespoon unsweetened cocoa powder

Instructions

Step 1: Preheat your oven and prepare cupcake pan

Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C) and line your cupcake pan with paper liners for easy removal once the cupcakes are baked.

Step 2: Combine dry ingredients

In a large mixing bowl, whisk together the all-purpose flour, sugar, unsweetened cocoa powder, baking powder, baking soda, and salt until well-incorporated.

Step 3: Mix wet ingredients and spices

In a separate medium-sized mixing bowl, whisk together the milk, vegetable oil, eggs, cooled coffee, and vanilla extract. Stir in the desired amount of cayenne pepper for that added kick of heat. Aim for a 1/8 teaspoon if looking for a subtle spicy note or work up to 1/4 teaspoon for a more intense heat experience.

Step 4: Combine wet and dry ingredients

Gradually pour the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients and mix gently using a spatula or a whisk. Be sure not to overmix; some small lumps in the batter are acceptable.

Step 5: Distribute the batter

Evenly distribute the cupcake batter amongst your prepared liners, filling each 2/3 of the way up.

Step 6: Bake

Transfer your pan into the preheated oven and bake for 18-20 minutes or until a toothpick inserted in the center of a cupcake comes out clean. Once fully baked, allow the cupcakes to cool in the pan for five minutes before transferring them to a wire rack to cool completely.

Step 7: Prepare the Cayenne Chocolate Ganache

During the cooling period, prepare the Cayenne Chocolate Ganache by placing the chocolate chips in a heatproof bowl.

Step 8: Heat the cream

Heat the heavy cream in a small saucepan over medium heat until it just begins to simmer. Be careful not to let it boil.

Step 9: Combine ganache

Once the cream has heated, pour it over the chocolate chips and allow it to sit for a minute before slowly stirring the mixture until smooth and glossy. Add the cayenne pepper and unsweetened cocoa powder to the ganache, whisking until well-incorporated.

Step 10: Decorate the cupcakes

Once the cupcakes have cooled, use a spoon or a piping bag with a small round tip to drizzle the Cayenne Chocolate Ganache over the top of each cupcake for a devilishly decadent finish.

Enjoy these fiery Cayenne Chocolate Cupcakes as you embark on a taste adventure during your Halloween celebrations! Remember to keep a glass of cold milk handy to ease the fiery sensation caused by the cayenne pepper. Enjoy the wickedly sweet and spicy journey!
