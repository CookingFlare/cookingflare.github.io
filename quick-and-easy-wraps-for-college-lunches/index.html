Quick and Easy Wraps for College Lunches


Are you tired of the same old boring sandwiches for lunch? It’s time to spice up your midday meal with some quick and easy wraps that are perfect for college students on the go. Wraps are not only delicious and versatile, but they are also a great way to pack in all the nutrients you need to power through those long study sessions. Whether you’re a fan of classic flavors or looking to experiment with new ingredients, these wrap recipes will surely become your go-to lunch options.

Classic Turkey and Cheese Wrap

Ingredients:

  • 1 large tortilla
  • 3 slices of turkey breast
  • 2 slices of cheddar cheese
  • 1 tablespoon of mayonnaise
  • 1/2 cup of shredded lettuce
  • 1/4 cup of diced tomatoes

Instructions:

  1. Lay the tortilla flat on a clean surface.
  2. Spread the mayonnaise evenly over the tortilla.
  3. Layer the turkey slices and cheddar cheese on top of the mayonnaise.
  4. Sprinkle the shredded lettuce and diced tomatoes over the turkey and cheese.
  5. Roll the tortilla tightly, tucking in the sides as you go.
  6. Cut the wrap in half diagonally and secure each half with toothpicks.

Veggie Hummus Wrap

Ingredients:

  • 1 large spinach tortilla
  • 1/4 cup of hummus
  • 1/2 cup of mixed greens
  • 1/4 cup of sliced cucumbers
  • 1/4 cup of shredded carrots
  • 1/4 cup of sliced bell peppers

Instructions:

  1. Spread the hummus evenly over the spinach tortilla.
  2. Layer the mixed greens, sliced cucumbers, shredded carrots, and sliced bell peppers on top of the hummus.
  3. Roll the tortilla tightly, ensuring all the filling is secure inside.
  4. Cut the wrap into bite-sized pinwheels for easy snacking.

BBQ Chicken Wrap

Ingredients:

  • 1 large whole wheat tortilla
  • 1/2 cup of shredded rotisserie chicken
  • 2 tablespoons of BBQ sauce
  • 1/4 cup of diced red onions
  • 1/4 cup of shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1/4 cup of chopped cilantro

Instructions:

  1. Drizzle the BBQ sauce over the whole wheat tortilla and spread it evenly.
  2. Layer the shredded rotisserie chicken, diced red onions, shredded cheddar cheese, and chopped cilantro on top of the BBQ sauce.
  3. Roll the tortilla tightly, ensuring the filling is well-secured.
  4. Slice the wrap into rounds and serve with extra BBQ sauce for dipping.

Greek Salad Wrap

Ingredients:

  • 1 large whole grain tortilla
  • 1/4 cup of tzatziki sauce
  • 1/2 cup of chopped romaine lettuce
  • 1/4 cup of diced tomatoes
  • 1/4 cup of sliced cucumbers
  • 1/4 cup of crumbled feta cheese
  • 1/4 cup of pitted Kalamata olives

Instructions:

  1. Spread the tzatziki sauce evenly over the whole grain tortilla.
  2. Layer the chopped romaine lettuce, diced tomatoes, sliced cucumbers, crumbled feta cheese, and pitted Kalamata olives on top of the tzatziki sauce.
  3. Roll the tortilla tightly, making sure the filling is well-distributed.
  4. Cut the wrap into halves and enjoy the Mediterranean flavors.

Conclusion

With these quick and easy wrap recipes, you can say goodbye to boring college lunches and hello to exciting, flavorful meals that will keep you fueled throughout the day. Feel free to get creative with the ingredients and tailor the wraps to your personal taste. Whether you’re in the mood for a classic turkey and cheese wrap or want to explore the vibrant flavors of a Greek salad wrap, these recipes are sure to become staples in your college lunch rotation. So, grab your tortillas and get wrapping!
