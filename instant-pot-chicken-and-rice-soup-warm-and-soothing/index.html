Instant Pot Chicken and Rice Soup: Warm and Soothing


There’s nothing quite like a warm and comforting bowl of chicken and rice soup on a chilly day. And with the help of your trusty Instant Pot, you can have this delicious and nourishing meal ready in no time. This recipe is perfect for those busy weeknights when you need something quick, easy, and satisfying. So grab your Instant Pot and let’s get cooking!

Ingredients

  • 1 pound boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1 cup diced carrots
  • 1 cup diced celery
  • 1 medium onion, diced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • 1 teaspoon dried rosemary
  • 1 teaspoon dried parsley
  • 6 cups chicken broth
  • 1 cup white rice
  • Salt and pepper, to taste

Instructions

  1. Start by preparing your ingredients. Dice the carrots, celery, and onion, and mince the garlic. Set aside.

  2. Season the chicken breasts with salt, pepper, thyme, rosemary, and parsley. This will add a wonderful depth of flavor to the soup.

  3. Preheat your Instant Pot by selecting the “Saute” function. Add a drizzle of olive oil to the pot, then add the seasoned chicken breasts. Cook for 2-3 minutes on each side, or until browned. This step helps seal in the juices and enhances the overall taste of the soup.

  4. Once the chicken is browned, remove it from the pot and set it aside. Add the diced carrots, celery, onion, and minced garlic to the Instant Pot. Saute for 3-4 minutes, or until the vegetables have softened slightly.

  5. Pour in the chicken broth, scraping the bottom of the pot to release any browned bits. These flavorful bits will infuse the soup with a rich and savory taste.

  6. Add the white rice to the pot, stirring well to combine. Return the browned chicken breasts to the pot, nestling them into the broth and vegetables.

  7. Secure the lid on the Instant Pot and set the valve to the “Sealing” position. Select the “Soup” function and adjust the cooking time to 10 minutes.

  8. Once the cooking time is complete, allow the pressure to naturally release for 10 minutes. Then, carefully switch the valve to “Venting” to release any remaining pressure.

  9. Remove the chicken breasts from the pot and shred them using two forks. Return the shredded chicken to the Instant Pot and give everything a good stir.

  10. Taste the soup and season with additional salt and pepper, if needed. The flavors will have melded together beautifully, creating a delicious and nourishing meal.

  11. Ladle the soup into bowls and serve hot. Garnish with fresh parsley, if desired.

Tips and Variations

  • Feel free to customize this recipe to your liking. You can add additional vegetables such as peas or corn, or even throw in some diced potatoes for extra heartiness.

  • If you prefer a creamier soup, you can stir in a splash of heavy cream or coconut milk at the end.

  • This soup makes for great leftovers. Simply store any leftovers in an airtight container in the refrigerator for up to 3 days.

  • If you don’t have an Instant Pot, you can easily adapt this recipe for the stovetop or slow cooker. Simply follow the same steps, adjusting the cooking time accordingly.

  • This Instant Pot Chicken and Rice Soup is not only delicious, but it’s also packed with nutrients. It’s a great way to get your protein, vegetables, and grains all in one bowl.

So the next time you’re craving a warm and soothing meal, give this Instant Pot Chicken and Rice Soup a try. It’s sure to become a family favorite!
