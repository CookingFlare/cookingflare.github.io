Classic Coffee Cake: A Perfect Pairing with Your Morning Brew


Nothing beats the comforting aroma of freshly brewed coffee in the morning. And what better way to complement your favorite cup of joe than with a slice of classic coffee cake? This timeless treat is the perfect balance of moist cake, cinnamon streusel, and a touch of sweetness. So, grab your apron and let’s dive into this delectable recipe!

Ingredients

For the cake:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1/2 cup sour cream
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt

For the streusel:

  • 1/2 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup packed brown sugar
  • 1/4 cup unsalted butter, melted
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease and flour a 9-inch round cake pan.

  2. In a mixing bowl, combine the flour, granulated sugar, baking powder, and salt. Cut in the softened butter using a pastry cutter or your fingertips until the mixture resembles coarse crumbs.

  3. In a separate bowl, whisk together the sour cream, milk, eggs, and vanilla extract until well combined.

  4. Gradually add the wet ingredients to the dry ingredients, mixing until just incorporated. Be careful not to overmix, as this can result in a dense cake.

  5. Pour half of the batter into the prepared cake pan, spreading it evenly.

  6. In a small bowl, mix together the streusel ingredients: flour, brown sugar, melted butter, and cinnamon. Sprinkle half of the streusel mixture over the batter in the pan.

  7. Pour the remaining batter over the streusel, spreading it evenly. Top with the remaining streusel, gently pressing it into the batter.

  8. Bake in the preheated oven for 40-45 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean. The streusel should be golden brown and slightly crispy.

  9. Remove the cake from the oven and let it cool in the pan for about 10 minutes. Then, transfer it to a wire rack to cool completely.

  10. Once completely cooled, slice and serve your classic coffee cake alongside a steaming cup of freshly brewed coffee. Enjoy the delightful combination of flavors and textures!

Tips and Variations

  • For an extra touch of indulgence, drizzle a simple glaze made with powdered sugar and milk over the cooled cake.
  • Enhance the cinnamon flavor by adding a teaspoon of ground cinnamon to the cake batter.
  • This recipe can be easily doubled if you’re serving a larger crowd or want leftovers for the next day.
  • Experiment with different toppings, such as chopped nuts or a sprinkle of cocoa powder.

Whether you’re starting your day or enjoying a well-deserved break, classic coffee cake is a delightful treat that pairs perfectly with your morning brew. With its tender crumb and irresistible streusel topping, this recipe is sure to become a favorite among coffee and cake lovers alike. So, go ahead and savor every bite of this timeless indulgence. Cheers to good mornings and delicious coffee cake!
