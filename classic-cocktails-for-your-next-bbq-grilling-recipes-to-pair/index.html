Classic Cocktails for Your Next BBQ: Grilling Recipes to Pair


When it comes to hosting a BBQ, delicious food and refreshing cocktails go hand in hand. While the grilled dishes take the spotlight, the right cocktail can elevate the entire outdoor dining experience. Classic cocktails bring a timeless charm to any BBQ gathering, and when paired with the right grilling recipes, they create a culinary symphony that tantalizes the taste buds. In this blog post, we’ll explore some classic cocktail recipes that perfectly complement popular BBQ dishes, adding a touch of sophistication and flavor to your outdoor feast.

Margarita & Grilled Shrimp Skewers

The zesty and tangy flavors of a classic Margarita perfectly complement the smoky, grilled goodness of shrimp skewers. To prepare the Margarita, combine fresh lime juice, tequila, and orange liqueur in a shaker with ice. Shake well and strain into a salt-rimmed glass. Garnish with a lime wedge, and your refreshing Margarita is ready to be enjoyed alongside succulent grilled shrimp skewers. The citrus notes of the cocktail enhance the natural sweetness of the shrimp, creating a delightful harmony of flavors.

Old Fashioned & BBQ Ribs

The timeless Old Fashioned cocktail, with its blend of bourbon, sugar, and bitters, is the ideal companion for a hearty dish of BBQ ribs. The rich, smoky flavors of the bourbon in the cocktail echo the savory essence of the grilled ribs, creating a match made in BBQ heaven. The sweetness of the cocktail balances the bold flavors of the ribs, making each bite a delight for the senses. Whether it’s baby back ribs or spare ribs, the Old Fashioned adds a touch of sophistication to this classic BBQ favorite.

Mojito & Grilled Chicken

For a lighter option, the refreshing Mojito is the perfect choice to pair with grilled chicken. Muddle fresh mint leaves and lime wedges with sugar, add white rum and soda water, and you have a delightful Mojito ready to be sipped alongside tender, smoky grilled chicken. The crispness of the cocktail’s mint and lime flavors complements the juiciness of the chicken, creating a refreshing and satisfying combination that is sure to please your guests.

Moscow Mule & Grilled Veggies

When it comes to pairing cocktails with grilled veggies, the Moscow Mule shines as a versatile and vibrant option. The zingy combination of vodka, ginger beer, and lime juice in a Moscow Mule adds a delightful kick to the charred, caramelized flavors of grilled vegetables. Whether it’s bell peppers, zucchini, or mushrooms, the cocktail’s effervescence and tangy notes bring out the natural sweetness and smokiness of the veggies, making for a delightful culinary experience.

Sangria & Grilled Fruit Skewers

To round off the BBQ feast, a pitcher of Sangria paired with grilled fruit skewers is the ultimate indulgence. The fruity and refreshing notes of Sangria, with red wine, brandy, and a medley of fresh fruits, harmonize beautifully with the caramelized sweetness of grilled fruit skewers. As the sun sets and the BBQ winds down, this pairing offers a delightful and elegant finale to the outdoor dining experience.

These classic cocktails and their perfect grilling recipe pairings are guaranteed to elevate your next BBQ gathering, impressing your guests with a symphony of flavors and a touch of timeless elegance. So fire up the grill, mix up these iconic cocktails, and get ready to savor the magic of classic pairings at your next BBQ extravaganza.
