A Delicious Italian-Style Eggplant Rollatini Recipe


Are you looking for a delicious and impressive dish to serve at your next dinner party? Look no further than this mouthwatering Italian-style Eggplant Rollatini recipe. This dish is a wonderful combination of flavors and textures, making it a perfect choice for a special occasion or a cozy family dinner. The best part? It’s surprisingly easy to make, and your guests will be impressed by your culinary skills. Let’s dive into the recipe and discover the secrets to making this delectable Eggplant Rollatini.

Ingredients

For the Eggplant:

  • 2 large eggplants, sliced lengthwise into 1/4-inch thick slices
  • Salt
  • Olive oil

For the Filling:

  • 1 1/2 cups ricotta cheese
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh basil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

For the Tomato Sauce:

  • 2 cups crushed tomatoes
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh basil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

For the Topping:

  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Fresh basil leaves for garnish

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Eggplant:

    • Lay the eggplant slices on a paper towel-lined baking sheet and sprinkle both sides with salt. Let them sit for about 30 minutes to draw out the moisture. Pat the eggplant slices dry with paper towels, then brush both sides with olive oil. Grill or broil the eggplant slices until they are tender and slightly charred, about 3-4 minutes per side. Set aside to cool.

  2. Make the Filling:

    • In a bowl, combine the ricotta cheese, Parmesan cheese, egg, chopped basil, salt, and pepper. Mix until well combined.

  3. Prepare the Tomato Sauce:

    • In a saucepan, heat a little olive oil over medium heat. Add the minced garlic and cook until fragrant. Pour in the crushed tomatoes and chopped basil. Season with salt and pepper. Simmer the sauce for about 10-15 minutes, then remove from heat.

  4. Assemble the Rollatini:

    • Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C). Spread a thin layer of the tomato sauce on the bottom of a baking dish. Take a grilled eggplant slice and place a spoonful of the ricotta filling at one end. Roll up the eggplant slice and place it seam-side down in the baking dish. Repeat with the remaining eggplant slices and filling.

  5. Bake the Rollatini:

    • Spoon the remaining tomato sauce over the rolled eggplant slices. Sprinkle the shredded mozzarella cheese over the top. Cover the baking dish with foil and bake for 20 minutes. Remove the foil and bake for an additional 10 minutes, or until the cheese is bubbly and golden.

  6. Serve and Enjoy:

    • Garnish the Eggplant Rollatini with fresh basil leaves and serve hot. Your guests will be impressed by the beautiful presentation and the incredible flavors of this dish.

This Italian-style Eggplant Rollatini is a fantastic way to enjoy the delicious flavors of Italy in a unique and satisfying dish. Whether you’re a fan of eggplant or simply looking for a new recipe to try, this dish is sure to become a favorite in your culinary repertoire. The creamy ricotta filling, savory tomato sauce, and tender grilled eggplant come together to create a harmonious blend of flavors that will delight your taste buds. Serve it as a main course with a side of crusty bread and a fresh green salad, and you have yourself a memorable and satisfying meal.

So, the next time you’re in the mood for a taste of Italy, give this Eggplant Rollatini recipe a try. Your family and friends will thank you for introducing them to this delightful dish, and you’ll enjoy the satisfaction of creating a restaurant-quality meal in the comfort of your own kitchen. Buon appetito!
