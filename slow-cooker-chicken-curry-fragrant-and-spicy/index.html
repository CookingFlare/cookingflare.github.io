Slow Cooker Chicken Curry: Fragrant and Spicy

4 min · 656 words

If you’re a fan of bold and flavorful dishes, then this Slow Cooker Chicken Curry recipe is perfect for you. With its aromatic spices and tender chicken, it’s a dish that will transport your taste buds to the vibrant streets of India. The best part? It’s incredibly easy to make, thanks to the magic of slow cooking. So, let’s dive in and learn how to create this fragrant and spicy masterpiece.

Ingredients

  • 2 pounds of boneless, skinless chicken thighs, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 1 large onion, finely chopped
  • 4 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 thumb-sized piece of ginger, grated
  • 2 tablespoons of curry powder
  • 1 teaspoon of ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon of ground coriander
  • 1 teaspoon of turmeric powder
  • 1 teaspoon of paprika
  • 1 teaspoon of chili powder (adjust according to your spice preference)
  • 1 can (14 ounces) of coconut milk
  • 1 cup of chicken broth
  • 2 tablespoons of tomato paste
  • 1 tablespoon of brown sugar
  • Juice of 1 lime
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • Fresh cilantro, for garnish

Instructions

  1. In a large bowl, combine the curry powder, cumin, coriander, turmeric, paprika, and chili powder. Mix well to create a fragrant spice blend.

  2. Place the chicken pieces in the slow cooker and season with salt and pepper. Add the chopped onion, minced garlic, and grated ginger. Mix everything together to ensure the chicken is coated with the aromatic mixture.

  3. In a separate bowl, whisk together the coconut milk, chicken broth, tomato paste, brown sugar, and lime juice. Pour the mixture over the chicken in the slow cooker, making sure all the chicken pieces are submerged.

  4. Cover the slow cooker and cook on low heat for 6-8 hours or on high heat for 3-4 hours. Slow cooking allows the flavors to meld together and the chicken to become incredibly tender.

  5. Once the cooking time is complete, give the curry a good stir. At this point, you can taste and adjust the seasoning if needed. If you prefer a thicker sauce, you can remove the lid and cook on high for an additional 30 minutes to reduce the liquid.

  6. Serve the Slow Cooker Chicken Curry over steamed basmati rice or with warm naan bread. Garnish with fresh cilantro for an extra burst of freshness.

Enjoy the fragrant aroma and spicy flavors of this Slow Cooker Chicken Curry. The tender chicken, combined with the rich coconut milk and aromatic spices, makes for a mouthwatering dish that will surely impress your family and friends. And the best part? It requires minimal effort, thanks to the convenience of slow cooking. So go ahead, embrace the flavors of India, and savor every bite of this fragrant and spicy delight.

Remember, cooking is all about experimenting and adding your own personal touch. Feel free to adjust the spices according to your preference, or even add some vegetables like bell peppers or potatoes for added texture. The possibilities are endless, so have fun and enjoy your culinary adventure!
