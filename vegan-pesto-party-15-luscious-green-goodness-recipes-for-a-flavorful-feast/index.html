Vegan Pesto Party: 15 Luscious Green Goodness Recipes for a Flavorful Feast


Are you a fan of fresh, flavorful, and vibrant dishes? If so, it’s time to dive into the world of vegan pesto recipes. These 15 creative and delectable recipes will not only satiate your taste buds but also impress your guests during your next gathering. From savory appetizers to sweet deserts, each dish features the luscious and versatile combination of basil, pine nuts, garlic, and olive oil that transforming everyday meals into something extraordinary.

Let the green party begin!

1. Pesto Chemist Bread Bombs

Fuel your #foodie adventures with these bite-sized pesto-stuffed bread bombs. Drizzled in equally delicious vegan garlic sauce, these “Chemist Bread Bombs” will have you questioning chemistry and culinary arts.

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups bread flour
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 1 tsp Instant Yeast
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • 2 tsp salt
  • 1 packed cup fresh basil leaves
  • 1/3 cup pine nuts, toasted
  • 4-5 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/2 cup olive oil
  • 1/2 cup nutritional yeast

Recipe

2. Vegan Pesto Stuffed Portobello Mushrooms

Transport your tastebuds to a magical, mushroomy realm with these hearty Pesto Stuffed Portobello Mushrooms. Topped with some refreshing cherry tomatoes, these mushrooms are sure to bring out the vegetarian in everyone.

Ingredients:

  • 4-6 large portobello mushrooms
  • 1 cup vegan pesto
  • Salt and pepper
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1/4 cup vegan Parmesan cheese

Recipe

3. Pesto, Mozzarella, and Sun-Dried Tomato Stuffed Zucchini Boats

These Zucchini Boats are stuffed with a delightful blend of pesto, gooey mozzarella, and jammy sun-dried tomatoes. The perfect healthy alternative to heavier dishes without compromising on flavor.

Ingredients:

  • 4 medium-sized zucchinis
  • 1 cup vegan mozzarella cheese, shredded
  • 1/2 cup vegan pesto
  • 1/3 cup sun-dried tomatoes, chopped
  • Salt and pepper

Recipe

4. Vegan Pesto Pasta Salad

A crowd-pleasing pasta salad infused with the vibrant flavors of basil and garlic. This is the perfect dish to enjoy outdoors in the sun during picnics or potlucks.

Ingredients:

  • 16 oz penne pasta
  • 1 1/2 cup vegan pesto
  • 1 cucumber, chopped
  • 1 bell pepper, diced
  • 1/2 red onion, finely chopped

Recipe

5. Grilled Pesto Pizza

Impress your friends at your next BBQ with this easy-to-make Grilled Pesto Pizza. layers of melty vegan cheese, and freshly confiscated basil, this smoky, charred crust is a game-changer.

Ingredients:

  • 1 pizza dough
  • 1/2 cup vegan pesto
  • 1/2 cup vegan mozzarella cheese, shredded
  • Cherry tomatoes, halved
  • Fresh basil leaves

Recipe

6. Vegan Pesto Hummus

Imbue your hummus with the flavors of the Mediterranean by incorporating pesto into the mix. This innovative spin on the traditional dip combines the cruncickpeas why chith aromatic basil, toe-curling garlic, and rich olive oil.

Ingredients:

  • 2 cans chickpeas
  • 2 tbsp tahini
  • 2-3 cloves garlic
  • 2-3 tbsp vegan pesto
  • Olive oil
  • Salt and pepper

Recipe

7. Spiralized Zucchini Pesto Pasta Salad

A heart-healthy twist on an Italian classic, this low-carb, gluten-free Zucchini Pesto Pasta Salad combines the robust flavors of verdant basil pesto and crisp vegetables to create a delicious, zesty salad.

Ingredients:

  • 3 large zucchinis, spiralized
  • 1 red bell pepper, chopped
  • 1/2 cucumber, chopped
  • 1/2 cup vegan pesto
  • 1/4 cup pine nuts, toasted

Recipe

8. Vegan Pesto Egg Wraps

Step aside scrambled eggs, because there’s a new morning routine in town. These Intriguingly colored Vegan Pesto Egg Wraps will have you questioning if they really are plant-based.

Ingredients:

  • 2 tbsp chickpea flour
  • 1/8 tsp turmeric
  • 1/8 tsp cumin
  • 3 tbsp water
  • 1 tbsp vegan pesto
  • Salt and pepper

Recipe

9. Blistered Green Beans with Vegan Pesto

Upgrade your green beans with a dollop of pesto, giving them a flavor boost that brings out their inherent sweetness.

Ingredients:

  • 2 lbs green beans
  • 1/2 cup vegan pesto
  • Salt and pepper

Recipe

10. Vegan Stuffed Pesto Peppers

These colorful, stuffed peppers make a delicious and nutritious meal that’s packed with the goodness of fresh basil pesto. Filled with quinoa and covered in vegan mozzarella, stuffed peppers are guaranteed to bring a sensational symphony of flavors to your table.

Ingredients:

  • 4 large bell peppers
  • 1 cup cooked quinoa
  • 1 cup vegan pesto
  • 1 cup vegan mozzarella cheese, shredded

Recipe

11. Pesto Potato Salad

Boring potlucks be gone! Pesto Potato Salad is here to electrify your potato game with its vibrant color and tantalizing flavors.

Ingredients:

  • 2 lbs potatoes, chopped into bite-sized pieces
  • 1/2 cup vegan pesto
  • 1/2 cup vegan mayonnaise
  • 2 stalks celery, chopped

Recipe

12. Vegan Pesto Pasta Casserole

For a nostalgic and filling meal, whip up this savory Vegan Pesto Pasta Casserole. Nestled between layers of homemade vegan pesto sauce and dotted with vegan cheese, this casserole asit’s reminiscent of lasagna, perfectly hearty and delicious.

Ingredients:

  • 1 box rotini pasta
  • 1/2 cup vegan pesto
  • 1 cup vegan mozzarella cheese, shredded
  • vegan Parmesan cheese, optional

Recipe

13. Vegan Pesto Spaghetti Squash

An incredible alternative to traditional spaghetti, vegan pesto spaghetti squash puts an inventive twist on a classic comfort dish. With the aromatic flavors of garlic and basil, this dish is sure to win hearts and delight the senses.

Ingredients:

  • 1 whole spaghetti squash
  • 1/2 cup vegan pesto
  • 1/4 cup pine nuts, toasted

Recipe

14. Vegan Pesto Veggie Wraps

Flexible, flavorful, and filled with fresh vegetables, these Vegan Pesto Veggie Wraps hit the spot anytime you need a quick pick-me-up snack.

Ingredients:

  • 10 large green lettuce leaves
  • 2 cups zucchini noodles
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1/2 cup bell pepper, sliced
  • 1/4 cup vegan pesto

Recipe

15. Vegan Pesto Grilled Cheese Sandwich

Elevate the classic comfort food of mac and cheese with the flavorsome twist of basil pesto. This Vegan Pesto Grilled Cheese is the ultimate indulgence for foodies looking for plant-based excitement.

Ingredients:

  • 2 slices vegan bread
  • 2 tbsp vegan pesto
  • 1/4 cup vegan cheddar cheese, shredded
  • 1/4 cup vegan mozzarella cheese, shredded

Recipe
