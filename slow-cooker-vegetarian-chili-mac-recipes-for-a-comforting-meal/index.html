Slow Cooker Vegetarian Chili Mac Recipes for a Comforting Meal


Are you in the mood for a hearty and comforting meal that’s also vegetarian-friendly? Look no further than these delicious slow cooker vegetarian chili mac recipes. Packed with flavor, protein, and wholesome ingredients, these recipes are sure to satisfy your cravings while providing a nutritious meal.

Recipe 1: Classic Vegetarian Chili Mac

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup dried macaroni
  • 1 can (15 ounces) kidney beans, rinsed and drained
  • 1 can (15 ounces) black beans, rinsed and drained
  • 1 can (15 ounces) diced tomatoes
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 1 bell pepper, diced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • Optional toppings: shredded cheese, chopped cilantro, sour cream

Instructions:

  1. In a slow cooker, combine the kidney beans, black beans, diced tomatoes, onion, bell pepper, garlic, chili powder, cumin, paprika, salt, and pepper. Stir well to combine.
  2. Cover and cook on low heat for 6-8 hours or on high heat for 3-4 hours.
  3. Cook the macaroni according to the package instructions until al dente. Drain and set aside.
  4. Just before serving, stir the cooked macaroni into the slow cooker. Allow it to heat through for 10-15 minutes.
  5. Serve hot with your choice of optional toppings such as shredded cheese, chopped cilantro, or sour cream.

Recipe 2: Creamy Butternut Squash Chili Mac

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup dried macaroni
  • 2 cups butternut squash, peeled and diced
  • 1 can (15 ounces) white beans, rinsed and drained
  • 1 can (15 ounces) diced tomatoes
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • 1/2 teaspoon smoked paprika
  • 1/2 cup vegetable broth
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • Optional toppings: grated Parmesan cheese, chopped parsley

Instructions:

  1. In a slow cooker, combine the butternut squash, white beans, diced tomatoes, onion, garlic, chili powder, cumin, smoked paprika, vegetable broth, salt, and pepper. Stir well to combine.
  2. Cover and cook on low heat for 6-8 hours or on high heat for 3-4 hours, until the butternut squash is tender.
  3. Using an immersion blender or a countertop blender, puree the mixture until smooth and creamy.
  4. Cook the macaroni according to the package instructions until al dente. Drain and set aside.
  5. Just before serving, stir the cooked macaroni into the slow cooker. Allow it to heat through for 10-15 minutes.
  6. Serve hot with optional toppings such as grated Parmesan cheese and chopped parsley.

Recipe 3: Spicy Lentil Chili Mac

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup dried macaroni
  • 1 cup dried lentils
  • 1 can (15 ounces) crushed tomatoes
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 jalapeno pepper, seeded and diced
  • 1 tablespoon chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • 1/2 teaspoon smoked paprika
  • 3 cups vegetable broth
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • Optional toppings: sliced green onions, hot sauce

Instructions:

  1. In a slow cooker, combine the lentils, crushed tomatoes, onion, garlic, jalapeno pepper, chili powder, cumin, smoked paprika, vegetable broth, salt, and pepper. Stir well to combine.
  2. Cover and cook on low heat for 6-8 hours or on high heat for 3-4 hours, until the lentils are tender.
  3. Cook the macaroni according to the package instructions until al dente. Drain and set aside.
  4. Just before serving, stir the cooked macaroni into the slow cooker. Allow it to heat through for 10-15 minutes.
  5. Serve hot with optional toppings such as sliced green onions and hot sauce.

These slow cooker vegetarian chili mac recipes are a perfect combination of comfort and nutrition. They are easy to prepare and can be customized with your favorite toppings for added flavor. Whether you’re cooking for yourself or feeding a crowd, these recipes are sure to become a household favorite. So grab your slow cooker and get ready to enjoy a comforting and delicious meal!
