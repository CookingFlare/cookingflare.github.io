Aromatic Thai Green Curry with Shrimp: A Fragrant Delight


If you’re a fan of Thai cuisine, you’re in for a treat with this aromatic and flavorful Thai green curry with shrimp. This dish is a fragrant delight that will transport your taste buds to the bustling streets of Bangkok. The combination of fresh herbs, spices, and succulent shrimp creates a symphony of flavors that is sure to impress your family and friends.

Ingredients

To create this mouthwatering Thai green curry, you’ll need the following ingredients:

  • 1 pound of fresh shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 2 cans of coconut milk
  • 3 tablespoons of green curry paste
  • 1 tablespoon of fish sauce
  • 1 tablespoon of palm sugar
  • 1 cup of chicken stock
  • 1 cup of Thai basil leaves
  • 1 cup of sliced bell peppers
  • 1 cup of bamboo shoots
  • 1 cup of sliced eggplant
  • 4-5 kaffir lime leaves
  • Fresh red chili, thinly sliced (optional for extra heat)
  • 2 tablespoons of cooking oil

Instructions

  1. Start by heating the cooking oil in a large pot over medium heat. Once the oil is hot, add the green curry paste and stir-fry it for a minute to release its aromatic flavors.
  2. Pour in one can of coconut milk and stir it with the curry paste until well combined. Allow the mixture to simmer for a few minutes to let the flavors meld together.
  3. Add the shrimp to the pot and cook them until they turn pink and opaque, which should take about 2-3 minutes. Be careful not to overcook the shrimp as they can become tough.
  4. Once the shrimp are cooked, pour in the remaining can of coconut milk and chicken stock. Stir the mixture gently to combine all the ingredients.
  5. Add the fish sauce and palm sugar to the pot, stirring well to ensure they are evenly distributed throughout the curry.
  6. Toss in the sliced bell peppers, bamboo shoots, and eggplant, allowing them to simmer in the curry until they are tender yet still slightly crisp.
  7. Tear the kaffir lime leaves into the pot and add the Thai basil leaves, gently stirring to infuse the curry with their aromatic essence.
  8. If you prefer your curry to have a bit of heat, you can sprinkle in some thinly sliced red chili at this stage.
  9. Once all the ingredients have simmered together, the Thai green curry with shrimp is ready to be served.

Serving

This fragrant Thai green curry with shrimp pairs beautifully with steamed jasmine rice, allowing you to soak up the rich and aromatic sauce. Garnish the curry with a few fresh basil leaves and a slice of lime for an extra burst of flavor. The vibrant colors and enticing aroma of this dish will make it a standout feature at any dinner table.

Conclusion

Aromatic Thai green curry with shrimp is a delightful dish that combines the bold flavors of Thai cuisine with the succulent goodness of shrimp. The fragrant blend of herbs, spices, and coconut milk creates a symphony of flavors that will tantalize your taste buds. Whether you’re a fan of spicy dishes or simply enjoy the exotic flavors of Thai cuisine, this recipe is sure to become a favorite in your culinary repertoire. So, roll up your sleeves, gather your ingredients, and embark on a culinary journey to Thailand with this aromatic green curry delight!
