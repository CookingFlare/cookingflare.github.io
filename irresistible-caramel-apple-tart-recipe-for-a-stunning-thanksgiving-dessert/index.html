Irresistible Caramel Apple Tart Recipe for a Stunning Thanksgiving Dessert


Thanksgiving is a time for family, gratitude, and of course, delicious food. If you’re looking to impress your guests with a show-stopping dessert, look no further than this irresistible caramel apple tart. With a buttery crust, sweet caramel, and tender, spiced apples, this dessert is sure to be the star of your Thanksgiving table. So, roll up your sleeves and let’s get baking!

Ingredients

For the Crust:

  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, cold and cubed
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1 large egg yolk
  • 2-3 tablespoons ice water

For the Filling:

  • 4-5 large apples, peeled, cored, and thinly sliced (use a mix of sweet and tart apples for depth of flavor)
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/4 cup unsalted butter
  • 1/4 cup heavy cream
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground nutmeg
  • Pinch of salt

For the Caramel Drizzle:

  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 3 tablespoons unsalted butter
  • 1/4 cup heavy cream
  • Pinch of salt

Instructions

Step 1: Make the Crust

  1. In a large bowl, combine the flour, sugar, and salt. Add the cold, cubed butter and use a pastry cutter or your hands to work the butter into the flour mixture until it resembles coarse crumbs.
  2. Add the egg yolk and 2 tablespoons of ice water, and mix until the dough comes together. If needed, add an additional tablespoon of ice water.
  3. Shape the dough into a disk, wrap it in plastic wrap, and refrigerate for at least 1 hour.

Step 2: Prepare the Filling

  1. In a large skillet, melt the butter over medium heat. Add the sliced apples, sugar, cinnamon, nutmeg, and a pinch of salt. Cook, stirring occasionally, until the apples are tender but still hold their shape, about 10-15 minutes. Remove from heat and stir in the vanilla extract. Allow the filling to cool slightly.

Step 3: Roll out the Dough

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C). On a lightly floured surface, roll out the chilled dough into a circle about 12 inches in diameter. Carefully transfer the dough to a 9-inch tart pan with a removable bottom, pressing it gently into the corners and trimming any excess dough.

Step 4: Assemble the Tart

  1. Arrange the cooked apple filling in an even layer over the prepared crust.

Step 5: Bake the Tart

  1. Place the tart on a baking sheet and bake for 30-35 minutes, or until the crust is golden brown and the apples are tender.

Step 6: Make the Caramel Drizzle

  1. In a small saucepan, heat the granulated sugar over medium heat, stirring constantly with a rubber spatula. The sugar will form clumps and eventually melt into a thick, amber-colored liquid.
  2. Once the sugar is completely melted, add the butter. Be careful as the mixture will bubble rapidly. Stir the butter into the caramel until it is completely melted, about 2-3 minutes.
  3. Slowly drizzle in the heavy cream while stirring. Allow the mixture to boil for 1 minute. Remove from heat and stir in the salt. Let the caramel cool for a few minutes.

Step 7: Drizzle the Caramel

  1. Drizzle the warm caramel over the baked tart.

Step 8: Serve and Enjoy!

  1. Allow the tart to cool for a few minutes before slicing. Serve warm, and enjoy the delightful combination of buttery crust, spiced apples, and luscious caramel.

Conclusion

There you have it – a stunning caramel apple tart that will leave your guests in awe this Thanksgiving. The buttery crust, tender apples, and decadent caramel come together to create a dessert that’s as delicious as it is beautiful. Whether you’re hosting a large gathering or having an intimate celebration, this irresistible tart is the perfect way to end your Thanksgiving feast. Happy baking, and happy Thanksgiving!
