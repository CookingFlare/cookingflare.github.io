Wrap It Up: 5 Flavorful Mediterranean Wrap Recipes


Wraps are a versatile and delightful way to enjoy a meal, especially when inspired by the vibrant flavors of the Mediterranean. From fresh vegetables to zesty sauces, Mediterranean wraps offer a delicious blend of tastes and textures that can satisfy any palate. Whether you’re looking for a quick lunch, a satisfying dinner, or a healthy snack, these five Mediterranean wrap recipes will elevate your cooking game and bring a taste of the Mediterranean to your kitchen. Let’s dive into these flavorful creations!

1. Classic Greek Chicken Wrap

Ingredients:

  • 2 large whole wheat tortillas
  • 1 cup cooked chicken breast, shredded
  • 1/2 cup Greek yogurt
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon lemon juice
  • 1 cup romaine lettuce, shredded
  • 1/2 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1/4 cup cucumber, diced
  • 1/4 cup red onion, thinly sliced
  • Feta cheese, crumbled (optional)
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the Dressing: In a bowl, mix the Greek yogurt, olive oil, oregano, garlic powder, lemon juice, salt, and pepper until well combined. This will be your zesty dressing.

  2. Assemble the Wrap: Lay the whole wheat tortillas flat on a clean surface. Spread half of the yogurt dressing on each tortilla.

  3. Add the Fillings: Evenly distribute the shredded chicken, romaine lettuce, cherry tomatoes, cucumber, red onion, and feta cheese (if using) over the tortillas.

  4. Wrap It Up: Fold the sides of the tortilla inward and roll it up tightly from the bottom to the top. Slice in half diagonally.

  5. Serve and Enjoy: Serve your classic Greek chicken wrap with extra dressing on the side for dipping. This wrap is perfect for a quick lunch or a light dinner.

2. Mediterranean Falafel Wrap

Ingredients:

  • 2 large pita bread or flatbreads
  • 1 cup falafel balls (store-bought or homemade)
  • 1/2 cup tahini sauce
  • 1 cup mixed greens (spinach, arugula, or kale)
  • 1/2 cup sliced cucumber
  • 1/2 cup diced tomatoes
  • 1/4 cup pickled red onions
  • Lemon wedges for serving

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the Falafel: If using store-bought falafel, heat them according to package instructions. If making homemade falafel, fry or bake them until golden brown.

  2. Warm the Pita: Lightly warm the pita bread or flatbreads in a skillet or microwave to make them more pliable.

  3. Assemble the Wrap: Spread a generous amount of tahini sauce on each pita. Layer the mixed greens, falafel balls, cucumber, tomatoes, and pickled onions.

  4. Wrap It Up: Fold the sides in and roll the pita tightly to enclose the fillings. Secure with a toothpick if necessary.

  5. Serve and Enjoy: Serve your Mediterranean falafel wrap with lemon wedges on the side for a refreshing kick. This wrap is not only delicious but also packed with plant-based protein.

3. Tuna Niçoise Wrap

Ingredients:

  • 2 large spinach tortillas
  • 1 can (5 oz) tuna in olive oil, drained
  • 1/2 cup green beans, blanched
  • 1/2 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 2 hard-boiled eggs, peeled and sliced
  • 1/4 cup black olives, pitted and sliced
  • 2 tablespoons Dijon mustard
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the Tuna Mixture: In a bowl, combine the drained tuna, blanched green beans, cherry tomatoes, sliced eggs, and olives. In a separate small bowl, whisk together the Dijon mustard, olive oil, salt, and pepper, then pour over the tuna mixture.

  2. Mix Well: Gently mix the ingredients until everything is well coated.

  3. Assemble the Wrap: Lay the spinach tortillas flat and spoon the tuna mixture evenly onto each tortilla.

  4. Wrap It Up: Fold the sides inward and roll from the bottom up to create a tight wrap.

  5. Serve and Enjoy: Cut the wrap in half and serve with a side of extra olives or a light salad. This wrap is a refreshing take on the classic Niçoise salad, perfect for lunch or a picnic.

4. Mediterranean Veggie Wrap

Ingredients:

  • 2 large whole wheat wraps
  • 1/2 cup hummus (store-bought or homemade)
  • 1 cup roasted red peppers, sliced
  • 1/2 cup artichoke hearts, drained and chopped
  • 1/2 cup zucchini, thinly sliced and grilled
  • 1/4 cup crumbled feta cheese
  • 1/2 cup arugula or mixed greens
  • 1 tablespoon balsamic glaze (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the Veggies: If using fresh zucchini, grill the slices until tender. You can also roast or sauté the vegetables as per your preference.

  2. Spread the Hummus: Lay the whole wheat wraps flat and spread a generous layer of hummus on each.

  3. Add the Fillings: Layer the roasted red peppers, artichoke hearts, grilled zucchini, feta cheese, and arugula on top of the hummus.

  4. Wrap It Up: Fold the sides of the wrap and roll it tightly from the bottom to the top.

  5. Serve and Enjoy: Slice the wrap in half and drizzle with balsamic glaze if desired. This veggie wrap is a delightful way to enjoy Mediterranean flavors while packing in all the nutrients.

5. Spicy Shrimp and Avocado Wrap

Ingredients:

  • 2 large flour tortillas
  • 1 pound shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon smoked paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon cayenne pepper
  • 1 avocado, sliced
  • 1 cup shredded cabbage (red or green)
  • 1/2 cup pico de gallo or salsa
  • Lime wedges for serving

Instructions:

  1. Cook the Shrimp: In a skillet, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the shrimp, smoked paprika, cayenne pepper, salt, and pepper. Cook for about 2-3 minutes on each side until the shrimp are pink and cooked through.

  2. Warm the Tortillas: Lightly warm the flour tortillas in a dry skillet or microwave.

  3. Assemble the Wrap: Lay the tortillas flat and layer the sliced avocado, cooked shrimp, shredded cabbage, and pico de gallo on each.

  4. Wrap It Up: Fold the sides in and roll tightly from the bottom to the top.

  5. Serve and Enjoy: Cut the wrap in half and serve with lime wedges on the side for squeezing over the top. This spicy shrimp wrap is perfect for those who enjoy a bit of heat in their meals!

Conclusion

Mediterranean wraps are a fantastic option for anyone looking to enjoy healthy, flavorful meals that are easy to prepare and incredibly satisfying. With a variety of ingredients to choose from, these recipes can easily be customized to suit your personal taste. Whether you decide to go with chicken, falafel, tuna, veggies, or shrimp, you’re sure to enjoy the fresh and vibrant flavors of the Mediterranean.

So, wrap it up and enjoy these delicious Mediterranean-inspired creations that are sure to impress your family and friends. Happy cooking!
