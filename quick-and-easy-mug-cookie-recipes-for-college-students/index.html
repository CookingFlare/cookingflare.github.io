Quick and Easy Mug Cookie Recipes for College Students


Being a college student often means juggling a hectic schedule, and finding time to satisfy your sweet tooth can be a challenge. That’s where mug cookies come in – they are the perfect solution for those late-night study sessions or when you need a quick treat to boost your energy. With just a few ingredients and a microwave, you can whip up a delicious, warm cookie in minutes. Here are three quick and easy mug cookie recipes that are sure to become your go-to indulgence during your college years.

Classic Chocolate Chip Mug Cookie

Ingredients:

  • 1 tablespoon of butter
  • 1 tablespoon of granulated sugar
  • 1 tablespoon of brown sugar
  • 3 drops of vanilla extract
  • Pinch of salt
  • 1 egg yolk
  • 1/4 cup of all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons of chocolate chips

Instructions:

  1. In a microwave-safe mug, melt the butter in the microwave for 10-15 seconds.
  2. Add the granulated sugar, brown sugar, vanilla extract, and salt to the melted butter. Stir until well combined.
  3. Mix in the egg yolk until smooth.
  4. Stir in the flour until the batter is smooth and free of lumps.
  5. Gently fold in the chocolate chips.
  6. Microwave the mug cookie for 40-60 seconds, or until the edges are set but the center is still slightly gooey.
  7. Let it cool for a minute before indulging in your warm, gooey chocolate chip mug cookie.

Peanut Butter and Oat Mug Cookie

Ingredients:

  • 2 tablespoons of peanut butter
  • 1 tablespoon of brown sugar
  • 1 egg yolk
  • 2 tablespoons of rolled oats
  • 1/4 teaspoon of baking powder
  • Pinch of salt
  • 1 tablespoon of milk

Instructions:

  1. In a microwave-safe mug, melt the peanut butter in the microwave for 10-15 seconds.
  2. Add the brown sugar and egg yolk to the melted peanut butter. Stir until well combined.
  3. Mix in the rolled oats, baking powder, and salt.
  4. Stir in the milk until the batter is smooth and thick.
  5. Microwave the mug cookie for 40-50 seconds, or until it puffs up and the center is set.
  6. Allow it to cool for a minute before digging into your warm and comforting peanut butter and oat mug cookie.

Snickerdoodle Mug Cookie

Ingredients:

  • 1 1/2 tablespoons of butter
  • 2 tablespoons of granulated sugar
  • 1 egg yolk
  • 1/4 teaspoon of vanilla extract
  • 1/4 cup of all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 teaspoon of cream of tartar
  • Pinch of salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon of ground cinnamon

Instructions:

  1. In a microwave-safe mug, melt the butter in the microwave for 10-15 seconds.
  2. Add the granulated sugar, egg yolk, and vanilla extract to the melted butter. Stir until well combined.
  3. Mix in the flour, cream of tartar, and salt until the batter is smooth.
  4. In a small bowl, combine the ground cinnamon with a teaspoon of granulated sugar.
  5. Sprinkle the cinnamon sugar mixture over the top of the cookie batter in the mug.
  6. Microwave the mug cookie for 50-60 seconds, or until it rises and the center is set.
  7. Let it cool for a minute before savoring your delightful snickerdoodle mug cookie.

Whether you’re craving the timeless classic of chocolate chip, the comforting combination of peanut butter and oats, or the nostalgic taste of snickerdoodle, these mug cookie recipes are your ticket to a quick and easy homemade treat. So, the next time you’re in need of a study break or a late-night snack, grab your favorite mug and whip up one of these delightful creations. Happy baking!
