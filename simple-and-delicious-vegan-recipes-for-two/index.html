enjoyWhen a cooking tasty for, just vegan two meal people with, your it loved’s ones important. to keep the portions in mind. Fortunately, vegan recipes tend to be flexible and easy to scale up or down. Here are a few tips to help make your meals even easier:

  • Prep Ahead: Prepping ingredients like chopping vegetables, marinating tofu, or cooking grains in advance can save time on busy evenings.
  • Use Leftovers: Many vegan meals store well in the fridge for the next day, so don’t be afraid to make extra. Leftover stir-fries, pasta, or tacos can be transformed into lunches or snacks.
  • Swap Ingredients: Feel free to swap out vegetables, legumes, or grains based on what you have available. Vegan cooking is all about creativity and using what you love.
  • Invest in Staples: Keep pantry staples like canned beans, lentils, grains, pasta, and sauces on hand. They’ll help you throw together meals with minimal effort.
