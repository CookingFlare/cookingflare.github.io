Tasty and Nutritious Chickpea and Quinoa Curry Stew

Are you looking for a delicious and healthy recipe that will satisfy your taste buds and nourish your body? Look no further! This Tasty and Nutritious Chickpea and Quinoa Curry Stew is the perfect dish to add to your repertoire. Packed with protein, fiber, and a medley of flavorful spices, this curry stew is sure to become a family favorite. Ingredients 1 cup quinoa 1 can chickpeas, drained and rinsed 1 onion, finely chopped 3 cloves garlic, minced 1 red bell pepper, diced 1 zucchini, diced 1 cup cauliflower florets 1 tablespoon curry powder 1 teaspoon ground cumin 1 teaspoon ground turmeric 1 teaspoon paprika 1 can coconut milk 2 cups vegetable broth Salt and pepper, to taste Fresh cilantro, for garnish Instructions Cook the quinoa according to the package instructions. Set aside. In a large pot, heat some oil over medium heat. Add the onion and garlic, and sauté until fragrant and translucent. Add the red bell pepper, zucchini, and cauliflower to the pot. Cook for a few minutes, until the vegetables start to soften. Sprinkle the curry powder, cumin, turmeric, and paprika over the vegetables. Stir to coat evenly. Pour in the coconut milk and vegetable broth. Bring the mixture to a simmer and let it cook for about 15 minutes, until the vegetables are tender. Add the cooked quinoa and chickpeas to the pot. Stir to combine and let it simmer for another 5 minutes, allowing the flavors to meld together. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Serve the Chickpea and Quinoa Curry Stew hot, garnished with fresh cilantro.
