10 Delicious Allergy-Friendly Wraps for Lunch


Wraps are one of the most versatile and satisfying meals you can prepare for lunch. Whether you’re on the go or enjoying a quiet afternoon at home, wraps are easy to assemble, customizable, and a great way to pack in a variety of flavors and nutrients. But for those with food allergies, finding safe and delicious options can sometimes be a challenge. Thankfully, there are countless ways to make allergy-friendly wraps that cater to various dietary restrictions, whether you’re gluten-free, dairy-free, nut-free, or following another allergy-conscious diet.

In this blog post, we’ll explore 10 mouthwatering allergy-friendly wraps that are both nutritious and full of flavor. Each wrap is designed to meet a wide range of dietary needs without compromising on taste. So, whether you’re packing a lunch for school, work, or a picnic, these wraps are guaranteed to hit the spot.

1. Chicken and Avocado Lettuce Wraps

If you’re looking for a low-carb, gluten-free, and dairy-free option, these chicken and avocado lettuce wraps are an excellent choice. Crisp lettuce leaves serve as the perfect “wrap” for tender chicken, creamy avocado, and fresh veggies. This simple wrap is packed with healthy fats, lean protein, and fiber, making it a great choice for anyone looking to stay energized throughout the day.

Ingredients:

  • 2 chicken breasts, grilled and sliced
  • 1 avocado, sliced
  • 1 cucumber, thinly sliced
  • 1 red bell pepper, thinly sliced
  • 8 large lettuce leaves (such as Romaine or Butterhead)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Lemon juice (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Season the chicken breasts with salt and pepper, then grill them until cooked through. Slice into thin strips.
  2. Lay the lettuce leaves flat on a clean surface.
  3. Place sliced chicken, avocado, cucumber, and bell pepper in the center of each lettuce leaf.
  4. Drizzle with a squeeze of lemon juice for added freshness.
  5. Roll up the lettuce leaves to encase the filling, and enjoy!

2. Sweet Potato and Black Bean Wraps

Packed with plant-based protein and fiber, this sweet potato and black bean wrap is hearty, filling, and allergen-friendly. The combination of roasted sweet potatoes and black beans provides a wonderful balance of earthy, savory flavors, while the avocado adds a creamy texture.

Ingredients:

  • 1 large sweet potato, peeled and diced
  • 1 cup cooked black beans (or 1 can, drained and rinsed)
  • 1 avocado, mashed
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • 1 teaspoon chili powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 4 gluten-free tortillas
  • Fresh cilantro for garnish (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C). Toss the diced sweet potatoes with cumin, chili powder, salt, and pepper. Spread them evenly on a baking sheet.
  2. Roast the sweet potatoes for 25-30 minutes, flipping halfway through, until they are tender and slightly caramelized.
  3. In a bowl, mash the avocado and season with a pinch of salt.
  4. Warm the gluten-free tortillas in a pan or microwave for a few seconds.
  5. To assemble, spread mashed avocado on each tortilla, then add a generous scoop of roasted sweet potatoes and black beans.
  6. Garnish with fresh cilantro, if desired, and roll up tightly. Serve immediately.

3. Turkey and Hummus Veggie Wrap

This turkey and hummus veggie wrap is a great protein-packed option for lunch. With its simple ingredients, it’s easy to make and fully customizable to suit your dietary preferences. The creamy hummus is a perfect substitute for mayonnaise or other creamy spreads, and it adds an extra layer of flavor without any dairy.

Ingredients:

  • 4 gluten-free tortillas
  • 4 slices of turkey breast (ensure it’s allergen-friendly)
  • 1/2 cup hummus (store-bought or homemade)
  • 1 carrot, julienned
  • 1 cucumber, sliced
  • 1/2 cup spinach leaves
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Lay the gluten-free tortillas flat on a clean surface.
  2. Spread a thin layer of hummus over each tortilla.
  3. Add a slice or two of turkey breast in the center of each wrap.
  4. Top with carrots, cucumber, and spinach leaves.
  5. Season with a pinch of salt and pepper.
  6. Roll up the wraps tightly, slice, and enjoy!

4. Vegan Tofu and Veggie Wrap

Tofu is an excellent source of plant-based protein and works wonderfully in wraps. Paired with a variety of fresh veggies, this vegan tofu and veggie wrap is satisfying, crunchy, and incredibly flavorful. It’s also nut-free, making it safe for those with nut allergies.

Ingredients:

  • 1 block firm tofu, drained and pressed
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon soy sauce or tamari (for gluten-free)
  • 1/2 cup shredded carrots
  • 1/2 cucumber, thinly sliced
  • 1/4 cup red cabbage, shredded
  • 4 gluten-free tortillas

Instructions:

  1. Slice the tofu into thin strips. Heat olive oil in a skillet over medium heat and sauté the tofu for 3-4 minutes on each side, until golden brown. Drizzle with soy sauce or tamari while cooking.
  2. Once the tofu is crispy, remove it from the skillet and set it aside.
  3. Lay the gluten-free tortillas flat and add a few strips of sautéed tofu to the center.
  4. Add shredded carrots, cucumber, and cabbage on top.
  5. Roll up the wraps tightly, slice, and serve.

5. Tuna Salad Lettuce Wraps

For those who love seafood, this tuna salad lettuce wrap is a healthy and allergy-friendly alternative to the traditional tuna salad sandwich. It’s gluten-free, dairy-free, and packed with protein and healthy fats from the tuna and olive oil.

Ingredients:

  • 1 can tuna, drained
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon Dijon mustard
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 1/4 cup chopped red onion
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 8 large lettuce leaves (such as Romaine)
  • 1/2 avocado, sliced (optional)

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, mix together the tuna, olive oil, Dijon mustard, lemon juice, red onion, salt, and pepper.
  2. Lay the lettuce leaves on a flat surface and divide the tuna mixture evenly among the leaves.
  3. If desired, add a few slices of avocado for added creaminess.
  4. Roll up the lettuce leaves to form wraps and enjoy!

6. Grilled Veggie and Pesto Wrap

This grilled veggie and pesto wrap is bursting with Mediterranean flavors. You can use your favorite pesto (make sure it’s dairy-free if you’re avoiding cheese) to add a rich, herbaceous flavor to the veggies. Whether you use zucchini, eggplant, or bell peppers, this wrap is perfect for veggie lovers.

Ingredients:

  • 1 zucchini, sliced
  • 1 red bell pepper, sliced
  • 1/2 eggplant, sliced
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1/4 cup pesto (dairy-free if needed)
  • 4 gluten-free tortillas

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the grill or grill pan over medium heat.
  2. Toss the zucchini, bell pepper, and eggplant with olive oil and grill for about 3-4 minutes on each side until they are tender and slightly charred.
  3. Spread pesto over each gluten-free tortilla.
  4. Layer the grilled vegetables on top of the pesto and roll up the tortillas.
  5. Slice in half and serve immediately.

7. Falafel and Tabouli Wrap

This Middle Eastern-inspired wrap features crispy falafel and refreshing tabouli, making it an excellent choice for anyone following a vegetarian or vegan diet. The combination of herbs and spices gives this wrap a bold flavor, and the tabouli adds a light, fresh crunch.

Ingredients:

  • 6-8 gluten-free falafel (store-bought or homemade)
  • 1 cup tabouli (made with quinoa for a gluten-free option)
  • 4 gluten-free tortillas
  • 1/4 cup tahini sauce (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Heat the falafel according to package instructions or cook them at home.
  2. Prepare tabouli by mixing cooked quinoa with parsley, mint, tomatoes, cucumber, lemon juice, and olive oil.
  3. Warm the gluten-free tortillas in a pan or microwave.
  4. Place a few falafel in the center of each tortilla, top with tabouli, and drizzle with tahini sauce if desired.
  5. Roll up the wraps and enjoy the vibrant flavors of the Middle East!

8. BBQ Chicken and Slaw Wraps

If you love barbecue flavors, this BBQ chicken and slaw wrap is perfect for you. The tangy barbecue sauce paired with a crunchy slaw makes this wrap both satisfying and flavorful. It’s great for a summer lunch or even a fun dinner option.

Ingredients:

  • 2 chicken breasts, grilled and shredded
  • 1/2 cup BBQ sauce (ensure it’s allergen-friendly)
  • 1 cup coleslaw mix
  • 1 tablespoon apple cider vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 4 gluten-free tortillas

Instructions:

  1. Grill the chicken breasts, then shred them with a fork.
  2. Toss the shredded chicken with BBQ sauce until well-coated.
  3. In a bowl, mix the coleslaw mix with olive oil and apple cider vinegar.
  4. Warm the gluten-free tortillas and layer the BBQ chicken and slaw on each one.
  5. Roll up tightly and serve.

9. Shrimp and Avocado Mango Wraps

For a fresh, tropical twist, these shrimp and avocado mango wraps will transport you straight to a beachside paradise. The combination of sweet mango, creamy avocado, and tender shrimp creates a delicious and refreshing wrap that’s perfect for a light lunch.

Ingredients:

  • 8-10 cooked shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 1 ripe mango, diced
  • 1 avocado, sliced
  • 1/4 cup cilantro, chopped
  • 4 gluten-free tortillas
  • Lime wedges for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Arrange the cooked shrimp on each gluten-free tortilla.
  2. Add the diced mango, avocado slices, and chopped cilantro.
  3. Squeeze a bit of lime juice over the filling for extra zing.
  4. Roll up the wraps, slice them in half, and serve immediately.

10. Eggplant Parmesan Wraps

For those who enjoy Italian flavors, these eggplant parmesan wraps are a great allergy-friendly alternative to the classic dish. Baked eggplant slices are layered with marinara sauce and fresh basil, all wrapped up in a gluten-free tortilla. They’re perfect for a comforting and satisfying lunch.

Ingredients:

  • 1 eggplant, sliced into rounds
  • 1 cup marinara sauce
  • 1/4 cup fresh basil, chopped
  • 1/4 cup gluten-free breadcrumbs
  • 4 gluten-free tortillas

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C). Coat the eggplant slices with breadcrumbs and bake for 20-25 minutes, until golden and crispy.
  2. Warm the gluten-free tortillas.
  3. Layer the baked eggplant slices on each tortilla, then spoon some marinara sauce on top and sprinkle with fresh basil.
  4. Roll up the wraps and serve.

These 10 allergy-friendly wraps are perfect for anyone looking for delicious, satisfying lunches without the worry of food allergies. From savory chicken and tofu options to fresh, vibrant vegetable wraps, there’s something for everyone. Try them all to discover your favorite!
