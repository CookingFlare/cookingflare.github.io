Delicious Instant Pot Butter Chicken Recipes for Indian Food Lovers


Are you a fan of Indian cuisine? Do you love the rich and aromatic flavors that come with every bite? If so, then this blog post is for you! Today, we will be diving into the world of Instant Pot Butter Chicken recipes, a dish that has become a favorite among Indian food lovers around the globe.

Introduction

Butter chicken, also known as Murgh Makhani, is a classic North Indian dish that consists of succulent chicken cooked in a creamy and buttery tomato-based sauce. Traditionally, this dish is prepared by simmering the chicken in a tandoor or clay oven, but with the help of an Instant Pot, you can achieve the same delicious results in a fraction of the time!

Recipe 1: Classic Instant Pot Butter Chicken

Ingredients:

  • 1 kg chicken, cut into pieces
  • 1 cup plain yogurt
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 1 large onion, finely chopped
  • 4 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1-inch piece of ginger, grated
  • 2 teaspoons garam masala
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 teaspoon cumin powder
  • 1 teaspoon coriander powder
  • 1 teaspoon chili powder (adjust according to your spice preference)
  • 1 cup tomato puree
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh cilantro, for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Start by marinating the chicken. In a large bowl, combine the yogurt, ginger, garlic, garam masala, turmeric powder, cumin powder, coriander powder, and chili powder. Mix well, then add the chicken pieces and coat them thoroughly with the marinade. Let it sit for at least 30 minutes or overnight for best results.

  2. Turn on your Instant Pot and select the “Saute” function. Melt the butter and add the chopped onion. Cook until the onion turns golden brown, stirring occasionally. This step will infuse the dish with a rich flavor.

  3. Add the marinated chicken to the Instant Pot, along with any remaining marinade. Stir well to combine with the onions. Cook for about 5 minutes, until the chicken is lightly browned.

  4. Pour in the tomato puree and season with salt. Stir to combine everything evenly.

  5. Close the lid of the Instant Pot and make sure the pressure valve is set to “Sealing.” Select the “Pressure Cook” or “Manual” function and set the timer for 10 minutes on high pressure.

  6. Once the cooking time is up, allow the pressure to release naturally for 10 minutes, then carefully do a quick release to release any remaining pressure.

  7. Open the lid and stir in the heavy cream. If you prefer a thicker sauce, you can select the “Saute” function again and simmer for a few minutes until desired consistency is reached.

  8. Garnish with fresh cilantro and serve hot with naan bread or steamed rice. Enjoy the creamy and flavorful Instant Pot Butter Chicken!

Recipe 2: Healthy Instant Pot Butter Chicken (Dairy-Free)

If you’re looking for a healthier version of the classic Instant Pot Butter Chicken without compromising on taste, this recipe is perfect for you. It’s dairy-free yet still packed with flavor!

Ingredients:

  • 1 kg chicken, cut into pieces
  • 1 cup coconut milk
  • 2 tablespoons ghee or cooking oil
  • 1 large onion, finely chopped
  • 4 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1-inch piece of ginger, grated
  • 2 teaspoons garam masala
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 teaspoon cumin powder
  • 1 teaspoon coriander powder
  • 1 teaspoon chili powder
  • 1 cup tomato puree
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh cilantro, for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Follow the same steps as Recipe 1 for marinating the chicken.

  2. In the Instant Pot, select the “Saute” function and heat the ghee or cooking oil. Add the chopped onion and cook until golden brown.

  3. Add the marinated chicken to the Instant Pot, along with any remaining marinade. Cook for about 5 minutes, until the chicken is lightly browned.

  4. Add the garlic, ginger, garam masala, turmeric powder, cumin powder, coriander powder, and chili powder. Stir well to coat the chicken evenly with the spices.

  5. Pour in the tomato puree and coconut milk. Season with salt and stir everything together.

  6. Close the lid of the Instant Pot and set the pressure valve to “Sealing.” Select the “Pressure Cook” or “Manual” function and set the timer for 10 minutes on high pressure.

  7. Once the cooking time is up, allow the pressure to release naturally for 10 minutes, then carefully do a quick release to release any remaining pressure.

  8. Open the lid and give the butter chicken a good stir. Adjust the seasoning if needed.

  9. Garnish with fresh cilantro and serve hot with your favorite rice or roti. Enjoy the dairy-free version of this classic dish guilt-free!

Conclusion

Instant Pot Butter Chicken is a delightful dish that brings together the flavors of India in a creamy and satisfying way. Whether you prefer the classic recipe or a healthier dairy-free version, these Instant Pot Butter Chicken recipes are sure to impress your taste buds and leave you craving for more. So, grab your Instant Pot and get ready to indulge in the deliciousness of Indian cuisine right in your own kitchen!
