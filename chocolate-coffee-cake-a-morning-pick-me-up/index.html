Chocolate Coffee Cake: A Morning Pick-Me-Up


Are you a coffee lover who also enjoys a sweet treat in the morning? Look no further than this delectable Chocolate Coffee Cake recipe! Packed with the rich flavors of chocolate and the aromatic essence of coffee, this cake is the perfect way to start your day on a high note. So grab your apron, preheat your oven, and let’s dive into this delicious recipe!

Ingredients

For the cake:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/2 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup strong brewed coffee, cooled
  • 1/2 cup vegetable oil
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/2 cup milk

For the streusel topping:

  • 1/2 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 2 tablespoons unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 2 tablespoons cold unsalted butter, cut into small pieces

For the glaze:

  • 1 cup powdered sugar
  • 2 tablespoons brewed coffee, cooled
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease and flour a 9-inch round baking pan.

  2. In a large mixing bowl, whisk together the flour, sugar, cocoa powder, baking powder, baking soda, and salt.

  3. In a separate bowl, combine the cooled brewed coffee, vegetable oil, eggs, vanilla extract, and milk. Mix well.

  4. Gradually add the wet ingredients to the dry ingredients, stirring until just combined. Be careful not to overmix.

  5. Pour the batter into the prepared baking pan, spreading it evenly.

  6. Now, let’s make the streusel topping. In a small bowl, combine the flour, sugar, cocoa powder, and cold butter pieces. Use a fork or pastry cutter to cut the butter into the dry ingredients until the mixture resembles coarse crumbs.

  7. Sprinkle the streusel topping evenly over the cake batter.

  8. Bake for 35-40 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.

  9. While the cake is baking, let’s prepare the glaze. In a small bowl, whisk together the powdered sugar, brewed coffee, and vanilla extract until smooth. Set aside.

  10. Once the cake is done baking, remove it from the oven and let it cool in the pan for 10 minutes. Then, transfer it to a wire rack to cool completely.

  11. Drizzle the glaze over the cooled cake, allowing it to drip down the sides.

  12. Slice and serve this heavenly Chocolate Coffee Cake with a steaming cup of your favorite coffee. Enjoy the harmonious combination of rich chocolate and invigorating coffee flavors!

This Chocolate Coffee Cake is not only a delicious morning treat but also a delightful dessert to share with friends and family. The aroma of freshly brewed coffee and the indulgent taste of chocolate will make your taste buds dance with joy. So, the next time you need a pick-me-up in the morning, bake this irresistible cake and savor every bite. Happy baking!

Remember to adjust the quantities according to your preferences and dietary restrictions. Feel free to experiment with different flavors, such as adding a hint of cinnamon or a sprinkle of chopped nuts. Get creative and make this recipe your own!
