How to Make Cookies Without Eggs: 5 Delicious Recipes


Baking cookies is a beloved pastime that brings comfort and joy to many. However, if you find yourself without eggs or are catering to vegan diets or egg allergies, you might wonder if you can still enjoy that sweet, chewy goodness. The answer is a resounding yes! In this blog post, we’ll explore five delicious cookie recipes that you can whip up without using eggs. Each recipe is crafted to ensure you don’t compromise on taste or texture. Let’s dive into the world of egg-free baking!

1. Classic Eggless Chocolate Chip Cookies

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 tsp baking soda
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1/2 cup brown sugar
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/4 cup unsweetened applesauce (as an egg substitute)
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 cup semi-sweet chocolate chips

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the Oven: Set your oven to 350°F (175°C) and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.

  2. Mix Dry Ingredients: In a medium bowl, whisk together the flour, baking soda, and salt. Set aside.

  3. Cream the Butter and Sugars: In a large bowl, beat the softened butter, brown sugar, and granulated sugar until creamy and smooth.

  4. Add Applesauce and Vanilla: Stir in the applesauce and vanilla extract until fully combined.

  5. Combine Ingredients: Gradually add the flour mixture to the wet ingredients, mixing until just combined. Fold in the chocolate chips.

  6. Scoop and Bake: Drop spoonfuls of dough onto the prepared baking sheet, spacing them about 2 inches apart. Bake for 10-12 minutes or until the edges are golden.

  7. Cool and Enjoy: Allow the cookies to cool on the sheet for a few minutes before transferring them to a wire rack to cool completely.

These cookies will be soft, chewy, and filled with gooey chocolate goodness—perfect for any occasion!

2. Vegan Peanut Butter Cookies

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup natural peanut butter
  • 1/2 cup brown sugar
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/4 cup maple syrup
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 tsp baking soda
  • 1/4 tsp salt

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the Oven: Begin by preheating your oven to 350°F (175°C) and lining a baking sheet with parchment paper.

  2. Mix Wet Ingredients: In a large bowl, combine the peanut butter, brown sugar, granulated sugar, maple syrup, and vanilla extract. Mix until smooth.

  3. Combine Dry Ingredients: In another bowl, whisk together the flour, baking soda, and salt.

  4. Combine Mixtures: Gradually add the dry ingredients to the wet mixture, stirring until a dough forms.

  5. Shape the Cookies: Scoop tablespoon-sized amounts of dough and roll them into balls. Place them on the prepared baking sheet and flatten them slightly with a fork, creating a crisscross pattern.

  6. Bake: Bake for 10-12 minutes, or until the edges are golden. The cookies will firm up as they cool.

  7. Cool and Serve: Allow to cool on the baking sheet for a few minutes before transferring to a wire rack.

These vegan peanut butter cookies are rich, nutty, and simply irresistible!

3. Cinnamon Sugar Snickerdoodles

Ingredients:

  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 tsp baking soda
  • 1/2 tsp cream of tartar
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/4 cup unsweetened applesauce
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar (for rolling)
  • 1 tbsp ground cinnamon (for rolling)

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the Oven: Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C) and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.

  2. Sift Dry Ingredients: In a bowl, sift together the flour, baking soda, cream of tartar, and salt.

  3. Cream Butter and Sugar: In another bowl, cream the softened butter and sugar until light and fluffy.

  4. Add Applesauce and Vanilla: Mix in the applesauce and vanilla extract.

  5. Combine Mixtures: Gradually add the dry ingredients to the wet mixture, stirring until combined.

  6. Prepare Cinnamon Sugar: In a small bowl, mix the 1/4 cup of sugar with ground cinnamon.

  7. Form and Roll Cookies: Scoop tablespoon-sized balls of dough, roll them in the cinnamon-sugar mixture, and place them on the baking sheet.

  8. Bake: Bake for 10-12 minutes, until the edges are set and the tops are slightly cracked.

  9. Cool: Allow to cool on the baking sheet for a few minutes before moving them to a wire rack.

These snickerdoodles are a delightful twist on the classic, with a lovely cinnamon flavor that will have you craving more!

4. Oatmeal Raisin Cookies

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup rolled oats
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 tsp baking soda
  • 1/2 tsp cinnamon
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1/2 cup brown sugar
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/4 cup unsweetened applesauce
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 cup raisins

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the Oven: Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C) and prepare a baking sheet with parchment paper.

  2. Combine Dry Ingredients: In a bowl, mix together the oats, flour, baking soda, cinnamon, and salt.

  3. Cream Butter and Sugars: In a separate bowl, cream the softened butter, brown sugar, and granulated sugar until fluffy.

  4. Incorporate Applesauce and Vanilla: Add the applesauce and vanilla extract, mixing until well combined.

  5. Combine Mixtures: Gradually add the dry ingredients to the wet ingredients, mixing until just combined. Fold in the raisins.

  6. Scoop and Bake: Drop spoonfuls of dough onto the prepared baking sheet, spacing them out. Bake for 10-12 minutes or until golden.

  7. Cool: Let the cookies cool on the baking sheet for a few minutes before transferring to a wire rack.

These oatmeal raisin cookies are chewy, hearty, and filled with the goodness of oats and raisins!

5. Lemon Sugar Cookies

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 tsp baking powder
  • 1/4 tsp baking soda
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/4 cup unsweetened applesauce
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • Zest of 1 lemon
  • 2 tbsp lemon juice
  • Additional sugar for rolling

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the Oven: Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C) and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.

  2. Combine Dry Ingredients: In a bowl, whisk together the flour, baking powder, baking soda, and salt.

  3. Cream Butter and Sugar: In a large bowl, cream the softened butter and sugar until light and fluffy.

  4. Add Applesauce and Flavorings: Mix in the applesauce, vanilla extract, lemon zest, and lemon juice.

  5. Combine Ingredients: Gradually add the dry ingredients to the wet mixture, stirring until just combined.

  6. Form Cookies: Roll tablespoon-sized portions of dough into balls and roll them in sugar.

  7. Bake: Place them on the baking sheet and bake for 10-12 minutes, or until the edges are lightly golden.

  8. Cool and Enjoy: Let cool on the baking sheet for a few minutes before transferring to a wire rack to cool completely.

These lemon sugar cookies offer a refreshing twist, with a perfect balance of sweetness and tartness.

Conclusion

Baking cookies without eggs is not only possible but can also be incredibly delicious! Whether you’re looking for a classic chocolate chip cookie, a nutty peanut butter delight, or a refreshing lemon treat, these recipes provide a variety of options that cater to all tastes and dietary needs.

With simple substitutions like applesauce and creative flavor combinations, you can enjoy the joy of baking and the satisfaction of indulging in freshly baked cookies. So gather your ingredients, preheat your oven, and get ready to fill your kitchen with the delightful aroma of homemade cookies. Happy baking!
