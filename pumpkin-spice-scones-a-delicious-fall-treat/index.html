Pumpkin Spice Scones: A Delicious Fall Treat


As the leaves start to change and the air becomes crisp, there’s nothing quite like the comforting aroma of pumpkin spice wafting through the kitchen. And what better way to embrace the flavors of fall than with a batch of homemade Pumpkin Spice Scones? These delectable treats are the perfect combination of warm spices, tender crumb, and just the right amount of sweetness. So, roll up your sleeves, preheat the oven, and let’s dive into the delightful world of Pumpkin Spice Scones!

Ingredients

For the Scones:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/3 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 tablespoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground nutmeg
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground cloves
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground ginger
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, cold and cut into small pieces
  • 1/2 cup pumpkin puree
  • 1/4 cup heavy cream
  • 1 large egg
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

For the Glaze:

  • 1 cup confectioners’ sugar
  • 2-3 tablespoons milk
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C) and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.

  2. In a large bowl, whisk together the flour, sugar, baking powder, salt, cinnamon, nutmeg, cloves, and ginger.

  3. Add the cold butter to the dry ingredients and use a pastry cutter or your fingertips to work the butter into the flour mixture until it resembles coarse crumbs.

  4. In a separate bowl, combine the pumpkin puree, heavy cream, egg, and vanilla extract. Whisk until smooth.

  5. Pour the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients and stir until just combined. Be careful not to overmix the dough.

  6. Transfer the dough to a floured surface and gently pat it into a 1-inch thick circle. Cut the dough into 8 wedges and place them on the prepared baking sheet.

  7. Bake for 15-18 minutes or until the scones are golden brown and a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean. Transfer the scones to a wire rack to cool.

  8. While the scones are cooling, make the glaze by whisking together the confectioners’ sugar, milk, and vanilla extract until smooth. Drizzle the glaze over the cooled scones and allow it to set before serving.

Serving Suggestions

These Pumpkin Spice Scones are best enjoyed fresh out of the oven with a steaming cup of your favorite coffee or tea. The warm, buttery aroma of the scones combined with the cozy flavors of pumpkin spice will transport you to a picturesque autumn day.

Tips for Success

  • For an extra indulgent touch, sprinkle some cinnamon sugar on top of the scones before baking.
  • If you don’t have a pastry cutter, you can use two knives or even your hands to cut the butter into the dry ingredients.
  • Make sure not to overwork the dough, as this can result in tough scones. A light hand is key to achieving a tender crumb.

Embrace the Flavors of Fall

Whether you’re hosting a fall brunch, treating your family to a special weekend breakfast, or simply craving a cozy baking project, these Pumpkin Spice Scones are sure to delight your taste buds and fill your home with the essence of the season. So, gather your ingredients, embrace the warmth of the spices, and savor every delicious bite of these delightful fall treats.
