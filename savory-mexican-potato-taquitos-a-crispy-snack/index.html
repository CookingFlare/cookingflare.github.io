Savory Mexican Potato Taquitos: A Crispy Snack


In the world of Mexican cuisine, taquitos are a beloved snack that has gained popularity globally. These crispy, flavorful bites are typically filled with meat, cheese, or vegetables, and are perfect for munching on while watching a game, attending a party, or simply as a quick pick-me-up. Today, we’re going to explore a unique twist on the classic taquito recipe: Savory Mexican Potato Taquitos. These mouthwatering treats combine the comfort of mashed potatoes with the bold flavors of Mexico, resulting in a snack that’s both familiar and excitingly new.

Ingredients

To make these Savory Mexican Potato Taquitos, you’ll need the following ingredients:

  • 2 large potatoes, peeled and diced
  • 1/4 cup vegetable oil
  • 1 small onion, diced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon smoked paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 8-10 corn tortillas
  • 1 cup shredded Monterey Jack cheese (or your preferred cheese)
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh cilantro (optional)
  • Salsa, sour cream, and avocado for serving (optional)

Instructions

  1. Make the potato filling: In a large pot, heat the vegetable oil over medium heat. Add the diced onion and cook until translucent, about 5 minutes. Add the minced garlic and cook for an additional minute.
  2. Add the diced potatoes, cumin, smoked paprika, salt, and black pepper to the pot. Stir to combine, then reduce heat to low and simmer for 15-20 minutes, or until the potatoes are tender and easily mashed.
  3. Mash the potatoes: Remove the pot from the heat and use a potato masher or a fork to mash the potatoes until they’re smooth and creamy.
  4. Assemble the taquitos: In a large skillet, heat about 1/2 inch of vegetable oil over medium-high heat. Once hot, place a tortilla in the oil and cook for about 30 seconds on each side, until slightly softened and pliable. Repeat with the remaining tortillas.
  5. Fill and roll the taquitos: Spoon about 1/4 cup of the potato filling onto the center of a tortilla, leaving a 1-inch border around the edges. Sprinkle a pinch of shredded cheese on top of the potatoes.
  6. Roll the taquito tightly but gently, making sure the filling is secure and the edges are aligned. Repeat with the remaining tortillas and filling.
  7. Fry the taquitos: In the same skillet used for warming the tortillas, heat about 1/2 inch of vegetable oil over medium-high heat. Once hot, add a few of the rolled taquitos to the oil (do not overcrowd).
  8. Fry the taquitos for about 2-3 minutes on each side, until crispy and golden brown. Repeat with the remaining taquitos.
  9. Serve: Serve the Savory Mexican Potato Taquitos hot with your preferred toppings, such as salsa, sour cream, and avocado. Garnish with chopped cilantro, if desired.

Tips and Variations

  • For an extra crispy coating, sprinkle a pinch of cornstarch or flour on the taquitos before frying.
  • Experiment with different types of cheese, such as queso fresco or Oaxaca cheese, for unique flavor profiles.
  • Add some heat to your taquitos by incorporating diced jalapeños or serrano peppers into the potato filling.
  • Serve the taquitos as a snack, appetizer, or even as a side dish for your favorite Mexican-inspired meals.

Conclusion

Savory Mexican Potato Taquitos are a game-changer for anyone looking to elevate their snack game. With their crispy exterior, creamy potato filling, and bold Mexican flavors, these taquitos are sure to please even the pickiest of eaters. Whether you’re a seasoned cook or a beginner in the kitchen, this recipe is easy to follow and requires minimal ingredients. So go ahead, give it a try, and discover a new favorite snack that’s sure to become a staple in your household.
