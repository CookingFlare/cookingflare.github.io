Easy and Elegant Tea Sandwich Ideas for Your Summer Picnic


Summer is the perfect time to enjoy a relaxing picnic outdoors with family and friends. And what better way to elevate your picnic experience than with easy and elegant tea sandwiches? These delightful finger sandwiches are not only visually appealing but also bursting with flavor. Here are some creative tea sandwich ideas that will impress your guests and make your summer picnic a memorable one.

1. Cucumber and Dill Tea Sandwiches

Ingredients:

  • 8 slices of white bread
  • 1 large cucumber, thinly sliced
  • Fresh dill leaves
  • Cream cheese
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Spread a thin layer of cream cheese on each slice of bread.
  2. Arrange the cucumber slices on half of the bread slices, slightly overlapping them.
  3. Sprinkle with fresh dill leaves and season with salt and pepper.
  4. Top with the remaining bread slices.
  5. Trim the crusts from the sandwiches and cut them into desired shapes, such as triangles or squares.
  6. Serve chilled and enjoy the refreshing combination of flavors.

2. Smoked Salmon and Cream Cheese Tea Sandwiches

Ingredients:

  • 8 slices of pumpernickel bread
  • Smoked salmon slices
  • Cream cheese
  • Fresh dill sprigs
  • Lemon zest

Instructions:

  1. Spread a generous layer of cream cheese on each slice of bread.
  2. Place a few slices of smoked salmon on half of the bread slices.
  3. Garnish with fresh dill sprigs and a sprinkle of lemon zest.
  4. Top with the remaining bread slices.
  5. Cut into small rectangles or squares for an elegant presentation.
  6. Serve chilled and savor the rich and delicate combination of flavors.

3. Chicken Salad Tea Sandwiches

Ingredients:

  • 8 slices of whole wheat bread
  • Cooked chicken breast, shredded
  • Mayonnaise
  • Dijon mustard
  • Celery, finely chopped
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Lettuce leaves

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, mix together the shredded chicken, mayonnaise, Dijon mustard, and celery.
  2. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  3. Spread a layer of the chicken salad on each slice of bread.
  4. Top with lettuce leaves.
  5. Trim the crusts and cut into desired shapes.
  6. Serve at room temperature and enjoy the satisfying combination of flavors and textures.

4. Caprese Tea Sandwiches

Ingredients:

  • 8 slices of ciabatta bread
  • Fresh mozzarella cheese, sliced
  • Tomato, sliced
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • Balsamic glaze

Instructions:

  1. Layer the mozzarella cheese, tomato slices, and fresh basil leaves on four slices of bread.
  2. Drizzle with balsamic glaze.
  3. Top with the remaining bread slices.
  4. Cut into small squares or rectangles.
  5. Serve at room temperature and relish the harmonious blend of flavors.

5. Egg Salad and Watercress Tea Sandwiches

Ingredients:

  • 8 slices of rye bread
  • Hard-boiled eggs, mashed
  • Mayonnaise
  • Dijon mustard
  • Fresh dill
  • Watercress leaves

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, mix together the mashed hard-boiled eggs, mayonnaise, Dijon mustard, and fresh dill.
  2. Spread the egg salad on each slice of bread.
  3. Top with watercress leaves.
  4. Trim the crusts and cut into desired shapes.
  5. Serve chilled and indulge in the creamy and herbaceous flavors.

These easy and elegant tea sandwich ideas are perfect for your summer picnic. They are simple to prepare, visually appealing, and packed with delicious flavors. Whether you’re hosting a casual gathering or a formal event, these tea sandwiches will add a touch of sophistication to your spread. So grab your picnic basket, prepare these delightful sandwiches, and enjoy a memorable outdoor dining experience with your loved ones. Happy picnicking!
