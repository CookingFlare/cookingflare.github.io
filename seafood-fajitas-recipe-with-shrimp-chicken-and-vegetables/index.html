Seafood Fajitas Recipe with Shrimp, Chicken, and Vegetables


If you’re looking for a flavor-packed, colorful, and satisfying dish to serve at your next gathering or simply for a delicious family dinner, then seafood fajitas with shrimp, chicken, and vegetables might just be the answer. This recipe brings together the succulent flavors of shrimp and chicken with a medley of vibrant vegetables, all seasoned to perfection and served sizzling hot. Whether you’re a seafood lover, a chicken aficionado, or a vegetable enthusiast, this dish has something for everyone. Let’s dive into the tantalizing world of seafood fajitas and discover how to bring this mouthwatering creation to life in your own kitchen.

Ingredients

For the Fajita Seasoning:

  • 2 teaspoons chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon smoked paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon onion powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon cayenne pepper
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper

For the Seafood and Chicken:

  • 1 pound large shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 1 pound boneless, skinless chicken breasts, thinly sliced

For the Vegetables:

  • 2 bell peppers, thinly sliced (preferably a mix of colors)
  • 1 large red onion, thinly sliced
  • 1 zucchini, thinly sliced
  • 1 yellow squash, thinly sliced

Additional Ingredients:

  • 3 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 lime, juiced
  • Fresh cilantro, chopped, for garnish
  • Warm flour tortillas
  • Optional toppings: sour cream, guacamole, salsa

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Fajita Seasoning: In a small bowl, mix together the chili powder, ground cumin, smoked paprika, garlic powder, onion powder, cayenne pepper, salt, and black pepper. Set aside.

  2. Marinate the Seafood and Chicken: Place the shrimp and sliced chicken in separate bowls. Drizzle each with 1 tablespoon of olive oil, half of the prepared fajita seasoning, and the juice of half a lime. Toss to coat evenly and allow to marinate for at least 15 minutes.

  3. Sauté the Vegetables: In a large skillet or cast-iron pan over medium-high heat, add 2 tablespoons of olive oil. Once hot, add the sliced bell peppers, red onion, zucchini, and yellow squash. Sprinkle with the remaining fajita seasoning. Sauté for 5-7 minutes, or until the vegetables are tender-crisp and slightly charred. Remove the vegetables from the pan and set aside.

  4. Cook the Seafood and Chicken: In the same skillet, add the marinated shrimp and cook for 2-3 minutes on each side until they turn pink and opaque. Transfer the cooked shrimp to a plate. Next, add the marinated chicken to the skillet and cook for 5-6 minutes, or until it’s no longer pink in the center and has a nice sear.

  5. Assemble the Seafood Fajitas: Return the sautéed vegetables to the skillet with the cooked chicken, then add the cooked shrimp. Squeeze the juice of the remaining half of the lime over the mixture and toss everything together.

  6. Serve: Warm the flour tortillas in a dry skillet or microwave. Serve the seafood fajita mixture on the warmed tortillas, garnish with fresh cilantro, and offer optional toppings such as sour cream, guacamole, and salsa.

  7. Enjoy: Dive into the delicious combination of flavors and textures in each bite. The succulent seafood, tender chicken, and colorful vegetables come together harmoniously, creating a truly delightful dining experience.

Conclusion

Seafood fajitas with shrimp, chicken, and vegetables bring a delightful twist to the classic fajita dish. The marriage of fresh seafood, juicy chicken, and vibrant vegetables, all seasoned with a tantalizing blend of spices, is sure to captivate the taste buds of anyone who’s fortunate enough to savor this culinary creation. So, whether you’re hosting a dinner party or simply seeking to elevate your weeknight meal, consider adding seafood fajitas to your recipe repertoire. This dish offers a symphony of flavors and textures that will leave a lasting impression on all who indulge in its savory embrace.

This seafood fajitas recipe offers a delightful combination of flavors and textures that’s sure to captivate the taste buds of seafood and chicken lovers alike. The vibrant colors and sizzling aroma of this dish are guaranteed to add a touch of excitement to any meal. Whether it’s a family dinner or a gathering with friends, seafood fajitas provide a delicious and satisfying culinary experience that’s hard to resist.
