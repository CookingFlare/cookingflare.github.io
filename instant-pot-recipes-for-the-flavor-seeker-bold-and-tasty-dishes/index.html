Instant Pot Recipes for the Flavor-Seeker: Bold and Tasty Dishes


When it comes to spicing up your culinary repertoire and packing every bite with explosive flavor, the Instant Pot is an invaluable kitchen tool. This multifunctional pressure cooker makes it easier than ever to whip up dishes from around the world, producing bold and complex flavors in a fraction of the time compared to traditional stovetop or slow-cooker methods. If you’re someone who craves vibrant seasoning, tender textures, and rich aromas, these Instant Pot recipes will take your everyday cooking to the next level. Get ready to tantalize your taste buds with bold and tasty dishes that your family and friends will love!

1. Thai Red Curry Chicken

Elevate your dinner table with a dish that’s creamy, spicy, and wonderfully aromatic. Thai red curry chicken is a crowd-pleaser that pairs perfectly with steamed jasmine rice or rice noodles.

Ingredients:

  • 1 ½ lbs boneless, skinless chicken thighs, cut into chunks
  • 1 tbsp coconut oil
  • 3 tbsp red curry paste (adjust to taste)
  • 1 can (14 oz) coconut milk
  • 1 red bell pepper, sliced thinly
  • 1 zucchini, sliced into half-moons
  • 1 tbsp fish sauce
  • 2 tsp brown sugar
  • 1 lime, juiced
  • Fresh Thai basil for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Select the “Sauté” mode on your Instant Pot, and heat the coconut oil. Add the red curry paste and cook for 1-2 minutes until fragrant.
  2. Add the chicken and sauté until coated in the curry paste, about 3 minutes.
  3. Stir in the coconut milk, fish sauce, and brown sugar. Cancel the “Sauté” mode, secure the lid, and select “Pressure Cook.” Set the timer for 8 minutes.
  4. Once the cooking time is complete, perform a quick release. Remove the lid and stir in the bell peppers and zucchini, allowing the residual heat to slightly cook the vegetables while maintaining their crunch.
  5. Add lime juice to brighten the flavors and garnish with fresh Thai basil leaves before serving.

2. Smoky Chipotle Pulled Pork

This smoky and spicy pulled pork is an instant favorite for tacos, sandwiches, or even served atop a bowl of creamy polenta.

Ingredients:

  • 3 lbs pork shoulder, cut into large chunks
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 onion, thinly sliced
  • 3 garlic cloves, minced
  • 2 canned chipotle peppers in adobo sauce, diced
  • 1 cup chicken broth
  • 1 tbsp smoked paprika
  • 1 tsp cumin
  • 1 tsp oregano
  • 1 tsp salt
  • Juice of 1 orange

Instructions:

  1. On “Sauté” mode, heat olive oil in your Instant Pot. Brown the pork shoulder in batches to create a caramelized crust, then remove and set aside.
  2. Add onions and garlic to the pot, cooking until softened. Stir in the chipotle peppers, chicken broth, smoked paprika, cumin, oregano, and salt.
  3. Return the pork to the pot and pour in the orange juice. Lock the lid and set the Instant Pot to “Pressure Cook” for 60 minutes.
  4. Allow the pressure to release naturally for 15 minutes before carefully performing a quick release. Use two forks to shred the pork in the sauce. Serve with warm tortillas or your favorite side dishes.

3. Moroccan Chickpea Stew

For a vegetarian option packed with exotic flavors, Moroccan chickpea stew is the perfect choice. It’s hearty, savory, and spiced with a delicious blend of warm seasonings.

Ingredients:

  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 3 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 tsp ground cinnamon
  • 1 tsp ground cumin
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • ½ tsp ground ginger
  • 2 cups vegetable broth
  • 1 can (14 oz) diced tomatoes
  • 1 can (14 oz) chickpeas, drained and rinsed
  • 1 medium sweet potato, cubed
  • ½ cup dried apricots, chopped
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh cilantro for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Turn the Instant Pot to “Sauté” mode. Heat olive oil, then cook the onions and garlic until softened. Stir in cinnamon, cumin, paprika, and ginger, cooking for 1 minute to release the spices’ aroma.
  2. Add the vegetable broth, diced tomatoes, chickpeas, sweet potato, and dried apricots. Stir to combine.
  3. Secure the lid and cook on “Pressure Cook” mode for 10 minutes.
  4. Once the cooking cycle is complete, perform a quick release and stir the stew well. Season with salt and pepper, and garnish with fresh cilantro.

4. Coconut-Lime Cilantro Rice

Sometimes, it’s the side dishes that steal the spotlight. This coconut-lime cilantro rice is infused with tropical flavors that will perfectly complement your bold mains.

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups jasmine rice, rinsed
  • 1 can (14 oz) coconut milk
  • 1 ¼ cups water
  • ½ tsp salt
  • Juice of 1 lime
  • Zest of 1 lime
  • ¼ cup fresh cilantro, chopped

Instructions:

  1. Add the rinsed rice, coconut milk, water, and salt to the Instant Pot. Give it a quick stir to ensure the rice doesn’t stick to the bottom.
  2. Seal the lid and set the Instant Pot to “Pressure Cook” for 5 minutes. Allow the pressure to release naturally for 10 minutes before performing a quick release.
  3. Fluff the rice with a fork, then gently mix in the lime juice, lime zest, and cilantro. Serve warm with your favorite entrees.

5. Indian Butter Chicken

This velvety, buttery chicken curry is full of complex spices and indulgent flavors. Best served with naan bread or basmati rice, this dish is sure to please any flavor enthusiast.

Ingredients:

  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 1 large onion, finely diced
  • 3 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 tbsp ginger, minced
  • 1 tbsp garam masala
  • 1 tsp ground turmeric
  • 1 tsp chili powder
  • 2 tsp paprika
  • 1 ½ lbs boneless, skinless chicken thighs, cut into chunks
  • 1 can (14 oz) tomato sauce
  • ¾ cup heavy cream
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh cilantro for garnish

Instructions:

  1. On “Sauté” mode, melt the butter in the Instant Pot. Add the onion, garlic, and ginger, cooking until softened. Mix in garam masala, turmeric, chili powder, and paprika, stirring until fragrant.
  2. Add the chicken and tomato sauce, and stir to coat the chicken in the spice mixture. Secure the lid and cook on “Pressure Cook” mode for 10 minutes.
  3. When cooking is finished, allow the pressure to release naturally for 5 minutes, then perform a quick release. Stir in the heavy cream, adjusting salt and pepper to your liking. Garnish with fresh cilantro before serving.

6. Korean Beef Bowls

Quick, savory, and packed with umami, Korean beef bowls are a flavorful way to reimagine ground beef for weeknight dinners.

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 2 tbsp sesame oil
  • 3 garlic cloves, minced
  • ¼ cup soy sauce
  • ⅓ cup brown sugar
  • 1 tsp grated ginger
  • 1 tsp chili flakes
  • 1 bunch green onions, chopped
  • Cooked white rice for serving

Instructions:

  1. Using the “Sauté” function, cook the ground beef with sesame oil until browned. Add in the garlic, soy sauce, brown sugar, grated ginger, and chili flakes, cooking until saucy and fragrant.
  2. Cancel the “Sauté” function and let the beef mixture cool slightly. Stir in the green onions just before serving.
  3. Spoon over a bowl of white rice and add optional garnishes like sesame seeds or kimchi for extra flavor.

The beauty of Instant Pot cooking is how it transforms bold flavors into meals that are incredible in both taste and convenience. Whether you’re a spice enthusiast or just looking to add a little excitement to your weekly menu, these recipes ensure your Instant Pot earns its permanent spot on your countertop. Grab your ingredients, unleash your adventurous palate, and get cooking!
