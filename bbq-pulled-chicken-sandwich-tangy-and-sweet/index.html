BBQ Pulled Chicken Sandwich: Tangy and Sweet


Are you craving a delicious and satisfying meal that combines the tanginess of BBQ sauce with the succulent flavor of pulled chicken? Look no further! In this recipe, we’ll show you how to make a mouthwatering BBQ pulled chicken sandwich that will leave you wanting more.

Ingredients

To make this delectable sandwich, you will need the following ingredients:

  • 2 pounds of boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1 cup of your favorite BBQ sauce
  • 1/4 cup of apple cider vinegar
  • 2 tablespoons of brown sugar
  • 1 tablespoon of Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 teaspoon of garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon of onion powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon of smoked paprika
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 4 hamburger buns

Instructions

  1. In a slow cooker, combine the BBQ sauce, apple cider vinegar, brown sugar, Worcestershire sauce, garlic powder, onion powder, smoked paprika, salt, and pepper. Stir well to combine.

  2. Add the chicken breasts to the slow cooker and coat them evenly with the sauce mixture.

  3. Cook the chicken on low heat for 6-8 hours or on high heat for 3-4 hours, until the chicken is tender and easily shreds with a fork.

  4. Once the chicken is cooked, remove it from the slow cooker and shred it using two forks. Place the shredded chicken back into the slow cooker and stir it into the sauce to coat it evenly.

  5. Toast the hamburger buns until they are lightly golden and crispy.

  6. Assemble your BBQ pulled chicken sandwiches by placing a generous amount of the shredded chicken onto the bottom half of each bun. Top with your favorite toppings, such as coleslaw, pickles, or sliced onions.

  7. Place the top half of the bun on the sandwich and press gently to hold everything together.

  8. Serve your tangy and sweet BBQ pulled chicken sandwiches immediately and enjoy!

Tips and Variations

  • If you don’t have a slow cooker, you can also cook the chicken in a large pot on the stovetop over low heat. Just make sure to stir occasionally to prevent sticking.

  • Feel free to adjust the amount of BBQ sauce and spices according to your taste preferences. If you like it spicier, add a dash of hot sauce or cayenne pepper.

  • For an extra kick of flavor, you can also add a splash of liquid smoke to the sauce mixture.

  • If you want to save time, you can use store-bought rotisserie chicken instead of cooking the chicken from scratch. Simply shred the rotisserie chicken and mix it with the BBQ sauce.

  • This BBQ pulled chicken is also great for meal prep. Make a large batch and store it in the refrigerator for up to 3-4 days. It reheats well and can be used in various dishes, such as tacos, salads, or quesadillas.

Conclusion

There you have it – a tantalizing BBQ pulled chicken sandwich recipe that combines the tanginess of BBQ sauce with the succulent flavors of shredded chicken. The slow cooking process ensures that the chicken is tender and infused with the delicious sauce. Whether you’re hosting a backyard barbecue or simply looking for a satisfying meal, this sandwich is sure to impress. So fire up the grill, grab your favorite toppings, and get ready to indulge in this mouthwatering creation. Enjoy!
