Flavorful Thai Pineapple Fried Rice


Are you craving a taste of Thailand in your own kitchen? Look no further than this delectable and aromatic Thai Pineapple Fried Rice recipe. Bursting with vibrant flavors and textures, this dish is a true crowd-pleaser that will transport your taste buds to the bustling streets of Bangkok.

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups of cooked jasmine rice
  • 1 cup of fresh pineapple chunks
  • 1 red bell pepper, diced
  • 1 yellow bell pepper, diced
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1/2 cup of peas
  • 2 eggs, beaten
  • 2 tablespoons of soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons of fish sauce
  • 1 tablespoon of curry powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon of turmeric powder
  • 1/4 cup of roasted cashews
  • Fresh cilantro for garnish
  • Lime wedges for serving

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the Ingredients:

    • Cook the jasmine rice according to package instructions and set aside to cool.
    • Cut the pineapple into chunks, dice the bell peppers, finely chop the onion, and mince the garlic.
    • Beat the eggs in a small bowl and set aside.

  2. Stir-Fry the Ingredients:

    • Heat oil in a large pan or wok over medium-high heat.
    • Add the minced garlic and chopped onion, and sauté until fragrant.
    • Push the garlic and onion to the side of the pan, then pour the beaten eggs into the empty space. Scramble the eggs until cooked through.
    • Add the diced bell peppers and peas to the pan, and stir-fry for a few minutes until slightly softened.

  3. Add Rice and Flavors:

    • Add the cooked jasmine rice to the pan, breaking up any clumps as you stir.
    • Drizzle the soy sauce, fish sauce, curry powder, and turmeric powder over the rice. Mix well to ensure the rice is evenly coated with the seasonings.

  4. Incorporate Pineapple and Cashews:

    • Gently fold in the fresh pineapple chunks and roasted cashews, allowing them to heat through.
    • Continue to stir-fry the rice mixture for another 2-3 minutes until everything is well combined and heated evenly.

  5. Garnish and Serve:

    • Remove the pan from the heat and taste for seasoning, adjusting with more soy sauce or fish sauce if needed.
    • Garnish the Thai Pineapple Fried Rice with fresh cilantro and serve with lime wedges on the side for an extra burst of citrusy flavor.

Enjoy the Burst of Thai Flavors!

There you have it – a mouthwatering Thai Pineapple Fried Rice recipe that is sure to impress your family and friends. Whether you’re looking to spice up your weeknight dinners or host a Thai-inspired feast, this dish is a winner. The combination of sweet pineapple, savory rice, and fragrant spices creates a symphony of flavors that will leave you craving more. So, roll up your sleeves, grab your wok, and get ready to savor a taste of Thailand in every bite!

Remember, the key to perfecting this dish lies in the balance of flavors and the freshness of ingredients. Feel free to customize the recipe by adding your favorite protein or vegetables to make it your own. Let the aroma of Thai spices fill your kitchen and transport you to a culinary journey through the vibrant streets of Southeast Asia. Happy cooking!
