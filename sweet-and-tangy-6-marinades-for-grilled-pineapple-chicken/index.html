Sweet and Tangy: 6 Marinades for Grilled Pineapple Chicken


Grilled pineapple chicken is a delicious and flavorful dish that combines the sweetness of pineapple with the tanginess of marinades. Whether you’re hosting a summer barbecue or simply craving a tropical-inspired meal, these 6 marinades will take your grilled pineapple chicken to the next level. Each marinade offers a unique combination of flavors that will tantalize your taste buds and leave you wanting more. So let’s dive in and discover these mouthwatering marinades!

1. Pineapple Teriyaki Marinade

This marinade combines the sweetness of pineapple juice with the savory flavors of soy sauce, ginger, and garlic. The pineapple juice tenderizes the chicken while adding a burst of tropical flavor. To make this marinade, simply mix together 1 cup of pineapple juice, 1/4 cup of soy sauce, 2 tablespoons of minced ginger, and 4 cloves of minced garlic. Marinate the chicken for at least 2 hours before grilling for maximum flavor.

2. Honey Lime Marinade

The combination of honey and lime gives this marinade a perfect balance of sweetness and tanginess. To make this marinade, mix together 1/4 cup of honey, the juice of 2 limes, 2 tablespoons of olive oil, and a pinch of salt and pepper. Marinate the chicken for at least 1 hour before grilling to allow the flavors to infuse.

3. Spicy Pineapple Marinade

If you like a little kick in your grilled pineapple chicken, this marinade is for you. The sweetness of pineapple is balanced with the heat of jalapenos and the smokiness of paprika. To make this marinade, blend together 1 cup of pineapple chunks, 2 jalapenos (seeds removed for less heat), 2 cloves of garlic, 1 tablespoon of paprika, and a pinch of salt. Marinate the chicken for at least 4 hours before grilling for maximum flavor.

4. Citrus Herb Marinade

This marinade combines the bright flavors of citrus with the aromatic herbs of oregano and thyme. To make this marinade, mix together the juice of 1 orange, the juice of 1 lemon, 2 tablespoons of olive oil, 1 tablespoon of dried oregano, 1 tablespoon of dried thyme, and a pinch of salt and pepper. Marinate the chicken for at least 1 hour before grilling to allow the flavors to develop.

5. Ginger Sesame Marinade

This Asian-inspired marinade is a perfect blend of flavors that will transport you to the tropics. The sweetness of pineapple is complemented by the nuttiness of sesame oil and the spiciness of ginger. To make this marinade, whisk together 1/4 cup of pineapple juice, 2 tablespoons of soy sauce, 1 tablespoon of sesame oil, 1 tablespoon of grated ginger, and a pinch of salt and pepper. Marinate the chicken for at least 2 hours before grilling for maximum flavor.

6. Coconut Curry Marinade

For a truly exotic twist on grilled pineapple chicken, try this coconut curry marinade. The creaminess of coconut milk is enhanced by the aromatic spices of curry powder and cumin. To make this marinade, mix together 1 cup of coconut milk, 2 tablespoons of curry powder, 1 tablespoon of ground cumin, and a pinch of salt. Marinate the chicken for at least 4 hours before grilling to allow the flavors to meld together.

Grilled pineapple chicken is a versatile and crowd-pleasing dish that can be enjoyed on its own or paired with a variety of sides. The marinades mentioned above add depth and complexity to the dish, making it a true delight for your taste buds. So the next time you fire up the grill, give one of these sweet and tangy marinades a try and prepare to be amazed by the explosion of flavors. Happy grilling!
