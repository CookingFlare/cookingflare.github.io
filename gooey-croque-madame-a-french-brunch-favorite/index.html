Gooey Croque Madame: A French Brunch Favorite


If you’re a fan of brunch and French cuisine, then you’re in for a treat with this mouthwatering recipe for Gooey Croque Madame. This classic French dish is a favorite among brunch enthusiasts, and for good reason. With its oozy cheese, rich béchamel sauce, and perfectly cooked egg, it’s the ultimate indulgence for those lazy weekend mornings. So, roll up your sleeves and let’s dive into this delectable recipe that will transport you straight to the streets of Paris.

Ingredients

For the sandwich:

  • 8 slices of thick-cut bread (white or whole wheat)
  • 8 slices of ham
  • 8 slices of Gruyere cheese
  • Dijon mustard, to taste

For the béchamel sauce:

  • 4 tablespoons unsalted butter
  • 4 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • 2 cups whole milk
  • A pinch of nutmeg
  • Salt and pepper, to taste

For the topping:

  • 4 eggs
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • Fresh parsley, chopped (for garnish)

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).

  2. Start by making the béchamel sauce. Melt the butter in a saucepan over medium heat. Once melted, add the flour and whisk continuously for about 2 minutes, until it forms a smooth paste.

  3. Gradually pour in the milk, whisking constantly to avoid any lumps. Continue whisking until the sauce thickens and comes to a gentle boil. Reduce the heat to low, add the nutmeg, salt, and pepper, and let it simmer for 5 minutes, stirring occasionally. Set aside.

  4. Take two slices of bread and spread a thin layer of Dijon mustard on one side of each slice. Place a slice of ham and a slice of Gruyere cheese on top of one slice, and then cover it with the second slice of bread, mustard side down. Repeat this process to make a total of 4 sandwiches.

  5. Heat a non-stick skillet over medium heat. Place the sandwiches in the skillet and cook for about 2 minutes on each side, until golden brown. Remove from the skillet and set aside.

  6. Now, it’s time to assemble the Croque Madame. Place the cooked sandwiches on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper. Spoon the béchamel sauce generously over each sandwich, making sure to cover the top completely.

  7. Carefully crack an egg onto the center of each sandwich. Sprinkle with salt and pepper to taste.

  8. Transfer the baking sheet to the preheated oven and bake for about 10-12 minutes, or until the eggs are cooked to your liking and the cheese is melted and bubbly.

  9. Once cooked, remove the baking sheet from the oven and allow the sandwiches to cool for a few minutes. Sprinkle with fresh parsley for a pop of color and added freshness.

  10. Serve your Gooey Croque Madame sandwiches warm, and get ready to indulge in the ultimate French brunch experience. The combination of the gooey cheese, creamy béchamel sauce, and perfectly cooked egg is simply irresistible.

Conclusion

The Gooey Croque Madame is a French brunch favorite for a reason. Its combination of flavors and textures is a true delight for the taste buds. So, why not bring a taste of France to your brunch table? This recipe is sure to impress your guests and leave them craving for more. Give it a try and let the deliciousness speak for itself. Bon appétit!

Note: Remember to adjust the cooking time if you prefer your eggs more or less cooked. Additionally, feel free to experiment with different types of cheese or add a touch of your favorite herbs to customize the flavor to your liking.
