Picnic Pies: Sweet and Savory Delights


Whether you’re planning a family picnic or a casual outdoor gathering with friends, picnic pies are the perfect addition to your spread. These delightful handheld treats come in both sweet and savory varieties, making them versatile and appealing to a wide range of tastes. In this blog post, we’ll explore some delicious picnic pie recipes that are sure to elevate your outdoor dining experience.

Classic Chicken and Leek Picnic Pies

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups cooked chicken, shredded
  • 1 leek, finely sliced
  • 1 cup chicken broth
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 sheets of puff pastry
  • 1 egg, beaten (for egg wash)

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. In a saucepan, melt the butter over medium heat. Add the leek and cook until softened.
  3. Add the shredded chicken to the saucepan and stir to combine with the leek.
  4. Pour in the chicken broth and heavy cream. Season with salt and pepper, then simmer for 10-15 minutes until the mixture thickens slightly.
  5. Roll out the puff pastry sheets and cut them into squares or circles, depending on your preference.
  6. Spoon the chicken and leek mixture onto the pastry, leaving a border around the edges.
  7. Brush the edges of the pastry with the beaten egg, then place another piece of pastry on top and crimp the edges to seal the pies.
  8. Brush the tops of the pies with the remaining egg wash.
  9. Bake in the preheated oven for 20-25 minutes or until the pastry is golden brown.

These classic chicken and leek picnic pies are a savory delight that will impress your picnic guests. The combination of tender chicken, aromatic leeks, and flaky puff pastry makes for a mouthwatering treat that’s perfect for outdoor dining.

Decadent Chocolate and Raspberry Picnic Pies

Ingredients:

  • 1 1/2 cups fresh raspberries
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 tablespoon cornstarch
  • 1 cup dark chocolate, chopped
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • 2 sheets of shortcrust pastry
  • 1 egg, beaten (for egg wash)

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. In a bowl, combine the fresh raspberries, granulated sugar, and cornstarch. Gently mix until the raspberries are coated.
  3. In a saucepan, heat the heavy cream until just below boiling. Remove from heat and add the chopped dark chocolate. Stir until the chocolate is completely melted and the mixture is smooth.
  4. Roll out the shortcrust pastry sheets and cut them into squares or circles.
  5. Spoon the chocolate mixture onto the pastry, then top with the raspberry mixture.
  6. Brush the edges of the pastry with the beaten egg, then place another piece of pastry on top and crimp the edges to seal the pies.
  7. Brush the tops of the pies with the remaining egg wash.
  8. Bake in the preheated oven for 15-20 minutes or until the pastry is golden brown.

These decadent chocolate and raspberry picnic pies are a sweet indulgence that will satisfy any dessert lover’s cravings. The rich, velvety chocolate combined with the tartness of fresh raspberries creates a flavor sensation that’s perfect for a picnic dessert.

Conclusion

Whether you prefer savory or sweet flavors, picnic pies are a fantastic addition to any outdoor gathering. Their portability, delicious fillings, and versatile nature make them a crowd-pleasing option for picnics, potlucks, and more. Experiment with different fillings and pastry types to create your own signature picnic pies and elevate your outdoor dining experience. So, pack your picnic basket, grab a blanket, and enjoy these delightful sweet and savory picnic pies at your next outdoor adventure!
