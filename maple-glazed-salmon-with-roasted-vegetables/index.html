Maple Glazed Salmon with Roasted Vegetables


There’s something incredibly satisfying about a perfectly cooked piece of salmon glazed in a rich, sweet, and stickiesyrup. Pair that with some tender, caramelized vegetables, and you’ve got yourself a sumptuous meal that’s sure to impress. Today we’re going to share our secret recipe for Maple Glazed Salmon with Roasted Vegetables, a dish that we are confident will soon become your go-to meal for special occasions.

Ingredients:

  • 4 boneless, skin-on salmon fillets
  • 1/2 cup pure maple syrup
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 3 tablespoons apple cider vinegar
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon minced ginger
  • 1 teaspoon toasted sesame oil
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • (For the vegetables - adjust according to your preference)
  • 2 medium heads broccoli
  • 2 large carrots, peeled and sliced
  • 1 red onion, chopped
  • 1 yellow bell pepper, sliced
  • Olive oil
  • Salt and black pepper to taste

Instructions:

We’ll start with the salmon, as it needs to marinate before cooking.

  1. In a bowl, whisk together maple syrup, soy sauce, apple cider vinegar, garlic, ginger, and sesame oil. Season with salt and black pepper to taste.

  2. Place the salmon fillets in a shallow dish or tray, skin side down, and pour the marinade over them. Make sure every piece of salmon is well-coated. Let it sit for about fifteen minutes while you preheat the oven to 425°F (220°C).

Time to prepare the vegetables:

  1. Cut the broccoli into florets, and peel and slice the carrots. Feel free to use any other veggies you like and love – consider adding some sliced mushrooms, pearl onions, or even parsnips.

  2. Place the vegetables on a large baking sheet and drizzle with olive oil. Season with salt and black pepper. Toss well to evenly coat.

  3. Place the baking sheet in the oven and roast until the vegetables are tender and slightly caramelized, about 20 to 25 minutes.

While the vegetables roast, let’s cook the salmon:

  1. Once the oven is hot, place a wire rack over a baking sheet lined with aluminum foil (for easy cleanup). Place the salmon fillets skin side down onto the wire rack.

  2. Pour any remaining marinade over the salmon.

  3. Bake the salmon in the preheated oven for about 12 to 15 minutes or until it reaches an internal temperature of 145°F (or 63°C) in its thickest part. The salmon should be slightly translucent in the center when cut with a knife.

  4. With a spatula, remove the salmon from the oven and immediately brush generously with the remaining maple glaze.

  5. Leave the salmon in the oven for another five minutes to let the glaze do its magic, then remove and let it cool for a few minutes. Serve hot, garnished with some fresh herbs or scallions if you like.

Our Maple Glazed Salmon with Roasted Vegetables is ready to be enjoyed! This delectable combo of rich, smoky, and sweet flavors is bound to become a fan favorite. Whether you’re looking for a delicious main course that’s healthful and easy to prepare, or simply hoping to impress your loved one, this dish fits the bill. Try it out, and soon enough, it’ll become one of your favorite go-to recipes, too.
