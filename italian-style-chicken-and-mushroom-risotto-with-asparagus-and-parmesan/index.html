Italian-Style Chicken and Mushroom Risotto with Asparagus and Parmesan


If you’re looking for a comforting yet elegant dish that’s bursting with flavors, this Italian-style chicken and mushroom risotto with asparagus and Parmesan is a must-try. The creamy texture of the risotto, combined with the earthy flavors of mushrooms, the freshness of asparagus, and the savory notes of Parmesan, creates a truly delightful culinary experience. Let’s dive into the details of this delicious recipe.

Ingredients

  • 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1 cup Arborio rice
  • 4 cups chicken broth
  • 1 cup dry white wine
  • 1 shallot, finely chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 8 oz cremini mushrooms, sliced
  • 1 bunch asparagus, trimmed and cut into 1-inch pieces
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley for garnish

Instructions

  1. Begin by preparing the chicken. Season the chicken breasts with salt and pepper. In a large skillet, heat 1 tablespoon of olive oil over medium-high heat. Add the chicken breasts and cook until golden brown and cooked through, about 6-7 minutes per side. Once cooked, transfer the chicken to a cutting board and let it rest for a few minutes before slicing it into thin strips.

  2. In a separate large, deep pan, heat the remaining tablespoon of olive oil over medium heat. Add the chopped shallot and minced garlic, and sauté until fragrant, about 2 minutes.

  3. Add the Arborio rice to the pan and cook, stirring constantly, for 2-3 minutes or until the rice becomes translucent around the edges.

  4. Pour in the white wine and cook, stirring, until the liquid has been absorbed by the rice.

  5. Begin adding the chicken broth, one ladle at a time, stirring constantly and allowing each addition of broth to be absorbed before adding the next. Continue this process until the rice is creamy and al dente, about 18-20 minutes.

  6. While the risotto is cooking, in a separate skillet, melt the butter over medium heat. Add the sliced mushrooms and sauté until they are golden brown and tender, about 5-6 minutes. Season with salt and pepper to taste.

  7. Once the risotto is ready, stir in the sautéed mushrooms, sliced chicken, and grated Parmesan cheese. Mix well to combine all the ingredients.

  8. In a pot of boiling salted water, blanch the asparagus for 2-3 minutes until they are bright green and tender-crisp. Drain and immediately transfer the asparagus to a bowl of ice water to stop the cooking process. Drain again and set aside.

  9. Gently fold the blanched asparagus into the risotto, reserving a few pieces for garnish.

  10. Serve the Italian-style chicken and mushroom risotto with asparagus and Parmesan in individual bowls, garnished with the remaining asparagus pieces and a sprinkle of fresh parsley.

Conclusion

This Italian-style chicken and mushroom risotto with asparagus and Parmesan is a delightful dish that brings together the best of Italian flavors. It’s perfect for a cozy dinner at home or for impressing guests at a special gathering. The creamy texture of the risotto, the tender chicken, the earthy mushrooms, the vibrant asparagus, and the rich Parmesan all work together to create a symphony of tastes that will leave everyone wanting more. Give this recipe a try and savor the magic of Italian cuisine right in your own kitchen!
