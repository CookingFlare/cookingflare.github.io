Savory One Pot BBQ Pulled Chicken


If you’re looking for a delicious and easy meal that will satisfy the whole family, then you’ve come to the right place. This savory one pot BBQ pulled chicken recipe is a game-changer. It’s packed with flavor, incredibly tender, and best of all, it’s a breeze to make. Whether you’re hosting a backyard barbecue, a casual get-together, or simply want to treat yourself to a comforting meal, this recipe is sure to become a staple in your kitchen.

Ingredients

  • 2 lbs boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1 cup BBQ sauce
  • 1/2 cup ketchup
  • 1/4 cup brown sugar
  • 1/4 cup apple cider vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 tablespoon Dijon mustard
  • 1 teaspoon smoked paprika
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon onion powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon cayenne pepper (optional for a kick)
  • Hamburger buns, for serving

Instructions

  1. In a large pot or Dutch oven, combine the BBQ sauce, ketchup, brown sugar, apple cider vinegar, Worcestershire sauce, Dijon mustard, smoked paprika, garlic powder, onion powder, black pepper, salt, and cayenne pepper (if using). Stir the mixture until well combined.

  2. Add the chicken breasts to the pot and turn them in the sauce to coat evenly. Bring the mixture to a simmer over medium heat.

  3. Once the sauce begins to simmer, reduce the heat to low, cover the pot, and let the chicken cook for 1.5 to 2 hours, or until it is tender and easily shreds with a fork. Be sure to stir the chicken occasionally to ensure it cooks evenly and doesn’t stick to the bottom of the pot.

  4. Once the chicken is fully cooked and tender, use two forks to shred it directly in the pot, mixing it with the flavorful sauce.

  5. Allow the shredded chicken to simmer in the sauce for an additional 10-15 minutes to absorb all the delicious flavors.

  6. Serve the savory BBQ pulled chicken on hamburger buns and enjoy the mouthwatering goodness.

Tips and Serving Suggestions

  • For an extra smoky flavor, you can use hickory or mesquite-flavored BBQ sauce.
  • This pulled chicken is perfect for topping baked potatoes, nachos, or even pizza for a unique twist.
  • Feel free to customize the seasoning to suit your taste preferences. Add more heat with extra cayenne pepper, or adjust the sweetness by tweaking the brown sugar and BBQ sauce ratio.

Conclusion

This savory one pot BBQ pulled chicken recipe is a real winner when it comes to easy, crowd-pleasing meals. The tender, flavorful chicken paired with the convenience of a one pot cooking method makes it a go-to for busy weeknights or laid-back weekends. So, gather your ingredients, fire up the stove, and get ready to savor every delicious bite of this irresistible dish. Your taste buds will thank you!
