Homemade Nut-Free Guacamole Recipe


Guacamole is a versatile and delicious dip that is perfect for any occasion. Whether you’re hosting a party, having a snack, or simply craving something flavorful, guacamole never disappoints. However, for those with nut allergies, store-bought guacamole can be a risky choice due to potential cross-contamination. But fear not, because I have the perfect homemade nut-free guacamole recipe for you to enjoy without any worries.

Ingredients

  • 3 ripe avocados
  • 1 medium tomato, diced
  • 1/2 cup finely chopped red onion
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh cilantro
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 jalapeño pepper, seeded and minced
  • 1 lime, juiced
  • 1/2 teaspoon cumin
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Avocados: Cut the avocados in half, remove the pits, and scoop out the flesh into a mixing bowl.

  2. Mash the Avocado: Use a fork to mash the avocados to your desired consistency. Some people prefer chunky guacamole, while others like it smooth. It’s all about personal preference!

  3. Add the Flavor: Add the diced tomato, chopped red onion, minced garlic, jalapeño pepper, and chopped cilantro to the bowl with the mashed avocado.

  4. Season and Mix: Squeeze the juice of one lime over the mixture. Add the cumin, salt, and pepper. Gently mix all the ingredients together until well combined.

  5. Taste and Adjust: Give the guacamole a taste and adjust the seasonings to your liking. You can add more lime juice, salt, or pepper as needed.

  6. Chill and Serve: For the best flavor, cover the guacamole with plastic wrap directly on the surface to prevent browning. Refrigerate for at least 30 minutes before serving.

Tips for Perfect Guacamole

  • Use ripe avocados for the best flavor and texture. They should yield to gentle pressure when ripe.
  • If you want to make the guacamole in advance, store it in an airtight container with plastic wrap directly on the surface to minimize browning.
  • Adjust the amount of jalapeño based on your spice preference. For a milder guacamole, remove the seeds and membranes from the pepper.

Serving Suggestions

This nut-free guacamole is incredibly versatile and can be enjoyed in various ways. Here are a few serving suggestions to inspire you:

  • Serve as a dip with tortilla chips, pita chips, or vegetable sticks for a classic snack.
  • Use as a topping for tacos, burritos, or nachos to add a burst of fresh flavor.
  • Spread on toast or sandwiches for a creamy and vibrant kick.
  • Pair with grilled meats or seafood for a refreshing accompaniment.

Final Thoughts

Homemade nut-free guacamole is a simple, wholesome, and allergy-friendly option that everyone can enjoy. By making it at home, you have complete control over the ingredients, ensuring a safe and delicious experience for those with nut allergies. So, next time you’re craving guacamole, whip up a batch of this nut-free version and savor every bite without any worries. Enjoy!
