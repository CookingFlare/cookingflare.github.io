Low-Carb Quiche Recipes for a Delicious Breakfast


When it comes to breakfast, few dishes are as versatile and satisfying as quiche. Traditionally made with a pastry crust, quiche can be heavy on carbs, which might not align with everyone’s dietary preferences. Luckily, low-carb quiche recipes are a fantastic alternative that allows you to enjoy all the delicious flavors without the guilt. Whether you’re following a ketogenic diet, watching your carbohydrate intake, or simply looking for a nutritious breakfast option, these low-carb quiche recipes will delight your taste buds and keep you feeling full.

The Beauty of Low-Carb Quiche

Low-carb quiche is not only delicious but also incredibly adaptable. You can fill it with your favorite vegetables, proteins, and cheeses, making it a perfect dish for using up leftovers. The best part? These recipes are easy to prepare, making them ideal for busy mornings or meal prep for the week ahead.

Let’s dive into some delightful low-carb quiche recipes that will elevate your breakfast game!

Classic Low-Carb Quiche Lorraine

Ingredients

  • For the crust:

    • 1 cup almond flour
    • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
    • 1/4 cup butter, melted
    • 1/2 teaspoon salt
    • 1 egg

  • For the filling:

    • 6 large eggs
    • 1 cup heavy cream
    • 1 cup cooked bacon, crumbled
    • 1 cup shredded Swiss cheese
    • 1/4 cup chopped green onions
    • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. Prepare the crust: In a mixing bowl, combine almond flour, grated Parmesan, melted butter, salt, and egg. Mix until a dough forms. Press the mixture into the bottom and up the sides of a 9-inch pie dish. Bake for 10 minutes until lightly golden.
  3. Make the filling: In another bowl, whisk together the eggs and heavy cream. Stir in the crumbled bacon, shredded Swiss cheese, chopped green onions, salt, and pepper.
  4. Combine and bake: Pour the filling into the pre-baked crust. Bake for 30-35 minutes or until the filling is set and the top is lightly browned. Cool for a few minutes before slicing.

Enjoy!

This Classic Low-Carb Quiche Lorraine is rich and indulgent, perfect for a weekend brunch or a special occasion. Pair it with a side salad for a complete meal.

Spinach and Feta Low-Carb Quiche

Ingredients

  • For the crust:

    • 1 cup coconut flour
    • 1/4 cup butter, softened
    • 2 eggs
    • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
    • 1/2 teaspoon salt

  • For the filling:

    • 6 large eggs
    • 1 cup heavy cream
    • 2 cups fresh spinach, chopped
    • 1 cup crumbled feta cheese
    • 1/2 cup diced bell peppers
    • 1/4 cup chopped parsley
    • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. Prepare the crust: In a mixing bowl, combine coconut flour, softened butter, eggs, garlic powder, and salt. Mix well and press into a greased 9-inch pie dish. Bake for 10 minutes.
  3. Make the filling: Whisk together the eggs and heavy cream in a separate bowl. Stir in the chopped spinach, feta cheese, diced bell peppers, parsley, salt, and pepper.
  4. Combine and bake: Pour the filling into the pre-baked crust. Bake for 30-35 minutes or until the filling is firm and golden. Allow it to cool before slicing.

Enjoy!

This Spinach and Feta Low-Carb Quiche is bursting with flavor and nutrients. It’s a great way to sneak in some greens at breakfast while savoring the tanginess of feta cheese.

Mushroom and Cheese Low-Carb Quiche

Ingredients

  • For the crust:

    • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
    • 2 ounces cream cheese, softened
    • 1 large egg
    • 1/2 cup almond flour
    • 1/4 teaspoon salt

  • For the filling:

    • 6 large eggs
    • 1 cup heavy cream
    • 1 cup mushrooms, sliced
    • 1 cup shredded cheddar cheese
    • 1/4 cup chopped chives
    • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. Prepare the crust: In a microwave-safe bowl, combine mozzarella cheese and cream cheese. Microwave on high for 30 seconds, then stir until melted and smooth. Mix in the egg, almond flour, and salt until well combined. Press the mixture into a greased 9-inch pie dish and bake for 10 minutes.
  3. Make the filling: In a bowl, whisk together the eggs and heavy cream. Sauté the sliced mushrooms in a pan until tender, then add them to the egg mixture along with the cheddar cheese, chives, salt, and pepper.
  4. Combine and bake: Pour the filling into the pre-baked crust. Bake for 30-35 minutes or until set and golden on top. Let it cool for a few minutes before serving.

Enjoy!

This Mushroom and Cheese Low-Carb Quiche is earthy and satisfying, making it a favorite among mushroom lovers. Serve it warm or at room temperature for a delightful breakfast.

Broccoli and Cheddar Low-Carb Quiche

Ingredients

  • For the crust:

    • 1 1/2 cups almond flour
    • 1/4 cup coconut oil, melted
    • 2 large eggs
    • 1 teaspoon Italian seasoning
    • 1/2 teaspoon salt

  • For the filling:

    • 6 large eggs
    • 1 cup heavy cream
    • 2 cups broccoli florets, steamed and chopped
    • 1 cup shredded cheddar cheese
    • 1/4 cup diced onion
    • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. Prepare the crust: In a bowl, mix almond flour, melted coconut oil, eggs, Italian seasoning, and salt until a dough forms. Press into a greased 9-inch pie dish. Bake for 10 minutes.
  3. Make the filling: In another bowl, whisk the eggs and heavy cream. Stir in the steamed broccoli, cheddar cheese, diced onion, salt, and pepper.
  4. Combine and bake: Pour the filling into the pre-baked crust. Bake for 30-35 minutes or until the quiche is set. Allow cooling before slicing.

Enjoy!

This Broccoli and Cheddar Low-Carb Quiche is a fantastic way to incorporate vegetables into your breakfast. The combination of broccoli and cheddar creates a comforting and wholesome dish.

Tips for Perfect Low-Carb Quiche

  1. Experiment with Fillings: Don’t be afraid to get creative! You can use different vegetables, meats, and cheeses based on your preferences. Zucchini, bell peppers, asparagus, and sausage are all great options.
  2. Make it Ahead: Quiches are perfect for meal prep. You can make a big batch at the beginning of the week and store individual slices in the fridge for quick breakfasts.
  3. Storage: Keep leftover quiche in an airtight container in the fridge for up to 4 days. Reheat in the oven or microwave before serving.
  4. Serve with Sides: Pair your quiche with avocado slices, a fresh salad, or a dollop of sour cream for added flavor and nutrition.

Conclusion

Low-carb quiche recipes are a delightful way to start your day without sacrificing flavor or nutrition. With endless variations and combinations, you can tailor each recipe to suit your taste. Whether you prefer the classic flavors of Quiche Lorraine or the wholesome goodness of broccoli and cheddar, these recipes are sure to satisfy your morning cravings. So grab your ingredients, preheat that oven, and get ready to enjoy a delicious, low-carb breakfast that will keep you fueled throughout the day!
