Slow Cooker Beef Stroganoff: A Creamy Delight


As the chill of winter sets in, there's no better way to warm up on a cold evening than with a hearty, comforting dish that's been simmering away in the slow cooker all day. Slow Cooker Beef Stroganoff is just that kind of recipe - a creamy, savory, and satisfying meal that's perfect for a family dinner or a cozy night in with friends. The Story Behind the Recipe Traditional Beef Stroganoff is a Russian dish that's been a staple of fine dining for centuries. Named after Count Pavel Stroganov, a 19th-century Russian aristocrat, this dish was first created as a simple, flavorful meal for the Count's dinner parties. However, over the years, it has evolved to suit the tastes of various cuisines around the world, and our slow cooker version is a classic twist on the original. Slow Cooker Beef Stroganoff Recipe This recipe is easy to make and requires minimal preparation, making it perfect for a weeknight dinner or a quick weekend meal. Here's what you'll need: Ingredients

1 pound beef strips (sirloin or ribeye work well) 1 onion, sliced 2 cloves of garlic, minced 1 cup beef broth 1 tablespoon tomato paste 2 tablespoons Dijon mustard 1 teaspoon paprika 1/2 teaspoon salt 1/4 teaspoon black pepper 8 ounces sour cream 1 cup mushrooms, sliced 1 cup noodles (wide or narrow egg noodles work best) Chopped fresh parsley or chives, for garnish

Instructions

Prepare the Beef

In a large bowl, whisk together the beef broth, tomato paste, mustard, paprika, salt, and pepper. Add the sliced beef and marinate for at least 30 minutes, or up to 2 hours in the refrigerator.

Sauté the Aromatics

In a large skillet, heat a tablespoon of oil over medium-high heat. Add the sliced onion and cook until caramelized, stirring occasionally, about 8-10 minutes. Add the minced garlic and cook for another minute, until fragrant.

Transfer to the Slow Cooker

In the slow cooker, add the cooked beef, sautéed onions and garlic, and sliced mushrooms. Stir to combine.

Cook and Stew

Cook on low for 8-10 hours or high for 4-6 hours. The beef should be tender and fall apart easily.

Finish with Sour Cream and Noodles

Stir in the sour cream and cook for an additional 10 minutes, until heated through. Cook the noodles according to package instructions and stir into the slow cooker. Serve hot, garnished with chopped parsley or chives. Tips and Variations

For a creamier sauce, add more sour cream or a splash of heavy cream. To add a bit of spice, stir in some Dijon mustard or red pepper flakes. Substitute the noodles with rice,spirals, or even roasted vegetables for a low-carb option.

Nutritional Information (approximate) Per serving (serves 4-6):

Calories: 450 Protein: 35g Fat: 25g Saturated fat: 12g Cholesterol: 60mg Carbohydrates: 25g Fiber: 2g Sugar: 5g Sodium: 450mg

Freezer-Friendly and Meal Prep Options This recipe freezes beautifully and is perfect for meal prep. Simply cook the beef and sauce, portion it out into individual servings, and store in the freezer for up to 3 months. Reheat and serve over noodles for a quick, satisfying dinner. Conclusion Slow Cooker Beef Stroganoff is a rich and satisfying meal that's perfect for a chilly evening or a busy day. With its tender beef, creamy sauce, and fluffy noodles, it's a dish that will become a family favorite in no time. And with its simple preparation and long cooking time, it's perfect for meal prep or a convenient weeknight dinner.
