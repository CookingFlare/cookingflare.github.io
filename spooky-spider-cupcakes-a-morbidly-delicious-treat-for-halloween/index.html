Spooky Spider Cupcakes: A Morbidly Delicious Treat for Halloween


Halloween is a magical time for decorations, costumes, and of course, sweet treats! If you’re looking for a delightful way to frighten and delight your friends and family, look no further than the Spooky Spider Cupcakes. These eerie black frosted cupcakes are not only a feast for the eye but also an explosion of flavor in every bite. As you dive into this morbidly delicious recipe, you shall feel as if you’ve slipped into the abyss of a Halloween wonderland.

Ingredients

To get started on your Spooky Spider Cupcakes, gather these essential ingredients:

  • 1 box of devil’s food cake mix
  • 1 cup of water
  • 1/2 cup of vegetable oil
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 tsp of vanilla extract
  • 2 cups of powdered sugar
  • 2-3 tbsp of black food coloring
  • 1 cup of semi-sweet chocolate chips
  • 12 Nielsen-Massey Black Licorice Wheels
  • 1 tub of vanilla buttercream frosting

Directions

Step 1: Bake the Cupcakes

  1. Begin by preheating your oven to 350°F (175°C) and preparing a cupcake tin with liners.
  2. In a large mixing bowl, combine the devil’s food cake mix, water, vegetable oil, eggs, and vanilla extract.
  3. Mix according to the box instructions until the batter is smooth and well combined.
  4. Carefully pour the batter into the cupcake liners, filling them 2/3 of the way full.
  5. Place the lined cupcake tin in the oven and bake for 18-20 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center of a cupcake comes out clean.
  6. Once baked, remove the cupcakes from the oven and allow them to cool completely on a wire rack.

Step 2: Prepare the Black Frosting

  1. In a separate large mixing bowl, sift together the powdered sugar to eliminate any lumps.
  2. Add 2 tablespoons of black food coloring to the powdered sugar and mix until it forms a smooth, dark paste.
  3. Gradually incorporate the black paste into the vanilla buttercream frosting using a stand or hand mixer until the desired black color is achieved.

Step 3: Make the Spider Legs

  1. Melt the semi-sweet chocolate chips in a microwave-safe bowl in 20-second intervals, stirring frequently until smooth.
  2. Take a small piece of the melted chocolate and use a toothpick to create eight thin lines on a piece of wax paper.
  3. Allow the chocolate legs to cool and harden.

Step 4: Assemble the Cupcakes

  1. Attach a star-tip frosting nozzle to a pastry bag, then fill the bag with the black frosting.
  2. Using your fingers, carefully break the hardened chocolate legs into smaller pieces.
  3. Pipe a circle of black frosting around the edge of each cooled cupcake to create the spider web.
  4. Place the cupcake in a spider silhouette cookie cutter (or a created shape using a pattern on wax paper) to create a true web look.
  5. Arrange the chocolate legs around the cupcake where they naturally appear on a spider’s body.
  6. Attach two black licorice wheels to the top of the cupcake legs using a small amount of frosting to act as the spider’s body and head.
  7. Your spooky spider cupcakes are ready to serve and devour!

Enjoy These Spooky Spider Cupcakes

These Spooky Spider Cupcakes are a delight in both presentation and taste, perfect for horror movie nights, Halloween parties, or an eerie treat for your little monsters. With their dangerously good combination of flavors and enchanting appearance, these treats are sure to become the favorite addition to your Halloween repertoire. So grab your cauldron whisk and bake these creepy, crawly, black-frosted spider delights for a truly witching time! Happy Halloween!
