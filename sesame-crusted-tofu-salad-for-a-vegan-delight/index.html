Sesame Crusted Tofu Salad for a Vegan Delight


If you’re looking for a delicious and nutritious vegan salad that will leave you feeling satisfied, look no further than this Sesame Crusted Tofu Salad. Packed with flavor and texture, this salad is a delightful combination of crispy tofu, fresh vegetables, and a tangy sesame dressing. It’s the perfect dish for a light lunch or a refreshing dinner.

Ingredients

  • 1 block of firm tofu, drained and pressed
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons cornstarch
  • 2 tablespoons sesame seeds
  • 4 cups mixed salad greens
  • 1 cucumber, thinly sliced
  • 1 carrot, julienned
  • 1 red bell pepper, thinly sliced
  • 1/4 cup fresh cilantro, chopped
  • 1/4 cup roasted peanuts, chopped

Sesame Dressing

  • 2 tablespoons tahini
  • 2 tablespoons rice vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon maple syrup
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon sesame oil
  • 1 garlic clove, minced
  • 1 teaspoon grated fresh ginger
  • 2 tablespoons water

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 400°F (200°C) and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.

  2. Cut the tofu into 1-inch cubes and place them in a shallow bowl. Drizzle the soy sauce over the tofu and gently toss to coat. Let it marinate for 10 minutes.

  3. In a separate shallow bowl, combine the cornstarch and sesame seeds. Mix well.

  4. Take each tofu cube and roll it in the cornstarch and sesame seed mixture, pressing gently to adhere. Place the coated tofu cubes on the prepared baking sheet.

  5. Bake the tofu in the preheated oven for 15 minutes, turning halfway through, until golden brown and crispy.

  6. While the tofu is baking, prepare the salad. In a large bowl, combine the mixed salad greens, cucumber, carrot, red bell pepper, cilantro, and roasted peanuts. Toss gently to combine.

  7. In a small bowl, whisk together all the ingredients for the sesame dressing until smooth and creamy.

  8. Once the tofu is done baking, remove it from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes.

  9. Add the crispy tofu to the salad and drizzle the sesame dressing over the top. Toss gently to coat everything evenly.

  10. Serve the Sesame Crusted Tofu Salad immediately and enjoy the burst of flavors and textures in every bite.

Nutritional Information

  • Calories: 220
  • Fat: 12g
  • Carbohydrates: 16g
  • Protein: 14g
  • Fiber: 4g

This Sesame Crusted Tofu Salad is not only delicious but also incredibly nutritious. The crispy tofu provides a good source of plant-based protein, while the fresh vegetables add fiber, vitamins, and minerals to your meal. The sesame dressing adds a creamy and tangy element that ties all the flavors together. It’s a vegan delight that will leave you feeling energized and satisfied.

So, why wait? Give this recipe a try and experience the wonders of Sesame Crusted Tofu Salad for yourself. It’s a simple yet flavorful dish that will impress your taste buds and provide a healthy option for your vegan lifestyle. Enjoy!
