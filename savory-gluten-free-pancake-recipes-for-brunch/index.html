Savory Gluten-Free Pancake Recipes for Brunch


Who says pancakes are only for breakfast? With these savory gluten-free pancake recipes, you can enjoy a delicious and satisfying brunch that will leave your taste buds wanting more. Whether you follow a gluten-free diet or simply enjoy exploring new flavors, these recipes are sure to impress.

1. Spinach and Feta Pancakes

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup gluten-free flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1 cup fresh spinach, chopped
  • 1/2 cup crumbled feta cheese
  • 2 green onions, thinly sliced
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup milk
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil

Instructions:

  1. In a large mixing bowl, combine the gluten-free flour, baking powder, salt, and black pepper.
  2. Add the chopped spinach, crumbled feta cheese, and green onions to the dry ingredients. Mix well.
  3. In a separate bowl, whisk together the eggs, milk, and olive oil. Pour the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients and stir until just combined.
  4. Heat a non-stick skillet over medium heat. Spoon about 1/4 cup of batter onto the skillet for each pancake.
  5. Cook until the edges are set and bubbles form on the surface of the pancake. Flip and cook for an additional 2-3 minutes, or until golden brown.
  6. Serve the spinach and feta pancakes warm with a dollop of Greek yogurt and a sprinkle of fresh herbs.

2. Smoked Salmon and Dill Pancakes

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup gluten-free pancake mix
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh dill
  • 4 ounces smoked salmon, thinly sliced
  • 1/4 cup cream cheese
  • Lemon wedges, for serving

Instructions:

  1. In a mixing bowl, combine the gluten-free pancake mix, baking powder, salt, black pepper, and chopped dill.
  2. Add enough water to the dry ingredients to make a thick batter. Mix until well combined.
  3. Heat a non-stick skillet over medium heat. Spoon about 1/4 cup of batter onto the skillet for each pancake.
  4. Cook until the edges are set and bubbles form on the surface. Flip and cook for an additional 2-3 minutes, or until golden brown.
  5. Once cooked, spread a thin layer of cream cheese on each pancake. Top with a slice of smoked salmon and a squeeze of fresh lemon juice.
  6. Roll up the pancakes and serve warm. Garnish with additional dill, if desired.

3. Mushroom and Herb Pancakes

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup gluten-free flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1 cup sliced mushrooms
  • 2 tablespoons chopped fresh herbs (such as parsley, thyme, or rosemary)
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup milk
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil

Instructions:

  1. In a large mixing bowl, combine the gluten-free flour, baking powder, salt, and black pepper.
  2. Add the sliced mushrooms and chopped fresh herbs to the dry ingredients. Mix well.
  3. In a separate bowl, whisk together the eggs, milk, and olive oil. Pour the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients and stir until just combined.
  4. Heat a non-stick skillet over medium heat. Spoon about 1/4 cup of batter onto the skillet for each pancake.
  5. Cook until the edges are set and bubbles form on the surface. Flip and cook for an additional 2-3 minutes, or until golden brown.
  6. Serve the mushroom and herb pancakes warm with a drizzle of balsamic glaze and a sprinkle of grated Parmesan cheese.

Conclusion

These savory gluten-free pancake recipes offer a delightful twist to traditional brunch fare. Whether you choose the spinach and feta, smoked salmon and dill, or mushroom and herb variation, you’re in for a flavorful and satisfying meal. Enjoy these pancakes with your favorite toppings and savor the deliciousness of gluten-free brunch.
