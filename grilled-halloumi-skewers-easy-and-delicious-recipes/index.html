Grilled Halloumi Skewers: Easy and Delicious Recipes


If you’re a fan of grilling and looking to add a new twist to your BBQ menu, then grilled halloumi skewers are a must-try! These easy and delicious recipes are perfect for summer gatherings, backyard parties, or even a simple weeknight dinner.

Why Choose Halloumi?

Halloumi is a semi-hard cheese that originated in Cyprus. It is known for its unique texture and high melting point, which makes it ideal for grilling. The cheese has a salty and tangy flavor that pairs well with a variety of ingredients, making it a versatile choice for skewers.

Recipe 1: Mediterranean Halloumi Skewers

Ingredients:

  • 250g halloumi cheese, cut into cubes
  • 1 red bell pepper, cut into chunks
  • 1 yellow bell pepper, cut into chunks
  • 1 red onion, cut into wedges
  • 10 cherry tomatoes
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your grill to medium-high heat.
  2. In a large bowl, combine the olive oil, lemon juice, minced garlic, dried oregano, salt, and pepper. Mix well.
  3. Add the halloumi cubes, bell peppers, red onion, and cherry tomatoes to the bowl. Toss gently to coat the ingredients with the marinade.
  4. Thread the marinated halloumi and vegetables onto skewers, alternating between the ingredients.
  5. Place the skewers on the preheated grill and cook for about 4-5 minutes per side, or until the halloumi is lightly charred and the vegetables are tender.
  6. Remove the skewers from the grill and let them cool for a few minutes before serving.

These Mediterranean halloumi skewers are bursting with flavors and are perfect for a healthy and satisfying meal. Serve them with a side of tzatziki sauce and a fresh salad for a complete and delicious summer feast.

Recipe 2: Halloumi and Vegetable Skewers with Pesto

Ingredients:

  • 250g halloumi cheese, cut into cubes
  • 1 zucchini, sliced into rounds
  • 1 yellow squash, sliced into rounds
  • 1 red onion, cut into wedges
  • 10 cherry tomatoes
  • 2 tablespoons pesto sauce
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your grill to medium-high heat.
  2. In a small bowl, combine the pesto sauce, olive oil, salt, and pepper. Mix well.
  3. Place the halloumi cubes, zucchini slices, yellow squash slices, red onion wedges, and cherry tomatoes in a large bowl. Pour the pesto mixture over the ingredients and toss gently to coat them evenly.
  4. Thread the marinated halloumi and vegetables onto skewers, alternating between the ingredients.
  5. Grill the skewers for about 3-4 minutes per side, or until the halloumi is golden brown and the vegetables are tender.
  6. Remove the skewers from the grill and let them rest for a few minutes before serving.

These halloumi and vegetable skewers with pesto are a delightful combination of flavors. The pesto adds a burst of freshness to the grilled halloumi and vegetables, making this dish a standout at any BBQ gathering.

Recipe 3: Halloumi and Pineapple Skewers with Teriyaki Glaze

Ingredients:

  • 250g halloumi cheese, cut into cubes
  • 1 cup pineapple chunks
  • 2 tablespoons teriyaki sauce
  • 1 tablespoon honey
  • 1 tablespoon sesame oil
  • 1 tablespoon sesame seeds

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your grill to medium-high heat.
  2. In a small bowl, whisk together the teriyaki sauce, honey, sesame oil, and sesame seeds.
  3. Thread the halloumi cubes and pineapple chunks onto skewers, alternating between the ingredients.
  4. Brush the teriyaki glaze over the skewers, making sure to coat them evenly.
  5. Place the skewers on the preheated grill and cook for about 3-4 minutes per side, or until the halloumi is nicely charred and the pineapple is caramelized.
  6. Remove the skewers from the grill and let them cool for a couple of minutes before serving.

These halloumi and pineapple skewers with teriyaki glaze offer a delightful combination of sweet and savory flavors. The teriyaki glaze adds a tangy and slightly sweet note to the grilled halloumi and pineapple, making it a crowd-pleasing option for any BBQ or summer party.

Conclusion

Grilled halloumi skewers are not only easy to make but also incredibly delicious. Whether you prefer a Mediterranean twist, a pesto-infused dish, or a sweet and savory combination, these recipes will surely satisfy your taste buds. So fire up your grill, gather your favorite ingredients, and get ready to enjoy these mouthwatering halloumi skewers at your next outdoor gathering.
