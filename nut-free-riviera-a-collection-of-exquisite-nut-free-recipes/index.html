Nut-Free Riviera: A Collection of Exquisite Nut-Free Recipes


Nuts might be considered as essential components in many culinary cuisines, but for those with an allergy or a simple preference to avoid it, the nut-free diet can sometimes feel overwhelming. Especially when you are craving rich Mediterranean and Riviera-inspired flavors, finding nut-free recipes could pose a challenge. Today, we are bringing you a collection of exquisite nut-free Riviera recipes, which will ensure you can indulge in the luxurious flavors of the Mediterranean without compromising your dietary restrictions.

  1. Warm Farro Salad with Tomato and Feta Cheese

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup (190 g) farro
  • 2 cups (480 ml) of water or vegetable broth
  • 3 cups mixed cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 2 tablespoons of extra virgin olive oil
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/4 cup (15 g) chopped fresh basil
  • 1/4 cup (15 g) chopped fresh parsley
  • 6 ounces (170 g) crumbled feta cheese
  • Salt and freshly ground black pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Rinse the farro under cold water and set aside.

  2. In a large pot, bring water or vegetable broth to a low boil, add farro, and cook for about 30-40 minutes or until tender.

  3. Let it cool to room temperature then place it in a large serving bowl.

  4. Heat the olive oil in a medium-sized skillet over medium heat. Add minced garlic and cook until fragrant (about 2 minutes). Do not let it burn!

  5. Add cherry tomatoes to the skillet and cook for about 10 minutes until they soften. Season to taste with salt and pepper.

  6. Combine the cooked farro, sautéed tomatoes, and garlic, fresh herbs to the same serving bowl, and gently toss.

  7. Lastly, add crumbled Feta cheese, and gently mix to combine.

  8. Enjoy the warm, nut-free Mediterranean flavors of this delicious dish!

  9. Pan-Seared Sardines with Citrus and Olive Salad

Ingredients:

  • 1 tablespoon of extra virgin olive oil
  • 4 fresh sardines (about 4-5 oz each), cleaned and gutted
  • Salt and freshly ground black pepper
  • Juice and zest of 1 lemon
  • 1/4 cup (15 g) chopped fresh parsley
  • 1/4 cup (15 g) chopped fresh dill

Instructions:

  1. Pat the sardines dry using a paper towel. Heat the olive oil in a heavy-bottomed skillet over medium-high heat. Season both sides of the sardines generously with salt and pepper.

  2. When the oil is hot, add the sardines in the skillet (you may need to cook them in batches in order not to overcrowd the pan). Cook the sardines for about 3-4 minutes on each side or until golden and crispy.

  3. In the meantime, in a separate small bowl, combine lemon juice, zest, chopped parsley, and dill. Make sure to mix well.

  4. Remove the sardines from the skillet and let them rest on a plate while you arrange the salad around them. Spoon the citrus and olive salad over the sardines and serve immediately.

  5. Creamy Zucchini and Mint Soup

Ingredients:

  • 1 tablespoon of extra virgin olive oil
  • 2 medium zucchini (about 2-3 cups), roughly chopped
  • 1 cup (240 ml) of vegetable broth
  • 1 cup (240 ml) of unsweetened almond milk
  • 1 tablespoon fresh mint leaves, finely chopped
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper
  • 1/4 cup (15 g) chopped fresh basil for garnish
  • A drizzle of extra virgin olive oil for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Heat the olive oil in a large pot over medium heat. Add the chopped zucchini and cook for about 5-7 minutes.
  2. Pour the vegetable broth into the pot and bring to a low boil. Cook for another 10 minutes, or until the zucchini becomes tender.
  3. Remove the pot from heat and let it cool slightly before transferring the contents to a blender. Add the unsweetened almond milk, mint leaves, salt, and pepper.
  4. Blend until smooth and creamy, being careful not to overfill the blender.
  5. Taste and adjust the seasoning as necessary. Once the soup has reached your desired flavor profile, pour it back into the pot and warm gently over low heat.
  6. Once warmed, serve the soup in bowls. Garnish each bowl with chopped fresh basil and a drizzle of extra virgin olive oil.

Conclusion

There you have it – exquisite and delightful nut-free Riviera recipes to indulge in without compromising your taste buds. These dishes are as diverse as they are delicious, offering you not only the flavors of the Mediterranean coast but also catering to those with nut allergies.

Now, you are not limited to missing out on classic Riviera and Mediterranean flavors. Enjoy this collection of superb, mouth-watering, and nut-free recipes that come with a touch of the Mediterranean paradise right in your kitchen. Cheers to a new realm of culinary possibilities!
