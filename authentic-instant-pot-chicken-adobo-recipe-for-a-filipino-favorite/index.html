Authentic Instant Pot Chicken Adobo Recipe for a Filipino Favorite


Chicken Adobo is a classic Filipino dish that is loved by many for its rich and flavorful taste. Traditionally, it is cooked slowly in a pot, allowing the chicken to absorb all the delicious flavors of the sauce. However, with the convenience of the Instant Pot, you can now enjoy this mouthwatering dish in a fraction of the time. In this recipe, we will show you how to make an authentic Instant Pot Chicken Adobo that will surely satisfy your cravings.

Ingredients

  • 1.5 kg chicken, cut into serving pieces
  • 1/2 cup soy sauce
  • 1/2 cup vinegar
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 onion, sliced
  • 2 bay leaves
  • 1 teaspoon whole peppercorns
  • 1 tablespoon brown sugar
  • 1 tablespoon cooking oil
  • Salt, to taste

Instructions

  1. Start by marinating the chicken. In a bowl, combine the soy sauce, vinegar, minced garlic, sliced onion, bay leaves, and whole peppercorns. Mix well.
  2. Add the chicken pieces to the marinade, ensuring they are fully coated. Let the chicken marinate for at least 30 minutes, or for best results, refrigerate overnight.
  3. After marinating, remove the chicken from the marinade and set aside. Be sure to reserve the marinade for later use.
  4. Preheat the Instant Pot by selecting the sauté function. Once hot, add the cooking oil and brown the chicken on all sides. This will help seal in the flavors.
  5. Once the chicken is browned, remove it from the Instant Pot and set it aside.
  6. Pour the reserved marinade into the Instant Pot and add the brown sugar. Stir well to dissolve the sugar.
  7. Return the browned chicken to the Instant Pot, making sure to coat each piece with the marinade mixture.
  8. Close the Instant Pot lid and set the valve to the sealing position. Select the pressure cook function and set the timer for 15 minutes on high pressure.
  9. Once the cooking time is complete, allow the pressure to naturally release for 10 minutes. Then, carefully do a quick release of the remaining pressure.
  10. Open the Instant Pot and transfer the chicken adobo to a serving dish. Garnish with fresh cilantro or green onions, if desired.
  11. Serve the Authentic Instant Pot Chicken Adobo with steamed rice and enjoy!

Tips and Variations

  • For a twist, you can add some coconut milk to the marinade mixture. This will give the chicken adobo a creamy and slightly sweet flavor.
  • If you prefer a thicker sauce, you can remove the chicken after cooking and simmer the sauce in the Instant Pot using the sauté function until it reaches your desired consistency.
  • Leftover chicken adobo can be stored in the refrigerator for up to three days. The flavors will intensify over time, making it even more delicious.

Conclusion

With the help of the Instant Pot, you can now enjoy the delicious flavors of Authentic Chicken Adobo in a fraction of the time. This Filipino favorite is loved for its rich and savory taste, and with this recipe, you can recreate it in your own kitchen. Whether you’re cooking for your family or hosting a dinner party, this Instant Pot Chicken Adobo will surely impress. So, grab your Instant Pot and get ready to indulge in a truly authentic Filipino dish. Savor each bite and let the flavors transport you to the vibrant streets of the Philippines.
