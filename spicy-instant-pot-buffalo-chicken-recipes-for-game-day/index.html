Spicy Instant Pot Buffalo Chicken Recipes for Game Day


When it comes to game day, there’s nothing quite like the thrill of gathering with friends and family, cheering for your favorite team, and indulging in mouth-watering snacks. One dish that never fails to steal the spotlight is spicy Buffalo chicken. And what better way to prepare it than in the Instant Pot? This versatile kitchen appliance not only saves time but also locks in flavors, making your Buffalo chicken tender, juicy, and bursting with heat. In this blog post, we’ll explore a variety of spicy Instant Pot Buffalo chicken recipes, ensuring that your game day feast is a touchdown!

Why Choose Instant Pot for Buffalo Chicken?

The Instant Pot has revolutionized home cooking, especially for those who love bold flavors in a fraction of the time. Here are just a few reasons why the Instant Pot is the perfect choice for preparing spicy Buffalo chicken:

  • Time-Saving: Traditional slow cooking can take hours, but the Instant Pot significantly reduces cooking time without sacrificing flavor.
  • Flavor Infusion: The pressure cooking method locks in moisture and flavors, allowing the spices and sauces to penetrate the chicken more deeply.
  • Easy Cleanup: With fewer pots and pans to wash, you can focus on enjoying the game rather than scrubbing dishes.

Essential Ingredients for Spicy Buffalo Chicken

Before diving into the recipes, it’s important to gather all the necessary ingredients. Here’s a list of what you’ll need:

  • Chicken: Boneless, skinless chicken breasts or thighs work best.
  • Buffalo Sauce: You can use store-bought or make your own. A combination of hot sauce, butter, and vinegar is classic.
  • Spices: Garlic powder, onion powder, cayenne pepper, and paprika add depth to the flavor.
  • Broth: Chicken broth or stock keeps the chicken moist while adding additional flavor.
  • Toppings: Consider blue cheese crumbles, fresh celery, or a drizzle of ranch dressing for serving.

Recipe 1: Classic Spicy Instant Pot Buffalo Chicken

Ingredients:

  • 2 pounds boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1 cup Buffalo sauce
  • 1/2 cup chicken broth
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon onion powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon cayenne pepper (adjust to taste)
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the Chicken: Season the chicken breasts with salt, pepper, garlic powder, onion powder, cayenne pepper, and paprika. Rub the spices evenly over the chicken.

  2. Add to Instant Pot: Place the seasoned chicken in the Instant Pot. Pour the Buffalo sauce and chicken broth over the chicken.

  3. Pressure Cook: Close the lid and ensure the valve is set to sealing. Select the manual or pressure cook setting and set the timer for 10 minutes.

  4. Release Pressure: Once the cooking time is up, allow for a natural pressure release for 5 minutes, then quick release any remaining pressure.

  5. Shred the Chicken: Remove the chicken from the pot and shred it using two forks. Return the shredded chicken to the pot and stir to combine with the sauce.

  6. Serve: Serve the spicy Buffalo chicken on buns, in tacos, or over a bed of greens. Top with blue cheese crumbles or ranch dressing for extra flavor.

Recipe 2: Spicy Instant Pot Buffalo Chicken Dip

This creamy and spicy dip is perfect for game day snacking. It’s easy to make and always a crowd-pleaser!

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups shredded spicy Buffalo chicken (use the recipe above)
  • 8 ounces cream cheese, softened
  • 1 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1/2 cup ranch dressing
  • 1/2 cup Buffalo sauce
  • 1/4 cup green onions, chopped (for garnish)

Instructions:

  1. Shred the Chicken: Use the shredded spicy Buffalo chicken from the previous recipe.

  2. Combine Ingredients: In a mixing bowl, combine the shredded chicken, cream cheese, shredded cheddar cheese, ranch dressing, and Buffalo sauce. Mix until well combined.

  3. Transfer to Instant Pot: Pour the mixture into the Instant Pot.

  4. Pressure Cook: Close the lid and set the valve to sealing. Select the manual or pressure cook setting for 5 minutes.

  5. Release Pressure: Once cooking is complete, perform a quick release.

  6. Serve: Stir the dip until smooth and creamy. Serve with tortilla chips, celery sticks, or crackers. Garnish with chopped green onions for an added kick.

Recipe 3: Spicy Instant Pot Buffalo Chicken Tacos

Give your taco night a spicy twist with these Buffalo chicken tacos that are sure to become a favorite.

Ingredients:

  • 2 pounds boneless, skinless chicken thighs
  • 1 cup Buffalo sauce
  • 1/2 cup chicken broth
  • 1 tablespoon taco seasoning
  • Corn tortillas
  • Toppings: shredded lettuce, diced tomatoes, avocado, cilantro, lime wedges

Instructions:

  1. Season the Chicken: Season the chicken thighs with taco seasoning, salt, and pepper.

  2. Add to Instant Pot: Place the seasoned chicken in the Instant Pot. Pour the Buffalo sauce and chicken broth over the chicken.

  3. Pressure Cook: Close the lid, set the valve to sealing, and cook on manual for 12 minutes.

  4. Release Pressure: Allow for a natural pressure release for 5 minutes, then quick release any remaining pressure.

  5. Shred and Mix: Shred the chicken and mix it with the sauce in the pot.

  6. Assemble Tacos: Warm the corn tortillas and fill them with the spicy Buffalo chicken. Top with shredded lettuce, diced tomatoes, avocado, cilantro, and a squeeze of lime for a refreshing finish.

Recipe 4: Spicy Instant Pot Buffalo Chicken Sliders

These sliders make for an easy and delicious game day appetizer that everyone will love.

Ingredients:

  • 2 pounds boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1 cup Buffalo sauce
  • 1/2 cup chicken broth
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon onion powder
  • Slider buns
  • Toppings: sliced pickles, blue cheese dressing, shredded lettuce

Instructions:

  1. Season the Chicken: Season the chicken breasts with garlic powder, onion powder, salt, and pepper.

  2. Add to Instant Pot: Place the chicken in the Instant Pot and pour the Buffalo sauce and chicken broth over it.

  3. Pressure Cook: Seal the lid and set the Instant Pot to cook for 10 minutes.

  4. Release Pressure: After cooking, let the pressure release naturally for 5 minutes before doing a quick release.

  5. Shred the Chicken: Remove the chicken and shred it with two forks, mixing it back into the sauce.

  6. Assemble Sliders: Place the spicy Buffalo chicken on slider buns and top with sliced pickles, blue cheese dressing, and shredded lettuce.

Tips for Perfecting Your Spicy Buffalo Chicken

  1. Adjust the Heat: Feel free to adjust the level of spiciness based on your preference. Add more cayenne for extra heat or use a milder sauce for a less spicy version.

  2. Experiment with Flavors: Add different spices or ingredients to customize your Buffalo chicken. Consider smoked paprika for a smoky flavor or honey for a touch of sweetness.

  3. Use Leftovers Creatively: If you have leftover Buffalo chicken, use it in salads, wraps, or as a pizza topping. It’s versatile and can elevate many dishes.

  4. Pair with Sides: Complement your spicy Buffalo chicken with classic game day sides like celery sticks, carrot sticks, blue cheese dip, or potato wedges.

Conclusion

Spicy Instant Pot Buffalo chicken recipes are the perfect addition to your game day spread. With these easy and delicious recipes, you can impress your guests without spending hours in the kitchen. Whether you’re serving classic Buffalo chicken, creamy dip, flavorful tacos, or sliders, these dishes are sure to be a hit. So gather your friends, turn up the volume on the game, and enjoy the delicious flavors of your homemade spicy Buffalo chicken. Game on!
