Top 10 Recipes for Juicy Grilled Shrimp


Grilled shrimp is one of the most flavorful and versatile dishes you can prepare for any occasion. Whether you’re hosting a summer BBQ, enjoying a weeknight dinner, or serving an elegant appetizer, shrimp cooked on the grill has a unique, smoky flavor that elevates every meal. The key to grilling shrimp perfectly is ensuring that they remain juicy, tender, and full of flavor. In this blog post, we’ll dive into the top 10 recipes for juicy grilled shrimp, providing you with a variety of marinades, seasonings, and techniques to take your grilled shrimp to the next level.

1. Classic Lemon Garlic Grilled Shrimp

A simple and timeless recipe that never goes out of style. The lemon and garlic combination creates a tangy, savory marinade that enhances the natural sweetness of the shrimp without overpowering it.

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 lemon (zested and juiced)
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 1/4 tsp red pepper flakes (optional)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley, chopped for garnish

Instructions:

  1. In a mixing bowl, combine the garlic, lemon juice, lemon zest, olive oil, red pepper flakes (if using), salt, and pepper.
  2. Add the shrimp to the marinade and toss to coat. Cover and refrigerate for 15-30 minutes, allowing the flavors to meld.
  3. Preheat your grill to medium-high heat. Thread the shrimp onto skewers for easier grilling.
  4. Grill the shrimp for about 2-3 minutes on each side or until they turn pink and opaque.
  5. Garnish with freshly chopped parsley and serve with additional lemon wedges on the side.

2. Cajun Spiced Grilled Shrimp

For those who enjoy a bit of heat and bold flavors, this Cajun spiced grilled shrimp recipe delivers just that. The smoky, spicy blend of Cajun seasoning infuses the shrimp with a rich, zesty flavor profile.

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 1 tbsp Cajun seasoning
  • 1/2 tsp paprika
  • 1/4 tsp garlic powder
  • 1/4 tsp onion powder
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a small bowl, mix the Cajun seasoning, paprika, garlic powder, onion powder, olive oil, salt, and pepper.
  2. Toss the shrimp in the seasoning mix until they are evenly coated.
  3. Let the shrimp sit for 15-20 minutes to absorb the flavors.
  4. Preheat your grill to medium-high heat. Skewer the shrimp or place them directly on the grill.
  5. Grill the shrimp for 2-3 minutes per side or until they are fully cooked.
  6. Serve with a squeeze of lime and a side of rice or grilled vegetables.

3. Tropical Coconut Grilled Shrimp

This tropical-inspired recipe brings the taste of the islands to your backyard grill. The coconut milk marinade gives the shrimp a rich, creamy texture, while the pineapple adds a hint of sweetness.

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 1/2 cup coconut milk
  • 2 tbsp lime juice
  • 1 tbsp honey
  • 1/4 tsp ground ginger
  • 1/4 tsp ground turmeric
  • Salt to taste
  • 1/4 cup shredded coconut (optional)

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, whisk together the coconut milk, lime juice, honey, ginger, turmeric, and salt.
  2. Add the shrimp to the marinade and refrigerate for 30 minutes to an hour.
  3. Preheat your grill to medium heat. Thread the shrimp onto skewers.
  4. Grill the shrimp for 2-3 minutes on each side until they are cooked through and have grill marks.
  5. If desired, sprinkle with shredded coconut before serving. Pair with a tropical fruit salad for a complete meal.

4. Herb-Infused Grilled Shrimp

If you’re a fan of fresh, aromatic herbs, this recipe is perfect for you. A blend of parsley, basil, and thyme gives the shrimp a fragrant, fresh flavor that’s perfect for a light summer meal.

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 tbsp fresh parsley, chopped
  • 1 tbsp fresh basil, chopped
  • 1 tbsp fresh thyme, chopped
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a small bowl, mix the olive oil, parsley, basil, thyme, lemon juice, salt, and pepper.
  2. Toss the shrimp in the herb mixture, ensuring they are fully coated.
  3. Allow the shrimp to marinate for about 15-20 minutes.
  4. Preheat your grill to medium-high heat and grill the shrimp for about 2-3 minutes on each side until they are opaque and pink.
  5. Garnish with extra fresh herbs before serving, and enjoy with a side of roasted vegetables or a light salad.

5. Spicy Sriracha Grilled Shrimp

For those who love a fiery kick, this sriracha grilled shrimp recipe is the one for you. The combination of spicy sriracha sauce and the subtle sweetness of honey creates the perfect balance of heat and flavor.

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 2 tbsp sriracha sauce
  • 1 tbsp honey
  • 1 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp rice vinegar
  • 1 tsp sesame oil
  • 1/2 tsp garlic powder
  • Sesame seeds for garnish

Instructions:

  1. In a small bowl, whisk together the sriracha sauce, honey, soy sauce, rice vinegar, sesame oil, and garlic powder.
  2. Toss the shrimp in the sauce and marinate for 20-30 minutes.
  3. Preheat your grill to medium-high heat. Thread the shrimp onto skewers or use a grill basket.
  4. Grill the shrimp for about 2-3 minutes on each side or until fully cooked.
  5. Garnish with sesame seeds and serve with steamed rice or noodles to balance the heat.

6. Garlic Parmesan Grilled Shrimp

For a savory, buttery experience, garlic parmesan grilled shrimp is the perfect option. The rich garlic butter and the sharpness of parmesan cheese create a decadent flavor profile that is sure to impress.

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 4 tbsp butter, melted
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/4 cup grated parmesan cheese
  • 1 tbsp fresh parsley, chopped
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, mix the melted butter, minced garlic, parmesan cheese, parsley, salt, and pepper.
  2. Toss the shrimp in the garlic butter mixture and allow to marinate for 10-15 minutes.
  3. Preheat your grill to medium-high heat. Place the shrimp on the grill and cook for 2-3 minutes per side.
  4. Once cooked, remove the shrimp from the grill and drizzle any remaining garlic butter on top.
  5. Serve immediately with a side of pasta or fresh vegetables.

7. Smoky Chipotle Grilled Shrimp

If you’re a fan of smoky flavors, this chipotle grilled shrimp recipe is a must-try. The smoky chipotle pepper combined with the tanginess of lime creates an irresistible, zesty marinade.

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1-2 chipotle peppers in adobo sauce, minced
  • 1 tbsp lime juice
  • 1 tsp ground cumin
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, combine olive oil, chipotle peppers, lime juice, ground cumin, salt, and pepper.
  2. Toss the shrimp in the marinade and refrigerate for at least 20-30 minutes.
  3. Preheat your grill to medium-high heat and grill the shrimp for 2-3 minutes on each side.
  4. Serve the shrimp with a side of cilantro-lime rice or grilled vegetables for a smoky, flavorful meal.

8. Pesto Grilled Shrimp

If you love the vibrant flavors of basil pesto, this pesto grilled shrimp recipe is a fantastic choice. The herbal richness of the pesto pairs beautifully with the tender shrimp, creating a dish that’s full of fresh, bright flavors.

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 1/4 cup pesto sauce (store-bought or homemade)
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Toss the shrimp in the pesto sauce and olive oil, ensuring they are well coated.
  2. Let the shrimp marinate for 15-20 minutes to absorb the flavors.
  3. Preheat your grill to medium-high heat. Grill the shrimp for 2-3 minutes per side.
  4. Serve with a side of pasta or a simple green salad to complement the fresh pesto.

9. Asian-Inspired Grilled Shrimp

This Asian-inspired grilled shrimp recipe combines the savory depth of soy sauce, the sweetness of honey, and the aromatic flavors of ginger and garlic. It’s a simple yet flavorful option for any meal.

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 3 tbsp soy sauce
  • 2 tbsp honey
  • 1 tbsp sesame oil
  • 1 tbsp rice vinegar
  • 1 tsp grated ginger
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • Sesame seeds for garnish

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, combine soy sauce, honey, sesame oil, rice vinegar, ginger, and garlic.
  2. Add the shrimp to the marinade and let it sit for 15-20 minutes.
  3. Preheat your grill to medium-high heat and grill the shrimp for 2-3 minutes on each side.
  4. Garnish with sesame seeds and serve with steamed jasmine rice or stir-fried vegetables.

10. Chili-Lime Grilled Shrimp

For a zesty and refreshing twist, chili-lime grilled shrimp is a crowd-pleasing recipe. The combination of spicy chili powder and tangy lime creates an irresistible marinade.

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 1 tbsp chili powder
  • 1 tbsp lime juice
  • 1 tsp cumin
  • 1/2 tsp paprika
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh cilantro for garnish

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, mix the chili powder, lime juice, cumin, paprika, salt, and pepper.
  2. Toss the shrimp in the marinade and let it sit for 15-20 minutes.
  3. Preheat your grill to medium-high heat and grill the shrimp for 2-3 minutes per side.
  4. Garnish with freshly chopped cilantro and serve with a side of avocado salad or corn on the cob.

Grilled shrimp is an excellent choice for anyone looking for a quick, flavorful, and healthy meal. Whether you’re in the mood for something spicy, tangy, or savory, these 10 grilled shrimp recipes offer a variety of delicious options to suit any taste. The versatility of shrimp combined with the smoky goodness of the grill makes it a perfect addition to your next cookout or weeknight dinner. So fire up that grill, choose your favorite recipe, and enjoy a plate of juicy, perfectly cooked shrimp!
