Easy Homemade Breadsticks: Perfect Side Dish Recipe


If you’re looking for a simple and delicious side dish to accompany your favorite meals, look no further than these easy homemade breadsticks. Whether you’re serving up a hearty pasta dish, a fresh salad, or a comforting bowl of soup, these breadsticks are the ideal addition to complete your meal. With just a few basic ingredients and a little bit of time, you can whip up a batch of these flavorful breadsticks that are sure to impress your family and friends.

Ingredients

  • 2 ¼ teaspoons of active dry yeast
  • 1 ½ cups of warm water
  • 3 ¼ cups of all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons of granulated sugar
  • 1 teaspoon of salt
  • 3 tablespoons of olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon of garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon of dried oregano
  • ½ teaspoon of dried rosemary
  • ½ teaspoon of dried thyme
  • ¼ cup of grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 tablespoons of melted butter

Instructions

  1. Activate the Yeast: In a small bowl, dissolve the yeast in the warm water and let it sit for about 5 minutes until it becomes frothy.

  2. Prepare the Dough: In a large mixing bowl, combine the flour, sugar, and salt. Add the activated yeast and olive oil to the dry ingredients. Mix until a dough forms.

  3. Knead the Dough: Transfer the dough to a floured surface and knead for about 5-7 minutes until it becomes smooth and elastic.

  4. First Rise: Place the dough in a greased bowl, cover it with a damp cloth, and let it rise in a warm place for about 1 hour or until doubled in size.

  5. Shape the Breadsticks: Preheat the oven to 400°F (200°C). Punch down the risen dough and divide it into 12 equal parts. Roll each part into a long breadstick shape and place them on a lined baking sheet.

  6. Flavorful Topping: In a small bowl, combine the garlic powder, oregano, rosemary, thyme, and Parmesan cheese. Brush the breadsticks with melted butter and sprinkle the seasoning mix on top.

  7. Second Rise: Cover the shaped breadsticks with a damp cloth and let them rise for another 20-30 minutes.

  8. Bake to Perfection: Once the breadsticks have risen, bake them in the preheated oven for 15-20 minutes or until golden brown.

  9. Serve and Enjoy: Remove the breadsticks from the oven and let them cool for a few minutes before serving. These delightful breadsticks are best enjoyed warm, alongside your favorite dishes.

Tips and Variations

  • For a cheesy twist, sprinkle some shredded mozzarella on top of the breadsticks before baking.
  • Experiment with different herbs and spices to customize the flavor of the breadsticks to your liking.
  • Serve the breadsticks with a side of marinara sauce or garlic butter for dipping.

These easy homemade breadsticks are a fantastic way to elevate any meal with their irresistible aroma and delightful taste. Whether you’re a seasoned baker or just starting out, this recipe is perfect for anyone looking to add a special touch to their dining experience. So, roll up your sleeves, get your hands into the dough, and savor the satisfaction of creating these delectable breadsticks from scratch. Enjoy!
