Quick and Easy Nut-Free Stir-Fried Noodles Recipe


Are you craving a delicious and satisfying meal that’s quick and easy to make? Look no further! This Nut-Free Stir-Fried Noodles recipe is the perfect choice for a busy weeknight dinner. Packed with flavors and textures, this dish will leave you wanting more. Plus, it’s nut-free, making it a great option for those with allergies or dietary restrictions.

Ingredients

  • 8 ounces of noodles (choose your favorite type)
  • 2 tablespoons of vegetable oil
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 small onion, thinly sliced
  • 2 carrots, julienned
  • 1 red bell pepper, thinly sliced
  • 2 cups of broccoli florets
  • 1 cup of snap peas
  • 3 tablespoons of soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon of oyster sauce
  • 1 tablespoon of sesame oil
  • 1 teaspoon of brown sugar
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Chopped green onions for garnish

Instructions

  1. Cook the noodles according to the package instructions. Once cooked, drain and set aside.

  2. In a large skillet or wok, heat the vegetable oil over medium-high heat. Add the minced garlic and sliced onion, and sauté until fragrant and slightly golden.

  3. Add the julienned carrots, sliced red bell pepper, broccoli florets, and snap peas to the skillet. Stir-fry for about 3-4 minutes, or until the vegetables are crisp-tender.

  4. In a small bowl, whisk together the soy sauce, oyster sauce, sesame oil, brown sugar, salt, and pepper. Pour the sauce over the vegetables and stir to coat evenly.

  5. Add the cooked noodles to the skillet and toss everything together until well combined. Continue stir-frying for another 2-3 minutes, or until the noodles are heated through.

  6. Taste and adjust the seasoning if needed. If you prefer a spicier dish, you can add a dash of chili flakes or sriracha sauce.

  7. Remove from heat and garnish with chopped green onions.

  8. Serve hot and enjoy your delicious Nut-Free Stir-Fried Noodles!

Tips and Variations

  • Feel free to customize this recipe by adding your favorite protein, such as tofu, chicken, shrimp, or beef. Simply cook the protein separately and toss it in with the noodles and vegetables in step 5.

  • For an extra burst of flavor, you can add a squeeze of fresh lime juice or sprinkle some sesame seeds on top before serving.

  • If you’re looking to make this dish gluten-free, you can use gluten-free noodles and opt for a gluten-free soy sauce.

  • Experiment with different vegetables based on your preference or what you have on hand. Some great options include mushrooms, zucchini, cabbage, or bean sprouts.

  • Leftovers can be stored in an airtight container in the refrigerator for up to 3 days. Reheat in a skillet or microwave before enjoying.

Conclusion

This Quick and Easy Nut-Free Stir-Fried Noodles recipe is a fantastic option for a satisfying and flavorful meal. With its simple preparation and versatile nature, you can easily adapt it to suit your taste preferences or dietary needs. Whether you’re cooking for yourself or a crowd, this dish is sure to impress. So, grab your ingredients and give it a try! You won’t be disappointed.
