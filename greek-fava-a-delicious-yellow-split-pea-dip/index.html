Greek Fava: A Delicious Yellow Split Pea Dip


Greek cuisine is known for its flavorful and healthy dishes, and one such dish that should definitely be on your radar is Greek Fava. This delicious yellow split pea dip is not only packed with nutrients but also bursting with Mediterranean flavors. Whether you’re a fan of Greek cuisine or simply looking to try something new, Greek Fava is a must-try recipe that will leave you craving for more. So, without further ado, let’s dive into the world of Greek Fava!

Ingredients

To make Greek Fava, you’ll need the following ingredients:

  • 1 cup yellow split peas
  • 1 large onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 4 cups vegetable broth
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons lemon juice
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • Fresh parsley, for garnish

Instructions

  1. Start by rinsing the yellow split peas thoroughly under cold water. This helps to remove any impurities and ensures a clean taste.

  2. In a large pot, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the chopped onion and minced garlic, and sauté until they become translucent and fragrant.

  3. Once the onion and garlic are cooked, add the rinsed split peas to the pot. Stir the peas well, making sure they are coated in the aromatic mixture.

  4. Add the bay leaf and vegetable broth to the pot, and bring the mixture to a boil. Once it starts boiling, reduce the heat to low and let it simmer for about 40-45 minutes, or until the split peas are tender and cooked through.

  5. While the split peas are simmering, make sure to skim off any foam that rises to the surface. This will help to achieve a smoother texture for the dip.

  6. Once the split peas are fully cooked, remove the pot from the heat and discard the bay leaf. Using an immersion blender or a food processor, blend the mixture until smooth and creamy. If the dip is too thick, you can add a little bit of vegetable broth or water to achieve the desired consistency.

  7. Stir in the lemon juice and season with salt and pepper to taste. Adjust the seasoning according to your preference.

  8. Transfer the Greek Fava to a serving dish and garnish with fresh parsley. You can also drizzle a little bit of olive oil on top for added flavor.

  9. Serve the Greek Fava dip at room temperature or chilled, alongside some warm pita bread or freshly cut vegetables. It makes for a perfect appetizer or snack that will impress your guests or satisfy your cravings.

Enjoy the Flavors of Greece!

Greek Fava is a crowd-pleasing dish that combines simplicity with incredible taste. The creamy texture of the yellow split peas, combined with the subtle flavors of onion, garlic, and lemon, creates a dip that is both comforting and refreshing. Whether you’re planning a Greek-themed dinner party or simply want to add a touch of Mediterranean flair to your meals, Greek Fava is a versatile recipe that can be enjoyed in various ways.

So, why not give this delicious yellow split pea dip a try? It’s easy to make, packed with nutrients, and will transport your taste buds to the sunny shores of Greece. Prepare to indulge in the flavors of the Mediterranean and let Greek Fava become your new favorite dip!
