Decadent Pumpkin Cheesecake Brownies Recipe for a Heavenly Thanksgiving Dessert


Thanksgiving is a time for indulgence, and what better way to treat yourself and your loved ones than with a decadent dessert that combines the best of two worlds? Enter the Decadent Pumpkin Cheesecake Brownies – a heavenly combination of rich chocolate brownies and creamy pumpkin cheesecake. With the perfect balance of flavors and textures, these brownies are sure to be the star of your Thanksgiving feast.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup unsalted butter, melted
  • 2 cups granulated sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 4 large eggs
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup canned pumpkin puree
  • 1/2 cup cream cheese, softened
  • 1/4 cup powdered sugar
  • 1 teaspoon pumpkin pie spice

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C) and line a 9x9-inch baking pan with parchment paper.
  2. In a large mixing bowl, combine the melted butter, granulated sugar, and vanilla extract. Mix well until the sugar is fully dissolved.
  3. Add the eggs, one at a time, beating well after each addition.
  4. In a separate bowl, whisk together the flour, cocoa powder, and salt. Gradually add this dry mixture to the wet mixture, stirring until just combined.
  5. Pour two-thirds of the brownie batter into the prepared baking pan, spreading it evenly.
  6. In a small mixing bowl, combine the pumpkin puree, cream cheese, powdered sugar, and pumpkin pie spice. Mix until smooth and creamy.
  7. Carefully spread the pumpkin cheesecake mixture over the brownie batter in the pan.
  8. Drop spoonfuls of the remaining brownie batter on top of the pumpkin cheesecake layer, creating a marbled effect.
  9. Use a butter knife to swirl the two batters together, creating a beautiful pattern.
  10. Bake in the preheated oven for 35-40 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out with a few moist crumbs.
  11. Remove from the oven and let the brownies cool completely in the pan before cutting into squares.
  12. Serve these decadent pumpkin cheesecake brownies as a heavenly Thanksgiving dessert, and watch as your guests’ faces light up with delight.

Tips and Variations

  • For an extra indulgent twist, top each brownie square with a dollop of whipped cream and a sprinkle of cinnamon before serving.
  • If you’re feeling adventurous, add a handful of chocolate chips or chopped nuts to the brownie batter for added texture and flavor.
  • These brownies can be made ahead of time and stored in an airtight container in the refrigerator for up to three days. Just make sure to bring them to room temperature before serving.

Conclusion

There you have it – a recipe for Decadent Pumpkin Cheesecake Brownies that will make your Thanksgiving dessert table shine. With the perfect balance of rich chocolate and creamy pumpkin cheesecake, these brownies are a true crowd-pleaser. So go ahead, indulge your sweet tooth and impress your loved ones with this heavenly treat. Happy Thanksgiving!
