Chocolate Hazelnut Swirl Bread: A Decadent Delight


There’s something magical about the combination of chocolate and hazelnuts – it’s a flavor combination that’s simply divine. This chocolate hazelnut swirl bread is a delectable creation that brings together the best of both worlds, taking your taste buds on a heavenly journey. Incorporating these two popular ingredients into a loaf of bread is not only mouth-watering but also a delightful twist on a classic. Join us as we guide you through creating this scrumptious and decadent treat.

Ingredients

  • 2 1/4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 tsp active dry yeast
  • 1/4 cup cocoa powder
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/4 cup warm water
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1/2 cup whole milk
  • 1/4 cup unsalted butter, melted
  • 1/4 cup hazelnuts, coarsely chopped
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract

Instructions

  1. In a large mixing bowl, combine 2 1/4 cups of all-purpose flour, 2 teaspoons of active dry yeast, 1/4 cup of cocoa powder, and 1/4 cup of granulated sugar. Mix thoroughly.

  2. In a separate bowl, dissolve 1/4 cup of warm water with 2 large eggs and 1/2 cup of whole milk. Add the wet ingredients to the dry ingredients while stirring continually.

  3. Pour in 1/4 cup of melted unsalted butter and 1/4 cup of coarsely chopped hazelnuts. Mix well, then stir in 1 teaspoon of vanilla extract.

  4. Once the mixture is well combined, knead the dough on a lightly floured surface for approximately 10 minutes, or until it becomes smooth and elastic.

  5. Place the dough in a greased bowl, cover it with a damp cloth, and allow it to rise in a warm environment for about 1 hour, or until it has doubled in size.

  6. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C) and line a loaf pan with parchment paper.

  7. Divide the risen dough into two equal portions. Roll each portion into a 12x8-inch rectangle on a lightly floured surface.

  8. In a small bowl, mix together 2 tablespoons of granulated sugar and 1 tablespoon of cocoa powder for the swirl. Spread half of this mixture on one of the rectangles, leaving a 1/4-inch border on the shorter edges.

  9. Carefully position the other rectangle on top of the sugar-cocoa mixture, aligning the edges. Press down slightly to seal the dough.

  10. Use a sharp knife to slice the bread into 1-inch-wide strips. Arrange these strips side-by-side in the prepared loaf pan, using the parchment paper to prevent sticking.

  11. Cover the pan with a damp cloth and let the dough rise again for 30 minutes.

  12. Bake the bread in the preheated oven for 25-30 minutes or until the top is golden brown and the bread sounds hollow when tapped.

  13. Remove the loaf from the oven and allow it to cool in the pan for a few minutes. Carefully transfer the bread onto a wire rack to cool completely.

  14. Once cooled, slice the bread and enjoy your homemade chocolate hazelnut swirl bread with a warm beverage or as a delicious snack.

Conclusion

This scrumptious chocolate hazelnut swirl bread is a delight for the senses and a fantastic addition to any breakfast or brunch. Its rich flavors and textures will have your family and friends far from disappointed. Indulge in this delectable creation today and find a whole new love for the wonderful combination of chocolate and hazelnuts. Don’t hesitate to experiment with extra ingredients or toppings to truly make it your own!
