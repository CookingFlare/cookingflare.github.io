Seafood Stew Recipe with Cod, Shrimp, and Vegetables


There’s nothing quite like a hearty seafood stew to warm you up on a chilly evening. This seafood stew recipe combines the delicate flavors of cod and shrimp with the wholesome goodness of vegetables, creating a dish that’s not only delicious but also nutritious. Whether you’re a seafood lover or just looking to try something new, this recipe is sure to become a favorite in your household.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb (450g) cod fillets, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 1 lb (450g) medium shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 carrots, sliced
  • 2 stalks celery, chopped
  • 1 red bell pepper, diced
  • 1 cup (240ml) fish or vegetable stock
  • 1 can (14 oz/400g) diced tomatoes
  • 1/2 cup (120ml) dry white wine
  • 2 tbsp (30ml) olive oil
  • 1 tsp (5ml) dried thyme
  • 1 tsp (5ml) paprika
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley for garnish

Instructions

  1. In a large pot or Dutch oven, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the chopped onion and sauté until translucent, then add the minced garlic and cook for another minute.

  2. Add the carrots, celery, and red bell pepper to the pot, and sauté for a few minutes until the vegetables begin to soften.

  3. Pour in the white wine and let it simmer for a couple of minutes, allowing the alcohol to cook off and the flavors to meld.

  4. Stir in the diced tomatoes, fish or vegetable stock, dried thyme, and paprika. Season with salt and pepper to taste.

  5. Bring the stew to a gentle simmer, then add the cod pieces. Let the stew simmer for about 5 minutes, or until the cod is almost cooked through.

  6. Gently stir in the shrimp and continue to simmer for another 3-4 minutes, or until the shrimp turn pink and opaque.

  7. Taste the stew and adjust the seasoning if necessary. If you prefer a slightly thicker stew, you can mash some of the vegetables against the side of the pot to release their natural starch, which will help thicken the broth.

  8. Once the seafood is fully cooked and the flavors have melded beautifully, remove the pot from the heat and let the stew rest for a few minutes.

  9. Ladle the seafood stew into serving bowls, garnish with fresh parsley, and serve hot with crusty bread or over a bed of cooked white rice.

Enjoy the Aromas and Flavors

This seafood stew recipe is a celebration of fresh, vibrant flavors that come together to create a soul-warming dish. The tender cod, succulent shrimp, and colorful vegetables blend harmoniously in a fragrant broth that’s perfect for cozy family dinners or entertaining guests. The best part is that you can easily customize this recipe by adding your favorite seafood or adjusting the seasoning to suit your taste preferences.

So, the next time you’re craving a comforting and satisfying meal, consider whipping up a batch of this delightful seafood stew. It’s a culinary adventure that will transport your taste buds to the shores of the Mediterranean or the quaint fishing villages of New England. Dive into a bowl of this seafood delight and savor the goodness of the ocean in every spoonful. Cheers to good food and memorable moments around the dining table!
