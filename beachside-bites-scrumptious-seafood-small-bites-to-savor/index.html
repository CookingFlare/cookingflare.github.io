Beachside Bites: Scrumptious Seafood Small Bites to Savor


There’s nothing quite like dining on the beach, feeling the salty sea breeze and hearing the distant waves gently crash against the shore. For many, nothing pairs better with such an atmosphere than some scrumptious seafood small bites. In this blog post, we’ve gathered some of our favorite recipes that are perfect for enjoying by the beach.

Fresh and Effortless Mother of Pearl Oysters Craving something fresh and briny? Look no further than these delightful Mother of Pearl Oysters.

Ingredients:

  • 12 fresh Mother of Pearl Oysters
  • 1/4 cup coarse sea salt
  • 1 cup ice
  • Lemon wedges
  • Mignonette sauce (red wine vinegar, shallots, black pepper, and cracked ice)

Preparation:

  1. Begin by setting up a domino effect on your picnic blanket with the coarse sea salt. This will give your oysters the perfect platform to sit on and stay steady.

  2. Ensure that your Mother of Pearl Oysters are chilled for at least 30 minutes before serving to capture that perfect briny taste.

  3. To make the Mignonette sauce, mix the red wine vinegar, shallots, black pepper in a bowl and put it in the fridge until ready to use.

  4. Gently shuck the chilled oysters, making sure to keep the oyster liquor (the natural juice) for added flavor.

  5. Place the oysters on the salt bed, garnish with a squeeze of lemon and a spoonful of the Mignonette sauce on top. Serve immediately.

Ultra-Zesty Gulf Coast Shrimp Skewers Kick things up a notch with these spicy and zesty Gulf Coast Shrimp Skewers.

Ingredients:

  • 24 Gulf Coast shrimp (peeled, deveined, and tail-on)
  • 2 limes (juiced)
  • 1/2 cup olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon smoked paprika
  • 1/2 cup chopped cilantro
  • 2 cloves garlic (minced)
  • 1 jalapeno (diced, seeds removed)
  • 8 bamboo skewers (soaked in water for at least 30 minutes)

Preparation:

  1. In a bowl, whisk together the olive oil, the lime juice, garlic powder, chili powder, and smoked paprika.

  2. Add the shrimp to the bowl and coat with the oil mixture. Cover and leave them marinating in the fridge for at least 30 minutes.

  3. Preheat your grill to medium-high heat.

  4. In another small bowl, combine the cilantro, minced garlic, and jalapeno, reserving a sprinkle for garnish.

  5. Thread the shrimp onto the bamboo skewers, leaving a tiny gap between each one.

  6. Once the grill is hot, place the skewers on the grate and grill for 2-3 minutes on each side, or until the shrimp turns pink and opaque.

  7. Once cooked, brush the shrimp skewers with the extra marinade, and sprinkle the cilantro mix on top. Serve immediately.

Sassy and Sassy: Lobster Mac & Cheese Bites These bite-sized pieces of lobster-infused mac and cheese will have your taste buds dancing.

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb lobster meat (cut into bite-sized pieces)
  • 1 lb elbow macaroni pasta (cooked al dente)
  • 2 cups grated cheddar cheese
  • 1 cup grated Gruyere cheese
  • 2 cups heavy cream
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter
  • 1/2 cup flour
  • 1/4 tsp black pepper
  • 2 tablespoons fresh parsley (chopped, for garnish)

Preparation:

  1. Begin by melting the unsalted butter in a saucepan over medium heat.

  2. Add the flour, whisking until smooth and lightly golden. Gradually whisk in the heavy cream and black pepper, stirring constantly to prevent lumps from forming.

  3. Reduce the heat to low, and slowly mix in the cheddar and Gruyere cheeses until melted, creating a smooth cheese sauce.

  4. Combine the cooked macaroni and lobster pieces with the cheese sauce, tossing until well-coated. Season to taste.

  5. Using a spoon, scoop out small portions of the mac and cheese mixture, creating balls, then roll them in the reserved grated cheddar cheese.

  6. Place the lobster mac & cheese bites onto a parchment-lined baking sheet and bake for 10-12 minutes, or until golden and bubbly.

  7. Garnish with the fresh parsley and serve immediately with lemon wedges for a refreshing zesty kick.

Conclusion Whether you’re armed with a picnic blanket, a beach towel, or just a small table for two, these scrumptious small bites are perfect for beachside feasting. With fresh seafood, zesty flavors, and a laid-back vibe, try these recipes on your next beach voyage to savor each bite fully. Enjoy the splendor of your beachside setting with these simple yet mouthwatering treats.
