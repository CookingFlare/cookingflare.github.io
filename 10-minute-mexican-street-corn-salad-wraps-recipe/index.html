10-Minute Mexican Street Corn Salad Wraps Recipe


If you’re looking for a quick and delicious meal that embodies the vibrant flavors of Mexican street food, then you’ve come to the right place. These 10-Minute Mexican Street Corn Salad Wraps are bursting with fresh ingredients and zesty seasonings, all wrapped up in a soft tortilla. Not only are they incredibly easy to make, but they also make for a satisfying and healthy lunch or dinner option. Let’s dive right into this mouthwatering recipe!

Ingredients

  • 2 cups of corn kernels (fresh or frozen)
  • 1 small red onion, finely chopped
  • 1 jalapeno pepper, seeded and diced
  • 1/4 cup of fresh cilantro, chopped
  • Juice of 1 lime
  • 2 tablespoons of mayonnaise
  • 1/2 teaspoon of chili powder
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • 4 large tortillas

Instructions

  1. In a large skillet over medium heat, add the corn kernels and cook until they are lightly charred, stirring occasionally. This should take about 5 minutes.

  2. In a mixing bowl, combine the charred corn kernels, red onion, jalapeno pepper, and cilantro. Toss them together to create a flavorful medley of ingredients.

  3. In a small bowl, whisk together the lime juice, mayonnaise, chili powder, salt, and pepper. This creamy dressing will add a tangy kick to your corn salad.

  4. Pour the dressing over the corn mixture and toss until everything is evenly coated. The creamy dressing will perfectly complement the smoky flavors of the charred corn.

  5. Warm the tortillas in a dry skillet or microwave for a few seconds until they are pliable. This will make them easier to wrap.

  6. Divide the corn salad evenly among the tortillas, placing it in the center of each one. Fold the sides of the tortillas over the filling, then roll them up tightly to create a neat wrap.

  7. Serve your Mexican Street Corn Salad Wraps immediately and enjoy the explosion of flavors in every bite. You can also cut them in half for a more manageable size.

Serving Suggestions

These Mexican Street Corn Salad Wraps are incredibly versatile and can be customized to suit your taste preferences. Here are a few suggestions to elevate your culinary experience:

  • Add a sprinkle of crumbled queso fresco or cotija cheese for a creamy and salty element.
  • Drizzle some hot sauce or chipotle mayo on top to add a spicy kick.
  • Serve them alongside a refreshing side salad or a bowl of tortilla chips and salsa.
  • Experiment with different tortilla flavors, such as spinach or sun-dried tomato, to add a colorful twist.

Final Thoughts

With just a handful of ingredients and a mere 10 minutes of your time, you can whip up these delicious Mexican Street Corn Salad Wraps that are sure to impress. The combination of charred corn, zesty lime dressing, and crunchy vegetables is a match made in culinary heaven. Whether you’re looking for a quick lunch or a delightful dinner option, these wraps are a guaranteed crowd-pleaser. So, go ahead and give this recipe a try – your taste buds will thank you!
