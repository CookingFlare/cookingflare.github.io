Homemade Chicken Nuggets: Easy Recipe for Kids


Are you tired of feeding your kids store-bought chicken nuggets that are loaded with preservatives and questionable ingredients? Why not try making your own homemade chicken nuggets? Not only are they healthier, but they also taste incredibly delicious! In this blog post, we will share with you a simple and easy recipe for homemade chicken nuggets that your kids will absolutely love. So, let’s get started!

Ingredients

  • 1 pound boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1 cup breadcrumbs
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 2 large eggs, beaten
  • Cooking spray

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C) and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
  2. Cut the chicken breasts into bite-sized pieces, approximately 1-inch in size.
  3. In a shallow bowl, combine the breadcrumbs, grated Parmesan cheese, garlic powder, paprika, salt, and black pepper. Mix well.
  4. Dip each piece of chicken into the beaten eggs, making sure to coat it completely.
  5. Next, roll the chicken in the breadcrumb mixture, pressing gently to ensure the breadcrumbs adhere to the chicken.
  6. Place the coated chicken nuggets onto the prepared baking sheet, making sure to leave space between each nugget.
  7. Spray the tops of the nuggets lightly with cooking spray. This will help them achieve a crispy texture.
  8. Bake the chicken nuggets in the preheated oven for 15-20 minutes, or until they are golden brown and cooked through.
  9. Remove the nuggets from the oven and let them cool for a few minutes before serving.

Serving Suggestions

These homemade chicken nuggets are versatile and can be enjoyed in various ways. Here are a few serving suggestions:

  1. Serve the nuggets as a main dish with a side of fries and a fresh salad.
  2. Create a DIY nugget bar by setting out different dipping sauces like ketchup, honey mustard, and barbecue sauce. Let your kids have fun experimenting with different flavors.
  3. Make a chicken nugget wrap by placing a few nuggets in a tortilla with some lettuce, tomato, and a drizzle of ranch dressing.
  4. Use the nuggets as a topping for a homemade pizza. Simply chop them up and sprinkle them over your favorite pizza toppings before baking.

Tips and Tricks

  • For extra flavor, you can add some dried herbs like oregano or thyme to the breadcrumb mixture.
  • If you want a gluten-free version, simply replace the breadcrumbs with gluten-free breadcrumbs or crushed cornflakes.
  • To ensure the nuggets are evenly cooked, try to cut the chicken pieces into similar sizes.
  • If you prefer a crispier texture, you can broil the nuggets for an additional 1-2 minutes after baking.

Conclusion

Making homemade chicken nuggets for your kids is not only a healthier option but also a fun and rewarding activity. With just a few simple ingredients, you can create a delicious and wholesome meal that your kids will request over and over again. So, why not give this easy recipe a try and see the smiles on your little ones’ faces? Enjoy!
