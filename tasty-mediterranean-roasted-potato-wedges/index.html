Tasty Mediterranean Roasted Potato Wedges


If you're tired of the same old boring potato recipes, it's time to elevate your culinary game with a side dish that will transport your taste buds to the sun-kissed Mediterranean shores. Our take on roasted potato wedges takes the humble spud to new heights with a flavorful blend of Mediterranean spices and herbs. Imagine the savory flavors of Italy, the brightness of Greece, and the warmth of North Africa, all in one addictive side dish. ** Servings: 6-8** Ingredients:

4-5 large potatoes 1/4 cup extra virgin olive oil 2 tbsp lemon juice 1 tsp smoked paprika 1 tsp garlic powder 1 tsp dried oregano 1/2 tsp salt 1/4 tsp black pepper 1/4 cup crumbled feta cheese 1/4 cup chopped kalamata olives 1/4 cup chopped artichoke hearts 1/4 cup chopped sun-dried tomatoes Fresh parsley, chopped (optional)

Instructions: Step 1: Preheat and Prep Preheat your oven to 425°F (220°C). Rinse the potatoes and dry them thoroughly with paper towels. Peel the potatoes and cut them into 8 wedges each. Place the wedges in a bowl and drizzle with olive oil. Toss to coat the potatoes evenly. Step 2: Mix and Marinate In a small bowl, whisk together lemon juice, smoked paprika, garlic powder, dried oregano, salt, and black pepper. Pour the marinade over the potato wedges and toss to coat. Let the potatoes marinate for at least 30 minutes to allow the flavors to penetrate the potato flesh. Step 3: Roast to Perfection Line a baking sheet with parchment paper and arrange the potato wedges in a single layer. Roast in the preheated oven for 20-25 minutes or until the potatoes are tender and golden brown. Step 4: Add the Mediterranean Magic While the potatoes are roasting, mix together crumbled feta cheese, chopped olives, artichoke hearts, and sun-dried tomatoes in a small bowl. After the potatoes have roasted for 20 minutes, remove them from the oven and increase the oven temperature to broil (high). Sprinkle the Mediterranean mix over the potatoes and return them to the oven for an additional 2-3 minutes, or until the cheese is melted and bubbly. Step 5: Serve and Enjoy Remove the potatoes from the oven and garnish with chopped fresh parsley if desired. Serve immediately, perfect as a side dish for your favorite Mediterranean-inspired meals or as a snack on its own. The Mediterranean Touch: Elevating a Simple Dish Traditional Greek cuisine relies heavily on olive oil, which not only adds flavor but also provides a healthy dose of monounsaturated fats. The combination of smoked paprika and feta cheese creates a smoky, savory flavor profile reminiscent of Mediterranean barbecue. By incorporating artichoke hearts and sun-dried tomatoes, we add a burst of umami flavor that elevates the dish beyond the ordinary. Tips and Variations

For a spicy kick, add some red pepper flakes to the marinade or sprinkle them on top of the potatoes before broiling. Swap the artichoke hearts for chopped hearts of palm or roasted garlic scapes for a different twist. If you can't find feta cheese, substitute with ricotta or cottage cheese for a similar tangy flavor. Consider using other types of potatoes, such as Yukon gold or sweet potatoes, for a slightly sweeter flavor.

Whether you're hosting a dinner party or just need a quick and delicious side dish, our Mediterranean roasted potato wedges are sure to impress. The combination of flavors and textures will transport you to the warm, sun-drenched Mediterranean climate, where the perfect blend of taste and culture awaits. So go ahead, give it a try and taste the Mediterranean magic!
