Deliciously Creamy Lemon Curd Tart: A Citrusy Delight


If you’re a fan of citrusy desserts, then this Deliciously Creamy Lemon Curd Tart is sure to become a favorite in your recipe collection. The zesty and vibrant flavor of lemon curd combined with the buttery, crisp crust creates a dessert that is both refreshing and indulgent. Whether you’re hosting a dinner party or simply treating yourself to a delightful sweet treat, this Lemon Curd Tart is the perfect choice.

Ingredients

For the Crust:

  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, cold and cubed
  • 1/4 cup confectioners’ sugar
  • Pinch of salt
  • 1 egg yolk
  • 1-2 tablespoons ice water

For the Lemon Curd Filling:

  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/4 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 3 large eggs
  • 1/2 cup fresh lemon juice
  • Zest of 2 lemons
  • Pinch of salt

Instructions

Making the Crust:

  1. In a food processor, pulse the flour, confectioners’ sugar, and salt together.
  2. Add the cold, cubed butter and pulse until the mixture resembles coarse crumbs.
  3. Add the egg yolk and 1 tablespoon of ice water, and pulse until the dough comes together. If needed, add an additional tablespoon of ice water.
  4. Turn the dough out onto a lightly floured surface and shape it into a disk. Wrap in plastic wrap and refrigerate for at least 30 minutes.
  5. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  6. Roll out the chilled dough on a lightly floured surface and carefully transfer it to a 9-inch tart pan with a removable bottom. Press the dough into the pan and trim any excess.
  7. Line the crust with parchment paper and fill with pie weights or dried beans. Blind bake for 15 minutes. Remove the weights and parchment paper, then bake for an additional 10-12 minutes, or until the crust is golden. Allow it to cool completely.

Making the Lemon Curd Filling:

  1. In a heatproof bowl set over a pot of simmering water, whisk together the sugar, lemon juice, lemon zest, eggs, and salt.
  2. Add the softened butter and continue to whisk the mixture constantly until it thickens, about 10-12 minutes.
  3. Once the lemon curd has thickened, remove it from the heat and let it cool slightly.
  4. Pour the lemon curd into the cooled tart crust and smooth the top with a spatula.
  5. Refrigerate the tart for at least 2 hours, or until the lemon curd is set.

Serving

When ready to serve, carefully remove the tart from the pan and place it on a serving plate. Slice the Deliciously Creamy Lemon Curd Tart into wedges and garnish with a dollop of whipped cream or a sprinkle of confectioners’ sugar. The tart can be stored in the refrigerator for up to 3 days, though it’s unlikely to last that long once you and your guests take the first bite!

Final Thoughts

The Deliciously Creamy Lemon Curd Tart is a delightful balance of sweet and tangy flavors, and it’s the perfect dessert for any occasion. The buttery crust provides a wonderful contrast to the vibrant lemon curd, making each bite a true sensation for the taste buds. Whether you’re a seasoned baker or new to the world of pastry, this recipe is worth the effort. So, roll up your sleeves, gather the ingredients, and treat yourself to a slice of this citrusy delight. Your taste buds will thank you!
