Spice of Life: 4 Exotic Rubs for Vegetarian BBQ


There’s something magical about firing up the grill and infusing your favorite vegetables with tantalizing flavors. Whether you’re a vegetarian or simply looking to explore new flavors, these four exotic rubs will take your vegetarian BBQ to the next level. From smoky and spicy to tangy and sweet, these rubs will add depth and complexity to your grilled creations. So, grab your apron and let’s dive into the world of vegetarian BBQ!

1. Smoky Chipotle Rub

If you’re a fan of bold flavors and a hint of heat, this smoky chipotle rub is just what you need. The combination of chipotle powder, smoked paprika, garlic powder, brown sugar, and a touch of salt creates a smoky and spicy flavor profile that will elevate your grilled vegetables to new heights.

Ingredients:

  • 2 tablespoons chipotle powder
  • 1 tablespoon smoked paprika
  • 1 tablespoon garlic powder
  • 1 tablespoon brown sugar
  • 1 teaspoon salt

Instructions:

  1. In a small bowl, combine all the ingredients and mix well.
  2. Rub the mixture generously onto your vegetables, making sure to coat them evenly.
  3. Let the vegetables marinate for at least 30 minutes before grilling.
  4. Grill the vegetables over medium heat until they are tender and slightly charred, about 8-10 minutes per side.
  5. Serve hot and enjoy the smoky goodness!

2. Tangy Tandoori Rub

Transport your taste buds to the vibrant streets of India with this tangy tandoori rub. Bursting with a blend of aromatic spices, this rub will infuse your vegetables with a delightful tanginess and a touch of warmth. Get ready for a flavor explosion!

Ingredients:

  • 2 tablespoons ground cumin
  • 1 tablespoon ground coriander
  • 1 tablespoon paprika
  • 1 tablespoon turmeric
  • 1 tablespoon garam masala
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon black pepper
  • Juice of 1 lemon

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, combine all the spices, salt, and black pepper.
  2. Squeeze in the lemon juice and mix well to form a paste.
  3. Generously coat your vegetables with the tandoori rub, ensuring each piece is well-covered.
  4. Allow the vegetables to marinate for at least 1 hour before grilling.
  5. Grill the vegetables over medium-high heat until they are tender and have a nice char, approximately 10-12 minutes per side.
  6. Serve with a side of cooling mint yogurt sauce for a perfect balance of flavors.

3. Sweet and Spicy Korean Rub

Add a touch of Korean flair to your vegetarian BBQ with this sweet and spicy rub. The combination of Korean chili flakes, brown sugar, soy sauce, and ginger will give your vegetables a delightful kick, while the sweetness adds a perfect balance. Get ready to experience a symphony of flavors!

Ingredients:

  • 2 tablespoons Korean chili flakes (gochugaru)
  • 1 tablespoon brown sugar
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon sesame oil
  • 1 teaspoon grated ginger
  • 1 teaspoon minced garlic
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, combine the Korean chili flakes, brown sugar, soy sauce, sesame oil, ginger, garlic, and salt.
  2. Mix well until the sugar has dissolved and the ingredients are well combined.
  3. Rub the mixture onto your vegetables, ensuring they are evenly coated.
  4. Allow the vegetables to marinate for at least 1 hour before grilling.
  5. Grill the vegetables over medium heat until they are tender and caramelized, approximately 8-10 minutes per side.
  6. Serve with a side of steamed rice and kimchi for a complete Korean BBQ experience.

4. Zesty Moroccan Rub

Indulge in the exotic flavors of Morocco with this zesty rub. The combination of aromatic spices, such as cumin, coriander, and cinnamon, along with the freshness of lemon zest, will transport you to the bustling markets of Marrakech. Get ready for a burst of flavors!

Ingredients:

  • 2 tablespoons ground cumin
  • 1 tablespoon ground coriander
  • 1 tablespoon ground paprika
  • 1 tablespoon ground cinnamon
  • Zest of 1 lemon
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon black pepper

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, combine the ground cumin, ground coriander, ground paprika, ground cinnamon, lemon zest, salt, and black pepper.
  2. Mix well to ensure all the spices are evenly distributed.
  3. Generously rub the mixture onto your vegetables, making sure to coat them thoroughly.
  4. Allow the vegetables to marinate for at least 30 minutes before grilling.
  5. Grill the vegetables over medium-high heat until they are tender and have a beautiful char, approximately 10-12 minutes per side.
  6. Serve with a side of couscous and a drizzle of yogurt sauce for a taste of Morocco.

Get ready to embark on a culinary adventure with these four exotic rubs for vegetarian BBQ. Whether you’re hosting a backyard gathering or simply indulging in a delicious meal, these rubs will take your grilled vegetables to the next level. So, fire up the grill, try out these flavorful rubs, and let your taste buds dance with delight. Enjoy the Spice of Life!
