Creamy Peach Sorbet: Sweet and Juicy


If you’re looking for a refreshing and delightful summer treat, look no further than this creamy peach sorbet recipe. Bursting with the sweet and juicy flavors of ripe peaches, this frozen dessert is the perfect way to cool down on a hot day. The best part? It’s incredibly easy to make and requires just a few simple ingredients. So, roll up your sleeves, grab some fresh peaches, and let’s dive into the delightful world of creamy peach sorbet.

Ingredients:

  • 6 ripe peaches, peeled, pitted, and chopped
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the Peaches: Start by preparing the peaches. Peel the peaches, remove the pits, and chop them into chunks. The riper the peaches, the sweeter and more flavorful your sorbet will be.

  2. Make the Peach Puree: Place the chopped peaches in a blender or food processor and blend until smooth. If you prefer a smoother sorbet, you can strain the puree through a fine-mesh sieve to remove any pulp, but if you love the texture, you can leave it as is.

  3. Sweeten the Puree: In a saucepan, combine the peach puree, granulated sugar, and lemon juice. Cook over medium heat, stirring occasionally, until the sugar has completely dissolved. This step helps to enhance the natural sweetness of the peaches and create a smooth sorbet base.

  4. Chill the Mixture: Once the peach mixture has cooled to room temperature, cover it and refrigerate for at least 2 hours, or until thoroughly chilled. This step is crucial to ensure that your sorbet freezes properly and has the perfect texture.

  5. Whip the Cream: In a separate bowl, whip the heavy cream and vanilla extract until stiff peaks form. This will add a luxurious creaminess to the sorbet and balance out the sweet peach flavor.

  6. Combine and Freeze: Gently fold the chilled peach mixture into the whipped cream until well combined. Be careful not to overmix, as you want to maintain the airy texture of the cream. Once combined, transfer the mixture to a freezer-safe container and freeze for at least 4 hours, or until firm.

  7. Serve and Enjoy: When ready to serve, let the sorbet sit at room temperature for a few minutes to soften slightly. Scoop the creamy peach sorbet into bowls or cones, and garnish with fresh peach slices or a sprig of mint for a beautiful presentation.

Tips and Variations:

  • For an extra burst of flavor, consider adding a splash of peach liqueur or a pinch of cinnamon to the peach puree.
  • If you prefer a dairy-free version, you can substitute the heavy cream with coconut cream for a luscious coconut peach sorbet.
  • Experiment with different fruit combinations by adding a handful of raspberries or a squeeze of lime juice to the peach puree for a unique twist on this classic sorbet.

Final Thoughts:

Creamy peach sorbet is a fantastic way to make the most of ripe, in-season peaches, and it’s a delightful dessert that will impress your family and friends. Whether you’re hosting a summer barbecue or simply craving a refreshing treat, this sweet and juicy sorbet is sure to be a hit. So, indulge in the flavors of summer and whip up a batch of this creamy peach sorbet to savor every spoonful of this frozen delight. Cheers to the sweet and juicy pleasures of homemade sorbet!
