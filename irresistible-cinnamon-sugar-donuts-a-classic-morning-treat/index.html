Irresistible Cinnamon Sugar Donuts: A Classic Morning Treat


There’s something truly magical about the aroma of freshly baked cinnamon sugar donuts wafting through the house in the early hours of the morning. These classic treats have been a staple of breakfast tables for generations, and for good reason – they are simply irresistible. If you’ve ever longed to recreate that warm, comforting experience in your own kitchen, you’re in luck. Today, we’re diving into the art of making homemade cinnamon sugar donuts that will rival any bakery’s offering.

Ingredients

For the Donuts:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 3/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 2 tsp baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp baking soda
  • 1/2 tsp ground nutmeg
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 3/4 cup buttermilk
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 tbsp unsalted butter, melted
  • 2 tsp vanilla extract

For the Cinnamon Sugar Coating:

  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 2 tsp ground cinnamon
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, melted

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Dough: In a large bowl, whisk together the flour, sugar, baking powder, baking soda, nutmeg, and salt. In a separate bowl, whisk together the buttermilk, eggs, melted butter, and vanilla extract. Gradually add the wet ingredients to the dry ingredients, stirring until just combined. Be careful not to overmix the dough.

  2. Shape the Donuts: Turn the dough out onto a well-floured surface and roll it out to a 1/2-inch thickness. Using a donut cutter or two differently sized round cutters, cut out the donuts and donut holes. Place them on a parchment-lined baking sheet and let them rest while you heat the oil.

  3. Fry the Donuts: In a large, heavy-bottomed pot, heat about 2 inches of vegetable oil to 350°F. Carefully add a few donuts to the hot oil, being sure not to overcrowd the pot. Fry for about 1-2 minutes per side, until golden brown and cooked through. Transfer the fried donuts to a paper towel-lined plate to drain and cool slightly.

  4. Coat with Cinnamon Sugar: In a shallow bowl, mix together the granulated sugar and ground cinnamon. While the donuts are still warm, dip them in the melted butter, then roll them in the cinnamon sugar mixture until evenly coated.

  5. Serve and Enjoy: These delectable cinnamon sugar donuts are best enjoyed fresh, while still warm and fluffy. Pair them with a steaming cup of coffee or a cold glass of milk for the ultimate morning indulgence.

Tips and Tricks

  • For a fun twist, you can add a pinch of cardamom or ginger to the cinnamon sugar mixture for an extra layer of flavor.
  • If you don’t have buttermilk on hand, you can make a quick substitute by adding 1 tablespoon of white vinegar or lemon juice to a cup of milk and letting it sit for 5 minutes.
  • To ensure perfectly round donuts, use a floured biscuit cutter or the rim of a drinking glass to cut out the dough.

Final Thoughts

The aroma of cinnamon sugar donuts baking in the oven is enough to make anyone’s morning brighter. With this simple yet indulgent recipe, you can bring that warmth and comfort into your own home. Whether you’re treating yourself to a leisurely weekend brunch or surprising your loved ones with a special breakfast, these homemade donuts are sure to be a hit. So go ahead, dust off your apron, and get ready to delight your senses with these irresistible cinnamon sugar donuts – a classic morning treat that never goes out of style.
