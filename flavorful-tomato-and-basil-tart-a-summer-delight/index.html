Flavorful Tomato and Basil Tart: A Summer Delight


Summer is the perfect time to indulge in the fresh flavors of the season, and what better way to do so than with a delightful tomato and basil tart? This savory dish combines the sweetness of ripe tomatoes with the aromatic essence of basil, all nestled in a buttery, flaky crust. Whether you’re hosting a summer brunch or looking for a light and flavorful dinner option, this tomato and basil tart is sure to impress your guests and tantalize your taste buds.

Ingredients

For the Crust:

  • 1 1/4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, cold and cut into cubes
  • 2-4 tablespoons ice water

For the Filling:

  • 4-5 large ripe tomatoes, thinly sliced
  • 1/4 cup fresh basil, thinly sliced
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

Step 1: Prepare the Crust

  1. In a large bowl, whisk together the flour and salt.
  2. Add the cold butter cubes to the flour mixture and use a pastry cutter or fork to cut the butter into the flour until the mixture resembles coarse crumbs.
  3. Gradually add the ice water, 1 tablespoon at a time, and mix until the dough comes together.
  4. Shape the dough into a disk, wrap it in plastic wrap, and refrigerate for at least 30 minutes.

Step 2: Preheat the Oven

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).

Step 3: Assemble the Tart

  1. On a lightly floured surface, roll out the chilled dough into a circle large enough to fit into a 9-inch tart pan.
  2. Gently press the dough into the tart pan, trimming any excess dough from the edges.
  3. Prick the bottom of the crust with a fork and line it with parchment paper. Fill the crust with pie weights or dried beans to prevent it from puffing up while baking.
  4. Bake the crust for 15 minutes, then remove the parchment paper and weights, and continue baking for another 10 minutes, or until lightly golden. Set aside to cool.

Step 4: Prepare the Filling

  1. In a small bowl, mix together the minced garlic and olive oil. Set aside.
  2. In a separate bowl, combine the shredded mozzarella and grated Parmesan cheese.

Step 5: Assemble the Tart

  1. Arrange the sliced tomatoes in overlapping concentric circles over the cooled crust.
  2. Drizzle the garlic and olive oil mixture over the tomatoes.
  3. Sprinkle the basil over the tomatoes, followed by the cheese mixture.
  4. Season the tart with salt and pepper to taste.

Step 6: Bake the Tart

  1. Place the assembled tart in the preheated oven and bake for 30-35 minutes, or until the crust is golden brown and the cheese is bubbly.
  2. Remove the tart from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes before slicing.

Serving Suggestions

This flavorful tomato and basil tart is best enjoyed fresh out of the oven, allowing the savory aroma of basil and tomatoes to fill the air. Serve it as a light lunch alongside a crisp green salad, or cut it into smaller portions for a delightful appetizer at your next summer gathering. The tart pairs beautifully with a chilled glass of white wine or a refreshing iced tea, making it the perfect addition to any summer menu.

Conclusion

With its buttery crust, ripe tomatoes, fragrant basil, and gooey cheese, this tomato and basil tart is a celebration of summer flavors. Whether you’re a seasoned home cook or just starting your culinary journey, this recipe is a delightful way to embrace the best of the season. So, roll up your sleeves, gather the freshest ingredients, and create a summer delight that will have everyone asking for seconds. Cheers to a season of good food, good company, and unforgettable flavors!
