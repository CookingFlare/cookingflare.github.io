Vegan Coconut Cream Pie: Creamy and Coconutty


If you’re a fan of creamy, coconut-infused desserts, then you’re going to love this vegan coconut cream pie recipe. This luscious pie is a delightful combination of rich coconut cream, flaky pie crust, and a hint of vanilla, creating a dessert that’s both indulgent and refreshing. Whether you’re a seasoned vegan or simply looking to explore new dairy-free dessert options, this recipe is sure to become a favorite in your kitchen.

Ingredients

For the Crust:

  • 1 1/2 cups of almond flour
  • 1/4 cup of coconut oil, melted
  • 2 tablespoons of maple syrup
  • 1/2 teaspoon of salt

For the Coconut Cream Filling:

  • 2 cans of full-fat coconut milk, refrigerated overnight
  • 1/2 cup of powdered sugar
  • 1 teaspoon of vanilla extract
  • 1/2 cup of unsweetened shredded coconut

For the Coconut Whipped Cream:

  • 1 can of full-fat coconut milk, refrigerated overnight
  • 2 tablespoons of powdered sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon of vanilla extract

Instructions

Step 1: Prepare the Crust

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. In a bowl, combine the almond flour, melted coconut oil, maple syrup, and salt. Mix until the mixture resembles coarse crumbs.
  3. Press the mixture into a 9-inch pie dish, making sure to evenly cover the bottom and sides.
  4. Bake the crust for 10-12 minutes, or until it’s golden brown. Allow it to cool completely.

Step 2: Make the Coconut Cream Filling

  1. Open the refrigerated cans of coconut milk and carefully scoop out the solid coconut cream, leaving the liquid behind. Place the cream in a mixing bowl.
  2. Add the powdered sugar and vanilla extract to the bowl of coconut cream. Using a hand mixer, beat the mixture on high speed until it becomes light and fluffy.
  3. Gently fold in the unsweetened shredded coconut.
  4. Spread the coconut cream filling evenly over the cooled pie crust.

Step 3: Prepare the Coconut Whipped Cream

  1. Open the refrigerated can of coconut milk and scoop out the solid coconut cream into a mixing bowl.
  2. Add the powdered sugar and vanilla extract to the bowl of coconut cream. Beat the mixture on high speed until it becomes smooth and creamy.
  3. Dollop the coconut whipped cream over the coconut cream filling.

Step 4: Chill and Serve

  1. Place the assembled pie in the refrigerator and chill for at least 4 hours, or until the filling is set.
  2. Before serving, you can sprinkle some additional shredded coconut on top for garnish if desired.

Enjoy Your Vegan Coconut Cream Pie!

This vegan coconut cream pie is a testament to the fact that dairy-free desserts can be just as decadent and satisfying as their traditional counterparts. With its velvety coconut filling, nutty crust, and a cloud-like coconut whipped cream topping, this pie is a celebration of the tropical flavors and the versatility of vegan ingredients. Whether you’re hosting a dinner party or simply treating yourself to a sweet indulgence, this recipe is a must-try for anyone who appreciates the delightful combination of creamy and coconutty flavors. So go ahead, slice into this luxurious pie and savor every spoonful of this vegan delight!
