One Pot Mushroom and Spinach Tortellini


Are you looking for a quick and easy meal that is not only delicious but also requires minimal cleanup? Look no further than this One Pot Mushroom and Spinach Tortellini recipe! Packed with flavors, this dish is perfect for busy weeknights when you want something comforting and satisfying without spending hours in the kitchen.

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 small onion, diced
  • 3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 8 ounces of mushrooms, sliced
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1/2 teaspoon red pepper flakes (optional)
  • 1 cup vegetable broth
  • 1 can diced tomatoes
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • 8 ounces fresh or frozen tortellini
  • 2 cups fresh spinach
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Grated Parmesan cheese for garnish

Instructions

  1. In a large pot, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the diced onion and minced garlic, and sauté until fragrant and translucent, about 2-3 minutes.

  2. Add the sliced mushrooms to the pot and cook until they start to soften and release their juices, about 5 minutes. Stir in the dried thyme, dried oregano, and red pepper flakes (if using) to enhance the flavors.

  3. Pour in the vegetable broth and diced tomatoes with their juices. Bring the mixture to a simmer and let it cook for about 10 minutes to allow the flavors to meld together.

  4. Stir in the heavy cream and bring the mixture back to a gentle simmer. Add the tortellini to the pot and cook according to the package instructions until they are tender and cooked through.

  5. Once the tortellini is cooked, add the fresh spinach to the pot. Stir it gently until it wilts down and incorporates into the sauce.

  6. Season the dish with salt and pepper to taste. The amount will depend on your preference and the saltiness of the vegetable broth used.

  7. Serve the One Pot Mushroom and Spinach Tortellini hot, garnished with grated Parmesan cheese. The creamy sauce, tender tortellini, and earthy mushrooms make for a delightful combination that will have everyone reaching for seconds.

Tips and Variations

  • Feel free to customize this recipe by adding other vegetables such as bell peppers or zucchini. Simply sauté them along with the mushrooms for added flavor and texture.
  • If you’re looking for a lighter version, you can substitute the heavy cream with half-and-half or whole milk. The sauce will still be creamy, but with fewer calories.
  • For a vegan-friendly option, use plant-based tortellini and swap the heavy cream with coconut milk or your favorite non-dairy alternative.
  • Sprinkle some fresh herbs like parsley or basil on top before serving for an extra pop of freshness.
  • This dish reheats well, so you can enjoy the leftovers for lunch or dinner the next day. Simply store them in an airtight container in the refrigerator and reheat gently on the stovetop.

With just one pot and a handful of ingredients, you can create a comforting and flavorful meal that will satisfy both your taste buds and your craving for simplicity. Give this One Pot Mushroom and Spinach Tortellini recipe a try and let it become a staple in your weeknight dinner rotation.
