Savory Low Carb Bacon-Wrapped Green Beans


Looking for a delicious and healthy side dish that will impress your friends and family? Look no further than these savory low carb bacon-wrapped green beans! With just a few simple ingredients, you can create a flavorful dish that is sure to be a hit at any gathering.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound fresh green beans
  • 8 slices of bacon
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C) and line a baking sheet with parchment paper or aluminum foil for easy cleanup.

  2. Wash the green beans thoroughly and trim off the ends. Pat them dry with a paper towel to remove any excess moisture.

  3. In a large bowl, toss the green beans with olive oil, garlic powder, salt, and black pepper. Make sure the green beans are evenly coated with the seasonings.

  4. Take a slice of bacon and wrap it tightly around a small bundle of green beans. Repeat this process until all the green beans are wrapped in bacon. Place the bacon-wrapped green beans on the prepared baking sheet, seam side down, to prevent them from unraveling.

  5. Bake the green beans in the preheated oven for 20-25 minutes, or until the bacon is crispy and the green beans are tender. If desired, you can broil them for an additional 2-3 minutes to get an extra crispy texture.

  6. Remove the bacon-wrapped green beans from the oven and let them cool for a few minutes before serving. This will allow the bacon to firm up, making it easier to handle.

  7. Serve the savory low carb bacon-wrapped green beans as a side dish with your favorite main course. They pair well with grilled chicken, steak, or even roasted vegetables.

Tips and Variations

  • For an extra kick of flavor, sprinkle some grated Parmesan cheese over the green beans before baking.

  • If you prefer your bacon-wrapped green beans to be slightly sweet, drizzle them with a small amount of maple syrup or honey before baking.

  • Experiment with different seasonings to suit your taste. You can try adding smoked paprika, cayenne pepper, or even a dash of hot sauce for a spicy twist.

  • If you’re short on time, you can partially cook the bacon before wrapping it around the green beans. This will ensure that the bacon is crispy without overcooking the green beans.

  • Leftovers can be stored in an airtight container in the refrigerator for up to 3 days. Reheat them in the oven or microwave before serving.

In Conclusion

These savory low carb bacon-wrapped green beans are a delicious and easy-to-make side dish that will elevate any meal. With their crispy bacon exterior and tender green bean interior, they offer the perfect combination of flavors and textures. Whether you’re hosting a dinner party or simply looking for a tasty side dish to enjoy with your family, give this recipe a try. Your taste buds will thank you!

Note: This recipe is low in carbohydrates, making it suitable for those following a low carb or keto diet. However, please consult with a healthcare professional or nutritionist before making any significant changes to your diet.
