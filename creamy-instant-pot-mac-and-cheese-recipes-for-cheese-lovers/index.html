Creamy Instant Pot Mac and Cheese Recipes for Cheese Lovers


If you’re a cheese enthusiast and a fan of creamy, indulgent dishes, then you’re in for a treat! We’re diving into the world of Instant Pot Mac and Cheese, exploring various recipes that will satisfy your cheese cravings and leave you coming back for more. Whether you’re hosting a family gathering, a cozy dinner for two, or simply craving some cheesy goodness, these recipes are sure to hit the spot.

Classic Creamy Mac and Cheese

Let’s start with a timeless favorite – the classic creamy mac and cheese. With the help of your Instant Pot, you can achieve that rich, velvety texture without the need for constant stirring. Here’s how to make it:

Ingredients:

  • 16 oz elbow macaroni
  • 4 cups chicken or vegetable broth
  • 2 cups heavy cream
  • 3 cups shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Place the elbow macaroni, broth, and butter in the Instant Pot.
  2. Close the lid and set the pot to manual high pressure for 4 minutes.
  3. Once done, perform a quick pressure release and open the lid.
  4. Stir in the heavy cream, cheddar, mozzarella, Parmesan, garlic powder, salt, and pepper until the cheese is melted and the sauce is smooth.
  5. Let it sit for a few minutes to thicken before serving.

Five-Cheese Blend Mac and Cheese

For the ultimate cheese lover’s experience, this five-cheese blend mac and cheese recipe is a must-try. The combination of different cheeses creates a depth of flavor that will tantalize your taste buds.

Ingredients:

  • 16 oz cavatappi pasta
  • 4 cups water
  • 1 cup whole milk
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • 1/4 cup unsalted butter
  • 1/2 tsp dry mustard
  • 1/2 tsp onion powder
  • 1/2 tsp paprika
  • 1 1/2 cups shredded sharp cheddar cheese
  • 1 cup shredded Gruyère cheese
  • 1 cup shredded fontina cheese
  • 1/2 cup shredded provolone cheese
  • 1/2 cup shredded Asiago cheese
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Place the cavatappi, water, and butter in the Instant Pot.
  2. Close the lid and set the pot to manual high pressure for 4 minutes.
  3. Once done, perform a quick pressure release and open the lid.
  4. Stir in the milk, heavy cream, dry mustard, onion powder, paprika, cheddar, Gruyère, fontina, provolone, Asiago, salt, and pepper until the cheese is melted and the sauce is creamy.

Bacon and Jalapeño Mac and Cheese

For those who enjoy a little kick of heat and the savory goodness of bacon, this bacon and jalapeño mac and cheese recipe will elevate your mac and cheese game to a whole new level.

Ingredients:

  • 16 oz macaroni
  • 4 cups chicken broth
  • 1 cup whole milk
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • 1/4 cup unsalted butter
  • 2 cups shredded sharp cheddar cheese
  • 1 cup shredded pepper jack cheese
  • 1/2 cup chopped cooked bacon
  • 2 fresh jalapeños, seeded and diced
  • 1/2 tsp garlic powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Place the macaroni, chicken broth, and butter in the Instant Pot.
  2. Close the lid and set the pot to manual high pressure for 4 minutes.
  3. Once done, perform a quick pressure release and open the lid.
  4. Stir in the milk, heavy cream, cheddar, pepper jack, bacon, jalapeños, garlic powder, salt, and pepper until the cheese is melted and the sauce is creamy.

Conclusion

Whether you prefer the classic creamy mac and cheese, the decadent five-cheese blend, or the bold flavors of bacon and jalapeño, the Instant Pot has revolutionized the way we can enjoy this comfort food favorite. These recipes are just the beginning – feel free to experiment with different cheese combinations, add-ins, and seasonings to create your own signature Instant Pot mac and cheese masterpiece. So grab your Instant Pot and let’s get cooking – your creamy, cheesy indulgence awaits!
