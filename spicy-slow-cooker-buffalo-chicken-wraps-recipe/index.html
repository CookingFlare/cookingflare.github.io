Spicy Slow Cooker Buffalo Chicken Wraps Recipe


If you’re looking for a dish that combines convenience with flavor, look no further than these Spicy Slow Cooker Buffalo Chicken Wraps. This recipe is perfect for busy weeknights or game day gatherings, ensuring that you can enjoy a hearty and delicious meal without spending hours in the kitchen. With just a few simple ingredients, you can create a mouthwatering wrap that packs a punch of flavor and spice.

Why Choose Slow Cooker Buffalo Chicken?

Using a slow cooker transforms simple ingredients into a delectable meal that’s bursting with flavor. The beauty of slow cooking is in the way it allows the chicken to absorb all the zesty buffalo sauce, resulting in tender, juicy meat that practically falls apart. Not only is this recipe easy to prepare, but it also offers versatility. Whether you’re serving it as a main dish or as an appetizer for your next party, these wraps will impress your guests and satisfy your cravings.

Ingredients You’ll Need

To make Spicy Slow Cooker Buffalo Chicken Wraps, gather the following ingredients:

  • For the Chicken:

    • 2 pounds boneless, skinless chicken breasts
    • 1 cup buffalo sauce (adjust to taste for more or less heat)
    • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
    • 1 teaspoon onion powder
    • 1 teaspoon smoked paprika
    • Salt and pepper to taste

  • For the Wraps:

    • 4 large tortillas (flour or whole wheat)
    • 1 cup shredded lettuce
    • 1 cup diced tomatoes
    • 1 cup shredded cheese (cheddar or your choice)
    • ½ cup ranch or blue cheese dressing (optional)
    • Fresh cilantro or green onions for garnish (optional)

Step-by-Step Instructions

1. Prepare the Chicken

Begin by placing the boneless, skinless chicken breasts into the slow cooker. Sprinkle the garlic powder, onion powder, smoked paprika, salt, and pepper over the chicken. Pour the buffalo sauce on top, ensuring that the chicken is well coated. The amount of buffalo sauce can be adjusted based on your heat preference, so feel free to experiment!

2. Slow Cook

Cover the slow cooker with its lid and set it to low heat. Allow the chicken to cook for 6 to 8 hours, or until it is tender and easily shreds with a fork. If you’re short on time, you can also set it to high heat for 3 to 4 hours.

3. Shred the Chicken

Once the chicken is cooked, use two forks to shred it directly in the slow cooker. Stir the shredded chicken in the buffalo sauce to ensure that every piece is well coated. This is where the magic happens – the chicken absorbs all the flavors, making it incredibly delicious.

4. Assemble the Wraps

Now that your buffalo chicken is ready, it’s time to assemble the wraps. Lay a tortilla flat on a clean surface. Start by adding a generous portion of the shredded buffalo chicken in the center. Top it with shredded lettuce, diced tomatoes, and shredded cheese. If you enjoy a creamy element, drizzle some ranch or blue cheese dressing on top. For an extra burst of flavor, sprinkle some fresh cilantro or chopped green onions.

5. Roll It Up

Fold in the sides of the tortilla and then roll it tightly from the bottom to the top, ensuring that all the fillings are secured inside. Repeat this process with the remaining tortillas and ingredients.

6. Serve and Enjoy

Your Spicy Slow Cooker Buffalo Chicken Wraps are now ready to be served! Cut the wraps in half diagonally for a beautiful presentation. These wraps pair wonderfully with a side of celery sticks or carrot sticks, and a small bowl of ranch or blue cheese dressing for dipping.

Tips for the Perfect Buffalo Chicken Wraps

  • Customize the Heat: Adjust the level of spiciness by choosing a mild or hot buffalo sauce. You can also add diced jalapeños for an extra kick.
  • Add Veggies: Feel free to throw in other vegetables like bell peppers or avocado for added texture and flavor.
  • Make it a Meal Prep: These wraps are perfect for meal prep. Assemble them and store them in the fridge for up to 3 days. Just wrap them tightly in plastic wrap or place them in an airtight container.
  • Leftover Ideas: If you have leftover buffalo chicken, use it for salads, pizzas, or even in a quesadilla.

Nutritional Information

While the nutritional values can vary based on specific ingredients and portion sizes, here’s a general breakdown for one wrap:

  • Calories: Approximately 400
  • Protein: 30g
  • Carbohydrates: 30g
  • Fat: 15g
  • Fiber: 2g

This recipe is not only delicious but also provides a good balance of protein and carbohydrates, making it a satisfying option for lunch or dinner.

Final Thoughts

Spicy Slow Cooker Buffalo Chicken Wraps are a fantastic addition to your recipe repertoire. They’re simple to make, incredibly tasty, and sure to be a hit with family and friends. Whether you’re celebrating with a crowd or enjoying a quiet night in, these wraps deliver on flavor and satisfaction.

So, the next time you find yourself in need of a quick, delicious meal, remember this recipe. With just a few ingredients and a slow cooker, you can create a dish that’s not only easy but also packed with flavor. Enjoy the spicy goodness of these wraps and don’t be surprised when everyone asks for seconds!
