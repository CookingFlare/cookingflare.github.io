Easy One-Pot Spanish Dishes for Busy Weeknights


When the hustle and bustle of the week sets in, the last thing you want is to spend hours in the kitchen preparing dinner. Luckily, Spanish cuisine offers a plethora of one-pot dishes that are not only easy to prepare but also bursting with flavor. These recipes are perfect for busy weeknights, allowing you to enjoy a delicious meal without the stress. In this blog post, we will explore a variety of easy one-pot Spanish dishes that will become staples in your weeknight meal rotation.

1. One-Pot Spanish Chicken and Rice

Ingredients:

  • 4 chicken thighs, skinless and boneless
  • 1 cup long-grain rice
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 red bell pepper, chopped
  • 1 can (14 oz) diced tomatoes
  • 2 cups chicken broth
  • 1 teaspoon smoked paprika
  • 1 teaspoon saffron threads (optional)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Sauté the Chicken: In a large pot or Dutch oven, heat a tablespoon of olive oil over medium-high heat. Season the chicken thighs with salt, pepper, and smoked paprika. Add the chicken to the pot and brown on both sides, about 5-7 minutes. Remove the chicken and set aside.

  2. Cook the Aromatics: In the same pot, add the chopped onion and minced garlic. Sauté for about 3 minutes until the onion becomes translucent. Add the red bell pepper and continue to cook for another 2 minutes.

  3. Add Rice and Tomatoes: Stir in the rice, diced tomatoes, chicken broth, and saffron threads. Mix well to combine all the flavors.

  4. Simmer: Return the browned chicken thighs to the pot. Bring the mixture to a boil, then lower the heat, cover, and let it simmer for about 20-25 minutes or until the rice is cooked and has absorbed most of the liquid.

  5. Garnish and Serve: Once cooked, fluff the rice with a fork and garnish with fresh parsley before serving. This dish is hearty and satisfying, perfect for a family dinner.

2. One-Pot Spanish Chorizo and Lentil Stew

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup green lentils, rinsed
  • 2 chorizo sausages, sliced
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 carrots, chopped
  • 1 can (14 oz) diced tomatoes
  • 4 cups vegetable broth
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • 1 bay leaf
  • Olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh cilantro for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Cook the Chorizo: In a large pot, heat a tablespoon of olive oil over medium heat. Add the sliced chorizo and cook until browned, about 5 minutes. Remove the chorizo and set aside, leaving the oil in the pot.

  2. Sauté Vegetables: Add the chopped onion, garlic, and carrots to the pot. Sauté for about 5 minutes until the vegetables are tender.

  3. Combine Ingredients: Stir in the lentils, diced tomatoes, vegetable broth, cumin, and bay leaf. Add the cooked chorizo back into the pot and season with salt and pepper.

  4. Simmer: Bring the stew to a boil, then reduce the heat to low. Cover and let it simmer for 30-35 minutes, or until the lentils are tender.

  5. Serve: Remove the bay leaf and adjust seasoning if necessary. Serve hot, garnished with fresh cilantro. This stew is not only filling but also packed with protein and flavor.

3. One-Pot Spanish Seafood Paella

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup Arborio rice
  • 1 pound mixed seafood (shrimp, mussels, and squid)
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 red bell pepper, chopped
  • 1 can (14 oz) diced tomatoes
  • 2 cups fish or seafood broth
  • 1 teaspoon smoked paprika
  • 1 teaspoon saffron threads (optional)
  • Olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Lemon wedges for serving

Instructions:

  1. Sauté Aromatics: In a large paella pan or skillet, heat a tablespoon of olive oil over medium heat. Add the chopped onion, garlic, and red bell pepper. Sauté until the vegetables are tender, about 5 minutes.

  2. Add Rice and Spices: Stir in the Arborio rice, smoked paprika, and saffron threads. Cook for 2 minutes, stirring constantly.

  3. Add Tomatoes and Broth: Pour in the diced tomatoes and seafood broth. Bring to a boil, then reduce the heat to low.

  4. Add Seafood: Gently place the mixed seafood on top of the rice. Cover and cook for about 15-20 minutes, or until the seafood is cooked through and the rice is tender.

  5. Serve: Remove from heat and let it sit for a few minutes. Serve with lemon wedges for squeezing over the top. This vibrant dish is sure to impress.

4. One-Pot Spanish Vegetable and Chickpea Stew

Ingredients:

  • 1 can (15 oz) chickpeas, rinsed and drained
  • 1 zucchini, chopped
  • 1 bell pepper, chopped
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can (14 oz) diced tomatoes
  • 4 cups vegetable broth
  • 1 teaspoon smoked paprika
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • Olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Sauté Vegetables: In a large pot, heat a tablespoon of olive oil over medium heat. Add the onion and garlic, sautéing until translucent, about 3 minutes.

  2. Add Remaining Veggies: Stir in the zucchini and bell pepper, cooking until they soften, about 5 minutes.

  3. Combine All Ingredients: Add the chickpeas, diced tomatoes, vegetable broth, smoked paprika, and cumin. Season with salt and pepper.

  4. Simmer: Bring to a boil, then reduce heat to low. Cover and simmer for about 20 minutes, allowing the flavors to meld.

  5. Serve: Garnish with fresh parsley before serving. This healthy stew is packed with nutrients and flavor, making it a perfect weeknight meal.

5. One-Pot Spanish Beef and Rice Skillet

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 cup long-grain rice
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can (14 oz) diced tomatoes
  • 2 cups beef broth
  • 1 teaspoon smoked paprika
  • 1 teaspoon oregano
  • Olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Grated cheese for serving (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Brown the Beef: In a large skillet, heat a tablespoon of olive oil over medium heat. Add the ground beef, breaking it up with a spoon. Cook until browned, about 5-7 minutes. Drain excess fat if necessary.

  2. Add Aromatics: Stir in the chopped onion and garlic, cooking until the onion is translucent, about 3 minutes.

  3. Combine Ingredients: Add the rice, diced tomatoes, beef broth, smoked paprika, and oregano. Season with salt and pepper.

  4. Cook the Rice: Bring to a boil, then reduce the heat to low. Cover and simmer for about 20-25 minutes, or until the rice is tender and liquid is absorbed.

  5. Serve: Fluff the rice with a fork and top with grated cheese if desired. This dish is a comforting mix of beef and rice that is sure to satisfy.

Conclusion

These easy one-pot Spanish dishes are perfect for busy weeknights when you want something delicious without spending hours in the kitchen. From the hearty chicken and rice to the vibrant seafood paella, each recipe is designed to be simple yet full of flavor. Not only will you save time on cleanup, but you’ll also delight your family with authentic Spanish flavors. So next time you’re pressed for time, remember these quick and satisfying meals to keep your weeknights delicious and stress-free!
