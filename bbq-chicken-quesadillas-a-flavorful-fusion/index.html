BBQ Chicken Quesadillas: A Flavorful Fusion


When it comes to fusion cuisine, BBQ Chicken Quesadillas are the ultimate comfort food for people who love culinary experiments. The idea of BBQ Chicken Quesadillas may sound simple, but it is pure genius – combine the smoky flavors of BBQ chicken with the subtle crunchiness of quesadillas to create a new dish that quickly becomes a crowd favorite. Keep reading to learn how to make this mouth-watering meal yourself!

To begin, gather the following ingredients:

  • 1 pound boneless, skinless chicken thighs
  • 1/2 cup barbecue sauce (your favorite brand or homemade)
  • 1/4 cup freshly grated cheddar cheese
  • 1/4 cup grated pepper jack cheese
  • 6 large flour tortillas
  • 1/2 red onion, thinly sliced
  • 2 jalapeno peppers, seeded and minced
  • Salt and black pepper, to taste
  • Vegetable oil, for grilling or frying
  • Your favorite toppings: sour cream, cilantro, pico de gallo, etc.

Now that you have your ingredients ready, let’s dive into the steps to create Restaurant-quality BBQ Chicken Quesadillas at home.

Step 1: Marinate the Chicken

In a large bowl, combine chicken thighs, barbecue sauce, and a dash of salt and black pepper. Mix well, cover, and marinate the chicken in the refrigerator for at least an hour. This step ensures that the chicken absorbs the flavor of the sauce, creating a delicious and tender BBQ chicken.

Step 2: Cook the Chicken

Preheat your grill or grill pan over medium-high heat. Once hot, remove the chicken from the marinade, and cook it for about 6-7 minutes on each side, or until fully cooked and the internal temperature reaches 165°F (74°C). Set aside the cooked chicken and let it cool down to room temperature.

Step 3: Make the Quesadilla

Once the chicken is cooled, shred it into bite-size pieces using two forks. For each quesadilla:

  1. Take a flour tortilla and spread a thin layer of shredded cheddar cheese over one side.
  2. Add a layer of shredded BBQ chicken on the cheese.
  3. Top with a layer of pepper jack cheese.
  4. Sprinkle thinly sliced red onion and minced jalapeños on top of the cheese.
  5. Fold the tortilla in half to enclose the filling.

Step 4: Cook the Quesadilla

Heat a grill pan, griddle, or skillet over medium-high heat and brush with a thin layer of vegetable oil. Place the quesadilla on the preheated surface, and cook for about 2-3 minutes per side, or until the tortilla is crispy, and the cheese is melted and bubbly.

Step 5: Serve and Enjoy

Remove the quesadilla from the heat, let it cool down slightly, and serve. BBQ Chicken Quesadillas are best enjoyed with your favorite toppings like sour cream, cilantro, pico de gallo, and a wedge of lime to squeeze over the top. Bon appétit!

This delightful fusion of BBQ Chicken Quesadillas marries the comfort of classic quesadillas with the tangy flavors of barbecue sauce, making it a perfect meal for any occasion. Whether you’re hosting a backyard barbecue or looking for a tasty, versatile dish to please a crowd, BBQ Chicken Quesadillas are sure to score big. So, gather your ingredients and surprise your loved ones with this new take on an old favorite. Enjoy!
