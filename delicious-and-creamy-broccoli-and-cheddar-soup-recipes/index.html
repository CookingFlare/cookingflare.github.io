Delicious and Creamy Broccoli and Cheddar Soup Recipes


There’s nothing quite like a bowl of warm, creamy soup to comfort the soul, and when it comes to comfort food, broccoli and cheddar soup ranks high on the list. This delicious and indulgent soup is a perfect blend of wholesome broccoli, sharp cheddar, and a touch of cream, creating a velvety texture and rich flavor that’s hard to resist. Whether you’re looking for a hearty meal on a cold winter’s day or a simple yet satisfying dish for any time of the year, this broccoli and cheddar soup recipe is sure to hit the spot.

Ingredients

  • 4 cups of fresh broccoli florets
  • 1 medium onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 4 cups of chicken or vegetable broth
  • 1 cup of heavy cream
  • 2 cups of sharp cheddar cheese, shredded
  • 3 tablespoons of butter
  • 3 tablespoons of all-purpose flour
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Broccoli: Start by washing the broccoli florets thoroughly and chopping them into bite-sized pieces. Set them aside for later use.

  2. Sauté the Aromatics: In a large pot or Dutch oven, melt the butter over medium heat. Add the chopped onion and minced garlic, and sauté until they are soft and fragrant.

  3. Create the Roux: Sprinkle the flour over the sautéed aromatics and stir constantly for about 2 minutes to create a roux. This will help thicken the soup and give it a velvety texture.

  4. Add the Broth: Slowly pour in the chicken or vegetable broth, stirring continuously to avoid any lumps from forming. Bring the mixture to a gentle boil, then reduce the heat to a simmer.

  5. Simmer the Broccoli: Add the chopped broccoli to the pot and let it simmer in the broth until it becomes tender, but still vibrant green. This should take approximately 10-15 minutes.

  6. Blend the Soup: Using an immersion blender or transferring the mixture to a blender in batches, carefully blend the soup until it reaches your desired consistency. Some prefer a chunky texture, while others may enjoy a smoother finish.

  7. Add the Cream and Cheese: Pour in the heavy cream and stir well. Gradually add the shredded cheddar cheese, stirring until it melts and incorporates into the soup, creating a luxurious and creamy base.

  8. Season to Perfection: Taste the soup and adjust the seasoning with salt and pepper according to your preference. Remember that the cheddar cheese will contribute some saltiness, so be cautious when adding additional salt.

  9. Serve and Enjoy: Ladle the creamy broccoli and cheddar soup into bowls and garnish with a sprinkle of extra shredded cheddar or a drizzle of cream, if desired. Pair it with crusty bread, crackers, or a simple green salad for a delightful meal.

Tips and Variations

  • Extra Creaminess: For an even creamier texture, you can blend a portion of the cooked broccoli with the broth before adding the cheese and cream.
  • Vegetarian Option: To make this soup vegetarian-friendly, simply use vegetable broth instead of chicken broth.
  • Customize the Cheese: While sharp cheddar is a classic choice, feel free to experiment with different types of cheese like gruyère, gouda, or a combination of your favorites.
  • Toppings and Garnishes: Elevate the presentation and flavor of the soup by adding crispy bacon bits, a dollop of sour cream, or a sprinkle of chopped chives on top.

In Conclusion

Creamy broccoli and cheddar soup is a timeless classic that never fails to satisfy. Its smooth texture, comforting warmth, and rich flavor make it a beloved choice for casual weeknight dinners, elegant luncheons, or as a starter for a special gathering. With its simple yet sophisticated preparation, this soup is a testament to the beauty of uncomplicated, wholesome ingredients coming together in perfect harmony. So, next time you’re craving a bowl of comfort, give this recipe a try and savor the delightful combination of creamy broccoli and sharp cheddar in every spoonful.
