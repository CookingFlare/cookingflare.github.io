Irresistible Garlic Knots for Bread Lovers


Are you a bread lover? Do you find yourself constantly craving that warm, comforting aroma of freshly baked bread? If so, you’re in for a treat! In this recipe, we’re going to delve into the delightful world of garlic knots – a beloved classic with a flavorful twist that will leave you reaching for more.

Ingredients

Before we dive into the recipe, let’s take a look at the ingredients you’ll need to create these irresistible garlic knots.

  • 4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 1/4 tsp active dry yeast
  • 1 1/2 cups warm water
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 tsp sugar
  • 1 1/2 tsp salt
  • 6 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, melted
  • 2 tbsp fresh parsley, finely chopped
  • Salt to taste

Instructions

Step 1: Prepare the Dough

In a large mixing bowl, combine the warm water, sugar, and active dry yeast. Let it sit for about 5 minutes until the mixture becomes frothy. This indicates that the yeast is active and ready to use.

Next, add the olive oil and salt to the yeast mixture. Gradually stir in the all-purpose flour, 1 cup at a time, until a dough forms. Knead the dough on a floured surface for about 5-7 minutes, or until it becomes smooth and elastic.

Place the dough in a greased bowl, cover it with a clean kitchen towel, and let it rise in a warm place for about 1 hour, or until it doubles in size.

Step 2: Shape the Knots

Once the dough has risen, punch it down to release the air. Divide the dough into small portions and roll each portion into a long rope. Tie each rope into a knot and tuck the ends underneath to secure them.

Step 3: Bake the Knots

Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C). Place the knots on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper, leaving some space between each knot to allow for expansion. Bake for 15-20 minutes, or until the knots turn golden brown.

Step 4: Prepare the Garlic Butter

While the knots are baking, prepare the garlic butter by melting the unsalted butter in a saucepan over low heat. Add the minced garlic and cook for 1-2 minutes, stirring constantly. Remove from heat and stir in the chopped parsley.

Step 5: Coat the Knots

Once the garlic knots are out of the oven, brush them generously with the garlic butter mixture while they’re still warm. Sprinkle a pinch of salt over the knots for an extra burst of flavor.

Enjoy Your Irresistible Garlic Knots

Now that your garlic knots are beautifully coated and infused with the aromatic garlic butter, it’s time to indulge in their irresistible flavor. Serve them as a delightful appetizer, a side dish to complement your favorite pasta, or simply enjoy them on their own.
