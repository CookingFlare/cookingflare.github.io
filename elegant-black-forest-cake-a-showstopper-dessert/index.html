Elegant Black Forest Cake: A Showstopper Dessert


There’s nothing quite like a decadent black forest cake to make a statement at any occasion. With its rich layers of chocolate cake, luscious whipped cream, and tart cherries, this showstopper dessert is sure to impress even the most discerning palates. Whether you’re hosting a dinner party or simply want to treat yourself to a slice of indulgence, this elegant black forest cake recipe is the perfect choice.

Ingredients

For the cake:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 cups granulated sugar
  • 3/4 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons baking soda
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 cup whole milk
  • 1/2 cup vegetable oil
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 1 cup boiling water

For the cherry filling:

  • 2 cups pitted cherries (fresh or frozen)
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 2 tablespoons cornstarch
  • 1/4 cup water

For the whipped cream:

  • 2 cups heavy cream
  • 1/4 cup powdered sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease and flour two 9-inch round cake pans.

  2. In a large mixing bowl, combine the flour, sugar, cocoa powder, baking powder, baking soda, and salt. Whisk together until well combined.

  3. Add the eggs, milk, vegetable oil, and vanilla extract to the dry ingredients. Beat on medium speed for about 2 minutes, until the batter is smooth.

  4. Stir in the boiling water, mixing until the batter is thin. Don’t worry, it’s supposed to be thin!

  5. Divide the batter equally between the prepared cake pans. Smooth the tops with a spatula to ensure even baking.

  6. Bake in the preheated oven for 30 to 35 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center of the cakes comes out clean.

  7. While the cakes are baking, prepare the cherry filling. In a saucepan, combine the pitted cherries, granulated sugar, cornstarch, and water. Cook over medium heat, stirring constantly, until the mixture has thickened. Remove from heat and let it cool completely.

  8. Once the cakes have cooled, carefully remove them from the pans and place them on a wire rack to cool completely.

  9. In a chilled mixing bowl, beat the heavy cream, powdered sugar, and vanilla extract until stiff peaks form.

  10. To assemble the cake, place one layer of the chocolate cake on a serving plate. Spread a generous amount of whipped cream over the top, followed by a layer of the cherry filling. Repeat this process with the second layer of cake.

  11. Finish the cake by spreading a thin layer of whipped cream around the sides and piping rosettes on top. You can also garnish with chocolate curls or fresh cherries if desired.

  12. Refrigerate the cake for at least 4 hours before serving to allow the flavors to meld together.

Enjoy this elegant black forest cake as the centerpiece of your dessert table. Its rich chocolate flavor, combined with the tartness of the cherries and the lightness of the whipped cream, will leave your guests craving for more. Whether it’s a special occasion or just a regular day, this showstopper dessert is sure to impress and satisfy even the most discerning taste buds.
