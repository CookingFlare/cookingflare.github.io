Slow Cooker Creamy Tortellini Soup: A Cheesy Delight


Are you craving a cozy and comforting soup that’s easy to make and packed with flavor? Look no further than this Slow Cooker Creamy Tortellini Soup! This hearty and satisfying soup is the perfect dish for a chilly evening or a lazy weekend. The best part? It’s incredibly easy to prepare, allowing you to enjoy a delicious homemade meal with minimal effort. Let’s dive into the recipe and discover the magic of this cheesy delight.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound Italian sausage, casings removed
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 4 cups chicken broth
  • 1 can (14 ounces) diced tomatoes, undrained
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1/4 teaspoon red pepper flakes
  • 1 package (9 ounces) refrigerated cheese tortellini
  • 3 cups fresh spinach
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • Fresh basil, for garnish

Instructions

  1. Cook the Sausage: In a skillet over medium heat, cook the Italian sausage, breaking it into crumbles as it browns. Once fully cooked, transfer the sausage to a paper towel-lined plate to drain any excess grease.

  2. Prepare the Slow Cooker: Place the cooked sausage, diced onion, minced garlic, chicken broth, diced tomatoes, dried basil, dried oregano, salt, black pepper, and red pepper flakes in the slow cooker. Stir to combine all the ingredients.

  3. Cook on Low Heat: Cover the slow cooker and cook the soup on low for 6-7 hours, allowing the flavors to meld together and create a rich, savory base.

  4. Add the Tortellini and Spinach: About 30 minutes before serving, add the refrigerated cheese tortellini and fresh spinach to the slow cooker. Stir gently to submerge the tortellini and spinach in the flavorful broth.

  5. Stir in the Cream and Cheese: Pour in the heavy cream and grated Parmesan cheese, stirring until the cheese is fully melted and the soup takes on a velvety, creamy texture.

  6. Serve and Enjoy: Ladle the warm and creamy tortellini soup into bowls, garnishing each serving with fresh basil for a pop of color and an extra layer of aromatic flavor.

Tips and Tricks

  • For a lighter version of this soup, you can use turkey sausage and substitute half-and-half for the heavy cream.
  • Feel free to customize the soup with your favorite vegetables, such as diced carrots or sliced mushrooms, to add extra nutrition and texture.
  • Leftovers can be stored in an airtight container in the refrigerator for up to 3 days, although it’s so delicious, it might not last that long!

Conclusion

With its combination of savory Italian sausage, tender cheese-filled tortellini, and creamy broth, this Slow Cooker Creamy Tortellini Soup is a true crowd-pleaser. Whether you’re cooking for your family or entertaining guests, this recipe is sure to impress with its rich flavors and comforting appeal. Embrace the convenience of your slow cooker and delight in the aroma of this cheesy, soul-warming soup. So, gather your ingredients, set your slow cooker, and get ready to savor every spoonful of this delightful creation. Cheers to good food and happy hearts!
