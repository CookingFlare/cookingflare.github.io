Healthy Low Carb Broccoli Cheddar Soup Recipe


Broccoli cheddar soup is a classic comfort food that’s loved by many. However, traditional recipes often contain high amounts of carbs and unhealthy ingredients. Today, I’m here to share with you a healthy low carb version of broccoli cheddar soup that is not only delicious but also nourishing for your body.

Ingredients

  • 2 heads of broccoli, florets separated
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 small onion, diced
  • 3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 4 cups vegetable broth
  • 1 cup unsweetened almond milk
  • 1 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. In a large pot, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the diced onion and minced garlic, and sauté until the onion becomes translucent and fragrant.

  2. Add the broccoli florets to the pot and sauté for a few minutes until they start to soften slightly.

  3. Pour in the vegetable broth and bring the mixture to a boil. Reduce the heat to low and let it simmer for about 15-20 minutes or until the broccoli is tender.

  4. Using an immersion blender or a regular blender, puree the soup until smooth and creamy. If using a regular blender, be sure to blend in batches to avoid any accidents.

  5. Return the soup to the pot and stir in the almond milk. Heat the soup over low heat until it is warmed through.

  6. Gradually add the shredded cheddar cheese, stirring constantly until it melts and incorporates into the soup. Season with salt and pepper to taste.

  7. Once the cheese has melted and the soup is heated through, remove it from the heat.

  8. Serve the healthy low carb broccoli cheddar soup hot, garnished with a sprinkle of shredded cheddar cheese if desired.

Why Choose This Recipe?

This healthy low carb broccoli cheddar soup recipe is perfect for those who are following a low carb or keto diet, or simply looking for a lighter version of this classic soup. By using fresh broccoli and unsweetened almond milk, we’ve eliminated unnecessary carbs and reduced the overall calorie content of the soup. The addition of cheddar cheese provides a rich and creamy flavor without compromising on the healthiness of the dish.

Broccoli is packed with essential nutrients like fiber, vitamins A and C, and potassium. It’s also low in calories, making it an excellent choice for those who are watching their weight. By incorporating broccoli into this soup, you’re not only adding a delicious flavor but also boosting the nutritional value of your meal.

The use of vegetable broth instead of heavy cream further reduces the fat content of the soup, without sacrificing its creamy texture. The unsweetened almond milk adds a subtle nutty flavor and creaminess, while keeping the carbs low.

With this healthy low carb broccoli cheddar soup recipe, you can enjoy a comforting and satisfying meal without any guilt. It’s perfect for those chilly evenings when you crave something warm and nourishing.

So, give this recipe a try and let us know how you like it. Enjoy the goodness of broccoli and the indulgence of cheddar cheese, all in one delicious bowl of soup. Stay healthy and happy!
