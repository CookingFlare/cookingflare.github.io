Homemade Pizza Rolls: A Crowd-Pleasing Appetizer


If you’re looking for a delicious and crowd-pleasing appetizer that’s perfect for any occasion, homemade pizza rolls are the way to go. These bite-sized snacks are packed with all the flavors of a classic pizza, and they’re incredibly easy to make. Whether you’re hosting a party, game night, or simply craving a savory treat, these homemade pizza rolls are sure to be a hit. In this blog post, we’ll walk you through a step-by-step guide to creating these delectable bites and share some tips to make them even more irresistible.

Ingredients

Before we get started, let’s gather all the ingredients we’ll need to make these mouthwatering homemade pizza rolls:

  • 1 pound pizza dough
  • 1/2 cup pizza sauce
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/4 cup pepperoni slices
  • 1/4 cup finely chopped bell peppers
  • 1/4 cup finely chopped onions
  • 1/4 cup finely chopped mushrooms
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1/2 teaspoon red pepper flakes (optional)
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • Marinara sauce for dipping

Instructions

Now that we have all our ingredients ready, it’s time to roll up our sleeves and get cooking!

Step 1: Prepare the Pizza Dough

Start by preheating your oven to 400°F (200°C). Roll out the pizza dough on a lightly floured surface into a rectangle, about 1/4 inch thick.

Step 2: Add the Toppings

Spread the pizza sauce evenly over the rolled-out dough, leaving a small border around the edges. Sprinkle the mozzarella cheese over the sauce, followed by the pepperoni slices, bell peppers, onions, and mushrooms. Then, sprinkle the Parmesan cheese, garlic powder, dried oregano, and red pepper flakes (if using) over the toppings.

Step 3: Roll It Up

Starting from one of the longer edges, tightly roll the dough into a log, making sure to seal the seam at the bottom. Using a sharp knife, cut the log into 1-inch thick slices.

Step 4: Bake to Perfection

Place the pizza rolls on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper, leaving some space between each roll. Brush the tops of the rolls with olive oil. Bake in the preheated oven for 15-20 minutes, or until the rolls are golden brown and the cheese is bubbly.

Step 5: Serve and Enjoy

Once the pizza rolls are ready, remove them from the oven and let them cool for a few minutes. Serve the rolls warm with marinara sauce for dipping and watch them disappear in no time!

Tips for Success

  • Customize the toppings: Feel free to get creative with the toppings. You can add cooked sausage, ham, bacon, or even pineapple for a unique twist.
  • Make-ahead: You can prepare the pizza rolls ahead of time and refrigerate them until you’re ready to bake. This makes them a convenient option for entertaining.
  • Freeze for later: These pizza rolls freeze beautifully. Simply arrange the unbaked rolls on a baking sheet and place them in the freezer until firm. Once frozen, transfer them to a resealable bag and store for up to 2 months. When ready to bake, simply pop them in the oven straight from the freezer, adding a few extra minutes to the baking time.

Homemade pizza rolls are a versatile and crowd-pleasing appetizer that’s perfect for any occasion. With their crispy exterior, gooey cheese, and flavorful toppings, they are sure to be a hit at your next gathering. Whether you’re serving them as a snack, appetizer, or even a fun dinner option, these homemade pizza rolls are guaranteed to impress. So, gather your ingredients and get ready to delight your guests with these irresistible bites!
