10 Easy Gluten-Free and Dairy-Free Soups for Allergy Relief


Soup is more than just comfort food—it’s nourishment, warmth, and often the go-to meal for battling allergies and sensitivities. For those with dietary restrictions, the task of finding recipes that are both gluten-free and dairy-free can become a challenge. Thankfully, plenty of flavorful, wholesome options exist. These 10 easy soup recipes are designed for people seeking a bit of allergy relief, whether you’re dealing with gluten intolerance, dairy sensitivities, or simply looking for soothing, nutrient-rich meals.

1. Classic Chicken & Vegetable Soup

Perfect for immune support and soothing an upset body, this chicken-based soup is a staple for allergy relief.

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb shredded cooked chicken
  • 1 medium onion, diced
  • 2 medium carrots, diced
  • 2 celery stalks, chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 4 cups gluten-free chicken stock
  • 1 bay leaf
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Heat olive oil in a large pot. Sauté onion, garlic, carrots, and celery until softened.
  2. Add the shredded chicken, chicken stock, bay leaf, and season with salt and pepper.
  3. Bring to a boil, then simmer for 20–25 minutes. Remove the bay leaf before serving.

2. Red Lentil Soup with Turmeric

This vibrant soup is naturally thickened by red lentils and carries nourishing anti-inflammatory benefits from turmeric.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup red lentils, rinsed
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 4 cups vegetable broth
  • Lemon wedges for serving
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Heat olive oil in a saucepan and sauté onion and garlic until fragrant.
  2. Stir in turmeric and red lentils, cooking for 1–2 minutes.
  3. Add vegetable broth, bring to a boil, then reduce heat and simmer for about 25 minutes until lentils are soft.
  4. Blend slightly for a creamier texture or leave chunky. Serve with a squeeze of lemon juice for added brightness.

3. Hearty Italian Minestrone

This naturally gluten-free minestrone pairs fresh vegetables with beans for a filling and flavorful meal.

Ingredients:

  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 3 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 zucchini, chopped
  • 1 carrot, diced
  • 1 cup green beans, cut into 1” pieces
  • 1 can diced tomatoes (14 oz)
  • 3 cups gluten-free vegetable broth
  • 1 can cannellini beans (14 oz), drained
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • 1 tsp dried basil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Heat olive oil and sauté onion, garlic, carrot, zucchini, and green beans until fragrant.
  2. Add diced tomatoes, cannellini beans, vegetable broth, and seasonings.
  3. Simmer for 30 minutes to meld the flavors.

4. Spiced Coconut Carrot Soup

This dairy-free soup achieves a creamy richness with coconut milk. It’s lightly spiced and packed with vitamins from carrots.

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb carrots, peeled and chopped
  • 1 medium onion, diced
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 3 cups gluten-free vegetable broth
  • 1 cup coconut milk
  • 1 tsp ground cumin
  • 1/2 tsp ground coriander
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Heat oil in a pot and sauté onion and garlic. Add cumin and coriander.
  2. Mix in carrots, vegetable broth, and coconut milk. Simmer for 20 minutes until the carrots soften.
  3. Blend until smooth and season to taste before serving.

5. Tomato Basil Soup

Bright, tangy, and immensely comforting, this tomato soup is a classic with a dairy-free twist.

Ingredients:

  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 medium onion, diced
  • 3 garlic cloves, minced
  • 3 cups diced tomatoes
  • 1 cup unsweetened coconut milk
  • 2 cups vegetable broth
  • 1 tsp dried basil or fresh basil leaves, chopped
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Sauté onion and garlic in olive oil until translucent.
  2. Add diced tomatoes, basil, coconut milk, and vegetable broth.
  3. Simmer for 15–20 minutes, then blend to your desired consistency.

6. Sweet Potato & Ginger Soup

This natural antioxidant-rich soup combines sweet potatoes with the warming spice of ginger, making it perfect for allergy relief.

Ingredients:

  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 medium onion, chopped
  • 2 large sweet potatoes, peeled and cubed
  • 1 tsp fresh ginger, grated
  • 3 cups gluten-free vegetable broth
  • 1 cup coconut milk
  • Salt to taste

Instructions:

  1. Sauté onion with olive oil, then add ginger and sweet potatoes.
  2. Pour in vegetable broth and coconut milk, and simmer for 25 minutes.
  3. Blend until creamy for a rich, smooth consistency.

7. Zucchini and Leek Soup

A light, nutrient-dense soup that’s naturally gluten- and dairy-free, perfect for quick and easy meals.

Ingredients:

  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 leeks, washed and chopped
  • 2 medium zucchini, sliced
  • 4 cups gluten-free vegetable broth
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Sauté leeks with olive oil until tender.
  2. Add zucchini slices and vegetable broth, simmering for 20 minutes.
  3. Blend for a silky texture, or serve chunky, depending on preference.

8. Pumpkin Soup with Coconut Cream

Take advantage of fall flavors with this aromatic pumpkin soup, enriched with dairy-free coconut cream.

Ingredients:

  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 3 cups pumpkin puree
  • 2 cups vegetable broth
  • 1 cup coconut cream
  • 1/2 tsp nutmeg
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Heat oil, then sauté garlic. Add pumpkin puree, nutmeg, and vegetable broth.
  2. Simmer for 15 minutes, then swirl in the coconut cream. Blend for extra creaminess if desired.

9. Thai Coconut Soup with Lemongrass

This soup bursts with exotic flavors from lemongrass and lime paired with creamy coconut milk—an excellent allergy-friendly recipe.

Ingredients:

  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 tsp fresh ginger, grated
  • 1 stalk lemongrass
  • 1 cup coconut milk
  • 3 cups vegetable broth
  • 1 cup mushrooms, sliced
  • Juice of 1 lime
  • Salt to taste

Instructions:

  1. Sauté garlic, ginger, and lemongrass in olive oil.
  2. Add vegetable broth, coconut milk, and mushrooms. Simmer for 15–20 minutes.
  3. Finish with lime juice and serve hot.

10. Roasted Butternut Squash Soup

This velvety soup brings autumn vibes with roasted butternut squash and an allergy-friendly dairy-free preparation.

Ingredients:

  • 1 medium butternut squash, peeled, cubed, and roasted
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 3 cups gluten-free vegetable broth
  • 1 cup coconut milk
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Sauté onion and garlic, then add roasted butternut squash.
  2. Pour in vegetable broth and coconut milk, simmering for 20 minutes.
  3. Blend until smooth and creamy.

Final Thoughts

Finding meals that cater to gluten-free and dairy-free needs doesn’t mean sacrificing flavor or variety. These soups are perfect for nourishing your body while avoiding allergens. Whether you’re craving something classic, exotic, creamy, or hearty, these recipes deliver in both taste and health benefits. Bookmark these ideas for meal prep, busy weeknights, or simply a helping hand during allergy season.
