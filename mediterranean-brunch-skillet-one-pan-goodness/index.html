Mediterranean Brunch Skillet: One-Pan Goodness


Are you looking for a delicious and nutritious brunch recipe that’s quick and easy to make? Look no further than this Mediterranean Brunch Skillet! Packed with vibrant flavors and wholesome ingredients, this one-pan wonder is perfect for lazy weekends or busy mornings when you want a satisfying meal without the fuss.

Ingredients

  • 4 eggs
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 small onion, diced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 red bell pepper, diced
  • 1 yellow bell pepper, diced
  • 1 zucchini, diced
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1/2 cup pitted Kalamata olives, sliced
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried thyme
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley, chopped (for garnish)

Instructions

  1. Heat the olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Add the diced onion and minced garlic, and sauté until fragrant and translucent.

  2. Add the diced bell peppers and zucchini to the skillet, and cook for another 5 minutes until they start to soften.

  3. Stir in the cherry tomatoes, Kalamata olives, dried oregano, and dried thyme. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Cook for an additional 2 minutes to allow the flavors to meld together.

  4. Create little wells in the mixture and crack the eggs directly into them. Cover the skillet and cook for about 5 minutes, or until the eggs are cooked to your desired level of doneness. If you prefer your eggs runny, cook for a shorter time; if you like them fully cooked, leave them a bit longer.

  5. Remove the skillet from heat and garnish with freshly chopped parsley.

  6. Serve the Mediterranean Brunch Skillet directly from the pan, allowing everyone to scoop out a portion of the deliciousness. Pair it with a crusty bread or warm pita for a complete meal.

Why You’ll Love It

The Mediterranean Brunch Skillet is a fantastic choice for those who appreciate the vibrant flavors of the Mediterranean cuisine. Packed with fresh vegetables, tangy olives, and aromatic herbs, this dish is a true celebration of the region’s culinary heritage.

Not only is this recipe bursting with flavors, but it’s also incredibly versatile. Feel free to customize it with your favorite vegetables or add a sprinkle of feta cheese for an extra touch of creaminess. You can even top it with a drizzle of balsamic glaze or a dollop of Greek yogurt for added richness.

One of the best things about this recipe is that it’s all cooked in just one pan, which means minimal cleanup and more time to enjoy your meal. It’s perfect for lazy weekends when you want to savor a leisurely brunch without spending hours in the kitchen.

So, whether you’re hosting a brunch gathering with friends or simply treating yourself to a delicious morning meal, the Mediterranean Brunch Skillet is sure to satisfy your taste buds and leave you feeling nourished and energized.

Conclusion

With its vibrant flavors, wholesome ingredients, and easy preparation, the Mediterranean Brunch Skillet is a must-try recipe for any brunch enthusiast. Gather your favorite ingredients, grab a skillet, and whip up this one-pan wonder to enjoy a taste of the Mediterranean right at your breakfast table. So, go ahead and give it a try – your taste buds will thank you!
