Luscious Lavender: Lavender Lemon Springtime Macarons


Welcome to the world of delicate and delightful macarons with a springtime twist! If you’re a fan of floral and citrus flavors, get ready to indulge in the luscious combination of lavender and lemon in these exquisite macarons. This recipe is perfect for those looking to elevate their baking skills and create a sophisticated treat that’s perfect for any occasion. So, roll up your sleeves, gather your ingredients, and let’s dive into the art of making Lavender Lemon Springtime Macarons.

Ingredients:

For the Macaron Shells:

  • 1 3/4 cups powdered sugar
  • 1 cup almond flour
  • 3 large egg whites, at room temperature
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/2 teaspoon lavender extract
  • Purple food coloring (optional)

For the Lemon Buttercream Filling:

  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1 1/2 cups powdered sugar
  • 1 tablespoon fresh lemon juice
  • 1 teaspoon lemon zest
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/4 teaspoon lavender extract
  • Yellow food coloring (optional)

Instructions:

Making the Macaron Shells:

  1. Prepare the Almond Mixture: In a food processor, combine the powdered sugar and almond flour. Pulse the mixture until fine and well combined. Sift the mixture through a fine-mesh sieve into a large mixing bowl, discarding any larger almond pieces.

  2. Whip the Egg Whites: Using a hand or stand mixer, beat the egg whites on medium speed until foamy. Gradually add the granulated sugar while continuing to beat. Increase the speed to high and beat until stiff, glossy peaks form. Add the vanilla extract, lavender extract, and food coloring (if using) and beat until combined.

  3. Fold the Dry Ingredients: Gently fold the almond mixture into the whipped egg whites in three additions, ensuring that the mixture is well incorporated without overmixing. The batter should be thick and smooth, with a lava-like consistency.

  4. Pipe the Macarons: Transfer the batter to a piping bag fitted with a round tip. Pipe 1-inch circles onto parchment-lined baking sheets. Tap the baking sheets firmly against the counter to release any air bubbles. Let the piped macarons sit at room temperature for 30 minutes to form a skin.

  5. Bake and Cool: Preheat the oven to 300°F (150°C). Bake the macarons for 15-18 minutes, rotating the pans halfway through baking. Once baked, let the macarons cool completely on the baking sheets before carefully removing them.

Making the Lemon Buttercream Filling:

  1. Cream the Butter: In a mixing bowl, beat the softened butter until creamy and smooth.

  2. Add the Flavors: Gradually add the powdered sugar, lemon juice, lemon zest, vanilla extract, lavender extract, and food coloring (if using) to the creamed butter. Beat until the filling is light and fluffy.

  3. Assemble the Macarons: Pair the cooled macaron shells based on similar sizes. Transfer the lemon buttercream filling to a piping bag and pipe a small dollop onto the flat side of one shell. Gently sandwich another shell on top, creating a delightful macaron sandwich.

  4. Chill and Enjoy: Once assembled, refrigerate the macarons for at least 24 hours to allow the flavors to meld and the texture to mature. Serve the macarons at room temperature and savor the delightful combination of lavender and lemon with every bite.

Conclusion:

These Lavender Lemon Springtime Macarons are a celebration of the vibrant flavors of spring. The delicate floral notes of lavender combined with the zesty brightness of lemon create a harmonious balance that’s sure to impress your taste buds. Whether you’re hosting a springtime gathering, a bridal shower, or simply indulging in a delightful treat, these macarons are a perfect choice. So, embrace the art of baking and treat yourself to the luscious flavors of spring with these exquisite macarons. Happy baking!
