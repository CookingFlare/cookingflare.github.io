Delicious Green Bean Casserole Recipe for a Crowd-Pleasing Side Dish


Are you looking for a delectable side dish that will leave your guests asking for more? Look no further than this scrumptious green bean casserole recipe. Perfect for family gatherings, holiday dinners, or potlucks, this crowd-pleasing dish is sure to become a favorite. With its creamy texture, crispy topping, and irresistible flavor, this green bean casserole will elevate any meal to new heights.

Ingredients:

  • 2 cans (10.5 ounces each) cream of mushroom soup
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1/4 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 6 cups cooked green beans (fresh or frozen)
  • 2 2/3 cups French’s Crispy Fried Onions

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C).

  2. In a large bowl, mix the cream of mushroom soup, milk, black pepper, and garlic powder until well combined.

  3. Add the cooked green beans to the mixture and stir until the beans are evenly coated.

  4. Transfer the green bean mixture to a 3-quart casserole dish, spreading it out evenly.

  5. Bake the green bean casserole in the preheated oven for 25 minutes.

  6. Once the casserole has baked for 25 minutes, remove it from the oven and sprinkle 1 1/3 cups of French’s Crispy Fried Onions over the top.

  7. Return the casserole to the oven and bake for an additional 5 minutes, or until the onions are golden brown and crispy.

  8. Remove the casserole from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes before serving.

Tips and Tricks:

  • For a fresher flavor, consider using fresh green beans instead of frozen.
  • Add a dash of hot sauce or Worcestershire sauce to the soup mixture for an extra kick of flavor.
  • If you’re short on time, you can prepare the casserole in advance and store it in the refrigerator until you’re ready to bake it.
  • Experiment with different types of onions or toppings to customize the casserole to your liking.

Why This Recipe Works:

This green bean casserole recipe is a classic for a reason. The combination of creamy mushroom soup, tender green beans, and crunchy fried onions creates a symphony of flavors and textures that is simply irresistible. The dish is easy to prepare, making it perfect for busy weeknights or special occasions when you want to impress your guests without spending hours in the kitchen.

Serving Suggestions:

This green bean casserole pairs perfectly with roasted turkey, baked ham, or grilled chicken. It also complements other side dishes such as mashed potatoes, cranberry sauce, or roasted vegetables. Whether you’re hosting a cozy dinner at home or a festive gathering with friends and family, this green bean casserole is sure to be a hit.

Conclusion:

With its creamy, comforting goodness and simple yet satisfying ingredients, this green bean casserole recipe is a must-try for anyone who enjoys delicious, home-cooked meals. Whether you’re a seasoned cook or a novice in the kitchen, this recipe is guaranteed to deliver a side dish that will have everyone coming back for seconds. So, roll up your sleeves, gather your ingredients, and get ready to delight your taste buds with this delightful green bean casserole. Enjoy!
