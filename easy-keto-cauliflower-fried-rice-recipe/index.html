Easy Keto Cauliflower Fried Rice Recipe


If you’re looking for a delicious and satisfying low-carb meal, this Easy Keto Cauliflower Fried Rice Recipe is the perfect choice. Packed with flavor, nutrients, and a satisfying texture, this dish is a fantastic alternative to traditional fried rice. Whether you’re following a ketogenic diet or simply looking to incorporate more vegetables into your meals, this recipe is sure to become a new favorite in your kitchen.

Ingredients

  • 1 medium head of cauliflower
  • 2 tablespoons of olive oil
  • 3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 2 carrots, diced
  • 1/2 cup of peas
  • 2 eggs, beaten
  • 4 tablespoons of soy sauce or coconut aminos
  • 1 teaspoon of sesame oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Green onions for garnish

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Cauliflower: Start by washing the cauliflower and removing the leaves and stem. Cut the cauliflower into florets and place them in a food processor. Pulse until the cauliflower resembles rice. If you don’t have a food processor, you can also use a grater to achieve a similar texture.

  2. Cook the Eggs: Heat a tablespoon of olive oil in a large pan over medium heat. Add the beaten eggs and scramble them until they are cooked through. Once done, remove the eggs from the pan and set them aside.

  3. Cook the Aromatics: In the same pan, add another tablespoon of olive oil. Sauté the minced garlic and chopped onion until they become fragrant and slightly translucent.

  4. Add Vegetables: Stir in the diced carrots and cook for a few minutes until they begin to soften. Then, add the peas and continue to cook for an additional 2-3 minutes.

  5. Incorporate Cauliflower Rice: Add the cauliflower rice to the pan, and stir to combine with the vegetables. Cook for about 5-7 minutes, stirring occasionally, until the cauliflower is tender yet still slightly crisp.

  6. Season the Dish: Pour in the soy sauce or coconut aminos, sesame oil, and season with salt and pepper to taste. Stir well to ensure that the flavors are evenly distributed throughout the dish.

  7. Finish and Serve: Once the cauliflower fried rice is cooked to your liking, return the cooked eggs to the pan and stir to combine. Remove the pan from the heat and garnish with chopped green onions before serving.

Tips and Variations

  • For added protein, you can incorporate cooked chicken, shrimp, or tofu into the dish.
  • Feel free to customize the vegetables based on your preferences. Bell peppers, broccoli, or even mushrooms can be great additions to this recipe.
  • To enhance the flavor, consider adding a dash of ginger or a sprinkle of red pepper flakes for a hint of heat.

Final Thoughts

This Easy Keto Cauliflower Fried Rice Recipe is a versatile and flavorful dish that can be enjoyed as a main course or a side dish. With its vibrant colors and savory aroma, it’s a feast for the senses that will leave you feeling satisfied and nourished. Whether you’re following a keto lifestyle or simply looking for a healthier alternative to traditional fried rice, this recipe is a testament to the fact that healthy eating can be both delicious and fulfilling. Give it a try and savor every flavorful bite!
