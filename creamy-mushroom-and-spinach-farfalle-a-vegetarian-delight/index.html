Creamy Mushroom and Spinach Farfalle: A Vegetarian Delight


If you’re in the mood for a comforting and indulgent vegetarian pasta dish, look no further than this creamy mushroom and spinach farfalle recipe. This dish is a delightful combination of earthy mushrooms, vibrant spinach, and perfectly cooked farfalle pasta, all brought together with a luscious, creamy sauce. Whether you’re a vegetarian or simply looking to incorporate more meatless meals into your diet, this recipe is sure to satisfy your cravings and impress your family and friends.

Ingredients

  • 12 ounces farfalle pasta
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 medium onion, finely chopped
  • 3 garlic cloves, minced
  • 10 ounces cremini mushrooms, sliced
  • 2 cups fresh spinach leaves
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley for garnish

Instructions

  1. Cook the Farfalle: Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Add the farfalle pasta and cook according to the package instructions until al dente. Drain the pasta, reserving 1/2 cup of the pasta water, and set aside.

  2. Sauté the Aromatics: In a large skillet, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the chopped onion and sauté for 3-4 minutes until it becomes translucent. Add the minced garlic and cook for an additional 1-2 minutes until fragrant.

  3. Add the Mushrooms: Add the sliced cremini mushrooms to the skillet with the onions and garlic. Cook for 5-7 minutes, stirring occasionally, until the mushrooms are golden brown and have released their juices.

  4. Introduce the Spinach: Once the mushrooms are cooked, add the fresh spinach leaves to the skillet. Cook for 2-3 minutes, or until the spinach wilts and reduces in volume.

  5. Create the Creamy Sauce: Pour the heavy cream into the skillet with the mushroom and spinach mixture. Stir gently to combine all the ingredients. Allow the cream to simmer for 3-4 minutes until it thickens slightly.

  6. Incorporate the Pasta: Add the cooked farfalle pasta to the skillet with the creamy mushroom and spinach sauce. Toss everything together gently, ensuring the pasta is well coated with the sauce. If the sauce seems too thick, add some of the reserved pasta water to loosen it up.

  7. Season and Serve: Stir in the grated Parmesan cheese, and season the dish with salt and pepper to taste. Give everything a final toss to ensure the flavors are well distributed. Garnish with fresh parsley before serving.
