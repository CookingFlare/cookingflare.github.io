Crispy Maple-Glazed Brussels Sprouts: A Sweet and Savory Side


If you’re looking for a delicious and unique side dish to elevate your next meal, look no further than these crispy maple-glazed Brussels sprouts. This recipe takes the humble Brussels sprout and transforms it into a sweet and savory delight that will have your taste buds singing. Whether you’re a Brussels sprout enthusiast or a skeptic, this dish is sure to win you over with its irresistible flavor and satisfying crunch.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb Brussels sprouts, trimmed and halved
  • 2-3 tablespoons olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1/4 cup maple syrup
  • 2 tablespoons balsamic vinegar
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/4 cup chopped pecans (optional)

Instructions

  1. Preheat and Prep: Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C). While the oven is heating, prepare the Brussels sprouts by trimming the ends and cutting them in half. Place the halved sprouts in a large mixing bowl.

  2. Toss in Flavor: Drizzle the Brussels sprouts with olive oil and season with salt and pepper. Toss the sprouts in the oil, ensuring they are evenly coated.

  3. Roast to Perfection: Spread the Brussels sprouts in a single layer on a baking sheet, ensuring they are not overcrowded. Roast in the preheated oven for 20-25 minutes, or until they are crispy and golden brown.

  4. Create the Glaze: While the Brussels sprouts are roasting, prepare the maple glaze. In a small saucepan, combine the maple syrup, balsamic vinegar, and minced garlic. Simmer the mixture over medium heat for 5-7 minutes, or until it has thickened slightly.

  5. Glaze the Sprouts: Once the Brussels sprouts are crispy and golden, remove them from the oven and transfer them back to the mixing bowl. Drizzle the maple glaze over the roasted sprouts and toss to coat them evenly.

  6. Optional Crunch: For an extra layer of texture and flavor, sprinkle the glazed Brussels sprouts with chopped pecans before serving.

  7. Serve and Enjoy: Transfer the maple-glazed Brussels sprouts to a serving dish and delight in the sweet, savory, and crispy goodness. These sprouts make a delightful side to any meal, whether it’s a festive holiday feast or a casual weeknight dinner.

Final Thoughts

These crispy maple-glazed Brussels sprouts are a delightful fusion of sweet and savory flavors, making them a perfect addition to your culinary repertoire. Whether you’re a fan of Brussels sprouts or looking to convert skeptics, this recipe is sure to impress. The crispy texture, combined with the sweet and tangy glaze, creates a dish that is both comforting and sophisticated. So, the next time you’re in the mood for a side dish that’s a little bit special, give these maple-glazed Brussels sprouts a try and savor the irresistible combination of flavors.

So, there you have it – a simple yet sophisticated recipe for crispy maple-glazed Brussels sprouts that will elevate any meal to new heights. If you’re in the mood for a side dish that’s both sweet and savory with a satisfying crunch, this recipe is a must-try. Enjoy!
