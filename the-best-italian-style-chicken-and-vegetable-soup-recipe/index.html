The Best Italian-Style Chicken and Vegetable Soup Recipe


There’s nothing quite like a hearty, flavorful bowl of soup to warm you up and satisfy your taste buds. This Italian-style chicken and vegetable soup is a perfect combination of robust flavors and wholesome ingredients, making it a comforting and nutritious choice for any meal. Whether you’re looking for a quick weeknight dinner or a soothing dish to enjoy on a lazy Sunday afternoon, this soup is sure to become a staple in your recipe collection.

Ingredients

  • 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 3 garlic cloves, minced
  • 2 carrots, diced
  • 2 celery stalks, diced
  • 1 zucchini, diced
  • 1 can (14 oz) diced tomatoes
  • 6 cups chicken broth
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 cup small pasta (such as ditalini or small shells)
  • Fresh parsley, chopped, for garnish

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Chicken: Season the chicken breasts with salt and pepper. In a large pot, heat 1 tablespoon of olive oil over medium-high heat. Add the chicken breasts and cook until golden brown on both sides and cooked through, about 6-7 minutes per side. Remove the chicken from the pot and let it rest for a few minutes. Once cooled, shred the chicken using two forks and set aside.

  2. Sauté the Aromatics: In the same pot, add the remaining tablespoon of olive oil. Sauté the chopped onion and garlic until they become soft and fragrant, about 3-4 minutes.

  3. Add the Vegetables: Stir in the diced carrots, celery, and zucchini. Cook for another 5 minutes, allowing the vegetables to slightly soften.

  4. Simmer the Soup: Pour in the diced tomatoes and chicken broth. Add the dried oregano, dried basil, and the shredded chicken. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Bring the soup to a boil, then reduce the heat and let it simmer for about 20 minutes, allowing the flavors to meld together.

  5. Cook the Pasta: Meanwhile, in a separate pot, cook the small pasta according to the package instructions. Drain and set aside.

  6. Combine and Serve: Once the soup has simmered, add the cooked pasta to the pot. Stir to combine all the ingredients. Taste and adjust the seasoning if needed. Serve the soup hot, garnished with freshly chopped parsley.

Tips and Variations

  • For a richer flavor, you can add a parmesan rind to the soup while it simmers.
  • Feel free to customize the vegetables based on your preferences or what’s in season. You can add bell peppers, spinach, or even some green beans to the mix.
  • If you prefer a thicker soup, you can mash some of the cooked vegetables against the side of the pot with a spoon before adding the pasta.

Final Thoughts

This Italian-style chicken and vegetable soup is a versatile dish that’s both satisfying and nourishing. With the perfect balance of tender chicken, vibrant vegetables, and aromatic herbs, each spoonful is a delightful experience. Whether you’re cooking for a crowd or simply craving a cozy meal at home, this recipe is a must-try. Embrace the comforting flavors of Italy with this soul-warming soup that will surely become a beloved favorite in your kitchen. Enjoy!
