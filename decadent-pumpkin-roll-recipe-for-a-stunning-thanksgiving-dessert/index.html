Decadent Pumpkin Roll Recipe for a Stunning Thanksgiving Dessert


Thanksgiving is just around the corner, and what better way to impress your family and friends than with a stunning and decadent dessert? Look no further than this mouthwatering Decadent Pumpkin Roll recipe. With its rich flavors and beautiful presentation, this dessert is sure to be the star of your Thanksgiving table.

Ingredients

For the pumpkin roll:

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground nutmeg
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground ginger
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 3 large eggs
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 2/3 cup canned pumpkin puree

For the filling:

  • 8 ounces cream cheese, softened
  • 1 cup powdered sugar
  • 1/4 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C). Grease and line a 15x10-inch jelly roll pan with parchment paper.

  2. In a medium bowl, whisk together the flour, baking powder, cinnamon, nutmeg, ginger, and salt. Set aside.

  3. In a large mixing bowl, beat the eggs and granulated sugar until thick and pale yellow in color. Add the pumpkin puree and mix until well combined.

  4. Gradually add the dry ingredients to the wet ingredients, mixing until just combined. Be careful not to overmix.

  5. Pour the batter into the prepared jelly roll pan, spreading it evenly with a spatula.

  6. Bake for 12-15 minutes, or until the top springs back when lightly touched.

  7. While the pumpkin roll is baking, prepare a clean kitchen towel by dusting it generously with powdered sugar.

  8. Once the pumpkin roll is done baking, immediately invert it onto the prepared kitchen towel. Carefully peel off the parchment paper.

  9. Starting at one short end, gently roll the pumpkin roll and the towel together. Place it seam side down on a wire rack and let it cool completely.

  10. While the pumpkin roll is cooling, prepare the filling. In a mixing bowl, beat the cream cheese, powdered sugar, butter, and vanilla extract until smooth and creamy.

  11. Once the pumpkin roll has cooled completely, carefully unroll it from the towel.

  12. Spread the cream cheese filling evenly over the pumpkin roll, leaving a small border around the edges.

  13. Gently roll the pumpkin roll back up, this time without the towel. Place it on a serving platter, seam side down.

  14. Cover the pumpkin roll with plastic wrap and refrigerate for at least 1 hour, or until the filling is firm.

  15. To serve, slice the pumpkin roll into thick slices and garnish with a dusting of powdered sugar.

Tips and Variations

  • If you prefer a more pronounced spice flavor, you can increase the amount of cinnamon, nutmeg, and ginger in the pumpkin roll batter.

  • For an extra touch of decadence, you can add chopped pecans or walnuts to the cream cheese filling.

  • Serve the pumpkin roll with a dollop of whipped cream or a scoop of vanilla ice cream for an indulgent treat.

  • This recipe can be made ahead of time and stored in the refrigerator for up to 3 days. Just make sure to wrap it tightly in plastic wrap to prevent drying out.

Now that you have this delightful Decadent Pumpkin Roll recipe in your arsenal, you can confidently impress your loved ones with a stunning Thanksgiving dessert. The combination of moist pumpkin cake and creamy cream cheese filling is truly irresistible. Enjoy and have a wonderful Thanksgiving!
