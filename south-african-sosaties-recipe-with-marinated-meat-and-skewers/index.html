South African Sosaties Recipe with Marinated Meat and Skewers


If you’re looking to add a touch of South African flavor to your grilling repertoire, then you’re in for a treat with this Sosaties recipe. Sosaties are a traditional South African dish featuring marinated meat skewered with a variety of flavorful ingredients. The combination of tender meat and aromatic marinade makes Sosaties a standout dish for any occasion, from casual gatherings to special celebrations. Let’s dive into the details of this mouthwatering recipe and get ready to elevate your grilling game!

Ingredients:

  • 2 pounds of lamb, beef, or chicken, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 2 large onions, chopped
  • 2 large bell peppers, cut into chunks
  • 1 cup dried apricots
  • 1 cup white vinegar
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1 cup water
  • 3 bay leaves
  • 1 tablespoon curry powder
  • 1 teaspoon ground turmeric
  • 1 teaspoon ground coriander
  • 1 teaspoon ground ginger
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Wooden skewers, soaked in water

Marinade Preparation:

  1. Begin by preparing the marinade. In a saucepan, combine the white vinegar, sugar, water, bay leaves, curry powder, turmeric, coriander, ginger, salt, and pepper. Bring the mixture to a gentle boil, stirring to dissolve the sugar and spices. Allow the marinade to cool completely.

Marinating the Meat:

  1. Place the meat, onions, bell peppers, and dried apricots in a large bowl. Pour the cooled marinade over the meat and vegetables, ensuring that everything is well-coated. Cover the bowl and refrigerate for at least 4 hours, or preferably overnight, to allow the flavors to meld.

Skewering and Grilling:

  1. Once the meat has marinated sufficiently, it’s time to assemble the Sosaties. Thread the marinated meat, onions, peppers, and apricots onto the soaked wooden skewers, alternating the ingredients for a colorful and flavorful arrangement.

  2. Preheat your grill to medium-high heat. Place the assembled Sosaties on the grill and cook for approximately 10-15 minutes, turning occasionally, until the meat is cooked to your desired level of doneness and the vegetables have developed a delicious char.

Serving Suggestion:

  1. Serve the Sosaties hot off the grill, accompanied by a fresh salad, fluffy rice, or traditional South African side dishes for a complete and satisfying meal. The tender meat, infused with the rich flavors of the marinade, paired with the sweetness of the apricots and the smokiness from the grill, creates a sensory delight that’s sure to impress your guests.

Conclusion:

Sosaties are a fantastic addition to any grilling occasion, offering a unique blend of sweet, savory, and aromatic flavors that will transport your taste buds to the vibrant culinary landscape of South Africa. Whether you’re hosting a summer barbecue or simply craving a taste of something new, this Sosaties recipe is a must-try. So fire up the grill, gather your ingredients, and get ready to savor the irresistible allure of South African cuisine with these delectable marinated meat skewers. Cheers to good food and great company!

Enjoy your culinary adventure with Sosaties, and let the tantalizing flavors of South Africa take center stage at your next gathering.
