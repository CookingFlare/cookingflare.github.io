Mouthwatering Low Carb Chicken Alfredo Recipe


If you’re on a low carb diet but still crave creamy and indulgent dishes, then this mouthwatering low carb chicken alfredo recipe is perfect for you. Creamy, rich, and satisfying, this dish is a great way to enjoy the flavors of classic alfredo without the guilt of high carb content. Plus, it’s easy to make and perfect for a cozy night in or to impress guests at a dinner party. Let’s dive into the details of creating this delectable low carb chicken alfredo dish.

Ingredients

For the Chicken:

  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil

For the Alfredo Sauce:

  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1/2 cup grated parmesan cheese
  • 4 tablespoons unsalted butter
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley for garnish

Additional:

  • 1 tablespoon olive oil for cooking
  • 2 zucchinis, spiralized into “zoodles”
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

Step 1: Prepare the Chicken

  1. Season the chicken breasts with salt and pepper on both sides.
  2. In a large skillet, heat 2 tablespoons of olive oil over medium-high heat.
  3. Add the seasoned chicken breasts to the skillet and cook for about 6-7 minutes on each side, or until golden brown and cooked through. Remove the chicken from the skillet and set aside.

Step 2: Make the Alfredo Sauce

  1. In the same skillet, add 1 tablespoon of olive oil and minced garlic. Sauté the garlic until fragrant.
  2. Add the heavy cream and butter to the skillet, stirring constantly until the butter is melted and the mixture is simmering.
  3. Gradually add the grated parmesan cheese, stirring continuously until the sauce is smooth and thickened. Season with salt and pepper to taste.

Step 3: Prepare the Zoodles

  1. Using a spiralizer, spiralize the zucchinis into long, thin strands resembling spaghetti.
  2. In a separate skillet, heat a small amount of olive oil over medium heat.
  3. Add the zoodles to the skillet and sauté for 2-3 minutes, or until just tender. Season with salt and pepper to taste.

Step 4: Assemble the Dish

  1. Place a portion of zoodles on each plate.
  2. Slice the cooked chicken breasts and arrange them on top of the zoodles.
  3. Pour the creamy alfredo sauce over the chicken and zoodles.
  4. Garnish with fresh parsley.

Step 5: Serve and Enjoy

  1. Serve the low carb chicken alfredo immediately while it’s still warm.
  2. Pair it with a crisp green salad or steamed vegetables for a complete and satisfying meal.

Conclusion

This mouthwatering low carb chicken alfredo recipe is a delightful way to enjoy a classic comfort food while staying on track with your low carb lifestyle. The creamy alfredo sauce complements the tender, juicy chicken and the zoodles perfectly, creating a dish that’s both visually appealing and incredibly flavorful. Whether you’re cooking for yourself or entertaining guests, this recipe is sure to impress and satisfy. Try it out and savor the deliciousness of low carb indulgence!
