Tangy and Zesty Lemon Herb Rice with Grilled Lemon Pepper Chicken


If you’re looking for a fresh and flavorful dish to add some zing to your dinner table, this tangy and zesty lemon herb rice with grilled lemon pepper chicken is just the ticket. The combination of citrusy lemon, fragrant herbs, and succulent grilled chicken is sure to tantalize your taste buds and leave you craving more. Let’s dive into the recipe and get cooking!

Lemon Herb Rice

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup long-grain white rice
  • 2 cups chicken broth
  • Zest of 1 lemon
  • Juice of 1 lemon
  • 2 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley
  • 1 tablespoon chopped fresh thyme
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a medium saucepan, bring the chicken broth to a boil.
  2. Stir in the rice, lemon zest, and lemon juice.
  3. Reduce the heat to low, cover, and simmer for 18-20 minutes, or until the rice is tender and the liquid is absorbed.
  4. Once the rice is cooked, fluff it with a fork and stir in the chopped parsley and thyme.
  5. Season with salt and pepper to taste.

The fragrant combination of lemon zest, fresh herbs, and fluffy rice creates a vibrant and aromatic side dish that perfectly complements the grilled lemon pepper chicken.

Grilled Lemon Pepper Chicken

Ingredients:

  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • Zest of 1 lemon
  • Juice of 1 lemon
  • 1 tablespoon lemon pepper seasoning
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a small bowl, whisk together the olive oil, lemon zest, lemon juice, lemon pepper seasoning, minced garlic, salt, and pepper.
  2. Place the chicken breasts in a shallow dish or resealable plastic bag and pour the marinade over them, ensuring they are well coated.
  3. Cover or seal the dish/bag and marinate in the refrigerator for at least 30 minutes, or up to 4 hours for maximum flavor.

Grilling Instructions:

  1. Preheat the grill to medium-high heat.
  2. Remove the chicken from the marinade, allowing any excess to drip off.
  3. Grill the chicken for 6-7 minutes on each side, or until it is no longer pink in the center and the juices run clear.

Stovetop Instructions:

  1. Heat a grill pan or skillet over medium-high heat.
  2. Cook the chicken for 6-7 minutes on each side, or until it is no longer pink in the center and the juices run clear.

Once the chicken is cooked through, remove it from the grill or stovetop and let it rest for a few minutes before serving.

Serving Suggestion

To serve, spoon a generous portion of the tangy and zesty lemon herb rice onto each plate and top it with a grilled lemon pepper chicken breast. Garnish with a sprinkle of fresh parsley and a lemon wedge for an extra burst of citrus flavor.

This delightful dish is perfect for a casual weeknight dinner or a special weekend gathering. The bright, refreshing flavors of the lemon herb rice and the succulent grilled chicken will make this recipe a new favorite in your culinary repertoire. Enjoy every zesty bite!

So, there you have it – a tantalizing recipe for tangy and zesty lemon herb rice with grilled lemon pepper chicken that’s sure to impress. Try it out and let the vibrant flavors speak for themselves!
