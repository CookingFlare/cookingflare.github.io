The Classic Old Fashioned Cake: A Timeless Whiskey Delight


When it comes to classic desserts, nothing quite satisfies like a beautifully crafted cake. And when you infuse it with a touch of timeless sophistication and a hint of whiskey, you get a dessert that’s truly unforgettable. The Old Fashioned Cake is a delightful treat that combines the rich flavors of whiskey with the moist, tender crumb of a traditional cake. Whether you’re looking to impress guests at a dinner party or simply indulge in a luxurious dessert, this recipe is sure to delight your taste buds.

Ingredients

For the Cake:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 ½ cups granulated sugar
  • 1 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 4 large eggs
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • ½ teaspoon baking soda
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • ½ cup buttermilk
  • ½ cup whiskey

For the Whiskey Glaze:

  • ½ cup unsalted butter
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • ¼ cup water
  • ½ cup whiskey

Instructions

  1. Preheat and Prepare: Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease and flour a 10-inch Bundt pan.

  2. Mix the Dry Ingredients: In a bowl, whisk together the flour, baking powder, baking soda, and salt. Set aside.

  3. Cream the Butter and Sugar: In a separate large bowl, cream together the butter and sugar until light and fluffy.

  4. Incorporate Eggs and Flavorings: Beat in the eggs, one at a time, then stir in the vanilla extract.

  5. Alternate Adding Dry Ingredients and Buttermilk: Gradually add the dry ingredients to the creamed mixture, alternating with the buttermilk. Mix until just combined.

  6. Add Whiskey: Gently fold in the whiskey until the batter is smooth and well combined.

  7. Bake the Cake: Pour the batter into the prepared Bundt pan and smooth the top. Bake for 50 to 60 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.

  8. Prepare the Glaze: While the cake is baking, prepare the whiskey glaze. In a saucepan, combine the butter, sugar, and water. Bring to a boil over medium heat, stirring constantly. Reduce the heat and simmer for 5 minutes. Remove from heat and stir in the whiskey.

  9. Glaze the Cake: Once the cake is done, remove it from the oven and let it cool in the pan for 10 minutes. Invert the cake onto a wire rack. While the cake is still warm, poke holes in the top with a skewer and slowly pour the whiskey glaze over the cake, allowing it to soak in.

  10. Serve and Enjoy: Allow the cake to cool completely before slicing and serving. The rich, moist texture and the subtle hint of whiskey in every bite make this Old Fashioned Cake a true delight for any occasion.

Tips and Tricks

  • For a non-alcoholic version, you can substitute the whiskey in both the cake and the glaze with apple juice for a delicious twist.

  • Experiment with different types of whiskey to find the flavor profile that best suits your palate. Whether it’s a smoky Scotch or a smooth bourbon, the choice of whiskey can greatly impact the overall taste of the cake.

  • If you’re feeling adventurous, consider adding a handful of chopped pecans or walnuts to the cake batter for an added crunch and nutty flavor.

There you have it – a classic Old Fashioned Cake recipe that’s sure to become a staple in your dessert repertoire. The rich, moist cake infused with the warm, earthy notes of whiskey is a true testament to the timeless appeal of this beloved dessert. Whether you’re celebrating a special occasion or simply indulging in a well-deserved treat, this cake is bound to leave a lasting impression on all who have the pleasure of tasting it. So, roll up your sleeves, preheat that oven, and get ready to bake a dessert that’s as timeless as it is delicious. Cheers!
