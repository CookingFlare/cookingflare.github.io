Deliciously Creamy Coconut Cream Pie


Coconut lovers, rejoice! Today, I’m sharing with you a recipe for a deliciously creamy coconut cream pie that will transport you to a tropical paradise with every bite. This dessert is a perfect balance of rich coconut flavor, creamy custard, and a flaky crust that will leave you craving for more. So, let’s get started with this mouthwatering recipe!

Ingredients

  • 1 pre-baked 9-inch pie crust
  • 1 cup sweetened shredded coconut
  • 2 ½ cups whole milk
  • ½ cup granulated sugar
  • ⅓ cup all-purpose flour
  • ¼ teaspoon salt
  • 4 large egg yolks
  • 2 tablespoons unsalted butter
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • ½ cup heavy cream
  • 2 tablespoons powdered sugar
  • ½ teaspoon coconut extract

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C).

  2. Spread the shredded coconut evenly on a baking sheet and toast it in the preheated oven for about 5 minutes, or until golden brown. Stir occasionally to ensure even toasting. Set aside to cool.

  3. In a medium-sized saucepan, combine the milk, sugar, flour, and salt. Whisk until well combined and no lumps remain.

  4. Place the saucepan over medium heat and cook the mixture, stirring constantly, until it thickens and comes to a gentle boil. This usually takes around 10-15 minutes.

  5. In a separate bowl, whisk the egg yolks until smooth. Slowly pour about half a cup of the hot milk mixture into the egg yolks while whisking continuously. This step, known as tempering, prevents the eggs from curdling.

  6. Pour the tempered egg mixture back into the saucepan with the remaining milk mixture. Cook over medium heat, stirring constantly, until the custard thickens further, about 2-3 minutes.

  7. Remove the saucepan from the heat and add the butter, vanilla extract, and half of the toasted coconut. Stir until the butter is melted and the mixture is well combined.

  8. Pour the custard into the pre-baked pie crust and smooth the top with a spatula. Cover the surface of the custard with plastic wrap, ensuring it touches the surface to prevent a skin from forming. Chill the pie in the refrigerator for at least 4 hours, or until set.

  9. In a mixing bowl, whip the heavy cream, powdered sugar, and coconut extract until stiff peaks form. Spread the whipped cream over the chilled pie, covering the custard completely.

  10. Sprinkle the remaining toasted coconut on top of the whipped cream. For an extra touch, you can lightly toast some additional coconut and sprinkle it around the edges of the pie.

  11. Slice and serve your deliciously creamy coconut cream pie chilled. Enjoy the tropical flavors and indulge in the velvety smoothness of each bite!

Conclusion

This deliciously creamy coconut cream pie is the perfect dessert to satisfy your coconut cravings and impress your family and friends. With its flaky crust, luscious custard filling, and a generous topping of whipped cream and toasted coconut, this pie is sure to become a favorite in your household. So, why not treat yourself and your loved ones to a taste of the tropics with this easy-to-make and absolutely delightful coconut cream pie? Go ahead and give it a try, and let me know how it turns out!
