Hocus Pocus Munchkins: Spooky Halloween Cookie Recipe


Dive into the magical world of Halloween with this enchanting Hocus Pocus Munchkins recipe! Stir up some holiday spirit and treat your loved ones to these whimsical, mouthwatering cookies that will leave everyone spellbound. Are you ready to embark on a delightful baking adventure? Grab your apron, preheat your oven, and let’s get cooking!

Ingredients

  • 2 3/4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1 1/2 cups brown sugar
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 1 cup semi-sweet chocolate chips
  • 1 cup candy-coated chocolates (M&M’s or similar)
  • 1/2 cup chopped walnuts (optional)
  • 1 large egg, beaten (for egg wash)
  • Powdered sugar (for dusting)

Instructions

Step 1: Prepare the Cookie Dough

In a large bowl, combine the flour, baking powder, and baking soda. Give it a light whisk to evenly distribute the dry ingredients.

Using another large bowl or your stand mixer, cream together the softened butter, brown sugar, and granulated sugar until the mixture is light and fluffy. This might take about 3-4 minutes. Add the two large eggs, one at a time, followed by the vanilla extract. Mix until well combined.

Gradually add the dry ingredients to the wet mixture, and continue to mix until a soft, sticky dough forms. Be sure not to over-mix, as this can lead to tough cookies.

Step 2: Mix in Add-ins

Gently fold the semi-sweet chocolate chips, candy-coated chocolates, and chopped walnuts (if using) into your cookie dough until uniformly distributed throughout the batter.

Step 3: Shape the Munchkins

Using an ice cream scoop or two spoons, portions of dough into 1 1/2-inch balls and place them on a parchment-lined or greased cookie sheet. Then, gently press down on each cookie to achieve a mini-patty shape. This is where the magic begins, as these “Munchkins” resemble the iconic character from The Wizard of Oz!

Step 4: Preheat the Oven

Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C) and ensure the cookie sheets are ready to go.

Step 5: Bake the Cookies

Bake the tray of Munchkins for 9-11 minutes, or until the edges are lightly golden and the centers are set. Keep an eye on them to avoid overbaking!

Step 6: Cool & Dust Cookies

Allow the Hocus Pocus Munchkins to cool on the cookie sheet for about 5 minutes before transferring them to a wire rack to cool completely. While they’re still warm, brush each cookie with the beaten egg to add a shiny, delectable glaze, and then dust generously with powdered sugar.

As they cool, a magical transformation occurs. The sugar glaze hardens, revealing a subtle sparkly appearance - almost like the cookies have received a sprinkle of enchanted fairy dust!

Step 7: Enjoy Your Spooky Treats

Once fully cooled, gather your little Halloween ghouls and goblins around the table to enjoy these Hocus Pocus Munchkins! Feel free to serve them alongside a tall glass of milk or hot cocoa to truly capture the essence of Halloween.

These spellbinding delights are not only perfect for the spookiest night of the year but are sure to become a cherished cookie recipe that you’ll revisit every Halloween season.

Unleash your creative baking skills and give this Hocus Pocus Munchkin recipe a try; your family and friends will be grateful for the enchanted treats. Maybe next time, involve your little witches and warlocks in the process – they’ll have a blast making these magical Munchkins!

Have a bewitching good time this Halloween with these mesmerizing cookies! Happy baking, my friends!
