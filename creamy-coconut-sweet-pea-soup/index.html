Creamy Coconut Sweet Pea Soup


When the weather cools and the desire for something warm and comforting arises, a creamy soup can be the perfect solution. There’s something about the smooth texture of a velvety soup paired with the bright flavors of seasonal ingredients that makes for the ultimate cozy meal. One such soup that brings together a delightful balance of rich creaminess and fresh, sweet flavors is Creamy Coconut Sweet Pea Soup. This simple yet sophisticated dish is perfect for both weeknight dinners and special gatherings, offering a unique twist on traditional pea soup by infusing it with the richness of coconut milk.

In this recipe, we’ll guide you through the process of creating this creamy, vibrant, and flavorful soup from scratch. Whether you’re cooking for a family, entertaining guests, or simply looking for a new go-to recipe for colder days, this Coconut Sweet Pea Soup is sure to impress.

Ingredients for Creamy Coconut Sweet Pea Soup

To create a truly comforting and rich soup, you’ll need to gather the following ingredients:

  • Frozen Sweet Peas – 4 cups (about 500g): The star of this dish. The peas give the soup its natural sweetness and vibrant green color.
  • Coconut Milk – 1 can (400ml): Full-fat coconut milk adds a creamy, luscious texture and a slight tropical flavor that pairs beautifully with the peas.
  • Vegetable Broth – 4 cups: This adds the base flavor and enhances the soup’s depth without overpowering the natural sweetness of the peas.
  • Yellow Onion – 1 medium, diced: Adds a subtle sweetness and savory undertone.
  • Garlic – 2 cloves, minced: For that fragrant and aromatic base flavor.
  • Fresh Ginger – 1-inch piece, grated: Ginger brings a gentle heat and a zesty freshness that balances the coconut milk.
  • Olive Oil – 1 tablespoon: Used for sautéing the onions and garlic, helping to release their natural flavors.
  • Lemon Juice – 2 teaspoons: Adds a zesty contrast to the sweetness of the peas and coconut milk.
  • Salt and Pepper – To taste: Enhance the overall flavors and season the soup.
  • Fresh Herbs (optional) – A handful of fresh mint or parsley, for garnish and added freshness.

Optional Garnishes:

  • Toasted coconut flakes
  • A drizzle of coconut milk
  • Fresh herbs (parsley, mint)
  • Crumbled croutons or a sprinkle of chili flakes for a bit of heat

How to Make Creamy Coconut Sweet Pea Soup

Now that you have all your ingredients ready, let’s dive into the steps for making this delightful creamy soup. The process is simple, and the results are so satisfying.

Step 1: Sauté the Aromatics

Start by heating the olive oil in a large pot over medium heat. Add the diced onion and sauté for about 5 minutes, or until the onion becomes soft and translucent. The aroma of sautéing onions will fill your kitchen, setting the stage for the layers of flavor to come.

Next, add the minced garlic and grated ginger to the pot. Stir everything together and cook for another 2 minutes. You want the garlic to become fragrant, but be careful not to burn it, as it could turn bitter. The combination of onion, garlic, and ginger creates a beautiful base for the soup, providing savory, aromatic, and spicy notes.

Step 2: Add the Peas and Broth

Once your aromatics are ready, add the frozen sweet peas to the pot. Stir the peas into the onion, garlic, and ginger mixture, allowing them to thaw slightly and take on the flavors from the aromatics.

Next, pour in the vegetable broth, making sure that the peas are fully submerged in the liquid. The broth adds a savory depth to the soup and helps create a light but flavorful base. Bring everything to a simmer over medium heat and cook for 10-12 minutes, or until the peas are tender and fully cooked. This will allow the peas to release their natural sweetness into the broth, creating a flavorful soup base.

Step 3: Incorporate the Coconut Milk

Once the peas are fully cooked, it’s time to add the coconut milk. Pour in the entire can of coconut milk, stirring to combine. The coconut milk will add a creamy, luscious texture to the soup and complement the sweetness of the peas. Bring everything back to a simmer and cook for another 5-7 minutes, allowing the flavors to meld together.

At this point, you may want to taste the soup and adjust the seasoning with salt, pepper, and a little lemon juice to balance the flavors. The acidity of the lemon juice will brighten up the richness of the coconut milk and provide a fresh contrast to the sweetness of the peas.

Step 4: Blend the Soup

Once the soup has simmered and everything is nicely combined, it’s time to blend the soup into a creamy and smooth consistency. You can use an immersion blender for convenience, or transfer the soup in batches to a regular blender. Blend until the soup is smooth and velvety, making sure there are no large chunks left behind. The result should be a thick, creamy soup with a beautiful green color.

If you prefer a slightly thinner consistency, you can add a little more vegetable broth or water to reach your desired texture.

Step 5: Serve and Garnish

Once your soup is blended to perfection, pour it into bowls and garnish with fresh herbs like mint or parsley for an added layer of freshness. You can also add a drizzle of coconut milk for extra creaminess, or a sprinkle of toasted coconut flakes for a fun texture contrast. For a touch of heat, sprinkle some chili flakes or top with crunchy croutons.

This Coconut Sweet Pea Soup is incredibly versatile. It pairs wonderfully with a slice of crusty bread, a fresh salad, or even grilled cheese sandwiches for a complete meal.

Tips and Variations

Make It Spicy:

If you enjoy a bit of spice, you can add a pinch of red pepper flakes or some diced jalapeños when you sauté the garlic and onions. The heat from the chili will complement the sweetness of the peas and the creaminess of the coconut milk for a more complex flavor profile.

Add Vegetables:

This recipe is highly adaptable to include other vegetables. Feel free to add a handful of spinach, kale, or even zucchini for additional nutrients and flavor. Just be sure to cook them until tender before blending them into the soup.

Make It Vegan:

This soup is naturally vegan, so you can enjoy it without any modifications. The combination of coconut milk and vegetable broth ensures that the soup is rich, creamy, and satisfying without the need for any dairy or meat.

Use Fresh Peas:

If fresh sweet peas are in season, feel free to use them instead of frozen peas. Just make sure to blanch them briefly in boiling water before adding them to the soup. Fresh peas will give the soup a slightly different texture but will still create a deliciously creamy result.

Add Protein:

To make this soup heartier, consider adding a source of plant-based protein. Cooked lentils, chickpeas, or even cubes of tofu can easily be added during the simmering stage. This makes the soup more filling and turns it into a complete meal.

Make It Ahead:

This soup stores well in the fridge for 3-4 days, so it’s perfect for meal prep. You can also freeze it in an airtight container for up to 3 months. Simply reheat it on the stovetop or in the microwave when you’re ready to enjoy it.

Why You’ll Love Coconut Sweet Pea Soup

This Coconut Sweet Pea Soup combines a few simple ingredients to create a satisfying and nourishing meal that’s both comforting and refreshing. The sweetness of the peas perfectly complements the creaminess of the coconut milk, while the aromatics of onion, garlic, and ginger add a savory foundation.

Whether you’re looking for a light lunch, a healthy appetizer, or a hearty dinner, this soup fits the bill. It’s a crowd-pleaser and a great way to incorporate more vegetables into your diet without sacrificing flavor.

The recipe is incredibly customizable, making it easy to adapt to different tastes and dietary preferences. It’s an ideal dish for those following a vegan or gluten-free diet, and it can be enjoyed by anyone who loves bold, comforting flavors.

Conclusion

Incorporating fresh ingredients like sweet peas and coconut milk into your meals is a great way to explore new flavors while keeping things simple and nutritious. Creamy Coconut Sweet Pea Soup is an excellent addition to your recipe repertoire, bringing together a delightful combination of rich, savory, and sweet elements that work harmoniously together.

Try making this recipe the next time you’re in the mood for something cozy, creamy, and full of flavor. You’ll be pleasantly surprised by how easy it is to make and how much everyone will enjoy it!
