A Taste of the Mediterranean: Delicious Hummus Wrap Recipes


If you’re looking for a quick and healthy meal that’s bursting with flavor, look no further than the Mediterranean. With its vibrant colors, bold flavors, and fresh ingredients, Mediterranean cuisine is a true delight for the senses. And what better way to experience this culinary journey than with delicious hummus wrap recipes? Whether you’re a vegetarian, vegan, or simply looking to add more plant-based options to your diet, these wraps are sure to satisfy your cravings.

The Classic Mediterranean Wrap

Let’s start with a classic. This wrap combines the creamy goodness of hummus with a medley of fresh vegetables and tangy feta cheese. Here’s what you’ll need:

  • 1 large whole wheat tortilla
  • 2 tablespoons of your favorite hummus
  • 1/4 cup of chopped cucumbers
  • 1/4 cup of sliced tomatoes
  • 1/4 cup of sliced red onions
  • 2 tablespoons of crumbled feta cheese
  • Handful of fresh spinach leaves

Spread the hummus evenly on the tortilla, leaving a small border around the edges. Layer the cucumbers, tomatoes, red onions, feta cheese, and spinach leaves on top of the hummus. Roll the tortilla tightly, tucking in the sides as you go. Slice the wrap in half and enjoy!

The Greek-inspired Wrap

For a taste of Greece, try this flavorful wrap that combines hummus with traditional Greek ingredients. Here’s what you’ll need:

  • 1 large whole wheat tortilla
  • 2 tablespoons of hummus (plain or flavored)
  • 1/4 cup of diced cucumbers
  • 1/4 cup of diced tomatoes
  • 1/4 cup of sliced Kalamata olives
  • 2 tablespoons of crumbled feta cheese
  • Handful of fresh spinach leaves
  • 1 tablespoon of tzatziki sauce (optional)

Spread the hummus evenly on the tortilla, leaving a small border around the edges. Layer the cucumbers, tomatoes, Kalamata olives, feta cheese, and spinach leaves on top of the hummus. If you’re feeling adventurous, add a drizzle of tzatziki sauce for an extra burst of flavor. Roll the tortilla tightly, slice, and savor the taste of Greece!

The Mediterranean Veggie Wrap

If you’re looking for a veggie-packed wrap that’s bursting with Mediterranean flavors, this recipe is for you. Here’s what you’ll need:

  • 1 large whole wheat tortilla
  • 2 tablespoons of roasted red pepper hummus
  • 1/4 cup of sliced bell peppers (red, yellow, and green)
  • 1/4 cup of sliced zucchini
  • 1/4 cup of sliced red onions
  • Handful of baby spinach leaves
  • 1 tablespoon of crumbled goat cheese

Spread the roasted red pepper hummus evenly on the tortilla, leaving a small border around the edges. Layer the bell peppers, zucchini, red onions, spinach leaves, and crumbled goat cheese on top of the hummus. Roll the tortilla tightly, slice, and enjoy the explosion of flavors!

The Vegan Delight Wrap

For our vegan friends, this wrap is a true delight. Packed with plant-based protein and fresh ingredients, it’s a healthy and satisfying option. Here’s what you’ll need:

  • 1 large whole wheat tortilla
  • 2 tablespoons of sun-dried tomato hummus
  • 1/4 cup of sliced avocado
  • 1/4 cup of diced tomatoes
  • 1/4 cup of sliced cucumbers
  • Handful of mixed salad greens
  • Sprinkle of nutritional yeast (optional)

Spread the sun-dried tomato hummus evenly on the tortilla, leaving a small border around the edges. Layer the avocado, tomatoes, cucumbers, mixed salad greens, and a sprinkle of nutritional yeast (if desired) on top of the hummus. Roll the tortilla tightly, slice, and relish in the vegan deliciousness!

Wrap it Up!

There you have it – a taste of the Mediterranean in the form of delicious hummus wraps. These recipes are versatile, allowing you to customize them with your favorite ingredients and flavors. Whether you’re enjoying them for lunch, dinner, or as a snack, these wraps are a wholesome and satisfying choice. So grab your tortillas, whip up some hummus, and let your taste buds take a trip to the Mediterranean!
