Flavorful Instant Pot Beef and Potato Stew Recipe


There’s nothing quite like a hearty beef and potato stew to warm you up on a chilly evening. This Instant Pot recipe takes the classic stew to a whole new level, infusing it with rich flavors and tender meat that will have your taste buds singing. Plus, the convenience of the Instant Pot means you can have this delicious meal on the table in a fraction of the time it takes to make traditional stew. So, let’s dive into this flavorful Instant Pot beef and potato stew recipe and get ready to impress your family and friends with your culinary skills!

Ingredients

  • 2 pounds of beef chuck, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 4 large potatoes, peeled and cubed
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 3 carrots, sliced
  • 3 celery stalks, chopped
  • 4 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 4 cups of beef broth
  • 1 cup of red wine
  • 2 tablespoons of tomato paste
  • 2 bay leaves
  • 1 teaspoon of dried thyme
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 tablespoons of olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons of all-purpose flour
  • Chopped fresh parsley for garnish

Instructions

  1. Sear the Beef: Start by seasoning the beef with salt and pepper. Set your Instant Pot to the “Saute” setting and add the olive oil. Once the oil is hot, add the beef in batches and sear until browned on all sides. Remove the beef from the Instant Pot and set it aside.

  2. Saute the Vegetables: Add the onion, carrots, celery, and garlic to the Instant Pot and saute for a few minutes until they begin to soften.

  3. Deglaze the Pot: Pour in the red wine and scrape the bottom of the pot to loosen any browned bits. Let the wine simmer for a few minutes to reduce slightly.

  4. Add the Beef and Potatoes: Return the beef to the Instant Pot, along with the cubed potatoes. Sprinkle the flour over the mixture and stir to coat everything evenly.

  5. Add the Flavor: Pour in the beef broth, add the tomato paste, bay leaves, and thyme. Give everything a good stir to combine all the ingredients.

  6. Pressure Cook: Secure the lid on the Instant Pot and set the vent to the sealing position. Cook on high pressure for 30 minutes, then allow for a natural pressure release for 10 minutes before carefully releasing the remaining pressure.

  7. Finish and Serve: Once the pressure has released, carefully open the lid and give the stew a good stir. Taste and adjust the seasoning with salt and pepper if needed. Ladle the stew into bowls, sprinkle with chopped fresh parsley, and serve hot.

Tips for Success

  • Use a well-marbled cut of beef like chuck for the most flavorful and tender results.
  • Don’t skip the step of deglazing the pot with red wine – it adds a depth of flavor that is truly delicious.
  • For a thicker stew, you can mix a tablespoon of cornstarch with a little water and stir it into the stew after pressure cooking, then set the Instant Pot to the “Saute” setting and cook for a few more minutes until thickened.

Final Thoughts

This Instant Pot beef and potato stew is a perfect example of comfort food at its finest. The tender beef, hearty potatoes, and aromatic vegetables come together in a rich, flavorful broth that will warm you from the inside out. Whether you’re cooking for a crowd or just craving a cozy meal at home, this recipe is sure to become a favorite in your rotation. So, dust off your Instant Pot and get ready to savor every spoonful of this delectable stew!
