Satisfying Low Carb Chicken and Broccoli Casserole


Are you looking for a delicious and satisfying low carb dish that the whole family will love? Look no further than this mouthwatering Satisfying Low Carb Chicken and Broccoli Casserole. Packed with flavor and nutrients, this casserole is the perfect combination of creamy, cheesy, and healthy. Plus, it’s super easy to make!

Ingredients

  • 2 cups cooked chicken, shredded
  • 2 cups broccoli florets
  • 1 cup cauliflower florets
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1 cup chicken broth
  • 1 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C) and lightly grease a 9x13-inch baking dish.

  2. In a large mixing bowl, combine the cooked chicken, broccoli florets, and cauliflower florets. Toss them together until well mixed.

  3. In a separate bowl, whisk together the heavy cream, chicken broth, minced garlic, dried thyme, salt, and black pepper. Pour this mixture over the chicken and vegetables in the mixing bowl.

  4. Sprinkle half of the shredded cheddar cheese and Parmesan cheese over the chicken and vegetables. Stir everything together until evenly coated.

  5. Transfer the mixture to the prepared baking dish and spread it out evenly. Top with the remaining shredded cheddar cheese and Parmesan cheese.

  6. Cover the baking dish with foil and bake for 25 minutes. Then, remove the foil and continue baking for an additional 10-15 minutes, or until the cheese is golden and bubbly.

  7. Remove the casserole from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes before serving. This will allow the flavors to meld together and make it easier to portion.

  8. Serve the Satisfying Low Carb Chicken and Broccoli Casserole hot and enjoy!

Why Choose This Low Carb Casserole?

This Satisfying Low Carb Chicken and Broccoli Casserole is not only delicious, but it’s also packed with nutrients. The combination of chicken, broccoli, and cauliflower provides a good dose of protein, vitamins, and fiber. Plus, it’s low in carbs, making it a perfect option for those following a low carb or keto diet.

By using heavy cream and a blend of cheddar and Parmesan cheese, this casserole achieves a creamy and cheesy texture without any added carbs. It’s a great way to satisfy your cravings while still sticking to your low carb lifestyle.

Another great thing about this casserole is its versatility. Feel free to add your favorite low carb vegetables or swap out the chicken for another protein source like turkey or tofu. You can also experiment with different herbs and spices to suit your taste preferences.

Conclusion

In conclusion, this Satisfying Low Carb Chicken and Broccoli Casserole is a must-try recipe for anyone looking to enjoy a delicious and healthy low carb meal. With its creamy and cheesy texture, combined with the goodness of chicken and broccoli, this casserole will surely become a family favorite.

So why wait? Gather your ingredients and give this recipe a go. Your taste buds and waistline will thank you!
