Tropical Delights: 6 Marinades for Grilled Mango


Mangoes are the quintessential taste of summer, and what better way to celebrate this tropical fruit than by grilling it to perfection? Grilled mango is a versatile ingredient that can be used in both sweet and savory dishes, making it a delightful addition to any summer menu. To elevate the flavor of your grilled mango, we present six tantalizing marinades that will take your taste buds on a journey through the tropics.

1. Citrus Splash

Ingredients:

  • 1/4 cup orange juice
  • 2 tablespoons lime juice
  • 1 tablespoon honey
  • 1 teaspoon grated ginger
  • 1/2 teaspoon chili flakes
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, whisk together the orange juice, lime juice, honey, grated ginger, chili flakes, salt, and pepper.
  2. Pour the marinade over the sliced mango and let it sit for at least 30 minutes before grilling. The citrusy flavors will infuse the mango with a zesty kick, perfect for pairing with grilled fish or shrimp.

2. Coconut Rum Infusion

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 cup coconut milk
  • 2 tablespoons dark rum
  • 1 tablespoon brown sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • Pinch of cinnamon
  • Pinch of nutmeg

Instructions:

  1. Combine the coconut milk, dark rum, brown sugar, vanilla extract, cinnamon, and nutmeg in a shallow dish.
  2. Place the mango slices in the marinade and let it soak for at least 1 hour. The coconut rum infusion adds a hint of tropical decadence to the grilled mango, making it a delightful dessert on its own or a topping for ice cream.

3. Spicy Mango Tango

Ingredients:

  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 1 teaspoon Sriracha sauce
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1/2 teaspoon paprika
  • 1/4 teaspoon garlic powder

Instructions:

  1. Whisk together the olive oil, soy sauce, Sriracha sauce, ground cumin, paprika, and garlic powder in a bowl.
  2. Coat the mango slices with the spicy marinade and let them marinate for 45 minutes. The Spicy Mango Tango marinade adds a fiery kick to the grilled mango, making it a perfect companion for grilled pork or chicken.

4. Honey Lime Glaze

Ingredients:

  • 2 tablespoons honey
  • 2 tablespoons lime juice
  • 1 teaspoon lime zest
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground coriander
  • Pinch of salt

Instructions:

  1. Mix together the honey, lime juice, lime zest, ground coriander, and salt in a bowl.
  2. Brush the marinade onto the mango slices and let them sit for 20 minutes. The sweet and tangy flavors of the Honey Lime Glaze will caramelize beautifully on the grilled mango, making it a delightful addition to salads or as a side dish.

5. Minty Mojito Magic

Ingredients:

  • 1/4 cup fresh mint leaves, chopped
  • 2 tablespoons white rum
  • 1 tablespoon lime juice
  • 1 tablespoon honey
  • Pinch of salt

Instructions:

  1. Combine the chopped mint leaves, white rum, lime juice, honey, and salt in a shallow dish.
  2. Add the mango slices to the marinade and let them soak for at least 1 hour. The Minty Mojito Magic marinade infuses the mango with a refreshing burst of flavor, perfect for serving alongside grilled chicken or seafood.

6. Teriyaki Tropics

Ingredients:

  • 1/4 cup pineapple juice
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon brown sugar
  • 1 teaspoon grated ginger
  • 1 clove garlic, minced

Instructions:

  1. Whisk together the pineapple juice, soy sauce, brown sugar, grated ginger, and minced garlic in a bowl.
  2. Marinate the mango slices in the Teriyaki Tropics sauce for at least 30 minutes. The sweet and savory flavors of the teriyaki marinade complement the grilled mango beautifully, making it an ideal addition to stir-fries or skewers.

Grilled mango is a delightful way to bring a taste of the tropics to your table, and with these six tantalizing marinades, you can elevate this succulent fruit to new heights of flavor. Whether you prefer sweet, spicy, or savory, there’s a marinade to suit every palate. So fire up the grill, grab some ripe mangoes, and let the tropical delights begin!
