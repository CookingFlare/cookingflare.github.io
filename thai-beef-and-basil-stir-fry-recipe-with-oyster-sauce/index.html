Thai Beef and Basil Stir Fry Recipe with Oyster Sauce


Stir-frying is a popular cooking technique that originated in China but has since spread its influence across various cuisines, including Thai cuisine. Thai Beef and Basil Stir Fry with Oyster Sauce is a flavorful and aromatic dish that combines tender beef, fresh basil, and a savory oyster sauce. In this recipe, we will guide you through the steps of creating this delicious Thai stir fry in the comfort of your own kitchen.

Ingredients

To make this mouthwatering Thai Beef and Basil Stir Fry, you will need the following ingredients:

  • 1 pound of beef, thinly sliced (flank steak or sirloin works well)
  • 2 tablespoons of vegetable oil
  • 3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 red chili, thinly sliced (adjust according to your spice preference)
  • 1 onion, sliced
  • 1 bell pepper, sliced
  • 2 cups of fresh basil leaves
  • 2 tablespoons of oyster sauce
  • 1 tablespoon of soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon of fish sauce
  • 1 teaspoon of sugar
  • A pinch of black pepper

Instructions

Now that we have assembled the ingredients, let’s dive into the cooking process:

  1. Heat a wok or a large skillet over high heat. Once hot, add the vegetable oil and swirl it around to coat the surface.

  2. Add the minced garlic and sliced red chili to the hot oil. Stir-fry for about 30 seconds until fragrant, being careful not to burn the garlic.

  3. Add the thinly sliced beef to the wok and stir-fry for about 2-3 minutes until it is browned and cooked through. Remove the beef from the wok and set it aside.

  4. In the same wok, add the sliced onion and bell pepper. Stir-fry for about 2 minutes until they start to soften.

  5. Return the cooked beef to the wok with the vegetables. Stir in the oyster sauce, soy sauce, fish sauce, sugar, and black pepper. Mix everything well to ensure that the beef and vegetables are coated evenly with the sauce.

  6. Finally, add the fresh basil leaves to the wok and stir-fry for another minute until the basil leaves wilt slightly.

  7. Remove the wok from the heat and serve the Thai Beef and Basil Stir Fry with steamed jasmine rice or noodles.

Tips and Variations

  • For a spicier version, you can increase the amount of red chili or even add some Thai bird’s eye chili if you enjoy a fiery kick.

  • If you prefer a vegetarian option, you can substitute the beef with tofu or your favorite vegetables like mushrooms, baby corn, or snap peas.

  • Feel free to experiment with other sauces like hoisin sauce or sweet chili sauce to add different flavors to your stir fry.

  • Garnish your dish with some freshly chopped cilantro or a squeeze of lime juice for an extra burst of freshness.

Final Thoughts

Thai Beef and Basil Stir Fry with Oyster Sauce is a simple yet satisfying dish that brings together the bold flavors of Thailand. With its tender beef, aromatic basil, and savory oyster sauce, this stir fry is sure to become a favorite in your household. So grab your wok, gather the ingredients, and get ready to embark on a culinary adventure that will transport your taste buds to the vibrant streets of Thailand!
