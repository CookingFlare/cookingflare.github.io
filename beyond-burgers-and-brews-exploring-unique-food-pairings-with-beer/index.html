Beyond Burgers and Brews: Exploring Unique Food Pairings with Beer


When it comes to classic food pairings with beer, burgers are often at the top of the list. However, in recent years, the rise of plant-based alternatives has opened up a whole new world of possibilities for pairing beer with innovative and delicious dishes. In this blog post, we’ll explore the concept of pairing Beyond Burgers with a variety of brews, showcasing the versatility and flavor potential of these meatless patties. Whether you’re a dedicated vegan, a curious omnivore, or simply looking to expand your culinary horizons, these unique food pairings are sure to tantalize your taste buds and elevate your beer-drinking experience.

The Beyond Burger: A Plant-Based Delight

Before we delve into the art of pairing Beyond Burgers with beer, let’s take a moment to appreciate the star of the show: the Beyond Burger itself. Made from a blend of plant-based ingredients such as peas, mung beans, and rice, the Beyond Burger offers a satisfying and meat-like texture that appeals to both vegans and meat-eaters alike. With its juicy, flavorful profile, this plant-based patty provides the perfect canvas for creative food pairings that complement its rich, umami taste.

Pairing #1: Citrusy IPA and Spicy Sriracha Beyond Burger

For our first beer pairing, we turn to the refreshing and zesty flavors of an IPA. The citrusy notes and hoppy bitterness of an IPA perfectly offset the heat of a spicy Sriracha Beyond Burger. The bold flavors of the burger are complemented by the bright, refreshing qualities of the IPA, creating a harmonious balance that will leave your palate craving more.

Pairing #2: Stout and Smoky BBQ Beyond Burger

Next up, we have a hearty Stout paired with a smoky BBQ Beyond Burger. The robust, roasted flavors of a Stout beer provide a deep, malty backdrop for the savory, smoky notes of the BBQ Beyond Burger. This pairing is a match made in flavor heaven, as the richness of the Stout enhances the complexity of the burger, resulting in a truly indulgent dining experience.

Pairing #3: Wheat Beer and Mediterranean Beyond Burger

Moving into lighter territory, a crisp and refreshing Wheat Beer beautifully complements the Mediterranean flavors of a Beyond Burger topped with hummus, olives, and fresh herbs. The bright, effervescent nature of the Wheat Beer serves as a delightful contrast to the earthy and herbaceous elements of the Mediterranean Beyond Burger, creating a dynamic interplay of flavors that dances across the palate.

Pairing #4: Pale Ale and Umami Mushroom Beyond Burger

Last but certainly not least, we turn to a classic Pale Ale to accompany the rich, savory goodness of an umami-packed Mushroom Beyond Burger. The caramel malt sweetness and hoppy bitterness of a Pale Ale provide a perfect foil to the deep, earthy flavors of the Mushroom Beyond Burger, resulting in a pairing that is both comforting and full of depth.

Elevate Your Beer and Burger Experience

As the culinary world continues to embrace plant-based alternatives and craft brewing reaches new heights, the opportunities for unique and delightful food pairings are endless. Whether you’re hosting a casual gathering with friends or simply treating yourself to a special meal, exploring the combination of Beyond Burgers and brews opens up a world of flavor exploration and culinary creativity. So, the next time you reach for a beer, consider the exciting possibilities of pairing it with a delicious, plant-based Beyond Burger. Your taste buds will thank you.

In conclusion, the art of pairing Beyond Burgers with beer offers a tantalizing journey of flavors, textures, and experiences. From zesty IPAs to rich Stouts, there’s a beer out there to complement every unique twist on the classic burger. So, grab your favorite brew, fire up the grill, and embark on a culinary adventure that will forever change the way you think about beer and burgers. Cheers to exploring new horizons in food and drink!
