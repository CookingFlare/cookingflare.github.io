Savory Scallops with Bacon and Pea Puree


Scallops are a delicacy that can be enjoyed in a variety of ways. One delicious and savory way to prepare scallops is by pairing them with bacon and pea puree. The combination of the sweet and tender scallops, the smoky bacon, and the vibrant pea puree creates a dish that is both visually appealing and bursting with flavor. In this recipe, we will guide you through the process of creating this delectable dish.

Ingredients:

  • Fresh scallops
  • Bacon
  • Peas (fresh or frozen)
  • Mint leaves
  • Lemon juice
  • Olive oil
  • Salt and pepper

Instructions:

  1. Start by preparing the pea puree. In a small pot, bring water to a boil and add the peas along with a pinch of salt. Cook until the peas are bright green and tender. This usually takes about 2 minutes for fresh peas and 4 minutes for frozen peas [1].

  2. Drain the peas and transfer them to a blender. Add lemon juice, salt, pepper, and a drizzle of olive oil. Blend until smooth. Add mint leaves and blend again until the mint is just combined, but still visible in small pieces [1].

  3. Set the pea puree aside and move on to the bacon. In a skillet, cook the bacon over medium heat until it becomes brown and crispy. This usually takes about 7-8 minutes. Once cooked, remove the bacon from the skillet and set it aside on a paper towel to drain excess grease [2].

  4. In the same skillet, sear the scallops in the bacon fat over high heat. Make sure the skillet is hot before adding the scallops. Sear them until they turn golden on the outside and remain soft on the inside. This should take a few minutes on each side, depending on the size of the scallops [3].

  5. Once the scallops are cooked, remove them from the skillet and place them on a paper towel-lined plate to absorb any excess oil. Season both sides of the scallops with salt and pepper [1].

  6. To serve, divide the pea puree onto plates. Top the puree with the seared scallops and crumble the crispy bacon over the dish. You can also garnish with some whole peas, if desired [4].

  7. Serve the savory scallops with bacon and pea puree immediately and enjoy!

This recipe is perfect for a special occasion or a romantic dinner. The combination of flavors and textures will impress your guests and leave them wanting more. So why not give it a try and indulge in this delightful dish?

Remember, cooking is all about experimentation and personal preference. Feel free to adjust the seasoning and ingredients according to your taste. Enjoy your culinary adventure!

Note: Scallops are delicate and can easily become overcooked, resulting in a rubbery texture. It’s important to monitor their cooking time closely to ensure they remain tender and succulent.
