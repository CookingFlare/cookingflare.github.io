Sensational Summer Pies: Bright and Fresh Recipes for the Season


Summer is in full swing, and what better way to celebrate the season than with a delightful, refreshing pie? Whether you’re hosting a backyard barbecue or simply craving a sweet treat after a long day, these sensational summer pies are sure to satisfy your taste buds. Bursting with bright flavors and made with seasonal fruits, these pies are the perfect addition to any summer gathering. So, let’s dive right in and explore some delectable pie recipes that will make your taste buds dance with joy.

1. Strawberry Basil Pie

This unique twist on a classic strawberry pie combines the sweetness of ripe strawberries with the aromatic freshness of basil. The result is a pie that is both sweet and savory, perfect for those hot summer days.

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups fresh strawberries, hulled and sliced
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/4 cup cornstarch
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1 tablespoon fresh lemon juice
  • 2 tablespoons fresh basil, finely chopped
  • 1 pre-made pie crust, baked

Instructions:

  1. In a large bowl, combine the sliced strawberries, sugar, cornstarch, salt, lemon juice, and basil. Mix well until the strawberries are coated evenly.
  2. Transfer the mixture to the pre-baked pie crust, spreading it out evenly.
  3. Place the pie in the refrigerator and let it set for at least 4 hours, or until the filling is firm.
  4. Serve chilled and garnish with fresh basil leaves, if desired.

2. Mango Coconut Cream Pie

Transport yourself to a tropical paradise with this delicious mango coconut cream pie. The creamy coconut filling perfectly complements the sweet and tangy mango, creating a pie that is truly irresistible.

Ingredients:

  • 2 ripe mangoes, peeled and diced
  • 1 cup coconut milk
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/4 cup cornstarch
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1 pre-made pie crust, baked
  • Whipped cream, for garnish

Instructions:

  1. In a blender or food processor, puree one of the diced mangoes until smooth. Set aside.
  2. In a medium saucepan, whisk together the coconut milk, sugar, cornstarch, and salt. Cook over medium heat, stirring constantly, until the mixture thickens.
  3. Remove the saucepan from the heat and stir in the pureed mango. Mix well.
  4. Pour the mango coconut filling into the pre-baked pie crust and spread it out evenly.
  5. Refrigerate the pie for at least 2 hours, or until the filling is set.
  6. Once the pie has cooled, garnish with the remaining diced mango and a dollop of whipped cream.

3. Blueberry Lemon Meringue Pie

This vibrant blueberry lemon meringue pie is a delightful combination of tart and sweet flavors. The juicy blueberries and tangy lemon curd are perfectly balanced by the light and fluffy meringue topping.

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups fresh blueberries
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/4 cup cornstarch
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • Zest and juice of 1 lemon
  • 1 pre-made pie crust, baked
  • 3 egg whites
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar

Instructions:

  1. In a medium saucepan, combine the blueberries, sugar, cornstarch, salt, lemon zest, and lemon juice. Cook over medium heat, stirring occasionally, until the mixture thickens and the blueberries burst.
  2. Pour the blueberry filling into the pre-baked pie crust and spread it out evenly.
  3. In a separate bowl, beat the egg whites until soft peaks form. Gradually add the sugar, continuing to beat until stiff peaks form.
  4. Spread the meringue over the blueberry filling, making sure to seal the edges.
  5. Bake the pie in a preheated oven at 350°F (175°C) for 10-15 minutes, or until the meringue is golden brown.
  6. Allow the pie to cool completely before serving.

4. Watermelon Lime Pie

Cool down on a hot summer day with this refreshing watermelon lime pie. The juicy watermelon combined with the tangy lime creates a pie that is both light and invigorating.

Ingredients:

  • 4 cups seedless watermelon, cubed
  • 1/4 cup fresh lime juice
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/4 cup cornstarch
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1 pre-made graham cracker pie crust

Instructions:

  1. In a blender or food processor, puree the watermelon until smooth. Set aside.
  2. In a medium saucepan, whisk together the lime juice, sugar, cornstarch, and salt. Cook over medium heat, stirring constantly, until the mixture thickens.
  3. Remove the saucepan from the heat and stir in the watermelon puree. Mix well.
  4. Pour the watermelon lime filling into the graham cracker pie crust and spread it out evenly.
  5. Refrigerate the pie for at least 3 hours, or until the filling is set.
  6. Serve chilled and garnish with fresh lime slices for an extra burst of citrus flavor.

These sensational summer pies are just the tip of the iceberg when it comes to exploring the endless possibilities of pie-making during the summer season. Get creative and experiment with different fruits, flavors, and crusts to create your own unique summer pie creations. Whether you prefer a classic fruit pie or a more adventurous flavor combination, these recipes are guaranteed to make your taste buds sing with delight. So, gather your favorite summer ingredients and start baking your way to pie perfection. Happy pie-making!
