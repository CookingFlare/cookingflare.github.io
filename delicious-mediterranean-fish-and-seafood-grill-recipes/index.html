Delicious Mediterranean Fish and Seafood Grill Recipes


Who doesn’t love the flavors of the Mediterranean? The combination of fresh ingredients, aromatic herbs, and succulent seafood is a match made in culinary heaven. If you’re a fan of grilling and seafood, then you’re in for a treat! In this blog post, we’ll explore some mouthwatering Mediterranean fish and seafood grill recipes that are sure to impress your family and friends.

Grilled Lemon Herb Mediterranean Sea Bass

Ingredients:

  • 2 whole sea bass fillets
  • 2 lemons, sliced
  • 4 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1/4 cup of fresh parsley, chopped
  • 1/4 cup of fresh dill, chopped
  • 1/4 cup of extra virgin olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your grill to medium-high heat.
  2. In a small bowl, combine the minced garlic, chopped parsley, chopped dill, and olive oil. Mix well to create a marinade.
  3. Season the sea bass fillets with salt and pepper, then place them on a large piece of aluminum foil.
  4. Pour the marinade over the fish, making sure to coat both sides. Place the lemon slices on top of the fillets.
  5. Fold the aluminum foil to create a packet, sealing the fish and marinade inside.
  6. Place the foil packet on the grill and cook for approximately 10-12 minutes, or until the fish is cooked through and flakes easily with a fork.
  7. Carefully remove the foil packet from the grill and transfer the fish to a serving platter.
  8. Serve the Grilled Lemon Herb Mediterranean Sea Bass with a side of roasted vegetables or a fresh salad. Enjoy!

Grilled Shrimp Skewers with Mediterranean Marinade

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound of large shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 2 tablespoons of lemon juice
  • 2 tablespoons of extra virgin olive oil
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon of dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon of dried basil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Soak wooden skewers in water for at least 30 minutes to prevent them from burning on the grill.
  2. In a medium-sized bowl, combine the lemon juice, olive oil, minced garlic, dried oregano, dried basil, salt, and pepper. Mix well to create a marinade.
  3. Add the peeled and deveined shrimp to the marinade and toss to coat. Let the shrimp marinate for at least 15 minutes.
  4. Preheat your grill to medium heat.
  5. Thread the marinated shrimp onto the soaked skewers, making sure to leave a little space between each shrimp.
  6. Place the shrimp skewers on the preheated grill and cook for approximately 2-3 minutes per side, or until the shrimp turn pink and opaque.
  7. Remove the shrimp skewers from the grill and serve them hot with a squeeze of fresh lemon juice. They pair perfectly with a refreshing tzatziki sauce or a simple Greek salad.

Grilled Octopus with Lemon and Olive Oil

Ingredients:

  • 1 octopus, cleaned and tenderized
  • 3 tablespoons of extra virgin olive oil
  • Juice of 1 lemon
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon of dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your grill to medium-high heat.
  2. In a small bowl, whisk together the olive oil, lemon juice, minced garlic, dried oregano, salt, and pepper to create a marinade.
  3. Place the tenderized octopus in a shallow baking dish and pour the marinade over it, making sure to coat all sides.
  4. Let the octopus marinate for at least 30 minutes, allowing the flavors to penetrate the meat.
  5. Place the marinated octopus on the preheated grill and cook for approximately 3-4 minutes per side, or until the tentacles curl and the meat becomes tender.
  6. Carefully remove the grilled octopus from the grill and transfer it to a serving platter.
  7. Drizzle with a little extra olive oil and sprinkle with fresh lemon juice for an added burst of flavor.
  8. Serve the Grilled Octopus with Lemon and Olive Oil as an appetizer or as part of a seafood-focused meal. It pairs beautifully with a glass of chilled white wine.

Final Thoughts

Grilling fish and seafood with Mediterranean-inspired flavors is a surefire way to elevate your outdoor cooking game. These delicious recipes showcase the vibrant and aromatic ingredients that make Mediterranean cuisine so beloved. Whether you’re hosting a backyard barbecue or simply enjoying a quiet evening at home, these grill recipes are guaranteed to impress. So fire up the grill, embrace the flavors of the Mediterranean, and savor every bite of these delectable dishes. Cheers to good food and good company!

Note: Remember to check local regulations and recommendations regarding sustainable seafood choices before purchasing your ingredients.
