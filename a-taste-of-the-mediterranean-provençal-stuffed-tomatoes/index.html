A Taste of the Mediterranean: Provençal Stuffed Tomatoes


The Mediterranean region is renowned for its vibrant and flavorful cuisine, and one dish that truly captures the essence of this culinary tradition is Provençal Stuffed Tomatoes. This delightful dish combines the freshness of ripe tomatoes with the rich and aromatic flavors of the Mediterranean, creating a perfect balance of taste and texture. Whether you’re a seasoned chef or an amateur cook looking to explore new flavors, this recipe is sure to impress your taste buds and transport you to the sunny shores of the Mediterranean.

Ingredients:

  • 6 large, ripe tomatoes
  • 1 cup cooked quinoa
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1/2 cup Kalamata olives, pitted and chopped
  • 1/4 cup fresh basil, finely chopped
  • 1/4 cup fresh parsley, finely chopped
  • 1/4 cup pine nuts, lightly toasted
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the Tomatoes:

    • Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
    • Cut off the tops of the tomatoes and carefully scoop out the pulp and seeds, leaving the tomato shells intact. Sprinkle the insides of the tomatoes with a pinch of salt and place them upside down on a paper towel to drain excess moisture.

  2. Prepare the Filling:

    • In a skillet, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the chopped onion and garlic, and sauté until soft and translucent.
    • In a large bowl, combine the cooked quinoa, sautéed onion and garlic, chopped olives, basil, parsley, pine nuts, and Parmesan cheese. Season with salt and pepper, and mix until well combined.

  3. Stuff the Tomatoes:

    • Gently fill each tomato with the quinoa mixture, pressing down lightly to pack the filling.

  4. Bake the Stuffed Tomatoes:

    • Place the stuffed tomatoes in a baking dish and drizzle with a little olive oil. Bake in the preheated oven for 25-30 minutes, or until the tomatoes are tender and the filling is golden brown.

  5. Serve and Enjoy:

    • Remove the stuffed tomatoes from the oven and let them cool for a few minutes. Garnish with additional fresh herbs if desired, and serve warm as a delicious appetizer or side dish.

Why Provençal Stuffed Tomatoes?

The beauty of Provençal Stuffed Tomatoes lies in its simplicity and versatility. This dish is not only a feast for the senses but also a celebration of the wholesome ingredients and bold flavors that define Mediterranean cuisine. The combination of juicy tomatoes, fragrant herbs, and savory quinoa filling creates a harmony of tastes that will leave you craving for more.

Moreover, this recipe offers a healthy and nutritious option for those looking to incorporate more plant-based meals into their diet. The use of quinoa, a protein-rich grain, makes this dish a satisfying and wholesome choice for vegetarians and vegans alike.

In addition to its delightful taste, Provençal Stuffed Tomatoes are a visually stunning dish that will elevate any dining table. The vibrant colors of the tomatoes, combined with the earthy tones of the filling, make for an impressive presentation that is as pleasing to the eye as it is to the palate.

In Conclusion

Provençal Stuffed Tomatoes are a true embodiment of the Mediterranean approach to cooking – simple, fresh, and bursting with flavor. Whether you’re hosting a dinner party or simply craving a taste of the Mediterranean, this recipe is a must-try for anyone who appreciates the joys of home-cooked, wholesome food.

So, gather your ingredients, embrace the flavors of the Mediterranean, and let the aroma of Provençal Stuffed Tomatoes fill your kitchen. With every bite, savor the essence of the Mediterranean and experience the magic of this timeless dish. Buon appetito!
