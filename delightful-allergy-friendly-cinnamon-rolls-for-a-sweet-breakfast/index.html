Delightful Allergy-Friendly Cinnamon Rolls for a Sweet Breakfast


If you have allergies or dietary restrictions, finding delicious and satisfying breakfast options can be a challenge. But fear not! Today, I’m here to share a recipe for delightful allergy-friendly cinnamon rolls that will make your mornings extra sweet. These cinnamon rolls are free from gluten, dairy, and nuts, making them a safe and scrumptious choice for those with dietary restrictions. So, let’s dive right in and get baking!

Ingredients

For the dough:

  • 2 cups gluten-free all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 tablespoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 cup vegan butter, melted
  • 3/4 cup unsweetened almond milk (or any non-dairy milk of your choice)
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

For the filling:

  • 1/2 cup brown sugar
  • 2 tablespoons ground cinnamon
  • 2 tablespoons vegan butter, melted

For the icing:

  • 1 cup powdered sugar
  • 1-2 tablespoons unsweetened almond milk (or any non-dairy milk of your choice)
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C) and prepare a baking dish by greasing it lightly with vegan butter or lining it with parchment paper.

  2. In a large mixing bowl, combine the gluten-free all-purpose flour, granulated sugar, baking powder, and salt. Mix well until all the ingredients are evenly distributed.

  3. Add the melted vegan butter, almond milk, and vanilla extract to the dry ingredients. Mix everything together until a sticky dough forms. Don’t worry if the dough is slightly sticky; this will result in soft and tender cinnamon rolls.

  4. Dust a clean surface with gluten-free flour and transfer the dough onto it. Knead the dough gently for a couple of minutes until it becomes smooth and elastic. If the dough is too sticky, you can add a little more flour.

  5. Roll out the dough into a rectangular shape, about 1/4 inch thick. Make sure the dough is evenly rolled out to ensure uniform cinnamon rolls.

  6. In a small bowl, combine the brown sugar and ground cinnamon for the filling. Mix well.

  7. Using a pastry brush, spread the melted vegan butter evenly over the rolled-out dough. Sprinkle the cinnamon-sugar mixture generously over the buttered dough, ensuring that it covers the entire surface.

  8. Starting from one end, carefully roll the dough into a tight log. If the dough sticks to the surface, use a spatula to lift it gently.

  9. Using a sharp knife, slice the rolled dough into 1 1/2-inch thick slices. Place the slices in the prepared baking dish, leaving a little space between each roll.

  10. Bake the cinnamon rolls in the preheated oven for 20-25 minutes or until they turn golden brown and fragrant.

  11. While the cinnamon rolls are baking, prepare the icing. In a small bowl, whisk together the powdered sugar, almond milk, and vanilla extract until smooth and creamy.

  12. Once the cinnamon rolls are done, remove them from the oven and let them cool for a few minutes. Drizzle the icing over the warm cinnamon rolls, allowing it to seep into the crevices.

  13. Serve the allergy-friendly cinnamon rolls warm and enjoy the irresistible aroma and taste!

Final Thoughts

These allergy-friendly cinnamon rolls are a true breakfast delight! With their soft and fluffy texture, sweet cinnamon filling, and delectable icing, they are sure to satisfy your cravings. Whether you have dietary restrictions or simply want to try a new twist on a classic breakfast treat, these cinnamon rolls are the perfect choice. So, gather your ingredients, preheat your oven, and get ready to indulge in a sweet and allergy-friendly breakfast that will leave you wanting more!
