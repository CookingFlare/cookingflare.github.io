Meanwhile, in a large skillet, cook the pancetta over medium heat until crispy. Remove from heat and set aside. In a bowl, whisk together the egg yolks, Parmesan cheese, salt, and pepper. Drain the cooked spaghetti, reserving a small amount of the cooking water. Immediately transfer the drained spaghetti to the skillet with the pancetta. Stir well to coat the pasta. Pour in the egg yolk mixture and toss quickly to combine, ensuring the eggs don’t scramble. If the mixture seems too dry, add a splash of the reserved cooking water to loosen it up. In this blog post, we’ll explore three mouthwatering chicken recipes that are designed for two people, perfect for those busy weeknights when you want something simple, tasty, and satisfying. Recipe 1: Lemon Herb Chicken Skillet Ingredients: 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts 1 tablespoon olive oil 2 cloves garlic, minced 1 teaspoon dried oregano 1 teaspoon dried thyme Juice of 1 lemon Salt and pepper to taste Fresh parsley for garnish Instructions: In a large skillet, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Season the chicken breasts with salt and pepper, then add them to the skillet. Cook for 5-6 minutes on each side, or until golden brown and cooked through. Remove the chicken from the skillet and set aside. In the same skillet, add the minced garlic, dried oregano, and dried thyme. Cook for 1-2 minutes, stirring constantly. Return the chicken to the skillet and pour the lemon juice over the top. Allow the chicken to simmer in the lemon and herb mixture for 2-3 minutes. Garnish with fresh parsley and serve with your choice of side dishes. Lemon Herb Chicken with Roasted Broccoli Ingredients: 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts 1 lemon (juice and zest) 1 tablespoon olive oil 1 teaspoon dried Italian herbs (or a mix of oregano, thyme, and rosemary) 1 head of broccoli, cut into florets Instructions: Preheat the oven to 400°F (200°C). Place the broccoli florets on a baking sheet. Toss with half the olive oil and a sprinkle of salt and pepper. Place the chicken on a separate baking sheet or in an oven-safe dish. Roast both the chicken and broccoli in the oven for 20–25 minutes. Flip the chicken halfway through for even cooking. Serve the juicy, herb-infused chicken alongside the roasted broccoli for a well-balanced dinner.
