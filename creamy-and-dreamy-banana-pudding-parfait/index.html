Creamy and Dreamy Banana Pudding Parfait


If you’re craving a delightful and luscious dessert that’s both easy to make and absolutely delicious, then you’re in for a treat! This creamy and dreamy banana pudding parfait is a delightful twist on the classic banana pudding, and it’s sure to impress your family and friends. The combination of velvety pudding, ripe bananas, and crunchy cookies creates a symphony of flavors and textures that will leave everyone wanting more. So, let’s dive into the recipe and create this heavenly dessert!

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups of whole milk
  • 1/2 cup of granulated sugar
  • 1/4 cup of cornstarch
  • 1/4 teaspoon of salt
  • 4 large egg yolks
  • 2 tablespoons of unsalted butter
  • 2 teaspoons of pure vanilla extract
  • 3 ripe bananas, sliced
  • 1 cup of heavy cream
  • 2 tablespoons of powdered sugar
  • 1 teaspoon of vanilla extract
  • 2 cups of vanilla wafer cookies, crushed

Instructions:

  1. In a medium saucepan, combine the whole milk, granulated sugar, cornstarch, and salt. Whisk the mixture until the cornstarch is fully dissolved.

  2. Place the saucepan over medium heat and cook the mixture, stirring constantly, until it thickens and comes to a gentle boil. This should take about 10-12 minutes.

  3. In a separate bowl, whisk the egg yolks. Gradually whisk in about 1 cup of the hot milk mixture into the egg yolks to temper them. Then, pour the egg mixture back into the saucepan with the remaining milk mixture.

  4. Cook the pudding for an additional 2 minutes, stirring constantly. Remove the saucepan from the heat and stir in the butter and vanilla extract until well combined.

  5. Transfer the pudding to a bowl and cover the surface with plastic wrap to prevent a skin from forming. Refrigerate for at least 3 hours, or until the pudding is completely chilled.

  6. In a large bowl, whip the heavy cream, powdered sugar, and vanilla extract until stiff peaks form.

  7. To assemble the parfaits, layer the bottom of serving glasses or jars with a spoonful of crushed vanilla wafer cookies. Add a layer of sliced bananas, followed by a layer of the chilled banana pudding. Repeat the layers until the glasses are filled, ending with a dollop of the whipped cream on top.

  8. Garnish the parfaits with a sprinkle of crushed vanilla wafer cookies for an extra crunch.

  9. Chill the parfaits in the refrigerator for at least 30 minutes before serving to allow the flavors to meld together.

Tips and Tricks:

  • For an extra flavor boost, consider adding a drizzle of caramel or chocolate sauce between the layers.
  • If you’re short on time, you can use instant vanilla pudding mix as a shortcut for the homemade pudding.
  • Feel free to customize the parfaits with additional toppings such as chopped nuts, shredded coconut, or fresh berries.

Now that you have the recipe for this indulgent banana pudding parfait, it’s time to get into the kitchen and whip up this sweet delight. Whether you’re hosting a dinner party or simply treating yourself to a special dessert, these creamy and dreamy parfaits are guaranteed to be a hit. Enjoy every spoonful of this luscious treat and savor the delightful combination of flavors in every layer. Happy cooking!
