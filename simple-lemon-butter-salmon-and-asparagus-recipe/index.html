Simple Lemon Butter Salmon and Asparagus Recipe


If you’re looking for a delicious and healthy meal that’s easy to prepare, look no further than this Simple Lemon Butter Salmon and Asparagus recipe. This dish is not only bursting with flavor but also packed with nutrients, making it a perfect choice for a wholesome dinner. Let’s dive into the details of this delightful recipe.

Ingredients:

  • 4 salmon fillets
  • 1 bunch of fresh asparagus
  • 2 tablespoons of olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons of unsalted butter
  • 4 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 lemon
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the Oven: Start by preheating your oven to 400°F (200°C). This will ensure that the salmon and asparagus cook evenly and thoroughly.

  2. Prepare the Asparagus: Wash the asparagus thoroughly and trim off the woody ends. Place the asparagus on a baking sheet and drizzle with 1 tablespoon of olive oil. Season with salt and pepper, then toss to coat evenly.

  3. Season the Salmon: Pat the salmon fillets dry with a paper towel and place them on the same baking sheet as the asparagus. Drizzle the remaining tablespoon of olive oil over the salmon and season with salt and pepper.

  4. Lemon Butter Mixture: In a small saucepan, melt the unsalted butter over medium heat. Add the minced garlic and sauté for a minute until fragrant. Squeeze the juice of half a lemon into the butter mixture and stir well.

  5. Assemble and Bake: Pour the lemon butter mixture over the salmon fillets, making sure to coat them evenly. Slice the remaining half of the lemon into thin rounds and place them on top of the salmon. Transfer the baking sheet to the preheated oven and bake for 12-15 minutes, or until the salmon is cooked through and the asparagus is tender.

  6. Serve and Enjoy: Once the salmon and asparagus are cooked to perfection, remove them from the oven. Garnish with freshly chopped parsley for a pop of color and added freshness. Serve the salmon and asparagus hot, and savor the delightful flavors of this simple yet elegant dish.

Nutritional Benefits:

Salmon is renowned for its high omega-3 fatty acid content, making it incredibly beneficial for heart health and overall well-being. Paired with the nutrient-rich asparagus, this dish provides a healthy dose of vitamins and minerals, including vitamin K, folate, and antioxidants. The lemon butter adds a zesty and indulgent touch, elevating the flavors while keeping the dish light and refreshing.

Final Thoughts:

This Simple Lemon Butter Salmon and Asparagus recipe is a testament to the fact that healthy eating can be both uncomplicated and incredibly satisfying. Whether you’re cooking for a family dinner or hosting a small gathering, this dish is sure to impress with its vibrant flavors and wholesome appeal. Give it a try and relish in the joy of preparing a nourishing meal that delights the senses.

So there you have it – a simple yet sophisticated recipe that celebrates the natural flavors of salmon and asparagus. Enjoy the process of creating this delectable dish, and savor every bite knowing that you’ve treated yourself to a wholesome, homemade meal. Cheers to good food and good health!
