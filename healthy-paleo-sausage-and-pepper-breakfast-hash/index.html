Healthy Paleo Sausage and Pepper Breakfast Hash


Are you tired of the same old boring breakfast options? Do you want to start your day with a delicious and nutritious meal that will keep you energized throughout the morning? Look no further! This Healthy Paleo Sausage and Pepper Breakfast Hash is the perfect way to kickstart your day.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound of paleo-friendly sausage (choose your favorite flavor)
  • 2 bell peppers (red, yellow, or green), diced
  • 1 medium-sized sweet potato, peeled and cubed
  • 1 small onion, diced
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons of olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Optional toppings: avocado slices, fresh cilantro, hot sauce

Instructions

  1. In a large skillet, heat the olive oil over medium-high heat. Add the diced onion and minced garlic and sauté until fragrant and translucent, about 2-3 minutes.

  2. Add the cubed sweet potato to the skillet and cook for about 5 minutes, stirring occasionally, until the sweet potato begins to soften.

  3. Push the sweet potato to one side of the skillet and add the sausage to the other side. Break up the sausage into smaller pieces using a spatula and cook until browned and cooked through, about 7-8 minutes.

  4. Once the sausage is cooked, mix it together with the sweet potato in the skillet. Add the diced bell peppers and season with salt and pepper to taste. Cook for an additional 5 minutes, or until the peppers have softened slightly.

  5. Remove the skillet from heat and let it cool for a few minutes before serving. Top with avocado slices, fresh cilantro, or your favorite hot sauce for an extra burst of flavor.

Why Choose Paleo?

The Paleo diet focuses on consuming whole, unprocessed foods that were available to our ancestors during the Paleolithic era. By eliminating grains, dairy, and processed sugars, the Paleo diet promotes a healthier lifestyle and can lead to weight loss, increased energy levels, and improved digestion.

This Healthy Paleo Sausage and Pepper Breakfast Hash is a perfect example of a Paleo-friendly meal. It includes high-quality protein from the sausage, a variety of colorful vegetables, and healthy fats from the olive oil and avocado. This combination of nutrients will keep you feeling satisfied and fueled until your next meal.

Final Thoughts

Breakfast is often referred to as the most important meal of the day, and with this Healthy Paleo Sausage and Pepper Breakfast Hash, you’ll be starting your day off on the right foot. Not only is it delicious and easy to make, but it’s also packed with nutrients that will keep you energized and focused throughout the morning.

So why settle for a boring bowl of cereal or a plain piece of toast when you can enjoy a flavorful and satisfying breakfast hash? Give this recipe a try and see for yourself how delicious healthy eating can be!

Remember, while this recipe is paleo-friendly, it can be enjoyed by anyone looking for a tasty and nutritious breakfast option. Whether you’re following a specific diet or simply looking to incorporate more whole foods into your meals, this Healthy Paleo Sausage and Pepper Breakfast Hash is a recipe you won’t want to miss.

Enjoy and happy cooking!
