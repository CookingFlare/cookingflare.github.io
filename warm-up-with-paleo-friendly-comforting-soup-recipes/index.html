Warm Up with Paleo-Friendly Comforting Soup Recipes


When the weather turns chilly, there’s nothing like a warm, comforting bowl of soup to lift our spirits and soothe our souls. But, as the paleo diet gains popularity, many of us are looking for ways to adapt our favorite comfort food recipes to fit our new lifestyle. In this post, we’ll explore some delicious and paleo-friendly comforting soup recipes that are sure to warm your heart and belly.

Creamy Broccoli Soup

This creamy broccoli soup is a paleo twist on the classic comfort food. By using coconut cream instead of traditional heavy cream, we can enjoy the rich and creamy texture without sacrificing our dietary goals.

Ingredients:

  • 2 tablespoons of coconut oil
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 4 cups of broccoli florets
  • 4 cups of chicken broth
  • 1/2 cup of coconut cream
  • 1 teaspoon of salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon of black pepper
  • 1/4 teaspoon of paprika

Instructions:

  1. In a large pot, heat the coconut oil over medium heat. Add the chopped onion and cook until softened, about 5 minutes.
  2. Add the minced garlic and cook for an additional 1-2 minutes, until fragrant.
  3. Add the broccoli florets, chicken broth, coconut cream, salt, black pepper, and paprika. Bring the mixture to a boil, then reduce the heat and simmer for 20-25 minutes, or until the broccoli is tender.
  4. Use an immersion blender to puree the soup until smooth. Alternatively, you can transfer the soup to a blender and blend until smooth, then return it to the pot.

Spicy Sweet Potato Soup

This spicy sweet potato soup is a perfect way to warm up on a chilly day. By adding a kick of heat from the cayenne pepper, we can add depth and complexity to this comforting soup.

Ingredients:

  • 2 tablespoons of coconut oil
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 2 large sweet potatoes, peeled and diced
  • 4 cups of chicken broth
  • 1 can of diced tomatoes
  • 1 teaspoon of ground cumin
  • 1/2 teaspoon of smoked paprika
  • 1/4 teaspoon of cayenne pepper
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a large pot, heat the coconut oil over medium heat. Add the chopped onion and cook until softened, about 5 minutes.
  2. Add the minced garlic and cook for an additional 1-2 minutes, until fragrant.
  3. Add the diced sweet potatoes, chicken broth, diced tomatoes, ground cumin, smoked paprika, and cayenne pepper. Bring the mixture to a boil, then reduce the heat and simmer for 25-30 minutes, or until the sweet potatoes are tender.
  4. Use an immersion blender to puree the soup until smooth. Alternatively, you can transfer the soup to a blender and blend until smooth, then return it to the pot.

Butternut Squash Soup

This butternut squash soup is a delicious and comforting paleo-friendly option for the fall and winter seasons. By roasting the squash before blending it into a soup, we can bring out its natural sweetness and depth of flavor.

Ingredients:

  • 1 large butternut squash, peeled and cubed
  • 2 tablespoons of coconut oil
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 4 cups of chicken broth
  • 1/2 cup of coconut cream
  • 1 teaspoon of salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon of black pepper
  • 1/4 teaspoon of nutmeg

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 400°F (200°C).
  2. Place the cubed butternut squash on a baking sheet and drizzle with coconut oil. Roast in the oven for 30-40 minutes, or until the squash is tender and caramelized.
  3. In a large pot, heat the coconut oil over medium heat. Add the chopped onion and cook until softened, about 5 minutes.
  4. Add the minced garlic and cook for an additional 1-2 minutes, until fragrant.
  5. Add the roasted butternut squash, chicken broth, coconut cream, salt, black pepper, and nutmeg. Bring the mixture to a boil, then reduce the heat and simmer for 10-15 minutes, or until the soup is heated through.
  6. Use an immersion blender to puree the soup until smooth. Alternatively, you can transfer the soup to a blender and blend until smooth, then return it to the pot.

Conclusion

These paleo-friendly comforting soup recipes are perfect for warming up on a chilly day. By using coconut cream and other paleo-friendly ingredients, we can enjoy the rich and comforting flavors of our favorite soups without sacrificing our dietary goals. Whether you’re in the mood for something creamy and rich or spicy and savory, there’s a paleo-friendly soup recipe on this list that’s sure to hit the spot. So go ahead, grab a spoon, and dig in!
