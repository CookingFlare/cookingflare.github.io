Wholesome Dairy-Free Banana Pancakes Recipe


Who doesn’t love pancakes for breakfast? They are fluffy, delicious, and the perfect way to start your day. But what if you’re following a dairy-free diet? Don’t worry, I’ve got you covered with this wholesome dairy-free banana pancakes recipe. These pancakes are not only dairy-free but also packed with nutrients. So, let’s get cooking!

Ingredients

  • 2 ripe bananas
  • 1 cup oat flour
  • 1/2 cup almond milk
  • 1 tablespoon maple syrup
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon cinnamon
  • Pinch of salt

Instructions

  1. In a mixing bowl, mash the ripe bananas until smooth and creamy.

  2. Add the oat flour, almond milk, maple syrup, vanilla extract, baking powder, cinnamon, and salt to the bowl. Mix well until all the ingredients are combined and you have a smooth batter.

  3. Heat a non-stick pan or griddle over medium heat. You can lightly grease the pan with cooking spray or coconut oil if desired.

  4. Pour about 1/4 cup of the pancake batter onto the pan for each pancake. Cook for 2-3 minutes, or until bubbles start to form on the surface.

  5. Flip the pancakes and cook for an additional 2-3 minutes, or until golden brown.

  6. Repeat the process with the remaining batter until all the pancakes are cooked.

  7. Serve the pancakes warm and top them with your favorite toppings. Fresh berries, sliced bananas, chopped nuts, or a drizzle of pure maple syrup are all great options.

Nutritional Information

  • Calories: 250
  • Fat: 4g
  • Carbohydrates: 50g
  • Fiber: 6g
  • Protein: 5g

Why Choose Dairy-Free Pancakes?

Dairy-free pancakes are a great option for those who are lactose intolerant or following a dairy-free diet. They are also a healthier alternative for anyone looking to reduce their dairy consumption. By using almond milk instead of regular milk, you not only avoid the lactose found in dairy but also add a nutty flavor to the pancakes.

Benefits of Using Ripe Bananas

Ripe bananas are the secret ingredient in this recipe. Not only do they add natural sweetness to the pancakes, but they also provide a host of health benefits. Ripe bananas are easier to digest and are rich in dietary fiber, potassium, and antioxidants. They also give the pancakes a moist texture, making them even more delicious.

The Power of Oat Flour

Oat flour is a gluten-free alternative to regular flour that adds a nutty flavor and a soft texture to the pancakes. It is also rich in fiber, which aids digestion and helps you feel full for longer. Oats are a great source of complex carbohydrates, providing sustained energy throughout the day.

Final Thoughts

These wholesome dairy-free banana pancakes are the perfect breakfast treat for anyone, whether you’re following a dairy-free diet or not. They are easy to make, delicious, and packed with natural goodness. So, next time you’re craving pancakes, give this recipe a try and enjoy a guilt-free breakfast that will leave you feeling satisfied and nourished.
