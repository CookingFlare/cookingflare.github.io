A Taste of Parisian Cafés: Croque Monsieur Sandwich


If there’s one thing that instantly transports you to the charming streets of Paris, it’s the enticing aroma wafting from the local cafés. And when it comes to classic French café fare, the Croque Monsieur sandwich reigns supreme. This delectable creation is a delightful combination of crispy bread, creamy béchamel sauce, rich ham, and gooey cheese. It’s a true Parisian treat that will make your taste buds dance with joy.

Ingredients

To recreate the magic of a Parisian café right in your own kitchen, gather the following ingredients:

  • 8 slices of thick-cut white bread
  • 8 slices of ham
  • 2 cups of Gruyère cheese, grated
  • 4 tablespoons of unsalted butter
  • 4 tablespoons of all-purpose flour
  • 2 cups of whole milk
  • 1/4 teaspoon of ground nutmeg
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).

  2. In a saucepan, melt the butter over medium heat. Once melted, add the flour and whisk continuously for about 2 minutes to create a roux.

  3. Gradually pour in the milk while whisking constantly to ensure a smooth sauce. Continue whisking until the sauce thickens and coats the back of a spoon. This should take around 5 minutes.

  4. Remove the saucepan from the heat and season the béchamel sauce with nutmeg, salt, and pepper. Set it aside.

  5. Take four slices of bread and spread a generous amount of béchamel sauce on one side of each slice.

  6. Place a slice of ham on top of the béchamel sauce on each bread slice.

  7. Sprinkle a handful of grated Gruyère cheese over the ham on each slice.

  8. Cover each sandwich with the remaining four slices of bread.

  9. In a large skillet, melt a tablespoon of butter over medium heat. Place two sandwiches in the skillet and cook for about 2-3 minutes on each side, until the bread turns golden brown and the cheese melts.

  10. Repeat step 9 for the remaining two sandwiches.

  11. Transfer the cooked sandwiches to a baking sheet and place them in the preheated oven for about 5 minutes, or until the cheese is fully melted and bubbly.

  12. Once done, remove the Croque Monsieur sandwiches from the oven and let them cool for a few minutes.

  13. Cut each sandwich diagonally into two triangles and serve hot.

The Perfect Pairing

To truly savor the Parisian café experience, pair your Croque Monsieur sandwich with a cup of rich and aromatic French press coffee. The combination of the crispy sandwich and the robust coffee will transport you to the bustling streets of Paris, where locals and tourists alike indulge in this classic delicacy.

Bon Appétit!

Indulging in a Croque Monsieur sandwich is like taking a bite out of the Parisian joie de vivre. The layers of flavors, the crispy texture, and the creamy béchamel sauce all come together to create a culinary masterpiece. Whether you’re hosting a brunch, preparing a quick lunch, or simply treating yourself, this iconic French sandwich is a must-try. So, embrace the Parisian spirit, gather your ingredients, and embark on a flavor-filled journey to the streets of Paris with a homemade Croque Monsieur sandwich. Bon appétit!
