Easy and Flavorful Chicken Kebabs with Middle Eastern Spices


Chicken kebabs are one of the most versatile and delicious dishes, perfect for any occasion. With their tender, juicy meat infused with the rich flavors of Middle Eastern spices, these kebabs will elevate any meal. Whether you’re grilling in the backyard or cooking indoors, chicken kebabs are a crowd-pleasing option that combines simplicity with bold flavor.

In this recipe, we’ll show you how to prepare chicken kebabs with a delightful Middle Eastern twist. The secret lies in the vibrant blend of spices, including cumin, paprika, coriander, and a touch of cinnamon, which gives the chicken a unique taste that is aromatic and savory. The result is a dish that’s sure to impress your family, friends, or guests. This recipe is easy to follow and can be adapted to suit your preferences.

Ingredients for Chicken Kebabs

For this recipe, you will need the following ingredients:

  • Chicken breast or thighs (boneless, skinless) – 1.5 pounds, cut into 1-inch cubes
  • Olive oil – 3 tablespoons
  • Lemon juice – 2 tablespoons, fresh
  • Garlic – 3 cloves, minced
  • Onion – 1 small, grated or finely chopped
  • Ground cumin – 1 teaspoon
  • Ground coriander – 1 teaspoon
  • Ground paprika – 1 teaspoon
  • Ground cinnamon – 1/2 teaspoon
  • Turmeric – 1/2 teaspoon
  • Salt – 1 teaspoon
  • Black pepper – 1/2 teaspoon
  • Chili flakes (optional) – 1/4 teaspoon (for a spicy kick)
  • Fresh parsley – 2 tablespoons, chopped (for garnish)
  • Wooden or metal skewers – for grilling

For the marinade:

  • Plain yogurt – 1/2 cup (helps tenderize the chicken and adds creaminess)
  • Olive oil – 2 tablespoons
  • Lemon juice – 1 tablespoon
  • Ground cumin – 1 teaspoon
  • Ground coriander – 1 teaspoon
  • Ground paprika – 1/2 teaspoon
  • Fresh garlic – 2 cloves, minced
  • Fresh ginger – 1 tablespoon, grated (optional for added flavor)
  • Salt – 1 teaspoon
  • Black pepper – 1/2 teaspoon

Preparing the Chicken Marinade

The first step in creating these juicy and flavorful chicken kebabs is preparing the marinade. A good marinade is essential for infusing the chicken with flavor and ensuring that it stays moist during cooking.

  1. Mix the Marinade: In a medium-sized bowl, combine the plain yogurt, olive oil, lemon juice, ground cumin, ground coriander, ground paprika, minced garlic, grated ginger, salt, and black pepper. Whisk everything together until smooth and well blended.

  2. Marinate the Chicken: Add the chicken cubes to the bowl, ensuring they are fully coated with the marinade. Use your hands or a spoon to gently mix the chicken and marinade, making sure each piece is covered in the flavorful mixture.

  3. Rest the Chicken: Cover the bowl with plastic wrap or a lid, and refrigerate for at least 1 hour to allow the spices to infuse into the chicken. For the best results, marinate the chicken for 4-6 hours or overnight. The longer the chicken marinates, the more flavorful and tender it becomes.

Preparing the Skewers

While the chicken is marinating, it’s a good time to prepare the skewers for grilling. If you’re using wooden skewers, soak them in water for at least 30 minutes before use to prevent them from burning on the grill. If you’re using metal skewers, you can skip this step.

  1. Prepare Vegetables (optional): If you’d like to add some vegetables to your kebabs, bell peppers, red onions, cherry tomatoes, and zucchini are all great options. Cut them into 1-inch chunks and set them aside.

  2. Assemble the Skewers: Once the chicken has finished marinating, begin threading the chicken pieces onto the skewers. If you are using vegetables, alternate the chicken pieces with the veggies on the skewers. Make sure to pack the pieces closely together to ensure even cooking.

  3. Preheat the Grill: Whether you’re grilling on a charcoal grill or a gas grill, preheat your grill to medium-high heat (around 400°F/200°C). You can also cook the kebabs under a broiler if you don’t have access to a grill.

Grilling the Chicken Kebabs

Now that your chicken and vegetables are on the skewers and the grill is ready, it’s time to cook the kebabs. Follow these simple steps for perfectly cooked, juicy chicken kebabs:

  1. Place the Kebabs on the Grill: Position the skewers on the grill, making sure they’re not overcrowded. You want enough space between the kebabs for the heat to circulate evenly, allowing the chicken to cook through without becoming too charred.

  2. Grill the Chicken: Grill the kebabs for about 6-8 minutes on each side, turning them carefully with tongs. The chicken should reach an internal temperature of 165°F (75°C) to ensure it is fully cooked.

  3. Check for Doneness: To check if the chicken is done, pierce one of the pieces with a meat thermometer or knife. The juices should run clear, and the chicken should be opaque throughout.

  4. Optional Step – Add Some Flavor Boost: About 2 minutes before finishing, brush the kebabs with a little extra olive oil or melted butter. You can also sprinkle some fresh lemon juice or additional spices at this stage for extra flavor.

Serving the Chicken Kebabs

Once the chicken kebabs are cooked to perfection, it’s time to serve them! Here are a few ideas for how to serve your Middle Eastern-inspired kebabs:

  • With Pita Bread or Flatbread: Warm pita or flatbreads are perfect for wrapping around the kebabs. Add some fresh vegetables like lettuce, cucumbers, and tomatoes for a delicious wrap. You can also spread a little hummus or garlic sauce for extra flavor.

  • On a Bed of Rice or Couscous: Serve your kebabs over a bed of fluffy rice or couscous to soak up the juices. A simple rice pilaf with saffron or a Mediterranean-style couscous salad with olives and herbs will complement the flavors beautifully.

  • With a Side of Salad: Pair your kebabs with a fresh, light salad. A classic Middle Eastern salad of chopped cucumbers, tomatoes, red onions, and parsley with a lemon-olive oil dressing is the perfect balance to the rich spices in the kebabs.

  • With Yogurt Sauce: A cooling yogurt sauce with mint or garlic works wonders with the spiced chicken. Simply combine plain yogurt with chopped mint, garlic, a squeeze of lemon juice, and a pinch of salt.

Tips for the Best Chicken Kebabs

  • Marinate the Chicken Longer: If you have the time, marinate the chicken for a longer period. Overnight marination will allow the flavors to penetrate the meat deeply, resulting in tender and flavorful kebabs.

  • Use Chicken Thighs for Extra Juiciness: While chicken breasts are lean and work well in kebabs, chicken thighs offer more flavor and juiciness due to their higher fat content. If you prefer more tender and moist kebabs, consider using boneless, skinless chicken thighs.

  • Experiment with Spices: Middle Eastern cuisine has a rich variety of spices. Feel free to experiment with other spices like sumac, za’atar, or allspice for a different twist on the flavors.

  • Keep the Chicken Pieces Uniform: For even cooking, try to cut the chicken pieces into uniform sizes. This ensures that all the pieces cook at the same rate, preventing some from being overcooked while others are undercooked.

  • Don’t Overcrowd the Skewers: Leave a little space between each piece of chicken so that the heat can circulate properly. Overcrowding the skewers can cause the chicken to steam instead of grill, resulting in less flavor and a tougher texture.

Conclusion

These chicken kebabs with Middle Eastern spices are a simple yet impressive dish that packs a punch of flavor. The combination of marinated chicken, fresh vegetables, and aromatic spices makes for a delicious and versatile meal. Whether you’re grilling outdoors or cooking indoors, these kebabs are sure to be a hit with your family and friends. They can be served as part of a larger meal or as a stand-alone dish, and they pair well with a variety of sides, from rice to salads to flatbreads.

By following this recipe, you’ll be able to enjoy a delicious taste of the Middle East with minimal effort. So fire up your grill or preheat your oven, and let the mouthwatering aroma of these chicken kebabs fill your kitchen!
