Hearty One Pot Chili Mac and Cheese


As the winter chill sets in, there’s nothing quite like a warm, comforting bowl of mac and cheese to lift our spirits. But why settle for a plain old macaroni dish when you can elevate it to a whole new level with the addition of hearty, slow-cooked chili? Introducing our Hearty One Pot Chili Mac and Cheese recipe, a game-changing twist on a classic comfort food.

The Magic of One Pot Cooking

One pot cooking is a revelation for busy home cooks. With minimal cleanup and maximum flavor, it’s a wonder why anyone would bother with multiple pots and pans. In this recipe, we’ll be using a large Dutch oven to cook our macaroni, chili, and cheese mixture all in one go. The result is a rich, velvety sauce and perfectly cooked pasta, all infused with the deep flavors of slow-cooked chili.

The Chili: A Flavorful Foundation

At the heart of our One Pot Chili Mac and Cheese is, of course, the chili. We’re using a classic beef and tomato-based chili recipe, slow-cooked to perfection in the Dutch oven. The key to a great chili is a good balance of flavors, so we’re using a combination of ground beef, onions, garlic, and a blend of spices to create a rich, savory flavor profile. And don’t even get us started on the importance of using high-quality canned tomatoes – they make all the difference in the world.

The Mac and Cheese: A Creamy, Cheesy Masterpiece

Now that we have our chili foundation, it’s time to add the mac and cheese. We’re using a combination of macaroni, cheddar, and Parmesan cheese to create a creamy, cheesy sauce that’s sure to satisfy even the most discerning palates. And the best part? We’re cooking it all in one pot, so there’s no need to worry about separate sauces or complicated layering. It’s a one-pot wonder, if you will.

The Recipe: A Hearty, One Pot Chili Mac and Cheese

Here’s the recipe in all its glory:

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1 large onion, diced
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can (14.5 oz) diced tomatoes
  • 1 can (15 oz) red kidney beans, drained and rinsed
  • 1 teaspoon chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1/2 teaspoon paprika
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • 1 lb macaroni
  • 2 cups grated cheddar cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 cups beef broth
  • 1/4 cup milk
  • 2 tablespoons butter

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (180°C).
  2. In a large Dutch oven, cook the ground beef over medium-high heat, breaking it up with a spoon as it cooks, until it’s fully browned.
  3. Add the diced onion and minced garlic to the pot, cooking until the onion is translucent.
  4. Add the chili powder, cumin, paprika, salt, and pepper to the pot, stirring to combine.
  5. Add the diced tomatoes, red kidney beans, and beef broth to the pot, stirring to combine.
  6. Bring the mixture to a simmer, then reduce the heat to low and cook, covered, for 30 minutes.
  7. Add the macaroni to the pot, stirring to combine.
  8. In a small bowl, whisk together the flour and milk until smooth. Add the butter and whisk until melted.
  9. Pour the flour mixture over the macaroni and chili mixture, stirring to combine.
  10. Bring the mixture to a simmer, then reduce the heat to low and cook, covered, for 20-25 minutes, or until the macaroni is al dente and the sauce is creamy and cheesy.
  11. Stir in the grated cheddar and Parmesan cheese until melted and smooth.
  12. Serve hot, garnished with chopped fresh parsley or chives, if desired.

The Verdict: A Hearty, One Pot Chili Mac and Cheese for the Ages

And there you have it – a Hearty One Pot Chili Mac and Cheese recipe that’s sure to become a family favorite. With its rich, slow-cooked chili and creamy, cheesy macaroni, it’s a dish that’s sure to satisfy even the heartiest of appetites. So go ahead, gather your loved ones around the table, and dig in to a comforting, one-pot wonder that’s sure to become a staple in your household.
