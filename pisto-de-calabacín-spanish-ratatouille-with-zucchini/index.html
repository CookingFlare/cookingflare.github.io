Pisto de Calabacín: Spanish Ratatouille with Zucchini


If you’re a fan of Mediterranean flavors and hearty vegetable dishes, then Pisto de Calabacín, also known as Spanish Ratatouille with Zucchini, is a must-try recipe. This traditional Spanish dish is bursting with vibrant colors and robust flavors, making it a perfect addition to your summer menu. So, let’s dive into the world of Spanish cuisine and learn how to make this delicious and nutritious dish!

Ingredients

To make Pisto de Calabacín, you’ll need the following ingredients:

  • 4 zucchinis, diced
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 1 red bell pepper, diced
  • 1 yellow bell pepper, diced
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 4 ripe tomatoes, peeled and diced
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • Fresh parsley, chopped (for garnish)

Instructions

  1. Heat the olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Add the onion and garlic, and sauté until they become translucent and fragrant.

  2. Add the diced zucchinis, red and yellow bell peppers to the skillet. Cook for about 5 minutes, stirring occasionally, until the vegetables start to soften.

  3. Stir in the diced tomatoes, paprika, dried oregano, salt, and pepper. Reduce the heat to low and let the mixture simmer for about 20 minutes, allowing the flavors to meld together.

  4. Once the vegetables are tender and the flavors have developed, remove the skillet from the heat. Give it a taste and adjust the seasoning if needed.

  5. Serve the Pisto de Calabacín hot, garnished with fresh parsley. It can be enjoyed as a main dish, served with crusty bread, or as a side dish alongside grilled meats or fish.

Why Pisto de Calabacín?

Pisto de Calabacín is not only a delicious and versatile dish, but it’s also a healthy addition to your diet. Zucchinis, the star ingredient of this recipe, are low in calories and packed with essential nutrients like vitamin C, vitamin A, and potassium. They also provide dietary fiber, which aids in digestion and promotes a healthy gut.

This Spanish Ratatouille is a great way to incorporate more vegetables into your meals, and it can easily be customized to suit your taste preferences. You can add other vegetables like eggplant or mushrooms, or even throw in some cooked chickpeas for extra protein. The options are endless!

In Conclusion

Pisto de Calabacín is a delightful dish that showcases the vibrant flavors of Spanish cuisine. Its simplicity and versatility make it a go-to recipe for both busy weeknights and special occasions. So, why not give it a try and savor the taste of Spain in your own kitchen? Your taste buds will thank you!

Remember, cooking is all about experimentation and personalization, so feel free to add your own twist to this recipe. Whether you enjoy it as a main course or a side dish, Pisto de Calabacín is sure to impress your family and friends with its rich flavors and colorful presentation.

¡Buen provecho! (Enjoy your meal!)
