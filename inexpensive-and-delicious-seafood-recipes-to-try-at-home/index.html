Inexpensive and Delicious Seafood Recipes to Try at Home


Are you a seafood lover on a budget? Look no further! In this blog post, we have compiled a list of inexpensive and delicious seafood recipes that you can easily prepare at home. These recipes are not only budget-friendly but also packed with flavors that will leave you craving for more. So, let’s dive right in!

1. Garlic Shrimp Pasta

Preparation time: 20 minutes | Cooking time: 15 minutes | Serves: 4

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound of shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 8 ounces of linguine or spaghetti
  • 4 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons of olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon of butter
  • 1/4 cup of white wine (optional)
  • Juice of 1 lemon
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley, chopped for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Cook pasta according to package instructions until al dente. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a large skillet, heat olive oil and butter over medium heat. Add minced garlic and sauté until fragrant.
  3. Add shrimp to the skillet and cook until they turn pink, about 2-3 minutes per side.
  4. If desired, deglaze the skillet with white wine and let it cook for another minute.
  5. Squeeze the lemon juice over the shrimp and season with salt and pepper.
  6. Add the cooked pasta to the skillet and toss everything together until well coated.
  7. Serve the garlic shrimp pasta hot, garnished with fresh parsley.

2. Baked Lemon Herb Salmon

Preparation time: 10 minutes | Cooking time: 20 minutes | Serves: 4

Ingredients:

  • 4 salmon fillets
  • 2 tablespoons of olive oil
  • Juice of 1 lemon
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon of dried dill
  • 1 teaspoon of dried thyme
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Lemon slices for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 400°F (200°C). Line a baking dish with parchment paper.
  2. Place the salmon fillets in the prepared baking dish.
  3. In a small bowl, whisk together olive oil, lemon juice, minced garlic, dried dill, dried thyme, salt, and pepper.
  4. Pour the marinade over the salmon fillets, making sure they are well coated.
  5. Bake the salmon in the preheated oven for 15-20 minutes, or until it flakes easily with a fork.
  6. Garnish with lemon slices and serve hot.

3. Spicy Cajun Shrimp Tacos

Preparation time: 15 minutes | Cooking time: 10 minutes | Serves: 4

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound of shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 1 tablespoon of Cajun seasoning
  • 1 tablespoon of olive oil
  • 8 small flour tortillas
  • 1 cup of shredded lettuce
  • 1/2 cup of diced tomatoes
  • 1/4 cup of diced red onion
  • 1/4 cup of mayonnaise
  • 2 tablespoons of hot sauce
  • Fresh cilantro, chopped for garnish
  • Lime wedges for serving

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, toss the shrimp with Cajun seasoning until well coated.
  2. Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat. Add the seasoned shrimp and cook for 2-3 minutes per side until they turn pink and slightly charred.
  3. In a small bowl, mix mayonnaise and hot sauce together to make a spicy mayo.
  4. Warm the flour tortillas in a dry skillet or microwave.
  5. Assemble the tacos by spreading a spoonful of spicy mayo on each tortilla. Top with shredded lettuce, diced tomatoes, diced red onion, and cooked shrimp.
  6. Garnish with fresh cilantro and serve with lime wedges for squeezing over the tacos.

Conclusion

Enjoying delicious seafood dishes doesn’t have to break the bank. These inexpensive and flavorful recipes will satisfy your seafood cravings without draining your wallet. So, gather your ingredients and give these recipes a try. Your taste buds will thank you!
