Paleo Banana Bread Muffin Recipes: 5 Delicious and Grain-Free Options

Are you a fan of banana bread but trying to follow a paleo diet? Look no further! In this article, we will share with you five delicious and grain-free paleo banana bread muffin recipes that are sure to satisfy your cravings. These recipes are not only healthy and nutritious but also easy to make. So, let’s jump right into it! 1. Classic Paleo Banana Bread Muffins Ingredients: 3 ripe bananas, mashed 3 eggs 1/4 cup almond butter 1/4 cup coconut oil, melted 1 teaspoon vanilla extract 1/2 cup coconut flour 1/2 teaspoon baking soda 1/4 teaspoon salt Optional: chocolate chips or chopped nuts for topping Instructions: Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C) and line a muffin tin with paper liners. In a large mixing bowl, combine the mashed bananas, eggs, almond butter, coconut oil, and vanilla extract. Mix well until smooth. Add the coconut flour, baking soda, and salt to the bowl. Stir until all the ingredients are well combined. Divide the batter evenly among the muffin cups, filling them about two-thirds full. If desired, sprinkle chocolate chips or chopped nuts on top of each muffin. Bake in the preheated oven for 20-25 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center of a muffin comes out clean. Remove from the oven and let the muffins cool in the tin for a few minutes before transferring them to a wire rack to cool completely. 2. Chocolate Chip Paleo Banana Bread Muffins Ingredients: 3 ripe bananas, mashed 3 eggs 1/4 cup almond butter 1/4 cup coconut oil, melted 1 teaspoon vanilla extract 1/2 cup coconut flour 1/4 cup cacao powder 1/2 teaspoon baking soda 1/4 teaspoon salt 1/2 cup dairy-free chocolate chips Instructions: Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C) and line a muffin tin with paper liners. In a large mixing bowl, combine the mashed bananas, eggs, almond butter, coconut oil, and vanilla extract. Mix well until smooth. Add the coconut flour, cacao powder, baking soda, and salt to the bowl. Stir until all the ingredients are well combined. Fold in the dairy-free chocolate chips. Divide the batter evenly among the muffin cups, filling them about two-thirds full. Bake in the preheated oven for 20-25 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center of a muffin comes out clean. Remove from the oven and let the muffins cool in the tin for a few minutes before transferring them to a wire rack to cool completely. 3.
