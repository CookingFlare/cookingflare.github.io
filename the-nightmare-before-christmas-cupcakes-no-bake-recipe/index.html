The Nightmare Before Christmas Cupcakes: No Bake Recipe


Embrace the enchanting world of Tim Burton’s “The Nightmare Before Christmas” this Halloween by baking these scrumptious no-bake cupcakes that will entice everyone with their spooky yet delightful flavors. Let’s create a perfect blend of pumpkin pie, chocolate, and gingerbread for a special twist on traditional seasonal treats.

Ingredients

Pumpkin Pie Filling:

  • 1 1/2 cups heavy cream
  • 14 oz sweetened condensed milk
  • 1 (15 oz) can of pumpkin puree (NOT pumpkin pie filling)
  • 1 tsp ground cinnamon
  • 1/2 tsp ground ginger
  • 1/4 tsp ground cloves
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 4 oz cream cheese, softened
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract

Chocolate Cupcakes:

  • 8 oz high-quality white chocolate, chopped
  • 1/4 cup vegetable shortening
  • Food coloring (green and black)
  • 20 oz dark chocolate, chopped
  • Cupcake liners

Gingerbread Frosting:

  • 1 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 3 cups powdered sugar
  • 1/4 cup molasses
  • 1/2 tsp ground cinnamon
  • 1/2 tsp ground ginger
  • 1/4 tsp ground cloves
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 2 tsp vanilla extract
  • Orange food coloring

Instructions

Let’s break down the process into logical steps to create these delightful treats:

Pumpkin Pie Filling:

  1. In a large mixing bowl, gently whisk together the sweetened condensed milk, pumpkin puree, cinnamon, ginger, cloves, and salt. Set aside.
  2. In a separate bowl, whip the heavy cream and cream cheese until soft peaks form.
  3. Gently fold the whipped cream mixture into the pumpkin mixture until fully combined. Stir in the vanilla extract.
  4. Cover the bowl and refrigerate for at least 1 hour to let the flavors meld together.

Chocolate Cupcakes:

  1. Place the white chocolate in a heatproof bowl set over a saucepan of simmering water, and stir until melted and smooth.
  2. Remove the bowl from the heat, and stir in the vegetable shortening. Mix until smooth. Add in a few drops of black food coloring to create a dark gray color. Set aside to cool.
  3. Melt the dark chocolate in a separate heatproof bowl over a saucepan of simmering water. Remove from heat and gently mix in the cooled white chocolate mixture until smooth.
  4. Line a baking sheet with parchment paper, and use a spoon or small cookie scoop to form small chocolate cupcakes, spacing them at least 1 inch apart. Place the baking sheet in the freezer for 20 minutes, or until firm.

Gingerbread Frosting:

  1. In a large mixing bowl, beat the butter until creamy and light. Gradually add the powdered sugar, alternating with the molasses. Mix in the cinnamon, ginger, cloves, and salt.
  2. Whip in the vanilla extract and orange food coloring until the frosting is light and fluffy. Set aside.

Assembling the Cupcakes:

  1. Take the chocolate cupcakes out of the freezer, and place one into the bottom of each cupcake liner.
  2. Fill each cupcake with a generous amount of pumpkin pie filling. 3.雪球 it with a dollop of the gingerbread frosting, and you’ll have a delightful Nightmare Before Christmas inspired treat.

Now that you’ve followed these steps and created these delectable treats, gather your friends and family around to celebrate Halloween and the enchanting world of “The Nightmare Before Christmas.” Enjoy baking and savoring these spooky cupcakes, and don’t forget to share the magic with your loved ones!
