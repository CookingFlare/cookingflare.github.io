Greek Lemon Chicken: A Tangy Twist on a Classic Dish


When it comes to Mediterranean cuisine, Greek food holds a special place with its vibrant flavors and fresh ingredients. One dish that truly captures the essence of Greek cooking is Greek Lemon Chicken. This tangy, aromatic dish is a perfect balance of savory and citrusy flavors, making it a delightful addition to any meal. In this recipe, we will explore how to create this classic dish with a unique twist that will leave your taste buds singing.

Ingredients:

  • 4 bone-in, skin-on chicken thighs
  • 4 bone-in, skin-on chicken drumsticks
  • 1/4 cup extra virgin olive oil
  • 1/3 cup fresh lemon juice
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1/2 cup chicken broth
  • 1 lemon, thinly sliced
  • Fresh parsley for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Marinate the Chicken: In a large bowl, whisk together the olive oil, lemon juice, minced garlic, dried oregano, dried thyme, paprika, salt, and black pepper. Place the chicken thighs and drumsticks in the bowl, making sure to coat them evenly with the marinade. Cover the bowl and refrigerate for at least 2 hours, allowing the flavors to infuse into the chicken.

  2. Preheat the Oven: Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C).

  3. Sear the Chicken: Heat a large oven-safe skillet over medium-high heat. Once the skillet is hot, add the chicken thighs and drumsticks, skin side down. Sear the chicken for 3-4 minutes on each side until they develop a golden-brown crust.

  4. Add the Broth and Lemon Slices: Pour the chicken broth into the skillet, then add the thinly sliced lemon on top of the chicken pieces.

  5. Bake: Transfer the skillet to the preheated oven and bake for 35-40 minutes, or until the chicken is cooked through and the skin is crispy.

  6. Serve: Once the chicken is done, remove the skillet from the oven and let it rest for a few minutes. Garnish with fresh parsley and serve the Greek Lemon Chicken hot, accompanied by your favorite sides.

The Tangy Twist:

To add a unique twist to this classic recipe, consider incorporating a touch of honey into the marinade. The addition of honey introduces a subtle sweetness that beautifully complements the tangy lemon and savory herbs, elevating the flavor profile of the dish to a whole new level.

Serving Suggestions:

Greek Lemon Chicken pairs wonderfully with a variety of sides, such as fluffy couscous, roasted vegetables, or a crisp Greek salad. For a complete Mediterranean feast, consider serving it alongside some warm pita bread and a dollop of tzatziki sauce.

Final Thoughts:

With its zesty, citrus-infused flavor and succulent, tender meat, Greek Lemon Chicken is a dish that never fails to impress. Whether you’re hosting a gathering or simply craving a taste of the Mediterranean, this recipe is sure to become a staple in your culinary repertoire. Embrace the tangy twist on this classic dish and savor the vibrant flavors of Greece in every bite.

So, roll up your sleeves, gather your ingredients, and let your kitchen be filled with the irresistible aromas of Greek Lemon Chicken. It’s time to embark on a culinary journey to the sun-drenched shores of Greece, one delectable bite at a time. Opa!
