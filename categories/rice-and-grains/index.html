Mouthwatering Lemon and Herb Quinoa Salad

In a medium-sized saucepan, bring the water to a boil. Add the quinoa and reduce the heat to low. Cover and simmer for 15 minutes, or until the quinoa is tender and the water has been absorbed. While the quinoa is cooking, prepare the dressing. In a small bowl, whisk together the lemon zest, lemon juice, olive oil, parsley, mint, dill, salt, and pepper. Set aside. Once the quinoa is cooked, remove it from the heat and let it cool for a few minutes. In a large mixing bowl, combine the cooked quinoa, cherry tomatoes, cucumber, and red onion. Pour the dressing over the quinoa mixture and toss gently to coat everything evenly. Taste and adjust the seasoning if needed. Cover the bowl with plastic wrap and refrigerate for at least 30 minutes to allow the flavors to meld together. Before serving, give the salad a good stir to ensure that all the ingredients are well combined. Serve chilled and enjoy! Tips and Variations You can add some crumbled feta cheese or sliced avocado to the salad for extra creaminess and flavor. If you prefer a bit of crunch, you can add some toasted pine nuts or slivered almonds to the salad. Feel free to customize the herbs according to your taste. Basil, cilantro, or tarragon would also work well in this recipe. This salad makes a great side dish or a light lunch. You can also serve it as a filling for wraps or stuffed into pita bread for a quick and nutritious meal on the go.
