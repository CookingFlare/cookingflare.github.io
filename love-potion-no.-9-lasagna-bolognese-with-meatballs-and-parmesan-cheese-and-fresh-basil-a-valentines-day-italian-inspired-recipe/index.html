Love Potion No. 9 Lasagna Bolognese with Meatballs and Parmesan Cheese and Fresh Basil: A Valentine's Day Italian-Inspired Recipe


Love Potion No. 9 Lasagna Bolognese with Meatballs and Parmesan Cheese and Fresh Basil: A Valentine’s Day Italian-Inspired Recipe

Indulge in a romantic Italian-inspired dish this Valentine’s Day with our Love Potion No. 9 Lasagna Bolognese with Meatballs and Parmesan Cheese and Fresh Basil recipe. This hearty and flavorful dish is perfect for a cozy night in with your loved one. Get ready to impress with this mouthwatering recipe that combines the richness of a classic Bolognese sauce, the succulence of tender meatballs, and the freshness of aromatic basil. Let’s dive right into the recipe!

Ingredients:

  • 9 lasagna noodles
  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 pound ground pork
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 carrot, finely chopped
  • 1 celery stalk, finely chopped
  • 1/2 cup red wine
  • 2 cups crushed tomatoes
  • 1 cup beef broth
  • 1/4 cup tomato paste
  • 1 tablespoon dried oregano
  • 1 tablespoon dried basil
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Fresh basil leaves, for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).

  2. In a large pot, bring salted water to a boil. Cook the lasagna noodles according to the package instructions until al dente. Drain and set aside.

  3. In a mixing bowl, combine the ground beef, ground pork, finely chopped onion, minced garlic, finely chopped carrot, and finely chopped celery. Mix well until all the ingredients are evenly incorporated.

  4. Shape the meat mixture into bite-sized meatballs and set them aside.

  5. Heat a large skillet over medium heat and add a drizzle of olive oil. Add the meatballs and cook until browned on all sides. Remove the meatballs from the skillet and set them aside.

  6. In the same skillet, add the finely chopped onion, minced garlic, finely chopped carrot, and finely chopped celery. Cook until the vegetables are softened and lightly browned.

  7. Pour in the red wine and let it simmer for a few minutes to reduce.

  8. Add the crushed tomatoes, beef broth, tomato paste, dried oregano, dried basil, salt, and black pepper to the skillet. Stir well to combine.

  9. Bring the sauce to a simmer and let it cook for about 20 minutes, allowing the flavors to meld together.

  10. While the sauce simmers, prepare a 9x13-inch baking dish by lightly greasing it with olive oil.

  11. To assemble the lasagna, spread a thin layer of the Bolognese sauce on the bottom of the baking dish. Place three lasagna noodles on top.

  12. Arrange a layer of meatballs over the noodles and sprinkle with grated Parmesan cheese.

  13. Repeat the layers of sauce, noodles, meatballs, and Parmesan cheese until you run out of ingredients, finishing with a layer of sauce and Parmesan cheese on top.

  14. Cover the baking dish with aluminum foil and bake in the preheated oven for 30 minutes.

  15. Remove the foil and sprinkle the shredded mozzarella cheese over the top. Return to the oven and bake for an additional 15 minutes, or until the cheese is melted and golden.

  16. Once the lasagna is cooked, remove it from the oven and let it rest for a few minutes before serving.

  17. Garnish with fresh basil leaves for an added touch of freshness and aroma.

  18. Slice the lasagna into portions and serve hot, accompanied by a side salad or garlic bread.

Enjoy this Love Potion No. 9 Lasagna Bolognese with Meatballs and Parmesan Cheese and Fresh Basil recipe with your special someone this Valentine’s Day. The layers of rich flavors and comforting textures are sure to create a memorable dining experience. Buon appetito!

Note: This recipe serves 6-8 people, so feel free to adjust the quantities accordingly for a more intimate dinner for two.
