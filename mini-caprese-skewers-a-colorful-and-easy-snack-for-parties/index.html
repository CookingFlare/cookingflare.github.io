Mini Caprese Skewers: A Colorful and Easy Snack for Parties


Are you looking for a delicious and visually appealing snack to impress your party guests? Look no further than Mini Caprese Skewers! These bite-sized appetizers are not only easy to make but also bursting with flavors that will leave everyone wanting more. Whether you’re hosting a fancy dinner party or a casual get-together, these mini skewers are sure to be a hit.

Ingredients

To make these delightful Caprese skewers, you will need the following ingredients:

  • Cherry tomatoes
  • Mini mozzarella balls (also known as bocconcini)
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • Balsamic glaze
  • Extra virgin olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Start by gathering your ingredients and skewers. You can use toothpicks or small bamboo skewers for this recipe.

  2. Wash the cherry tomatoes and basil leaves thoroughly. Pat them dry with a paper towel.

  3. Take a toothpick or skewer and thread one cherry tomato onto it, followed by a basil leaf and then a mini mozzarella ball. Repeat this process until you have used up all the ingredients.

  4. Arrange the Caprese skewers on a platter or a serving tray, leaving a bit of space between each one.

  5. Drizzle the skewers with balsamic glaze and a generous amount of extra virgin olive oil. This will add a touch of sweetness and richness to the overall flavor.

  6. Season the skewers with a pinch of salt and pepper to enhance the taste. Remember to go easy on the salt, as the mozzarella balls already have a mild salty flavor.

  7. Serve the Mini Caprese Skewers immediately or refrigerate them for later. The skewers can be made ahead of time and stored in the refrigerator for up to a day. Just make sure to cover them with plastic wrap to prevent them from drying out.

Tips and Variations

  • If you want to add a bit of crunch to your skewers, you can include a small piece of crispy bacon or prosciutto between the tomato and mozzarella.

  • For a fancy presentation, you can serve the skewers in a glass or shot glass filled with a bed of arugula or mixed greens.

  • If you’re feeling adventurous, you can experiment with different types of cheese, such as feta or goat cheese, instead of mozzarella.

  • To make the balsamic glaze at home, simply simmer balsamic vinegar in a saucepan until it thickens and reduces by half. Let it cool before drizzling it over the skewers.

  • These Mini Caprese Skewers are not only great for parties but also make a fantastic appetizer for picnics or potlucks. They are portable and easy to eat, making them perfect for any occasion.

Final Thoughts

Mini Caprese Skewers are a fantastic choice for those who want to add a touch of elegance and freshness to their party spread. With just a few simple ingredients and minimal preparation, you can create a stunning and delicious appetizer that will impress your guests. The combination of juicy cherry tomatoes, creamy mozzarella, and fragrant basil leaves is a classic one that never fails to please. So, the next time you’re planning a gathering, give these Mini Caprese Skewers a try and watch as they disappear in no time!
