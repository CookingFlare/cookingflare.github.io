Wholesome Keto Blueberry Almond Flour Muffins


When it comes to keto-friendly baked goods, finding a recipe that is both nutritious and delicious can be a challenge. But fear not, because I have the perfect solution for you – Wholesome Keto Blueberry Almond Flour Muffins! These muffins are packed with wholesome ingredients and bursting with juicy blueberries, making them the ultimate guilt-free treat. And the best part? They are incredibly easy to make!

Ingredients

  • 2 cups almond flour
  • 1/4 cup coconut flour
  • 1/4 cup erythritol (or your preferred keto-friendly sweetener)
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 3 large eggs
  • 1/2 cup unsweetened almond milk
  • 1/4 cup melted coconut oil
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 cup fresh blueberries

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C). Line a muffin tin with paper liners or grease with coconut oil.

  2. In a large bowl, whisk together the almond flour, coconut flour, erythritol, baking powder, baking soda, and salt. Make sure all the dry ingredients are well combined.

  3. In a separate bowl, beat the eggs. Add in the almond milk, melted coconut oil, and vanilla extract. Mix until everything is well incorporated.

  4. Pour the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients and stir until a thick batter forms. Be careful not to overmix.

  5. Gently fold in the fresh blueberries, being careful not to crush them.

  6. Divide the batter evenly among the muffin cups, filling each one about three-fourths full.

  7. Bake in the preheated oven for 20-25 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center of a muffin comes out clean.

  8. Remove the muffins from the oven and let them cool in the tin for 5 minutes. Then transfer them to a wire rack to cool completely.

  9. Once the muffins are completely cooled, you can enjoy them right away or store them in an airtight container for up to 3 days.

Why choose Wholesome Keto Blueberry Almond Flour Muffins?

These muffins are not only delicious, but they are also packed with nutritional benefits. Almond flour is an excellent alternative to traditional flour, as it is low in carbs and high in healthy fats and fiber. It provides a rich, nutty flavor and a moist texture to your baked goods.

Blueberries, on the other hand, are not only sweet and juicy but also loaded with antioxidants and vitamins. They add a burst of freshness and a pop of color to these muffins.

By using keto-friendly sweeteners like erythritol, you can satisfy your sweet tooth without compromising your diet. Erythritol is a natural sugar alcohol that has zero calories and does not raise blood sugar levels, making it a perfect choice for those following a keto lifestyle.

These Wholesome Keto Blueberry Almond Flour Muffins are a great option for breakfast on the go, a midday snack, or even a dessert. They are versatile, easy to make, and will surely satisfy your cravings without derailing your keto goals.

So, what are you waiting for? Give this recipe a try and indulge in some guilt-free deliciousness!
