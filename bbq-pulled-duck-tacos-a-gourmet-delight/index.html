BBQ Pulled Duck Tacos: A Gourmet Delight


Have you ever craved a flavor sensation that exceeds ordinary pulled pork? Look no further than BBQ pulled duck tacos! This unique spin on a classic street food guarantees to delight your taste buds and impress your guests. Featuring tender duck meat, homemade barbecue sauce, and an array of fresh toppings, this feasting experience is the epitome of culinary excellence.

To bring this gourmet delight to life, you’ll need the following ingredients for approximately six servings:

For the Pulled Duck Meat:

  • 1 duck (4-5 pounds)
  • Salt and freshly ground black pepper
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • 1 large onion, chopped
  • 1-2 cloves garlic, chopped
  • 2 cups barbecue sauce
  • 1/2 cup chicken broth

For the Toppings:

  • 1/2 cup red cabbage, shredded
  • 1/2 cup carrots, shredded
  • 1 avocado, sliced
  • 1/2 cup fresh cilantro leaves, chopped
  • 1/4 cup lime juice
  • 1 jalapeno, thinly sliced (optional)

For the DVD Taco Assembly:

  • 12 small corn tortillas
  • Optional garnish: fresh lime wedges, chopped onion, and hot sauce

Bringing the BBQ Pulled Duck Tacos to Life

Preparing the Pulled Duck Meat

  1. Start by seasoning the duck generously with salt and black pepper. Set aside for about 30 minutes to allow the flavors to permeate the meat.

  2. In a large Dutch oven or heavy-bottomed pot, heat the vegetable oil over medium-high heat. Once the oil is shimmering, add the seasoned duck and sear it for about 4 minutes on each side until a golden-brown crust forms.

  3. Once the duck is browned, remove it from the pot, leaving the rendered duck fat behind. Cover the duck with aluminum foil and let it rest for a few minutes to retain the moisture.

  4. In the same pot, sauté the chopped onion and garlic until fragrant. Add the barbecue sauce and chicken broth, bringing the mixture to a gentle simmer.

  5. Return the duck to the pot, cover with the flavorful sauce, and continue simmering for about 1.5 to 2 hours, always ensuring the pot remains covered and the heat is low. This will allow the duck to cook gently and fall apart at the mere touch of a fork.

Preparing the Toppings

While the duck is simmering away, prepare the colorful and refreshing toppings. Combine the shredded cabbage and carrots in one bowl, with the sliced avocado in another. Mix the cilantro, lime juice, and jalapeño (if using) for the homemade salsa and set aside.

Mastering the Perfect BBQ Pulled Duck Tacos

  1. Once the duck is cooked and reaches an internal temperature of 165°F, shred the meat using two forks. Discard the bones and return the pulled duck to the delicious sauce, allowing it to soak up the flavors even more.

  2. Warm your corn tortillas on a flat grill or stovetop, ensuring a soft and pliable texture perfect for rolling.

  3. Assemble your BBQ pulled duck tacos by placing a couple of tablespoons of pulled duck meat onto each tortilla, then adding a handful of shredded vegetables, a drizzle of the homemade cilantro-lime salsa, and finally your choice of garnish.

Witness the dazzling color and vibrant flavors of this BBQ pulled duck taco feast. Spritz with a wedge of fresh lime for zing, and fine-tune the flavors to your liking with extra hot sauce or chopped onion. Share and enjoy these smoky, succulent BBQ pulled duck tacos with friends and family on a sunny day - this unforgettable culinary experience is better shared with loved ones!

So, the next time you crave an extraordinary barbecue experience, bust out the slow cooker and prepare yourself for succulent pulled duck tacos that redefine the concept of BBQ perfection. From your kitchen to your plate, savor each delightful bite of this gourmet delight.
