Rich and Creamy Mac and Cheese for Cold Days


When the chilly weather sets in, there’s nothing quite like a comforting bowl of rich and creamy mac and cheese to warm you up from the inside out. This classic comfort food is a go-to favorite for many, and it’s no wonder why – the combination of gooey cheese, tender pasta, and a hint of warmth from the oven is simply irresistible. Whether you’re looking for a quick weeknight meal or a dish to impress at a dinner party, this mac and cheese recipe is sure to hit the spot.

Ingredients

Before we dive into the cooking process, let’s gather the ingredients for this delectable mac and cheese:

  • 16 oz (450g) elbow macaroni
  • 1/2 cup (115g) unsalted butter
  • 1/2 cup (60g) all-purpose flour
  • 4 cups (950ml) whole milk
  • 2 cups (200g) sharp cheddar cheese, shredded
  • 1 cup (100g) Gruyère cheese, shredded
  • 1 cup (100g) Parmesan cheese, grated
  • 1/2 teaspoon Dijon mustard
  • 1/4 teaspoon nutmeg
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 cup (100g) panko breadcrumbs
  • 2 tablespoons (30g) unsalted butter, melted

Instructions

1. Prepare the Macaroni

Start by preheating your oven to 375°F (190°C). In a large pot of salted boiling water, cook the macaroni according to the package instructions until al dente. Once cooked, drain the macaroni and set it aside.

2. Make the Cheese Sauce

In a separate large saucepan, melt the 1/2 cup of butter over medium heat. Once melted, whisk in the flour to create a roux. Cook the roux, stirring constantly, for about 2 minutes until it turns a light golden color. Slowly pour in the milk while whisking continuously to avoid lumps. Continue to cook the sauce, stirring frequently, until it thickens and coats the back of a spoon.

Next, reduce the heat to low and add the cheddar, Gruyère, and Parmesan cheeses to the sauce, stirring until they melt and the sauce becomes smooth. Stir in the Dijon mustard, nutmeg, salt, and pepper, adjusting the seasoning to your taste.

3. Combine and Bake

Add the cooked macaroni to the cheese sauce, stirring until the pasta is evenly coated. Transfer the creamy mac and cheese mixture to a greased 9x13-inch baking dish.

In a small bowl, combine the panko breadcrumbs with the melted 2 tablespoons of butter, then sprinkle the mixture evenly over the mac and cheese.

4. Bake to Perfection

Place the baking dish in the preheated oven and bake for 25-30 minutes, or until the mac and cheese is bubbly and the breadcrumbs are golden brown.

5. Serve and Enjoy

Once out of the oven, allow the mac and cheese to cool for a few minutes before serving. This rich and creamy mac and cheese is best enjoyed hot, straight from the oven. The combination of three cheeses, a hint of Dijon mustard, and a touch of nutmeg creates a flavor profile that’s both comforting and sophisticated.

Conclusion

So there you have it – a decadent, rich and creamy mac and cheese recipe that’s perfect for those chilly days when all you crave is a comforting and indulgent meal. Whether you’re cuddled up on the couch with a movie or entertaining friends and family, this mac and cheese is sure to be a hit. So, go ahead, gather the ingredients, follow the simple steps, and treat yourself to a bowl of pure comfort.
