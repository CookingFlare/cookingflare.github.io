Chocolate Mint Whoopie Pies: A Fun and Festive Dessert


If you’re looking for a delightful treat that’s perfect for any occasion, look no further than these delectable Chocolate Mint Whoopie Pies. With a rich chocolate flavor and a refreshing hint of mint, these whoopie pies are sure to be a hit at your next gathering. Whether you’re hosting a party, attending a potluck, or simply craving a sweet indulgence, these delightful desserts are a must-try.

Ingredients

For the Chocolate Mint Cookies:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 large egg
  • 1 teaspoon pure vanilla extract
  • 1 teaspoon peppermint extract
  • 1/2 cup buttermilk

For the Mint Buttercream Filling:

  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 2 cups confectioners’ sugar
  • 1-2 tablespoons milk
  • 1/2 teaspoon peppermint extract
  • Green food coloring (optional)

Instructions

Chocolate Mint Cookies:

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C) and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
  2. In a medium bowl, whisk together the flour, cocoa powder, baking powder, baking soda, and salt.
  3. In a separate large bowl, cream together the butter and sugar until light and fluffy. Add the egg, vanilla extract, and peppermint extract, and beat until well combined.
  4. Gradually add the dry ingredients to the wet ingredients, alternating with the buttermilk, and mix until the batter is smooth.
  5. Using a spoon or cookie scoop, drop rounded tablespoons of batter onto the prepared baking sheet, spacing them about 2 inches apart.
  6. Bake for 10-12 minutes, or until the cookies are set. Allow them to cool on the baking sheet for a few minutes before transferring them to a wire rack to cool completely.

Mint Buttercream Filling:

  1. In a large bowl, beat the butter until creamy. Gradually add the confectioners’ sugar and beat until smooth and fluffy.
  2. Add the milk, peppermint extract, and a few drops of green food coloring (if using) and beat until the filling is light and creamy.

Assembling the Whoopie Pies:

  1. Once the cookies are completely cooled, spread a generous amount of the mint buttercream filling onto the flat side of half of the cookies.
  2. Top with the remaining cookies to create delicious Chocolate Mint Whoopie Pies.

Serving and Storage

These Chocolate Mint Whoopie Pies are best enjoyed fresh, but they can be stored in an airtight container at room temperature for up to 3 days. If you’re making them ahead of time, consider assembling the whoopie pies just before serving to ensure the cookies stay soft and the filling stays fresh.

Final Thoughts

Whether you’re a fan of mint chocolate treats or simply looking for a fun and festive dessert to impress your guests, these Chocolate Mint Whoopie Pies are a wonderful option. The combination of rich chocolate cookies and creamy mint filling is a match made in dessert heaven. So, next time you’re in the mood for a sweet, indulgent treat, give these whoopie pies a try and watch them disappear in no time!
