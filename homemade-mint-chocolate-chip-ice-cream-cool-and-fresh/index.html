Homemade Mint Chocolate Chip Ice Cream: Cool and Fresh


If there’s one dessert that can instantly transport you to a state of pure bliss, it’s homemade mint chocolate chip ice cream. The refreshing taste of mint combined with the indulgent crunch of chocolate chips creates a symphony of flavors that is simply irresistible. In this recipe, I’ll guide you through the process of creating your own batch of this cool and fresh delight right in the comfort of your kitchen.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups heavy cream
  • 1 cup whole milk
  • 3/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 teaspoon pure peppermint extract
  • A handful of fresh mint leaves
  • 1 cup semi-sweet chocolate chips
  • Green food coloring (optional)

Instructions

  1. Infuse the Cream: In a saucepan, combine the heavy cream, whole milk, and fresh mint leaves. Gently heat the mixture over low heat until it’s just about to simmer. Turn off the heat and let the mixture steep for 20-30 minutes. This will infuse the cream with the delightful essence of fresh mint.

  2. Prepare the Base: After the cream mixture has steeped, strain out the mint leaves and return the infused liquid to the saucepan. Add the granulated sugar and heat the mixture over low heat, stirring until the sugar is completely dissolved. Remove the pan from the heat and stir in the peppermint extract. If you desire a more vibrant green color, add a few drops of green food coloring at this stage.

  3. Chill the Mixture: Transfer the mint-infused cream base to a bowl and cover it with plastic wrap, ensuring that the wrap touches the surface of the mixture to prevent a skin from forming. Refrigerate the mixture for at least 4 hours, or preferably overnight, to allow the flavors to meld and intensify.

  4. Churn the Ice Cream: Once the mixture is thoroughly chilled, pour it into your ice cream maker and churn according to the manufacturer’s instructions. In the final minutes of churning, add in the semi-sweet chocolate chips, allowing them to distribute evenly throughout the ice cream.

  5. Freeze and Enjoy: Transfer the freshly churned ice cream to a freezer-safe container, pressing a piece of parchment paper directly onto the surface of the ice cream before sealing the container with a lid. Freeze for at least 4 hours, or until the ice cream is firm.

Serving Suggestions

Scoop generous servings of the homemade mint chocolate chip ice cream into bowls or cones, garnishing with a sprig of fresh mint for an extra pop of color and flavor. For added indulgence, drizzle a bit of melted chocolate over the top or sprinkle with additional chocolate chips.

Final Thoughts

Homemade mint chocolate chip ice cream is a true delight for the senses. With its vibrant green hue, refreshing mint flavor, and decadent chocolate chips, it’s the perfect treat for a hot summer day or a cozy night in. Plus, the satisfaction of creating your own ice cream from scratch is simply unbeatable. So, gather your ingredients, set aside some time, and embark on a journey to create this cool and fresh dessert that will surely become a household favorite.
