Easy Rice and Beans Recipe: A Budget-Friendly and Filling Meal


Looking for a delicious and budget-friendly meal that will satisfy your hunger? Look no further than this easy rice and beans recipe! Packed with flavor and nutrients, this dish is not only filling but also easy on your wallet. Whether you’re a student, a busy professional, or just looking to save some money, this recipe is a must-try.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of rice
  • 2 cups of water
  • 1 can of black beans (drained and rinsed)
  • 1 small onion (diced)
  • 2 cloves of garlic (minced)
  • 1 bell pepper (chopped)
  • 1 tablespoon of olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon of cumin
  • 1 teaspoon of paprika
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Start by cooking the rice. In a medium-sized saucepan, combine the rice and water. Bring to a boil, then reduce the heat to low and cover. Let the rice simmer for about 15-20 minutes or until all the water is absorbed and the rice is tender.

  2. While the rice is cooking, heat the olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Add the diced onion, minced garlic, and chopped bell pepper. Sauté until the vegetables are tender and fragrant, about 5 minutes.

  3. Once the vegetables are cooked, add the drained and rinsed black beans to the skillet. Stir in the cumin, paprika, salt, and pepper. Cook for an additional 5 minutes, allowing the flavors to meld together.

  4. Once the rice is cooked, fluff it with a fork and add it to the skillet with the beans and vegetables. Gently mix everything together until well combined.

  5. Cover the skillet and let the rice and beans simmer for another 5 minutes, allowing the flavors to further develop.

  6. Remove from heat and let the dish cool slightly before serving. You can garnish with fresh cilantro, a squeeze of lime juice, or even some shredded cheese if desired.

Serving Suggestions

This easy rice and beans recipe can be enjoyed on its own as a satisfying vegetarian meal. However, it also makes a great side dish to accompany grilled chicken, fish, or roasted vegetables. You can even use it as a filling for burritos or tacos, adding some salsa, guacamole, and sour cream for a complete and delicious meal.

Benefits of Rice and Beans

Not only is this dish delicious and budget-friendly, but it also offers numerous health benefits. Rice is a great source of carbohydrates, providing energy to fuel your day. Beans, on the other hand, are packed with protein, fiber, and essential vitamins and minerals. Together, rice and beans create a complete protein, making this recipe a nutritious option for vegetarians and vegans.

Conclusion

In conclusion, this easy rice and beans recipe is a fantastic option for those looking for a budget-friendly and filling meal. With simple ingredients and straightforward instructions, you can whip up this dish in no time. Enjoy it as a standalone meal or as a versatile side dish. Not only will it satisfy your hunger, but it will also nourish your body with essential nutrients. Give it a try and see for yourself how delicious and satisfying a budget-friendly meal can be!
