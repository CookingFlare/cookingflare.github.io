Vegan Apple Pie Recipe with a Flaky Crust and a Whipped Coconut Cream


There’s nothing quite like the aroma of a freshly baked pie wafting through the house. And when it’s a vegan apple pie with a flaky crust and a dollop of whipped coconut cream, the experience is simply divine. Whether you’re a seasoned baker or a newbie in the kitchen, this recipe is a must-try for anyone looking to indulge in a delicious, plant-based dessert.

Ingredients

For the Crust:

  • 2 ½ cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup vegan butter, chilled and cubed
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 6-8 tablespoons ice water

For the Filling:

  • 6-7 medium-sized apples, peeled, cored, and thinly sliced
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/4 cup brown sugar
  • 2 tablespoons lemon juice
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 teaspoon nutmeg
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 3 tablespoons cornstarch

For the Whipped Coconut Cream:

  • 1 can (14 oz) coconut cream, chilled overnight
  • 1-2 tablespoons powdered sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Instructions

Making the Crust:

  1. In a large bowl, combine the flour and salt. Add the chilled vegan butter and use a pastry cutter or your fingers to work the butter into the flour until it resembles coarse crumbs.
  2. Gradually add the ice water, one tablespoon at a time, and mix until the dough comes together.
  3. Divide the dough into two equal parts, shape each into a disc, wrap them in plastic wrap, and refrigerate for at least 1 hour.

Preparing the Filling:

  1. In a large bowl, toss the sliced apples with lemon juice to prevent browning.
  2. In a separate bowl, mix the granulated sugar, brown sugar, cinnamon, nutmeg, salt, and cornstarch.
  3. Add the sugar mixture to the apples and toss until the apples are evenly coated.

Assembling the Pie:

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. On a floured surface, roll out one disc of the chilled dough to fit a 9-inch pie dish. Transfer the rolled-out dough to the pie dish.
  3. Fill the pie crust with the prepared apple filling.
  4. Roll out the second disc of dough and place it over the filling. Trim any excess dough and crimp the edges to seal the pie.
  5. Use a sharp knife to make a few slits in the top crust to allow steam to escape during baking.
  6. Optional: Brush the top crust with a little non-dairy milk and sprinkle with sugar for a golden finish.

Baking the Pie:

  1. Place the pie on a baking sheet to catch any drips and bake for 45-50 minutes, or until the crust is golden brown and the filling is bubbling.
  2. If the edges of the pie start to brown too quickly, cover them with foil and continue baking.

Making the Whipped Coconut Cream:

  1. Open the chilled can of coconut cream and scoop the solidified cream into a mixing bowl, leaving behind any remaining liquid.
  2. Add powdered sugar and vanilla extract to the cream.
  3. Use a hand mixer or a whisk to whip the cream until it becomes light and fluffy.

Serving the Pie:

  1. Allow the pie to cool for at least 30 minutes before slicing.
  2. Serve slices of the vegan apple pie with a generous dollop of whipped coconut cream on top.
  3. Enjoy the delightful combination of sweet, spiced apples, flaky crust, and creamy coconut goodness.

Final Thoughts

This vegan apple pie with a flaky crust and whipped coconut cream is a true crowd-pleaser, perfect for any occasion, from holiday gatherings to casual weeknight desserts. The buttery, flaky crust pairs beautifully with the warm, cinnamon-spiced apple filling, and the whipped coconut cream adds a delightful creaminess that will leave everyone coming back for seconds.

So, the next time you’re craving a comforting, homemade treat, give this vegan apple pie recipe a try. Your taste buds—and your friends—will thank you!
