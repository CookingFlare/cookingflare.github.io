Delicious Vegetarian Sandwiches for Meatless Mondays


When it comes to creating a satisfying, flavorful meal that doesn’t involve meat, sandwiches are a fantastic option. Whether you’re looking to switch things up for Meatless Monday or simply want to embrace more plant-based meals, vegetarian sandwiches offer a wealth of variety and taste. From classic flavors to inventive new combinations, these meatless options can satisfy even the most die-hard meat lovers.

In this post, we’ll explore a range of vegetarian sandwich recipes that are not only delicious but also packed with nutrients. These easy-to-make sandwiches will ensure your Meatless Monday meals are anything but boring. Get ready to discover your new favorite meat-free sandwich!

1. Classic Veggie and Hummus Sandwich

Ingredients:

  • 2 slices whole grain bread
  • 1/2 cup hummus (store-bought or homemade)
  • 1/2 cucumber, thinly sliced
  • 1/2 avocado, sliced
  • 1/4 red onion, thinly sliced
  • A handful of fresh spinach leaves
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Spread a generous layer of hummus on both slices of bread.
  2. Layer the cucumber, avocado, red onion, and spinach on one slice.
  3. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  4. Top with the second slice of bread and press gently to hold everything together.
  5. Slice in half and enjoy!

This sandwich is perfect for those who enjoy the creamy texture of hummus combined with the freshness of vegetables. The cucumber and avocado offer a satisfying crunch, while the spinach adds a burst of nutrients. This veggie-packed sandwich is a great option for a quick lunch or light dinner.

2. Grilled Vegetable and Pesto Sandwich

Ingredients:

  • 2 slices sourdough or baguette bread
  • 1/2 cup basil pesto (store-bought or homemade)
  • 1/2 eggplant, sliced into rounds
  • 1/2 zucchini, sliced into rounds
  • 1/2 red bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your grill or stovetop grill pan.
  2. Brush the eggplant, zucchini, and bell pepper slices with olive oil, and season with salt and pepper.
  3. Grill the vegetables for about 3-4 minutes per side, or until they are tender and have grill marks.
  4. Spread pesto on both slices of bread.
  5. Layer the grilled vegetables on one slice of bread.
  6. Top with the second slice of bread and press lightly.
  7. Slice and serve.

Grilled vegetables add a smoky depth of flavor to this sandwich, while the pesto brings a fragrant, herby kick. This is an excellent choice for a more substantial, savory sandwich that is still completely plant-based.

3. Avocado and Chickpea Salad Sandwich

Ingredients:

  • 2 slices whole wheat bread
  • 1/2 ripe avocado, mashed
  • 1/2 cup canned chickpeas, drained and mashed
  • 1 tablespoon tahini
  • 1 teaspoon lemon juice
  • 1 tablespoon fresh parsley, chopped
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • A handful of lettuce or arugula

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, mash together the avocado, chickpeas, tahini, lemon juice, parsley, salt, and pepper until well combined.
  2. Spread the chickpea and avocado mixture onto one slice of bread.
  3. Top with a handful of lettuce or arugula.
  4. Place the second slice of bread on top and press gently.
  5. Slice and enjoy!

The creamy texture of avocado pairs perfectly with the heartiness of chickpeas, creating a filling and flavorful sandwich. The tahini adds a nutty richness, and the fresh parsley gives it a vibrant kick. This sandwich is great for a light yet satisfying meal.

4. Roasted Sweet Potato and Black Bean Sandwich

Ingredients:

  • 2 slices whole grain or multigrain bread
  • 1 small sweet potato, peeled and cubed
  • 1/2 cup canned black beans, drained and rinsed
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • 1 teaspoon smoked paprika
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 tablespoon fresh cilantro, chopped
  • A handful of lettuce or greens

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C).
  2. Toss the sweet potato cubes in olive oil, cumin, smoked paprika, salt, and pepper. Spread them in a single layer on a baking sheet.
  3. Roast the sweet potatoes for 25-30 minutes, or until tender and golden, stirring halfway through.
  4. While the sweet potatoes are roasting, heat the black beans in a small saucepan over medium heat until warmed through.
  5. Once the sweet potatoes are done, assemble the sandwich by layering the roasted sweet potatoes, black beans, and cilantro on one slice of bread.
  6. Top with a handful of lettuce or greens.
  7. Place the second slice of bread on top and press gently.
  8. Slice and serve.

This sandwich is a hearty, satisfying meal that combines the natural sweetness of roasted sweet potatoes with the rich flavor of black beans. The cumin and smoked paprika provide a warm, earthy flavor profile, making it a perfect choice for a filling meatless meal.

5. Caprese Sandwich with Balsamic Glaze

Ingredients:

  • 2 slices ciabatta or baguette bread
  • 1 ripe tomato, sliced
  • 1/4 cup fresh mozzarella, sliced
  • 1 tablespoon fresh basil leaves
  • 1 tablespoon balsamic glaze
  • Olive oil for drizzling
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Arrange the tomato slices, mozzarella, and fresh basil on one slice of bread.
  2. Drizzle with a little olive oil and balsamic glaze.
  3. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  4. Place the second slice of bread on top and press gently.
  5. Slice and serve.

This classic Italian-inspired sandwich offers a perfect balance of flavors. The fresh mozzarella pairs beautifully with the juicy tomatoes, and the balsamic glaze adds a touch of sweetness and acidity. It’s an incredibly simple yet delicious sandwich that’s perfect for summer or any time of year.

6. Mediterranean Falafel Pita Sandwich

Ingredients:

  • 1 pita bread
  • 4-5 falafel balls (store-bought or homemade)
  • 1/4 cup tzatziki sauce
  • 1/4 cucumber, sliced
  • 1/4 red onion, thinly sliced
  • A handful of mixed greens (arugula, spinach, etc.)
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Heat the falafel balls in the oven according to package instructions, or fry them if homemade.
  2. Cut the pita bread in half to form pockets.
  3. Stuff each pita pocket with the warm falafel balls.
  4. Add the cucumber, red onion, and mixed greens.
  5. Drizzle with tzatziki sauce and season with salt and pepper.
  6. Serve immediately.

Falafel is a fantastic plant-based protein, and when paired with tzatziki sauce, it creates a deliciously refreshing sandwich. The cucumber and red onion add crunch, while the greens bring freshness. This Mediterranean-inspired sandwich is flavorful and satisfying.

7. Grilled Cheese with Tomato and Pesto

Ingredients:

  • 2 slices sourdough bread
  • 2 tablespoons pesto (store-bought or homemade)
  • 2 slices mozzarella or cheddar cheese
  • 2-3 slices tomato
  • Butter for grilling

Instructions:

  1. Butter one side of each slice of bread.
  2. On the unbuttered side, spread pesto on one slice of bread.
  3. Layer the cheese and tomato slices on top of the pesto.
  4. Place the second slice of bread, buttered side facing out, on top.
  5. Heat a skillet over medium heat. Grill the sandwich for 3-4 minutes on each side, until golden brown and the cheese has melted.
  6. Slice and serve.

Grilled cheese is a comfort food staple, and adding pesto and fresh tomato elevates it to new heights. The pesto brings an herby, savory punch, while the tomatoes add juiciness and freshness. This is a perfect vegetarian take on the classic grilled cheese sandwich.

8. Tofu Banh Mi Sandwich

Ingredients:

  • 2 slices baguette or crusty bread
  • 1/2 block firm tofu, pressed and sliced
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 1 teaspoon sesame oil
  • 1/4 cucumber, thinly sliced
  • 1/4 carrot, julienned
  • Fresh cilantro leaves
  • 1-2 slices jalapeño (optional)
  • 1 tablespoon vegan mayonnaise

Instructions:

  1. In a small bowl, marinate the tofu slices with soy sauce and sesame oil for at least 10 minutes.
  2. Heat a non-stick pan over medium heat and cook the tofu slices until golden brown on both sides.
  3. Spread vegan mayonnaise on one side of the bread.
  4. Layer the tofu, cucumber, carrot, jalapeño, and cilantro in the baguette.
  5. Close the sandwich, slice, and enjoy!

This vegetarian take on the classic Vietnamese Banh Mi is packed with flavor. The tofu, marinated in soy sauce and sesame oil, takes center stage, while the pickled vegetables and fresh cilantro bring brightness and crunch. The addition of spicy jalapeños adds a bit of heat, perfect for those who like a little kick.

Conclusion

Whether you’re new to Meatless Monday or looking for fresh ideas to diversify your vegetarian meals, these sandwiches offer a variety of flavors and textures that will keep you coming back for more. From creamy avocado and hummus to spicy tofu and grilled vegetables, there’s a sandwich here for every taste. Not only are these vegetarian sandwiches delicious and nutritious, but they’re also easy to prepare and perfect for any occasion. So, grab your bread, fill it with fresh ingredients, and enjoy a satisfying meatless meal that’s full of flavor!
