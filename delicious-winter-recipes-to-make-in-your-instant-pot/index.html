Delicious Winter Recipes to Make in Your Instant Pot


As winter settles in, the allure of warm, hearty meals grows irresistible. The Instant Pot has become a beloved kitchen companion, allowing home cooks to whip up delicious winter recipes with minimal effort and maximum flavor. Whether you’re seeking comfort food or healthy options to keep you energized during the chilly months, this list of winter recipes for your Instant Pot will keep your taste buds satisfied and your kitchen warm.

1. Instant Pot Beef Stew

Nothing screams winter like a classic beef stew. The Instant Pot makes it easy to create a tender and flavorful dish that warms the soul.

Ingredients:

  • 2 pounds beef chuck, cut into 1-inch cubes
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 4 cups beef broth
  • 4 carrots, sliced
  • 3 potatoes, cubed
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • 1 bay leaf
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Sauté the Beef: Turn the Instant Pot to the sauté mode. Add olive oil and sear the beef cubes until browned on all sides. Remove and set aside.
  2. Sauté the Vegetables: In the same pot, add onions and garlic. Sauté until fragrant.
  3. Combine Ingredients: Return the beef to the pot and add carrots, potatoes, thyme, bay leaf, salt, and pepper. Pour in beef broth.
  4. Pressure Cook: Close the lid and set the Instant Pot to pressure cook on high for 35 minutes.
  5. Release Pressure: Once done, allow for a natural release for 10 minutes, then quick release the remaining pressure.
  6. Serve: Remove the bay leaf and serve hot with crusty bread.

2. Instant Pot Chicken and Rice

This one-pot meal is not only comforting but also incredibly simple to prepare. It’s perfect for busy weeknights.

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound boneless, skinless chicken thighs
  • 1 cup long-grain rice
  • 2 cups chicken broth
  • 1 cup frozen peas and carrots
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Sauté Chicken: Set the Instant Pot to sauté and add the chicken thighs. Brown on both sides, then remove and set aside.
  2. Cook Aromatics: Add onions and garlic to the pot, sautéing until translucent.
  3. Add Rice: Stir in the rice and toast for a minute, allowing it to absorb the flavors.
  4. Combine Ingredients: Place the chicken back in the pot, add chicken broth, peas, carrots, paprika, salt, and pepper.
  5. Pressure Cook: Close the lid and set to pressure cook on high for 10 minutes.
  6. Release Pressure: Perform a quick release and fluff the rice before serving.

3. Instant Pot Lentil Soup

Packed with protein and fiber, lentil soup is a fantastic way to warm up on a cold day. The Instant Pot makes it quick and easy.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup lentils, rinsed
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 carrots, diced
  • 2 celery stalks, diced
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 6 cups vegetable broth
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • 1 teaspoon thyme
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Juice of 1 lemon

Instructions:

  1. Sauté Vegetables: On the sauté setting, add onions, carrots, and celery. Cook until softened, then stir in garlic.
  2. Add Lentils and Spices: Add lentils, vegetable broth, cumin, thyme, salt, and pepper.
  3. Pressure Cook: Close the lid and cook on high pressure for 15 minutes.
  4. Release Pressure: Allow for a natural release for 10 minutes, then quick release the remaining pressure.
  5. Finish: Stir in lemon juice before serving for a bright flavor kick.

4. Instant Pot Creamy Mushroom Risotto

This creamy risotto is a comforting dish that is easy to make in your Instant Pot, bringing a touch of luxury to your winter meals.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup Arborio rice
  • 2 cups vegetable broth
  • 1 cup mushrooms, sliced
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/2 cup white wine (optional)
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Sauté Mushrooms: On the sauté setting, melt butter and add mushrooms. Cook until they release moisture and begin to brown. Remove and set aside.
  2. Cook Aromatics: Add onion and garlic to the pot, cooking until translucent.
  3. Add Rice: Stir in Arborio rice and toast for 1-2 minutes.
  4. Add Liquids: Pour in white wine (if using) and vegetable broth. Stir to combine.
  5. Pressure Cook: Close the lid and cook on high pressure for 6 minutes.
  6. Release Pressure: Perform a quick release. Stir in cooked mushrooms and Parmesan cheese, mixing until creamy.

5. Instant Pot Chili

Chili is the ultimate winter comfort food, and the Instant Pot makes it a breeze to prepare. Customize it with your favorite beans and spices.

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound ground beef or turkey
  • 1 can kidney beans, drained
  • 1 can black beans, drained
  • 1 can diced tomatoes
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Sauté Meat: Use the sauté function to brown the ground meat until cooked through. Drain excess fat if necessary.
  2. Add Vegetables: Stir in onion and garlic, cooking until softened.
  3. Add Remaining Ingredients: Mix in beans, diced tomatoes, chili powder, cumin, salt, and pepper.
  4. Pressure Cook: Close the lid and set to pressure cook on high for 15 minutes.
  5. Release Pressure: Allow for a natural release for 10 minutes, then quick release the remaining pressure.

6. Instant Pot Mashed Potatoes

Creamy mashed potatoes are a must-have side dish during winter, and the Instant Pot makes the process quick and easy.

Ingredients:

  • 2 pounds potatoes, peeled and cubed
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1/4 cup butter
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Cook Potatoes: Add cubed potatoes to the Instant Pot and cover with water. Close the lid and cook on high pressure for 8 minutes.
  2. Release Pressure: Perform a quick release.
  3. Mash: Drain water, add milk and butter, and mash until creamy. Season with salt and pepper.

7. Instant Pot Apple Crisp

For a sweet finish to your winter meals, try making a delightful apple crisp in your Instant Pot. It’s a perfect way to use seasonal apples.

Ingredients:

  • 4 cups apples, peeled and sliced
  • 1 cup oats
  • 1/2 cup brown sugar
  • 1/4 cup flour
  • 1 teaspoon cinnamon
  • 1/4 cup melted butter

Instructions:

  1. Prepare Apples: Place sliced apples at the bottom of the Instant Pot.
  2. Mix Topping: In a bowl, combine oats, brown sugar, flour, cinnamon, and melted butter until crumbly.
  3. Combine: Sprinkle the topping over the apples.
  4. Pressure Cook: Add 1 cup of water to the pot, close the lid, and cook on high pressure for 10 minutes.
  5. Release Pressure: Allow for a natural release. Serve warm.

Conclusion

Winter is the perfect time to embrace the warmth of home-cooked meals, and the Instant Pot provides an efficient way to do so. With these delicious winter recipes, you can enjoy a variety of comforting dishes that are sure to please the whole family. From savory stews to sweet desserts, your Instant Pot will become your go-to kitchen appliance this season. So, gather your ingredients, set your Instant Pot, and enjoy the delightful flavors of winter!
