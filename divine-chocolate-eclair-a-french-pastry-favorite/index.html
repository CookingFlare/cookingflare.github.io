Divine Chocolate Eclair: A French Pastry Favorite


If you’re a fan of French pastries, then you’re in for a treat! In this blog post, we’re going to explore the exquisite world of Divine Chocolate Eclair. This delectable dessert has been a favorite among pastry enthusiasts for generations, and for good reason. With its light and airy choux pastry, filled with a luscious chocolate cream, and topped with a glossy chocolate glaze, the Divine Chocolate Eclair is a true masterpiece of French culinary art.

Ingredients

To create this divine dessert, you’ll need the following ingredients:

  • For the choux pastry:

    • 1 cup water
    • 1/2 cup unsalted butter
    • 1 cup all-purpose flour
    • 4 large eggs

  • For the chocolate cream filling:

    • 2 cups whole milk
    • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
    • 1/3 cup cornstarch
    • 4 large egg yolks
    • 6 ounces dark chocolate, chopped
    • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract

  • For the chocolate glaze:

    • 4 ounces dark chocolate, chopped
    • 1/2 cup heavy cream
    • 1 tablespoon unsalted butter

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 425°F (220°C). Line a baking sheet with parchment paper.

  2. In a saucepan, combine the water and butter over medium heat. Stir until the butter has melted and the mixture begins to boil. Remove from heat.

  3. Add the flour to the saucepan and stir vigorously until a smooth dough forms. Return the saucepan to low heat and continue stirring for about 2 minutes to cook the flour.

  4. Transfer the dough to a mixing bowl and let it cool for a few minutes. Then, add the eggs one at a time, beating well after each addition, until the dough is smooth and shiny.

  5. Spoon the dough into a piping bag fitted with a large round tip. Pipe 4-inch (10 cm) long strips onto the prepared baking sheet, leaving some space between each strip.

  6. Bake the choux pastry in the preheated oven for 15 minutes, then reduce the heat to 375°F (190°C) and bake for an additional 20 minutes, or until golden brown and puffed. Remove from the oven and let cool completely on a wire rack.

  7. While the choux pastry is cooling, prepare the chocolate cream filling. In a saucepan, heat the milk until it begins to steam. In a separate bowl, whisk together the sugar, cornstarch, and egg yolks until well combined.

  8. Slowly pour the hot milk into the egg mixture, whisking constantly. Return the mixture to the saucepan and cook over medium heat, stirring constantly, until it thickens and comes to a boil.

  9. Remove the saucepan from heat and add the chopped dark chocolate and vanilla extract. Stir until the chocolate has melted and the mixture is smooth. Transfer the chocolate cream to a bowl, cover with plastic wrap, and chill in the refrigerator for at least 2 hours.

  10. Once the choux pastry and chocolate cream are completely cooled, it’s time to assemble the eclairs. Using a sharp knife, make a small slit along the side of each pastry strip.

  11. Spoon the chocolate cream into a piping bag fitted with a small round tip. Insert the tip into the slit of each pastry strip and gently squeeze to fill the eclairs with the chocolate cream. Be careful not to overfill them.

  12. To make the chocolate glaze, place the chopped dark chocolate, heavy cream, and butter in a microwave-safe bowl. Microwave in short intervals, stirring between each interval, until the chocolate has melted and the mixture is smooth.

  13. Dip the top of each filled eclair into the chocolate glaze, allowing any excess glaze to drip off. Place the glazed eclairs on a wire rack to set.

  14. Once the glaze has set, your Divine Chocolate Eclairs are ready to be enjoyed! Serve them chilled for the perfect indulgence.

Conclusion

The Divine Chocolate Eclair is a true delight for any dessert lover. With its delicate choux pastry, rich chocolate cream filling, and glossy chocolate glaze, it’s a treat that will impress both your taste buds and your guests. So why not channel your inner French pastry chef and give this recipe a try? Indulge in the divine flavors of this classic French favorite and experience the magic of a perfectly crafted chocolate eclair. Bon appétit!
