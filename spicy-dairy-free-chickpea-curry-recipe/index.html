Spicy Dairy-Free Chickpea Curry Recipe


This Spicy Dairy-Free Chickpea Curry Recipe is a delightful and flavorful dish that will satisfy your cravings for a hearty and spicy meal. Packed with protein-rich chickpeas and aromatic spices, this curry is not only delicious but also suitable for those following a dairy-free diet. It’s a perfect option for vegans or anyone looking to incorporate more plant-based meals into their routine.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups cooked chickpeas
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 thumb-sized ginger, grated
  • 2 tomatoes, diced
  • 1 green chili, finely chopped
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 teaspoon cumin powder
  • 1 teaspoon coriander powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon red chili powder (adjust according to your spice preference)
  • 1 teaspoon garam masala
  • 1 cup coconut milk
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh cilantro, for garnish

Instructions

  1. Heat the vegetable oil in a large pan over medium heat. Add the chopped onion and sauté until it becomes translucent.

  2. Add the minced garlic, grated ginger, and green chili to the pan. Sauté for another minute, stirring constantly to prevent burning.

  3. Add the diced tomatoes to the pan and cook until they soften and release their juices. This should take about 5 minutes.

  4. In a small bowl, mix together the turmeric powder, cumin powder, coriander powder, red chili powder, and garam masala. This spice blend will add a depth of flavor to the curry.

  5. Add the spice blend to the pan and stir well to coat the onion and tomato mixture. Cook for a minute or two to allow the spices to bloom and release their aroma.

  6. Now, add the cooked chickpeas to the pan and mix well with the spice mixture. Ensure that the chickpeas are evenly coated.

  7. Pour in the coconut milk and season with salt according to your taste. Stir everything together and bring the curry to a gentle simmer.

  8. Reduce the heat to low, cover the pan, and let the curry simmer for about 15 minutes. This will allow the flavors to meld together and the chickpeas to absorb the delicious curry sauce.

  9. After 15 minutes, remove the lid and give the curry a good stir. Check the seasoning and adjust if needed.

  10. Serve the Spicy Dairy-Free Chickpea Curry hot, garnished with fresh cilantro. It pairs perfectly with steamed rice, naan bread, or even a fresh salad.

Enjoy the Spicy Dairy-Free Chickpea Curry!

This Spicy Dairy-Free Chickpea Curry Recipe is a fantastic option for those looking to enjoy a flavorsome and satisfying meal without dairy products. The combination of chickpeas, aromatic spices, and creamy coconut milk creates a delightful curry that will leave you wanting more. Whether you follow a dairy-free diet or simply enjoy exploring new flavors, this recipe is sure to become a favorite in your kitchen.

Try this recipe today and experience the delightful blend of spices and textures that this Spicy Dairy-Free Chickpea Curry has to offer. Enjoy the richness of flavors and the satisfaction of a wholesome plant-based meal.
