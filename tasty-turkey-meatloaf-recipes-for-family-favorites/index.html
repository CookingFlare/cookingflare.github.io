Tasty Turkey Meatloaf Recipes for Family Favorites


Meatloaf is a classic comfort food that brings families together around the dining table. It’s a versatile dish that can be made with various meats, but today, we’re going to focus on turkey meatloaf recipes that are both tasty and healthy. Turkey meatloaf is a great alternative to traditional beef meatloaf, as it is lower in fat and calories while still delivering a rich and satisfying flavor. So, let’s dive into some mouthwatering turkey meatloaf recipes that are sure to become family favorites!

1. Classic Turkey Meatloaf

Prep time: 15 minutes
Cook time: 1 hour
Total time: 1 hour 15 minutes
Serves: 6

Ingredients:

  • 1 ½ pounds ground turkey
  • 1 cup breadcrumbs
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 carrot, grated
  • 1 celery stalk, finely chopped
  • 2 tablespoons ketchup
  • 2 tablespoons Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 tablespoon Dijon mustard
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. In a large bowl, combine the ground turkey, breadcrumbs, onion, garlic, carrot, celery, ketchup, Worcestershire sauce, Dijon mustard, thyme, oregano, salt, and pepper. Mix well until all the ingredients are evenly incorporated.
  3. Transfer the mixture to a greased loaf pan and shape it into a loaf.
  4. Bake the meatloaf in the preheated oven for about 1 hour or until the internal temperature reaches 165°F (74°C).
  5. Remove the meatloaf from the oven and let it rest for 5-10 minutes before slicing and serving.

2. BBQ Glazed Turkey Meatloaf

Prep time: 20 minutes
Cook time: 45 minutes
Total time: 1 hour 5 minutes
Serves: 4

Ingredients:

  • 1 ½ pounds ground turkey
  • 1 cup panko breadcrumbs
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 1 red bell pepper, finely chopped
  • 1 egg, lightly beaten
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 tablespoon Dijon mustard
  • ½ cup barbecue sauce, plus extra for glazing
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. In a large bowl, combine the ground turkey, breadcrumbs, onion, red bell pepper, egg, garlic, Worcestershire sauce, Dijon mustard, barbecue sauce, salt, and pepper. Mix well until all the ingredients are evenly incorporated.
  3. Transfer the mixture to a greased loaf pan and shape it into a loaf.
  4. Bake the meatloaf in the preheated oven for 30 minutes.
  5. Remove the meatloaf from the oven and brush the top with additional barbecue sauce.
  6. Return the meatloaf to the oven and bake for an additional 15 minutes or until the internal temperature reaches 165°F (74°C).
  7. Let the meatloaf rest for 5-10 minutes before slicing and serving.

3. Italian Turkey Meatloaf

Prep time: 20 minutes
Cook time: 1 hour
Total time: 1 hour 20 minutes
Serves: 8

Ingredients:

  • 2 pounds ground turkey
  • 1 cup breadcrumbs
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • ½ cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 cup marinara sauce, divided
  • 2 tablespoons chopped fresh basil
  • 1 tablespoon chopped fresh parsley
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. In a large bowl, combine the ground turkey, breadcrumbs, onion, garlic, mozzarella cheese, Parmesan cheese, ½ cup marinara sauce, basil, parsley, oregano, thyme, salt, and pepper. Mix well until all the ingredients are evenly incorporated.
  3. Transfer the mixture to a greased loaf pan and shape it into a loaf.
  4. Spread the remaining ½ cup marinara sauce over the top of the meatloaf.
  5. Bake the meatloaf in the preheated oven for about 1 hour or until the internal temperature reaches 165°F (74°C).
  6. Remove the meatloaf from the oven and let it rest for 5-10 minutes before slicing and serving.

Conclusion

These tasty turkey meatloaf recipes are not only delicious but also a healthier alternative to traditional beef meatloaf. Whether you choose the classic version, the BBQ glazed variation, or the Italian-inspired creation, your family is sure to enjoy these flavorful and satisfying dishes. So, gather your ingredients, preheat your oven, and let the aroma of these turkey meatloaves fill your kitchen. It’s time to bring smiles to your loved ones’ faces with these family-favorite recipes!
