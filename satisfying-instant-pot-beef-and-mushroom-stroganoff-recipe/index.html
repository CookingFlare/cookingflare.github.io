Satisfying Instant Pot Beef and Mushroom Stroganoff Recipe


If you’re a fan of hearty, comforting meals that are easy to prepare, then this Instant Pot beef and mushroom stroganoff recipe is sure to become a staple in your kitchen. This classic dish is given a modern twist with the help of the Instant Pot, resulting in tender beef, flavorful mushrooms, and a rich, creamy sauce that will leave you craving for more. Let’s dive into the details of this satisfying recipe and get ready to impress your family and friends with this delightful dish.

Ingredients

  • 2 pounds beef stew meat, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 pound cremini mushrooms, sliced
  • 2 cups beef broth
  • 1 tablespoon Dijon mustard
  • 1 tablespoon Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 cup sour cream
  • 1/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley
  • 1 pound egg noodles, cooked according to package instructions

Instructions

  1. Start by setting your Instant Pot to the “Sauté” function and heat the olive oil. Add the beef stew meat in batches and brown it on all sides. Remove the beef from the pot and set it aside.

  2. In the same pot, add the chopped onion and sauté until it becomes translucent. Add the minced garlic and cook for another minute, stirring constantly.

  3. Add the sliced mushrooms to the pot and cook until they are tender and any liquid they release has evaporated.

  4. Pour in the beef broth, Dijon mustard, Worcestershire sauce, paprika, salt, and pepper. Stir well to combine all the ingredients.

  5. Return the browned beef to the pot, ensuring it is submerged in the liquid. Secure the lid and set the Instant Pot to cook on high pressure for 20 minutes. Once the cooking time is complete, allow for a natural pressure release for 10 minutes before quick releasing any remaining pressure.

  6. In a small bowl, whisk together the sour cream and flour until smooth. Stir this mixture into the beef and mushroom mixture in the Instant Pot until the sauce thickens.

  7. Serve the beef and mushroom stroganoff over the cooked egg noodles, garnished with chopped fresh parsley.

Tips and Tricks

  • For an extra depth of flavor, consider adding a splash of red wine to the beef and mushroom mixture before pressure cooking.
  • If you prefer a thicker sauce, you can increase the amount of flour used to thicken the sour cream mixture.
  • Leftovers can be stored in an airtight container in the refrigerator for up to 3 days and reheated for a quick and delicious meal.

Conclusion

The Instant Pot beef and mushroom stroganoff is a wonderful example of how a classic recipe can be adapted to modern cooking methods without compromising on flavor. The convenience of using an Instant Pot makes this dish perfect for busy weeknights or lazy weekends when you crave a satisfying meal without spending hours in the kitchen. Give this recipe a try and experience the comforting flavors of this timeless dish with a contemporary twist. Enjoy!
