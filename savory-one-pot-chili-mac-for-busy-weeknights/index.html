Savory One-Pot Chili Mac for Busy Weeknights


When you’re juggling a hectic schedule, dinner often becomes a rushed affair or a dilemma of what to cook that’s quick, satisfying, and doesn’t require a sink full of dishes afterward. That’s where this Savory One-Pot Chili Mac comes to the rescue! It’s the perfect solution for those busy weeknights when you crave comfort food without the fuss.

Ingredients You’ll Need

Gather these simple ingredients to whip up this delicious dish:

  • Ground Beef: Adds hearty flavor and protein.
  • Onion and Garlic: For aromatic depth.
  • Bell Peppers: Adds sweetness and crunch.
  • Chili Powder and Cumin: Essential spices for that classic chili flavor.
  • Crushed Tomatoes: Provides a rich, tomatoey base.
  • Beef Broth: Adds savory depth to the dish.
  • Elbow Macaroni: The star ingredient that cooks right in the sauce.
  • Kidney Beans: Optional, for added texture and protein.
  • Cheese: Optional, for a creamy finish.
  • Salt and Pepper: To season, to taste.
  • Fresh Parsley or Cilantro: For garnish and freshness.

Step-by-Step Instructions

Step 1: Brown the Beef

Start by heating a large pot or deep skillet over medium-high heat. Add the ground beef, breaking it up with a spoon or spatula as it cooks. Stir occasionally until the beef is browned and cooked through, usually about 5-7 minutes.

Step 2: Sauté Aromatics

Once the beef is browned, add chopped onion and minced garlic to the pot. Sauté for a few minutes until the onion becomes translucent and fragrant, enhancing the base flavors of your chili mac.

Step 3: Add Vegetables and Spices

Next, stir in diced bell peppers. The vibrant colors and crunch of the peppers add freshness to the dish. Sprinkle in chili powder and ground cumin, stirring well to coat the meat and vegetables evenly with the spices. This step builds the foundation of robust flavors.

Step 4: Simmer with Tomatoes and Broth

Pour in crushed tomatoes and beef broth, stirring to combine everything. Bring the mixture to a gentle boil, then reduce the heat to medium-low. Let it simmer uncovered for about 10 minutes, allowing the flavors to meld together and the sauce to slightly thicken.

Step 5: Cook the Macaroni

Add elbow macaroni directly into the pot, stirring well to ensure it’s submerged in the sauce. Cover the pot with a lid and let it simmer for an additional 10-12 minutes, or until the pasta is tender and has absorbed most of the liquid. Stir occasionally to prevent sticking.

Step 6: Finish and Serve

Once the pasta is cooked to your liking, taste and adjust seasoning with salt and pepper as needed. If using kidney beans, add them at this stage and stir until heated through. For a creamy finish, stir in shredded cheese until melted and smooth.

Step 7: Garnish and Enjoy!

Remove the pot from the heat. Sprinkle chopped fresh parsley or cilantro on top for a burst of color and freshness. Serve your savory one-pot chili mac hot in bowls, perhaps with a side of crusty bread or a simple green salad.

Tips for Success

  • Customize Your Veggies: Feel free to swap bell peppers with diced tomatoes, corn, or even spinach for added nutrition.
  • Make It Spicy: Add a pinch of red pepper flakes or diced jalapeños for an extra kick.
  • Meal Prep: This dish reheats well, making it ideal for leftovers or meal prep for the week ahead.
  • Freeze It: Freeze leftovers in airtight containers for up to 3 months. Thaw overnight in the refrigerator and reheat on the stovetop or microwave.

Final Thoughts

This Savory One-Pot Chili Mac is a lifesaver for busy nights when you crave a comforting, homemade meal without spending hours in the kitchen. It’s versatile, satisfying, and sure to become a favorite in your household. Plus, with just one pot to clean up, dinner cleanup is a breeze! So, the next time you’re looking for a hearty dish that ticks all the boxes, give this recipe a try and enjoy the deliciousness it brings to your table. Bon appétit!
