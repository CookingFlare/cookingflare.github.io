Authentic Pad Thai Recipe for Home Cooks


If you’re a fan of Thai cuisine and looking to recreate the authentic flavors of Pad Thai in your own kitchen, you’re in the right place. Pad Thai is a beloved stir-fried noodle dish that balances sweet, sour, salty, and spicy flavors harmoniously. This recipe will guide you through the steps to achieve that perfect balance and create a delicious Pad Thai that will impress your family and friends.

Ingredients:

  • 8 oz. dried flat rice noodles
  • 2 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/2 cup firm tofu, cubed
  • 8-10 large shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/4 cup tamarind paste
  • 3 tbsp fish sauce
  • 1-2 tbsp palm sugar
  • 1/2 cup bean sprouts
  • 1/4 cup crushed peanuts
  • 2 green onions, chopped
  • Lime wedges for serving

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the Noodles:

    • Soak the rice noodles in warm water for about 30 minutes until they are pliable but still firm to the touch. Drain and set aside.

  2. Make the Sauce:

    • In a small bowl, mix the tamarind paste, fish sauce, and palm sugar. Adjust the levels of sweetness and tanginess to your preference.

  3. Stir-Fry the Ingredients:

    • Heat oil in a wok or large pan over medium-high heat. Add garlic and stir until fragrant. Add tofu and shrimp, and cook until the shrimp turns pink and the tofu is lightly browned.

  4. Add Eggs:

    • Push the ingredients to one side of the pan and crack the eggs into the empty space. Scramble the eggs until they are cooked through.

  5. Combine Everything:

    • Add the soaked rice noodles to the pan, followed by the prepared sauce. Toss everything together gently to coat the noodles evenly.

  6. Add Final Touches:

    • Stir in bean sprouts and half of the chopped green onions. Cook for another minute until the bean sprouts are slightly wilted.

  7. Serve:

    • Transfer the Pad Thai to serving plates. Garnish with crushed peanuts, the remaining green onions, and lime wedges on the side for squeezing over the dish.

Now, you’re ready to enjoy your homemade Authentic Pad Thai! The combination of flavors and textures in this dish is sure to transport you to the bustling streets of Thailand. Experiment with the recipe, adjust the ingredients to your liking, and don’t be afraid to make it your own.

Cooking Pad Thai at home allows you to appreciate the art of Thai cuisine and share a taste of Thailand with your loved ones. Gather your ingredients, fire up the stove, and embark on a culinary journey that celebrates the vibrant and delicious world of Pad Thai. Happy cooking!

Remember, the key to a great Pad Thai lies in the balance of flavors and the freshness of the ingredients. Don’t be afraid to get creative and add your own twist to this classic recipe. Enjoy every bite, and let the magic of Pad Thai brighten up your dining table!

From soaking the noodles to perfecting the sauce and stir-frying the ingredients, this recipe captures the essence of traditional Thai flavors.
