One-Pot Beef and Bean Chili: Hearty and Spicy Recipes


Whether you’re hosting a cozy gathering or simply craving a comforting meal, this One-Pot Beef and Bean Chili is a sure crowd-pleaser. Packed with hearty beef, flavorful spices, and an assortment of beans, this chili is the perfect balance of meaty, spicy, and satisfying. The best part? It all comes together in just one pot, making cleanup a breeze. Let’s dive into the recipe and get ready to enjoy a bowl of warmth and deliciousness!

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 bell pepper, diced
  • 1 can (14 oz) diced tomatoes
  • 2 cans (15 oz each) kidney beans, drained and rinsed
  • 1 can (15 oz) black beans, drained and rinsed
  • 1 can (15 oz) corn kernels, drained
  • 2 cups beef broth
  • 2 tablespoons chili powder
  • 1 tablespoon cumin
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Optional toppings: shredded cheese, sour cream, chopped cilantro

Instructions:

  1. Brown the Beef: In a large pot or Dutch oven, brown the ground beef over medium heat until no longer pink. Drain any excess fat.

  2. Add Aromatics: Add the diced onion, minced garlic, and bell pepper to the pot. Cook until the vegetables are soft and aromatic.

  3. Spice it Up: Sprinkle the chili powder, cumin, paprika, salt, and pepper over the meat and vegetables. Stir well to coat everything in the spices.

  4. Simmer with Tomatoes: Pour in the diced tomatoes with their juices. Let the mixture simmer for a few minutes to allow the flavors to meld together.

  5. Beans and Corn: Add the kidney beans, black beans, and corn kernels to the pot. Stir to combine all the ingredients.

  6. Pour in the Broth: Pour the beef broth into the pot, ensuring all the ingredients are submerged. Bring the chili to a simmer.

  7. Cook to Perfection: Cover the pot with a lid and let the chili simmer for about 30-40 minutes, stirring occasionally. This allows the flavors to develop and the chili to thicken.

  8. Serve and Enjoy: Once the chili has reached your desired consistency, taste and adjust the seasoning as needed. Serve hot, garnished with your favorite toppings like shredded cheese, sour cream, or fresh cilantro.

Tips and Variations:

  • For an extra kick, add a chopped jalapeño or a dash of hot sauce to the chili.
  • To make it vegetarian, simply swap the beef for plant-based crumbles or extra beans.
  • This chili tastes even better the next day, so feel free to make it ahead of time and let the flavors intensify overnight.

Conclusion:

With its bold flavors and comforting warmth, this One-Pot Beef and Bean Chili is a go-to recipe for any occasion. Whether you’re a chili aficionado or trying it for the first time, this hearty and spicy dish is sure to become a favorite in your household. So, grab a bowl, ladle out a generous serving, and savor the deliciousness of this soul-soothing chili. Bon appétit!

Remember, good food is meant to be shared, so don’t hesitate to whip up a batch of this chili and invite your loved ones to gather around the table. Happy cooking and happy eating!
