Egg-Free Sweet Potato Hash: A Flavorful and Nutritious Breakfast

Are you looking for a delicious and nutritious breakfast option that is both egg-free and packed with flavor? Look no further than this mouthwatering Egg-Free Sweet Potato Hash recipe! Whether you have dietary restrictions or simply want to switch up your breakfast routine, this dish is sure to satisfy your taste buds and keep you energized throughout the day. Ingredients 2 medium-sized sweet potatoes, peeled and cubed 1 red bell pepper, diced 1 green bell pepper, diced 1 small red onion, finely chopped 2 cloves of garlic, minced 2 tablespoons olive oil 1 teaspoon paprika ½ teaspoon cumin ½ teaspoon chili powder Salt and pepper to taste Fresh cilantro, for garnish Instructions In a large skillet, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the minced garlic and sauté until fragrant, about 1 minute. Add the cubed sweet potatoes to the skillet and cook for 10-12 minutes, or until they start to soften. Stir occasionally to ensure even cooking. Once the sweet potatoes are slightly tender, add the diced red and green bell peppers, as well as the finely chopped red onion. Continue cooking for an additional 5-6 minutes, or until the vegetables are cooked through and slightly caramelized. Sprinkle the paprika, cumin, chili powder, salt, and pepper over the sweet potato mixture. Stir well to evenly coat the vegetables with the spices. Cook for another 2-3 minutes to allow the flavors to meld together. Remove the skillet from heat and transfer the sweet potato hash to a serving dish. Garnish with fresh cilantro for an extra burst of freshness and color. Serve the Egg-Free Sweet Potato Hash hot and enjoy this flavorful and nutritious breakfast!
