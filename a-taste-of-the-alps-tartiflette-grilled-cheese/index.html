A Taste of the Alps: Tartiflette Grilled Cheese


If you’re a fan of comfort food and crave the hearty flavors of the Alps, then you’re in for a treat with this delightful twist on a classic dish. The Tartiflette Grilled Cheese combines the rich, creamy goodness of tartiflette with the crispy, gooey perfection of a grilled cheese sandwich. It’s a marriage made in culinary heaven, and once you try it, you’ll understand why this dish has become a beloved favorite in many households.

The Origins of Tartiflette

Before we dive into the recipe, let’s take a moment to appreciate the origins of tartiflette. This beloved French dish hails from the Savoie region of the French Alps and is traditionally made with potatoes, reblochon cheese, lardons, and onions. It’s a comforting and indulgent dish that warms the soul, making it the perfect candidate for a creative reinterpretation.

The Ingredients

To create the perfect Tartiflette Grilled Cheese, you’ll need the following ingredients:

  • Sourdough bread
  • Butter
  • Reblochon cheese (or a similar soft, creamy cheese)
  • Cooked and diced potatoes
  • Cooked bacon or lardons
  • Sautéed onions
  • Fresh thyme
  • Salt and pepper to taste

The Method

Step 1: Prepare the Ingredients

Start by cooking and dicing the potatoes, sautéing the onions until they are golden brown, and cooking the bacon or lardons until they are crispy. Once these ingredients are ready, it’s time to assemble the sandwich.

Step 2: Build the Sandwich

Take two slices of sourdough bread and generously butter one side of each slice. Place one slice, butter side down, on a preheated skillet or griddle. Layer on a generous amount of the diced potatoes, followed by the sautéed onions, crispy bacon or lardons, and a few sprigs of fresh thyme. Top it off with a generous portion of sliced reblochon cheese and then place the second slice of bread on top, butter side up.

Step 3: Grill to Perfection

Grill the sandwich over medium heat until the bread is golden brown and crispy, and the cheese is melted and gooey. Make sure to flip the sandwich halfway through to ensure even browning and melting.

Step 4: Serve and Enjoy

Once the Tartiflette Grilled Cheese is cooked to perfection, remove it from the heat and let it cool for a minute before slicing it in half. Serve it hot and enjoy the gooey, cheesy, and comforting flavors of the Alps in every bite.

The Perfect Pairing

This indulgent sandwich pairs beautifully with a crisp, refreshing salad or a warm bowl of soup. The combination of flavors and textures in the Tartiflette Grilled Cheese makes it a versatile dish that can be enjoyed on its own or as part of a larger meal.

In Conclusion

The Tartiflette Grilled Cheese is a delightful fusion of French comfort food and the classic grilled cheese sandwich. It’s a dish that celebrates the rich and indulgent flavors of the Alps while offering a playful twist on tradition. Whether you’re hosting a cozy dinner party or simply craving a comforting meal, this recipe is sure to become a beloved favorite in your culinary repertoire. So, gather your ingredients and get ready to savor the taste of the Alps in every bite!
