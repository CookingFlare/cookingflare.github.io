Fast and Simple Pasta Dishes for Quick Weeknight Dinners


As the sun sets on another busy day, cooking a quick yet satisfying meal can be a daunting task. Nevertheless, pasta dishes offer a multitude of options that are not only widely available but can be prepared in no time. This versatility makes them ideal for weeknight dinners, especially when paired with fresh ingredients and personalized flavors. Here are ten inventive yet straightforward pasta recipes that you and your family will love.

  1. Pesto Spaghetti with Cherry Tomatoes In a large skillet, heat 2 tablespoons of olive oil over medium heat. Add 1 cup of cherry tomatoes, 2 cloves minced garlic, and 2 tablespoons of fresh basil. Allow the mixture to simmer for 3-4 minutes, followed by adding 1/4 cup of pesto sauce and 1/2 cup of grated parmesan cheese. Combine cooked spaghetti with the sauce, seasoning with salt and pepper as needed.
  2. Creamy Mushroom Pasta Bake Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C). In a large skillet, heat 2 tablespoons of butter over medium heat. Add 1 cup of sliced mushrooms and 2 cloves of minced garlic, allowing the mixture to cook until the mushrooms are tender. Transfer the mixture to a large baking dish and add 1 cup of cooked spaghetti, 1 cup of cream, 1/4 cup of grated cheddar cheese, and 1/4 cup of grated mozzarella cheese. Bake for 10-12 minutes, or until the cheese is melted and bubbly.
  3. Lemon Spaghetti Aglio e Olio Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil and cook 1 cup of spaghetti according to the package instructions. In a large skillet, heat 1/4 cup of olive oil over medium-low heat. Add 3-4 cloves minced garlic and 1/4 cup of lemon juice. Remove the garlic cloves and discard. Add 1 cup of cooked spaghetti to the skillet, tossing to coat with the lemon sauce. Garnish with toasted breadcrumbs and a sprinkle of parmesan cheese.
  4. Asparagus and Prosciutto Pasta with Thyme Cook 1 cup of spaghetti according to the package instructions. In a large skillet, heat 2 tablespoons of olive oil over medium heat. Add 4-5 slices of prosciutto and cook until crispy. Add 1 cup of asparagus spears, 1/4 cup of chicken broth, and 2 sprigs of fresh thyme. Simmer for 2-3 minutes, or until the asparagus is tender. Combine cooked pasta with the vegetable mixture and add 2 tablespoons of grated parmesan cheese.
  5. Simple Pasta Primavera Cook 1 cup of pasta of choice (bow-tie or penne) according to the package instructions. In a large skillet, heat 1 cup of mixed vegetables (such as broccoli, carrots, and bell peppers) with 2 tablespoons of olive oil. Add 1 cup of chicken broth and 1 cup of shredded cheddar cheese. Simmer the mixture for 3-4 minutes or until the vegetables are tender. Combine cooked pasta and the vegetable mixture, then add 1/4 cup of chopped parsley and parmesan cheese to taste.
  6. Spicy Shrimp Pasta in White Wine In a large skillet, heat 2 tablespoons of olive oil over medium heat. Add 1 cup of sliced onions and 2 cloves of minced garlic and cook until the onions are translucent. Add 1 cup of white wine and 1 cup of cherry tomatoes. Simmer the mixture for 3-4 minutes or until the liquid has reduced slightly. Combine cooked pasta and the tomato mixture, add 1/2 cup of cooked shrimp, and season with salt and pepper as needed.
  7. Garlic Roasted Vegetable Primavera Preheat the oven to 400°F (200°C). In a large mixing bowl, toss 1 cup of mixed vegetables (such as zucchini, bell peppers, and carrots) with 2 tablespoons of olive oil and 2 cloves of minced garlic. Spread the vegetables on a baking sheet and roast for 10-12 minutes, or until tender. Cook 1 cup of pasta of choice (bow-tie or penne) according to the package instructions. Combine cooked pasta and roasted vegetables, adding 2 tablespoons of grated parmesan cheese and basil.
  8. Simple Pesto Chicken Pasta Preheat the oven to 400°F (200°C). Steam 1 cup of fully cooked chicken breast until juicy and separate into shreds. In a large mixing bowl, combine 1/4 cup of pesto sauce, 1/2 cup of shredded mozzarella cheese, and 1 cup of cooked spaghetti. Add the chicken and toss to coat. Transfer the mixture to a baking dish and top with 1/4 cup more of shredded cheese and additional basil for garnish. Bake in the oven for 8-10 minutes or until the cheese is melted.
  9. Sausage and Pepper Pasta Twirl Cook 1 cup of rotini pasta according to the package instructions. In a large skillet, heat 1/4 cup of olive oil over medium heat. Add sliced onions and 2 cloves of minced garlic and cook until translucent. Add 1 cup of sliced sausage and 2 medium bell peppers. Cook until the sausage is browned and fragrant. Simmer with 1/4 cup of chicken broth for an additional 2-3 minutes. Combine cooked pasta with the sausage mixture and top with parmesan cheese and parsley.
  10. Roasted Tomato and Basil Pasta Preheat the oven to 400°F (200°C). Toss 2 cups of cherry tomatoes with 2 tablespoons of olive oil, 2 cloves of minced garlic, and 2 sprigs of basil on a baking sheet and roast for 10-12 minutes, or until tender. Cook 1 cup of spaghetti according to the package instructions. Combine cooked pasta with the roasted tomatoes, 2 tablespoons of grated parmesan cheese, and basil. Season with salt and pepper as desired. These diverse and uncomplicated pasta dishes offer perfect solutions for your weeknight meals. With their considerable flavor profiles, they're certain to make your dinner time both enjoyable and fulfilling. Feel free to personalize each recipe according to your preferences to cater to your family's tastes.
