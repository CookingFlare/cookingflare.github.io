Creamy Chocolate Hazelnut Ice Cream: A Nutella Lover's Dream


Indulge in the decadent flavors of creamy chocolate and rich hazelnut with this irresistible recipe for Creamy Chocolate Hazelnut Ice Cream. Made with the beloved spread Nutella, this frozen treat is a dream come true for any Nutella lover. With its smooth texture and intense flavor, this ice cream is sure to satisfy your sweet tooth and leave you craving for more.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups heavy cream
  • 1 cup whole milk
  • 3/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/2 cup Nutella
  • 1/4 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/2 cup chopped hazelnuts

Instructions

  1. In a medium saucepan, combine the heavy cream, whole milk, and granulated sugar. Heat the mixture over medium heat, stirring occasionally, until it reaches a simmer.

  2. In a separate bowl, whisk together the Nutella, unsweetened cocoa powder, and salt until well combined and smooth. Add this mixture to the saucepan with the cream and milk, and whisk until fully incorporated.

  3. Continue to heat the mixture over medium heat, stirring constantly, until it thickens slightly and coats the back of a spoon. Be careful not to let it boil.

  4. Once the mixture has thickened, remove it from the heat and stir in the vanilla extract. Let the mixture cool to room temperature, then cover it and refrigerate for at least 4 hours, or until chilled.

  5. Once the mixture is chilled, transfer it to an ice cream maker and churn according to the manufacturer’s instructions. This usually takes about 20-25 minutes.

  6. While the ice cream is churning, toast the chopped hazelnuts in a dry skillet over medium heat until fragrant and lightly browned. Remove from heat and let them cool.

  7. Once the ice cream has reached a soft-serve consistency, transfer it to a lidded container, and gently fold in the toasted hazelnuts. Make sure they are evenly distributed throughout the ice cream.

  8. Cover the container and freeze the ice cream for at least 4 hours, or until firm.

  9. When ready to serve, scoop the Creamy Chocolate Hazelnut Ice Cream into bowls or cones and enjoy the luscious combination of chocolate and hazelnut flavors.

Tips and Variations

  • For an extra indulgence, drizzle some warm Nutella over the ice cream before serving.
  • You can also add some crushed hazelnut cookies or chocolate chips to the ice cream for added texture and flavor.
  • If you prefer a softer ice cream, let it sit at room temperature for a few minutes before scooping.
  • This recipe can be easily doubled or halved depending on your needs.
  • Experiment with different toppings like whipped cream, caramel sauce, or chocolate shavings to take this ice cream to the next level.

Conclusion

Creamy Chocolate Hazelnut Ice Cream is the ultimate dessert for Nutella enthusiasts. With its velvety texture, rich chocolate flavor, and crunchy hazelnut pieces, this frozen treat is sure to impress. Whether enjoyed on a hot summer day or as a special treat any time of the year, this ice cream is guaranteed to satisfy your cravings and leave you wanting more. So go ahead, indulge in this Nutella lover’s dream and treat yourself to a scoop (or two) of pure delight.
