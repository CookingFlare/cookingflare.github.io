Flavorful Instant Pot Beef and Bean Chili Recipe


Chili has always been a classic comfort food, and with the Instant Pot, you can enjoy a delicious and flavorful beef and bean chili in no time. This recipe is perfect for those busy weeknights when you crave something hearty and satisfying. The Instant Pot’s pressure cooking function allows for a quick and efficient cooking process, infusing the flavors together perfectly. So, let’s dive right into the recipe!

Ingredients

  • 1 ½ pounds of beef chuck, cut into bite-sized cubes
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 red bell pepper, diced
  • 1 jalapeño pepper, seeded and chopped (optional for an extra kick)
  • 2 cans (14 ounces each) of diced tomatoes
  • 1 can (15 ounces) of kidney beans, drained and rinsed
  • 1 can (15 ounces) of black beans, drained and rinsed
  • 2 tablespoons of chili powder
  • 1 tablespoon of cumin
  • 1 teaspoon of oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 cups of beef broth
  • 2 tablespoons of vegetable oil

Instructions

  1. Set your Instant Pot to the sauté function and heat the vegetable oil. Add the beef cubes and cook until browned on all sides. This will take about 5 minutes. Remove the beef from the Instant Pot and set it aside.

  2. In the same pot, add the onion, garlic, red bell pepper, and jalapeño pepper (if using). Sauté for about 3 minutes until the vegetables soften.

  3. Add the chili powder, cumin, oregano, salt, and pepper to the pot. Stir well to coat the vegetables with the spices.

  4. Pour in the diced tomatoes, kidney beans, black beans, and beef broth. Give everything a good stir to combine all the ingredients.

  5. Return the beef to the Instant Pot, making sure to submerge it in the liquid.

  6. Close the Instant Pot lid and set the valve to the sealing position. Select the pressure cook function and set the timer to 20 minutes.

  7. Once the cooking time is complete, allow the pressure to release naturally for 10 minutes, then perform a quick release to release any remaining pressure.

  8. Carefully open the Instant Pot lid, taking note of the hot steam. Give the chili a good stir, and if desired, let it simmer on the sauté function for an additional 10 minutes to thicken the sauce.

  9. Serve the flavorful Instant Pot beef and bean chili hot, garnished with your favorite toppings such as shredded cheese, sour cream, chopped green onions, or fresh cilantro.

Tips and Variations

  • For a spicier chili, leave the seeds in the jalapeño pepper or add a pinch of cayenne pepper.
  • Feel free to add additional vegetables to the chili, such as corn, diced zucchini, or diced carrots.
  • If you prefer a thicker chili, you can mash some of the beans against the side of the Instant Pot to thicken the sauce.
  • Leftovers can be stored in an airtight container in the refrigerator for up to 3 days or frozen for up to 3 months.

With the Instant Pot, you can enjoy a flavorful and satisfying beef and bean chili without spending hours in the kitchen. This recipe is perfect for any occasion and can easily be customized to suit your taste preferences. So, grab your Instant Pot and give this recipe a try – you won’t be disappointed!
