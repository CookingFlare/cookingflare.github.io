Raspberry Tiramisu with Ladyfingers Recipe: A Creamy and Decadent Vegan Dessert


If you’re a fan of traditional tiramisu but follow a vegan diet, then this Raspberry Tiramisu with Ladyfingers recipe is for you! Made with creamy layers of raspberry-infused cashew cream and delicate ladyfingers soaked in strong coffee, this vegan twist on a classic Italian dessert is sure to satisfy your sweet tooth.

Ingredients

For the raspberry-infused cashew cream:

  • 2 cups raw cashews, soaked overnight
  • 1 cup fresh raspberries
  • 1/2 cup maple syrup
  • 1/4 cup coconut oil, melted
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/2 teaspoon lemon zest
  • Pinch of salt

For the ladyfingers:

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 cup almond flour
  • 1/4 cup cornstarch
  • 1/4 cup powdered sugar
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup almond milk
  • 1/4 cup maple syrup
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

For assembly:

  • 1/2 cup strong brewed coffee, cooled
  • 1 tablespoon cocoa powder, for dusting
  • Fresh raspberries, for garnish

Instructions

Step 1: Prepare the Raspberry-Infused Cashew Cream

  1. Drain and rinse the soaked cashews, then transfer them to a high-speed blender or food processor.
  2. Add the fresh raspberries, maple syrup, melted coconut oil, vanilla extract, lemon zest, and a pinch of salt to the blender or food processor.
  3. Blend on high until smooth and creamy, scraping down the sides as needed. The consistency should be similar to a thick pudding. Set aside.

Step 2: Make the Ladyfingers

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C) and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
  2. In a medium-sized mixing bowl, whisk together the all-purpose flour, almond flour, cornstarch, powdered sugar, baking powder, and salt.
  3. In a separate bowl, combine the almond milk, maple syrup, and vanilla extract.
  4. Gradually pour the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients, whisking until well combined and smooth.
  5. Transfer the batter into a piping bag fitted with a round tip nozzle.
  6. Pipe the batter onto the prepared baking sheet, forming ladyfinger shapes that are about 3 inches long and 1 inch wide.
  7. Bake for 12-15 minutes, or until the ladyfingers are golden brown and slightly firm to the touch.
  8. Remove from the oven and let them cool completely on a wire rack.

Step 3: Assemble the Raspberry Tiramisu

  1. Pour the cooled strong brewed coffee into a shallow dish.
  2. Dip each ladyfinger into the coffee, allowing it to soak for a few seconds on each side. Be careful not to oversoak, as the ladyfingers should remain firm.
  3. Arrange a layer of coffee-soaked ladyfingers in the bottom of a rectangular glass or ceramic dish.
  4. Spread a layer of the raspberry-infused cashew cream over the ladyfingers, using a spatula to ensure an even distribution.
  5. Repeat the process, layering soaked ladyfingers and cashew cream until you have used all the ingredients, finishing with a layer of cashew cream on top.
  6. Using a fine mesh sieve, dust the cocoa powder over the top of the tiramisu.
  7. Cover the dish with plastic wrap and refrigerate for at least 4 hours, or overnight, to allow the flavors to meld and the dessert to set.
  8. Just before serving, garnish with fresh raspberries for an extra pop of color and flavor.

Step 4: Enjoy!

Serve chilled and enjoy this creamy and decadent vegan Raspberry Tiramisu with Ladyfingers with your loved ones. The combination of the rich cashew cream, the delicate coffee-soaked ladyfingers, and the tartness of fresh raspberries creates a dessert that is both indulgent and refreshing. It’s a perfect treat for special occasions or when you simply crave a little something sweet.
