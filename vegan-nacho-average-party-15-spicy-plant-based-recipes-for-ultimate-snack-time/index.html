Vegan Nacho Average Party: 15 Spicy Plant-Based Recipes for Ultimate Snack Time


Are you planning a party without any animal products? Embrace the power of plants and satisfy your cravings with these 15 hot and satisfying vegan nacho recipes. Go beyond the classic chips and cheese with these next-level plant-based snacks inspired by Mexican street food and festival tastes. Prepare to heat up your next gathering with these spicy and scrumptious vegan treats.

1. Vegan Jalapeño Popper Nachos

  • Ingredients:

    • 1 bag tortilla chips
    • 1 can black beans, rinsed and drained
    • 1 cup shredded vegan cheese
    • 4-6 jalapeños, seeded and finely chopped
    • 1/4 cup chopped fresh cilantro
    • 1/4 cup vegan sour cream
    • 1/4 cup finely chopped red onion
    • 1/4 cup finely chopped tomato

  • Instructions:

    1. Preheat oven to 400°F (200°C).
    2. Arrange tortilla chips on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper.
    3. Spread black beans, followed by vegan cheese, jalapeños, and cilantro on top.
    4. Bake for 15 minutes or until cheese is melted and bubbly.
    5. Remove from oven, top with sour cream, red onion, and tomato.
    6. Serve immediately.

2. Vegan Carne Asada Nachos

  • Ingredients:

    • 1 bag tortilla chips
    • 2 zucchini, julienned and stir-fried until browned
    • 1 red bell pepper, julienned and stir-fried until browned
    • 1 cup shredded vegan cheese
    • 1 avocado, peeled, pitted, and sliced
    • 1/4 cup finely chopped purple onion
    • 1/4 cup finely chopped cilantro
    • 2 limes, cut into wedges

  • Instructions:

    1. Arrange tortilla chips on a large plate or baking sheet.
    2. Sprinkle the stir-fried zucchini and red bell pepper on top.
    3. Add vegan cheese and avocado slices.
    4. Serve topped with purple onion, cilantro, and lime wedges for squeezing.

3. Ultra Crispy Cauliflower Nachos

  • Ingredients:

    • 1 large head of cauliflower, cut into bite-sized florets
    • 1 cup aquafaba (liquid from canned chickpeas)
    • 1 cup all-purpose flour
    • 1 cup cornstarch
    • 2 cups unsweetened plant-based milk
    • 1 tsp garlic powder
    • 1 tsp cumin
    • Black salt (kala namak)
    • Salt, to taste
    • Chopped jalapeños or, sambal oelek
    • 1 cup vegan sour cream
    • 1 avocado, diced
    • Chopped cilantro

  • Instructions:

    1. Preheat the oven to 425°F (218°C). Line a baking sheet with aluminum foil.
    2. Pat the cauliflower dry and toss with garlic powder, cumin, and salt to taste. Arrange them on the lined baking sheet.
    3. In a mixing bowl, whisk aquafaba and flour until smooth.
    4. Add cornstarch, plant-based milk, and black salt, mixing well until a smooth batter is formed.
    5. Dip cauliflower into the batter and arrange back on the baking sheet, making sure to coat each piece with batter.
    6. Bake for 20 minutes until golden brown.
    7. Top crispy cauliflower pieces with jalapeños or sambal, vegan sour cream, avocados, and cilantro.

4. Chipotle BBQ Jackfruit Nachos

  • Ingredients:

    • 1 bag tortilla chips
    • 1 can jackfruit, drained and chopped
    • 1/4 cup water
    • 3 tbsp chipotle BBQ sauce
    • 1 cup shredded vegan cheese
    • 1/2 cup vegan mayo
    • Fresh cilantro, chopped
    • Fresh lime juice
    • 1/4 red onion, finely chopped

  • Instructions:

    1. In a saucepan, combine jackfruit, water, and BBQ sauce. Simmer until the jackfruit is tender.
    2. Arrange tortilla chips on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper.
    3. Top chips with jackfruit mixture, followed by vegan cheese.
    4. Bake at 400°F (200°C) for 10 minutes or until cheese is bubbly.
    5. Mix vegan mayo with fresh cilantro, lime juice, and red onion.
    6. Remove nachos from the oven, let cool for a few minutes, then drizzle the dressing overtop.

5. Spicy Mango Salsa Nachos

  • Ingredients:

    • 1 bag tortilla chips
    • 2 ripe mangoes, diced
    • 1 red onion, finely chopped
    • 1 jalapeño, seeded and finely chopped
    • Juice of 3 limes
    • 1/4 cup finely chopped cilantro
    • Salt, to taste

  • Instructions:

    1. In a bowl, mix diced mangoes, chopped onion, jalapeño, lime juice, cilantro, and salt to taste.
    2. Arrange tortilla chips on a large platter or baking sheet.
    3. Sprinkle mango salsa generously on top.
    4. Serve immediately for the best results.

Conclusion

Spice up your next party with these 15 blazing hot vegan nacho recipes. Each scrumptious dish offers a unique twist on traditional nacho toppings, featuring a diverse range of plant-based ingredients that create bold flavors. Celebrate the power of plants with a feast of spiciness, texture, and eye-catching presentation that guests are sure to adore, and experience a competition-free, plant-based nacho fiesta. Your vegan journey is just getting started – expand your culinary skills to incorporate many more of these fiery delights for unforgettable gatherings. Enjoy!
