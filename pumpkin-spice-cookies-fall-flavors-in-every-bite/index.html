Pumpkin Spice Cookies: Fall Flavors in Every Bite


Are you ready to embrace the cozy flavors of fall? Look no further than these delectable Pumpkin Spice Cookies! Bursting with warm spices and the comforting taste of pumpkin, these cookies are the perfect treat to enjoy during the autumn season. Whether you’re hosting a gathering or simply craving a sweet indulgence, these cookies will satisfy your taste buds and fill your home with the delightful aroma of fall.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 cup canned pumpkin puree
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground nutmeg
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cloves
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C) and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.

  2. In a large mixing bowl, cream together the softened butter and granulated sugar until light and fluffy.

  3. Add the pumpkin puree and vanilla extract to the bowl, and mix until well combined.

  4. In a separate bowl, whisk together the all-purpose flour, baking powder, baking soda, ground cinnamon, ground nutmeg, ground cloves, and salt.

  5. Gradually add the dry ingredients to the wet ingredients, mixing until just combined. Be careful not to overmix.

  6. Using a cookie scoop or a tablespoon, drop rounded portions of dough onto the prepared baking sheet, spacing them about 2 inches apart.

  7. Bake for 12-14 minutes, or until the edges are lightly golden. The centers may still appear soft, but they will firm up as the cookies cool.

  8. Remove the baking sheet from the oven and allow the cookies to cool on the sheet for 5 minutes before transferring them to a wire rack to cool completely.

  9. Once cooled, store the cookies in an airtight container to maintain their freshness.

Serving Suggestions

These Pumpkin Spice Cookies are delicious on their own, but you can take them to the next level by adding a drizzle of cream cheese icing or a sprinkle of powdered sugar. Pair them with a warm cup of spiced chai tea or a pumpkin latte for the ultimate fall treat. They also make a wonderful homemade gift during the holiday season.

Tips and Tricks

  • For a chewier texture, refrigerate the cookie dough for 30 minutes before baking.
  • If you prefer a stronger pumpkin flavor, add an extra teaspoon of pumpkin pie spice to the dough.
  • Experiment with different mix-ins, such as chopped nuts or chocolate chips, to add extra texture and flavor to these cookies.

Conclusion

With their irresistible blend of pumpkin and warm spices, these Pumpkin Spice Cookies are sure to become a fall favorite in your household. Whip up a batch and savor the flavors of autumn in every bite. Whether you’re enjoying them with loved ones or indulging in a moment of self-care, these cookies are a delightful treat that captures the essence of the season. So go ahead, embrace the coziness of fall and let these cookies transport you to a world of fall flavors and comforting aromas.
