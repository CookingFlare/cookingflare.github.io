Mango Cardamom Lassi: Mango Cardamom Lassi Mocktail Recipe


If you’re looking for a refreshing and exotic beverage to beat the summer heat, look no further than Mango Cardamom Lassi. This delicious and creamy mocktail combines the sweetness of ripe mangoes with the aromatic flavors of cardamom, creating a tantalizing blend of flavors that will leave you craving for more.

Ingredients

To make this delightful Mango Cardamom Lassi, you will need the following ingredients:

  • 2 ripe mangoes, peeled and pitted
  • 1 cup plain yogurt
  • 1/2 cup milk (you can use regular or dairy-free alternatives)
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground cardamom
  • 1 tablespoon honey or sugar (optional)
  • Ice cubes

Instructions

  1. In a blender, combine the ripe mangoes, plain yogurt, milk, ground cardamom, and honey or sugar (if desired). Blend until smooth and creamy.

  2. Taste the mixture and adjust the sweetness according to your preference by adding more honey or sugar if needed.

  3. Add a few ice cubes to the blender and blend again until the mixture is chilled and frothy.

  4. Once blended to your desired consistency, pour the Mango Cardamom Lassi into tall glasses.

  5. Garnish with a sprinkle of ground cardamom on top for an extra burst of flavor.

  6. Serve immediately and enjoy the refreshing and tropical flavors of this Mango Cardamom Lassi mocktail.

Tips and Variations

  • To enhance the presentation of your Mango Cardamom Lassi, you can serve it in chilled copper glasses or garnish with fresh mint leaves.

  • For a creamier texture, you can add a scoop of vanilla ice cream or frozen yogurt to the blender along with the other ingredients.

  • If you prefer a thinner consistency, you can add more milk or water to the mixture and adjust the sweetness accordingly.

  • Experiment with different varieties of mangoes to discover unique flavor profiles. Alphonso and Kesar mangoes are particularly popular for their rich and sweet taste.

  • If you’re feeling adventurous, you can also add a pinch of ground cinnamon or nutmeg to the lassi for an interesting twist.

Health Benefits

In addition to its refreshing taste, Mango Cardamom Lassi offers several health benefits:

  1. Mangoes are rich in vitamins A and C, as well as dietary fiber. They are also a great source of antioxidants, which help protect the body against free radicals.

  2. Cardamom is known for its digestive properties and can help alleviate indigestion, bloating, and other digestive issues.

  3. Yogurt provides probiotics, which promote a healthy gut and strengthen the immune system.

  4. This lassi is a good source of calcium, which is essential for strong bones and teeth.

Conclusion

Mango Cardamom Lassi is not only a delicious and refreshing mocktail but also a great way to enjoy the health benefits of mangoes, yogurt, and cardamom. With its tropical flavors and creamy texture, this lassi is perfect for sipping by the poolside or as a refreshing treat on a hot summer day. So go ahead, whip up a batch of Mango Cardamom Lassi, and transport your taste buds to a tropical paradise. Cheers to good health and indulgence!
