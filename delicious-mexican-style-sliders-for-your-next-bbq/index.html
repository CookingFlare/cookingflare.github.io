Delicious Mexican-Style Sliders for Your Next BBQ


Get ready to fire up your grill and tantalize your taste buds with these mouthwatering Mexican-Style Sliders that are perfect for your next backyard BBQ. These flavorful little morsels of joy combine the best of Mexican cuisine with the classic American slider, making them a crowd-pleaser for any gathering. Ingredients To create these amazing Mexican-Style Sliders, you'll need the following ingredients:

1 pound ground beef (80/20 or 70/30 lean to fat ratio works best) 1/2 medium onion, finely chopped 1/2 medium bell pepper, finely chopped 2 cloves of garlic, minced 1 jalapeño pepper, finely chopped 1 tablespoon cumin 1 teaspoon chili powder 1/2 teaspoon paprika 1/4 teaspoon cayenne pepper (optional) 8-10 hamburger buns 4 ounces pepper jack cheese, sliced 1/4 cup chopped fresh cilantro 1/4 cup chopped fresh scallions 1/4 cup crumbled queso fresco (or feta cheese as a substitute) Salt and pepper to taste Cooking oil or non-stick cooking spray

Instructions To begin, preheat your grill to medium-high heat (around 375°F). While it's heating up, prepare the toppings and condiments. Chop the onion, bell pepper, and jalapeño pepper; mince the garlic; and crumble the queso fresco. Also, don't forget to toast the buns lightly on the grill. Next, in a large skillet, cook the ground beef over medium heat, breaking it up with a wooden spoon as it cooks, until it's browned and no longer pink. Drain the excess fat and set the skillet aside. Now it's time to assemble the sliders. Spread a teaspoon of mayonnaise or sour cream on the bottom bun, followed by a few slices of pepper jack cheese, a sprinkle of cumin and chili powder, and a spoonful of the cooked ground beef. Add a few chopped scallions and a sprinkle of cilantro for garnish. To add an extra layer of flavor to the sliders, grill a few slices of the chopped onion and bell pepper on the grill and place them on top of the beef. Serving Suggestions These Mexican-Style Sliders are perfect for a variety of situations. Serve them at your next backyard BBQ for a crowd-pleasing snack, or use them as a topping for tacos or nachos at your family's next fiesta. If you're feeling fancy, try adding some sliced avocado or Mexican street corn to the mix for added creaminess and flavor. Tips and Variations To make these sliders even more exciting, try adding some diced ham, cooked chorizo, or sliced Mexican chorizo to the ground beef mixture. Also, don't be afraid to experiment with different levels of heat by adjusting the amount of jalapeño pepper or adding some diced habanero peppers to the mix. If you want to take your sliders to the next level, try serving them with a side of spicy chipotle ranch or a dollop of tangy pico de gallo. Conclusion With these delicious Mexican-Style Sliders, you'll be the star of your next family gathering or backyard BBQ. The combination of ground beef, grilled onions and bell peppers, and Mexican seasonings is a match made in heaven. They're easy to make, customizable to your taste, and will be devoured in no time. So fire up the grill, get creative, and enjoy the rave reviews from your family and friends!

Alternative Uses:

Use leftover Mexican-Style Sliders to top tacos or nachos for an amazing twist on a classic snack. Serve sliders at a potluck or party to impress your fellow foodies. Adding some diced chicken or turkey to the ground beef mixture can make these sliders leaner and extra flavorful.
