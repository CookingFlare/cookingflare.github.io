Delicious Mango Tart Recipe: A Tropical Twist for Your Dessert


As the summer months approach, our taste buds crave something refreshing and sweet to beat the heat. What better way to satisfy this craving than with a delicious mango tart? This tropical twist on the classic tart recipe is a perfect treat for any occasion, whether it’s a family gathering or a dinner party with friends.

Ingredients

For the crust:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup confectioners’ sugar
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, chilled and cut into small pieces
  • 1 large egg, beaten
  • 1 tablespoon ice-cold water

For the filling:

  • 3 ripe mangos, diced
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 2 tablespoons cornstarch
  • 1 tablespoon unsalted butter, melted
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/4 cup heavy cream
  • 1 egg, beaten
  • Confectioners’ sugar, for dusting

Instructions

Crust

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. In a large bowl, whisk together the flour, confectioners’ sugar, and salt.
  3. Add the cold butter and use a pastry blender or your fingers to work it into the flour mixture until it resembles coarse crumbs.
  4. Gradually pour in the beaten egg and ice-cold water, mixing until the dough comes together in a ball.
  5. Wrap the dough in plastic wrap and refrigerate for at least 30 minutes.
  6. On a lightly floured surface, roll out the dough to a thickness of about 1/8 inch (3 mm).
  7. Transfer the dough to a 9-inch (23 cm) tart pan with a removable bottom.
  8. Trim the edges and press the dough into the corners of the pan.

Filling

  1. In a large bowl, combine the diced mango, granulated sugar, and cornstarch.
  2. Add the melted butter, salt, and vanilla extract, and mix until the mango is well coated.
  3. Stir in the heavy cream and beaten egg until the mixture is smooth.
  4. Pour the filling into the prepared tart crust.
  5. Bake the tart for 40-45 minutes, or until the crust is golden brown and the filling is set.
  6. Remove the tart from the oven and let it cool completely on a wire rack.

Assembly

  1. Once the tart has cooled, dust it with confectioners’ sugar.
  2. Serve the tart at room temperature, or refrigerate it for up to 24 hours before serving.

Tips and Variations

  • To ensure that the crust is flaky and tender, make sure to keep the butter cold and the dough refrigerated for at least 30 minutes.
  • If you prefer a more intense mango flavor, you can use 4-5 mangos instead of 3.
  • To add a bit of crunch to the tart, sprinkle some chopped nuts or shredded coconut over the filling before baking.
  • If you want to make the tart ahead of time, you can bake it and then refrigerate it for up to 24 hours before serving.

Conclusion

This delicious mango tart recipe is a perfect dessert for any occasion. The combination of sweet and tangy mango, flaky crust, and creamy filling is sure to impress your guests. Whether you’re a beginner or an experienced baker, this recipe is easy to follow and requires minimal ingredients. So go ahead, give it a try, and indulge in the sweet and tropical flavors of this delicious mango tart!
