IPA and Indian: Spicy Food Pairings with IPAs


When it comes to food and drink pairings, few combinations can rival the delightful interaction of spicy dishes and IPAs (India Pale Ales). Let’s dive into understanding this perfect match and show you how to elevate your culinary experience.

What is an IPA and Why It Pairs Well with Spicy Foods

Originating in the UK in the 18th century, IPAs were brewed with high alcohol content and extra hops. This was to help preserve and fortify these beers during the long voyage from England to India. Over the years, IPAs have evolved, with American breweries adding their twist of floral and citrus notes, often complemented by a bitter hop kick.

Spicy dishes, on the other hand, feature an array of flavors such as chilies, curry, and ginger. They tend to stimulate our taste buds and tickle our palate, making it an ideal pair with the strong and refreshing character of IPAs.

Why Spicy Foods and IPAs Are a Winning Combination

The interplay of flavors between a well-crafted Indian dish and a distinguishable IPA create a thrilling culinary experience. The progressive heat of a curry dish, for instance, can be tempered by the citrus and tropical fruit flavors found in some IPAs. The hoppy bitterness in an IPA further accentuates and contrasts the heat of a chili dish.

Moreover, spicy food can help enhance the aroma and flavor profile of an IPA. The heightened senses, thanks to the spices, allow you to gain a deeper appreciation of the digital nuances woven into your chosen IPA.

The Science Behind the Pairing

Chemical reactions occur on our taste buds when we consume food, converting the molecules and molecules found in components of our food into a signal to be relayed to our brains. The calories in spicy ingredients like chilies are burnt off, causing the production of endorphins which makes our body release pain-killing chemicals.

The alcohol present in IPAs stimulates our senses, enhancing the perception of aroma and flavor. New flavor notes can emerge as the senses are elevated, providing an unparalleled gastronomic experience.

Five Fantastic Pairings for Your Next Meal

To bring your Indian and IPA experience to life, consider these delicious combinations:

  1. Goan Fish Curry with a Hazy IPA - The tropical fruit notes of a hazy IPA complement the savory spices found in this Goan dish.
  2. Chicken Tikka Masala with a West Coast IPA - The citrus and resinous hop flavors balance the subtle heat in this popular Indian dish.
  3. Thai Green Curry with a DIPA (Double IPA) - The coconut milk in this curry pairs precisely with the tropical fruit tones of a DIPA.
  4. Lamb Rogan Josh with a Scottish Ale - The maltiness of a Scottish Ale easily handles the inviting warmth of this classic Indian dish.
  5. Butter Chicken with a Dark Ale - The rich, robust flavors of a dark ale can comfortably Challenge the bold nature of a butter chicken.

UNLEASH YOUR CULINARY JOURNEY

Don’t shy away from exploring new pairings or introducing different heat levels to your dishes. Your culinary adventure awaits, with endless IPAs primed and ready to complement your spicy creations.

After all, food, like life, needs its twists and turns. Boldly announce your love for IPAs with the heat of spices; it’s the unconventional that makes this pairing truly unforgettable. So, let the journey begin - experiment and find your perfect spicy-foods and IPAs match!
