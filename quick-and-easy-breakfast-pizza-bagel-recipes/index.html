Quick and Easy Breakfast Pizza Bagel Recipes


Looking for a delicious and satisfying breakfast that’s quick to make? Look no further than these mouthwatering breakfast pizza bagel recipes! Packed with flavor and ready in minutes, these recipes are perfect for those busy mornings when you need something delicious and filling to start your day. So, grab your favorite bagels and let’s get cooking!

1. Classic Bacon and Egg Pizza Bagel

Ingredients:

  • 2 bagels, sliced in half
  • 4 slices of bacon
  • 4 large eggs
  • 1 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • Fresh chives, chopped (for garnish)

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C).
  2. Place the bacon slices on a baking sheet and bake for 10-12 minutes until crispy. Remove from the oven and set aside.
  3. While the bacon is cooking, toast the bagel halves in a toaster or under the broiler until lightly golden.
  4. In a non-stick skillet, fry the eggs to your desired doneness. Season with salt and pepper.
  5. Place the toasted bagel halves on a baking sheet. Top each half with a slice of bacon, followed by a fried egg. Sprinkle shredded cheddar cheese over the eggs.
  6. Bake in the preheated oven for 5-7 minutes or until the cheese is melted and bubbly.
  7. Remove from the oven and garnish with fresh chives. Serve hot.

2. Veggie Lover’s Pizza Bagel

Ingredients:

  • 2 bagels, sliced in half
  • 1/2 cup pizza sauce
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/4 cup sliced bell peppers
  • 1/4 cup sliced mushrooms
  • 1/4 cup sliced black olives
  • 1/4 cup sliced cherry tomatoes
  • Fresh basil leaves (for garnish)

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C).
  2. Place the bagel halves on a baking sheet. Spread a generous amount of pizza sauce on each half.
  3. Sprinkle shredded mozzarella cheese evenly over the sauce.
  4. Top the bagels with sliced bell peppers, mushrooms, black olives, and cherry tomatoes.
  5. Bake in the preheated oven for 10-12 minutes or until the cheese is melted and bubbly.
  6. Remove from the oven and garnish with fresh basil leaves. Serve hot.

3. Ham and Cheese Breakfast Pizza Bagel

Ingredients:

  • 2 bagels, sliced in half
  • 1/4 cup cream cheese
  • 1/2 cup diced ham
  • 1/2 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 2 large eggs
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • Fresh parsley, chopped (for garnish)

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C).
  2. Place the bagel halves on a baking sheet. Spread a thin layer of cream cheese on each half.
  3. Sprinkle diced ham evenly over the cream cheese.
  4. Top the bagels with shredded mozzarella cheese.
  5. Create a small well in the center of each bagel and crack an egg into each well.
  6. Season the eggs with salt and pepper.
  7. Bake in the preheated oven for 10-12 minutes or until the eggs are cooked to your desired doneness and the cheese is melted and golden.
  8. Remove from the oven and garnish with fresh parsley. Serve hot.

Enjoy these quick and easy breakfast pizza bagel recipes that are sure to satisfy your taste buds and keep you energized throughout the day. Experiment with different toppings and create your own unique combinations. Whether you’re a meat lover or a veggie enthusiast, there’s a breakfast pizza bagel recipe for everyone. So, start your day off right with a delicious homemade breakfast that’s as easy as it is tasty!
