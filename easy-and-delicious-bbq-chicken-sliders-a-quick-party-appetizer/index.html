Easy and Delicious BBQ Chicken Sliders: A Quick Party Appetizer


Are you looking for a crowd-pleasing appetizer that’s easy to make and full of flavor? Look no further than these mouthwatering BBQ chicken sliders! Perfect for parties or casual get-togethers, these sliders are a surefire hit that will leave your guests craving for more. With just a few simple ingredients and minimal prep time, you can whip up a batch of these delectable sliders in no time.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1 cup BBQ sauce
  • 1/4 cup mayonnaise
  • 1 tablespoon Dijon mustard
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon onion powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Slider buns

Instructions

  1. Preheat your grill to medium-high heat. Season the chicken breasts with salt, pepper, garlic powder, and onion powder.

  2. Place the chicken breasts on the preheated grill and cook for 6-8 minutes per side, or until the internal temperature reaches 165°F (74°C).

  3. While the chicken is grilling, prepare the BBQ sauce by combining the BBQ sauce, mayonnaise, and Dijon mustard in a small bowl. Mix well until smooth and creamy.

  4. Once the chicken is cooked through, remove it from the grill and let it rest for a few minutes. Then, using two forks, shred the chicken into bite-sized pieces.

  5. Transfer the shredded chicken to a large bowl and pour the prepared BBQ sauce over it. Mix well until the chicken is evenly coated with the sauce.

  6. To assemble the sliders, slice the slider buns in half horizontally. Place a generous amount of the BBQ chicken mixture on the bottom half of each bun.

  7. If desired, you can add additional toppings such as sliced pickles, coleslaw, or cheese to enhance the flavor and texture of the sliders.

  8. Finally, place the top half of the bun on each slider and secure with a toothpick if needed. Serve immediately and enjoy!

Tips and Variations

  • If you don’t have a grill, you can also cook the chicken breasts in a skillet over medium-high heat. Simply cook for 6-8 minutes per side, or until cooked through.

  • Feel free to customize your sliders by adding your favorite toppings or condiments. Sliced avocado, crispy bacon, or even a dollop of ranch dressing can take these sliders to the next level of deliciousness.

  • If you’re serving a larger crowd, simply double or triple the recipe to accommodate the number of guests.

  • These sliders can be prepared ahead of time and stored in the refrigerator. Simply assemble the sliders without the top bun, wrap them tightly in plastic wrap or aluminum foil, and refrigerate. When ready to serve, heat them in the oven at 350°F (175°C) for 10-15 minutes or until warmed through.

  • For a spicier twist, add a dash of hot sauce or a pinch of cayenne pepper to the BBQ sauce mixture.

These Easy and Delicious BBQ Chicken Sliders are the ultimate party appetizer that will have your guests coming back for seconds. With their juicy shredded chicken and tangy BBQ sauce, they are guaranteed to be a hit at any gathering. So fire up the grill and get ready to impress your friends and family with this quick and flavorful dish. Enjoy!
