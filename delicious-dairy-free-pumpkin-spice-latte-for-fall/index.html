Delicious Dairy-Free Pumpkin Spice Latte for Fall


As the leaves begin to change and the air becomes crisp, there’s nothing quite like sipping on a warm, comforting drink to celebrate the arrival of fall. And what better way to embrace the season than with a delicious dairy-free pumpkin spice latte? This recipe combines the rich flavors of pumpkin, aromatic spices, and creamy non-dairy milk to create a cozy beverage that will leave you feeling warm and satisfied. Whether you’re lactose intolerant, vegan, or simply looking for a healthier alternative, this dairy-free pumpkin spice latte is sure to become a seasonal favorite.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of unsweetened almond milk (or any non-dairy milk of your choice)
  • 2 tablespoons of pumpkin puree
  • 1 tablespoon of maple syrup
  • 1/2 teaspoon of vanilla extract
  • 1/2 teaspoon of pumpkin pie spice
  • 1/2 cup of strong brewed coffee or 1-2 shots of espresso
  • Coconut whipped cream (optional)
  • Ground cinnamon (for garnish)

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Pumpkin Spice Mixture: In a small saucepan, combine the pumpkin puree, pumpkin pie spice, and maple syrup. Cook over medium heat, stirring constantly, for 2-3 minutes until the mixture is fragrant. This step helps to enhance the flavors of the pumpkin and spices.

  2. Steam the Non-Dairy Milk: While the pumpkin spice mixture is cooking, gently heat the almond milk in a separate saucepan over medium heat. Be careful not to let it boil. Once the milk is warm, remove it from the heat and froth it using a milk frother or a whisk until it becomes creamy and frothy.

  3. Brew the Coffee or Espresso: While the milk is heating, brew a strong cup of coffee or prepare 1-2 shots of espresso, depending on your preference. If using a coffee machine, opt for a dark roast to complement the rich flavors of the pumpkin spice.

  4. Combine the Ingredients: In a large mug, pour the brewed coffee or espresso. Add the pumpkin spice mixture and vanilla extract, and stir well to combine. Then, carefully pour the frothed almond milk over the coffee and pumpkin mixture, using a spoon to hold back the foam.

  5. Top with Garnishes: If desired, top your dairy-free pumpkin spice latte with a dollop of coconut whipped cream and a sprinkle of ground cinnamon. The whipped cream adds a decadent touch, while the cinnamon enhances the aromatic flavors of the drink.

  6. Enjoy the Cozy Delight: Grab your favorite blanket, find a cozy spot, and savor the delightful flavors of this dairy-free pumpkin spice latte. Whether you’re enjoying it on a brisk morning or as an evening treat, this beverage is perfect for embracing the comforts of fall.

Tips for Customization

  • Sweetness Level: Adjust the amount of maple syrup to suit your taste preferences. If you prefer a sweeter drink, simply add an extra drizzle of maple syrup.

  • Spice Intensity: Feel free to adjust the quantity of pumpkin pie spice according to your preference for a more or less pronounced spicy flavor.

  • Milk Options: While this recipe uses almond milk, you can experiment with other non-dairy milks such as oat, soy, or coconut milk to find the perfect creamy texture and flavor for your dairy-free pumpkin spice latte.

With this simple recipe, you can indulge in the delightful flavors of fall without compromising on taste or dietary preferences. Whether you’re hosting a gathering or simply treating yourself to a cozy moment, this dairy-free pumpkin spice latte is a delightful addition to your seasonal beverage repertoire. Cheers to the warmth and flavors of autumn!
