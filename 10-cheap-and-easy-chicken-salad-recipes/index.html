10 Cheap and Easy Chicken Salad Recipes


Whether you’re trying to stretch your grocery budget or looking for a quick and nutritious meal, chicken salads are the perfect option. They’re incredibly versatile, packed with protein, and can be customized to fit your taste. Here are 10 affordable and simple chicken salad recipes that are bound to make your taste buds happy while keeping your wallet intact.

1. Classic Chicken Salad

Sometimes, you just can’t go wrong with a traditional chicken salad. This recipe is simple yet flavorful, made with ingredients you likely already have in your kitchen.

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups shredded cooked chicken
  • 1/2 cup mayonnaise
  • 1/4 cup celery, finely chopped
  • 1/4 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon Dijon mustard
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a large bowl, combine the shredded chicken, mayonnaise, celery, garlic powder, and Dijon mustard.
  2. Mix well until everything is evenly coated.
  3. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  4. Serve on crackers, as a sandwich filling, or over a bed of fresh greens.

2. Greek Yogurt Chicken Salad

For a healthier twist on the classic recipe, swap out mayonnaise with Greek yogurt. This version is equally creamy but comes with a boost of protein and fewer calories.

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups diced cooked chicken
  • 1/2 cup plain Greek yogurt
  • 1/4 cup diced cucumbers
  • 1/4 cup crumbled feta cheese
  • 1 tablespoon fresh dill, chopped
  • Juice of 1/2 lemon
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Combine the chicken, Greek yogurt, cucumbers, feta cheese, dill, and lemon juice in a bowl.
  2. Stir until well mixed and creamy.
  3. Add salt and pepper to your liking.
  4. Serve in a whole wheat pita or with fresh veggies as a dip.

3. Southwest Chicken Salad

Bring a little spice to your lunch routine with this bold and zesty Southwest chicken salad, perfect for taco night or meal prep.

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups shredded cooked chicken
  • 1/2 cup black beans, rinsed and drained
  • 1/4 cup corn kernels
  • 1/4 cup diced red bell pepper
  • 1/4 cup cilantro, finely chopped
  • 1/3 cup salsa
  • 1/4 cup sour cream or Greek yogurt
  • 1/2 teaspoon cumin
  • Juice of 1/2 lime

Instructions:

  1. In a large bowl, combine chicken, black beans, corn, red bell pepper, and cilantro.
  2. Add salsa, sour cream, cumin, and lime juice. Mix until everything is evenly coated.
  3. Adjust seasoning if necessary and serve in a tortilla wrap or over greens.

4. Curried Chicken Salad

Take your chicken salad to the next level with the warm, aromatic flavors of curry powder. This recipe is simple yet exotic.

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups diced cooked chicken
  • 1/2 cup mayonnaise or Greek yogurt
  • 1/4 cup raisins or chopped dried apricots
  • 1/4 cup diced apples
  • 1/4 cup sliced almonds
  • 1 teaspoon curry powder
  • Pinch of salt

Instructions:

  1. Mix all the ingredients in a large bowl, ensuring the chicken is well coated.
  2. Taste and adjust the seasoning, adding more curry powder if desired.
  3. Serve as a sandwich filling or on lettuce leaves for a low-carb option.

5. BBQ Ranch Chicken Salad

This smoky and creamy BBQ Ranch chicken salad is a crowd-pleasing recipe that’s both affordable and fuss-free.

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups shredded cooked chicken
  • 1/4 cup ranch dressing
  • 1/4 cup BBQ sauce
  • 1/4 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1/4 cup diced tomatoes
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Combine the chicken, ranch dressing, BBQ sauce, cheddar cheese, and tomatoes in a bowl.
  2. Stir until well mixed.
  3. Taste and add salt and pepper as needed.
  4. Enjoy in a sandwich, wrap, or on its own!

6. Asian-Inspired Chicken Salad

This Asian-inspired chicken salad blends savory and tangy flavors, making it a refreshing choice for lunch or dinner.

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups shredded cooked chicken
  • 1/2 cup shredded cabbage
  • 1/4 cup grated carrots
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons rice vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon sesame oil
  • 1 teaspoon honey
  • 1/4 cup chopped scallions
  • Sesame seeds for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Mix soy sauce, rice vinegar, sesame oil, and honey in a small bowl to make the dressing.
  2. In a larger bowl, toss chicken, cabbage, carrots, and scallions.
  3. Pour the dressing over the salad and mix well.
  4. Sprinkle sesame seeds on top and serve chilled.

7. Buffalo Chicken Salad

If you love the kick of Buffalo sauce, this chicken salad recipe is calling your name. Itâ€™s perfect for game day or a quick snack.

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups shredded cooked chicken
  • 1/4 cup hot sauce (Buffalo-style)
  • 1/4 cup ranch or blue cheese dressing
  • 1/4 cup diced celery
  • 2 tablespoons crumbled blue cheese (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Combine shredded chicken, hot sauce, ranch or blue cheese dressing, and celery in a bowl.
  2. Stir until fully combined.
  3. Top with crumbled blue cheese if desired and serve with crackers or celery sticks.

8. Honey Mustard Chicken Salad

Sweet and tangy, this honey mustard chicken salad is a delicious option for when youâ€™re craving something different.

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups diced cooked chicken
  • 1/4 cup honey mustard dressing
  • 1/4 cup chopped green onions
  • 1/4 cup diced crunchy vegetables (like cucumbers or celery)
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Toss all ingredients together in a bowl until well coated.
  2. Adjust seasoning as needed and serve as a sandwich filling or over greens.

9. Pesto Chicken Salad

Turn your leftover pesto into a bright and herby chicken salad. This flavorful recipe is sure to impress.

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups shredded cooked chicken
  • 1/4 cup pesto
  • 2 tablespoons mayonnaise or Greek yogurt
  • 1/4 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1 tablespoon grated Parmesan cheese

Instructions:

  1. Mix together the pesto and mayonnaise in a small bowl.
  2. Toss the chicken, cherry tomatoes, and Parmesan in a separate bowl.
  3. Add the pesto mixture to the chicken and stir until evenly coated.
  4. Serve on bread rolls, over a salad, or enjoy as is.

10. Avocado Chicken Salad

Add some creamy, nutrient-rich avocado to your chicken salad for a fresh and satisfying dish.

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups shredded cooked chicken
  • 1 ripe avocado, mashed
  • 1/4 cup diced red onion
  • 1/4 cup diced tomatoes
  • Juice of 1/2 lime
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a large bowl, mash the avocado until smooth.
  2. Add shredded chicken, red onion, tomatoes, and lime juice to the bowl.
  3. Mix until all ingredients are well combined.
  4. Season with salt and pepper to taste, and serve immediately.
