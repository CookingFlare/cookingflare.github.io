BBQ Shredded Chicken: Easy and Versatile Recipes for Sandwiches


Are you tired of the same old sandwiches for lunch? Looking for a quick and easy way to add some excitement to your meals? Look no further than BBQ shredded chicken! With its smoky flavor and tender texture, BBQ shredded chicken is a versatile ingredient that can be used in a variety of delicious sandwich recipes. In this blog post, we’ll explore some easy and mouth-watering recipes that will take your sandwich game to the next level.

Classic BBQ Chicken Sandwich

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups cooked and shredded chicken
  • 1 cup BBQ sauce
  • 4 hamburger buns
  • Optional toppings: sliced onions, pickles, coleslaw

Instructions:

  1. In a saucepan over medium heat, combine the shredded chicken and BBQ sauce. Stir well to coat the chicken evenly.
  2. Cook for 5-7 minutes, or until the chicken is heated through.
  3. Toast the hamburger buns lightly, if desired.
  4. Divide the BBQ chicken evenly among the buns.
  5. Top with your favorite toppings, such as sliced onions, pickles, or coleslaw.
  6. Serve warm and enjoy!

BBQ Chicken Wrap

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups cooked and shredded chicken
  • 1 cup BBQ sauce
  • 4 large tortillas
  • 1 cup shredded lettuce
  • 1/2 cup diced tomatoes
  • 1/2 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • Ranch or blue cheese dressing for drizzling

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, mix the shredded chicken with BBQ sauce until well coated.
  2. Lay out the tortillas and divide the BBQ chicken evenly among them.
  3. Top each tortilla with shredded lettuce, diced tomatoes, and shredded cheddar cheese.
  4. Drizzle with your choice of dressing.
  5. Roll up the tortillas tightly, tucking in the sides as you go.
  6. Slice the wraps in half and serve.

BBQ Chicken Sliders

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups cooked and shredded chicken
  • 1 cup BBQ sauce
  • 12 slider buns
  • Sliced cheese (optional)
  • Pickles (optional)

Instructions:

  1. In a large skillet, combine the shredded chicken and BBQ sauce over medium heat. Stir well to coat the chicken evenly.
  2. Cook for 5-7 minutes, or until the chicken is heated through.
  3. Toast the slider buns lightly, if desired.
  4. Divide the BBQ chicken evenly among the buns.
  5. Add sliced cheese and pickles, if desired.
  6. Serve warm and enjoy!

BBQ Chicken Panini

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups cooked and shredded chicken
  • 1 cup BBQ sauce
  • 8 slices of bread
  • Sliced cheese (cheddar, mozzarella, or pepper jack)
  • Butter or cooking spray for grilling

Instructions:

  1. In a mixing bowl, combine the shredded chicken with BBQ sauce until well coated.
  2. Preheat a panini press or grill pan over medium heat.
  3. Assemble the sandwiches by placing shredded chicken and sliced cheese between two slices of bread.
  4. Lightly butter the outer sides of the bread or spray with cooking spray.
  5. Grill the sandwiches in the panini press or grill pan until the bread is golden and crispy, and the cheese is melted.
  6. Slice the paninis in half and serve hot.

BBQ shredded chicken is a game-changer when it comes to sandwiches. Its bold and smoky flavors elevate any recipe, making it the perfect choice for a quick and delicious meal. Whether you prefer a classic sandwich, a wrap, sliders, or a panini, these easy and versatile recipes are sure to satisfy your cravings. So grab some BBQ sauce, cook up some shredded chicken, and get ready to enjoy some seriously tasty sandwiches!
