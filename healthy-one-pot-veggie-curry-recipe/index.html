Healthy One Pot Veggie Curry Recipe


Are you looking for a delicious and healthy meal that’s easy to make and packed with flavor? Look no further than this one pot veggie curry recipe! Whether you’re a vegetarian, vegan, or just looking to incorporate more plant-based meals into your diet, this recipe is sure to become a staple in your kitchen. Not only is it incredibly satisfying, but it’s also a great way to get a variety of vegetables into your diet.

Ingredients:

  • 2 tablespoons of coconut oil
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon of grated fresh ginger
  • 1 tablespoon of curry powder
  • 1 teaspoon of ground turmeric
  • 1 teaspoon of ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon of ground coriander
  • 1/2 teaspoon of red pepper flakes (adjust to taste)
  • 1 can of coconut milk
  • 1 cup of vegetable broth
  • 1 cup of diced sweet potatoes
  • 1 cup of diced carrots
  • 1 cup of cauliflower florets
  • 1 cup of green beans, trimmed and halved
  • 1 cup of diced bell peppers
  • 1 cup of frozen peas
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh cilantro for garnish

Instructions:

  1. In a large pot, heat the coconut oil over medium heat. Add the chopped onion and sauté until translucent, about 5 minutes.

  2. Add the minced garlic and grated ginger to the pot, and sauté for an additional 2 minutes.

  3. Stir in the curry powder, turmeric, cumin, coriander, and red pepper flakes, and cook for another 2 minutes, stirring constantly to toast the spices.

  4. Pour in the coconut milk and vegetable broth, and bring the mixture to a gentle simmer.

  5. Add the diced sweet potatoes, carrots, and cauliflower to the pot, and cook for 10-15 minutes, or until the vegetables are just beginning to soften.

  6. Stir in the green beans, bell peppers, and frozen peas, and continue to simmer for an additional 5-7 minutes, until all the vegetables are tender.

  7. Season the curry with salt and pepper to taste, and adjust the seasoning as needed.

  8. Serve the veggie curry hot, garnished with fresh cilantro, and enjoy with steamed rice or naan bread.

Why This Recipe?

This one pot veggie curry recipe is not only incredibly delicious, but it’s also packed with nutrient-rich vegetables and aromatic spices. The combination of turmeric, cumin, and coriander not only adds depth of flavor but also provides anti-inflammatory and antioxidant properties. Additionally, the coconut milk adds a creamy richness to the curry while keeping it dairy-free.

One of the best things about this recipe is its versatility. You can easily customize it based on the vegetables you have on hand or your personal preferences. Feel free to add in some spinach, eggplant, or even chickpeas for added protein.

Moreover, this recipe is perfect for meal prep. It keeps well in the refrigerator for several days, and the flavors actually develop and intensify over time, making it a great option for busy weeknights.

So, whether you’re cooking for a crowd or just for yourself, this healthy one pot veggie curry is a surefire way to delight your taste buds and nourish your body at the same time. Give it a try and watch it become a beloved go-to in your recipe collection!

With its vibrant mix of vegetables and aromatic spices, this one pot veggie curry recipe is a fantastic addition to any healthy eating plan. Whether you’re a seasoned chef or a novice cook, this recipe is sure to impress with its simplicity and bold flavors. Enjoy the delightful experience of cooking and savoring this wholesome dish!
