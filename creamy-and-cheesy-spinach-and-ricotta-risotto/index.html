Creamy and Cheesy Spinach and Ricotta Risotto


Risotto, with its creamy texture and versatility, is a beloved dish in Italian cuisine. Today, we’re diving into a delightful variation that combines the richness of ricotta cheese with the earthy flavors of spinach, resulting in a dish that’s as comforting as it is satisfying. This Creamy and Cheesy Spinach and Ricotta Risotto is perfect for a cozy dinner at home or a gathering with friends. Let’s get started on crafting this delicious meal together.

Ingredients

For the Risotto:

  • 1 ½ cups Arborio rice
  • 4 cups vegetable or chicken broth
  • 1 cup dry white wine
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 cups fresh spinach, chopped
  • 1 cup ricotta cheese
  • ½ cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

For Garnish (optional):

  • Extra grated Parmesan cheese
  • Freshly chopped parsley

Instructions

Step 1: Prepare the Broth

  1. Start by heating the vegetable or chicken broth in a saucepan over low heat. You want to keep it warm throughout the cooking process.

Step 2: Sauté the Aromatics

  1. In a large, heavy-bottomed pot or Dutch oven, heat the olive oil and 1 tablespoon of butter over medium heat.
  2. Add the chopped onion and sauté for about 3-4 minutes until it becomes translucent.
  3. Stir in the minced garlic and cook for another minute until fragrant.

Step 3: Toast the Rice

  1. Add the Arborio rice to the pot with the onions and garlic. Stir continuously for 1-2 minutes until the rice becomes lightly toasted and opaque.

Step 4: Deglaze with Wine

  1. Pour in the white wine and stir gently. Allow the rice to cook until the wine is absorbed completely, stirring occasionally.

Step 5: Cook the Risotto

  1. Begin adding the warm broth to the rice mixture, one ladleful at a time. Allow each addition of broth to be absorbed before adding the next, stirring frequently. This process will take about 18-20 minutes. The rice should be tender but still slightly firm (al dente) when done.

Step 6: Add Spinach and Cheeses

  1. When the risotto is almost done, stir in the chopped spinach and cook for another 2-3 minutes until the spinach is wilted.
  2. Remove the pot from heat. Stir in the ricotta cheese, grated Parmesan cheese, and the remaining tablespoon of butter until everything is well combined and creamy.
  3. Season with salt and pepper to taste.

Step 7: Serve

  1. Spoon the creamy spinach and ricotta risotto onto serving plates or into bowls.
  2. If desired, garnish with extra grated Parmesan cheese and freshly chopped parsley.
  3. Serve immediately while warm and enjoy the rich, creamy flavors of this comforting dish.

Tips for Perfect Risotto

  • Consistency: Risotto should have a creamy consistency, achieved by adding broth gradually and stirring constantly.
  • Rice Quality: Arborio rice is ideal for risotto due to its high starch content, which creates that creamy texture.
  • Variations: Feel free to add other vegetables or proteins such as mushrooms or grilled chicken to customize your risotto.

Final Thoughts

This Creamy and Cheesy Spinach and Ricotta Risotto is a dish that embodies the heartiness and warmth of homemade comfort food. With its creamy texture and rich flavors, it’s sure to become a favorite in your culinary repertoire. Whether you’re cooking for a cozy night in or entertaining guests, this risotto promises to impress with its delicious combination of spinach, ricotta cheese, and Parmesan. Enjoy the process of cooking this comforting dish and savor every creamy bite.

Now that you have the recipe at your fingertips, gather your ingredients and give it a try. Embrace the Italian tradition of risotto-making and delight in the delightful flavors that await. Bon appétit!
