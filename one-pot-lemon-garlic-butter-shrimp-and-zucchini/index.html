One Pot Lemon Garlic Butter Shrimp and Zucchini


If you’re looking for a quick and delicious weeknight dinner that’s bursting with flavor, then you’ve come to the right place. This one-pot lemon garlic butter shrimp and zucchini dish is a perfect combination of succulent shrimp, tender zucchini, and a rich, flavorful sauce that comes together in just one pot, making cleanup a breeze. Let’s dive into the recipe and explore how to create this mouthwatering dish in your own kitchen.

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound of large shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 2 medium zucchinis, sliced into half moons
  • 4 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1/4 cup of chicken broth
  • 2 tablespoons of unsalted butter
  • 1 tablespoon of olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon of lemon zest
  • 2 tablespoons of freshly squeezed lemon juice
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • Fresh chopped parsley for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the Shrimp:

    • Start by patting the shrimp dry with paper towels to ensure they cook evenly. Season the shrimp with salt and black pepper to taste.

  2. Sear the Shrimp:

    • In a large skillet, heat the olive oil over medium-high heat. Add the seasoned shrimp to the skillet and cook for 1-2 minutes on each side until they turn pink and opaque. Once cooked, transfer the shrimp to a plate and set aside.

  3. Saute the Zucchini:

    • In the same skillet, add the sliced zucchini and minced garlic. Saute for 3-4 minutes until the zucchini is tender and the garlic is fragrant.

  4. Create the Sauce:

    • Deglaze the skillet with the chicken broth, scraping up any browned bits from the bottom of the pan. Add the butter, lemon zest, and lemon juice to the skillet, stirring until the butter is melted and the sauce is well combined.

  5. Combine the Ingredients:

    • Return the cooked shrimp to the skillet, tossing them in the flavorful lemon garlic butter sauce. Allow the shrimp to reheat for a minute or two while coating them with the sauce.

  6. Serve and Garnish:

    • Once the shrimp is heated through, remove the skillet from the heat. Garnish the dish with freshly chopped parsley for a pop of color and added freshness.

  7. Enjoy!

    • Your one-pot lemon garlic butter shrimp and zucchini is ready to be served. This dish pairs beautifully with a side of rice, pasta, or crusty bread to soak up the delicious sauce.

Tips and Variations:

  • If you prefer a bit of heat, consider adding a pinch of red pepper flakes to the sauce for a spicy kick.
  • For a low-carb option, substitute zucchini noodles for the sliced zucchini to create a light and refreshing alternative.
  • Experiment with different herbs and seasonings such as thyme, basil, or a sprinkle of Parmesan cheese to customize the flavors to your liking.

Final Thoughts:

This one-pot lemon garlic butter shrimp and zucchini recipe is a fantastic way to elevate your weeknight cooking without spending hours in the kitchen. The combination of tangy lemon, savory garlic, and succulent shrimp creates a dish that is both elegant and approachable. With minimal cleanup and maximum flavor, it’s a winning recipe for busy individuals and families alike. Give it a try and savor the delightful flavors of this easy and impressive dish!
