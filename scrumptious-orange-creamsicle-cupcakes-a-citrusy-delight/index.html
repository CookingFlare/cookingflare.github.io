Scrumptious Orange Creamsicle Cupcakes: A Citrusy Delight


If you’re a fan of citrusy treats that remind you of summertime, then these Scrumptious Orange Creamsicle Cupcakes are a must-try! Bursting with the refreshing flavors of orange and cream, these cupcakes will transport you to a sunny paradise with every bite. The combination of tangy orange zest, velvety smooth cream, and moist cake is a match made in heaven. So, let’s dive right into the recipe and get ready to indulge in this citrusy delight!

Ingredients

For the cupcakes:

  • 1 ½ cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 ½ teaspoons baking powder
  • ¼ teaspoon salt
  • ½ cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 tablespoon orange zest
  • ½ cup freshly squeezed orange juice
  • ½ cup whole milk

For the cream filling:

  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 2 tablespoons powdered sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 tablespoon orange zest

For the orange cream cheese frosting:

  • 8 ounces cream cheese, softened
  • ½ cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 4 cups powdered sugar
  • 2 tablespoons freshly squeezed orange juice
  • 1 tablespoon orange zest

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C) and line a muffin tin with cupcake liners.

  2. In a medium-sized bowl, whisk together the flour, baking powder, and salt. Set aside.

  3. In a separate large bowl, cream together the softened butter and granulated sugar until light and fluffy. This will take about 2-3 minutes using an electric mixer.

  4. Beat in the eggs, one at a time, ensuring each egg is fully incorporated before adding the next. Then, mix in the vanilla extract and orange zest.

  5. Gradually add the dry ingredients to the wet ingredients, alternating with the orange juice and milk. Begin and end with the dry ingredients, mixing until just combined. Be careful not to overmix, as this can result in dense cupcakes.

  6. Fill each cupcake liner about 2/3 full with the batter. Bake for 18-20 minutes or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.

  7. While the cupcakes are baking, prepare the cream filling. In a chilled bowl, whip the heavy cream, powdered sugar, vanilla extract, and orange zest until soft peaks form. Set aside.

  8. Once the cupcakes are done baking, remove them from the oven and let them cool completely on a wire rack.

  9. Once cooled, use a cupcake corer or a small paring knife to remove the center of each cupcake, creating a well for the cream filling.

  10. Fill each cupcake well with a generous dollop of the cream filling, making sure not to overfill.

  11. For the orange cream cheese frosting, beat the softened cream cheese and butter together until smooth and creamy. Gradually add the powdered sugar, orange juice, and orange zest, mixing until well combined.

  12. Pipe or spread the orange cream cheese frosting onto each cupcake, covering the cream filling.

  13. For an extra touch, garnish each cupcake with a sprinkle of orange zest.

  14. Serve and enjoy these Scrumptious Orange Creamsicle Cupcakes as a delightful treat for any occasion!

Conclusion

These Scrumptious Orange Creamsicle Cupcakes are a citrusy delight that will satisfy your cravings for a refreshing and indulgent dessert. The combination of tangy orange zest, creamy filling, and fluffy frosting creates a symphony of flavors that will leave you wanting more. Whether you’re hosting a summer party or simply treating yourself to a sweet treat, these cupcakes are sure to impress. So, grab your apron and get ready to bake these mouthwatering delights - your taste buds will thank you!
