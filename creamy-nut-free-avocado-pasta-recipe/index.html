Creamy Nut-Free Avocado Pasta Recipe


If you’re looking for a delicious and nutritious pasta recipe that’s also nut-free, then you’ve come to the right place. This creamy avocado pasta is not only easy to make but also incredibly satisfying. Avocado provides the creamy texture without the need for any nuts, making it a perfect option for those with nut allergies or anyone looking for a healthier alternative to traditional creamy pasta dishes.

Ingredients

  • 2 ripe avocados
  • 1/2 cup fresh basil leaves
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon fresh lemon juice
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • 1 pound of your favorite pasta

Instructions

Step 1: Prepare the Avocado Sauce

  1. Cut the avocados in half, remove the pits, and scoop the flesh into a food processor or blender.
  2. Add the fresh basil leaves, minced garlic, olive oil, lemon juice, salt, and pepper to the food processor or blender.

Step 2: Blend Until Smooth

  1. Blend the ingredients until the mixture is smooth and creamy, scraping down the sides as needed to ensure everything is well combined.

Step 3: Cook the Pasta

  1. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil.
  2. Add the pasta and cook according to the package instructions until al dente.
  3. Once the pasta is cooked, reserve about 1/2 cup of the pasta water and then drain the pasta.

Step 4: Combine the Pasta and Sauce

  1. In a large mixing bowl, toss the cooked pasta with the creamy avocado sauce, adding a splash of the reserved pasta water as needed to thin out the sauce and help it coat the pasta evenly.

Step 5: Serve and Enjoy

  1. Divide the creamy nut-free avocado pasta among serving plates.
  2. Garnish with additional basil leaves, a drizzle of olive oil, and freshly cracked black pepper, if desired.
  3. Serve immediately and enjoy this delicious and nutritious meal!

Why Choose This Recipe?

This creamy nut-free avocado pasta recipe is not only a delightful alternative to traditional creamy pasta dishes, but it’s also a great way to incorporate healthy fats and fresh ingredients into your meal. Avocados are packed with nutrients, including heart-healthy monounsaturated fats, fiber, and various vitamins and minerals. By using avocados as the base for the creamy sauce, you’re not only avoiding nuts but also adding a rich, luscious texture to the pasta without the need for heavy cream or cheese.

Additionally, this recipe is incredibly versatile. You can customize it to suit your taste preferences by adding extras such as cherry tomatoes, grilled chicken, or sautéed vegetables. It’s also a great way to introduce more plant-based meals into your diet, as the creaminess of the avocado sauce makes it a satisfying option for both vegetarians and vegans.

Final Thoughts

Creamy nut-free avocado pasta is a simple yet elegant dish that’s perfect for a quick weeknight dinner or a gathering with friends. The vibrant green color and fresh flavors make it a crowd-pleaser, and the fact that it’s nut-free and packed with nutrients is an added bonus. Give this recipe a try, and you’ll discover a new go-to dish that’s both delicious and wholesome. Enjoy!
