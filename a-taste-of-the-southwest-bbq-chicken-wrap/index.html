A Taste of the Southwest: BBQ Chicken Wrap


If you’re looking for a delicious and satisfying dish that embodies the flavors of the Southwest, then look no further than the BBQ chicken wrap. This mouthwatering recipe combines the smoky, tangy flavors of barbecue sauce with tender grilled chicken, crisp vegetables, and creamy dressing, all wrapped up in a warm tortilla. Whether you’re hosting a summer cookout, packing a picnic, or simply craving a flavorful meal, this BBQ chicken wrap is sure to hit the spot.

Ingredients:

For the BBQ Chicken:

  • 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1 cup of your favorite BBQ sauce
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

For the Wrap:

  • 4 large flour tortillas
  • 1 cup shredded lettuce
  • 1 cup diced tomatoes
  • 1/2 cup diced red onion
  • 1 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1/2 cup ranch or blue cheese dressing

Instructions:

  1. Begin by marinating the chicken breasts in 1/2 cup of BBQ sauce for at least 30 minutes. This will infuse the chicken with the rich flavors of the sauce.

  2. Preheat your grill or grill pan over medium-high heat. Brush the chicken breasts with olive oil and season with salt and pepper.

  3. Grill the chicken for 6-7 minutes on each side, or until it is cooked through and has beautiful grill marks. Baste the chicken with the remaining 1/2 cup of BBQ sauce during the last few minutes of grilling.

  4. Once the chicken is cooked, transfer it to a cutting board and let it rest for a few minutes before slicing it into thin strips.

  5. To assemble the wraps, warm the tortillas in a dry skillet or in the microwave for a few seconds to make them more pliable.

  6. Spread a generous dollop of ranch or blue cheese dressing down the center of each tortilla.

  7. Arrange a portion of shredded lettuce, diced tomatoes, and red onion on top of the dressing.

  8. Place a portion of the sliced BBQ chicken on top of the vegetables, and then sprinkle with shredded cheddar cheese.

  9. To wrap the BBQ chicken wraps, fold in the sides of the tortilla, and then roll it up tightly from the bottom, tucking in the filling as you go.

  10. Slice the wraps in half on a diagonal and serve immediately, or wrap them tightly in foil for a picnic or on-the-go meal.

Serving Suggestions:

These BBQ chicken wraps are versatile and can be served with a variety of sides to complement the flavors of the Southwest. Consider pairing them with a crisp green salad, corn on the cob, or a side of tangy coleslaw. For a heartier meal, serve the wraps with a side of seasoned sweet potato fries or grilled corn on the cob brushed with lime and chili butter.

Conclusion:

The BBQ chicken wrap is a delightful fusion of smoky, zesty, and creamy flavors that capture the essence of Southwest cuisine. Whether you’re enjoying it at a summer gathering, a family dinner, or a casual lunch, this recipe is sure to be a crowd-pleaser. With its simple preparation and bold flavors, the BBQ chicken wrap is a fantastic way to savor the taste of the Southwest in every delicious bite.
