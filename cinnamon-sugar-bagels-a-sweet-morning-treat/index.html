Cinnamon Sugar Bagels: A Sweet Morning Treat


There’s something truly magical about the aroma of freshly baked goods wafting through the kitchen in the early hours of the morning. One delightful treat that never fails to bring comfort and joy to the start of the day is a warm, homemade Cinnamon Sugar Bagel. With a crisp exterior, soft interior, and a perfect balance of cinnamon sweetness, these bagels are a delightful indulgence that pairs perfectly with a steaming cup of coffee or tea.

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups warm water
  • 2 1/4 teaspoons (1 packet) active dry yeast
  • 4 cups bread flour
  • 1 tablespoon brown sugar
  • 2 teaspoons cinnamon
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons salt
  • 2 tablespoons granulated sugar
  • 1 egg, beaten

Instructions

  1. Activate the Yeast: In a small bowl, combine the warm water, brown sugar, and yeast. Let it sit for about 5-10 minutes until frothy.

  2. Mix the Dough: In a large mixing bowl, combine the flour, salt, and cinnamon. Pour in the yeast mixture and mix until a dough forms. Knead the dough on a floured surface for about 10 minutes until smooth and elastic.

  3. First Rise: Place the dough in a greased bowl, cover with a damp towel, and let it rise in a warm place for about 1 hour or until doubled in size.

  4. Shape the Bagels: After the dough has risen, punch it down and divide it into 8 equal portions. Shape each portion into a ball, then press your thumb through the center to form a hole. Stretch the dough to create a bagel shape.

  5. Second Rise: Place the shaped bagels on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper. Cover and let them rise for another 30-45 minutes.

  6. Boil the Bagels: Preheat the oven to 425°F (220°C). In a large pot, bring water to a boil and add the granulated sugar. Boil each bagel for about 1-2 minutes per side. Remove and place them back on the baking sheet.

  7. Bake: Brush the bagels with the beaten egg and bake in the preheated oven for 20-25 minutes or until golden brown.

  8. Cinnamon Sugar Coating: In a bowl, mix granulated sugar and cinnamon. While the bagels are still warm, toss them in the cinnamon sugar mixture until coated evenly.

  9. Enjoy!: Let the bagels cool slightly before serving. These Cinnamon Sugar Bagels are best enjoyed fresh and warm, either on their own or with a spread of cream cheese.

Tips and Variations

  • Add Raisins: For a fruity twist, mix in some raisins into the dough before shaping the bagels.
  • Cream Cheese Frosting: Drizzle or spread a creamy cream cheese frosting on top for an extra indulgent treat.
  • Nutty Crunch: Sprinkle chopped nuts like pecans or almonds on top of the cinnamon sugar coating for added texture.

Whether you’re looking to impress your family with a special weekend breakfast or simply craving a cozy morning treat, these homemade Cinnamon Sugar Bagels are sure to delight your senses and fill your home with warmth. Embrace the simple pleasure of baking from scratch and savor the sweet rewards of your efforts. Happy baking and even happier munching!
