Crispy Baked Green Chile Chicken Taquitos for a Spicy Snack


If you’re a fan of Mexican cuisine and love a fiery kick in your snacks, then you’re in for a treat! These Crispy Baked Green Chile Chicken Taquitos are the perfect appetizer or snack for any occasion. Packed with flavor, these taquitos are sure to satisfy your cravings. And the best part? They’re baked, not fried, making them a healthier alternative without compromising on taste. So let’s dive right in and discover how to make these delicious taquitos!

Ingredients

  • 1 pound boneless, skinless chicken breasts, cooked and shredded
  • 1 cup diced green chiles (canned or roasted)
  • 1 cup shredded Monterey Jack cheese
  • 1/2 cup chopped fresh cilantro
  • 1/2 cup diced red onion
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon chili powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 12 small flour tortillas
  • Cooking spray

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 425°F (220°C). Line a baking sheet with parchment paper or lightly grease it with cooking spray.

  2. In a large mixing bowl, combine the shredded chicken, diced green chiles, Monterey Jack cheese, cilantro, red onion, minced garlic, ground cumin, chili powder, salt, and black pepper. Mix well until all the ingredients are evenly combined.

  3. Take a flour tortilla and spoon about 2 tablespoons of the chicken mixture onto one end of the tortilla. Roll it up tightly, ensuring that the filling is secure inside. Place the rolled taquito seam-side down on the prepared baking sheet. Repeat this process with the remaining tortillas and filling.

  4. Lightly spray the taquitos with cooking spray to help them crisp up in the oven. Bake for 15-20 minutes or until the taquitos are golden brown and crispy.

  5. Serve the Crispy Baked Green Chile Chicken Taquitos hot, accompanied by your favorite dipping sauce such as salsa, guacamole, or sour cream. They are perfect for snacking, appetizers, or even as a main dish when paired with a side salad.

Tips and Variations

  • If you prefer a spicier kick, you can add some diced jalapeños or a few dashes of hot sauce to the chicken mixture.
  • Experiment with different types of cheeses like cheddar or pepper jack to add a unique twist to the taquitos.
  • To make the taquitos gluten-free, use corn tortillas instead of flour tortillas.
  • These taquitos can be prepared in advance and frozen. Simply assemble the taquitos, place them on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper, and freeze until firm. Once frozen, transfer them to a freezer-safe bag or container. When ready to bake, simply follow the baking instructions, adding a few extra minutes to the cooking time.

Conclusion

These Crispy Baked Green Chile Chicken Taquitos are a delightful combination of flavors and textures. The tender shredded chicken, spicy green chiles, and melted cheese create a mouthwatering filling that is perfectly encased in a crispy tortilla shell. Whether you’re hosting a party, looking for a quick snack, or simply craving something spicy, these taquitos are a must-try. So gather your ingredients, preheat your oven, and get ready to enjoy this fiery, yet satisfying, snack sensation!
