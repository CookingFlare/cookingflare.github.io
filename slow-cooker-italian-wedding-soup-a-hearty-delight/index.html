Slow Cooker Italian Wedding Soup: A Hearty Delight


Are you in the mood for a delicious and comforting soup that will warm your soul? Look no further than this Slow Cooker Italian Wedding Soup recipe! Packed with flavorful ingredients and cooked to perfection in your trusty slow cooker, this soup is sure to become a family favorite. Whether you’re planning a cozy night in or hosting a dinner party, this hearty delight is a crowd-pleaser that will leave everyone satisfied.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1/2 cup breadcrumbs
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley
  • 1 egg, lightly beaten
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 carrots, peeled and sliced
  • 2 celery stalks, sliced
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 6 cups chicken broth
  • 1 cup small pasta of your choice (such as orzo or ditalini)
  • 4 cups fresh spinach, chopped
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. In a large bowl, combine the ground beef, breadcrumbs, Parmesan cheese, parsley, and egg. Mix well to combine all the ingredients. Shape the mixture into small meatballs, approximately 1 inch in diameter.

  2. Heat a drizzle of olive oil in a skillet over medium heat. Add the meatballs and cook until browned on all sides, about 5-7 minutes. Remove the meatballs from the skillet and set aside.

  3. In the slow cooker, combine the chopped onion, sliced carrots, sliced celery, and minced garlic. Pour in the chicken broth and stir to combine.

  4. Add the meatballs to the slow cooker and season with salt and pepper to taste. Cover and cook on low heat for 6-8 hours or on high heat for 3-4 hours, until the vegetables are tender and the flavors have melded together.

  5. About 30 minutes before serving, stir in the pasta and chopped spinach. Cover and continue cooking until the pasta is al dente.

  6. Taste and adjust the seasoning if needed. Ladle the soup into bowls and serve hot. You can garnish each bowl with additional grated Parmesan cheese and a sprinkle of fresh parsley for added flavor.

Tips for Success

  • For an extra burst of flavor, consider adding a splash of white wine or a sprinkle of Italian seasoning to the soup.
  • If you prefer a thicker soup, you can add a slurry of cornstarch and water during the last 30 minutes of cooking. This will give the soup a slightly thicker consistency.
  • Leftovers can be stored in the refrigerator for up to 3 days or frozen for longer storage. Simply reheat on the stovetop or in the microwave for a quick and satisfying meal.

Final Thoughts

Slow Cooker Italian Wedding Soup is a comforting and hearty dish that brings together the flavors of tender meatballs, vegetables, and pasta in a flavorful broth. The slow cooking process allows the ingredients to meld together, creating a soup that is rich in taste and aroma. Whether you’re a seasoned cook or a beginner in the kitchen, this recipe is easy to follow and will impress anyone who tries it.

So, gather your ingredients, set up your slow cooker, and let it work its magic. In just a few hours, you’ll have a delicious pot of Italian Wedding Soup that will warm your heart and satisfy your cravings. Enjoy this delightful soup on a chilly day or any time you’re in need of some comfort food.
