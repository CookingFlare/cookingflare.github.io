Thai Basil Stir Fry Recipe with Beef and Bell Peppers


If you’re a fan of Thai cuisine, you’re in for a treat with this Thai Basil Stir Fry recipe featuring tender beef and vibrant bell peppers. This dish is a perfect blend of savory, spicy, and aromatic flavors that will surely tantalize your taste buds. Whether you’re a seasoned home cook or just starting out in the kitchen, this recipe is simple to follow and promises a delicious end result. Let’s dive into the details and get cooking!

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound flank steak, thinly sliced
  • 2 tablespoons oyster sauce
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon fish sauce
  • 1 tablespoon sugar
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 shallots, thinly sliced
  • 2-3 Thai chilies, chopped (adjust to taste)
  • 1 red bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 yellow bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 cup fresh Thai basil leaves
  • Cooking oil
  • Steamed jasmine rice, for serving

Instructions:

  1. Marinate the Beef: In a bowl, combine the thinly sliced flank steak with oyster sauce, soy sauce, and fish sauce. Let it marinate for about 15-20 minutes while you prepare the other ingredients.

  2. Prepare the Aromatic Base: Heat a wok or large skillet over medium-high heat and add a splash of cooking oil. Once the oil is hot, add the minced garlic, shallots, and chopped Thai chilies. Stir-fry for a minute until fragrant.

  3. Stir Fry the Beef: Add the marinated beef to the wok and stir-fry for 2-3 minutes until the beef is just cooked through. Be sure not to overcook the beef to keep it tender and juicy.

  4. Add Bell Peppers: Toss in the sliced red and yellow bell peppers and continue stir-frying for another 2-3 minutes until the peppers are slightly softened but still vibrant in color.

  5. Season and Finish: Sprinkle the sugar over the stir-fry and give it a good mix. Finally, add the fresh Thai basil leaves and stir-fry for another minute until the basil wilts and releases its aromatic fragrance.

  6. Serve: Remove the stir-fry from the heat and transfer it to a serving dish. The Thai Basil Stir Fry is best enjoyed hot, served over steamed jasmine rice.

Tips for Success:

  • Slice the Beef Thinly: For the best texture and quick cooking, ensure that the flank steak is thinly sliced against the grain.
  • Adjust Spice Level: Thai chilies can vary in heat, so adjust the quantity based on your preference for spiciness.
  • Fresh Thai Basil: The vibrant flavor of fresh Thai basil is essential for this dish. If unavailable, substitute with sweet basil as a last resort.

Final Thoughts:

This Thai Basil Stir Fry with Beef and Bell Peppers is a delightful dish that brings the authentic flavors of Thailand to your kitchen. The combination of tender beef, crisp bell peppers, and aromatic Thai basil creates a harmony of flavors that is truly irresistible. Whether you’re looking to impress guests or simply treat yourself to a flavorful meal, this recipe is a wonderful addition to your culinary repertoire. So, gather your ingredients and embark on a culinary adventure with this Thai-inspired stir fry!

This recipe is a celebration of Thai cuisine, combining the aromatic flavors of basil, tender beef, and vibrant bell peppers. It’s a simple yet rewarding dish that will elevate your home cooking experience. Whether you’re a fan of Thai food or new to its flavors, this recipe is sure to impress. So, roll up your sleeves and get ready to stir-fry your way to a delicious meal!
