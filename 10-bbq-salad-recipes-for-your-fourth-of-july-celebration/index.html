10 BBQ Salad Recipes for Your Fourth of July Celebration


The Fourth of July is the perfect time for family and friends to gather, enjoy great food, and celebrate the summer. One of the most iconic parts of this holiday is the BBQ, where sizzling meats and smoky flavors take center stage. However, no BBQ is complete without a variety of refreshing and vibrant salads that complement the bold flavors of the grill.

Salads can bring a burst of color and texture to your table, offering a perfect balance to the richness of BBQ meats. Whether you’re hosting a big backyard cookout or attending a casual gathering, these 10 BBQ salad recipes will elevate your celebration and impress your guests.

1. Grilled Corn and Avocado Salad

A fresh, summery salad packed with smoky and creamy flavors, this grilled corn and avocado salad will be a hit at your BBQ. The grilled corn brings out a sweet smokiness that pairs beautifully with the buttery avocado. Toss in some red onion, cherry tomatoes, and a zesty cilantro-lime dressing, and you have a vibrant and satisfying salad.

Ingredients:

  • 4 ears of corn, husked
  • 2 ripe avocados, diced
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1/4 red onion, finely chopped
  • 1/4 cup cilantro, chopped
  • Juice of 2 limes
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Directions:

  1. Preheat your grill to medium-high heat.
  2. Place the corn on the grill and cook, turning occasionally, until lightly charred (about 8-10 minutes).
  3. Remove the corn from the grill and let it cool slightly before cutting the kernels off the cob.
  4. In a large bowl, combine the grilled corn, diced avocados, cherry tomatoes, and red onion.
  5. Drizzle with lime juice, olive oil, and toss everything together. Add cilantro and season with salt and pepper to taste.

2. BBQ Chicken Caesar Salad

Take the classic Caesar salad to the next level by adding tender, smoky BBQ chicken. This hearty and flavorful salad is a great way to serve up protein while still keeping things light and fresh. The rich Caesar dressing paired with grilled chicken creates the perfect balance of smoky and creamy.

Ingredients:

  • 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 2 tbsp BBQ sauce
  • 6 cups romaine lettuce, chopped
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/2 cup croutons
  • 1/4 cup Caesar dressing

Directions:

  1. Preheat your grill to medium-high heat.
  2. Brush the chicken breasts with BBQ sauce and season with salt and pepper.
  3. Grill the chicken for 6-8 minutes per side, or until fully cooked.
  4. Let the chicken rest for a few minutes before slicing it into strips.
  5. In a large bowl, combine the romaine lettuce, Parmesan cheese, croutons, and Caesar dressing.
  6. Top the salad with the sliced BBQ chicken and toss to combine. Serve immediately.

3. Grilled Peach and Burrata Salad

This light and refreshing salad is the perfect blend of sweet and savory. Grilled peaches add a caramelized sweetness that pairs wonderfully with the creamy burrata cheese. The addition of arugula and a balsamic glaze ties everything together, creating a salad that feels both elegant and easy.

Ingredients:

  • 4 peaches, halved and pitted
  • 8 oz burrata cheese
  • 4 cups arugula
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tbsp honey
  • 1/4 cup balsamic glaze
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Directions:

  1. Preheat your grill to medium-high heat.
  2. Brush the cut sides of the peaches with olive oil and season with salt and pepper.
  3. Grill the peaches for 4-5 minutes, until they have nice grill marks and are slightly softened.
  4. Arrange the arugula on a platter and top with the grilled peach halves and burrata cheese.
  5. Drizzle with honey and balsamic glaze, then season with salt and pepper before serving.

4. Southwest Black Bean Salad

This hearty, protein-packed salad is a crowd-pleaser at any BBQ. The combination of black beans, corn, tomatoes, and a spicy lime dressing creates a bold and zesty flavor profile. It’s a great side dish for BBQ meats or can even serve as a light vegetarian main dish.

Ingredients:

  • 2 cans black beans, drained and rinsed
  • 1 cup corn kernels (fresh or frozen)
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1/4 cup red onion, finely diced
  • 1/4 cup cilantro, chopped
  • 1 jalapeno, finely diced
  • Juice of 2 limes
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp cumin
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Directions:

  1. In a large bowl, combine the black beans, corn, cherry tomatoes, red onion, cilantro, and jalapeno.
  2. In a small bowl, whisk together the lime juice, olive oil, cumin, salt, and pepper.
  3. Pour the dressing over the salad and toss until well combined. Chill for 30 minutes before serving to allow the flavors to meld together.

5. Grilled Vegetable Salad with Feta

For a more substantial vegetarian option, this grilled vegetable salad is full of flavor and texture. The smoky grilled vegetables combined with creamy feta and a tangy vinaigrette create a satisfying dish that’s perfect alongside BBQ meats.

Ingredients:

  • 1 zucchini, sliced into rounds
  • 1 red bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 yellow bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 red onion, sliced into rings
  • 1/2 cup feta cheese, crumbled
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tbsp balsamic vinegar
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Directions:

  1. Preheat your grill to medium heat.
  2. Toss the zucchini, bell peppers, and onion in olive oil, balsamic vinegar, oregano, salt, and pepper.
  3. Grill the vegetables for 4-5 minutes per side, until they are charred and tender.
  4. Once the vegetables are grilled, slice them into bite-sized pieces and place them in a large bowl.
  5. Toss with crumbled feta and serve warm or at room temperature.

6. Caprese Pasta Salad

If you’re looking to add some carbs to your BBQ spread, a Caprese pasta salad is a delicious and refreshing option. The combination of pasta, fresh tomatoes, mozzarella, and basil is a classic that never goes out of style. Toss it with a light balsamic vinaigrette for a burst of flavor.

Ingredients:

  • 8 oz pasta (penne or rotini works best)
  • 1 pint cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 8 oz fresh mozzarella balls, halved
  • 1/4 cup fresh basil, chopped
  • 1/4 cup balsamic vinaigrette
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Directions:

  1. Cook the pasta according to package instructions. Drain and rinse with cold water to cool.
  2. In a large bowl, combine the cooled pasta, cherry tomatoes, mozzarella, and basil.
  3. Drizzle with balsamic vinaigrette and toss gently to coat. Season with salt and pepper and serve chilled.

7. Cucumber and Tomato Salad with Dill

This crisp and refreshing salad is perfect for hot summer days when you want something light and cooling. The combination of cucumbers and tomatoes is classic, while the fresh dill adds a fragrant touch that makes this salad stand out.

Ingredients:

  • 2 cucumbers, sliced
  • 2 cups cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1/4 red onion, thinly sliced
  • 2 tbsp fresh dill, chopped
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tbsp white wine vinegar
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Directions:

  1. In a large bowl, combine the cucumber, cherry tomatoes, and red onion.
  2. Drizzle with olive oil and white wine vinegar.
  3. Toss gently and sprinkle with fresh dill, salt, and pepper. Serve immediately.

8. Greek Salad with Grilled Chicken

A classic Greek salad is always a good choice for a BBQ, and adding grilled chicken makes it a complete and satisfying dish. The salty olives, crisp cucumbers, and creamy feta are perfectly complemented by the smoky grilled chicken, making this salad a hearty and delicious side.

Ingredients:

  • 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1/2 cup Kalamata olives
  • 1 cucumber, diced
  • 1/2 red onion, thinly sliced
  • 1/2 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1/4 cup feta cheese, crumbled
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tbsp red wine vinegar
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Directions:

  1. Preheat your grill to medium-high heat.
  2. Season the chicken breasts with olive oil, salt, and pepper, then grill for 6-8 minutes per side, until fully cooked.
  3. Let the chicken rest for a few minutes before slicing it into strips.
  4. In a large bowl, combine the olives, cucumber, red onion, tomatoes, and feta.
  5. Add the sliced chicken and drizzle with olive oil and red wine vinegar. Toss everything together and serve.

9. Watermelon and Feta Salad

If you’re looking for a light and refreshing salad that captures the essence of summer, this watermelon and feta salad is a must-try. The juicy watermelon, tangy feta, and fresh mint create a vibrant dish that pairs perfectly with BBQ meats or can even stand alone as a light meal.

Ingredients:

  • 4 cups watermelon, cubed
  • 1/2 cup feta cheese, crumbled
  • 2 tbsp fresh mint, chopped
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • Juice of 1 lime
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Directions:

  1. In a large bowl, combine the watermelon cubes, feta, and mint.
  2. Drizzle with olive oil and lime juice.
  3. Toss gently and season with salt and pepper. Serve chilled.

10. Coleslaw with a Twist

Coleslaw is a BBQ classic, but this version adds a fun twist by incorporating apples and walnuts. The sweet apples balance out the tangy dressing, while the crunch of the walnuts adds texture. This unique take on coleslaw will be a hit at your next Fourth of July gathering.

Ingredients:

  • 4 cups shredded cabbage
  • 1 apple, julienned
  • 1/4 cup walnuts, chopped
  • 1/4 cup mayonnaise
  • 1 tbsp apple cider vinegar
  • 1 tbsp Dijon mustard
  • 1 tbsp honey
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Directions:

  1. In a large bowl, combine the shredded cabbage, apple, and walnuts.
  2. In a small bowl, whisk together the mayonnaise, apple cider vinegar, Dijon mustard, honey, salt, and pepper.
  3. Pour the dressing over the cabbage mixture and toss until everything is well coated. Serve immediately or refrigerate for up to an hour before serving.

With these 10 BBQ salad recipes, you’re sure to have a spread that will make your Fourth of July celebration even more memorable. Whether you’re grilling up some burgers or enjoying a plate of ribs, these vibrant salads will add the perfect balance to your summer feast. Enjoy!
