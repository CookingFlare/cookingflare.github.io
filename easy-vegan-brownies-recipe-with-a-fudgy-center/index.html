Easy Vegan Brownies Recipe with a Fudgy Center


If you’re craving a decadent dessert that’s vegan-friendly, look no further than these easy vegan brownies with a delightfully fudgy center. This recipe is a game-changer for anyone who wants to indulge in a rich, chocolatey treat without using any animal products. Whether you’re a seasoned vegan or just looking to explore plant-based baking, these brownies are sure to satisfy your sweet tooth and impress your friends and family.

Ingredients

For the Brownies:

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/2 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup unsweetened applesauce
  • 1/4 cup coconut oil, melted
  • 1/4 cup almond milk
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

For the Fudgy Center:

  • 1/2 cup dairy-free chocolate chips
  • 1/4 cup almond milk

Instructions

  1. Preheat the Oven: Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C) and lightly grease an 8x8 inch baking pan.

  2. Prepare the Fudgy Center: In a small saucepan, combine the dairy-free chocolate chips and almond milk. Heat over low heat, stirring constantly, until the chocolate chips are completely melted and the mixture is smooth. Set aside to cool slightly.

  3. Mix the Dry Ingredients: In a large mixing bowl, whisk together the all-purpose flour, granulated sugar, cocoa powder, baking powder, and salt.

  4. Add the Wet Ingredients: Add the unsweetened applesauce, melted coconut oil, almond milk, and vanilla extract to the dry ingredients. Stir until well combined and no lumps remain.

  5. Assemble the Brownies: Pour half of the brownie batter into the prepared baking pan, spreading it evenly. Then, drizzle the fudgy center mixture over the batter in the pan. Carefully spread the remaining brownie batter over the fudgy center.

  6. Bake and Cool: Place the baking pan in the preheated oven and bake for 25-30 minutes, or until the edges are set and the center is slightly firm to the touch. Allow the brownies to cool completely in the pan before slicing and serving.

  7. Enjoy!: Once cooled, slice the brownies into squares and serve. These fudgy vegan brownies are best enjoyed with a glass of almond milk or a scoop of dairy-free ice cream.

Tips for Success

  • For an extra indulgent twist, sprinkle some dairy-free chocolate chips or chopped nuts on top of the brownie batter before baking.
  • Make sure to cool the brownies completely before slicing to achieve the perfect fudgy texture.
  • Store any leftover brownies in an airtight container at room temperature for up to 3 days, or in the refrigerator for up to a week.

Conclusion

These easy vegan brownies with a fudgy center are a true delight for anyone looking to enjoy a sweet, chocolatey treat while following a vegan lifestyle. The combination of simple, plant-based ingredients and a rich, fudgy texture makes these brownies a must-try for both vegans and non-vegans alike. So, gather your ingredients and get ready to bake up a batch of these irresistible vegan brownies that are sure to become a staple in your dessert repertoire.
