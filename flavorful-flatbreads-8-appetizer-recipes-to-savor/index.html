Flavorful Flatbreads: 8 Appetizer Recipes to Savor


Flatbreads have always been a staple in many cuisines around the world, and for good reason. They’re easy to make, versatile, and can be paired with a wide range of toppings to suit any taste. In this post, we’ll explore 8 flavorful flatbread recipes that are perfect for appetizers, and show you how to elevate your snack game with these delicious and easy-to-make treats.

Recipe 1: Garlic and Rosemary Flatbread

Start with a classic combination: garlic and rosemary. This simple flatbread recipe is a great way to get started, and the aroma of roasted garlic and rosemary will fill your kitchen.

Ingredients:

  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon sugar
  • 1/2 cup warm water
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 sprigs fresh rosemary, chopped
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil

Instructions:

  1. In a large mixing bowl, combine flour, salt, and sugar. Gradually add warm water and mix until a dough forms.
  2. Knead the dough for 5-7 minutes until smooth and elastic.
  3. Divide the dough into 4-6 equal pieces, depending on desired thickness.
  4. Roll out each piece into a thin circle.
  5. Brush with olive oil, and sprinkle with minced garlic and chopped rosemary.
  6. Bake in a preheated oven at 425°F (220°C) for 10-12 minutes, or until golden brown.

Recipe 2: Spicy Shrimp Flatbread

Add some heat to your flatbread game with this spicy shrimp recipe. The combination of succulent shrimp, spicy harissa, and creamy feta cheese is a match made in heaven.

Ingredients:

  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon sugar
  • 1/2 cup warm water
  • 1/2 cup cooked and peeled shrimp
  • 2 tablespoons harissa
  • 1/4 cup crumbled feta cheese
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil

Instructions:

  1. Follow the same instructions as the garlic and rosemary flatbread recipe.
  2. Sprinkle cooked shrimp, harissa, and crumbled feta cheese on top of the flatbread.
  3. Bake in a preheated oven at 425°F (220°C) for 10-12 minutes, or until golden brown.

Recipe 3: Fig and Prosciutto Flatbread

Elevate your flatbread game with this sweet and savory combination of figs and prosciutto. The sweetness of the figs pairs perfectly with the salty, crispy prosciutto.

Ingredients:

  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon sugar
  • 1/2 cup warm water
  • 1/2 cup chopped fresh figs
  • 4 slices prosciutto, chopped
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil

Instructions:

  1. Follow the same instructions as the garlic and rosemary flatbread recipe.
  2. Sprinkle chopped figs and prosciutto on top of the flatbread.
  3. Drizzle with olive oil and bake in a preheated oven at 425°F (220°C) for 10-12 minutes, or until golden brown.

  • 1/2 cup warm water
  • 1/2 cup sautéed mushrooms (such as cremini or shiitake)
  • 1 tablespoon truffle oil
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil

Instructions:

  1. Follow the same instructions as the garlic and rosemary flatbread recipe.
  2. Sprinkle sautéed mushrooms and truffle oil on top of the flatbread.
  3. Drizzle with olive oil and bake in a preheated oven at 425°F (220°C) for 10-12 minutes, or until golden brown.
