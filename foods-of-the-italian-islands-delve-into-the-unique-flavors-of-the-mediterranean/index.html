Foods of the Italian Islands: Delve into the Unique Flavors of the Mediterranean


Italy’s archipelagos boast a delectable variety of dishes that can’t be found anywhere else due to their unique geographical positions and Mediterranean climates. As you explore the culinary gems of these islands, you’ll discover an incredible fusion of flavors reflecting the regions’ diverse cultural influences while still maintaining each island’s unique identity. Let’s explore the mouth-watering dishes that can only be found on Italy’s magnificent islands.

1. Capresi-style Pasta

Capri, the famous island off the coast of Naples, boasts some of the most remarkable dishes, including Capresi-style Pasta. With a delightful mix of fresh ingredients, this pasta dish combines regional herbs, tomatoes, and aromatic olive oil with creamy ricotta and salty Pecorino cheese. This delightful dish travels you on a culinary journey across the azure waters of the Mediterranean, as you savor each flavorful bite.

  • Ingredients: Spaghetti, fresh tomatoes, pecorino cheese, ricotta, extra virgin olive oil, basil, garlic, salt, and pepper.
  • Instructions:
    1. Cook the spaghetti according to package instructions, but make sure to maintain some firmness as you desire al dente.
    2. In a separate saucepan, gently heat olive oil with minced garlic. Add fresh cherry tomatoes (cut in half) Cook for 10 minutes, or until the tomatoes are soft but not turning to mush.
    3. Add crumbled ricotta, grated pecorino cheese, torn basil leaves, salt, and pepper to the sauce. Stir until well combined and the cheese has melted.
    4. Combine the cooked spaghetti with the tomato-ricotta sauce. Toss well to ensure that each strand is coated evenly with the flavorful sauce.
    5. Serve immediately in preheated bowls, garnishing with more grated cheese, fresh basil leaves, and a drizzle of high-quality extra virgin olive oil.

2. Sesame Seabream

Thanks to the island of Procida, the vibrant city of Naples and the nearby streets of Posillipo have embraced a rather distinct fish recipe that is now enjoyed worldwide. With its diverse population and cultural influence, Procida’s cuisine unapologetically experiments with ingredients from distant lands, such as sesame seeds, eventually leading to the creation of this exquisite dish.

  • Ingredients: Bream fish, sesame seeds, garlic, parsley, lemon, salt, and pepper.
  • Instructions:
    1. Preheat the oven to 190째C (366째F). Line a baking tray with parchment paper.
    2. Clean and dry the bream, then score the fish diagonally on both sides. Season generously with salt, pepper, and garlic inside the fish’s body cavity.
    3. Cover the fish entirely with sesame seeds, pressing them gently onto the fish’s skin.
    4. Drizzle with a bit of lemon juice and place on a lined baking sheet.
    5. Bake in the preheated oven for approximately 20 minutes or until the fish is cooked through and flakes easily with a fork.

3. Sicilian Arancini

No visit to the Italian island of Sicily is complete without tasting its famous Arancini. Despite their name, which means “little oranges,” these treats are rice balls filled with a mouth-watering combination of hearty ingredients. As you savor an Arancini, you’ll taste the history of Sicily in your mouth, as these delectable morsels display influences from all those who have occupied the island over the centuries.

  • Ingredients: Rice, tomato sauce, mozzarella, ragu, bread crumbs, olive oil, seasoned ricotta, and parmesan cheese.
  • Instructions:
    1. Cook the rice in plenty of boiling salted water, as per package instructions, until it’s cooked but still slightly firm (al dente). Allow the rice to cool.
    2. Combine the cooked rice with beaten egg, chopped mozzarella, and a generous amount of tomato sauce mixed with ragu.
    3. Form golf-ball-sized portions of the rice mixture, create an indentation in the middle, and place a spoonful of seasoned ricotta and parmesan cheese in the center. Close the indentation, ensuring the mixture holds together.
    4. Roll each rice ball in seasoned bread crumbs, then set aside on a tray for later deep frying.
    5. Heat olive oil in a deep pot to approximately 180째C (356째F). Carefully lower the rice balls into the hot oil and fry for 4-5 minutes or until they are a golden brown color.
    6. Place the arancini on a paper towel to drain excess oil before serving.

4. Amalfi Coast Lemon Risotto

The Amalfi Coast is renowned for its delectable lemons, which are utilized in mouth-watering dishes like the exquisite lemon risotto. This dish elegantly combines the crispest, zesty notes with creamy risotto while adding earthy tones from saffron. Savory, sweet, and tangy, this is truly a dish that captures the essence of Italy’s magic coastline.

  • Ingredients: Arborio rice, lemon zest, lemon juice, wildflowers, saffron, brown butter, shallots, white wine, parmesan cheese, and chicken broth.
  • Instructions:
    1. Cook the Arborio rice in chicken broth (to taste) according to the standard risotto method until al dente.
    2. In a separate pan, gently heat olive oil and cook the finely chopped shallots until they turn soft and translucent. Add the rice in two batches, stirring continuously, allowing each batch to saut챕 for a few minutes before adding the next.
    3. Splash in the white wine, and let it simmer until the alcohol evaporates.
    4. In a separate small dish, soak saffron in warm water and set aside.
    5. When the rice has absorbed the liquid in the pan, add the saffron-infused water, then stir in the saffron.
    6. Pour the lemon zest and juice in while continuing to stir.
    7. Once the desired consistency is reached and the rice is creamy, add the cheese and brown butter, mixing until well incorporated.
    8. Serve immediately, garnishing with wildflowers for a pop of color.

As you can see, Italy’s islands offer a splendidly diverse cuisine that embeds their unique charm in every well-balanced, tangy, and sumptuous bite. These dishes not only honor the island’s fresh produce but also pay homage to the rich history and flavors that have shaped them. Immortalize your travel memories by indulging in these mouthwatering culinary delights, one spoonful at a time.
