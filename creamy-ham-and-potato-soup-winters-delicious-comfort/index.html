Creamy Ham and Potato Soup: Winter's Delicious Comfort


There’s nothing quite like a warm bowl of soup to satisfy your cravings on a cold winter day. And when it comes to comforting and hearty soups, Creamy Ham and Potato Soup takes the crown. With its rich and velvety texture, savory chunks of ham, and tender potatoes, this soup is the ultimate winter comfort food. So grab your apron and get ready to indulge in a bowl of deliciousness!

Ingredients

  • 1 pound of cooked ham, diced
  • 4 medium-sized potatoes, peeled and cubed
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 4 cups of chicken broth
  • 2 cups of whole milk
  • 1 cup of heavy cream
  • 2 tablespoons of butter
  • 2 tablespoons of all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon of dried thyme
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley for garnish

Instructions

  1. In a large pot, melt the butter over medium heat. Add the chopped onion and minced garlic, and sauté until the onion becomes translucent and fragrant.

  2. Add the diced ham to the pot and cook for a few minutes until it starts to brown slightly. This will enhance the flavor of the soup.

  3. Sprinkle the flour over the ham and onion mixture, and stir well to coat everything evenly. Cook for another minute or two to cook off the raw flour taste.

  4. Slowly pour in the chicken broth, stirring constantly to prevent any lumps from forming. Bring the mixture to a gentle boil.

  5. Add the cubed potatoes and dried thyme to the pot. Reduce the heat to medium-low and let the soup simmer until the potatoes are tender, about 15-20 minutes.

  6. Once the potatoes are cooked, pour in the whole milk and heavy cream. Stir well to combine and bring the soup back to a simmer. Allow it to cook for an additional 5 minutes to let the flavors meld together.

  7. Season the soup with salt and pepper to taste. Remember to taste as you go and adjust the seasoning according to your preference.

  8. Remove the pot from the heat and let the soup cool slightly before serving. This will allow the flavors to develop further.

  9. Ladle the creamy ham and potato soup into bowls and garnish with fresh parsley. The vibrant green color of the parsley adds a pop of freshness to the dish.

  10. Serve the soup hot and enjoy the comforting flavors that will warm you up from the inside out.

Tips and Variations

  • If you prefer a chunkier soup, you can blend only a portion of the soup using an immersion blender or a regular blender. This will give you a creamy base with some potato and ham chunks for added texture.

  • Feel free to add your favorite vegetables to the soup, such as carrots, celery, or corn. These additions will not only boost the nutritional value but also add extra flavor and color to the dish.

  • To make the soup even more indulgent, sprinkle some shredded cheddar cheese on top and let it melt before serving. The cheesy goodness will take this soup to a whole new level of deliciousness.

  • Leftovers can be stored in an airtight container in the refrigerator for up to 3-4 days. Simply reheat on the stovetop or in the microwave for a quick and satisfying meal.

Creamy Ham and Potato Soup is the epitome of comfort food. Its smooth and creamy texture, combined with the smoky flavor of the ham and the earthiness of the potatoes, will warm your soul on those chilly winter nights. So why not treat yourself and your loved ones to a bowl of this winter’s delicious comfort? It’s guaranteed to put a smile on everyone’s face!
