Vegan Strawberry Shortcake: Sweet and Summery


Summer is the perfect time to indulge in sweet and refreshing desserts, and what better way to celebrate the season than with a classic Vegan Strawberry Shortcake? This delightful treat combines juicy, ripe strawberries with fluffy, tender cake and luscious whipped cream, all while being completely plant-based. Whether you’re a seasoned vegan or simply looking to explore new flavors, this Vegan Strawberry Shortcake recipe will surely become a staple in your dessert repertoire.

The Perfect Vegan Shortcake

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 tablespoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup vegan butter, chilled and cubed
  • 3/4 cup non-dairy milk (such as almond or soy)
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 425°F (220°C) and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
  2. In a large mixing bowl, whisk together the flour, sugar, baking powder, baking soda, and salt.
  3. Add the chilled vegan butter to the dry ingredients and use a pastry cutter or two forks to cut the butter into the flour mixture until it resembles coarse crumbs.
  4. In a separate bowl, mix the non-dairy milk and vanilla extract.
  5. Pour the wet ingredients into the dry mixture and stir until just combined. Be careful not to overmix.
  6. Turn the dough out onto a lightly floured surface and gently knead it a few times until it comes together.
  7. Pat the dough into a 1-inch thick round and use a biscuit cutter to cut out shortcakes. Place them on the prepared baking sheet.
  8. Bake for 15-18 minutes, or until the shortcakes are golden brown. Let them cool completely before assembling the strawberry shortcakes.

Fresh Strawberry Filling

Ingredients:

  • 4 cups fresh strawberries, hulled and sliced
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice

Instructions:

  1. In a large bowl, combine the sliced strawberries, sugar, and lemon juice. Toss gently to coat the strawberries and let them sit at room temperature for about 30 minutes. This will allow the strawberries to release their natural juices and become even more flavorful.

Fluffy Whipped Cream

Ingredients:

  • 1 can of full-fat coconut milk, chilled in the refrigerator overnight
  • 1/4 cup powdered sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Instructions:

  1. Carefully open the chilled coconut milk without shaking it. Scoop out the solid coconut cream that has risen to the top and place it in a mixing bowl, leaving behind the coconut water.
  2. Using a hand mixer or stand mixer, whip the coconut cream, powdered sugar, and vanilla extract together until light and fluffy.

Assembling the Vegan Strawberry Shortcake

  1. To assemble the shortcakes, slice each cake in half horizontally.
  2. Spoon a generous amount of the macerated strawberries onto the bottom half of each shortcake.
  3. Top the strawberries with a dollop of whipped coconut cream.
  4. Place the other half of the shortcake on top and garnish with additional strawberries and a sprig of fresh mint, if desired.

Enjoying the Fruits of Summer

The Vegan Strawberry Shortcake is a delightful way to celebrate the abundance of fresh strawberries during the summer months. This plant-based version of a classic dessert is not only delicious but also showcases the versatility of vegan ingredients. Whether you’re hosting a summer gathering or simply craving a sweet treat, this Vegan Strawberry Shortcake is sure to impress and satisfy.

So, take advantage of the seasonal bounty and treat yourself to this delightful dessert that perfectly captures the essence of summer in every bite. Happy baking and indulging in the sweet and summery flavors of this Vegan Strawberry Shortcake!
