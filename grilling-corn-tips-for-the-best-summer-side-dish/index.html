Grilling Corn: Tips for the Best Summer Side Dish


There’s nothing quite like the taste of grilled corn on a warm summer day. The smoky char, the sweet kernels bursting with flavor – it’s the ultimate summer side dish. Whether you’re hosting a backyard barbecue or just looking to elevate your weeknight dinner, grilling corn is a must-try technique. In this blog post, we’ll share some tips and tricks to help you achieve perfectly grilled corn every time.

Choosing the Right Corn

When it comes to grilling corn, the first step is selecting the right ears. Look for fresh, vibrant corn with bright green husks and plump kernels. The husks should feel slightly moist and tightly wrapped around the corn. Avoid corn with dry, brown husks or kernels that appear shriveled.

Preparing the Corn

Before grilling, it’s important to prepare the corn properly. Start by peeling back the husks, but leave them attached at the base. Remove the silk by hand or using a brush. Rinse the corn under cold water to remove any remaining silk.

Soaking the Corn

To ensure tender, juicy corn, consider soaking the ears in water before grilling. This step helps prevent the corn from drying out and reduces the risk of burning. Fill a large bowl or sink with cold water and place the corn, husks and all, in the water. Allow the corn to soak for at least 15 minutes, or up to an hour for maximum moisture retention.

Seasoning Options

Grilled corn is incredibly versatile, and you can season it to suit your taste. Here are a few popular seasoning options:

  1. Butter and Salt: Classic and simple, slather the grilled corn with melted butter and sprinkle with salt for a timeless flavor combination.

  2. Mexican Street Corn: Also known as elote, this popular Mexican street food is a flavor explosion. Spread the grilled corn with mayonnaise, sprinkle with crumbled cotija cheese, chili powder, and a squeeze of fresh lime juice.

  3. Herb Butter: Mix softened butter with minced fresh herbs like cilantro, parsley, or thyme. Slather this flavorful butter on the grilled corn for an aromatic twist.

  4. Parmesan and Garlic: Combine grated Parmesan cheese with minced garlic, salt, and pepper. Sprinkle this mixture over the grilled corn for a savory, umami-packed experience.

Feel free to get creative with your seasonings and experiment with different flavor combinations. Grilled corn is a blank canvas just waiting to be transformed!

Grilling Techniques

Now that your corn is prepped and seasoned, it’s time to grill! Here are a few grilling techniques to consider:

  1. Direct Heat: Preheat your grill to medium-high heat. Place the corn directly on the grates and grill for about 10-15 minutes, turning occasionally, until the kernels are tender and slightly charred.

  2. Indirect Heat: If you prefer a more gentle cooking method, opt for indirect heat. Preheat one side of your grill to medium-high heat and leave the other side off. Place the corn on the cooler side, close the lid, and grill for 15-20 minutes, turning occasionally.

  3. Foil-Wrapped: For a fuss-free grilling experience, wrap each ear of corn in aluminum foil after seasoning. Place the foil-wrapped corn on the grill and cook for 15-20 minutes, turning occasionally, until tender.

Serving Suggestions

Grilled corn is delicious on its own, but you can take it to the next level by serving it with complementary ingredients. Here are a few serving suggestions:

  • Cilantro-Lime Butter: Mix softened butter with fresh cilantro and a squeeze of lime juice. Serve the grilled corn with a dollop of this flavorful butter.

  • Chipotle Mayo: Combine mayonnaise with adobo sauce from a can of chipotle peppers. Spread this smoky, spicy mayo on the grilled corn for a kick of flavor.

  • Grilled Corn Salad: Cut the kernels off the grilled corn and toss them with cherry tomatoes, red onion, avocado, and a simple vinaigrette for a refreshing summer salad.

  • Grilled Corn Salsa: Dice the grilled corn and combine it with diced tomatoes, red onion, jalapeno, cilantro, lime juice, and salt for a vibrant salsa.

Conclusion

Grilling corn is a fantastic way to elevate this summer staple and create a mouthwatering side dish. With these tips and techniques, you’ll be able to achieve perfectly grilled corn every time. So fire up the grill, gather your favorite seasonings, and get ready to indulge in the smoky, sweet goodness of grilled corn. Happy grilling!
