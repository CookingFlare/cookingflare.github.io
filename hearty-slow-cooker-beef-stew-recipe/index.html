Hearty Slow Cooker Beef Stew Recipe


  1. In a large bowl, combine the flour, salt, and pepper. Toss the beef stew meat in the flour mixture until evenly coated.

  2. Heat the vegetable oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat. Add the coated beef stew meat to the skillet and cook until browned on all sides. Transfer the meat to a slow cooker.

  3. In the same skillet, add the diced onion and minced garlic. Cook until the onion is translucent and the garlic is fragrant, about 5 minutes. Transfer the onion and garlic mixture to the slow cooker.

  4. Add the sliced carrots, celery, and frozen peas to the slow cooker.

  5. In a separate bowl, whisk together the beef broth, red wine (if using), tomato paste, bay leaves, dried thyme, dried rosemary, and paprika. Pour the mixture into the slow cooker, covering all the ingredients.

  6. Cover the slow cooker and cook on low heat for 8 hours, or until the beef is tender and the flavors have melded together.

  7. Once the stew is ready, remove the bay leaves and discard. Serve the beef stew hot, garnished with fresh parsley.

Serving Suggestions

  • Serve the beef stew with crusty bread or over a bed of mashed potatoes for a comforting and hearty meal.
  • Pair the stew with a side salad or steamed vegetables for a balanced and nutritious dinner.
  • Store any leftovers in an airtight container in the refrigerator for up to 3 days. Reheat gently on the stovetop or in the microwave before serving.

Final Thoughts

This Hearty Slow Cooker Beef Stew is the perfect dish for those chilly nights when you crave something warm and comforting. The slow cooking process allows the flavors to develop and the beef to become tender and melt-in-your-mouth delicious. With the addition of carrots, celery, and peas, this stew is not only hearty but also packed with nutritious vegetables.

To make this stew even more flavorful, we added red wine and a combination of herbs and spices like bay leaves, thyme, rosemary, and paprika. These ingredients infuse the stew with a rich and aromatic taste that will keep you coming back for more.

Whether you’re feeding a crowd or simply want to enjoy leftovers throughout the week, this recipe is a winner. The slow cooker does all the hard work for you, so you can sit back and relax while your kitchen fills with the incredible aroma of a home-cooked meal.

So, grab your slow cooker and get ready to indulge in a bowl of this Hearty Slow Cooker Beef Stew. It’s the ultimate comfort food that will warm your soul and satisfy your taste buds. Enjoy!
