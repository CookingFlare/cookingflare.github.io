Creamy Butternut Squash Risotto: A Winter Delight


When the winter chill sets in, there’s nothing quite like a warm, comforting meal to lift your spirits. Creamy butternut squash risotto is the perfect dish to bring a touch of luxury to your winter evenings. This velvety, flavorful risotto is a delightful blend of creamy Arborio rice, rich butternut squash, and aromatic herbs, creating a dish that is both elegant and satisfying. Whether you’re cooking for a special occasion or simply craving a cozy night in, this recipe is sure to impress.

Ingredients

  • 1 small butternut squash, peeled, seeded, and diced
  • 5 cups vegetable or chicken broth
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 1/2 cups Arborio rice
  • 1/2 cup dry white wine
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • Salt and freshly ground black pepper, to taste
  • Fresh sage leaves, for garnish

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Butternut Squash: Preheat the oven to 400°F (200°C). Place the diced butternut squash on a baking sheet, drizzle with olive oil, and season with salt and pepper. Roast for 25-30 minutes or until tender and caramelized. Set aside.

  2. Simmer the Broth: In a saucepan, bring the vegetable or chicken broth to a gentle simmer over medium heat. Once simmering, reduce the heat to low to keep the broth warm.

  3. Sauté the Aromatics: In a large, heavy-bottomed pot, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the chopped onion and garlic, and sauté until softened and fragrant, about 3-4 minutes.

  4. Toast the Rice: Add the Arborio rice to the pot and stir to coat the grains with the oil. Cook for 2-3 minutes, stirring constantly, until the rice becomes translucent around the edges.

  5. Deglaze with Wine: Pour in the white wine and stir the rice until the liquid is absorbed.

  6. Add the Squash: Add the roasted butternut squash to the pot and stir to combine with the rice.

  7. Cook the Risotto: Begin adding the warm broth to the pot, one ladleful at a time, stirring frequently and allowing each addition of broth to be absorbed before adding more. Continue this process for about 18-20 minutes, or until the rice is creamy and al dente.

  8. Finish with Parmesan: Stir in the grated Parmesan cheese until melted and well combined. Season with salt and pepper to taste.

  9. Serve and Garnish: Spoon the creamy butternut squash risotto into individual bowls, garnish with fresh sage leaves, and an extra sprinkle of Parmesan, if desired.

Final Thoughts

Creamy butternut squash risotto is a luxurious dish that embodies the essence of winter comfort. The creamy texture, combined with the earthy sweetness of butternut squash, makes this risotto a true delight for the senses. Whether you’re looking to impress guests or simply treat yourself to a special meal, this recipe is a must-try for any winter occasion. Pair it with a glass of your favorite wine and enjoy the warmth and richness of this delightful winter dish.

So, next time you’re seeking a comforting and elegant meal during the winter months, consider making creamy butternut squash risotto. It’s a dish that embodies the coziness of the season and is sure to become a favorite in your culinary repertoire. Cheers to winter delights and the joy of savoring a beautifully crafted dish!
