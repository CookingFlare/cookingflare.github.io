Chickpea and Spinach Curry Ingredients: 1 can of chickpeas (drained and rinsed) 3 cups fresh spinach 1 onion (finely chopped) 2 cloves garlic (minced) 1-inch ginger (grated) 1 can coconut milk 2 tablespoons curry powder Salt and pepper to taste 1 tablespoon olive oil Fresh cilantro for garnish Instructions: Heat olive oil in a pan over medium heat. Add chopped onions and sauté until translucent. Stir in garlic and ginger, cooking for another minute until fragrant. Add the curry powder, chickpeas, and coconut milk. Simmer for 10 minutes, allowing flavors to meld. Stir in the spinach until wilted. Season with salt and pepper. Serve hot, garnished with fresh cilantro.
