Sweet Pineapple Coconut Bread: A Taste of Paradise


If you’re looking for a tropical escape without leaving your kitchen, then this Sweet Pineapple Coconut Bread recipe is just what you need. The combination of sweet pineapple and luscious coconut will transport your taste buds to a sunny beach, making it the perfect treat for breakfast, brunch, or a mid-afternoon snack. This moist and flavorful bread is simple to make and will have your home smelling like a tropical paradise in no time. Let’s dive into the recipe and bring a taste of paradise to your table.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 cup crushed pineapple, drained
  • 1/2 cup sweetened shredded coconut
  • 1/2 cup buttermilk
  • 1/4 cup pineapple juice

Instructions

  1. Preheat and Prepare: Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C) and grease a 9x5 inch loaf pan.

  2. Mix Dry Ingredients: In a medium bowl, whisk together the flour, baking powder, baking soda, and salt.

  3. Cream Butter and Sugar: In a separate large bowl, cream together the softened butter and granulated sugar until light and fluffy.

  4. Add Eggs and Vanilla: Beat in the eggs, one at a time, then stir in the vanilla extract.

  5. Incorporate Pineapple and Coconut: Gently fold in the crushed pineapple and shredded coconut.

  6. Alternate Flour Mixture and Buttermilk: Beat in the flour mixture alternately with the buttermilk, mixing until just incorporated. Stir in the pineapple juice.

  7. Bake: Pour the batter into the prepared loaf pan and smooth the top. Bake for 60 to 70 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.

  8. Cool and Enjoy: Allow the bread to cool in the pan for 10 minutes before transferring to a wire rack to cool completely.

Serving Suggestions

This Sweet Pineapple Coconut Bread is delightful on its own, but you can take it to the next level by serving it with a dollop of whipped cream or a sprinkle of powdered sugar. For an extra tropical touch, consider adding a side of fresh pineapple slices or a scoop of coconut ice cream. Pair it with a cup of freshly brewed coffee or a fruity iced tea for a truly indulgent experience.

Tips for Success

  • Drain Pineapple Well: Make sure to drain the crushed pineapple thoroughly to avoid excess moisture in the bread.

  • Proper Mixing: Be careful not to overmix the batter to ensure a tender texture.

  • Storage: Store the bread in an airtight container at room temperature for up to 3 days, or freeze for longer storage.

Conclusion

Indulge in the tropical flavors of Sweet Pineapple Coconut Bread and let your senses take a mini-vacation to a sun-soaked island. This easy and delightful recipe is perfect for sharing with loved ones or savoring by yourself. Whether you’re hosting a brunch or simply craving a taste of paradise, this bread is sure to become a favorite in your recipe collection. So, roll up your sleeves, gather your ingredients, and let the aromas of pineapple and coconut fill your kitchen with warmth and happiness. Enjoy!
