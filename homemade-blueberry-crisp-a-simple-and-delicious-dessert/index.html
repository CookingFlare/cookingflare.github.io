Homemade Blueberry Crisp: A Simple and Delicious Dessert


If you’re a fan of sweet and fruity desserts, then you’re in for a treat with this Homemade Blueberry Crisp recipe. Bursting with juicy blueberries and topped with a crunchy oatmeal crust, this dessert is a crowd-pleaser that is both simple to make and incredibly delicious. Whether you’re hosting a summer barbecue or simply craving a comforting dessert, this Blueberry Crisp is sure to satisfy your sweet tooth.

Ingredients

  • 4 cups fresh blueberries
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour

For the Crisp Topping:

  • 1 cup old-fashioned oats
  • 1/2 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup packed brown sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, cold and cut into small pieces

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C) and lightly grease a 9x9-inch baking dish.

  2. In a large mixing bowl, gently toss the blueberries with lemon juice, granulated sugar, and flour until well coated. Transfer the blueberry mixture to the prepared baking dish, spreading it out evenly.

  3. In a separate bowl, combine the oats, flour, brown sugar, cinnamon, and salt for the crisp topping. Mix well to ensure all the ingredients are evenly distributed. Add the cold, diced butter to the mixture and use your fingers or a pastry cutter to cut the butter into the dry ingredients until the mixture resembles coarse crumbs.

  4. Sprinkle the crisp topping evenly over the blueberry mixture in the baking dish, covering it completely.

  5. Place the baking dish in the preheated oven and bake for about 40-45 minutes, or until the topping is golden brown and the blueberries are bubbling.

  6. Once done, remove the Blueberry Crisp from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes before serving. This dessert is best enjoyed warm, either on its own or with a scoop of vanilla ice cream.

Tips and Variations

  • Frozen Blueberries: If fresh blueberries are not in season or not readily available, you can also use frozen blueberries for this recipe. Simply thaw them before using and proceed with the recipe as directed.

  • Nuts and Spices: For an extra flavor boost, you can add chopped nuts such as almonds, pecans, or walnuts to the crisp topping. Additionally, spices like nutmeg or ginger can be added to the blueberry mixture for a warm and aromatic twist.

  • Serving Suggestions: While this Blueberry Crisp is delicious on its own, you can also pair it with whipped cream, a dollop of Greek yogurt, or a drizzle of caramel sauce for added indulgence. It can also be served alongside a cup of hot coffee or tea for a delightful dessert experience.

  • Make-ahead and Storage: If you want to prepare this dessert ahead of time, you can assemble the blueberry mixture and the crisp topping separately. Cover and refrigerate them until you’re ready to bake. Simply combine the two and bake as directed when you’re ready to serve. Leftovers can be stored in an airtight container in the refrigerator for up to 3 days.

So there you have it - a simple and delicious Homemade Blueberry Crisp recipe that is sure to impress your family and friends. With its vibrant blueberry filling and irresistible oatmeal crust, this dessert is a delightful combination of sweet and crunchy. Give it a try and let your taste buds rejoice in the flavors of summer!
