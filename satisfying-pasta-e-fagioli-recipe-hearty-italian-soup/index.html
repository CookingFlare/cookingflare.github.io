Satisfying Pasta e Fagioli Recipe - Hearty Italian Soup


If there’s one dish that captures the heart and soul of Italian comfort food, it’s Pasta e Fagioli. This hearty soup, hailing from the heart of Italy, is a delicious blend of pasta, beans, and savory flavors that will warm you from the inside out. Whether you’re looking for a cozy meal on a chilly evening or a wholesome dish to share with loved ones, Pasta e Fagioli is the perfect choice. In this recipe, we’ll explore how to create this soul-satisfying Italian classic right in your own kitchen.

Ingredients

To start, you’ll need the following ingredients:

  • 1 cup ditalini pasta
  • 1 can (15 oz) cannellini beans, drained and rinsed
  • 1 can (15 oz) red kidney beans, drained and rinsed
  • 4 slices of pancetta, diced
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 carrots, diced
  • 2 celery stalks, diced
  • 4 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 can (14 oz) diced tomatoes
  • 4 cups chicken or vegetable broth
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • 1 bay leaf
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley for garnish
  • Grated Parmesan cheese for serving

Instructions

Step 1: Prepare the Base

In a large pot, cook the diced pancetta over medium heat until it becomes crispy and releases its flavorful oils. Add the chopped onion, carrots, and celery to the pot, and sauté until the vegetables become tender. Then, add the minced garlic and cook for an additional minute until fragrant.

Step 2: Build the Flavor

Once the vegetables are tender, pour in the diced tomatoes and allow the mixture to simmer for a few minutes, allowing the flavors to meld together. Next, add the cannellini beans, red kidney beans, dried oregano, dried thyme, bay leaf, and chicken or vegetable broth to the pot. Stir well and bring the soup to a gentle boil.

Step 3: Add the Pasta

When the soup reaches a gentle boil, add the ditalini pasta to the pot. Reduce the heat to a simmer and let the soup cook until the pasta is al dente, stirring occasionally to prevent sticking. This should take about 10-12 minutes.

Step 4: Adjust the Seasoning

Once the pasta is cooked to perfection, taste the soup and season with salt and pepper according to your preference. Remember, the pancetta and broth may already add saltiness to the dish, so be sure to taste before adding too much salt.

Step 5: Serve and Enjoy

Ladle the Pasta e Fagioli into bowls, ensuring each serving includes a generous portion of the savory beans, pasta, and aromatic broth. Garnish with freshly chopped parsley and a sprinkle of grated Parmesan cheese for an extra touch of flavor. Serve with crusty bread for a truly satisfying meal.

Final Thoughts

Pasta e Fagioli is a celebration of simple, wholesome ingredients that come together to create a truly satisfying dish. Whether you’re a fan of Italian cuisine or simply looking to add more heartwarming recipes to your repertoire, this hearty soup is a must-try. From its rich flavors to its comforting aroma, Pasta e Fagioli is sure to become a favorite in your household. So, gather your ingredients, embrace the spirit of Italian cooking, and savor every spoonful of this delightful soup. Buon appetito!
