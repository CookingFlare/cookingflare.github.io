10-Minute Meals for College Students on the Go


Being a college student can be challenging, especially when it comes to finding time to eat a proper meal. With busy schedules, countless assignments, and limited cooking skills, it’s no wonder that many students resort to unhealthy fast food options. But fear not! In this blog post, I will share some quick and easy recipes that can be cooked in just 10 minutes, perfect for college students on the go.

1. Avocado Toast with a Twist

Ingredients:

  • 1 ripe avocado
  • 2 slices of whole wheat bread
  • 1 tablespoon of olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon of lemon juice
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Optional toppings: sliced tomatoes, feta cheese, or smoked salmon

Instructions:

  1. Toast the slices of bread until golden brown.
  2. While the bread is toasting, mash the avocado in a bowl until smooth.
  3. Add olive oil, lemon juice, salt, and pepper to the mashed avocado and mix well.
  4. Spread the avocado mixture evenly on the toasted bread slices.
  5. Top with your choice of optional toppings for added flavor.
  6. Enjoy your delicious and nutritious avocado toast!

2. Veggie Stir-Fry

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup of mixed vegetables (carrots, bell peppers, broccoli, snap peas, etc.)
  • 2 tablespoons of soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon of sesame oil
  • 1 clove of garlic, minced
  • Optional toppings: sesame seeds, chopped green onions, or crushed red pepper flakes

Instructions:

  1. Heat sesame oil in a pan over medium-high heat.
  2. Add minced garlic and sauté until fragrant.
  3. Add the mixed vegetables to the pan and stir-fry for about 5 minutes until they are cooked but still crisp.
  4. Pour soy sauce over the vegetables and toss to coat evenly.
  5. Sprinkle with optional toppings for added flavor and serve immediately.
  6. Enjoy your quick and nutritious veggie stir-fry!

3. Mediterranean Wrap

Ingredients:

  • 1 whole wheat tortilla
  • 2 tablespoons of hummus
  • 1/4 cup of diced cucumber
  • 1/4 cup of diced tomatoes
  • 1/4 cup of crumbled feta cheese
  • Fresh spinach leaves

Instructions:

  1. Lay the tortilla flat on a clean surface.
  2. Spread the hummus evenly on the tortilla.
  3. Layer the diced cucumber, tomatoes, feta cheese, and spinach leaves on top of the hummus.
  4. Roll the tortilla tightly, tucking in the sides as you go.
  5. Cut the wrap in half diagonally for easier handling.
  6. Enjoy your flavorful Mediterranean wrap on the go!

4. Microwave Mug Omelette

Ingredients:

  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 tablespoons of milk
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Optional fillings: chopped vegetables, shredded cheese, or cooked bacon

Instructions:

  1. In a microwave-safe mug, whisk together eggs, milk, salt, and pepper until well combined.
  2. Stir in your choice of optional fillings.
  3. Microwave the mug on high for 1 minute, then stir.
  4. Microwave for an additional 1-2 minutes until the omelette is fully cooked and set.
  5. Let it cool for a minute before enjoying your fluffy mug omelette.

5. Greek Yogurt Parfait

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup of Greek yogurt
  • 1/2 cup of granola
  • 1/2 cup of mixed berries (strawberries, blueberries, raspberries)
  • Honey or maple syrup for drizzling

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl or a to-go container, layer Greek yogurt, granola, and mixed berries.
  2. Drizzle honey or maple syrup over the top for added sweetness.
  3. Repeat the layers until all the ingredients are used.
  4. Refrigerate until ready to eat or enjoy immediately.

With these 10-minute meals, you can ditch the fast food and fuel your body with delicious and nutritious food even when you’re on the go. Remember, eating well doesn’t have to be time-consuming or complicated. Happy cooking, college students!
