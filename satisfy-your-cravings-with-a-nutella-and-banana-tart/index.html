Satisfy Your Cravings with a Nutella and Banana Tart


Are you ready to take your dessert game to the next level? Look no further because we have a delectable treat that will satisfy all your sweet cravings. Introducing the mouthwatering Nutella and Banana Tart! This delightful dessert combines the rich, chocolatey goodness of Nutella with the natural sweetness of bananas, all nestled in a buttery, flaky crust. Whether you’re entertaining guests or simply indulging in a solo treat, this tart is sure to impress. Let’s dive into the recipe and bring this irresistible dessert to life in your own kitchen.

Ingredients

For the Crust:

  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, cold and cut into cubes
  • 1 large egg yolk
  • 2-3 tablespoons ice water

For the Filling:

  • 1 cup Nutella
  • 3 ripe bananas, sliced
  • 1/4 cup chopped hazelnuts (optional, for garnish)

Instructions

Step 1: Prepare the Crust

  1. In a large mixing bowl, combine the flour, sugar, and salt.
  2. Add the cold butter to the flour mixture and use a pastry cutter or your fingertips to work the butter into the flour until it resembles coarse crumbs.
  3. In a small bowl, whisk together the egg yolk and 2 tablespoons of ice water. Gradually add the egg mixture to the flour mixture, stirring with a fork until the dough starts to come together. If needed, add an additional tablespoon of ice water.
  4. Turn the dough out onto a lightly floured surface and knead it gently until it forms a smooth ball. Flatten the ball into a disk, wrap it in plastic wrap, and refrigerate for at least 30 minutes.

Step 2: Roll Out the Dough and Assemble the Tart

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. On a lightly floured surface, roll out the chilled dough into a circle about 12 inches in diameter.
  3. Carefully transfer the rolled-out dough to a 9-inch tart pan with a removable bottom. Press the dough into the bottom and up the sides of the pan, trimming any excess dough.

Step 3: Fill and Bake the Tart

  1. Spread the Nutella evenly over the bottom of the tart crust.
  2. Arrange the sliced bananas on top of the Nutella in a decorative pattern.
  3. If desired, sprinkle the chopped hazelnuts over the bananas.
  4. Place the tart on a baking sheet and bake in the preheated oven for 25-30 minutes, or until the crust is golden brown.

Step 4: Serve and Enjoy

  1. Once baked, remove the tart from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes.
  2. Carefully remove the tart from the pan and transfer it to a serving platter.
  3. Slice the Nutella and Banana Tart into wedges and serve it warm or at room temperature.
  4. For an extra touch, you can dust the tart with powdered sugar before serving.

Conclusion

There you have it - a simple yet irresistible recipe for a Nutella and Banana Tart that will leave everyone craving for more. The combination of creamy Nutella, sweet bananas, and buttery crust creates a symphony of flavors that is hard to resist. Whether it’s for a special occasion or a casual dessert at home, this tart is a winner. So, gather your ingredients and get ready to impress your taste buds with this delightful creation. Happy baking!
