No-Churn Strawberry Chocolate Chip Ice Cream


When warm weather hits or when you just want a refreshing indulgence, ice cream is the quintessential treat. But what if you don’t have an ice cream maker or want to avoid the fuss of churning? Enter the no-churn strawberry chocolate chip ice cream—a luscious, creamy dessert that requires minimal effort but delivers maximum delight.

This recipe combines the natural sweetness and vibrant flavor of fresh strawberries with the rich bite of semi-sweet chocolate chips, all folded into a smooth, creamy base that freezes perfectly without an ice cream machine. Whether it’s a family gathering, a casual dinner, or a spontaneous dessert craving, this ice cream fits every occasion exquisitely.

Why No-Churn Ice Cream?

Traditional ice cream making involves churning to prevent large ice crystals, resulting in a creamy texture. No-churn recipes simplify this by using whipped cream and sweetened condensed milk to mimic that silky consistency without any special equipment.

This method saves you from standing over your ice cream maker, watching a spinning bowl or worrying about textured lumps. Instead, you get smooth, scoopable ice cream with a homemade touch, perfect for strawberry and chocolate lovers alike.

Ingredients & Their Roles

Understanding each ingredient helps you tweak and customize the recipe for your taste and dietary needs.

  • Heavy Cream: The primary source of fat, which ensures the ice cream is rich and velvety. When whipped, heavy cream incorporates air, providing lightness and volume.
  • Sweetened Condensed Milk: Adds sweetness and acts as a thickener. Because it’s concentrated, it reduces the formation of ice crystals, keeping the ice cream creamy.
  • Fresh Strawberries: The star ingredient, delivering a natural fruity flavor, moisture, and color.
  • Vanilla Extract: Enhances overall flavor depth and rounds off the sweetness.
  • Chocolate Chips: For that irresistible crunch and chocolatey contrast to the strawberry’s tartness. Mini chips or finely chopped pieces distribute more evenly.
  • Lemon Juice (Optional): Balances sweetness with a touch of brightness, highlighting the freshness of the strawberries.

Step-by-Step Instructions for No-Churn Strawberry Chocolate Chip Ice Cream

1. Prepare the Strawberries

  • Rinse about 2 cups of fresh strawberries thoroughly.
  • Remove the stems and slice the berries thinly.
  • Toss the slices gently with 1-2 tablespoons of sugar (adjust depending on your sweetness preference).
  • Allow the strawberries to macerate for 15-20 minutes. This softens them, drawing out their natural juices and intensifying flavor.

2. Puree or Chop

Decide on your texture preference:

  • For a smoother ice cream, lightly puree the macerated strawberries in a blender or food processor, leaving some small chunks for texture.
  • For chunkier texture, simply chop the strawberries finely and fold them directly into the base.

3. Whip the Cream

  • Using an electric mixer, whip 2 cups of heavy cream until stiff peaks form.
  • This step is critical; properly whipped cream provides the airiness needed to avoid iciness.

4. Combine Condensed Milk and Flavoring

  • In a separate bowl, mix one 14-ounce can of sweetened condensed milk with 1 teaspoon of vanilla extract.
  • Incorporate the strawberry mixture, stirring gently until evenly combined.
  • Optionally, add 1 teaspoon of fresh lemon juice to brighten the flavor.

5. Fold Ingredients Together

  • Carefully fold the whipped cream into the condensed milk and strawberry mixture using a spatula.
  • The folding action should preserve as much air as possible to keep the ice cream light.
  • Once combined, fold in ½ to 1 cup of mini chocolate chips gently.

6. Freeze and Set

  • Transfer the mixture into a freezer-safe container, preferably with a lid.
  • Smooth the top and cover with plastic wrap if no lid is available to prevent ice crystals.
  • Freeze for at least 6 hours or preferably overnight to set completely.

7. Serve

  • Before serving, let the ice cream sit at room temperature for 5-10 minutes to soften slightly for easier scooping.
  • Scoop into bowls or cones, garnish with extra fresh strawberry slices or a drizzle of melted chocolate for an extra special touch.

Tips for Perfect No-Churn Ice Cream

  • Use chilled heavy cream: Cold cream whips better and provides more volume.
  • Don’t overmix: Overfolding whipped cream can cause it to deflate, resulting in a denser ice cream.
  • Strawberry substitutions: Raspberries or blueberries can be used similarly; adjust sugar to compensate for their tartness.
  • Chocolate chip variety: Feel free to experiment—dark chocolate chips for bitterness, white chocolate chips for sweetness, or even chunks of your favorite chocolate bar.
  • Storage: Keep the ice cream airtight in the freezer to prevent freezer burn and maintain texture.

Creative Variations to Try

  • Strawberry Cheesecake No-Churn: Swirl in softened cream cheese (about 4 oz) with the condensed milk for a tangy twist. Add graham cracker crumbs right before freezing for texture.
  • Mint Chocolate Chip Strawberry: Add a teaspoon of mint extract and replace regular chocolate chips with dark chocolate chunks for a cool refresh.
  • Vegan No-Churn Option: Use coconut cream instead of heavy cream and sweetened condensed coconut milk to enjoy a dairy-free version that still whips beautifully.

Nutritional Snapshot

This homemade strawberry chocolate chip ice cream is rich and indulgent, thanks to the cream and condensed milk. However, it contains fresh fruit and no artificial ingredients or preservatives, making it a wholesome option compared to many store-bought varieties. Portion control is still key, but knowing exactly what’s in your ice cream provides peace of mind.

Wrapping It Up

Making no-churn strawberry chocolate chip ice cream at home is as satisfying as it is simple. It captures the essence of summer with juicy strawberries and decadent chocolate chips beautifully mingling in a creamy, dreamy frozen treat. Once you master this base recipe, the possibilities for customizing flavors and add-ins are endless—making ice cream night at home both fun and delicious.

Gather fresh strawberries, whip some cream, and get ready to create a scoop of happiness that’s sure to cool you down and sweeten your day. Give it a try, and enjoy the delightful blend of creamy texture, fruity brightness, and chocolatey bursts—no ice cream maker needed!
