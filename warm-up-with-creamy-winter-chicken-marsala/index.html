Warm Up with Creamy Winter Chicken Marsala


As the winter chill sets in, there’s nothing quite like a hearty, comforting meal to warm you up from the inside out. This creamy winter chicken Marsala recipe is the perfect dish to indulge in during the colder months. It’s a rich and flavorful Italian-American classic that combines tender chicken with a luscious Marsala wine sauce, creating a dish that will delight your taste buds and warm your soul.

Ingredients

  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • 1/2 cup all-purpose flour
  • 4 tablespoons unsalted butter
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 8 ounces baby bella mushrooms, sliced
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup Marsala wine
  • 1 cup chicken broth
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 2 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley

Instructions

  1. Start by preparing the chicken breasts. Season each breast with salt and black pepper to taste. Then, gently dredge the chicken in the flour, shaking off any excess.

  2. In a large skillet, melt 2 tablespoons of butter with 1 tablespoon of olive oil over medium-high heat. Once the butter is foamy, add the chicken breasts and cook for about 4-5 minutes on each side, or until they are golden brown and cooked through. Transfer the chicken to a plate and cover with foil to keep warm.

  3. In the same skillet, melt the remaining butter and olive oil. Add the sliced mushrooms and sauté them until they are golden brown and tender. Stir in the minced garlic and cook for an additional 1-2 minutes.

  4. Pour the Marsala wine into the skillet, scraping up any browned bits from the bottom of the pan. Allow the wine to simmer and reduce by half.

  5. Next, add the chicken broth to the skillet and bring the mixture to a simmer. Let it cook for a few minutes until the liquid reduces slightly.

  6. Pour in the heavy cream and stir to combine. Allow the sauce to simmer for a few more minutes until it thickens slightly.

  7. Return the chicken breasts to the skillet, nestling them into the creamy Marsala sauce. Simmer for an additional 5-7 minutes, allowing the flavors to meld together.

  8. Sprinkle the chopped parsley over the chicken Marsala just before serving. This adds a pop of freshness and color to the dish.

Serving Suggestions

Creamy winter chicken Marsala pairs beautifully with a variety of sides. Consider serving it over a bed of al dente fettuccine or creamy mashed potatoes to soak up the velvety sauce. A simple green salad or steamed vegetables would also complement this dish perfectly, adding a touch of freshness and crunch to the meal.

Conclusion

This creamy winter chicken Marsala recipe is a true crowd-pleaser and a wonderful way to warm up during the chilly winter months. The rich, creamy sauce combined with the tender chicken and earthy mushrooms make for a truly satisfying and indulgent meal. Whether you’re cooking for a special occasion or simply craving a comforting dinner, this dish is sure to impress. So, embrace the winter season and treat yourself to a bowl of this creamy, dreamy chicken Marsala – you won’t be disappointed!
