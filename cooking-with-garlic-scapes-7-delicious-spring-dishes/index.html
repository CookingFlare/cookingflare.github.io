Cooking with Garlic Scapes: 7 Delicious Spring Dishes


As spring arrives, the culinary world is filled with an abundance of fresh, seasonal ingredients. One of the most underrated and underutilized is the humble garlic scape. A garlic scape is the curly green shoots that grow from the top of a garlic bulb, and they’re packed with flavor and nutrients. In this post, we’ll explore the world of cooking with garlic scapes and provide you with 7 delicious spring dishes to try.

Garlic Scape Pesto

One of the most popular ways to use garlic scapes is to make a delicious pesto sauce. Simply blend the scapes with some olive oil, Parmesan cheese, and a pinch of salt, and you have a sauce that’s perfect for pasta, pizza, or as a dip. To take it to the next level, add some toasted pine nuts and a squeeze of fresh lemon juice.

Garlic Scape Soup

Garlic scapes add a depth of flavor and a pop of color to this creamy soup. Simply sauté the scapes in some butter until they’re softened, then add in some chicken or vegetable broth and simmer until the scapes are tender. Blend the mixture until smooth, then stir in some heavy cream and a pinch of nutmeg.

Garlic Scape and Asparagus Risotto

This creamy risotto is a perfect showcase for the flavors of garlic scapes and asparagus. Simply sauté the scapes and asparagus in some olive oil until they’re tender, then add in some Arborio rice and cook until it’s lightly toasted. Add in some white wine and cook until the liquid is almost completely absorbed, then stir in some chicken broth and grated Parmesan cheese. Finish the dish with a drizzle of truffle oil and a sprinkle of parsley.

Garlic Scape and Lemon Chicken

This bright and citrusy chicken dish is a perfect way to showcase the flavors of garlic scapes. Simply marinate chicken breasts in a mixture of olive oil, lemon juice, garlic scapes, and a pinch of salt, then grill or bake until cooked through. Serve with a side of roasted vegetables and a sprinkle of fresh parsley.

Garlic Scape and Spinach Stuffed Shells

These creamy stuffed shells are a perfect way to get your garlic scape fix. Simply cook pasta shells according to the package instructions, then fill them with a mixture of ricotta cheese, garlic scapes, spinach, and a pinch of nutmeg. Top with marinara sauce and grated mozzarella cheese, then bake until golden brown.

Garlic Scape and Mushroom Tart

This savory tart is a perfect showcase for the earthy flavors of garlic scapes and mushrooms. Simply sauté the scapes and mushrooms in some butter until they’re tender, then add in some all-purpose flour and cook until the mixture is lightly toasted. Stir in some heavy cream and a pinch of nutmeg, then pour the mixture into a pre-baked tart crust. Top with grated Parmesan cheese and bake until golden brown.

Garlic Scape and Feta Stuffed Burgers

These savory burgers are a perfect way to get your garlic scape fix on a summer evening. Simply mix minced garlic scapes into your burger mixture, then top with crumbled feta cheese and a sprinkle of fresh parsley. Grill or pan-fry until cooked through, then serve on a toasted bun with your favorite toppings.

In conclusion, garlic scapes are a versatile and delicious ingredient that can be used in a variety of dishes. Whether you’re looking for a creamy sauce, a flavorful soup, or a savory tart, garlic scapes are the perfect addition to your springtime cooking repertoire. So next time you’re at the farmer’s market or grocery store, be sure to pick up a bunch of garlic scapes and get cooking!
