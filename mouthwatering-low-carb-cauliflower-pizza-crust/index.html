Mouthwatering Low Carb Cauliflower Pizza Crust


Are you craving pizza but want to stick to your low carb diet? Look no further than this mouthwatering low carb cauliflower pizza crust recipe! With just a few simple ingredients, you can enjoy a delicious, guilt-free pizza that won’t derail your healthy eating goals.

Ingredients

  • 1 medium-sized head of cauliflower
  • 1/2 cup grated parmesan cheese
  • 1/2 cup mozzarella cheese
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1 egg, lightly beaten

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 425°F (220°C). Line a baking sheet with parchment paper and set aside.

  2. Wash the cauliflower thoroughly and remove the leaves and stem. Cut the cauliflower into florets and place them in a food processor. Pulse until the cauliflower resembles rice-like grains.

  3. Transfer the cauliflower rice to a microwave-safe bowl and cover with a microwave-safe plate. Microwave on high for 5-6 minutes, or until the cauliflower is tender. Allow it to cool for a few minutes.

  4. Once the cauliflower has cooled, transfer it to a clean kitchen towel or cheesecloth. Squeeze out as much liquid as possible. This step is crucial to achieve a crispy crust.

  5. In a large bowl, combine the cauliflower rice, grated parmesan cheese, mozzarella cheese, dried oregano, garlic powder, salt, black pepper, and beaten egg. Mix well until all the ingredients are evenly incorporated.

  6. Transfer the cauliflower mixture onto the prepared baking sheet. Use your hands to shape it into a round pizza crust, about 1/4 inch thick. Make sure to press it firmly so that it holds together.

  7. Bake the crust in the preheated oven for 15-20 minutes, or until it turns golden brown and crispy around the edges.

  8. Once the crust is ready, remove it from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes. At this point, you can add your favorite toppings such as tomato sauce, cheese, vegetables, and protein.

  9. Return the pizza to the oven and bake for an additional 10-15 minutes, or until the cheese is melted and bubbly.

  10. Once the pizza is done, remove it from the oven and let it cool slightly before slicing and serving.

Enjoy Your Low Carb Delight!

There you have it - a mouthwatering low carb cauliflower pizza crust recipe that satisfies your pizza cravings without the guilt. The combination of cauliflower, cheese, and spices creates a flavorful and crispy crust that pairs perfectly with your favorite toppings. Whether you’re following a low carb diet or simply looking for a healthier alternative to traditional pizza crust, this recipe is a must-try!

So go ahead and give this recipe a go. Experiment with different toppings and find your perfect combination. Get ready to indulge in a delicious pizza while staying on track with your healthy eating goals. Trust us, you won’t even miss the traditional dough crust!

Remember to share this recipe with your friends and family who are also looking for a low carb pizza alternative. They’ll thank you for it! Enjoy and happy cooking!
