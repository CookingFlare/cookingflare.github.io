Italian Cannoli Recipe with Ricotta and Chocolate Chips


If you’re looking to indulge in a classic Italian dessert, look no further than Cannoli. These delightful treats are a staple in Italian cuisine and are sure to satisfy your sweet tooth. The combination of creamy ricotta cheese and rich chocolate chips wrapped in a crispy shell creates a flavor explosion that will leave you craving more. In this recipe, I’ll guide you through the process of making homemade Cannoli shells and filling them with a delectable ricotta and chocolate chip mixture. So, roll up your sleeves and let’s get cooking!

Ingredients

For the Cannoli shells:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons granulated sugar
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 2 tablespoons unsalted butter, cold and cubed
  • 1/2 cup dry white wine
  • Vegetable oil, for frying

For the filling:

  • 2 cups ricotta cheese
  • 1 cup powdered sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/2 cup mini chocolate chips

Instructions

  1. In a large bowl, whisk together the flour, sugar, and salt. Add the cold butter and use your fingers to rub it into the dry ingredients until the mixture resembles coarse crumbs.

  2. Gradually add the white wine to the flour mixture, stirring until a dough forms. Turn the dough out onto a lightly floured surface and knead it for about 5 minutes, or until smooth and elastic. Wrap the dough in plastic wrap and let it rest at room temperature for 30 minutes.

  3. While the dough is resting, prepare the filling. In a medium bowl, combine the ricotta cheese, powdered sugar, and vanilla extract. Mix well until smooth. Stir in the mini chocolate chips and refrigerate until ready to use.

  4. After the dough has rested, divide it into two equal portions. Roll out each portion on a lightly floured surface until it is about 1/8 inch thick. Use a round cookie cutter or a glass to cut out circles that are approximately 4 inches in diameter.

  5. Heat vegetable oil in a deep fryer or a large pot to 375°F (190°C). Carefully wrap each dough circle around a cannoli tube, overlapping the edges and sealing them with a bit of water. Place the wrapped tubes in the hot oil and fry until golden brown, about 2-3 minutes. Use tongs to remove the shells from the oil and transfer them to a paper towel-lined plate to drain and cool.

  6. Once the shells have cooled, carefully slide the cannoli tubes out from the shells. Fill a piping bag fitted with a large star tip with the ricotta mixture. Pipe the filling into both ends of each cannoli shell, making sure to fill them completely.

  7. Dust the filled cannoli with powdered sugar and garnish with additional chocolate chips if desired. Serve immediately and enjoy!

Tips and Variations

  • For added flavor, you can add a teaspoon of orange zest or a splash of orange liqueur to the ricotta filling.
  • If you don’t have cannoli tubes, you can use metal or wooden dowels as a substitute. Just make sure they are clean and grease-free.
  • Get creative with your toppings! Instead of dusting with powdered sugar, try drizzling the cannoli with melted chocolate or caramel sauce.

Now that you have the recipe for homemade Cannoli with ricotta and chocolate chips, it’s time to unleash your inner Italian chef. These delightful treats are perfect for any occasion - whether it’s a family gathering, a dinner party, or simply a sweet indulgence for yourself. So, gather the ingredients, follow the steps, and get ready to savor the flavors of Italy in every bite. Buon appetito!
