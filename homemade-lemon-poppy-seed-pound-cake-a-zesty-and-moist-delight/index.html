Homemade Lemon Poppy Seed Pound Cake: A Zesty and Moist Delight


Are you searching for the perfect recipe to satisfy your craving for a tangy and moist dessert? Look no further! This homemade Lemon Poppy Seed Pound Cake is the ultimate treat for citrus lovers. Bursting with vibrant flavors and a delightful texture, this recipe is sure to become a favorite in your household.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 1/2 cups granulated sugar
  • 1 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 4 large eggs
  • 1/2 cup Greek yogurt
  • 1/4 cup fresh lemon juice
  • 2 tablespoons lemon zest
  • 1 tablespoon poppy seeds
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease and flour a standard-sized loaf pan.

  2. In a large mixing bowl, cream together the softened butter and granulated sugar until light and fluffy. This should take about 3-4 minutes.

  3. Add the eggs to the bowl, one at a time, beating well after each addition. Make sure to scrape down the sides of the bowl to ensure all ingredients are well incorporated.

  4. In a separate bowl, whisk together the flour, baking powder, baking soda, and salt. Set aside.

  5. Combine the Greek yogurt, lemon juice, lemon zest, poppy seeds, and vanilla extract in a small bowl.

  6. Gradually add the dry ingredients to the butter mixture, alternating with the yogurt mixture. Begin and end with the dry ingredients, mixing well after each addition.

  7. Pour the batter into the prepared loaf pan and smooth the top with a spatula. Tap the pan lightly on the countertop to release any air bubbles.

  8. Bake for 50-60 minutes or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean. The top of the cake should be golden brown.

  9. Remove the pound cake from the oven and allow it to cool in the pan for 10 minutes. Transfer it to a wire rack to cool completely.

  10. Once the cake has cooled, you can serve it as is or add a simple lemon glaze for an extra burst of flavor. To make the glaze, combine 1 cup of powdered sugar with 2 tablespoons of fresh lemon juice. Drizzle the glaze over the top of the cake, allowing it to drip down the sides.

  11. Slice and enjoy your homemade Lemon Poppy Seed Pound Cake with a cup of tea or coffee. The zesty lemon flavor combined with the subtle crunch of the poppy seeds will leave you craving for more!

Tips and Variations

  • For a more intense lemon flavor, you can add an extra tablespoon of lemon zest to the batter.
  • If you prefer a lighter texture, you can substitute half of the all-purpose flour with cake flour.
  • This pound cake is delicious on its own, but you can also serve it with a dollop of whipped cream or a scoop of vanilla ice cream.
  • For a gluten-free version, you can use a gluten-free flour blend instead of all-purpose flour.

So, what are you waiting for? Gather your ingredients, preheat the oven, and get ready to indulge in the zesty and moist delight of homemade Lemon Poppy Seed Pound Cake. With its bright citrus flavor and tender crumb, this cake is bound to impress your family and friends. Enjoy!
