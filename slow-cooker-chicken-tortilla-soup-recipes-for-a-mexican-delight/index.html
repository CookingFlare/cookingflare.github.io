Slow Cooker Chicken Tortilla Soup Recipes for a Mexican Delight


If you’re in the mood for a hearty and flavorful Mexican dish, look no further than Slow Cooker Chicken Tortilla Soup. This classic recipe is packed with bold flavors, tender chicken, and a variety of comforting ingredients that will warm you up from the inside out. The best part? It’s incredibly easy to make and perfect for a busy weeknight or a lazy Sunday. Let’s dive into the delicious world of Slow Cooker Chicken Tortilla Soup!

Ingredients

Before we get started, let’s gather the ingredients for this mouthwatering dish:

  • 1 pound boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1 can (14.5 oz) diced tomatoes
  • 1 can (10 oz) enchilada sauce
  • 1 medium onion, chopped
  • 1 can (4 oz) chopped green chile peppers
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 4 cups chicken broth
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • 1 teaspoon chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 1 cup frozen corn
  • 1 tablespoon chopped fresh cilantro

For garnish:

  • Tortilla strips or chips
  • Shredded cheese
  • Avocado slices
  • Lime wedges
  • Sour cream

Now that we have all our ingredients, let’s move on to the preparation.

Instructions

  1. Begin by placing the chicken breasts in the bottom of the slow cooker.

  2. Next, add the diced tomatoes, enchilada sauce, chopped onion, green chile peppers, minced garlic, chicken broth, cumin, chili powder, salt, black pepper, and bay leaf to the slow cooker. Give it a gentle stir to combine all the ingredients.

  3. Set the slow cooker to low heat and let the soup simmer for 6-8 hours, or until the chicken is cooked through and tender.

  4. Once the chicken is fully cooked, remove it from the slow cooker and shred it using two forks. Return the shredded chicken to the slow cooker and add the frozen corn and chopped cilantro. Stir well to incorporate all the ingredients.

  5. Let the soup cook for an additional 30 minutes to allow the flavors to meld together.

  6. Before serving, taste the soup and adjust the seasoning with salt and pepper if needed.

  7. When ready to serve, ladle the soup into bowls and garnish with tortilla strips or chips, shredded cheese, avocado slices, a squeeze of lime juice, and a dollop of sour cream.

Tips and Variations

  • To add a smoky flavor to the soup, you can include a chipotle pepper in adobo sauce along with the other ingredients.
  • If you prefer a thicker soup, you can add a can of drained and rinsed black beans.
  • For an extra kick of heat, sprinkle some chopped jalapeños on top of the soup before serving.

Serving Suggestions

Slow Cooker Chicken Tortilla Soup pairs beautifully with warm cornbread, a fresh green salad, or a side of Mexican rice. It’s also a fantastic dish to serve at gatherings or potlucks, as it can easily be doubled or tripled to feed a crowd.

Conclusion

With its rich flavors and comforting warmth, Slow Cooker Chicken Tortilla Soup is a true Mexican delight that will satisfy your cravings for a delicious, home-cooked meal. Whether you’re a busy parent, a working professional, or simply a lover of flavorful cuisine, this easy and versatile recipe is sure to become a staple in your kitchen. So, grab your slow cooker and get ready to enjoy a bowl of this soul-soothing soup that celebrates the vibrant flavors of Mexico. Buen provecho!
