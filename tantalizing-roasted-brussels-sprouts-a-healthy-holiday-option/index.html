Tantalizing Roasted Brussels Sprouts: A Healthy Holiday Option


Brussels sprouts often get a bad rap, but when prepared correctly, they can be a delightful addition to any holiday meal. In this recipe, we’ll be roasting the Brussels sprouts to perfection, bringing out their natural sweetness and adding a tantalizing touch to your holiday spread.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound of fresh Brussels sprouts
  • 2 tablespoons of olive oil
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon of sea salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon of black pepper
  • 1/2 teaspoon of smoked paprika
  • 1/4 teaspoon of red pepper flakes (optional)
  • Fresh lemon wedges (for serving)

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C).

  2. Start by preparing the Brussels sprouts. Trim off the tough ends and remove any outer leaves that may be damaged or wilted.

  3. In a large mixing bowl, combine the olive oil, minced garlic, sea salt, black pepper, smoked paprika, and red pepper flakes (if using). Stir well to create a flavorful marinade.

  4. Add the Brussels sprouts to the mixing bowl and toss them gently, ensuring that each sprout is coated evenly with the marinade. Let them sit for a few minutes to absorb the flavors.

  5. Transfer the Brussels sprouts to a baking sheet lined with parchment paper, arranging them in a single layer. This will ensure that they roast evenly.

  6. Place the baking sheet in the preheated oven and roast for about 25-30 minutes, or until the Brussels sprouts are tender and lightly browned. Remember to give them a gentle shake or stir halfway through the cooking time to ensure even browning.

  7. Once the Brussels sprouts are done, remove them from the oven and let them cool slightly. Squeeze fresh lemon juice over the roasted sprouts for an extra burst of flavor before serving.

Serving Suggestions

These tantalizing roasted Brussels sprouts can be enjoyed as a side dish or even as a vegetarian main course. Here are a few suggestions for serving them:

  1. Serve them alongside your favorite roasted meats, such as turkey, chicken, or beef, for a hearty holiday meal.

  2. Toss them with cooked pasta and grated Parmesan cheese for a simple yet satisfying vegetarian pasta dish.

  3. Add them to a salad with mixed greens, cherry tomatoes, and crumbled feta cheese for a refreshing and nutritious lunch option.

  4. Serve them as part of a festive veggie platter, alongside other roasted vegetables like carrots, sweet potatoes, and bell peppers.

Health Benefits of Brussels Sprouts

Not only are these roasted Brussels sprouts incredibly delicious, but they also come with a range of health benefits. Here are just a few reasons why you should consider adding Brussels sprouts to your holiday menu:

  • Brussels sprouts are rich in vitamins A, C, and K, which are essential for supporting a healthy immune system and promoting overall well-being.

  • They are packed with fiber, which aids in digestion and helps maintain a healthy gut.

  • Brussels sprouts contain antioxidants that can help protect against chronic diseases and reduce inflammation in the body.

  • They are low in calories and high in nutrients, making them a great option for those looking to maintain a healthy weight.

Conclusion

With their tantalizing flavors and impressive health benefits, roasted Brussels sprouts are a must-try addition to your holiday table. From their crispy exterior to their tender interior, these little green gems are sure to impress even the most skeptical eaters. So, why not give them a chance this holiday season and elevate your meal with this healthy and delicious option?
