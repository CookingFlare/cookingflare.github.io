Pasta with Lemon Garlic Shrimp: A Bright and Flavorful Seafood Dish


If you’re a fan of seafood and craving a dish that’s both bright and flavorful, look no further than this delightful recipe for Pasta with Lemon Garlic Shrimp. This dish combines the succulent taste of shrimp with the zesty kick of lemon and garlic, all nestled in a bed of perfectly cooked pasta. It’s a dish that’s sure to impress your family and friends at any gathering. So, let’s dive into the details of how to create this delectable meal.

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound of large shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 12 ounces of pasta (linguine or spaghetti works well)
  • 3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1/4 cup of fresh lemon juice
  • 1 teaspoon of lemon zest
  • 1/4 cup of olive oil
  • 1/4 cup of chopped fresh parsley
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • Red pepper flakes (optional, for a spicy kick)
  • Grated Parmesan cheese for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Cook the Pasta: Begin by cooking the pasta according to the package instructions. Make sure to cook it al dente, as it will continue to cook later in the dish. Once it’s done, drain the pasta and set it aside.

  2. Prepare the Shrimp: In a large skillet, heat the olive oil over medium-high heat. Add the minced garlic and sauté for about a minute until fragrant. Be careful not to let it brown. Then, add the shrimp to the skillet and season with salt, black pepper, and red pepper flakes if you desire a bit of heat. Cook the shrimp for 2-3 minutes on each side until they turn pink and opaque.

  3. Add the Lemon: Once the shrimp are cooked, pour in the fresh lemon juice and lemon zest. Stir the ingredients together, allowing the shrimp to soak up the bright flavors of the lemon and garlic. Let the mixture simmer for another minute.

  4. Combine the Pasta and Shrimp: Now, add the cooked pasta to the skillet with the shrimp. Toss everything together gently, ensuring that the pasta gets coated with the flavorful lemon garlic sauce. If the mixture seems a bit dry, you can add a splash of pasta cooking water to loosen it up.

  5. Finish and Serve: To finish, sprinkle the chopped fresh parsley over the pasta and shrimp. Toss once more to distribute the parsley evenly. Then, taste and adjust the seasoning if necessary. Once everything is well combined, it’s time to serve. Portion the pasta onto plates and garnish with a sprinkle of grated Parmesan cheese for an extra savory touch.

Now, sit back and savor the delightful combination of tender shrimp, tangy lemon, and aromatic garlic with every forkful of this Pasta with Lemon Garlic Shrimp. It’s a dish that’s bursting with fresh flavors and is sure to become a favorite in your recipe collection. Enjoy!

Conclusion:

Pasta with Lemon Garlic Shrimp is a fantastic dish that brings together the best of seafood and zesty flavors. Whether you’re hosting a dinner party or simply treating yourself to a special meal, this recipe is a winner. The bright, citrusy notes of the lemon perfectly complement the succulent shrimp and create a dish that’s both vibrant and comforting. So, next time you’re in the mood for a seafood delight, give this recipe a try and prepare to be amazed by the wonderful flavors it has to offer.
