Massaman Curry Recipe with Lamb and Potatoes


If you’re a fan of rich and aromatic curries, then you’re in for a treat with this Massaman Curry Recipe with Lamb and Potatoes. Originating from Thailand, Massaman curry is known for its unique blend of flavors that combine the richness of Indian spices with the creaminess of coconut milk. In this recipe, we’ll be using tender lamb and potatoes to create a hearty and satisfying meal that will leave you craving for more.

Ingredients

  • 500g lamb, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 2 tablespoons Massaman curry paste
  • 1 can (400ml) coconut milk
  • 2 large potatoes, peeled and cubed
  • 1 onion, thinly sliced
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 thumb-sized piece of ginger, grated
  • 1 cinnamon stick
  • 2 cardamom pods
  • 2 star anise
  • 1 tablespoon fish sauce
  • 1 tablespoon tamarind paste
  • 1 tablespoon palm sugar
  • Handful of roasted peanuts, for garnish
  • Fresh cilantro, for garnish
  • Steamed rice, to serve

Instructions

  1. Heat a large pan over medium heat. Add the lamb and cook until browned on all sides. Remove the lamb from the pan and set aside.

  2. In the same pan, add the onions, garlic, and ginger. Sauté until the onions are soft and translucent.

  3. Add the Massaman curry paste to the pan and cook for a minute, stirring constantly to release the flavors.

  4. Return the lamb to the pan and stir to coat it with the curry paste.

  5. Pour in the coconut milk and add the potatoes, cinnamon stick, cardamom pods, and star anise. Stir well to combine.

  6. Bring the curry to a boil, then reduce the heat and let it simmer for about 1 hour, or until the lamb is tender and the potatoes are cooked through.

  7. In a small bowl, mix together the fish sauce, tamarind paste, and palm sugar. Add this mixture to the curry and stir well to incorporate the flavors.

  8. Continue to simmer the curry for another 10 minutes, allowing the flavors to meld together.

  9. Remove the cinnamon stick, cardamom pods, and star anise from the curry.

  10. Serve the Massaman curry over steamed rice, garnished with roasted peanuts and fresh cilantro.

Tips and Variations

  • If you prefer a milder curry, you can reduce the amount of Massaman curry paste used.
  • Feel free to add other vegetables like bell peppers or carrots to the curry for added texture and flavor.
  • For a vegetarian version, you can replace the lamb with tofu or your favorite meat substitute.
  • If you like your curry with a bit of heat, you can add a sliced red chili or some chili flakes to the curry when cooking.
  • Leftovers can be stored in an airtight container in the refrigerator for up to 3 days. Simply reheat before serving.

Enjoy the Flavors of Massaman Curry

Indulge in the rich and comforting flavors of Massaman Curry with Lamb and Potatoes. This Thai-inspired dish is perfect for a cozy night in or when you want to impress your guests with a delicious and aromatic meal. The tender lamb, creamy coconut milk, and fragrant spices come together to create a dish that is both satisfying and full of flavor. Serve it over steamed rice for a complete and hearty meal that will leave everyone asking for seconds. So, gather your ingredients, fire up the stove, and embark on a culinary journey to Thailand with this mouthwatering Massaman Curry recipe.
