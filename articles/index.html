Vegan Cinnamon Rolls: Fluffy and Sweet Morning Delights

There’s nothing quite like the smell of freshly baked cinnamon rolls wafting through the kitchen in the morning. These fluffy, sweet treats are a beloved classic, and with this vegan twist, you can enjoy them guilt-free! Whether you’re a seasoned baker or a beginner in the kitchen, this recipe is sure to impress. So, roll up your sleeves and let’s get baking! Ingredients For the Dough: 1 cup non-dairy milk (such as almond or oat milk) 1 packet (2 1/4 tsp) active dry yeast 1/4 cup granulated sugar 1/3 cup vegan butter, melted 1/2 tsp salt 4 cups all-purpose flour, plus extra for dusting For the Filling: 1/2 cup vegan butter, softened 3/4 cup brown sugar 2 1/2 tbsp ground cinnamon For the Frosting: 1/4 cup vegan cream cheese, softened 1/4 cup vegan butter, softened 1 cup powdered sugar 1/2 tsp vanilla extract Instructions Step 1: Prepare the Dough Warm the non-dairy milk in a small saucepan until it reaches about 110°F. Pour the warm milk into a large mixing bowl and sprinkle the yeast on top. Let it sit for 5 minutes until foamy. Add the sugar, melted vegan butter, salt, and 2 cups of flour to the yeast mixture. Stir until well combined. Gradually add the remaining flour, 1/2 cup at a time, until the dough comes together. Turn the dough out onto a floured surface and knead for 5-7 minutes until smooth and elastic. Step 2: Let the Dough Rise Place the dough in a greased bowl, cover with a clean kitchen towel, and let it rise in a warm place for 1 hour or until doubled in size. Step 3: Roll and Fill Once the dough has risen, punch it down and roll it out on a floured surface into a 16x21 inch rectangle. Spread the softened vegan butter over the dough, then sprinkle the brown sugar and cinnamon evenly on top. Starting from the long edge, tightly roll up the dough and pinch the seam to seal. Step 4: Slice and Bake Cut the rolled dough into 12 equal pieces and place them in a greased 9x13 inch baking pan. Cover with a kitchen towel and let them rise for an additional 30 minutes. Preheat the oven to 350°F and bake the rolls for 25-30 minutes, or until golden brown. Step 5: Make the Frosting In a medium bowl, beat the softened vegan cream cheese and butter together until smooth. Add the powdered sugar and vanilla extract, and beat until creamy. Step 6: Frost and Enjoy Once the cinnamon rolls are done baking, let them cool for a few minutes before spreading the frosting on top. Serve warm and enjoy the fluffy, sweet, vegan goodness! Final Thoughts These vegan cinnamon rolls are the perfect way to start your day. They’re soft, gooey, and packed with the warm flavors of cinnamon and sugar. Whether you’re serving them for breakfast, brunch, or as an indulgent snack, they’re sure to be a hit with vegans and non-vegans alike. So, next time you’re craving a comforting treat, whip up a batch of these fluffy and sweet morning delights! ...
