Irresistible Keto Cheesy Bacon Brussels Sprouts Recipe


Brussels sprouts often get a bad rap, but these little green gems are a powerhouse of flavor and nutrition. When combined with crispy bacon and rich, gooey cheese, they transform into a dish that even the pickiest eaters will love. This Keto Cheesy Bacon Brussels Sprouts recipe is perfect for anyone looking to maintain a low-carb diet without sacrificing taste. Whether you’re serving it as a side dish for dinner or enjoying it as a snack, this recipe will surely impress.

Why Choose Brussels Sprouts?

Brussels sprouts are not just a trendy vegetable; they are packed with essential nutrients. They are low in calories and high in fiber, making them an excellent choice for anyone on a keto diet. Rich in vitamins C and K, Brussels sprouts also contain antioxidants that help combat oxidative stress in the body. Adding bacon and cheese not only enhances their flavor but also adds healthy fats, making this dish a complete meal on its own.

Ingredients

To create your perfect batch of Keto Cheesy Bacon Brussels Sprouts, gather the following ingredients:

  • 1.5 pounds Brussels sprouts (fresh, trimmed and halved)
  • 6 slices of bacon (chopped into small pieces)
  • 2 cups shredded cheddar cheese (or a mix of your favorite cheeses)
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 4 cloves garlic (minced)
  • 1 teaspoon onion powder
  • 1 teaspoon smoked paprika
  • Salt and pepper (to taste)
  • Fresh parsley (for garnish)

Cooking Instructions

Step 1: Preparing the Brussels Sprouts

  1. Trim and Halve: Start by washing the Brussels sprouts thoroughly. Trim the ends and remove any yellow or wilted leaves. Cut each sprout in half to ensure they cook evenly.

  2. Preheat the Oven: Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C). This temperature is perfect for roasting the Brussels sprouts and achieving that crispy texture.

Step 2: Cooking the Bacon

  1. Crisp the Bacon: In a large oven-safe skillet, cook the chopped bacon over medium heat until crispy. This should take about 5-7 minutes. Once crispy, use a slotted spoon to remove the bacon and set it aside, leaving the rendered fat in the skillet.

  2. Sauté the Garlic: In the same skillet, add the minced garlic to the bacon fat and sauté for about 30 seconds until fragrant. Be careful not to burn the garlic, as it can turn bitter.

Step 3: Roasting the Brussels Sprouts

  1. Add Brussels Sprouts: Place the halved Brussels sprouts into the skillet with the sautéed garlic. Drizzle with olive oil and season with onion powder, smoked paprika, salt, and pepper. Toss everything together to ensure the sprouts are evenly coated.

  2. Roast: Spread the Brussels sprouts in an even layer and roast in the preheated oven for 20-25 minutes, or until they are tender and golden brown. Stir halfway through to promote even cooking.

Step 4: Creamy Cheese Mixture

  1. Prepare the Cheese Sauce: While the Brussels sprouts are roasting, combine the heavy cream and half of the shredded cheese in a saucepan over low heat. Stir until the cheese is melted and the mixture is smooth.

  2. Combine Ingredients: Once the Brussels sprouts are done roasting, remove them from the oven. Pour the cheese sauce over the Brussels sprouts and sprinkle the crispy bacon on top. Gently toss everything together.

Step 5: Final Baking

  1. Top with Cheese: Sprinkle the remaining shredded cheese over the top of the Brussels sprouts and bacon mixture.

  2. Bake Again: Return the skillet to the oven and bake for an additional 10-15 minutes, or until the cheese is bubbly and golden brown.

Step 6: Serve and Enjoy

  1. Garnish: Once out of the oven, let the dish cool for a few minutes. Garnish with freshly chopped parsley for a pop of color and added freshness.

  2. Serve: This dish is best served warm and can be enjoyed as a side or a main dish.

Tips for the Best Keto Cheesy Bacon Brussels Sprouts

  • Choose Fresh Brussels Sprouts: Freshness is key to ensuring the best flavor and texture. Look for firm, tight sprouts with a vibrant green color.

  • Experiment with Cheeses: While cheddar cheese is a classic choice, feel free to mix in other cheeses like mozzarella, gouda, or even blue cheese for a unique twist.

  • Add Some Heat: If you like a bit of spice, consider adding red pepper flakes to the cheese sauce for a kick.

  • Make It Ahead: You can prepare the Brussels sprouts and cheese mixture ahead of time. Just store it in the fridge and bake it when you’re ready to serve.

Nutritional Information

This Keto Cheesy Bacon Brussels Sprouts recipe is not only delicious but also packed with nutrients. Here’s a rough estimate of the nutritional breakdown per serving (assuming 6 servings):

  • Calories: 350
  • Fat: 30g
  • Carbohydrates: 8g
  • Fiber: 4g
  • Protein: 20g

Final Thoughts

This Irresistible Keto Cheesy Bacon Brussels Sprouts recipe is an excellent way to incorporate more vegetables into your diet while enjoying a rich and satisfying meal. The combination of crispy bacon, creamy cheese, and tender Brussels sprouts creates an explosion of flavors that will keep you coming back for more. Perfect for any occasion, this dish is sure to impress family and friends alike. So why wait? Grab your ingredients and whip up this irresistible recipe today! Enjoy the delightful experience of transforming humble Brussels sprouts into a cheesy, savory sensation that everyone will rave about.
