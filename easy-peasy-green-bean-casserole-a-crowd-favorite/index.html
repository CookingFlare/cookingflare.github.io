Easy Peasy Green Bean Casserole: A Crowd Favorite


Are you looking for a delicious and easy-to-make side dish that will impress your friends and family? Look no further than the classic green bean casserole! This timeless dish has been a staple at gatherings and holiday feasts for generations, and for good reason. It’s simple to prepare, bursting with flavor, and is sure to please even the pickiest of eaters.

The Perfect Ingredients

To create the perfect green bean casserole, you’ll need the following ingredients:

  • 4 cups of fresh green beans, trimmed and halved
  • 1 can (10.5 ounces) of cream of mushroom soup
  • 3/4 cup of milk
  • 1/8 teaspoon of black pepper
  • 1 1/3 cups of French’s Crispy Fried Onions

Cooking Instructions

  1. Preheat the Oven: Begin by preheating your oven to 350°F (175°C).

  2. Cook the Green Beans: Bring a pot of water to a boil and blanch the green beans for 5 minutes. Once done, immediately transfer them to a bowl of ice water to stop the cooking process. Drain and set aside.

  3. Prepare the Sauce: In a separate bowl, mix the cream of mushroom soup, milk, and black pepper until well combined.

  4. Combine the Ingredients: Add the blanched green beans to the mushroom soup mixture and stir until the beans are well coated.

  5. Bake to Perfection: Transfer the mixture to a casserole dish and bake in the preheated oven for 25 minutes, or until the casserole is bubbly and heated through.

  6. Add the Finishing Touch: Sprinkle the French’s Crispy Fried Onions over the top of the casserole and return it to the oven for an additional 5 minutes, or until the onions are golden and crispy.

  7. Serve and Enjoy: Once done, remove the casserole from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes before serving.

Why It’s a Crowd Favorite

The green bean casserole is beloved for its creamy and savory flavor, as well as its satisfying crunch. The combination of tender green beans, rich mushroom sauce, and crispy fried onions creates a mouthwatering experience that keeps people coming back for seconds. It’s the perfect accompaniment to a variety of main dishes, from roasted meats to vegetarian entrees, making it a versatile addition to any meal.

Tips for Customization

While the classic green bean casserole is a timeless favorite, there are endless opportunities for customization to suit your taste preferences. Consider adding ingredients like bacon, cheese, or even a touch of garlic to elevate the flavors. You can also experiment with different types of onions or breadcrumbs for a unique twist on the traditional recipe.

In Conclusion

Easy Peasy Green Bean Casserole is a timeless dish that has stood the test of time for good reason. It’s simple to prepare, bursting with flavor, and is sure to be a hit with your friends and family. Whether you’re hosting a holiday gathering or simply looking to add a special touch to your weeknight dinner, this crowd-pleasing dish is a must-try. So, roll up your sleeves, gather your ingredients, and get ready to delight your taste buds with this classic favorite.
