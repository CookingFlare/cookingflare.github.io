Slow Cooker Greek Lemon Chicken: Fresh and Zesty


If you’re looking for a delicious and easy-to-make meal that will transport your taste buds to the sunny shores of Greece, then this Slow Cooker Greek Lemon Chicken recipe is just what you need. With its fresh and zesty flavors, this dish is perfect for a lazy Sunday lunch or a comforting weeknight dinner. The best part? You can simply set it and forget it, as your slow cooker does all the work for you. So let’s dive in and discover how to make this mouthwatering Greek-inspired dish.

Ingredients

  • 2.5-3 pounds of chicken pieces (such as drumsticks, thighs, or a combination)
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 4 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1/2 cup chicken broth
  • 1/4 cup freshly squeezed lemon juice (approximately 2 lemons)
  • Zest of 1 lemon
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley

Instructions

  1. In a small bowl, whisk together the olive oil, minced garlic, dried oregano, dried thyme, salt, and black pepper. This flavorful mixture will serve as a marinade for the chicken.

  2. Place the chicken pieces in a large ziplock bag or a shallow dish. Pour the marinade over the chicken, making sure to coat each piece evenly. Seal the bag or cover the dish and let the chicken marinate in the refrigerator for at least 1 hour, or overnight for maximum flavor.

  3. After marinating, transfer the chicken and any remaining marinade into the slow cooker. Add the chicken broth, freshly squeezed lemon juice, and lemon zest. Give it a gentle stir to combine all the flavors.

  4. Cover the slow cooker and cook on low heat for 6-8 hours, or until the chicken is tender and fully cooked. The slow cooking process allows the flavors to meld together, resulting in a succulent and fragrant dish.

  5. Once the chicken is cooked, use a slotted spoon to transfer it to a serving platter. Garnish with freshly chopped parsley to add a pop of color and freshness.

  6. Serve the Slow Cooker Greek Lemon Chicken with your favorite sides, such as roasted potatoes, steamed vegetables, or a Greek salad. The tender chicken, infused with the tangy lemon and aromatic herbs, pairs beautifully with a variety of accompaniments.

  7. Enjoy this delightful Greek-inspired dish with your family and friends. The slow cooker does all the hard work for you, allowing you to savor every bite of this fresh and zesty Slow Cooker Greek Lemon Chicken.

Conclusion

In conclusion, this Slow Cooker Greek Lemon Chicken recipe brings the vibrant flavors of Greece right into your kitchen. The combination of tangy lemon, fragrant garlic, and aromatic herbs creates a dish that is both comforting and refreshing. With the convenience of a slow cooker, you can effortlessly prepare this delicious meal that will leave your taste buds dancing. So go ahead, give this recipe a try, and let the flavors of Greece transport you to a Mediterranean paradise.
