Creamy Tomato Basil Chicken Penne Bake: A Hearty and Comforting Dish


If you’re in the mood for a comforting and hearty meal that will satisfy the whole family, look no further than this delicious Creamy Tomato Basil Chicken Penne Bake. This dish is a perfect combination of tender chicken, al dente penne pasta, and a rich, creamy tomato basil sauce, all baked to perfection with a generous topping of melted cheese. Whether you’re cooking for a special occasion or simply craving a cozy weeknight dinner, this recipe is sure to become a staple in your kitchen.

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound penne pasta
  • 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts, diced
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can (28 ounces) crushed tomatoes
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/4 cup fresh basil, chopped
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C) and lightly grease a 9x13 inch baking dish.

  2. Cook the penne pasta according to the package instructions until al dente. Drain and set aside.

  3. In a large skillet, heat the olive oil over medium-high heat. Add the diced chicken and cook until golden brown and cooked through. Remove the chicken from the skillet and set aside.

  4. In the same skillet, add the minced garlic and sauté for a minute until fragrant. Pour in the crushed tomatoes and bring to a simmer.

  5. Stir in the heavy cream, Parmesan cheese, fresh basil, dried oregano, salt, and pepper. Allow the sauce to simmer for 5-7 minutes, stirring occasionally, until it thickens slightly.

  6. Add the cooked penne pasta and cooked chicken to the skillet with the tomato basil cream sauce. Toss everything together until the pasta and chicken are evenly coated with the sauce.

  7. Transfer the creamy chicken and pasta mixture to the prepared baking dish. Sprinkle the shredded mozzarella cheese over the top, covering the entire surface.

  8. Bake in the preheated oven for 20-25 minutes, or until the cheese is bubbly and golden brown.

  9. Remove the Creamy Tomato Basil Chicken Penne Bake from the oven and let it rest for a few minutes before serving.

Serving Suggestion:

This Creamy Tomato Basil Chicken Penne Bake pairs wonderfully with a fresh green salad and a slice of crusty bread. It’s a versatile dish that can be served as the main course for a family dinner or as a delightful side dish at a potluck or gathering with friends. The flavors of the creamy tomato basil sauce combined with the tender chicken and perfectly cooked penne pasta will leave everyone coming back for seconds.

Conclusion:

Creamy Tomato Basil Chicken Penne Bake is the ultimate comfort food that brings together the classic flavors of tomato, basil, and cheese in a satisfying and wholesome dish. With simple ingredients and easy preparation, this recipe is a fantastic addition to any home cook’s repertoire. Whether you’re a beginner in the kitchen or an experienced chef looking for a crowd-pleasing meal, this Creamy Tomato Basil Chicken Penne Bake is a must-try. Enjoy the warmth and comfort of this delightful dish with your loved ones, and savor every creamy, cheesy bite.
