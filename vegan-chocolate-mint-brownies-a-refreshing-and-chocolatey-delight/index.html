Vegan Chocolate Mint Brownies: A Refreshing and Chocolatey Delight


If you’re a fan of all things chocolate and mint, then you’re in for a treat with this vegan chocolate mint brownies recipe. These delectable treats are the perfect combination of refreshing mint and rich, indulgent chocolate, all while being completely plant-based. Whether you’re a seasoned vegan or just looking to incorporate more plant-based options into your diet, these brownies are sure to impress your taste buds and satisfy your sweet tooth.

Ingredients

For the Brownies:

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/2 cup brown sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup unsweetened applesauce
  • 1/4 cup almond milk
  • 1/4 cup coconut oil, melted
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

For the Mint Frosting:

  • 1 1/2 cups powdered sugar
  • 3 tablespoons coconut oil, melted
  • 2-3 tablespoons almond milk
  • 1/2 teaspoon peppermint extract
  • Green food coloring (optional)

Instructions

Step 1: Preheat the Oven

Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C) and lightly grease a square baking pan.

Step 2: Prepare the Brownie Batter

In a large mixing bowl, whisk together the flour, cocoa powder, granulated sugar, brown sugar, baking powder, and salt. In a separate bowl, combine the unsweetened applesauce, almond milk, melted coconut oil, and vanilla extract. Pour the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients and stir until just combined.

Step 3: Bake the Brownies

Pour the brownie batter into the prepared baking pan and spread it out evenly. Place the pan in the preheated oven and bake for 25-30 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean. Once baked, remove the brownies from the oven and allow them to cool completely.

Step 4: Prepare the Mint Frosting

In a medium bowl, whisk together the powdered sugar, melted coconut oil, almond milk, and peppermint extract until smooth. Add a few drops of green food coloring if desired, to achieve a minty green hue.

Step 5: Frost the Brownies

Once the brownies have cooled, spread the mint frosting evenly over the top. For an extra touch, sprinkle some dairy-free chocolate chips or chopped fresh mint leaves on top of the frosting.

Serving and Storing

These vegan chocolate mint brownies are best enjoyed chilled, allowing the mint flavor to shine through. Cut them into squares and serve them as a delightful dessert for any occasion. Leftovers can be stored in an airtight container in the refrigerator for up to 5 days.

Final Thoughts

These vegan chocolate mint brownies are a delightful fusion of rich chocolate and refreshing mint, making them a perfect dessert option for vegans and non-vegans alike. With their fudgy texture and luscious mint frosting, these brownies are bound to become a favorite in your recipe collection. So, why not treat yourself and your loved ones to a batch of these irresistible vegan chocolate mint brownies today?
