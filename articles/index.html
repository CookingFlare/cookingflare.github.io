Sheet Pan Salmon with Veggies: A Nutritious and Effortless Dinner

Are you looking for a quick and healthy dinner option that requires minimal effort and delivers maximum flavor? Look no further than Sheet Pan Salmon with Veggies! This simple and nutritious recipe is perfect for busy weeknights when you want to whip up a delicious meal without spending hours in the kitchen. Ingredients 4 salmon fillets 2 cups of broccoli florets 2 cups of cherry tomatoes 1 red bell pepper, sliced 1 yellow bell pepper, sliced 1 red onion, sliced 4 tablespoons of olive oil 4 cloves of garlic, minced 1 tablespoon of lemon juice Salt and pepper to taste Fresh herbs for garnish (optional) Instructions Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C) and line a baking sheet with parchment paper. Arrange the salmon fillets on the prepared baking sheet, leaving enough space for the veggies. In a small bowl, whisk together the olive oil, minced garlic, lemon juice, salt, and pepper. This flavorful marinade will infuse the salmon and veggies with a burst of deliciousness. Drizzle half of the marinade over the salmon fillets, ensuring they are evenly coated. Set aside the remaining marinade for later use. In a large mixing bowl, combine the broccoli florets, cherry tomatoes, bell peppers, and red onion. Toss the veggies with the remaining marinade until they are well coated. Spread the veggies around the salmon fillets on the baking sheet, making sure they are in a single layer. This will allow them to roast evenly. Place the baking sheet in the preheated oven and bake for 15-20 minutes, or until the salmon is cooked through and flakes easily with a fork. The veggies should be tender and slightly charred. Once cooked, remove the sheet pan from the oven and allow the salmon and veggies to cool for a few minutes. Transfer the salmon and veggies to plates, garnish with fresh herbs if desired, and serve immediately. Nutritional Benefits Sheet Pan Salmon with Veggies is not only delicious but also packed with essential nutrients. Salmon is an excellent source of omega-3 fatty acids, which are known for their heart-healthy benefits. It is also rich in high-quality protein, vitamins, and minerals. The combination of colorful veggies adds fiber, antioxidants, and a variety of vitamins to your meal. This dish is a great way to incorporate a balanced mix of protein, healthy fats, and vegetables into your diet. ...
