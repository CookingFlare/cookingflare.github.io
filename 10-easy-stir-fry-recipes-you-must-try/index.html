10 Easy Stir Fry Recipes You Must Try


Stir-frying is a quick and versatile cooking method that can turn a handful of ingredients into a flavorful dish in no time. Whether you’re a busy parent looking for a simple dinner solution or a cooking enthusiast wanting to explore new flavors, stir-frying is a great technique to master. In this blog post, we’ve curated 10 easy stir-fry recipes that are sure to tantalize your taste buds. Let’s dive right in!

1. Classic Chicken Stir Fry

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb boneless, skinless chicken breast, sliced
  • 2 cups mixed vegetables (bell peppers, broccoli, carrots)
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp oyster sauce
  • 1 tsp sesame oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Heat oil in a wok or large skillet.
  2. Add garlic and chicken, stir-fry until chicken is cooked through.
  3. Toss in the vegetables, soy sauce, and oyster sauce.
  4. Cook until vegetables are tender-crisp.
  5. Finish with a drizzle of sesame oil. Serve hot over rice.

2. Beef and Broccoli Stir Fry

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb flank steak, thinly sliced
  • 2 cups broccoli florets
  • 1 onion, sliced
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp honey
  • 1 tsp ginger, grated
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced

Instructions:

  1. Sear steak in a hot pan until browned, then set aside.
  2. Stir-fry garlic, ginger, and onions until fragrant.
  3. Add broccoli and cooked steak back to the pan.
  4. Pour in soy sauce and honey, cook until sauce thickens.
  5. Serve over steamed rice.

3. Shrimp and Asparagus Stir Fry

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 1 bunch asparagus, trimmed and cut into pieces
  • 1 red bell pepper, sliced
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp rice vinegar
  • 1 tsp honey
  • Red pepper flakes for heat

Instructions:

  1. Cook shrimp until pink, then set aside.
  2. Stir-fry asparagus and bell pepper until tender.
  3. Return shrimp to the pan, add soy sauce, vinegar, honey, and red pepper flakes.
  4. Cook for a few more minutes and serve hot.

4. Veggie Lover’s Tofu Stir Fry

Ingredients:

  • 1 block firm tofu, cubed
  • 2 cups mixed vegetables (snap peas, mushrooms, baby corn)
  • 3 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp hoisin sauce
  • 1 tsp sriracha
  • Green onions for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Pan-fry tofu until golden brown, then set aside.
  2. Stir-fry vegetables until slightly charred.
  3. Add tofu back to the pan, drizzle with soy sauce, hoisin, and sriracha.
  4. Garnish with green onions and serve over noodles.

5. Pork and Pineapple Stir Fry

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb pork loin, thinly sliced
  • 1 cup pineapple chunks
  • 1 red bell pepper, diced
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp brown sugar
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • Cashews for crunch

Instructions:

  1. Brown pork in a skillet, then remove.
  2. Cook bell pepper until softened.
  3. Add pork back to the pan with pineapple, soy sauce, sugar, and garlic powder.
  4. Sprinkle with cashews and enjoy!

6. Sweet and Sour Chicken Stir Fry

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb chicken thighs, cut into chunks
  • 1 cup bell peppers, diced
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 1/2 cup pineapple juice
  • 3 tbsp ketchup
  • 2 tbsp vinegar
  • 1 tbsp cornstarch

Instructions:

  1. Brown chicken in a wok, then set aside.
  2. Stir-fry peppers and onion until tender.
  3. Mix pineapple juice, ketchup, vinegar, and cornstarch.
  4. Return chicken to the pan, pour sauce over, and cook until thickened.

7. Spicy Garlic Ginger Vegetable Stir Fry

Ingredients:

  • Assorted vegetables (zucchini, snow peas, carrots)
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1-inch ginger, grated
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp chili paste
  • Cilantro for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Stir-fry garlic and ginger until aromatic.
  2. Add vegetables and cook until crisp-tender.
  3. Drizzle with soy sauce and chili paste, toss well.
  4. Garnish with cilantro and serve hot.

8. Teriyaki Salmon Stir Fry

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb salmon fillet, cubed
  • 1 cup sugar snap peas
  • 1 carrot, julienned
  • 3 tbsp teriyaki sauce
  • 1 tbsp honey
  • Sesame seeds for topping

Instructions:

  1. Sear salmon in a pan until golden, then remove.
  2. Stir-fry snap peas and carrot until bright.
  3. Return salmon to the pan, add teriyaki sauce and honey.
  4. Sprinkle with sesame seeds before serving.

9. Eggplant and Tofu Stir Fry

Ingredients:

  • 1 large eggplant, cubed
  • 1 block tofu, diced
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp honey
  • 1 tsp chili flakes
  • Green onions for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Fry tofu until crisp, then set aside.
  2. Stir-fry eggplant until tender.
  3. Add tofu back to the pan, drizzle with soy sauce, honey, and chili flakes.
  4. Top with green onions and enjoy over rice.

10. Mushroom and Spinach Stir Fry

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups mushrooms, sliced
  • 3 cups fresh spinach
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tbsp balsamic vinegar
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • Parmesan cheese for topping

Instructions:

  1. Sauté garlic and mushrooms until browned.
  2. Add spinach and cook until wilted.
  3. Drizzle with balsamic vinegar and olive oil.
  4. Serve with a sprinkle of Parmesan on top.

Stir frying is not only a convenient way to whip up a meal in minutes, but it also allows you to experiment with different flavor combinations and textures. These 10 easy stir-fry recipes are just the beginning—feel free to customize them with your favorite ingredients and sauces. Get your wok ready and start stir-frying your way to delicious meals!

Remember, the key to a successful stir fry is high heat, fresh ingredients, and a little bit of creativity. So, grab your chopsticks and get cooking!

Enjoy exploring these 10 easy stir-fry recipes and elevate your home cooking game! If you have any questions or want more recipe ideas, feel free to reach out. Happy cooking!
