Delicious Low Carb Chicken Stir-Fry Recipe


If you’re on the lookout for a healthy, flavorful, and low-carb meal option, look no further than this delicious low carb chicken stir-fry recipe. Packed with vibrant vegetables, tender chicken, and a savory sauce, this dish is a fantastic way to satisfy your cravings while staying on track with your health goals.

Ingredients

For the Chicken Marinade:

  • 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts, thinly sliced
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon rice vinegar
  • 1 teaspoon sesame oil
  • 1 teaspoon minced ginger
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced

For the Stir-Fry:

  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • 1 red bell pepper, thinly sliced
  • 1 yellow bell pepper, thinly sliced
  • 1 cup snap peas
  • 1 cup broccoli florets
  • 1 small onion, thinly sliced
  • 3 green onions, chopped
  • 1/4 cup chicken broth
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon oyster sauce
  • 1 teaspoon sesame oil
  • 1 teaspoon cornstarch
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Marinate the Chicken: In a bowl, combine the soy sauce, rice vinegar, sesame oil, ginger, and garlic. Add the sliced chicken breasts to the marinade and toss to coat. Allow the chicken to marinate for at least 15 minutes.

  2. Prepare the Sauce: In a small bowl, whisk together the chicken broth, soy sauce, oyster sauce, sesame oil, and cornstarch. Set the sauce aside for later use.

  3. Stir-Fry the Chicken: Heat the vegetable oil in a large skillet or wok over high heat. Add the marinated chicken to the skillet and stir-fry for 3-4 minutes or until cooked through. Remove the chicken from the skillet and set it aside.

  4. Cook the Vegetables: In the same skillet, add a bit more oil if needed. Add the red and yellow bell peppers, snap peas, broccoli, and onion. Stir-fry for 3-4 minutes or until the vegetables are tender-crisp.

  5. Combine and Finish: Return the cooked chicken to the skillet with the vegetables. Pour the prepared sauce over the chicken and vegetables. Toss everything together until the sauce thickens and coats the chicken and vegetables evenly. Season with salt and pepper to taste.

  6. Serve: Garnish the chicken stir-fry with chopped green onions and serve hot. You can enjoy it on its own or with a side of cauliflower rice for a complete low-carb meal.

Nutritional Information

This low carb chicken stir-fry is not only delicious but also a healthy meal option. Packed with lean protein and an array of colorful vegetables, it’s a great way to get your nutrients while keeping your carb intake in check. Plus, the vibrant mix of vegetables provides an array of essential vitamins and minerals.

Final Thoughts

This low carb chicken stir-fry recipe is a wonderful addition to your collection of healthy and flavorful meals. It’s versatile, quick to make, and can easily be customized with your favorite vegetables or spices. Whether you’re following a low-carb diet or simply looking for a tasty and nutritious meal, this chicken stir-fry is sure to become a household favorite. Enjoy this satisfying dish and savor the flavors of a wholesome, low-carb lifestyle!
