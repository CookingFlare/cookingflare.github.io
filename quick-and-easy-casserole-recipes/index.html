Quick and Easy Casserole Recipes


Casseroles are a beloved staple in many households, offering convenience, versatility, and the ability to feed a crowd with minimal effort. Whether you’re preparing a weeknight dinner, hosting a potluck, or simply craving a comforting meal, these quick and easy casserole recipes are sure to hit the spot. From classic combinations to creative twists, there’s something here for everyone to enjoy.

1. Classic Beef and Potato Casserole

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 4 cups diced potatoes
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 1 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1 can (10.75 oz) condensed cream of mushroom soup
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C). Grease a 9x13 inch baking dish.
  2. In a skillet, cook ground beef and onion over medium heat until beef is browned and onion is tender. Drain excess fat.
  3. Layer half of the diced potatoes in the prepared baking dish. Season with salt and pepper.
  4. Spread half of the cooked beef and onion mixture over the potatoes.
  5. Pour half of the condensed soup evenly over the beef layer.
  6. Repeat the layers - potatoes, beef mixture, and soup.
  7. Cover the dish with aluminum foil and bake for 45 minutes.
  8. Remove foil, sprinkle shredded cheddar cheese on top, and bake uncovered for an additional 15 minutes or until cheese is melted and bubbly.

This hearty Beef and Potato Casserole is a comforting dish that’s perfect for chilly evenings or family gatherings. The layers of savory beef, tender potatoes, and creamy soup create a satisfying meal in one dish.

2. Chicken Enchilada Casserole

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups cooked shredded chicken
  • 1 can (15 oz) black beans, drained and rinsed
  • 1 can (10 oz) red enchilada sauce
  • 1 cup shredded Mexican blend cheese
  • 1/2 cup sour cream
  • 1 can (4 oz) diced green chilies
  • 6 corn tortillas, cut into quarters

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease a 9x9 inch baking dish.
  2. In a large bowl, mix together shredded chicken, black beans, diced green chilies, and half of the enchilada sauce.
  3. Layer half of the corn tortilla quarters on the bottom of the baking dish.
  4. Spread half of the chicken mixture over the tortillas.
  5. Sprinkle half of the shredded cheese over the chicken mixture.
  6. Repeat the layers - tortillas, chicken mixture, and cheese.
  7. Drizzle the remaining enchilada sauce evenly over the top layer.
  8. Dollop sour cream over the casserole.
  9. Cover the dish with aluminum foil and bake for 30 minutes.
  10. Remove foil and bake uncovered for an additional 10 minutes until bubbly and heated through.

This Chicken Enchilada Casserole offers all the flavors of traditional enchiladas in an easy-to-make casserole format. It’s perfect for Mexican food enthusiasts and a great way to use up leftover chicken.

3. Broccoli and Cheese Rice Casserole

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups cooked rice
  • 2 cups steamed broccoli florets
  • 1 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/4 cup breadcrumbs
  • 2 tablespoons butter, melted
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease a 9x9 inch baking dish.
  2. In a large bowl, combine cooked rice, steamed broccoli florets, shredded cheddar cheese, milk, grated Parmesan cheese, salt, and pepper.
  3. Spread the mixture evenly in the prepared baking dish.
  4. In a small bowl, mix together breadcrumbs and melted butter. Sprinkle over the casserole.
  5. Cover the dish with aluminum foil and bake for 30 minutes.
  6. Remove foil and bake uncovered for an additional 10-15 minutes until the top is golden brown and bubbly.

This Broccoli and Cheese Rice Casserole is a comforting side dish or vegetarian main course that’s creamy, cheesy, and packed with wholesome ingredients. It’s a hit with both kids and adults alike.

4. Tuna Noodle Casserole

Ingredients:

  • 8 oz egg noodles, cooked and drained
  • 2 cans (5 oz each) tuna, drained
  • 1 cup frozen peas
  • 1 cup sliced mushrooms
  • 1 can (10.75 oz) condensed cream of mushroom soup
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 cup crushed potato chips (optional topping)

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C). Grease a 9x13 inch baking dish.
  2. In a large bowl, combine cooked egg noodles, drained tuna, frozen peas, sliced mushrooms, condensed cream of mushroom soup, milk, shredded cheddar cheese, salt, and pepper.
  3. Spread the mixture evenly in the prepared baking dish.
  4. If desired, sprinkle crushed potato chips over the top for added crunch.
  5. Cover the dish with aluminum foil and bake for 30 minutes.
  6. Remove foil and bake uncovered for an additional 10-15 minutes until bubbly and heated through.

Tuna Noodle Casserole is a classic comfort food that’s quick to assemble and always satisfying. It’s perfect for busy weeknights when you need a hearty meal without a lot of fuss.

5. Breakfast Casserole with Sausage and Hash Browns

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb breakfast sausage, cooked and crumbled
  • 4 cups frozen hash brown potatoes, thawed
  • 1 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • 6 large eggs
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1/4 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon onion powder

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease a 9x13 inch baking dish.
  2. In a large bowl, whisk together eggs, milk, salt, pepper, garlic powder, and onion powder.
  3. Layer thawed hash brown potatoes evenly in the prepared baking dish.
  4. Sprinkle cooked and crumbled breakfast sausage over the potatoes.
  5. Pour the egg mixture evenly over the sausage and potatoes.
  6. Sprinkle shredded cheddar cheese over the top.
  7. Cover the dish with aluminum foil and bake for 40 minutes.
  8. Remove foil and bake uncovered for an additional 10-15 minutes until eggs are set and the top is golden brown.

This Breakfast Casserole with Sausage and Hash Browns is perfect for brunch or breakfast-for-dinner. It’s hearty, flavorful, and can be customized with your favorite breakfast ingredients.

Conclusion

These quick and easy casserole recipes offer a variety of flavors and ingredients to suit any palate and occasion. Whether you’re craving a classic comfort dish or want to try something new, these recipes are designed to be simple yet delicious. With minimal preparation and easy cleanup, casseroles are a practical choice for busy individuals and families. Next time you’re looking for a satisfying meal that’s sure to please everyone at the table, give one of these recipes a try. Your taste buds will thank you!
