Tasty Dairy-Free Mediterranean Stuffed Peppers


If you’re looking for a delicious and healthy meal that’s bursting with Mediterranean flavors, then these Tasty Dairy-Free Mediterranean Stuffed Peppers are the perfect choice. Packed with wholesome ingredients and spices, these stuffed peppers are a delightful combination of savory, sweet, and tangy flavors that will leave your taste buds craving for more. Whether you’re a seasoned cook or a novice in the kitchen, this recipe is simple to follow and guaranteed to impress your family and friends.

Ingredients

For the Stuffed Peppers:

  • 6 large bell peppers (any color)
  • 1 cup quinoa, rinsed
  • 1 ¾ cups vegetable broth
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can (14 oz) diced tomatoes, drained
  • 1 can (14 oz) chickpeas, drained and rinsed
  • ½ cup kalamata olives, pitted and chopped
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

For the Lemon Tahini Sauce:

  • ½ cup tahini
  • ¼ cup water
  • 2 tablespoons lemon juice
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • Salt to taste

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Peppers:

    • Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
    • Cut the tops off the bell peppers and remove the seeds and membranes. Place the peppers in a baking dish and set aside.

  2. Cook the Quinoa:

    • In a medium saucepan, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the chopped onion and garlic, and sauté until softened.
    • Add the quinoa and toast for 1-2 minutes, stirring frequently.
    • Pour in the vegetable broth and bring to a boil. Reduce the heat to low, cover, and simmer for 15-20 minutes, or until the quinoa is cooked and the liquid is absorbed.

  3. Prepare the Filling:

    • In a large bowl, combine the cooked quinoa, diced tomatoes, chickpeas, chopped olives, dried oregano, dried basil, salt, and pepper. Mix well to combine.

  4. Stuff the Peppers:

    • Spoon the quinoa mixture into the prepared bell peppers, pressing down gently to pack the filling.

  5. Bake the Stuffed Peppers:

    • Cover the baking dish with foil and bake for 25-30 minutes, or until the peppers are tender.

  6. Make the Lemon Tahini Sauce:

    • In a small bowl, whisk together the tahini, water, lemon juice, minced garlic, and salt until smooth and creamy.

  7. Serve and Enjoy:

    • Drizzle the stuffed peppers with the lemon tahini sauce before serving. Garnish with fresh herbs, if desired.

Tips and Variations

  • You can customize the filling by adding other Mediterranean ingredients such as artichoke hearts, sun-dried tomatoes, or pine nuts.
  • For a protein boost, you can include cooked lentils or grilled tofu in the filling.
  • These stuffed peppers can be made in advance and stored in the refrigerator for up to 3 days. Simply reheat them in the oven before serving.

Final Thoughts

These Tasty Dairy-Free Mediterranean Stuffed Peppers are a celebration of vibrant Mediterranean flavors and wholesome ingredients. Whether you’re following a dairy-free diet or simply looking for a satisfying meatless meal, these stuffed peppers are a winner. The combination of fluffy quinoa, hearty chickpeas, and zesty lemon tahini sauce creates a culinary experience that’s both comforting and invigorating. So, roll up your sleeves and get ready to savor the delightful taste of the Mediterranean with every bite of these tantalizing stuffed peppers. Happy cooking!
