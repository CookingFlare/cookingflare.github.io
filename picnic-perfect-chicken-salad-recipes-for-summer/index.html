Picnic Perfect Chicken Salad Recipes for Summer


Summer is the perfect time to enjoy the great outdoors, and what better way to do that than with a picnic? Picnics are a wonderful opportunity to relax, unwind, and share a meal with loved ones. And when it comes to picnic food, chicken salad is a classic choice that never disappoints. In this blog post, we will share three delicious and refreshing chicken salad recipes that are perfect for your summer picnics. So grab your picnic basket and let’s get cooking!

1. Tangy Lemon Herb Chicken Salad

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups cooked chicken, shredded
  • 1/2 cup mayonnaise
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 1 teaspoon Dijon mustard
  • 2 tablespoons fresh herbs (such as basil, parsley, or dill), chopped
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Lettuce leaves for serving
  • Sliced bread or rolls for serving

Instructions:

  1. In a large bowl, combine the shredded chicken, mayonnaise, lemon juice, Dijon mustard, fresh herbs, salt, and pepper. Mix well to combine.
  2. Taste and adjust the seasoning if necessary.
  3. To serve, place a lettuce leaf on a slice of bread or a roll. Spoon the chicken salad on top and cover with another slice of bread or roll.
  4. Repeat with the remaining ingredients to make more sandwiches.
  5. Pack the sandwiches in your picnic basket along with some fresh fruits and drinks, and you’re ready to go!

2. Crunchy Apple Pecan Chicken Salad

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups cooked chicken, cubed
  • 1/2 cup mayonnaise
  • 1/4 cup Greek yogurt
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 1 apple, cored and diced
  • 1/2 cup celery, thinly sliced
  • 1/2 cup pecans, chopped
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Lettuce leaves for serving
  • Crackers or croissants for serving

Instructions:

  1. In a mixing bowl, combine the chicken, mayonnaise, Greek yogurt, lemon juice, diced apple, sliced celery, chopped pecans, salt, and pepper. Mix well until all the ingredients are evenly coated.
  2. Taste and adjust the seasoning if needed.
  3. To serve, place a lettuce leaf on a cracker or split croissant. Spoon the chicken salad on top and cover with another cracker or croissant.
  4. Repeat with the remaining ingredients to make more servings.
  5. Pack the sandwiches along with some crunchy vegetables and a refreshing beverage, and you’re ready to enjoy a delightful picnic!

3. Zesty Southwest Chicken Salad Wraps

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups cooked chicken, shredded
  • 1/2 cup mayonnaise
  • 1/4 cup sour cream
  • 2 tablespoons lime juice
  • 1 teaspoon cumin powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon chili powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon onion powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Flour tortillas
  • Sliced avocado, tomatoes, and lettuce for serving

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, combine the shredded chicken, mayonnaise, sour cream, lime juice, cumin powder, chili powder, garlic powder, onion powder, salt, and pepper. Mix well until everything is well combined.
  2. Taste and adjust the seasoning according to your preference.
  3. To serve, spread a generous amount of the chicken salad mixture onto a flour tortilla. Top with sliced avocado, tomatoes, and lettuce.
  4. Roll up the tortilla tightly and secure with toothpicks if necessary.
  5. Pack the wraps in your picnic basket along with some tortilla chips and salsa for a complete Southwestern picnic experience.

With these three tantalizing chicken salad recipes, your summer picnics are guaranteed to be a hit. Whether you prefer the tangy and refreshing flavors of the lemon herb salad, the crunchy goodness of the apple pecan salad, or the zesty kick of the Southwest wrap, there’s something for everyone to enjoy. So gather your loved ones, head outdoors, and savor the flavors of summer with these picnic perfect chicken salad recipes. Happy picnicking!
