Satisfying Chicken and Cashew Stir Fry with a Crunch


Are you craving a delicious and satisfying meal that’s quick and easy to make? Look no further than this mouthwatering Chicken and Cashew Stir Fry recipe! Packed with flavor, protein, and a delightful crunch, this dish is sure to become a favorite in your recipe collection. Let’s dive into the details of how to create this delectable stir fry that will leave your taste buds singing.

Ingredients

For the Stir Fry:

  • 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 1 cup roasted cashews
  • 1 red bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 green bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 cup snap peas
  • 1 small onion, sliced
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon fresh ginger, grated
  • 3 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons oyster sauce
  • 1 tablespoon sesame oil
  • 1 teaspoon cornstarch
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

For Garnish:

  • Sliced green onions
  • Sesame seeds

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Chicken: In a bowl, combine the chicken pieces with 2 tablespoons of soy sauce and let it marinate for about 15 minutes.

  2. Create the Sauce: In a separate bowl, mix the remaining soy sauce, oyster sauce, sesame oil, and cornstarch. Stir until the cornstarch is fully dissolved.

  3. Heat the Wok: Place a wok or large skillet over medium-high heat and add 1 tablespoon of vegetable oil. Once the oil is hot, add the marinated chicken and stir-fry until it’s fully cooked. Remove the chicken from the wok and set it aside.

  4. Stir Fry the Vegetables: In the same wok, add the remaining tablespoon of vegetable oil. Toss in the garlic and ginger, and stir-fry until fragrant. Then, add the sliced onion, red and green bell peppers, and snap peas. Continue to stir-fry until the vegetables are slightly tender but still crisp.

  5. Combine and Finish: Return the cooked chicken to the wok and pour in the prepared sauce. Stir everything together until the sauce evenly coats the chicken and vegetables. Add the roasted cashews and toss to combine. Season with salt and pepper to taste.

  6. Serve and Garnish: Transfer the stir fry to a serving dish and garnish with sliced green onions and sesame seeds.

The Perfect Crunch

The highlight of this Chicken and Cashew Stir Fry is the satisfying crunch of the roasted cashews. Not only do they add a wonderful texture to the dish, but they also bring a rich, nutty flavor that complements the savory chicken and vibrant vegetables. Each bite is a delightful balance of tender chicken, crisp vegetables, and the irresistible crunch of cashews.

A Versatile Dish

One of the best things about this stir fry is its versatility. You can easily customize it to suit your taste preferences and dietary needs. For a vegetarian option, simply omit the chicken and add extra vegetables or tofu. If you prefer a spicier kick, consider adding a dash of chili flakes or a drizzle of sriracha sauce. The possibilities are endless, making this recipe a go-to for busy weeknights and relaxed weekend meals alike.

In Conclusion

This Chicken and Cashew Stir Fry is a dish that satisfies on every level – from its robust flavors to its satisfying crunch. It’s a perfect balance of protein, vegetables, and nutty goodness that will leave you feeling nourished and content. So, the next time you’re in need of a quick and flavorful meal, give this recipe a try and savor the delightful combination of tender chicken and crunchy cashews.

Enjoy!
