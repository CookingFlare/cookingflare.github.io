Grilling Mediterranean Style: Flavorful Recipes


When it comes to grilling, the Mediterranean region offers a treasure trove of flavors and ingredients that can elevate your barbecue game to a whole new level. From succulent meats to fresh vegetables and aromatic spices, Mediterranean-style grilling is all about creating dishes that burst with vibrant colors and bold flavors. In this blog post, we’ll explore some delightful and easy-to-make recipes that will transport your taste buds to the sun-drenched shores of the Mediterranean.

Grilled Lemon Herb Chicken Skewers

Prep Time: 20 minutes
Cook Time: 10 minutes
Servings: 4

Ingredients

  • 1.5 lbs of boneless, skinless chicken breasts, cut into chunks
  • 2 lemons, juiced and zested
  • 3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons of fresh thyme, chopped
  • 2 tablespoons of fresh rosemary, chopped
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 tablespoons of olive oil
  • Wooden skewers, soaked in water for 30 minutes

Instructions

  1. In a bowl, combine the lemon juice, lemon zest, minced garlic, chopped thyme, rosemary, salt, pepper, and olive oil. Mix well to create the marinade.
  2. Add the chicken chunks to the marinade and toss to coat evenly. Cover the bowl and let the chicken marinate in the refrigerator for at least 1 hour.
  3. Preheat the grill to medium-high heat.
  4. Thread the marinated chicken chunks onto the soaked wooden skewers.
  5. Grill the skewers for 4-5 minutes on each side or until the chicken is cooked through and has a nice charred exterior.
  6. Serve the grilled lemon herb chicken skewers with a fresh green salad and some grilled vegetables for a complete Mediterranean-inspired meal.

Grilled Mediterranean Vegetable Platter

Prep Time: 15 minutes
Cook Time: 10 minutes
Servings: 6

Ingredients

  • 2 zucchinis, sliced lengthwise
  • 2 yellow bell peppers, quartered and seeded
  • 1 large eggplant, sliced into rounds
  • 1 pint of cherry tomatoes
  • 1/4 cup of olive oil
  • 3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 2 teaspoons of dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh basil for garnish

Instructions

  1. Preheat the grill to medium heat.
  2. In a small bowl, whisk together the olive oil, minced garlic, dried oregano, salt, and pepper.
  3. Brush the sliced vegetables with the seasoned olive oil mixture.
  4. Place the vegetables on the grill and cook for 3-4 minutes on each side or until they are tender and have beautiful grill marks.
  5. Arrange the grilled vegetables on a platter and garnish with fresh basil.
  6. Drizzle any remaining seasoned olive oil over the vegetables before serving.

Grilled Greek Lamb Burgers

Prep Time: 25 minutes
Cook Time: 12 minutes
Servings: 4

Ingredients

  • 1.5 lbs of ground lamb
  • 1/2 cup of crumbled feta cheese
  • 1/4 cup of finely chopped red onion
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon of fresh mint, chopped
  • 1 tablespoon of fresh oregano, chopped
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 4 burger buns
  • Tzatziki sauce, lettuce, and tomato slices for serving

Instructions

  1. In a large bowl, combine the ground lamb, crumbled feta cheese, chopped red onion, minced garlic, chopped mint, chopped oregano, salt, and pepper. Mix gently until the ingredients are well combined.
  2. Divide the lamb mixture into 4 equal portions and shape them into burger patties.
  3. Preheat the grill to medium-high heat.
  4. Grill the lamb burgers for 5-6 minutes on each side or until they are cooked to your desired level of doneness.
  5. Toast the burger buns on the grill for a minute or two.
  6. Assemble the grilled lamb burgers on the toasted buns with a dollop of tzatziki sauce, lettuce, and tomato slices.

These Mediterranean-inspired grilling recipes are perfect for adding a touch of sunshine and freshness to your outdoor cooking adventures. Whether you’re hosting a summer barbecue or simply enjoying a cozy evening on the patio, these flavorful dishes are sure to impress your family and friends. So fire up the grill, embrace the Mediterranean spirit, and savor the delightful flavors of the Mediterranean right in your own backyard!
