Wholesome Breakfast Muffins: Perfect Grab-and-Go Recipe


Are you tired of rushing out the door in the morning with no time to sit down and enjoy a proper breakfast? Well, say goodbye to those hectic mornings because I’ve got the perfect solution for you – wholesome breakfast muffins! These delicious and nutritious muffins are the ultimate grab-and-go breakfast option that will keep you fueled and satisfied throughout the day.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups whole wheat flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon cinnamon
  • 1/2 cup honey or maple syrup
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1/2 cup Greek yogurt
  • 1/4 cup unsweetened applesauce
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 cup grated carrots
  • 1/2 cup chopped nuts (walnuts, pecans, or almonds)
  • 1/2 cup raisins or dried cranberries

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C) and line a muffin tin with paper liners or grease it with cooking spray.

  2. In a large mixing bowl, whisk together the whole wheat flour, baking powder, baking soda, salt, and cinnamon.

  3. In a separate bowl, beat the eggs and then stir in the honey or maple syrup, Greek yogurt, applesauce, and vanilla extract until well combined.

  4. Gradually add the wet ingredients to the dry ingredients and mix until just combined. Be careful not to overmix the batter.

  5. Gently fold in the grated carrots, chopped nuts, and raisins or dried cranberries until evenly distributed.

  6. Divide the batter evenly among the prepared muffin cups, filling each about 3/4 full.

  7. Bake for 20-25 minutes or until a toothpick inserted into the center of a muffin comes out clean.

  8. Allow the muffins to cool in the tin for 5 minutes before transferring them to a wire rack to cool completely.

Why Wholesome Breakfast Muffins?

These wholesome breakfast muffins are not only delicious but also packed with nutrients to kick-start your day. The whole wheat flour provides fiber and complex carbohydrates for sustained energy, while the carrots add a dose of vitamin A and antioxidants. The Greek yogurt and nuts contribute protein and healthy fats, making these muffins a well-rounded breakfast option.

Perfect for Meal Prep

One of the best things about these muffins is that you can easily make a batch ahead of time and store them in an airtight container for up to a week. This makes them an ideal meal prep option for busy mornings or a quick snack throughout the day.

Customize to Your Liking

Feel free to customize these muffins to suit your taste preferences. You can swap the grated carrots for zucchini or sweet potato, and experiment with different nuts and dried fruits. You can even add a tablespoon of chia seeds or flaxseeds for an extra nutritional boost.

Final Thoughts

Wholesome breakfast muffins are a game-changer for anyone looking to simplify their morning routine without sacrificing nutrition. So, the next time you find yourself pressed for time, grab one of these muffins and head out the door knowing that you’ve nourished your body with a wholesome and satisfying breakfast.
