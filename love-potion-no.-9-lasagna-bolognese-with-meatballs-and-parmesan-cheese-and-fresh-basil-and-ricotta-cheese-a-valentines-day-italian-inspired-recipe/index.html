Love Potion No. 9 Lasagna Bolognese with Meatballs and Parmesan Cheese and Fresh Basil and Ricotta Cheese: A Valentine's Day Italian-Inspired Recipe


Love Potion No. 9 Lasagna Bolognese with Meatballs and Parmesan Cheese and Fresh Basil and Ricotta Cheese: A Valentine’s Day Italian-Inspired Recipe

Valentine’s Day is the perfect occasion to treat your loved one to a special and romantic dinner. And what better way to celebrate than with a homemade Italian-inspired dish that will make their heart skip a beat? Introducing Love Potion No. 9 Lasagna Bolognese with Meatballs and Parmesan Cheese and Fresh Basil and Ricotta Cheese – a mouthwatering recipe that combines all the elements of a classic Italian meal with a touch of love potion magic.

Ingredients

For the Meatballs:

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1/2 cup breadcrumbs
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley
  • 1 egg, lightly beaten
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste

For the Bolognese Sauce:

  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 carrot, finely chopped
  • 1 celery stalk, finely chopped
  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1/2 cup red wine
  • 1 can (28 ounces) crushed tomatoes
  • 2 tablespoons tomato paste
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste

For the Lasagna:

  • 12 lasagna noodles, cooked according to package instructions
  • 2 cups ricotta cheese
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Fresh basil leaves for garnish

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).

  2. In a large bowl, combine all the meatball ingredients and mix well. Roll the mixture into small meatballs, about 1 inch in diameter.

  3. Heat the olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Add the meatballs and cook until browned on all sides. Remove the meatballs from the skillet and set aside.

  4. In the same skillet, add the onion, garlic, carrot, and celery. Cook until the vegetables are tender, about 5 minutes.

  5. Add the ground beef to the skillet and cook until browned. Drain any excess fat.

  6. Stir in the red wine, crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, dried basil, dried oregano, salt, and pepper. Simmer the sauce for about 20 minutes, until it thickens slightly.

  7. In a large baking dish, spread a thin layer of the Bolognese sauce. Arrange a layer of cooked lasagna noodles on top.

  8. Spread a layer of ricotta cheese over the noodles, followed by a layer of meatballs. Sprinkle with grated Parmesan cheese and shredded mozzarella cheese.

  9. Repeat the layers until all the ingredients are used, ending with a layer of Bolognese sauce and a sprinkle of Parmesan and mozzarella cheese.

  10. Cover the baking dish with foil and bake in the preheated oven for 30 minutes. Remove the foil and bake for an additional 15 minutes, until the cheese is golden and bubbly.

  11. Remove the lasagna from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes. Garnish with fresh basil leaves.

  12. Serve the Love Potion No. 9 Lasagna Bolognese with Meatballs and Parmesan Cheese and Fresh Basil and Ricotta Cheese to your loved one and watch as their taste buds fall under the spell of this delicious and enchanting dish.

Conclusion

Valentine’s Day is a time to celebrate love, and what better way to do so than with a homemade Italian-inspired meal? This Love Potion No. 9 Lasagna Bolognese with Meatballs and Parmesan Cheese and Fresh Basil and Ricotta Cheese is the perfect dish to impress your loved one and show them just how much you care. So, grab your apron, gather the ingredients, and let the magic of this delightful recipe create a memorable and romantic evening for you and your special someone. Buon appetito!
