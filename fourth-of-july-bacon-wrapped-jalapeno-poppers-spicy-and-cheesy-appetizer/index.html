Fourth of July Bacon-Wrapped Jalapeno Poppers: Spicy and Cheesy Appetizer


Are you looking for a show-stopping appetizer that will elevate your Fourth of July celebration? Look no further than these irresistible bacon-wrapped jalapeno poppers! These spicy and cheesy treats are the perfect combination of flavors and textures, making them a crowd favorite at any gathering. Whether you’re hosting a backyard BBQ or attending a potluck, these jalapeno poppers are sure to be a hit. Plus, they’re easy to make and can be prepared ahead of time, allowing you to spend more time with your loved ones on this special holiday.

Ingredients

  • 12 large jalapeno peppers
  • 8 ounces cream cheese, softened
  • 1 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon smoked paprika
  • 12 slices bacon, cut in half
  • Toothpicks

Instructions

  1. Preheat your grill to medium-high heat. If you prefer, you can also use a grill pan or preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C).

  2. While the grill is heating up, prepare the jalapenos. Cut each jalapeno in half lengthwise and use a spoon to remove the seeds and membranes. This step will help reduce the heat of the peppers, making them more enjoyable for everyone.

  3. In a mixing bowl, combine the softened cream cheese, shredded cheddar cheese, garlic powder, and smoked paprika. Mix until well combined.

  4. Carefully fill each jalapeno half with the cream cheese mixture. Make sure to evenly distribute the filling, ensuring that each bite is packed with cheesy goodness.

  5. Once all the jalapeno halves are filled, wrap each one with a half slice of bacon. Secure the bacon in place by inserting a toothpick through the popper.

  6. Place the bacon-wrapped jalapeno poppers on the preheated grill or in the oven. If using a grill, close the lid and cook for 12-15 minutes, or until the bacon is crispy and the peppers are tender. If using the oven, bake for 20-25 minutes, or until the bacon is cooked to your desired level of crispiness.

  7. Once the poppers are cooked, carefully remove them from the grill or oven and allow them to cool for a few minutes before serving. Remember to caution your guests about the hot filling inside!

Serving Suggestions

These bacon-wrapped jalapeno poppers are delicious on their own, but you can take them to the next level by serving them with a side of ranch dressing or a zesty dipping sauce. The coolness of the ranch dressing provides a delightful contrast to the heat of the jalapenos, while a tangy dipping sauce adds an extra layer of flavor.

Make-Ahead Tips

If you’re planning to entertain a large group, you can prepare the jalapeno poppers in advance and refrigerate them until you’re ready to cook. Simply cover the prepared poppers with plastic wrap and store them in the refrigerator for up to 24 hours. This make-ahead option allows you to spend more time enjoying the festivities and less time in the kitchen.

Final Thoughts

The combination of spicy jalapenos, creamy cheese, and smoky bacon makes these Fourth of July bacon-wrapped jalapeno poppers an irresistible appetizer that will leave your guests reaching for more. Whether you’re celebrating with family, friends, or neighbors, these poppers are sure to be a hit at any Fourth of July gathering. So, fire up the grill, get those jalapenos stuffed, and get ready to impress your guests with this unforgettable appetizer! Happy Fourth of July!
