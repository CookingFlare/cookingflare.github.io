Classic Sweet and Sour Pork Stir Fry


Are you craving a delicious and satisfying meal that combines the perfect balance of sweet and sour flavors? Look no further than the classic Sweet and Sour Pork Stir Fry! This timeless dish is a favorite among many, offering a delightful mix of tender pork, colorful vegetables, and a mouthwatering sauce that will tantalize your taste buds. In this recipe, we will guide you through the steps to create this culinary masterpiece in the comfort of your own kitchen.

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound pork loin, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 1 red bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 green bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 onion, sliced
  • 1 cup pineapple chunks
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/2 cup ketchup
  • 1/4 cup rice vinegar
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 1/4 cup brown sugar
  • 1 tablespoon cornstarch
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Cooked rice, for serving

Instructions:

  1. Marinate the Pork: In a bowl, combine the pork pieces with a pinch of salt, pepper, and a tablespoon of soy sauce. Let it marinate for at least 30 minutes to allow the flavors to infuse.

  2. Prepare the Sauce: In a separate bowl, whisk together the ketchup, rice vinegar, soy sauce, brown sugar, and cornstarch until well combined. This will be the delicious sweet and sour sauce that brings the dish together.

  3. Stir-Fry the Pork: Heat a wok or large skillet over high heat. Add a tablespoon of vegetable oil and stir-fry the marinated pork until it is browned and cooked through. Remove the pork from the pan and set it aside.

  4. Cook the Vegetables: In the same pan, add another tablespoon of oil. Stir-fry the garlic, bell peppers, onion, and pineapple until they are slightly softened but still crisp.

  5. Combine Everything: Return the cooked pork to the pan with the vegetables. Pour the sweet and sour sauce over the mixture and stir well to coat everything evenly. Allow the sauce to thicken and bubble.

  6. Serve and Enjoy: Once the sauce has thickened to your desired consistency, remove the pan from the heat. Serve the Sweet and Sour Pork Stir Fry hot over a bed of steamed rice.

Tips for Success:

  • Prep Ahead: Cut all your ingredients and prepare the sauce ahead of time for a quicker cooking process.
  • Customize: Feel free to add other vegetables like carrots, snow peas, or water chestnuts for added crunch and flavor.
  • Adjust Seasonings: Taste the sauce as you go and adjust the sweetness or tanginess according to your preference.

With its vibrant colors, savory pork, and the perfect blend of sweet and sour notes, this Classic Sweet and Sour Pork Stir Fry is sure to become a regular in your meal rotation. Impress your family and friends with this easy-to-follow recipe that delivers big on taste. So, roll up your sleeves, grab your wok, and get ready to delight your senses with this delectable dish!

Remember, cooking is all about creativity and enjoyment, so don’t be afraid to experiment and make this recipe your own. Happy cooking!
