Instant Pot Lemon Herb Salmon: Fresh and Vibrant


When it comes to preparing delicious and nutritious meals quickly, the Instant Pot is a true game-changer. One of the standout recipes you can make in your Instant Pot is Lemon Herb Salmon. This dish combines the fresh flavors of lemon and herbs with tender, perfectly cooked salmon, creating a vibrant and healthy meal that’s sure to impress. Whether you’re cooking for yourself, your family, or a group of friends, this recipe is both simple and sophisticated, making it a perfect choice for any occasion.

Ingredients

To make this delightful Instant Pot Lemon Herb Salmon, you’ll need the following ingredients:

Main Ingredients

  • 4 salmon fillets (each about 6 ounces)
  • 1 lemon, thinly sliced
  • 2 tablespoons of olive oil
  • 3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1/4 cup of fresh dill, chopped
  • 1/4 cup of fresh parsley, chopped
  • 1/4 cup of fresh basil, chopped
  • 1 teaspoon of salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon of black pepper
  • 1/2 cup of chicken or vegetable broth

Optional Garnishes

  • Extra lemon slices
  • Fresh herbs

Instructions

Preparing the Salmon

  1. Season the Salmon: Start by patting the salmon fillets dry with paper towels. This helps the seasoning stick better. Season each fillet generously with salt and black pepper on both sides.

  2. Prepare the Herb Mixture: In a small bowl, combine the minced garlic, chopped dill, parsley, and basil. Mix well until all the herbs and garlic are evenly distributed.

  3. Add Lemon Slices: Place a few thin slices of lemon on top of each salmon fillet. This not only adds flavor but also keeps the salmon moist during cooking.

Setting Up the Instant Pot

  1. Add Broth: Pour the chicken or vegetable broth into the bottom of your Instant Pot. This liquid will create the steam needed to cook the salmon without drying it out.

  2. Insert Trivet: Place a trivet or steamer basket inside the Instant Pot. This keeps the salmon elevated above the liquid, allowing it to steam evenly.

Cooking the Salmon

  1. Arrange the Fillets: Carefully place the seasoned salmon fillets on the trivet, making sure they are evenly spaced and not overlapping. Sprinkle the herb mixture evenly over the top of the fillets.

  2. Pressure Cook: Secure the lid on the Instant Pot, ensuring the valve is set to ‘Sealing’. Select the ‘Pressure Cook’ or ‘Manual’ setting and set the timer for 3 minutes on high pressure.

  3. Natural Release: Once the cooking cycle is complete, allow the pressure to release naturally for 5 minutes. Then, carefully switch the valve to ‘Venting’ to release any remaining pressure. Remove the lid once safe to do so.

Final Touches

  1. Serve: Using tongs, gently transfer the salmon fillets to serving plates. Garnish with extra lemon slices and fresh herbs if desired. Serve immediately while hot.

Tips for the Best Lemon Herb Salmon

  • Fresh Herbs: Using fresh herbs like dill, parsley, and basil enhances the flavor and aroma of the dish. If fresh herbs are not available, you can use dried herbs, but reduce the quantity by half.
  • Quality Salmon: Opt for high-quality, wild-caught salmon if possible. It tends to have a better flavor and texture compared to farm-raised salmon.
  • Avoid Overcooking: Salmon cooks quickly in the Instant Pot. Avoid overcooking by sticking to the recommended cooking time. The residual heat will continue to cook the salmon slightly after it’s been removed from the pot.
  • Lemon: The addition of lemon slices not only infuses the salmon with a bright, citrusy flavor but also helps keep it moist and tender.

Nutritional Benefits

Salmon is a nutritional powerhouse, providing numerous health benefits:

  • Rich in Omega-3 Fatty Acids: These essential fats support heart health, reduce inflammation, and promote brain function.
  • High in Protein: Salmon is an excellent source of high-quality protein, which is vital for muscle repair and growth.
  • Packed with Vitamins and Minerals: Salmon is rich in vitamins B12 and D, as well as selenium, which supports immune function and overall health.
  • Low in Calories: Despite being nutrient-dense, salmon is relatively low in calories, making it an excellent choice for those looking to maintain a healthy weight.

Serving Suggestions

Instant Pot Lemon Herb Salmon pairs beautifully with a variety of side dishes. Here are some ideas to complete your meal:

Vegetables

  • Roasted Asparagus: Drizzle asparagus with olive oil, season with salt and pepper, and roast in the oven until tender.
  • Steamed Broccoli: Lightly steam broccoli florets and toss with a squeeze of lemon juice and a sprinkle of sea salt.
  • Sautéed Spinach: Quickly sauté fresh spinach in a bit of olive oil and garlic until wilted.

Grains

  • Quinoa: Cook quinoa according to package instructions and fluff with a fork. Add a touch of lemon zest for extra flavor.
  • Brown Rice: Serve salmon over a bed of brown rice for a satisfying and nutritious meal.
  • Couscous: Prepare couscous with vegetable broth and stir in some chopped fresh herbs for a light and flavorful side.

Salads

  • Mixed Greens: Toss mixed greens with a light vinaigrette made from olive oil, lemon juice, and Dijon mustard.
  • Cucumber Salad: Combine sliced cucumbers, red onion, and dill with a simple dressing of olive oil and vinegar.
  • Tomato and Avocado Salad: Dice tomatoes and avocados, then mix with chopped basil and a drizzle of balsamic glaze.

Bread

  • Crusty Bread: A slice of warm, crusty bread is perfect for soaking up any leftover lemon-herb juices from the salmon.
  • Garlic Bread: Toast baguette slices with a smear of garlic butter and a sprinkle of parsley for a delicious accompaniment.

Storing and Reheating

This Lemon Herb Salmon stores well, making it ideal for meal prep and leftovers.

Refrigeration

  • Store: Allow the salmon to cool completely before transferring it to an airtight container. Store in the refrigerator for up to 3 days.
  • Reheat: To reheat, place the salmon in a microwave-safe dish and cover loosely with a microwave-safe lid. Heat in short intervals until warmed through. Alternatively, reheat gently on the stovetop over low heat.

Freezing

  • Store: For longer storage, wrap each salmon fillet tightly in plastic wrap and place in a freezer-safe bag or container. Freeze for up to 2 months.
  • Reheat: Thaw overnight in the refrigerator before reheating as mentioned above.

Conclusion

Instant Pot Lemon Herb Salmon is a fresh, vibrant, and incredibly easy dish that brings together the best of quick cooking and gourmet flavor. Its combination of tender salmon, aromatic herbs, and zesty lemon creates a meal that’s both satisfying and healthy. Perfect for busy weeknights or elegant dinners, this recipe is sure to become a favorite in your culinary repertoire. So, grab your Instant Pot and enjoy the delightful taste of Lemon Herb Salmon today!
