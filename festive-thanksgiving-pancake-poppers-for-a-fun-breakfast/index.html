Festive Thanksgiving Pancake Poppers for a Fun Breakfast


Thanksgiving is just around the corner, and you’re probably thinking about what to serve your family and friends for breakfast. Why not try something new and exciting? Introducing Festive Thanksgiving Pancake Poppers, a delicious and fun twist on traditional pancakes. These bite-sized treats are perfect for a special occasion like Thanksgiving, and they’re sure to impress your guests.

What are Pancake Poppers?

Pancake Poppers are essentially mini pancakes filled with sweet or savory ingredients. They’re easy to make and can be customized to fit your taste preferences. For Thanksgiving, we’ll be making a festive version filled with cranberry orange filling and topped with a sweet glaze.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 large egg
  • 2 tablespoons melted butter
  • 1/4 cup cranberry orange filling (see below for recipe)
  • Confectioners’ sugar, for dusting
  • Sweet glaze (see below for recipe)

Cranberry Orange Filling:

  • 1 cup fresh or frozen cranberries
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 2 tablespoons orange juice
  • 1 tablespoon orange zest

Instructions:

  1. In a large bowl, whisk together flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt.
  2. In a separate bowl, whisk together milk, egg, and melted butter.
  3. Add the wet ingredients to the dry ingredients and stir until just combined. The batter should still be slightly lumpy.
  4. Heat a non-stick skillet or griddle over medium heat.
  5. Drop the batter by tablespoonfuls onto the skillet or griddle.
  6. Cook for 2-3 minutes, until bubbles appear on the surface and the edges start to dry.
  7. Flip the pancakes and cook for an additional 1-2 minutes, until golden brown.
  8. Allow the pancakes to cool slightly before filling.
  9. Cut a small hole in the top of each pancake and fill with cranberry orange filling.
  10. Dust with confectioners’ sugar and serve with sweet glaze.

Sweet Glaze:

  • 1 cup powdered sugar
  • 2 tablespoons orange juice
  • 1 tablespoon melted butter

Instructions:

  1. Whisk together powdered sugar, orange juice, and melted butter until smooth.
  2. Drizzle over the pancakes before serving.

Tips and Variations:

  • To make the pancakes ahead of time, cook and cool them completely, then fill and glaze just before serving.
  • Substitute the cranberry orange filling with other sweet or savory ingredients, such as Nutella, peanut butter, or chopped nuts.
  • Add a sprinkle of cinnamon or nutmeg on top of the pancakes for extra flavor and texture.
  • Serve the pancakes with a side of maple syrup or whipped cream for an extra-special treat.

Conclusion:

Festive Thanksgiving Pancake Poppers are the perfect way to start your holiday celebration. They’re easy to make, delicious, and fun to eat. With their sweet and tangy cranberry orange filling and sweet glaze, they’re sure to be a hit with your family and friends. So why not try something new this Thanksgiving and make some Festive Thanksgiving Pancake Poppers? Your taste buds will thank you!
