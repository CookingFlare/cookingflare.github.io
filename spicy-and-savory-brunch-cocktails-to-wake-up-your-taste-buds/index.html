Spicy and Savory Brunch Cocktails to Wake Up Your Taste Buds


Brunch is not just about the food – it’s also about the drinks! When it comes to brunch cocktails, we often think of the classic mimosa or Bloody Mary. While these are undeniably delicious choices, why not spice things up a bit? Literally! Let’s explore some spicy and savory brunch cocktails that will truly wake up your taste buds and elevate your brunch experience.

Spicy Sriracha Bloody Mary

Ingredients:

  • 2 oz vodka
  • 4 oz tomato juice
  • 1/2 oz lemon juice
  • 1/2 tsp Worcestershire sauce
  • 1/4 tsp horseradish
  • 1/4 tsp Sriracha sauce
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Celery stalk and pickled vegetables for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Rim a glass with salt and fill it with ice.
  2. In a shaker, combine vodka, tomato juice, lemon juice, Worcestershire sauce, horseradish, Sriracha, salt, and pepper.
  3. Shake well and strain into the prepared glass.
  4. Garnish with a celery stalk and pickled vegetables.
  5. Enjoy the spicy kick of this revamped classic!

Jalapeño Pineapple Margarita

Ingredients:

  • 2 oz tequila
  • 1 oz triple sec
  • 2 oz pineapple juice
  • 1/2 oz lime juice
  • 1/4 oz agave nectar
  • 2-3 slices of fresh jalapeño
  • Chili powder and salt for rimming
  • Pineapple wedge for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Rim a glass with chili powder and salt.
  2. In a shaker, muddle the jalapeño slices.
  3. Add tequila, triple sec, pineapple juice, lime juice, and agave nectar to the shaker.
  4. Shake well with ice and strain into the prepared glass.
  5. Garnish with a pineapple wedge for a tropical touch.

Spicy Michelada

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 lime, cut into wedges
  • Tajin or chili powder
  • 1 oz hot sauce
  • 1 oz Worcestershire sauce
  • 1/2 oz soy sauce
  • 1 bottle of light beer
  • Ice

Instructions:

  1. Rim a glass with Tajin or chili powder and salt.
  2. Squeeze the lime wedges into the glass and drop them in.
  3. Add hot sauce, Worcestershire sauce, and soy sauce to the glass.
  4. Fill the glass with ice and pour the beer over the top.
  5. Stir gently and enjoy this refreshing and spicy beer cocktail.

Smoky Chipotle Michelada

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 lime, cut into wedges
  • Chipotle chili powder
  • 2 oz mezcal
  • 1 oz lime juice
  • 1/2 oz agave nectar
  • 1/2 oz hot sauce
  • 1 bottle of Mexican lager
  • Ice

Instructions:

  1. Rim a glass with chipotle chili powder and salt.
  2. Squeeze the lime wedges into the glass and drop them in.
  3. In a shaker, combine mezcal, lime juice, agave nectar, and hot sauce with ice.
  4. Strain into the prepared glass and top with Mexican lager.
  5. Stir gently and savor the smoky and spicy flavors.

Spicy Cucumber Gin Fizz

Ingredients:

  • 2 oz gin
  • 1 oz fresh lime juice
  • 1/2 oz simple syrup
  • 3-4 slices of cucumber
  • 2-3 slices of jalapeño
  • Club soda

Instructions:

  1. In a shaker, muddle the cucumber and jalapeño slices.
  2. Add gin, lime juice, and simple syrup to the shaker with ice.
  3. Shake well and strain into a glass filled with ice.
  4. Top with club soda for a refreshing fizz.
  5. Garnish with a cucumber slice for a crisp finish.

These spicy and savory brunch cocktails are sure to add a zing to your weekend brunch gatherings. Whether you’re a fan of the bold flavors of a Bloody Mary or the smoky allure of a mezcal-based cocktail, there’s something for everyone to enjoy. So, next time you’re hosting or attending a brunch, consider shaking up one of these tantalizing drinks to complement your meal and awaken your taste buds in the most delightful way!

Cheers to flavorful and invigorating brunch experiences!
