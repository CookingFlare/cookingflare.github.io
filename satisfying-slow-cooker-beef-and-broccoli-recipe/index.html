Satisfying Slow Cooker Beef and Broccoli Recipe


Are you in the mood for a mouthwatering and satisfying meal that practically cooks itself? Look no further than this delectable Slow Cooker Beef and Broccoli recipe. Packed with flavor and tender, juicy beef, this dish is sure to become a family favorite. The best part? You can set it and forget it, allowing the slow cooker to work its magic while you go about your day. Let’s dive into the details of this irresistible recipe!

Ingredients

  • 2 pounds beef chuck roast, sliced into thin strips
  • 1 cup beef broth
  • ½ cup low-sodium soy sauce
  • ⅓ cup brown sugar
  • 1 tablespoon sesame oil
  • 3 garlic cloves, minced
  • 4 cups broccoli florets
  • 2 tablespoons cornstarch
  • 2 tablespoons water
  • Cooked white rice, for serving
  • Sesame seeds and sliced green onions for garnish

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Beef: In a bowl, whisk together the beef broth, soy sauce, brown sugar, sesame oil, and garlic. Place the beef strips in the slow cooker and pour the sauce over the top. Gently stir to ensure the beef is well-coated. Cover and cook on low heat for 4-5 hours, or until the beef is tender.

  2. Add the Broccoli: About 30 minutes before the beef is done, add the broccoli florets to the slow cooker, stirring gently to combine with the beef. Cover and continue cooking until the broccoli is tender-crisp.

  3. Thicken the Sauce: In a small bowl, dissolve the cornstarch in water. Stir the cornstarch mixture into the slow cooker. Cover and cook on high heat for an additional 30 minutes, or until the sauce has thickened.

  4. Serve and Enjoy: Once the beef and broccoli are cooked to perfection, serve the dish over a bed of cooked white rice. Garnish with sesame seeds and sliced green onions for an extra pop of flavor and visual appeal.

Tips and Tricks

  • For an extra kick of heat, add a dash of red pepper flakes to the sauce mixture.
  • If you prefer a sweeter sauce, increase the amount of brown sugar to suit your taste.
  • Feel free to customize this recipe by adding other vegetables such as bell peppers or snap peas for added color and nutrition.

Why Slow Cooker Beef and Broccoli?

This recipe is a game-changer for busy individuals and families. The slow cooker does all the heavy lifting, transforming simple ingredients into a flavorful and satisfying meal. The tender beef paired with the vibrant broccoli creates a delightful harmony of textures and flavors. Plus, the aroma that fills your home as this dish simmers is simply irresistible.

With minimal prep and maximum flavor, it’s a true winner in the world of slow cooker meals.
