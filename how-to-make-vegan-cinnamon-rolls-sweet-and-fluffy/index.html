How to Make Vegan Cinnamon Rolls: Sweet and Fluffy


Cinnamon rolls are a beloved treat that can brighten any morning or sweeten up an afternoon snack. But what if you’re following a vegan lifestyle? Fear not! This guide will walk you through the process of making delicious vegan cinnamon rolls that are sweet, fluffy, and utterly irresistible. With simple ingredients and straightforward instructions, you’ll have a batch of warm, gooey cinnamon rolls that everyone will enjoy, regardless of their dietary preferences.

Ingredients

For the Dough:

  • 4 cups all-purpose flour (plus more for dusting)
  • 1 packet (2 ¼ teaspoons) active dry yeast
  • ½ cup almond milk (or any plant-based milk)
  • ¼ cup coconut sugar (or granulated sugar)
  • ¼ cup coconut oil (melted)
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon (for flavoring the dough)

For the Filling:

  • ½ cup brown sugar (packed)
  • 2 tablespoons ground cinnamon
  • ¼ cup coconut oil (softened or melted)
  • ¼ cup chopped nuts (optional, such as pecans or walnuts)

For the Frosting:

  • 1 cup powdered sugar
  • 2 tablespoons almond milk (or any plant-based milk)
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Equipment Needed

  • Mixing bowls
  • Rolling pin
  • Baking dish (9x13 inches)
  • Kitchen towel
  • Whisk
  • Measuring cups and spoons

Step-by-Step Instructions

Step 1: Prepare the Dough

  1. Activate the Yeast: In a small bowl, warm the almond milk until it’s lukewarm (about 110°F). Sprinkle the yeast over the warm milk and let it sit for about 5-10 minutes until it becomes frothy. This step is crucial as it proves that your yeast is active.

  2. Combine Dry Ingredients: In a large mixing bowl, combine the flour, coconut sugar, salt, and ground cinnamon. Whisk these ingredients together until they are well mixed.

  3. Mix Wet Ingredients: In another bowl, whisk together the melted coconut oil, vanilla extract, and the activated yeast mixture.

  4. Combine Wet and Dry Ingredients: Pour the wet mixture into the bowl with the dry ingredients. Stir until a shaggy dough forms. You can use a spatula or your hands for this process.

  5. Knead the Dough: Transfer the dough onto a floured surface and knead it for about 8-10 minutes until it becomes smooth and elastic. If the dough is too sticky, sprinkle a little more flour as needed.

  6. Let it Rise: Form the dough into a ball and place it in a greased bowl. Cover it with a kitchen towel and let it rise in a warm place for about 1-2 hours, or until it has doubled in size.

Step 2: Prepare the Filling

  1. Mix the Filling Ingredients: In a small bowl, combine the brown sugar, ground cinnamon, and softened coconut oil. Mix until it forms a paste. If you’re adding nuts, chop them finely and incorporate them into the mixture.

Step 3: Roll Out the Dough

  1. Prepare the Surface: Once the dough has risen, punch it down to release the air. Transfer it back to a floured surface.

  2. Roll the Dough: Using a rolling pin, roll the dough into a rectangle that is about ¼ inch thick. Aim for a size of approximately 12x18 inches.

  3. Spread the Filling: Evenly spread the cinnamon filling over the rolled-out dough. Leave a small border around the edges to prevent the filling from spilling out when you roll it up.

Step 4: Form the Rolls

  1. Roll the Dough: Starting from the long edge, carefully roll the dough into a tight log. Once rolled, pinch the seam to seal it.

  2. Cut the Rolls: Using a sharp knife or a bench scraper, cut the log into 12 equal pieces. You can adjust the size according to your preference; smaller rolls will yield more pieces.

Step 5: Second Rise

  1. Arrange in Baking Dish: Place the cinnamon rolls in a greased baking dish, leaving some space between each roll for them to expand.

  2. Cover and Rise Again: Cover the dish with a kitchen towel and let the rolls rise for another 30-45 minutes, or until they have puffed up.

Step 6: Bake the Cinnamon Rolls

  1. Preheat the Oven: While the rolls are rising, preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C).

  2. Bake: Once the rolls have risen, place them in the preheated oven. Bake for 25-30 minutes, or until they are golden brown and cooked through.

Step 7: Prepare the Frosting

  1. Mix the Frosting: In a small bowl, combine the powdered sugar, almond milk, and vanilla extract. Whisk until smooth. Adjust the consistency by adding more almond milk or powdered sugar if needed.

Step 8: Glaze and Serve

  1. Cool Slightly: Once the cinnamon rolls are done baking, remove them from the oven and let them cool for about 10 minutes.

  2. Drizzle the Frosting: Drizzle the frosting over the warm cinnamon rolls. The heat from the rolls will slightly melt the frosting, creating a delicious glaze.

  3. Enjoy: Serve warm and enjoy your delicious vegan cinnamon rolls with a cup of coffee or tea!

Tips for the Perfect Vegan Cinnamon Rolls

  • Use Fresh Yeast: Always check the expiration date on your yeast. Fresh yeast ensures your rolls rise properly and become fluffy.
  • Temperature Matters: Make sure the almond milk is warm but not hot when activating the yeast. Too hot can kill the yeast, while too cold won’t activate it.
  • Experiment with Flavors: Feel free to add different spices like nutmeg or ginger to the dough or filling for a unique twist.
  • Make Ahead: You can prepare the rolls the night before. After cutting them, place the rolls in the fridge overnight and let them come to room temperature before baking in the morning.
  • Storage: Keep any leftovers in an airtight container at room temperature for up to 3 days. They can also be frozen for up to 2 months. Just thaw and warm them up before serving.

Conclusion

Making vegan cinnamon rolls can be a fun and rewarding experience. With their sweet and fluffy texture, these rolls are sure to please everyone, whether they follow a vegan diet or not. By following this straightforward recipe, you’ll create a delightful treat that fills your kitchen with a warm, inviting aroma. So gather your ingredients, roll up your sleeves, and enjoy the process of baking these delightful vegan cinnamon rolls!
