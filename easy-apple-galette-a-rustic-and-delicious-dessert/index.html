Easy Apple Galette: A Rustic and Delicious Dessert


Are you craving a delightful dessert that captures the essence of cozy evenings and rustic charm? Look no further than the easy apple galette. This simple yet elegant dessert is a perfect combination of flaky pastry, sweet apples, and warm spices, making it a delightful treat for any occasion.

The Allure of Apple Galette

The apple galette, with its free-form crust and beautifully arranged apple slices, exudes a rustic elegance that is hard to resist. It’s a dessert that effortlessly combines the comforting flavors of cinnamon and nutmeg with the natural sweetness of apples, creating a symphony of flavors that dance on your taste buds.

Ingredients

  • For the Pastry:

    • 1 1/4 cups all-purpose flour
    • 1/2 teaspoon salt
    • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, chilled and cubed
    • 1/4 cup ice water

  • For the Filling:

    • 3-4 large apples, such as Granny Smith or Honeycrisp, peeled, cored, and thinly sliced
    • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
    • 2 tablespoons brown sugar
    • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
    • 1/4 teaspoon ground nutmeg
    • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
    • 1 tablespoon all-purpose flour

  • For Assembly:

    • 1 egg, beaten (for egg wash)
    • 1 tablespoon turbinado sugar (for sprinkling)

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Pastry:

    • In a large bowl, combine the flour and salt. Add the chilled, cubed butter and use a pastry cutter or your fingers to work the butter into the flour until the mixture resembles coarse crumbs.
    • Gradually add the ice water, a little at a time, and mix the dough until it comes together. Shape the dough into a disk, wrap it in plastic wrap, and refrigerate for at least 30 minutes.

  2. Prepare the Filling:

    • In a separate bowl, combine the thinly sliced apples, granulated sugar, brown sugar, cinnamon, nutmeg, lemon juice, and flour. Toss the mixture until the apples are evenly coated.

  3. Assemble the Galette:

    • Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C) and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
    • On a lightly floured surface, roll out the chilled pastry dough into a rough circle about 12 inches in diameter. Transfer the dough to the prepared baking sheet.
    • Arrange the apple slices in the center of the dough, leaving a border of about 2 inches. Fold the edges of the dough over the filling, pleating as needed.
    • Brush the pastry with the beaten egg and sprinkle the edges with turbinado sugar.

  4. Bake and Enjoy:

    • Bake the apple galette for 35-40 minutes, or until the crust is golden brown and the apples are tender.
    • Allow the galette to cool slightly before slicing. Serve it warm, perhaps with a scoop of vanilla ice cream or a dollop of freshly whipped cream.

Why You’ll Love It

The apple galette is a dessert that embodies simplicity and elegance. Its rustic appearance and warm, fragrant aroma will draw everyone to the table. Whether you’re hosting a casual gathering or simply want to treat yourself to something special, this easy apple galette is the perfect choice.

Conclusion

In conclusion, the easy apple galette is a delightful dessert that celebrates the natural beauty of apples and the joy of simple, homemade pastry. Its charm lies in its rustic appearance and the way it effortlessly blends the flavors of seasonal fruit and warm spices. Embrace the art of rustic baking with this delicious apple galette, and let its simple elegance become a staple in your dessert repertoire.
