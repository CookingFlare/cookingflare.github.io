BBQ Lettuce Wraps: Light and Fresh Grilled Delights


Are you looking for a healthy and delicious recipe to add to your summer grilling repertoire? Look no further than these BBQ Lettuce Wraps! This recipe combines the smoky flavors of the grill with the light and crisp freshness of lettuce, creating a mouthwatering dish that’s perfect for a light lunch or as an appetizer at your next cookout.

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound of ground chicken or turkey
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 red bell pepper, diced
  • 1 cup of corn kernels
  • 1 teaspoon chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 head of butter lettuce, leaves separated and washed
  • Optional toppings: diced tomatoes, avocado slices, shredded cheese, sour cream, salsa

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your grill to medium-high heat.

  2. In a large skillet, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the onion and garlic, and sauté until they are soft and fragrant.

  3. Add the ground chicken or turkey to the skillet, breaking it up with a spatula, and cook until it is no longer pink.

  4. Stir in the diced bell pepper and corn, and cook for an additional 3-4 minutes until the vegetables are slightly softened.

  5. Season the mixture with chili powder, cumin, salt, and pepper, stirring to ensure the spices are well distributed.

  6. Transfer the filling to a bowl and set it aside.

  7. Place the lettuce leaves on the preheated grill for 30-60 seconds, just until they are slightly charred and wilted. Be careful not to overcook them, as they should still hold their shape and texture.

  8. Remove the lettuce leaves from the grill and let them cool for a minute or two.

  9. To assemble the BBQ Lettuce Wraps, spoon a generous portion of the chicken or turkey filling into each lettuce leaf.

  10. Top with your choice of optional toppings, such as diced tomatoes, avocado slices, shredded cheese, sour cream, or salsa.

  11. Serve the BBQ Lettuce Wraps immediately and enjoy the fresh, smoky flavors straight from the grill!

Tips for Grilling Lettuce:

Grilling lettuce may seem unconventional, but it adds a unique smoky flavor and a delightful charred crunch to your dish. When grilling lettuce for wraps or salads, it’s essential to choose a sturdy variety like romaine or butter lettuce, as they can hold up to the heat without wilting too much. Keep the leaves intact while grilling to prevent them from falling apart or becoming too soft.

Why You’ll Love These BBQ Lettuce Wraps:

These BBQ Lettuce Wraps are a fantastic alternative to traditional heavy, carb-laden wraps or tacos. The combination of light and fresh lettuce with the smoky, flavorful grilled filling creates a dish that’s both satisfying and refreshing. Plus, they’re incredibly versatile, allowing you to customize the toppings to suit your taste preferences.

So, fire up the grill and give these BBQ Lettuce Wraps a try! They’re a delightful departure from the ordinary and are sure to become a new favorite in your summer cooking lineup. Enjoy the light, fresh, and grilled delights straight from your backyard BBQ!
