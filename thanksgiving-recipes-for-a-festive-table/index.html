Thanksgiving Recipes for a Festive Table


Thanksgiving is a time for gathering with loved ones and enjoying delicious food together. If you’re looking to impress your guests and create a festive atmosphere at your table this Thanksgiving, we’ve got you covered with some delightful recipes that will surely make your celebration memorable. From traditional dishes to creative twists, these recipes are sure to please every palate at your holiday feast.

Herb-Roasted Turkey

Ingredients:

  • 1 whole turkey (12-14 pounds)
  • 1/2 cup butter, softened
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons fresh rosemary, chopped
  • 2 tablespoons fresh thyme, chopped
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 325°F (165°C).
  2. Rinse the turkey and pat it dry with paper towels.
  3. In a small bowl, mix the softened butter, garlic, rosemary, thyme, salt, and pepper.
  4. Gently separate the turkey skin from the breast meat and rub the herb butter mixture under the skin.
  5. Place the turkey on a roasting pan and roast it in the preheated oven for about 3 to 4 hours, or until the internal temperature reaches 165°F (74°C).
  6. Let the turkey rest for 20 minutes before carving.

Cranberry Pecan Stuffing

Ingredients:

  • 1 loaf of bread, cubed and toasted
  • 1 cup dried cranberries
  • 1 cup chopped pecans
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 stalks celery, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 cups chicken broth
  • 1/4 cup butter
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a skillet, melt the butter and sauté the onion, celery, and garlic until softened.
  2. In a large bowl, combine the toasted bread cubes, cranberries, pecans, sautéed vegetables, eggs, chicken broth, salt, and pepper.
  3. Mix well and transfer to a baking dish.
  4. Cover with foil and bake at 350°F (175°C) for 30 minutes, then uncover and bake for an additional 15 minutes until golden.

Sweet Potato Casserole

Ingredients:

  • 4 cups mashed sweet potatoes
  • 1/2 cup brown sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1/4 cup butter, melted
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/2 cup chopped pecans
  • 1/2 cup brown sugar
  • 1/3 cup all-purpose flour
  • 3 tablespoons butter, melted

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. In a bowl, mix the mashed sweet potatoes, 1/2 cup brown sugar, eggs, milk, 1/4 cup melted butter, and vanilla extract.
  3. Spread the mixture in a baking dish.
  4. In another bowl, combine the pecans, 1/2 cup brown sugar, flour, and 3 tablespoons melted butter.
  5. Sprinkle the topping over the sweet potato mixture.
  6. Bake for 25-30 minutes until the top is golden brown.

Pumpkin Pie with Whipped Cream

Ingredients:

  • 1 9-inch unbaked pie crust
  • 1 can (15 ounces) pumpkin puree
  • 1 can (14 ounces) sweetened condensed milk
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground ginger
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground nutmeg
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • Whipped cream for topping

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 425°F (220°C).
  2. In a bowl, mix the pumpkin puree, sweetened condensed milk, eggs, spices, and salt until smooth.
  3. Pour the mixture into the unbaked pie crust.
  4. Bake for 15 minutes, then reduce the temperature to 350°F (175°C) and bake for an additional 35-40 minutes until the filling is set.
  5. Let the pie cool before serving with whipped cream.

These Thanksgiving recipes are sure to add a touch of warmth and flavor to your festive table. Whether you’re hosting a large gathering or a small intimate dinner, these dishes will surely delight your guests and make your Thanksgiving celebration truly special. Enjoy the process of cooking and sharing these delicious meals with your loved ones. Happy Thanksgiving!
