Elevate Your Brunch with These Herbal Infused Cocktails


Brunch is a delightful blend of breakfast and lunch where you can savor both sweet and savory dishes. But why stop at delicious food when you can take your brunch experience to the next level with herbal infused cocktails? These refreshing and aromatic drinks will add a sophisticated touch to your brunch gathering, impressing your guests and tantalizing their taste buds. Let’s explore some unique and flavorful herbal infused cocktail recipes that will elevate your next brunch affair.

Lavender Lemonade Mimosa

Prep Time: 10 minutes | Total Time: 10 minutes | Serves: 4

Ingredients:

  • 1 bottle of champagne
  • 1 cup of freshly squeezed lemon juice
  • ½ cup of lavender simple syrup
  • Fresh lavender sprigs for garnish

Instructions:

  1. In a pitcher, combine the champagne, freshly squeezed lemon juice, and lavender simple syrup.
  2. Stir gently to mix the ingredients together.
  3. Pour the lavender lemonade mimosa into champagne flutes.
  4. Garnish each glass with a fresh lavender sprig.
  5. Serve immediately and enjoy the delightful floral aroma and tangy flavor of this enchanting cocktail.

Rosemary Grapefruit Spritz

Prep Time: 15 minutes | Total Time: 15 minutes | Serves: 4

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups of freshly squeezed grapefruit juice
  • 1 cup of sparkling water
  • ½ cup of rosemary infused simple syrup
  • Ice cubes
  • Fresh rosemary sprigs and grapefruit slices for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Fill each glass with ice cubes.
  2. In a pitcher, mix the freshly squeezed grapefruit juice and rosemary infused simple syrup.
  3. Pour the grapefruit mixture into the glasses over the ice.
  4. Top off each glass with sparkling water.
  5. Garnish with fresh rosemary sprigs and grapefruit slices.
  6. Indulge in the invigorating combination of tangy grapefruit and aromatic rosemary in this delightful spritz.

Basil Cucumber Cooler

Prep Time: 12 minutes | Total Time: 12 minutes | Serves: 4

Ingredients:

  • 1 ½ cups of gin
  • ½ cup of freshly squeezed lime juice
  • 1 cup of cucumber juice
  • ¼ cup of basil infused simple syrup
  • Thinly sliced cucumbers and fresh basil leaves for garnish

Instructions:

  1. In a shaker, combine the gin, freshly squeezed lime juice, cucumber juice, and basil infused simple syrup.
  2. Shake well to thoroughly mix the ingredients.
  3. Fill glasses with ice and pour the cucumber cooler over the ice.
  4. Garnish each glass with thinly sliced cucumbers and fresh basil leaves.
  5. Delight in the cool and herbaceous notes of this rejuvenating cocktail.

Thyme Honey Bourbon Smash

Prep Time: 10 minutes | Total Time: 10 minutes | Serves: 4

Ingredients:

  • 1 ½ cups of bourbon
  • ½ cup of freshly squeezed lemon juice
  • ¼ cup of honey
  • Fresh thyme sprigs
  • Lemon slices for garnish

Instructions:

  1. In a pitcher, mix the bourbon, freshly squeezed lemon juice, and honey.
  2. Add fresh thyme sprigs to the mixture and muddle gently to release the herb’s essence.
  3. Fill glasses with ice and pour the bourbon smash over the ice.
  4. Garnish each glass with a slice of lemon and a sprig of fresh thyme.
  5. Savor the rich and earthy flavors of this aromatic bourbon smash.

These herbal infused cocktails will undoubtedly add an extra layer of sophistication and flavor to your brunch experience. With their captivating aromas and delightful tastes, they are sure to be the highlight of your gathering. Impress your guests and elevate your brunch to new heights with these enchanting and refreshing drinks.

So, next time you’re planning a brunch affair, consider incorporating these herbal infused cocktails into your menu. Your guests will be pleasantly surprised with the innovative and delightful flavors that await them. Cheers to a brunch experience that is nothing short of extraordinary!
