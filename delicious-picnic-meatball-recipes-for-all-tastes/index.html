Delicious Picnic Meatball Recipes for All Tastes


Are you planning a picnic and looking for delicious and easy-to-make recipes that everyone will enjoy? Look no further than these mouthwatering picnic meatball recipes! From classic flavors to unique twists, these recipes are sure to satisfy everyone’s taste buds. So grab your picnic blanket and get ready to indulge in these delectable meatball dishes.

1. Tangy BBQ Meatballs

Preparation time: 15 minutes Cooking time: 25 minutes Servings: 4-6

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1/2 cup breadcrumbs
  • 1/4 cup milk
  • 1/4 cup finely chopped onion
  • 1/4 cup finely chopped parsley
  • 1 egg
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1 cup barbecue sauce
  • 1/4 cup honey
  • 2 tablespoons apple cider vinegar

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 375째F (190째C) and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
  2. In a large bowl, combine the ground beef, breadcrumbs, milk, onion, parsley, egg, garlic powder, paprika, salt, and black pepper. Mix well until all the ingredients are evenly incorporated.
  3. Shape the mixture into bite-sized meatballs and place them on the prepared baking sheet.
  4. Bake the meatballs for 20 minutes, or until they are cooked through and slightly browned.
  5. In a separate bowl, whisk together the barbecue sauce, honey, and apple cider vinegar.
  6. Once the meatballs are cooked, transfer them to a large skillet and pour the barbecue sauce mixture over them.
  7. Cook the meatballs in the sauce over medium heat for 5 minutes, stirring occasionally, until the sauce thickens and coats the meatballs.
  8. Serve the tangy BBQ meatballs on a platter or skewer them for easy picnic enjoyment.

2. Italian Style Meatball Subs

Preparation time: 20 minutes Cooking time: 30 minutes Servings: 4

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound ground pork
  • 1/2 pound ground beef
  • 1/2 cup breadcrumbs
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/4 cup finely chopped fresh basil
  • 1/4 cup finely chopped fresh parsley
  • 1/4 cup finely chopped onion
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 egg
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • 4 sub rolls
  • 1 cup marinara sauce
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 375째F (190째C) and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
  2. In a large bowl, combine the ground pork, ground beef, breadcrumbs, Parmesan cheese, basil, parsley, onion, garlic, egg, salt, and black pepper. Mix well until all the ingredients are evenly incorporated.
  3. Shape the mixture into meatballs, approximately 1 inch in diameter, and place them on the prepared baking sheet.
  4. Bake the meatballs for 20 minutes, or until they are cooked through and lightly browned.
  5. Slice the sub rolls lengthwise, but not all the way through, creating a pocket for the meatballs.
  6. Spread marinara sauce inside each sub roll, then place 4-5 meatballs in each roll.
  7. Top the meatballs with shredded mozzarella cheese.
  8. Bake the meatball subs in the oven for an additional 10 minutes, or until the cheese is melted and bubbly.
  9. Serve the Italian style meatball subs warm, and if you’re planning a picnic, wrap them individually in foil for easy transportation.

3. Sweet and Sour Chicken Meatballs

Preparation time: 25 minutes Cooking time: 30 minutes Servings: 6-8

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound ground chicken
  • 1/2 cup breadcrumbs
  • 1/4 cup finely chopped green bell pepper
  • 1/4 cup finely chopped onion
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 egg
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1/4 cup cornstarch
  • 1/4 cup water
  • 1/4 cup apple cider vinegar
  • 1/4 cup ketchup
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons honey
  • 1 tablespoon brown sugar
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil

Instructions:

  1. In a large bowl, combine the ground chicken, breadcrumbs, green bell pepper, onion, garlic, egg, salt, and black pepper. Mix well until all the ingredients are evenly incorporated.
  2. Shape the mixture into meatballs, approximately 1 inch in diameter.
  3. In a separate bowl, whisk together the cornstarch and water until smooth.
  4. In a saucepan, combine the apple cider vinegar, ketchup, soy sauce, honey, brown sugar, and vegetable oil. Stir in the cornstarch mixture.
  5. Cook the sauce over medium heat, stirring constantly, until it thickens and comes to a boil.
  6. Gently add the meatballs to the sauce and coat them evenly.
  7. Reduce the heat to low and simmer the meatballs in the sauce for 20 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  8. Serve the sweet and sour chicken meatballs over steamed rice or as a standalone dish during your picnic.

Enjoy these delicious picnic meatball recipes and make your outdoor gathering memorable with these flavorful and satisfying dishes. Whether you prefer tangy BBQ, Italian-style subs, or sweet and sour chicken, there is a meatball recipe here to suit every taste. So pack your picnic basket and savor these scrumptious meatball creations in the great outdoors. Happy picnicking!

Note: Remember to keep your picnic food properly chilled or insulated during transportation to maintain food safety standards.
