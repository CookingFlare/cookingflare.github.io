Italian-Style Chicken and Vegetable Frittata Recipe


If you’re craving a taste of Italy with a twist, this Italian-style chicken and vegetable frittata recipe is the perfect dish to satisfy your culinary desires. Frittata, a versatile egg-based dish, is a beloved part of Italian cuisine, and this recipe infuses it with the savory goodness of chicken and a medley of vibrant vegetables. Whether you’re hosting a brunch, preparing a quick weeknight meal, or looking for a protein-packed dish to fuel your day, this frittata is a delightful choice that can be enjoyed at any time.

Ingredients

  • 6 large eggs
  • 1 cup cooked chicken, shredded
  • 1 cup bell peppers, diced (a mix of red, yellow, and green for vibrant color)
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1/2 cup red onion, finely chopped
  • 1/2 cup fresh spinach, roughly chopped
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/4 cup milk
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Note: Feel free to customize the vegetable selection based on your preferences or what’s available in your kitchen.

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C).

  2. In a large bowl, whisk together the eggs, milk, Parmesan cheese, dried oregano, salt, and pepper until well combined. Set aside.

  3. In a 10-inch oven-safe skillet, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the minced garlic and sauté for about 30 seconds until fragrant.

  4. Add the diced bell peppers and chopped red onion to the skillet. Sauté for 3-4 minutes until the vegetables begin to soften.

  5. Toss in the shredded chicken and cherry tomatoes. Cook for an additional 2-3 minutes, allowing the flavors to meld together.

  6. Spread the chopped spinach evenly over the chicken and vegetable mixture in the skillet.

  7. Carefully pour the egg mixture over the top, ensuring that it evenly coats the entire skillet. Gently shake the skillet to allow the egg mixture to settle around the ingredients.

  8. Cook the frittata on the stovetop for 4-5 minutes until the edges start to set.

  9. Transfer the skillet to the preheated oven and bake for 15-20 minutes, or until the frittata is set in the center and the top is lightly golden.

  10. Once cooked, remove the skillet from the oven and let the frittata rest for a few minutes before slicing and serving.

Serving Suggestions

This Italian-style chicken and vegetable frittata is delightful when served warm, but it also makes for a delicious cold leftover meal. Pair it with a crisp green salad dressed with balsamic vinaigrette for a refreshing contrast, or serve it alongside some crusty bread for a more substantial meal. For a brunch spread, consider adding a side of fresh fruit and a selection of cheeses to complement the flavors of the frittata.

Conclusion

With its colorful array of vegetables, tender shreds of chicken, and the rich creaminess of the eggs, this Italian-style frittata is a celebration of flavors and textures that will surely captivate your taste buds. Whether you’re a fan of Italian cuisine or simply looking to add more variety to your cooking repertoire, this recipe is a wonderful way to explore the culinary traditions of Italy from the comfort of your own kitchen. Embrace the simplicity and versatility of the frittata, and savor the delightful combination of ingredients that make this dish a true standout.

Buon appetito!
