Wholesome and Flavorful Middle Eastern Beef Recipes


Middle Eastern cuisine is known for its bold flavors and wholesome ingredients. If you’re a fan of beef and want to explore the rich culinary heritage of the Middle East, then you’re in for a treat. In this blog post, we’ll be sharing some delightful and flavorful Middle Eastern beef recipes that are sure to tantalize your taste buds. So, let’s dive right in!

1. Shawarma Beef Skewers

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound beef sirloin, thinly sliced
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon ground coriander
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground turmeric
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, mix together the olive oil, minced garlic, ground cumin, ground coriander, paprika, ground turmeric, salt, and pepper.
  2. Add the thinly sliced beef to the marinade and coat it well. Let it marinate for at least 30 minutes.
  3. Preheat your grill to medium-high heat.
  4. Thread the marinated beef slices onto skewers.
  5. Grill the skewers for about 3-4 minutes per side, or until cooked to your desired level of doneness.
  6. Serve the Shawarma Beef Skewers with warm pita bread, tzatziki sauce, and a side of fresh salad.

2. Lebanese Beef Kofta

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 small onion, finely grated
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1/2 cup fresh parsley, finely chopped
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground coriander
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a mixing bowl, combine the ground beef, finely grated onion, minced garlic, fresh parsley, ground cumin, ground coriander, ground cinnamon, salt, and pepper.
  2. Mix the ingredients together until well combined.
  3. Divide the mixture into small portions and shape them into oval-shaped patties.
  4. Preheat your grill or stovetop griddle to medium-high heat.
  5. Cook the beef kofta patties for about 4-5 minutes per side, or until they are cooked through.
  6. Serve the Lebanese Beef Kofta with warm pita bread, hummus, and a side of tabbouleh salad.

3. Persian Beef Stew (Ghormeh Sabzi)

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound beef stew meat, cubed
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup fresh parsley, finely chopped
  • 1 cup fresh cilantro, finely chopped
  • 1 cup fresh fenugreek leaves, finely chopped (optional)
  • 1/2 cup dried kidney beans, soaked overnight
  • 1 teaspoon ground turmeric
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a large pot, heat the olive oil over medium heat.
  2. Add the chopped onion and minced garlic to the pot and sauté until they become translucent.
  3. Add the cubed beef stew meat to the pot and cook until browned on all sides.
  4. Add the chopped fresh parsley, cilantro, and fenugreek leaves to the pot. Stir well.
  5. Drain the soaked kidney beans and add them to the pot.
  6. Season the stew with ground turmeric, ground cumin, salt, and pepper. Mix well.
  7. Add enough water to cover the ingredients in the pot.
  8. Cover the pot and simmer the stew on low heat for about 2-3 hours, or until the beef is tender and the flavors are well incorporated.
  9. Serve the Persian Beef Stew with steamed basmati rice and a side of pickled vegetables.

These Middle Eastern beef recipes are just a taste of the incredible flavors that this region has to offer. Whether you’re hosting a dinner party or simply looking to explore new culinary horizons, these dishes are sure to impress. So, put on your apron, gather your ingredients, and embark on a flavorful journey through the Middle East. Enjoy!
