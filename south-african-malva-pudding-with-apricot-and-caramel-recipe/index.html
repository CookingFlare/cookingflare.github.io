South African Malva Pudding with Apricot and Caramel Recipe


If you’re a fan of rich, indulgent desserts, then you’re in for a treat with this South African Malva Pudding with Apricot and Caramel recipe. This delectable dessert is a beloved classic in South African cuisine, known for its moist, spongy texture and irresistible sweet flavors. Traditionally served warm with a decadent caramel sauce, this pudding is sure to become a favorite in your recipe collection.

Ingredients

For the Pudding:

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1 pinch of salt
  • 1 tablespoon smooth apricot jam
  • 1 tablespoon vinegar
  • 1 egg
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1 tablespoon butter
  • 1 teaspoon apricot jam
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice

For the Caramel Sauce:

  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 1 cup cream
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla essence

Instructions

For the Pudding:

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (180°C) and grease a baking dish.

  2. In a large bowl, sift together the flour, baking soda, and salt.

  3. In a separate bowl, mix the apricot jam, vinegar, egg, and sugar until well combined.

  4. In a small saucepan, melt the butter and add the teaspoon of apricot jam. Stir until smooth.

  5. In a separate bowl, mix the baking soda, milk, and lemon juice. Add this mixture to the egg and sugar mixture.

  6. Gradually add the dry ingredients to the wet ingredients, mixing until just combined.

  7. Pour the batter into the greased baking dish and bake for 30-40 minutes, or until the pudding is golden and a skewer inserted into the center comes out clean.

For the Caramel Sauce:

  1. In a saucepan, heat the sugar over medium heat, stirring constantly until it melts and turns a golden caramel color.

  2. Add the butter and stir until melted and combined with the caramelized sugar.

  3. Slowly pour in the cream while stirring continuously. Be careful as the mixture will bubble up.

  4. Stir in the vanilla essence and continue to cook for 2-3 minutes until the sauce thickens slightly.

  5. Remove from heat and let the caramel sauce cool slightly before serving.

Serving

To serve, poke holes in the warm pudding using a skewer and pour half of the warm caramel sauce over the top, allowing it to soak into the pudding. Serve the remaining caramel sauce on the side. This delectable dessert is best enjoyed warm and pairs perfectly with a dollop of whipped cream or a scoop of vanilla ice cream.

Conclusion

South African Malva Pudding with Apricot and Caramel is a delightful dessert that combines the comforting flavors of apricot, caramel, and warm sponge cake. Whether you’re hosting a dinner party or simply craving a sweet indulgence, this recipe is sure to impress. The rich, moist texture of the pudding, combined with the luscious caramel sauce, makes it a true crowd-pleaser. So, roll up your sleeves, gather your ingredients, and treat yourself to a taste of South African culinary tradition with this irresistible Malva Pudding recipe.
