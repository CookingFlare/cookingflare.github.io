Tarta de Nueces: Spanish Walnut Tart Done Right


If you’re a fan of rich, nutty desserts, then Tarta de Nueces, or Spanish Walnut Tart, is an absolute must-try. This delectable dessert combines the earthy flavor of walnuts with a buttery, flaky crust, resulting in a delightful treat that’s perfect for any occasion. In this recipe, we’ll walk you through the steps to create the perfect Tarta de Nueces that will have your taste buds singing with joy.

Ingredients

For the Crust:

  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, chilled and cubed
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • A pinch of salt
  • 1 egg yolk
  • 2-3 tablespoons ice water

For the Filling:

  • 2 cups walnuts, finely chopped
  • 1 cup brown sugar
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • 1/4 cup unsalted butter
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • A pinch of salt

Instructions

Making the Crust

  1. In a large mixing bowl, combine the flour, sugar, and salt. Add the chilled butter cubes and use a pastry cutter or your fingertips to rub the butter into the flour mixture until it resembles coarse crumbs.

  2. Add the egg yolk and 2 tablespoons of ice water to the flour mixture. Stir with a fork until the dough starts coming together. If needed, add an additional tablespoon of ice water. Be careful not to overwork the dough.

  3. Shape the dough into a disk, wrap it in plastic wrap, and refrigerate for at least 30 minutes.

  4. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).

  5. Once chilled, roll out the dough on a floured surface to fit a 9-inch tart pan. Carefully transfer the dough to the pan, pressing it gently into the bottom and sides. Trim any excess dough.

  6. Prick the bottom of the crust with a fork and line it with parchment paper. Fill the crust with pie weights or dried beans to prevent it from puffing up during baking.

  7. Bake the crust for 15 minutes, then remove the parchment paper and weights. Continue baking for an additional 10-15 minutes, or until the crust is golden brown. Set aside to cool while you prepare the filling.

Making the Filling

  1. In a saucepan, combine the brown sugar, heavy cream, and butter. Cook over medium heat, stirring constantly, until the sugar is dissolved and the mixture is smooth. Remove from heat and let it cool slightly.

  2. In a separate bowl, whisk together the eggs, vanilla extract, and salt.

  3. Gradually pour the warm sugar mixture into the egg mixture, whisking constantly to prevent the eggs from scrambling.

  4. Stir in the chopped walnuts until they are well coated with the sugar mixture.

Assembling and Baking

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C).

  2. Pour the walnut filling into the pre-baked tart crust, spreading it out evenly.

  3. Place the tart on a baking sheet and bake for 25-30 minutes, or until the filling is set and the crust is golden.

  4. Allow the tart to cool completely before slicing and serving.

Enjoy Your Tarta de Nueces!

Once cooled, slice your Tarta de Nueces into elegant portions and savor the rich, nutty flavor of this delightful Spanish Walnut Tart. Whether served with a dollop of whipped cream or a scoop of vanilla ice cream, this dessert is sure to impress your family and friends. So, don’t wait any longer – get ready to indulge in the heavenly flavors of Tarta de Nueces!
