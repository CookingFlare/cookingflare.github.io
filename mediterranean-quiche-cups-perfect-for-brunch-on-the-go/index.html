Mediterranean Quiche Cups: Perfect for Brunch On-the-Go


Who doesn’t love a delicious and satisfying brunch? The perfect combination of breakfast and lunch, brunch offers a leisurely way to start the day. However, sometimes our busy schedules make it difficult to enjoy a leisurely meal. That’s where Mediterranean Quiche Cups come in. These delightful and portable quiche cups are the ideal solution for a brunch on-the-go. Packed with Mediterranean flavors, they are not only convenient but also bursting with taste.

Ingredients

To make these Mediterranean Quiche Cups, you will need the following ingredients:

  • 1 package of ready-made pie crusts
  • 1 cup of cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1 cup of baby spinach, chopped
  • 1/2 cup of feta cheese, crumbled
  • 1/4 cup of black olives, sliced
  • 4 eggs
  • 1/2 cup of milk
  • 1 teaspoon of dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C). Grease a muffin tin or line it with cupcake liners.

  2. Roll out the ready-made pie crusts and use a round cookie cutter or a glass to cut out circles slightly larger than the muffin tin wells. Press the circles into the wells, ensuring they cover the bottom and sides.

  3. In a bowl, whisk together the eggs, milk, dried oregano, salt, and pepper until well combined.

  4. Divide the cherry tomatoes, baby spinach, feta cheese, and black olives evenly among the prepared pie crusts.

  5. Pour the egg mixture over the fillings in the pie crusts, filling each cup about three-quarters full.

  6. Bake the quiche cups in the preheated oven for 20-25 minutes, or until the tops are golden brown and the eggs are set.

  7. Remove the quiche cups from the oven and allow them to cool slightly before removing them from the muffin tin.

  8. Serve the Mediterranean Quiche Cups warm or at room temperature. They can be enjoyed on their own or paired with a fresh salad for a complete brunch experience.

Why Mediterranean Quiche Cups?

Mediterranean Quiche Cups are the perfect brunch option for several reasons. Firstly, their portable nature makes them ideal for those busy mornings when you need to grab a quick bite on your way out. Simply pack them in a container or wrap them in foil, and you’re ready to go.

Secondly, the Mediterranean flavors in these quiche cups add a unique twist to the traditional quiche. The combination of cherry tomatoes, baby spinach, feta cheese, and black olives creates a harmonious blend of tangy, savory, and fresh flavors that will awaken your taste buds.

Lastly, these quiche cups are incredibly versatile. You can easily customize the fillings to suit your preferences or use up any ingredients you have on hand. Try adding roasted red peppers, sun-dried tomatoes, or even a sprinkle of fresh herbs for an extra burst of flavor.

In Conclusion

Mediterranean Quiche Cups are a fantastic option for brunch on-the-go. With their convenient size and delicious flavors, they are sure to become a favorite in your brunch repertoire. Whether you’re heading to work, going on a picnic, or simply looking for a quick and tasty meal, these quiche cups have got you covered. Give them a try and elevate your brunch game to a whole new level!
