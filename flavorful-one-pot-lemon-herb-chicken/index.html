Flavorful One Pot Lemon Herb Chicken


If you’re looking for a delicious and easy one-pot meal, this Flavorful One Pot Lemon Herb Chicken is the perfect recipe for you. Packed with vibrant flavors and simple to make, this dish is sure to become a family favorite. The combination of zesty lemon, aromatic herbs, and tender chicken creates a delightful culinary experience that will leave everyone wanting more. Let’s dive into the recipe and discover how to create this mouthwatering dish in just a few simple steps.

Ingredients

  • 4 bone-in, skin-on chicken thighs
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 1 cup chicken broth
  • 1 lemon, sliced
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • 1 teaspoon dried rosemary
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley for garnish

Instructions

  1. Sear the Chicken: Begin by heating the olive oil in a large, oven-safe skillet over medium-high heat. Season the chicken thighs with salt and pepper, then place them skin side down in the skillet. Sear for about 5-6 minutes on each side until golden brown. Once done, remove the chicken from the skillet and set it aside.

  2. Sauté the Aromatics: In the same skillet, add the minced garlic and chopped onion. Sauté for a few minutes until the onion becomes translucent and the garlic is fragrant.

  3. Deglaze the Pan: Pour in the chicken broth and use a wooden spoon to scrape up any browned bits from the bottom of the skillet. This will infuse the broth with all the delicious flavors from the seared chicken and aromatics.

  4. Add the Chicken and Herbs: Return the seared chicken thighs to the skillet, nestling them into the liquid. Add the dried thyme and rosemary, then place the lemon slices on top of the chicken.

  5. Simmer and Bake: Bring the liquid to a gentle simmer, then cover the skillet and transfer it to a preheated oven. Let the chicken bake at 375°F (190°C) for 25-30 minutes, or until the chicken is cooked through and the skin is crispy.

  6. Garnish and Serve: Once the chicken is done, remove the skillet from the oven. Garnish the dish with freshly chopped parsley for a pop of color and added freshness. Serve the chicken hot, accompanied by your favorite sides such as roasted vegetables, mashed potatoes, or a crisp salad.

Enjoy Your Flavorful Creation

The aroma of zesty lemon and fragrant herbs will fill your kitchen as you prepare this Flavorful One Pot Lemon Herb Chicken. The tender, juicy chicken paired with the bright flavors of lemon and the earthy notes of thyme and rosemary make this dish a true delight for the senses. Whether you’re cooking for a special occasion or simply craving a comforting meal, this recipe is versatile and always a crowd-pleaser. Plus, with the convenience of cooking everything in just one pot, cleanup is a breeze.

So, gather your ingredients, preheat your oven, and get ready to savor every bite of this delectable dish. Once you experience the harmonious blend of flavors in this Flavorful One Pot Lemon Herb Chicken, it’s sure to earn a permanent spot in your recipe collection. Happy cooking!
