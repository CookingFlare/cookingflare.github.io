Butternut Squash and Spinach Lasagna: A Vegetarian Fall Dish


If you’re looking for a hearty and delicious vegetarian dish to savor during the fall season, look no further than Butternut Squash and Spinach Lasagna. This comforting and flavorful lasagna is filled with layers of creamy butternut squash, tender spinach, and gooey cheese. It’s the perfect dish to warm you up on a chilly evening and impress your friends and family at your next dinner gathering. So, let’s dive right into the recipe!

Ingredients

  • 1 butternut squash, peeled, seeded, and cut into thin slices
  • 8 lasagna noodles
  • 2 cups fresh spinach
  • 2 cups ricotta cheese
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh basil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1/2 teaspoon nutmeg
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Olive oil for drizzling

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. In a large pot of salted boiling water, cook the lasagna noodles according to the package instructions until al dente. Drain and set aside.
  3. Meanwhile, in a separate pot of boiling water, blanch the butternut squash slices for about 5 minutes until slightly tender. Drain and set aside.
  4. In a mixing bowl, combine the ricotta cheese, minced garlic, chopped basil, dried oregano, nutmeg, salt, and pepper. Mix well until all the ingredients are evenly incorporated.
  5. In a 9x13-inch baking dish, spread a thin layer of the ricotta cheese mixture on the bottom.
  6. Arrange a layer of cooked lasagna noodles on top of the ricotta cheese mixture.
  7. Layer half of the blanched butternut squash slices on top of the lasagna noodles.
  8. Add a layer of fresh spinach leaves on top of the butternut squash slices.
  9. Sprinkle a generous amount of mozzarella and Parmesan cheese over the spinach layer.
  10. Repeat the layers starting with the ricotta cheese mixture, followed by the lasagna noodles, butternut squash slices, spinach, and cheese until all the ingredients are used.
  11. Finish with a layer of lasagna noodles and sprinkle the remaining mozzarella and Parmesan cheese on top.
  12. Drizzle some olive oil over the lasagna to prevent it from drying out while baking.
  13. Cover the baking dish with aluminum foil and bake in the preheated oven for 30 minutes.
  14. Remove the foil and continue baking for an additional 10-15 minutes, or until the cheese is golden and bubbly.
  15. Once baked, remove the lasagna from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes before serving.
  16. Slice into portions and garnish with fresh basil leaves if desired.
  17. Serve hot and enjoy the delightful flavors of this Butternut Squash and Spinach Lasagna!

Tips and Variations

  • To save time, you can use no-boil lasagna noodles instead of cooking regular lasagna noodles. Just layer them directly into the dish without boiling.
  • For a vegan version, substitute the ricotta cheese with tofu ricotta and use vegan cheese alternatives.
  • Add a layer of sautéed mushrooms or caramelized onions for an extra burst of flavor.
  • Feel free to experiment with other types of cheese like fontina or goat cheese for a unique twist.
  • Serve the lasagna with a side of garlic bread and a crisp green salad for a complete and satisfying meal.

Conclusion

Butternut Squash and Spinach Lasagna is a vegetarian fall dish that will please even the most devout meat-lovers. With its creamy butternut squash, vibrant spinach, and layers of melted cheese, it’s a comforting and flavorful option for any occasion. Whether you’re hosting a dinner party or simply craving a cozy meal on a cool evening, this lasagna is sure to impress. So, gather your ingredients, follow the simple steps, and indulge in the deliciousness of this vegetarian delight.
