Vegan Cinnamon Rolls: A Warm and Gooey Breakfast Treat


Indulge in the comforting aroma of freshly baked vegan cinnamon rolls that are sure to satisfy your sweet tooth and warm your heart. These fluffy and gooey treats are a perfect way to start your day with a touch of cinnamon-infused bliss. Whether you’re a vegan or simply looking for a healthier alternative to traditional cinnamon rolls, this recipe will not disappoint. So, roll up your sleeves and let’s get baking!

Ingredients

For the dough:

  • 2 ¼ cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 ½ teaspoons instant yeast
  • 1 cup almond milk, warmed
  • ¼ cup coconut oil, melted
  • ¼ cup maple syrup
  • ½ teaspoon salt

For the filling:

  • ½ cup brown sugar
  • 2 tablespoons ground cinnamon
  • 3 tablespoons coconut oil, softened

For the glaze:

  • 1 cup powdered sugar
  • 2 tablespoons almond milk
  • ½ teaspoon vanilla extract

Instructions

  1. Prepare the dough:

    In a large mixing bowl, combine the all-purpose flour, instant yeast, and salt. Mix well.

  2. Make the dough rise:

    In a separate bowl, whisk together the warmed almond milk, melted coconut oil, and maple syrup. Pour the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients and mix until a soft dough forms.

    Transfer the dough to a floured surface and knead for about 5 minutes, until the dough becomes smooth and elastic.

    Place the dough in a greased bowl, cover it with a clean kitchen towel, and let it rise in a warm place for about 1 hour, or until it doubles in size.

  3. Prepare the filling:

    While the dough is rising, prepare the cinnamon sugar filling. In a small bowl, mix together the brown sugar and ground cinnamon.

  4. Roll out the dough:

    Once the dough has risen, transfer it to a floured surface and roll it out into a rectangle, approximately ¼ inch thick.

    Spread the softened coconut oil evenly over the dough, leaving a small border around the edges.

    Sprinkle the cinnamon sugar mixture over the coconut oil, ensuring it covers the entire surface.

  5. Roll and slice:

    Starting from one of the longer edges, tightly roll the dough into a log. Use a sharp knife to slice the log into 12 equal-sized rolls.

    Place the rolls in a greased baking dish, leaving a little space between each roll to allow for rising.

  6. Let it rise again:

    Cover the baking dish with a clean kitchen towel and let the rolls rise for another 30 minutes.

  7. Bake to perfection:

    Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C). Once the rolls have risen, bake them for 20-25 minutes, or until they turn golden brown.

  8. Glaze it up:

    While the rolls are baking, prepare the glaze by whisking together the powdered sugar, almond milk, and vanilla extract until smooth.

  9. Serve and enjoy:

    Once the cinnamon rolls are ready, remove them from the oven and let them cool for a few minutes. Drizzle the glaze over the warm rolls, allowing it to seep into the cracks and crevices.

    Serve these delicious vegan cinnamon rolls warm and gooey for the ultimate breakfast treat. They pair perfectly with a steaming cup of coffee or a refreshing glass of almond milk.

Conclusion

These vegan cinnamon rolls are a delightful twist on a classic breakfast favorite. With their soft, fluffy texture and irresistible cinnamon sugar filling, they are sure to become a staple in your morning routine. Whether you’re a vegan or simply looking to explore new plant-based recipes, these rolls are a delicious way to start your day. So, gather your ingredients, preheat your oven, and prepare to indulge in the warm and gooey goodness of these vegan cinnamon rolls. Enjoy!
