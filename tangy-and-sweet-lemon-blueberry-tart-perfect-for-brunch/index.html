Tangy and Sweet Lemon Blueberry Tart: Perfect for Brunch


Are you looking for a delightful and refreshing dessert to serve at your next brunch gathering? Look no further! This Tangy and Sweet Lemon Blueberry Tart is the perfect choice. With its vibrant flavors and delicate texture, it will surely impress your guests and leave them wanting more.

Ingredients

For the crust:

  • 1 ½ cups of graham cracker crumbs
  • 6 tablespoons of unsalted butter, melted
  • ¼ cup of granulated sugar

For the filling:

  • 1 (14-ounce) can of sweetened condensed milk
  • ½ cup of freshly squeezed lemon juice
  • 1 tablespoon of lemon zest
  • 3 large egg yolks

For the blueberry topping:

  • 2 cups of fresh blueberries
  • ¼ cup of granulated sugar
  • 1 tablespoon of cornstarch
  • 1 tablespoon of water

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C).

  2. In a medium-sized bowl, combine the graham cracker crumbs, melted butter, and granulated sugar. Mix well until the mixture resembles wet sand.

  3. Press the crust mixture into the bottom and up the sides of a 9-inch tart pan. Make sure to pack it tightly and evenly.

  4. Bake the crust in the preheated oven for 10 minutes, or until it turns golden brown. Remove from the oven and set aside to cool.

  5. In a separate bowl, whisk together the sweetened condensed milk, lemon juice, lemon zest, and egg yolks until well combined.

  6. Pour the lemon filling into the cooled crust and spread it evenly.

  7. Bake the tart for 15 minutes, or until the filling is set. Remove from the oven and let it cool completely.

  8. While the tart is cooling, prepare the blueberry topping. In a saucepan, combine the blueberries, granulated sugar, cornstarch, and water. Cook over medium heat, stirring occasionally, until the mixture thickens and the blueberries start to burst. This should take about 5 minutes.

  9. Remove the blueberry topping from the heat and let it cool slightly.

  10. Once both the tart and blueberry topping have cooled, spoon the blueberry mixture over the lemon filling, spreading it evenly.

  11. Place the tart in the refrigerator for at least 2 hours, or until it is fully chilled and set.

  12. When ready to serve, remove the tart from the refrigerator and carefully remove it from the tart pan.

  13. Slice the tart into wedges and garnish with fresh blueberries, lemon zest, or a dusting of powdered sugar, if desired.

Enjoy this tangy and sweet Lemon Blueberry Tart as the perfect dessert for your next brunch gathering. Its zesty lemon flavor combined with the burst of blueberries will surely delight your taste buds. Plus, the vibrant colors make it a stunning centerpiece for any table. So go ahead, indulge in this delectable treat and impress your guests with your culinary skills!

Note: This recipe yields approximately 8 servings.

The Tangy and Sweet Lemon Blueberry Tart is a fantastic addition to your brunch spread, offering a refreshing and satisfying end to the meal. So why wait?
