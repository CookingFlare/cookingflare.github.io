BBQ Seitan Sliders: A Plant-Based Crowd Pleaser


If you’re looking for a mouth-watering dish that’ll impress both vegetarians and meat-eaters alike, look no further than these delectable BBQ seitan sliders. Featuring tender and flavorful seitan, these sliders are the perfect addition to any BBQ gathering or potluck, satisfying even the most skeptical of carnivores. Let’s dive into the scrumptious world of seitan and discover how to make this crowd-pleasing plant-based delight.

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb seitan, cut into thin strips
  • ½ cup barbeque sauce (homemade or store-bought)
  • 2 tbsp vegan Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 tbsp liquid smoke
  • 2 tbsp minced garlic
  • 1 cup vegan mayonnaise
  • 1 cup shredded vegan cheese
  • 12 small slider buns
  • Your favorite BBQ seasonings

Instructions:

  1. Begin by preheating your oven to 350°F (175°C). This will ensure the seitan strips cook evenly and remain moist.
  2. In a large bowl, whisk together the barbeque sauce, Worcestershire sauce, liquid smoke, and minced garlic. You can use your favorite store-bought BBQ sauce or make your own for a more personalized flavor.
  3. Add the seitan strips to the bowl, ensuring they’re evenly coated in the savory sauce mixture. Toss in your favorite BBQ seasonings to further enhance the flavor.
  4. Place the coated seitan strips on a baking sheet, arranging them in a single layer. Pour any remaining sauce over the strips to ensure they’re equally flavored.
  5. Bake the seitan strips in the preheated oven for about 25 minutes, flipping halfway through to ensure even cooking.
  6. While the seitan is cooking, prepare your slider buns according to your preference. You can toast them, warm them in the oven, or serve them cold.
  7. Once the seitan strips are cooked to perfection, remove them from the oven and let them cool for a few minutes.
  8. Assemble your sliders by spreading a layer of vegan mayonnaise on each bun’s inside surface. Layer the cooked seitan strips over the mayonnaise on both the top and bottom buns.
  9. Sprinkle the vegan cheese over the seitan strips, allowing it to melt slightly under the warm buns.
  10. Complete your sliders with more toppings and condiments of your choice, such as pickles, onions, or hot sauce.
  11. Serve your BBQ seitan sliders immediately for maximum enjoyment and watch them vanish!

Tips for Success:

  • Ensure the seitan strips are evenly coated in the BBQ sauce mixture to achieve maximum flavor and tender texture.
  • Use a quality BBQ sauce and seasonings to create a blockbuster taste.
  • Gates BBQ sauce and Jack Daniel’s BBQ sauce are winning options for extra smoky, bold flavor.
  • Experiment with different types of seitan, such as smoked or honey-gyucanfu-style, to create a unique flavor profile.
  • Opt for fresh, artisanal buns for an elevated presentation and a softer texture.
  • Making extra BBQ seitan is a great idea for future snacks, sandwiches, and meal prep.

Barbeque gatherings call for an array of mouth-watering flavors, and these BBQ seitan sliders offer up something special and satisfying for all to enjoy. Impress your friends and family with this plant-based crowd-pleaser, and don’t be surprised when they ask for seconds! With these BBQ seitan sliders, you’ll elevate your barbeque game and turn skeptics into believers. Happy cooking!
