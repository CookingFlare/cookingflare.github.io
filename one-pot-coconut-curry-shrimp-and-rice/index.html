One Pot Coconut Curry Shrimp and Rice


If you’re in the mood for a flavorful and satisfying meal that comes together in no time, then this One Pot Coconut Curry Shrimp and Rice is the perfect dish for you. Packed with exotic flavors and vibrant colors, this dish is a feast for the senses. The combination of succulent shrimp, fragrant jasmine rice, and creamy coconut curry sauce creates a harmonious blend of textures and flavors that will leave you craving for more. Best of all, it’s a one-pot wonder, making cleanup a breeze. Let’s dive into the recipe and bring a taste of the tropics to your dinner table.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound (450g) large shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 1 1/2 cups (300g) jasmine rice
  • 1 can (14 oz/400ml) coconut milk
  • 1 1/2 cups (350ml) chicken or vegetable broth
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 red bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 tablespoon curry powder
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1 tablespoon fresh ginger, grated
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons brown sugar
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh cilantro for garnish
  • Lime wedges for serving

Instructions

  1. Sauté Aromatics: Heat some oil in a large pot over medium heat. Add the chopped onion and sauté until it turns translucent. Then, add the minced garlic, grated ginger, and sliced red bell pepper. Sauté for a few more minutes until the peppers start to soften.

  2. Add Spices: Sprinkle in the curry powder, turmeric, cumin, and paprika. Stir the spices into the aromatics until they become fragrant, releasing their flavors.

  3. Add Rice and Liquid: Add the jasmine rice to the pot and stir to coat it with the aromatic mixture. Pour in the coconut milk and chicken or vegetable broth. Season with soy sauce and brown sugar. Stir well to combine all the ingredients.

  4. Simmer: Bring the mixture to a gentle boil, then reduce the heat to low. Cover the pot and let it simmer for about 15 minutes, or until the rice is almost tender and most of the liquid has been absorbed.

  5. Add Shrimp: Gently stir in the shrimp, making sure they are nestled into the rice and sauce. Cover the pot and let the shrimp cook for another 5-7 minutes, or until they turn pink and opaque.

  6. Adjust Seasoning: Taste the curry and adjust the seasoning with salt and pepper if needed.

  7. Serve: Once the shrimp is cooked through and the rice is tender, remove the pot from the heat. Give the curry a gentle stir to incorporate the flavors. Serve the One Pot Coconut Curry Shrimp and Rice in bowls, garnished with fresh cilantro and lime wedges on the side.

The One Pot Coconut Curry Shrimp and Rice is a delightful dish that brings together the richness of coconut milk, the warmth of aromatic spices, and the succulence of shrimp. It’s a versatile recipe that can be easily customized to suit your taste preferences. You can add extra vegetables such as spinach, peas, or cherry tomatoes to amp up the nutritional value and add more color to the dish. Additionally, you can adjust the level of spiciness by adding more or less curry powder and paprika to suit your palate.

Not only is this dish a treat for your taste buds, but it also makes for a visually stunning presentation. The vibrant reds, yellows, and greens of the curry are a feast for the eyes. Whether you’re hosting a dinner party or simply treating yourself to a special meal, this One Pot Coconut Curry Shrimp and Rice is sure to impress.

So, the next time you’re craving a taste of the tropics, whip up this easy and delicious dish that will transport you to a culinary paradise. With its aromatic flavors and creamy texture, this One Pot Coconut Curry Shrimp and Rice is a true celebration of the vibrant and diverse world of cuisine.

Enjoy your culinary adventure and savor every spoonful of this mouthwatering dish!
