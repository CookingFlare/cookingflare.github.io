Vegan S'mores Brownies: A Campfire Classic


When it comes to classic campfire treats, nothing quite compares to the gooey, chocolatey goodness of s’mores. The combination of crunchy graham crackers, melty chocolate, and toasted marshmallows is a timeless favorite for outdoor gatherings. However, for those following a vegan diet, traditional s’mores are off-limits due to the presence of dairy and gelatin. But fear not, because I’ve got a delectable solution for you – Vegan S’mores Brownies!

The Perfect Vegan Twist

These Vegan S’mores Brownies are a delightful twist on the classic campfire treat. They capture the essence of s’mores with a vegan-friendly twist, making them perfect for anyone looking to indulge in a cruelty-free and delicious dessert. The rich, fudgy brownie base, combined with the sweetness of vegan marshmallows and the irresistible crunch of graham crackers, creates a symphony of flavors that will leave your taste buds dancing.

Ingredients

To create these delectable Vegan S’mores Brownies, you’ll need the following ingredients:

  • 1 ½ cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/2 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup vegetable oil
  • 1 cup non-dairy milk (such as almond or oat milk)
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 cup vegan mini marshmallows
  • 1/2 cup vegan chocolate chips
  • 1/2 cup crushed vegan graham crackers

Instructions

Step 1: Prepare the Brownie Batter

Start by preheating your oven to 350°F (175°C) and greasing a square baking pan. In a large mixing bowl, combine the flour, sugar, cocoa powder, baking powder, and salt. Mix well to ensure that all the dry ingredients are fully incorporated. Then, add the vegetable oil, non-dairy milk, and vanilla extract to the dry ingredients, and stir until the batter is smooth and free of lumps.

Step 2: Assembly

Once the brownie batter is ready, pour it into the greased baking pan, spreading it out evenly. Next, sprinkle the vegan mini marshmallows and chocolate chips over the top of the batter, gently pressing them into the surface. Finally, sprinkle the crushed vegan graham crackers over the marshmallows and chocolate chips, creating a delightful layer of crunch.

Step 3: Bake to Perfection

Place the pan in the preheated oven and bake the brownies for 25-30 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out with a few moist crumbs. Be careful not to overbake, as you want the brownies to remain fudgy and moist. Once done, remove the brownies from the oven and let them cool before slicing into squares.

The Ultimate Vegan Indulgence

These Vegan S’mores Brownies are the epitome of indulgence, offering a heavenly combination of sweet, chocolatey, and crunchy flavors in every bite. Whether you’re enjoying them at a campfire gathering, a cozy night in, or as a special treat for friends and family, these brownies are sure to be a hit.

So, the next time you’re craving the nostalgic charm of s’mores, whip up a batch of these Vegan S’mores Brownies and treat yourself to a cruelty-free delight that captures the essence of a campfire classic. Happy vegan baking!
