Delicious and Easy Instant Pot Dessert Recipes


If you’re a fan of desserts but don’t have much time to spend in the kitchen, then Instant Pot dessert recipes are about to become your new best friend. The Instant Pot, known for its versatility and time-saving capabilities, can also work wonders when it comes to creating mouthwatering desserts. From creamy cheesecakes to decadent puddings, the Instant Pot can handle it all. So, if you’re ready to satisfy your sweet tooth without breaking a sweat, here are some delicious and easy Instant Pot dessert recipes to try.

1. Instant Pot Cheesecake

Ingredients:

  • 1 ½ cups graham cracker crumbs
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, melted
  • 16 oz cream cheese, softened
  • 1/2 cup sour cream
  • 2/3 cup granulated sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, mix the graham cracker crumbs, sugar, and melted butter. Press the mixture into the bottom of a springform pan.
  2. In another bowl, beat the cream cheese, sour cream, and sugar until smooth. Add the eggs and vanilla extract, and mix until well combined.
  3. Pour the cream cheese mixture over the crust in the springform pan.
  4. Cover the pan with foil and place it on the trivet in the Instant Pot with 1 cup of water.
  5. Cook on high pressure for 35 minutes, then natural release for 10 minutes.
  6. Once done, remove the cheesecake from the Instant Pot and let it cool. Refrigerate for at least 4 hours before serving.

2. Instant Pot Chocolate Lava Cakes

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter
  • 4 oz semi-sweet chocolate
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 egg yolks
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 tsp salt

Instructions:

  1. Grease ramekins with butter and dust with cocoa powder.
  2. In a microwave-safe bowl, melt the butter and chocolate. Let it cool slightly.
  3. In another bowl, whisk the eggs, egg yolks, and sugar until light and fluffy. Stir in the vanilla extract.
  4. Gently fold the chocolate mixture into the egg mixture.
  5. Add the flour and salt, and fold until just combined.
  6. Divide the batter among the prepared ramekins.
  7. Place the trivet in the Instant Pot with 1 cup of water. Arrange the ramekins on the trivet.
  8. Cook on high pressure for 8 minutes, then quick release the pressure.
  9. Carefully remove the ramekins from the Instant Pot and let them cool for a few minutes before serving.

3. Instant Pot Rice Pudding

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup arborio rice
  • 3 cups whole milk
  • 1/3 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1/4 tsp ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • Raisins and chopped nuts for garnish (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Rinse the rice under cold water.
  2. In the Instant Pot, combine the rice, milk, sugar, vanilla extract, cinnamon, and salt.
  3. Close the lid and cook on high pressure for 20 minutes, then natural release for 10 minutes.
  4. Stir the rice pudding and let it cool slightly.
  5. Serve warm, topped with raisins and chopped nuts if desired.

These Instant Pot dessert recipes are not only delicious but also incredibly easy to make. Whether you’re entertaining guests or simply craving a sweet treat, the Instant Pot can help you whip up impressive desserts in no time. So, dust off your Instant Pot and get ready to indulge in some delightful homemade sweets!
