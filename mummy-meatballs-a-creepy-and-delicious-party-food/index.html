Mummy Meatballs: A Creepy and Delicious Party Food


Are you ready to take your Halloween party to the next level? Look no further than these Mummy Meatballs – a spooky and savory treat that will leave your guests both terrified and delighted. With just a few simple ingredients and a touch of creativity, you can create a dish that is as delicious as it is eerie. So, grab your apron and let’s get cooking!

Ingredients

For the meatballs:

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1/2 cup breadcrumbs
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/4 cup milk
  • 1/4 cup finely chopped onion
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

For the mummy wrap:

  • 1 sheet puff pastry, thawed
  • 1 egg, beaten (for egg wash)

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).

  2. In a large bowl, combine the ground beef, breadcrumbs, Parmesan cheese, milk, onion, garlic, oregano, basil, salt, and pepper. Mix well until all the ingredients are evenly incorporated.

  3. Shape the mixture into small meatballs, about 1 inch in diameter. Place the meatballs on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper, making sure to leave some space between each one.

  4. Bake the meatballs in the preheated oven for 15-20 minutes, or until cooked through and browned.

  5. While the meatballs are baking, roll out the puff pastry on a lightly floured surface. Cut the puff pastry into thin strips, about 1/4 inch wide.

  6. Once the meatballs are cooked, remove them from the oven and let them cool slightly. Wrap each meatball with a strip of puff pastry, leaving a small gap near the top to create the mummy’s “face.”

  7. Place the wrapped meatballs back on the baking sheet and brush them with the beaten egg wash. This will give them a golden brown color once baked.

  8. Return the meatballs to the oven and bake for an additional 10-15 minutes, or until the puff pastry is golden and crispy.

  9. Remove the mummy meatballs from the oven and let them cool for a few minutes before serving. You can serve them with a side of marinara sauce for dipping, or simply enjoy them on their own.

Spook-tacular Serving Suggestions

To make your Mummy Meatballs even more frightfully delicious, consider these spooky serving suggestions:

  • Arrange the mummy meatballs on a platter with a dollop of marinara sauce in the center, creating the effect of blood oozing from the mummies.
  • Place a pair of edible googly eyes on each mummy meatball, giving them a playful and creepy appearance.
  • Serve the mummy meatballs alongside other Halloween-themed treats, such as “witch finger” breadsticks or “zombie brain” dip.

Conclusion

Mummy Meatballs are the perfect addition to any Halloween party or spooky gathering. Not only are they creepy and fun, but they also taste absolutely delicious. The combination of seasoned ground beef and flaky puff pastry creates a tantalizing texture that will have your guests coming back for more. So, gather your ingredients, channel your inner mad scientist, and prepare to delight your guests with this ghoulishly good party food. Happy cooking, and Happy Halloween!

Note: The Mummy Meatballs can be made in advance and reheated before serving. Simply store them in an airtight container in the refrigerator and reheat in the oven at 350°F (175°C) for 10-15 minutes, or until heated through.
