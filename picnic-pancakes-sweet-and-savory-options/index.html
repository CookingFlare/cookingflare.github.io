Picnic Pancakes: Sweet and Savory Options


Are you planning a delightful picnic with your loved ones? If so, why not elevate your outdoor dining experience with delicious picnic pancakes? Picnic pancakes are a versatile and easy-to-make dish that can be enjoyed by both kids and adults alike. In this blog post, we’ll explore a variety of sweet and savory picnic pancake options that will surely satisfy your taste buds. So, pack your picnic basket, grab your favorite toppings, and let’s dive into the world of picnic pancakes!

Sweet Picnic Pancakes

  1. Classic Maple Syrup - Sometimes, simplicity is key. Drizzle your fluffy pancakes with golden maple syrup for a classic and timeless sweet treat. The combination of the soft pancakes and the rich, sweet syrup is a match made in heaven.

  2. Fresh Fruits - Take advantage of the abundance of fresh fruits during picnic season. Top your pancakes with a colorful medley of sliced strawberries, blueberries, raspberries, and bananas. The burst of freshness and natural sweetness will add a delightful twist to your pancakes.

  3. Decadent Chocolate - Indulge your sweet tooth with a sprinkle of chocolatey goodness. Melt some chocolate chips and generously drizzle them over your pancakes. For an extra touch of elegance, add a dollop of whipped cream and a sprinkle of cocoa powder.

  4. Nutella and Hazelnuts - If you’re a fan of the iconic chocolate-hazelnut spread, this topping is for you. Spread a generous layer of Nutella on your pancakes and sprinkle some chopped hazelnuts on top. The creamy Nutella combined with the crunchy hazelnuts will create a delightful texture and flavor.

Savory Picnic Pancakes

  1. Bacon and Cheese - Bring a savory twist to your picnic by adding crispy bacon and melted cheese to your pancakes. Cook some bacon until it’s nice and crispy, then crumble it over your pancakes. Add a handful of shredded cheese and let it melt to perfection. The combination of the savory bacon and the gooey cheese will create a mouthwatering experience.

  2. Spinach and Feta - For a lighter and healthier option, try incorporating spinach and feta into your pancakes. Sauté some fresh spinach until wilted, then mix it into your pancake batter along with crumbled feta cheese. Cook as usual and enjoy the subtle tanginess and earthiness of this savory delight.

  3. Smoked Salmon and Cream Cheese - Elevate your picnic experience with a touch of elegance. Top your pancakes with thinly sliced smoked salmon and a generous smear of cream cheese. Garnish with fresh dill or chives for an extra burst of flavor. This combination will make you feel like you’re having a luxurious brunch by the seaside.

  4. Avocado and Tomato - Embrace the freshness of summer by adding avocado and tomato to your pancakes. Slice some ripe avocados and juicy tomatoes, then layer them on top of your pancakes. Finish with a sprinkle of salt, pepper, and a drizzle of olive oil. This savory pancake option is both delicious and nutritious.

Conclusion

Picnic pancakes offer a delightful and versatile twist to your outdoor dining experience. Whether you prefer sweet or savory flavors, there’s a pancake topping combination that will satisfy your taste buds. So, the next time you plan a picnic, don’t forget to pack some pancake batter and your favorite toppings. Enjoy the warm sunshine, the company of your loved ones, and the deliciousness of picnic pancakes. Bon appétit!
