Savory Salmon and Quinoa Salad: A Healthy and Satisfying Option

Who says healthy food has to be boring? This savory salmon and quinoa salad is not only packed with nutrients, but it’s also incredibly delicious. With a perfect balance of flavors and textures, this salad is a satisfying option for lunch or dinner. Plus, it’s easy to make and can be customized to suit your taste preferences. Let’s dive into the recipe! Ingredients 1 cup cooked quinoa 1 pound salmon fillet 2 tablespoons olive oil 1 tablespoon lemon juice Salt and pepper, to taste 2 cups mixed greens 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved 1/2 cup cucumber, diced 1/4 cup red onion, thinly sliced 1/4 cup feta cheese, crumbled 2 tablespoons fresh dill, chopped Instructions Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C). Place the salmon fillet on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper. Drizzle with olive oil and lemon juice, then season with salt and pepper to taste. Bake the salmon for about 12-15 minutes, or until it flakes easily with a fork. Remove from the oven and let it cool slightly. While the salmon is baking, prepare the quinoa according to the package instructions. Once cooked, fluff it with a fork and set aside to cool. In a large salad bowl, combine the mixed greens, cherry tomatoes, cucumber, red onion, feta cheese, and fresh dill. Break the cooked salmon into bite-sized pieces and add it to the salad bowl. Add the cooked quinoa to the bowl and gently toss all the ingredients together. Serve the salad immediately, or refrigerate for later. Tips and Variations Feel free to add other vegetables like bell peppers or avocado for extra flavor and nutrition. If you prefer a warm salad, you can lightly sauté the mixed greens and cherry tomatoes before adding them to the bowl. To make a creamy dressing, whisk together Greek yogurt, lemon juice, olive oil, and a pinch of salt and pepper. Drizzle it over the salad just before serving. This salad can be enjoyed as a main course or as a side dish. Pair it with some crusty bread for a complete meal. Why Choose Salmon and Quinoa? Salmon is a fantastic source of omega-3 fatty acids, which are essential for heart health and brain function. It’s also rich in high-quality protein, making it a great choice for those looking to increase their protein intake. Quinoa, on the other hand, is a gluten-free grain that is packed with fiber, vitamins, and minerals. It’s a complete protein source, meaning it contains all nine essential amino acids. Together, salmon and quinoa create a powerhouse of nutrients that will leave you feeling satisfied and nourished. ...
