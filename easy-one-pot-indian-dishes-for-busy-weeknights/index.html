Easy One-Pot Indian Dishes for Busy Weeknights


We all crave flavorful and comforting meals, especially on those hectic weeknights when time seems to fly. Looking for recipes that can be prepared in no more than 30 minutes, with minimal cleanup and no fuss is quite natural. One-pot dishes, featuring popular Indian spices and aromatics like cumin seeds, coriander powder, and turmeric, have become a staple in many busy households. Within this blog, you'll find a variety of tasty, one-pot recipes from multiple Indian regions, focusing on convenience, taste, and nutritional value to make your meal planning a breeze. Reshmi Handi - A Popular Hyderabadi Dish Quick and delicious, Reshmi Handi offers the ultimate taste experience with minimal effort. This classic, slow-cooked dish, initially from the kitchens of the ancient Nizams of Hyderabad, is now a staple in Indian cuisine. Meaning 'golden-pot', Reshmi Handi has evolved into an adaptable recipe that combines youthful tact, a bit of spice, and adds comfort to the occasion. Preparation Time: 15 minutes Cooking Time: 12 minutes Ingredients:

• Ground meat or lamb, boneless • Green chilies

Finely chopped onion

Tomatoes sauce (20ml/Approx.)

• Refined Turmeric powder (or manually milled) (7g/Approx.) • Salt

H Punjabi ghee (23g) Also available in normal / vegetable / vegetarian option.
