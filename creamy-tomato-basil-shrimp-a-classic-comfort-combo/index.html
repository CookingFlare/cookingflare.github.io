Creamy Tomato Basil Shrimp: A Classic Comfort Combo

3 min · 535 words

If you’re a fan of classic comfort food and seafood, then you’re in for a treat with this delightful recipe for Creamy Tomato Basil Shrimp. This dish combines the richness of creamy tomato sauce with the succulent flavors of shrimp, creating a mouthwatering combination that will leave you craving for more. Whether you’re planning a cozy dinner at home or looking to impress your guests with a restaurant-worthy meal, this recipe is sure to be a hit.

Ingredients

To make this Creamy Tomato Basil Shrimp, you’ll need the following ingredients:

  • 1 pound of medium-sized shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 2 tablespoons of olive oil
  • 4 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 1 can (14 ounces) of diced tomatoes
  • 1 cup of heavy cream
  • 1/4 cup of fresh basil leaves, chopped
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Heat the olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Add the minced garlic and chopped onion, and sauté until they become fragrant and slightly golden.

  2. Add the peeled and deveined shrimp to the skillet and cook them until they turn pink and opaque. This usually takes about 2-3 minutes per side. Be careful not to overcook the shrimp, as they can become rubbery.

  3. Once the shrimp are cooked, remove them from the skillet and set them aside.

  4. In the same skillet, add the diced tomatoes (with their juice) and stir them into the garlic and onion mixture. Allow the tomatoes to simmer for about 5 minutes, until they start to break down and release their juices.

  5. Reduce the heat to low and pour in the heavy cream. Stir well to combine the cream with the tomato mixture, creating a smooth and creamy sauce. Simmer for another 5 minutes to allow the flavors to meld together.

  6. Return the cooked shrimp to the skillet and stir them into the creamy tomato sauce. Make sure each shrimp is coated with the sauce. Allow the shrimp to heat through for another 2 minutes.

  7. Finally, add the chopped basil leaves to the skillet and stir them into the sauce. The fresh basil will infuse the dish with a vibrant and aromatic flavor.

  8. Season the Creamy Tomato Basil Shrimp with salt and pepper according to your taste preferences. Remember to taste the sauce before adding any additional seasoning, as the canned tomatoes may already contain some salt.

  9. Serve the Creamy Tomato Basil Shrimp over a bed of cooked pasta, such as linguine or fettuccine, or with a side of crusty bread to soak up the delicious sauce. Garnish with additional fresh basil leaves for an extra touch of elegance.

Conclusion

Creamy Tomato Basil Shrimp is a classic comfort combo that brings together the rich flavors of tomatoes, cream, and shrimp in a delightful and satisfying dish. The creamy tomato sauce pairs perfectly with the tender shrimp, while the fresh basil adds a pop of freshness and aroma. Whether you’re cooking for yourself or hosting a dinner party, this recipe is sure to impress. So go ahead, give it a try, and indulge in this comforting and delicious meal.
