Instant Pot Creamy Chicken Mushroom Soup: The Ultimate Comfort Food


When the weather turns chilly, there’s nothing quite like a hearty bowl of creamy soup to warm you up from the inside out. And what better way to achieve that than with a bowl of Instant Pot Creamy Chicken Mushroom Soup? This recipe combines tender chicken, earthy mushrooms, and a velvety smooth broth to create a comforting dish that is as satisfying as it is delicious. Whether you’re looking for a quick weeknight meal or a cozy weekend indulgence, this recipe has you covered.

Why Choose the Instant Pot for This Recipe?

The Instant Pot has revolutionized home cooking, offering a quicker way to make meals that traditionally take hours. With the Instant Pot, you can prepare complex dishes like chicken mushroom soup in a fraction of the time. The pressure cooking function locks in flavor and moisture, giving you tender chicken and perfectly cooked vegetables in minutes. Not only does this save time, but it also intensifies the flavors, making your soup taste like it’s been simmering for hours.

Ingredients for the Instant Pot Creamy Chicken Mushroom Soup

To make this creamy chicken mushroom soup, you’ll need the following ingredients:

  • Chicken breasts or thighs (boneless, skinless) – You can use chicken breasts for a leaner soup, or chicken thighs for a richer flavor.
  • Mushrooms – Cremini, white button, or a mix of mushrooms works well for this recipe. Their earthy flavors enhance the richness of the soup.
  • Yellow onion – For a subtle sweetness and depth of flavor.
  • Garlic – Fresh garlic cloves provide aromatic flavor that complements the other ingredients.
  • Carrots – Adding a touch of sweetness and color to balance the savory components.
  • Celery – For freshness and crunch.
  • Chicken broth – This serves as the base for the soup, and you can use low-sodium or homemade chicken stock for a more flavorful option.
  • Heavy cream – This gives the soup its signature creamy texture. You can substitute with half-and-half for a lighter version or coconut milk for a dairy-free option.
  • Butter – A little bit of butter adds richness to the soup.
  • Thyme and bay leaves – These herbs bring an aromatic, comforting essence to the soup.
  • Salt and pepper – To taste, for enhancing all the natural flavors in the soup.

How to Make Instant Pot Creamy Chicken Mushroom Soup

Step 1: Sauté the Vegetables and Chicken

The first step in making this creamy chicken mushroom soup is to sauté your aromatics and chicken in the Instant Pot. This will help develop a rich base flavor before you add the liquid.

  1. Set your Instant Pot to the “Sauté” mode and allow it to heat up. Once hot, melt 1 tablespoon of butter in the pot.
  2. Add the onion, carrots, and celery to the pot and sauté for about 3-4 minutes, or until the vegetables begin to soften.
  3. Add the garlic and sauté for another 1-2 minutes until fragrant.
  4. Season the vegetables with a pinch of salt and pepper.
  5. Push the vegetables to the side of the pot, then add your chicken (whole breasts or thighs) to the pot and sear each side for about 2-3 minutes. The chicken should be slightly browned but not fully cooked at this stage.

Step 2: Add the Mushrooms and Broth

Once the chicken has been seared, it’s time to add the mushrooms and broth.

  1. Stir in the mushrooms, allowing them to cook for 2-3 minutes to release their moisture and begin to soften.
  2. Pour in the chicken broth, making sure the chicken is mostly submerged. Scrape any browned bits from the bottom of the pot with a wooden spoon to incorporate those flavors into the soup.

Step 3: Pressure Cook the Soup

Now comes the magic of the Instant Pot! The pressure cooking function will help cook everything quickly and bring out the flavors.

  1. Add the thyme and bay leaves to the pot for that aromatic, herby depth.
  2. Close the lid of the Instant Pot and ensure the valve is set to “Sealing.” Set the Instant Pot to Manual (Pressure Cook) on high for 10 minutes.
  3. Once the cooking time is up, allow the Instant Pot to release pressure naturally for 5-10 minutes before carefully turning the valve to “Venting” to release the remaining pressure.

Step 4: Shred the Chicken and Add the Cream

Once the pressure has been fully released, it’s time to finish off the soup.

  1. Open the lid and remove the chicken breasts or thighs from the pot. Use two forks to shred the chicken into bite-sized pieces. Return the shredded chicken to the pot.
  2. Stir in the heavy cream to give the soup its creamy consistency. You can adjust the amount of cream depending on how rich you want the soup to be.
  3. Taste and adjust seasoning with additional salt and pepper as needed. If you like a more intense flavor, you can add a little more thyme or some extra garlic.

Step 5: Serve and Enjoy!

Once the soup is well-combined and creamy, it’s ready to serve. Ladle the soup into bowls, garnish with some fresh parsley or thyme if desired, and enjoy!

Tips for the Best Instant Pot Creamy Chicken Mushroom Soup

  • Use bone-in chicken for more flavor: If you have the time and don’t mind a bit of extra effort, you can use bone-in chicken thighs or breasts for an even richer flavor. Just be sure to remove the bones after the pressure cooking step.
  • Make it lighter: If you’re looking to cut back on the richness, use half-and-half or coconut milk instead of heavy cream. You can also use a lighter broth or add a splash of white wine for extra depth.
  • Customize the vegetables: While mushrooms, carrots, and celery are traditional in this recipe, feel free to mix in other vegetables like peas, corn, or spinach for added texture and flavor.
  • Leftovers: This soup stores well in the fridge for up to 3 days and can be frozen for up to 3 months. Just make sure to let the soup cool completely before transferring it to an airtight container for storage.

Why This Soup is the Ultimate Comfort Food

This Instant Pot Creamy Chicken Mushroom Soup is truly the perfect comfort food for several reasons:

  • Rich and creamy: The combination of heavy cream, chicken, and mushrooms creates a velvety smooth soup that feels indulgent without being overwhelming.
  • Easy to make: The Instant Pot takes the pressure off, literally! The sauté and pressure cook functions allow you to make this soup quickly without losing any of the flavor.
  • Perfect for meal prep: The soup is filling and can easily be made in large batches. It’s a great option for lunch throughout the week or even as a starter for dinner.
  • Comforting flavors: The earthy mushrooms, tender chicken, and creamy broth create the kind of warmth and coziness that you crave when you need a pick-me-up.
  • Customizable: You can adjust the creaminess, the seasoning, and even the vegetables to your personal taste. It’s a flexible recipe that can be made just the way you like it.

Final Thoughts

The Instant Pot Creamy Chicken Mushroom Soup is a perfect meal to enjoy on a chilly day or any time you want a comforting, filling bowl of goodness. It combines the rich flavors of tender chicken, mushrooms, and a creamy broth, all prepared quickly in the Instant Pot. Whether you’re cooking for yourself or for a family, this soup will soon become a staple in your recipe rotation.

The ease of preparation, coupled with the depth of flavor, makes it a win for busy weeknights and lazy weekends alike. So the next time you’re craving something warm, creamy, and satisfying, reach for this recipe. You won’t regret it!
