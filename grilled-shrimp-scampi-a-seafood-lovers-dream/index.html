Grilled Shrimp Scampi: A Seafood Lover's Dream


If you’re a seafood lover like me, then you’re in for a treat with this delectable Grilled Shrimp Scampi recipe. This dish is a perfect blend of succulent shrimp, zesty garlic, tangy lemon, and aromatic herbs, all brought together on the grill for a delightful smoky flavor. Whether you’re hosting a summer barbecue or simply craving a taste of the ocean, this recipe is sure to impress your taste buds and leave you craving for more.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound large shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/4 cup fresh parsley, chopped
  • 1 teaspoon red pepper flakes
  • 1/4 cup lemon juice
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 tablespoons unsalted butter
  • 1 tablespoon fresh basil, chopped
  • Wooden skewers, soaked in water

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Marinade: In a bowl, combine the olive oil, minced garlic, chopped parsley, red pepper flakes, lemon juice, salt, and pepper. Mix well to create a flavorful marinade for the shrimp.

  2. Marinate the Shrimp: Add the peeled and deveined shrimp to the marinade, ensuring that each piece is coated evenly. Cover the bowl and refrigerate for at least 30 minutes to allow the flavors to infuse into the shrimp.

  3. Preheat the Grill: While the shrimp is marinating, preheat your grill to medium-high heat. If you’re using wooden skewers, remember to soak them in water for at least 30 minutes to prevent them from burning on the grill.

  4. Skewer the Shrimp: Thread the marinated shrimp onto the soaked wooden skewers, ensuring they are secure but not overcrowded. This will make it easier to grill the shrimp and prevent them from falling through the grates.

  5. Grill the Shrimp: Once the grill is hot, place the skewered shrimp directly on the grill grates. Grill for 2-3 minutes on each side, or until the shrimp turns pink and slightly charred. Be careful not to overcook the shrimp, as they can become rubbery.

  6. Prepare the Scampi Sauce: In a small saucepan, melt the butter and add the remaining minced garlic. Cook for a minute until the garlic becomes fragrant. Remove from heat and stir in the fresh basil and grated Parmesan cheese.

  7. Serve and Enjoy: Once the shrimp are grilled to perfection, remove them from the skewers and place them on a platter. Drizzle the scampi sauce over the grilled shrimp, allowing the flavors to meld together. Garnish with fresh parsley and a squeeze of lemon juice for an extra burst of freshness.

Why Grilled Shrimp Scampi?

Grilled Shrimp Scampi is a seafood lover’s dream for several reasons. Firstly, grilling the shrimp adds a delightful smoky flavor that enhances the natural sweetness of the seafood. The combination of garlic, lemon, and herbs in the marinade and scampi sauce creates a harmonious blend of flavors that perfectly complements the shrimp. Furthermore, this recipe is versatile and can be served as an appetizer, a main course, or even added to a fresh salad for a light and satisfying meal.

In Conclusion

Grilled Shrimp Scampi is a dish that celebrates the essence of seafood in a simple yet elegant manner. With just a few ingredients and a hot grill, you can elevate your dining experience and transport yourself to a seaside paradise. Whether you’re a seasoned chef or a novice in the kitchen, this recipe is a must-try for anyone who appreciates the wonders of fresh seafood. So fire up the grill, gather your ingredients, and get ready to savor every succulent bite of this seafood delight.

So there you have it, a tantalizing recipe for Grilled Shrimp Scampi that will have you longing for more. Get ready to impress your guests or simply treat yourself to a gourmet seafood experience right in the comfort of your own backyard. Happy grilling!
