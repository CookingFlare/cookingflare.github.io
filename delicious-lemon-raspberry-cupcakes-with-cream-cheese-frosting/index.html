Delicious Lemon Raspberry Cupcakes with Cream Cheese Frosting


If you’re a fan of tangy lemon and sweet raspberries, then you’re in for a treat with these delicious Lemon Raspberry Cupcakes with Cream Cheese Frosting. Bursting with fresh flavors and topped with a creamy frosting, these cupcakes are sure to impress your friends and family.

Ingredients

For the cupcakes:

  • 1 ½ cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 ½ teaspoons baking powder
  • ¼ teaspoon salt
  • ½ cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • ½ cup whole milk
  • Zest of 1 lemon
  • 1 cup fresh raspberries

For the cream cheese frosting:

  • 8 ounces cream cheese, softened
  • ½ cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 4 cups powdered sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C) and line a muffin tin with cupcake liners.

  2. In a medium-sized bowl, whisk together the flour, baking powder, and salt. Set aside.

  3. In a large mixing bowl, cream together the softened butter and granulated sugar until light and fluffy. This should take about 2-3 minutes.

  4. Beat in the eggs, one at a time, ensuring each egg is fully incorporated before adding the next one. Then, mix in the vanilla extract.

  5. Gradually add the dry ingredients to the wet ingredients, alternating with the milk. Begin and end with the dry ingredients, mixing until just combined.

  6. Gently fold in the lemon zest and fresh raspberries, being careful not to overmix.

  7. Spoon the batter into the prepared cupcake liners, filling each one about two-thirds full.

  8. Bake for 18-20 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center of a cupcake comes out clean.

  9. While the cupcakes are cooling, prepare the cream cheese frosting. In a large mixing bowl, beat together the softened cream cheese and butter until smooth and creamy.

  10. Gradually add the powdered sugar, one cup at a time, mixing well after each addition.

  11. Stir in the vanilla extract until fully incorporated.

  12. Once the cupcakes have completely cooled, pipe or spread the cream cheese frosting onto each cupcake.

  13. For an extra touch, you can garnish each cupcake with a fresh raspberry or a sprinkle of lemon zest.

Serving Suggestions

These Lemon Raspberry Cupcakes with Cream Cheese Frosting are a delightful treat for any occasion. Serve them at birthday parties, bridal showers, or simply as a special dessert to enjoy with your loved ones. The tangy lemon and juicy raspberries pair perfectly with the creamy and slightly tangy cream cheese frosting.

Storage

If you have any leftovers (although it’s hard to resist eating them all at once), store the cupcakes in an airtight container in the refrigerator. They will stay fresh for up to 3 days.

Final Thoughts

These Lemon Raspberry Cupcakes with Cream Cheese Frosting are a true delight. The combination of zesty lemon, sweet raspberries, and creamy frosting creates a flavor explosion in every bite. Whether you’re a seasoned baker or just starting out, these cupcakes are sure to impress. So, gather your ingredients and get ready to indulge in a little slice of cupcake heaven. Enjoy!
