Classic Chocolate Peanut Butter Cup Cookies: A Match Made in Heaven


If there’s one flavor combination that never fails to delight the taste buds, it’s the timeless pairing of chocolate and peanut butter. The rich, indulgent taste of chocolate combined with the creamy, nutty goodness of peanut butter creates a symphony of flavors that is simply irresistible. And what better way to celebrate this heavenly duo than with a batch of classic chocolate peanut butter cup cookies? These delectable treats are a surefire way to satisfy your sweet tooth and leave you craving for more.

Ingredients

For the Cookie Dough:

  • 1 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 cup packed brown sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2/3 cup cocoa powder
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt

For the Filling:

  • 1 cup creamy peanut butter
  • 1/2 cup powdered sugar

For the Topping:

  • 24 mini peanut butter cups, unwrapped

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C) and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.

  2. Prepare the Cookie Dough: In a large mixing bowl, cream together the softened butter, granulated sugar, and brown sugar until light and fluffy. Add the eggs, one at a time, beating well after each addition. Stir in the vanilla extract.

  3. In a separate bowl, whisk together the flour, cocoa powder, baking soda, and salt. Gradually add the dry ingredients to the creamed mixture and mix well to form a smooth dough.

  4. Make the Filling: In a small bowl, combine the creamy peanut butter and powdered sugar until well blended.

  5. Assemble the Cookies: Take a tablespoon of cookie dough and flatten it in the palm of your hand. Place a teaspoon of the peanut butter filling in the center and wrap the dough around it, shaping it into a ball. Place the filled dough balls on the prepared baking sheet, spacing them about 2 inches apart.

  6. Bake in the preheated oven for 8-10 minutes, or until the edges are set. Remove from the oven and immediately press a mini peanut butter cup into the center of each cookie. Allow the cookies to cool on the baking sheet for 5 minutes before transferring them to a wire rack to cool completely.

  7. Enjoy: Once cooled, these classic chocolate peanut butter cup cookies are ready to be enjoyed. The combination of soft, fudgy chocolate cookie with a surprise burst of creamy peanut butter and a mini peanut butter cup topping is simply divine.

Tips and Variations

  • For an extra indulgent twist, try using dark chocolate peanut butter cups for a more intense flavor.
  • If you’re a fan of chunky peanut butter, feel free to use it in the filling for added texture.
  • These cookies also make a delightful accompaniment to a scoop of vanilla ice cream, creating a mouthwatering dessert option.

There you have it, a simple yet irresistible recipe for classic chocolate peanut butter cup cookies. Whether you’re baking for a special occasion or simply indulging in a sweet treat, these cookies are guaranteed to be a hit. So go ahead, gather your ingredients, and let the aroma of freshly baked chocolate and peanut butter goodness fill your kitchen. Happy baking!
