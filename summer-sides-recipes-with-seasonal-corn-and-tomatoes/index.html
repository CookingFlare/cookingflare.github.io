Summer Sides: Recipes with Seasonal Corn and Tomatoes


Summer is the perfect time to enjoy the vibrant flavors and colors of fresh produce. And when it comes to summer sides, corn and tomatoes take center stage. These two ingredients are not only delicious but also versatile, making them ideal for creating a variety of mouthwatering dishes. In this blog post, we’ll explore some exciting recipes that showcase the best of corn and tomatoes. Get ready to elevate your summer meals with these delightful summer sides!

1. Grilled Corn and Tomato Salad

Ingredients:

  • 4 ears of corn, husked
  • 2 cups cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1 small red onion, thinly sliced
  • 1/4 cup fresh basil leaves, torn
  • 2 tablespoons extra-virgin olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons balsamic vinegar
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your grill to medium-high heat.
  2. Grill the corn, turning occasionally, until lightly charred, about 10 minutes. Set aside to cool.
  3. Once cooled, cut the corn kernels off the cob and transfer them to a large mixing bowl.
  4. Add the cherry tomatoes, red onion, and basil to the bowl.
  5. In a small bowl, whisk together the olive oil, balsamic vinegar, salt, and pepper. Pour the dressing over the salad and toss gently to combine.
  6. Let the salad sit for at least 10 minutes to allow the flavors to meld together.
  7. Serve chilled and enjoy the burst of flavors in every bite!

2. Creamy Tomato and Corn Soup

Ingredients:

  • 4 ears of corn, husked
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 1 small onion, diced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 4 cups vegetable broth
  • 4 cups diced tomatoes, fresh or canned
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh basil leaves for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Cut the corn kernels off the cob and set aside. Reserve the cobs.
  2. In a large pot, melt the butter over medium heat. Add the onion and garlic, and cook until softened, about 5 minutes.
  3. Add the corn kernels to the pot and cook for another 5 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  4. Add the vegetable broth and the corn cobs to the pot. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat and simmer for 15 minutes.
  5. Remove the corn cobs from the pot and discard.
  6. Using an immersion blender or a regular blender, puree the soup until smooth. If using a regular blender, be sure to blend in batches and vent the lid to prevent steam from building up.
  7. Return the soup to the pot and add the diced tomatoes. Simmer for another 10 minutes.
  8. Stir in the heavy cream and season with salt and pepper to taste.
  9. Ladle the soup into bowls, garnish with fresh basil leaves, and serve with crusty bread for a comforting summer meal.

3. Cheesy Tomato and Corn Casserole

Ingredients:

  • 4 cups corn kernels
  • 2 cups cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1/2 cup mayonnaise
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C). Grease a baking dish with butter or cooking spray.
  2. In a large mixing bowl, combine the corn kernels, cherry tomatoes, cheddar cheese, mayonnaise, Parmesan cheese, parsley, salt, and pepper. Mix well to combine.
  3. Transfer the mixture to the prepared baking dish and spread it out evenly.
  4. Bake in the preheated oven for 25-30 minutes, or until the top is golden brown and bubbly.
  5. Remove from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes before serving.
  6. This cheesy tomato and corn casserole makes for a delightful side dish that pairs perfectly with grilled meats or can even be enjoyed as a main course for vegetarians.

Conclusion

These summer sides are a celebration of the seasonal bounty of corn and tomatoes. From the light and refreshing grilled corn and tomato salad to the comforting creaminess of the tomato and corn soup, and the cheesy goodness of the tomato and corn casserole, these recipes are sure to impress your family and friends at your next summer gathering. So, make the most of the summer season and indulge in the flavors of corn and tomatoes with these delightful dishes. Enjoy the vibrant colors, the sweet juiciness, and the incredible taste that summer produce brings to the table!
