Homemade Instant Pot Applesauce Recipe for a Sweet Treat


Who doesn’t love the comforting aroma of freshly cooked applesauce wafting through the house? Making applesauce from scratch is not only a delicious treat, but it’s also a great way to use up those extra apples sitting in your fruit bowl. And what better way to make it than with the help of your trusty Instant Pot? In this recipe, we’ll show you how to whip up a batch of homemade Instant Pot applesauce that will leave your taste buds begging for more.

Ingredients

  • 8-10 apples (any variety you prefer)
  • 1 cup water
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar (optional, adjust according to your taste)
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon

Instructions

  1. Start by peeling, coring, and roughly chopping the apples. You don’t need to worry about making them perfectly uniform since they’ll be cooked down later.

  2. Place the chopped apples into the Instant Pot insert, followed by the water, sugar (if using), lemon juice, and ground cinnamon.

  3. Give everything a good stir to ensure the ingredients are well combined.

  4. Secure the lid on your Instant Pot and set it to manual high pressure for 5 minutes. Make sure the valve is set to the sealing position.

  5. Once the cooking time is up, allow for a natural release of pressure for about 10 minutes. Afterward, you can manually release any remaining pressure.

  6. Carefully remove the lid, and you’ll be greeted with the fragrant aroma of perfectly cooked apples.

  7. Using a potato masher or immersion blender, mash the cooked apples until you reach your desired consistency. If you prefer a chunkier texture, lightly mash the apples. For a smoother sauce, blend until it’s silky and smooth.

  8. Taste the applesauce and adjust the sweetness by adding more sugar if desired. Remember, the sweetness of the apples may vary, so it’s best to adjust according to your taste.

  9. Allow the applesauce to cool before transferring it to airtight containers. You can store it in the refrigerator for up to a week or freeze it for longer shelf life.

  10. Serve the homemade Instant Pot applesauce chilled or warm, depending on your preference. It’s perfect as a snack, a topping for pancakes or waffles, or as a healthy alternative to sugary desserts.

Tips and Variations

  • Experiment with different apple varieties to find your favorite flavor combination. Granny Smith apples add a tartness, while Honeycrisp or Fuji apples bring a natural sweetness to the sauce.

  • For a spiced twist, add a pinch of nutmeg or cloves along with the cinnamon.

  • If you prefer your applesauce to be completely smooth, strain the cooked apples through a fine-mesh sieve to remove any remaining bits of peel or texture.

  • Get creative with your serving suggestions! Top your applesauce with a dollop of Greek yogurt, sprinkle some granola for crunch, or drizzle with a touch of honey for added sweetness.

Making homemade Instant Pot applesauce is a breeze, and the end result is a flavorful, wholesome treat that will delight your taste buds. So grab some fresh apples, dust off your Instant Pot, and get ready to indulge in the sweet goodness of this classic favorite. Enjoy!
