Decadent Chocolate Peppermint Cupcakes: A Holiday Indulgence


If you’re looking for a show-stopping dessert to impress your guests during the holiday season, look no further than these Decadent Chocolate Peppermint Cupcakes. With rich chocolate flavors and a refreshing hint of peppermint, these cupcakes are the perfect indulgence for any festive occasion. So, grab your apron and get ready to embark on a baking adventure that will leave your taste buds dancing with joy.

Ingredients

For the cupcakes:

  • 1 and 3/4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 and 1/2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1/2 cup boiling water
  • 1/2 cup whole milk
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

For the peppermint buttercream frosting:

  • 1 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 4 cups powdered sugar
  • 1/4 cup whole milk
  • 1 teaspoon peppermint extract
  • Red food coloring (optional)
  • Crushed candy canes, for garnish

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C). Line a muffin tin with cupcake liners and set aside.

  2. In a medium-sized bowl, whisk together the flour, baking powder, baking soda, salt, and cocoa powder. Set aside.

  3. In a separate small bowl, dissolve the cocoa powder in boiling water until smooth. Add the milk to the cocoa mixture and stir well. Set aside.

  4. In a large mixing bowl, cream together the softened butter and granulated sugar until light and fluffy. Add the eggs, one at a time, beating well after each addition. Stir in the vanilla extract.

  5. Gradually add the dry ingredients to the butter mixture, alternating with the cocoa-milk mixture. Begin and end with the dry ingredients, mixing until just combined.

  6. Divide the batter evenly among the prepared cupcake liners, filling each about two-thirds full. Bake for 18-20 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center of a cupcake comes out clean.

  7. While the cupcakes are cooling, prepare the peppermint buttercream frosting. In a mixing bowl, beat the softened butter until creamy. Gradually add the powdered sugar, milk, and peppermint extract, beating until smooth and fluffy. Add a few drops of red food coloring if desired, for a festive touch.

  8. Once the cupcakes have cooled completely, frost them generously with the peppermint buttercream frosting. Sprinkle crushed candy canes over the top for a delightful crunch and a pop of peppermint flavor.

  9. Serve these Decadent Chocolate Peppermint Cupcakes at your holiday gathering and watch as your guests’ eyes light up with delight. These cupcakes are not only visually stunning but also incredibly delicious, with the perfect balance of chocolate and peppermint flavors.

Enjoy this holiday indulgence with a cup of hot cocoa or a glass of cold milk. These cupcakes are sure to become a new favorite in your festive dessert repertoire. So, go ahead and treat yourself and your loved ones to a taste of holiday magic with these Decadent Chocolate Peppermint Cupcakes. Happy baking!

Please note that the baking time may vary depending on your oven, so keep a close eye on the cupcakes as they bake. Make sure to store any leftover cupcakes in an airtight container to maintain their freshness.
