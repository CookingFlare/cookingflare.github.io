Sous Vide Lemon Herb Chicken: A Fresh and Zesty Recipe


Welcome to our latest culinary adventure! Today, we’re diving into the world of sous vide cooking to create a mouthwatering dish that’s bursting with fresh flavors - Sous Vide Lemon Herb Chicken. This cooking method ensures that the chicken is perfectly cooked, juicy, and infused with the bright, zesty essence of lemon and fragrant herbs. Let’s jump right into this delightful recipe and explore the art of sous vide cooking!

Ingredients:

  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 2 lemons, zested and juiced
  • 4 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons of fresh thyme, finely chopped
  • 2 tablespoons of fresh rosemary, finely chopped
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Olive oil

Instructions:

Step 1: Preparing the Chicken

  • Start by seasoning the chicken breasts with salt and pepper.
  • Place the seasoned chicken breasts in a large resealable bag or vacuum-seal them, ensuring they are in a single layer.

Step 2: Creating the Marinade

  • In a bowl, combine the lemon zest, lemon juice, minced garlic, chopped thyme, chopped rosemary, and a drizzle of olive oil.
  • Pour the marinade over the chicken, ensuring that each breast is thoroughly coated.

Step 3: Sous Vide Cooking

  • Preheat your sous vide water bath to 145°F (63°C).
  • Once the water reaches the desired temperature, carefully submerge the bag of chicken breasts into the water bath, ensuring they are fully submerged.
  • Cook the chicken for 1.5 to 2 hours, allowing the flavors to infuse and the chicken to reach the perfect level of tenderness.

Step 4: Finishing Touches

  • After the cooking time is up, carefully remove the bag of chicken from the water bath.
  • Heat a skillet over medium-high heat and add a splash of olive oil.
  • Sear the chicken breasts for 1-2 minutes on each side, creating a golden-brown crust while locking in the flavors.

Step 5: Serving

  • Once the chicken is beautifully seared, transfer it to a cutting board and let it rest for a few minutes.
  • Slice the chicken and serve it with a drizzle of the cooking juices, a sprinkle of fresh herbs, and additional lemon wedges for a burst of citrusy freshness.

Why Sous Vide Lemon Herb Chicken?

Sous vide cooking is a game-changer when it comes to achieving perfectly tender and juicy chicken. The gentle cooking process ensures that the chicken retains its moisture while absorbing the vibrant flavors of the lemon and herbs. The result is a dish that’s not only visually appealing but also incredibly delicious.

The zesty combination of lemon and fragrant herbs adds a refreshing twist to the classic chicken dish, making it an ideal choice for a light and flavorful meal. Whether you’re hosting a dinner party or simply craving a wholesome dinner, this recipe is sure to impress your taste buds and those of your guests.

By embracing the sous vide method, you can elevate your culinary skills and create a dish that’s as impressive as it is delightful.
