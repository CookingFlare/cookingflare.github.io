Rise and Shine: 8 Breakfast Recipes for Transforming Leftover Dinner Rolls


Hello food lovers! Are you tired of wasting those leftover dinner rolls from last night’s feast? Well, fret no more! In this blog post, we will show you how to transform those stale dinner rolls into delicious and satisfying breakfast treats. From savory to sweet, these recipes will have you waking up with a smile and eager to dig in. So, let’s rise and shine together with these 8 breakfast recipes!

1. Breakfast Egg Cups

Ingredients:

  • Leftover dinner rolls
  • Eggs
  • Shredded cheese
  • Your choice of toppings (e.g., diced bell peppers, cooked bacon, chopped spinach)

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. Slice the dinner rolls in half horizontally and gently scoop out the soft center to create a well.
  3. Place the hollowed-out rolls on a baking sheet.
  4. Crack an egg into each roll, making sure not to overflow.
  5. Sprinkle shredded cheese and your preferred toppings onto each egg.
  6. Bake for about 15-20 minutes, or until the eggs are cooked to your desired doneness.
  7. Serve hot and enjoy!

2. French Toast Roll-Ups

Ingredients:

  • Leftover dinner rolls
  • Eggs
  • Milk
  • Cinnamon
  • Vanilla extract
  • Your choice of fillings (e.g., Nutella, cream cheese, jam)

Instructions:

  1. In a shallow dish, whisk together eggs, milk, cinnamon, and vanilla extract.
  2. Slice the dinner rolls in half horizontally.
  3. Spread your desired filling on each slice.
  4. Roll up the slices tightly.
  5. Dip the roll-ups into the egg mixture, ensuring they are fully coated.
  6. Heat a non-stick skillet over medium heat and cook the roll-ups until golden brown on all sides.
  7. Serve warm with a sprinkle of powdered sugar or a drizzle of maple syrup.

3. Breakfast Bread Pudding

Ingredients:

  • Leftover dinner rolls
  • Milk
  • Eggs
  • Sugar
  • Vanilla extract
  • Cinnamon
  • Your choice of mix-ins (e.g., raisins, chopped nuts, chocolate chips)

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. Tear the dinner rolls into bite-sized pieces and place them in a greased baking dish.
  3. In a separate bowl, whisk together milk, eggs, sugar, vanilla extract, and cinnamon.
  4. Pour the mixture over the torn dinner rolls, making sure all the pieces are soaked.
  5. Add your preferred mix-ins and gently stir to distribute them evenly.
  6. Bake for approximately 30-35 minutes, or until the top is golden brown and the pudding is set.
  7. Allow it to cool slightly before serving. Enjoy warm or at room temperature.

4. Breakfast Sliders

Ingredients:

  • Leftover dinner rolls
  • Cooked breakfast meat (e.g., sausage patties, bacon)
  • Fried or scrambled eggs
  • Sliced cheese
  • Optional condiments (e.g., ketchup, mayonnaise)

Instructions:

  1. Slice the dinner rolls in half horizontally.
  2. Layer your choice of cooked breakfast meat, eggs, and cheese onto the bottom half of each roll.
  3. Spread condiments, if desired, on the top half of each roll.
  4. Place the top half onto the bottom half to create a slider.
  5. Heat a skillet or griddle over medium heat and toast the sliders until the cheese melts and the rolls are golden brown.
  6. Serve warm and enjoy these delightful breakfast sliders.

5. Breakfast Bread Pockets

Ingredients:

  • Leftover dinner rolls
  • Scrambled eggs
  • Chopped vegetables (e.g., bell peppers, onions, mushrooms)
  • Shredded cheese
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. Slice the dinner rolls in half horizontally.
  3. Gently scoop out the soft center of each roll to create a pocket.
  4. In a bowl, combine scrambled eggs, chopped vegetables, shredded cheese, salt, and pepper.
  5. Spoon the egg mixture into each bread pocket until filled.
  6. Place the filled pockets on a baking sheet and bake for approximately 10-15 minutes, or until the cheese is melted and the pockets are heated through.
  7. Serve warm and savor the deliciousness!

6. Breakfast Strata

Ingredients:

  • Leftover dinner rolls
  • Cooked breakfast meat (e.g., ham, sausage)
  • Shredded cheese
  • Milk
  • Eggs
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. Tear the dinner rolls into bite-sized pieces and place them in a greased baking dish.
  3. Layer cooked breakfast meat and shredded cheese over the torn dinner rolls.
  4. In a separate bowl, whisk together milk, eggs, salt, and pepper.
  5. Pour the milk mixture over the ingredients in the baking dish, ensuring all the pieces are soaked.
  6. Bake for approximately 40-45 minutes, or until the top is golden brown and the strata is set.
  7. Let it cool slightly before serving. Enjoy this hearty breakfast casserole!

7. Breakfast Bread Pudding Muffins

Ingredients:

  • Leftover dinner rolls
  • Milk
  • Eggs
  • Sugar
  • Vanilla extract
  • Cinnamon
  • Your choice of mix-ins (e.g., berries, chopped nuts, dried fruit)

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. Tear the dinner rolls into small pieces and place them in a bowl.
  3. In a separate bowl, whisk together milk, eggs, sugar, vanilla extract, and cinnamon.
  4. Pour the milk mixture over the torn dinner rolls, making sure all the pieces are soaked.
  5. Add your preferred mix-ins and gently stir to distribute them evenly.
  6. Grease a muffin tin and divide the mixture evenly among the cups.
  7. Bake for approximately 20-25 minutes, or until the tops are golden brown and the muffins are set.
  8. Allow them to cool slightly before removing from the tin. Enjoy these portable breakfast treats!

8. Breakfast Bread Pizzas

Ingredients:

  • Leftover dinner rolls
  • Tomato sauce
  • Shredded cheese
  • Your choice of toppings (e.g., cooked bacon, sliced bell peppers, mushrooms, onions)

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C).
  2. Slice the dinner rolls in half horizontally.
  3. Place the halved rolls on a baking sheet.
  4. Spread tomato sauce on each roll.
  5. Sprinkle shredded cheese and your preferred toppings onto each roll.
  6. Bake for about 10-15 minutes, or until the cheese is melted and bubbly.
  7. Serve hot and enjoy these mini breakfast pizzas.

There you have it, 8 delicious and inventive breakfast recipes to transform your leftover dinner rolls into morning delights. Who knew that stale bread could be so versatile and flavorful? Now you can enjoy a satisfying breakfast while minimizing food waste. Rise and shine with these creative recipes, and let your taste buds dance with joy!
