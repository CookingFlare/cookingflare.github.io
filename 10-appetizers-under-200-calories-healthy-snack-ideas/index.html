10 Appetizers Under 200 Calories: Healthy Snack Ideas


When it comes to snacking, many of us tend to opt for unhealthy options that are high in calories and low in nutrients. However, it’s essential to make smart choices that not only satisfy our cravings but also support our overall health and well-being. In this post, we’ll explore 10 delicious appetizer recipes that are under 200 calories, perfect for a quick and healthy snack.

1. Spicy Edamame Dip

  • Servings: 4
  • Calories: 140
  • Ingredients: 1 cup edamame, 1/4 cup low-fat plain Greek yogurt, 1 tablespoon lemon juice, 1 teaspoon olive oil, 1/2 teaspoon ground cumin, 1/4 teaspoon cayenne pepper, Salt and pepper to taste
  • Instructions: Preheat oven to 400°F (200°C). Rinse edamame and pat dry. In a bowl, combine yogurt, lemon juice, olive oil, cumin, cayenne pepper, salt, and pepper. Add edamame and toss to coat. Bake for 15-20 minutes or until tender. Serve with carrot sticks or pita chips.

2. Baked Sweet Potato Fries

  • Servings: 4
  • Calories: 160
  • Ingredients: 2 large sweet potatoes, 1 tablespoon olive oil, Salt to taste
  • Instructions: Preheat oven to 400°F (200°C). Cut sweet potatoes into fry shapes. In a bowl, toss sweet potatoes with olive oil and salt. Line a baking sheet with parchment paper and bake for 20-25 minutes or until crispy.

3. Mini Caprese Salads

  • Servings: 8
  • Calories: 120
  • Ingredients: 1 pint cherry tomatoes, 8 ounces fresh mozzarella, 1/4 cup fresh basil leaves, 1 tablespoon olive oil, Salt and pepper to taste
  • Instructions: Cut cherry tomatoes in half and place on a plate. Top each tomato with a slice of mozzarella and a basil leaf. Drizzle with olive oil and season with salt and pepper.

4. Grilled Shrimp Skewers

  • Servings: 4
  • Calories: 180
  • Ingredients: 1 pound large shrimp, 1/4 cup olive oil, 2 cloves garlic, minced, 1 tablespoon lemon juice, Salt and pepper to taste
  • Instructions: Preheat grill to medium-high heat. Thread shrimp onto skewers, leaving a small space between each shrimp. In a bowl, whisk together olive oil, garlic, and lemon juice. Brush the mixture onto the shrimp. Season with salt and pepper. Grill for 2-3 minutes per side or until pink and cooked through.

5. Roasted Chickpeas

  • Servings: 4
  • Calories: 140
  • Ingredients: 1 can chickpeas, 1 tablespoon olive oil, Salt to taste
  • Instructions: Preheat oven to 400°F (200°C). Rinse chickpeas and pat dry. In a bowl, toss chickpeas with olive oil and salt. Spread on a baking sheet and roast for 30-35 minutes or until crispy.

6. Avocado and Feta Bruschetta

  • Servings: 4
  • Calories: 160
  • Ingredients: 1 ripe avocado, 1/2 cup crumbled feta cheese, 1 baguette, 1 tablespoon olive oil, Salt and pepper to taste
  • Instructions: Preheat oven to 400°F (200°C). Slice baguette into 1-inch pieces and place on a baking sheet. Drizzle with olive oil and season with salt and pepper. Bake for 5-7 minutes or until lightly toasted. Top with avocado and feta cheese.

7. Spinach and Artichoke Dip

  • Servings: 4
  • Calories: 180
  • Ingredients: 1 cup frozen spinach, thawed and drained, 1 can artichoke hearts, 1 cup low-fat plain Greek yogurt, 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese, 1/2 teaspoon lemon juice, Salt and pepper to taste
  • Instructions: In a bowl, combine spinach, artichoke hearts, yogurt, Parmesan cheese, and lemon juice. Season with salt and pepper. Serve with carrot sticks or pita chips.

8. Mini Quiches

  • Servings: 8
  • Calories: 160
  • Ingredients: 1 cup pie crust dough, 1 cup mixed vegetables (bell peppers, onions, mushrooms), 1 cup shredded cheddar cheese, 2 large eggs, Salt and pepper to taste
  • Instructions: Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C). Roll out pie crust dough and cut into 8 equal pieces. In a bowl, whisk together eggs and a pinch of salt and pepper. Add mixed vegetables and cheddar cheese. Pour into pie crusts and bake for 15-20 minutes or until golden brown.

9. Baked Zucchini Fries

  • Servings: 4
  • Calories: 140
  • Ingredients: 2 medium zucchinis, 1 tablespoon olive oil, Salt to taste
  • Instructions: Preheat oven to 400°F (200°C). Cut zucchinis into fry shapes. In a bowl, toss zucchinis with olive oil and salt. Line a baking sheet with parchment paper and bake for 20-25 minutes or until crispy.

10. Mini Caponata

  • Servings: 4
  • Calories: 160
  • Ingredients: 1 cup eggplant, 1 cup celery, 1 cup capers, 1/4 cup olive oil, 2 tablespoons lemon juice, Salt and pepper to taste
  • Instructions: Cut eggplant and celery into small pieces. In a bowl, combine eggplant, celery, capers, olive oil, and lemon juice. Season with salt and pepper. Serve with crusty bread or crackers.

These 10 delicious appetizers under 200 calories are perfect for a quick and healthy snack. From spicy edamame dip to mini caponata, each recipe is not only tasty but also packed with nutrients. Whether you’re looking for a snack to take on-the-go or a light bite to enjoy with friends, these recipes are sure to satisfy your cravings without compromising your health goals.
