Paleo Chicken and Rice Bowl with Roasted Vegetables: A Healthy and Filling Recipe


Are you looking for a delicious and nutritious meal that is also easy to prepare? Look no further! This Paleo Chicken and Rice Bowl with Roasted Vegetables is a perfect recipe for a quick and healthy dinner. Made with fresh and wholesome ingredients, this dish is not only flavorful but also packed with nutrients. In this recipe, we will show you how to cook chicken breast, brown rice, and a variety of roasted vegetables, all in one bowl.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound boneless, skinless chicken breast
  • 1 cup brown rice
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup mixed vegetables (such as broccoli, carrots, and bell peppers)
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 tablespoons freshly squeezed lemon juice

Instructions

Step 1: Prepare the Chicken

Preheat the oven to 400°F (200°C). Rinse the chicken breast and pat it dry with a paper towel. In a small bowl, mix together the olive oil, garlic, and thyme. Brush the mixture evenly over both sides of the chicken breast. Season with salt and pepper to taste.

Step 2: Cook the Brown Rice

In a medium saucepan, bring 2 cups of water to a boil. Add the brown rice and reduce the heat to low. Cover the saucepan and simmer for 20-25 minutes, or until the water is absorbed and the rice is cooked. Fluff the rice with a fork and set it aside.

Step 3: Roast the Vegetables

Toss the mixed vegetables with 1 tablespoon of olive oil, salt, and pepper. Spread the vegetables out in a single layer on a baking sheet. Roast in the preheated oven for 20-25 minutes, or until the vegetables are tender and lightly browned.

Step 4: Cook the Chicken

Place the chicken breast on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper. Roast in the preheated oven for 15-20 minutes, or until the chicken is cooked through and reaches an internal temperature of 165°F (74°C).

Step 5: Assemble the Bowl

Once the chicken and rice are cooked, it’s time to assemble the bowl. Slice the cooked chicken into thin strips. Divide the cooked brown rice and roasted vegetables between two bowls. Top each bowl with a slice of chicken and drizzle with freshly squeezed lemon juice. Serve immediately and enjoy!

Tips and Variations

  • To add some extra flavor to the dish, you can add a sprinkle of paprika or chili powder to the chicken and vegetables before roasting.
  • If you prefer a different type of grain, you can substitute the brown rice with quinoa or cauliflower rice.
  • To make this recipe more substantial, you can add some sliced avocado or a fried egg on top of the chicken and rice.

Conclusion

This Paleo Chicken and Rice Bowl with Roasted Vegetables is a delicious and nutritious meal that is perfect for a quick and easy dinner. With its fresh and wholesome ingredients, this dish is not only flavorful but also packed with nutrients. Whether you’re looking for a healthy meal option or just want to try something new, this recipe is sure to please. Give it a try and enjoy the benefits of a Paleo diet!
