Vegan Lemon Pound Cake: Tart and Moist


Are you looking for a delightful vegan dessert that’s bursting with citrusy flavor and has a moist, tender crumb? Look no further than this Vegan Lemon Pound Cake recipe! This indulgent yet wholesome treat is perfect for any occasion, whether you’re hosting a brunch, celebrating a special event, or simply craving a zesty, satisfying dessert. Packed with the tangy essence of lemons and the richness of a classic pound cake, this vegan version will surely win over the hearts and taste buds of vegans and non-vegans alike.

Ingredients

For the Cake:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/2 cup unsweetened almond milk
  • 1/2 cup coconut oil, melted
  • 1/4 cup fresh lemon juice
  • Zest of 2 lemons
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt

For the Lemon Glaze:

  • 1 cup powdered sugar
  • 2-3 tablespoons fresh lemon juice
  • Zest of 1 lemon

Instructions

Step 1: Preheat and Prepare

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease a 9x5 inch loaf pan and line it with parchment paper, leaving an overhang on the sides for easy removal.

Step 2: Mix the Dry Ingredients

  1. In a large mixing bowl, whisk together the flour, baking powder, baking soda, and salt until well combined.

Step 3: Combine Wet Ingredients

  1. In another bowl, whisk together the melted coconut oil and granulated sugar until smooth. Add the almond milk, lemon juice, lemon zest, and vanilla extract, and mix until fully incorporated.

Step 4: Combine and Bake

  1. Gently fold the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients, stirring until just combined. Be careful not to overmix.
  2. Pour the batter into the prepared loaf pan and smooth the top with a spatula.
  3. Bake for 45-55 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.
  4. Once baked, remove the cake from the oven and let it cool in the pan for 10 minutes. Then, transfer it to a wire rack to cool completely.

Step 5: Prepare the Lemon Glaze

  1. In a small bowl, whisk together the powdered sugar, fresh lemon juice, and lemon zest until smooth and pourable.

Step 6: Glaze the Cake

  1. Once the cake has cooled, drizzle the lemon glaze over the top. Let the glaze set for a few minutes before slicing and serving.

Serving Suggestions

This Vegan Lemon Pound Cake is perfect on its own, but you can take it to the next level by serving it with a dollop of coconut whipped cream, a scoop of dairy-free vanilla ice cream, or a side of fresh berries. The bright, citrusy flavor of the cake pairs beautifully with a variety of toppings, making it a versatile dessert that’s sure to impress.

Storage Tips

Store any leftover cake in an airtight container at room temperature for up to 3 days, or in the refrigerator for up to a week. You can also freeze individual slices for longer storage.

Final Thoughts

The Vegan Lemon Pound Cake is a true testament to the fact that vegan desserts can be just as decadent and satisfying as their non-vegan counterparts. With its vibrant lemon flavor, tender crumb, and luscious glaze, this cake is a delightful treat for anyone looking to indulge in a cruelty-free, citrusy delight. Whether you’re a seasoned vegan baker or simply looking to expand your culinary horizons, this recipe is a must-try. So gather your ingredients, preheat your oven, and get ready to savor the tangy, moist goodness of this Vegan Lemon Pound Cake!
