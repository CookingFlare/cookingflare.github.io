Classic Tequila Sunrise Cupcakes: A Tropical Boozy Treat


If you’re a fan of cupcakes and love a little tropical twist, then these Classic Tequila Sunrise Cupcakes are the perfect treat for you! These boozy delights are inspired by the iconic tequila sunrise cocktail, featuring the vibrant colors and delicious flavors that make it a favorite among cocktail enthusiasts. Whether you’re hosting a summer party or simply want to indulge in a tropical dessert, these cupcakes are sure to impress.

Ingredients

For the cupcakes:

  • 1 and 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 and 1/2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/2 cup milk

For the tequila sunrise frosting:

  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 3 cups powdered sugar
  • 2 tablespoons tequila
  • 1 tablespoon grenadine syrup
  • Zest of 1 orange

For the garnish:

  • Orange slices
  • Maraschino cherries

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C) and line a muffin tin with cupcake liners.

  2. In a medium-sized bowl, whisk together the flour, baking powder, and salt. Set aside.

  3. In a separate bowl, cream together the softened butter and granulated sugar until light and fluffy. Add in the eggs, one at a time, beating well after each addition. Stir in the vanilla extract.

  4. Gradually add the dry ingredients to the butter mixture, alternating with the milk. Begin and end with the dry ingredients, mixing until just combined.

  5. Divide the batter evenly among the cupcake liners, filling each about two-thirds full. Bake for 18-20 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean. Remove from the oven and let the cupcakes cool completely on a wire rack.

  6. While the cupcakes are cooling, prepare the tequila sunrise frosting. In a large bowl, beat the softened butter until creamy. Gradually add the powdered sugar, mixing until smooth. Stir in the tequila and orange zest until well combined.

  7. Divide the frosting into two separate bowls. In one bowl, mix in the grenadine syrup until the frosting turns a vibrant red. Leave the other bowl of frosting as is.

  8. Once the cupcakes are completely cooled, pipe or spread the orange frosting onto half of the cupcakes, creating a smooth layer. For the other half, pipe or spread the grenadine frosting, creating a beautiful contrast of colors.

  9. Garnish each cupcake with an orange slice and a maraschino cherry to mimic the classic tequila sunrise cocktail.

  10. Serve these delectable treats at your next gathering and watch as your guests are amazed by the stunning appearance and delightful flavors of these Classic Tequila Sunrise Cupcakes.

Tips and Variations

  • For an extra boozy kick, you can brush the cooled cupcakes with a mixture of tequila and orange juice before frosting them. Just be sure not to overdo it, as you don’t want the cupcakes to become too soggy.

  • If you want a non-alcoholic version, simply omit the tequila and replace it with orange juice in the frosting. The cupcakes will still be bursting with tropical flavors.

  • Experiment with different garnishes to make these cupcakes your own. Try adding a sprinkle of coconut flakes or a drizzle of chocolate sauce for an extra touch of decadence.

  • Store any leftover cupcakes in an airtight container at room temperature for up to three days. Just be sure to consume responsibly!

So, there you have it! Classic Tequila Sunrise Cupcakes, a tropical boozy treat that will transport you to a sunny paradise with every bite. These cupcakes are not only visually stunning but also packed with delightful flavors that will leave your taste buds craving more. Give them a try, and let the tropical vibes take over your dessert table!
