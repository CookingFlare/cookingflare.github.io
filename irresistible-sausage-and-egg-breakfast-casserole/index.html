Irresistible Sausage and Egg Breakfast Casserole


There’s something special about starting your day with a delicious and hearty breakfast. And what better way to kickstart your morning than with an irresistible sausage and egg breakfast casserole? Packed with flavor and loaded with wholesome ingredients, this dish is sure to become a family favorite. So, let’s dive right into the recipe!

Ingredients

  • 1 pound of breakfast sausage
  • 6 cups of cubed day-old bread
  • 2 cups of shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1 ½ cups of milk
  • 6 large eggs
  • 1 teaspoon of dried mustard
  • 1 teaspoon of salt
  • ½ teaspoon of black pepper
  • ½ teaspoon of paprika

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease a 9x13 inch baking dish and set aside.

  2. In a large skillet, cook the breakfast sausage over medium heat until browned and cooked through. Make sure to break it up into crumbles as it cooks. Once done, remove from heat and set aside.

  3. In the greased baking dish, spread half of the cubed bread evenly across the bottom. Sprinkle half of the cooked sausage and half of the shredded cheddar cheese over the bread.

  4. Repeat the layers with the remaining bread, sausage, and cheese.

  5. In a medium-sized bowl, whisk together the milk, eggs, dried mustard, salt, black pepper, and paprika until well combined.

  6. Pour the egg mixture evenly over the layers in the baking dish. Press down lightly with a spatula to ensure all the bread is soaked in the mixture.

  7. Cover the baking dish with aluminum foil and let it sit in the refrigerator for at least 2 hours or overnight. This step allows the flavors to meld together and the bread to fully absorb the egg mixture.

  8. When you’re ready to bake, remove the casserole from the refrigerator and let it sit at room temperature for 15 minutes.

  9. Bake the casserole, uncovered, in the preheated oven for 45-50 minutes, or until the top is golden brown and the center is set. You can check for doneness by inserting a toothpick into the center – if it comes out clean, the casserole is ready.

  10. Remove from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes before serving. Cut into squares and garnish with fresh herbs, if desired.

Serving Suggestions

This sausage and egg breakfast casserole is incredibly versatile and can be enjoyed on its own or paired with a variety of sides. Here are a few serving suggestions to get you started:

  • Fresh fruit salad: The sweetness of juicy berries or sliced tropical fruits complements the savory flavors of the casserole perfectly.

  • Crispy bacon: Serve a few strips of crispy bacon alongside the casserole for an extra indulgent breakfast experience.

  • Toast or English muffins: Toast up some bread or English muffins and serve them on the side for a complete breakfast spread.

  • Green salad: Add a touch of freshness to your meal with a simple green salad dressed in a light vinaigrette.

Final Thoughts

The irresistible sausage and egg breakfast casserole is a true breakfast delight that will satisfy even the heartiest of appetites. Its savory flavors, comforting textures, and make-ahead convenience make it an ideal choice for busy mornings or special occasions. So, gather your ingredients, prepare the casserole the night before, and wake up to a delicious breakfast that will make your taste buds sing. Enjoy!
