Indian Butter Chicken Tikka Masala Recipe with Naan Bread


If you’re a fan of Indian cuisine, you’ve probably indulged in the rich and flavorful delights of Butter Chicken Tikka Masala. This iconic dish is a perfect blend of tender chicken, aromatic spices, and a luscious creamy tomato-based sauce. Paired with soft and pillowy naan bread, it’s a meal that’s guaranteed to transport your taste buds to the vibrant streets of India. Today, we’re going to delve into the art of creating this culinary masterpiece right in your own kitchen.

Ingredients

For the Chicken Marinade:

  • 1.5 pounds boneless, skinless chicken thighs, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 1 cup plain yogurt
  • 1 tablespoon ginger garlic paste
  • 1 tablespoon garam masala
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric
  • Salt to taste
  • Freshly ground black pepper

For the Butter Chicken Sauce:

  • 3 tablespoons butter
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • 2 large onions, finely chopped
  • 1 tablespoon ginger garlic paste
  • 1 tablespoon garam masala
  • 1 tablespoon ground cumin
  • 1 tablespoon paprika
  • 1 teaspoon ground turmeric
  • 1 can (14 oz) tomato puree
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1 tablespoon honey
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh cilantro for garnish

For the Naan Bread:

  • 3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon active dry yeast
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 3 tablespoons plain yogurt
  • 1 cup warm water
  • 2 tablespoons melted butter

Instructions

Marinating the Chicken:

  1. In a large bowl, combine the yogurt, ginger garlic paste, garam masala, ground cumin, paprika, turmeric, salt, and black pepper.
  2. Add the chicken pieces to the marinade and ensure they are well-coated. Cover the bowl and refrigerate for at least 2 hours, or preferably overnight for the flavors to meld.

Making the Butter Chicken Sauce:

  1. In a large skillet, melt the butter and vegetable oil over medium heat.
  2. Add the chopped onions and sauté until they turn translucent.
  3. Stir in the ginger garlic paste, garam masala, ground cumin, paprika, and turmeric. Cook for a few minutes until the raw aroma dissipates.
  4. Pour in the tomato puree and simmer the sauce for about 10 minutes, allowing it to thicken.
  5. Add the marinated chicken along with any remaining marinade to the sauce. Cook until the chicken is tender and cooked through.
  6. Pour in the heavy cream and honey, stirring gently to incorporate. Let the sauce simmer for an additional 10 minutes.
  7. Season with salt according to your preference.

Preparing the Naan Bread:

  1. In a large mixing bowl, combine the flour, yeast, sugar, and salt.
  2. Make a well in the center and add the yogurt and warm water. Knead the mixture until it forms a smooth and elastic dough.
  3. Cover the bowl with a damp cloth and let the dough rise in a warm place for about 1 hour, or until it has doubled in size.
  4. Preheat a skillet or griddle over medium-high heat.
  5. Divide the dough into small balls and roll them out into oval or round shapes.
  6. Brush the naan bread with melted butter and place them on the skillet. Cook for about 2 minutes on each side, until they puff up and develop a golden brown color.

Serving the Dish

Garnish the Butter Chicken Tikka Masala with freshly chopped cilantro and serve it hot alongside the warm and comforting naan bread. Your senses will be awakened by the tantalizing aromas and the explosion of flavors with every mouthful.

Conclusion

The Indian Butter Chicken Tikka Masala with Naan Bread is a celebration of traditional Indian spices and culinary expertise. By following this recipe, you have the opportunity to recreate the magic of Indian cuisine in your own home. Whether you’re hosting a dinner party or simply craving a luxurious feast, this dish is sure to impress and satisfy. So, roll up your sleeves, gather your ingredients, and embark on a culinary journey to savor the delights of India.
