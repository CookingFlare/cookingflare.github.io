Light and Flavorful Stir-Fry Noodle Recipes for a Quick Weeknight Meal


Are you tired of spending hours in the kitchen after a long day at work? Do you crave a delicious and satisfying meal that can be whipped up in no time? Look no further! In this blog post, we will explore a collection of light and flavorful stir-fry noodle recipes that are perfect for a quick weeknight meal. These recipes are not only easy to make, but they are also packed with healthy ingredients and bursting with incredible flavors. So, let’s dive right in!

1. Vegetable Stir-Fry Noodles

Ingredients:

  • 8 ounces of rice noodles
  • 2 tablespoons of vegetable oil
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 small onion, thinly sliced
  • 1 carrot, julienned
  • 1 bell pepper, thinly sliced
  • 1 cup of broccoli florets
  • 1 cup of snap peas
  • 2 tablespoons of soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon of oyster sauce
  • 1 teaspoon of sesame oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Cook the rice noodles according to the package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  2. Heat the vegetable oil in a large skillet or wok over medium-high heat.
  3. Add the minced garlic and sliced onion to the skillet and sauté until fragrant and slightly softened.
  4. Add the carrot, bell pepper, broccoli, and snap peas to the skillet. Stir-fry for 3-4 minutes or until the vegetables are tender-crisp.
  5. In a small bowl, whisk together the soy sauce, oyster sauce, and sesame oil. Pour the sauce over the vegetables and stir to coat.
  6. Add the cooked rice noodles to the skillet and toss until everything is well combined.
  7. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  8. Serve hot and enjoy!

2. Teriyaki Chicken Noodle Stir-Fry

Ingredients:

  • 8 ounces of egg noodles
  • 1 tablespoon of vegetable oil
  • 1 pound of boneless, skinless chicken breast, thinly sliced
  • 1 cup of sliced mushrooms
  • 1 red bell pepper, thinly sliced
  • 1 cup of snow peas
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1/4 cup of teriyaki sauce
  • 2 tablespoons of hoisin sauce
  • 1 tablespoon of honey
  • 1 teaspoon of sesame oil
  • Sesame seeds for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Cook the egg noodles according to the package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  2. Heat the vegetable oil in a large skillet or wok over medium-high heat.
  3. Add the sliced chicken breast to the skillet and cook until browned and cooked through. Remove from the skillet and set aside.
  4. In the same skillet, add the mushrooms, bell pepper, snow peas, and minced garlic. Stir-fry for 3-4 minutes or until the vegetables are tender-crisp.
  5. In a small bowl, whisk together the teriyaki sauce, hoisin sauce, honey, and sesame oil. Pour the sauce over the vegetables and stir to coat.
  6. Add the cooked egg noodles and cooked chicken to the skillet. Toss until everything is well combined.
  7. Garnish with sesame seeds and serve hot.

3. Spicy Shrimp Stir-Fry Noodles

Ingredients:

  • 8 ounces of udon noodles
  • 1 tablespoon of vegetable oil
  • 1 pound of large shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 red bell pepper, thinly sliced
  • 1 cup of sliced cabbage
  • 1 cup of bean sprouts
  • 2 tablespoons of soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon of oyster sauce
  • 1 tablespoon of Sriracha sauce (adjust to taste)
  • 1 teaspoon of sesame oil
  • Chopped green onions for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Cook the udon noodles according to the package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  2. Heat the vegetable oil in a large skillet or wok over medium-high heat.
  3. Add the minced garlic and sliced bell pepper to the skillet and sauté until fragrant and slightly softened.
  4. Add the shrimp to the skillet and cook until pink and cooked through. Remove from the skillet and set aside.
  5. In the same skillet, add the sliced cabbage and bean sprouts. Stir-fry for 2-3 minutes or until the vegetables are slightly wilted.
  6. In a small bowl, whisk together the soy sauce, oyster sauce, Sriracha sauce, and sesame oil. Pour the sauce over the vegetables and stir to coat.
  7. Add the cooked udon noodles and cooked shrimp to the skillet. Toss until everything is well combined.
  8. Garnish with chopped green onions and serve hot.

Conclusion

With these light and flavorful stir-fry noodle recipes, you can enjoy a quick and delicious meal without sacrificing taste or health. Whether you prefer a vegetable-packed stir-fry or crave the combination of teriyaki chicken or spicy shrimp, these recipes will surely satisfy your cravings. So, the next time you’re short on time but still want a fantastic homemade meal, give these stir-fry noodle recipes a try. Happy cooking!
