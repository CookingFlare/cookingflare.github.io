Spicy Shredded Beef Barbacoa: A Mexican Street Taco Favorite


If you’re a fan of Mexican cuisine and love the bold flavors of street tacos, then you’re in for a treat! Today, we’re diving into the delicious world of Spicy Shredded Beef Barbacoa, a mouthwatering dish that will transport your taste buds straight to the streets of Mexico.

The Perfect Cut of Meat

To achieve that perfect melt-in-your-mouth texture, you’ll need to start with the right cut of beef. Look for a well-marbled piece, such as beef chuck or brisket. These cuts are not only flavorful but also tender when cooked low and slow.

The Flavorful Marinade

The secret to a truly delicious Spicy Shredded Beef Barbacoa lies in the marinade. A combination of spices, herbs, and a touch of acidity will infuse the meat with an explosion of flavors. Here’s what you’ll need:

  • 4 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 medium-sized onion, finely chopped
  • 2 tablespoons of chipotle peppers in adobo sauce, finely minced
  • 1 tablespoon of ground cumin
  • 1 tablespoon of dried oregano
  • 1 tablespoon of smoked paprika
  • 2 teaspoons of salt
  • 1 teaspoon of black pepper
  • Juice of 2 limes
  • 1/4 cup of apple cider vinegar
  • 1/4 cup of beef broth

Combine all the marinade ingredients in a bowl and mix well. Place your chosen cut of beef in a resealable plastic bag and pour the marinade over it. Seal the bag and refrigerate for at least 4 hours, or preferably overnight, to allow the flavors to penetrate the meat.

Slow Cooked Perfection

When it comes to cooking Spicy Shredded Beef Barbacoa, patience is key. This dish is traditionally prepared by slow-cooking the meat until it becomes tender and easily shreds apart. Here’s how to achieve that succulent texture:

  1. Preheat your oven to 325°F (163°C).
  2. Remove the marinated beef from the refrigerator and let it come to room temperature.
  3. Heat a large oven-safe pot or Dutch oven over medium-high heat.
  4. Add a tablespoon of oil to the pot and sear the beef on all sides until browned. This step helps to develop rich flavors.
  5. Once the beef is seared, pour in the remaining marinade along with an additional 1/2 cup of beef broth.
  6. Cover the pot with a tight-fitting lid and transfer it to the preheated oven.
  7. Allow the beef to cook for 3-4 hours, or until it’s tender enough to easily shred with a fork.
  8. Check the pot occasionally to ensure there is enough liquid. If it starts to dry out, add more beef broth as needed.

Shredding and Serving

Once the beef is tender and easily shreds apart, it’s time to witness the fruits of your patience and labor. Remove the pot from the oven and transfer the meat to a cutting board. Using two forks, shred the beef into bite-sized pieces.

Now, let’s assemble the perfect Spicy Shredded Beef Barbacoa street taco:

  1. Warm up some corn tortillas on a hot skillet or griddle until they’re pliable.
  2. Spoon a generous amount of the shredded beef onto each tortilla.
  3. Top with your favorite garnishes, such as chopped onions, fresh cilantro, and a squeeze of lime juice.
  4. If you like it spicy, feel free to add some hot sauce or pickled jalapeños.

And there you have it – a tantalizing plate of Spicy Shredded Beef Barbacoa street tacos ready to be devoured! The tender beef, infused with bold flavors, combined with the freshness of the garnishes, will transport you straight to the vibrant streets of Mexico.

So, gather your friends and family, and enjoy the ultimate Mexican street taco experience with this Spicy Shredded Beef Barbacoa recipe. ¡Buen provecho!
