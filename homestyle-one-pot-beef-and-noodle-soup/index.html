Homestyle One Pot Beef and Noodle Soup

3 min · 542 words

There’s nothing quite like a steaming bowl of homemade soup to warm you up on a chilly evening. This Homestyle One Pot Beef and Noodle Soup is the perfect comfort food that’s hearty, flavorful, and incredibly easy to make. Whether you’re cooking for your family, hosting a casual dinner, or simply craving a satisfying meal, this recipe is sure to become a favorite in your kitchen.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound beef stew meat, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 4 carrots, sliced
  • 3 celery stalks, chopped
  • 8 cups beef broth
  • 2 cups water
  • 2 bay leaves
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • 1 teaspoon dried rosemary
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 8 ounces wide egg noodles
  • Fresh parsley, for garnish

Instructions

  1. In a large pot or Dutch oven, heat some oil over medium-high heat. Add the beef stew meat and brown it on all sides. This step adds a deep, rich flavor to the soup.

  2. Once the beef is browned, add the diced onion and minced garlic to the pot. Sauté for a few minutes until the onion is soft and translucent.

  3. Add the sliced carrots and chopped celery to the pot, and stir everything together. Let the vegetables cook for a few minutes until they begin to soften.

  4. Pour in the beef broth and water, and bring the mixture to a gentle boil. Add the bay leaves, dried thyme, dried rosemary, salt, and pepper. Stir well to combine all the flavors.

  5. Reduce the heat to low and let the soup simmer for about 1-2 hours, or until the beef is tender and the flavors have melded together beautifully.

  6. When the soup is almost ready, add the wide egg noodles to the pot. Let them cook for about 8-10 minutes, or until they are tender but still slightly firm to the bite.

  7. Once the noodles are cooked, taste the soup and adjust the seasoning if needed. Remember to remove the bay leaves before serving.

  8. Serve the Homestyle One Pot Beef and Noodle Soup hot, garnished with a sprinkle of fresh parsley for a pop of color and freshness.

Tips and Variations

  • For an extra burst of flavor, you can add a splash of Worcestershire sauce or a drizzle of balsamic vinegar to the soup.
  • If you prefer a thicker broth, you can mix a tablespoon of cornstarch with a little water and stir it into the soup during the last few minutes of cooking.
  • Feel free to customize the vegetables in the soup based on your preferences. You can add mushrooms, bell peppers, or even some leafy greens like spinach or kale.

Final Thoughts

This Homestyle One Pot Beef and Noodle Soup is a timeless classic that brings together the heartiness of beef and the comfort of noodles in one delicious bowl. Its rich broth, tender beef, and wholesome vegetables make it a complete meal that’s perfect for any occasion. Whether you’re looking for a quick weeknight dinner or a soul-warming dish to share with loved ones, this recipe is a winner every time. So, grab a spoon and savor the comforting goodness of this homestyle favorite!
