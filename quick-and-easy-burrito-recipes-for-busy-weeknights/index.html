Quick and Easy Burrito Recipes for Busy Weeknights


When the clock is ticking and your schedule is packed, finding time to prepare a delicious and satisfying meal can feel overwhelming. That’s where the versatile burrito comes to the rescue! Quick and easy burrito recipes can transform your busy weeknights into culinary adventures, allowing you to whip up flavorful meals that the whole family will love. Read on to discover a variety of burrito recipes that are not only simple to make but also packed with nutrients and taste.

Why Burritos?

Burritos are a fantastic meal option for busy weeknights for several reasons:

  1. Versatility: You can fill them with virtually anything—meat, beans, vegetables, cheese, and sauces. The possibilities are endless!
  2. Quick Preparation: Most burrito recipes can be made in under 30 minutes, making them perfect for those hectic evenings.
  3. Make-Ahead Friendly: Burritos can be prepped in advance and stored in the fridge or freezer, allowing for quick reheating.
  4. Kid-Friendly: Kids love burritos, and they’re a great way to sneak in some veggies.

Essential Ingredients

Before diving into our recipes, let’s look at some essential ingredients you may need for your quick burritos:

  • Tortillas: Flour or corn tortillas are the foundation of any burrito. Flour tortillas are more pliable, while corn tortillas offer a gluten-free option.
  • Proteins: Ground beef, chicken, turkey, or plant-based proteins like black beans or lentils can serve as the primary filling.
  • Vegetables: Bell peppers, onions, spinach, and corn are great choices for adding flavor and nutrition.
  • Cheese: Shredded cheese like cheddar, Monterey Jack, or queso fresco enhances the taste and texture.
  • Sauces: Salsa, hot sauce, or sour cream can add a finishing touch.

Recipe 1: Classic Beef Burrito

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 packet taco seasoning
  • 4 large flour tortillas
  • 1 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1 cup lettuce, shredded
  • 1 tomato, diced
  • 1/2 cup sour cream
  • Salsa for serving

Instructions

  1. Cook the Beef: In a skillet over medium heat, brown the ground beef until fully cooked. Drain excess fat. Add taco seasoning and follow package instructions to incorporate seasoning evenly.

  2. Assemble the Burritos: Lay a tortilla flat on a clean surface. In the center, add a portion of the seasoned beef, followed by cheese, lettuce, and diced tomatoes.

  3. Wrap It Up: Fold the sides of the tortilla over the filling and then roll from the bottom up to create a tight burrito.

  4. Serve: Slice in half and serve with sour cream and salsa on the side.

Cooking Tip

For added flavor, you can grill the wrapped burritos in a skillet until the tortillas are slightly crispy.

Recipe 2: Veggie Burrito with Black Beans

Ingredients

  • 1 can black beans, drained and rinsed
  • 1 cup cooked rice (brown or white)
  • 1 cup bell peppers, diced
  • 1 cup corn (frozen or canned)
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • 4 large whole wheat tortillas
  • 1 cup avocado, sliced
  • 1/2 cup salsa

Instructions

  1. Sauté the Vegetables: In a skillet, combine bell peppers and corn. Sauté for about 5 minutes until tender. Add black beans and cumin, stirring until heated through.

  2. Prepare the Burrito: Place a tortilla on a flat surface. Add some rice, the veggie mixture, and avocado slices.

  3. Wrap and Serve: Fold the sides over the filling and roll the burrito tightly. Serve with salsa for dipping.

Cooking Tip

Feel free to add your favorite spices or hot sauce to the veggie mixture for an extra kick!

Recipe 3: Chicken and Rice Burrito

Ingredients

  • 2 cups shredded rotisserie chicken
  • 1 cup cooked rice
  • 1 cup shredded Monterey Jack cheese
  • 1/2 cup green onions, chopped
  • 4 large flour tortillas
  • 1 cup guacamole

Instructions

  1. Mix Ingredients: In a bowl, combine shredded chicken, cooked rice, cheese, and green onions.

  2. Assemble the Burritos: Lay a tortilla on a flat surface. Spoon the chicken mixture in the center and top with guacamole.

  3. Roll and Enjoy: Fold the sides in and roll tightly. Slice in half to serve.

Cooking Tip

For a crunchy texture, consider adding some crushed tortilla chips inside the burrito for an unexpected crunch!

Recipe 4: Breakfast Burrito

Ingredients

  • 4 large eggs
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1 cup cooked sausage, crumbled
  • 1 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • 4 large flour tortillas
  • 1/2 cup salsa

Instructions

  1. Scramble the Eggs: In a bowl, whisk together eggs and milk. Pour into a heated skillet and cook, stirring frequently, until scrambled.

  2. Combine Fillings: In a bowl, mix scrambled eggs, sausage, and cheese.

  3. Wrap It Up: Place a portion of the mixture on each tortilla. Roll tightly and serve with salsa.

Cooking Tip

Experiment with adding veggies like diced bell peppers or spinach into the egg mixture for a heartier breakfast!

Recipe 5: Spicy Shrimp Burrito

Ingredients

  • 1 pound shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 2 tablespoons taco seasoning
  • 4 large flour tortillas
  • 1 cup cabbage, shredded
  • 1 avocado, sliced
  • 1/2 cup chipotle sauce

Instructions

  1. Cook the Shrimp: In a skillet, toss shrimp with taco seasoning and cook until pink and opaque, about 3-4 minutes.

  2. Prepare the Burrito: Lay a tortilla flat and add cooked shrimp, shredded cabbage, and avocado slices.

  3. Wrap and Serve: Roll the burrito tightly and drizzle with chipotle sauce before serving.

Cooking Tip

Add some lime juice or cilantro for a refreshing twist to the shrimp filling!

Recipe 6: Quinoa Burrito Bowl

Ingredients

  • 1 cup cooked quinoa
  • 1 can black beans, drained and rinsed
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1 avocado, diced
  • 1/2 cup corn
  • 1/2 cup cilantro, chopped
  • 4 large tortillas (optional)

Instructions

  1. Combine Ingredients: In a bowl, mix cooked quinoa, black beans, tomatoes, avocado, corn, and cilantro.

  2. Serve as a Bowl: For a burrito bowl, serve the mixture in a bowl with tortilla chips on the side. Alternatively, fill a tortilla with the mixture to create a burrito.

Cooking Tip

This recipe is highly customizable—feel free to add any leftover veggies or proteins!

Meal Prep Tips for Burritos

When time is of the essence, meal prepping can be a game-changer. Here are some tips to make your burrito nights even easier:

  1. Pre-Cook Proteins: Cook and season proteins like chicken, beef, or beans in advance, so they’re ready to go during the week.

  2. Chop Vegetables Ahead of Time: Pre-chop your vegetables and store them in airtight containers to save time.

  3. Batch Cooking Rice or Quinoa: Prepare a large batch of rice or quinoa at the start of the week that can be used in various burrito recipes.

  4. Freeze Burritos: Make a batch of burritos and freeze them. When you need a quick meal, just reheat in the microwave or oven.

  5. Use Leftovers: Get creative with leftovers by adding them to burritos. It’s a great way to minimize food waste while enjoying a delicious meal.

Conclusion

Quick and easy burrito recipes are the perfect solution for busy weeknights. With their versatility, speed, and kid-friendly appeal, you can enjoy a satisfying meal without sacrificing time or taste. Whether you prefer classic beef burritos, veggie-packed options, or breakfast delights, there’s a burrito recipe for every palate. So gather your ingredients and get ready to roll—fantastic burrito nights await you!
