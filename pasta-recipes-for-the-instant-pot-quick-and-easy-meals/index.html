Pasta Recipes for the Instant Pot: Quick and Easy Meals


Cooking pasta in the Instant Pot can be a game-changer for busy weeknights or when you’re searching for meals that come together quickly but don’t skimp on flavor. This versatile appliance is known for streamlining cooking processes, reducing time spent in the kitchen while delivering delicious results. From creamy classics to rich tomato-based dishes, one-pot pasta meals made in the Instant Pot are not only convenient but also packed with flavor and texture. In this guide, I’ll share some of the best pasta recipes for the Instant Pot that are quick, easy, and sure to satisfy everyone in your household.

Why Make Pasta in the Instant Pot?

The beauty of cooking pasta in the Instant Pot lies in its simplicity. You don’t need multiple pots or strainers, and everything cooks together, allowing the flavors to meld perfectly. Plus, the pressure-cooking aspect ensures that the pasta absorbs all the divine flavors from the sauce. Whether you’re short on time or looking for a foolproof way to make dinner, the Instant Pot has got you covered.

With a variety of recipes to explore, cooking pasta this way also minimizes cleanup, making it an excellent option for those who want a hearty meal without worrying about a pile of dirty dishes.

Tips for Cooking Perfect Pasta in the Instant Pot

Before diving into the recipes, here are a few tips to ensure success with your Instant Pot pasta creations:

  1. Liquid Ratio is Key: Pasta needs the right amount of liquid to cook properly. Generally, you’ll need enough broth, water, or sauce to cover the pasta completely.

  2. Cooking Time: The pressure cooking time for pasta is typically half the time listed on the box, minus one minute. Always err on the side of less time since you can leave the pasta in the pot on “Keep Warm” for a few extra minutes if needed.

  3. Layer Ingredients Carefully: To prevent the Instant Pot from showing a burn notice, avoid stirring thick sauces or ingredients like cheese into the bottom of the pot before pressure cooking. Instead, layer these ingredients on top of the pasta and stir after cooking.

  4. Manual Release with Caution: Quick-release the pressure carefully, as starchy foods can cause splattering. Use a dish towel to shield your hand or allow the pot to release naturally for a minute or two before venting completely.

With these tips in mind, let’s get into some mouthwatering Instant Pot pasta recipes that you’ll want to add to your rotation.

Recipe 1: Instant Pot Classic Spaghetti with Meat Sauce

This recipe is a true comfort food classic, made easier with the Instant Pot. You’ll skip boiling water and end up with a single pot of perfectly cooked spaghetti and rich meat sauce.

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound ground beef or Italian sausage
  • 1 small onion, diced
  • 3 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • 1 jar (24 ounces) marinara sauce
  • 4 cups water or low-sodium chicken broth
  • 12 ounces spaghetti, broken in half
  • Grated Parmesan, for serving
  • Fresh parsley, for garnish (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Set the Instant Pot to sauté mode. Add ground beef or sausage and cook until browned, breaking it up with a spoon. Drain any excess fat if necessary.
  2. Stir in the diced onion, garlic, oregano, basil, salt, and pepper. Cook for 2–3 minutes until the onion softens and the mixture is fragrant.
  3. Press “Cancel” to turn off the sauté mode. Spread the meat mixture evenly across the bottom of the Instant Pot.
  4. Pour the marinara sauce over the meat, followed by the water or broth. Do not stir.
  5. Layer the broken spaghetti on top, ensuring it is evenly distributed. Lightly press it down with a spoon to ensure it’s submerged in the liquid.
  6. Seal the lid and set the pressure to high for 7 minutes. Once the cooking time is up, perform a quick release.
  7. Open the lid carefully and stir the pasta with the sauce. Let it sit for a minute or two if the sauce appears thin, as it will thicken slightly.
  8. Serve topped with grated Parmesan and fresh parsley.

Recipe 2: Creamy Chicken Alfredo Pasta

For fans of rich, creamy pasta, this Instant Pot chicken Alfredo is a dream come true. It’s a one-pot version of the decadent Italian-American classic, featuring tender chunks of chicken and a silky Parmesan sauce.

Ingredients:

  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 1 pound chicken breast, cut into thin strips
  • 3 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • 4 cups chicken broth
  • 8 ounces fettuccine, broken in half
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • Chopped parsley, for garnish

Instructions:

  1. On sauté mode, melt the butter in your Instant Pot. Add the chicken strips, garlic, garlic powder, salt, and pepper. Cook until the chicken is lightly browned, but not fully cooked, about 3–4 minutes. Press “Cancel.”
  2. Pour in the chicken broth, followed by the fettuccine. Spread the pasta over the broth evenly, pressing it down to submerge. Do not stir.
  3. Secure the lid and set the pressure to high for 6 minutes. Perform a quick release when done.
  4. Stir in the heavy cream and Parmesan cheese until the sauce is smooth and creamy. Let the pasta sit for a minute or two to thicken slightly.
  5. Serve warm, garnished with parsley if desired.

Recipe 3: Instant Pot Vegetarian Pesto Pasta

This vibrant and fresh pesto pasta comes together quickly in the Instant Pot and skips the hassle of boiling water or separately steaming vegetables.

Ingredients:

  • 3 cups uncooked penne pasta
  • 4 cups vegetable broth
  • 1 medium zucchini, diced
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • ½ cup store-bought or homemade basil pesto
  • ¼ cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • Salt and pepper, to taste

Instructions:

  1. Add the penne pasta and vegetable broth into the Instant Pot. Layer the diced zucchini and cherry tomatoes over the top. Do not stir.
  2. Secure the lid and cook on high pressure for 6 minutes. Perform a quick release.
  3. Stir the pesto and Parmesan cheese into the cooked pasta until evenly coated. Taste and adjust seasoning with salt and pepper.
  4. Serve immediately, with an extra sprinkle of cheese if desired.

Wrapping Up Your Instant Pot Pasta Adventure

These recipes showcase just how versatile and efficient the Instant Pot can be for cooking pasta dishes, from creamy and indulgent sauces to lighter, veggie-packed options. Not only are these meals simple to prepare, but they also allow you to explore endless variations by switching up the proteins, vegetables, and sauces to match your cravings or dietary needs.

Cooking pasta in the Instant Pot is a lifesaver for anyone with a busy schedule, making it easier to enjoy comforting, homemade meals with minimal effort. Whether it’s a classic spaghetti night or a crowd-pleasing Alfredo, these recipes are sure to delight your family and friends.

So, fire up your Instant Pot and get ready to enjoy quick, satisfying pasta meals that don’t compromise on flavor!
