Wraps for a Quick Dinner: Easy Recipes to Try


If you’re ever in a rush and looking for a quick, nutritious, and delicious dinner idea, wraps are the perfect solution. Versatile, easy to prepare, and full of flavor, wraps can be filled with anything from fresh vegetables to hearty proteins, offering endless possibilities to suit your taste. Whether you’re cooking for yourself, your family, or a group of friends, wraps are an ideal dinner choice that can be customized to everyone’s preferences.

In this post, we’ll explore some easy and tasty wrap recipes you can whip up in no time, perfect for a quick dinner. From vegetarian options to meat lovers’ creations, you’re bound to find a recipe that suits your needs.

Why Choose Wraps for Dinner?

Before we dive into the recipes, let’s take a moment to appreciate why wraps are such a fantastic dinner option. Here’s why you should consider wraps for your next meal:

  • Quick and Easy: Wraps are simple to prepare, often requiring no cooking or minimal prep time.
  • Customizable: The fillings are endlessly adaptable based on what you have in your fridge. From fresh veggies to grilled chicken, wraps can be made with virtually any ingredient.
  • Healthy: With the right ingredients, wraps can be packed with nutrients, providing protein, fiber, and essential vitamins.
  • Portable: Wraps are easy to eat on the go, making them a great choice for a quick dinner even if you’re busy.
  • Minimal Cleanup: Compared to other dinner options that require several pots and pans, wraps usually only need a cutting board, knife, and a bowl for mixing.

Now that we know why wraps are a fantastic dinner choice, let’s get into some delicious wrap ideas that you can try at home.

1. Classic Chicken Caesar Wrap

Ingredients:

  • 2 chicken breasts (grilled or roasted)
  • 1 large tortilla wrap (flour or whole wheat)
  • 1 cup romaine lettuce (chopped)
  • 1/4 cup Caesar dressing
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/4 cup croutons (optional for added crunch)

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the Chicken: If you haven’t already, cook the chicken breasts. You can either grill or roast them. Once cooked, slice the chicken into thin strips.
  2. Assemble the Wrap: Lay the tortilla on a flat surface and spread a tablespoon of Caesar dressing in the center.
  3. Add the Fillings: Add the chopped romaine lettuce, grilled chicken, grated Parmesan, and croutons.
  4. Wrap it Up: Fold in the sides of the tortilla and roll it tightly into a wrap.
  5. Serve: Slice the wrap in half for easy handling, and enjoy your Classic Chicken Caesar Wrap!

This wrap is perfect for a quick dinner. The creamy Caesar dressing pairs perfectly with the grilled chicken and crispy lettuce, giving you a refreshing yet filling meal.

2. Veggie Delight Wrap

Ingredients:

  • 1 large tortilla wrap
  • 1/2 cup hummus (or any preferred spread)
  • 1/2 cup spinach leaves
  • 1/4 cup shredded carrots
  • 1/4 cup cucumber (thinly sliced)
  • 1/4 cup red bell pepper (sliced)
  • 1/4 cup avocado (sliced)
  • 1 tablespoon feta cheese (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Spread the Hummus: Lay the tortilla flat on a clean surface and spread the hummus evenly across the middle of the tortilla.
  2. Add the Veggies: Layer the spinach leaves, shredded carrots, cucumber slices, red bell pepper slices, and avocado on top of the hummus.
  3. Add Feta (Optional): If you’re using feta cheese, sprinkle it over the veggies for added flavor.
  4. Wrap it Up: Fold the sides in and roll the tortilla tightly around the fillings.
  5. Serve: Slice the wrap into halves and serve as a light and healthy dinner option.

This Veggie Delight Wrap is packed with fresh vegetables and healthy fats from the avocado, making it a nutritious and satisfying choice for a quick dinner.

3. BBQ Chicken Wrap

Ingredients:

  • 2 chicken breasts (grilled and shredded)
  • 1/4 cup BBQ sauce
  • 1 large tortilla wrap
  • 1/2 cup coleslaw mix (store-bought or homemade)
  • 1/4 cup cheddar cheese (shredded)
  • Fresh cilantro (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Cook and Shred the Chicken: Grill or roast the chicken breasts until fully cooked. Once cooked, shred the chicken with a fork.
  2. Mix with BBQ Sauce: Toss the shredded chicken in BBQ sauce until well-coated.
  3. Prepare the Wrap: Lay the tortilla on a flat surface. In the center, add a generous portion of the BBQ chicken.
  4. Top it Off: Add the coleslaw mix and shredded cheddar cheese on top of the BBQ chicken.
  5. Wrap it Up: Fold the sides in and roll the tortilla to encase the filling.
  6. Serve: Garnish with fresh cilantro (optional) for an extra burst of flavor and serve.

This BBQ Chicken Wrap is a tangy and smoky dinner that’s quick and delicious. The creamy coleslaw balances the bold BBQ flavor, making each bite irresistible.

4. Steak and Avocado Wrap

Ingredients:

  • 1 steak (flank steak or sirloin)
  • 1 large tortilla wrap
  • 1/4 avocado (sliced)
  • 1/4 cup salsa
  • 1/4 cup sour cream (optional)
  • 1/4 cup shredded lettuce
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil

Instructions:

  1. Cook the Steak: Season the steak with salt and pepper, and cook it to your preferred level of doneness. Once cooked, allow it to rest for a few minutes before slicing it thinly.
  2. Prepare the Wrap: Lay the tortilla on a clean surface. Spread the salsa in the center.
  3. Assemble the Wrap: Add the sliced steak, avocado slices, and shredded lettuce. If you like, top with sour cream for added richness.
  4. Wrap it Up: Fold the sides of the tortilla and roll it tightly.
  5. Serve: Slice the wrap in half and enjoy a delicious, hearty dinner.

This Steak and Avocado Wrap is a filling meal with tender steak, creamy avocado, and the freshness of salsa and lettuce.

5. Mediterranean Falafel Wrap

Ingredients:

  • 3-4 falafel balls (store-bought or homemade)
  • 1 large tortilla wrap
  • 1/2 cup tzatziki sauce
  • 1/4 cup cucumber (sliced)
  • 1/4 cup tomato (diced)
  • 1/4 cup red onion (sliced)
  • Fresh parsley (for garnish)

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the Falafel: If you’re using store-bought falafel, cook them according to the package instructions. If you’re making them from scratch, prepare your falafel mix and cook the balls until golden and crispy.
  2. Spread Tzatziki: Lay the tortilla flat and spread tzatziki sauce in the center.
  3. Add the Fillings: Layer the cooked falafel, cucumber slices, diced tomato, and red onion on top of the tzatziki sauce.
  4. Wrap it Up: Fold in the sides of the tortilla and roll it tightly.
  5. Serve: Garnish with fresh parsley and serve this Mediterranean-inspired wrap for a refreshing, vegetarian dinner.

This Mediterranean Falafel Wrap is full of fresh flavors, with creamy tzatziki, crispy falafel, and crisp vegetables—an ideal quick dinner option that’s also plant-based.

6. Buffalo Chicken Wrap

Ingredients:

  • 2 chicken breasts (grilled or shredded)
  • 1/4 cup buffalo sauce
  • 1/4 cup ranch dressing
  • 1 large tortilla wrap
  • 1/4 cup shredded lettuce
  • 1/4 cup diced celery
  • 1/4 cup shredded mozzarella cheese

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the Chicken: Grill or roast the chicken breasts and shred them once cooked.
  2. Mix with Buffalo Sauce: Toss the shredded chicken in buffalo sauce until fully coated.
  3. Prepare the Wrap: Lay the tortilla flat and drizzle ranch dressing in the center.
  4. Assemble the Wrap: Add the buffalo chicken, shredded lettuce, diced celery, and mozzarella cheese.
  5. Wrap it Up: Fold the sides in and roll tightly to encase the filling.
  6. Serve: Slice and enjoy the bold flavors of buffalo sauce paired with creamy ranch.

The spicy buffalo chicken and creamy ranch make this wrap a fantastic choice for a quick and satisfying dinner.

Conclusion

Wraps are the perfect go-to for a quick and easy dinner that doesn’t compromise on flavor. Whether you’re craving something fresh, savory, or spicy, these wrap recipes are versatile enough to satisfy every craving. The best part? You can customize your wraps with whatever ingredients you have on hand, making them ideal for using up leftovers or experimenting with new flavors.

With these easy wrap recipes, you can bring variety to your dinner table without spending hours in the kitchen. So, next time you need a quick dinner solution, grab a tortilla and start wrapping up something delicious!
