Savory Winter Pies with a Vegetarian Twist


Winter is the perfect time to indulge in comforting, hearty meals, and what better way to warm up than with a delicious savory pie? In this blog post, we’re going to explore some mouthwatering vegetarian savory pie recipes that are perfect for the winter season. Whether you’re a vegetarian or simply looking to incorporate more plant-based meals into your diet, these recipes are sure to satisfy your taste buds and keep you cozy during the colder months.

Butternut Squash and Spinach Pie

Ingredients:

  • 1 butternut squash, peeled and cubed
  • 2 cups fresh spinach
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1/2 cup crumbled feta cheese
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 package of store-bought puff pastry

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. In a large skillet, sauté the onion and garlic until softened.
  3. Add the butternut squash cubes and cook until tender.
  4. Stir in the fresh spinach and cook until wilted.
  5. Season the mixture with thyme, salt, and pepper.
  6. Remove the skillet from the heat and let the mixture cool slightly.
  7. Roll out the puff pastry and line a pie dish with it.
  8. Spoon the butternut squash and spinach mixture into the pastry-lined dish.
  9. Sprinkle the crumbled feta and Parmesan cheese over the top.
  10. Cover the pie with another sheet of puff pastry, crimp the edges to seal, and make a few slits in the top crust to allow steam to escape.
  11. Bake for 35-40 minutes, or until the pastry is golden brown.

This Butternut Squash and Spinach Pie is a delightful combination of sweet and savory flavors, making it a perfect addition to your winter menu.

Lentil and Mushroom Pie

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup green or brown lentils
  • 2 cups vegetable broth
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 8 ounces mushrooms, sliced
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 sheet of store-bought shortcrust pastry

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. In a saucepan, combine the lentils and vegetable broth. Bring to a boil, then reduce the heat and simmer for 20-25 minutes, or until the lentils are tender.
  3. In a separate skillet, heat the olive oil and sauté the onion and garlic until softened.
  4. Add the sliced mushrooms and cook until they release their moisture.
  5. Stir in the cooked lentils, thyme, parsley, salt, and pepper.
  6. Roll out the shortcrust pastry and line a pie dish with it.
  7. Spoon the lentil and mushroom mixture into the pastry-lined dish.
  8. Cover the pie with another sheet of pastry, crimp the edges to seal, and make a few slits in the top crust.
  9. Bake for 30-35 minutes, or until the pastry is golden brown.

This hearty Lentil and Mushroom Pie is a great way to enjoy the earthy flavors of winter in a comforting pie form.

Conclusion

These vegetarian savory pies are not only delicious and satisfying, but they also offer a creative twist on traditional winter comfort food. Whether you’re hosting a dinner party or simply craving a cozy night in, these recipes are guaranteed to be a hit. Feel free to experiment with different vegetable and cheese combinations to suit your taste. Embrace the warmth and comfort of winter with these savory vegetarian pies, and let your creativity in the kitchen shine!

So there you have it – two delicious savory pies with a vegetarian twist to add some warmth and flavor to your winter evenings. Enjoy!
