Easter Quiche Recipes: Savory Pies for a Crowd


The Easter season is a time for family gatherings and delicious meals. If you’re looking for a dish that is both impressive and easy to make, look no further than Easter quiche recipes. These savory pies are not only visually appealing but also bursting with flavors that will leave your guests asking for seconds. In this blog post, we’ll explore three mouthwatering Easter quiche recipes that are perfect for feeding a crowd.

1. Spinach and Feta Quiche

Ingredients:

  • 1 pre-made pie crust
  • 1 cup fresh spinach leaves, chopped
  • 1/2 cup crumbled feta cheese
  • 1/4 cup diced red onion
  • 4 large eggs
  • 1 cup milk
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Press the pre-made pie crust into a 9-inch pie dish.
  3. In a medium-sized bowl, whisk together the eggs and milk until well combined. Season with salt and pepper.
  4. Spread the chopped spinach, crumbled feta cheese, and diced red onion evenly over the pie crust.
  5. Pour the egg mixture over the ingredients in the pie crust, ensuring they are fully covered.
  6. Bake for 35-40 minutes, or until the quiche is set and the crust is golden brown.
  7. Allow the quiche to cool for a few minutes before slicing and serving.

2. Ham and Cheese Quiche

Ingredients:

  • 1 pre-made pie crust
  • 1 cup diced ham
  • 1 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1/4 cup diced green bell pepper
  • 4 large eggs
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Press the pre-made pie crust into a 9-inch pie dish.
  3. In a medium-sized bowl, whisk together the eggs and heavy cream until well combined. Season with salt and pepper.
  4. Spread the diced ham, shredded cheddar cheese, and diced green bell pepper evenly over the pie crust.
  5. Pour the egg mixture over the ingredients in the pie crust, ensuring they are fully covered.
  6. Bake for 35-40 minutes, or until the quiche is set and the crust is golden brown.
  7. Allow the quiche to cool for a few minutes before slicing and serving.

3. Mushroom and Swiss Quiche

Ingredients:

  • 1 pre-made pie crust
  • 1 cup sliced mushrooms
  • 1 cup shredded Swiss cheese
  • 1/4 cup diced shallots
  • 4 large eggs
  • 1 cup half-and-half
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Press the pre-made pie crust into a 9-inch pie dish.
  3. In a medium-sized bowl, whisk together the eggs and half-and-half until well combined. Season with salt and pepper.
  4. Spread the sliced mushrooms, shredded Swiss cheese, and diced shallots evenly over the pie crust.
  5. Pour the egg mixture over the ingredients in the pie crust, ensuring they are fully covered.
  6. Bake for 35-40 minutes, or until the quiche is set and the crust is golden brown.
  7. Allow the quiche to cool for a few minutes before slicing and serving.

Conclusion

These Easter quiche recipes are versatile, delicious, and perfect for feeding a crowd. Whether you choose the spinach and feta, ham and cheese, or mushroom and Swiss variation, you’re sure to impress your guests with these savory pies. So, this Easter, why not add some quiche to your menu and make your celebration even more memorable? Enjoy!
