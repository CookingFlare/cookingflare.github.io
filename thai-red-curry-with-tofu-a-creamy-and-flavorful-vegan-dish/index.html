Thai Red Curry with Tofu: A Creamy and Flavorful Vegan Dish


If you’re looking for a delicious and satisfying vegan dish that’s packed with flavor, then look no further than this Thai Red Curry with Tofu recipe. This creamy and aromatic dish is a perfect balance of spicy, sweet, and savory flavors, and it’s incredibly easy to make. Whether you’re a seasoned Thai food enthusiast or just dipping your toes into the world of vegan cooking, this recipe is sure to impress your taste buds and leave you craving more.

Ingredients

To start with, you’ll need the following ingredients:

  • 14 oz firm tofu, drained and cubed
  • 2 tbsp red curry paste
  • 1 can (14 oz) coconut milk
  • 1 cup vegetable broth
  • 1 red bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 yellow bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 small onion, thinly sliced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tbsp fresh ginger, grated
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp maple syrup
  • 1 tbsp lime juice
  • 1 cup broccoli florets
  • 1 cup snap peas
  • Fresh cilantro for garnish
  • Cooked jasmine rice for serving

Instructions

Step 1: Prepare the Tofu

Start by draining the tofu and cutting it into cubes. Then, heat a large non-stick skillet over medium heat and add the tofu cubes. Cook for 5-7 minutes, turning occasionally, until the tofu is golden and slightly crispy. Once done, set the tofu aside.

Step 2: Make the Curry

In the same skillet, add the red curry paste and cook for about a minute to release its flavors. Then, pour in the coconut milk and vegetable broth, stirring to combine. Add the sliced bell peppers, onion, garlic, and ginger to the skillet. Bring the mixture to a simmer and let it cook for 10-15 minutes, until the vegetables are tender.

Step 3: Add the Flavors

Next, stir in the soy sauce, maple syrup, and lime juice. Adjust the seasoning to your taste by adding more soy sauce or maple syrup if desired. Then, add the broccoli florets and snap peas to the skillet, allowing them to cook for an additional 3-5 minutes until they are tender-crisp.

Step 4: Combine and Serve

Finally, add the cooked tofu back into the skillet, gently stirring to combine it with the curry. Let the curry simmer for a few more minutes to allow the flavors to meld together. Once ready, remove the skillet from the heat.

Step 5: Serve and Enjoy

Scoop the creamy and aromatic Thai red curry with tofu over a bed of jasmine rice. Garnish with fresh cilantro and a wedge of lime for an extra pop of flavor. Then, serve it hot and savor every mouthful of this delightful vegan dish.

Conclusion

This Thai Red Curry with Tofu recipe is a fantastic way to introduce yourself to the vibrant and exotic flavors of Thai cuisine, all while enjoying a satisfying vegan meal. The creamy coconut milk, the bold red curry paste, and the tender tofu come together to create a dish that’s both comforting and exciting. Whether you’re cooking for yourself or entertaining guests, this recipe is sure to be a hit. Plus, it’s a great way to add more plant-based meals to your repertoire without sacrificing flavor or satisfaction. So, go ahead, give this recipe a try, and let your taste buds revel in the delightful flavors of this creamy and flavorful vegan Thai red curry with tofu.
