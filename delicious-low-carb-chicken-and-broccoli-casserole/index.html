Delicious Low Carb Chicken and Broccoli Casserole


Are you looking for a delicious low carb meal that the whole family will love? Look no further than this mouthwatering Chicken and Broccoli Casserole recipe. Packed with flavor and wholesome ingredients, this dish is a perfect addition to your weekly meal rotation. Plus, it’s easy to make and can be prepared ahead of time, making it a convenient option for busy weeknights. Let’s dive into the recipe and discover how to make this delightful casserole!

Ingredients

  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 4 cups fresh broccoli florets
  • 1 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1 cup sour cream
  • 1/2 cup mayonnaise
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon onion powder
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven: Start by preheating your oven to 375°F (190°C). Grease a 9x13 inch baking dish and set it aside.

  2. Prepare the Chicken: Season the chicken breasts with salt, pepper, and paprika. In a large skillet over medium-high heat, cook the chicken until it’s no longer pink in the center, about 6-7 minutes per side. Once cooked, chop the chicken into bite-sized pieces.

  3. Steam the Broccoli: In a pot fitted with a steamer basket, bring water to a boil. Add the broccoli florets to the basket and steam for 4-5 minutes, or until they are bright green and slightly tender. Remove from the heat and set aside.

  4. Make the Sauce: In a mixing bowl, combine the sour cream, mayonnaise, minced garlic, onion powder, and grated Parmesan cheese. Mix until well combined.

  5. Assemble the Casserole: Place the steamed broccoli in the greased baking dish. Top with the cooked chicken pieces. Pour the sauce over the chicken and broccoli, ensuring that everything is evenly coated. Sprinkle the shredded cheddar cheese over the top.

  6. Bake: Cover the baking dish with foil and bake in the preheated oven for 25 minutes. Then, remove the foil and bake for an additional 10-15 minutes, or until the cheese is golden and bubbly.

  7. Serve: Once the casserole is done, let it rest for a few minutes before serving. This will allow the flavors to meld together for an even more delicious experience.

Why You’ll Love This Casserole

This Low Carb Chicken and Broccoli Casserole is a winner for so many reasons. Not only is it incredibly flavorful, but it’s also a great way to incorporate more vegetables into your diet. The combination of tender chicken, vibrant broccoli, and creamy, cheesy sauce is simply irresistible. Additionally, this recipe is low in carbohydrates, making it a fantastic option for those looking to reduce their carb intake without sacrificing taste.

Make It Your Own

Feel free to customize this recipe to suit your taste preferences. You can add extra vegetables such as bell peppers or mushrooms for more variety. If you’re a fan of spice, consider sprinkling some red pepper flakes into the sauce for a kick of heat. For a crunchy topping, mix almond flour with a bit of melted butter and sprinkle it over the casserole before baking.

In Conclusion

This Delicious Low Carb Chicken and Broccoli Casserole is a delightful and wholesome dish that’s perfect for any occasion. Whether you’re cooking for your family or hosting a dinner party, this recipe is sure to impress. With its simple preparation and incredible flavor, it’s bound to become a favorite in your recipe collection. Give it a try and savor the goodness of this comforting casserole!
