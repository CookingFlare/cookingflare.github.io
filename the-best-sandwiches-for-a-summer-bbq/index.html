The Best Sandwiches for a Summer BBQ


Summer BBQs are all about bringing people together over good food, laughter, and sunshine. While burgers and hot dogs often take center stage at these gatherings, the humble sandwich deserves a moment in the spotlight. This versatile, hand-held meal is perfect for outdoor get-togethers, offering endless options to suit everyone’s taste buds. From hearty and savory to light and refreshing, sandwiches can truly shine at your next cookout.

Here’s a guide to crafting the ultimate summer BBQ sandwich lineup. Whether you’re cooking for a crowd or enjoying a quiet evening outdoors, these sandwiches are guaranteed to please.

1. Grilled Chicken Caprese Sandwich

Nothing says summer quite like the flavors of a classic Caprese salad. Pairing these vibrant ingredients with grilled chicken creates a sandwich that’s both fresh and satisfying.

Ingredients:

  • Boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • Olive oil
  • Salt and freshly ground black pepper
  • Italian seasoning
  • Fresh mozzarella, sliced
  • Juicy, ripe tomatoes, sliced
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • Balsamic glaze
  • Ciabatta rolls or any crusty bread

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your grill to medium-high heat.
  2. Season the chicken breasts with olive oil, salt, pepper, and Italian seasoning. Grill for 6-8 minutes per side or until cooked through.
  3. Slice the ciabatta rolls in half and lightly toast on the grill.
  4. Assemble the sandwich by layering a grilled chicken breast, a few slices of fresh mozzarella, tomato slices, and fresh basil leaves.
  5. Drizzle with balsamic glaze and top with the other half of the roll. Serve immediately for a refreshing bite that screams summer.

2. BBQ Pulled Pork Sliders

Pulled pork sliders are a BBQ staple, and for good reason. They’re smoky, tender, and packed with flavor. These bite-sized sandwiches are perfect for feeding a crowd and are sure to be a hit.

Ingredients:

  • Pork shoulder (also known as pork butt)
  • Dry rub (paprika, brown sugar, garlic powder, cayenne, salt, and pepper)
  • BBQ sauce
  • Slider buns
  • Coleslaw (store-bought or homemade for added crunch)

Instructions:

  1. Massage the pork shoulder with the dry rub, ensuring it’s evenly coated. Slow-cook it for several hours or roast it at a low temperature in your oven until the meat is tender and shreddable.
  2. Once cooked, shred the pork with forks and toss with your favorite BBQ sauce.
  3. Lightly toast the slider buns on the grill.
  4. Build the sliders by piling the pulled pork onto the bottom bun, topping with a spoonful of coleslaw, and adding the top bun. Serve warm for maximum enjoyment.

3. Grilled Vegetable and Hummus Sandwich

For vegetarians or those craving a lighter option, this grilled vegetable and hummus sandwich is a fantastic choice. The smoky flavor of charred veggies paired with creamy hummus creates an irresistible combination.

Ingredients:

  • Zucchini, sliced lengthwise
  • Bell peppers, sliced into wide strips
  • Portobello mushrooms, stems removed
  • Olive oil
  • Salt and pepper
  • Hummus (traditional, roasted red pepper, or your favorite variety)
  • Arugula or baby spinach
  • Multigrain or sourdough bread

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the grill to medium heat. Drizzle the zucchini, bell peppers, and mushrooms with olive oil and season with salt and pepper. Grill until tender and slightly charred, about 5-7 minutes per side.
  2. Spread a generous layer of hummus on one side of each bread slice.
  3. Layer the warm grilled vegetables and fresh arugula or spinach on top of the hummus.
  4. Close the sandwich and enjoy warm or at room temperature. These are perfect for picnic-style BBQs!

4. Smoked Turkey and Avocado Club

A twist on the classic club sandwich, this version includes smoked turkey and creamy avocado for a sandwich that’s both flavorful and filling.

Ingredients:

  • Smoked turkey breast, thinly sliced
  • Crispy bacon
  • Ripe avocado, sliced
  • Lettuce (such as romaine or butter lettuce)
  • Tomato, thinly sliced
  • Mayonnaise
  • Toasted sandwich bread (whole wheat or white)

Instructions:

  1. Toast the bread until golden brown.
  2. Spread a layer of mayonnaise on one side of each slice.
  3. Assemble the sandwich by layering smoked turkey, bacon, avocado slices, lettuce, and tomato between two slices of bread.
  4. Secure with toothpicks if serving as halves or leave whole for a hearty bite.

5. Shrimp Po’ Boy with Spicy Remoulade

Transport your taste buds to the Cajun coast with this Shrimp Po’ Boy that’s packed with crispy shrimp and zesty remoulade sauce. This is a coastal-inspired summer BBQ favorite.

Ingredients:

  • Large shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • Buttermilk
  • Yellow cornmeal
  • All-purpose flour
  • Cajun seasoning
  • Oil for frying
  • French baguette or hoagie rolls
  • Lettuce or shredded cabbage
  • Tomato slices
  • Spicy remoulade sauce (Mayonnaise, Dijon mustard, hot sauce, lemon juice, paprika, garlic powder)

Instructions:

  1. Marinate the shrimp in buttermilk for 15-20 minutes.
  2. In a separate bowl, combine cornmeal, flour, and Cajun seasoning.
  3. Heat oil in a deep pan over medium-high heat. Dredge the shrimp in the cornmeal mixture and fry until golden brown, about 2-3 minutes per side. Drain on paper towels.
  4. Lightly toast the baguette or hoagie rolls. Spread the remoulade sauce onto the bread, then layer the fried shrimp, lettuce or cabbage, and tomato slices.
  5. Serve warm and get ready to wow your guests.

6. Steak and Chimichurri Sandwich

For the steak lovers in the crowd, this sandwich is pure indulgence. The vibrant chimichurri sauce brightens the grilled beef for an incredible flavor combination.

Ingredients:

  • Flank steak or skirt steak
  • Olive oil
  • Salt and pepper
  • Chimichurri sauce (parsley, cilantro, garlic, red wine vinegar, olive oil, red pepper flakes)
  • Artisan rolls or baguette

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the grill to high heat. Rub the steak with olive oil, salt, and pepper. Grill for 3-5 minutes per side for medium-rare, then let rest before slicing thinly.
  2. Slice the rolls or baguette and toast lightly on the grill.
  3. Spread a generous amount of chimichurri sauce on the bread, then layer the steak slices on top.
  4. Serve as-is or with an additional drizzle of chimichurri for extra flavor.

Tips for Sandwich Success at Your Summer BBQ

  • Fresh Bread is Key: Whether you prefer crusty baguettes, pillowy brioche, or hearty multigrain, fresh bread makes a huge difference.
  • Prep Ahead: Save time by marinating meats, preparing sauces, or chopping vegetables in advance.
  • Add a Crispy Element: Consider adding toasted nuts, pickles, or crispy lettuce for added texture.
  • Offer Variety: To cater to all tastes and dietary preferences, provide a mix of meat, vegetarian, and seafood options.
  • Serve Condiments on the Side: Let guests customize their sandwiches with condiments like mustard, mayo, or hot sauce.

These sandwich recipes bring the best of flavor, convenience, and creativity, making them a perfect fit for any backyard barbecue. Mix and match a few of these to appeal to your guests, and you’ll have a diverse spread that keeps everyone happy. Whether it’s smoky pulled pork, grilled veggies, or indulgent steak, these sandwiches are the ultimate complement to a day of sunshine and good company.
