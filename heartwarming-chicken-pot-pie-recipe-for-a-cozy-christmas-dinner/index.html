Heartwarming Chicken Pot Pie Recipe for a Cozy Christmas Dinner


The holiday season is a time for gathering with loved ones and savoring heartwarming meals that bring comfort and joy. This Christmas, treat your family and friends to a delightful Chicken Pot Pie that embodies the spirit of the season. This classic dish is a perfect blend of flaky pastry, savory chicken, and wholesome vegetables, making it an ideal centerpiece for a cozy Christmas dinner.

Ingredients

For the Crust:

  • 2 1/2 cups of all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup (2 sticks) of unsalted butter, chilled and cubed
  • 1 teaspoon of salt
  • 6-8 tablespoons of ice water

For the Filling:

  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts, cubed
  • 1 cup of carrots, diced
  • 1 cup of frozen peas
  • 1/2 cup of celery, diced
  • 1/3 cup of unsalted butter
  • 1/3 cup of all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon of salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon of black pepper
  • 1/4 teaspoon of celery seed
  • 1 3/4 cups of chicken broth
  • 2/3 cup of milk

Instructions

Making the Crust:

  1. In a large bowl, combine the flour and salt. Cut in the butter until the mixture resembles coarse crumbs.
  2. Gradually add the ice water, tossing with a fork until the dough holds together.
  3. Divide the dough in half, shape into two discs, wrap in plastic wrap, and refrigerate for at least 1 hour.

Preparing the Filling:

  1. Preheat the oven to 425°F (220°C).
  2. In a saucepan, combine the chicken, carrots, peas, and celery. Add water to cover and boil for 15 minutes.
  3. Remove from heat, drain, and set aside.

Making the Sauce:

  1. In a separate saucepan, cook the butter over medium heat until melted.
  2. Stir in the flour, salt, pepper, and celery seed until well blended.
  3. Gradually stir in the chicken broth and milk. Simmer over medium-low heat until the mixture thickens.

Assembling the Pie:

  1. Roll out one of the pastry discs to fit a 9-inch pie plate. Place in the bottom of the dish.
  2. Pour the chicken mixture over the pastry.
  3. Pour the hot liquid mixture over the chicken.
  4. Roll out the remaining pastry and place over the filling. Seal the edges and cut slits in the top crust.

Baking the Pie:

  1. Bake for 30 to 35 minutes, or until the pastry is golden brown and the filling is bubbly.
  2. Cool for 10 minutes before serving.

Serving Suggestion

This heartwarming Chicken Pot Pie pairs beautifully with a side of buttery mashed potatoes and a crisp, green salad. For a festive touch, garnish with a sprinkle of fresh parsley before serving. As you cut into the golden crust and reveal the steaming, flavorful filling, you’ll witness the delight on your loved ones’ faces, making this dish the perfect addition to your cozy Christmas dinner.

Conclusion

With its rich flavors and comforting aroma, this Chicken Pot Pie recipe is sure to become a beloved tradition at your holiday gatherings. Whether you’re hosting a small family dinner or a festive gathering with friends, this dish is a delightful way to spread Christmas cheer and create lasting memories. Embrace the joy of the season by sharing this heartwarming meal with those you hold dear, and let the spirit of Christmas truly come alive around your dinner table.
