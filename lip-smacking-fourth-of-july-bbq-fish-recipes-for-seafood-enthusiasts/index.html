Lip-Smacking Fourth of July BBQ Fish Recipes for Seafood Enthusiasts


The Fourth of July is just around the corner, and what better way to celebrate than with a sizzling BBQ featuring some mouthwatering fish recipes? Whether you’re a seafood enthusiast or just looking to add some variety to your holiday menu, these lip-smacking Fourth of July BBQ fish recipes are sure to impress your guests and leave them craving for more. From zesty grilled salmon to succulent shrimp skewers, these recipes will take your Fourth of July celebrations to the next level.

Grilled Citrus Salmon

Ingredients:

  • 4 salmon fillets
  • 1 lemon, juiced
  • 1 lime, juiced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley for garnish

Instructions:

  1. In a small bowl, whisk together the lemon juice, lime juice, minced garlic, and olive oil.
  2. Season the salmon fillets with salt and pepper, then brush them with the citrus marinade.
  3. Preheat the grill to medium-high heat and lightly oil the grates.
  4. Place the salmon fillets on the grill and cook for 4-5 minutes on each side, or until the fish flakes easily with a fork.
  5. Remove the salmon from the grill and garnish with fresh parsley before serving.

Spicy Grilled Shrimp Skewers

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound large shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon cayenne pepper
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Wooden skewers, soaked in water

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, combine the olive oil, minced garlic, paprika, cayenne pepper, salt, and pepper.
  2. Add the shrimp to the bowl and toss until they are evenly coated with the spicy marinade.
  3. Thread the shrimp onto the soaked wooden skewers.
  4. Preheat the grill to medium heat and lightly oil the grates.
  5. Grill the shrimp skewers for 2-3 minutes on each side, or until they are pink and opaque.
  6. Serve the spicy grilled shrimp skewers with a squeeze of fresh lemon juice.

Cedar-planked Halibut with Herb Butter

Ingredients:

  • 4 halibut fillets
  • 1 cedar plank, soaked in water for at least 1 hour
  • 1/4 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 2 tablespoons chopped fresh herbs (such as parsley, dill, and chives)
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a small bowl, combine the softened butter, chopped herbs, minced garlic, salt, and pepper.
  2. Season the halibut fillets with salt and pepper, then spread the herb butter over the top of each fillet.
  3. Preheat the grill to medium-high heat and place the soaked cedar plank on the grates.
  4. Arrange the halibut fillets on the cedar plank and grill for 12-15 minutes, or until the fish is cooked through.
  5. Carefully remove the cedar-planked halibut from the grill and serve hot.

Tangy BBQ Swordfish Steaks

Ingredients:

  • 4 swordfish steaks
  • 1/2 cup BBQ sauce
  • 2 tablespoons honey
  • 2 tablespoons apple cider vinegar
  • 1 teaspoon Dijon mustard
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a small bowl, whisk together the BBQ sauce, honey, apple cider vinegar, Dijon mustard, salt, and pepper.
  2. Brush the swordfish steaks with the tangy BBQ marinade and let them sit for 15-20 minutes.
  3. Preheat the grill to medium heat and lightly oil the grates.
  4. Grill the swordfish steaks for 4-5 minutes on each side, or until they are charred on the outside and cooked to your desired doneness.
  5. Serve the tangy BBQ swordfish steaks with a drizzle of extra BBQ sauce.

Conclusion

This Fourth of July, treat your friends and family to a BBQ feast they won’t soon forget with these lip-smacking fish recipes. From the smoky flavors of cedar-planked halibut to the zesty kick of grilled citrus salmon, there’s something for every seafood enthusiast to enjoy. So fire up the grill, grab your favorite fish, and get ready to impress your guests with these delectable Fourth of July BBQ dishes. Happy grilling!
