Quick and Easy Mediterranean Recipes


When it comes to cooking, Mediterranean cuisine offers a delightful array of flavors, colors, and textures. Whether you’re a busy professional, a parent juggling multiple responsibilities, or simply someone who loves good food, quick and easy Mediterranean recipes can be a lifesaver. From fresh salads to savory seafood dishes and hearty grain-based meals, this collection of recipes embodies the essence of the Mediterranean diet while being convenient for your busy lifestyle.

1. Greek Salad with Lemon Oregano Dressing

Ingredients:

  • 2 large tomatoes, diced
  • 1 cucumber, sliced
  • 1 red onion, thinly sliced
  • 1 green bell pepper, chopped
  • 200g feta cheese, crumbled
  • 1/4 cup Kalamata olives
  • 3 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons fresh lemon juice
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a large bowl, combine the tomatoes, cucumber, red onion, and bell pepper.
  2. Add the crumbled feta cheese and Kalamata olives to the bowl.
  3. In a small bowl, whisk together the olive oil, lemon juice, and dried oregano to make the dressing.
  4. Drizzle the dressing over the salad and toss gently to coat.
  5. Season with salt and pepper to taste before serving.

2. Grilled Lemon Garlic Shrimp

Ingredients:

  • 500g large shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • Zest of 1 lemon
  • 2 tablespoons fresh lemon juice
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley for garnish

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, combine the shrimp, minced garlic, lemon zest, lemon juice, olive oil, and oregano. Toss to coat the shrimp evenly.
  2. Season the shrimp with salt and pepper.
  3. Preheat a grill or grill pan over medium-high heat.
  4. Thread the shrimp onto skewers and grill for 2-3 minutes on each side until they turn pink and opaque.
  5. Garnish with fresh parsley before serving.

3. One-Pan Mediterranean Chicken and Couscous

Ingredients:

  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1 cup uncooked couscous
  • 1 1/2 cups chicken broth
  • 1 can (400g) diced tomatoes
  • 1 red bell pepper, chopped
  • 1 yellow bell pepper, chopped
  • 1 red onion, chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Season the chicken breasts with salt, pepper, and dried oregano.
  2. In a large ovenproof skillet, heat olive oil over medium-high heat and brown the chicken on both sides. Remove the chicken from the skillet and set aside.
  3. In the same skillet, sauté the red onion, bell peppers, and garlic until softened.
  4. Add the diced tomatoes, couscous, and chicken broth to the skillet, stirring to combine.
  5. Return the chicken to the skillet, nestling it into the couscous mixture.
  6. Cover the skillet and simmer for 15-20 minutes until the chicken is cooked through and the couscous is tender.
  7. Garnish with fresh parsley before serving.

Conclusion

These quick and easy Mediterranean recipes are perfect for those hectic days when you crave a delicious, healthy meal without spending hours in the kitchen. By embracing the vibrant flavors and simple preparation methods of Mediterranean cuisine, you can elevate your culinary repertoire while maintaining a convenient approach to cooking. Whether it’s a refreshing Greek salad, succulent grilled shrimp, or a comforting one-pan chicken and couscous dish, these recipes are sure to become staples in your busy kitchen. Enjoy the flavors of the Mediterranean effortlessly and savor every bite!
