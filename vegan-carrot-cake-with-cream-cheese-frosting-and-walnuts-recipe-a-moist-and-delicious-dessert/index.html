Vegan Carrot Cake with Cream Cheese Frosting and Walnuts Recipe: A Moist and Delicious Dessert

3 min · 492 words

Carrot cake is a classic dessert loved by many, but what if you’re following a vegan lifestyle? Don’t worry, because I’ve got the perfect recipe for you! This Vegan Carrot Cake with Cream Cheese Frosting and Walnuts is not only moist and delicious, but it’s also completely plant-based. So let’s dive right in and learn how to make this delectable treat!

Ingredients

For the cake:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 1/2 cups granulated sugar
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 2 teaspoons ground cinnamon
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground nutmeg
  • 1/2 cup unsweetened applesauce
  • 1/2 cup vegetable oil
  • 1/2 cup almond milk (or any non-dairy milk of your choice)
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 2 cups grated carrots
  • 1 cup crushed pineapple, drained
  • 1/2 cup chopped walnuts

For the cream cheese frosting:

  • 1 (8 oz) package vegan cream cheese, softened
  • 1/4 cup vegan butter, softened
  • 3 cups powdered sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C) and grease a 9-inch round cake pan.

  2. In a large mixing bowl, whisk together the flour, sugar, baking powder, baking soda, salt, ground cinnamon, and ground nutmeg until well combined.

  3. Add the applesauce, vegetable oil, almond milk, and vanilla extract to the dry ingredients. Mix until everything is well incorporated.

  4. Fold in the grated carrots, crushed pineapple, and chopped walnuts. Make sure they are evenly distributed throughout the batter.

  5. Pour the batter into the prepared cake pan and smooth the top with a spatula.

  6. Bake in the preheated oven for 35-40 minutes or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.

  7. While the cake is baking, prepare the cream cheese frosting. In a separate mixing bowl, beat the vegan cream cheese and vegan butter together until creamy and smooth.

  8. Gradually add the powdered sugar, one cup at a time, and continue beating until well combined. Stir in the vanilla extract.

  9. Once the cake has completely cooled, spread the cream cheese frosting evenly over the top. For an extra touch, you can sprinkle some chopped walnuts on top.

  10. Slice and serve! Enjoy this moist and flavorful Vegan Carrot Cake with Cream Cheese Frosting and Walnuts with a cup of tea or coffee.

Tips and Variations

  • If you prefer a gluten-free version, you can substitute the all-purpose flour with a gluten-free flour blend.
  • Feel free to add some raisins or shredded coconut to the cake batter for added texture and flavor.
  • For a nut-free option, omit the walnuts or replace them with sunflower seeds or pumpkin seeds.
  • This cake can be stored in an airtight container in the refrigerator for up to 5 days.

So there you have it, a scrumptious Vegan Carrot Cake with Cream Cheese Frosting and Walnuts that will satisfy your cravings for a moist and delicious dessert. Whether you’re vegan or not, this cake is sure to impress your friends and family. Give it a try and let me know how it turns out!
