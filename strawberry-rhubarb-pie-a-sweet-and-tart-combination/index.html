Strawberry Rhubarb Pie: A Sweet and Tart Combination


When it comes to classic summer desserts, few treats can rival the timeless appeal of a homemade strawberry rhubarb pie. This delightful confection combines the sweet juiciness of fresh strawberries with the tartness of rhubarb, resulting in a flavor profile that is both refreshing and satisfying. Whether you’re a seasoned baker or a novice in the kitchen, this recipe is sure to become a favorite in your repertoire.

The Perfect Pie Crust

Before we delve into the luscious filling, let’s start with the foundation of any great pie: the crust. For this recipe, we’ll be making a traditional buttery pie crust that complements the fruity filling. Here’s what you’ll need:

Ingredients:

  • 2 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup unsalted butter, chilled and cubed
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon granulated sugar
  • 1/4 to 1/2 cup ice water

Instructions:

  1. In a large mixing bowl, combine the flour, salt, and sugar.
  2. Add the chilled butter cubes to the flour mixture and use a pastry cutter or your fingertips to work the butter into the flour until it resembles coarse crumbs.
  3. Gradually drizzle in the ice water, mixing gently with a fork, until the dough begins to come together.
  4. Divide the dough in half, shape each half into a disc, and wrap them in plastic wrap. Refrigerate for at least 1 hour before rolling out.

The Luscious Filling

Now, let’s turn our attention to the star of the show: the tantalizing strawberry rhubarb filling.

Ingredients:

  • 3 cups fresh rhubarb, chopped
  • 3 cups fresh strawberries, hulled and sliced
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/4 cup cornstarch
  • 1 teaspoon lemon zest
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1 egg, beaten (for egg wash)

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C).
  2. In a large bowl, gently toss together the rhubarb, strawberries, sugar, cornstarch, lemon zest, lemon juice, vanilla extract, and cinnamon until the fruit is evenly coated.
  3. Roll out one disc of the chilled pie crust and carefully transfer it to a 9-inch pie dish.
  4. Pour the strawberry rhubarb filling into the prepared pie crust, spreading it out into an even layer.
  5. Roll out the second disc of pie crust and place it over the filling. Trim any excess dough and crimp the edges to seal the pie.
  6. Brush the top crust with the beaten egg to create a golden sheen when baked.
  7. Use a sharp knife to make a few small slits in the top crust to allow steam to escape during baking.

Baking and Serving

Once the pie is assembled, it’s time to bake it to golden perfection.

Instructions:

  1. Place the pie on a baking sheet to catch any drips, then transfer it to the preheated oven.
  2. Bake for 20 minutes, then reduce the oven temperature to 350°F (175°C) and continue baking for an additional 35-40 minutes, or until the crust is golden and the filling is bubbling.
  3. Remove the pie from the oven and allow it to cool for at least 2 hours before slicing and serving.

A Slice of Summer Bliss

When you finally slice into the strawberry rhubarb pie, you’ll be greeted by a sight and aroma that captures the essence of summer. The vibrant red filling, encased in flaky, golden crust, is a testament to the beauty of seasonal produce and the joy of homemade baking. Each bite offers a delightful contrast of flavors, as the sweetness of the strawberries mingles with the tangy bite of rhubarb, creating a symphony of taste that is nothing short of irresistible.

Whether you enjoy it on its own, à la mode with a scoop of vanilla ice cream, or accompanied by a dollop of freshly whipped cream, this strawberry rhubarb pie is a true celebration of the season. So, gather your ripe strawberries and vibrant rhubarb, roll up your sleeves, and embark on a baking adventure that will yield a dessert worthy of any occasion.

In conclusion, the strawberry rhubarb pie is a delightful marriage of sweet and tart, encapsulated in a flaky, buttery crust. Its timeless appeal and irresistible flavors make it a standout dessert that is sure to leave a lasting impression on anyone who has the pleasure of savoring a slice. So, embrace the abundance of summer’s bounty and treat yourself to the incomparable joy of baking and indulging in this classic pie.
