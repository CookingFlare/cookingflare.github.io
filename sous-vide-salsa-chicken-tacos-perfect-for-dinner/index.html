Sous Vide Salsa Chicken Tacos: Perfect for Dinner


If you’re looking for a delicious, easy-to-make dinner that is both flavorful and impressive, look no further than Sous Vide Salsa Chicken Tacos. This recipe takes a classic taco to the next level with juicy, tender chicken cooked to perfection using the sous vide method, paired with a fresh and vibrant salsa for a flavor-packed meal. Whether you’re feeding your family, hosting a dinner party, or meal prepping for the week, these tacos are sure to become a staple in your cooking routine.

What is Sous Vide Cooking?

Sous vide, which means “under vacuum” in French, is a method of cooking food slowly in a water bath at a precisely controlled temperature. This technique results in perfectly cooked dishes with incredible flavor and texture. By cooking the chicken at a low temperature for an extended period, it retains its moisture and becomes incredibly tender—creating a taco filling that is juicy and bursting with flavor.

Why Sous Vide Salsa Chicken Tacos?

The combination of sous vide chicken and fresh salsa brings a delightful contrast of textures and tastes. The chicken itself is cooked in its juices, ensuring it stays moist and tender, while the salsa adds a fresh, zesty kick that complements the richness of the chicken perfectly. Here are some reasons why sous vide salsa chicken tacos are a fantastic choice for dinner:

  • Tender, juicy chicken: The sous vide method ensures the chicken remains moist and tender, preventing it from drying out as it often does when cooked using traditional methods.
  • Quick prep, minimal effort: Once the chicken is in the sous vide bath, it requires little hands-on attention, making it perfect for busy weeknights.
  • Flavorful salsa: The vibrant salsa enhances the dish with fresh vegetables, herbs, and a touch of heat, making each bite bursting with flavor.
  • Versatile: You can serve these tacos in various ways—on soft tortillas, lettuce wraps, or even as a salad. You can also make them spicier or milder depending on your preferences.

Ready to dive into this delicious recipe? Let’s get started!

Ingredients

For the Chicken:

  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 2 teaspoons chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • ½ teaspoon onion powder
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil

For the Salsa:

  • 2 medium tomatoes, diced
  • ½ red onion, finely chopped
  • 1 jalapeño pepper, seeds removed and finely chopped (adjust to your spice level)
  • 1 tablespoon fresh lime juice
  • 2 tablespoons chopped fresh cilantro
  • 1 teaspoon olive oil
  • Salt and pepper, to taste

For the Tacos:

  • 8 small corn tortillas (or flour tortillas, if preferred)
  • Fresh cilantro for garnish
  • Lime wedges, for serving
  • Sour cream or guacamole (optional)

Instructions

Step 1: Prepare the Chicken for Sous Vide

The first step in making your sous vide salsa chicken tacos is to prepare the chicken. Start by seasoning the chicken breasts with the chili powder, cumin, garlic powder, onion powder, salt, and pepper. Rub the seasoning into the chicken to ensure it’s evenly coated.

Once your chicken is seasoned, place it into a vacuum-seal bag or a resealable freezer bag. If you’re using a vacuum-sealer, seal the bag tightly. For a resealable freezer bag, use the water displacement method to remove air by sealing the bag almost completely, then submerging it in water to push out the remaining air before sealing it fully.

Step 2: Sous Vide Cooking

Fill your sous vide container with water and set the temperature to 145°F (63.8°C). This temperature will ensure your chicken stays moist and tender. Once the water has reached the desired temperature, place the bagged chicken into the water bath. Cook for 1.5 to 2 hours, ensuring the chicken is fully submerged.

One of the great things about sous vide cooking is that it’s forgiving. The chicken will stay at the perfect temperature throughout the cooking process, so there’s no risk of overcooking. You can even cook it in advance and store it in the fridge for a few hours until you’re ready to assemble your tacos.

Step 3: Prepare the Salsa

While the chicken is cooking, it’s time to prepare the fresh salsa. This vibrant and refreshing topping is the perfect complement to the tender chicken.

Start by dicing the tomatoes and finely chopping the red onion. Remove the seeds from the jalapeño and finely chop it, adjusting the amount based on your heat preference. In a medium-sized bowl, combine the diced tomatoes, onion, and jalapeño. Add the lime juice, fresh cilantro, and olive oil, then season with salt and pepper to taste. Toss the ingredients together gently until well mixed.

Set the salsa aside to allow the flavors to meld while the chicken finishes cooking.

Step 4: Finish the Chicken

Once your sous vide chicken has finished cooking, remove the bag from the water bath. Carefully take the chicken out of the bag, and discard any excess juices. At this point, the chicken is incredibly tender, and you could easily shred it using just a fork. To add a bit more flavor and texture, we recommend giving the chicken a quick sear.

Heat 1 tablespoon of olive oil in a skillet over medium-high heat. Once the oil is hot, add the cooked chicken breasts and sear them for about 1-2 minutes on each side, until golden brown and slightly crispy. This step adds a delicious layer of flavor and texture to the chicken.

After searing, remove the chicken from the skillet and allow it to rest for a few minutes before slicing it into thin strips.

Step 5: Assemble the Tacos

Now comes the fun part—assembling the tacos! Warm your tortillas in a dry skillet or microwave for about 20-30 seconds until they’re pliable and heated through.

Place the sliced chicken onto each tortilla and top with a generous spoonful of the fresh salsa. Garnish with chopped cilantro and a squeeze of lime juice. For an extra touch of creaminess, you can add a dollop of sour cream or a spoonful of guacamole.

Step 6: Serve and Enjoy!

Your sous vide salsa chicken tacos are now ready to be enjoyed! Serve them immediately while the chicken is warm, and savor the juicy, flavorful chicken paired with the zesty salsa and fresh toppings. Whether you’re enjoying them for a casual dinner with family or serving them at a gathering with friends, these tacos are sure to impress.

Tips for Success

  • Adjust the spice level: The heat from the jalapeño can vary, so feel free to add more or less depending on your preference. If you prefer a milder salsa, you can omit the jalapeño altogether or replace it with a milder pepper.
  • Add variety to your toppings: In addition to cilantro and lime, try adding sliced avocado, shredded lettuce, or crumbled queso fresco to your tacos for extra flavor and texture.
  • Meal prep: The sous vide chicken can be cooked in advance and stored in the fridge for up to 3 days, making this recipe perfect for meal prepping. Simply heat the chicken when you’re ready to assemble your tacos.
  • Use your favorite salsa: While homemade salsa is always a winner, you can also use store-bought salsa if you’re short on time. Just make sure to choose a fresh variety that complements the chicken.

Pairing Suggestions

While these tacos are delicious on their own, you can elevate the meal with some great side dishes and drinks. Here are some pairing ideas:

  • Side dishes: Serve your tacos with a side of Mexican street corn (elote), refried beans, or a simple salad with avocado and corn.
  • Drinks: Pair your tacos with a refreshing margarita, ice-cold beer, or a light fruit-based mocktail for a festive, flavor-packed meal.

Conclusion

Sous vide salsa chicken tacos are a perfect dinner option that combines the ease of sous vide cooking with the vibrant, fresh flavors of Mexican cuisine. The sous vide method ensures that your chicken is cooked to perfection, resulting in juicy, tender chicken that’s full of flavor. The homemade salsa adds the perfect amount of zing and freshness, making each bite of these tacos a delightful experience. Whether you’re making them for a weeknight dinner or a special occasion, these tacos are sure to become a family favorite. Give them a try, and get ready to enjoy the best tacos you’ve ever tasted!
