Healthy One Pot Quinoa and Vegetable Stir Fry

Are you looking for a delicious and nutritious meal that can be prepared quickly and with minimal cleanup? Look no further than this Healthy One Pot Quinoa and Vegetable Stir Fry recipe! Packed with vibrant colors, flavors, and nutrients, this dish is a perfect option for those looking to incorporate more plant-based meals into their diet. Ingredients 1 cup quinoa 2 cups water 2 tablespoons olive oil 1 onion, thinly sliced 2 cloves garlic, minced 1 red bell pepper, thinly sliced 1 yellow bell pepper, thinly sliced 1 zucchini, thinly sliced 1 cup broccoli florets 1 cup snap peas 1 cup mushrooms, sliced 1 tablespoon soy sauce 1 tablespoon sesame oil 1 tablespoon rice vinegar Salt and pepper to taste Instructions Rinse the quinoa under cold water and drain well. In a large pot, bring the water to a boil. Add the quinoa and reduce the heat to low. Cover and simmer for about 15 minutes, or until the quinoa is tender and the water is absorbed. Remove from heat and set aside. In the same pot, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the onion and garlic and sauté until the onion is translucent and fragrant. Add the bell peppers, zucchini, broccoli, snap peas, and mushrooms to the pot. Stir fry for about 5 minutes, or until the vegetables are tender-crisp. In a small bowl, whisk together the soy sauce, sesame oil, and rice vinegar. Pour the sauce over the vegetables in the pot and stir well to coat. Add the cooked quinoa to the pot and toss everything together until well combined. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Cover the pot and let the stir fry sit for a few minutes to allow the flavors to meld together. Serve hot and enjoy!
