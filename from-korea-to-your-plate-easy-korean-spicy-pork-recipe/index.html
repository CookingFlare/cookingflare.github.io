From Korea to Your Plate: Easy Korean Spicy Pork Recipe


If you’re a fan of Korean cuisine or looking to explore new flavors, you’re in for a treat! Today, we’re diving into the world of Korean cooking with a delicious and easy-to-make recipe: Korean Spicy Pork.

Korean cuisine is known for its bold flavors and unique combinations of spices. This dish, in particular, showcases the perfect balance of heat, sweetness, and tanginess. The tender and succulent pork, coated in a fiery red sauce, will tantalize your taste buds and leave you craving for more.

Ingredients

To recreate this mouthwatering dish in your own kitchen, you’ll need the following ingredients:

  • 1.5 pounds of pork belly, thinly sliced
  • 1 onion, thinly sliced
  • 3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons of gochujang (Korean red pepper paste)
  • 1 tablespoon of soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon of honey
  • 1 tablespoon of rice wine vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon of sesame oil
  • 1 tablespoon of vegetable oil
  • 1 teaspoon of grated ginger
  • 1/2 teaspoon of black pepper
  • 1/4 teaspoon of salt
  • 2 green onions, sliced (for garnish)

Instructions

Now that we have all the ingredients ready, let’s get started with the cooking process:

  1. In a mixing bowl, combine the gochujang, soy sauce, honey, rice wine vinegar, sesame oil, grated ginger, black pepper, and salt. Mix well until the ingredients are fully incorporated.

  2. Heat a large skillet or wok over medium-high heat. Add the vegetable oil and swirl it around to coat the surface of the pan.

  3. Add the sliced pork belly to the hot pan and cook for about 3-4 minutes until it starts to brown and crisp up.

  4. Next, add the sliced onion and minced garlic to the pan. Stir-fry for another 2-3 minutes until the onion becomes translucent and fragrant.

  5. Reduce the heat to medium-low and pour the prepared sauce over the pork and onion mixture. Stir well to ensure every piece of pork is coated in the flavorful sauce.

  6. Cover the pan and let the pork simmer in the sauce for about 10-15 minutes, stirring occasionally. This will allow the flavors to meld together, and the pork to become tender.

  7. Once the pork is cooked through and tender, remove the lid and increase the heat to medium-high. Stir-fry for an additional 2-3 minutes to allow the sauce to thicken and caramelize slightly.

  8. Remove from heat and transfer the Korean Spicy Pork to a serving dish. Garnish with sliced green onions for an extra touch of freshness and color.

Serving Suggestions

Now that your Easy Korean Spicy Pork is ready, it’s time to serve and enjoy! This dish pairs perfectly with steamed rice, which helps to balance out the heat and adds a comforting element to the meal. You can also serve it with a side of kimchi and pickled vegetables for a complete Korean dining experience.

The beauty of this recipe lies in its versatility. Feel free to customize it according to your preferences. If you prefer spicier flavors, add a bit more gochujang. If you enjoy a sweeter taste, increase the amount of honey. Don’t be afraid to experiment and make it your own!

Conclusion

Korean Spicy Pork is a delightful dish that brings the vibrant flavors of Korea to your plate. With its perfect balance of spice, sweetness, and tanginess, it’s sure to become a favorite in your household. The best part is, it’s incredibly easy to make, requiring just a few simple ingredients and minimal prep time.

So why not take a culinary journey to Korea and whip up this Easy Korean Spicy Pork recipe tonight? Your taste buds will thank you, and you’ll have a new go-to dish that’s sure to impress family and friends. Enjoy!
