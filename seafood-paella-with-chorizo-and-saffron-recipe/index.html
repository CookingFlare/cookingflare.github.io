Seafood Paella with Chorizo and Saffron Recipe


If you’re looking for a show-stopping dish that’s bursting with flavor, then look no further than this Seafood Paella with Chorizo and Saffron recipe. Paella, a traditional Spanish dish, is a one-pan wonder that brings together a beautiful medley of seafood, savory chorizo, and aromatic saffron-infused rice. This recipe is perfect for entertaining or for a special family dinner. Let’s dive into the details of creating this mouthwatering dish!

Ingredients

  • 1 pound jumbo shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 1 pound mussels, cleaned and debearded
  • 1 pound clams, scrubbed
  • 8 ounces chorizo sausage, sliced
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 4 garlic cloves, minced
  • 2 cups Arborio rice
  • 1 teaspoon saffron threads
  • 1 teaspoon smoked paprika
  • 1 can (14 ounces) diced tomatoes
  • 4 cups chicken broth
  • 1 cup dry white wine
  • 1 red bell pepper, diced
  • 1 cup frozen peas
  • Fresh parsley, for garnish
  • Lemon wedges, for serving

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Seafood: Season the shrimp with salt and pepper. Set aside. Discard any mussels or clams that are open or cracked. Rinse them under cold water and set aside.

  2. Sear the Chorizo: In a large paella pan or a wide, shallow skillet, cook the chorizo over medium heat until it releases its oils and turns slightly crispy. Remove the chorizo from the pan and set it aside.

  3. Sauté Aromatics: In the same pan, add the chopped onion and cook until it becomes translucent. Add the minced garlic and cook for another minute, stirring constantly.

  4. Infuse the Rice: Stir in the Arborio rice, saffron threads, and smoked paprika, coating the rice with the flavorful oils in the pan. Toast the rice for a couple of minutes until it becomes slightly translucent.

  5. Add Liquid and Simmer: Pour in the diced tomatoes, chicken broth, and dry white wine. Stir well and bring the mixture to a simmer. Let it cook for about 10 minutes, stirring occasionally.

  6. Layer the Seafood: Nestle the seared chorizo, shrimp, mussels, clams, and diced red bell pepper into the rice mixture. Cover the pan and let the seafood cook for 10-15 minutes, or until the mussels and clams have opened and the shrimp is pink and opaque.

  7. Finish with Peas and Garnish: Scatter the frozen peas over the paella and let them cook for another 5 minutes. Once the seafood is fully cooked and the rice has absorbed the liquid, remove the pan from the heat. Garnish the paella with freshly chopped parsley.

  8. Serve and Enjoy: Serve the Seafood Paella with Chorizo and Saffron hot, with lemon wedges on the side for a bright, citrusy finish.

Tips for Success

  • Use a wide, shallow pan to ensure the rice cooks evenly and develops that coveted crispy layer on the bottom known as “socarrat.”
  • Feel free to customize the seafood selection based on your preferences and what’s available at your local market. Other options include scallops, squid, or even lobster tail.
  • For a vegetarian twist, omit the seafood and chorizo, and instead, load the paella with a variety of vegetables like artichokes, bell peppers, and peas.

Final Thoughts

This Seafood Paella with Chorizo and Saffron recipe is a celebration of flavors and textures, bringing together the bounty of the sea with the rich, smoky notes of chorizo and the delicate aroma of saffron. Whether you’re serving a crowd or simply indulging in a memorable dinner at home, this dish is sure to impress. Embrace the communal spirit of paella-making, and enjoy the process of creating this vibrant, aromatic masterpiece.

So, roll up your sleeves, gather your ingredients, and prepare to transport your taste buds to the sunny shores of Spain with this irresistible Seafood Paella with Chorizo and Saffron recipe. Cheers to good food, good company, and the joy of cooking!
