Vegan Casserole Crusade: 20 Hearty, Layered & Flavor-Packed Recipes for Potluck & Beyond


Welcome, fellow food enthusiasts! Prepare yourself for a culinary adventure as we dive headfirst into the world of vegan casserole masterpieces. This guide is here to help you discover new and inventive recipes that will satisfy your taste buds and those of your dinner guests. From plant-based protein-packed layers to tantalizing textures and rich flavors that stretch beyond your expectations, these 20 vegan casserole recipes will be sure to leave you craving more. So whether you’re new to the vegan lifestyle or simply looking to try something new, let’s jump right in!

1. Vegan Spinach and Ricotta Stuffed Shells Casserole

Can you imagine a world without the comforting embrace of stuffed shells? Us neither. This vegan concoction will have everyone asking for seconds, and they’ll never know the secret is spinach, tofu, and nutritional yeast.

Ingredients:

  • 1 box jumbo shell pasta
  • 400 grams silken tofu
  • 1 cup nutritional yeast
  • 1 cup chopped spinach
  • 1 cup vegan ricotta
  • 1 cup marinara sauce

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F.
  2. Cook pasta shells according to package directions until al dente.
  3. In a food processor, blend tofu, nutritional yeast, vegan ricotta, and spinach until smooth.
  4. Fill each shell with the spinach and tofu mixture.
  5. Layer shells in a baking dish, pouring marinara sauce over the top.
  6. Bake for 30 minutes or until heated through.

2. Sweet Potato and Lentil Shepherd’s Pie

A vegan Shepherd’s pie doesn’t have to be a thing of the past! This casserole with a twist features creamy sweet potatoes and hearty lentils layered together, making for a satisfying dish perfect for potlucks.

Ingredients:

  • 1 kg sweet potatoes, peeled and cubed
  • 1 kg cooked lentils
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup vegetable broth
  • 1 can diced tomatoes
  • 1 tsp dried thyme

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 400°F.
  2. Boil sweet potatoes until tender, then mash with a splash of veggie broth.
  3. In a skillet, sauté onion and garlic until browned, then add lentils, diced tomatoes, and thyme.
  4. Transfer lentil mixture to a baking dish, then top with sweet potato mash.
  5. Bake for 25 minutes or until heated through.

3. Quinoa and Black Bean Enchilada Casserole

This colorful casserole is fully-loaded with flavors you’ll crave over and over again. Quinoa and black beans make a formidable duo begging to be smothered in a rich enchilada sauce.

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups cooked quinoa
  • 2 cans black beans, drained, and rinsed
  • 1 red bell pepper, diced
  • 1 small onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup enchilada sauce
  • 1 can diced green chiles
  • 1 cup shredded vegan cheese

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F.
  2. In a skillet, cook the onion, bell pepper, and garlic until softened.
  3. Combine cooked quinoa, black beans, cooked veggies, enchilada sauce, green chiles, and 3/4 cup of shredded vegan cheese.
  4. Transfer mixture to a baking dish and top with remaining cheese.
  5. Bake for 20 minutes or until cheese is melted and bubbly.

4. Tofu and Veggie Lasagna

You haven’t lived until you’ve experienced tofu lasagna. Get your veggies in while enjoying lasagna’s iconic layers and gooey, melty cheese.

Ingredients:

  • 9-12 lasagna noodles
  • 400 grams firm tofu
  • 1 red bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 zucchini, sliced
  • 1 cup marinara sauce
  • 1 jar vegan ricotta
  • 1 cup vegan mozzarella cheese

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F.
  2. Cook lasagna noodles according to package directions, then drain and set aside.
  3. In a skillet, sauté bell pepper and zucchini until tender.
  4. In a food processor, blend tofu, vegan ricotta, sautéed veggies, and 1/4 cup vegan mozzarella.
  5. Layer noodles, marinara sauce, and tofu-veggie mixture, ending with a noodle and sauce on top.
  6. Sprinkle remaining vegan mozzarella and bake for 30 minutes.

5. Eggplant Parmesan Casserole

The epitome of comfort may very well be this eggplant parmesan casserole, where the comforting flavors of fried eggplant and gooey vegan cheese come together in heavenly harmony.

Ingredients:

  • 2 medium eggplants, sliced into 1/4-inch circles
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup unsweetened almond milk
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 can diced tomatoes
  • 1 cup vegan parmesan
  • 1 cup vegan mozzarella cheese

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 400°F.
  2. In a shallow dish, combine flour and garlic powder.
  3. In another dish, mix almond milk and vegan parmesan.
  4. Dip eggplant slices into flour, then milk mixture and layer in a baking dish, alternating with layers of tomatoes and vegan cheese.
  5. Bake for 40 minutes or until the top is golden brown and bubbly.
