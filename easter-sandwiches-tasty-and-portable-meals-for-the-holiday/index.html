Easter Sandwiches: Tasty and Portable Meals for the Holiday


Easter is a time of joy, celebration, and, of course, delicious food. Whether you’re planning a picnic, a family gathering, or just want a simple yet satisfying meal, Easter sandwiches are the perfect solution. These tasty and portable meals are not only convenient but also a great way to make the most of your holiday. In this blog post, we’ll explore some creative and mouthwatering Easter sandwich recipes that will elevate your holiday dining experience.

Classic Deviled Egg Salad Sandwich

Ingredients:

  • 6 hard-boiled eggs, chopped
  • 1/4 cup mayonnaise
  • 1 tablespoon Dijon mustard
  • 1/4 cup finely chopped celery
  • 2 tablespoons finely chopped red onion
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 8 slices of your favorite bread
  • Lettuce leaves
  • Tomato slices

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, combine the chopped eggs, mayonnaise, Dijon mustard, celery, and red onion.
  2. Season the mixture with salt and pepper to taste.
  3. Spread the egg salad generously on 4 slices of bread.
  4. Top each with lettuce leaves and tomato slices.
  5. Cover with the remaining bread slices to make sandwiches.
  6. Cut the sandwiches in half and secure with toothpicks if needed.

This classic deviled egg salad sandwich is a delightful twist on the traditional deviled eggs, making it a perfect addition to your Easter menu. The creamy and flavorful egg salad paired with the freshness of lettuce and tomatoes creates a harmonious blend of textures and tastes.

Honey Glazed Ham and Pineapple Sandwich

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound thinly sliced honey glazed ham
  • 8 slices of pineapple
  • 8 slices of Swiss cheese
  • 8 slices of Hawaiian sweet bread
  • Dijon mustard
  • Butter

Instructions:

  1. Preheat a skillet over medium heat.
  2. Spread one side of each bread slice with Dijon mustard.
  3. Assemble the sandwiches by layering ham, pineapple, and cheese on the Dijon mustard side of 4 bread slices.
  4. Top with the remaining bread slices, Dijon mustard side down.
  5. Lightly butter the outsides of the sandwiches.
  6. Place the sandwiches in the skillet and cook until the bread is golden brown and the cheese is melted.
  7. Serve warm.

This mouthwatering sandwich combines the savory sweetness of honey glazed ham with the tropical flavors of pineapple and the richness of Swiss cheese. The Hawaiian sweet bread adds a touch of sweetness, making it a delightful Easter treat for both kids and adults.

Spring Vegetable and Herbed Cream Cheese Sandwich

Ingredients:

  • 8 ounces cream cheese, softened
  • 2 tablespoons finely chopped fresh herbs (such as dill, chives, and parsley)
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 8 slices of whole grain bread
  • Thinly sliced cucumber
  • Thinly sliced radishes
  • Baby spinach leaves

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, mix the softened cream cheese with the fresh herbs, garlic powder, salt, and pepper.
  2. Spread the herbed cream cheese on one side of each bread slice.
  3. Layer the cucumber, radishes, and spinach on 4 bread slices.
  4. Top with the remaining bread slices to make sandwiches.
  5. Cut the sandwiches diagonally and serve.

This vibrant and refreshing sandwich celebrates the flavors of spring with crisp vegetables and a creamy herbed cream cheese spread. It’s a delightful option for a lighter Easter meal without compromising on taste.
