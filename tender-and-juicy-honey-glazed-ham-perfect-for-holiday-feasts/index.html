Tender and Juicy Honey Glazed Ham: Perfect for Holiday Feasts


The holiday season is a time for family, friends, and delicious feasts that warm the soul. And what better way to make your holiday table shine than with a tender and juicy honey glazed ham? This mouthwatering centerpiece is sure to impress your guests and leave them craving for more. In this recipe, we will guide you through the step-by-step process of preparing the perfect honey glazed ham that will have everyone coming back for seconds.

Ingredients

To create this tantalizing dish, you will need the following ingredients:

  • 1 fully cooked bone-in ham (8-10 pounds)
  • 1 cup honey
  • 1/2 cup brown sugar
  • 1/4 cup Dijon mustard
  • 2 tablespoons apple cider vinegar
  • 1 teaspoon ground cloves

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 325°F (165°C). Place the ham on a rack in a large roasting pan, with the cut side facing down.

  2. In a small saucepan, combine the honey, brown sugar, Dijon mustard, apple cider vinegar, and ground cloves. Heat the mixture over low heat, stirring constantly, until the sugar has dissolved and the glaze is smooth and well combined.

  3. Brush half of the glaze generously over the ham, making sure to cover all sides. Reserve the remaining glaze for later.

  4. Cover the roasting pan tightly with aluminum foil, ensuring that it is sealed to keep the moisture inside. This will help the ham stay tender and juicy.

  5. Place the ham in the preheated oven and bake for approximately 15 minutes per pound, or until the internal temperature reaches 140°F (60°C). Use a meat thermometer to check the temperature.

  6. Once the ham has reached the desired temperature, remove it from the oven and carefully remove the foil. Increase the oven temperature to 425°F (220°C).

  7. Brush the remaining glaze over the ham, allowing it to coat the surface evenly. Return the ham to the oven, uncovered, and continue baking for an additional 15-20 minutes, or until the glaze has caramelized and formed a beautiful golden crust.

  8. Once the ham is done, remove it from the oven and let it rest for 10-15 minutes before carving. This will allow the juices to redistribute, resulting in a more flavorful and succulent ham.

  9. Carve the ham into thin slices and serve it warm. The tender meat, combined with the sweet and sticky glaze, will surely delight your taste buds.

Serving Suggestions

This honey glazed ham pairs perfectly with a variety of side dishes, making it an ideal choice for holiday feasts. Serve it alongside creamy mashed potatoes, roasted vegetables, or a fresh green salad. You can also offer a selection of mustards or chutneys as condiments to enhance the flavors even further.

Conclusion

Preparing a tender and juicy honey glazed ham may seem like a daunting task, but with this simple recipe, you’ll be able to create a show-stopping centerpiece for your holiday table. The combination of sweet honey, tangy Dijon mustard, and aromatic cloves will elevate the flavor of the ham to new heights. So gather your loved ones, celebrate the joy of the season, and indulge in the delectable goodness of this honey glazed ham. Your holiday feast will be one to remember. Enjoy!
