Mouthwatering Instant Pot Beef and Broccoli Stir-Fry Recipes

Are you craving a delicious and satisfying meal that is quick and easy to make? Look no further than these mouthwatering Instant Pot Beef and Broccoli Stir-Fry recipes. With the convenience of the Instant Pot, you can have a flavorful and nutritious meal on the table in no time. So, grab your apron and let’s get cooking! Recipe 1: Classic Beef and Broccoli Stir-Fry Ingredients: 1 pound beef sirloin, thinly sliced 2 cups broccoli florets 1/2 cup low-sodium soy sauce 1/4 cup brown sugar 2 cloves garlic, minced 1 teaspoon fresh ginger, grated 1 tablespoon cornstarch 1/4 cup water 2 tablespoons vegetable oil Salt and pepper, to taste Steamed rice, for serving Instructions: In a small bowl, whisk together the soy sauce, brown sugar, garlic, ginger, cornstarch, and water. Set aside. Season the beef slices with salt and pepper. Set your Instant Pot to the sauté function and heat the vegetable oil. Add the beef slices to the Instant Pot and cook until browned on all sides. Remove the beef and set aside. Add the broccoli florets to the Instant Pot and sauté for 2-3 minutes, until slightly tender. Return the beef to the Instant Pot and pour in the soy sauce mixture. Secure the lid on the Instant Pot and set the vent to the sealing position. Cook on high pressure for 5 minutes, then do a quick release of the pressure. Serve the beef and broccoli stir-fry over steamed rice and enjoy! Recipe 2: Spicy Sesame Beef and Broccoli Stir-Fry Ingredients: 1 pound beef flank steak, thinly sliced 2 cups broccoli florets 1/4 cup low-sodium soy sauce 2 tablespoons hoisin sauce 1 tablespoon sesame oil 1 tablespoon Sriracha sauce 2 cloves garlic, minced 1 tablespoon cornstarch 1/4 cup water 2 tablespoons vegetable oil Salt and pepper, to taste Sesame seeds, for garnish Steamed rice, for serving Instructions: In a small bowl, whisk together the soy sauce, hoisin sauce, sesame oil, Sriracha sauce, garlic, cornstarch, and water. Set aside. Season the beef slices with salt and pepper. Set your Instant Pot to the sauté function and heat the vegetable oil. Add the beef slices to the Instant Pot and cook until browned on all sides. Remove the beef and set aside. Add the broccoli florets to the Instant Pot and sauté for 2-3 minutes, until slightly tender. Return the beef to the Instant Pot and pour in the sauce mixture. Secure the lid on the Instant Pot and set the vent to the sealing position. Cook on high pressure for 5 minutes, then do a quick release of the pressure. Serve the spicy sesame beef and broccoli stir-fry over steamed rice, garnish with sesame seeds, and enjoy! Recipe 3: Teriyaki Beef and Broccoli Stir-Fry Ingredients: 1 pound beef tenderloin, thinly sliced 2 cups broccoli florets 1/2 cup teriyaki sauce 2 tablespoons honey 2 cloves garlic, minced 1 tablespoon cornstarch 1/4 cup water 2 tablespoons vegetable oil Salt and pepper, to taste Green onions, sliced, for garnish Steamed rice, for serving Instructions: In a small bowl, whisk together the teriyaki sauce, honey, garlic, cornstarch, and water. Set aside. Season the beef slices with salt and pepper. Set your Instant Pot to the sauté function and heat the vegetable oil. Add the beef slices to the Instant Pot and cook until browned on all sides. Remove the beef and set aside. Add the broccoli florets to the Instant Pot and sauté for 2-3 minutes, until slightly tender. Return the beef to the Instant Pot and pour in the teriyaki sauce mixture. Secure the lid on the Instant Pot and set the vent to the sealing position. Cook on high pressure for 5 minutes, then do a quick release of the pressure. Serve the teriyaki beef and broccoli stir-fry over steamed rice, garnish with sliced green onions, and enjoy! Mouthwatering Instant Pot Beef Tacos with Homemade Salsa Recipes

If you’re craving a delicious and satisfying meal that’s easy to make, look no further than these mouthwatering Instant Pot beef tacos with homemade salsa recipes. This recipe is perfect for a weeknight dinner or a casual get-together with friends and family. The combination of tender, flavorful beef and fresh, zesty salsa is sure to be a hit with everyone at the table. Instant Pot Beef Tacos Ingredients: 2 pounds beef chuck roast, cut into chunks 1 tablespoon olive oil 1 onion, diced 4 cloves garlic, minced 1 cup beef broth 1 tablespoon chili powder 1 teaspoon cumin 1 teaspoon paprika 1 teaspoon oregano Salt and pepper to taste Instructions: Turn on the Instant Pot and select the “Saute” setting. Add the olive oil and brown the beef in batches until golden brown on all sides. Remove the beef and set it aside. Add the diced onion to the Instant Pot and cook until softened, then add the minced garlic and cook for another minute. Return the beef to the Instant Pot and add the beef broth, chili powder, cumin, paprika, oregano, salt, and pepper. Close the lid and set the Instant Pot to “Manual” for 45 minutes. Once the cooking time is complete, allow the pressure to release naturally for 10 minutes before using the quick release. Shred the beef using two forks and return it to the cooking liquid to keep it moist and flavorful. Homemade Salsa Ingredients: 4 ripe tomatoes, diced 1/2 red onion, finely chopped 1 jalapeno, seeded and minced 1/4 cup fresh cilantro, chopped Juice of 1 lime Salt and pepper to taste Instructions: In a mixing bowl, combine the diced tomatoes, chopped red onion, minced jalapeno, and chopped cilantro. Squeeze the lime juice over the mixture and toss to combine. Season the salsa with salt and pepper to taste, and refrigerate for at least 30 minutes to allow the flavors to meld together. Assembling the Tacos Once the beef is ready and the salsa has had time to chill, it’s time to assemble the tacos. Warm up some corn or flour tortillas, spoon some of the tender beef onto each tortilla, and top it off with a generous helping of the homemade salsa.
