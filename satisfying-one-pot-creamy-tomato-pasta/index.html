Satisfying One Pot Creamy Tomato Pasta


If you’re looking for a quick and satisfying dinner that’s easy to make and requires minimal cleanup, then this One Pot Creamy Tomato Pasta recipe is perfect for you. This recipe combines the rich flavors of a creamy tomato sauce with the simplicity of a one-pot meal, making it an ideal choice for busy weeknights or lazy weekends. Plus, with just a few simple ingredients, you can create a delicious and comforting dish that will please the whole family.

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 3 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 can (14 oz) crushed tomatoes
  • 2 cups vegetable broth
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1/2 teaspoon red pepper flakes
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • 8 oz penne pasta
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • Fresh basil leaves for garnish

Instructions

  1. In a large pot or deep skillet, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the chopped onion and cook until translucent, about 3-4 minutes. Add the minced garlic and cook for an additional 1-2 minutes, stirring frequently to prevent burning.

  2. Pour in the crushed tomatoes and vegetable broth, then add the dried basil, oregano, red pepper flakes, salt, and black pepper. Stir to combine all the ingredients and bring the mixture to a gentle boil.

  3. Once the sauce is simmering, add the penne pasta and stir well to ensure the pasta is evenly coated in the sauce. Reduce the heat to medium-low and cover the pot. Allow the pasta to cook for about 12-15 minutes, stirring occasionally, until it is al dente and the sauce has thickened.

  4. Once the pasta is cooked to your liking, pour in the heavy cream and grated Parmesan cheese. Stir the pasta until the cheese has melted and the cream is fully incorporated into the sauce. If the sauce seems too thick, you can add a splash of vegetable broth to loosen it up.

  5. Remove the pot from the heat and let the pasta rest for a few minutes. This will allow the sauce to thicken further and the flavors to meld together.

  6. Serve the One Pot Creamy Tomato Pasta in individual bowls, garnished with fresh basil leaves for a pop of color and a hint of freshness. You can also sprinkle some extra Parmesan cheese on top for an added cheesy indulgence.

Tips and Variations

  • For a protein boost, you can add cooked chicken, shrimp, or even crispy bacon to the pasta.
  • If you prefer a spicy kick, increase the amount of red pepper flakes or add a pinch of cayenne pepper.
  • Feel free to swap the penne pasta for your favorite pasta shape, such as spaghetti, fusilli, or rigatoni.
  • To make the dish vegetarian-friendly, use vegetable rennet-free Parmesan cheese or a vegan alternative.
  • Leftovers can be refrigerated and reheated the next day, making this One Pot Creamy Tomato Pasta a great option for meal prep.

Final Thoughts

This One Pot Creamy Tomato Pasta recipe is a versatile and comforting dish that is sure to become a staple in your recipe collection. It’s a perfect balance of rich and creamy flavors with the tanginess of tomatoes, all cooked in one pot for minimal cleanup. Whether you’re cooking for a family dinner, a cozy date night, or simply craving a satisfying meal, this recipe is a winner. So, next time you’re in the mood for a delicious and hassle-free pasta dish, give this recipe a try and savor every creamy, tomato-infused bite. Enjoy!
