Delicious Spinach and Artichoke Dip Recipe for Your Next Party


Are you planning a party and looking for the perfect appetizer to wow your guests? Look no further than this delicious Spinach and Artichoke Dip recipe! Creamy, cheesy, and packed with flavor, this dip is sure to be a crowd-pleaser. Whether you’re hosting a casual get-together or a formal dinner party, this dish is versatile enough to fit any occasion.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup frozen spinach, thawed and drained
  • 1 cup canned artichoke hearts, drained and chopped
  • 1/2 cup mayonnaise
  • 1/2 cup sour cream
  • 1/2 cup cream cheese, softened
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/2 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/2 teaspoon onion powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).

  2. In a large bowl, mix together the mayonnaise, sour cream, cream cheese, Parmesan cheese, mozzarella cheese, garlic, onion powder, basil, and oregano. Stir until well combined.

  3. Add the thawed spinach and chopped artichoke hearts to the mixture. Stir until the ingredients are evenly distributed throughout the dip.

  4. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Remember to go easy on the salt, as the Parmesan cheese already adds a good amount of saltiness to the dip.

  5. Transfer the dip to an oven-safe dish, spreading it out evenly.

  6. Bake in the preheated oven for 20-25 minutes, or until the top is golden and bubbly.

  7. Remove from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes before serving.

Serving Suggestions

This Spinach and Artichoke Dip is best enjoyed warm and paired with your favorite dippables. Here are a few suggestions to get you started:

  • Tortilla chips: The classic choice for dipping, tortilla chips provide a satisfying crunch that complements the creamy dip perfectly.
  • Pita bread: Cut pita bread into wedges and toast them in the oven for a few minutes until they’re crisp. These make a great alternative to tortilla chips.
  • Vegetable sticks: For a healthier option, serve the dip with an assortment of fresh vegetable sticks, such as carrots, celery, and bell peppers. The colorful veggies add a refreshing contrast to the rich dip.
  • Crostini: Slice a baguette into thin rounds, brush with olive oil, and toast in the oven until golden and crispy. These homemade crostini are perfect for scooping up the dip.

Tips and Variations

  • For an extra kick of flavor, add a few dashes of hot sauce or crushed red pepper flakes to the dip mixture.
  • Want to make it ahead of time? Prepare the dip up to the baking step and refrigerate until you’re ready to serve. Just remember to adjust the baking time accordingly, as the dip will be cold.
  • If you’re a fan of gooey cheese, sprinkle some extra mozzarella on top of the dip before baking. This will create a cheesy crust that’s hard to resist.

So there you have it - a delicious Spinach and Artichoke Dip recipe that’s perfect for your next party. With its creamy texture and irresistible flavors, this appetizer is guaranteed to be a hit among your guests. Give it a try and watch as it disappears within minutes. Enjoy!
