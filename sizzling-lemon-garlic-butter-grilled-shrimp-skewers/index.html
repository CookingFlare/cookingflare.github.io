Sizzling Lemon Garlic Butter Grilled Shrimp Skewers


There’s nothing quite like the sizzle of shrimp on the grill, especially when they’re coated in a zesty lemon garlic butter marinade. These grilled shrimp skewers are a perfect addition to any summer cookout or backyard barbecue. The combination of tangy lemon, savory garlic, and rich butter creates a mouthwatering flavor that will have your guests coming back for more. Plus, grilling the shrimp on skewers not only makes for a stunning presentation but also ensures that each succulent morsel is perfectly cooked. Let’s dive into the details of this delectable recipe.

Ingredients:

  • 2 pounds of large shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 1/2 cup of melted butter
  • 4 cloves of garlic, minced
  • Zest of 2 lemons
  • Juice of 1 lemon
  • 1 teaspoon of salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon of black pepper
  • 1/4 teaspoon of red pepper flakes (optional)
  • Fresh parsley, chopped, for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the Marinade: In a bowl, combine the melted butter, minced garlic, lemon zest, lemon juice, salt, black pepper, and red pepper flakes. Mix well to ensure that the flavors are evenly distributed.

  2. Marinate the Shrimp: Place the peeled and deveined shrimp in a large resealable plastic bag or a shallow dish. Pour the lemon garlic butter marinade over the shrimp, making sure they are well-coated. Seal the bag or cover the dish and refrigerate for at least 30 minutes to allow the flavors to infuse the shrimp.

  3. Preheat the Grill: While the shrimp are marinating, preheat your grill to medium-high heat. If you’re using wooden skewers, soak them in water for at least 30 minutes to prevent them from burning on the grill.

  4. Skewer the Shrimp: Thread the marinated shrimp onto the skewers, making sure to leave a little space between each shrimp to ensure even cooking.

  5. Grill the Shrimp: Place the shrimp skewers on the preheated grill and cook for 2-3 minutes on each side, or until they turn pink and opaque. Be careful not to overcook the shrimp, as they can become tough and rubbery.

  6. Serve and Enjoy: Once the shrimp are grilled to perfection, carefully remove them from the skewers and transfer them to a serving platter. Sprinkle with freshly chopped parsley for a pop of color and added freshness. These sizzling lemon garlic butter grilled shrimp skewers are best enjoyed hot off the grill, so don’t hesitate to dig in and savor the irresistible flavors.

Tips for Success:

  • Use fresh shrimp for the best flavor and texture.
  • Adjust the amount of red pepper flakes to suit your spice preference.
  • If using wooden skewers, make sure to soak them thoroughly to prevent burning on the grill.
  • Serve the grilled shrimp skewers with a side of rice, a crisp salad, or grilled vegetables for a well-rounded meal.

There you have it – a simple yet sensational recipe for sizzling lemon garlic butter grilled shrimp skewers that’s sure to impress your family and friends. Whether you’re hosting a summertime gathering or simply craving a taste of the sea, these flavorful shrimp skewers are a must-try. So fire up the grill, gather your ingredients, and get ready to elevate your outdoor cooking game with this irresistible dish. Happy grilling!
