Wholesome Gluten-Free Cauliflower Fried Rice: A Healthy Twist


Are you looking for a delicious and healthy alternative to traditional fried rice? Look no further! This Wholesome Gluten-Free Cauliflower Fried Rice recipe is sure to satisfy your cravings while providing a nutritious twist. Packed with flavor and loaded with veggies, this dish is perfect for those following a gluten-free or low-carb diet. So let’s get cooking!

Ingredients

  • 1 medium-sized cauliflower head
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 2 carrots, diced
  • 1 cup frozen peas
  • 2 tablespoons gluten-free soy sauce
  • 2 teaspoons sesame oil
  • 2 eggs, beaten
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Optional toppings: green onions, sesame seeds

Instructions

  1. Start by preparing the cauliflower “rice.” Cut the cauliflower into florets and pulse in a food processor until it resembles rice grains. Be careful not to over-process, as you want a rice-like texture. Set aside.

  2. Heat the olive oil in a large skillet or wok over medium heat. Add the minced garlic and chopped onion, and sauté until fragrant and slightly golden.

  3. Add the diced carrots to the skillet and cook for about 3-4 minutes, until they start to soften. Stir in the frozen peas and cook for an additional 2 minutes.

  4. Push the vegetables to one side of the skillet and add the beaten eggs to the other side. Allow the eggs to cook for a few seconds undisturbed, then scramble them with a spatula until fully cooked.

  5. Now, it’s time to add the cauliflower “rice” to the skillet. Stir everything together, making sure all the ingredients are well-combined. Cook for about 5-7 minutes, until the cauliflower is tender but not mushy.

  6. Drizzle the gluten-free soy sauce and sesame oil over the cauliflower fried rice. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Continue to cook for an additional 2 minutes, allowing the flavors to meld together.

  7. Remove the skillet from heat and give the fried rice a final toss. This will ensure that all the ingredients are evenly distributed and the flavors are well-incorporated.

  8. Serve the Wholesome Gluten-Free Cauliflower Fried Rice hot, garnished with chopped green onions and sesame seeds if desired. Enjoy this healthy twist on a classic Chinese dish!

Nutritional Information

This Wholesome Gluten-Free Cauliflower Fried Rice is not only delicious, but it’s also packed with nutrients. Here’s a breakdown of the approximate nutritional information per serving:

  • Calories: 250
  • Fat: 12g
  • Carbohydrates: 25g
  • Fiber: 8g
  • Protein: 12g

Final Thoughts

Wholesome Gluten-Free Cauliflower Fried Rice is a fantastic option for those looking to enjoy a healthier version of a beloved dish. With the cauliflower “rice” replacing the traditional grains, you can indulge without the guilt. Plus, the addition of vibrant veggies makes this dish both visually appealing and packed with essential nutrients.

Whether you’re following a gluten-free diet or simply looking to incorporate more vegetables into your meals, this recipe is a winner. It’s easy to prepare, customizable to your taste preferences, and perfect for a quick weeknight dinner or a weekend gathering with friends.

So why not give this Wholesome Gluten-Free Cauliflower Fried Rice recipe a try? Your taste buds and your body will thank you!
