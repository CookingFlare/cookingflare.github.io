Savory and Filling Picnic Soups


When it comes to picnics, sandwiches and salads often steal the show. But why not bring something warm and comforting to your outdoor feast? Enter savory and filling picnic soups. These delicious and easy-to-make soups are perfect for a day out in nature, offering a cozy and satisfying meal that will leave you feeling nourished and content. So pack up your thermos and let’s dive into the world of savory picnic soups!

Tomato Basil Soup

Nothing beats the classic combination of ripe tomatoes and fragrant basil. Tomato basil soup is a picnic favorite that can be enjoyed both hot and cold. Start by sautéing onions and garlic in olive oil until translucent. Add in fresh tomatoes, vegetable broth, and a handful of fresh basil leaves. Simmer the mixture until the tomatoes are soft and tender. Blend everything together until smooth, and season with salt and pepper to taste. Serve with a sprinkle of Parmesan cheese and crusty bread for a complete and satisfying meal.

Corn Chowder

Corn chowder is a hearty and creamy soup that is perfect for a picnic on a breezy summer day. Begin by sautéing onions, celery, and carrots in butter until they become tender. Add in diced potatoes and fresh corn kernels, and cook until the potatoes are fork-tender. In a separate saucepan, heat milk and flour until it thickens, then pour it into the vegetable mixture. Stir in some fresh thyme and season with salt and pepper. Let the soup simmer for a few minutes to allow the flavors to meld together. Serve with a sprinkle of chopped fresh parsley for a pop of color.

Lentil Soup

Lentil soup is a nutritious and filling option that is packed with protein and fiber. Start by sautéing onions and garlic in olive oil until fragrant. Add in diced carrots, celery, and red bell pepper, and cook until they start to soften. Rinse and drain dried lentils, then add them to the pot along with vegetable broth, diced tomatoes, and a blend of aromatic spices like cumin, paprika, and turmeric. Let the soup simmer until the lentils are tender. Serve with a squeeze of lemon juice for a refreshing touch.

Mushroom Barley Soup

Mushroom barley soup is a rich and earthy option that will warm you up on a chilly picnic day. Begin by sautéing onions and garlic in butter until golden brown. Add in sliced mushrooms and cook until they release their juices. Stir in barley, vegetable broth, and a bay leaf for added flavor. Let the soup simmer until the barley is tender and has absorbed the flavors of the mushrooms. Season with salt and pepper to taste, and garnish with fresh parsley for a burst of freshness.

Conclusion

Savory and filling picnic soups are a fantastic addition to any outdoor gathering. They provide warmth, comfort, and a delightful twist to the typical picnic fare. Whether you’re a fan of classic tomato basil soup, creamy corn chowder, hearty lentil soup, or earthy mushroom barley soup, there’s a picnic soup for everyone’s taste buds. So next time you plan a picnic, don’t forget to pack a thermos filled with one of these delicious creations. Your family and friends will thank you for it!
