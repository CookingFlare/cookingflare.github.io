BBQ Meatball Sliders: Mini Sandwich Recipes for BBQ Parties


If you’re looking for a crowd-pleasing dish to serve at your next BBQ party, look no further than these delicious BBQ meatball sliders. These mini sandwiches are not only easy to make, but they’re also packed with flavor and are sure to be a hit with your guests. Whether you’re hosting a backyard get-together, a game day gathering, or just craving some comforting finger food, these BBQ meatball sliders are the perfect choice.

Ingredients

For the Meatballs:

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1/2 cup breadcrumbs
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon onion powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste

For the BBQ Sauce:

  • 1 cup ketchup
  • 1/2 cup brown sugar
  • 1/4 cup apple cider vinegar
  • 2 tablespoons Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 teaspoon smoked paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste

For the Sliders:

  • 12 slider buns
  • Sliced cheddar cheese
  • Sliced red onion
  • Fresh parsley, chopped (for garnish)

Instructions

  1. Preheat your grill to medium-high heat.

  2. In a large mixing bowl, combine the ground beef, breadcrumbs, Parmesan cheese, egg, garlic powder, onion powder, salt, and pepper. Mix the ingredients until well combined.

  3. Shape the meat mixture into small meatballs, about 1 inch in diameter.

  4. Place the meatballs on the preheated grill and cook for 10-12 minutes, turning occasionally, until they are browned and cooked through.

  5. While the meatballs are cooking, prepare the BBQ sauce. In a saucepan over medium heat, combine the ketchup, brown sugar, apple cider vinegar, Worcestershire sauce, smoked paprika, garlic powder, salt, and pepper. Simmer the sauce for 10-15 minutes, stirring occasionally, until it thickens.

  6. Once the meatballs are cooked, brush them generously with the BBQ sauce and continue to cook for an additional 2-3 minutes, allowing the sauce to caramelize.

  7. Slice the slider buns in half and place a slice of cheddar cheese on the bottom half of each bun.

  8. Arrange the BBQ meatballs on top of the cheese, and then add a few slices of red onion.

  9. Drizzle any remaining BBQ sauce over the meatballs, and sprinkle with chopped parsley for a pop of color and freshness.

  10. Place the top half of the slider buns on the meatballs to complete the sliders.

  11. Serve the BBQ meatball sliders on a platter and watch them disappear as your guests dig in and enjoy!

Tips for Success

  • For an extra kick of flavor, consider adding a slice of pickled jalapeno or a dollop of coleslaw to each slider.
  • If you’re short on time, you can prepare the meatballs and BBQ sauce ahead of the party and simply reheat them on the grill before assembling the sliders.
  • Don’t be afraid to customize the sliders with your favorite cheese or toppings to suit your taste.

These BBQ meatball sliders are a fantastic addition to any BBQ party menu. They’re easy to eat, packed with smoky, tangy flavors, and are guaranteed to satisfy even the heartiest appetites. So fire up the grill, gather your friends and family, and get ready to indulge in these irresistible mini sandwich creations. Happy grilling!
