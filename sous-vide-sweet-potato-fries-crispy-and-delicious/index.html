Sous Vide Sweet Potato Fries: Crispy and Delicious

3 min · 616 words

If you’re a fan of sweet potato fries, you know that achieving the perfect balance of crispy exterior and tender interior can be a bit of a challenge. Traditional methods of frying or baking often result in fries that are either too soft or too crispy. However, with the sous vide cooking technique, you can achieve sweet potato fries that are perfectly cooked through and wonderfully crispy on the outside. In this recipe, we’ll walk you through the steps to create mouthwatering sous vide sweet potato fries that are sure to become a household favorite.

Ingredients

  • 2 large sweet potatoes, peeled and cut into fries
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

Step 1: Prepare the Sous Vide Bath

Fill a large pot or container with water and attach the sous vide precision cooker. Set the temperature to 194°F (90°C) and allow the water to come to the specified temperature.

Step 2: Prepare the Sweet Potatoes

After peeling and cutting the sweet potatoes into fry shapes, place them in a large resealable bag. Add the olive oil, garlic powder, paprika, salt, and pepper to the bag. Seal the bag tightly and massage the sweet potatoes to ensure they are evenly coated with the seasonings.

Step 3: Sous Vide Cooking

Once the water reaches the desired temperature, carefully lower the bag of seasoned sweet potato fries into the water bath. Ensure that the fries are fully submerged. Cook the sweet potatoes in the sous vide bath for 1 hour to 1 hour and 30 minutes, allowing them to become tender while maintaining their shape.

Step 4: Ice Bath

After the sweet potatoes have finished cooking, remove the bag from the water bath and immediately transfer the fries to an ice bath. This step will halt the cooking process and help the fries retain their shape.

Step 5: Drying the Fries

Once the sweet potato fries have cooled in the ice bath, carefully remove them and pat them dry with paper towels. It’s essential to remove excess moisture to ensure the fries develop a crispy exterior during the final cooking stage.

Step 6: Final Crisping

Preheat the oven to 425°F (220°C). Arrange the dried sweet potato fries on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper, ensuring they are in a single layer with space between each fry. Place the baking sheet in the preheated oven and bake the fries for 15-20 minutes, turning them halfway through, until they develop a crispy exterior.

Step 7: Serve and Enjoy

Once the sweet potato fries are golden brown and crispy, remove them from the oven and season with additional salt if desired. Serve the fries hot, paired with your favorite dipping sauce or as a delightful side dish to complement any meal.

By using the sous vide method, you can achieve sweet potato fries that boast a perfect balance of tenderness and crispiness, elevating this beloved side dish to a new level. Whether you’re hosting a gathering or simply treating yourself, these sous vide sweet potato fries are a delightful addition to any occasion. Experiment with different seasonings and dipping sauces to customize the flavors to your liking, and enjoy the wonderful texture and taste of these crispy, flavorful fries.

So there you have it – a simple yet innovative way to create irresistibly crispy and delicious sweet potato fries using the sous vide technique. Give this recipe a try and elevate your sweet potato fry game to new heights!
