Vegan Dessert Bars: Easy Recipes to Satisfy Your Sweet Tooth


When it comes to satisfying your sweet tooth, vegan dessert bars are a delightful option that combines flavor, texture, and health benefits. These bars are not only easy to make, but they also cater to a variety of dietary preferences without compromising on taste. Whether you’re looking for a quick snack, a healthy dessert, or a treat to impress your friends, these vegan dessert bars are sure to please.

Why Choose Vegan Dessert Bars?

Vegan dessert bars are a fantastic choice for several reasons:

  • Healthier Ingredients: Most vegan recipes utilize whole ingredients, reducing processed sugars and unhealthy fats.
  • Versatility: Vegan bars can cater to various dietary restrictions, making them suitable for almost anyone.
  • Simplicity: Many recipes require minimal effort and time, perfect for busy lifestyles.
  • Delicious Flavors: With the right combination of ingredients, vegan dessert bars can be just as indulgent as traditional desserts.

In this blog post, we’ll explore several easy vegan dessert bar recipes that will satisfy your cravings while keeping things plant-based. Grab your apron, and let’s get baking!

1. No-Bake Chocolate Peanut Butter Bars

Ingredients

  • 1 cup rolled oats
  • 1 cup natural peanut butter
  • 1/2 cup maple syrup
  • 1/2 cup cocoa powder
  • 1/4 cup dark chocolate chips (vegan)
  • A pinch of salt

Instructions

  1. Mix the Base: In a large bowl, combine rolled oats, peanut butter, maple syrup, cocoa powder, and a pinch of salt. Stir until well combined. If the mixture is too thick, add a tablespoon of water to reach a better consistency.

  2. Press into Pan: Line an 8x8 inch baking dish with parchment paper. Press the mixture evenly into the bottom of the dish.

  3. Melt the Chocolate: In a microwave-safe bowl, melt the dark chocolate chips in 30-second intervals, stirring in between until smooth.

  4. Top the Bars: Pour the melted chocolate over the oat mixture, spreading it evenly with a spatula.

  5. Chill: Refrigerate for at least 2 hours, or until firm. Cut into squares and enjoy!

These no-bake chocolate peanut butter bars are rich, satisfying, and perfect for a quick energy boost.

2. Almond Joy Vegan Bars

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups almond flour
  • 1 cup shredded unsweetened coconut
  • 1/2 cup maple syrup
  • 1/4 cup cocoa powder
  • 1/2 cup almond butter
  • 1/4 cup vegan chocolate chips
  • A pinch of salt

Instructions

  1. Prepare Base: In a mixing bowl, combine almond flour, shredded coconut, maple syrup, cocoa powder, almond butter, and salt until a dough forms.

  2. Press into Pan: Spread the mixture into an even layer in a lined 8x8 inch baking dish.

  3. Melt Chocolate: Melt the vegan chocolate chips using the microwave method described above.

  4. Top with Chocolate: Drizzle or spread the melted chocolate on top of the coconut-almond base.

  5. Chill and Serve: Refrigerate for a couple of hours until firm. Cut into bars and savor the tropical flavors of coconut and almond!

3. Raspberry Coconut Vegan Bars

Ingredients

  • 1 cup oats
  • 1 cup pitted dates
  • 1/2 cup almond butter
  • 1 cup fresh or frozen raspberries
  • 1/2 cup shredded coconut
  • A squeeze of lemon juice
  • A pinch of salt

Instructions

  1. Blend the Base: In a food processor, blend oats and dates until they form a sticky mixture. Add almond butter, salt, and a squeeze of lemon juice, then pulse until combined.

  2. Add Raspberries and Coconut: Gently fold in raspberries and shredded coconut.

  3. Press and Chill: Transfer the mixture to a lined baking dish, pressing down firmly. Refrigerate for about 2 hours.

  4. Cut and Enjoy: Once set, cut into bars and enjoy the refreshing taste of raspberries paired with coconut!

4. Chocolate Chip Cookie Dough Bars

Ingredients

  • 1 cup chickpeas, drained and rinsed
  • 1/2 cup almond butter
  • 1/4 cup maple syrup
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1/2 cup oat flour
  • 1/3 cup vegan chocolate chips
  • A pinch of salt

Instructions

  1. Blend Chickpeas: In a food processor, add chickpeas, almond butter, maple syrup, vanilla extract, and salt. Blend until smooth.

  2. Add Dry Ingredients: Add oat flour and blend again until combined. Stir in chocolate chips.

  3. Press into Pan: Spread the cookie dough mixture into a lined 8x8 inch baking dish.

  4. Chill: Refrigerate for at least an hour before cutting into squares.

These cookie dough bars are a delightful treat that captures the essence of classic cookie dough without the eggs!

5. Matcha Bliss Bars

Ingredients

  • 1 cup cashews (soaked for 2 hours)
  • 1/4 cup maple syrup
  • 1/4 cup coconut oil, melted
  • 1/4 cup almond flour
  • 2 tablespoons matcha powder
  • A pinch of salt

Instructions

  1. Blend Cashews: Drain and rinse the soaked cashews. In a food processor, blend them with maple syrup, melted coconut oil, almond flour, matcha powder, and salt until smooth.

  2. Press Mixture: Pour the mixture into a lined baking dish, smoothing the top.

  3. Freeze: Place in the freezer for about 2 hours, or until firm.

  4. Cut and Serve: Once firm, cut into bars and enjoy the vibrant flavors of matcha!

6. Pumpkin Spice Dessert Bars

Ingredients

  • 1 cup rolled oats
  • 1/2 cup almond flour
  • 1 cup pumpkin puree
  • 1/2 cup maple syrup
  • 1 tsp pumpkin pie spice
  • 1/4 cup walnuts (optional)
  • A pinch of salt

Instructions

  1. Mix Ingredients: In a bowl, combine rolled oats, almond flour, pumpkin puree, maple syrup, pumpkin pie spice, walnuts, and salt until well mixed.

  2. Spread in Pan: Pour the mixture into a lined baking dish and spread evenly.

  3. Bake: Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C) and bake for 25-30 minutes or until golden brown.

  4. Cool and Cut: Allow to cool before cutting into squares. These bars are perfect for fall or any time you crave pumpkin spice!

Conclusion

Vegan dessert bars are an excellent way to indulge in sweet treats while maintaining a healthy lifestyle. With simple ingredients and easy preparation, these recipes can become your go-to options for satisfying your sweet cravings. From the rich flavors of chocolate and peanut butter to the refreshing taste of fruits and nuts, there’s a vegan dessert bar for everyone.

Experiment with these recipes, modify ingredients to your liking, and share them with family and friends. You’ll not only enjoy delicious treats but also promote the joy of plant-based eating. Happy baking!
