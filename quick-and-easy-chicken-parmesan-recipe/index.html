Quick and Easy Chicken Parmesan Recipe


If you’re looking for a delicious and satisfying meal that won’t take hours to prepare, then this quick and easy chicken parmesan recipe is just what you need. With simple ingredients and straightforward steps, you can have a flavorful Italian-inspired dish on the table in no time. Whether you’re cooking for your family or hosting a casual dinner with friends, this recipe is sure to impress without causing you any unnecessary stress.

Ingredients:

  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 1/2 cups breadcrumbs
  • 1 cup grated parmesan cheese
  • 2 cups marinara sauce
  • 1 1/2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Fresh basil leaves for garnish
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Olive oil

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the Oven: Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C) and lightly oil a baking dish to prevent the chicken from sticking.

  2. Prepare the Chicken: Place the chicken breasts between two sheets of plastic wrap and gently pound them to an even thickness, about 1/2 inch. Season both sides with salt and pepper.

  3. Coating the Chicken: Set up three shallow dishes. Place the flour in one, beat the eggs in another, and mix the breadcrumbs and parmesan cheese in the third. Dredge each chicken breast in the flour, shaking off any excess, dip into the beaten eggs, and then coat with the breadcrumb and parmesan mixture, pressing gently to adhere.

  4. Cook the Chicken: In a large skillet, heat a few tablespoons of olive oil over medium-high heat. Add the coated chicken breasts and cook for about 3-4 minutes on each side, or until they are golden brown. Transfer the browned chicken to the prepared baking dish.

  5. Add the Sauce and Cheese: Spoon a generous amount of marinara sauce over each chicken breast, and then sprinkle with shredded mozzarella cheese.

  6. Bake: Place the baking dish in the preheated oven and bake for 20-25 minutes, or until the chicken is cooked through, the cheese is bubbly and golden, and the sauce is hot and bubbly.

  7. Serve: Once the chicken parmesan is cooked, remove it from the oven and let it rest for a few minutes. Garnish with fresh basil leaves before serving.

Tips and Variations:

  • For a lighter version, you can bake the coated chicken breasts instead of pan-frying them.
  • Feel free to add a sprinkle of Italian seasoning or dried oregano to the breadcrumb mixture for an extra flavor boost.
  • Serve the chicken parmesan over a bed of spaghetti or with a side of garlic bread and a crisp green salad for a complete meal.

Conclusion:

This quick and easy chicken parmesan recipe is a perfect go-to option for busy weeknights or when you need to whip up a crowd-pleasing dish in a hurry. With its crispy exterior, tender chicken, gooey cheese, and savory marinara sauce, it’s a classic comfort food that never fails to disappoint. So, the next time you’re in need of a hassle-free yet delicious dinner idea, give this recipe a try and watch it become a new favorite in your household. Enjoy!
