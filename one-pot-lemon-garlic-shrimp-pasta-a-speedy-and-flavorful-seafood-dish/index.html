One-Pot Lemon Garlic Shrimp Pasta: A Speedy and Flavorful Seafood Dish


If you’re looking for a quick and delicious seafood dish that will impress your family and friends, then look no further than this One-Pot Lemon Garlic Shrimp Pasta recipe. Not only is this dish bursting with flavor, but it’s also incredibly easy to make, requiring just one pot for minimal cleanup. Let’s dive into the details of this mouthwatering recipe that’s sure to become a staple in your culinary repertoire.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound of large shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 8 ounces of fettuccine or linguine pasta
  • 4 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1/4 cup of fresh lemon juice
  • 2 tablespoons of olive oil
  • 1/2 teaspoon of red pepper flakes
  • 3 cups of baby spinach
  • 1/2 cup of grated Parmesan cheese
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley for garnish

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Shrimp: Begin by seasoning the shrimp with a pinch of salt and black pepper. Set them aside while you move on to the next steps.

  2. Cook the Pasta: In a large pot or deep skillet, bring water to a boil and cook the pasta according to the package instructions. Once the pasta is al dente, drain it and set it aside.

  3. Sauté the Aromatics: In the same pot or skillet, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the minced garlic and red pepper flakes, sautéing for about 1 minute until the garlic becomes fragrant.

  4. Add the Shrimp: Toss the seasoned shrimp into the pot and cook for 2-3 minutes until they turn pink and opaque.

  5. Incorporate the Spinach and Pasta: Stir in the baby spinach and cooked pasta, ensuring that the spinach wilts and the pasta is well-coated with the flavors in the pot.

  6. Finish with Lemon Juice and Cheese: Pour in the fresh lemon juice and grated Parmesan cheese, stirring to combine all the ingredients. Adjust the seasoning with salt and pepper to your liking.

  7. Serve and Garnish: Once everything is well combined and heated through, it’s time to dish up this delectable One-Pot Lemon Garlic Shrimp Pasta. Garnish with fresh parsley for a pop of color and added freshness.

Serving Suggestions

This dish pairs beautifully with a crisp white wine, such as a Sauvignon Blanc or Pinot Grigio. You can also serve it with a side of garlic bread or a simple green salad to round out the meal. For those who enjoy a bit of heat, a sprinkle of additional red pepper flakes can elevate the flavors even further.

Final Thoughts

In under 30 minutes, you can create a restaurant-quality meal right in your own kitchen. The combination of zesty lemon, pungent garlic, tender shrimp, and al dente pasta makes for a truly satisfying dining experience. Whether you’re cooking for a weeknight dinner or entertaining guests, this One-Pot Lemon Garlic Shrimp Pasta is a recipe that delivers on both speed and flavor. Give it a try and watch as it becomes a fast favorite among your loved ones!

So, there you have it – a simple yet impressive recipe that showcases the best of seafood and pasta in one delightful dish. Enjoy the process of creating this culinary masterpiece and savor every flavorful bite. Happy cooking!
