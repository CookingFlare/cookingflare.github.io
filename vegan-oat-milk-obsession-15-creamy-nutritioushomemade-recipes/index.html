Vegan Oat Milk Obsession: 15 Creamy & NutritiousHomemade Recipes


Ah, oat milk! The creamy, dreamy, and oh-so-nutritious liquid gold that has captured the hearts and taste buds of vegans across the globe. If you love oat milk and are interested in discovering new ways to incorporate it into your daily meals and beverages, then you’ve come to the right place. Feast your eyes on our 15 unique and delectable vegan oat milk recipes!

1. Oat Milk Smoothie Bowl

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup oat milk
  • 1 frozen banana
  • 1/2 cup mixed berries
  • optional: superfood powders like spirulina or maca
  • toppings: granola, nuts, chia seeds, and fresh fruit

Instructions:

  1. Blend the oat milk, banana, mixed berries, and superfood powders (if using) until smooth.
  2. Pour the smoothie into a bowl and top with granola, nuts, chia seeds, and freshly sliced fruit.
  3. Dive in and enjoy your creation!

2. Creamy Vegan Oat Milk Pasta

Ingredients:

  • 200g pasta of choice
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 4 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1/2 cup vegetable broth
  • 1 cup oat milk
  • optional: nutritional yeast, black pepper, and red pepper flakes
  • salt to taste

Instructions:

  1. Cook pasta according to package instructions until al dente.
  2. Heat olive oil in a saucepan over medium heat, then add minced garlic and sauté until fragrant.
  3. Stir in vegetable broth and oat milk, bringing the mixture to a gentle simmer.
  4. Reduce heat to low, add nutritional yeast, black pepper, and red pepper flakes if using, and let it thicken.
  5. Toss cooked pasta in the sauce and season with salt to taste.
  6. Maple syrup to your vegan oat milk pasta endeavor!

3. Oat Milk Banana Bread

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup oat milk
  • 2 ripe bananas, mashed
  • 1/3 cup melted coconut oil
  • 1/2 cup cooked and cooled quinoa (for added protein)
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1/2 cup chopped walnuts
  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 3/4 cup sugar
  • 1 tsp cinnamon

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F and grease a 9x5 inch loaf pan.
  2. In a large bowl, whisk oat milk, mashed bananas, coconut oil, quinoa, and vanilla extract.
  3. In a separate bowl, combine baking soda, flour, sugar, and cinnamon.
  4. Combine wet and dry ingredients, stirring in chopped walnuts.
  5. Pour the batter into the prepared loaf pan and bake for 1 hour, or until a toothpick inserted in the center comes out clean.
  6. Let it cool before slicing. [/code:1]Enjoy the aromatic embrace of your vegan oat milk banana bread!

4. Creamy Sweet Potato Soup

Ingredients:

  • 2 large sweet potatoes, peeled and cubed
  • 1 cup oat milk
  • 2 cups vegetable broth
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1-white onion, diced
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 1/2 cup chopped carrots
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a large pot, heat olive oil over medium heat, then sauté onions and garlic until tender.
  2. Add sweet potatoes, carrots, and vegetable broth, bringing the mixture to a boil.
  3. Reduce heat, cover, and let it simmer until vegetables are tender, about 20 minutes.
  4. Use an immersion blender to puree the soup, or transfer to a blender.
  5. Stir in oat milk, then season with salt and pepper to taste.
  6. Don’t forget to share this luscious vegan oat milk sweet potato soup with loved ones!

5. No-Bake Peanut Butter Oat Milk Bars

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups rolled oats
  • 1 cup oat milk
  • 1/2 cup peanut butter
  • 1/4 cup coconut oil (melted)
  • 1/4 cup honey
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • Pinch of salt
  • Optional: chopped nuts, dried fruit, and a drizzle of dark chocolate

Instructions:

  1. In a large microwave-safe bowl, combine oats, oat milk, peanut butter, coconut oil, and honey. Stir until well combined.
  2. Microwave for 2 minutes and then stir in vanilla extract and salt if using.
  3. Allow mixture to cool for 5 minutes then press into a lined or greased 8x8 inch baking dish.
  4. Optional: For an extra treat, top with chopped nuts, dried fruit, and a drizzle of dark chocolate.
  5. Refrigerate for at least 2 hours, slice, and serve! Enjoy your irresistible vegan oat milk peanut butter bars with a cup of tea.

6. Oat Milk Matcha Latte

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup oat milk
  • 1 tsp matcha powder
  • 1 tbsp honey or maple syrup

Instructions:

  1. In a small saucepan, gently warm your oat milk.
  2. Combine oat milk, matcha powder, and sweetener of choice in a blender and blend until smooth and frothy.
  3. Pour your luscious matcha latte into your favorite mug and sip away your worries.

7. Vegan Chocolate Truffles with Oat Milk Ganache

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup oat milk
  • 1 cup dark chocolate chips
  • 1/2 cup coconut oil
  • 1/4 cup cocoa powder
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract

Instructions:

  1. Warm oat milk then add chocolate chips, stirring occasionally until melted and combined.
  2. Whisk in coconut oil, cocoa powder, and vanilla extract until smooth.
  3. Refrigerate for 2 hours, or until set.
  4. Scoop out a heaping tablespoon of the ganache and roll into truffles.
  5. Enjoy this delectable vegan oat milk chocolate truffle treat with friends and family.

8. Creamy Oat Milk Alfredo Sauce

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup oat milk
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese (can sub nutritional yeast for a completely dairy-free version)
  • Salt to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a saucepan, heat olive oil over medium heat. Add the minced garlic and saute until aromatic.
  2. Slowly whisk in the oat milk and bring it to a simmer; reduce heat and stir in parmesan cheese (or nutritional yeast) and salt until the sauce thickens.
  3. Serve this rich vegan oat milk Alfredo sauce over pasta, vegetables, or your favorite dish.

9. Overnight Oats with Oat Milk Swirl and Fresh Berries

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 cup oats
  • 1 cup oat milk
  • 1/4 cup Greek yogurt (vegan option: coconut or almond yogurt)
  • 2 tbsp maple syrup
  • 1/2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1/4 tsp cinnamon
  • Fresh berries
  • Chopped nuts
  • Optional maple syrup for drizzling

Instructions:

  1. In a jar, combine oats, Greek yogurt, and oat milk. Add maple syrup, vanilla extract, and cinnamon.
  2. Refrigerate overnight, or for at least 4 hours.
  3. In the morning, give the oats a good stir and add your favorite toppings such as fresh berries, chopped nuts, and a drizzle of maple syrup.

10. Vegan Oat Milk Ice Cream

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups oat milk
  • 3/4 cup maple syrup
  • 1/4 cup vegan chocolate chips
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • Optional: toppings like nuts, fruit, sprinkles

Instructions:

  1. In a blender, combine oat milk, maple syrup, chocolate chips, and vanilla extract. Blend until smooth.
  2. Pour the mixture into an ice cream maker and churn until set, following the manufacturer’s instructions (around 25-30 minutes).
  3. Transfer to a freezer-safe container, add toppings if desired, and let it freeze for at least 4 hours before serving.

11. Creamy Vegan Oat Milk Soup

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup rolled oats
  • 1 cup oat milk
  • 4 cups vegetable broth
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 2 carrots, chopped
  • 1 celery stalk, chopped
  • 1/2 cup sliced mushrooms
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Optional: lemon juice

Instructions:

  1. In a large soup pot, sauté onions, garlic, carrots, celery, and mushrooms over medium heat until veggies become soft and tender.
  2. Add rolled oats, vegetable broth, and oat milk to the pot, stirring to combine.
  3. Bring the mixture to a boil, then reduce heat and simmer for 30 minutes.
  4. Taste soup and adjust the seasoning with salt and pepper, add a squeeze of lemon juice if desired.

12. Vegan Oat Milk Barbecue Sauce

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup oat milk
  • 1 cup ketchup
  • 1/4 cup apple cider vinegar
  • 1/4 cup maple syrup
  • 1 tbsp dijon mustard
  • 1 tsp smoked paprika
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 1/2 tsp onion powder

Instructions:

  1. In a saucepan, combine oat milk, ketchup, apple cider vinegar, maple syrup, dijon mustard, smoked paprika, garlic powder, and onion powder.
  2. Whisk ingredients together, then bring the mixture to a simmer.
  3. Once hot, turn the heat off, and let it cool.
  4. Use this vegan oat milk barbecue sauce on your favorite grilled or smoked meats.

13. Oat Milk Coconut Curry

Ingredients:

  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tbsp freshly grated ginger
  • 1 can (14 oz) coconut milk
  • 1/2 cup oat milk
  • 2 tbsp curry powder
  • 1 tbsp ground coriander
  • Salt to taste

Instructions:

  1. Heat the olive oil in a large pan over medium heat, then sauté onions, garlic, and ginger until fragrant and soft.
  2. Whisk the coconut milk and oat milk together, and pour it into the pan.
  3. Stir in the curry powder, coriander, and salt, then let the mixture simmer for 10 minutes.
  4. Serve this creamy vegan oat milk coconut curry over rice, quinoa, or your favorite grain.

14. Vegan Oat Milk Latte Equation

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup oat milk
  • 1 tsp espresso or strong coffee
  • 1 tbsp maple syrup (or sweetener of choice)
  • Optional: vegan whipped cream or a sprinkle of cinnamon

Instructions:

  1. In a small saucepan, gently warm oat milk.
  2. Add espresso or coffee, maple syrup, and optional vegan whipped cream or cinnamon to a mug.
  3. Top off with the warm oat milk and enjoy!

15. Vegan Oat Milk Chocolate Ganache

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup oat milk
  • 1 cup dark chocolate chips
  • 1/4 cup maple syrup
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract

Instructions:

  1. In a saucepan, gently heat oat milk then add chocolate chips and maple syrup, stirring until completely smooth.
  2. Whisk in the vanilla extract and let ganache cool to room temperature.
  3. Pour the ganache over a cake, cupcakes, or your favorite dessert for a luscious vegan oat milk chocolate ganache treatment.

There you have it: an incredible selection of vegan oat milk recipes to seamlessly blend into your daily routine. They are all delicious, creamy, and comforting, each unique in their very own way. Oat milk has never been so versatile and satisfying, so go ahead and indulge in these delectable concoctions.
