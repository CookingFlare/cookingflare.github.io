BBQ Chicken Nachos: Crowd-Pleasing Recipes


If you’re looking for a show-stopping appetizer or a deliciously indulgent snack, BBQ Chicken Nachos are the answer. This crowd-pleasing dish combines the irresistible flavors of barbecue chicken, gooey melted cheese, fresh veggies, and crispy tortilla chips. Whether you’re hosting a game night, a casual get-together, or simply craving a flavorful treat, these BBQ Chicken Nachos are sure to be a hit. In this blog post, we’ll explore a couple of mouth-watering variations of this beloved dish, along with tips for perfecting each element.

Classic BBQ Chicken Nachos

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups shredded cooked chicken
  • 1 cup barbecue sauce
  • 1 bag of tortilla chips
  • 2 cups shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1 cup diced tomatoes
  • 1/2 cup diced red onion
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh cilantro
  • 1/2 cup sour cream
  • 1/4 cup thinly sliced green onions

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. In a bowl, mix the shredded chicken with barbecue sauce until well coated.
  3. Arrange a layer of tortilla chips on a large baking sheet.
  4. Sprinkle half of the shredded cheese over the chips.
  5. Spread the barbecue chicken evenly over the cheese-topped chips.
  6. Add another layer of tortilla chips on top of the chicken.
  7. Sprinkle the remaining shredded cheese over the second layer of chips.
  8. Place the baking sheet in the preheated oven and bake for 10-15 minutes, or until the cheese is melted and bubbly.
  9. Remove the nachos from the oven and sprinkle diced tomatoes, red onion, and cilantro over the top.
  10. Drizzle sour cream and sprinkle green onions over the assembled nachos.
  11. Serve immediately and enjoy!

Loaded BBQ Chicken Nachos

For those who love a little extra indulgence, Loaded BBQ Chicken Nachos take the classic recipe to a whole new level.

Additional Ingredients:

  • 1/2 cup cooked and crumbled bacon
  • 1/2 cup pickled jalapeños
  • 1/4 cup sliced black olives
  • 1/4 cup diced red bell pepper
  • 1/4 cup sliced avocado
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley

Simply follow the instructions for the Classic BBQ Chicken Nachos, and once the nachos are out of the oven, sprinkle the additional ingredients over the top for a flavor explosion that will leave your guests coming back for more.

Tips for Perfect BBQ Chicken Nachos

  • Customize the Heat: Adjust the level of spiciness by choosing a milder or hotter barbecue sauce, and add more or fewer jalapeños to suit your preference.
  • Prevent Soggy Nachos: To avoid soggy nachos, try layering the chips and toppings for better distribution and optimal crispiness.
  • Make it Ahead: Prepare the shredded chicken and toppings in advance, then assemble and bake the nachos just before serving for a stress-free experience.

BBQ Chicken Nachos are a versatile and satisfying dish that brings people together over a shared love of delicious food. Whether you opt for the classic recipe or go all out with loaded toppings, these nachos are guaranteed to be a hit at any gathering. So, gather your ingredients, preheat your oven, and get ready to delight your guests with a sensational batch of BBQ Chicken Nachos!
