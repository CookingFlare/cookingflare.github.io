10 Easy Sandwiches You Can Make in Under 15 Minutes


Sandwiches are the ultimate comfort food, versatile and quick to prepare, making them perfect for breakfast, lunch, dinner, or even a snack. Whether you’re in a rush or looking for a tasty meal without much fuss, there’s a sandwich out there that’s just right for you. In this post, we’ll explore 10 easy sandwiches that you can make in under 15 minutes, perfect for busy mornings or lazy afternoons. These recipes are packed with flavor, creativity, and all the ingredients you probably already have at home. Let’s dive in!

1. Classic BLT (Bacon, Lettuce, and Tomato)

A BLT is a timeless classic, and for good reason—it’s simple, satisfying, and packed with flavors that work together perfectly.

Ingredients:

  • 2 slices of bread (white, whole wheat, or your favorite variety)
  • 3-4 slices of crispy bacon
  • Fresh lettuce (romaine or iceberg)
  • Tomato, sliced
  • Mayonnaise
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Cook the bacon until it’s crispy. You can do this on the stovetop or in the microwave for quicker results.
  2. Toast the slices of bread until they are golden brown.
  3. Spread mayonnaise generously on one side of each slice of bread.
  4. Layer on the crispy bacon, followed by fresh lettuce and tomato slices.
  5. Season with a pinch of salt and pepper.
  6. Close the sandwich, slice, and serve.

This sandwich is so quick and easy, it’s perfect for breakfast, lunch, or even a light dinner.

2. Avocado Toast Sandwich

If you’re looking for a healthy yet indulgent sandwich, avocado toast with a twist is a must-try. The creamy avocado pairs beautifully with toasted bread, and you can add extra toppings to make it more flavorful.

Ingredients:

  • 2 slices of bread (sourdough, rye, or multigrain)
  • 1 ripe avocado
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Lemon juice
  • Red pepper flakes (optional)
  • Olive oil (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Toast your slices of bread until they’re crisp and golden brown.
  2. In a bowl, mash the ripe avocado with a fork until smooth.
  3. Add a squeeze of lemon juice, salt, and pepper to taste.
  4. Spread the avocado mixture generously on one slice of the toasted bread.
  5. For added flavor, drizzle a little olive oil on top and sprinkle with red pepper flakes.
  6. Place the second slice of bread on top, slice, and enjoy.

This sandwich is not only quick to make but also packed with healthy fats and vitamins, making it a great option for a nutritious lunch or snack.

3. Grilled Cheese Sandwich

A grilled cheese sandwich is always a comforting option, and when you need something quick and easy, it’s the perfect go-to meal. You can add a variety of cheeses for extra flavor, but the classic version is unbeatable.

Ingredients:

  • 2 slices of bread (white, whole wheat, or any bread you prefer)
  • 2 slices of cheese (cheddar, mozzarella, or American)
  • Butter

Instructions:

  1. Butter one side of each slice of bread.
  2. Place the cheese slices between the two slices of bread, buttered side facing outward.
  3. Heat a skillet over medium heat and place the sandwich in the pan.
  4. Grill the sandwich for about 3-4 minutes on each side until golden brown and the cheese is melted.
  5. Slice and serve immediately with a side of pickles or tomato soup.

The grilled cheese is a warm, cheesy delight that takes just minutes to prepare, making it the ultimate comfort food.

4. Turkey and Cranberry Sandwich

If you’re craving something a little different, a turkey and cranberry sandwich offers the perfect balance of savory and sweet flavors. It’s great for using up leftover turkey or for a quick deli-style sandwich.

Ingredients:

  • 2 slices of bread (whole grain or your choice)
  • 3-4 slices of turkey breast
  • Cranberry sauce
  • Leafy greens (spinach or arugula works well)
  • Cream cheese (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Spread a thin layer of cranberry sauce on one slice of bread.
  2. Add a layer of turkey slices on top of the cranberry sauce.
  3. Add some leafy greens for a fresh, crunchy bite.
  4. If desired, spread a thin layer of cream cheese on the other slice of bread for a creamy touch.
  5. Close the sandwich and serve immediately.

This sandwich is perfect for fall and can be enjoyed year-round, especially when you want something a little more festive.

5. Egg Salad Sandwich

An egg salad sandwich is a hearty, protein-packed option that’s quick to prepare and always satisfying. It’s a great way to use up hard-boiled eggs and create a delicious, creamy filling.

Ingredients:

  • 2 slices of bread (white, whole wheat, or rye)
  • 3 hard-boiled eggs, chopped
  • 2 tablespoons mayonnaise
  • 1 teaspoon mustard
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh dill (optional)

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, combine the chopped hard-boiled eggs with mayonnaise, mustard, salt, and pepper.
  2. Mix everything together until well combined and creamy.
  3. Spread the egg salad mixture on one slice of bread.
  4. Top with the second slice of bread, slice, and enjoy.

This sandwich is incredibly filling and perfect for lunch or a light dinner.

6. Hummus and Veggie Sandwich

For a vegetarian option, a hummus and veggie sandwich is both nutritious and delicious. You can load it up with a variety of fresh vegetables and enjoy it with a creamy hummus spread.

Ingredients:

  • 2 slices of bread (whole grain or pita)
  • 2-3 tablespoons of hummus
  • Sliced cucumber
  • Sliced tomatoes
  • Lettuce or spinach
  • Red onion slices (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Spread a generous layer of hummus on one slice of bread.
  2. Layer the cucumber, tomato, lettuce, and red onion on top.
  3. Season with a pinch of salt and pepper.
  4. Top with the second slice of bread and slice.

This sandwich is fresh, light, and packed with nutrients, making it a great choice for lunch or a quick snack.

7. Chicken Caesar Sandwich

A Chicken Caesar sandwich is a delicious twist on the classic Caesar salad. It’s a great way to enjoy the flavors of a Caesar salad in a sandwich form.

Ingredients:

  • 2 slices of bread (preferably a baguette or ciabatta)
  • 1 cooked chicken breast, sliced
  • Romaine lettuce, chopped
  • Caesar dressing
  • Grated Parmesan cheese
  • Croutons (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Slice the cooked chicken breast into thin pieces.
  2. Toss the chopped lettuce with Caesar dressing.
  3. Layer the lettuce, chicken slices, and grated Parmesan cheese on one slice of bread.
  4. If desired, add a few croutons for extra crunch.
  5. Top with the second slice of bread, slice, and serve.

This sandwich is a great way to enjoy the flavors of Caesar salad in a more portable and satisfying form.

8. Caprese Sandwich

A Caprese sandwich brings the flavors of Italy straight to your plate. With fresh mozzarella, tomatoes, basil, and a drizzle of balsamic glaze, it’s the perfect sandwich for a light and fresh meal.

Ingredients:

  • 2 slices of ciabatta or baguette
  • Fresh mozzarella, sliced
  • Tomato, sliced
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • Balsamic glaze
  • Olive oil (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Slice the ciabatta or baguette into two pieces.
  2. Layer the fresh mozzarella slices on one slice of bread.
  3. Add the tomato slices and fresh basil leaves.
  4. Drizzle with balsamic glaze and a little olive oil if desired.
  5. Top with the second slice of bread, slice, and enjoy.

This sandwich is refreshing, flavorful, and perfect for a quick lunch or a light dinner.

9. Peanut Butter and Banana Sandwich

For something sweet and satisfying, a peanut butter and banana sandwich is an excellent choice. The combination of creamy peanut butter and sweet banana makes for a tasty, energy-boosting meal.

Ingredients:

  • 2 slices of bread (whole wheat or white)
  • 2 tablespoons peanut butter
  • 1 ripe banana, sliced

Instructions:

  1. Spread peanut butter on one slice of bread.
  2. Layer the banana slices on top of the peanut butter.
  3. Close the sandwich with the second slice of bread, slice, and serve.

This sandwich is perfect for a quick breakfast, a midday snack, or a post-workout pick-me-up.

10. Tuna Salad Sandwich

A tuna salad sandwich is an all-time favorite, packed with protein and flavor. It’s simple to make and perfect for any time of the day.

Ingredients:

  • 2 slices of bread (whole wheat, rye, or your preferred choice)
  • 1 can of tuna, drained
  • 2 tablespoons mayonnaise
  • 1 tablespoon Dijon mustard
  • Celery, finely chopped (optional)
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, combine the drained tuna, mayonnaise, mustard, and finely chopped celery (if using).
  2. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  3. Spread the tuna salad on one slice of bread.
  4. Top with the second slice of bread, slice, and enjoy.

This sandwich is a quick, hearty meal that’s both delicious and satisfying.

Final Thoughts

These 10 easy sandwiches are perfect for when you’re short on time but craving something delicious. Each one can be made in under 15 minutes, and they offer a variety of flavors and ingredients to suit all preferences. Whether you’re in the mood for something savory, sweet, or a combination of both, these sandwiches are sure to hit the spot. So, next time you’re in a rush or just want a quick, tasty meal, give one of these recipes a try—you won’t be disappointed!
