Brunch Cocktails with Cider: Crisp and Refreshing Ideas


Brunch is the perfect occasion to gather with friends and family, indulge in delicious food, and enjoy a light, refreshing cocktail. When it comes to brunch cocktails, there’s something uniquely delightful about using cider as the base. Cider offers a crisp, slightly tangy flavor that pairs wonderfully with a variety of fruits, herbs, and spirits, making it the ideal ingredient for creating vibrant and enjoyable drinks.

If you’re looking for something more than the usual mimosas or Bloody Marys, cider-based cocktails offer an exciting twist. These drinks are not only refreshing but also versatile, allowing for both light and bold flavors that complement the laid-back atmosphere of brunch. Whether you’re hosting a brunch gathering or simply treating yourself to a leisurely morning drink, cider cocktails will certainly elevate the experience.

In this post, we’ll explore a variety of brunch cocktails with cider, each designed to be crisp, refreshing, and full of flavor. From fruity concoctions to herb-infused twists, these drinks are perfect for any brunch celebration. Let’s dive into some exciting ideas for your next brunch cocktail menu!

1. Apple Cider Mimosa

A twist on the classic mimosa, the Apple Cider Mimosa brings the warmth and tartness of cider together with the light effervescence of champagne. This cocktail is a perfect balance of sweet and tangy, offering a refreshing start to any brunch. The smoothness of the cider pairs wonderfully with the crisp bubbles of champagne, creating a lively and refreshing drink.

Ingredients:

  • 2 ounces apple cider (preferably unfiltered for a fuller flavor)
  • 2 ounces chilled champagne or sparkling wine
  • 1 teaspoon maple syrup (optional, for a touch of sweetness)
  • Apple slices (for garnish)
  • Cinnamon stick (optional, for garnish)

Instructions:

  1. Pour the apple cider into a flute glass, filling it about halfway.
  2. Slowly top with the champagne or sparkling wine until the glass is almost full.
  3. If desired, add a teaspoon of maple syrup for an extra hint of sweetness.
  4. Stir gently to combine.
  5. Garnish with a thin apple slice or a cinnamon stick for an aromatic touch.

This drink is both visually stunning and deliciously balanced, perfect for those who enjoy a slightly sweeter, more aromatic brunch cocktail.

2. Cider Sangria

Sangria isn’t just for summer – it can be just as delightful at brunch with the addition of cider. This Cider Sangria combines seasonal fruits, a touch of brandy, and cider for a bright and flavorful beverage. The fruits you choose can vary depending on the season, but apples, oranges, and pears are perfect options for adding a refreshing burst of natural sweetness to the drink.

Ingredients:

  • 1 bottle dry cider (750 ml)
  • 1/4 cup brandy
  • 1/4 cup orange liqueur (like Triple Sec or Cointreau)
  • 1 apple, thinly sliced
  • 1 pear, thinly sliced
  • 1 orange, thinly sliced
  • A few sprigs of fresh thyme or rosemary
  • Ice

Instructions:

  1. In a large pitcher or bowl, combine the cider, brandy, and orange liqueur.
  2. Add the thinly sliced apple, pear, and orange.
  3. Stir in the sprigs of thyme or rosemary for a hint of herbal fragrance.
  4. Let the sangria sit in the fridge for at least an hour, allowing the flavors to meld together.
  5. Serve the sangria over ice, garnished with additional fruit slices or herbs if desired.

This Cider Sangria is a fantastic option for a larger brunch gathering, as it’s easy to make in advance and always a crowd-pleaser. It’s both light and flavorful, with just enough complexity to make each sip interesting.

3. Cider Mojito

If you love mojitos but are looking for a more autumn-inspired twist, the Cider Mojito is a great choice. The combination of fresh mint, lime, and cider makes for a drink that’s both refreshing and slightly spiced. The cider adds an additional layer of flavor that pairs beautifully with the crisp mint and tart lime.

Ingredients:

  • 2 ounces apple cider
  • 1 ounce white rum
  • 1/2 ounce lime juice (freshly squeezed)
  • 6-8 fresh mint leaves
  • 1/2 ounce simple syrup (or to taste)
  • Ice
  • Lime wheel (for garnish)
  • Mint sprig (for garnish)

Instructions:

  1. Muddle the mint leaves and lime juice together in the bottom of a glass to release the mint’s oils.
  2. Add the white rum, apple cider, and simple syrup to the glass.
  3. Fill the glass with ice and stir to combine.
  4. Garnish with a lime wheel and a sprig of fresh mint for a refreshing touch.

The Cider Mojito is perfect for those who enjoy a cocktail that’s both invigorating and herbaceous. It’s a drink that’s easy to make and sure to be a hit at any brunch table.

4. Cider Whiskey Sour

For a more robust cocktail, the Cider Whiskey Sour brings together the rich flavor of whiskey with the crispness of cider and the tang of lemon. This cocktail strikes a perfect balance between the deep warmth of whiskey and the bright, refreshing notes of cider, making it an ideal choice for brunch if you’re looking for something a little more complex.

Ingredients:

  • 2 ounces whiskey (bourbon works great)
  • 1 ounce apple cider
  • 3/4 ounce lemon juice (freshly squeezed)
  • 1/2 ounce simple syrup (or to taste)
  • Ice
  • Lemon twist (for garnish)

Instructions:

  1. In a cocktail shaker, combine the whiskey, apple cider, lemon juice, and simple syrup.
  2. Fill the shaker with ice and shake vigorously for 15 seconds.
  3. Strain the cocktail into a glass filled with fresh ice.
  4. Garnish with a twist of lemon for an aromatic touch.

The Cider Whiskey Sour has a perfect balance of tart, sweet, and savory, making it an excellent drink for those who prefer stronger spirits with a refreshing twist.

5. Spiced Cider Mule

If you love Moscow Mules, then the Spiced Cider Mule is a must-try. This drink uses the warming flavors of ginger beer combined with spiced apple cider to create a cocktail that’s perfect for cooler mornings. The addition of fresh lime juice and a splash of vodka ties everything together for a vibrant, zingy drink.

Ingredients:

  • 2 ounces vodka
  • 4 ounces spiced apple cider
  • 4 ounces ginger beer
  • 1/2 ounce lime juice (freshly squeezed)
  • Ice
  • Lime wedge (for garnish)
  • Cinnamon stick (for garnish)

Instructions:

  1. In a copper mug or glass, combine the vodka, spiced apple cider, and lime juice.
  2. Fill the mug with ice and stir gently to combine.
  3. Top with ginger beer and stir again.
  4. Garnish with a lime wedge and a cinnamon stick for a festive touch.

The Spiced Cider Mule is the ultimate combination of sweet, spicy, and tart, with just the right amount of kick from the vodka and ginger beer. It’s a perfect choice for a brunch cocktail that’s both satisfying and refreshing.

6. Cider Bellini

For those who enjoy a more sophisticated brunch drink, the Cider Bellini is a wonderful alternative to the traditional mimosa. This cocktail combines cider with fruit puree (peach, in particular) to create a smooth, slightly sweet drink that’s both elegant and refreshing.

Ingredients:

  • 2 ounces apple cider
  • 2 ounces peach puree (or puree of your choice)
  • 2 ounces chilled sparkling wine or champagne
  • Peach slice (for garnish)

Instructions:

  1. In a flute glass, combine the apple cider and fruit puree.
  2. Slowly top with sparkling wine or champagne until the glass is full.
  3. Stir gently to combine.
  4. Garnish with a slice of peach for a touch of elegance.

The Cider Bellini is a refined brunch cocktail that adds a fruity twist to your typical sparkling wine drinks. It’s perfect for those who appreciate a lighter, more delicate flavor.

Conclusion

Cider is a fantastic base for brunch cocktails due to its versatility, crispness, and ability to pair well with a variety of ingredients. Whether you prefer something light and bubbly, rich and spicy, or herbaceous and refreshing, there’s a cider-based cocktail for every palate. The drinks we’ve explored here are perfect for enhancing any brunch experience, offering a refreshing balance of flavors that will have your guests coming back for more.

Next time you’re planning a brunch gathering, skip the typical mimosas and try one of these exciting cider cocktails. From the bright and fruity Apple Cider Mimosa to the more robust Cider Whiskey Sour, these cocktails are sure to bring something new and delightful to the table. Cheers to great company and delicious drinks!
