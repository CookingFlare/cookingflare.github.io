Greek-style Lemon and Herb Quinoa Salad: A Light and Nutritious Dish


Are you looking for a refreshing and healthy dish that’s bursting with Mediterranean flavors? Look no further than this Greek-style Lemon and Herb Quinoa Salad! Packed with protein-rich quinoa, vibrant herbs, and zesty lemon, this salad is a perfect option for a light lunch, a side dish, or a satisfying main course. Let’s dive into the details of this delightful recipe and explore the tantalizing blend of flavors and textures that make it so irresistible.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup quinoa
  • 2 cups water or vegetable broth
  • 1 cucumber, diced
  • 1 pint cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1/2 red onion, finely chopped
  • 1/2 cup Kalamata olives, pitted and sliced
  • 1/4 cup fresh parsley, chopped
  • 1/4 cup fresh mint, chopped
  • 1/3 cup extra virgin olive oil
  • 1/4 cup freshly squeezed lemon juice
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Crumbled feta cheese (optional, for garnish)

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Quinoa: Rinse the quinoa under cold water using a fine-mesh strainer. In a medium saucepan, bring the water or vegetable broth to a boil. Add the quinoa, reduce the heat to low, cover, and simmer for 15-20 minutes, or until the quinoa is tender and the liquid is absorbed. Once cooked, fluff the quinoa with a fork and transfer it to a large mixing bowl to cool.

  2. Prepare the Dressing: In a small bowl, whisk together the olive oil, lemon juice, minced garlic, dried oregano, salt, and pepper to create the flavorful dressing.

  3. Combine the Ingredients: Add the diced cucumber, halved cherry tomatoes, chopped red onion, sliced Kalamata olives, fresh parsley, and fresh mint to the bowl with the cooled quinoa.

  4. Toss with the Dressing: Pour the prepared dressing over the quinoa and vegetable mixture. Gently toss the salad until all the ingredients are evenly coated with the dressing.

  5. Chill and Serve: Cover the salad and refrigerate for at least 30 minutes to allow the flavors to meld together. Before serving, give the salad a final toss and adjust the seasoning if necessary. If desired, garnish with crumbled feta cheese for an extra burst of flavor.

Nutritional Benefits

This Greek-style Lemon and Herb Quinoa Salad is not only a culinary delight but also a nutritional powerhouse. Quinoa is a complete protein, making it an excellent choice for vegetarians and vegans. It’s also rich in fiber, iron, and magnesium, contributing to a healthy digestive system and overall well-being. The abundance of fresh vegetables and herbs in this salad provides a generous dose of vitamins, antioxidants, and phytonutrients, supporting immune function and promoting vibrant health.

Serving Suggestions

This versatile salad can be enjoyed in various ways. Serve it as a light lunch alongside grilled chicken or fish for a complete and satisfying meal. It also makes a delightful side dish for barbecues, picnics, or potlucks. For a heartier option, consider adding grilled shrimp or tofu to create a protein-packed entrée. The bright and invigorating flavors of this salad make it a perfect addition to any summer gathering or as a quick and nourishing weekday meal.

Final Thoughts

The Greek-style Lemon and Herb Quinoa Salad is a celebration of simplicity and freshness. With its vibrant colors, robust flavors, and nutrient-dense ingredients, this dish embodies the essence of Mediterranean cuisine.
