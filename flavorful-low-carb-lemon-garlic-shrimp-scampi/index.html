Flavorful Low Carb Lemon Garlic Shrimp Scampi


If you’re on the lookout for a dish that’s bursting with flavor and low in carbs, then this Lemon Garlic Shrimp Scampi recipe is a must-try. Packed with the zesty tang of lemon and the savory aroma of garlic, this dish is a delightful combination of freshness and indulgence. Plus, it’s low in carbs, making it a perfect choice for those who are mindful of their carb intake. Without further ado, let’s dive into the recipe and discover how to create this delectable dish in the comfort of your own kitchen.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound of large shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 4 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1/4 cup of fresh lemon juice
  • 1/4 cup of chicken broth
  • 2 tablespoons of unsalted butter
  • 2 tablespoons of olive oil
  • 1/4 teaspoon of red pepper flakes
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • Chopped fresh parsley for garnish
  • Zoodles or cauliflower rice for serving (optional)

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Shrimp: Begin by patting the shrimp dry with paper towels and seasoning them with a pinch of salt and black pepper.

  2. Sauté the Shrimp: In a large skillet, heat the olive oil over medium-high heat. Add the seasoned shrimp to the skillet and cook for 1-2 minutes on each side until they turn pink and opaque. Once done, transfer the shrimp to a plate and set them aside.

  3. Create the Scampi Sauce: In the same skillet, melt the butter over medium heat. Add the minced garlic and red pepper flakes, and sauté for about 1 minute until the garlic becomes fragrant.

  4. Add the Liquid Ingredients: Pour in the chicken broth and fresh lemon juice, stirring gently to combine all the flavors. Let the sauce simmer for 2-3 minutes until it slightly reduces.

  5. Combine the Shrimp and Sauce: Return the cooked shrimp to the skillet, tossing them in the flavorful scampi sauce until they are evenly coated. Cook for an additional 1-2 minutes to ensure the shrimp are heated through.

  6. Serve and Garnish: Once the shrimp are fully coated with the sauce, remove the skillet from the heat. Garnish the dish with chopped fresh parsley for a pop of color and freshness.

  7. Optional Serving Suggestion: For a low-carb twist, serve the lemon garlic shrimp scampi over a bed of zoodles or cauliflower rice, allowing the vibrant flavors to shine through without the added carbs.

Enjoy Your Flavorful Creation

There you have it – a tantalizing, low-carb Lemon Garlic Shrimp Scampi that’s sure to impress your taste buds. With its vibrant citrusy notes and the comforting aroma of garlic, this dish is a delightful symphony of flavors. Whether you’re cooking for a special occasion or simply treating yourself to a gourmet meal, this recipe is a winner.

So, why not embark on a culinary adventure and whip up this delightful Lemon Garlic Shrimp Scampi? It’s a mouthwatering way to savor the goodness of seafood while keeping your carb intake in check. Give it a try and enjoy the culinary journey of creating this flavorsome dish right in your own kitchen!
