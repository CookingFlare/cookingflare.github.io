Tarragon Treats: Delightful Fresh Herb Recipes


Tarragon is a versatile herb that adds a unique flavor and aroma to dishes. Its delicate and slightly bittersweet taste pairs well with a variety of foods, making it a favorite among chefs and home cooks alike. In this blog post, we will explore some delightful fresh herb recipes that highlight the beauty of tarragon. So, let’s dive right in!

1. Tarragon Chicken Salad

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups cooked chicken, shredded
  • 1/2 cup mayonnaise
  • 1 tablespoon Dijon mustard
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 1/4 cup finely chopped red onion
  • 2 tablespoons chopped fresh tarragon
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a large bowl, combine the shredded chicken, mayonnaise, Dijon mustard, and lemon juice. Mix well until all the ingredients are evenly coated.
  2. Add the chopped red onion and fresh tarragon to the bowl. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  3. Gently fold all the ingredients together until well combined.
  4. Serve the tarragon chicken salad on a bed of lettuce or as a sandwich filling. Enjoy the delightful flavors of tarragon in every bite!

2. Tarragon Buttered Shrimp

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 4 tablespoons unsalted butter
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons chopped fresh tarragon
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Heat a large skillet over medium heat and melt the butter.
  2. Add the minced garlic to the skillet and sauté for about a minute until fragrant.
  3. Add the shrimp to the skillet and cook for 2-3 minutes on each side until they turn pink and opaque.
  4. Sprinkle the chopped fresh tarragon over the shrimp and season with salt and pepper to taste. Stir well to coat the shrimp evenly.
  5. Cook for an additional minute to allow the flavors to meld together.
  6. Serve the tarragon buttered shrimp as an appetizer or as a main course with a side of rice or pasta. Your taste buds will thank you!

3. Tarragon Infused Lemonade

Ingredients:

  • 4 cups water
  • 1 cup freshly squeezed lemon juice
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/4 cup fresh tarragon leaves, lightly crushed
  • Ice cubes for serving

Instructions:

  1. In a saucepan, combine the water, lemon juice, and granulated sugar. Stir well until the sugar is dissolved.
  2. Add the fresh tarragon leaves to the saucepan and bring the mixture to a boil over medium heat.
  3. Reduce the heat to low and let the tarragon infuse into the lemonade for about 10 minutes.
  4. Remove the saucepan from the heat and strain the lemonade to remove the tarragon leaves.
  5. Allow the lemonade to cool to room temperature, then refrigerate until chilled.
  6. Serve the tarragon infused lemonade over ice cubes for a refreshing and flavorful drink. It’s the perfect beverage for a hot summer day!

Conclusion

Tarragon is undoubtedly a herb that brings a delightful twist to any dish. Whether you’re a seasoned chef or a beginner cook, these fresh herb recipes will allow you to explore the versatility of tarragon and enhance your culinary skills. From the tangy tarragon chicken salad to the succulent tarragon buttered shrimp, and the refreshing tarragon infused lemonade, these recipes are sure to impress your taste buds and leave you craving for more. So, go ahead and embrace the magic of tarragon in your kitchen. Happy cooking!
