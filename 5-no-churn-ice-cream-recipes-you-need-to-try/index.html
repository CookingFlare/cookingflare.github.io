5 No-Churn Ice Cream Recipes You Need to Try


Nothing beats the creamy indulgence of ice cream, especially during warmer months, but sometimes the thought of hauling out an ice cream maker can make homemade treats seem like more effort than they’re worth. That’s where no-churn ice cream steps in—a quick, hassle-free solution for creating luxurious frozen desserts with minimal equipment and maximum flavor. If you’ve never tried your hand at no-churn ice cream, get ready to discover five incredible recipes that will not only impress your taste buds but also wow anyone you share them with.

No-churn ice cream is a game-changer for those who love homemade desserts. The beauty of these recipes lies in their simplicity, requiring no special equipment beyond a bowl, a whisk, and a freezer. With just a few ingredients and a bit of creativity, you can whip up desserts that rival your favorite ice cream shop. Here are five irresistible recipes that we promise will become household favorites.

1. Classic Vanilla No-Churn Ice Cream

Sometimes, simplicity speaks volumes. This classic vanilla no-churn ice cream delivers all the creamy goodness of traditional ice cream without needing a fancy machine. Perfect as a base to pair with brownies, pies, or fruit—or even just on its own.

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups heavy cream
  • 1 cup sweetened condensed milk
  • 1 tablespoon pure vanilla extract

Instructions:

  1. In a large mixing bowl, whip the heavy cream until stiff peaks form.
  2. Slowly drizzle in the sweetened condensed milk while gently folding it into the whipped cream.
  3. Add the vanilla extract and give the mixture one final fold to incorporate the flavor throughout.
  4. Transfer the mixture to a freezer-safe container, smoothing the surface with a spatula. Cover with a lid or plastic wrap.
  5. Freeze for 4–6 hours or overnight until firm. Scoop and serve!

Tip: Enhance this base by mixing in crushed cookies, chocolate chips, or swirls of caramel just before freezing.

2. Decadent Chocolate No-Churn Ice Cream

Chocolate lovers, rejoice! This rich and creamy no-churn chocolate ice cream is every cocoa enthusiast’s dream. Its smooth texture and bold flavor make it a standout treat.

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups heavy cream
  • 1 cup sweetened condensed milk
  • 1/2 cup cocoa powder
  • 1 teaspoon espresso powder (optional, for enhanced chocolate flavor)

Instructions:

  1. Whip the heavy cream in a large bowl until you achieve stiff peaks.
  2. In a separate bowl, mix the sweetened condensed milk, cocoa powder, and espresso powder until smooth.
  3. Gradually fold the chocolate mixture into the whipped cream, ensuring no streaks remain.
  4. Pour the mixture into a freezer-safe container and cover tightly.
  5. Freeze for 4–6 hours or overnight.

Serving Suggestion: Top with shaved chocolate or drizzle with hot fudge for an extra indulgent experience.

3. Strawberry Cheesecake No-Churn Ice Cream

Take your dessert to new heights with this creamy and tangy strawberry cheesecake ice cream. Featuring bursts of fruity freshness balanced with the smooth flavor of cream cheese, this recipe is summer in every bite.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1 cup sweetened condensed milk
  • 4 ounces cream cheese, softened
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 cup fresh strawberries, chopped

Instructions:

  1. In a blender or food processor, purée the fresh strawberries until smooth. Set aside.
  2. In a mixing bowl, beat the cream cheese until light and fluffy. Stir in the sweetened condensed milk and vanilla extract until fully combined.
  3. Whip the heavy cream in a separate bowl until stiff peaks form, then gently fold it into the cream cheese mixture.
  4. Swirl the strawberry purée into the mixture, leaving ripples for a beautiful marbled effect.
  5. Transfer to a container, cover, and freeze for at least 4 hours.

Pro Tip: Add graham cracker crumbs or chunks for an authentic cheesecake crunch.

4. Salted Caramel Pretzel No-Churn Ice Cream

Sweet and savory are a match made in heaven, and this salted caramel pretzel no-churn ice cream captures it flawlessly. Crunchy pretzel bits and rich caramel swirls create layers of complex flavor and texture.

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups heavy cream
  • 1 cup sweetened condensed milk
  • 1/2 cup salted caramel sauce
  • 1 cup pretzels, roughly crushed

Instructions:

  1. Whip the heavy cream until stiff peaks form and set aside.
  2. Mix the sweetened condensed milk with half of the salted caramel sauce in a separate bowl.
  3. Slowly fold the caramel mixture into the whipped cream, leaving it smooth and creamy.
  4. Gently fold in half of the crushed pretzels.
  5. Pour the mixture into a freezer-safe container, then drizzle the remaining caramel sauce on top and sprinkle the remaining pretzels for texture.
  6. Cover and freeze for 6–8 hours for maximum crunch and flavor.

Serving Tip: Serve alongside chocolate-dipped pretzel rods for a cohesive treat.

5. Coconut Mango No-Churn Ice Cream

Transport your taste buds to the tropics with this coconut mango ice cream. It’s refreshing, slightly fruity, and creamy, with a texture as smooth as a summer breeze.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1 cup coconut cream
  • 1 cup sweetened condensed milk
  • 2 ripe mangoes, puréed

Instructions:

  1. Blend the mangoes until smooth and set aside.
  2. In a mixing bowl, whisk the coconut cream and sweetened condensed milk together until homogeneous.
  3. Whip the heavy cream in a separate bowl until stiff peaks form, then fold it gently into the coconut mixture.
  4. Swirl in the mango purée, leaving ribbons for visual appeal.
  5. Pour into a container, cover, and freeze for at least 4 hours before serving.

Garnishing Idea: Sprinkle toasted coconut flakes on top for added crunch and depth of flavor.

No-Churn Ice Cream Hacks to Elevate Your Treats

While these recipes are divine on their own, you can make your no-churn ice cream creations even more unique with some easy add-ons:

  • Mix-ins: Stir in crushed cookies, candy pieces, or dried fruit for more texture.
  • Swirls: Layer sauces like chocolate syrup, caramel, or fruit purée into the mixture before freezing.
  • Toppings: Add whipped cream, nuts, sprinkles, or fresh fruit just before serving for a decorative touch.

Why No-Churn Ice Cream is the Ultimate Dessert Solution

The brilliance of no-churn ice cream lies in its versatility and simplicity. With no heavy-duty equipment required, anyone can create creamy, flavorful ice cream at home using just a few basic ingredients. The recipes above are merely a starting point—once you’ve mastered the technique, you can experiment with endless combinations to suit your tastes.

Whether you’re hosting a summer barbecue, planning a romantic dessert spread, or simply treating yourself to something special, these no-churn ice cream recipes are destined to become reliable favorites. Dive into the world of easy homemade ice cream and discover just how satisfying it is to craft your own frozen creations.
