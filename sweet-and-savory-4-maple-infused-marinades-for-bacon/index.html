Sweet and Savory: 4 Maple-Infused Marinades for Bacon


If there’s one ingredient that can turn an ordinary dish into something extraordinary, it’s maple syrup. Its rich, sweet, and slightly smoky flavor adds a unique touch to any recipe, especially when it comes to bacon. Whether you’re a fan of sweet or savory flavors, these 4 maple-infused marinades will take your bacon to a whole new level.

Classic Maple Glaze

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 cup maple syrup
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 teaspoon Dijon mustard
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, whisk together the maple syrup, soy sauce, Dijon mustard, and black pepper until well combined.
  2. Place the bacon strips in a resealable plastic bag and pour the marinade over them.
  3. Seal the bag and massage the marinade into the bacon, ensuring each strip is coated.
  4. Refrigerate for at least 30 minutes, or overnight for a more intense flavor.
  5. Cook the bacon as desired, and enjoy the delicious caramelized glaze with every bite.

Smoky Maple Rub

Ingredients:

  • 1/4 cup maple syrup
  • 2 tablespoons smoked paprika
  • 1 tablespoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon onion powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon cayenne pepper
  • Salt to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a small bowl, combine the maple syrup, smoked paprika, garlic powder, onion powder, cayenne pepper, and salt.
  2. Rub the mixture onto both sides of the bacon strips, ensuring an even coating.
  3. Let the bacon sit at room temperature for 15 minutes to allow the flavors to meld.
  4. Grill or bake the bacon until crispy, and savor the smoky, slightly spicy essence of this unique marinade.

Maple Bourbon Glaze

Ingredients:

  • 1/4 cup maple syrup
  • 2 tablespoons bourbon
  • 1 tablespoon apple cider vinegar
  • 1 teaspoon Worcestershire sauce
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper

Instructions:

  1. In a saucepan, combine the maple syrup, bourbon, apple cider vinegar, Worcestershire sauce, and black pepper.
  2. Simmer the mixture over medium heat, stirring occasionally, until it thickens slightly.
  3. Brush the glaze onto the bacon during the last few minutes of cooking to impart a sweet, tangy, and slightly boozy flavor.

Maple Mustard Marinade

Ingredients:

  • 1/3 cup maple syrup
  • 2 tablespoons whole grain mustard
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon minced garlic
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried thyme
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Whisk together the maple syrup, whole grain mustard, olive oil, minced garlic, dried thyme, salt, and pepper in a bowl.
  2. Place the bacon in a shallow dish and pour the marinade over it, ensuring each strip is coated.
  3. Cover and refrigerate for at least 1 hour, allowing the bacon to absorb the savory-sweet flavors.
  4. Pan-fry or bake the bacon until golden and crisp, and relish the delightful combination of maple and mustard.

Bacon lovers, rejoice! These maple-infused marinades are sure to elevate your bacon game, whether you prefer it sweet, smoky, or tangy. Experiment with these recipes and discover your favorite way to enjoy maple-infused bacon. The versatility of maple syrup knows no bounds, and when paired with bacon, it creates an irresistible symphony of flavors. So, grab a bottle of maple syrup and a pack of bacon, and let the culinary adventure begin!
