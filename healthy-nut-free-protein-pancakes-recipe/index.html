Healthy Nut-Free Protein Pancakes Recipe


If you’re looking for a delicious and nutritious breakfast option that is both protein-packed and nut-free, then these Healthy Nut-Free Protein Pancakes are the perfect choice! These pancakes are not only easy to make, but they are also incredibly delicious and will keep you feeling satisfied and energized throughout the day.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup rolled oats
  • 1 cup unsweetened almond milk
  • 2 ripe bananas
  • 2 tablespoons ground flaxseed
  • 2 tablespoons chia seeds
  • 2 tablespoons honey or maple syrup
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon cinnamon
  • Pinch of salt
  • Cooking spray or coconut oil, for greasing the pan

Instructions

  1. In a blender or food processor, blend the rolled oats until they turn into a fine flour-like consistency.

  2. In a large mixing bowl, mash the ripe bananas until they are smooth and creamy.

  3. Add the almond milk, ground flaxseed, chia seeds, honey or maple syrup, vanilla extract, baking powder, cinnamon, and salt to the mashed bananas. Mix well until all the ingredients are combined.

  4. Gradually add the oat flour to the wet ingredients, stirring continuously until a smooth batter forms. Let the batter sit for a few minutes to allow the chia seeds to gel and thicken the mixture.

  5. Heat a non-stick skillet or griddle over medium heat. Lightly grease the pan with cooking spray or coconut oil.

  6. Pour 1/4 cup of the pancake batter onto the preheated pan for each pancake. Cook for 2-3 minutes, or until bubbles start to form on the surface. Flip the pancake and cook for an additional 1-2 minutes, or until golden brown.

  7. Repeat the process with the remaining batter, adding more cooking spray or coconut oil as needed.

  8. Serve the pancakes warm with your favorite toppings such as fresh berries, sliced bananas, a drizzle of honey or maple syrup, or a dollop of Greek yogurt.

Nutritional Information

  • Serving Size: 1 pancake
  • Calories: 120kcal
  • Protein: 5g
  • Carbohydrates: 22g
  • Fat: 2g
  • Fiber: 4g
  • Sugar: 6g

Benefits of Nut-Free Protein Pancakes

These Healthy Nut-Free Protein Pancakes are not only delicious but also come with a range of benefits:

  1. High Protein Content: Thanks to the combination of oats, flaxseed, and chia seeds, these pancakes are packed with plant-based protein, making them an excellent choice for those following a vegetarian or vegan diet.

  2. Nut-Free Alternative: Many people have nut allergies or prefer to avoid nuts for various reasons. These pancakes provide a tasty and safe option for those looking for a nut-free breakfast alternative.

  3. Fiber-Rich: Oats and chia seeds are excellent sources of dietary fiber, which aids in digestion and helps keep you feeling fuller for longer.

  4. Energy-Boosting: The combination of complex carbohydrates from the oats and natural sugars from the bananas provides a sustained release of energy, keeping you fueled throughout the day.

  5. Versatile and Customizable: These pancakes can be customized to suit your taste preferences. Feel free to add in your favorite mix-ins such as chocolate chips, shredded coconut, or chopped fruit for an extra burst of flavor.

So, why not give these Healthy Nut-Free Protein Pancakes a try? They are the perfect way to start your day on a nutritious and delicious note. Enjoy!
