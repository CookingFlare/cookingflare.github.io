10 Mouthwatering Wrap Recipes for a Vegetarian Feast


If you’re a vegetarian looking for delicious and satisfying meal options, look no further! Wraps are the perfect choice for a quick and easy meal that can be enjoyed at any time of the day. Packed with flavor, these 10 mouthwatering wrap recipes will surely leave you satisfied and craving for more. So, let’s dive right in!

1. Greek Chickpea Wrap

Start your vegetarian feast with this Greek-inspired wrap. Fill a whole wheat tortilla with a mixture of cooked chickpeas, diced cucumbers, cherry tomatoes, kalamata olives, crumbled feta cheese, and a drizzle of tzatziki sauce. Roll it up and enjoy the burst of Mediterranean flavors!

2. Mexican Black Bean Wrap

Get a taste of Mexico with this flavorful wrap. Spread a layer of refried black beans on a tortilla, then top it with chopped lettuce, diced tomatoes, sliced avocado, shredded cheese, and a dollop of salsa. Roll it up and savor the zesty combination of ingredients.

3. Thai Peanut Tofu Wrap

For a burst of Asian flavors, try this Thai-inspired wrap. Marinate tofu in a mixture of soy sauce, lime juice, and peanut butter, then pan-fry until golden. Place the tofu on a tortilla, along with shredded carrots, sliced bell peppers, fresh cilantro, and a drizzle of peanut sauce. Roll it up and enjoy the perfect blend of sweet, savory, and spicy.

4. Caprese Wrap

Indulge in the classic flavors of Italy with this Caprese wrap. Layer slices of fresh mozzarella, ripe tomatoes, and fresh basil leaves on a tortilla. Drizzle with balsamic glaze and sprinkle with salt and pepper. Roll it up and savor the simplicity and elegance of this timeless combination.

5. Mediterranean Hummus Wrap

Transport yourself to the sunny shores of the Mediterranean with this delicious wrap. Spread a generous layer of hummus on a tortilla, then top it with sliced cucumbers, cherry tomatoes, red onion, kalamata olives, crumbled feta cheese, and a sprinkle of dried oregano. Roll it up and enjoy the fresh and vibrant flavors.

6. Spicy Buffalo Cauliflower Wrap

For those who love a little heat, this wrap is sure to satisfy your cravings. Toss cauliflower florets in a mixture of hot sauce, melted butter, and garlic powder, then bake until crispy. Place the cauliflower on a tortilla, along with shredded lettuce, diced celery, and a drizzle of ranch dressing. Roll it up and experience the perfect balance of spicy and cool.

7. Indian Curry Chickpea Wrap

Experience the aromatic flavors of India with this curry chickpea wrap. Sauté chickpeas with onions, garlic, and a blend of curry spices until fragrant. Place the mixture on a tortilla, along with sliced cucumbers, shredded carrots, fresh cilantro, and a dollop of yogurt sauce. Roll it up and enjoy the explosion of Indian spices.

8. BBQ Jackfruit Wrap

Get your barbecue fix with this unique and delicious wrap. Shred jackfruit and simmer it in your favorite barbecue sauce until tender. Place the jackfruit on a tortilla, along with sliced red onion, pickles, and a drizzle of vegan mayo. Roll it up and savor the smoky and tangy flavors.

9. Mediterranean Quinoa Wrap

For a protein-packed option, try this Mediterranean quinoa wrap. Cook quinoa according to package instructions, then mix it with diced cucumbers, cherry tomatoes, chopped parsley, crumbled feta cheese, and a squeeze of lemon juice. Place the mixture on a tortilla, roll it up, and enjoy the fresh and nutritious combination.

10. Asian Sesame Veggie Wrap

End your vegetarian feast with this delightful Asian-inspired wrap. Toss a mixture of julienned carrots, cucumber, red cabbage, and bell peppers in a tangy sesame dressing. Place the mixture on a tortilla, sprinkle with sesame seeds, and roll it up to experience the crunch and the vibrant flavors.

There you have it - 10 mouthwatering wrap recipes for a vegetarian feast! Whether you’re craving Mediterranean, Mexican, Asian, or classic flavors, these wraps are sure to satisfy your taste buds. So, grab your tortillas and get ready to indulge in a vegetarian feast like no other. Enjoy!
