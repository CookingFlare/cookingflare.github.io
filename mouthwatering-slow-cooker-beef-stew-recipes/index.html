Mouthwatering Slow Cooker Beef Stew Recipes


If you’re looking for a hearty and delicious meal that is packed with flavor, then slow cooker beef stew is the answer. There’s something magical about letting the ingredients simmer and meld together for hours, resulting in a dish that will make your taste buds dance with joy. In this blog post, we will explore three mouthwatering slow cooker beef stew recipes that are sure to become family favorites.

Classic Slow Cooker Beef Stew

Ingredients:

  • 2 pounds beef stew meat, cut into cubes
  • 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 4 carrots, peeled and sliced
  • 3 potatoes, peeled and cubed
  • 2 cups beef broth
  • 1 cup red wine (optional)
  • 2 bay leaves
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • 1 teaspoon dried rosemary

Instructions:

  1. In a large bowl, combine the flour, salt, and pepper. Toss the beef cubes in the flour mixture until evenly coated.
  2. Heat the vegetable oil in a skillet over medium-high heat. Brown the beef cubes on all sides, then transfer them to the slow cooker.
  3. In the same skillet, sauté the onion and garlic until softened. Add them to the slow cooker along with the carrots, potatoes, beef broth, red wine (if using), bay leaves, thyme, and rosemary. Stir to combine.
  4. Cover the slow cooker and cook on low for 8 hours or on high for 4 hours, or until the beef is tender.
  5. Remove the bay leaves before serving. Enjoy this classic slow cooker beef stew with crusty bread or mashed potatoes.

Spicy Slow Cooker Beef Stew

Ingredients:

  • 2 pounds beef stew meat, cut into cubes
  • 2 tablespoons chili powder
  • 1 tablespoon cumin
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon cayenne pepper (adjust to your spice preference)
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 jalapeño pepper, seeded and diced
  • 4 carrots, peeled and sliced
  • 2 bell peppers, diced
  • 3 potatoes, peeled and cubed
  • 2 cups beef broth
  • 1 can diced tomatoes
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh cilantro for garnish

Instructions:

  1. In a small bowl, combine the chili powder, cumin, paprika, and cayenne pepper. Rub the spice mixture onto the beef cubes.
  2. Heat the vegetable oil in a skillet over medium-high heat. Brown the beef cubes on all sides, then transfer them to the slow cooker.
  3. In the same skillet, sauté the onion, garlic, and jalapeño pepper until softened. Add them to the slow cooker along with the carrots, bell peppers, potatoes, beef broth, and diced tomatoes. Season with salt and pepper.
  4. Cover the slow cooker and cook on low for 8 hours or on high for 4 hours. Adjust the cooking time depending on your desired tenderness of the beef.
  5. Serve the spicy slow cooker beef stew garnished with fresh cilantro. This fiery stew pairs well with rice or cornbread.

Asian-Inspired Slow Cooker Beef Stew

Ingredients:

  • 2 pounds beef stew meat, cut into cubes
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons hoisin sauce
  • 1 tablespoon honey
  • 1 tablespoon rice vinegar
  • 2 tablespoons cornstarch
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1-inch piece ginger, grated
  • 4 carrots, peeled and sliced
  • 1 red bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 cup snap peas
  • 3 cups beef broth
  • 2 tablespoons sesame oil
  • Green onions for garnish

Instructions:

  1. In a small bowl, whisk together the soy sauce, hoisin sauce, honey, rice vinegar, and cornstarch. Set aside.
  2. Heat the vegetable oil in a skillet over medium-high heat. Brown the beef cubes on all sides, then transfer them to the slow cooker.
  3. In the same skillet, sauté the onion, garlic, and ginger until fragrant. Add them to the slow cooker along with the carrots, red bell pepper, snap peas, beef broth, and sesame oil. Stir in the sauce mixture.
  4. Cover the slow cooker and cook on low for 8 hours or on high for 4 hours. The beef should be tender and the flavors well combined.
  5. Serve the Asian-inspired slow cooker beef stew garnished with green onions. This stew is best enjoyed over steamed rice or noodles.

These mouthwatering slow cooker beef stew recipes are perfect for those days when you want a comforting and satisfying meal without spending hours in the kitchen. The slow cooker does all the work for you, allowing you to sit back, relax, and enjoy a delicious homemade stew. Give these recipes a try and let your taste buds savor the flavors of tender beef, aromatic vegetables, and rich broth.
