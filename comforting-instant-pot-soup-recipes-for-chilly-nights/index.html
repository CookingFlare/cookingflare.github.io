Comforting Instant Pot Soup Recipes for Chilly Nights


Are you ready to cozy up with a warm bowl of soup on those chilly nights? Look no further because we have compiled a list of comforting Instant Pot soup recipes that will warm both your body and soul. These recipes are not only delicious but also quick and easy to make using your trusty Instant Pot. So, grab a spoon and get ready to indulge in these hearty and flavorful soups!

1. Hearty Vegetable Minestrone

Ingredients:

  • 1 onion, diced
  • 2 carrots, sliced
  • 2 celery stalks, chopped
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 zucchini, diced
  • 1 can of diced tomatoes
  • 1 can of kidney beans, drained and rinsed
  • 4 cups of vegetable broth
  • 1 teaspoon of dried basil
  • 1 teaspoon of dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 cup of small pasta, such as macaroni or ditalini
  • Fresh parsley for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Set your Instant Pot to sauté mode and heat some olive oil. Add the onion, carrots, celery, and garlic, and sauté until softened.
  2. Add the zucchini, diced tomatoes, kidney beans, vegetable broth, dried basil, dried oregano, salt, and pepper. Stir well to combine.
  3. Close the lid and set the Instant Pot to high pressure for 8 minutes.
  4. Once the cooking time is up, do a quick release of the pressure. Open the lid and stir in the pasta.
  5. Set the Instant Pot to sauté mode again and cook for an additional 5 minutes or until the pasta is cooked through.
  6. Serve hot, garnished with fresh parsley.

2. Creamy Tomato Basil Soup

Ingredients:

  • 1 onion, diced
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 can of diced tomatoes
  • 2 cups of vegetable broth
  • 1 cup of heavy cream
  • 1/4 cup of fresh basil leaves, chopped
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Grated Parmesan cheese for serving

Instructions:

  1. Set your Instant Pot to sauté mode and heat some olive oil. Add the onion and garlic, and sauté until fragrant.
  2. Add the diced tomatoes, vegetable broth, heavy cream, salt, and pepper. Stir well to combine.
  3. Close the lid and set the Instant Pot to high pressure for 10 minutes.
  4. Once the cooking time is up, do a quick release of the pressure. Open the lid and stir in the fresh basil leaves.
  5. Use an immersion blender to blend the soup until smooth and creamy.
  6. Serve hot, topped with grated Parmesan cheese.

3. Thai Coconut Curry Soup

Ingredients:

  • 1 onion, diced
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon of red curry paste
  • 1 can of coconut milk
  • 4 cups of vegetable broth
  • 1 red bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 cup of sliced mushrooms
  • 1 cup of diced tofu or cooked chicken
  • 2 tablespoons of soy sauce
  • Juice of 1 lime
  • Fresh cilantro for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Set your Instant Pot to sauté mode and heat some olive oil. Add the onion, garlic, and red curry paste, and sauté until fragrant.
  2. Add the coconut milk, vegetable broth, red bell pepper, mushrooms, tofu or chicken, soy sauce, and lime juice. Stir well to combine.
  3. Close the lid and set the Instant Pot to high pressure for 5 minutes.
  4. Once the cooking time is up, do a quick release of the pressure. Open the lid and stir in some fresh cilantro.
  5. Serve hot, garnished with additional cilantro.

4. Classic Chicken Noodle Soup

Ingredients:

  • 1 onion, diced
  • 2 carrots, sliced
  • 2 celery stalks, chopped
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 4 cups of chicken broth
  • 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 2 bay leaves
  • 1 teaspoon of dried thyme
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 cups of egg noodles
  • Fresh parsley for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Set your Instant Pot to sauté mode and heat some olive oil. Add the onion, carrots, celery, and garlic, and sauté until softened.
  2. Add the chicken broth, chicken breasts, bay leaves, dried thyme, salt, and pepper. Stir well to combine.
  3. Close the lid and set the Instant Pot to high pressure for 10 minutes.
  4. Once the cooking time is up, do a natural release of the pressure for 10 minutes, then do a quick release to release any remaining pressure.
  5. Open the lid and remove the chicken breasts. Shred the chicken using two forks and return it to the Instant Pot.
  6. Set the Instant Pot to sauté mode again and add the egg noodles. Cook for an additional 5 minutes or until the noodles are cooked through.
  7. Serve hot, garnished with fresh parsley.

Conclusion

These comforting Instant Pot soup recipes are perfect for those chilly nights when you need something warm and satisfying. From the hearty vegetable minestrone to the creamy tomato basil soup, and the flavorful Thai coconut curry soup to the classic chicken noodle soup, there’s a soup for every taste bud. So, grab your Instant Pot and start cooking up some deliciousness. Stay warm and enjoy!
