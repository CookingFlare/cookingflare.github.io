Mummy Pumpkin Pie: A Scary Good Halloween Dessert


Halloween is just around the corner, and what better way to celebrate than with a spine-chilling dessert that will leave your guests in awe? Introducing the Mummy Pumpkin Pie, a frighteningly delicious treat that combines the flavors of pumpkin, spices, and a touch of sweetness. This hauntingly delightful dessert will surely become the centerpiece of your Halloween feast. So, grab your apron, put on your witch’s hat, and let’s get baking!

Ingredients

For the crust:

  • 2 cups of all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon of salt
  • 1/2 cup of unsalted butter, cold and cubed
  • 1/4 cup of ice water

For the filling:

  • 1 can (15 ounces) of pumpkin puree
  • 1 cup of heavy cream
  • 3/4 cup of granulated sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 teaspoon of vanilla extract
  • 1 teaspoon of ground cinnamon
  • 1/2 teaspoon of ground ginger
  • 1/4 teaspoon of ground nutmeg
  • 1/4 teaspoon of ground cloves
  • A pinch of salt

For the mummy topping:

  • 1 cup of heavy cream
  • 2 tablespoons of powdered sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon of vanilla extract
  • Candy eyes (available at baking supply stores)

Instructions

Step 1: Prepare the Crust

  1. In a large bowl, whisk together the flour and salt. Add the cold cubed butter and use a pastry cutter or your fingers to cut the butter into the flour until the mixture resembles coarse crumbs.
  2. Gradually add the ice water, a tablespoon at a time, while tossing the mixture with a fork. Mix until the dough comes together and is no longer crumbly.
  3. Shape the dough into a disk, wrap it in plastic wrap, and refrigerate for at least 30 minutes.

Step 2: Make the Filling

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. In a mixing bowl, combine the pumpkin puree, heavy cream, granulated sugar, eggs, vanilla extract, cinnamon, ginger, nutmeg, cloves, and salt. Whisk until well combined and smooth.

Step 3: Assemble the Pie

  1. On a lightly floured surface, roll out the chilled dough into a circle that’s about 1/4-inch thick.
  2. Gently transfer the rolled-out dough to a 9-inch pie dish, pressing it into the bottom and sides. Trim any excess dough hanging over the edges.
  3. Pour the pumpkin filling into the prepared pie crust, spreading it evenly.

Step 4: Bake the Pie

  1. Place the pie on a baking sheet and bake for 45-50 minutes, or until the center is set and the crust is golden brown.
  2. Remove the pie from the oven and let it cool completely on a wire rack.

Step 5: Create the Mummy Topping

  1. In a mixing bowl, whip the heavy cream, powdered sugar, and vanilla extract until stiff peaks form.
  2. Transfer the whipped cream to a piping bag fitted with a small round tip or a Ziploc bag with the corner snipped off.
  3. Pipe the whipped cream in a crisscross pattern over the cooled pie, leaving small gaps for the mummy’s eyes.
  4. Gently press the candy eyes into the whipped cream at the top of the pie.

Step 6: Serve and Enjoy!

Now that your Mummy Pumpkin Pie is complete, it’s time to unleash the Halloween spirit and indulge in this spooky, yet scrumptious dessert. Slice it up and serve it to your guests, or enjoy a slice all to yourself. The creamy pumpkin filling, combined with the buttery crust and whimsical mummy topping, will make this Halloween treat an unforgettable experience for everyone.

Remember, the Mummy Pumpkin Pie is best served chilled, so refrigerate any leftovers. This dessert is perfect for Halloween parties, family gatherings, or simply as a way to treat yourself during the spooky season. Get creative with your mummy design, and have fun making this scary good dessert!

So, are you ready to embrace the Halloween spirit and create a dessert that will both frighten and delight? Gather your ingredients and let the Mummy Pumpkin Pie cast its spell on you and your loved ones. Happy Halloween and happy baking!

*Note: This recipe serves 8-10 people, but feel free to adjust the quantities according to your needs.
