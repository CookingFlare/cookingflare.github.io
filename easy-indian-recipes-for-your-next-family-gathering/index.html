Easy Indian Recipes for Your Next Family Gathering


Family gatherings are a time to come together, share love, and create lasting memories. When it comes to planning a successful family get-together, food plays a crucial role in bringing everyone together. Indian cuisine is a perfect choice for family gatherings as it offers a diverse range of dishes that cater to various tastes and dietary restrictions. In this article, we will explore some easy Indian recipes that are perfect for your next family gathering. Chenna Chilla (Indian-Style Pancakes) Chenna chilla is a popular Indian breakfast dish that can be easily adapted to serve a crowd. This recipe is a variation of the traditional batter-based pancake, but with a twist. Ingredients:

1 cup gram flour 1/2 cup all-purpose flour 1/2 cup yogurt 1/4 cup water 1/2 teaspoon cumin powder 1/2 teaspoon garam masala powder Salt, to taste Vegetables of your choice (e.g., bell peppers, onions, tomatoes, spinach) Vegetable oil or ghee for brushing

Instructions:

In a large bowl, combine gram flour, all-purpose flour, and a pinch of salt. Add yogurt, water, cumin powder, garam masala powder, and mix well. Add the vegetables of your choice and mix until they are evenly distributed throughout the batter. Heat a non-stick pan or griddle over medium heat. Pour a ladle full of the batter onto the pan. Brush with a little oil or ghee. Cook until the pancake is golden brown and crispy. Serve hot with chutney or raita.

Chana Masala (Chickpea Curry) Chana masala is a comforting and flavorful recipe that is perfect for a family gathering. This recipe is easy to make and can be customized to suit your taste. Ingredients:

1 can chickpeas 1 onion 2 cloves garlic 1 teaspoon cumin seeds 1 teaspoon coriander powder 1 teaspoon cumin powder 1 teaspoon garam masala powder 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder Salt, to taste 2 tablespoons vegetable oil Fresh cilantro, for garnish

Instructions:

Heat oil in a pan over medium heat. Add cumin seeds and let them sizzle for a few seconds. Add onion and sauté until it is translucent. Add garlic and sauté for another minute. Add the spices and sauté for a minute. Add the chickpeas and sauté for a few minutes. Add water and let the curry simmer until the flavors are absorbed. Garnish with fresh cilantro and serve with naan or rice.

Vegetable Biryani (Mixed Vegetable Rice) Vegetable biryani is a popular Indian dish that is perfect for a family gathering. This recipe is easy to make and can be customized to suit your taste. Ingredients:

1 cup basmati rice 1 cup mixed vegetables (e.g., carrots, peas, cauliflower, potatoes) 1 onion 2 cloves garlic 1 teaspoon cumin seeds 1 teaspoon coriander powder 1 teaspoon cumin powder 1 teaspoon garam masala powder 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder Salt, to taste 2 tablespoons vegetable oil Fresh cilantro, for garnish

Instructions:

Heat oil in a pan over medium heat. Add cumin seeds and let them sizzle for a few seconds. Add onion and sauté until it is translucent. Add garlic and sauté for another minute. Add the mixed vegetables and sauté for a few minutes. Add the spices and rice, and mix well. Add water and let the mixture simmer until the rice is cooked. Garnish with fresh cilantro and serve hot.

Mattar Paneer (Peas and Cheese Curry) Mattar paneer is a creamy and flavorful recipe that is perfect for a family gathering. This recipe is easy to make and can be customized to suit your taste. Ingredients:

1 cup paneer 1 cup peas 1 onion 2 cloves garlic 1 teaspoon cumin seeds 1 teaspoon coriander powder 1 teaspoon cumin powder 1 teaspoon garam masala powder 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder Salt, to taste 2 tablespoons vegetable oil 1 cup heavy cream Fresh cilantro, for garnish

Instructions:

Heat oil in a pan over medium heat. Add cumin seeds and let them sizzle for a few seconds. Add onion and sauté until it is translucent. Add garlic and sauté for another minute. Add the peas and paneer and sauté for a few minutes. Add the spices and heavy cream, and mix well. Let the mixture simmer until the peas are cooked. Garnish with fresh cilantro and serve hot.
