Hearty Keto Cheesy Bacon and Spinach Stuffed Chicken Breast Recipe


If you’re looking for a delicious, hearty, and keto-friendly meal, look no further! This Cheesy Bacon and Spinach Stuffed Chicken Breast recipe is a flavor-packed dish that’s sure to satisfy your cravings while keeping you on track with your low-carb lifestyle.

Ingredients

  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 8 slices of bacon
  • 1 cup of fresh spinach, chopped
  • 1 cup of shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup of grated parmesan cheese
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon of Italian seasoning
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Olive oil

Instructions

Step 1: Preparing the Chicken Breasts

Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C). Place the chicken breasts on a cutting board and carefully slice them horizontally to create a pocket in each breast. Be sure not to cut all the way through. Season the inside and outside of each breast with salt, pepper, and Italian seasoning.

Step 2: Filling the Chicken Breasts

In a skillet over medium heat, cook the bacon until it’s crispy. Once done, transfer the bacon to a paper towel to drain excess grease. In the same skillet, add the chopped spinach and minced garlic. Sauté the spinach until it’s wilted. Remove the skillet from the heat and let the spinach mixture cool for a few minutes.

In a bowl, combine the mozzarella cheese, parmesan cheese, and the cooled spinach mixture. Crumble the crispy bacon into the bowl and mix everything together until well combined.

Step 3: Stuffing the Chicken Breasts

Using a spoon, carefully stuff each chicken breast with the cheesy bacon and spinach mixture, ensuring they are evenly filled. Secure the openings of the chicken breasts with toothpicks to prevent the filling from spilling out during cooking.

Step 4: Cooking the Stuffed Chicken Breasts

In a clean skillet, heat a small amount of olive oil over medium-high heat. Once hot, sear the stuffed chicken breasts for 2-3 minutes on each side until they develop a golden-brown crust. This will help lock in the flavors.

Transfer the seared chicken breasts to a baking dish and bake them in the preheated oven for 20-25 minutes, or until the chicken is cooked through and the cheese is melted and bubbly.

Step 5: Serving the Dish

Once the chicken breasts are cooked, remove them from the oven and let them rest for a few minutes. Carefully remove the toothpicks before serving. You can slice the stuffed chicken breasts and serve them with a side of fresh salad or roasted vegetables for a complete and satisfying keto meal.

Conclusion

This Hearty Keto Cheesy Bacon and Spinach Stuffed Chicken Breast recipe is a delightful combination of savory flavors that will impress your taste buds and keep you feeling satisfied. Whether you’re following a keto diet or simply looking for a comforting and wholesome meal, this dish is a winner. Give it a try and enjoy a delicious homemade dinner that’s bound to become a new favorite in your recipe collection.
