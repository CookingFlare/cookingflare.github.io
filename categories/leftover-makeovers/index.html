Risotto Arancini Ingredients: Leftover Risotto Bread Crumbs Eggs Olive Oil Instructions: Take your leftover risotto and shape it into small balls. Shepherd’s Pie Stuffed Peppers Ingredients: Leftover Shepherd’s Pie Bell Peppers Cheese Instructions: Cut the tops off bell peppers and remove the seeds. Fill the peppers with leftover shepherd’s pie. Top with cheese and bake in the oven until the peppers are tender and the cheese is melted. Enjoy a delicious twist on traditional shepherd’s pie in a colorful and fun presentation. 3. Pulled Pork Tacos Ingredients: Leftover Pulled Pork Tortillas Avocado Salsa Lime Instructions: Warm up the pulled pork in a pan. Mashed Potato Pancakes Ingredients: Leftover Mashed Potatoes Flour Eggs Chives Instructions: Mix leftover mashed potatoes with flour, eggs, and chopped chives to form a thick batter. Heat a pan with oil and spoon the batter to make pancakes. Cook until golden brown on both sides and serve hot with a dollop of sour cream for a crispy and comforting dish. 5. Vegetable Fried Rice Ingredients: Leftover Cooked Rice Mixed Vegetables Soy Sauce Egg Instructions: Heat a wok with oil and add mixed vegetables to stir-fry.
