Greek Lemon Garlic Roasted Shrimp: A Quick and Easy Appetizer


If you’re looking for a delicious and hassle-free appetizer that will impress your guests, look no further than Greek Lemon Garlic Roasted Shrimp. Bursting with Mediterranean flavors, this dish is a crowd-pleaser that can be whipped up in no time. With the tanginess of lemon, the boldness of garlic, and the succulent shrimp, this appetizer is sure to leave your taste buds craving for more.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound of large shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 4 cloves of garlic, minced
  • Juice of 2 lemons
  • 1/4 cup of olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon of dried oregano
  • 1/2 teaspoon of dried thyme
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley, chopped (for garnish)

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C).

  2. In a mixing bowl, combine the minced garlic, lemon juice, olive oil, dried oregano, dried thyme, salt, and pepper. Mix well to create a marinade.

  3. Add the peeled and deveined shrimp to the marinade. Toss the shrimp gently to ensure they are well-coated. Let the shrimp marinate for about 15 minutes, allowing the flavors to infuse.

  4. While the shrimp is marinating, prepare a baking sheet lined with parchment paper or aluminum foil for easy cleanup.

  5. Once the shrimp has marinated, transfer them to the prepared baking sheet, arranging them in a single layer.

  6. Pour any remaining marinade over the shrimp, making sure to evenly distribute the garlic and herbs.

  7. Place the baking sheet in the preheated oven and roast the shrimp for 8-10 minutes, or until they turn pink and opaque. Be careful not to overcook them, as they can become rubbery.

  8. Once the shrimp are cooked, remove them from the oven and let them cool for a few minutes.

  9. Transfer the roasted shrimp to a serving platter, garnish with freshly chopped parsley, and serve warm.

Serving Suggestions

Greek Lemon Garlic Roasted Shrimp is incredibly versatile and can be enjoyed in various ways. Here are a few serving suggestions to get you started:

  • Serve the shrimp as an appetizer with toothpicks for easy handling. Your guests will love popping these flavorful bites into their mouths.

  • Pair the shrimp with a side of creamy tzatziki sauce for a refreshing and cooling dip. The tanginess of the tzatziki complements the lemon and garlic beautifully.

  • Turn the roasted shrimp into a light and refreshing salad by tossing them with mixed greens, cherry tomatoes, cucumbers, and feta cheese. Drizzle with a simple lemon vinaigrette for a burst of freshness.

  • Serve the shrimp over a bed of fluffy couscous or steamed rice for a more substantial meal. The flavors of the roasted shrimp will infuse into the grains, creating a delightful combination.

Conclusion

Greek Lemon Garlic Roasted Shrimp is a quick and easy appetizer that brings the vibrant flavors of the Mediterranean to your table. With just a handful of ingredients and minimal preparation, you can create a dish that will impress your guests and leave them wanting more. So, the next time you’re in need of a crowd-pleasing appetizer, give this recipe a try. Your taste buds will thank you!
