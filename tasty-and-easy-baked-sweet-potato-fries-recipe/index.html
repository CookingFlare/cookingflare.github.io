Tasty and Easy Baked Sweet Potato Fries Recipe


If you’re looking for a healthier alternative to traditional french fries, then baked sweet potato fries are the perfect choice. These crispy, flavorful fries are not only delicious but also packed with nutrients. Plus, they’re easy to make and can be customized with your favorite seasonings. In this recipe, I’ll walk you through the simple steps to create the perfect batch of baked sweet potato fries that will have your taste buds singing.

Ingredients:

  • 2 large sweet potatoes
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • Optional: fresh herbs such as rosemary or thyme for added flavor

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the Oven: Start by preheating your oven to 425°F (220°C). This will ensure that your sweet potato fries become wonderfully crispy in the oven.

  2. Prepare the Sweet Potatoes: Wash the sweet potatoes thoroughly and pat them dry with a clean kitchen towel. Peel the sweet potatoes if desired, although leaving the skin on adds extra fiber and nutrients to your fries. Cut the sweet potatoes into evenly sized fries to ensure they cook uniformly.

  3. Seasoning: In a large bowl, toss the sweet potato fries with olive oil, garlic powder, paprika, salt, and black pepper until they are evenly coated. You can also add fresh herbs at this stage for an extra burst of flavor.

  4. Arrange on Baking Sheet: Line a baking sheet with parchment paper or aluminum foil for easy cleanup. Spread the seasoned sweet potato fries in a single layer on the baking sheet, making sure they are not overcrowded. Crowding the fries can cause them to steam rather than crisp up.

  5. Bake: Place the baking sheet in the preheated oven and bake the sweet potato fries for 20-25 minutes, flipping them halfway through the cooking time. Keep a close eye on them during the last few minutes of baking to prevent burning.

  6. Serve and Enjoy: Once the sweet potato fries are golden brown and crispy, remove them from the oven and let them cool for a few minutes. Serve them alongside your favorite dipping sauce, such as garlic aioli, ketchup, or sriracha mayo, and enjoy!

Tips for Perfect Sweet Potato Fries:

  • To achieve maximum crispiness, make sure the sweet potato fries are spread out in a single layer on the baking sheet.
  • Don’t overcrowd the baking sheet, as this can cause the fries to steam rather than bake.
  • For an extra flavor boost, experiment with different seasonings such as cumin, chili powder, or even a sprinkle of Parmesan cheese.
  • If you prefer a sweeter flavor, you can add a touch of cinnamon and a drizzle of honey before baking.

Baked sweet potato fries are not only a healthier alternative to regular fries, but they also make a delightful snack or side dish for any meal. They’re versatile, customizable, and oh-so-delicious. So, the next time you’re craving something savory and satisfying, give this simple recipe a try. Your taste buds will thank you!
