The Best Italian-Style Chicken Cacciatore Recipe You'll Ever Try


If you’re a fan of Italian cuisine and love the rich flavors of tomatoes, peppers, and herbs, then you’re in for a treat with this amazing Italian-style Chicken Cacciatore recipe. This classic dish is bursting with bold flavors and is sure to become a family favorite. So, roll up your sleeves and let’s get cooking!

Ingredients

  • 4 chicken thighs
  • 4 chicken drumsticks
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 red bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 yellow bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 can (14 oz) diced tomatoes
  • 1 can (6 oz) tomato paste
  • 1 cup chicken broth
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • Fresh parsley, for garnish

Instructions

  1. Start by seasoning the chicken thighs and drumsticks with salt and pepper. Heat the olive oil in a large skillet or Dutch oven over medium-high heat.

  2. Add the chicken pieces to the skillet and cook until golden brown on all sides, about 5 minutes per side. Remove the chicken from the skillet and set it aside.

  3. In the same skillet, add the chopped onion and minced garlic. Sauté until the onion becomes translucent and the garlic becomes fragrant, about 2-3 minutes.

  4. Add the sliced bell peppers to the skillet and cook for another 2 minutes until they start to soften.

  5. Now, it’s time to add the diced tomatoes, tomato paste, chicken broth, dried oregano, dried basil, and dried thyme. Stir everything together until well combined.

  6. Return the chicken to the skillet, ensuring it’s submerged in the tomato mixture. Reduce the heat to low, cover, and let it simmer for about 45 minutes to an hour, until the chicken is tender and cooked through.

  7. Once the chicken is cooked, remove it from the skillet and transfer it to a serving platter. Tent it with foil to keep warm.

  8. Increase the heat to medium-high and let the sauce simmer uncovered for about 10 minutes, or until it thickens slightly.

  9. Ladle the flavorful sauce over the chicken and garnish with fresh parsley. Serve hot with your favorite pasta or crusty bread.

Tips and Variations

  • For a spicier kick, add some crushed red pepper flakes to the sauce while it simmers.
  • If you prefer boneless chicken, you can use chicken breasts instead of thighs and drumsticks. Just adjust the cooking time accordingly to ensure they don’t dry out.
  • Feel free to add other vegetables to the dish, such as mushrooms or zucchini, to make it even more hearty and flavorful.
  • Leftovers can be stored in an airtight container in the refrigerator for up to 3 days. Simply reheat in a skillet or microwave before serving.

Conclusion

There you have it – a tantalizing Italian-style Chicken Cacciatore recipe that will transport your taste buds straight to Italy. The combination of tender chicken, flavorful tomatoes, and aromatic herbs creates a dish that is both comforting and satisfying. Whether you’re cooking for a special occasion or simply craving a taste of Italy, this recipe is sure to impress. So, gather your ingredients and get ready to enjoy a truly unforgettable meal. Buon appetito!
