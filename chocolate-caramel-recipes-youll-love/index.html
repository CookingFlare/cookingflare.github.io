Chocolate-Caramel Recipes You'll Love


Chocolate and caramel – two of the most irresistible ingredients in the world of sweets. When combined, they create an indulgent treat that’s bound to satisfy any dessert lover. Whether you’re preparing a cozy night in, baking for a celebration, or simply craving a sweet snack, these chocolate-caramel recipes will definitely hit the spot.

In this blog post, we’ll explore some of the best chocolate-caramel recipes that are easy to make, utterly delicious, and sure to impress friends and family alike. From gooey caramel-filled chocolates to decadent cakes, we’ve got a variety of options that will make your taste buds sing.

1. Chocolate-Caramel Truffles

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 8 oz dark chocolate, chopped
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter
  • 1/2 cup brown sugar
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1/4 tsp sea salt
  • Cocoa powder, for dusting

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the Caramel Filling: In a small saucepan, combine the butter and brown sugar. Stir over medium heat until the mixture begins to bubble and thicken, about 3-4 minutes. Add the heavy cream and continue to stir until smooth. Remove from heat and stir in the vanilla extract and sea salt. Let it cool completely to room temperature.

  2. Make the Chocolate Ganache: In a heatproof bowl, place the chopped dark chocolate. Heat the cream in a small saucepan over medium heat until it just starts to simmer, then pour it over the chocolate. Stir until the chocolate melts and the ganache becomes smooth.

  3. Assemble the Truffles: Once the caramel has cooled and the ganache is ready, take a spoonful of caramel and roll it into a small ball. Then, spoon some ganache over the caramel ball and roll it into a perfect sphere. Set the truffle on a parchment-lined tray.

  4. Coat with Cocoa Powder: Once all truffles are formed, dust them lightly with cocoa powder. Let them firm up in the fridge for about 30 minutes before serving.

These chocolate-caramel truffles are so rich and creamy, with the perfect balance of smooth chocolate and gooey caramel. They’re a wonderful gift idea or an elegant treat for any occasion.

2. Caramel Chocolate Lava Cake

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter
  • 4 oz dark chocolate, chopped
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 egg yolks
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1/2 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/4 cup caramel sauce

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the Oven and Ramekins: Preheat your oven to 425°F (220°C). Grease four ramekins with butter and dust them with cocoa powder. Set aside.

  2. Make the Chocolate Batter: In a microwave-safe bowl, melt the butter and dark chocolate together in 30-second increments, stirring in between until smooth. Allow the chocolate mixture to cool slightly. In a separate bowl, whisk together the sugar, eggs, and egg yolks until thick and pale. Stir in the vanilla extract and the melted chocolate mixture until combined.

  3. Add the Flour: Gently fold in the flour and salt. Divide the batter evenly between the prepared ramekins, filling them halfway.

  4. Add Caramel Filling: Spoon about a tablespoon of caramel sauce into the center of each ramekin, then cover with the remaining batter, ensuring the caramel is fully encased.

  5. Bake the Lava Cakes: Place the ramekins on a baking sheet and bake for 12-14 minutes. The edges should be set, but the center should still be soft and slightly gooey.

  6. Serve Immediately: Let the lava cakes sit for 1-2 minutes before running a knife around the edges to release them from the ramekins. Carefully invert onto plates and serve with a scoop of vanilla ice cream or whipped cream.

These caramel chocolate lava cakes are a showstopper. The molten caramel center that oozes out when you cut into it pairs perfectly with the rich, dark chocolate.

3. Chocolate-Caramel Pecan Bars

Ingredients:

  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/2 cup cold unsalted butter, cubed
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1 cup chopped pecans
  • 1/2 cup caramel sauce
  • 1 cup semisweet chocolate chips

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the Crust: Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C). In a food processor, combine the flour, sugar, and salt. Add the cold butter and pulse until the mixture resembles coarse crumbs. Press this mixture into the bottom of a greased 9x9-inch baking pan to form a crust. Bake for 12-15 minutes until lightly golden.

  2. Make the Caramel Layer: While the crust is baking, heat the caramel sauce in a small saucepan over low heat. Add the chopped pecans and stir to combine.

  3. Assemble the Bars: Once the crust is done, remove it from the oven and pour the caramel-pecan mixture over the hot crust. Spread it out evenly.

  4. Add the Chocolate Topping: Sprinkle the chocolate chips evenly over the caramel layer. Return the pan to the oven and bake for an additional 10-12 minutes until the chocolate is melted.

  5. Cool and Cut: Allow the bars to cool completely in the pan before cutting them into squares.

These bars are the perfect blend of crunchy pecans, creamy caramel, and rich chocolate. They’re great for a party or a special treat with a cup of coffee.

4. Chocolate-Covered Caramel Apples

Ingredients:

  • 6 large apples
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1 cup brown sugar
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter
  • 1/4 cup light corn syrup
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 2 cups semisweet chocolate chips
  • 1 tbsp vegetable oil

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the Apples: Wash and dry the apples thoroughly. Insert wooden sticks or skewers into the top of each apple and set them aside on a parchment-lined baking sheet.

  2. Make the Caramel: In a medium saucepan, combine the heavy cream, brown sugar, butter, and corn syrup. Bring the mixture to a boil, stirring constantly. Allow it to simmer for 8-10 minutes until it thickens and reaches the desired consistency. Stir in the vanilla extract.

  3. Coat the Apples: Dip each apple into the hot caramel, swirling to coat evenly. Let any excess caramel drip off, then set the apples aside to cool slightly.

  4. Prepare the Chocolate: In a heatproof bowl, melt the chocolate chips and vegetable oil in the microwave, stirring every 30 seconds until smooth and glossy.

  5. Dip in Chocolate: Once the caramel has set on the apples, dip each apple into the melted chocolate, swirling to coat evenly. Return the apples to the baking sheet and allow the chocolate to harden before serving.

These chocolate-covered caramel apples are a fun and festive treat that can be enjoyed year-round, but they’re especially popular during the fall and holiday seasons.

5. Caramel Chocolate Brownies

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, melted
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1/2 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/3 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1/2 cup caramel sauce
  • 1/2 cup semisweet chocolate chips

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the Oven: Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease a 9x9-inch baking pan or line it with parchment paper.

  2. Make the Brownie Batter: In a medium bowl, whisk together the melted butter and sugar until smooth. Add the eggs, one at a time, followed by the vanilla extract. In a separate bowl, combine the flour, cocoa powder, and salt. Gradually add the dry ingredients to the wet ingredients and stir until combined.

  3. Add Caramel: Pour half of the brownie batter into the prepared pan and spread it out evenly. Drizzle a few tablespoons of caramel sauce over the batter, then swirl it gently with a toothpick.

  4. Add Chocolate Chips: Top the caramel layer with the remaining brownie batter and sprinkle chocolate chips on top.

  5. Bake the Brownies: Bake for 20-25 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out with only a few moist crumbs. Let the brownies cool completely before cutting them into squares.

These caramel chocolate brownies are a rich and fudgy treat, perfect for any chocolate lover.

Conclusion

Chocolate and caramel are a dynamic duo that creates some of the most delicious desserts imaginable. Whether you’re looking for a quick fix or an elegant dessert to impress your guests, these recipes are sure to satisfy your cravings. The combination of rich, smooth chocolate with the buttery sweetness of caramel never goes out of style, and with these recipes, you’ll be able to enjoy this timeless pairing in a variety of creative ways.

So go ahead, dive into the world of chocolate and caramel, and discover your new favorite dessert!
