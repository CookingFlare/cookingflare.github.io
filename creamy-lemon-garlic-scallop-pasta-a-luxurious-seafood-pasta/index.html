Creamy Lemon Garlic Scallop Pasta: A Luxurious Seafood Pasta


If you’re looking to indulge in a luxurious seafood pasta dish, then look no further than this Creamy Lemon Garlic Scallop Pasta recipe. Bursting with flavors of lemon, garlic, and perfectly seared scallops, this dish is sure to impress even the most discerning palates. With its creamy sauce and al dente pasta, it’s a dish that brings together the best of both land and sea.

Ingredients

  • 8 ounces linguine or spaghetti
  • 1 pound fresh scallops
  • 4 tablespoons unsalted butter, divided
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1 lemon, zest and juice
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • Fresh parsley, for garnish

Instructions

  1. Cook the pasta according to the package instructions until al dente. Drain and set aside.

  2. While the pasta is cooking, prepare the scallops. Pat them dry with a paper towel to remove any excess moisture. Season them generously with salt and pepper.

  3. In a large skillet, melt 2 tablespoons of butter over medium-high heat. Once the butter has melted and is sizzling, add the scallops to the skillet. Cook for about 2-3 minutes on each side until they turn golden brown. Remove the scallops from the skillet and set aside.

  4. In the same skillet, melt the remaining 2 tablespoons of butter over medium heat. Add the minced garlic and cook until fragrant, about 1 minute.

  5. Pour in the heavy cream and add the lemon zest and juice. Stir well to combine and let the sauce simmer for 2-3 minutes until it thickens slightly.

  6. Return the cooked pasta to the skillet and toss it in the creamy lemon garlic sauce until well coated. Cook for an additional 2-3 minutes to allow the flavors to meld together.

  7. Gently place the seared scallops on top of the pasta, arranging them in an attractive manner. Reduce the heat to low and cover the skillet for a minute or two to warm the scallops through.

  8. Remove from heat and garnish with fresh parsley. Serve immediately and enjoy!

Tips and Variations

  • For a touch of freshness, you can add some chopped cherry tomatoes or baby spinach to the pasta during the last minute of cooking.

  • If you’re not a fan of scallops, this recipe works equally well with shrimp or even chunks of white fish like cod or halibut.

  • To enhance the lemon flavor, you can add a few drops of lemon oil or a teaspoon of lemon zest to the dish.

  • If you prefer a lighter version of this pasta, you can use half-and-half or whole milk instead of heavy cream. Just keep in mind that the sauce won’t be as rich and creamy.

  • Serve this dish with a side of crusty bread or a simple green salad to complete the meal.

Whether you’re planning a special dinner for two or hosting a dinner party, this Creamy Lemon Garlic Scallop Pasta is sure to impress. The combination of tender scallops, creamy sauce, and zesty lemon creates a harmonious blend of flavors that will leave your taste buds begging for more. So go ahead and treat yourself to this luxurious seafood pasta – you deserve it!
