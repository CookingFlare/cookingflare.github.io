Paleo Creamy Tomato Soup: A Hearty and Healthy Recipe for Fall


As the leaves begin to change and the air turns crisp, there’s nothing quite like a warm bowl of soup to embrace the flavors of fall. This Paleo Creamy Tomato Soup is not only hearty and satisfying but also aligns perfectly with a healthy lifestyle. Packed with nutritious ingredients and bursting with flavors, this recipe will become a staple in your autumn meal rotation.

Why Choose Paleo?

The Paleo diet focuses on whole, unprocessed foods that our ancestors would have consumed. This means plenty of vegetables, fruits, nuts, seeds, and lean proteins. By eliminating grains, legumes, and dairy, the Paleo approach encourages a cleaner diet that can lead to improved health and well-being. This creamy tomato soup is a perfect example of how delicious and satisfying Paleo meals can be.

Ingredients You’ll Need

To create this delicious Paleo Creamy Tomato Soup, gather the following ingredients:

  • 2 tablespoons olive oil: A healthy fat that adds flavor and richness.
  • 1 medium onion, chopped: Provides a sweet base flavor.
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced: For a robust aroma and taste.
  • 2 cans (28 ounces each) crushed tomatoes: The star of the soup, providing depth and sweetness.
  • 2 cups vegetable broth: A flavorful liquid base.
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil: Adds a fragrant herbal note.
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano: Complements the tomatoes beautifully.
  • 1/2 teaspoon red pepper flakes: For a hint of heat (optional).
  • 1 can (13.5 ounces) coconut milk: Creates a creamy texture without dairy.
  • Salt and pepper to taste: Enhances all the flavors.
  • Fresh basil leaves for garnish: Adds a touch of freshness.

Preparation Steps

Now that you have all your ingredients ready, let’s dive into the preparation of this comforting soup.

Step 1: Sauté the Aromatics

In a large pot, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Once hot, add the chopped onion and sauté for about 5 minutes until it becomes translucent. Stir in the minced garlic and cook for an additional minute, allowing the garlic to become fragrant but not browned.

Step 2: Incorporate the Tomatoes and Broth

Next, add the crushed tomatoes to the pot, followed by the vegetable broth. Stir well to combine. This is where the soup starts to take shape, and the vibrant red color begins to emerge.

Step 3: Season the Soup

Add the dried basil, oregano, and red pepper flakes (if using) to the pot. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Bring the mixture to a gentle simmer, allowing the flavors to meld together for about 15-20 minutes.

Step 4: Blend for Creaminess

Once the soup has simmered, it’s time to blend it into a creamy consistency. You can use an immersion blender directly in the pot for convenience or transfer the soup in batches to a countertop blender. Blend until smooth, and then return the soup to the pot.

Step 5: Add the Coconut Milk

Pour in the coconut milk, stirring well to combine. This addition not only makes the soup creamy but also lends a subtle sweetness that balances the acidity of the tomatoes. Allow the soup to heat through for an additional 5 minutes.

Step 6: Taste and Adjust

Before serving, taste the soup and adjust the seasoning as needed. You may want to add a bit more salt, pepper, or herbs to suit your palate.

Serving Suggestions

To serve your Paleo Creamy Tomato Soup, ladle it into bowls and garnish with fresh basil leaves for a pop of color and flavor. This soup pairs wonderfully with a side salad or some roasted vegetables for a complete meal.

If you’re looking for a little extra crunch, consider serving it with some homemade Paleo crackers or almond flour bread. The combination of creamy soup and crunchy bread is simply delightful!

Nutritional Benefits

This Paleo Creamy Tomato Soup is not only delicious but also packed with nutritional benefits:

  • Tomatoes: Rich in vitamins C and K, potassium, and antioxidants like lycopene, which have been linked to heart health.
  • Coconut Milk: Provides healthy fats, particularly medium-chain triglycerides (MCTs), which can support energy levels and metabolism.
  • Olive Oil: A source of healthy monounsaturated fats and antioxidants, promoting heart health.
  • Garlic and Onions: Both are known for their immune-boosting properties and can help reduce inflammation.

Storing and Reheating

If you find yourself with leftovers, you can store the soup in an airtight container in the refrigerator for up to 5 days. The flavors will continue to develop as it sits. When you’re ready to enjoy it again, simply reheat on the stove over medium heat until warmed through.

Freezing for Later

This soup also freezes beautifully! Allow it to cool completely, then transfer to freezer-safe containers. It can be stored in the freezer for up to 3 months. When you’re ready to eat, thaw overnight in the refrigerator and reheat on the stove.

Conclusion

Embrace the flavors of fall with this hearty and healthy Paleo Creamy Tomato Soup. It’s perfect for cozy nights in, family gatherings, or meal prep for the week ahead. With its rich flavors, creamy texture, and nutritional benefits, this soup is sure to warm your soul and nourish your body.

So, gather your ingredients, follow the steps, and enjoy a bowl of this comforting soup that celebrates the essence of autumn. Your taste buds will thank you, and your body will appreciate the wholesome, nourishing ingredients. Happy cooking!
