Peanut Butter Cup Blondies: Decadent Peanut Butter Indulgence


If you’re a peanut butter lover, get ready to indulge in the ultimate treat – Peanut Butter Cup Blondies. These decadent, melt-in-your-mouth blondies are a delightful blend of rich peanut butter flavor and sweet chocolatey goodness. Whether you’re hosting a gathering, craving a homemade dessert, or simply want to treat yourself, these Peanut Butter Cup Blondies are sure to steal the show.

Ingredients

For the Blondies:

  • 1 cup unsalted butter, melted
  • 2 cups light brown sugar, packed
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 tablespoon pure vanilla extract
  • 1 cup creamy peanut butter
  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup chocolate chips

For the Topping:

  • 1 1/2 cups milk chocolate chips
  • 1 cup peanut butter chips
  • 12 peanut butter cups, chopped

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease and line a 9x13-inch baking pan with parchment paper, leaving an overhang for easy removal.

  2. In a large mixing bowl, whisk together the melted butter and brown sugar until well combined. Add the eggs and vanilla extract, and mix until smooth.

  3. Stir in the creamy peanut butter until fully incorporated into the mixture.

  4. In a separate bowl, whisk together the flour, baking powder, and salt. Gradually add the dry ingredients to the wet ingredients, mixing until just combined. Be careful not to overmix.

  5. Gently fold in the chocolate chips until evenly distributed throughout the batter.

  6. Pour the batter into the prepared baking pan and spread it out into an even layer.

  7. In a small microwave-safe bowl, melt the milk chocolate chips and peanut butter chips together in 30-second intervals, stirring between each interval, until smooth and fully melted.

  8. Drizzle the melted chocolate and peanut butter mixture over the blondie batter, then use a knife to swirl it into the batter.

  9. Sprinkle the chopped peanut butter cups on top of the batter, pressing them in slightly.

  10. Bake the blondies in the preheated oven for 25-30 minutes, or until the top is golden brown and a toothpick inserted into the center comes out with a few moist crumbs.

  11. Allow the blondies to cool completely in the pan before lifting them out using the parchment paper overhang. Transfer to a cutting board and slice into squares.

Serving Suggestions

These Peanut Butter Cup Blondies are best enjoyed with a tall glass of cold milk or a scoop of vanilla ice cream. They make a perfect addition to any dessert spread, potluck, or special occasion. Store any leftovers in an airtight container at room temperature for up to 3 days, or in the refrigerator for up to 1 week.

Conclusion

Peanut Butter Cup Blondies are a delightful fusion of rich, buttery blondie base, luscious peanut butter, and decadent chocolate. Whether you’re a seasoned baker or a novice in the kitchen, this recipe is sure to impress and satisfy your sweet cravings. So, grab your apron, gather your ingredients, and get ready to indulge in the irresistible combination of peanut butter and chocolate with these delightful Peanut Butter Cup Blondies.
