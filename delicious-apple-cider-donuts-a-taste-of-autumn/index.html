Delicious Apple Cider Donuts: A Taste of Autumn


Fall is the perfect time to indulge in warm and comforting treats, and what better way to celebrate the season than with delicious apple cider donuts? These delightful treats are a quintessential autumn treat, combining the flavors of fresh apples, warm spices, and sweet glaze. Whether you’re enjoying them with a cup of hot apple cider or sharing them with friends and family, apple cider donuts are sure to bring a taste of autumn to your kitchen.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground nutmeg
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup apple cider
  • 1/4 cup milk
  • 2 tablespoons unsalted butter, melted
  • 1 large egg
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Glaze

  • 1 cup powdered sugar
  • 2 tablespoons apple cider
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease a donut pan with cooking spray or butter and set aside.

  2. In a large mixing bowl, whisk together the flour, sugar, baking powder, cinnamon, nutmeg, and salt. Set aside.

  3. In a separate bowl, whisk together the apple cider, milk, melted butter, egg, and vanilla extract until well combined.

  4. Pour the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients and stir until just combined. Be careful not to overmix; a few lumps are okay.

  5. Spoon or pipe the batter into the prepared donut pan, filling each cavity about 2/3 full.

  6. Bake for 12-15 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into a donut comes out clean.

  7. While the donuts are baking, prepare the glaze. In a small bowl, whisk together the powdered sugar, apple cider, and vanilla extract until smooth.

  8. Once the donuts are done baking, remove them from the oven and let them cool in the pan for a few minutes. Then, transfer them to a wire rack to cool completely.

  9. Dip each donut into the glaze, allowing any excess to drip off. You can glaze the donuts once or twice for a thicker coating, depending on your preference.

  10. Place the glazed donuts back on the wire rack to set for about 10 minutes. The glaze should become slightly firm to the touch.

  11. Serve and enjoy your delicious apple cider donuts!

Tips and Variations

  • For an extra burst of flavor, consider adding a teaspoon of orange zest to the batter.
  • Sprinkle a little cinnamon-sugar mixture on top of the glaze for an added touch of sweetness and spice.
  • If you don’t have a donut pan, you can use a mini muffin pan instead. Just adjust the baking time accordingly.

Conclusion

Delicious apple cider donuts are a delightful treat that embodies the flavors and spirit of autumn. With their warm spices, sweet glaze, and comforting texture, these donuts are perfect for enjoying on a crisp fall morning or sharing with loved ones. So, embrace the season and indulge in the taste of autumn with these delicious apple cider donuts. Your kitchen will be filled with the aroma of cinnamon and apples, creating a cozy and inviting atmosphere that is sure to warm your heart and satisfy your taste buds.
