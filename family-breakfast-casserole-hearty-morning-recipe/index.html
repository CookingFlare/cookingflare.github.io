Family Breakfast Casserole: Hearty Morning Recipe


Are you tired of the same old breakfast routine? Do you want to treat your family to a hearty and delicious breakfast that will keep them satisfied until lunchtime? Look no further than this Family Breakfast Casserole recipe! Packed with savory flavors and wholesome ingredients, this casserole is the perfect way to start your day.

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound of breakfast sausage
  • 6 slices of bread, cubed
  • 1 cup of shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1 cup of diced bell peppers
  • 1 cup of diced onions
  • 8 large eggs
  • 2 cups of milk
  • 1 teaspoon of garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon of mustard powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C) and grease a 9x13 inch baking dish.

  2. In a skillet over medium heat, cook the breakfast sausage until browned and crumbled. Remove from the skillet and set aside.

  3. In the same skillet, sauté the diced bell peppers and onions until they are softened and fragrant. Remove from heat and set aside.

  4. In a large bowl, whisk together the eggs, milk, garlic powder, mustard powder, salt, and pepper.

  5. Place the cubed bread in the greased baking dish, then top with the cooked sausage, sautéed vegetables, and shredded cheddar cheese.

  6. Pour the egg mixture over the top of the casserole, making sure to evenly distribute it.

  7. Cover the baking dish with foil and bake for 45 minutes. Then, remove the foil and continue baking for an additional 15 minutes, or until the casserole is set and the top is golden brown.

  8. Once done, remove the casserole from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes before slicing. Garnish with fresh parsley before serving.

Why This Recipe Is Perfect for Family Breakfast:

This Family Breakfast Casserole is the ultimate morning comfort food. It’s not only delicious but also incredibly versatile. You can customize it with your favorite ingredients such as mushrooms, spinach, or even leftover roasted vegetables. The best part is that you can prepare it the night before and simply pop it in the oven in the morning, making it perfect for busy weekdays and lazy weekends alike.

The combination of savory sausage, fluffy eggs, and gooey cheese creates a symphony of flavors that will have your family coming back for seconds. Plus, the addition of bell peppers and onions adds a delightful crunch and sweetness to every bite.

Not to mention, this casserole is a great way to sneak in some extra veggies into your family’s diet without compromising on taste. It’s a win-win situation!

So, the next time you want to treat your loved ones to a special breakfast, whip up this Family Breakfast Casserole and watch as their faces light up with delight. Whether it’s a leisurely Sunday brunch or a quick weekday meal, this hearty casserole is sure to become a family favorite in no time.

Final Thoughts:

In conclusion, this Family Breakfast Casserole is a delicious and satisfying way to kickstart your day. With its simple preparation and delectable flavors, it’s the perfect recipe to bring the family together around the breakfast table. So, grab your ingredients and get cooking – your family will thank you for it!
